Chaurus are large, carnivorous [arthropods](Lore_Arthropod.md) found in caves deep below the surface of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Load-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Child Best-2)</sup> and on the surface in places like the swamps of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup> They possess highly poisonous saliva as well as a powerful bite.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Load-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Chaurus are bred and raised by the [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md), who use them as guards or slay them for their chitin.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Falmer Stuff-5)</sup> In appearance they are large black insectoid creatures with four limbs, they possess blue sensory organs and large mandibles on their head, they posses pincers on the end of their tail. Mature **[Chaurus Hunters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chaurus_Hunter)** differ in that their body proportions are thinner, their two front limbs are more prominent, and they are supported by large newly grown wings.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup>

## Life Cycle
Chaurus begin their lives in small blue eggs that are laid in large clusters and externally fertilized by males.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Deshaan-6)</sup> Though an alternative reproductive method where Chaurus inject eggs into living beings has also been observed.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> When Chaurus hatch they start out as small hatchlings known as chitterlings, which possess lightly colored chitin compared to the black adults.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Chitterling-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Chaurus that grow especially large are known as Chaurus Reapers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Skyrim Reaper-10)</sup>

When a Chaurus reaches the end of its natural lifespan, its body undergoes a dramatic transformation. After days of death-like stillness, it is reborn as a fledgeling Chaurus Hunter that bursts free from the husk of the Chaurus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Load-1)</sup>

## Uses
Chaurus chitin is strong, light, and perfectly suited for pieces of armor.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BLChitin-11)</sup> The Falmer make much use of tamed chaurus, breeding them and using their chitin for their arms, armor, and other amenities.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Falmer Stuff-5)</sup> Armor and weapons made of Chaurus chitin have also seen use by the Blackreach Vanguard,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Motif-12)</sup> and rarely in the [Bloodfall Kingdom](Lore_Bloodfall_Kingdom.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> [Chaurus eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chaurus_Eggs) and [chaurus hunter antennae](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chaurus_Hunter_Antennae) are often harvested for their [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) properties.

Chaurus meat is used in a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) [dish](Lore_Nord_Cuisine.md#Meats_and_Fish) called Chaurus Pie. The midsection of a Chaurus is roasted on a spit, basted with sauce made from tomatoes, water, pepper, salt and honey boiled together. When the basting and cooking is done, the combination is then baked into a pie together with potatoes, carrots and apples, turnips can optionally be added, too. This pie is then baked until browned.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

Occasionally others besides the Falmer have tamed Chaurus or kept them as pets, it is said that when raised from an egg they are capable of forming attachments and will obey commands.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Chitterling-8)</sup> The [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) tamed Chaurus in their experiments in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), using them as guards.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
