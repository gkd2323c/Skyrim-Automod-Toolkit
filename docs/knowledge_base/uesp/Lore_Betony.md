[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/DF-map-Betony.png/200px-DF-map-Betony.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Betony.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Betony.png) Map of Betony circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) The Isle of Betony,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NWOB-1)</sup> called **Betnikh** by the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOB-2)</sup> is a strategically important island in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), off the coast of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup>

Besides being a prosperous fishing island,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NWOB-1)</sup> Betony is also known for its lush, fertile interior. Its habitat includes the rare and exotic sixteen-eyed Habanoo caterpillar.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HC-4)</sup> Perhaps because of this rich reputation, it has been a frequent target of pirates and others throughout the years.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup> Any would-be attackers must face Betony's steep cliffs and defensible nature, which may have inspired early legends claiming it was home to a mighty rock monster that flung its craggy spines into the sea, threatening sailors who drifted too close.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AAOB-5)</sup> The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampirism.md) bloodline in the region is the [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md), and the regional deity is [Mara](Lore_Mara.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-load-Betnikh.jpg/200px-ON-load-Betnikh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Betnikh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Betnikh.jpg) Betony wayshrine Skyspire Keep was carved from the rock of the cliffs during the reign of Emperor [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md), and has been touted as impenetrable and invincible, having held against countless attacks.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AAOB-5)</sup> [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) inhabited the island for untold years, but in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), despite the reputation of their defenses, they were driven off by War Chief Yzzgol and his [Seamount Clan](Lore_Seamount_Clan.md), who renamed the island Betnikh.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONKhazun-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Online-7)</sup> When the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) was formed, Bethikh was still ruled by the Seamount Clan, who did not initially join it. The Clan only joined the Covenant after the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) helped them repel the invading [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md)'s branch led by [Vardan](Lore_Vardan.md) and decided the fate of a dangerous Ayleid relic in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Online-7)</sup>

Over the years, Betony changed hands again. Bretons once again took control and re-inhabited the island, though politically, they eventually came under the loose control of the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md). The island is perhaps best known for triggering the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md) in [3E 403](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_403). The island's ruler, Lord [Mogref](Lore_Mogref.md), asked King [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) for protection against pirates and brigands. When Lysandus agreed, Sentinel attempted to enforce its own claim to Betony, resulting in a direct war between the two strongest powers in the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NWOB-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FWOB-8)</sup> With Daggerfall's victory in the war, Betony fell under their sway, leaving it solely in the hands of Bretons once more.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup> In the period leading up to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), the town of Betony was a major settlement on the island,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DFDemo-9)</sup> although its political capital was Daggerfall City.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup>

### Ruins
- **Carved Hills** — A woodland in the island's southeast.
- **Carzog's Demise** — Ayleid ruin in northeast Betony.
- **Eyearata** — Small Ayleid ruin on an island off the coast of Betony, west of Moriseli.
- **Hold of Wickcroft** — The ruins were known to be inhabited by vampires circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405)
- **Moriseli** — Ayleid ruin in northwest Betony.
- **[Seaveil Spire](Lore_Seaveil_Spire.md)** — Ancient [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) ruins that are located on an atoll somewhere off the coast of Betony. The islet's lagoon is noted for a strange underwater anomaly discovered by the [Tower](Lore_The_Tower.md) — [worshipping](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) Nedic tribe who settled the atoll: a vast air pocket beneath the water's surface, maintained by some unknown force throughout the millennia.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
- **Skyspire Keep** — [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) era fortress carved into the cliffs of Betony.
- The **Temple of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md)** — Emperor [Pelagius Septim III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md), died after a brief fever in [3E 153](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_153) in this temple.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TMOP-11)</sup> At some point, the temple was destroyed and members of the [Apostles of Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Apostles_of_Light) took every stone and rebuilt it into the [Howling Halls](Lore_Howling_Halls.md). The halls are located in the sorrowed hills of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SIRa.27kheran-12)</sup>
