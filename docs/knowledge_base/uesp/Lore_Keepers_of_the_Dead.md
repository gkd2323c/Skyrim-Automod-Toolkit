# Keepers of the Dead

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/ON-concept-Keeper_of_the_Dead.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Keeper_of_the_Dead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Keeper_of_the_Dead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Keeper_of_the_Dead.jpg) Uniform of the Keepers of the Dead [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/ON-head_marking-Necrom_Armiger_Face_Markings.jpg/200px-ON-head_marking-Necrom_Armiger_Face_Markings.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-head_marking-Necrom_Armiger_Face_Markings.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-head_marking-Necrom_Armiger_Face_Markings.jpg) Mark of the Dead Keepers *The voice of the dead must be listened to.
Descendants must always be directed to the ancestors they seek.
The homes of the dead will be cleaned regularly.
Offerings shall be left at the tombs of the dead.
None shall disturb the rest of the ancestor spirits.
When the time comes, treat the remains of the deceased with dignity.
The dead must cared for.
All these duties I swear to uphold upon the life I live and the one I shall inhabit thereafter.*
— *[Oath of the Keepers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oath_of_the_Keepers)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oot K-1)</sup> The Keepers of the Dead (or the **Dead Keepers**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TS34S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PNK:5D-3)</sup> are a hierarchy of priests and attendants in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) who serve as the caretakers of the large Necropolis expanse in [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md), the City of the Dead. While an independent order,<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore_talk:Keepers_of_the_Dead)*]</sup> they ultimately answer to the authority of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGadayn-4)</sup> They are responsible for handling the deceased [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) who are brought and prepared for burial with the appropriate rituals within the Catacombs of Necrom.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1E-5)</sup> Wooden ramps on the city's staircases serve to transport the bodies.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VG:TP-6)</sup> As a sign of great honor and sacrifice, often part of the family member's will, ancestors may grant that part of their remains be retained to serve as part of a ghost fence protecting the clan's shrine and family precincts. The stewards handle the arrangement, where the ancestor's body part (and sometimes their entire skeleton/preserved corpse) is incorporated with solemn magic and ceremony into a clan ghost fence.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Aat D-7)</sup> Heavy cold-iron shackles and ice [staves](Lore_Staves.md) used by the keeper guards to keep recalcitrant undead and unruly ancestor spirits in line.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DKS-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Mortal Chill-9)</sup> The keepers store the [Indigo Tomes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Indigo_Tomes) in the vaults of the city,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ATUQ-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-LA-11)</sup> and are in charge of other monasteries in the area.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-In N-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESO-13)</sup>

The ancient traditions of the Keepers predate the formation of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1E-5)</sup>
