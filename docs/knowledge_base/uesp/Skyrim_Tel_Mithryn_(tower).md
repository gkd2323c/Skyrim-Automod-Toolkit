[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn_Tower.jpg) Tel Mithryn Tower **Tel Mithryn** is the main tower and home of [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) and his apprentice [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md) in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).

It is by far the largest of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, between [Tel Mithryn Apothecary](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.md) to the north and [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.md) to the west.

## Tel Mithryn (tower)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Transport.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Transport.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Transport.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Transport.jpg) The levitation shaft [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Staff_Enchanter.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Staff_Enchanter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Staff_Enchanter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Tower_Staff_Enchanter.jpg) The staff enchanter room The entrance leads into the bottom of an enchanted vertical shaft, which allows you to float up and down between the entrance and the living quarters above. It is possible to fall and die from the upper area if the levitation activator is not used; [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) should be asked to remain outside for this reason (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The levitation shaft is in the middle of the circular main chamber, with a railing around the edge. The chamber features many tables, sets of shelves, and crates holding a wide variety of [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), and [scrolls](Skyrim_Scrolls.md). The scattered ingredients include seven samples of [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), as Neloth enjoys canis root tea, brewed by his steward. Against the railing next to the arrival point is a table holding large smeltable Dwemer items like [solid Dwemer metal](Skyrim_Solid_Dwemer_Metal.md), with a second table holding similar Dwemer items nearby to the southeast. Farther to the southeast are two tables covered with maps of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), respectively. To the northeast is a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), an owned bedroll, and a few bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and [empty wine bottles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle). To the northwest are two [arcane enchanters](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) set into two of the tables. Four [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) and one [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) fungi grow near the outer wall among the tables and crates to the northwest and southwest.

To the south is a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_Enchanter_Key) -locked round door, beyond which is Neloth's [staff enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md), a third arcane enchanter, and the *[Black Book: The Hidden Twilight](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Hidden_Twilight_(book).md)*. The key is obtained from Neloth after completing the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md). It is possible to buy [unenchanted staves](Skyrim_Staves.md#Unenchanted_Staves) from Neloth and create your own staves using [heart stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone) at the staff enchanter. To the east is a storeroom containing another chest, several sacks, some empty and unusable barrels, and more crates. To the north is a bedroom containing a single owned bed and more scattered full and empty wine bottles. To the west is the withered part of the tower, which will be replaced by a small prison holding two [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) after completing the quest [Healing a House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_a_House).

## Notes
- After completing the quest [Telvanni Research](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telvanni_Research), a number of potions and scrolls in the tower become unowned and free to take.
- If you commit a crime and Neloth becomes hostile, you may have difficulty escaping the tower, as the levitation trigger will become inactive.
- This location is also known as "Tel Mithryn Tower", one of the potential locations mentioned in the [*Scribbles of a Madman*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scribbles_of_a_Madman).

## Bugs
- If a follower follows you into the tower, it's possible they will not exit with you when you leave. If you reenter the tower, and the follower wasn't tagged [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md), you may find them dead on the upper or lower platforms. This is most likely caused by their inability to interact with the tower's levitation shaft, causing them to fall and die. This can be avoided by asking them to wait outside before you enter.
- Although Neloth tells you he will reserve a bed and a chest for your use after completing the quest [Old Friends](Skyrim_Old_Friends.md), there will still be no chest clearly reserved for your use, and the bed in the steward's house will remain the only unowned bed or bedroll in the settlement. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- The tower's local map shows two exits, but there is actually only one.
- The locked door can sometimes disappear, giving you free access to the black book and staff enchanter before completing the quest to obtain the key for the door. <sup>**?**</sup>
- It's possible, due to the tower's layout, for both Talvas and Neloth to fall down the shaft during their normal schedule. Normally, they will use the levitation mechanism to float back to the upper level, but if this happens to Neloth during the beginning of Old Friends (before he divines where Illdari is), he won't levitate himself back to the top and the quest will be stuck, requiring you to reload a save from before it happened. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The door to Neloth's staff enchanter blocks navigation when open, which can lead to some of his quests failing to run because he won't leave the room. The collision on the mesh apparently cuts the navmesh when it shouldn't. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. The door mesh has been modified so both halves will pull up into the ceiling to avoid this.
- Incomplete and/or broken mavmeshes can fail to prevent NPCs from walking off the edge of the platform where the "elevator" is. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.5, fixes this bug.
