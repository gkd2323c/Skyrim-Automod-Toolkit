# Witch Knights

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/ON-crown_store-Witch_Knight_Charger.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Witch_Knight_Charger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Witch_Knight_Charger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Witch_Knight_Charger.jpg) Witch Knight Charger [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/ON-place-Markarth_Stables.jpg/200px-ON-place-Markarth_Stables.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Markarth_Stables.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Markarth_Stables.jpg) Witch Knight mounts in the Markarth Stables ca. 2E 582 Witch Knights or **Witch-Knights**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WKD-1)</sup> were a [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [faction](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md#Factions) of mounted [knightly](Lore_Knights.md) warriors who were known for breeding [Witch Knight Charger horses](Lore_Horse.md#Witch_Knight_Charger). During the Reachfolk invasion of the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), they were profoundly impressed by the power and splendor of [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [mounted knights](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md). They decided to breed their own hardy northern steeds native to the [Druadach Mountains](Lore_Druadach_Mountains.md) such as distrustful, tenacious, temperamental, and belligerent [Reachmares](Lore_Horse.md#Reachmare) to the same size.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WKC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Witch-Knight Chargers were seen in the stables of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MS-5)</sup>

Little is known of the Witch Knights themselves, except that their armor was usually medium weight, and designed for both spellcasting and traditional combat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WKD-1)</sup> Knights of the Reachfolk origins were not unheard of, though their connection to the Witch Knights is unknown.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Dreadhorn_Firebrand-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Chieftain_Frostfang-7)</sup>
