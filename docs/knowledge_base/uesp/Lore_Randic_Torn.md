Randic Torn was an Elden Yokeda of the [Yokudan Empire](Lore_Yokudan_Empire.md). He was a commoner who took over the government of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) after the assassination of [Mansel Sesnit](Lore_Mansel_Sesnit.md) in [1E 617](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_617). He tried to continue the work of Sesnit and unify the Yokudan Empire after almost three centuries of internal conflict. He revived the age-old rift between the [sword singers](Lore_Sword-singers.md) and the commoners by introducing restrictions on the wearing of swords. "Torn's Sword-Hunt", as it was known, meant that only the singers were allowed to wear swords, which distinguished them from the rest of the population.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTHATH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGTNBat M-2)</sup> His controversial decrees led to a burst of emigration of Yokudans to the rocky islands east of the continent.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SH-3)</sup> Although Torn did much to settle the empire into its pre-strife ways, by the time of his death in [1E 737](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_737) internal disturbances still had not been completely eliminated. Upon his death, a vicious civil war broke out.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTHATH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGTNBat M-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Randic Torn reigned for a total of 120 years, having a significantly longer lifespan compared to modern humans.
