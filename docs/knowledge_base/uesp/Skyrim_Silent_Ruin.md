[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-place-Silent_Ruin.jpg/200px-SR-place-Silent_Ruin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Silent_Ruin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Silent_Ruin.jpg) Silent Ruin **Silent Ruin** is a small [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md). It is north of the Dwarven city. The building is devoid of any [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) or [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md); however, you do need to keep an eye out for traps.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
Outside the building against the northeast corner is a large brass pipe with a [crimson nirnroot](Skyrim_Crimson_Nirnroot.md) growing beside it. A path north of the building leads down to a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) camp with two tents and two Falmer. Four [dead salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) hang from a rack next to the tents, and there is a [geode vein](Skyrim_Geode_Vein.md) just northeast of the two tents.

### Silent Ruin
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Silent_Ruin.jpg) Interior view The entrance is on the southwest side of the building, and is unlocked. It leads into a single room, with a short flight of stairs leading down to the main floor. Two huge stone pillars to the northeast and southwest support a very high roof. Opposite the entrance is a throne on a slightly raised platform. On the seat of the throne is a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(book).md)*; however, care is needed, as there is a [pressure plate](Skyrim_Pressure_Plate.md) in front of the throne that triggers a [spinning blade trap](Skyrim_Spinning_Blades.md). To the right of the throne is a crimson nirnroot growing in a large metal urn. Finally, in the northwest corner, there is a set of Dwemer shelves against the west wall holding various small Dwemer items, and to the right, against the north wall, is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md).

## Bugs
- Followers will not follow you into the building, and instead will run north, possibly attempting to leave Blackreach to find you.
