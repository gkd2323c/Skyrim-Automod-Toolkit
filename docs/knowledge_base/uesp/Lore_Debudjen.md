Debudjen was a potentially fictional female [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) mentioned in the historical-fiction series, [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md). She was encountered by the [titular character](Lore_Edward.md) and his companions in a village of [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) in a high meadow near [Raven Spring](Lore_Raven_Spring.md). In the tale, the party accompanying Edward met [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) and her on Loredas the 5th of Mid Year. She was described as smaller than Akatosh and polite.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Another female dragon, the esteemed peer of Akatosh [Ma-Tylda](Lore_Ma-Tylda.md) is introduced in King Edward series.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup>
- The French translation of the book *[A Child's Tamriel Bestiary](Lore_A_Child%27s_Tamriel_Bestiary.md)* mentions [Jills](Lore_Jill.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ACTBFrench-2)</sup> which have been present in several unofficial texts,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-500COTOYTR-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-NMI-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-MKA-5)</sup> and defined as female Dragons responsible for repairing linear Time after a [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md).<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-NHOTSIT-6)</sup> As a female Dragon, Debudjen may be one of the Jills.
