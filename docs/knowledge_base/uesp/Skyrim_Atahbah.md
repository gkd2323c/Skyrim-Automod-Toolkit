[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-npc-Atahbah.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Atahbah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Atahbah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Atahbah.jpg) Atahbah outside of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) **Atahbah** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [pawnbroker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker) in [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad)'s [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). She will become a [fence](Skyrim_Fence_(merchant).md) for [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) members after completing the quest [Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest).

Atahbah wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). She is equipped with a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest)**: Deliver [moon sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar) to [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad).

## Standard Merchandise
This is the merchandise found in Atahbah's [Merchant Chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant_Chest). These items are only available if purchased from the merchant; they cannot be pickpocketed or stolen. They are restocked every two days.

## Notes
- Internally, all data refers to her as **Atahba**, which is also the name of a Khajiit previously [seen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Atahba) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* also refers to her as such.
- The guide also identifies her as Ri'saad's wife, although this is never stated in-game or in the files.
