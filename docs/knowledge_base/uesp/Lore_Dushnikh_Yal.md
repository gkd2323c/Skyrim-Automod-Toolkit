## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/ON-place-Village_of_the_Lost_03.jpg/200px-ON-place-Village_of_the_Lost_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Village_of_the_Lost_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Village_of_the_Lost_03.jpg) Dushnikh's Shadow longhouse in the [Village of the Lost](Lore_Village_of_the_Lost.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the stronghold was built onto the side of a mountain and was ruled by Chieftain Ugorz. Sometime before the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), his envious son Begnar sought to be chieftain and made a deal with the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) Grexev. To fulfill the terms of the deal, Grexev caused a rockslide that swallowed the entire stronghold, with Begnar's hopes that his father would be one of the casualties, but to his misfortune he too had perished in the rockslide and the stronghold was dragged into [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s [Village of the Lost](Lore_Village_of_the_Lost.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONBegnar-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOHoles-3)</sup> It would come to be known as Dushnikh's Shadow as daedra and undead tortured the spirits of the village's inhabitants, but former chieftain Ugorz later managed to escape with the help of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESO-4)</sup>

Following Molag Bal's defeat and the end of the Planemeld it is unknown if Dushnikh's Shadow was returned to Tamriel or if the ruins remained in the realm. Whatever the case, by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Dushnikh Yal had become an isolated but prosperous stronghold, with one longhouse and an [Orichalcum](Lore_Orichalcum.md) mine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> It was led by Chief Burguk, son of wise woman Murbul and the previous chief, who was married to hunt-wife Arob, forge-wife Gharol, and Shel. His older brother Oglub worked in the mines and his younger brother Ghorbash left to travel with the [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) for many years before returning to the stronghold, and had great respect for his leadership. His sons Nagrub and Umurn were strong warriors, uneasy with the prospect of one day fighting each other for chiefdom, while his daughter Lash left the stronghold to work in [Karthwasten](Lore_Karthwasten.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> Lash's mother Gharol enlisted the help of the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) to deliver a sword for her daughter.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRGharol Message-5)</sup>
