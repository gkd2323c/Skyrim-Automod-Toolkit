[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius.jpg) Florentius Baenius [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Florentius_Baenius_02.jpg) Florentius at [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) **Florentius Baenius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Restoration Master) added by the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), who you recruit into the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). He is a master [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md), and will sell you a wide variety of [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) -related items. He also sells the spells [Stendarr's Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Aura) and [Vampire's Bane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Bane).

Florentius believes himself to be a direct channel of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) which leads to some interesting and humorous dialogue. This is a frequent topic of conversation for Dawnguard members [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md) and [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard). It is also the source of some previous friction in his relationship with the Dawnguard leader, [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md). After you join the Dawnguard and learn that you can find the [Moth Priest](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) at [Forebears' Holdout](Skyrim_Forebears%27_Holdout.md) during [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md), Sorine Jurard will request that you speak with Isran about recruiting Florentius. Isran will eventually agree, and inform you that Florentius can be found in [Ruunvald](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruunvald_Excavation), beginning the quest [Bolstering the Ranks](Skyrim_Bolstering_the_Ranks.md). You must travel there and fight your way through many [charmed vigilants](Skyrim_Charmed_Vigilant.md) and the warlock [Minorne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Minorne) to save Florentius.

Initially, Florentius wears [monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monk_Robes), a pair of [steel plate boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots), a pair of [steel plate gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets), and an [amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md). Later, he dons a set of Dawnguard equipment, including gray [Dawnguard heavy armor](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Armor.md) with [Dawnguard boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Boots.md) and [Dawnguard gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Gauntlets.md). He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), and has a 25% chance of carrying an additional 1-9 gold.

## Quest-Related Events

### Bolstering the Ranks
Upon speaking to him, he will express:

- *"I knew it! I knew Arkay would save me. I asked for help, and he sent you! You are a very welcome addition to this dreary place, my friend. I owe both you and Arkay a great deal. I'm sure I'll manage to repay him later, but you... What can I do to thank you?"*

- **You can meet me at Fort Dawnguard.**
- *"I suppose I could... what, praytell, is there?"*

- **Isran needs your help.**
- *"Isran? My help? Is this... some kind of a joke? Did Arkay put you up to this? Isran's done nothing but mock me. He's never given me the respect I deserve."*

Either of these dialogue choices will lead to:

- **Please, we need your help.**
- *"Look, I've just gotten myself out of quite a mess here, in case you haven't noticed, and while I appreciate your help, I... What's that? No, that's not what I... Yes, but... Are you sure? Really? Fine. Arkay says it's a good idea for me to go. I don't agree, but he's not the sort of fellow you can just ignore. I'll see you at Fort Dawnguard, then. Don't worry, Arkay will show me the way."*

He will then move to Fort Dawnguard, where he remains from then on.

### Rescue

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"Arkay's been watching over me for years now. He says he's not too sure about you yet, though."*
- *"I once killed thirty vampires with my bare hands, you know. Ask Arkay, he'll tell you."*
- *"Don't you worry about me. I've been through worse. I'll come out of this just fine."*
- *"It is my sworn duty to see Arkay's wrath delivered to all the undead. It's usually a good bit of fun, too."*
- *"Arkay be with you, my friend."*
- *"What can I do for you?"*

After he is back at Fort Dawnguard, if you ask him what he has for sale, Florentius may say:

- *"Potions my friend, potions! Possibly some spells. Maybe some other things I've forgotten about. Actually I'm not sure. Let's see, shall we?"*
- *"Oh, well... some... things, I suppose. Remedies, cures, a poison or two."*
- *"Look, I'll be honest with you. I have no idea. But I'm sure we can find something worth your time."*

You can also ask him about the Dawnguard's history, to which he will reply:

- **Do you know anything about the old Dawnguard?**
- *"Oh, I've read a few things. Heard a few more things. Arkay's told me a bit as well. The story they'd like you to believe is that they were such a noble bunch, doing the honorable thing and saving lives. Only I hear that's not quite how it happened. Secret organization, you see. No one to blame when homes are broken into, things stolen. Heard they'd go on midnight raids, pillaging and looting, and then stash the spoils somewhere, guarded by their sigil. Arkay tells me the Sun Fire spell can break open those caches, but I've never found one. I think he might be lying to me."'*

If your skill in Restoration is less than 90, asking Florentius for Restoration training will result in the response:

- *"A noble cause. Arkay likes the idea very much, so I'm happy to help."*

If your skill in Restoration is at 90 or above, he will instead say:

- *"You know more than I do now. Arkay has us both beaten, but he's not giving up all his secrets."*

He will exit conversations with:

- *"Arkay bless you, friend."*
- *"We'll speak again soon."*
- *"Farewell."*

In combat, Florentius may use taunts such as:

- *"Arkay and I will stand triumphant!"*
- *"You can't beat us!"*
- *"You'll be meeting my friend Arkay very, very soon!"*

## Notes
- Whether or not Florentius actually converses with Arkay is debatable. However, there is a precedent in the Sybil of Dibella, who is stated by [Hamal](Skyrim_Hamal.md) to be perpetually in direct communion with Dibella. Florentius also regularly displays facts and knowledge that couldn't have been obtained in a conventional manner.
- If you rescue Florentius during [Bolstering the Ranks](Skyrim_Bolstering_the_Ranks.md) when [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md) is also active, he may also be present when [Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) reads [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md)'s [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll_(Sun).md).
