[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/DF-map-Antiphyllos.png/200px-DF-map-Antiphyllos.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Antiphyllos.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Antiphyllos.png) Antiphyllos ca. 3E 405 Antiphyllos is a region in northern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and is part of the greater [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. The region's monarchy was once connected to the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md), the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) family that ruled the province during its time under the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md). Some of their last known descendants were Grandee [Zizzeen](Lore_Zizzeen.md) of Antiphyllos and his relative, High King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWAHD-2)</sup> The local [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of the region have the utmost respect for [Divad Hunding](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md), one of the great [sword-singers](Lore_Sword-singers.md) of Redguard history. They observe the holiday, [Divad Etep't](Lore_Divad_Etep%27t.md) on the 12th of [Sun's Height](Lore_Sun%27s_Height.md), which is a day of mourning dedicated to the warrior.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-3)</sup> The [Anthotis](Lore_Anthotis.md) clan of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) and the [Dust Witches](Lore_Witches.md) are active in the region.

## History
It is unknown when Antiphyllos was founded though supposedly, Divad Hunding was interred in a private tomb somewhere in the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-3)</sup> And while sources suggest that he was actually kept in the [Hall of Heroes](Lore_Hall_of_Heroes.md) in southern [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOShores-4)</sup> modern-day sources claim that his tomb in Antiphyllos is almost certainly genuine.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-3)</sup>

The Grandees of Antiphyllos, specifically Zizzeen of the Crowns, were related to the Phyllocid Dynasty,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TABKF-5)</sup> the family that ruled Hammerfell during the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md)'s reign.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWAHD-2)</sup> One of these nobles, Grandeya Fanesh married Grandee [Makala](Lore_Makala.md), who himself descended from High King [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md). Their only issue was Fahara'jad, a [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) noble, and a former Prince of Antiphyllos that became the King of Sentinel after his predecessor, [Ramzi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ramzi), died during the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) outbreak.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TEGH-6)</sup> His son, the heir apparent, Prince [Azah](Lore_Azah.md) was known as the *"Lion of Antiphyllos."*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) in [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the County of Antiphyllos was ruled by an unnamed Countess. It was neighbored by several other nations including [Ayasofya](Lore_Ayasofya.md) to the northeast, [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) to the northwest, and the Alik'r Desert all around. When the province was incorporated in the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), the county's patron deity was [Mara](Lore_Mara.md), who is represented through the [Benevolence of Mara](Lore_Benevolence_of_Mara.md). The only other city in the region was Jalallaaria on the northern border.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFAnti-7)</sup> When the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) took place on the 9th of [Frostfall](Lore_Frostfall.md) in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the Kingdom of Sentinel took over the northern section of Hammerfell, including Antiphyllos. Under Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md), they controlled everything from [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md) on the west coast to [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam_(county).md) on the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TWITW-8)</sup>

## City of Antyphyllos
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/DF-Location-27939-Automap.png/200px-DF-Location-27939-Automap.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-27939-Automap.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-27939-Automap.png) Map of Antyphyllos ca. 3E 405 **Antyphyllos** is the capital of the Antiphyllos region and it is located in the southern section of the area,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-IBMap Antho-9)</sup> before it seamlessly merges with the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TA-10)</sup> Prince Fahara'jad would often hunt for birds in a place called the Garden of the Grandees with his [ivory](Lore_Ivory.md) bow. According to *[The All-Beneficent King Fahara'jad](Lore_The_All-Beneficent_King_Fahara%27jad.md)*, he had a frightful encounter with a [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) and after the resulting intervention from [Onsi](Lore_Onsi.md), he ran to the Holy Temple to consult with the Priest of Onsi, who realized it was the first part of the Prophecies of Monarchy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TABKF-5)</sup>
