[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/ON-place-Sea_Keep.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sea_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sea_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sea_Keep.jpg) Sea Keep ca. 2E 582 Sea Keep (formerly known as **Aldveyond Beldaburk**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup> is a stronghold found in the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), amidst the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). This bulwark serves as the first line of defense for the west coast and [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md), it is an impenetrable fortress with exponential power that can overwhelm an entire fleet. Underneath Sea Keep is [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) ruins that channels [magic](Lore_Magic.md) across the stronghold through fonts, giving it the power that it is known for.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPandermalion-2)</sup>

## History
Sea Keep has been present for many untold years, built atop of Aldmeri ruins that supply its immense power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPandermalion-2)</sup> In ancient times, it was known as Aldveyond Beldaburk but it eventually took the name it has now, *"Sea Keep."*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup> It was designed to be impregnable from the outside, however, it can be compromised from the inside. This did not happen for thousands of years until in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when the Storm Sharks, a prominent [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) militia at the time had infiltrated Sea Keep's defenses and take claim of the garrison. The Sea Mage, Urlsar had conjured a powerful hurricane from inside the fort, planning to wreak havoc across the coastline with it. The commander of Sea Keep, Pandermalion delved into the ancient ruins with a [newcomer](Lore_Soulless_One.md) to sabotage them from the inside, and together with the Storm Shark's forsaken captain, Castire, the three managed to prevent the disaster. The [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) had later dubbed this event as the *"Sack of Sea Keep."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOStorming-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Sea Keep was an active settlement. It was ruled by Countess Calina and it had a rivalry with [Corgrad Wastes](Lore_Corgrad_Wastes.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> Sea Keep was neighbored by several settlements, including [Karnwasten Moor](Lore_Karnwasten_Moor.md) to the north, Marbruk Brook to the northeast, and White Guard to the east.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup>
