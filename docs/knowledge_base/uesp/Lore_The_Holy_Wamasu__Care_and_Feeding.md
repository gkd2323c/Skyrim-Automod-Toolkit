The Holy Wamasu: Care and Feeding *Tips on feeding a wamasu* *
---
While the holy [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md) will eat anything, a wise keeper ensures a variance in its diet to keep the sacred beast happy and content. When the wamasu is content, the keeper is happy … and safe.

Do not overfeed the holy wamasu, however! A bloated lightning dragon discharges its excess in several ways. Some are distasteful; others are dangerous.

Experiment with a mix of [mud crabs](Lore_Mudcrab.md), [kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md), and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md). Remember, the holy wamasu prefers a live diet, so keep its meal both healthy and whole until feeding time.
