[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg) Hrotanda Vale circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/OB-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg/200px-OB-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Hrotanda_Vale.jpg) Hrotanda Vale circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Hrotanda Vale is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) near [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md) on the edge of the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md), near the fringes the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) and [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the entrance to Hrotanda had been recently boarded off. The nearby Fort Rayles was in the controlled territory of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), and the [Shadowed Path](Lore_Shadowed_Path.md) necromancers had murdered the owner of a tower just north of the ruins and set up a base of operations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins had opened and the subterranean passages occupied by a group of bandits.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
