[3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Elenglynn (also called **Elynglenn**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TKOTN-1)</sup> is an [Ayleid ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) located in the western edge of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md), in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is specifically located in middle of the [Imperial Reserve](Lore_Imperial_Reserve.md), between [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md) and [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBElenglynn-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBCyrodiil-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-quest-Paths_of_Chaos.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Paths_of_Chaos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Paths_of_Chaos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Paths_of_Chaos.jpg) A vision of Hermaeous Mora confronting Ithelia within the citadel of Elenglynn (ESO) Elenglynn was an active settlement during the time of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) until their demise after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) of Elenglynn worshipped the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md), and the settlement contained a shrine to her.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONPaths Of Chaos-4)</sup> At that time, Elenglynn was a proud citadel, and its people were wise and beautiful. Ithelia forged a pact with the Ayleids of Elenglynn to protect them and "open the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md) before them".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruins of Elenglynn were occupied by the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md), who were working to restore Ithelia to power. Ithelia herself visited the ruin when she was trying to recover her memories after escaping from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s prison.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONPaths Of Chaos-4)</sup> The Altmer scholar Leramil theorized that Elenglynn was most likely a temple or a monastery during the time of the Ayleids. By this time, little of the Ayleid architecture endured intact aboveground, although the trap-ridden maze of the ruin and its vaults remained underground.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONPaths Of Chaos-4)</sup>

In the early [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Sir [Amiel Lannus](Lore_Amiel_Lannus.md) and his cohorts, Sir Caius and Sir Torolf of the [Knights of the Nine](Lore_Knights_of_the_Nine.md), quested for the [Cuirass of the Crusader](Lore_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader.md), an artifact unseen since the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). After their battle with the [Wyrm](Lore_Dragon.md) of Elynglenn, they managed to recover the holy relic and their story became widespread, elevating the Knights of the Nine's status in the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TKOTN-1)</sup> By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins were taken over by a coven of [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) and guarded by the undead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBElenglynn-2)</sup>
