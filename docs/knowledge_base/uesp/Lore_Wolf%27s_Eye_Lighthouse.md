[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/ON-place-Wolf%27s_Eye_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-ON-place-Wolf%27s_Eye_Lighthouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Wolf%27s_Eye_Lighthouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Wolf%27s_Eye_Lighthouse.jpg) Solitude Lighthouse circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-place-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Lighthouse.jpg) Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Wolf's Eye Lighthouse also known as **Solitude Lighthouse** is a lighthouse at the northernmost coast of [Haafingar Hold](Lore_Haafingar.md). The lighthouse is very important for trade, with it being crucial for sailors navigating to [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) by preventing ships crashing into the rocky terrain. The lighthouse has been around since at least [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). The lighthouse is looked after by a keeper, with the keeper in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) being a retired [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) sailor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

In 4E 201, a bandit group known as the Blackblood Marauders wanted to put out the lighthouse in order to loot an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) trade ship known as the Icerunner.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
