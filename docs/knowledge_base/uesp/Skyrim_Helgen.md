[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-place-Helgen_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Helgen_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Helgen_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Helgen_02.jpg) Helgen before the dragon attack **Helgen** is a [town](Skyrim_Towns.md) in eastern [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-place-Helgen.jpg/200px-SR-place-Helgen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Helgen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Helgen.jpg) Helgen after the dragon attack It is the location where you begin the game, as you are transported there to be executed. Largely run by the [Imperial military](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), the town features several fortified towers and buildings. Shortly after your arrival, the settlement comes under attack by the ancient [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md), and you are forced to escape through [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md).

If you return to the town after visiting [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), the ruins will be inhabited by a group of [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md).

## Notes
- During the introductory sequence before the initial quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) remembers [Vilod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilod), who used to make [mead with juniper berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mead_with_Juniper_Berry). If you later return to Helgen, bottles of this mead may be found on the second floor of the partly destroyed inn you jumped into during Unbound.
- Two additional residents are present during the introductory sequence; these unnamed NPCs are referenced only as `MQ101Generic Civilian Nord Male` and `MQ101Generic Civilian Nord Female` in the game data. They're seen carrying firewood and a bucket inside the gate when the carriage enters town, but are never seen again.
- Helgen doesn't count as a discovered location, and therefore won't count toward the [Explorer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Explorer) achievement.
- Helgen is the default destination for bad AI targets, meaning that bugged NPCs may end up wandering nearby.
- Approximately halfway down the road to [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md), a small group of bandits has set up camp under an overhanging ledge. The bandits guard a novice-locked chest and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Refugees](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Refugees)*.
- The locked gate, bandits, barrels, chest, potions, mead bottles, Cast Iron Pot, Wooden Bowl, Kettle, Basket, Wooden Ladle and Tankard respawn; burnt corpses, the wardrobe, and the end table don't respawn.
- The double bed at the inn is considered “owned”; however, if you choose to escape Helgen with Hadvar, you will be allowed to sleep in the bed after becoming friends with his uncle Alvor.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays Helgen in its original state; the previous one displayed Helgen in its destroyed state.

## Bugs
- The mead with juniper berries isn't properly enabled during your arrival and will only appear upon your later return to Helgen. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
- Vilod and Matlara are left alive after the dragon attack and can be seen wandering Helgen after its destruction. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- When [Valdimar](Skyrim_Valdimar.md) is assigned as a Housecarl for [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md), he may be found here instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Bandits, containers, and items don't have their encounter zone set and will respawn despite being in a "no reset" zone. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.0.8, fixes this bug.
