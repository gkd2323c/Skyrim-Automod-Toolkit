A Warning to the Aldmeri Dominion **by [Erystera Ligen](Lore_Erystera_Ligen.md)** *A Daggerfall Covenant ultimatum delivered to the Aldmeri Dominion*
---
The overlords of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) are hereby put on notice: renounce your ambitions of aggression and return to your islands and jungles, or the wrath of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) shall fall upon you like a hammer of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md).

We are well aware that the Aldmeri plan nothing less than a return to the Elven domination of the other races, particularly Men and [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md). They wish to overturn the legacies of the [First](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) and [Second Empires](Lore_Second_Empire.md) and wipe them from history. This we shall not allow. Never again will free Men and Orcs submit to the tyranny of Elven oppression!

The arrogant High Elves of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) are clearly the driving force behind the Dominion. We of the Covenant have shown, through our acceptance of the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md), that we can live with the Elves in peace, but Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) insists on war. Her invasion of the continent of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) is nothing but naked aggression. The Dominion has no territorial rights in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). If the Altmer do not return to Summerset, their invading armies will be destroyed.

The [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have traded with our [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) kingdoms for ages, but they've made a mistake in allying themselves with the perfidious [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of Summerset. Though we of the Covenant wish them no ill will, if they maintain their alliance with the Dominion, they will suffer the same fate at our hands as the High Elves. They must reconsider, or they must face our wrath.
