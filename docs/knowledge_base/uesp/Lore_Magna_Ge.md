# Magna Ge

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/RG-constellation-Empty.jpg/200px-RG-constellation-Empty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-constellation-Empty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-constellation-Empty.jpg) The [stars](Lore_Stars.md) of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) Magna Ge (or **Magne-Ge**), are a family of divine beings that fled [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) soon after its creation, following [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md), the architect.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOMN-3)</sup> These [Anuic](Lore_Anu.md) spirits left tears in the veil of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) as they retreated to [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), allowing its light to reach [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). The Magna Ge are now synonymous with the [stars](Lore_Stars.md), which they created.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOMN-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Those who separated from Magnus as he withdrew from creation are sometimes called **Star-Orphans**, and were recognized by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) as the Nine Coruscations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOMN-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-VBOH-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-MUW-7)</sup> Magnus created his offspring from aether, with his daughters emerging from forges. These offspring comprised the Magna Ge, which included the Nine Coruscations.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAS-8)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-9C-9)</sup>

[Mnemoli the Blue Star](Lore_Mnemoli_the_Blue_Star.md), or Mnemo-Li, is the most well-known Magna Ge. She is associated with "un-time" events, and it is said that she was visible even in the daytime sky during the [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md). Merid-Nunda was once one of the Magna Ge before she consorted with the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and became the Daedric Prince [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md). [Xero-Lyg](Lore_Xero-Lyg.md) is a third identified Magna Ge. Mnemoli, Merid-Nunda and Xero-Lyg were referred to as sisters, and together with six other Magna Ge: [Iana-Lor](Lore_Iana-Lor.md), [Londa-Vera](Lore_Londa-Vera.md), [Sheza-Rana](Lore_Sheza-Rana.md), [Unala-Se](Lore_Unala-Se.md), [Valia-Sha](Lore_Valia-Sha.md), and the eldest of them all, the Prime Archon, they formed the group of the Nine Coruscations.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-9C-9)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOMN-3)</sup> A [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) folktale also claims the moonlet [Baar Dau](Lore_Baar_Dau.md) originated as a wayward Magna Ge named Una who came to rest above Nirn to console it after the other Magna Ge departed, but this story is uncorroborated.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBD-2)</sup>

[Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md) credits the Magna Ge with creating [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) in "the bowels of [Lyg](Lore_Lyg.md)".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MDC4-10)</sup> The Order of the Waking Flame embraced Sister [Celdina](Lore_Celdina.md)'s beliefs that Mehrunes Dagon was created by the Magna Ge to unmake the flawed creation of Mundus in accordance to Magnus's plans.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DCT-11)</sup> To them, Mundus is a place of pain and suffering, thus they looked to Dagon to move past their mortal perils, hoping that he would purge the impurities of existence so that it may emerge anew.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Eo MDV1-12)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONCeldina-13)</sup> This contrasted the ideas of Lyranth, who believes that when the Magna Ge fled, they spat on the conviction of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) that remained to finish creating Mundus, and Dagon's emergence and sphere may be reflective of the destruction caused by their abandonment.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-LAMDDSE-14)</sup>

Before it was pillaged by the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) in [2E 541](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_541),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-15)</sup> the Priory of the Golden Staff, a monastery in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) dedicated to Magnus, held forbidden texts that described the Magna Ges' search for a tool that could unmake Nirn's flaws and thus restore it to Magnus's design, and this tool was said to be Mehrunes Dagon.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

## Known Star-Orphans

### The Nine Coruscations
- **[Iana-Lor](Lore_Iana-Lor.md)**
- **[Londa-Vera](Lore_Londa-Vera.md)**
- **[Merid-Nunda](Lore_Merid-Nunda.md)** — The Daedric Prince Meridia, a fallen Magna Ge.
- **[Mnemo-Li](Lore_Mnemo-Li.md)** — The most well-known Magna Ge, associated with "un-time" events.
- **The [Prime Archon](Lore_Ithelia.md)** — The forgotten Daedric Prince Ithelia.
- **[Sheza-Rana](Lore_Sheza-Rana.md)**
- **[Unala-Se](Lore_Unala-Se.md)**
- **[Valia-Sha](Lore_Valia-Sha.md)**
- **[Xero-Lyg](Lore_Xero-Lyg.md)** — Sister of Mnemoli and Merid-Nunda.

### Other
- **[Una](Lore_Baar_Dau.md)** — A wayward Magna Ge who, according to the Dunmeri folklore, became the Baar Dau moonlet.
