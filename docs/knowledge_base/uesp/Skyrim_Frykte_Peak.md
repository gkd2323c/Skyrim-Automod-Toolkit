[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-place-Frykte_Peak.jpg/200px-SR-place-Frykte_Peak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frykte_Peak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frykte_Peak.jpg) Frykte Peak, seen from Saering's Watch **Frykte Peak** is one of the highest points on Solstheim, near [Saering's Watch](Skyrim_Saering%27s_Watch.md). The marker for this peak does not appear on the compass until you have [climbed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing) nearly to the top.

Frykte Peak is easiest to reach with a [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) —using [Summon Arvak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arvak)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> is recommended. It is reachable on foot with enough persistence, but requires some creative jumping. See [this article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing) for tips on mountain climbing.
