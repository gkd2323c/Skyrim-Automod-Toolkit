[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/ON-place-Malabal_Tor.jpg/200px-ON-place-Malabal_Tor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Malabal_Tor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Malabal_Tor.jpg) Malabal Tor circa 2E 582 Malabal Tor is a region deep in the Green of northwestern [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The area is populated by [hoarvors](Lore_Hoarvor.md) and [stranglers](Lore_Strangler.md). The western coastline is known as the [Broken Coast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Broken_Coast). East of it is the [Xylo River Basin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xylo_River_Basin), and to the northeast is [Silvenar Vale](Lore_Silvenar_Vale.md). The capital [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md), the spiritual home of all [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), is in the southeast area of the region. Malabal Tor is also dotted with numerous smaller Bosmeri settlements and ports.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> Little light reaches the forest floor; those who wish to see the moons and stars must climb the highest bluffs to get a good glimpse of the night sky.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTOTH-2)</sup> Legends speak of a pool in an ancient glade somewhere in the forest which may grant immortality.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OI-3)</sup>

Malabal Tor is bounded to the north by the mouth of the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md), which empties into the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). The shrouded islands off the coast of Malabal Tor are both beautiful and treacherous. Horseshoe Island is the largest of the region's islands, found in the mouth of the Strid.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> While plotting an invasion of the region circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) planned to use the nearby [Tempest Island](Lore_Tempest_Island.md) as a base for their assault, and counted on its history of bizarre inclement weather to provide them cover.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIB-5)</sup> [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) journeyed to Malabal Tor in the summer,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> and in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the walking city rooted itself along the northwestern shores of the region.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE3V-6)</sup>
