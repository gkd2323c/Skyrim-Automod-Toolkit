# Deep Druids

Deep Druids, or **Druids of the Deep**, are [druids](Lore_Druids.md) that are an enigma, even thought to be myth. They worshipped and communed with the unknowable entities that call the abyss beneath the seas their home. Their existence was evident by their ancient relics left behind on Sea Haven Bay on [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md). Commodore Malfiarre, who terrorized the seas for a decade, damned his pirate fleet to undeath with magic created by the Deep Druids. They were unable to be slain, leading to their enemies to resort to burying them to prevent them from rising again. Only when the relics used for the ritual were destroyed, was their curse lifted.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The Deep Druids are speculated to be the [Eldertide Circle](Lore_Eldertide_Circle.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
