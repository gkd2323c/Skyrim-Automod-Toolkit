[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-npc-Xander.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Xander.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Xander.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Xander.jpg) Xander **Xander** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade) who lives aboard the ship, the [Red Wave](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md). He used to be the captain of a ship known as the Argent Raptor, but now he is just a lowly crewman for [Safia](Skyrim_Safia.md).

Xander wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991) with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). He carries a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich) along with an additional small amount of gold. He wields a leveled one handed weapon, which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.
