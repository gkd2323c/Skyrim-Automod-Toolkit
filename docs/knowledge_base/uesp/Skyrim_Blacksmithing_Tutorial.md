## Quick Walkthrough
1. Forge an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) at the [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md).
2. Temper the iron dagger at the [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md).
3. Craft [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) at the [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md).
4. Craft a [hide helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md) at the forge.
5. Temper the hide helmet at the [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Craft a Dagger
Take the [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) from the quest giver and then use the [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) to create an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). As soon as the dagger is crafted, return it to the quest giver. Now they will ask you to sharpen the dagger with another iron ingot. Go to the [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and sharpen the iron dagger, and present it to the smith.

### Craft a Helmet
Secondly, they will ask you to craft some [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md). Go to the [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and craft leather, and again speak to the quest giver. Now you have to create a [hide helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md) at the forge. Take the supplies you are given and use the forge to create the helmet, and then return to the smith.

Lastly, you have to temper the helmet. Use the [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve the hide helmet further, and return to the quest giver to complete the quest. They will allow you to keep the helmet and dagger that you have created as a reward.

## Notes
- Because this quest has a lot of objectives that can be completed very quickly, it is very useful if you are trying to earn the [Hero of the People](Skyrim_Hero_of_the_People.md) achievement.
- You can only initiate this quest during the day, when the quest giver is at work.

## Bugs
- Do not try to sharpen the blade or temper the helmet before you are told to, as the objective can not be completed until then. You will need to get another iron dagger or hide helmet to complete it.
- [Lod](Skyrim_Lod.md) was supposed to be able to offer this quest as well, but he was left out of the quest condition formlist. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=17707))
