[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-Lodvar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lodvar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lodvar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lodvar.jpg) Lodvar **Lodvar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](Skyrim_Lumberjack.md) who lives in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) at [Horgeir's House](Skyrim_Horgeir%27s_House.md). He is the brother of [Horgeir](Skyrim_Horgeir.md), who runs the [mill](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.md). They disagree on whether they are deep in debt or if business is booming.

Lodvar begins a long day of work by waking up at 8am and walking down to the mill. He works tirelessly at the mill, loading logs to be chopped by the mill until 6pm. He then walks back up to Horgeir's house and cooks a meal at the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in the house. Tired from working hard at the mill all day, he goes to bed at 10pm.

Lodvar wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd), and [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1). He carries [Horgeir's house key](Skyrim_Horgeir%27s_House_Key.md) and some [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). In combat, he relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
When approached, Lodvar has little interest in talking, saying:

- *"Keep walking, stranger."*
- *"If you're looking for handouts, look somewhere else."*
- *"Got nothing to spare for travelers nor refugees, here."*
- *"You want anything in this life, you gotta take it. Just not from me."*

## Conversations
While he is pretty rough to people he doesn't know, he also shows a more romantic side when talking to his brother's wife, [Olda](Skyrim_Olda.md):

- **Lodvar:** *"I'm thinking of going for a walk up the mountain later. Would you like to come?"*
- **Olda:** *"Oh, that sounds nice. Yes, I think I'll join you."*
- **Lodvar:** *"I'll bring a blanket and some wine."*

His romantic interests become even more obvious when talking to his brother:

- **Horgeir:** *"One of these days, we'll have to find you a good woman like my Olda."*
- **Lodvar:** *"Yes, I think I'd enjoy a woman like your wife."*
- **Horgeir:** *"Well then, it's too bad my wife is already taken."*
- **Lodvar:** *"Yes, it's a great shame. Gods willing, I'll find someone just like her one day."*
