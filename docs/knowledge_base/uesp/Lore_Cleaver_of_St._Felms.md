# Cleaver of St. Felms

The Cleaver of St. Felms the Bold (or **St. Felms' Cleaver**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>) is a one-handed axe which belonged to the [eponymous saint](Lore_Felms.md) of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md).

In or before [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), a [Buoyant Armiger](Lore_Buoyant_Armiger.md) named [Mendel Eves](Lore_Mendel_Eves.md) carried it into battle against [ash vampires](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md) on the northeastern slopes of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) and died.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-2)</sup> The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) recovered the holy relic and then received it as a reward for various services to the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Morrowind-3)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Cleaver of St Felms was in the possession of Vesparth the Toe, a descendant of the Armigers who took refuge within Ashfall's Tear in [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). She along with the refugee's inhabitants were seen as heretical under the New Temple's belief. Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) was alerted to the cult's existence when they attempted to recover several additional artifacts. It is unknown if the Dragonborn agreed to help the cult and recruited Vesparth into their service, or if it was wiped out.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Got T-4)</sup> Regardless, the cleaver and other relics were retrieved from the hideout, and this arsenal was used to defeat remnants of the [Sixth House](Lore_Sixth_House.md)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive-5)</sup> led by the Dunmer Erden Relvel, who became a priest of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) after becoming corrupted by his mask.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
