Gorieus was an emperor of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) during the fifth century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Like most Alessian Emperors, he was not a man of great humor. His coronation in [1E 461](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_461) was a somber ceremony, in accordance with the rules of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md), but was still attended by many of the famous figures of the day including [Darloc Brae](Lore_Darloc_Brae.md) the Golden Beast of Anequina, the High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), [Kjoric the White](Lore_Kjoric_the_White.md), his son [Hoag](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md), representing their kingdoms and giving honor to the Empire. Despite the Empire's intolerance of all [elves](Lore_Mer.md), [Ryain Direnni](Lore_Ryain_Direnni.md) (in service to the Imperial court of High Rock), [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) Hortator [Indoril Nerevar](Lore_Indoril_Nerevar.md), and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [Dumac Dwarfking](Lore_Dumac_Dwarfking.md) attended, diplomatically representing [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md), all in relative peace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTR-1)</sup>

One of the guests, Kjoric the White, later rebelled against him, and Gorieus' forces killed him in the [Battle of Sungard](Lore_Battle_of_Sungard.md). His military defeat at the hands of King [Rislav Larich](Lore_Rislav_Larich.md) of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) in [1E 478](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_478) began the downfall of the Alessian Empire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTR-1)</sup> Rislav's unlikely victory over Gorieus' legions was seen by some at the time as divine favor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anti-2)</sup>
