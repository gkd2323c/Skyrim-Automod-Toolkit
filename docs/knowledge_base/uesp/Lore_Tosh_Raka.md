# Tosh Raka

Tosh Raka is the emperor of [Ka Po' Tun](Lore_Ka_Po%27_Tun_(place).md) (or the "Tiger-Dragon Empire"). Through unknown methods, he purportedly transformed himself into a [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup> This transformative process is also said to have turned him into a god,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGFP-2)</sup> with one source going as far as to state he could be an Akaviri avatar of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) and possibly holds a grudge against his "mirror brother".<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MK-3)</sup> He is allegedly the largest Dragon in the world,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup> and may resemble a hundred foot tall [tiger](Lore_Tiger.md) with [bat](Lore_Bat.md) -like wings.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-TPWormgod-4)</sup>

Since at least the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), it has been known Tosh Raka plans to conquer [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). If he is successful, he plans to set his sights on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup> Much of what is recorded in the book [*Mysterious Akavir*](Lore_Mysterious_Akavir.md) supposedly came from the scrolls and works of Tosh Raka.<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-AN-5)</sup>

## Notes
- His name is similar to [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), the Dragon God of Time. It is suggested to be a wordplay on the terms "Aka" and "Tosh", both meaning Dragon,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MK-3)</sup> but depending on usage or placement, "Tosh" can alternatively mean "Tiger" or "Time",<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-APAYQ-6)</sup> a link to how dragons are synonymous with time.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MK-3)</sup>
- [Toshrakhat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Toshrakhat) was the dragon tongue name given to [Ja'darri](Lore_Ja%27darri.md) by [Nahfahlaar](Lore_Nahfahlaar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESONahfahlaar-7)</sup> It is unknown if the similarity to Tosh Raka is intended to be related.
