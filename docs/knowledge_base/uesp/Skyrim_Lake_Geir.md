[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-place-Lake_Geir.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Geir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Geir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Geir.jpg) Lake Geir **Lake Geir** is a lake in the western [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

[Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) is located on the shores of Lake Geir. In the center of the lake there is an island which is home to [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md). [Catfish](Skyrim_Catfish.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> can be found swimming in the waters here.

Geographically, the lake is fed by the [Treva River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River) and is drained by [Darkwater River](Skyrim_Darkwater_River.md) (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes
- On the eastern shore is a row boat containing two sacks beached opposite a small island with a tree stump. On the island there are two [treasure hunters](Skyrim_Treasure_Hunter_(bandit).md) who will attack upon seeing you. They have dug up an unlocked chest containing minor loot. You will find two [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) and a [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) that can be harvested on the island. Further inland is their camp including a small tent, two bedrolls, an open, empty chest, four sacks and three barrels, one of which has on it a bottle of [Honningbrew mead](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Mead.md), a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), and a [note](Skyrim_Note_(treasure).md) directing you to the treasure on the island. There are also three rabbits hanging beside the tent that can be harvested. There is also a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) next to where the rabbits are hanging.
- The name "Lake Geir" is sourced from the [Skyrim Prima Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide).
