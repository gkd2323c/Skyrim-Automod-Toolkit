# Statues

Overview | [Notable Figures](Lore_Statues_Notable_Figures.md) — [Aedra](Lore_Statues_Aedra.md) — [Daedric Princes](Lore_Statues_Daedric_Princes.md) — [Tribunal & Saints](Lore_Statues_Tribunal_and_Saints.md) — [Other Deities](Lore_Statues_Other_Deities.md) — [Other Statues](Lore_Statues_Other_Statues.md) — [Generic Statues](Lore_Statues_Generic_Statues.md) [![SR-misc-Ysgramor Statue.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-misc-Ysgramor_Statue.png/200px-SR-misc-Ysgramor_Statue.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Ysgramor_Statue.png) **Statues** and sculptures play an important role for many races and cultures across [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), and have been used for various purposes throughout history. They serve as a testament to the artistic and creative abilities of various peoples and have been created to express a wide range of ideas, beliefs, and emotions. One of the most significant aspects of statues is their ability to preserve history. They offer a visual representation of past events, people, and cultures that have shaped Nirn—especially for extinct races (such as the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) and [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md)) where first-hand sources are nonexistent. Sometimes, ancient or lost knowledge is recorded in statuary and bas-reliefs and has been re-discovered in later eras.

Statuary and sculptures allow some to gain a deeper understanding of their history and heritage and connect with their ancestors in a tangible way. This is especially true for [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), who maintain a connection with their original homeland of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) through the numerous forms of art. Statues and sculptures can also serve as a symbol of hope, strength, and resilience. For the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) they can inspire individuals to strive towards greatness and can act as a reminder of the accomplishments and contributions of the people who came before. Statues of great leaders and heroes, for instance, encourage the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) to emulate their qualities and work towards achieving their greatness. Moreover, statues and sculptures can also serve an important role in the various religions on Nirn, from the worship of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md), the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md), the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) and many others. These can be a source of pride for communities and help to create a sense of identity and belonging, and act as a reminder of the unique qualities and characteristics of a particular region or culture. Many statues can be animated to serve as [golems](Lore_Golem.md).
