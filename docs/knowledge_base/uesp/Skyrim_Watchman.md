[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-npc-Watchman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Watchman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Watchman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Watchman.jpg) Watchman The **Watchman** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) doorman found at [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md), guarding the entrance to [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). He wears [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md), and carries an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). Unlike the rest of Castle Volkihar's vampire inhabitants, the Watchman is a thrall.

## Related Quests
- **[Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md)**: Choose your side!

## Quest-Related Events
When you first visit the castle, he'll stop you, saying:

- *"Begone! You're not welcome here!"*

Then, upon seeing [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) with you, he'll be shocked and say:

- *"Lady Serana's back! Open the gate!"*

He will then lead you both into the castle.

He has several other lines of dialogue when idling:

- *"Lord Harkon's a great man. Best show him the proper respect."*
- *"I watch the gate. Ain't much else to tell."*
- *"After all these years, Lady Serana's back. Now that's something."*
- *"Way I hear it, Lady Serana's been gone a real long time."*

## Dialogue
If interacted with before Bloodline:

- *"Go on, get out of here!"*
- *"You're not welcome here."*
- *"Go away! We don't suffer trespassers!"*

## Notes
- The watchman will disappear from the game once you complete Bloodline, regardless of your decision.

## Bugs
- The Watchman may not recognize Serana when you finish escorting her to the castle, and won't open the portcullis. - Put a plate or platter directly on the portcullis, then use [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md).
- Put a plate or platter on one of the diagonal walls on either side of the portcullis and clip through.
- Wait until Bloodline is active before discovering Castle Volkihar. He should now recognize Serana properly and open the portcullis.
- The Watchman may simply open the portcullis without saying anything. <sup>**?**</sup>
