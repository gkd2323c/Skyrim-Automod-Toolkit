## Ingredients
2 guaranteed samples can be found in 2 different locations:

- 1 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))
- 1 carried by [Arngeir](Skyrim_Arngeir.md) ([High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md))

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is a "common" ingredient.

Remaining ways to obtain already-harvested honeycombs all have relatively low probabilities. The best bets are:

- [Peddlers](Skyrim_Peddler.md) may be randomly encountered in the wilderness (typically being attacked). These peddlers have a 25% chance of carrying 1-5 samples.
- Various ingredient-specific barrels have a 17% chance of containing 1-5 samples.
- [Vaermina devotees](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vaermina_Devotee) have a 3% chance of carrying one.
- All types of [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) as well as pots hanging from cooking spits have a 3% chance of containing one.
- [Warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md) have a 0.9% chance of carrying one.
- If you adopt a child, you may find some in their chest.
- With the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion, [werebears](Skyrim_Werebear.md) may carry one sample of honeycomb.

## Plants
Honeycomb is only harvested from beehives, not vacant beehives. There are 22 hives in 18 different locations. Locations with multiple hives are:

- 4 around [Honeystrand Cave](Skyrim_Honeystrand_Cave.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 centered 300 ft NW of [Halldir's Cairn](Skyrim_Halldir%27s_Cairn.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-47542&y=-92044)
- 2 around [Redoran's Retreat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran%27s_Retreat) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Fortify Block](Skyrim_Fortify_Block.md) effect for this ingredient is 2 instead of 4. This is only relevant if honeycomb and [Briar Heart](Skyrim_Briar_Heart.md) are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 50% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Fortify Block potion.
- Honeycomb is luminescent.

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot normally be bought from apothecary merchants, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
- Honeycomb is missing the Vendor Item Food Raw keyword. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
