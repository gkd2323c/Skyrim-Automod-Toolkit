[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Valindor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valindor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valindor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valindor.jpg) Valindor on the back porch of Honeyside **Valindor** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who was born in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). He spent most of his childhood playing in the towering trees that grow there with his brethren. He became the apprentice of a master armorer in [Silvenar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silvenar) and was later urged by his master to take over his business; however, he turned it down and decided to wander [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) instead. Now living in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and working at [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md), he almost regrets his decision to leave home.

Valindor starts off his day by waking up at 4am and having a long breakfast for two hours in [his house](Skyrim_Valindor%27s_House.md). At 6am, he heads off to Riften Fishery and starts working for three hours. At 9am, he goes outside to the docks and works on the fishing boat for three hours until noon. Then, he heads back inside the fishery and works for three hours until 3pm. After that, he goes outside to the boat again and works for another three hours. At 6pm, he finally stops work and proceeds to [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) to consume his dinner and rest for four hours. At 10pm, he returns to his house to sleep for six hours until 4am, and repeats his schedule the following day.

Valindor wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). He carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valindor%27s_House_Key) to his house, a leveled potion, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). He wields a leveled dagger in combat, which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.

## Conversations
Valindor can often be seen engaging in conversations with other Riften locals while he is at the bunkhouse.

**[Svana](Skyrim_Svana_Far-Shield.md)**: *"How do you do it, Valindor? How do you work at Black-Briar Meadery and the [Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) without getting exhausted?"*
**Valindor**: *"Oh, it's not so terrible. I actually find the work quite relaxing."*
**Svana**: *"Relaxing? They must be working you to the bone! You should rest before you do yourself in."*
**Valindor**: *"Not to worry, dear Svana. I promise you I can handle it, but your concern is quite flattering."* **Valindor**: *"[Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md), you really need to stop using that skooma. It's very bad for your health and I wouldn't want to see anything happen to you."*
**Wujeeta**: *"I can't, Valindor. It won't let me go. It's taking over my life."*
**Valindor**: *"It's an evil substance. Whoever's selling it to you should be brought to justice."*
**Wujeeta**: *"If there was any justice in the world, I'd just get this over with and die peacefully."* He's also shown to be friendly when giving to the local beggars. When [Snilf](Skyrim_Snilf.md) asks him for a coin, he'll reply:

*"Certainly, my good man. Here."* Similarly, [Edda](Skyrim_Edda.md) will get a:

*"Certainly, good lady. Here."*
## Unused Conversations
Valindor was also meant to have a conversation with fellow Bosmer [Ungrien](Skyrim_Ungrien.md), but as it's set to take place inside of [the local meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md), which he never visits, the scene can never play.

**Ungrien**: *"I don't know if I can stand the abuse here much longer. They treat us like outcasts."*
**Valindor**: *"The Dark Elves and the Wood Elves have never been the best of friends. Be patient, they'll come around soon enough!"*
**Ungrien**: *"Wretched Dark Elves. It's just my luck I ended up working under one of them."*
**Valindor**: *"Chin up, Ungrien! Remember, you have a roof over your head and food in your belly thanks to the very same Dark Elves. Be thankful."*
## Notes
- The way Valindor's last three greetings are set up imply that they might have once belonged to a topic you could ask him about, as they all follow each other in corresponding fashion.
- Valindor was originally intended to work at both Riften Fishery and Black-Briar Meadery, an unusual feat, as noted by Svana in a scene that still takes place in the final game. There's also the scene he was meant to have with Ungrien, but there are even more leftovers pointing to this: Valindor is part of the factions for both locations, as well as the lock lists for both locations, and [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md) is marked as his boss, internally.
