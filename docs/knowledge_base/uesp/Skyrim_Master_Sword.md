[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Master_Sword.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Master_Sword.png) Master Sword The Master Sword is a legendary sword obtained using [amiibo](Skyrim_Amiibo.md) or in a chest upon the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md). It is only available in the [Nintendo Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) version of the game.

## Notes
- The [Master Sword](https://zeldawiki.wiki/wiki/Master_Sword) is a recurring item in the *[The Legend of Zelda](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda)* series. For more information, see the [Wikipedia article](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Master_Sword).
