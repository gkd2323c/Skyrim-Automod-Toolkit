[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Smuggler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Smuggler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Smuggler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Smuggler.jpg) Smuggler The **Smuggler** is a dead [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found near a fireplace in [Mara's Eye Den](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Eye_Den.md).

According to his [journal](Skyrim_Smuggler%27s_Journal.md), which can be found on a nearby table, he was hired to move some crates, a job that seemed perfectly innocent. He then stole the crates, placed them in the den and opened them up, only to find them filled with dead bodies. He stripped the bodies of valuables, not knowing that they were actually sleeping [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md), and paid the price for his greed.

His weapon of choice is random and leveled (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), or a [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md). He also carries a bit of random loot, food, and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or some gold. He is clad in randomly determined [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md); at all levels, there is a 50% chance it will be [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5 there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md), and at level 19 it can be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.
