Molag Grunda is a powerful [Winged Twilight](Lore_Winged_Twilight.md). She is the daughter of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), although it is unclear what this relationship means to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOGGLS-1)</sup> She has been described as being sarcastic and rude.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCadwell-2)</sup>

Molag Grunda was once charged with guarding the path across the Chasm of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), which divides the Shrouded Plain and the Black Garrison from the Fist of Stone.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOGeo-3)</sup> She was known to be a cruel mistress, and the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) sent to serve at her court were never happy with their assignment.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOGGLS-1)</sup> She kept a pet [flesh atronach](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md) named Tiny for protection.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOChasm-4)</sup>

The need to defend the Chasm's crossing grew when the Endless Stair was constructed by the [Mad Architect](Lore_Mad_Architect.md) in the Fist of Stone circa [2E 579](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_579), making the region's protection essential to proceed with Molag Bal's attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) with [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOChasm-4)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) an invasion force from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) came to Coldharbour to end the Planemeld, assembling in the [Hollow City](Lore_Hollow_City.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOChasm-4)</sup> The invaders attacked the Chasm to secure passage to the Endless Stair, and Molag Grunda was slain in her gatehouse along with Tiny.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOChasm-4)</sup>

Some time after this, Molag Grunda came to Tamriel, where she consorted with a lowly [frost atronach](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md) named Nomeg Gwai.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWVamp-5)</sup> They made their lair in the uncharted cave of Dubdilla, in the [Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Molag Bal was not pleased with this match, and in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) he sent the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) to kill them in return for a [vampirism](Lore_Vampirism.md) cure.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWVamp-5)</sup> Molag Grunda's soul then returned to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), where she and her lover were punished by Molag Bal.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWVamp-5)</sup>
