[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-npc-Barkeep.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Barkeep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Barkeep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Barkeep.jpg) The barkeep tending his bar at Cragslane Cavern The **Barkeep** is a hostile [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found in [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md) serving drinks to the [gamblers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gambler). He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0) and is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality). He carries a considerable amount of leveled gold, ranging from 25 to 125 gold, and the [Craglane chest key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Craglane_Chest_Key) which opens the chest behind his bar.
