[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-place-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg) Brunwulf Free-Winter's House **Brunwulf Free-Winter's House** is the home of [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

It is a one story house, with a partial loft area located in Windhelm near the main entrance. Next door to [Aretino Residence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aretino_Residence), almost directly opposite [Calixto's House of Curiosities](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities). It consists of only one zone, *[Brunwulf Free-Winter's House](#Brunwulf_Free-Winter.27s_House)*.

## Brunwulf Free-Winter's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brunwulf_Free-Winter%27s_House.jpg) The interior of the Free-Winter household The entrance has a novice level lock. The entry leads straight into the main living area, with a practice dummy and a pile of firewood against the south wall. There are quite a few sacks of [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) around the whole house. To the west of the entrance is a flight of wooden stairs up to a small loft area and behind the stairs is a small square wooden table with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and a couple of wedges of cheese on top. Against the west wall is a small set of shelves holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bottle of Nord mead. Against the north wall is a dresser containing [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes) with two [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot) and a [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka) on top. Further to the right still against the north wall is a long wooden table holding a [mammoth snout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Snout), three [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), three bulbs of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a bunch of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a salt pile, a loaf of bread, a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) and three [slaughterfish eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg). Right of this is a fire pit with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) over it and a chair in front. By the chair is a copy of *[Immortal Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Immortal_Blood)*. Beside the fire pit is a narrow wooden table holding a haunch of raw [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and a loaf of bread, with three wall shelves holding various skulls beside the table and hanging from the wall beside the shelves is a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow). There is a doorway through into a bedroom to the east, with a bookcase to the left holding four common books. To the right of the doorway is a wall shelf holding a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor), [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers), [shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield) and [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet), a [potion of the berserker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_the_Berserker) and an [iron greatsword](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md) leaning against the wall beside it. Under the shelf is a long unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing gold. To the right, in an alcove on the south wall is a large round wooden table holding an [iron battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe) and [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull).

The bedroom is very small consisting of a single owned bed opposite the doorway in the northeastern corner, with a respawning [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack) on the floor by the head of the bed, which contains leveled items. Against the east wall is a small unit holding a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips). In the southeast corner is a corner shelf holding a [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health), with a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum) on the floor beside it. To the right, against the south wall is a small cupboard with an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace) and eight loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on top. The loft area is very small and only contains a couple of barrels, one of which contains food.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom right of the entrance door.
- All containers; with the exception of most barrels, sacks, and the knapsack; are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
