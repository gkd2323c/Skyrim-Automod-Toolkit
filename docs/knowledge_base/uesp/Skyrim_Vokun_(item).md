[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-item-Vokun.jpg/200px-SR-item-Vokun.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Vokun.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Vokun.jpg) Vokun Vokun is a steel [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) obtained by killing the eponymous [Vokun](Skyrim_Vokun_(creature).md) within [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md). The artifact's enchantments increase your [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md), [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md), and [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skills by twenty points when worn. It can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [steel ingots](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A Scroll For Anska](Skyrim_A_Scroll_For_Anska.md)**: Help [Anska](Skyrim_Anska.md) trace her roots in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md).

## Notes
- Placing Vokun on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses it gained from tempering.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- *Vokun* means "Shadow" in the [dragon tongue](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a [special holder](Skyrim_Special_Holder.md) for this item.
