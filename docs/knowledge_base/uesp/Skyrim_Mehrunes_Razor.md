[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor.jpg) Mehrunes' Razor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor_02.jpg/200px-SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mehrunes%27_Razor_02.jpg) The Razor in its scabbard Mehrunes' Razor is a dagger and a [Daedric artifact](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md) crafted by [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) that has the chance to instantly kill any opponent.

Even without the disintegrate armor effect from its [Oblivion counterpart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Mehrunes%27_Razor_Items#Mehrunes_Razor), it is a very powerful dagger. The damage dealt is equal to an unenchanted [Daedric dagger](Skyrim_Daedric_Dagger.md) while weighing half as much.

It is received after the quest [Pieces of the Past](Skyrim_Pieces_of_the_Past.md) if the demands of Mehrunes Dagon are met and [Silus Vesuius](Skyrim_Silus_Vesuius.md) is killed. The dagger can be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) using an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md); however, the dagger does not benefit from any Smithing perks (See [Bugs](#Bugs) section).

## Related Quests
- **[Pieces of the Past](Skyrim_Pieces_of_the_Past.md)**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of Mehrunes' Razor and reforge this deadly blade.

## Notes
- If equipped, guards may comment on the dagger, asking *"Who did you have to kill to get that blade?"*
- [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> and [Karstaag](Skyrim_Karstaag.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> are the only NPCs immune to the razor's instant kill effect.
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- The chance that Mehrunes' Razor will instantly kill an enemy should be 1% according to a comment in its script, but due to an oversight it is actually 2/101 which means that the real chance for an instant kill is just under 2%. **Mod Notes**: Confirmed through Creation Kit.
- This weapon is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk.
- This dagger sounds like a one-handed sword when drawn and sheathed. <sup>**?**</sup>
