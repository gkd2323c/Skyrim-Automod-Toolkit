## Quick Walkthrough
1. *(Optional)* Enter [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md) Forge.
2. Locate all four [Fahlbtharz Forge Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fahlbtharz_Forge_Gem).
3. Take the Unenchanted Weapon from [Hand Kenro Hlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Kenro_Hlan) at Fahlbtharz Forge.
4. Place the weapon and the four gems into the forge to obtain [Trueflame](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trueflame).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### An Unenchanted Weapon
Finding any of the four Dwarven Forge Gems hidden throughout Solstheim or entering the Temple in Raven Rock and stumbling upon the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest).md) quest will begin this quest.

Trueflame is located in the Dwarven Forge in Fahlbtharz where [Hand Kenro Hlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Kenro_Hlan) will attack you. He is in possession of Trueflame, although it appears as an Unenchanted Weapon without the enchanted flames it traditionally bears. He also has a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Kenro%27s_Note) giving direction, and a sample of [Pyroil Tar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pyroil_Tar) which will be needed once you have all four Gems.

### Fahlbtharz Forge Gem Locations
The gems are found:

1. On the body of [Hand Kenro Hlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hand_Kenro_Hlan).
2. On the body of the [buyer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer) after following the [Redoran guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) during [Buyer Beware](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer_Beware).
3. In the armory in [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md).
4. On the body of [Erden Relvel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel) at the end of the quest [Ashen Heart](Skyrim_Ashen_Heart.md).

### Reforging the Blade of Nerevar
With the Unenchanted Blade and all four gems in hand, you must return to Fahlbtharz Forge and place the four gems within the forge's receptacles. You will then need to apply the pyroil tar to the blade and place it in the sword slot; doing so will complete this quest and reignite the weapon with a visually impressive [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) enchantment. Congratulations, you now possess [Trueflame](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trueflame), the legendary Blade of [Nerevar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nerevar).

## Notes
You must also complete all the other related quests in order to obtain the 4 [Fahlbtharz Forge Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fahlbtharz_Forge_Gem%7C).

These quests are : [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest)%7C.md) [Her Word Against Theirs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Her_Word_Against_Theirs%7C) [Buyer Beware](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer_Beware%7C) [Careless Curation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Careless_Curation%7C) [Ashen Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Heart%7C) (All previous quests must be completed to unlock the armory and the Ashen Heart quest)

## Bugs
- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](https://images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](Skyrim_Switch.md) The animations for creating Trueflame will cause the screen to freeze on the [Nintendo Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md). You need to close and restart the game to continue. - The easiest way to bypass this bug is by pressing A to insert the blade, followed immediately by waiting for an hour, skipping the animation that freezes the game.
- This bug can be solved by going into sneak mode, then placing the Unenchanted Weapon and opening the character menu simultaneously. Without exiting the menu, press start and manually save the game. After that, exit both menus. If the screen is still frozen, close and restart the game, loading up the mid-animation save. After that, you should be able to take the Trueflame without issue.
- Sometimes the pyroil tar does not apply to the unenchanted weapon. - To fix this bug, try unequipping all apparel items then continue with the quest normally.
- If you add the gems to the forge and ignite the forge before inserting the unenchanted weapon, you may not be able to finish the quest normally. You can use the console to advance the quest, as discussed below, but you will still have the unenchanted weapon in your inventory, unless you also remove it with console commands. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The game may freeze if you perform the Trueflame ritual too quickly. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
