[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-place-Haafingar.jpg/200px-SR-place-Haafingar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haafingar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haafingar.jpg) Haafingar, a hold in northwest Skyrim **Haafingar** is a relatively small [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in northwest [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), containing the provincial capital [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). As the home of the provincial capital, Haafingar is the center of [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) rule in Skyrim. It only becomes Stormcloak-run if you join the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) and complete the entire questline, including both [The Battle for Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Hraggstad.md) and the [Battle for Solitude](Skyrim_Battle_for_Solitude.md).

Haafingar is dominated by a large ridge that runs the length of the hold. Pine forests and meadows cover the southern slopes down to the [Karth River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River), while the north side is colder, and dominated by mountains and frozen coastline. Solitude itself sits on a massive natural archway that extends over the mouth of the Karth from the eastern spur of the ridge.

## Flora and Fauna
Haafingar's coastline is ideal for the growth of [nordic barnacles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle) and [clams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam) in the water and [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root) and [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass) on land. Near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), [fishing](Skyrim_Fish.md) can be enjoyed around [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md) where [dragonflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly) play over the water. A fair amount of [nightshade plants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) grow inside the capital. [Mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella), [mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower), and [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle) can be found in the lower parts of the hold while only [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) and sporadic mountain flowers can be found in the snow-covered tail of the [Druadach Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Mountains).
