[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-creature-Dwarven_Spider_Renovator.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Dwarven_Spider_Renovator.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Spider_Renovator.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Spider_Renovator.jpg) Dwarven Spider Renovator The **Dwarven Spider Renovator** is a friendly [Dwarven spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) added by [Nchuanthumz: Dwarven Home](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz__Dwarven_Home.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> that can be constructed in [Nchuanthumz](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md).

After it is activated in the Manufactory, it will scuttle into the house's pipes. It can be summoned three times via the three levers upstairs, to clean three sections of the house over a period of 24 hours each. Each lever can only be activated once. After the third and final renovation, the Renovator disappears for good.
