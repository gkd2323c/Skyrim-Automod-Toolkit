[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-npc-Bjormund_Wind-Strider.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bjormund_Wind-Strider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjormund_Wind-Strider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjormund_Wind-Strider.jpg) Bjormund Wind-Strider's corpse Bjormund Wind-Strider is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) and former [Crimson Dirk](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) who is encountered during the quest [Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md). His body is found at the very top of the tower at [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md), at the bottom of the stairs which lead to the book [Withershins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Withershins), after the player has battled past the enemies that occupy the tower. When he chased the same bounty as you, [Crowstooth](Skyrim_Crowstooth.md) killed him. As part of the reward for the quest (any spoils of Crowstooth's), you may take his unique armor.

Bjormund wears the [Dragonbone Mail](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Mail.md), [Dragonplate Insulated Boots](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Insulated_Boots.md), [Dragonplate Insulated Gauntlets](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Insulated_Gauntlets.md) and a [Dragonplate Insulated Helmet](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Insulated_Helmet.md), and carries [Nord Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), an [Amulet of Akatosh](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Akatosh.md), and a random selection of loot.
