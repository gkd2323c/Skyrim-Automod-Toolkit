# Skooma

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/MW-item-Moon_Sugar_and_Skooma.jpg/200px-MW-item-Moon_Sugar_and_Skooma.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Moon_Sugar_and_Skooma.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Moon_Sugar_and_Skooma.jpg) Moon sugar, a skooma pipe and skooma (Morrowind) Skooma is a hallucinatory narcotic refined from [moon sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTE-1)</sup> Extremely addictive, its victims are left in constant, alternating states of delusional euphoria and lethargy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1Elsweyr-2)</sup> Sustained use of the drug seemingly results in permanent confusion and mental deterioration,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBSkooma Den-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> as well as loss of teeth.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The drug can be smoked through a water pipe or consumed in liquid form; some of the apparatus used can be quite elaborate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1Elsweyr-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBSkooma Den-4)</sup> When poor quality ingredients—like moldy moon sugar—are used in its production, the resulting "tainted" skooma has more extreme side effects including death. As spoiled moon sugar is cheaper to obtain, this practice is regrettably common.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONKahiri-7)</sup> Regardless of the quality of the skooma consumed, withdrawal from the drug can have mental effects with violent consequences.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SFK-8)</sup>

## History, Culture, and Mythology
[Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Madness, is thus known as the [Skooma Cat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skooma_Cat) among the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), "for what is crazier than a cat on skooma?"<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-WCMA-9)</sup> Some stories about the Mad God claim that skooma is the medium which the Prince speaks through the protection of the [Lunar Lattice](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONHaduras-10)</sup> Because of the prevalence of moon sugar in Khajiiti culture, skooma addiction has proven to be a particular menace for Khajiit with a "sweet tooth".<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup><sup>[[*citation needed*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Template:Fact)]</sup> However, the "sacred sugar" as it is also referred to, does have use in certain Khajiit ceremonies, where it is strictly controlled by the Moon Bishops.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTE-1)</sup> The visions seen during these rituals are seen as messages from the [Twin](Lore_Jode.md) [Moons](Lore_Jone.md), but the user runs the risk of attracting Sheogorath's attention.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONSizenza-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

In the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), it is said that the drug was originally developed by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SKWujeeta-14)</sup> The drug has been banned throughout history by both the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) and the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).

Skooma is typically smuggled along with moon sugar.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-WSSH-15)</sup> However, a seemingly skooma-specific smuggling route existed between [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) and [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md) in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-CDJ1-16)</sup> The traffic of skooma has led to violent criminal conflicts both individual and organized.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-SKJiub-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-OBCJ-18)</sup>

## Refinement and Effects
The liquids used to refine skooma from moon sugar are highly flammable.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-LW-19)</sup> Skooma is used to hide the taste of [Redmaiden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Redmaiden), a drug for [Nirnborn](Lore_Nirn.md) mortals to keep themselves sane for a short time in the daedric realm of [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-IOTS-20)</sup>

Although addiction to skooma is commonly thought to be incurable,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1Elsweyr-2)</sup> cures have been achieved through both alchemy and the elimination of the addict's own self-hatred.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-SKHH-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-CSE-22)</sup> Some medical practices, such as the application of numbing agents, are said to relieve the symptoms of withdrawal.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-Numbing Needles-23)</sup>

### Variants
Balmora Blue is a highly illegal variety made primarily from Moon Sugar, with many other ingredients added later in the process to increase its potency. When [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) was in its prime before the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), Balmora Blue was a lucrative underworld commodity, and its value became exponentially higher when the city was destroyed.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-My Buddy Erikur-24)</sup>

In the [third century](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#Third_Century) of the Fourth Era, Redwater Skooma, a far more potent form of skooma, was refined using water from the [Bloodspring of Lengeir's Feast](Lore_Redwater_Spring.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It was used by [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) to exploit skooma addicts for blood.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-SKVVR-25)</sup>
