# Nightmare Host

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/DF-map-Dwynnen.png/200px-DF-map-Dwynnen.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Dwynnen.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Dwynnen.png) Dwynnen The Nightmare Host was an undead horde led by a [lich](Lore_Lich.md) consisting of [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md), [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md), [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), and [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md). They served the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) and fought in the [Battle of Wightmoor](Lore_Battle_of_Wightmoor.md), which was one of many conflicts during the [greater conflict](Lore_Camoran_Usurper%27s_Invasion.md) in which the Camoran Usurper invaded western [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTET-2)</sup>

## History
Prior to his conquest over the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTET-2)</sup> concurrent to the [Battle of Dragontooth](Lore_Battle_of_Dragontooth.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDC-3)</sup><sup>:8</sup> the Camoran Usurper sent an undead horde called the Nightmare Host to control the Barony of [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) for currently unknown reasons.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTET-2)</sup> According to [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md) legends, the horde was led by a lich controlling an army of ghosts, skeletons, vampires, and zombies, all the while held up in Castle Wightmoor. They were destroyed by [Othrok](Lore_Othrok.md), blessed by the [gods](Lore_Divines.md), who emerged from the wilderness with an army of men and animals in order to secure Dwynnen.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup> They also fought the [Order of the Raven](Lore_Order_of_the_Raven.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDesc-5)</sup> This event was known as the Battle of Wightmoor and took place in the capital city of Dwynnen on the 5th of [Sun's Dawn](Lore_Sun%27s_Dawn.md) in [3E 253](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_253).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup>

The people of the Dwynnen Barony celebrate a [holiday](Lore_Holidays.md) called Othroktide on the 5th of Sun's Dawn each year not just as a memorial to their liberation from the Nightmare Host,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFHolidays-4)</sup> but also to honor Baron Othrok himself,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HOTIB-6)</sup> who has since been remembered as a hero among the Bretons. Othrok's divine purpose is a central theme in Othroktide.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Camoran Usurper, according to legends also utilized an army that by legend consisted entirely of the undead and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) to conquer [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). In fact, it was mostly composed of [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), however.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BHOTE-7)</sup> Whether it was connected to the Nightmare Host is unknown, however.
- In the book *[The Third Era Timeline](Lore_The_Third_Era_Timeline.md)*, Haymon Camoran is described as a Lich. The book also hints at a possible connection to the Nightmare Host, though it is uncertain whether Camoran led the Host personally or some other Lich under his command led them in his stead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTET-2)</sup>
