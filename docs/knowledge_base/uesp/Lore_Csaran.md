Csaran was the second Count of [Vitharn](Lore_Vitharn.md) in the [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). He was the son of [Vitraen](Lore_Vitraen.md), the first Count of Vitharn, and his wife, Countess Mawean. He had a sister named Nweala.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup>

Csaran's mother and father believed that with the proper political influence, Csaran could usurp [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) and lead the Shivering Isles into a prosperous age. Vitraen refused even to acknowledge Sheogorath, thinking himself and his heirs to be the irrefutable rulers of the Isles. This amused the Madgod to no end, so he allowed Csaran to marry Sheen-in-Glade, the daughter of an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) midwife.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup>

Csaran eventually succeeded his father as count. He fathered a son with Sheen-in-Glade, who disappeared from the Shivering Isles at the age of twenty. Although Sheen-in-Glade was considered an excellent wife to Csaran, he frequently engaged in adultery. His adultery eventually drove Sheen-in-Glade insane. Csaran was obsessively nepotistic and distrustful of anyone with whom he shared no blood relation, including his wife. As Csaran's paranoia grew, he and Sheen-in-Glade shared their bed with decreasing frequency, and he began an incestuous relationship with his sister Nweala. His incestuous relationship with Nweala produced a son—the malformed and sadistic heir apparent, [Cesrien](Lore_Cesrien.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup>

Csaran later died, and Cesrien succeeded him as Count of Vitharn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FOV-1)</sup> Csaran was entombed beside Sheen-in-Glade in Vitharn's Mausoleum.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VM-2)</sup>
