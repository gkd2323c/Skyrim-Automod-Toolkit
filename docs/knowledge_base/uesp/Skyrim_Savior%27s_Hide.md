[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-item-Savior%27s_Hide.jpg/200px-SR-item-Savior%27s_Hide.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Savior%27s_Hide.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Savior%27s_Hide.jpg) Savior's Hide The Savior's Hide is an artifact given to you by [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) as one of the rewards for the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md). It is classified as [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md). Its enchantment grants a fifteen percent [resist magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) effect, as well as a [resist poison](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md) effect that halves poison damage. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md), and having the [Ebony Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Smithing) perk doubles the improvement.

## Related Quests
