*A gout of fire that does 8 points per second. Targets on fire take extra damage.* **Flames** is a novice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) and sets targets on fire, dealing 30% of the immediate spell damage and scaling down quadratically over 0.1 seconds, commencing 1 second after impact.

## Effects
- [Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), 8 pts for 1 sec
- [Intense Flames Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 15 secs; if the [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames), raises damage to 10/s at first rank or 12/s at second rank
- [Aspect of Terror](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aspect_of_Terror), raises damage by 10 pts (15 if both ranks of Augmented Flames are unlocked first).

## Notes
- Like most Fire spells, it can be used to set off [Fire Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Rune) or [oil slicks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks) from a safe distance (though [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) or [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md) may be preferable due to their greater range). In addition, casting it or merely having it ready to cast in an area with [flammable gas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flammable_Gas) will result in a massive explosion.
- Because all players start with this spell, the tome is only useful as decoration or as an item to sell to merchants.
- In the original Skyrim with no DLC, Flames was worth 20 XP/s, on par with [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) and [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md) for XP per Magicka point spent. [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) knocked this down to 14 XP/s, probably unintentionally, making it much less favorable for XP gain.
