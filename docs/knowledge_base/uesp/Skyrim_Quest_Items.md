# Quest Items


Quest items have no weight, even if they state otherwise in the menu. Some quest items will have no weight even before the apparent start of the quest. This can occur when the quest is running in the background, but has no journal entries yet.

Items that can be improved have the necessary material provided in the table. If improvement will benefit from a perk, that is also given. Improving enchanted items always requires the Arcane Blacksmith perk.

## Dragon Claws
Dragon claws are used to open circular puzzle doors in [Nordic ruins](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md). [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, and [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup> each contain a special holder for all ten dragon claws in the base game. [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md#Interior)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)</sup> has holders for all eleven, with the two halves of the Amethyst Claw fitting into a single slot.

## Notes
- You can remove quest weapons from your inventory by placing them on weapon racks. If you obtain a quest-related weapon before starting a quest, start the quest, then remove/take the weapon from a rack, the quest will proceed normally. <sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>
- Amren's Family Sword, Queen Freydis's Sword and Dravin's Bow can all be reobtained by you after the quests end: the first and the last can be pickpocketed from their owners, while the Queen's Sword can be bought (or also pickpocketed) from Oengul. You can also buy Calcelmo's Ring from Calcelmo after it is delivered to him.
