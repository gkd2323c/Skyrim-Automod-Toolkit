The Blessings of Hircine *A call for Bosmer to follow the will of Hircine*
---
This book was removed from the game after several updates and may not reflect current lore.

---
It is the will of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) that the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) become as [we were](Lore_Ooze.md) in the [Dawn Age](Lore_Dawn_Era.md). Before [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) trapped us in a single shape, before he told us our story and took away our freedom.

The [gift of Hircine](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) is the gift of a second shape. The sacrament of Hircine is the scent of prey on the wind, the taste of blood on the tongue. Praise Hircine and his Houndsmen! Rise up and reject the tyranny of shape and story!
