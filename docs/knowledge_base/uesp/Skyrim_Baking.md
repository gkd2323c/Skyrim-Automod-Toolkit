# Baking


Baking allows you to combine raw food and ingredients to create finished dishes. Baked food provides more substantial benefits than raw food. All of the recipes listed below are available at the beginning of the game even if their associated ingredients are not. For recipe information related to cooking, see [this page](Skyrim_Cooking.md).

Several [Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) also add pre-built baking locations. A baking surface can be found in [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup> and an oven in [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup>, [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Farming.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, [Nchuanthumz](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz__Dwarven_Home.md)</sup> and [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>.
