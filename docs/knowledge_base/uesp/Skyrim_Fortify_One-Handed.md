*Alchemy description:* **One-handed weapons do <mag>% more damage for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **One-handed attacks do <mag>% more damage.**
**Fortify One-handed** increases the damage of [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md).

- [Hanging Moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md)

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to [enchant](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) custom items with Fortify One-handed if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Bracers/Gauntlets, Necklaces, and Rings *[of Wielding](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_One-Handed)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Wielding*, *of Major Wielding*, *of Extreme Wielding*, *of Eminent Wielding*, and *of Peerless Wielding.*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Cicero's Clothes](Skyrim_Cicero%27s_Clothes.md)
- [Deathbrand Gauntlets](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Gauntlets.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Fists of Randagulf](Skyrim_Fists_of_Randagulf.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>
- [Her Hand Gauntlets](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Gauntlets.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
- [Jester's Clothes](Skyrim_Jester%27s_Clothes.md)
- [Kenro's Helmet](Skyrim_Kenro%27s_Helmet.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
- [Nightingale Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Gloves)

[Linwe's Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe%27s_Gloves) can technically be disenchanted, allowing you to learn a special version of Fortify One-handed named **Shadowstrike**. However, Shadowstrike cannot be used to enchant any items because no enchanting slots are enabled for the effect.

A unique version of [Ebony Spell Knight Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Knight_Armor_Items#Ebony_Spell_Knight_Armor)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md)</sup> with Fortify One-handed is a reward for completing [Crypt of the Heart](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md)</sup> and choosing the corrupted heart ending. It is possible to disenchant it, but not advisable to do so as the effect cannot normally be applied to body apparel.

## Blessings
- The [Blessing of Boethiah](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Boethiah.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> provides a 10% Fortify One-handed effect for 8 hours.
- The [Elytra's Fierce Aura](Skyrim_Elytra%27s_Fierce_Aura.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup> provides +10 Fortify One-handed for 5 minutes.

## Notes
- The name of the enchanting version of this effect is actually "Fortify One- **H** anded" instead of "Fortify One-handed".
- The [Call to Arms](Skyrim_Call_to_Arms.md) spell includes a secondary Fortify One-handed effect, which directly increases the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill of allies.

## Bugs
- Fortify One-handed does not affect the damage of daggers. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
