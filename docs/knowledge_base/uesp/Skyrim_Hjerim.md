[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-interior-Hjerim_11.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hjerim_11.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_11.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_11.jpg) Main Reception [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-interior-Hjerim_10.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hjerim_10.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_10.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_10.jpg) Secret Room Located in the Valunstrad district, the name translated from the ancient Nordic language means "Home of Frost". The property once belonged to Friga Shatter-Shield, daughter of [Tova Shatter-Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tova_Shatter-Shield), but it was abandoned after Friga was killed.

Purchasing the house before completing [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md) will begin that quest as soon as the house is entered for the first time. Prior to purchasing any of the upgrades, Hjerim features little more than a wardrobe, poor quality double bed, and a chest. However the upgrades available for Hjerim include a large armory with many display cases, weapon and shield plaques, mannequins for displaying items, and a number of bookshelves. Hjerim also features a secret chamber hidden behind a false-backed wardrobe, which can be turned into an [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) and [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) room. It is also one of only two houses where purchasing the children's bedroom doesn't remove any of the other upgrades (the other is [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md)).

Hjerim can be purchased having sided with either the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) or [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). Once allied with the Stormcloaks, the [Rescue from Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad.md) quest must be completed before [Jorleif](Skyrim_Jorleif.md) will make the house available for sale. However, if [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) has been given to the Stormcloaks as part of [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md), it may be necessary to liberate [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) before Jorleif will make this house available. If the Imperial Legion is chosen as an ally, then the defeat of the Stormcloaks is required before it is available to buy.

The manor is found in the far northwest corner of the city, next door to [House of Clan Shatter-Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield) and opposite [House of Clan Cruel-Sea](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:House_of_Clan_Cruel-Sea). ## Hjerim
Prior to purchasing any upgrades, when entering the house you will find blood stains on the floor, a few bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and various pieces of clutter scattered on the floor of the first floor. In an alcove to the south is an empty [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). To the west is a separate area with a doorway connecting it to the main room. In this area is a small unit where you find the pile of [leaflets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beware_the_Butcher!), a [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butcher_Journal_1), and a [strange amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strange_Amulet) all relating to the related quest. There is also a normal tall wardrobe containing a selection of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots) and the tall wardrobe with a false back giving access to the room where the butcher performs his disgusting rites in an effort to raise his sister who died several years ago. The room contains many skulls and bones scattered all across the floor and on the table, some of which are cleaned while others are still covered in gore and blood.

Upstairs is mostly empty, with exception of the master bedroom, which contains a poor quality, large king-sized bed, coming out from the north wall, slightly away from the wall. There is a square wooden table and two chairs against the south wall. Against the east wall is a small wardrobe containing more fine clothes and boots.

**When purchasing upgrades, it's best to start with getting the house cleaned** (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

With each upgrade purchased, decorations will include several floor coverings and goat horn lights on stands, walls, chandeliers and shelves, etc. The exception is the hidden room that is now used for alchemy and enchanting as it gets neither additional light nor floor coverings. For details of what is included in each room see [below](#Upgrades.2C_by_Room).

## Upgrades, by Room

### Clean Up That Murderer's Mess
The house is cleaned throughout, removing all blood stains, and the chest that was against the south wall is removed (make sure you aren't storing anything in the chest before purchasing this upgrade). All the clutter, etc gets removed, while in the secret room, this is fully cleaned, the bones, skulls and even the table that Calixto Corrium's sister was laid out on. A set of shelves against the west wall is added. All the items in the master bedroom remain.

### Kitchen
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-interior-Hjerim_08.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hjerim_08.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_08.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_08.jpg) Kitchen This furnishes the room that is immediately on the right when entering the house. There is a fire pit in the northwestern corner, while in the middle of the north wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging in front. To the left is an ornamental cauldron with a low table in front, and a rack of [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat), two [raw rabbit legs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg), four [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot), two sprigs of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulbs all on top. To the right of the fireplace is a pile of firewood with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) sticking out of it. Above the wood, hanging from three racks are two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), two bunches of [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md), two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

Against the east wall, to the right of the fireplace is a small square table and two chairs. The table holds fine crockery including [jugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug), [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), [bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), etc. There is a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), two portions of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) contained in a bowl, a [boiled creme treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and two bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine).

Further to the right of the table set back in an alcove are two sets of shelves, they hold three bottle of alto wine, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), four bottles of Nord mead, two goblets, a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [honey nut treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat), two [potions of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration), five random [potions of lasting potency](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Lasting_Potency), and two random [potions of extra magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka). Against the south wall are a pair of wall shelves, the lower shelf holds a pile of sacks, while the top shelf holds two salt piles and several wedges of cheese.

On the far side of the doorway is a small, empty bookcase, beside this is another set of shelves which holds a raw venison haunch, an eidar cheese wheel and a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese). Against the west wall is a table that appears to be used for preparing the food. There is a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, four bottles of Nord mead, a bowl containing a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie), a raw joint of [beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), a [tomato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), a couple of pieces of cheese, several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and a bowl overflowing with [red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and [green apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple). On the floor beside the fire pit is a cast iron pot containing five tomatoes.

### The Child's Bedroom

### Housecarl's Bedroom
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-interior-Hjerim_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hjerim_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_05.jpg) Housecarl's bedroom Once you become Thane of Eastmarch, entering the second bedroom upstairs, there is a square wooden table and chair in an alcove on the east wall laid for one with fancy crockery. A single bed is against the south wall in another alcove, while in a third alcove on the west wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Against the north wall is a dresser also containing clothes and having a solution of strength and a [philter of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Philter_of_Haggling) on top.

## Notes
- The list of upgrades in the house guidebook given to you by Jorleif after purchasing the house lists incorrect upgrades.
- Even before Hjerim has been purchased as your house, resting on the main bed will render you well-rested. With the added bonus of four safe storage containers, it can be used as a home before it has been purchased.
- Hjerim used to belong to the now deceased Friga Shatter-Shield - this can be confirmed during the quest [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md), where you can ask [Tova Shatter-Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tova_Shatter-Shield) for the key to Hjerim, referring to it as "your daughter's house".

## Bugs
- There are a few bugs which affect all houses, see the [bugs](Skyrim_Houses.md#Bugs) section of the Houses page for details and fixes. [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-interior-Hjerim_09.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hjerim_09.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_09.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hjerim_09.jpg) Main reception upgrade, without cleaning
- The option to buy "Clean up the murderer's mess" may disappear if you purchase the Living Room upgrade first, but the "mess" will still be there. This seems to be a scripting error, as the living room option disables the cleaning option, which does not disable itself. It should reappear after the next purchase. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4.15, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can use the console command `DF492.Disable` to clean up the mess.
- Due to incorrect conditions, the steward may prematurely tell you there are no more improvements that can be made to Hjerim even if the killer's mess is still present. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- When purchasing the option to clean up the murderer's mess, entering the house can cause a black screen and full freeze if the chest had anything added to it, even if it was the original contents. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. The chest has now been unlinked from the enable parent marker and will remain behind when the mess is removed.
- It is possible that the chest near the master bed will not be present, even though it is listed as included. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can fix this issue by entering Hjerim, opening the console, and typing the following: `prid 10FD82`, press enter, then `enable`. This should make the chest appear.
- This can be caused during [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md). If you confront [Wuunferth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wuunferth_the_Unliving) directly instead of going to [Jorleif](Skyrim_Jorleif.md) with your suspicions, the chest will not appear when you buy the property later.
- The [Windhelm Home Decorating Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm_Home_Decorating_Guide) talks about a guest room which is not an available purchase option for Hjerim. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
- The two dagger display cases in the master bedroom are buggy: - Most daggers placed in the lower case cannot be retrieved (Dragon Priest style daggers have been reported to be removable).
- Daggers placed in the upper case are extremely difficult to retrieve (Daggers with unusual thicknesses, such as Keening, may be removable by facing the case from just the right angle).
- Multiple daggers can be placed in the same case. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If the previous solution doesn't work: Open the console, click the dagger that is stuck in the case, enter `getpos z`, note the decimal number that is shown, enter `setpos z N` where N is the decimal number +10. For example, if `getpos z` prints 216.06, then you should enter `setpos z 226.06`. This command moves the dagger so it's floating above the case, where you can easily grab it. It works for the top and bottom cases.
