# Soulrazer Knight

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-creature-The_Sable_Knight.jpg/200px-ON-creature-The_Sable_Knight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Sable_Knight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Sable_Knight.jpg) The Sable Knight Soulrazer Knights (or **Hollow Sentinels**) are [Golem](Lore_Golem.md) -like entities resembling knightly suits of armor which are animated by [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) energies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They are primarily found on [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOVSK-2)</sup> but have also been found on other islands on the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They are an enigma, with people speculating as to who created them. For their nature, theories exist on them being manifestations of a being's instincts, with the willpower being what keeps them moving. On origins, one possibility is that they were created by [druids](Lore_Druids.md) as they are often seen protecting sacred sites, though others believe at minimum a mage was involved.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTSK-4)</sup>

Lord Leobert Oris, knight commander of the Death's Valor Knightly Order, transformed himself into a Soulrazer Knight after abandoning his knightly creed and flaying himself of all his flesh. His spirit was placed into a suit of armor, and his ritual also condemned all within the Order's keep to undeath.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSS-5)</sup>

The Sable Knight was one of the first Soulrazer Knights conjured by a necromancer to aid in battle. After the conjurer's death, it roamed around High Isle and spread its necromantic energies, reanimating those it has slain.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONSKQ-6)</sup>

Soulrazer mounts also exist, including those shaped like [bears](Lore_Bear.md), [guars](Lore_Guar.md), [senches](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), [steeds](Lore_Horse.md), and [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESODCC-7)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Corelanya](Lore_Clan_Corelanya.md) necromancer Valendir took a form resembling a Soulrazer Knight when his spirit was channeled into the suits of armor in his manor.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GH-8)</sup> Additionally, Blind Path cultists serving [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md) utilized crystalline constructs resembling Soulrazer Knights.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> It is unknown if this is simply asset reuse.
