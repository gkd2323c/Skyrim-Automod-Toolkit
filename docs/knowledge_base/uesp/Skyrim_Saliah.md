[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-npc-Saliah.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Saliah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saliah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saliah.jpg) Saliah **Saliah** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who is [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dawnguard_Outrider) after completing either [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) for the [Volkihar vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) or [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) for the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). If you approach her, she'll say she has no time to talk as she is on urgent business for the Dawnguard, and she'll demand that you stand aside. She carries a [key](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Cache_Key.md) to Dawnguard caches and her [orders](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Orders_-_Saliah.md) from the Dawnguard. If you have sided with [Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) and the vampires, she will be hostile toward you. Saliah is labeled as protected if you side with the Dawnguard in A New Order. There is a chance she will be riding a horse when you first encounter her.

## Dialogue

- *"Forgive me, but I am on urgent business for the Dawnguard. Stand aside!"*
- *"Stand aside!"*

## Notes
- Saliah does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female Redguard face.
- Saliah can most commonly be found directly west of [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md), and south of [Silverdrift Lair](Skyrim_Silverdrift_Lair.md) at a 3-way crossroads.
- Below are maps showing the relative locations of the Dawnguard caches. Saliah is usually encountered near one of these locations, or en route between them.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Morthal_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg/200px-SR-place-Morthal_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Morthal_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg) [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-place-Nightgate Inn_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg/200px-SR-place-Nightgate Inn_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Nightgate Inn_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg) [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Winterhold_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg/200px-SR-place-Winterhold_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold_Dawnguard Cache Location.jpg) [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md)

- The Winterhold cache is described as being "under the great arch". This is a Nordic arch visible to the west as you head south on the road out of Winterhold, in a mountain pass just north of the [Shrine of Azura](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Azura.md). If you reach [Whistling Mine](Skyrim_Whistling_Mine.md), you've gone too far.

## Bugs
- Despite her orders not involving [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), she may be encountered on her way there on the bridge near [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md). If you follow her for a bit, she will die as her pathfinding will make her drown in the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md) after turning right just before reaching the city.
