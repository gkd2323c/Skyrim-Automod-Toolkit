# Dellese Isles

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |
| Continent | [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) |
| Province | [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) |

The Dellese Isles (or **Dellesia**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Hahd-1)</sup> are a region of islands found within [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). They are considered one of the major lands (along with [Greater Bretony](Lore_Greater_Bretony.md), the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) [tribes](Lore_River_Horse_Bretons.md), and the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md)) that the clans of the province inhabit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1H-2)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-POT-3)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-4)</sup> They are related to the former Dallesian peninsula.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Hahd-1)</sup>

## Notes
- It is unknown where exactly the Isles are found. *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* head writer [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson) thinks they may be located outside of [Iliac Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iliac_Bay), likely in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Ted-5)</sup> - On a map from *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)*, an unknown set of what seem to be islands can be seen northwest of High Rock.
- One of the unwritten myths of *[The Monomyth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Monomyth)* was associated with Dellese.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-Mono2-6)</sup>
