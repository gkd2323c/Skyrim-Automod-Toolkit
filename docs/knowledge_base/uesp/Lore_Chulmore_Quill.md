Chulmore Quill was the ringmaster and announcer of the [Quill Circus](Lore_Quill_Circus.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCQ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFTQC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Black Arrow-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Loranna-4)</sup> Quill was originally born as a minor noble of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), and was named **Lord Dygbal** of County Ivytyde—but ran away to join the circus when he was younger.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Loranna-4)</sup>
