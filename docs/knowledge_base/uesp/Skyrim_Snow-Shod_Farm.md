## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg) Snow-Shod Farm There are two buildings on the lot: a farmhouse and a windmill. In the field, there are four bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) , ten [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and five [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants, all of which [Addvild](Skyrim_Addvild.md) will pay you for gathering. There is a [Gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), two patches of [Scaly Pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota) mushrooms, a [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), a [blue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) and a [purple mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) all growing in and around the farm. There are also four [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md), two nests for collecting [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) and one [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) that produces [milk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk) all on the farm.

### Snow-Shod Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm.jpg) Snow-Shod Farm interior Entering the farmhouse brings you straight into the main living room, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand against the northeast wall. Coming out from the southeast wall is a large wooden table with benches either side, with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) on top. There are [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks and barrels all around the farmhouse. On the southwest wall is a wall shelf holding clutter, with a chair against the same wall and a cupboard further to the right of the chair. The cupboard holds a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and three loaves of bread. Above the cupboard, hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). A doorway to the northwest leads to the top of a flight of stairs leading down to the cellar. The stairs descend to the northeast.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Farm_02.jpg) Snow-Shod Farm bedroom At the bottom is a storage area with a pile of firewood and a couple of straw bales under the stairs and against the southwest wall is a set of shelves that hold several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), two loaves of bread, several pieces of cheese and a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Reality & Other Falsehoods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reality_%26_Other_Falsehoods)*. There is a dresser to the left, against the southeast wall with a bottle of wine atop. To the left is a doorway through into their bedroom which consists of a double bed coming out from the middle of the southeast wall, with a wall shelf above the bed holding a bottle of alto wine and a dresser at the foot of the bed with a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl) on top. In the eastern corner is an end table with a copy of *[Rising Threat, Vol. IV](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._IV)*, a chair against the southwest wall and a wardrobe in the northwest corner containing [clothing](Skyrim_Clothing.md).

## Notes
- The farmyard is one of the common places you can encounter the [revelers](Skyrim_Reveler.md).
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.
