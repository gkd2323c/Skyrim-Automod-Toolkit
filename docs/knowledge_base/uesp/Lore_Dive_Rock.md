[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/LG-cardart-Dive_Rock_Fall.png/200px-LG-cardart-Dive_Rock_Fall.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Dive_Rock_Fall.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Dive_Rock_Fall.png) Dive Rock (Legends) Dive Rock is a mountain peak located in the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md), in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Dive Rock is reportedly the highest natural observation point across the entire province,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AJ-1)</sup> with a clear view of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBView-2)</sup> Although the tip of Dive Rock provides unparalleled views of the Imperial Province, falling from the edge is lethal.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OB-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The site is infamous for the deaths of those who have been pushed over the edge.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Not only known for its vistas, the peak is also the source of a local legend called the [Horror of Dive Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Horror_of_Dive_Rock), a monster that killed dozens of people and even [cattle](Lore_Cow.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AJ-1)</sup>

## History
During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the northeastern portion of central Cyrodiil was occupied by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). Dive Rock and its mountain range fell within these borders.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONEbonheart-7)</sup>

In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md), [Agnar the Unwavering](Lore_Agnar_the_Unwavering.md), and his wife, [Svenja Snow-Song](Lore_Svenja_Snow-Song.md), traveled across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in hopes of finding and slaying the [Uderfrykte Matron](Lore_Uderfrykte_Matron.md), the progenitor of the [Udyrfrykte](Lore_Udyrfrykte.md) that massacred Thirsk in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). Around [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), they tracked the matron to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where a hunter told them about the Horror of Dive Rock, which Agnar and Svenja concluded was the matron. They established a camp on the overlook at Dive Rock, and after three days of watching, they spotted the matron. Once they confronted the matron, Svenja was quickly struck down and devoured, forcing Agnar to retreat to the camp. The chieftain made final preparations to attack the beast, but he was killed as well.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AJ-1)</sup>

That same year, the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) stumbled upon the camp, discovered Agnar's body and journal, and slew the Uderfrykte Matron.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-OBHorror-8)</sup> Around that same time, a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble named Andre Labouche attempted to scale Dive Rock, but fell to his death while climbing the sheer cliff face.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBAndre-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
