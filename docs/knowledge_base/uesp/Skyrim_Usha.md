[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-npc-Usha.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Usha.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Usha.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Usha.jpg) Usha **Usha** is a dead [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) found north of [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md) behind a barred gate over the stream. By climbing the mountain east of the gate, you can make your way around to jump down into the wooden structure behind it. Usha lies dead on a bedroll with a few [empty skooma bottles](Skyrim_Empty_Skooma_Bottle.md) strewn about his side. On the table next to him are three bottles of [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md), a lantern, a coin purse, and a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_to_Usha.md) from his lover. On another table, you'll find six chunks of [gold ore](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md). To the right of this table is an unlocked chest with a pickaxe sitting on top, and an East Empire Company strongbox in front of it.
