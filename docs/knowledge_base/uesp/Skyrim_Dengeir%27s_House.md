## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There is a small fenced garden at the front of the house with a tanning rack, wooden bench and several unusable barrels along the wall of the house. At the rear of the property the area has several tree stumps and a [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch), most of the area at the back is used by the wood mill which is owned and run by [Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md). There is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and several tree trunks by the river running behind the house.

### Dengeir's House
Upon entering there are two single owned beds, where [Tekla](Skyrim_Tekla.md) and [Thadgeir](Skyrim_Thadgeir.md) sleep, coming out from the southwest wall, with a dresser between them. On the dresser is a copy of *[Lost Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends)*. Opposite the entrance is a fireplace, to the left is a food barrel with a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel) on top. There are three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on the mantel and a kettle containing four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) by the hearth. To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table and chair with bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), two potatoes, three [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) on top. Above the table are two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and to [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging from racks as well as two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), two garlic braids and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging from the ceiling.

Further to the right on the northwest wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and three bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). In front of this is a long fancy table holding a bottle of wine and one of alto wine, three loaves of bread, a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese) and two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). To the right, long the northeast wall is a chair and wardrobe with a [sabre cat pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt) on top. At the end of the room, coming out from the southeast wall is a queen sized bed with end tables either side. The left of these holds a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling), with a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) on the floor to the left of it. The end table to the right of the bed holds four loose septims. There is an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe) under the bed and an unlocked non-respawning [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing leveled items at the foot of it.

Along the southwest wall at this end of the house is a food barrel and a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). Around the corner on the southeast wall is a cupboard holding two common books, a copy of *[The Tale of Dro'Zira](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Tale_of_Dro%27Zira)* and a copy of *[Flight from the Thalmor](Skyrim_Flight_from_the_Thalmor.md)*. There are a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) and bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) on top of the cupboard and on the floor, to the right is a flower basket are three more pine thrush eggs.

## Notes
- All dressers, cupboards, and end tables in the location are non-respawning.
- Based on many internal names, the name of the relevant key, as well as its name on the local map, it seems that this house was originally known as "Dengeir's **Hall**" instead.
- If [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) will take Falkreath during Civil War or trading hold from Imperial control during Season Unending, all items on this house are free take without penalty.
