[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-ingredient-Elves_Ear.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Elves_Ear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Elves_Ear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Elves_Ear.jpg) Dried elves ear branches **Elves Ear** is harvested from branches of **Dried Elves Ear** that are found hanging to dry inside homes and other buildings, typically side by side with [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) and [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). It is not possible to harvest fresh elves ear directly from plants. Note that it is not related to [Falmer ear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Ear), which actually comes from elves (specifically, [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md)).

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

## Notes
Elves Ear is very similar in appearance to real-world Sage, which is a culinary herb, similar to how Elves Ear is portrayed in the game (as it is often found in kitchens).
