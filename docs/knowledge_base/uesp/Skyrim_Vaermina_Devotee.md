[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-Vaermina_Devotee.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vaermina_Devotee.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vaermina_Devotee.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vaermina_Devotee.jpg) Vaermina Devotee **Vaermina Devotees** are worshippers of the Daedric Prince [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md). They reside in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) protecting the [Skull of Corruption](Skyrim_Skull_of_Corruption.md). However, when an Orc war party attacked the temple they released the Miasma, a gas that put everybody to sleep. After [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md) unsealed the temple so he could destroy the Skull of Corruption, the Miasma lifted and the Orcs and devotees began to reawaken.

The devotees wear [Vaermina robes](Skyrim_Vaermina_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000bacd7). They carry a leveled [potion of restore magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md) and one sample of a common-type [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md).
