[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-npc-Parven.png/200px-SR-npc-Parven.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Parven.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Parven.png) Parven **Parven** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). According to the [note](Skyrim_Please_Read_Aloud.md) you must read before the dinner begins, he's the brother of the anonymous person who arranged the dinner. He only shows up when the player is a vampire.
