[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Greenmead is a small region between the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) and the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) in west-central [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is specifically located on the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md), east of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md). The town of [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md) was the region's premier settlement<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONGreenmead-1)</sup> and was built around a lone [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) watchtower known as the Eye of the Greenmead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONJurana-2)</sup> There is a cave right on the Gold Road known as **Greenmead Cave** and despite its location deep in the mainland, it contains a water source and even rare giant [mudcrabs](Lore_Mudcrab.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBGreenmead-3)</sup>

When the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) occurred in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), central Cyrodiil was taken over by the alliances. The Greenmead region was wholly claimed by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) and Vlastarus was a significant point of contention between the armies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONGreenmead-1)</sup> In that same time, the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) built [dark anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md) across the region. One was built in Greenmead, between Vlastarus and [Ceyatatar](Lore_Ceyatatar.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONDolmen-4)</sup>
