<sup>†</sup> *These are used as crafting materials, but not in alchemy.* A **Daedra Heart** (or **aortas daedrae**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is an ingredient that can be harvested from slain [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), primarily [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md). In older times, they could be found on many other varieties of Daedra, including [Clannfears](Lore_Clannfear.md), [Daedroths](Lore_Daedroth.md), [Golden Saints](Lore_Golden_Saint.md), [Hungers](Lore_Hunger.md), [Ogrims](Lore_Ogrim.md), and [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md). These beings, to the extent that they can still be encountered, now produce other ingredients. But Dremora have consistently been a reliable source for Daedra Hearts.
