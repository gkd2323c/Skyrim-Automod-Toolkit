Ode to a Horker **A Poem by [Drusk](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Drusk) the Would-Be Bard** *A poem about horkers*
---
I sit upon a [silent dock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jehanna_Docks) and look upon a frozen sea
To stare at [horkers](Lore_Horker.md) frolicking so fancy free.

In water and on ice they scamper
Their barks and calls like so much banter.

I laugh with glee at the large bull, Albakross
Whose antics remind me of a bucking horse.
