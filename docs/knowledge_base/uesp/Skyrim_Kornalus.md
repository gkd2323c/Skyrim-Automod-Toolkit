[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Kornalus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kornalus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kornalus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kornalus.jpg) Kornalus **Kornalus Frey** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [warlock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) who can be found in the ruined fort of [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md), conducting disturbing experiments on the unfortunate [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) residing there.

He wears [Black Mage Robes](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Black_Mage_Robes) and Black Mage Boots, and carries a dagger of up to Elven quality.

## Dialogue
If snuck up on, he can be overheard muttering to himself:

- *"Perhaps some more Glow Dust in the next batch of potions. That should increase their resistance."*
- *"Let's see if this works..."*
- *"No, but that won't account for the size issue. Blast, if only so many of these creatures hadn't escaped."*

When you approach him, he will confront you, before attacking you:

- *"What? How did you get this far?"*

If you can sneak into the room unnoticed and pull the lever keeping the spiders in their enclosure, Kornalus will yell out:

- *"What? No! Get back in your cage!"*

## Notes
- Kornalus' physical appearance is randomly generated; he may look different in each playthrough.
- There is no known connection between him and [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md)
- The key Kornalus holds is called [Kornalus Frey's key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kornalus_Frey%27s_Key). It cannot be pickpocketed from him; the only way to get it is to kill him and take it from his body.
- A slightly longer conversation can be seen in the game data, but appears to have been removed in the final version. Kornalus would have confronted you, saying:

- *"What? Who are you? How did you get this far?"*
- **Your weird experiments are over, wizard.**
- *"Your corpse will feed my spiders for weeks. Now die, you fool!"*

- If you raise Kornalus with a raise dead spell ([dead thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md), [dread zombie](Skyrim_Dread_Zombie.md), etc.), any time he enters combat, he will use the second line of fight dialogue seen above.
