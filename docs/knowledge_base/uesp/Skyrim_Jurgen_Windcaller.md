[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-npc-Jurgen_Windcaller.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jurgen_Windcaller.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jurgen_Windcaller.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jurgen_Windcaller.jpg) Jurgen Windcaller **Jurgen Windcaller** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) hero who can be found in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). He wears [Greybeard's robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes) along with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots).

A prominent war leader in the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md), Jurgen Windcaller used his voice as a weapon and a way to grow in strength, like many Tongues of the time. This changed for Jurgen, however, when the Nordic armies suffered an ignominious defeat at [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). Jurgen would use the defeat as inspiration to discover the [Way of the Voice](Lore_Way_of_the_Voice.md) and found the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md).

For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Jurgen_Windcaller.md).

## Dialogue
When spoken to, Jurgen may comment on his devout followers, the Greybeards.

*"My disciples still follow the difficult path - the Way of the Voice is neither wide, nor easy. But if you stray from wisdom then to Sovngarde you will not return."* He will also warn you:

*"Fate drives you, but you follow your own path. Choose wisely, lest you wander into evil."*
## Notes
- You must retrieve the [Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller) from his tomb in the Nordic ruin [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) during the quest [The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](Skyrim_The_Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller.md).
- When the player approaches his grave after taking the horn, they can absorb a dragon soul from it. What this means is unknown.
- The inscription on his tomb in Ustengrav is "Windcaller" in [Daedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Alphabet).
