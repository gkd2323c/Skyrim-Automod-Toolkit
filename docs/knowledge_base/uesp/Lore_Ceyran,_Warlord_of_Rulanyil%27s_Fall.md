Ceyran, Warlord of Rulanyil's Fall **by [Merethic Society](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Merethic_Society)** *A short biography of a minor Ayleid warlord*
---
[Ceyran](Lore_Ceyran.md) was a minor [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) warlord of the middle [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), most famous for building and losing three separate dominions during his long life. It is not clear whether he was a refugee from the Ayleid purges in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), or was born after his clan fled to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Rumored to be a devotee of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), Ceyran was eventually killed by an unknown assassin in [1E 1102](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1102).
