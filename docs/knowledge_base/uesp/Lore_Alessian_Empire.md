The Alessian Empire, also called the **First Empire**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSOHR-1)</sup> the **Empire of Cyrodiil**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALSS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOO-3)</sup> and the **Empire of Men**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBMOP-4)</sup> was established by the former [Nede](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede) slaves of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) in [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243) following the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3AEM-5)</sup> The Empire traces its founding to the moment when Saint [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) received the [Dragonfires](Lore_Dragonfires.md) and the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md) from [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md); she used the Amulet to drive the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) out of the mortal realm.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-To SA-6)</sup> Their empire would last for over two thousand years. Akatosh told Alessia that as long as the Dragonfires remained lit and [her heirs](Lore_Dragonborn.md) wore the Amulet of Kings, the gates of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) would remain shut and [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) would be spared the depredations of the Daedra.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-To SA-6)</sup>

Only a few rulers of the First Empire are now known: Queen Alessia, its founder and spiritual leader; [Belharza the Man-Bull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Belharza), Alessia's heir by her consort [Morihaus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morihaus), crowned in [1E 266](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_266); [Gorieus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorieus), crowned in [1E 461](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_461); and [Hestra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hestra), who expanded the reaches of the Empire all the way to [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) in [1E 1029](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1029).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLoading-7)</sup> One of the defining events of the Empire was the rise of the zealously monotheistic [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order), which followed the teachings of the prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup> The Order's influence spread rapidly until, in [1E 361](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_361), its puritanical doctrines were formally adopted and enforced by the Empire.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-9)</sup> Thereafter, the arch-prelates of the Order were said to wield almost as much power as the emperors themselves.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CATAYQ-10)</sup> It was a sect of the Order called the Marukhati Selective that was responsible for the [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md) known as the "Middle Dawn".<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-WWYDB-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-VFTDB-12)</sup>

The Alessian Empire's territory fluctuated considerably throughout its history. At its inception it encompassed East [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), including the [Nibenay Valley](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nibenay_Valley) and the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), which later became the centerpiece of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md); West Cyrodiil remained a patchwork of independent [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) kingdoms that resisted the Alessian Order's dogma.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-RTR-13)</sup> A number of Ayleid vassal-states also briefly existed under the Empire's umbrella until the xenophobic Alessian Order methodically extinguished them.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-9)</sup> The allied city-state of [Miscarcand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Miscarcand) initially resisted the Alessian Order by force, but over time its inhabitants fled to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) to avoid persecution.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> Following the defeat and death of High King [Kjoric the White](Lore_Kjoric_the_White.md) in [1E 478](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_478), the Empire seized portions of southern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but failed in its bid to acquire the Kingdom of [Skingrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skingrad).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-RTR-13)</sup> A westward expansion and attempt to annex High Rock was likewise thwarted at the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors) in [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482), which would be remembered as one of the Order's most disastrous mistakes.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-PGE1High-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SATD-16)</sup> High Rock would later be inducted into the Empire in [1E 1029](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1029) after the Empress [Hestra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hestra) defeated King [Styriche](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Styriche), the Vampire of [Verkarth](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md), only to reassert its independence in [1E 2305](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2305) in response to the Alessian Order's excesses.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSOHR-1)</sup> In [1E 1033](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1033) the Alessians under Hestra invaded [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) to suppress the pirates led by [Red Bramman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Red_Bramman) operating from the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-PGE3A-17)</sup> An unknown Alessian Emperor agreed to fund the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md)'s campaign against the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) in [1E 2241](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2241).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-BABO-18)</sup> Under the command of King [Bendu Olo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bendu_Olo), the fleet sailed to Thras in [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260) to mete vengeance upon the Sload for releasing the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) on the people of Tamriel.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

The final days of the Empire arrived in [1E 2321](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2321), when tensions within the Alessian Order's bloated priesthood gave way to internal strife, prompting the kingdoms of West Cyrodiil to completely sever ties with the Empire and establish their own government, the [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup> Hemorrhaging money and land, the Empire finally broke under the strain and the [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md) erupted.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup> After a decade of violence that wiped out half of the population of the [Iliac Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iliac_Bay)<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-NWOB-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-AHOD-21)</sup> and saw the loss of the Order's extensive monastic complex at [Lake Canulus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lake_Canulus),<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-COTF-22)</sup> among untold other atrocities, the war ended in [1E 2331](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2331) with the dissolution of the Alessian Order<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup> and the enthronement of a puppet emperor by the king of Skingrad.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-Ot Wo R-23)</sup> Cyrodiil and Tamriel would remain divided until the rise of [Reman I](Lore_Reman.md) and the foundation of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) in [1E 2703](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2703).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup>
