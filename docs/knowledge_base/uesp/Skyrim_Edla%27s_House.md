## Walkthrough

### Exterior
Along the west wall is a bench, a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), and a pen adjacent to Wulf's house holding a [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md), with a straw bale against the side of the house. Behind the house is a framed fishing net and an open shed containing a fish barrel, a rack holding five [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), and two more salmon on and next to a wooden block. Along the east wall is a partly buried crate. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Edla's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/SR-interior-Edla%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Edla%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Edla%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Edla%27s_House.jpg) Kitchen The entrance leads into a room with a small fire pit with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) on a stand behind. There is a second stand with a cast iron pot containing two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and a [carrot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot) to the right. On your left as you enter are a pair of shelves containing two [eidar cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and one of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). Hanging from the ceiling is a flat rack with three fillets of [horker meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat) and a joint of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison). On a second rack hanging from the ceiling are five salmon, a sample of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid. Under this is a narrow table with a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) on. To the right of the entrance are two more wall shelves containing two bottles of alto wine and in a wooden bowl a piece of [honeycomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honeycomb).

Against the north wall heading to the back of the room are three small steps underneath the flight of stairs up to the top floor. On your right, under the stairs is a wood pile, a crate, two sacks and a barrel. Against the south wall is a barrel and sack, beside these is a long table and two chairs. On the table is a mead barrel, bowl of [venison stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Stew), a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a wooden plate with a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and two wooden bowls one contains a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), the other a [rabbit haunch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_Haunch). In the southeast corner is a doorway to a twin bedroom. Against the back wall to the west is a wardrobe with a wooden plate containing two [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and to the right of it a kettle containing a fillet of horker meat and a cabbage and a basket containing three potatoes. There is also a stool with a cast iron pot containing a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes) next to a chair with a bottle of wine on the floor. Between the west wall and the stairs up is a doorway to a storage room.

On your left as you enter the storeroom is a set of shelves containing two horker tusks, a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) and a [deer pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt). Against the north wall is a narrow table with a sack on top. Against the east wall is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) set into a table with a shelf above it containing two [potions of cure disease](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease), a [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell), an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel), a [draught of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Strength) and a sample of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton). On the left is a hanging rack with a sample of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), frost mirriam and garlic braid. To the right is a sack and barrel. Against the south wall is a set of shelves containing an eidar cheese wheel, a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots), two [iron helmets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) and a bottle of alto wine.

Entering into the twin bedroom you will find a single bed in front of you in the southeast corner and on your left another single bed against the east wall. Above this bed is a shelf containing a bowl with two [flutes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute) inside.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-interior-Edla%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Edla%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Edla%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Edla%27s_House_02.jpg) Loft Upstairs looks like a typical loft. There is an open crate with three [cups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup) in. Behind that a fish barrel on it's side and an upright barrel with two [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate) and a cup on with a small set of shelves behind them. There are crates, bucket, and baskets all over, but none holding anything interesting. There is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on one of the crates with a wood pile behind it. There are three sacks on the floor and a second small set of shelves near the back containing a copy of *[Ahzidal's Descent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal%27s_Descent)* and a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl). Then there is another barrel and set of shelves off to the side.
