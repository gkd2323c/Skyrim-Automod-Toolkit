# Cascadia

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/BL-item-Cascadia.jpg/200px-BL-item-Cascadia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Cascadia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Cascadia.jpg) Cascadia (Blades) Cascadia is an enchanted sapphire ring of either [Maormeri](Lore_Maormer.md) or [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) origins. Some sources believe that the ring was crafted by the Sea Elves, but others claim that it was a failed attempt of a Nord enchanter to create a water-breathing ring. Although it does not provide the ability to breathe underwater, the sounds of the ocean can be heard from its stone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Casc-1)</sup> It also provides a boon to the absorptive abilities of the wearer, improves their willpower by granting them a defensive boon while casting spells, and enhances their melee combat capabilities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Casc2-2)</sup>

Around [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), it came into possession of [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-3)</sup>
