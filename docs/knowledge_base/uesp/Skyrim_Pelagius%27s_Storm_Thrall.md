[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-creature-Pelagius%27s_Storm_Thrall.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Pelagius%27s_Storm_Thrall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Pelagius%27s_Storm_Thrall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Pelagius%27s_Storm_Thrall.jpg) Pelagius's Storm Thrall **Pelagius's Storm Thrall** is a nigh-invincible [storm atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md) that can be found in [The Mind of a Madman](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_a_Madman.md) during the [Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md) quest, specifically during the "Pelagius' Paranoia" section. During this quest, two atronachs will be fighting arena style in a sunken box. Using the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) on the one assigned to you will cause it to change elements, possibly becoming this thrall.
