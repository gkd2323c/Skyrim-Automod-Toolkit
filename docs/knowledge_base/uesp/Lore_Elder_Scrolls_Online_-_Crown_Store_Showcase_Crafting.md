# Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Crafting

This page lists **crafting** entries from the [Crown Store Showcase](Lore_Elder_Scrolls_Online_-_Crown_Store_Showcase.md).

## Motifs

### Crown Crafting Motif: Assassins League
"A secret society of assassins, a guild of killers for hire—now that makes sense. That's just good business. And like every good business, we have standards. You wish to join us, and dress like one of our league? Then follow these instructions to craft a—dare I say it—killer outfit." –The Gray Maybe (January 2017)

Motif book and style items that enable crafting in the Assassins League style. Garb yourself appropriately for those times when you bring about someone's end. (March 2019)

### Crown Crafting Motif: Bloodforge Style
"Clanfellows, in the name of the [Hunt-Father](Lore_Hircine.md), we follow these sacred schema that define the trappings of we who work the [Bloodroot Forge](Lore_Bloodroot_Forge.md)." –Stoneheart the Heartstone

### Crown Crafting Motif: Celestial
"Prepare thyself to adopt the divine appearance of the [Constellations](Lore_Constellations.md) Incarnate! As above, so below: this was ever our creed. And thus, O [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) below, to craft thy arms and armor, thou shalt follow this guidance and these models. Grasp this motif quickly, while ye may. So saith the Guardian of Stars!"

### Crown Crafting Motif: Dreadhorn Style
"Wayward sons and daughters, embrace [Domihaus](Lore_Domihaus.md) and set to work to outfit our reborn clan—as the [Dreadhorn](Lore_Dreadhorn_Clan.md)!" –Gherig Bullblood

### Crown Crafting Motif: Grim Harlequin
Do you like dark humor, grim but funny? We've got a stylish motif for you! As Chamberlain [Haskill](Lore_Haskill.md) explains, "My Lord [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) has directed me to compile a guide for those seeking to create 'Grim Harlequin' arms and armor, so that the Mad God's mortal servants may have a way to make all their equipment look the same. Of course, this standardization runs counter to all other cult practices, and is insane on the face of it. What's your point?"

### Crown Crafting Motif: Ra Gada Style
The [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) or "Warrior Wave" of settlers from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) actually took place in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md)'s ninth century. The second, or "Tavan" wave, revered holy [Tava](Lore_Tava.md), Lady of the Air. Thus the Tavans' arms and armor are replete with bird imagery, which makes for a very distinctive crafting style.

### Crown Crafting Motif: Stalhrim Frostcaster Style
"The most curious aspect of this Frostcaster clothing was its extensive use of an unfamiliar translucent blue-white material labeled '[Stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md),' a sort of crystalline metal unlike anything I've ever seen. It was so strange that I laid hands upon it, rubbing and tapping. It was rigid, faceted yet smooth, and gave off a distinct chill." —Doctor [Alfidia Lupus](Lore_Alfidia_Lupus.md)

### Crown Crafting Motif Bundle: Thieves Guild
Now you can purchase the motif book and style items that enable crafting in the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) style! Here are some of Kari Coin-Counter's notes on the style: "Thieves Guild gloves may look like warrior's gauntlets above the wrist, but the fingers must be sheathed in soft kid or chamois so as not to interfere with the delicate manipulation so critical to thieving. And note the way our axes have hooked blades so they can act as prying tools when necessary."

### Crown Motif: Mercenary
This enables crafting in the utilitarian and militaristic Mercenary style. As mercenary captain Gaspard Emery says, "With the rise of the Three Alliances and the return of general warfare across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), mercenary services are once again in high demand. The mercenaries are back!"

### Crown Mimic Stone
Crafters! Are you tired of hunting for that elusive Goldscale or [Malachite](Lore_Malachite.md)? Mimic stones act as universal style items, enabling crafting in any known style! And they're available in quantities to suit any budget.

### Fang Lair Crafting Motif
Use to learn the [Fang Lair](Lore_Fang_Lair.md) crafting style. Also teaches the equivalent Fang Lair Outfit Styles. "For too long the magical discipline of [Necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) has been disregarded as a mere subset of [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md), or worse, dismissed outright because it is 'evil.' That changes now!" – [Orryn the Black](Lore_Orryn_the_Black.md)

### Malacath Cult
Forge-Wife Gurzbog gra-Gulog says, "Listen up, smiths! We don't pound hot metal around here just because it's fun. We forge for the glory of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)! Shoddy work will not be tolerated. You want to make items in the Malacath Cult style? Well, there's only one right way to do it, so pay attention!"

### Scalecaller Crafting Motif
Use to learn the Scalecaller crafting style. Also teaches the equivalent Scalecaller Outfit Styles. "The followers of the renegade [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md), Zaan, outfitted themselves in Scalecaller armor made of metal forged to resemble Dragonscale and bonemold crafted to resemble Dragonbone." – Jorvuld Davaux

### Trinimac Cult
The Venerable Uugus, Priest of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md), says, "The [Elves](Lore_Elves.md) have forgotten the warrior ideals of Trinimac, but we [Orsimer](Lore_Orsimer.md) keep them alive! As the Children of Trinimac, we embody his stoic courage and bold audacity. The arms we bear and the armor we wear shine with [gold](Lore_Gold.md), in remembrance of Trinimac's golden skin. And they are accented with polished [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), in token of [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md)'s blood when Trinimac tore out his [heart](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md)."

### Tsaesci Crafting Motif
Use this motif to craft items in the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) style. "Much may not be shared outside the true bloodlines, but this was never true of our artisanship. Would you follow the Serpentine Ways of Making? Then here, armorer, are your models." –Kiasa-Veda

## Outfit Styles

### Elder Scrolls Artifact: Sinweaver
The original [Sinweaver](Lore_Sinweaver.md)'s peerless craftsmanship made it the envy of Elven swordsmiths for generations. But something else stirred within the blade. Something dark and unknowable.

### Ilambris Arms Pack
The Ilambris Arms Pack provides an Outfit look for weapons in the style of the Ilambris twins from the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md) dungeon.

### Molag Kena Arms Pack
Arm yourself like a monarch of the [Xivkyn](Lore_Xivkyn.md)! This pack includes a complete set of Outfit styles for [Molag Kena](Lore_Molag_Kena.md) weapons that match the Undaunted awards.
