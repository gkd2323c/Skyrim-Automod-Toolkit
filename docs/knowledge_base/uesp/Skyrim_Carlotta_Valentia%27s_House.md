[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-place-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg) Carlotta Valentia's House **Carlotta Valentia's House** is a house in the Wind District of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), where [Carlotta Valentia](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia.md) lives with her daughter [Mila](Skyrim_Mila_Valentia.md).

The house is next door to [House of Clan Battle-Born](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Battle-Born.md), opposite [Amren's House](Skyrim_Amren%27s_House.md), it is split into four rooms and two floors. It contains only one zone, *[Carlotta Valentia's House](#Carlotta_Valentia.27s_House)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is in the southwestern corner of the Wind District, the first house on the left after ascending the steps to the left of the main gate. There is a [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) bush by the house at the top of the stairs from the Plain's District. The door is nearly always locked with a novice level lock.

### Carlotta Valentia's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.jpg) Carlotta's kitchen The first room serves as both kitchen and dining room, the main feature is the fire pit in the middle of the floor with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) over it. Hanging above the fire is a rack holding three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). To the right of the door are two chairs next to a small table with two [honey nut treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat) and a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) on top, beside the table against the east wall are some small shelves with candles, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and [cups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup) on them. On the north wall there are some wall shelves holding some general household kitchen items, and on a set of shelves in the northwest corner are three bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a couple of pieces of goat cheese, a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), and several [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks, with some more sacks leaning against the west wall.

The next room is the living room, on a bench to the left, against the east wall is a copy of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun)*, written by [Mikael](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mikael), another resident of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and involved in Carlotta's [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You). Above the bench is a wall shelf holding a copy of *[Of Crossed Daggers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers)* and household items. There are three chairs by a small table in the northwestern corner, which is sometimes used for eating meals at, with a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). Above the table are two shelves with a joint of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), a loaf of bread, a bowl of red apples and general items. The cupboard in the southwest corner holds two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), cups and other general items. There is also a narrow table under the stairs holding three bulbs of garlic in a bowl and some food sacks.

Under the stairs is the door to Mila's bedroom, it contains a single owned child's bed in the northwest corner, beside which is an end table holding a [long taffy treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat) and a [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). At the foot of the bed is a sack and on a square wooden table with two chairs in the southeast corner is a copy of *[Kolb & the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon)* and a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House_02.jpg) Carlotta's bedroom At the top of the stairs and above Mila's bedroom is a small landing with two chairs a low table and a cupboard containing a couple of common books. Carlotta's bedroom is to the north and consists of a lage four-poster double bed with an end table on either side against the north wall. On the right-hand end table is a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). On the floor to the left of the bed is a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Enchanter's Primer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Primer)*. Against the south wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a chair beside it. Under the eaves to the east is an unlocked non-respawning [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing gold and to the right is a round wooden table and chair. On the table is another potion of minor healing, a sweet roll and a bowl of [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato).

Carlotta rises at 8am and leaves immediately for her stall, and Mila rises at 9am and also leaves without eating breakfast. They do not return home during the day. Mila returns at 9pm to eat dinner and then goes to bed and Carlotta will return at 11pm after spending a few hours in [the Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) after closing her stall. She goes straight to bed and they both stay in bed until the morning, when they rise to resume their daily activities.

## Notes
- The [key to Carlotta Valentia's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House) is carried by Carlotta.
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) on the bottom right-hand corner of the building.
- Completing Carlotta's quest [A Few Words with You](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You) makes low value items freely available, and also the use of Carlotta's double bed, but not Mila's.
- All cupboards, dressers, and end tables in the location are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
