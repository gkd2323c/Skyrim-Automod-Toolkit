Leave me alone!"* A **Redguard Woman** may be [encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Alik.27r_Accusation) on the roads being questioned by two [Alik'r Warriors](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Warrior.md). She is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) and can only be encountered once the Alik'r Warriors show up in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) as part of the quest [In My Time Of Need](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:In_My_Time_Of_Need). When the quest is completed, it is no longer possible to meet the woman.

She wears a randomly determined set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (8 variations), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) (4 variations), and has a 50% chance of wearing a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat) (3 variations). She only carries a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
If you eavesdrop on the conversation, it becomes apparent the Alik'r means business:

**Alik'r #1**: *"Listen, wench, we're on to you. We know who you are, so you'd better come with us right now!"*
**Woman**: *"I don't know who you think I am, and I don't care. Leave me alone!"*
**Alik'r #1**: *"I don't have time for this foolishness. Now come along quietly and we won't have to get rough."*
**Alik'r #2**: *"Brother, I think she's telling the truth..."*
**Woman**: *"I'm not going anywhere. Now back off before I do something drastic."*
**Alik'r #2**: *"She doesn't have the scar."*
**Alik'r #1**: *"What? Oh... you're right."*
**Alik'r #2**: *"We've made a mistake. Now move along before that mouth of yours gets you into trouble."*
**Woman**: *"Next time you're looking to pester someone, make sure it's the right person!"*

After the conversation, it is possible to approach the annoyed woman and she will not hesitate to provide her honest opinion on the Alik'r:

*"Foreigners. What a nuisance!"* **What was that all about?** *"They thought I was some other Redguard woman! They just wouldn't leave me alone. I hope they get lost in the wilderness."*
