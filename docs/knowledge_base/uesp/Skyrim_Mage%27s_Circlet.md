[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-item-Mages_Circlet.jpg/200px-SR-item-Mages_Circlet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mages_Circlet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mages_Circlet.jpg) The Mage's Circlet The Mage's Circlet is a circlet given to you by [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md) for alerting him of the need to acquire [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) to stop an impending disaster, in the quest [Good Intentions](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md). Its enchantment grants a [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) effect, which increases your available [Magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) pool.

## Related Quests
- **[Good Intentions](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md)**: Find and consult the [Augur of Dunlain](Skyrim_Augur_of_Dunlain.md).
