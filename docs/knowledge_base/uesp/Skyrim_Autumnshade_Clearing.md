[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-place-Autumnshade_Clearing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Autumnshade_Clearing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Autumnshade_Clearing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Autumnshade_Clearing.jpg) Autumnshade Clearing **Autumnshade Clearing** is a circular glade inhabited by a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) and its attending [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md).

It is located north of [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md) and southwest of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md).

## Autumnshade Clearing
There are up to three leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) supporting the leveled [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) in the clearing. Around the area are several plants and fungi that can be harvested, including several patches of [blue mountain flower](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [purple mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella), [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md), [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota) and [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md). There are also a few skeletal remains around the clearing. A shallow cave at the west end of the clearing holds the bodies of two [hunters](Skyrim_Hunter.md), one of which contains a copy of the [archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Father Of The Niben](Skyrim_Father_Of_The_Niben.md)*.

[Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) guards will sometimes refer to "trouble" at the clearing, speculate that it's due to spriggans, and add the location to your map.

## Notes
- The [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[Racial Phylogeny](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Racial_Phylogeny)* can be found to the north of the clearing in a small [unmarked location](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Unmarked_Ruins_and_Monuments).
- The [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill book *[The Gold Ribbon of Merit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gold_Ribbon_of_Merit)* can be found in a hunter's tent southeast of the clearing.
- There are several [iron ore veins](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) around the camp to the north, east-northeast, east-southeast, south, and west-northwest.
- A total of 18 [canis roots](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md) can be found in the area.
- Autumnshade Clearing also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Autumnshade_Clearing) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
