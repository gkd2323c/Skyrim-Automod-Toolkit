# Axer Rock

| --- | --- |
| Type | Port |
| Region | [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) |

Axer Rock is a port located in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), relatively close to [High Elven waters](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) (which is also known to be the speediest way to the Rock, in comparison to other shipping routes). Trade goods on Axer Rock are often shipped to and from mainland [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), with it trading often with the [kingdom](Lore_Hero.md#Dynasty_of_Odar_the_Brave) founded by [Odar the Brave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Odar_the_Brave). Some mainlanders consider selling goods to Axer Rock to be "too risky"—at times, Axer Rock shipments are prone to being stolen by pirates.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESCAR-1)</sup>

The port itself is home to a local population of craftsman, including but not limited to: shippers, artisans, bakers, and antique booksellers. A particular baker from the port became famous throughout the [Empire](Lore_Empire_of_Tamriel.md), and its artists are described as renowned.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESCAR-1)</sup>
