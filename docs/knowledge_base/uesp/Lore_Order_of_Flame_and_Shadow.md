# Order of Flame and Shadow

The Order of Flame and Shadow was a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) active in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md) at some point before the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). It was a mysterious Knightly Order, and as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) it was disbanded. According to some, it was nothing more than a legend. Tomes and histories indicate this order was comprised of a sole knight, known as the **Knight of Flame and Shadow**, who passes the mantle to a protege after an extensive and secretive training regiment. The Knight of Flame and Shadow had been sighted on a few occasions over the centuries, both working alongside the nobility and also actively entreating an uprising among the common folk. Little is known about the identity, motivations, and whereabouts of this line of knights. As of 2E 582, the Knight of Flame and Shadow hasn't been heard from for a long period of time. It was speculated that the last holder of the title perished before they were able to pass on the legacy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup>
