[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-item-Mask_of_Vivec.jpg/200px-SR-item-Mask_of_Vivec.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mask_of_Vivec.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mask_of_Vivec.jpg) Mask of Vivec The Mask of Vivec is a unique heavy helmet.

It once belonged to [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), one of the [three great heroes](Lore_Tribunal.md) of Morrowind who saved the Dunmer people time and time again. Vivec, [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) were revered as gods for their deeds and came to be known as the Tribunal. At some point around [3E 427](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind) or [3E 433](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion), Vivec himself disappeared under mysterious circumstances, with conflicting reports of what happened. After the disaster of the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), the [official worship of the Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) collapsed and the [New Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md#The_New_Temple) rose, with Vivec and his fellow Tribunes [reduced to](Lore_Reclamations.md) saints. However, a few devotees survived persecution and came to [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) in search of a new home. As it happens, the Mask of Vivec would also reappear on Solstheim during [4E 201](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

[Matriarch Drevlan](Skyrim_Matriarch_Drevlan.md), if allied with, will give you the quest to rescue the Mask from a corrupt Redoran Guard and the equally corrupt buyer he is selling the priceless holy relic to. Later, it and the other two masks of the tribunal serve as keys to a [puzzle](Skyrim_Ashen_Heart.md), and may be kept afterwards.

## Notes
According to history, the mask was originally formed out of cursed volcanic ash when [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) sent his [Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) to infect Vivec and his followers, entombing them all in casts of ash. However, Vivec survived and wept bitterly for his people, with his tears of grief weakening his ash prison until he could break out and heal the others. The Mask is the last remaining piece of this ash cast. In the modern day, it is tempered with an Ebony Ingot which would seem to have little to do with its origins. However, ebony smithing is also linked with bitter tears in the legend of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s mighty battleaxe [Wuuthrad](Lore_Wuuthrad.md), which was allegedly forged by Ysgramor's son [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md) after Ysgramor wept for the loss of his own people following the [Night of Tears](Lore_Night_of_Tears.md).
