[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Thorek.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thorek.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorek.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorek.jpg) Thorek **Thorek** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) worshipper of [Vaermina](Skyrim_Vaermina.md) living in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md). When Nightcaller Temple was attacked by an Orc war party intending to destroy the [Skull of Corruption](Skyrim_Skull_of_Corruption.md), the worshippers released the Miasma, a gas that put everyone to sleep. Years later, when the Miasma is finally lifted, he and [Veren Duleri](Skyrim_Veren_Duleri.md) will attempt to defend the Skull of Corruption from [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md), whom they consider a traitor for fleeing the Temple.

He wears a set of [Vaermina robes](Skyrim_Vaermina_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000bacd7). He wields a leveled sword that may be enchanted depending on your level.

Thorek knows the following spells: [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Events

### Waking Nightmare
You first encounter Thorek in the [Dreamstride](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md), conversing with Veren Duleri, another Vaermina devotee:

**Thorek**: *"The orcs have breached the inner sanctum, Brother Veren."*
**Veren**: *"We must hold. We can't allow the Skull to fall into their hands."*
**Thorek**: *"But... no more than a handful of us remain, brother."*
**Veren**: *"Then we have no choice. The Miasma must be released."*
**Thorek**: *"The Miasma? But, brother..."*
**Veren**: *"We have no alternative. It's the will of Vaermina."*

Veren will instruct you to "activate the barrier and release the Miasma", then continue the conversation:

**Veren**: *"Brother Thorek, we must remain here and guard this Skull with our lives if necessary."*
**Thorek**: *"Agreed. To the death."*
**Veren**: *"Then let it be done. Farewell, my brothers!"*

You will encounter Thorek and Veren again as you and Erandur reach the Skull of Corruption.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg) Erandur meets his old friends **Erandur**: *"Wait... Veren... Thorek... you're alive!"*
**Veren**: *"No thanks to you, Casimir."*
**Erandur**: *"I no longer use that name. I'm Erandur, Priest of Mara."*
**Veren**: *"You're a traitor. You left us to die and then ran before the Miasma took you."*
**Erandur**: *"No. I... I was scared. I wasn't ready to sleep."*
**Veren**: *"Enough of your lies! I can't allow you to destroy the Skull, Priest of Mara."*
**Erandur**: *"Then you leave me no choice!"*

You and Erandur will have to fight the two Vaermina devotees. Upon death, Thorek will say:

- *"Vaermina forgive me..."*

- Thorek shares his name with [another Nord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Thorek) in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind).
- There is another version of Thorek with the Base ID `00024278` and Ref ID `000242a8`. He is used in the scene where you enter the Dreamstride. He wears the same outfit but will always have a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).
