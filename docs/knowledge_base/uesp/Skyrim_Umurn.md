[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-npc-Umurn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Umurn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Umurn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Umurn.jpg) Umurn at the workbench **Umurn** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_GuardOrc1H.md) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He works the forge along with his mother, [Gharol](Skyrim_Gharol.md).

Umurn is just one of many sons of [Chief Burguk](Skyrim_Chief_Burguk.md) and with the lesser-ranked forge-wife as his mother, Umurn has no choice but to work around the clock. He helps his mother at the forge every day between 8am and 8pm and then dives into [Dushnikh Mine](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Mine.md) where he spends the entire night either mining, wandering, or eating.

He carries a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md) and a [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) (up to Orcish quality at level six) with a number of leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md). Even if he never gets to sleep there, he also carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md), along with a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When you approach him, he will tell you a bit about his life in Dushnikh Yal:

- *"I work the forge, with my mother Gharol. We mine our own ore and shape our own metal."*
- *"One day I will shape a weapon fit for a chief. Maybe that chief will be me."*
- *"I help in the mines as well as the forge."*
- *"One day, I must fight my brother [Nagrub](Skyrim_Nagrub.md) for chiefdom. It will not be easy."*

### Conversations
When working the forge, he will have random conversations with his mother:

**Gharol**: *"Imagine the heads of your enemies as you forge. Let your hatred work the steel."*
**Umurn**: *"Yes, mother."*

**Umurn**: *"The metal has been stubborn today. It refuses to bend."*
**Gharol**: *"Save your anger for the forge. The steel cannot hear your whining."*
**Umurn**: *"Sorry, mother. I'll focus on my work."*

**Gharol**: *"Ah, is there any weapon finer than an Orc blade?"*
**Umurn**: *"No, mother. It's no wonder our enemies fear the sight of an Orc warrior."*
