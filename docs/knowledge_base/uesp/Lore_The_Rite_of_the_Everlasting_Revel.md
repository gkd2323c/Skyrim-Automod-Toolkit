The Rite of the Everlasting Revel **by [Reveler Adept Tasegil](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Reveler_Adept_Tasegil)** *A description of Sanguine's rite of the Everlasting Revel*
---
For the faithful of [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md), there's no truer way to worship the Prince of Pleasure than by partaking in one of his rambunctious revels. Carnal delights of every description, including feast and flesh, dance and drink, fill these marvelous events.

But even the most raucous revels must eventually come to an end. The revelers grow weary, the indulgences monotonous. Somehow, enjoying oneself can become a chore. For the Prince of Revels, no shame could be greater.

And so, he created the rite of the Everlasting Revel—a mystic festival that appears wherever the Prince so desires. It's a revel that never grows old, constantly refreshed in new and exotic locales, with an endless parade of participants. Sanguine's faithful join the Prince there, eternally indulging their innermost desires for his sake. For those who have never visited the [Realms of Revelry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Realms_of_Revelry) for themselves, it's as close as one can get to that magical place on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).

To attain entry to the rite of the Everlasting Revel, one must simply walk through the festival gate wherever it appears to them. Finding it is easy—just listen for the sound of riotous laughter, the flapping of silk banners, and drunken choruses of loud and bawdy song. Once inside, pay homage to your generous host by indulging in the numerous libations, both magical and mundane. Satisfaction comes in many forms, and Sanguine's most faithful are sometimes gifted with a new shape entirely, spun out of their deepest desires made reality. After all, is it not easier to loosen one's inhibitions when presented with a new face or body?

Sanguine's indulgent attitude toward mortals can be seen in the mundane carnival trappings his revel mimics. Many of the Prince's more hesitant followers find it easier to let themselves be swept away if they feel like children at a carnival, with enticing games and new diversions around every corner. For his part, the Prince seems to enjoy devising new and challenging attractions for his followers, even if he never expects them to win.

Even the most ardent reveler will rarely see the Prince directly. It's said that Sanguine prefers to adopt a new face or body, of any race, gender, or form, to truly experience the rite among his followers. Any alluring face peeking out from behind a tent or strolling through the revel could be the Prince. One never knows if Sanguine is the one laughing next to them at some new carnival trick, and that is his gift to his revelers—myriad delights, and the freedom to enjoy them all.
