# Knights of the Wheel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/DF-map-Abibon-Gora.png/200px-DF-map-Abibon-Gora.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Abibon-Gora.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Abibon-Gora.png) Partial map of the region of Abibon-Gora The Knights of the Wheel were the Knightly Order based in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). They were active during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and were known to protect and represent the royal family and the city of [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md) in all military situations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-D2-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>:100</sup>
