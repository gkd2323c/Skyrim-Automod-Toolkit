*When I was a little girl, you were someone I looked up to and admired. As my older cousin, you taught me so much and inspired me to not only seek perfection, but to do so in the most creative and enlightened manner as possible. When I returned from my travels, you were one of the first of Summerset's nobles to support me and accept my ascension to the throne. You are regal, beautiful, and cultured in ways I can never match.*
—Ayrenn<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup> **Proxy Queen** Alwinarwe was a member of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md)'s royal family who lived during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md)'s older cousin, and governed [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) in her place while Ayrenn was handling the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)'s business in mainland Tamriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoad-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RI-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/ON-render-Proxy_Queen_Alwinarwe.jpg/200px-ON-render-Proxy_Queen_Alwinarwe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Proxy_Queen_Alwinarwe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Proxy_Queen_Alwinarwe.jpg) Proxy Queen in the Alinor Palace Little is known about Alwinarwe's past. She was a niece of the Gardener,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PQA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TG-5)</sup> and a cousin of Ayrenn. She and Ayrenn had a strong relationship in their youth, with Ayrenn admiring Alwinarwe during that time. When Ayrenn returned to the Summerset Isles in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580), Alwinarwe was among the first of Summerset's nobles to support her and accept her ascension to the throne.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup>

After the Aldmeri Dominion's formation in 2E 580, Queen Ayrenn was often obligated to leave Summerset Isle to handle affairs. The [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) government needed someone at the helm while their Queen was away, so Alwinarwe was appointed as Ayrenn's proxy and charged with governing Summerset in her stead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoad-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup> She oversaw the influx of newcomers after Ayrenn issued a decree that opened Summerset's borders to foreigners.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PQA-4)</sup> Although Alwinarwe personally objected to her cousin's decree, she respectfully enacted Ayrenn's will.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup> This sparked opposition from Summerset's nobility,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARo OB-6)</sup> although this sentiment was not shared by all.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ACf OB-7)</sup> Despite their difference of opinion, Alwinarwe was considered to be among the most trusted members of Ayrenn's family.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCPQA-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-QA-8)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Alwinarwe was the target of a plot by High Magistrate [Rinwaray](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rinwaray) of The [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md), who intended to use [a device](Lore_Seht%27s_Affect_Inducer.md) invented by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) to compel the Proxy Queen to act against Ayrenn's decree and close Summerset's borders.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> That same year, the Proxy Queen met with an ambassador from the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) in an attempt to broker peace between the Pact and the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CC-10)</sup>

Alwinarwe was warned by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) about the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) threat that loomed over the [College of Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) and issued a document that allowed them to investigate and later save the College from the Daedra's attack.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TTS-11)</sup> Partially due to her decision, the [Crystal-Like-Law](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) was later given additional protections.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ANA-12)</sup> She also granted an agent of [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md) access to the [College of Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) during [The Triad](Lore_The_Triad.md)'s invasion of Summerset.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TTS-11)</sup>

During the same year, she became the target of an assassination plot orchestrated by the [Ceythalmor](Lore_Ceythalmor.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Wisteria-13)</sup> The Ceythalmor believed she embodied everything wrong with the Aldmeri Dominion, an ineffectual puppet ruler unwilling to use her power to end the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md). The Ceythalmor planned to replace her with another cousin of Ayrenn who was carefully selected from the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md)'s inner circle and ready to stake his claim.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TSOL-14)</sup> However, the assassination attempt was foiled by the intervention of the Garland Ring,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-OCAF-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Tanlorin-16)</sup> a faction led by her uncle, the Gardener. Following this incident, the Garland Ring was incorporated into the Proxy Queen's court.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PQA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TG-5)</sup>
