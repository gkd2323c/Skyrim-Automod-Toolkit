[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg/200px-SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg) A Steel Dagger Valdr's Lucky Dagger is a unique [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) given by the eponymous [Valdr](Skyrim_Valdr.md) as a reward for helping him clear out [Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern.md), as part of its [related quest](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md). Its enchantment increases the chance of a [critical hit](Skyrim_Damage.md#Critical_Strikes) by 25%. As the weapon's enchantment is not one in the traditional sense, it will last indefinitely. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon requires a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md), and the benefits are doubled with the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md)**: Help an [injured hunter](Skyrim_Valdr.md) avenge his fallen companions.

## Notes
- Besides the critical chance bonus, this dagger has a critical damage rating of 5, whereas normal steel daggers only have a rating of 2.
- Valdr's Lucky Dagger's enchantment cannot be disenchanted as it isn't a traditional weapon enchantment, because of this, [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md)'s effect applies to this weapon.
