# Old Orcish

**Old Orcish** is the ancient language of the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), and most likely the precursor to the language known as [Orcish](Lore_Orcish.md). It is unknown when the langage became distinct from the other [Elven languages](Lore_Ehlnofex.md), but the first written record of the language dates back to the rule of King [Torug gro-Igron](Lore_Torug_gro-Igron.md), before the first siege of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) in [1E 950](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_950); nor is there any evidence to how it evolved into modern Orcish, which in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417) was well established as the language of the Orcs of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> Seeing that there is evidence for the Old Orcish language having been largely forgotten in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONKharsthun-2)</sup> it is questionable if the modern language is directly descended from Old Orcish, also due to the scarcity of information about both the ancient and the modern language.

## Old Orcish Grammar
**Articles**

- Neither definite nor indefinite articles are used.

*Torug dulg krazak eb Jur ugo sim ren tum beshkar* / *Torug climbed the summit and placed himself in a cairn of his own making.*

**Plurals**

- Nouns in the plural do not seem to inflect.

*voshu Ornim...rohi Ornim* / *worthy Orc...lesser Orcs*

**Pronouns**

- 3rd person personal pronouns are often omitted.

*Goltragga Torug ne rohi Ornim lochan norgim krazak* / *Chief Torug will allow no lesser Orcs to find interment above [him].*

## Comparing Old Orcish to Other Languages
- *Magicka - magic, enchantment* - *cf.* Aldmeris, Ayleidoon & [Tamrielic](Lore_Imperial.md) *Magicka*
- *Ne - not allow* - *cf.* Aldmeris *ne- (not)*
- *Ornim - Orc* - *cf.* Aldmeris *Orsimer*, & [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md) *Ogiim*

## Literature
Like many other ancient languages, Old Orcish lacks a linguistic corpus, its literature being preserved, in a vast enough quantity for linguistic analysis, in but one known sample of poetry: "[Torug ag Krazak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Torug_ag_Krazak)" (translated into [Cyrodilic](Lore_Imperial.md) as "[Torug at the Summit](Lore_Torug_at_the_Summit,_Complete_Translation.md)"), a series of inscriptions at the tomb of [Torug gro-Igron](Lore_Torug_gro-Igron.md), the founder of the first Orsinium. Apart from this, little remains of this language but scattered words and phrases.

| Old Orcish | Translation |
| --- | --- |
| Torug ag Krazak, Uhz <br> Goltragga Torug ne murimush lochan sim <br> Goltragga Torug ne rohi Ornim lochan norgim krazak | Torug at the Summit, One <br> Chief Torug will not allow the looters to find his remains. <br> Chief Torug will allow no lesser Orcs to find interment above him. |
| Torug ag Krazak, Uhg <br> Goltragga Torug dek vorkhim lorak eb norgimin sim <br> Torug dulg krazak eb Jur ugo sim ren tum beshkar | Torug at the Summit, Two <br> Chief Torug gathered up his armored bracer and his dying body. <br> He climbed to the summit and placed himself in a cairn of his own making. |
| Torug ag Krazak, Aht <br> Torug gesh Glushun Zugka* ugo magicka lorish <br> Uba eb uba urgalick voshu Ornim tarask Torug golzarga ubeshka | Torug at the Summit, Three <br> Then he laid Sorrow's Kiss* upon his enchanted armlet. <br> Forever waiting for a worthy Orc to claim his most cherished possession. |

- * There was debate by the translator as to whether the faded letters in the original inscription contained the word "*zugka*", meaning "kiss", or "*zugra*", meaning "curse".
