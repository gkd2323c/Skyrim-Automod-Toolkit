# Unused Creatures

This page lists all unique **unused creatures** in Skyrim and its add-ons. None of these creatures are present in the game; only those in [test cells](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Test_Cells) can be encountered without spawning them via console codes. Their descriptions on this page treat them as they would be if they were implemented in the game. Some of the locations, [quests](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md), and buildings mentioned in their descriptions do not exist either, yet are treated on this page as if they do. For information on unused content in general, please see the [related page](Skyrim_Unused_Content.md).

### Centaur
Centaurs were planned to be in the game early on, and had concept art created for them. Due to difficulties with modeling and time constraints to meet the release date, they were ultimately scrapped.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-creature-Karinda.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Karinda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Karinda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Karinda.jpg) Karinda **Karinda** is [Dagny](Skyrim_Dagny.md)'s pet [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md). Karinda is the same color as the [Whiterun Stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables) horse you can buy, but has no saddle. Much like [Frost](Skyrim_Frost.md), it will stay in position if you mount another horse.

Karinda was intended to be killed by Dagny's brother [Frothar](Skyrim_Frothar.md) during [The Whispering Door](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Whispering_Door), as a result of falling under the influence of [Mephala](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whispering_Lady). However, all portions of the quest involving [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md)'s children turning violent were removed from the final release of the game.

Karinda is flagged as invulnerable, and will have infinite stamina and be impervious to damage if spawned.
