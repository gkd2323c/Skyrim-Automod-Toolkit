This **Thief** is a hostile enemy found lurking around the supposed grave of the [Gray Fox](Skyrim_Stranger.md) in the [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) graveyard. You will encounter him during the related quest, and he will attack you on sight to prevent you from recovering the [Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](Skyrim_Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.md).

He carries the *[Thief's Note](Skyrim_Thief%27s_Note.md)*, which you will need to read as part of the related quest.

## Related Quests
- **[The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](Skyrim_The_Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.md)</sup>: Investigate the legend of the Gray Fox.

## Notes
- Although he is apparently a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), you will not be [expelled](Skyrim_Reparations.md) from the guild for defending yourself against him.
