## Notes
- Fang of Haynekhtnamet has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Fang_of_Haynekhtnamet)* and *ESO*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Fang_of_Haynekhtnamet.md).
- The Fang of Haynekhtnamet has the same base damage as a [Madness Dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Madness_Dagger), but is lighter, making it arguably the strongest dagger in the game.
