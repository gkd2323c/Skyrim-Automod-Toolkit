*All power flows from the gods.*
—Methal Seran<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TC-1)</sup> Methal Seran was a powerful [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md), and esteemed scholar, priest,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ald.27ruhn-3)</sup> and a Diviner within the ranks of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md). In addition to his mastery of Conjuration, he was also an accomplished mentor in the arts of [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md) and [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md), and a skilled practitioner of [Restoration](Lore_Restoration.md) and [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MS-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png/200px-LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png) Methal Seran conjuring a Flame Atronach Little is known of Methal Seran's past. He hailed from the ancient [Seran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Seran) family, one of the thirty revered families of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-30RF-5)</sup> with historical ties to [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SAT-6)</sup> At some point he joined the Tribunal Temple and climbed its ranks.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MS-4)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), he was already considered an elderly and eminent priest and scholar,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ald.27ruhn-3)</sup> holding the rank of Diviner within the Temple.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MS-4)</sup> As of then, in addition to his mastery of Conjuration,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TMP-7)</sup><sup>:235</sup> he was also a powerful practitioner of Mysticism and Alteration, disciplines in which he was known to instruct others, as well as Restoration and Illusion. He was one of the resident priests at the Ald'ruhn Temple, where he served the people of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) by providing a variety of healing concoctions and offering his magical knowledge to devoted members of the Temple.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MS-4)</sup>

He was a relative of Olyn Seran, another grand conjurer who fled Morrowind and, by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), lived in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) wilderness.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-OS-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AF-9)</sup>
