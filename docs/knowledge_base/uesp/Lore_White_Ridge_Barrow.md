[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) White Ridge Barrow is an ancient Nordic barrow found in the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md) of northwestern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). This ancient tomb is notable for containing a [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md), the Sallow Regent [Black Book](Lore_Black_Books.md) and an Imbuing Chamber used to create [spider scrolls](Lore_Spider_Crafting.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BBSR-2)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a scribe lived near White Ridge Barrow. He was known for his intense fascination with spiders, bordering on obsession. During this time, Lady Clarisse Laurent sent him scrolls containing research on the Spider Scrolls retrieved in Bal Fel from Griskild.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LCL-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AWOT-4)</sup>
