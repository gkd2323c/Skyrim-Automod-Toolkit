# Coprolite

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/ON-creature-Vulk%27esh.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Vulk%27esh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Vulk%27esh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Vulk%27esh.jpg) A vulk'esh Coprolite is a type of fossilized excrement or dung, often found in the form of solid, mineralized feces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IC-1)</sup>

[Vulk'esh](Lore_Vulk%27esh.md) excrement has a tendency to harden into dense, heavy, igneous chunks that bear a resemblance to slag. These chunks have potential applications.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IC-1)</sup>
