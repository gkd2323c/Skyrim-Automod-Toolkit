[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-creature-Otar_the_Mad.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Otar_the_Mad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Otar_the_Mad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Otar_the_Mad.jpg) Otar the Mad **Otar the Mad** is one of the eight high-ranked [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) in Skyrim. He is found in [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md), in a locked coffin before the barrow's [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md). To get to him, you have to defeat his two keepers— [Torsten](Skyrim_Guardian_Torsten.md) and [Saerek](Skyrim_Guardian_Saerek.md) —and take their skull keys.

He carries his eponymous mask, [Otar](Skyrim_Otar.md), which increases resistance to shock, fire, and frost damage. His weapon is the [Staff of the Storm Wall](Skyrim_Staff_of_the_Storm_Wall.md).

Otar knows the following spells: [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md), [Command Daedra](Skyrim_Command_Daedra.md), [Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md), [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), [Lightning Cloak](Skyrim_Lightning_Cloak.md), and [Thunderbolt](Skyrim_Thunderbolt.md).
