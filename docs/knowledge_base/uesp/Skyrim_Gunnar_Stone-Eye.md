He does not seemingly make it out of Helgen alive.

He wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and wears [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md) and [gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md).

## Dialogue
During the attack on Helgen, after your jump from the tower to the destroyed inn, [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) will shout at [Haming](Skyrim_Haming.md), making sure he gets to safety. He will then ask Gunnar to keep an eye on him:

- **Hadvar**: *"Gunnar, take care of the boy. I have to find [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) and join the defense."*
- **Gunnar**: *"Gods guide you, Hadvar."*

This is the last you will see of Gunnar, but since Haming is one of the few survivors, he obviously kept his word to Hadvar.
