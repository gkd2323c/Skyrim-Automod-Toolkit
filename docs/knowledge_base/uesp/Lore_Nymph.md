[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-sound-Nymph.ogg) The laughter of a Nymph Nymphs (also called **Woodland Nymphs**) are a mysterious [race](Lore_Race.md) of [magical](Lore_Magic.md) spirits that resemble traditionally beautiful, naked women.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFNymph-2)</sup> They are known to live in the forests and woodlands of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region and the island of [Gorne](Lore_Gorne.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PSI-3)</sup> often residing in lakes.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Histpry DF-4)</sup> They are said to be very intelligent and have been known to refer to others as "mortals", suggesting that they possess a greater lifespan.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NG-5)</sup> Nymphs have their own melodious [tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Nymph_(skill)), which sounds similar to [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleids.md) and [faerie](Lore_Faerie.md) but shares none of the vocabulary.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NG-5)</sup> They are known to be aggressive towards those that cannot speak it. Nymphs have some inherent magical abilities, and are capable of draining their opponents' health and stamina. Nymphs can only be harmed by [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) of [elven](Lore_Moonstone.md) craft or better.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFNymph-2)</sup>

There are many [promiscuous](Lore_Sex.md) and downright pornographic tales about Nymphs, but in actuality, they are often quite shy, and hard to earn the trust of.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NG-5)</sup> Though there are accounts that show they can be mischievous in nature, distracting mortals with their bodies, and they are fans of drinking [rum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rum).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-The Menagerie-6)</sup> Nymphs allegedly were incredibly knowledgeable about the behavior and varieties of deep woodland creatures, supposedly knowing of hidden types of [flowers](Lore_Flora.md), [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md), and other creatures too elusive to have ever been seen by man.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NG-5)</sup>

Studying the nature of Nymphs was historically seen as frivolous among the scholar community, due to the common perceived simplicity of the race. In those circles, Nymphs were typically believed to be promiscuous and not much else. Curiously, [Vondham Barres](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vondham_Barres), one of the few scholars who tried to study the character and culture of Nymphs for reasons other than lust, disappeared from the civilized world not long after he made peaceful contact with one.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NG-5)</sup>

## Uses and Etymology
The [hair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nymph_hair) of a Nymph can be used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Some taverns have Nymphs as their namesake.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-8)</sup> [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md) are sometimes known as "Water Nymphs",<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KCAHC-9)</sup> or otherwise compared to Nymphs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGV-1)</sup> They are similarly an all-female race of spirits known to have sexual encounters with other races.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-NSMH-10)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TND-11)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GRAX-12)</sup> However, they are claimed to be unrelated to the Nymphs of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGV-1)</sup> Nymphs are not to be confused with the young of certain animal species such as [Death Hoppers](Lore_Death_Hopper.md), [Elytra](Lore_Elytra.md), and [Nix-Oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md), which are also known as Nymphs.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-13)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-14)</sup>
