[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/AR-place-Chasetown.jpg/200px-AR-place-Chasetown.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Chasetown.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Chasetown.jpg) Chasetown during the Imperial Simulacrum Chasetown (historically called **Chastown**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-HRP-2)</sup> is a village found in western [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), on the mountains near the city-state of [Sunkeep](Lore_Sunkeep.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup>

The village of Chastown has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Chasetown was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Fhalan and had a rivalry with [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn.md) and [Kelps Yat](Lore_Kelps_Yat.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Chasetown is neighbored by several settlements, including [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) to the north and [Sunkeep](Lore_Sunkeep.md) to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup>
