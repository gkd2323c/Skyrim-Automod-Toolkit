[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-npc-Gianna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gianna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gianna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gianna.jpg) Gianna preparing a dish **Gianna** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) cook in the [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) Emperor's Tower. She is the castle's cook, and is assigned to assist the [Gourmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Balagog_gro-Nolob) when the Emperor visits [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). She is eager to take the unique opportunity to not only work with the Gourmet, but to serve the Emperor as well. After the quest, she disappears from the game.

Gianna wears a [chef's tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Tunic) along with a [chef's hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#00018801) and defends herself with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She also carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Related Quests
- **[To Kill an Empire](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md)**: Attempt to assassinate the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) while disguised as the [Gourmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourmet).

## Notes
- You can [pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) Gianna if you have unlocked the *Misdirection* perk and *Perfect Touch* perk, but only after she delivers the soup to the Emperor. The iron dagger is the only thing you can pickpocket from her until the Emperor is dead; then, the chef's hat and chef's tunic are available after his death.
- If Gianna survives the attack, she will continue to repeat "Hold it together, Gianna, hold it together."
- Gianna also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Gianna) in *[Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Castles)*.

## Bugs
- During the quest, Gianna may not talk to you if you are inflicted with certain diseases. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
