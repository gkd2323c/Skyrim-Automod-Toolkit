They cannot be obtained from already-dead skeevers found roasting over fires—those skeevers yield [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) instead.

## Ingredients
69 guaranteed samples can be found in 35 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 8 in [Fort Dunstad Commander's Quarters](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad_Commander%27s_Quarters.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 4 in [Honningbrew Basement](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Basement.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 4 in [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 4 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 4 in [Volskygge Passages](Skyrim_Volskygge_Passages.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 3 in [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 3 in [Bleak Falls Temple](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md#Bleak_Falls_Temple) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 3 in [Tolvald's Cave](Skyrim_Tolvald%27s_Cave.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))

In addition, there are 20 spider web sacs in 9 different locations that always contain skeever tails. Locations with multiple web sacs are:

- 4 in [Honningbrew Basement](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Basement.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 3 in [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 3 in [Bleak Falls Temple](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md#Bleak_Falls_Temple) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 3 in [Volskygge Passages](Skyrim_Volskygge_Passages.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 2 in [Skybound Watch Pass](Skyrim_Skybound_Watch_Pass.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Skeever tails are dropped by all varieties of [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md), including [venomfang skeevers](Skyrim_Venomfang_Skeever.md) and the [Skeever Guardian Spirit](Skyrim_Skeever_Guardian_Spirit.md).
