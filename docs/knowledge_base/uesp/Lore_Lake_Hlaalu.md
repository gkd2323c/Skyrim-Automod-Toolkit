[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/ON-place-Redolent_Loam_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Redolent_Loam_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Redolent_Loam_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Redolent_Loam_02.jpg) Lake Hlaalu Lake Hlaalu is a lake in southwestern [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), in the western part of the [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) region. The city of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md), capital of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md), is situated on its southern banks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup> Lake Hlaalu is used by Narsis as its water source.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RDDTW-2)</sup> It is named for the [Great House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md). Lake fish typically found in Deshaan can be found in Lake Hlaalu, such as [Gourami](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gourami), the [Ide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ide), and the rare [Mud Lamprey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mud_Lamprey) and [Old Man Gar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Old_Man_Gar).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFishing-3)</sup>

On the lake's eastern shore is an [old ruin](Lore_Elven_Ruins.md) called the Lake Hlaalu Retreat. It contains a magically-embued crafting site, one of many all over Tamriel. As theorized by the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) adventurer, Pacrooti, they are created by master craftworkers, who at the height of their artistry, manifest their abilities and infuse them into their stations, so that any item crafted there would have the same quality as their original works.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MAMAPAYQ-4)</sup>
