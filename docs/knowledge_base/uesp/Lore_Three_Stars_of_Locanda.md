# Three Stars of Locanda

The **three stars of Locanda** are a group of three [stars](Lore_Stars.md). In the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, the three stars of Locanda glowing in the night sky stood for unholy alliances.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVR-1)</sup> It is unknown whether they were part of any of the major [Constellations](Lore_Constellations.md) or not.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-perktree-Vampirism.jpg/200px-SR-perktree-Vampirism.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-perktree-Vampirism.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-perktree-Vampirism.jpg) Vampire constellation - At the time of *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* (when this lore was created), the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedra.md) are often associated with stars, with "[Daedric stars](Lore_Daedra_Stars.md)" shining bright on certain nights, signaling dire portents.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
- The quest mentioning the three stars of Locanda is associated with a Vampiric Relic, and the stars are said to appear as a consequence of this quest occuring.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVR-1)</sup> Of note, a [vampiric](Lore_Vampire.md) constellation later appeared in *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* as the [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md) perk tree.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
