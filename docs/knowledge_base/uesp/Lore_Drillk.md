Drillk was a [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) madman who resided in the village of [Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(place).md). He believed himself to be the last living member of his race<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDril-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLod-2)</sup> and was able to survive the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) that otherwise wiped out most of the Kothringi by absorbing an Ayleid Keystone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDril-1)</sup> Seclusion for over 20 years<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DJ-3)</sup> seemingly warped his mind and he was deluded into thinking he was royalty to the point of using the [royal *we*](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Royal_we) and believing that his fellow villagers were still alive.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDril-1)</sup> In the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was informed of a plot by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) to retrieve the keystone and use it for malicious intent.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLod-2)</sup> The Vestige with the help of the deranged Drillk freed the spirit of Drillk's deceased Kothringi friends, and also accessed the Temple of Sul in Zuuk where he imprisoned an Ayleid, Morilye. The imprisoned Ayleid spirit admits that Drillk absorbed the keystone before turning hostile and being put down by the Vestige.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMor-4)</sup> When Drillk finally comes to realize that he indeed absorbed the Keystone (and that he is only alive because of it) he requests that the Vestige perform a ritual to pass the keystone onto them, thus ending his life and allowing him to pass on with the rest of his people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDril-1)</sup> It is unknown if the Vestige chose to absorb the keystone or spare his life.
