[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Vulkwasten (also called **Vulkwasten Wood**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a [graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md) settlement located on the seaside hills of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md), in the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The town is well known for its fermented [alcoholic beverages](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THOWE-2)</sup> which is made by a family of brewers for generations.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONElruin-3)</sup>

## History
Sometime in [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578), the [Wood Orcish](Lore_Wood_Orcs.md) chieftain, Orhg was publicly executed in Vulkwasten, and his hanging was depicted on silver-plated embroider plates.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONContraband-4)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the mage, Firuin tried to sabotage the [rotmeth](Lore_Rotmeth.md) production by aggravating the [timber mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) and breaking into the brewery. An [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) helped rally the mammoths and defeated the mage. The brew, however, was stolen and taken to Balding Hill, a landmark northeast of town and west of [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md). The adventurer was able to save the rotmeth.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONRotten-5)</sup> At around the same time, the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) assembled the Council of Province Generals in their guildhall in Vulkwasten to discuss the death of the previous guildmaster, Jofnir Iceblade.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONDeed-6)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Vulkwasten Wood was an active settlement. It was ruled by Duke Argorn and had a rivalry with Longvale.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Vulkwasten Wood was neighbored by several settlements, including Ebon Ro to the northeast, [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) to the west, and [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md) to the south.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ARMap-7)</sup>
