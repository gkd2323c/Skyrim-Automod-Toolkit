**Conjure Wrathman** is an expert-level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a [Wrathman](Skyrim_Wrathman.md) to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure Wrathman](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy), greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health by 100.

## Notes
- The Wrathman is undead, and thus subject to spells that affect the undead.
- The Wrathman has the highest health of any summoned creature when not counting Creation Club summons.
