[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/OB-place-Blackwood.jpg/200px-OB-place-Blackwood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Blackwood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Blackwood.jpg) Blackwood circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) Blackwood circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Blackwood is the swampland east of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) along the Trans-Niben, adjacent to the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) border. The waters from this swamp drain south into [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). Blackwood also shares many common traits with the Imperial Province of Black Marsh, such as climate, geography, and wildlife, due to it being directly adjacent to the province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA-1)</sup> The region's name comes from the Blackwood Forest, which is generally dark and woodsy,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA-1)</sup> but it also references the thoghatt, the charcoal warriors of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) tradition.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBJa.27Fazir-2)</sup>

Located along the [Green Road](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Green_Road) is the headquarters of the [Knights of the White Stallion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Knights_of_the_White_Stallion), a knightly chivalric order chartered by the Count of Leyawiin. The Imperial town of Slough Point is found in the region, close to the Black Marsh border.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA-1)</sup> The land to the west of the Lower Niben was ceded to Cyrodiil by Elsweyr in an agreement between the Count of Leyawiin and the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) just prior to [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), following a long-running dispute over the border. It is not uncommon for some to get lost in the trackless marshes east of [Fort Blueblood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Blueblood), on the nebulous border between Blackwood and Argonia.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Due to their similar climate it is sometimes believed Blackwood is a natural part of Black Marsh, and the noble Nibenese of Blackwood have fought back against the spread of Black Marsh's influence for generations.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Interview2-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-5)</sup>

When the [Reman Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) dissolved in [2E 431](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_431), a gradual depopulation of war-torn central Cyrodiil occurred as some fled to Blackwood and other areas of the province.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-5)</sup>

## Notable Places
- [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) — The Imperial capital of County Leyawiin and the largest settlement in Blackwood.
- [Gideon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gideon) — An ancient city in Black Marsh.
- [Rockgrove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rockgrove) — An Argonian settlement in Black Marsh, known for its ancient [xanmeer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xanmeer).
- [Fort Blueblood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Blueblood) — A ruined military fort located southeast of Leyawiin
- [Water's Edge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Water%27s_Edge) — A settlement situated on the northwestern shore of the Lower Niben.
- Blankenmarch — A village northeast of Leyawiin, along the [Yellow Road](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yellow_Road).
- [Border Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Border_Watch) — A village close to the border to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), inhabited entirely by [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).
- Slough Point — A town in Blackwood. One notable building here is the [Census and Excise](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Census_and_Excise) office.

## Fauna
The Badger Bears of southern Blackwood were nearly extinct in the wild when they were saved by the intervention of Imperial noble Viscount Glorion of Leyawiin, who rescued the species by breeding them as mounts.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONBadger Bear-6)</sup> [Kotu Gava](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kotu_Gava), [Haj Mota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Haj_Mota), as well as harmless frogs, monkeys, and snakes can be found on the border with Black Marsh.

## Flora
[Domica Redwort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Domica_Redwort), [Somnalius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Somnalius), [Dragon's Tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragon%27s_Tongue_Plant), [Pitcher Plants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pitcher_Plant), [St. Jahn's Wort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:St._Jahn%27s_Wort), [Sacred Lotuses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sacred_Lotus), [Tiger Lilies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tiger_Lily), and [Nirnroots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nirnroot) are plants native to the region. Native fungi include [Stinkhorns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stinkhorn), [Clouded Funnels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Clouded_Funnel), and [Bog Beacons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bog_Beacon).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Oblivion-7)</sup>
