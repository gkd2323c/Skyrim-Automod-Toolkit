## Winterhold Imperial Camp
It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the south, in a row are the Legate's tent, command tent, and hospital tent. The Legate's tent contains an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. The command tent contains an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. The hospital tent contains six bed rolls, two of them occupied by wounded soldiers. Outside is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), a low table with a random [potion of health](Skyrim_Potion_of_Health.md) and two random [potions of healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md), and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](Skyrim_Cure_Poison.md), potions of [cure disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md), and [leather strips](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md). Beside the tent are some barrels. West of the campfire are two rows of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. Northwest of the fire is where the [Imperial quartermaster](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md) works, in front of his tent. His workstation consists of an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) , and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), with various [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Weapons.md) and [steel weapons](Skyrim_Steel_Weapons.md) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) on display on a table in front and inside his tent. North of the fire are two picketed [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. There is a [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) near the fire, and a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush near the small tents.

Any time [Winterhold Hold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes
- Three ore veins are near the camp: a [moonstone ore](Skyrim_Moonstone_Ore.md) vein southwest of the camp, and two [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) veins northwest of the camp.
