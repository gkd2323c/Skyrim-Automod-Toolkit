[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-item-Pickaxe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Pickaxe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Pickaxe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Pickaxe.jpg) A Pickaxe **Rocksplinter** is a unique, unobtainable [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md). Rocksplinter cannot be obtained through normal means, is unenchanted, and cannot be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md).

Originally, it was intended to be a special pickaxe for the miner [Odfel](Skyrim_Odfel.md), as evidenced by his comments about possessing it: *"Mining [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) takes a lot of strength and special reinforced tools. I must have broken five or six pickaxes in the last few months alone. But now that I've got Rocksplinter here, I can cut through stone like a hot knife through butter."*

## Bugs
- Rocksplinter was at one point meant to be obtainable, and references to it in the world still exist. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, addresses this issue. The item is now carried by Odfel.
- Rocksplinter does one damage by default, versus the five of normal pickaxes. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
- Rocksplinter is not classified as a War Axe, meaning in combat its damage is not affected by [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) perks or fortify effects. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
