[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-npc-Ronthil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ronthil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ronthil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ronthil.jpg) Ronthil **Ronthil** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) living in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). He is an assistant to [Feran Sadri](Skyrim_Feran_Sadri.md). He has a store and is an expert [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md).

He wears a set of red colored [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), and carries random [leveled bandit items](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), as well as a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold. He knows three leveled [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells along with [Drain Life](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md) and [Vampiric Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md). Like most vampires, he is subject to the vulnerabilities and resistances associated with [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md).

## Dialogue
When you enter Volkihar Keep for the first time during [Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md), attempting conversation with Ronthil will have him say the following in annoyance:

- *"No, no. You must speak with [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md), not me!"*

Afterwards, he will be more welcoming, as he will wish you well or remark on allying with the Volkihar when you pass by:

- *"I hope you find life in the castle to be to your liking."*
- *"It is good to have powerful allies, is it not?"*
- *"Feran is so good to me, allowing me to be his assistant."*

If you are in [vampire lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) form, Ronthil will remark:

- *"I hope you're not trying to lord your abilities over me."*

You can ask him what he does in the keep.

- *"Hello there."*
- **What do you do here?**
- *"Whatever is required! I am happy to do whatever is asked of me. I only wish to be useful. Often I take on tasks that [Garan](Skyrim_Garan_Marethi.md) or Feran find distasteful, or... beneath them. If I can remain useful, those above me will see fit to keep me here."*

Before you trade with him, he will often mention his eagerness to serve, saying one of the following:

- *"I am more than happy to buy or sell whatever you might need."*
- *"I'm sorry I don't have more to offer."*
- *"I hope I have something that interests you. I always wish to be of service."*
- *"You need something? I am happy to help, if I can."*
- *"I've scrounged up a few things. Here, please, take a look."*

If asked for training, he will likewise reply:

- *"Of course! It is my duty to serve, and I am happy to do it!"*

However, if your Speech skill is at least 75, he will instead tell you:

- *"I am afraid you're beyond my meager abiliities * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *. I am sorry I cannot serve you better."*

He will bid you farewell with one of the following:

- *"I look forward to our next meeting."*
- *"Thank you. I'll be going."*
- *"Good hunting."*

If cured of vampirism before [Kindred Judgement](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgement.md):

- *"You're mortal? What are you doing here like that? No, no, you must get away! If I'm seen talking to you, they'll blame me!"*

After Lord Harkon's speech during [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Vampire).md), he'll say:

- *"A Moth Priest?"*

If you speak to him during Kindred Judgement, he'll say:

- *"Our Lord will be pleased, I'm sure. I knew you could do it."*
- *"I knew you would be victorious."*

### Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-npc-Ronthil_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ronthil_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ronthil_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ronthil_02.jpg) Ronthil approaches Feran Sadri While inside Volkihar Keep, you may witness him offering his services to Feran Sadri or [Vingalmo](Skyrim_Vingalmo.md), though both of them are annoyed at his overeager approach:

- **Ronthil**: *"Do you... do you need anything, Feran?"*
- **Feran Sadri**: *"What?"*
- **Ronthil**: *"Can I assist you? You know I live to serve."*
- **Feran Sadri**: *"No, I don't need anything right now. Please stop asking."*
- **Ronthil**: *"Yes, of course."*

- **Ronthil**: *"Vingalmo, is there... can I do anything for you?"*
- **Vingalmo**: *"I don't recall addressing you, worm."*
- **Ronthil**: *"No, I just... I thought perhaps..."*
- **Vingalmo**: *"In the unlikely event that I ever require you for some reason, I shall find you. Until then I see no reason for us to be speaking."*
- **Ronthil**: *"Very good. I'll just... I'll be going now."*

You'll also see Feran asking him about supplies:

- **Feran Sadri**: *"Ronthil, where is the last batch of alchemy ingredients I asked you for?"*
- **Ronthil**: *"Ah, yes. That. There were... well, complications."*
- **Feran Sadri**: *"I don't want to hear about it. I want what I asked for."*
- **Ronthil**: *"Yes, Feran. Of course. I'll see to it."*

## Notes
- Unlike the other members of the clan, Ronthil doesn't rest in a coffin. Instead, he can be found sleeping behind the corner bookshelf in the castle's study.
- When you enter Volkihar Keep for the first time during Bloodline, Ronthil is standing on the balcony overlooking the dining room. There is a brief window before Lord Harkon addresses you directly. During this window, all the residents of Volkihar Keep will be friendly and interact with you normally. With good timing, you can stand on the dining table, jump toward the balcony and initiate dialogue with Ronthil, before Lord Harkon speaks to you. This will allow you to purchase [Heal Undead](Skyrim_Heal_Undead.md), which is ordinarily impossible if you choose to side with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).
- Ronthil is the only vampiric Wood Elf in the entire game if the player joins the Dawnguard. Otherwise he will hold that uniqueness until [Valindor](Skyrim_Valindor.md) is turned in the [New Allegiances](Skyrim_New_Allegiances.md) quest.
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

- *"Once a wizard, Ronthil never really took to the idea of being a vampire. He was useful enough to weasel his way into the court and will now do whatever he must to stay there. He's a total sycophant and will gladly grovel at the feet of whatever master is in front of him so long as his needs are met. He annoys the majority of the other vampires, who have little or no respect for him, but he's good enough at what he does that they tolerate him."*
