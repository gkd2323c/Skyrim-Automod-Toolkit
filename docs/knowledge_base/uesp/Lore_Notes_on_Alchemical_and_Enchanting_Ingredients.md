***Agents and Reagents: The Bounty of Mundus*** is a short tie-in book to *[Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*, which came in *[The Hero's Guides to The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Hero%27s_Guides_to_The_Elder_Scrolls_Online)* set. It is the journal of [Gargrell Sorick](Lore_Gargrell_Sorick.md), second apprentice to [Defessus Lector](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Defessus_Lector) titled *Notes on Alchemical and Enchanting Ingredients*, and catalogs potion ingredients used by them.
