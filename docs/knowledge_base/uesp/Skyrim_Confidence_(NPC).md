[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Confidence.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Confidence.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Confidence.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Confidence.jpg) Confidence **Confidence** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad's](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Mad.md) [mind](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_a_Madman.md). During the related quest, the much larger [Anger](Skyrim_Anger.md) will be beating up Confidence, and you will be tasked with evening the odds with the help of the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md).

Confidence wears the common bartender [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md#0005b6a0).

## Related Quests
- **[The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md)**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](Skyrim_Pelagius_Wing.md).

## Notes
- Unlike most of the other NPCs in the Mind of Madness, he can be pickpocketed, but only after Anger is defeated.
