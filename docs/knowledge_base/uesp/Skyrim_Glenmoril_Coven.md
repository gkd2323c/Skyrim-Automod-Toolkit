[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-place-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg/200px-SR-place-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg) Glenmoril Coven **Glenmoril Coven** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) northwest of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) containing [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md) or [Glenmoril Witches](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch.md). It contains only one zone, *[Glenmoril Cave](#Glenmoril_Cave)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Blood's Honor](Skyrim_Blood%27s_Honor.md)**: Claim the head of a [Glenmoril Witch](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch.md) for [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md).
- **[Purity](Skyrim_Purity.md)**: Help a member of the Circle give up their beasthood. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
As you approach the cave, it becomes clear all is not well. The local plant life is dead, and there are mutilated shrines along the path to the cave. The path starts immediately to the north of an [unmarked ruined tower](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Unmarked_Ruins_and_Monuments) and has a number of switchbacks as it climbs toward the cave entrance. You are likely to encounter a random [creature](Skyrim_Creatures.md) or two along this path. As you near the top, there are numerous [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) symbols formed from the bones and antlers of large elk, spriggan and goat heads on stakes, and taproots hanging from trees. A patch of [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) grows on a tree stump near the entrance, with several troll and animal skulls on either side. The entrance is marked by lit braziers on stands.

### Glenmoril Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven.jpg) Glenmoril Cave Upon entering, a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) is in an alcove straight ahead where the tunnel first turns to the right. There are three enemies in the large chamber nearby. A [hag](Skyrim_Hag.md) patrols near a campfire in the middle, and there is a leveled creature (such as a [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md), [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), or [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md)) hiding in the darkness to the right. There is an additional weak creature by the western tunnel on the upper level. There is a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md) next to the central pillar and another just to the northwest. Due south of the fire is a large [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a basket holding a sprig of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) flowers, a pair of [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) next to a patch of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) fungus, and two skulls to go with the two nearby rib cages. There are ramps leading upwards on either side and four tunnels leading out from the center room: two on the lower level and two on the higher level.

#### Lower North Tunnel
The northern tunnel on the lower level leads to a small room, where a hag is standing on a stake-studded ledge on the left. An earthen ramp leads up to it starting behind a rock pillar in front. There is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel with a lit goat horn lantern on top, and to the right is an open crate containing two [rock warbler eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) and a [briar heart](Skyrim_Briar_Heart.md), with a birds' nest beside it holding another rock warbler egg. Farther to the right is an unlocked wooden chest.

#### Lower West Tunnel
The twisting tunnel to the west on the lower level leads to a small room with a waterfall. There is a single hag by the waterfall on the lower far side of the chamber and nothing else.

#### Upper North Tunnel
The tunnel to the north on the upper level leads to a medium-sized room. After following the tunnel, turn left to go around the stake studded edge. There is a creature at this point and a hag farther on near a table with a built-in [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md). On the table is a dead [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md), a goat's head, a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), and a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Tragedy in Black](Skyrim_A_Tragedy_in_Black.md)*.

#### Upper West Tunnel
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glenmoril_Coven_02.jpg) The upper western chamber Before the western tunnel on the upper level, there is a patch of [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot) on the left and another on the right inside the tunnel. There is also an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) in the tunnel, on the left-hand wall after turning the corner by the brazier. The passage opens into a chamber dominated by a shallow pool with a path around it to the right. There is a leveled creature around the corner and a hag on the far left side standing in front of a hagraven-style tent. There is another Namira's rot and a bleeding crown on the right. Across from these sits another pillar, behind which is an open crate containing two [slaughterfish eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg) and three [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales).

Beside the tent is a bloodied altar of Hircine with a random [poison](Skyrim_Poisons.md), one of three random potions (a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), or [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina)), and a nightshade flower. On the other side of the tent is a boss-level unlocked chest. Inside the tent there are two barrels of [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and an open crate containing a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[Horror of Castle Xyr](Skyrim_Horror_of_Castle_Xyr.md)*, two more slaughterfish eggs, and three more slaughterfish scales. By another pillar at the southern end of the pool are three [white caps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) and a bleeding crown.

## Notes
- During the quest [Blood's Honor](Skyrim_Blood%27s_Honor.md), the hags will be replaced by the Glenmoril Witches. The witches are unique hagravens; for details on their background, see the [lore article](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md). However, the cave itself, including its layout and all loot, does not respawn.
- The bloodied altar in the upper west chamber is a shrine to [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md).
