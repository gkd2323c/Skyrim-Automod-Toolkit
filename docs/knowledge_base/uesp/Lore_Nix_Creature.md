# Nix Creature

Nix Creature (or simply **Nix**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Manual-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DEDH-2)</sup> is a term that describes members of a family of distantly related insectoid [arthropod](Lore_Arthropod.md) species native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nix-3)</sup> The main members of this family are [Nix-Hounds](Lore_Nix-Hound.md) and [Nix-Oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) (as well as the Torchnix subspecies).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nix-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) are considered to be distant cousins to Nix-Hounds but it is unclear if they are classified as Nixes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Manual-1)</sup> Primitive versions of these species were known to exist in Morrowind in the distant past. These creatures were darker in coloration than their more modern counterparts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) of Bal Sunnar once used [time magic](Lore_Time_Magic.md) to summon some of these primitive Nix-Hounds, Nix-Oxen, and Kwama from the past for use as guardians.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) were known to tame nix creatures since the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), and would herd the creatures using nix-prods.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> All three species are culturally important to the modern [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md). They are referred to in the Dunmer swear "couldn't give a nix".<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Nix-Blood is an ailment in Morrowind, though it is unclear if it is linked with nix creatures.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> One Redguard used the phrase "nix-bane" amongst various other derogatory insults to refer to a [Gremlin](Lore_Gremlin.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-RGTrithik-10)</sup> Nil-Bright the Nix is a figure from the esoteric *[Magne-Ge Pantheon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Magne-Ge_Pantheon)*.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Magne-11)</sup> [Nixads](Lore_Nixad.md) are a form of insectoid [fey](Lore_Faerie.md), though it is unknown if they have any relation to nix creatures.
