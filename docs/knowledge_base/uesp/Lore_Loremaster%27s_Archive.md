# Loremaster's Archive

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/LO-misc-Loremaster%27s_Archive.jpg/300px-LO-misc-Loremaster%27s_Archive.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Loremaster%27s_Archive.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Loremaster%27s_Archive.jpg) Image used for the Loremaster's Archive until the [Imperial City DLC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Imperial_City_(DLC)) was released The **Loremaster's Archive** is a series created by [Zeni Max](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Zeni Max_Online_Studios) alongside the release of *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*. The series focuses on revealing new [lore books](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Library), and various lore-related questions are answered by prominent [authors](Lore_Books_by_Author.md) or entities of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and beyond. These characters were played by the game's loremasters, which in order are [Lawrence Schick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Lawrence_Schick), [Leamon Tuttle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Leamon_Tuttle), and [Michael Zenke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Zenke). The series can be found on the official [ESO website](https://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/news/category/loremasters-archive).

Loremaster's Archives used to be answered by mailing in questions. This has been retired in favor of questions being answered on the game's [official forums](https://forums.elderscrollsonline.com/en/categories/lore), where related threads occasionally happen.

The series has a total of 59 installments, 54 of which involved a Q&A:

- [Lawrence Schick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Lawrence_Schick) wrote a total of 44 Loremaster's Archives during his tenure as Loremaster, 39 of which involved a Q&A. He was succeeded by Leamon Tuttle. It is unconfirmed if everything listed under the books subsection were written by Schick, but these books are supplementary materials included to read alongside the Archive. Some were written before the Q&A came out, while others were written for the Archive and were later added into the game. Some have yet to be added into the game.
- [Leamon Tuttle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Leamon_Tuttle) wrote a total of 2 Loremaster's Archive Q&As during his tenure as Loremaster. He was succeeded by Michael Zenke.
- [Michael Zenke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Zenke) has written a total of 13 Loremaster's Archive Q&As so far during his tenure as Loremaster.

Since the *House Telvanni* Loremaster's Archive entry was published on March 14, 2023, the context of the interviews has shifted, with the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) being the in-universe host. Specifically, *The Arcanists* Loremaster's Archive entry from May 24, 2023 revealed that the interviews are being hosted by [Gabrielle Benele](Lore_Gabrielle_Benele.md). The *Malacath and Maelstrom* entry from March 7, 2024 revealed that older interviews, specifically Fa-Nuit-Hen's, were part of the same series in-universe. With the *Tamriel's Holidays* Archive from December 19, 2024, [Amalien](Lore_Amalien.md) has taken over this series.

## Notes
- Loremaster's Archive content has been referenced on a few occasions: - [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) was first mentioned as a center of [Dibellan](Lore_Dibella.md) worship in *[Courting Practices in Tamriel](Lore_Augustine_Viliane_Answers_Your_Questions.md)*. Gideon's [Temple of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Temple_of_Dibella) was later featured in ESO's *[Blackwood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Blackwood_(Chapter))* Chapter.
- One of the [loading screen tips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Loading_Tips) in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* contains a quote from *[An Interview with Haskill](Lore_Chamberlain_Haskill_Answers_Your_Questions.md)*.
- The [Flame Monarch's Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown) and [Flame Knight's Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Flame_Knight%27s_Helmet) in *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)* are references to *[The Maelstrom Arena - Part One](Lore_Lord_Fa-Nuit-Hen_and_Tutor_Riparius_Answer_Your_Questions.md)*.
- The in-game ESO text *[What's an Arcanist?](Lore_What%27s_an_Arcanist%3F.md)* mentions Dhulef's interview from *[Tamriel's Dungeons](Lore_Loremaster%27s_Archive_-_Tamriel%27s_Dungeons.md)*.
- The term "[paleonymic](Lore_Paleonymic.md)" from *[Ranks and Hierarchy of the Daedra](Lore_Lyranth_the_Foolkiller_Answers_Your_Questions.md)* appears in *[Archmagister Mavon's Ascension](Lore_Archmagister_Mavon%27s_Ascension.md)* and [Filer Jun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Filer_Jun)'s dialogue in ESO's [Infinite Archive](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Infinite_Archive).
