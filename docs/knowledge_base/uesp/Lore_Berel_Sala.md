Berel Sala was the chief [Ordinator](Lore_Ordinators.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gen Dialogue Ordinator-1)</sup> and leader of the Ordinators of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the Order of the Watch during the war with [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) and [his forces](Lore_Sixth_House.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PN-2)</sup> Berel's high status earned him titles such as **Grandmaster**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ho J-3)</sup> **Captain of the Watch**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PN-2)</sup> and **Lord High Archordinator**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NFTA-4)</sup> Berel Sala was seen as zealous and fanatic,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWVedam-5)</sup> believing that any dissent from the Temple faith was unacceptable, and that only a resolute, unified faithful could hope to defeat Dagoth Ur and his legions.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BG-6)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) an outlander began to fulfill [prophecies of the fated Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine_Prophecy.md). This was of grave concern to Berel Sala who saw this outsider and their followers as a threat to the resolute faith of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) faithful. Berel did his part to try and discredit and stop the rise of this figure.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PN-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BS-7)</sup>

[Duke Vedam Dren](Lore_Vedam_Dren.md) was discomfited by the fanatical nature of Sala, and employed the outlander to campaign the Temple Archcanon Tholer Saryoni to control and lessen the grasping influence of the Archordinator.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWVedam-5)</sup> Saryoni eventually agreed to speak with Berel and give him less control over the Temple going forward.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Saryoni-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CTO-9)</sup>

Despite his best attempts to sway them away from supporting the outsider,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWMiner-10)</sup> certain [Great House](Lore_Great_Houses.md) leaders gleefully anticipated the fury of the polarizing Sala,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWDratha-11)</sup> who could do nothing as the outlander began to continue to fulfill prophecies, and be named Hortator of all three Vvardenfell Great Houses and also the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) of the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-HN-12)</sup>

The influence of the [Hero](Lore_Hero.md) became too much for Sala or any naysayer to dispute, and eventually the Nerevarine was given audience with [Lord Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) himself, who announced he was ending persecution of the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md), as well as the Nerevarine, and that the Nerevarine would in fact become the Temple's champion. Vivec claimed that despite Berel and his Ordinators serving valiantly in the war against Dagoth Ur, that the Temple had erred in trusting his judgment and mistaking his zeal for dedication.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MWVivec-13)</sup>
