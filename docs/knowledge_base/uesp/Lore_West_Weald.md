[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/OB-place-West_Weald.jpg/200px-OB-place-West_Weald.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-West_Weald.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-West_Weald.jpg) West Weald [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/OB-map-West_Weald.jpg/200px-OB-map-West_Weald.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-West_Weald.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-West_Weald.jpg) Map of the West Weald The West Weald is an open countryside of the [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) region in southern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), well known for its vineyards and tomato farms. The city of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) is located here. Within its province, the West Weald shares it western and northern borders with the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md) and its western border with the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). A portion of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) region called the "North Weald" reaches the northern border of the West Weald. To the south of the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md) it borders the [Northern Woods](Lore_Northern_Woods.md) region, sometimes called the "South Weald", within the provinces of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The West Weald is also known for its population of [West Weald Bears](Lore_Black_Bear.md), some of the most dangerous in Cyrodiil.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The [caracal lynx](Lore_Caracal.md) is native to south-central Tamriel, and roams from the savannahs of [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md) in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) to as far north as the West Weald in Cyrodiil.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLynx-3)</sup>

## History

### Merethic and First Eras
The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruled over the West Weald during the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). As a result, the region is dotted by numerous [Ayleid ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md). Based on archaeological evidence, it is known that many of these Ayleid city-states worshipped a forgotten [deity](Lore_Gods.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> In truth, these Ayleids were [Daedraphiles](Lore_Daedraphiles.md) who worshipped the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOPaths Chaos-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGambit-7)</sup> All knowledge of Ithelia's existence was lost when [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) imprisoned her and wiped all memory of her from existence.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Many of her former followers departed Cyrodiil for Valenwood after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), forming part of the [Ayleid Diaspora](Lore_Ayleid_Diaspora.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Beragon-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Following the collapse of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md), a [Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md) kingdom arose in [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md). The kingdom was ceded to the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) by King Dorald Larich in [1E 478](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_478), resulting in his swift beheading at the hands of his brother, [Rislav](Lore_Rislav_Larich.md). Rislav was quickly crowned as the new King of Skingrad, and defended the kingdom from an attack by the forces of Emperor [Gorieus](Lore_Gorieus.md). This military defeat preserved Skingrad's independence and ultimately led to the downfall of the Alessian Empire.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

### Second Era
The West Weald was incorporated into the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) as part of County Skingrad.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOCalantius-12)</sup> The fertile land of the Weald led to the region becoming known for its famous [wines](Lore_Wine.md) and [cheeses](Lore_Cheese.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONDIGR-13)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ithelia was freed from her imprisonment by [Torvesard](Lore_Torvesard.md), awakening her forgotten [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) servants.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Torvesard Quote-14)</sup> The [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) descendants of her former Ayleid servants also learned of Ithelia's existence, in an event known as the Day of Remembering. Led by King [Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md), these Bosmer "recallers" formed a militant cult known as the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md) in service to the Prince, seeking to reclaim their ancestral legacy and restore what was forgotten by conquering the West Weald.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> The Recollection utilized the destructive power of [Wildburn seeds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn_Seeds) to awaken buried Ayleid magicka in the [Strid Vale](Lore_Strid_Vale.md) region of the West Weald. Almost overnight, a new forest greatly resembling the Valenwood emerged on the opposite side of the Strid.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ESOSeeds-18)</sup> Nantharion then led a group of peaceful Bosmer settlers from [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md) into this new forest, which he named the Dawnwood. As the self-appointed King of Dawnwood, Nantharion established the town of Vashabar, building it atop the lost Ayleid ruins of Miscarcand.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Mt CN-19)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGambit-7)</sup>

In parallel, the Daedric forces of [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md) began invading [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), summoned from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) by ancient Mirrormoor mosaics dotted throughout the West Weald and imbued with a desire to restore the memory of their forgotten Prince.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Ithelia Fot FP-21)</sup> Ithelia herself manifested on Nirn in an initial state of confusion, drawn to the West Weald by its familiarity.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOPaths Chaos-5)</sup> With the aid of Nantharion, she succeeded in reclaiming her divine power within Miscarcand, although Nantharion was killed in the attempt.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGambit-7)</sup> This ended the threat posed by the Recollection, leaving the political fate of the Dawnwood and the [Dawnway](Lore_Dawnway.md) Bosmer uncertain.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOCalantius-12)</sup> Ithelia was subsequently banished to the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md), allowing Hermaeus Mora to once again wipe all memory of her existence.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

### Third Era
The West Weald was incorporated into the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) upon its founding by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup>

By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), no trace of the Dawnwood remained, with the landscape of the Strid Vale reverting to resemble the rest of the Weald.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-24)</sup> By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), County Skingrad was ruled by a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md), [Janus Hassildor](Lore_Janus_Hassildor.md).<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-25)</sup>

### Fourth Era
Skingrad was occupied by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) during the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) when forces led by Lord [Naarifin](Lore_Naarifin.md) marched out of hidden camps in northern Elsweyr, flanking the Imperial defenses along the Valenwood border. The war ended in [4E 175](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_175) with the destruction of the Dominion's main invasion force.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-26)</sup>
