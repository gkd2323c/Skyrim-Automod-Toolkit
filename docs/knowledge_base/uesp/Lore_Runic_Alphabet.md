# Runic Alphabet

The **Runic Alphabet** is a set of strokes representing individual syllables found on [runestones](Lore_Enchanting.md#Runestones_and_Glyphs) used for [enchanting](Lore_Enchanting.md). They are used by the mysterious [rune-language](Lore_Rune-Language.md) inscribed upon these magical runes.

Each stroke contains 1 or more lines and is associated with a single syllable in the rune's name. The strokes most often overlap, and are usually shown in order from left to right, though there are certain exceptions. Notably, the Ma and Ra strokes, being closer to horizontal, are usually the last syllable despite their position in the rune.
