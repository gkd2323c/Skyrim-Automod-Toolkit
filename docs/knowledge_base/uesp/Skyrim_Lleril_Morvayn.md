[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-npc-Lleril_Morvayn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lleril_Morvayn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lleril_Morvayn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lleril_Morvayn.jpg) Lleril Morvayn **Lleril Morvayn** is the [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) Councilor of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) and ruler of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). His actual influence doesn't spread far beyond Raven Rock without the resources of the ebony mine. He took over from his mother, [Brara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Mistress_Brara_Morvayn), in [4E 65](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_65), and has been described as a fair and compassionate leader. However, a few bad decisions have made him a target for a possible assassination plot, revealed during the quest [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md).

He wakes at 6am and has a two-hour breakfast in [Morvayn Manor](Skyrim_Morvayn_Manor.md) with his friend and Second Councilor of Raven Rock, [Adril Arano](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md), and Adril's wife [Cindiri](Skyrim_Cindiri_Arano.md). He then moves to his throne and stays there until 7pm, when he eats a two-hour dinner at the nearby table. After drifting around for a bit, he goes to bed in the upstairs bedroom, locking the door behind him.

He wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8713) and [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993) and carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Manor_Key) to the manor, an [elven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword), and a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Dialogue
Lleril has various greetings depending on your quest actions. From when you arrive on Solstheim, he may greet you with either:

*"Whether you're simply visiting or staying here, I think you'll find our citizens will welcome you with open arms."* *"Second Councilor Adrano * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * can assist you with anything you might need."* *"Anything you can contribute to Raven Rock will be greatly appreciated... and rewarded appropriately, of course."* **(Not completed the quests [March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead) or [The Final Descent](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md))** *"On behalf of House Redoran and as Councilor of Raven Rock, I bid you welcome."* **(Not completed The Final Descent)** *"Don't let the state of Raven Rock fool you. Our desire for Raven Rock's return to its former glory is as strong as [The Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md) itself."* *"Your contributions have earned you citizenship here, friend. I'll always consider you one of us."* **(The Final Descent completed)** *"Traveling beyond The Bulwark will be much safer as the result of your victory at [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md). Many thanks."* **(March of the Dead completed)** *"I feel I owe you my life for thwarting the [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) plot to have me assassinated. My deepest thanks."* **(Served Cold completed)** *"The mines are open and the ebony has begun to flow because of your discovery. My eternal gratitude and blessings go with you, friend."* **(Served Cold completed)** When asked about House Redoran, he will say,

## Notes
- Upon death, he was supposed to say, *"I join you in death, father..."* but since he's always essential this line can never be heard. - It's possible that Lleril having this line is a developer oversight, as it's identical to the line used by [Vendil Ulen](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md) upon his death. Unlike Vendil's, though, Lleril's father didn't play an important role in the [story](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._III) of Raven Rock. It's actually unknown who exactly Lleril's father is, as his mother's [original husband](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Remas_Morvayn) passed away years before her moving to the colony, and another husband is never referenced anywhere.
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads *"Lleril Morvayn is the son of Brara Morvayn, the House Redoran Councilor who settled in Raven Rock after the devastation of Vvardenfell. He took over as councilor from his mother when Brara died early in the Fourth Era. On more than one occasion, attempts on his life have been made by members of House Hlaalu, which has made Lleril quite paranoid. The dire economic and security situation has been weighing heavily on his shoulders."*
