Lady Essenia (also known as the **Blood Queen**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOGwendis-1)</sup>) was a master alchemist and the high chamberlain of [Greymoor Keep](Lore_Greymoor_Keep.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RNCV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLOB-3)</sup> Essenia was a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) ancient, and prior to [Rada al-Saran](Lore_Rada_al-Saran.md)'s arrival, she ruled over Greymoor Keep.

Lady Essenia is credited with the creation of Chaotica Vampiris, a strain of vampirism that only infects those already afflicted with vampirism.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RNCV-2)</sup> The afflicted are transformed into [Bloodknights](Lore_Bloodknight.md), pale monstrosities that are stronger and faster than normal vampires.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RNCV-2)</sup> The Blood Queen sought to elevate her vampiric condition, and succeeded in doing so in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when a vampire sired by [Count Verandis Ravenwatch](Lore_Verandis_Ravenwatch.md) appeared in her castle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RNTHOV-4)</sup> Lady Essenia used Verandis's blood to empower herself, transforming into a magical abomination similar to [Harrowing Reaper](Lore_Harrowing_Reaper.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLOB-3)</sup> She was slain by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) that same day.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLOB-3)</sup>
