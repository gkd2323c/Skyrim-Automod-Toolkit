Chapter 21: Surviving the Rift **by [Rilaso](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Rilaso)** *A cautionary guide warning against the dangers of the Rift*
---
The key to surviving [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) is to never go there. The Rift is no place for anyone but [Nords](Lore_Nord.md). If you're not a Nord and you end up in the Rift, you will die in a horrible fashion.

If you don't freeze to death or get lost and fall off a cliff, you're going to be killed by [wild animals](Lore_Bestiary.md). [Bears](Lore_Bear.md) and [sabre cats](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md) are only too happy to eat you. [Mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) go out of their way to step or sit on travelers. [Giants](Lore_Giant.md) will kick you into the highest reaches of the nearest tree.

But for my money, the Nords are the most dangerous part of the Rift. They have hair-trigger tempers, an enormous capacity for [mead](Lore_Mead.md), and very sharp [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) that they swing around constantly.

If they don't drink you to death or choke you with their noxious bodily odors, they'll chop off your head. You'll be in a tavern, say the wrong thing to a Nord, and next thing you know, your head will be flying through the air, spraying another layer of blood over the already gore-caked walls.

So, don't go to the Rift.
