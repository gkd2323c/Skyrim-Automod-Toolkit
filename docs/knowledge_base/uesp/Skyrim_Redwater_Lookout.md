## Notes
- If still alive, the two lookouts will be renamed to "[Vampire's Thrall](Skyrim_Vampire%27s_Thrall.md)" when you leave, and lose their unique dialogue.

## Bugs
- It is possible for NPCs to be renamed "Vampire's Thrall" on first meeting.
