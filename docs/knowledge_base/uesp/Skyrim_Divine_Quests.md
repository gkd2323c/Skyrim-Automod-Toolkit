# Divine Quests


- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).
- **[The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen) for [Danica Pure-Spring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring).
- **[The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md)**: Help the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.
- **[The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md)**: Find a new [Sybil](Skyrim_Fjotra.md) for the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md).
