## Quick Walkthrough
1. Locate and bring three suitable [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) to Delphine.
2. Induct these followers into the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md).
3. Ask [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md) about [dragon lairs](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) to start the repeatable quest [Dragon Hunting](Skyrim_Dragon_Hunting.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Help Wanted
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-npc-Delphine_35.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Delphine_35.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delphine_35.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delphine_35.jpg) Delphine inducting [Lydia](Skyrim_Lydia.md) into the Blades After completing [Alduin's Wall](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Wall.md), you may notice that the [Blades'](Skyrim_Blades.md) mission of ridding Skyrim of [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) may be rather daunting for just two people. Ask [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md) about her apparent staffing shortage and she will reply, *"The [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) hunted us down, remember? It wasn't exactly great for recruitment. But we have a headquarters of a sort now. We'll rebuild the Blades. Someday."* As you are the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md), and the Blades are sworn to protect you, you might be inclined to help Delphine find new recruits. When you offer, she will say, *"If you find anyone you think would make a good recruit, I can certainly take a look at them. Remember, though, being a Blade is a lifelong commitment. Their loyalty has to be with us once they're in."*

### Recruiting
To recruit a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) into the Blades, simply bring your new recruit to Delphine in [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) for introductions. Since being a Blade is binding, Delphine will ask you for confirmation: *"Are you sure? I'll need to ask them to take an oath to leave their old life behind and stay here from now on."* Confirm your selection and Delphine will agree. She will then give the oath to your first recruit:

| **Delphine**: | *"You there. The Dragonborn wishes to give you the chance to join the lost guardians of Tamriel. The dragon slayers. The Blades. Do you wish to become a Blade?"* |
| --- | --- |
| **Follower**: | *"I do."* |
| **Delphine**: | *"Are you willing to trade away all claims and titles of your former life? To live here and devote yourself to protecting Tamriel from danger?"* |
| **Follower**: | *"I do."* |
| **Delphine**: | *"Then by my right as acting grandmaster, I name you a Blade, with all the privileges, rights, and burdens that brings. Godspeed."* |

### Happy Hunting
You can bring two more recruits to Delphine for the oath taking. After the third, she will say *"That's enough recruits for now. Ask Esbern about the dragon lairs he's been studying. We'll have the recruits do some hunting with you."* Speak to Esbern to complete the quest and begin the [Dragon Hunting](Skyrim_Dragon_Hunting.md) repeatable quest.

## Notes
- You can still ask recruited Blades to follow you, but they will be situated at Sky Haven Temple rather than their place of usual residence. - If you induct your spouse into the Blades, then you can still move your spouse to a house you own. If you choose to live at your spouse's home, you will be directed to Sky Haven Temple.
- All [permanent followers](Skyrim_Followers.md#Permanent_Followers) are eligible to join the Blades except [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) and those from the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md).
- When a new recruit joins the Blades, they automatically leave your service, so a new follower can be recruited. Bugs affecting your ability to obtain new followers may be solved in this manner, though only three followers may be recruited.
- Hirelings that join the Blades can be hired for free after they move to the temple.
- If one of the new recruits dies for whatever reason, they cannot be replaced by inducting another follower. It is therefore best to choose [essential characters](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md).
- If the optional quest [Paarthurnax](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(quest).md) has started, you can still recruit followers for Delphine. When it comes time to talk to Esbern however, he will not finish this quest or start the Dragon Hunting quest until Paarthurnax is slain.
- Blades armor is Heavy Armor, so recruits who do not favor that skill will be at a disadvantage. Similarly, Skyrim's Blades only have one-handed swords, so followers unskilled in One-handed may be at a disadvantage. Both of these however do not apply for essential characters. - If playing without the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), followers who are skilled with this equipment are: [Cosnach](Skyrim_Cosnach.md), [Kharjo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kharjo), [Uthgerd the Unbroken](Skyrim_Uthgerd_the_Unbroken.md), [Golldir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir), [Vorstag](Skyrim_Vorstag.md), [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md), [Ria](Skyrim_Ria.md), [Torvar](Skyrim_Torvar.md), [Borgakh the Steel Heart](Skyrim_Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.md), [Ogol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ogol), and [Celann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Celann).
- If using the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) 2.0.9. or higher, followers who are skilled with this equipment are: [Erik the Slayer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erik_the_Slayer), [Kharjo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kharjo), [Adelaisa Vendicci](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adelaisa_Vendicci), [Golldir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir), [Ria](Skyrim_Ria.md), [Torvar](Skyrim_Torvar.md), [Borgakh the Steel Heart](Skyrim_Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.md), [Ghorbash the Iron Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ghorbash_the_Iron_Hand), and [Celann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Celann).
- With or without the Unofficial Skyrim patch, most of the player's Housecarls also make excellent Blades since they mostly specialise in Heavy Armor, sword and shield fighting, and can level up to 50 which is higher than most other followers. Exceptions are Valdimar, who favors Light Armor, and Rayya, who favors dual-wielding rather than sword and shield. They are also all "protected" and thus should not be killable by anybody but the player.

## Bugs
- Any armor given to Blades inductees prior to their induction may be removed from their inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.

- Due to this bug, it appears that dragon priest masks are also lost. This renders the quest in [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bromjunaar_Sanctuary) impossible to complete.
- When dismissing Blade followers who were formerly [housecarls](Skyrim_Housecarl.md), they will say *"I will be back at your house if you need me, Thane"*, though they will actually return to Sky Haven Temple.
- Sometimes, followers recruited into the blades will travel back to where you recruited them, even though Delphine accepted them. A possible fix could be to load before recruiting the previous Blade (only possible if this wasn't the first follower you introduced to the Blades), recruit that previous Blade again and try again with your second one.
- Esbern may be unavailable when you try to talk to him. There is no dialogue option and he just says 'Did you say something?'.
- Followers may attack one another killing one of your blades members.
