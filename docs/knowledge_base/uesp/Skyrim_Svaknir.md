He wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d). He is equipped with a [honed ancient Nord sword](Skyrim_Honed_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md). Near the end of the dungeon, a copy of *[King Olaf's Verse](Skyrim_King_Olaf%27s_Verse.md)* can be found next to his body. He is involved in the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) storyline via the [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md) quest.

In the last room of the crypt, Svaknir challenges the remains of [King Olaf One-Eye](Skyrim_Olaf_One-Eye.md) to a duel, seeking revenge for his imprisonment at the ancient High King's hands. In his part of the Poetic Edda, the living history of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), Svaknir had denounced the king as a fraud. Svaknir claimed that Olaf and his men came upon a weakened Numinex and took advantage of it to capture the dragon, and that Olaf then fabricated the tale of his battle to ensure his place as Jarl and possibly to aid in his bid to become High King. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Svaknir.md).

Svaknir can also be found sitting on a rock in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md) during the [main quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md) if [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md) has been completed.

## Notes
- There are in fact three versions of Svaknir: - The ghost found in Dead Men's Respite has a base ID of `00016C87` and a ref ID of `00016C88`.
- His body at the end of the said dungeon is actually considered to be a container in game data, and has a base ID of `00018E73`. Other than the verse beside it, only a few gold coins can be found on the body.
- The third version is found in Sovngarde. He has a base ID of `000EA57A` and a ref ID of `000EA57D`, and has the same outfit and weapon as his ghost.
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* refers to him multiple times as "Yrsarald", suggesting that this was his original name.
