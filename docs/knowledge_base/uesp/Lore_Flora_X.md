## [Xal-Vakka Aloe](Lore_Aloe_Vera.md)

## Xthari
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/ON-flora-Xthari.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Xthari.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Xthari.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Xthari.jpg) Xthari Xthari is an orange flower that grows in Black Marsh. The leaves are extremely poisonous, but the petals, which are coated in a greasy substance, act as a powerful curative. The downside to using these petals in an antidote to poison is that the recovery will be very painful. When added to the proper reagents, the petals turn the mixture pink.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)
