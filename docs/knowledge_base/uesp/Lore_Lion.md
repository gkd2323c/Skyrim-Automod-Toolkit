*This cat was easily as big on four legs as Roggvir was on two. A thick mane of hair and a wide snout gave it the look of a venerable Khajiiti trader, though it didn’t use sarcasm to get its point across, preferring a growl and a raking of formidable clawed paws. The fur headdress continued down the back of sand-colored hair, which changed to a darker tone at the tail tuft. Just below the surface, we could see the rippling muscles of the beast’s haunches, as it readied to attack.*
— *[Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Kyne%27s_Challenge:_A_Hunter%27s_Companion)* Lions are large, predatory [felines](Lore_Cat.md) found mainly in the savannahs and arid regions of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lion-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Senche-Lion-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMT4-3)</sup> Female lions, known as **Lionesses**, lack the distinctive manes of the males.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lion-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SLness Mount-4)</sup> Lions are known as proud beasts, and live in large groups known as prides.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Pride-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONNoble Riverhold Senche Lion-6)</sup>

Many of the cats known as lions in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Lion** or **Senche-Lioness**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Senche-Lion-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SLness Mount-4)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-7)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-7)</sup> Lions and senche-lions are not to be confused with [mountain lions](Lore_Mountain_Lion.md).

## Variants
Desert Lions are a species found in the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The Black Mane Lion is evidently named after the black mane their species has.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONBlack Mane Lion-10)</sup> White Lions are known for their all-white fur.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-White-11)</sup>

### Senche-Lions
White Senche-Lions are very rare, as smugglers claim they can make a year's fortune selling them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Black Senche-Lions are known for their completely black fur.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Lion-13)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-Daggerfall_Covenant.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-Daggerfall_Covenant.png) A lion was the symbol of the Daggerfall Covenant
### Domestication
Lions and Senche-Lions are often kept as mounts or pets.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-14)</sup> Khajiit have been able to tame senche-lions with particular ease.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Kyne C-15)</sup><sup>:155-156</sup>

Black Mane Lions are used by the [Bi-Lunar Guard](Lore_Bi-Lunar_Guard.md) as mounts at the Palace of the Mane near [Torval](Lore_Torval.md), and riding one near them is considered taboo.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONBlack Mane Lion-10)</sup> In Elsweyr, the White Lion is considered a highly prestigious mount. When King [Keirgo](Lore_Keirgo.md) married Queen [Eshita](Lore_Eshita.md), his [Orcrest](Lore_Orcrest.md) Honor Guard, twenty strong, were all mounted on mature White Lions.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-White-11)</sup>

The Black Senche-Lion is the breed preferred by thieves as mounts.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Lion-13)</sup> Senche-Lion prides are known to roam near the city of [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md), and are used as mounts and for herding livestock by the pastoral tribes of Northern Anequina. The lions of these prides have been dubbed Noble Riverhold Senche-Lions.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONNoble Riverhold Senche Lion-6)</sup>

Lions are often trained for use as pit-fighting beasts, serving as opponents in arenas.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-16)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

### Uses
In Elsweyr, senche-lion fur is sometimes used to line blankets,<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-18)</sup> and their teeth used to make stylish tail loops.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-19)</sup> Redguards are known to use lion hide treated with an oil of [snake](Lore_Snake.md) venom and ground [beetle](Lore_Beetle.md) carapace to polish their swords.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-20)</sup>

Lions are among the creatures that compose the [Chimeras](Lore_Chimera.md) of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) archipelago, originally created by the [Druids of Galen](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-TBOG-21)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-FDTZ-23)</sup>

### Art, Religion, and Influence
Lions are commonly featured in art and heraldry. They are depicted often in Khajiit, [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md), [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md), and [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) culture. The [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) famously used a lion as their symbol, with [King Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md)'s [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) flying a lion's paw as their banners.

Many children have stuffed toys of lions. It is said there isn't a Khajiit around who didn't own or know someone who owned their very own "Sali the Senche-Lion" doll.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

Lions have some prominence in Khajiiti religion and mythology. The Khajiiti god [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md) is known as "The White Lion".<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-25)</sup> [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md) was said to have bestowed a lion of pure light upon his champion for use as a mount. It is said wherever this Blessed Highmane Lion's paws fell, light and justice soon followed thereafter.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-26)</sup> Another notable lion in Khajiit mythology is the black senche-lion Umbrage, who was said to be the thief god [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md)'s mount. They are rumored to be able to talk and even briefly fly.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Lion-13)</sup>

Lions are referred to metaphorically twice in the *[The 36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*, with it being stated that "The [Sun](Lore_Sun.md) shall be eaten by lions, which cannot be found yet in [Veloth](Lore_Morrowind.md)."<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-27)</sup> As well as later when the "Parliament of Craters" claims they do not recognize the Divine authorities of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), whether they be "lion or serpent or mathematician".<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-28)</sup>
