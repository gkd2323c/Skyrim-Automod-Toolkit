## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read *[Bounty: Fangtusk](Skyrim_Bounty__Fangtusk.md)*.
2. Obtain an [Arctic Grayling](Skyrim_Arctic_Grayling.md).
3. Travel northeast of [Yngol Barrow](Skyrim_Yngol_Barrow.md).
4. Place the grayling on the rock.
5. Kill Fangtusk.
6. Search Fangtusk for the [Vampiric Ring](Skyrim_Vampiric_Ring.md).
7. Speak to the steward of Windhelm.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This quest can be started after [Crustacean Extermination](Skyrim_Crustacean_Extermination.md) has been completed by talking to Viriya:

- **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
- *"Of course. It's all in this note."*

She will hand you the [bounty](Skyrim_Bounty__Fangtusk.md), read it and you will learn of a monsterous horker known as Fangtusk, which has been terrorizing fishermen north of Hollyfrost Farm.

You need to obtain an arctic grayling and then head northeast of [Yngol Barrow](Skyrim_Yngol_Barrow.md) to a rock by water that has two skeletons lying next to it, place the arctic grayling on the rock and Fangtusk will emerge from the icy water.

Once you have killed Fangtusk, inspect his body and you will find [vampire dust](Skyrim_Vampire_Dust.md), [vampire gloves](Skyrim_Vampire_Gloves.md), and a [Vampiric Ring](Skyrim_Vampiric_Ring.md), suggesting Fangtusk ate a vampire.

Take this ring to the steward of Windhelm and speak to them:

- **Fangtusk has been slain.**
- *"It's really dead, then? It may be dead now, but... where did it come from?"*
- **An enchanted ring in Fangtusk's gut seems to have been the source of its power.**
- *"You've done well."*
- **Travellers should be safe now. At least, safe from giant vampire horkers.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you."*
- **Alright, I've held up my end of the bargain. Time to pay up.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you."*

You will then be rewarded 200 gold.

## Bugs
- The bounty on Fangtusk may not be marked as a quest item, even though you need to read it to advance the quest. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.4, fixes this bug.
