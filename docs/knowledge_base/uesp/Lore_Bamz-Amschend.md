[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/ON-interior-Clockwork_City_Vaults.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Clockwork_City_Vaults.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Clockwork_City_Vaults.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Clockwork_City_Vaults.jpg) Bamz-Amschend circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Bamz-Amschend is a vast [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin located beneath the city of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).

Before the disappearance of the Dwemer, the halls of Bamz-Amschend once hosted a Dwemer Council Meet.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Co N-1)</sup> The city also housed the Skybreak Gallery, where the Dwemer constructed the [Karstangz-Bcharn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Karstangz-Bcharn), a giant machine capable of controlling weather above the ground. [Radac Stungnthumz](Lore_Radac_Stungnthumz.md), a Dwarven weaponsmith, once lived and worked in the city. Bamz-Amschend was also connected to a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) ruin of Norenen-dur.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/TR-place-Bamz-Amschend%2C_Skybreak_Gallery.jpg/200px-TR-place-Bamz-Amschend%2C_Skybreak_Gallery.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Bamz-Amschend,_Skybreak_Gallery.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Bamz-Amschend,_Skybreak_Gallery.jpg) The Karstangz-Bcharn in the Skybreak Gallery in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) The city of Mournhold was built at the site of Bamz-Amschend. It was ultimately destroyed in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) by [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and subsequently rebuilt upon the ruins of the old city.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-2920Book-3)</sup> Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruins of Bamz-Amschend housed [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s miniature [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) (or at least its Tamrielic representation). It was located in Seht's Vault, in the deepest part of the ruin. At the time, hardly anyone knew about the ruin and Sotha Sil's realm within, but a few individuals, including the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), [Barilzar](Lore_Barilzar.md), and [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md), did pass through Bamz-Amschend while en route to the Clockwork City.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESO-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESODivayth-5)</sup>

Bamz-Amschend was rediscovered in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), after an attack of Sotha Sil's [fabricants](Lore_Fabricants.md) orchestrated by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) uncovered an entrance to the ruin on [Plaza Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Plaza_Brindisi_Dorom.md). During the attack, the halls became a site of a brief skirmish between the fabricants and Dwarven constructs. The ruin was later visited by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), who investigated the attack on behalf of Almalexia. By the Sacred Lady's command, the Nerevarine activated the weather machine in the Skybreak Gallery to cause an ash storm in Mournhold as a show of power, and later he met the spirit of Radac Stungnthumz, who helped him enchant [Trueflame](Lore_Trueflame.md), the reforged blade of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup>

It is unknown what became of Bamz-Amschend after the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), but since Mournhold itself was left mostly intact, the ruin likely didn't suffer much damage either.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRY-6)</sup>
