# Crimson Oath

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/ON-npc-Crimson_Oath_Elementalist.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Crimson_Oath_Elementalist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Crimson_Oath_Elementalist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Crimson_Oath_Elementalist.jpg) A Crimson Oath Elementalist The Crimson Oath are a group of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) loyal to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). They are mainly comprised of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) warriors who have a desire to maraud across Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lt COA-1)</sup> They claim to be Dagon's most trusted, but according to the Waking Flame they are nothing more than brute killers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Dagon assigned them to protect the [Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md) cultists constructing the Antecedent Cataclyst in the Dread Cellar. They were rather bored with their posting to the Cellar,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lt COA-1)</sup> and viewed the Waking Flame as foolish mortals only fit for submission, being quite evidently jealous that Dagon entrusted them with the Cataclyst.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> A group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) and [Imperial Battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) later attacked the Cellar and destroyed the Antecedent Cataclyst.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-QD-3)</sup>
