[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png/200px-LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png) Felldir, Hakon and Gormlaith's spirits.(Legends) Hakon One-Eye was an [Atmoran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atmoran) hero and [Tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tongue) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). Hakon, [Felldir the Old](Lore_Felldir_the_Old.md), and [Gormlaith Golden-Hilt](Lore_Gormlaith_Golden-Hilt.md) were the leaders of the rebellion against [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-2)</sup> They were among the first mortals to learn how to wield the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), apart from the [Dragon Priests](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGATT-3)</sup> They were taught by [Paarthurnax](Lore_Paarthurnax.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Party-4)</sup> During the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), Hakon sought the help of [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), the First Dragonborn, in defeating Alduin, but Miraak refused to help.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Miraak-5)</sup> Undeterred, Hakon and his companions fought against Alduin atop the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md). Finding that they had no means to achieve a final victory, the companions successfully sent the wyrm forward through time using an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md). In the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), when the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) entered the realm of [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md) to defeat Alduin, the spirits of Hakon and his compatriots joined the fray in the final battle against their nemesis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-2)</sup> He also aided the Last Dragonborn in their other battles, when called to their aid with the Call of Valor.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HOE-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-COV-7)</sup>

He fought alongside a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) warrior, whose descendant Darns Gilvio was a member of a [Sai Sahan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sai_Sahan)'s [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DG-8)</sup>

He was reviled by the [Order of the New Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Order_of_the_New_Moon) and remembered alongside [Khunzar-ri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khunzar-ri) as one of the individuals who contributed to the fall of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TMZ-9)</sup>
