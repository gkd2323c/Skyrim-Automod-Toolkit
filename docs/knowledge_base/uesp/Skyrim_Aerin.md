[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Aerin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aerin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aerin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aerin.jpg) Aerin **Aerin** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) residing in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He rescued [Mjoll the Lioness](Skyrim_Mjoll_the_Lioness.md) from outside the Dwemer ruins of [Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md) and nursed her back to health. He spends most of his time following her around. He frequently expresses the desire to travel outside Riften, to see if things are as bad everywhere as they are in Riften.

He sleeps from 2am to 6am, starting his day with a one-hour breakfast. At noon, he and Mjoll come to [the Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) to eat lunch and converse, staying there for six hours. He spends the rest of his time at Mjoll's side and patrolling the city with her. If Mjoll is currently [following](Skyrim_Followers.md) you, however, he will instead be found enjoying some time alone at his house.

He wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000cee80) and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aerin%27s_House_Key) to [his house](Skyrim_Aerin%27s_House.md), as well as a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Quest-Related Events

### Spread the Love
Aerin will gladly [accept](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love) a [bulletin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara) from the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md):

**Here, brother. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"I visit the temple sometimes, but perhaps not often enough. Thanks for bringing this to me."*
## Dialogue
When spoken to:

*"I've never seen Mjoll this upset about anything. The city really gets to her."* *"Mjoll told me that it isn't this bad everywhere. Maybe one day I'll venture out and see if she's right."* *"Mjoll's brought me nothing but safety and piece * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * of mind... and some lessons with the blade."* *"I hope that someday this city will be free from corruption and we can all live in peace."* When you bid farewell to him, he will say:

*"Watch yourself around here, it isn't safe."* You can also see him give to the poor and say, *"Yes, I hope this helps."* to [Snilf](Skyrim_Snilf.md) and [Edda](Skyrim_Edda.md).

## Conversations
Aerin frequently converses with Mjoll:

**Mjoll**: *"I had another run-in with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)."*
**Aerin**: *"Be careful, Mjoll. The Thieves Guild has [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) at her back. One snap of her fingers, and you could end up in [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md)... or worse."*
**Mjoll**: *"They represent the reason I'm here. I can't just ignore them, Aerin."*
**Aerin**: *"I know. I just don't want you to leave; you're the only good thing that's happened to this city in a long time."* **Mjoll**: *"Sometimes I'm not certain that the people of Riften want to be helped."*
**Aerin**: *"I think some of them do, Lady Mjoll. They're just too frightened to step up and do something about it like you are."*
**Mjoll**: *"Aerin, you never cease to amaze me. You always see the good in everything."*
**Aerin**: *"It's only because I've had an excellent teacher."* **Aerin**: *"You've been in Riften for quite some time now, Lady Mjoll. I fear that you soon may leave us."*
**Mjoll**: *"Riften has problems of its own. Now is not the time for exploration and discovery."*
**Aerin**: *"I'm relieved to hear you say that. If you were to leave, I'd miss you terribly."*
**Mjoll**: *"Aerin, I owe you my life. It would take a threat to all of Skyrim for me to depart."* They may talk to each other inside Aerin's house:

**Aerin**: *"Well, you were right. These so called 'Gutter Saints' live in the sewers beneath."*
**Mjoll**: *"I suspected as much. The real question now is do we do something about it ourselves or do we speak to the Jarl?"*
**Aerin**: *"I say we go down there and clear the place out, just like old times."*
**Mjoll**: *"No, my friend. That would be reckless. We have to help, but help in a way that doesn't directly place anyone in harm's way. For now, give this '[Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md)' a wide berth."* **Mjoll**: *"Aerin, have you seen my ring? The one with the inscription?"*
**Aerin**: *"No, I haven't. Don't tell me you've lost it."*
**Mjoll**: *"I hope not. That ring is irreplaceable."*
**Aerin**: *"I'll look around the house and if I find it, I'll bring it to you right away."*
