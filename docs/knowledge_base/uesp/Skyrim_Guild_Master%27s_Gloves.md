[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.jpg) Guild Master's Gloves The Guild Master's Gloves are the gauntlets for the unique set of equipment given to the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), and is part of the quest reward for completing [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md). Alongside this item, this set of equipment includes [armor](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Boots.md), and a [hood](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Hood.md). Its appearance is similar to that of the [Thieves Guild Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Gloves) used by the regular members, except for the darker color for the leather, and the buckle made out of a lighter metal. As it is essentially an upgrade to the regular set of guild armor, these gloves have a superior armor rating and gold value to the basic set, although it weighs more. Like its similar appearance, the Guild Master's Gloves also have a correlative, yet more powerful, enchantment to the more common version of this equipment. The gloves [fortify lockpicking](Skyrim_Fortify_Lockpicking.md) ability by 35 points, versus 15 points for normal Thieves Guild Gloves and 25 points for the upgraded version. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this item requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md)**: Become the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Notes
- There is another version of these gloves used by [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md), which have a better armor rating, but lacks the enchantment that this copy has on it. However, his version cannot be acquired under normal circumstances, as it is not marked as a playable item.
