*"I have already written about how First Kinlord Sundavar brought Clan Corelanya out of Summerset and established a Corelanya kingdom in Hammerfell at Salas En. A proud mercantile realm, they flourished under the protection of their Daedric patrons. To Azura they looked for knowledge and understanding. Nocturnal they revered as their protector. And to Molag Bal they turned when they needed the strength to defy their foes, the Giant Goblins of the desert and the Alessian Order.
Since the hostile desert tribes blocked their way inland, Sundavar Corelanya's heirs turned their ambitions to the sea. From hidden havens on Cape Shira, Corelanya galleys sailed in search of rich lands for their merchants to exploit. Few roamed farther or brought home more riches than Sundavar's great-granddaughter, Iniel. Born in 1E 667 to Kinlord Roliondil, the kinheir grew up to be an intrepid explorer and a bold conqueror, eagerly working to extend the dominions that would one day be hers."*
— *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC2-1)</sup> by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md) **Kinlord** Roliondil Corelanya was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) nobleman and one of the leaders of the [Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) kingdom at [Salas En](Lore_Salas_En.md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC2-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/ON-place-Salas_En.jpg/200px-ON-place-Salas_En.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Salas_En.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Salas_En.jpg) Salas En [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg) Iniel, Roliondil's daughter Little is known of Kinlord Roliondil's life and reign. With hostile desert tribes barring their path inland, Roliondil, just like [Sundavar](Lore_Sundavar_Corelanya.md)'s other heirs, turned his ambitions seaward. From concealed havens along Cape Shira, Corelanya galleys set sail in search of distant, fertile lands ripe for mercantile expansion. Among the enemies faced by Sundavar and his heirs, including Roliondil, were the desert's [Giant Goblins](Lore_Giant_Goblin.md) and the zealots of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md). Like many of his kin, Roliondil maintained devotion to Daedric patrons, chiefly [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md), [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), and [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC2-1)</sup>

His daughter and designated heir, [Iniel](Lore_Iniel_Corelanya.md), was born in [1E 667](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_667). Not long after her return to Hammerfell—sometime following her failed campaign in Solstice in 1E 726—she ascended to the role of Kinlady, presumably upon the death of Roliondil.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC2-1)</sup>

Roliondil is briefly mentioned in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC2-1)</sup>
