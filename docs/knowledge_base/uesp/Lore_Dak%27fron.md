[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Dak'fron is found Dak'fron is a dry region within north-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the greater [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, dominated by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). It is one of the most barren wastelands in the province, and [giant scorpions](Lore_Scorpion.md) migrate from the "blighted Dak'fron" to the marginally lusher Alik'r Desert as an invasive species.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Climate Map-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DUG-2)</sup> Dak'fron borders the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r.md) to the northwest, [Santaki](Lore_Santaki_(county).md) to the north, and the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Sisters of Kykos are found in Dak'fron and the dominant vampire bloodline is the [Anthotis](Lore_Anthotis.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Dak'fron is known for its fine [amethysts](Lore_Amethyst.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AMit A-5)</sup>

After the events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Dak'fron became part of the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md). Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Ayaan-si, High Prophet of [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md), had called upon the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) of the north to rebel against [Lhotun](Lore_Lhotun.md), and had financed a number of forays against the borders in Dak'fron.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
