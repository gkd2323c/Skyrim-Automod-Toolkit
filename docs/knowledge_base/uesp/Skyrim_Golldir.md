[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-npc-Golldir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Golldir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir.jpg) Golldir **Golldir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) found outside [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md). He will appeal to you for help in stopping a [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md), [Vals Veran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran), from defiling his [dead ancestors](Skyrim_Draugr.md). After the related quest, he will remain outside of the tomb and becomes a potential [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). If he is [recruited for the Blades](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md), he will relocate to [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md).

Golldir wears a full set of [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) armor, excluding the helmet. He is equipped with a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) and a leveled shield. He also carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb_Key) to the tomb entrance, a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_from_Agna), a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_to_Golldir), and three random leveled potions of either [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), [restore magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Magicka), or [restore stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Ancestral Worship
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Golldir_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Golldir_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_02.jpg) *"Ah! Oh, by Kyne you startled me."* When you first approach Golldir outside, he will exclaim:

- *"Ah! Oh, by Kyne you startled me. There's a necromancer around here so watch yourself."*

He will then initiate conversation with you by saying:

- *"Can you help me? He's in the tomb doing Gods know what with my dead relatives."*

- - **I don't have time right now.**
- *"Fine, but I've locked the door so don't think you're getting in there without me."*

- - **Who's in the tomb?**
- *"Vals Veran. My family has never really seen eye to eye with him and he has finally gone off the deep end. He's gone in to defile our family tomb by using our ancestors for his filthy dark elf necromancy. My aunt went in after him, but she hasn't come out yet and I'm afraid to go in by myself."*

- - - **You let your aunt go in there by herself?**
- *"Sigh... I'm not proud of it. I'm terrified of that place and Aunt Agna knows it. My Da locked me in there in a drunken rage when he left us... three days in there eating the offerings left for our dead before Aunt Agna found me."*

- - - **Let's go.**
- *"Great! I'll unlock the door and meet you inside, then you can lead the way."*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Golldir_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Golldir_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_03.jpg) *"We're not killing them, they were already dead."* He will then unlock the door leading into the tomb, and you are now directed to proceed inside. Once you are inside the tomb, Golldir will tell you:

- *"We should get after Vals Veran before he does more damage here."*

If spoken to:

- **It doesn't bother you that we're killing your ancestors?**
- *"We're not killing them, they were already dead. We're helping them back to Sovngarde."*

- **Let's go.**
- *"That dark elf is about to be a dead elf."* or *"Vals Veran will die for what he's done."*

Golldir will now become a 'temporary' follower at this point. Proceeding onward, if Golldir catches you taking any items from the tomb, he will allow you to keep them, but will say:

- *"Hey! Those belong to my family! Sigh... fine, take whatever you want, so long as you help me get rid of Vals."*

Once you find [Aunt Agna](Skyrim_Agna.md)'s body, he will exclaim:

- *"No! Aunt Agna! Talos, why didn't I go in with her?"*

Attempting to open the door, he will also state:

- *"He's barred the door. Gods only know how he's... defiling the bodies of my ancestors in there! Agna once told me there is a secret room deeper in where they buried disgraced members of the family. Maybe that will get us into the main chamber..."*
- **Where is this secret door?**
- *"I'll tell you more about it when we are nearby."*

Continuing forward, once you arrive near the secret room, he will exclaim:

- *"This looks like the area Agna told me about. She said that the bear would show the way..."*

Pulling the chain next to the bear statue will reveal a hidden passageway, and Golldir will say:

- *"You found it! The passage goes through the sarcophagus... I don't like this... but we'd better keep going."*

Proceeding onward and before entering through the doors into the main chamber, he will tell you:

- *"Okay, I think this door should lead to the main burial chamber where Vals is holed up. Let's get in there and put an end to this."*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-npc-Golldir_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Golldir_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Golldir_04.jpg) *"I'll return my ancestors to Sovngarde, and you with them!"* Once you enter the main chamber, a short conversation between Golldir and [Vals Veran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran) will commence:

- **Vals:** *"The dead should be made to serve the living, not the other way around!"*
- **Golldir:** *"I'll return my ancestors to Sovngarde, and you with them!"*
- **Vals:** *"Sovngarde is a myth, you s'wit! And now you can join your ancestors in service to me!"*

Once Vals is dead however, Golldir will thank you:

- *"Thank you for your help with Vals. Please, take this and leave me to see to Aunt Agna and my other family members."*

If you approach him again he will tell you:

- *"I have things to see to, but go ahead."*

- - **What will you do now?**
- *"Whatever I can to put my family here back to rest. After that? Probably just stick around here. Saw some good game on my way here, and I'd like to stay close for a while."*

- - **Goodbye.**
- *"Thanks again for your help."*

## Bugs
- Even though he states that his "da" locked him up in the tomb when he was young, the note from his Aunt Agna mentions his brother locking him in instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.

- It is possible for Golldir to be stuck in the sneak animation. Toggling sneak does not fix this issue. He stays in this state even if [Ancestral Worship](Skyrim_Ancestral_Worship.md) has been completed.
