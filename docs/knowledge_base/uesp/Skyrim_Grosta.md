[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-npc-Grosta.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grosta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grosta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grosta.jpg) Grosta **Grosta** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) that owns and operates [Heartwood Mill](Skyrim_Heartwood_Mill.md). She lives there with her husband [Leifnarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifnarr) and son [Gralnach](Skyrim_Gralnach.md). She is generally warm towards you, but ultimately comes off as hardened by the disappearance of her husband. She expresses little discernible emotion as she matter-of-factly states that the mill will go under in a few seasons without him.

Grosta will buy any [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) you bring to her, and will also sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed. If her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) is raised high enough, she will also allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

Grosta wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). She carries a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). In combat, she relies on a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

- *"Hmph. If you see my good-for-nothing husband Leifnarr on your journeys tell him he's no longer welcome here."*
- *"Men are utter pigs... they only care about one thing."*
- *"You men are utter pigs... you only care about one thing."*
- *"Men are complete pigs, am I right? All they care about is one thing... and you know exactly what I mean."*
- *"If I ever catch my husband in bed with another woman, he's going to find himself homeless."*

When spoken to:

- **Is running a mill difficult?**
- *"With just myself and one child, it's damn near impossible. With Leifnarr gone, it's really put me in a terrible bind. I'm having trouble making ends meet. At this rate, we won't last more than a few more seasons at best. Then I'll just have to sell the old place."*

When exiting conversation:

- *"Quit taking advantage of us and this world might be a better place, you got that?"*
- *"Don't let the men take advantage of you, always be on your guard."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Flight or Fight
If you ask her what happened to her husband, she'll begin to rant:

- **What happened to Liefnarr *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*?**
- *"He said he was going east towards the border of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) to trade some of our grain to some folks at [Broken Helm Hollow](Skyrim_Broken_Helm_Hollow.md). I waited and waited, and he never came back. Probably shacked up with some elven whore. Good riddance to him I say. If I only knew where he was, I'd sure give him a piece of my mind."*

- - **I don't like to interfere in family affairs.**
- *"Doesn't matter. I'm sure he's long gone by now anyway."*

- - **I'll try and find him.**
- *"Good. If you see him, tell him Grosta said he can go straight to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md)... he's not welcome here any more."*

- - ** I found Liefnarr's *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* body.**
- *"No, it can't be... oh my... and all this time, I was thinking he'd been unfaithful to me. I feel so stupid."*

- **Why did Leifnarr go to Broken Helm Hollow?**
- *"Well, he said that a few men have been going by to some of the local farms promising a good price from all the local mills and farms. Leifnarr said they'd pay double what you can usually get at the markets. The catch was, he had to head out there with our wood. So not only is Leifnarr gone, but a good bit of our supplies as well."*

When exiting conversation, she'll tell you:

- *"Remember to tell that bastard I never want to see his ugly face again!"*

When you return, she'll ask:

- *"So, any luck finding my louse of a husband?"*
- **Actually, I discovered Leifnarr's body.**
- *"No, it can't be... oh my... and all this time, I was thinking he'd been unfaithful to me. I feel so stupid. I've been a fool, wasting my time sitting here helpless when I could have sent help sooner. Perhaps this is all my doing... What's done is done. Here, I want you to have this. Bringing me closure was kind of you."*

When greeted, she'll now say:

- *"Despite what you discovered it's still comforting to know my husband is at peace."*
