## Walkthrough
The watchtower is just north of Shor's Stone along the main road in the pass between [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) and [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). Depending on the faction that currently controls the Rift, the watchtower contains either the corpses of [Riften guards](Skyrim_Riften_Guard.md) or [Imperial soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md). The view to the north from the top of the tower includes [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and the northern coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) beyond.

Outside the entrance to the watchtower is a campfire with the bodies of two guards nearby. Through the doorway into the tower is a small room with wooden supports at regular intervals. Against the east wall is the start of several flights of stairs that spiral up to the top of the tower. Against the west wall, immediately on your left as you enter, are four weapon racks holding a selection of predominately [iron weapons](Skyrim_Iron_Weapons.md). Farther to the right is a long wooden table holding two pieces of cheese, two bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), and a letter (of which there is both an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Letter.md) and a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Watchtower_Guard%27s_Letter.md) version) promising reinforcements and warning of an impending attack. There are no signs of either the reinforcements or the attacking forces. On the ground under the stairs is a healing potion along with several crates and sacks containing [food](Skyrim_Food.md). As you climb the stairs, you will pass a barrel with a quiver of [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md) on top and see several patches of blood splattered against the walls and stairs. At the top are the bodies of two more unfortunate guards, a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing minor loot, and more sacks of food. All of the guards have all their belongings still on them and can be looted.

## Bugs
- At times, the letter cannot be activated. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.

- If the letter cannot be activated, sometimes looking at the ground below the table still makes it possible to interact.
- The dead Riften guards are called "Riften Guards" even though they aren't actually in Riften. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, addresses this issue. They will now be called "Rift Guards".

- This won't be an issue if the Rift is under Imperial control, as the dead Imperial soldiers will properly be called "Imperial Soldiers".
