—Songamdir<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup> Songamdir was the **Packmaster to Hircine**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WC-2)</sup> the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of the Hunt, serving as both his priest and a powerful [werewolf](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) with formidable [magical abilities](Lore_Magic.md). He was an ancient hunter, dedicated to teaching and initiating new werewolves into Hircine's pack.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/ON-npc-Thoreki.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Thoreki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Thoreki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Thoreki.jpg) Thoreki, one of Songamdir's packmates *Hircine gave me power beyond other werewolves when he chose me for his Packmaster. I can control my form, and I am as much a wolf as I am a man.*
—Songamdir<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup> The exact age of Songamdir is unknown, though by his own account, he was entrusted with the responsibility of overseeing Hircine's pack and wielding the powers that came with it. By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he was incredibly old,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup> and his name was invoked in ancient scrolls as a great hunter.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Scroll-3)</sup> By that time, Songamdir had already been performing his duties for a long period and could be found by those seeking to join Hircine’s pack at shrines dedicated to the Daedric Prince scattered throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Quest-4)</sup> He was often accompanied by the pack [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Wolf-5)</sup> Some of the known locations included the shrine northeast of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), near [Fort Greenwall](Lore_Greenwall.md) in [the Rift Hold](Lore_The_Rift.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md); the northeastern part of the Viridian Woods in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md), the region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md); and in the [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) region of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), just north of the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) settlement of Pa'alat, near [Fort Sphinxmoth](Lore_Fort_Sphinxmoth.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Quest-4)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shrines-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Firralthel-7)</sup>

According to one of his pack mates, it is believed that Hircine bestowed lycanthropy upon the first hunters as a gift, encouraging them to worship him. To honor these hunters, Hircine is said to have taken their cunning and fused it with the strength and form of beasts. It is rumored that these original hunters still live on in the [Great Hunt](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md), Hircine's own realm, to this day. Whether these words refer to Songamdir himself, however, was uncertain.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Thoreki-8)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Werehunter's Collars for large [dogs](Lore_Dog.md) or wolves could be found across Tamriel. Many of these collars bore the personal sigil of Songamdir.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WC-2)</sup>

## Duties
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/ON-quest-Hircine%27s_Gift.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Hircine%27s_Gift.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Hircine%27s_Gift.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Hircine%27s_Gift.jpg) The statue of Hircine in the hunting grounds where Songamdir performs his initiation. [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-npc-Songamdir_Werewolf.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Songamdir_Werewolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Songamdir_Werewolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Songamdir_Werewolf.jpg) Songamdir in his werewolf form *I show pups like you how to make the most of his gift. I'm like a flea-bitten, shirtless priest of Hircine!*
—Songamdir<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup> One of his key roles was to train newly initiated werewolves, teaching them how to fully harness the power of lycanthropy as they prepared to join Hircine's pack.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Thoreki-8)</sup> It was said that those newly infected with [Sanies Lupinus](Lore_Sanies_Lupinus.md), who had yet to fully transform, could easily track Songamdir by his scent, with the pounding in their blood serving as a guide when they were near.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Thoreki-8)</sup> His powers, bestowed by Hircine, greatly exceeded those of other werewolves—he had complete control over his transformations and, as he stated, was as much wolf as man. He also possessed the ability to take on the form of a true wolf. Songamdir was also known for opening portals to Hircine's realm, the Hunting Grounds, to initiate new pack members chosen from those afflicted with Sanies Lupinus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Quest-4)</sup> Remarkably, he was even able to provide initiations for individuals without souls, such as [Vestiges](Lore_Vestige_(creature).md) though it required something extra to fully induct them into the brotherhood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Hircine-9)</sup> As the Packmaster, he presided over the ritual admission of some of the werewolves, said to be older than the stars. This initiation involved communing with Hircine, a ritual hunt in the Hunting Grounds, and a shared feast of the hunted prey.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Songamdir-1)</sup>

Hircine was known to hunt alongside his own pack within the boundaries of his realm, the Hunting Grounds. One such event took place during Crown Prince [Attrebus Mede](Lore_Attrebus_Mede.md)'s visit to the Hunting Grounds in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), though there is no known evidence to suggest whether Songamdir was present on that occasion.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TIC-10)</sup><sup>:244</sup>
