Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal, also spelled **Ada'Soon Dir-Kamal**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1Sky-1)</sup> was the King of [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(place).md), a nation of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).

In [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), he led an army of his people in an invasion of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), reportedly in search of a person or object roughly translated as the "[Ordained Receptacle](Lore_Ordained_Receptacle.md)".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSAI-2)</sup> Dir-Kamal's army landed in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) near the city of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), which they subsequently besieged and sacked.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JTS-3)</sup> [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md), the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md), was summoned to help repel the invasion of the "Akaviri demon" (sources differ on who summoned him).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JTS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAH-4)</sup> Dir-Kamal decided to bypass [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) and retreat from Skyrim, instead setting his sights on [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) refused to let him get away and pursued him into [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), where his army was crushed between them, a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) army led by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), and a third force of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) Shellbacks.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JTS-3)</sup> Remnants of Dir-Kamal's army survived this battle and resurfaced years later in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where they seized the Imperial Throne from the successors of the Emperor [Attrebus](Lore_Attrebus_(warlord).md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1-E-5)</sup>
