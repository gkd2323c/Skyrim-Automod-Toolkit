[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/OB-place-Lord_Rugdumph%27s_Estate.jpg/200px-OB-place-Lord_Rugdumph%27s_Estate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lord_Rugdumph%27s_Estate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lord_Rugdumph%27s_Estate.jpg) Lord Rugdumph's Estate circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/ON-place-Shurgak_Clan_Estate.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shurgak_Clan_Estate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shurgak_Clan_Estate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shurgak_Clan_Estate.jpg) Shurgak Clan Estate circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lord Rugdumph's Estate (historically called **Shurgak Clan Estate**) is a settlement located in White Fall Valley, in the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is connected to [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) to the south via minor roads.

In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Shurgak Clan Estate was a wealthy [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) stronghold, possessing both a traditional fortified encampment and a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) -style manor house. During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the estate was initially within the territory controlled by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), although the area changed hands many times over the course of the war. The estate was ruled by Warchief Azgarub, and was an entirely Orcish settlement. The Shurgak Clan was hostile to outsiders, and even servants would attack trespassers on sight. Azgarub was killed that year, and a raider named Krodak planned to loot the manor for treasure.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the estate had been inherited by an Orc noble named Lord Rugdumph gro-Shurgak, from whom the estate got its current name. It consisted of a farm and servants quarters, as well as a manor house built in the Cheydinhal style, with influences from [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) architecture. That year, Rugdumph's daughter Lady Rogbut gra-Shurgak was kidnapped by [ogres](Lore_Ogre.md). She was rescued by the local Cheydinhal [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) branch.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
