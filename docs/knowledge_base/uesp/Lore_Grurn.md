Grurn was a [Lich](Lore_Lich.md) warrior who rose out of the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) with an undead army to overtake the Imperial-controlled islet [Firemoth](Lore_Firemoth.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWSellus-1)</sup> Grurn's victory cost the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) control of the entire island, the strategic fort therein, and a legion artifact called the [Ward of Akavir](Lore_Ward_of_Akavir.md), a shield made of [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) skin that Grurn would take to personally wielding. Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Imperial Captain Sellus Gravius tasked the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) with leading a crew of mercenaries to retake the Ward of Akavir and avenge the Legions' previous defeat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWSellus-1)</sup> The Nerevarine and mercenaries were able to rescue J'Hanir, a hostage [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) legionnaire who had survived for years in the island's caverns by eating mushrooms. They then drove back the undead army and confronted the powerful Grurn, who used the Ward in battle but was ultimately struck down. The Nerevarine then returned the shield to the Legion in Vvardenfell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWSAF-2)</sup>
