[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-item-Ring_of_Masser.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Masser.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Masser.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Masser.jpg) Ring of Masser The Ring of Masser is a [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) found on the body of [Gunther Frost-Foot](Skyrim_Gunther.md). It can be exchanged with [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md) for a set of [Daedric mail](Skyrim_Daedric_Mail.md) armor, or kept for personal use. It is a [Khajiiti](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) artifact.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRD-1)</sup> Its appearance is identical to a generic [silver ruby ring](Skyrim_Silver_Ruby_Ring.md). The Ring of Masser cannot be disenchanted.
