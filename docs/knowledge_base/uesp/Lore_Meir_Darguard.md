[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/AR-place-Meir_Darguard.jpg/200px-AR-place-Meir_Darguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Meir_Darguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Meir_Darguard.jpg) Meir Darguard (Arena) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/AR-map-High_Rock_towns.png/200px-AR-map-High_Rock_towns.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-High_Rock_towns.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-High_Rock_towns.png) Map of High Rock Meir Darguard is a settlement located on the west-central region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), southwest of the city-state, [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> The settlement shares its name with the **Darguard Ridge**, in the vicinity of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONTorg-2)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Meir Darguard was an active settlement. It was ruled by Baron Andoryan and had a rivalry with [Kings Guard](Lore_Kings_Guard.md). The only temple in the town was the [Conclave of Riana](Lore_Conclave_of_Riana.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Meir Darguard was neighbored by several settlements, including [Eagle Brook](Lore_Eagle_Brook.md) to the southwest, Meir Thorvale to the east, and [White Haven](Lore_White_Haven.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Cut files from [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) mention that Giant-killer Egil was one of [King Vrage](Lore_Vrage.md)'s most trusted warriors and a terror on the battlefield, until he was slain by a [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) wizard at the Battle of Meri Darguard.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Rivenspire CC-4)</sup>
