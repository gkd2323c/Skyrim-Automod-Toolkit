[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-npc-Revyn_Sadri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Revyn_Sadri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Revyn_Sadri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Revyn_Sadri.jpg) Revyn Sadri having dinner at New Gnisis Cornerclub [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Viola_Giordano.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Viola_Giordano.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viola_Giordano.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viola_Giordano.jpg) Revyn's unintended victim **Revyn Sadri** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Speechcraft Journeyman) living in the city of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), in the Gray Quarter. He owns and runs a small pawn shop, [Sadri's Used Wares](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Used_Wares), which also serves as his home. He becomes a possible [marriage partner](Skyrim_Marriage.md) after you complete his [favor quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:That_Was_Always_There). He is also the brother of the Cruel-Sea clan employee, [Idesa Sadri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Idesa_Sadri).

Revyn Sadri begins his day at 8am by waking up and opening up his shop. He will tend to his store for twelve hours before closing down and having a short nap. At 8:30pm, he will leave his property to go have dinner at the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub). He will finally return to his home and place of business at 12:30am, so that he may sleep. He will continue sleeping until 8am, at which time he will wake up to repeat his schedule.

Revyn wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to defend himself. Besides that, he carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Used_Wares_Key) to his store as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When you approach Revyn, he will assure you of the good nature of his operation.

*"All my goods are legitimate, which is more than I can say for some."* He will also comment on his higher than average success level for a Dark Elf in Windhelm, by claiming he enjoys giving back to the community:

*"I like to share my good fortune with those who deserve it."* He may also advertise newly acquired products by saying:

*"Trader dropped by recently with some lovely pieces, if you'd like to have a look."* Should the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) take Windhelm and end the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), he will make a comment on how he enjoys the new state of affairs:

*"Can't say I mind the sight of all the Imperial guards. It's a change for the better."* He also has a few other greetings.

*"Make yourself at home, please. Let me know if I can be of any assistance."* If you idle in his shop without speaking to him, he may say one of the following:

*"I'm always happy to see a customer, but I won't deal in stolen goods. That's trouble I don't need."* *"Have a look around. I buy and sell just about anything."* You can ask him about his wares.

**Who supplies your goods?** *"I've scraped this collection together by whatever means I could.
I do some business with the [trade caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md), and I've picked up a number of items from adventurers like yourself.
A few intrepid souls even brought some items back from [Morvunskar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morvunskar), the fort southwest of the city."* While working, he will end conversations by saying:

*"Do come again!"* After work hours, he will instead say:

*"Very well."* If you complete **That Was Always There** by doing what he asks, you can ask him to train you in speechcraft.

**I'd like training in the art of Speech.** *"Well, you've come to the right place."* The local [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) and [Aval Atheron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron) will both occasionally direct you to his shop, citing it as a good place to unload unwanted goods.

### Quest-Related Events

### That Was Always There
You can ask him if any of his goods are stolen, to which he will reveal a grave error he's made.

**Nothing in here is stolen? Nothing at all?** *"Of course nothing in here is stolen. Only a careless, shameful, idiotic fetcher would do something as stupid as to buy pilfered goods....
Oh by [Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azura), I've made a terrible mistake. I bought a [gold ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viola%27s_Gold_Ring), and [Viola Giordano](Skyrim_Viola_Giordano.md) has been missing a ring that looks just like it."* You can accept a quest from him at this point.

**I could return the ring for you.** *"It's not that easy. She'd go to the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) if she knew I was even remotely involved. You have to get it to her some other way.
Look, it's dangerous, but if you sneak the ring into her [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viola_Giordano%27s_House). Put in * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * a dresser or something. I will make it worth your while."* You can also reject the quest.

**Tough luck.** *"You're telling me."* If you accept the quest, he'll call out to you as you leave.

*"Don't let Viola catch you in her house."* If you return to him before completing the task, he'll remind you of what to do.

*"Sneak that ring back to Viola before we both get caught."* If you accept the quest, you will either have the option of doing as Revyn says and breaking into her home and returning the ring, or just returning the ring to her directly. If you reveal the truth to Viola, she will state that she intends to punish Revyn for his crime, and give you some gold as compensation as well as allow you to marry her in the future. Revyn's disposition towards you will drop due to this, but he will still offer his merchant services.

Completing the quest by planting the ring will have him instead ask if the job is done when you next visit him.

*"Have you taken care of that... little problem?"* **I placed Viola Giordano's ring in her house.** *"Thank you. Thank you! Azura's Prophecy always guide you to fortune.
Speaking of fortune. Here. Everything I earned from my last shipment. It's yours."* If you complete the quest by planting the ring, he will gracefully thank you for your services with gold. You will then be able to accept [speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md) from him up until level 50, and gain the ability to marry him.

### Unused Dialogue
Revyn originally played a role in [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md), as you were meant to come across a set of special robes during your investigation in Hjerim, and showing them to Viola would result in her suggesting you visit Revyn. An empty topic for this remains in the files:

**Do you know anything about these robes?** And while the dialogue isn't actually linked, this response from Revyn was very likely meant to follow:

*"Are you joking? Even I wouldn't sell something like that.
They definitely have some magic in them, I can tell you that much. But it's nothing I know about."*

**Revyn:** *"Well, I'll want to meet them next time they're around. Maybe you can give an introduction."* **Niranye:** *"I'll consider it. Many of the people I deal with are... secretive. You know how it is."* **Revyn:** *"Of course! I undersand * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * completely. The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
- His shop may be locked during normal business hours, and entering will count as trespassing, although Revyn is standing at the counter. - Wait for a day to get him to reopen his shop.
- If Revyn dies, [Aval Atheron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aval_Atheron) is supposed to take over as the new shopkeeper at the store. Unfortunately, Aval has his own market stall already and therefore cannot actually take over the post. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. [Idesa Sadri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Idesa_Sadri) has been selected to fill the backup alias and will take over when Revyn dies.
- If you marry him and opt to move in with him, he may no longer offer vendor or training services, although everything in his shop will be marked as owned by you. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. It was due to a bad dialogue condition check.
