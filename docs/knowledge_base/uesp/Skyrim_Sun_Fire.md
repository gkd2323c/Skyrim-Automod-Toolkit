- *Ball of sunlight that does 25 points of damage to undead.*

**Sun Fire** is an apprentice level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), which will do 25 damage to any [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) target over 2 seconds. It has no effect on non-undead.

## Effects
- [Sun Bane](Skyrim_Sun_Damage.md), 25 pts

## Perks
- [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md) increases magnitude by 25% and duration by 50%.
- [Restoration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Restoration_Dual_Casting.md) increases duration by 120%, but does **not** increase effect level.

## Notes
- As a counterbalance to only affecting undead, Sun Fire has a significantly lessened cost per damage ratio compared to [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md). Even with two points of Augmented Flames and all other things equal, Firebolt cannot match the efficiency that Sun Fire has, especially after accounting for the high degree of afterburn damage that Sun Fire inflicts. - Due to plenty of enemies in Skyrim being undead - namely, the [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) - this makes Sun Fire very valuable for any mage character to do much more damage than they normally could with Destruction spells.
- This spell is normally unavailable if you join the vampires, as [Sorine Jurard](Skyrim_Sorine_Jurard.md) will be hostile to you. However, if you use a [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(effect).md) spell or item, you can calm her down so that she'll sell it to you. You must do this before you get quest [Destroying the Dawnguard](Skyrim_Destroying_the_Dawnguard.md) by [Garan Marethi](Skyrim_Garan_Marethi.md) or [Fura Bloodmouth](Skyrim_Fura_Bloodmouth.md).
- [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md) has dialogue that indicates the Sun Fire spell can be used to open old Dawnguard caches. While the [caches](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Cache_Key.md) exist, there is nothing linking them to Sun Fire in any way.

## Bugs
- The spell tome for this spell looks like a [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) book, but it is actually Restoration. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
- This spell will not damage reanimated enemies unless they were classed as undead *before* being killed. This is due to a missing condition check in the spell's settings (the spell only checks for the Actor Type Undead keyword instead of also checking the Is Undead condition). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- There is a copy of this spell underneath the enchanting room in Fort Dawnguard. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.3, addresses this issue. The book has been moved to a shelf near the enchanting table.
