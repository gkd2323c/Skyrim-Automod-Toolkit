[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/DF-map-Tulune.png/200px-DF-map-Tulune.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Tulune.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Tulune.png) Map of Tulune Tulune is a coastal city-state of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Situated at the most western end of the province, it is bordered by [Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Glenumbra_Moors_(barony).md) to the north, [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md) to the east, and [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) to the southeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The largest city is the city of Tulune. The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline is the [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md), and the regional deity is [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

## History
During the Third Era, it was an independent barony, but after the Warp in the West, it became a vassal of the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). One known export of Tulune is carrots.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## City of Tulune
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/DF-Location-14956-Automap.png/200px-DF-Location-14956-Automap.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-14956-Automap.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-14956-Automap.png) Map of Tulune ca. 3E 405 Tulune is the walled city of its namesake barony, located in the western part of the barony. It has several shops where people can buy and sell goods, which include: general stores, weapon smiths, armorers, and potion shops. The city has multiple guild halls, such as the Fighters Guild, Mages Guild, Thieves Guild, and Dark Brotherhood. The city's patron deity - Julianos - has a temple dedicated to him.
