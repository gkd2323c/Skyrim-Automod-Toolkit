[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-npc-Coldhearted_Gravedigger.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Coldhearted_Gravedigger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Coldhearted_Gravedigger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Coldhearted_Gravedigger.jpg) Coldhearted Gravedigger The **Coldhearted Gravedigger** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm).md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). She is one of ten possible targets.

The Gravedigger wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd) and a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat.

## Related Quests
