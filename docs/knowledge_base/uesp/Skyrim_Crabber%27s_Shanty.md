[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty.jpg/200px-SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty.jpg) Exterior of the shack **Crabber's Shanty** is a small run-down [shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) on the [River Hjaal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal).

The shack is home to a lone [fisherman](Skyrim_Fisherman.md). It contains only one zone, which is the exterior zone *[Crabber's Shanty](#Crabber.27s_Shanty)*.

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crabber%27s_Shanty_02.jpg) Inside the shanty There are two doorways into the shanty and several holes in the roof and a large glassless window in the wall, making it barely better than sleeping outside. One doorway faces east and the other faces west. There are several sacks and a barrel containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) around the shanty; one small sack outside the south side of the shanty isn't marked as owned and therefore can be emptied freely. Outside the doorway to the east on a small piece of decking is an open crate containing two dead [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) that can be harvested freely. The fisherman seems to spend all of his time sitting on the edge of the decking. There is also a [bucket](Skyrim_Bucket.md) and an unsearchable barrel near the fisherman. There is a [clam](Skyrim_Clam.md) in the stream near the shanty that can be harvested for [clam meat](Skyrim_Clam_Meat.md), while several mudcrabs are found up and down the stream from here.

### Crabber's Shanty
Inside the shanty, there is a single owned bed in the southeastern corner. Against the west wall is a small unit holding another dead mudcrab that can be freely harvested. To the right of this is a basket containing three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). Against the north wall is a set of shelves holding three [tankards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard), bottles of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md), two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), three [carrots](Skyrim_Carrot.md), and six [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato). The tankards and bottles of ale can be freely taken, but the vegetables are owned.

## Notes
- The barrels in the house are [respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md); however, the sacks are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the small sack, tankards, and bottles of ale as owned like everything else.
- The [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) Creation adds a [fishing rod](Skyrim_Fishing_Rod.md), a pair of fishing boots, and a book.
- Version 4.3.3 marks everything added with the Fishing Creation as owned like everything else.

## Bugs
- [Fast traveling](Skyrim_Fast_Travel.md) to this location can sometimes cause the player to spawn high in the air, only to fall to their death.
- Crabber's Shanty is erroneously marked as being in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. It has been set to be part of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) instead.
