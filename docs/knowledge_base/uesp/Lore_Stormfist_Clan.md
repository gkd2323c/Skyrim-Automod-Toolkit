The Stormfist Clan was a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) clan of [Whiterun Tundra](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md), known for their prowess at battle and armor crafting. Their founder and ancestor was Ogra Stormfist, the powerful matriarch who ruled the clan for nearly fifty years. The clan's involvement was pivotal in numerous conflicts, such as in the Battle of Whiterun Hold and the Massacre at Dialmarch. However, their engagement in the Siege of Windhelm during the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md) in [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572) ended in their downfall and isolation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSC-1)</sup>

Before the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) Invasion, members of the clan befriended [Fildgor Strong-Prince](Lore_Fildgor.md), son of Queen [Mabjaarn Flame-Hair](Lore_Mabjaarn_Flame-Hair.md) of Eastern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Later, thirteen of the strongest and bravest of the Stormfists joined him in his coming-of-age trials.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ET-2)</sup> Now known as the Stormfist Brigade, the team garnered legendary reputation as fierce adventurers who vanquished bandits and monsters and looted treasures. They were instrumental in helping to fend off the Akaviri, and stood by Fildgor when he lay claim to the throne left by his sister Queen [Nurnhilde](Lore_Nurnhilde.md). However, his brother [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md) objected, and to prevent civil war, the two clashed steel in single combat. Fildgor was defeated and exiled by Jorunn, who chastened the Stormfists for supporting him.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBW-3)</sup> The clan refused to obey him or even acknowledge his newfound authority, and isolated themselves in their domain to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSC-1)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Fildgor returned from exile with an army of Stormfists and [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), ready to usurp Jorunn.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESO-4)</sup>
