## The North Wind
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windhelm_Fishing.jpg) Fishing supplies The vessel has several crates and unusable barrels on deck, along with a couple of chairs. You will find both the captain, [Kjar](Skyrim_Kjar.md), and [Dalan Merchad](Skyrim_Dalan_Merchad.md) on deck at all times; however, the other member of the crew, [Voldsea Giryon](Skyrim_Voldsea_Giryon.md), will be found wandering [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), and will happily speak to you, explaining that she's glad they are laying over in Windhelm for a while, and that she likes to walk when on land to keep her legs strong. Speaking with [Dalan Merchad](Skyrim_Dalan_Merchad.md), on the other hand, has him complaining and wishing they were underway. The captain will ask you to assist him with a [problem](Skyrim_Harsh_Master.md), and will reward you with a year's salary (a leveled amount of gold) if you accept and complete the quest.
