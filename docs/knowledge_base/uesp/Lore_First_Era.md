**Note:** The First Era of Tamriel is also sometimes referred to as the *First Age.*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWEnnbjof-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HA-2)</sup> The early-to-middle part is known as the *Classical Period*.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HR-3)</sup>

## First Century
1E 0 — The [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) is founded by [King Eplear](Lore_Eplear.md). - The Camoran Dynasty of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) is founded by King Eplear.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3V-4)</sup>
- The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) use this newly formed kingdom as a buffer from the influence of the [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) ([Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md)) kingdom. The [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) becomes an independent city-state, and the heart of the Ayleid Empire.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BTAOM-5)</sup>

1E 20 — The [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) first appears in recorded history. - The [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) sage and author [Voernet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Voernet) writes of his trip to the Isle of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) to meet with [Iachesis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iachesis), the Ritemaster of the Psijics, the counselors of kings and proponents of the "Elder Way," taught to them by the [original race](Lore_Ehlnofey.md) that inhabited [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The historical account makes Voernet one of the earliest known Bretons in history.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FOA-6)</sup>

1E 68 — The last [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) "invasion" arrives in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). - Two Atmoran ships laden with corpses beg to make port in Tamriel, marking the final hostile action from the continent.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-7)</sup>

## Second Century
1E 113 — [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) is born.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> 1E 140 — [Harald](Lore_Harald.md)'s forces stumble upon and attack [Forelhost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Forelhost), the last stronghold of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md). - When Harald's army breaches the stronghold, most of the cultists commit suicide by poisoning the stronghold's water supply, which also ends up killing much of the attacking force.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SSSJ-8)</sup>

1E 143 — [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) is crowned High King of the Nords. - Harald, the 13th descendant of the legendary [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s line, relinquishes his ancestral holdings in Atmora, and consolidates Skyrim as an independent kingdom with [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) as its capital. He is later recognized as the first historical ruler of the Nords.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FC-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PKH-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup>

1E 184 — King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) establishes the first Moot of the Nords.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONKBP-12)</sup> 1E 198 — The Scouring of Wendelbek. - The [Narfinsel Schism](Lore_Narfinsel_Schism.md), a late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) internal conflict pitting [Aedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aedra) -worshiping [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) clans against [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshiping clans, comes to a climax at [Wendelbek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wendelbek). King [Glinferen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Glinferen) of [Atatar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atatar) led a combined force of Daedraphile warriors against the traditionalist [Barsaebics](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barsaebics) of Ayleidoon. The Barsaebics were driven out of the [Heartland](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heartland) into northwest [Argonia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Argonia), ending organized opposition to Daedra-worship in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ASIV-13)</sup>

## Third Century
1E 200 — [Khosey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khosey) writes the [Tamrilean Tractates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tamrilean_Tractates). - Khosey transcribes a firsthand account of the discovery of the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) by a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) hunting party.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-PGE1High-14)</sup> The Tractates also mention what is believed to be the earliest recorded ancestor of [House Tharn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tharn), one "[Tharanus Ye Redde-Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tharanus_Ye_Redde-Hand)", who historians assert may have served the Ayleids as a clerk.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-HTON-15)</sup>

1E 221 — High King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) dies at the age of 108. - With Harald's death, his eldest son [Hjalmer](Lore_Hjalmer.md) becomes the next High King of Skyrim.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FC-9)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup>

1E 222 — High King [Hjalmer](Lore_Hjalmer.md) dies. - With Hjalmer's death, the second son of Harald, [Vrage the Gifted](Lore_Vrage.md), assumes the throne of Skyrim.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-KE10-16)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>

1E 227 — The Gradual Massacre occurs. - One of the participants in the massacre was the notorious [Tharhan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tharhan) the Mutilant. Later scholars theorized that Tharhan and the [Tharn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tharn) ancestor Tharanus Ye Redde-Hand were one and the same.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-HTON-15)</sup>

1E 240 — The [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) Conquests occur. - From 1E 222 - 272, the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) expands across [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), swallowing them up by 1E 240.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FC-9)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-KE10-16)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-PGE1S-17)</sup> Some sources interpret the Skyrim Conquests to have run from 1E 240 to [1E 415](#1E_416).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-TLST-18)</sup>

1E 242 — The [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) erupts in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). - [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md), also known as the Slave Queen, leads the predominantly human slaves against the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) Empire, with the help of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and some rebel Ayleid rulers. They eventually conquer all of Cyrodiil.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-SATD-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-PGE3C-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TSOP-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup>

1E 243 — Taking of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-PGE3All-23)</sup> - The [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) finally falls into the hands of the rebels, marking the end of Ayleid supremacy in Cyrodiil, and the Slave Queen [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) founds the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). The remaining Ayleids that opposed Alessia seek refuge with [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)'s [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) and the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), while the rebel Ayleid lords who sided with Alessia continue to rule parts of Cyrodiil as vassals of the new Empress Alessia.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup> Some famous human heroes are [Morihaus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morihaus), who leads the attack of White-Gold Tower, and [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md), who single-handedly defeats many of the Ayleid kings, including the Ayleids' demigod champion [Umaril the Unfeathered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Umaril_the_Unfeathered).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TSOP-21)</sup>
- From 1E 243 to [498](#1E_498), the new kingdom of humans, under the rule of Empress Alessia and her descendants, prospers and rules the nearest kingdoms of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md). They build a strong national identity as they gradually become more united. The Empress Alessia creates the new religion of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), fusing the pantheons of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and Cyrodiilic tribes, who followed [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) deities.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-SATD-19)</sup>

1E 244 — [Kyne's Aegis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kyne%27s_Aegis) is founded by Fuldimar Hookhand.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ONload Kyne-24)</sup> 1E 246 — [Daggerfall City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daggerfall) is founded.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup> - The north half of the [Iliac Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iliac_Bay), including the current province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), is conquered by invading [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), who bring a rough sort of civilization with them. One of the Nords' first civilized acts is a census - the *[Book of Life](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Book_of_Life)*. Listed on page 933 of the book is this entry: "North of the Highest bluffs, south of the moors, west of the hills, and east of the sea is called DAGGERFALL. 110 men, 93 women, 13 children under 8 years of age, 58 cows, 7 bulls, 63 chickens, 11 cocks, 38 hogs live here."<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-AHOD-25)</sup>

1E 266 — Empress Alessia dies.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-COTF-26)</sup> - [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) (or [Shezarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shezarr) in the form of Pelinal<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TSOP-21)</sup>) arrives at the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and transforms the dying Alessia into the first of the Cyrodilic saints. Thus, Empress Alessia becomes the first gem in the Cyrodilic [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md). This is the beginning of the Covenant between the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) and Akatosh, which helps to protect [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-TOSA-27)</sup><sup>[[28]](#cite_note-TAOK-28)</sup>

## Fourth Century
Undated — Rise of the Alessian Order - In the early 300s, many surviving Ayleid communities in human-ruled areas were obliterated one by one by the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order), founded by the prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup>

1E 330 — The [Druids of Galen](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md) flee [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) - Seeking self-governance without the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md)'s interference, as well as freedom from the religious persecution coming from the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order), the Druids of Galen leave High Rock and go on an exodus to the [Systres Archipelago](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Systres_Archipelago).<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-ONWD-29)</sup><sup>[[30]](#cite_note-ONSHV1-30)</sup>

1E 340 — [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) forms a trade treaty with the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3V-4)</sup> 1E 355 — Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) rises to prominence. - Clan Direnni, the only known [High Elven](Lore_Altmer.md) ruling family remaining in human lands, claims power in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) through political maneuvering and various machinations.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup> However, some consider their rule in the years leading up to its establishment as part of the Direnni Hegemony as well.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-ONSHV1-30)</sup><sup>[[31]](#cite_note-EOTD-31)</sup><sup>[[32]](#cite_note-ESODR-32)</sup>

1E 358 — Skyrim attacks the Western Reach - Emperor Ami-El of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) lends his forces to Skyrim in a military campaign against the Direnni Hegemony in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md). Anti-Aldmer sentiments rise as Skyrim loses more and more of Greater Bretony to the Direnni.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-33)</sup>

1E 361 — The [Alessian Doctrines](Lore_Alessian_Doctrines.md) are enforced. - The [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order) becomes the dominant political factor in the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), and their doctrines are enforced throughout [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup> The doctrines impose the very strict religious teachings of the prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md), who, after reportedly receiving visions from the ghost of the Slave Queen [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md), begins to question Elven rule, calling for a suppression of Elven elements within the pantheon of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md).<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE1C-34)</sup> The Order enforces strict codes of behavior which preclude most forms of entertainment.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup> The remaining [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) lordships, once tolerated by the Alessian Empire, are abolished.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup>

1E 369 — High King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md) dies and the [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) begins<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup>. - When High King Borgas of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) is killed during the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) and the [Moot](Lore_Moot.md) fails to elect [Jarl Hanse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jarl_Hanse) as the next ruler of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the War of Succession erupts across the [Nordic Empire](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md). The conflict leads to the Empire's dissolution as its territories in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) break away. The war ends in [1E 420](#1E_420) with the [Pact of Chieftains](Lore_Pact_of_Chieftains.md).<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-AHOD-25)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-PGE1S-17)</sup>

1E 374 — The Ayleid vassal-state of [Nenalata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nenalata) is massacred by the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order). - Nenalata, the last remaining Ayleid city in Cyrodiil is given an ultimatum to leave the province or face extermination. King [Laloriaran Dynar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Laloriaran_Dynar) gathers those who wished to leave and begins an exodus to the lands of the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md). Shortly afterwards the Alessian Army arrives at Nenalata and massacres all who choose to stay behind.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup><sup>[[35]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-36)</sup>

1E 376 — The traditional [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) emperors are overthrown. - The traditional tenets of the Yokudan Empire are overthrown, and although each successive emperor remains a figurehead for the empire, his powers are very much reduced. The [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) people see 300 years of almost continuous civil war between provincial lords, warrior monks, and brigands, all fighting each other for land and power before the appearance of the hero [Hunding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frandar_Hunding).<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-RTHATH-37)</sup><sup>[[nb 3]](#intnote_nb3)</sup>

1E 393 — The [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order) sacks [Malada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Malada), an ancient [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) temple.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-COTF-26)</sup>
## Fifth Century
1E 416 — The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) are driven out of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). - The united [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) drive the Nords, weakened from the [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md), out of modern-day Morrowind, which was then called [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md). [Nerevar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nerevar) and [Dumac](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dumac) become leaders of the [First Council](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:First_Council) of Resdayn, and it flourishes under their leadership. Peace among the Chimer and the Dwemer brings unprecedented prosperity to their land.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-PGE3All-23)</sup><sup>[[37]](#cite_note-PGE1M-38)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>
- The Dwemer clan [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) rebel against this alliance with the Chimer and self-exile themselves. According to legend, "the Rourken chieftain threw his mighty hammer, [Volendrung](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Volendrung), across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), promising to lead his clansmer to 'wherever it should fall.'" The land where they would arrive was thus called Volenfell (i.e., [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)).<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-39)</sup>

1E 420 — The [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) ends. - The Skyrim War of Succession ends with the Pact of Chieftains. The war resulted in Skyrim losing control of the other provinces in northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and though their borders continue to shift occasionally, they never regain their former stature.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-PGE1S-17)</sup>
- [Olaf One-Eye](Lore_Olaf_One-Eye.md) is crowned High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-PKO-40)</sup>

1E 420 — Clan Rourken settles in Volenfell.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-41)</sup> 1E 448 — [Rislav Larich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rislav_Larich) is born.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup> 1E 452 — Olaf One-Eye dies.<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-PKO-40)</sup> 1E 461 — Coronation of Emperor [Gorieus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorieus) of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). - The ceremony is attended by many of the most famous people of the time, including [Rislav Larich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rislav_Larich), [Darloc Brae](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Darloc_Brae), [Kjoric the White](Lore_Kjoric_the_White.md), [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md), [Indoril Nerevar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nerevar), [Dumac Dwarfking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dumac), and [Ryain Direnni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ryain_Direnni).<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup>

After 1E 461 — Ryain Direnni buys Balfiera and begins a process of gradually conquering vast parts of High Rock, Hammerfell and Skyrim.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup> 1E 463 — The destruction of [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md)'s Grand Archive - A group of [Merrunz](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Merrunz) cultists burn the city's archives, destroying most of the Elsweyr's [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) records.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-Oot KMT-42)</sup>

1E 470 — Approximate date of the discovery of the [Flask of Lillandril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Flask_of_Lillandril). - A group of elves living in the port city of [Lillandril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lillandril) discover a magical flask with the ability to absorb all magic cast at it.<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-EA-43)</sup>

1E 472 — [Skingrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skingrad) and [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) cease feuding. - After decades of skirmishing over common territory, Skingrad and Kvatch reach a peaceful resolution, presumably due to a royal marriage.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup>

1E 477 — After years of fighting, Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) conquers [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and large parts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup><sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup> 1E 478 — [Kjoric the White](Lore_Kjoric_the_White.md) dies. - The High King of Skyrim is killed fighting the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in the [Battle of Sungard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Battle_of_Sungard).<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup>

1E 478 — King [Rislav Larich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rislav_Larich) of [Skingrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skingrad) routs an [Alessian](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) army led by Emperor [Gorieus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorieus). - Following a plague that swept through Western [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), King Mhorus Larich of Skingrad dies and the throne of Skingrad passes to his eldest surviving son, Dorald, an Alessian priest who immediately cedes the kingdom to the Empire. Outraged, Dorald's younger brother, Rislav, executes Dorald and is crowned the new king. Emperor Gorieus mobilizes his army to reclaim Skingrad for the Empire, but his forces are ultimately routed by Rislav's well-positioned archers and the timely assistance of King Justinius of Kvatch. Rislav's feat inspires additional acts of defiance against the Empire: the other kings of the Colovian West form an alliance with Skingrad and Kvatch, Ryain Direnni outlaws the Alessian Reform in High Rock and mounts incursions into Imperial territory, and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) under High King [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md) joins the resistance.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RTR-35)</sup>

1E 480 — [Ysmir Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) takes the throne in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[43]](#cite_note-PKW-44)</sup> 1E 480 — The [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) push back the [Alessians](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). - [Aiden Direnni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aiden_Direnni) leads a series of minor but important victories against the Alessians in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[44]](#cite_note-TFL-45)</sup>

1E 482 — The [Battle of Glenumbria Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbria_Moors.md) - The battle between the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order) and the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) Elves of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), under the command of [Aiden Direnni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aiden_Direnni). The Order is soundly defeated by the Elves at the cost of much of the Elven power in High Rock. The [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) High King [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md) is killed during the battle.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-22)</sup>
- The province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), under the new High King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md), submits to a fiery reinstatement of the traditional [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) pantheon. Alessian temples are destroyed, and remnants of its priesthood seek refuge in the [Heartlands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heartlands).<sup>[[45]](#cite_note-FSOKW-46)</sup>

1E 484 — [The Hollow City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Hollow_City) is created. - After a trap by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), a city near Nenalata is pushed through [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and into [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). Wards from [Meridia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Meridia) prevent Bal from interfering with the city, creating a permanent pocket free of his influence within Bal's own realm.<sup>[[35]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-36)</sup><sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Groundskeeper-47)</sup>

1E 2241 — [Bendu Olo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bendu_Olo) proclaims his intent to punish [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) for the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md). - The Colovian Sailor-King of Anvil petitions the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) for commission and received it in short order. With the blessing of the emperor, Olo claimed the title of baron-admiral of the Imperial fleet and began assembling an armada of retaliation.<sup>[[93]](#cite_note-ONSHV3-95)</sup>

1E 2243 — Bendu Olo begins building the All Flags Navy fleet of ships. - Within two years of his proclamation, the baron-admiral and the founding members of the All Flags Navy arrive in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) Archipelago and begin assembling the Systres Shipyards in [Gonfalon Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gonfalon_Bay).<sup>[[93]](#cite_note-ONSHV3-95)</sup>

1E 2249 — Bendu Olo completes his first compliment of All Flags Navy ships - The naval base and the All Flags fleet stand ready to make war upon the Sload.<sup>[[93]](#cite_note-ONSHV3-95)</sup>

1E 2250 — The All Flags Navy wages war on the Sload - What began as skirmishes in the early 1E 2250s transitioned to all-out war by 1E 2258.<sup>[[93]](#cite_note-ONSHV3-95)</sup>

1E 2260 — The [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) launch their final assault on Thras - After claiming several major victories at sea against the Sload and their great sea beasts, Olo and his great fleet (comprising ships from every nation of Tamriel) sails against the Sload in retribution for the Thrassian Plague, sinking their kingdom with mighty magics.<sup>[[93]](#cite_note-ONSHV3-95)</sup><sup>[[78]](#cite_note-TRHOKE-80)</sup><sup>[[94]](#cite_note-JOT-96)</sup>

## Twenty-Fourth Century
1E 2305 — [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) secedes from the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). - In response to the excesses of the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order), High Rock secedes from the Alessian Empire, over a thousand years after being admitted. The [Bangkorai Garrison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bangkorai_Garrison) successfully fends off the wrathful Legions of Piety and Grace under Abbot-General [Priscus Mactator](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Priscus_Mactator), who had been sent to forcibly reclaim the wayward region.<sup>[[71]](#cite_note-BSOHR-73)</sup>

1E 2321 — The [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md) begins. - The War of Righteousness breaks out when Western [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) isolates itself from the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) and establishes the [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md). This follows years of internal strife within the [Alessian Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alessian_Order)'s bloated priesthood and territorial movements to curtail or outlaw the Order's religion.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE1C-34)</sup><sup>[[26]](#cite_note-COTF-26)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-TLST-18)</sup>

1E 2327 — The [Systres Isles](Lore_Systres.md) is purchased by a consortium of High Rock coin-barons. - The [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md)' wealth was completely exhausted by 1E 2326. To raise additional capital for the war, Colovian kings began selling non-essential territory, including the Systres Isles. Led by Duchess Martinne Guimard, coin-barons purchased the archipelago for an undisclosed sum.<sup>[[95]](#cite_note-ONSHV4-97)</sup>

1E 2331 — The War of Righteousness ends. - After a decade of conflict, the War of Righteousness concludes and the Alessian Order and Empire are dissolved. The Empire's former territories solidify their independence, and its heartland, Cyrodiil, splits into Eastern and Western halves that continue to diverge culturally over the following centuries.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE1C-34)</sup>

## Twenty-Fifth Century
1E 2484 — [Mount Firesong](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mount_Firesong) erupts for the second time in recorded history. - The catastrophic explosion shattered the ancient shipyards of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), completely destroyed a burgeoning settlement on [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md), cracked the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) monument, and claimed the lives of nearly one-third of the archipelago's inhabitants.<sup>[[96]](#cite_note-ONSHV5-98)</sup>

post-1E 2484 — The Green Years - A short but significant renaissance of [druidic](Lore_Druid.md) faith in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) following the eruption of Mount Firesong. The [Stonelore Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stonelore_Circle) saved the island folk from the brink of death and helped them create a sprawling pastoral and agricultural community that supplanted the rule of the noble class and coin-barons.<sup>[[96]](#cite_note-ONSHV5-98)</sup>

## Twenty-Eighth Century
1E 2702 — [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) moves to Gardners Estate. - The entire population of the city is forced to move into the walled estate of the Gardners as protection against pirates, [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) raiders, and the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md).<sup>[[67]](#cite_note-WJOTB-69)</sup>

1E 2703 — The First [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) Invasion occurs - [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) of Akavir land in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), cutting a swathe of destruction through [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), spurring [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman.md) to rally the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovia.md) and [Nibenean East](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nibenay_Valley). Cyrodiil is united for the first time in centuries. The unified army defeats the Akaviri at the [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) along the Cyrodiil-Skyrim border.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-7)</sup> Some sources claim the invasion began in 1E 2700.<sup>[[97]](#cite_note-LOTD-99)</sup>

1E 2703 — The [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) is established - Following the defeat of the Akaviri by [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I, the Cyrodiils find themselves not only united as a nation, but responsible for the further protection of the northern human kingdoms at large.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE1C-34)</sup>

1E 2704 — Emperor [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I divides [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) between [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) - In an attempt to limit the possibility of the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) uniting against him, Emperor Reman Cyrodiil divided their territory between the provinces of Skyrim and High Rock. The natives resisted fiercely, and there was never a decade when the troops of Solitude or Evermore wouldn't be sent in an attempt to pacify them.<sup>[[98]](#cite_note-EGTNBat M-100)</sup>

1E 2704 — The Green Years are abolished by Emperor [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I - On 11 Sun's Dusk, emissaries of the newly formed [Reman Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_Empire) arrive on the shores of [Gonfalon Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gonfalon_Bay) and decree that the archipelago is once again the lawful property of Cyrodiil before reinstating the Breton coin-barons as its rulers.<sup>[[99]](#cite_note-ONSHV6-101)</sup>

1E 2714 — [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) is conquered by the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md). - After centuries of unrelenting warfare along the border with [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and a plague from [Thras](Lore_Thras.md), the land of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) finally falls to the Second Empire.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3V-4)</sup> Although the province became occupied by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and the Kingdom of Grahtwood was established, they continued to experience resistance from some natives for several years.<sup>[[100]](#cite_note-TFARORB-102)</sup><sup>[[101]](#cite_note-ONload Golden-103)</sup>

1E 2729 — The city-state of [Southpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Southpoint) was founded on the 1st of Rain's Hand. - After Governor [Zantonius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zantonius) had fought a raid of [Wood Orcs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wood_Orcs) at [Reman's Bluff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman%27s_Bluff), he was awarded a plot of land for a fort. But he converted it into a fortified settlement, which later became Southpoint.<sup>[[102]](#cite_note-Southpoint-104)</sup>

1E 2762 — Emperor [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I dies.<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup> 1E 2762 — Emperor [Kastav](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kastav) is crowned.<sup>[[104]](#cite_note-HKEAYQ-106)</sup> 1E 2786 — Reman II is born.<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup> 1E 2790 — [Tjurhane Fyrre](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tjurhane_Fyrre) is born. - The great Ayleid (Wild Elf) sage Tjurhane Fyrre is born.<sup>[[105]](#cite_note-TWE-107)</sup>

## Twenty-Ninth Century
1E 2801 — Emperor [Kastav](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kastav) takes hostages in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). - Emperor Kastav orders the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) to seize hostages from [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) and [Hroldan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hroldan) to ensure that the jarls would meet their conscription quotas. This is the latest in a series of such orders, and the official objections of the Master at [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) are denied per usual.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2804 — The [Winterhold Rebellion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Winterhold_Rebellion) begins. - A rebellion breaks out in Winterhold. The [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) refuses orders to be sent to suppress the revolt, as it violates their Oath of Allegiance.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2804 — Emperor [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) assumes the throne of Cyrodiil. - Reman II deposes the incompetent [Kastav](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kastav) and is credited with negotiating an end to the Winterhold Rebellion and leading the Second Empire into a golden age. He would later begin a war of conquest against Argonia and Morrowind.<sup>[[107]](#cite_note-RTLOA-109)</sup> His coronation took place later in 1E 2812.<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup>

1E 2805 — [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) is besieged. - An [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) commander named [Kalien](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kalien), rejected from the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), is sent to suppress the Winterhold Rebellion. The locals soon besiege the Dragonguard's temple in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), as they do not distinguish between Akaviri. The siege is lifted one year later.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2811 — The [Blackwater War](Lore_Blackwater_War.md) begins. - A military expansion is launched by [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) against Argonia, eventually resulting in the formation of the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[108]](#cite_note-TBW-110)</sup><sup>[[81]](#cite_note-PGE3A-83)</sup>

1E 2812 — Kastav dies. - After living in exile for several years, the deposed emperor dies in the [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) region of Black Marsh.<sup>[[109]](#cite_note-EKE-111)</sup>

1E 2812 — Construction of [Alduin's Wall](Lore_Alduin%27s_Wall.md) begins. - The construction of an elaborate sculpture called "Alduin's Wall" at [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) begins.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2813 — Cyrodilic replaces [High Elven](Lore_Aldmeris.md). - Across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), Cyrodilic, the precursor to the modern Tamrielic language, replaces High Elven as the language of all legal documents.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDC-11)</sup>

1E 2815 — Reports of [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) persist in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). - Though the number of dragons in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) had been diminishing since the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md) thousands of years before, the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) at [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) continue to receive sporadic, unverifiable reports of dragons to the east.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2818 — Construction of Alduin's Wall is completed. - Emperor [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) himself arrives at [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) to officially dedicate the Wall and consecrate the Blood Seal of the temple.<sup>[[106]](#cite_note-AOTD-108)</sup>

1E 2820 — [Eric of Guis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eric_of_Guis) lives among the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md). - The emissary of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) wrote many vitriolic rants against the denizens of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) during his time there. These were later accepted as "reliable" accounts by human scholars of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[110]](#cite_note-PGE1Ald-112)</sup>

1E 2820 — Governor Pomptinus is appointed the ruler of [Craglorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Craglorn) by [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) - During his administration he built the sewers beneath [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md), considered to one of his greatest public works undertaken.<sup>[[111]](#cite_note-ONloadelin-113)</sup>

1E 2828 — The "Great Burn" of [Gideon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gideon) happens.<sup>[[112]](#cite_note-114)</sup> 1E 2837 — The province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) is created. - The [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) succeeds in seizing a large part of the swamplands of Argonia to create the province of Black Marsh. Its provincial status was revoked after the dissolution of the Second Empire.<sup>[[92]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-94)</sup>

1E 2840 — The [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md) begins. - [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) declares war on [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The war drags on for 80 years.<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup>

1E 2843 — Emperor [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II) dies. - According to the inscription on his tomb in Sancre Tor, he dies "in battle against the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md)".<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup>

1E 2843 — Emperor [Brazollus Dor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brazollus_Dor) is crowned. - The little-known Brazollus Dor disdains the responsibilities of governing, preferring to spend his time at his country estate near [Skingrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skingrad) while his Potentate, [Sidri-Ashak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sidri-Ashak), handled all matters of state. This is said to be the emperor's wisest decision, as Sidri-Ashak proved to be a gifted administrator and man of integrity.<sup>[[104]](#cite_note-HKEAYQ-106)</sup>

1E 2871 — [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) slay the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Krahjotdaan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Krahjotdaan) in the southern [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md).<sup>[[113]](#cite_note-AOD-115)</sup> 1E 2877 — Emperor [Reman III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_III) is crowned.<sup>[[103]](#cite_note-OBSancre-105)</sup> 1E 2899 — Empress [Tavia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tavia) is accused of treason and imprisoned in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). - [Reman III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_III), the last emperor of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md), sends his wife to a prison in [Gideon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gideon).<sup>[[81]](#cite_note-PGE3A-83)</sup>

## Thirtieth Century
1E 2902 — Reign of Jinninji-ri, [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[114]](#cite_note-ONGShazah-116)</sup> 1E 2906 — The Khuras mines collapse. - The extensive mines in [Glenumbra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Glenumbra), once famous as a source of crystals and gems, are abandoned after a ceiling collapse renders most of the tunnels inaccessible.<sup>[[115]](#cite_note-ONloadkhu-117)</sup>

1E 2911 — The [War of the Uvichil](Lore_War_of_the_Uvichil.md) begins.<sup>[[88]](#cite_note-PGE3Sum-90)</sup> 1E 2917 — The War of the Uvichil ends.<sup>[[88]](#cite_note-PGE3Sum-90)</sup> 1E 2920 — [Gilverdale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gilverdale) is destroyed by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). - The [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) is summoned by an amateur to destroy the entire settlement.<sup>[[116]](#cite_note-2920L-118)</sup>

1E 2920 — The last year of the First Era. - Emperor [Reman III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_III) and his son and heir Prince [Juilek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Juilek) are both assassinated by the [Morag Tong](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morag_Tong). This follows the sacking of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) fortress of Black Gate, breaking a truce between the Empire and Morrowind. [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Versidue-Shaie) assumes the Imperial Throne, and declares the start of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[116]](#cite_note-2920L-118)</sup>
- [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) destroys [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). [Almalexia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Almalexia) and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) are too late to prevent its destruction, but manage to banish Mehrunes Dagon back to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[116]](#cite_note-2920L-118)</sup>
