Jahar Fuso'ja *The description of the Mane's qunan*
---
Early in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) donated this qanun to the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) in exchange for allowing [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) to attend and teach here. At the time, high-ranking Khajiit officials sought ways to preserve and spread their culture against the encroaching [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) rule of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md).

This qanun was played at the celebration to announce the end of the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) that devastated Elsweyr. Named Jahar Fuso'ja, or Instrument of Celebration, today it reminds us to glory in life and never forgot the old songs.
