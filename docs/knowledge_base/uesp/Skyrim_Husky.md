[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-creature-Husky.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Husky.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Husky.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Husky.jpg) Husky **Huskies** are [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) variants added by the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md). They can be found accompanying members of [the Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) in battle, or also with members of the [Vigil of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md). They drop [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat), and also have a 10% chance of dropping minor loot, including leveled rings, gems, and gold.

## Notes
- Two Huskies, [Bran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bran) and [Sceolang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sceolang), can be used as [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md#Creatures) if you side with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) in Dawnguard's [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests).

## Bugs
- Huskies may not appear at Fort Dawnguard, or in the game at all, and may require a mod or console command to access them.
