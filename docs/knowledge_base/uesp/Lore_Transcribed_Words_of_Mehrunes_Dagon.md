# Transcribed Words of Mehrunes Dagon

Transcribed Words of Mehrunes Dagon *A recorded speech of the Prince of Destruction, prior to his attack in Mournhold circa 1E 2920*
---
Lead Writer Bill Slavicsek created a lorebook for an [ESO Live](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:ESO_Live) stream, which was a reward for hitting the $7000 mark for the [Extra Life](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Extra_Life_(fundraiser)) donations. It was read by [Steven French](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steven_French), who is the voice actor for [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) in *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*. It was streamed [at this link here](https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1220973938) (27:04-29:38) on December 1, 2021. An official article [about the stream](https://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/news/post/61295) itself can be found in ESO's official site, though the reading was a surprise.

---
[He](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) means to [bind me](Lore_Coldharbour_Compact.md). Me, the Hope of Blood, the [Sea's Flame Redeemer](Lore_Mythic_Dawn_Commentaries_4.md), and the [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) -Pride's End.

Hear me, [kyn](Lore_Dremora.md), of what consequence is an [Ash-Elf's](Lore_Dunmer.md) words? By what artifice does he think to hinder me? A mortal begetting gears and springs is a mortal still, ever weak, ever kindling for the pyre of my will.

So, let the Clockmaker come. I shall sunder his bones just as I sundered his [childhood home](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md), for I am Mehrunes Dagon, there is no dam I cannot breach, no field I cannot burn.

All the pale wonders of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) are mine to destroy. The mortal's souls shall blaze like sparks from a hammer blow, ignited and lost in one thunderous stroke.

Do you hear me, kyn? Arise for the Lord of Fire and Flood. Arise for the Prince of Disaster and Destruction. I wield the [Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mehrunes_Razor) that cleaves nations in twain. I wake the storm that shears flesh from bone, I call forth the winds of long rending anguish.

Draw your swords, kyn! Arise, for I am the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md)' howling redeemer. Fight and die, and fight again. Reap blood and ash, and I shall deliver you a [blazing paradise](Lore_Paradise.md).

I am Mehrunes Dagon, and I shall not be denied.
