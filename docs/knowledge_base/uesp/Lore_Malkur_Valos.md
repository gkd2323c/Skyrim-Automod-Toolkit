Malkur Valos was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) member of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and an expert on [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).

Malkur was born the only child of the wizard Maros Valos. When Malkur's mother died in childbirth, Maros summoned a [Golden Saint](Lore_Golden_Saint.md) to protect and care for his son. Malkur was taken to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) by his Daedric guardian and raised among the [Aureals](Lore_Aureal.md) after Maros was killed in a mage's duel. Under their tutelage he acquired steadfast principles of honor and duty, although the inescapable influence of the Isles imbued him with a measure of unpredictability. Eventually, Malkur was drawn to return to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), where his natural magical ability caught the attention of [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md). After joining the Mages Guild, the principles and objectives of the guild became the unwavering core driving his every action, though that was not always apparent to superficial observers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TNM-1)</sup>

Following the [Soulburst](Lore_Soulburst.md) of [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578), Malkur was seemingly involved in investigating [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s growing influence, as evidenced by his encounter with [Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md) in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) in [2E 581](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_581).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Malkur was among those assembled by the Mages Guild to take part in the invasion of Coldharbour.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> However, after entering the portal on the island of [Stirk](Lore_Stirk.md), the invasion force was scattered across the realm by Molag Bal's defenses. Along with a group of fellow mages, Malkur ended up near the Court of Contempt, where the harsh laws of Coldharbour are enforced. The group was beset by [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) and brought before the court, although Malkur himself evaded capture by transforming into a [scamp](Lore_Scamp.md). The trial was nothing but a farce, and the mages were quickly condemned to death. With the help of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Malkur fought through the Daedric guards and rescued his peers from the court.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

He then journeyed to [the Hollow City](Lore_The_Hollow_City.md), where the scattered invasion force was assembling. He was responsible for opening and maintaining portals to Tamriel, which brought the Hollow City to life and allowed for supply lines and reinforcements to enter Coldharbour safely.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Malkur also partook in the invasion's final assault, when he entered the Endless Stair and helped put an end to the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Many prominent figures active in the decade following the onset of the Planemeld were immortalized in a painting commemorating their adventures. Among them was Malkur, portrayed in a conversation with [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) and [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

The famed summoner Gavis Velas and his brother Ovis Velas were descendants of Malkur.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TNM-1)</sup> They moved to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). Ovis was killed after publicly assaulting the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), and Gavis died attempting to avenge him.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
