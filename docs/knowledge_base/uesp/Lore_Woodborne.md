Lord Woodborne was a minor noble of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) in the early [Fifth Century of the Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md#Fifth_Century), and betrothed to [Princess Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md), daughter of [King Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-revenge-1)</sup>

At some point around the year [3E 400](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_400), the [Totem of Tiber Septim](Lore_Totem_of_Tiber_Septim.md) was found in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. Legends held that this artifact—unseen since [the end of the Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_896) —allowed its bearer to control the equally-legendary [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md), a colossal brass golem of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) make said to have been used by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) to conquer all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It is unclear who found the Totem, but by [3E 403](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_403) it had made its way into Lord Woodborne's hands.

3E 403 also saw the conclusion of the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md), a petty land squabble between the kingdoms of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) that had escalated into an all-out war. On the eve of what was to be the decisive battle at [Cryngaine Field](Lore_Cryngaine_Field.md), a delegation from Wayrest—which included Woodborne—visited the Daggerfall contingent to speak with Daggerfall's King [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md). During the meeting, Lord Woodborne crept up behind Lysandus and stabbed him.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The king died quickly, and his son [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md) was crowned the new king shortly before leading his troops to victory.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFC-3)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) sent an [agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) to the Iliac Bay to investigate the death of King Lysandus. Over time, the trail eventually led to [Woodborne Hall](Lore_Woodborne_Hall.md) in northwestern Wayrest, where the Agent discovered evidence that Woodborne was planning to seize the throne of Wayrest—and presumably use the power of Numidium to conquer the surrounding lands.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LReve-4)</sup> Lord Woodborne was eventually cornered and slain by the Agent, and with his dying breath revealed his co-conspirator in this sordid affair to be none other than King Gothryd.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-revenge-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Lord Woodborne was a member of the [Knights of the Rose](Lore_Knights_of_the_Rose.md), the knightly order of the Kingdom of Wayrest.
