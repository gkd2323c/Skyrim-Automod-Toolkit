[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter.jpg) A hunter [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-npc-Fugitive_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fugitive_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fugitive_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fugitive_02.jpg) A hunter fires an arrow at a fugitive **Hunters** are friendly people (Nord, Imperial, Redguard, or Wood Elf) who can be found in the wild, stalking and attacking [wildlife](Skyrim_Creatures.md) such as [elk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elk). They will also help kill any wildlife you are attacking. They traverse the wilds alone or in small groups, sometimes accompanied by [dogs](Skyrim_Dog.md) or [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). Hunters are automatically aggressive to most wildlife and will attempt to kill them.

They carry a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) and some [iron arrows](Skyrim_Ammunition.md), and can usually be traded with.

They share dialogue with generic [fishermen](Skyrim_Fisherman.md):

*"I've been hunting and fishing in these parts for years."* *"It's not like my poaching is hurting anyone. The Jarl can hardly eat every deer now can he?"* *"There's enough for all of us if you aren't greedy."* When traveling the wilderness, occasionally a [fugitive](Skyrim_Fugitive.md) can be [seen](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Hey_you_there.2C_take_this) being pursued by a hunter. The fugitive will approach you and give you an item (usually rare and enchanted). Moments later, the hunter will come by and ask which way the fugitive went. You can either tell the truth, lie, or give him the item. After the conversation, the hunter will most likely find the fugitive and kill him.

The hunter will ask you, *"Did you see someone run past just now?"* Pointing to the fugitive will have him reply, *"Great! Thanks! Bastard stole from me. I'll catch him though!"* When you ask if the item is his, he will be confused, but thankful still: *"Yes! How'd you get this? Never mind, here. It's not much, but its * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * something. I'm going to track down that thief and murder him!"* If you deny that you saw someone, he will say, *"Well, if you do, grab him and yell. Bastard stole from me."* Whichever your answer was, he will continue his chase.

### Within [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)
- West of the [Guardian Stones](Skyrim_Guardian_Stones.md). It consists of a rack of [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), a small tent with a bedroll, a chest and a bottle of Nord mead all next to a boat. With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation installed, the camp will also have a copy of the [fishing map for Falkreath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Map_-_Falkreath), a fishing pole and a [cooked brook bass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Brook_Bass) by the fire. Some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) will also be found near the shore. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=48&y=-60456&zoom=17&legacy=true)

## Notes
- Hunters will turn on your [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md). When ridden, the hunters show no interest in the horse, but will attack the horse the moment you dismount. Care should be taken when dismounting a horse in the wild near hunters to avoid this issue. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, addresses this issue. This was due to incomplete faction relationships.
- Some hunters can be found at [unmarked camps](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Hunter.2C_Fishermen_and_Other_Camps) at which you may trade. Sometimes, the camp will be empty (for whatever reason), and you can find a horse to ride there. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Some hunters are infected with [lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md), and change into [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) when you [encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Human_changing_into_werewolf) them at night. They become hostile and attack when in werewolf form. Even if you are a werewolf when you encounter them, they will still become hostile. (In fact they will shout "Kill the beast!" or a similar line, shoot an arrow or two at you, and then transform into a werewolf and finish the battle that way.) <sup>**?**</sup>
- With the *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* add-on, a hunter who was bitten by a vampire may approach you and request a [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md) potion. If you comply, the hunter will give you 100 gold and reveal the location of a vampire lair. Only a found or purchased potion will work; one created using [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) won't. Afterwards, the hunter will be available for bartering with once again. If you cannot, the hunter will flee in terror.
- You can buy from hunters, your money will be reduced normally and you will get the item, but when selling to them your item will disappear and you will get no money. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.

## Dialogue
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-npc-Hunter_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hunter_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter_03.jpg) *"Test yourself against the beast with us."* In one particular encounter, a group of four will be preparing to hunt a [Netch](Skyrim_Netch.md). One will approach you and say:

*"We have come south to test our might against a Netch, will you join us?"* **What's a netch?** *"The giant air-floater from the dark elf lands. Only the brave dare to hunt them, only the strong survive them. Come. Test yourself against the beast with us. We all will share in the glory of the hunt!"* Three options are presented next.

**Do you think you'll win?** *"A warrior expects nothing and therefore is ready for anything. If our aim and our hearts are true, the All-Maker will grant us strength. If not, today is as good a day to die as any other. Either way, they'll songs of our bravery around the campfires for many years."* **I'll join you.** *"Well met, friend. May we fight or die well."* **Good luck.** *"Luck? Luck will play no part today. We will earn our victory or return to the All-Maker."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-npc-Hunter_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hunter_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunter_02.jpg) A group of Hunters attacking a Bull Netch After this they will attack the netch. If they manage to kill the creature, they will repeat the following lines:

*"It was a good hunt."* *"Our victory will bring meat and bones to our people and glory to our families."* *"The beast was strong, but we were stronger."* *"They will sing stories of this day."* If a hunter should die during the hunt, the surviving members will say:

*"The beast was not the only one to fall this day. May the All-Maker welcome our brethren with open arms."* *"We will honor and sing praises of the fallen."* You may also encounter a hunter and their dog in the wilderness of Solstheim.
