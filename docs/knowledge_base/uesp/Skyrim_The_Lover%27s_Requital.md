## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Sibbi Black-Briar](Skyrim_Sibbi_Black-Briar.md) in [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md).
2. Locate [Svidi](Skyrim_Svidi.md) and talk to her.
3. Tell Sibbi the truth or lie to him.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Murderer in the Jail?
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-quest-The_Lover%27s_Requital_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Lover%27s_Requital_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Lover%27s_Requital_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Lover%27s_Requital_02.jpg) Sibbi is planning to take action! If you enter the [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md), you will come across [Sibbi Black-Briar](Skyrim_Sibbi_Black-Briar.md) residing in one of the jail cells. You may ask him how he ended up in jail, he will respond with: *"Look, we all have our flaws; mine is that women can't get enough of me. So I had this little affair going on while I was betrothed to a beautiful girl named Svidi. Well, she finds out and she tells her brother, Wulfur. Then her brother attacks me with a knife... I mean, I had to defend myself"*

Now, he is very angry about that girl. *"I'd do anything to have that whore's head on a platter."* All he needs to know is where she is. If you can help him find his fiancee, he promises you'll be well compensated. You may ask him for more information about Svidi. He will respond with: *"She was a young woman... buxom, with long flowing black hair. She used to sing the most lovely songs to me when we were courting. I've never heard a voice so beautiful. All that talent will go to waste when I'm finished with her."*

### A Bard in the Inn?
According to Sibbi's description, you may find such a woman in the [Vilemyr Inn](Skyrim_Vilemyr_Inn.md) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). Now, she is a [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md) under the name of [Lynly Star-Sung](Skyrim_Lynly_Star-Sung.md). You may tell her that you know she is Svidi, she will initially deny it. *"No, I'm sorry. You must have me mistaken for someone else. I've never even heard that name before."* You can now choose to persuade or intimidate her with your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skills.

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| You have nothing to fear from me. **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: Please, I beg you, don't tell Sibbi where I am. He'll kill me! | **Passed**: Return to Sibbi |
| **Failed**: I'm sorry, I don't know what you're talking about. | **Failed**: Nothing | |
| Stop lying or I'll beat it out of you! **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: Please, I beg you, don't tell Sibbi where I am. He'll kill me! | **Passed**: Return to Sibbi |
| **Failed**: I've been hurt worse before. | **Failed**: Nothing | |
| Never mind. My mistake. | I should say so. I'd be careful in the future before you confront someone without all of the facts! | Nothing |

If you are successful she will continue: *"Sibbi's been spreading lies about me, you must hear me out."*

You may ask Svidi to tell you her side of the story, she will reply with: *"Sibbi and I were to be married. We were supposed to be happy together forever. Then I found that wretched poetry from [Svana](Skyrim_Svana_Far-Shield.md). When I confronted Sibbi about it, he threatened to kill me. I was scared so I told my brother Wulfur. He went to talk to Sibbi for me and... and... Sibbi killed him. My brother wasn't even armed! When I heard what Sibbi did, I fled. Now you've found me, and you're going to tell him where I am."*

### A Choice in Your Hand
Return to Sibbi and tell him you have got some information about Svidi. He will be very glad. *"Good, good. Just point me in the right direction and I'll do the rest."* At this point, you will have to make a decision: tell him the truth or lie to him.

| Option | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| She's in Ivarstead using the name Lynly. | Ivarstead? So close to Riften? She's an even bigger fool than I thought. |
| She's made her way east into Morrowind. | So, the whore thinks the Black-Briar reach ends at the border of Skyrim, eh? She'll soon discover her mistake. |
| Never mind. I was mistaken. | You idiot. You came all the way down here for that? |

Whichever option you choose between the first two, Svidi will not be killed, and Sibbi will give you his [key](Skyrim_Keys.md#Sibbi.27s_Stash_Key) to a long chest found in the basement (at the bottom of the stairs) of the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) in Riften, saying *"You've done me a great service, my friend. Allow me to compensate you for the information."* The chest will always contain one random weapon, two poisons, and some gold, along with the [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Prowler%27s_Profit.md) gems, if applicable. Sibbi's gratitude will also allow you to take any low-value items from the meadery.

## Notes
- Sibbi's chest is non-respawning, so its contents will be determined on your first visit to the meadery and won't change after that.
- Lynly Star-Sung remains alive all the time even if you tell Sibbi the truth.

## Bugs
- Despite the fact that Sibbi says that Lynly Star-Sung has dark hair, her hair is blonde instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.3, addresses this issue. Lynly now has black hair to comply with Sibbi's description.
- Killing Lynly will fail the quest, despite Sibbi having dialogue afterwards for when you do. This is caused by the `defaultsetstagealias` script attached to her alias. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.3, fixes this bug.
- The game incorrectly runs the speechcraft check against Lynly herself rather than you, making it impossible to pass without the [Amulet of Articulation](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Articulation.md#Amulet_of_Articulation). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- If you intimidate Lynly, she does not appropriately harbor bitterness towards you due to the relationship values not being adjusted. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes when you go to talk to Sibbi after speaking with Lynly, you can't engage in conversation through the bars, even though there is a pointer over his head and he is up close. You might need to pick the lock and go into his cell. <sup>**?**</sup>
