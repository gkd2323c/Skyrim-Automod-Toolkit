It was released on April 5, 2018 for 300 Creation Credits.

The quest added by this Creation was made by [unoctium](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/4108480).

## Official Summary
*To obtain the legendary Shadowrend – an enchanted sword or battleaxe of tremendous power – you need only face the most worthy foe imaginable: yourself. Quest implemented by Richard "unoctium" Edwards (Quest "Through a Glass, Darkly" starts by claiming the weapon in the hot springs near the Atronach Stone.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend_(item).md), a weapon which originally [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shadowrend) in the *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* expansion for [*Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion). There are two versions of the weapon, a [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword) and a [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe), but you can only possess one at a time. Upon installation, the quest [Through a Glass, Darkly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Through_a_Glass,_Darkly) will begin and you will be directed to a geyser pool in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) where you must defeat a [shadowy clone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadow) of yourself in order to claim Shadowrend. After completing the quest, you can return to the geyser at any time to switch between the two versions of the weapon.
