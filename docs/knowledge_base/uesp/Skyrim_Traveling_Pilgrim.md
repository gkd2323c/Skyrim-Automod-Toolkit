The **Traveling Pilgrim** can be encountered during radiant [side quests](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Vampire_Side_Quests) for the vampires if you ally with them when playing [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md). Their appearance as a traveling pilgrim is a disguise to cover their true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunter. They wear a set of [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Ragged_Boots.md), and [cap](Skyrim_Ragged_Cap.md). They carry a [Dawnguard War Axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md), an enchanted [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md), three [steel bolts](Skyrim_Steel_Bolt.md), and an enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md).

If you ask which shrine they're traveling to, they'll reply *"Which shrine? Well, I... I'm going to them all. Yes. One at a time. Until I've visited all the shrines to all the gods in Skyrim."* Accusing them of being disguised will result in an offended *"I beg your pardon. I don't know what you're getting at, but whatever it is, it's not appreciated."*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Traveling_Pilgrim.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Traveling_Pilgrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Pilgrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Pilgrim.jpg) The Traveling Pilgrim This **Traveling Pilgrim** can be encountered during the radiant [Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md) quest for [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md). His appearance as a traveling pilgrim is a disguise to cover his true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) and you will be tasked with eliminating him. He wears a set of [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Ragged_Boots.md), and [cap](Skyrim_Ragged_Cap.md).

He carries an enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) and an enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md), and drops [Vampire Dust](Skyrim_Vampire_Dust.md) and some random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) when he dies.

If you ask which shrine he's traveling to, he'll reply *"Which shrine? Well, I... I'm going to them all. Yes. One at a time. Until I've visited all the shrines to all the gods in Skyrim."* Accusing him of being disguised will result in an offended *"I beg your pardon. I don't know what you're getting at, but whatever it is, it's not appreciated."*
