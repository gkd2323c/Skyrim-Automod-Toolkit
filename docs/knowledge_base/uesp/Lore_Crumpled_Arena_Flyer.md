Come one, come all! Witness a jubilee of carnage and savagery!

The [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) Arena is open for business! Come day, come night, come anytime you feel the need to watch mortals bleed profusely, squirm in agony, then die in innovative and humiliating ways!
The festivities will continue unabated until the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) is complete, or we run out of mortals.

Don't wait! Howls of fury and screams of pain await!

In our [Brutal Lord](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s name,
Dredaza the Ringmaster
