Letter from an Indoril Retainer **by [Bandusil Falvani](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Bandusil_Falvani)** *A survey of the Bal Foyen area*
---
[![ON-concept-Bal Foyen 02.png](https://images.uesp.net/5/57/ON-concept-Bal_Foyen_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Bal_Foyen_02.png)
- Kinswoman Suldryn,

- I arrived ahead of schedule in [Bal Foyen](Lore_Bal_Foyen.md) to begin my survey, and completed a thorough tour of the area. This small region of the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) has little of note beyond Fort Zeren, which I inspected. The fort is in decent repair, and it is garrisoned appropriately for the area's relative calm. The soldiers stationed here are fit and alert, and train regularly, I am assured. Some small farming settlements, established by [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) freed from slavery by the [Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), are nearby. Shabby mud huts have been built up near their [guar](Lore_Guar.md) pens, an eyesore in otherwise fine land. Should there be any significant trouble here, the fort may require reinforcement, but it should be adequate to protect the area and the small civilian population for now. There is little else to report, so I will be moving on shortly.

- May [the Three](Lore_Tribunal.md) Guide You,

- Bandusil Falvani

- Retainer, [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md)
