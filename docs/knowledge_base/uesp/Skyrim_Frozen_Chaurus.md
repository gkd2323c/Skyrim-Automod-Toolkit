[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-creature-Frozen_Chaurus.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Frozen_Chaurus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frozen_Chaurus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frozen_Chaurus.jpg) Frozen Chaurus The **Frozen Chaurus** is one of the frozen creatures during the quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md). They cannot be attacked while frozen, but once they break out they are similar to regular [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), complete with poison spit. Upon death, they break up into ice shards and cannot be looted. They are only found in the temple, lining the path to [Arch-Curate Vyrthur](Skyrim_Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.md). During the end confrontation of Touching the Sky, several of them are released at once, which can lead to you being easily surrounded.
