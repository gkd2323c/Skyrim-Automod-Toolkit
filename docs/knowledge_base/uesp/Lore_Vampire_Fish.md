## Vampire Fish
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Vampire_Fish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Vampire_Fish.png) A Vampire Fish The **Vampire Fish** is a type of large fish that can be found in underground dark pools in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), baring sharp fangs.

## Venomfish
