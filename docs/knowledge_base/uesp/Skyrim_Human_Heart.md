[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-ingredient-Human_Heart.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Human_Heart.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Human_Heart.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Human_Heart.jpg) A human heart **Human Hearts** are used in Alchemy or at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md). They can be found in locations where people have performed the Black Sacrament ritual to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), along with [human flesh](Skyrim_Human_Flesh.md). Hearts cannot be taken from dead humans.

This ingredient's alchemical effects are identical to [mort flesh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mort_Flesh)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup>.

## Ingredients
11 guaranteed samples can be found in 11 different locations:

- [Aretino Residence](Skyrim_Aretino_Residence.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- [Black-Briar Manor](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)), in a locked room in the basement
- [Brittleshin Pass](Skyrim_Brittleshin_Pass.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- [Bruca's Leap Redoubt](Skyrim_Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 110 ft N of [Dragontooth Crater](Skyrim_Dragontooth_Crater.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-141554&y=52471)
- [Ilinalta's Deep](Skyrim_Ilinalta%27s_Deep.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 250 ft SSW of [Lost Valley Redoubt](Skyrim_Lost_Valley_Redoubt.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-92202&y=-41584)
- [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))

With [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, 5 additional samples become available:

- 4 in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md), inside a locked display case
- 1 in [Redwater Spring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Spring) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that it can only be randomly found in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). However, they do **not** appear in the inventories of apothecary merchants (even if the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk has been unlocked). Unlike several other ingredients that are not stocked by apothecary merchants, though, human hearts can be sold to merchants. Other possible locations for samples include:

- 20% chance of being dropped by the [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) boss in [The Cursed Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe) quest.
- Rare-type apothecary's satchels have a 6% chance of containing one.
- [Falmer magic casters](Skyrim_Falmer.md#Shaman), [warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md), and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) have a 0.7% chance of carrying one.
- Random reward from quest [The Straw that Broke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Straw_that_Broke).

## Notes
- A human heart is one of two items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a [Daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) (requires the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone)). The Daedra heart can then be used to craft Daedric weapons and armor.
