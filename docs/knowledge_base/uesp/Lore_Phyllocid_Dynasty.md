# Phyllocid Dynasty

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-npc-King_Fahara%27jad.jpg/200px-ON-npc-King_Fahara%27jad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Fahara%27jad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Fahara%27jad.jpg) Fahara'jad, a descendant of the Phyllocid Dynasty *Know then, O Prince, that the Phyllocid Dynasty did rule in Hammerfell while e'er the province was incorporate in the Second Empire, and of this line of kings, though some were wise and some were foolish, all were noble. And the last of the line, High King Ar-Azal, was most noble of all, for he it was who, for a time, brought an end to the strife of brother against brother, of Crown against Forebear.*
— *[The Worthy Ar-Azal, His Deeds](Lore_The_Worthy_Ar-Azal,_His_Deeds.md)* The Phyllocid Dynasty was the royal family of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) that governed the province from the ancient capital of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md) and later in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) during its time under the regime of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) and the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). Each monarch is adorned with the Diadem of [Diagna](Lore_Diagna.md) when graced with the title of the High King.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAHD-1)</sup>

## History
They ruled as High Kings from the ancient capital and the faction's main holding, Hegathe. The Crowns were formed during the province's annexation into the Second Empire, alongside the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) that later stationed themselves in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup>

When High King Ja-Fr passed away, his throne was given to his young son, [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), whose ascension was reluctantly and passive-aggressively accepted by both factions. It was apparent to him that the true power he wielded was minimal and he sought guidance from his mentor, the Admirable Zaqeeb, who gave him a libation of [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md). In his dreams, he was approached by the World-Eater himself, who gave him a riddle and guidance to the Shrine of [Tava](Lore_Tava.md) in Hegathe. The next morning, he visited the Fresco of the Goshawk there and discovered the answer to the riddle, the union of two houses. He married [Arlimahera](Lore_Arlimahera.md), daughter of the Crowns Grandee in [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), and [Fereshtah](Lore_Fereshtah.md), daughter of the Forebears Grandee in Sentinel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAHD-1)</sup>

Ar-Azal was the last monarch of the dynasty but his bloodline lived on through the royalty from [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md). The Crowns Grandee, [Zizzeen](Lore_Zizzeen.md) descended from the Phyllocid Dynasty, and his relative, Grandeya Fanesh would bear her only child; Ar-Azal's great-grandnephew and the Forebears Prince of Antiphyllos, [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAHD-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TABKF-3)</sup> He would later assume the mantle of High King after his [predecessor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ramzi) died during the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) outbreak.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TIEGt TH-4)</sup> The High King married [Za-Rifah](Lore_Za-Rifah.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRLo S-5)</sup> the member of the esteemed al-Danobia family of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Zeira-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PL-7)</sup> who in turn gave birth to three children, the eldest daughter, [Maraya](Lore_Maraya.md), the middle child, Lakana, and the youngest, the heir apparent, [Azah](Lore_Azah.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRLo S-5)</sup>
