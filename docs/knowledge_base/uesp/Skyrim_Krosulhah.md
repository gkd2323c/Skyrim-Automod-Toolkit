[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/SR-creature-Krosulhah.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Krosulhah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Krosulhah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Krosulhah.jpg) Krosulhah **Krosulhah** is a unique frost [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) found outside [Nchardak](Skyrim_Nchardak.md) during [The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md).

His name means "Sorcerer Day Mind" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md)**: Travel to the realm of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md) to learn the rest of [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md).

## Dialogue
When encountered, he hovers above the ruins and bellows before attacking you and [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).

*"Miraak has commanded your death. So it shall be."* He will taunt you during the battle:

*"When Miraak returns, all will bow before him"* *"Soon, Miraak will return to resume his rightful junaar over Solstheim!"* He shares several lines of dialogue with [Sahrotaar](Skyrim_Sahrotaar.md), as they are both in the faction "[Combat dialogue for dragons allied to Miraak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat_dialogue_for_dragons_allied_to_Miraak)":

*"My thuri Miraak is the only true Dovahkiin."* *"Krin mey. You are a fool to challenge Miraak. His thu'um is supreme."* *"Your death has been foretold."* *"Aam uv dir. Those that will not serve Miraak must die."* If you use [Dragonrend](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md) on Krosulhah, he will comment on it:

*"Bein Rotmulaagge! What foul Words are these?* *"No dovah would stoop to such vile tahrovin."* Once he has landed on the ground, he will say:

*"Now we will see whose thu'um is the stronger!"* *"You are strong, Dovahkiin. Rinik mul. But not strong enough."*
## Notes
- Krosulhah is more talkative than most other dragons, taunting you throughout the fight. If you inflict [Dragonrend](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md) upon Krosulhah, he will have specific comments pertaining to that attack.

## Bugs
- According to game data, Krosulhah is intended to be a [Serpentine Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Serpentine_Dragon), but instead has the appearance of a regular dragon. One result of this is that his blood textures don't match his body. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
