[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-place-The_Lightless_Oubliette.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Lightless_Oubliette.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Lightless_Oubliette.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Lightless_Oubliette.jpg) The Lightless Oubliette The Lightless Oubliette is tower built in emulation of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) that functions as a prison. It was built to primarily house entities who can manipulate light to magical ends.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is surrounded by ruins and [azure plasm](Lore_Azure_Plasm.md), and is filled with Daedra and Soul Shriven. Further inside is an area known as the Lightless Cell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOHG-2)</sup> [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md) described seeing the tower spattered with blood and excrement.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDo O-3)</sup>

[Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md) was imprisoned here for millennia, until being freed by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOHG-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
