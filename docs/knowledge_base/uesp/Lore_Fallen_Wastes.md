[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/ON-map-Fallen_Wastes.jpg/200px-ON-map-Fallen_Wastes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Fallen_Wastes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Fallen_Wastes.jpg) Map of Fallen Wastes circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Fallen Wastes** is the southernmost region of the greater region of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md). The entirety of the Fallen Wastes is considered geographically to be on the [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) side of the border, although around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) it was controlled by the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) city-state of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) and considered a part of High Rock by the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HRQ-3)</sup> The capital of the region is the city-state of [Hallin's Stand](Lore_Hallin%27s_Stand.md), located on the northern end of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md), just before the [Bjoulsae Estuary](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md). The ancient ruins of the [Hall of Heroes](Lore_Hall_of_Heroes.md) is the gateway to the Redguard afterlife, the [Far Shores](Lore_Far_Shores.md). Many Redguard heroes were buried in the Hall of Heroes, including [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) and [Makela Leki](Lore_Makela_Leki.md). Among other large ruins in the region are Nilata Ruins and Razak's Wheel, which are Ayleid and Dwemer respectively. The [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md) marks the northern end of the region and connects both provinces. The eastern gates lead into the region of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

When High Rock decided to secede in [1E 2305](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2305), the Legions of Piety and Grace under the command of Abbot-General [Priscus Mactator](Lore_Priscus_Mactator.md) were sent to quell them. Though the legions are said to have filled the Fallen Wastes from end to end, they found themselves unable to get past the Bangkorai Garrison,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BSOHR-4)</sup> which was then under the rule of Baroness Falinne Guimard.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRHOKE-5)</sup> After a five-month siege, they gave up and returned to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BSOHR-4)</sup>
