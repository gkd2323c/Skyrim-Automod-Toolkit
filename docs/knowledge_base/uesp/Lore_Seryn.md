Saint Seryn the Merciful is the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) patron [saint](Lore_Temple_Saints.md) of [Brewers, Bakers, and Distillers](Lore_Dunmer_Cuisine.md). Seryn could heal all diseases at the price of taking the disease upon herself. Tough-minded and fearless, she took on the burdens of others and bore those burdens to an honored old age.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWdialogue-2)</sup> Seryn was canonized by the Tribunal, and shrines to her could be found in Tribunal temples.

All [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) shrine offerings grant cures of common diseases, of blight diseases, and of afflictions of poison. Offerings made to the shrine of Saint Seryn additionally grant Seryn's Shield, giving resistance to poisons.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Additionally, various restoration spells named after Seryn were known to be used and sold by healers of Vvardenfell.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
