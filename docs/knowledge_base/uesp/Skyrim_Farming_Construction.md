# Farming Construction


The following is a list of possible construction options available for your farm. It is not necessary to purchase or build all furnishing options; you can pick and choose to better dictate style. However, all furnishing options are in a pre-arranged location and only ingredients and standard items can be moved. Oftentimes, the name of the furnishing option only indicates the primary object to be added, and actually includes various additional [containers](Skyrim_Containers.md) and accessories.

The main difference between this and [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) construction is that you will not require any [Sawn Logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md) for your farm. Instead, you will need to gather [Firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) to use.

Increases the productivity of the farm. |
| [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Stable.png) | Stable | 5 Firewood, 3 Nails | Builds a stable. Allows a horse to be purchased for the farm. |
| [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Mill.png) | Windmill | 2 Firewood, 2 Iron Fittings, 2 Quarried Stone | Upgrades the silo into a windmill and adds a grain mill for turning wheat into flour or straw. |

The total requirements above amount to: 4 [Iron Ingot](Skyrim_Iron.md#Iron_Ingot), 21 [Firewood](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Firewood), 2 [Hinge](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Hinge), 1 [Lock](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Lock), 3 [Iron Fittings](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Iron_Fittings), 12 [Quarried Stone](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Quarried_Stone), 7 [Nails](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Nails), 5 [Straw](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Straw).
