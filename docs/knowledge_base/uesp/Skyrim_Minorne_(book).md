The Scripture of Minorne **by [Moric Sidrey](Skyrim_Moric_Sidrey.md)**
---
Glory be to [Minorne](Skyrim_Minorne.md)! Glory be to the mistress of all! My life for you, oh beautiful savior! Where once my feeble ramblings seemed so grand, I now realize they are but scratches on parchment, unworthy of you. Oh, that I could properly describe you, I would write a thousand testaments to you! Damn my tiny thoughts, if only I were wiser!

Minorne asks that we bring more here for her, more to worship her and do her bidding! I have sent word to the [Hall of the Vigilants](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md) to come. A simple lie was told, for they would not understand. Not until they saw her, o glorious Minorne!

But she is fearful! There are fools in this world that do not heed to her beautiful voice. The guard, [Florentius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md), sent from the Beacon, he still prays to [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), an absentee god who pales in comparison to Minorne! I will pray to the goddess I can see! May he rot in his cage!

Oh, sweet, sweet, Minorne...
