[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Drelas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Drelas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drelas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drelas.jpg) Drelas in his house **Drelas** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) who lives alone in his [cottage](Skyrim_Drelas%27_Cottage.md) south of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). He is not overly prone to conversation; if you decide to enter his cottage, he will attack with powerful magic and a random leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) quality). He wears a random magical robe and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12), and keeps an assortment of random magical items with him such as [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md) and [alchemical](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md).

His house contains an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) and [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md), as well as a number of alchemical ingredients scattered about.

## Notes
- Due to his high aggression, if the fighting spills outside he will kill his own chickens.
- Although the door to his house is unlocked, entering still counts as trespassing, and taking anything other than the items looted from his corpse counts as theft.
