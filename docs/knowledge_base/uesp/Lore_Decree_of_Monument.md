Decree of Monument *A plaque commemorating the struggles of those who fled Morrowind following the Red Year*
---
[4 E 20](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_20)
This tower once served as a meeting place where those brave souls who achieved safe passage to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) would find loved ones, and leave notice for others who could not be found.

Let it stand in honor of those who had the strength and spirit to accept Skyrim's Offer "untithed to any thane or [hold](Lore_Hold.md), and self-governed, with free worship, with no compensation to Skyrim or the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) except as writ in the [Armistice](Lore_Armistice.md) of old wheresoever those might still apply, and henceforth let no Man or [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) say that the [Sons and Daughters of Kyne](Lore_Nord.md) are without mercy or honor."

We, the [Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of Skyrim, hereby decree this site as monument to the struggle of those who fled their native home of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) in the time following the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md).
