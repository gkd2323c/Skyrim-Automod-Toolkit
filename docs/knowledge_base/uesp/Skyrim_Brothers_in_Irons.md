## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read *[Nightgate Inn Patron's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightgate_Inn_Patron%27s_Note)* in [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md).
2. Investigate the barrel behind the inn.
3. Read the *[Undercover Guard's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Undercover_Guard%27s_Journal)*.
4. Investigate the [camp site](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Haafingar#Clam_Digger.27s_Camp).
5. Inspect Pale-Eyes' body.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-quest-Brothers_in_Irons_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Brothers_in_Irons_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Brothers_in_Irons_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Brothers_in_Irons_02.jpg) The barrel behind the inn To start this quest, read the *[Nightgate Inn Patron's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightgate_Inn_Patron%27s_Note)* in [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md). The note mentions the barrel behind the Inn, investigate the barrel and you will find *[Undercover Guard's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Undercover_Guard%27s_Journal)*. The journal mentions that a wanted criminal, [Pale-Eyes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale-Eyes) is camping out west of Northwatch Keep.

As you arrive at the camp, you will find a Thalmor wizard and two Thalmor soldiers, once you have dealt with them you can search the corpse of Pale-Eyes to find a full set of enchanted [iron plate](Skyrim_Iron.md#Iron_Plate_Armor) armor. Once you take [his journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale-Eyes%27_Journal), the quest will finish.

## Notes
- The camp is actually at a secondary location known as the "Clamdigger's Camp" not far from the Icewater Jetty.
- The Undercover Guard's Journal can remain as a quest item.
