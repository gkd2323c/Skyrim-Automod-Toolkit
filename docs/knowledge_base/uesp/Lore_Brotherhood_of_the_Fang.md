# Brotherhood of the Fang

The Brotherhood of the Fang is one of many [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) clerical orders. The Lunar Clergy comprises various monastic orders, including the Brotherhood of the Fang, the [Broken Paw](Lore_Broken_Paw.md), the [Order of the Crescent](Lore_Order_of_the_Crescent.md), and the Order of the Claw. While much remains unknown about the Brotherhood of the Fang, it was active during [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azahrr-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kauzanabi-jo-2)</sup>
