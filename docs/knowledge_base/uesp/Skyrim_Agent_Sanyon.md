**Agent Sanyon** is a dead [Thalmor Justiciar](Skyrim_Thalmor.md#Justiciars) found at the Shrine of Talos in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md). Though his corpse is labeled as **Thalmor Soldier**, he carries [orders](Skyrim_Thalmor_Orders.md) from [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md) which indicates his name. He is dressed in the gear of a Justiciar Mage with [hooded Thalmor robes, gloves, and boots](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thalmor_Equipment). As revealed by the orders, he was investigating Talos worship in the [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md) area, but was evidently killed in a resulting skirmish.

He is not to be confused with [Sanyon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanyon), a High Elf cannibal who can be found in [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave) during the quest [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md).
