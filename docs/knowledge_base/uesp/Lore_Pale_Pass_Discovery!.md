FORT PALE PASS DISCOVERED!
First Era Secrets Revealed! *A newspaper article on reporting the discovery of the fabled Fort Pale Pass* *
---
Fort [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md), the fabled headquarters of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s First Era [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) invaders, has been located by an agent of the Countess of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md). This fortress was thought to be long lost to the ages, buried in the frozen wastes of the Jeral *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* Mountains. Thanks to an expedition funded by Her Ladyship Narina Carvain, Countess of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md), the secret entrance to this great ruin was found.

Previously, scholars have offered no persuasive account for why the Akaviri juggernaut, having swept aside Tamriel's defenders, should collapse suddenly and completely crossing the [Jeral Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*. Now evidence uncovered at the site indicates a great landslide had covered the fort, trapping the hapless Akaviri commanders within, leaving the Akaviri columns leaderless and isolated in the alpine wilderness passes.
