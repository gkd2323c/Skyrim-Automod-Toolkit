# Lariat Family


[Gallivere Lariat](Lore_Gallivere_Lariat.md) was consort of [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md) after the death of [Pelagius Septim III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md). With her, he was the father of [Uriel Septim IV](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_IV), also known as Uriel Lariat. At some point Uriel Lariat became King of Wayrest, and was adopted into Septim Dynasty by Emperor [Cassynder](Lore_Cassynder.md). Cassynder was succeeded by his adopted half-brother as an Emperor. Despite the adoption and distant relation between Lariats and Septims, Uriel's lack of a close connection to the Septim bloodline led to lingering sedition against his rule. Few of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) could be persuaded to accept him fully as a blood descendant of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMOP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE-2)</sup>
