It has an ancient history, and was once a fortress turned major port city in the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region, controlled by [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md).

## History
The city's history began as an Indoril fortress named Varnim Watch, which stood in the land of [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md) since Velothi's time and had been torn asunder.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-2)</sup> It had become an established town by the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), where the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) had already built a number of large buildings but had not yet constructed a wall around the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Davon's Watch was sacked by a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) raiding party led by [Unnvald Ironhand](Lore_Unnvald_Ironhand.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), before the signing of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the city was put under siege by the invading [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md). Despite the advice of his ancestors, [Grandmaster Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md) released the ancient [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) [Balreth](Lore_Balreth.md), originally a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) general, who had been imprisoned in [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md) for centuries. Balreth destroyed the Covenant forces utterly, freeing Davon's Watch, and was subsequently rebound by the Pact.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) also had a brief presence in the city.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

By the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Darnim Watch was no longer a significant settlement. It was ruled by Lord Ulfar and it had a rivalry with [Helnim Wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helnim_Wall) and [Old Run](Lore_Old_Run.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Dragon Glade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragon_Glade) to the northeast, [Eagle Moor](Lore_Eagle_Moor.md) to the southwest, [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) to the west, [Helnim Wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helnim_Wall) to the north, [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) to the south, and Old Keep to the southwest.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Arena-8)</sup>
