### Crab Meat and Scuttle

- 2 handfuls of [scuttle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scuttle)
- 4 pinches of [wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md)
- 1 [large kwama egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Large_Kwama_Egg)
- the [meat of one mudcrab](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crab_Meat) (two portions)
- 1 handful of chopped [bittergreen](Lore_Bittergreen_Petals.md)

Beat eggs, wickwheat, and scuttle in a large bowl. Slowly stir in crabmeat and bittergreen. Bake covered in a hot over *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* for one half hour to one hour (when a knife comes out clean).

### The Hound and Rat

- 1 pie crust
- 1 pound of ground meat (mixed [rat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rat_Meat) and [hound](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hound_Meat))
- a hand and a half of cooked [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md)
- 1 handful of scuttle
- 1 [small kwama egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Small_Kwama_Egg)
- a pinch of [ash salts](Lore_Ash_Salts.md)

Cook the mixed meat in a pan over an open flame. When the meat begins to brown, add the saltrice. Stir for a few moments and add the scuttle and kwama egg. When the kwama egg is fully cooked and the scuttle has melted, pour from the pan into the pie crust. Sprinkle with ash salts and cover the pie crust. Bake for one quarter hour in a hot oven.
