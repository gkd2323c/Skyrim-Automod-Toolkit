[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-item-Hevnoraak.jpg/200px-SR-item-Hevnoraak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hevnoraak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hevnoraak.jpg) Hevnoraak Hevnoraak is an iron [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) gained by killing [Hevnoraak](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md) within the [Nordic Ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) of [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md). The enchantment provides poison and disease immunity for all diseases and poisons except for ones that ignore disease and poison resistance. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot).

## Notes
- Placing Hevnoraak on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bromjunaar_Sanctuary) will remove any bonuses that it gained from tempering.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning that they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- Hevno means "brutal" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
- As with other equipment which provides disease immunity, Hevnoraak will protect against all diseases except those contracted by traps, as [detailed here](Skyrim_Resist_Disease.md).
- Despite both the Resist Disease and Resist Poison enchantments being bright green in color (when viewed from the inventory, on other pieces), the enchantment color of Hevnoraak is red.
