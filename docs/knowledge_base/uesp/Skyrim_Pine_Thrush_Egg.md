Rock warbler nests (yielding [rock warbler eggs](Skyrim_Rock_Warbler_Egg.md)) are named identically to pine thrush ones ("Birds' Nest"), but are more common in rocky regions.

## Ingredients
107 guaranteed samples can be found in 44 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 in [Sylgja's House](Skyrim_Sylgja%27s_House.md) ([Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md))
- 5 in [Sorli's House](Skyrim_Sorli%27s_House.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 5 in [Dengeir's House](Skyrim_Dengeir%27s_House.md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 5 in [Lemkil's Farmhouse](Skyrim_Lemkil%27s_Farmhouse.md) ([Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md))
- 4 in [Odfel's House](Skyrim_Odfel%27s_House.md) ([Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md))
- 4 in [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 4 in [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 4 in [Mzulft Aedrome](Skyrim_Mzulft_Aedrome.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 4 in [Falion's House](Skyrim_Falion%27s_House.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- Pine thrush eggs are also categorized as [food](Skyrim_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- With the "Green Thumb" perk from the Alchemy skill tree, you can get 2 eggs per "harvest" of a nest.
