[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Breton_%28NPC%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Breton_%28NPC%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Breton_(NPC).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Breton_(NPC).jpg) A dead Breton male in [Stony Creek Cave](Skyrim_Stony_Creek_Cave.md) These **Bretons** are all male [citizens](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and wear randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance they may carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.

They can be found in the following locations:

| - [Stony Creek Cave](Skyrim_Stony_Creek_Cave.md), in the entrance. <br> - [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md), inside a cage in [Sild the Warlock's](Skyrim_Sild_the_Warlock.md) chamber. <br> - [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md), locked inside a cage near the [Word Wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md). <br> - [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), on the floor next to a table. <br> - [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), at the entrance to a small dead end off the main cavern. <br> - [Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole.md), at the bottom of a sinkhole. <br> - [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md), in front of a fire in a kitchen. <br> - [Lost Tongue Overlook](Skyrim_Lost_Tongue_Overlook.md), inside a cage. <br> - [Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md), on a rock near the tower. |
