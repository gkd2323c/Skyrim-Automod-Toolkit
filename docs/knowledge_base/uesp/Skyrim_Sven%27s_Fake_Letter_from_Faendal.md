Sven's Fake Letter from Faendal **by [Sven](Skyrim_Sven.md)** *A forged note meant to make Faendal look unfavorable to Camilla* *
---
Dear [Camilla](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md),
I know I have called upon you at your house many times, and while we may be growing close, I need you to put any desires you may have for me aside. I am a true-born son of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and I could never befoul my bloodline by courting an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md). I hope we can remain true friends, provided you understand your people's place in the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), and respect me as such.
Sincerely, [Faendal](Skyrim_Faendal.md)
