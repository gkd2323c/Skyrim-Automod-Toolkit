Notes on Yngol Barrow *A scholar's transcription of ancient Nord writings in a Skyrim barrow*
---
What a peculiar place the [barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yngol_Barrow) turned out to be. I hadn't explored for very long before reaching this elaborate room and gate locking me from going further into the crypt.

I confess it - I'm relieved! This place puts the fear of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) into me. There are some carvings in this room. I'll attempt to transcribe them for the [College](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) so I have something to show for this effort.

*All was so in [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md)*

*land of truth and our home *

*Man in his throne, *

*so should he be* [Whale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Whale) in the sea,

so should he be [Eagle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eagle) in Sun's Sky,

so should he be [Snake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Snake) in the weed

so should he be Sorrow! For the [Sea-Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) took [Yngol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yngol)

Prize Brother of Sail from Atmora's Fleet And none on land

nor sky, nor sea would ever again

** ** ** ** ** *be as should be* Fascinating, these ancient [Nords](Lore_Nord.md). I wonder what other secrets are hidden here? Surely there can't be harm in trying to go a little further, and there is a lever here which must open the gate beyond.
