Ormellius Goldwine<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Name)</sup> was the Count of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). He had two sons. Around [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), family strife over inheritance in Kvatch claimed the lives of both of Goldwine's sons. After peace was restored, Goldwine entered a period of mourning, and as of 3E 432, he had not designated a new heir.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3C-1)</sup>

After the assassinations of Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_VII.md) and his heirs on 27 Last Seed, [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Daedric forces of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) opened Oblivion Gates outside Kvatch and attacked the city, laying waste to it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Savlian-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hirtel-3)</sup> The Count and his guards managed to close the gates of Kvatch Castle; however, the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) soon overran the castle and killed Goldwine in his quarters.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBf CK-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Berich-5)</sup> The Hero of Kvatch and some of the surviving Kvatch guards later retook the castle from the Daedra, where they found Goldwine's body and retrieved his [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) signet ring.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBf CK-4)</sup>
