Erlendr ** the Quick**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Prima-1)</sup><sup>:153</sup> was an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) warrior from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He and four companions embarked on a quest to find [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). However, Erlendr, [Hunroor](Lore_Hunroor.md), and [Nikulas](Lore_Nikulas.md) were betrayed by the wizard [Grimkell](Lore_Grimkell.md), who turned them into stone pillars at Brodir Grove on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were avenged by [Ulfgar the Unending](Lore_Ulfgar_the_Unending.md), who killed the wizard. All of the friends eventually did find their way to Sovngarde.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon2-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup>
