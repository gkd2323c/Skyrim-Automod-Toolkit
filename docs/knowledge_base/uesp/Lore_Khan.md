# Khan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg/200px-MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg) Hortator Indoril Nerevar, Great Ashkan of the Chimer Khan<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> is a word describing the **chief** of a tribe, used in the historical [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and modern [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) cultures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NMAS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khan Dia2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Khan Dia1-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGT-4)</sup> The [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) hero [Indoril Nerevar](Lore_Indoril_Nerevar.md) is described as the "great khan" or "great ashkhan" of the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md), and also is described as a **king** by westerners.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NMAS-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo F-5)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup> The Missing God [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) is featured in the myths of all the [Elven races](Lore_Elf.md) across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) —therefore the suffixation of "-khan" would be derived from the [Aldmeri root language](Lore_Aldmeris.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TM-6)</sup>
