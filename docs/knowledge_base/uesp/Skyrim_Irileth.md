[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Irileth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irileth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irileth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irileth.jpg) Irileth **Irileth** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade) who serves as [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) to [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), and remains by his side at all times within [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md). Although it is highly uncommon for a Dunmer to serve as housecarl in Skyrim, Irileth and Balgruuf fought in many battles together when they were younger. When Balgruuf was named as Jarl of Whiterun, she demanded that she be his housecarl, on account of their long-forged battle-bond.

Irileth wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), along with a matching pair of [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots). She fights with a [Steel Sword of Cold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Sword_of_Cold). When attacking at range, she uses [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) and an [elven bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Bow), regardless of character level. She also makes frequent use of [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic, namely [Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) spells.

### The Fallen
During the quest [The Fallen](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md), after you have convinced Balgruuf to use his palace as a dragon trap, Irileth will only comment:

*"I don't know how you talked the Jarl into this plan of yours."* She will head up to the great porch of Dragonsreach and scout the landscape for dragons saying nothing but:

*"Let's get this over with."* When [Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) arrives, she will draw her bow and do her best to get him to land without killing him. When he is down, she will shout:

*"Get it into the trap before it kills us all!"* Once the dragon is stuck in the trap, she will position herself at a safe distance while you talk to Odahviing.

### The World-Eater's Eyrie
After a while, when the quest [The World-Eater's Eyrie](Skyrim_The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie.md) is initiated, she will start wandering around the captured dragon and will, when [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md) turns up and makes bad suggestions to the captured dragon, say:

*"Farengar, very bad idea. Even for you."* Once Odahviing is freed, she will go down to the dragon and ensure it keeps its promise. When you climb onto the dragon she will comment:

*"You're either the bravest person I've ever met, or the biggest fool."*
### Battle for Whiterun (Imperials)
After [Legate Quentin Cipius](Skyrim_Legate_Quentin_Cipius.md) and Jarl Balgruuf's meeting, Irileth will approach Proventus Avenicci about her concerns regarding the guards sent from the palace to fight the invading Stormcloaks. <sup>[*verification needed — Is this a one-time event after the meeting or ambient dialogue that takes place after Message to Whiterun (Imperials)?*]</sup>

**Irileth**: *"I am concerned about the Jarl's safety. Too many of our guards have been redeployed to fight the Stormcloaks."* **Proventus**: *"What would you have me do? If we do not send men to fight in the war, you'll find yourself guarding one of Ulfric's men."* **Irileth**: *"And if an assassin slips past the night watch, we won't have a Jarl at all. Without Balgruuf's leadership, Whiterun will descend into chaos."* **Proventus**: *"Nonsense. If Balgruuf falls, someone wil take his place, for better or worse. In the meantime, we have a war to win. And win it we must."*
### Battle for Whiterun (Stormcloaks)
Once the battle starts, Irileth will be at Dragonsreach with Balgruuf. Upon reaching the throne room you will be greeted to this exchange:

"Halt in the name of the Jarl Balgruuf the Greater!"** **Balgruuf**: *"It's a little late for that, don't you think?"* **Irileth**: *"Stay back, lord!"* **Balgruuf**: *"I'll be damned if I let this rabble take my city without raising my own sword."* **Irileth**: *"Protect the Jarl with your lives!"* After this conversation, Irileth will attack you until you get Balgruuf to surrender.
