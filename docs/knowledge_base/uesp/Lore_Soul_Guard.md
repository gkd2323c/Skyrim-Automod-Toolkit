### Bonemen
*We die.
We pray.
To live.
We serve.*
— *The Boneman's Oath* (listen Link ⏵) The [Bonemen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boneman) are skeletal undead that serve the Ideal Masters for eternity without a will of their own.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSBLS-2)</sup> ​They are often ghostly, and can be tinted black or red.

### Keeper
The [Keepers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Keeper) (or **Boneyard Keepers**) are towering servants of the Ideal Masters who reside in the Soul Cairn. Charged with the incarceration of [Valerica](Lore_Valerica.md), they herd imprisoned souls and drain their energy to maintain the barrier in front of the Boneyard.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Valerica-4)</sup>

### Mistmen
The [Mistmen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mistman) are floating, ghostly undead that "blend in the glory of the transcendent spirit." Therefore, they see death as a cleansing of fears and shame, and a state of serenity. They are content with the Soul Cairn, which they view as a refuge for peace and love. They are described as collective manifestations of happy spirits that are in service to the Ideal Masters.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BSMM-5)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/BS-creature-Ghost.gif/200px-BS-creature-Ghost.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-creature-Ghost.gif) Mistman (Battlespire)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-creature-Mistman.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Mistman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mistman.jpg) Mistman (Skyrim)

### Reaper
The [Reaper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reaper) is the shadowy figure of an executioner with burning red eyes. It can be summoned in the [Soul Cairn](Lore_Soul_Cairn.md) once three Reaper gem fragments are placed on an altar within its lair. It is known to vomit green bile during combat. Those fortunate enough to survive an encounter with the Reaper may find that it has a Daedra's heart in its chest. As such, the Reaper may be a creature of Daedric origin as opposed to a mere specter.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRReaper-6)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/SR-creature-Reaper.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Reaper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Reaper.jpg) Reaper (Skyrim)

### Wrathmen
*From fifty Fathers
Frozen in slavepast
Rip from the wraithloom
Sunder the lifeweave
Lock tight in earthgrip
Hold firm in gravefast*
— *The Ritual for Ending of Wrathmen* [Wrathmen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wrathman), known singularly as the **Soul Which Never Dies**, either appear as [wraiths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wraith) or [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) -like skeletal undead, but are ultimately husks of their former selves. They are void of most emotions, except that they are in a state of eternal suffering,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSWM-7)</sup> and can only truly be freed from their bonds of torment through a ritual.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BSBRE-8)</sup> Should one encounter wrathmen, they will seek to sate their hunger for life by feasting on them.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSWM-7)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/BS-creature-Wraith_02.png/200px-BS-creature-Wraith_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-creature-Wraith_02.png) Wrathman (Battlespire)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/SR-creature-Wrathman.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Wrathman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wrathman.jpg) Wrathman (Skyrim)
