# Orichalc Scalpel

The Orichalc Scalpel was an [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) broadsword wielded by [King Emeric Cumberland](Lore_Emeric.md). Its owner described it as flashing and humming "like a blade in a sawmill". During an engagement against a group of [Oldgate](Lore_Oldgate.md) Lancers as part of [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), Emeric, without his armor for protection, cut through several of the Lancers with the aid of the blade and his personal guard. This would prove to be the turning point of an attempt to breach [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) during a siege of the city, forcing the attackers to continue their siege.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Its name suggests that is was made of [orichalcum](Lore_Orichalcum.md).
