[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The **Tundra Plain** or **Tundra Plains**, also known as the **Whiterun Tundra**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSC-1)</sup> is an ecological region that takes up a majority of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) along with small parts of [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md), [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), and [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is a vast plain with few trees, mostly flat but occasionally broken up by hills and outcrops. The Plain is dotted with a number of odd barrows and [Giant](Lore_Giant.md) camps, while the center is dominated by [Fort Greymoor](Lore_Fort_Greymoor.md) and [Lone Mountain](Lore_Lone_Mountain.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup>

## Geography
The western section is the [Tundra Plateau](Lore_Tundra_Plateau.md), a rocky area nearing the Reach, where [Fort Sungard](Lore_Fort_Sungard.md) is situated. The central and largest section is the Western Plains (or Greymoor Foothills), which include [Rorikstead](Lore_Rorikstead.md) and the foothills around [Fort Greymoor](Lore_Fort_Greymoor.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ho JG-3)</sup> The eastern section is the White River Valley, and the northwest section bounded by the Karth and [Hjaal](Lore_River_Hjaal.md) rivers is the Hjaalmarch Hills.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> A lone mountain is found in the center of the otherwise empty terrain, and stands as the only mountain in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup>

It encompasses much of west-central Whiterun, bordered by the [Brittleshin Hills](Lore_Brittleshin_Hills.md) and [Pine Forest](Lore_Pine_Forest.md) to the south,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> the [Karth River Forest](Lore_Karth_River_Forest.md) and [Sundered Hills](Lore_Sundered_Hills.md) across the west, the [Drajkmyr Marsh](Lore_Drajkmyr_Marsh.md), [Skyborn Range](Lore_Skyborn_Range.md), and [Giants' Gap](Lore_Giants%27_Gap.md) in the north, and finally, the [White River](Lore_White_River.md) running through the mountains of Throat of the World and Shearpoint that lead towards [Frostwater Tundra](Lore_Frostwater_Tundra.md) to the east.
