You receive two wings for each butterfly you catch. They can be used in conjunction with a [blue mountain flower](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md) and a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md) to create the second most valuable (monetarily) mixture in the game. It has the same alchemical effects as [chaurus hunter antennae](Skyrim_Chaurus_Hunter_Antennae.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> and [ancestor moth wings](Skyrim_Ancestor_Moth_Wing.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.

## Ingredients
13 guaranteed samples can be found in 11 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 2 in [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [Palace of the Kings Upstairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Butterflies appear at butterfly spawning sites. Each spawning site generates up to three butterflies, with a random mix of both blue and [orange](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monarch_Butterfly) varieties, between 6am and 6pm. The same sites also generate [luna moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth) and [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) between 8pm and 11pm. There are 515 spawning sites in 143 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 21 around [Talking Stone Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Talking_Stone_Camp) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 17 inside [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 14 around [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 13 around [Lund's Hut](Skyrim_Lund%27s_Hut.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 13 around [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 12 around [Swindler's Den](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swindler%27s_Den) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 11 inside [Shadowgreen Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowgreen_Cavern) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 11 around [Whiterun Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Imperial_Camp) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 11 around [Whitewatch Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whitewatch_Tower) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 10 around [Autumnshade Clearing](Skyrim_Autumnshade_Clearing.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 10 around [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))

Butterflies, [luna moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth), & [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) can respawn if you exit the cell and reenter. In cities this can be done easily by going in & out of the main gate or by entering and exiting a building. There is a spawn point outside [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and spawn points inside and outside of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) that can be repeatedly collected quickly.

## Notes
All four alchemical properties are shared with [chaurus hunter antennae](Skyrim_Chaurus_Hunter_Antennae.md) and [ancestor moth wings](Skyrim_Ancestor_Moth_Wing.md). Combining blue butterfly wings with either of the two ingredients will therefore result in all four effects being discovered at once.
