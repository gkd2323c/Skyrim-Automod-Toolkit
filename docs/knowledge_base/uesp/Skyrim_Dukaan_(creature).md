[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-creature-Dukaan.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Dukaan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dukaan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dukaan.jpg) Dukaan attacking **Dukaan** is a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) entombed within [White Ridge Sanctum](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Sanctum.md) in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). He carries an [eponymous mask](Skyrim_Dukaan_(item).md).

He is a frost-based dragon priest, able to cast [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md) and a unique spell called Frost Stream, which is similar to the [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md) shout. He also uses the spells [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), and a unique version of the new spell [Conjure Seeker](Skyrim_Conjure_Seeker.md), which specifically summons a [high seeker](Skyrim_High_Seeker.md).

## Notes
- Dukaan means "dishonor" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
