# Greymarch

| --- |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SI-place-Ordered_Fringe.jpg/200px-SI-place-Ordered_Fringe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Ordered_Fringe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Ordered_Fringe.jpg) The Isles, taken by Order *Madness always yields to the march of Order!*
—Priest of Order<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po O-1)</sup> The **Greymarch** is an event that happens at the end of every era in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), intended to wipe out the entire realm so that [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) must rebuild it. It starts with the appearance of large [crystalline obelisks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Obelisk_of_Order) that emerge from the ground. These obelisks produce and power [Jyggalag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jyggalag)'s minions, the [Knights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Knight_of_Order) and [Priests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Priest_of_Order) of Order.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SISheogorath-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-3)</sup> The Greymarch is the end of the cycle that the Daedric Princes cursed Jyggalag to endure every era.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TP-4)</sup> It is characterized by irresistible brute force, with very little in the way of subterfuge or any variation to previous methods.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SISheogorath-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SI-quest-Symbols_of_Office_03.jpg/200px-SI-quest-Symbols_of_Office_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-Symbols_of_Office_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-Symbols_of_Office_03.jpg) Sheogorath transforming into Jyggalag It is unknown when the Greymarch first began. Long ago, before recorded time, the Daedric Prince of Order, Jyggalag, was growing in power and influence, spreading across realms and dimensions.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SIJyggalag-5)</sup> The other Princes, growing fearful of Jyggalag's power, cursed him to become an embodiment of what he hated most: chaos and madness. The Prince of Madness, Sheogorath, was born that day. Sheogorath turned Jyggalag's domain into the Shivering Isles, a realm reflecting his madness, and once every era he would become Jyggalag once more to destroy the Isles and reclaim his realm. With that accomplished, he would transform back into Sheogorath, who would be left to rebuild his realm.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SISheogorath-2)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SI-quest-The_End_of_Order_2.jpg/200px-SI-quest-The_End_of_Order_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-The_End_of_Order_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-The_End_of_Order_2.jpg) Jyggalag's return to the realm Sheogorath has attempted several methods to halt the Greymarch, including summoning mortal champions to the Shivering Isles to fight back against the forces of Order. Sheogorath then disappears to become Jyggalag. The Greymarch ends when Jyggalag destroys the Shivering Isles,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SISheogorath-2)</sup> It is unknown if all such mortal champion mantles the role of the Mad God, as only one has ever successfully beat Jyggalag and ended his curse.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SI-6)</sup> It is possible that [Arden-Sul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arden-Sul) was a mortal who had mantled Sheogorath previously, as suggested by the beliefs of the [Zealots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zealots) and [Heretics](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heretics),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> [Haskill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Haskill), a [Vestige](Lore_Vestige_(creature).md) who serves as Lord Sheogorath's chamberlain, came about from such a mantling.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CHAYQ-9)</sup> Other methods Sheogorath has used to try and stop the Greymarch include filling up a pit with clouds or clowns.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SISheogorath-2)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Haskill-10)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) arrived in the Isles to play the role of the mortal champion. The hero mantled Sheogorath, and also defeated Jyggalag and the forces of Order.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SI-6)</sup> In doing so, they finally broke the cycle by defeating Jyggalag in combat, and Jyggalag himself stated he was free from the curse.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SI-6)</sup> It is worth noting Sheogorath believed that the hero was a novel approach,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SI-6)</sup> despite having been mantled by mortals in the past; as Sheogorath, the Hero of Kvatch stated that the title of Mad God is one which "gets passed down from me to myself every few thousand years".<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SRSheogorath-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

Few mortal inhabitants of the Isles ever live to see a second Greymarch; although they do not age without Sheogorath's will, few seem to survive the event.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SI-6)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CHAYQ-9)</sup>

## Notes
- Sheogorath states that the Greymarch happens at the end of every era, but no indication is given that these eras are the same as Tamrielic [Eras](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eras). Some sources state that a Greymarch occurs once every 1,000 years,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TP-4)</sup> while Sheogorath himself claims a few thousand.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SRSheogorath-11)</sup>
