## History
Carinar was born in the Reach as a son of the chief of the [Quicktalon Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Quicktalon_Clan) long before [1E 2502](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2502),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-1)</sup> before the times "anyone can remember".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Preview-2)</sup>

He eventually plotted against his father and killed him. His actions led to the sundering of the Quicktalon Clan, which split into two factions - Spiritblood Clan, which supported Carinar, and the other, loyal to the deceased chieftain. Carinar decided to move south. One of his followers was legendary [Aydolan](Lore_Aydolan.md), the storyteller who was singing tales of the triumph and strength of Carinar during this travel. Carinar bestowed him the title of the first [Vateshran](Lore_Vateshrans.md) of the Spiritblood Clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-1)</sup>

Little is known of his legacy, but the Spiritblood Clan inhabited the southern parts of the Reach, over a thousand years after the foundation of the clan, in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-A_Challenge_of_Worth-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vateshran.27s_Rites-4)</sup> and was famed for its valiant and powerful warriors.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VH-5)</sup>
