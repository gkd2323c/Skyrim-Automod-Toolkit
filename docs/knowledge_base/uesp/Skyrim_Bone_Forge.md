Any other combination results in a dead skeleton.

## Notes
- The minion summoned using the forge does not count towards your maximum number of summons, making it a free permanent follower.
- It does not regenerate health and is protected, being downed when its health is too low instead of being outright killed. It can be healed using the [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing) and [Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead) spells.
