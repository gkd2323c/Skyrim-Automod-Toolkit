## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
2. Go to the specified dungeon and retrieve the staff.
3. Return it to Neloth in exchange for a different staff.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Asking [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) if you can aid him in his research may have him make a more personal request. He will explain his fascination with the staffs of [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md), the original Shadowmage, and will reveal that he has located one such staff in a random dungeon. The location is [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md), and can include dungeons both on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Dragonborn_Places.md) and in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md). Skyrim is far more likely, as there are considerably more potential locations there. Retrieve the staff from the specified dungeon and return it to Neloth. He will reward you with a random generic staff from his own collection.

## Notes
- The quest can be done multiple times. At lower levels, it is one of the only side quests you will get from Neloth.
- The location of the staff will be a [bandit camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Bandit_Camps), [dragon lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Dragon_Lairs), [dragon priest lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Dragon_Priest_Lairs), [draugr crypt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Draugr_Crypts), [Falmer hive](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Falmer_Hives), [Forsworn camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Forsworn_Camps), [hagraven nest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Hagraven_Nests), [vampire lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Vampire_Lairs), or [warlock lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs); only locations which have a boss container will be chosen.

## Bugs
- If the dungeon is in Skyrim, the quest marker will point towards the Skyrim map marker in Solstheim. After collecting the staff, the quest marker will **not** be pointing to the Solstheim map marker in Skyrim.
- This quest may also choose dungeons with sections becoming inaccessible if cleared earlier, like [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md).
