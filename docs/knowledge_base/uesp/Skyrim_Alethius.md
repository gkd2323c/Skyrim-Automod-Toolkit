[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-npc-Alethius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alethius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alethius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alethius.jpg) Alethius **Alethius** is a deceased [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) whose corpse can be found just before the entrance to [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel), within the Nchuand-Zel Excavation Site underneath [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He wears a full set of [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md), and carries a [note](Skyrim_Alethius%27s_Notes.md) that begins the quest [The Lost Expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition). He was assigned to guard the research expedition into Nchuand-Zel, but met his untimely death, likely by [Nimhe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nimhe), the giant [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) guarding the entrance to Nchuand-Zel, whose lair is just above Alethius's body.

## Related Quests
- **[The Lost Expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition)**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel).

## Notes
- His note is written to someone named Salonia, and [two](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Carvain) [NPCs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Caelia) with that first name exist in Skyrim. However, neither of them appear to have any connection to him.
- It's possible that he was created with [Bassianus Axius](Skyrim_Bassianus_Axius.md) as a base, as they share the same voice type and it would explain Alethius' otherwise random faction inclusions.

## Bugs
- Due to the fact that he's part of the [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, addresses this issue. He has been removed from the crime faction.
