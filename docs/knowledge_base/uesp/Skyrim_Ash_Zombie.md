[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg) Ash Zombie **Ash Zombies** are [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) victims who have been transformed into disfigured [ash creatures](Skyrim_Ash_Creatures.md) by [Erden Relvel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel) using the power of the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur). As part of this transformation, Erden removes the soft tissue of the upper face down to the bone, as Dagoth Ur did. However, Erden goes a step further and the zombies' lips are ritualistically stitched closed. The zombies have no dialogue as a result.

Ash Zombies are relatively weak enemies, as they only attack with their fists. They can be encountered attacking [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) and guarding Erden Relvel at the [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md) graveyard, and a group of them can also attack you randomly as a [world interaction](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md).
