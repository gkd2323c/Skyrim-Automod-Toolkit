Treaty of the Three Clans *A declaration of peace between Reach three tribes*
---
Let it be known that the most committed warriors of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) gathered here on the 3rd day of Frostfall to put aside their differences and join together against their shared enemy, the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).

These clans were represented:
[Rageclaw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rageclaw_Clan), [Boneshaper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boneshaper_Clan), [Stonetalon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stonetalon_Clan)
