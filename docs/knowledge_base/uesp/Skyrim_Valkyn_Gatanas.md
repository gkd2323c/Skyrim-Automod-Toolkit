[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-npc-Valkyn_Gatanas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valkyn_Gatanas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valkyn_Gatanas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valkyn_Gatanas.jpg) Valkyn Gatanas [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-creature-Daedric_Horse_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Daedric_Horse_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Daedric_Horse_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Daedric_Horse_02.jpg) Gatanas tending to his Daedric Horse Valkyn Gatanas is one of the main antagonists of [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). He and [Valkyn Methats](Skyrim_Valkyn_Methats.md) are to serve as heralds for the coming of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). The artifact [Scourge](Skyrim_Scourge.md) can be found on his body after his final demise, as well as a pair of [Daedric Gauntlets of Negation](Skyrim_Daedric_Gauntlets_of_Negation.md) that allowed him to wield it.

## Notes
- Gatanas previously appeared in *[Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Dremora_Gatanas)*.
- Prior to [Patch 1.6.629](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#Version_1.6.629), Gatanas did not possess the Daedric Gauntlets of Negation. This item was added in an update to the Creation to explain his ability to wield Scourge despite being a [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md), though this was unnecessary as Daedra are only banished upon using Scourge's keyword 'Equality.'
