## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to an innkeeper.
2. Read the [Letter from Shogarz gro-Batul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Shogarz_gra-Batul).
3. Eliminate the bandits in [Lost Knife Hideout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Knife_Hideout).
4. Retrieve the Headman's Cleaver.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Talking to any innkeeper will give you this quest. They will hand you a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Shogarz_gra-Batul) that explains that remnants of a group of bandits called the Greencap Bandits have stolen the Headman's Cleaver of the [Bloodfall Kingdom](Lore_Bloodfall_Kingdom.md). The Bloodfall Queen wants vengeance and will grant the Headman's Cleaver to whoever eliminates the Greencap Bandits.

Once you have read the letter, travel to [Lost Knife Hideout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Knife_Hideout). There are two parts to the cave system - Lost Knife Cave and Lost Knife Hideout. In Lost Knife Cave, there will be several Greencap Bandits you will have to fight to find the entrance to Lost Knife Hideout. You will have to defeat more Greencap Bandits inside, until you reach the area with cages. This is where the thief, known as the 'Darkwater Snake', will be located, as well as several Greencap Bandits. Defeat the Darkwater Snake and loot his body for the stolen cleaver, as well as a full set of unenchanted Orcish armor. [Shogarz gro-Batul's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shogarz_gro-Batul) dead body will be lying in another cage, and looting him will give you [Shogarz's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shogarz%27s_Journal). [Urzog's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urzog%27s_Journal) will also be sitting on one of the tables.

## Bugs
- It is possible that the [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Shogarz_gra-Batul) from Shogarz doesn't appear in your inventory after getting the quest. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) A workaround is either to either enter `setstage cc BGSSSE068_Quest 30` in the console to update the quest stage, or `player.additem FExxx801 1` to place the letter in your inventory.
