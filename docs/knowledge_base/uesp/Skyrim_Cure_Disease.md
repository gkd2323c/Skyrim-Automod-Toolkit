*Alchemy description:* **Cures all diseases.**
**Cure Disease** is a magical effect that cures all of the target's [diseases](Skyrim_Disease.md). The effect will also cure [Sanguinare Vampiris](Skyrim_Sanguinare_Vampiris.md), but only within the first three days after infection.

Other than potions, methods of curing diseases include:

- The [Blessings](Skyrim_Blessings.md) received from altars.
- The [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md), by simply asking one of them to cure you.
- [Garlic Bread](Skyrim_Garlic_Bread.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- [Ironwood Soup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ironwood_Soup)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup>

Since Cure Disease does not rely on magnitude, simply eating an ingredient can be as effective as drinking a potion. This always works for Hawk Feathers, which have Cure Disease as the first effect, and can work for other ingredients if you have taken ranks of the [Experimenter](Skyrim_Experimenter.md) perk (see Notes).

## Notes
- Unlike past Elder Scrolls games, there is no way to cast a Cure Disease spell, as one does not exist in the game.
- One good source of Cure Disease potions and alchemy ingredients are members of the [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md), who nearly always carry Cure Disease potions and/or ingredients with the effect. [Vigilants of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilants_of_Stendarr.md) also often carry one or more Cure Disease potions. [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> has some Cure Disease potions as well.
- If you have one or more ranks of the Alchemy perk "Experimenter" and eat an ingredient with a Cure Disease effect while having a disease (such as Brain Rot), it will be cured because the property of the ingredient was discovered. However, such ingredients where Cure Disease isn't the first effect (such as Mudcrab Chitin) will not work to cure diseases contracted afterwards when consumed, as you are no longer "discovering" the potential effects of the ingredient.
- Cure Disease potions will not cure [lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md). They also won't work on [vampirirsm](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) if you wait too long.
- With the *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* add-on, you can give an infected [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md) a Cure Disease potion in exchange for 100 gold and the location of a vampire lair. Only a found or purchased potion will work; one created using Alchemy won't. Afterwards, the hunter will be available for bartering with once again. If you cannot, the hunter will flee in terror.
- All ingredients that share this effect only have positive effects, except for Chokeweed<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> and Hunger Tongue<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup>.
