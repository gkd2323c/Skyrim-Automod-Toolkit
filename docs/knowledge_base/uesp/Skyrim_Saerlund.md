[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-npc-Saerlund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Saerlund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saerlund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saerlund.jpg) Saerlund **Saerlund**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md), is the son of Jarl [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) and brother of [Harrald](Skyrim_Harrald.md). He lives in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

In conversation he reveals that, despite his family being supporters of [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), he despises Ulfric, and accuses him of being selfish in that he only wants power, and not freedom. Because of his dislike of Ulfric, his family think Saerlund is sick, to which end Laila asks her court wizard, [Wylandriah](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md), to diagnose and treat. To make matters worse, according to Saerlund, Laila and her family have shunned Saerlund to the point of denying him his inheritance as well as threatening him with disownment for his disloyalty. It would also seem that the staff of Mistveil Keep are against him as well, as staff members are often seen pushing him around.

Now disowned by his family and incarcerated like a "common criminal", Saerlund spends most of his time sitting on a bench behind the jarl's throne. However, he does get a couple breaks from this sentence. He has breakfast at 8am, lunch at noon and dinner at 8pm, all meals lasting for two hours. He may be allowed to eat, but Saerlund never sleeps and can always be found sitting on the bench when he is not eating.

Saerlund wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8713) with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). He wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality, and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Dialogue
Should you approach him, Saerlund will quickly think that you are going to ridicule him just like his family has:

*"Come to gloat, have we? To poke fun at the jarl's youngest son?"* *"I can feel you laughing behind my back."* *"Did Harrald send you to taunt me?"* You can ask him why you would want to ridicule him:

**Why would I ridicule you?** *"Because I did something not a soul in this bloodstained house of war has the backbone to do... dared to speak my mind. I dared speak of the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and the lies that have been spread by Ulfric, the leader of the Stormcloaks. Now my mother's stripped me of my heritage and incarcerated me here like a common criminal and my brother has all but disowned me. Be wary what you say around here, friend. You'll find not all take kindly to 'insurrection.'"* **What sort of lies has Ulfric been spreading?** *"Ulfric only cares about one thing... Ulfric. He's ordained himself the future High King of Skyrim and steps on anyone that gets in his way. He's begun a rebellion against those that wish to eradicate the worship of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) and uses it as his rallying cry. His cause may be true, but the man is a lie... all he holds in his heart his * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * lust for the throne."*

## Notes
- He and Harrald will not leave after Imperial annexation of Riften; in addition, his dialogue will not change after Laila has been exiled. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.

- It seems like the developers didn't actually intend to exile Saerlund. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states: *"Should Laila ever become exiled due to the Empire gaining control of Riften, Saerlund will remain behind and live in the Keep in defiance of his mother's beliefs"*.
- The wording that Saerlund uses when stating *"incarcerated me here like a common criminal"* seems to imply he would be located at Riften Jail at one point, although there are no traces of this to be found anywhere else.
