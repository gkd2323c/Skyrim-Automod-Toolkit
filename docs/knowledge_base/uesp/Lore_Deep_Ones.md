# Deep Ones

The Deep Ones are a [race](Lore_Races.md) of beings who dwell deep underground beneath the town of [Hackdirt](Lore_Hackdirt.md), an isolated [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) settlement in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md). They are worshipped as [gods](Lore_Gods.md) by the inhabitants of Hackdirt, who built the Chapel of the Brethren in their honor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> Worshippers who have changed the most are known as the Brethren. They possess large eyes and a strong sense of smell, and dwell underground in the Hackdirt Caverns to be closer to the Deep Ones. The other residents descend into the caverns at night for "Gatherings" where they worship the Deep Ones; tunnels were dug connecting each house to the caverns for this purpose.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jiv-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/OB-npc-Hackdirt_Brother_92c5a.jpg/200px-OB-npc-Hackdirt_Brother_92c5a.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-npc-Hackdirt_Brother_92c5a.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-npc-Hackdirt_Brother_92c5a.jpg) One of the Brethren; note the large eyes [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-audio-Hackdirt_Caverns.ogg) Growls from within Hackdirt Caverns Hackdirt was refounded<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) as a mining town by Irlav Moslin. Digging in the mines, the villagers discovered natural caverns running beneath the town, and supposedly discovered the Deep Ones.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jiv-2)</sup> Irlav met with the Chief of the Deep Ones, who taught Irlav his language and his runes, passing on the ancient lore of his people. The villagers recorded this lore in the *[Bible of the Deep Ones](Lore_Bible_of_the_Deep_Ones.md)* and devoted themselves to following these teachings for eternity.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Bot DO-4)</sup> This bible was written using [Daedric runes](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md), and was an excerpt from the obscure text *[N'Gasta! Kvata! Kvakis!](Lore_N%27Gasta!_Kvata!_Kvakis!.md)* written by the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Bot DO-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The Deep Ones helped the inhabitants of Hackdirt, making them rich, but demanding blood sacrifice and worse in return.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jiv-2)</sup> These activities ultimately resulted in the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) being called in, resulting in deaths and the destruction of buildings.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Honditar-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> The arrival of the soldiers drove off the Deep Ones, who never came back.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jiv-2)</sup>

People continued to live at Hackdirt and in the caverns below, including Vlanhonder Moslin, the grandson of Irlav. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), around thirty years after the Legion's intervention, the villagers planned to bring back the Deep Ones through ritual sacrifice. They captured an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) named Dar-Ma, the daughter of a trader from [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), and imprisoned her in the caverns. However, this plan was foiled when the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) arrived at Hackdirt in search of Dar-Ma, resulting in her rescue and the deaths of many of the Brethren.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

The only remaining presence of the Deep Ones is a low growling which can be heard throughout the Hackdirt Caverns.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-OBPrima-10)</sup><sup>:249</sup>
