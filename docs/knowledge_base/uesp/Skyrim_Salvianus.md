[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-npc-Salvianus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Salvianus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Salvianus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Salvianus.jpg) Salvianus **Salvianus** is a retired soldier who fought in the [Great War](Lore_Wars.md#The_Great_War) between the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) and the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). He subsequently went mad and moved into [The Ratway Warrens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway_Warrens) (below [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)). He spends his days sitting in his cell drinking, eating bread or sleeping on his mat. In his room, which he never leaves, is an Imperial Officer's Helmet, a randomly enchanted [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a leveled potion of Pickpocketing, and a leveled potion of Light Feet. He is one of the two inhabitants of the Warrens that do not turn hostile when entering their room.
