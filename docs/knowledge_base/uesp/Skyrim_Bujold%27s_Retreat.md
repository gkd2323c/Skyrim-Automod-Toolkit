[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat.jpg) Bujold's Retreat **Bujold's Retreat** is a [camp](Skyrim_Camps.md) east of [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) containing Nords exiled from the hall by [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md).

It is named for the group's leader, [Bujold the Unworthy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold_the_Unworthy).

## Related Quests
- **[Elmus Favor Quest (mead)](Skyrim_Elmus_Favor_Quest_(mead).md)**: Retrieve one bottle of [Ashfire Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ashfire_Mead) for [Elmus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elmus).
- **[Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md)**: Help [Bujold the Unworthy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold_the_Unworthy) retake [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md). *
- **[The Chief of Thirsk Hall](Skyrim_The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall.md)**: Help out a [Riekling Chief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Chief) at [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md). *

* It is only possible to complete one of these quests, as completion of one will automatically cause the other to fail. However, you can start and complete the first two objectives in the [riekling chief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Chief)'s quest, then change allegiance before agreeing to his third objective by deciding to instead support the Nords camped at Bujold's Retreat.

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bujold%27s_Retreat_02.jpg) Fishing supplies and boats The camp consists of five small tents, a larger tent, and three campfires. Each of the small tents contains an owned hay pile, and the larger tent contains three. All eight hay piles and all low-value items in the camp will be free to use and take after completing a [favor for Elmus](Skyrim_Elmus_Favor_Quest_(mead).md).

To the northwest is a campfire with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)) hanging over it and two small tents. Two bowls of [horker stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Stew) are on a rock next to the campfire just outside the west tent. Between the tents are four sacks, an unusable barrel, two crates, and a basket containing five [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). The north tent contains a random [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet), with a wood pile and a [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) behind the tent.

To the southeast is another campfire surrounded by a bench and the other three small tents. To the left of the northeast tent is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), and a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack), with a random [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) and a quiver of arrows inside the tent. To the right of the east tent is a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). To the left of the southwest tent are two bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and three sacks on and next to a tree stump, with a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) inside the tent and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) to the right of the tent.

To the southwest up a short slope is the larger tent, flanked by a third campfire and a bench. The tent contains a knapsack, an apprentice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and a table and bench holding a random helmet, a random [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), three bottles of ale, a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead), a [horker loaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf), a random [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), and a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse).

To the east on the shore below the camp is a small dock with two rowboats, with three sacks on the pier and a fourth sack near a stack of three crates on land. Next to the crates is a framed fishing net holding an owned [silverside perch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch) and an unowned [histcarp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Histcarp) and [cyrodilic spadetail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail). The [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) Creation adds a fishing station at the end of the pier.

Many plants grow in and around the camp, including [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower), [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw), [trama roots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root), and [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod). Nearby [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) may also engage the camp's inhabitants.

## Notes
- The camp will be abandoned if you help its inhabitants retake Thirsk Mead Hall, leaving only the remains of the two lower campfires and an overturned unusable bench. Any items stored in the containers prior to the Nords' departure will therefore be lost. The map marker will be removed as well.
