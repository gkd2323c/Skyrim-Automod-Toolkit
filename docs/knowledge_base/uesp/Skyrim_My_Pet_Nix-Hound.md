## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read the [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Nix-Hound.md).
2. Buy a nix-hound from [Revus Sarvani](Skyrim_Revus_Sarvani.md) or [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Nix-Hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), this quest no longer appears in your journal. The Nix-Hound can still be acquired from Revus.**

Once you've installed the [Nix-Hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), you will receive this quest and a [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Nix-Hound_(Revus).md), which directs you to talk to [Revus Sarvani](Skyrim_Revus_Sarvani.md) at his camp west of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). Revus is not an essential NPC, so it is possible to kill him prior to installing this Creation. If this occurs, you will instead receive a [different notice](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Nix-Hound_(Geldis).md) directing you to [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) at [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

Travel to Solstheim and locate the specified NPC. Offer to purchase the nix-hound for 400 gold and you will be given a [spell tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Teleport_Pet_Nix-Hound.md) that teaches you a spell to teleport the nix-hound to you. You will be directed to introduce yourself to the nearby nix-hound to complete the quest. It will now serve as an animal follower, with the added ability to carry items, provide you with random [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), or be sent to wait at a specific city or player house.

## Notes
- The Nix-Hound Creation also adds enemy nix-hounds to the wilds of Solstheim.
- The pet Nix-Hound behaves identically to other [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) pets, such as the [mudcrab pet](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md) added by the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.md) Creation.
- The Nix-Hound can be purchased by speaking directly to the Nix-Hound instead of Revus.

## Bugs
- It may be impossible to purchase the pet Nix-Hound in some non-English languages. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.640, fixes this bug.
