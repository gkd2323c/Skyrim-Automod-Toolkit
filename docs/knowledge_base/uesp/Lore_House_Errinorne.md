# House Errinorne

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-npc-High_Kinlady_Estre.jpg/200px-ON-npc-High_Kinlady_Estre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-High_Kinlady_Estre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-High_Kinlady_Estre.jpg) High Kinlady Estre of House Errinorne House Errinorne was a noble family from the city of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). They were present on the island of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) since the late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), descending from royal [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) blood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOEstre-1)</sup> House Errinorne ruled Skywatch as High Kinlords and High Kinladies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOContraband1-2)</sup> Off the coast of the city is [Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md), which was used as a shipping and warehouse complex for the merchant city, part of the old [Buraniim Sea-Complex](Lore_Buraniim_Sea-Complex.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AEIII-3)</sup> It also contained the Errinorne Academy.

## History
The House of Errinorne is known to have existed since the late-Merethic Era.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOContraband1-2)</sup> Some sources claim that Kinlady Entila of Clan Auralus, known for her quest for the Egg of the World, was a member of House Errinorne.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLoad EF-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOContraband2-5)</sup> The house claimed the island east of Skywatch as Errinorne Isle, which became the warehouse complex for the city and nearby [Buraniim Isle](Lore_Buraniim_Isle.md). Its main settlement was the Errinorne Academy, a large school on the island's easternmost hills. However, when the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) invaded Skywatch in [1E 1301](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1301), they took control of the island and used it as their base for the infamous [Sack of Skywatch](Lore_Sack_of_Skywatch.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AEIII-3)</sup> After the Sload's defeat decades later, House Errinorne never rebuilt the academy and it fell into ruin.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOLoad TVK-6)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Skywatch was ruled by High Kinlady [Estre](Lore_Estre.md) of Errinorne. Estre was married to Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md), who was the heir apparent to the Throne of [Alinor](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md) following the disappearance of his older sister, [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md), in [2E 573](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_573). However, in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580), Ayrenn returned and was crowned Queen of Alinor over Naemon.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ATUQ-7)</sup> Estre was quite unhappy about Ayrenn's return, as she wanted to be queen.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOAyrenn-8)</sup> After the formation of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), Estre formed the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md), an [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) supremacist group that sought to dethrone the Queen and destroy the Dominion.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-WDt V-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Lit ES-10)</sup>

In 2E 582, Estre, along with High Kinlord Rilis XIII of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md) hosted Ayrenn and her entourage during her sojourn in Auridon.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MDAA-11)</sup> While Ayrenn was in Skywatch, an Eye of the Queen infiltrated the Veiled Heritance's base on Errinorne Isle, where they discovered that Estre was its leader.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Wt V-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TVF-13)</sup> The Eyes of the Queen then revealed to Ayrenn that Estre was the Veiled Heritance's leader. Estre promptly escaped by teleportation and began operating more overtly.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TVF-13)</sup> Estre and her [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) subsequently fought Ayreen's agents in northern Auridon, but she was defeated and killed attempting to conquer Firsthold.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOSAT-14)</sup> What became of House Errinorne following these events is unknown.

## Errinorne Isle
[Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md), also called the **Veiled Isle** is an island found off the coast of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Errinorne Isle is named after House Errinorne, a noble house that ruled [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md). Errinorne Island was used as a shipping and warehouse complex for the city of Skywatch, but it was later abandoned after the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) took control of the island. The only landmark is the Veiled Keep, which is found in the center of the island.
