## Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp
It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [charred skeever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide) cooking over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Around the campfire are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. West of the campfire are two picketed [horses](Skyrim_Horses.md). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing. To the north is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a round table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. To the northwest is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and an end table. To the left of the commander's tent is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). East of the campfire is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). Beside the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) and some barrels. To the south is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster), consisting of an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons) and [steel weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md).

Any time [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](Skyrim_Joining_the_Legion.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
