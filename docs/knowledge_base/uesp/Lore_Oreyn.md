# Oreyn

The Oreyn family is an [Elvish](Lore_Elf.md) bloodline that consisted of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) that at one point in time ruled a kingdom in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/SR-item-Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.jpg/200px-SR-item-Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.jpg) Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw The earliest known member of the family, [King Faume Toad-Eye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Faume_Toad-Eye), was the leader of this Valenwood clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> Faume's heir, a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWMalacath-2)</sup> who would come to be known as [Oreyn Bearclaw](Lore_Oreyn_Bearclaw.md), was a respected clan hunter and eventually succeeded his father as the leader of the clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup>

Bearclaw accomplished various heroic deeds, most famously the slaying of Glenhwyfaunva, the witch-serpent of the Elven wood, which brought everlasting peace to his clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRHOBB-3)</sup> Bearclaw eventually succumbed to the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md), his legendary [Helm](Lore_Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.md) was enshrined as a monument for future generations of the clan to remember his greatness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup> The clan eventually was divided, and in the process the Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw was lost.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup>

In what could have been no longer than a few decades after Bearclaw's death, one of his descendants, a Bosmer named [Gadnuth Oreyn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gadnuth_Oreyn) aligned with the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) assassin [Naryu Virian](Lore_Naryu_Virian.md) to retrieve the lost Helm of his ancestor, claiming that if he returned the prestigious Helm to his clan, he would be named Treethane. The pair succeeded in retrieving the Helm.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Naryu-4)</sup>

Five generations removed from King Toad-Eye, circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) a Dunmer by the name of [Farvyn Oreyn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Farvyn_Oreyn) had risen to some renown and resided in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). At this time, the name of Oreyn Bearclaw was still widely known, and he was still known as "slayer of beasts and protector of man and mer."<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWGeneric O-5)</sup> For being an Oreyn, Farvyn received great adoration and even a stipend from the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWFarvyn-6)</sup> Deeds such as single handedly slaying a [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) and disposing of troubling [Netches](Lore_Netch.md) were attributed to Farvyn by the locals, who viewed him as a heroic, powerful battlemage.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWGeneric F-7)</sup> He was ultimately far from what he seemed and both he and his personal guards were killed by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWMalacath Quest-8)</sup> His death was ordered by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md), who had wrongly believed Farvyn to be the last of Oreyn Bearclaw's bloodline, but there was at least one other.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWMalacath-2)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBModryn-9)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) another Dunmer, [Modryn Oreyn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Modryn_Oreyn) had established himself as a high-ranking member of the [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), he was known as a no-nonsense, mighty warrior.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Modryn worked alongside the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to root out corruption in the rival [Blackwood Company](Lore_Blackwood_Company.md), eventually destroying the faction altogether. The Hero of Kvatch became the new Master of the Fighters Guild and was given the Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw by Modryn.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBTH-11)</sup>

## Disputed Legacy
The most famous member of Clan Oreyn, Bearclaw is tied to some level of controversy when it comes to his deeds. Oreyn Bearclaw had an [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) ally by the name of Kharag gro-Khar who by some accounts, was the rightful bearer of the Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw and responsible for the various heroics.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWMalacath-2)</sup>

Orcish belief, championed by Malacath himself maintains this as the truth of the matter with the Orcs referring to the Helm as the Helm of Kharag gro-Khar.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWMalacath-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Naryu-4)</sup> The Bosmer see it as a legitimate Bosmer relic and instead remember Kharag as the deceiver who took credit for Bearclaw's deeds and also stealing his sacred Helm.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Naryu-4)</sup> Dunmer and other Morrowind natives see the Oreyn family as true heroes.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWGeneric F-7)</sup> The Empire seems to endorse the Oreyn family to a degree as well, giving members of the family an Imperial stipend.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWFarvyn-6)</sup>

As for the descendants of Bearclaw, Modryn and Gadnuth before him believed the oral history passed down their bloodline of the legacy of Bearclaw.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBModryn-9)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Naryu-4)</sup> Farvyn on the other hand, seems to have believed the disputed account and merely enjoyed the spoils and glory that came with being an Oreyn.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWFarvyn-6)</sup> A scholarly account of various artifacts found in Tamriel, penned by the Last Living [Dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), [Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), recounts the Oreyn Bearclaw telling of events, and makes no mention of the Kharag gro-Khar narrative.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup>
