[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SI-place-Passwall.jpg/200px-SI-place-Passwall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Passwall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Passwall.jpg) Passwall circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Passwall is the main settlement within [the Fringe](Lore_Fringe.md), a region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) separated by high walls from the realm proper. Mortals who find themselves in this realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) are usually restricted to the Fringe until they have received Lord [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s "gift" of madness and can enter the realm through the Gates of Madness. Many such individuals take up residence in Passwall while they wait for Sheogorath's blessing to take hold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SI-quest-Retaking_The_Fringe.jpg/200px-SI-quest-Retaking_The_Fringe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-Retaking_The_Fringe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-quest-Retaking_The_Fringe.jpg) The [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md) invading Passwall Passwall has stood on the edge of the Shivering Isles since the realm was borne from the mists of time. It serves as a defensible outpost for the realm's [Aureal](Lore_Aureal.md) and [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md) armies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SIDesha-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIUdico-3)</sup> The Fringe was historically the first region of the Isles conquered during every [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Retaking-4)</sup> and the ancient complex of [Xeddefen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xeddefen) that runs beneath Passwall seems to exist to facilitate this invasion.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SIDesha-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIUdico-3)</sup>

The residents of Passwall at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) reportedly found the town's buildings abandoned and well-provisioned upon their arrival. The first of these arrivals was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) madman named Shelden, who proclaimed himself mayor.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) passed through the town and killed the [Gatekeeper](Lore_Gatekeeper.md) guarding the Gates of Madness, allowing the residents of Passwall access to the rest of the Isles.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Following the Gatekeeper's death, the Fringe was temporarily replaced by the facility at [Xedilian](Lore_Xedilian.md) as the destination for adventurous mortals attempting to enter the realm.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

When the Greymarch began later that year, the Fringe fell to the [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md), and the remaining residents of Passwall either scattered or joined [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md) to become Priests of Order. The army of Sheogorath made its last stand at the ruins of Passwall, and managed to hold the line long enough for the Hero of Kvatch to shut down the Obelisk of Order in Xeddefen, halting the invasion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Retaking-4)</sup> The Fringe was eventually retaken once the Greymarch was halted, although few of Passwall's residents remained.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

Passwall was still in existence circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), at which point it was supposedly home to a talking [grapefruit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grapefruit) named Stanley.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

## Notes
- The town is not to be confused with the [Passwall](Lore_Passwall_(spell).md) spell, a [spell effect](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Passwall) in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* which allowed the caster to tunnel through solid walls. Knowledge of this spell had been lost by [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
