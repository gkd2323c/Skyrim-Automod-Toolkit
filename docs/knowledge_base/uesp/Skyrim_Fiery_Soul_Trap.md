*Enchanting description:* **If target dies within <dur> seconds, fills a soul gem. Burns the target for <mag> points. Targets on fire take extra damage.**
**Fiery Soul Trap** is a unique version of the more common [Soul Trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md) effect. It is found only on the [Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls](Skyrim_Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.md) in the boss room of [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md), which can then be disenchanted to learn the effect. It has a couple of interesting properties that makes it different from the normal soul trap effect:

- As the name indicates, it does [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) as well as traps souls.
- Second, it is affected by both [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) and [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) perks that relate to its effects as well as the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) perks affecting Fire Damage and Soul Trap.

The second property means that the number of charges you get out of enchanted items made with this effect are calculated using not only your Enchanting skill but also your Conjuration and Destruction skills. This is because this effect is essentially a script that casts versions of the soul trap and fire damage effects separately when activated. The strength of the fire damage is increased only by the [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) perk.

## Notes
- Whether a glitch or intentional, if you strike a corpse with a weapon holding this enchantment, you will receive a message that the soul trap effect was resisted.
- Though the number of charges is increased based on all 3 relevant skills (Enchanting, Conjuration, and Destruction), only the duration of the soul trap effect is taken into account when enchanting. This allows an extremely high number of charges (1000+) to be available if using a [Grand Soul](Skyrim_Grand_Soul_Gem.md) and setting the soul trap to a 1 second duration. It is unknown whether this is intentional.
- The fire damage magnitude of the enchantment is fixed and isn't affected by anything that increases enchantment magnitude, including Enchanting Skill, Enchanter perks, the Fire Enchanter perk or Fortify Enchanting potions. Augmented Flames perks, which act as fire damage multipliers, will increase the damage to 12 and 15 points respectively because they aren't multipliers for enchantment magnitude.
- Enemies that are immune to magic (such as [Orchendor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor)) are also immune to all forms of Soul Trap. This includes Fiery Soul Trap. The Fire Damage effect from this enchantment still works on them unless they're immune to fire.
- Dragons cannot be soul trapped, since instead dragon souls are absorbed automatically in your character. However, the Fire Damage effect from this enchantment still works on them.
- Dwarven automatons also cannot be soul trapped, since they don't have souls in the first place. However, the Fire Damage effect from this enchantment still works on them.
- If you have the Extra Effect perk from the Enchanting skill tree, you can put both the regular Soul Trap and this version of it on the same weapon, though doing this has no particular use.
- If you do not desire to disenchant the unique weapon for this enchantment, you can mimic it with the Extra Effect perk and the Fire Damage and Soul Trap enchanting effects. You will not get as many charges out of the weapon (due to the double enchantment), but you can make the fire enchantment far stronger than what is available with this one.
- [Followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) using weapons enchanted with the Fiery Soul Trap effect may fill all of their soul gems of the type used by the trapped soul after only trapping one soul. (For instance, a follower killing a soul trapped Frost Troll while carrying 17 Common Soul Gems fills them all, instead of just one.)
