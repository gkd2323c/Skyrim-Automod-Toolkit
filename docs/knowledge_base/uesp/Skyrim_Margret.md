[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Margret.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Margret.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Margret.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Margret.jpg) Margret in her room at the Silver-Blood Inn **Margret** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) visiting [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) from her home in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

As you enter Markarth for the first time, you may notice her shopping at a market stall, busy discussing her purchase with the stall manager. [Weylin](Skyrim_Weylin.md) will suddenly grab her and attempt to kill her. You can either let this happen, or save Margret by killing Weylin. If saved, she will be thankful and give you a necklace.

Saving Margret can be helpful in later quests having to do with investigating the marketplace attack. Since Margret will still be alive, you can ask her directly about the attack instead of having to ask multiple unwilling witnesses. While speaking with Margret in the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) during [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md), if you ask her what she is doing in Markarth, she may reveal herself to be an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) agent, her attention focused on the Markarth Treasury deed. She has the intention of either buying or stealing it for [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) and the Imperials, but has been unsuccessful thus far. Her [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Margret%27s_Journal) reveals that the Silver-Bloods are [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) sympathizers.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f). She is equipped with a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Margret%27s_Room) to her room.

## Dialogue
Before the attack in the market:

*"I'm shopping. Excuse me."* After the attack:

*"I should never have come to Markarth."* *"Oh it's you. The one from the market."* Margret will give you a necklace if you saved her.

*"By the gods, that man nearly killed me. You saved my life. Thank you. Here, I was going to bring this to my sister, but I think you should have it."* You can ask her some questions, but she denies knowing anything.

**Do you know why he was after you?** *"No. I don't have any idea. I was just shopping for a pendant for my sister back in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)."* **That man said something about the "Forsworn."** *"I've heard of them. Something about the men who live in the hills and attack caravans. I'm sorry. I don't know anymore. I'm just visiting here from Cyrodiil."* If she survived, you can talk to her during your investigation into the attack.

*"I'm sorry, I'm still a bit dizzy from everything."* You have three options:

**What are you doing in Markarth?** *"Just visiting. I had some business here. That's all."* **Any idea why Weylin attacked you?** *"No. I was just buying jewelry for my sister in Cyrodiil. I've never even seen that man before."* **Do you remember anything about the attack?** *"Not much. One moment I was buying a necklace, and the rest is just a blur."* After choosing the first option, you can dig for further information.
