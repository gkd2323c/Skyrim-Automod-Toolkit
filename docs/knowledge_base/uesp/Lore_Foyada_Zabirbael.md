[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/MW-place-Foyada_Zabirbael.jpg/200px-MW-place-Foyada_Zabirbael.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Zabirbael.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Zabirbael.jpg) Foyada Zabirbael Foyada Zabirbael is one of the several [foyadas](Lore_Foyada.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the island district of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The volcanic ravines (or "fire-rivers") are valleys formed by lava flows from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), functioning as drainage basins for lava when the volcano is active, or alternatively forming pathways to travel on when dormant and devoid of lava.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen F-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWAjira-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup>

The foyada is found in [Molag Amur](Lore_Molag_Amur.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWMap-4)</sup> stretching northeast from [Lake Nabia](Lore_Lake_Nabia.md), past [Mount Kand](Lore_Mount_Kand.md), all the way to [Mount Assarnibibi](Lore_Mount_Assarnibibi.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Lil Advice-5)</sup> Foyada Zabirbael was filled with lava at the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AMap-6)</sup> The foyada had dried up by the time of [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).
