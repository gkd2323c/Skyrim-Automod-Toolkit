[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Irlof.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irlof.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irlof.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irlof.jpg) Irlof **Irlof** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md). Along with [Alding](Skyrim_Alding.md) and [Gjuk](Skyrim_Gjuk.md), he forms the crew of [Blood Horker](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md) Captain [Stig Salt-Plank](Skyrim_Stig_Salt-Plank.md). He and the rest of the crew only exist during [Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md), after you give the logbook to [Orthus Endario](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario), where they are found in the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md), partaking in their regular "festivities".

For the whole duration of the quest, he sits at a bench inside the inn, enjoying a tankard of mead or eating bread.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots) and [hide bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers).
