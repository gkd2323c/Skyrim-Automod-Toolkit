[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-interior-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg) Shadowfoot Sanctum interior **Shadowfoot Sanctum** is a small player home located in [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) beneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). The map marker for the exterior entrance is called **Riften Docks**.

You will be directed to buy the house from [Vekel the Man](Skyrim_Vekel_the_Man.md) for 7,500 gold during the related quest.

The house consists of one area, *[Shadowfoot Sanctum](#Shadowfoot_Sanctum)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(quest).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>: Purchase a cozy sewer home ideal for a thief.

## Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shadowfoot_Sanctum.jpg) The Riften Docks entrance This house possesses two entrances, allowing for easy access to the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(place).md) without having to enter Riften itself. The main entrance is located behind a gate in [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md), in the room with the executioner's block just across from [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). The second, hidden entrance is an underwater trap door located in the murky pool in the forge area of the sanctum, which leads to a flowing sewer grate outside the walls of Riften. This entrance can be found in the water just south of the western city gates. This Creation adds a new map marker for the Riften Docks which allows for easy access to Shadowfoot Sanctum, as it will place you on the bridge leading to the [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md), conveniently overlooking the sewer grate.

## Interior

### Shadowfoot Sanctum
Entering from the Ratway, you will find yourself in a narrow sewer passage with running water and the skeletal remains of some poor unfortunate trapped behind a sewer grate. This skeleton possesses some minor treasure. Following this passage, you will come to an open area containing a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), a small [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md). This room also possesses a banner with a [Guild Shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) as well as a murky pool with a hidden trapdoor leading to Riften Docks.

The next room serves as a well-lit dining area and reading room resembling a typical Riften basement. It contains a [shrine of Akatosh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Akatosh) and a unique painting. Within one of the wardrobes you will find a false back panel leading to a trophy room with nineteen mannequins, a chest surrounded by three [piles of gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pile_of_Gold), a well-stocked [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md), an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md), and weapon racks. One of the shield plaques displays a (likely ill-gotten) [Riften Guard's Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Shield). This room also contains holders for all ten [dragon claws](Skyrim_Dragon_Claw.md) from the base game, the five [bugs in a jar](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Bugs_in_a_Jar), the [Right Eye of the Falmer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Right_Eye_of_the_Falmer), the [Spider Control Rod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Control_Rod) (the one for [Aicantar's Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aicantar%27s_Spider), not the one from [Forgotten Seasons](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons_(quest).md)), the five [paragons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Unique_Items) from the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), [Chillrend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chillrend), [Grimsever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grimsever), the [Nightingale Bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Bow) and [Nightingale Blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Blade), as well as all of the [dragon priest masks](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) in the base game and [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Armor_Artifacts.md#Dragon_Priest_Masks), with the exception of [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(item).md). In addition, there are shelves for all of the items collected for [The Litany of Larceny](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Litany_of_Larceny). However, these will not activate until after the item has been turned in, so the items will need to be removed from the Thieves Guild afterwards and moved here instead.

Returning to the main dining room, you will find three side rooms behind closed doors. One of these leads to a kitchen, which contains an [oven](Skyrim_Baking.md) and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md). Two [Glass Jars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Jar), items unique to this Creation, can be found on a top shelf in here. Another side room contains two child-sized beds and a target dummy for your [adopted children](Skyrim_Adoption.md). Of note in this room is another Glass Jar found on one of the shelves next to some [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), along with a [shrine of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth) on the shelf above. The final side room is the master bedroom, containing a double bed with a [shrine of Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mara) on the shelf above it, next to a [Jeweled Goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jeweled_Goblet) and [Jeweled Pitcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jeweled_Pitcher).

## Notes
- Behind the wall, southeast of the trophy room, is an inaccessible urn containing a copy of all items displayable in the room, presumably for testing purposes. You can retrieve these items by clipping through the wall.

## Bugs
- Mannequin heights can change at random, either sinking into their bases or floating above them.
- Chillrend does not mount onto its named rack correctly, sticks out of the rack, or out of the mannequin to right side of the rack. - This can be fixed by walking as close to the rack as possible, looking down, and selecting "Place". You may need to try this multiple times.
- Adopted children will not move to this house if they are living in another player home. Instead, they will continue to live at the house they were in previously.
- Sometimes the False Panel Door will malfunction and an invisible barrier will block you from the leaving the Trophy Room. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Underwater entry crashes the game occasionally. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The items contained in the urn mentioned above can be accessed by executing a dual-wield power attack and using [netch jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly) or another paralyzing ingredient when the character is clipping through the wall. <sup>**?**</sup>
