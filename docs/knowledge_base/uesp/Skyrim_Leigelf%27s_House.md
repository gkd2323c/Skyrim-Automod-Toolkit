## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There are a few steps up to a porch on the front of the house, on the porch are two [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and a wooden bench at one end and several piles of firewood at the other. The house faces the bay and is novice-locked the majority of the time.

### Leigelf's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Leigelf%27s_House.jpg) Leigelf's house This is another single-room home, on your left upon entering is a set of shelves holding two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and has several pieces of cheese on top. Above are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the left is a basket on the floor containing two leeks and to the right is a food barrel. In the middle of the east wall and coming out from the wall is a large double bed with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of it. On the floor to the left of the bed are four bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). To the right of the bed is a table holding a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) and six loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md).

The fireplace is in the middle of the south wall, opposite the door and has a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front of it, a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantelpiece and a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on the hearth. To the left is a hanging rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and to the left of these is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table still against the south wall. On the table is a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), three [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge).

In the northwestern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are three bottles of Nord mead, a pickaxe, five loose coins and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse).
