The Pillar of Thras is a thousand foot tall spiral of coral that stands on the largest island of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md), in the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md). The [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) are known to perform [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) sacrifices by pushing victims from the top of the pillar. These sacrifices are sometimes known to disappear into [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) before being dashed on the rocks below, although not even the Sload seem to understand why only some are taken.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

It has been speculated that the Pillar of Thras was built by the Sload as a replacement for the [Coral Tower](Lore_Coral_Tower_(Thras).md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAAYQ2-2)</sup> which was destroyed by the [All-Flags Navy](Lore_All-Flags_Navy.md) circa [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260). The Coral Tower seemingly had a portal to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) at its apex<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JTE-3)</sup> and is theorised to have had a purpose similar to the other [Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md) of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), acting as both a focus and a projector of mystical power. However, it is unknown if either structures are true Towers, or simply failed imitations similar to the [Doomcrag](Lore_Doomcrag.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAAYQ2-2)</sup>

Rumors of the Sloads' plans to reconstruct their Tower had already spread to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), although it is unknown when construction commenced. It is speculated that the Pillar was built by the Sload by magically inducing coral to grow rapidly skyward, in a similar manner to many of their more mundane structures.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAAYQ2-2)</sup>
