[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/OB-place-Fort_Sejanus.jpg/200px-OB-place-Fort_Sejanus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Sejanus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Sejanus.jpg) Fort Sejanus circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Fort Sejanus (also known as **Sejanus Outpost**) is a ruined military fort located southeast of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is located at the intersection of the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md) and the [Yellow Road](Lore_Yellow_Road.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> The ruins possess an underground complex, including catacombs on the lowest level.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> The fort shares its name with the **Sejanus Hunting Grounds**, a region of open terrain to the northeast where the ruins of [Nagastani](Lore_Nagastani.md) can be found.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a small fort called Sejanus Outpost was located here. It was initially held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), although the outpost changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Its strategic position was critical to maintain supply lines.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The fort was abandoned by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) during the relatively peaceful [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Fort Sejanus was no more than a ruin, controlled by a hostile group of [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) and their [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) minions. Despite the conjurers' presence, the fort's catacombs were haunted by [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>
