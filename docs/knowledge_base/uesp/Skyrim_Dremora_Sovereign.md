[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-npc-Dremora_Sovereign.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dremora_Sovereign.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Sovereign.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Sovereign.jpg) Dremora Sovereign **Dremora Sovereign** is a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) warrior whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos) as part of the [Arms of Chaos](Skyrim_Arms_of_Chaos.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins) in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: [Dremora Sehtkynaz](Skyrim_Dremora_Sehtkynaz.md), [Dremora Lyrkynaz](Skyrim_Dremora_Lyrkynaz.md), and Dremora Sovereign. Dremora Sovereign is the third Dremora to appear, and will be summoned as soon as you kill Dremora Lyrkynaz.

Dremora Sovereign utilizes powerful [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) magic in combat and can summon a [flame atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md). He carries a [Daedric Sword of the Inferno](Skyrim_Daedric_Sword_of_the_Inferno.md) along with an [Orange Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Sigil_Stone). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) when killed.

## Related Quests
- **[The Arms of Chaos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Arms_of_Chaos.md)</sup>: Complete a mage's quest to reassemble two powerful staves.

## Notes
- His Daedric Sword of the Inferno will be present regardless of your level, potentially giving you access to a powerful weapon early on.
