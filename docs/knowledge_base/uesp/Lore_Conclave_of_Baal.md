# Conclave of Baal

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/AR-map-Stormhold.png/200px-AR-map-Stormhold.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Stormhold.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Stormhold.png) Map of Stormhold marking the Conclave of Baal The Conclave of Baal was a religious temple that was prominent across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), in the late years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). It had regional chapters across the provinces, in major cities such as [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) and [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md), as well as smaller settlements such as [Silgrad Tower](Lore_Silgrad_Tower.md) and [Moonguard](Lore_Moonguard.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTemples-1)</sup>

Sometime during the Simulacrum, an initiate of the conclave in [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md) sought to impress his masters by conducting a spell of total destructive potential at the [Vaults of Gemin](Lore_Vaults_of_Gemin.md). However, their performance had failed and the senior brothers were forced to channel the [magical](Lore_Magic.md) energy somewhere else, into the vaults themselves. This event caused the stronghold to collapse and the loss of plenty of knowledge, such as the tablet that depicts [Murkwood](Lore_Murkwood.md)'s location.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARIdolan-2)</sup>

Several nights later, the leader of the Conclave, Idolan Lancaster, was approached by the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) after they acquired the sixth piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md). The warrior sought the help of the conclave to find the seventh piece in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Idolan offered the solution if they could retrieve the tablet in the Vaults of Gemin. After they managed to obtain the tablet, Lancaster was able to decipher its message and led them to Murkwood.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMurkwood-3)</sup>

## Notes
- It is never revealed who Baal is, but excluding the real world Phoenician deity, the closest person it could be is [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), who was known as *Moloch Baal* in concept art for *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFBaal-4)</sup>
