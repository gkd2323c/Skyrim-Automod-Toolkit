Greenward was a legendary [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) figure in the lore of northern [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md), and the first [Viridian Sentinel](Lore_Viridian_Sentinel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVS-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/ON-statue-Viridian_Woods_Statue.jpg/200px-ON-statue-Viridian_Woods_Statue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Viridian_Woods_Statue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Viridian_Woods_Statue.jpg) A statue representing the Viridian Sentinel<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTT-2)</sup> [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/ON-quest-The_Will_of_the_Woods_03.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Will_of_the_Woods_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Will_of_the_Woods_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Will_of_the_Woods_03.jpg) Princess Elara becoming the new Viridian Sentinel According to the legend of the Viridian Sentinel the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) conquerors of Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) once made an agreement with the [Earth Bones](Lore_Earthbones.md) to safeguard their settlements scattered throughout the region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) from the dangers of the wilderness. However, at some point in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), when their former subjects, the Bretons, took over, they lacked such an agreement and consequently, their farms in the region of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) were slowly overwhelmed by nature. The people were on the verge of famine, and it was then that Greenward, back then a young boy, prayed to the God of Mercy [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) for aid. Legend claims that he received a vision from a [kingfisher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kingfisher) that told him how to stop the beasts. This led to him learning how to create a new pact with the forest, which granted him the ability to control the natural creatures. At that moment, Greenward became the first Viridian Sentinel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVS-1)</sup>

Greenward took on the task of walking the border between the vanquished lands and the wilderness, acting as a bridge between civilization and nature, telling the beasts his name and claiming the land as vanquished. He was successful in turning them back. He continued to walk the border until he grew old, and a young girl who had been spoken to by a bird took up the task when he passed away.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVS-1)</sup>

## Legacy
The line of Viridian Sentinels continued to follow in Greenward's footsteps for generations, spanning several centuries until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). During this time, it was Stenwick Gurles who was responsible for maintaining the balance and harmony between the Breton settlements and the natural world.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SG-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Demara-4)</sup> It is unknown whether he was succeded by [Elara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elara), the former princess of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) or not.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWOTW-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elara-6)</sup>
