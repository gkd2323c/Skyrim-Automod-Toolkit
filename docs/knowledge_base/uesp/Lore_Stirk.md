[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/ON-place-Stirk.jpg/200px-ON-place-Stirk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stirk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stirk.jpg) Stirk, [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Stirk is an island off the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Initially settled by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) and legendary for being where the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) was founded,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLady Clarisse Laurent-2)</sup> it has since been used by pirates for settling disputes through trial by combat.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Load-3)</sup> Though claimed at times by [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), Cyrodiil, and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), the uninhabited island is really a no-man's-land.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Load-3)</sup> Its neutral status during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) made [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) choose it as a meeting ground for the leaders of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) to launch their attack on [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), as organized by the guilds.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOGalerion-4)</sup>

## Geography
The terrain of Stirk is a mix of shallow beaches on the northwest and southeast and rugged rocky hills in between, surrounded by jagged rocks. The coast is dotted with [palm trees](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Palm_Tree) and Ayleid docks, while the Stirk Interior<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOGalerion-4)</sup> is lightly forested by what appear to be [acacias](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Acacia), along with other trees. The Portal Valley is found nestled in the mountains of the north-central part of the island. There are no known permanent settlements on the island.

## History
The historic island was at one time colonized by the Ayleids, whose white limestone [ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleid_Ruins) dominate the tropical landscape. There are also dolmen-like [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) pillars found on the island,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dolmen-5)</sup> but it is unknown whether the Ayleids or some other civilization constructed them. In the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), it was where the leaders of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) assembled the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) to do battle with the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOGalerion-4)</sup> In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), although the island was claimed by [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), the Gold Coast, and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), it had little activity besides the pirates in the area, who used the Ayleid ruins as arenas for settling disputes through trial by combat.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Load-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) a meeting took place here between the three leaders of the Three Banners War, as Stirk was considered neutral territory. The meeting was facilitated by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), and aimed to discuss a possible invasion of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) in response to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). Before an agreement could be reached, Molag Bal sent [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) to the island to kill the alliance leaders. The attackers were repelled, and the leaders quickly gave their consent for an invasion, having seen the Prince's influence firsthand. A force was then assembled from the ranks of the two guilds, and a portal to Coldharbour was opened on the island.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Online-6)</sup>

The island was considered by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) to be Cyrodiil's territory circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MCMap-7)</sup> but around [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the island appeared to be unaffiliated with any province on certain maps.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-OCMap-8)</sup>

## Culture
Stirk Pork-and-Beets is a gourmet dish named after the island. It is a type of stew containing [pork](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pig), beets, herbs, and bananas.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
