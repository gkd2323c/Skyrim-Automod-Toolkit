# Lord's Mail

The Lord's Mail (also called the **Armor of Morihaus**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup> or the **Gift of Kynareth**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LGT-2)</sup> is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) given to mortals by [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), one of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md). It is an ancient plated [adamantium](Lore_Adamantium.md) or [mithril](Lore_Mithril.md) cuirass of unsurpassable quality, considered to be heavy armor. It grants the wearer the power to absorb or regenerate health, resist the effects of spells, and resist or even cure poison. It is said that whenever Kynareth deigns the wearer unworthy, the Lord's Mail will be taken away and hidden for the next chosen one. The Mail was originally owned during the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) by the bull [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md), a [demigod](Lore_Demigod.md) and early cultural hero of the [Cyro-Nordics](Lore_Cyro-Nord.md) who was closely associated with Kynareth and [the Lord](Lore_The_Lord.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TL-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR.2FDF-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The cuirass was rumored to have later been taken from Morihaus by Kynareth herself due to his arrogance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup> The cuirass is considered an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) relic,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LGT-2)</sup> and is accepted as a uniform in the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MW-6)</sup> Indeed, the armor has been known to bear the red dragon sigil of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) on its breastplate.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CC-7)</sup>

## History
[Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), at one point befriended the sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWYB-8)</sup> At Fyr’s behest, Yagrum Bagarn wrote and compiled centuries of personal notes into a book that would become known as *[Tamrielic Lore](Lore_Tamrielic_Lore.md)*,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-9)</sup><sup>:241</sup> listing the Lord's Mail among the artifacts of "unimaginable significance". By the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), copies had spread across Tamriel, offering readers rare insights into these legendary items,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TA-10)</sup> and Fyr himself hoped to utilize this knowledge in his search for the Lord's Mail and the other artifacts detailed in the book.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWFR-11)</sup>

The cuirass was uncovered by the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in an unknown province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup> In the events leading up to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), a clue to the mail's location was discovered by a [Glenmoril Witch](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) living in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. In return for kidnapping the witch's great-granddaughter to become the witch's successor, she revealed the clue to a questing member of a knightly order, who retrieved it from the dungeon where it lay. However, there are conflicting accounts that the artifact centered around this story was actually a different one altogether, with [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) or [Auriel's Shield](Lore_Auriel%27s_Shield.md) taking its place in the story.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DFTA-12)</sup>

Later in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Mail came into the possession of the Imperial Legion's [Order of Ebonheart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Order_of_Ebonheart). It was stored in a shrine in [Castle Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md), the seat of Imperial authority on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the cuirass was stolen by Furius Acilius, a member of the Legion, who then deserted and fled into the underground cave network beneath Ebonheart. The sword [Chrysamere](Lore_Chrysamere.md) was also stolen by a thief within the legion, and was now in the possession of Draramu Hloran, a powerful sorceress found in the [Abanabi Caves](Lore_Abanabi_Caves.md) near [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md). And so, A [Knight Protector](Lore_Nerevarine.md) of the Legion was tasked with recovered the two artifacts from the thieves, which they complied with, and gave both of them to Varus Vantinius, the Knight of the Imperial Dragon on Vvardenfell.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MWLords-13)</sup> The Knight Protector later challenged Vantinius for the title of Knight of the Imperial Dragon; the two dueled in the arena of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), and Vantinius was slain, allowing the victor to claim both artifacts from his corpse.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MWGD-14)</sup> Later that year, both artifacts were sold to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md), who put it on display in her Museum of Artifacts in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TRTM-15)</sup>

Sometime after the end of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) came across the Lord's Mail in their journey. The cuirass is thought to have been looted from an extravagant chest.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

On the 15th of Last Seed, [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Emperor [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md) wrote a formal request to General [Tullius](Lore_Tullius.md), the Military Governor of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), ordering that Imperial relics be placed back into the service of the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) in order to galvanize the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md)'s cause during the ongoing [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). The Emperor singled out the Lord's Mail, which had recently gone missing, and ordered that it be reclaimed by the Legion in order to demonstrate that Kynareth favored the Empire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LGT-2)</sup> The armor had been stolen by a group of [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) mercenaries who sought to sell it to the highest bidder and discredit the Empire. However, the armor at this time bore a curse which would cause any who wore it to be overcome with an uneasy fatigue.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) learned of the Emperor's orders and intercepted the mercenaries at their camp on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md), south of [Fort Greymoor](Lore_Fort_Greymoor.md). The Lord's Mail was then brought to the Shrine of Kynareth at [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md)'s Temple of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) and cleansed of its curse.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CC-7)</sup>
