[3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Dark Fissure is an isolated cave found deep in the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md), within the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

The Dark Fissure contains one of the four mysterious altars in Cyrodiil that are necessary in the creation of a [Black Soul Gem](Lore_Black_Soul_Gem.md), a phenomenon known as the *"Shade of the Revenant."* The Dark Fissure is found in the smaller region of Mist Morrow Vale.

Like various other natural caverns in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), it is theorized to have served as a capillary for the living body of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), and once pulsed with the living essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) who created it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

## History
It is unknown when the altar at the Dark Fissure was established but by the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), an abandoned and undiscovered [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) was present where the cave is currently located. An adventurer from [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md) traveled to the Dark Fissure amidst the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), to explore undiscovered Ayleid ruins. But she was later killed by unknown sources.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLeft-2)</sup> As the Alliance War raged across central Cyrodiil, the Dark Fissure was considered within the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md)'s territory of the region.

Many years later in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Dark Fissure was inhabited by a coven of [Necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) and an anchorite of the Worm God, [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md). The anchorite lived in isolation from civilization, conducting the Shade of the Revenant, and producing Black Soul Gems. At the time, the Arch-Mage of the [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) was revealed to have been involved in necromancy, with knowledge on the altar locations throughout Cyrodiil. Master-Wizard Raminus Polus sent a [Conjurer](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) of the guild to investigate the Dark Fissure, where they uncovered the altar. The Conjurer returned to the [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md) with the knowledge on the creation of Black Soul Gems.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBNecromancer-3)</sup>

## Notes
- A "Dark Fissure" may also refer to small portals akin to the [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md) of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), which are gateways to his plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). These Dark Fissures would open in the wilderness, without any setup like the Dark Anchors. They were somewhat prominent during Molag Bal's attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) in 2E 582.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
