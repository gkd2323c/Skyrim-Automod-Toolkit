Philea Nielus was the Imperial Ambassador serving within [Vvardenfell District](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mehrunes Razor-1)</sup>

## History

### Rise of the Drothmeri Army
Ambassador Nielus was concerned with the emergence of [Frathen Drothan](Lore_Frathen_Drothan.md), a [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) Arch-Mage out of [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md), who was building an army in search of the Daedric artifact [Mehrunes' Razor](Lore_Mehrunes%27_Razor.md), which she believed would give him the confidence to attempt toppling the Imperial regime in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). King [Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md) initially chose not to intervene with Drothan, believing that Drothan would get no support in his cause from any of the Great Houses, which proved to be true. But after Nielus received new intelligence from her sources on Drothan's receiving funding for his mercenary army, she wrote a letter to Helseth's advisor, Firthan Andoren, urging him to convince the King to reconsider his stance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mehrunes Razor-1)</sup>
