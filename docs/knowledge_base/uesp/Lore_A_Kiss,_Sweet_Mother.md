A Kiss, Sweet Mother *A description of the Black Sacrament in order to enlist the Dark Brotherhood*
---
So you wish to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md)? You wish to see someone dead? Pray, child. Pray, and let the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md) hear your plea.

You must perform that most profane of rituals - the Black Sacrament.

Create an effigy of the intended victim, assembled from actual body parts, including a heart, skull, bones and flesh. Encircle that effigy with candles.

The ritual itself must then commence. Proceed to stab the effigy repeatedly with a dagger rubbed with the petals of a [Nightshade](Lore_Nightshade.md) plant, while whispering this plea:

"Sweet Mother, sweet Mother, send your child unto me, for the sins of the unworthy must be baptized in blood and fear."

Then wait, child, for the Dread Father [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) rewards the patient. You will be visited by a representative of the Dark Brotherhood. So begins a contract bound in blood.
