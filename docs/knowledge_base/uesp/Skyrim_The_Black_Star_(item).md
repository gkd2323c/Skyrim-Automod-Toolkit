[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-item-The_Black_Star.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Black_Star.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Black_Star.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Black_Star.jpg) The Black Star The Black Star is an altered Daedric [Artifact](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) awarded to you by [Nelacar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nelacar) for cleansing the soul of [Malyn Varen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malyn_Varen) from the star as part of the quest [The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star). It is the alternative award to the original version of the artifact [Azura's Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azura%27s_Star). The main benefit of acquiring The Black Star is the bonus that it offers to [enchanters](Skyrim_Enchanting.md); the star is essentially a reusable black soul gem. Enchanting objects with The Black Star merely uses up the soul that it was carrying, but the star will be able to be reused to store another soul like a regular soul gem. The Black Star also has one clear advantage over Azura's Star: it can capture both black and white souls, unlike Azura's Star, which can only capture white souls.

## Bugs
- Although it's described as only able to hold black souls, all types of souls can be captured. This may either be a bug, or Nelacar may be mistaken when stating this to the player. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. The Black Star will now accept black souls ONLY.

- Further research into the lore behind black soul gems indicates that although not ideal, they are supposed to be able to carry any type of soul. The only lore restriction on them is the process by which they are made, which is irrelevant in terms of Skyrim loot. As a result, version 3.0.7 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch) modifies the fix from Unofficial Skyrim Patch 2.0.1 to only apply to the Black Star, which is definitively mentioned in lore and in-game dialogue as ONLY accepting black souls.
