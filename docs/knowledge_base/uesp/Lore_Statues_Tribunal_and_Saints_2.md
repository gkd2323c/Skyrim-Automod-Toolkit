| [Ra Abah](Lore_Ra_Abah.md) was a renowned [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) warrior who fought alongside [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) in the [War of the Singers](Lore_War_of_the_Singers.md) against Emperor [Hira](Lore_Hira.md).<sup>[[43]](#cite_note-ONSai-45)</sup> After his death, a statue of him was built atop his honored tomb in the Valley of the Blade. During the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) traveled to the sword-school to acquire the Ring of Stendarr's Mercy, but encountered [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) and his Daedric minions. Mannimarco raised the bones of the ancient hero in an attempt to stop Sai Sahan, but he was ultimately defeated.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-ONVo B-43)</sup> | [![ON-statue-Ra Abah.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/3/39/ON-statue-Ra_Abah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Ra_Abah.jpg) ESO |
