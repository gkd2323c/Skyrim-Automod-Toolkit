[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-place-Yorgrim_Overlook.jpg/200px-SR-place-Yorgrim_Overlook.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yorgrim_Overlook.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yorgrim_Overlook.jpg) Yorgrim Overlook **Yorgrim Overlook** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Dwellings.md) on the southern slope of the mountain range directly south of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). It is also referred to as **Yorgrim's Overlook**.

This structure is slightly northeast of [Forsaken Cave](Skyrim_Forsaken_Cave.md) and south of [Mount Anthor](Skyrim_Mount_Anthor.md).

## Yorgrim Overlook
The path leading to the overlook starts just to the right of [Forsaken Cave](Skyrim_Forsaken_Cave.md) heading northeast; near the top of this path are stairs up to the overlook, with statues flanking the ruin. The entrance is similar to several Nordic Barrows around [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md); however, where the entrance doors would normally be found, there is instead a locked gate of Expert level blocking access to an unlocked chest. There are two upright sarcophagi within the ruin, with one in each corner. As you approach, one or both of the sarcophagi will burst open and [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) will emerge. There are stone tables beside these sarcophagi; the one to the west has two [burial urns](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md) on top, and the one to the east is broken and has three [steel ingots](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) on top.

With the [Alternative Armors - Dragonscale](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dragonscale.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, the undead [Ulfnir Bone-Skin](Skyrim_Ulfnir_Bone-Skin.md) will be found here as part of the related quest. You will be tasked with recovering his set of [studded Dragonscale armor](Skyrim_Studded_Dragonscale_Armor.md) from the chest within the barrow. The remains of [Fathrys](Skyrim_Fathrys.md) can be found next to the expert-locked gate, having seemingly fallen victim to Ulfnir.
