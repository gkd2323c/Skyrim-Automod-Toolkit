[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-npc-Murbul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Murbul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Murbul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Murbul.jpg) Murbul **Murbul** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [destruction mage](Skyrim_CombatMageDestruction.md) and wise woman of [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the Orc stronghold southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She is the mother of [Chief Burguk](Skyrim_Chief_Burguk.md) and also functions as the local apothecary, selling a variety of [merchandise](#Restocking_Merchandise) if you are blood-kin of the orcs.

As town elder, Murbul has her own room in [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) and sleeps there every night between midnight and 8am. After waking up she heads over to her tent and spends the entire day offering her services, at times using the [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) or the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). At 8pm she was supposed to relax in the longhouse with a book, but doesn't, due to a [bug](#Bugs). Instead, she stays in the tent and offers services until midnight.

She wears a pair of [ragged boots](Skyrim_Ragged_Boots.md), [Forsworn gauntlets](Skyrim_Forsworn_Gauntlets.md), [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) and a [Stormcloak officer helmet](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Helmet.md). She carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) to the longhouse and wields a [steel](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) or, starting at level six, an [Orcish dagger](Skyrim_Orcish_Dagger.md). She also carries a random selection of [middle-class items](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common), including food, jewelry and gold.

## Dialogue

- *"Orc poisons are to be feared, if you wish to trade."*
- *"I have an old cure for Rockjoint. Common in little Orc children."*
- *"I am Burguk's mother and wise woman to our tribe, outlander."*
- *"Malacath rewards the strong and punishes the weak."*

### Conversations

- **Burguk:** *"Tell me, mother, does Malacath favor our stronghold?"*
- **Murbul:** *"He is pleased. Ours is the strongest tribe in all the Reach."*

## Notes
- In the game's Dialogue Views files, there is dialogue for a favor quest known internally as `Favor016Quest`. In the finished game's files seen through the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), only a single basic script remains of this quest. But the Dialogue Views show all the dialogue that was attached to it:

- **Anything I can do to help?**
- *"I am the wise woman. I guide our tribe and make sure we stay strong. I also make poisons for our hunters' arrows, and potions to keep our fighting spirits high. How about I give you something for your blade, to welcome you to our people? I just need a Skeever Tail and a Falmer Ear.*

- **I have the Skeever Tail and a Falmer Ear.**
- *"Good. Let me mix those for you. Here. A poison that will squeeze the life out of your enemies. And some coin, for your trouble. Mauloch smile on you, outlander.*

- This dialogue doesn't specify a speaker, so it's unknown which stronghold's wise woman would've given this quest. It's also unclear what would've triggered the line differences. One possibility is that one variant would've been used for Orcs and one for non-Orcs, though it seems more likely that one was an early, basic version, with the other being the more finalized one.
