And we will be forever."* **Ruki** is the [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and the wife of [Fenrig](Skyrim_Fenrig.md). During the quest [The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md), she can be found constantly combing the area surrounding [Gjukar's Monument](Skyrim_Gjukar%27s_Monument.md) for Fenrig, having received word that his most recent battle did not end well. She wears standard farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6), with matching [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5) and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209aa).

## Related Quests
- **[The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md)**: Help the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.

## Quest-Related Events

### The Book of Love
Ruki is obviously worried about the fate of her lover, saying:

- *"I'm searching for my Fenrig. He was marching with Gjukar's men, who they say were wiped out here. I've turned over every body, though, and I can't find him. Please help me look. He has a bright red beard and hair."*
- **The last battle here was hundreds of years ago.**
- *"Are you a fool? Look at the bodies around you. Even if you're blind, surely you smell the blood."* and
- **I don't see any bodies here.**
- *"I don't have time for your foolishness. Either help me or be gone."*

She will then say:

- *"Come, help me search for Fenrig."*
- *"He must be here somewhere..."*

When the two dead lovers are finally reunited, a brief conversation will take place: **Ruki**: *"Fenrig! You're alive!"*
**Fenrig**: *"Of course I am. What brings you here?"*
**Ruki**: *"I had heard that Gjukar's men were wiped out. I came to find you."*
**Fenrig**: *"But that battle isn't expected until tomorrow... Ruki, what's going on?"*
**Ruki**: *"I'm so confused. What's happening?"*
**Fenrig**: *"It doesn't matter. I'm here. We're together now. And we will be forever."*

Together they float up into the sky to spend eternity with one another among the stars.
