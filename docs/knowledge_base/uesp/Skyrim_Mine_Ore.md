## Walkthrough
If you talk to one of the quest givers, they will offer to pay you for any ore you mine. Each quest-giver will only accept a single type of ore, as listed in the above table. You can also mine ore without talking to a quest giver, and either sell them the ore later or use the ore yourself (e.g., by [smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) it). For most ores you are paid its standard base value, with the exception of Iron Ore, for which you receive 7 gold instead of 2 gold. The amount you are paid is fixed, and does not depend upon your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill or perks. All ore of the specified type will be purchased.

Information on how and where to mine is provided at [Mining](Skyrim_Mining.md).

## Achievements
An [Achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) is related to mining ore:

- [![SR-achievement-Hard Worker.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-achievement-Hard_Worker.png/30px-SR-achievement-Hard_Worker.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Hard_Worker.png) **[Hard Worker](Skyrim_Hard_Worker.md)** (10 points/Bronze) — [Chop wood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md), [mine ore](Skyrim_Mining.md), and [cook food](Skyrim_Cooking.md) - To obtain this achievement you do not need to sell the ore, or even talk to any of the quest givers. Simply activating an ore vein is sufficient.

## Notes
- This quest does not have any stages or objectives.
- This quest can be repeated indefinitely.
- The dialogue option to sell the ore only appears if you have the specified type of ore in your inventory.
- The quest giver will buy all the ore of the specified type that you are carrying, so if you want to save any, you must remove it from your inventory first.
- Money you earn from mining ore is fixed, and if the ore is sold through direct dialogue rather than the merchant screen, is not affected by your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill. This makes the quest one of the few ways to [make money](Skyrim_Making_Money.md) without increasing any skills, which will help control your level. This is useful when wanting to [train](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in a certain skill as many times as possible. It is also one of the few ways to earn money without doing any questing, merchanting, or crafting.
- Completing this quest at Redbelly Mine will raise your favor with the owner, Grogmar, and he will make you [blood-kin to the Orcs](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) as a result.
- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) quest for the quest giver's hold.
