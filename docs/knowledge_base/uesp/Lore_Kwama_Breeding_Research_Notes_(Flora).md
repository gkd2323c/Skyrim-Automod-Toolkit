Kwama Breeding Research Notes *Notes on the effects of various flora on kwama* *
---
[Ash Yam](Lore_Ash_Yam.md): No effect. [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) will eat it if they are peckish.

[Bittergreen](Lore_Bittergreen.md): No effect. Empty Bittergreen pods are attractive sitting locations for [workers](Lore_Kwama_Worker.md) and [scribs](Lore_Kwama_Scrib.md). I've seen [warriors](Lore_Kwama_Warrior.md) attempt to enter the pods when they obviously can't fit. Stupid bugs. Keep these out of the mine.

[Black Anther](Lore_Black_Anther.md): No effect on the kwama, and we have to send operatives near [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) to find it, which is risky. Suggested to cease testing.

[Black Lichen](Lore_Black_Lichen.md): The kwama hate it. Use it only to keep them out of spaces we don't want them to be near, like our food stores.

[Bungler's Bane](Lore_Bungler%27s_Bane.md): No effect and hard to find in reasonable supply.

[Chokeweed](Lore_Chokeweed.md): No effect.

[Comberry](Lore_Comberry.md): No effect and extremely hard to find in the wild. If we need more it's recommended to steal some from the merchants in Vivec City.

[Corkbulb Root](Lore_Corkbulb_Root.md): Not the effect we are looking for, although it seems useful for treating kwama with any form of paralysis. Continued testing is recommended.

[Draggle-tail](Lore_Draggle_Tail.md): None in supply and it's not yet worth sending scouts to the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md).

[Fire Fern](Lore_Fire_Fern.md): Kwama love this stuff, while at the same time it appears to make them agitated. More research on this is recommended.

[Gold Kanet](Lore_Gold_Kanet.md): No effect.

[Green Lichen](Lore_Green_Lichen.md): Can't find it anywhere. Every recommended location has so far turned out to be a dead end.

[Hackle-Lo](Lore_Hackle-Lo.md): No effect.

[Heather](Lore_Heather.md): No effect.

[Kresh Fiber](Lore_Kresh_Fiber.md): Kwama hate it, and not in the way we're interested in. They defecate on it as soon as it's discovered. Keep all supply out of Matus-Akin.

[Luminous Russula](Lore_Luminous_Russula.md): Grows naturally around the area and can be found in the mine as well. Kwama consume it often. No obvious effects on behavior.

[Marshmerrow](Lore_Marshmerrow.md): No effect aside from potential feed.

[Muck](Lore_Muck.md): Toxic to Kwama. DO NOT USE!

[Nirnroot](Lore_Nirnroot.md): Do not use on its own anywhere near the kwama. It appears to make them lethargic, yet they crave it once exposed. We had to put a few warriors down after the primary exposure. We don't want to lose any more, as there are not many of these beasts to begin with.

[Red Lichen](Lore_Red_Lichen.md): No effect.

[Roobrush](Lore_Roobrush.md): No effect.

[Saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md): Common kwama feed. We keep a supply of this in the storage area surrounded by a moderate amount of Black Lichen.

[Scathecraw](Lore_Scathecraw.md): No effect.

[Slough Fern](Lore_Slough_Fern.md): No effect.

[Stoneflower](Lore_Stoneflower.md): Only found in the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md). Too risky to acquire. I speculate it will have no effect regardless.

[Trama Root](Lore_Trama_Root.md): No effect.

[Violet Coprinus](Lore_Violet_Coprinus.md): We have found this growing naturally in Matus-Akin. Potential natural kwama feed. No effect.

[Wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md): Makes kwama even stupider than they already are. More research needed.

[Willow Flower](Lore_Willow_Flower.md): No effect.

[Hypha Facia](Lore_Hypha_Facia.md): Sometimes Found in Matus-Akin, no obvious effect.
