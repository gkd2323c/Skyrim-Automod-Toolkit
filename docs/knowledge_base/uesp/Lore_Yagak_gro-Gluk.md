Yagak gro-Gluk was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) [smith](Lore_Smithing.md) who played a crucial role in the process of reforging and reigniting the [Trueflame](Lore_Trueflame.md), a legendary [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md) of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup> He was considered to be among the best weaponsmiths in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and the best one in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) by the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) goddess [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Alma-2)</sup> Yagak was also an owner of a nearly full set of extremely rare [adamantium](Lore_Adamantium.md) armor and a weapon made of the same material.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-YGG-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg/200px-TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg) Trueflame As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Yagak gro-Gluk was a citizen of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). During that time, the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was tasked by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) to reforge the [Trueflame](Lore_Trueflame.md) as a step to retaking their station and reuniting [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup>

The sword had been broken into three pieces, one which the goddess provided. Another two were held by [King Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Hlaalu_Helseth.md)'s champion, [Karrod](Lore_Karrod.md) and [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md). Nerevarine ultimately obtained all the fragments. Once the pieces were recovered, they contacted Yagak and requested him to reconstruct the blade. Yagak successfully reforged the blade, but its [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) was not restored. He shared the rumor of [Radac Stungnthumz](Lore_Radac_Stungnthumz.md) with the hero, who later found the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md)'s ghost in [Bamz-Amschend](Lore_Bamz-Amschend.md). With his help the blade was finally reforged and reignited.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup>
