# Infernium Forge

| Infernium Forge | |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Dwemer Ruin |
| Continent | [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) |

The Infernium Forge is a [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) known for producing pitch-black [animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) that radiate fire, giving them a burning hot appearance. The [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) scholar Guylaine Marilie noted the [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md) and [spiderlings](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md) from the site are unusual and distinctive, indicating that they all came from the hand of a single [Dwemer artisan](Lore_Smithing.md). Constructs originating from the Infernium Forge had found their way into the hands of collectors across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ID-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IDS-2)</sup>
