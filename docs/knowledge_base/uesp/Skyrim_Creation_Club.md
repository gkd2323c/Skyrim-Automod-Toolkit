# Creation Club


The service entered beta on September 28, 2017 and officially launched on October 4, 2017. It was never available for [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md).

All 74 Creations are included in the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md). Special Edition and [Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) users also receive free access to four Creations: [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md), [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md), [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), and [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md). Several other discounted Creations were offered for free either on a permanent or temporary basis. On Switch, the Anniversary Upgrade with all the Creations is available, but not the Creation Club itself.

As of December 5, 2023, Creation Club has been retired and the Verified Creator Program is intended to be its replacement. While Creation Club Creations are considered official content, [Verified Creator Program](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Verified_Creator_Program) Creations are considered third party [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods).

For a spoiler-free guide to help you find all of the Creation Club content, see [Creation Club First Time Players](Skyrim_Creation_Club_First_Time_Players.md).

## Creations
There are many Creation Club Creations that are available for purchase. They are organized into categories, including a Featured category that showed off Creations that were new or on sale.

Creations may occasionally be put on sale or made available for free; for example, Dwarven Armored Mudcrab was usually 100% off.

- The Sword, Backpack and Nix-Hound Bundle + Nix-Hound (save 50 credits)
- Weapon Rack Bundle + Stendarr's Hammer + Staff of Hasedoki/Staff of Sheogorath (save 50 credits)
- The Elder Scrolls 25th Anniversary Bundle + any combination of Creations it contains which cost 850 credits or less combined (too many to list separately)
- Elianora Home Bundle + Tundra Homestead (save 50 credits)

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) On a PC with a non-English keyboard, registering for Creation Club can be difficult. The text field will not accept the normal way to enter an "@" needed for the email address. - You can try switching your keyboard to use an English layout, or attempting other common shortcuts to produce an @ (e.g. `Shift` + `2`, `Shift` + `'`, `Shift` + `Ä`).
