[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-Esmond_Tyne.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Esmond_Tyne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Esmond_Tyne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Esmond_Tyne.jpg) Esmond Tyne's corpse **Esmond Tyne**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enc Class Bandit Melee), was a thief who stole a [formula](Skyrim_Bonemold_Formula.md) for improved [bonemold](Skyrim_Bonemold.md) armor from [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md). His corpse can be found near the entrance of [Castle Karstaag Caverns](Skyrim_Castle_Karstaag_Caverns.md), presumably killed by the [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md) there. He wears a [variant of Thieves Guild armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Armor) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Boots) and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Gloves). In addition to the bonemold formula, he also carries an enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md), some [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md) and gold.
