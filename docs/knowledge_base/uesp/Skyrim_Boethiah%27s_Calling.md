## Quick Walkthrough
1. *(Optional)* Read *[Boethiah's Proving](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Proving.md)*.
2. Travel to the [Sacellum of Boethiah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah) and speak to the Priestess of Boethiah.
3. Sacrifice a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) on the Altar of Sacrifice.
4. Kill all the cult members.
5. Go to [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md) and kill Boethiah's [current champion](Skyrim_Champion_of_Boethiah.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Starting the Quest
You can start the quest any time after reaching level 30, either by reading a copy of *[Boethiah's Proving](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Proving.md)* or traveling directly to the [Sacellum of Boethiah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah) and talking to the Priestess of Boethiah or killing every one of the cultists. If you wait, you may be attacked randomly in the world by Boethiah cultists, who will have a copy of *Boethiah's Proving* on their corpse.

- *Note:* See *[Boethiah's Proving](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Proving.md)* for other locations of the book. The various copies of the book appear once you reach level 30.

### The Shrine of Boethiah
First, you will have to make your way to the [Sacellum of Boethiah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sacellum_of_Boethiah). Follow the road northeast from the [Windhelm Stables](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables.md) up to [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md). Nearby is a road that leads into the mountains, up to the Sacellum. When you arrive at the Sacellum you will find two cultists fighting in a small arena. There are some more cultists and a Priestess of Boethiah watching them. Speak to the priestess. She will tell you that you need to prove that "your tongue can wield a lie". If you kill the followers of Boethiah, the Daedric Prince will appear and give the quest instructions herself; this will only change the order in which you complete the tasks. If you approach the shrine at level 29 or below, the quest won't start, and there will be no one there.

### The Sacrifice
The priestess will tell you that you need to gain someone's trust and then sacrifice them before Boethiah. She will give you the [Blade of Sacrifice](Skyrim_Blade_of_Sacrifice.md) to use for the deed. Lead any *non- [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md)* [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) to the shrine and activate command mode (holding 'use' or via the "I need you to do something" conversation option). Highlight the pillar and you should see "Use the Pillar of Sacrifice". Tell them to do this and they will move towards it and be pulled in and stuck to it. If the follower is essential, the pillar will not be triggered.

Kill the follower using any means (despite what the Priestess says, you do not have to use the Blade of Sacrifice). Once your follower is dead, Boethiah will use the body as a conduit to tell you and all of the cultists that she has a task for whomever is left standing after a battle to the death. This results in all the cultists attacking one another (and you). Be prepared for a tough fight, as they can be formidable opponents.

Alternatively, you can leave your follower on the Pillar of Sacrifice, kill all of the cultists until one is left, lead the last cultist to the Pillar of Sacrifice, get the last cultist's health to low, then kill your follower and the last cultist simultaneously. If done correctly, your follower will survive, their services should be available once again, and Boethiah will take the form of the last cultist instead.

If you have already killed all the followers of Boethiah, she will then possess the corpse of the last follower you slew and the quest proceeds as normal. See [Notes](#Notes).

### The New Champion
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg) The Ebony Mail After making the sacrifice and killing all the cultists, Boethiah will again use a body as a conduit (it may not be the same one) and tell you she needs a new champion. To become the new champion you must kill the old champion and his bandits at [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md), northwest of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md).

Despite Boethiah's order to kill them stealthily, being detected does not result in quest failure or any other penalty. Nonetheless, most of the bandits inside the mine are busy with their backs to you and can be assassinated. A few are standing in pools of flammable liquid that can be ignited with fire spells. It's difficult to sneak up on Boethiah's Champion because of the direction he faces when sitting, but if you climb on top of his shack, you can drop down behind him. Once you defeat Boethiah's Champion, you must equip his [Ebony Mail](Skyrim_Ebony_Mail.md). Boethiah will then speak to you one last time, telling you that you are her new champion and giving you the Ebony Mail as your reward.

## Notes
- [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) would have either given you a second [Daedric Quest](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md), or a second part to this one, in which you would have killed [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s Jarl [Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md). The quest is (mostly, see [Bugs](#Bugs)) inaccessible in the final game, but data for it can still be found [in the game files](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Bidding.md).
- One or more of the bandits in Knifepoint Ridge might start a fight with the old Champion if they get close enough to him to be hurt by the poison damage done by the Ebony Mail when they are searching for you.
- If your follower survives using the alternate method, they will revert to their original equipment, and anything you gave them may be lost. To avoid this, remove everything from their inventory except for their original weapons and armor.
- While killing your follower as a sacrifice does not result in any bounties, faction-specific reactions, such as [Rejoining the College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College) if you kill any of the apprentice followers, may still get triggered.
- If [Champion of Boethiah](Skyrim_Champion_of_Boethiah.md) is calmed down, unique dialogue with him will become available.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
