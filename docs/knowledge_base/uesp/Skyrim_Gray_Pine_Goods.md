### Exterior
The building has a balcony at the front, which is accessible from the rear via a flight of wooden stairs or from the door on the top floor of the house. Underneath the balcony to the left of the entrance is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel. To the right of the property is another flight of stairs ascending to the top of a wall that serves as a lookout point for the town guards.

### Gray Pine Goods
Upon entry, Bolund will be in the process of leaving (see [bugs](#Bugs)). The counter is to the right of the entrance and contains several items. Against the southwest wall is a set of shelves and a cupboard, both of which are filled with food, beverages, and [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md). A food barrel is in the west corner, and a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) is under the counter. Against the southeast wall is a dresser with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), and three [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) on top. A ceiling rack containing three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, and two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) hangs above the dresser.

Opposite the door on the southeast wall is a fireplace with four [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging from a rack to the left. Further to the left is a food barrel and a set of shelves containing an iron mace. There is a flight of stairs leading up to the living quarters against the northeast wall with several barrels, a pile of firewood, and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot underneath.

At the top of the stairs is a long wooden table against the southeast wall. On the table are several pieces of cheese, two pieces of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat), three bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a couple of loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and three [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). To the right is a set of shelves holding only clutter. Coming out from the southwest wall are two owned single beds with a round table between them and an unlocked chest to the left. On the table are two bottles of alto wine and five [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md). In the northern corner above the stairs are several sacks of foodstuffs and a couple of barrels. A door leading out onto the balcony is located in the middle of the northwest wall.
