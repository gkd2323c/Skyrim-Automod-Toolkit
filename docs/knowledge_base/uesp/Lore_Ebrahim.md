*Truly the Divines have shown me the answer. For has not the greatest Forebear Grandee, Ebrahim of Sentinel, a fair and lissome daughter, Fereshtah? And has not the greatest Crown Grandee, Murahd of Rihad, a fierce and clever daughter, Arlimahera?*
— [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup> **Grandee** Ebrahim of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) was a prominent [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) nobleman active during the mid– [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He was a contemporary of High King [Ja-Fr](Lore_Ja-Fr.md) and his successor, [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), and the greatest Forebear Grandee of his time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/ON-place-Sentinel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sentinel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) Sentinel in the Second Era Little is known of Ebrahim's life. He hailed from the kingdom of Sentinel, and by the time of High King Ja-Fr's death and Ar-Azal's accession to the throne, he was regarded as the greatest Grandee among the Forebears. He also had a daughter, [Fereshtah](Lore_Fereshtah.md), known for her grace and beauty.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

When Ar-Azal became king, he enjoyed little support from either faction: the Forebears distrusted him because he was a [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md), while the Crowns resented him because his father had failed to destroy the Forebears. Guided by a vision, Ar-Azal sought to mend the divide by [marrying](Lore_Marriage.md) both Fereshtah, daughter of Ebrahim, the greatest Grandee of the Forebears and [Arlimahera](Lore_Arlimahera.md), daughter of Grandee [Murahd](Lore_Murahd.md) of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), the greatest Grandee of the Crowns, thus bringing Hammerfell closer to unification.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>
