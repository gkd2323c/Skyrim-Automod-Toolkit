# Tuff

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/ON-quest-Nirnroot_Wine.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Nirnroot_Wine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Nirnroot_Wine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Nirnroot_Wine.jpg) Red Mountain Tuff found around Lake Rumare Tuff is a type of rock formed from volcanic ash and fragments that have been compacted and cemented together. [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) ash from its eruption could be found in tuff around [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RMA-2)</sup> It was used as an ingredient in an [alcoholic beverage](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md) called [Nirnroot](Lore_Nirnroot.md) Wine.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Zimar-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Wilminn-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Thalara-5)</sup>
