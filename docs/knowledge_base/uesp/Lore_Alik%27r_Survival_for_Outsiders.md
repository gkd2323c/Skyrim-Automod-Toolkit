Alik'r Survival for Outsiders (Excerpt) *Advice for traveling through the Alik'r Desert*
---
[![ON-concept-Redguard medium armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/d/d2/ON-concept-Redguard_medium_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Redguard_medium_armor.png)
- Now that we've discussed the importance of a guide and some of the common dangers posed by indigenous creatures, if you're still convinced you'd like to travel through the deadly [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), you must be properly clothed. As you can tell from our review of aggressive beasts, you will absolutely need protection, but not just any armor will do.

- First, it's important to cover yourself fully to avoid painful burning from the sun. Even the back of the neck and top of the head must be covered. I strongly recommend that you purchase one of the typical leather armors worn by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). The cloth components breathe surprisingly well, and the leather plates offer excellent lightweight protection. Consider a covering for the mouth, as sandstorms are common, and breathing in grit is rather unpleasant.
