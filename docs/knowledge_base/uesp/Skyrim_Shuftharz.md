[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-npc-Shuftharz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Shuftharz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shuftharz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shuftharz.jpg) Shuftharz working at her forge **Shuftharz** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) living in [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). She will only trade with you if you are [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs. She will also buy [Orichalcum](Skyrim_Orichalcum.md) ore if you have any.

Shuftharz lives much of her life dedicated to working the forge at the Stronghold. She wakes up at 8am and immediately heads to the forge. She works at it for twelve hours, offering her various services. After a long day of working hard at the forge, she will head inside [Larak's Longhouse](Skyrim_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) at 8pm for a four hour dinner. After finishing her dinner, she will head to bed at midnight and resume her schedule the next day.

Shuftharz wears a set of [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080697) along with a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c1) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699). She carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) to Larak's Longhouse and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). She also carries a variety of weapons, including a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), and 20 [arrows](Skyrim_Arrow.md), all of which can be up to Orcish in quality.

## Dialogue
While she's at the forge offering her services:

- *"You need some armor, outlander?"*
- *"How about an axe? [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) smiles on the axe-wielder."*

When she's not at the forge:

- *"We can trade in the morning, outlander."*

At any time:

- *"The forge is strong and so is our chief, [Larak](Skyrim_Chief_Larak.md). I'm content."*
- *"My son, Olur, learns the forge well. His work brings me pride."*

## Conversations
**Chief Larak**: *"Your muscles ripple when you work the forge. It's pleasing."*
**Shuftharz**: *"Thank you, my chief."*
