It is acquired through the [Caught in a Web](Skyrim_Caught_in_a_Web.md) quest. Tempering the Ice Blade requires a [Steel Ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
