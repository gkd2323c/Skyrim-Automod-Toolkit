Letter from Tyra Blood-Fire **by [Tyra Blood-Fire](Skyrim_Tyra_Blood-Fire.md)** *A letter challenging you to prove yourself*
---
My name is [Tyra Blood-Fire](Skyrim_Tyra_Blood-Fire.md). Like [Sottilde](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Sottilde) the Wet Ear before me, I am an orphan child of [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md). Many years ago, I took a band of misfits and rogues and turned them into the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md). In our heyday, they called us thieves, bandits, apostates and killers. And they were right. We were all of those things.

But so is the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) who taxes the poor to starvation, the soldiers who rape and pillage the towns, the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) who would erase our gods, and the guardsman who would behead the innocent.

I know your story. If there's going to be a judge for me, I don't want it to be them. Not the Empire, not the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md), and not the [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md). Just you and the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md).

But first I want you to prove you're a worthy judge.

Go to the docks in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). There, you'll meet a [beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(person).md) in need of your aid. Some would offer him [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). Others, [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) or [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md). Offer him the alms you think will best serve him.

Once you have proved your measure and your mercy, you must then show your mettle. I will wait for you at the [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) [shrine](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos) west of Windhelm.

When at last we meet, I ask that you speak only with your blade. For while the tongue lies, the sword is true.

-Tyra
