[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-npc-Alesan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alesan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alesan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alesan.jpg) Alesan **Alesan** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). He makes money by bringing food to the miners of [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) and [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md).

He sleeps on a bedroll at the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) every night between 9pm and 8am. He wakes up to go straight to work, running through the town and delivering food from the inn workers, [Karita](Skyrim_Karita_(bard).md) and [Thoring](Skyrim_Thoring.md), to the local miners: [Gjak](Skyrim_Gjak.md), [Karl](Skyrim_Karl.md), [Lond](Skyrim_Lond.md), and [Borgny](Skyrim_Borgny.md). At 6pm, he switches to feeding the chickens outside the inn, and can be seen carrying a handled basket.

He wears a set of [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes#000467bf) and [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8) and carries two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), along with an [assortment of random food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child).

## Dialogue
If the time is between 8am and 6pm, he will be busy doing his job, and will tell you such as he runs past you:

*"Sorry! Gotta run!"* *"One side, comin' through!"* *"Move it, move it!"* *"I'm late! Gotta run!"* If you approach him when it is at least 6pm, he may complain by saying:

*"I'm so tired..."* *"Whew! I'm worn out."* **What's the hurry?** *"I'm running food and supplies to the miners. Don't pay much, but they let me sleep by the fire in the inn most nights."* **Where are your parents, kid?** *"They're... dead. My pa was a sailor. Took me with him on his last trip. After he got sick, they... put us off here and left without us. He... died a few days later. I've tried to make do since then, but... it's been hard. Real hard."* You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](Skyrim_Child.md#Adoption) him. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) with a child's bed and container.

After you end conversation with him:

*"Nice talkin' with you."*
## Notes
- Alesan is the only [child](Skyrim_Child.md) in Dawnstar.

## Bugs
- If you adopt him, Alesan may sometimes not be able to receive gifts. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- If you adopt him, Alesan can get stuck in a loop, entering and exiting the cellar repeatedly, similar to [Lydia](Skyrim_Lydia.md) sitting and rising from the chair in [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md). <sup>**?**</sup> - Talking to Alesan and having him do something may fix this.
- Alesan's inventory can slowly become clogged up with baskets he never uses for anything due to a bad scripting setup. This means if you adopt him it could be impossible to give him gifts. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.5, addresses this issue. The script causing this has been removed and the baskets will be removed from his inventory.
