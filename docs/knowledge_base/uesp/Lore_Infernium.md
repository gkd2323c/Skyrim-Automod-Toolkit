Inferniums are large beastial [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that dwarf most known Daedra in both size and hideousness, resembling a great fanged insect, [leech](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Leech), or monstrous [caterpillar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Caterpillar) with numerous teeth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VI-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OI-2)</sup> They are many-legged beasts that chase after their prey, screaming and spewing molten rock. They are also occasionally referred to as **Lavapedes** but it is uncharacteristic of their morphology.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VD-3)</sup> They are very intelligent.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mazandi-4)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-concept-Infernium.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Infernium.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Infernium.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Infernium.jpg) An Infernium (ESO) Inferniums are huge enough to easily devour beasts as large as [horses](Lore_Horse.md). Although they do not have a preference in what it consumes they typically are obsessed with concepts of upheaval and revolution. Despite their bestial appearance, Inferniums do not pursue these ends in a crude, general sense. They are aware of where political and cultural power rests. High-ranking [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) routinely fall prey to attacks of Inferniums. They are attacked far more often than low-ranking Varlets who typically enjoy fewer defenses. Inferniums also consume powerful items such as grand [soul gems](Lore_Soul_Gem.md), master [lucents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lucent), and valuable extraplanar relics.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OI-2)</sup> They are also known to hoard trinkets such as jewelry and precious gems but also collect armor and magical items.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VI-1)</sup>

They can be found in [the Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), typically wherever there is anything or anyone of potential, prestige, or authority. They can also be encountered in the plane of [Deadlight](Lore_Deadlight.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-D-5)</sup> Inferniums are typically aligned with [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OI-2)</sup> though some could be found in the [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IN-6)</sup> Occasionally Inferniums are accompanied by [Havocrels](Lore_Havocrel.md). According to some sources Inferniums need that assistance. It was theorized that some Havocrels are attempting to train Inferniums. Certain Inferniums are noted to reform far more frequently than any other routinely killed Daedra. In some cases, the process of reformation can take only a few days.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VI-1)</sup> Although the time of reformation is short, it is inconsistent and varies between the specimen.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VD-3)</sup>

According to the records of the Scribes of Mora Inferniums managed to take down one of the [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md) clans back in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mazandi-4)</sup>

## Notes
- [Skitterises](Lore_Skitteris.md) resemble Inferniums, but their size is of a regular insect.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SK-7)</sup>
- Inferniums and Skitterises both resemble [Siege Crawlers](Lore_Siege_Crawler.md), giant Daedric siege engines.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IN-6)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Legends SC-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Oblivion SC-9)</sup> Inferniums specifically were the living inspiration for the incredible Crawlers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Deadlands_Preview.E2.80.94The_Zone-10)</sup>
- [House Hexos](Lore_House_Hexos.md) weapons were designed to be effective against Inferniums.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-CMHH-11)</sup>
