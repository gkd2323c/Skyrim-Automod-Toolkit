Boss found a buyer for that [sword](Skyrim_Bloodthirst.md) we looted going through the crates downstairs. Says we're to deliver it to [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) come morning.

Truth is the thing creeps me out, I'm glad to be rid of it.
