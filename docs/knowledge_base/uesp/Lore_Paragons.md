# Paragons

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/SR-item-Paragons.jpg/200px-SR-item-Paragons.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Paragons.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Paragons.jpg) The Paragons [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LG-cardart-Barter.png/200px-LG-cardart-Barter.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Barter.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Barter.png) Some of the Paragons on sale (Legends) The Paragons, also known as the **Paragon Stones** are a collection of five fabled large, ornate oval [gemstones](Lore_Crystals.md) set in ancient, intricately wrought silver crafted by the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PRIMA-2)</sup><sup>:995</sup> Each of the Paragons was fashioned from a distinct gemstone: [amethyst](Lore_Amethyst.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Amethyst_Paragon-3)</sup> [diamond](Lore_Diamond.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Diamond_Paragon-4)</sup> [emerald](Lore_Emerald.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Emerald_Paragon-5)</sup> [ruby](Lore_Ruby.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ruby_Paragon-6)</sup> and [sapphire](Lore_Sapphire.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Sapphire_Paragon-7)</sup>

The Paragons were used within the [Chantry of Auri-El](Lore_Chantry_of_Auri-El.md) in the [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md), where they served to activate portals on the Paragon Platform, granting access to different parts of the temple complex.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PP-8)</sup>

## History
Little is known about the precise origins of the Paragons, though they were originally utilized by the Snow Elves within the Chantry of Auri-El to navigate the [wayshrine](Lore_Wayshrines.md) portal network.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PP-8)</sup> By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Ruby Paragon had reportedly circulated in various locations across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) as contraband.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> At some unknown point in history, the Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald Paragons may have passed through the hands of traders and found their way into an unidentified marketplace.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Barter-9)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), all known Paragons had resurfaced in the Forgotten Vale, guarded by [Frost Giants](Lore_Frost_Giant.md) who had stolen them. They may have been discovered by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) during their travels.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-FG-10)</sup>

## Paragons

### Amethyst Paragon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg/150px-SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg) Amethyst Paragon Amethyst Paragon opened the entrance to an area above the Darkfall Grotto guarding a treasure.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Amethyst_Paragon-3)</sup>

### Diamond Paragon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg/150px-SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg) Diamond Paragon Diamond Paragon opened the entrance to an area above the Glacial Crevice guarding a treasure.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Diamond_Paragon-4)</sup>

### Emerald Paragon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-item-Emerald_Paragon.jpg/150px-SR-item-Emerald_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Emerald_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Emerald_Paragon.jpg) Emerald Paragon Emerald Paragon opened the entrance to the Forgotten Vale Overlook, an area that overlooked the giant lake where [Voslaarum](Lore_Voslaarum.md) and [Naaslaarum](Lore_Naaslaarum.md) resided. Among the treasures guarded by it was an ancient Snow Elven text *[Journal of Mirtil Angoth](Lore_Journal_of_Mirtil_Angoth.md)*.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Emerald_Paragon-5)</sup>

### Ruby Paragon
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Ruby_Paragon.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Ruby_Paragon.png) Ruby Paragon circa 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-item-Ruby_Paragon.jpg/150px-SR-item-Ruby_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ruby_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ruby_Paragon.jpg) Ruby Paragon Ruby Paragon opened the entrance to Forgotten Vale Forest, populated by Frost Trolls and a Falmer. As of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) it was also a location of [Auriel's Shield](Lore_Auriel%27s_Shield.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ruby_Paragon-6)</sup>

### Sapphire Paragon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg/150px-SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) Sapphire Paragon Sapphire Paragon opened the entrance to the Inner Sanctum, with an abundance of gemstones, elven armor and treasures.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Sapphire_Paragon-7)</sup>
