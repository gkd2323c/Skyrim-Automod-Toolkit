## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Sybille Stentor](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md).
2. Kill the Master Vampire in [Pinemoon Cave](Skyrim_Pinemoon_Cave.md).
3. Return to Sybille Stentor for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speak to [Sybille Stentor](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md) in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) throne room and ask what her problem is. She will tell you about a local nest of [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) which you should wipe out.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_02.jpg) The first area of Pinemoon Cave Make your way to [Pinemoon Cave](Skyrim_Pinemoon_Cave.md), west of Solitude. It is a very small cave with only four chambers, and an assortment of vampires and wolves. You only need to kill the Master Vampire to complete the quest. He can be found in the third chamber, a large room with pews facing an altar. The Master Vampire can be found on the altar area. However, he has two companions who will also attack if you are detected. They will reanimate a corpse of a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord_(dead).md) which lies on a table in the southern part of the chamber.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_the_Vampire_03.jpg) Vampires attacking If you do clear out the cave, there is a locked chest next to the altar. Also do not forget the fourth chamber, the sleeping quarters, which contains the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Incident at Necrom](Skyrim_Incident_at_Necrom.md)* and another chest.

### Reward
Exit the way you came in and return to Sybille Stentor for your reward, which is [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md) and a single free skill boost in Illusion.

## Notes
- Because this is a favor quest which affects Sybille's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [Thane of Haafingar](Skyrim_Thane_of_Haafingar.md) quest.
