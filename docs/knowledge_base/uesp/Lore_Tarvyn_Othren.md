# Tarvyn Othren

| Tarvyn Othren | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) | Gender | Male |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg) Iron Atronach Guar Tarvyn Othren was a pioneer [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) mage. He is accredited with the summoning of the first [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) [guar](Lore_Guar.md) in history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAG-1)</sup>

At some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), on a drunken dare, he decided to perform the conjuration. The summoned guar immediately killed his companions. Consumed by grief, Othren sought redemption for his heinous act. Ever since then, he only rode on that guar as a reminder of his mistake.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAG-1)</sup>

Despite the morbid origins of his discovery, by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the art of conjuring Iron Atronachs shaped like guar was popularized among conjuration mages.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAG-1)</sup>
