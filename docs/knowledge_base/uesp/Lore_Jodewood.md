[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-map-Jodewood.jpg/200px-ON-map-Jodewood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Jodewood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Jodewood.jpg) Map of Jodewood circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/ON-place-Jodewood.jpg/200px-ON-place-Jodewood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jodewood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jodewood.jpg) Jodewood Jodewood is the southernmost subregion of the greater [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) region. Jodewood is a border region between the provinces of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), mainly lying within the borders of the former. The [Crescent River](Lore_Crescent_River.md) headwaters are here, flowing north to [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md) at the edge of the March. The capital of the area is the city of [Rawl'kha](Lore_Rawl%27kha.md), a trading post in between the two provinces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The town of Willowgrove is a minor settlement known for its apiary and its brewery, which is used to make honeymead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The Do'Krin Monastery is an ancient [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) temple dating back to the time of [Darloc Brae](Lore_Darloc_Brae.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The ruins of [Thormar](Lore_Thormar.md) are a known gathering place for worshippers of [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md), who have been known to enter the [Five Finger Dance](Lore_Five_Finger_Dance.md) from here.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BDB-4)</sup>
