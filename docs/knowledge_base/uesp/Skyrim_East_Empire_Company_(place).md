[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-place-East_Empire_Company_%28place%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-East_Empire_Company_%28place%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-East_Empire_Company_(place).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-East_Empire_Company_(place).jpg) East Empire Company The **East Empire Company** is the regional office for the [company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) on the docks of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). The office is almost completely bare and run down before completing the relevant quest. It is located between the [Clan Shatter-Shield Office](Skyrim_Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.md) and the western end of the docks.

## East Empire Company
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-East_Empire_Company_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-East_Empire_Company_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-East_Empire_Company_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-East_Empire_Company_02.jpg) Afterwards When you first visit the office, it is dark, dusty, and full of cobwebs. There are round wooden tables in alcoves to the east and west, with the latter holding the [business ledger](Skyrim_Business_Ledger.md) and a level-locked non-respawning [strongbox](Skyrim_Strongbox.md) containing random items on top. There is a partition wall splitting the building into two rooms, with a doorway in the middle joining them. The rest of the office contains a few crates and [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks, but not much else, except empty shelves.

After completing the quest [Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md), the office and company will be restored. Once restored, the office is fairly well lit, clean, and tidy. There are still crates, barrels, and sacks of foodstuffs all around both rooms. There is a set of shelves to the left of the doorway holding a [draught of strength](Skyrim_Draught_of_Strength.md), a [solution of strength](Skyrim_Solution_of_Strength.md), a [philter of the defender](Skyrim_Philter_of_the_Defender.md), a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), and a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md). To the right of the doorway is a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a bottle of wine and a bottle of Nord mead on top. To the east, against the wall is a long wooden table holding a [bowl](Skyrim_Bowl.md) and a bottle of Nord mead. Through the doorway into the second room, there is a set of shelves holding copies of *[Brief History of the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brief_History_of_the_Empire)* (the complete series), *[Report: Disaster at Ionith](Skyrim_Report__Disaster_at_Ionith.md)*, *[Mixed Unit Tactics](Skyrim_Mixed_Unit_Tactics.md)*, *[Lost Legends](Skyrim_Lost_Legends.md)*, *[The Cake and The Diamond](Skyrim_The_Cake_and_The_Diamond.md)*, and *[Darkest Darkness](Skyrim_Darkest_Darkness.md)*. In the northeastern corner, on a crate is a bottle of alto wine, and in the southeastern corner, in a bucket on the floor is a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md).

## Notes
- The dressers in the location are non-respawning.
