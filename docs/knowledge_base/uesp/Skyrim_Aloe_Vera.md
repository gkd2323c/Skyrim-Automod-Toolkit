[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg) Aloe Vera Leaves [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg) Aloe Vera plant Aloe Vera Leaves are an ingredient that were added via the official `_Resource Pack.esl` file that was included with [Special Edition Patch 1.6.1130](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#1.6.1130), released on December 5, 2023. They can usually be purchased from the [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Notably, Aloe Vera Leaves are fully compatible with the [Greenhouse](Skyrim_Greenhouse.md) mechanic introduced by [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md). This means that you can plant it in any plot of [Fertile Soil](Skyrim_Fertile_Soil.md) and harvest two samples of the ingredient from the resulting Aloe Vera plant.
