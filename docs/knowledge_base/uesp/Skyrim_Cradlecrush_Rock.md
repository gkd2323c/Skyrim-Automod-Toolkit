## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock_02.jpg) Huge cheese The camp is near the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) downstream from [Valtheim Towers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valtheim_Towers) and [Darkshade](Skyrim_Darkshade.md). To reach it, follow the road east from Valtheim Towers. Shortly after a hairpin bend to the right at the bottom of the hill, you will see a ford on your left with an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) visible on the other side. Follow this path up to reach the camp.

A single [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) occupies the camp, which consists of a central campfire that has been extinguished, two [mammoth cheese bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl), a stacked woodpile with a dead painted [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) on top which can be looted for [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef) and a [cow hide](Skyrim_Cow_Hide.md), an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) with a leveled healing potion and a leveled stamina potion next to it, and a huge pile of straw that probably serves as the giant's bed, though you will never see him sleep in it. Among the ashes of the fire are three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), three [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [sliced Eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), and an unusually large [Eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), the largest of these in the game. A single stake with a [charred skeever](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) is cooking over the fire. A few feet west of the campfire, a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md), a leveled enchanted helm, and a ring can be found on three separate skeletons that appear to have been crushed by a huge rock.

## Notes
- 320 feet east-northeast of the camp is an [unmarked location](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md) containing a [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos), an unlocked chest, and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Mid Year, v6](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Mid Year,_v6)*.
- Cradlecrush Rock [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Cradlecrush) as a [fort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Forts) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
