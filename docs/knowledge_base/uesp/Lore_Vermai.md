*Zenaide * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * is sharp. Runs things real smooth. And Vermai... well... they certainly are vicious, aren't they?*
—A [Spider Daedra](Lore_Spider_Daedra.md)'s thoughts on the Vermai Clan<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Spider-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Vermai are a type of gaunt and sharp-toothed [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that serve as dark minions of the lesser [Daedra lords](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRC-2)</sup> They are aggressive and mindless, and typically attack all who approach. It is said that the Vermai have no thoughts, and therefore cannot fear.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sot D-3)</sup> They have sometimes responded to attempts at communication, but never in any intelligible way, considered to be one of the most idiotic Daedra.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BSConcept Art-4)</sup>

Those who summon them can control them through pacifying helmets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRC-2)</sup> At some point during the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the prodigal student Faindor of [Agea Relle](Lore_Agea_Relle.md) summoned Vermai through alchemy to serve him; out of spite, Nerien'eth had those same Daedra torture him.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TS-5)</sup> [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md), a servant of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), summoned Vermai to guard his tower and kept them under control through helmets. The "Vermai Clan" served Zenaida Nacarra, aiding [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) in his invasion of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
