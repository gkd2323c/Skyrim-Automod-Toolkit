# Godhead

— *[Waking Dreams of A Starless Sky](Lore_Black_Book__Waking_Dreams.md)* - [Bilius Felcrex](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Bilius_Felcrex) Godhead is a term with various meanings relating to [divinity](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gods) within the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md). It may be used to refer to a group of gods under one name, such as [Almsivi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Almsivi), the Triune godhead of [Almalexia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Almalexia), [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), and [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DD-1)</sup> It can also be used as a synonym for [apotheosis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lifespan#Apotheosis), the spiritual process by which mortals ascend to godhood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Po T-2)</sup>

**The Godhead** is seemingly also the name for an esoteric entity<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MDC3-3)</sup> of disputed existence.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAA-4)</sup> The entity is said to be plagued by madness and split personalities, and is recognized by some beliefs as the creator of the Aurbis by way of dreaming it into existence. For this, the entity is known as the **First Dreamer**.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-5)</sup> It is said within the nature of the Dreamer lies the secret of the [Psijic Endeavor](Lore_Psijic_Endeavor.md),<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Mot PE-6)</sup> a process by which mortals are charged with transcending the gods.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo FTDE-7)</sup> Those who achieve this goal, entering a state known as [CHIM](Lore_CHIM.md), are said to experience an ineffable sense of the Godhead and escape the limitations of the Aurbis.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Mot PE-6)</sup> The supposed state beyond CHIM, known as Amaranth, is said to involve [mantling](Lore_Mantling.md) the Godhead and dreaming in the same way.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BBWD-8)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-5)</sup>

## Notes
- [Anu the Everything](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Anu) bears similar characteristics to the Godhead in some cultures, encompassing all of reality and having an unknown true face.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MTHot W-10)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TTi SV1-11)</sup>
- The [Acharyai](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Acharyai) is the name of the supposed first spirit or spirits in existence, the distant ancestor or ancestors of the typically accepted et'Ada: the Aedra, Magna Ge, and Daedra. They are acknowledged by the Psijic Order and considered fundamental to their complex system of Ancestor Worship, known to them as the Old Ways.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TOW-12)</sup>
