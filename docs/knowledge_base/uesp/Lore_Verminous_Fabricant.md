Verminous Fabricants are [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricant.md) created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) that resemble lean bipedal [reptiles](Lore_Reptile.md). They have long necks, long tails, a horn on their noses, and various metallic limbs and spikes, which they utilize to attack their prey.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ASo F-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVFA-2)</sup> Sotha Sil created them as imitations of [extinct creatures](Lore_Verminous.md) of the same name that once roamed across [Velothi](Lore_Velothi.md) [lands](Lore_Morrowind.md), to serve as a reminder of a thing that once was. He sought to perfect them (in his eyes) with an amalgam of flesh and metal.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LACW-3)</sup> Aside from being quick, agile, and being able to fit into small spaces,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ASo F-1)</sup> they also are capable of spinning into their target, generating shockwaves, and as a last resort, self-destructing.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVFA-2)</sup> They are powered by an [animo core](Lore_Animo_Cores.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and can be contained within fabrication tanks.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Verminous Fabricants dwell in [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ASo F-1)</sup> but have in occasion made it to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRAttack Mournhold Quest-6)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/TR-interior-Clockwork_City.jpg/200px-TR-interior-Clockwork_City.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-interior-Clockwork_City.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-interior-Clockwork_City.jpg) Fabricant Machine (Tribunal) Before their extinction and subsequent mechanomagical recreation by Sotha Sil, the original reptiles the fabricants were based on, were known simply as the "Verminous" and they dwelled in ancient Velothi lands.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LACW-3)</sup>

Verminous fabricants were one of Sotha Sil's earlier creations. As part of the preliminary work on his lifelong project, the Clockwork City, Sotha Sil stored some of his early memories in Mnemonic Fragments within the [Dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) of [Kemel-Ze](Lore_Kemel-Ze.md). A wing there was gifted to him as a sign of the alliance between the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Armas-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Protus-8)</sup> One of the memories here showcases his work on an early Verminous Fabricant, where he comments that it eventually will wear down and crumble, but at that moment it was the definition of perfection. And so, self-repair was how he would "circumvent inevitable deterioration".<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Protus-8)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-prerelease-Clockwork_City_06.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Clockwork_City_06.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Clockwork_City_06.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Clockwork_City_06.jpg) A pack of Verminous Fabricants (ESO) Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a portal to the Clockwork City inexplicably opened up inside the [Abanabi Caves](Lore_Abanabi_Caves.md) when Divayth Fyr undertook to extend [Tel Fyr](Lore_Tel_Fyr.md) there, spilling out Refabricants and Fabricants from the Halls of Fabrication. The Hunter-Killer Positrox and Negatrix were a pair of formidable Verminous Fabricants encountered there. The Fabricant threat was neutralized when a large group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) accompanied Divayth Fyr into the cave and entered the Clockwork City portal, defeated the Assembly General, and disabled the Fabricants' production. Divayth then studied the portal.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> During this year, Verminous Fabricants along with other creatures across space and time also manifested within the Dwarven ruin of Nchuthnkarst in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> due to the temporal and spatial distortions caused by a malfunctioning [Dwemer device](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md) there.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Thaddeus_Cosma-11)</sup>

When the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) came to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) after [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s fall in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) sought to trick, entrap, and destroy them as part of an insidious plot. She first turned on Sotha Sil and slew him in his [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), then unleashed its mechanical inhabitants into her own city in order to frame her old friend, among them the Verminous Fabricants.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRAttack Mournhold Quest-6)</sup> Her ruse lured the Nerevarine to Sotha Sil's city, where she hoped that the Nerevarine would be killed by the inhabitants. When the Nerevarine persevered, Almalexia tried to finish the job herself. But her powers failed her, and she died at the Nerevarine's hand in the Clockwork City.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TRAlmalexia-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TRMad God Quest-13)</sup>

The Clockwork City and its people, creatures, and constructs were still around in the Fourth Era. Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a battle occurred between the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) and [Mecinar](Lore_Mecinar.md), and the fate of the city and its inhabitants remains unknown. Some stories claim that the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) destroyed the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md) that powered the Clockwork City, and its inhabitants were forced to abandon it as it ceased to function.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-LRTCCTCF-14)</sup> Another account holds that the hero used the Heart to harness its power and fought Mecinar on equal ground, allowing the Heart to live on along with the city.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-LRTCCKJE-15)</sup>

## Uses
Verminous Fabricants have been miniaturized for keeping as pets.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup> The artifact known as [Barilzar's Mazed Band](Lore_Barilzar%27s_Mazed_Band.md) and an iteration of the [Imperfect](Lore_Imperfect.md) had the ability to summon them.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup> Verminous Fabricant Elixirs, extracted from the remains of the creatures, give an increase to one's speed.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup> The Nerevarine possibly used these elixirs to out-maneuver the dangerous traps of the Clockwork City during their quest to confront Almalexia.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TRMad God Quest-13)</sup>

## Notes
- Verminous Fabricants are modeled after dinosaurs.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-20)</sup><sup>:317</sup> They share many visual similarities to [dromaeosaurs](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dromaeosauridae) or [troodontids](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Troodontidae) specifically.
- [Verminous Catfish](Lore_Verminous_Catfish.md) are a species of [fish](Lore_Fish.md) that were once presumed extinct but could be found in the Clockwork City, though it is unknown if they share any qualities with the Verminous or Verminous Fabricants.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-ESOVC-21)</sup>
