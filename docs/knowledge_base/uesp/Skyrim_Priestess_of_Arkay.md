[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-npc-Priestess_of_Arkay.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Priestess_of_Arkay.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Priestess_of_Arkay.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Priestess_of_Arkay.jpg) Priestess of Arkay The **Priestess of Arkay** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [priestess](Skyrim_Priest.md) devoted to [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), the god of burials and funeral rites. She wears [hooded monk robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Monk_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000bacd7). She also carries an [amulet of Arkay](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Arkay.md).

It is assumed she died during the attack on Helgen as she is not seen again afterwards.

## Related Quests
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
She attempts to deliver last rites to you and the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) soldiers that are being executed at [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) before the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack, but she is interrupted by a defiant Stormcloak rebel:

**Priestess**: *"As we commend your souls to [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), blessings of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md) upon you, for you are the salt and earth of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), our beloved...."*
**Stormcloak Soldier**: *"For the love of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), shut up and let's get this over with."*
**Priestess**: *"As you wish."*
**Stormcloak Soldier**: *"Come on, I haven't got all morning. My ancestors are smiling at me, Imperials. Can you say the same?"*
