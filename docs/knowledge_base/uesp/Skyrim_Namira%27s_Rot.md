It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

- In [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md), down the stairs from Alftand's entrance, directly southwest and under the porch of a Dwemer building patrolled by [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) is a patch of Namira's Rot where the mushrooms are almost ten times larger than those in regular patches.
