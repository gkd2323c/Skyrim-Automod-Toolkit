[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/OB-place-White_Rose_River.jpg/200px-OB-place-White_Rose_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-White_Rose_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-White_Rose_River.jpg) The White Rose River circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/ON-place-White_Rose_River_bed.jpg/200px-ON-place-White_Rose_River_bed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-White_Rose_River_bed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-White_Rose_River_bed.jpg) A bridge over the dry bed of the White Rose River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The White Rose River is an odd, roughly H-shaped river in central [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). There are two separate sources, both rising in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md), just south of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md). Both flow due north and are joined halfway along their lengths by a large stream before separating again and passing under the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md), emptying into [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md). This odd formation means a large island is formed between the river's lower reaches and its "cross-piece".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HT-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the river was completely dry,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup> although water had returned by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OB-4)</sup> Fort Roebeck can be found near the western source of the river, while [Pell's Gate](Lore_Pell%27s_Gate.md) and [Fort Homestead](Lore_Fort_Homestead.md) lie near the eastern bank. The [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of [Vindasel](Lore_Vindasel.md) are located near the western mouth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup>
