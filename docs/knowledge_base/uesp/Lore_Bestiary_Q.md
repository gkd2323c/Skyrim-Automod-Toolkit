## Quail
Quails are a type of [bird](Lore_Birds.md). Some toys, like stuffed giant [centipedes](Lore_Centipede.md) with googly eyes, were adorned with painted patterns resembling quail eggs, giving the illusion that the eyes followed those who looked at them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> Taverns were also named after these birds, such as the Roosting Quail.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HAB-2)</sup>

## [Quasigriff‎‎](Lore_Quasigriff.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/ON-creature-Aurielic_Quasigriff.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Aurielic_Quasigriff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Aurielic_Quasigriff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Aurielic_Quasigriff.jpg) A Quasigriff [Quasigriffs](Lore_Quasigriff.md) are large, feathered, wingless quadrupeds that are related to [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md). They were created through a combination of wizardry and selective breeding. According to their creator, [Irastimil Direnni](Lore_Irastimil_Direnni.md), their wingless frame kept their upkeep costs low. Although they were created artificially, various breeds of Quasigriffs can be found in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md), [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md), [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), and even in the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Quasigriff)
