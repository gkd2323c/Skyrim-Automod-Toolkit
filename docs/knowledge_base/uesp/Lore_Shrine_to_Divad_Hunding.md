Shrine to Divad Hunding *A plaque dedicated to Divad Hunding, famed Yokudan sword-singer*
---
In memory of [Divad Hunding](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md), son of [Frandar](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md). Aided his father in the defeat of Emperor [Hira](Lore_Hira.md) and the settlement of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).

Divad broke the [Goblinkins](Lore_Goblin.md)' hold over Hammerfell with the [creation of five mighty swords](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall_Preview/REDGUARD.TXT#The_Song_of_Divad).
