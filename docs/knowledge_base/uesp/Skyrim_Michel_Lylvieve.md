[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-npc-Michel_Lylvieve.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Michel_Lylvieve.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Michel_Lylvieve.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Michel_Lylvieve.jpg) Michel Lylvieve **Michel Lylvieve** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) with her husband [Azzada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azzada_Lylvieve), son [Clinton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clinton_Lylvieve), and daughter [Julienne](Skyrim_Julienne_Lylvieve.md).

Michel wakes up early at 8am and will start doing chores around the house for four hours. At midday she sits down with her family for lunch which takes up two hours, after which she will head outside to work. She will work outside for six hours straight, before heading back inside to have dinner with her family. After two hours of this, she relaxes around the house for two more hours before heading to bed at midnight.

She carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_House_Key) to [the family's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family%27s_House), and a simple [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She also wears a standard set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa).

## Dialogue
Her greetings can be:

*"If you're looking for a room, you want the [Four Shields](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern)."* *"You look like you've seen your share of adventure."* *"It's no easy thing to make a living in the wilds of Skyrim."* *"This land's tough and unforgiving, but beautiful, too."* *"You hear all this talk about a war? I hope it never comes here."*
## Conversations
She can often be seen conversing with her husband:

**Michel**: *"Did you read that letter from Markarth yet?"*
**Azzada**: *"Yes, just this morning. It was from Logrolf. He wrote to thank us for the food we sent."*
**Michel**: *"And how is that old warrior doing these days?"*
**Azzada**: *"Not well. He's convinced that his house is haunted, so he's sleeping on the street."*
**Michel**: *"Oh dear. I wish there was something we could do to help him."*
**Azzada**: *"As do I. That man saved my life. I hope that someone helps him, as he once helped me."*
