# Thieves Guild Armor Set

[![SR-item-Thieves Guild Armor Female.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Female.jpg/200px-SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Female.jpg) [![SR-item-Thieves Guild Armor Male.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Male.jpg/200px-SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Male.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Male.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Thieves_Guild_Armor_Male.jpg) The Thieves Guild Armor Set is a set of armor obtained for joining the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) and completing the quest *[Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md)*. Appropriately for an organization focusing on stealth, the set consists entirely of [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md). Every piece of the set requires [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) to be improved.

The armor set comes with a variety of [enchantments](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) already placed on the armor, providing the following benefits:

- [Fortify Carry Weight](Skyrim_Fortify_Carry_Weight.md), 20 pts
- [Fortify Pickpocket](Skyrim_Fortify_Pickpocket.md), 15 pts
- [Fortify Lockpicking](Skyrim_Fortify_Lockpicking.md), 15 pts
- [Fortify Barter](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md), 10 pts

After the completion of *[Scoundrel's Folly](Skyrim_Scoundrel%27s_Folly.md)*, you will be given the opportunity to receive an upgraded version of one of the pieces of the set. The upgraded version improves the armor rating, weight, value, and enchantment of whichever piece is chosen except for the gloves (which do not receive a lower weight with its improved version).

The [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) of Skyrim will be able to recognize the faction affiliation of this armor on sight, possibly causing them to comment:

- *"I know Thieves Guild armor when I see it. You're not fooling anyone."*
- *"Try to hide it all you want. I know you're in the Thieves Guild... and so do the other guards."*

All pieces of any Thieves Guild Armor set may be worn/improved but cannot be forged by the player.
