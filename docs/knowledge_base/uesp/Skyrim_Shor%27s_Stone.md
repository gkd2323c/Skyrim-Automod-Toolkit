[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/SR-place-Shor%27s_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shor%27s_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shor%27s_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shor%27s_Stone.jpg) Shor's Stone **Shor's Stone** is a mining [town](Skyrim_Towns.md) in the northern [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

It is north-northwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) in the pass between the Rift and [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). The town's name is taken from the [Nordic](Skyrim_Nord.md) god [Shor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shor). The residents are having trouble with [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) that have infested the local [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) mine, [Redbelly Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine).

## Notes
- An [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) and a [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) are due north of [Sylgja's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja%27s_House).
- Shor's Stone is the only town besides [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) that operates with its own [guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shor%27s_Stone_Guard).
- Animals spawn very close to Shor's Stone, which may occasionally cause fights to break out between the villagers and animals.
- Music tends to play irregularly in the area, as you can hear one song fade and another replace it.
- Shor's Stone also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Shor%27s_Stone) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
