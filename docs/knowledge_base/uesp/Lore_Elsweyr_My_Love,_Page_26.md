Elsweyr My Love, Page 26 *A page from a fragmented play* *
---
Tigerius Falco: "Shashara, your green eyes are so green … like green-colored gems!"

Shashara: "Sweet Tigerious, this one wishes we never have to part."

Tigerius Falco: "Your grace is a gift from the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), Shashara. I would do anything for you."

Shashara: "Except leave the [Imperial army](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md)."

Tigerius Falco: [shifts nervously] "The army has been good to me. Took me in when I was an orphan on the streets of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)."
