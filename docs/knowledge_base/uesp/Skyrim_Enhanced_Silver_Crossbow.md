# Silver

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-item-Silver_Ingot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Silver_Ingot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver_Ingot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver_Ingot.jpg) Silver Ingot [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-item-Silver_Ore.jpg/200px-SR-item-Silver_Ore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver_Ore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver_Ore.jpg) Silver Ore [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/SR-activity-Mining_%28Silver%29.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Mining_%28Silver%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Silver).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Silver).jpg) Silver Ore Vein **Silver** is a valuable metal that can be used to [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) silver jewelry, and an armor set with the addition of the [Alternative Armors - Silver](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It is also notable as the primary source of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md)'s wealth, due to the infamous [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), owned by the influential [Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md) clan. As a result, a popular saying is:

- *Blood and silver are what flows through Markarth....*

Large amounts of silver can be found in Markarth, both in the mine and in various homes and businesses in the city itself.

Five silver weapons are available in the game: [Silver Swords](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Sword), [Silver Greatswords](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Greatsword), [Silver Bolt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Bolt)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup>, [Silver Crossbow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Crossbow)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Expanded_Crossbow_Pack.md)</sup>, and [Enhanced Silver Crossbow](Skyrim_Enhanced_Silver_Crossbow.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Expanded_Crossbow_Pack.md)</sup>. They do 20 points of extra damage to [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md), including [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) and [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). You cannot forge these silver weapons; the only way to obtain them is by looting from [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) members and lairs.

## Mineral Sources
In addition to the following guaranteed sources, the best random locations are:

- Silver Ore may be sold by blacksmiths and general goods merchants starting at level 3.
- Silver Ingots may be sold by [Moth gro-Bagol](Skyrim_Moth_gro-Bagol.md) at all levels.

Silver Ore can also be created by [transmuting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Transmute_Mineral_Ore) [Iron Ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) (and can in turn be transmuted into [Gold Ore](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md)).

There are a total of 95 veins, 30 ores, and 119 ingots available in the game. Only notable locations are included in the following list, including places where large amount of the mineral may be found and places that are particularly easy to access. The [Silver Veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Silver_Veins) category contains a more complete list of places containing single veins. All exterior ore veins are shown on [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=the+Skyrim+map&search=Mine Ore Silver). Note, however, that only 50 veins are shown on the map at one time; to see the rest of the veins, click on "Next" at the bottom of the list of results.

## Silver Smithing
Silver jewelry can be [forged](Skyrim_Forge.md) from the start of the game, without the need to unlock any [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks, while Silver Armor requires the [Advanced Armors](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors.md) perk.

[](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)
### Silver Armor<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)</sup>
Silver Armor is a set of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) added by the [Alternative Armors - Silver](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). A full set can be recovered through the quest [When the Cat's Away](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)</sup>, while also appearing in random loot starting at level 10. Forging Silver Armor requires the Advanced Armors perk.

Its armor rating surpasses [Orcish armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Armor) but weighs more, also no shield exists.

The set lacks material keywords, and their absence would normally prevent the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk, however there is a script attached to the set that overrides that behavior and checks to see if the perk should be applied/removed whenever a piece of the set is added or removed. A similar script is used for [Daedric Mail](Skyrim_Daedric.md#Daedric_Mail_CC) and [Dwarven Mail](Skyrim_Dwarven.md#Dwarven_Mail CC).

## Notes
- 1 Silver Ingot is needed for the quest [The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure).
- Silver Ingots are also needed to improve the [Bloodskal Blade](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Blade.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>.
- Unlike in *Morrowind* and *Oblivion*, you don't need to use Silver or [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) weapons to defeat magical enemies.
- Ore veins of all types sometimes do not respawn normally. See [Mining bugs](Skyrim_Mining.md#Bugs).
- In Cidhna Mine, mining silver ore will complete any jail sentence in the Reach, except during [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md), in which you need to escape manually.
- Although two flawless garnets are needed to craft a Silver Jeweled Necklace, three gems are seen on the pendant. Additionally, the one in the center appears like a ruby while the two the border it have the correct garnet coloring.
- [Silver Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Sword) and [Silver Greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Greatsword) weapons cannot be forged.

## Bugs
- Manually mining silver ore in Cidhna Mine can make it impossible to serve your sentence properly. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.5, fixes this bug.
- Silver weapons cannot be improved at grindstones. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- The [Bloodskal Blade](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Blade.md) is a silver weapon but does not have the script to handle the silver weapon perk attached to it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.11, fixes this bug.
