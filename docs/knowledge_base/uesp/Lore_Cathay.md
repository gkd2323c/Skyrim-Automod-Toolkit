# Cathay

*When Jode is waxing and Jone is full
Then shall be born the Cathay
Whose stride will lay forth the Khajiiti kingdoms
And hold fast our faiths and traditions
Let them say
We are all one in ja-Kha'jay.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/2/26/Mwaxing_Sfull.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Sfull.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Sfull.jpg) Cathay are born under when Masser is waxing and Secunda is full<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Cathay are a bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Like the [Suthay-raht](Lore_Suthay-raht.md), the Cathay have upright pointed ears, fur covering their entire bodies, and long tails. However, Cathay are slightly larger and stronger than Suthay-raht, around the size of most [Men](Lore_Men.md) and [Mer](Lore_Mer.md). Unlike the Suthay-raht, the Cathay are plantigrades, walking flat on their foot and with a skeletal structure much more similar to that of Men and Mer.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ob Khajiit-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRKhajiit-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOKhajiit-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-GN-6)</sup>

The Cathay are among the most commonly seen Khajiit throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ob Khajiit-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRKhajiit-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOKhajiit-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Blades Khajiit-7)</sup>
