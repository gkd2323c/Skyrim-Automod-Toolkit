Cheetahs are a type of medium-sized [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ra Gada-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFPainting-2)</sup> They are known for their fast running speed,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bot C-3)</sup> and have a distinct spotted fur pattern.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CBM-4)</sup>

Many of the cats known as cheetahs in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Cheetah**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ra Gada-1)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup> Many [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have cheetah-like spotted fur patterns.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CBM-4)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
The ancient [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of the second [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) warrior wave were known to tame Senche-Cheetahs as mounts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ra Gada-1)</sup> Cheetahs are the subject of paintings popular in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, depicting a woman laying naked next to cheetahs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFPainting-2)</sup> Cheetahs are the namesake of the "Boots of the Cheetah" a pair of fur boots that increase the wearer's speed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bot C-3)</sup>
