[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-item-Armor_of_the_Old_Gods.jpg/200px-SR-item-Armor_of_the_Old_Gods.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Armor_of_the_Old_Gods.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Armor_of_the_Old_Gods.jpg) Armor of the Old Gods The Armor of the Old Gods is a special version of [Forsworn Armor](Skyrim_Forsworn_Armor.md) given to you by [Kaie](Skyrim_Kaie.md) for helping the leader of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md), [escape Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md). Alongside the chest piece, this armor set also includes a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots_of_the_Old_Gods.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Gauntlets_of_the_Old_Gods.md), and a [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet_of_the_Old_Gods.md). This item shares its appearance with basic Forsworn equipment, weighs half as much, offers two fewer points of armor, and is considerably more valuable. The Armor of the Old Gods has also been enchanted to [fortify destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) spells by fifteen points. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this item requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md)**: Escape from [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md)!

## Notes
- It is possible to obtain this armor set, as well as the reward from [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md). Run through the mine with Madanach until you get this equipment, then stall him while the other Forsworn escape the area. This will give you the opportunity to kill him, thus earning you the animosity of the surviving Forsworn prisoners, which they will express by attacking you once you leave. After defeating them, you can talk to Thonar, who will give you his [family ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Family_Ring) as a sign of his thanks.
