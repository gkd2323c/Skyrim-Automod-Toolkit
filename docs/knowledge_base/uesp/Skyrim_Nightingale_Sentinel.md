[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-npc-Nightingale_Sentinel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nightingale_Sentinel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nightingale_Sentinel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nightingale_Sentinel.jpg) A Nightingale Sentinel **Nightingale Sentinels** are the guardians of [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md) who stand guard in the Twilight Sepulcher. They are equipped with a full set of Nightingale weapons and armor, a copy of [Karliah's bow](Skyrim_Karliah%27s_Bow.md), and [elven arrows](Skyrim_Elven_Arrow.md). However, only the arrows are present when pickpocketing or looting a sentinel.

## Related Quests
- **[Darkness Returns](Skyrim_Darkness_Returns.md)**: Walk the [Pilgrim's Path](Skyrim_Twilight_Sepulcher.md) and return the [Skeleton Key](Skyrim_Skeleton_Key.md) to its rightful place.

## Dialogue
When alerted to the presence of an enemy:

- *"I sense a presence..."*
- *"Hmm? Does someone live among the dead?"*

When detecting an enemy:

- *"Mortal fool! No one hides from the dead!"*
- *"Death comes for you now!"*
- *"Who dares disturb my rest?"*
- *"You are not welcome here!"*
- *"All the living shall fear the dead!"*

## Notes
- Nightingale Sentinels are not considered undead in the game data. Thus, they are detected by the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md) spell instead of [Detect Dead](Skyrim_Detect_Dead.md).
