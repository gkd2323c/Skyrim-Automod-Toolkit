[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Aswala Stables is a town near the coast of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) near the port-town of [Tava's Blessing](Lore_Tava%27s_Blessing.md). It is known for its expert [horse](Lore_Horse.md) -breeders, specifically regarding the [Yokudan Chargers](Lore_Yokudan_Charger.md), an iconic breed of horse that comes from the main island of [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md), on the lost continent of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot YC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THo S-2)</sup> It is also the home of the Aswala family of horse-breeders, known members of the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LHAYQ-3)</sup> Much like the larger cities such as [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) and [Hallin's Stand](Lore_Hallin%27s_Stand.md), the town of Aswala Stables is overseen by a Grandee.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOContra-4)</sup>

## History
The Aswala Stables are known for their family of horse-breeders of the Yokudan Charger, a breed that was brought by [Yaghoub](Lore_Yaghoub.md) from Yokuda during the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) incursion in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Hot IB-5)</sup> Since then the horses have been used by [Gallants of Hammerfell](Lore_Gallants_of_Hammerfell.md), a knightly order of [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) that operate across the Alik'r Desert.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot YC-1)</sup>

The Aswala family, historically members of the Crowns were allies of the High Kingship that reigned from [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), specifically with the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md). The Yokudan Chargers that were supplied by the Aswala Stables were in constant demand in Sentinel, due to the shortage of [camels](Lore_Camel.md). This deal with two opposing factions was orchestrated by [Merric at-Aswala](Lore_Merric_at-Aswala.md), member of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), to further maintain the union of the parties at the time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LHAYQ-3)</sup>

However, in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) invasion had rippled across some of Tamriel's core regions, including the Alik'r Desert. The [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) had at one point seized control over the Aswala Stables, leaving the town in complete ruins, and at the center was the [dark anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md) of the [Hollow Wastes](Lore_Hollow_Wastes.md). On several occasions, the Worm Cult attempted to connect the dark anchor to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) but their endeavors had been thwarted every time by passing [adventurers](Lore_Soulless_One.md) and even the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESODolmen-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOAnchors-7)</sup>
