# Order of Talos

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/OB-place-Weynon_Priory.jpg/200px-OB-place-Weynon_Priory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Weynon_Priory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Weynon_Priory.jpg) Weynon Priory The Order of Talos was a religious order dedicated to spreading the teachings and worship of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md). They worshipped Talos as both a [god](Lore_Gods.md) and a [patron saint](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They had close ties to the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), and many members of the Blades retired to positions in the order.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> [Weynon Priory](Lore_Weynon_Priory.md) was at one time run by the order, since as early as [3E 360](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_360).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The most notable member of the order is, perhaps, Grandmaster [Jauffre](Lore_Jauffre.md), who lived at the priory for a time before returning to [Cloud Ruler Temple](Lore_Cloud_Ruler_Temple.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Following the signing of the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), the Blades were formally disbanded and the worship of Talos was banned in the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> As such, it could be assumed that the Order of Talos is no longer in existence.
