Baron Othrok was a Baron of [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md), who ruled the barony in the middle of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Although the Baron definitely existed, it is difficult to separate the truth from legends that have arisen around him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup> Othrok's mother was Nefristia, a High Priestess. He also had two sisters, whose names were Idris and Morena (who is oddly described as a "Dawn Succubus").<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-JLDND-2)</sup>

One legend deals with the foundation of the barony itself, and how, in [3E 253](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_253), the Baron led an army of animals and men in a victorious battle against the undead armies of Castle Wightmoor at the [Battle of Wightmoor](Lore_Battle_of_Wightmoor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Army)</sup> Although it is unclear whether this is literally true or not, the people of the barony celebrate a holiday called Othroktide on the 5th of Sun's Dawn each year in memory of the battle.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Baron is even more famous as the man who stopped the invasion of the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) in [3E 267](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_267).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup> Although the credit given to the Baron is probably overstated, it was at the [Battle of Firewaves](Lore_Battle_of_Firewaves.md) —not in Dwynnen itself, but nearby—that the combined armies of Dwynnen, [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md), [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md), and [Kambria](Lore_Kambria.md) defeated the Usurper and restored peace to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Baron Othrok was [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson)'s character during the [*D&D* game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Pen_%26_Paper_Worldbuilding) that happened between the development of *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* and *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*. The book *[The Fall of the Usurper](Lore_The_Fall_of_the_Usurper.md)* follows the story of that campaign.
- | [^1](#note_Army) | It is stated in the book *The Fall of the Ursurper* that "*Othrok was blessed with [sic] by gods and given an army of men and animals to destroy the dead.*" The exact meaning of this phrase is up for debate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOTU-1)</sup> However, *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)* states that "*Certainly [Camoran Ursuper's] army was largely supernatural, but so was that of his archfoe, the Baron of Dwynnen."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDC-4)</sup> |
