[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-npc-Jesper.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jesper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jesper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jesper.jpg) Jesper in Rockwallow Mine **Jesper** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works at [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md) under the direction of [Gestur Rockbreaker](Skyrim_Gestur_Rockbreaker.md).

Like the rest of the miners in Stonehills, Jesper's purpose in life is hard work. He sleeps in the neighboring campsite every night between 10pm and 7am then heads directly into Rockwallow Mine where he works until 6pm. He then enjoys a two-hour breakfast with his colleagues and spends the rest of his evening chopping wood at the [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) in the camp.

He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Longhouse) to the nonexistent longhouse, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Jesper will quickly express that his only current interest is to work at the mine.

- *"I'm just trying to make a living. I've no interest in trouble."*

- *"My business is my own. Why can't others see that?"*

- *"The mine gives us purpose. Something many of us have wished for."*

### Conversations
Occasionally, his employer Gestur Rockbreaker checks up on him while inside the mine:

- **Gestur**: *"Everything well, Jesper?"*
- **Jesper**: *"Yes sir, fine sir."*
- **Gestur**: *"Good, good. Keep up the hard work. It'll all pay off."*
