[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) The garrison at the Bangkorai Pass circa 2E 582 Priscus Mactator was an Abbot-General within the military branch of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md).

He led the Legions of Piety and Grace in an attempt to bring the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) back into the [First Empire](Lore_First_Empire.md) in [1E 2305](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2305). Under his leadership, the fanatical legions marched through the [Fallen Wastes](Lore_Fallen_Wastes.md) towards the garrison at the [Bangkorai Pass](Lore_Bangkorai_Pass.md), which had always been the first line of defense for the Breton Kingdoms against invaders from the east. However, the garrison proved impenetrable, and after a five-month siege, Mactator was forced to admit defeat and retreat back to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in disgrace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSOHR-1)</sup>
