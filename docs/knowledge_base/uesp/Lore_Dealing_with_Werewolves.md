Dealing with Werewolves **by [Venustinius Perquitienus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Venustinius_Perquitienus)** *Notes on how to deal with werewolves*
---
Whether you're stationed at a garrison at [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) or suffering [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) inhospitality in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) subject must know the signs of the terrible affliction of [Lycanthropy](Lore_Lycanthropy.md). With attacks by [creatures](Lore_Bestiary.md) infected with [Sanies Lupinus](Lore_Sanies_Lupinus.md) on the rise, it is your duty to learn the following and behave accordingly.

Is there an overabundance of [canis root](Lore_Canis_Root.md) in casks and market stalls? Have you witnessed the locals rubbing this root on neighboring trees and fences? Have you followed strange animal tracks, only to find them disappear? Do the village temples house beggars with vivid nightmares or with deep claw wounds to their faces or bodies? Does the wolf howl when there are none to be found? Then [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md) (or worse still, [werebears](Lore_Werebear.md)) may be active in your jurisdiction.

Werewolves are sturdy hybrids with powerful jaws and claws on both hands and feet. They stand three hands taller than an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) and exhibit severe bloodlust. If you encounter one, attempt to flee at all costs unless you feel supremely confident in your [arms](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor. If possible, thrust the indigenous population into the path of the lycanthrope, so it sates its appetite on them while you retreat to cover or your mount.

If you must fight a werewolf, arm yourself with any [silver](Lore_Silver.md) weapon, as these have proven extremely effective. However, prepare for severe gash wounds and the possibility of becoming infected. Should this occur, report to your superior for final rites to [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) and immediate execution.
