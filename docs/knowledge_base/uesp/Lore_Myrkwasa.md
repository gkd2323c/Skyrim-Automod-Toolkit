[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/ON-place-Myrkwasa.jpg/200px-ON-place-Myrkwasa.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Myrkwasa.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Myrkwasa.jpg) Myrkwasa circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Myrkwasa is a region in western [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md). It borders the [Hollow Wastes](Lore_Hollow_Wastes.md) to the east. The region contains the city of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md). The most important places are the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin [Salas En](Lore_Salas_En.md), the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin [Santaki](Lore_Santaki.md), and the great necropolis of [Tu'whacca's Throne](Lore_Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) had a camp in the southern part of the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>
