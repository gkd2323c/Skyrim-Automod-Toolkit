- *"A magical shield that when blocked with, surrounds it's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * wielder with a holy aura that harms the undead. Might come in handy."* — [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md)

The Dawnguard Rune Shield is a powerful shield received as part of the [Lost Relic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Relic) quest. When you block with this shield, an aura similar to [Stendarr's Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Aura) will appear around you that does harm to undead.

## Notes
- While this shield is equipped, due to its aura effect, NPCs will comment as if you had a Cloak spell on, saying *"That spell looks dangerous... keep your distance."* This happens even if you're not in combat and just carrying the shield without activating the aura.
- Because the magic effect is internally realized as script rather than an enchantment, the shield can be tempered without the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. It cannot, however, be further enchanted.
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- The shield's aura may sometimes stop working. - Dropping and picking up the shield again seems to fix this.
