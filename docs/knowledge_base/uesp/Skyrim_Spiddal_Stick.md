It can be harvested from Spiddal Sticks, poisonous yellow flowers found growing in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md). When approached too closely, they release [poison gas](Skyrim_Poison_Damage.md) that damages health over time and causes you to cough and wheeze, similar to [Darkfall Passage](Skyrim_Darkfall_Passage.md)'s [Poison Blooms](Skyrim_Poison_Bloom.md).

## Notes
- Spiddal Stick previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spiddal_Stick) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
- In *Oblivion*, both living and dead Spiddal Sticks could be found. A single dead Spiddal plant appears: the withered plant in the Mythic Dawn Temple that appears upon your return from the Deadlands.
