[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The Brittleshin Hills are a small mountain range of [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md) north of [Lake Ilinalta](Lore_Lake_Ilinalta.md) that make up the border with [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The sharp and protruding snowy peaks are dominated by [Bleak Falls Barrow](Lore_Bleak_Falls_Barrow.md), and overlook the origin of the White River, as well as the town of [Riverwood](Lore_Riverwood.md) just across the border in Whiterun Hold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> **Brittleshin Pass** allows movement through the mountains.

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), two [necromancers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Necromancy) from [Olvenveld](Lore_Olenveld.md) established themselves within Brittleshin Pass, where they intended to open a portal to the [Soul Cairn](Lore_Soul_Cairn.md). They managed to acquire the [Staff of Hasedoki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Hasedoki) via local smugglers. They planned to release Hasedoki's soul from the staff to earn the favor of the [Ideal Masters](Lore_Ideal_Masters.md), although they were both killed by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) before this could occur.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
