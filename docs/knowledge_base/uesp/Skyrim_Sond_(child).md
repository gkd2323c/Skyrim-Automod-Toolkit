[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-npc-Sond_%28child%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sond_%28child%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sond_(child).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sond_(child).jpg) Sond **Sond** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who can be [encountered randomly](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dwemer_Junk_Peddlers) out in the wilds together with his friend [Bottar](Skyrim_Bottar.md). He wears red colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). He carries two [Dwemer cogs](Skyrim_Dwemer_Cog.md), a piece of [Dwemer scrap metal](Skyrim_Dwemer_Scrap_Metal.md), and a [knife](Skyrim_Knife_(item).md).

He will sell you a waypoint to [Deep Folk Crossing](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md) for one septim, or will give it to you for free if you choose to intimidate him. He has some items for sale.

## Dialogue
When first encountered:

- *"Hey there. Check out what my friend and I found. I can sell some of it if you want!*"
- **Where did you find this, boy?**
- *"I found them near the old Dwemer bridge in the far west. I'll mark it down for you on your map for a small fee."*

- - **Fine, you little swindler. Here's a septim. Now where's this bridge?**
- *"Thank you! You'll find the ruins right here."*

- - **Show me where it is, before I take you over my knee.**
- *"Fine, I'll show you, you big bully."*
