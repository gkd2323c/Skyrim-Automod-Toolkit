[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Bracers.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Bracers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Bracers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Bracers.jpg) Ulfric's Bracers [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg) Ulfric's clothing, while worn Ulfric's Bracers are the gauntlets used by the eponymous [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). They share their basic design with [fine armguards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Armguards), although they are made out of different materials.

## Notes
- The bracers appear as fine armguards when worn by a female.

## Bugs
- Although they are considered to be light armor, Ulfric's bracers will not benefit from Light Armor perks, as they lack the proper keyword for it.
- This item will not appear as the right color in the inventory, instead appearing to look like the fine armguards it is based on. Its world model will still have its unique appearance, however. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, addresses this issue. Ulfric's bracers will now appear as they should when viewed in the inventory.
- Ulfric's bracers cannot be improved at a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. It enables upgrading with [Leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) but does not use any [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks.
- The bracers will be mostly invisible on a female character; only the parts covering the back of the hands can be seen.
