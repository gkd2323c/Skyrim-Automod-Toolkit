Statue of Sir Byric *A plaque describing the origins of the Knights of the Flame*
---
Dedicated to Sir [Byric](Lore_Byric.md) of the Flame, who set fire to the fields surrounding the [Alcaire Castle](Lore_Alcaire_Castle.md), holding back invaders from [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) in [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542).

The [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire.md) Knights were renamed [Knights of the Flame](Lore_Knights_of_the_Flame.md) to honor his bold action and determination.
