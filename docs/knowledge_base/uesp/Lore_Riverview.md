[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/AR-place-Riverview.jpg/200px-AR-place-Riverview.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverview.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverview.jpg) Riverview during the Imperial Simulacrum River View (or **Riverview**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a village found north of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md) in west-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the inland area between [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) and [Hubalajad's Bay](Lore_Hubalajad%27s_Bay.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup>

The village of River View has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Riverview was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Blotten and had a rivalry with [Shady Grove](Lore_Shady_Grove.md) and [Thorstad Place](Lore_Thorstad_Place.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Riverview is neighbored by several settlements, including [Vulnim Gate](Lore_Vulnim_Gate.md) to the north as well as [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) and Lainebon Place to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
