# Fate-Bearers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/ON-npc-Fate-Bearer_Initiate.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Fate-Bearer_Initiate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Fate-Bearer_Initiate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Fate-Bearer_Initiate.jpg) A Fate-Bearer The Fate-Bearers are a group of prophetic assassins based at their shrine of the changing fates out of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). They use the rare gift of [sight](Lore_Sight.md) to see what the future holds, eliminate targets that have the potential to cause unmitigated disasters. Good and evil have no bearings on their visions, but it is the actions of individuals they focus on.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Louna-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSK-4)</sup> Many of their members are of [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) descent.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Louna-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Fate Bearers-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-No V-6)</sup>

Evidently, while their visions are accurate, their actions are not infallible, as their methods of interpreting the visions are subjective.
