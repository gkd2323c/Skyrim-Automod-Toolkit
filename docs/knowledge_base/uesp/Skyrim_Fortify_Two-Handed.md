*Alchemy description:* **Two-handed weapons do <mag>% more damage for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Two-handed attacks do <mag>% more damage.**
**Fortify Two-handed** increases the damage of [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md).

## Alchemy
The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a potion of Fortify Two-handed:

- [Angler Larvae](Skyrim_Angler_Larvae.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Dragon's Tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md)
- [Emperor Parasol Moss](Skyrim_Emperor_Parasol_Moss.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Fly Amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md)
- [Heart of Order](Skyrim_Heart_of_Order.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**1.25** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.27** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))
- [Juvenile Mudcrab](Skyrim_Juvenile_Mudcrab.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Troll Fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat)

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Two-handed if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Bracers/Gauntlets, Necklaces, and Rings *[of Sure Grip](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Two-Handed)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Sure Grip*, *of Major Sure Grip*, *of Extreme Sure Grip*, *of Eminent Sure Grip*, and *of Peerless Sure Grip.*
- [Ironhand Gauntlets](Skyrim_Ironhand_Gauntlets.md)
- [Ring of Minor Striking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Minor_Striking)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Fists of Randagulf](Skyrim_Fists_of_Randagulf.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Call to Arms](Skyrim_Call_to_Arms.md) spell includes a secondary Fortify Two-handed effect, which directly increases the [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) skill of allies.
- The [Elytra's Fierce Aura](Skyrim_Elytra%27s_Fierce_Aura.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup> provides +10 Fortify Two-handed for 5 minutes.
- The name of the enchanting version of this effect is actually "Fortify Two- **H** anded" instead of "Fortify Two- **h** anded".
