Evacuation Order **by Porcius Sisenna** *Details on the planned evacuation of White Rose Prison*
---
All guards are to pack and assemble in the courtyard at dawn for evacuation of White Rose Prison. We will be returning home to serve as additional security for the capital.

No need to worry about the prisoners, they won't be going anywhere, what with the keystone binding them to this place. Have your equipment and belongings packed and be ready to travel at dawn. Tomorrow we march for [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

Porcius Sisenna, Administrator
