**Vampire Cattle** are NPCs found in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). They are used to feed the [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) within the castle. They wear [roughspun tunics](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps). They frequently moan in pain and ask to be freed, but there is no way to help them.

Once you agree to become a Vampire Lord, Harkon tells you that he can provide all the feeding you will require. There are usually two vampires feeding on a cattle each in the dining hall. These cattle are impossible to feed on; however, you may feed on others in another area of the keep. The thralls can be fed upon as many times as needed. It is not possible to kill them by feeding on them.

## Dialogue
*"Where... am I..."* *"Need... to sleep..."* *"What's happening..."* *"Who are you?..."* *"Please... No more..."* *"Let me go..."* *"Why me..."*
## Notes
- All of the cattle in the slave pens will vanish without a trace once the Dawnguard are joined and Kindred Judgment is started.
- If [survival mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, feeding on Vampire Cattle can help you keep your [Hunger](Skyrim_Hunger.md) under control.
