Hrelvesuu is a [daedroth](Lore_Daedroth.md) who serves [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), whom tasked her with wreaking havoc and terror on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) of Nchurdamz in Vvardenfell has been her lair since at least [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOBal-1)</sup> and serves as a sanctuary that she teleports to when she is in danger.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWLarienna-2)</sup> She has a fascination with the "toys of mortals".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOBal-1)</sup> She is associated with a powerful armor known as The Bestial Rampart.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMMN-3)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), Hrelvesuu served as a general in [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s service,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Anchor G-4)</sup> and was ordered to defend a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md). However, she was vanquished by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), and the Anchor was destroyed.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Anchor H-5)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Hrelvesuu had been in several confrontations with an Imperial warrior by the name of Larienna Maerina. Maerina would come to hold a vendetta against Hrelvesuu, and pursued her the world over. During their second to last battle, Larienna dealt Hrelvesuu a grievous wound, which forced the Daedroth to teleport back to Nchurdamz and recuperate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWLarienna-2)</sup> Larienna hired a [member](Lore_Nerevarine.md) of the Fighters Guild stationed at [Wolverine Hall](Lore_Wolverine_Hall.md) under the pretense that they would aid her in wiping out [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) in the ruins.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWHrundi-6)</sup> In reality, she required aid in finishing off the daedroth. Larienna offered the treasure within the ruins to the guild member who was contracted to assist her.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWLarienna-2)</sup> Hrelvesuu was slain and the treasures she guarded were free to claim. Among them was the enchanted Dwemer spear Illkurok, which allowed the user to see in the dark and increased their aptitude with the spear. Despite the death of Hrelvesuu, Larienna felt uneasy, as the room the Daedroth dwelled in held a mysterious crystal, and she remained in the ruins to investigate it further.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWBN-7)</sup>
