# Order of the Scarab

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/LO-misc-Knight_of_the_Scarab_at_Totambu.jpg/200px-LO-misc-Knight_of_the_Scarab_at_Totambu.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Knight_of_the_Scarab_at_Totambu.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Knight_of_the_Scarab_at_Totambu.jpg) Knight of the Scarab The Knights of the Scarab are [Gallants](Lore_Gallant.md) serving as the official guard for the royal family of [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md) in Northern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Only the most refined and loyal warriors of the region are considered for initiation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Typically initiates must travel to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins of [Bthzark](Lore_Bthzark.md) on the island of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) and "wrestle its [mechanical men](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) back into shape" before they can join the Knights of the Scarab.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-2)</sup> They are known to use shields adorned with [scarab](Lore_Scarab.md) symbols.

As a squire, Sir Lathon was preparing to join the Knights of the Scarab in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), but received a holy calling and joined the [Knights of the Nine](Lore_Knights_of_the_Nine.md) instead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
