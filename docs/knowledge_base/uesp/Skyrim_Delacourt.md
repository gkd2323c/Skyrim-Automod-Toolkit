[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-npc-Delacourt.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Delacourt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delacourt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delacourt.jpg) Delacourt **Delacourt** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [rogue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue) and the resident [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md) of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). He spends his entire day at [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md), either entertaining the customers or sitting for a meal. He can [sing](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) *Ragnar the Red* or *Age of Aggression*.

He wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
He may cheerfully tell you:

*"Keep in song in your heart to ward off the gloom."* *"Perhaps one day, I'll write a song about you!"*
