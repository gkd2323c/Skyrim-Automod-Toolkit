[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-npc-Seasoned_Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Seasoned_Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Seasoned_Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Seasoned_Hunter.jpg) Seasoned Hunter The **Seasoned Hunter** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who is spawned by the side of the road just outside of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) chooses a contract who asks the player to kill this NPC in the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). The seasoned hunter is one of ten potential targets chosen by the [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quest system.

He wears a full set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), including the [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), [bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md). He wields an [ancient Nord bow](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Bow.md) with twenty [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md). Not knowing about the player's intentions, the hunter pays little attention and says, "*Look, I'm busy. Please go bother somebody else.*"

## Related Quests
- **[The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md)**: Yet another child has prayed to their [mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md).

## Notes
- This NPC can be killed more easily than the other potential targets chosen by the radiant quest system. The location where this NPC is spawned is just out of the line of sight of the [Falkreath guards](Skyrim_Falkreath_Guard.md) and, thus, the kill can be accomplished without incurring a bounty.
