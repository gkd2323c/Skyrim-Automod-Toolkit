[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-npc-Farkas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Farkas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Farkas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Farkas.jpg) Farkas **Farkas** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) and member of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md). While, as his twin brother [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) puts it, *"his brains are not his strong suit"*, Farkas is respected as a skilled warrior.

He and Vilkas belong to the Companions' [inner circle](Skyrim_The_Circle.md) and, as such, are both [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md).

Farkas appears morally ambivalent about being a werewolf, and is hesitant to use his [Beast Form](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Form). He can be loose with his tongue, often showing impertinence to his elders. However, he warms up quickly to you when met, and is quick to protect his friends.

He wears a random set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor) and is armed with a [Skyforge steel greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Greatsword) (or an [iron greatsword](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md) at lower levels). He also carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and the [key to the Whiterun gate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Gate_Key).

He offers both miscellaneous quests and master level [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md). During the [journey to Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md), Farkas will refuse to accompany you past the outer chambers, admitting that he has developed a fear of giant spiders following your initiation trial in the quest [Proving Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proving_Honor).

## Dialogue
When first meeting him outside [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md):

*"You look strong. Come to Jorrvaskr and be a Companion!"* When approaching him:

*"My brother Vilkas is a better talker than me. He should be around someplace."* **Who are the Companions?** *"The Companions are my family. We fight so that other people don't have to. We bring honor and glory to ourselves and each other."* **Why did you join the Companions?** *"Vilkas and I have been here since we were little whelps. Our father, Jergen, raised us here. Even Vignar couldn't remember Companions younger than us!"* **What does it mean to be a Companion?** *"I've never been a smart one. But the Companions welcome anyone with the heart of a warrior. When we step into battle, we fight for our own name, and the name of the Companions."* **Where do you stand on the war?** *"Too confusing for me. Empire, Nords, Talos. Who cares? Just tell me who needs bludgeoning."* **Can I join the Companions?** *"Kodlak is the one who figures out who can be a Companion. So talk to him. It's out of my hands."* Asking his opinion on being a werewolf after becoming [initiated](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silver_Hand) within the ranks of the Circle, he will answer the following:

**What's it like being a werewolf?** *"I like being stronger when I become the wolf. But [Kodlak](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md) says it is bad for the soul, and I trust him."* His greetings can be:

*"Skjor says I have the strength of Ysgramor, and my brother has his smarts."* *"Some people don't think I'm smart. Those people get my fist. But you, I like."* *"Getting tired of hanging around talking all day."* *"Thinking I need to train some more."* *"I'm here to help."* *"For Kodlak!"* **(During Glory of the Dead)** *"Vilkas says you're the new Kodlak. I don't know what that means, but you seem honorable, so that's good."* **(After Glory of the Dead)** *"Oh, you don't need to worry about me. I do what I'm told. Congratulations!"* **(After becoming the new Harbinger)**
## Quest-Related Events

### Take Up Arms
After bringing [Aela](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md) her shield, she states the following:

*"Here, let's have Farkas show you where you'll be resting your head."* [Skjor](Skyrim_Skjor.md) will call for Farkas, who appears rushing into the room. His dialogue depends on whether or not you met the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) at [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md).

**Skjor:** *"Farkas!"* **Farkas:** *"Did you call me?"* **Aela:** *"Of course we did, icebrain. Show this new blood where the rest of the whelps sleep."* **Farkas:** *"New blood? Oh, I remember you. Come on, follow me." OR "New blood? Oh, hello. I'm Farkas. Come on, follow me."* Farkas will then walk you to your quarters, all the while giving some background information:

*"Skjor and Aela like to tease me, but they're good people. They challenge us to be our best."* *"Nice to have a new face around. It gets boring here sometimes. I hope we keep you. This can be a rough life."* *"The quarters are up here. Just pick a bed and fall in it when you're tired. Tilma will keep the place clean. She always has."* Once arriving at the whelps' quarters:

*"All right, so here you are. Looks like the others are eager to meet you. Come to me or Aela if you're looking for work. Once you've made a bit of a name for yourself, Skjor and Vilkas might have things for you to do. Good luck. Welcome to the Companions."* Which he will follow up by saying:

*"By the way, if you're looking for something to do..."* This ends Take Up Arms, and Farkas will offer you one of his radiant quests.

### Proving Honor
Approaching Farkas inside Jorrvaskr will lead to him to informing you that Skjor was looking for you.

*"Skjor was looking for you, earlier."* **What does he want?** *"Don't know. He just said he needed to talk to you before you do anything else. I don't like making him angry, but there is some work for you if you want it."* Skjor assigns you a mission to retrieve a [fragment of Wuuthrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fragment_of_Wuuthrad) from [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md), with Farkas acting as your Shield-Brother and observer. He will explain this will be the final test for Companion membership. After the conversation, you will be directed to Farkas:

*"I hope you've readied yourself."* **You're going to be my Shield-Brother?** *"So I'm told. Let's see if you impress."* Farkas will become your follower, dismissing any of your previous followers. Speaking to him allows for additional dialogue:

**Who was this scholar?** *"A smart man came and told us about a blade piece. Skjor thinks you should find it, and I'm supposed to watch you."* **Why did Skjor call this my Trial?** *"I watch you to make sure you are honorable. If you are honorable and strong, then I can call you brother/sister."* **What is Wuuthrad?** *"Ysgramor was the hero who started the Companions. Wuuthrad was his weapon. He came from the ancient homeland and killed all the elves. But not all of them, because some of them are still here."* **I'll meet you at Dustman's Cairn** *"Don't delay, Shield-Brother/Sister."* Once entering Dustman's Cairn:

*"Looks like someone's been digging here, and recently. Tread lightly."* *"Be careful around the burial stones. I don't want to haul you back to Jorrvaskr on my back."* At a certain point, you will trap yourself behind a gate and trigger a scene:

*"Now look what you've gotten yourself into. No worries, just sit tight. I'll find a release."* *"What was that?"* At this point, five [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) members enter the room.

**Silver Hand Member:** *"Killing you will make for an excellent story."* **Farkas:** *"None of you will be alive to tell it."* Farkas will then transform into a werewolf, kill all the Silver Hand members, and open the gate. Once back in his human form he will speak to you once more:

*"I hope I didn't scare you."* After killing all Silver Hand members, opening the gate, and returning in human form. **What was that?** *"It's a blessing given to some of us. We can be like wild beasts. Fearsome."* **You're going to make me a werewolf?** *"Oh, no. Only the Circle have the beastblood. Prove your honor to be a Companion. "Eyes on the prey, not the horizon." We should keep moving. Still the draugr to worry about."* When speaking to Farkas after the encounter:

*"We should keep moving."* **The Companions are werewolves?** *"Not everyone, but all in the Circle are. It's a secret to everybody."* **Who are the Silver Hand?** *"Bad people who don't like werewolves. So they don't like us either."* After obtaining the Wuuthrad Fragment:

*"We need to get back to Jorrvaskr."* Once you have recovered the fragment of Wuuthrad from Dustman's Cairn and returned to Jorrvaskr, Vilkas will tell you to stand before the Circle for judgement. Harbinger [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md) will then start off:

**Kodlak:** *"Brothers and Sisters of the Circle, today we welcome a new soul into our mortal fold. This man/woman has endured, has challenged and has showed his/her valor. Who will speak for him/her?"* **Farkas:** *"I stand witness for the courage of the soul before us."* **Kodlak:** *"Would you raise your shield in his/her defense?"* **Farkas:** *"I would stand at his/her back, that the world might never overtake us."* **Kodlak:** *"And would you raise your sword in his/her honor?"* **Farkas:** *"It stands ready to meet the blood of his/her foes."* **Kodlak:** *"And would you raise a mug in his/her name?"* **Farkas:** *"I would lead the song of triumph as our mead hall reveled in his/her stories."* **Kodlak:** *"Then this judgment of this Circle is complete. His/her heart beats with fury and courage that have united the Companions since the days of the distant green summers. Let it beat with ours, so the mountains may echo and our enemies may tremble at the call."* **Everyone:** *"It shall be so."*

### Glory of the Dead
Starting off this quest, the remaining Circle members have a discussion in the [Underforge](Skyrim_The_Underforge.md) about Kodlak's final wishes. Farkas speaks only speaks once during this debate, adding that:

"Kodlak did not care for vengeance."The Circle eventually agrees to head to [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) to cure Kodlak's lycanthropy so he can ascend to [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). Arriving at the tomb and progressing through, Farkas will withdraw from the fight once reaching a spider den, stating the following:

*"I can't go any further, Shield-Brother/Sister."* **What's the matter?** *"Ever since Dustman's Cairn, the big crawly ones have been too much for me. Everyone has his weakness, and this one is mine. I'm not proud, but I will stay back with Vilkas. Give my regards to Ysgramor."* He then leaves you and Aela to finish the quest.

### Purity
After Glory of the Dead, you can speak to him again for more radiant quests. Eventually, he will voice his distain of being a werewolf, offering the quest [Purity](Skyrim_Purity.md).

**Is there any work to be done?** *"Of course, there is always work. My mind is someplace else, though."* **What troubles you?** *"I think that maybe Kodlak was right. As a werewolf, I can't be a good Nord. I want to be clean, like he was, and go to Sovngarde when I finally die."* **I will help you cure yourself.** *"I would be honored for you to accompany me, Harbinger."* **This is a personal matter, and I leave it to you.** *"Thank you, Harbinger."* **(Declining the quest)** *"Aela said we would need a Hagraven's head. I already have one of them, but she said it was a special kind. And that you knew where to get more. Let's go find one!"* **(If you don't have a Glenmoril Witch head.)** *"You still have one of the witch's heads. This is good. Let's go to the Tomb. We can do the cleansing there."* **(If you have a Glenmoril Witch head.)** After obtaining a Glenmoril Witch head, Farkas can be spoken to:

*"Did you take the head?"* **I have it.** *"This is good. Could you show the way to the tomb again?"* After clearing Ysgramor's Tomb and defeating Farkas' wolf spirit:

*"Is it over?"* **Are you ok?** **or** **Get up. Did it work?** *"It's like relaxing into a warm mug of spiced mead. I'm losing aches I didn't know I had. This is how a warrior should feel. Alive and aware. Not clouded with thoughts of the hunt. Thank you for your help. I'm going to stay in the tomb for a while. My shame kept me out before, but now I want to see it."*

But still, I'd like to see for myself."* **Let's go kill a dragon.** *"Well, that's one way to find out if they're real. I even know where we might find one. Lead on!"* After defeating the dragon:

*"Now that was a battle!"* **You fought well.** *"Thank you, Harbinger."*
**Next time, try harder.** *"I will train better for the future."* **Let's go.** *"You go if you want to. I've never seen bones this large, and want to make sure I remember them so I can tell the story right. Otherwise Vilkas will just say I was making it up."* *"We bring honor to the Companions."*

## Notes
- "Farkas" is the Hungarian word for wolf, as well as a given name or surname. Similarly, his brother's name "[Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md)" is the Lithuanian word for wolf.
- Farkas is in the faction [Guard Faction](Skyrim_Guard Faction_(makes_an_actor_a_guard).md). This makes him chase you on sight when you have a high bounty in any given [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md).
- Farkas's class is "[Trainer Heavy Armor Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Heavy Armor Master)", which results in mismatches between his skills and his default equipment; for example, while his default weapon is a two-handed one, he is more proficient with one-handed weapons. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, addresses this issue. The class has had its skills changed to a basic duplicate of the [Combat Warrior2H](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Warrior2H) class, with an additional point in the Heavy Armor skill.
- Farkas is the only male member of the Circle who does *not* wear the [Wolf Armor](Skyrim_Wolf_Armor.md) of the Companions. On occasion though, his armor will randomly change either after certain Companions quests or finding him as a random encounter in Skyrim. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, addresses this issue. His outfit was changed from Steel Armor to Wolf Armor.
- During the events of [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md), a [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) member will refer to Farkas' status as a member of the Circle by stating the following; "He wears that armor, he dies,", despite Farkas not wearing the Wolf Armor associated with the Circle.
- Farkas has unused dialogue, originally allowing for an option to ask him "What's it like being a werewolf?" he would reply with *"I think there are some things that... oh, wait. Are you trying to trick me? You are not a full-blood yet. We can't talk about that."*

## Bugs
- Farkas is initially armed with a Skyforge Steel Greatsword, but will lose it during [Proving Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proving_Honor#Bugs). See that quest's page for details and a solution. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes after [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md), Farkas will follow you and there is no speech option that lets you part ways with him.
- After finishing the Companions quest line, he and/or Vilkas may not have a dialogue option to get work from them, thus making the [Purity](Skyrim_Purity.md) quest impossible to obtain. - This occurs after taking only one or a few [Glenmoril Witches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Witch)' heads during the [Blood's Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood%27s_Honor) quest (or not keeping the heads). Fix: Acquire at least four heads total (one to give to quest-giver Kodlak Whitemane, and one each for Farkas and [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) later, and one to keep in inventory when talking to them). If Blood's Honor is already complete, you can return to [Glenmoril Coven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Coven) for more heads. With at least two heads in inventory, save just before speaking again to Farkas or Vilkas; he will take a head, but you need two still in inventory if you need to talk to the other as well to get around the same dialogue problem. This will make the Purity quest available, and the NPC(s) should give you radiant quests normally after it is complete. See [Skyrim:Purity#Bugs](Skyrim_Purity.md#Bugs) for more technical details.
- Sometimes Farkas follows you and initiates conversation repeatedly. If you are far enough in the Companions quest line, you can ask him to follow you and then tell him to part ways and he stops. - This is due to Farkas being in the faction [Guard Faction](Skyrim_Guard Faction_(makes_an_actor_a_guard).md), meaning he will attempt to initiate dialogue with you if you have a sufficiently high [bounty](Skyrim_Crime.md) like guards do, but unlike actual guards, he will not turn hostile after the conversation and thus will start a conversation again. Clearing your bounty should resolve the issue.
- Farkas is presumably supposed to be made non-essential after the events of [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md), like the other members of the circle, however he is left essential afterwards.
