- *Caster is invisible for 15 seconds. Activating an object or attacking will break the spell. Can breathe water for 15 seconds. Improved night vision for 15 seconds.*

Orum's Aquatic Escape is an adept level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes the caster to become [invisible](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) and gain [water breathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md) and [night vision](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md) for 15 seconds.

## Effects
- [Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md), for 15 secs
- [Water Breathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md), for 15 secs
- [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md), for 15 secs

## Perks
- [Illusion Dual Casting](Skyrim_Illusion_Dual_Casting.md), multiplies duration by 2.2 (rounded down)
