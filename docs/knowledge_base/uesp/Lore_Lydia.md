Lydia is a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) housecarl who resided in [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), originally serving under Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DA-1)</sup> She became known for her sworn service to carry the [Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md)'s burdens all across Skyrim.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Castles Di-3)</sup> In addition to a sword and shield,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Lydia was known to wield a restored ancient Nord battleaxe smithed at the [Skyforge](Lore_Skyforge.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Lydia was appointed as a housecarl to the Last Dragonborn after they became a [Thane](Lore_Thane.md) of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), as a reward for slaying the [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Mirmulnir](Lore_Mirmulnir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DA-1)</sup> Lydia explained that she was sworn to the Dragonborn and that she would guard the Dragonborn and all they owned with her life.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRLydia-6)</sup> Reports claim that Lydia accompanied the Dragonborn on many adventures.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Castles Di-3)</sup> Notably she accompanied them on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), where she met the [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) [Frea](Lore_Frea.md). Lydia admired how well Frea fought for a Skaal.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTOM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRLydia2-8)</sup>
