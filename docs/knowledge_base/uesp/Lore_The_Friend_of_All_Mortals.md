The Friend of All Mortals **by [the Resolute Templar of Ska'vyn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Resolute_Templar_of_Ska.27vyn)** *On Stendarr, the God of Mercy and Justice*
---
*"Come to me, [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md), for without you, I might be deaf to the manswarm*
*murmurings of thy people, and forgetting their need for comfort and wisdom, I*
*might indulge myself in vain scribblings."*

Call him Stendarr, call him [Stuhn](Lore_Stuhn.md), call him what you will, but the God of Mercy and Justice is the friend to all the mortals of the [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), whether they acknowledge him or not. Yea, even the heretic [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) may use his [magic](Lore_Magic.md) of defense and healing, even so the scaled folk of [Argonia](Lore_Argonia.md), for Stendarr in his benevolence draws no distinction between those who rightfully worship him and those who, in their ignorance and error, do not.

Join with me, then, in a prayer to Stendarr for guidance, that we may look with mercy upon our fellow mortals, that we may show the compassion to help them at need, and find the strength to eradicate abominations that threaten us all.
