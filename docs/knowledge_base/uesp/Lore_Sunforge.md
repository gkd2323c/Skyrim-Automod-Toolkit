[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Skaven marked on a map of Hammerfell Sunforge is a region of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOFirilanya-1)</sup> supposedly located in central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> bordered by [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) to the northwest and [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) to the northeast, along the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md), and containing the cities of [Ska'vyn](Lore_Ska%27vyn.md), [Verkarth](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md), and [Cliff Keep](Lore_Cliff_Keep.md). The [Irk](Lore_Irk_River.md) and [Tark Rivers](Lore_Tark_River.md) are found to the south.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map-3)</sup> Buildings here may have a layer of patina on top.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Patina-4)</sup>

In the countryside outside of Skaven is one of the [Halls of the Virtues of War](Lore_Halls_of_Virtue.md), where there are still people who follow the old ways and are believed by some to be able to form a [Shehai](Lore_Shehai.md) sword.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Nf RH-5)</sup> The [Ket Keptu](Lore_Keptu.md) were native to central Hammerfell,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CM:12-6)</sup> and were believed to have had cultural interchange with the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOKeptu Skull-7)</sup> The roving [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of the region bred the local [Painted Wolves](Lore_Wolf.md#Painted_Wolf) into sure-footed mounts to cross the terrain.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOPainted Wolf-8)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CSS-9)</sup> The Firedancers of central Hammerfell wear scandalously little clothing by local standards, which may be why they protect their modesty—and identities—by hiding their faces behind Flamebrow Fire Veils.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FFV-10)</sup> As a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) holiday, [Saturalia](Lore_Saturalia.md) is not celebrated in central Hammerfell.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-NLFI-11)</sup>
