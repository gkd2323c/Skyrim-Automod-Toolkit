[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-flora-Crimson_Nirnroot.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Crimson_Nirnroot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Crimson_Nirnroot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Crimson_Nirnroot.jpg) A crimson nirnroot plant **Crimson Nirnroot** is a special variant of [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) that can be found exclusively in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md). It has the same alchemical properties as normal nirnroot (other than details of the two damage effects). Similar to regular nirnroot, the crimson variety makes a humming sound that lets you know when a plant is nearby. It also regrows if you leave Blackreach for ten days.

Aside from the unique [jarrin root](Skyrim_Jarrin_Root.md), crimson nirnroot creates the largest magnitude [damage health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md) poisons in the game; see the [alchemy effects](Skyrim_Alchemy_Effects.md) page for full details. It also makes the largest magnitude [damage stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md) poisons in the game.

## Ingredients
Already-harvested samples of crimson nirnroot are not available. There are none sitting out on tables or shelves, and they cannot be bought from merchants (even with the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk) or found in random loot.

## Plants
44 crimson nirnroot plants are found inside [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md):

- [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory): 1 inside (in a pot), 1 to the southwest (near Dwarven Centurion Master).
- Following the river from north end: 2 before the fall (left and right), 2 down the fall (left and right), 1 under two pipes, 1 under big mushrooms, 1 near wooden bridge.
- [Silent City Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_City_Catacombs): 1 at the entrance.
- [Debate Hall](Skyrim_Debate_Hall.md): 1 at the entrance.
- [Hall of Rumination](Skyrim_Hall_of_Rumination.md): 1 outside top exit.
- [Silent Ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Ruin): 1 inside (in a pot), 1 behind.
- [Reeking Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reeking_Tower): 2 near the entrance, 1 outside top exit. 1 to the southeast (east of Falmer camp). 1 to the east (with Corundum Ore Vein).
- Great Lift at Mzinchaleft: 1 on the west side, 1 to the south (near patrolling Giant).
- Dwemer city: 1 to the south (at riverside).
- [Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md): 1 under northern bridge, 1 in southern stairway. From the lower end of the southern stairway, 1 to the west (between two falls) and 1 to the south (near Falmer camp).
- Following the river from Tower of Mzark to Derelict Pumphouse: 1 on the left (near waterfall), 1 on the right (in a pot at a mini shrine-like alcove), 1 on the left near waterfalls.
- [Derelict Pumphouse](Skyrim_Derelict_Pumphouse.md): 1 between two falls to the west, accessible with [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) from the top. 1 down the falls, 1 under another fall to the south, 1 inside (lower floor, through underwater gates with valve).
- Raldbthar Deep Market: 1 to the west (near Falmer camp).
- South of War Quarters (stairways): 1 at the lower end, 1 under outlet pipe in the middle, 1 at the peak (with two trolls).
- North of [War Quarters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Quarters): 1 on the wall crossing the road, 1 to the east of it (along with two dwarven buildings), 1 a bit north of it.
- Great Lift at Alftand: 1 right in front, 1 behind.
- Alftand Cathedral: 1 at start, 1 at end of the pipe under ballista.

## Notes
- Crimson nirnroot and regular nirnroot are not affected by the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) perk [Green Thumb](Skyrim_Green_Thumb.md), and therefore even with the perk, you still only collect a single sample from each plant.
- Combining Nirnroot and Crimson Nirnroot will unlock all ingredient effects for both plants.
