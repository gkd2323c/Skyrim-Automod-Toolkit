[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-item-Yisra%27s_Necklace.jpg/200px-SR-item-Yisra%27s_Necklace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Yisra%27s_Necklace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Yisra%27s_Necklace.jpg) Yisra's Necklace Yisra's Necklace is an [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md) found on the charred corpse of [its eponymous owner](Skyrim_Yisra.md), a former [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) apprentice. Her body can be found in a circle of scorched earth east of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), along the shore immediately south of [Yngvild](Skyrim_Yngvild.md). In appearance, the necklace is identical to a [silver necklace](Skyrim_Silver_Necklace.md), though it is worth ten less gold. It does not possess an enchantment.

## Notes
- This necklace was meant to play a part in an unfinished quest for the College of Winterhold, entitled *[The Missing Apprentices](Skyrim_The_Missing_Apprentices.md)*. In it, Phinis Gestor would have tasked you with finding four missing apprentices and bringing back an identifying item to prove their fate. If you brought him Yisra's necklace, he would have said *"Gods, this is hers, isn't it? She will certainly be missed."* Even though Gestor will talk about the apprentices, and their corpses and items can be found, bringing the items to him has no effect.
- Yisra's corpse is set to respawn meaning you can get infinite copies of Yisra's necklace. - Version 4.2.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) modifies that and prevents them from respawning.
