[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg) Servos Rendas **Servos Rendas**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md), can be found lying dead in [White Ridge Sanctum](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md). He is in a locked cage with his [journal](Skyrim_Servos%27_Journal.md) and some [notes](Skyrim_Spider_Experiment_Notes.md) along with the [Imbuing Chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) and a knapsack full of [albino spider pods](Skyrim_Albino_Spider_Pod.md). He wears [novice robes of destruction](Skyrim_Novice_Robes_of_Destruction.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c).

He and his sister, [Merilar](Skyrim_Merilar_Rendas.md), discovered how receptive [albino spiders](Skyrim_Albino_Spider.md) are when exposed to rubies. Unfortunately, Merilar's power and the fumes from their tests drove her insane, causing her to lock Servos in a cage to rot.
