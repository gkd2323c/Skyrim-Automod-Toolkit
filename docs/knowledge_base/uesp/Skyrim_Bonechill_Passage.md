## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-place-POIPine Forest15.jpg/200px-SR-place-POIPine Forest15.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POIPine Forest15.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POIPine Forest15.jpg) Ruined shack Following a dirt path south from the road between Falkreath and [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) east of [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md) will take you past an unmarked [ruined shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#Prospector.27s_Shack:_Bonechill_Ridge) that was attacked by the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) occupying [Ancient's Ascent](Skyrim_Ancient%27s_Ascent.md) to the south. The path curves east around an outcrop of rock before proceeding south to a large stone square arch over the steps leading up to the cave entrance. A [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) is just west of the base of the steps.

### Bonechill Passage
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bonechill_Passage.jpg) Interior Upon entering, you are in an ice tunnel heading south. To the left is a skeleton with a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) beside it and a pair of random [boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots) opposite. A leveled snow animal or creature (which may be a [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md), [ice wolf](Skyrim_Ice_Wolf.md), [ice wraith](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), or [snowy sabre cat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat)) guards the path ahead. Moving forward, to the right is a mammoth skull with a dead [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and a leveled [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) lying beside it. Farther along and also to the right is a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) next to another skeleton and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Mid Year, v6](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Mid Year,_v6)*.

The tunnel narrows and bends to the east, then opens into an unroofed section with a bridge across a chasm and a leveled snow animal or creature on the far side. To the left at the start of the bridge is a dead [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md). Under the bridge are two lootable skeletons and a random [potion of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina). Across the bridge, the tunnel rises quite sharply, turns twice, and leads to the exit near Ancient's Ascent.

## Notes
- Among other items described on the [dedicated page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Falkreath#Prospector.27s_Shack:_Bonechill_Ridge), the ruined shack contains a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill book *[Purloined Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purloined_Shadows)*.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Some users have experienced a crash to desktop after entering Bonechill Passage. This crash appears to have been fixed in an early patch, though there is no mention in any patch notes. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The issue can be worked around by playing in windowed mode while inside the cave.
