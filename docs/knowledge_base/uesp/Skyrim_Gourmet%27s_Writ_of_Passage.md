Gourmet's Writ of Passage
---
By order of his eminence, [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md).

The possessor of this Writ of Passage - known throughout the Empire as the celebrated chef and author the "Gourmet" - is granted full and unrestricted access to any areas, information, or supplies necessary to fulfill the duty of the Emperor's personal cook.
