## Gar
The **Gar** (or **Garpike**) is a type of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md) and [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md).

- The **Druadach Garpike** can be found in lakes around [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).
- The **Harbour Gar** and the **Heinous Gar** can be found in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).
- The **Old Man Gar** can be found in the lakes of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).
- The **Osheeja Gar** can be found in Black Marsh and are caught by the [Naka-Desh](Lore_Naka-Desh.md) tribe.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Glassfish
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-ingredient-Glassfish.png/200px-SR-ingredient-Glassfish.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Glassfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Glassfish.png) A glassfish The **Glassfish** is a type of fish that can be found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

Their natural habitats are deep subterranean waterways, glassfish can also be found in the open waters of warm, clear lakes when they swim above ground for spawning.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Glassfish.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Goby
The **Goby** is a type of fish.

- The **Briarthorn Goby** can be found in the foul waters of Arkthzand Caverns in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).
- The **Dragon Goby** can be found in the rivers of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- The **Eel-Goby** can be found in the foul waters of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md).
- The **Marbled Goby** can be found in the rivers of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md).
- The **Obsidian Goby** can be found in the foul waters of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).
- The **Pilgrim Goby** can be found in the rivers of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Grayling
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Arctic_Grayling.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Arctic_Grayling.png) An Arctic Grayling The **Grayling** is a type of long, tapered fish that are related to the salmon. They can be found in the rivers of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md).

- The **Arctic Grayling** can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) and in freezing waters in Skyrim. They are called the "Lady of the Ice" and are described as elegant fish by fishermen. They are known to be prey to Horkers.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Arctic_Grayling.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
