Dementia is a region of [the Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) located in the marshy southern lowlands of the main landmass. It represents the dark and violent side of Sheogorath, and is mostly made up of swamps and forests of dead trees. The Low Road and Pinnacle Road are the two main routes through the region. Two peninsulas can be found here: the Heretic's Horn and the Madgod's Boot. The marshes of Shallow Grave and the [Hill of Suicides](Lore_Hill_of_Suicides.md) are also important landmarks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SIDementia-2)</sup>

The villages of [Deepwallow](Lore_Deepwallow.md) and [Fellmoor](Lore_Fellmoor.md) offer relative safety from the monsters that roam the swamps. The ruins of [Vitharn](Lore_Vitharn.md) are a place of ill repute, once populated by souls cursed by Sheogorath. The Halls of [Pinnacle Rock](Lore_Pinnacle_Rock.md) contain the Wellspring of the [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md). The lawbreakers of the region are imprisoned in the root tunnels of Corpserot Passage. [Knifepoint Hollow](Lore_Knifepoint_Hollow.md) and [Xedilian](Lore_Xedilian.md) are also located here.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SIDementia-2)</sup> The Chateau of the Ravenous Rodent was Sheogorath's "family estate" and is located in the darkest part of Dementia.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONValaste-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSheogorath-4)</sup>

The [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) owns a collection of items from Dementia. The [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md), using these items, were able to deduce that the bail on Saint and the Seducer necklace was from Dementia, specifically Vitharn.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONBail Anti-5)</sup>
