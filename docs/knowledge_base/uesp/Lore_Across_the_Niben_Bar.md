Across the Niben Bar *(Niben River Song)* *
---
From [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md)
The [Niben's](Lore_Niben_River.md) back is wide
Good ships along [Leyawiin-quay](Lore_Leyawiin.md)
Await the morning tide.

In [Aleswell](Lore_Aleswell.md) we took on ore
At [Weye](Lore_Weye.md), Colovian red
Timber we shipped in [Bravil-port](Lore_Bravil.md)
Then sailed for [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) Head.

CHORUS
One, two! One, two! Cast far, cast true!
Hey leadsman, call your mark!
Shoal-water lurks in foggy murk
Across the Niben Bar!

In [Torval](Lore_Torval.md) we bought [sugar cane](Lore_Moon_Sugar_Cane.md)
In [Southpoint](Lore_Southpoint.md) furs and dyes
Then sold it all at [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md)
And drank in [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md)!

The [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) has silk and wine
We turn for home again
In [Leyawiin-town](Lore_Leyawiin.md) my sweetheart pines
To see her sailing-man!

CHORUS
One, two! One, two! Cast far, cast true!
Hey leadsman, call your mark!
For [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md) dwell beneath the swells
Across the Niben Bar!

CHORUS
One, two! One, two! Cast far, cast true!
Hey leadsman, call your mark!
For sailors roam upon the foam
Across the Niben Bar!
