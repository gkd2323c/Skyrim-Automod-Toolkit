Conversation with Adibazzi, Trader **by [Adibazzi](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Adibazzi)** *The frustrations of a Khajiiti merchant when dealing with Bosmer*
---
[![ON-concept-Grahtwood tree.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/0b/ON-concept-Grahtwood_tree.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Grahtwood_tree.png)
- "This one has much patience, but it is stretched thinner than a dancing-cat's veil here. I have come to trade, and I have fine goods for reasonable prices, but these [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) act as if it's all a game. Well, in truth, trading is a game, but they are playing a different one than Adibazzi is and they won't share the rules.

- We should haggle, bargain, argue over quality and cost until we come to a deal. Instead, they want to ask riddles or want to have a hunting contest. One asked that we should each guess the number of leaves on a giant tree and count them, and whoever was closest would take all of the other's goods. Nonsense and [sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md) -dreams! [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) does not have time for this!"
