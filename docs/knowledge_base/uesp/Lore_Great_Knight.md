Lord **Magnessen**<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFpreview-1)</sup> was **Great Knight of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md)** in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), serving as their leader.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-2)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFLady-3)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFC-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFpreview-1)</sup>

## Biography

### Early History
Magnessen and his sister, [Brisienna](Lore_Brisienna.md), were born as nobles in the secret society of families comprising the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFGen Dia-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARManual-6)</sup> Members of the Blades are trained generation after generation to be the finest warriors of Tamriel; Magnessen and his sister inferably underwent such training.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFGen Dia-5)</sup> As is customary in the Blades, Magnessen received a scar over his right eyebrow as a proclamation of his alignment.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Scar-7)</sup>

### Recreating the Numidium
Circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Blades of Iliac Bay were finishing their recreation of the [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) (a great [golem](Lore_Golem.md) once used by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) to build his Empire four hundred years ago, which was destroyed shortly after Tiber's conquests were done) after centuries of work.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFC-4)</sup>

### Fate
The Great Knight's fate following the [Miracle of Peace](Lore_Miracle_of_Peace.md) remains unknown.

## Notes
- His full title is the Great Knight of the Blades of His Imperial Majesty.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFpreview-1)</sup>
- A cut letter from *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* would have had the Great Knight send a missive to the [player](Lore_Agent.md) when some time after they obtain the [Totem of Tiber Septim](Lore_Totem_of_Tiber_Septim.md), prompting them to deliver the Totem to him at [Castle Llugwych](Lore_Castle_Llugwych.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFpreview-1)</sup>
