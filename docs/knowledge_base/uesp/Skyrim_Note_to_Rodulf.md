Note to Rodulf **by [Hajvarr Iron-Hand](Skyrim_Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.md)** *A note warning a bandit about messing with his blind uncle*
---
Rodulf,

Your little stunts try my patience. I know my [uncle](Skyrim_Ulfr_the_Blind.md) has issues, but he *is* our watchman and you *will* respect him. No more sneaking in and out. No more games with his [ledger](Skyrim_Ulfr%27s_Book.md), or nails on his chair. One more 'joke' and you'll see how funny a day in the cage can be.

[Hajvarr](Skyrim_Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.md)
