[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-place-Winterhold_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Winterhold_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold_02.jpg) Winterhold and the College standing on what remains of Hsaarik Head Hsaarik Head is a headland located at the extreme northern tip of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s [Broken Cape](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Broken_Cape), where the city of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) is built upon.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRMap-1)</sup> According to tradition, it is where the last fleet from [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), under the command of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md), made landfall in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Some believed that the [resting place](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yngol_Barrow) of [Yngol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yngol), the son of Ysgramor, was located far below the rocky face of Hsaarik Head.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-YATSG-4)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Yngol)</sup>

The headland was once much larger and more populated until the [Great Collapse](Lore_Great_Collapse.md) of [4E 122](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_122), when large portions of it fell into the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), taking entire city districts of Winterhold with it.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBCodex SRMap-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Kraldar-6)</sup> The island that the Skytemple Ruins is located on in the Fourth Era was once contiguous with the rest of Hsaarik Head,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBCodex SRMap-5)</sup> before the Collapse tore down the land connecting it and sundered it from the mainland.
