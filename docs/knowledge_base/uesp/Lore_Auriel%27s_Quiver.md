# Auriel's Quiver

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Quiver.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Quiver.png) Auriel's Quiver Auriel's Quiver is a mysterious ivory case, embossed with gold. It may be associated with the elven god [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md) and his legendary [bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) and [shield](Lore_Auriel%27s_Shield.md).

At some point, the quiver was given "scholarly markings" to identify it. It was being circulated throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Nothing else is known about the quiver.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
