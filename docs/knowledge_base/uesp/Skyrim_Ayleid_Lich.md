[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg) [Norion the Undying](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Norion_the_Undying), an enemy Ayleid Lich [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-creature-Ayleid_Lich.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ayleid_Lich.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ayleid_Lich.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ayleid_Lich.jpg) A Conjured Ayleid Lich **Ayleid Liches** are enemies added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) Creation. They are undead magic users with similar stats and behaviors to [Dragon Priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), wielding destruction staves and using both ranged magic and summoned minions to attack as they float above the ground.

The only Ayleid Lich you can encounter in the flesh is [Norion the Undying](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Norion_the_Undying), who can be found in [Rielle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle) and must be fought during the related quest. Once you have completed the questline of the Creation, you can create a [spell tome](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Ayleid_Lich) at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to summon a spectral Ayleid Lich to fight for you.

## Related Quests
- **[The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause_(quest).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>: Investigate the activities of the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) cult.

## Notes
- Summoned Liches are spectral and translucent, giving them a very different appearance from Norion the Undying. They do not possess [Staves](Skyrim_Staves.md), instead using both hands for spells.
- Summoned Liches can cast very powerful [Reanimation spells](Skyrim_Reanimate_(spells).md) up to [Dread Zombie](Skyrim_Reanimate_(spells).md#Dread_Zombie), meaning that [Draugr Deathlords](Skyrim_Draugr.md#Draugr_Deathlord) can be recruited by a Lich during combat.
