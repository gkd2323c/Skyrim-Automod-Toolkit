## Interactions With Other Species
[Men](Lore_Man.md) and [Elves](Lore_Elf.md) hang up constructed bird houses for various species of birds to live in. These appear both on posts as well as within trees themselves, and encourage local populations of birds to cohabit within towns, villages, and cities. Further, bird baths and feeders are also made and used as accommodations.
