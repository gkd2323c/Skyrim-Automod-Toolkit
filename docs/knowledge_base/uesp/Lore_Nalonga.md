# Nalonga

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Nalonga [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg/200px-RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) Map of Nalonga by Maiko K'Elmar Nalonga is a large island in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), south of the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls), that is possibly considered a part of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago. A rather large bay cuts into the southern coast. The similarly sized island of [Ravan](Lore_Ravan.md) neighbors it to the southeast, with the smaller [Asil Yelir](Lore_Asil_Yelir.md) between them. [Siuol Yelir](Lore_Siuol_Yelir.md) is just south of Nalonga.

In the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), there was a settlement named Zanzar on the northwest side of the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
