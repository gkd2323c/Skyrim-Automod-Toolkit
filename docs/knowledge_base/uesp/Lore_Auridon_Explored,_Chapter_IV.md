[Auridon Explored](Lore_Auridon_Explored.md), Chapter IV **by [Fenlil the Wayfarer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fenlil_the_Wayfarer)** *A fragmented travel guidebook covering the isle of Auridon*
---
This [island](Lore_Buraniim_Isle.md) is fascinating. It was clearly once a hub for the [ancient](Lore_Ancients.md) [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), perhaps part of a larger structure that extended out into the sea. Now it's used most often as a retreat from the city life in nearby [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md).

I've found some evidence here that the resurgent [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) cults, the [Worm cultists](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md), are making preparations to construct some kind of dolmen here. I'll have to warn the local authorities in Skywatch.
