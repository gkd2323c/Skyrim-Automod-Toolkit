[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/ON-load-Deshaan.jpg/200px-ON-load-Deshaan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Deshaan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Deshaan.jpg) Lake Amaya Lake Amaya (also called **Lake Thir**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CC-1)</sup> is a large lake in the center of the [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) region of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It surrounds the city of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) and meets the docks of [Shad Astula](Lore_Shad_Astula.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> It is fed by the [Thir River](Lore_Thir_River.md), a large waterway that runs through the province.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DSH-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MWMap-4)</sup> Local [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) tribes use the waters of the lake for primitive irrigation, allowing the fertile black soil of the Lake Amaya basin to sustain both plants and animals.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTMorrowind-5)</sup>
