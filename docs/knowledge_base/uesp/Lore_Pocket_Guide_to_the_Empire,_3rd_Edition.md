# Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition

Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition **by the [Imperial Geographical Society](Lore_Imperial_Geographical_Society.md)** *An illustrated guidebook detailing the provinces of the Empire, circa 3E 432*
---
**The Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition** was a paper bound booklet included with the Collectors Edition of *[TES IV: Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. The timeframe of the guide is contemporary to the game, which itself is set during the rule of [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the last year of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

**You can read about the Guide [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire:_3rd_Edition).**

The following pages are transcripts of the guide. Formatting and capitalization are done according to the American printed version that came with the game.
