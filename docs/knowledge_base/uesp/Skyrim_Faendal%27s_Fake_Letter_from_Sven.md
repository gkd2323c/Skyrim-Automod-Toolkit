Faendal's Fake Letter from Sven **by [Faendal](Skyrim_Faendal.md)** *A forged note meant to make Sven look unfavorable to Camilla*

My Dearest [Camilla](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md),

I yearn to have you as my own,
Washing my linens,
And my fine blond hair,
To cook my dinner from my stove,
And tend to my house while I wander.

Yours Truly,
[Sven](Skyrim_Sven.md)
