*A party crasher! Wicked little party crasher! Did you think we would not notice your wicked intrusion into our new banquet hall?*
—A Fire Daedra<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Fire Daedra are a variety of horned humanoid [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md), considered among the most dangerous creatures in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFD-2)</sup> They are intelligent and possess some spell casting abilities, they can only be harmed by [mithril](Lore_Mithril.md) or better weapons and are greatly resistant to fire-based spells.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They are described as hot-blooded, fast, tricky thinkers. They are known to be impulsive, and smart enough to fall for subtle scams.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> They are speakers of both the Daedric [language](Lore_Linguistics.md) and [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md), as such they are sometimes capable of being negotiated with.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDaedric-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup>

They apparently have an enmity for [Frost Daedra](Lore_Frost_Daedra.md), though they are able to put this aside when serving mutual masters. Fire Daedra are considered to take things less seriously than Frost Daedra, who are said to not "understand the pleasures of the dance".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFrost-6)</sup> They seem to be most commonly used by their masters, the [Daedra Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), as agents of destruction in its least subtle form.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-7)</sup> Many Fire Daedra were known to serve as retainers of [Faydra Shardai](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md)'s [clan](Lore_Shardai_Clan.md), in service to [Clan Dagon](Lore_Clan_Dagon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup>

## History
The Fire Daedra of Shardai Clan served alongside the [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai%27s_Clan.md) and [Dremora Clan](Lore_Dremora_Clan.md) during [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s occupation of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md). On Faydra's orders, the Fire Daedra helped to invade the [Shade Perilous](Lore_Shade_Perilous.md) along with their hated enemies, the Frost Daedra. To prevent infighting, Faydra made a pact with [Moath](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md), forcing them to work together.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFrost-6)</sup> This relationship did not hold, as an [apprentice](Lore_Hero.md) from the Battlespire, following the retreating Daedric armies, brought the Elemental Daedra into conflict over control of the realm.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSL4-8)</sup>

As a result of the apprentice's actions, the invasion of Shade Perilous failed, with the Frost Daedra receiving the brunt of the blame. As a result the Fire Daedra received greater standing with Mehrunes Dagon, and were given the privilege of serving as the Greater Hounds in the [Great Hunt](Lore_Great_Hunt.md) hosted at the [Chimera of Desolation](Lore_Chimera_of_Desolation.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFire-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFrost-6)</sup> Though they failed to stop the apprentice there as well.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BSL5-9)</sup>

## Notes
- Fire Daedra exist in *Daggerfall* alongside [Fire Atronachs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fire_Atronach), which are typically [Golems](Lore_Golem.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-HFAQUEST-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AHing-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ATRMAN-12)</sup> but some quests treat them as Daedra comparable to the [Flame Atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) present in later games.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ATRQUEST-13)</sup> The Daedric Flame Atronachs have also sometimes been referred to as Fire Daedra.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-14)</sup>
