[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-npc-Rhiada.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rhiada.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rhiada.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rhiada.jpg) Rhiada **Rhiada** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and a servant of the Silver-Blood family at the [Treasury House](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

She is [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md)' wife, and is with child. Her connections to the Silver-Blood family provide ample opportunity for Eltrys to investigate the [recent killings](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md).

She wears a set of [tavern clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tavern_Clothes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000d1921). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Eltrys%27s_Room) to Eltrys' room as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"I'm just a servant. Don't pay me any mind."* *"Working in the Treasury House isn't so bad. I earn good money."* *"[Ildene](Skyrim_Nana_Ildene.md) has been working in the Treasury House since before I was born."* *"Sometimes I hear [Yngvar](Skyrim_Yngvar_the_Singer.md) singing in his room. He's actually quite good."* If spoken to:

**Who's in charge here?** *"You want [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md). He handles all the family business. If you're here to talk politics, though, you'd want his brother [Thongvor](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md). He spends most of his time in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md)."* **I want to know about the Silver-Bloods.** *"It's an old family. Very well-respected throughout the Reach. The Silver-Bloods run nearly all the lands in this Hold, as well as the [local inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md). And of course, they own [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), the finest prison and source of silver in Skyrim."* **What kind of patrons do you have?** *"For one, the landowners of the Reach. It goes without saying that they rely heavily on us for our silver and support. All the miners, farmers, and laborers in the Reach get their daily wages through us. [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md) handles that part of our business. He has a way with the workers."* Ending conversation will have her say either:

*"I have records I need to tend to."* *"What? Oh. Bye."*

**Betrid:** *"Rhiada, did you clean the tables today? They're filthy!"* **Rhiada:** *"I'm sorry, Betrid. I'll clean them right away."* **Betrid:** *"No, don't bother. You're useless, as always. You were one of [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella)'s faithful?"* **Betrid:** *"I misspoke. Never mind what I said. Now get back to work."*

## Notes
- Eltrys never refers to Rhiada by name, he just vaguely refers to his "wife". Rhiada herself will also never mention him directly, she only acknowledges their marriage in a scene between her and Thonar. - This does not change even after her husband's death due to The Forsworn Conspiracy, no new dialogue will be added for her about the event.
- Rhiada carries a key to Eltrys' room in [The Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md), and she's also part of the respective factions. Seeing as Eltrys' room holds two haystacks to sleep on, it seems likely that Rhiada was once meant to sleep with him. In the final game, she has her own bed in the Treasury House where she sleeps alone.
- If [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md), [Frabbi](Skyrim_Frabbi.md), [Hreinn](Skyrim_Hreinn.md), and [Hroki](Skyrim_Hroki.md) all die, Rhiada will technically be the one to take over the Silver-Blood Inn, though she will remain in the Treasury House.
- It's possible that Rhiada was meant to play a role in the [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) **MS01C**, as one of the only remnants of said quest is an empty barrel known as "Rhiada's Wardrobe" with the internal name `MS01CRhiadas Wardrobe`.
