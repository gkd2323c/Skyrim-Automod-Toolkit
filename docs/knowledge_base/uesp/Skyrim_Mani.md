[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Mani.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mani.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mani.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mani.jpg) Mani **Mani** was a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) whose family recently took over the [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md) after moving from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Unhappy with life in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), he was [planning](Skyrim_Mani%27s_Letter.md) to return to Hammerfell once his father [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md) returned from town. Unfortunately, before that could happen [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) lurking within [Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss_(place).md) finished tunneling into the lighthouse cellar and attacked the family. His mother [Ramati](Skyrim_Ramati.md) was killed instantly and they dragged him and his sister [Sudi](Skyrim_Sudi.md) into the Abyss before killing him. His body can be found near the Abyss entrance, impaled by a [Falmer sword](Skyrim_Falmer_Sword.md).

Mani wore a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0003452e) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0003452f). He carried an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md).
