[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-npc-Hroki.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hroki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hroki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hroki.jpg) Hroki **Hroki** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) that works in the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She is the daughter of [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md) and [Frabbi](Skyrim_Frabbi.md) and the sister of [Hreinn](Skyrim_Hreinn.md).

Hroki wakes up at 8am and immediately starts doing her chores. She works for an hour before heading out to the marketplace where she will browse for 11 hours. After this, she heads back to the inn and resumes her work until he she goes to bed at midnight.

Hroki wears a set of [tavern clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tavern_Clothes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000d1921). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common) as well as an additional 50 gold.

## Dialogue
When inside the inn, Hroki might greet you by saying:

*"I'm just here to clean up."* *"Don't mind the yelling. My parents are always going at each other."* (If neither of her parents are dead.) *"My father is more generous than he lets on. Gives me plenty of spending money."* (If neither of her parents are dead.)
## Conversations
She can sometimes be found arguing with [Hogni Red-Arm](Skyrim_Hogni_Red-Arm.md) in the marketplace:

**Hogni**: *"You there, pretty lady. You need some fresh meat for your family? Just look at the blood coming from this cut...."*
**Hroki**: *"That's disgusting, get that rotting carcass away from me!"*
**Hogni**: *"What's wrong? You have to look at the blood closely to see the quality of the meat. How else will you know it's fresh?"*
**Hroki**: *"I don't need to know if it's fresh from this distance."* **Hroki**: *"Ugh. You're the most disgusting merchant I have ever seen. The guards should take you away."*
**Hogni**: *"The only people I offend are cheats who don't pay me enough gold coin, or idiots who don't know good meat when I throw it at their faces."*
**Hroki**: *"If you ever think of throwing meat at me, I will have you dragged off to Cidhna Mine so fast the guards will yank those filthy arms of yours off."*
**Hogni**: *"What do I do to deserve such hatred? You give people good food and good prices, and all they do is insult you."* She may also talk with [Kerah](Skyrim_Kerah.md) at her stall about special jewelry:

**Hroki**: *"Kerah, do you have anything... exotic? Something no other woman in Markarth would be wearing."*
**Kerah**: *"I do have one set of bracelets in a style that is popular with the nobility in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md)."*
**Hroki**: *"Sentinel? That's in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), correct? And you say these bracelets are only worn by the nobility?"*
**Kerah**: *"They are popular with the nobility there, yes. This particular bracelet is a copy of one worn by a Crown Princess."*
**Hroki**: *"Hmm. Maybe in a few seasons I could afford such a treasure."* **Kerah**: *"Care to try out a new ring, or perhaps my daughter could make you a necklace..."*
**Hroki**: *"Oh, that sounds lovely."*
## Notes
- Hroki is also the name of a [Whiterun guard](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md) who was taken away by the dragon, [Mirmulnir](Skyrim_Mirmulnir.md).
- The scenes she has with Hogni and Kerah in the marketplace all internally refer to [Betrid Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Betrid_Silver-Blood.md) instead, suggesting they were originally written for her.

## Bugs
- Hroki is the only adult in the game that is assigned the "child" class, though it is more of an oddity than anything else since it doesn't actually make her a child or invulnerable as they are. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. Hroki is now part of the "citizen" class.
