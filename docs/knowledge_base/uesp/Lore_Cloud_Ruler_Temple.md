[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/OBR-wallpaper-steam-Cloud_Ruler_Temple.jpg/200px-OBR-wallpaper-steam-Cloud_Ruler_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-wallpaper-steam-Cloud_Ruler_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-wallpaper-steam-Cloud_Ruler_Temple.jpg) Cloud Ruler Temple, [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Cloud Ruler Temple was an ancient stronghold of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) located high in the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) near [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md). It is one of the most dramatic remaining examples of Akaviri architecture.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cyrus-1)</sup>

Built by [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) at the founding of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md), it served as their headquarters. Even as they became known as the Blades, the Emperor's agents continued to occupy Cloud Ruler Temple until the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jauffre-2)</sup> Legend has it the swords of those slain protecting the [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md) were hung within the Great Hall of the temple.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-t Ra Fot B-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Baurus-4)</sup> The archives of the temple were believed to have been the primary repository of the oldest Blades lore.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TDE-5)</sup>

The temple was almost lost in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). A [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md) was opened above the stronghold and the entire fortress was lifted out of the ground, to be transported to Bal's realm of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). The Planemeld was ended by a [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) invasion force, who entered Coldharbour and successfully destroyed the Planar Vortex with the aid of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Following the assassination of [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) and his heirs in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), his bastard son [Martin](Lore_Martin_Septim.md) was brought to Cloud Ruler Temple by the Blades for protection against the forces of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). While at the temple, Martin and the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) worked towards retrieving the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md) from the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md), eventually opening a portal to [Gaiar Alata](Lore_Gaiar_Alata.md) in the Great Hall.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Following Martin's death at the conclusion of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), the Blades fortified themselves deep within Cloud Ruler Temple, waiting for a new Dragonborn to emerge when they would be called upon again.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-t Ra Fot B-3)</sup>

The [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) laid siege to Cloud Ruler Temple during the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). It was considered a prime target due to the Blades having led several sabotage missions in the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), such as the Falinesti Incident.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TDE-5)</sup> Its archives were mostly destroyed during the siege, though a lone agent, Acilius Bolar, survived the attack and fled to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BW-8)</sup>

## Notes
- Cloud Ruler Temple is just one of several strongholds built by the Akaviri Dragonguard. Others include [Wind Scour Temple](Lore_Wind_Scour_Temple.md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and [Storm Talon Temple](Lore_Storm_Talon_Temple.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).
