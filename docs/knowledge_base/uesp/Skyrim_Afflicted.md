[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Afflicted.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Afflicted.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Afflicted.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Afflicted.jpg) Afflicted The **Afflicted** are a collection of Bretons 'blessed' by [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) with a wasting disease. For whatever reason the Daedric Prince chose to then isolate them in [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md), where you are sent during the Daedric quest [The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure). They have unusually red skin and the ability to [vomit poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vile_Vapor) at close range. When the quest is completed, various **Afflicted Remnants** may be found roaming Skyrim and they will attack you on sight. The Afflicted are weak adversaries at most levels, but can overwhelm in groups of casters.

## Related Quests
- **[The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure)**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor) for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

## Notes
- Afflicted inside Bthardamz cannot respawn, since the interior of the ruin is set not to respawn. The ones outside, however, can respawn if you leave the dungeon long enough during and after The Only Cure. - Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) prevents the ones outside the dungeon from respawning.

## Bugs
