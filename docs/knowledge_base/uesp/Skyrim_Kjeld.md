[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-npc-Kjeld.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kjeld.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjeld.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjeld.jpg) Kjeld **Kjeld** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and an innkeeper living in [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md). He runs the local [Braidwood Inn](Skyrim_Braidwood_Inn.md) with his wife [Iddra](Skyrim_Iddra.md). Normally he does not provide any services, but if Iddra dies, he will take over renting out the inn's rooms. Kjeld also spends time working in [Steamscorch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine) and at the town campfire with the other villagers. He may [purchase](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) any [malachite ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malachite_Ore) you have, which he suggests mining from Steamscorch Mine. His son, [Kjeld the Younger](Skyrim_Kjeld_the_Younger.md), also lives in Kynesgrove and mentions that his father runs the town. Kjeld implies that he's seeing other women several times.

He sleeps from midnight to 2am, then heads to Steamscorch Mine to work for 4 hours before taking a break, having a one-hour breakfast at 6am. He goes to work again between 7am to 10am. After this, his workday ends and for the rest of the day he can be found hanging around near the town campfire or Braidwood Inn. At 6pm he has a one-hour dinner.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [upper-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Rich).

## Dialogue
He may greet you with one of the following:

- *"Welcome to Kynesgrove. If you want to do business here, you'll have to deal with me."*

- *"If it's malachite you're after, you've come to the right place. I'll pay for any you dig up out of Steamscorch Mine."*

He may also tell you about himself and his family.

- *"Kjeld speaks for Kynesgrove. Anyone who says otherwise is aching for a fight."*

- *"[Roggi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roggi_Knot-Beard) and I do the work around here. My wife and brats just sit around on their brains wasting time."*

- *"My wife is a good woman, but every man needs a little variety, you know?"*

If you ask him to tell you about Kynesgrove, he will disparage the locals.

- **What can you tell me about Kynesgrove?**
- *"A bunch of lazy rabble trying to make a living. Can't get better workers in because everyone's afraid of the tomb up above the mine. Dead dragons... it hasn't moved since Ysgramor and it's not going to start now. Superstitious milk drinkers."*

If you have killed [Sahloknir](Skyrim_Sahloknir.md) as part of the quest [A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md), Kjeld's answer will be different:

- **What can you tell me about Kynesgrove?**
- *"So it seems the milk drinkers were right. That dragon was nearly the death of us. All we can do now is rebuild."*

## Notes
- Kjeld is marked as a foe to [Tormir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tormir), a miner from [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md), in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Creation_Kit). Tormir is a single mother to her daughter, [Hrefna](Skyrim_Hrefna.md), and while Tormir speaks bad of Hrefna's father, he's never named. It's possible that the relationship marker, coupled with Tormir's dialogue and Kjeld's own implications that he cheats on his wife, are leftovers of what once was a more straightforward plot point.
- If Iddra dies, Kjeld takes over as publican of the inn. However, if both innkeepers are dead, Kjeld the Younger won't take over.

## Bugs
