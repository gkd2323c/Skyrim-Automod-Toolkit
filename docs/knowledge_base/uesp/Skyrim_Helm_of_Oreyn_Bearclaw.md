Improving the Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw requires two [Bone Meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Notes
- The Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw)* and *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.md).
- Although the Arch Necromancer refers to the Helm as "clearly" a bear skull in the [note](Skyrim_Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw_(book).md) found on the same table, it more closely resembles a real-life Smilodon skull with the elongated canine teeth and short feline snout. This would make it technically a [Sabre Cat](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) skull.
