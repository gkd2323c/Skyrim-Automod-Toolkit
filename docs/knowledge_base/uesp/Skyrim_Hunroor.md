[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-npc-Hunroor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hunroor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunroor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hunroor.jpg) Hunroor relaxing in the Hall of Valor **Hunroor** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He and four companions embarked on a quest to find [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). However, Hunroor, [Erlendr](Skyrim_Erlendr.md), and [Nikulas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nikulas) were betrayed by the wizard [Grimkell](Lore_Grimkell.md), who turned them into stone pillars at Brodir Grove on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were avenged by [Ulfgar the Unending](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfgar_the_Unending), who killed the wizard. All of the friends eventually did find their way to Sovngarde. He is one of the few named warriors in the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md). He wanders around the west wing of the hall.
