A conversation with Orbath gro-Agdurz **by [Orbath gro-Agdurz](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Orbath_gro-Agdurz)** *An Orc suspects necromancy occurring on Betnikh*
---
[![ON-concept-Skeleton.png](https://images.uesp.net/4/48/ON-concept-Skeleton.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Skeleton.png)
- "I don't care who laughs at me; I know what I saw, and there isn't a doubt in my mind. There's [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) happening on [the island](Lore_Betnikh.md), plain and simple, but no one will take me seriously. Just because I mistook the Wayshrine for a portal to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) during that huge storm doesn't mean I don't know what I'm talking about. And, look, anyone seeing that rock on the cliffs against the sun like that would've run back to town screaming '[Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md)!' I mean, it looked exactly like a monster!

- I know for a fact that I saw [bones walking](Lore_Skeleton.md); I heard that horrible chanting coming from the cave and saw the moonlight on a bare, chattering skull! There's no way I'm wrong this time, 'excitable' imagination or no."
