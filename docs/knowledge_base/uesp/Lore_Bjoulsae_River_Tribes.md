# Bjoulsae River Tribes

This article is about the Breton tribes. For the Redguard tribe, see [Silverhoof Horsemen](Lore_Silverhoof_Horsemen.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg/150px-LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg) River Horse Bretons The Bjoulsae River Tribes<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONABS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup> (also known as the **Horse-People of the Bjoulsae River**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1EIL-3)</sup> the **River Horse Bretons**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-4)</sup> or simply the **horsemen**)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TM-5)</sup> are the indigenous groups of tribal [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) that live in the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) region. While information on them is scarce, the horsemen are a nomadic, primitive folk that wear fur armor, bone necklaces,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1EIL-3)</sup> and turbans.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TM-5)</sup> As the name suggests, they are known for their [horses](Lore_Horse.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup> Their battle strategies and tactics are described as straightforward, which is the "way of the horse men", in the book *[The Mirror](Lore_The_Mirror.md)*.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TM-5)</sup>

The Bjoulsae River Tribes participated in the [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONABS-1)</sup> Their greatest legacy is the modern-day Kingdom of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md), which was founded in [1E 983](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_983) after they helped suppress the Orcs, which opened trade through the Bjoulsae River region. According to the Decretal of Fiefdoms by Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md), the city's earliest leader was Barron Guy Marde of ye Rivver Banke, an ancestor of the [ruling house](Lore_House_Guimard.md) of Evermore during the mid-Second Era.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-4)</sup> One renowned horseman of the Bjoulsae River Tribes was Mindothrax, a master of defensive combat, who fought in many battles until he perished at the battle of [Ain-Kolur](Lore_Ain-Kolur.md) against a rival River Horse Breton tribe led by Chief Iymbez.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TM-5)</sup> They are known to cremate their dead.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CP1-6)</sup>

## Notes
- Another group known as "[Horsemen](Lore_Silverhoof_Horsemen.md)" are tribal [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) that live in [Silverhoof Vale](Lore_Silverhoof_Vale.md), southwest of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md). They are an offshoot of the [Yokudans](Lore_Yokudans.md) that evidently arrived in northern High Rock almost three centuries prior to the main exodus from Yokuda and its arrival in Hammerfell.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-THFo S-7)</sup> There is no known connection between the Horsemen of Silverhoof and the Horsemen of the Bjoulsae River.
