## Water Breathing
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Waterbreath.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Waterbreath.png) Waterbreathing (Oblivion) [Water Breathing](Lore_Water_Breathing.md) (also spelled as **Waterbreathing**) is an [alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) spell that lets the user temporarily breathe underwater. As the book, *[Breathing Water](Lore_Breathing_Water.md)* describes it, [magicka](Lore_Magicka.md) flows through the user's entire body and is fueled by their chaotic and reversed perception of the universe around them, which is typically how the School of Alteration functions, as explained by Seryne Relas, the Third Era master of alteration in the book. The spell can be cast not only on the wielder, but onto a specific or group of targets.

Those who cannot naturally use it or use any kind of spells can attain water breathing via [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md). Certain ingredients create a potion of water breathing when mixed such as [pearl](Lore_Pearl.md), [hackle-lo leaf](Lore_Hackle-Lo_Leaf.md), [luminous russula](Lore_Luminous_Russula.md), [onion](Lore_Onion.md), and [water root pod pit](Lore_Water_Root_Pod_Pit.md).

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Water_Breathing), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Water_Breathing), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Water_Breathing), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(spell).md)
