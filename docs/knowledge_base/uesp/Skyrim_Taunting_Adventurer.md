[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Taunting_Adventurer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Taunting_Adventurer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Taunting_Adventurer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Taunting_Adventurer.jpg) An Orc Taunting Adventurer The **Taunting Adventurer** is a [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Taunting_Adventurer) either in the wilderness or in an [inn](Skyrim_Inns.md). There is a 25% chance they will appear as a male [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md), a 25% chance of appearing as a female Orc, a 25% chance of appearing as a female [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md), and a 25% chance of appearing as a male [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md). They will simply be named by their race when you see them in gameplay.

The adventurer wears a complete set of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor), including cuirass, boots, gauntlets, shield, and has a 50% chance of wearing a helmet, all items leveled to the best possible quality. They either wield a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), or [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe). They also carry leveled potions [of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and [of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), along with a random [ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ingots), some [ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ore), and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
When you approach the adventurer, they will condescendingly say:

*"What's a milk drinker like you doing out here? Go home to your mother."* **You shouldn't talk to me like that.** *"Why? What are you gonna do, cry?"* | Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Back off. Now. (Intimidate).** | **Passed**: *"No need to get rough!"* <br> **Failed**: *"I don't have to take that from you!"* | **Passed**: No fight. <br> **Failed**: Adventurer turns hostile. |
| **Listen, I don't want trouble. (Persuade).** | **Passed**: *"Who said anything about trouble? Now get out of my face."* <br> **Failed**: *"Gods, you are boring!"* | **Passed**: Nothing. <br> **Failed**: Adventurer turns hostile. |

**Sorry, I'll be leaving now.** *"That's right."* If you close dialogue with them:

*"Get out of my face."*
