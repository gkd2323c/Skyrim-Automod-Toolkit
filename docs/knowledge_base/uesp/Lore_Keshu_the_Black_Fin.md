Keshu the Black Fin was a great [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) hero and founder of a movement for an advanced Saxhleel society. Upon the founding of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), she was named general of Pact forces in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and fought briefly in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Keshu-1)</sup> In later life, she became the governor of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).
