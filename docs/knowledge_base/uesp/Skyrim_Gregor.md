[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-npc-Gregor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gregor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gregor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gregor.jpg) Gregor **Gregor** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) found in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). He becomes your personal [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) when you have earned the title of a [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md). He seems to patrol around [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md), constantly circling the building.

Gregor is outfitted with a set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor). Oddly, he is equipped with a [steel greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword) instead of a one-handed weapon, unlike the other housecarls. Despite this, he still carries a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield).

## Dialogue
In addition to the standard housecarl dialogue, Gregor has several unique lines. If you ask him to follow, he'll remark:

*"I'm with you, Thane."* At that point, he'll greet you with:

*"What is it, Thane?"* After the conversation he may utter:

*"Onward, then."* If you tell him to wait, he'll loyally point out:

*"Don't be long. My place is by your side."* He will subsequently greet you with:

*"I await your command, Thane."* Possibly adding the following as you end the conversation:

*"I'll wait for you here."* When you ask him to trade items, he'll joyfully claim:

*"What's mine is yours, Thane."* If you tell him it's time to part ways, he'll head back to the White Hall after saying his goodbyes with:

*"As you command, Thane. I'll wait at the White Hall in Dawnstar until you need me again."* If you've finished at least one room of Heljarchen Hall, he'll instead make his way over there after saying goodbye with:

*"As you command, Thane. I'll guard Heljarchen House * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * until you return."*
## Notes
- He is the only housecarl to carry a two-handed weapon, despite being more proficient in the [One-handed skill](Skyrim_One-handed.md) and carrying a shield. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. His steel greatsword is replaced with a steel sword to complement his Combat Warrior1H class, matching the majority of other housecarls.

## Bugs
- Gregor may fail to settle at [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) and instead patrol one of your other properties. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.1, fixes this bug.
