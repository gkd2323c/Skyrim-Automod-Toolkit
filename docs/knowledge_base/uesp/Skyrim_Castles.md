# Castles

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) The Blue Palace in Solitude This page lists all the **castles** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Solitude Palace.png) **[Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md)** — The current seat of power for Solitude, Haafingar, and Skyrim; and home to the High King of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blue+Palace)
- **[Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md)** — A large fortress in Solitude which acts as the headquarters of the Imperial Legion and residence of the Emperor in Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Dour)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A large castle on an island far northwest of Solitude connected to Volkihar Keep. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Volkihar+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A large fortress on an island far northwest of Solitude connected to Castle Volkihar which serves as the Volkihar vampire clan's home. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Volkihar+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle.png) **[Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md)** — The residence of the Jarl of Whiterun Hold, the hold's seat of political power, and the location of the hold's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dragonsreach)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle_4.png) **[Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> — A Nordic hall player home in the Reach, north of Bilegulch Mine. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Hendraheim+%28CC%29)
- **[Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md)** — The residence of Idgrod Ravencrone, the Jarl of Hjaalmarch. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Highmoon+Hall)
- **[Jarl's Longhouse (Falkreath)](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md)** — The seat of Falkreath's government and home to its jarl. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Jarl%27s+Longhouse+%28Falkreath%29)
- **[Jarl's Longhouse (Winterhold)](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md)** — A large two-story house in Winterhold, and the court of the city's Jarl. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Jarl%27s+Longhouse+%28Winterhold%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png) **[Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md)** — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mistveil+Keep)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Palace.png) **[Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md)** — A large castle at the far end of the Valunstrad district in Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Palace+of+the+Kings)
- **[The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md)** — A large two-story structure that sits on a hillside overlooking most of Dawnstar. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+White+Hall)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Keep.png) **[Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md)** — An ancient Dwarven keep built into the mountainside, used as a palace by the Jarl of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Understone+Keep)
