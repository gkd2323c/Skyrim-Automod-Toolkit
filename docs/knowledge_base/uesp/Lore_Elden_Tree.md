# Graht-oak

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/ON-place-Vinedusk_Village_Graht-oak.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vinedusk_Village_Graht-oak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vinedusk_Village_Graht-oak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vinedusk_Village_Graht-oak.jpg) Graht-oak *Sprawling across both banks of the river stood the mighty graht-oak city, with groves and orchards of lesser trees crowding it like supplicants before their king. At a lesser scale, the tree that formed the moving city would have been extraordinary: gnarled and twisted with a gorgeous crown of gold and green, dripping with vines and shining with sap. At a mile tall and half as wide, it was the most magnificent thing Scotti had ever seen.*
— *[A Dance in Fire, Chapter 2](Lore_A_Dance_in_Fire,_Chapter_2.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ADIF2-1)</sup> The graht-oaks are massive, ambulatory trees native to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) are known to build entire cities in the branches of these great trees, most famously [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ADIF2-1)</sup> In their mighty boughs, Bosmer live alongside the [ape](Lore_Ape.md) -like [Imga](Lore_Imga.md) in a mobile ecosystem, coaxing the living wood into pathways winding around hide structures nestled in hollows.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRKR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AA-3)</sup> The canopy of the trees is so large that it is sometimes called the *second sky*, or the *middle green*.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGFalicia-4)</sup> Emblematic of their [reverence for nature](Lore_Green_Pact.md), the Bosmeri [Tower](Lore_Tower.md), [Green-Sap](Lore_Green-Sap.md), is represented in each and all of the graht-oaks, rather than in a single erected structure as with [White-Gold](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) and [Ada-Mantia](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AE4TET-5)</sup>

The **Elden Tree** is an even larger subspecies of the graht-oak and one of the oldest living things in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are generally worshipped as focal points of the Green's essence. The cities, [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) and Falinesti are the only settlements built from the Elden Tree.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoad Elden-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLoad Autumn-7)</sup> The southeast region of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md) is known for its abundance of graht-oak trees, more than any other parts of Valenwood, hence its name.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONLoad Grahtwood-8)</sup>
