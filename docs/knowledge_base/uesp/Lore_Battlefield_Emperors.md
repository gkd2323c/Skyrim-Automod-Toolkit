# Battlefield Emperors

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/ON-npc-Orpheon_the_Tactician.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Orpheon_the_Tactician.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Orpheon_the_Tactician.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Orpheon_the_Tactician.jpg) Orpheon the Tactician, one of the few Battlefield Emperors of nominal importance The Battlefield Emperors (also known as "**Pretenders**")<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Clivia ESO-1)</sup> was a term<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lep Seclusa Backstory-2)</sup> given for the series of [Emperors](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) that claimed the [Ruby Throne](Lore_Ruby_Throne.md) for themselves at the height of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). These emperors generally hailed from the three alliances and were characterized for their incredibly short reigns. For the most part, their names were lost to history.<sup>[[nb 1]](#cite_note-ESOTBWEs-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CATAYQ2-5)</sup>

## Known Battlefield Emperors
- Morkulth<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Aw EM-6)</sup> — a soldier from the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) who was crowned emperor after his victory at the [Battle of Chalman Keep](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md#Siege_Warfare).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWSa CK-7)</sup>
- [Orpheon the Tactician](Lore_Orpheon_the_Tactician.md) —an [arcanist](Lore_Arcanist.md) who abandoned his former alliance and was rumored to have [dragonblood](Lore_Dragonborn.md) in his ancestry.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lt O-8)</sup> Was slain on Lep Seclusa when he and his army tried to claim an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) hidden there.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lep Seclusa Backstory-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-UEESO-9)</sup>
- [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md)<sup>[[nb 2]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

## Notes
1. [^](#cite_ref-ESOTBWEs_4-0) During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), when each alliance gained control of the keeps around the Imperial City, they would crown a figurehead emperor from among their warriors.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAWar-3)</sup>
2. [^](#cite_ref-10) It is possible the Vestige could have been crowned emperor during the time of the Three Banners War, but it is not confirmed. Empress [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md) would make reference to the Vestige's status as a former/current battlefield emperor of Cyrodiil if informed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Clivia ESO-1)</sup>
