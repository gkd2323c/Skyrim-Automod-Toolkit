[Ordinator Edict](Lore_Ordinator_Edict.md), Mandate Seven **by [Inquisitor Nivos Uveran](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Inquisitor_Nivos_Uveran)** *Ordinator decrees for the citizens of Vivec* *
---
Let it be known:

The Order of Inquisition has received reports of outsiders wearing the holy [Ordinator](Lore_Ordinator.md)'s Regalia, to wit, the [Golden Mask of Devotion](Lore_Golden_Mask_of_Devotion.md) and Robes of Triune Faith. This is a clear breach of the Seventeenth Dictum of Piety. Any member of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) laity who wears the Regalia shall be subject to swift and merciless sanction. Any non-Dunmer seen wearing the Regalia shall be killed on sight. Such is the price of blasphemy.

You are hereby warned.

Victory for [the Three](Lore_Tribunal.md),

Inquisitor Nivos Uveran
