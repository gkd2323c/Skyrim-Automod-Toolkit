Mantra of Expulsion *A prayer to Jone and Jode*
---
Blessed Moons, name our hearts with your dancing.

Honored [Jode](Lore_Jode.md), hasten our paws to prayer.

Honored [Jone](Lore_Jone.md), strengthen our claws for your greater glory.

[Ghost moon](Lore_Ghost_Moon.md), be gone! In the Moons' bright light, vanish!

The Lunar Lattice sweetens all life. It is right to praise the moons. Praise them with all your heart.
