[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-npc-Ingrid.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ingrid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ingrid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ingrid.jpg) Ingrid watching the execution **Ingrid** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [food vendor](Skyrim_Food_Vendor.md) who lives in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) at the start of the game. During the first scenes she shouts *"Death to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md)!"* right after a Stormcloak rebel is executed. She is killed when [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) destroys the city.

She wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0001be1b), and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
