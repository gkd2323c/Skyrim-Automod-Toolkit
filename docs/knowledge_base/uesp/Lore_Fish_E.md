## Eel
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/ON-furnishing-Solitude_Tray%2C_Fresh_Eel_02.png/200px-ON-furnishing-Solitude_Tray%2C_Fresh_Eel_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Solitude_Tray,_Fresh_Eel_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Solitude_Tray,_Fresh_Eel_02.png) Solitude eel [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Eel.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Eel.png) An Eel **Eels** are a species of long and slimy fish. [Morays](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moray) are a distinct variety of eel.

- **Azure Eel** can be found in the foul waters of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). They are known for being cold and slippery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CL-1)</sup>
- **Conger-eel**, a species of eel mentioned as the [garrote](Lore_Garrote.md) -material of choice for Lord Zuthorix the Eel-Strangler.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Serving Platter-2)</sup>
- **Coral Eels** are found off the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice.md) and serve as a source of food for the island's [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) inhabitants.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
- **Cutthroat Eel** can be found in the foul waters of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).
- **Geels-ha Butcher Eel** is a type of massive river eel present in [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) folklore that swallow [haj mota](Lore_Haj_Mota.md) whole and whose gizzards are believed to be the source of bog-blue [jasper](Lore_Jasper.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
- **Leechtooth Eel** can be found on the foul waters of Arkthzand Caverns in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).
- **Mustard Eel** can be found in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).
- **Nedic Eel** can be found in [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- **Netch-Hook Eel** can be found in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).
- **Reaper's Eel** can be found in [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md).
- **Ribbon Eel** can be found in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).
- **Ripper Eel** is an aggresive species of eel that is trained to hunt [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) illegally crossing canals in [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md). Though they do not attack people who have rubbed themselves with eel-slime.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIC-5)</sup>
- **Sand Eel** can be found in the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).
- **Sewer Eel** can be found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).
- **Slag Eel** can found in the foul waters of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).
- **Snapper Eel** can be found in [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- **Snipe Eel** can be found in [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).
- **Swamp Eel** can be found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- **Thrassian Eel** can be found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md), off the coasts of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).
- **Trapjaw Eel** could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).
- **Two-Headed Glow-Eel** is a species of two-headed eel. According to legend, one known as known as Fefmem and Gemalleir was among [Ysgramor's](Lore_Ysgramor.md) pets of renown that were among his [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-FHMC-7)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Eelpout
**Eelpout** are a species of fish.

- **Matron Eelpout** can be found in the cold lake waters of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Enmegalabzu
**Enmegalabzu** are a species of [anglerfish](Lore_Angler.md) that can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It was previously thought to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
