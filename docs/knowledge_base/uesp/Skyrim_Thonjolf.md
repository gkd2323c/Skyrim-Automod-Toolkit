[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-npc-Thonjolf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thonjolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thonjolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thonjolf.jpg) Thonjolf **Thonjolf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Classes.md#Citizen) residing in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). He lives with [Kraldar](Skyrim_Kraldar.md) in the latter's [house](Skyrim_Kraldar%27s_House.md), where he sleeps from 10pm to 6am, then wanders around the house all day.

Thonjolf wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries his [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Kraldar%27s_House.md) to the house along with a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

Should the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) take over Winterhold, Kraldar will replace the exiled [Korir](Skyrim_Korir.md) as [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), and will keep Thonjolf by his side, no longer as a mere servant, but as his [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). He and Kraldar will move into the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) down the street, where he will hold court with Jarl Kraldar between 10am and 4pm, go to bed at 11pm and sleep until 6am, and he will return to Kraldar's old home whenever he has time.

Should the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) retake Winterhold after giving it away as a concession in a [temporary truce](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md), Thonjolf will enter exile with Kraldar, relocating across Skyrim to [Erikur's House](Skyrim_Erikur%27s_House.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), while Jarl Korir and his family will return from exile in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

## Dialogue

- *"Kraldar's family was once very powerful in Winterhold. It's my honor to assist him even now."*
- *"Kraldar has big plans for Winterhold. We'd be better off if he was in charge."*
- *"I'm just the Housecarl. If you need something, you should speak to Kraldar."*
- *"I'm very busy, you know. I'm the only one Kraldar can rely on to assist him."*
- *"I'm not one to complain, but I'm not sure why Kraldar insists on keeping Malur Seloth around."*
- *"Some people can't be trusted to accomplish anything that's asked of them. They don't take pride in their work."*

## Conversations
Whether or not he is jarl, Kraldar will often end up chatting with Thonjolf out of boredom:

- **Kraldar:** *"So, what's the latest exciting news about goings-on in Winterhold?"*
- **Thonjolf:** *"There is no news, I'm afraid."*
- **Kraldar:** *"None? Nothing at all? Nothing from the College, even?"*
- **Thonjolf:** *"I shall inform you the moment something noteworthy happens."*
- **Kraldar:** *"Yes, that'll be fine. Thank you."*

- **Thonjolf:** *"Will you be needing anything special today?"*
- **Kraldar:** *"Why don't you see if Arch-Mage Aren is interested in joining me for a meal?"*
- **Thonjolf:** *"Again? Are you sure? He still hasn't answered your last several requests."*
- **Kraldar:** *"Well, he's been very busy. Perhaps today he'll have some free time."*
- **Thonjolf:** *"As you wish."*

As housecarl, Thonjolf may also attempt to make a request of Malur Seloth:

- **Thonjolf:** *"Malur, I need you to take care of something."*
- **Malur:** *"No, I don't think so. Not today."*
- **Thonjolf:** *"I haven't even said what it is."*
- **Malur:** *"Doesn't really matter."*
