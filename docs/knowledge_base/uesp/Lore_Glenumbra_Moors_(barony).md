[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Glenumbra Moors was found Glenumbra Moors was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) barony in southwestern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). To the northeast was [Northmoor](Lore_Northmoor.md); east lay [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md), while [Tulune](Lore_Tulune.md) was situated to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFmap-1)</sup> The provincial capital was called Glenumbra, and the regional deity was [Mara](Lore_Mara.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

The province celebrated three local holidays. On the 8th of Sun's Dawn, the people of Glenumbra Moors remembered the battle between [Aiden Direnni](Lore_Aiden_Direnni.md) and the Alessian Army in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) in a holiday called the [Day of Release](Lore_Day_of_Release.md). On the 8th of Sun's Dusk, the province celebrated the [Moon Festival](Lore_Moon_Festival.md), celebrating the ancient goddess of the moons, [Secunda](Lore_Secunda.md). On the 23rd of Frost Fall there was another holiday called "[Broken Diamonds](Lore_Broken_Diamonds_(holiday).md)", in remembrance of the death of [Kintyra Septim II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md). The author [Ryston Baylor](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ryston_Baylor) wrote a book, also called [Broken Diamonds](Lore_Broken_Diamonds.md), about the holiday and its origin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hot IB-3)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), this region became a part of the kingdom of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TWit T-4)</sup>
