From the journal of Logang Foxbite **by [Logang Foxbite](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Logang_Foxbite)** *A journal entry recounting a giant's fight with a pack of wolves*
---
[![ON-concept-Giant.png](https://images.uesp.net/5/55/ON-concept-Giant.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Giant.png)
- Don't think I'll be having problems with the [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md) that were scaring up the [chickens](Lore_Chicken.md) now. Heard a commotion outside early this morning, so I grabbed a torch and my axe and ran out there. I about dropped 'em both when I spotted, not more than a stone's throw away, a [giant](Lore_Giant.md) surrounded by the pack. I've never seen a giant this far south; I don't know what he was doing down here.

- Behind him, lying down, was young tyke of a [mammoth](Lore_Mammoth.md). I couldn't tell if it was hurt or dead, but the wolves were trying to get at it, hopping in and out and dodging the giant's club. Well, wasn't long before a swing of that thing (big as a tree trunk) caught a wolf square in the side and sent it flying up over a tree! The rest of the pack turned tail after that! I did the same—wasn't going to risk being out there with an angry giant—and he and the calf were gone before mid-day.
