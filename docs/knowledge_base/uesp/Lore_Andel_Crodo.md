# Andel Crodo

Andel Crodo was a high-ranking [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) member of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md), playing a pivotal role within the [Second Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). He implemented a non-intervention policy in regions outside the Dominion, a stance that faced opposition from some members within the Thalmor. Some believed that persisting with this policy would ultimately lead to the downfall of all the Elder Races. Little information is available regarding his other policies or the precise timeframe of his life, but it is documented that he was active in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The character of Andel Crodo serves as a tribute to the founder of The Essential Site, a significant website in The Elder Scrolls community. Ultimately, The Essential Site merged with Xanathar's Library to form [The Imperial Library](https://www.imperial-library.info/).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-RFM-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-HBYATYDNJU-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-DGI-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-MMHL-5)</sup>
