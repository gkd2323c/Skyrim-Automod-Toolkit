[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-flora-Bleeding_Crown_Plant.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Bleeding_Crown_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Bleeding_Crown_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Bleeding_Crown_Plant.jpg) A bleeding crown plant [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-book-Bleedingcrown.png/150px-SR-book-Bleedingcrown.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Bleedingcrown.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Bleedingcrown.png) Bleeding crown as depicted in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* **Bleeding Crowns** are red-capped mushrooms that are commonly found on cave floors.

Bleeding crown and [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) have both [Fortify Block](Skyrim_Fortify_Block.md) and [Resist Magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) effects, making it easy to create a dual-effect defensive potion.

- *"The caps of these mushrooms do indeed appear smeared with blood, though it is often hard to see in the dark, damp places in which they grow. Not uncommonly found in Skyrim, their abundance is countered by the difficulty in acquiring them. As any experienced herbalist knows, the darkest of caves often conceal far more than mere mushrooms."*
- *"Nonetheless, these potent fungi, when combined with certain powdered [antlers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers), produce a mixture rendering one quite [susceptible to poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weakness_to_Poison). Their usefulness is also quite apparent when mixed with [Lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), creating a substance [highly resistant to magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md)." - Agneta Falia in the [Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md).*

## Ingredients
28 guaranteed samples can be found in 19 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 in [Tolvald's Gap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Gap) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 2 in [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 centered 210 ft NE of [Bilegulch Mine](Skyrim_Bilegulch_Mine.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-74983&y=-37079)

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
