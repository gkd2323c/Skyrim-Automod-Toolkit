[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-npc-Vagrant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vagrant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vagrant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vagrant.jpg) A Vagrant **Vagrants** are three [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md#NPCs_and_Creatures) NPCs found in [The Ratway Vaults](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway_Vaults) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

They wear random clothes, boots, gloves, and a hat. They wield an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and use a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) (up to Imperial quality at level 7) and twenty leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow) for ranged attacks. Additionally they carry a selection of raw [food](Skyrim_Food.md), a leveled amount of gold, and have a good chance of carrying a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).
