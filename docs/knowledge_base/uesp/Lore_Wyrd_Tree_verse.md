Wyrd Tree verse *A poem about the Wyrd Tree*
---
[![ON-concept-Wyrd Tree.png](https://images.uesp.net/c/c3/ON-concept-Wyrd_Tree.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Wyrd_Tree.png)
- Deep in the wood the [Wyrd Tree](Lore_Wyrd_Tree.md) grows
- Ancient beyond [mer](Lore_Mer.md) or [man](Lore_Man.md).
- It hides away more than it shows
- Secrets from when time began

- Roots that conceal legends long dead
- Each leaf a story untold
- What lost knowledge waits, yet unread,
- Among twig and limb of old?
