Valindor is a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) living in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). Being born and raised in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), as a child he played in the towering trees, living among his brethren.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup> He later apprenticed in the holy city [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md) under a master armorer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup> He evidently was a great student as his master urged him to take over his business on his deathbed but Valindor instead chose to leave Valenwood and wander [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup>

He would eventually settle in the [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) city of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), where he would take employment at the city's [fishery](Lore_Fish.md) and [meadery](Lore_Black-Briar_Mead.md). Valindor lived a humble life and was a pleasant member of the community, staying busy between his two jobs, giving to those in need, and encouraging [addicts](Lore_Drugs.md) to become sober.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup> Valindor however, occasionally expressed regrets over the simple life and routine he had become accustomed to and wondered if he should move on,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup> little did he know a massive shakeup to his life was coming.

Valindor turned out to be a blood descendant of [Lord Harkon](Lore_Harkon.md), the [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md) ruler of [Volkihar](Lore_Volkihar.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vingalmo-2)</sup> Only the descendants of Harkon could be conferred his blood to become a [Vampire](Lore_Vampire.md), and only under a certain alignment of the [stars](Lore_Stars.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vingalmo-2)</sup> The Volkihar identified Valindor as one of these such descendants along with the right aligning of the stars.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vingalmo-2)</sup> The clan sent an individual to Riften to turn Valindor as they wished to have an informant in his area of the province.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vingalmo-2)</sup> After receiving the unholy blood, Valindor was grateful for his transformation and considered it a gift.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valindor-1)</sup>
