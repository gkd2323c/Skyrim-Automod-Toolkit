## Quick Walkthrough
1. Befriend the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) by dealing with the Skooma trade.
2. Complete three additional miscellaneous quests in Riften.
3. Purchase [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md).
4. Return to the Jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become Thane of the Rift. The Jarl requires you to buy the house in town as well as completing a number of miscellaneous quests. Once this is achieved you can become Thane.

In order to receive this quest you must first aid the current jarl, [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) or [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md), by dealing with the skooma trade in Riften. This involves completing the quests [Helping Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helping_Hand), [The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid), and [Supply and Demand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand).

With Helping Hand and The Raid already completed, you must now complete three more [Miscellaneous Quests](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md) specifically in Riften (for a total of five); completion of miscellaneous quests in other Rift settlements does not count, they do not have to be Favor quests like in other holds, and miscellaneous quests in Riften that were added by DLC also do not count. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. [Investing](Skyrim_Investor.md) in Rift stores does not count for this Thaneship quest either.

You must also purchase [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md), which costs at least 5,000 gold (see [bugs](Skyrim_Honeyside.md#Bugs)). Simply purchasing it is enough, there is no requirement to decorate, although it becomes more useful if decorated.

### Reward
After completing the necessary quests and purchasing [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md), the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of the Rift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_the_Rift), is a random, leveled, enchanted weapon. Despite its name, it can be nearly any weapon, including a non-bladed weapon like a Mace. There is a 50% chance that it will be one-handed and a 50% chance that it will be two-handed. You will also receive a housecarl named [Iona](Skyrim_Iona.md), who will be waiting for you at Honeyside.

## Notes
- Unlike every other Thane quest, the "Assist the people of <Hold>" part does not appear in the journal, and must be completed *before* the Jarl will make you the offer. A new quest "Speak to the Jarl of Riften" will appear under Miscellaneous when you have completed enough miscellaneous objectives.
- The quests which gain the favor of the jarl— [Helping Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helping_Hand), [The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid) and [Supply and Demand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand) —are required tasks to complete before becoming named Thane of the Rift even if the Empire takes Riften, although you will be allowed to buy [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) immediately.
- The guest bedroom in Honeyside will also be decorated at the end of this quest.

## Bugs
- "The Blade of the Rift" is never actually given as a reward: instead, you will receive a generic un-renamed enchanted weapon. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- The reward you receive is generated randomly at the beginning of the game, so will always be iron. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- Visiting Honeyside for the first time before becoming named Thane of Riften may prevent you from ever being able to be named Thane of Riften. After purchasing the house, immediately speak with the Jarl to avoid this bug. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- It is possible that the dialogue with the Jarl will not start, no matter how many people you've helped. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) In the console, enter `setstage Freeform Riften Thane 10`. The quest should progress normally from there.
- Sometimes the "Speak to the Jarl of Riften" step gets stuck in the miscellaneous section of the journal. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) In the console, enter `setstage Freeform Riften Thane 10`, and then `setobjectivecompleted Freeform Riften Thane 10 1`.
- If you have fully completed this quest and the Jarl changes during the civil war, you are unable to become thane again. <sup>**?**</sup>
