Estlainder was the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) **Elder** of the Council of Wizards of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md). Esthlainder was greatly respected by his fellow mages; he was an imposing figure decked out in indigo blue robes with gold trim, a high pointed wizard hat, carrying a beautiful intricately carved staff.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> In the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) prodigy by the name of [Gyron Vardengroet](Lore_Gyron_Vardengroet.md) arrived to the Crystal Tower in the hopes of learning more about magic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup>

Esthlainder approached Gyron and informed him that his arrival was foretold by the [gods](Lore_Gods.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> Esthlainder and the other masters taught Gyron all they could but the day came where Esthlainder informed Gyron that the Crystal Tower could teach him no more and it was time for him to move on and seek further wisdom.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> Esthlainder gave Gyron a package to open when he was clear of the Tower. Inside the package for Gyron were robes, a pointed wizard hat, and an intricately carved staff identical to that of Esthlainder's.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> Esthlainder left a letter explaining that the garments were indestructible and the staff had great magical properties, he also informed Gyron he would be known from then on as the Sage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup>
