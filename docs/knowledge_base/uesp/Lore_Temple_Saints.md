The Temple Saints are the most honored ancestors of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), canonized by the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) for deeds they performed during their lifetime.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot S-1)</sup> They are celebrated as models of virtue and piety, often invoked for aid and protection. After the fall of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), the three Tribunes were re-appointed as saints by the [New Temple](Lore_New_Temple.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup>

### Greater Saints
The greater saints of the Temple,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup> listed with their proper spheres, are:

- Saint [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) the Warden<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup> – Healers, Teachers<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLG-4)</sup>
- Saint [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) the Magus<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup> – Artificers, Wizards<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLG-4)</sup>
- Saint [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) the Poet – Artists, Rogues<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLG-4)</sup>
- Saint [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) the Captain – Warriors, Statesmen,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot S-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup> Redoran<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mot DGH-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NARM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SN-7)</sup>
- Saint [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) the Pilgrim – Outcasts, Seekers,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen DG-3)</sup> Hlaalu<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mot DGH-5)</sup>

### Lesser Saints
The lesser saints of the Temple, listed with their proper spheres, are:<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot S-1)</sup>

- Saint [Rilms](Lore_Rilms.md) the Barefooted – Pilgrims, Beggars
- Saint [Aralor](Lore_Aralor.md) the Penitent – Tanners and Miners
- Saint [Seryn](Lore_Seryn.md) the Merciful – Brewers, Bakers, Distillers
- Saint [Felms](Lore_Felms.md) the Bold – Butchers, Fishmongers
- Saint [Roris](Lore_Roris.md) the Martyr – Furnishers and Caravaners
- Saint [Olms](Lore_Olms.md) the Just – Sailors, Chandlers, Clerks, Indoril<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mot DGH-5)</sup>
- Saint [Delyn](Lore_Delyn.md) the Wise – Potters, Glassmakers
- Saint [Meris](Lore_Meris.md) the Peacemaker – Farmers, Laborers
- Saint [Llothis](Lore_Llothis.md) the Pious – Tailors, Dyers, Dres<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mot DGH-5)</sup>
- Saint [Vorys](Lore_Vorys.md) the Immolant<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> – Telvanni<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mot DGH-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Sept10AMA-10)</sup>
- Saint [Jiub](Lore_Jiub.md) the Eradicator of the Winged Menace<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SJO-11)</sup>

In death, the [souls](Lore_Soul.md) of Olms, Felms, and Llothis were captured by Sotha Sil in [black soul gems](Lore_Black_Soul_Gem.md) and brought to his [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), where they were bound to mechanical [fabricant](Lore_Fabricants.md) bodies in an attempt to grant them immortality. Known as the **Clockwork Saints**, these creations were eventually driven mad by their mechanical existence.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-12)</sup>
