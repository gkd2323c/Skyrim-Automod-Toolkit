# Silver Hand


The Silver Hand is the historical enemy of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md), as they are aware that members of the Companions, particularly of their upper echelon, [The Circle](Skyrim_The_Circle.md), are lycanthropes. According to [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md), the two groups have repeatedly bled each other. To spite the Companions, the Silver Hand twice attempts to steal scattered pieces of [Wuuthrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wuuthrad), the shattered axe of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) which was of great importance to them.

The [Purity of Revenge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purity_of_Revenge) quest has you eradicate the Silver Hand for good.

To learn more of the Silver Hand and their history, see the [lore article](Lore_Silver_Hand.md).

## Notes
- Silver Hand members are apparently somewhat paranoid about being able to cure [diseases](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Diseases), most likely due to their regular contact with werewolves and vampires. They have a 25% chance of carrying one or two [potions of cure disease](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease), and 5-20% chances of carrying 1-3 samples of each of the four [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) with a cure disease effect (charred skeever hide, hawk feathers, mudcrab chitin, and vampire dust).
- Krev the Skinner is a [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) boss, whose race and gender is randomly determined when they spawn.
- Silver Hand members inherit much of their data from Bandit [Leveled Lists](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists) with some data, such as their name and inventory, being overwritten. As a result: - Despite all having the same name, they vary in level and strength. Their stats range from those of Bandits to Bandit Marauders.
- They use [bandit idle dialogue](Skyrim_Bandit.md#Dialogue) when they are unaware of your presence.
- If you hit a Silver Hand corpse with a soul trapping weapon, instead of receiving the standard message of "Silver Hand resisted Soul Trap", it will instead say "Bandit resisted..." or "Bandit Marauder resisted..."
- Some Silver Hand members might not use silver weapons, and instead have anything from Steel to Nordic (not Ancient Nordic) weapons in their inventories. This might be due to the notes mentioned above.
- Radiant Silver Hand dungeons have a heavy association with spectator pitfighting, animal abuse, and Skooma distribution.
- Radiant Silver Hand locations are often near a treasure chest or treasure map.
- Radiant Silver Hand dungeons may respawn Silver Hand members instead of their regular bandits after the Companions questline is finished.
- Since the Silver Hand radiant quests are available for a short interval, it is possible to complete only 2 out of 3 per playthrough.
- With version 2.0.9 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), Driftshade Refuge and Gallows Rock will no longer be populated by Silver Hand members once the quests for those locations are finished.
- With version 2.1.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), the Silver Hand members at Driftshade Refuge will now be reserved for Purity of Revenge, and will be replaced with regular [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) once the dungeon resets after the latter quest is finished. Gallows Rock, however, will turn into a no-reset zone, and will be unclearable during The Silver Hand.
