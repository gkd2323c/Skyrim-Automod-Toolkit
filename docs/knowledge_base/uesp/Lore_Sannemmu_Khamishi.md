*You wonder how such simple, nomadic barbarians can come to have such elaborate and sophisticated garments and tools? Then learn this: for the Ashlander, simplicity is a choice, not a necessity. We follow the precepts of the Good Daedra, and our practices and preferences are in accord with their teachings.*
—Sannemmu Khamishi<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup> Sannemmu Khamishi was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [mage](Lore_Magic.md) and the wise woman of the [Erabenimsun Tribe](Lore_Erabenimsun_Tribe.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AWW-2)</sup> She was a devoted follower of the [Good Daedra](Lore_Good_Daedra.md), keeper of many secrets, and the author of [a text](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crafting_Motif_48__Ashlander_Style) describing [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) combat gear and apparel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/ON-concept-Ashlander_Armor.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Ashlander_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Ashlander_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Ashlander_Armor.jpg) Ashlander apparel described by Sannemu Khamishi [![](https://images.uesp.net/d/dc/MW-banner-Erabenimsun_Tribal_Flag.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-banner-Erabenimsun_Tribal_Flag.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-banner-Erabenimsun_Tribal_Flag.png) The Erabenimsun Tribe banner Little is known about Sannemmu Khamishi's life. She was an Ashlander of the Erabenimsun Tribe, who lived sometime after Magister [Varkenel](Lore_Varkenel.md) created the [Vvardvarks](Lore_Vvardvark.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TVE-3)</sup> which she referenced in her writings. At some point she attained the position of the Wise Woman,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup> and as such became the spiritual leader of her tribe.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AWW-2)</sup>

Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she compiled texts on Ashlander traditions surrounding combat equipment and attire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup> In her writings, she emphasized the importance of the crescent [moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moon) as an ancient and sacred symbol, used sparingly but with great significance. According to the tales of her people, should certain prophesied events come to pass, the crescents would be full once more, and their axes would be raised in a time of hardship and glory. Swords and kris daggers likewise often bore crescent imagery. The importance of symbolism carried over into the design of the mages' weapons as well, with [staves](Lore_Staff.md) crowned by one, two, or even three symbols of the Good Daedra, framed by a double crescent of chitin and bone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AS-1)</sup>

By 2E 582, the position of Wise Woman of the Erabenimsun was held by Zenammi.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WWZ-4)</sup>
