[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-npc-Liesl_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Liesl_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Liesl_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Liesl_2.jpg) Liesl **Liesl** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) sailor found at the [Water Stone](Skyrim_Water_Stone.md) in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).

Initially from [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), Liesl, along with fellow sailors [Hjalfar](Skyrim_Hjalfar.md), [Benkum](Skyrim_Benkum.md), and [Palevius Lex](Skyrim_Palevius_Lex.md), decided to cast anchor along the western coast of Solstheim and immediately fell prey to [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s spell. She works continually at the stone, oblivious to the world around her. Once the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the entire crew will return to their boat on the coast. When you leave the area, they will all disappear from the game.

Liesl wears [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Miner%27s_Clothes.md#0006ff43) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).

## Dialogue
She shares her dialogue lines with her fellow sailors:

- *"Think we've got to get back to our ship now."*
- *"We're already late. Not sure why we decided to take such an extended shore time."*
- *"When the tide goes, we'll go with it."*
- *"It's a long way back to Windhelm from here."*
- *"Hope our shipments are still good. How long has it been?"*
- *"Best be shoving off now."*

## Notes
- There's an unused NPC in the base game named [Liesl](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Liesl) who was intended to be a warrior in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)'s arena.
