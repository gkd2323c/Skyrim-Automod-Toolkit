[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Torch.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Torch.png) Torch A **Torch** is a [miscellaneous item](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md) that can be held in one hand to light up the surrounding area. The duration of a torch is 240 seconds, and the radius of its light is 512. Other items, such as lanterns and candlesticks, cannot be used as sources of light.

Torches, despite having a value, cannot be bought from or sold to any [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md), even with the Merchant perk. Holding a torch improves [Warmth](Skyrim_Fortify_Warmth.md) by 50 points when [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active.

It is in color.

## Notes
- Torches you give to [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) will never burn out. - Some followers have a torch in their inventory by default.
- Torches cannot be rotated or zoomed in on like other items in your inventory.
- Torches are the only "Miscellaneous" item you can equip and the only one you can favorite.
- You can remove a torch from its sconce to darken an area to avoid detection if enemies are nearby. Be careful not to blow your cover in the process. - Only torches with brown handles can be removed; the ones with black handles cannot be taken.
- If you drop a lit torch into a body of water, its flame won't go out.
- If you bash an enemy while holding a torch, the enemy will briefly catch fire, and progress towards your [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill will improve slightly.
- If you bash oil-based [traps](Skyrim_Traps.md) while holding a torch or if you drop a lit torch into them, they will be set on fire.
- If you approach flammable gas traps while holding a torch, that will be enough to ignite the vapors, and a massive explosion will result.
- If you place torches into the cupboard behind the counter in [Riverwood Trader](Skyrim_Riverwood_Trader.md), they won't add to what [Lucan](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md) has for sale.
- If you extinguish the fire at [Solitude Lighthouse](Skyrim_Solitude_Lighthouse.md) during [Lights Out!](Skyrim_Lights_Out!.md), place torches into the fire pit and travel to somewhere else, the torches and the lighthouse beacon will be lit by the time you return. However, when viewed from a distance, there will still be no visible light.
- If you sit or activate a lumber pile, wood chopping block, ore vein, or crafting facility while holding a torch, it will be extinguished, though it won't be removed from your inventory.
- With the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on, torches can help you safely navigate the dark areas in two sections of [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md) ([Filament and Filigree](Skyrim_Apocrypha_(Filament_and_Filigree).md) and [The Sallow Regent](Skyrim_Apocrypha_(The_Sallow_Regent).md)).
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, torches will wear out over time, and can get snuffed out in rainy weather, so loot them as needed.
- If you un-equip a torch before the 240 seconds expire, the next time you equip, the timer will reset and you will have a new 240 seconds before the torch expires.
