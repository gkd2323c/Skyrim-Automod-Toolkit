# Ruddy Fangs

The Ruddy Fangs are a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) bandit pride<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOElianna-1)</sup> from [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRF-2)</sup> They have the biggest presence near [the Scar](Lore_The_Scar.md) of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), but are a scourge upon all of Elsweyr. Their main source of income is smuggling things to and from Elsweyr, and one of their biggest products is [Skooma](Lore_Skooma.md). Joining the Ruddy Fangs require you to throw out any morals, and the only way to get out of the business is death.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRF-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Ruddy Fangs began preying on refugees fleeing the rage of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) unleashed from the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOElianna-1)</sup> Later that year, they took significant blows as a result of the [Soulless One](Lore_Soulless_One.md)'s interference, such as putting a stop to a smuggling operation in [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md), <sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTRA-3)</sup> and slaying their leader, Zayreba Sharpclaw.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOMM-4)</sup>
