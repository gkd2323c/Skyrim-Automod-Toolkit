[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-npc-Yar_gro-Gatuk.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Yar_gro-Gatuk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yar_gro-Gatuk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yar_gro-Gatuk.jpg) Yar gro-Gatuk **Yar gro-Gatuk** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md). He is one of the hunters who appear in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

Yar wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd) and carries a small amount of gold. In combat, he relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) along with a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) supplemented with up to 20 leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrow.md), both of which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.
