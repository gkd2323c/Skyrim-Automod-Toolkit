# Atronach

**Atronach** may refer to three things:

- [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md), unaligned daedra composed of various elements
- [Golem](Lore_Golem.md), artificial constructs given life through magical means
- [The Atronach](Lore_The_Atronach.md), a constellation, also known as The Golem
