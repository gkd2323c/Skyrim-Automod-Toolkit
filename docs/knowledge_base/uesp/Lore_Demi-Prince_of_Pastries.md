# Demi-Prince of Pastries

| Demi-Prince of Pastries | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Demiprince](Lore_Demiprince.md) | Gender | Female |

The Demi-Prince of Pastries is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [Demiprince](Lore_Demiprince.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sheo-1)</sup> Little is known of her, though as an offspring of a [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) or [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) and a lesser entity such as a mortal she might have inherited certain aspects of her Princely parent. She may also be lacking in characteristics such as foresight, memory, cruelty, or willfulness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Although her Daedric parent is unknown, [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), the Daedric Prince of Madness, considered her to be one of the few worthy of wielding the [Wabbajack](Lore_Wabbajack.md). Her exact age is also unknown, though she predates the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), which took place circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Sheogorath noted that she hadn't been seen for some time at that point.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sheo-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Demi-Prince of Pastries should not be confused with [Chef Donolon](Lore_Donolon.md), a [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) chef who went by the title Prince of Pastry and also dealt with Daedric Princes.
