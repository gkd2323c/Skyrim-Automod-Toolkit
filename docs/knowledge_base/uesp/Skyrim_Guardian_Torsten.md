[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-creature-Guardian_Torsten.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Guardian_Torsten.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Guardian_Torsten.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Guardian_Torsten.jpg) Guardian Torsten in Ragnvald Canal **Guardian Torsten** is a unique, boss-level [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) that resides in [Ragnvald Canal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnvald_Canal).

He fights with an [ancient Nord war axe](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_War_Axe.md) and can cast various ice-based spells, such as [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) and [Conjure Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md). He may carry a small amount of gold and a gemstone, and may also drop [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) when killed.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- Because Ragnvald's minimum level is 24, only the level 21 version of Guardian Torsten will ever be encountered.
