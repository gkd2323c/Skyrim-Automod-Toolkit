[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-place-Breezehome.jpg/200px-SR-place-Breezehome.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Breezehome.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Breezehome.jpg) Breezehome **Breezehome** is a house that can be purchased in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-interior-Breezehome_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Breezehome_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_03.jpg) Entryway and kitchen Breezehome is the first home offered to you during the main quest. Breezehome is the cheapest [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) available for purchase in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but it is also the only house in Skyrim that does not have an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md).

The house is conveniently located just inside the entrance to Whiterun, next to [Warmaiden's](Skyrim_Warmaiden%27s.md). This is useful for a character who frequently uses [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md), as a full set of the stations are right next door.

To purchase the home, you must first complete the [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md) quest for the Jarl of Whiterun. Upon completion he will permit you to purchase the house from [Proventus Avenicci](Skyrim_Proventus_Avenicci.md) for 5,000 gold. The total cost of the house (including all upgrades) is 6,800 gold, or 6,550 gold if you have [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed and choose to forgo the [alchemy laboratory](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) in favor of a children's bedroom.

If you are having trouble finding Proventus you can get a quest marker on him through a miscellaneous task given by his daughter [Adrianne Avenicci](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adrianne_Avenicci), who can usually be found outside of Warmaiden's, just inside the gates to [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

If you have completed [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), [Lydia](Skyrim_Lydia.md) will be appointed as your housecarl and a small bedroom will be added upstairs for her. Her bedroom includes a dresser, an end table, a sack, and an owned bed.

Once purchased, Proventus will provide a book, *[Whiterun Home Decorating Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Home_Decorating_Guide)*, which details the various house upgrades available from him. Proventus will only sell upgrades while actually inside [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md). If he is on the [Great Porch](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md#Great_Porch) or his bedroom, the option to decorate your house will not be available.

If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), Proventus will move to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and no longer offer you any upgrades. Instead, [Brill](Skyrim_Brill.md) will be able to sell you upgrades in Dragonsreach after that.

It consists of only one zone, which is the interior.

## House Items
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-interior-Breezehome_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Breezehome_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_04.jpg) Master bedroom from doorway When first purchased, prior to any upgrades being bought, it is dirty, covered with dust and cobwebs. It has a double bed and an empty chest upstairs. Downstairs there is a long wooden table and bench. The only other items are crates, a couple of unusable chairs and an unlit fire pit. If you have already been made a [Thane](Skyrim_Thane_of_Whiterun.md), Lydia's room will already be fully furnished, cleaned and walled off from the rest of the loft. There are no other internal walls. There are items that are in the house when you purchase upgrades (see below).

- The [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) contains some rare [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md).
- Two [iron greatswords](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md) and one [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) are placed on a shield plaque above the bed after you purchase the bedroom upgrade. The other plaque and racks are empty upon purchase.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-interior-Breezehome_06.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Breezehome_06.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_06.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_06.jpg) Alchemy lab [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-interior-Breezehome_08.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Breezehome_08.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_08.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Breezehome_08.jpg) Child's bedroom - This is the only purchasable home in the base game that you do not have to purchase in order to become thane. If you complete the quest [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) while [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) is [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), you will become thane, with or without Breezehome.
- You may want to remove [books](Skyrim_Books.md) from the bookcase in the Alchemy Lab before converting it to a Child's Bedroom with the Hearthfire add-on. Books will remain in the room and the ones from the bottom shelf will be under the bed, rendered inaccessible.
- The apothecary's satchel is safe. if you replace the Alchemy Lab with the Children's Bedroom, it will still hold the same items when reverted back to an Alchemy Lab.
- The contents of the chest on the floor in the Children's Bedroom are emptied onto the top chest and remain there, should you change back to a Child's bedroom.<sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>
- Other than exceptions outlined above, all other containers are safe and [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md).
- Adopted children living here will have unique dialogue comments about Whiterun: - *I like it here. The tree in the park is so pretty!* (girls)
- *[Braith](Skyrim_Braith.md) is a big meanie. She keeps telling [Lars](Skyrim_Lars_Battle-Born.md)' n me what to do. I don't wanna play with her anymore.* (girls)
- *I played with [Lucia](Skyrim_Lucia.md) in the park today. I feel sorry for her.* (girls)
- *Dragonsreach is so cool! All the kids there are mean, though.* (boys)
- *Lars is such a milk-drinker. He can't even stand up to a girl.* (boys)
- If you ask your spouse "How are the kids?" they will reply with one of the following, depending on their [voicetype](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md#Adoption_Dialogue): - *As well as can be expected. Dear, this house would work for the two of us, but with children? We need to find a bigger place.* (Arrogant)
- *Just fine. Whiterun is a lovely city, and there are so many other children here- they all love playing together.* (Neutral)
- *Couldn't be better. Whiterun is perfect for a child- great weather, plenty of space, friends to play with. What more could you want?* (Nord)
- *Fine, fine. Plenty of kids here to play and spar with. Plenty of trades to learn. It's a good place to raise a child.* (Orc)
- During [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), the house will be key-locked once again, and the Breezehome key won't work. You'll regain access once the battle is finished.
- Initially, you would've encountered a squatter, [Terek](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Terek), in Breezehome, but he did not make it into the final game.

## Bugs
- There are a few bugs which affect all houses, see the [bugs](Skyrim_Houses.md#Bugs) section of the Houses page for details and fixes.
- If you remove a book from the bookcase on the main floor (beside the firepit) that is already placed on this bookcase, the bookcase may freeze, leaving you without access to any books within the case. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.3.7, fixes this bug.
- Although, theoretically, Lydia is supposed to sleep in the housecarl's bedroom, she never seems to, and instead has the unfortunate habit of intruding upon your bedroom and using the chair inside. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- After installing [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), you may encounter a bug when purchasing decor for Breezehome. After initially purchasing the alchemy lab, it will not disappear from the list of available decor; it will remain constantly available for purchase and will not appear in Breezehome. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can also use the following console commands to fix the issue. Note that these commands do not require you to actually buy the alchemy lab upgrade.
```
prid e4ef9
disable
prid e4ec8
enable
```
- The weapon plaque in the loft and the weapon rack near the front door may be inaccessible. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0, fixes this bug.
- The apothecary's satchel in the alchemy room may respawn despite the house being a no-reset zone. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- Upon completing the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), Breezehome will be unlocked even if you have not purchased the house. However, the steward still requires you to purchase the house for 5000 gold.
- The option to replace the alchemy laboratory with a child's bedroom may not disappear after being purchased, and the change will not take place. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- The option to purchase a child's bedroom as well as the bedroom may not be available while Avenicci is on the balcony. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.1, fixes this bug.

- You can buy it from him when he enters [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md).
