# Eodoric

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-clothing-Eodoric.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-clothing-Eodoric.png) An Eodoric (Daggerfall) An Eodoric is a type of attire bearing a seemingly [golden](Lore_Gold.md) aesthetic, sometimes (and sometimes not) containing a set of armbands, a brassiere, a skirt, or loincloth, as well as an ornate collar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Clothing DF-1)</sup> Eodorics were worn throughout the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The meaning of the word "Eodoric" is unknown; designer [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson) noted that during the making of *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*, the artists created many clothing items for player characters and he had to name them -- when he couldn't figure out what name would apply, sometimes he would make up a name, such as in the case of the Eodoric item.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Ted Posts-3)</sup> - "Eodor" is a masculine term in [Old English](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Old_English) (which is an alternative spelling of the word "Edor"), possibly meaning "enclosure", "house", "protector", "prince", or "lord".<sup>[[LANG 1]](#cite_note-Runestone-4)</sup>
