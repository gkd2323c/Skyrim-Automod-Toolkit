## Faendal's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-interior-Faendal%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Faendal%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Faendal%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Faendal%27s_House.jpg) Faendal's House, interior There is only one room to the house and it is crammed with items. Starting to the right and moving counter-clockwise, there is a small dining table with two chairs, followed by a single bed, a non-respawning end table and a non-respawning cupboard, and then the fireplace. Next is a food table with a bench, then a respawning food barrel and an armor table, and finally another non-respawning cupboard. To the right of the bed is a respawning chest and there is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) by the fire, and a small non-respawning chest under the armor table. In addition to the standard stag's head above most fireplaces in Skyrim's homes, Faendal has a deer's head above his small dining table, and a slaughterfish mounted on either side of his bed.

There is a [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull) by the bed, a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) by the fire, and on the armor table, a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow), two [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow), an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet), a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), and a [banded iron shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield). There is a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) on the non-respawning cupboard beside this table, behind some books on the shelf with the [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and the [potion of true shot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_True_Shot). On the shelf above that are two [sabre cat pelts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt), and four loose iron arrows. The books on the cupboard next to the door include *[Killing - Before You're Killed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed)*, *[Scourge of the Gray Quarter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge_of_the_Gray_Quarter)*, *[Pension of the Ancestor Moth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pension_of_the_Ancestor_Moth)*, and *[Ode To The Tundrastriders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ode_To_The_Tundrastriders)*. The end table beside the bed holds a copy of *[Herbane's Bestiary: Ice Wraiths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Ice_Wraiths)*.

There are a few [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and a good deal of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) about the house. Above the fire is a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), and hanging above the food table and the cupboard by the bed are four portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), three portions of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), and two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. There are four [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and four [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging above the food table, which is set with two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [raw rabbit leg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg), a [pheasant breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast), two loaves and one piece of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), one serving each of [baked potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes) and [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), one serving each of [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), and a varied selection of cheese: [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel). Three more bottles each of wine and alto wine can be found, one of each on the armor table, end table, and small dining table. The small dining table also holds an additional loaf of bread.

## Notes
- The front door has an apprentice-level lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Faendal%27s_House) to which is carried by Faendal. The key can be freely taken from him if he becomes your [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).
- After completing the quest [A Lovely Letter](Skyrim_A_Lovely_Letter.md) in Faendal's favor, all low value items become free to take, and the bed becomes usable.
- Directly behind the southwest corner of the house is a [stone quarry](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md), if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.
- A respawning food barrel sits on the east side of the porch, and another respawning food barrel can be found just north of the potato plants in the garden.
- The chest under the armor table is safe storage, but none of the other containers are safe.
