It is within the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Despite having little significance on the surface, Ald Redaynia is a historical settlement, dating back as far as the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BTAOM-1)</sup> and once containing a small remote fishing village as far as [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTV-2)</sup>

## History
The site that would be Ald Redaynia was discovered in the middle [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), when [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorers mapped out the coasts of Vvardenfell, and establish wizard towers across the coast. A tower was erected at Ald Redaynia, as well as places such as [Bal Fell](Lore_Bal_Fell.md) and [Tel Mora](Lore_Tel_Mora.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BTAOM-1)</sup> For an unspecified amount of time, Ald Redaynia had hosted a remote fishing village, being one of the two found on the islands of Sheogorad (the other being [Dagon Fel](Lore_Dagon_Fel.md) to the east).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTV-2)</sup> At one point, a War-Wizard lived on the top level of Ald Redaynia, and wielded the [Vampiric Ring](Lore_Vampiric_Ring.md), an ancient artifact of immense evil created by a cult of [Vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> This War-Wizard eventually passed away, and his skeleton continued to live on in the tower, guarding the [Bittercup](Lore_Bittercup.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

In the late years of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), in [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlander.md) had discovered a baby on the shores of Ald Redaynia whom they believed was the [Nerevar Incarnate](Lore_Nerevarine.md). The [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) was named [Peakstar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Peakstar), and she attempted to fulfill the [Nerevarine Prophecy](Lore_Nerevarine_Prophecy.md), but later died at the hands of an [Ash Vampire](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> During [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) outbreak in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the ruined tower of Ald Redaynia contained the artifact known as the Bittercup, which is sacred to the followers of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md). A member of the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) was able to confiscate the Bittercup after defeating the Skeleton War-Wizard.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
