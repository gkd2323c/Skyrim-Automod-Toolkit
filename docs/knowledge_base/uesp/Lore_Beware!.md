Beware!
---
Climb the hill,

Unlatch the door.

Therein you shall find

Rare goods galore!

Whirring and hissing

And squeaking abound.

Could those be rats,

Or gears you have found?

Use items with care

In this noisy place,

Lest [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) awaken

And give you good chase!
