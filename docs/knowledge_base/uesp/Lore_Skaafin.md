Skaafin are a race of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) found in the [Fields of Regret](Lore_Fields_of_Regret.md) that serve [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Seryn-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Divine Intervention-2)</sup> In appearance, they have yellow skin, red eyes, long black horns on their head, with blonde, brown, or bald haircuts. Physiologically, they resemble [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), having pointed ears and similar facial structure, though they come in varying sizes between three to six feet tall. The Skaafin appear to have some form of a caste system; the taller Skaafin have more sophisticated [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and stronger armor, often serving as leaders. Skaafin smaller in stature are more feral, having a primitive vocabulary and fight using little more than their hands and magic.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ambient Dialogue-4)</sup>

When manifesting on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), Skaafin are known to corporealize rapidly out of [Chaotic Creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md), sprouting from the ground in orange-colored goo.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAVD-5)</sup> Skaafin can use a variety of spells, such as projectile flares, and masquers can create "Masques", spectral clones that explode to attack their target.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSkaafin-6)</sup> They are known to communicate using mirrors, showing what is on the other side of a different mirror instead of a reflection.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOMissing Prophecy-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> They have some respect for feats that are achieved through trickery or guile.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AMA-9)</sup> As shapeshifters, Clavicus Vile and [Barbas](Lore_Barbas.md) are known to take the form of Skaafin outside of their domain.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CV-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Barbas-11)</sup>

## History
In [2E 210](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_210), Magister Mavon Ules successfully bound a Skaafin named Mysissa after stumbling upon her [paleonymic](Lore_Paleonymic.md). Such spells were usually done to [Scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) and other lowly Daedra, and none had ever been attempted with Skaafin or similarly ranked Daedra, making Mavon's spell much more unique and powerful and cementing him as the Archmagister of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md). The bound Mysissa was prolific and detailed in her hatred of Mavon, detailing her life under his service despite being forbidden to write down any of his secrets. It is thought that she had a role in Mavon's disappearance and that she disposed of any evidence of her paleonymic before leaving the plane.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AMA-9)</sup>

During the Daedric War conspiracy in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the forces of [The Triad](Lore_Daedric_Triad.md) began using Skaafin as their minions on Tamriel. In [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), they sabotaged a [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) mission to Andrano Ancestral Tomb, with even [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) knowing so little about them beforehand that he described them as "strange Daedra".<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Divine Conundrum-12)</sup> They would soon after appear in Odirniran and Kaushtarari to retrieve [Sunna'rah](Lore_Sunna%27rah.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Divine Intervention-2)</sup> storming Seht's Vault after they obtained it.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Divine Restoration-13)</sup> Having entered the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), they were also found in the Ventral Terminus protecting Chancellor Gascone Dusant.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Deepening Shadows-14)</sup> In [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), they mysteriously appeared in the Monastery of Serene Harmony in [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md), later discovered to be the work of the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOQueens Decree-15)</sup>

## Notes
- When [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) visited the Fields of Regret in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), Clavicus Vile held a game for him to win [Iszara](Lore_Iszara.md)'s soul at the risk of losing his own soul, where each result was hidden between two doors. In order to figure out the riddle, Cyrus had to consult two Daedra referred to only as [guardians](Lore_Guardian_(Clavicus_Vile).md); one would always lie, and one would always tell the truth. These two Daedra had yellow skin, similarly to Skaafin.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Redguard-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Guardian-17)</sup>
