[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/OBR-place-Chorrol_03.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Chorrol_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Chorrol_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Chorrol_03.jpg) A statue of St. Osla Saint Osla,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBPrima-1)</sup><sup>:361</sup> also known as the **Saint of Sancre Tor**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gt C-2)</sup> was a [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) [healer](Lore_Restoration.md). She was present at the [Sack of Sancre Tor](Lore_Sack_of_Sancre_Tor.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBPrima-1)</sup><sup>:361</sup> an early battle during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md) that took place in [2E 852](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_852) at the city of [Sancre Tor](Lore_Sancre_Tor.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bo ST-3)</sup> She would later be canonized as an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [saint](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md) by [the Church](Lore_The_Church.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gt C-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBPrima-1)</sup><sup>:361</sup>

In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a statue titled "[The Touch of the Healer's Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Touch_of_the_Healer%27s_Hand)" was erected in the town of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), just inside the main gate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBPrima-1)</sup><sup>:361</sup> Known locally as "the fountain",<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> this statue depicts Saint Osla ministering to the fallen at the Sack of Sancre Tor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBPrima-1)</sup><sup>:361</sup> It was erected in memory of all who died in that great battle. The district near the town's main gate came to be known as Fountain Gate following its construction. At some point, the statue seemingly was indeed a fountain and was surrounded by a pool, but by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) it had dried up.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gt C-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
