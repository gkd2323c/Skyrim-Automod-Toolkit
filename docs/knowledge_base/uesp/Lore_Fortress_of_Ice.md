Shalidor would later recount the Fortress as among his most accomplished feats.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shalidor-1)</sup>

## History
Shalidor once made a deal with [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) in which he wagered [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md) for a chance at the [Folium Discognitum](Lore_Folium_Discognitum.md) to complete his work on [Glamoril](Lore_Glamoril.md). He lost the wager and came to regret it and vowed to get Eyevea back at any cost. He soon became so obsessed with his work to retrieve Eyevea that he ignored his wife entirely and left his home for days on end, resulting in her leaving him.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CCS-4)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) was composing a map to find the hidden location of [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md). A group of knights from the Fortress of Ice attacked a small caravan destined for the guild, containing a missing tablet that could be used to decipher a part of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) that would complete a map to Labyrinthian.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Thelen Kaarn-5)</sup> Thelen Kaarn, the leader of the Winterhold Mages Guild, enlisted the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) to retrieve the tablet from the Fortress of Ice, which was occupied by the thieving knights, as well as [snow wolves](Lore_Snow_Wolf.md) and [ice golems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ice_Golem). Upon obtaining the tablet and delivering it to Kaarn, the Champion was given the location of Labyrinthian, which contained the second piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md). <sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Arena-6)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Loyalist pilgrims of the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) that wanted to re-trace the steps of the Eternal Champion would visit the same locations the champion had traveled to on their journey.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
