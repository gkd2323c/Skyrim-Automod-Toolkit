# Cold-Flame Atronach

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/ON-creature-Cold-Flame_Atronach.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Cold-Flame_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Cold-Flame_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Cold-Flame_Atronach.jpg) A Cold-Flame Atronach Cold-Flame Atronachs are a powerful [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) native to a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) called the [Fourth Sinus of Takubar](Lore_Fourth_Sinus_of_Takubar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DDCFA-1)</sup> The cold-flame atronach summons an essence of cold fire, and has a freezing ambiance that notably drops the temperature nearby, the same way the [flame atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) gives off heat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DDCFA-1)</sup>

Cold-flame atronachs came to serve Molag Bal after an incident involving a flame atronach which led to their banishment from Coldharbour. A concerned denizen, [Denogorath the Dread Archivist](Lore_Denogorath.md) wanted a hovering sentry that possessed the flame atronach's same expressionless violence to fill the void they left behind.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DDCFA-1)</sup> After searching over 37,000 planes and realms, he found a suitable replacement on the Fourth Sinus of Takubar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DDCFA-1)</sup> Cold-flame atronachs served as sentries in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DDCFA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCFA-2)</sup>

Like other atronachs, it can be summoned in different forms: [Peregrine Direnni](Lore_Peregrine_Direnni.md) was the first to summon one in the shape of a [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CFWA-3)</sup>
