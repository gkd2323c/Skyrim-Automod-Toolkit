#### Oghma
[Oghma](Lore_Oghma.md), **the Wife of All Enigma**, is the wife of [Xarxes](Lore_Xarxes.md), god of ancestry, secrets, hidden knowledge, and death. They are both present in [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) and [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) religion. According to the Altmeri and Bosmeri tradition she was created by Xarxes from his favorite moments of history. She is associated with the Altmeri tradition of keeping records of one's life.

#### The One, the Supreme Spirit Akatosh
[The One](Lore_The_One.md) was the name of the Supreme Spirit worshipped by the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md), a strictly monotheistic cult devoted to singularity which ruled the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).

#### Onsi, Boneshaver
Notable warrior god of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md), Onsi taught Mankind how to pull their knives into swords.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup> He is mentioned several times in *[The Book of Circles](Lore_The_Book_of_Circles.md)* by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md), who wrote that those forging a sword should recite the "Oblation of Onsi" upon completion. A tale of King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md)'s youth claimed that Onsi personally saved him from a vengeful [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TABKJ-2)</sup>

#### Orkey, Old Knocker
A loan-god of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), who seem to have taken up his worship during [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) rule of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md). He combines aspects of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) and [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Vo F:TN-3)</sup> According to [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) scholars, the Nords believe they once lived as long as [Elves](Lore_Mer.md) until Orkey appeared; through heathen trickery, he fooled them into a bargain that "bound them to the count of winters", and the Nordic lifespan was reduced to six years due to Orkey's foul magic. Through unknown means, Shor showed up and removed the curse, throwing most of it onto the nearby [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup>

Nordic legend goes into greater detail with the story of Orkey and clarifies some of the details. Their stories claim that Orkey had long plagued them, and had stolen their years away while they were still in [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). During the reign of High King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), Orkey summoned [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md), and nearly every Nord was "eaten down" to six years old by the Time-Eater. King Wulfharth, who had become a boy, pleaded to [Shor](Lore_Shor.md) for help. Shor's own ghost then fought Alduin on the spirit plane, as he did at the beginning of time, and he won, and Orkey's folk, the Orcs, were ruined. It was by watching this battle in the sky that Wulfharth learned how to use the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) to save his people. His effort, while successful, cost him his life.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FSOKW-4)</sup>

#### Orkha
**Orkha** is a demon in the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) religion. It is said to have followed [Boethra](Lore_Boethra.md) back from her exile through the Many Paths. Orkha spoke solely in curses of affliction, and was banished by [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md), [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md), and Boethra, but would not die.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWS-6)</sup>

#### The Ox
The [Ox](Lore_Ox_(god).md) was an important figure in the mythology of certain [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) tribes of the [Deathlands](Lore_Deathlands.md). In their tradition, the People had Four Parents: the great [Dragon of Time](Lore_Akatosh.md), the [Mother Serpent](Lore_Mother_Serpent.md), the [Fat Mother](Lore_Fat_Mother.md), and the Ox. The Ox was revered as the parent who bears the People on its back to their final rest.
