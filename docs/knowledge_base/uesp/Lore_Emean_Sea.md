[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SI-place-Emean_Sea_02.jpg/200px-SI-place-Emean_Sea_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Emean_Sea_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Emean_Sea_02.jpg) The Emean Sea, west of the [Fringe](Lore_Fringe.md) The **Emean Sea** is the vast body of water that surrounds the entire western half of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It borders the [Fringe](Lore_Fringe.md) and the Isle of Flame, as well as running the entire length of the Laughing Coast. While the specific point where the Emean Sea ends and the [Enjaen Sea](Lore_Enjaen_Sea.md) begins are not entirely clear, they seem to merge between Saints Watch and the Isle of Flame, and [Heretic's Horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heretic%27s_Horn) and Madgod's Boot.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
