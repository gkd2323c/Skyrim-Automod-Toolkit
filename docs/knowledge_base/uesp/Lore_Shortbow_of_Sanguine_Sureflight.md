# Shortbow of Sanguine Sureflight

The Shortbow of Sanguine Sureflight is an [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) shortbow known to improve the archery skills of the wielder.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOSS-1)</sup> As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) it was found in the living quarters of the mushroom tower in the settlement of [Tel Aruhn](Lore_Tel_Aruhn.md) on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOSS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Threads of the Webspinner](Lore_Threads_of_the_Webspinner.md) is a collection of 27 pieces of extravagant enchanted clothing and jewelry associated with both [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) and [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Eno MW-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SI-4)</sup> Despite having a similar name, the Shortbow of Sanguine Sureflight is not considered to be one of the Threads.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TOTW-5)</sup>
