[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg) Rift Stormcloak Camp **Rift Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [camp](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Camps.md) in [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), located northeast of [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md) and west of [Rift Watchtower](Skyrim_Rift_Watchtower.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp_empty.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp_empty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp_empty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rift_Stormcloak_Camp_empty.jpg) Before Stormcloak occupation This camp will only be present if the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) hold [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and the rest of the Rift, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) are camped here under the command of [Gonnar Oath-Giver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gonnar_Oath-Giver), except during the related quest, when [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) takes command. Like similar military camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has no [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md).

## Rift Stormcloak Camp
It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Beside the campfire is an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). Around the area are three small tents containing unowned hay piles. South of the campfire is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers, a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), and an unlocked chest containing various items to treat the sick, including potions of [cure poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison), potions of [cure disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md), and [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips). To the right of the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md). To the left of the hospital tent are some barrels. East of the campfire is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a round table, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time. West of the campfire is the workstation for the [Stormcloak quartermaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster), consisting of a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). A long wooden table and a crate hold various [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Weapons) and [steel weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md). Northwest of the campfire is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). Northeast of the campfire is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and an end table.

Any time [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](Skyrim_Joining_the_Legion.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.
