Vampire Naturalist's Journal **by [Vampire Naturalist](Skyrim_Vampire_Naturalist.md)** *Notes on a cruel vampire's experiments in pain* *
---
While my siblings may be obsessed with the taste that fear imbues in the blood of mortals, I find that pain lends a much more potent sweetness. And nothing is quite so capable of causing pain as the [venom](Skyrim_Frostbite_Venom.md) of a [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md).

But alas, the usual method of harvesting frostbite venom involves killing the spider - a costly and dangerous venture. Far too little venom can be obtained through violent methods for the experiments I wish to conduct.

To that end, I have captured a frostbite spider from deeper in the [cave](Skyrim_Cronvangr_Cave.md) so that I may regularly collect its venom, which seems to be unusually potent. I discovered that it appears to have some measure of intelligence, and I have been attempting to teach it to obey certain orders. Oh how delicious it would be to have a tamed spider entangle my prey on command!

I think I will name it [Arachnia](Skyrim_Arachnia.md).
