[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-npc-Erwan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Erwan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erwan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erwan.jpg) Erwan Erwan is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) hedge wizard encountered at [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md) during the related quest. She and her bandit companions are hostile and will attack on sight.

Erwan is a former member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) and appears to be quite mentally disturbed; she has killed the merchant [Gunther](Skyrim_Gunther.md) and now considers his corpse to be her "best friend". Gunther had been sent her way by her brother Edward as a "gift", along with a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v5](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v5)* which describes her earlier life.

She wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and wields an [elven war axe](Skyrim_Elven_War_Axe.md). She also carried a [Dwarven dagger](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md), a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow), and eight [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), as well as her [journal](Skyrim_Erwan%27s_Journal.md) and a [letter from Edward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Edward). She utilizes [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) spells in combat such as [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md). You will be required to kill her and read her journal as part of the related quest.
