[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Hod.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hod.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hod.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hod.jpg) Hod at the sawmill **Hod** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), operating the wood mill there. He lives on the southeast edge of town and works his small farm and the mill with his wife, [Gerdur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gerdur). They have a son named [Frodnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar).

He will buy firewood for 5 gold per piece, and will also sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed. If his disposition is raised high enough, he will also allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

Hod wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Gerdur%27s_House) to his house as well as [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe).

## Conversations
Hod can be seen having a conversation with his wife Gerdur at their mill:

**Gerdur**: *"Can the sawmill handle those logs? They look a might * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * big."*
**Hod**: *"Ay, it can. Wouldn't want them any bigger though."*
**Gerdur**: *"And the blade?"*
**Hod**: *"Gettin' dull. We'll need a new one soon."*
**Gerdur**: *"I'll talk to [Alvor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor) about getting us a new one."*

In the evening, Hod will go to the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md). Depending on who is present with him in the inn, he may have conversations with three different people.

[Alvor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor) may ask Hod for some raw materials for smithing:

**Alvor**: *"I got a new order in for shields. I'm gonna need some hardwood planks. None of that soft pine this time."*
**Hod**: *"Ay, we can get that for ya. We just finished the timber for those catapults."*
**Alvor**: *"War may be good for business, but it's hard on families."*
**Hod**: *"A hard truth, that is. Let's drink to the fallen ones."*

Hod advises [Camilla](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md) about her [love triangle](Skyrim_A_Lovely_Letter.md) with [Faendal](Skyrim_Faendal.md) and [Sven](Skyrim_Sven.md):

**Hod**: *"You need to choose between those two boys. It ain't fair to Sven or Faendal to keep tuggin' on their heart strings like that."*
**Camilla**: *"It's bad enough my brother is harping on me to pick one. Don't you start too."*
**Hod**: *"I'm just sayin' you should pick one to settle down with."*

[Embry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Embry) goes up to Hod and asks for a drink:

**Embry**: *"How's about a drink, Hod."*
**Hod**: *"You'll be wantin' me to pay for them?"*
**Embry**: *"It's only right seein' as your * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * the important lumber man."*
**Hod**: *"You come do an honest day's work at my mill, and I'll pay you an honest day's wages. Then we'll drink."*

Hod can also order a drink from Delphine or Orgnar:

**Hod**: *"I'll have some mead."* **Orgnar:** *"Coming right up."* **or** **Delphine:** *"Certainly."*
## Bugs
- Hod doesn't go to work each day. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.

- If he is not at the mill at 8am, entering his house (legally or not) should prompt him to make his way to the mill. - If Hod is not at the mill during business hours, use the lumber pile, then the lever, then wait one hour. He shows up during this time if Hearthfire is installed because Hod must be present when using his mill.
- The mill that Hod works on is sometimes put into an infinite usage cycle, rendering the entire mill unusable by you for the remainder of the game save. This may be related to the fact that Hod has trouble showing up to work. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Hod may stand in his front yard and perform his lumber mill working motions.
