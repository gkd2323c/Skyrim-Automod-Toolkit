# Royal Guard

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/TR-prerelease-Mournhold_01.jpg/200px-TR-prerelease-Mournhold_01.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-prerelease-Mournhold_01.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-prerelease-Mournhold_01.jpg) A Royal Guard patrolling the Royal Palace Courtyard (Tribunal) The Royal Guard was a private and elite group of guards dedicated to protecting [Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Hlaalu_Helseth.md), from when he was the Prince of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) to when he became the King of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They are made up of strong and capable warriors picked directly by Helseth for their loyalty and bravery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRTienius-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRMero-2)</sup> Their original leader was **Tienius Delitian**, who had known Helseth since he was a child in Emperor [Uriel VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md)'s court. Not only was he an accomplished soldier, but he was politically savvy and an excellent judge of character.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRMero-2)</sup>

Their armored uniform is a medium set of red plates/purple chainmail with galea-style visors, while their non-armor house uniform is a simple black cloak, dark red sun tunic, and breeches.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGA-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-4)</sup> They also wield a blade called the King's Oath, [daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) claymore given only to those that swear an oath to the King after undergoing great trials. It also bears a curse that kills thieves and traitors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRTienius-1)</sup>

While its leader, Tienius Delitian had known Helseth as a child, other members of the Royal Guard had been following him since his time in [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRMero-2)</sup> When he lost his power struggle for the kingdom to his step-sister, [Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md), he and his mother, [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md) were banished from the kingdom. So his Royal Guard followed suit to Morrowind, where Helseth became its new King and stayed in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AGAD-5)</sup> The Royal Guard was based in the city's Royal Palace and had their own quarters.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRPalace-6)</sup> Their presence in the city brought them contempt from the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Mournhold_Temple.md) and the city's patron Tribunal, [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), who believed that the [High Ordinators](Lore_High_Ordinators.md) were better peacekeepers then King Helseth's "handpicked thugs".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRGavas-7)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TRAlmalexia-8)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) appeared in Mournhold after King Helseth failed to assassinate them.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TRBrotherhood-9)</sup> They decided to work with Tienius Delitian on matters related to the King, such as rumors about his several assassinations and finding what the Temple really thinks about him.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TRSpeak-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TRInformant-11)</sup> They also looked into conspiracies against Helseth, like a traitor in the Royal Guards' ranks, and discovered the journalist who spread rumors about the King.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TRDisloyalty-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TRMuckrake-13)</sup>

What became of the Royal Guard after that is unknown. Helseth's nephew, Goranthir served in the Royal Guard for a time in the late Third Era.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-LRPMM-14)</sup> Mournhold was sacked in [4E 6](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_6) during the [Accession War](Lore_Accession_War.md) and the capital was moved to [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-DBAdril-15)</sup> By the start of the third century, the Royal Palace was empty.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TMFN-16)</sup>
