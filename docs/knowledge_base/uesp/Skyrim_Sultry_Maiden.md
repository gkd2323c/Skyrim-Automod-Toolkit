[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-npc-Sultry_Maiden.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sultry_Maiden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sultry_Maiden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sultry_Maiden.jpg) Sultry Maiden The **Sultry Maiden** is the third piece from Pelagius' past created after using the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) on the terrors during [Pelagius' Night Terrors](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md#Pelagius.27_Night_Terrors). The third time you use the Wabbajack on [Pelagius](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Tormented.md), a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) will appear which, upon another use of the Wabbajack, will be turned into one of Pelagius' sultry maidens from his early days.

## Related Quests
- **[The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md)**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](Skyrim_Pelagius_Wing.md).

## Notes
- Her Editor ID refers to her as the "Mare Maiden" instead.
- While she's a unique NPC in most regards, the Sultry Maiden has her facial data completely copied over from [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md).

## Bugs
- The Sultry Maiden's face is discolored from her body due to broken facegen data. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13383))
- The Sultry Maiden also features a male voice, and may speak of having lost her family members, all generic lines apparently meant for unrelated cut content. This only happens if she speaks to you as you get close to her, as you are unable to initiate a dialogue. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13383))
