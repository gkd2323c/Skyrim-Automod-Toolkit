## Related Quests
- **[Animal Extermination](Skyrim_Animal_Extermination_(B).md)**: Fulfill a contract given to the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) to clear out a beast den. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Shalidor's Insights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Insights)**: Discover the writings of Shalidor and benefit from his wisdom. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Walkthrough
As you approach the outcropping, a single predator will likely attack you. Once it is dealt with, you can safely search the creature's den. It appears a foolish [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md) decided to camp here and paid with his life. His body lies on the blood-splattered ground next to a fallen cooking spit. Some lootable skeletal remains are farther back next to two unusable bedrolls and a level-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). A copy of the *[Argonian Account, Book 2](Skyrim_Argonian_Account,_Book_2.md)* rests on a boulder beside the chest, with a random [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) next to it.

The hunter's lean-to on the right side of the outcropping shelters a total of ten [pelts](Skyrim_Animal_Hides.md#Unprocessed) of various animals. An [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md), a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), and a [bear trap](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) are near the lean-to. On the left side of the outcropping is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md). A [leg of goat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat), a piece of [horse meat](Skyrim_Horse_Meat.md), and some [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison) hang from the ceiling on a rope. Two clusters of [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md), an [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md), and a [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot) grow around the camp.

Follow the nearby stream south to find a [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) and a [gold ore vein](Skyrim_Gold_Ore_Vein.md) near the river. You may encounter a few more predators in the area as well.

## Notes
- Only the level-locked chest, skeletal remains, and apothecary's satchel respawn. All other items, including the hunter's body, only appear on your first visit. Therefore, any radiant quest that requires recovering an item from this location can be completed even after it has been cleared.
