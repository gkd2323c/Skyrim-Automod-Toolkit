[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Abibon-Gora is found Abibon-Gora (also known as **Albion-Gora**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOR-1)</sup> is a region that rests on the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It is a humid subtropical region with beaches and grasslands on its coastlines, and forests throughout its interior.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Climate Map-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup> It shared borders with [Kairou](Lore_Kairou.md) to the north and the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r.md) to the east when it was a barony in the Third Era. It is unknown how far south it goes, but the entire region from Abibon-Gora down to the peninsula of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md) is a lush plant-filled rainforest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-4)</sup> [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) predominate the region, with [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md) as the favorite deity. Sophists and mystics at his chapel engage in friendly debates during gatherings.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Iyosias-5)</sup> The capital city is [the city of Abibon-gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-D1-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-D2-7)</sup> and the locality is protected by the [Knights of the Wheel](Lore_Knights_of_the_Wheel.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-D3-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DC-9)</sup><sup>:100</sup>

After Redguards moved into the area, the local [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) found that their methods of criminal justice provided a good source of material for experiments in necromancy. Despite efforts from the Redguards to rid themselves of the Sload, reports of them surviving as far north as Abibon-Gora lasted into the Third Era.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-10)</sup>

The people of the region celebrate a holiday called [Drigh R'Zimb](Lore_Drigh_R%27Zimb.md) on the 1st of Mid Year, a festival held in honor of the [sun](Lore_Sun.md). No other Redguards worship the sun on this day, so scholars have suggested that the holiday might be a direct translation of older Redguard festivals from before their migration to Tamriel from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DH-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-HOIB-12)</sup> They are also well known for their martial arts. Some swings and stances were developed in Albion-Gora, and many counter-attacks, feints, and blocks were imported from Yokuda.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOR-1)</sup>

After the Warp in the West, Abibon-Gora became a part of the Kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-10)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TWit W-13)</sup>
