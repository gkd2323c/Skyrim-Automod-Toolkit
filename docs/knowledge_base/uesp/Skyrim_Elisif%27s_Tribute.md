## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md).
2. Place [Torygg's War Horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torygg%27s_War_Horn) at the shrine of Talos [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=52654&y=8111&zoom=16&legacy=true).
3. Return to Elisif.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Personal Request
After you complete [The Man Who Cried Wolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf), [Elisif's](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md) goodwill towards you has increased. So much so that she extends a tentative trust in you by making a personal request regarding her late husband, [High King Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md). She will tell you, *"When we buried my husband I made offerings to all the gods... except [Talos](Lore_Talos.md)."* This is because Talos worship is outlawed in the Empire. She would like you to take [Torygg's War Horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torygg%27s_War_Horn) and place it at a specific [Shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos). Although she doesn't worship Talos herself, her husband would have wanted a proper [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) burial done in this fashion.

### The Shrine of Talos
Travel to the Shrine of Talos in the wilderness of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md), due north of [The Ritual Stone](Skyrim_The_Ritual_Stone.md). Follow the path near [Shimmermist Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shimmermist_Cave), along the northern bank of the river, and you can find the shrine behind some trees. As you approach the shrine, [Agent Lorcalin](Skyrim_Agent_Lorcalin.md) and a [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) soldier will appear and attack you, as Lorcalin is under [orders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lorcalin%27s_Orders) from the [Thalmor emissary](Skyrim_Elenwen.md) to kill any Talos worshippers he can find. Once the horn is placed in front of the statue of Talos and the quest updates, you are free to take it away at any time without affecting the quest's progress, and it will no longer be marked as a quest item.

### The Jarl's Good Grace
Return to Elisif at the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) where she will reaffirm her belief in you and allow you to purchase [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) as well as take the next step to becoming a [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md).

## Notes
- Prior to completing the prerequisite quest, the horn can be pickpocketed from Elisif. It will be marked as a quest item and cannot be removed from your inventory without completing the quest.
- Fighting the Thalmor is not necessary to finish the quest; they can be [calmed](Skyrim_Calm.md) or evaded in order to activate the statue's base.
- The Thalmor will always spawn the first time you are at the shrine, regardless of whether you're doing the quest.

## Bugs
- The Thalmor may not spawn at all because of a random encounter such as a dragon attack, although this doesn't affect the quest.
- Alternatively, the Thalmor may spawn at the shrine itself, frequently sitting on one of the benches.
