[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-place-Serpent%27s_Bluff_Redoubt.jpg/200px-SR-place-Serpent%27s_Bluff_Redoubt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpent%27s_Bluff_Redoubt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpent%27s_Bluff_Redoubt.jpg) Serpent's Bluff Redoubt **Serpent's Bluff Redoubt** is a small [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) [camp](Skyrim_Camps.md) and ruin southwest of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). It contains only one zone, *[Serpent's Bluff Ruins](#Serpent.27s_Bluff_Ruins)*.

The campsite is solely inhabited by Forsworn, while their [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) matriarch and several more Forsworn allies reside within the ruins.

## Related Quests

#### [Radiant Quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md)
- **[Bounty: Forsworn](Skyrim_Bounty__Forsworn.md)**: Kill a [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) leader for a bounty reward.
- **[The Ghost of Old Hroldan](Skyrim_The_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan.md)**: Put an [ancient ghost](Skyrim_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan.md) to rest.
- **[Kill the Forsworn Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Forsworn_Leader_(Jarl).md)**: Do a favor for [Jarl Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md) by killing a [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) leader.
- **[Lisbet's Missing Shipment](Skyrim_Lisbet%27s_Missing_Shipment.md)**: Retrieve a missing Dibella Statue for a struggling store.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The redoubt is atop a steep incline leading southwest from Rorikstead. The campsite is home to numerous Forsworn, including a Forsworn Briarheart. The lower part of the camp has three tents containing bedrolls around a campfire in the middle. The first tent also contains an end table with a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) on top. The third contains a small unit with a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), one of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), and a random [potion of healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md) on the shelves. To the right are two more tents, with a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) and [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) between them. One tent contains an apprentice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) with a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) propped up against it along with a bedroll and table. The other tent has a table with two random [two-handed weapons](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md) and a random [heavy helmet](Skyrim_Armor.md#Helmets_2). To the left is a bookcase holding two common books including a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](Skyrim_The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle.md)* and a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). Beside this is a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md).

Farther uphill are two more tents separated by a pair of long wooden tables and benches. On the tables are an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) stabbed into a tactical map of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) at [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). The tent to the left is where the Briarheart sleeps, and contains a boss-level chest at the foot of a double bed with a set of shelves beside it holding a [leg of goat roast](Skyrim_Leg_of_Goat_Roast.md), a [potato](Skyrim_Potato.md), a [deer hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md), and a random potion of healing. On either side of the entrance are a dresser and a small unit. The dresser has an [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md) and random enchanted [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md) on top, while the unit holds a [sliced goat cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Goat_Cheese.md) and a medium coin purse. The second tent contains only a bedroll and a wooden bench, with a flight of stairs to the right that leads to the entrance to *Serpent's Bluff Ruins*. West of the camp, near a ruined tower, is a chest.

### Serpent's Bluff Ruins
There are six enemies (including a hagraven boss) and many traps inside this small area. The entrance leads to a well-lit passage that turns to the northeast and descends a flight of stairs. At the bottom is a corridor with a wooden table on the left with a copy of *[The "Madmen" of the Reach](Skyrim_The_%22Madmen%22_of_the_Reach.md)*, a random [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), a set of [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md), and a leveled [potion of resist shock](Skyrim_Potion_of_Resist_Shock.md). There is a pressure plate on the floor that is easily spotted that triggers a [poison dart trap](Skyrim_Dart_Trap.md). The corridor continues to the southeast with barred openings on the left that enable you to see into the main chamber ahead. There is a [tripwire](Skyrim_Tripwire.md) partway along this passage that triggers a [spear trap](Skyrim_Spear_Trap.md) from the side wall. To the right of the door at the end of the passage is a Forsworn sitting in a chair with a common book at their feet. On your right, just after the tripwire, is a dresser partially buried in rubble containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) with a random [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) on top. Past this is a second pressure plate that triggers a [spike wall](Skyrim_Spike_Wall.md). To the right of the door is a large fireplace with a random [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md) on the ground in front of it. The door is unlocked and leads into another narrow passage with stairs leading down. At the bottom there is yet another pressure plate; this one triggers a [battering ram](Skyrim_Battering_Ram.md) ahead. At the end of the passage, you will find a dresser that is covered in blood with an iron dagger on top. To the right is a caved-in section with a random [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) among the rubble. To the left is a doorway into the main chamber seen from above.

The main chamber contains two Forsworn and a hagraven. Both the hagraven and one of the Forsworn are on a raised section to the north, while the other Forsworn is wandering the lower level. In the middle of the lower level is a large stone table with a [weight-sensitive plate](Skyrim_Weight_Sensitive_Plate.md) and items of clutter. You will need to activate the plate by dropping an item onto it, as the door only remains open as long as the plate is activated; one of the otherwise useless items on the table can be used for this. In the eastern corner is an adept-locked door to a room under the upper level. The room contains a cupboard with a large coin purse and three common books on the shelves, a [steel warhammer](Skyrim_Steel_Warhammer.md) on the floor, and a set of shelves that are partially buried in rubble holding a set of random [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md), a small coin purse, and two common books. Finally, at the rear of the room is an apprentice-locked chest containing minor loot.

Climbing the stairs, at the top is a barrel containing [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and a plinth with a *[Spell Tome: Frostbite](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Frostbite.md)*. Once you are past the wooden gate, there is a T-junction with a table opposite with a random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) or [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md) on top. To the right is an open door with a barrel of ingredients to the right and inside the room there is a random [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) on the floor by the southeast wall. To the left is a pile of straw with five [hagraven feathers](Skyrim_Hagraven_Feathers.md) among it. In the eastern corner, in front of the head of an elk with mammoth tusks wrapped around it, is an unlocked boss leveled chest. To the left is a cupboard with three common books, a bowl of [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md), two pieces of [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md), a [nightshade](Skyrim_Nightshade.md) flower, two random [potions of magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), and an empty random [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) on the shelves. Against the northwest wall is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) with a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Twin Secrets](Skyrim_Twin_Secrets.md)* on top. Next to this is a wooden table with a [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), a wooden bowl containing two [slaughterfish eggs](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Egg.md), and two empty random soul gems on top. Back in the passage there is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and a [garlic braid](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md). Taking the left fork at the T-junction there is a pair of random [boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots) on the floor near the top of a flight of stairs leading down. At the bottom is another wooden gate with a button on the right that lowers it. Beyond is a natural tunnel containing four [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md), three [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), and two [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) fungi; this tunnel leads to the exit which is southwest of the entrance.

## Notes
- To open the gate, you need to activate the plate. While looking at an item, press and hold down the action button; instead of looting the item, this will allow you to easily drag it on top of the pressure plate. You can also walk on the plate and then use the [Slow Time](Skyrim_Slow_Time.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) to pass through the open gate.
- An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) can be found north-northwest of camp, right next to the rock underneath the camp fortifications.
- This is the only Forsworn camp not located in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md).
- Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) assigns Serpent's Bluff Redoubt to the Reach instead.
