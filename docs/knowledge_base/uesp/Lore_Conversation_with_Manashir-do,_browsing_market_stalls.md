Conversation with Manashir-do, browsing market stalls **by [Manashir-do](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Manashir-do)** *The opinions of a Khajiit looking for suitable armor*
---
[![ON-concept-Khajiit medium armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/0d/ON-concept-Khajiit_medium_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Khajiit_medium_armor.png)
- “No, no, no. Hmmm. Not cloth—too many threads to chase, too much to get in the way and distract. Pretty, though. Much better for relaxing and enjoying sweets in than for fighting. Very nice embroidery, very fine.

- Metal? Only if [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) wants to let the enemy know that he's approaching! I am a skilled fighter, yes, but no need to give free warnings. And how would I stretch and bend? Everyone knows it is better to dodge than to be hit, better to pounce than lumber slowly forward. Ah! What about this? Sturdy but flexible, room around the joints ... does it match my spots?”
