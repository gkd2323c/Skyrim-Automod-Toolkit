[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-npc-Belchimac.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Belchimac.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belchimac.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belchimac.jpg) Belchimac **Belchimac** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives and works in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md).

He sleeps in [Karthwasten Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) from midnight to 8am, and works in [Sanuarach Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanuarach_Mine) between 8am and 8pm. He spends the rest of the evening relaxing in the barracks.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Karthwasten_Miners%27_Barracks) to the miner's barracks and a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Heart of Dibella
When asked if he knows where a [young girl](Skyrim_Fjotra.md) who lives here is:

- **I'm looking for a young girl who lives around here.**
- *"Yeah, we're all looking for something."*

## Dialogue
Before you reopen the mines, he'll be depressed, saying:

- *"The good mine is closed and the [other mine](Skyrim_Fenn%27s_Gulch_Mine.md) has no ore. Gods have cursed me."*

He'll keep his attitude when they're open:

- *"Sauranach * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * mine is open again. How am I supposed to handle all this work?"*

## Conversations
He can often be seen talking to other miners:

- **[Enmon](Skyrim_Enmon.md)**: *"When are those [Silver-Blood men](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver-Blood_Mercenary) going to leave?"*
- **Belchimac**: *"Never. They've shut us down forever, Enmon. The Divines have abandoned us."*

- **Belchimac**: *"We're doomed. The mine is going to stay closed, and we'll up * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * be carted of * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * to [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) as slaves."*
- **Enmon**: *"Only criminals get sent to Cidhna Mine. They're not going to send us there."*
- **Belchimac**: *"Oh, they'll find some way to send us there. It'll be just my luck."*

- **Belchimac**: *"Damn [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md). Always getting in the way of us honest folk."*
- **[Ragnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar)**: *"What did you say? You lazy coward, I'll cut your throat."*
- **Belchimac**: *"Nothing, Ragnar. Please... don't hurt me."*
- **Ragnar**: *"I... I'm sorry, Belchimac. I didn't mean that."*

- **Ragnar**: *"You're always talking about the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). You aren't thinking of joining them, are you?"*
- **Belchimac**: *"Maybe I will. What do you think about that?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"I'm warning you. Those people are killers. You'd never last a day with them."*

- **Belchimac**: *"I don't like bunking with [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md). It's not... clean."*
- **[Lash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh)**: *"If you want me to leave, you could challenge me to a fight. I promise to stop once you've lost an arm."*
- **Belchimac**: *"What I meant to say was... I love having an Orc here in the barracks. They're so... pleasant."*

- **Lash**: *"Why do your people claim the [Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) is theirs? The Orcs have been in these lands just as long."*
- **Belchimac**: *"You wouldn't understand, Lash. Your people aren't smart enough for politics."*
- **Lash**: *"Is that so? I'll remember that next time we're in the mine tunnels... alone."*
- **Belchimac**: *"Did... did I say you weren't smart? I meant to say that I'm not smart, Lash. I'm just not smart enough to answer your questions."*
