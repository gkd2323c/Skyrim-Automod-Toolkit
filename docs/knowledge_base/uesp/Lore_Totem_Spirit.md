# Totem Spirit

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/ON-creature-Atrozu.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Atrozu.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Atrozu.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Atrozu.jpg) Atrozu, a totem spirit of Kalmur Clan (ESO) Totem Spirits are spiritual beings manifested through unity and family bonds.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vo C-1)</sup> They are physical manifestations of clans and families. They embody memories of the past, bonds between clan members, and collective hopes for the future.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khazasha-2)</sup>

Some [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) clans, such as the Kalmur Clan, were known to commune with totem spirits. The spirit of their clan, [Atrozu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atrozu) assumed the shape of a [bear](Lore_Bear.md) and was typically protected by the clan wise woman, who in turn communed with it for advice when necessary.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khazasha-2)</sup>

## Known Totem Spirits
