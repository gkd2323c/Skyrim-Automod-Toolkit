[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/DF-map-Satakalaam.png/200px-DF-map-Satakalaam.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Satakalaam.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Satakalaam.png) Satakalaam ca. 3E 405 Satakalaam was a county on the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), on the eastern coast of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). To its east lay [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth_(fiefdom).md); to the south, [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md); while [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md) was situated to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> Its [provincial seat](Lore_Satakalaam.md) shared the same name.

On 3rd of Sun's Dusk, the region celebrated a holiday named "Serpent's Dance", which seemed to celebrate a now-forgotten serpent god dating back to the time when the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) lived in their former home of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Hot IB-2)</sup> In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md) bloodline predominated among [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) in the region.
