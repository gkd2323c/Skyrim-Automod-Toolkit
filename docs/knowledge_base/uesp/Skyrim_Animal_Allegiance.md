**Animal Allegiance** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) that causes nearby animals to fight for you.

## Related Quests
- **[Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde_(quest).md)**: Discover the treasure of [Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde.md).
- **[Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md)**: Cure [Kodlak](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md)'s spirit and become Harbinger of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).

## Possible Uses
- Evening the odds if outnumbered.
- Forcing peaceful animals to stop so they can be killed while hunting.
- Forcing [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) and [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) to attack their [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) keepers.
- Turning [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) against their [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md).

## Notes
- When the shout is used and a follower is already actively fighting an animal, the animal will continue to fight your follower.
- The shout penetrates doors and walls, so it can be used to start a fight between chaurus and Falmer in areas of caves that you have not yet accessed.
- This shout cannot be used to directly counter a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md)'s similar power after the spriggan has already cast it, as the original power takes precedence.
- The word wall at [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) can be reached by [climbing the mountain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing), bypassing the need to complete [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md).
