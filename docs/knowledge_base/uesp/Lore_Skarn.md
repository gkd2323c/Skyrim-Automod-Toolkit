# Skarn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/ON-concept-Firesong_Style.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Firesong_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Firesong_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Firesong_Style.jpg) Firesong Armaments [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Firesong_Skarn.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Firesong_Skarn.png) Firesong Skarn Skarn is a metamorphic rock. The [Druids](Lore_Druids.md) of the [Firesong Circle](Lore_Firesong_Circle.md) were known to craft their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor from volcanic rock and living skarn sourced from the [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md), their sacred site, using it for both protection and adornment. They shaped the skarn using volcanic hammers and [obsidian](Lore_Obsidian.md) implements.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Style-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FST-3)</sup>
