[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/SR-npc-Rin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rin.jpg) Rin Rin is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) added by the [Farming](Skyrim_Farming.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). He is the son of [Jonquil](Skyrim_Jonquil.md) and [Urval](Skyrim_Urval.md). He appears as a child's skeleton at the well east of [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md), and as a [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) inside the plantation house.

## Related Quests
- **[The Unquiet Dead](Skyrim_The_Unquiet_Dead.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Farming.md)</sup>: Lay the spirits of Goldenhills Plantation to rest.

## Quest-Related Events
After reading [his journal](Skyrim_Rin%27s_Journal.md), you will be directed to find the old well, located to the east of Goldhills Plantation. There, a child's skeleton can be found, along with his wooden sword. Returning the wooden sword to the plantation house and placing it on the table in his room will grant peace to Rin and his family's spirits, causing them to appear in the dining area. Speak to Rin to complete the quest.

- **Your family can rest in peace now.**
- *"Thanks a lot! Here. Take this. For all your hard work."*

Rin and his parents then dematerialize, ending the quest.
