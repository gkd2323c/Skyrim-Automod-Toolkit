[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly.jpg) Hela's Folly, shipwreck and campsite **Hela's Folly** is a [shipwreck](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks) northeast of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). The fast travel point is just to the south of the bow of the wreck. The camp is a few paces to the west-southwest.

[Salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md) and [Arctic Chars](Skyrim_Arctic_Char.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> can be found swimming in the waters here.

## Hela's Folly
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hela%27s_Folly_02.jpg) Deekus' campsite There is a makeshift camp next to the wreck which is the home of an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) named [Deekus](Skyrim_Deekus.md). Everything in the camp is marked as owned and is counted as stealing if taken, even after you kill him. The camp consists of a lean-to, beneath which are a bedroll, a [Dibella statue](Skyrim_Dibella_Statue.md), and an open crate containing two [amethysts](Skyrim_Amethyst.md), a [sapphire](Skyrim_Sapphire_(item).md), and a filled [lesser soul gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem). To the right of the lean-to is a novice-locked chest "protected" by two [bear traps](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) —one in front and one behind. There are three crates between the chest and a campfire, which has three [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) [cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md) over it. To the north is another crate with a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a loose [coin](Skyrim_Gold.md), an amethyst, and an [emerald](Skyrim_Emerald.md) on top. There are two barrels by the crate, and on top of one of them are a [soul gem fragment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment) and a sapphire. On the shore, to the east of the wreck is a [clam](Skyrim_Clam.md) that can be harvested for [clam meat](Skyrim_Clam_Meat.md).

Another chest can be found underwater in the northwest part of the ship. There are also several gems underwater, almost directly underneath the chest. This includes one [ruby](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby) and one sapphire on the lower deck of the submerged half, between some crates near a plant.

## Notes
- If you are sent here to retrieve the [Helm of Winterhold](Skyrim_Helm_of_Winterhold.md) for [Jarl Korir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jarl_Korir), it will be in the chest owned by Deekus, and thus considered theft. Korir will still accept it to complete the quest, however.
