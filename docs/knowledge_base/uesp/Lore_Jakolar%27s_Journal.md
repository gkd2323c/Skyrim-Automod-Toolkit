Jakolar's Journal **by [Jakolar](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Jakolar)** *The record of an old Nord's efforts to resurrect his dead wife*
---
— Forced to leave [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). Jarl [Svargrim](Lore_Svargrim.md) believes the method lost, my research pointless. That's fine. I'm not doing this for him.

— Thirty days since. I've traveled south and east, visiting as many barrows as I can. The [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) are no threat to me, though the stronger ones take much of my energy to dominate fully. I need rest, but there is no time.

— Turned away at the gates of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md). Despite my care with the body, even the enchantments to dim the smell, it seems most are not comfortable with an old [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) carrying his wife's corpse into the town square.

— I saw her this time. A ghostly figure that cried out for me to stop. I tried to tell her it has to be this way. I know it must be tormenting her to be so bound, but it is only temporary. Soon she will live on eternally. I will never lose her again.

— Arrived at [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md). I kept the body and my supplies hidden just outside the village. No one here knows anything about the draugr curse that could help me, but no matter. At nightfall, I will move into the barrow and see what I can learn. At the very least, it will give me a place to rebind her.

I'm sorry, my dear. Just a little longer.
