# Turquoise

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-trait_material-Turquoise.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-trait_material-Turquoise.png) Turquoise **Turquoise** is a blue/green gemstone often used on weaponry and for decorative purposes. It is known to be mined in [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Turquoise glass treated with glow-spells is sometimes passed off as [Welkynd Stones](Lore_Welkynd_Stones.md) by scam artists.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> For the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), the three turquoise discs arranged vertically symbolize the [Three Kinetic Forces](Lore_Conclave_of_the_Eleven_Forces.md#Eleven_Forces) represented in archery. Some of their quivers are decorated with these designs, and some of their belt buckles are embellished with them as well.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM61-3)</sup>
