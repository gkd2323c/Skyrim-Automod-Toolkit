## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/ON-place-Chalman_Keep.jpg/200px-ON-place-Chalman_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Chalman_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Chalman_Keep.jpg) Chalman Keep circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/LG-cardart-Battle_of_Chalman_Keep.png/200px-LG-cardart-Battle_of_Chalman_Keep.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Battle_of_Chalman_Keep.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Battle_of_Chalman_Keep.png) The Battle of Chalman Keep During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Chalman was an expansive and strategically important keep. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was initially located within the territory held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), and its central location made it essential for control of the Imperial City.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> A notable three-way battle known as the Battle of Chalman Keep<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> occurred here between Pact, [Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) and [Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) forces, when the fort was taken from the Covenant by a Dominion invasion force, after which the Dominion crowned a figurehead [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) from among their warriors.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The keep changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> Among the veterans of the battles at Chalman Keep were two who would later become members of the Stirk Fellowship. One was a forces' [blacksmith](Lore_Smithing.md) who had lost his leg to a soldier during the fighting there. This led to infighting within the faction, which was composed of representatives from all three alliances.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LATSFAS-5)</sup>

The fort was abandoned by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) during the relatively peaceful [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Fort Chalman was no more than a ruin infested with undead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup> It was among the ruins explored by [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) adventurers that were sent out by [Gortwog](Lore_Gortwog.md) to seek treasure for the glory of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OBAdventurer-7)</sup>
