# Ateia

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-item-Pantea%27s_Flute.jpg/200px-SR-item-Pantea%27s_Flute.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Pantea%27s_Flute.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Pantea%27s_Flute.jpg) An Ateian Flute (Skyrim) The Ateia family is an old and famous [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) clan of flutists that often contribute to the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), the western city of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). As stated before, they are known for their affinity with flutes and fifes. One of the earliest known members was the legendary flutesmith, Parteron Ateia, who made several well-revered flutes, which are mostly used, and even kept in secrecy by the Ateia family, though some are found all throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AF-1)</sup>

## History
One of their earliest known members was Parteron Ateia, the legendary flutesmith. He spent most of his life created many well-crafted flutes until his inevitable death. Before he died, he created the [Ateian Fife](Lore_Ateian_Fife.md) and donated it to the Bards College to be enjoyed for centuries.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AF-1)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Pantea Ateia was a dean and master performer at the Bards College, who even performed for the late High King, [Torygg](Lore_Torygg.md). In light of the [Skyrim Civil War](Lore_Skyrim_Civil_War.md), she canceled her musical tour to all of Skyrim's courts and supported the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) in their cause.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRPantea-2)</sup>

She was given an old flute that was passed down through her family for seventeen generations, which was later stolen by her former student, Larina.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRPantea-2)</sup> She sent an [adventurer](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) to retrieve the flute, which was tracked down to Hob's Fall Cave, an ice cavern and necromancer coven in the [glaciers](Lore_Ice_Fields.md) of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md). The flute was safety returned.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRFlute-3)</sup> Pantea Ateia later performed at the tragic wedding of [Vittoria Vici](Lore_Vittoria_Vici.md), in which the subject was assassinated by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRBound-4)</sup>
