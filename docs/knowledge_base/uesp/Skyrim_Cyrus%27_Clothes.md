Cyrus' Clothes are a set of unique clothes once worn by the legendary [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md). They can be found alongside [Cyrus' Boots](Skyrim_Cyrus%27_Boots.md) and [Cyrus' Sabre](Skyrim_Cyrus%27_Sabre.md) during [The Restless](Skyrim_The_Restless.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Restless](Skyrim_The_Restless.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup>: Search for an ancient pirate ship.

## Notes
- These clothes were originally worn by [Cyrus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Cyrus) in *[Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard)*. It is his only outfit in the game and cannot be unequipped. - A similar [Swift-Winds Sailor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Swift-Winds_Sailor) costume is available in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:ESO), which predates *Redguard* chronologically.
