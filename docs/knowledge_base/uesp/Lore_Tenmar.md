[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-concept-Tenmar_Forest.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Tenmar_Forest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Tenmar_Forest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Tenmar_Forest.jpg) Tenmar Forest [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/LO-map-Elsweyr_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Elsweyr_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](File_LO-map-Elsweyr_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg.md) [](File_LO-map-Elsweyr_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg.md) A map of Elsweyr showing the location of Tenmar Forest The Tenmar Forest, also known as the **Tenmar Jungle**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>, in southern [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) is a large region lying between [Torval](Lore_Torval.md), the seat of the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md), and the busy seaport of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup> Primarily known for the production and export of [moon sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moon_Sugar), its sugarcane groves are venerated and diligently guarded by the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup>

The forest is home to two subspecies of Khajiit rarely found elsewhere, the Dagi and Dagi-raht.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MUT-3)</sup> The Tenmar Forest once contained the ancient Kingdom of Tenmar, one of the original sixteen states of Elsweyr. It was known for its jungle and tree-folk.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sixteen-4)</sup>

Tenmar Senche-Tigers and Tenmar Senche-Leopards are fearsome predators of the Forest, however, the Leopards are also bred by Khajiit for hunting.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) named [Maarselok](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Maarselok), spread an infectious disease known as [Azure Blight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azure_Blight) to the local fauna and flora, which corrupted them, and caused them to become extremely aggressive to all. The source of the disease was from a mountain in Tenmar, and was destined to spread into [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) if not stopped. Fortunately, a group of [Undaunted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Undaunted) and a [changeling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Changeling) killed Maarselok.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> [Selene](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Selene) would subsequently go on to work on to work to cleanse the Azure Blight, which had persisted past the dragon's death, from the land.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Allanwen-7)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Tenmar Forest had its name loaned from the region. It was ruled by Baroness Rajjan. It had a rivalry with Portneu View and Darvulk Haven.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Arena-8)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Black Heights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Heights) in the east, and Chasegrove in the northwest.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Map Arena-9)</sup>
