[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma.jpg) Steel-Blue Entoloma [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma_%28Fertile_Soil%29.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma_%28Fertile_Soil%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma_(Fertile_Soil).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Steel-Blue_Entoloma_(Fertile_Soil).jpg) Steel-Blue Entoloma showing [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) compatibility **Steel-Blue Entoloma** is a mushroom found only in [Gromm's Pass](Skyrim_Gromm%27s_Pass.md), a cave added by the [Goblins](Skyrim_Goblins.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It is sometimes dropped by [Goblins](Skyrim_Goblin.md). It can also be purchased from the [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).

Notably, Steel-Blue Entoloma is fully compatible with the [Greenhouse](Skyrim_Greenhouse.md) mechanic introduced by [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md). This means that you can plant it in any plot of [Fertile Soil](Skyrim_Fertile_Soil.md) and harvest up to three samples of the ingredient from this domesticated variety.
