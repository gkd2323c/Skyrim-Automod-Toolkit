[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-npc-Attendant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Attendant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Attendant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Attendant.jpg) Attendant The **Attendant** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit wizard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enc Class Bandit Wizard) found in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md), where he works the [Redwater Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma) booths. He carries Redwater Skooma and a [Redwater Den Backrooms Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den_Backrooms_Key).

If you go through the quest [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md), he will appear as a [Vampire's Thrall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall) on your way out of the area, though he will go about his duties as normal.
