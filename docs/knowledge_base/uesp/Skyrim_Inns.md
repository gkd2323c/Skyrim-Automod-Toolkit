# Inns


Inns are good places to get quests: all [bounty quests](Skyrim_Bounty_Quests.md) are obtainable from the innkeepers, asking for rumors will lead you to certain quests, and many people who have [miscellaneous quests](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md) frequent the inns.

### Other
The following locations are either inns or taverns by common definition, but are not considered to be inns by the game. You will not receive the Well Rested bonus if you sleep at these locations, and quests that target inns (e.g., [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md)) will not target these locations.
