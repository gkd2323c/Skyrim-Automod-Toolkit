Talen-Lah's Journal **by [Talen-Lah](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Talen-Lah)** *Musings on the dark power within Naj-Caldeesh* *
---
The structure of this [xanmeer](Lore_Xanmeer.md) is odd. While others are built tall and proud, defying the many truths of [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) and standing in opposition to the natural order of decay, this one is carved into the earth. It is hidden and reeks of death. Plant death. Insect death. Even the slow-moving death of stone. It's not proud or tall, it's inviting and hidden. It feels like a tomb. It feels like home. Maybe that is what drew me here. After remaining a mystery for so long, Naj-Caldeesh is finally ready for occupants once again.

The true intention of xanmeers is lost. Those cowards who lived before had some purpose for them, but the ancient ways were flawed. Naj-Caldeesh though, the way it fills my spines with excitement, could only be used for one thing. Death. There's a power here that calls to my claws and invites me to harvest spirits from the earth.

Whatever their purpose for this xanmeer, the ancients protected it well. Stumbling across that fool Seekxilts was a gift from beyond the grave. Without the leaves of her incompetence, we never would have discovered the entrance to this structure. Of course, she didn't ask any questions about our motives or why we needed the xanmeer. That idiot should never walk with a tall spine again. Now Naj-Caldeesh is ours, not hers.

There is something deep within this xanmeer. Down by the roots. I can feel it. The heart of the xanmeer and the source of all its [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) power. Seekxilts theorized that there was something the ancients wanted to keep hidden. She thought whatever it held was somehow too dangerous for the ancients to leave unburied. I scoffed before, but now I know that she's right.

It's the keystone. The source of the pull I feel is bound to the rock that holds this entire structure together. Everything rests on the keystone and the original purpose of Naj-Caldeesh. I know my magic can bend the keystone to the whims of my faction, the Wuj-Laxul.
