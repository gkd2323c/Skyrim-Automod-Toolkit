[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/ON-place-Balefire_Island.jpg/200px-ON-place-Balefire_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Balefire_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Balefire_Island.jpg) Balefire Island ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-npc-Grivier_Bloodcaller.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Grivier_Bloodcaller.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Grivier_Bloodcaller.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Grivier_Bloodcaller.jpg) Grivier Bloodcaller Balefire Island is an island located off the coast of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md), north of [Eagle's Brook](Lore_Eagle%27s_Brook.md) and [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md). It was under the government of the province of the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BI-1)</sup>

At some point before the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) the isle was a place of a burial of a signifcant number of dead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AWORM-2)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the isle was infiltrated by Grivier Bloodcaller, a member of the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Grivier_Bloodcaller-3)</sup> a faction led by the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [gravesinger](Lore_Necromancy.md) [Angof](Lore_Angof.md), whose main goal was to conquer the region of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOG-4)</sup>

Grivier discovered that the isle served as the burial grounds in the past and began raising the dead for the Bloodthorn Cult's cause.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AWORM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Grivier_Bloodcaller-3)</sup> His machinations were stopped in the same year by a [wandering adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who defeated the necromancer,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Grivier_Bloodcaller-3)</sup> and partook in the defeat of the entire Bloodthorn Cult.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ATGQ-5)</sup>
