## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md).
2. *(Optional)* Speak to [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md).
3. *(Optional)* Enter using the sewer.
4. Proceed to [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md).
5. *(Optional)* Obtain the key to Aringoth's safe.
6. Burn three bee hives.
7. Open [Aringoth](Skyrim_Aringoth.md)'s safe.
8. Retrieve the [Goldenglow Bill of Sale](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Bill_of_Sale_(safe).md).
9. Return to Brynjolf.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Sweeter than Golden Honey
Once [Mercer](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md) has allowed you into the Guild, he'll challenge you with a new job. [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md) was once the Guild's sweetest deal. The Guild made sure that the honey flowed constantly to [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)'s [meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) by keeping the workforce in line and scaring off potential competitors. In return it was allowed to extort huge sums of money from [Aringoth](Skyrim_Aringoth.md), the owner. However, the shipments have suddenly ceased and when [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) was sent to investigate she found an army of mercenaries. Maven isn't pleased and it's your job to teach Aringoth a lesson by burning down three bee hives and clearing out his safe.

Before leaving, pay Vex a visit and she will tell you about a secret sewer entrance on the northwest side which will make it significantly easier to enter unseen.

### As Loud as an Angry Beehive...
Your first objective is to burn the beehives, which you can reach in two ways. The simplest route is to wade ashore on the northernmost island, dispatch the numerous mercenaries protecting the estate and then burn the hives. A stealthier solution is to wait until dusk, swim to the rocky outcrop to the west of the beehives and then clamber ashore, hidden by a small wooden wall.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_03.jpg) The secret sewer entrance leading into the estate To set the beehives on fire you can use a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md), the [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md) Shout, or a [fire-based spell](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md#Spells); simply standing next to a hive with a torch or spell equipped will suffice. A rapid execution is important as three mercenaries will come rushing to investigate, so quickly burn the three southernmost beehives and then retreat behind the wall. Jump back into the lake and circle around to the northern island. Be careful not to burn more than three beehives, or you will forfeit your gold reward.

### ...and as Silent as a Skeever
Wade ashore underneath the wooden platform on the northwestern end of the island and dive into the sewer grate nearby. The sewer is only inhabited by a few [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md#Skeever), three of which can be taken out by using an oil slick and some fire. About half-way through is an adept-lock cell where you'll find the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Guide to Better Thieving](Skyrim_Guide_to_Better_Thieving.md)*. After leaving that room continue down the corridor and you will find a ladder back to the surface. Climb it and you will resurface next to the back entrance of the estate, which has an expert level lock and lands you in a more favorable location than the front door.

Alternatively, you can sneak your way to the front door, which is unlocked, or take out the mercenaries and enter through either door.

### Breaking and Entering
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/SR-item-Queen_Bee_Statue.jpg/200px-SR-item-Queen_Bee_Statue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Queen_Bee_Statue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Queen_Bee_Statue.jpg) The Queen Bee Statue in Aringoth's quarters Inside the estate you will find numerous mercenaries which you can either kill or sneak around. You should eventually arrive at a corridor patrolled by a mercenary while two more sit at a table. Avoid them by sneaking into the nearby dining room and you'll find yourself in front of a gate leading to the basement and a ladder leading to the second floor.

Heading upstairs will earn you more loot and you'll be able to take the safe's key off [Aringoth](Skyrim_Aringoth.md). The first mercenary can be avoided by slipping into a room on the left, traversing it and then moving down the corridor hugging the wall and using a wardrobe to screen you. In the last room, slowly sneak behind the back of a mercenary sitting at a table and pick open the door. Here you will find Aringoth and you can retrieve the safe key by either pickpocketing him, killing him or persuading him to hand it over. You must pass a hard-level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade Aringoth and, if you fail, he will attack you, alerting the nearby mercenary. There is also a [Queen Bee Statue](Skyrim_Queen_Bee_Statue.md) on the nightstand next to the bed which can be sold to [Delvin](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md).

### Sweep the Safe
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Loud_and_Clear_02.jpg) The safe containing Aringoth's riches Whether or not you have the key, unlock the metal gate once the mercenary patrolling the corridor has turned around and enter the basement. Sneak past or dispose of two mercenaries in the first room. Using a spell to set fire to the oil puddle leading up to his chair can aid you with dispatching the last mercenary. If you wish to remain undetected, sneak to the dark corner behind him, shoot an arrow down the corridor and slip away when he charges off to investigate. Alternatively, [invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) or the [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) [Throw Voice](Skyrim_Throw_Voice.md) will also work.

Pick the safe or open it with its key and retrieve the [Goldenglow Bill of Sale](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Bill_of_Sale_(safe).md) and some gold. Before you leave, loot the nearby chest and scoop up some gold purses. A gate will lead you back into the sewers, so exit them and swim away to the shore.

### Reaping the Rewards
Head back to the guild and talk to Brynjolf. As long as you've completed the job to the letter, he'll be very pleased and reward you with a leveled amount of gold, as detailed below. If you burn more than three beehives, you will not get a reward, with Brynjolf saying, *"You've made a mess of things and Maven's furious. I told you not to burn more than three of the hives! I've smoothed things over with her for now, but you can forget your cut."* Either way, he'll also point you to Maven Black-Briar, who has requested you for a special job.

## Notes
- After speaking with Mercer, three [Thief Cache](Skyrim_Thief_Cache.md) barrels will appear around Riften.
- If [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has been completed, a leveled [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) may attack the estate and mercenaries during the mission.

## Bugs
- When trying to burn the hives at Goldenglow Estate with a torch or a single-shot fire spell (like Firebolt and Fireball), the hive may not catch on fire the first time and may require that you hit it multiple times before it starts to burn. This may cause Brynjolf to act as if you had burned more than three hives, however, and result in you not getting paid even though you only burned three. - To avoid this, try using the Flames spell as it seems to work more effectively.
- You can also alter Brynjolf's reaction by entering the following console command: `Set Papyrus Quest Var TG02 p TG02Quest Failure_var 0` before talking to him.
