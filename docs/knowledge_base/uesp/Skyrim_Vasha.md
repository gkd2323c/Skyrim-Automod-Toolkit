[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-npc-Vasha.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vasha.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vasha.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vasha.jpg) Vasha [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-npc-Vasha_Unmasked.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vasha_Unmasked.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vasha_Unmasked.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vasha_Unmasked.jpg) Vasha unmasked **Vasha**, a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is one of the three hostages in the [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) quest. You first encounter him in the [Abandoned Shack](Skyrim_Abandoned_Shack.md).

He claims that he is an *"Obtainer of goods, taker of lives, and defiler of daughters."* The nature of the encounter implies that he is either a merchant, soldier, and womanizer, or a thief, murderer, and rapist. Of the three hostages, he is the only one who actually remains calm when faced with his imminent death.

He wears a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md#000cee80) and [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md#000cee82), and also an [execution hood](Skyrim_Execution_Hood.md).

## Related Quests
- **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md)**: Repay your debt to [the Family](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), and show you are willing to kill on command.

## Quest-Related Events
As you approach him for the first time, Vasha will offer you:

- *"Tell you what. You release me, and I promise my associates won't hunt you down like an animal and butcher you in the street. It's a win-win."*

At other times, he can be found saying:

- *"Whoever this is, clearly we got off on the wrong foot. Ah, but no worries. This is not the first time I have been bagged and dragged."*
- *"Come now. Whatever the problem, we can talk about it like civilized folk. Hmm?"*
- *"Hello? Hello? Ah, I know you're listening."*
- *"A man in my position makes lots of enemies. But what is a life-threatening situation if not an opportunity for mutual benefit? Hmmm?"*
- *"You'll let me go. I know you will. This is all just part of the game we play..."*

When spoken to directly:

- **Who are you?**
- *"Ahhh... Vasha, at your service. Obtainer of goods, taker of lives, and defiler of daughters. Have you not heard of me? Perhaps I will have my people carve my name in your corpse, as a reminder."*
- **Would someone pay to have you killed?**
- *"Me? Ha! Are you serious?"*

You have two dialogue options:

| Your Options | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **Answer me, or I'll paint this room with your blood! (Intimidate)** | *"Fool! Don't you get it? I live in the shadow of death every day. A knife in every doorway, a nocked arrow on every rooftop! If one of my enemies wouldn't pay to have me killed, I'd take it as a personal insult."* |
| **Come on, you can tell me. We're all friends here. (Persuade)** | *"He he he he he he he. The real question is, "Would someone pay to have me killed... again." A day goes by without someone trying to gut me in the street, I get disappointed."* |

If you kill him he will scream,

- *"Aiiiieee!"*

[Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) will comment on his fate, depending on who else has been killed:

- *"The conniving Khajiit. Cat like that was sure to have enemies. It's no wonder you chose him."*
- *"Hmm. The [fearless wife and mother](Skyrim_Alea_Quintus.md), and the underhanded Khajiit. Had to be one of them, right? Interesting choice."*
- *"The [timid warrior](Skyrim_Fultheim_the_Fearless.md) and the sly Khajiit. Must have been one or the other, right?"*
- *"Well, well, aren't we the over-achiever? Three possibilities, three victims. Must have been one of them, right? So why take chances..."*

If you choose to kill Astrid instead, you can choose to listen to Vasha saying:

- *"Come now, my friend. Haven't we played this game long enough? Untie me, and we can all go our separate ways."*
- **(Cut the captive's bonds)**
- *"Ah, you see? That wasn't so hard, now was it? Let's the two of us just forget we ever met, hmm?"*

Any time you talk to him after release, he will thank you with:

- *"Ah, sweet freedom. Now that's more like it. You have my thanks, stranger."*

## Notes
- Despite his claims of having his associates "hunt you down like an animal and butcher you in the street", no one will be sent after you should you kill Vasha.
- He is the only one of the hostages who will not comment on another captive's death.
- Using *Clairvoyance* during the associated quest will point to him out of the three potential targets.
