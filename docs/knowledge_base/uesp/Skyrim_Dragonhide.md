**Dragonhide** is a master level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell which nullifies 80% of all physical damage for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Armor - Dragonhide](Skyrim_Armor_(effect).md), for 30 secs

## Perks
- The [Stability](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stability) perk increases the duration to 45 seconds.
- The [Alteration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Alteration_Dual_Casting.md) perk increases the duration to 66 secs, if cast with both hands. - These perks will stack, for a maximum duration of 99 seconds.

## Notes
- This works differently from the lower-level [Armor spells](Skyrim_Armor_(spells).md). While active, you will only receive 20% damage from **all** *physical* attacks. For as long as you do not exchange any equipment, you retain your displayed overall armor rating from prior to casting. Any shift in equipment results in a new displayed overall armor rating of 1. The displayed equipment value for each item in your inventory is 0 for as long as Dragonhide is active, but as long as you do not exchange any equipment while Dragonhide is active, your damage reduction from Dragonhide and from your previous armor rating will stack.
- Since Dragonhide modifies the base damage of physical attacks that hit you, it isn't very effective for training armor skills, as they gain XP depending on the base damage of attacks.
- Due to a bug, Dragonhide is one of only two master level spells that can be dual cast (the other is [Guardian Circle](Skyrim_Guardian_Circle.md)). <sup>[*verification needed — What is the new cost?*]</sup>
- This spell stacks with the damage reduction gained from armor rating. Given that physical damage reduction from armor is capped at 80%, (567 displayed armor rating) this means you can achieve a 96% physical damage reduction while this spell is active.
- Dragonhide does not protect you from physical traps.
- With Necromage, a Vampiric Dragonborn will have the spell active for 68 seconds
