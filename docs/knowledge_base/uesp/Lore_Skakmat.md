Skakmat is a [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) who lived in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), serving as the [familiar](Lore_Familiar.md) of the renowned mystic [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md), the Dowager Queen of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EWR-3)</sup> During the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md), Nulfaga foresaw doom for Daggerfall and her son, King [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md). After he continued to ignore her warnings, the sorceress retired to [Castle Shedungent](Lore_Shedungent.md), leaving Skakmat behind to report on happenings in the kingdom.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NWOB-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FWOB-5)</sup>

Nulfaga's departure did cause Lysandus to reconsider the war, but an attempt at peace failed and the kingdoms of Daggerfall and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) set up for a climactic battle at [Cryngaine Field](Lore_Cryngaine_Field.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NWOB-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FWOB-5)</sup> The fighting had just begun when a sudden, unnatural fog descended upon the field of battle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NWOB-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FWOB-5)</sup> While historians have attributed this phenomenon to "foul [daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) magical tricks"<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FWOB-5)</sup> it was actually created by Skakmat, under Nulfaga's orders, as one final attempt to halt the battle and spare her son from death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup> The fog blinded the combatants, and when it lifted King Lysandus was found dead, shot by an archer.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NWOB-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FWOB-5)</sup> In reality, the "king" was an impersonator, shot by Daggerfall's Prince [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md) in a successful attempt to feign his father's death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup> Ironically, when Nulfaga received word of Lysandus' death at the battle, it drove her mad.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Notes
- Skakmat is never mentioned in any of the games. It exists as a character in the data files of *[TES II: Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*, and its role in the War of Betony is mentioned in *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)*.
- According to an interview with developer [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson), it was originally planned that you would befriend Skakmat and he would show you the way to Nulfaga's home, Shedungent.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Ted-1)</sup>
- Mark Jones claims to have had modeled a dragon on his website, but had promptly lost thoses files.
- Considering that Dragons have their own [language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md), it is unknown what his true name was; the name "Skakmat" has no known translation in the Dragon language and contains only two syllables, rather than the usual three found in most Dragon names.
- In 2017, Julian Le Fay has stated that while he has only a very vague memory of working on this dragon, he did enjoy the name as Skakmat is danish for "[checkmate](https://da.wikipedia.org/wiki/Skakmat)"<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Reddit AMA-7)</sup>
