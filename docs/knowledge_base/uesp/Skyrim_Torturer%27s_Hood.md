[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-item-Torturer%27s_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Torturer%27s_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torturer%27s_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torturer%27s_Hood.jpg) Torturer's Hood The Torturer's Hood is a helmet used by certain dubious figures, such as the [torturer](Skyrim_Torturer.md) encountered in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). It shares its appearance with a generic [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Hood). This item is marked as "unplayable" and cannot be obtained in the original release of the game, but can be legitimately obtained and used in the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md). If obtained during [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), it can be a good helmet for new characters since its armor rating is similar to an [Elven Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Helmet).

## Notes
- Randomly encountered [Thieves](Skyrim_Thief_(wilderness).md) in the wilderness have a 50% chance to be wearing this or a mage's hood.

## Bugs
- The Torturer's hood, like other [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Hood.md) variants, has no model for vampires. Vampires attempting to equip the hood will appear bald. This also enables player vampires to equip it alongside other headgear. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.5.62.0.8, fixes this bug.
