[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone.jpg) The Apprentice Stone **The Apprentice Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) scattered across Skyrim.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_(active).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-The_Apprentice_Stone_(active).jpg.md) The Apprentice Stone when it's activated *Those under the sign of The Apprentice recover Magicka faster, but are more susceptible to Magicka damage.* Activating the Apprentice Stone increases [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) by 100% and increases [weakness to magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md) by 100%.

The stone is set in the typical arrangement of standing stones on the northeast end of a small marsh archipelago. Little else is found here other than plants typical to the marshes of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md). A [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) plant is growing just to the west of the stone structure, at the base of a large tree. Another is at the base of the Apprentice stone itself. [Swamp fungal pods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod) are also growing in the stone's circle and more can be found at the base of a tree just to the right of the circle of stones. A [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) is found on the shoreline to the southeast of the circle.

## Notes
- Although the loading screen tip states the Apprentice Stone doubles both magicka regeneration and magicka, only magicka regeneration is doubled.
- East-southeast of the Stone, a Nordic burial pit has partially sunk into the swamp, and has been taken over by several [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md). A novice treasure chest, several burial urns, and a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Locked Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room)* can be found here.
