[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/AR-place-Stonehills.jpg/200px-AR-place-Stonehills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Stonehills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Stonehills.jpg) Stonehills (Arena) Stonehills is a mining community found in the region of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), within the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is found east of [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md). Stonehills contains Rockwallow Mine, which is a small [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iron) mine and has been maintained by a long-running family of miners.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:448</sup>

## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Stonehills was an active settlement. It was ruled by Duke Vlrarke, and it had a rivalry with [Dragon Bridge](Lore_Dragon_Bridge.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup> Stonehills was neighbored by several settlements, including [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md) to the northeast, and [Dunstad Grove](Lore_Dunstad_Grove.md) to the southwest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup>

Stonehills was founded as a bustling mining-town from a family in Hjaalmarch, the founder built a home where his successors lived to this day, by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), his great-granddaughter, Sorli the Builder administrated the settlement, which had been reduced to the single home and a campsite for the miners,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:439</sup> after a couple decades of various wars.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:448</sup> By then, it had also become a very profitable investment of Thane Bryling of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), where the resources are promptly shipped to Solitude.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRSorli-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRPactur-5)</sup> A [giant](Lore_Giant.md) camp dubbed **Stonehill Bluff** had propped up near the town by that time, with a bounty on the head of the giant wreaking havoc throughout the hold.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Skyrim BG-6)</sup> Powdered mammoth tusks were also taken from the camp to repair the [White Phial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:White_Phial).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skyrim TWP-7)</sup>
