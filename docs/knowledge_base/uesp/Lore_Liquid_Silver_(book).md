Liquid Silver *Notes on a prized metal* *
---
Minerals and ores of all types fill [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md)'s mountains. [Gold](Lore_Gold.md), [silver](Lore_Silver.md), [iron](Lore_Iron.md) —they can all be found in great quantities. But there are rare metals found here and nowhere else on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). One such rare metal is the elusive [liquid silver](Lore_Liquid_Silver.md).

[Alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md) prize liquid silver and blacksmiths covet it, even though neither has found a practical use for the slippery metal. Yet.

Liquid silver is a heavy, viscous metal thought to have healing or life-prolonging properties. It is also believed to have a severely adverse effect on [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md). Bolts infused with liquid silver, therefore, are a rare and highly prized component in any [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) member's arsenal. The [Argent Mine](Lore_Argent_Mine.md) in Wrothgar was once a rich source of the rare metal, but the mine hasn't been in service for many years.
