*Kinlord Ryethamil, on the other hand, was quiet and studious, a leader in the College of Tomes. He was a cautious ruler who checked his younger brother's ambitions, preferring to serve as a steward for what he had inherited rather than seek to add to his dominions.*
— *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)* by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup> **Kinlord** Ryethamil Corelanya was the leader of the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) in the early years of the 27th century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He was also the leader of the College of Tomes of the Collegium Praxis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>

## History

### Reign
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/ON-place-Sunport.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sunport.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sunport.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sunport.jpg) Sunport [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt Little is known of Ryethamil's early years. Ryethamil came of age during the period of peace and stability secured by the Peace of Xor-Hist, established by his predecessor, Kinlady [Torinwe](Lore_Torinwe_Corelanya.md). This accord ensured a lasting truce between the Corelanya [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) and the Stone-Nest [Argonians](Lore_Argonians.md), allowing Ryethamil to inherit a realm free from external conflict, at least for a time. However, within the palaces and colleges of Sunport, the shadow of an ancient bargain with darker forces loomed ever nearer, threatening to unravel the peace Ryethamil had inherited.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>

His younger brother, [Istamil](Lore_Istamil_Corelanya.md), was a bold and ambitious warrior with grand visions of expanding Clan Corelanya's influence, dreams he was willing to pursue even through the use of darker [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) powers. Ryethamil was a member of the College of the Tomes of the Collegium Praxis, eventually rising to lead the institution. Ryethamil became Kinlord of Clan Corelanya and the leader of Sunport in [1E 2607](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2607). Reserved and scholarly by nature, he ruled with caution, acting more as a caretaker of his inheritance than a conqueror. He often tempered Istamil's more aggressive ambitions, leading to growing tension between them. This rivalry culminated in [1E 2631](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2631), when Istamil engineered a formal duel, slew Ryethamil, and seized power for himself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>

### Legacy
Following his death, Ryethamil was laid to rest in the Corelanya Crypt, alongside all other Kinrulers except for his successor Istamil.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RK-2)</sup> Ryethamil features prominently in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>
