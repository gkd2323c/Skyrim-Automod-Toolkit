Lord Castellian was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble from the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Before his death, he was the head of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md)'s elder council and served directly under King [Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md) as one of his advisors. Castellian owned several houses across the kingdom and enjoyed his privacy, almost in excess. He also had a sister named Lady Pasipha, another member of Wayrest's court at the time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFBlackmail-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFRobe-2)</sup>

When Castellian was born is unknown, however, he eventually rose to his position as the most powerful and senior member of Wayrest's court.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFRobe Dial-3)</sup> In the years after Eadwyre married [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), the question of succession had been on the people's minds across Wayrest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TDCN-4)</sup> The subject had especially distracted Lord Castellian<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFBlackmail Dial-5)</sup> but in time, he became very vocal about his desire to marry Princess [Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md), Eadwyre's daughter. However, it was rumored that the Princess had no love for him.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFRobe Dial-3)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), he left his position in [Castle Wayrest](Lore_Castle_Wayrest.md) to go on a temporary holiday.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFRobe-2)</sup> It was at that time, Prince [Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md) and Princess Elysana, in their bid for the kingdom's throne, sent an [Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) to manipulate Lord Castellian. Helseth sent them a blackmail letter regarding his sister, Lady Pasipha. The letter was delivered in time and Castellian backed legislation to put Helseth in favor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFBlackmail-1)</sup>

In the end, Castellian lost his life when Elysana planned his assassination. The Princess sent the Agent to deliver a finely made cloak to him at his home. Unknown to them, the cloak was [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) to summon three [Daedric seducers](Lore_Daedra_Seducer.md) and attack its wearer. Castellian was killed by the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and his guards tried to kill the Agent.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFRobe-2)</sup> When news of his death became public, all that was known was that he disappeared from his home while on holiday, but King Eadwyre suspected it was foul play.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFRobe Dial-3)</sup>

During her reign, Queen Elysana employed three Castellian siblings as her spymaster. The first was the oldest sister Tynnea Castellian, the middle brother Thryrl, and the younger brother Ryvad.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LRPSeamstress-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-LRPBlue Dawn-7)</sup> How they are related to Lord Castellian from her father's reign is unknown.
