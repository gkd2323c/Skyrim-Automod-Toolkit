## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/ON-npc-Regent_Karinwe.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Regent_Karinwe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Regent_Karinwe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Regent_Karinwe.jpg) Karinwe, Estunirion's daughter [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt
### Reign
Little is known about the life and rule of Estunirion. At some point, he fathered a daughter, Karinwe,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RK-1)</sup> who succeeded him as Regent in [2E 439](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_439).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSEA-2)</sup> According to Karinwe, little of note occurred during her father's tenure, as the peace and stability established by the first Regent, [Vinutilmo](Lore_Vinutilmo_Corelanya.md), in [1E 2633](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2633) endured. Like his predecessors, Estunirion was known for following Vinutilmo's devout example, rejecting darker [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) influences and placing his faith in the guiding protection of the Three Queens.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOCC5-3)</sup>

### Legacy
Estunirion died in [2E 439](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_439),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSEA-2)</sup> and was laid to rest in the Corelanya Crypt, alongside the other regents and most kinlords and kinladies who came before him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RK-1)</sup> The name Estunirion was carried by other Corelanyan [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) following his death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Estunirion-4)</sup>
