# Fangs of Sithis

The Fangs of Sithis are a set of [pre-Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md) weapons, taking the form of either two large eggs,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Online-1)</sup> or a set of dual daggers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Online2-2)</sup>

The Fangs were gifted to the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md) by Sithis, the Dark Husband, who in turn gifted them to the [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscales.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONShavara-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It is said that a single Shadowscale wielding the Fangs could lay waste to entire villages not just via mass killings, but also by causing crop-failures and famine. However, most are unable to safely wield the Fangs, as they feed upon the wielder's mind and soul, causing the weak-minded either to go mad, or become mindless agents of Sithis who are devoid of free will.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONLooks-5)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), one Fang was stolen from its hiding place inside Sunscale Strand by Captain Nahrevii of the [Renrijra Maor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Renrijra_Maor). The other was recovered by a team led by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md). With the exception of the Vestige, each of the Fang's wielders ultimately went mad. As a result, the Captain slaughtered her crew, and an explorer with the Vestige's expedition betrayed his cohorts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Online-1)</sup>

From there, the fate of the Fangs is unknown. It is said that either the Fangs of Sithis were peacefully recovered by a group of Shadowscales who confronted the Vestige once they had recovered them both, leaving them with a replica made in the Fangs' image; or that the Vestige was gifted a single Fang to wield by an aspect of Sithis, who appeared to them just as the confrontation began to turn violent.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Online2-2)</sup>
