## Explicit Instructions
- Starting at the barricade directly in front of [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md), move due east down towards the lake.
- The log on the lakeshore with [Mora Tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) growing on it is your first landmark.
- Take a small path up from the lake behind that log to two [gold ore](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md) veins.
- Drop down back to the lakeshore and take your first path up from the shore. There should be a large evergreen tree growing in the path.
- Continue through past two more evergreens and then a large boulder.
- In the open snowy area look for two small boulders in a nook along the mountain. The chest is behind them.

## Bugs
- The chest may not be there at all, even if you are holding the map. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.3, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The chest can be spawned to the hidden spot by using the console command `"ec3d5".placeatme 10e05e`.
