[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-item-Aegisbane.jpg/200px-SR-item-Aegisbane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Aegisbane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Aegisbane.jpg) Aegisbane Aegisbane is an [iron warhammer](Skyrim_Iron_Warhammer.md) and the ancestral weapon of [Clan Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.md). It had recently been stolen by the bandit [Alain Dufont](Skyrim_Alain_Dufont.md), who managed to get close to the clan through the seduction of a friend of the family, [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md). This weapon's enchantment causes five points of [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) upon hit, along with [slowing](Skyrim_Slow.md) them down by fifty percent for three seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Aegisbane requires one [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

While in Windhelm with the warhammer equipped, guards may comment on the weapon's recent history by saying: *"Ah, Aegisbane. Sigil of Clan Shatter-Shield. But didn't that get stolen awhile back? Hmph. Must be rememberin' wrong."* or *"That hammer... Why that's Aegisbane, heirloom of Clan Shatter-Shield! Last I heard, it was stolen by a couple of swindlers. Alain and... Muiri. Yeah."*

## Related Quests
- **[Mourning Never Comes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mourning_Never_Comes)**: Help [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md) get her revenge.

## Notes
- Despite the comments you'll get from the Windhelm guards about Aegisbane, you'll never be able to properly return it to the Shatter-Shield family.
- Aegis was a shield in Greek mythology, so the name of this weapon translates to "shield bane" (a reference to Clan Shatter-Shield).
