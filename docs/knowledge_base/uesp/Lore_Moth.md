Moths are a type of flying insect similar to [butterflies](Lore_Butterfly.md) that are valued for both alchemical reasons and cultural reasons. Their larvae are known as [caterpillars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Caterpillar).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ACAV2-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BGTGB-2)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/ON-banner-Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.png/100px-ON-banner-Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.png) The Cult of the Ancestor Moth's banner, depicting an Ancestor Moth
### Nord
In the totemic religion of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), precursor to the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) pantheon, the Silver Moth has been considered to correspond with Dibella. This worship continued with the migration to Tamriel and Skyrim where the Atmoran animal gods eventually became known by their modern names.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DATN-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSOG-4)</sup> Some Nords in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) got the Silver Moth as a tattoo.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

### Imperial
Moths have a large cultural presence for Imperials, with the Ancestor Moth being sacred to the Imperials. The Nibenese are known to use the ancestor silk of the gypsy-moth to craft exotic cloth, and export to others across the provinces. The singing and hymnal vocals of the ancestors are collected into a special silk-gathering ceremony, which is the ancestor silk. In the Nibenay, this silk harvest is a sacred tradition that dates back to their early history. But to the Cult of the Ancestor Moth, they are one of the same. The cult harvest the silk and undergo a magical ritual to enchant the fabric.

The Tribunal God [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) referred to the Legion as **moths**.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The Legion is also known to name some of its forts with a -moth suffix such as Buckmoth,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BLF-7)</sup> Firemoth,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FF-8)</sup> Hawkmoth,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-HLG-9)</sup> Moonmoth,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MLF-10)</sup> and Sphinxmoth.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-FS-11)</sup>

## Species

### Ancestor Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-ingredient-Ancestor_Wing.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Ancestor_Wing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Ancestor_Wing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Ancestor_Wing.jpg) Ancestor Moth The [Ancestor Moth](Lore_Ancestor_Moth.md) is a rare indigenous gypsy- moth of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and [Ancestor Glades](Lore_Ancestor_Glade.md) across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are sacred to the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) of Cyrodiil, specifically, the Cyro-Nords of the [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md). An order of monks, known as the [Cult of the Ancestor Moth](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md), revere and honor the moths, believing they house [fjyrons](Lore_Fjyron.md), the loving spirits of their ancestors, which keep them alive and grant wisdom and knowledge to those they come upon.

Ancestor moths have been known to have very long lifespans, as ancestor moths that served under [Belharza](Lore_Belharza.md) during their corporeal stage in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) were still in use by the Empire at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

### Azurian Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg) Azurian Moth Azurian Moths are a species of large migratory water-moths, most often encountered by sailors navigating the [Azurian Sea](Lore_Azurian_Sea.md). These translucent insects appear to be made entirely out of [water](Lore_Water.md). They sometimes land on passing ships, and Tamrielic sailors have been known to keep them as pets.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

### Blood Moth
**Blood Moths** are found near [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) in Black Marsh.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TIC-14)</sup><sup>:46</sup>

### Bluewhisper Moth
**Bluewhisper Moths** are found near rivers near [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) in Cyrodiil. Their silk is harvested by Imperials and used for high-end clothing, with the silk having a gossamer appearance but being remarkably strong.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-15)</sup>

### Bright Moons Lunar Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/ON-crown_store-Bright_Moons_Lunar_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Bright_Moons_Lunar_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Bright_Moons_Lunar_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Bright_Moons_Lunar_Moth.jpg) Bright Moons Lunar Moth **Bright Moons Lunar Moths** are found in Summerset and in Elsweyr. Moon-Priests of the Khajiit believe that when one follows you, the blessings of Jone and Jode flutter close behind.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

### Carmine Wood Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/ON-crown_store-Carmine_Wood_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Carmine_Wood_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carmine_Wood_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carmine_Wood_Moth.jpg) Carmine Wood Moth Nearly driven to extinction a century ago by Nibenese, who used their brilliant ruby wing dust as rouge, **Carmine Wood Moths** made a comeback in a different area: [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Friendly to a fault, they are now favored companions among alchemists.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

### Dusklight Lunar Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/ON-crown_store-Dusklight_Lunar_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Dusklight_Lunar_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Dusklight_Lunar_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Dusklight_Lunar_Moth.jpg) Dusklight Lunar Moth **Dusklight Lunar Moths** are blue-purple moths that are often found near quiet, tree-shaded lakes. Legends claim they glide on starlight and rest upon the Moons' reflections in the water. If one follows you away, good luck is said to bless your path.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-18)</sup>

### Evermore Painter's Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/ON-crown_store-Evermore_Painter%27s_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Evermore_Painter%27s_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Evermore_Painter%27s_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Evermore_Painter%27s_Moth.jpg) Evermore Painter's Moth **Evermore Painter's Moths** are moths that are often used for pigment in painting. Artists of High Rock keep sanctuaries for these colorful creatures.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

### Feather Moth
**Feather Moths**,<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TS-20)</sup> also known as **feather-moths** are found on the island of [Summserset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Tesurcarion-21)</sup> They are considered beautiful, but quite a nuisance. Strong-smelling acorns are sometimes used to repel them.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TS-20)</sup> In [Russafeld](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Russafeld), some vintners intentionally plant flowers to attract the bugs essential to the health of the vines. Without feather-moths, Opal Skin [grapes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grape_Vine) cannot grow properly.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Tesurcarion-21)</sup>

### Island Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/ON-creature-Island_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Island_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Island_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Island_Moth.jpg) Island Moth **Island Moths** are found on the islands of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) and [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) of [the Systres](Lore_Systres.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

### Luna Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg) Luna Moth **Luna Moths** are nocturnal moths that have thin, almost ephemeral wings that seem too delicate to hoist anything into the air, giving them an almost magical appearance. Their wings can be used for creating poisons that damage magicka, and for potions of invisibility.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-23)</sup>

### Mustardseed Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/ON-crown_store-Mustardseed_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Mustardseed_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Mustardseed_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Mustardseed_Moth.jpg) Mustardseed Moth **Mustardseed Moths** are known for their brilliant yellow hues and are found flitting through the deciduous fall forests of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) almost unseen. Each year, some fly beyond the Heartland's borders in all directions.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

### Orchid Whisper Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/ON-crown_store-Orchid_Whisper_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Orchid_Whisper_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Orchid_Whisper_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Orchid_Whisper_Moth.jpg) Orchid Whisper Moth The [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md) Caravan Company first found **Orchid Whisper Moths** while traveling from [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md). Though they were attracted to the campfires, they didn't burn themselves. Instead, they settled on the caravan's goods, then followed them as if guarding them.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-25)</sup>

### Phoenix Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/ON-creature-Phoenix_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Phoenix_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Phoenix_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Phoenix_Moth.jpg) Phoenix Moth **Phoenix Moths** are moths native to [Galen](Lore_Galen.md), known for their possible regenerative powers and affinity with ash and fire.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Phoenix-26)</sup><sup>[[26]](#cite_note-DC-27)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BGTGB-2)</sup> They are believed to be echoes of ancient times of wild magic.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-LA-28)</sup>

[Druids](Lore_Druids.md) say they create these moths,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BGTGB-2)</sup> which are manifested from the flames of Mount Firesong and flutter away on the breeze. However, others ares skeptical saying that they start as hot caterpillars that ignite and then emerge from coal like a cocoon.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Phoenix-26)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BGTGB-2)</sup>

### Silver Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-concept-Moth_Mural.jpg/200px-SR-concept-Moth_Mural.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-Moth_Mural.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-Moth_Mural.jpg) Dibella as the Silver Moth In Totemic [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) Religion, [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md) is represented by a **Silver Moth**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSOG-4)</sup>

### Springlight Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/ON-crown_store-Springlight_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Springlight_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Springlight_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Springlight_Moth.jpg) Springlight Moth Originally from the [Tenmar Jungle](Lore_Tenmar.md), **Springlight Moths** spread via caravans and carry the scent of fresh spring rains with every flap of their wings.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-29)</sup>

### Winter Moth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/ON-creature-Winter_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Winter_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Winter_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Winter_Moth.jpg) Winter Moth **Winter Moths** are found in the depths of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md) underneath Skyrim.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-30)</sup>
