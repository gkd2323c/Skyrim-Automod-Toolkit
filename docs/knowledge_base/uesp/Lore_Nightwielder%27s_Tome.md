The Nightwielder's Tome is a book written by [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md) in runes that cannot be read by mortal eyes. Attempting to study the contents of the freezing pages will cause your skin to crawl and your vision to worsen. It was theorized that Azra managed to write the tome by being able to see with more than just his eyes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONWUS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-To S-3)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [nisswo](Lore_Nisswo.md)'s apprentice in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) sought to carry out his master's dream of uncovering the Nightwielder's Tome, as unfinished business led to his soul to stay on the mortal plane. The Tome is an artifact written by Azra Nightwielder in runes that cannot be read by mortal eyes. The nisswo see things without using their eyes, and the Argonian speculated that perhaps Azra used a similar method in its writing. The Argonian tasked an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) with retrieving the tome from the [Xi-Tsei](Lore_Xi-Tsei.md) [Xanmeer](Lore_Xanmeer.md), which was taken over by the Sul-Xan, an Argonian tribe of [Dagonites](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). The apprentice gave Azra's Tome to the [Clutch of Nisswo](Lore_Clutch_of_Nisswo.md) for safekeeping.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONWUS-1)</sup>
