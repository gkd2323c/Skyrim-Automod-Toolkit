# Khajiit Caravans


The caravans were split into three main groups, each with different leaders and routes.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ma-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KT-1)</sup> There was also one other Khajiit caravan operating in Skyrim at the time, but they were massacred by bandits along the road.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PRIMA-2)</sup><sup>:593</sup> It's unknown if they were also a part of Ri'saad's caravans, or if they were unaffiliated merchants. All of the caravans had at least one main merchant and had guards to act as protection and enforcers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KT-1)</sup> The caravaneers were not allowed within the walls of the cities in Skyrim,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ah-5)</sup> as they had a reputation for carrying illicit goods.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> At least some of the caravans were involved in the trade of illegal substances, such as [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skooma) and [Sleeping Tree sap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sleeping_Tree_Sap).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> All of the caravans carried many rare curios and ingredients from the other provinces.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> The caravans were known to harbor travelers,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-To D-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> though they were not sympathetic to the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ri-3)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the caravans received help from the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) on several occasions. Ri'saad had told them that the caravans had been beset by two strange new groups of bandits wearing golden and dark armor. Which led to the Dragonborn uncovering the plot of the mad mage [Thoron](Lore_Thoron.md). After the bandits were dealt with, the caravans were able to come into possession of the same weapons and armor the bandits used.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-BOP-14)</sup> Additionally, Ri'saad and the Dragonborn secured an alliance between the Khajiit Caravans and the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TGCFQ-15)</sup>

One of the caravans, led by Ma'dran, was sold a set of rare [Daedric armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Equipment) by a member of the infamous [Crimson Dirks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crimson_Dirks), Erwan. The Last Dragonborn had heard this after killing the bandit and purchased the armor. They may have even traded a Khajiit artifact, the [Ring of Masser](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ring_of_Masser) (taken from Erwan) for it.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
