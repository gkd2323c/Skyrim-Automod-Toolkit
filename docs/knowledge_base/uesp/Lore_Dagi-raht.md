# Dagi-raht

*When Jode is waning and Jone is waning
Then shall be born the Dagi-Raht
Hidden Mystics of the Jungle Weave
They carry down the magic of old
And divine the knowledge we will need
To protect the secrets of Torval.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/f/f8/Mwaning_Swaning.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Swaning.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Swaning.jpg) Dagi-raht are born when Masser and Secunda are waning<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Dagi-raht are a less common bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Similar to the [Dagi](Lore_Dagi.md), Dagi-raht's facial features typically resemble that of [lynxes](Lore_Lynx.md), but some variations exist.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESODagi-raht-3)</sup> They are slightly larger than the Dagi, but the difference between them is considered the slightest of all furstocks.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup> They also prefer to live among the trees rather than on the ground,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESORaiffi-5)</sup> and are reported to live in the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup>

Due to their stature and light frame, they are able to dwell in the higher jungle branches that cannot hold a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md)'s weight.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MUT-6)</sup> Additionally, they are suspected to have more talent with magic than outsider stereotypes would suggest, though this is a generality. Much like with other races, levels of talent vary between individuals.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESORaiffi-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MUT-6)</sup> They are said to be especially knowledgeable of [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup>
