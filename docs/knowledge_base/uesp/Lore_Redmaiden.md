# Redmaiden

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/ON-npc-Elisa_Velain.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Elisa_Velain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Elisa_Velain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Elisa_Velain.jpg) A Redmaiden user (ESO) Redmaiden is a [drug](Lore_Drugs.md) enabling mortal residents of [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) to protect themselves from the Drain, an ailment afflicting those who dwell in Fargrave for extended periods without entering a pact with a [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). Redmaiden serves to mitigate the effects of the Drain, yet ceasing its usage results in the exacerbation of Drain symptoms.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KL-1)</sup> The healers of Fargrave considered street medicine as subpar, but Redmaiden proved effective for the pactless.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Q-2)</sup> Its effects are transient, as it temporarily wards off the effects of the Drain, ensuring the sanity of mortals only for a brief period.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IOTS-3)</sup> Redmaiden exhibits various other side effects, notably causing redness in the eyes of its users. To alleviate this symptom among those affected by Redmaiden's adverse effects, a solution known as redeye fading agent is applied to clear the eyes of the notorious redness.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TR-4)</sup> It was believed that returning to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) might aid in managing the symptoms of the Drain, although it is uncertain whether this is indeed true.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EV-5)</sup>

To produce Redmaiden, one requires the following ingredients: volcanic ash mixed with [daedrat](Lore_Daedrat.md) entrails, usually with a small addition of [Skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) to mask the taste; pulverized tum-tho tree seed; and the death blood fluid of a Drain-crazed mortal, all combined in a small vessel. Some daedra were known to stockpile it for multiple cycles. It is sometimes served to mortals in a small vial to ensure their return within a fortnight.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IOTS-3)</sup> In appearance Redmaiden is a luminescent red substance, sometimes stored in cylindrical containers.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DQI-6)</sup>

Redmaiden functions as a drug that, when consumed regularly, can tether a mortal to Fargrave much like entering into a pact with a Daedra would accomplish. It is a scarce commodity, challenging to manufacture. The [Grasp of the Stricture](Lore_Grasp_of_the_Stricture.md) was recognized for strictly controlling its distribution.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GKZ-7)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a group of mortal inhabitants from Fargrave coordinated the theft of Redmaiden from the Grasp in order to assist those among them afflicted by the Drain.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MD-8)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EV-5)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-B-9)</sup> An investigation ensued regarding the incident, yet it remains uncertain whether the case was ultimately resolved.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SP-10)</sup>
