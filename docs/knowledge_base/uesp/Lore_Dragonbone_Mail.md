# Dragonbone Mail

The Dragonbone Mail, or the **Dragonbone Cuirass**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRTM-1)</sup> is thought to be one of the greatest artifacts any collector or hero could own. While many fine pieces of armor have been constructed with real [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) bone, the Dragonbone Mail was enchanted by the first [Imperial Battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md) of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), [Zurin Arctus](Lore_Zurin_Arctus.md), in the early years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). It's described as a truly exquisite piece of work, and many have sought to possess it.

The properties of the cuirass allow the wearer to resist fire, and to damage an enemy with a blast of fire. Little is known about the involvement of Zurin Arctus with the enchantment of the cuirass, but an old tale speaks of a debt that he owed to a traveling warrior. Like the warrior, the Dragonbone Mail never stays put for long.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup>

## History
[Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), at one point befriended the sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWYB-3)</sup> At Fyr’s behest, Yagrum wrote and compiled centuries of personal notes into a book that would become known as *[Tamrielic Lore](Lore_Tamrielic_Lore.md)*,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-4)</sup><sup>:241</sup> which listed the Dragonbone Mail among artifacts of "unimaginable significance." However, during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the book was titled *[Tamrielic Artifacts](Lore_Tamrielic_Artifacts.md)* and did not include the cuirass,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TA-5)</sup> which was added in later editions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup> Fyr himself hoped to utilize Yagrum's findings in his search for the Dragonbone Mail and the other artifacts detailed in the book.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWFR-6)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) reportedly recovered the cuirass from the flooded [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins of [Mudan](Lore_Mudan.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Later that year, they sold the mail to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md), who put it on display in the [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) Museum of Artifacts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRTM-1)</sup>

Sometime in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the Dragonbone Mail was stolen from a cargo shipment belonging to the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) by Bjormund Wind-Strider of the Crimson Dirks. As he was a descendant of a lineage that once served as [Dragonguards](Lore_Dragonguard.md), he felt that wearing armor fashioned from the remains of Dragons was his destiny. Seeking to prove himself, he took a bounty to slay an influential member of the [Forsworn](Lore_Forsworn.md), Crowstooth, but was overwhelmed and killed by their gathering forces at Arcwind Point in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) subsequently sought out the bounty and recovered the cuirass from the corpse of Bjormund.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRBC-7)</sup>
