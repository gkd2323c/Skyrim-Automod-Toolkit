## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) and enter [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md).
2. Read the [mysterious note](Skyrim_Mysterious_Note_(Staff_of_Sheogorath).md) and take the [Fork of Horripilation](Skyrim_Fork_of_Horripilation.md).
3. Kill the two specified [bull netches](Skyrim_Bull_Netch.md) using the fork and loot the artifacts from their bodies.
4. Craft the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath_(item).md) using a [Staff Enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Something Waiting for You
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/SR-quest-Put_A_Fork_In_It.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Put_A_Fork_In_It.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Put_A_Fork_In_It.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Put_A_Fork_In_It.jpg) The mysterious message from Sheogorath A [mysterious note](Skyrim_Mysterious_Note_(Staff_of_Sheogorath).md) can be found pinned to a keg on the ground floor of [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) cornerclub in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), along with the [Fork of Horripilation](Skyrim_Fork_of_Horripilation.md). The items will be hard to miss, as they are surrounded by a green glow before you approach.

The note outlines a proposed combat challenge from [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), the Daedric Prince of Madness. You are instructed to use the fork to kill two specific [bull netches](Skyrim_Bull_Netch.md) on Solstheim, and recover two "familiar" artifacts from their bodies. Specifically, you will need to recover the [Branch of the Tree of Shades](Skyrim_Branch_of_the_Tree_of_Shades.md) and [Ciirta's Eye](Skyrim_Ciirta%27s_Eye.md), two items which originally appeared in the [Symbols of Office](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Symbols_of_Office) quest in *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)*.

### Netch Hunt
You must take the fork in order to accept Sheogorath's combat trial. Note that while the fork is literally a kitchen utensil and has very low damage, it is neither [generic cutlery](Skyrim_Fork_(item).md), which is not a weapon, nor the harder to find [fork *weapon*](Skyrim_Fork_(weapon).md), which is weapon type "other" - it actually has the [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) weapon type, allowing your skills and perks to interact with it normally (e.g. it will make no noise when sneak attacking), although both fork weapons do the same base damage of 1. It also weighs *12 times* as much as a normal fork, equivalent to the weight of a [Daedric Dagger](Skyrim_Daedric_Dagger.md). It has a special enchantment that allows you to deal damage to the Bull Netches while holding the fork, which are otherwise immune to damage.

The first bull netch is located close by on the [Isle of Basalt](Skyrim_Isle_of_Basalt.md), a small volcanic island directly west of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md). Kill the beast as instructed and claim Ciirta's Eye from its corpse. The second netch can be found due west of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md), next to an icy waterfall. It will drop the Branch of the Tree of Shades when killed. Once you have obtained both artifacts, you will need to use them to craft your true reward: the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath_(item).md).

### Staff Crafting
In addition to the two items obtained from the netches, crafting the Staff of Sheogorath will require two [Heart Stones](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) and access to a [Staff Enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md). Heart Stones are readily available on Solstheim, and can be [mined](Skyrim_Mining.md) from [deposits](Skyrim_Heart_Stone_Deposit.md) all over the island. In order to use the Staff Enchanter in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md), the player must complete the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md) for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md). However, any staff enchanter will do, such as the one added by the [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md) or [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md) Creations.

Once you have obtained the necessary crafting ingredients and access to the Staff Enchanter, you can craft the Staff of Sheogorath to complete the quest. The staff has a powerful area of effect [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) enchantment.

## Notes
- With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) Creation installed, the Isle of Basalt where you find one of the netches is also home to several [Bliss Bugs](Skyrim_Bliss_Bug.md) as well as a [Monarch Butterfly](Skyrim_Monarch_Butterfly.md) and [Green Butterfly](Skyrim_Green_Butterfly.md). These appear regardless of whether or not you have the Staff of Sheogorath Creation installed.
- At release, you would receive this quest upon installing the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) and be instructed to travel to [Solstheim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Dragonborn:Solstheim). Upon arriving on the island, you would then be directed to the cornerclub. This was changed by the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update.
- The recommended Staff Enchanter to use for this is the one at Myrwatch due to this also requiring two Heart Stones (that are readily available near the enchanter there.) The other staff enchanters are the one at Tel Mithryn (which first requires completion of the side quest "Reluctant Steward") and the one at Bloodchill Manor.
