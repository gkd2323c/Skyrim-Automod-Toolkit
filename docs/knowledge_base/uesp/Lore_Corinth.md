—In-game description in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* Corinthe (alternatively known as **Corinth**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBAOT-3)</sup> is one of the eight major cities in the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup> located in the very heart of the province.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Back in ancient times, Corinthe was the seat of power for one of sixteen kingdoms that dominated ancient Elsweyr, each of them mastered a specific discipline. The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of Corinthe were master carpenters and lumberers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSK-5)</sup>

## Layout and Geography
Corinthe is located in the very heart of the province, specifically on the northern edge of the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LOMap-6)</sup> in its wooden highlands.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSK-5)</sup> It is also located near savannah country, where creatures such as the [Desert Lynx](Lore_Desert_Lynx.md) and pastoral Khajiiti tribes can be found.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLynx-7)</sup> Corinthe is neighbored by several settlements, including Heimthor Mount to the west-northwest, [Helkarn Land](Lore_Helkarn_Land.md) to the north, and Portneu View to the southwest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup>

## History
Long before recorded history was established, modern-day Elsweyr was split into sixteen ancestral clans that spanned across the province and eventually elevated into the status of kingdoms. Corinthe was one of the ancient kingdoms and it was known for its carpenters and builders.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSK-5)</sup> In [1E 463](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_463), the Grand Archive in Corinthe was burned to the ground by a group of [Merrunz](Lore_Merrunz.md) worshippers, which destroyed most of the irreplaceable, ancient text from the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). All that was left was painstakingly preserved by [Darloc Brae](Lore_Darloc_Brae.md), the Golden Beast of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), who placed them in smaller archives and vaults throughout the province.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OOTKMT-9)</sup>

Like the other ancient kingdoms, Corinthe was dismantled in the wake of the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AAPAI-11)</sup> The southern Khajiiti clans were in shambles, but were guided to re-construction by the Khajiit of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md), who had adapted to [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) and [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) customs. With even further help from the clan of moon-priests, the Pa'alatiin, the south took to this new way of life and in turn, created a schism of ideologies with the north.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-HOPAO-12)</sup> Corinthe and other kingdoms in the south came together as the modern-day Kingdom of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AAPAI-11)</sup> Years later in [2E 565](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_565),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-HOSAO-13)</sup> Elsweyr was caught in the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md), but slowly and surely, they overcame it. It was the Clan Mother of Corinthe, Mizaba-ko, who first identified how the flu spread from person to person.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TEATC-14)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city-state of Corinth was an active settlement. It was ruled by King Ak'nir and had a rivalry with [Torval](Lore_Torval.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> In that same time, news of the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md)'s quest for the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md) reached Corinthe and the local [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md)'s loremaster, Turamane ap' Kolthis. The champion met with Kolthis, who promised to help them if they could retrieve a tablet stolen by Sir Galandir, and his order of warrior-priests held out in the [Temple of Agamanus](Lore_Temple_of_Agamanus.md). After the champion recovered the tablet, Kolthis used it to decipher an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) and uncover the location of the Halls of Colossus.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ARColossus-15)</sup>

## Culture and Society

- *For more information, see the: **[Culture](Lore_Khajiit.md#Culture)** and **[Society](Lore_Khajiit.md#Society)** sections on the Khajiit page.*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-crown_store-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg) Pelletine Mustang, used by the First Corinthe Hussars The Khajiit of Corinthe believe that the Dark Moons Lynx is a worldly incarnation of Southpaw, the legendary mount of the Khajiiti god of thieves, [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md) the Footpad.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ONDark-16)</sup> This breed of lynx gets its name from its dark fur, which drew comparisons to the [Dark Moons](Lore_Dark_Moons.md), a phenomenon that appears in a dark eclipse. The First Corinthe Hussars is a calvary of warriors from the eponymous city and the [horse](Lore_Horse.md) of their choice was the Pellitine Mustang, a hybrid breed of the [zebras](Lore_Zebra.md) of Pellitine, and the larger Anequine Mustang horse.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONMustang-17)</sup> The [Kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md) can be found in the Corinthe region and the people [roast](Lore_Khajiit_Cuisine.md#Roast_Kagouti) it with [apples](Lore_Apple.md) and millet.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONRoast-1)</sup>

Ever since the Eternal Champion defeated [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) and ended the Imperial Simulacrum, Imperial loyalists partook in a pilgrimage all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and re-traced the champion's steps throughout their quest.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-AMM-18)</sup> Their first stop in Elsweyr would be Corinthe before they travel to the Temple of Agamanus and eventually the Halls of Colossus. By [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), the Gold-Cat, Ya'Tirrje managed a large-scale [skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) operation that spanned Elsweyr's cities, such as Corinthe, while he resided in luxury in Senchal.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-PGE3-19)</sup> Circa [4E 188](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_188), Corinthe was home to one of the three remaining [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) sanctuaries left on Tamriel. When the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) ordered it closed the same year, the members of the chapter integrated themselves into the [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) Sanctuary.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CJ2-8)</sup>
