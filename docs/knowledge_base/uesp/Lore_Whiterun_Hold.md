[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-symbol-Whiterun.png/200px-SR-symbol-Whiterun.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-symbol-Whiterun.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-symbol-Whiterun.png) The symbol for Whiterun [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg) Whiterun Hold Whiterun Hold is a [hold](Lore_Hold.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), located roughly in the middle of the province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOS-1)</sup> Roads are numerous and well maintained, and visibility across the [tundra plains](Lore_Tundra_Plain.md) is excellent. The hold is relatively flat, surrounded the by mountains of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) to the west of the [Tundra Plateau](Lore_Tundra_Plateau.md), the [Skyborn Range](Lore_Skyborn_Range.md) of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and the [Yorgrim Forest](Lore_Yorgrim_Forest.md) of [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md) to the north, and the [Brittleshin Hills](Lore_Brittleshin_Hills.md) of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md) to the south.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:571</sup> However, Whiterun's southeastern corner is dominated by the gigantic and soaring [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md) —the highest mountain in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:526</sup> The peak of [Lone Mountain](Lore_Lone_Mountain.md) is found in the center of the hold.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup>

Whiterun is certainly one of the most well-tracked holds, with main roads and excellent access west to the Reach, with a border road north through the town of Rorikstead and up toward [Dragon Bridge](Lore_Dragon_Bridge.md). The plains have a number of odd barrows and giant camps to investigate, and the center is dominated by [Fort Greymoor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Greymoor) and the surrounding [Greymoor Foothills](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greymoor_Foothills). The road here allows travel to the north, up through the Skyborn mountains to reach the dreaded [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md) in Hjaalmarch. Further east is Whiterun and the roaring [White River](Lore_White_River.md) and town of Riverwood on the southern border with Falkreath. Another road stretches north from here, into the wilds of the Pale. There's yet another road that skirts the northern foothills of the Throat of the World, following [White River Valley](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:White_River_Valley) into [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md). Although the first of the 7,000 steps that pilgrims climb to reach the summit of Throat of the World begins in [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md) (in [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md)), the actual mountain lies within Whiterun's domain.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:526</sup>

It borders six of the other eight holds in Skyrim, the exceptions being [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MOS-3)</sup> [Giants](Lore_Giant.md) are often seen traversing the landscape while herding [mammoths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mammoth) to grazing areas or back to their camps.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup> Surrounding the eponymous capital, [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), are fertile plains dotted with farms that supply the food for much of Skyrim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOS-1)</sup> The other notable settlements in the hold are [Rorikstead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rorikstead) and [Riverwood](Lore_Riverwood.md).

Whiterun Hold was one of five holds to form the Kingdom of [Western Skyrim](Lore_Western_Skyrim.md) after High King [Logrolf](Lore_Logrolf.md)'s death.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TCOF-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LEOERZI-6)</sup> However, by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) had not received an envoy from Whiterun for ages. At the time, Whiterun Hold was ruled by Jarl [Morryn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morryn), a distant relative of the Solitude royal family and perhaps Skyrim's greatest diplomat at the time.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONRigurt-7)</sup>
