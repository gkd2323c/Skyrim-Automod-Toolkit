It is located behind the outdoor campfire where the townsfolk gather every night.

It is the home of local miner [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md) who lives there alone. It consists of one area only, *[Sylgja's House](#Sylgja.27s_House)*.

## Sylgja's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sylgja%27s_House.jpg) Inside Sylgja's House As is common with houses in smaller settlements, the fireplace and the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) are located opposite to the front door. There are three gold coins sitting on the mantelpiece. The southern part of the house holds little of interest – only two barrels, one of them with a huge [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) on top. Next to the fireplace is a dinner table with some food scattered around, two [pine thrush eggs](Skyrim_Pine_Thrush_Egg.md), a clove of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three slices of [seared slaughterfish](Skyrim_Seared_Slaughterfish.md), two loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [leek](Skyrim_Leek.md) and a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). Adjacent to the table stands a cupboard with clutter, with a [salt pile](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md) and a sample of [troll fat](Skyrim_Troll_Fat.md) on top.

The northern corner of the house holds a set of shelves with two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and one of regular [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), as well as a few common books. A chest in the corner holds a sparse amount of items and the dresser underneath holds [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md). Following the wall is an end table that holds clutter and has a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) on top and a food barrel. The corner also contains Sylgja's huge dinner table, which is set up for four although Sylgja always eats alone. It holds three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [sliced goat cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Goat_Cheese.md), two bottles of alto wine and one regular.

The double bed stands in the eastern alcove which also has a huge wardrobe with clothes, with a [sabre cat pelt](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat_Pelt.md) on top. There is also a small table with two bottles of [nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) and a chest with valuables at the foot of the bed. Two end tables hold clutter; one has four gold coins sitting on top, while the other has a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) and a [potion of haggling](Skyrim_Potion_of_Haggling.md) on top. On the floor, Sylgja has a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md) and a hidden coin purse.

## Notes
- The chest at the foot of Sylgja's bed is *not* safe for storage.
