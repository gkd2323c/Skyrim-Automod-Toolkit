One Dragon Two Dragon *A poem about Dragons* *
---
*This book is a reference to * [One Fish, Two Fish, Red Fish, Blue Fish](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Fish,_Two_Fish,_Red_Fish,_Blue_Fish) * by [Dr. Seuss](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dr._Seuss). It also has a physical depiction, which can be found the cover can be found [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-One_Dragon_Two_Dragon.jpg) and the [1st page here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-One_Dragon_Two_Dragon_02.jpg).*

---

- One Dragon, two Dragon
- Red Dragon, blue Dragon.

- One set free from a cage
- Many in the sky form a murderous rage.

- Some are cruel, some are fast
- Some will flame you as they pass.

- Dragons will freeze you
- Or burn you to bits
- Fling you with shouts
- Or drop you in pits!

- Look to the sky
- At dawn or at night
- You may see Dragons
- In murderous flight!

- What makes Dragons mad?
- Why do they stay?
- I do not know
- I just run away!
