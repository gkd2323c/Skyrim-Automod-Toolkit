- *Absorb **40** points of health per second from target, for **4** seconds.*

**Touch of Death** is an expert level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that [absorbs the health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) of a target, upon projectile impact, and heals the caster over 4 seconds.

## Effects
- [Absorb Health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md), 40 points for 4 seconds.

## Perks
- [Expert Destruction](Skyrim_Expert_Destruction.md), reduces the spell cost by 50% to 158 magicka.

## Notes
- [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md#Dwarven_Sphere), [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md#Dwarven_Spider), and [Dwarven Centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md#Dwarven_Centurion) are all resistant to *Touch of Death*. As such, no damage will be dealt by the absorption effect, nor will any health be received in return.
- This spell also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes#Touch_of_Death) in the [Spell Tomes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes) [official add-on](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads) for *[The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
