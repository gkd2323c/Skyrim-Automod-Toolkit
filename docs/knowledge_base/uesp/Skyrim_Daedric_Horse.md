[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-creature-Daedric_Horse.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Daedric_Horse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Daedric_Horse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Daedric_Horse.jpg) A Daedric Horse The **Daedric Horse** is a creature added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

The original one lives in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md), and will likely be the first creature you see once you enter. It belongs to [Valkyn Gatanas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valkyn_Gatanas), who is a lieutenant of the Daedric Prince Mehrunes Dagon. The Daedric Horse will be hostile to you and you may have to kill it.

After defeating the horse and its master, you can loot one of the containers behind them for a [spell tome](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Daedric_Horse) to summon and ride your very own Daedric Horse.
