*Stamina regenerates 10x faster for 60 seconds, once per day.* **Adrenaline Rush** is a [Greater Power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Powers) possessed by all [Redguards](Skyrim_Redguard.md), which allows them once a day to increase their rate of stamina regeneration ten-fold for 60 seconds.
