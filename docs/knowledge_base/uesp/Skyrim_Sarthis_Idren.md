[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/SR-npc-Sarthis_Idren.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sarthis_Idren.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sarthis_Idren.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sarthis_Idren.jpg) Sarthis Idren is ready to fight for his Skooma business. **Sarthis Idren** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) dealer based at the [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md). He is a source of displeasure for the [jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), who have tried to get rid of him. Unfortunately for them, he has corrupted some of [Riften's guards](Skyrim_Riften_Guard.md), and they tip him off when the Jarl organizes a raid on the warehouse.

He wears leveled armor and carries [a key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sarthis%27s_Key).

## Related Quests
- **[The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid)**: Help the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) to uncover and stop the illegal [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) trade in town.
