[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-place-Lake_Geir_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Geir_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Geir_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Geir_02.jpg) Lake Geir circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-place-Lake_Geir.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lake_Geir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Geir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Geir.jpg) Lake Geir circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lake Geir is a lake in the western [Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md). [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md) is built on its western shores. The lake is fed by [Treva River](Lore_Treva_River.md) and is drained by [Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md). It is named after [Archmage Geirmund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Geirmund), a [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) wizard who died nearby in a duel with Sigdis Gauldurson, the son of [Archmage Gauldur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gauldur).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DVN-1)</sup> A tomb for the Archmage can still be found on the largest island in the lake.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
