[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Eldjar Bear-Skinner was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>

He became Chieftain of Thirsk by slaying the previous chieftain, [Caccino Aurelia](Lore_Caccino_Aurelia.md), after he was revealed to be a fraud and then presented his tongue as a battle trophy. He ruled as chieftain for five years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
