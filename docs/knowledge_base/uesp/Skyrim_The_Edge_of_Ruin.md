## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Stony Creek Cave](Skyrim_Stony_Creek_Cave.md).
2. Take [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge_(item).md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an unmarked quest to recover [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge_(item).md), a fabled bow. The [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) boss in [Stony Creek Cave](Skyrim_Stony_Creek_Cave.md) is in possession of the bow.

Defeat the bandit and claim it as your own to finish the quest. You'll also find many types of cheese on the bandit: an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), [sliced eidar cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Eidar_Cheese.md), [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), and [sliced goat cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Goat_Cheese.md). You might want to bring a pickaxe to mine the ore here as well.

## Notes
- Ruin's Edge originally [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Ruin%27s_Edge) in *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)*. It was an enchanted [Dark Seducer](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md) bow given to you as a quest reward by [Lady Syl](Lore_Syl.md), Duchess of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md).
- This quest would originally be added to your journal upon installing the [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). This was changed with the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update, allowing you to discover the bow through natural gameplay.
- The leveled list that adds cheese to the bandit boss is called `cc BGSSSE004_LItem Cheese For Everyone`, referring to one of [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md)'s favorite lines.
- With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, a unique [Bliss Bug in a Jar](Skyrim_Bliss_Bug_in_a_Jar.md) can be found in Stony Creek Cave. This appears regardless of whether or not you have the Ruin's Edge Creation installed.
