Notes of Andandon, ambassador **by [Andandon](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Andandon)** *Notes from a Dominion ambassador regarding Valenwood*
---
[![ON-concept-Greenshade load screen.png](https://images.uesp.net/3/31/ON-concept-Greenshade_load_screen.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Greenshade_load_screen.png)
- We have made great strides here with our less civilized kin. Near our own settlements you can even see the beginnings of control over the wilderness. It is true that there is beauty here, though it is chaotic and unchecked. It has been difficult to establish what we have; our dear allies are quite touchy when it comes to the woods, but they understand the benefits that our influences convey.

- I will continue to work tirelessly to prove that we will preserve the most important parts. The lives of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) residing among us are much improved already. Incidences of parasites, theft, and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worship are on the decline. There has, of course, been some resistance from staunch traditionalists to our cultural leadership and enhancements, but I suspect it will subside as more of our allies reap the rewards of a more civilized life.
