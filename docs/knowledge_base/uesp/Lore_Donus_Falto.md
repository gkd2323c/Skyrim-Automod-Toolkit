Donus Falto was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) from the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), he was a commoner from [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) who fled after the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) took over and forced its young men into their ranks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTertius-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVyctoria-2)</sup> His disappearance was regarded as one of the primary catalysts of the rebellion against the legion.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVyctoria-2)</sup> After leaving Cheydinhal, Donus went to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and found refuge in an alliance's sewer base.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDonus-3)</sup>

Donus Falto was the son of Tertius and Kirandas Falto and hailed from Cheydinhal. His favorite treat was his mother's sweetrolls,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTertius-1)</sup> but he hated it when they became stale.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKirandas-4)</sup> When the [Empire of Cyrodiil](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md) collapsed as a result of the Three Banners War and the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Imperial Legion stationed in Cheydinhal assumed complete control, turned its population into [slaves](Lore_Slave.md), and stole all of their possessions. While the people were put to work and forced to feed the soldiers, young able-bodied men were forced from their homes and conscripted into the legion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTertius-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVyctoria-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSylvian-5)</sup> Donus had no desire to be conscripted into the legion, so before the legionaries went to the Faltos' house, Donus escaped into the wilderness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTertius-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVyctoria-2)</sup> His parents were devastated, and Tertius left the city to search for him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTertius-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKirandas-4)</sup> Some of Cheydinhal's townsfolk had already started rebelling against the legion,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSylvian-5)</sup> but Donus' flight incited many other citizens to also take up arms against the legionaries.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONVyctoria-2)</sup>

After leaving Cheydinhal, Donus and two friends traveled to the Imperial City.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDonus-3)</sup> With the bridges to the Imperial City destroyed,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONIC-6)</sup> Donus and his friends had to swim across the [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) -infested waters of [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md). Donus managed to swim to City Isle, but the slaughterfish killed his friends. The guards did not allow Donus to use the tunnels to go up into the Imperial City, and he was unable to swim back to the mainland, so he was stuck in the Imperial Sewers, where he found refuge with one of the alliances.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDonus-3)</sup>
