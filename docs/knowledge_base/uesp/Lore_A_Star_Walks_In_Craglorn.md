A Star Walks In Craglorn **by [Adandora the Tale-weaver](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Adandora_the_Tale-weaver)** *A Tale of Romance and Adventure*
---
He fell from the sky, a star descended from [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) to walk among the commonfolk. Some called him Celestial. Others, the Warrior. But me? I called him "my love."

I met my love on a desolate road, not far from the town of [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md). I was moving quickly through the darkness, trying to avoid the notice of the [Iron Orcs](Lore_Iron_Orcs.md) and [Scaled Court](Lore_Scaled_Court.md) soldiers who seemed to be around every turn and bend. It was around just such a bend in the landscape that I literally ran into the tall, powerfully built man in the ancient armor.

He caught me before I fell, steadying me with a strong hand that literally sparkled like the stars in the sky. "Where do you run to in such a hurry, fair maiden," he said. His voice was deep and resonate, sending shivers throughout my body.

* * *
Damn it! This is terrible! Perhaps I should stick to writing true accounts of the happenings in this [wartorn region](Lore_Craglorn.md). I never was very good at pure fiction. Oh, wait! I have another idea. I wonder if the Warrior is fond of peacock confit and aurum potabile?

Maybe I will keep writing and see how this turns out after all.
