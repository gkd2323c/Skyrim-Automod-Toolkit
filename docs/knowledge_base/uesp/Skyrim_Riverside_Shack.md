[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-place-Riverside_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverside_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack.jpg) Riverside Shack **Riverside Shack** is a small shack east of [Gallows Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock) and west-southwest of [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md), along the eastern bank of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md).

It is a derelict house, with holes in the roof and walls, and no front door or windows. It is home to a leveled [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md).

### Riverside Shack
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Shack_02.jpg) Riverside Shack When first discovered, it becomes very clear what happened to the inhabitants, as a leveled hostile [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) will jump out and attack you. Subsequent visits will normally find the animal outside the shack.

It is more a sort of lean-to, having only a front and side walls, and a roof built up against a rock face that is used as the rear wall. Half of the front wall is an opening with three [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) hanging from the eaves. At the rear, against the rock face is a campfire with a cooking spit with two metal pots hanging over it. These contain two pieces of [mudcrab chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin) and a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks). By the fire is the gory remains of the owner, who at the time of the attack was apparently reading the book *[Pirate King of the Abecean](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean)*, which you can find on the floor next to an overturned chair and a [fine hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Hat). To the right, also against the rock face, is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md). Against the southeast side wall is a tall wardrobe and a single unowned bed, with a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots) under the foot of the bed. At the other end of the shack, against the northeast side wall is an apprentice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) which contains [Treasure Map III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_III) (the map doesn't respawn when the chest does). There is a small set of shelves to the left holding some common [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots). Finally against the northwest wall, by the opening is a long wooden table with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a [fishing map for Eastmarch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Map_-_Eastmarch)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Rislav The Righteous](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rislav_The_Righteous)* and four common books on top and a couple of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) underneath.
