[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-item-Torment.jpg/200px-SR-item-Torment.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torment.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torment.jpg) Torment Torment is a unique [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), wielded by [Valkyn Methats](Skyrim_Valkyn_Methats.md) in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md). Its enchantment deals fire damage. Torment has the highest base damage and highest reach of all one-handed swords.

## Notes
- Torment has a nearly identical appearance to the [Daedric Longsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Daedric_Longsword) in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind).
- The [Daedric lettering](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) on the sword reads "PUTREFACTION".
