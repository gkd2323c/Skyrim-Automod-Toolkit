# Dawnguard Items


## Alchemy
**Ingredients**

- **[Ancestor Moth Wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth_Wing)** — Can be acquired by harvesting [Ancestor Moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth) that have landed on plant surfaces.
- **[Chaurus Hunter Antennae](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Hunter_Antennae)** — Dropped by [Chaurus Hunters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Hunter) and [Chaurus Hunter Fledglings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Hunter_Fledgling).
- **[Gleamblossom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gleamblossom)** — Harvested from the flower of the same name.
- **[Poison Bloom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poison_Bloom)** — Harvested from the plant of the same name.
- **[Yellow Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower)** — Harvested from the yellow-flowered variety of Mountain Flower.

**Potions**

- **[Potion of Blood](Skyrim_Potion_of_Blood.md)** — Equivalent to feeding on blood for vampires; vampires heal 100 points of Health.
- **[Redwater Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma)** — Special variety of [Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) made with the blood from Bloodspring of Lengeir's Feast in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md).
- **[Soul Husk Extract](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Husk_Extract)** — Resist 25% of magic for 60 seconds; interrupts the soul drain effect in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

**Recipes**

- **[Frenzy Poison Recipe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frenzy_Poison_Recipe)** — Provides a [recipe](Skyrim_Recipes.md) to yield the [Frenzy](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md) effect.

## Armor
- **[Shellbug Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug_Helmet)** — Available as a [craftable](Skyrim_Smithing.md) helmet, made using parts from the new [Shellbug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug).
- 11 pieces of **new [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) armor** are available, which can be found [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Armor), with a <sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> distinguishing the newly-added content from content available in the original game.
- 9 pieces of **[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) armor** are newly available, listed [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Armor).
- 5 pieces of base **[vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) and [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) armor** are newly available, listed [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Equipment). - In addition, 16 enchanted versions of Vampire Armor are listed in the appropriate sections on [this page](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md), with a <sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> distinguishing the newly-added content from content available in the original game.

## Artifacts
- **[Aetherial Crown](Skyrim_Aetherial_Crown.md)** — Created at the [Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge) as one of three possible rewards for [Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages).
- **[Aetherial Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Shield)** — Created at the Aetherium Forge as one of three possible rewards for Lost to the Ages.
- **[Aetherial Staff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Staff)** — Created at the Aetherium Forge as one of three possible rewards for Lost to the Ages.
- **[Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md)** — Allegedly created and used by the elven god [Auri-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auri-El). Received from [Gelebor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knight-Paladin_Gelebor) during the quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md).
- **[Auriel's Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Auriel%27s_Shield)** — Allegedly created and used by the elven god Auri-El. Wielded by the boss [Falmer Warmonger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falmer_Warmonger) in [Forgotten Vale Forest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale_Forest).
- **[Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md)** — A chalice used to drink from the Bloodspring at [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md) to gain [Great Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_of_the_Ancients).
- **[Dawnguard Rune Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Axe)** — A relic from the original [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). Received through the [Lost Relic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Relic) quest.
- **[Dawnguard Rune Hammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Hammer)** — A relic from the original Dawnguard. Received through the Lost Relic quest.
- **[Dawnguard Rune Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Shield)** — A relic from the original Dawnguard. Received through the Lost Relic quest.
- **[Harkon's Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Harkon%27s_Sword)** — Carried by [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md). The enchantment is unique in that it only works if wielded by a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md).
- **[Locket of Saint Jiub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locket_of_Saint_Jiub)** — Reward from [Jiub](Skyrim_Jiub.md) for completing the quest [Impatience of a Saint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Impatience_of_a_Saint). One of only two amulets in the game to provide its own individual [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) bonus.

6 amulets were meant to affect abilities of the Vampire Lord form, but are unattainable by the player through any standard means:

- **[Amulet of Bats](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Bats), [Amulet of Drain Magicka](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Drain_Magicka), [Amulet of Draining Grip](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Draining_Grip), [Amulet of Mist Form](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Mist_Form), [Amulet of Supernatural Reflexes](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Supernatural_Reflexes), [Amulet of the Empath](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_the_Empath)** — Note: This Amulet of Bats is a variant of the obtainable one..

**Rings**

- **[Ring of The Beast](Skyrim_Ring_of_The_Beast.md)** — Affects abilities of the Vampire Lord form. Found during the [Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic) quest.
- **[Ring of the Erudite](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Erudite.md)** — Affects abilities of the Vampire Lord form. Found during the Rings of Blood Magic quest.
- **[Bone Hawk Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Hawk_Ring)** — Found within Harkon's Throne room.

5 rings were meant to affect abilities of the Vampire Lord form, but are unattainable by the player through any standard means:

- **[Ring of Corpse Curse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Corpse_Curse), [Ring of Draining Claws](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Draining_Claws), [Ring of the Gargoyle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_the_Gargoyle), [Ring of Reanimate Corpse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Reanimate_Corpse), [Ring of Vampiric Grip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Vampiric_Grip)**

## Weapons
- **[Akaviri Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Akaviri_Sword)** — Carried by [Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md). Can only be obtained by using the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) perk [Misdirection](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Misdirection).
- **[Dawnguard War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_War_Axe)** — A new [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) faction weapon. Comparable to the [Orcish War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe) in weight, and the [Dwarven War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_War_Axe) in base damage.
- **[Dawnguard Warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Warhammer)** — A new Dawnguard faction weapon. Comparable to the [Orcish Warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Warhammer) in weight, and the [Dwarven Warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Warhammer) in base damage.
- **[Bloodcursed Elven Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrow)** — Shrouds the sun in darkness if shot at the sun with [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md). Can be made by [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) after completing the quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md).
- **[Frost Giant Club](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Giant_Club)** — Club used by [Frost Giants](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md). Cannot be equipped by the player.
- **[Prelate's Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Prelate%27s_Mace)** — Similar to [Elven Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Mace) in appearance, wielded by [Knight-Paladin Gelebor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knight-Paladin_Gelebor). Cannot be obtained.
- **[Sunhallowed Elven Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrow)** — Causes sunburst attacks to nearby targets if shot at the sun with Auriel's Bow. Can be made by Knight-Paladin Gelebor after completing the quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md).
- **[Zephyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zephyr)** — A unique [Dwarven bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Bow) which fires 30% faster than a standard bow. Found in [Arkngthamz](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arkngthamz).

Additionally, these weapons have been newly added to the game:

- 9 **bolts** (for use with crossbows) have been newly added by the add-on, listed [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolt). One of these, the [Test Bolt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Test_Bolt), is not available for player use.
- 4 types of base **crossbows** have been newly added to the game; information for these is listed [here](Skyrim_Crossbow.md). - In addition, 32 enchanted versions are listed in the appropriate sections on [this page](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md), with a <sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> distinguishing the newly-added content from content available in the original game.
- 9 new **dragonbone weapons** can be crafted from [dragon bones](Skyrim_Dragon_Bone.md). Information on these new weapons can found [here](Skyrim_Dragonbone.md).
- 9 **new staves** have been added by the add-on. The enchantments on 8 of these staves are identical to staves of the same name available in the original game, but their appearance is different. The exception is the [Staff of Ruunvald](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Ruunvald), which is unique.

## Notes
- The **[Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md)** available in the original game is renamed to "Elder Scroll (Dragon)" when the Dawnguard add-on is installed, and is used for the quest [Seeking Disclosure](Skyrim_Seeking_Disclosure.md).
- The **[Werewolf Pelt](Skyrim_Werewolf_Pelt.md)**, which existed in the original game but could not be obtained without [console](Skyrim_Console.md) commands, can now be found in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) in [Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)'s quarters.
- Standard **[arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows)** present in the original game can now be crafted using [Firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) and the appropriate metal ingot. The appropriate [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks are necessary to craft each variety of arrow, but they still cannot be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md).
