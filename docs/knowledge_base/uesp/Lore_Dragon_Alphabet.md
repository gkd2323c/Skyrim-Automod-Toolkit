# Dragon Alphabet


The shape of the letters is constrained by their need to be written using three large talons and a vestigial fourth digit called the dewclaw. Thus, all of the character symbols consist of a maximum of three scratches occasionally punctuated with a dot from the dewclaw.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-SDS-2)</sup>

| A | AA | AH | B | D | E | EI | EY | F | G | H | I | II | IR | J | K | L |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A | 1 | 4 | B | D | E | 2 | 9 | F | G | H | I | 3 | 7 | J | K | L |
| | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | | |
| M | N | O | OO | P | Q | R | S | T | U | UU | UR | V | W | X | Y | Z |
| M | N | O | 8 | P | Q | R | S | T | U | 5 | 6 | V | W | X | Y | Z |

The letter "C" does not usually appear, due to the fact that the letter is unused in the Dragon Language. See [Legends Card Packs](#Legends_Card_Packs) below for details. The character "0" is a part of the Dragon Alphabet Font. It goes unused as there is no associated letter.

## Examples

### Song of the Dragonborn
The first text in the Dragon Alphabet appeared in a [*Game Informer* post](http://www.gameinformer.com/b/news/archive/2011/01/06/february-cover-revealedesv-the-elder-scrolls-v-skyrim.aspx) about their February 2011 edition, along with a post about [translating the cover](http://www.gameinformer.com/b/news/archive/2011/01/06/translating-the-cover.aspx). The original article contained the following samples of Dragon text:

| Dragon alphabet | Latin alphabet | Translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| DOV4K3N DOV4K3N | DOVah Kii N DOVah Kii N | Dragonborn Dragonborn |
| N1L OK ZIN LOS V4R3N | Naa L OK ZIN LOS Vah Rii N | By his honor is sworn |
| W4 D2N VOKUL M4FAER1K 4ST V1L | Wah Dei N VOKUL Mah FAERaa K ah ST Vaa L | To keep evil forever at bay |
| 4RK FIN NOROK P1L GR1N | ah RK FIN NOROK Paa L GRaa N | And the fiercest foes rout |
| FOD NUST HON ZINDRO Z1N | FOD NUST HON ZINDRO Zaa N | When they hear triumph's shout |
| DOV4K3N F4 HIN KOG1N MU DR1L | DOVah Kii N Fah HIN KOGaa N MU DRaa L | Dragonborn for your blessing we pray |
| | | |
| 4RK FIN KEL LOST PROD4 | ah RK FIN KEL LOST PRODah | And the scrolls have foretold |
| DO VED V3NG KO FIN KR4 | DO VED Vii NG KO FIN KRah | Of black wings in the cold |
| TOL FOD Z9M4 WIN K2N M9Z FUND2N | TOL FOD Zey Mah WIN Kei N Mey Z FUNDei N | That when brothers wage war come unfurled |
| ALDUIN F9N DO JUN | ALDUIN Fey N DO JUN | [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) bane of kings |
| KRUZ3K VOKUN ST1DNAU | KRUZii K VOKUN STaa DNAU | Ancient shadow unbound |
| VOTH 1N B4LOK W4 D3VON FIN L2N | VOTH aa N Bah LOK Wah Dii VON FIN Lei N | With a hunger to swallow the world |

Coincidentally, this poem is also the song that is heard throughout the game when the player enters combat with a dragon or if the player lets the game run on the main menu without selecting an option.

Originally known as "Dragon Poem", it was revealed through the in-game book *[Songs of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_Skyrim)* that this is only a portion of a longer work. Loading Screen text name the work *Song of the Dragonborn*. The full version, in both English and the Dragon Tongue, can be found in *Songs of Skyrim*.

### E3 Word Wall
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/Lore-dragon-Dragon_Text.jpg/200px-Lore-dragon-Dragon_Text.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Lore-dragon-Dragon_Text.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Lore-dragon-Dragon_Text.jpg) The Word Wall at E3 2011 At [E3 2011](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electronic_Entertainment_Expo_2011), Bethesda's booth displayed a Word Wall (right) which contained the following sample of Dragon text:

| Dragon alphabet | Latin alphabet | Translation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 4RK OND DR9 | ah RK OND DRey | And lo, did |
| S4ROT | Sah ROT | Mighty |
| H2MVERLUND | Hei MVERLUND | Heimverlund |
| M9Z NOL HEVNO | Mey Z NOL HEVNO | come from the brutal |
| BROM MED STRUN | BROM MED STRUN | North, like a storm |
| DO UZN4G1R | DO UZNah Gaa R | Of unbridled |
| N4KR3N NOL | Nah KRii N NOL | Vengeance from |
| SOVNGARDE | SOVNGARDE | Sovngarde |
| NIM1R | NIMaa R | Itself |

- "[Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md)" is the Nordic idea of the afterlife, a part of [Aetherius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherius).
- The same text appears on the dragon wall statue included with the collector's edition of the game.
- This is also the text for the first Word Wall for the shout [Storm Call](Skyrim_Storm_Call.md).

### Legends Card Packs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/LG-misc-Skyrim_Legendary_Pack.jpg/200px-LG-misc-Skyrim_Legendary_Pack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-misc-Skyrim_Legendary_Pack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-misc-Skyrim_Legendary_Pack.jpg) Skyrim Legendary card pack [Heroes of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Heroes_of_Skyrim) Legendary card packs in [*The Elder Scrolls: Legends*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends) feature the Dragon Alphabet, but it is used to replace English characters rather than words in the Dragon Language. Legendary packs display the following phrases:

| Dragon alphabet | Latin alphabet |
| --- | --- |
| WALK IN THE PA OF THE LEGENDS | WALK IN THE PA[TH] OF THE LEGENDS |
| UNLEASH THE STRENGTH OF LEGENDS AND CONQUER ALL | UNLEASH THE STRENGTH OF LEGENDS AND CONQUER ALL |
| THE POWER OF LEGENDS AWAITS YOU | THE POWER OF LEGENDS AWAITS YOU |

This is the only instance of the letter "C" in the Dragon Alphabet.
