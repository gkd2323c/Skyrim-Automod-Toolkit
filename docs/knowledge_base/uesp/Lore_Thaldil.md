*The first Briar was a skilled hunter called Thaldil. She single-handedly protected Brackenleaf from being destroyed by Colovians during the Imperial occupation of Grahtwood.
She followed the guidance of Brackenleaf and established the Briars.*
— Glaras<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glaras-1)</sup> Thaldil was a talented [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) hunter and the founder of [Brackenleaf's Briars](Lore_Brackenleaf%27s_Briars.md), an organization dedicated to protecting the ancient tree [Brackenleaf](Lore_Brackenleaf_(tree).md) and its surroundings.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glaras-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/ON-quest-Bosmer_Insight.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Bosmer_Insight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Bosmer_Insight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Bosmer_Insight.jpg) The Brackenleaf [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/ON-place-Brackenleaf.jpg/200px-ON-place-Brackenleaf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brackenleaf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brackenleaf.jpg) The settlement of Brackenleaf During the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) occupation of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glaras-1)</sup> at some point between [1E 2714](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2714), and [2E 430](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_430),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE-2)</sup> Thaldil took it upon herself to single-handedly protect the Brackenleaf from the relentless attacks of the [Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md) forces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glaras-1)</sup>

Inspired by the guidance of Brackenleaf, Thaldil founded the Brackenleaf's Briars, a dedicated group committed to safeguarding Brackenleaf. With the establishment of the Briars, Thaldil no longer had to protect Brackenleaf alone, as the group shared the responsibility of safeguarding him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glaras-1)</sup> Over the years, the Brackenleaf's Briars grew in size and influence, and eventually the [settlement named after the Brackenleaf](Lore_Brackenleaf_(settlement).md) was established. It is unclear, however, whether Thaldil herself was the founder of the settlement or not.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Brackenleaf-3)</sup>
