# Remnants Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/ON-npc-Remnants_Clan.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Remnants_Clan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Remnants_Clan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Remnants_Clan.jpg) Members of the Remnants Clan *To pierce the void and see our promise held.
To form a lock that holds tight the door.
To bind us all through ancient wisdom.*
—Incantation that opens the way to the Crux The Remnants Clan<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QI:Pot R-1)</sup> (or **Remnant Clan**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-QI:JFR-3)</sup> also called the **Remnants of Apocrypha** or simply the **Remnants**)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jot FR-4)</sup> is an organization of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) in service to [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), once sworn to defend [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). They are based in the Cenotaph, an ancient fortress floating in Oblivion.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The order sees themselves as protectors of knowledge, and they often steal artifacts, tomes, and other objects being researched that they deem too powerful for others.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRT-6)</sup>

Unlike most Dremora, the Remnants reject the hierarchical structures common to their kind and have a completely synchronized organization with no ranks or leaders. Due to the nature of their servitude, every member of the Clan is part of a mental web that empowers them and connects them to every other member.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tyss-7)</sup> Being separated from the web is mentally scarring to the individuals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FG-2)</sup>

## History
The group that became the Remnants began alone and drifting through the Void. They heard the voice of Mora communicating a deep need for champions to defend his realms of Apocrypha against invaders seeking to steal knowledge. Mora had selected the Remnants for this task and they obliged, discovering two items upon arrival in Apocrypha: a spyglass and a key. They each radiated a power that surged from Apocrypha through the Void and into a place they did not know. Their essences merged with these relics to open a gateway into a small pocket realm known as the Crux, and their minds and body merged into one whole hivemind in service of Mora as they entered of. A written pact awaiting the Remnants already contained their signatures, confirming to them that serving the realm had been their fate all along.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jot FR-4)</sup>

The Dreamcarver Clan led a strike against the Remnants that broke their order, allowing them to take control of the Cenotaph in an attempt to claim the Remnants' power as their own. The Remnants keep their pact with Hermaeus Mora locked away inside the Crux, which is the core of their power and the target of the Dreamcarver assault.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Nf U-8)</sup>

The fate of the Clan after the Dreamcarver assault is ultimately unknown.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FG-2)</sup>
