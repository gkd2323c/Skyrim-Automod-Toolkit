Pine thrush nests (yielding [pine thrush eggs](Skyrim_Pine_Thrush_Egg.md)) are named identically to rock warbler ones ("Birds' Nest"), but are more common in forested regions.

## Ingredients
102 guaranteed samples found in 58 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 in [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 5 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 5 in [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 4 in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 4 in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Sources
The birds' nests that yield rock warbler eggs are most common in rocky regions. There are 133 such nests, found in 46 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 20 around [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 11 around the [Reach Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Reach_Stormcloak_Camp.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 9 around [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 9 around the region east of [Left Hand Mine](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Mine.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-157725&y=1445)
- 8 around [Reachcliff Cave](Skyrim_Reachcliff_Cave.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 7 around [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 5 around [Blind Cliff Cave](Skyrim_Blind_Cliff_Cave.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 5 centered 600 ft ESE of [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-134243&y=-28629)

## Notes
- Rock warbler eggs are also categorized as [food](Skyrim_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- With the "Green Thumb" perk from the Alchemy skill tree, you can get 2 eggs per "harvest" of a nest.
- Version 3.0.11 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch) replaces the Rock Warbler nest at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) with a [Hawk's nest](Skyrim_Hawk%27s_Egg.md).
