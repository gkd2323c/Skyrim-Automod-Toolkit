## History
During the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), Mackamentain became home to one of the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md), Ayleid statues originally held in the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) which were dispersed to cities across Cyrodiil for safekeeping.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBUmbacano-2)</sup> Mackamentain subsequently fell into ruin.

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins were inhabited by [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) and [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md). The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) entered the ruins and retrieved the ancient Ayleid statue, which was later sold to [Umbacano](Lore_Umbacano.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBCollector-3)</sup>
