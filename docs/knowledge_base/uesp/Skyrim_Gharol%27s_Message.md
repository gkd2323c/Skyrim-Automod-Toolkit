## Quick Walkthrough
1. Find [Gharol](Skyrim_Gharol.md) at [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md).
2. Deliver the sword to [Lash gra-Dushnikh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh) at [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Mother's Pride
Gharol is a [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). If you're permitted to enter the stronghold, you may talk to her about her weapon business. She will tell you that while she occasionally trades with the outside world, Orcs infuse their weapons with their private emotions: *"An Orc blade is a very personal thing. Anger, regret, pride. All these things are put into our steel."* In the conversation you will discover that Gharol's daughter, [Lash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh), chose to leave the stronghold, breaking with [Orc custom](Lore_Orc.md) and bringing shame and grief to her mother. Gharol tells you that she has forged a sword to channel her feelings about her daughter's betrayal, and to send her daughter a message. Then she will ask you to honor her with a task: bring the sword to her daughter in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md); Lash will know what it means.

### Message Received
Travel to Karthwasten and deliver the sword to Lash. She will reply, *"So that's it, then. 'Don't come back.' Thank you. Here. You honored my mother's task and should be rewarded."* The forsaken daughter will reward you with leveled gold and ask you to leave her alone.
