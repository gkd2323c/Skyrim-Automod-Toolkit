[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-npc-Elrindir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Elrindir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elrindir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elrindir.jpg) Elrindir **Elrindir** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [fletcher](Skyrim_Fletcher_(class).md) who owns [The Drunken Huntsman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman) with his brother, [Anoriath](Skyrim_Anoriath.md). He can be found behind the counter at The Drunken Huntsman at all times. He will buy and sell items all day and night.

Elrindir wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d). He is equipped with a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe) and a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) supplemented by twenty [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"Ah, hello my [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md)/[Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md)/[Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) friend. In the market for some hunting supplies?"* **(For Nords, Argonians, or Khajiit)**
- *"Ah, kinsman. How my heart soars to look upon a brother Bosmer. Please, how can I serve your hunting needs?"* **(For male Bosmer)**
- *"Ah... How my heart soars to look upon a sister Bosmer. Please, how can I serve your hunting needs?"* **(For female Bosmer)**
- *"Hello, friend. In the market for some hunting supplies?"* **(For any race and sex not mentioned above)**
- *"[Hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) or [horker](Skyrim_Horker.md), it matters not. We've got the arrows to fell any game."*
- *"So what can I get you?"*
- *"If you have need of hunting supplies then you've come to the right place."*
- *"We sell bows, arrows and [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md). A unique combination, to be sure."*
- *"I'll be happy to assist you if you'd like to purchase something."*
- *"I can tell you're no stranger to the wilds. I'm sure we can meet your hunting needs."*

When spoken to:

- **Why the name "Drunken Huntsman"?**
- *"Yes, I hear that question often. My brother Anoriath and I came up with it one night after consuming... rather too much [mead](Lore_Mead.md). After our drinks, we went out for a moonlight hunt. We got separated, and in his drunken state my brother mistook me for a deer. He shot an arrow that pierced my... well, my rump. After such a memorable adventure, we knew we had the name for our shop."*

- **Who should I talk to for work?**
- *"Try [Hulda](Skyrim_Hulda.md), the innkeeper at the [Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare). It's just up the road, near the market. You should check in at [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md), too. There's so much going on these days, the Jarl or his Steward might have need of help."*

**OR**

- *"Try [Olfina](Skyrim_Olfina_Gray-Mane.md), the new innkeeper at the Bannered Mare. It's just up the road, near the market. You should check in at Dragonsreach, too. There's so much going on these days, the Jarl or his Steward might have need of help."* **(If Hulda is dead)**

## Conversations
Elrindir can be seen having random conversations with his brother Anoriath, as outlined below:

- **Anoriath**: *"I've been thinking of hunting out beyond the boundaries of [Whiterun hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md)."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Again? You just had an 'extended hunt' last month. To the Pine Forest, wasn't it."*
- **Anoriath**: *"That was three days. I'm thinking more like three weeks."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Three weeks? You're mad! You can't keep the stand closed for that long!"*
- **Anoriath**: *"Why not? Business has been terrible. Most folk would rather hunt their own meat than buy ours."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Nonsense. That's just an excuse for you to avoid working, and 'commune with the greenwood like our fathers of old' and blah blah."*
- **Anoriath**: *"Congratulations, brother. You've been living in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) so long, you've become a Nord."*
- **Elrindir**: *"At least the Nords don't shirk their responsibilities."*

- **Elrindir**: *"Our stock of arrows is running a bit low."*
- **Anoriath**: *"All right. I'll fletch some more when I get the time."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Good. I'll do the same. We've still got plenty of shafts from that [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md) shipment."*

- **Anoriath**: *"It's been two months, and no word from father."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Don't worry, brother. I'm sure he's fine. We've waited longer before receiving a letter."*
- **Anoriath**: *"Might be worth riding out to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), see if a messenger ship's come in."*
- **Elrindir**: *"If it'll put your mind at ease, then I agree. But not now, all right? We need the business from the stand."*

## Notes
- If you chose to [kill Anoriath](Skyrim_Kill_Anoriath.md) for the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), Elrindir will have a negative disposition towards you, even if you weren't detected.
- Elrindir is marked as a friend of [Olava the Feeble](Skyrim_Olava_the_Feeble.md). However, the two will never meet or interact in-game, nor will they ever mention each other.
