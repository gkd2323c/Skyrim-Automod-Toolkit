[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-npc-Ulfgar_the_Unending.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulfgar_the_Unending.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulfgar_the_Unending.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulfgar_the_Unending.jpg) Ulfgar the Unending in Sovngarde **Ulfgar the Unending** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), believed to have been a member of [Ysgramor](Skyrim_Ysgramor.md)'s [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Companions.md#Five_Hundred_Companions). For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Ulfgar_the_Unending.md).

He can be found in the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md), along with his companions [Nikulas](Skyrim_Nikulas.md), [Erlendr](Skyrim_Erlendr.md), and [Hunroor](Skyrim_Hunroor.md). He wears a set of [studded armor](Skyrim_Studded_Armor.md) and a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He is armed with a one-handed weapon and a two-handed weapon, both leveled and [ancient Nord](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Draugr_Equipment) in quality, in addition to an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md). He has no unique dialogue.
