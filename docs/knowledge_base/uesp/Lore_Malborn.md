Malborn is a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) who became a crucial informant for the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). His real name isn't Malborn but an alias. The [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) wiped out his family in [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) during a purge, with their murders being covered up as an accident caused by a fire. Ever since the death of his family, he developed a hatred for the Thalmor, and at some point got in contact with [Delphine](Lore_Delphine.md), an agent of the blades.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRDelphine-1)</sup>

Delphine later arranged a meeting between Malborn and the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). There, Malborn discussed plans to smuggle the Dragonborn's supplies into the [Thalmor Embassy](Lore_Thalmor_Embassy.md) during a party hosted by [Elenwen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elenwen), the First Emissary of the Thalmor in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). At the party, after the Dragonborn influenced a guest to cause a distraction, Malborn quietly led the Dragonborn into a back room to retrieve their supplies and infiltrate the Embassy. Later, Malborn was apprehended by Thalmor soldiers and escorted to a dungeon, where the Dragonborn was in the middle of collecting information and freeing a prisoner.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DI-2)</sup> Malborn was rescued,<sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Survival)</sup> and fled to [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), where he would later reunite with the Dragonborn. He ended up asking for their help dealing with a Thalmor assassin, and when that was done, Malborn fled to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FTA-3)</sup>

## Notes
[1.](#note_Survival) Malborn may have died at the hands of the Thalmor in the embassy, or during the process of the escape.
