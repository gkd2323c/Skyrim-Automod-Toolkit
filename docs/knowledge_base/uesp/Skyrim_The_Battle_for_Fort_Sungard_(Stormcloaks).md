## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md).
2. Meet the men preparing for the attack.
3. Take the fort.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Battle for the fort
After finishing the previous quest and returning to [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md), he will ask you to meet the men preparing for an attack on [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md). Head over to the men just north of the fort, and when you reach them they will start running to the fort. Your journal will tell you to take over Fort Sungard by defeating the enemy, and it will keep track of the percentage of enemies left. Simply head up to the fort and assist the Stormcloak soldiers in defeating the Imperials. There are a few wooden barricades out the front of the fort—three hits from a weapon will destroy them. After you have taken the percentage of troops left to kill to zero, the quest will end and you will have to return to [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) for the next part of [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md).

### Achievements
One [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) is unlocked at the end of this quest:

- [![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/30px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) **[War Hero](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Hero)** (10 points/Bronze) — Capture [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) or [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md)

## Notes
- Soldiers' bodies disappear shortly after they have been killed.
- If [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) is given to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) then this quest will not be given to you.
- Completing this quest will send [Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md) to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md), and install [Thongvor Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of The Reach.
- Once this quest is finished, the favor quest [Search and Seizure](Skyrim_Search_and_Seizure.md) for [Ondolemar](Skyrim_Ondolemar.md) will be permanently unavailable. If this quest is finished while Search and Seizure is active, you will fail the favor quest immediately. In addition, Ondolemar will perish. His remains will be buried in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md) in Markarth.

## Bugs
- If you approach [Jarl Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md), [Raerek](Skyrim_Raerek.md), or [Faleen](Skyrim_Faleen.md) in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) after this quest has been completed, they may attack you on sight. Since all three are essential, they cannot be killed. After the civil war has ended, they will revert back to their non-hostile behavior. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The Thalmor Soldiers who followed Ondolemar before this quest will continue to respawn in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) after the Stormcloaks take Markarth. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
