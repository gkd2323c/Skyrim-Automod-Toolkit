Dwarfhome was a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) city of the [Clan Rourken](Lore_Clan_Rourken.md) located somewhere in the region of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGH-1)</sup>

## History
Little is known about the history of Dwarfhome, but it was founded sometime after [1E 420](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_420), when the Rourken Clan of the Dwemer arrived in the Deathlands from the east.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3H-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DI1-3)</sup> At some point, a race of [giant goblins](Lore_Giant_Goblin.md) invaded through their [Goblin Gate](Lore_Goblin_Gate.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Early-4)</sup> They commanded a vast army and appeared in the heart of the province, pillaging and razing towns while driving the Dwarves from the region.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGH-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Early-4)</sup> The displaced Rourken ultimately shared the fate of all Dwemer, vanishing mysteriously around [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DI1-3)</sup>

When the [Yokudans](Lore_Yokudans.md) conquered Hammerfell, they made use of the abandoned Dwemer cities, claiming them as their own. By the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), every other former Dwemer city in Hammerfell except Dwarfhome was still inhabited by the [Redguards](Lore_Redguards.md). It came to be known as the Ghost City of Dwarfhome. The city was also mentioned in *[Notes For Redguard History](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Notes_For_Redguard_History)* by the famous Redguard scholar [Destri Melarg](Lore_Destri_Melarg.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGH-1)</sup>
