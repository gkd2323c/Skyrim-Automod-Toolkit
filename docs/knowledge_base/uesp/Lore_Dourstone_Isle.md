[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/ON-place-Dourstone_Isle.jpg/200px-ON-place-Dourstone_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dourstone_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dourstone_Isle.jpg) Dourstone Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/ON-load-Dourstone_Vault.jpg/200px-ON-load-Dourstone_Vault.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Dourstone_Vault.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Dourstone_Vault.jpg) Dourstone Vault Dourstone Isle is an island located off the coast in southeastern [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). An old ruined [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) tower was the only notable landmark of the isle,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aelif-1)</sup> but as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the tower was abandoned by its original builders and inhabitants. The tower contained an entrance to the underground complex known as the Dourstone Vault.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dourstone_Isle-2)</sup>

The ruins were briefly reoccupied in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) and the Daedric minions of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), acting as a lair within which the Daedra could sacrifice mortal souls to fill the [Mortuum Vivicus](Lore_Mortuum_Vivicus.md). The Daedra were swiftly purged from island by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), which was under the effective control of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) at the time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## Notes
- In *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*, there is an island found in southwest [Anticlere](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Anticlere) at approximately the same spot Dourstone Isle is located, meaning they may be one and the same.
