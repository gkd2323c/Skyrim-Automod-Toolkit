A Midnight Ambush *Notes on the fate of Moon-Scythe, the infamous Khajiiti warrior* *
---
The infamous [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) warrior Moon-Scythe and a small entourage made their way toward Willowgrove under cover of night. In a stunning display of night-time manuevering *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*, they were ambushed by [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) defenders and slaughtered.
