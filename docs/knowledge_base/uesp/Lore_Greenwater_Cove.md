Greenwater Cove (or simply **Greenwater**) is a port town located in northern [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), on the western coasts of [Vafe](Lore_Vafe.md), south of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), and east of [Dawnbreak](Lore_Dawnbreak.md).

## Layout & Geography
Greenwater Cove sits between craggy cliffs and jagged promontories form the white granite mountains to the east of the town.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIEGTSI-1)</sup> The unwalled settlement provides a safe haven for ships traversing the [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md), which separates Auridon from [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Greenwater-2)</sup>

Greenwater's surroundings have towering escarpments, secluded coves, hidden sea grottos, lush colored woodlands, and pale off-white sand dunes. The locals try to tame the rugged but beautiful countryside, however, much of western Vafe is wild, untouched coasts and forests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIEGTSI-1)</sup> This environment also gives wildlife of all sorts, like giant snakes, the chance to spawn and thrive.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nestmother-3)</sup>

## History
It is unknown when Greenwater Cove was exactly founded, but its port and its ancient [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) defenses dates to the Late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), when it was commanded the construction of the Eye of the Ancients,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup> an artifact capable of autonomously destroying enemy ships powered by a series of [Welkynd Stones](Lore_Welkynd_Stone.md), kept jealously by town's authorities. The Stones provided the energy to channel a beam of pure fire towards the objectives.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Weapon-5)</sup> The Eye was built upon the eastern cliffs of Vafe to defend the strategic Greenwater Sound. The technology necessary to its construction was lost by the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). However, this Aldmeri artifact was still in function by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) and capable of sinking an entire enemy fleet.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ancient-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eye-7)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/ON-quest-Eye_of_the_Ancients.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Eye_of_the_Ancients.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Eye_of_the_Ancients.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Eye_of_the_Ancients.jpg) The Eye of the Ancients [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) are aware of the existence of the Eye of the Ancients and its powerful beam. However, they also know most of the time is inactive and must be put into function by the locals. This information was very useful when the Sea Vipers, led by Captain Parondo, under the orders of Admiral Herculoa and with the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md)'s assistance, took the town by sea in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), so easily and swiftly that the Canonreeve was unable to activate the Eye,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Captain-8)</sup> and the First Auridon Marines barely had a chance to react.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Scout-9)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup> After Greenwater fell to the Maormer invaders, the First Auridon Marines established a cordon around the town, with the only goal to hold the Sea Vipers there until reinforcements came.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Scout-9)</sup> Fortunately, [a member](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the [Eyes of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md) came to town and managed to kill Captain Parondo and activate the Eye of the Ancients, allowing the Marines to retake Greenwater.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eye-7)</sup> Many citizens were killed during the Maormer sack of the town, including the Canonreeve, researchers from the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and many First Auridon Marines.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Elodinar-10)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup> Rebuilding works began just after the last of the Maormer were repelled to the sea, however, much of the town was already razed down.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup> The Maormer fleet that threatened Auridon, the Sea Viper, was driven into the seas around Auridon by the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), seeking their own victories in the war between the alliances. The First Auridon Marines and the Dominion Navy stood ready to halt the advance of the Maormer and gave instructions to the coastal townsfolk to report any sign of Maormer activity at once to their nearest authority.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Fang-11)</sup>

After the Veiled Heritance conspiracy in [Mathiisen](Lore_Mathiisen.md) was suppressed by Eye of the Queen agents and both the former Canonreeve and Forgemaster were replaced after their execution in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Unveiling-12)</sup> High Kinlord Rilis XIII was not happy about the town's implication in the conspiracy against the crown and decided to try to supply their First Auridon Marines with steel from the forges of Greenwater Cove, however, his attempt was unsuccessful.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Hyamir-13)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eye-7)</sup>

## Government
A Canonreeve is tasked to rule the settlement, who is also responsible for activating the Eye of the Ancients in case of an attack by sea.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Weapon-5)</sup>

## Culture
The town's main role is to defend the northern entry to the strategic Greenwater Sound, protected by the Eye of the Ancients of enemy fleets.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eye-7)</sup> The Mages Guild studied the relic for a long time but were unable to truly understand how it works.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Fiirenir-14)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ancient-6)</sup> However, most of the time, the Eye is inactive to avoid friendly fire towards the trading and warships passing by the busy docks of the town.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cirenwe-4)</sup>

Greenwater Cove's forges give some of the best [steel](Lore_Steel.md) from all of Auridon, second only to those of Mathiisen, however, unlike the latter, Greenwater is only capable of supplying local needs.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Hyamir-13)</sup>

Husbandry is also an important activity in Greenwater. The farms around the town supply meat to Auridon and even export some of their product to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Valiano-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Angren-16)</sup>

## Notes
- The Eye of the Ancients is an artifact very similar in function and concept to the historical Archimedes heat ray. Chronists described that during the Siege of Syracuse (c. 214–212 BC), Archimedes destroyed enemy ships with fire. Many copper mirrors were used to focus sunlight onto approaching ships, causing them to catch fire.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Archimedes-17)</sup>
