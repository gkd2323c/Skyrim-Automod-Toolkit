# Stormkiss

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/BL-item-Stormkiss.jpg/200px-BL-item-Stormkiss.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Stormkiss.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Stormkiss.jpg) Stormkiss (Blades) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/MW-quest-Ennbjof%27s_Nord_Burial.jpg/200px-MW-quest-Ennbjof%27s_Nord_Burial.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Ennbjof%27s_Nord_Burial.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Ennbjof%27s_Nord_Burial.jpg) Tukushapal's Caverns The Stormkiss is a legendary enchanted axe that belonged to the [Olmgerd the Outlaw](Lore_Olmgerd.md), a bastard son of [Harald Hand-Free](Lore_Harald.md) who fought and died in present-day [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), where he was buried "as befits a Nord lord's son": on his ship, in the Tukushapal caverns, with his riches and his enchanted axe at some point in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), after the death of his father in [1E 221](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_221).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ennbjof-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Plaque-2)</sup>

The axe was lost for centuries until the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) found and unearthed it from an ancient tomb in Morrowind in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BStormkiss-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ENB-4)</sup>

Circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180) it was believed to have been claimed by [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BStormkiss-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Blades-5)</sup>

Although there is no doubt that the axe was enchanted, various accounts portray it differently. According to some the Stormkiss was imbued with powerful shock enchantment,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MStormkiss-6)</sup> but other accounts claim it was imbued with the frost enchantment that also drained the magicka from the target.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BStormkiss-3)</sup>
