# Crosier of St. Llothis

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/MW-item-Crosier_of_Saint_Llothis.png/200px-MW-item-Crosier_of_Saint_Llothis.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Crosier_of_Saint_Llothis.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Crosier_of_Saint_Llothis.png) The Crosier of St. Llothis (Morrowind) The Crosier of St. Llothis is a [staff](Lore_Staff.md) sacred to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) as a holy relic. The undefeated spirit of St. [Llothis](Lore_Llothis.md) the Pious is said to be contained within it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-1)</sup> However, the staff's fitness for combat is questionable, as the staff's enchantments actually have various adverse effects on the wielder.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Item-2)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the Captain of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armiger.md) [Voruse Bethrimo](Lore_Voruse_Bethrimo.md) took the staff into battle against [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s forces on [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). To protect his retreating subordinates, the Captain mounted a last stand near Dagoth Ur's citadel, and the staff was lost.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-1)</sup> The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) soon recovered the relic, and then received it as a reward for various services to the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Morrowind-3)</sup>
