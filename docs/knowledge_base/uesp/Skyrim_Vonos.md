[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-npc-Vonos.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vonos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vonos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vonos.jpg) Vonos **Vonos** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) and the leader of the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md). He wears [Mythic Dawn robes](Skyrim_Mythic_Dawn_Robes.md), [steel Nordic gauntlets](Skyrim_Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets.md) and [steel cuffed boots](Skyrim_Steel_Cuffed_Boots.md), and carries a [Daedric dagger](Skyrim_Daedric_Dagger.md) and leveled loot. After killing him, [his journal](Skyrim_Vonos%27_Journal.md) can be found on his body.
