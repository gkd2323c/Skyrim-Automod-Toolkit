# Insect God

| *[Perrif](Lore_Alessia.md)'s original tribe is unknown, but she grew up in Sard, anon [Sardarvar Leed](Lore_Sardavar_Leed.md), where the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md) herded in men from across all the [Niben](Lore_Niben.md): [kothri](Lore_Kothringi.md), [nede](Lore_Nede.md), [al-gemha](Lore_Al-Gemha.md), [men-of-'kreath](Lore_Men-of-kreath.md) (though these were later known to be imported from the North), [keptu](Lore_Keptu.md), [men-of-ge](Lore_Men-of-ge.md) (who were eventually destroyed when the Flower King [Nilichi](Lore_Nilichi.md) made great sacrifice to an insect god named [lost]), [al-hared](Lore_Al-Hared.md), [men-of-ket](Lore_Men-of-ket.md), others; but this was [Cyrod](Lore_Cyrod.md), the heart of the imperatum saliache, where [men](Lore_Men.md) knew no freedom, even to keep family, or choice of name except in secret, and so to their alien masters all of these designations were irrelevant.* <br> — *[The Adabal-a](Lore_The_Adabal-a.md)* |
| --- |

A deity whose name was lost to history, known only as an Insect God, was venerated by Flower King [Nilichi](Lore_Nilichi.md). The [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) [Men-of-ge](Lore_Men-of-ge.md) were eventually "destroyed" when the Flower King made a great sacrifice to this long-forgotten insect god.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TA-1)</sup>

## Notes
- It has been stated that the Insect God is an entity that may not fit within the established pantheons.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-2)</sup>
