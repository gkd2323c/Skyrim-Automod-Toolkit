[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-item-Chrysamere.jpg/200px-SR-item-Chrysamere.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Chrysamere.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Chrysamere.jpg) Chrysamere Chrysamere is a [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) artifact. It is acquired through [The Lost Paladin](Skyrim_The_Lost_Paladin.md) quest, which has you claim the sword from the [Lost Paladin](Skyrim_Lost_Paladin.md) at [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md). Its enchantment is unique among weapons, as it is defensive rather than offensive. Tempering Chrysamere requires an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), a [Ruby](Skyrim_Ruby.md), and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[The Lost Paladin](Skyrim_The_Lost_Paladin.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Chrysamere.md)</sup>: Find and claim the Sword of Heroes.

## Notes
- Chrysamere also appears in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Chrysamere)*, *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Chrysamere)*, *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Chrysamere)*, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Chrysamere_Style), and *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Chrysamere)*.
- When equipped, Chrysamere produces the same visual effect as the Breton racial ability [Dragonskin](Skyrim_Dragonskin.md).
- Chrysamere has no usage cost, so its charges will never drain.
