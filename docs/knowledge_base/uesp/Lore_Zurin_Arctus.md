Zurin Arctus (also known as **the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md)**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup> or by his [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) name **Arnand the Fox**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WWYDB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IWTB-3)</sup> was a [human](Lore_Man.md) spellcaster active during the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), who served as the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md) under Emperor [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md). He was instrumental in the activation of [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md), a colossal construct of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) origin, used by the Emperor in his conquest of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), by channeling his own "heart"—his life force—into a massive soul gem called the [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md). Arctus would later come to regret this act and ultimately destroyed the automaton, but he was killed in the process before he could reclaim his heart. He later returned as an undead being known as the Underking,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFMannimarco-5)</sup> though *[The Arcturian Heresy](Lore_The_Arcturian_Heresy.md)* instead attributes the title to [Ysmir Kingmaker](Lore_Wulfharth.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAH-6)</sup> In children's tales, the Underking was portrayed as a fearsome old bugbear who was believed to be the most powerful mage to ever live.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFGD-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RTBS-8)</sup> In [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), Zurin was also occasionally associated with the mage-god [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup>

## History
*"Each Event is preceded by Prophecy. But without the Hero, there is no Event."*
— *Zurin Arctus, the Underking* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/MW-book-Divine_Metaphysics_p3.jpg/200px-MW-book-Divine_Metaphysics_p3.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-book-Divine_Metaphysics_p3.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-book-Divine_Metaphysics_p3.jpg) Numidium Zurin Arctus was a battlemage and master tactician<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAOWM-9)</sup> who served General [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) in the forces of King [Cuhlecain](Lore_Cuhlecain.md) during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md). He is said to have crowned the general as Emperor [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) after the assassination of Cuhlecain<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAH-6)</sup> and was instrumental in their victory at the Battle of Five Bridges.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAOWM-9)</sup> Grand Vizier Zurin Arctus became the first to hold the title of Imperial Battlemage under Tiber Septim,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-10)</sup> and was one of the Emperor's trusted advisors; it was popularly rumored that Arctus had helped broker the [Armistice](Lore_Armistice.md) with [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), in which the Empire received [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OM-11)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/f/fa/DF-item-Totem.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-item-Totem.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-item-Totem.png) The Totem of Tiber Septim Numidium was a colossal [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) automaton, designed to be powered by the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWBaladas-12)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAH-6)</sup> This was not available, but Arctus was able to devise an alternative power source, the [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md) —a gigantic gem infused with the battlemage's own "heart" (life force).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-4)</sup> Arctus also crafted the [Totem of Tiber Septim](Lore_Totem_of_Tiber_Septim.md), a magical artifact that allowed a bearer of royal lineage to control the golem. At the close of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Tiber Septim used Numidium to conquer all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-4)</sup>

With the conquest of Tamriel complete, Tiber Septim began using Numidium to crush neutral royal families within the Empire, aiming to enthrone Imperial loyalists in their place. Zurin Arctus was horrified at this abuse of power, and attempted to put a stop to it by reclaiming his heart. However, both Arctus and Numidium were destroyed in the attempt. The Mantella was blown into [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), his heart still trapped within it. Without his heart, the battlemage couldn't truly die. He was left in a semi-slumber in a subterranean vault waiting for an opportunity to reclaim his heart. In this undead incarnation, Zurin Arctus became known as the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md), and from time to time sent out his forces to search for the Mantella.

In *[Where Were You When the Dragon Broke?](Lore_Where_Were_You_..._Dragon_Broke.md)*, [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md), the King of Worms, claims that Zurin Arctus could recall where he was when the [Dragon Broke](Lore_Dragon_Break.md), under the moniker of Arnand the Fox.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WWYDB-2)</sup>

## Artifacts
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Dragonbone_Mail.jpg/150px-SR-item-Dragonbone_Mail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragonbone_Mail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragonbone_Mail.jpg) Dragonbone Mail
#### [Dragonbone Mail](Lore_Dragonbone_Mail.md)
The [Dragonbone Mail](Lore_Dragonbone_Mail.md), or the **Dragonbone Cuirass**, is thought to be one of the greatest artifacts any collector or hero could own. While many fine pieces of armor have been constructed with real [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) bone, the Dragonbone Mail was enchanted by the first [Imperial Battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md) of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), Zurin Arctus, in the early years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). It's described as a truly exquisite piece of work, and many have sought to possess it.

The properties of the cuirass allow the wearer to resist fire, and to damage an enemy with a blast of fire. Little is known about the involvement of Zurin Arctus with the enchantment of the cuirass, but an old tale speaks of a debt that he owed to a traveling warrior. Like the warrior, the Dragonbone Mail never stays put for long.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/3/38/DF-item-Mantella.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-item-Mantella.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-item-Mantella.png) Mantella
#### [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md)
The [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md) was a massive green gem, a "Crux of Transcendence" that contained the "heart", or life force, of the entity known to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) as the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md). Twice, the Mantella was used to power the gigantic brass golem, [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) —first by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) when he conquered Tamriel, and again during the events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md).

The Mantella was lost after Tiber Septim's conquests. For most of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Mantella was hidden on a plane of [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) known as the [Mantellan Crux](Lore_Mantellan_Crux.md). It was later located by [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md) the sorceress and recovered by an [agent](Lore_Hero.md#The_Agent) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), which led directly to the Miracle of Peace.
