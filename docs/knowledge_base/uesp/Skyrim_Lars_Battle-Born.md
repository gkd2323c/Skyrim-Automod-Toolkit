[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Lars_Battle-Born.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lars_Battle-Born.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lars_Battle-Born.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lars_Battle-Born.jpg) Lars Battle-Born **Lars Battle-Born** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) of [Alfhild](Skyrim_Alfhild_Battle-Born.md) and [Idolaf Battle-Born](Skyrim_Idolaf_Battle-Born.md) living in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). He can be found walking around Whiterun or inside his [family's home](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Battle-Born.md). He may also be seen around the city being bullied by another child named [Braith](Skyrim_Braith.md).

Lars wears [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Battle-Born) to the clan's house, a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Battle-Born_Farmhouse) to the [clan's farmhouse](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md), a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child), and a small amount of gold.

Your adopted boys may comment on him: *"Lars is such a milk-drinker. He can't even stand up to a girl."*
