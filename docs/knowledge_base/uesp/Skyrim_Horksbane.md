[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Steel Mace.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Steel Mace.png) Horksbane Horksbane is a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace) found next to the corpse of [Saden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saden), its former owner, on [Horker Island](Skyrim_Horker_Island.md). It is identical to a normal steel mace in all respects. Horksbane was meant to deal 20 more damage to [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md), but doesn't due to a [bug](Skyrim_Horksbane.md#Bugs). Despite its effect, it can be enchanted further. Horksbane can be tempered with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), with the improvement from the tempering being doubled if the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk is also unlocked.

*[Saden's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saden%27s_Journal)* sheds some light on how Horksbane was created. In life, Saden was a member of the Blood Horkers pirate gang. Their leader, [Haldyn](Skyrim_Haldyn.md), ordered Saden and some other pirates to scout out islands that could be used for an outpost. When the arrived on Horker Island, several of Saden's crew members were set upon by the horkers living there and killed. The sight of his crew members being slaughtered made Saden so angry that his anger coursed into the steel mace he was holding, creating Horksbane.

## Bugs
- The perk that Horksbane was supposed to add to deal more damage to horkers isn't set up properly, and has no effect. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.02, fixes this bug.
- Horksbane respawns, allowing multiple copies to be obtained. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.05, fixes this bug.
