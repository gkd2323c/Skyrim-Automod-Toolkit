# College of Winterhold (faction)


The secretive nature of the College mages and the Nords' natural distrust of Magic have contributed to widespread distrust of the College and its members. The destruction of most of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) in 4E 122 only worsened the situation, though no actual evidence exists to suggest the College's involvement. The College's leader, Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md), seems to be aware of the College's reputation, but doesn't seem too eager to do anything about it.

## [The College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-place-College_of_Winterhold.jpg/200px-SR-place-College_of_Winterhold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-College_of_Winterhold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-College_of_Winterhold.jpg) The College of Winterhold The threadbare Hold City of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) has seen most of its population driven away. The center of life in these parts nowadays is the College of Winterhold. Once a prominent, influential location in Skyrim, Winterhold has fallen on hard times, but is still a haven for mages in Skyrim, a safe refuge from distrustful Nords. Largely self-sufficient, the College of Winterhold is quite content to be isolated from the rest of the province, although a more peaceful coexistence with the outside world is always preferred.

The College of Winterhold is situated on a cliff overlooking the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). Several decades ago, much of the cliff fell into the sea, taking nearly all of the original city with it. Only a few buildings remain, though somehow the College of Winterhold has largely been untouched by the damage. It now resides on a free-standing pillar of rock and ice. Inside, the College is split into three distinctive towers: Halls of [Countenance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance) and [Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md), where apprentices and senior mages reside; and the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements), where the Arch-Mage resides, gatherings are held, and [The Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum) (the College's great library) is kept. Below the College lies [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden), a warren of icy tunnels where the remnants of long-forgotten experiments reside.

## Membership

### Joining
To join the College of Winterhold, you must first travel to Winterhold. You can hear about how to get to the College via the [College Objective Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:College_Objective_Quest). The first quest is a "gatekeeper" of sorts, called [First Lessons](Skyrim_First_Lessons.md).

### Benefits
- Master-level [trainers](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md), [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) and [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md).
- Expert-level trainers in [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md), [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) and [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md).
- Access to alchemy supplies, as well as an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) and an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md).
- Access to shopkeepers that sell various [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md).
- Access to various [skill books](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md).
- Multiple potential [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md).
- Storage area.
- A free bed to sleep in.
- [Under Saarthal](Skyrim_Under_Saarthal.md) and [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) need to be started to gain access to word walls for [Ice Form](Skyrim_Ice_Form.md) and [Slow Time](Skyrim_Slow_Time.md) shouts, respectively.
- Many expensive things to steal (such as staves, soul gems and potions that respawn).

### Suspension
Committing murder against a College of Winterhold member will trigger the [Rejoining the College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College) quest. You will be required to pay a fine to Tolfdir in order to be reinstated.

### Leadership
Once the quest line is complete, you are granted the position of Arch-Mage, replacing the now-deceased Arch-Mage Savos Aren. This gives you possession of the Arch-Mage's Quarters and a vast number of free items, ranging from soul gems to alchemy ingredients. You are also given the [Archmage's Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Archmage%27s_Robes) (15% less magicka cost on all spell schools, +50 Magicka and 100% faster magicka regeneration).

- **[Onmund's Request](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund%27s_Request)**: Help [Onmund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund) negotiate the return of a family amulet from [Enthir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md)) Requires completion of [Under Saarthal](Skyrim_Under_Saarthal.md).
- **Repeatable quests** - Given by [Drevis Neloren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drevis_Neloren) - **[Out of Balance](Skyrim_Out_of_Balance.md)**: Purify the focus points for magical energy around the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md).

([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Restocking Soul Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restocking_Soul_Gems)**: Help [Sergius Turrianus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sergius_Turrianus) to fill his stock with [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md).
- Given by [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) - **[Aftershock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aftershock)**: Put an end to the rupture caused by the Eye of Magnus. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Rejoining the College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College)**: Make amends for breaking the College of Winterhold rules.
- **[Tolfdir the Absent-Minded](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir_the_Absent-Minded)**: Help [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) find his missing [alembic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir%27s_Alembic).
- Given by [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) - **[Fetch me that Book!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fetch_me_that_Book!)**: Fetch a book for [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Shalidor's Insights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shalidor%27s_Insights)**: Discover the writings of Shalidor and benefit from his wisdom. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### Master Skill Quests
- **[Alteration Ritual Spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration_Ritual_Spell)**: Acquire [Kahvozein's Fang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kahvozein%27s_Fang) and use it to get [heartscales](Skyrim_Dragon_Heartscales.md) from a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) for [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- **[Destruction Ritual Spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Destruction_Ritual_Spell)**: Learn the most powerful [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells Skyrim has to offer.
- **[Illusion Ritual Spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Ritual_Spell)**: Find some books about [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) magic to further your Illusion skills.
- **[Restoration Ritual Spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration_Ritual_Spell)**: Find the [Augur of Dunlain](Skyrim_Augur_of_Dunlain.md) and learn more about [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) magic.

### Membership Required Quests
These are not College of Winterhold quests. However, it is impossible to complete them normally without joining the College.

- **[Creature of Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Creature_of_Legend)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Wild_Horses.md)</sup>: Track down and tame the last living [Unicorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn).
- **[Forbidden Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend)**: Investigate the forgotten [Gauldur Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends).
- **[Forgotten Names](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Names)**: Summon the [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) pirate, [Velehk Sain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Velehk_Sain).
- **[Forgotten Vale Books Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale_Books_Quest)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>: Bring the Ancient Falmer Tome to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md).
- **[No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md)**: Find the [24 gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) to restore the Crown of Barenziah.
- **[Swift as an Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swift_as_an_Arrow)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Arcane_Archer_Pack.md)</sup>: Learn how to craft and use [Telekinesis Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Arrow).

- **[Rogue Wizard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue_Wizard)**: Deal with a rogue wizard for [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir). ([unfinished](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md))

## Notes
- There are four unimplemented optional quests for the College that are only visible by using the command console. They are "[Filling Soul Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Filling_Soul_Gems)", "[Research Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Research_Thief)", "[The Missing Apprentices](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Missing_Apprentices)", and "[Rogue Wizard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue_Wizard)".

## Bugs
- Oddly, since the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) is marked as being owned by the Winterhold crime faction, if you help anyone in the hold (including [Kjar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kjar) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and ironically enough, anyone from the College), you will be able to take things from the longhouse without it being considered theft. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19319)) A new faction was created specifically for the residents of the longhouse in order to set ownership correctly.
