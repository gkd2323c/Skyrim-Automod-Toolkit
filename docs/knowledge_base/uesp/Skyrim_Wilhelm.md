[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-npc-Wilhelm.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wilhelm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilhelm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilhelm.jpg) Wilhelm **Wilhelm** is the [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) innkeeper of [Vilemyr Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). Upon raising Wilhelm's disposition, he will become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

Wilhelm wears standard barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) with some [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0). He wields a leveled dagger (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality), and carries a leveled [gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Leveled_Gems) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Lifting the Shroud](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lifting_the_Shroud)**: Investigate the haunted [barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md) outside of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).
- **[The Bonds of Matrimony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bonds_of_Matrimony)**: Find someone in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md).
- **[The Straw that Broke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Straw_that_Broke)**: Discover what is plaguing [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi)'s mind.
- **[Under the Table](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Under_the_Table)**: Deliver a smuggled Black-Briar Mead keg to the inn at [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

*"Welcome to the Vilemyr Inn. If there's anything I can get you, just let me know."* *"We don't get many visitors through here, unless their * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * headed up to High Hrothgar of course."* *"Need a room? Maybe a drink? You name it."* *"Let me know if I can get you anything."* *"If I were you, I'd keep away from the barrow on the east side of town... it's haunted."* *"Now that our "ghost" has been unmasked, I hope we see more visitors in town than just the occasional pilgrim."* When spoken to:

**Where can I learn more about magic?** *"I've got no interest in magic users. No use for their kind at all. They're way up north in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), and that's fine with me. I don't even like our Jarl havin' a court wizard."* **Anything you can tell me about High Hrothgar?** *"The Greybeards are a solitary lot. I don't think they've ever ventured outside their monastery. We get the occasional pilgrim passing through here on their way to the summit, but almost all of them have returned disappointed."* **Heard any rumors lately?** *"I heard tell of a lad in Windhelm. Aventus Aretino. They say he's been doing the Black Sacrament, trying to contact the Dark Brotherhood. Damn fool."* When exiting conversation:

*"If you're headed up to the monastery, watch your step... it's a long way down."* *"If you pass through again, stop by for a drink."* If you sit down he will shout out:

*"Lynly, would you mind taking care of the customer please?"*
## Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-npc-Wilhelm_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wilhelm_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilhelm_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilhelm_02.jpg) *"What's the point? **Wilhelm**: *"I think you should continue with your performances."*
**Lynly**: *"What's the point? I'm not very good, and it's only for the same few people every night."*
**Wilhelm**: *"I think you play beautifully. It would be a shame if you stopped."*
**Lynly**: *"All right, Wilhlem * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *. I suppose it's the least I can do for you."*

**Lynly**: *"I think I saw it again. That... ghost. It was over by the barrows."*
**Wilhelm**: *"That thing's evil, Lynly! I told you to keep away from there!"*
**Lynly**: *"I'm sorry. I was curious... I didn't believe the stories. I won't go over there ever again."*
**Wilhelm**: *"See that you don't. I promised to keep you safe and I'm not going to break my word."*

**Wilhelm**: *"You heading up to High Hrothgar soon, [Klimmek](Skyrim_Klimmek.md)?"*
**Klimmek**: *"I wish I didn't have to. It's quite a difficult climb."*
**Wilhelm**: *"Take your time then. It isn't as though the Greybeards are going to complain."*
**Klimmek**: *"I know, but I don't want to let them down. I guess I better start thinking about it soon."*

**Wilhelm**: *"Have you spoken to [Fastred](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fastred) yet?"*
**[Bassianus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bassianus_Axius)**: *"No. I'll tell you, Wilhelm, if I could sweep her away from here tomorrow I'd do it, but Klimmek still has so much to learn."*
**Wilhelm**: *"Klimmek is doing just fine. You should follow your heart."*
**Bassianus**: *"Perhaps one day, but not today."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Lifting the Shroud
If you ask about the barrow he mentioned earlier:

**Tell me more about the barrow.** *"There ain't much more to tell... they're haunted and you should stay away. Look, I've seen one of the spirits with my very own eyes. When it glared at me, I swear it burned right through my soul."* **Do the spirits haunt your town as well?** *"Fortunately, they seem to be sticking to the barrow. I think they're guarding it. Certainly isn't helping my business any; who'd want to rent a room anywhere near a haunted barrow?"* **Sorry, I can't help you.** *"Don't blame you one bit."* **I could investigate for you.** *"If you think there's anything you can do, be my guest."* **Actually, you need to take a look at this. (Give journal)** **(If you already have the journal)** *"Let me see that! ...I can't believe this... It was all just a fabrication of this Wyndelius character? I can't believe we were so stupid. Well, least I can do is give you something for taking care of him. If you won't accept it as a payment, consider it a gift."* **Has anyone ever explored the barrow?** *"About a year or two ago, some fella named [Wyndelius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wyndelius_Gatharian) came through; said he was some kind of a treasure hunter. I warned him not to go in there, just like I warned you. The very next night we heard screams from the barrow, and that was it. We never saw him again."* When you exit conversation:

*"Keep your eyes open in the barrow... that's how they get you!"* When you bring back [Wyndelius's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wyndelius%27s_Journal):

**I found this in Shroud Hearth Barrow. (Give journal)** *"Let me see that! ...I can't believe this... It was all just a fabrication of this Wyndelius character? I can't believe we were so stupid. Well, least I can do is give you something for taking care of him. If you won't accept it as a payment, consider it a gift."* He will then give you the [sapphire dragon claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_Dragon_Claw), an item needed to explore the barrow further.

### The Straw that Broke
During [The Straw that Broke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Straw_that_Broke), [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi) will send you to look for his sister, Reyda, and will mention that Wilhelm told him that she is coming back soon. You can now ask Wilhelm about Narfi:

**What's the story with Narfi?** *"He's harmless. He's been in a state ever since his sister Reyda disappeared over a year ago. He just keeps to himself in what's left of his folk's farmhouse across the river."* **You told Narfi she's coming back?** *"I just said that to make the poor guy feel better. I'm pretty sure she's dead. Reyda would gather ingredients from the small island in the river east of here. Then one day, she just vanished. I tried to look for her, but she never turned up."* **Anything dangerous on that small island to the east?** *"I've seen some sort of a cave entrance over there. Folks call it '[Geirmund's Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Geirmund%27s_Hall),' but I don't know why. Probably best if you avoid it for now... it didn't seem to do Reyda any good."* He will then mark Geirmund's Hall on your map.

If you bring him the news of Reyda's demise, he will respond just like Narfi (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

### Under the Table
During [Under the Table](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Under_the_Table), [Romlyn Dreth](Skyrim_Romlyn_Dreth.md) will give you some smuggled [Black-Briar Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) to deliver to Wilhelm. If you choose to bring the mead to Wilhelm:

**I have a delivery from Romlyn.** *"All right, pipe down. You want the whole blessed place to hear you? The arrangement called for a trade. I hope you'll find this suitable."*
## Notes
- A note from Wilhelm, entitled *[An Apology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:An_Apology)*, can be found on the counter in the [Pawned Prawn](Skyrim_Pawned_Prawn.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). It is addressed to the store owner, [Bersi Honey-Hand](Skyrim_Bersi_Honey-Hand.md), and is a letter apologizing because he refuses to go into [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md) to obtain valuable trinkets since he thinks the barrow is haunted.
- He was supposed to be able to have a conversation with [Gwilin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gwilin), but due to a script oversight the following conversation can never be heard:

**Wilhelm**: *"I heard [Temba](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm) yelling at you yesterday. Why do you let her treat you like that?"* **Gwilin**: *"If you'll pardon the expression, she's far more bark than bite."* **Wilhelm**: *"Perhaps, but abuse is abuse. I don't think I could stand it."* **Gwilin**: *"There's much more to worry about in life then a bit of a temper, my friend."*
