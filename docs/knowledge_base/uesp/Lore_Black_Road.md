[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/OB-map-Black_Road.jpg/200px-OB-map-Black_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Black_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Black_Road.jpg) Map showing the route of the Black Road The Black Road road runs east to west between the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md). It traverses the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md), passing through [Fort Ash](Lore_Fort_Ash.md) and [Weynon Priory](Lore_Weynon_Priory.md). Just to the east of Chorrol, the road joins with the [Orange Road](Lore_Orange_Road.md). West of Chorrol, the road continues on into the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md) toward [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), passing [Battlehorn Castle](Lore_Battlehorn_Castle.md).

The Black Road extends beyond Cyrodiil's border and into southeast Hammerfell. The first settlement after crossing the border is the village of Stonedale, south-southwest of [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap HF-2)</sup>
