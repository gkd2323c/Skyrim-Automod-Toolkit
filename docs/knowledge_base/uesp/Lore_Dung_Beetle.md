Scarabs are a variety of [beetle](Lore_Beetle.md) that have cultural significance in various parts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They have been used for their shells and as pets, and many hold them as an important [symbol](Lore_Symbols.md). [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) have all placed importance on the scarab.

## Biology and Ecology
Scarabs are known to be found in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SElytra-1)</sup> and a giant variety has been found underground in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Dung beetles are species of scarab beetle that pick through refuse and collect and roll dung into balls to survive.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FETE-4)</sup> Dung beetles are sometimes kept as pets, and can be used to clean a dinner table between courses.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> [Assassin Beetles](Lore_Assassin_Beetle.md) are a large variety of scarab beetle found in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and other places.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The smaller ancestors of assassin beetles were known to the [Yokudans](Lore_Yokudan.md) as [Samara](Lore_Samara.md) Scarabs.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-THOS-7)</sup>

## Cultural Significance
Scarabs have significant symbolic and practical importance to the Dunmer. The **Black Scarab** is the House Symbol of [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) and it is considered sacred by its members, their armors are often adorned with the symbol.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CM52R-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Scarab elytra are used in the crafting of House Redoran equipment, Redoran shields appear to be fashioned from the overlapping chitin plates of a gigantic scarab.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SElytra-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CM52R-8)</sup>

In the [36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md), [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) reiterates a speech by [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) to [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md). In this speech Boethiah refers to [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) as "the Frame-maker, the Scarab".<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-36L10-10)</sup> The [Commentaries](Lore_Mythic_Dawn_Commentaries.md) of [Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md) also speak to this divine significance of the Scarab. Mankar claims [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) is the realm of Lorkhan in some of his teachings with the phrase 'TAMRIEL AE DAEDROTH'.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBMankar-11)</sup> In his commentaries, he conveys the same message with the phrase 'SCARAB AE AURBEX'.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MDC-12)</sup> Seemingly linked to this concept, the Sixth [Walking Way](Lore_Walking_Ways.md) has been referred to as the Scarab.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-36L30-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-36L29-14)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-VT-15)</sup> In an account of the legend of Boethiah and [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md), [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) and his [followers](Lore_Orc.md) were balled into a singular sphere as "the scarab gathers dung".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FETE-4)</sup>

The Dwemer seemed to place some significance on the symbol of the scarab. They notably depicted [the Steed](Lore_The_Steed.md) [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) as a scarab.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-16)</sup> [Dwemer Scarabs](Lore_Dwarven_Scarab.md) are [animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) that resemble organic scarabs that vary in size, with the most notable example being the giant inanimate one in the Dwemer ruins of [Bthzark](Lore_Bthzark.md) on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), which could be transformed into the shape of a Man.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-17)</sup> There is a Redguard knightly order named the [Order of the Scarab](Lore_Order_of_the_Scarab.md), who adorn their shields with scarab symbols. Typically initiates of the order must travel to the ruins of Bthzark and "wrestle its mechanical men back into shape" before they can join the Knights of the Scarab.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-18)</sup>
