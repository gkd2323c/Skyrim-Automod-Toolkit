[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-item-Drainblood_Battleaxe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Drainblood_Battleaxe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainblood_Battleaxe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainblood_Battleaxe.jpg) A Drainblood Battleaxe A Drainblood Battleaxe is a translucent [Honed Ancient Nord Battle Axe](Skyrim_Honed_Ancient_Nord_Battle_Axe.md) wielded by some of the ghostly [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) found within [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md). It is a part of a set of similar phantom weapons, alongside the [Drainheart Sword](Skyrim_Drainheart_Sword.md) and [Drainspell Bow](Skyrim_Drainspell_Bow.md). Its physical appearance would be identical to the mundane version of this weapon were it not for its spectral look and the particles that flow out of it while moving with it equipped. A Drainblood Battleaxe is enchanted to [absorb health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) for fifteen points upon hit. It is not possible to [improve](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon.

## Bugs
- The weapon cannot be tempered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. It can now be tempered using [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md), and it now benefits from and requires the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk.
- A Drainblood Battleaxe's enchantment is too costly for its charge to allow for more than one use of its enchantment's ability. Its enchantment was most likely supposed to have a similar charge to that of the Drainheart Sword, which can be used 25 times before needing to be recharged. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.31, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12046)) The enchantment cost is lowered to 1.
