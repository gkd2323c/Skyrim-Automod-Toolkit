*My mother was a Companion. And her mother. And all the women in my family, back to Hrotti Blackblade. I stayed with my father in the woods until I was old enough for my Trial. We hunted everything there was to hunt. Good training. Ma didn't live long enough to see me join, but I fight to honor her and all my Shield-Sisters through time.*
— [Aela the Huntress](Lore_Aela_the_Huntress.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATH-1)</sup> **Shield-Sister** Hrotti Blackblade was an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmoran.md) warrior and one of the members of the original [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Companions.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>:155</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg) Ysgramor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/LG-cardart-Aela_the_Huntress.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Aela_the_Huntress.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Aela_the_Huntress.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Aela_the_Huntress.jpg) Aela the Huntress, Hrotti's descendant Little is known about the life of Hrotti Blackblade. As one of the original Five Hundred Companions,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>:155</sup> she hailed from Atmora, swore the Oath of the Companions, and answered [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s call to venture south. It is unknown which ship she served aboard, but each crew was expected to make its own way, seeking its fate beneath the open sun.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOTR-3)</sup>

Little else is known of her deeds in Mereth, but her line of daughters continued to serve as proud Shield-Sisters of the Companions for generations. Their legacy endured at least until [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), when [Aela the Huntress](Lore_Aela_the_Huntress.md) claimed direct descent from her and fought in honor of all her Shield-Sisters throughout the ages.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>:155</sup>

## Notes
- Blackblade was also the name of a weapon associated with [Gauldur](Lore_Gauldur.md) and his sons, though any connection between it and Hrotti Blackblade was unclear.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GB-4)</sup>
