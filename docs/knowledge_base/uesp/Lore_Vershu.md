*We shed our old skins and arose reborn in the service of his Empire and the coiled-king. I'll shed this skin as well, when it's time for a new beginning.*
—Chevalier Renald<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup> Chevalier Renald was a knight descended from the [Akaviri protectors](Lore_Dragonguard.md) of [Reman Cyrodiil I](Lore_Reman.md), serving as a bodyguard for [Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup> under the name of **Vershu**, snake-captain of the [Akaviri Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup> After the fall of the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md), he took the identity of Renald upon himself and became the protector of the northern west.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup> Renald is the focus of the second chapter of the mythical legend [*Remanada*](Lore_Remanada.md). In the text, he is described as having been under the instruction of a pig. As such, he has been referred to as **Blade of the Pig**.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>

## History

### Akaviri Potentate
Renald and his fellow knights were said to have vampire blood (either [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) or literal [vampire](Lore_Vampirism.md) blood) which allowed them to live for ages past Reman and kept guard of his ward, Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md). However, Renald himself stated he was not a vampire, and that his longevity came from a different path he had chosen.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup> He and [Grundwulf](Lore_Grundwulf.md), an apprentice of the [Greybeards](Lore_Greybeards.md), were brothers-in-arms for centuries.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup> Renald once went under the identity of **Vershu**, and under both identities, served as the snake-captain of the Dragonguard. He identified as Renald when [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md) was assassinated by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) via a poisoned black dart.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBo D-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup> He thereafter "became the protector of the northern west".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup> With the Dragonguard disgraced and disbanded, Renald and many of his fellow members were forced to exile themselves and go into hiding.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup>

### Reign of Emperor Aquilarios
At some time around [2E 579](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_579), Renald and his fellow Dragonguard began shadowing the [Five Companions](Lore_Five_Companions.md) before parlaying with them at [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md). Renald revealed that he knew much about their quest to find the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md), and offered to assist [Emperor Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) in locating their prize. Renald and Varen clasped arms and swore an accord on their honor, though [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) remained skeptical of its value to this Renald. Abnur would soon write to his daughter [Clivia](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), asking her to discreetly learn more about Renald and his company, suspecting he had connections far too close to the Emperor.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Meet The Character-4)</sup>

### Interregnum
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/ON-npc-Chevalier_Renald.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Chevalier_Renald.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Chevalier_Renald.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Chevalier_Renald.jpg) Chevalier Renald during the Interregnum During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), specifically in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) encountered Chevalier Renald, who had the appearance of an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md). However, his red eye color and dark hair are said to indicate a significant amount of Akaviri heritage.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Meet The Character-4)</sup> He was also at least 600 years old at the time, having served under Reman III. He claimed that the Akaviri Dragonguard shed their old skins and were reborn in the service of the Empire, and in time he too will "shed his skin",<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup> leaving the meaning of skin-shedding up for interpretation.

When the Vestige met Renald, his focus was turning his ally [Grundwulf](Lore_Grundwulf.md) away from the vampiric [Hollowfang Clan](Lore_Hollowfang_Clan.md). Using his skills as a Dragonguard, along with the aid of the Hollowfang, Grundwulf managed to trap the dragon Sahrotnax, allowing for the opportunity to drain the Dragon for his blood. The Moongrave tribe convinced Grundwulf that drinking the blood of the dragon would allow him to become a [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md), making him eligible to succeed the Ruby Throne. Renald enlisted the aid of the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md), but was too late in preventing Grundwulf from absorbing Sahrotnax's blood. The Dragon's blood empowered Grundwulf, who attacked Renald and the Undaunted, and was slain as a result.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOChevalier Renald-1)</sup>

### Rise of General Talos
In the years preceding the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), Renald discovered the prowess of [Cuhlecain](Lore_Cuhlecain.md) and then supported him towards the throne of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), with Cuhlecain later becoming the "[Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) Zero".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1:C-5)</sup> Only later would it be revealed that Renald did this in order to [become closer](Lore_Tiber_War.md#List_of_Septim.27s_Subordinates) to General Talos Stormcrown, who would go on to become [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), Emperor of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R-2)</sup> Renald's order of knights later became the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Fireside Reman-6)</sup>
