[3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Fanacas is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) found within the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md), in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). In its time, Fanacas was a mining hold for the [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) slaves.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTo N-1)</sup> For the longest time, it contained one of the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md), religious artifacts that were once displayed at the [Temple of the Ancestors](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md). It was among the only modern Ayleid sites that still bore their ancient names by the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBUmbacano-2)</sup>

## History
According to the [Tamrilean Tractates](Lore_Tamrilean_Tractates.md) in [1E 200](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_200), there was a Nedic slave overseer named [Tharanus Ye Redde-Hand](Lore_Tharanus_Ye_Redde-Hand.md) in the Fanacas mines. He was, in fact, one of the earliest known members of the noble [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), one of the most well-known families of the [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTo N-1)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) raged across central Cyrodiil. The ruins of Fanacas, along with the northeast were considered part of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md)'s territory. The ruins were located on the outskirts of [Farragut Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Farragut_Keep).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFanacas-3)</sup> There was a dark and evil presence in the ruins, as if several sacrifices and offerings had taken place during the hold's active years. The Enchantress Mabrel Pierel traveled to Fanacas, preparing a ritual that would have given her immense power.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Wi F-4)</sup> However, she was defeated by a [wandering soldier](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the alliances.

During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins of Fanacas were plundered by a [treasure hunter](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md), who obtained one of the Ten Ancestors for the Ayleid enthusiast [Umbacano](Lore_Umbacano.md) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBCollector-5)</sup> At the time, the ruins contained a coven of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OBFanacas-6)</sup>
