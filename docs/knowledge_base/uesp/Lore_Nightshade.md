## Narcissus
The Narcissus plant is commonly found along the banks of rivers and lakes in High Rock. It has a small purple flower, surrounded by a deep green leaf. The edible flower is very sweet and quite tasty. Legend has it that when eaten, it has the ability of increasing one's charisma for a short while.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>:128</sup>

**Found in:** - [The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)

## [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nirnroot)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/OB_Flora_Nirnroot_Plant.jpg/200px-OB_Flora_Nirnroot_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB_Flora_Nirnroot_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB_Flora_Nirnroot_Plant.jpg) Nirnroot A very rare plant that grows near water. It gives off a low hum when nearby and has amazing magical potential. Originally yellow-leaved, the plant mutated drastically during the Sun's Death event of [1E 668](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_668). Due to its magical nature, it sensed as a species its impending extinction and used the ash salts found falling from the sky as a new source of food. This allowed the plant to live without sunlight. As the ash content of Cyrodiil's soils diminished, so too did the plant's numbers and it is once again on the brink of extinction.

[Crimson Nirnroot](Lore_Crimson_Nirnroot.md) is a rare type of nirnroot unique to Blackreach, a massive cavern beneath Skyrim.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Nirnroot), [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Nirnroot), [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Nirnroot)
