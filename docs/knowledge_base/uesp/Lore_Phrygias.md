[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/DF-map-Phrygias.png/200px-DF-map-Phrygias.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Phrygias.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Phrygias.png) Map of Phrygias Phrygias was a barony on the western coast of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> It existed in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2920Book-2)</sup> as well as in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup> It was surrounded by the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) to the east, [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) and [Urvaius](Lore_Urvaius.md) to the south, and [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup>

The [Garlythi](Lore_Garlythi.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline, and the regional deity was [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md). The provincial seat was the city of Phrygias.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup> Phrygias was one of the primary members of a coalition that fought and defeated the forces of the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) in the massive [Battle of Firewaves](Lore_Battle_of_Firewaves.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TFOTU-4)</sup>

The events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) led to most of the forests in Phrygias being burned down.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WITW-5)</sup>
