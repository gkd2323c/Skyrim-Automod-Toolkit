<center>CHIM</center>

![T](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/OBR-book-Fancy_Font_T.png/45px-OBR-book-Fancy_Font_T.png)
he [Tower](Lore_The_Towers.md) touches all the mantles of Heaven, brother-noviates, and by its apex one can be as he will. More: be as he was and yet changed for all else on that path for those that walk after. This is the third key of Nu-mantia and the secret of how [mortals become makers](Lore_Psijic_Endeavor.md), and makers back to mortals. The [Bones of the Wheel](Lore_Eight_Divines.md) need their flesh, and that is mankind's heirloom.

![O](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/OBR-book-Fancy_Font_O.png/45px-OBR-book-Fancy_Font_O.png)
ath-breakers beware, for their traitors run through the nymic-paths, runner dogs of prolix gods. The [Dragon's Blood](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) have hidden ascension in six-thousands years of [aetherial labyrinth](Lore_Aetherius.md), which is [Arena](Lore_Tamriel.md), which they yet deny is [Oathbound](Lore_Convention.md). By the Book, take this key and pierce the divine shell that encloses the mantle-takers! The skin of gold! SCARAB AE AURBEX!

![W](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/OBR-book-Fancy_Font_W.png/45px-OBR-book-Fancy_Font_W.png)
oe to the Oath-breakers! Of the skin of gold, the [Xarxes Mysteriuum](Skyrim_Mysterium_Xarxes.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* says "Be fooled not by the forlorn that ride astray the roadway, for they lost faith and this losing was caused by the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) who would know no other planets." Whereby the words of [Lord Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) instructs us to destroy these faithless. "Eat or bleed dry the gone-forlorn and gain that small will that led them to walk the path of [Godhead](Lore_Godhead.md) at the first. Spit out or burn to the side that which made them delay. Know them as the [Mnemoli](Lore_Magna_Ge.md)."

I, the Mankar of stars, am with you, and I come to take you to my Paradise where the Tower-traitors shall hang on glass wracks until they smile with the new revolution.
