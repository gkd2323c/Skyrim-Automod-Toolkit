[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-item-Dragonbane.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dragonbane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragonbane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dragonbane.jpg) Dragonbane Dragonbane is an [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) katana found in [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md). As the name would suggest, Dragonbane does additional damage to [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) if used against them. The blade will do [shock damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) when used against other opponents instead, as suggested by its unique lightning effect. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Dragonbane requires one [quicksilver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, and the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk makes tempering twice as effective. It cannot be broken down for its enchantments.
