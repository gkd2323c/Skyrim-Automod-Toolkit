[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Rune.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rune.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rune.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rune.jpg) Rune in the Ragged Flagon Cistern **Rune**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief), is a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). As a child, he was found abandoned on a shipwreck on the north coast by a fisherman and has been trying to find out about his past through a runed stone that was found on him.

After waking up at 8am, he skips breakfast and just loiters around the central area of the Cistern for most of the day. At 10pm he heads over to the training room and practices his blade skills on one of the targets for four hours. At 2am he heads for his bed, which is found in the tunnel leading to the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md).

He wears the standard [Thieves Guild armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Armor), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Gauntlets) and [boots](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Boots_Alt) and is equipped with a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality) supplemented by 12 leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow).

## Dialogue
Rune may greet you with any of the following lines:

*"Name's Rune. Yeah, rune... just like you're thinking."* *"Have no idea what my birth name really is, and I frankly don't care."* *"Being brought up by a poor family, I had to learn how to steal if I wanted to make ends meet."* *"[Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) actually caught me trying to pick his pocket in the market. If he was anyone else I would have gotten away with it."* *"I'm glad Brynjolf decided to let me join. Been trying to make some coin for him ever since."* *"I've never seen anyone with skills like yours. I just wanted to let you know that if you need anything, you can talk to me."* *"Something I can help with or do you just want to talk?*"*"My brother/sister in crime... what'd you need?"* You can inquire about his name, leading to a conversation about his mysterious past.

**How did you get your unusual name?** *"My father told me he found me as a young boy in the wreckage of a ship that sank off of the coast near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). All he found in my pocket was a tiny smooth stone inscribed with some sort of strange runes."* **Do you know what they mean?** *"No one does. I've even taken the damn thing to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). I must have spent every last coin I've made with the Guild trying to find out what it means."* **Maybe you aren't meant to know.** *"Perhaps. They could be nonsense... inane scribbles done by someone in idle boredom. But if not... if they actually mean something, they might tell me where I'm from... what ship was I on. Everything."* **So who gave you the name?** *"Actually, the fisherman who found me, the man I call my father gave it to me. Thought it was fitting I suppose. I never changed it, because it never felt right to do so."* **I'll keep an eye out for anything.** *"I appreciate that."* Before you convince the guild of [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md)'s innocence, he'll appear confused:

*"They say that Karliah murdered [Gallus](Skyrim_Gallus.md), but I'm not so sure."* After though, he'll be impressed:

*"How can Karliah remain so calm when [Mercer](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md) had her on the run for that long? Amazing."* If you are suspended from the guild, he'll show empathy:

*"It's a shame you got booted from the Guild... things were going so well."*

## Notes
- A *[note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:No_Word_Yet)* can found in the Cistern, written by a man hired to search for Rune's parents.
- Based on his story, it's possible that the shipwreck that he was found on was the wreck of the [Orphan's Tear](Skyrim_Orphan%27s_Tear.md), although this is never specifically addressed.
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads *"As a child, Rune was rescued from a sinking ship in the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). He was taken in by a fisherman who discovered a small stone covered in unidentifiable runes. It took Rune several weeks to recover, only to discover that he couldn't remember who he was. Shown the mysterious stone, Rune vowed someday to discover his identity. He lived a happy childhood with the fisherman. When he reached the age of 16, Rune set out on adventure and met Brynjolf."* Some of the details given here don't line up with what Rune says in-game, while others are never addressed in-game at all.

## Bugs
- Rune, like all other members of the Guild, has unique lines for [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md). Like all other lower-ranking Guild members, Rune was meant to have one unique line if spoken to before the ceremony, and one if spoken to afterwards. However, the written dialogue for the former line was seemingly copied into the latter. Both lines actually say *"I think everyone's waiting for the ceremony to get going."* The lines don't have the same voice files, they are different takes. Due to this, Rune's post-ceremony line may come across as very out-of-place.
