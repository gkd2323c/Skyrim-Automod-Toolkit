## Quest Giver
This is a radiant quest; however, unlike most of the other thane quests, you can only receive it when [Vignar Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md) is Jarl of Whiterun. If Balgruuf is the jarl, you can only become Thane of Whiterun by completing [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md).

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Assist the people of Whiterun Hold.
2. Purchase [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md).
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become Thane of Whiterun. In order for Vignar to be jarl, you have to have completed [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md) for the Stormcloaks. Completing this quest befriends him automatically.

Speak with the jarl and he will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People). You must assist five people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md) perk by investing in their stores.

You must also purchase [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md), which costs 5,000 gold, if you have not done so already. Simply purchasing it is enough, there is no requirement to decorate, although it becomes more useful if decorated.

### Other Quests
The following quests also increase the disposition of citizens of Whiterun, and therefore count towards this objective:

- **[Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md)**: Inform the [Jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) about the dragon attack on [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md).
- **[A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md)**: Prove you are Dragonborn to [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md) by slaying the dragon [Sahloknir](Skyrim_Sahloknir.md).
- **[The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen) for [Danica Pure-Spring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring).
- **[The Golden Claw](Skyrim_The_Golden_Claw.md)**: A [shopkeeper](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md) wants you to retrieve his [family heirloom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw) from a [bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift).
- **[Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md)**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md) from the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).

### Reward
After helping five inhabitants and purchasing [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md), the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of Whiterun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Whiterun), is a random, leveled, enchanted blade. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword (two-handed).

- - This is changed with version 2.1.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch).
