Healing Herbs of Northwest Tamriel **by [Ulnil Tildarin](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ulnil_Tildarin)** *A guide to making healing poultices and medicinal broths*
---
There are many wonderful [herbs](Lore_Flora.md) growing throughout the northwest region of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Here are some of the best for use in healing poultices and medicinal broths.

Herbal tonics, tinctures, infusions, and decoctions, utilized to relieve fevers, sore throats, stomach ailments, headaches, body aches, and other illnesses can be created using the following herbs:

— Moth nettles
— [Clickweed](Lore_Clickweed.md)
— Red coldberry leaves
— [Yellow clover](Lore_Yellow_Clover.md)
— [Brandelion](Lore_Brandelion.md)

Herbal poultices and other necessary components for any healer's kit (including remedies for bug bites and sun burn, healing salves, and wound cleansers) can be created using the following herbs:

— [Stendarr's wort](Lore_Stendarr%27s_Wort.md)
— [Comfrey](Lore_Comfrey.md)
— [Valendula](Lore_Valendula.md)
— [Blue yarrow](Lore_Blue_Yarrow.md)
— [Gemweed](Lore_Gemweed.md)
— [Healer's purse](Lore_Healer%27s_Purse.md)
