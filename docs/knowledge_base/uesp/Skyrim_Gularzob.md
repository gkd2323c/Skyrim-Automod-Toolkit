[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-npc-Gularzob.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gularzob.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gularzob.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gularzob.jpg) Gularzob **Gularzob** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc1H) found in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). At first, he's just a simple guard but then becomes chosen by [Malacath](Skyrim_Malacath.md) to be the new Chieftain after the [curse on Largashbur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe) has been lifted. Even he is confused why Malacath has honored him to replace [Yamarz](Skyrim_Chief_Yamarz.md), but vows to try his best to live up to the Orc code.

As chief, his appearance and daily routine do not change much at all. He still patrols the stronghold daily and sleeps in the [longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md).

He wears some [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) along with [Stormcloak officer bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md) and a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He wields a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), which can be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality starting at level 6.

## Related Quests
- **[The Cursed Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe)**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) of [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) by [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md).

## Dialogue
Before the curse has been lifted, he will express confidence in [Yamarz](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yamarz)'s leadership skills as the Chieftain:

- *"We have sworn loyalty to Yamarz. He will see us through these dark times."*

After the curse has been lifted and he is made Chieftain:

- *"I will do my best to lead the tribe, but I don't know why Malacath chose me."*
- *"I am honored to have been chosen to lead. But I know that being picked by Malacath does not mean that I am ready."*
- *"Yamarz betrayed us all. I will never allow that to happen again, so long as I rule."'*
- *"Do not raise arms against my people, and you shall always have a place here, Champion of Malacath."*
- *"Atub is wise beyond her years. I take her advice seriously, and you should as well."*

## Bugs
- Gularzob is not marked as essential and is only a level 6 warrior, meaning he can easily be killed by various creatures that may spawn near the stronghold. This includes the hostile giant from The Cursed Tribe quest, which will respawn regardless of whether you complete the quest or not (see [quest bugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe#Bugs)). Due to this oversight, if Gularzob is killed before the quest ends, Malacath will still name him the new chief as if he was alive. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, addresses this issue. He is essential until after completing the quest. In addition, since version 1.2.5 of the patch, the giant is prevented from respawning.
