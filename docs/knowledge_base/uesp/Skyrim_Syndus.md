[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Syndus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Syndus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Syndus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Syndus.jpg) Syndus **Syndus** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [fletcher](Skyrim_Fletcher_(class).md) who operates a shop at [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). He will move into [The Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) after you complete one of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)'s "special" jobs for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md). His shop contains various pieces of archery equipment, most notably a [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) that can be found on the counter.

Syndus will spend all his time serving his shop while seated on a chair. The only time he will not barter with you is if you have offended the Thieves Guild in some way. He will not offer his services until you have made [reparations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reparations) to the Thieves Guild.

Syndus wears a set of merchant's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff45) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He carries a leveled bow, which can be up to [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality, along with twelve leveled arrows of the best possible quality and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
Syndus thinks quite highly of himself along with his wares and may greet you with:

*"If you're in the market for the finest bows money can buy, you're talking to the right person."* *"I've got all sorts of weapons for the discerning marksman. What can I get you?"* *"Skilled, lethal and good-looking. Yep, it's a curse."* If you have offended the guild, he will only angrily remark:

*"I'm surprised you're even showing your face around here after what you did."*

## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads *"Syndus has strung bows for as long as he could remember. He has spent most of his life plying his trade in various places all over Tamriel, until finally settling in Solitude. After the city fell back under the Thieves Guild's influence, Syndus decided to apply his knowledge of the ranged weapons for the darker side of the law and made contact with the Guild hoping to strike up a relationship."*
