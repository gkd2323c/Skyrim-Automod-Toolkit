## Walkthrough
The sparse ruins are at the top of a large glacial mound on an island off the north coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are guarded outside by two archer skeletons and three melee skeletons. To the west is an altar overlooking the sea holding a dead skeleton and a leveled [poison](Skyrim_Potions.md#Poisons).

To the east through a novice-locked wooden door is a small burial chamber containing an apprentice-locked boss-level chest guarded by a leveled draugr slumbering in an upright sarcophagus opposite the door. Next to the chest are a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Before the Ages of Man](Skyrim_Before_the_Ages_of_Man.md)*, a leveled [regeneration potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Regeneration.md), and a leveled [lasting potency potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Lasting_Potency.md).
