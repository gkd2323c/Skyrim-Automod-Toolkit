[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/ON-map-Frostwater_Tundra.jpg/200px-ON-map-Frostwater_Tundra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Frostwater_Tundra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Frostwater_Tundra.jpg) Map of Frostwater Tundra circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Frostwater Tundra** is a region located in the hold of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), which is a part of the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Frostwater Tundra consists of the pine forests of **Haunted Wood** that form a ring shape around the hold,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-So S-1)</sup> as well as, the hills that border the autumnal forests of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> The stronghold known as [Fort Amol](Lore_Fort_Amol.md) serves as the seat of power in the region, as well as, the primary settlement in the land. Minor settlements inhabit the area such as [Darkwater Crossing](Lore_Darkwater_Crossing.md) and Wittestadr, but Fort Amol has held much significance than the rest. The ancient keep known as [Mistwatch](Lore_Mistwatch.md) has been used by the [Ternion Monks](Lore_Ternion_Monks.md), a religious group who revere the Ancient Nordic Pantheon, which consists of animals such as the [Whale](Lore_Whale.md) or the [Eagle](Lore_Eagle.md).

The [Aalto](Lore_Aalto.md) sulfur pools of Eastmarch are sometimes considered to be partially within the Tundra, as there are transitional areas where they are mixed, but are more often seen as their own region.
