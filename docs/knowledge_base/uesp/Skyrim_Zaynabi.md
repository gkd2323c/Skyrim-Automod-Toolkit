[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-npc-Zaynabi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Zaynabi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaynabi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaynabi.jpg) Zaynabi **Zaynabi** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [scout](Skyrim_CombatScout.md) who follows [Ahkari](Skyrim_Ahkari.md)'s [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). She will serve as a [fence](Skyrim_Fence_(merchant).md) for [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) members after the [Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest.md) is completed.

Zaynabi wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). She is equipped with a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).
