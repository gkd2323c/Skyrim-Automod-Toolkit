Wild Horse Notes *Notes on where to find wild horses* *
---
Spotted Grey - Near [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), north of [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md).

Dapple Brown - Near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), across the river from [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md), near a Dragon Mound.

Chestnut - East of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), not far from the roadside.

Red - West of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), in the open planes.

Spotted White - Lower [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md), in the cliffs north of [Shore's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*.

Pale Mare - East of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), north of [Hollyfrost Farm](Skyrim_Hollyfrost_Farm.md), in the forest nearing the shoreline.

Black - West of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), past the nearby ruins.

If I had the coin, I'd hire a wizard to use [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) and help me find these horses.
