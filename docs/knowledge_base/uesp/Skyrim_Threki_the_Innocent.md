[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-npc-Threki_the_Innocent.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Threki_the_Innocent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Threki_the_Innocent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Threki_the_Innocent.jpg) Threki the Innocent, in Riften Jail **Threki the Innocent** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) imprisoned in [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md). She wears only a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and a pair of [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps). She is one of three prisoners initially held in the Riften Jail; the two others are [Molgrom Twice-Killed](Skyrim_Molgrom_Twice-Killed.md) and [Sibbi Black-Briar](Skyrim_Sibbi_Black-Briar.md).

If spoken to, she claims she is innocent and blames the corrupt nature of [the city](Skyrim_Riften.md) for her sentence; she says she was thrown into jail only for speaking her mind about [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), whom she believes to be a liar and deceiver. This evidently did not go well with the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) -sympathizing population.

## Dialogue
When approached, she will show outrage over her imprisonment and can possibly say:

*"Let me out of here, damn it! I'm not a criminal!"* *"This is what happens to you when you speak your mind in this city."* *"They threw me in here because I called Ulfric a liar; a deceiver."* *"Ulfric doesn't care about [Talos](Lore_Talos.md). He yearns for the throne of Skyrim and he'll say anything to rally people to his side."* *"But now, because of Ulfric's greed, countless lives have been lost."* *"On second thought, maybe I'm better off in here. At least I'll die with a clear conscience."*
## Notes
- If the Imperial Legion controls Riften, she will still remain in prison. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. She is disabled from the game.
- If you steal from the Riften Jail, Threki may send [Hired Thugs](Skyrim_Hired_Thug.md) after you. This doesn't make sense, as it is unknown where she got the gold to hire the thugs, and which belongings you could have taken that were hers. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.4, fixes this bug.
- It's possible to access Threki's cell, but you must be a vampire. You need to be arrested or open the cell you'd be put in if you were arrested. Wait until Threki is asleep (in the bed closest to your cell), sneak close to the wall where she sleeps, and press the feed option. You'll be transported into her cell to feed, but it's impossible to get out without committing a crime and being arrested again.
