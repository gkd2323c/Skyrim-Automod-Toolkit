[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) Cespar as shown on the Map of West Tamriel Cespar (also spelled **Caspar**) is a large island off the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), south of [Herne](Lore_Herne_(island).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mo WT-1)</sup>

## History
Sometime in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the former noblewoman, Lady Benwyne Thronebane was exiled to the island of Cespar and lived there since at least [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). When the Painted Eye took over the citadel [Borderwatch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Borderwatch) in southern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), they made a demand to free Lady Thronebane but it was later revealed that all of their demands were a ruse.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTPE-2)</sup> The island was considered by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) to be part of Hammerfell's territory in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MCMap-3)</sup> By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the island appeared to be unaffiliated with any province on certain maps.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OCMap-4)</sup>

Tralan the Two-Blade, a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) swordsman who became one of Emperor [Titus Mede I](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md)'s most valued men, hailed from Cespar.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIC-5)</sup><sup>:98</sup>

## Notes
- The original spelling **Caspar** is seen on the map of West Tamriel, but the island is later called Cespar in *The Infernal City* due to most fan maps at the time misspelling the name.
