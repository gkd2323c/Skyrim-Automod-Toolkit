[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg) A Bristleback **Boar Tusks** are ingredients dropped by [bristlebacks](Skyrim_Bristleback.md) and [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md).

Any [Fortify Stamina](Skyrim_Fortify_Stamina.md) potion made with a boar tusk lasts for 300 seconds rather than the standard 60. The potions are 7.5 times more valuable than typical Fortify Stamina potions, which outperforms [Giant's Toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md) for making the second most expensive potions (the most expensive potions all involve [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>). There are eight potions with 43 combinations that are all more valuable than the most expensive potion made with Giant's Toes that does not involve Boar Tusks or Salmon Roe, though the most expensive with Boar Tusk has one combination involving Giant's Toe (Boar Tusk, Giant's Toe, and [Large Antlers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Large_Antlers)).

## Ingredients
15 guaranteed samples can be found in the following 2 locations:

- 14 in [Bristleback Cave](Skyrim_Bristleback_Cave.md)
- 1 in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md), on a shelf next to an alchemy table

## Creatures
Bristlebacks will nearly always drop either a boar tusk, [boar meat](Skyrim_Boar_Meat.md) or both. Other than Bristleback Cave, the best location to find bristlebacks is [Castle Karstaag Caverns](Skyrim_Castle_Karstaag_Caverns.md).
