# Creatures

This page provides a list of all **creature** types in *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*.

- **[Animals](Skyrim_Animals.md)** – Animals comprise various mundane creatures that have no magical or weapon-wielding abilities.
- - [Ash Hopper](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) • [Bristleback](Skyrim_Bristleback.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Chicken](Skyrim_Chicken.md) • [Cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) • [Deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) • [Dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) • [Fox](Skyrim_Fox.md) • [Goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) • [Horker](Skyrim_Horker.md) • [Horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) • [Mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) • [Mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) • [Nix-Hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound_(creature).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Nix-Hound.md)</sup> • [Rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit.md) • [Sabre Cat](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) • [Skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) • [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) • [Wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md)

- **[Ash Creatures](Skyrim_Ash_Creatures.md)** – Horrific monsters associated with [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). Note that [Ash Zombies](Skyrim_Ash_Zombie.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup> are considered [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md), not creatures.
- - [Ash Guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

- **[Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md)** – Daedra are creatures from the planes of Oblivion. Note that [Golden Saints](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>, [Dark Seducers](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup> and [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) are [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md), not creatures, and therefore are not listed here.
- - [Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) • [Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Frost_Atronach.md) • [Lurker](Skyrim_Lurker.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Seeker](Skyrim_Seeker.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md)

- **[Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md)** – Dwarven Automatons are ancient, robot-like machines encountered in [Dwarven Ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md).
- - [Dwarven Ballista](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ballista.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Dwarven Centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) • [Dwarven Sphere](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) • [Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md)

- **[Monsters](Skyrim_Monsters.md)** – Monsters are creatures with various magical abilities, ranging from magical attacks to various magical resistances.
- - [Burnt Spriggan](Skyrim_Burnt_Spriggan.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) • [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) • [Elytra Nymph](Skyrim_Elytra_Nymph.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup> • [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) • [Fire Wyrm](Skyrim_Fire_Wyrm.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Frost Giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> • [Frostbite Spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) • [Gargoyle](Skyrim_Gargoyle.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> • [Giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) • [Goblin](Skyrim_Goblin.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Goblins.md)</sup> • [Hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) • [Ice Wraith](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md) • [Magic Anomaly](Skyrim_Magic_Anomaly.md) • [Netch](Skyrim_Netch.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Riekling](Skyrim_Riekling.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Spiders](Skyrim_Spiders.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) • [Troll](Skyrim_Troll.md) • [Werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) • [Wisp](Skyrim_Wisp.md)

- **[Passive Creatures](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md)** – Passive creatures are completely unable to attack or harm you.
- - [Aquatic Creatures](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Aquatic_Creatures) • [Food](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Food) • [Insects](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Insects) • [Other Creatures](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Other_Creatures)
