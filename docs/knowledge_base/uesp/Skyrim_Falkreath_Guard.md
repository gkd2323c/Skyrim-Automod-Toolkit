[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Falkreath_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Falkreath_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Falkreath_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Falkreath_Guard.jpg) Falkreath Guard **Falkreath Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md). Should the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) forces gain control of Falkreath, they will be replaced by [Stormcloak guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Guard) under the service of [Jarl Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md).

Falkreath guards are clad in a set of Falkreath guard's equipment. This includes a [Falkreath guard's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Armor), a [Falkreath guard's helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Helmet), and a [Falkreath guard's shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Guard%27s_Shield). They also wear a pair of [fur boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots). They are equipped with an [Imperial sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword) and an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow) supplemented with 20 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). They also carry a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a 50% chance of a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), and a variety of [leveled loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists).
