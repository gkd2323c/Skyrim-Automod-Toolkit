The Library of Andule *Instructions on how to enter the center of the library*
---
[Herein](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md) lies the knowledge of the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of the [High Velothi culture](Lore_Chimer.md#High_Velothi_Culture). Light the Braziers of Knowledge and Wisdom before admittance can be gained. In darkness, the Wards of Andule yield to no [mer](Lore_Mer.md).

Trust in the Prophet [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md). Follow the path of tiles from [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) to [Exodus](Lore_Veloth.md#The_Exodus) to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and like the [Velothi](Lore_Chimer.md), you will find what you seek.
