[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-item-Ring_of_the_Moon.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_the_Moon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Moon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Moon.jpg) Ring of the Moon The Ring of the Moon is a ring that can be bought from [Majni](Skyrim_Majni.md) in [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md) if you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md). The ring's appearance is identical to a [silver garnet ring](Skyrim_Silver_Garnet_Ring.md) and also has the same weight. Its enchantment [increases the duration of your werewolf howls by 25%](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Howl.md). Multiple copies of this ring can be bought once Majni's merchant chest resets.

## Notes
- While the ring applies the effect *Frostmoon Howl* to display the enchantment text, the actual effect is applied by a perk also called *[Frostmoon Howl](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Howl.md)* and is added separately upon transformation.
