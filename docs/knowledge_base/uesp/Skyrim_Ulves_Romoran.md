[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Ulves_Romoran.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulves_Romoran.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulves_Romoran.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulves_Romoran.jpg) Ulves Romoran **Ulves Romoran** is the [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) cook of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md), working for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md). He can be found in the [Tel Mithryn Kitchen](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.md).

Ulves wears some farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f). He wields an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen_Key) and a small amount of gold.

Initially, he can be found at night mindlessly working on the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md). After the stone is [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), he'll then head back to Tel Mithryn. He'll now spend all of his time cooking in the kitchen, stopping only to sleep from 10pm to 6am.

## Dialogue
Once you free him from the stone he will say:

*"Oh dear. Why am I out here? I need to get back and make Master Neloth's tea."* *"This is just dreadful. Dreadful."* His greetings:

*"Damnable ash! No matter how much I wash the food, it still gets in it."* *"I hope you're not expecting a meal. I've barely got enough for them that live here."* *"Still can't figure out why I was working on that damned pillar."* *"I'd better tell Varona to bring apples and cabbage from [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)."* **([Varona Nelas](Skyrim_Varona_Nelas.md) alive)** *"I can't believe Varona is dead."* **(Varona Nelas dead)**
### Conversations
Before Varona dies, he can be seen having the following conversations with her:

**Varona**: *"Where is your list of things you need from Raven Rock?"*
**Ulves**: *"I would have had it ready, but Master Neloth wanted his meal early."*
**Varona**: *"Well I can't wait forever. Master Neloth keeps asking if I have his soul gems yet."*
**Ulves**: *"Well you'll just have to wait. Last time I brought his meal up late he asked if I wanted to be a part of one of his experiments."*

**Ulves**: *"If you're here about the [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root) tea, it's almost ready."*
**Varona**: *"I don't know how he drinks that stuff."*
**Ulves**: *"At least you don't have to taste every time to see if it's brewed right."*

During the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md), while you're searching for Varona, you can ask him if he's seen her:

**Have you seen Varona?** *"Is it tea time already? Well, Neloth's just going to have to wait. Varona went to Raven Rock to arrange for supplies."* As you exit conversation:

*"The Raven Rock road is dangerous. I hope nothing happened to Varona."* After you find Varona dead:

**Varona is dead. I found her body on the road.** *"Dead? Truly? Oh, Varona. She didn't deserve this. This is a dark day for Tel Mithryn. Go tell Master Neloth. When I'm done here, I'll prepare her grave."*
