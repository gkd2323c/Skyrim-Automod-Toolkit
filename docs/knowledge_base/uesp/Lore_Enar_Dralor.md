Enar Dralor was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) member of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) who served in both the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) and [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) orders of the faith. He held the rank of Ordinator [Knight](Lore_Knights.md) Commander in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ED-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg/200px-MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg) Ghostgate It is unknown when Enar Dralor was born. Despite being an Armiger,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOV-3)</sup> and a member of the Dralor family with known ties to [Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Redoran-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Manor-5)</sup> and [Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TD-6)</sup> he was a member of the [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md), as he wore the [Daedric Face of Terror](Lore_Daedric_Faces.md#Daedric_Face_of_Terror), a relic permitted only to members of this House.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFTDE-7)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), he already held the rank of Knight Commander of the Ordinators,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup> and the rank of Disciple within the Tribunal Temple. He was also a Buoayant Armiger of an unknown rank. As of then he was stationed at [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md), where he helped safeguard the rest of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) from the threat contained within the [Ghostfence](Lore_Ghostfence.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ED-1)</sup> He was known as a person of importance at Ghostgate. Alongside him, Ordinator Grand Commander [Ralyn Othravel](Lore_Ralyn_Othravel.md) and Grand Marshal [Galdal Omayn](Lore_Galdal_Omayn.md) of the Buoyant Armigers were stationed there.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup>

He donned high quality [malachite](Lore_Malachite.md) armaments and wielded a glass jinkblade and shield. Dralor also held the honor of donning the Daedric Face of Terror,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ED-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFOT-8)</sup> a helmet associated with [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) and illegal for any but members of the House Indoril to wear.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFTDE-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Although the Buoyant Armigers and the Ordinators were seen as rivals,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GDB-9)</sup> there were instances where commanders led both orders into battle. One such example was Ordinator [Rangidil Ketil](Lore_Rangidil_Ketil.md), who commanded both the Ordinators and the Buoyant Armigers in [Abernanit](Lore_Abernanit.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DBOA-10)</sup>
