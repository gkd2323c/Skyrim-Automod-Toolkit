[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/AR-place-Corten_Mont.jpg/200px-AR-place-Corten_Mont.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Corten_Mont.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Corten_Mont.jpg) Cartmont during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Cartmont (also called **Corten Mont**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> is a location found in south-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), found in between the city-states of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) and [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>

Cartmont has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> and was also referred to as Corten Mont by the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-HRP-3)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Corten Mont was an active settlement was ruled by Lady Vaman-si and had a rivalry with [Karnver Falls](Lore_Karnver_Falls.md) and [Chaseguard](Lore_Chaseguard.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> Cartmont is neighbored by several settlements, including [Helland](Lore_Helland.md) to the east, [Riverpoint](Lore_Riverpoint.md) to the north, and [Roseguard](Lore_Roseguard.md) to the west.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>
