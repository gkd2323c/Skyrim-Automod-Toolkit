Not all dragonflies are orange dartwings; some are [blue dartwings](Skyrim_Blue_Dartwing.md). The two types of dartwings share the [Fortify Pickpocket](Skyrim_Fortify_Pickpocket.md) alchemical effect.

- *"A surprising number of insects survive in Skyrim's climate, many thriving in the lower, southern areas of the province. Dragonflies can be found in a great many places, and while catching them can be a daunting prospect, the reward is well worth the effort. It was beyond the scope of my research to determine whether the orange and blue dragonflies are fully different breeds or merely simple color variations, but through experimentation I found that the **orange dragonfly**, when combined with the very hardy [barnacles](Skyrim_Nordic_Barnacle.md) found along the coast, transfer some of their flighty nature, giving the herbalist some [very nimble fingers](Skyrim_Fortify_Pickpocket.md)."* —Agneta Falia in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*.

## Creatures
Dragonflies spawn at 769 sites, in 138 different locations, typically near [bodies of water](Skyrim_Bodies_of_Water.md) (rivers, lakes, or ponds). Each spawning site can generate a random mix of up to 10 orange- and blue-winged dragonflies. The dragonflies are spawned between 5am and 8pm; some of the spawning sites instead generate [torchbugs](Skyrim_Torchbug.md) at night.

## Notes
- Both the orange and blue dartwings have their wings on backwards.
- Dartwings can be difficult to catch, due to their small size (making them harder to see) and speed. Using a word of a shout (such as [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) or [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md)) causes them to fall to the ground. However, if you do this over deep or swiftly moving water, it will complicate finding them afterwards.
- Orange Dartwings are only considered "uncommon" ingredients in the apothecary merchant chest lists. In [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Containers.md#Apothecary.27s_Satchel) and enemy spellcasters' pockets, they are considered a "common" ingredient.
- Orange dartwings are called "orange dragonfly wing" in alchemy recipes in the game world.
