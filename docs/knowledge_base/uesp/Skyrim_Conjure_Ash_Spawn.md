**Conjure Ash Spawn** is an adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons an [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Notes
- Not affected by the [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls) Perk.
