[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Rayya.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rayya.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rayya.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rayya.jpg) Rayya **Rayya** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) found in the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). She becomes your personal [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) when you have earned the title of [Thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md).

She wears a set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor) and an [Alik'r hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alik%27r_Hood). She dual-wields two [scimitars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar).

## Notes
- Rayya is one of the few [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) in Skyrim who will dual wield as long as shields and two-handed weapons are not in her inventory. Like all followers who are capable of [dual wielding](Skyrim_Combat.md#Dual-wielding_2), Rayya must be in actual combat before she will unsheathe the second weapon but will only unsheathe one weapon while out of combat.
- Rayya is the only non-Nord housecarl given to you.

## Bugs
- Rayya's two starting scimitars are listed twice in the inventory menu, but each listing is "stacked", showing "Scimitar (2)". When you remove one, a "stack" of two is removed; the same applies to the second "stack". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- When you remove one scimitar from Rayya's inventory, she unequips both even though one still remains in her inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes when you try to dismiss her, she will not return to her "home". She will not wait when in that state.
