[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-npc-Bagrak.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bagrak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bagrak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bagrak.jpg) Bagrak in Mor Khazgur **Bagrak** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc1H) living in [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). She is the daughter of [Sharamph](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sharamph), and thus the sister of [Chief Larak](Skyrim_Chief_Larak.md). She wears a leveled set of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md) and wields a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md) and a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), all of which may also be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality. She also carries a pair of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers), a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common) and carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Larak's Longhouse](Skyrim_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md).

## Dialogue
When spoken to she can say:

- *"You should see my mother, Sharamph, if you need a cure or a poison."*
- *"[Shuftharz](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shuftharz) can forge you some armor, outlander."*
- *"Larak is strong, but he has much to prove as chief."*
- *"[Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) protect you."*

## Unused Dialogue
Bagrak has unused dialogue tied to a quest she was once meant to give you. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

- **I'd like to join you on the hunt.**
- *"Follow me. And don't stop until the dominant male is dead."*

This line would've presumably been used if you spoke to her while hunting before your prey was dead:

- *"Follow me, outlander."*

After you did kill the target, she'd reward you:

- *"A short hunt, but you did well. Here, on behalf of my late husband."*

If you spoke to her after this before you returned to the stronghold:

- *"I'll meet you back at the stronghold."*

The line where she mentions her "late husband" is interesting, as it implies Bagrak was possibly married off to another stronghold at some point in the past, as Orcish tribal women generally don't remain in their home strongholds.

## Notes
- The game data lists Bagrak as `In Law Grandchild` of Sharamph and as spouse to Larak, leading to some confusion.
