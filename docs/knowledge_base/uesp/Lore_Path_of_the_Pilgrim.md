Path of the Pilgrim **by [Bikkus-Ze](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Bikkus-Ze), Hissmir Oblate** *Notes on the pilgrims of Hissmir*
---
While many of the purportedly civilized nations refer to our homeland as [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), the pilgrims and priests of [Hissmir](Lore_Hissmir.md) know better. The lands of Argonia are far from a garbage heap. The soil is rich and full of life. Plant and animal life flourishes. The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) speaks to those who listen.

Our [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) brothers and sisters who venture outside miss a piece of themselves if they are unable to commune with the Hist. It is for this reason pilgrims from across the land travel to Hissmir and offer up themselves to the trials and communing.
