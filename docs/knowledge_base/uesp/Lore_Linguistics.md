Linguistics is the study of languages and scripts throughout the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md). [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md), a scholar of languages, theorized that tongues may have an intrinsic [magical](Lore_Magic.md) property known as **word-magic**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Words-1)</sup> Words are sometimes used in magical incantations, which can be subverted by the use of silencing magic.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Silence-2)</sup>
