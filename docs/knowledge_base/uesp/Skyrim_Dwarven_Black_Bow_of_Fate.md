[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-item-Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.jpg) The Dwarven Black Bow of Fate The Dwarven Black Bow of Fate is a unique [Dwarven bow](Skyrim_Dwarven_Bow.md) found in the Dwarven ruin of [Kagrumez](Skyrim_Kagrumez.md) after completing all three puzzles. The bow has a similar structural shape to a regular Dwarven bow, except that it is made of [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) and its color scheme is much darker. It does one more point of base damage than other Dwarven Bows, putting it on par with [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md#Elven_Bow) and [Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic#Nordic_Bow).

Its enchantment gives a 50% chance of absorbing 25 points from the target's [Health](Skyrim_Health.md), [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md), and/or [Magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md), meaning that on any given strike you may absorb points for none, one, two, or all three (similarly to [Chaos Damage](Skyrim_Chaos_Damage.md)). It can be tempered with an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) with the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Smithing.md#Arcane_Blacksmith) perk unlocked, and benefits from the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[The Kagrumez Gauntlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Kagrumez_Gauntlet)**: Survive the [Kagrumez](Skyrim_Kagrumez.md) trials to claim a unique prize.

## Notes
- The [Arcane Archer Pack](Skyrim_Arcane_Archer_Pack.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds matching black Dwarven arrows called [Soul Stealer Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Stealer_Arrow), which can be found next to the bow and can subsequently be [crafted](Skyrim_Crafting.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
- This item cannot be disenchanted.
- When the bow is equipped and drawn, there are orange, glowing designs around the grip which resemble the patterns on the casings of [Elder Scrolls](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md). There are also intricate patterns that appear to be constellations near the grip, as well.
- [Harkon's sword](Skyrim_Harkon%27s_Sword.md) is one other weapon in the game with a similar enchantment, though its enchantment only works if the wielder is a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md), and all three attributes are absorbed at once, not the chance absorption that there is with the Black Bow of Fate.
