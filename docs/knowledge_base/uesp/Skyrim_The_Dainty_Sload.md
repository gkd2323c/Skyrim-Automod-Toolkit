## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).
2. Find out the mission details from [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
3. Acquire [Balmora Blue](Skyrim_Balmora_Blue.md) from [Sabine Nytte](Skyrim_Sabine_Nytte.md).
4. Travel to the [Dainty Sload](Skyrim_Dainty_Sload.md).
5. Plant the Balmora Blue.
6. Return to Erikur.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Special Job
Once you've completed five [jobs](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Special_Job_Quests) in the city of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), speak to [Delvin](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) in [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) to receive a special job. Delvin explains that he has received word from Solitude about an opportunity. [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md) is an influential [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) and businessman in Solitude. Apparently, he is *"lookin' to run some kind of a [shill job](Skyrim_The_Shill_Job.md)"*. Completing this job will not only earn you a reward, it is a chance for the Guild to regain some influence in the city. Travel to Solitude and speak with Erikur, who can be found during the day at the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md).

When you speak to Erikur, he explains the story behind the job: *"Nothing raises my ire more than having an agreement broken. It's bad for business and it wastes time. Captain Volf of the [Dainty Sload](Skyrim_Dainty_Sload.md) has decided to test my patience on this matter by neglecting to honor a trade agreement we had established."* Your job is to frame Volf for possession of an illicit substance by acquiring some very rare, very illegal [Balmora Blue](Skyrim_Balmora_Blue.md) and planting it in his footlocker.

### From One Pirate to Another
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-quest-The_Dainty_Sload.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Dainty_Sload.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Dainty_Sload.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Dainty_Sload.jpg) Sabine's Footlocker, hidden beneath the dock leading to the Red Wave. Your contact for the contraband is a crew member aboard the pirate ship, the [Red Wave](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md). The Red Wave is docked outside the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) just outside Solitude. Your contact, [Sabine Nytte](Skyrim_Sabine_Nytte.md), will be on deck, waiting for you. Tell her what you're looking for, and she will ask for 1,500 gold for some Balmora Blue, claiming that she's the only person in Tamriel who can obtain it. If you accept her price, she gives you the key and directs you to the contraband, which is in a locked chest directly under the docks adjacent to The Red Wave. If you do not wish to pay such inflated prices, sneak into the bottom deck of the ship and locate the Red Wave Safe, which is guarded by a Master-level lock. Inside, you will learn from a note that the contraband is located "under the Wave", leading you to Sabine's Footlocker (though reading the note is not necessary for you to loot the contraband). Swim down below the docks and pick the Expert-level lock to the chest and take the Balmora Blue. Note that both locks can be skipped by pickpocketing the corresponding keys off of Sabine first.

### A Pirate Breaks the Law
Make your way out to the Dainty Sload, anchored near [Solitude's Lighthouse](Skyrim_Solitude_Lighthouse.md). There are two [corsairs](Skyrim_Corsair.md) guarding the entrance to the interior of the ship. One remains looking out to sea, while another roams the deck. Engage them or sneak below deck through the unlocked door. Inside, there are more corsairs to deal with. Whether by sneaking or defeating the corsairs, head down the stairs into the bottom of the ship, then back up again until you reach the Captain's room.

The ship's [first mate](Skyrim_First_Mate.md) guards the area and has a high detection level. The Captain's Chest is your target and you can pick the adept lock, or you can kill the First Mate and take the key. On the table is an [Unusual Gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) and the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill book *[A Dance in Fire, V6](Skyrim_A_Dance_in_Fire,_v6.md)*. Plant the Balmora Blue and make your way back to the entrance.

### Revenge is Sweet
Travel back to Erikur at the Blue Palace (or at his home), who is very pleased with the fruits of your labor. He'll reward you with a random [spell tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md) for a novice or apprentice level Destruction or Illusion spell, and the assurance that he will re-open any doors in Solitude to facilitate the Guild's return.

Report back to Delvin. He'll give you your pay, and will compliment you on your actions, *"Erikur assures me that Captain Volf will spend pretty much the rest of his life clapped in irons. Best of all, he's also pledged to open doors for us in Solitude and to get things rollin' again"*. He then points out that the added infamy you earned the guild has attracted a new merchant to The Ragged Flagon.

## Notes
- If the Dainty Sload has been cleared of its inhabitants prior to starting this quest, the ship and its inhabitants will be fully reset, including the First Mate.
- When the quest is completed, you can retrieve more Balmora Blue from the chest.
- After completing this quest, three [Thief Cache](Skyrim_Thief_Cache.md) barrels will appear around Solitude.
- Despite Erikur and Delvin mentioning that Captain Volf will spend the rest of his years in Solitude Jail, he cannot be found in any of the cells and in fact does not appear in the game at all.
- You can kill the crew of the Dainty Sload, including the First Mate, without any repercussions.

## Bugs
- Erikur may have glitched off the map. This usually occurs in games where a lot of days have passed. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Type into console: `prid 000198A9` (this selects Erikur) followed by `moveto player` (this moves Erikur to your current location).
- He can be reached by getting underneath the regular map. To do so, hop onto the Angeline's Aromatics roof and use Whirlwind Sprint to reach the lower roof of Bits and Pieces. Head west along the roof and hop into the space that joins to the roof of Radiant Raiment, then navigate to Erikur's quest marker.
- Sometimes Sabine Nytte will get stuck inside the Red Wave and not come out. This is problematic as you are considered to be trespassing when inside the ship. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) One way get around the bug is to type `setcellownership solituderedwave` into the console.
- Another way to fix it is to pickpocket Sabine and get caught. She will then get up to attack you, followed by her fellow shipmates. Run outside and they will follow. Lower your weapons to surrender and she and her shipmates will stop fighting and walk towards the entrance to the ship. Before they get there, talk to Sabine and ask her about the illegal substance.
- The Dainty Sload ship may not render correctly, causing you to fall through the floor and become unable to enter the ship. - Reloading a previous save fixes this.
- The Balmora Blue may not drop into your inventory after you've taken it from the chest. - Balmora Blue may drop in your inventory in food selection instead of potions selection.
- Reloading a previous save fixes this.
- If you do not have a previous save available, it is impossible to complete the quest. Which makes the achievement "One With the Shadows" also impossible to obtain.
- Sometimes Delvin Mallory will talk to you about starting the quest, however the quest will not start and the option about the job will not appear again in Delvin's dialogue options. - Reloading a save before speaking to Delvin about the quest may fix this.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Type into console: `setstage TGTQ02 10`. This will start the quest.
- The Balmora Blue that you need to plant in the Captain's Chest on the ship is never removed from the chest after planting it there. The Balmora Blue also loses its quest item status afterwards, allowing you to take it back out of the Captain's Chest, which make no sense at all since it would be taken from there as evidence against Captain Volf. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
