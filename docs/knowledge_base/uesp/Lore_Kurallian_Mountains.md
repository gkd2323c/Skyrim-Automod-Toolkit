[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/ON-place-The_Doomcrag_03.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Doomcrag_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Doomcrag_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Doomcrag_03.jpg) The Doomcrag Spire in the Kurallians The Kurallian Mountains are a mountain range in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), specifically in the region of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md). The Kurallian Mountains make up the mountain range throughout Rivenspire, encompassing both the city-state of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md), as well as the spire known as the [Doomcrag](Lore_Doomcrag.md). Shornhelm is found upon the foothills of the Kurallian, along with the towns of Crestshade and Fell's Run.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

## History
[Gyron Vardengroet](Lore_Gyron_Vardengroet.md), otherwise known as the Sage, had initially traveled through the foothills to reach Shornhelm, where he went under the tutelage of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) Guildmaster, Morkledder. When his training was ultimately complete from the Council of Wizards at the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md), the Sage retired deep into the Kurallian Mountains, where he built a magical fortress where only those deemed worthy would enter.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Ever since then, monuments of the Sage had existed all throughout the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), from as far as [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
