Journal *A dying man's last thoughts* *
---
Well, after all my years living in these woods, it looks like the [Rockjoint](Skyrim_Rockjoint.md) will finally be the end of me. I guess that's fine. All my friends are long dead. The only one left is poor [Meeko](Skyrim_Meeko.md). He was always a loyal companion, and I know he'll be able to take care of himself. I hope someday I'll see him again.
