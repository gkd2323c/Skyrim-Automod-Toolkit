—Aneshi the Shrine Keeper<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup> Aneshi was a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) scholar and custodian who served as the Castellan of Rimmen Palace during the rule of King [Hemakar](Lore_Hemakar.md) and Queen [Numara](Lore_Numara.md). Later, he became the **Keeper of the Shrine** of [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md), a revered Khajiiti hero.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup> He was an expert on all the heroes and gods of the Khajiiti pantheon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup> As a keeper of records, Aneshi transcribed many legends and tales associated with Khunzar-ri and held the title of **Keeper of Legends for the 16 Kingdoms**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Khun A-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Khun T-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/LG-cardart-Frostfall_Coup.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Frostfall_Coup.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Frostfall_Coup.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Frostfall_Coup.jpg) The Frostfall Coup [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/ON-npc-Khunzar-ri.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Khunzar-ri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Khunzar-ri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Khunzar-ri.jpg) The spirit of Khunzar-ri Little is known of Aneshi's past. At some point, he married Zaima,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup> and became the castellan of Rimmen Palace, serving under King Hemakar.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LGR-5)</sup> While serving as castellan, he oversaw the palace operations and assumed responsibility for the training and education of Princess [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup> who was born in [2E 563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TFC-6)</sup> His wife served as the princess's governess.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup> He had extensive knowledge of the palace and survived [Euraxia Tharn](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md)'s conquest and the [Frostfall Coup](Lore_Frostfall_Coup.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LGR-5)</sup> In [2E 576](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_576),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TFC-6)</sup> this event led to the purge of the royal family.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LGR-5)</sup> Both he and his wife managed to survive the purge of the palace, staying close to the castle for the time being. His wife acted as a spy and passed along information until the Euraxians caught her and killed her in the palace courtyard. Following Zaima's capture and execution, Aneshi fled the city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup> Unknown to him, Princess Khamira managed to survive unlike her parents and siblings. She kept this information secret and refrained from contacting him, hoping it would ensure his safety.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup>

After losing his wife, he sought peace. He traveled south of the city to restore the ancient shrine of Khunzar-ri, which brought him the solace he sought after his beloved's passing. When he arrived, it wasn't a shrine yet—just tumbled walls and crumbling ruins. Aneshi worked hard to preserve the site and restore it to its former glory. As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he still had much work to do in that regard. Despite this, the shrine was visited by pilgrims, [Moon-Singers](Lore_Moon-Singers.md), and traders alike, many came to pay homage to the shrine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup> He transcribed many tales of the legendary hero. Among the known ones were: *[Khunzar-ri and the Lost Alfiq](Lore_Khunzar-ri_and_the_Lost_Alfiq.md)*,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Khun A-3)</sup> *[Khunzar-ri: Origin](Lore_Khunzar-ri__Origin.md)*,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Khun O-7)</sup> as well as *[Khunzar-ri: Tales](Lore_Khunzar-ri__Tales.md)*,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Khun T-4)</sup> including tomes such as *[Khunzar-ri and the Twelve Ogres](Lore_Khunzar-ri__Tales,_One.md)*,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Khun T1-8)</sup> and *[One of the Times Anequina Saved Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri__Tales,_Two.md)*.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Khun T2-9)</sup>

As of that year, he was already an elderly individual. During the rebellion of the Khajiit against Euraxia's terror, Lord Gharesh-ri, the Speaker of the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md), sent the rightful heir to the throne of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), Khamira, whom Aneshi knew personally from her childhood years, along with [an agent](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to obtain information on how to access the palace grounds.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LGR-5)</sup> He provided the aid and information they needed, instructing them to enter the palace through the crypts to avoid attracting attention. A forgotten passage in the crypts led to the royal kitchens. He wrote down directions to the location of his hidden master key,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AN-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AN2-11)</sup> which opened a door in the sewer under the palace that led to the crypts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-RMK-12)</sup> He also spoke briefly with Khamira in private before the mission, discussing the old king, Zaima, and life in the palace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aneshi-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Khamira-2)</sup> His assistance was instrumental to the mission, as it aided [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md), Khamira and the agent's infiltration of the castle, contributing to the eventual death of Euraxia and the restoration of the Khajiit monarchy.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Abnur_Tharn-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-2Q-14)</sup>
