It is acquired through the [Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md) quest. If you are on the Imperial side, it will be used by the [Stormcloak Champion](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Champion.md) and can be acquired after defeating him, however, if you are on the Stormcloak's side it will be given to you to use against the [Imperial Champion](Skyrim_Imperial_Champion.md).
