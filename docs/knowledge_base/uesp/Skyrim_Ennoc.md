[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Ennoc.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ennoc.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennoc.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennoc.jpg) Ennoc inside Left Hand Mine **Ennoc** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in the [Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) for the [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine) in which he works.

Ennoc will spend all of his time mining, not ever breaking from his duties to eat or sleep. He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699), and he carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks) to the Barracks, and some [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
