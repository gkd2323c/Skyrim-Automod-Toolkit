## Ingredients
49 guaranteed samples can be found in 32 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 3 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 2 in [Solitude Blacksmith](Skyrim_Solitude_Blacksmith.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in the [Thalmor Embassy, Elenwen's Solar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy,_Elenwen%27s_Solar) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Vittoria Vici's House](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Can be created at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) from: 1 [Amethyst](Skyrim_Amethyst.md) + 1 [Soul Gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) + 1 [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile)

## Creatures
Void salts are dropped by all varieties of storm atronach, including [Pelagius's storm thrall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius%27s_Storm_Thrall), [potent storm atronachs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Storm_Atronach), and [storm thralls](Skyrim_Storm_Thrall_(creature).md).

## Notes
- 1 sample of void salts is needed at a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve [Nightingale armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Armor), [Nightingale boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Boots), [Nightingale gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Gloves), or [Nightingale hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Hood) (requires the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk).
- Void salts are needed at the Atronach Forge to create various storm atronach-related items, namely: a hostile storm atronach, a [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach), a [Staff of the Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Storm_Atronach), or a *[Spell Tome: Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Storm_Atronach)*.
- Void salts are also one of 4 items needed at the Atronach Forge to create random enchanted [Daedric armor](Skyrim_Daedric.md#Daedric_Armor) (requires the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone)).
- Once certain [schematics](Skyrim_Dwemer_Exploding_Shock_Bolt_Schematic.md) are discovered during the [Ancient Technology](Skyrim_Ancient_Technology.md) quest, one sample of void salts is required for crafting ten [Exploding Steel Bolt of Shock](Skyrim_Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Shock.md) or ten [Exploding Dwarven Bolt of Shock](Skyrim_Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Shock.md) at [Gunmar's Forge](Skyrim_Gunmar%27s_Forge.md).<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup>
- Although void salts come from Storm Atronachs (which are immune to shock damage), void salts instead have the Weakness to Shock effect.

## Bugs
- Fine-cut void salts may cause all of your regular void salts to be unusable to upgrade blacksmithing items such as the Nightingale armor. - To fix this, simply give the fine-cut void salts to Captain Wayfinder in Dawnstar.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Alternatively, remove all of your void salts (as they all have the same ID) by using the command `player.removeitem 3AD60 X` (`X` being how many you have both fine-cut and regular combined). Then type `player.additem 3AD60 X` to get them back. You should now be able to use your void salts again.
- Combining void salts with the fine-cut void salts will consume two void salts from your ordinary void salt pile, so long as you have two that can be consumed, allowing you to keep the fine-cut void salts. Doing this reveals all four effects of the void salts.
