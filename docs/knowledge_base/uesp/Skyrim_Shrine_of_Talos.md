[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-place-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg) Shrine of Talos in Markarth The **Shrine of Talos** is a small shrine in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) dedicated to the worship of the god [Talos](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrine_of_Talos.jpg) Inside the Shrine of Talos It is a small, one-room location that is usually empty. The statue of the forsaken deity is the well-lit center-piece of the otherwise gloomy interior. A [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos) can be found directly in front of the statue; activating it gives you the [Blessing of Talos](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Talos.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).
