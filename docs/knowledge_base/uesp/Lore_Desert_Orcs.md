# Desert Orcs

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/RG-comic-Page_24-Orcs.png/200px-RG-comic-Page_24-Orcs.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_24-Orcs.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_24-Orcs.png) The Ra Gadan invasion [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/ON-map-Hallowed_Wastes.jpg/200px-ON-map-Hallowed_Wastes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Hallowed_Wastes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Hallowed_Wastes.jpg) Map of Hollow Wastes circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) Bangkorai Garrison The Desert Orcs, also known as the **Desert Clans** were [Orcs](Lore_Orcs.md) that inhabited the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup> The presence of Orcs in Hammerfell predates the arrival of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) military forces from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md). [Elves](Lore_Mer.md) and later [Manmeri](Lore_Breton.md) established an outpost at what is now the city of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) in order to protect fisherfolk and seafaring merchants from inland desert Orc marauders, giant [Goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) raiders, and other hazards of the desert.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3H-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGH-3)</sup>

[Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) sources suggest that the disorganized Orcs were swiftly defeated by the Ra Gada when they arrived.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3H-2)</sup> [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) accounts say that the Ra Gada intended to drive the Orcs from Hammerfell to make way for the [king and his royal family](Lore_Na-Totambu.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Origin-4)</sup> waiting on [Herne](Lore_Herne_(island).md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1H-5)</sup> The arrivals of the Ra Gada Warrior Waves led to the decimation of the other local populations, including the Goblins,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TKF-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3H-2)</sup> [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TCOH-7)</sup> [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3H-2)</sup> and [Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONloadash-8)</sup>

The desert Orcs of the [Hollow Wastes](Lore_Hollow_Wastes.md) initially resisted the Ra Gada invaders, but as the Redguard kingdoms gained strength, their clans and strongholds began to fall. Despite facing the common enemy clans continued to bicker.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup> Circa [1E 874](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_874),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BSHR-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AMOB-10)</sup> a warrior-chief named [Thulgeg](Lore_Thulgeg.md) came of age and united the remaining desert clans to defend Clan Korkhugg's holds against the forces of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md). After securing a victory, he was named Warlord by the assembled chieftains, who swore loyalty to him. Realizing that his united Orc forces were not strong enough to overcome the Redguard cities, Thulgeg made the decision to lead his people in an exodus from their ancestral lands toward [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), defeating all Redguard forces sent to stop them. Numerous Goblin tribes allied with them, significantly increasing their numbers. In the [Fallen Wastes](Lore_Fallen_Wastes.md), the Redguards of [Hel Ra](Lore_Hel_Ra_Citadel.md) attempted to halt their advance, but Thulgeg, leading a vanguard of a thousand warriors, carved a path through their enemies, ensuring the desert clans could continue their journey.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BSHR-9)</sup> With a clever strategy, he outmaneuvered the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) of [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md) and successfully guided his people to the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md), the home of [Wrothgarian Orcs](Lore_Wrothgarian_Orcs.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BSHR-9)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SC-11)</sup> There, they contributed to the founding of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup> By the time they arrived, Orsinium already was established.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-PGE1-12)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BSHR-9)</sup> Though the arrival of these Orcish refugees from Hammerfell bolstered the stength of the forces already stationed there by a considerable degree.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-PGE1-12)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), several Orcish strongholds were active in the Alik'r Desert and most of the neighboring desert countries, such as [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md), [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md), [Dak'fron](Lore_Dak%27fron.md), and [Tigonus](Lore_Tigonus_(fiefdom).md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-13)</sup>
