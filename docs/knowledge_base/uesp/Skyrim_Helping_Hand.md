## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md) about her job.
2. Give her a healing potion.

## Detailed Walkthrough
If you walk near [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md), she will cry about losing her job and beg for help. Talk to her, and she will reveal that she has an addiction to [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) and that she wants to cure herself. She will ask for a healing potion in order to heal herself. If you probe further she will mention where she got the skooma from, *"I got mine off of a [merchant ship](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)"*, which ties in with another [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dainty_Sload).

You have three options: agree to give her a potion, agree to find one for her, or refuse to help. If you refuse to give her a potion, the option remains to agree to give her one. If you agree to give her a potion, the lowest type of standard healing potion (e.g. [Potion of Minor Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing)) in your inventory will be removed, and she will reward you with a leveled unenchanted ring while thanking you, *"Your kindness will never be forgotten"*.

Dialogue is closed at this point and the first part of the [next quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid) is immediately added to the miscellaneous quests.

## Notes
- Healing spells do not work and she does not recognize crafted healing potions. She can only take a standard healing potion.
- This is the first of three quests that are required to unlock the option to become [Thane of the Rift](Skyrim_I_Done_Got_Thaned!.md) for [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) or [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md). There are additional requirements noted on that quest's page.
- Raising Wujeeta's disposition with this quest will allow you to freely take items from the fish barrels inside Riften Fishery and around the nearby fishing boats.

## Bugs
- Wujeeta is not essential, and if she dies before this quest and the next are complete, you cannot become Thane of the Rift unless the Imperials take control of the Rift, either through [negotiation](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or [force](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial).md).
