Selene is a [Changeling](Lore_Changeling.md), a rare forest spirit found in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). She is also a powerful sorceress and the High Priestess of [Shagrath](Lore_Shagrath.md), the Goddess of Spiders. She possesses the ability to shapeshift, which she has been witnessed using to take the form of a giant [spider](Lore_Spider.md) and a female humanoid.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Her domain is known as [Selene's Web](Lore_Selene%27s_Web.md) and her followers know her as the **Forest Mother**.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-l ONngclaw-2)</sup>

## History

### Dawn Era
Originally, Selene was a reclusive forest spirit who had done nothing to harm mortals. Time meant little to her, and it is believed that she is a remnant of the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md) who was never given a concrete form by [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md), thus making her one of the earliest creatures of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt CS-3)</sup>

### Second Era
*I am a... spirit. A being of the ancient forest. Some call my kind Changelings.*
—Selene In the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the Great Mage [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) attempted to set up the first continental branch of the newly-formed [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in the [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) capital of [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md), and was sent by [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md) to explore the uncharted wilderness of Selene's Web in an attempt to deter him. Taking up the challenge, Galerion and his guild mages entered Selene's domain and were captured by her. Although hostile to their presence, Selene was curious, and shifted forms from that of a spider to an [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) woman with Galerion's features. She eventually agreed to release the mages provided the Guild would never intrude uninvited again and would bargain on her behalf to prevent the Bosmer from encroaching on her territory. The Silvenar and [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md) were unsettled to discover the existence of a Changeling, but agreed to respect the boundaries of her domain.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt CS-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Warlock Carindon of the Mages Guild betrayed Galerion's accord and launched an expedition into the forests of Selene's Web to research Selene. Unbeknownst to the other expedition members, Carindon planned to kill Selene and steal her soul for dissection, despite a deal she had made with the guild to be left in peace. Carindon also hired the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) for protection. At the time, Selene was worshipped by a tribe of Bosmer and several [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), to whom she granted "spirit life" upon death. Along with her army of woodland creatures, her followers attempted to keep the intruders at bay. Only Carindon and the Undaunted succeeded in reaching her inner sanctum, where Selene was slain and her soul captured. Carindon planned to publish a codex from the knowledge he would obtain from her spirit. He planned to use her aura for various potions, and study her ability to spin "arcane threads" in order to utilize it in a multitude of spell types.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Web-4)</sup>

Later that year, after the Imperial battlemage [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) inadvertently unleashed the [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) from their containment in the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md), many came out of hiding after learning of [Kaalgrontiid](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md)'s return. The Dragon [Maarselok](Lore_Maarselok.md) took residence near the mountains within the [Tenmar](Lore_Tenmar.md) Valley, near the Valenwood and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) border. There, he emitted the dreadful [Azureblight](Lore_Azure_Blight.md) from his body, a dangerous blight that served to twist all life to his will, part of a plot to create an "azure world". Carindon, Selene, the Undaunted, and a pair of Bosmer mediators working for King [Camoran Aeradan](Lore_Camoran_Aeradan.md) was the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) king of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). His reign began after a civil war for sovereignty with his cousin, [Gelthior Camoran](Lore_Gelthior_Camoran.md), but later, he was among the leaders that spearheaded the first iteration of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), as well as, part of the original Inner Council of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md). sought to stop the corruption from spreading to the [Elden Tree](Lore_Elden_Root.md). Carindon reluctantly agreed to loosen his grip on Selene so that she could assist the mediators by cleansing the Azureblight. Selene demanded her freedom in exchange for her assistance, to which the mediators agreed. In the final battle with Maarselok, his corruption took root in Selene, twisting her to his will. The Undaunted and Maarselok fought over control of Selene, but she managed to shake off his influence. After Maarselok was slain, Carindon tried to back down from his promise to release Selene, infuriating her. The mediators intervened, ensnaring Carindon and allowing her to escape.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOScalebreaker-5)</sup> Selene then worked to heal the damage Maarselok caused, steering clear of mortals as she restored the valley.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAllanwen-6)</sup>

The significance of Selene meant that she was documented within the [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). There, copies of various entities were subsequently created as [maligraphies](Lore_Maligraphy.md), creatures composed of vivified ink that manifest from the contents of books and serve as mindless extensions of the [Daedric Lord](Lore_Daedric_Lord.md) [Tho'at Replicanum](Lore_Tho%27at_Replicanum.md). A maligraphic copy of Selene was formed during Tho'at's invasion of the Archive, but it was destroyed by a mortal adventurer, aided by augmentations granted directly by [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md). The defeated maligraphy of Selene faded into a puddle of ink.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAIA-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MM-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Enemies-9)</sup>

### Third Era
Some time after this, a now free Selene constructed a fortress in Selene's Web. She began consorting with undead, and infused her lifeforce into a great jewel, the Heart of Selene. During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), she began to spread her dark influence throughout Valenwood, draining corpses of all fluid and raising them as living dead with no memories of their former existence. The city of Elden Root sent out scouts to investigate, and Selene sent back an emissary with their decapitated heads. She gave the Bosmer an ultimatum: "surrender Valenwood or die". With no standing army, the peaceful Bosmer had no means to defeat her army of spiders and undead. Queen Ulandra of Elden Root struck a deal with the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md), offering to reveal the sacred location of [Elden Grove](Lore_Elden_Grove.md) in exchange for the Heart of Selene. The Champion then infiltrated Selene's Web and claimed the jewel. With it, Ulandra was able to blackmail Selene into calling off her attacks with her life now hanging in the balance.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
