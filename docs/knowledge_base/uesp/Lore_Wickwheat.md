# Wickwheat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Wickwheat.jpg) Wickwheat (Morrowind) Wickwheat is a common wild [ashland grain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grain) found in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It typically grows in the province's plains like the [Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and its grains contain some modest magical properties.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWWickwheat-1)</sup> For the native [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of Morrowind, it is a cheap and easy to come by ingredient for [cooking](Lore_Dunmer_Cuisine.md) and [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAF-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RCS-3)</sup>

Wickwheat is used to make all sorts of food, from common dishes like wickwheat toast,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BJ-4)</sup> crackers and crisps,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AFATD3-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSOT34S-6)</sup> to regional dishes like [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md)'s Crab Meat and Scuttle,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RCS-3)</sup> and [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md) wickwheat [ale](Lore_Ale.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONNarsis-7)</sup> The [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) clergy in [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) used to eat wickwheat crackers with a topping representing the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AFATD3-5)</sup>

It is also an easily accessible ingredient for alchemy. In Morrowind, wickwheat mixed equal parts with [corkbulb root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Corkbulb_Root), [marshmerrow](Lore_Marshmerrow.md), and/or [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md) can create a potion of restore health.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAF-2)</sup> For any villager or pauper, it is easier to make a poultice like this than to consult a healer or priest.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-THFG-8)</sup> Some of its positive properties include restoring one's health and fortifying one's willpower, but its negatives include [paralysis](Lore_Paralysis.md) and damaging one's intelligence.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWWickwheat-1)</sup>

The [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md) regularly use wickwheat to build their huts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TIOW-9)</sup> These structures, known as yurts, are made with other materials as well as such as wood, hides, and sinew.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-EGT-10)</sup> Farming settlements like the [Velothi](Lore_Velothi.md) village of [Vos](Lore_Vos.md) harvest wickwheat and sell it.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-11)</sup>
