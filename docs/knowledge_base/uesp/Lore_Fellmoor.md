[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SI-place-Fellmoor.jpg/200px-SI-place-Fellmoor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Fellmoor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Fellmoor.jpg) Fellmoor circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Fellmoor is a village in the [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It lies along the western coast, southeast of [the Fringe](Lore_Fringe.md), and can be reached via Pinnacle Road. The settlement is dedicated to [Water Root Pod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Water_Root_Pod) farming. These bulbous plants are grown in shallow water and harvested for their [Pod Pits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Water_Root_Pod_Pit).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

Fellmoor is noted for the many varieties of [wine](Lore_Wine.md#Fellmoor_Wine) produced there. Fellmoor Red Wine, Fellmoor Spore Wine, and Fellmoor Swamp Wine are all popular beverages among the mortal inhabitants of the Isles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the farm was being run by a neurotic [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) woman named Cindanwe, whose strong work ethic made her unpopular with the field hands.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
