[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-item-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg) Ruin's Edge Ruin's Edge is a [dark](Skyrim_Dark.md) [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) artifact with an animated eyeball just above the grip. It is acquired through the quest [The Edge of Ruin](Skyrim_The_Edge_of_Ruin.md), which has you claim the bow from bandits in [Stony Creek Cave](Skyrim_Stony_Creek_Cave.md). Its enchantment applies a random effect. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Ruin's Edge requires a [Daedra Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Notes
- Ruin's Edge previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Ruin%27s_Edge) as a [Dark Seducer](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) bow in *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)*, and is still described as one in the Creation's official description. - [Dark Seducers](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md) along with their [dark equipment](Skyrim_Dark.md) were later added to the game with the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) Creation.
- *[Oblivion Remastered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion_Remastered)* changed the appearance of Ruin's Edge to resemble the Creation Club version.
- Data suggests a "Burden" effect that [slowed](Skyrim_Slow.md) the target to 50% speed for 20 seconds may have been intended as an additional effect of the bow. Another unused, and possibly related, effect is called "Reduce Carry Weight", which had the following description: *Reduce carrying capacity increases by <mag> for <dur> seconds.*

- Demoralize is the same effect as Fear, causing certain level enemies to flee from combat for X amount of time.
