[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Katla.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Katla.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Katla.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Katla.jpg) Katla **Katla** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who runs [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) southwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Although she is married to [Snilling](Skyrim_Snilling.md), he doesn't help her out with the farm as he would "only hurt himself out here". Surprisingly, her husband concurs and hints at how she has outgrown him in terms of strength. They have a son, [Knud](Skyrim_Knud.md), who wants to become a farmer when he grows up. Katla believes in honest pay for honest work, and will therefore [pay you](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md) for harvesting [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md).

She gets up at 6am and starts her workday at the farm. She stops working at 10pm and relaxes inside the house until 12am, when she goes to bed.

Katla wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) along with a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Katla%27s_Farm) to the farm.

## Dialogue
Living the busy life of a farmer, Katla will greet you quite briefly:

*"Can I help you?"* *"My husband handles the indoor work. Just as well - he'd only hurt himself out here."* *"It's hard work keeping Solitude fed, but I wouldn't trust any other family to do it."*
## Conversations
When Katla is with her family, they may sometimes converse:

**Katla**: *"Another gorgeous gray day, right dear?"*
**Snilling**: *"It's too cold for my bones, but that never seems to stop you, dear."*
**Knud**: *"I'm gonna go play soldier with a tree!"*
**Katla**: *"That's nice, son. Just don't stomp on the crop like last time!"*
**Snilling**: *"I'll see if I can't get some work done inside, where it's warm."*
## Bugs
- Katla doesn't have the required keyword to pay for cabbage picked on her farm if you try to sell them. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
- Selling crops to Katla will allow you to ride the horses at [Solitude Stables](Skyrim_Solitude_Stables.md) for free, due to her being in the same ownership faction as the stablemaster. The problem is that horses cost 1,000 gold, making them way too valuable to let stablemasters let you ride them for free after completing a small favor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19062)) The ownership of the horses was changed to just Geimund, rather than the faction, preventing this issue.
