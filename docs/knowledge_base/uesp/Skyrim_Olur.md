[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-npc-Olur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Olur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olur.jpg) Olur **Olur** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) living in the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md), found west of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and northeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He is the son of tribe leader [Chief Larak](Skyrim_Chief_Larak.md) and his forgewife [Shuftharz](Skyrim_Shuftharz.md).

As the son of the forgewife, Olur spends a lot of time working the forge with his mother, and he helps her smith every day between 8am and 8pm. As any young Orc, he is then expected to work the local [Mor Khazgur Mine](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur_Mine.md) which he does from 8pm and all through the night, until it is time to help out at the forge again. He never sleeps, but occasionally takes a nightly meal inside the mine and talks to his fellow miners, [Gul](Skyrim_Gul.md) and [Ghamorz](Skyrim_Ghamorz.md).

He wields a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) and carries a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) (both weapons go to Orcish quality at level 6) with a number of leveled arrows (Orcish quality at level 9). He wears a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Blacksmith%27s_Apron.md#0006ff37) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69e). He carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Larak's Longhouse](Skyrim_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
He is not very talkative and will only explain briefly about his daily routine:

- *"I work in the forge and the mines. Mother says it pleases her and our people."*
- *"Even good hammers warp metal if struck without care."*
- *"Mother tells me to shape metal with focus and strength."*

If he happens to run into his father, Chief Larak, they will have a brief conversation, during which Olur will choose between two different replies:

- **Larak**: *"The way you and your mother forge weapons is impressive.*"
- **Olur**: *"It's simple Orc smithing practice, my chief. Nothing that hasn't been passed down for years."*
- **Larak** *"I served in the Legion. Perhaps I'll show you some of their techniques one of these days."*
