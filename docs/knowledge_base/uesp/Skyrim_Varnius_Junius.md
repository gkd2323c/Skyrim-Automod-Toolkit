[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Varnius_Junius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Varnius_Junius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Varnius_Junius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Varnius_Junius.jpg) Varnius Junius **Varnius Junius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) from [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). He is first found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) appealing to [Jarl Elisif](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md) to investigate strange occurences in [Wolfskull Cave](Skyrim_Wolfskull_Cave.md). Until Varnius' appeal is heard, you cannot interact with him. Once he's been heard, Varnius will return to his hometown of Dragon Bridge and go to the [Four Shields Tavern](Skyrim_Four_Shields_Tavern.md). He will wander around the tavern throughout the rest of the day, only stopping when he goes to sleep at midnight until 8am.

Varnius wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests

### The Man Who Cried Wolf
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf_03.jpg)"*I swear to you, unnatural magics are coming from that cave!*"When you enter the Blue Palace for the very first time, you will witness Varnius plea to jarl Elisif for help with some strange occurrences near [Wolfskull Cave](Skyrim_Wolfskull_Cave.md):

- **Varnius**: *"I swear to you, unnatural magics are coming from that cave! There are strange noises and lights! We need someone to investigate!"*
- **Elisif**: *"Then we will immediately send out a legion to scour the cave and secure the town. Haafingar's people will always be safe under my rule."*
- **Varnius**: *"Th...thank you, my Jarl thank you."*
- **[Sybille](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md)**: *"Your eminence, my scrying has suggested nothing in the area. Dragon Bridge is under imperial control. This is likely superstitious nonsense."*
- **[Falk](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md)**: *"Perhaps a more... tempered reaction... might be called for?"*
- **Elisif**: *"Oh. Yes, of course you are right. Falk, tell [Captain Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md) I said to assign a few extra soldiers to Dragon Bridge."*
- **Varnius**: *"Thank you, Jarl Elisif. But about the cave..."*
- **Falk**: *"I will have someone take care of the cave as well Varnius, you can rest easy. You're dismissed."*

## Dialogue
When first met, Varnius will be actively trying to speak with the jarl, and will actively try and brush you off.

- *"I'm trying to speak to the Jarl."*

Afterwards, he will go back to his hometown of Dragon Bridge, something which he will tell you:

- *"I've got what I came for, I better get back to Dragon Bridge."*

He can now also be spoken with about his troubles:

- **Aren't you worried about [Wolfskull Cave](Skyrim_Wolfskull_Cave.md)?**
- *"I've done my part, Dragon Bridge will have extra guards. [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md) will find a way to take care of the cave."*

Back in Dragon Bridge, Varnius will greet you in different ways, painting him as a law-abiding citizen:

- *"Respect the law, and you'll have no trouble in Dragon Bridge."*
- *"Mind you don't cross Tassius * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *. He's in charge around here."*
- *"Don't start any trouble and I think we'll get along just fine."*
- *"At least, if you're gonna cause trouble, go somewhere else, okay? Please?"*

## Unused Dialogue
Varnius' final greeting has a player response line attached, for some reason, reading:

- **Are you a mercenary? You won't find much work here.**

This is odd for multiple reasons. One is that the line is a greeting and doesn't give you the option to respond, and there are no indicators that you were meant to talk any further to Varnius besides this. Secondly, the line does not seem related to any of Varnius' greetings, so those might have been re-recorded, but the line might've also been intended for Tasius (see [notes](#Notes)) who you might actually confuse for a mercenary.

## Rumors
Before meeting Varnius, one of the rumors the local innkeeper can provide you with is:

- *"Varnius Junius stopped by here on his way to court. He was worried about something. I'm sure Falk Firebeard could tell you what."*

After he's spoken to the jarl about his issue, members of the court may also offer their opinion of him. Steward Falk Firebeard may say:

- *"I'll be honest with you, I was planning to let that go. Varnius is a bit jumpy at the best of times."*

While court wizard Sybille Stentor is a lot more blunt, simply stating:

- *"Varnius Junius is a fool. I'm sure nothing will come of it."*

However, when Varnius is proven right, Falk will change his tune:

- *"I should have paid more heed to Varnius' warnings. I won't make that mistake again."*

Afterwards, you may also overhear [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) mention him when buying fruit at the marketplace:

- **Sorex**: *"I'll need a whole crate of fruit. That Varnius Junius went through quite a few of our supplies."*
- **[Jala](Skyrim_Jala.md)**: *"The guy in from Dragon Bridge? He didn't seem the type."*
- **Sorex**: *"You never can tell."*

## Notes
- The "Tassius" he refers to in dialogue is actually an NPC that does not appear in the final game, [Tasius Tragus](Skyrim_Tasius_Tragus.md). Tasius is marked as Varnius' boss. - Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) removes this reference altogether.
- None of the other inhabitants of Dragon Bridge will ever speak to, acknowledge or mention Varnius in any way, and the same goes for him.
