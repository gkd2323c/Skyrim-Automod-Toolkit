[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-npc-Arob.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arob.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arob.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arob.jpg) Arob guarding the wall **Arob** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc1H) and the hunts-wife of [Chief Burguk](Skyrim_Chief_Burguk.md) in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the Orc stronghold southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

While Burguk's favored wife [Shel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shel) gets to be near the chief around the clock, Arob and forge-wife [Gharol](Skyrim_Gharol.md) practically never see their husband and spend all their time doing their duties in the hold. Arob and her son [Nagrub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nagrub) share the same responsibilities, patrolling the stronghold day and night, without ever eating or sleeping, at times scouting the surrounding wilderness for prey.

She wears a set of leveled armor, excluding the helmet; at level 1 she wears [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md), at level 6 she wears [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), at level 18 she wears [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor) and at level 25 she wears [orcish armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Armor). She never sleeps but carries a spare set of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) along with the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md). She wields leveled weapons as well; at level 1 she uses a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md); at level 6 she uses an [orcish war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe) and an [orcish bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Bow). She carries at least twenty leveled arrows and a selection of [middle-class food and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached she will briefly introduce herself and her opinion on Chief Burguk:

- *"I am the hunts-wife of this stronghold."*
- *"Burguk is a fine chief. He brings strength and wealth to the stronghold."*

She may also recommend a few service providers in the hold:

- *"[Murbul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Murbul) knows many poisons and cures if you need them."*
- *"You should see Gharol if you're looking for a blade or armor."*

## Conversations
During the long days and nights, Arob and Nagrub will occasionally share their happiness:

**Nagrub**: *"Ah, the air is good today. I welcome the smell of blood from all the kills I will make."*
**Arob**: *"Patience, my son. The hunter days * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * not welcome the blood of his prey, he waits for it."*

**Arob**: *"The air has the smell of beasts. The hunt is good today."*
**Nagrub**: *"Yes, mother. I'm eager to prove my skills today."*
