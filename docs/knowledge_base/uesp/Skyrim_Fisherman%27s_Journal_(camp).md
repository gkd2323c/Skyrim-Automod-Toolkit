Fisherman's Journal **by [Advard](Skyrim_Advard.md)**
---
I can't believe I let [Skeggr](Skyrim_Skeggr.md) talk me into this again. If he just wanted to get drunk and swap stories, we could have done that in front of a nice fire back in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), instead of on this godforsaken mud flat.

"But the fish are biting, [Advald](Skyrim_Advard.md)!" "The catch will more than pay for the trip!". Yeah, right- if we don't get iced in first. His boat's already taking on water. I guess we'll try again tomorrow. If we don't catch something then, I'm heading home, with or without him.
