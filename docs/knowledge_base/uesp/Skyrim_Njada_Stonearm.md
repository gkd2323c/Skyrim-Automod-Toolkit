[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-npc-Njada_Stonearm.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Njada_Stonearm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Njada_Stonearm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Njada_Stonearm.jpg) Njada Stonearm outside Jorrvaskr **Njada Stonearm** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Block Expert) and member of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md). She resides in [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) within the walls of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

When you enter Jorrvaskr for the first time, you will find her engaged in a brawl with [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md), a fight that she always wins. She is an expert [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) for [Block](Skyrim_Block.md), and if you are a member of the Companions she will train you up to level 75. She isn't very social and likes to keep to herself. During the day she can be found around Jorrvaskr, either outside by the training dummies or inside, where she eats meals and sleeps in the living quarters.

She will remain unimpressed with you once you become the Harbinger, but will still [follow orders](Skyrim_Followers.md) and accompany you into battle. However, at this point she becomes a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md), and can be recruited as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).

Njada is clad in a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor), including matching pairs of [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots), as well as a [hide helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet). She is equipped with a [Skyforge steel sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Sword) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield) for close combat, and a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) with [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) for ranged combat. She also carries a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

But that doesn't mean I like it."* *"What guidance could you offer me?"* *"What now?"* *"You don't belong here. What do you think you have to offer?"* When spoken to:

**Why did you join the Companions?** *"So I wouldn't have to answer stupid questions from idiots."* **Who are the Companions?** *"Are you looking to join? I wouldn't get your hopes up. The short answer is: we fight. The long answer is... eh, forget it. You're not worth my time."* **Who's in charge around here?** *"You don't just waddle in here and get to speak to the boss. I'll miss our training sessions."*
