[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/ON-map-Icewind_Peaks.jpg/200px-ON-map-Icewind_Peaks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Icewind_Peaks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Icewind_Peaks.jpg) Map of Icewind Peaks circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Icewind Peaks** is a region located in the hold of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), which is a part of the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The Icewind Peaks are more mountainous than the rest of Eastmarch and is considered less hospitable, as a result, there are no settlements in the area, at least, not in the long run. The village of [Cragwallow](Lore_Cragwallow.md) is on the northern edge of the area and the encampment known as Jorunn's Stand served as a staging ground for the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). The ancient temple known as [Skuldafn](Lore_Skuldafn.md) was built by the long forgotten [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) and serves as a gateway to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md), the afterlife for the Nords.
