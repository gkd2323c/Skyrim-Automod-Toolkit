Scouting Report on Gristmung Hold **by [Scout Zirpar Faram](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Scout_Zirpar_Faram)** *Report about the history of Gristmung Hold* *
---
Report to Seline Jerick of the Stirk Fellowship, from Scout Zirpar Faram

We know this much from the [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md) Archives. Gristmung Hold was constructed on the foundations of an ancient Daedric edifice which dates to before the first founding of Solstice by the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md). It was an abandoned ruin until the arrival of the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md). Upon the founding of Sunport, the Corelanyas rebuilt portions of the hold to guard an important bottleneck between the eastern and western parts of the island. Situated atop a small plateau, the location made for an excellent defensive position, and the hold was sparsely reinforced. The citadel ceased to be a major military outpost with the peace between the Stone-Nest tribes and the Corelanya Elves. In the years that followed, it was a major trading hub for both groups, and Stone-Nest Argonians guarded its walls. With the rise of the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md), the site was captured by [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md)'s forces shortly before the Writhing Wall was erected. The fate of the original Stone-Nest guards is unknown, but they are presumed lost.

Following the creation of the Writhing Wall, the Worm Cult has apparently significantly restored and rebuilt the original Daedric structure in a style more fitting for [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) than [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Captured cultists indicate that the hold has been expanded to cover the entire plateau, using recruited and enslaved Stone-Nest workers. Daedric support troops and devices have been imported to reinforce the location.

With the fall of the Writhing Wall, I will continue to press on to report on other sites in Eastern Solstice. Other scouts may provide additional information.
