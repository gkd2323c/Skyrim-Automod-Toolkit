The Everscriven Tome *A short text on the god Xarxes*
---
Penitent, know that the secret knowledge of the world has a guardian. Know that every triumph you achieve in your daily life, every quiet moment of success, is recorded by the One Who Watches. The great tree of life tracing the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) people is kept and held close by [Xarxes](Lore_Xarxes_(god).md) himself, the scribe of the [Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md).

To complete your venerations here, intone: "By Five and Three I speak the secret words to the One Who Watches. May each of my days be worthy of script in his tomes."
