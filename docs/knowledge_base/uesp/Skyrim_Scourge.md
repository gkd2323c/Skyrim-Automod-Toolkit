[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-item-Scourge.jpg/200px-SR-item-Scourge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Scourge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Scourge.jpg) Scourge Scourge is a unique [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md) associated with the Daedric Prince [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md). The mace is wielded by [Valkyn Gatanas](Skyrim_Valkyn_Gatanas.md) in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md), its enchantment is able to banish summoned Daedra.

## Notes
- Scourge previously appeared in *[Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Enchantments#2017)* and *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Scourge)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Scourge.md).
- According to *Battlespire* lore, when a Daedra attempts to invoke the mace's power via the keyword 'Equality' they would be banished. [Cartogriffi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Cartogriffi%27s_Posts) has said they were unaware of its appearance in *Battlespire* when designing [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md), hence it being found on a Dremora. - In [Patch 1.6.629](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#Version_1.6.629), [Valkyn Gatanas](Skyrim_Valkyn_Gatanas.md) was given the [Daedric Gauntlets of Negation](Skyrim_Daedric_Gauntlets_of_Negation.md) to explain how he could wield it, though it is unknown if he planned to use the keyword.
