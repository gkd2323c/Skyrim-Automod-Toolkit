# Sabre Cat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-creature-Sabre_Cat.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Sabre_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sabre_Cat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sabre_Cat.jpg) A Sabre Cat [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-creature-Snowy_Sabre_Cat.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Snowy_Sabre_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snowy_Sabre_Cat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snowy_Sabre_Cat.jpg) A Snowy Sabre Cat [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-creature-Vale_Sabre_Cat.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Vale_Sabre_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vale_Sabre_Cat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vale_Sabre_Cat.jpg) A Vale Sabre Cat **Sabre Cats** are large, powerful, and fast felines that, according to *[Cats of Skyrim](Skyrim_Cats_of_Skyrim.md)*, *"have evolved two dangerously sharp front teeth"*. Found throughout the wilds of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md), they are lethal foes that will attack immediately. All sabre cats will usually abandon any current prey they are stalking or fighting and direct their voracious appetites toward you. Sabre cats may carry [Witbane](Skyrim_Witbane.md), a disease which reduces magicka regeneration by 50%.

They dislike water, so you can use this to your advantage. As they are naturally stealthy creatures, you may not notice a sabre cat approaching until it's too late. Sabre Cats move much faster than most enemies, and are almost impossible to run from; however, they do not turn very fast, so you can sometimes avoid their damage more effectively by dodging around them.

Snowy sabre cats are white, significantly stronger, and have more health, as well as 50% [frost resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md). They predominate at high elevations, and can typically be found in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md), [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md), and other various snowy places in Skyrim. Two are guaranteed to be found in the mountains northeast of [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). This is the only fixed location for them in the game.

Vale sabre cats<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> are exclusive to the [Darkfall Passage](Skyrim_Darkfall_Passage.md) and [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), and the light patches on their pelts glow in darkened environments.

Sabre cats can also be found in random [encounters](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md), in pairs: two sabre cats can be found attacking a mammoth, two sabre cats can also be found attacking a single bandit (the cats may be either the tan or snowy versions, depending on your level when encountering them) and two dead sabre cats are found with the [old orc](Skyrim_Old_Orc.md). Unlike the previous two encounters, these spawn dead.
