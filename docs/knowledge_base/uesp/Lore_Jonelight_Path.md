[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/ON-place-Jonelight_Path.jpg/200px-ON-place-Jonelight_Path.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jonelight_Path.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jonelight_Path.jpg) Jonelight Path The Jonelight Path is a [realm](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) used by [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) heroes as a shortcut to travel between the areas of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKhun-1)</sup> It is a place between all places<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKham-2)</sup> and is named for [Jone](Lore_Jone.md), the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) name for the smaller of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md)'s two [moons](Lore_Moons.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The realm appears to be a collection of floating islands connected by the paths of stars.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-JP-4)</sup> The realm was described as full of [stars](Lore_Stars.md) and moonlight.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKham-2)</sup>

It can be accessed in order to cross vast distances in a short amount of time. It is dangerous without an experienced guide. Prolonged stay in the realm may cause hearts of mortals to burst apart and their souls to be torn apart. Falling through gaps between the stars that act as parts of the bridges between floating isles in the realm is fatal. It is believed that only the swiftest champions of Azurah can survive in the realm for long.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKhun-1)</sup>

One of the means of accessing the realm is utilizing the power stored in the body of Khunzar-ri. Ribs, femurs and claws are known to hold the power that can open the gateway to the Jonelight Path. [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md), who was attuned to the Lunar Lattice via the [Shadow Dance](Lore_Shadow_Dance.md), could use her attunement to open [portals](Lore_Portals.md) leading to the realm, after drawing upon the power stored in a piece of Khunzar-ri's remains to amplify her Dance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKhun-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKham-2)</sup>

Passage through the Jonelight Path was likened to climbing the 'liminal forces that bind each here to every there'. How far and where one can reach through the Jonelight Path depends on the traveler. [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was incapable of traveling far, but Khunzar-ri could utilize the Jonelight Path to reach any place, as only he has heard Azurah's whispers and climbed the forbidden tree. Places where the Jonelight Path connects with the Nirn were described as 'liminal of here touches the liminal of there'.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKhun-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/ON-place-Jonelight_Path_09.jpg/200px-ON-place-Jonelight_Path_09.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jonelight_Path_09.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Jonelight_Path_09.jpg) Jonelight Path (ESO)
### Merethic Era
The realm was visited by legendary Khajiiti hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) and his kra'jun: [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md), [Nurarion the Perfect](Lore_Nurarion.md), [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md), and [Cadwell](Lore_Cadwell.md) during their adventures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKhun-1)</sup>

### Second Era
The realm was once again used to travel between distant places in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). The portal to the Jonelight Path was opened by Queen [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md) of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) in the [Tideholm](Lore_Tideholm.md), the island which was headquarters of the iteration [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) led by [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md). Queen Khamira, the descendant of the Anequina Sharp-Tongue opened portal with the usage of the power of the claw of [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md), the ancient Khajiiti hero in order to reach the normally inaccessible island of [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESONMR-5)</sup> She used the knowledge that she could access from the memories of her ancestor Anequina Sharp-Tongue.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKham-2)</sup> She traveled through the Jonelight Path with her own kra'jun - consisting of herself, Sai Sahan and [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md). They were guided by the spirit of the previous planar traveler - the legendary Khunzar-ri himself.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESONMR-5)</sup>
