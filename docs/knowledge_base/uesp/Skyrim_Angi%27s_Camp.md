[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp.jpg) Angi's Camp **Angi's Camp** is a cabin and camp southeast of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). [Angi](Skyrim_Angi.md), a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) from [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), is the only occupant. She is in hiding due to her killing the two drunken [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) soldiers who murdered her parents. Angi is very skilled with the [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), and is willing to teach [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) for free through target practice competition. For each of six subtasks, you will gain one skill point in Archery, providing an easy way to train the skill. Mastering all six tasks additionally yields [Angi's Bow](Skyrim_Angi%27s_Bow.md), a unique non-enchanted [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md).

For details on the archery training you can perform here, see [this page](Skyrim_Angi.md#Archery_Practice).

## Getting There
Getting to Angi's Camp can be difficult. It's high up in the mountains, and the marked paths there do not start close to the camp itself.

Starting from [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md), head south across the main road and onto a side road marked by stacked stones to the right. Follow this path to a pond, then head west along another side road until you reach a dead end. At this point, turn around and take the unmarked higher path, keeping the stacked stones to your left (two of them will have flags attached) until you reach yet another path. You'll soon reach a fork; the left path goes to a small hunting camp (along with [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md), if [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) is installed), while the right leads on. The main path comes to another fork after a while. Going straight will lead to [Bloodlet Throne](Skyrim_Bloodlet_Throne.md), while going right will finally lead to Angi's Camp (watch out for bear traps).

Alternatively, start at Bloodlet Throne and head west along the road to reach the final intersection before Angi's Camp.

## Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_02.jpg) The shooting range The camp is really a shack with no front door, and the windows are just holes with shutters. There is a firepit outside to the west, with a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) by the fire and a lean-to full of firewood behind it. There is a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) outside to the right of the doorway against the side of the shack, with a food barrel and a small set of empty shelves beside it. To the left of the doorway on the wall of the shack are three weapon racks together. There is a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush by the northern corner of the shack, as well as a path heading north and down two flights of stairs to Agni's archery range where she is willing to give you free lessons. The range is marked out with wood, and has four targets at different distances. There is a second snowberry bush beside the range.

## Angi's Camp
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Angi%27s_Camp_03.jpg) Shack interior Inside the shack, on your left as you enter against the northwest wall is a long wooden table and bench. On the table is a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). In the northern corner is a barrel containing common ingredients, and along the northeastern wall is a firepit with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) over it. To the right against the same wall is a non-respawning cupboard. On the shelves are two sacks, a pair of [farm boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5), and a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000cee80). There is a small bedroom to the right of the cupboard, with the doorway in the southeastern wall, again with no door. There is a barrel and a crate on your right as you enter the shack, and to the left of the crate hanging from a roof strut is a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid, a sample of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and a sample of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md).

In the bedroom area, on your left as you enter is an unlocked chest containing leveled items. In the southern corner is a double bed, and beside this is a non-respawning end table which contains [treasure map V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_V), while on top is a copy of the Archery [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Gold Ribbon of Merit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gold_Ribbon_of_Merit)*.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. A previous USKP patch added music to a number of locations, but for some unknown reason, Angi's Camp was left out.
