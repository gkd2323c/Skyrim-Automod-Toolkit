[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Riften_Guard_Jailor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Riften_Guard_Jailor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Riften_Guard_Jailor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Riften_Guard_Jailor.jpg) Riften Guard Jailor **Riften Guard Jailors** are [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldiers](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) who can be found guarding the [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md), sitting at the jail chair and looking for trespassers. They are replaced by [Imperial Guard jailors](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard_Jailor.md) when the Stormcloaks are driven from the city.

They wear the [Riften guard's armor](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Armor.md) and [helmet](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Helmet.md) along with a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md) and a random [Amulet of the Divines](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Amulet_of_the_Divines). They are equipped with the [Riften Guard's shield](Skyrim_Riften_Guard%27s_Shield.md), a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) with fifteen [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md), a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), and a random [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md). They also carry the [key](Skyrim_Riften_Jail_Key.md) to the jail, a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md), a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a drink, some food, and some gold change (up to nine in amount).

## Dialogue
Upon entering Riften Jail, the jailor will walk up to you and say:

- *"Hey, you're not supposed to be down here. Official business only."*

You have four dialogue options in total. If [I Done Got Thaned!](Skyrim_I_Done_Got_Thaned!.md) is completed, you can tell the jailor that you are a thane, and have him let you proceed.

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **"It's fine, I'm allowed to be here." (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"Oh, my mistake then. Sorry, you can go on ahead."* | **Passed**: He will let you through |
| **Failed**: *"Sure, and I'm Queen Barenziah. Now tell me another one."* | **Failed**: Nothing happens | |
| **"Can I pass now?" (<Bribe Cost> gold)** | **Passed**: *"Sure, that'll do just fine. Go on ahead."* | **Passed**: He will let you through |
| **Failed**: *"That's just insulting. Not even a proper bribe?"* | **Failed**: Nothing happens | |
| **"I'm the Thane of Riften."** | *"Forgive me milord/milady, I didn't recognize you! Please proceed."* | He will let you through |
| **"Sorry, I'll leave."** | *"Bloody tourists."* | Nothing happens |

Alternatively, you can walk away from the jailor, who will tell you:

- *"Ignore me all you want, you still aren't getting in."*

Talking to him again will have him ask: *"What are you doing down here?"* and you will have the same dialogue options as before.

If you have successfully persuaded him to let you pass, he can be heard saying:

- *"The prisoners are in rare form, as usual."*
- *"Watch your step in here. The stonework gets slippery."*
- *"Is it daylight out? Night? I lose track down here."*
- *"A pleasure, thane."* **(If you have completed I Done Got Thaned!)**

## Notes
- Unlike his [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) [counterpart](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard_Jailor.md), the Riften Guard Jailor does not carry a key to the evidence chest.
