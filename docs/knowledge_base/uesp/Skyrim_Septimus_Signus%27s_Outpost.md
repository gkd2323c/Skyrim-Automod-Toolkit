[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg/200px-SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg) Septimus Signus's Outpost **Septimus Signus's Outpost** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) north of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) inhabited by [Septimus Signus](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus.md). It contains only one zone, *[Septimus Signus's Outpost](#Septimus_Signus.27s_Outpost)*.

Septimus moved a mysterious Dwemer lockbox to this remote location for study. He is an expert on the lore of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), but his knowledge and interaction with them has mostly destroyed his mind. He claims that "Inside is the heart. The heart of a god! The heart of you. And me." However, the lockbox actually contains the book *[Oghma Infinium](Skyrim_Oghma_Infinium.md)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png/200px-SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost_02.png) Outpost at dusk The cave is on a small remote icy island off the north coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It may be reached by walking a short distance north of the College of Winterhold then continuing north over drift ice and the island featuring [Skytemple Ruins](Skyrim_Skytemple_Ruins.md). The outpost is accessed through a trapdoor on the south side of the small icy island, next to a moored rowboat.

### Septimus Signus's Outpost
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Discerning_the_Transmundane.jpg) Dwemer lockbox The trapdoor opens into a narrow descending ice tunnel that ends on a ledge above the main chamber; following the ledge leads to an earthen ramp that circles the chamber counterclockwise. At the bottom is the Dwemer lockbox. Opposite the lockbox is a small square wooden table holding two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), two bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), a [goblet](Skyrim_Goblet.md), and a [jug](Skyrim_Jug.md), with a second jug on the ground. A nearby cupboard holds three bowls of [fire salts](Skyrim_Fire_Salts.md), a filled [petty soul gem](Skyrim_Petty_Soul_Gem.md), a filled [common soul gem](Skyrim_Common_Soul_Gem.md), copies of *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](Skyrim_Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law.md)*, *[Boethiah's Proving](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Proving.md)*, *[Chimarvamidium](Skyrim_Chimarvamidium.md)*, *[Dwemer History and Culture](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_History_and_Culture)*, *[Dwemer Inquiries Vol I](Skyrim_Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_I.md)*, *[II](Skyrim_Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_II.md)*, and *[III](Skyrim_Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_III.md)*, *[Effects of the Elder Scrolls](Skyrim_Effects_of_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)*, and *[Pension of the Ancestor Moth](Skyrim_Pension_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md)*, and two copies of *[Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls](Skyrim_Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)*, which is written by Septimus himself.

## Notes
- *Boethiah's Proving* will not appear until you reach level 30.
- [The Chill](Skyrim_The_Chill.md), [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md)'s jail, is on an island northeast of the outpost.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
