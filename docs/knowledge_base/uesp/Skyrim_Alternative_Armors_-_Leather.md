## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of Leather armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi.* (Quest "Doubled Edged" starts by reading "Zaharia's Note" at Cliffside Retreat south of Dragon Bridge.)
