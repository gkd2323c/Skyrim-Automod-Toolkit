## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A twisting path runs all the way from the north side of Morthal to the cave. There are two patches of [bleeding crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown) near the entrance, one of which can only be picked by carefully approaching the lit brazier to the right, as moving too close to the entrance will trigger entry. There is a skull on the stones to the left.

### Movarth's Lair
The entry tunnel leads to a wooden platform over a room with two [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). There is a ramp to the left that descends into the chamber. There is nothing else of interest in this first room, except the dark tunnel to the south which leads to another room, where a [vampire's thrall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall) is sitting at a table with a lantern. Beyond the thrall, along a short twisting path, is a small room with three carts filled with the remains of a few people, and an [imp stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool) growing in the middle of the room. There is a hole in the rock wall to the left, but it is too high to reach.

The path deeper into the network of caverns is to the north of where the vampire's thrall sat. Another vampire's thrall is working in this room, burying corpses in a hole in the northwest portion of the room. There are three piles of rocks around the room littered with remains. There is a table to the left of the entrance with some generic clothes, another to the right with more generic clothes, and a bleeding crown growing at the base of the second table. By the western wall is a cart and a table with more clothes. By this table is a pickaxe, a barrel with a potion of minor magicka, and beyond that a bleeding crown. The corpses of three [Nords](Skyrim_Nord_(dead).md) and a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard_(dead).md) lie in the hole, and the only thing of note is that the Redguard carries a lockpick. Lastly, on the other side of the room, there is a bedroll and another bleeding crown.

The next tunnel is to the north, and splits into a higher and lower pathway. The higher path is bridged with wooden planks, and there is an iron ore vein in the wall. This path leads around the wall to a bedroom of sorts. The lower path leads to the main chamber, where the master vampire and one of his followers are feasting. If stage 100 of the quest [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md) has been reached, the former will be Movarth Piquine; otherwise, it will be a generic vampire boss. There are three other branches from the main chamber: one to the northeast (where you can find the second iron ore vein), one to the east, and one to the south. There is a vampire in the tunnel to the south, and another in the tunnel to the northeast, who will detect any hostilities in the main room and rush to their master's aid.

There isn't much in the main room. The table is covered with food and drink, with a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), two small coin purses, and a fair bit of loose gold. The table also holds an enchanter's potion, as well as remains of the vampires' victims, clothes, armor, bones, etc. Lastly, there are two [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot) growing between the table and the southern tunnel.

The tunnel to the northeast leads to the other side of the bedroom area that the higher wooden path lead to. There are two imp stools growing in the northeast tunnel. The room is split in two: one part with a single bed, and another section with three bedrolls. By the single bed is a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Sun's Dawn, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Sun%27s_Dawn,_v2)*, [Movarth's boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Movarth%27s_Boots) (which provide a 15% sneaking bonus), and a shelf with a large assortment of boots (which includes a pair of [Imperial light boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots) and a pair of [Stormcloak officer boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md)). The only item of note by the bedrolls is a potion of minor healing on a dresser.

Behind Movarth's throne to the east in the main chamber is a wooden ramp to a small room with another vampire. There is a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) and an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) here, along with a set of shelves holding a potion of healing and a copy of *[Immortal Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Immortal_Blood)*, a book about Movarth.

The tunnel to the south leads to one final room. There is a vampire's thrall on the platform to the left. To the right is a wooden platform with a bedroll, a chest, and a leveled poison on a dresser. At the top of the stone ramp is another imp stool, with a large coin purse, a bow, and a few leveled arrows on the cobweb-covered table to the left. A tunnel to the right leads to the opening above the small room with the three carts filled with remains, providing a convenient return to the entrance.

## Notes
- [Alva](Skyrim_Alva.md) will appear in the bedroom area of the cave if you did not kill her during the quest Laid to Rest. She will engage the other vampires in combat. - Don't kill Alva in the cave, or that will count as a crime, and [Hroggar](Skyrim_Hroggar.md)'s disposition towards you will drop to -2.
- By default, Movarth's appearance is randomly-generated. With the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on, he will always appear as a Nord.
- According to a [Development Map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-map-Development_Map_02.png), this location was originally called **Ghoruun Hall**. References to "Ghoruun" still appear multiple times in the final game data, most often in scripts tied to the quest. Moreover, the original vampire master inhabiting the hall was apparently intended to be a different NPC named **Morgus Ghoruun**, who was either changed into or replaced by Movarth in the final game.

## Bugs
- Movarth's Lair cannot be marked as cleared, as none of its inhabitants are tagged as a boss. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you want to update the "cleared" status, enter the following command in the console: `Set Location Cleared 0001BDFC 1`
- The fix from Unofficial Skyrim Patch 1.3.2 created a new bug, as the "boss" vampires it introduced did not respawn and may therefore break radiant quests. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.4, fixes this bug.
- During the quest, once you kill Movarth and make your way out of the cave, [Helgi's Ghost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helgi%27s_Ghost) begins speaking her dialogue line immediately, and attempting to speak with her and initiate the only available dialogue line about avenging her and her mother causes her to stop speaking and disappear. It can be assumed that she was supposed to have a forcegreet, and the available dialogue option was supposed to trigger her dialogue. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. The line of dialogue they had her able to use should not have been enabled since you can't use it until she's begun her hello line anyway, and that hello line is what disables her.
- Using the ramp in the corridor leading to the main boss chamber can cause your follower to briefly be stuck, then disappear and return to the previous room due to a broken navmesh. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
- If Alva is killed here during the quest, [Hroggar](Skyrim_Hroggar.md)'s and [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md)'s dispositions towards you will take a big hit, and fall to -2. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- If you shoot Movarth undetected, he may run and eventually get stuck in a wall where you cannot hit him. Leaving and reentering the dungeon will reset him to his initial position in his chair. <sup>**?**</sup>
- A crash may occur when approaching the lair for the quest [Totems of Hircine](Skyrim_Totems_of_Hircine.md). <sup>**?**</sup>
