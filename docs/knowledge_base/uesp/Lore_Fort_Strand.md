[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/OB-place-Fort_Strand.jpg/200px-OB-place-Fort_Strand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Strand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Strand.jpg) Fort Strand circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/ON-place-Fort_Strand.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fort_Strand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fort_Strand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fort_Strand.jpg) Fort Strand circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Fort Strand is a ruined fort located just east of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md), in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It has an extensive underground complex, including a large natural chasm and a subterranean chamber called the Great Dome.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>

The fort was already a crumbling ruin by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), at which time it served as a smuggler den. A chest of [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) martial relics was taken to the ruins that year from a dig site at Tribune's Folly. The researchers hired the Dragonstar Caravan Company to recover the relics, as the chest was guarded by supernatural shadowy guardians sent by Lord [Faleria](Lore_Nunex_Faleria.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODB-2)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the fort was home to a marauder gang. A local [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) named Bjalfi the Contemptible brought the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) mace Rockshatter to the ruins when he joined the gang that year. He was killed when his deserted wife sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to the ruins to recover the mace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>
