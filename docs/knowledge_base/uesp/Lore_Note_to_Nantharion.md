Note to Nantharion **by [King Camoran Aeradan](Lore_Aeradan_Camoran.md)** *A letter sent to King Nantharion by King Aeradan*
---
[Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md), what in [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md) are you up to?

I've got a stack of letters here, from [the Queen](Lore_Ayrenn.md), from various ambassadors, from [Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) diplomats, three from [Count Calantius](Lore_Count_Calantius.md) alone. What are you doing across the [River Strid](Lore_Strid_River.md)?

I want you or a representative here in [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) yesterday, do you hear me? The governors and magistrates that make up the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) are demanding answers, and I can't keep stalling.

Your family and I go way back, son. You know as well as I do that we're always on a thin branch with these people. Please don't make my job any harder than it already is.

You're a loyal subject of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). I know it. I expect to hear from you soon.

[King Camoran Aeradan](Lore_Aeradan_Camoran.md)
