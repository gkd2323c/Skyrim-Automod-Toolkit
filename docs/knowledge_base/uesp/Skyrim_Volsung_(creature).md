[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-creature-Volsung.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Volsung.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Volsung.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Volsung.jpg) Volsung at the word wall on Volskygge Peak **Volsung** is one of the eight high-ranked [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) in Skyrim. Volsung drops an [eponymous mask](Skyrim_Volsung_(item).md), which makes [prices better](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md) by 20%, [increases carrying weight](Skyrim_Fortify_Carry_Weight.md) by 20 points, and gives [waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md).

Volsung can be found in [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md) in northwestern Skyrim. Note that you can just climb the mountain above the entrance and skip the dungeon entirely.

## Related Quests
- **[Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge_(quest).md)**: Climb your way to the evil of [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md).

## Notes
- Volsung is extremely skilled in [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md), [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md), [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) and [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md), with a level of 100 in each of the aforementioned skills.

## Bugs
- When bursting free from his coffin, Volsung may not fight back and only backpedal after casting a Frost Cloak (or not casting it at all), leading to a really easy fight.
