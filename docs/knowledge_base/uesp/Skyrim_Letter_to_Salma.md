Letter to Salma *A letter from a concerned father warning his daughter of the world's dangers, and her partner*
---
[Daughter](Skyrim_Salma.md) -
I may seem like a doting old fool, but I still don't like this. The world is a more dangerous place than you realize, and hunting for treasure is a fool's life. Tread carefully, and keep half an eye on [Beem-Ja](Skyrim_Beem-Ja.md). He should be able to protect you, but do not trust him. Come home as soon as you've tired of this charade.
