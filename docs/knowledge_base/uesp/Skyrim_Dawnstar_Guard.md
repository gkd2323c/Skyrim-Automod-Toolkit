[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-npc-Dawnstar_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dawnstar_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnstar_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnstar_Guard.jpg) Dawnstar Guard **Dawnstar Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md). Should the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) forces gain control of Dawnstar, they will be replaced by [Imperial guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Guard) under the service of [Jarl Brina](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md).

Dawnstar guards are clad in a set of Pale guard's equipment. This includes a [Pale guard's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Armor), a [Pale guard's helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Helmet), and a [Pale guard's shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Guard%27s_Shield). They also wear a pair of [fur gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Gauntlets) and a pair of [fur boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots). They are equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) supplemented with [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). They also carry a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch).
