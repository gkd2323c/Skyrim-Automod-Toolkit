| Statuary or bas-reliefs of [Namira](Lore_Namira.md) are uncommon, but those that are known to exist typically depict her as a woman in some form. Given Namira is often associated with [slugs](Lore_Slug.md), spiders, and other things that inspire instinctive revulsion in mortals, her statuary sometimes reflects this. For example, statues and bas-reliefs from the mid-Second Era (such as the one found in the [Temple of Seven Riddles](Lore_Maw_of_Lorkhaj.md)) depicts Namira in a horrifying skeletal from, with crab-like legs protruding from her torso.<sup>[[115]](#cite_note-ONMistressof Decay-117)</sup><sup>[[116]](#cite_note-TBOD-118)</sup> <br> <br> A statue of Namira could be found in the wilds of Cyrodiil during the late-Third Era.
