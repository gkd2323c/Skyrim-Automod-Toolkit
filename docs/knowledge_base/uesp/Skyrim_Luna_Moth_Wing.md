[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Luna_Moth.jpg) A luna moth [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-book-Mothwing.png/150px-SR-book-Mothwing.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Mothwing.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Mothwing.png) Luna moth wings as shown in the book *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* **Luna Moth Wings** can be acquired by catching [luna moths](Skyrim_Luna_Moth.md). You receive two wings for each moth you catch. Luna moths only come out at night but their glowing wings make them easy to spot.

- *"As with the dragonflies, I was taken aback by the number of butterflies, moths, and other insects that manage to thrive in Skyrim. The Luna moth is especially beautiful; its thin, almost ephemeral wings seem too delicate to hoist anything into the air, giving them an almost magical appearance. Indeed, that sense extends to the properties they exhibit when crushed and distilled (an action I admit was difficult to perform at first, no matter my resolve to discover all that Skyrim has to offer. These creatures are simply that beautiful!) While they can be used for creating poisons that damage magicka, I feel that would be a waste of their potential. Do not be alarmed if, when the wings are broken down almost completely, they appear to almost disappear under your mortar. That very quality makes the wings quite effective in potions of invisibility!"* —Agneta Falia in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*.

## Ingredients
18 guaranteed samples can be found in 11 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 5 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in the [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Luna moths appear at insect spawning sites, but only between 8pm and 11pm. Each spawning site generates up to three nocturnal insects, with a random mix of both luna moths and [torchbugs](Skyrim_Torchbug.md). These sites also typically generate [blue](Skyrim_Blue_Butterfly_Wing.md) and [monarch](Skyrim_Butterfly_Wing.md) butterflies between 6am and 6pm. In total, there are 526 spawning sites in 144 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 22 around [Talking Stone Camp](Skyrim_Talking_Stone_Camp.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 17 inside [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 14 around [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 13 around [Lund's Hut](Skyrim_Lund%27s_Hut.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
