[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-npc-Madesi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Madesi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madesi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madesi.jpg) Madesi at Riften Grand Plaza **Madesi** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [pawnbroker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker) and a stall [merchant](Skyrim_Merchants.md) selling jewelry in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He has a rivalry with fellow merchant [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md), a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) whose "miracle cures" have been drawing business away from Madesi. If your character is an Argonian, he will refer to you as "marsh-friend". Otherwise, he will call you a "land-strider". He will ask for [your help](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ringmaker) in collecting supplies for a new ring he wants to make, and will reward you with 400 gold and a leveled necklace. His [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you will also increase.

He gets up at 6am and heads to his stall at the grand plaza. While tending the stall, he can be found [conversing](#Conversations) with his customers and trying to interest passersby to buy his jewelry. At 8pm, he closes his shop and eats at [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) until midnight, then goes to sleep in [Beggar's Row](Skyrim_Beggar%27s_Row.md).

Madesi wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d), and is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Madesi%27s_Market_Key) to his stall, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When idle at his stall, he will promote his goods.

*"Beautiful baubles and gleaming gemstones over here!"* *"Jewelry with legendary Argonian craftsmanship!"* *"Buying and selling fine jewelry here!"* When you speak to him, he says:

*"If anything pleases the eye, don't hesitate to make me an offer."* *"If you have any jewelry to sell, I'd be glad to pay you a fair sum in gold for it."* *"Greetings, honored friend. Could I interest you in some fine jewelry?"* If you see him when he is not working, he'll say:

*"Come see me in the market if you're looking for the finest custom jewelry in all of Skyrim."* Upon ending the conversation, he will wish you well. If you are not an Argonian:

*"Safe travels, landstrider."* If you are an Argonian:

*"Good journey, marsh-friend."* You can ask him about his business.

**How's business?** *"Not well I'm afraid. That Brynjolf... he keeps draining the people's pockets with his ridiculous miracle cures.
A few months ago it was [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) salve and now he's got something new.
Not that there's anything that can be done about it. He's in good with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)."*
## Quest-Related Events

#### Ringmaker
To start the quest, you'll need to ask him if he makes his own jewelry.

**Do you make your own jewelry?** *"I do. Sadly, I'm one of the few traditional Saxhleel Jewelers that remains in Tamriel; it's becoming a lost art.
Only trouble is gathering materials has become hazardous. The roads to the mines and to the other cities are thick with bandits... and worse.
Would you be interested in gathering some materials for me? I'd certainly be happy to compensate you."* **Sure, I'll get them for you.** *"Thank you, land-strider/marsh-friend.
I'm seeking a small list of items to complete my next creation: two [flawless sapphires](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Sapphire), a [mammoth tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk) and a chunk of [gold ore](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md)."* When you have the requested items, you can tell him such, and he'll give you 100 gold for each item you bring him. His reply will depend on what you give him.

**I have your flawless sapphires.** *"Good, these will make wonderful settings for my rings."* **I have a mammoth tusk for you.** *"The ivory from this tusk will be perfect for the inlay on my amulets."* **I have a chunk of gold ore for you.** *"Perfect. Melting this down will give me plenty of gold for those necklaces I started."* Once you've brought him everything, ask him if he needs anything else.

**Did you need anything else?** *"No, I think I have enough here to get started.
Here, let me give you this. It's the least I can do for all your trouble."* He will reward you with a leveled necklace.

You can ask him about the term "Saxhleel". If you're not an Argonian:

**Saxhleel?** *"Forgive me. It's a term I haven't used in some time. While most in Tamriel call my kind Argonian, we prefer the term Saxhleel."* If you are an Argonian:

**Saxhleel?** *"Forgive me. It's a term I haven't used in some time. While most in Tamriel would call us Argonian, we prefer the term Saxhleel."* Either way, he will continue.

*"Madesi was actually my original Saxhleel name. Unlike others, I decided to embrace tradition and keep it that way."*

Please come by soon; not for me, but for yourself."* **Madesi:** *"Very well, I'll try milady."* [Shadr](Skyrim_Shadr.md) also stops by.

**Shadr:** *"Here's another septim, Madesi. How much more do I need?"* **Madesi:** *"Only thirty more and it'll be all yours. ## Notes
- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) on his market stall.
- It seems like Madesi was at one point a Khajiit merchant by the name of "M'desi" instead. A script called `TG00Mdesi Script` still bears this early name, and [unused dialogue for Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md#Unused_Dialogue) also refers to M'desi, rather than Madesi.
