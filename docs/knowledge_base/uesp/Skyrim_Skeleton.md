[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-creature-Skeleton.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Skeleton.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeleton.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeleton.jpg) A Skeleton **Skeletons** are the reanimated bones of the dead. They are often found with [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) in crypts and ruins, and may also be summoned by [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) in combat.

They are fully [resistant to poison](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md) and paralysis and have a 25% [resistance to frost](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md), but, given their low health level and poor equipment, they are vulnerable to other types of attack, making them relatively easy opponents. Skeletons encountered around graveyards or in [Nordic Ruins](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) will use various Ancient Nord Weapons; one-handed, two-handed, and archer skeletons all exist.

Leveled **Skeleton Mages** appear in some locations. They have the same spell selection as draugr spellcasters.

The *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) features the [Boneman](Skyrim_Boneman.md) and [Mistman](Skyrim_Mistman.md) as skeleton variants in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). They are similar to their regular counterparts in both abilities and appearance.

The [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) features the [Skeleton Champions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Champion), [Skeleton Marksmen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Marksman), [Skeleton Minions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Minion), and [Skeleton Warlocks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Warlock) as skeleton variants. They are similar to their regular counterparts in both abilities and appearance.

## Gallows Hall Creation
The [Gallows Hall](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) lets you summon a permanent Skeleton follower using the [Bone Forge](Skyrim_Bone_Forge.md).

- It is summoned using 1 [Bone Meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal) and 1 [Greater Soul Gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Soul_Gem).
- The Skeleton summoned using the forge does not count towards your maximum number of summons, making it a free permanent follower.
- It does not regenerate health and is protected, being downed when its health is too low instead of being outright killed. It can be healed using the [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing) and [Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead) spells.

## Notes
- Skeletons do not have magicka, despite some being mages.
