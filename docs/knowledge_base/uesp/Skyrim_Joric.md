[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-npc-Joric.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Joric.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Joric.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Joric.jpg) Joric in Morthal **Joric** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) living in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) with his sister [Idgrod the Younger](Skyrim_Idgrod_the_Younger.md). His parents are [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) and [Aslfur](Skyrim_Aslfur.md). If Hjaalmarch is captured by the Stormcloaks, Joric and Idgrod will be exiled to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

Joric wakes up around 6am and slowly starts his day wandering around the longhouse. He then heads at 8am to wander around town for two hours, with his big sister in tow to watch over him. By 10am he slows down again to wander about, until 12am, where starts running for two more hours. When it's finally 3pm, he'll make his way over to the workers at the local mill, and sits down on the ground to observe them for 2 hours. He then wanders around some more until it's finally his bed time again at 9:35pm for nine hours of rest.

He wears [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). He carries a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child) and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
He may have the same power of visions as the other people in his family:

*"You're different, aren't you? Not like anyone else. Not in Morthal, or Skyrim."* **(First time speaking to you)** *"[Gorm](Skyrim_Gorm.md) thinks I'm mad. He doesn't say it, but I know he thinks it. Is he right?"* *"I get lost sometimes. I'm not sure where I go, but I'm not... I'm not here."* *"It's [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md). He...He did it. I don't know what it was, but he did it."* *"My mother sees things. She doesn't know why, but she does. Only sometimes, she doesn't see me at all."* If he's been exiled to the Blue Palace, he will only say these lines instead of his other dialogue:

*"I don't understand. Why can't we go home?"* *"I can't sleep here. The dreams are even worse than before."*
## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

*"Joric is the youngest child of Idgrod Ravencrone and Aslfur. He's a young boy who seems to have lost his mind at a very early age. He doesn't say much, but when he does speak, he is oddly insightful, even if his words make little sense."*
## Bugs
- Joric is assigned a bed upstairs but seems to have trouble using it. When he's up there he cannot get into bed, and stays up all night, either sitting on the chair in the same room or occasionally popping from one spot to another. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. The bed was neither owned by Joric nor marked as a child's bed, which made it impossible for him to use it.
