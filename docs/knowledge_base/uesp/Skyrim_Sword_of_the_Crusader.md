[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Sword_of_the_Crusader.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Sword_of_the_Crusader.png) Sword of the Crusader The Sword of the Crusader is a [Sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) artifact. It is acquired through the [Relics of the Crusader](Skyrim_Relics_of_the_Crusader.md) quest, which has you claim the sword and other relics from the [Bandit Thieves](Skyrim_Bandit_Thief.md) at the [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md). Tempering the Sword of the Crusader requires a [Steel Ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
