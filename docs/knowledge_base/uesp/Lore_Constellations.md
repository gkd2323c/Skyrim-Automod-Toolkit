# Constellations


There are generally accepted to be thirteen constellations in the sky,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Cosmology-2)</sup> sometimes called the **Thirteen Patrons**.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSD-8)</sup> There are three **guardian constellations**, known to the Reachmen as the **Reach Guardians**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARANA-1)</sup> and the **Guardians in the Sky**<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CMWG-9)</sup>: the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md), the [Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Mage), and the [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief), each associated with three other constellations, called their **charges** (known as the **Lesser Stars** to the Reachfolk).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARANA-1)</sup> Each of these twelve constellations corresponds to one of the twelve [months of the year](Lore_Calendar.md). The thirteenth sign, the [Serpent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Serpent), is neither guardian nor charge.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Coyle-10)</sup>

## Culture
Some Reachfolk Witches can read the stars and see the future and hidden truths in their light.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARANA-1)</sup> According to them the Guardians in the Sky work together to protect the Lesser Stars from the malevolent Sky Tale known as the Snake in the Stars.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARANA-1)</sup> Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the [Wayward Guardians](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wayward_Guardians) led by [Arana](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arana) recognized the power of the Reach Guardians and invoked their names for the protection against the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CMWG-9)</sup>

Similarly to Reachfolk, the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) believe that skies are linked to tales and stories. According to them the sky bridges time and nature, and measures both, as "the sky mirrors the sea, and the sea also reflects its mirror".<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Girnalin-11)</sup>

The [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [Sapiarchs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sapiarchs) observe the skies in search of signs and portents - patterns and phenomena related to constellations are considered among them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ATUQ-12)</sup>

[Yokudans](Lore_Yokuda.md) believed that the stars guide ones destiny, from birth to death and beyond.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Avik-13)</sup> Their songs and poetry often focused on the stars.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Coyle-10)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TWC-14)</sup>

[Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) knew the power of the stars, building orreries and observatories to watch the constellations and who they protected<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Erasmo-15)</sup>, they had a particular interest in the [Serpent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Serpent) constellation. Dwemer runes were also used to represent constellations.

The [Druids](Lore_Druid.md) of old praised the stars, the [Ritual](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Ritual) constellation above all.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ONWD-16)</sup>

The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md) are commonly associated with the practice of Aetherial magic. They were known for their fascination with the constellations, but it was not connected to religious practices, but magical study.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MOTMS-7)</sup>

Circa [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578), three Guardian signs — the Warrior, the Mage, and the Thief — fell from the sky and took on a mortal form known as [Celestials](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Celestial).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Arnure-17)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-SOE-18)</sup>

The [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) had their own names for the constellations.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-CM3-19)</sup>

## The Apprentice
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/MW-birthsign-Apprentice.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Apprentice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Apprentice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Apprentice.jpg) The Apprentice The [Apprentice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Apprentice) (known as the **Automaton** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md)) is a constellation of eleven stars which is in the night sky during [Sun's Height](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sun%27s_Height). It is one of the [Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Mage)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Apprentice are thought to have an affinity for magic, but also a vulnerability to magic.

## The Atronach
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/MW-birthsign-Atronach.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Atronach.jpg) The Atronach The [Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Atronach) (sometimes the **Golem**, known as the **Warmachine** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md)) is a constellation of ten stars which is in the night sky during [Sun's Dusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sun%27s_Dusk). It is one of the [Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Mage)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Atronach are thought to be natural sorcerers with deep reserves of magicka, but that cannot generate their own magicka.

## The Lady
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/MW-birthsign-Lady.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Lady.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lady.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lady.jpg) The Lady The [Lady](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lady) is a constellation of four stars which is in the night sky during [Hearthfire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hearthfire). It is one of the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Lady are thought to be kind and tolerant.

## The Lord
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/MW-birthsign-Lord.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Lord.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lord.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lord.jpg) The Lord The [Lord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lord) is a constellation of nineteen stars which is visible in the night sky during [First Seed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:First_Seed). It is one of the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Lord are thought to be stronger and healthier, although they are sometimes referred to as [Trollkin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Trollkin) due to their innate weakness to fire.

## The Lover
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/MW-birthsign-Lover.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Lover.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lover.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Lover.jpg) The Lover The [Lover](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lover) is a constellation of twelve stars which is in the night sky during [Sun's Dawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sun%27s_Dawn). It is one of the [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Lover are thought to be graceful and passionate.

## The Mage
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/MW-birthsign-Mage.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Mage.jpg) The Mage The [Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Mage) (also known as the **Wizard**, the **Sage**, the **Mechanist** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), and the **Witch in the Stars** or simply the **Witch** to the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md)) is a constellation of twenty-seven stars and the planet [Julianos](Lore_Julianos_(planet).md), which is in the night sky during the month of [Rain's Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rain%27s_Hand). Along with [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md) and [the Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief), it is a Guardian constellation, and its charges are the [Apprentice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Apprentice), the [Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atronach), and the [Ritual](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Ritual). Those born under the sign of the Mage are thought to have more magicka and a talent for spellcasting. They are also thought to be arrogant and absent-minded.

## The Ritual
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/MW-birthsign-Ritual.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Ritual.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Ritual.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Ritual.jpg) The Ritual The [Ritual](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Ritual) (known as the **Laboratory** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md)) is a constellation of seven stars which is in the night sky during [Morning Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morning_Star). It is one of the [Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Mage)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Ritual are thought to have various abilities depending on the aspects of the moons and planets. It is consistently depicted by most cultures as taking the shape of the [Aurbic Eye](Lore_Aurbic_Eye.md).

## The Serpent
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg) The Serpent The [Serpent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Serpent) (known as the **Snake in the Stars** to the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md), or simply the **Snake** to both Reachmen and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md)) is a constellation of four [unstars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Unstars) which is not relegated to being in the night sky during a particular time of the year. Unlike stars, the unstars that form the Serpent move about the sky and do not emit varliance. The Serpent's motions are considered to be unpredictable, though they can be predicted to a degree. Sometimes it is deemed impossible, however. Those born under the sign of the Serpent are thought to have no characteristics in common except being the most blessed and the most cursed.

## The Shadow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/MW-birthsign-Shadow.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Shadow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Shadow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Shadow.jpg) The Shadow The [Shadow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Shadow) is a constellation of five stars which is in the night sky during [Second Seed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Second_Seed). It is one of the [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Shadow are thought to have the ability to hide in shadows.

## The Steed
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/MW-birthsign-Steed.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Steed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Steed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Steed.jpg) The Steed The [Steed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Steed) is a constellation of eight stars which is in the night sky during [Midyear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Midyear). It is one of the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Steed are thought to be impatient and always hurrying from one place to another. It is typically depicted as a horse. According to some the Steed is prominent in the southern sky during the [summer solstice](Lore_Solstice_(astronomy).md#Summer_Solstice).

## The Thief
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/MW-birthsign-Thief.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Thief.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Thief.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Thief.jpg) The Thief The [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief) (known as the **Hunter** to the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md)) is a constellation of eighteen or seventeen stars and the planet [Arkay](Lore_Arkay_(planet).md) which is in the night sky during [Evening Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Evening_Star). It is a Guardian constellation, and its charges are the [Lover](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lover), the [Shadow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Shadow), and the [Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Tower). Those born under the sign of the Thief are thought to take risks and evade harm. Their luck is thought to run out eventually, cutting their lives short.

## The Tower
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/MW-birthsign-Tower.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Tower.jpg) The Tower The [Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Tower) is a constellation of twelve stars which is in the night sky during [Frostfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frostfall). It is one of the [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Tower are thought to have a knack for finding gold and opening locks.

## The Warrior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/MW-birthsign-Warrior.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Warrior.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Warrior.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Warrior.jpg) The Warrior The [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md) (known as the **Headsman** to the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md)) is a constellation of thirty or twenty-eight stars and the planet [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh_(planet).md) which is in the night sky during [Last Seed](Lore_Last_Seed.md). It is one of the Guardian constellations, and its charges are the [Lady](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lady), the [Steed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Steed), and the [Lord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lord). Those born under the sign of the Warrior are thought to be short-tempered and skilled with [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md).

## Other
Other constellations exist, although they are unnamed.

Several constellations resemble various skills and are associated with the three Guardian Constellations:

- The Mage: Alteration, Conjuration, Destruction, Enchanting, Illusion, Restoration<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-SRSkills-20)</sup>
- The Thief: Alchemy, Light Armor, Lockpicking, Pickpocket, Sneak, Speech<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-SRSkills-20)</sup>
- The Warrior: Archery, Block, Heavy Armor, One-handed, Smithing, Two-handed<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-SRSkills-20)</sup>

Similar other constellations include the Vampirism constellation resembling a [bat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bat) or [Vampire Lord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vampire_Lord), and the Werewolf constellation associated with the [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bloodmoon) which resembles a [werewolf](Lore_Werewolf.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-21)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), several ancient Altars of Celestial Convergence were excavated in [Craglorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Craglorn) by the [Antiquarian Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Antiquarian_Circle). These ancient [Nedic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede) relics depicted an unknown constellation, which was speculated by the antiquarians to have been an attempt to rewrite the stars by merging traditional archetypes into new wholes.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup> That same year, an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) mage named [Cassipia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cassipia) attempted to copy the Nedes by becoming a Celestial. Calling herself [the Exalted Viper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Exalted_Viper), Cassipia planned to usurp the Serpent's place in the sky, but she was slain in her mortal form before she could ascend.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-23)</sup>
