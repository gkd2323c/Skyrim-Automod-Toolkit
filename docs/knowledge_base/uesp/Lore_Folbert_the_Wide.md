Folbert **the Wide** was the King of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), an ally of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) during [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), and an instrumental figure in the second or greater [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EG-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/ON-npc-Oracle_Marieve.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Oracle_Marieve.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Oracle_Marieve.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Oracle_Marieve.jpg) Oracle Marieve, the seer who foretold Folbert's demise His predecessor was King [Bergamot](Lore_Bergamot_(person).md), whom he succeeded at some point between [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560) and [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567), after he perished during the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) epidemic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Toa MC3-2)</sup> He aided [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) during Ranser's War and was instrumental in the second or greater Daggerfall Covenant of [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567). Around that time, Folbert expressed his belief that Wayrest's preeminence was mere luck. It was believed that should Wayrest overextend itself, Folbert would seize the opportunity to return Daggerfall to the forefront in High Rock.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EG-1)</sup> It is unknown when he died, but as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Kingdom of Daggerfall was ruled by King Casimir.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KC-3)</sup>

Folbert's death was foreseen by [Oracle](Lore_Sight.md) Marieve.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VG-4)</sup>
