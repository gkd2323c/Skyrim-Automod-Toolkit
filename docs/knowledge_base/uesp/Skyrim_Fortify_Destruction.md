*Alchemy description:* **Destruction spells are <mag>% stronger for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Destruction spells cost <mag>% less to cast.**
There are two distinct **Fortify [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md)** effects: one from [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) -created potions and one granted by [enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) armor. Their effects are as follows.

## Alchemy
In potions, Fortify Destruction increases the magnitude of Destruction magic.

## Enchanting
In enchantments, Fortify Destruction decreases the [cost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Cost) of Destruction spells and the use cost of Destruction-based enchantments.

The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Destruction if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Bracers, Circlets, Helmets, Rings, Necklaces, and Robes *[of Destruction](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Destruction)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Destruction*, *of Major Destruction*, *of Extreme Destruction*, *of Eminent Destruction*, and *of Peerless Destruction.*
- [Hooded Thalmor Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes)
- [Thalmor Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Robes)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Archmage's Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Archmage%27s_Robes)
- [Armor of the Old Gods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_of_the_Old_Gods)
- [Diadem of the Savant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Diadem_of_the_Savant)
- [Mentor's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mentor%27s_Ring)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

## Notes
- Fortify Destruction potions always have a duration of 60 seconds, no matter the amount of Fortify Alchemy apparel worn or Alchemy perks and skill level. The only thing that gets stronger is the boosted power of Destruction effect. With the [Fortify Restoration glitch](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), it's possible to make Fortify Destruction potions that will turn even Novice level spells such as [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) into spells strong enough to even combat Dragons and other high health enemies.
- Fortify Destruction enchantments not only decrease the cost of Destruction spells, but also the cost of each use of a Destruction-type enchanted weapon, such as one enchanted with fire or health absorption. This also works with staves, either greatly increasing the uses of the weapon, or even outright eliminating the need to recharge them with soul gems.
- While Fortify Destruction can't be enchanted onto heavy armor [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heavy_Gauntlets) through normal gameplay, the [Ebony Spell Knight Gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Spell_Knight_Gauntlets)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md)</sup> obtained at the end of the [Crypt of the Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crypt_of_the_Heart)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md)</sup> quest by choosing not to purify the [Corrupted Human Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corrupted_Human_Heart) possesses this enchantment with an effect of 25%.
