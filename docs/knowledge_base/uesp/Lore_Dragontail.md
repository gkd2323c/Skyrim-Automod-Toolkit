[![](https://images.uesp.net/e/ef/DF-place-Dragontail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Dragontail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Dragontail.jpg) Dragontail Dragontail was the main city within the unconquered territories of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) immediately prior to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dragontail-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWITW-2)</sup> The mountains bordered many other regions, including [Tigonus](Lore_Tigonus_(fiefdom).md), the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), [Santaki](Lore_Santaki_(county).md), [Dak'fron](Lore_Dak%27fron.md), [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md), [Kozanset](Lore_Kozanset_(barony).md), [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md), and [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth_(fiefdom).md), and it completely surrounded [Ephesus](Lore_Ephesus_(march).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map-3)</sup> The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline of the region was [Khulari](Lore_Khulari.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup>
