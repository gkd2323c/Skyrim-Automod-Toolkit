## History
Tigonus is a historical name for the northeast portion of the Iliac Bay's main rocky region west of the Dragontail Mountains, going as far back as [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTigonus-5)</sup> It bordered the [Hollow Wastes](Lore_Hollow_Wastes.md) to the west and the [Fallen Wastes](Lore_Fallen_Wastes.md) of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) to the east. The region contained the towns of [Kozanset](Lore_Kozanset.md) and [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md), and important sites such as the Lost City of the Na-Totambu, the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins [Volenfell](Lore_Volenfell.md) and Yldzuun, the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) ruin Hoon Ding's Watch, and the Motalion Necropolis.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTigonus-5)</sup> Tigonus's primary population consists of [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) but other races and subcultures also live in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFTigonus-2)</sup> [Barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md) live in places such as the Community of Thoshki and the Stronghold of Nhurhton. [Giants](Lore_Giant.md) live in strongholds such as Rattedred and the Fortress of Thurnem, and [harpies](Lore_Harpy.md) roost in dungeons like the Hezdoth Nest and Tower Blallr. [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) also live in Tigonus ruins like Khiz's Guard and Sekaus.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-6)</sup> The [Anthotis](Lore_Anthotis.md) bloodline is the most dominant one in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFTigonus-2)</sup> The Haunt of Verezezzar and Castle Viscount Darkomam are examples of [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) haunts in the Tigonus.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-6)</sup> When the fiefdom was founded is unknown.

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Tigonus briefly existed as a fiefdom. In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the Iliac Bay experienced a magical phenomenon known as the [Miracle of Peace](Lore_Miracle_of_Peace.md), which resulted in the annexation of several regions into one of the four active kingdoms. Tigonus, along with the rest of the southern Iliac Bay was absorbed into the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), at the time ruled by Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWITW-7)</sup> During the war with [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md), a [skeletal dragon](Lore_Bone_Dragon.md) was sent to attack the forces of [Laretha Warhaft](Lore_Talin_Warhaft.md) stationed in the fortress Kalaan within Tigonus, who managed to defeat it.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWarofthe Wormgod-4)</sup>

## Fiefdom of Tigonus
The fiefdom of Tigonus was a political entity that briefly existed around [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405). It was ruled by an unnamed Lady and its main patron deity was [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md), who is represented through the [House of Dibella](Lore_House_of_Dibella.md) temple. At the time, it was neighbored by the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) to the south, Ayasofya to the southwest, and the Dragontail Mountains to the east. Many cities dotted the region's landscape, from both the swamps to the wetlands. These included Antelibuton, Atruza, Bubohabia, Daminia, Gyselah, Kalaan, Kalallaa, Lastabia, Makaja, Makaka-Ij, Makumbazar, Rahnoccosia, Syra-Kom, Wadijuthua, Zagorana, and Zenirijet.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFTigonus-2)</sup>
