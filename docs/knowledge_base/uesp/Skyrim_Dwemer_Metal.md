# Dwarven

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-item-Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.jpg) Dwarven Metal Ingot **Dwarven** [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) are medium-quality items of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) design that are made from Dwarven Metal Ingots. The secret of creating Dwarven Metal was lost with the Dwemer, and therefore it is one of the two metals that can't be [mined](Skyrim_Mining.md), the other being [Steel](Skyrim_Steel.md). However, the Dwemer left large amounts of scrap metal behind in their [Dwarven Ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md), and this scrap metal can be [smelted](Skyrim_Smelter.md) into ingots.

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md).

## Overview
Six different types of scrap Dwemer metal can be [smelted](Skyrim_Smelting.md) to yield Dwarven Metal Ingots. The following lists all of these scraps, along with the number of ingots that each produces when smelted and the total number of guaranteed samples that can be found.

Other metallic items that are found in Dwarven Ruins cannot be smelted, including various other types of [Dwemer scrap metal](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md#Dwemer_Metal_.28Not_Smeltable.29) and [Dwemer dishes](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Dwemer_Dishes). A rule of thumb is that if it starts with the word "Dwemer" it cannot be converted, not even [Dwemer Scrap Metal](Skyrim_Dwemer_Scrap_Metal.md) (see [Bugs](#Bugs) for a workaround on regular Dwemer Scrap Metal); however [*Bent* Dwemer Scrap Metal](Skyrim_Bent_Dwemer_Scrap_Metal.md) can be smelted. The [Small Dwemer Lever](Skyrim_Small_Dwemer_Lever.md) is the only exception; its name doesn't start with Dwemer, but it still cannot be smelted.

Positive delta values are listed in green, and negative ones are listed in red.

- Dwarven Smithing requires 30 [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill.
- Dwarven armor is level 12 [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md), made from Dwarven Metal Ingots, [Steel Ingots](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md), [Iron Ingots](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md), and [Leather Strips](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md).
- Dwarven weapons are level 12 weapons, made from Dwarven Metal Ingots, Steel Ingots, Iron Ingots, and Leather Strips.

## Mineral Sources
In addition to the following guaranteed sources, the best random locations are:

- Dwarven Metal Ingots may be sold by blacksmiths starting at level 6 and general goods [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) starting at level 4.
- All varieties of [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) have a 20% chance of dropping Bent Dwemer Scrap Metal.
- All varieties of [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) have an 8% chance of dropping Bent Dwemer Scrap Metal.
- The other five types of Dwemer metal each have a 10% chance of 1-3 samples being dropped by all varieties of Dwarven Spheres and [Dwarven Centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md).
- All types of Dwemer metal can be randomly found in chests and other loot containers in Dwarven Ruins.
- If the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk is unlocked, [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) will have a few bits of Dwemer metal and items for sale (this is the scrap metal and Dwemer items that are lying on the floor, tables and other surfaces around him: it can also be easily stolen, or picked up for free if your disposition with him is good, and will disappear from his sale menu if you pick it up.)

## Dwarven Smithing
Dwarven Smithing is the first perk in the Heavy Armor branch of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill's perk tree. Unlocking it requires a Smithing skill of 30, and the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk must have already been unlocked. Dwarven Smithing allows both Dwarven armor and Dwarven weapons to be crafted.

The Dwarven Smithing perk also causes tempering of Dwarven weapons and armor to be twice as effective. This perk applies to both unenchanted Dwarven gear and enchanted gear ([generic armor](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md), [generic weapons](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md), or [custom](Skyrim_Enchanting.md)), as long as the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk has been unlocked.

### Dwarven Armor
Dwarven armor is a variety of heavy armor that is only better than [Iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) and [Steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) (and *unimproved* [Bonemold](Skyrim_Bonemold.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>) armors. It appears in leveled lists starting at level 12 (enchanted varieties at level 13). All items can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) using 1 [Dwarven Metal Ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md), and having the Dwarven Smithing perk doubles the quality improvement.

The [Fearsome Fists](Skyrim_Fearsome_Fists.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds the Brawler's dwarven gauntlets, a new gauntlet variant. The base pair can be crafted and differ only in appearance, while a pair with a 12 point [Fortify Unarmed](Skyrim_Fortify_Unarmed_Damage.md) enchantment can be bought from vendors and found in random loot.

### Dwarven Weapons
Dwarven [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) are better than [Iron](Skyrim_Iron.md), [Steel](Skyrim_Steel.md), and [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) weapons. They appear in leveled lists starting at level 12 (enchanted varieties at level 13). All items except ammunition can be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) using 1 Dwarven Metal Ingot, and having the Dwarven Smithing perk doubles the quality improvement. Ammunition can only be smithed if the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) has been installed.

[†](#note_Bow) The base gold value used for all [generic enchanted](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md) versions of the Dwarven Bow is 95 instead of 270. This means that the enchanted weapons are 175 gold cheaper than they should be, and in several cases are cheaper than the unenchanted bow. This bug is shared by several types of bow in the game. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.

[‡](#note_Dawnguard) This item can only be acquired or crafted by siding with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). See [Ancient Technology](Skyrim_Ancient_Technology.md) for details. [¥](#note_Enhanced) The Enhanced Dwarven Crossbow has an additional property: *Attacks with this crossbow ignore 50% of armor.* [±](#note_Forge) Crafting crossbows and exploding bolts is only possible at [Gunmar's forge](Skyrim_Forge.md#Gunmar.27s_Forge) in [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md), which does not appear until after [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) (must side with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md)). Normal bolts can be crafted at any forge.
## Notes
- The [Ancient Knowledge](Skyrim_Ancient_Knowledge.md) ability, gained from the quest [Unfathomable Depths](Skyrim_Unfathomable_Depths.md), is supposed to make Dwarven armor 25% more effective when worn (see [Bugs](Skyrim_Ancient_Knowledge.md#Bugs)).
- Purchasing any Dwarven armor or weapon will cause the [Buy Dwarven artifact](Skyrim_Buy_Dwarven_artifact.md) quest to start.
- One Dwarven metal ingot is needed at [workbenches](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve [Spellbreaker](Skyrim_Spellbreaker.md).
- The Dwarven Bow has two unique varieties: [Zephyr](Skyrim_Zephyr.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> and the [Dwarven Black Bow of Fate](Skyrim_Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>.
- If you have *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* installed, you can find Dwarven Bolts on dead [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md).
- A full suit of Dwarven armor can be found at [Nchuand-Zel Armory](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel_Armory.md), inside an expert-locked door to the right of [Erj](Skyrim_Erj.md).
- All Honed Ancient Nord weapons gain from the Dwarven Perk despite requiring Steel Ingots to temper.
- Wearing a full set of Dwarven Mail armor, you can benefit from either the [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md#Matching_Set) or [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) matching set perk. Any combination of Dwarven and Dwarven Mail armor will benefit from the Heavy Armor Matching Set perk only.

## Bugs
- Inconsistently, regular Dwemer scrap metal cannot be smelted, while bent Dwemer scrap metal can. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13050))
- NPCs are not able to properly use Dwarven crossbows due to an animation bug. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=4304))
- Dwemer Scrap Metal is not classified as ores or ingots by merchants, and therefore cannot be sold to blacksmiths or jewelers without the [Merchant perk](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.

- This bug is also fixed with version 1.6.1130 of the [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch.](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md)
