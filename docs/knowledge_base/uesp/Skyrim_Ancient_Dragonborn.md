[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-npc-Ancient_Dragonborn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ancient_Dragonborn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ancient_Dragonborn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ancient_Dragonborn.jpg) Ancient Dragonborn The **Ancient Dragonborn** will appear if your health falls below 50% when the second or third word of the [Dragon Aspect](Skyrim_Dragon_Aspect.md) shout is active. He appears as an invisible man equipped in a set of spectral armor identical to the armor that you gain from using Dragon Aspect. The Ancient Dragonborn will behave like a normal follower, but cannot be interacted with and can't be talked to. Once he has killed all visible enemies within close proximity, he will disappear.

In combat, the Ancient Dragonborn will attack with a [Nord Hero Battle Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Hero_Battle_Axe) and can use the [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md), [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md), and [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) [dragon shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Notes
- He cannot be attacked; magic and weapons pass straight through him.
- He can be disarmed by draugr using the Disarm shout, such as deathlords.
- There is a destruction ritual spell named "Conjure Ancients" which is not available in the game. It summons an Ancient Dragonborn, and per every 5 dragon souls an additional one is summoned.
- The Ancient Dragonborn is not affected by the Twin Souls perk meaning the player can have two summons and an Ancient Dragonborn at the same time.
