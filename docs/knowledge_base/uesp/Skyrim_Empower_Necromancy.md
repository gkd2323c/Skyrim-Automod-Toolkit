*Enchanting description:* **Conjuration spells last <mag>% longer. Increases summon limit by <1> for lesser conjured or reanimated undead.**
**Empower Necromancy** increases the duration of [Conjuration spells](Skyrim_Conjuration_Spells.md) and increases the summon limit for lesser undead.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to [enchant](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) custom items with this effect if you find one of the following items and disenchant it.

- [Elite](Skyrim_Elite_Necromancer_Robes.md) and [Ascendant](Skyrim_Ascendant_Necromancer_Robes.md) Necromancer Robes

## Related Effects

## Notes
Even with a [black soul gem](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) and [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) at level 100, it is not possible to regain the original potency of the enchantment (20% for Elite Necromancer Robes and 25% for Ascendant Necromancer Robes).
