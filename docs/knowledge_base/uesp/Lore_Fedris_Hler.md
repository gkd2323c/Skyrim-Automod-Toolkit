Fedris Hler was a leader of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) who served the goddess [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) in her holy city [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). Fedris had served under Almalexia for what the goddess herself described as a "very long time".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRAlmalexia-1)</sup> Fedris originally was an assassin of some renown and carried out some unsavory chores for Ayem.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRBarenziah-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRPlitinius-3)</sup> Fedris would eventually become the Goddess' Chief Steward,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FH-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-GDD-5)</sup> as well as the leader of the [Hands of Almalexia](Lore_Hands_of_Almalexia.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRBarenziah-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRHelseth-6)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) arrived to Mournhold and became wrapped up in service to the Temple. The Nerevarine completed tasks for Fedris before later being entrusted to work with Almalexia directly.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TRAAA-7)</sup> When Almalexia confronted and was killed by the Nerevarine, few were able to come to terms with or believe the news, including Fedris Hler, who rejected the premise that his Lady had been slain.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FH-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Alma Rula](Lore_Alma_Rula.md) is a Temple leadership role. This figure leads all of the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) and resides in the capital city of Almalexia.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Gen Dialogue Ordinator-8)</sup> Considering that Fedris both resides in Mournhold, and is the leader of the Hands of Almalexia, along with having the power to deploy High Ordinators,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FH-4)</sup> with the High Ordinators also deferring to his leadership,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> it is possible that Fedris is the Alma Rula of his time.
- The Alma Rula of 3E 427 was said to have found themselves embroiled in a heated conflict about Temple doctrine with vocal critics.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Galsa-10)</sup>
