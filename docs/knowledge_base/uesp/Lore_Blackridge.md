## History
In [4E 170](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_170),<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Captain Martius Silius and his soldiers came to Blackridge seeking food and refuge after having been ambushed by [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md). The town had nothing to spare, as the previous harvest had been disastrous. After the town elder refused, Silius stabbed him through the chest, and the soldiers killed everyone in town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Boregis Visit-2)</sup> Martius Silius was tried for the murders on the 30th of Frostfall, [4E 171](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_171), but the verdict never came, because the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) declared the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) that day, and the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) called all soldiers to fight in the war.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Boregis Visit-2)</sup>

In [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), [the Warrior](Lore_Hero.md#Warrior) visited the abandoned Blackridge while investigating a series of murders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Visit-1)</sup> There, they encountered Boregis, a survivor of the massacre ten years earlier, who revealed that he was murdering the culprits one by one for revenge. After a confrontation, the Warrior defeated Boregis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Visit-1)</sup>
