[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/BS-misc-Battlespire_Cinematic_Render.jpg/200px-BS-misc-Battlespire_Cinematic_Render.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-misc-Battlespire_Cinematic_Render.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-misc-Battlespire_Cinematic_Render.jpg) The Battlespire surrounded by celestial bodies Slipstream Realms are points within the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) at which transliminal forces balance. They operate as transition zones where the conceptual truths of different realms, such as [time](Lore_Akatosh.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Fa Nuit Hen2-1)</sup> can coexist. They were first discovered and given their name by the [Imperial Mananauts](Lore_Imperial_Mananauts.md), who used the regions to acclimate their minds to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) by gentle degrees.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Fa Nuit Hen-2)</sup>

Though there are multiple Slipstream Realms, the most notable is the singularly named **Slipstream Realm**, within which the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) is found, encompassing the entirety of the realm. It is located between several overlapping realms of Oblivion at the edge of the [Void](Lore_Void.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lucilla-3)</sup> It may be [accessed](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) from [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) via a pillar of light, linking to the [Weir Gate](Lore_Weir_Gate.md) on the Spire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Fa Nuit Hen-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Doors-4)</sup> Several moderately sized celestial bodies were known to orbit around the Battlespire,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Planets-5)</sup> but it is unknown what relation they had to the Slipstream Realm.

## Notes
- The exact nature of the Battlespire's realm is conflicted. Lucilla Caprenia, after staying at the fortress for over a decade, described its location as intersecting with several planes at the edge of Oblivion, beyond which only the Void remained.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lucilla-3)</sup> However, the *[Battlespire Athenaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Battlespire_Athenaeum)* places the fortress deep within [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-6)</sup>
- [Maelstrom](Lore_Maelstrom.md) has similar properties to Slipstream Realms,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Fa Nuit Hen-2)</sup> but is more accurately classified as an Oblivious Vortex.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Riparius-7)</sup>
