[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Tsun%27s_Judgement.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Tsun%27s_Judgement.png) Tsun's Judgement Tsun's Judgement is a unique [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md). It is acquired through the [Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md) quest. If you are on the Imperial side, it will be used by [Klija Wolf-Claw](Skyrim_Klija_Wolf-Claw.md) or the [Stormcloak Champion](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Champion.md) and can be acquired after defeating them, however, if you are on the Stormcloak's side it will be given to you to use against the [Imperial Champion](Skyrim_Imperial_Champion.md).
