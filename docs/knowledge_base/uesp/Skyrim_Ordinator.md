[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/SR-npc-Ordinator.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ordinator.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ordinator.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ordinator.jpg) Ordinator The **Ordinators** are guards in the [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) Tribunal Temple faction added by the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md) Creation. The Ordinators are initially hostile, but they will become friendly if you join the Tribunal Temple. If you decide to betray or attack the worshippers, the Ordinators will revert to hostile.

Ordinators wear a set of [Indoril Armor](Skyrim_Indoril_Armor.md).
