[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-npc-Imperial_Deserter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_Deserter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Deserter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Deserter.jpg) Imperial Deserter The **Imperial Deserter** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) found in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md) in one of the booths set up for enjoying [Redwater Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma). He wears a set of light [Imperial soldier armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Imperial_Soldier_Armor) with no helmet, and carries an [Imperial shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Shield.md) and [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md).

After the [Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) is filled during the quest of the [same name](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md), he along with the other addicts will be found dead when exiting the caverns.

## Dialogue
If you approach him, he'll recognize you from [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and try to justify his actions:

- *"Wha- who... you're that one from Helgen. Barely made it out of there myself. I hurt my back and I- I just need something for the pain."*

If spoken to again:

- *"I ain't in the mood to talk. Ohhhh..."*
