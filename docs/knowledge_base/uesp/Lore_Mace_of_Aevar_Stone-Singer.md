# Mace of Aevar Stone-Singer

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/BM-item-Mace_of_Aevar_Stone-Singer.jpg/200px-BM-item-Mace_of_Aevar_Stone-Singer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-item-Mace_of_Aevar_Stone-Singer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-item-Mace_of_Aevar_Stone-Singer.jpg) The Mace of Aevar Stone-Singer (Bloodmoon) The Mace of Aevar Stone-Singer (also called the **Mace of Aevar Stonesinger**) is a prized [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) artifact.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup> According to legend, the eponymous hero used the mace to amplify the Song of the Earth, returning the Gift of the Earth and making the land rich again for the Skaal.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ASS-2)</sup>

It is traditionally awarded to anyone who completes the Ritual of the Gifts, a sort of pilgrimage around [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) practiced by the Skaal. Though a magnificent and lethal [stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md) weapon which inflicts frost damage on foes, the mace is also extraordinarily heavy. In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) completed the ritual and became the only known outsider to have received the mace from the Skaal.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup>
