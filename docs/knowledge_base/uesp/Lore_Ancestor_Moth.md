# Ancestor Moth

— [Imperial Geographic Society](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Geographic_Society), *[Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition/Cyrodiil](Lore_Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition_Cyrodiil.md)* The Ancestor Moth is a rare indigenous gypsy- [moth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moth) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1st_Cyrodiil-1)</sup> and [Ancestor Glades](Lore_Ancestor_Glade.md) across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dexion-2)</sup> They are sacred to the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) of Cyrodiil, specifically, the Cyro-Nords of the [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md). An order of monks, known as the [Cult of the Ancestor Moth](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md), revere and honor the moths, believing they house [fjyrons](Lore_Fjyron.md), the loving spirits of their ancestors, which keep them alive and grant wisdom and knowledge to those they come upon.

## Culture
According to the Cult of the Ancestor Moth, mortal [souls](Lore_Souls.md) are the [Aedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aedra) essence at the core of every mortal's existence. They can be likened to the wings of a butterfly, scaled and full of vessels which become filled with the experiences of mortal existence. It is said that upon death a process of dissipation whereby some of these accumulated contents disperse. These essences, the Fjyrons or "will to peace", are believed to retain a connection to both the grand fabric of [creation](Lore_Aurbis.md) and to all aspects of the soul in all their [destinations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Existence). Thus because, all aspects of a soul remain connected, tapping into the Fjyrons allows one who knows how to receive the erudition of the ancestors. Knowledge and guidance from beyond mortality, from the [existence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherius) beyond the present and the past and the known world, where time is irrelevant, thus allowing one to glimpse the cosmic tapestry and its threads. The Ancestor Moths of the Order are said to imbibe the Fjyrons, becoming animated by the Fjyrons of the ancestor spirits, shepherding that wisdom and passing it through the generations. Thus the moths represent a connection to the Aedra and the "endless Aedric coil of mortal souls" through the experiences of every mortal who has ever lived, and their trilling forms a sort of primal augur which allows the Order to read and interpret the contents of the Elder Scrolls which contain knowledge said to exceed even the [gods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gods).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-POTAM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONCrassius Viria-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAESMP-7)</sup> They believe the song of the Ancestor moth enables their augurs to glean truths from the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONCrassius Viria-6)</sup> This process is known as the [Ritual of the Ancestor Moth](Lore_Ritual_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md). The moths maintain a connection to the ancient [magic](Lore_Magic.md) that allows the Moth Priest to decipher them. Moths emanate a soft harmonious trilling that when amplified taps into a form of primal augur. This allows the moths themselves to become a conduit for deciphering the scrolls. By having the moths close to the Moth Priest, they can utilize the conduit and share the moth's augury.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dexion-2)</sup>

[Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) are unable to read Elder Scrolls, as ancestor moths refuse to communicate with them. According to *Herma-Mora and the Moth*, [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Forbidden Knowledge, long ago sought the secrets of ancestor moths and their connections to the fjyrons of mortal souls, so that he may access the secrets of the Elder Scrolls. He encountered a lone moth who refused to give away any confidential information but managed to bargain with the Great Eye a deal. To showcase that the Daedric Prince was wise and merciful despite greatly wanting to obtain the secrets behind the Elder Scrolls, ancestor moths from then on would wear his eyes on their wings, branded on them by the Prince himself.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAESMP-7)</sup>

The Nibenese are known to use the [ancestor silk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ancestor_Silk) of the gypsy-moth to craft exotic cloth, and export to others across the provinces. Fjyrons can be sung into the silk produced by the Ancestor Moths. When the silk is spun into cloth and embroidered with the genealogy of the appropriate ancestor, it creates garments of extraordinary power, in this manner the singing and hymnal vocals of the ancestors are collected into a special silk-gathering ceremony, which is the ancestor silk. In the Nibenay, this silk harvest is a sacred tradition that dates back to their early history. But to the Cult of the Ancestor Moth, they are one of the same. The cult harvest the silk and undergo a magical ritual to enchant the fabric. The higher order Monks of the cult are able to forgo the magical ritual needed to enchant the silk and instead merely wear the moths themselves.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1st_Cyrodiil-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-POTAM-4)</sup> Some believe their silk shawls hold memories of the dead.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Ass-8)</sup>

One monk of the Cult of the Ancestor Moth believed that the moths were first stolen from the [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAESMP-7)</sup>

## Biology
The Ancestor Moth is attracted to the [Canticle Tree](Lore_Canticle_Tree.md), specifically the crushed grain of the canticle bark. The cult would also recite mantras, to maintain a spiritual connection and skin contact with the Ancestor Moth. They endure such a process to keep a cosmic balance. If the monk is interrupted, the Ancestor Moth would burst in the air, and only come back when the monk continues their mantras.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1st_Cyrodiil-1)</sup> As mentioned before, the Ancestor Moth produces the ancestor silk, which is crimson-red and is prime material for making the strongest type of Light Armor.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The [Moth's wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ancestor_Moth_Wing) are known to contain alchemic properties, such as fortify [Conjuration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Conjuration), but also damaging stamina. The Moth chrysalis is green and is known to faintly smell like fresh grass, and clean water. They are considered very valuable, known to have been in circulation during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
