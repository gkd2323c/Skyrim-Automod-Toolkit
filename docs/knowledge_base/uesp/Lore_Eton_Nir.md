[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/ON-place-Eton_Nir.jpg/200px-ON-place-Eton_Nir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Eton_Nir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Eton_Nir.jpg) Eton Nir circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Eton Nir is the tallest mountain found in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), within the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-3rd_Summerset-1)</sup> Eton Nir consists of an abundance of ruins, as well as expansive tunnel systems that connect each coast. The city-state of [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) is found on the highest alps of Eton Nir.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The **Eton Nir Grotto** is one of the many underground caverns that are found across the massif.

## Geography
The high slopes also contain a breed of [Gryphon](Lore_Gryphon.md) known as the *Azure Gryphon*, which has a distinct blue tint on their feathers, which is attributed as either their natural camouflage, or a gift from the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Further up in the peaks of Eton Nir lies the *Snowcap Gryphon*, which are pale snow white, and live in the clouds of Eton Nir. They were considered a myth for most of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Gryphons are commonly used by the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of Eton Nir, including the [Welkynars](Lore_Welkynars.md), a knightly order of Cloudrest and Eton Nir.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

There are dark rumors across the province that say that Eton Nir contains many treasures,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> as well as hidden ruins of the [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), and even the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-3rd_Summerset-1)</sup> A Temple dedicated to [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) is found deep in the Eton Nir Grotto. The cave systems of [Karnwasten](Lore_Karnwasten.md) and [King's Haven Pass](Lore_King%27s_Haven_Pass.md) go through Eton Nir and are used as alternate paths from the main road. The former is known to be used by smugglers and pirates that bring contraband into Summerset Isle. The [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) is found near the center of the mountain massif.
