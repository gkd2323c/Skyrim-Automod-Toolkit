[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-npc-Guthrum.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Guthrum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Guthrum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Guthrum.jpg) Guthrum **Guthrum** is an aging [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) who resides in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) aboard the permanently docked ship, [The Sea Squall](Skyrim_The_Sea_Squall.md).

Like the other crewmembers on the Sea Squall, Guthrum spends all of his time on deck, wandering, sweeping, eating or drinking. He never sleeps but may sit down on one of the available chairs and relax.

He wields an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe) and wears a regular set of [merchant clothing](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). He carries a random selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
If approached he can say:

*"I plan to spend all the days I got left on the water. It's where I belong."* *"Once sailing gets in your blood, it don't get out."* *"I been a sailor for so long, I could navigate these rivers an' seas blindfolded."* *"[Cap'n Wayfinder's](Skyrim_Captain_Wayfinder.md) as green as seaweed, but he's learnin'."*
