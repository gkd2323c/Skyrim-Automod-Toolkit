## Ingredients
1 guaranteed sample can be found in the following location:

- [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Fish barrels have a 25% chance of containing Cyrodilic spadetails.

## Creatures
Fish appear at fish spawning sites. Each spawning site generates one to three fish, which are randomly selected from various types of fish; dragonflies are typically seen hovering over the spawning sites. In ponds and shallow waters, spadetails have a 20% chance of appearing; in deeper water, spadetails have a 14% chance of appearing. There are 691 spawning sites in 128 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:
