## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Daighre%27s_House.jpg) Inside Daighre's house
### Exterior
It is a single story Dwemer dwelling in a small settlement, where the miners from the [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine) live. The entrance is frequently left unlocked. The [miner's barracks](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) are directly opposite. There is a [juniper tree](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries) growing on the roof.

### Daighre's House
Inside it is a single roomed house with a large fireplace in the middle of the north wall with a pot hanging in front of it containing a [grilled chicken breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Chicken_Breast) and a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks). To the left of the fireplace is an unlocked non-respawning [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing leveled items, with a basket on the floor containing two [gourds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) and [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel on either side of it. Coming out from the west wall is a long wooden table with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a grilled chicken breast and a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) in a cast iron pot on top of the table. On a ledge on the west wall is a [deer hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a loaf of bread and a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese). Against the south wall is a dresser with a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) on top and containing [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes). At the other end of the room there are two single owned beds, [Daighre](Skyrim_Daighre.md)'s is along the north wall and [Erith](Skyrim_Erith.md)'s is along the east wall. There is a ledge along the east wall with a copy of *[Horker Attacks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks)* and a couple of food sacks on top, with a rack hanging from the ceiling above holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). There is a food barrel in the southeastern corner with a crate and sack to the right. On the crate is a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips).

## Notes
- Aside from most barrels and sacks, all containers in the house are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
