Volrina Quarra was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) and the matriarch of the [Quarra Clan](Lore_Quarra_Clan.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Volrina was born in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she worked for the wizard [Barilzar](Lore_Barilzar.md), retrieving objects vital for the mage's research from dangerous locations. However, she quit after her brother was killed by vampires of the [Berne Clan](Lore_Berne_Clan.md) during the job in the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin of Galom Daeus. Volrina managed to escape the ruin and vowed to take revenge against the Berne as soon as she could come up with a "foolproof plan". Later, Volrina met [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who was trying to locate the Dwemer devices which Barilzar needed to build a tonal inverter. Volrina pointed them to Galom Daeus, and the Vestige retrieved one of the necessary components from there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VN-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMW-2)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/MW-npc-Volrina_Quarra.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Volrina_Quarra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Volrina_Quarra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Volrina_Quarra.jpg) Volrina Quarra in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Eventually, Volrina became a vampire herself. It is unknown if she was turned against her will or if it was a part of her plan to take revenge against the Berne. Regardless, Volrina started her own bloodline, the Quarra Clan, which subsequently waged war against the other two clans of Vvardenfell, the Berne and the [Aundae](Lore_Aundae_Clan.md).

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the clan operated out of the Dwarven ruin of Druscashti in the northern [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md), but some of the members dwelled in various ancestral tombs around the island.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MW-3)</sup> Volrina Quarra was a prideful vampire, going as far as sending a new blood to slay [Irarak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Irarak), a self-proclaimed god who managed to gather a small cult of followers. Volrina saw him as a mockery of vampires and had him killed as a result.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWCLI-4)</sup> In the same year, a vial of Quarra blood, which Volrina kept locked in her room in Druscashti, became an object of desire of both [Raxle Berne](Lore_Raxle_Berne.md), the patriarch of the Berne clan,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWBQ-5)</sup> and Mistress [Dratha](Lore_Dratha.md) of [Tel Mora](Lore_Tel_Mora.md). According to some sources, Volrina was slain during an attempt to retrieve the potion.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWBMD-6)</sup>
