[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-npc-Pelagius_the_Suspicious.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Pelagius_the_Suspicious.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pelagius_the_Suspicious.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pelagius_the_Suspicious.jpg) Pelagius the Suspicious **Pelagius the Suspicious** is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Mad.md)'s [mind](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_a_Madman.md). He observes an arena battle between two [atronachs](Skyrim_Daedra.md) and is flanked on either side by [bodyguards](Skyrim_Bodyguard_(Pelagius).md).

Pelagius the Suspicious wears a set of [dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armor.md) along with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Dwarven_Boots.md).
