Ateian Fife *The description of a legendary flute*
---
One of the last flutes crafted by the legendary [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) flutesmith, Parteron Ateia. Parteron's flutes were rare and coveted. This is the last one he finished working on before he died, which he specifically donated to the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) to be enjoyed and preserved for the centuries to come.

Though some of his fifes are still used around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), most are kept hidden away by collectors or the supremely talented flautists within the [Ateia](Lore_Ateia.md) family.
