[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-item-Ring_of_Instinct.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Instinct.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Instinct.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Instinct.jpg) Ring of Instinct The Ring of Instinct is a ring that can be bought from [Majni](Skyrim_Majni.md) in [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md) if you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md). The ring's appearance is identical to a [silver amethyst ring](Skyrim_Silver_Amethyst_Ring.md) and also has the same weight. Its enchantment [causes time to slow for 20 seconds when you enter your werewolf form](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Instinct.md). Multiple copies of these rings can be bought once Majni's merchant chest resets.
