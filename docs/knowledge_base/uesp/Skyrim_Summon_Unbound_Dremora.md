**Summon Unbound Dremora** is a [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons an [unbound Dremora](Skyrim_Unbound_Dremora.md). It can only be cast in one location, atop the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) in the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md), and only during the [Conjuration Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Conjuration_Ritual_Spell.md) quest.

## Effects
- [Summon](Skyrim_Conjure.md) [Unbound Dremora](Skyrim_Unbound_Dremora.md) for 999 seconds.

## Notes
- Other summons will not be dispelled when you cast this spell.
- Unlike other summons, this one can be soul trapped
