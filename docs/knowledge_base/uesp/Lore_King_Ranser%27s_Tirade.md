King Ranser's Tirade **by [King Ranser](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#King_Ranser)** *King Ranser's ravings surrounding the Battle of Markwasten Moor*
---
I may have lost [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md), but [this war](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) is far from over! The damnable [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) is overconfident. He will make a mistake, and my brilliance will leap to take advantage of every misstep and stumble along the way.

Retreating—no, strike that! King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md) doesn't retreat!

My strategic occupation of this tor shall be remembered as the most important military victory in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md)'s history. Let them throw all the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) they have at me. This tor will stand forever! I shall win this war!
