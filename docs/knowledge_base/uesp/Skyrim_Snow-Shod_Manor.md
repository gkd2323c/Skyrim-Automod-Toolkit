## Walkthrough

### Snow-Shod Manor
The entrance is kept adept-locked most of the time. Upon entering you are in a large open room with a flight of stairs up on your left, with stairs leading down to the cellar behind and several [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and sacks around the stairwell. A long end table is against the north wall, on top of which is a [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), a [purple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), and two [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower), as well as a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md). There is a chair opposite the entrance against the east wall, with a wall shelf above holding two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). To the right of the chair is a cupboard and a set of shelves in the southeast corner. These hold a bunch of lavender, a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of wine, copies of *[Rising Threat, Vol. III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rising_Threat,_Vol._III)*, *[Killing - Before You're Killed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed)*, and *[Dwemer Inquiries Vol II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Inquiries_Vol_II)*, an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), and a couple sacks. Against the south wall is an end table that holds a bottle of alto wine with a chair on either side, and against the west wall another long end table holding an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and a [mammoth tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Tusk).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor.jpg) Dining room There is a doorway in the northeast corner leading through into the dining area, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand against the west wall, a cupboard containing only clutter against the north wall, and a wooden dining table and benches in the middle of the room. On the table are three [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), two portions of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a bottle of alto wine, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel). Hanging from the ceiling are two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid, and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the west is the staircase leading down to the cellar.

At the bottom of the stairs, against the south wall is a large pile of firewood and several bales of straw, with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) leaning against the firewood. To the east is a storage area full of various crates, barrels, and sacks. To the north is another storage area with a disused table and chairs, two sets of shelves, and a cupboard containing clutter. On the shelves are various pieces of cheese, two [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a load of [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and several loaves of bread. To the right of the cupboard is a small round table holding a mead barrel.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snow-Shod_Manor_02.jpg) Snow-Shod's bedroom To the right at the top of the stairs is a landing and corridor, with a bedroom to the east belonging to [Asgeir Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md), a double bedroom to the west shared by [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Vulwulf_Snow-Shod.md) and his wife, [Nura Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Nura_Snow-Shod.md), and at the end of the corridor, by some barrels, sacks, and a crate, another bedroom; it would have been their daughter's room, who died after joining the [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md).

Asgeir's bedroom is L-shaped, long, and narrow, and consists of a single owned bed against the east wall, with a small low table beside it. On the table is a bottle of wine. To the right is a chair, and against the west wall is a long end table with an iron sword and [shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) on top. Opposite the door, against the east wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Lilija's old room consists of a single owned bed against the east wall, with an end table beside it against the north wall, and a wardrobe containing clothes beside that. Against the west wall is an unlocked wooden [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot, and opposite the door is a long end table with a bunch of lavender on top.

The parents' room contains an owned double bed coming out from the south wall, with a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and two loose coins underneath, an end table to the left, and a wall shelf above it. On the end table is a copy of *[Yellow Book of Riddles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Book_of_Riddles)*, and on the shelf are two bottles of wine. At the foot of the bed is a long end table, and to the right of the bed is a fireplace. Along the west wall is a pile of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), while in the northwest corner is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a loaf of bread and a bottle of wine on top of the table. Against the north wall is a tall wardrobe containing clothes.
