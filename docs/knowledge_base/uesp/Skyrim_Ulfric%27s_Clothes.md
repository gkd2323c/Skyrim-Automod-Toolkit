It shares its basic design with a [fur-trimmed cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur-Trimmed_Cloak), but Ulfric's clothes are grey.

## Notes
- It appears as a fur-trimmed cloak when worn by a female.

## Bugs
- This item will not appear as the right color in the inventory, instead appearing to look like the fur-trimmed cloak it is based off of. Its world model will still have its unique appearance, however. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, addresses this issue. Ulfric's clothes will now appear as they should when viewed in the inventory.
