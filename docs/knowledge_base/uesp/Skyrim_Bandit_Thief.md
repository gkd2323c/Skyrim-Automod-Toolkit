# Bandit Thief

In the original version of the [Divine Crusader](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), two **Bandit Thieves** could be found at [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md). They had stolen the Crusader's Relics and were killed during the [related quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader). However with the release of [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), the creation was reworked so that the relics are found on two dead NPCs, [Eigorn](Skyrim_Eigorn.md) and [Oren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oren), as it did not fit the lore of the Crusader's Relics for bandits to be able to wear them.

## Related Quests
- **[Relics of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>: Reclaim the holy relics of Pelinal Whitestrake.
