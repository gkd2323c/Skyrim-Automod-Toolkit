[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-npc-Gloth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gloth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gloth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gloth.jpg) Gloth **Gloth** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who works for [Severio Pelagia](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia.md) at [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md) outside [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

While Severio owns a house in Whiterun and only shows up during the day, Gloth and fellow helper, [Nimriel](Skyrim_Nimriel.md), practically live their entire lives on the farm. Gloth sleeps in the farmhouse between midnight and 9am, and always starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. He heads outside at 9am and spends the entire day either feeding the [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) or raking the hay piles near the house. At 7pm he retires to the farmhouse for a bit of food and relaxation until his bedtime at midnight.

He is quite poor and has nothing in his pockets. He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) with [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5) and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa).
