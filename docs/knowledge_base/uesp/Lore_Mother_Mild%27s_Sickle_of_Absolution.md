# Mother Mild's Sickle of Absolution

*It wasn't always crops that Mara harvested.*
— [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup> Mother Mild's Sickle of Absolution is a lesser known [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) associated with goddess [Mara](Lore_Mara.md). Little is known of the sickle or of the practices it was tied to, though it was said to have been associated with blood rites long before such rituals became linked with sinister or macabre connotations. According to [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md), Mara did not always harvest crops alone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup>
