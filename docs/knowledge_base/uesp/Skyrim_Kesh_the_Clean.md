[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean.jpg) Kesh the Clean [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kesh_the_Clean_02.jpg) *"I worship Peryite, yes, because sometimes the world can only be cleansed by disease."* **Kesh the Clean**, a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [spellsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Spellsword), is a worshipper of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) who can be found at the [Shrine to Peryite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_to_Peryite). If you are at least level ten, he will give you the list of items you need in order to speak with Peryite, and thus begin the Daedric quest [The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure).

He spends much of his time working at the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) present at the shrine. When he is not working on his Alchemy, he can be found cooking at the nearby [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md). He sometimes stops for a meal or drink, though he does not sleep as he lacks a place to bed down.

Kesh wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697), a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f), and an [Alik'r hood](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Hood.md). He is equipped with two leveled [daggers](Skyrim_Dagger.md), both of which can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality. He also carries a leveled [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md).

Once you have reached level ten, you may encounter an [Afflicted refugee](Skyrim_Afflicted_Refugee.md) in your travels. If asked, he will say he is fleeing to [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and as he is in a hurry, he will direct you to Kesh: *"Kesh could tell you more. I just want out of Skyrim as quickly as possible."*

## Related Quests
- **[The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure)**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor) for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Only Cure
Upon greeting Kesh:

- *"Ah, a wanderer, yes? No? Pilgrim, perhaps? You have come to commune with Peryite, Taskmaster and blighted Lord, yes?"*
- **Tell me about Peryite.**
- *"He is the pus in the wound. Oh, proper ones curl their noses, but it's pus that drinks foul humors and restores the blood. I worship Peryite, yes, because sometimes the world can only be cleansed by disease."*
- **May I commune with Peryite?**

If you have not reached level ten, he will refuse to help:

- *"Ah, no. Don't think you've got the stuff. Come back when you've seen a bit more in life, perhaps."*

Otherwise he will agree and explain:

- *"Not everyone has the stomach required to entreat my Lord. But Kesh likes you, friend. There is a way Peryite may speak to us who will take Him in. If you wish to commune with Him, we'll need the incense."*
- **Tell me about this incense.**
- *"Yes, the incense. Fetch for me a [deathbell](Skyrim_Deathbell.md) flower, one [silver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), a [flawless ruby](Skyrim_Flawless_Ruby.md), and some [vampire dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust). Then I will show you how."*
- **Where can I find the items you want?**
- *"This one would try a shop. You might prefer the direct route, hm? Deathbell grow in the frozen tundra. You might nip into a mine for the ruby and silver. Vampire Dust is tricky. [Vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md), you know."*

When you bring Kesh the ingredients for the incense:

- **I have the items you requested.**
- *"Ah, have you? Let Kesh have them."*

He will walk to the cauldron, commenting on the ingredients:

- *"Yes, yes. This will create a fine fume."*

Once the incense is ready, he will tell you:

- *"That should do. Now - Inhale deeply."*

Upon doing so, your vision will blur as ghostly [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md) appear before you; they are an aspect of Peryite, who will command you to enter [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md) and kill [Orchendor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor), his traitorous worshipper.

After the aspect fades, you may ask Kesh where Bthardamz is:

- **Where is Bthardamz?**
- *"Not far, not far. This one looks upon it on clear days. Look to the west, at the foot of the mountain. The Dwarven ruins there. Bthardamz."*
- **Who is Orchendor?**
- *"Orchendor? Kesh knew him. He's an Overseer. Shepherd. Gathers the [Afflicted](Skyrim_Afflicted.md), contains the festering wound. Orchendor and his Afflicted are meant to stand ready, awaiting Peryite's command to cover the world with his Blessing."*

If you speak to Kesh after killing Orchendor:

- **May I commune with Peryite again?**
- *"The vapors are waning, but not yet gone. Take a breath. Peryite will speak if He sees fit."*
