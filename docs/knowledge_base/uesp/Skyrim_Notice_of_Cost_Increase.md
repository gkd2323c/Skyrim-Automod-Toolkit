Notice of Cost Increase **by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)** *A notice to an innkeeper about a price increase for mead*
---

- [Keerava](Skyrim_Keerava.md),

- As of this month, I'm afraid we're going to have to double the cost for [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md). As you're no doubt well aware, there's quite a bit of civil unrest occurring within [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). With this unrest comes increased costs to us for shipment protection, ingredients and labor. I think you'll find your customers more than willing to share in this burden by absorbing the cost through a price increase I recommend you institute immediately. Failure to comply with our new costs will result in an immediate interruption of Black-Briar Mead.

- [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)
