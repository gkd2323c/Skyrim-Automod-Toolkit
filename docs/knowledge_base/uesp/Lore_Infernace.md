[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/ON-furnishing-Ashen_Infernace_Gate_02.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Ashen_Infernace_Gate_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Ashen_Infernace_Gate_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Ashen_Infernace_Gate_02.jpg) Gate to Infernance [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg) A summoned Flame Atronach Infernace (also known as **DOP 6**) is a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). It is a realm of extreme heat and molten rock, and is the home of the [Flame Atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md). It is well known to all [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) due to the ability to summon subservient atronachs from the realm. The [Fourth Sinus of Takubar](Lore_Fourth_Sinus_of_Takubar.md), a realm of extreme cold, is an inversion of Infernace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Despite being considered a highly-organized and physical realm, the reality of Infernace is not a projection of the mind of any particular [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). Instead, it was created as an extension of its Flame Atronach inhabitants. Realms such as Infernace are termed 'collective realms', and are considered relatively exceptional. However, collective realms are also rather mundane and uniform, and the endless magma and fumaroles of Infernace are no exception.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FNHQA-2)</sup>

The clan structure of Infernace is unknown, and no hierarchy has been observed due to the difficulties involved in distinguishing individual Flame Atronachs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FNHQA-2)</sup> The realm was once visited by the [Demiprince](Lore_Demiprince.md) [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md), who is said to have worn the [Flame Monarch's Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FNHQA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Some weapons and armor are named for Infernace,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ao I-4)</sup> such as "The Unbroken", a strange flame staff belonging to the Planar Inhibitor, constructed of some unfamiliar material thought to have come from Infernace itself.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Unbroken-5)</sup>
- The ancient [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) used ensorcelled gates to conjure atronach from Infernace.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AIG-6)</sup>
