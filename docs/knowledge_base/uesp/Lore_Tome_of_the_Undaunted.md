Tome of the Undaunted *A record of individuals who took the Pledge of the Undaunted*
---
This tome comes in versions signed by the player character and not. The signed version is presented here.

---
We, the undersigned, declare our intention to take the Pledge of the Undaunted. Be we longstanding members of the guild, or newly blooded blades, we will dare to do what others will not. We will delve beneath [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), we will seek out knowledge and treasure where it lies unseen. We will stand against the darkness.

And we will return [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md).
