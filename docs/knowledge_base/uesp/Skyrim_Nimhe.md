[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-creature-Nimhe.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Nimhe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nimhe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nimhe.jpg) Nimhe **Nimhe** is a giant [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) living in [Nchuand-Zel](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel.md), an ancient [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) castle located deep within a mountain behind [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) in the city of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

According to [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md), who has been placed in charge of curating artifacts recovered from Nchuand-Zel, his workers had come to regard the giant frostbite spider as such a significant threat that they decided to name her "Nimhe", which translates as "The Poisoned One". She spends her time waiting in ambush in a large hole in the Excavation Site ceiling just before the entrance to Nchuand-Zel. The corpse of [Alethius](Skyrim_Alethius.md) that lies near her lair is clear evidence of the challenge Nimhe has been presenting to Calcelmo and his workers.

## Related Quests
- **[Nimhe, the Poisoned One](Skyrim_Nimhe,_the_Poisoned_One.md)**: Kill the giant frostbite spider blocking access to [Nchuand-Zel](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel.md).

## Notes
- Nimhe can be encountered before acquiring the related quest from Calcelmo by picking the lock on the excavation site door.
- "Nimhe" is Irish for "poisonous".
