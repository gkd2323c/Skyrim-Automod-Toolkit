Burnt Scripture *A page fragment written in Ta'agra*
---
[Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) close their eyes
In the dreamless sleep of death they fall
Until below the world they lie
Forever lost in darkness
And the True Moon reigns supreme

Zennrili an Zennji tas jaadi atha'a.
Shabar aydithozay zaigu di sallidadna jaadi ranjith.

Raj'kono viti shabar morna.
An thzi kha'jay t'harithse!
