[Several people](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alchemy_Tutorial) recommend [combining](Skyrim_Recipes.md#Notes) wheat with [blisterwort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort) to make a [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) potion. With [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, three wheat can be used to [create](Skyrim_Milling.md) a [sack of flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md).

## Ingredients
78 guaranteed samples can be found in 45 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 4 in [Fellglow Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellglow_Keep) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 4 in [Mauhulakh's Cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mauhulakh%27s_Cellar) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 4 in [Ogmund's House](Skyrim_Ogmund%27s_House.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 4 in [Treva's Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 3 in [Blind Cliff Bastion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blind_Cliff_Bastion) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Gallows Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gallows_Rock) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 3 in the [Guard Tower](Skyrim_Guard_Tower.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 3 in [Hag Rock Redoubt Ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hag_Rock_Redoubt_Ruin) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 3 in the [Thalmor Embassy, Barracks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy,_Barracks) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 3-8 guaranteed samples sold by [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md)
- 3-8 guaranteed samples sold by [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md)
- 3-7 guaranteed samples sold by the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md)

## Notes
- Wheat can be sold to most of the owners of the above-listed farms for the quest [Gather Wheat](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md), specifically: [Addvild](Skyrim_Addvild.md), [Alfhild Battle-Born](Skyrim_Alfhild_Battle-Born.md), [Azzada Lylvieve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azzada_Lylvieve), [Belyn Hlaalu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belyn_Hlaalu), [Bolfrida Brandy-Mug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug), [Boti](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boti), [Katla](Skyrim_Katla.md), [Reldith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reldith), [Synda Llanith](Skyrim_Synda_Llanith.md), or [Tulvur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tulvur).
- It is also categorized as [raw food](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Food), and therefore it can be sold to innkeeper merchants.
- Wheat that is grown at farms has an invisible, solid column in the center; this is easily seen when trying to walk over bunches that have been harvested. Player grown wheat does not have this property.
