[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-place-The_Chill.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Chill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Chill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Chill.jpg) The Chill [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Chill.jpg) The Chill jail The Chill is a small glacial island off the [coast](Lore_Northern_Coast.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is located north of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) in the [ice fields](Lore_Ice_Fields.md) of the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRWinterhold-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:799</sup> The island is home to an ice cave that serves as Winterhold's jail,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup> where prisoners are often left to die. This island prison is guarded only by [Frost Atronachs](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Chill-3)</sup> The guards in Winterhold will often tell recently captured criminals to "enjoy the Chill", before imprisoning them there.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The island is quite [mineral](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Minerals) rich, with ore deposits of [corundum](Lore_Corundum.md), [gold](Lore_Gold.md), [iron](Lore_Iron.md), and [silver](Lore_Silver.md) all being present. The island and surrounding ice floes are home to wildlife such as [horkers](Lore_Horker.md), [ice wolves](Lore_Ice_Wolf.md), [snowy sabre cats](Lore_Snowy_Sabre_Cat.md), and [snow bears](Lore_Snow_Bear.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Chill-3)</sup>
