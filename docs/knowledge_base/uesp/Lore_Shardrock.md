[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/OB-place-Shardrock.jpg/200px-OB-place-Shardrock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Shardrock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Shardrock.jpg) Shardrock circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Shardrock is a small farming settlement in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), north of the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md) between [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) and [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md). It is located in the [Strid Vale](Lore_Strid_Vale.md), close to where the West Weald meets the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>

The farmstead is located in the heart of [bear](Lore_Bear.md) country, which has historically proved dangerous for inhabitants and livestock. Known collectively as West Weald Bears, the local population are a mix of different bear species, with sightings of [black](Lore_Black_Bear.md), [brown](Lore_Brown_Bear.md), and [great bears](Lore_Great_Bear.md) reported over the centuries.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bear Season-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

## History
A farm existed at Shardrock since at least the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). A farmhouse built in the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) style was overrun by jungle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md) used a [Wildburn seeds](Lore_Wildburn_Seeds.md) to awaken [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) magicka buried in the earth, causing a new forest to grow almost overnight. Known as the Dawnwood, this new forest closely resembled the [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and was swiftly colonised by [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) from [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSeeds-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mt CN-5)</sup> Some of these Bosmer settlers fell victim to bear attacks at Shardrock. The overgrown farm was ultimately abandoned and left to the bears.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), nothing remained of the Dawnwood, and Shardrock was once again an active farm. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the farm was home to a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) farmer, Thorley Aethelred, who tended a small flock of [sheep](Lore_Sheep.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> After losing a number of his sheep to bear attacks, Thorley hired the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to thin out the local bear population.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bear Season-2)</sup>

During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), an Oblivion Gate opened close to Shardrock, but the farm was unaffected.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
