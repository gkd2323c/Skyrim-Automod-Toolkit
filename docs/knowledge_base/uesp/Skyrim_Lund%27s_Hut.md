## Related Quests
- **[When the Cat's Away](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)</sup>: Find a stash of [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) armor.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To reach the building, take the road northwest out of Rorikstead and follow it until you come to a dirt path on the left leading to the shack. It is a single-level, fairly rundown building with a fenced-off porch area at the front. There are four skeevers using a couple moldy straw bales for bedding outside the building. Northwest of the shack a few paces is a cairn with a lit candle and a [silver ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring) (which may have fallen to the ground) on the edge and a basket containing three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on the ground. To the left of the building is a tree with two [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) by the trunk. There are several [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle) and more blue mountain flowers lining the path leading from the road to the shack.

### Lund's Hut
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lund%27s_Hut.jpg) A skeever infestation The building is unlocked and contains two more skeevers. The corpse of the original owner, [Lund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lund), is in a single unusable bed in the eastern corner of this one-room structure. The place is a mess: [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), furniture, and [food](Skyrim_Food.md) are scattered around the room. There is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot at the foot of his bed (in Anniversary Edition this chest requires a key and cannot be picked; the key is obtained by completing the quest [When the Cat's Away](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away)), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) in a cast iron pot under the bed, and a [blue dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing) right in the corner between the bed and the wall, under a basket. Beside the bed, against the southeast wall is an end table with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) on top. Along the rest of the southeast wall are a sack and barrel of foodstuffs, with a barrel of [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) behind the first. In front of these is a low table with a mead barrel and two bottles of [Honningbrew mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead) on top. To the right of the barrels, against the southwest wall is a long wooden table, under which are two [pheasant breasts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast). On the table are a bottle of [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), a bottle of alto wine, and several red apples.

To the right of the table are a wooden bench and a couple of chairs lying on their backs and various items of footwear and clothes underneath. To the right is a fireplace that is still lit, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) and spit in front and a mounted skeever head above. On the mantelpiece is a [blue butterfly wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly_Wing), and in front of the hearth are three wooden bowls; two contain pheasant breasts and the other contains a [chicken breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken_Breast). In the western corner is another long wooden table and bench with a patch of [imp stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool) fungus growing beside it, and in the corner behind it are two more blue dartwings.

In the northern corner is an open crate containing a [butterfly wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_Wing), a sample of [giant lichen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen), a joint of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), and various items of clothing, with a second butterfly wing between the crate and the wall. Along the northeast wall is a set of shelves with three common books, with more on the floor in front. Between the shelves and the entrance is another wooden bench, with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) on top and a wall shelf above holding a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md). Hanging from the ceiling above are a [garlic braid](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md) and a bunch of [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md).
