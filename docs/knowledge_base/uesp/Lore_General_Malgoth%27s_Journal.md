General Malgoth's Journal **by [General Malgoth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#General_Malgoth)** *The journal of General Malgoth's conflict against Vullain*
---
Day 1

A record of the war to unite the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of northern [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) under the leadership of [General Malgoth](Lore_Malgoth.md).

Concluded that war is the best option. The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) do not fear the Bosmer. Inter-tribal war is a small price to pay to provide a strong, united force against our enemies.

The war council agrees [Vullain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vullain) will be the first to fall. They forsake [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) for the deceptions of the [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md) and are little more than a weak band of hunters. They will serve the Bosmer best as an example to the other villages.

Day 2

Victory is ours. An easy triumph. Lost one soldier when he fell from a tree. Three wounded. After fulfilling the [Meat Mandate](Lore_Green_Pact.md), we will be prepared to take the rest of the villages.

Day 2 continued

Treachery! The Bosmer of Vullain poisoned their bodies. A foul trick, a mockery of the Meat Mandate!

Begged the Nereids for a cure. They look at us with what I can only assume is disgust. They can't be bothered to kill us. They know our fate, as do we.
