Daynas Valen's Journal **by [Daynas Valen](Skyrim_Daynas_Valen.md)** *Details Valen's efforts to hunt down the Gauldur Legend*
---
**18th of Morning Star, [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201)**

How long has it been since I first heard the [Gauldur](Skyrim_Gauldur.md) legend? Eighty years? A hundred? I can think of nothing else now. My goal is at hand.

After years of searching, I finally traced the [Ivory Claw](Skyrim_Forbidden_Legend.md) to a collector in [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md). His death was necessary. But I have it. At last, I have it.

**22nd of Morning Star, 4E 201**

Three days of nonstop riding. I reached [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) ahead of my pursuers, and took passage on the Icerunner, bound for [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) once more.

**29th of Morning Star, 4E 201**

The other passengers are becoming suspicious. Returning from dinner, I caught one brute rooting around in my quarters. He almost found the claw. Had the captain not intervened, I would have flayed him alive.

I hoped to spend the voyage sequestered in my room, reviewing my notes and preparing spells, but I need to deflect some attention from myself. I'll have to mingle, pretend to be just another petty mage. For now.

**2nd of Sun's Dawn, 4E 201**

The brute claims to be an adventurer, off to seek his fortune in the ruins of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) with his companions. Perhaps I can turn this to my advantage, use them as fodder for the traps and [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) within the tombs. I can see the greed in his eyes. Yes, that should work.

Three weeks to Solitude, the captain says. If I have to suffer these fools longer than that, I'll swim.

**27th of Sun's Dawn, 4E 201**

With the brute and his companions in tow, I led our band across the marsh to [Folgunthur](Skyrim_Folgunthur.md), where we made camp for the night. Little has changed since my first expedition ten years ago. But this time, I have the claw. And I will have the amulet.

I spent all night preparing a synopsis of my notes, in case some fragment of the tale or piece of ancient lore may be needed to bypass the wards within the tomb. Then I cast all my books and scrolls into the fire, and reduced my life's work to ashes. At long last, today is the day of reckoning. If I cannot have [the amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gauldur_Amulet), no one will.
