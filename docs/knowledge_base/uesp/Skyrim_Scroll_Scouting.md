## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) *or* Speak to [Septimus Signus](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus.md) at [his outpost](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.md)

## Detailed Walkthrough
After the completion of the quest [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet.md), you will be told by [Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) that all three Elder Scrolls must be obtained in order for him to read the whole prophecy and points you towards the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) to gather more information.

Gain access to the College by either passing [Faralda](Skyrim_Faralda.md)'s test or persuading her to let you through (requires a [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill of 100), and head to the [Arcanaeum](Skyrim_The_Arcanaeum.md). Here you will find [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md), who will fill you in on some of the Elder Scrolls lore and bring out two books for you: *[Effects of the Elder Scrolls](Skyrim_Effects_of_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)* and *[Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls](Skyrim_Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)*.

Reading the latter starts the quest [Discerning the Transmundane](Skyrim_Discerning_the_Transmundane.md); once you speak to Urag about [Septimus Signus](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus.md), this quest will be completed. Alternatively, you can skip the College and head directly to [Septimus Signus's Outpost](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.md) and talk to the man himself, which will also complete this quest.

If you have already obtained and subsequently sold the Elder Scroll to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) (only possible after completing [Alduin's Bane](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Bane.md)), you must buy back the Elder Scroll. He'll sell it back for 4,000 gold, but he can be persuaded to sell it for half price if you are the Arch-Mage or 3,000 gold if you aren't.

## Notes
- If you're doing Discerning the Transmundane as part of this quest and have Serana with you, you may want to avoid Alftand to get to Blackreach, as she will frequently raise nearby corpses, including the ones you'll want later, during the blood harvest portion of that quest.
