[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-flora-Creep_Cluster_2.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Creep_Cluster_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Creep_Cluster_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Creep_Cluster_2.jpg) Creep Cluster plant **Ash Creep Cluster** is an ingredient harvested from creep cluster plants unique to [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). The plant looks similar to the red-orange [creep cluster](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md) found in mainland [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but this type has a yellow-tan color and different alchemical properties.

Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 1 outside [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md), on a shelf in the southwestern tower.
- 1 in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md), on a shelf next to an alchemy table.

## Notes
- While not available from alchemists on the mainland, Ash Creep Cluster can sometimes be found for sale on Solstheim, namely alchemists and the general goods merchant [Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md).
- While the Ash Creep Cluster plant looks much different than the mainland version (being yellow instead of orange-red), the picked ingredient uses the same model as the standard Creep Cluster.
