## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to the innkeeper of [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
2. Talk to [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) at [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
You can ask [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) in [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) for any recent rumors. He may tell you that [Ralis](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) is looking to excavate [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

Travel to Kolbjorn Barrow, where you will find Ralis. After speaking with him you will begin the next quest, "[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)".
