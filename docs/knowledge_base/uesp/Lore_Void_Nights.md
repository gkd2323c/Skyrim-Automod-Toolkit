# Void Nights


Regardless of the truth of the matter, the Khajiit credited the Thalmor as their saviors. Within fifteen years, [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) influence in Elsweyr had so diminished that the Empire was unable to respond effectively to the coup of [4E 115](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_115) which dissolved the Elsweyr Confederacy and recreated the ancient kingdoms of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) and [Pelletine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pelletine) as client states of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGW-3)</sup>
