[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-item-Locket_of_Saint_Jiub.jpg/200px-SR-item-Locket_of_Saint_Jiub.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Locket_of_Saint_Jiub.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Locket_of_Saint_Jiub.jpg) Locket of Saint Jiub The Locket of Saint Jiub is given to you by [Jiub](Skyrim_Jiub.md) for completing the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md) quest [Impatience of a Saint](Skyrim_Impatience_of_a_Saint.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>. It is unusual in that it is one of only two amulets in the game to provide an [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) bonus, the other being the [Amulet of Articulation](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Articulation.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Impatience of a Saint](Skyrim_Impatience_of_a_Saint.md)**: Collect the ten missing pages of [Saint Jiub](Skyrim_Jiub.md)'s Opus.

## Notes
- The armor rating does improve with a higher [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill, and getting hit while wearing it will increase the Light Armor skill.

## Bugs
- The locket doesn't benefit from any Light Armor perks.
- Despite being flagged as Light Armor, the locket cannot be tempered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.21, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=11978))
