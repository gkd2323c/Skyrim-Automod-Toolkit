Vykosa the Ascendant was a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) huntress and [Werewolf Lord](Lore_Bestiary_W.md#Werewolf_Lord)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVykosa-1)</sup> who, according to a legend, was a first turned;<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ACFL-2)</sup> one of the original select few beings that were afflicted with [Lycanthropy](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) directly by [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSAYQ-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive3-4)</sup> She had the ability to control other werewolves, and assembled an enormous pack with plans to conquer all of Tamriel.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOAV-5)</sup> She kept giant [dire wolves](Lore_Dire_Wolf.md) as pets.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOAry Zel-6)</sup>

## History
Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Vykosa formed the [Moon Hunter Pack](Lore_Moon_Hunter_Pack.md) and took over Moon Hunter Keep, which was initially a fortress of the [Silver Dawn](Lore_Silver_Dawn.md), an organization dedicated to hunting down werewolves.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOAV-5)</sup> From there, she plotted to take over Tamriel, exact revenge on Hircine, and take down anyone that stood in her way.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-THCC-7)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Lt M-8)</sup> She gathered one of the largest werewolf packs known at the time, consisting of both willing and unwilling members. Many werewolves heard tales of her pack defeating the Silver Dawn, and flocked to Vykosa under the pretense that there was safety in numbers.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RJ-9)</sup> In their minds, it would be far more difficult for prospective hunters to kill them if they joined Vykosa's swelling ranks.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RJ-9)</sup> She ordered Balorgh to infiltrate the [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md). She intended to strike against Hircine's realm after taking over Tamriel.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-LTB-10)</sup>

Vykosa possessed the ability to bend the will of most werewolves, and enslaved many of her victims, forcing them into her ranks.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RJ-9)</sup> She was able to further empower her followers after enthralling the Silver Dawn's resident werewolf expert at the time, instructing him to research ways in which werewolves could be empowered or altered.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LN321UR-11)</sup> Vykosa found a way to transform werewolves into hulking monsters through the use of inhumane experiments.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LN321UR-11)</sup> When a werewolf behemoth transformed, they grew far larger and stronger than their unaltered kin, and would prove to be nigh unstoppable warriors should they survive the process.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LN321UR-11)</sup> Vykosa's plot was foiled in 2E 582 by the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md), who entered Moon Hunter Keep, destroyed her pack and killed her.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Online-12)</sup> Her severed head was kept as a trophy.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Online-12)</sup> Emblems of Vykosa, disks embossed with her symbol, relics of her notorious pack, could be found throughout the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) during the Interregnum.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-EOV-13)</sup>
