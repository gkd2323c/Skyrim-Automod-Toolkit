—Phrastus of Elinhir<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-1)</sup> Erokii Ruins, or simply **Erokii**, is an ancient ruin in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md), said to have once been the greatest Ayleid city in northwest Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-1)</sup> The great spire above Erokii, now known as the Doomcrag, has been likened to the [Adamantine Tower](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md) in construction, and it has been speculated that it may, at one point, have been built as one of [the Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-1)</sup> It housed the Light of Life, or Lattanya in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md), a relic which helped plants grow and healed illnesses. Pilgrimages to the summit of the Doomcrag would often occur daily. Following a bloody conflict between the Ayleid settlers and the original inhabitants of the area, the relic was tainted, and became Abagandra, the Lightless Remnant, a beacon of death.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

A number of local legends surround Erokii and the Doomcrag, and these can be found in the tomes [Dire Legends of the Doomcrag](Lore_Dire_Legends_of_the_Doomcrag.md) and [Tears of Anurraame](Lore_Tears_of_Anurraame.md).

Prince Anumaril of Erokii was returned as a [Wight](Lore_Wight.md) and was slain by [the Warrior](Lore_Hero.md#Warrior) ca. [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PAOE-3)</sup>
