[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-item-Skeleton_Key.jpg/200px-SR-item-Skeleton_Key.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Skeleton_Key.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Skeleton_Key.jpg) Skeleton Key The Skeleton Key is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) Artifact acquired after killing [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md). The Skeleton Key is an unbreakable lockpick, making it rather useful to enterprising thieves.

The Skeleton Key also has a secondary, less known function as being used to keep open Ebonmere, a well from which luck flows into the world. You are tasked with returning the key to Ebonmere in the [Twilight Sepulcher](Skyrim_Twilight_Sepulcher.md) to restore luck to the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md). Until then, you may retain the key indefinitely for your own use.

## Related Quests
- **[Blindsighted](Skyrim_Blindsighted.md)**: Stop [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md).
- **[Darkness Returns](Skyrim_Darkness_Returns.md)**: Walk the [Pilgrim's Path](Skyrim_Twilight_Sepulcher.md) and return the Skeleton Key to its rightful place.

## Notes
- Despite Mercer Frey's ability to use the Skeleton Key to unlock many non-standard locks such as Nordic puzzle doors, the player is restricted to only those locks that could be picked normally. Locks which require a key and other lock types that do not use the standard lockpicking minigame cannot be bypassed with the Skeleton Key.

## Bugs
- The [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) skill may not increase when using the Skeleton Key.
- The Skeleton Key may not work if you don't have any lockpicks in your inventory. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- Despite being a Daedric Artifact, the Skeleton Key does not count towards the [Oblivion Walker](Skyrim_Oblivion_Walker.md) achievement. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.2, fixes this bug.
