Hervic Fambure was a master [smith](Lore_Smithing.md) of [Betony](Lore_Betony.md). Little is known about them, except that they were a smith of some renown, but as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), they were already dead, and considered famous. Although little is known of their achievements, they were also known to craft vessels such as pitchers. A number of exquisite pitchers crafted by Hervic Fambure were found all around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the tumultuous period of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup> Hervic Fambure, similarly to many other smiths of that time,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CF-2)</sup> was known to stamp their products with their mark.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup>

## Notes
- As Hervic Fambure was noted to be master smith of Betony, and lived before 2E 582, they likelly lived in the island prior to the [Seamount Clan](Lore_Seamount_Clan.md)'s invasion of Betony and renaming of the isle to Betnikh. This invasion took place nine generations before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), after the reign of Emperor [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Bet-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONCorner Ant-4)</sup>
