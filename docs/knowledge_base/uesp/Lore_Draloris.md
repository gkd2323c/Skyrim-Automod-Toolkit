The terrain near it is made up of badlands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-1)</sup>

## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Dragon Glade was an active settlement. It was ruled by Baroness Vaynth and had a rivalry with [Velothis Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Velothis_Haven).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DG-4)</sup> Dragon Glade was neighbored by several settlements, including [Darnim Watch](Lore_Darnim_Watch.md) to the south, [Helnim Wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helnim_Wall) to the north, and [Greenheights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenheights) to the east.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
