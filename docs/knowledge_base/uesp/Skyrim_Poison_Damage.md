**Poison Damage** is a special type of damage that is only available in a few specific cases. In addition to the damage it does, poison heavily obscures your vision for a few seconds.

Normally, [poison resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md) only applies to damage done by poisoned weapons (even if it's a non-damaging poison). But there are a few sources of damage that are specifically "poison" and not any particular element. They are as follows:

- The [Ebony Mail](Skyrim_Ebony_Mail.md) artifact automatically applies a Poison Cloak effect, which damages all enemies within melee range, similar to the various Cloak spells in the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) school. (This spell can actually be cast as a regular spell if you give it to yourself via the console: `player.addspell f23c8`.)
- The ranged attacks of [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) and [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) do poison damage, as do the weapons of some [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md).
- [Dart traps](Skyrim_Dart_Trap.md) do poison damage to anyone they hit.
- [Poison blooms](Skyrim_Poison_Bloom.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> will release a cloud of poisonous gas if not harvested immediately. [Spiddal Stick](Skyrim_Spiddal_Stick.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> reacts to your approach the same way.
- The [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on adds a spell in the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) school called [Poison Rune](Skyrim_Poison_Rune.md), which does poison damage to all targets within range when it's set off.
- The [Mora's Agony](Skyrim_Mora%27s_Agony.md) power added by [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md), found in the [Black Book: The Hidden Twilight](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Hidden_Twilight_(quest).md), does poison damage. [Miraak's Staff](Skyrim_Miraak%27s_Staff.md) also produces this effect, and [Miraak's Robes](Skyrim_Miraak%27s_Robes.md) may cause a similar effect when the wearer is hit.
- [Poison Spiders](Skyrim_Poison_Spider.md#Poison_Spiders)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> inflict poison damage.

## Notes
- [Vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) and [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) are 100% immune to poison, as are [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) such as [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md), [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), [zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md).
- Additionally, [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) and [Redguards](Skyrim_Redguard.md) are 50% resistant to poison.
