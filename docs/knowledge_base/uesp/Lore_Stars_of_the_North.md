# Stars of the North

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg/200px-ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg) The Thief, associated with the Stars of the North The Stars of the North are a group of [stars](Lore_Stars.md). According to some, the connection between the [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) of [the Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md), a winter sign, and the Stars of the North was evident.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JODT-1)</sup>

One of the [Dwemeri](Lore_Dwemer.md) puzzles in [Nchuand-Zel](Lore_Nchuand-Zel.md) involved the constellations. Although Dionus Trutor linked the Thief to the north, because of its connection to the Stars of the North, it was [the Lord](Lore_The_Lord.md) that was associated with the cardinal direction of the north.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JODT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AFOS-2)</sup>
