Elenwen was the First Emissary of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

During the Great War she served as an interrogator, where she oversaw the interrogation of Ulfric Stormcloak following his capture. He was made to falsely believe that the information he gave up under torture aided the capture of the Imperial City, before being allowed to escape.

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), during the time of the [Skyrim Civil War](Lore_Skyrim_Civil_War.md), she oversaw the Thalmor Justiciars and the outlaw of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) worship within Skyrim in accordance to the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LO-3)</sup> She resided in the [Thalmor Embassy](Lore_Thalmor_Embassy.md), located in [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) hold.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup> She would often host lavish parties at the embassy, inviting Skyrim's most influential and powerful people. During one such party she unknowingly met the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DI-5)</sup> After the Dragonborn infiltrated and escaped from the embassy, Elenwen was reportedly furious.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SGD-6)</sup> Reports conflict on whether she attended a temporary truce between the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md) hosted by the [Greybeards](Lore_Greybeards.md) and whether she was asked to leave on [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s request.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SU-7)</sup>

## Notes
- In *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* there is a renegade Altmer mage named [Elenwen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Elenwen) found in Dun-Ahhe, and in *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)* there is a young Altmer woman also named [Elenwen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Elenwen) from [Silsailen](Lore_Silsailen.md) who seeks to oust the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md) from her town, but there has been no confirmation that either of them are the same individual as the [Elenwen from *Skyrim*](Skyrim_Elenwen.md).
