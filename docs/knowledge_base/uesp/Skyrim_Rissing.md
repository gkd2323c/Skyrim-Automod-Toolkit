[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-npc-Rissing.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rissing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rissing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rissing.jpg) Rissing with a drawn bow **Rissing**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

He is the first living hunter encountered after meeting Sinding.

Rissing wears a full set of [steel armor](Skyrim_Steel_Armor.md), excluding the helmet. In melee combat, he wields a leveled [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He also wields a [long bow](Skyrim_Long_Bow.md) for ranged combat, which he supplements with [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md). He carries a small sum of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md)**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md)'s Great Hunt.

## Quest-Related Events

### Ill Met By Moonlight
When spoken to, he will say:

- *"Hircine chose you to lead the Great Hunt. So lead."*

If you side with Sinding, he will be the first to see you and shout:

- *"What the... we've been betrayed! Look out!"*
