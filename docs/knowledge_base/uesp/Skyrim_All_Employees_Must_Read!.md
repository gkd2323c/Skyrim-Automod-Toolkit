All Employees Must Read! **by [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md)** *A memo to employees of the Black-Briar Meadery in reference to a prank*
---
Notice to all employees:

I've discovered my bed filled with [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) droppings that were obviously placed there on purpose by an employee who felt that it was an amusing prank. You have until [Middas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Middas) to either come forward on your own or point me in the direction of the perpetrator of this practical joke, otherwise all employees will be subject to half wages for the next three months.

[Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md)
