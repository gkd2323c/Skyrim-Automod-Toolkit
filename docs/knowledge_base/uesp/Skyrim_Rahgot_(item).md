[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Rahgot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Rahgot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Rahgot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Rahgot.jpg) Rahgot Rahgot is an orichalcum [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) obtained by killing the eponymous [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md) within the [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md), [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md). The artifact's enchantment increases your [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) by 70 points. It can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [orichalcum ingots](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ingot.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult.md)**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) and defeat [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md) to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](Skyrim_Captain_Valmir.md).

## Notes
- Placing Rahgot on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses it gained from tempering.
- The editor ID for the mask is "Armor Dragon Priest Mask **Orichalum** Helmet" instead of "Orichalcum".
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a [special holder](Skyrim_Special_Holder.md) for this item.
- Rahgot is one of two pieces of headwear in the game that has a Fortify Stamina enchantment; the other (at also ten point less in strength) is [Visage of Mzund](Skyrim_Visage_of_Mzund.md). Rahgot's enchantment is equal in strength to an item of Peerless Stamina.
