Torug at the Summit, Complete Translation *A translated text surrounding the death and resting place of Orsinium's founder*
---
*Note: The original version of this text is called [Torug ag Krazak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Torug_ag_Krazak).*

---
Chief [Torug](Lore_Torug.md) will not allow the looters to find his remains.
Chief Torug will allow no lesser [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) to find interment above him.

* * *

Chief Torug gathered up his [armored bracer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Armlet_of_Torug) and his dying body.
He climbed to [the summit](Lore_Sorrow.md) and placed himself in a cairn of his own making.

* * *

Then he laid Sorrow's Kiss upon his enchanted armlet.
Forever waiting for a worthy Orc to claim his most cherished possession.
