[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/AR-place-Oaktown.jpg/200px-AR-place-Oaktown.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Oaktown.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Oaktown.jpg) Oaktown during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Third_Era_Wars.md#Imperial_Simulacrum) Oaktown (also called **Andrethis**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-1)</sup><sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Name)</sup> is a settlement near the southwestern border of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> in the [western mountain](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) region, where it is an occupied territory of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Oaktown was an active settlement. It was ruled by Duke Nisik and had a rivalry with [Eagle Moor](Lore_Eagle_Moor.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Oaktown was neighbored by several settlements, such as [Heimlyn Keep](Lore_Heimlyn_Keep.md) to the east, [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md) to the northwest, and [Riverbridge](Lore_Riverbridge.md) to the north.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>
