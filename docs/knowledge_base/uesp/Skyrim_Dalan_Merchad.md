[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Dalan_Merchad.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dalan_Merchad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dalan_Merchad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dalan_Merchad.jpg) Dalan Merchad **Dalan Merchad** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who works at the [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) docks. He serves as a sailor aboard the ship [The North Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_North_Wind), under the ship's captain, [Kjar](Skyrim_Kjar.md).

He despises Windhelm and with the ship permanently docked, Dalan has no choice but to wander around on deck all day and night, without getting any sleep, at times pausing to sit down and eat.

Dalan wears merchant [clothing](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223C) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d). He wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) in combat, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached:

- *"Never much cared for Windhelm. The place is depressing."*
- *"I'm a lot happier out here on the deck than I am wandering around in that ruin of a city."*
- *"I heard those [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) out west live in caves. Can't be any worse than trying to live in Windhelm."*
- *"Can't wait until we raise anchor. Anytime the boat's not moving, I start to get impatient."*
