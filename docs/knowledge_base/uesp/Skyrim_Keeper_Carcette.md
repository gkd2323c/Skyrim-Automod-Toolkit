[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-npc-Keeper_Carcette.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Keeper_Carcette.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Keeper_Carcette.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Keeper_Carcette.jpg) Keeper Carcette in the Hall of the Vigilant **Keeper Carcette**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md), is the Keeper of the Vigil in Skyrim, the leader of the [Vigilants of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) in the province. She is found in the [Hall of the Vigilant](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md) where, according to her followers, she dispenses healing or justice as necessary. She spends her day studying the items on the table in front of the Shrine of Stendarr and resting each night between 12am and 8am.

Carcette wears [apprentice robes of Restoration](Skyrim_Apprentice_Robes_of_Restoration.md) along with [steel plate gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets), [steel plate boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots), and an [Amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md). She carries a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a leveled enchanted warhammer, a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), a [potion of cure disease](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease), and three leveled potions of healing.

With the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, the Hall of the Vigilant will be destroyed by [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) once you reach level 10 or have completed the quest [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md). According to [Vigilant Tolan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilant_Tolan), Keeper Carcette was killed in the attack, although her body is not present at the ruins of the Hall.

## Dialogue

- *"I am Keeper of the Vigil here in Skyrim. We bring Stendarr's Mercy to the innocent and His Justice to the Daedra."*

- **I'd like training in Restoration magic.**
- *"I'll gladly share my knowledge with you."*

## Notes
- She can sometimes be found carrying a [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) weapon, despite it going against everything she and the other Vigilants of Stendarr say and do in the game regarding Daedric objects. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
