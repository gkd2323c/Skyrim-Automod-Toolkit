Ragmaer, Nord woodcutter **by [Ragmaer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ragmaer)** *A woodcutter's thoughts on the Alliance War*
---
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-Nord.png)
- "What do I think of an alliance with the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) and [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md)? Times are bad—for us and them. The [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) invasion ten years back hurt us all, there's the troubles in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) now, and we've got enemies sprouting up left and right. The [Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md)'s on our borders and up to who-knows-what with the damn [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), well, no true-blooded [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) can stand idle with [High Elves](Lore_Altmer.md) on the march.

- It'll be a fight to protect [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), that's for sure. Would it be better to stand by ourselves? I know some think so. I'm no coward, but I'm not stupid, either. The [Skald-King](Lore_Jorunn.md) is right; we need allies, even if I can't exactly call all of 'em 'friends.'"
