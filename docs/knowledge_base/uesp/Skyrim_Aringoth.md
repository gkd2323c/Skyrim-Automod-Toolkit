[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Aringoth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aringoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aringoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aringoth.jpg) Aringoth in the Goldenglow Estate **Aringoth** is the [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) owner of [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md), a bee farm outside of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) which used to supply [honey](Skyrim_Honey.md) to the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md). The [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) made sure the operations ran smoothly and would extort considerable payment from Aringoth. Lately, he has stopped shipments, fortified the island and hired an army of mercenaries, infuriating [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) and causing the Guild to single him out for punishment.

Befitting his status, Aringoth wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086991) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086993). He is armed with a leveled dagger, leveled bow, and up to 144 arrows of various qualities up to daedric, all limited by your level. He also carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Cellar_Key) to his cellar, a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Safe_Key) to his safe and up to 45 gold coins.

## Quest-Related Events

### Loud and Clear
Once you've broken into the estate, Aringoth will be found crouching in his room on the second floor. As soon as you come close he'll spit:

- *"Worthless mercenaries. I didn't think Maven or [Mercer](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md) would allow me to get away with this, but I had little choice."*
- **I just want the key to your safe.**
- *"I can't. If I do, I may as well cut my own throat."*

- - **What makes you think I won't? (Persuade)**
- *"Fine. Take it! Once the new owner finds out I gave in, I'm as good as dead anyway."* (Success)
- *"I don't believe you. That's not your way."* (Fail)

- - - - **New owner? What are you talking about?**
- *"I've already said too much. I gave you what you came here for, now go. Leave me in peace."*

- - **Then I'll take it off your corpse!**
- *"Give it your best shot!"*

- - **Forget it. I'll just open it myself.**
- *"No! I'm not about to let you ruin everything I've worked so hard for!"*

If spoken to again if you got the key from him peacefully:

- *"Go, leave me in peace."*

## Notes
- If left alive, he will remain crouched in the corner for the rest of the game.
