# House Branquette

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/ON-npc-King_Ranser_03.jpg/200px-ON-npc-King_Ranser_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Ranser_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Ranser_03.jpg) King Ranser, last monarch from House Branquette House Branquette was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble house and royal dynasty from the region of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).

The earliest known historical reference to House Branquette is found in a potentially forged [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) decree that named the house as their Breton Royal Delegates in Rivenspire. In [2E 522](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_522), [Hurlburt](Lore_Hurlburt.md), the twenty-first count of House Branquette, became the King of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md). Hurlburt was considered by some as the greatest post- [Reman](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) monarch of Shornhelm. He wed Countess [Iphilia](Lore_Iphilia_Montclair.md) of [House Montclair](Lore_House_Montclair.md), with whom he fathered a son named [Phylgeon](Lore_Phylgeon_Montclair.md) in [2E 532](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_532). He also fathered an illegitimate elder son, [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md), with a [House Tamrith](Lore_House_Tamrith.md) cousin. In [2E 546](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_546), Hurlburt died, instigating a succession dispute between Phylgeon and Ranser over the throne of Shornhelm. Although Ranser was illegitimate, Houses Branquette and Tamrith supported him over the legitimate son, Phylgeon. Ultimately, Ranser narrowly won the throne of Shornhelm by presenting to the Council of the North the long-lost Direnni decree that named House Branquette as their Breton Royal Delegates in Rivenspire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SCCOTH-1)</sup>

After becoming King of Shornhelm, Ranser fathered a daughter named [Rayelle](Lore_Rayelle.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOAM6-2)</sup> After [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) of Cumberland became the King of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) in [2E 563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563), he began courting Rayelle. However, before the marriage contracts were worked out, Emeric chose instead to marry Princess [Maraya](Lore_Maraya.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), infuriating Ranser and leading to the start of [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) in [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOAM6-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFOKR-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTTDC-4)</sup> Ranser's murder at the Battle of Traitor's Tor in [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567) ended Ranser's War and the line of House Branquette. After Ranser's death, much of House Branquette's domain was parceled out to Houses Tamrith and [Dorell](Lore_House_Dorell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SCCOTH-1)</sup>
