[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/OB-place-Gottlefont_Priory.jpg/200px-OB-place-Gottlefont_Priory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Gottlefont_Priory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Gottlefont_Priory.jpg) Gottlesfont Priory circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/ON-place-Gottlesfont_Priory.jpg/200px-ON-place-Gottlesfont_Priory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Gottlesfont_Priory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Gottlesfont_Priory.jpg) Gottlesfont Priory circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Gottlesfont Priory (or **Gottlefont Priory**) is a monastic settlement located off the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md) in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

The priory has strong ties to academia. [Haderus of Gottlesfont](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Haderus_of_Gottlesfont) was a famed author,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> and scholar [Alexandre Hetrard](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Alexandre_Hetrard) stayed at Gottlesfont during his visit to the nearby [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of [Ceyatatar](Lore_Ceyatatar.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## History
Before the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Gottlesfont was home to a great chapel dedicated to the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md). However, bands of looting soldiers killed the abbot and priests and stole the priory's relics, and the few remaining inhabitants were forced to close the priory temporarily.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the priory was only a shadow of its former self. It had a small chapel dedicated to the [Nine Divines](Lore_Nine_Divines.md), and the Sisters of Gottlesfont were responsible for harvesting [Steel Blue Entoloma](Lore_Steel_Blue_Entoloma.md) and [Lady's Mantle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lady%27s_Mantle) for charity.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
