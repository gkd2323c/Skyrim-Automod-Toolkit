## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read [Verrick's Note](Skyrim_Verrick%27s_Note.md). The note is located in the green room inside the Guardian Vault, on a table to the left of the torture devices.
2. Go to [Faldar's Tooth](Skyrim_Faldar%27s_Tooth.md), and find a note on one of the bodies after the fight there.
3. Read [Cage Bandit's Note](Skyrim_Cage_Bandit%27s_Note.md).
4. Go to [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md), defeat the denizens there and find the sword [Bloodthirst](Skyrim_Bloodthirst.md) among the loot there.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Halfway through [The Guardian Vault](Skyrim_The_Guardian_Vault.md) during the [Dawnfang/Duskfang Quest](Skyrim_A_Soul_Divided.md), in the green room on a table to the left of the torture devices, there is a note with a dagger through it. On reading [Verrick's Note](Skyrim_Verrick%27s_Note.md), a miscellaneous side quest is added, 'Investigate Faldar's Tooth.' Leave the Ratway and travel there.

Arriving at [Faldar's Tooth](Skyrim_Faldar%27s_Tooth.md), there is a bandit labeled 'Bouncer' and 2 pit wolves outside. As you enter, there is another bandit labeled 'Bouncer' immediately inside. Straight ahead through the narrow passageway, turn right into the main room. You come out on a wooden deck, with barrels and sacks to your left, and stairs down to your right. The room has a cage in the middle, with 2 fighting wolves inside. There are 2 gamblers and a barkeep who will all become hostile to you as you enter. As the fighting starts, a levelled bandit labeled 'the butcher' will come out from the back room. This enemy has strong levelled gear, so be careful. Along the wall, there is a gambler cage, which has inside gold, many coin purses, skooma, a healing potion, a levelled chest, and the [Cage Bandit's Note](Skyrim_Cage_Bandit%27s_Note.md), giving you a new miscellaneous objective to 'Retrieve [Bloodthirst](Skyrim_Bloodthirst.md) from [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md)'.

As you get to [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md), you will enter into a small corridor, with a staircase leading down into a large room. There are many raised platforms with empty crypts along the walls, and a cafe and chains hanging from the ceiling. As you walk down, there is a [Vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) sitting in the room who will attack. If you have [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, this may be a [Volkihar Vampire](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md). There are 3 [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) to the left of the room who will join the attack. Once you deal with the enemies, you can continue forward through the room. There is an adept-locked chest in the southeast corner.

From here, the western raised platform has a doorway leading to a narrow walkway. You come out into a nicer looking small room with a high levelled Vampire. The room has an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) to the right, a [dead bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md) and a cage in front of you, a large table with platters along the wall to your left, and a small table with 3 chairs in the middle. As you walk in you'll see 2 beds to the side, an [enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) in an alcove next to the cage, and a double bed with a bookshelf to the right of it and a boss chest on its left. This chest contains [Bloodthirst](Skyrim_Bloodthirst.md) and some levelled loot. As you collect Bloodthirst the objective will complete, and you're free to leave back the way you came in.
