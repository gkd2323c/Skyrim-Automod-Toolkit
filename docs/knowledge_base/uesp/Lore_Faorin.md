## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/ON-npc-Medone.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Medone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Medone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Medone.jpg) Medone ca. 2E 582 Little is known of the youth of Faorin. At some point, before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) he became the chieftain of Hillhunters and fathered a daughter, Medone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Faorin-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Medone-3)</sup> During that time, as of [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568), the Hillhunters were counted among the most prominent clans of the Reach.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) descended onto the Reach to search for an ancient artifact of great power. Many of the Reachfolk clans moved to [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) at the behest of Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md). The Hillhunter Clan, however, traveled to [Briar Rock](Lore_Briar_Rock_Ruins.md) instead. Led by Faorin, they hoped to parley and seek refuge with the [Thornroot Clan](Lore_Thornroot_Clan.md). Some members of the clan questioned his decision to travel to Briar Rock, but they ultimately complied.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Bedene-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Faorin-1)</sup> The Thornroots turned against the Hillunters, and refused to parley. The Hillhunters were instead imprisoned and made unwilling subjects of the [briarheart ritual](Lore_Briarheart.md). The Thornroot hagravens sought to create an army of briarheart berserkers rather than provide protection for all clans.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Medone-3)</sup>

His daughter, Medone, managed to escape the Thornroots.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAD-5)</sup> She enlisted the help of a [traveler](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to liberate her clan and father, as well as burn the briarheart tree in Dead Crone Rock. The traveler fought their way through Briar Rock and burned down the tree and freed the members of the Hillhunter Clan, including Chieftain Faorin, who was imprisoned in the tower,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Marolan-6)</sup> as Hagravens decided to save him for a special ritual related to the creation of the new Briarheart Tree.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Faorin-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAD-5)</sup> They later moved on to parlay with the [Wayward Guardians](Lore_Wayward_Guardians.md), led by [Arana](Lore_Arana.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONBetrayal-7)</sup>

After Faorin and the clan were liberated from the Thornroots, they found shelter in the mountains. Once the threat of the Gray Host ended in the Reach, they relocated to the lowlands. Chieftain Faorin decided that Medone was ready to lead the clan and continued to prepare her for that role.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Faorin-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Medone-3)</sup>

Together with his daughter, he attended the ceremony announcing the peace agreement between the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and Reachmen.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SC-8)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Faorin-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Medone-3)</sup>
