# Hee-Tepsleel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-crown_store-Hermit_Crab.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Hermit_Crab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Hermit_Crab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Hermit_Crab.jpg) Hermit Crab [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/ON-place-Murkmire.jpg/200px-ON-place-Murkmire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murkmire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murkmire.jpg) Murkmire The Hee-Tepsleel are one of the many [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tribes of the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), they inhabited [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), a coastal region in southern parts of the province. They are noted for their agriculture, growing and trading alchemical ingredients with tribes like the [Black-Tongue Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black-Tongue_Tribe).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOM-1)</sup>

In his work *[Tribes of Murkmire: Tribal Connections](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tribes_of_Murkmire__Tribal_Connections)*, [Emmanubeth Hurrent](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Emmanubeth_Hurrent) of the Wayfarers' Society of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) provided a description of the Hee-Tepsleel tribe, showcasing their inter-tribal relations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOM-1)</sup>

Hee-Tepsleel consider [hermit crabs](Lore_Hermit_Crab.md) a delicacy. Xoko, an Argonian chef from the Hee-Tepsleel tribe, described their meat as "as juicy as the fruit about to drop off a tree."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HC-2)</sup>
