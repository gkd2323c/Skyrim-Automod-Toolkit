[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/AR-place-North_Hall.jpg/200px-AR-place-North_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-North_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-North_Hall.jpg) Helland during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Helland (or **North Hall**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> is a town found in south-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), found in between the city-states of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md), [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), and [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup>

Helland has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of North Hall was an active settlement was ruled by Baroness Krolunki and had a rivalry with [Riverpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverpoint).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Helland is neighbored by several settlements, including [Cartmont](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cartmont) to the west, Vulkneu Town to the north, Stonedale to the east, and [Stonemoor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stonemoor) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> It was still in existence during the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-4)</sup>
