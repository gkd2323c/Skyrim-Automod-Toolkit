High Priestess Solgra was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) priestess and the head of the Priests of Trinimac in [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md)'s Temple of Ire, from the Mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).

Solgra was a [City-Orc](Lore_City-Orcs.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCAlga-1)</sup> who studied at the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) for many years before traveling across Southern Tamriel. She eventually ended up in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), where she first heard the words of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md), a god tied to Orcish history and venerated by the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of the isles. There, she discovered her true calling. Solgra believed she was destined to restore the Orsimer to their past, reviving ancient traditions, and embracing the ideals of truth, honor, unity, and the worship of Trinimac.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSolgra-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she was sent to Orsinium on the request of Forge-Mother Alga to help revive the ancient traditions of truth, honor, and unity. There she met the [Vestige](Lore_Vestige.md), who was also there on the behest of the forge-mother.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ITO-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSolgra-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCAlga-1)</sup> Later, as the Vestige returned from defeating the [Winterborn](Lore_Winterborn.md), Solgra was conversing with [Bazrag gro-Fharun](Lore_Bazrag_gro-Fharun.md), concerning the religions of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md) and [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md), with her meaning no disrespect to Bazrag.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FKAG-4)</sup>

Solgra was attacked by a member of the Malacath Fanatics, Mazogug the Bold. After the Vestige defeated Mazogug and the Malacath Fanatics, Solgra mentioned that Mazogug claimed Bazrag gave the order to attack her, in reality this was a plot by Kurog, and his mother Alga, to frame Bazrag and discredit the worship of Malacath. <sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FKAG-4)</sup> Solgra received news that one of the clan chiefs was taken captive by Trinimac extremists known as the [Vosh Rakh](Lore_Vosh_Rakh.md). She asked the Vestige to go to [Fharun Stronghold](Lore_Fharun_Stronghold.md) and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) adventuress [Eveli Sharp-Arrow](Lore_Eveli_Sharp-Arrow.md) to rescue the chieftain.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSAC-5)</sup>

Solgra tasked the Vestige, Eveli, the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) noble Talviah Aliaria, and an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) adventurer named, Flies-In-Wind, in infiltrating the Vosh Rakh to gain information about their plans.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAOAK-6)</sup>

Conflicting reports state that Solgra was either assassinated by the Vosh Rakh or survived. If Solgra was killed, her body was laid in state at Kurog's funeral. If Solgra survived the encounter with the Vosh Rakh, thanks to Eveli blocking an arrow from reaching her, she would watch over a wounded Eveli and attend Kurog's funeral.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TAOAK2-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TKG-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BOAKH-9)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BOAKH-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LLTK-10)</sup>
