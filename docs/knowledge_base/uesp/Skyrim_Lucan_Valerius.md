[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-npc-Lucan_Valerius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lucan_Valerius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucan_Valerius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucan_Valerius.jpg) Lucan Valerius at the counter of his shop, Riverwood Trader **Lucan Valerius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [pawnbroker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker), and the owner of the [Riverwood Trader](Skyrim_Riverwood_Trader.md) in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md). He lives upstairs with his sister [Camilla Valerius](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md), who runs the store with him. The store was robbed by [Arvel the Swift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift), who stole an antique [golden claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw) and retreated to [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md). Lucan will task you with retrieving it.

He and his sister came to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) some time after the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md). He disagrees with her ideas about how to run the store, and wants her to choose which one of her local love interests to marry<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Camilla_Valerius-2)</sup>. He wears a set of lower class merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). He carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Riverwood_Trader) to Riverwood Trader as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common), and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Golden Claw
When you enter the Riverwood Trader for the first time, you can witness an argument between Lucan and Camilla regarding the stolen golden claw:

**Camilla**: *"Well one of us has to do something!"*
**Lucan**: *"I said no! No adventures, no theatrics, no thief-chasing!"*
**Camilla**: *"Well what are you going to do then, huh? Let's hear it!"*
**Lucan**: *"We are done talking about this. Oh, a customer. Sorry you had to hear that."*

His greetings before talking to him about it:

*"I better get back to cleaning the store. What a mess."* *"Damn thieves. As if things weren't bad enough."* *"I don't know what you overheard, but the Riverwood Trader is still open. Feel free to shop."* If you ask him about the argument:

**Did something happen?** *"Yes, we did have a bit of a... break-in. But we still have plenty to sell. Robbers were only after one thing. An ornament, solid gold. In the shape of a dragon's claw."* **I could help you get the claw back.** *"You could? I've got some coin coming in from my last shipment. It's yours if you bring my claw back. If you're going after those thieves, you should head to Bleak Falls Barrow, northeast of town."* Camilla will then question Lucan's plan of sending you to reacquire the claw:

**Camilla**: *"So this is your plan, Lucan?"*
**Lucan**: *"Yes. So now you don't have to go, do you?"*
**Camilla**: *"Oh really? Well I think your new helper here needs a guide."*
**Lucan**: *"Wh- no... I.... Oh, by the Eight, fine. But only to the edge of town!"*

His greetings while the quest is ongoing:

*"Don't let my sister do anything foolish."* *"The sooner you find the claw, the sooner our lives can get back to normal."* *"Show those thieves not to steal from Lucan Valerius!"* *"I'll need to lock up the store. Thank you so much for agreeing to help us."* *"I should lock up the store now. Thank you again."* When you return with the claw:

**I found the golden claw.** *"You found it? Ha ha ha. There it is. Strange... it seems smaller than I remember. Funny thing, huh? I'm going to put this back where it belongs. I'll never forget this. You've done a great thing for me and my sister."* If Camilla is dead and he has a low relationship with you:

*"Thanks. I guess this was the least you could do after what happened to Camilla."* If Camilla is dead:

*"Thanks. I only wish Camilla was here to see it. Here, take this. You've done a good thing for the Riverwood Trader."*

## Notes
- If Lucan dies, Camilla will take over the store. If they both die, it will be abandoned completely.
- Contrary to what Lucan says, Bleak Falls Barrow is west of Riverwood.
- If you place items in the cupboard behind Lucan's counter, they will show up in his inventory. Purchasing stolen items you place in there will remove their stolen tags. Don't put torches in there, though, as they won't add to what Lucan has for sale.

## Bugs
- If you invest 500 gold into his shop (using the [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md) perk), it adds 10,000 gold instead of 500 gold to his inventory. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.9, fixes this bug.
