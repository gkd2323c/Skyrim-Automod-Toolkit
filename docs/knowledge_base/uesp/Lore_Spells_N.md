## Night Eye
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Nighteye.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Nighteye.png) Night Eye (Oblivion) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-effect-Night_Eye.jpg) **Night Eye** (also stylized as **Night-Eye**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBNight-1)</sup> is an illusion spell that grants the wielder the ability to see in the dark. The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have the innate ability to use night eye,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESKhajiit-2)</sup> as well as [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TESVampire-3)</sup> Those that cannot naturally use it or use any kind of spells can attain night eye via [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md). Some ingredients, when mixed to create a potion of night eye include a [bear's pelt](Lore_Bear_Pelt.md), [daedroth teeth](Lore_Daedroth_Teeth.md), a [kagouti's hide](Lore_Kagouti_Hide.md) and [Viper's Bugloss leaves](Lore_Viper%27s_Bugloss_Leaves.md).

When the ruined forts of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) were previously active, an alchemist, who was also a practicing winemaker developed a special, magical [wine](Lore_Wine.md) called [Shadowbanish Wine](Lore_Shadowbanish_Wine.md), which granted the consumer night eye. It was ideally used during lookouts on cold nights to warm up.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBNerussa-4)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Night_Eye), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Night-Eye), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md)

**Spell schools:** [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md)
