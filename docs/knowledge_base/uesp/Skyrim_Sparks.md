*Lightning that does 8 points of shock damage to Health and Magicka per second.* **Sparks** is a novice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [shock damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) to Health and Magicka.

## Effects
- [Sparks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), 8 pts for 1 sec
- [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md), 200 pts for 1 sec; if the [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Shock), raises damage to 10/s at first rank or 12/s at second rank

## Notes
- Like most Lightning spells, it can be used to set off [Lightning Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Rune) from a safe distance.
- Has greater range than the other novice level Destruction spells, [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md) and [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md), and also being instant-effect it's harder to dodge, though the higher casting cost may be a problem for beginning mages.
- It is useful for its ability to disintegrate wounded enemies at a low magicka cost once the disintegration perk has been unlocked.
