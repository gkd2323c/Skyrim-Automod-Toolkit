[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-place-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg) Ancient's Ascent **Ancient's Ascent** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) southwest of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). Its [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) teaches part of the [Animal Allegiance](Skyrim_Animal_Allegiance.md) [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Bounty: Dragon](Skyrim_Bounty__Dragon.md)**: Kill a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) in its [lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Dragon Seekers](Skyrim_Dragon_Seekers.md)**: Go to a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) with [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) or [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) and kill the dragon. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg/200px-SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-word_wall-Ancient%27s_Ascent.jpg) Word wall The route to the word wall is through [Bonechill Passage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonechill_Passage), a short cave. The word wall can also be reached by using a [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) to climb the mountains around the area or on foot by approaching from [Bloodlet Throne](Skyrim_Bloodlet_Throne.md). There are three [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) plants to the right of the path after exiting Bonechill Passage. There are a couple wide flights of stairs leading up to a plateau in front of the word wall, with lit braziers at the bottom and top of each flight.

Before the [dragons' return](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), the word wall will be guarded by a few leveled enemies.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md), which will attack as soon as you approach from the cave.

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has been completed, the boss chest is by the base of the platform holding the word wall.

## Notes
- Ancient's Ascent is one of the locations suggested by [Marise Aravel](Skyrim_Marise_Aravel.md) as a place to find [ice wraith teeth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth) for her [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Cold).
- [Climbing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing) the rocks behind the wall provides a shortcut to [Bloodlet Throne](Skyrim_Bloodlet_Throne.md).
