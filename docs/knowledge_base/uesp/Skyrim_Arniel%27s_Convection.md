**Arniel's Convection** is a destruction spell that deals [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) and sets targets on fire. It is not intended to be used in combat, but is instead needed during the quest [Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md) to heat Dwarven Convectors.

## Effects
- [Flames](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 1 pt for 3 secs

## Notes
- While Arniel's Convection is not affected by the Novice Destruction perk (since its spell object doesn't have an assigned casting perk), it is possible to reduce the casting cost to 0 by raising your destruction skill.
- Like most fire spells, it can be used to set off [fire runes](Skyrim_Fire_Rune.md) or [oil slicks](Skyrim_Traps.md#Oil_Slicks.2FOil_Lanterns) from a safe distance (though [firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) or [fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md) may be preferable due to their greater range).
