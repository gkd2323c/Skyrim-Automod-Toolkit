Cassynder Septim (sometimes enumerated as **Cassynder I**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-1)</sup> was the fourteenth [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for two years, from [3E 200](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_200) to [3E 202](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_202). He was preceded by his mother, [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md), and was succeeded by his younger brother, [Uriel IV](Lore_Uriel_IV.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup>

## Biography
Cassynder Septim was born around the year [3E 150](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_150) and was the only child of Emperor [Pelagius III](Lore_Pelagius_III.md) and Empress Regent [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MOP-3)</sup> Katariah became pregnant with Cassynder after she visited Pelagius in his cell in the asylum where he had been committed due to his madness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MOP-3)</sup> Cassynder had a younger half-brother, [Uriel Lariat](Lore_Uriel_IV.md). At some point, Cassynder became King of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md). However, he was in poor health by [3E 193](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_193), so he abdicated in favor of Uriel, whom he had legally adopted.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup>

In [3E 200](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_200), Katariah was killed in a skirmish in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), so Cassynder was crowned Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), but he was still in poor health.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MOP-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTET-4)</sup> Because Cassynder was the son of a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) and a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), he was half-elven but aged like a Breton, so he was already middle-aged when he acceded to the throne.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup> Nevertheless, as the only true blood relation of Pelagius III, and thus [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), he was obligated to accept the Ruby Throne.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup> The [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) assumed much responsibility during Cassynder's reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-1)</sup> Cassynder's reign was short-lived, as he died only two years after acceding to the throne in [3E 202](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_202).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTET-4)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Death)</sup> Cassynder had no children, so Uriel was crowned Emperor Uriel Septim IV.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-2)</sup>

By the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), a haunted fort near the town of [Rivercrest](Lore_Rivercrest.md) bore his name.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Jt FC-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BWd-6)</sup>

## Notes
- | [^](#note_Death) | *[Brief History of the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brief_History_of_the_Empire)* makes two contradictory statements regarding Cassynder's year of death. First, it says that "in two years, [after his reign began] he was dead"; In the next volume, however, it contradicts itself by saying that Cassynder died "three years after [his accession]". It is likely that the second statement is incorrect, as *[The Third Era Timeline](Lore_The_Third_Era_Timeline.md)* lists Cassynder's death as occurring in 3E 202, which is two years after he became emperor. |
