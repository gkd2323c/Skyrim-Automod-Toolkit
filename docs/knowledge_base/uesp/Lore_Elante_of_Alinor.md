Elante of Alinor was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) archaeologist from [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She was a scholar of [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) shrines, and authored a set of [research notes](Lore_Elante%27s_Notes.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EN-1)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Elante and her colleague Badama Andarys uncovered a Daedric ruin in a cave found along [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)'s northern coastline in the [Sheogorad](Lore_Sheogorad.md) region. This turned out to be the fabled Tomb of [Mordrin Hanin](Lore_Mordrin_Hanin.md). The expedition ended in disaster when their summonings failed to conjure anything more than [scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) to aid their excavation and the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) looted the cave and tomb of treasure, killing Badama in the process.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EN-1)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Elante's research pointed her towards a cave along [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md), which likely contained a Daedric shrine. She hired a new recruit from the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) to help her navigate the cave and slay the many hostile Daedra who inhabited it. Elante discovered a lost shrine to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) deep inside the cave, which helped to further the understanding of Daedra.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oblivion-3)</sup>
