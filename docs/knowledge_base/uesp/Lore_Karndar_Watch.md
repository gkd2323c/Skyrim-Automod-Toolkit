[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-place-Karndar_Watch.jpg/200px-ON-place-Karndar_Watch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Karndar_Watch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Karndar_Watch.jpg) Karndar Watch circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Karndar Watch (or **Kardnar Watch**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> is an ancient village located in the Diren Valley region of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), within the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Karndar Watch is a small but historic settlement, as it is the origin of the Laws of Karndar Watch, created in [ME 1180](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_1180). The laws are ancient and very comparable to the ones established by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) in the [northeast](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

By the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Karndar Watch had become a derelict ruined settlement. Though one of the main roads on the island led up to it, it received little contact from the populace outside of worshipers who'd set up shrines at the ruins and the occasional traveling merchant.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAnglalas-4)</sup> The grounds were overrun by [imps](Lore_Imp.md) and an [indrik](Lore_Indrik.md) inhabited the area.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> The settlement was still marked on maps during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), but it is unknown if it was re-inhabited by that time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup>

By the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Karndar Watch was once again an active settlement, being ruled by Lady Zenosara. It had a rivalry with [Silsailen Point](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silsailen_Point).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, one of them being [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) in the west, and was located across the sea from [Belport Run](Lore_Belport_Run.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Map Arena-6)</sup>
