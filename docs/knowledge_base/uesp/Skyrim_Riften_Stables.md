[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-place-Riften Stables.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riften Stables.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riften Stables.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riften Stables.jpg) Riften Stables **Riften Stables** is a [stable](Skyrim_Stables.md) outside the northern entrance to [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). It is owned and run by [Hofgrir Horse-Crusher](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md) with the help of his assistant [Shadr](Skyrim_Shadr.md).

A dappled grey [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) can be bought for 1000 gold.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The stables are just to the right of the main gate into [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), with space for two [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md) in separate stalls. There is an empty cart and three barrels, two of which contain [food](Skyrim_Food.md) and the third is a fish barrel that can contain raw [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat) fillets, various small [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) fish, [clam meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat) and [salt piles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile). To the right of the stables, among some trees is where a [Khajiit Caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) lead by [Ahkari](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ahkari) camp when they are at Riften. There is also a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) plant among the same trees and behind the stables towards the rear exit for [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) is a [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root) plant near some rocks. There is also a [carriage](Skyrim_Carriage.md) in front of the stables driven by [Sigaar](Skyrim_Sigaar.md), who is willing to take you to any of the main cities and towns.

### Riften Stables
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-interior-Riften_Stables.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Riften_Stables.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riften_Stables.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riften_Stables.jpg) Hofgrir's living quarters Entering the stables takes you into the main living room, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) against the opposite wall and a wall shelf above holding clutter. To the right of the cooking pot is a set of shelves holding a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), two [boiled creme treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), two [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), several pieces of cheese, a [green](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple) and two [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple). There is a food barrel in the western corner. Against the northwest wall is a dresser with a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) on top and a green apple on the floor in front. In the northern corner is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a loaf of bread and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) on the table and hanging from the ceiling above are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md).

There are a pair of double doors to the southeast that open into a bedroom consisting of two single owned beds in the northern and eastern corners, with a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) under the eastern one and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot, at the foot of the northern one. There is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and a barrel with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) on top against the southeast wall. On the southwest wall is a wall shelf holding a bottle of wine and copies of *[Alduin is Real](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alduin_is_Real)*, *[An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_An_Explorer%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*, *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir)*, *[Cats of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cats_of_Skyrim)*, *[Watcher of Stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Watcher_of_Stones)*, *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun)*, *[Horker Attacks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks)* and *[A Dream of Sovngarde](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dream_of_Sovngarde)*.

## Notes
- Northeast of the stables, the ruins of a small, round tower can found nestled among the rocky ledges of a large hill. Within the fallen walls sits a [satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel) and a [Shrine of Zenithar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar) atop a stone table, next to a long-dead skeleton.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the stables.

## Bugs
- If you buy a horse from [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) and the horse is killed, attempting to purchase a new horse from the same stable results in 1000 gold being deducted, and [Geimund](Skyrim_Geimund.md) stating that your horse is "the one with the saddle", despite no saddled horse appearing at the stables there. Instead, your new horse spawns here. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- The horses here are able to be taken for free if you befriend Hofgrir or Shadr, due to the horses being owned by the stables faction specifically. The problem is that horses cost 1,000 gold, making them way too valuable to let stablemasters let you ride them for free after completing a small favor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19062)) The ownership of the horses was changed to just Hofgrir, rather than the faction, preventing this issue.
