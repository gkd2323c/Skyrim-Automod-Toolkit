[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/ON-place-Throne_Aligned.jpg/200px-ON-place-Throne_Aligned.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Throne_Aligned.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Throne_Aligned.jpg) The Throne Aligned The **Cogitum Centralis** is [Sotha Sil's](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) seat of power in the [Brass Fortress](Lore_Brass_Fortress.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is located in the maintenance tunnels and subterranean chambers of the Mechanical Fundament underneath the Fortress.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCCAG-2)</sup> It has an automated defense system, and is controlled by a master factotum that is tasked with protecting the Throne Aligned, the control center of the Clockwork City.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WSL-3)</sup> The Cogitum was designed to be virtually impenetrable.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The Throne Aligned is also where Sotha Sil stored his thoughts and notes, and is deadly for a mortal to access.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-GDTTARTCC-5)</sup>

In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Sotha Sil inhabited the control center known as the Dome of Sotha Sil and was guarded by two [imperfects](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperfect), giant factotums. It was also the place where Sotha Sil met his end at the hands of Almalexia.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOCWC-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TRTMG-7)</sup> It was part of the sections the Nerevarine traversed in the Third Era. Those sections include the Maintenance and the Outer and Inner Flooded Halls; the Halls of Delirium, Theuda, Sallaemu, and Mileitho; the Central Gearworks; the Chamber of Sohleh; and the Domes of Kasia, Serlyn, Udok, the Imperfect, and Sotha Sil. They have been theorized to be the same place as the Cogitum Centralis.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PHLI-8)</sup>
