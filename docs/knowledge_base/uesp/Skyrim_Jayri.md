[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Jayri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jayri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jayri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jayri.jpg) Jayri **Jayri** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) camped out in [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md). She came to [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) for protection from the [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md), but the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) won't let her in. She shows up in the canyon during the quest [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md).

Like the other refugees, Jayri can do very little in the camp and will just wander around, at times using the [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) or the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). She never sleeps. She wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5).

## Dialogue
When approached, she will complain:

- *"Look at the size of that fort. They must have room for a few more in there."*
- *"I worry it'll never be safe to go home."*
- *"It's not safe out there. Horrible creatures attacking towns in the night... If they won't let me in, at least I can stay here."*
- *"If the Dawnguard can't stop these vampires, who can?"*
