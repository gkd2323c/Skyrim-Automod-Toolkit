*A Thunderbolt that does 60 points of shock damage to Health and half that to Magicka.* **Thunderbolt** is an expert level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [shock damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) to Health and Magicka.

## Effects
- [Thunderbolt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), 60 pts for 0+0.1 sec
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md), 200 pts for 1 sec; if the [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Shock), raises damage to 75 at first rank or 90 at second rank

## Notes
- Thunderbolt is essentially just a much more powerful (and expensive) version of [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), with added sound effects and screen shake.
- Like most Lightning spells, it can be used to set off [Lightning Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Rune) from a safe distance.
- It has much shorter range than the equivalent [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md) and [Icy Spear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Icy_Spear) spells, but the fact that it is cast instantly instead of as a projectile makes it much easier to hit moving targets so long as they're within range.
- Thunderbolt is very useful for fowling (Hawks, Bone Hawks, Felsaad Terns). As long as the birds aren't flying too high, they can be hit with only a little bit leading. Best to use where the fallen bird can be reached.
