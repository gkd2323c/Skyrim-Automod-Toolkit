[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Milore_Ienth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Milore_Ienth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Milore_Ienth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Milore_Ienth.jpg) Milore Ienth **Milore Ienth** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Alchemy Expert) located in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), living on her [farm](Skyrim_Ienth_Farm.md) with her husband [Garyn](Skyrim_Garyn_Ienth.md). She provides expert level [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md). She grows or harvests most of her own ingredients, of which she has a vast array.

Milore Ienth wears a pair of [Dunmer shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Shoes) and a [Dunmer outfit](Skyrim_Clothing.md#xx03706b). She wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) to defend herself and carries a [key to her house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ienth_Farmhouse_Key) on her person. Milore will also carry a variety of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

Milore starts her day at 6am when she gets up from her bed. She will allow herself two hours for breakfast before heading outside to the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) outside of her home to sell her wares. Milore will continue to provide her market services until 8pm, at which time she will head back inside to work at the alchemy lab inside her home. She will continue to brew potions until midnight, when she will either head to the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) (where she will be entranced and unable to engage in conversation) or if the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), retire to her bed for the day. She will wake up at 6am the next day to repeat her schedule.

*"Looking for any potions? Ingredients? Alchemical supplies?"* *"If you're looking for any exotic ingredients, just ask. I might have what you need."* *"My husband Garyn maintains our farm. Without him, Raven Rock would likely go hungry."* *"You sure showed Miraak who's in charge around here!"* *"Destroying the ash spawn has made it safer to work in the fields. Thank you."* **(When you stop the [ash spawn attacks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead))** *"I don't know who sent you here, but you've given us something to build on here. I hope someday we can repay you for your kindness."* **([Reopened the mines](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md))** After she is [freed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md) from [Miraak's](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) influence, she may remark:

*"Just thinking that some madman was inside my mind. Gives me shivers just thinking about it."* *"You sure showed Miraak who's in charge around here!"* When spoken to:

**How has Raven Rock needed your help?** *"As you can imagine, the eruption of the Red Mountain took an awful toll on Solstheim. Hunting for game has been scarce, and the waters are apparently too tainted for fishing. According to [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md), my husband's been Raven Rock's most important food source for decades."* **Where did you learn your alchemy skills?** *"After we left Morrowind, we found ourselves in Skyrim. We wandered around a bit and ended up in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) where we took temporary residence with [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md), the town's alchemist. I was fascinated by her work, and over a period of a few years she taught me quite a bit."* **Why did you leave Morthal?** *"It wasn't long before we became homesick... we were the only Dunmer in Morthal. When word reached us that a settlement of Dunmer was forming on Solstheim, we were thrilled. It's been difficult, but I'm not regretting it one bit. In fact, I'm glad we've been able to stay and help."* When exiting conversation:

*"Remember, I have plenty of potions and ingredients if you're ever in the market."*
