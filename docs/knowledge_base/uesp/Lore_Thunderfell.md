# Thunderfell

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/BL-item-Thunderfell.jpg/200px-BL-item-Thunderfell.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Thunderfell.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Thunderfell.jpg) Thunderfell (Blades) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/BL-item-Stormcaller.jpg/200px-BL-item-Stormcaller.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Stormcaller.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Stormcaller.jpg) Stormcaller (Blades) Thunderfell is a mythical mace rumored to be imbued with the power of a hundred [storm atronachs](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md). It was also known as the **Skeleton's Bane** because of its effect on these creatures. It was imbued with an [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) that strikes those hit with the mace with lightning. It also hindered the stamina and magicka's reserves of those struck.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Thunderfell-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Stormcaller-2)</sup>

Mythical Thunderfell inspired artisans and weaponsmiths to create another mace, the **Stormcaller**. It was inferior to Thunderfell and was said to contain the power of forty storm atronachs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Stormcaller-2)</sup>

It is unknown whether the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md#Warrior) wielded Thunderfell and Stormcaller, but as of [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180) both weapons were in use.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-3)</sup>
