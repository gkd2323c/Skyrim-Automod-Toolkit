# Auridon Paladins

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/ON-place-Auridon_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Auridon_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Auridon_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Auridon_02.jpg) Auridon (ESO) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/LG-cardart-Auridon_Paladin.png/200px-LG-cardart-Auridon_Paladin.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Auridon_Paladin.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Auridon_Paladin.png) Auridon Paladin (Legends) Auridon Paladins were an [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) militant order known for their elite warriors and mages. These [paladins](Lore_Paladins.md) were known for their high-quality training, amazing willpower, fighting skills, and reliability. The main objective of the Auridon Paladins was to defend the isle of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), the gateway in and out of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) from the threat of the ambitious outsiders and to stop the rebel factions of the native High Elves from disturbing the order and peace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LCL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AP-2)</sup>
