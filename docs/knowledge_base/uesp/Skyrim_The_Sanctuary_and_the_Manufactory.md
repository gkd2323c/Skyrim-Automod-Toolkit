## Quick Walkthrough
1. To start this quest head to the Frozen Hearth inn within Winterhold.
2. Go inside and into one of the rooms to find [Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts' Journal, v1](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v1).
3. Head to the [Frostroot Cave](Skyrim_Frostroot_Cave.md) and find the [Nchuanthumz](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md) entrance.
4. Access Nchuanthumz's Manufactory
5. Find the Argonian [Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts)'s body near a control panel. Examine the corpse for clues.
6. Read [Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts' Journal, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v2).
7. Use one of the two buttons to activate the Dwarven Sphere Overseer.
8. Collect 3 Dwemer Cogs, 3 Dwemer Actuators and a Dwemer Oil.
9. Return to the Dwarven Sphere Overseer and bring the manufactory back online.
10. Collect a Centurion Dynamo Core and use it to construct a Dwarven Spider Renovator.
11. Summon the Dwarven Spider Renovator to clean a section of the house.
12. Wait 24 hours for the renovations to finish. A few rooms will be fully renovated and furnished.
13. Repeat this 2 more times to fully renovate the home.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Go to [The Frozen Hearth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), [Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts' Journal, v1](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v1) is on a table in the left room when facing to the innkeeper, read it to start the quest. Quest maker will direct to [Frostroot Cave](Skyrim_Frostroot_Cave.md). Head to and enter the cave, fight three bandits and walk along a short way to Nchuanthumz.

Once you enter [Nchuanthumz](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md), head straight and deal with a few skeevers and three bandits until you reach the lift to Nchuanthumz's Manufactory. Use the lift and then continue straight forward until you reach the corpse of [Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts), examine the body to find a [Dwemer Actuator](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Actuator) and his [second journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v2).

The journal lists the items required to repair the assembly lines, with the required items being one [Dwarven oil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Oil), three [Dwemer cogs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Cog), and three Dwemer Actuators. The room already contains everything you need. You should already have one Dwemer Actuator from Artifacts' own inventory; the other two are on the two tables on either side of the room, alongside the cogs and the oil. Collect all the required items and activate the Dwarven Sphere Overseer button. The quest markers will direct you to press either the right or left button to do so.

The Dwarven Sphere Overseer will emerge from the scuttle on the right side of the room. Follow the Dwarven Sphere Overseer and give it the repair parts by interacting with it. You can then activate the manufactory; if you already have a [Centurion Dynamo Core](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Centurion_Dynamo_Core) then you can head straight to the room on the left, however if you don't have one, go to the room on the west side of the manufactory to find a destroyed Dwarven Centurion Master, whose body has a core on it.

Place the Centurion Dynamo Core into the Dynamo Actuator and then activate the button next to it. This will build a Dwarven Spider Renovator, head back to the lift and go back up to Nchuanthumz. There are three sections of the house to clear with the spider, each takes 24 hours once the spider is summoned using one of the levers. Once all three sections are complete, the quest will finish and you will have a fully furnished Dwarven house.
