## Walkthrough
The ruins are most easily accessed by passing through the [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) -infested [Darklight Tower](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower.md) and heading southeast into the mountains. They consist of a few broken pillars and collapsed buildings; the one farthest to the east contains an unlocked [Dwemer chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). In front of this is a Dwemer floor lantern in a stone pit, with the bodies of two [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md) lying next to it, along with a few pieces of [Dwemer metal](Skyrim_Dwemer_Metal.md) and an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). As you approach the ruins, three [wisps](Skyrim_Wisp.md) will be seen roaming the area. Approaching the lantern will trigger the appearance of their [wispmother](Skyrim_Wispmother.md). The only other items of interest are a few [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes in the area.

## Bugs
- The two dead hags may appear with their clothing unequipped.
- The wisps don't seem to respawn.
- You may have to attack the lantern in the center in order to trigger the wispmother event.
