[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-place-Alftand.jpg/200px-SR-place-Alftand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alftand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alftand.jpg) Alftand circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Alftand is a [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) located within the [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md) region of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Alftand is unique among Dwemer ruins in that it is the only one built partially atop an [active glacier](Lore_Ice_Fields.md). In addition to its unique location, the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) of Alftand had their own unique style of iconography.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tattoos-1)</sup> The Great Lift at Alftand, which leads into [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), can be found in [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), overlooking the main road and [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md). According to [Septimus Signus](Lore_Septimus_Signus.md), Alftand is "The point of puncture, of first entry, of the tapping."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRSeptimus-2)</sup>

## History
In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Sulla Trebatius led an expedition of adventurers into Alftand in search of hidden treasures. His group was stalked and captured by the [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) before eventually succumbing to madness and killing each other. Later that year, after consulting with [Septimus Signus](Lore_Septimus_Signus.md), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) traveled to Alftand in order to retrieve an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) hidden in Blackreach.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Also during this year, the Dragonborn would discover the schematics for a Dwarven fishing rod that was rumored to be in Alftand.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
