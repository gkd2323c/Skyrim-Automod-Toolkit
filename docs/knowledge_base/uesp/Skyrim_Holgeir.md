**Holgeir** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warlord. He fell in love with the huntress [Fjori](Skyrim_Fjori.md) on the field of battle, where they fought until her sword broke his axe and his shield dulled her blade. With Fjori and Holgeir united, there came a time of peace for the clans of the forest. When Holgeir was bitten by a poisonous snake, Fjori journeyed to obtain a cure from the Akavir which instantly cured him. However, then Fjori too was bitten by a snake, and she died immediately due to exhaustion from her journey. Holgeir had a [great tomb](Skyrim_Ansilvund.md) built, and after its completion, he took his own life, hoping to rejoin her in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).

During the quest [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md), a conjurer named [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven), distraught over the loss of her husband Saeel Al-Skaven, is raising an army of [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) which includes Fjori in its ranks, and Holgeir as well.

As a Draugr or a Dragon priest he is equipped with whatever is appropriate for generic creatures of same type, as a ghost he wears [noble clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Noble_Clothes) with a pair of [fur-lined boots](Skyrim_Fur-lined_Boots.md) and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

- **Fjori**: *"Thank you for releasing us from her spell."*
- **Holgeir**: *"Now we can rest in peace once again. Come Fjori, my love..."*
- **Fjori**: *"Take this with our gratitude."*
