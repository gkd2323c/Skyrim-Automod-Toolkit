[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4b/SR-place-Falkreath_Hold.jpg/200px-SR-place-Falkreath_Hold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Hold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Hold.jpg) Pine forest in Falkreath **Falkreath Hold** is a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in southern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with its capital in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). It is initially affiliated with the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), but can be captured by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) after completing [Rescue from Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad.md). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md).

It is the second southernmost hold in Skyrim, after [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). It borders [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in the south, across the [Jerall Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jerall_Mountains), and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the west. Other than its capital, Falkreath, the only notable town in the hold is [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), the first settlement in Skyrim to be attacked by a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md).

Falkreath Hold is blanketed by characteristic pine forests and described by its citizens as being covered in a fine mist that makes it seem seasonless. The mountains rising above Falkreath are well above the snow line striking a stark comparison to the green of the rest of the hold. The most distinguishing geographic feature is [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md) which covers a large portion of the central region and acts as the source for the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md).

The town has the largest cemetery in Skyrim that houses generations of the dead, due to a large number of battles that have taken place over the years. The citizens take pride in this and many of the local shops and the farm derive their names from their close proximity to so much death.

## Flora and Fauna
The pine forest of Falkreath is very rich in [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle), [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella), and all common varieties of [mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower). [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md) is home to a large population of different [fish species](Skyrim_Fish.md) which can be found near [the Lady Stone](Skyrim_The_Lady_Stone.md) and by the start of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md). The hold is one of the few places where [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) is fairly common. In the snowy eastern parts only the [snowberry bush](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) thrives.

## Falkreath Hold Quests
All quests that are started in or are related to Falkreath Hold (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### [Main Quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md)
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.
- **[Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md)**: Retrieve the [Dragonstone](Skyrim_Dragonstone.md).

### [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) Civil War Quests
- **[Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md)**: Drive the Imperials from Skyrim. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Rescue from Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad.md)**: Rescue [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) soldiers from the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and capture the [fort](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### [Daedric Quests](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md)
- **[Boethiah's Calling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boethiah%27s_Calling)**: Become [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md)'s new champion.
- **[A Daedra's Best Friend](Skyrim_A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend.md)**: Help [Barbas](Skyrim_Barbas.md) reunite with his [master](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md).
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md)**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md)'s Great Hunt.
- **[Pieces of the Past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past)**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor) and reforge this deadly blade.

### [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md)
- **[Rjorn's Drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rjorn%27s_Drum)**: Retrieve a [drum](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md#Rjorn.27s_Drum) for [Giraud Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md).

### [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)
- All the Dark Brotherhood primary quests except for [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md), and most of the other quests received before Hail Sithis!, are related to Falkreath Hold.

### [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)
- **[Darkness Returns](Skyrim_Darkness_Returns.md)**: Walk the [Pilgrim's Path](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher) and return the [Skeleton Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Key) to its rightful place.
- **[No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md)**: Find the [24 gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) to restore the Crown of Barenziah.

### [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md)
- **[Blood's Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood%27s_Honor)**: Claim the head of a [Glenmoril Witch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glenmoril_Witch) for [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md).

### [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md) including [Divine Quests](Skyrim_Divine_Quests.md)
- **[The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen) for [Danica Pure-Spring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring).
- **[The Golden Claw](Skyrim_The_Golden_Claw.md)**: A [shopkeeper](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md) wants you to retrieve his [family heirloom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw) from a [bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift).
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).
- **[Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md)**: Help an [injured hunter](Skyrim_Valdr.md) avenge his fallen companions.

### [Creation Club Quests](Skyrim_Creation_Club_Quests.md)
- **[The Dwarven Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dwarven_Crown)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md)</sup>: Gather all the components of a powerful Dwarven Crown from within a [forgotten Dwarven ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns).
- **[The Dwarven Horse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dwarven_Horse)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md)</sup>: Reassemble an ancient Dwarven [vamidium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vamidium).
- **[Forgotten Seasons](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons_(quest).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md)</sup>: Shut down a malfunctioning Dwarven weather machine.
- **[Once A Hunter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Once_A_Hunter)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.md)</sup>: Track down a hunter with a dangerous past.
- **[Pets of Skyrim](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim_(quest).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim.md)</sup>: Find trained pets around Skyrim.
- **[The Rising Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Rising_Dead)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup>: A group of [Necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) plan to summon an army of [zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md).
