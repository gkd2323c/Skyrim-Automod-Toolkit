# Sarpa

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) Black Marsh, the homeland of the Sarpa Sarpa are a breed and tribe of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) that live in the inner parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), close to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> Sarpa have some distinct differences in their appearance compared to the Argonians most commonly seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> as they are described as winged. They and several of the other [Middle Argonia](Lore_Middle_Argonia.md) tribes are reputed to kill outsiders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup>

## Notes
- A winged Argonian, Red-Rock-Shadow, was introduced into the [*War of the Wormgod*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loranna%27s_RP/War_of_the_Wormgod) role-playing campaign by [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson). As a prior RP was also the debut of the [Naga](Lore_Naga.md) Argonians, it is likely this Argonian was meant to be of the Sarpa. In the RP, Ted Peterson describes her as having large leathery wings attached to her arms, and being capable of flight.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWormgod-3)</sup>
