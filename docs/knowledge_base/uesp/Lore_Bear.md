# Bear

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/ON-creature-Bear_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Bear_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Bear_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Bear_02.jpg) A bear Bears are large, often hostile creatures found across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They will attack those who infringe on their territory, although they often roar or growl in an effort to intimidate potential threats before attacking. They are believed to orignate from the frozen continent of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> with some legends saying they can be found on [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md) as well.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MBC-2)</sup>

There are several varieties of bears. [Black bears](Lore_Black_Bear.md) are black-colored and are usually the smallest kind of bear found throughout Tamriel, although those found in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) are notably among the largest bears seen anywhere.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They can be summoned by [spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md) for protection. [Grizzly bears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grizzly_Bear) are dark red colored and can be found throughout Tamriel, and the [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) can summon them for protection. [Brown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brown_Bear) or [cave bears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cave_Bear) are brown-colored and are larger and more powerful than the black bears variety. [Snow bears](Lore_Snow_Bear.md) are white-colored bears that can be found in the most cold parts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They are more resistant to frost than other bears varieties and can cause frostbite to those that are damaged by it. [Plague bears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Plague_Bear) are found on Solstheim and are often diseased.

Bears are known to carry infectious diseases such as [yellow tick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yellow_Tick), [bone break fever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bone_Break_Fever) and [Ataxia](Lore_Ataxia.md). Bears are hunted for their pelts, which can be tanned into leather, and their claws, for their potential use in alchemy. [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) are known to use Black Bear musk oil as a fragrance.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Musk Oil-4)</sup> Bears that are cursed by necromancers become baneful and easily enraged. Bears that have been transformed in this manner show no fear of death, making them ideal for use in battle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Bears were the mount of choice for the [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) Royal Calvary; the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) would use gem-studded bear mount bridles and dress their bears with parade dress bugle and silk displaying the Royal Orsinium Symbol.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ROCB-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GSBMB-7)</sup>

The Skaal see bears as the sacred embodiment of a warrior's strength and thus as a gift from the [All-Maker](Lore_All-Maker.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BMSkaal-8)</sup> The [Boots of the Crusader](Lore_Crusader%27s_Relics.md) were once guarded by a bear called the Forest Guardian at the Shrine of Kynareth in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-NF-9)</sup> [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials include slaying the Ursine Guardian, one of the seven guardian beasts of Skyrim.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-KST-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SRFroki-11)</sup> The bear is among the many animals carved onto [dragon claws](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md#Temples) and their respective puzzles in Nordic ruins.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SRDragon Claws-12)</sup> [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s aspect of strength takes the form of a bear. As the Father of Manbeasts,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TBOD-13)</sup> the Prince is also responsible for the creation of [werebears](Lore_Werebear.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-AOLH-14)</sup>

Some [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) [vamidia](Lore_Vamidium.md) were constructed in the shape of bears, and conjurers are able to compel [Atronachs](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) to adopt the form of bears.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) have shaped [useful lizards](Lore_Lizard-Steed.md) to a form resembling that of Tamriel's bears.

## Breeds

### Badger Bear
A species of bear with a black and white fur patterns similar to that of [badgers](Lore_Badger.md). One variety that ressembles brindle badgers is native to [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). They were close to extinction before Viscount Glorion of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) began breeding them as mounts. Another that resemble colovian badgers are found in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and were used as mounts by the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md)

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Badger_Bear)

A smaller species of bear found throughout the woodlands of Tamriel. Some [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md) have the ability to magically summon bears to defend themselves or attack passersby. ### Plague Bear
Of similar size to the Grizzly Bear, these beasts are more resistant against the surrounding nature than its cousin, and known to carry diseases.

- **Found in:** [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Plague_Bear)

### Psijic Bear Examplar
Summoned by members of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) as a mount, a Psijic Bear Examplar resembles a glowing, golden, translucent bear, and is intended to represent the ideal form of a bear.

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Psijic_Bear_Exemplar)

### Snow Bear
A white bear with an icy bite often found on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) as well as in the high mountains and icy plains of Skyrim. They are sometimes used as mounts. It is said that the pelts of these animals have innate magical qualities that help guard against Solstheim's brutal frost and snow. The [smiths](Lore_Smithing.md) of Solstheim can make use of these pelts to craft powerfully protective Snow Bear armor.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

- Variations: Atmoran Snow Bears
- **Found in:** [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Snow_Bear), [Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bear), [Skyrim Very Special Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim VSE:Enemies#Snow_Bear), [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Dungeons#Encounters)
