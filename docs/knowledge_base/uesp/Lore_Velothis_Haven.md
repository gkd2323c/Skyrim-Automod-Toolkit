[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/AR-place-Glen_Haven.jpg/200px-AR-place-Glen_Haven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Glen_Haven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Glen_Haven.jpg) The town of Glen Haven Velothis Haven<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWFalura Llervu-1)</sup> (otherwise known as **Glen Haven**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup> is a town located deep within the territory of the Great [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), within the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Velothis Haven is neighbored by several settlements, including [Firewatch](Lore_Firewatch.md) in the west, [Helnim Wall](Lore_Helnim_Wall.md) in the south, and [Karththor Heights](Lore_Karththor_Heights.md) in the east.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup>

The settlement was either named after [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md), the father of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) race, or the [Velothi](Lore_Velothi.md), another name for the Chimeri people.

## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town was ruled by Duke Turfar. The town also had a rivalry with [Dragon Glade](Lore_Dragon_Glade.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Falura Llervu, a slave pretending to be a high-born Telvanni lady, claimed to be from Velothis Haven, the daughter of Andrano Llervu, lord of Tel Llervu. However, given the circumstances under which this claim was made, the veracity and factual accuracy of this statement cannot be confirmed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWFalura Llervu-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWZN-4)</sup>
