# Talos Cult

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/MW-quest-Talos_Cult_Conspiracy.jpg/200px-MW-quest-Talos_Cult_Conspiracy.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Talos_Cult_Conspiracy.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Talos_Cult_Conspiracy.jpg) A Talos Cult shrine *We are those who worship the true following of the Legions. Protecting the Glory of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), the [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md).*
—A member of the Talos Cult<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGeneric-1)</sup> The Talos Cult, variously also referred to as the **Cult of Talos**,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CA-2)</sup> **Tiberius Imperator Cult**, and **Citizenship Cult**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Po M-3)</sup> is a religious group that expressly reveres [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> founder of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), as a divine god-hero.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Savant-5)</sup> Combined with the worship of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), the Talos Cult forms part of the whole [Imperial Cult](Lore_Imperial_Cult.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Savant-5)</sup> It had a significant following in the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGeneric-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ROCW-6)</sup> and the [Red Templars](Lore_Red_Templars.md) had some affiliation with it.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CA-2)</sup> The Cult had its start as the **Cult of Tiber Septim**, one of the prominent [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenese.md) cults of Cyrodiil during the living reign of the Emperor himself.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1C-7)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), fringe members of the Talos Cult in the Imperial Legion were part of a conspiracy to assassinate [Emperor Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) during a rumored visit to [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), due to his perceived weak rule. They began to recruit new members from the Deathshead Legion garrison in [Gnisis](Lore_Gnisis.md), requiring them to swear an oath that they "shall die to put a strong man back on the [throne](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-NFOM-8)</sup> This was prevented by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), who executed the conspirators by order of General Darius.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

Following the signing of the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), the worship of Talos was banned in the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup> As such, it could be assumed that the Talos Cult no longer exists in any official capacity.
