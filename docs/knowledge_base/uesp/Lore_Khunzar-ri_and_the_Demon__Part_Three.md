Khunzar-ri and the Demon: Part Three **An ancient Khajiiti myth as recorded by Moon-Singer Satarri** *A Khajiiti legend detailing Khunzar-ri's conflict with dragons* *
---
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) roamed with wild abandon, the hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) gathered champions to deal with a [terrible demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his horde. With [Nurarion the Perfect](Lore_Nurarion_the_Perfect.md) and [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md), he sought a third companion.

The demon said, "Oh, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md), you amuse us! How can three tiny morsels ever stand against us?" Khunzar-ri laughed, "We may be tiny, but we are persistent! And tricky, too, as you will see."

Then Khunzar-ri turned to his Kra'Jun. "One more we need, a Khajiiti queen of renown. [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md), who loves to bask in the [glow of the Big Moon](Lore_Jode%27s_Light.md) when she reflects and thinks deep thoughts."

These are the words and the words are true.
