[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/MW-place-Vos.jpg/200px-MW-place-Vos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Vos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Vos.jpg) Vos ca. 3E 427 Vos was a village located in the northeast corner of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The settlement was historically occupied by [Velothi](Lore_Ashlanders.md#Velothi) farmers who grew [marshmerrow](Lore_Marshmerrow.md) and [wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md), and faced constant harassment from [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) raiders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-1)</sup> The town had also been territory of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), whether it was under the mage-lords of [Tel Mora](Lore_Tel_Mora.md) to the east or [Tel Vos](Lore_Tel_Vos.md) to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONGray-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/MW-place-Tel_Mora.jpg/200px-MW-place-Tel_Mora.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Tel_Mora.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Tel_Mora.jpg) Tel Mora, east across the strait When Vos was built is unknown, however, the Velothi have farmed its lands for centuries. Likewise, when its longest-known ruler, Mistress Dratha of Tel Mora first had this claim is also unknown but by the late Third Era, she was considered the oldest member of House Telvanni.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWAryon-3)</sup> At an unknown time, one of the mythical [luminaries](Lore_Luminary.md) called [the Netch](Lore_The_Netch.md) appeared on Vvardenfell and various tales of unusual luck began to spread en masse. One such story was when a child ran away from Vos to nearby Esutanamus, where they were surrounded by monsters. The Netch saved the boy's life by turning them into [hens](Lore_Chicken.md). After the search party found him, they held a feast and ate the chickens.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FOTN-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) worked with the Telvanni wizard Savarak Fels to kill Mistress Dratha, and take over Tel Mora and Vos. They first poisoned Tel Mora's tower, leaving Dratha incapacitated. And with the Telvanni Council's support afterward, Savarak became Vos' magistrate. Savarak received funding from House Hlaalu and soldiers from the Black Snails mercenary group, with Selvil Island as their meeting ground. After their scheme was exposed, Dratha's mouth, Llayne Sadri contacted the Telvanni Council and with guards uprooted all of Vos to arrest Savarak. Before he could escape, he was defeated by an [Outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) helping Llayne Sadri on Selvil Island.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONReclaim-5)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Master Aryon of Tel Vos became the new Lord of Vos and helped develop the settlement. With his powers, he protected the villagers from raiders and used [mycoturgy](Lore_Mycoturgy.md) to build the new tradehouse<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-1)</sup> owned by its [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) publican Burcanius Varo,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWVaro-6)</sup> and the village's docks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-1)</sup> Maela Kaushad, a wise woman was named Vos' hetwoman.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWSedyni-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWGen Rumors-8)</sup> What became of Vos throughout the rest of the Third Era is unknown but in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5), the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) as it is remembered, [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) erupted and destroyed most of Vvardenfell. A relief effort was organized to restore settlements that were hit the hardest but whether Vos was rebuilt or abandoned is unknown.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TRY1-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TRY2-10)</sup>

### Known Rulers
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/MW-npc-Maela_Kaushad.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Maela_Kaushad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Maela_Kaushad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Maela_Kaushad.jpg) Hetwoman Maela Kaushad For the longest time, Vos and its environs had been ruled by Mistress [Dratha](Lore_Dratha.md) of nearby [Tel Mora](Lore_Tel_Mora.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONGray-2)</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Mistress Dratha was poisoned and confined in her tower. In the meantime, Savarak Fels became the Magistrate of Vos.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONReclaim-5)</sup> By the late Third Era, Master Aryon of Tel Vos became the town's new mage-lord.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen Vos-1)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BB-11)</sup>

Much like other villages in Morrowind such as Marog and [Gnisis](Lore_Gnisis.md), a village elder known as a hetman is appointed as its administrative leader. These hetman can also act as stewards to their respective House lords.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWGen Hetman-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ROKZ-13)</sup>

- The **Second Era** - Wizard-Magistrate Savarak Fels *([2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582))* – *While Mistress Dratha was incapacitated in Tel Mora, Savarak was appointed by House Telvanni as Vos' leader.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONGray-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONReclaim-5)</sup>
- The **Third Era** - Hetwoman Maela Kaushad *(ca. [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427))*<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWSedyni-7)</sup> – *A former Ashlander wise woman that married a villager and left the tribes to settle in Vos.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWGen Rumors-8)</sup>*
