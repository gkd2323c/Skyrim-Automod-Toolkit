# Pahmar-raht

*When Jode is Full and Jone is Waning
Then shall be born the Pahmar-Raht
Greatest of generals and strategists
They lead our soldiers to war
With a roar that can shatter mountains
And a cry that can embolden armies
Even one can turn the tide of battle.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/e/e8/Mfull_Swaning.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mfull_Swaning.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mfull_Swaning.jpg) Pahmar-raht are born when Masser is full and Secunda is waning<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Pahmar-raht are a giant bipedal [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) that have plantigrade legs and are very tall. They are described as the greatest generals and strategists among the Khajiit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> Due to them being the strongest of furstocks, they are usually deployed as bodyguards and warriors.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sharjo Dialogue-3)</sup> They are sometimes ridden upon by the much smaller [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MUT4-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> Like the shorter [Pahmar](Lore_Pahmar.md) furstock, most resemble [Senche-Tigers](Lore_Senche-Cat.md#Tigers),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Pahmar-raht Appearance-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Survival G-6)</sup> but Pahmar-raht differ in that they are larger and more dangerous.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Survival G-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup>

[Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md), a legendary Khajiit hero, was a Pahmar-raht.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSC-7)</sup>
