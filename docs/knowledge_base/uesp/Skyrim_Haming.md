[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-npc-Haming.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Haming.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haming.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haming.jpg) Haming in Froki's Shack [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-quest-Unbound_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Unbound_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Unbound_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Unbound_04.jpg) [Gunnar Stone-Eye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunnar_Stone-Eye) protecting Haming at Helgen **Haming** is an eleven-year-old [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Froki's Shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Froki%27s_Shack). Originally from the town of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), he moves in with his grandfather, [Froki Whetted-Blade](Skyrim_Froki_Whetted-Blade.md), after Helgen is destroyed. He is the son of the late [Torolf](Skyrim_Torolf.md) and [Matlara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matlara), who are killed by [a dragon](Skyrim_Alduin.md) during its attack on Helgen. Haming is one of the few people to make it out of Helgen alive after the dragon attack. He is described by Froki as a good and strong lad.

Haming wears red colored [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8), and is equipped with a simple [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.

## Quest-Related Events

### Unbound
You first encounter Haming in the opening sequence, expressing his desire to watch the [Imperial soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and the prisoners at the beginning of the game. However, his father, [Torolf](Skyrim_Torolf.md), sends him inside:

**Haming**: *"Who are they, daddy? Where are they going?"* **Torolf**: *"You need to go inside, little cub."* **Haming**: *"Why? I want to watch the soldiers."* **Torolf**: *"Inside the house. Now."* **Haming**: *"Yes, papa."* Later, when you encounter [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) again after jumping from the destroyed inn, Hadvar is trying to get him out of danger:

*"Haming, you need to get over here. Now!"* *"That a boy. You're doing great."* Haming runs and makes it to safety but his father does not survive the attack of fire breath from [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md). Before Torolf's death, he may say to Haming:

*"I'm done for, little cub. Go. Run for it!"* *"That's it, son. Make me proud."* - [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-quest-Unbound_22.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Unbound_22.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Unbound_22.jpg) Hadvar warning Haming to get to safety

## Dialogue
If you encounter him at Froki's Shack he can say:

*"I'm eleven years old, and I could take you."* *"Someday I'm going to kill a dragon all by myself."* When spoken to directly:

**Do you like living here?** *"It ain't so bad. I miss living with my parents back in Helgen, though. Froki can't cook and there's nobody to talk to up here."* **Were your parents killed at Helgen?** *"Yeah, but don't say anything about it to Froki. I don't think he understands."* **Oh, I see.** *"It's okay, I know what happened at home. With the dragon and all."* If you ask him to tell you about himself:

**Tell me about yourself.** *"Froki won't let me hunt on my own yet, but he never shuts up about killing his first stag when he was eleven."*
## Notes
- Many internal names refer to Haming as `Torri`.
- There are, in fact, two versions of Haming: - The first version is encountered in Helgen. He has a base ID of `00013642` and a ref ID of `000AAF98`.
- The second version is the one in Froki's Shack. He has a base ID of `000C029C` and a ref ID of `000C029E`.
- Haming has no crime faction assigned and will therefore talk to [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) and [Vampire Lords](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.
