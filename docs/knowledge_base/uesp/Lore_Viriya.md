Viriya is a retired [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) adventuress and well-known master [fisherman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fish), who worked at the [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) fishery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-L.26L-2)</sup> There she attempted to breed [mudcrabs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mudcrab) in the hope of selling their meat on the town's market. She was seen as somewhat strange by her peers for her love of the creatures.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Her primary work at the fishery involved facilitating special fishing requests and bounties from across the province. A responsibility she shared with fellow master fisherman, [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Lore_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md), up until her eventual retirement from the fishery. After which the duty of handling the distribution of the fishing requests fell solely on Swims-In-Deep-Water.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Letter-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) ventured to the Riften docks to meet Viriya in the hopes of honing their fishing skills.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AA-1)</sup> Viriya tasked the Dragonborn with various errands, including catching a juvenile mudcrab for her to keep as a pet, which she named Snippy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-IAP-5)</sup>

One of the many errands she assigned the Dragonborn was dealing with an infestation of mudcrabs that were threatening fishermen on the shore west of [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md). She gifted the Dragonborn an [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) fishing rod that belonged to her father after it was resolved.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CE-6)</sup> Later on, Viriya sent an urgent message to the Dragonborn when the mudcrabs returned. Viriya and the Dragonborn soon joined forces with an army of guards to defeat a giant [Guardian Spirit](Lore_Guardian_Spirit.md) in the form of an [Emperor Crab](Lore_Emperor_Crab.md) that threatened Dawnstar.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-WB-7)</sup>

Afterward, Viriya decided to retire from the fishing business.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Letter-4)</sup> She tasked the Dragonborn with helping her collect supplies for a crab-based stall in Riften's Grand Plaza and gifted them a special amulet as a reward.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-EOTL-8)</sup>
