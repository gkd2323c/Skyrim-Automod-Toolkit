## Jagged Crown
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Jagged_Crown.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Jagged_Crown.png) Jagged Crown The [Jagged Crown](Lore_Jagged_Crown.md) is a symbol of office for the High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), a symbol of the might and power of the ruler of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) going back to the time of High King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) ([1E 143](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_143) - [221](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_221)), or even before. Legend holds that it was forged from the bones and teeth of ancient [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) by Harald himself. A portion of the power of every king who has worn it is said to be contained within.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Jagged_Crown.md)

## Jagged Lens of Compulsion
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/ON-item-Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion.jpg/200px-ON-item-Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion.jpg) Jagged Lens of Compulsion The [Jagged Lens of Compulsion](Lore_Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion.md) was an ancient [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) relic from the migration from [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md). In the hands of the wrong mage, the lens can be used to entrance mortals, making them slaves to the user's will. Despite its dangerous nature, the academy of [Shad Astula](Lore_Shad_Astula.md) decided to keep the relic in its original location in the temple, still linked to the Shad Astula Underhalls.

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the Jagged Lens of Compulsion was used by General Darvasen to enslave the students of Shad Astula and bolster the dwindling ranks of the Maulborn. Darvasen, the former assistant to Arch-Mage Valeyn, was previously expelled from the school for experimenting on another student using the Jagged Lens. A disgruntled Darvasen passed his knowledge of the Lens on to the Maulborn, and assisted them in their invasion of the Academy.

Shortly before the invasion occurred, [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) snuck into Shad Astula by dishonestly acquiring an invitation to the exclusive Academy, and stopped Darvasen with the help of three other students. The Jagged Lens was destroyed to free the students who had been enslaved during the Maulborn incursion.

**Found in:** - [Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Vision_Quest)

## Jewel of Fire
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/AR-endcut2-Deluxe.png/200px-AR-endcut2-Deluxe.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-endcut2-Deluxe.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-endcut2-Deluxe.png) Jewel of Fire The [Jewel of Fire](Lore_Jewel_of_Fire.md) was a rare gem that was said to be a [fallen star](Lore_Aetherial_Fragment.md) which still glowed with an inner flame. The Jewel played a large role in the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

In [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389), [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) used the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) to imprison [Emperor Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md), as well as killing his apprentice [Ria Silmane](Lore_Ria_Silmane.md) so word of his betrayal would not reach the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md). To ensure no one could free the Emperor, Tharn attempted to destroy the staff. However, as the Staff of Chaos was indestructible, it was instead split into eight pieces which he then hid in dangerous locations throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). As a final failsafe in the event that the staff was reassembled, Tharn rendered it useless by draining the magic energy of the Staff into the Jewel of Fire, which was in turn hidden beneath the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md).

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Staff_of_Chaos)

## Jewel of the Sphinx
The [Jewel of the Sphinx](Lore_Jewel_of_the_Sphinx.md) was a highly valued artifact, regarded as one of the most renowned relics of [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s priesthood. It was described as Khenarthi's prized jewel.

## Jode's Core
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/ON-item-Jode%27s_Core.jpg/200px-ON-item-Jode%27s_Core.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Jode%27s_Core.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Jode%27s_Core.jpg) Jode's Core [Jode's Core](Lore_Jode%27s_Core.md) is a powerful, lunar artifact that has been coveted by even the likes of dragons for thousands of years. It has an appearance of a large, glowing blue sphere with power overflowing that makes it comparable to the appearance of a star. It is located in the [Plane of Jode](Lore_Plane_of_Jode.md), a realm associated with [Jode](Lore_Jode.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Jode%27s_Core_(quest))
