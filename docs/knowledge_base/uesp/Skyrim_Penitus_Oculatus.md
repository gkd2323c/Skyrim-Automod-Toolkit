## Notes
- Even if the player aids the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) faction in gaining control over [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the Penitus Oculatus outpost remains active in Dragon Bridge, despite Stormcloak patrols, which will attack Oculatus-affiliated soldiers. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. If both the civil war for the Stormcloaks and the Dark Brotherhood questline are completed, they will be removed.
- "Penitus Oculatus" is Latin for "seeing inward". However, Latin is a very contextual language, and would realistically mean something more like "inside watchers" or "internal security".
- Penitus Oculatus members typically wear the [Penitus Oculatus Helmet](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Helmet.md), [Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Armor), [Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Boots), and [Bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Bracers), but not all of them. Some members (notably Commander Maro) do not wear the helmet.
