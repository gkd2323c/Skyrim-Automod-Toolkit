This section describes unmarked places on the island of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) added by the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on. They usually aren't directly related to any quests (though several of them contain East Empire Company strongboxes, related to the [Pain in the Necklace](Skyrim_Pain_in_the_Necklace.md) quest).

## Northern Mountains

### Bloodskal Copse
File:SR-place-Bloodskal Copse.jpg Bloodskal Copse This is in a small natural harbor southwest of Bloodskal Barrow and northwest of Raven Rock. During the quest Deathbrand, a small Reaver rowboat is moored near this copse of trees. Defeat the Reavers here (who were discussing the treasure they'd dug up and were unwilling to open it) before prying the adept Ancient Chest open with your lockpicks. This chest is not present unless the quest has begun.

### Desolate Hunter's Camp
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-place-Reaver%27s_End.jpg/200px-SR-place-Reaver%27s_End.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reaver%27s_End.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reaver%27s_End.jpg) Reaver's End Southwest of [Hrothmund's Barrow](Skyrim_Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.md), north-northeast of Moesring Pass, you'll find a cindering fire at the center of a few crates, barrels, and sacks, with a dead reaver slumped next to it. Two Rieklings will ambush you when you approach. On the north side of the fire, an apprentice-locked strongbox sits atop a crate. In front of that crate is an expert-locked East Empire Company strongbox. A total of three helmets can be found here, including a bonemold helmet, a fur helmet, and a Skaal hat. On the south side of the fire, to the right of the dead reaver is a wooden chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=41009&y=72590&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Dragon Mound: Frozen Shoals
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound_02.jpg) Frozen Shoals Dragon Mound [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound.jpg/200px-SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Dragon_Mound.jpg) Frozen Shoals Dragon Mound In the far northeastern corner of the map, north of Frossel and [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md), there are three islands which form a triangle. The northwest of these three islands is home to a [dragon burial mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md). A [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) lies in wait here, along with several [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md). Just northwest of the dragon mound is a novice-locked chest next to some skeletal remains. Scattered across the island are ten [clams](Skyrim_Clam.md), three [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) plants, and two [pearl oysters](Skyrim_Pearl_Oyster.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=60487&y=100884&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Forgotten Spoils
[![SR-place-Forgotten Spoils.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-place-Forgotten_Spoils.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forgotten_Spoils.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forgotten_Spoils.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forgotten_Spoils.jpg) Head northwest from Hrothmund's Barrow, looking for a sapphire Geode, and the summit of a long cascading waterfall that tumbles down to Castle Karstaag. On the rocky outcrops at the top of the waterfall is an adept chest to loot alongside a sword and a [steel plate](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Helmet.md) (or leather) helmet (helmet is sometimes clipped into the rock).

### Giant Nirnroot Island
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island.jpg/200px-SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island.jpg) Giant Nirnroot Island [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant_Nirnroot_Island_02.jpg) Giant Nirnroot On a large island in the far northeastern corner of the map, north of [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md), there is a small collection of six giant [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) plants, each of which yield three ingredients when harvested. Two [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) and a giant spriggan matron can be found here. On the east side of the island is a small boat containing an unlocked chest, an ingredient barrel, a knapsack, an apothecary's satchel, three sacks, a coin purse, and an East Empire Company strongbox. The boat can sometimes become invisible, leaving only the reflection of the lantern revealing its position.<sup>[*verification needed — Observed with PC*]</sup> A dead [Alchemist](Skyrim_Alchemist.md) can be found lying facedown next to a giant nirnroot plant. Scattered across the island are two regular nirnroot plants, eleven [pearl oysters](Skyrim_Pearl_Oyster.md), and four spiky grass plants which yield [ashen grass pods](Skyrim_Ashen_Grass_Pod.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=75725&y=102808&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Horker Iceberg
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-place-Horker_Stalhrim_Island.jpg/200px-SR-place-Horker_Stalhrim_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Stalhrim_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Stalhrim_Island.jpg) Horker Stalhrim Island Directly northeast of [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) is a small island guarded by two horkers. Two [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) deposits are located on the island. Mining them, you can find a leveled enchanted [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md) in the right one, and the lootable corpse of a [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) in the left one. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=28333&y=99959&zoom=16&legacy=true)

With the [Alternative Armors - Stalhrim Fur](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Stalhrim_Fur.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, this island becomes involved with the quest [Ancient Ice](Skyrim_Ancient_Ice.md), during which you will be sent here to face [Skjol the Tongueless](Skyrim_Skjol_the_Tongueless.md) in battle. After defeating him, you will be able to claim the full set of Stalhrim Fur armor found within one of the sarcophagi on the island.

### Lurker Ambush (Tidal Pool)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-place-Lurker_Ambush.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lurker_Ambush.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lurker_Ambush.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lurker_Ambush.jpg) Lurker Ambush West-northwest of [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md), you will find a small inlet of the larger bay that surrounds the shoal. When you approach it, a leveled [Lurker](Skyrim_Lurker.md) may rise up from the shallow waters and attack you. Three [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) grow in and near the inlet, and two [orichalcum](Skyrim_Orcish.md) deposits can be mined to the west and northwest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=61748.2&y=85050&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Mudcrab Tidal Pools
This part of the shoreline is north of the Water Stone and south of Bristleback Cave. During Deathbrand, the water in one of the tidal pools recedes revealing an ancient chest which contains the Deathbrand Gauntlets.

Rieklings can attack the mudcrabs found here.

### Riekling Barrel Hoard
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-place-Riekling_Ambush.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riekling_Ambush.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riekling_Ambush.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riekling_Ambush.jpg) Riekling Ambush From [Damphall Mine](Skyrim_Damphall_Mine.md), head north until you see a few patches of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md). Turn left at the next rock and you'll see a wooden platform with many barrels, a chest, and a couple crates. An East Empire Company strongbox sits on the crate to the left. Beware, as Rieklings will [ambush](Skyrim_Riekling_Barrel.md) you when you approach, popping out of the barrels and the snow. The remaining barrels contain foodstuffs. Using the [Aura Whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) shout will reveal the Rieklings' locations before they emerge; using this you can strike them before they burst from the snow. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=24683&y=61231&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Riekling Chest
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/SR-place-Another_Random_Chest.jpg/200px-SR-place-Another_Random_Chest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Another_Random_Chest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Another_Random_Chest.jpg) Another random chest Follow the shoreline southwest of [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) to find a novice-locked wooden chest covered in riekling painting and with a ring of [riekling spears](Skyrim_Riekling_Spear.md) sticking from the sides of it against the cliff side. It is approximately an equal distance between the landing and [Bristleback Cave](Skyrim_Bristleback_Cave.md), on the northwestern corner of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). There are two [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md) on the shore in front of it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=19550&y=89100&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Riekling Outpost Island
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-place-Riekling_Island.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riekling_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riekling_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riekling_Island.jpg) Riekling Island On a small island in the far northeastern corner of the map, northwest of [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md) and north of [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md), there is a small tribe of [Rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md). There are two spiky grass plants which yield [ashen grass pods](Skyrim_Ashen_Grass_Pod.md), five [pearl oysters](Skyrim_Pearl_Oyster.md), and three [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) plants on the shore of the island, with the Riekling camp at the center. On the east side of the camp is a small tent with an unlocked chest and two sacks inside and a knapsack just outside. Near the northwest Riekling structure is another unlocked chest, a few sacks, and a coin purse. Beside the chest is another small tent which contains a sack and a satchel. At the very top of the south Riekling structure is an East Empire Company strongbox, a knapsack, and a coin purse. Below the top structure among the rocks is a final chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=66899&y=92629&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Spiky Grass Island
[![SR-place-Spiky Grass Island.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-place-Spiky_Grass_Island.jpg/200px-SR-place-Spiky_Grass_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Spiky_Grass_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Spiky_Grass_Island.jpg) A tiny island with two dying trees, four [spiky grass](Skyrim_Spiky_Grass.md) and a nirnroot.

### The Ever-Anchored Ship
[![SR-place-Everanchored Ship.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-place-Everanchored_Ship.jpg/200px-SR-place-Everanchored_Ship.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Everanchored_Ship.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Everanchored_Ship.jpg) This ship and two rowboats on the shore can be found before the Water Stone is cleansed, with the crew being bound to it. You cannot board the ship, and it will disappear after freeing the crew.

### The Fisherman's Haul
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-place-Fishing_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fishing_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fishing_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fishing_Camp.jpg) Fishing Camp Southwest of [Damphall Mine](Skyrim_Damphall_Mine.md), you'll find a small fishing camp with a [bristleback boar](Skyrim_Bristleback.md). The camp is mostly bare, consisting of a fishing rod, a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f), one bottle each of [matze](Skyrim_Matze.md) and [shein](Skyrim_Shein.md), one crate of dead [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and one of [ash yams](Skyrim_Ash_Yam.md) and two [fish barrels](Skyrim_Containers.md#Fish_Barrel). You can also fish here with the fishing supplies. There is also an empty fishing boat nearby. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=21802&y=53072&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Waterfall and Grazing Netch
[![SR-place-Waterfall Netches.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-place-Waterfall_Netches.jpg/200px-SR-place-Waterfall_Netches.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Waterfall_Netches.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Waterfall_Netches.jpg) A herd of Netch can be found at the base of this waterfall that flows next to the Water Stone, along with a nirnroot, spiky grass and clams.

## Southern Ash Lands

### Ashfallow Reaver Camp
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-place-Reaver_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Reaver_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reaver_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reaver_Camp.jpg) Reaver Camp Across the water west of [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md), you'll find a small camp inhabited by two [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md). The camp consists of a fire with two hanging pots—one of which contains a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) —a chair, an apprentice-locked chest, and a sheltered bedroll. An East Empire Company strongbox can be found next to the bedroll. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=61902&y=40264&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Basalt Causeway
[![SR-place-Basalt Causeway.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-place-Basalt_Causeway.jpg/200px-SR-place-Basalt_Causeway.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Basalt_Causeway.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Basalt_Causeway.jpg) This is a tiny outcrop of basalt columns west of Tel Mithryn, and east of the mouth of the Harstrad River. During the quest Deathbrand, four or five Ash Spawn have slain the [two Treasure Hunters](Skyrim_Treasure_Hunter_(dead).md) who uncovered the chest at this location. Pick the expert-leveled lock on the chest and claim the Deathbrand Boots.

### Bloodied Grove
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-place-Ash_Spawn_Ambush.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ash_Spawn_Ambush.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ash_Spawn_Ambush.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ash_Spawn_Ambush.jpg) Ash Spawn Ambush Northwest of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md), upstream from where [Revus Sarvani](Skyrim_Revus_Sarvani.md) tends to Dusty the [silt strider](Skyrim_Silt_Strider.md), you will find a dead [reaver](Skyrim_Reaver.md) leaning up against a tree next to an unlocked chest, with three lootable skeletons scattered about. As you approach, three leveled [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) will emerge from the ground and attack. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=64316.6&y=33364.5&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Dragon Mound: Temple Foothills
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-place-Isinfier_Dragon_Mound.jpg/200px-SR-place-Isinfier_Dragon_Mound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isinfier_Dragon_Mound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isinfier_Dragon_Mound.jpg) Isinfier Dragon Mound A [dragon burial mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md) can be found directly west of [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md), atop some rocks. There is also a novice-locked chest just south of the mound, up the hill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=61958&y=48899&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Driftwood Isle
[![SR-place-Driftwood Isle.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-place-Driftwood_Isle.jpg/200px-SR-place-Driftwood_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Driftwood_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Driftwood_Isle.jpg) This small tidal island can be found to the very west of [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md) and north of the Isle of Basalt. A lone tree can be found here alongside some driftwood and 3 samples of spiky grass.

### Dunmeri Camp
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-place-Dark_Elf_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dark_Elf_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dark_Elf_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dark_Elf_Camp.jpg) Dark Elf Camp Far north of the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md), southwest of [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md) (just east of [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md) if you have started [Lost Legacy](Skyrim_Lost_Legacy.md)), you'll find a small camp where two [Dark Elves](Skyrim_Dark_Elf_(dead).md) lie dead. The camp is mostly bare, consisting of a chest, a pot over a dry firepit, a barrel, and a knapsack, all of which are empty. The tent, however, contains an East Empire Company strongbox, as well as several bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md). You may encounter [Rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md) nearby. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=73573&y=49533&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Gold Miners' Floodgate
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-place-Usha%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Usha%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Usha%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Usha%27s_Camp.jpg) Usha's Camp Follow the stream west of [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md) north to a barred gate. You can actually unbar the gate from the outside by aiming at the gap between the gates, but you won't be able to climb up onto the platforms once inside. It's tricky, but possible, to reach the lowermost platform by moving as far upstream as you can, facing the platform, and simultaneously pressing forward and jump. Instead of climbing the platforms inside, climb the mountain on the side and jump down to the wooden structure behind the gate. [Usha](Skyrim_Usha.md) lies dead on a bedroll with a few empty skooma bottles strewn about his side. On the table next to him is a lantern, three bottles of skooma, a coin purse, and a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_to_Usha.md) from his lover. On another table, you'll find six loose [gold ores](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md). To the right of this table is an unlocked chest with a pickaxe sitting on top, and an East Empire Company strongbox in front of it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=65438&y=47265&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Hrodulf's Last Stand
[![SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg) Located on the southern tip of Solstheim, south of Hrodulf's House. [Hrodulf](Skyrim_Hrodulf.md) is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md), he was presumably killed by the [burnt spriggan](Skyrim_Burnt_Spriggan.md) standing over his body.

Next to Hrodulf's body is an apprentice-locked strong box and a chest, both of which contain a decent amount of loot. There is an [amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md) and a [gold diamond ring](Skyrim_Gold_Diamond_Ring.md) in the boat resting on one of the wharfs.

### Impact Crater
[![SR-place-Impact Crater.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-place-Impact_Crater.jpg/200px-SR-place-Impact_Crater.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Impact_Crater.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Impact_Crater.jpg) Two burnt spriggans are ready to fight if you tread on their ashen territory-an impact crater with a piece of molten rock from Red Mountain, still glowing. Two heart stone deposits can also be found here.

### Impact Crater (Tel Mithryn)
[![SR-place-Impact Crater 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-place-Impact_Crater_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Impact_Crater_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Impact_Crater_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Impact_Crater_02.jpg) This impact crater has flame cloaked spiders and jumping flame spiders surrounding it, alongside burning trees. A heart stone deposit can also be found here.

### Isle of Basalt
[![SR-place-Isle of Basalt.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-place-Isle_of_Basalt.jpg/200px-SR-place-Isle_of_Basalt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isle_of_Basalt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isle_of_Basalt.jpg) A small island west of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) that offers little but a great view of the southern parts of Solstheim. With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, [green](Skyrim_Green_Butterfly.md) and [purple butterflies](Skyrim_Purple_Butterfly.md) and [bliss bugs](Skyrim_Bliss_Bug.md) can be found flying around the basalt columns during the day.

As part of the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath.md) Creation, you will be sent here during the quest [Put A Fork In It](Skyrim_Put_A_Fork_In_It.md) to kill a [bull netch](Skyrim_Bull_Netch.md).

### Lurker Ambush (Ashfallow Dunes)
[![SR-place-Lurker Ambush 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-place-Lurker_Ambush_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lurker_Ambush_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lurker_Ambush_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lurker_Ambush_02.jpg) As you complete the Dragonborn Main Quest, Lurkers begin to appear across Solstheim, usually close to the coast. Just southeast of Ashfallow Citadel, on the sand dunes north of the Sun Stone, is an area in which a Lurker can spawn.

### Lurker Ambush (Tel Mithryn Peninsula)
West of the Sun Stone, in a small bay immediately north of the small graveyard, two lurkers wait to arise from the sea. It can be difficult to trigger their emergence. A detect life spell will show them (in blue) below the surface of the water.

### Miner Immolation
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-place-Miner%27s_End.jpg/200px-SR-place-Miner%27s_End.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Miner%27s_End.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Miner%27s_End.jpg) Miner's End Southeast of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md), near the shore, you can find two levels of terrace. Two ash spawn will come out of the ground and attack you. You can find a [heart stone deposit](Skyrim_Mining.md#Deposits) and a burnt corpse on each terrace. In addition, you can find a knapsack on the lower level, along with a pickaxe and a bottle of flin on the upper level. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=79025&y=21232&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Reavers' Folly
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-place-Ash_Hopper_Island.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ash_Hopper_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ash_Hopper_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ash_Hopper_Island.jpg) Ash Hopper Island South of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md), you'll find a small island with some crumbling ruins, guarded by three [ash hoppers](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper.md). The island features a small camp with two bloodied bed rolls (one of which can be used to sleep), a strongbox, a chest, an apothecary's satchel, a box with two bowls of [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) and a pair of [bonemold gauntlets](Skyrim_Bonemold_Gauntlets.md), a food barrel, and two dead [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md). There is a small boat on the southern side of the island, with a sack inside and a pickaxe on a crate nearby. Scattered throughout the island are three spiky grass plants which yield standard [grass pods](Skyrim_Grass_Pod.md), as well as one [heart stone](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) deposit. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=27160&y=23006&zoom=16&legacy=true)

There is a knapsack, an apothecary's satchel, and a [ruby geode](Skyrim_Ruby_Geode.md) next to the stump. ### The Miner's Lament
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Miner_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Miner_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Miner_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Miner_Camp.jpg) Miner Camp Directly west of [Brodir Grove](Skyrim_Brodir_Grove.md), you can find a small camp where an unfortunate [Miner](Skyrim_Miner.md) met his demise. He lies beneath a wagon and some loose rocks and four [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) ores. Next to his nearby tent is a crate holding a strongbox, an [emerald](Skyrim_Emerald.md), an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md), and two bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md). Three more bottles of Nord mead are in an overturned bucket in front of the tent, along with a pair of boots. Just east of the miner's body are two emerald geodes and a gold ore vein behind them, and to the north and west of his body is a gold ore vein and two creep clusters which yield [ash creep clusters](Skyrim_Ash_Creep_Cluster.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=37533&y=44560&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Wilderness Shrine of Kynareth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-place-Wilderness_Shrine_of_Kynareth.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wilderness_Shrine_of_Kynareth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wilderness_Shrine_of_Kynareth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wilderness_Shrine_of_Kynareth.jpg) Shrine of Kynareth Travel to [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md). Turn around and follow the coast south until you see a downed tree to your left. It will be near a large patch of grass, an [ash creep cluster](Skyrim_Ash_Creep_Cluster.md), and three [scathecraw](Skyrim_Scathecraw.md). There should be a small gap between the tree and the rock just to its right (west). The [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) should be almost directly south from your position. If you look south into the rocky hills, you will see a trail marker cairn. Head toward the marker to find a second trail marker cairn; look a few feet east of that second cairn to find the shrine on top of a lantern. Beside the shrine are an [amulet of Kynareth](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Kynareth.md), [flin](Skyrim_Flin.md), two [trama roots](Skyrim_Trama_Root.md), three [ash yams](Skyrim_Ash_Yam.md), and an [albino spider pod](Skyrim_Albino_Spider_Pod.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=23822&y=39380&zoom=16&legacy=true)
