**Conjure Zombie** is a Novice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Plague of the Dead](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Effects
- [Conjure Zombie](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy), Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health with 100.
