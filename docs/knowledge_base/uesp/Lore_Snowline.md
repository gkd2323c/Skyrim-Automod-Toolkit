[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SK-render-Snowline.png/200px-SK-render-Snowline.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-render-Snowline.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-render-Snowline.png) Snowline circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397) Snowline is a tiny village located high in the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), on the border of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is located in the [Bend'r-mahk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bend%27r-mahk) region of the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md). The mountainous snowy landscape surrounding Snowline is noted for the number of [herbs](Lore_Flora.md) and [crystal](Lore_Crystals.md) deposits found there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SK-1)</sup>

## History
Circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397), [goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) and bandit raids on the village made the area dangerous for Snowline's inhabitants.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> During the [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md), agents of Hammerfell and Skyrim were both stationed in Snowline in order to investigate the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md)'s position on the war.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> In truth, the Empire was in the midst of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) and was under [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md)'s control. Tharn was secretly fanning both sides of the conflict in order to create an [Umbra' Keth](Lore_Umbra%27_Keth.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
