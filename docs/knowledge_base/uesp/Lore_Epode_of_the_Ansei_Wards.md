Epode of the Ansei Wards **by [Weltan of Sentinel](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Weltan)** *A poem on the self-sacrifice of three Ansei*
---
When [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) came from Old [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md)
In [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) were found
[Men of aspect tusked](Lore_Orc.md) and fearsome, that we
Hunted to the ground

[Others](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) there were also, [Elven](Lore_Mer.md), secret
Raising sacred dead
[Necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) lurked in desert towers
To Hammerfell they'd fled

Desert hid them no more from the righteous
Since the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) hie
Back they fought with evil [magics](Lore_Magic.md), heinous
Dead once more to die

Stricken were Ra Gada in their god-faith
Banned from striking kin
Dead, awakened by the wicked wizards
Smote us from within

[Anseis](Lore_Ansei.md) three then furnished all an answer
'Gainst the risen dead
Maja, Radan, and Halelah spake of
Wards to curb the dread

[Mighty swords they forged](Lore_Ansei_Wards.md) from their own [shehai](Lore_Shehai.md)
The people to defend
Guarding Redguards' consecrated bodies
From unholy end

To [Tu'whacca](Lore_Tu%27whacca.md) sacrificed the Anseis
Essence of their minds
Gave their animus to seal the treaty
That protects and binds
