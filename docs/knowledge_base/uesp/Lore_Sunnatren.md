# Sunnatren

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/ON-crown_store-Umaril%27s_Radiance_Elk.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Umaril%27s_Radiance_Elk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Umaril%27s_Radiance_Elk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Umaril%27s_Radiance_Elk.jpg) Umaril's Radiance Elk, a mount used in battle against the Sunnatren *The Unfeathered sought victory over the Sunnatren. 'Their banners portray crude elks. Let them tremble as we defeat them on such beasts.' A season ended before I finished my work. May they forever remain loyal to him.*
—Gandravanya's Journal, translated<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-URE-1)</sup> The Sunnatren were a group or culture of which little is known. They were enemies of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md), and the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) Sorcerer-King [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril.md) sought their defeat. The Sunnatren were known to fly banners that portrayed crude [elks](Lore_Elk.md); as a result, Umaril had elks trained as war-mounts to be used against them, hoping that his forces could inspire fear atop such beasts. The elks took a season to train,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-URE-1)</sup> though the result of the campaign against the Sunnatren is unknown.
