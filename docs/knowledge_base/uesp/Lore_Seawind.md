Seawind was a province of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RHH-1)</sup> known for its grand port city of Arch.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RHH-1)</sup>

## History
Little is known of the Seawind's history. At some point, shortly after the [War of the Singers](Lore_War_of_the_Singers.md), [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) led the remnants of his [Ansei](Lore_Ansei.md) followers to the grand port city of Arch in the province of Seawind. There, Hunding had a fleet of ships prepared. The singers departed from their desert homeland, eventually journeying eastward toward [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RHH-1)</sup>

The state of Seawind or its remnants after the sinking of a major portion of Yokuda in [1E 792](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_792) is unknown.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-2)</sup> Writings by [Destri Melarg](Lore_Destri_Melarg.md), a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) scholar, make brief mention of the province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RHH-1)</sup>
