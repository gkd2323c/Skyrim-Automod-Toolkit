# Cheese

*Wonderful! Time for a celebration... Cheese for everyone! Wait, scratch that. Cheese for no one. That can be just as much of a celebration, if you don't like cheese. True? You've run a maze like a good little rat, but no cheese for you yet.*
—Sheogorath<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBSheogorath-1)</sup> [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/ON-place-Cheese_Shelf.png/200px-ON-place-Cheese_Shelf.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cheese_Shelf.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cheese_Shelf.png) A variety of cheeses Cheese ([Yoku](Lore_Yoku.md): *Mlou*)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA.27r-2)</sup> is a dairy product used as [food](Lore_Cuisine.md). It is made when milk ferments and is pressed into a more solid product.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA.27r-2)</sup> The milk used to create cheese can come from various mammalian animals, such as [cows](Lore_Cow.md), [echatere](Lore_Echatere.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBul-3)</sup> [goats](Lore_Goat.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MJ-4)</sup> [mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md), [sheep](Lore_Sheep.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Uuras-5)</sup> [giant bats](Lore_Giant_Bat.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LHALAYQ-6)</sup> and even rodents.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Os Cs-7)</sup> It may be consumed as part of simple meals with soup, bread, and tea,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KE:IX-8)</sup> or with wine in more refined meals.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAA3-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Scroll Caan-10)</sup> Cheese has [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) properties, and can be used in potions to fortify willpower, resist paralysis and fire, restore fatigue, or in poisons to damage luck and agility.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBCheese Wedge-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-OBCheese Wheel-12)</sup>

It can come in many shapes, such as truckles called cheese wheels,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-OBCheese Wheel-12)</sup> or liquid forms called cheese dips,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-13)</sup> and can be stored in barrels and crates.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SN-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ARI-15)</sup> Giant [rats](Lore_Rat.md) consume cheese and may infest areas containing it,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ARI-15)</sup> sometimes being lured by its strong scent.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-BWSheogorath-16)</sup> Rats can be subject to experiments where they explore mazes to find a piece of cheese.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBSheogorath-1)</sup> Cheese wheels laced with poison are sometimes used to lure and kill [skeevers](Lore_Skeever.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/BT-Cheeses_of_Tamriel_Trailer.png/200px-BT-Cheeses_of_Tamriel_Trailer.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-Cheeses_of_Tamriel_Trailer.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-Cheeses_of_Tamriel_Trailer.png) A spread of various cheeses and fruit [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) County is famous for its wines and cheeses.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Gt S-18)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-OBDion-19)</sup> The Border Watch Inn can be found in the town of [Borderwatch](Lore_Borderwatch.md) and is famed for its large collection of cheese, including the rare Olroy Cheese.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-BWSheogorath-16)</sup> [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) nobles may have multiple cheese courses at long dinner parties.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Bi W-20)</sup>

The [Blue Palace](Lore_Blue_Palace.md) in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) employs a royal cheesemonger, a hereditary office. Milk is regularly brought to the palace for inspection and the cheesemonger has exact standards for producing Solitude Eidar.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-cheeses-21)</sup>

[Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of madness, is quite fond of cheese and will often cater celebrations with it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBSheogorath-1)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-22)</sup> His followers are subject to many insane conspiracies involving cheese,<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-23)</sup> such as the price of cheese being connected to the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md),<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-OBMuurine-24)</sup> or [Thadon](Lore_Thadon.md) faking his death to live underground in a house made of cheese.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-OBKishashi-25)</sup> Sometimes they simply hoard or carry large amounts of cheese.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-26)</sup>

[Cheesemancy](Lore_Cheesemancy.md) is an arcane art focused on the manipulation of cheese. Those who practice this art are known as Cheesemancers. While it has a loose connection to the mad inhabitants of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md),<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Cheesemancer-27)</sup> its existence is recognized among the common people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-SG-28)</sup><sup>:139-140</sup>

The cheese is brined and pressed within these baskets, with the latter process imbuing the rind of the normally cream-colored cheese with a verdant hue. The consumption of Greenedge, customarily served at the end of feasts, is something of a spectacle. Young hunters make a game of holding the rush-encased cheese over a flaming torch until its casing burns away and the cheese transforms into a near-molten state. ### Eidar Scrolls
**The Eidar Scrolls** are cheese-based prophecy-recipes. As a mockery of the [moth priests](Lore_Moth_Priests.md), when the prophecy cheeses are consumed, the results are said to reveal the future, at the cost of one's sanity.

*The Eidar Scrolls: The Merethic Cheeses, Vol 2* is a sealed treatise on the creation and consumption of eldritch cheeses and ingredients.

The [Order of the Eye](Lore_Order_of_the_Eye.md) believed there are hidden patterns among the recipes, and hunted the Scrolls down.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-43)</sup>

### King Jorunn's Automatic Cheesewheel Vivisector
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-item-King_Jorunn%27s_Automatic_Cheesewheel_Vivisector.jpg/100px-ON-item-King_Jorunn%27s_Automatic_Cheesewheel_Vivisector.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-King_Jorunn%27s_Automatic_Cheesewheel_Vivisector.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-King_Jorunn%27s_Automatic_Cheesewheel_Vivisector.jpg) King Jorunn's Automatic Cheesewheel Vivisector **King Jorunn's Automatic Cheesewheel Vivisector** is a cheese-cutting machine that [Jorunn the Skald-King](Lore_Jorunn.md) commissioned to be created for a banquet, with the central component of the device being a Dwarven crescent blade. It served as Skyrim's one and only machine powered cheese slicer.<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-44)</sup><sup>[[43]](#cite_note-45)</sup> The blade was kept in the Mages Guild in Windhelm before being stolen by the Thieves Guild.

## Notes
- Citizens of Tamriel, especially [Nords](Lore_Nord.md),<sup>[[44]](#cite_note-MWDialog-46)</sup> may carry pieces of cheese on their person.<sup>[[45]](#cite_note-DBo A-47)</sup>
- [Scuttle](Lore_Scuttle.md) is a local dish on Vvardenfell that is similar to cheese and made from the flesh of local beetles.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-TAF-48)</sup> It can be cooked alongside saltrice and cheese to make a dish called scuttlerice.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-Scuttlerice-49)</sup> Fancy taverns may also serve cheeses and scuttle alongside each other with pan roasted oysters, kwama crisps, and smoked slaughterfish.<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-50)</sup>
