[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockmaster.jpg/200px-SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockmaster.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockmaster.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockmaster.jpg) Dockmaster The **East Empire Dockmaster** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) found in the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md). He loiters around the warehouse all the time, eating and sleeping at random times.

He wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c), along with some [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d) and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#0004223b). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
The Dockmaster will simply greet you with:

*"Good day to you."* *"Do you have business with the East Empire Company?"* *"I will thank you not to interrupt me in my tasks."*
## Notes
- Similar to his [dockworkers](Skyrim_East_Empire_Dockworker.md), he does not get named in-game, although his ref bears the name **Regulus Naevius**, implying he was once meant to be named.
- It seems that the Dockmaster was at one point intended to be a backup for [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md)'s role as East Empire Company vendor. The `Dialogue Solitude` quest has an alias denoting him as such, but he has no other traces that this was ever followed up on, as he has no relevant factions, AI, or anything else. He also has the same lines as Vittoria, recorded in his voice type, for use in two scenes involving her that were meant to play at the Solitude docks, which he never goes to.
