# Golden Mask of Devotion

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Indoril_Helmet.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Indoril_Helmet.png) Golden Mask of Devotion (Skyrim) *So long as she wears the mask, the Ordinator is considered a faultless hand of the Three, and is authorized to commit any martial action deemed necessary to protect the True Faith. *
—High Ordinator Vermethys Indoril<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AGTLV-1)</sup> Golden Masks of Devotion are a type of [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) full-face mask made of [gold](Lore_Gold.md) and chitin,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IH2-2)</sup> reserved for use by the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AGTLV-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OE7-3)</sup> By [material](Lore_Metals.md), they are informally referred to as the **golden masks**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HZ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TTIS-5)</sup> or **golden helms**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HZ-4)</sup> By association with the Order that wears them, they are known as **Ordinator masks**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MOM-6)</sup> **Ordinator helms**,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DS-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MQI-8)</sup> or **Ordinator helmets**,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CM49-9)</sup> They are also called **Indoril helmets**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IH2-2)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-IH-10)</sup> or **Indoril masks**,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-IDM-11)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup> after the style associated with the Order.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Golden Masks of Devotion were considered sacred,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Her Hands Dialogue-13)</sup> as they were official symbols honoring [Saint](Lore_Temple_Saints.md) [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup> They were believed to depict his face,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-IDM-11)</sup> and according to some, represented the only true depiction.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg/200px-MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-misc-Anniversary_Art_2024.jpg) Indoril Nerevar *Justice wears but one face, and so the stern visage of Temple justice is stamped upon the faceplate of every Militant Ordinator helm. Above is our soaring forward crest, pointing toward heresy, which we always attack and from which we never retreat. Three Gods, One True Faith. *
—High Ordinator Vermethys Indoril<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CM49-9)</sup> Symbolically, in addition to honoring Saint Nerevar,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-IDM-11)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup> Golden Masks of Devotion symbolized the unity of Justice, showing that it bears only a single, unyielding face. Each Ordinator helm bore the stern countenance of Temple justice on its faceplate, topped by a soaring crest that forever pointed toward heresy.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CM49-9)</sup>

Donning the Golden Mask of Devotion was the final step in the proper vesting procedure of the Ordinators, aside from the equipping of weapons. Before the mask could be worn, it first had to be anointed with mineral oil. The Ordinator then pressed their forehead to the helm to anoint themselves, and only after this ritual did they don the mask. From that moment, all actions taken by the Ordinator were considered protected by divine mandate. So long as they wore the mask, they were regarded as a faultless hand of the Three and was authorized to undertake any martial action deemed necessary to protect the Tribunal Temple.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AGTLV-1)</sup> Donning the mask by a non-Ordinator was considered an act of blasphemy and a clear violation of the Seventeenth Dictum of Piety. By law, any member of the Dunmer laity who wore it was subject to swift and merciless sanction.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OE7-3)</sup> Ordinators were further authorized to immediately execute any non-Dunmer found wearing the mask.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OE7-3)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-IH-10)</sup>

While the masks of the [Hands of Almalexia](Lore_Hands_of_Almalexia.md) differed somewhat in design and material, they carried comparable,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Her Hands Helmets-14)</sup> if not greater significance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AGTLV-1)</sup> The consequences for wearing them were equally severe.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Her Hands Dialogue-13)</sup>

The practice of designing and wearing masks modeled after important figures in Dunmeri culture was not uncommon.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-DFO-15)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-DFTDE-16)</sup> While the Golden Mask of Devotion was made from gold,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TTIS-5)</sup> there were many other masks in Dunmeri culture, some mundane,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOMAsks-17)</sup> and some [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md). The latter were known as [Daedric Faces](Lore_Daedric_Faces.md). One of them, the Daedric Face of Terror represented Nerevar's more terrible aspect as Hortator and [Padomaic](Lore_Padomay.md) champion.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DFOT-18)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-DFTDE-16)</sup>
