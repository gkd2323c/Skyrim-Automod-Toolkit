## Notes
- The Venomfang Skeever is encountered during the [Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits) quest in [Honningbrew Basement](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Basement).
- Skeevers may be present in the [cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cellar) addition of Hearthfire homes. Any NPCs that reside in your house may mention that they heard odd noises coming from the basement. [One of these skeevers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cellar#Notes) is sometimes non-aggressive and may be spared to become a pet.<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- [Adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) male [children](Skyrim_Child.md) may also bring home and adopt a unique skeever, [Biter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Biter).<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- [Corpulus Vinius](Skyrim_Corpulus_Vinius.md), the owner of [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), claims that skeevers weren't always as large as they are now.
- A [greater power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Power) named [Polymorph Skeever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Polymorph_Skeever) exists in game data which would have allowed you to transform into a skeever. However, the power is bugged and not found in the game.
