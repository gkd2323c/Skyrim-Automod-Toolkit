Amaund Motierre was a member of the [Motierre](Lore_Motierre.md) family,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AM-1)</sup> a very old and powerful [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) family firmly established in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Babette-2)</sup> The family used its influence to secure ties to the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md). He took part in the conspiracy to assassinate Emperor [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AM-1)</sup>

## History
Around [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Amaund took part in a conspiracy with the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) to assassinate Emperor Titus Mede II, using an amulet specifically crafted for Elder Council members to pay for expenses.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DRSilence-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Delvin-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AMSL-5)</sup> He performed the Black Sacrament and then contacted the Brotherhood's agent. Alongside his loyal bodyguard, Rexus, who had pledged to serve Amaund Motierre until his final breath, he traveled to [Volunruud](Lore_Volunruud.md), where he sealed the deal with the Brotherhood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AM-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Rexus-6)</sup> The Dark Brotherhood speculated he was motivated by a desire to gain more power.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Astrid-7)</sup>

He asserted, however, that in [3E 41](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_41), Emperor [Pelagius Septim I](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_I.md) was murdered in the [Temple of the One](Lore_Temple_of_the_One.md) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), struck down by a Dark Brotherhood assassin. His death brought about a crucial shift in Imperial policy. He compared the current situation to one from the early [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and stated that there are those who seek a similar change.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AM-1)</sup>
