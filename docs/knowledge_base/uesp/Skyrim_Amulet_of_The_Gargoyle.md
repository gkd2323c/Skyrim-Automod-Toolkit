[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-item-Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.jpg) Amulet of The Gargoyle The Amulet of The Gargoyle is an [amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet) retrieved as part of the [Amulets of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest. The chain of the amulet is made of small bones and vertebra, with a small animal skull with green jewels covering its eye sockets serving as a pendant. Its enchantment has no effect when you are in human form, but causes your [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) ability [Summon Gargoyle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Gargoyle) to summon two [gargoyles](Skyrim_Gargoyle.md) instead of one.

## Bugs
- The amulet can be enchanted again despite already having an enchantment. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.23, fixes this bug.
- The additional Gargoyle summoned by this amulet will advance progress for the Vampire Lord when killed by the player.
