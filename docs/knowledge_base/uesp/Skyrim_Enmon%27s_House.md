## Enmon's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House.jpg) Ground floor The ground floor is used as the kitchen, dining, and storage area of the home. A non-respawning dresser immediately to the right of the door holds three [potions of enhanced stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina) and two [pickaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). A respawning food barrel sits in the northwest corner, with a well stocked set of shelves to its left which holds a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), and two bottles each of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on the top shelf, while the middle shelf holds three [eidar cheese wheels](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md) and three [goat cheese wheels](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), and the bottom shelf holds a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md) and six [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). To the left of these shelves is a non-respawning cupboard holding three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and three bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on the top shelf and one bottle each of wine and alto wine below. Sitting before the cupboard and shelves is a dining table set with three loaves of bread, two [goat cheese wedges](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), three bottles of alto wine, and two servings of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat).

On the southern wall, some hanging foodstuffs can be found on the right side, including two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, some dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), and two portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). The fireplace is situated to the left, with two bottles of Nord mead on the mantel and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) at its base. Hanging to the left above the fireplace are four freshly caught [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). A small round table near the fireplace holds a goat cheese wedge, a bottle of Nord mead, a loaf of bread, and a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). Towards the southeast corner, beneath the stairs, a set of shelves holds three [silver ores](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) on the top shelf, an additional pickaxe and five [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on the middle shelf, and a copy of the book *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)* along with three pairs of miner [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000e3445) on the bottom shelf. A respawning food barrel sits on each side of the base of the stairs.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Enmon%27s_House_02.jpg) Upstairs Going upstairs will lead to the bedroom area of the home. Along the south wall, a set of shelves can be found holding an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) and various kitchenware. Directly ahead, on the western wall, is a double bed (belonging to the [Karthwasten Enmons House Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten Enmons House Faction)) with a small non-respawning chest resting at its foot. A small table to the right holds five gold coins, and a non-respawning dresser to the left holds three bottles of Nord mead and a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe). A door leads to the balcony of the house on the north wall, and a single square table with a chair is situated just to the right of it.

## Notes
- The front door and balcony door both have a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Enmon%27s_House) to which is carried by both Enmon and Mena.
- The balcony, which is accessible through the upstairs area of the home, hosts a bench which holds two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md).
- During [The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md), the doors to Enmon's House will lock if night falls while inside it. Unlocking the door at this point is considered a crime. Waiting until morning causes the door to unlock.
