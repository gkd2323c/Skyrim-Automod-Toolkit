# Yneslea

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |

Yneslea is a region of land that lies somewhere between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> It was conquered by [Uriel Septim V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_V) in [3E 279](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_279) during the opening stages of his campaign against the Dragon Land.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> By [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288), unrest had broken out, causing the emperor to divert two legions that had been intended as reinforcements to pacify the inhabitants instead, ultimately contributing to the failure of the invasion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup>
