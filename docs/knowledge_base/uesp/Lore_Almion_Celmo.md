**Archmage** Almion Celmo was a renowned mage and [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) from the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), and the creator of the [Ice Blade of the Monarch](Lore_Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.md).

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/MW-item-Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.jpg/200px-MW-item-Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.jpg) The Ice Blade of the Monarch Little is known of Almion Celmo's background and past. They hailed from the Summerset Isles and was a highly regarded mage, holding the title of Archmage. Celmo created the [Ice Blade of the Monarch](Lore_Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.md), a claymore enchanted with the soul of a [Frost Monarch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frost_Monarch), a stronger variant of the [Frost Atronach](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-L2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-L3-3)</sup> It was known to cause serious frost damage and freeze anyone who feels its blade.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-M1-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-M2-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-L4-6)</sup>

The Ice Blade of the Monarch was intended for use in the assassination of a king of a distant land by a warrior Thurgnarr Assi, who sought to become the ruler of that realm. However, the attempt failed, leading to Thurgnarr Assi's death and Almion Celmo's imprisonment.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-M1-4)</sup> According to certain sources, including the writings of the last living [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), [Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the Archmage was as an evil individual.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-L2-2)</sup>

The weapon was rumored to have a mind of its own as it passed through the hands of various malefactors without settling in one place for long.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-M1-4)</sup> The Ice Blade of the Monarch appeared in both [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md) and [Fourth](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) eras, respectively.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-M3-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRCW-8)</sup>
