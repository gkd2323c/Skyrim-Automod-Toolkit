*Listen, knave, and cease your prattle. My Lord, Jagar Tharn, the mortal Emperor of Tamriel, and your Lord Dagon are ordering affairs high above your counsel. Tell someone in authority that I MUST speak with his Lordship.*
—Sirran Angada (listen Link ⏵) Sirran Angada was a mortal noble<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSAngada-2)</sup> and leechcrafter retainer of [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSBittor-3)</sup> who was instrumental in the fall of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSAngada-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Battlespire-4)</sup> Angada used a special Transmorph spell<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSBittor-3)</sup> to take on the appearance of [Lomegan Mariel](Lore_Lomegan_Mariel.md), the Emperor's Personal Liaison to the Battlespire,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSBittor-3)</sup> which allowed them to slip past the [battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-JTL-5)</sup>

Once inside the Battlespire, Angada bribed one of the Battlemages, [Paxti Bittor](Lore_Paxti_Bittor.md), to open the portal which allowed [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s forces to attack the facility. Once Dagon's forces had taken over, Angada was assigned to read the vagaries of the magicka tides for Lord Dagon. Angada may or may not have been slain for their treason against the Empire by an [apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) who had been trapped in the Battlespire during the attack.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSAngada-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSLevel6-6)</sup>

## Notes
- An unused response from a [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) in *[Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Battlespire)* indicates that Sirran Angada bore the title of "**Syndic**" under Jagar Tharn's [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dremora Talk-7)</sup> In English, "syndic" is an uncommon noun referring to a government magistrate.<sup>[[LANG 1]](#cite_note-wiktionary-8)</sup>
- Sirran Angada is referred to by both feminine<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSZN-9)</sup> and masculine<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSBittor-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-KRP-10)</sup> pronouns in different places. That latter may be due to Angada posing as Lomegan Mariel (a male character) during and before the events of the game—Paxti Bittor's dialogue hints toward this.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSBittor-3)</sup>
