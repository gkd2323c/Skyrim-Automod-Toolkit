# Wabbajack

This article is about the artifact. For the book, see [Wabbajack (book)](Lore_Wabbajack_(book).md).

The Wabbajack is a Daedric [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). It takes the appearance of a Daedric [staff](Lore_Staff.md) that is usually engraved with angry or sad gaping faces at the top. As befits the Prince of Madness, his artifact is unpredictable in its effects. The staff has the power to transform its target into a completely random creature. This can be helpful to the wielder, turning a fearsome opponent into a docile animal; similarly, it can be detrimental, transforming a relatively weak enemy into a powerful monster.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFWabbajack-1)</sup> The staff can produce other effects, such as damaging, healing, turning to stone, or instantly killing the target.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRWabbajack-2)</sup> The staff's victims that are turned into animals, and are cooked after the fact, become the meat known as Wabbajerky. The meat has created debates on whether eating it would be considered cannibalism.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wabbajerky-3)</sup> Sheogorath finds only a few worthy of Wabbajack's "absurdly sweet power".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOChaos Magic-4)</sup>

## History
Sometime in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the staff came into the possession of Prince Maleel al-Akir, a formidable [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) warrior. In death, Maleel was entombed in the necropolis of Asakala in the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) along with his generals and army. During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), the dead of Asakala rose from their graves due to the ongoing conflict in the region. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Arch-Mage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) and the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) recovered the Wabbajack from Maleel, who guarded it fiercely even in undeath. The Vestige was then commanded to use the staff on denizens of [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) as part of Sheogorath's trials.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSM-5)</sup>

Sometime either before or after the start of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), a member of the Outer Watch encountered the Wabbajack wielded by a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) cultist. They are thought to have banished the staff, or decided to flee but suffered from its transformation effects.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Cyrod Gazetteer-6)</sup>

An anonymous author wrote about how he obtained the staff sometime shortly after he ascended the throne of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) in the book *[Wabbajack](Lore_Wabbajack_(book).md)*. In his search for the [Oghma Infinium](Lore_Oghma_Infinium.md), the author was tricked into summoning Sheogorath, believing him to be [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md). After serving the prince, Sheogorath's servant gave him the Wabbajack.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-W-7)</sup>

In the events leading up to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), an [unknown agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) summoned Sheogorath and dispatched a battlemage who had displeased the Prince. In return, the agent was given the Wabbajack from a worshipper of Sheogorath.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFSheogorath-8)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Sheogorath was aided by the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) in tormenting the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) settlement of [Border Watch](Lore_Border_Watch.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The inhabitants were very superstitious, and were driven into a state of frenzy when the Prince simulated their K'sharra Prophecy, said to mark the end of the world. These occurrences were reported in a special edition of the Black Horse Courier newspaper entitled *Rain of Burning Dogs!*.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBSheogorath-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ROBD-10)</sup> It is unknown whether this took place before or after the Hero assumed the position of Sheogorath; regardless, the Hero received the Wabbajack from either Sheogorath or [Haskill](Lore_Haskill.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBSheogorath-9)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Shivering-11)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) unknowingly entered the mind of the dead [Pelagius Septim III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md), tasked with convincing Sheogorath to return to the Shivering Isles. He agreed to return, but only if the Dragonborn could escape Pelagius' mind. Sheogorath decided to assist the Dragonborn by providing the Wabbajack, along with instructions on how to use it to remedy Pelagius of his insanity. Having completed several challenges and conquering the dead emperor's inner fears, the Dragonborn was able to return to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), but not before being given the Wabbajack by Sheogorath as a parting gift.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Skyrim-12)</sup>
