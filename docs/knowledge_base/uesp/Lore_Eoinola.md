**Vateshran** Eoinola was a known [historian and storyteller](Lore_Vateshrans.md) among the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-STOTDFB1-1)</sup> Little is known about their life, but they were devoted to spreading the tales of [Lorkh](Lore_Lorkh.md) the Sower of Flesh, and the creation. They lived before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Two quotes related to the [Void](Lore_Void.md), creation, Lorkh, [his heart](Lore_Dark_Heart.md) and the early [Men](Lore_Men.md) were attributed to them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NZ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDD-3)</sup>

## Quotes
- *"And unto the mountains they fled, for the world grew dark with shadows which sprung from the heart of Lorkh, who though greatly sundered still believed in the light of Man."*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NZ-2)</sup>
- *"It was there in the darkness that Lorkh understood. Nothing does not exist. Where there is nothing there is possibility. And so he found a space in the Void where all that is could be."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDD-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Wayward Guardians](Lore_Wayward_Guardians.md) were familiar with a vateshran's account suggesting that their Reachfolk ancestors had inhabited the region since ancient times, "when the world was young".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ardanir-4)</sup>
