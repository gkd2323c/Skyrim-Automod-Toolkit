[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-npc-Stormcloak_Courier.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Stormcloak_Courier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Courier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Courier.jpg) A Stormcloak Courier The **Stormcloak Courier** can be encountered on a few occasions during gameplay. If you did not receive a suggestion from [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) to join the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) after completing [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), and haven't yet joined either side in the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), it's possible to encounter the Stormcloak Courier randomly, and they may even follow you into dungeons. Speaking to them will result in them telling you to head to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) to speak with [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) to join up. They can also be encountered during [A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md), when you must retrieve their [documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm Cloak_Documents) for [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) if you've joined the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). Attacking him outside of the related quest is considered a [crime](Skyrim_Crime.md).

They wear a [Stormcloak cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Cuirass), [fur boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots), [fur gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Gauntlets), and either a [hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet), [scaled](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Helmet), or [Stormcloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Helmet) helmet. They carry a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) and 12 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and either a one-handed [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md) with a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) or a two-handed weapon.

## Related Quests
- **[A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md)**: Find and deliver some forged [documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm Cloak_Documents) to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).
- **[Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md)**: Prove yourself worthy of being a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebel.
