[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Imperial_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Mage.jpg) The male mage **Imperial Mages** are members of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) that are only present during the dragon attack on [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). They wear a set of [Imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md), a [torturer's hood](Skyrim_Torturer%27s_Hood.md) and a pair of [Imperial light boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md). They can cast [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) and two versions of [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md). It is unknown if they survived the attack.
