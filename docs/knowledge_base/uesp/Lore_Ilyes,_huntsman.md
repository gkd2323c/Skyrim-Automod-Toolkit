[![ON-concept-Breton medium armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/b/be/ON-concept-Breton_medium_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Breton_medium_armor.png)
- "It's not the most welcoming terrain, I'll give you that, but my family's hunted [these lands](Lore_Rivenspire.md) for generations. We make a decent living selling off [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md) and [giant bat](Lore_Giant_Bat.md) pelts, and [spider](Lore_Spider.md) venom fetches a nice sum, considering the danger of collecting it.

- It's all about being prepared, of course. You need a good bow—you don't want the damn things getting too close to you. If they do, you need the right armor. It's got to be sturdy enough to keep the fangs out, but tromping around in heavy armor won't serve you too well out here; you need to do too much climbing, and you need to be quick and quiet for a successful hunt."
