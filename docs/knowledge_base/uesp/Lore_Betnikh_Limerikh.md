Betnikh Limerikh *A Limerick about an Orc from Betnikh* *
---
There once was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) from [Betnikh](Lore_Betnikh.md)
Of warmth he said he was sick
But the gales of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md)
Tossed his arse in a drift
Now his buns he can't unstick!
