[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Borri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Borri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borri.jpg) Borri **Borri**, an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest), is one of the reclusive [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) in [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md) who refuses to speak, lest a mere whisper of their voice unleashes destruction. Borri teaches you a word of the [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) [shout](Skyrim_Shout.md) during [The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md). He will react when interacted with, greeting you with a muttered "Dovahkiin" and causing the surroundings to rumble in doing so.

Borri wears a set of [Greybeard's robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes) with the matching [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Hood) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots). He carries a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), one sample of [ice wraith teeth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth), an [amulet of Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md), and one sample of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md).

He will start his day at 4am, rising from bed to meditate inside facing the windows, on the mats, changing to a different window each hour. At 12:30pm, he will meditate in the courtyard, as well as in the courtyard tower, occasionally using the [Clear Skies](Skyrim_Clear_Skies.md) shout while up there. At 4:30pm he will meditate in front of the shrine in the main entry hall, and at 8pm he will meditate in the main entry hall, rotating positions in a clockwise direction every hour until retiring to his sleeping quarters at 12:30am to read; finally retiring to bed at 2am. He will then sleep for two hours and repeat his daily routine.

Borri knows the following shouts: [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md), [Dismay](Skyrim_Dismay.md), [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md), [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md), [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md), [Ice Form](Skyrim_Ice_Form.md), [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md), and [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md).

Aside from his greeting to you, the only other word ever heard by Borri is the shout *"Bex!"* during the quest The Way of the Voice. The shout in English means *open*.
