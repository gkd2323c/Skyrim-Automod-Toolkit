## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Romlyn Dreth](Skyrim_Romlyn_Dreth.md).
2. Make your decision: - Turn in Romlyn to [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md).
- Deliver the keg to [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) in the inn at [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
If you snoop around [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md), you'll discover a [memo](Skyrim_Attention_Employees!.md) to employees lying around speaking out about recent thefts. Speak to [Romlyn Dreth](Skyrim_Romlyn_Dreth.md) and you'll find out that he is the source of the problem. He can be found in the lower level of the meadery at roughly after 1pm and [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) in the evening. Romlyn has been selling [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) from underneath the meadery at a lower cost. He states that he can no longer get the mead out of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and offers to cut you in on the action if you deliver the mead to his customer.

### Delivering the Mead
Should you choose to deliver the mead for Romlyn all you need to do is take it to [Vilemyr Inn](Skyrim_Vilemyr_Inn.md) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). The bartender there is named [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) and will thank you for the delivery offering you some leveled jewelry in return. You can speak to Romlyn again to make additional deliveries.<sup>[*verification needed — one time quest in SSE*]</sup>

### Turning in Romlyn
All you need to do to turn in Romlyn is speak to Indaryn and select the special dialogue option. Indaryn will thank you and give you some leveled gems.

## Notes
- Either choice will make the low-level items in [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) freely available, including [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) barrels.

## Bugs
- If you turn Romlyn in to Indaryn, he will keep working at the meadery and continue to sell his under-the-table Black-Briar Mead, despite saying *"A Dreth never forgets a traitor."* each time you talk to him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. Romlyn will be jailed if you turn him in and he will no longer offer to sell you any mead.
