**Paralyze** is an expert level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell which causes [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) on its target for 10 seconds.

## Effects
- [Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 10 secs
- [Stagger Push](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 100 pts

## Perks
- [Stability](Skyrim_Stability.md), raises duration to 15 secs
- [Alteration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Alteration_Dual_Casting.md), increase duration to 22 secs, if cast with both hands

## Notes
- *Stability* and *Alteration Dual Casting* perk effects stack rendering its duration to 33 secs, if cast with both hands.
- [Magic resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) reduces the spell's duration by given percent.
- Targets that are immune to paralysis may still be [staggered](Skyrim_Stagger.md).
- Although Paralyze is an Expert-level spell and only supposed to be in random loot at around levels 35+, rarely it can be found in "boss chest" loot at levels 30 and under.
- Dragons, Dwarven automatons, Ghosts, and most hovering creatures are entirely immune to Paralyze and all other forms of paralysis.
- [Fortify Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md) potions increase the duration of the Paralyze spell. If you utilize the [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) glitch, it is possible to make Fortify Alteration potions which will vastly increase the duration of the Paralyze spell, and any other Alteration spell, even without the Stability or dual casting perks.
- Paralyze has no impact data set information linked because there is no data set to link it with.

## Bugs
- If you Soul Trap a paralyzed target, they may get up and play their walking animation upon death. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Killing someone who is paralyzed may result in them not being able to be looted. To avoid this bug, you can paralyze someone and then attack them until their health is very low, then when they begin to stand back up, land the killing blow/spell. <sup>**?**</sup>
