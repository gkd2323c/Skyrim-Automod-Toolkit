King Farangel's Beer Ballad *A drinking song dedicated to the first king of Wayrest*
---
Here it was [Farangel](Lore_Farangel.md) first
Set foot upon [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md),
Claimed this land for Gardner house
And built Farangel Dock.

Sent his ships both far and wide
To trade in distant lands,
Cleared the woods for [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) town
Above [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) sands.

First Wayrest king, and father of
The [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble peers.
High Rock Bretons, raise this toast:
"For Farangel, four beers!"

(Drink)
