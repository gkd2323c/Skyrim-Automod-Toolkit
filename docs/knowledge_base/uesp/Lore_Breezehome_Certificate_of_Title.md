Certificate of Title **by [Proventus Avenicci](Skyrim_Proventus_Avenicci.md)** *A deed to a house for an honorary citizen of Whiterun*
---
In the name of [Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), Jarl of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md), may it be known in the present and future that I, Proventus Avenicci, steward to the Jarl, by virtue of the authority vested in me, by this charter, grant and transfer to the [Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) and his heirs the property known as Breezehome, in the Plains district of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) on the present day.

Proventus Avenicci
Steward of Whiterun
