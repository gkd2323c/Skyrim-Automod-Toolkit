If he survives the dinner, he can later be found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) at [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), but in fact he will be disabled after walking out of [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md).
