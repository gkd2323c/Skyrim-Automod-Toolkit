# Azure Plasm

Azure Plasm, also known as **Azure Plasma**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vot L-1)</sup> or the **Blood of Coldharbour**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LZD-2)</sup> is the name given to the unformed [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) in the realm of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup> It is the substance from which both the [realm](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) of Coldharbour itself and the physical forms of its inhabiting Daedra are formed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fox-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LSP-5)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LMA_FNH-6)</sup> Chaotic creatia takes a different but planar-appropriate form in every realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). In Coldharbour, a realm known for mimicking and stealing parts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), it takes the form of ubiquitous pools of blue slime which functions similarly to water, and is even inhabitable for many aquatic creatures such as fish.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Coldharbour Angler-7)</sup> The [Village of the Lost](Lore_Village_of_the_Lost.md), a border realm of Coldharbour created during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), was notably covered in a sea of the fluid while it formed.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

When a [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) native to Coldharbour is slain or banished, its morphotype, or vestige, is sent back there. Within Coldharbour are secret grottoes where the process of plasm-accretion can be witnessed. It is here the Azure Plasm drains from pools adjacent to the grottoes and slowly coalesces around a vestige to eventually reform the Daedra. It is usually a terrifying and lengthy process for the Daedra involved, although it can be prevented through resurrection with the use of [Soul Magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The process is identical for mortals whose soul is under the protection of a [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), such as [Soul Shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md); the Azure Plasm coalesces around a vestige and forms a corporeal body with the semblance of the shape it wore in life.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup> Should this individual die, the nature of the vestige is such that their body simply reforms upon death and the cycle repeats itself.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Jaseen-10)</sup>

A phenomenon that involves Chaotic Creatia causing a physical transformation in regions of Nirn has been observed around sites of Oblivion incursion. During the Planemeld, the Chaotic Creatia of Coldharbour would seep into Nirn in areas around [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md), and, responding to the will of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), gradually transform the infested land to mirror Coldharbour itself.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GBAYQ-11)</sup>

The first [Daedric Titan](Lore_Daedric_Titan.md) is said to have been formed when the skeleton of the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Boziikkodstrun](Lore_Boziikkodstrun.md) was submerged in a pool of Azure Plasm, an act which is said to have given the Titan its body, brain, and brawn.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DDTT-12)</sup>
