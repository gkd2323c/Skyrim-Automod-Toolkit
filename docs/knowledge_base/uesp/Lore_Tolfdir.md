[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/LG-cardart-CRDL_03_001.png/200px-LG-cardart-CRDL_03_001.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-CRDL_03_001.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-CRDL_03_001.png) Tolfdir (Legends) Tolfdir was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) mage who taught at the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). He eventually took on the role of the College's Master Wizard. Despite seeming foolish or forgetful at times, he was actually one of the best [Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alteration) scholars in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRMirabelle-1)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Tolfdir took a group of new students including the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FL-2)</sup> on a field trip to the archaeological dig at [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md). He and the Dragonborn discovered the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md) hidden inside [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md) and Tolfdir brought it back to the College for study. Tolfdir's knowledge of magical lore helped identify that the markings on the Eye were unique, as they were neither [Elven](Lore_Aldmeris.md), [Daedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Alphabet), nor [Ayleid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleid_Language).

Sometime later, [Ancano](Lore_Ancano.md), the College's guest [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) mage advisor, seized control of the Eye by magic, generating a powerful energetic field. Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Lore_Savos_Aren.md) and Master Wizard [Mirabelle Ervine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mirabelle_Ervine) confronted him, but a massive explosion ensued that killed the Arch-Mage and severely wounded Mirabelle. Tolfdir asked the Dragonborn to help save [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) from a swarm of Magic Anomalies unleashed by the explosion that threatened the city <sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRCQ-3)</sup>.

Tasked by Mirabelle to retrieve the [Staff of Magnus](Lore_Staff_of_Magnus.md) from [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md), the Dragonborn returned to find Tolfdir and other mages stranded outside the College. Tolfdir explained they had been forced out by the Eye's magical barrier, which was increasing out of control, and that Mirabelle had died from her injuries. Tolfdir instructed the Dragonborn to use the Staff to break through it, after which he and the Dragonborn confronted Ancano, and the Dragonborn defeated him, saving the College and the city. Tolfdir became the new Master Wizard succeeding Mirabelle, and appointed the Dragonborn as the new Arch-Mage.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RTU-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRCQ-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSOM-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TEOM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-7)</sup>

By studying the [heartscales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragon_Scales) of a [dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragons), cut with [Kahvozein's Fang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kahvozein%27s_Fang), a blade sharp enough to make precise carvings from such material, Tolfdir perfected Ebonyflesh into the powerful defensive [spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spells) known as Dragonhide.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARS-8)</sup>
