The **Ice Fish** is a type of fish that can be found in the rivers of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).

## Ide
The **Ide** is a type of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Ilyadifish
The **Ilyadifish** is a type of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). They seem to be named after the legendary [Ilyadi](Lore_Ilyadi.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Inconnu

## Inkfish

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
