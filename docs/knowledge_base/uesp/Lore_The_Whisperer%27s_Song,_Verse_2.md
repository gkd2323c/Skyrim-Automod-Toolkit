[The Whisperer's Song](Lore_The_Whisperer%27s_Song.md), Verse 2 *Three-part song of the Whisperer* *
---
For moons and more

the words I bore

were whispering to the meek.

Their hearts and minds

became all mine,

surrendered without a squeak!
