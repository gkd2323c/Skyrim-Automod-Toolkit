It is the home of the two unhappy and ever-nagging sawmill workers, [Kodrir](Skyrim_Kodrir.md) and [Leifur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifur). It consists of one area, *[Anga's Mill Common House](#Anga.27s_Mill_Common_House)*.

## Anga's Mill Common House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.jpg) Interior view The front door opens to the sight of the fireplace, which holds three gold coins on the mantel and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) at its base, where Leifur often cooks in the evening. In the southwestern area sit three respawning food barrels along with a well-dressed dinner table with two loaves and one piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), and one bottle of regular [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). Atop one of the barrels is a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md). Across from the cooking pot stands a non-respawning cupboard with clutter, with some [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) and a [pile of salt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) on top. At the foot of the cupboard is a basket containing three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), and a respawning food barrel.

Next to the fireplace is a kettle containing four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and another dinner table with plenty of food: three slices of [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), two potatoes, a cluster of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), and three loaves of bread. Above the table hangs more food, some [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md), two samples of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), and two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md). There are also some game underneath the ceiling, two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). On the same wall stands a set of shelves with two bottles of wine and one of alto wine, and the [quest](Skyrim_Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest).md) -related book *[The Legend of Red Eagle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle)*. Next up are a non-respawning dresser with clothes and a small non-respawning chest with very minor loot. The dresser stands behind one of the three owned beds in the sleeping area. Next to the first bed, a non-respawning end table contains clutter and has a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) on top.

Behind the next owned bed sits a respawning food barrel and a non-respawning wardrobe with a [sabre cat pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt) on top. Opposite the wardrobe is a small table with two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). In the eastern corner are another non-respawning end table with clutter, a hidden medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b), a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000330b3), and a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling). The respawning chest at the foot of the bed contains minor loot, same as the non-respawning end table in the opposite corner. Two respawning food barrels are nearby. The end table has four gold coins on top, as well as a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Biography of the Wolf Queen](Skyrim_Biography_of_the_Wolf_Queen.md)*.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House) to which is carried by both Kodrir and Leifur.
- There are three respawning food barrels near the door, two to the south and one to the north. A [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) rests atop a crate on the north side.
- Leifur and Kodrir never sleep; they just return here and idle around all night, be it eating or just standing about.
