# Codex Scientia

This article is about the in-universe book. For the game guide, see [Books:Codex Scientia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Codex_Scientia).

The ***Codex Scientia*** is **the Book of Knowledge** which ancient prophets wrote of, describing a powerful scroll that when possessed can "buy riches beyond imagination and have the power to crush one's enemies," containing illuminations worthy of those who seek "ultimate power"—leading an adventurer through the trials and terrors of the [Arena of Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CD-1)</sup>

## Notes
- *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](Lore_The_Daggerfall_Chronicles.md)*, also referred to as a Book of Knowledge<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dag Hint-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFCD-3)</sup> and *Codex Scentia II*,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TDC-4)</sup> was a tome contemporary to the late Third Era authored by the [Scenarist Guild](Lore_Scenarist_Guild.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dag Hint-2)</sup> It is unknown how the Scenarists' work relates to the original Codex Scientia scroll described by the ancient prophets.
- The [Oghma Infinium](Lore_Oghma_Infinium.md) is also referred to as the "Book of Knowledge".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFHermaeus-5)</sup>
