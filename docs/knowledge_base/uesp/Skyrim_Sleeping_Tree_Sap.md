[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg) A bottle of Sleeping Tree Sap **Sleeping Tree Sap** is a potion that gives the player a drug-like effect. It [slows down movement](Skyrim_Slow.md) and [increases health](Skyrim_Fortify_Health.md) by 100 points for 45 seconds. In addition, it provides the rare effect [Drugged](Skyrim_Drugged.md) for 45 seconds. This effect causes your screen to be tinted purple and become blurry.

It possesses the most powerful narcotic effect in the game, and is one of only two sources of the Drugged effect, the other being [Redwater Skooma](Skyrim_Redwater_Skooma.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.

After completing the quest [Sleeping Tree Cave](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md), [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) will buy the sap for 150 gold.

## Availability
Sleeping Tree sap is much more difficult to obtain than skooma. The only merchants who sell the sap are randomly encountered [skooma dealers](Skyrim_Skooma_Dealer_(NPC).md), who always carry two bottles. Using the spigot attached to the [Sleeping Tree](Lore_Sleeping_Tree.md) at [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) will produce one vial of Sleeping Tree sap. Additionally, with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on installed, one sample can be found atop the first [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) within [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md).
