[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-npc-Winterhold_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Winterhold_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Winterhold_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Winterhold_Guard.jpg) Winterhold Guard **Winterhold Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Korir](Skyrim_Korir.md). Should the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) forces gain control of Winterhold, they will be replaced by [Imperial guards](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard.md) under the service of [Jarl Kraldar](Skyrim_Kraldar.md).
