- *Correspondence discovered with "The Ballad of [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md)," a long and unfinished moon-singer composition dated to the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). *

* * *

My dear friend Talbira,

When you receive this, it will be more than a year since I entered partnership with Zerith-var. I cannot believe it has been that long already! He is much changed from when I first met him. Or perhaps I should say he is much more himself. You have known him longer and would know better. I find he is more the high-minded acolyte of the [Hidden Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_Hidden_Moon.md) that you described than the aloof hothead who took [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md)'s teachings of self-love too seriously that I first judged him to be.

When you chose me for partnership with one of the Order of the Hidden Moon, I had notions that traveling with a [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) would be dour and serious. A true test of my faith. Instead, my days are filled with travel and song, meaningful work, and good conversation. Zerith is warm and easy-going, making friends wherever we travel. But he takes his service to Azurah seriously, endeavoring never to stray from his twilight path. I am every day in awe of his commitment, for he makes it look so very easy.

We have journeyed far, tending to those most in need of my healing songs or Zerith's blade. And we talk of you fondly and often. You'll smile to know that I, too, have now had to tear this [cat](Lore_Cat.md) away from watching [games](Lore_Recreation.md) of five-claw cards or war dice. Thank Azurah that we have little [gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gold_Coins) with which he might gamble. But we are all right. Zerith's upbringing gave him light fingers, so we are never in want of anything—rather, never anything that another might sorely miss.

And while I am happier than I have been in some time, there is no denying that we face many dangers in our travels. Everything from [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) to [beasts](Lore_Bestiary.md) that stalk the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md). I am little good at fighting or defending, not being a warrior like Zerith. But I endeavor to learn songs of battle and train with him further. I will look out for him, the same as he watches over me. This I vow.

But look at me, going on like this. The purpose of this letter was to send you the rubbings you asked for from the Ko'krin Zhab. I send with it a vial of the sacred waters found within. May both aid you in the songs you write in honor of the [Laughing Lion](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md). Perhaps Zerith and I can hear your progress when next we meet in person.

Azurah guide you,

Ravith
