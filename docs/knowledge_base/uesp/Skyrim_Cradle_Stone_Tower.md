[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Tower.jpg) Cradle Stone Tower **Cradle Stone Tower** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers) west of [Bard's Leap Summit](Skyrim_Bard%27s_Leap_Summit.md) containing a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).

After scaling the Forsworn-filled hills at [Lost Valley Redoubt](Skyrim_Lost_Valley_Redoubt.md) and crossing the waterfall at Bard's Leap Summit, follow the trail up the west hill, looting a chest along your way to this remote tower. An alternate and more adventurous route to the tower is by [climbing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Climbing) the rocky formation from the unmarked crevice where [Frofnir Trollsbane](Skyrim_Frofnir_Trollsbane.md) is located [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-105567&y=-31508.2&zoom=17&legacy=true). By doing so, you can bypass the Forsworn-infested redoubt entirely.

## Related Quests
- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The tower has a total of four floors to it. The lowest level is accessed via a novice-locked gate. Inside at the top of the stairs is a broken stone table with a random [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) and [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md) (see [notes](#Notes)) on top and a [burial urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md) beside it. The stairs lead down to a caved-in section with an apprentice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). There is no access from this level to the second floor. Outside of the tower is a wooden ramp that leads up to the second floor where you will encounter the only occupant of the tower, a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md). The second floor also contains a sideboard opposite the doorway, holding a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Sithis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis)* with a set of shelves to the left holding a random [magicka potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka) and a flight of wooden stairs leading up to the third floor.

At the top of the stairs there is a [pressure plate](Skyrim_Pressure_Plate.md) that triggers a [spellcaster trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spellcaster_Trap) that fires [lightning bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md) that is situated across the broken floorboards to the north. There is an [urn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urn) to the left and a broken sideboard holding a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) to the right of the trap. Against the west wall is another flight of wooden stairs leading to the top floor; to the left of this is a random [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) sticking out of the rubble. The top floor is the smallest floor and only has a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) on it. It does offer a nice view as well.

## Notes
- A [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) is located right outside the entrance to the tower.
- An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is located a short distance southwest.
- The random dagger and helmet don't appear to respawn.

## Bugs
- If you have already cleared Lost Valley Redoubt and fast travel to Cradle Stone Tower, the fast travel may instead place you at the word wall section of Lost Valley Redoubt. This bug will correct itself after you have cleared Cradle Stone Tower.
- The [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Sithis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis)* may not be present. See the bug on the book page itself for more information.
