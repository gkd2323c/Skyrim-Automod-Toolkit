**Note**: This quest no longer appears in your journal following the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update.

2. Locate the Ancient Tome Chest within.

## Detailed Walkthrough
**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Arcane Accessories](Skyrim_Arcane_Accessories.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), this quest no longer appears in your journal. Nevertheless, the rewards can still be obtained by finding the lost library yourself.**

The quest involves travelling to [Hob's Fall Cave](Skyrim_Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) to locate a long-lost archive of spell tomes. The cave is filled with [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) and [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) enemies. The quest marker will lead you to an "Ancient Tome Chest" in a reading area found roughly halfway through the dungeon. For more precise directions, see the [dungeon walkthrough](Skyrim_Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.md#Hob.27s_Fall_Cave). Open the chest to complete the quest.

The chest contains all 16 [new spell tomes](Skyrim_Arcane_Accessories_Items.md#Spell_Tomes) added by the Creation. Reading these will teach you the following spells:

- | - *[Unbounded Flames](Skyrim_Unbounded_Flames.md)* <br> - *[Unbounded Freezing](Skyrim_Unbounded_Freezing.md)* <br> - *[Unbounded Storms](Skyrim_Unbounded_Storms.md)* <br> - *[Paralysis Rune](Skyrim_Paralysis_Rune.md)* <br> - *[Pride of Hirstaang](Skyrim_Pride_of_Hirstaang.md)* <br> - *[Elemental Flare](Skyrim_Elemental_Flare.md)* <br> - *[Elemental Bolt](Skyrim_Elemental_Bolt.md)* <br> - *[Elemental Burst](Skyrim_Elemental_Burst.md)* | - *[Elemental Blast](Skyrim_Elemental_Blast.md)* <br> - *[Orum's Aquatic Escape](Skyrim_Orum%27s_Aquatic_Escape.md)* <br> - *[Fenrik's Welcome](Skyrim_Fenrik%27s_Welcome.md)* <br> - *[Mara's Wrath](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Wrath.md)* <br> - *[Choking Grasp](Skyrim_Choking_Grasp.md)* <br> - *[Strangulation](Skyrim_Strangulation.md)* <br> - *[Hangman's Noose](Skyrim_Hangman%27s_Noose.md)* <br> - *[Touch of Death](Skyrim_Touch_of_Death.md)* |
| --- | --- |

The spells obtained from these tomes are very powerful, as they use far less [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) than anything available in the base game with the same effect. If gameplay balance is a concern, you should note that you are not required to read or even take the tomes in order to complete the quest.
