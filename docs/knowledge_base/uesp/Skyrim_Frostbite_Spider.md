# Frostbite Spider


When at range they spit [poisonous venom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poison_Damage) (spell **Frostbite Poison**, Health + Stamina Damage, 5 pts for 5 seconds), even if turned partly or fully away from their victims, and can leap onto their targets to attack with their bite. Without [poison resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md), you will be poisoned by nearly every hit from these monsters. Their previous victims, [dessicated corpses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dessicated_Corpse), can sometimes be found nearby.

## Notes
- Two giant frostbite spiders can be found in a [random encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) in the wilds, fighting over a non-lootable desicated corpse. If one of the spiders survives (or if they notice you before they kill each other), one or both will turn on you, instead.
- The largest of the frostbite spiders can easily outrun you if you attempt to get away on foot without the aid of a horse, even if you sprint.
- Despite there being two color variations, you can only craft the orange frostbite spider as a trophy.
