**Summon Arvak** is an apprentice level conjuration spell added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) that summons the undead horse [Arvak](Skyrim_Arvak.md) to ride or to aid you in combat. Arvak can be summoned in places where you cannot bring a standard horse. In addition to the Soul Cairn, as mentioned in the description, you can also summon him in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md), [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md) and [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>. You cannot summon him in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md) (see notes) or [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md), however.

## Effects
- [Summon](Skyrim_Summon.md) [Arvak](Skyrim_Arvak.md), for 60 secs, if you remain unmounted. Arvak stays summoned if you stay mounted.

## Perks
[Conjuration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) - Doubles Arvak's summoned timer, from 60 seconds to 120.

## Notes
- Sometimes it will seem that when you cast the spell you fail to summon him but it may be that he has spawned on a ledge somewhere above or below you. Get to a place where there are none of these and try again. You can also move away from the area where you summoned him and he might follow you, negating the need to cast the spell again.
- Summoning Arvak can still be done when you have 100% spell absorption, unlike other summons which would be absorbed.
- Although Arvak appears to be undead, he is considered by the game to be only an animal and is therefore not affected by undead-related spells.
- The duration is listed as 60 seconds, but Arvak will actually stick around indefinitely provided you are on his back. Once you dismount, if 60 seconds have passed, he will disappear.
- You cannot summon Arvak in walled cities, such as [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
- One use for this spell is if you are too encumbered to fast travel. So long as you are outside, you can summon Arvak, hop on his back, and then travel anywhere you need to no matter how much you are carrying. Very useful if you're moving all your possessions to a new house, for example.
- Arvak takes up a summoning slot, so casting a second [Conjure](Skyrim_Conjure.md) spell (or a third with the Twin Souls perk) will make him disappear.
- Although you generally can't summon Arvak in the Forgotten Vale, you may or may not be able to summon him depending on what version you use (such as the Nintendo Switch version, etc.).

The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue.
