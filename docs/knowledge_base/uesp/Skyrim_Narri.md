[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-npc-Narri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Narri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Narri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Narri.jpg) Narri **Narri** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who works at the [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md), an inn located in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). She works for [Valga Vinicia](Skyrim_Valga_Vinicia.md).

Narri wakes up at 8am, and immediately heads outside, relaxing just outside the inn. Then, at 4pm, she heads back in the inn, working until 1am, when she finally goes to sleep.

Narri wears a set of [tavern clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tavern_Clothes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000d1921). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_The_Dead_Man%27s_Drink) to the inn as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Like most Falkreath residents, Narri is fully aware of the dreary atmosphere and looks forward to anything exciting:

*"Shor's bones! A handsome man in Falkreath!"* **(If you are male)** *"You're going to have the men around here wrapped around your finger in no time."* **(If you are female)** *"I've half a mind to ask if you'll take me on one of your adventures, but I can't just leave Valga on her own."* *"Not much exciting happens around here. You're as like as not to be bored to death if you stay too long."* *"A strong drink will chase off that chill in the air."* When working inside the inn she may also say:

*"Anything I can get you?"* *"Can I get you a drink?"*
## Conversations
She can often be seen conversing with other Falkreath citizens:

**Narri**: *"At some point we should hire [Tekla](Skyrim_Tekla.md) on here. If she could just spend less time serving Dengeir..."*
**Valga**: *"I wish I could afford to pay you both, Narri. I really do."* **[Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md)**: *"Narri! This mead tastes like water and the meat has gone off."*
**Narri**: *"The mead is same as it ever was and the meat is fresh, maybe your sense of taste has "gone off.""*
**Dengeir**: *"The impertinence! You never would have talked to me like that when I was Jarl."*
**Narri**: *"We had this same argument every week, even when you were Jarl."* **[Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md)**: *"Hey gossip, bring me a drink."*
**Narri**: *"Bolund, you're going to die of thirst if you keep calling me that."* **Tekla**: *"Narri, do you think the war will ever end?"*
**Narri**: *"I think the war is just an excuse. If it does end there will be a new reason and a new place."*
**Tekla**: *"But what about the banning of Talos worship? Isn't that worth fighting about?"*
**Narri**: *"I don't know about you, tekla * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, but all my prayers are silent and who I pray to is still my own to decide."*
## Bugs
- Narri, as part of the [Server](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Server) faction, is supposed to trigger the option to buy food from her menu when you take a seat inside the inn; however, due to her not being in the correct factions, such option is not available, so she'll keep asking you if you would like something to eat, with no possibility to reply. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, add her to the [Dead Mans Drink faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Mans_Drink) by targeting her and using the console command `[addtofaction](Skyrim_Console.md#addtofaction) 000a6bfb 1`.
- Narri was meant to take over the inn in case something happened to Valga Vinicia, but she can't due to a backup alias not being defined in the Falkreath Quest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
