[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/OB-place-Fort_Ash.jpg/200px-OB-place-Fort_Ash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Ash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Ash.jpg) Fort Ash (Oblivion) Fort Ash is a ruined keep located deep in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md), in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Fort Ash is strategically located on the [Black Road](Lore_Black_Road.md), between the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md) and the city of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md). During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Fort Ash was one of the six vital keeps that determined control of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAsh-1)</sup>

## History
Fort Ash was the site of battle early in the [Colovian Revolt](Lore_Colovian_Revolt.md), between the Colovians under Duke [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) and the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) of Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md). It was the revolution's first defeat in the conflict, but after the Duke took control of the Imperial Legion from Chorrol, he was able to push the Emperor back into the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) and eventually take the Imperial City.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EFEV-2)</sup> Years later, the Three Banners War had swept across central Cyrodiil and each of the three alliances took one-third of the region for themselves. The [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) seized the northwest and subsequently Fort Ash. It was part of the [Transitus Network](Lore_Transitus_Network.md), which connected it throughout central Cyrodiil and the other keeps. It was surrounded by flatland and small outposts that facilitated mines, a lumbermill, and a farm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAsh-1)</sup>

Like many other keeps in Cyrodiil, Fort Ash was active until it was later abandoned sometime during the relative peace of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBNerussa-3)</sup> By the late Third Era, Fort Ash was inhabited by an unknown tribe of [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md), who defended their home from highwaymen and treasure hunters alike.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBAsh-4)</sup>
