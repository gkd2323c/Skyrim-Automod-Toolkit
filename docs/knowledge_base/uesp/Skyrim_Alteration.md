# Alteration

**Alteration**, one of the five schools of [magic](Skyrim_Magic.md) in Skyrim, affects the world around it by altering the laws of reality and manipulating it to one's own accord. Increasing this skill reduces magicka cost when casting Alteration spells. Historical information about the School of Alteration is provided in the [lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alteration). The Alteration skill tree has a total of 10 perks, requiring a total of 14 perk points to fill.

In-game Description: *The School of Alteration involves the manipulation of the physical world and its natural properties. This skill makes it easier to cast spells like Waterbreathing, magical protection, and Paralysis.*

## Summary
Alteration contains a range of defensive and utility effects.

- **[Magic Armor](Skyrim_Armor_(effect).md)** spells provide a magical armor protection. The perk multiplies the magnitude of the flesh spells. The ranks in the perk magnify the armor value by a factor of 2, 2.5, and 3 respectively.
- **[Magic Resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md)**; up to a passive 30% bonus is available via perks.
- **[Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md)** via the *Atronach* perk. This is one of only three ways to obtain spell absorption.
- **Utility spells** like *[Candle/Magelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light)*, *[Waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md)*, *[Telekinesis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telekinesis_Effect)*, *[Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md)* and *[Detect Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Detect_Dead)* are all of general use when exploring.
- **[Equilibrium](Skyrim_Equilibrium.md)** converts health into magicka.
- **[Transmute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Transmute_Mineral_Ore)** allows you to turn [Iron Ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) into [Silver Ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) and Silver Ore into [Gold Ore](Skyrim_Gold_Ore.md).
- **[Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md)** is the only really offensive aspect of this School of Magic.

In some ways the protective spells in Alteration are an alternative to the [Wards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ward) found in the school of [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md).

### Spell List Notes
- The **base cost** for spells is the amount of [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) that would be necessary to cast the spell at **0 skill** without any perks. The magicka cost at 100 skill is 41% less than the base cost; the cost can also be reduced via enchanted apparel and by unlocking various perks. See [Spell Cost](Skyrim_Magic_Overview.md#Spell_Cost) for the full magicka cost equation.
- Skill level indicates which skill perk needs to be unlocked to decrease the spell's magicka cost. "N/A" is used for special spells whose cost is not affected by any perks.
- You will always know the spells [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md) and [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) by the time you start [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), regardless of your race, and depending on your race, other spells will also be known: - [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) will also know [Fury](Skyrim_Fury.md).
- [Bretons](Skyrim_Breton.md) will also know [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md).
- [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) will also know [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md).
- [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) start the game with 50 extra magicka and can use [Highborn](Skyrim_Highborn.md) to generate magicka quickly.
- If you lack the required magicka amount to cast a spell, a "You don't have enough magicka" message will appear on the screen.
- The "Where to Get" column only lists *guaranteed* locations for obtaining the spell tomes. Most spell tomes are also available in random loot; see [Spell Tome Locations](Skyrim_Spells.md#Spell_Tome_Locations) and the table below for details.
- For higher-level spell tomes to become available for purchase, your skill in the school must be high enough (see the table below). Also, master-level spell tomes can only be acquired once that school's ritual spell [quest](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md#Master_Skill_Quests) has been completed.
- All spell tomes have a weight of 1.
- Skill XP can be modified by scripts and by the game engine itself under various circumstances. The exact XP you receive may vary from the numbers listed here.
- Magicka cost and skill XP are rounded down, both in game and on this page. Because of this, you may find that you cannot cast a spell even though it appears you have exactly enough magicka to do so.

### Gaining Skill XP
- Casting higher level spells gives generally faster skill increases (Novice<Apprentice<Adept<Expert<Master).
- [Regenerate Magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) effects help reduce downtimes while training.
- It is possible to reduce or even completely eliminate the magicka cost of spells by stacking [Fortify Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration) enchanting effects, which is very helpful for Alteration training. The [Secret of Arcana](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Arcana)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> power does the same thing, but only for 30 seconds.

#### Casting with Enemies Nearby
Repeatedly casting high-duration Alteration spells (like *Oak Flesh*, *Stone Flesh*, *Iron Flesh*, or *Ebony Flesh*) while in a combat situation will quickly increase one's Alteration skill. An effective method for doing so very quickly:

1. Get a melee based follower and/or conjured melee atronach (Familiar, Frost Atronach, Ash Spawn, or Dremora Lord) to attack an enemy or group of enemies. The player can usually cast their alteration spell from safety during combat while the follower and/or conjured pet do actual fighting. This allows for leveling and questing at the same time. Casting a flesh spell in one hand and Bound Sword in the other will level both Alteration and Conjuration at the same time.
2. Find a place with an enemy or enemies that only use melee attacks that also has a high platform or place otherwise out of reach of the enemies. Preferably, kill all but one of the enemies: Bleakwind Basin works very well for this purpose. Alternatively, along the shore of the Sea of Ghosts, the Horkers can be made aggressive without them actually attacking, allowing one to move far enough away to use the Wait function but still close enough to train the skill. And then there's [Roadside Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Ruins), where a Spriggan Matron will notice the player, but she cannot attack since she's trapped inside. Slaughterfish can also be exploited by getting their attention and then standing on a nearby shore to cast.
3. With both hands, continually cast the highest level Alteration armor spell. Casting the higher-level spells progresses the skill faster (Oak < Stone < Iron < Ebony), so it might be advisable to take a break part way through to go buy them once you've reached a level where the better spells are available. Often the enemy will run far enough away from your location to allow you to use the Wait function. If so, wait an hour to get more Magicka to keep casting the spell, or just wait until it refills itself. The giants in [Bleakwind Basin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleakwind_Basin) will almost assuredly run away, though, if you're standing on the rectangular pillar near their fire. Mammoths never run away from you when they can't reach you. Using this method, one can go from 0 Alteration to 100 Alteration in roughly 40 minutes, but it depends on total Magicka available, particular spells cast, and the location chosen.
4. Use a follower or permanent summon to continuously attack an NPC that cannot be killed. If your ally can keep the NPC on their knees, this leaves you free to repeatedly cast a spell.
5. Once the [Quiet Casting](Skyrim_Quiet_Casting.md) perk from the Illusion school of magic and the Throw Voice shout from [Shearpoint](Skyrim_Shearpoint.md) have been acquired, one can misdirect enemies to and fro, and cast any of the "...-flesh" spells while they are searching for you. Using a magicka regeneration potion is useful in this scenario. An extra added benefit is that you can constantly sneak around enemies while casting, increasing your Sneak skill as well.
6. The Paralyze spell is the perfect spell for Alteration training. If you reduce magicka cost to 0 using enchanted items, dual casting it on an enemy can level you up so fast that it might be considered game-breaking. With the Mage Stone, one can train Alteration from 15 to 100 in less than 5 minutes. It can be used to level up by making it Legendary over and over again.

#### Magelight
Magelight can be cast anywhere for a small amount of XP, such as while walking to some distant destination, making this a sure but slow way to level Alteration. The spell cost is deducted from your Magika when it is cast, but the XP is earned when the spell makes contact with a surface. If the spell reaches its maximum distance before making contact, no XP is given. This peculiarity isn't normally seen when Magelight is cast inside dungeons. It seems that Magelight gives XP based on the travel distance of the spell. Dual casting and the Stability perk will allow the spell to travel longer distances, resulting in more XP gain.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SR-misc-Magelight_Mountain.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Magelight_Mountain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Magelight_Mountain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Magelight_Mountain.jpg) The mountaintop to cast at, just outside Solitude and facing north If you're willing to abuse glitches, Magelight can be cast at a mountaintop just outside the main gate of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) for an extremely large amount of XP, making it possible to reach level 100 in only a few minutes. (The large XP gains at this location seem to be related to passing through multiple planes of weather effects.) Similarly to Paralyze, this can be used to rapidly gain character levels by repeatedly making Alteration Legendary, and can be so powerful as to be considered game-breaking.

#### Water Breathing Trick
After you learn *Water Breathing*, another way to quickly level Alteration is to stand in water and repeatedly cast *Water Breathing*. You don't actually have to be underwater, just standing in it, although this only works with pools of water that you can actually dive into (so the small streams in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) won't work, but the river outside will, as will the pool just outside the entrance to [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md)).

#### Casting Detect Life Spell in Cities
The *[Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md)* spell increases your Alteration experience depending on the number of targets it is currently detecting. Using it on your horse in the wilderness is almost useless, while using it in crowded location (the market in Riften during the day, for instance) gives incredible training speed. The more living objects in range, the better. Depending on the amount of NPCs within range, it should be possible to level Alteration from 15 to 100 in around 20 minutes in the following locations.

Between 8am and 5pm the markets in most walled cities can be used to power-level Alteration. Riften's, Whiterun's, and Windhelm's markets have particularly high numbers of NPCs, while standing between the market and Castle Dour in Solitude will boost the low market numbers with soldiers from the castle grounds.

Later in the day moving to the main tavern will allow for more power-leveling. The [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) (Markarth), [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) (Riften), [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) (Solitude), [the Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) (Whiterun), and [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) (Windhelm) are the most populated inns in the walled cities. [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> in Raven Rock also has high numbers.

After the Civil War is over, either the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) (Stormcloaks) or the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) (Imperial Army) will be packed with exiled Jarls and their most loyal allies.

#### Casting Telekinesis
Holding any object in the air with *[Telekinesis](Skyrim_Telekinesis.md)* without picking it up also gives a very high amount of experience. If the suspended object touches any NPC they will become hostile though, so be careful. If you can get a total of 100% magicka reduction cost (such as through the use of the [Secret of Arcana](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Arcana)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> power), and fast travel while holding an object, the game will act as if you had kept casting for the whole duration of the travel, and level Alteration appropriately. For example, fast traveling between Riften and Markarth will increase your Alteration skill to 100, even if you start from 15.

- A good way to level Alteration using Telekinesis is to put an object near the cistern entrance of the Thieves Guild. When you exit the Thieves Guild, keep the hatch closed (do not pull the chain) and drop your object. Then cast Telekinesis on it and fast travel somewhere on the map. You will level Alteration and the object remains near the Thieves Guild entrance. This way, you can Legendary Alteration and re-level it over and over without having to go and find the object that got away.

#### Transmuting Ore
Outside combat, you can gain a substantial amount of Alteration experience if you know the Transmute spell by using it to transmute iron ore into silver and gold ore. Even at low levels of Alteration, it should be possible to cast Transmute (an Adept level spell), although if you have been busy mining it may take a long while to turn all of your iron into gold (which you can then smith into jewelry for Smithing experience). The Transmute spell is not available from spell merchants, but the spell book is in two fixed locations in dungeons.
