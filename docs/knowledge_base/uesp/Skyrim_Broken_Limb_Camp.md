## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Broken_Limb_Camp_02.jpg) A giant at the camp The camp is on the edge of the hot springs in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) near the bank of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md). Two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) occupy the camp, which is surrounded by the typical painted stones, [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md), and bones. The central campfire has been extinguished, with a [dragon's tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md) plant growing among the burnt remnants. An unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) and two [mammoth cheese bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl) are south of the campfire. More dragon's tongue, [jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md), [creep cluster](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md) plants, and a birds' nest are in the vicinity.
