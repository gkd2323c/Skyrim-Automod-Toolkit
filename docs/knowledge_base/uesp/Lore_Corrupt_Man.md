# Corrupt Man

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-crown_store-Skaal_Maul_Dance.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Skaal_Maul_Dance.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Skaal_Maul_Dance.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Skaal_Maul_Dance.jpg) Skaal Maul Dance The Corrupt Man is an obscure entity viewed as a pawn of [the Adversary](Lore_The_Adversary.md). The [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) engage in a ritual dance every 5th of [Last Seed](Lore_Last_Seed.md) to commemorate the victory over this entity. During their training, Skaal warriors practice dances using their preferred [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md). Maul-focused warriors perform a special dance on this day to mark their triumph over the Corrupt Man.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Emote-1)</sup>

*Troubled Thoughts of a Corrupt Man* is a book penned by the fabled prisoner of [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md), who also wrote *Sanguine's Rites and Realms*. These writings discussed realms of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) without [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), often leading to their destruction due to their unguided paths. Signs of these ill-fated planes appeared in the reflective waters of the [Myriad Realms of Revelry](Lore_Myriad_Realms_of_Revelry.md) or within [Quagmire](Lore_Quagmire.md)'s dreamscapes, extensively documented in these two books. The connection between these texts and this entity remains uncertain.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OTNOO-2)</sup>
