*Alchemy description:* **Warmth is increased by <mag> points for <dur> seconds.**
**Fortify Warmth** increases your [Warmth](Skyrim_Warmth.md) rating. The effect is moot when [Survival mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is toggled off.

The effect is only available in [food](Skyrim_Food.md#Hot_Soups) cooked with [Fire Salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts). It is not found in spells, magic items, or ingredients. [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) gain 15 points of Fortify Warmth as a racial passive, [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) gain 10 points, and [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) gain 25.
