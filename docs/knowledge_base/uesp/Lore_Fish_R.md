## Ray
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Stingray.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Stingray.png) A Ray The **Ray** is a type of flat fish, not to be confused with airborne [Rays](Lore_Ray.md).

- The **Devil Ray** can be found in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md), off the coast of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- The **Pyandonean Ray** can be found in the Southern Sea, off the coasts of [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md).
- The **River Stingray** can be found in the rivers of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
