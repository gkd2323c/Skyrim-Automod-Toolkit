# Robe of St. Roris

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/MW-item-Robe_of_St_Roris.jpg/200px-MW-item-Robe_of_St_Roris.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Robe_of_St_Roris.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Robe_of_St_Roris.jpg) Robe of St Roris Robe of St. Roris is a piece of clothing sacred to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) as a holy relic. It is the robe [Saint](Lore_Tribunal_Saints.md) [Roris](Lore_Roris.md) wore when he was slain. According to the doctrine of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) it was blessed with his blood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> The robe was renowned for its ability to restore the fatigue and vitality of those who wore it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROSR-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/MW-quest-Control_the_Ordinators.jpg/200px-MW-quest-Control_the_Ordinators.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Control_the_Ordinators.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Control_the_Ordinators.jpg) Robe of St. Roris hidden in the chest Roris wore his robe when he was captured by [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) before the [Arnesian War](Lore_Arnesian_War.md). He was subsequently tortured by Argonian sorcerers for not renouncing his faith in the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md). His death was the cause of the Arnesian War as the Dunmer wanted justice and vengeance.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lot S-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWdialogue-4)</sup> The robe was allegedly blessed by his blood upon his death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup>

By the year [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the Robe of St. Roris was located within a cavern known as Assemanu, a place revered by followers of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md). This robe was considered a holy relic. Archcanon Tholer Saryoni of the Tribunal Temple made a request to the representative of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md), who was working on behalf of Duke [Vedam Dren](Lore_Vedam_Dren.md). Tholer Saryoni asked them to retrieve the robe in exchange for Tholer's assistance and influence over the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md). Whether the robe was ultimately delivered to the Archcanon remains unknown.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CTO-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup>
