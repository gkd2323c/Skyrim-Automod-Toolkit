# Penitent

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/ON-item-Shrine_to_Trinimac.jpg/200px-ON-item-Shrine_to_Trinimac.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Shrine_to_Trinimac.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Shrine_to_Trinimac.jpg) Altmeri Shrine depicting Penitent The Penitent, the **Blade of Courage** (also named **Vosh Rakh** in [Orcish](Lore_Old_Orcish.md)), is the legendary blade of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md) used in ancient times.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VR-1)</sup> Statues depicting Trinimac often show him holding his sword, and worshipers of the god swear by it for aid.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Priests of Trinimac consider themselves blessed by the holy sword Penitent, and that his blessing is his sword and shield being at one's side to protect them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TP-3)</sup> The [Vosh Rakh](Lore_Vosh_Rakh.md), an Orcish extremist faction, named themselves after the blade's Orcish name as they considered themselves a manifestation of it to "cut a path through stifling traditions",<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VR-1)</sup> with some members bearing the rank "penitent".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VRMember-4)</sup> The name of the sword is often used as a possessive adjective in regards to worship of Trinimac.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Trinimacs Penitent-5)</sup>

[Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) also wields a sword,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Malacath-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOMalacath-7)</sup> which he is also seen wielding in depictions in statuary, as does [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) who some myths identify as Trinimac instead,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FETE-8)</sup> but it is unknown if there is any connection.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-LA-9)</sup>
