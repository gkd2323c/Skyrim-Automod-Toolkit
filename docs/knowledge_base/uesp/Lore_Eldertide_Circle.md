# Eldertide Circle

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/ON-npc-Archdruid_Ellic.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Archdruid_Ellic.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Archdruid_Ellic.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Archdruid_Ellic.jpg) Archdruid Ellic of the Eldertide Circle The Eldertide Circle is one of the three prominent [Druidic Circles](Lore_Druids.md) in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md) and live in enclaves throughout the islands such as [Tuinh](Lore_Tuinh.md) in [Galen](Lore_Galen.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VGTG-1)</sup> and the [Coral Cliffs](Lore_Coral_Cliffs.md) in [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoad Coral-2)</sup> They feuded with the Breton [coin-barons](Lore_Coin-barons.md) appointed by the [Reman Empire](Lore_Reman_Empire.md) in [1E 2704](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2704).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONSHV6-3)</sup> The Eldertide believe that the civilization of Man and Mer has chained nature down, and thus they seek to balance the natural world and mortal's greed by washing it away, metaphorically and physically.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCC-5)</sup>

The Eldertide are able to create the [Reefborn](Lore_Reefborn.md), creatures of [coral](Lore_Coral.md) that mimic Nirn's creatures, a process that requires living sacrifices.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCC-5)</sup> It is unknown if the Eldertide are also responsible for Reefborn that are present in other islands found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The Oakensoul Ring [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) showcases druidic master craftsmanship with some Direnni influence. It represents the authority of the Draoife, and its power comes from the various components that make up the ring's anatomy. The ring's stones [larimar](Lore_Larimar.md) gems represents the Eldertide and contain draoifoglyphs, the druidic runes.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OSRCodex-7)</sup>

At least some people speculated that the [Deep Druids](Lore_Deep_Druids.md) were in fact of the Eldertide Circle.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/ON-render-New_Life_2024.jpg/200px-ON-render-New_Life_2024.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-New_Life_2024.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-New_Life_2024.jpg) New Life Winter Storm Robes of the Eldertide Circle The Eldertide along with the [Firesong](Lore_Firesong_Circle.md) and [Stonelore](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md) circles, originally made up the unified group known as the [Druids of Galen](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md) before they dissolved into these three major sects, each with their own interpretation of Y'ffre's will.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESODR-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONSHV1-10)</sup> No longer bound to a line of druidic kings,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-LABHI-11)</sup> their symbology disappeared almost overnight.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Despite their separation, to symbolize their unity matters more and that they share the same goals, the druidic circles came to shaping knot-work into a [triquetra](Lore_Triquetra.md),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> a symbol that would become adopted by civilized Bretons.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> They observe the [New Life Festival](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md) with stargazing sessions and the study of the [stars](Lore_Stars.md). Festive robes are worn for the occasion.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-NLWSR-15)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) sought to research druidic magic. The volatile magic of the Eldertide was prioritized.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
