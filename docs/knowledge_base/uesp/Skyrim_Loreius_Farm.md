[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-place-Loreius_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Loreius_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Loreius_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Loreius_Farm.jpg) Loreius Farm **Loreius Farm** is a small farm in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md), north of [Whitewatch Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whitewatch_Tower).

It is home to [Vantus Loreius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius) and his wife, [Curwe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Curwe). Like other farms, the farm can provide fresh produce, and you will not incur any bounties for harvesting the plants here.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The farm is set back from the road with a path leading up to the single story farmhouse. The farm boasts a windmill that powers a [grain mill](Skyrim_Grain_Mill.md). Behind this are two large fenced fields. The first one holds six bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), fourteen [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants, sixteen [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and nine [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). The other is where the [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) live and where the [chicken nest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) is located. There are several [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks inside the mill and a couple of food barrels just outside the fields, by the fence. The front of the house has a fenced deck with a couple more barrels of foodstuffs at one end and a chair at the other. Around the farm are five [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) plants. The front door is novice-locked most of the time.

### Loreius Farm
The house is a single-room dwelling with a set of shelves immediately on the left as you enter. These hold two leeks, a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, five potatoes, and four common books Several pieces of cheese, two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) and a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) lie on top of the shelves and three bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), two garlic braids and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hang from the ceiling above. To the right is a food barrel and coming out from the southeast wall is an owned double bed with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of it. A round wooden table sits to the right of the bed with two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) and a common book on top. Against the southwest wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and a hanging rack to the right holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

Opposite the door on the southwest wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand in front, while on the mantelpiece is a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md). To the right of the fireplace is a long wooden table and bench with two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two pine thrush eggs, a cabbage, several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a potato and a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) on top and a [potion of extra magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka) on the floor underneath. Finally, in the northern corner is a small square wooden table with two bottles of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and five loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on it.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg) *"So many knife wounds."* - If you head up the hill directly west of the farm, you will come across a small depression littered with the remains of slaughtered [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md). Two [poachers](Skyrim_Poacher.md) will watch your approach warily and attack if you get too close. [Vantus Loreius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius) and his wife, [Curwe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Curwe), may be overheard talking about it. Despite what they say, the boneyard is west of the farm, not northwest.
- If you turn in [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) during [Delayed Burial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial), you'll be able to take things from the farmhouse for free without it being considered theft. Unfortunately, both farmers will dead by the time you return, and the guard who patrols the road will remark *"You don't want to go up there, citizen. It's a terrible sight. The farmer and his wife murdered in the middle of the night. So many knife wounds."* - If you turn in Cicero during Delayed Burial then wait too long to return to the farm, Vantus and Curwe's corpses won't be there.
- Unlike some other grain mills, the grain mill at this farm cannot be used to create a [sack of flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> if you have [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, as it lacks an activator.
- Two [corundum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) can be found northeast of the farm.
- Although the owners here are farmers, you cannot [sell crops](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md) to them.
