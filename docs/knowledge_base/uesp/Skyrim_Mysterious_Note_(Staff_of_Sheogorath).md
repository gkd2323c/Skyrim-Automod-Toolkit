Mysterious Note **by [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md)** *A letter challenging the reader to an unorthodox combat trial* *
---
Dear person who happened upon this letter, it is your lucky day! Nearby you will find the [Fork of Horripilation](Skyrim_Fork_of_Horripilation.md). Oh, how I do love cutlery! "But what is it?" you must be asking. Well, horripilation is the standing of hairs on end due to cold, fear, or excitement -- and excitement is indeed upon us, my friend!

Take this fork, and use it to kill two special [Bull Netches](Skyrim_Bull_Netch.md) in single combat. Each will yield an artifact: The [Eye of Ciirta](Skyrim_Ciirta%27s_Eye.md), and the [Branch of the Tree of Shades](Skyrim_Branch_of_the_Tree_of_Shades.md). Warriors of another age might find these familiar. Combine these together with some [Heart Stones](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) at a [Staff Enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md) for a special surprise!

If you're successful, you'll be rewarded quite handsomely, with the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath_(item).md) (handsome, indeed!) If not, well... I do love to watch a good fight!

Yours Truly,
Lord [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md)
