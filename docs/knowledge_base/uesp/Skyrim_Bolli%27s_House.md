[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-place-Bolli%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bolli%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bolli%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bolli%27s_House.jpg) Bolli's House **Bolli's House** is a small, two-story residence with a cellar, near the city gates in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). It is owned by [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md), who lives there with his wife [Nivenor](Skyrim_Nivenor.md). It is the first house on the left when entering Riften from the north. It consists of only one zone, which is the [interior](#Bolli.27s_House).

## Bolli's House
Upon entering there is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) on a stand to your left against the north wall, with a small square wooden table and two chairs to the right along the same wall. On the table are two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). In the northeast corner, under the stairs that lead to the top floor, is a food barrel. There are stairs up and down to the basement, both against the east wall. Against the south wall is a long end table holding a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-interior-Bolli%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bolli%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bolli%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bolli%27s_House.jpg) Bolli and Nivenor's bedroom The stairs leading up take you straight into Bolli and Nivenor's bedroom, which consists of a double bed coming out from the middle of the west wall, with a dresser at the foot of the bed that holds a bunch of lavender and a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Requested_Report) that discusses Bolli's suspicions regarding his wife cheating. To the left of the bed, against the south wall, is a wall shelf holding a wooden bowl containing three [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower). Under this is a basket containing three pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), which is next to a fireplace. On the mantel are copies of *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)* and *[Kolb & the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon)*. There are two chairs pulled up in front of the fireplace. Opposite, against the north wall, is an unlocked wooden [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing gold, with a tall wardrobe to the left along the same wall.

Descending the stairs to the basement brings you to a storage area with a set of shelves against the north wall. They hold a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), several pieces of cheese, four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). A round wooden table is to the left with a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) underneath it, and hanging from a rack above are two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). There is a doorway in the middle of the west wall, with a cupboard of clutter and a large vat to the left of it and a pile of firewood under the stairs opposite. Through the doorway there are several bales of straw against the south wall and several food barrels against the north wall. Hanging from the doorway are three more salmon.

## Notes
- The front door has an adept lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolli%27s_House_Key) to which is carried by Bolli and Nivenor.
- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the left of the entrance door.
- All containers in the house, with the exception of most barrels and sacks, are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
