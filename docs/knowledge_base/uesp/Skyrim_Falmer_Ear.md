## Ingredients
43-46<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 17-18<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 7 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 6 in [Forelhost Stronghold](Skyrim_Forelhost_Stronghold.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 5 in [Tolvald's Crossing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Crossing) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 3 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in [Irkngthand Arcanex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand_Arcanex) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))
- 2 in [Black-Briar Manor](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Nightcaller Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightcaller_Temple) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
All [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), including [Falmer Nightprowlers](Skyrim_Falmer_Nightprowler.md) and [Falmer Shadowmasters](Skyrim_Falmer_Shadowmaster.md), have a 63% chance of dropping 1-2 Falmer ears. The best place to find them is in [Falmer hives](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Falmer_Hives).
