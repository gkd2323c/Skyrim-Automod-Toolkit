## History

### Reign
Kevarian Corelanya inherited the realm from his aunt, [Psynande](Lore_Psynande_Corelanya.md), following her death in [1E 1194](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1194).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTK-2)</sup> Though both her era and that of Kevarian's grandfather [Orlemar](Lore_Orlemar_Corelanya.md) were marked by rising tensions with the Argonians, it was under Kevarian's rule that the conflict between Solstice's two peoples intensified to its most violent stage. During his reign, three brief wars erupted. The three conflicts occurred in [1E 1172](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1172), [1E 1190](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1190), and [1E 1194](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1194). During these wars, Corelanya forces invaded Stone-Nest [xanmeer](Lore_Xanmeer.md) cities, while Stone-Nest warbands penetrated deeply into Corelanya estates, even reaching the walls of Sunport.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> Kevarian fell in the last of these battles, dying honorably in combat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTK-2)</sup>

### Legacy
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt *Kinlord Kevarian the Defender (reigned 1E 1165 - 1E 1194)
Nephew of Psynande, civilizer of the wilds. Slain in honorable battle.*
— *[Tombs of the Kinrulers](Lore_Tombs_of_the_Kinrulers.md)*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTK-2)</sup> Following his death, leadership of Clan Corelanya passed through several kinlords. Amid the prolonged stalemate with Argonian forces, a series of clan leaders were assassinated, plunging the realm into further instability. It was not until the rise of Kinlady [Torinwe](Lore_Torinwe_Corelanya.md) that a call for peace was finally championed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> Orlemar was laid to rest in the Corelanya Crypt and remembered as the warrior and "civilizer of the wilds".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTK-2)</sup> Kevarian features prominently in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup>
