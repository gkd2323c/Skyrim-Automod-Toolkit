[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-npc-Thug.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thug.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thug.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thug.jpg) A thug **Thugs** are [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warriors](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) that can be randomly encountered after completing the quest [Promises to Keep](Skyrim_Promises_to_Keep.md).

If you let [Louis Letrush](Skyrim_Louis_Letrush.md) take [Frost](Skyrim_Frost.md) at the end of the quest, a thug can be [encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Louis_Letrush_vs_Thug) attacking Louis. If you keep Frost instead, you may be [randomly attacked](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Thug_sent_by_Louis_Letrush) by a thug sent by Louis.

## Bugs
- It is possible to encounter a random event Thug that will not attack and cannot be spoken to.
