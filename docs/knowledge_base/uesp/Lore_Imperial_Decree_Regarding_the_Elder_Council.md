Imperial Decree Regarding the Elder Council *List of appointments to the Elder Council at the start of Emperor Durcorach's reign*
---
1 [Sun's Dawn](Lore_Sun%27s_Dawn.md), [2E 534](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_534)

At the pleasure of His Imperial Majesty [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md), the Black Drake, first of his name, first of his house, by law of conquest Emperor of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and Protector of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), let the following be made known to all citizens of the [Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Empire_of_Cyrodiil). The [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) is hereby established as follows:

In token of his long experience and proven loyalty to the Ruby Throne, High Chancellor [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) shall remain in his position as a member of the Elder Council and chief advisor to the Emperor.

Legate [Tarnian Lovidicus](Lore_Tarnian_Lovidicus.md) of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) is hereby seated as representative of the [Trans-Niben](Lore_Trans-Niben.md) and named Chief Councilor, in appreciation of the general respect and honor in which he is held throughout Cyrodiil.

[Ertus Vandacia](Lore_Ertus_Vandacia.md) of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) shall be appointed to the Elder Council for his service and assistance in establishing the reign of the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md).

Councilor Sophus of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) shall remain in his position as representative of Blackwood, with the full confidence of His Majesty the Emperor.

Councilor [Itinia](Lore_Itinia.md) shall remain in her position as representative of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md), with the full confidence of His Majesty the Emperor.

Councilor Orlandus Plavi is hereby banished from the realm, on pain of death.

Councilor Hestraia Silan is hereby sentenced to death by quartering, such sentence to be carried out immediately.

Councilor Martiax Granciola is hereby sentenced to death by immolation, such sentence to be carried out immediately.

In light of his absence from these proceedings, Councilor Sergan Euraphus is hereby named criminal, traitor, and enemy of the Ruby Throne. A bounty of one thousand gold drakes is offered for his capture and return to the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), dead or alive.

Lord Pheomas Lucasta of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) is hereby seated as representative at large, in token of the general respect and honor in which he is held throughout Cyrodiil.

[Kelgan](Lore_Kelgan.md) the Taker of Teeth, also named the Troll-Breaker, is hereby seated as representative at large, in token of his great enthusiasm for carrying out His Imperial Majesty's will and his many fell deeds in various battles of His Majesty's recent campaigns.

So let it be written.
