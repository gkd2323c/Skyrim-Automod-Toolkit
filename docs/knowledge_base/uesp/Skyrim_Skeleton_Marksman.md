[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-creature-Skeleton_Marksman.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Skeleton_Marksman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeleton_Marksman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeleton_Marksman.jpg) Skeleton Marksman **Skeleton Marksmen** are undead beings summoned by an Apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell, [Conjure Skeleton Marksman](Skyrim_Conjure_Skeleton_Marksman.md) added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

They are equipped with a Skeleton Marksman Bow with 297 Skeleton Marksman Arrows for combat purposes.

## Bugs
- Skeleton Marksman Arrows can be looted from fallen Skeleton Marksman enemies. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
