It was released on April 5, 2018 for 200 Creation Credits. Nix-Hounds were previously seen in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind).*

## Official Summary
*Found in the wilderness of Solstheim, a tamed Nix-Hound can be adopted as your faithful companion. Nix-Hounds can carry items, and scrounge for useful ingredients. (Pet can be purchased from Revus Sarvani near Tel Mithryn or Geldis Sadri at the The * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Retching Netch Corner Club in Raven Rock if the former is deceased. Hostile Nix-Hounds can be found in the Solstheim wilderness.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds [Nix-Hounds](Skyrim_Nix-Hound_(creature).md) to the island of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). While the majority are hostile enemies encountered in the wilderness, a tame Nix-Hound is available for purchase as a pet from [Revus Sarvani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Revus_Sarvani) (or [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) if Revus is already dead). Upon installation, you used to receive a [notice of sale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Notice_of_Sale_-_Nix-Hound) which would begin the quest [**My Pet Nix-Hound**](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:My_Pet_Nix-Hound) and will direct you to the relevant NPC to purchase the Nix-Hound for 400 gold.
