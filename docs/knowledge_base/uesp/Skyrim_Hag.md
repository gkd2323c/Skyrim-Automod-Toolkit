[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-npc-Hag_Frost.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hag_Frost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hag_Frost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hag_Frost.jpg) Hag (Frost) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Hag_Shock.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hag_Shock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hag_Shock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hag_Shock.jpg) Hag (Shock) **Hags** are generic, hostile female NPCs that can be randomly encountered. The three versions differ in their magic specialization and in terms of possible races:

| - The first version (`00074F83`) specializes in [Fire Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), and may be [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md), [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), or [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md). <br> - The second version (`00074F84`) specializes in [Frost Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage), and may be Altmer, Breton, Imperial, or [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md). <br> - The third version (`00074F85`) specializes in [Shock Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), and may be Altmer, Breton, Dunmer, or Imperial. <br> <br> The loot is usually common loot for witches, which consists a robe, a dagger, pair of shoes and some gold. |
