[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-creature-Wounded_Frostbite_Spider.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Wounded_Frostbite_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wounded_Frostbite_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wounded_Frostbite_Spider.jpg) Wounded Frostbite Spider The **Wounded Frostbite Spider** is a version of [Frostbite Spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) that is encountered in [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md) as part of the quest [The Golden Claw](Skyrim_The_Golden_Claw.md). This giant spider has significantly more health than regular frostbite spiders.
