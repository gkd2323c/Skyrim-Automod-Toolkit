[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/DF-place-Sentinel.png/200px-DF-place-Sentinel.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Sentinel.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Sentinel.png) Sentinel circa 3E 405 *And the shores were green with the leaves of pomegranate, and fig, and olive, and the men of the Grandee saw this and were hungry, and sought to come ashore, despite the warnings and cries of the port-rabble.*
— *[Sentinel, the Jewel of Alik'r](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sentinel,_the_Jewel_of_Alik%27r)* Sentinel is a great city of the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), and the capital of the [kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md). It is located in the northwestern hills of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), overlooking the [Iliac Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iliac_Bay) to the north,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and bounded by the surrounding [Alik'r Desert](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alik%27r_Desert) to the south.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-STJOA-5)</sup> However, some fertile fields can be found along the coast near the city,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoading-6)</sup> and the city is actually a major population center in Hammerfell.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE3O-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-POT-8)</sup>

The city is an exotic retreat for the nobility of [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daggerfall) and [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), who delight in the cooking, craftsmanship, and plays which can be found there.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-9)</sup> Many great and famous people throughout history have called Sentinel their home, including the renowned poet [Weltan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Weltan) and the famous Redguard hero [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TA-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-EOTAW-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Redguard-12)</sup>

### [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/ON-place-Sentinel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sentinel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) Sentinel circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-place-Sentinel_Docks.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sentinel_Docks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel_Docks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel_Docks.jpg) Sentinel Docks Sentinel and the surrounding area have long been notorious for widespread, underground [necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Necromancy).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-RGCyrus-23)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), under the reign of the Forebear King [Fahara'jad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fahara%27jad), Sentinel came under the threat of attack from an undead army. The army was vanquished, though, in what the Forebear king asserted was a [divine](Lore_Eight_Divines.md) intervention.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-SAMITA-24)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), control of Hammerfell reverted to the hereditary monarchy of the Na-Totambu. To the chagrin of the Forebears, the new High King boldly moved his throne from the old capital Hegathe to the more prosperous Sentinel. By this time, Sentinel had mastered a third of the trade in the entire Iliac Bay. Upon the death of High King [Thassad II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Thassad_II) in [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862), the Forebears retook their city by force. The vengeful Crown Prince [A'tor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:A%27tor) set sail to the city to avenge his father, resulting in one of the bloodiest massacres in Tamrielic history. When [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) swept in and reestablished [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) control over the province, it was seen as a humanitarian intervention.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-9)</sup>

### [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/DF-place-Sentinel_Palace_01.png/200px-DF-place-Sentinel_Palace_01.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Sentinel_Palace_01.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Sentinel_Palace_01.png) Samaruik circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/DF-map-Sentinel_%28city%29.jpg/200px-DF-map-Sentinel_%28city%29.jpg)](File_DF-map-Sentinel_(city).jpg.md) [](File_DF-map-Sentinel_(city).jpg.md) Map of Sentinel (Daggerfall) During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city-state of Sentinel was an active settlement, being known as the *"Guardian of [Starfall Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Starfall_Bay)."* It was ruled by King Caacte, and it had a rivalry with [Hegathe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hegathe).<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Arena-25)</sup> Sentinel was neighbored by several settlements, including [Chasetown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chasetown) to the south, and [Lainlyn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lainlyn) to the east.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-Map Arena-26)</sup>

Despite the defeat during the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md) circa [3E 402](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_402), the city of Sentinel continued to flourish, and it was renowned at the time as the place to go to earn some quick gold or find a bit of adventure.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-DFTutorial-27)</sup> Hammerfell was controlled from the city's throne.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-DFMynisera-28)</sup> An unsuccessful assassination attempt on the life of Queen [Akorithi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Akorithi) was widely attributed to jealous Daggerfall royalty, though this was never proven.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-WAP-29)</sup>

### [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md)
<center>*Above the sea Sentinel shines,
Her domes agleam in the Iliac dawn.
Her people throng the bazaars, and find
Their way between man's walls of stone.
But even in the towered town,
Sandals tread tracks in Alik'r sand.*
— *[On the Immortality of Dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:On_the_Immortality_of_Dust)*</center> In [4E 42](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_42), Altmer dissidents who had fled to Sentinel from the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) in [Alinor](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) were attacked by Thalmor operatives in what became known as the [Night of Green Fire](Lore_Night_of_Green_Fire.md). Their efforts to fight back against the Thalmor were in vain and, by the time [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) soldiers arrived, the entire refugee district of the city had been destroyed. According to Legate Fasendil, an Altmer stationed in Sentinel at the time as a member of the Imperial Legion, the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) was not content with killing dissidents at home, so they went to Hammerfell to finish the job.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-LF-30)</sup> Thalmor agents were also harassing and possibly murdering refugees in [Balfiera](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Balfiera) and Sentinel around [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48); the victims were all of mixed blood or had associations considered by the Dominion to be "unclean."<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-TIC-31)</sup>

Animosity between the Crowns and Forebears was sometimes so great that the factions have been known to leave each other to their own fates during foreign invasions.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-19)</sup> But in [4E 173](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_173), when the Crown city Hegathe found itself besieged during the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) by the seemingly unstoppable forces of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), a Forebear army from Sentinel came to their aid and broke the siege. This led to the reconciliation of the two factions.<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-TGW-32)</sup>

## Notes
- *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* was originally conceived as a fighting game featuring a tournament that took the player to each of Tamriel's cities to challenge different gladiatorial teams. According to a file from that stage of development left behind in the final game, Sentinel's gladiatorial team would have been called "the Deathbringers".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-33)</sup> *[Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Castles)* brought back many of these arena teams to compete in an event known as the Dragon Games, but renamed Sentinel's arena team into the "Sentinel Guardians".<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-34)</sup>
- The [Osseous Crux](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Osseous_Crux) was brought to the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in Sentinel for study, though it vanished within three weeks.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-TVC-35)</sup>
