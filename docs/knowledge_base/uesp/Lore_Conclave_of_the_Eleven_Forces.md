# Conclave of the Eleven Forces

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/ON-place-Keep_of_the_Eleven_Forces.jpg/200px-ON-place-Keep_of_the_Eleven_Forces.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Keep_of_the_Eleven_Forces.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Keep_of_the_Eleven_Forces.jpg) The Keep of the Eleven Forces [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/ON-tribute-psijic-Sage_Counsel.png/200px-ON-tribute-psijic-Sage_Counsel.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-psijic-Sage_Counsel.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-psijic-Sage_Counsel.png) Sage Counsel The Conclave of the Eleven Forces is the Council of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), based on the Isle of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oriandra-1)</sup> It is also known as the **Council of Artaeum**.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FOA-2)</sup> The Conclave is the ruling body of the Order, named after the Eleven Forces of nature that they honor and follow as part of the Old Ways. It supports the Ritemaster in matters of study, policy, and important research.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oriandra-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MTC-3)</sup> It is said that members of the conclave possess immense magical power.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oriandra-1)</sup>

Headed by Mystic Iachesis in its beginnings,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RI-4)</sup> the Council and the Isle disappeared from existence in the Second Era, only to return years later without many of the original Council members or Iachesis.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FOA-2)</sup> Near the end of the Third Era, the Council and the Isle of Artaeum were led by [Loremaster Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FOA-2)</sup> The Council was founded when Altmer Elders from [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) rebelled against the change of ways from those of [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris.md) to the ones now considered customary in the Isles. The Psijic Order was founded by the elders and the Psijics still practice the Old Ways of Aldmeris, or Mysticism.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3SI-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOW-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MTUV-7)</sup> They were formerly based in the Keep of the Eleven Forces when they still operated in Summerset.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Summerfall-8)</sup>
