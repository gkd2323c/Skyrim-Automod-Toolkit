[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/MW-place-Gnaar_Mok.jpg/200px-MW-place-Gnaar_Mok.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Gnaar_Mok.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Gnaar_Mok.jpg) Gnaar Mok Gnaar Mok was a financially deprived fishing village located on an island in the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md) region of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt V-1)</sup> The local [Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) manor dominated the village; the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) also operated out of a dilapidated hut in town. The road to Gnaar Mok from [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md) was a long and exceptionally unpleasant walk, made perilous by the lack of trails or marked routes and casual travelers being ill-equipped for the attacks of wild beasts and brigands, making it preferable for them to instead keep to the roads and travel services.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTA-2)</sup>

During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Gnaar Mok was not a settlement but rather the site of a **Arenim Manor**, consisting of a mansion and a warehouse; however, an area known as Gnaar Mok was a stop for [Silt Strider](Lore_Silt_Strider.md) caravaneers during this time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Councilor Dolvara of [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) resided in the manor, and was later assassinated in it by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-APW-4)</sup>

In [3E 410](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_410), Sixth House bases were founded near Gnaar Mok, with its operatives exploiting smuggler organizations and communications to spread their influence among victims unbalanced by [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s dream sendings.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DUP-5)</sup> By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), a number of huts had been built around the manor, turning it into the settlement of Gnaar Mok. That year the [Boots of Blinding Speed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boots_of_Blinding_Speed) were seen in the possession of a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) by the name of Peminie, who asked the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) for safe passage to Gnaar Mok. The Nerevarine complied, and received the boots upon leading her there. The Nerevarine was then informed by locals that Peminie was in fact a wanted felon; upon interrogation, she became hostile and had to be killed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Morrowind-6)</sup>

## Notes
- "Gnaar Mok" shares a phonetic similarity to "[Noormoc](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Noormoc)", a location on mainland Morrowind mentioned in the *[36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_5.md)*. It is unknown if there is any connection between the two locations, as Noormoc was in existence prior to the splitting of Vvardenfell from the mainland, which the Sermons say occurred due to the activation of the [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md).
