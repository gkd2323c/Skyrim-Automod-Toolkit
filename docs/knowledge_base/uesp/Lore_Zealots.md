# Zealots

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SI-npc-Zealot.jpg/200px-SI-npc-Zealot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Zealot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Zealot.jpg) A Zealot The Zealots of [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) are a group of outcast mortals found in [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md). They follow the Demented teachings of [Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md) to a fault, believing him to be a mortal aspect of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). They believe Sheogorath himself to be a living god, and go to extreme and painful lengths to prove their devotion to him. The Zealots revel in violence, and attack all outsiders unless they wear their robes. Robes are brought to settlements in Dementia as a way for citizens to join the Zealots, although converts are often killed if they are not true of heart or if they fail the painful initiation rituals. The Zealots are noted for their rituals involving the summoning and binding of [Flesh Atronachs](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They are also often found in the company of [Shambles](Lore_Shambles.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Zealots have a particular vendetta against the [Heretics](Lore_Heretics.md), their [Manic](Lore_Mania.md) counterparts. The highest ranks of the Zealots were said to be "ascended".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Zealots were known for their drinking vessels made of brown clay, decorated with symbols from [Mania](Lore_Mania.md). These jugs were allegedly unpleasant to look at and had been discovered in various locations across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) as of the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TZ-4)</sup>
