[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) Kairou, at the bottom of the map Kairou is a region on the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It is bordered by [Pothago](Lore_Pothago.md) to the north, [Myrkwasa](Lore_Myrkwasa_(fiefdom).md) and the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r.md) to the east, and [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> Its main city is the city of Kairou.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DK-2)</sup>

On the 20th of [Evening Star](Lore_Evening_Star.md), Kairou celebrates a special event called [Chil'a](Lore_Chil%27a.md). It is the blessing of the new year in the region and is both a sacred day and a festival. The archpriest and the baroness each consecrate the ashes of the old year in a solemn ceremony, then street parades, balls, and tournaments conclude the event.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DK-2)</sup>

The regional deity of Kairou is [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md), and the dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline of the region is the [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DK-2)</sup> After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Kairou became a part of the kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWit W-3)</sup>
