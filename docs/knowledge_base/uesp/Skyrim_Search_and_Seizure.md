## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Ondolemar](Skyrim_Ondolemar.md).
2. Steal [Ogmund](Skyrim_Ogmund.md)'s [Amulet of Talos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund%27s_Amulet_of_Talos) from [his house](Skyrim_Ogmund%27s_House.md).
3. Return to Ondolemar.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Thalmor's Task
Ondolemar is a [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) [Justiciar](Skyrim_Thalmor_Justiciar.md) living in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). You can find him pacing in front of the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md)'s throne room in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md), accompanied by two [Thalmor Soldiers](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md). Talk to him and he will tell you his duty in Markarth. *"I was sent here to lead the Thalmor's interests in this corner of Skyrim. It's my mission to root out all [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) worship in this city."* As a member of the Thalmor, he doesn't recognize Talos as a god and thinks Talos does not deserve to have a place in their pantheon. In his opinion, citizens of the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) have a responsibility to cease their heretical worship. Then he will pay some attention to you. *"You're awfully inquisitive, aren't you? I like that. Perhaps you'd care to solve a little problem I'm having?"* He knows that [Ogmund](Skyrim_Ogmund.md) the skald, an old and respected man, worships Talos in his home, but the jarl has been reluctant to have him arrested. Ondolemar wants you to break in to [Ogmund's home](Skyrim_Ogmund%27s_House.md) and find evidence for him.

### To Catch a Bard
Ogmund's house is just above the market stalls. You may need to pick a novice-level lock and get into his house. There is a chest in the north part of the house holding [Ogmund's Amulet of Talos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund%27s_Amulet_of_Talos). This is the evidence Ondolemar requires. Steal the amulet and leave the house quietly. If you run into the authorities, do not resist arrest and make the guards turn hostile to you, as Ondolemar will attack you as well, and it will be impossible for you to talk to him during the fight. Pay your fine and the amulet won't be removed from your inventory.

### Reward
Bring the amulet to Ondolemar. He believes it will be all he needs to have Ogmund taken care of. He graciously thanks you for your assistance and rewards you with leveled gold.

| Levels | Reward |

| 20–29 | 800 |
| 30–39 | 1,000 |
| 40+ | 1,200 |

## Notes
- If the Reach is given to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) in [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or conquered for them after [The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md), this quest becomes unavailable, as Ondolemar will no longer be present. Likewise, killing Ogmund before the quest will also render it permanently unavailable.
- If the Stormcloaks capture Markarth while this quest is in progress or if you kill Ogmund, you will fail it immediately.
- Completing this quest will also grant you access to the [Thalmor Headquarters](Skyrim_Thalmor_Headquarters.md) without it being considered as trespassing if you enter, and taking anything from there will no longer count as theft.
- Completion of this quest and a [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill of 50 or better are required to convince Ondolemar to provide a distraction for you in [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md).
- After this quest, using [Ondolemar](Skyrim_Ondolemar.md) as a distraction during [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) will cause his disposition toward you to fall to -2. Doing Diplomatic Immunity first and then finishing this quest will cause his disposition toward you rise to +1.
- Although Ondolemar is a member of the Thalmor, and technically not a citizen of the Reach as he's only posted in Understone Keep, helping him via this quest still counts towards helping people of the Reach if you're working on becoming [Thane of the Reach](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Reach.md).
- An option to bring the amulet to Ogmund was apparently planned, and is available in the code, but there is no dialogue that allows for it. Quest stage 25 appears to be related to this, as setting it with console commands will mark the quest completed and sets Ogmund and Ondolemar's relationship to you to 1 and -1, respectively.
