[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Vals_Veran.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vals_Veran.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vals_Veran.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vals_Veran.jpg) Vals Veran readying a frost spell **Vals Veran** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) who believes the dead are there to serve the living. He is discovered by [Golldir](Skyrim_Golldir.md) defiling [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md) with his 'filthy dark elf necromancy'. After learning that Vals Veran was attempting to resurrect Hillgrund and their other ancestors, [Aunt Agna](Skyrim_Agna.md) went in alone to try and stop him.

Vals wears [necromancer robes](Skyrim_Necromancer_Robes.md) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000C5D12).

## Related Quests
- **[Ancestral Worship](Skyrim_Ancestral_Worship.md)**: Help [Golldir](Skyrim_Golldir.md) save his [aunt](Skyrim_Agna.md) and stop a necromancer from defiling his [ancestral barrow](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md).

## Quest-Related Events
When you and Golldir confront him, he will have a short conversation with your companion and then attack:

**Vals:** *"The dead should be made to serve the living, not the other way around!"*
**Golldir:** *"I'll return my ancestors to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md), and you with them!"*
**Vals:** *"Sovngarde is a myth, you s'wit! And now you can join your ancestors in service to me!"*
