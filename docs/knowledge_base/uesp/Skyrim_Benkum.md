[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-npc-Benkum_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Benkum_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Benkum_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Benkum_2.jpg) Benkum **Benkum** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) found at the [Water Stone](Skyrim_Water_Stone.md) in Solstheim.

Initially from [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), Benkum, along with fellow sailors [Liesl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Liesl), [Hjalfar](Skyrim_Hjalfar.md), and [Palevius Lex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palevius_Lex), decided to cast anchor along the western coast of Solstheim and immediately fell prey to [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s spell. He works around the clock at the stone, oblivious to the world around him. Once the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the entire crew will return to their boat on the coast. When you leave the area, they will all disappear from the game.

He wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1), and he carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).

## Dialogue
He shares his dialogue lines with his fellow sailors:

- *"Think we've got to get back to our ship now."*
- *"We're already late. Not sure why we decided to take such an extended shore time."*
- *"When the tide goes, we'll go with it."*
- *"It's a long way back to Windhelm from here."*
- *"Hope our shipments are still good. How long has it been?"*
- *"Best be shoving off now."*

## Notes
- There's an unused NPC in the base game named [Benkum](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Benkum) who was intended to manage the wagers for the removed arena in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).
