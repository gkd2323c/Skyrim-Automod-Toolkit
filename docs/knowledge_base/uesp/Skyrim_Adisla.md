[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-npc-Adisla.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Adisla.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adisla.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adisla.jpg) Adisla tending to her crops at [Hlaalu Farm](Skyrim_Hlaalu_Farm.md) **Adisla** is an aging [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) working the land for [Belyn Hlaalu](Skyrim_Belyn_Hlaalu.md) at [his farm](Skyrim_Hlaalu_Farm.md) east of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

Adisla crawls out of her bed at 6am and spends the first hour of the day wandering around the [farmhouse](Skyrim_Hlaalu_Farm.md), then sits down and enjoys a one-hour breakfast. At 8am she heads outside and starts working the land. She keeps going for twelve hours until 8pm when she heads back inside for a one-hour dinner. She spends the rest of the evening performing miscellaneous tasks in the farmhouse until she finally goes to bed at midnight.

She wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to defend herself and a copy of the [Hlaalu Farmhouse Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hlaalu_Farmhouse_Key). Besides those, she also has a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor) in her inventory.

## Dialogue
Speaking to Adisla will have her provide a few comments about herself:

- *"Starting to get too old to work all day, but hopefully Master Hlaalu will let me slow down a bit."*
- *"Every now and then someone from the city comes by. Think they aren't used to seeing a Nord working for a Dark Elf."*
- *"Better to work in the cold than in the heat, I say. Though some sun would be nice."*

## Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belyn_Hlaalu_02.jpg) Adisla and Belyn Adisla will talk with her employer, Belyn, on a few occasions. One will have Adisla ask him about a wheat shipment:

- **Adisla**: *"I was wondering if you had ordered the shipment of that frostwheat, sir."*
- **Belyn**: *"Yes, but that fool [Endario](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario) can't get it in. I'll thrash him when I get back to the city, don't you worry."*
- **Adisla**: *"I wasn't wanting to get anyone in trouble, sir. Just wanting to get the new planting started, is all."*
- **Belyn**: *"Yes, wouldn't want to miss the lush summer we have ahead."*

In another conversation between the two, Belyn will order Adisla to perform repairs around the farm, and criticize her resource usage:

- **Belyn**: *"Make sure the back fence is repaired. I swear I saw some [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md) in nibbling the leaves yesterday. "*
- **Adisla**: *"Oh, no skeevers out here, sir. They can't stand the cold."*
- **Belyn**: *"Maybe, but the way you burn through all that wood to keep the cabin toasty, they might mistake this place for [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md)."*
- **Adisla**: *"Sorry, sir. These old bones do like a bit of warmth in the evening. There's no need to make fun."*
- **Belyn**: *"You just need to remember that there's a war on, and firewood isn't cheap these days."*

In another, Belyn will ask her about [Captain Lonely-Gale](Skyrim_Captain_Lonely-Gale.md):

- **Belyn**: *"So have you ever actually spoken to Captain Lonely-Gale?"*
- **Adisla**: *"By the gods, he's just a retired ship's captain."*
- **Belyn**: *"Maybe to you, but he's just so... his story is..."*
- **Adisla**: *"He's just a guard captain. And not a very good one, at that."*
