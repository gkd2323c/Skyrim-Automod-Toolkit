[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/SR-npc-Scavenger.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Scavenger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scavenger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scavenger.jpg) A scavenger attacking [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-misc-Scavenger.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Scavenger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Scavenger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Scavenger.jpg) Scavenger at work The **Scavenger** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who is [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Scavenger) in the wilderness with the corpses of three [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) soldiers and three [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) soldiers. One of each type is naked, and the scavenger is in the process of looting another soldier. He wears a [Stormcloak cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Cuirass) with [Imperial boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots) and an [Imperial officer's helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Officer%27s_Helmet). He carries an [Imperial light shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Shield), an [iron battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Battleaxe), an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow), and four [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow).
