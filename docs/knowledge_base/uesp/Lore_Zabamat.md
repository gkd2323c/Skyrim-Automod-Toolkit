[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-map-Zabamat.jpg/200px-ON-map-Zabamat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Zabamat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Zabamat.jpg) Map of Zabamat circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). **Zabamat** is the northwestern region of the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region. Zabamat is home to a wide variety of farms and other settlements. The region borders the [Daen Seeth](Lore_Daen_Seeth.md) region to the east and the [Varanis](Lore_Varanis.md) region to the west. The capital is the city of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md), located on an island west of [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md). [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md) is a fortress located along the pass between Stonefalls and [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The ruins known as the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(place).md) are just south of Ebonheart and was once the site of a war between the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and the [Nede](Lore_Nede.md). The town of Lukiul Uxith is an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) populated town that means an Assimilated Nest in [Jel](Lore_Jel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> [Vivec's Antlers](Lore_Vivec%27s_Antlers.md) was an infamous battlefield during the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md), which led to the formation of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).
