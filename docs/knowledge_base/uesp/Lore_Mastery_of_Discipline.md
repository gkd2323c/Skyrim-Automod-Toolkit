Mastery of Discipline **by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frandar_Hunding)** *Frandar Hunding's insights on achieving mastery*
---
In the season of life in which I passed through the [Rain's Hand](Lore_Rain%27s_Hand.md), more than 90 duels did I face to prove myself invincible. I learned the 38 grips, the 750 offensive and 1800 defensive stances, and the 9000 strikes that I would practice for all my future days.

With [Onsi](Lore_Onsi.md)'s blessing, the [sword-singer](Lore_Sword-singer.md) must do the same to achieve Mastery of Discipline.
