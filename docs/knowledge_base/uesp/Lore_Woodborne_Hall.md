[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/DF-place-Woodborne_Hall.png/200px-DF-place-Woodborne_Hall.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Woodborne_Hall.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Woodborne_Hall.png) Woodborne Hall (Daggerfall) Woodborne Hall was the ample castle of the minor noble and traitor [Lord Woodborne](Lore_Woodborne.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md). The Hall itself was built by Lord Woodborne to withstand invasions and so was designed with a multitude of levers that only allowed access when pulled in the correct order.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-1)</sup> It is located on the north shore of Wayrest's lake, near the three-way point between Wayrest, [Menevia](Lore_Menevia_(county).md), and the [Orsinium Area](Lore_Orsinium_Area.md). Just to the southeast is the village of Tuncart.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFWoodborne-2)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the [Agent](Lore_Hero.md) was told of the location of Woodborne Hall by [King Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md)'s ghost. The Agent entered Woodborne Hall and discovered evidence that Woodborne was planning to seize the throne of Wayrest—and presumably use the power of [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) to conquer the surrounding lands. Lord Woodborne was eventually cornered and slain by the Agent in the Hall, leaving the fate of Woodborne Hall unknown.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFRevenge-3)</sup>

At around the same time, the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) learned that Lord Woodborne had the [Totem of Tiber Septim](Lore_Totem_of_Tiber_Septim.md) and laid siege on Woodborne Hall. Though the hall eventually fell and many Blades died in battle, the totem was nowhere to be found.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFTotem-4)</sup>
