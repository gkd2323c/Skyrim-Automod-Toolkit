[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Tynan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tynan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tynan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tynan.jpg) Tynan **Tynan** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) who lives in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He works as a henchman and bodyguard for [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md).

He spends all of his time in [Nepos's house](Skyrim_Nepos%27s_House.md), sweeping the floor and keeping up appearances, although he carries the [same secret](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) as his master and the other servants. He never eats or sleeps. Once Nepos reveals his scheme to you during the quest [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md), Tynan becomes hostile and must be killed.

He wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Nepos%27s_House) to Nepos's house, as well as a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

## Notes
- Tynan's death can be avoided by pickpocketing and reading [Thonar's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar%27s_Journal) or [Nepos's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos%27s_Journal) instead of questioning them, then going directly to [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md). - Alternatively, it can be avoided by going directly to Eltrys after questioning [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md).
