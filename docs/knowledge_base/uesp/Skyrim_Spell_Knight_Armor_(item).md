# Spell Knight Armor (item)

Three sets of heavy Spell Knight Armor variants are added to the game by the [Spell Knight Armor](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) (themed after the [Order of the Crypt](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md#Fourth_Era), a [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md) knightly order). One set of the iron and steel variants can be obtained through the [related quest](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md), and after completion all three sets can be crafted at a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md). While each set are of high quality for their material, no set contains a shield.

### Iron Spell Knight Armor
The [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) variant is the weakest of the three in terms of base stats. All pieces have the same weight as regular [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md). However, the cuirass' armor rating is slightly higher than [banded iron armor](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Armor.md). The other pieces are more impressive on that regard, tying with [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor). No [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks are needed to craft these, though that means they also do not benefit from any possible temper bonus. All items can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) using 1 [Iron Ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md).

### Steel Spell Knight Armor
In second place is the [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) variant, that shares its weight with the regular [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor). The cuirass is on par with [improved bonemold armor](Skyrim_Improved_Bonemold_Armor.md) in terms of armor rating, while the other pieces tie with [ebony armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Armor.md). They require the [Steel Smithing perk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) to craft, as well as getting double the effectiveness when [tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) them, requiring a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) for each piece.

### Ebony Spell Knight Armor
The [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) variant is the strongest of the three, sharing its weight with the regular [ebony armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Armor.md). The boots, gauntlets and helmet possess the highest base armor rating for their respective slots in the game. They require the [Ebony Smithing perk](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md) to craft, as well as getting double the effectiveness when [tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) them, requiring an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) for each piece.

## Notes
- There are two versions of enchanted Ebony Spell Knight Armor to be chosen at the end of the Crypt of the Heart questline. It's worth noting that if you refuse to purify the knight's heart, the cuirass you acquire is the only one that reinforces onehanded damage throughout the vanilla gameplay.
