# Goat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-concept-Goat.jpg/200px-SR-concept-Goat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-Goat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-Goat.jpg) A goat Goats are passive creatures that are commonly kept as livestock in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). In the wild, they can usually be found on mountain slopes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGoat Loc-1)</sup> Baby goats are known as **kids**.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Goats are hunted for their hide, meat, and horns.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRGoat Loot-3)</sup> Goats are also kept by mortals for their milk, which can be drunk or used to make cheese.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRGoat Cheese-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VSM-5)</sup> Goats are also kept as pets.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOGoat Pet-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-In Game Goat-7)</sup> Every part of the goat is suitable for eating, except the horns.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SMFM-8)</sup> Goat horns are used to make a variety of products, such as bugles<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OPTF-9)</sup> and holders for light sources, such as candles.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SRGoat Horn-10)</sup>

Goats are commonly used as sacrifices in Daedric rituals,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESONSG-11)</sup> and some are bred explicitly for this purpose.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOSBG-12)</sup> The white goats of [Ninendava](Lore_Ninendava.md) are rumored to lead mortals through some of said rituals.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESONSG-11)</sup> The brown goats that hail from the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) are stronger than they appear.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESODTG-13)</sup> The goats of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) are hardy and well-suited to the region's rugged terrain and changeable climate.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOWBG-14)</sup> [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) goatherds tend to prize their Buck Goats, which have a tendency to be intelligent and pugnacious.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOWBG-14)</sup> These goats make for good companions.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOWBG-14)</sup> Goat specters can be found in [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOMOS-15)</sup> The goats that live along the mountains of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) have shaggy pelts.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SRGoat App-16)</sup>

Some of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) clans ritually sacrifice goats as a form of offering to the ghosts of venerated ancestors and ancient heroes.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>
