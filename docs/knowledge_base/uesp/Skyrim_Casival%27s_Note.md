Casival's Note **by Gathers-the-Coin** *Some final thoughts from Gathers-the-Coin*
---
Over the course of his life, a man wears many faces, and goes by many names. Son, father, fugitive, bandit. I never thought I would include "[Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) writer" among those titles, but so it goes.

When [Peladius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Peladius) taught me how to write, I didn't think I'd take to it the way I have. Maybe if the gods had given me a pen instead of a sword, I would've spent my life writing books instead of sacking caravans. Well, too late to whine about it now.

As for the name, it came from a conversation I had with Pale-Eyes one night at The Grey Mare in [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md). I asked him why Argonian names were so literal, and his reply was, "Why aren't yours?"

I laughed, realizing I was bested. So I asked him to give me an Argonian name, one that suited someone of my ilk.

And so from then on, I was Gathers-the-Coin.

The funny thing about an Argonian name is, I wasn't sure if it was meant to define the person I was or the one I aspired to be. I suppose the point is moot now. I'll probably die in this tower without a septim to my name. But at least if I keep writing, people will know our story, and know that we once lived.
