[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-creature-Vahlok_the_Jailor.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Vahlok_the_Jailor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vahlok_the_Jailor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vahlok_the_Jailor.jpg) Vahlok the Jailor **Vahlok the Jailor** is a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) entombed within [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md) in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). He was once the ruler of Solstheim, tasked with watching over the traitor, [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md), after discovering Miraak's plot against their masters and defeating him in an epic battle.

Vahlok knows a variety of spells, including [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), [Command Daedra](Skyrim_Command_Daedra.md), [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Flame Cloak](Skyrim_Flame_Cloak.md), [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md), and [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Lost Legacy](Skyrim_Lost_Legacy.md)**: Travel to [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md) and defeat Vahlok the Jailor.

## Notes
- The story of Vahlok and Miraak is described in more detail in *[The Guardian and the Traitor](Skyrim_The_Guardian_and_the_Traitor.md)*.
- Vahlok means "guardian" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md). It's featured on the first [Word Wall of the Unrelenting Force shout.](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md#Unrelenting_Force)
- Vahlok is the only named dragon priest who does not have an eponymous mask.
- Many internal references refer to him by the name "**Toldar**".
