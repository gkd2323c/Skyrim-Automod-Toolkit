[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-Fisherman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fisherman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fisherman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fisherman.jpg) A Fisherman found at [Crabber's Shanty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crabber%27s_Shanty) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-Fisherman_%28dead%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fisherman_%28dead%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Fisherman_(dead).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Fisherman_(dead).jpg.md) The dead Fisherman at the Wreck of the Winter War (with [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) installed) Four **Fishermen** can be found in various locations. They can be male or female of any race, and each one wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) with farm [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots), and carries an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe). With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, they will be wearing [fishing apparel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fishing_Items#Apparel) instead and carry fishing rods. Unlike most hunters, fishermen do not offer trades.

## Dialogue
They have the same dialogue as generic [hunters](Skyrim_Hunter.md) encountered in the wilderness:

*"I've been hunting and fishing in these parts for years."* *"It's not like my poaching is hurting anyone. The Jarl can hardly eat every deer now can he?"* *"There's enough for all of us if you aren't greedy."*
