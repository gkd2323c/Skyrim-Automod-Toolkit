From a Breton travel journal *An observer's notes on Orcish battle preparation*
---
[![ON-concept-Orc Shield.png](https://images.uesp.net/3/3f/ON-concept-Orc_Shield.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Orc_Shield.png)
- To witness [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) preparing for battle is a fearsome sight indeed, especially for one used to the strictly-regimented organization of my fellow [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md). I recently had the "honor" of viewing their preparations while traveling alongside traders. Their drinking, burning of offerings to [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md), clashing of brutal weapons on shields, and guttural, violent chants whip them into a blood-crazed frenzy. This is a war-chant they repeated over and over, getting louder and more bloodthirsty with every recitation:

- "Blood! Honor!
- Steel and bone!
- Gore! Glory!
- Mauloch's Throne!"
