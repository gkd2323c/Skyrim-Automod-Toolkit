## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad) and read his [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad%27s_Note_on_Bandit_Attacks).
2. Locate and kill the Saints and the Seducers in their camps near Whiterun. You will then learn the location of their leaders.
3. Track down both [Kinthal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kinthal) and [Svarig](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Svarig) to their respective camps, kill them, and read their journals to begin the mission [Restoring Order](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md).
4. Return to Ri'saad for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-quest-Balance_of_Power_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Balance_of_Power_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Balance_of_Power_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Balance_of_Power_02.jpg) There are a total of four bandit camps to track down To begin the quest, locate the Khajiit trader [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad). He and his caravan often travel through Skyrim, but will stop for several days outside major cities such as Whiterun or Markarth. Upon locating him, you will have a new dialogue option to ask about the trouble his caravan has been running into. Wishing to remain discreet, he will give you [a note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad%27s_Note_on_Bandit_Attacks), which explains the situation. As the note states, recently his caravan has been beset by strange new bandits at two locations: one near [North Brittleshin Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:North_Brittleshin_Pass) and another near [Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Fort_Kastav.md). The only other information he can provide is that one group wears gleaming golden armor while the other wears dark armor.

Tracking down the two camps, you will find each location containing 3-4 bandits and a cage. Each group's leader will have a journal indicating the location of the leader of their respective gang, along with a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit%27s_Cage_Key) to the camp's cage. Inside each cage is an [Elytra Nymph](Skyrim_Elytra_Nymph.md) that you can talk to and obtain as a pet. Obtaining both pets will complete their respective miscellaneous quests. At the first Saints bandit camp, be sure to loot and read the *[Notes on the Blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Notes_on_the_Blacksmith)* off the corpse of the [Saints Bandit Leader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit_Leader) to begin a quest to unlock [Golden](Skyrim_Golden.md) and [Dark](Skyrim_Dark.md) smithing. At the first Seducers bandit camp is a knapsack containing the *[Bandit's Note on Hidden Treasure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit%27s_Note_on_Hidden_Treasure)*; reading it will start the [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Nerveshatter_(quest).md) to unlock a [Daedric artifact](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nerveshatter).

Using the information from the bandit leader journals, locate and kill the true gang leaders: [Kinthal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kinthal) for the Saints and [Svarig](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Svarig) for the Seducers. Be sure to loot the two unique soul gems at the altar where Kinthal is located. Combining the information from both journals will explain that the bandit gangs were funded by a wizard named [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md), and that he seems to be hiding out in the sewers beneath Solitude. Reading either of the leader's journals will start the next quest, [Restoring Order](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md), although you must read both journals to discover Thoron's exact location.

With this information in hand, return to Ri'saad and let him know the two groups are no longer a danger. He will thank you and reward you with 300 gold, thus completing the quest.

## Notes
- The camp near Fort Kastav is next to the soldiers that will aid during [Rescue from Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav.md). They will aid in the fight and start this quest if not already obtained.
- After you've killed Kinthal or Svarig (or both), you may be randomly attacked by bandits from either group, or both at the same time. One of the bandits will have a note from Thoron commanding them to kill you as revenge for their leader's death.
- With [Sunder & Wraithguard](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> also installed, a destroyed caravan loaded with Dwemer artifacts will appear right next to Svarig's camp whether you have started that creation's [main quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Legends_Lost) or not. Reading the [Carriage Driver's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage_Driver%27s_Note), which is found among the wreckage, will start that quest with the first stages skipped.
- This quest can also be triggered when stumbling upon a Seducer's camp by accident.
- If you trigger this quest without speaking to Ri'Saad first, you may have to find him (along the road between Whiterun and Markarth) and speak to him for the quest to complete. In some cases, you must do this in order to be able to continue the next quest, Restoring Order.

## Bugs
- After defeating Svarig, the quest to defeat him will not show as completed.
- After defeating the Saints bandit leader in Whiterun, the objective to defeat them may not be marked as completed.
- After defeating Kinthal, the quest to defeat him will not show as completed.
- After reading both journals, the next quest, Restoring Order, does not appear.
