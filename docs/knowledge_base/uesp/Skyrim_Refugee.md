[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SR-npc-Refugee_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Refugee_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Refugee_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Refugee_02.jpg) One of two male refugees [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-npc-Refugee_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Refugee_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Refugee_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Refugee_03.jpg) One of two male refugees Three **refugee** corpses can be found on the [second road](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&x=110606&y=62405&zoom=17&legacy=true) directly north of [Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Fort_Kastav.md), along with a dead [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) and a snow-covered wagon. Their belongings can be found strewn on the snow around the wagon. Among them are a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Enchanter's Primer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Primer)*, an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel), a coin purse, and various [food](Skyrim_Food.md) items.

The three refugees are all [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmers](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md). Each of the refugees wears randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat) (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance they may carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.

Beside one of the male refugees, there is a leveled [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe).
