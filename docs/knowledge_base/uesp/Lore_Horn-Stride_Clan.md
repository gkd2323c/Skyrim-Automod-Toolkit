# Horn-Stride Clan

*The battle is over and won. The Horn-Stride clan is gone. Banished to the dark below the crag.*
— *The Song of Gwyna*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TTWOT3-1)</sup> *The Horn-Stride was beaten back
But our heart was cut from us
We smile to one another in victory
The season will pass on to winter
And by next spring we will be gone
As fog in the light of day*
— *The Song of Gwyna.*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TTWOT3-1)</sup> Horn-Stride Clan was an ancient and legendary [clan](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). According to the tales they were active during the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#cite_note-Vateshran1-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#cite_note-Vateshran-3)</sup> They feature prominently in oral tales of the Reachfolk [Vateshrans](Lore_Vateshrans.md), though their depictions vary significantly from work to work. In some versions of the [Song of Gwyna](Lore_The_Translated_Works_of_Tosmorn,_III.md), the eponymous character [Gwyna](Lore_Gwyna.md) was portrayed as a huntress of the Horn-Stride Clan. However, in other depictions she was presented as a kindred to Chief [Rowolan](Lore_Rowolan.md), descendant of [Demigoddess](Lore_Demiprince.md) [Dearola](Lore_Dearola.md) instead. Vateshran songs state that the Horn-Stride clan was eradicated by Rowolan and his kin. This feat, however, cost chief Rowolan his life.

Although no exact date of Horn-Stride Clan's dissolution is provided, it is known that they were destroyed after [1E 930](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_930)<sup>[[nb 1]](#cite_note-Vateshran1-2)</sup> and long before [2E 302](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_302), when tales of their fall were considered ancient.<sup>[[nb 2]](#cite_note-Vateshran-3)</sup>
