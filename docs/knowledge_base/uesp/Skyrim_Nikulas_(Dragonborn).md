[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-npc-Nikulas_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nikulas_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nikulas_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nikulas_2.jpg) Nikulas **Nikulas** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md). He lives with his [mother](Skyrim_Edla.md) in their [house](Skyrim_Edla%27s_House.md).

He wears a [Skaal coat](Skyrim_Skaal_Coat.md), with a pair of [Skaal boots](Skyrim_Skaal_Boots.md) and a [Skaal hat](Skyrim_Skaal_Hat.md). He wields a [leveled one-handed weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md), and carries the [key](Skyrim_Edla%27s_House_Key.md) to his house and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

Nikulas wishes to leave the village and become an adventurer. However, his mother Edla will ask you to convince him to stay. You can intimidate or persuade him to stay in the village if your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill is high enough. A third option is to "help [him] get started" in which case you pay him money to start his life journey so he will agree to stay in the village for a little while longer. See the [quest page](Skyrim_Convince_Nikulas_to_stay_in_Skaal_Village.md) for details.

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"I've seen outsiders in our village before, but none were like you."*
- *"Perhaps I'll be the first Skaal to slay a dragon. That would be worthy of a tale!"*
- *"[Fanari](Skyrim_Fanari_Strong-Voice.md) says the Nords of Skyrim have [many gods](Lore_Nine_Divines.md), but none are as wise and powerful as the All-Maker."*
- *"Sometimes I wonder if we're the only Skaal. Could there be more villages like ours?"*
- *"I heard that you and [Tharstan](Skyrim_Tharstan.md) explored an [ancient crypt](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md). That sounds like an exciting adventure."* **(Completed [Lost Legacy](Skyrim_Lost_Legacy.md))**

If you leave conversation:

- *"You need to leave? Alright."*
- *"I'm sure you have more important things to do. Farewell, then."*

### Conversations
He can occasionally be seen arguing with his mother:

- **Edla**: *"I wonder sometimes if you are truly happy here, Nikulas."*
- **Nikulas**: *"I know why you're asking, mother. I am happy here, but I want to know more of the world outside our lands."*
- **Edla**: *"I know that Tharstan has filled your head with stories, but those stories don't tell of the dangers and hardships of the world."*
- **Nikulas**: *"I don't fear hardship and danger. I am Skaal. The only thing that I fear is not to follow my heart, and to die an old man filled with regret."*
- **Edla**: *"My heart is still wounded from the loss of your father. I cannot lose my only son so soon after. When time has worn away my grief, then you can go with my blessing."*
- **Nikulas**: *"Very well, mother. I will stay for now."*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Fate of the Skaal
During [The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md), Nikulas will be found helping [Storn](Skyrim_Storn_Crag-Strider.md) and [Finna](Skyrim_Finna.md) maintain the village's magical shield. Upon talking to him, he'll only say:

- *"Storn will protect us. We will be safe."*
- *"We are only safe so long as we concentrate..."*
- *"Do not break our concentration."*

### The Gardener of Men
When you [ask](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md) Storn to give out the secrets of the Skaal to [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md), Storn will end up betrayed and killed. During this, Nikulas may exclaim:

- *"[All-Maker](Lore_All-Maker.md), please help him!"*

As Storn lies dead, Nikulas will only say:

- *"[Frea](Skyrim_Frea.md)... what happened? What was that thing? I thought the village was safe now."*

If you speak to him, he'll say:

- *"I don't understand. I thought the village was safe. What are we going to do now?"*
- *"Poor Storn. I can't believe it."*

### Convince Nikulas to stay in Skaal Village

- **Your mother tells me that you want to leave the village.**
- *"That's right. I want to go out into the world and earn great glory. I want to hunt the deadliest creatures and claim vast riches. I am weary of living in this village. I want to do something with my life other than farm, fish and hunt deer. I know it must be hard for an adventurer such as you to understand this, but we Skaal live very boring lives."*

You have three options, all of which will attempt to make him stay in the village:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| "I do understand. In fact, I'm willing to help you get started." - "Take this. Just give your mother some more time." **(Bribe)** | *"You are? I admit, I was not expecting to hear that."* - **Passed**: *"Yes, you're right. She still mourns for my father, and having me near brings her comfort. You have my oath that I will not leave the village until my mother is ready to let me go."* <br> - **Failed**: *"I am grateful for your offer, outsider, but I am Skaal. All that I need, I was born with."* | **Passed**: Return to Edla <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| "The world is dangerous, and you're not ready for it." **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: *"Well, I know there is some danger, of course, but I didn't think it would be that bad. If the risk is truly so great, I will train with [Wulf Wild-Blood](Skyrim_Wulf_Wild-Blood.md) and hone my skills. Perhaps in three or four winters, I will truly be ready."* <br> **Failed**: *"There is no fear in my heart. My father's blood flows in my veins, and he was a mighty warrior. I will face any danger."* | **Passed**: Return to Edla <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| "You'd abandon your mother when she needs you most?" **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"There is truth in your words. She has not been herself since my father's death."* <br> **Failed**: *"I know that it would be difficult for her, at least for a time, but I am a man now. I have the right to choose what life I will."* | **Passed**: Return to Edla <br> **Failed**: Nothing |

If you have passed the speech check, Nikulas will end with:

- *"I can see that in my eagerness for adventure, I have forgotten my duties as a son and as a Skaal. You have helped me to see wisdom, and I thank you for that, Skaal-friend."*

## Notes
- Nikulas has dialogue recorded for after you have [freed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md) him from working at the [Wind Stone](Skyrim_Wind_Stone.md): *"We're the victims of some evil magic, but why?"*, *"I'm on my way to Skaal Village. Perhaps I'll see you there."* and *"You are a true hero. I thank you, friend."* as he returns to the village. However, he won't actually be found at the Stone, as he is in Skaal Village concentrating on the magical shield.
