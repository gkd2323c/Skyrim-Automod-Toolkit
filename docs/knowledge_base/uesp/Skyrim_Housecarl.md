# Housecarl


A few housecarls in the employ of jarls double as the general of their army, and are thus involved in planning war strategies. In terms of court politics, housecarls are the equal of [court wizards](Skyrim_Court_Wizard.md), but less important than [stewards](Skyrim_Steward.md) and, of course, the jarls. In summary, housecarls are faithful guardians who are very useful in both the royal courts of Skyrim and in the deepest depths of its ruins.

## Housecarls Available to You
<center>**"*I'll guard you, and all you own, with my life.*"**</center> Every personal housecarl:

- is primarily skilled in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) and [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md).
- reaches their maximum level at 50.
- is willing to commit any [crime](Skyrim_Crime.md) you request of them.

None of the housecarls appear in the game until you become a thane of their associated hold: Whiterun, Haafingar, The Rift, The Reach and Eastmarch in the original game (in each of which you can buy a house in the major city), with The Pale, Hjaalmarch and Falkreath Hold in the Hearthfire add-on (in each of which you can buy a plot of land to build a house in the country). There are no houses, plots of land or housecarls available in Winterhold.

Housecarls stay in their own rooms of their corresponding houses, except for the three added by Hearthfire, who patrol around the plot of land you have purchased. If the corresponding house for these three does not have an available bed to sleep in, they will go to a different player-built home (if one has a bed available) for the night, then spend the next morning walking back.

### [Argis the Bulwark](Skyrim_Argis_the_Bulwark.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg) Argis **Argis the Bulwark** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) living and working at [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Once you have purchased the home and attained the rank of [Thane](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Reach.md) of the Reach, Argis is appointed as your personal housecarl. ## Jarls' Housecarls
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Irileth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irileth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irileth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irileth.jpg) [Irileth](Skyrim_Irileth.md), housecarl of Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) Besides your personal housecarls, the jarls of Skyrim are also served by housecarls, who serve them as military advisors, generals, and personal bodyguards, although their exact function seems to vary from hold to hold (e.g., the housecarls of Winterhold seem to function more like generic courtiers than bodyguards).

Additionally, [Melaran](Skyrim_Melaran.md) and [Irnskar Ironhand](Skyrim_Irnskar_Ironhand.md) seem to function as housecarls for the thanes of Solitude, [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md) and [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md) respectively. They are never actively identified as such, but if their respective thane is killed, both will have dialogue indicating that they are serving as their provisional replacements. [Thadgeir](Skyrim_Thadgeir.md) may also be serving as his brother [Dengeir of Stuhn](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md)'s housecarl while he is thane, but this is not explicitly stated.
