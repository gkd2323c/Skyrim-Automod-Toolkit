[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Fort Linchal is an Imperial ruin found in the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md), in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Linchal is a historic landmark that dates back as far as before the [Empire of Cyrodiil](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md) in the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). In times, it was an outpost for the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and other times it once held a grand manor for a noble from the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLinchal Load-1)</sup>

## History
The earliest mention of Fort Linchal's activities was many years ago, when the county-seat, [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) was a large kingdom in [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCai H-2)</sup> Workers at the time of its construction noted a species of timid, orange and black [foxes](Lore_Fox.md) that defended their dens. They were displaced as construction proceeded but the workers tamed them and later took them home to the Imperial City, where the nobility took a liking to them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONTitian-3)</sup> The Linchal cohort rode south into [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) with the [Ontus cohort](Lore_Fort_Ontus.md) and the [Hastrel cohort](Lore_Fort_Hastrel.md) led by the famed General Thibaut of Kvatch. After the death of the General, the Linchal and Ontus cohorts fled back home but the Hastrel stayed and later built both Thibaut's Cairn and [Greenhill](Lore_Greenhill.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCai H-2)</sup>

The site was later the foundation of the Linchal Grand Manor, built as a summer home for an unnamed nobleman of the Imperial City in the style of [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenese.md) architecture. By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the noble had passed away<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLinchal Load-1)</sup> and his manor was made available for purchase.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLinchal-4)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Fort Linchal was just another ruined fort found across the great expanse of Cyrodiil, abandoned sometime during the relative peace of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBNerussa-5)</sup> What remained of the site was the old Temple of War and the Hall of Knights. It was inhabited by a coven of necromancers, who prepared an altar that created [Black Soul Gems](Lore_Black_Soul_Gem.md). They used a phenomenon called the [Shade of the Revenant](Lore_Shade_of_the_Revenant.md), where a purple light shines down from the night sky and the [Necromancer's Moon](Lore_Necromancer%27s_Moon.md), which enhances the soul gem. The altar in Fort Linchal was one of four of its kind found throughout the province at the time.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HSN-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-NM-7)</sup>
