Ruinachs are a race of four-armed [Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Daedra) that serve as [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s elite marauders. They are considered the Sword and the Voice of Dagon,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive1-1)</sup> and have come to refer to themselves as the **Fifth Hand of Mehrunes Dagon**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BPTMR-2)</sup> They are fanatical followers of their lord, and believe themselves an extension of his will. Indeed, as the physical manifestation of Dagon's fiery malevolence,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BPTMR-2)</sup> they share their lord's destructive tendencies. And although they are unintelligent, they make up for it in brutish strength.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LAMDDSE-3)</sup>

## History

### Genesis
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-render-Ruinach.jpg/200px-ON-render-Ruinach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Ruinach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Ruinach.jpg) Encounter with a Ruinach In [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920), the [Skeffington Coven](Lore_Skeffington_Coven.md) of High Rock took in an exiled Dunmeri woman named [Turala](Lore_Turala.md), the former lover of the Duke of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), [Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Brindisi_Dorom.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> However, after a trip to [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), the Skeffington Coven was massacred by an [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) raiding party. Turala was falsely led to believe that the massacre was the work of assassins sent by Brindisi Dorom himself. Enraged, the witch called forth the Prince of Destruction to destroy Mournhold and slay her former lover, and he complied.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

During the carnage, [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) and Sotha Sil arrived to stop the Prince. The battlefield was lit by a barrage of flame which fell indiscriminately. Almalexia plunged [Hopesfire](Lore_Hopesfire.md) into Dagon's flesh and carved him from within. Meanwhile, Sotha Sil, enacting vengeance for Ald Sotha, used "god-bronze whips" to lash the Prince into submission. Wounded, Sil allegedly whispered Dagon's Nymic, and the Prince "exploded throughout all time".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TTIS8-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-2920v11-7)</sup> The [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostles.md) believe that this battle between Mehrunes Dagon and the Tribunal marked the birth of "Sarmissonays'um ghoul-thing[s]", which emerged from Dagon's own flayed skin.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TTIS8-6)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BPTMR-2)</sup> The [Kyn](Lore_Kyn.md) see this as nothing more than "inane blunder".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BPTMR-2)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LAMDDSE-3)</sup>

### Dagon's Ambition
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ruinachs were deployed to the Blackwood region. [<sup>(?)</sup>](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Pages_Missing_Data)
