How We Came to Fly *A story about why Khenarthi carries True Cats to the Sands Behind the Stars*
---
Before [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md) defied [Ahnurr](Lore_Ahnurr.md) and the [world](Lore_Nirn.md) was born, [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md) flew swift and high, beyond even great [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md)'s reach. Though she was boundless and free, she had no one to share her joy with, so she begged her mother to give her someone to share the skies. [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md) gave her [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) in the night, [Magrus](Lore_Magrus.md) in the day, and [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) in the between, but each could only follow the path meant for them, and none could truly share Khenarthi's joy. Seeing this, Azurah spoke to Khenarthi a secret only they would share.

When one of Azurah's faithful [children](Lore_Khajiit.md) reached the end of their life, Khenarthi would snatch them up from [Nirni](Lore_Nirni.md)'s jealous claws and spirit them away to the [Sands Behind the Stars](Lore_Sands_Behind_the_Stars.md). Thus, our chosen people were granted a path to [Llesw'er](Lore_Llesw%27er.md), and Khenarthi could finally share her joy.
