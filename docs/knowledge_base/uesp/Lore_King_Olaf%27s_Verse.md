King Olaf's Verse *A lost part of the Poetic Edda, sung during the burning of Olaf's effigy in Solitude*
---
*Note: The blocks of numbers that appear in this text are actually unreadable smudges when seen in the game.*

*A special font is used to create these smudges, which appears as numbers in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) (i.e., "12 2345 123 12345123", etc.)*
---
O, [Olaf](Lore_Olaf_One-Eye.md), our subjugator, the one-eyed betrayer;
death-dealing demon and dragon-killing King.
Your legend is lies, lurid and false;
your cunning capture of [Numinex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Numinex), a con for the ages.

[![SR-book-King Olaf's Verse 01.png](https://images.uesp.net/8/83/SR-book-King_Olaf%27s_Verse_01.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-King_Olaf%27s_Verse_01.png)

Olaf grabbed power, by promise and threat;
From [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) to [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md), they fell to their knees;
But [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) stood strong, [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s truest protectors.
Olaf's vengeance was instant, inspired and wicked.

[![SR-book-King Olaf's Verse 02.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/0e/SR-book-King_Olaf%27s_Verse_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-King_Olaf%27s_Verse_02.png)

So ends the story of Olaf the liar, a thief and a scoundrel we of Solitude commit to the fire. In Solitude bards [train](Lore_Bards_College.md) for their service, they also gather each year and burn a King who deserves it.
