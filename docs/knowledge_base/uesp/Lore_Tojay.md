# Tojay

*When Jode is waxing and Jone is new
Then shall be born the Tojay
Dancers in the Dark
And Masters of Forbidden Arts
They tell us the will of the Moons
And wield the magicks beyond
So pure are their souls
Only Moonlight echoes forth.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/e/ec/Mwaxing_Snew.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Snew.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Snew.jpg) Tojay are born when Masser is waxing and Secunda is new<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Tojay are a [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Little is known of the Tojay, except that they live in the southern marshes and jungle regions of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup> such as in the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> They are said to be much like the [Cathay](Lore_Cathay.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup> Despite being depicted with digitigrade legs,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dancer-4)</sup> the Tojay have also been described as having plantigrade legs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup>

Older stories give them the title of "Dancers in the Dark", and claim they can tell the will of the Moons while wielding strange [magics](Lore_Magic.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> [Statues](Lore_Statues.md) are built that depict them as dancers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dancer-4)</sup>
