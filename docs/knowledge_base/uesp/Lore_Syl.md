Lady Syl was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) madwoman and Duchess of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) at the beginning of the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433). She resided in the House of Dementia in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Syl was known for her extreme paranoia; her madness was such that she saw plots and conspiracies everywhere, rendering her a prisoner in her own house. She is associated with two artifacts: [Ruin's Edge](Lore_Ruin%27s_Edge.md) and [Nerveshatter](Lore_Nerveshatter.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

At some point, Syl had a forbidden affair with [Thadon](Lore_Thadon.md), the Duke of Mania. When this controversy was discovered by her subjects, a conspiracy formed to assassinate Syl for associating with the enemy. In 3E 433, [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to Syl's court to become a courtier of the House of Dementia. The Hero was named Grand Inquisitor and successfully uncovered the conspiracy, which included citizens of [Crucible](Lore_Crucible.md) as well as a member of Syl's [Dark Seducer](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) personal guard.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

The Hero later began the Ritual of Accession to become a Duke or Duchess of Mania or Dementia, although it is unknown if they chose to replace Syl or Thadon. Regardless, the surviving ruler promptly defected to [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md) and became a [Priest of Order](Lore_Priest_of_Order.md), betraying the Army of Sheogorath and ultimately perishing within the tunnels of [the Fountainhead](Lore_The_Fountainhead.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
