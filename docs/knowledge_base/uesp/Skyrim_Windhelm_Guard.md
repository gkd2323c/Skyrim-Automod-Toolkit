[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-npc-Windhelm_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Windhelm_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Windhelm_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Windhelm_Guard.jpg) Windhelm Guard **Windhelm Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). These guards serve under Jarl [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). Should the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) forces gain control of Windhelm, they will be replaced by [Imperial guards](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard.md) under the service of Jarl [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md).
