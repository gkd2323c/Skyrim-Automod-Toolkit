[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-npc-Evethra.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Evethra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Evethra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Evethra.jpg) Evethra **Evethra** is an arcane blacksmith who researched how to make the arms and armaments from [Amber](Skyrim_Amber.md) and [Madness Ore](Skyrim_Madness_Ore.md), and has gone insane as a result. She is a part of the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), and killing her will yield you the necessary journal that will grant you the ability to craft Amber and Madness weapons and armor.

She wears the unique [Arcane Blacksmith's Apron](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith%27s_Apron.md) with a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e), and carries an [Iron Dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) as well as minor loot. She knows the [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md), or [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md) spell, as well as [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md), and [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).

She has set up camp at the Nordic ruins north of [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md), using the table as a true madwoman's altar: [bloody rags](Skyrim_Bloody_Rags.md), tools, and chunks of ore join the two ingots of [Refined Moonstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone) already present. An assortment of swords of varying materials, some enchanted, are stuck in the ground around the table.
