A Child's Tamriel Bestiary **by [Shane gro-Orath](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Shane_gro-Orath) (Clever, no? Ha, ha!)** *A children's rhyming alphabet book*
---
A is for [Alit](Lore_Alit.md), a two-legged lizard
With enough underbite to swallow a wizard

B is for [Banekin](Lore_Banekin.md), a mean [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) minion
Whose masters are found on the [planes of Oblivion](Lore_Planes_of_Oblivion.md)

C is for [Chaurus](Lore_Chaurus.md), big bug of the [snow lands](Lore_Skyrim.md)
Don't try to pet, or he'll snip off your hands!

D is for [Duneripper](Lore_Duneripper.md), of [desert so deep](Lore_Alik%27r.md)
It'll chomp off your legs from below as you sleep

E is for [Eagle](Lore_Eagle.md), so loved by the [Elves](Lore_Mer.md)
They plaster its image all over themselves

F is for [Faded Wraith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Faded_Wraith), haunting the tomb
Lurking in dark to deliver your doom

G is for [Giant](Lore_Giant.md), of Skyrim I've heard
He keeps Woolly [Mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md), and tends to his herd

H is for [Hoarvor](Lore_Hoarvor.md), huge tick of the wood
That's eager to catch you and drain all your blood!

I is for [Imp](Lore_Imp.md), an obnoxious pest
Who'll fry you with spells for the sake of a jest

J is for [Jackal](Lore_Jackal.md), who haunts [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md)
With mouth at the right height to nip out your eye

K is for [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md), in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) found
They live in bug tunnels far beneath the ground

L is for [Lamia](Lore_Lamia.md), snake-hag of the wild
Who hopes she might corner a lost little child

M is for [Mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md), annoyance ubiquitous
Nasty, unpleasant, ugly, iniquitous

N is for [Nix-Hounds](Lore_Nix-Hound.md), pack-hunting bugs
[Alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md) render their flesh to make [drugs](Lore_Drugs.md)

O is for [Ogre](Lore_Ogre.md), whose wits are quite dim
But if one should catch you, your prospects are grim

P is for [Pigs](Lore_Pig.md), relatives of the [Boars](Lore_Boar.md)
That [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) will ride instead of a [horse](Lore_Horse.md)

R is for [Raven](Lore_Raven.md), dark [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md)'s totem
I hear hers can talk, and then you can quote 'em!

S is for [Spriggan](Lore_Spriggan.md), root, bark, and bough
It hides in that thicket—it's watching you now!

T is for [Thunderbug](Lore_Thunderbug.md), vast shocking beetle
Its pincers will get you, or its serrated feet'll

U is for [Undead](Lore_Undead.md), [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md)'s slaves
Apocalypse comes when they rise from their graves

V is for [Vampire](Lore_Vampire.md), ready to drill ya
'Cause she's got a case of [Porphyric Hem'philia](Lore_Porphyric_Hemophilia.md)

W is for [Wisp](Lore_Will-o-the-Wisp.md), evil glow-ball that beckons
Don't follow, or you'll be seen no more, I reckons

X is for [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md), Daedric assassin
Who'll pop off your head on a whim as he's passin'

Y is for [Yokudan Warhorse](Lore_Yokudan_Warhorse.md), the [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md)
Knight's mount that he rides when he goes off to warfare

Z is for [Zombies](Lore_Zombie.md) —you knew they'd be last
Run, don't be walking: a few are quite fast!
