[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_Climate.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_Climate.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_Climate.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_Climate.png) The jungle region (dark green) of the Iliac Bay The Bantha, or the **Banthan Jungle,**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot IB-1)</sup> is a lush tropical jungle region in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The precise location of the Bantha is unknown; however, it is considered part of the Iliac Bay. The people of the Bantha are known to celebrate two holidays. [Aduros Nau](Lore_Aduros_Nau.md) is celebrated on the 28th of [Sun's Dawn](Lore_Calendar.md#Sun.27s_Dawn) to commemorate the transition from the winter, and the [Feast of the Tiger](Lore_Feast_of_the_Tiger.md) is observed on the 14th of [Last Seed](Lore_Last_Seed.md). Though the Feast of the Tiger presumably originated as a means of venerating an obscure [Tiger God](Lore_Tiger_God.md), it is now a thanksgiving.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot IB-1)</sup>

The [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md) holiday [Riglametha](Lore_Riglametha.md) translates into *"grateful-offering"* in the Banthan dialect of the [Old Redguard](Lore_Yoku.md) language. It is a festival which serves to thank the gods for their blessings.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ghraewaj-3)</sup> The Barony of Lainlyn is found in a forested area of the Iliac Bay, east of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).

## Notes
- The Bantha is one of several names of [regions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Region_Numbers) that are not assigned to a province in *[TES II: Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*, and so they are believed to be unused in-game. This may mean it is found in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).
