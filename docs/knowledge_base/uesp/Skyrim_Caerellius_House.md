## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance are three unusable barrels and an empty cart. To the right of the entrance are three crates, four unusable barrels, and three [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants. Along the back of the building on the waterfront is an ash yam urn among more crates. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

### Caerellius House
The entrance leads into a cooking and living area on the lower floor of the house. Along the south wall is a table holding four [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot), three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), four [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), two [green apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), three bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), two [goat cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), two [eidar cheese wedges](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), and a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), with copies of *[History of Raven Rock, Vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._I)*, *[Vol. II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._II)*, and *[Vol. III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._III)* on the wall ledge behind the table. In the southwest corner is a chair and two meat barrels, one of which has a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top. Along the west wall are three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging from a rack, a chair, four bottles of Nord mead on the wall ledge, three [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid hanging from the ceiling, and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand over a fire. In the northwest corner is an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox) on the wall ledge. Along the north wall is a copy of *[The Reclamations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reclamations)* on the wall ledge and a doorway to the next room. In the northeast corner is a meat barrel and copies of *[Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Confessions_of_a_Dunmer_Skooma_Eater)* and *[The House of Troubles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles)* on the wall ledge.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall are three sacks, two meat barrels, and a set of shelves holding a [boiled creme treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), four bottles of Nord mead, two bottles of wine, two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a sample of [Felsaad tern feathers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern_Feathers), and a cast iron pot containing three red apples. Along the north wall beneath the stairs are two chairs and a small round table holding two loaves of bread, and a wood pile.

The small circular room on the upper floor is the sleeping area. To the northwest at the top of the stairs is a sack and an end table holding two filled [lesser soul gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem), a large and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), and three loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md). To the southwest is an owned double bed. To the southeast is a chest, a sack, and a set of shelves holding a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing), and copies of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_of_Skyrim)*, *[Pirate King of the Abecean](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean)*, and *[The True Noble's Code](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_True_Noble%27s_Code)*.
