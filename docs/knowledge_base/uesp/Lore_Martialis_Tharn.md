[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/ON-item-Golden_Claw.jpg/200px-ON-item-Golden_Claw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Golden_Claw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Golden_Claw.jpg) Golden Claw Martialis Tharn was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the noble [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), and a [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MT-1)</sup> of the Stonefire Cult devoted to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRT-2)</sup> He was a subordinate of his relative [Javad Tharn](Lore_Javad_Tharn.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGC-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he led an attack on the Do'Krin Monastery against the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) monks of the Order of the Claw in order to retrieve the Golden Claw, a powerful relic from the era of Mane [Tullar-dra](Lore_Tullar-dra.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Azahrr-4)</sup> The Stonefire managed to tear down several shrines to the Bright Moons. These shrines were the keys to entering the sanctum. His endeavors were thwarted by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) who wielded the blessings of the Bright Moons and the spirit of the [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) who attuned their connection to Jone and Jode. Together the two managed to restore the altars damaged by the Stonefire Cult group led by Martialis. Martialis Tharn, who was attempting to channel energy from the Guardian Senche of the Do'Krin Sanctum to disarm the defenses to the relic was found by the Vestige, who fought, and eventually slain him, ending the threat to the Order of the Claw.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGC-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rid Thar Ri Datta-5)</sup>
