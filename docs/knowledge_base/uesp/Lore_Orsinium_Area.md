[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/DF-map-Orsinium_Area.png/200px-DF-map-Orsinium_Area.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Orsinium_Area.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Orsinium_Area.png) Orsinium Area ca. 3E 405 The Orsinium Area was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) political entity situated in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, inhabited by [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) and ruled from the city of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). It bordered [Menevia](Lore_Menevia_(county).md), [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), and the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md). The [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup>

## History
Before it was occupied by the Orcs of Orsinium, it was a large tract of unclaimed land between Menevia and Wayrest and in [3E 399](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_399), it was contested between two forces, [Gortwog gro-Nagorm](Lore_Gortwog_gro-Nagorm.md) and Lord Bowyn. Their duel to decide ownership took place in the palace of Bowyn's cousin, Lord Berylith in Wayrest, and both combatants used Orcish armor, which played a factor in Gortwog's victory. He decided to name the land in honor of his forefathers, Orsinium.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HOPTTO-2)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), the region became a part of the larger kingdom of Orsinium.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWi TW-3)</sup>
