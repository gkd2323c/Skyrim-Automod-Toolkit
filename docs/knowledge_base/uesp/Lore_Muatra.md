# Muatra

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/ON-item-Muatra.jpg/200px-ON-item-Muatra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Muatra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Muatra.jpg) Muatra, as seen on Vivec's back Muatra,<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> meaning **Milk Taker**, is a spear wielded by [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), one of the living gods of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV14-1)</sup> In his younger years, Vivec had a chance encounter with the Hortator of the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) [Indoril Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md), telling Nerevar that he would make a legend of the [netch](Lore_Netch.md) longhook in his possession and that it would be his weapon of choice in Nerevar's guard.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-WMBTM-2)</sup> The longhook was in his possession from the days of his youth where he bonded with his father Irdri who was a netchiman by trade.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOV37-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-LOV0-4)</sup>

During the event known as the Pomegranate Banquet in which Vivec and [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) made an unholy union, Vivec noticed his body was tenderly used and pointed this out; Molag Bal replied, stating that his love was accidentally shaped like a spear. Vivec bit new words onto Molag's spear so it could give more than ruin.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV14-1)</sup> Disaster was struck when the Chimer, Demons, and Monsters who bore witness to this took out their spears and imitated this biting which caused the earth to crack, spawning a new terrible race that was born of the biters and caused destruction. Vivec, however, had learned a new secret from Molag Bal when he had bitten him, and thus was able to change his own spear into a more terrible thing.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV14-1)</sup>

Vivec named his new spear Muatra. The spear in this new form had the ability to turn anyone Vivec touched with it barren and wither them to bones.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV14-1)</sup> Vivec used Muatra to destroy many things, including: the biters and their progeny;<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV14-1)</sup> his own progeny he created with Molag Bal, including eight particularly powerful children who escaped his initial wrath; the last of the [N'chorbal](Lore_N%27chorbal.md), which were terrible rock-skin bugs native to [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md);<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-DGP-5)</sup> and, according to some legends, even Nerevar himself.<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-DFTDE-6)</sup> Muatra, along with [Vivec's Lyre](Lore_Vivec%27s_Lyre.md), is often depicted in shrines and tapestries as the harp and spear are symbols of Vivec.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shrine MW-7)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cont Cufflink-8)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Banner ON-9)</sup>
