## First Century

2E 1 — Beginning of the Second Era.

- By decree of Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md), year one of the Second Era begins on the first of Morning Star. The marking of the new era commemorates the demise of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) and Versidue-Shaie's official assumption of stewardship of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2920L-2)</sup>

2E 4 — Versidue-Shaie establishes the Potentate wolf riders for the [Reman purges](Lore_Reman_Purges.md), causing a panic in [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-APWolf Cub-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-APWolf-4)</sup>
2E 5 — Companies using the new enchanted barding designed by Versidue-Shaie's battlemages received their orders to [hunt and kill Reman loyalists](Lore_Reman_Purges.md). Though initially only horses, ponies and more earned the special barding and training.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-APPony-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-APCharger-6)</sup>
2E 7 — The Potentate sabre cat mounted regiment combed Skyrim for [Reman loyalists](Lore_Reman_Purges.md), killing Solitude's wolves using "magical roars".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-APSabre Cub-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-APSabre Cat-8)</sup>
2E 11 — Versidue-Shaie converts the isle of [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) into a penal colony for [Reman](Lore_Reman_III.md) -aligned [political prisoners](Lore_Reman_Purges.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONSHV6-9)</sup>
2E 12 — [Tjurhane Fyrre](Lore_Tjurhane_Fyrre.md) publishes *Nature of Ayleidic Poesy*, an account of his people and religion.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TWE-10)</sup>
2E 23 — The first Imperial bear cavalry come into by the 10th Legion, walk through Leyawiin's streets each week in a bear parade.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-APBear Cub-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-APBear-12)</sup>
2E 25 — The Potentate bought several herds of camels for delivery to Versidue-Shaie, as part of the '[Reman desert pursuit](Lore_Reman_Purges.md)' where loyalists were chased by glowing camels from the enchanted barding.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-APCamel-13)</sup>
2E 27 — Dark Elf assassins [chase Reman's loyalists](Lore_Reman_Purges.md) around Red Mountain in Morrowind on luminous Potentate guar steeds, keeping the trained calves in Mournhold.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-APGuar Calf-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-APGuar-15)</sup>
2E 53 — A [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) cult gains footholds in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Pottery Codex-16)</sup>

## Third Century

2E 210 — Mavon Ules becomes Archmagister of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-AMs A-22)</sup>

- Magister Mavon performs a spell of immense power to bind the [Skaafin](Lore_Skaafin.md) Mysissa to his service, supposedly through the use of her [paleonymic](Lore_Paleonymic.md). This act cements his position as archmagister for almost a decade, when the title is then usurped by Magister Varona Githrano.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-AMs A-22)</sup>

2E 227 — [Tjurhane Fyrre](Lore_Tjurhane_Fyrre.md), the great [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) sage, dies.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TWE-10)</sup>
2E 230 — The first [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) is formed.

- Sickened by the foolishness of his superiors in the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) founds the new guild in [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-DFC-23)</sup>
- At around this time, a triad of [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) — [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) and [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) —make a deal with the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sload.md) K'Tora to acquire the Heart of Transparent Law—the Stone of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md). K'Tora uses mind magic to make [Ritemaster Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md), the leader of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), steal the Heart. Trying to foil K'Tora's plan, Iachesis makes a deal with Nocturnal to learn a spell with which to hide the Heart in a language that could only be used by mortals. Unbeknownst to all, Iachesis hides the Heart within himself—but soon forgets these events, due to K'Tora's magic.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-24)</sup> Shortly thereafter, the island of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) —home of the Psijic Order—is removed from Nirn by the Order and goes missing.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-FOA-25)</sup>

2E 231 — The [Order of the Lamp](Lore_Order_of_the_Lamp.md), a knightly order, is formed to protect the newly established Mages Guild.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-OOTMG-26)</sup>
2E 280 — Control of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) islands returns to the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md).

- On account of their close ties with Versidue-Shaie, [House Mornard](Lore_House_Mornard.md) inherit ever greater power in the Systres beginning in the 2E 110s. As Versidue-Shaie began consolidating resources in Cyrodiil to combat the mounting costs of ruling the fractious mainland, the Systres were effectively privatized and placed in the care of House Mornard.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONSHV6-9)</sup>

2E 283 — Potentate Versidue-Shaie declares martial law at the Council of Bardmont.

- Following the destruction of his fortress at [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md), Versidue-Shaie convenes the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) and declares martial law throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), warning the rebellious leaders of his vassal kingdoms to disband their private armies or face his wrath. This sparks 37 years of intense conflict, at the end of which Versidue-Shaie's Imperial legions stand victorious as the sole army in the land.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-HOTFG-27)</sup>

2E 283 — An ill-fated Imperial expedition discovers the entrance to the ruins of [Garlas Malatar](Lore_Garlas_Malatar.md).

- Guided by the writings of the late [Tjurhane Fyrre](Lore_Tjurhane_Fyrre.md), an Imperial expedition surveys the surface ruins of the Ayleid city of [Garlas Malatar](Lore_Garlas_Malatar.md) and uncovers the entrance to the city's depths. The cohort constructs Fort Mistwatch within the ruins to serve as a base while they plunder the city, but soon every member of the expedition is forcibly converted into [Meridian Purified](Lore_Meridian_Purified.md) by the guardians of the [Wrathstone](Lore_Wrathstone.md). The entrance to Garlas Malatar is once again lost, and the expedition's disappearance remains unexplained until three centuries later.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-DOMQ-28)</sup><sup>[[29]](#cite_note-FMMWTDOM-29)</sup>

2E 284 — The [Draggin Tale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Draggin_Tale) tavern on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) was established.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-30)</sup>
2E 299 — Potentate Versidue-Shaie attempts to remove [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md)'s bridges

- The Potentate's plan was to allow large sailing vessels to pass from [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) to the [River Niben](Lore_River_Niben.md). Leyawiin's Chamber of Legates, fearing that the additional merchant traffic would simply sail through Leyawiin without stopping, managed to stall the project until it was abandoned.<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-TGt L-31)</sup>

## Fourth Century

2E 301 — The kingdom of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) is founded.<sup>[[nb 1]](#cite_note-32)</sup>
2E 307 — The Ghost Harrowing occurs in Stormhaven.

- The Menevia Catacombs and Menevia Abbey were abandoned afterwards.<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-ONload Pariah-33)</sup>

2E 309 — The [Elsweyr Confederacy](Lore_Elsweyr.md) is founded.

- [Keirgo](Lore_Keirgo.md) and [Eshita](Lore_Eshita.md), rulers of the minor kingdoms [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) and [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md), marry. The kingdoms are combined to create the province of Elsweyr, sparking a great class war which almost results in outside intervention.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE3Sugar-34)</sup><sup>[[34]](#cite_note-PGE1E-35)</sup><sup>[[35]](#cite_note-EGTE-36)</sup>

2E 309 — Reign of [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md), [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-PGE1E-35)</sup><sup>[[33]](#cite_note-PGE3Sugar-34)</sup>
2E 311 — Mane Rid-Thar-ri'Datta reveals the Riddle'Thar Epiphany at Rawl'kha Temple.

- The revelation marks a key event in modern Khajiiti theology.<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-ONload Rawl-37)</sup>

2E 312 — The [Seamount Orcs](Lore_Seamount_Clan.md) take over [Betony](Lore_Betony.md) (approximate date).

- The invasion is said to have taken place nine generations before the Alliance War.<sup>[[37]](#cite_note-ONload Bet-38)</sup> Under Orcish rule, the island would be known as *Betnikh*.

2E 320 — [Dinieras-Ves, "The Iron"](Lore_Dinieras-Ves.md) founded [The Syffim](Lore_The_Syffim.md), the forerunner of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md).

- Since Potentate Versidue-Shaie had destroyed all armies but his own, the Syffim were intended to act as a hired army to suppress brigandage in the provinces.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-HOTFG-27)</sup>

2E 321 — The [Guild Act](Lore_Guild_Act.md) is approved.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-HOTFG-27)</sup>
2E 324 — Potentate Versidue-Shaie is murdered.

- The Potentate is murdered in his palace in [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md) by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md).<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-TBOD-39)</sup>

2E 324 — [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md) inherits the title of Potentate.

- Potentate Savirien-Chorak succeeds his father, Versidue-Shaie. Although noted to be a poor administrator who was prone to mishandling the crises that occurred during his reign, Savirien-Chorak is credited with reversing the ban on forming local armies, and accepting [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) into the Empire.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-HOTFG-27)</sup><sup>[[39]](#cite_note-HKEAYQ-40)</sup>

2E 326 — Fall of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md).

- Anequinan Tribal Rebels take the capital of Ne Quin-Al, killing most of the royal family. The [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) intervenes and instates a rotational system where control shifts between the desert tribes and city nobility based on the Moons.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-Aa PAI-41)</sup><sup>[[34]](#cite_note-PGE1E-35)</sup>

2E 342 — King Donel Deleyn of Daggerfall is crowned.

- Donel Deleyn, head of the noble House Deleyn, assumes the throne of Daggerfall.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-TIEGTTHR-42)</sup>

2E 358 — Earliest known written record of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md)

- A note was found beneath the floorboards of an abandoned house in the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) village of Jallenheim. Depicting a war between two shadowy organizations, scholars speculate that this is a rare piece of evidence of the war between the Morag Tong and its hated offshoot, the Dark Brotherhood. The Brotherhood was probably in existence before this date, but its secrecy shrouds its true origins, though it was most likely formed by several ex-members of the Morag Tong.<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-FAD-43)</sup>

2E 360 — Earliest known date of a person enlisting the services of the Dark Brotherhood (speculated)

- This reference was found in the journals of Queen [Arlimahera](Lore_Arlimahera.md) of Hegathe.<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-TBOD-39)</sup>

2E 365 — Siege of Vastyr

- A fleet of [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) pirates assaulted [Vastyr](Lore_Vastyr.md) on the island of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md) in one of the bloodiest moments in the island's history. Ships bombarded the city while sea mages created a large tidal wave that washed over the city and destroyed it.<sup>[[43]](#cite_note-ONSHVastyr-44)</sup><sup>[[44]](#cite_note-ONSo V-45)</sup>

2E 369 — The [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) end the alliance between the dragon [Nahfahlaar](Lore_Nahfahlaar.md) and King [Casimir II](Lore_Casimir_II.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md)

- Although Nahfahlaar loses his protector, he manages to escape slaughter.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-AOD-21)</sup>

2E 373 — The [Atlas of Dragons](Lore_Atlas_of_Dragons.md) is written

- Brother Mathnan compiles an incomplete list of dragons slain by the Dragonguard and Akaviri since the time of the order's founding.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-AOD-21)</sup>

## Fifth Century

2E 400 — Potentate Savirien-Chorak holds a celebration to commemorate four centuries of [potentate rule](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md).

- Festivities in the Imperial City include many games, revelries and a poetry competition.<sup>[[45]](#cite_note-PCo V-46)</sup>

2E 401 — King Donel Deleyn of Daggerfall dies.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-TIEGTTHR-42)</sup>
2E 401 — King Anton I of Daggerfall is crowned.

- On 17 Hearthfire, Prince Anton inherits the throne of his father Donel Deleyn, becoming King Anton I of Daggerfall.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Jo MS-47)</sup>

2E 406 — Scandal rocks the [Camoran](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) factions in [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).

- After it is revealed that the settlement of [Cormount](Lore_Cormount.md) might be the source of dozens of potential heirs, its population is nearly doubled in one week as opportunists, the curious, and the cunning arrive in town. For months thereafter, weddings were a daily occurrence.<sup>[[47]](#cite_note-TROC-48)</sup>

2E 408 — Queen Sylvie, mother of King Anton I dies.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Jo MS-47)</sup>
2E 409 — King Anton I survives an assassination attempt.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Jo MS-47)</sup>
2E 412 — Earliest mention of the name of the Dark Brotherhood.

- This reference was found in the journals of the Blood Queen Arlimahera of Hegathe. "...with the help of the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md) and her Dark Brotherhood, the secret arsenal my family has employed since my grandfather's time." It can be surmised that the Dark Brotherhood was already well organized in 2E 360 if her grandfather truly used them.<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-TBOD-39)</sup>

2E 418 — [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) is born.

- Abnur Tharn, the future Chancellor of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) is born into the [Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) family.<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-Cot FC4-49)</sup>

2E 420 — The settlement of New Joy is burned to the ground.

- Tensions reach boiling point between the native [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of Cormount and migrants of New Joy.<sup>[[47]](#cite_note-TROC-48)</sup>

2E 422 — Formation of Abah's Landing Merchant Lords

- Four mercantile houses begin working to elevate [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md) and create a center for trade and commerce between High Rock and Valenwood. Despite their best efforts, the mercantile houses slowly become more corrupt and eventually become major criminal enterprises and rulers of the city.<sup>[[49]](#cite_note-ALML1-50)</sup>

2E 425 — King Anton I survives a second assassination attempt.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Jo MS-47)</sup>
2E 428 — The [Order of the Silver Dawn](Lore_Order_of_the_Silver_Dawn.md) is founded.

- In response to the "Curse of the Crimson Moon" werewolf crisis in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), Adeliza Silver-Axe establishes the order after her beloved son is inflicted with [Lycanthropy](Lore_Lycanthropy.md).<sup>[[50]](#cite_note-PJ-51)</sup>

2E 430 — Potentate [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md) is assassinated, ending the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md).

- Savirien-Chorak and all of his heirs are murdered, ending the years of the Akaviri Potentate and the Second Empire of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-TBOD-39)</sup><sup>[[51]](#cite_note-PGE3C-52)</sup><sup>[[52]](#cite_note-PGE3V-53)</sup><sup>[[53]](#cite_note-PGE3All-54)</sup> Some historians date this event to 2E 431.<sup>[[54]](#cite_note-PGE1W-55)</sup>
- Orsinium loses the status of an Imperial territory as a result of the death of the last Potentate and the dissolution of the Second Empire.<sup>[[54]](#cite_note-PGE1W-55)</sup>

2E 431 — [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) splits into Eastern and Western kingdoms.

- After High King [Logrolf](Lore_Logrolf.md) is assassinated, Jarl [Svartr](Lore_Svartr.md) of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) challenges the legitimacy of Logrolf's heir, [Freydis](Lore_Freydis.md), and a [Moot](Lore_Moot.md) is convened. Although Freydis is accepted by the Crown of Verity and named High Queen in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), a partial Moot held in Solitude declares Svartr to be High King. From then on, Skyrim is divided into an Eastern kingdom, ruled by the successors of Freydis, and a Western kingdom, ruled by the successors of Svartr.<sup>[[55]](#cite_note-TCOF-56)</sup>

2E 431 — [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) is sacked by [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) forces.

- After losing its status as an Imperial territory, Orsinium is destroyed for the second time.<sup>[[56]](#cite_note-TRHOKE-57)</sup><sup>[[57]](#cite_note-OOS-58)</sup> Some sources claim it happened the following year.<sup>[[58]](#cite_note-TFOKR-59)</sup>

2E 432 — The [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) is re-established in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md).

- [Cavor Merula](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md), a warrior-priest of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), reforms the ancient order in response to the collapse of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md).<sup>[[59]](#cite_note-OOTH-60)</sup>

2E 434 — The [Pale Watch](Lore_Pale_Watch.md) arrive in Wrothgar

- King [Maxevian](Lore_Maxevian.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) sends the Pale Watch into [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) on a five year mission to ensure the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) never rebuild [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[60]](#cite_note-KMO-61)</sup>

2E 435 — The [Lamia](Lore_Lamia.md) Invasion

- The invasion is preceded by a storm that is believed to have originated at [the Island of Storms](Lore_Tempest_Island.md) off the northwestern coast of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[61]](#cite_note-MC-62)</sup>

2E 450 — King Anton I of Daggerfall dies.

- On 11 Hearthfire, King Anton I's forty-nine year reign ends with his death. He is sealed in a tomb with the spirit of Magiul Shiana, his former Seneschal, serving as his guardian.<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-Jo MS-47)</sup>

2E 450 — Queen Donella of Daggerfall is crowned.

- The daughter of Anton I, Donella, succeeds him to the throne of Daggerfall.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-TIEGTTHR-42)</sup>

2E 465 — [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) becomes Chancellor of [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md)

- Abnur Tharn heads the Elder Council and serves as the Imperial Battlemage to the Cyrodiilic Empire.<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-Cot FC4-49)</sup>

2E 467 — Jarl [Hakkvild Yashnag-Slayer](Lore_Hakkvild_Yashnag-Slayer.md) ends the [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) chiefdom in Skyrim.

- The Jarl of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) defeats [Yashnag gro-Yazgu](Lore_Yashnag_gro-Yazgu.md) and his champions in ritual trial by combat, and dissolves his thirty year chiefdom.<sup>[[57]](#cite_note-OOS-58)</sup> Orcish remnants flee west and establish Forsaken Stronghold in Bangkorai.<sup>[[62]](#cite_note-ONload Forsaken-63)</sup>

2E 467 — The [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) Bread Riots.

- The citizens of Kvatch revolt against the priests of the Cathedral of Akatosh after the red wheat blight devastates their crops for the second year in a row. Before rioters could burn down the city's Cathedral, the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) intervened and slaughtered half of the angry mob. By the next season the blight had run its course.<sup>[[59]](#cite_note-OOTH-60)</sup>

2E 478 — House Mornard exiles House Dufort from [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md)

- The culmination of centuries-long intrigues, assassinations and kidnappings came to a head when Duke Ruffe Mornard seized a full third of House Dufort's lands and exiled its leadership to Amenos, claiming sedition against the crown with a raft of forged documents and forced testimony.<sup>[[63]](#cite_note-ONSHV7-64)</sup>

2E 480 — Baz Swordbreaker's brigand army attacks Kvatch

- The charismatic [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) marauder leads his army to Kvatch, but is defeated by the Order of the Hour. The Primate of Kvatch thereafter grants the Order to form into an army during times of religious emergencies.<sup>[[59]](#cite_note-OOTH-60)</sup>

2E 485 — [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md) the Black Drake is born around this time.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-TIEGTTHR-42)</sup><sup>[[64]](#cite_note-SHot LE-65)</sup>
2E 486 — An Altmeri navy pursues a Maormeri fleet to [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md).

- After a fleet of Maormer ships is sighted off the coast of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md), King [Hidellith](Lore_Hidellith.md) orders his navy to give chase. The Altmer follow the Maormer through uncharted waters into an ambush near Pyandonea itself. Only one Altmeri ship survives to return to Alinor.<sup>[[65]](#cite_note-TMOP-66)</sup>

2E 489 — The Blacksap Movement is founded in [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md)

- The Cormount-based cultural organization hopes to formalize the Bosmer approach to the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md), thereby preventing the misunderstandings that led to burning of New Joy.<sup>[[47]](#cite_note-TROC-48)</sup>

2E 518 — [Moricar](Lore_Moricar.md), future emperor of Cyrodiil, is born.<sup>[[64]](#cite_note-SHot LE-65)</sup>
2E 521 — [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) is born.

- Emeric, the future King of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) and leader of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), is born to Pierric of Cumberland.<sup>[[66]](#cite_note-TATOAM-67)</sup>

2E 522 — King [Hurlburt](Lore_Hurlburt.md) of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) is crowned.

- The High King of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), Thassad II, dies from natural causes, being considerably ill and old at the time of his death. After his death, the leader of the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md), Baron [Volag](Lore_Volag.md), starts a revolution to reinstate the republic.<sup>[[141]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-151)</sup>

2E 864 — The [Battle of Hunding Bay](Lore_Battle_of_Hunding_Bay.md).

- Crown Prince [A'tor](Lore_A%27tor.md), son of Thassad II, heir to Hammerfell and leader of the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md), leads his forces in continual victory over the Forebears. Desperate, the Forebears ask Tiber Septim for help, who sends his forces led by Lord Admiral [Amiel Richton](Lore_Amiel_Richton.md), who destroys the Crown forces at the harbor of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md).<sup>[[141]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-151)</sup>

2E 864 — The Dwarven Reclamation Act is passed

- Giving the Imperial Archaeological Society free rein over Dwarven ruins.<sup>[[142]](#cite_note-Red NT-152)</sup>

2E 864 — The [Stros M'Kai Uprising](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai_Uprising.md).

- The date of the Redguard rebellion on the island of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) off the coast of Hammerfell. The [Restless League](Lore_Restless_League.md), led by siblings Cyrus and [Iszara](Lore_Iszara.md), destroys the Imperial fleet in the harbor of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) and defeats the small contingent of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) stationed on the island.<sup>[[143]](#cite_note-Redguard-153)</sup>
- Lord Amiel Richton, Provisional Governor of Stros M'Kai, dies at the hands of Cyrus, wielding a sword enchanted with the soul of Crown Prince A'tor.<sup>[[143]](#cite_note-Redguard-153)</sup>
- Baron Volag comes out of hiding and retakes the Forebear city of Sentinel. Following the two uprisings, Emperor Tiber Septim is forced to sign a [peace treaty](Lore_First_Treaty_of_Stros_M%27Kai.md) on terms more favorable to Hammerfell.<sup>[[143]](#cite_note-Redguard-153)</sup>

2E 865 — [Symmachus](Lore_Symmachus.md) is born.

- Symmachus, future general of the Imperial Legion and husband to Queen [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md), is born.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-DFC-23)</sup>

2E 882 — [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) reawakens.

- Dagoth Ur and his Ash Vampires awaken underneath the [Red Mountain,](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) where they bind themselves to the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md) and cause the mountain to erupt.<sup>[[144]](#cite_note-days_of_fire-154)</sup> They ambush the [Tribunes](Lore_Tribunal.md) during their annual ritual bathing in the Heart's power, and drove them off. From then on, the Tribunal was cut off from the Heart chamber, and would launch intermittent campaigns to attempt to force their way inside.<sup>[[145]](#cite_note-DUP-155)</sup>
- The event led to Sotha Sil to begin development on the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md), a synthetic replication of the Heart of Lorkhan.<sup>[[146]](#cite_note-Rt CC-156)</sup>

2E 893 — [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md) is born.

- Barenziah, future Queen of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and later [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), is born.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-DFC-23)</sup><sup>[[nb 7]](#cite_note-157)</sup>

2E 896 — Tiber Septim conquers Tamriel.

- Tiber Septim finishes conquering most of the lands of Tamriel with the aid of the Dwarven artifact [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) and the battlemage [Zurin Arctus](Lore_Zurin_Arctus.md), finally uniting Tamriel under the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md). The time of Tiber's reign is considered by many to have been a just and glorious one, among the best of Tamriel's history.<sup>[[147]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-158)</sup>
- The beginning of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) is pronounced by Tiber Septim at the end of the year.<sup>[[147]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-158)</sup>
