King Ramzi the Distrait was the ruler of the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He is remembered as a self-serving and weak-willed ruler who failed to protect his subjects from the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md). Ramzi regarded the plague as divine punishment from Ruptga for his personal failings. As his courtiers succumbed to the flu, Ramzi sealed himself in the [Samuruik](Lore_Samuruik.md) Palace and slowly starved to death because of his fear of contracting the virus, leaving the kingdom in chaos. As Sentinel fell into chaos, a young [Forebear](Lore_Forebear.md) noble named [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md), a descendant of High King [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), traveled from [Bergama](Lore_Bergama.md) and declared himself king of Sentinel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTH-1)</sup>

The Crowns believed Ramzi was usurped and acted with vehement repudiation against Fahara'jad; however, Forebear viziers and northern chieftains ratified his ascension.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SAMITA-2)</sup> Support for Fahara'jad was not unanimous. The [Crown](Lore_Crown.md) nobility in the southern cities of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md), [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md), [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md), and [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) supported [Grandee Zizzeen](Lore_Zizzeen.md)'s claim to the throne instead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRLo S-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cont Zizeen-4)</sup>
