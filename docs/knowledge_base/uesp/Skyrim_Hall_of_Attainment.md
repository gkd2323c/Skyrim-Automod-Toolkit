## Hall of Attainment

### Ground Floor
Each floor is split into eight sections or cubicles. There are three cubicles on each side with the entrance and stairwell opposite each other and separating them downstairs. Upstairs the dining room and stairwell do the same. The stairwell continues up to an exit onto the roof, from which you can access a similar entrance at the top of the [Hall of Countenance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance), and there are two entrances to the [Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum). These are on either side of the tower that reaches above the two halls. Most of the sleeping quarters contain barrels and sacks of [food](Skyrim_Food.md). There are also many bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). All wardrobes found within the hall contain various items of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes). There are many pieces of fine crockery including [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), [bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), etc. throughout the hall. Items in cubicles that are assigned to specific people are generally owned and taking them will be considered stealing.

Upon entering the hall there are moth-eaten college banners on either side wall and a focal point that is releasing magical energy upwards in the center of the lower level of the tower. Proceeding clockwise around the floor the first cubicle is [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir)'s sleeping quarters. His cubicle contains an owned single bed coming out from the wall with small bedside tables on either side of the bed. To the left is a tall wardrobe and a couple of chairs, while to the right on the floor are two [drums](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum) and a narrow wooden table against the wall with some [red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and [green apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple) on top. Above the table are two wall shelves holding three empty [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) —one [common](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem), one [lesser](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem) and one [petty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem) —as well as two [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) flowers, a bowl of [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm), a bowl of [fire salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts), a bowl of [frost salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md), an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing), two [swamp fungal pods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod), and a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat).

The second cubicle is [Brelyna Maryon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brelyna_Maryon)'s sleeping quarters and it contains an owned single bed coming out from the wall with a small bedside table and chair on either side of it. To the left of the bed is an unlocked long [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) and to the right are two tall wardrobes separated by a small wardrobe. On the cupboard are two filled soul gems, one common and one lesser. The third cubicle is [Onmund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Onmund)'s sleeping quarters. It contains an owned single bed coming out from the wall with a small cupboard to the left and an unlocked wooden chest to the right. On the left side of the bed are two small wardrobes separated by a tall wardrobe. On the smaller ones is a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [boiled creme treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), one red and two green apples. On the right of the bed are two chairs and a small round table with a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) on top.

Next is the stairwell with a few crates, barrels and straw bales at the bottom. The next cubicle is [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md)'s sleeping quarters, though he is never seen in the hall. It contains an owned single bed coming out from the wall with empty small bedside tables on either side. To the left of the bed are two more bales of straw, while to the right are some crates, a tall wardrobe and an unlocked chest. The next cubicle is [J'zargo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:J%27zargo)'s sleeping quarters which contains an owned single bed coming out from the wall with a small wardrobe on either side of the bed. To the left is an unlocked long chest. To the right are a pair of chairs separated by another tall wardrobe.

The final cubicle (the first one on the right when entering) is the sleeping quarters that are given to you when you first enter the College and begin the questline by gaining official access. It contains an unowned single bed coming out from the wall with small wardrobes on either side. The one on the left has a copy of *[Azura and the Box](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azura_and_the_Box)* atop. To the left of the bed is a chair and small round table between several barrels and a small table by the doorway with a bowl of [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md), a bowl of [vampire dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust) and two [ice wraith teeth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wraith_Teeth) on top. To the right are two tall wardrobes with a small wardrobe between. On the smaller wardrobe there is an empty common soul gem and three [skulls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skull) on top. By the doorway is another small table with three [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) and a bowl of [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm) on top, and on the floor at the rear of the room is a basket containing five bottles of Nord mead.

### Upper Floor
There is a hole in the center of the room with the magical energies from below rising into the ceiling. Around this is a low wall to prevent accidental falls. The first cubicle is [Enthir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir)'s sleeping quarters, where he spends most of his time. There is an owned single bed coming out from the wall, with small bedside tables on either side and an unlocked chest at the foot of the bed. On the left of the bed is a set of shelves holding three common books, a copy of *[Souls, Black and White](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Souls,_Black_and_White)*, ten bottles of Nord mead, six bottles of alto wine, and a bottle of wine (see [bugs](#Bugs)). To the left of the shelves is an unlocked chest, with a [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) leaning against it. To the right of the bed are two chairs, with a tall wardrobe between them.

The next cubicle is a dining room with a long wooden table and bench filled with [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md), and a narrow table to the right. Next is [Nirya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirya)'s cubicle, with an owned single bed coming out of the wall, and small wardrobes on either side. To the left of the bed is a set of shelves, a chair, and a drum on a barrel. To the right of the bed are two tall wardrobes with a small wardrobe between them. On top of the small wardrobe is a large coin purse and a small coin purse. The next cubicle is unused, and contains a large vat, several barrels, and some crates. It is full of cobwebs. The next cubicle is [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md)'s sleeping quarters, with a single bed coming out from the wall, several straw bales to the left, and two crates and two unusable barrels to the right.

The next cubicle has a wooden bench along the rear wall, with a large round wooden table and chairs to the left, and a narrow table and more chairs to the right. On top of the first table are two common books. The final cubicle is [Arniel Gane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arniel_Gane)'s sleeping quarters, with an owned single bed coming out from the wall, with small wardrobes on either side. There is a large coin purse on the wardrobe to the left. Also to the left of the bed are two sets of shelves holding samples of [imp stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool), [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap), and [Namira's Rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot) fungus, a basket containing four [spider eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg), three [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) flowers, three ice wraith teeth, a sample of [hagraven feathers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hagraven_Feathers), a bowl of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust), a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md), a bowl of frost salts, two deathbell flowers, and a bowl of [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal) (see [bugs](#Bugs)). To the right of the bed are two tall wardrobes with a small wardrobe between them. On top of the small wardrobe is a filled common soul gem, an empty petty soul gem, and a [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull).

## Notes
- The room that you are given contains four respawning food barrels. The other containers in that room are non-respawning and safe for storage. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The food barrels no longer respawn.
- The inhabitants of the downstairs rooms may wander into each others' rooms as well as yours and use the furniture, sometimes at unusual times. For example, you may find Tolfdir sitting in Brelyna Maryon's room, seemingly watching her sleep.

## Bugs
- Two of the bottles of Nord mead and a bottle of alto wine are unowned.
- Several [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) are shown as unowned.
- At some point in the game, you may be unable to sleep or wait in the Hall of Attainment because you are "being asked to leave".
- There is an unowned bed in the Hall of Attainment that's supposed to be owned by Arniel Gane. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
