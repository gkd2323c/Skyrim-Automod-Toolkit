## Shrine of Akatosh
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Akatosh.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Akatosh.png) A shrine of Akatosh grants:

[Blessing of Akatosh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Akatosh): *Magicka regenerates 10% faster.*

- [Regenerate Magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md), 10 pts for 8 hours
- [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=View+all+on+map&search=Shrine+of+Akatosh)

### Rorikstead
[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Rorikstead.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Rorikstead.jpg) This shrine is atop the hill directly north of [Frostfruit Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostfruit_Inn) in [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md).

**Offerings**:

- [Blue Dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Dartwing)
- [Dragon Scales](Skyrim_Dragon_Scales.md)
- *[Kolb & the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon)*

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-84223&y=15343)

### Twilight Valley
[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Twilight Valley.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Twilight_Valley.jpg) This shrine is placed inside of a tower atop a hill northeast of [Twilight Sepulcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) Hold.

**Offerings**:

- *[A Tragedy in Black](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Tragedy_in_Black)*
- *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)*
- [Potion of Enhanced Stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina)
- Two [Dragon's Tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue) samples

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-66608&y=-77872)

### Steamcrag Hillock
[![SR-place-Shrine of Akatosh Steamcrag Hillock.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Akatosh_Steamcrag_Hillock.jpg) This shrine is on a hill northeast of the [Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) and south and slightly east of [Steamcrag Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamcrag_Camp). The shrine is in the middle of some stone pillars, with several bones and entire skeletons scattered across the area. [Gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) and [corundum](Skyrim_Corundum.md) ore veins are nearby.

**Offerings**

- *[Breathing Water](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Breathing_Water)*
- [Iron War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe)
- [Skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skull)
- [Troll Skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=147317&y=-22145)

### Fort Sungard Shrine
This shrine is within the [Fort Sungard Shrine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Sungard_Shrine) at [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md), southeast of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). Only present if the fort is occupied by the Legion. By default, it is a shrine of Kynareth, and is a shrine of Talos if occupied by Stormcloaks.

### Skyborn Altar
[![SR-place-Skyborn Altar 04.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_04.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_04.jpg) This shrine is next to the [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) at [Skyborn Altar](Skyrim_Skyborn_Altar.md) if [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has not yet been started. A [Wispmother](Skyrim_Wisp.md) defends this location. The shrine shares its altar with a skeleton and [embalming tools](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Embalming_Tool).

**Offerings**:

- [Iron Warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer)
- Two [Petty Soul Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem)
- [Potion of Minor Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-5666&y=48380)

## Shrine of Arkay
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Arkay.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-construction-Shrine_of_Arkay.png) A shrine of Arkay grants:

[Blessing of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Arkay): *Increases your health by 25 points.*

- [Fortify Health](Skyrim_Fortify_Health.md), 25 pts for 8 hours
- [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=View+all+on+map&search=Shrine+of+Arkay)

### Wayward Pass
[![SR-place-Wayward Pass.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg) Found at the resting place of an ancient traveler in [Wayward Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wayward_Pass), alongside several graves.

**Offerings**:

- [Amulet of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Arkay)
- [Iron Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots)
- [Iron Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet)
- Two [Linen Wraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linen_Wrap)
- [Steel Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield)
- [Steel Sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md)
- [Red Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=76286&y=63066)

### Windhelm Hills
[![SR-place-Shrine of Arkay Windhelm Hills.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-place-Shrine_of_Arkay_Windhelm_Hills.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_of_Arkay_Windhelm_Hills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Arkay_Windhelm_Hills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_of_Arkay_Windhelm_Hills.jpg) Found northeast of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), south of [Snow Veil Sanctum](Skyrim_Snow_Veil_Sanctum.md). Disappears if the Empire gains control of Winterhold as the [Winterhold Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.md) replaces it.

**Offerings**:

- [Blue Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower)
- [Gold](Skyrim_Gold.md)
- Two [Petty Soul Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem)
- [Purple Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower)
- [Snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md)

[(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=145848&y=55973)

### Falkreath Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Falkreath) 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath)_02.jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath)_02.jpg.md) The [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md) has a total of three Shrines of Arkay, appropriate for Falkreath's focus on the grave. Two are placed in front of the Hall of the Dead, and the final one is inside the Hall. None of the shrines have any offerings.

### Markarth Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Markarth).jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) This shrine is in a small rest area inside the catacombs of the [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md). No offerings are placed next to the shrine.

### Riften Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Riften) 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Riften%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Riften%29_02.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften)_02.jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften)_02.jpg.md) The [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) Arkay shrine is placed in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften).md), beneath the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md). The shrine itself is placed next to the body preparation area.

**Offerings**:

- [Gold](Skyrim_Gold.md)
- Two [Blue Mountain Flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower)
- Two [Purple Mountain Flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower)

### Solitude Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Solitude).jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg.md) The [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).md) keeps its shrine on its first level on a shelf. An [iron warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer) is stored on the shelf beneath the shrine.

### Whiterun Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead (Whiterun) 04.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Whiterun%29_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Whiterun%29_04.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun)_04.jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun)_04.jpg.md) The [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun).md) keeps its shrine in a well-decorated room, with both sides leading to the shrine flanked by pews.

**Offerings**:

- Four [Skulls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skull)

### Windhelm Hall of the Dead
[![SR-interior-Hall of the Dead Windhelm.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_Windhelm.jpg) [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)'s [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm).md) keeps a shrine of Arkay in the heart of its crypt. A [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), [candlestick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Candlestick), and [goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet) have been placed next to the shrine.

### Druadach Redoubt
[Druadach Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt) has a shrine to Arkay on a ledge by the ramps, halfway along the northeastern wall. Two lit candles are on either side of the shrine. The shrine can only be activated by clipping out of bounds.

## Shrine of Dibella

- A sample of [Lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md)
- Two [Red Mountain Flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md)
- A [Silver Necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace)

### Temple of Dibella
[![SR-interior-Temple of Dibella 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-interior-Temple_of_Dibella_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Temple_of_Dibella_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_of_Dibella_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_of_Dibella_02.jpg) The [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) keeps a shrine inside the public portion of the Temple. Although the entire Temple is dedicated to Dibella and filled with possible offerings, there are no offerings placed near the actual shrine itself.

### Broken Tower Redoubt
[![SR-interior-Broken Tower Redoubt 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Broken_Tower_Redoubt_02.jpg) This shrine is found in the top section of the interior of [Broken Tower Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt). The Forsworn have covered the shrine in bones and blood. It is unclear if this was intended as an act of desecration or worship.

**Offerings**:

- [Bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl)
- [Goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet)
- [Jug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug)
