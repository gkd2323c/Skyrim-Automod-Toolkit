# The Tower

*This one sees … strength. Yes. The clenched fist and walls of stone.*
*This one sees claw marks upon cold, wet stone. Smells moss and memory. It is the Tower that guides you. Like a fortress, you may weather fire and claw.*
*But do not rely on strength alone. The strongest wall is the one that is hidden.*
—Baandari fortune-telling for those born under the Tower<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> This article is about the constellation. For the metaphysical structures, see [The Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md).

The Tower is a constellation of twelve stars which is in the night sky during [Frostfall](Lore_Frostfall.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It is one of the [Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Tower are thought to have a knack for finding gold and opening locks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

[Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) who followed the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) modeled their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor after the constellations. Each part of their armaments were crafted in honor of different constellation. Shields were linked to the Tower, "what protecteth ye [Celestials](Lore_Celestial.md) e'en as it supports the very world". They believed that Tower, both opens and closes the Way, and their shields were meant to represent its strength, "closing the way to the weapons of thy foes, yet opening when thou smitest on thine own account".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM41-4)</sup> Following the Tower's guidance, the Nedes built [Seaveil Spire](Lore_Seaveil_Spire.md), a submerged sanctuary devoted to the Tower.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSS-5)</sup>

The constellations are present in ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) poetry.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWC-6)</sup> In one of their songs The Tower is "defying through the night."<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Coyle-7)</sup>

The Dwemer associate the Tower with the letter n.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

According to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) the charges, or the Lesser Stars (the Tower among them) are protected by the Guardians in the Sky who stand together against [the Snake in the Stars](Lore_The_Snake_in_the_Stars.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARANA-9)</sup>

According to the [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) fortune tellers, the Tower is linked to strength.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Tower was also believed to "prove a stout refuge in time of need".<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Uriel-10)</sup>
