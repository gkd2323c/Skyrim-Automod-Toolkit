[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-item-Nightingale_Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Gloves.jpg) Nightingale Gloves The Nightingale Gloves are part of a set of armor given to you for joining the [Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). Its enchantment grants a [Fortify Lockpicking](Skyrim_Fortify_Lockpicking.md) and [Fortify One-Handed](Skyrim_Fortify_One-Handed.md) effect, making [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) less of a challenge and making you more deadly with [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) weapons. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Nightingale Gloves requires [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

The full set of Nightingale equipment also includes [armor](Skyrim_Nightingale_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Nightingale_Boots.md), and a [hood](Skyrim_Nightingale_Hood.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Trinity Restored](Skyrim_Trinity_Restored.md)**: Become one of the Nightingales, [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md)'s guardians.
