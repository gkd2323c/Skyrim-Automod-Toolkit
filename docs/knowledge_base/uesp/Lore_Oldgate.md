[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Oldgate (also spelled as **Old Gate**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a fortified border gate on the southern border of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md), a northern kingdom in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The fort is the gateway into Rivenspire for the rest of the province and it works adjacent to Newgate, another keep on the [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) end of the pass.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONOldgate-2)</sup> It is also the home of a militia called the **Oldgate Lancers**, who swore allegiance to the Kingdom of Rivenspire.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM6-3)</sup>

## History
When Oldgate is founded is unknown, however, the Oldgate Lancers have been established since at least the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). The militia was present in the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTreasures O-4)</sup> an important conflict between the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md) and the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Once-5)</sup> They joined their fellow warriors in the "Charge of the Shornhelm Knights".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTreasures O-4)</sup>

In [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md) of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) amassed an army from across Rivenspire and beyond to battle High King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) and the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md). The Oldgate Lancers were recruited to his side and played a significant role in the [civil war](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) that ensued afterward. They were part of the Shornhelm advance guard and were present at the siege on [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) before Ranser's main army even arrived in the city. Emeric and his troops met them on the battlefield and were able to overwhelm them with his [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) broadsword, the [Orichalc Scalpel](Lore_Orichalc_Scalpel.md). The Oldgate Lancers fled the scene while Emeric was able to bolster the perimeter.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM6-3)</sup>

When the war was over, the Kingdom of Rivenspire swore allegiance to the Daggerfall Covenant, and the Oldgate Lancers were absorbed into the greater Covenant Army.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AWC-6)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Baron [Wylon Montclair](Lore_Wylon_Montclair.md) seized control of Shornhelm and pitted the noble houses against each other. The Daggerfall Covenant was called in and first assembled themselves in Oldgate before retaking the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONOldgate-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONDarien-7)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Old Gate was an active settlement. It was ruled by Count Tristyval and had a rivaly with [Portdun Creek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Portdun_Creek).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Old Gate is neighbored by several settlements, including [Normar Heights](Lore_Normar_Heights.md) to the northeast, Shornhelm to the southwest, and Thorkan Park to the southeast.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARMap-8)</sup>
