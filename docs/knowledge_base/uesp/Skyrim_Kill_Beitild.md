## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get the contract from [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md)
2. Kill Beitild in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).
3. Claim your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Get the Contract
The assassination of [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md) is part of the first batch of contracts handed to you by [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md). After giving you the contract, he will readily tell you a bit about her background: *"She separated from her husband several months ago. Now the two run competing mining operations. Beitild's angry and desperate. A fighter."*

### Kill Beitild
In the town of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) it is common knowledge that Beitild and her husband [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md) are feuding, most evident by overhearing Leigelf arguing with the puzzled [Lond](Skyrim_Lond.md) in front of [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) and by talking to the participants themselves. Beitild now runs [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md), keeps a close eye on her workers at all times, and never misses the opportunity to bad-mouth the "fat-head owner" of the competing Quicksilver Mine.

When you approach Beitild, you will be given three dialogue options, you can only pick one of them and will not be able to choose another one by trying to talk to her again.

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *(Remain silent)* | *"I've a mine to run, so if you'll excuse me..."* | Beitild walks away |
| *"I represent the Dark Brotherhood. Your death is at hand!"* | *"What? Well, we'll see about that now, won't we?"* | Draws pickaxe and attacks |
| *"Someone wants you dead, Beitild."* | *"And let me guess, that someone is my so-called husband Leigelf? Well the feeling's mutual."* | Ends conversation |

An alternative is to use [stealth](Skyrim_Stealth.md) and kill Beitild in her sleep. She will return to her house at midnight and rest there until 8am. The lock on her front door can be picked or unlocked using the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Beitild%27s_House.md) she carries about her person.

When Beitild is disposed of, you can pay a visit to her husband for an emotionless reaction: *"My wife Beitild is dead. Can't say I miss that old sabre cat."*

### Reward
Return to Nazir in the [sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md) for a leveled gold reward. When you tell him Beitild is dead he will respond *"Of course she is. I hear the mining business is extremely cutthroat. And those hours... they're murder."*

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 250 |
| 10–19 | 400 |
| 20–29 | 500 |
| 30–39 | 600 |
| 40+ | 750 |

## Notes
- While this quest is active, it is impossible to [sell ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) to Beitild as only the three dialogue options mentioned above are available.
- It's quite possible for Beitild to have already died by the time you would normally get this contract quest. If this is the case, then the contract will just be skipped entirely.
- If [Silus Vesuius](Skyrim_Silus_Vesuius.md) is on his porch and you kill Beitild while outside, Silus will become hostile even if you remained undetected.
- Killing directly will render disposition with [Karl](Skyrim_Karl.md) and [Irgnir](Skyrim_Irgnir.md) to -2, so better way is to poison to avoid this happening.
