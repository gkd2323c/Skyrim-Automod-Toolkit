*I came here because High King Emeric hopes to avoid a fight.*
—Nilene Devierin<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup> **Lady** Nilene Devierin of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTDOM-2)</sup> (also spelled **Devieren**),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup> was a diplomat of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), serving High King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) as the **Royal Envoy to the Reach**. She also authored reports to High King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md), detailing her activities in the court of Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md) and providing a description of Ard Caddach himself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTDOM-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/ON-load-Emeric%27s_Dream.jpg/200px-ON-load-Emeric%27s_Dream.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Emeric%27s_Dream.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Emeric%27s_Dream.jpg) High King Emeric [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/ON-render-Ard_Caddach_02.jpg/200px-ON-render-Ard_Caddach_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Ard_Caddach_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Ard_Caddach_02.jpg) Ard Caddach Little is known of Nilene's past, but it is known that she hailed from the region of Stormhaven.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTDOM-2)</sup> At some point, she was appointed Royal Envoy to [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) by High King Emeric. She visited the city of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) multiple times prior to [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). In the first week of [Hearth Fire](Lore_Calendar.md#Month) in 2E 582, she traveled to the capital of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) to negotiate with Ard Caddach regarding raids along High Rock's borders. Upon arrival, she learned that before she could present High King Emeric's case to the Despot, she first needed to persuade his chief advisor, Lady Belain, to arrange a meeting.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup>

During her conversation with Lady Belain, which began with the usual pleasantries, Nilene was surprised by the hospitality extended to her, as she wasn't used to such treatment from Reach diplomats. Belain asked about her journey and if she was comfortable in her quarters in Markarth. Nilene then brought up the purpose of her visit, mentioning the intolerable activities of the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md) in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) and the Dark Witnesses in [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth.md). She expressed the High King's desire for peace along the borders and frustration over the raids by certain [Reachfolk clans](Lore_Reachfolk_Clans.md) and factions, imploring Belain to persuade Ard Caddach to control the unruly groups.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup>

However, Caddach faced constraints. He couldn't project weakness or servitude to Emeric and was hindered by the fractured clan society and the high autonomy of the independent clans, which he did not govern as a liege. Nilene was advised that the Bretons should weaken the factions themselves to increase Caddach's authority in the region. By assisting Ard Caddach, High King Emeric could streamline negotiations with a unified Reach under one ruler rather than dealing with numerous stubborn clans. Supporting Caddach was presented as aligning with High Rock's best interests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup>

When Nilene eventually met with Caddach, her concerns were dismissed, just as lady Belain predicted. She then wrote a report detailing her activities, and returned to High King Emeric without a satisfactory response.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup> She sent dispatches back to [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) summarizing the difficulties in negotiations. However, she later compiled another text, believing it might be useful to Emeric's court to understand the character of the man who rules in the Reach, providing a detailed description of Ard Caddach.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTDOM-2)</sup>
