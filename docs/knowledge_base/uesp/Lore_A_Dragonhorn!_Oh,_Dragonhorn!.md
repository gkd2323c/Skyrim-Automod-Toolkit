A Dragonhorn! Oh, Dragonhorn! **Translated by [Adept Izjadi](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Izjadi), Star Haven Record Keeper** *A poem about the Dragonhorn of Star Haven Adeptorium*
---

- The [Akavir](Lore_Akaviri.md) came one day
- To our Starry Haven
- [Warriors](Lore_Dragonguard.md) proud were they
- And not a one unshaven!

- They said that [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) were about
- And that we should be wary
- "We cannot stay, we cannot fight,
- We'll give you what we carry."

- A Dragonhorn! Oh, Dragonhorn!
- With just one many call
- Any Dragon high above
- Will have a mighty fall.

- A Dragonhorn! Oh, Dragonhorn!
- You'll stay in tower high
- And if the Dragons do attack
- They'll hear your mighty cry!
