[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-npc-Ulag.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulag.jpg) Ulag found dead **Ulag** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who can be found dead in [Sleeping Tree Cave](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md). He is carrying [a note](Skyrim_Ysolda%27s_Message.md) from [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) which starts the quest [Sleeping Tree Cave](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md).

He wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md) and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

As evident by the note in his pocket, he was an accomplice to Ysolda of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), who turns out to be a dealer of [Sleeping Tree Sap](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Sap.md). When you show her the note, she will grieve little about the demise of Ulag: *"So Ulag didn't make it passed * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * those giants then? More's the pity. If you found any of the sap, I'd be willing to pay you the same cut I offered him."*
