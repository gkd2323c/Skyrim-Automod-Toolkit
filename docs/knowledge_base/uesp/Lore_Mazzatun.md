[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Mazzatun is a ruined city found in the region of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md), within the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Colloquially known as the *"Puzzle City,"* Mazzatun is an expansive labyrinth of cramped walls, twisted hallways, and numerous dead-ends. The ruins are home to [Tsono-Xuhil](Lore_Tsono-Xuhil.md), a powerful, mad [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) tree that produces [Amber Plasm](Lore_Amber_Plasm.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tfa Iw RDEADV-1)</sup> a strange substance that drives whoever ingests or bathes in it mad.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NJ-2)</sup> The city was built and inhabited by its children, the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [Xit-Xaht](Lore_Xit-Xaht.md) tribe.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOHJ-3)</sup>

The city's summit was home to the Xit-Xaht tribe's uxith: nests lined the cliffside cradled beneath the Hist's whorling roots.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## History
Mazzatun has existed for centuries, and—at least up until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) —the Xit-Xaht never stopped building.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOHJ-3)</sup> Locals from neighboring tribes knew to avoid the ruins because "the Hist [was] crazy", and offered little information about the city to curious outsiders aside from warnings to stay away.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tfa Iw RDEADV-1)</sup> This Hist of Mazzatun produced Amber Plasm, which is not typical [Hist Sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md): it was likened to [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) by a foreign scholar. This odd substance has wept from Tsono-Xuhil since time immemorial.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NJ-2)</sup> When the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) broke out in [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560), a detachment of soldiers from Great [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) was sent to Mazzatun to clear out the Xit-Xaht, believing that they caused the plague. The Xit-Xaht later returned to the city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tfa Iw RDEADV-1)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Su-Zahleel tribe was kidnapped by the Xit-Xaht and forced into slavery to continue Mazzatun's ever-ongoing construction. One of the Su-Zahleel tribesmen evaded capture and hired the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) to kill the Xit-Xaht Tree-Minder, [Na-Kesh](Lore_Tree-Minder_Na-Kesh.md), and free the Su-Zahleel from enslavement. The Undaunted trekked through the ancient ruins, rescuing the Su-Zahleel elders and defeating Na-Kesh at the city's summit. The Su-Zahleel elders recognized that Tsono-Xuhil was suffering. In an effort to begin to allow the Hist to heal, they sent it into a deep slumber that lasted for an indeterminate amount of time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sap Stone-5)</sup>
