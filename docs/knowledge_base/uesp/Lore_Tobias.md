*You haven't been here long enough. My ship is ready to leave at sundown, but don't worry. I'll be here until then, to help you along if you need it. A lot can transpire in a day. We will see each other again.*
—Tobias (listen Link ⏵) Captain Tobias Hold-None<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tiber Meeting-1)</sup> was a [Cyro-Nordic](Lore_Cyro-Nord.md) ship's captain and mercenary. He was a close friend and ally of the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md), having known the boy since he was young.

## History
Tobias Hold-None was born during the late- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) in the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovian_West.md), near the battlefield of [Sancre Tor](Lore_Sancre_Tor.md). His [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) father, Borgas, had served as the captain of the House Guard of Far Falkreath Estate, a merchant family with ties to the Holds of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Borgas eventually married a Colovian daughter, but she died in the Sack of [Old Hrol'dan](Lore_Old_Hrol%27dan.md) scarcely two years later. Tobias grew up with stories and romantic notions of his "father's fair Skyrim," a land of savagery and battle far different than the relatively civilized Colovian West. As soon as he could, he traveled to [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) to serve as a marine in the Longboat Legions of the [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) Heirs. Ultimately, he deserted the ranks and became a smuggler, working the runs of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). At [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) he met [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md), a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) who had long dreamed of leaving [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) to sail the seas. When Cyrus killed his sister [Iszara](Lore_Iszara.md)'s husband, Hakan, Tobias took him in.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGArchive-2)</sup>

Tobias and Cyrus would continue on as mercenaries. Going on many adventures throughout Tamriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOOC-3)</sup> At some point in time, the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) crime boss, [S'rathra](Lore_S%27rathra.md) sent his band of brigands to a plantation outside of [Tear](Lore_Tear.md) to free his kinsmen from the [Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md) [slavers](Lore_Slavery.md). Tobias and Cyrus were among the brigands. Cyrus was able to free some of the slaves and it caused a giant revolt across the plantation. In the end, the Dres slavers were freed and the slaves were liberated.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOOC-3)</sup>

After the Raid on Tear, Tobias and Cyrus went their separate ways, keeping in touch only through their mutual fixer, S'rathra. Tobias took up a merchant profession after the raid, given his increasing lack of "fight" with age. In [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), Tobias' sent a letter to Cyrus asking to meet him at the Draggin Tale Inn, in [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), explaining that Iszara had relocated there after [Baron Volag](Lore_Volag.md)'s Purge in Northern Hammerfell and had gone missing. When they reunited, Tobias gave Cyrus all the information he knew of Iszara to help him find her.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Redguard-4)</sup>

It is unclear what became of Tobias after the fateful events that transpired at Stros M'kai. A dubious tale of Cyrus' later exploits mentions Tobias seemingly meeting his death at the hands of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tiber Meeting-1)</sup>

Claims of unknown origin suggest that Tobias inspired Cyrus with his stories to take command of the legendary ship *Dead Man's Dread* and set sail north to the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Restless-5)</sup>
