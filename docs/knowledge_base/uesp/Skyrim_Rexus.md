[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-npc-Rexus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rexus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rexus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rexus.jpg) Rexus waiting in the Bannered Mare **Rexus**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior2H.md), is the personal bodyguard of [Amaund Motierre](Skyrim_Amaund_Motierre.md).

Rexus wears a set of [studded Imperial armor](Skyrim_Studded_Imperial_Armor.md), and matching [boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md) and [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Bracers.md). He wields a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md) in combat.

## Related Quests
- **[The Silence Has Been Broken](Skyrim_The_Silence_Has_Been_Broken.md)**: Meet [Amaund Motierre](Skyrim_Amaund_Motierre.md) inside [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) and learn who he wants assassinated.
- **[Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md)**: One of the biggest [contracts](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) in [Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) history is yours to fulfill.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
During [The Silence Has Been Broken](Skyrim_The_Silence_Has_Been_Broken.md), he will greet you with:

- *"I have vowed to serve Amaund Motierre until my dying breath. Best remember that.*"

During your meeting with Motierre, he will hand you the [amulet](Skyrim_Jeweled_Amulet.md) and [the letter](Skyrim_Amaund_Motierre%27s_Sealed_Letter.md) saying:

- *"Here."*

After [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md) he will be surprised that you are alive saying:

- *"Well I'll be damned. We heard you were dead. Motierre's in the back room, if you've got business."*

## Notes
- Rexus may become hostile during either of the encounters with him. Yielding will have no effect.
- If you choose to kill his master after completing the quest, Rexus will sometimes move back to Volunruud and stay in the room where you first meet them.
