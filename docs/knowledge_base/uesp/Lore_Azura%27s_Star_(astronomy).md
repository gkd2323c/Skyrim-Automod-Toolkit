# Azura's Star (astronomy)

**Azura's Star** (also known as the **Twilight Star**) is a [star](Lore_Stars.md) which appears briefly at dawn and dusk, low on the horizon below the [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) of [the Steed](Lore_The_Steed.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is not to be confused with the artifact named [Azura's Star](Lore_Azura%27s_Star.md), a powerful soul gem and symbol of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History
Some say that [Lord Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) was born under a moon-and-star, implying he was born when a crescent [moon](Lore_Moons.md) and Azura's Star were visible in the night sky.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Nibani-4)</sup>
