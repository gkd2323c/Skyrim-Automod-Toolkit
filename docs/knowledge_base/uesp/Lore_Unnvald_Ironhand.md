Unnvald Ironhand was the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) Thane of Fullhelm during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He gained fame for leading Nords from across [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in a series of annual and biannual raids on [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) after a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) raid killed his daughter in [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). He became a celebrated hero in Skyrim, but when the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) formed in 2E 572, he was disgraced and exiled to Fullhelm Fort, a stronghold near the pass between the two provinces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> Unnvald's prized weapon was Bonebane, a two-handed sword.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Sword)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/ON-npc-Hallfrida.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Hallfrida.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Hallfrida.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Hallfrida.jpg) Hallfrida, Unnvald's granddaughter Unnvald hailed from Riften<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> and Clan Ironhand.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONContraband L-2)</sup> In his youth, he was an adventurer who explored old ruins and barrows. In one such place, he discovered his weapon, Bonebane. He claimed that a beautiful [ghost](Lore_Ghost.md) gifted him the sword and that it was originally held by one of the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup>

At some point, he settled down, married a woman,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> and fathered a daughter,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONUnnvald-3)</sup> who eventually had a child of her own named Hallfrida. The families lived in Riften, but when Hallfrida was very young, Dark Elves raided the city and burnt her parents' farm, leaving no survivors. Unnvald was with Hallfrida and his wife at his nearby stead when the raid occurred, allowing them to survive. The spring after the Dark Elf raid on Riften, Unnvald first took up raiding, and thereafter he would gather many warriors from across Skyrim and sail off once or twice a year to raid Morrowind.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> One year, a [skald](Lore_Skald.md) from the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) traveled with him on one of his raids to chronicle it. In the same year that the skald traveled with Unnvald, he and his raiders sacked [Davon's Watch](Lore_Davon%27s_Watch.md). Early in Hallfrida's life, Unnvald hired Thorulf as his housecarl, who remained by his side for many years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-npc-Housecarl_Thorulf.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Housecarl_Thorulf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Housecarl_Thorulf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Housecarl_Thorulf.jpg) Thorulf, Unnvald's housecarl One of Unnvald's most well-known battles was fought in the pass through the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) that connects the Rift and [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). In the battle, he successfully defended Fullhelm Fort from the [undead](Lore_Undead.md) conjured by the necromancers of [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md), a keep on the other side of the pass in Stonefalls.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BOTBM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TEGS-5)</sup> That battle was known as the Battle of the Bones, and a memorial depicting Unnvald was built just south of Fullhelm Fort. He claimed that it was built to honor the deceased, but his granddaughter believed it was constructed to taunt his enemies since it could not be seen from Fullhelm Fort but was particularly visible from Fort Virak. After the battle, Unnvald left Bonebane in a chest near the memorial, vowing to never pick it up again.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup>

[Statues](Lore_Statues.md) of Unnvald were built in Riften and Windhelm, and for a time, he was celebrated across Skyrim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> In [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), the Ebonheart Pact, an alliance between Nords, Dark Elves, and [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), formed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSAI-6)</sup> With the Nords of [Eastern Skyrim](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md) now in an alliance with the Dark Elves, the jarls were embarrassed by Unnvald's raids on Morrowind and offered him the Thanedom of Fullhelm. He did not want the thanedom because it was too far from his wife's grave, but the jarls said if he did not accept it, he would have to answer to the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md). He then accepted the Thanedom of Fullhelm and traveled to Fullhelm Fort to begin his forced exile. After the formation of the pact, the Ordinators banned songs about him, and the jarls pulled down his statues in Riften and Windhelm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Unnvald had become paranoid and believed the jarls were out to get him. His paranoia reached the point that he accused one of Jarl [Angjord](Lore_Angjord.md)'s messengers of being an assassin and beheaded him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> At around the same time, Thorulf, who had secretly become a [worm cultist](Lore_Worm_Cult.md), gifted Unnvald with a token of his faith, a shard of [Wuuthrad](Lore_Wuuthrad.md), which the housecarl then used to weaken the Thane's mind and manipulate him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONUnnvald-3)</sup> A couple of weeks later, undead invaded the area and assaulted Fullhelm Fort.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONUnnvald-3)</sup> Thorulf was ordered to retrieve Bonebane, but the housecarl claimed it was destroyed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONHallfrida-1)</sup> The [King's Arrow](Lore_The_Vestige.md), an agent of the Ebonheart Pact, ventured across the Rift to recover lost pieces of Wuuthrad and traveled to Fullhelm where they met Unnvald. The Thane refused to listen to their story, and Thorulf forced them to leave the Thane's Manor.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONA-7)</sup>

After leaving the manor, the King's Arrow defended the fort with a retrieved Bonebane. During the defense of the fort, Thorulf summoned a [skeleton](Lore_Skeleton.md) and told Unnvald that the undead had entered the manor. Unnvald went into a mad panic and attempted to burn down the manor while Thorulf fled. It is unknown if Unnvald died in the manor or if he lived, but Fullhelm Fort was saved, and the King's Arrow retrieved the shard of Wuuthrad.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONThunder-8)</sup>
