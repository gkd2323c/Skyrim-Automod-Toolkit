# Zeht

Zeht, Yokudan God of Farms, is the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) god of agriculture,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup> civil law, mathematics,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NJ.2FHB-2)</sup> and food,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ZCC-3)</sup> but he still finds a place in the pantheon of the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VOFTC-4)</sup>. He is similar to [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md), and sometimes worshiped in that name by the Forebears.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VOFTF-5)</sup> He renounced his father after the world was created, which is why [Ruptga](Lore_Ruptga.md) makes it so hard to grow food.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOF-1)</sup> He is the father of [Zeqqi](Lore_Zeqqi.md). He forsakes the land of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md), preventing most food from growing there.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lit C-6)</sup> Occasionally farming utensils of the Zeht's faithful such as digging spades are decorated with semi-precious stones and blessed by a priests of Zeht.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Contraband B-7)</sup> The white [camel](Lore_Camel.md) is believed to be sacred to Zeht and pious Redguards tend to bow to passing Cloud Camels.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ZCC-3)</sup>

Some sources claim that before deceiving the spirits, [Sep](Lore_Sep.md) stole one of the stars [Ruptga](Lore_Ruptga.md) was using to form the [Great Map](Lore_Astronomy.md), and put it in his pocket. When Sep was killed, the same star fell out of his mouth in the form of a pearl. The pearl fell to the land and was covered by sand, and so came to belong to Zeht. This Pearl is claimed to be the "Way and the Guide", the "light to the Far Shores and back again". When it is at last discovered again, [water](Lore_Water_Magic.md) will flow over all the previous Worldskins, and the time of Sep's Hunger will be over, the Hungry Stomach of Satakal at last sated. It is also said that the Hunger is what answers the call of the worlds to save them, and that the only real world is that of Satakal, as the stars cannot be devoured.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## Artifacts

### Aurbical Abacus
The [Aurbical Abacus](Lore_Aurbical_Abacus.md) was an ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) relic created by distilling the knowledge and wisdom of twenty generations of the priest-wizards of the Yokudan god Zeht. It had the physical appearance of having glass gears and ivory pistons, and suffused with the blue glow of star-magic. It was used to track the movements of the moons, constellations, and planets and calculate the exact turn of the seasons. It detected the directional origin of star-magicka, or "[varliance](Lore_Varliance.md)," and stored it as data. It can also predict the weather and even control it on a continental scale. It was brought to [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) after Yokuda sank, by the Zeht priesthood, where it was buried in the old [Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md) family tomb known as Bahraha's Gloom.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NJ.2FHB-2)</sup>
