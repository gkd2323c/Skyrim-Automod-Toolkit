[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-place-Largashbur.jpg/200px-SR-place-Largashbur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Largashbur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Largashbur.jpg) Largashbur circa 4E 201 Largashbur is an [Orc](Lore_Orcs.md) stronghold situated in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) on the southern edge of the [Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md). It sits in a forest right at the northern edges of the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) at the Border between [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>.

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_07.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_07.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_07.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_07.jpg) Yamarz at Fallowstone Cave During the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the clan lost control of the shrine of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) in the nearby Fallowstone Cave to [Giants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giants) and in turn, [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) decided to put a curse on the clan as a punishment. This resulted in repeated attacks by [Giants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giants) on the stronghold, which ultimately led to the shaman of the stronghold enrolling the help of a traveler to perform a ritual to summon [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) and plead for help. During the ritual, the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) explained the reason for the curse and ordered the chief Yamarz to retake the shrine. While the shrine was ultimately recovered, Yamarz was killed during this effort and [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) named Gularzob as the new chief of the stronghold <sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRThe Cursed Tribe-2)</sup>.

## Geography
**Largashbur** is located just south of the road between [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) and [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md). Near the southwestern shore of [Lake Honrich](Lore_Lake_Honrich.md), a small stream feeds into the lake east of the stronghold. The stronghold sits in a temperate mixed forest primarily made up of birch and pine trees right at the foot of the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>.

## Economy
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-place-Hunting_Hut_Largashbur.jpeg/200px-SR-place-Hunting_Hut_Largashbur.jpeg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunting_Hut_Largashbur.jpeg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunting_Hut_Largashbur.jpeg) Largashbur Hunting Hut Unlike most [Orc](Lore_Orcs.md) strongholds in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), **Largashbur** does not have a mine within the walls of the stronghold with only small deposits of [Orichalcum](Lore_Orichalcum.md) present. The economic activity of the settlement is instead focused on hunting and logging, with agricultural activity being limited to animal husbandry of [chickens](Lore_Chicken.md) and [goats](Lore_Goat.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>. There is a close economic relationship with other [Orc](Lore_Orcs.md) strongholds, with the settlement sending workers to temporarily live in Narzulbur in [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) during the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) and work in the [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) mine to assist with labour shortages there <sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>.
