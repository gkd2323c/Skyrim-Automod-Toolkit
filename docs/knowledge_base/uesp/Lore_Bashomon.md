Bashomon was the Prince of [Esroniet](Lore_Esroniet.md), an island in the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md), during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R:Da I-1)</sup> His rulership was threatened when Emperor [Uriel Septim V](Lore_Uriel_Septim_V.md) conquered the main port on the island, [Black Harbor](Lore_Black_Harbor.md), in [3E 282](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_282).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R:Da I-1)</sup> He would later surrender the island to Imperial authorities two years later in [3E 284](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_284).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R:Da I-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
