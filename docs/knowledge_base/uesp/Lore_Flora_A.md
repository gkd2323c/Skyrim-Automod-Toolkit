## Acacia
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Desert_Acacia.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Desert_Acacia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Desert_Acacia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Desert_Acacia.jpg) Acacia Acacia trees are natively found in the drier parts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), such as [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) though the plant has found its way into gardens around the continent. Known varieties of the Acacia include the Anequine Acacia, the Desert Acacia<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTrees Furnishing-1)</sup> and the White Acacia. Another known type of Acacia is not found naturally but is the result of corruption caused by the dragon [Maarselok](Lore_Maarselok.md)'s Azureblight leading the trees to exhibit unnatural coloring and growths.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAzureblight Acacia-2)</sup>

The seeds of some breeds of Acacia greatly resemble those of the [Strangler](Lore_Strangler.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOEslion-3)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Conservatory_Furnishings/Trees)

## Alkanet
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/OB-flora-Alkanet.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Alkanet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Alkanet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Alkanet.jpg) An Alkanet Plant A tall, flowering plant that is most common in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The flowers are usually found in groups of two to five and normally grow near rocks.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Alkanet Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alkanet_Flower)
## Alocasia
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SI-flora-Alocasia.jpg/200px-SI-flora-Alocasia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-flora-Alocasia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-flora-Alocasia.jpg) An Alocasia Plant Alocasia is a short, fruit-bearing plant that can be found in the [Elden Grove](Lore_Elden_Grove.md) of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), as well as all across [Mania](Lore_Mania.md), the northern region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).

The mortal residents of the Shivering Isles often use alocasia fruit in their cooking.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> In Valenwood, the local [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) would cover the alocasia leaves in silk in summer, essentially slowing its growth. This allows the fruit to grow larger and become sweeter. It is believed that [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) takes the fallen fruit as tribute. It is very similar to the [Water Hyacinth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Water_Hyacinth), although that flower does not bear fruit.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TBot GT-5)</sup> Daedric jam made from alocasia fruit has been known to leave Daedra feeling lightheaded and floaty, although mortals who consume it are driven to the brink of insanity.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Alocasia)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Alocasia Fruit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alocasia_Fruit)
## [Aloe Vera](Lore_Aloe_Vera.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/OB-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Aloe_Vera.jpg) An Aloe Vera plant **Aloe Vera** (or simply **aloe**) is a tall and leafy succulent plant found in warmer climates.

Aloe plants are found mainly in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), with pockets also located throughout the coastline and hills of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). Different aloe species can also be found in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), and even the fiery wastes of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md). Although not native to the province, aloe can be cultivated in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Aloe), [Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Aloe), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Aloe_Vera), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Aloe_Vera_Leaves.md)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Aloe Vera Leaves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aloe_Vera_Leaves)

## Arabesque Nut
Arabesque Nuts can be found growing wild in the [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) mountains. Their succulent nuts are often the only nourishment of adventurers who find themselves in the wilderness without rations. It is believed they have magical properties. When they are crushed and mixed with the milk of the agile footed mountain goat, a potion that allows the user to glide safely above the ground can be made.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DC-8)</sup><sup>:127</sup>

**Found in:** - [The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)

## Arrowroot Plant
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/OB-flora-Arrowroot.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Arrowroot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Arrowroot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Arrowroot.jpg) An Arrowroot Plant Arrowroot is a medium-sized, thick, rubbery tuber with tall leaves and small white flowers that comes from [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The plant is considered rare because it is scrawny and is rather unnoticeable. The most important part of the plant is the root because of its magical properties. Grinding it into paste can enhance the consumer's accuracy with projectile weaponry.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SFo T-9)</sup> Though it is commonly found in Valenwood, there is a relatively large concentration across the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md), between [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md). Every so often, they can also be found around the southern [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md), the [Larsius River](Lore_Larsius_River.md), and even the [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) outskirts.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Arrowroot)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Arrowroot](Lore_Arrowroot.md)
## Ash
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Heavy_Ash.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Heavy_Ash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Heavy_Ash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Heavy_Ash.jpg) An Ash tree A common tree that grows all around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), known for its denseness and flexibility.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CM16C-10)</sup>. It is generally found in regions such as [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), where it is often twisted and angled, but also heavy.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONKrrztrrb-11)</sup> It is also found in abundance in the eastern [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md), which is riddled with highlands and dotted with dense forests of ash and other trees. These are harvested by the logging industry of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md), which supplies the great demand for construction and weaponry.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-GCG-12)</sup> The sacred seed of the [Firesong Circle](Lore_Firesong_Circle.md) comes from the ash tree.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONKasorayn-13)</sup>

The wood is sanded and used to craft bows, [staves](Lore_Staves.md), and shields. It is generally stronger than [birch](Lore_Birch.md), but weaker than [mahogany](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mahogany), and is on par with [ironweed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ironweed), ironhide and [galatite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Galatite). Various factions from across the provinces use either ash or yew when they make their bows, among these include the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md),<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-CM33TG-14)</sup> and the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-CM40OOTH-15)</sup> Other groups such as the [Welkynar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Welkynar) uses it for the haft of their staves, and some factions like [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) import these materials, since the funguswood in their homeland is far more inferior than the ash or yew wood.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-CM51HH-16)</sup> Ash tree is also used in [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) imagery. [Vaia](Lore_Vaia.md)'s Golden Ash is a symbol of unity, strength, and the heritage of the Orc people.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-CST-17)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ash)

**Harvested Ingredient:** - [Rough Ash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ash)

## Ash Basil
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/ON-node-Ash_Basil.jpg/200px-ON-node-Ash_Basil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Ash_Basil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Ash_Basil.jpg) Ash Basil A small, low growing plant with strong-smelling leaves which are colored green from the base which fades to red at their extremities. The Ash Basil can found growing the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region and the leaves are known for their pungent scent and insect-repelling qualities.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Ash Basil Leaf-18)</sup> The local [Dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) also see the plant as a food source, those that feed upon it are said to secrete a superior, more durable [Dreugh Wax](Lore_Dreugh_Wax.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ESOJee-Lar-19)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bug_Off!)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Ash Basil Leaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Stonefalls_Quest_Items#Ash_Basil_Leaf)
## Ash Yam
