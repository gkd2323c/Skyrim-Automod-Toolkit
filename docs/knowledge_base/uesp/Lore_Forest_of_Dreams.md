# Forest of Dreams

The Forest of Dreams is a place associated with [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRKyne-1)</sup> the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) goddess of the Storm, widow of [Shor](Lore_Shor.md), the Blessed Warrior-Wife,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:354</sup> and a favored god of warriors.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Froki-4)</sup> According to certain beliefs of the ancient Nords, some servants of Kyne found eternal rest in the Forest of Dreams, such as Hela, who was commemorated with a [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) and remembered as a "friend to all beasts".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRKyne-1)</sup>

## Notes
- It is known that worshippers of Kyne ascend to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md) upon death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Froki-4)</sup> However, the exact location of the Forest of Dreams is unclear, as is its connection to Sovngarde, which is also described as dreamlike<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SSS-5)</sup> and associated with dreams.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ADOS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KJ-7)</sup>
