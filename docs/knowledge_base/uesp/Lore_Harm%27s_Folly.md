[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/OB-place-Harm%27s_Folly.jpg/200px-OB-place-Harm%27s_Folly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Harm%27s_Folly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Harm%27s_Folly.jpg) Harm's Folly Harm's Folly is a small farming settlement located in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), far to the northwest of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md). It is located on the foothills of the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md), in a region of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) which has been cleared for agriculture around [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## History
The future site of Harm's Folly was unoccupied circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Harm's Folly consisted of a single farmhouse. The farmhouse was home to a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) farmer, Corrick Northwode, and his wife, Kayleen. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Corrick departed Harm's Folly to purchase supplies, leaving his wife alone on the farm. The [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) that inhabited a nearby spent mine named Exhausted Mine chose this as their opportunity to attack Harm's Folly, killing Kayleen and looting her [jade](Lore_Jade.md) amulet. Corrick, having heard his wife's screams from afar, arrived back too late to save her from the war party, and subsequently buried her in a grave behind the farmhouse.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Barely a week after the attack, the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) arrived at Harm's Folly and spoke to Corrick. In his grief, Corrick asked the Hero to enter Exhausted Mine, kill every last Goblin within, and recover the stolen amulet. The Hero duly exacted Corrick's revenge and retrieved Kayleen's amulet from the corpse of the Goblin Netherboss, providing the widower with something to remember her by. For this service, Corrick rewarded the Hero with gems, and Kayleen's spirit blessed the Hero with her grace for paying respects at her gravesite.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), two Oblivion Gates opened close to Harm's Folly, but the farm was unaffected.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
