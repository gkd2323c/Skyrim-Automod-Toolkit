[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_04.jpg/200px-ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_04.jpg) Stonetalon Clan Camp at the Three Tribes Camp **Chieftain** Vivian Witchclaw was a [Reachman](Lore_Reachmen.md) leader of the [Stonetalon Clan](Lore_Stonetalon_Clan.md) active in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup> She made an alliance with two other Reachfolk clans: the [Rageclaws](Lore_Rageclaw_Clan.md) and the [Boneshapers](Lore_Boneshaper_Clan.md) and signed the treaty on 3rd of the Frost Fall in 2E 582.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTTC-2)</sup> The clans aligned themselves with the [Cult of the Black Worm](Lore_Cult_of_the_Black_Worm.md), which promised power and blessings of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Online:Bermund-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DU-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WN-5)</sup> and invaded [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTTC-2)</sup>

Under her leadership, the Stonetalon Clan participated in warfare-related activities. Her clan took over [Northwind Mine](Lore_Northwind_Mine.md), where her kinsmen, led by heiress Alona, were performing human sacrifices to establish a [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) coven. The Stonetalons were eventually driven off and the hagravens who inhabited the caverns killed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Tovisa-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-STM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BTC-8)</sup>

Ultimately the Reachmen alliance was defeated. Their allied forces, led by [Stral Blackthroat](Lore_Stral_Blackthroat.md), were vanquished in the [Vernim Woods](Lore_Vernim_Woods.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONShroud-9)</sup> and their attack on the Boulderfall Pass was stopped.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SOTH-10)</sup> Vivian, together with other clan leaders: [Kevinne Blightheart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kevinne_Blightheart) of the Boneshapers and [Lydi Snowpelt](Lore_Lydi_Snowpelt.md) of the Rageclaws was defeated by the [King's Arrow](Lore_The_Vestige.md) at the encampment west of Boulderfall Pass, as per request of dying [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) soldier Melril.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-RTC-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Melril-12)</sup> Clan remained active following her death,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOSS-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-EHSS-14)</sup> but the invasion of the Rift was thwarted.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONShroud-9)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TN-15)</sup>
