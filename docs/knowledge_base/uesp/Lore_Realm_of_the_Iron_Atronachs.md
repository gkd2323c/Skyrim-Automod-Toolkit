[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Guar.jpg) An Iron Atronach summoned from the realm and bound in the form of a [guar](Lore_Guar.md) mount The realm of the Iron Atronachs is an unnamed volcanic realm that drifts silently through the void of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). The realm is said to resemble a molten hellscape: impossibly dense and boiling with malice. It is the home of the [Iron Atronachs](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md), which are produced there in abundance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Iron Atro Web-1)</sup>

It might be assumed that this realm is a 'collective realm' similar to [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md), created as an extension of its Iron Atronach inhabitants. If so, the realm would be rather mundane and uniform in appearance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FNHQA-2)</sup>

This realm is well known to [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) due to the ability to summon subservient atronachs from there. However, few conjurers dare to call upon these towering behemoths due to their relentless appetite for destruction.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Iron Atro Web-1)</sup>
