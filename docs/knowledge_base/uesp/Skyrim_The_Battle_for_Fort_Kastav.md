## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) at the [Winterhold Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.md).
2. Meet the Stormcloaks west of the fort.
3. Take the fort.

## Detailed Walkthrough
You will only receive this quest if you chose to give [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) to the Imperial Legion during the quest [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). Go to the [Winterhold Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.md) and talk to [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md). Galmar will tell you to go to [Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Fort_Kastav.md) and meet the Stormcloaks preparing for the attack. Meet with the men west of the fort, and then attack the fort. Your journal will keep track of the percentage of enemies left. Once all the Imperials are dead, you will have to head back to [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) for the next part in the [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md) quest.

## Notes
- Completing this quest will exile [Kraldar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kraldar) and [Thonjolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thonjolf) to the [Erikur's House](Skyrim_Erikur%27s_House.md), and re-install [Korir](Skyrim_Korir.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of Winterhold.
