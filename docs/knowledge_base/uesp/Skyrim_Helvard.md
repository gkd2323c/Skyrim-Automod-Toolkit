[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-npc-Helvard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Helvard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Helvard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Helvard.jpg) Helvard **Helvard**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is the [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). He is a target of a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) [contract](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Helvard).

He wears a set of [scaled horn armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor), including matching [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots). He carries a [steel mace of Cold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace_of_Cold) as well as the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Jarl%27s_Longhouse) to the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md) and a few [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

She truly is the Steward of Falkreath."* *"[Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md) was a good Jarl in his day. Even if his day has passed."*
### Conversations
He may have a conversation with Jarl [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) and Nenya:

**Siddgeir**: *"Helvard! The guards are looking sloppy today. I want you to reduce their wages for the month."*
**Helvard**: *"Sir, I will shape them up but with the war on it is unwise to reduce their wages any further."* **OR** *"We can't afford to lose any men if we wish to keep Falkreath protected."*
**Siddgeir**: *"I would think a man of your military training would be able to do with less but so be it."*

**Helvard**: *"Preparing the defenses is almost impossible lately, every time we get a new war report our needs change."*
**Nenya**: *"The trade routes are affected as well. Procuring supplies is a guessing game. Weapons sell well though."*
**Helvard**: *"Mmmm, careful with that, Nenya, our merchants could be selling weapons to an invading force."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Kill Helvard
Like with most Dark Brotherhood targets, you may speak with Helvard before assassinating him. He'll say:

*"Yes, yes. What is it? We're all very busy here..."* You have three answers to choose from:

**(Remain silent)** *"Right. Well. If you have some kind of official business with the jarl, I'm afraid now's not the time. Go on then. Off with you."* **(he walks away)** **You protect the Jarl. But who protects you?** *"And why would I... Oh. Oh! Guards! Guards, I need assistance!"* **(he turns hostile and attacks, though nobody will come to his aid)** **The Dark Brotherhood has come!** *"Fiend! You will not leave here alive! Guards! Guards!"* **(he turns hostile and attacks, though nobody will come to his aid)** If you choose to remain silent, attempting to talk to him again:

*"By the love of the [Nine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nine_Divines), what is it with you sycophants and hangers on? The jarl is busy! And so am I, for that matter."*
## Notes
Helvard is the only member of the Jarl's Court in Falkreath that will never be replaced, whether or not the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) or the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) are in power.
