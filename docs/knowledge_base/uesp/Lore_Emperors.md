# Emperors


- [Tamrielic Emperors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tamrielic_Emperors), rulers of Tamriel, often seated in Cyrodiil - [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md), rulers of Cyrodiil during the Interregnum
- [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md), the ruling class of Yokuda who were run by a series of emperors, the last being Hira or Tarish-Zi
- The [Moon-Emperor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moon-Emperor), who ruled Elsweyr in the First Era
- High Kings of the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md), most notably Vrage, Harald and Borgas
- [Tosh Raka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tosh_Raka), an emperor of Ka Po' Tun
- [Dumac](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dumac), the king of the Second Empire of the Dwemer
- [Umaril the Unfeathered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Umaril), the Dread Castellan of the Ayleid Empire
