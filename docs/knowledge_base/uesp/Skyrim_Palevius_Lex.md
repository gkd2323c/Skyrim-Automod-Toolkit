[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-npc-Palevius_Lex.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Palevius_Lex.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Palevius_Lex.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Palevius_Lex.jpg) Palevius Lex **Palevius Lex** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) sailor found at the [Water Stone](Skyrim_Water_Stone.md) on Solstheim. He appears to pray at the stone, oblivious to the world around him.

Initially from [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), Palevius Lex, along with fellow sailors [Liesl](Skyrim_Liesl.md), [Hjalfar](Skyrim_Hjalfar.md), and [Benkum](Skyrim_Benkum.md), decided to cast anchor along the western coast of Solstheim and immediately fell prey to [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s spell. Palevius works around the clock at the stone, oblivious to the world around him. Once the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the entire crew will return to their boat on the coast. When you leave the area, they will all disappear from the game.

Palevius wears [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Miner%27s_Clothes.md#0006ff43) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). He carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).

## Dialogue
He shares his dialogue lines with his fellow sailors:

- *"Think we've got to get back to our ship now."*
- *"We're already late. Not sure why we decided to take such an extended shore time."*
- *"When the tide goes, we'll go with it."*
- *"It's a long way back to Windhelm from here."*
- *"Hope our shipments are still good. How long has it been?"*
- *"Best be shoving off now."*
