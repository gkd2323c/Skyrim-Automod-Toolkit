# Penitus Oculatus

— [Intendant Marall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Characters#Intendant_Marall)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup> Penitus Oculatus means "The Inner Eyes." As their name suggests, they are the eyes, the sword, and the shield of the [Mede Dynasty](Lore_Mede_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESLCl-2)</sup>

They seemingly took up the duties of the [Imperial Order of Blades](Lore_Blades.md) and [Imperial Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Guard) after the fall of the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md).

## History
In the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), as the [Septim's royal family](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md) decayed, and the [Dragonborn Emperors](Lore_Dragonborn.md#Dragonborn_Emperors) were no more, the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) saw fit to distance themselves from the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRAFOTB-3)</sup> espousing to no longer serve as the Imperial honor guard, and working from afar as an intelligence agency and "neutral" allies of the Empire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESLCl-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESLTFH-4)</sup>

Around this time, the Penitus Oculatus came into the fray, becoming the new intelligence, assassination, and security force of the new royal family.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESLCl-2)</sup> Unlike the Blades, the Penitus Oculatus is a purely Imperial organization.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRAFOTB-3)</sup> The Penitus Oculatus requires its members to have sharp senses and a willingness to kill. Their recruitment process is oftentimes a brutal one, as displayed by [Inspector Colin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Colin)'s task to assassinate an old man. The purpose of such a recruitment process was to weed out those unwilling to actually go through with murder and other usually immoral tasks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup>

Sometime in the early Fourth Era, a man named [Julius Primus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Julius_Primus) began calling himself the new [King of Worms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:King_of_Worms) but was eventually put down by the Penitus Oculatus.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LOS-5)</sup><sup>:Part 2, Chapter 6</sup>

Circa [4E 40](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_40), [Remar Vel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Characters#Remar_Vel) was the Administrator of the Penitus Oculatus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup>
