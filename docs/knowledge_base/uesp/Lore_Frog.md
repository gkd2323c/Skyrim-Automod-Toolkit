# Frog

**Frogs** (sometimes called **toads**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup> are small amphibians that can often be found wherever it is wet. Their young are known as tadpoles. Frogs are common in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) and are prevalent in [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) culture. The [Vossa-satl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vossa-satl) is an Argonian [instrument](Lore_Instruments.md) which uses living frogs from several species to create the instrument's unique sound.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup> Both Argonians and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) use frogs in their [cuisine](Lore_Cuisine.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) are known to race specially bred frogs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Species

### Aojee Toad
A species of toad found in Black Marsh. Argonians consider it a delicacy, even though the toad is poisonous. Eating too much of it leads to violent tremors and mouth frothing, followed by death. The Aojee Toad is used in the making of Aojee-Sakka, where it's served alongside a bowl of cold *hosh*, which is also poisonous, but the two poisons are actually antidotes to each other.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Lore](Lore_A_Culinary_Adventure,_Volume_4.md)

### [Bull-Horned Magma Frog](Lore_Bull-Horned_Magma_Frog.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-creature-Traux_the_Ancient.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Traux_the_Ancient.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Traux_the_Ancient.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Traux_the_Ancient.jpg) A bull-horned magma frog [Bull-Horned Magma Frogs](Lore_Bull-Horned_Magma_Frog.md) are large frogs which can be found on [Galen](Lore_Galen.md) and [Y'ffelon](Lore_Y%27ffelon.md). They are said to be a story given life, of which the first emerged from [Y'ffre's](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) Path.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bull-Horned_Magma_Frog)

### [Death Hopper](Lore_Death_Hopper.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/ON-creature-Death_Hopper.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Death_Hopper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Death_Hopper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Death_Hopper.jpg) A Death Hopper [Death Hoppers](Lore_Death_Hopper.md) are giant, carnivorous frogs that can be found in the swamps of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) and the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md). They are typically green, but can also be brown. Death hoppers are generally found in packs. They hunt by lurking beneath the surface of water, waiting for their prey to wander above them. They can swallow large prey whole. Death hoppers are extremely poisonous, and can spit globs of toxic matter over great distances. They are known to spawn in the foul waters of the West Weald: their tadpoles are referred to as nymphs.

Plague Hoppers are a [diseased](Lore_Disease.md) brown variety of Death Hoppers which are born in the [Pits](Lore_Pits.md). They could also be found in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) as a result of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md)'s infiltration of the realm. A tale about the Viridescent [Dragon Frog](Lore_Dragon_Frog.md) says that the green frogs once grew as large as [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), somewhat similar to Death Hoppers, but envied the Dragons their wings. Upon making a pact with [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md), they gained their wings but lost their size, becoming dragon frogs.

The [Sul-Xan](Lore_Argonian.md#Tribes) tribe is known to use death hopper vocal sacs when making armor.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Death_Hopper)

### [Dragon Frog](Lore_Dragon_Frog.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-pet-Kindlespit_Dragon_Frog.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Kindlespit_Dragon_Frog.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Kindlespit_Dragon_Frog.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Kindlespit_Dragon_Frog.jpg) A dragon frog [Dragon Frogs](Lore_Dragon_Frog.md) are a unique variety of frog native to certain areas of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), where they are commonly kept as pets for their striking appearance and pest control attributes. They are so named for their small wings and breath abilities; the Hammerfell varieties spitting fire, while those of Black Marsh spew a poisonous gas, which both species use to kill flying insects or other small prey before consumption. When they are ready to mate, female dragon frogs emit an odor that attracts the males, which can result in large gatherings as every frog nearby homes in on the scent.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Dragon_Frog)

### Falinesti Peeper
A rare breed of red frog native to [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) that lives among the reeds within Sacred Leap Grotto. A trial to join Brackenleaf's Briars requires a Falinesti Peeper to be eaten, after which the eater is able to see the true nature of the forest.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Falinesti_Peeper)

### Glowing Frog
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/ON-creature-Glowing_Frog.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Glowing_Frog.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Glowing_Frog.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Glowing_Frog.jpg) A glowing frog Glowing frogs are a species native to [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) and [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md). They are easily identified by their blue spots and distinctive orange glow.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Glowing_Frog)

### Indigo Tree Frog
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/ON-creature-Frog_04.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Frog_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_04.jpg) An indigo tree frog The indigo tree frog is a species of frog found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), which are used by [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) *vossa-satl* makers to create the instrument's unique sound. They are blue in color with black spots and live in groups, nesting in the high branches of trees and only come down to feed on insects such as [fleshflies](Lore_Fleshflies.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Indigo_Tree_Frog)

### Moss-Foot Croaker
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/ON-creature-Frog_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Frog_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_02.jpg) A moss-foot croaker Moss-foot croakers are a species of bright-green frogs found in the coastal marshes of Murmkire near Lilmoth. As they have no natural predators, they are known to be docile and playful creatures.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup> The Moss-Foot Croaker is prized for its croaks and is used in the creation of the vossa-satl, an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) musical instrument.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Moss-Foot_Croaker)

### Nettle-Toad
A species of toad found in Murkmire.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

**Found in:** - Lore

### Ruby Hopper
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/ON-creature-Frog_03.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Frog_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Frog_03.jpg) A ruby hopper The ruby hopper is a rare species of frog found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They are bright red in color and enjoy sunning themselves, however they are also very quick and are easily startled. [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) vossa-satl makers use these frogs for their pleasing croaking sounds.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ruby_Hopper)

### Sun-Blessed Toad
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/ON-creature-Sun-Blessed_Toad.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Sun-Blessed_Toad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Sun-Blessed_Toad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Sun-Blessed_Toad.jpg) A sun-blessed toad The sun-blessed toad is an especially rare species found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They are pale green in color and dappled with gold, and are rarely seen, preferring to stay in their mud pits and only emerging to mate every few seasons. As their croaks are highly prized by [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) vossa-satl makers, they developed a way to lure them out. This involves creating artificial toad musk out of [stench sedge](Lore_Flora_S.md#Stench_Sedge), [salt rush](Lore_Flora_S.md#Salt_Rush), and [joymallow](Lore_Flora_J.md#Joymallow) and applying it to a croak-wheel which mimics the mating croak of the Sun-Blessed Toad.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AGTGF-10)</sup> They are known to be quite fragile when excited.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nesh Deeka-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sun-Blessed_Toad)
