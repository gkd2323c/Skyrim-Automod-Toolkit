Against the Snakes **by [Denskar](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Denskar)** *A soldier's thoughts on the second Akaviri invasion*
---
This book describes the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md), referring to the enemies as "Snakes" and as being from "Snake island". The enemies fought by the future [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) during the Second Akaviri Invasion were the [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(race).md), who have been previously described as "Snow Demons". The more commonly encountered inhabitants from Akavir are the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md), who have been described as "Snakes" and other words for "Serpent" in the past. Whether this is a text revealing Tsaesci involvement in the Second Akaviri Invasion, the in-universe author derogatorily describing all Akaviri as being "Snakes", or a writing error where the actual author used "Snake" in the place of "Snow Demon" cannot be adequately determined.

---
Been thinking about our fight against the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md). I've got opinions. Think I'll write them down.

The whole [damned war](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md) could have been avoided if those idiots in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) had the sense to throw the [Snakes](Lore_Akaviri.md) back into the sea. [Shor](Lore_Shor.md) take them!

Still wish [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md) had moved to reinforce Windhelm. Lost some good men that day.

Jorunn fortified [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), but the Snakes rolled right past. Why go straight to the [Ashlands](Lore_Stonefalls.md)?

The fighting south of Riften, north of [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md), isn't given enough respect. Those men were pinned and outmanned, and still they held.

[Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) was the only reason the men kept fighting that day. Jorunn was just a pup. He couldn't rally a mead hall.

The Snakes push into [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) after the fall of Fort Virak. Why? What were they after?

Heard rumors some of the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) turned their blades on the Shellbacks at first. Couldn't stand the thought of being rescued by [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md). Damned fools.

Shor take the whole [Snake island](Lore_Akavir.md). Let them send another [tiger demon](Lore_Ka_Po%27_Tun_(race).md)! We'll take them again!
