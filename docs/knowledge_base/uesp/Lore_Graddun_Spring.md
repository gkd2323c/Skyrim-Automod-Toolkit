[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/AR-place-Graddun_Spring.jpg/200px-AR-place-Graddun_Spring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Graddun_Spring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Graddun_Spring.jpg) Graddun Spring during Imperial Simulacrum Graddun Spring is a coastal settlement located on the northwestern part of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), northwest of [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Graddun Spring was famous for its unguents, which were used as sweet-smelling hair creams by many of the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) inhabitants of the Summerset Isles to style their hair.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CG-3)</sup>

By the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Graddun Spring was an active settlement, being ruled by Lady Lorana. It had a rivalry with Marbruk Brook, a town located in the northern parts of northwestern Summerset Isle, east of [Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md), as well as road connections to [Corgrad Wastes](Lore_Corgrad_Wastes.md) and [Kings Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kings_Haven).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
