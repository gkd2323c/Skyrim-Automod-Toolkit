# Faoshor

Faoshor was one of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) sorcerer advisors of [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md) the [Black Drake](Lore_Blackdrake_Clan.md), who held a prominent position of trust and influence in the emperor's court in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). He was possibly a member of the [Tagh Droiloch](Lore_Tagh_Droiloch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HCP-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation_Tagh_Droiloch.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation_Tagh_Droiloch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation_Tagh_Droiloch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation_Tagh_Droiloch.jpg) Tagh Droiloch [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) Durcorach the Black Drake, Faoshor's liege Little is known about Faoshor's past. If he was a member of the [Tagh Droiloch](Lore_Tagh_Droiloch.md), then he and his fellow [witch-men](Lore_Witches.md) were responsible for the deal with [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) to have Durcorach crowned Emperor of Cyrodiil. Durcorach's sorcerers were charged with performing countless tasks in preparation for the day when the final part of the arrangement would be carried out and Dagon would claim his due with the [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md), but the exact role of Faoshor in the process is unknown.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ADi S-2)</sup>

As of [2E 536](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_536), Faoshor, alongside [Baarselg](Lore_Baarselg.md) and [Old Wunagh](Lore_Wunagh.md), served as a trusted confidant of Emperor Durcorach. [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md), the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md), speculated that Faoshor, as well as his two compatriots, were members of the Tagh Droiloch. Although they played an important role in Durcoarch's conquest, the Tagh Droiloch seemingly vanished into obscurity after Durcorach was crowned Emperor of Cyrodiil, leaving their motives and fate unknown. The exact details of their involvement in Durcorach's ascent to power and their ultimate goal remain a mystery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HCP-1)</sup>

Nothing is known about Faoshor's fate following the end of the reign of Durcorach, though Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md) was considered the scion of the Tagh Droiloch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-COTFC5-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Faoshor's name consists of two parts. One of the parts is identical to the beginning of the [Faolan](Lore_Faolan.md)'s name, which meant "Red Eagle" in the [language of the Reach](Lore_Reach-tongue.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RE-4)</sup> [Shor](Lore_Shor.md), on the other hand, is the name of [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) god,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DATN-5)</sup> known to share certain similarities with Reachfolk god [Lorkh](Lore_Lorkh.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GSOTR-6)</sup>
