[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/ON-place-Tanzelwil.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tanzelwil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tanzelwil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tanzelwil.jpg) Tanzelwil ca. 2E 582 Tanzelwil is a large ruin on the coastal highlands of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), the second-largest island in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). The [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) consider the ruins one of their most sacred sites on the island as it serves as a temple for the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md); ancestors to the island's people and a mausoleum to ancient kinlords.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Inner-1)</sup> New monarchs of the province will travel to Tanzelwil's Ancestral Chamber and quietly commune with the dead to retrieve their blessings.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAyrenn-2)</sup> The ruins are built in a large, forested valley that splits [Iluvamir](Lore_Iluvamir.md) from the rest of the island. It extends west over a plateau that looms over the [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONTanzelwil-3)</sup>

## History
At some point in time, the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) built a hidden, underground tomb in the ravine in the middle of the temple's compound, underneath a red cherry blossom tree. They also hid glyphs that could open it across the grounds.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLegacy-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) traveled to Tanzelwil with her entourage of confidants and the First Auridon Marines to retrieve the dead's blessing. However, upon arrival, she was denied by a spirit that claimed her ancestors disavowed her and revealed the involvement of both the [Veiled Queen](Lore_Estre.md) and Ayrenn's advisor, Norion. The mage-turned-advisor placed magical stones all over the temple and used them to corrupt the spirits. In response, Ayrenn sent her [Agent](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to rescue two priestesses, and they destroyed the stones along the way.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONName-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONStones-6)</sup> They later joined her in the ruins' inner chambers where they confronted Norion and Ayrenn received her ancestor's blessing and acknowledgment.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONRites-7)</sup>
