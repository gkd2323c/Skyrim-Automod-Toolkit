[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-npc-Indaryn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Indaryn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indaryn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indaryn.jpg) Indaryn **Indaryn** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) that [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) has appointed to run the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He is merely a puppet for Maven and not really allowed to make decisions on her behalf and, as a result, Indaryn struggles to keep his questionable staff in line. During [Under the Table](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Under_the_Table) you can choose to help him find out who is selling [Black-Briar Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) behind his back. Indaryn also seems to have fallen under the promiscuous spell of [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md), an indiscretion to which you will have him confess during the quest [Caught Red Handed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Caught_Red_Handed).

Indaryn has a room on the second floor of the Meadery where he sleeps from midnight to 6am. He starts the day with a one-hour breakfast at the neighboring [Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) then works the Meadery for twelve solid hours, either looking after his employees from the balcony above the vats, or helping out on the ground floor. At 7pm he returns to the Bee and Barb where he stays until his bedtime at midnight.

When searching the tasting room where young [Ungrien](Skyrim_Ungrien.md) tries his best to sell the mead, you will notice three angry notes scattered around, all of them from Indaryn, and all of them hinting at huge problems in the meadery. The *[first note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:To_Be_Read_Immedately!)* concerns a switch of the [Black-Briar Reserve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Reserve) into regular [Black-Briar Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) bottles, the *[second note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:All_Employees_Must_Read!)* concerns how the employees has filled Indaryn's bed with [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) droppings, while the *[third note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Attention_Employees!)*, found on the tasting counter, is a regular threat from Indaryn, on behalf of Maven. However, this note is dismissed by a *[note from Maven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_From_Maven)* found in Indaryn's bedroom.

Indaryn wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8713) and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and he carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Meadery_Key) to the meadery, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
If you approach him while he is at work, he will say:

*"I'm the brewmaster around here. What did you want?"* *"I'm quite busy. Need something or can I get back to work?"* If approached outside the Meadery he will instead say:

*"Yeah? What do you want? If this is about the meadery, we can talk later."* When spoken to:

**You run the Black-Briar Meadery?** *"Maven Black-Briar wants me to keep the mead flowing and that's what I aim to do despite all these lazy good-for-nothings that work for us. Ever since I took over as the foreman the production of our mead has never been more profitable. And if the meadery is profitable, I get rich. Get the picture?"* **Tell me about Maven Black-Briar.** *"Lady Maven could buy or sell anyone in Skyrim. I wouldn't be surprised if she's the one pulling the strings around this city. She's always welcome in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) and between you and me, I'm pretty sure she even has some pretty influential friends within the Empire. I'd also warn you not to cross her. She employs quite a few... troubleshooters to take care of messy problems."* When you exit conversation while he is at work, he will bid you farewell with:

*"Good, now maybe I can get back to work."* If exiting conversation when he is at the tavern, he will instead conclude:

*"Well, that was important."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Spread the Love
During the quest [Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love), you can hand him a pamphlet from the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md), and you will get a hateful response: : *"What's this nonsense? Bah."*

### Under the Table
During [Under the Table](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Under_the_Table) you will have the option to either deliver the keg to [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) or to tell Indaryn about Romlyn's undercover business. When you tell him:

**Did you know Romlyn is skimming from the meadery?** *"Aye, I suspected as much but I've never been able to catch that little sneak with proof."* **He gave me this keg to deliver.** *"That fetcher! I gave him the same keg just this morning and told him to store it in the cellars. Oh, he'll rot in prison for this one I promise you. As soon as Maven finds out, it's all over for him. Here, take this as a bit of a reward for nabbing our little thief."* He will then hand you some leveled jewelry, although Maven will never hear about the illegal mead-trade and Romlyn will remain an employee of the meadery indefinitely.

### Caught Red Handed
During the quest [Caught Red Handed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Caught_Red_Handed), you will discover the whereabouts of the promiscuous [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella) -worshipper [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md). When handing you the quest, [Svana Far-Shield](Skyrim_Svana_Far-Shield.md) will tell you that Haelga recently slept with no less than three different men, leaving them all with a [Mark of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mark_of_Dibella) as a token of her affection. When confronted with this accusation, Indaryn will become enraged:

**Haelga gave you a Mark of Dibella. I want it.** *"You're crazy! How dare you make such an accusation!"* You can then either persuade, intimidate, or give up. Note that your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill must be at least 50 for a successful persuasion:
