Fellrunners are flightless [birds](Lore_Bird.md) found primarily in desert regions such as [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) and [Northern Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). They can also be found in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and parts of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoc-1)</sup> Fellrunners are harmless, and as their name implies, will run at the sight of anything suspicious; this reflex is often used against them by hunters, due to the fact they will run too fast and trip, breaking their thin bones.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAIa T-2)</sup> They are incapable of flying and swimming due to their tiny wings.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAIa T-2)</sup>

## Uses
Fellrunner meat is described as being good for making jerky, but is tricky to cook without becoming dry. It is also described as being more like steak than poultry, and that it's "dark red and marbled with thin, delectable streaks of fat".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGGt C-3)</sup> One [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) researcher had discovered that if fellrunners were imbued with [wisp](Lore_Wisp.md) essence that it would cook the bird perfectly from within.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FF-4)</sup> Their feathers are also known to be used as quills<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> and are used on wooden dangle sticks.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Many species of fellrunner are kept as pets. Teal-Faced Fellrunners became popular as pets during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) after a flock was sold to [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md)'s menagerie in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
