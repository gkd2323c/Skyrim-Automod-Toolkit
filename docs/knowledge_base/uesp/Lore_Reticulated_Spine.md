[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-map-Reticulated_Spine.jpg/200px-ON-map-Reticulated_Spine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Reticulated_Spine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Reticulated_Spine.jpg) Map of Reticulated Spine [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-place-Shadowfen_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shadowfen_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadowfen_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadowfen_02.jpg) Reticulated Spine circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Reticulated Spine is a region located in northern [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) and is on the border of the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). To the north is the region of [Lagomere](Lore_Lagomere.md) in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), to the south is the region of [Venomous Fens](Lore_Venomous_Fens.md), and to the southeast is the region of [Leafwater](Lore_Leafwater.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Reticulated Spine-1)</sup>

The city of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md) is based on the northern river, built on the site of an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Silyanorn Load-2)</sup> [Stillrise Village](Lore_Stillrise_Village.md) was one of the last [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) settlements to survive after the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KFU-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AFA-4)</sup> The ruins of [Ten-Maur-Wolk](Lore_Ten-Maur-Wolk.md) were once built by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) for the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), but it was abandoned sometime later.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ten-Maur-Wolk Load-5)</sup> [Bogmother](Lore_Bogmother.md) was an ancient temple settlement<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGTBM-6)</sup> but was later abandoned and inhabited by [dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DAR-7)</sup>
