# Phen

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/DF-place-Shrines.png/200px-DF-place-Shrines.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Shrines.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Shrines.png) A shrine to Phen Phen was an obscure entity of importance to the inhabitants of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. Phen was worshipped there as of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Shrines to him, known as Shelters of Phen could be found in regions of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) such as [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md), [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md), [Bergama](Lore_Bergama_(barony).md), [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md), [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md), [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam_(county).md), and [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md). Shelters of Phen were also found in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in regions such as [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md), [Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Glenumbra_Moors_(barony).md), [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md), [Shalgora](Lore_Shalgora.md), [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md), and [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFShrines-1)</sup>
