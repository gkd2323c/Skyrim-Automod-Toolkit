## Echatere Moss
Echatere Moss is a plant that grows in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). It is sometimes confused for a weed because it thrives in freezing temperatures. Believed to be quite healthy, [echatere](Lore_Echatere.md) are known to graze upon it, but it's also been used in [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) cuisine.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Echatere Moss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Orsinium_Quest_Items#Echatere_Moss)
## Eldergleam
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg/200px-SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eldergleam.jpg) The Eldergleam circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The [Eldergleam](Lore_Eldergleam.md) is an ancient tree, thought to be one of the oldest living things in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It is located in a cave in the volcanic tundra of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), one of the Holds of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is sacred to worshippers of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Eldergleam Sap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eldergleam_Sap), [Eldergleam Sapling](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sapling.md)

## Emetic Russula
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-node-Emetic_Russula.jpg/200px-ON-node-Emetic_Russula.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Emetic_Russula.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Emetic_Russula.jpg) An Emetic Russula mushroom Emetic Russula have a red domed cap with a white stem. **Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Emetic Russula Cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Emetic_Russula_Cap) or [Emetic Russula](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Emetic_Russula)
## Emperor Parasol
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/MW-flora-Emperor_Parasol.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Emperor_Parasol.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Emperor_Parasol.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Emperor_Parasol.jpg) Emperor Parasol Emperor Parasols are a species of giant mushroom native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), where they can often be seen in the place of trees. [Emperor Parasol Moss](Lore_Emperor_Parasol_Moss.md) can often be found hanging from them. The [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) are known for their architectural dependence on the fungus, which they magically shape and cultivate into bridges, buildings and mage towers. See also: [Mushroom Tree](Lore_Mushroom_Tree.md)

## Emperor Parasol Moss
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-flora-Emperor_Parasol_Moss.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Emperor_Parasol_Moss.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Emperor_Parasol_Moss.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Emperor_Parasol_Moss.jpg) Emperor Parasol Moss Emperor Parasol Moss can often be found hanging from [Emperor Parasol](Lore_Emperor_Parasol.md) mushrooms native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Moss hanging from the Emperor Parasols on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) is known to be [used](Lore_Emperor_Parasol_Moss_(alchemy).md) in alchemy.

**Found in:** - [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Emperor Parasol Moss](Skyrim_Emperor_Parasol_Moss.md)
