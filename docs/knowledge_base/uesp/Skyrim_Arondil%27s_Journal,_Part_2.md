**Arondil's Journal**

<u>Day 8</u>
I have established a temporary work station in the cold caves, and have found multiple burial chambers within the snow and ice. While in the first chamber, I exhumed a few 'test subjects,' all female. I was stunned to find my mind wandering again to the women of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) as I examine my find. At first, I was slightly disturbed by my thoughts, but later I found myself reevaluating...

<u>Day 9</u>
The first trials went splendidly. Each subject was [reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) in a state of total complacency, bending to my every task and whim. I have ordered a few of my new [servants](Skyrim_Undead.md) to guard the island, while the rest will act as my personal entourage, standing by me at all times. Standing watch over me while I sleep...
