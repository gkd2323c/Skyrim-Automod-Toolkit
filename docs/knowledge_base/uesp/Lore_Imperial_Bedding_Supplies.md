# Imperial Bedding Supplies

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/MW-item-Pillow.png/200px-MW-item-Pillow.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Pillow.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Pillow.png) Pillow Imperial Bedding Supplies<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWInvoice-1)</sup> was a [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) -based [Imperial](Lore_Third_Empire.md) producer of pillows, blankets, and other bedding supplies that operated and had factories in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRTherdon-2)</sup> They were known to directly ship bedding supplies to clients on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) by boat.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWThelas Quest-3)</sup>

Pillows stuffed with [cliff racer](Lore_Cliff_Racer.md) plumes were a luxurious good offered by the factory.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWDrarayne-4)</sup>

## History
Imperial Bedding Supplies was a business established some years before 3E 426.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRTherdon-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWThelas Quest-3)</sup>

A few years before 3E 427, the market for bedding supplies started to dwindle.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRTherdon-2)</sup> The company mismanaged the venture capital that they had access to, spending it on accounting department offices in expensive [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) towers and chairs made out of [scamp](Lore_Scamp.md) skin. These circumstances initially lead to massively increased work days for the seamsters and seamstresses, followed by a wave of layoffs, with the ultimate fate of the business remaining unknown.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRTherdon-2)</sup>

## Notes
- While an exact location of the Imperial Bedding Supplies' pillow factory is unknown, limited information suggests a rough location: - [Therdon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Therdon) was laid off and had the time to move to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) within a single week.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRTherdon-2)</sup>
- [Drarayne Thelas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Drarayne_Thelas)'s order was shipped out of the factory on the 8th of Morning Star 3E 426,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWInvoice-1)</sup>, and the shipwreck was found somewhere after Last Seed 3E 427,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWThelas Quest-3)</sup> meaning the ship sank somewhere in the one and a half years between those dates.
