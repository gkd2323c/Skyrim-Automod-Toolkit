[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/ON-map-Stony_Basin.jpg/200px-ON-map-Stony_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Stony_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Stony_Basin.jpg) Map of Stony Basin circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Stony Basin** is a region located in the hold of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), which is a part of the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is the lowest point of the Rift, hence the name. The major settlement in the area is the township of [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md), which serves as an Ebony mine for the city-state of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). As you move westward, settlements become less abundant, the [Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md) was one of the oldest mead halls dedicated to the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md), who used the Hall as their base in the Rift. In the western basin, the [Vernim Woods](Lore_Vernim_Woods.md) populates the area, it houses some minor Nordic ruins and it had been targeted by the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md). The [Northwind Mine](Lore_Northwind_Mine.md) is situated on the mountains north of the Stony Basin and it has been a site of a successful mineshaft and a Word Wall.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SB-1)</sup>
