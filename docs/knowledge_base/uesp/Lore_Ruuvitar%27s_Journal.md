Memorandum on Mnemic Acquisition **by [Ruuvitar](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ruuvitar)** *Experimental notes on Hist sap*
---
Initial experiments uncovered a formula to duplicate the mnemic phenomenon. We've yet to replicate the processes utilized by a "Tree-Minder," yet our solution is both elegant and thorough.

The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) sap proves to be an invaluable by-product. I hope to bring many specimens back to the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) to further research possible uses.

Most [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) subjects prove uncooperative, but we've had some success using the extractions from the Hist as a crude form of coercive.

This warrants a full investigation, one which I will personally oversee.

— Alchemist [Ruuvitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ruuvitar)
