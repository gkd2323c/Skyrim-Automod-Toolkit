*"Yet another mortal stands before the Prince of Mathness. Do you believe yourself versed in matters mathematical? Step forward and face me!"*
—Zyrcifel, the "Daedric Prince of Mathness"<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zyrcifel-1)</sup> Zyrcifel, the self-proclaimed **[Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Mathness**, is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) servant of Lord [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), the Prince of Madness. He appears on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during failed summoning rituals, when Sheogorath's title is mispronounced as "mathness" instead of Madness. Zyrcifel is evidentially no true Daedric Prince; instead, he serves to torment mortals with endless mathematical questions. The only known method to free oneself of this curse is by sending Zyrcifel back to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) via ritual banishment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cintuudaer-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zyrcifel-1)</sup>

Zyrcifel appears dressed as a jester dressed in black and gold. He wears the Mask of Cheerful Slaughter, which displays the three faces of madness.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/ON-npc-Zyrcifel_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Zyrcifel_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Zyrcifel_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Zyrcifel_02.jpg) Banishment of Zyrcifel Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a mage named Cintuudaer from the [Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) College of Rituals bit his tongue while attempting to summon Sheogorath. As a result, he accidentally summoned Zyrcifel to the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md). Cintuudaer attempted to flee, but Zyrcifel followed him across the island for days, tormenting him with mathematical questions. Eventually, Cintuudaer managed to banish him with help from [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cintuudaer-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zyrcifel-1)</sup>
