# Blacksmith


[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-npc-Alvor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alvor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvor.jpg) A blacksmith **Blacksmiths** are various [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) scattered throughout Skyrim. They are often found at or near smithing equipment, and sell a wide variety of [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md), [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) and smithing equipment, including [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md), [ores, and ingots](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md).

## Notes
- - [Garakh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garakh) at [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) is a member of the [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith_(class).md) class, but does not function as a merchant due to an apparent [bug](#Bugs). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. Once fixed, she is affected by [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md), but not [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md).
- [Heimvar](Skyrim_Heimvar.md) is supposed to replace Beirand if something happens to him, but will not due to a [bug](#Bugs). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
