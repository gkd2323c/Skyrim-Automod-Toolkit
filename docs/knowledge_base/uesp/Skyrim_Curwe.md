[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-npc-Curwe.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Curwe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Curwe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Curwe.jpg) Curwe outside Loreius Farm **Curwe** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) and the wife of [Vantus Loreius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius). She lives with him on [their farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md). If you alert the guards to Cicero's activities during [Delayed Burial](Skyrim_Delayed_Burial.md), then Cicero will kill Curwe and her husband.

She wakes up every morning at 7am and starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. At 8am she heads outside and starts working the land, at times sweeping the porch. At 7pm she takes a one-hour dinner before she goes to bed early at 8pm.

Curwe wears a set of green farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Loreius_Famhouse) to the farmhouse, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

After betraying [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) during [Delayed Burial](Skyrim_Delayed_Burial.md), the corpse in Loreius Farm uses a different form, the refid is 0007f42c, the baseid is 0007f42a.

## Related Quests
- **[Delayed Burial](Skyrim_Delayed_Burial.md)**: Convince an unwilling [farmer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius) to help fix a stranded [jester's](Skyrim_Cicero.md) broken wagon wheel.

## Dialogue
Attempting to speak to her will result in only one response:

- *"You need something talk to my husband. Vantus Loreius. Or just Loreius, if it's all the same to you."*

## Conversations
While she doesn't talk much to strangers, she will often debate with her husband:

- **Curwe**: *"You know, I was thinking of expanding the herb garden. Make room for some new plants I'm planning."*
- **Vantus**: *"That should be all right. How much space were you planning?"*
- **Curwe**: *"Oh, half the field should do."*
- **Vantus**: *"Half the field? What could need half the field?"*
- **Curwe**: *"I just think pollinating would be easier if I could do a lot of plants at once."*
- **Vantus**: *"Uh... why don't we talk about this later?"*

- **Curwe**: *"I had a look at that boneyard the other day, Vantus. The one to the Northwest."*
- **Vantus**: *"I told you I don't like you going there. I've seen scavengers picking at the mammoths. They could be dangerous."*
