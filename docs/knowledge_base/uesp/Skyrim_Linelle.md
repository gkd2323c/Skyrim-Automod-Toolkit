[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-creature-Linelle.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Linelle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Linelle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Linelle.jpg) Linelle Linelle is a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) who is encountered during the quest [Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md).

She leads the forces of [Crowstooth](Skyrim_Crowstooth.md) in the latter's absence. [Linelle's note](Skyrim_Linelle%27s_Note.md) gives clues as to where Crowstooth herself has gone; reading it will direct you to [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md).
