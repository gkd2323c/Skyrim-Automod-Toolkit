### Alchemist's Camp: Evergreen Woods
[![SR-place-Alchemist Camp Evergreen Woods.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods.jpg/200px-SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods.jpg) [![SR-place-Alchemist Camp Evergreen Woods 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Alchemist_Camp_Evergreen_Woods_02.jpg) - POIPine Forest06

Located just north and above of [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md), this camp belonged to the dead alchemist that can be found at the Grove.

It is complete with an old, extinguished campfire with a metal pot hanging over it containing an [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). Beside the fire is a stump with a copy of the alchemy skill book *[De Rerum Dirennis](Skyrim_De_Rerum_Dirennis.md)* on top, and a small tent complete with bedroll. To the left of the tent are a crate and two [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks, with an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) and the [alchemist's journal](Skyrim_Alchemist%27s_Journal_(camp).md) on top. The journal refers to where the alchemist has camped as a "mushroom grove" and you'll find several patches of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) around the trees behind his tent.

There is also another nirnroot shortly upstream from the camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-50719&y=-62713&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Bandit Camp: Ilinalta Foothills
[![SR-place-Bandit Camp Ilinalta Foothills.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Ilinalta_Foothills.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Ilinalta_Foothills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Ilinalta_Foothills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Ilinalta_Foothills.jpg) - POIPine Forest20

South of [The Guardian Stones](Skyrim_The_Guardian_Stones.md) there is a camp inhabited by three bandits. The camp has three tents with a bedroll each, one [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) and a tanning rack. One of the bandits carries *[Treasure Map I](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_I.md)*. There is a coin purse and a satchel resting on a tree stump. A copy of the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill book *[Night Falls on Sentinel](Skyrim_Night_Falls_on_Sentinel.md)* can be found in one of the tents. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=3195&y=-66333&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Bandit Camp: Helgen Cliffs
[![SR-place-Bandit Camp Helgen Cliffs.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Helgen_Cliffs.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Helgen_Cliffs.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Helgen_Cliffs.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Camp_Helgen_Cliffs.jpg) - POIPine Forest28

West of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) is a camp consisting of a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), a chest, a potion of healing, a pair of hide boots, a long bow, three bedrolls and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill book *[The Refugees](Skyrim_The_Refugees.md)*. There is a [beehive](Skyrim_Beehive.md) in a tree north of the campfire, and there are three bear traps hidden in the grass. If you try to lockpick the chest, two archers and one melee bandit will appear from the west, even if you did not open the chest. Further attempts at lockpicking won't spawn additional bandits, but more bandits will spawn with later visits if the site has had time to reset. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=4240&y=-78883&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Bandit Bridge: Pinewatch
[![SR-place-Bandit Bridge Pinewatch.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-place-Bandit_Bridge_Pinewatch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bandit_Bridge_Pinewatch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Bridge_Pinewatch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit_Bridge_Pinewatch.jpg) - POIPine Forest17

Southwest of [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md). It consists of two wooden towers that straddle the [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) - [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) road, linked by a rope bridge. A bandit archer keeps watch atop each tower. Approaching the bridge activates a [boulder trap](Skyrim_Falling_Boulders.md) attached to the structure. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-13280&y=-80576&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Bandit Camp: Pinewatch Heights

Next to the tent lies a dead [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf_(dead).md) on a fallen tree, as well as an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). On subsequent visits the skill book may not be there, and the skill book or the dead elf might not be present on early visits if certain conditions have not yet been met. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-6428&y=-85559&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Riverwood Folly
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-place-Bleak_Falls_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bleak_Falls_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bleak_Falls_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bleak_Falls_Tower.jpg) Bleak Falls Tower - Bleak Falls Tower

**Riverwood Folly** or **Bleak Falls Tower** is a bandit-occupied Nordic tower located east of [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md), along the path between it and [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md). It is occupied by three leveled bandits; the first one lounges against a tree next to the path, the second paces back and forth across a bridge and through the entry door, and the third waits near the top of the tower. All three will only turn hostile if you approach the tower too closely. The tower contains only a few valuables: a table on the first level holds a [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), four barrels near the top contain foodstuffs and a few [salt piles](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md), while an unlocked chest at the very top contains leveled loot. Two [iron ore veins](Skyrim_Iron.md) can be found just south of the tower down a steep path. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=7203.19&y=-42702.8&zoom=17&legacy=true)
