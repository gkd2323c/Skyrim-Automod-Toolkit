Thalmor Diplomatic Corps Notice *A Thalmor guide on etiquette when interacting with Khajiiti and Wood Elf allies*
---

- Regarding our friends, the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md):
- Do NOT refer to them as "Cats."
- Do NOT serve them food intended for pet [cats](Lore_Cat.md).
- If you own a pet cat, do NOT call Khajiiti by your cat's name.
- Do NOT attempt to grab their tail—unless permission is granted.

- Regarding our friends, the [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md):
- Do NOT ask the Wood Elves about adding vegetables to their recipes.
- Do NOT cut down a tree in front of a Wood Elf.
- Do NOT ask how Wood Elf flesh tastes.
- Do NOT ask how to cook a Khajiit.

Our newfound friends in the [Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) stand beside you as comrades in arms and they deserve a measure of respect. Please speak to your local [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) representative if further clarification is required.
