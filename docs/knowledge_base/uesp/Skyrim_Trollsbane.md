[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-item-Trollsbane.jpg/200px-SR-item-Trollsbane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trollsbane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Trollsbane.jpg) Trollsbane Trollsbane is a [steel warhammer](Skyrim_Steel_Warhammer.md) and the signature weapon of [Frofnir Trollsbane](Skyrim_Frofnir_Trollsbane.md) before he was slain inside a [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md)'s lair between [Cradle Stone Tower](Skyrim_Cradle_Stone_Tower.md) and [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md). Its enchantment has the unique property to do fifteen points of [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) to any troll it hits, although it will have no effect against any other kind of creature. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Trollsbane requires a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the improvement from the tempering being doubled if the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk is also unlocked.
