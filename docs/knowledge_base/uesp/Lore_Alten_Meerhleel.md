[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-place-Alten_Meerhleel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Alten_Meerhleel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alten_Meerhleel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alten_Meerhleel.jpg) Alten Meerhleel ca. 2E 582 Alten Meerhleel (also called **Alten Meirhall**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a port settlement built on the lowlands of [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), in the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). The port was built as a way for the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tribes to connect with the other races and as a way to appease them, the original harbormaster built a [Teeba-Enoo](Lore_Teeba-Enoo.md) court north of the docks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAhkur-2)</sup>

## History
Alten Meerhleel was built by a Murkmire Argonian named Ahkur in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), who felt that the tribes should work in tandem with the outside races and decided that a port like Alten Meerhleel would help. At first, the tribes did not accept it and believed that it was too close to the swamps. So as a way to appease them, Harbormaster Ahkur built a Teeba-Enoo court, which keep the tribes happy and their warriors at bay. Ahkur hoped that Alten Meerhleel would evolve to the likes of Lilmoth<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAhkur-2)</sup> and despite its small stature, the port saw quite a few shipments and travelers from Lilmoth, and the Teeba-Enoo court became a popular attraction for the local tribes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONRienallah-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMinalz-4)</sup>

When the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) mapped out settlements across the province, they localized some names and henceforth, Alten Meerhleel was known as Alten Meirhall on official maps.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLive SF-5)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Alten Meirhall was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lady Judiana and had a rivalry with Longmont.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Alten Meirhall is neighbored by several settlements, including [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) to the southeast, Longmont to the far west-northwest, and Moonmarch to the far west.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARMap-6)</sup>
