[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-place-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg) Gromm's Pass **Gromm's Pass** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) located in [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), south of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md). Until a recent collapse, it served as an underground pass through the [Jerall Mountains](Skyrim_Jerall_Mountains.md) to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

It consists of one zone: [Gromm's Pass](#Gromm.27s_Pass).

## Related Quests
- **[Blue in the Face](Skyrim_Blue_in_the_Face.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Goblins.md)</sup>: Investigate rumors of the Blue God's return.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The cave entrance is marked by a lit brazier among the bones and blood. A [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) spawns right where the fast travel marker is, so be prepared for an ambush if you aren't approaching on foot. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) can be found to the right of the entrance.

### Gromm's Pass
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass.jpg) Gromm's Pass interior [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gromm%27s_Pass_02.jpg) Gogh next to the throne Not far from the entrance are two goblins guarding, on ground are seven dead skeletons. Head north and see the dead [Avanessa Calladius](Skyrim_Avanessa_Calladius.md), nearby are six dead skeletons. Head west there's another dead skeleton on ground, and a goblin guarding the north west corridor. Head west and find a large room with a pit at center, in the pit [Gogh](Skyrim_Gogh.md) is fighting against three goblins. West of the pit are two kneeled goblins worshipping a leader named [Blue God](Skyrim_Blue_God.md), the Blue God carries a unique staff [Goblin Totem Staff](Skyrim_Staves.md#Goblin_Totem_Staff)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Goblins.md)</sup>. There're several dead corpses in the large room and in the pit. Head west is a small room containing a boss chest. Head north and walk into a small room stocked plenty of [Steel-Blue Entolomas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel-Blue_Entoloma). North of the large room mentioned before is a corridor, north of the corridor is a small mining room, a goblin is face away to the player and mining. South of the corridor leads to a way blocked by spears and a chain, pull the chain to lower the spears and reach to pit in the large room mentioned before, [Gogh](Skyrim_Gogh.md) is in the pit and can be talked with to release him as a fighting type pet.

## Notes
- This cave is the only place where [Steel-Blue Entoloma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel-Blue_Entoloma) grow naturally.
- If [Gogh](Skyrim_Gogh.md) leaves your service as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), he will return to the throne room here.
- The name of the cave may be a reference to Gromm who is the manservant of Baenlin in Bruma.
