## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tumble_Arch_Pass_02.jpg) Tumble Arch Pass One [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) and one [mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) occupy the camp, which features the painted stones and mammoth ribs and skulls typical of giant camps. The camp has a large campfire and a pile of firewood with a dead [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) beside it. A [mammoth cheese bowl](Skyrim_Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl.md) is beside the fire, with three more to the west. Southeast of the fire against some rocks is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). Above the camp on the rocks to the south is a large stone bowl containing three sprigs of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) and a [giant club](Skyrim_Giant_Club.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes
- Up the mountain southeast of the camp is a skeleton on the ground with a [shrine of Julianos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Julianos) almost covered by snow and a [quicksilver ore vein](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Ore_Vein.md) nearby. This area is easier to reach from [Shearpoint](Skyrim_Shearpoint.md) to the south. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=70163&y=26768)
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces all [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) pieces here with the snow-covered version of the same plant.

## Bugs
- The giant club cannot be used by you, but can be picked up by a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) if you order them to do so. However, it will then be impossible to remove it from their inventory. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
