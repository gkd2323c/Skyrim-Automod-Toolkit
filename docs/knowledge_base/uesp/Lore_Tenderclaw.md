Tenderclaw *Notes on an ancient Khajiiti esraj*
---
One of the oldest [esraj](Lore_Esraj.md) known to exist, Tenderclaw cemented [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md)'s reputation as a destination for bards from across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). For years the renowned [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) skald Ahn-eks'kha played it during her week-long performances of the epic poem, The Death of [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md).

No musician since has achieved the perfect, enchanting sounds that Ahn-eks'kha coaxed from the instrument. Legend says Tenderclaw was gift from the [Khajiiti gods](Lore_Khajiit.md#Khajiiti_Pantheon). Whether she was chosen or not is something Ahn-eks'kha would never clarify, preferring the allure of mystery.
