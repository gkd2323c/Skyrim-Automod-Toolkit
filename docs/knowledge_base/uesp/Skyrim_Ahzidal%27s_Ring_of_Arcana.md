[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Gold Diamond Ring.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Gold Diamond Ring.png) Ahzidal's Ring of Arcana Ahzidal's Ring of Arcana is a ring that is part of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). It can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after the third phase of digging is completed during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). The ring's appearance is identical to a [gold diamond ring](Skyrim_Gold_Diamond_Ring.md) and also has the same weight. Its enchantment allows you to cast [Ignite](Skyrim_Ignite.md) and [Freeze](Skyrim_Freeze.md). In addition, if you wear this ring along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points.

## Notes
- When equipped by a follower, it will allow them to use the added spells even if they are not normally spellcasters. (Removing the ring from the follower's inventory does not remove the spell, you must use the console to remove the spell if you want)
- The ring may not be on its plinth when you get to it— [shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) from the draugr in the adjacent chamber can knock it into the pit of water. If this occurs, equipping the [Telekinesis](Skyrim_Telekinesis.md) spell can help you locate it (due to its longer range compared to the standard cursor).
