*We do not need to be schooled by a puny manling. For all their refusal to hew to rigors of lawful conduct, the Fire Daedra belong here. You do not. We shall expunge you.*
—A Frost Daedra<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Frost Daedra, also known as **Ice Daedra**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-3)</sup> are a variety of horned humanoid [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md), considered among the most dangerous creatures in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFD-4)</sup> They are intelligent and possess some spell casting abilities, they can only be harmed by [mithril](Lore_Mithril.md) or better weapons and are greatly resistant to cold-based spells.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> They are speakers of both the Daedric [language](Lore_Linguistics.md) and [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md), as such they are sometimes capable of being negotiated with.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDaedric-6)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup>

They apparently have an enmity for [Fire Daedra](Lore_Fire_Daedra.md), though they are able to put this aside when serving mutual masters. Frost Daedra are considered to take things more seriously than Fire Daedra, who claim to "understand the pleasures of the dance".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFire-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup> They are emissaries and assassins of the [Daedra Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) and are as cold in their murders as the infernal mist that surrounds them.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-3)</sup> Many Frost Daedra were known to serve as retainers of [Xivilai Moath](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md)'s [clan](Lore_Xivilai%27s_Clan.md), in service to [Clan Dagon](Lore_Clan_Dagon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup>

## History
The Frost Daedra of Xivilai's Clan served alongside the [Shardai](Lore_Shardai_Clan.md) and [Dremora Clan](Lore_Dremora_Clan.md) during [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s occupation of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md). On [Faydra](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md)'s orders, the Frost Daedra helped to invade the [Shade Perilous](Lore_Shade_Perilous.md) along with their hated enemies, the Fire Daedra. To prevent infighting, Moath made a pact with Faydra, forcing them to work together.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFire-7)</sup> This relationship did not hold, as an [apprentice](Lore_Hero.md) from the Battlespire, following the retreating Daedric armies, brought the Elemental Daedra into conflict over control of the realm.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSL4-8)</sup>

As a result of the apprentice's actions, the invasion of Shade Perilous failed, with the Frost Daedra receiving the brunt of the blame. As a result the Frost Daedra received lesser standing with Mehrunes Dagon, and were made to serve as the Lesser Dogs of the [Great Hunt](Lore_Great_Hunt.md) hosted at the [Chimera of Desolation](Lore_Chimera_of_Desolation.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BSFire-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSFrost-1)</sup> Though they failed to stop the apprentice there as well.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BSL5-9)</sup>

## Notes
- The book, *[The Art of War Magic](Lore_The_Art_of_War_Magic.md)* mentions an old story about a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) sorcerer who summoned an Ice Demon to scare his fellow soldiers into attacking the Nords.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAOWM-10)</sup> Whether the Ice Demon is specifically a Frost Daedra is unclear.
- Frost Daedra exist in *Daggerfall* alongside [Ice Atronachs](Lore_Ice_Atronach.md), which are typically [Golems](Lore_Golem.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-HFAQUEST-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AHing-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ATRMAN-13)</sup> but some quests treat them as Daedra comparable to the [Frost Atronachs](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md) present in later games.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ATRQUEST-14)</sup> The Daedric Frost Atronachs have also sometimes been referred to as Frost Daedra.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-15)</sup>
