**Angler Larvae** is an ingredient added by the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

It is a common small fish that can be caught by [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md) in freezing waters like the [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) fishing location near the boat. Multiple are guaranteed to spawn in Pilgrim's Trench on the ocean floor.
