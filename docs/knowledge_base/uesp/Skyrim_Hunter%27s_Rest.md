## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-place-Hunter%27s_Rest_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hunter%27s_Rest_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Rest_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Rest_02.jpg) Hunters resting This cozy one-room shelter in the hills south of [Moss Mother Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern) provides a welcome respite for weary adventurers. Outside, you'll find a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) hanging over a campfire. Several joints of [raw venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison) owned by the hunters are outside and inside the shack. An elk carcass on a table against the west wall of the shack can be freely harvested for its [hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md), meat, and possibly [small](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers) or [large antlers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Large_Antlers). Next to the table is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) is on the east side of the house. A [beehive](Skyrim_Bee.md) hangs from a flagpole east of the wood chopping block, and can be harvested freely for [bees](Skyrim_Bee.md), [beehive husks](Skyrim_Beehive_Husk.md), and [honeycomb](Skyrim_Honeycomb.md). A [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch) plant can be found just down the hill from the shack.

Inside the house, a [taproot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot) is behind the keg on the table. To the right of the table along the north wall is a row of three empty weapon racks. Two owned bedrolls lie on the floor to the south, with a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Father Of The Niben](Skyrim_Father_Of_The_Niben.md)* between them. An unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) against the east wall contains minor random loot, and beside the chest is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel. Above the chest is a weapon plaque mounted on the wall.

## Notes
- The two hunters here will often be asleep, making them excellent targets to improve your [Pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill and to feed on if you're a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md).

## Bugs
- A bottle of [frostbite venom](Skyrim_Frostbite_Venom.md) and a bowl of [glow dust](Skyrim_Glow_Dust.md) can be found underneath the table. The frostbite venom is in the gap between the table and the floorboard, and the glow dust is embedded into one of the table supports. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2, addresses this issue. Both items have been repositioned. They were also missing their ownership settings.
