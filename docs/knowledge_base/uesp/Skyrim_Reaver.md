# Reaver


## Reaver Types
While the stronger reaver types will not appear until after you've reached the levels shown, you will continue to meet low-level versions even at high levels. Boss-level reavers will always be at the highest possible level, and in some specific locations are identified as "Reaver Lord". Unlike the bandits found in Skyrim, all reavers you meet in Solstheim are [Dark Elves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Elves).

### One-Handed Melee Fighters
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder.jpg) A Reaver Marauder ready for battle These reavers are members of the [Bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) [NPC](Skyrim_NPCs.md) class and wield one-handed melee weapons and shields in battle. They carry one of the following weapons, depending on your level:

- **Mace:** [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace), [Orcish mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Mace), [Dwarven mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Mace), [Elven mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Mace) or [DB:Nordic Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Mace)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **Sword:** [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), [Orcish sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Sword), [Dwarven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Sword), [Elven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword) or [Nordic sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Sword)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **War Axe:** [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe), [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe), [Orcish war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe), [Dwarven war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_War_Axe), [Elven war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_War_Axe) or [Nordic war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_War_Axe)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

In addition to the weapons listed above, melee reavers are equipped with either light or heavy armor, the type of which depends on your level:

- **Cuirass:** [bonemold armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Armor), [chitin armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Armor), [chitin heavy armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Heavy_Armor), [fur armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor), [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor), [studded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor), [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md), or [scaled horn armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor)
- **Boots:** [bonemold boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Boots), [chitin boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Boots), [chitin heavy boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Heavy_Boots), [fur shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes), [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots), [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots), [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots)
- **Gauntlets:** 50% chance of [bonemold gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Gauntlets), [chitin bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Bracers), [chitin heavy gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Heavy_Gauntlets), [iron gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets), [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets), [steel Imperial gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Imperial_Gauntlets), or [steel plate gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets)
- **Helmet:** 50% chance of [bonemold helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Helmet), [chitin helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Helmet), [chitin heavy helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Heavy_Helmet),
- **Shield:** 20% chance of [bonemold shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Shield), [chitin shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Shield), [hide shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Shield), [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md), or [elven shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Shield)

Three reavers from each of the reaver types below (indicated with a <sup>[1]</sup> note) wear a different outfit, consisting of the following light armor pieces:

- **Cuirass:** fur armor, hide armor, studded armor, leather armor, scaled armor, or scaled horn armor
- **Boots:** fur shoes, hide boots, leather boots, or scaled boots
- **Gauntlets:** 50% chance of fur bracers, hide bracers, leather bracers, or scaled bracers
- **Shield:** 20% chance of a hide shield, iron shield, or elven shield

Reavers also have a small chance to carry a random assortment of items, which include [beverages](Skyrim_Food.md#Beverages), [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), [food](Skyrim_Food.md), [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), tools (a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), [axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), or [shovel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel)), [ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ingots), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). Reaver Plunderers and Marauders carry more gold and may carry additional lockpicks. The reavers marked with <sup>[1]</sup> below are more likely to have these miscellaneous items, since the [leveled list](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists) containing these items is listed twice in their inventories.

### Mages
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_%28Mage%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_%28Mage%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_(Mage).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_(Mage).jpg.md) A reaver mage wielding two powerful staves Reaver mages are members of the [Bandit Wizard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard) class and rely primarily on spellcasting in battle. They wear the following leveled armor:

- **Cuirass:** fur armor
- **Boots:** fur shoes, hide boots, leather boots, or scaled boots

Reaver and Reaver Outlaw mages carry an iron dagger, while Reaver Thug mages carry a steel dagger. Reaver Highwayman, Reaver Plunderer, and Reaver Marauder mages carry a leveled dagger (iron, steel, orcish, dwarven, or elven).

In addition to their gear, reaver mages carry a random assortment of items, including lockpicks, food, jewelry, [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), [books](Skyrim_Books.md), [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), a leveled [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) or [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md), and a small amount of gold. Reaver Plunderers and Marauders carry more gold and have a chance to carry more lockpicks than lower-level mages. On death, reaver mages can be looted for random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), and potions of [restore magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Magicka).

### Reaver Bosses
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_%28Boss%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_%28Boss%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_(Boss).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Reaver_Marauder_(Boss).jpg.md) A Reaver Marauder preparing a spell Boss-level reavers have both spells and weapons at their disposal, and are members of the [Bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:DLC2Enc Class Bandit Boss)<sup>([DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md))</sup> class. They wield one of the following one-handed weapons:

- **Mace:** steel mace, enchanted iron mace, orcish mace, enchanted steel mace, enchanted orcish mace, dwarven mace, enchanted dwarven mace, elven mace, enchanted elven mace, [glass mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Mace), enchanted glass mace, or [ebony mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Mace)
- **Sword:** steel sword, enchanted iron sword, orcish sword, enchanted steel sword, enchanted orcish sword, dwarven sword, enchanted dwarven sword, elven sword, enchanted elven sword, [glass sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Sword), enchanted glass sword, or [ebony sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Sword)
- **War Axe:** steel war axe, enchanted iron war axe, orcish war axe, enchanted steel war axe, enchanted orcish war axe, dwarven war axe, enchanted dwarven war axe, elven war axe, enchanted elven war axe, [glass war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_War_Axe), enchanted glass war axe, or [ebony war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_War_Axe)

In addition to their main weapons, reaver bosses also carry daggers: the Reaver Outlaw and Reaver Thug carry a steel dagger, the Reaver Highwayman carries an orcish dagger, and the Reaver Plunderer and Reaver Marauder both carry an elven dagger.

- **Cuirass:** bonemold armor(Level 1-17), chitin heavy armor (Level 18-24), [Nordic carved armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Carved_Armor) (Level 25+)
- **Boots:** bonemold boots (Level 1-17), chitin heavy boots (Level 18-24), [Nordic carved boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Carved_Boots) (Level 25+)
- **Gauntlets:** bonemold gauntlets (Level 1-17), chitin heavy gauntlets (Level 18-24), [Nordic carved gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Carved_Gauntlets) (Level 25+)
- **Helmet:** bonemold helmet (Level 1-17), chitin heavy helmet (Level 18-24), [Nordic carved helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Carved_Helmet) (Level 25+)

Boss-level reavers have a small chance to carry a random assortment of items, which include beverages, lockpicks, food, jewelry, gems, tools (a pickaxe, axe, or shovel), ingots, and a small amount of gold. Reaver bosses are more likely to have these miscellaneous items than most generic reavers, since the leveled list containing these items is listed twice in their inventories.

On death, reaver bosses can be looted for random potions, potions of restore health, and potions of restore magicka.

## Notes
- Reaver [melee fighters](#One-Handed_Melee_Fighters) and [archers](#Archers) are generally twice as likely to be male than female. In most cases, the leveled lists that spawn these reavers list each male reaver twice and each female reaver once.
- According to [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) data, Reavers were slated to also include [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) in their ranks at one point.
- [Reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) Reavers may still use their idling dialogue when following you out of combat, rather than the usual grunts of reanimated mainland NPCs.
- Reavers tend to carry doubles of certain loot (e.g., 2 Silver Rings, 2 Ales, etc.), unlike the Bandits of mainland Skyrim, which only carry one of each.
