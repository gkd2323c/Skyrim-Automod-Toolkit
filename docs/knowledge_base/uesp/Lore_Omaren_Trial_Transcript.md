The Final Words of Nilvyn Omaren **by [Nilvyn Omaren](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Nilvyn_Omaren)** *A warning from a condemned man*
---
I have, may it please the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), a few words to say before you render sentence.

First, as in all things, I praise the Tribunal. It is with a pious heart that I stand before the Three. Many here assembled have accused me of heresy, treason, and murder. But I tell you now, everything I have done—everything I have ever done—I have done for the greater glory of the Tribunal.

With respect, I must point out the hypocrisy which sends me to my death. Had I taken up the sword in defense of the rich, or the Temple, or my [House](Lore_House_Redoran.md), the sacrifices I have made would be rightly celebrated. My neck would be draped in [dragontongue](Lore_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md) and ribbon, not left bare, awaiting the axe. But because I acted on behalf of the bound and wretched, I am damned.

The scourge of [slavery](Lore_Slavery.md) is the defining sin of our people. While the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) prize their swords and the [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) prize their trees, we prize our blood-property. We built our [Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) on the backs of a proud and mysterious people whose lives we destroy with reckless abandon. We think ourselves better than them because of our true faith and our smooth skin. But I tell you this: there is more goodness in the heart of one [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) laborer than in all the hearts of all the landed oathmen of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) combined.

Our Lady of Mercy, Blessed [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), teaches us compassion and charity, but are we not like the gluttonous [netches](Lore_Netch.md) that kill out of greed? Are we not like the [child-Seht](Lore_Homilies_of_Blessed_Almalexia.md#Sotha_Sil_and_the_Scribs) who makes sport of suffering?

The esteemed Inquisitor asked me this under oath: "Are you not ashamed of what you have done?" I remained silent at the time, but now I say it freely: I feel no shame at all. I have no regrets. While I lament the death of those overseers, I tell you it was all for the good. One day soon there will come a reckoning. All our manifest sins will swell and consume us. We will deserve it.

Kill me if you wish. If I must forfeit my life in furtherance of justice, I say "let it be done!" But know this: you kill me in vain. Others will rise to take my place. The sons and daughters of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) will be free. And unless you change course, the children of the Tribunal will pay a great price in blood.

Now I am done. Do what you will.
