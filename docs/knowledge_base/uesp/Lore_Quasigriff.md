Quasigriffs are large, feathered, wingless quadrupeds that are related to [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md). They were created through a combination of wizardry and selective breeding. According to their creator, [Irastimil Direnni](Lore_Irastimil_Direnni.md), their wingless frame kept their upkeep costs low.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QG-1)</sup> Although they were created artificially, various breeds of Quasigriffs can be found in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BQ-2)</sup> [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AQ-3)</sup> [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SQ-4)</sup> and even in the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DQ-5)</sup>

Despite Irastimil's aim to create mounts that could maintain superiority over the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) steeds,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QG-1)</sup> a breed of quasigriffs was adopted by the Breton nobility themselves. Colorful Baysong Quasigriffs, with vibrant sea-tinted feathers, found their home in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md). Their creation was accidental, as they were never intended to be bred by the Direnni.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BQ-2)</sup>

Another, likely unintended variant of the quasigriffs, known as Dagonic Quasigriffs were a product of the daedric corruption of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md). They possessed dark plumage, sharpened claws, and were known to be prone to rage.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DQ-5)</sup>

Despite being artificial creations, some breeds of Quasigriffs are considered sacred by the faithful of [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md). Aurielic Quasigriffs, who typically possess golden plumage, are customarily taken from the nest almost as soon as they break through the shells of their eggs. Inhabitants of [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md) often pamper, preen and provide for these quasigriffs. Their fallen feathers are used as fletching by local archers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AQ-3)</sup>

The corruption of the Spellscar altered some Quasigriffs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SQ-4)</sup>
