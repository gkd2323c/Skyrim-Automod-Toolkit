[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/SR-npc-Jorgen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jorgen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jorgen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jorgen.jpg) Jorgen chopping wood **Jorgen** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) who runs the mill in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). He doesn't care about either side in the war, he just wants to get his work done. Not far from the mill is the [house](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md) he owns with his wife [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md). Jorgen wakes up at 6am and immediately starts working at the lumber mill. He will first chop wood for four hours, after which he will spend the next three transporting the cut pieces. After this, he will resume his cutting for three more hours, after which he might choose to do either until he heads back home to sleep at midnight.

Jorgen is also the bearer of the hilt of the fabled [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor), which has been in the possession of his family, Clan Axe-Bearer, for eight generations.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5), and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209aa). He is equipped with an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House) to his house, a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen%27s_Chest) to his chest, a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

He also owns a bed inside the [Moorside Inn](Skyrim_Moorside_Inn.md) which you can sleep in if you befriend him.

## Quest-Related Events

### Laid to Rest
Jorgen will be called upon to join the mob of townsfolk heading for [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md).

**Thonnir**: *"I'm going to Movarth's Lair to kill that monster. Are you with me?"*
**Lami**: *"Yes!"* **Jorgen**: *"Yeah!"* **[Lurbuk](Skyrim_Lurbuk.md)**: *"Take us!"* **[Benor](Skyrim_Benor.md)**: *"Yeah!"* **Thonnir**: *"Kill the vampire!"* **Benor**: *"Kill him!"* **Jorgen**: *"Let's kill him!"* **Lurbuk**: *"Destroy him!"* **Lami**: *"Kill him!"* **Thonnir**: *"Take us to Movarth's Lair!"* **(to you)** [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg) The mob outside [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md) However, once he arrives at the destination, he'll be quick to re-evaluate his situation.

**Lami**: *"Um...this place looks dangerous."* **Jorgen**: *"Yeah. Kind of scary too."* **Benor**: *"And it's full of vampires?"* **Thonnir**: *"Cowards! We must kill the vampires. We have to make them pay!"* **Lurbuk**: *"Of course. But why not let him/her go in first?"* **Thonnir**: *"Morthal is full of cowards! Let's go kill that monster."* **(to you)** He and the others will then retreat back to town, leaving you and Thonnir to face the vampire treat alone.

### Pieces of the Past
Jorgen's family was apparently one of the recipients of one of the pieces of Mehrunes' Razor. In his case, it's the hilt.

**I'm looking for pieces of Mehrunes' Razor.** *"Don't know what you're talking about, stranger."* **Really? Silus Vesuius says otherwise.** *"I've heard of him. My father had suspicions about his connection to the Mythic Dawn. Guess they were true. I don't need this. My family wasted 8 generations keeping that Razor safe from a dead cult. As far as I care, it can stay locked in my house."* You will then get some options to handle the situation:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| You won't miss it, then. **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: You want the hilt that bad? Fine. Here's the keys. It's locked in a chest inside my house. <br> **Failed**: I don't care, but my ancestors do. You can't have it. | **Passed**: Retrieve the Hilt <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| I'll pay for the hilt. **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: Divines know the mill could use some new blades. All right. It's yours. Here's the keys. It's locked in a chest inside my house. <br> **Failed**: Why don't you take your coin and keep walking? | **Passed**: Retrieve the Hilt <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| Give me the hilt, or things get ugly. **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: Easy. Here. These are the keys. The hilt is in a chest in my house, just don't hurt anyone. <br> **Failed**: You want a fight? You got it. | **Passed**: Retrieve the Hilt <br> **Failed**: Brawl |
| Never mind, then. | Best leave the past buried, I say. | Nothing |

If you were forced to brawl him, and then emerge victorious, he can be heard cursing:

*"Damn it. Why'd you have to walk into Morthal?"* Whatever your method may have been, if successful, he will give you the key to [his house](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md) west of the mill and another to the chest within it containing the hilt. You can also just take the hilt from the chest without speaking to Jorgen by picking the locks or pickpocketing the key(s) from Jorgen.

However, should you walk away without convincing Jorgen to give you the hilt, he will say this on subsequent meetings:

*"You're after that damn hilt? Go ahead, try and take it!"*
## Notes
- Jorgen also appears to be depicted on the card [Midnight Snack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Midnight Snack) in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*.
- Based on his Form ID, we can see that Jorgen wasn't created alongside the majority of Morthal's citizens, as they were all created in one batch with Jorgen (and [Lurbuk](Skyrim_Lurbuk.md)) being added at a later date. His wife, Lami, was part of this initial batch, and based on unused dialogue for her, it's implied they originally weren't married at all, with Jorgen's original wife having passed away, and Lami being a close friend.
