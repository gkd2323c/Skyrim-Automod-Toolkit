Varbarenth was [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md) of the [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) people. Not much is known about him, except that he was the son of Varbaril. The timing of his assumption of the Silvenar role and the identity of his [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md) counterpart is unknown. However, it is confirmed that he held the office as of [3E 80](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_80).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WQ1-1)</sup>
