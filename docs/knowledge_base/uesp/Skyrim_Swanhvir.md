[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Swanhvir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Swanhvir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Swanhvir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Swanhvir.jpg) Swanhvir in Rockwallow Mine **Swanhvir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) at [Rockwallow Mine](Skyrim_Rockwallow_Mine.md) in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md), the small mining settlement east of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md).

She sleeps in the camp near the mine entrance every night between midnight and 6am. Immediately after waking up, she heads into Rockwallow Mine and spends the next twelve hours working, relaxing, or eating. She emerges from the mine at 6pm and spends two hours dining with her fellow miners in the camp. At 8pm she starts working again, this time carrying lumber back and forth between the [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) and [Sorli's house](Skyrim_Sorli%27s_House.md) for two hours. The rest of the evening is spent strolling around until she climbs into her tent at midnight.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) for protection, and carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Longhouse.md) to the nonexistent longhouse, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Swanhvir seems to have a very bleak outlook on life.

- *"I'm just here to work the mine. Nothing else matters anymore."*

- *"The outside world means little to us, and we'd like it to stay that way."*

- *"When you work hard enough, really get into a rhythm, everything else just fades away."*

### Conversations
When inside the mine, she is under the watchful eye of [Gestur Rockbreaker](Skyrim_Gestur_Rockbreaker.md). Every once in a while, he will come over to her and briefly engage in conversation:

- **Gestur**: *"Let's make sure we're just as productive this month as we were last. If [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md)'s happy, we're happy."*
- **Swanhvir**: *"Yes sir, of course."*
- **Gestur**: *"All right. Keep up the good work."*
