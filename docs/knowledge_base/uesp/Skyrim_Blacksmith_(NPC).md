[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-npc-Blacksmith.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Blacksmith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blacksmith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blacksmith.jpg) Blacksmith The **Blacksmith** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) who can be found working the forge within [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) if you choose to side with the [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md) during the quest [Her Word Against Theirs](Skyrim_Her_Word_Against_Theirs.md). Before this quest, the forge will be dilapidated, but it will be repaired if you help recruit more members for the cult.

He offers a selection of blacksmithing goods, including several pieces of [Indoril armor](Skyrim_Indoril_Armor.md).
