Imperial Condolences **by Praefect Pius Bruccius**
---
[4E 175](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_175)

- [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) Citizen [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven),

It is with the deepest regret that I must inform you of the death of your husband Saeel.

He laid down his life in battle helping retake the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) from the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). He showed great courage in his final hour and his sacrifice will not be in vain. All considered him a man of high moral standing and a shining example of the best the Empire has to offer.

In this hour of your grief, please take solace in the fact that he gave up his life protecting the Empire which he held so dear.

Yours Sincerely, Imperial Praefect Pius Bruccius
