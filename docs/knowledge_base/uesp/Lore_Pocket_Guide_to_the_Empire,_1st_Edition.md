# Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition

Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition **by the [Imperial Geographical Society](Lore_Imperial_Geographical_Society.md) and '¥R'** *An illustrated guidebook detailing the provinces of the Empire, circa 2E 864*
---
**The Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition** was a paper bound booklet included in "The Elder Scrolls Adventures Volume R", the game manual for *[TES: Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard)*. The timeframe of the guide is contemporary to the game, which itself is set during the rule of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), the last decades of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).

**You can read about the Guide [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire:_1st_Edition).**

The following pages are transcripts of the guide. Formatting and capitalization are done according to the American printed version that came with the game. Underlined text represent footnotes and margin scribbles that appeared in the original guide. These were written by an Elf known only by the initials '[¥R](Lore_%C2%A5R.md)'. These footnotes can be viewed either by hovering the cursor over the underlined text, or by clicking the [nb] link at the end of the line. *Italicized text* in certain boxed sections in are also the notes of '¥R'.
