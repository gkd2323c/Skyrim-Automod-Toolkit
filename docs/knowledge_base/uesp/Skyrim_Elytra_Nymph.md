## Manic and Demented Elytra Nymph Pets
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-creature-Manic_Elytra_Nymph.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Manic_Elytra_Nymph.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Manic_Elytra_Nymph.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Manic_Elytra_Nymph.jpg) Manic Elytra Nymph pet [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-creature-Demented_Elytra_Nymph.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Demented_Elytra_Nymph.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Demented_Elytra_Nymph.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Demented_Elytra_Nymph.jpg) Demented Elytra Nymph pet Two Elytra Nymphs that you may obtain as pets will be encountered during the [Balance of Power](Skyrim_Balance_of_Power.md) quest, and there is one miscellaneous quest per pet. The [Manic Elytra Nymph](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Manic_Elytra_Nymph) can be found in the first [Saints Bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit) camp, while the [Demented Elytra Nymph](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Demented_Elytra_Nymph) can be found in the first [Seducers Bandit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seducers_Bandit) camp. If you have either one following you for long enough, you will get a notification that the Nymph has [Mystic Venom](Skyrim_Potions.md#Mystic_Venom) to harvest. The venom will replenish once every day for each nymph.
