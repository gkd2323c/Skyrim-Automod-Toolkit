[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Ingjard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ingjard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ingjard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ingjard.jpg) Ingjard **Ingjard** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) found in [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md). Like most other recruits, she is available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), and with [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, she is available as your [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md).

She arrives at Fort Dawnguard when you reach [stage 60](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md#Quest_Stages) of the quest [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md). She will then join the growing team of recruits hanging out in the western area and will spend her time wandering, relaxing, eating or using the various tools in the area. On every full hour, she may decide to train her [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) skills on the dummies in the northern cave for three hours. She sleeps on one of the cots between 8pm and 4am. During the [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md) quest, she will be in the crowd when [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md) holds his [speech](Skyrim_Isran.md#Kindred_Judgement), although she will not participate in the attack on [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).

She is clad in a set of Dawnguard equipment. This includes the [cuirass](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md) as well as matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Boots.md). She is equipped with a [Dawnguard warhammer](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Warhammer.md) and a [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) supplemented with fifteen [steel bolts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Bolt). She carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), and has a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

## Dialogue
When speaking to her, she will mention your accomplishments and offers to aid you:

- *"I hear you're doing a lot of the work in tracking down these vampires. If there's anything I can do to help, let me know."*

You can also ask her how she joined the Dawnguard:

- **How did you join the Dawnguard?**
- *"Something bad happens, word spreads. More bad things happen, people worry. The more they worry, the more they talk about it. I've been hearing rumors for a bit now. I was glad to find out not only the bad rumors were true."*

## Notes
- The Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch 3.0.1 changes it so that Ingjard participates in the attack on Castle Volkihar.
- Even though she is more proficient in Heavy Armor, she wears Light Armor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. Her armor is changed to the heavy versions of Dawnguard armor instead.
