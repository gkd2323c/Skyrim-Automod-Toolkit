[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-ingredient-Daedra_Heart.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Daedra_Heart.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Daedra_Heart.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Daedra_Heart.jpg) A Daedra heart **Daedra Hearts** are collected from slain [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md). In addition to their alchemical uses, they are also a key ingredient in making [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) armor and weapons, both at standard [forges](Skyrim_Forge.md) and at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md). These multiple uses, coupled with their rarity, make them extremely valuable items. Although Daedra hearts are rare, nearly all of their sources do respawn, meaning that obtaining sufficient Daedra hearts for creating Daedric gear may be difficult, but not impossible.

## Ingredients
Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 2 in [Nightcaller Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightcaller_Temple) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 1 in the [Hall of the Vigilant](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)) <sup>(Before level 10; see location page for details)</sup>
- 1 in [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 1 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))
- 1 in [Volkihar Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Ruins)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>
- 1 in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 2 guaranteed samples are sold by [Enthir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enthir) at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md).
- 38% chance of 1-6 samples being sold by [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md).
- 9% chance of 1-2 samples being sold by the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md).
- 1 can be obtained from [Balbus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Balbus) (either as a gift, pickpocketing him, or killing him).
- They can be created at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) from: 1 [Black Soul Gem](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) + 1 [Human Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Heart) (requires the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone)).
- Chests encountered in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> buildings and ruins have a high probability of containing Daedra hearts.

## Creatures
Daedra hearts are dropped by all varieties of [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md), i.e., Dremora Caitiffs, Churls, Kynreeves, Kynvals, Lords, and Markynaz, as well as named Dremora, namely [Velehk Sain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Velehk_Sain). Some places where Dremora can be encountered and killed include:

- The [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). During the quest [Pieces of the Past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past), four Dremora will appear around the shrine: two outside and two inside. After completing the quest, the Dremora in and around the shrine will continue to spawn every 10 days.
- During the quest [The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star), there are three Dremora.
- One is encountered after completing the [maze](Skyrim_Shalidor%27s_Maze.md) in [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md).

The [Reaper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reaper)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> and [Ebony Warrior](Skyrim_Ebony_Warrior.md) are each guaranteed to drop one sample.

## Notes
The key difference between [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) and [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) equipment is that the essence of a Daedric creature is bound into the equipment. Daedra hearts provide that essence, and therefore are critical for making all varieties of Daedric gear. Specifically:

- At [forges](Skyrim_Forge.md), if the [Daedric Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Smithing) perk has been unlocked, a Daedra heart can be combined with [ebony ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Ingot) and [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) to make: - All varieties of Daedric armor ([armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Boots), [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Gauntlets), [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Helmet), and [shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Shield)).
- All varieties of Daedric weapon ([battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Battleaxe), [bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Bow), [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Dagger), [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Greatsword), [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Mace), [sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Sword), [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_War_Axe), and [warhammer](Skyrim_Daedric_Warhammer.md)).
- At the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md), if upgraded using the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone), a Daedra heart can be combined with other items to make: - All varieties of Daedric armor.
- All varieties of Daedric weapon.
- A random enchanted Daedric armor.
- A random enchanted Daedric weapon.
- An [Unbound Dremora](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unbound_Dremora).
