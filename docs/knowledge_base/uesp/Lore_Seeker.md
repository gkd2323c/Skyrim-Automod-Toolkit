Seekers are grotesque, tentacled [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). They are servants of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), usually guarding tomes of forbidden knowledge,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBSeeker-1)</sup> and are masters of [illusion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Illusion) who use invisibility and decoy mirror images as distractions while they sap their target's strength, feeding off the ghosts of those seeking knowledge.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> Seekers have the power to banish prey with sound attacks. They are rarely seen outside the [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md).

They have the ability to drain their prey's energy, vitality and magicka as well as the knowledge from unfortunate mortals who cross its path.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBSeeker-1)</sup> While they appear bestial and imposing, they are not unintelligent. A journal of [Morian Zenas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morian_Zenas) about Apocrypha notes that seekers would converse with other Daedra in the realm, doing so with a lisp, and usually talking about the mage himself and Mora's intentions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOi A-3)</sup> They keep a variety of books, spellbooks, and scrolls with them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup>

More powerful seekers are called **Aspirants** or **High Seekers**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBSeeker-1)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fragment-Seeker-sinew_Thread.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fragment-Seeker-sinew_Thread.png) Seeker-sinew Thread A great deal of magicka can be expended to make coils of Seeker-sinew Thread, made from the sinews of a Seeker's corpse. When threaded through a needle and sewn into a piece of linen, this thread will make the linen move and undulate in a distasteful and organic fashion, still warm to the touch.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWCodex-4)</sup>

During the Second Era, there was a group of Hermaeus Mora worshippers who were known as Seekers; their connection to the Daedra of the same name, if any, is unknown.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ibrula-5)</sup>
