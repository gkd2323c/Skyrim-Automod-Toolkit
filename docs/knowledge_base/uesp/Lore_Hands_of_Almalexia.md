# Hands of Almalexia

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-tribute-almalexia-Hand_of_Almalexia.png/200px-ON-tribute-almalexia-Hand_of_Almalexia.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-almalexia-Hand_of_Almalexia.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-almalexia-Hand_of_Almalexia.png) Her Hands fighting off undead (Tales of Tribute) The Hands of Almalexia (or simply **Her Hands**) are private temple guards for the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) goddess [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), chosen from the most skilled warriors in the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md). Their bodies and souls are fortified by divine magic, and their armor and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) bear fearsome divine enchantments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TR-1)</sup>

## History

### Second and Third Era
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Hands were numerous, and were charged with guarding [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) along with the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) when it served as the capital of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). The [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was named a Hand of Almalexia while ridding [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) of the Maulborn menace.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALMAESO-2)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the Hands had been reduced to six individuals, and the [High Ordinators](Lore_High_Ordinators.md) had taken over guard duty of the city. They served as Almalexia's personal protectors as her divinity began to fail, and were led by her Chief Steward, [Fedris Hler](Lore_Fedris_Hler.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TR-1)</sup> By this time they had garnered a legendary reputation, with many, including the goddess herself, considering them to be the most terrible mortal warriors in the world.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TR-1)</sup> That year, the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) killed Hand Salas Valor after he left Almalexia's service.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

### Fourth Era
After the rise of the [New Temple](Lore_New_Temple.md), Almalexia was relegated to the position of [Temple Saint](Lore_Temple_Saints.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Worship of the Tribunal as gods was treated as a heresy by the New Temple, and worshippers of the Goddess was therefore persecuted.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> However, the Hands of Almalexia continued to exist. The tradition was passed down over several generations, with each new Hand having their body imbued with Her divine power.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the last remaining Hands served Matriarch Drevlan at a secret Tribunal temple on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) known as Ashfall's Tear. Hand Kenro Hlan attacked the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) within the ruins of Fahlbtharz and was killed, although it is unknown if the remaining two Hands survived.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Got T-7)</sup>
