# Order of the Cup

[![](https://images.uesp.net/e/e0/DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_%28face%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_(face).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_(face).png) Lord Plessington (Daggerfall) The Order of the Cup was a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) active in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Among their duties was serving as the protectors of the [Plessington Clan](Lore_Plessington_Clan.md). As of [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), they were led by Lord Plessington of [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md). As of then they were neither strong in numbers nor very powerful, but they were very eager to help in all the affairs of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Lord Plessington was known to send his knights off to right various wrongs, seemingly without ulterior motives. Knights often returned from those quests with a number of interesting stories.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-D1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-D2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DC-3)</sup><sup>:101</sup>

The events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) led to the incorporation of the fiefdom of Ykalon into the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). It also caused most of the forests in Ykalon to burn down.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WITW-4)</sup> It is unknown how this affected the members of the Plessington Clan, and knights of the Order of the Cup, however.
