[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-item-Ring_of_the_Wind.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_the_Wind.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Wind.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Wind.jpg) Ring of the Wind The Ring of the Wind is a ring artifact. It can be found while [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md). It increases movement speed by 15%.

The ring is a rare quality junk item, which has a 3% chance (8% chance with the [Dwarven Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Dwarven_Fishing_Rod.md)) of being caught when no fish is caught. See our [Fishing activity page](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Catch_Chances) for more details.

## Notes
- The ring is useful in [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) as there is no [fast travel](Skyrim_Fast_Travel.md).
- The ring previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Ring_of_the_Wind) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
- The ring's movement speed buff applies even when the player is overencumbered.

## Bugs
- Upon putting [Ildari Sarothril](Skyrim_Ildari_Sarothril.md) into Bleedout, the player is given a perk that adds a multiplier of 0.01 to the magnitude of [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) effects on the player. This persists until the perk is removed upon killing Ildari. This notably makes the [Ring of Khajiit](Skyrim_Ring_of_Khajiit.md) and Ring of the Wind very weak. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The perk can be removed using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `player.removeperk 0403d597`; it will be reacquired when Ildari is next put into Bleedout.
- The strength of the ring's enchantment can be increased by drinking a [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [Fortify Illusion](Skyrim_Fortify_Illusion.md) before equipping the ring. This buff will last until the ring is unequipped. - Depending on the potion's potency, the enchantment on the ring can increase or decrease.
- If the ring is equipped while you have the [Kindred Mage](Skyrim_Kindred_Mage.md) perk, the movement speed buff will be increased by 10%. The enchantment's strength will not update in the inventory, but the Active Effects menu will show the correct magnitude.
