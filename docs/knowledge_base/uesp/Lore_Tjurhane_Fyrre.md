Tjurhane Fyrre was considered one of the finest Sages of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md), which he founded in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVerita-1)</sup> He was one of the few known remaining [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md), being born in the late First Era and dying at the age of 357 in the late-early Second Era. Tjurhane Fyrre studied the history of his people, when it was the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md), to when it became the tribal people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). He was one of the few to publicly speak about his people and their religion, describing them as follows: "the nature of the tribes of Ayleid are multi-hued, their personalities often wildly different from their neighbor tribes."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Wild-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/ON-prerelease-Antiquarian%27s_Eye.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Antiquarian%27s_Eye.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Antiquarian%27s_Eye.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Antiquarian%27s_Eye.jpg) Antiquarian's Eye Based on the dates of his birth and death, Fyrre was born during the time of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md), beginning with [Kastav](Lore_Kastav.md)'s reign, and into the period of the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md), during the reign of [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md). In [2E 12](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_12), Tjurhane had released a book known as the *Nature of Ayleidic Poesy*, which is where his quote on the Ayleid tribes originates from.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Wild-2)</sup>

He was the founder of the Antiquarian Circle, the University of Gwylim's historical society.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Related to this was his invention of the Antiquarian's Eye, a tool that allowed the wielder's mind to travel vast distances and find buried relics. It was developed along with countless other scholarly tools. Before his death, Fyrre stored several Antiquarian Eyes in a vault in [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md), intended for the University's best and brightest to continue his work after he was gone, but Fyrre died before he could demonstrate the Eye's true potential.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVerita-1)</sup>

Some of Tjurhane Fyrre's last writings were about the life of [Narilmor](Lore_Narilmor.md), King of [Garlas Malatar](Lore_Garlas_Malatar.md), who sealed his city amidst the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). His writings were later taken by the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) following his death, courtesy of the University of Gwylim. His notes led to their interest in Garlas Malatar, where they built [Fort Mistwatch](Lore_Fort_Mistwatch_(Gold_Coast).md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
