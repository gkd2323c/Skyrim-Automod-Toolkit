## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance is an empty cart. Along the east wall is the entrance to a pen holding two [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md). Behind the house is a small vegetable plot containing two [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), five [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), four [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), and six [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), with two [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) in and near the goat pen. To the right of the entrance is a crate and two fish barrels. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Oslaf's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House.jpg) Home cooking The entrance leads into a small room with a fire pit in the center and a rack holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), two [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) samples, and two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. Beside the fire pit is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) on a stand. On your right against the east wall is a sideboard with a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on top. In the northeast corner is a small round table and two chairs. On the north wall are four shelves containing a loaf of bread, three [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a leek and a cast iron pot containing a cabbage. In the northwest corner is a set of shelves containing a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), three bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and two sacks. Against the west wall on a ledge are two more sacks. Three small steps lead up to the south into the main part of the house.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Oslaf%27s_House_02.jpg) Cozy living In the next room there is a wall shelf on your left with a goat cheese wheel, a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) and two [ash yams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam). On your right is a sack and in the northwest corner another small round table and three chairs. On the table is a bowl of [beef stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beef_Stew) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). Above the table on the north wall are two wall shelves containing three potatoes, a bottle of wine and a [honey nut treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat). In the southwest corner is a cupboard and on the shelves are two potatoes, a cabbage and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and on top of the cupboard is a sack. There is a narrow table against the south wall with two sacks underneath. Leading up to the upstairs is a flight of stairs against the east wall, with a doorway underneath, this leads to Aeta's bedroom that has a small square table and two chairs in front of you, with a [Horker loaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf) on a plate on the table and against the north wall a single bed with an end table has a copy of *[Words of the Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Words_of_the_Wind)* and a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) on top.

Climbing the stairs, there is an end table in the southeast corner with a low table and two chairs to the left. On the table is a copy of *[Kolb & the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon)*. To your left is a doorway into Oslaf and Finna's bedroom. As you enter there is a wardrobe on your left against the south wall with a chair next to it. In the northwest corner is an end table with a wooden bowl containing a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) on top. A double bed is to the right of it coming out from the north wall. Beside the bed against the east wall is an open crate with three pairs of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) in. Finally in the southeast corner is a small round table with a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) on top.
