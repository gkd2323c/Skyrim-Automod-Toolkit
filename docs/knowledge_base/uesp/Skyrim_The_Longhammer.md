[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-item-Orcish_Warhammer.jpg/200px-SR-item-Orcish_Warhammer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Orcish_Warhammer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Orcish_Warhammer.jpg) An Orcish Warhammer [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-quest-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Liar%27s_Retreat_03.jpg) Rahd, with Hammer The Longhammer is a unique [Orcish warhammer](Skyrim_Orcish_Warhammer.md) that belonged to the [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) [Rahd](Skyrim_Rahd.md) before he was killed during a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) attack as part of the [related quest](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat_(quest).md). It can be recovered from the Breton's dead grasp within [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md). Compared to the standard Orcish warhammer, the Longhammer weighs eight units less, while also having half the base value of the normal version. The true value of the weapon is its speed, as it has the same swinging speed as a mace. While it lacks an enchantment, one can be added after acquiring it to further improve its worth. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon requires an [orichalcum ingot](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ingot.md), with any improvements made being doubled if the [Orcish Smithing](Skyrim_Orcish_Smithing.md) perk is also unlocked.

Rahd can be found next to his signature weapon, having been killed by Falmer as a grisly sacrifice. The weapon can safely be removed from his dead grip, and should as it serves as the primary reward for the related quest. While Rahd was living, the name of the weapon also served as his nickname, as evidenced by a bandit chief calling out to him: *"Hey, Longhammer, set us up with a round of... what in Oblivion happened in here?"*

## Related Quests
- **[Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat_(quest).md)**: Retrieve the Longhammer from a fallen [bandit](Skyrim_Rahd.md).

## Notes
- This is the fastest two-handed weapon in the game which is not an artifact ([Ebony Blade](Skyrim_Ebony_Blade.md) being the fastest two-handed weapon).
- The Longhammer is affected by the [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md) shout, allowing even faster swings.
- The Longhammer swings 33.3% faster than a normal warhammer, surpassing even most greatswords in speed, and swinging at base mace speed. Because of that, it has a DPS that surpasses even a mighty [Daedric Warhammer](Skyrim_Daedric_Warhammer.md), and will eventually surpass even [Volendrung](Skyrim_Volendrung.md), as explained in the below table:

| Weapon | APS | Base | Base DPS | Smithed Damage | Smithed DPS |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [Daedric Warhammer](Skyrim_Daedric_Warhammer.md) | 0.6 | 27 | 16.2 | 37 | 22.2 |
| [Dragonbone Warhammer](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Warhammer.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> | 0.6 | 28 | 16.8 | 38 | 22.8 |
| The Longhammer | 0.8 | 21 | 16.8 | 31 | 24.8 |
| [Volendrung](Skyrim_Volendrung.md) | 0.7 | 25 | 17.5 | 35 | 24.5 |
