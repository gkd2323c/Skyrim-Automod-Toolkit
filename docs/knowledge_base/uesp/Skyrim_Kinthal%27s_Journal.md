Kinthal's Journal **by [Kinthal](Skyrim_Kinthal.md)** *Journal of the leader of the Saints bandits*
---
Even though we no longer raid with the [backstabbing Seducers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seducers_Bandit), we still work for that wizard [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md). He's been paying us handsomely to turn over [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad)'s caravan, looking for any trinkets that seem strange.

A week ago we found a [sword](Lore_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md) unlike anything we've ever seen. Thoron's face lit up when he saw it. "This could be it," he said. Something about a "bridge." He's probably lost it. No sword is worth how much he paid us for it. Won't hear us complaining, though.

We see Thoron once a month so he can have a look at our spoils. Everything he doesn't want, we split and sell. Nice and clean. When we're done, he skulks off back to that lair of his in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). What he does there is anyone's guess.
