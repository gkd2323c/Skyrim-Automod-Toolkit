# Blessings

This page documents the **blessings** that you can receive, typically by activating altars in temples. These are short-term blessings which act separately from long-term [Standing Stone](Skyrim_Standing_Stone.md) effects, and do not overwrite or interact with them. Typically, you will want any blessings you come across, unless you are specifically trying to bolster one effect.

## Shrine Blessings
Blessings are bestowed by praying at (activating) a shrine to the specified deity.

- Locations of the shrines of the Eight (or Nine) Divines, including those for [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md) added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), are listed at [Shrines](Skyrim_Shrines.md).
- The Shrine of Nocturnal is located inside the [Ragged Flagon Cistern](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern.md), once you are midway through the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest line.

You can only have one shrine blessing at a time. If you choose to pray at a second shrine, it will overwrite the previous blessing. Shrine blessings are active for a period of time measured in *real* time, not in-game time. So a blessing lasting 12 hours will be active during 12 playing hours.

## Notes
- Even if you betrayed Azura during [The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star), you will still be able to gain her blessing.
- Unlike other sources of Fortify Health, the Blessing of Arkay will not default the player to a minimum 1HP when the effect ends, so if you're under 25HP when the effect ends (either by passing enough time or replacing it with another blessing), you die.
- Blessings are considered to be "potion" effects with [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> enabled, and are thus modified by your level of [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md).

## Bugs
- For the player, all shrine blessings and the Blessing of the Stag Prince are scaled by any active [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) alchemy effect at the moment the blessing is applied. Scaled magnitudes stick until the blessing is removed or refreshed. In the 'Active Effects' list, the description will show the increased effect magnitude. **Mod Notes**: This is caused by the fact that the associated magic effects have their 'Magic Skill' field set to 'Restoration', which allows perk Alchemy Skill Boosts to scale the spell magnitudes by (1 + Restoration Power Mod/100). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=18867))
