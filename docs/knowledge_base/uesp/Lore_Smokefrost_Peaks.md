[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/ON-map-Smokefrost_Peaks.jpg/200px-ON-map-Smokefrost_Peaks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Smokefrost_Peaks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Smokefrost_Peaks.jpg) Map of Smokefrost Peaks circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Smokefrost Peaks is a fairly mountainous region situated in the region of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), within the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The Smokefrost Peaks consists of the southeast Rift, and is primarily located on the intersection where the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) meets the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md). Two of the region's centerpieces is [Lake Honrich](Lore_Lake_Honrich.md) on the northern end and the mountain that contains [Forelhost](Lore_Forelhost.md) in the southern end. The city-state of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) is found in the center, on the shores of Lake Honrich.

The region is known to contain [Smokefrost Rosewood](Lore_Rosewood.md), which is used in basic drinkware and other uses in the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The Smokefrost Peaks contains various ruins that hearken back to the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md), such as the aforementioned Forelhost, and Lost Tongue Overlook. The region is found on the base of [Mount Trolhetta](Lore_Mount_Trolhetta.md), a large volcano that meets with the two mountain ranges, and the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md). The region contains a road pass that leads into [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).
