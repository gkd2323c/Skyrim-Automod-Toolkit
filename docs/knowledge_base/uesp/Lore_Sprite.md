Sprites are playful [fay](Lore_Faerie.md) creatures that appear as floating orbs of light.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SJ-3)</sup> Those who specialize in [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) [magic](Lore_Magic.md) may be able to summon these spirits, or at least conjure images of them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Certain curses are capable of transforming people into sprites.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The sonnet "*[A Lissome Sprite](Lore_A_Lissome_Sprite.md)*", written by the poet [Nynelle Dumaris](Lore_Nynelle_Dumaris.md), is beloved on the isle of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md). While superficially a tale about a playful [nature spirit](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md), the sprite in actuality represents Nynelle's love for her island home.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALS-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TPCOV-6)</sup>

It is somewhat common to refer to other magical beings as sprites. [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md) are known to [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) as "water sprites".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KCAHC-7)</sup> Brimstone [nixads](Lore_Nixad.md) have been described as "little incendiary sprites".<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> [Imps](Lore_Imp.md) have been colloquially described as "trickster sprites".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KCAHC-7)</sup>
