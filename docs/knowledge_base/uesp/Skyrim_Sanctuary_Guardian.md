# Sanctuary Guardian

**Sanctuary Guardians** are the [Nordic](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of former [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) members destined in the afterlife to guard the halls of the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md). They will be unleashed by [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) in an attempt to stop you during [The Cure for Madness](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Sanctuary_Guardian_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sanctuary_Guardian_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sanctuary_Guardian_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sanctuary_Guardian_02.jpg) Sanctuary Guardian This **Sanctuary Guardian** specializes in close combat and has a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) of the best possible quality at his disposal (up to Daedric quality at level 46). He wears [shrouded armor](Skyrim_Shrouded_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Shrouded_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Shrouded_Gloves.md) and a [cowl](Skyrim_Shrouded_Cowl.md).

In combat they will have several unique lines, saying: *"You dare invade this Sanctuary?"*, *"Fall before the will of Sithis!"*, *"Unworthy!"*, *"You are not welcome here!"* and *"You can't kill what's already dead, mortal."* When defeated, their last dying words will be: *"Father..."* or *"Precious release..."*

## Notes
- The Sanctuary Guardians are members of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn_(class).md) class, which determines the level of their skills.
- Shrouded armor, boots, gloves and cowl which cannot be looted from their remains can be pickpocket from them if player has Perfect Touch perk.
