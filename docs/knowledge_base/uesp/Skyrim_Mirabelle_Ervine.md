[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine.jpg) Mirabelle Ervine **Mirabelle Ervine** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) and serves as master wizard of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md), reporting directly to Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md). After first gaining entrance into the college through [Faralda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Faralda), Mirabelle will welcome you to the premises, provide you with your robes, and give you a tour.

Mirabelle plays a key role in the College of Winterhold quest line (see [this section](#Quest-Related_Events)).

She wears [apprentice robes of alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Robes_of_Alteration) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c). All she carries on her is knowledge, in the form of the book *[On Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:On_Oblivion)*.

Not surprisingly for someone of her office, she is well-versed in the arcane arts and knows a wide array of spells, the ones she prefers and the only she will use in combat are: [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Flame Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Cloak), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md).

She sleeps between 3am and 8am every night in her room on the second floor of the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md), in the southwestern wing. She starts each day with a one-hour breakfast then enters the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements) and wanders around it for two hours. At 11am she heads into the courtyard for five hours, where she often reads her book or just strolls about. At noon she has a one-hour lunch in the Hall of Attainment then returns to the courtyard. Until late afternoon, she sometimes takes breaks from the chilly air and warms up in the Hall of Attainment or the [Hall of Countenance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance). At 4pm she returns to the Hall of Attainment and strolls around until her one-hour dinner at 6pm, which she enjoys with [Sergius Turrianus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sergius_Turrianus) in his room in the Hall of Countenance. She spends evenings in the Hall of Attainment, eating or wandering, until her regular bedtime at 3am.

You'll find Mirabelle coldly addressing [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md), a member of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md):

**Ancano**: *"Yes, of course. The Arch-Mage has my thanks."*
**Mirabelle**: *"Very good. Then we're done here."*

After that, she will greet you when you approach her:

*"Welcome to the College."* **I was told to come see you.** *"Another new student... I'm surprised at how many of you there are lately. Well, first you'll need these. Are you ready to begin?"* (She'll give you a [Novice hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Hood) and [Novice robes of Destruction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Robes_of_Destruction)) **I'd love to have a look around.** *"Wonderful. Please follow me, and don't wander off. The College of Winterhold has been a fixture in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) for thousands of years."* She'll stop in front of the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements), and inform you:

While technically in charge of the College, the Arch-Mage's responsibilities often keep him occupied. Thus, I run the day-to-day operations. Now, if you'll please follow me, I'll show you the living quarters."* If you try to talk to her during the tour she'll only repeat:

*"After I've finished showing you the grounds, we can talk."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirabelle_Ervine_03.jpg) *"This is where you'll be staying."* She'll then guide you to the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) explaining:

*"Unfortunately, we've had to implement more stringent entry procedures, due to some problems with the local [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md). We don't anticipate any real violence, but it never hurts to be prepared."* After a brief pause, she'll stop near the door:

*"Our newest members are housed here in the Hall of Attainment. I'll ask that you please keep your voice down while inside, as others may be working on research or... delicate experiments."* Once inside the hall, she'll say:

*"Now I'll show you to your quarters. You're going to be sharing space with your fellow Apprentices, who you'll meet shortly."* She'll show you to your room on the far right of the hall:

*"This is where you'll be staying. This bed and desk are yours. Please try and be considerate of others. Now, let's go back to the Hall of the Elements, where most of the members gather for lectures and study sessions."* Once outside again, she'll share more:

*"Initially you'll be learning from [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir), one of our most esteemed Wizards."* She will stop near the statue at the courtyard and tell you to meet up with the rest inside:

*"Tolfdir is likely already addressing the new Apprentices. Go on in, and if you have any problems, let one of our senior members know."* After giving you the tour of the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md), Mirabelle returns to her regular duties for a good portion of the College quest line.

**What do you know about the Psijic Order?** *"No more than anyone else, I don't think. They're a very old order. Pre-dated the [Imperial Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) by quite a bit. No one has seen or heard from them in over a hundred years now, though."*

When speaking to [Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azura) worshipper [Aranea Ienith](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md), you are pointed towards the College to find an elven mage that studies stars. When confronted with this question, Mirabelle will be upset:

**Is there an elven mage here that studies stars?** *""An elf that studies stars"? Where did you hear about that?"* **What is it? What happened?** *"Officially, nothing."* **Would you believe I was given a vision?** *"No. I wouldn't."'* Either way, she'll follow up by suggesting:

### Containment

If approached again:

*"If we can't stop this, what happens to the rest of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)?"* [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) initiates here.

Check with Urag gro-Shub in the Arcanaeum. See if he's aware of any references to this... thing that you and Tolfdir found. I'd like to know as much as we can about it."* The following scene between Urag and Mirabelle was also supposed to play in the Arcanaeum:

**Urag**: *"Here's something to consider, Mirabelle. "Night of Tears", by Dranor Seleth. Old book, one I'd forgotten about. This thing here... it might be what the book was about."*
**Mirabelle**: *"I don't see the relevance."*
**Urag**: *"He proposed that the real reason Saarthal was fought over by the Elves and the Nords was because something was unearthed there."*
### [Revealing the Unseen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen)
Additional dialogue was also recorded for Mirabelle when you return from [Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft) during **Revealing the Unseen**. You were originally intended to report back to her about the fate of the [Synod](Skyrim_Synod.md) researchers:

**I found the Synod in Mzulft. They're dead.** *"Really? Well, I do recall warning them it might not be the safest of locations. I hope this doesn't become a problem for us. Were you able to learn anything?"* **You don't care that they're dead?** *"I warned them it might be dangerous. It's not my fault they didn't listen. As long as the Synod don't blame us for this mess, I have more pressing matters to deal with."* **I found notes about a crystal, and the Oculory in Mzulft.** *"Hmm. I can't say I've any idea what they were up to. Have you found this observatory? You might be able to learn something from it. Keep looking, but be careful."* **They were trying to perform some sort of experiment, I think.** *"I can't imagine what that might be, but if it's safe, you might see if they were successful. You could get some useful information about whatever they were up to. Sounds like it won't be of use to them now, anyway."*
## Notes
- Mirabelle is voiced by [Mozhan Marnò](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mozhan_Marn%C3%B2).
- Mirabelle is the author of *[Per Your Requests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Per_Your_Requests)*, a note to [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) court wizard [Wylandriah](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md) in reply to a supposed letter she sent to the College.
- Mirabelle's facial data was copied over for the NPC [Sultry Maiden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sultry_Maiden) encountered in [The Mind of a Madman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_a_Madman).
