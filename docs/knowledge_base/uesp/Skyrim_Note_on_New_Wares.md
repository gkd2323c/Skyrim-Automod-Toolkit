Note on New Wares **by [Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md)** *New exotic goods for sale*
---
Many thanks for dealing with the ruffians that have plagued humble [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) such as this one for some time. Coin flows well once again.

[Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md) has new wares for sale that would interest one such as you. More of the arms and armor used by the Saints and Seducers have been found and can be yours, for a price. This one has also traded for strange new raw materials. Ri'saad knows not what they could be used for, but this one is sure you could find a way to put them to good use.

May your road lead you to warm sands.
