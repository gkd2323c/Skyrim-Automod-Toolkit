[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-place-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg/200px-SR-place-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg) Southfringe Sanctum **Southfringe Sanctum** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southeast of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) containing the conjurer [Bashnag](Skyrim_Bashnag.md) and his [coven](Skyrim_Spellsword_(NPC).md). It contains only one zone, *[Southfringe Sanctum](#Southfringe_Sanctum)*.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The entrance is marked by a lit brazier on a stand to the right. To the left is a table with an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md), an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md), and a set of [robes of quickening](Skyrim_Robes_of_Quickening.md) on top. There is a [spellsword](Skyrim_Spellsword_(NPC).md) working at the lab, who may be so absorbed in their work, they may be easy to sneak up upon. Prior to entering, to the north of the cave is a [silver ore vein](Skyrim_Silver_Ore_Vein.md) beneath a rock overhang. Continuing to the north, by the base of a tree on the cliffs where one can overlook [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md), a [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) you can't interact with and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) are found. In front of the chest is the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Warrior's Charge](Skyrim_The_Warrior%27s_Charge.md)*, and on the skeleton is an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md). Due west, uphill behind a large rock outcrop, is a second silver ore vein. Due south of the cave entrance, along the path to the blocked gate leading to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), lies a [moonstone ore vein](Skyrim_Moonstone_Ore_Vein.md). Two more moonstone ore veins can be found 510 feet southwest of the cave entrance.

### Southfringe Sanctum
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-interior-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Southfringe_Sanctum.jpg) Southfringe Sanctum interior The entrance leads into a natural passage heading west. There is a lantern on the ground shortly after entering the cave; just after this lantern, behind a plant on the other side of the tunnel, there is an unlocked chest. There is a spellsword patrolling this part of the tunnel. After a few paces, you come to a pair of wooden gates in a wider passage. Use caution, as the gates have a [frost rune](Skyrim_Frost_Rune.md) on them. Through the gates is a huge chamber filled with trees and vegetation on multiple levels. There is a spellsword standing on a ledge to the west of the gates. From the gates, two paths lead deeper into the cave system: a path heading west, beneath the spellsword, and another rising to the northwest. This second path ultimately leads to Bashnag, the leader of this spellsword coven, while the former path leads down to a [giant frostbite spider](Skyrim_Giant_Frostbite_Spider.md)'s lair, through a system of caves infested with more [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md).

Following the higher of the paths leads past two lit braziers on the ground on your right. Just past the second brazier, there's a flag on the left, by a mammoth rib cage. Turning south here then continuing forward leads to a unique [fox](Skyrim_Fox.md) named [Pumpkin](Skyrim_Pumpkin.md). Unfortunately, the cute little fox is hostile, and has likely eaten his last [chicken](Skyrim_Chicken.md) as you pass through the room. There is a small novice-locked cage containing a dead chicken behind Pumpkin, with two raw [chicken breasts](Skyrim_Chicken_Breast.md) beside the cage. Beyond this is a spellsword who is either working at an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) or mining an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md). Beside the anvil is a table holding a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), a piece of [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md), a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md), a random [enchanted](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md) [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), a random [enchanted](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md) [boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots), and two [leather strips](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md). There is also a small empty tent nearby. A short distance north of the iron ore vein is a silver ore vein, and by a tree in front of this is a large [urn](Skyrim_Urn.md), with a [burial urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md) by a large tree stump farther north.

Returning to the main path and continuing to follow it as it rises, you will encounter another spellsword working at another alchemy lab. By this lab the path turns to the west, before ending at a pair of wooden gates. The gates are novice-locked, and open to reveal a path descending into an open-roofed chamber. You will find Bashnag working at an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) at the end. He, like all the other diligent workers in this cave, may hardly seem to notice you. He carries a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[The Art of War Magic](Skyrim_The_Art_of_War_Magic.md)*. There is an unlocked boss chest to the right. A short distance before the enchanter, on the left and heading south, is a path that descends; to the right of the path is a small crevice in the rock wall with a lit brazier behind it. You can look through this and spy a spellsword in the room beyond. There is a second spellsword farther back, which enables you to take them out individually. The second spellsword is guarding a pair of unlocked wooden gates that block access to the beginning of the frostbite spiders' lair.

Past the gates, there are two spiders in the immediate vicinity, and an earthen bridge spanning the chamber; from this bridge you can reach a ramp leading up to two [egg sacs](Skyrim_Egg_Sac.md) that can be harvested. Beneath these is the passage that leads farther into the lair, and another short passage that leads to an area where you'll encounter [Selveni Nethri](Skyrim_Selveni_Nethri.md) caught in webbing. She's a [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) who was originally part of Bashnag's coven, but after a falling out, was chased and trapped down here. This was after Bashnag cursed her, making it impossible for her to cast any spells. She asks for your assistance in helping her escape, but will only leave once you've cleared the way. There are two more egg sacs in this lower passage, one of them behind Selveni. Use caution, as there are numerous spiders in this part of the cave system. As you pass through into the next chamber, which is circular, a giant frostbite spider will descend from above. The path follows the outer wall counterclockwise around the room and descends, passing an unlocked chest wedged behind a pillar on the left near the bottom. Just after the lowest point, there is another egg sac stuck to the ceiling that you can harvest. From this point, the passage rises steeply, before several turns lead back to the start of the two paths near the cave entrance. Once the caves are completely cleared, you can return and tell Selveni the coast is clear. There is no reward for completing this [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri.md).

## Notes
- The cave appears to be very buggy, with odd item placement and enemy behavior.
- A necromantic ritual site can be found north of the cave's entrance if the [Plague of the Dead](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) is installed. See the [quest page](Skyrim_The_Rising_Dead.md) for more information.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.

## Bugs
- Selveni may not leave the cavern, leaving the quest unfinished. This can be fixed by commanding a follower to attack her, which will push her through the exit and complete the quest. Using various shouts such as Ice Form and Unrelenting Force on her may also work, as well as using archery. Alternatively, when she pauses at the exit, simply leaving the cavern and then immediately reentering may update and complete the quest.
- Behind the chest near Bashnag is a small, but accessible, hole in the wall that leads to the void outside the level. To get back, just walk through any wall (they are all one-way).
- It's possible to explore the areas above Bashnag's room and enter the void part of the level or get stuck.
- Sometimes it is not possible to enter the cave. You will move into the zone where the game should start loading the inside of the cave, but nothing happens, and no "To Southfringe Sanctum" label appears. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, exit the game (with command `qqq`) and start it again, loading from where you are just in front of the cave. The cave should be accessible now. If the same happens when attempting to exit the cave, just use `coc Southfringe Sanctum Exterior`.
- Humanoid NPCs that are working will be non-hostile and will only become hostile once interacted with, making them easy prey for sneak attacks and [pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md).
- Although intended to be hostile to you, it is possible for Pumpkin the fox to spawn in a calm state. This may also be related to the fox spawning outside of its cage rather than being trapped within it.
- Pumpkin may also spawn in a hostile state but then become non-hostile when the spellswords are slain, provided he is not attacked in the process. <sup>**?**</sup>
