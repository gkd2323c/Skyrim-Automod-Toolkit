# Topaz

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/OB-item-Topaz_Family.jpg/200px-OB-item-Topaz_Family.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Topaz_Family.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Topaz_Family.jpg) Topaz in various lapidary stages Topaz is a kind of orange [crystal](Lore_Crystals.md) often used in the making of jewelry.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They have little use in enchanting or alchemy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Topaz dust can be ingested to fortify one's personality but damages their agility and luck.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
