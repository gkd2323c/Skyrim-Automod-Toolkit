Words of Donovan Jarnot, Daggerfall Covenant soldier **by [Donovan Jarnot](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Donovan_Jarnot)** *The thoughts of a soldier fighting in the Alliance War*
---
[![ON-concept-Siege weapon.png](https://images.uesp.net/5/58/ON-concept-Siege_weapon.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Siege_weapon.png)
- "I'm not so scared of war anymore. Turns out a lot of the stories they told back home aren't even true. I haven't seen any invincible [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) mages wearing nothing but lightning or [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) gods flying above the battlefield raining down death, and I've killed a bunch of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) without their blood melting my sword. I'm much more afraid of being hit by fiery debris from one of these catapults or getting skewered by a ballista bolt, in truth.

- I have seen the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) pouring out of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), though, and I know we have to stop it somehow. We can't let what's happened to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) happen to our homes in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and the best place to start will be winning this war and bringing some order back to Tamriel. I try to focus on one thing at a time though. Here, give me a hand fixing this thing up—we attack at dawn."
