## Quick Walkthrough
1. Visit the Riften Fishery.
2. Speak with Viriya.
3. Speak with Swims-In-Deep-Water.

## Detailed Walkthrough
The quests starts when the player first visits the [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md). There they will need to speak with [Viriya](Skyrim_Viriya.md) and [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md), the two experienced fishermen. Speaking with the two will start the [In A Pinch](Skyrim_In_A_Pinch.md) and [Catch of the Day](Skyrim_Catch_of_the_Day.md) quests, respectively.
