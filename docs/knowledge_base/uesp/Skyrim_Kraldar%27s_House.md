## Kraldar's House
To the left of the entrance is a single owned bed used by [Thonjolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thonjolf) with a couple of barrels to the right in the corner. On these are two [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) and a [honey nut treat](Skyrim_Honey_Nut_Treat.md). Against the south wall is a cupboard, while against the west wall is a long wooden table with a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bucket containing [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and a basket containing [red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and [green apples](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md) on top. Hanging above from the ceiling is a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. To the right is a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Under this, to the right of the table is a bucket containing [carrots](Skyrim_Carrot.md) and a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel.

To the right of these is a set of shelves with four bottles of alto wine, four bottles of wine, and a basket containing a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Breathing Water](Skyrim_Breathing_Water.md)*. To the right of this is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a kettle containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) on the hearth to the right. There is a round wooden table and chair with a bottle of alto wine and a sweet roll in front of the hearth and on the far side of the fireplace, still against the west wall is an expert-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). Coming out from the north wall is a double bed used by [Kraldar](Skyrim_Kraldar.md) with an end table with a [jug](Skyrim_Jug.md) and bottle of Nord mead on top. Against the east wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). To the right is a barrel and a dresser with four bulbs of garlic, two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), a bottle of [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md), and three bunches of elves ear on top. Hanging above are three garlic braids, a bunch of dried elves ear, and a bunch of dried frost mirriam.

If the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) obtain Winterhold in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), Kraldar becomes the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), moves out of this house and into the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md). Thonjolf, now Kraldar's [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md), will still return at night to sleep in the single bed.

## Notes
- The cell name of the house is actually `Winterhold Korirshouse` internally. All scenes that [Korir](Skyrim_Korir.md) has with his family inside the [jarl's longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) in the final game are also referred to as "Winterhold Korir's House Scenes". The location data refers to "Korri's House" instead, although this is in all likelihood just a typo.
- Completing any quest that results in Kraldar taking over as jarl, such as [Rescue from Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav.md) for the Imperial Legion or reporting to [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) about taking Fort Kastav during the [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md) quest, boosts your disposition with him, which in turn makes low value items freely available, as well as the use of both beds.
