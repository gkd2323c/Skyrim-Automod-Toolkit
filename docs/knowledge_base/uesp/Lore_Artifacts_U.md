## Ul'Vor Staff
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/ON-item-Ul%27Vor_Staff.jpg/200px-ON-item-Ul%27Vor_Staff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Ul%27Vor_Staff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Ul%27Vor_Staff.jpg) Ul'Vor Staff The **Ul'Vor Staff** was a mysterious magical stave that shared a name with the underwater [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) kingdom of [Ul'vor Kus](Lore_Ul%27vor_Kus.md). It was an ancient relic believed to originate from there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SQI-1)</sup> Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) it is known to have been in the possession of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). Around this time, a [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) named K'Tora invaded Artaeum and captured Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md). K'Tora attempted to extract the Ritemaster's deepest memories using the [Orbs of B'Raken Drel](Lore_Orbs_of_B%27Raken_Drel.md), but his plan was foiled when the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), with the aid of a Psijic named [Valsirenn](Lore_Valsirenn.md), retrieved the staff from the Psijic Relic Vaults and used it to breach K'Tora's mind barrier and kill him.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-V-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONBM-3)</sup>

## Unraveling Palimpsest
The [Unraveling Palimpsest](Lore_Unraveling_Palimpsest.md) was an [arcane](Lore_Magic.md) tome of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) the contents of which transform depending on the reader, taking a form unique to each. In actuality a manifest supernatural phenomenon created in response to the endless seas of knowledge within Apocrypha, the tome allows its reader to learn any secret as long as it pertains to their own life and [fate](Lore_Fate.md), in essence "unraveling" the secrets of their fateline. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [Azandar-al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) delved into Quires Wind, a facility in Apocrypha where the tome was stored, and used it to discern which defining moments of his life anchored the Adversary, a frayed fate intent on absorbing its alternate fates and replacing its originator, to him.

## Unraveling Staff
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/ON-item-Unraveling_Staff.jpg/200px-ON-item-Unraveling_Staff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Unraveling_Staff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Unraveling_Staff.jpg) The Unraveling Staff The [Unraveling Staff](Lore_Unraveling_Staff.md), occasionally called a wand, is a powerful [staff](Lore_Staff.md) artifact renowned for its ability to unravel any material. It was crafted in [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) by Anillyon. It is extremely dangerous, capable of dismantling buildings, trees, and even flesh of living beings.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Relics_of_Summerset#The_Unraveling_Staff)

## Umbra
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg) Umbra (Legends) The [Umbra Sword](Lore_Umbra.md) (or simply **Umbra**) is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) designed with the sole purpose of the entrapment of souls. Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ancient witch [Naenra Waerr](Lore_Naenra_Waerr.md) created the sword at the request of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), who wanted the sword as a tool of mischief which would send him souls from the mortal realm. Naenra created the sword but it was unstable, requiring Vile to give the witch a piece of his power to be imbued in the sword in order to stabilize it. However, Naenra (sometimes accredited with being [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) in disguise) supposedly tricked him, as the piece of Vile's power imbued in the sword became a sentient being which called itself **Umbra**. Naenra was executed for her evil creation, but not before she hid the sword. The sword took on a life of its own, remaining hidden until a worthy wielder was found. Umbra then slowly takes control of the wielder. The Umbra Sword has the ability to change shape, but it is always in the form of a bladed weapon. It has been reported as a black and silver claymore, a jet black longsword and as a black sword emblazoned with red markings. The sword is also known to have the ability to absorb the health and stamina of those it strikes.

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Umbra_Sword), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Umbra_(sword)), [*The Infernal City*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Infernal_City), [*Lord of Souls*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Lord_of_Souls), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Umbra_(item).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Umbra.md)</sup>, [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Umbra)
