It is connected by road to the nearby Lunar Fang Docks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> The fort possesses an underground complex with staff quarters.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

## History
The ruins were the site of an ancient battle during the period of [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) rule over Cyrodiil. Very little is known of this battle, other than that it involved both Ayleids and [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md). The bodies of the fallen were buried in a graveyard south of the watchtower.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GRN-3)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Variela's Watchtower was already in ruins, plagued by undead [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md) led by a skeletal watch captain. A researcher came to the ruins to study the ancient battle, but was thrown from his horse and perished at the hands of the undead. The watch captain was later defeated by soldiers from the ongoing [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GRN-3)</sup>

By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins of Fort Variela had been taken over by a group of [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>
