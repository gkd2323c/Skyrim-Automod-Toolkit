## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bleakwind_Basin_03.jpg) View of the camp Two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and three [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) occupy the camp, which is centered around a large campfire with two [charred skeevers](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) cooking on stakes. An expert-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) is southwest of the fire, with two [mammoth cheese bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl) and a dead [sabre cat](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) beside it. Two more mammoth cheese bowls can be found to the north, with two patches of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) and a tree stump with a patch of [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) growing nearby. To the northeast is a small pool with a [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) plant growing on the bank. Unlike most other giant camps, there are no [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md) and very few painted stones.

## Notes
- Bleakwind Basin is in the origin cell (coordinates 0,0) in the [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) worldspace grid. Certain loading errors may therefore cause you to be dropped into this cell.

## Bugs
- Fast travelling here can cause the mammoths to spawn high above the ground, then plummet to their deaths. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
