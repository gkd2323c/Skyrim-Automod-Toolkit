Notes on Dissection *Dissection notes on a Vigilant of Stendarr*
---
We were to leave the [Vigilants](Lore_Vigilants_of_Stendarr.md) for the ritual, but this one fought with the [Silver Hand](Lore_Silver_Hand.md). It was not clear who his master was.

After we fed, the master bade us to examine the body, to see what else we could learn of our condition.

Bone anatomy seems largely unchanged. Blood and organ samples have been taken to the alchemist.

The bite marks on the shoulder are curious. Need to rule out the possibility of werewolf contamination.
