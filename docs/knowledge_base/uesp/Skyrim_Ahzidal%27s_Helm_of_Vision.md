[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg) Ahzidal's Helm of Vision Ahzidal's Helm of Vision is an [ancient Nord helmet](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Helmet.md) that is part of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). It can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md) during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). This helmet is identical to a normal ancient Nord helmet, but provides four more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and weighs two points more. Its enchantment increases the [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) cost of your [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) and [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) spells by 25%, but allows them to be cast twice as far (without perks, see [notes](#Notes)). In addition, if you wear this helmet along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points. Ahzidal's helm can be tempered with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), with the [Advanced Armors perk](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors_(perk).md) doubling improvement.

## Related Quests
- **[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)**: Assist [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) in his search for the relics of [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md) in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

## Notes
- The enchantment isn't affected by the [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner) or [Rune Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rune_Master) perks. It will always increase the total range by the spell's base range (24 ft for Conjuration, 20 ft for runes).
- Ahzidal's Helm of Vision also increases the [Dawnguard Rune Hammer's](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Rune_Hammer.md) effective bashing rune placement range, and does so without the helmet's 25% increased "casting" cost being applied.
