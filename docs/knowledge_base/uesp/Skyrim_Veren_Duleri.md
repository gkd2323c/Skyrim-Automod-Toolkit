[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-npc-Veren_Duleri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Veren_Duleri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Veren_Duleri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Veren_Duleri.jpg) Veren Duleri **Veren Duleri** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [mage](Skyrim_Fire_Frost_Shock_Mage.md) and a worshipper of [Vaermina](Skyrim_Vaermina.md) living in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md). When Nightcaller Temple was attacked by an Orc war party intending to destroy the [Skull of Corruption](Skyrim_Skull_of_Corruption.md), the worshippers released the Miasma, a gas that put everyone to sleep. Years later, when the Miasma is finally lifted, he and [Thorek](Skyrim_Thorek.md) will attempt to defend the Skull of Corruption from [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md), whom they consider a traitor for fleeing the Temple.

He wears a set of [Vaermina robes](Skyrim_Vaermina_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000bacd7). He wields a leveled mace that may be enchanted depending on your level.

Veren knows the following spells: [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md)**: Relieve the people of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) from [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md)'s curse.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
You first encounter Veren Duleri in the [Dreamstride](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md), conversing with Thorek, another Vaermina devotee:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_2.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_2.jpg) Veren in the Dreamstride **Thorek**: *"The orcs have breached the inner sanctum, Brother Veren."*
**Veren**: *"We must hold. We can't allow the Skull to fall into their hands."*
**Thorek**: *"But... no more than a handful of us remain, brother."*
**Veren**: *"Then we have no choice. The Miasma must be released."*
**Thorek**: *"The Miasma? But, brother..."*
**Veren**: *"We have no alternative. It's the will of Vaermina."*

Veren will address you with:

- *"And what about you, Brother Casimir? Are you prepared to serve the will of Vaermina?"*
- **I've made my peace. I'm ready.**
- *"Then it's decided. Brother Casimir, you must activate the barrier and release the Miasma. Let nothing stop you."*

He will tell Thorek:

- *"Brother Thorek, we must remain here and guard this Skull with our lives if necessary."*
- **Thorek**: *"Agreed. To the death."*
- **Veren**: *"Then let it be done. Farewell, my brothers!"*

You will encounter Veren and Thorek again as you and Erandur reach the Skull of Corruption.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Waking_Nightmare_3.jpg) Erandur meets his old friends **Erandur**: *"Wait... Veren... Thorek... you're alive!"*
**Veren**: *"No thanks to you, Casimir."*
**Erandur**: *"I no longer use that name. I'm Erandur, Priest of Mara."*
**Veren**: *"You're a traitor. You left us to die and then ran before the Miasma took you."*
**Erandur**: *"No. I... I was scared. I wasn't ready to sleep."*
**Veren**: *"Enough of your lies! I can't allow you to destroy the Skull, Priest of Mara."*
**Erandur**: *"Then you leave me no choice!"*

You and Erandur will have to fight the two Vaermina devotees. As he dies, Veren will say:

- *"How could you, Erandur..."*

## Notes
- There is another version of Veren Duleri with the Base ID `0002427c` and Ref ID `000242a7`. He is used in the scene where you enter the Dreamstride. He wears the same outfit but will always have a [steel mace](Skyrim_Steel_Mace.md). This version of Veren looks quite different than the version encountered at the end of the quest, with a different hair color, beard, lips and eyes. He is the only person in the quest that has such a change, as both versions of Thorek look identical. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.5, addresses this issue. Both versions of Veren now use his pre-Dreamstride appearance.
