Bandit's Journal *A Bandit's account of taking over Fort Neugrad* *
---
For once, we actually caught a lucky break. With [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) closed and [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) wiped off the map, the last few soldiers up at [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md) were easy pickings. We should be able to loot the place and be out well before the [Jarl](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) has time to respond.

Finally found the keys to the Fort's old library. I sent [Brandish](Skyrim_Brandish.md) down to scour the place. Wouldn't normally waste the time, but I've heard rumor the wizards up north pay good coin for anything magical. At this point, we'll take what we can get. There's a ton of rotten food in the old cellars, but not much else worth taking.
