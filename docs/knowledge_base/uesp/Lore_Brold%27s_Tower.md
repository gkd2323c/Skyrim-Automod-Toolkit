[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/BL-quest-The_Wizard%27s_Tower.jpg/200px-BL-quest-The_Wizard%27s_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-The_Wizard%27s_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-The_Wizard%27s_Tower.jpg) Brold inside the Tower circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180) Brold's Tower, also known as the **Wizard's Tower**, is a haunted [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) found somewhere on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It is named for its sole living inhabitant, an eccentric [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) wizard called Brold, who resided here in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Tower-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tower Event-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/BL-decoration-Wizard%27s_Rings.jpg/200px-BL-decoration-Wizard%27s_Rings.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-decoration-Wizard%27s_Rings.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-decoration-Wizard%27s_Rings.jpg) One of the Wizard's Rings decorations donated to Rivercrest The origins of the tower's original Ayleid structure is unknown. It likely dates back to the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) or [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).

The Fourth Era wizard Brold was something of a recluse, choosing to keep the company of [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md) instead of people.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades Horse-3)</sup> As a hobby, he liked to create challenges for adventurers. To this end, he [conjured](Lore_Conjuration.md) a great many ghosts, filling his entire tower with the bound spirits of Ayleid warriors and [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md). He then spread word of his challenge, inviting intrepid adventurers to enter the tower and battle through a series of haunted chambers to reach him for a reward.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Blades Gavrus-4)</sup>

Circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md) was directed to the tower by Gavrus Lampronius, a friend of Brold's and a fellow resident of [Rivercrest](Lore_Rivercrest.md). The Warrior entered the tower and reached Brold, who was surprised that anyone had made it through the ghosts. Consequently, he had little to offer as reward aside from the contents of the tower.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Tower-1)</sup> Brold asked the Warrior to pass on his regards to his daughter Greidil Horse-Mouth, a cooper who also lived in Rivercrest.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Blades Brold-5)</sup>

Brold later sent word to Greidil to request that the Warrior return to the tower to participate in a second challenge. He had reconfigured the tower, requiring the Warrior to speak with the defeated ghosts and answer a series of riddles to pass through each chamber.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> As a reward for making it through the challenge and discovering all of the tower's secrets, Brold gifted the Warrior a pair of decorative Wizard's Rings which were magically enchanted to rotate. These had been preemptively delivered to Greidil in Rivercrest and were donated to the town's rebuilding efforts, impressing the local townsfolk.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Blades Brold-5)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

### Copy Tower
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/BL-event-Return_to_the_Wizard%27s_Tower.png/200px-BL-event-Return_to_the_Wizard%27s_Tower.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-event-Return_to_the_Wizard%27s_Tower.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-event-Return_to_the_Wizard%27s_Tower.png) The nether lich on the other side of Rivercrest's portal Rivercrest possessed a mysterious portal outside of town which would occasionally open. Each time, it would lead to strange places and realms filled with valuables such as [sigils](Lore_Sigil.md). On one occasion, a copy of Brold's Tower appeared on the other side of the portal. However, Brold was nowhere to be found. Instead, this version of the tower was filled with hostile ghosts, [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md), and a powerful [nether lich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nether_Lich). The denizens of this alternate version were invulnerable to all damage aside from bashing attacks and [shock](Lore_Shock.md) magic.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tower Event-2)</sup>

The Warrior entered this version of the tower repeatedly while the portal remained open, battling its strange denizens to recover valuables.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tower Event-2)</sup>

## Notes
- It is unclear where in Tamriel the Tower is located. Presuming Rivercrest is located nearby, it could be assumed that the Tower is somewhere in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), possibly in the [Bloodfall Kingdom](Lore_Bloodfall.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BLDestane-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-BFB-10)</sup>
