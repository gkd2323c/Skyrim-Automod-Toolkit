The Hoar Father (also written as the **Hoarfather**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARExclamations-1)</sup> or **Fodiiz Bormah** in the [Dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md))<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> was a philosophical figure that is revered in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Not much is known about the Hoar Father but his ancient teachings have been mainly recounted by the ancient [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), as his words have been documented on various [Word Walls](Lore_Word_Walls.md) across the province, including on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). His name is often exclaimed by the modern-day Nords, such as in the common expression "by the Hoarfather".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARExclamations-1)</sup>
