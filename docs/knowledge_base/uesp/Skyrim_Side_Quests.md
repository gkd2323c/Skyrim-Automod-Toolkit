# Side Quests


Side quests are listed separately in your journal. Unlike [miscellaneous quests](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md), they are not subcategorized, and are logged in your journal after completion. Side quests can be identified by the side quest knotwork (pictured above left) displayed next to the quest title in your journal and on each quest's page on this wiki.

Quests are sorted below by their usual starting location.

## Dungeons
These quests generally start when you approach or explore the associated dungeons and progress to completion within each dungeon. Only dungeon quests that are counted as side quests are listed here. The [Dungeon Quests](Skyrim_Dungeon_Quests.md) page provides a list of all dungeon quests, including both the below side quests and several additional miscellaneous quests.

- **[A Scroll For Anska](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska)**: Help [Anska](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anska) trace her roots in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md).
- **[Ancestral Worship](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestral_Worship)**: Help [Golldir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir) save his [aunt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Agna) and stop a [necromancer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran) from defiling his [ancestral barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb).
- **[Evil in Waiting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Evil_in_Waiting)**: Seal away an [ancient evil](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md).
- **[Infiltration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Infiltration)**: Help [Stalleo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalleo) take back [Treva's Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch).
- **[Repentance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance)**: Help [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) prevent her mother [Silvia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia) from transforming into a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).
- **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult.md)**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) and defeat [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md) to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](Skyrim_Captain_Valmir.md).
- **[Silenced Tongues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silenced_Tongues)**: Unlock the burial chamber of the Tongue Chieftain [Kvenel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kvenel_the_Tongue).
- **[The Lost Expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition)**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel). - This quest is also listed as a Markarth [city quest](#Cities) as the associated dungeon is within Markarth.
- **[The Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady.md)**: Find out what happened in [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md).

## Achievements
One [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) is obtained by completing side quests:

- [![SR-achievement-Sideways.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-achievement-Sideways.png/30px-SR-achievement-Sideways.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Sideways.png) **Sideways** (20 points/Bronze) — Complete 10 side quests - Completing any 10 qualifying side quests will unlock this achievement. Any quest that uses the side quest knotwork in your journal generally counts toward the achievement, but there are a few unusual cases: - The Black Book side quests, [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md), and [Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages) do not count toward the achievement, but are listed on this page and are treated as side quests in your journal.
- [The Affairs of Hagravens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Affairs_of_Hagravens) counts toward the achievement, but is not listed on this page and is treated as a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md) in your journal.
- [Delayed Burial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial) is listed on this page and counts toward the achievement, but is otherwise categorized as a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest and is marked in your journal with the Dark Brotherhood knotwork.
- [Alteration Ritual Spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration_Ritual_Spell) is not listed on this page and does not count toward the achievement, but is mistakenly marked in your journal with the side quest knotwork instead of the correct [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) knotwork.
- In most cases where a quest has two possible conclusions, either ending will qualify toward the achievement. Exceptions include [A Scroll For Anska](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska) and [The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md). See the individual quest pages for details.
- You can track your progress toward the achievement using the "Side Quests Completed" statistic on the "Quest" page of your journal's "General Stats".
