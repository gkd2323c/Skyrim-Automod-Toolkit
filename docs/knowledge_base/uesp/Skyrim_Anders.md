[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-creature-Anders.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Anders.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Anders.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Anders.jpg) Anders' skeletal remains **Anders** is a named skeleton found in the [Twilight Sepulcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher). His [message](Skyrim_Anders%27s_Message.md) suggests that he was the unscrupulous killer of [Nystrom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nystrom) and that he starved to death after trying to break into the sanctuary. It contains a leveled enchanted dagger in addition to the note and standard loot.
