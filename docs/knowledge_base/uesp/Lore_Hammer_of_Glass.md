Hammer of Glass *Notes on an Orcish crystal artifact*
---
The first [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) started not as a city but as an armed camp. Eventually, the camp grew into a village, and the village expanded into a town. In just a few short years, it became a sprawling, indefensible mass of people and buildings that were ripe for attack.

Forge-wife Mortuga, first and greatest of King [Torug](Lore_Torug.md)'s many wives, sought out a master mason to build a stone wall around the city and define its borders. He had to be a master crafter, as well as possessed of the skills of a warrior and a negotiator to complete the task in record time. She chose Kuthbarg the Steady to handle the job.

Kuthbarg did as he was tasked, raising strong walls around [Old Orsinium](Lore_Old_Orsinium.md) that can still be seen to this day. As a reward, he was given the Hammer of Glass—a hammer carved from a single crystal. It is believed that the striking sculpture still resides in the ruins of Old Orsinium.
