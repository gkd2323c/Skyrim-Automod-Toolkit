[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-npc-Treasure_Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Treasure_Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Treasure_Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Treasure_Hunter.jpg) A male treasure hunter **Treasure Hunters** are [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) that can be found digging for treasure near [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md). They will warn you away if approached too closely, and become hostile if their warning goes unheeded. Their belongings, including a [treasure note](Skyrim_Note_(treasure).md), can be found just above their excavation site next to the road.

## Bugs
- If talked to before becoming hostile, treasure hunters will speak the default dialogue for [hunters](Skyrim_Hunter.md): *"I've been hunting and fishing in these parts for years"* and it is possible to trade with them.
