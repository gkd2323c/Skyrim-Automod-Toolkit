Jolethe was a noble from the early-mid [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and was a member of the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md). She is most well-known as the Queen of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude_(kingdom).md) after her brother [Pelagius III](Lore_Pelagius_III.md) became the Emperor of Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMOP-1)</sup> Jolethe was the only daughter and second child of, at the time King [Magnus Septim](Lore_Magnus_Septim.md) and Queen [Hellena](Lore_Hellena.md) of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWQ8-2)</sup>

When the [Siege of Solitude](Lore_Siege_of_Solitude.md) took place in [3E 137](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_137), she was present in [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) with her mother. Along with her brother, father, her uncle Emperor [Cephorus I](Lore_Cephorus_I.md), and her aunt [Potema](Lore_Potema.md), they were reportedly the last members of the Septim family at the time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWQ8-2)</sup> After the battle, Pelagius III became the King of Solitude, but after the death of their father on the 8th of [Second Seed](Lore_Second_Seed.md), 3E 145, Pelagius and his wife, [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md) left for the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) so that he could become Emperor. Meanwhile, Jolethe took his place and became the Queen of Solitude.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMOP-1)</sup>
