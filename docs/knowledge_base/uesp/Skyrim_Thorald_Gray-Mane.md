[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane.jpg) Thorald Gray-Mane [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thorald_Gray-Mane_02.jpg) Thorald chained to the wall in Northwatch Keep **Thorald Gray-Mane** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) and a strong supporter of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). He was captured by the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) after aiding the Stormcloaks in battle, after which the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) took custody of him and started interrogating him in the dungeons of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). His brother [Avulstein](Skyrim_Avulstein_Gray-Mane.md) will task you with finding him. Once you rescue him, he will go into hiding. His mother is [Fralia Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Fralia_Gray-Mane.md).

He wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and a pair of [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps).

## Notes
- Sometimes, before starting the quest Missing in Action, Thorald can be found in Northwatch Keep; however, it is not possible to rescue him if the quest did not initiate or reach the point where you have to save him. Instead of his usual dialogue, he will continually ask what you are doing. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Even if you delay his rescue until the end of Civil War, and even if you use a peaceful method of releasing him using written official order from General Tullius, Thorald will not return to his family in Whiterun and will still have the same dialogue stating he is not safe in Whiterun and must fall in with the ranks of the Stormcloaks.
