### Altar in the Woods: Autumnshade
[![SR-place-Altar in the Woods.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods.jpg/200px-SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods.jpg) [![SR-place-Altar in the Woods 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_in_the_Woods_02.jpg) - POIFall Forest04

North of [Autumnshade Clearing](Skyrim_Autumnshade_Clearing.md) is an altar surrounded by stone structures. After dealing with the hostile [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) and [bone wolf](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md) you can find a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[Racial Phylogeny](Skyrim_Racial_Phylogeny.md)*, and some fire salts on the altar. An adept-locked container called a cage is mounted on the side of one of the stone structures and can be unlocked to yield a few dwemer items and a healing potion. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=153820&y=-71304&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Bandit's Shack: Autumnshade
File:SR-place-Bandit's Shack Autumnshade.jpg West of [Autumnshade Clearing](Skyrim_Autumnshade_Clearing.md) is a partially destroyed shack. Two bandits and their dog live here. The interior of the shack consists of two small rooms. One room has a bedroll and a chest beside it, while the other room has a fireplace with a harvestable [charred skeever](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), and a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill book *[Battle of Red Mountain](Skyrim_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md)*. An [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) vein is located behind the shack. If the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) is installed, this location will be marked as [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md), and the bandits will be replaced by [lookouts](Skyrim_Redwater_Lookout.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=135973&y=-75740&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Burning Farmhouse
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-place-POIFall Forest14.jpg/200px-SR-place-POIFall Forest14.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POIFall Forest14.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POIFall Forest14.jpg) Burning Farmhouse [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-place-Burning_Farmhouse.jpg/200px-SR-place-Burning_Farmhouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Burning_Farmhouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Burning_Farmhouse.jpg) Interior - POIFall Forest14

South-southeast of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) is a ruined, burning house. Just inside the main door to the right lies a burnt corpse holding a [Scroll of Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md) outside a ring of burning candles, while an unlocked chest to the left holds some leveled loot. A copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Horror of Castle Xyr](Skyrim_Horror_of_Castle_Xyr.md)* is found just outside the northern corner of the house, inside the opening of a hollow, rotting log. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=189400&y=-124856&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Darkwater Overhang
[![SR-place-Darkwater Overhang.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-place-Darkwater_Overhang.jpg/200px-SR-place-Darkwater_Overhang.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darkwater_Overhang.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darkwater_Overhang.jpg) - POIFall Forest11

East of [Pinepeak Cavern](Skyrim_Pinepeak_Cavern.md) is the lair of a troll. The bodies of two deceased Stormcloak soldiers are found here among the bones of other victims of the troll. A [note](Skyrim_Note_(corpse).md) can be found on the body of one of the Stormcloaks which suggests that they were sent to investigate recent attacks in the area in order to gain the locals' favor. At the back of the lair is an [orichalcum ore](Skyrim_Orcish.md) vein. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=83336&y=-51864&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Darkwater Stash
File:SR-place-Darkwater Stash.jpg North-northwest of [Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md), next to a rock face, are a pile of three barrels and a sack with a copy of the Pickpocket skill book *[Aevar Stone-Singer](Skyrim_Aevar_Stone-Singer.md)* on the ground between them. There is a [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md) growing nearby and a few paces to the northwest is an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=98819&y=-47914&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### The Poultry Reanimator: Lake Geir
[![SR-place-The Poultry Reanimator Lake Geir.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-place-The_Poultry_Reanimator_Lake_Geir.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Poultry_Reanimator_Lake_Geir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Poultry_Reanimator_Lake_Geir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Poultry_Reanimator_Lake_Geir.jpg) - POIFall Forest24

Southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) and south of [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md) is a novice necromancer at a curious "altar" reanimating chickens. A wooden box at the base of the altar contains a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md), a [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) and chicken eggs. The necromancer carries the conjuration skill book [*2920, Frostfall, v10*](Skyrim_2920,_Frostfall,_v10.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=91091&y=-74450&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### The Three Sentinels
File:SR-place-The Three Sentinels.jpg Along the road north out of Riften are three wooden watchtowers manned by Riften guards. There are some sacks and one adept-locked chest called "Confiscated Goods" in each tower. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=175110&y=-86015&zoom=17&legacy=true)

#### Notes
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) links the chests to enable parent markers and creates duplicate chests for when The Rift is under Imperial control.

#### Bugs
- The contents of the Confiscated Goods chests in each watchtower is unowned and free to take. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. This has been adjusted to set ownership correctly.

### Trappers' Dilemma
[![SR-place-Trappers' Dilemma.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/SR-place-Trappers%27_Dilemma.jpg/200px-SR-place-Trappers%27_Dilemma.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Trappers%27_Dilemma.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Trappers%27_Dilemma.jpg) - POIFall Forest18

To the east of [Greenwall Cave](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md)'s main entrance (the one exterior to the fort), there is a caged [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md). If you release the wolf, two [trappers](Skyrim_Trapper.md) will appear and attack you, with the wolf siding with you against them. If the wolf manages to survive the fight, it will not attack you, and will remain near the cage until the zone respawns. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=178716&y=-68217&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Wood Cutter's Camp: Lake Geir
[![SR-place-Wood Cutter's Camp Lake Geir.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-place-Wood_Cutter%27s_Camp_Lake_Geir.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wood_Cutter%27s_Camp_Lake_Geir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wood_Cutter%27s_Camp_Lake_Geir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wood_Cutter%27s_Camp_Lake_Geir.jpg) - POIFall Forest10, POIFall Forest10b

Northeast of [Honeystrand Cave](Skyrim_Honeystrand_Cave.md), southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), on a hillock is the remains of a woodcutter's camp consisting of a single small tent containing an unowned bedroll beside an extinguished campfire. A cooking spit has been knocked over and the corpse of a woodcutter is lying near the tent with a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) beside him. There is an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) just inside the tent and a [baked potato](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md) in a metal pot by the fire. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=84779&y=-72026&zoom=17&legacy=true)
