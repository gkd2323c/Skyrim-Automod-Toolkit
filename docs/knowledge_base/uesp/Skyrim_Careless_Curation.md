## Quick Walkthrough
1. Ask [Aphia Velothi](Skyrim_Aphia_Velothi.md) about the missing Curate.
2. Read *[Curate Melita's Plea](Skyrim_Curate_Melita%27s_Plea.md)*.
3. Travel to Stony Creek Cave.
4. Rescue or kill [Curate Melita](Skyrim_Curate_Melita.md).
5. Retrieve the Mask of Sotha Sil.

## Detailed Walkthrough
You will have to head to [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) and speak to [Aphia Velothi](Skyrim_Aphia_Velothi.md):

- **I understand a temple Curate has gone missing.**
- *"It would seem so."*

She will hand you the Curate's [plea](Skyrim_Curate_Melita%27s_Plea.md). The objective says to return to Skyrim and find [Curate Melita](Skyrim_Curate_Melita.md) in Kagrenzel. You can circumvent this entire location and simply head to [Stony Creek Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave). She's tied up in the waterfall chamber all the way at the back of the cave. You will have the option to either save her or end her life. The Mask of Sotha Sil can be acquired with both options. If her life is spared, she will return to [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) as an Apothecary vendor.

## Notes
- Her location intersects with other creation content. The bandits and bandit chief carrying the Ruin's Edge Bow and the Blissbug In A Jar from Saints and Seducers will all be in this same location together.
- When going directly to Stony Creek Cave, the quest marker nonetheless will point to the entrance of Kagrenzel until you speak to Melita and the quest completes.
