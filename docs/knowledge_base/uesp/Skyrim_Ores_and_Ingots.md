# Ores and Ingots

**Ores** and **Ingots** are various forms of metal that can be collected and [smithed](Skyrim_Smithing.md) to create and improve items. Ores can be extracted from veins found in [mines](Skyrim_Mines.md) and other areas around Skyrim, such as some caves and outdoors in rocky areas. Ores (already mined) and ingots can also occasionally be found in a wide variety of locations and on some people and objects. [Smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) ores yields ingots, which can then be used at [forges](Skyrim_Forge.md) to create armor, weapons, and jewelry, or at [grindstones](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and [workbenches](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve armor and weapons.

This page lists all items that are categorized by merchants as ores and ingots. These are traded by jewelry [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) as well as [blacksmiths](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) (including the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md) and [Stormcloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster) Quartermasters found at most [military camps](Skyrim_Military_Camps.md)). They can also be sold to general goods merchants. The following pages detail all sources and uses of the various minerals and metals:
