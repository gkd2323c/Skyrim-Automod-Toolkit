The Oracle (sometimes **Oracle of Hammerfell**) was a wise woman and [seer](Lore_Sight.md) from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> Although blind, she was said to be the wisest woman in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and traditionally served as counselor to the monarchs of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFOracle NPC-2)</sup> She "gave [her] eyes to the gods", but gained the ability to see the future "through their eyes".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFOracle Dialogue-3)</sup> The Oracle was assisted by a former noble known as the Acolyte.

A book written by the [Scenarist Guild](Lore_Scenarist_Guild.md), *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](Lore_The_Daggerfall_Chronicles.md)*, was found in a shrine to the Oracle after being smuggled out of their guildhall in the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CD-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/8/86/DF-npc-Oracle.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Oracle.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Oracle.png) The Oracle In [3E 402](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_402), the Oracle was the Chief Counselor of the advisers to King [Camaron](Lore_Camaron.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) when a land dispute with [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) began brewing into the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md). The Oracle counseled the king against war, foreseeing death and defeat, but was rebuffed and banished from the court.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NWOB-5)</sup> Sentinel lost the war and Camaron was killed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup>

The Oracle was on good terms with Sentinel during the reign of Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> During this period, she foresaw an attempt on the life of an uncooperative noble, whose death would cause havoc in the region. Upon the recommendation of the Archmagister of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), the Oracle (via her Acolyte) secured the services of an [agent](Lore_Hero.md#The_Agent) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), though it is not known how this adventure ended.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DFOracle-6)</sup>
