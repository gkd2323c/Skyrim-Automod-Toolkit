They and [orange dartwings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing) appear in roughly equal proportions hovering over water. A few seconds after being approached they will fly away and then appear to vanish, so they must be snatched quickly. The two types of dartwings share the [Fortify Pickpocket](Skyrim_Fortify_Pickpocket.md) alchemical effect.

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants. It cannot normally be bought from them, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants.

## Creatures
Dragonflies spawn at 769 sites, in 138 different locations, typically near [bodies of water](Skyrim_Bodies_of_Water.md) (rivers, lakes, or ponds). Each spawning site can generate a random mix of up to 10 orange- and blue-winged dragonflies. The dragonflies are spawned between 5am and 8pm; some of the spawning sites instead generate [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) at night. They will fly away after a few seconds so you need to be quick to catch them.

- 30 around [the Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 22 around the [Abandoned Shack](Skyrim_Abandoned_Shack.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 22 around the [Wreck of the Winter War](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 17 around [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 15 around [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 15 around [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))

## Notes
- Both the blue and orange dartwings have their wings on backwards.
- Dartwings can be difficult to catch, due to their small size (making them harder to see) and speed. Using a word of a shout (such as [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) or [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md)) causes them to fall to the ground. However, if you do this over deep or swiftly moving water, it will complicate finding them afterwards.
