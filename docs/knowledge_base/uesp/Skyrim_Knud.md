[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-Knud.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Knud.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Knud.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Knud.jpg) Knud **Knud** is the [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) of [Katla](Skyrim_Katla.md) and [Snilling](Skyrim_Snilling.md). He lives with his parents at [their farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

He wakes at 8am and wanders around the house until 10am, then he goes outside to do farm work for eight hours. He takes breaks at noon to have a one-hour meal at home. At 6pm he finishes working and returns home for a one-hour dinner. He stays up until 12am, which is his bedtime.

Knud wears blue [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Katla%27s_Farm) to the farmhouse.

## Dialogue
Knud may greet you with:

*"You're a guest. And father says we have to be polite to guests."* *"Mama said I could help with the goats!"* *"Father says I don't have the knack for reading, but mama says I won't need it to work the farm."*
## Conversations
When Knud is together with his family, they may talk to each other:

**Katla**: *"Another gorgeous gray day, right dear?"*
**Snilling**: *"It's too cold for my bones, but that never seems to stop you, dear."*
**Knud**: *"I'm gonna go play soldier with a tree!"*
**Katla**: *"That's nice, son. Just don't stomp on the crop like last time!"*
**Snilling**: *"I'll see if I can't get some work done inside, where it's warm."*
