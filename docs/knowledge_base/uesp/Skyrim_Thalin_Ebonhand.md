[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-npc-Thalin_Ebonhand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thalin_Ebonhand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thalin_Ebonhand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thalin_Ebonhand.jpg) Thalin Ebonhand **Thalin Ebonhand**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md), was the ship's helmsman serving as part of the crew under the legendary pirate king [Haknir Death-Brand](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md). Upon entering the final chamber of [Gyldenhul Barrow](Skyrim_Gyldenhul_Barrow.md) during the [related quest](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md), his spirit appears along with Haknir, [Garuk Windrime](Skyrim_Garuk_Windrime.md), and members of [Haknir's crew](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Crew.md), to fight you.

He is clad in a full set of [Nordic](Skyrim_Nordic.md) carved armor and he is wielding two leveled one handed weapons (up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality). As a wizard, he has a decent range of spells. He knows [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), and [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md). He also has a constant two point restore magicka ability and highly fortified [sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md).
