## Ingredients
43-45<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 33-34<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 4 in [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 in [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 in [Irkngthand Grand Cavern](Skyrim_Irkngthand_Grand_Cavern.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Jala's House](Skyrim_Jala%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Solitude Lighthouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Lighthouse) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
