Low Warden Dusk (formerly **Lord Warden Dusk**) is a [Grievous Twilight](Lore_Grievous_Twilight.md) and a powerful servant of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) who rose to prominence during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). He was in charge of running the [Imperial Prison](Lore_Imperial_City_Prison.md) during the Daedric occupation of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOSE-1)</sup> Cunning, brutal and cruel, the self-proclaimed "Lord Warden"<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLyranth-2)</sup> of the prison was very proud of the atrocities that were committed under his watch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IPDR-3)</sup> So much so, that he kept a meticulous record of all the indignities that had been committed in the prison; a tome which he coveted dearly.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOUrgarlag-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOWarden Tome-5)</sup> His servants sacrificed the inmates' souls to Molag Bal, and fashioned biological weapons out of their flesh.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOSE-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NJ-6)</sup>

Dusk, with the help of a mortal champion, is responsible for trapping the living essence of [Lyranth the Foolkiller](Lore_Lyranth.md)'s clanmates.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> He was defeated by the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) with the help of Lyranth, who trapped Dusk's animus inside a shield upon the destruction of his physical form.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOSE-1)</sup> She then bestowed the shield upon the Undaunted as a token of her gratitude before taking Dusk's place as Warden of the prison.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLyranth-2)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GLW-8)</sup> While trapped in the shield, he would often declare how he would eventually become free again and exact his revenge.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

Following his defeat at the Imperial City Prison, Dusk was eventually freed from the shield and demoted to Low Warden.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) led an attack on the Ossein Cage in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) to destroy the [Dolorus Cista](Lore_Dolorous_Cista.md). The cista was protected by Overfiend Kazpian, who used its powers to call upon the greatest servants of Coldharbour to defend it. Low Warden Dusk was among the summoned Daedra, but he and his shades were banished by the Fighters Guild and the cista was destroyed.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Notes
- Lord Warden Dusk was documented within the [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). There, copies of various entities were subsequently created as [maligraphies](Lore_Maligraphy.md), creatures composed of vivified ink manifested by stories from books, and serve as mindless extensions of the [Daedric Lord](Lore_Daedric_Lord.md) [Tho'at Replicanum](Lore_Tho%27at_Replicanum.md). A maligraphy copy of Dusk was formed, which was destroyed by adventurers who were aided by augmentations directly from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-LAIA-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MM-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Enemies-14)</sup>
