[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg/200px-SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpentstone_Isle.jpg) Serpentstone Isle circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Serpentstone Isle is a small icy island located in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), northeast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>

An ancient [Standing Stone](Lore_Standing_Stones.md#Standing_Stones) dedicated to [the Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md) can be found atop the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> According to legend, heroes can use this stone to rewrite their fate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> These ancient ruins attract [Ice Wraiths](Lore_Ice_Wraith.md) to the island, and as a result the place has traditionally been used by the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) to test a man's mettle.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

During the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md) circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md) used the island as a proving ground for potential recruits, sending them there to fight an Ice Wraith and prove their devotion to the cause.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
