[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-menu-Spell_Knight_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Spell_Knight_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Spell_Knight_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Spell_Knight_Armor.jpg) Spell Knight Armor **Spell Knight Armor** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds three variants of [Spell Knight Armor](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor_(item).md), the attire of the [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md) knightly order, the [Order of the Crypt](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md#Fourth_Era). The Creation was primarily made by [Elle H](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2527329), while the quest was created by [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Official Summary
Creation Club *Adorn the armor of the Spell Knight, the Keepers of the Crypt of Hearts, renowned throughout High Rock for their bravery and valor. Learn the story of the Spell Knight whose heart was stolen by a witch and obtain the armor that was their standard. The Spell Knight Armor comes in three variants, including iron, steel, and ebony! Creation by Elle H. Quest by Kris Takahashi (Quest "Crypt of the Heart" starts by reading Crypt of the Heart - Draft at the Silver-Blood Inn in Markarth.)* Sneak Peek - Mar 2019 *Adorning the armor of the Spell Knight, the Keepers of the Crypt of Hearts were knights renowned throughout High Rock for their bravery and valor. Yet when the heart of a knight was stolen by a witch, they vowed to guard not just the crypt in Shornhelm, but the hearts of all men. Learn the story of the Spell Knight and obtain the armor that was their standard. The Spell Knight Armor comes in three variants, including iron, steel, and ebony!*

## Notes
- This Creation features the [Beldama Wyrd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Beldama_Wyrd) as antagonists. Though the coven were once known for worshipping [Jephre](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jephre), even [tracing their origin back to him](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Witch_Cults_of_Northern_High_Rock), they have seemingly taken to [traditions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Briarheart) associated with [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). - It was [stated](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Cartogriffi%27s_Posts) that the witch covens [featured](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Witch_Covens#High_Rock) in *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* were reviewed, and it was chosen among them. The large gap of time between the Beldama's portrayal in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) and *[Skyrim Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)* factors into their much darker depiction.
- This Creation was originally announced as **Spellsword Armor**.
- Spell-Knights are elaborated on in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)'s [Legacy of the Bretons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Legacy_of_the_Bretons) as being a spellcasting knight archetype for Bretons.
- The Corrupted Human Heart and Simon Rodayne's Heart appear in the Ingredients category of your inventory but cannot be used in creating any potions at an alchemy table
