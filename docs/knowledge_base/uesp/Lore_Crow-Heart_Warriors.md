# Crow-Heart Warriors

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-hat-Crow-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Crow-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Crow-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Crow-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) Crow-Heart Skull Sallet [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-creature-Drascua.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Drascua.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drascua.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drascua.jpg) A Hagraven Crow-Heart Warriors are one of the numerous [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) groups. While many [Reachfolk clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) actively revile and fear [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Medone-1)</sup> some are known to revere and placate them in exchange for power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGTBangkorai-2)</sup> Crow-Heart Warriors can be found among the ranks of many of the latter. Hagraven matrons who lead clans often form a personal guard consisting of Crow-Heart Warriors. Those warriors are loyal to their matrons and are known for their distinctive helmets, which typically incorporate imagery of skulls of great predatory birds.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONStag Sallet-3)</sup>
