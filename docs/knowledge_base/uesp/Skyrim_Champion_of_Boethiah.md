[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-npc-Champion_of_Boethiah.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Champion_of_Boethiah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Champion_of_Boethiah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Champion_of_Boethiah.jpg) The Champion of Boethiah, wearing the Ebony Mail The **Champion of Boethiah** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) residing in [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md). Killing him is the final challenge in [Boethiah's Calling](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Calling.md). He is equipped with the [Ebony Mail](Skyrim_Ebony_Mail.md), as well as [ebony boots](Skyrim_Ebony_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Ebony_Gauntlets.md), and [shield](Skyrim_Ebony_Shield.md). He carries a leveled enchanted sword, which can be up to daedric in quality.

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Events

### Boethiah's Calling
He has unique dialogue explaining why he renounced [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), but due to his high aggression, you must [calm](Skyrim_Pacify_(effect).md) him in order to hear it.

- **So you're Boethiah's champion, eh?**
- *"That was a long time ago."*
- **Boethiah is displeased. She wants you dead.**
- *"The Prince of Plots usually gets what she wants. I'm surprised I'm not dead already. She must have had some reason to keep me alive so long."*

- - **This is what you want? To slave for others? What about your own destiny?**
- *"Mind my words or I'll mind them for you: I'm slave to no man, no god, and no [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). Boethiah talks about leaving your mark, a sign of your passing.... Well, you can make a mark on the world without treachery and murder. Whenever a man's life is saved by armor made with these hands, these hands have changed that man's destiny and his family's. So you can tell that heartless Daedra bitch I'm done doing her dirty work."*

- - **You aren't afraid to die?**
- *"A man who feels no fear has no heart. I know fear. But it does not control me. If you desire to fight me, then let's get this over with."*

- - **Tell me, what's it like being Boethiah's champion?**
- *"I told you. I'm done with all that. There's enough blood on these hands. I'm interested in creating things now."*

## Bugs
- The Champion of Boethiah will re-equip his armor if he is killed and revived with [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md), which can be exploited for infinite gold and powerful armor for your followers. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.5, addresses this issue. The patch makes him a ghost shortly after Ebony Mail is looted, so he can not be taken away from the cave. There is still a very small time window where he can be reanimated.
