Margret's Journal **by [Margret](Skyrim_Margret.md)** *An Imperial Legion spy's journal detailing a mission to acquire the Cidhna Mine*
---
Meeting at [the Treasury House](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md) later today. Took them long enough. These people act like they own everything.

[Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md) is the younger brother, but he's obviously the one in charge. Makes all the deals, bullies local landowners into selling to him. Even employs [that wispy girl](Skyrim_Rhiada.md) at the door to deter "trouble-makers" like me.

[General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) is growing impatient, but I'll bring back the deed to [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md). On my life, I won't allow a group of [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) sympathizers to own the prison to the most notorious criminals of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). They say no one escapes. Why? Is it really that secure?

Maybe I've played my hand too soon by rushing the confrontation with Thonar. There are shadows around every corner in this city, and I know I'm being watched.
