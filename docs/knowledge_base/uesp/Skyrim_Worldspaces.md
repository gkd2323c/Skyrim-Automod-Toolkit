# Worldspaces

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-place-Blackreach.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blackreach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackreach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackreach.jpg) Blackreach This page lists all the **worldspaces** in *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*. Worldspaces are continuous areas without doors between cells.

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Alftand Cathedral](Skyrim_Alftand_Cathedral.md)** — A large Dwarven ruin southwest of Winterhold containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Alftand)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small glade in a cavern east-southeast of Falkreath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ancestor+Glade+%28DG%29)
- **[Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — Hermaeus Mora's plane of Oblivion.
- **[Azura's Star Interior](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star_Interior.md)** — A small alternate dimension within Azura's Star containing Malyn Varen and any Dremora that he summons.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Blackbone Isle](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup> — A haunted island in the Sea of Ghosts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blackbone+Isle+%28CC%29)
- **[Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md)** — An immense, naturally lit cavern inhabited by hordes of Falmer and containing the ruins of a Dwemer city. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blackreach)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Blind Cliff Cave](Skyrim_Blind_Cliff_Cave.md)** — A medium-sized cave southwest of Karthwasten along the banks of the Karth River containing Forsworn. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blind+Cliff+Cave)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md)** — A small cave north of Falkreath containing animals and spriggans. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bloated+Man%27s+Grotto)
- **[Boneyard](Skyrim_Boneyard.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — An enclosed graveyard in the Soul Cairn.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Brinewater Grotto](Skyrim_Brinewater_Grotto.md)** — A small cave north of Solitude containing horkers or bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Brinewater+Grotto)
- **[Darkfall Passage](Skyrim_Darkfall_Passage.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small cave only accessible through Darkfall Cave serving as the only access to the Forgotten Vale.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Darkwater Pass](Skyrim_Darkwater_Pass.md)** — A small cave south-southwest of Darkwater Crossing containing Falmer and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Darkwater+Pass)
- **[Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — The wilderness area surrounding Fort Dawnguard, accessed through a cave entrance southeast of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dayspring+Canyon+%28DG%29)
- **[Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup> — A plane of Oblivion belonging to Mehrunes Dagon.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Deepwood Vale](Skyrim_Deepwood_Vale.md)** — A small valley far west of Solitude. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Deepwood+Redoubt)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle.png) **[Dragonsreach, Great Porch](Skyrim_Dragonsreach,_Great_Porch.md)** — The residence of the Jarl of Whiterun Hold, the hold's seat of political power, and the location of the hold's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dragonsreach)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Docks.png) **[East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md)** — A warehouse on the Solitude docks immediately southeast of the city. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=East+Empire+Company+Warehouse)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md)** — A small underground grove and worship site of the followers of Kynareth south-southwest of Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Eldergleam+Sanctuary)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Shipwreck.png) **[Emperor's Quarters](Skyrim_Emperor%27s_Quarters.md)** — The personal transport vessel of the Emperor, Titus Mede II. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+Katariah)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Fallowstone Cave](Skyrim_Fallowstone_Cave.md)** — A small cave northeast of Riften containing leveled animals, frostbite spiders, and giants. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Fallowstone+Cave)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Forebears' Holdout](Skyrim_Forebears%27_Holdout.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small cave southeast of Dragon Bridge. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Forebears%27+Holdout+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) **[Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — An isolated region in northwestern Skyrim once inhabited by the Snow Elves worshipping the god Auri-El. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Forgotten+Vale+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Frostmere Depths](Skyrim_Frostmere_Depths.md)** — A medium-sized Nordic ruin south-southwest of Dawnstar containing bandits and the unique wispmother the Pale Lady. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Frostmere+Crypt)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Giant's Grove](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Grove.md)** — A grove accessed through Fallowstone Cave containing a giant whose tribe has defiled a shrine of Malacath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Giant%27s+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Giant's Tooth](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup> — A small island in the Sea of Ghosts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Giant%27s+Tooth+%28CC%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Settlement.png) **[Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md)** — A self-contained worldspace far northeast of Skyrim containing Blood Horkers and their leader Haldyn. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Japhet%27s+Folly)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Camp.png) **[Karthspire](Skyrim_Karthspire.md)** — A large Forsworn camp and cave east of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Karthspire)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Labyrinthian Chasm](Skyrim_Labyrinthian_Chasm.md)** — A large Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr, skeletons, trolls, wisps, and the dragon priest Morokei. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Labyrinthian)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Labyrinthian, Tribune](Skyrim_Labyrinthian,_Tribune.md)** — A large Nordic ruin southeast of Morthal containing draugr, skeletons, trolls, wisps, and the dragon priest Morokei. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Labyrinthian)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Lost Valkygg](Skyrim_Lost_Valkygg.md)** — A small Nordic ruin northeast of Labyrinthian containing draugr. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lost+Valkygg)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Markarth.png) **[Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md)** — A major city in the southwest of Skyrim near the border of High Rock, and the capital of the Reach. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Markarth)
- **[Misty Grove](Skyrim_Misty_Grove.md)** — One of the Myriad Realms of Revelry created by Sanguine, where he is holding a feast with some of his followers.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern.md)** — A small, open-roofed cavernous grove lush with many kinds of flora and fungi, located due north of Hunter's Rest and northwest of Half-Moon Mill which is inhabited by bears and spriggans. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Moss+Mother+Cavern)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Red Eagle Ascent](Skyrim_Red_Eagle_Ascent.md)** — A small cave and two exterior camps east of Markarth containing Forsworn. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Red+Eagle+Redoubt)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Riften.png) **[Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)** — A major city in the southeast of Skyrim near the borders of Morrowind and Cyrodiil, and the capital of the Rift. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Riften)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Shadowgreen Cavern](Skyrim_Shadowgreen_Cavern.md)** — A small cave northwest of Solitude containing leveled spriggans and animals. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Shadowgreen+Cavern)
- **[Shalidor's Maze](Skyrim_Shalidor%27s_Maze.md)** — A small Nordic ruin east of Labyrinthian containing atronachs and a dremora. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Shalidor%27s+Maze)
- **[Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md)** — A large Nordic ruin with a portal to Sovngarde containing draugr, dragons, and a dragon priest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Skuldafn)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Solitude.png) **[Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)** — A major city on the northwest coast of Skyrim, and the capital of both Haafingar and the entire province. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Solitude)
- **[Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A plane of Oblivion haunted by lost souls.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Southfringe Sanctum](Skyrim_Southfringe_Sanctum.md)** — A small cave southeast of Helgen containing the conjurer Bashnag and his coven. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Southfringe+Sanctum)
- **[Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md)** — The Nordic afterlife.
- **[The Mind of a Madman](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_a_Madman.md)** — The mind of Pelagius the Mad.
- **[Volkihar Courtyard](Skyrim_Volkihar_Courtyard.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — The courtyard attached to Castle Volkihar and Volkihar Keep.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Whiterun.png) **[Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)** — A major city in the center of Skyrim, and the capital of Whiterun Hold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Whiterun)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Windhelm.png) **[Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)** — A major city in the northeast of Skyrim near the Dunmeth Pass to Morrowind, and the capital of Eastmarch. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Windhelm)
