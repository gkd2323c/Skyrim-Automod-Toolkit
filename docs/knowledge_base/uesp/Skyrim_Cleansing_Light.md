## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md).
2. Kill the boss at the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) lair.
3. Report back to Gunmar.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Gunmar will inform you that he has discovered a vampire lair and will task you with eliminating its head vampire. While you only need to kill the boss vampire in order to scatter its minions, he heartily recommends you thoroughly cleanse the lair of all vampires and thralls. He will then mark a random [vampire lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Vampire_Lairs) on your map.

Travel to the location and dispose of the vampire boss and other enemies. Once you are done, return to Gunmar for your reward: a leveled enchanted item.

## Bugs
- If the radiant system assigns [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md), you will be unable to complete this quest as there is no standard boss set at that location. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
