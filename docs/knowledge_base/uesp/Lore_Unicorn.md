The Unicorn is a mythical creature that is native prey to the [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ao LH-1)</sup> They resemble a white [horse](Lore_Horse.md) with a sharp [horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Unicorn_Horn), which curves slightly and has a delicate spiral pattern. It is commonly described as made of pure alabaster [ivory](Lore_Ivory.md), however in actuality, it is closer to [silver](Lore_Silver.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DGMedora-2)</sup> Although they can be found in the Hunting Grounds, an aspect of Hircine known as Alrabeg the Hunter brings these creatures into the mortal plane of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), to hunt them in new forests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ao LH-1)</sup>

The Unicorn is a very rare creature whose very existence is constantly doubted.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LHLAYQ-3)</sup> Hircine has described the creatures as the first named, but last tamed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBHircine-4)</sup> In a sense, creatures like this are considered the antithesis of the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). It is believed that the death of a unicorn strengthens the elements of darkness, while the death of [Fire Daedra](Lore_Fire_Daedra.md) strengthens the aspects of light.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TLat D-5)</sup> Unicorns are greatly valued as prey by Hircine, as a result have been driven nearly extinct on Nirn by his followers.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

It is said that the Unicorn possess great agility, with the ability to jump over nine-feet and fly in the air, despite lacking wings.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KEI-7)</sup> They are also described as the "brothers of the wind", wielding great speed, though they are not faster than a flying [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KEII-8)</sup> Unicorns are associated with luck.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> While the Unicorn wields immense agility, they are considered rather powerful, too quick to damage in physical combat, wielding dangerously strong hooves and a piercing horn. It is claimed that a single touch of its horn can kill an enemy. However, after the horn is used, it disintegrates, but can be regenerated within moments by the most powerful unicorns.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KEII-8)</sup> Because of its supernatural nature, unicorns can only be harmed by [silver](Lore_Silver.md), [daedric](Lore_Daedric.md), or [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) weapons.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Bearers of the [Huntsman's Warmask](Lore_Huntsman%27s_Warmask.md) were believed to receive visions from Hircine of his aspects. According to [Uraccanach](Lore_Uraccanach.md), the "flank of the unicorn" was among those aspects.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-HW-11)</sup>

## History
In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Dowager-Queen of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md) was in possession of the Great Unicorn's Horn, which was later used to free [Medora Direnni](Lore_Medora_Direnni.md) from her prison on the [Isle of Balfiera](Lore_Isle_of_Balfiera.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DFFM-12)</sup> During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), one of the last unicorns was hunted by the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) who sought the favor of Hircine. It was last sighted at Harcane Grove in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), guarded by a pack of [Minotaurs](Lore_Minotaur.md). The Hero claimed the unicorn's horn and offered it to Hircine, who in turn, gifted them with the [Savior's Hide](Lore_Savior%27s_Hide.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBHircine-4)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Unicorn were thought to have gone extinct on Nirn and were shrouded in legend, but they would remain a favorite subjects for poets and artists.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-BLIce Unicorn-13)</sup> A [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) assassin claims to have met a Unicorn on a moonlit night, and proceeded to "stab [it] in the throat with a crochet needle", though this statement was likely in jest.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SRGabriella-14)</sup> That same year, there were reportedly sightings of a Unicorn had begun to circulate in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), in the fall forest of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md). It was theorized by [Tolfdir](Lore_Tolfdir.md), Master of [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) at the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md), that the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) had transported one of the very last unicorns into the future, to prevent their utter extinction.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SJ-15)</sup> It was later discovered by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) near Lost Prospect Mine, who managed to tame the beast as their own mount and bring it to the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SRCo L-16)</sup>
