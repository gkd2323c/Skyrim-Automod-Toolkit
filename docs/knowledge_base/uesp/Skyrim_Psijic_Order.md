# Psijic Order


Throughout the quests given by the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md), in which the [Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md) acts as a plot device, a monk of the Psijic Order named [Quaranir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir) constantly warns you that the device is not something the world is prepared for and that it is your sole responsibility to prevent anyone channeling powers from the eye at all costs. Their intentions for, as well as their connection with, the Eye of Magnus remains unknown.

The extent of the Order's power is unknown, but members have been shown to be capable of either slowing or stopping time entirely as well as a form of teleportation, though the former ability was said by Quaranir to require great effort on the mage's part and could not be performed for very long. All members of the Psijic Order seen in Skyrim wear their unique eponymous robes, which cannot be obtained legitimately.

## Note
- The Order's name is spelled 'Psijic' in subtitles and in-game texts but is spelled 'Psiijic' in the game's item files (viewable with the Creation Kit). This discrepancy is normally never viewable in-game, as the Order's robes, gloves, boots, and hood are not obtainable without console commands.
- [*Soran's Journal*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal), added by the [Wild Horses](Skyrim_Wild_Horses.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), suggests that the Psijic Order may have transported one of the very last [Unicorns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn) forward in time following their extinction during [Hircine's quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Hircine) in [*Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion).
