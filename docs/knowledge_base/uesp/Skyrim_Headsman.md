[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-npc-Headsman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Headsman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Headsman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Headsman.jpg) The headsman from the opening of the game at Helgen [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-item-Headsman%27s_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Headsman%27s_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Headsman%27s_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Headsman%27s_Axe.jpg) The headsman's axe The **Headsman** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) executioner. He wears a set of unlootable and unplayable [executioner's robes](Skyrim_Executioner%27s_Robes.md), an [executioner's hood](Skyrim_Executioner%27s_Hood.md), [boots](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#000cf8b0), and [gloves](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#000cf8b1), and carries the [headsman's axe](Skyrim_Headsman%27s_Axe.md). He is one of the first NPCs you'll meet in gameplay, during the initial quest, [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md).

After you create your character at the start of the game and join [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md), and the other prisoners near the execution block, you'll watch the headsman decapitate one prisoner before you're called up for your turn. You are forced to make your way to the chopping block and kneel down to see the headsman lift his axe to execute you, but are saved just in time by a [dragon](Skyrim_Alduin.md) attack. The headsman is then knocked down by the force of Alduin landing on the tower nearby. He staggers to his feet, then he gets hit by Alduin's [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout and dies. His corpse can be seen lying on the ground next to the execution block.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- Two [additional headsmen](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Headsman) should have been included in a random [world encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md), but it was never implemented.
- When you first enter [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), you will also witness the execution of [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md) at the hands of [Ahtar](Skyrim_Ahtar.md), the jailor, for treason. He is also a headsman, but has his own separate page.
