[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-menu-Redguard_Elite_Armaments.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Redguard_Elite_Armaments.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Redguard_Elite_Armaments.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Redguard_Elite_Armaments.jpg) Redguard Elite Armaments **Redguard Elite Armaments** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) for [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). It features a quest involving a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) spy network called the [Remnants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Remnants) who have been betrayed to the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) by a corrupt noble. This Creation adds a new armor and weapon set, and the legendary saber [Boneshaver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boneshaver).

## Official Summary
*Connect with a network of Redguard agents known as the Remnants, and help them recover one of their own from the Thalmor. Includes a brand new set of light armor plus two new weapons, such as the legendary Yokudan sword Boneshaver! Created by Elle H and Jose Mc Callum "Shoeburglar." (Quest "Interception" starts by talking to Azadi in Shor's Stone far north of Riften.)*
