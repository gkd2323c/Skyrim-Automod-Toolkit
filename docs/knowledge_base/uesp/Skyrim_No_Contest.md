## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Viriya](Skyrim_Viriya.md) and read the [contest notice](Skyrim_Notice__Fishing_Contest.md).
2. Travel to [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) and make a wager with [Brutius](Skyrim_Brutius.md).
3. Locate the fishing spot east of Morthal.
4. Defend yourself.
5. Read *[Brutius's Journal](Skyrim_Brutius%27s_Journal.md)* and fish up the [Warlock's Ring](Skyrim_Warlock%27s_Ring.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
This quest can be started after [Heart of Gold](Skyrim_Heart_of_Gold.md) has been completed by talking to Viriya:

- **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
- *"You're headed to Morthal. It's all in this note."*

She will then hand you a [notice](Skyrim_Notice__Fishing_Contest.md) about a fishing contest in Morthal. Read it and then head to Morthal to meet up with [Brutius](Skyrim_Brutius.md), speak to him:

- **I'm here for the fishing contest. Are you ready to begin?**
- *"Yeah."*
- **You're Brutius? I expected someone younger. You're just a feeble old man.**
- *"I don't have to take that from you!"*
- **Shall we make a wager, then?**
- *"How much?"*
- **100 gold.**
- **500 gold.**
- **1000 gold!**
- *"A little coin always greases the wheels."*
- **Where shall we hold the contest?**
- *"Here's where you need to go."*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-quest-No_Contest_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Contest_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest_02.jpg) The fishing spot [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-quest-No_Contest_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Contest_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest_03.jpg) The fishing spot with [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md) installed The fishing contest spot is east of Morthal and will be marked on your map. When you arrive you will find out the contest is a trap, with Brutius and two bandits ambushing you. You may have to wait a little while for them to arrive. Brutius and the two bandits are all mages and will use magic attacks against you.

Once you have dispatched of the ambushers, loot the bandits who both have a full set of vagrant robes on and then search Brutius for his [journal](Skyrim_Brutius%27s_Journal.md). The journal tells of how Brutius has been leading other victims to the same ambush and how he dropped his ring in the river. Look for the fishing supplies by the river to fish for the ring. If you don't have a rod, Brutius has one on his body. Eventually you will fish up the [Warlock's Ring](Skyrim_Warlock%27s_Ring.md), ending the quest.

## Notes
- The fishing spot for this quest is just across the river from [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md), added by the [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). This Creation previously [made mention](Lore_Anora%27s_Journal_(old).md) of the Warlock's Ring, but this reference was removed soon after its release. It can therefore be assumed that the tower being fully in frame when you retrieve the ring during this quest is not coincidental.

## Bugs
- If Brutius or one of the bandits is raised before the rest are killed, the quest will be stuck at this stage and Brutius's journal will not be present on his body, no matter if you kill the ambusher after they have been raised. This may occur due a follower like [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) who often raises fallen enemies in battle. <sup>**?**</sup> - If this bug occurs due to your follower, reload a previous save and tell them to wait somewhere far away from the battle, so they will not join the fight and you will be able to advance the quest.
- Brutius may have 2 journals; taking both will make it impossible to read the book to progress. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `help Brutius` to get the [Form ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) of the book, followed by `player.drop <Form ID> 1`. The remaining book will then be readable.
- The contest notice remains marked as a quest item after the quest ends. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Use the console command `player.removeitem [xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 000B2E 1` to remove the item from your inventory.
