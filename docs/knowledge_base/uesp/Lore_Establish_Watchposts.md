Establish Watchposts **by Captain Priscus** *An Imperial officer's orders to establish watchposts in Cyrodiil*
---
Your orders, sergeant, are to take five soldiers and establish watchposts on high ground. Your area of responsibility is the region between [Drakelowe Keep](Lore_Drakelowe_Keep.md) and the village of [Cropsford](Lore_Cropsford.md). Go no farther north than the ruins at [Nornal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nornal).

Track all invaders within this area, regardless of which alliance the troops belong to. Estimates of group size and direction of movement are vital.

Send me daily reports. I expect we'll be at the bridge for some time to come.

— Captain Priscus
