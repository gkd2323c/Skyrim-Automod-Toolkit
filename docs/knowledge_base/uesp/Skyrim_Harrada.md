**Harrada** is an ingredient added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It can be harvested from **Harrada Root**, an aggressive vine found growing in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md). If approached too closely, depending on the angle of the stems, the plant will lash out at you and cause a small amount of health damage. Some individual plants only whip in one direction, while others open like a flower to attack in all directions. When harvested, they will lose only one of their stems and can still attack. They cannot be disabled.
