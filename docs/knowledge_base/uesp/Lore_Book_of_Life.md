# Book of Life

The Book of Life was a census record kept by the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Mentioned in *[A History of Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:A_History_of_Daggerfall)*, it is noted as *"one of the first civilized acts the Nords performed"*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHo D-1)</sup> It is believed to be the first census kept by humans on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The book was used to keep track of the population of newly conquered High Rock,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE-2)</sup> including [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), which was founded by the Nords as a fort-town to support their raids.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE2-3)</sup> On the settlement of Daggerfall the Book of Life says:

- *"North of the Highest bluffs, south of the moors, west of the hills, and east of the sea is called DAGGERFALL. 110 men, 93 women, 13 children under 8 years of age, 58 cows, 7 bulls, 63 chickens, 11 cocks, 38 hogs live here."*

The exact length of the Book of Life remains unknown, but the info on Daggerfall is found on page 933.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHo D-1)</sup>

## Notes
This census book seems to be the Tamrielic equivalent of the [Domesday Book](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Domesday_Book), with Nords replacing Normans and Bretons replacing British.
