[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-item-Shrouded_Robes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Robes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Robes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Robes.jpg) Shrouded Robes Shrouded Robes are the sinister looking cloaks that are designed for use by the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), its faction association clearly displayed through the emblem of the Black Hand emblazoned on the chest of the robes. Besides these ominous robes, this set of equipment also includes [hand wraps](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.md), a [hood](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hood.md), and [shoes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Shoes.md). The robes bear an enchantment that [fortifies destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), making it so that pain inflicting spells require fifteen percent less magicka to cast.
