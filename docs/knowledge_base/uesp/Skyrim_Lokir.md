[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Lokir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lokir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lokir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lokir.jpg) Lokir **Lokir** of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) thief. He is bound in the same prison carriage as [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md), [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) and you on the way to [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) at the start of the game. He stole a horse and was riding it past the border at the time of the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) ambush on the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). Apart from the player, Lokir is the only prisoner on the cart who is not a Stormcloak. When his name is called, he will be overwhelmed by the thought that he's going to be executed for his theft and make a run for it, only to be cut down by archers.

He wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and a pair of [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps), and is bound by a pair of [prisoner's cuffs](Skyrim_Prisoner%27s_Cuffs.md).

## Unused Dialogue
Originally, Lokir's characterization was quite a bit different as to how he's portrayed in the final game. He originally used the Male Sly Cynical voicetype instead of Male Coward, and this matches his earlier character. Unused dialogue for this version of Lokir still exist in the files, although they have no entries in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit), and as such, have no standard subtitles. These have been made from scratch. Also, while almost all of Lokir's dialogue is different, not all of Ralof's is, but it is still included here as to have the scene make sense.

**Lokir:** *"Hey, you. You're finally awake. Guards sure worked you over."* **Ralof:** *"I told those Imperial soldiers you weren't with us. But they just grabbed you. Like that thief over there."* **Lokir:** *"Wait. Who's that gagged fellow in the cart? He looks familiar."* **Ralof:** *"Watch your tongue. You're speaking to Ulfric Stormcloak, the true High King."* **Lokir:** *"That's the man who used the [Voice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Voice) to murder [High King Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md)? The leader of the rebellion?"* **Lokir:** *"Hey! You! Ulfric! You traitor! You're the reason we're here!"* **(to Ulfric)** **Ralof:** *"At least Sovngarde will be waiting for us. Our ancestors. Feasting. Mead."* **Lokir:** *"Gods. What I wouldn't give for some mead right now..."* **Lokir:** *"What brings you into the cold grip of the Empire anyway? I know you're not one of the rebels."* **(to you)** **Ralof:** *"What hold are you from, horse thief? [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md)? [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)? [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md)?"* **Lokir:** *"Why do you care?"* **Ralof:** *"Because there won't be any holds once the Empire has its way. No jarls to rule over them. Just Legion soldiers and martial law. Every man, woman, and child is part of this rebellion, horse thief. Everyone has to fight for the freedom of the [Nine Holds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nine_Holds)."* **Lokir:** *""Freedom of the Nine Holds"? I don't remember the Empire sweeping up every cutpurse in Skyrim before you bastards started butchering their soldiers!"* As your cart ride nears its end and you're made to exit:

**Lokir:** *"Why are we stopping?"* **Ralof:** *"Why do you think? End of the line. Let's go. Shouldn't keep the gods waiting for us."* **Lokir:** *"No, wait, we're not rebels! You gotta tell them, we weren't with you!"* **Ralof:** *"Face your death with some courage, thief."* After Hadvar has checked both Ulfric and Ralof off his list:

**Hadvar:** *"Next in line. Name?"* **Lokir:** *"Lokir, and I'm not a Stormcloak!"* **Hadvar:** *"It says right here you are. I'm sorry."* At this point, Lokir takes off running, yelling:

*"No please, all I did was steal a horse, I'm not a rebel! You can't do this!"* And at this point, possibly even before he could finish the line, Lokir is similarly shot dead while trying to escape. Notably, in this entire exchange, Lokir never mentions his hold or town of origin, and his dialogue also implies that Ulfric was on a different cart than you. Lokir was also meant to have the first speaking line in *Skyrim*, which was later slightly reworded and given to Ralof instead.

## Notes
- Lokir's death is [scripted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scripted_Deaths), making him guaranteed to be the first NPC in the game to die.
- Lokir states he is from Rorikstead, but he has no residence there and is never mentioned by anyone in town.
- A mod called [Lokir's Tomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Developer_Mods_Lokir%27s_Tomb) was created by [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda) as part of their online [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) tutorial. This mod is also briefly mentioned in an official video preview of the Creation Kit and [Steam Workshop](Skyrim_Steam_Workshop.md). According to the video, Lokir was born in 4E 173, which means he was 27 or 28 years old when he died.
- In [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Skyrim_-_The_Adventure_Game)'s card numbered 239 titled "Little Horse Thief", Lokir in his childhood climbs on top of a horse which then runs away with Lokir on top. The child is then found with the horse dead in a clearing by [the players](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md), and Lokir is about to be attacked by a [Mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md). The players however successfully return Lokir to Rorikstead.
