[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-place-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg) Sadri's Used Wares **Sadri's Used Wares** is a general shop in the Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

It is owned by [Revyn Sadri](Skyrim_Revyn_Sadri.md), a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) trader and common level [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) for [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md). It consists of one area only, *[Sadri's Used Wares](#Sadri.27s_Used_Wares)*.

**NOTE**: Like all other stores, Sadri's Used Wares has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietor and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## Sadri's Used Wares
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.jpg) Inside Sadri's Used Wares Without the displayed merchandise the main trading room becomes quite empty and consists mostly of valuable kitchen utensils. The huge counter has a copy of *[Ancestors and the Dunmer](Skyrim_Ancestors_and_the_Dunmer.md)* on top and, hidden behind the counter, a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill book *[2920, Second Seed, v5](Skyrim_2920,_Second_Seed,_v5.md)*. A few weapons are hung on the southeastern wall, including an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md), an [iron battleaxe](Skyrim_Iron_Battleaxe.md), and an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), along with three shields, an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md), a [hide shield](Skyrim_Hide_Shield.md), and a [banded iron shield](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Shield.md). Through the doorway to the south is a storage room with a wardrobe with random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md), an end table with clutter, and the store's [strongbox](Skyrim_Strongbox.md) on a set of shelves.

The doorway to the north leads to Sadri's private quarters where he sleeps every night between midnight and 8am. The southwestern corner contains a small table with a [salt pile](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md), a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), a slice of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), and some [baked potatoes](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md). Along the western wall is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). The adjacent corner has two food sacks and some shelves with several items, including an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md), a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a sample of [scaly pholiota](Skyrim_Scaly_Pholiota.md), a sample of [troll fat](Skyrim_Troll_Fat.md), three [red apples](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md), a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), and the [Business Ledger](Skyrim_Business_Ledger.md). The owned bed stands along the northern wall, with a handy bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) next to the pillow. The northeastern corner has a wardrobe with some assorted clothes. A dresser along the eastern wall contains clothes and has another bottle of wine and a bottle of Nord mead on top.

## Bugs
- If [Revyn Sadri](Skyrim_Revyn_Sadri.md) dies, [Aval Atheron](Skyrim_Aval_Atheron.md) technically becomes owner of Sadri's Used Wares. However, he never takes over running the store: he never enters the store, and even if he were to enter the store, he does not own the store's merchant chest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. [Idesa Sadri](Skyrim_Idesa_Sadri.md) has been selected to fill the backup alias and will take over when Revyn dies.
