[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg) Lake Yorgrim **Lake Yorgrim** is a lake in the eastern part of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md). It is the source of [River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim). Located on the banks of the lake is [Bronze Water Cave](Skyrim_Bronze_Water_Cave.md). The [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md) can be found to the northwest. Due east of the lake is a [dragon burial site](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md) belonging to [Viinturuth](Skyrim_Viinturuth.md).

The [Dwemer ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Ruins) of [Irkgthand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand) run underneath the lake.
