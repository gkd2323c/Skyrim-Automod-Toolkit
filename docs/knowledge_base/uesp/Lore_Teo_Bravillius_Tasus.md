Teo Bravillius Tasus was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) [Alessian](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) centurion who captured the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) city found at the future site of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md). When the Nedes founded a new city atop the Ayleid settlement, they named it "Bravil" in honor of Bravillius.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BP1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTN-2)</sup>
