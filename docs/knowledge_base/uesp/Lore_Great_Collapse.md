# Great Collapse


Nobody is certain of the true cause of the disaster, though some believed the [eruption of Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Year.md) had far-reaching consequences that were only felt years later.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Aren-2)</sup> Others, however, do not believe that, and instead believe the College was responsible.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Korir-5)</sup>
