[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/DF-map-Kozanset.png/200px-DF-map-Kozanset.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Kozanset.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Kozanset.png) Map of Kozanset Kozanset is a small, landlocked polity located in the wider [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It lies in a pocket of jungled land east of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWOTW-2)</sup> surrounded by the historically [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) -controlled countries of [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AUADC1-3)</sup> and [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3-4)</sup> though Kozanset's own allegiances are unknown.

## History
Though the region is primarily populated by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-1)</sup> other minor races and subcultures live in the area, such as the [barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md) of Castle Dhilpt and the Caormja Convergence, and the [giants](Lore_Giant.md) of Shucten Tower.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-5)</sup> The [Khulari](Lore_Khulari.md) clan of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) are active in Kozanset.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-1)</sup> To the west of the town Ramakawan is the [Bazidir Valley](Lore_Bazidir_Valley.md), a wide pass that cuts through the Dragontail Mountains.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWOTW-2)</sup>

By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Barony of Kozanset was ruled by an unknown Baroness and it was neighbored by the Dragontail Mountains to the southwest, Lainlyn to the north, and Totambu to the southeast.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFMap-6)</sup> Unlike most other countries, Kozanset's only city was its capital of the same name. When Hammerfell was incorporated into the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), the barony was given a patron deity in the form of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), who is represented through the [Temple of Kynareth](Lore_Temple_of_Kynareth.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-1)</sup>

When the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) occurred in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), Kozanset was annexed into the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), at the time ruled by Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWITW-7)</sup> By the reign of King [Lhotun](Lore_Lhotun.md), Ramakawan, a settlement in southwest Kozanset, was the site of his hidden jungle fortress. At the tail-end of the Third Era, war broke out with [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) from his fortress, [Scourg Barrow](Lore_Scourg_Barrow.md) and an army was formed to defeat him. The eastern army, led by Digitus Ero, gathered in Ramakawan before they embarked into the Dragontail Mountains to fight [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWOTW-2)</sup>

## The City of Kozanset
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/DF-map-Kozanset_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-map-Kozanset_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Kozanset_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Kozanset_(city).png) Kozanset city in the region [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/DF-place-Kozanset_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-place-Kozanset_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Kozanset_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Kozanset_(city).png) The City of Kozanset [Kozanset](Lore_Kozanset.md) is a small city in the open dunes of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It is a remote, farming settlement of little note, but its most notable native is [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md), a renowned [arcanist](Lore_Arcanist.md) and magical expert, despite his name suggesting otherwise. It is also the capital and the only city in the eponymous barony in south-central [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).
