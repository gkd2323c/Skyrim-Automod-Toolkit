# Aurbic Eye


The College refers to it as the **Eye of Shalidor** or **Eye of Magnus** (sharing a name with [the artifact](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md)), a symbol born from the legacy of [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) that looks upon the College with a burning gaze. Those who join the College have Shalidor's magic imprinted on their bodies invisibly, and as they pass trials marks of the Eye gradually begin to appear on them.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Eo SBM-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-STN-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> According to [Taleon Mythmaker](Lore_Taleon_Mythmaker.md) the College of Winterhold's eye symbolized leadership over both mundane and arcane mysteries.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DEC-8)</sup>

It is worn on circlets and hoods as a "[third](Lore_Third_Eye.md)" eye,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Magnus Art-9)</sup> and those who pass the final test during the trials find Shalidor's eye marked across their brow.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Eo SFM-10)</sup> In the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) lesson of the divine body, the phrase *"The Eye of Magnus is always upon us, in the spells and enchantments that devout Mages conjure."* is recited by religious preachers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMirnor-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOUurkar-12)</sup>
