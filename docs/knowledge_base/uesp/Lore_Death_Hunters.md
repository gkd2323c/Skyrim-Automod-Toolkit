# Death Hunters

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/ON-npc-Death_Hunters.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Death_Hunters.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Death_Hunters.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Death_Hunters.jpg) Death Hunters The Death Hunters were an organization of [vampire](Lore_Vampirism.md) and [werewolf](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) hunters. They operated throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the chaotic times of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DHW-2)</sup> Recruiting from various races, including [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DHA-3)</sup> [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DHW-2)</sup> [Nords](Lore_Nord.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DHAR-4)</sup> [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DHT-5)</sup> [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DHAR-4)</sup> and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DHT-5)</sup> they were driven by a dual motivation of both enjoying the fun of killing and adhering to their faith.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DHW-2)</sup> They utilized [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) and [magic](Lore_Magic.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DHA-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DHS-6)</sup> Engaging in conflicts with both werewolves and vampires, the Death Hunters frequently experienced casualties not only among the supernatural beings they hunted but also within their own ranks.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Encounters-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TW-8)</sup>
