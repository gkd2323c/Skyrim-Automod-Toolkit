[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/AR-quest-Murkwood.jpg/200px-AR-quest-Murkwood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-quest-Murkwood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-quest-Murkwood.jpg) Murkwood Murkwood is the legendary "dark forest that ever moves", located deep in the swamps of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It is specifically located on a river, northeast of [Blackrose](Lore_Blackrose.md) and west of Seaspring.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap Murk-2)</sup>

The [Conclave of Baal](Lore_Conclave_of_Baal.md), an organization based in [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md), can find the current location of the forest by reading the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) with the aid of an ancient tablet. Murkwood was used by [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) as a hiding place for one piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md). The [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) retrieved this piece on their quest to save [Emperor Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup>

The forest is home to many [Medusas](Lore_Medusa.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) farmers trade sacks of [fellrunner](Lore_Fellrunner.md) feathers with hunters. Just a single armband crafted from the pale down of a Fellrunner enables hunters to stealthily navigate the swamps of Murkwood without being detected.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NF-5)</sup>
