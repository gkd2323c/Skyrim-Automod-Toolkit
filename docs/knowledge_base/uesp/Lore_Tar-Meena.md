Tar-Meena, was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) scholar, a Master-Wizard of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), and the Keeper of the Mystic Archives at the [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md), in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She was widely regarded as an expert in [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) cults. She assisted the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) on their quest to defeat the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) by helping research the [Mythic Dawn Commentaries](Lore_Mythic_Dawn_Commentaries.md). Additionally, she assisted the hero in researching [Black Soul Gems](Lore_Soul_Gem.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBPath-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Necro Moon-3)</sup>
