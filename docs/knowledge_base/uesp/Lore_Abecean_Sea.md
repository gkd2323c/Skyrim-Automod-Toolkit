[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/OBR-place-Abecean_Sea.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Abecean_Sea.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Abecean_Sea.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Abecean_Sea.jpg) View of the Abecean Sea from the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) Map featuring the Abecean Sea The Abecean Sea (or the **Abecean** for short) is a body of water to the west of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) that sits between [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md). The sea is encompassed by the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MOWT-1)</sup>

The sea surrounds several islands. [Stirk](Lore_Stirk.md) is the easternmost and lies just off the western coast of Cyrodiil. Farther west is [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), which lies to the south of Hammerfell and is similarly dominated by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). Due west of Stros M'Kai is [The Chain](Lore_The_Chain.md), a series of three islands of which little is known, a lack that also to applies to [Cespar](Lore_Cespar.md), a larger island to the west-northwest. Just northwest of Cespar is [Herne](Lore_Herne_(island).md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MOWT-1)</sup> which was used as a temporary refuge by the Redguards as they migrated across the sea from their ancestral home in [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup> Lastly, at the western ends of the sea, are [The Systres](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Systres) and [Pankor](Lore_Pankor.md). The Systres is a group of three islands northwest of Summerset Isle, and Pankor lies to their northwest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MOWT-1)</sup>

There is an outlying and mysterious region in the Abecean Sea called the Graven Deep. It is tied to the disappearance of several ships over the years. An uncharted island somewhere within it contains an abandoned [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) facility.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONGraven.23Backstory_Description-3)</sup> A whirlpool located somewhere in the Abecean, known as the Maelstrom of Bal, is rumored to be a portal to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). Ships that ride its waters are said to disappear.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Flora and Fauna
Abecean whales have been hunted for their wax since at least the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCAWBW-5)</sup> The sea lends its name to the [Abecean Longfin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Abecean_Longfin), although the species more typically inhabits the waters of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).
