[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-npc-Kodrir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kodrir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kodrir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kodrir.jpg) Kodrir **Kodrir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) who lives and works at [Anga's Mill](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill.md). He works with [Leifur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifur) for the owner of the mill, [Aeri](Skyrim_Aeri.md). He sleeps in [Anga's Mill Common House](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.md).

He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001360d). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House) to the common house as well as a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

He gets up at 7am to start eating his breakfast and does so for one hour. Then he goes out to the mill where he works chopping wood, sharpening axes, and running for water. After a whopping twelve hours of work, he heads home to eat dinner, then he finally goes to bed at midnight.

## Dialogue
He's very unfriendly towards strangers, and will make this very clear when speaking with you.

- *"Strangers are always trouble."*

- *"I don't need to talk to you. Get lost."*

## Conversations
He can sometimes be seen conversing with his co-worker Leifur:

- **Leifur**: *"Whatever happened to [Ennodius](Skyrim_Ennodius_Papius.md)? He just up and left."*
- **Kodrir**: *"Don't know why you're asking me. But I'm glad that damn [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) is gone. Something wrong about him."*
- **Leifur**: *"You're all heart, Kodrir. I bet [Lady Mara](Lore_Mara.md) is weeping a special tear for you right now."*

- **Kodrir**: *"I swear that woman Aeri doesn't appreciate all the work I do for her damn mill."*
- **Leifur**: *"Oh, I've heard this one before. Watch out Divines, poor Kodrir curses you all for giving him life!"*
- **Kodrir**: *"Watch your mouth with me you pup! Who do you think you are?"*
- **Leifur**: *"The man doing your job?"*

## Notes
- Kodrir originally played a larger role in an [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#Freeform Angas Mill A), which would've handled his animosity towards Ennodius. A special bottle of [skooma](Skyrim_Kordir%27s_Skooma.md) related to the quest can still be found in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), although it isn't clear how it would've figured into the quest.
- The assassination target [Ennodius Papius](Skyrim_Ennodius_Papius.md) is considered to be a foe by Kodrir, marked as such by an internal Relationship marker. If you kill him during the [related quest](Skyrim_Kill_Ennodius_Papius.md), Kodrir may [reward you](Skyrim_An_enemy%27s_gratitude.md) for his death ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md)).
