- *Creatures and people up to level 20 flee from combat for 30 seconds.*

**Rout** is an expert level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes creatures and people up to level 20 to flee from combat for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Rout](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 20 pts in 15 ft for 30 secs

## Perks
- [Illusion Dual Casting](Skyrim_Illusion_Dual_Casting.md), multiplies effective level by 2.2 (rounded down).
- [Animage](Skyrim_Animage.md), animals 8 levels higher can be affected.
- [Kindred Mage](Skyrim_Kindred_Mage.md), people 10 levels higher can be affected.
- [Aspect of Terror](Skyrim_Aspect_of_Terror.md), all targets 10 levels higher can be affected
- [Master of the Mind](Skyrim_Master_of_the_Mind.md), allows spell to work on undead, daedra, and automata.

Note that most of these perks will stack, so the overall effective values for the spell with perks are as follows:

| | Max Lvl | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Single-cast | Dual-cast | |
| Default | 20 | 44 |
| Animage | 28 | 61 |
| Kindred Mage | 30 | 66 |
| Aspect of Terror | 30 | 66 |
| Animage + Aspect of Terror | 38 | 83 |
| Kindred Mage + Aspect of Terror | 40 | 88 |

*Master of the Mind* will *not* stack with *Kindred Mage* or *Animage*, which is primarily of interest when attempting to cast spells on [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md), when it will come up that Kindred Mage does not apply, even though vampires have black souls. [Death Hounds](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md) are not affected by *Animage*, but they are not animals to begin with; more generally, the perks will not combine when fighting people or animals raised by necromancers.
