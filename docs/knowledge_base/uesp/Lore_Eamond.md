Eamond was the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) King of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) in the late sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and the second monarch from [House Guimard](Lore_Guimard.md). After his father, King [Blaise](Lore_Blaise_Guimard.md), allied with King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) during [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), Eamond was wed to one of Emeric's cousins, [Arzhela](Lore_Arzhela.md), as a reward. In [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568), Eamond ascended to the throne after his father's death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-1)</sup> During his reign, he and Arzhela had two children, Prince Adrien and Princess [Elara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elara).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIEGTB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARE-3)</sup> He had a brother named Renchant.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAntieve-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Evermore faced concurrent invasions from the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md)'s [Seventh Legion](Lore_Seventh_Legion.md), led by Magus-General [Septima Tharn](Lore_Septima_Tharn.md), in the south,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BSo HR-5)</sup> and the Dark Witness [Reachman](Lore_Reachman.md) clan in the north.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAdrien-6)</sup> There are conflicting accounts of Eamond's actions during this time; one source states that Eamond gathered his troops at the [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md) and mounted a defense against the Seventh Legion.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BSo HR-5)</sup> Another source claims that Eamond and his forces fought the Reachmen at Northglen, where he and Adrien were ultimately killed in battle.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAdrien-6)</sup> Around this time, Elara was ambushed and presumed dead.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAntieve-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONStenwick-7)</sup>

After his death, with Adrien and Elara presumed dead, Arzhela became the ruler of Evermore, but she stepped aside and allowed Eamond's brother, Duke Renchant, to serve as regent, as she believed that he was better suited to handle the city's defense against the Reachman invasion.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONArzhela-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONGlarikha-9)</sup> As was tradition for the Kings of Evermore, Eamond's body was interred in the Bangkorai Garrison.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAdrien-6)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONArzhela-8)</sup> After Eamond's death, he was remembered fondly,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONBugbekh-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONMoumou-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONNyomie-12)</sup> but the rest of the royal family was slandered in what was most likely Imperial propaganda.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARE-3)</sup>
