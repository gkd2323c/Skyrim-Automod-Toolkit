The Founding of Zuuk **by [Ashalaku](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ashalaku), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)** *On the Kothringi tribesman Zuuk and the settlement named after him*
---
Lord [Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(person).md) returned to [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) after the fall of [Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md), founding a small settlement in the Shadowfen. After five generations of prosperity, our ancestors renamed it [Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(place).md) in honor of our Lord Founder. The original name of the settlement was lost to time.

We know little of Zuuk's time in the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md), but his wealth, honor, and generosity were renowned among the [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md). May his name be on the lips of his descendants for another five generations.
