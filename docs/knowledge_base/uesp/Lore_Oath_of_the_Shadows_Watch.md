Oath of the Shadows Watch
---
You are defenders of [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md).

You are enemies of the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md).

You are soldiers.

You serve your commanders and none other.

You will unite the [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md).

You will give your life.

You will destroy the [Hollow](Lore_Hollow.md).

Your name will be remembered forever.
