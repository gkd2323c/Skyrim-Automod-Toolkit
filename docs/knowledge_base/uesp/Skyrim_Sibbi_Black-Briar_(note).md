Sibbi Black-Briar **by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)** *A letter to Riften's steward outlining how a Black-Briar is to be treated in "prison"* *
---
A quick note regarding my son, [Sibbi](Skyrim_Sibbi_Black-Briar.md). He's to be treated as I'd expect you to treat any one of the Black-Briar family and all of his requests are to be honored without question. However, if he wishes to be released, you are not to do so unless I expressly authorize it. Sibbi needs to learn his place, and the only way that will happen is if he's kept under lock and key.

[Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)
