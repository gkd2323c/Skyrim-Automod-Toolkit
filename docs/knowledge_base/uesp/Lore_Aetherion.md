Aetherion is an experimental [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) created by the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) of [Skyreach](Lore_Skyreach.md) while they were researching the power of [nirncrux](Lore_Nirncrux.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup> It took the form of an ethereal [Daedric Titan](Lore_Daedric_Titan.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAe-2)</sup> a Daedric perversion of the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) that was created by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DDTT-3)</sup> Aetherion was close to immortal, and would simply condense back into a core of [Aetherial](Lore_Aetherius.md) energy when its corporeal form was destroyed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOUncaged-4)</sup> It was imprisoned within Skyreach Pinnacle in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) along with the lesser Celestials some time after [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808), when [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) invaders sealed the Nedic wards.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Scaled Court](Lore_Scaled_Court.md) unsealed the ruins in their attempt to understand the Nedes' usage of nirncrux.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup> This freed the dangerous Celestials within, forcing the cultists to retreat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup> The expedition leader, Mederic Vyger, subsequently left the Court and attempted to set things right.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup> He enlisted the aid of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and re-entered the ruins to fight back against the Celestials and restore the wards.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOUncaged-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMV-1)</sup> Aetherion appeared at the very end of the ritual in an attempt to stop Vyger, but the intruders destroyed his core and the wards were successfully restored.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOUncaged-4)</sup>

Following the restoration of the ancient wards in the Pinnacle and the defeat of Aetherion, he was imprisoned again in Skyreach.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOUncaged-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Despite the fact that Aetherion was created in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), he appears as a Daedric Titan. However, some sources state that the first Titan only came into existence during the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
