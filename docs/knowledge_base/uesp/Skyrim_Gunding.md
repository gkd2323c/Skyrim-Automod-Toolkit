[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-npc-Gunding.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gunding.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunding.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunding.jpg) Gunding **Gunding** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) trying to make a living in [Whistling Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whistling_Mine). He tries to convince himself that the mine can make money, and overworks himself trying to find anything.

He wears standard [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) with [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and carries an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

He sleeps on one of the available bedrolls near the entrance from 8pm to 8am. When he is awake he will wander around the mine, mining the few ore veins, using the cooking pot, or sitting at the table.

## Dialogue
When spoken to, Gunding will discuss the mine with:

- *"Be careful where you step in the mine. Don't want to cover up some valuable ore, or gems, or... Or something..."*
- *"Any time I get tired of working, I just think that maybe that next swing will make me rich. It keeps me going."*
- *"It's going to happen soon, I just know it. We'll strike something better than iron. Silver, or maybe even gold. You'll see."*
