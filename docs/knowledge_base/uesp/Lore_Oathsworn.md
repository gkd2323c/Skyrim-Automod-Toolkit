# Oathsworn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/ON-place-Oathsworn_Pit.jpg/200px-ON-place-Oathsworn_Pit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Oathsworn_Pit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Oathsworn_Pit.jpg) The Oathsworn Pit The Oathsworn are an elite group of Orcish combatants devoted to [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md). They are based out of their training ground in the Oathsworn Pit located in the [Reach](Lore_Reach.md). They are considered to be among the most fearsome warriors in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and are masters of multiple forms of combat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lot P-1)</sup>
