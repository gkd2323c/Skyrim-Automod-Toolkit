[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Black Marsh [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/ON-place-Onkobra_Kwama_Mine.jpg/200px-ON-place-Onkobra_Kwama_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Onkobra_Kwama_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Onkobra_Kwama_Mine.jpg) The Onkobra Kwama Mine Onkobra River (sometimes known as the **Onkabra**) <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CO-1)</sup> is a fast-moving stream that runs in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It source is found near the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). From there it flows eastward, in the region of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) along a muddy and dysfunctional road before entering the dark center of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

There is another portion of the Onkobra in the region of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md), south of the port-town of [Alten Corimont](Lore_Alten_Corimont.md).

The [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) had established a [Kwama Mine](Lore_Kwama.md) called the Onkobra Kwama Mine, but when Argonian slaves were able to overwhelm their slavers, they took over the Kwama Mines in Black Marsh, including the Onkobra Mine. This, however, did not end successfully. All the while, armillary spheres showcasing the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) etched in Onkobra Kwama Eggs were in circulation in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Red clay of the Onkobra delta makes for very distinctive pottery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CO-1)</sup>
