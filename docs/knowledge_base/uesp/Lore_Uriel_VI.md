Uriel Septim VI was the eighteenth [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for thirty years, from [3E 290](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_290) to [3E 320](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_320). He was preceded by his father, [Uriel V](Lore_Uriel_V.md), and was succeeded by his sister, [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup>

## Biography
Uriel Septim was born in [3E 285](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_285) to Emperor [Uriel V](Lore_Uriel_V.md) and his imperial consort, [Thonica](Lore_Thonica.md). Through his father's morganatic marriage, Uriel had two younger sisters, the twins [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md) and [Eloisa](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eloisa_Septim).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup> In [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288), Uriel V launched an invasion of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md) but was killed at the Battle of [Ionith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ionith) in [3E 290](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_290).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RDa I-4)</sup> After Uriel V's death, five-year-old Uriel was crowned Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup>

Following Uriel VI's coronation, Thonica was given a restricted regency until he reached his majority. The [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) retained the real power, however, as they had since the days of [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md). During Thonica's regency, the council enjoyed the unlimited and unrestricted power to enact laws and generate profits. Uriel VI slowly assumed positions of responsibility over the years, but both the council and Thonica, who enjoyed even her limited regency, were reluctant to allow him to rule.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup> Finally, in [3E 307](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_307), Uriel VI was allowed to rule on his own, but his position granted him little power except the imperial veto.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup>

Uriel VI regularly and vigorously exercised the imperial veto. He also used defunct spy networks and guard units to intimidate and coerce difficult members of the Elder Council. Morihatha was his staunchest ally, especially after her marriage to Baron [Ulfe Gersen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ulfe_Gersen) of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) brought her considerable wealth and influence. By [3E 313](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_313), Uriel VI could claim that he truly ruled Tamriel, having subdued the Elder Council and restored the emperor's power. As the Sage [Ugaridge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ugaridge) said, "Uriel V conquered [Esroniet](Lore_Esroniet.md), but Uriel VI conquered the Elder Council."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup>

Uriel VI and his three successors, Morihatha, [Pelagius IV](Lore_Pelagius_IV.md), and [Uriel VII](Lore_Uriel_VII.md), were suspicious enough of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) to refuse to allow their ambassadors from the [Isle of Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) within the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FOA-5)</sup> At some point, he is claimed to have journeyed to [Lynpan March](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lynpar_March) in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) with his sister and encountered King [Dead Wolf-Deer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dead_Wolf-Deer).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2E-6)</sup>

In [3E 320](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_320), Uriel VI died after falling from his horse and could not be saved by the finest Imperial healers, leading to Morihatha assuming the [Ruby Throne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ruby_Throne).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup> Morihatha finished the work that Uriel VI had begun and made the [Imperial Province](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) a true government under the emperor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup>
