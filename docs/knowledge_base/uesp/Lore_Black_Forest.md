[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/CT-Events-Halloween-Witches_Festival_2_%28Large%29.png/200px-CT-Events-Halloween-Witches_Festival_2_%28Large%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Halloween-Witches_Festival_2_(Large).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Halloween-Witches_Festival_2_(Large).png) The Black Forest The Black Forest is a forest in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) that was the home of [Morga](Lore_Morga.md), the Witch of the Black Forest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Desc-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ruling-2)</sup> Morga used the forest as a place to perform an insidious ritual taken over from her rival "the Warlock". She tricked the inhabitants of a [kingdom](Lore_Hero.md#Dynasty_of_Odar_the_Brave) bordering [Bloodfall](Lore_Bloodfall.md) into assisting her in defeating her rival, then began kidnapping its inhabitants for her ritual.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ruling-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Disappeared-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Wo TBF-4)</sup>

Eventually the inhabitants of the kingdom managed to confront and defeat Morga at her fortress of **Black Forest Keep**, which was guarded by many of her [Goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) and [undead](Lore_Undead.md) minions.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Wo TBF-4)</sup>
