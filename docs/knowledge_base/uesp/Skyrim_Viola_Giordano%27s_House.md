[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-place-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg) Viola Giordano's house **Viola Giordano's House** is the home of [Viola Giordano](Skyrim_Viola_Giordano.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)'s Valunstrad district.

Viola Giordano is the sole occupant of the large two-story Nordic-style house in one of the better neighborhoods of the city directly opposite the [house of Clan Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.md) and to the north of the graveyard and [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm).md), but she rarely spends much time there.

## Viola Giordano's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House.jpg) The interior of Viola Giordano's House The house is completely open-plan downstairs with a single room extending the length of the house and support pillars along the right-hand third of the house. The house has many food sacks around the place. Upon entering there is a chair in the northwestern corner, with some crates beside it and a [drum](Skyrim_Drum.md) on the floor. There is a wooden table in an alcove on the north wall, with a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md), a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) and [plate](Skyrim_Plate.md) on top. At the other end of the room is a matching alcove with another table in it, which has a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a [skeever tail](Skyrim_Skeever_Tail.md), two [Nordic barnacles](Skyrim_Nordic_Barnacle.md), three [rock warbler eggs](Skyrim_Rock_Warbler_Egg.md), a [salt pile](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md), a [pheasant breast](Skyrim_Pheasant_Breast.md), a rack of [dog meat](Skyrim_Dog_Meat.md) and a fillet of [horker meat](Skyrim_Horker_Meat.md) on the table and a bowl of [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) on the floor under it. To the left of the alcove is a set of shelves holing a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) and three [goblets](Skyrim_Goblet.md).

On the rear wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging in front of it and tables on either side. The table to the left has a salt pile on top and a basket in front containing a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md) and [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md), while the table to the right has a pile of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) including two loose pieces on top. There is a long wooden table and benches in front of the fireplace with two bottles of alto wine, a bowl of [red](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md) and [green apples](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md), a plate of three [salmon steaks](Skyrim_Salmon_Steak.md), another with a [baked potato](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md) and [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), a salt pile and two goblets. In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table with a mead barrel on top, with another alcove to the right with a long unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) in it and a set of shelves to the right holding various pieces of cheese and a bottle of Nord mead.

The stairs leading up to the top floor are against the south wall with a wall screening them from downstairs. On this south wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of alto wine and three common books, next to this is a chair. There are a couple of large empty vases next to the pillars and to the right when entering is a dresser which is used as the drop off point for returning the ring without Viola's notice which has a [flute](Skyrim_Flute.md) on top of it. There is a fourth alcove to the left of the dresser with a few barrels in it and some more crates against the south wall, to the left of the alcove.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Viola_Giordano%27s_House_02.jpg) The upstairs of Viola Giordano's house At the top of the stairs there is an alcove on the right with three [cow hides](Skyrim_Cow_Hide.md) piled on the floor. In front is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Along the edge of the stairwell are two more large empty vases and a low table and chair. On this table is a [solution of extra magicka](Skyrim_Solution_of_Extra_Magicka.md), a copy of *[The Book of Daedra](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Daedra.md)* and some [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and cheese. Against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a square wooden table to the right of it and a copy of *[Spirit of Nirn](Skyrim_Spirit_of_Nirn.md)* on top. Against the west wall, to the left of a doorway into [Viola](Skyrim_Viola_Giordano.md)'s bedroom are a couple of barrels and a long end table with a [lute](Skyrim_Lute.md) on top. To the right, against the north wall, is a set of shelves holding three common books and a bottle of each, alto wine, [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) and Nord mead. To the right of this is a chair and farther along an empty open crate and then an end table which has a [leather strip](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md) and a fancy [jug](Skyrim_Jug.md) and [candlestick](Skyrim_Candlestick.md) on top, with a drum on the floor beside it. Farther to the right, next to another alcove is a set of shelves holding seven more common books.

The L-shaped bedroom through the doorway at the western end of the floor contains a double bed coming out from the west wall with a long unlocked chest at the foot of the bed. To the left in the southwestern corner is an end table with a wash basin on a stand around the corner, with a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) on the floor beside it. To the right of another alcove to the south is a set of shelves holding a [potion of regeneration](Skyrim_Potion_of_Regeneration.md). In the alcove are two corner shelves with a bottle of wine on one and a solution of extra magicka on the other. In the middle of this space is a large round wooden table with a bottle of alto wine, a jug and goblet and bread and cheese on top. Beside the doorway is a bookcase holding nine common books, while on the other side of it is an open crate containing two pairs of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). There is another long unlocked chest in yet another alcove to the north with a tall wardrobe to the left of the alcove with a small wardrobe on the west wall to the left of the larger one.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom right of the entrance door and another one to the right before the arch in front of her house.
- [Vignar Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md) and his court (i.e., [Brill](Skyrim_Brill.md) and [Olfina Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Olfina_Gray-Mane.md)), as well as [Avulstein Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Avulstein_Gray-Mane.md), were supposed to go here if exiled, but it is impossible for this to happen, so they will never be seen here.
