[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-flora-Blue_Mountain_Flower.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Blue_Mountain_Flower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Blue_Mountain_Flower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Blue_Mountain_Flower.jpg) A blue mountain flower plant **Blue Mountain Flower** is harvested from the blue-flowered variety of mountain flower. Other varieties of mountain flower yield [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and [yellow mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, but the flowers have different alchemical properties. The blue, purple, and red mountain flowers are commonplace in the foothills and lower elevations of mountains (below the snowline). However, blue mountain flowers also occur above the snowline, making them some of the few plants that can survive in the snow (along with [snowberries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry)).

Blue mountain flowers and [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) contain both [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) and [Fortify Health](Skyrim_Fortify_Health.md) effects, allowing easy creation of dual-effect potions, especially since both ingredients are commonplace.

They can be used in conjunction with a [blue butterfly wing](Skyrim_Blue_Butterfly_Wing.md) and a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md) to create the most valuable (monetarily) mixture in the game.

## Ingredients
87 guaranteed samples can be found in 45 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 in [Solitude Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Catacombs) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 5 in [the Katariah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Katariah) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 4 in [Aeri's House](Skyrim_Aeri%27s_House.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 4 around [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- When gathering ingredients at night, note that Purple and Blue Mountain Flowers look nearly identical in the darkness. To avoid confusion, bring a light source (such as a torch, or the [candlelight](Skyrim_Candlelight.md) spell) to differentiate between them.
