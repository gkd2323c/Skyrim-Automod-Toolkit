# Kahvozein's Fang

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Kahvozien%27s Fang.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Kahvozien%27s Fang.png) Kahvozein's Fang (Skyrim) Kahvozein's Fang is a [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) dagger that dates back to the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). It is legendary throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and is known to be sharp enough to make precise carvings from the [scales](Lore_Dragon_Scales.md) of a [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-1)</sup> It was created by a particular sect of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md), one that worshipped the eponymous Kahvozein. They were very fond of ritual sacrifices, so much so that the high priest spent much time sharpening this dagger so that it was always ready for its next victim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-1)</sup>

The dagger fell into obscurity and was lost in one of the Dragon Priest monasteries. However, in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), an [explorer](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) of [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) dragon priest tombs discovered Kahvozein's Fang during their adventures across Skyrim. Their pursuit of magical education and interest in [the school of Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) brought them to [Tolfdir](Lore_Tolfdir.md), the Master Wizard of Alteration at the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md), who sent them to acquire the dagger and harvest Dragon Heartscales in exchange for the strongest spell of that magic class, Dragonhide.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOARS-2)</sup>
