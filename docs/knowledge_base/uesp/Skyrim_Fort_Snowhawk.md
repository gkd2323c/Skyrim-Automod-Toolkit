[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-place-Fort_Snowhawk.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Snowhawk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Snowhawk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Snowhawk.jpg) Fort Snowhawk **Fort Snowhawk** is a medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) west of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) initially occupied by [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) and [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). It contains two zones: *[Fort Snowhawk](#Fort_Snowhawk)* and *[Fort Snowhawk Prison](#Fort_Snowhawk_Prison)*.

The fort will be taken over by whoever wins the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), or by the faction currently holding Morthal if you first clear it of necromancers and skeletons.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The courtyards outside the fort are quite confusing, with many small interconnected areas with arches and flights of stairs leading to different levels. You can tackle this eroding stronghold in a number of ways: by storming the front gate, slipping in through the hole in the southeast wall, or leaping over any of the crumbling battlements. The main arch leading into the courtyard is to the south, with several barricades in front, and the main entrance into the fort opposite. A host of [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) and their [skeletal minions](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) defend the fort's exterior. There are numerous [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels around the fort as well as a [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) and [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush growing in the courtyard and several places where the walls have crumbled that have been reinforced with wooden palisades. The western quadrant includes two gated stables, while the eastern quadrant has a covered well, with a wooden table beside it and two [long bows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) and a quiver of [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) on top. Opposite the table are two archery targets and a practice dummy. To the left of these is the entrance to *[Fort Snowhawk Prison](#Fort_Snowhawk_Prison)* with an alternative ground-level entrance to the interior of the fort opposite. Beside the well is a flight of stairs up, with a [pressure plate](Skyrim_Pressure_Plate.md) at the top, that doesn't appear to have any effect, with a campfire and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging over it nearby. There is also another table here with an [imperial shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Shield) on top, regardless of who controls the fort. A third entrance to the interior of the fort can be found on an upper level to the north. A ruined tower is in the northeastern corner of the fort with an expert-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) among the masonry, take care as it can be a bit tricky getting back out, especially if overencumbered. You'll need to jump onto the chest then jump out or fast travel if no enemies are around.

### Fort Snowhawk
The main entrance to the south leads into a wide passage with a wooden door opposite that is barred from the other side and stairs leading up to the left and down to the right. Care is needed when climbing the stairs to the left as there is a [frost rune](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Rune) trap at the bottom of the stairs that leads to the upper entrance which is guarded by two skeletons. Beside the entrance is a cupboard with a [Scroll of Circle of Protection](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Circle_of_Protection), a bowl of [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) on the shelves. Beside the cupboard is a ladder to a trapdoor out on top of the main tower, there is nothing out there apart from the view. Backtracking down the stairs, to the right of the entrance is a cupboard with a random empty [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), a set of [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes), and four common [books](Skyrim_Books.md) including *[An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_An_Explorer%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* which may add several places to your main map, unless you have already found them. By the top of the stairs leading down is a barrel of [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). At the bottom a flooded passage heads to the northeast past a junction, with a decrepit kitchen on the right and a short passage on the left that ends after a couple of paces because it has been boarded up. Ahead is a flight of stairs up to a wooden gate with a button on the right that lowers it. The kitchen contains a wardrobe, a set of shelves holding two [potions of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina) and an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace). Beside the shelves is a cupboard that holds only [ruined books](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book), but with a random [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe) on the floor in front. Against the side wall is a wooden table with a random [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) stabbed into it and two [charred skeever hides](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide) on a plate on top, while hanging above are two bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). In the eastern corner is an alcove containing an adept-locked chest.

Beyond the wooden gate is a large room containing seven unowned single beds. There are double doors opposite leading outside at ground-level opposite the entrance to the prison. There are small counters either side of the passage, with [sabre cat pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt) on the one and a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two bunches of dried elves ear hanging from racks over the other. There is dresser against the southeast wall with a *[Spell Tome: Candlelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Candlelight)* on top. In the southern and western corners are stairs leading up to a balcony over the passage, with two doors leading off of it.

The lower of the two leads to a two-level room containing a level necromancer and several crafting stations. There is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) opposite the door with a small unit beside it, a counter against the northeast wall, a cupboard in the corner and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) in front. There are stairs leading up to the upper level, where there a total of five sets of shelves, an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) and a long wooden table. On the unit, shelves and counter are a total of; two [butterfly wings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly_Wing), two [blue butterfly wings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly_Wing), a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), two [taproots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot), a sample of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton), a [thistle branch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch), a sample of [bleeding crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown), a sample of [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot), a random potion of magicka, a random [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), two sets of black mages robes, three bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), three bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum), three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md), twenty-nine books, including a copy of *[The Aetherium Wars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars)*, a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), a sprig of snowberries, several cheese wheels, an iron mace, a potion of minor healing, a potion of minor stamina, and two food sacks. On the table is a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer%27s_Letter) that explains what happened at the fort recently and where the dead [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) came from, a copy of [Fort Snowhawk quarters key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Snowhawk_Quarters_Key) and a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Dance in Fire, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v2)*. There is a [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) banner hanging above the shelves even when the fort is held by the necromancers.

There is also a door to the southeast leading into a passage that leads from the other door on the previous balcony toward the southwest. The passage contains two more sets of shelves separated by a wooden table with a small unit opposite, these hold in total; four loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a sample of bleeding crown, a [blue mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower), two bowls of [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm), a sample of [mudcrab chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin), two bunches of lavender, a [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) flower, a bowl of bone meal and six more common books. At the end of the passage are two doors; one expert-locked in the front and an unlocked one on the left, that leads into a bedroom containing a leveled necromancer unless you've already dealt with them. The bedroom also contains an unowned double bed in the eastern corner with a wooden table to the left and an apprentice-locked chest at the foot of the bed. On the table are two deathbell flowers, a *[Spell Tome: Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Raise_Zombie)*, three common books and another copy of the Fort Snowhawk quarters key. There is a dresser and wardrobe in the room both containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and behind the door a small shelf with an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) and two pieces of [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root).

Through the expert-locked door, which is opened using the key taken from the bedroom and crafting room, is a large round room with an altar in the center and the boss leveled necromancer wandering around it. There is a skeleton lying on the altar and a random [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) on the altar. An unlocked boss chest is against the wall to the southeast, with a random [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer) beside it on the floor. There are a total of four sets of shelves around the room holding a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, twenty-four more books, a sprig of snowberries, two random soul gems; one filled, one empty and several food sacks. To the southwest is a door barred from this side, with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on the floor in front. This opens opposite the main exit from the fort.

### Fort Snowhawk Prison
You may find the prison deserted. The entrance leads directly into a room that has suffered quite extensive damage from a cave-in. There are two torture racks among the masonry and behind these is a warhammer randomly chosen from a leveled list, and farther back, in an area which can be reached with a bit of jumping, is a set of shelves that have fallen flat that holds two random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), a random empty soul gem, a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), and some ruined books. On your right when entering is a blood-soaked table with the corpse of a bandit and a random empty soul gem on top. Beside this is a cupboard with two random soul gems; one empty, one filled, and a [scroll of Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Raise_Zombie) on the shelves and another medium coin purse hidden behind. A doorway to the northeast has an [elemental rune](Skyrim_Traps.md#Elemental_Runes) in front of it.

Through the doorway is another blood-soaked table with a bowl of bone meal and an Orcish mace on top. Hanging above are two small cages, one contains a skeleton and the other another dead bandit, both of which can be looted. There are curved stairs leading down to the right. At the bottom is a large fireplace with an apprentice-locked chest beside it and a cupboard to the right of that holding another random empty soul gem, a random potion of magicka, a random [potion of resist element](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire), a [cow hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cow_Hide) and a food sack. A long wooden table and chair are to the left of the passage leading to the cell, on top of which is a [satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel), a random [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md), a copy of *[The Firmament](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament)* and a small coin purse. Along the passage there is an arcane enchanter in a large cell with a torture rack and masses of human bones and opposite is an empty cell. At the end of the passage another cell on the left contains an unlocked chest, a cell containing the bodies of two bandits lying on hay piles and a third cell door with a tunnel behind leading to an alternative exit. There are a number of fungi along the tunnel and a ledge that stops you from using this as an entrance. Take care dropping off the ledge as there are four [bear traps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap) on the lower level as well as the corpse of another bandit. There are a total of four bleeding crown, six [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) and two [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) fungi.

## Notes
- It is very possible for the necromancers to exit an area as you're entering it or to flee from battle (via a loading screen door). In some cases, one leaves but when you follow there will be more than one.
- The main tower has three entrances. The top door contains two skeletons, the middle door (front entrance) contains a magic trap and a necromancer, and the lowest door (across from the prison door) contains a necromancer and a skeleton.
- Fort Snowhawk also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Snowhawk) as a settlement in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)*, and is mentioned in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. For historical information, see the [lore article](Lore_Snowhawk.md).

## Bugs
- If this is not the first [Civil War Fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md#Civil_War_Forts) you clear, after clearing it of [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) and [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), soldiers will never garrison and enemies respawn instead. This is due to a hidden quest not ending after clearing the first Civil War Fort. See this [talk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:talk_Fort_Hraggstad) section for info. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The console command `resetquest CWFort Observer` can be used to reset the hidden quest, then clear the fort again. Repeat this for every Civil War Fort you want to be garrisoned.
