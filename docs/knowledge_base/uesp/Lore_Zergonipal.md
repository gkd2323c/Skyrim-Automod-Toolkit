[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/MW-place-Zergonipal.jpg/200px-MW-place-Zergonipal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Zergonipal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Zergonipal.jpg) Zergonipal circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Zergonipal is a [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) located along the northern coast of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), deep in the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) and just east of [Bthuand](Lore_Bthuand.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> The ruin's interior contains a shrine dedicated to [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md), [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Outcasts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Zergonipal was located next to an active [lava flow](Lore_Foyada.md). The area was plagued by hostile [flame atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md), as well as less dangerous vermin such as [fiendroths](Lore_Fiendroth.md), [fetcherflies](Lore_Fetcherfly.md), and [skavengers](Lore_Skavenger.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> A crafting site located at Zergonipal was believed to imbue items crafted here with a certain artfulness, perhaps the result of local reality being transformed by the [divine](Lore_Gods.md) spark of creativity exercised by the master craftworkers who had come before.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

As the threat from [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) began to grow in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) were no longer able to guard isolated Daedric ruins like Zergonipal.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the ruins had become occupied by a hostile group of [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) worshippers and their [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) minions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>
