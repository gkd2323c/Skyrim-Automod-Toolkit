# Ruby Throne

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/ON-load-White-Gold_Tower_Throne_Room.jpg/200px-ON-load-White-Gold_Tower_Throne_Room.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-White-Gold_Tower_Throne_Room.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-White-Gold_Tower_Throne_Room.jpg) The Throne Room (ESO) The Ruby Throne (sometimes called the **Dragon Throne**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGArchive-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ocato Dia-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mankar Dia-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBMD-4)</sup> **Imperial Throne**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFC-5)</sup> **Throne of the [Arena](Lore_Tamriel.md)**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARMIS-6)</sup> or **Throne of Cyrodiil**)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-To C-7)</sup> is the royal seat of the [Cyrodilic Emperor](Lore_Emperors.md) at the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md) in the [Cyrodiil City](Lore_Cyrodiil_City.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TOC-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-COTFC4-9)</sup> The Throne Room is located at the base of the palace and is considered to be the most important metaphysical focal point in [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). Some would say that it exists almost adjacent to all of time and space in [aedric](Lore_Aedra.md) creation.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONLoad Throne-10)</sup>

## History
It is that after the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) sorcerer-kings dismembered [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) into eight pieces, they left his head in the White-Gold Tower's throne room for [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md) to find it later. Together, they exchanged their last words. Pelinal spoke of his regrets and [Umaril](Lore_Umaril.md)'s potential return.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TSOP7-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TA-12)</sup>

The [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) controlled the Ruby Throne for a thousand years.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-BSHR-13)</sup> After the fall of the Alessian Order, King Desynan of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) placed a puppet emperor on the Ruby Throne to manage the Imperial City, but over time, Cyrodiil became lawless and a shadow of its former glory.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-OTWOR-14)</sup> No one sat on the throne for the longest time until [Reman I](Lore_Reman_I.md) appeared in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). According to the stories in the *[Remanada](Lore_Remanada.md)*, the shepherdess named [Sed-Yenna](Lore_Sed-Yenna.md) brought him as a child, along with the Amulet of Kings to the Imperial Palace, and placed him on the Ruby Throne. At that moment, he spoke as an adult, "I AM CYRODIIL COME".<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Remanada-15)</sup> Years later, the last of his line, [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md) was assassinated on his throne in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) by his mistress, [Corda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Corda), according to the historical-fiction, *[2920, The Last Year of the First Era](Lore_2920,_The_Last_Year_of_the_First_Era.md)*.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-2920V12-16)</sup> [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) then assumed throne, followed by his son, [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Lot D-17)</sup>

In [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577), Duke [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) and his rebellion attacked the Imperial City to overthrow the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) and claim the title of Emperor. Varen stabbed Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md) through the heart and watched him die at the base of the Ruby Throne. Immediately after, he declared himself Emperor and took up Leovic's wife, [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md) as his own.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-COTFC4-9)</sup>
