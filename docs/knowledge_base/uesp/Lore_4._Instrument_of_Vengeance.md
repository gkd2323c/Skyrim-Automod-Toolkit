4. Instrument of Vengeance *A plaque commemorating a series of events involving the All Flags Navy*
---
Between [1E 2249](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2249) and [1E 2259](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2259), the shipyards worked night and day to turn out the massive number of vessels needed to bolster the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md). By [2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260), the armada numbered some 130 ships of various sizes flying under the banners of the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md), [Colovians](Lore_Colovia.md), [Elves](Lore_Elves.md), [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), and even freebooters and mercenaries. These same nations also sent thousands of sailors, marines, adventurers, and support personnel in perhaps the greatest allied action in [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) history. The fleet was ready. It was time to unleash Tamriel's instrument of vengeance on the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).

With the powerful Elf mage [Syrabane](Lore_Syrabane.md) at his side, Baron-Admiral [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) issued the order and the allied armada sailed out of the [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) port toward the [Coral](Lore_Coral.md) Kingdoms of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md). From the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and into the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md) the fleet sailed, using enchanted [lodestones](Lore_Lodestone.md) to navigate and keep the numerous ships in formation and on course. When they reached the [Thrassian](Lore_Thras.md) Archipelago after sailing through a fierce storm, they found the islands surrounded by a dense fog. Bendu appeared on each ship as a magical projection and ordered the fleet to attack the largest tower and lay siege to its coral tower.

Many ships were lost, but the Sload paid the price for unleashing their dastardly plague upon Tamriel. Baron-Admiral Bendu Olo led the surviving ships of the fleet back to High Isle in victory!
