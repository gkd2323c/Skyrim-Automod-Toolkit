There are two distinct **Fortify [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md)** effects: one from [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) -created potions and one granted by [enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) armor. Their effects are as follows.

## Alchemy
In potions, Fortify Conjuration increases the duration of Conjuration magic.

The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a potion of Fortify Conjuration:

## Enchanting
In enchantments, Fortify Conjuration decreases the [cost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Cost) of Conjuration spells, and the use cost of Conjuration based enchantments. It does not increase the skill level for Conjuration which appears in the [skills sub-menu](Skyrim_Skills.md).

The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Conjuration if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Bracers, and Helmet *[of Conjuring](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Conjuration)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Conjuring*, *of Major Conjuring*, *of Extreme Conjuring* *of Eminent Conjuring*, and *of Peerless Conjuring.*
- All varieties of Robes, Circlets, and Imperial Helmets *of Conjuration* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Conjuration*, *of Major Conjuration*, *of Extreme Conjuration* *of Eminent Conjuration*, and *of Peerless Conjuration.*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Archmage's Robes](Skyrim_Archmage%27s_Robes.md)
- [Bloodworm Helm](Skyrim_Bloodworm_Helm.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md)</sup>
- [Mentor's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mentor%27s_Ring)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Necromancer Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer_Amulet)

## Notes
- Equipping four 25% Fortify Conjuration enchanted pieces of apparel means that any and all Conjuration spells can be cast for free. This cost reduction also applies to Conjuration-based staves (such as Staff of the Flame Atronach) and weapons (melee and bows/crossbows with the banishing and soul trap enchantments).
- If you utilize the [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) glitch, you can craft Fortify Conjuration potions that will make standard summons and reanimations last far longer than 60 seconds (or 120 with the appropriate perks). As [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) can only be used on people to make permanent zombies, you can craft potions that will make anything raised by, for example, [Dread Zombie](Skyrim_Dread_Zombie.md) (which can be cast on more than just NPCs) last for a very long time, though they still crumble to ash when the timer runs out or they are killed (and thus cannot be reanimated again, unlike Dead Thrall).
