Stone of Atonement *A tablet describing the Order of the Hidden Moon's motivations*
---
What deeper grief can a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) suffer than witnessing a true cat's fall to [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md)? Watching the shining faces of brothers and sisters suddenly struck black as midnight—creased with snarls of rage and sorrow. Our people curse the [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md), but let us never forget that every dancing thrall of Namiira was once a pure child of [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md), and an inheritor of the [Ja-Kha'jay](Lore_Ja-Kha%27jay.md).

No Khajiit is beyond redemption. By [pressing](Lore_Necromancy.md) the dro-m'Athra's [souls](Lore_Soul.md) into the [bones](Lore_Bone.md) of our fallen kin, we give them an opportunity for atonement. For the peace found only in twilight. So, pay no heed to those who shrink from our calls to the Dark. No price is too great for a soul made clean.
