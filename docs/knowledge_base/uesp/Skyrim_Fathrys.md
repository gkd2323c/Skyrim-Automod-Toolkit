[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-creature-Fathrys.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Fathrys.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fathrys.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fathrys.jpg) Fathrys The [skeletal](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) remains of **Fathrys**, a young [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) who was once a member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md), can be found next to the locked gate within [Yorgrim Overlook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yorgrim_Overlook). According to *[The Crimson Dirks, v4](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v4)*, he journeyed to the ruin to recover the set of [studded Dragonscale armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Dragonscale_Armor) located there in order to woo a woman named Zaharia. He was instead killed by [Ulfnir Bone-Skin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfnir_Bone-Skin), the undead warrior who guards the ruins.

Fathrys' skeleton is equipped with [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), and a [hide helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md). An assortment of other items can be found in his remains, including a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow), eleven [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), a [silver garnet ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Garnet_Ring), [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), an [empty wine bottle](Skyrim_Empty_Wine_Bottle.md), [baked potatoes](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md), a *[Poem for Zaharia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poem_for_Zaharia)*, two [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).
