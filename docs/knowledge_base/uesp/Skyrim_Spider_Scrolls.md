## Notes
- Up to nine spiders can be summoned at any one time, five elemental spiders, one oil spider, one pack spider, one mind control spider and one glowing spider and they do not count towards the summoning limit placed on [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spells, meaning that they will not despawn when you use a summoning spell.
- The spiders do not have a time limit with the exception of the glowing spider and mind control spider, and thus will remain summoned until they are destroyed. They will also fast travel with you. They will follow you from Solstheim to Skyrim. They will follow you into a shop or house (which can get pretty crowded). They don't seem to follow when you visit Apocrypha, but will be waiting for you when you return.
- The spiders will turn hostile to you immediately if they are hit by any offensive ability of yours that does any damage. This is only relevant to the Cloaked spiders however, as both the Jumping and Exploding variants will explode immediately upon damage. The ability to damage them is not affected by the [Companion's Insight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Companion%27s_Insight) effect.
- The Exploding spider types can be propelled by the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout or an arrow for a short time before they will explode.
- There is a single respawning albino spider egg from which you can obtain albino spider pods for creating scrolls, located just south of [Bloodskal Barrow](Skyrim_Bloodskal_Barrow.md), near a [shrine of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth).
- If a flawless gem is used in any of the elemental recipes, six scrolls will be produced by the recipe instead of three.
- The [Web Mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Web_Mother)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Contest.md)</sup> in [Cronvangr Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave) launches Exploding Poison Spiders in combat.
