[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-npc-Vanryth_Gatharian.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vanryth_Gatharian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vanryth_Gatharian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vanryth_Gatharian.jpg) Vanryth Gatharian **Vanryth Gatharian** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) who establishes a weapon store in front of the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)'s [Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) once you have [improved](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Upgrading_the_Thieves_Guild) the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md). He sells enchanted weapons as well as armor and the materials to make them. His store is filled with shelves of armor, boots and gauntlets. If you buy enough items from him, he will allow you take some items from his store.

Vanryth spends all day and night serving his store while seated on a chair. The only time he will not offer you his services is if you have offended the Thieves Guild and will continue not to until you complete the [Reparations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reparations) quest.

He wears a set of merchant's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). He carries a leveled sword which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality along with a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
He may greet you with:

*"Looking to fix your armor?"* *"Be glad to repair any damage to your Guild armor."* *"Can I interest you in anything? Some fine armor perhaps?"* When you have upset the Thieves Guild:

*"Come back when you're in the Guild's good graces."*

## Notes
- Although never confirmed, it is implied that he is the same Vanryth who taught [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md) how to repair and smith [bonemold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold), as his name, his occupation, his location and his association with the Thieves Guild are a possible match to Glover's story.
