## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance are five [scathecraw](Skyrim_Scathecraw.md) plants, a food urn, two unusable barrels, and an empty cart. To the right of the entrance is a [trama root](Skyrim_Trama_Root.md), three more scathecraw plants, and five unusable barrels. Along the sides and back of the building on the waterfront are two ash yam urns, three unusable barrels, and three crates. A [Redoran guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) is posted outside the entrance, which is unlocked.

### Morvayn Manor
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-interior-Morvayn_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Morvayn_Manor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morvayn_Manor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Morvayn_Manor.jpg) Morvayn Manor throne room The entrance leads into the main room, where First Councilor [Lleril Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) holds court on his throne, protected by a Redoran guard posted next to the entrance. Along the west wall opposite the entrance are three chairs and a table holding a bottle each of [flin](Skyrim_Flin.md), [matze](Skyrim_Matze.md), [shein](Skyrim_Shein.md), [sujamma](Skyrim_Sujamma.md), and [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), three bowls of [horker and ash yam stew](Skyrim_Horker_and_Ash_Yam_Stew.md), two [ash yams](Skyrim_Ash_Yam.md), an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md), and a piece of [cooked boar meat](Skyrim_Cooked_Boar_Meat.md). To the south of the table are two benches along the east and west walls. Along the curved south wall is a chair often occupied by Second Councilor [Adril Arano](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md), the throne, and a sideboard holding a bottle each of matze, shein, and flin, a sample of [Felsaad tern feathers](Skyrim_Felsaad_Tern_Feathers.md), and copies of *[Bone, Part I](Skyrim_Bone,_Part_I.md)*, *[Bone, Part II](Skyrim_Bone,_Part_II.md)*, and *[The True Noble's Code](Skyrim_The_True_Noble%27s_Code.md)*. The wall ledge holds the following items, starting from behind the throne along the south wall and ending at the doorway to the next room along the north wall: bottles of sujamma, matze, shein, sujamma, flin, and shein, copies of all three volumes of *[History of Raven Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock)*, both volumes of *[The Red Year](Skyrim_The_Red_Year.md)*, *[The Poison Song, Book I](Skyrim_The_Poison_Song,_Book_I.md)*, and *[The Poison Song, Book II](Skyrim_The_Poison_Song,_Book_II.md)*, two bottles of wine, a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of sujamma, and copies of *[The House of Troubles](Skyrim_The_House_of_Troubles.md)* and *[Words of the Wind](Skyrim_Words_of_the_Wind.md)*.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md), a wood pile, a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand over a fire, a braid of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) and a [dead rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) hanging from a rack, and a food urn. Along the north wall beneath the stairs is a set of shelves holding three loaves of bread, a bottle each of wine, alto wine, matze, shein, and flin, two [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), four ash yams, an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), an eidar cheese wedge, two [goat cheese wheels](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), and a [sliced goat cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Goat_Cheese.md). Above the set of shelves are two dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two dried [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and two garlic braids hanging from the ceiling, and to the right of the set of shelves is a sack, two food urns, an ingredient urn, an ash yam urn, and a crate.

The small circular room on the upper floor contains three food urns, an ash yam urn, a wood pile, three crates, one of which has a sample of Felsaad tern feathers on top, two sacks, an unusable barrel, and a straw bale. An adept-locked door on the south wall leads to the second zone, *Morvayn Manor Chambers*.

### Morvayn Manor Chambers
The entrance leads into Adril and [Cindiri Arano](Skyrim_Cindiri_Arano.md)'s bedroom. In the southwest corner is a set of shelves holding two bottles of wine, two bottles of shein, a bottle of matze, a copy of *The Poison Song, Book II*, a [mammoth tusk](Skyrim_Mammoth_Tusk.md), an ash yam, a sliced goat cheese, and two eidar cheese wheels. On the wall ledge near the set of shelves are three more bottles of alto wine, a bottle of shein, and a bottle of wine, and to the right of the set of shelves is a bucket containing three more ash yams. In the northwest corner is a chair and a small round table holding a piece of [cooked beef](Skyrim_Cooked_Beef.md), with a bottle each of shein and matze on the wall shelf above the table, and an unlocked [East Empire Company strongbox](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Strongbox.md) on the wall ledge to the right of the table. In the southeast corner is an owned double bed, with two bottles of shein, a bottle of flin, and a bottle of matze on the wall ledge to the right of the bed. In the northeast corner is a bench. Along the north wall is a door to the next room.

The next room is circular, and serves as Lleril Morvayn's bedroom. Along the west wall is an expert-locked display case containing a [chitin heavy armor](Skyrim_Chitin_Heavy_Armor.md) cuirass and a [chitin heavy helmet](Skyrim_Chitin_Heavy_Helmet.md). Along the north wall is an owned double bed, with a sideboard at its foot holding copies of *History of Raven Rock, Vol. I* and *II*, two notes titled *[House Redoran's Reply](Skyrim_House_Redoran%27s_Reply.md)* and *[Adril's Survey Results](Skyrim_Adril%27s_Survey_Results.md)*, and a [lute](Skyrim_Lute.md). Along the east wall is a chair and a set of shelves holding three bottles of flin, a bottle of matze, a bottle of sujamma, copies of *[The Five Far Stars](Skyrim_The_Five_Far_Stars.md)* and *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*, a [garnet](Skyrim_Garnet.md), and an [emerald](Skyrim_Emerald.md). Although it is possible to jump onto the rafters under the domed ceiling, there are no items on the rafters.

## Bugs
- There isn't enough bed space in Morvayn Manor for the Arano couple to be able to sleep in their assigned upstairs area. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. Their bed has been upgraded to a double, and the navmesh has been adjusted to fit the new sleeping arrangements.
- The East Empire Company strongbox here is unlocked, rather than expert-locked like almost all other East Empire Company strongboxes. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Several buildings in Raven Rock, including Morvayn Manor, were incorrectly assigned the [music](Skyrim_Music.md) heard in [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md) rather than the default Solstheim music heard in other houses. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.1, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=11969))
- The music bug listed above also affects the Morvayn Manor Chambers. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=17929))
