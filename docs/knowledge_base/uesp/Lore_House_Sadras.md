# House Sadras

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/LG-cardart-Sadras_Agent.png/200px-LG-cardart-Sadras_Agent.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Sadras_Agent.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Sadras_Agent.png) An agent of House Sadras (Legends) House Sadras is one of the five [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBMorvayn-1)</sup> At the beginning of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), following the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) and the eruption of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5), [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) was stripped of its Great House status and removed from the Grand Council of Morrowind.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DBArano-2)</sup> House Sadras, whose prior status and holdings are unknown, was selected to fill the fifth seat on the Council.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DBMorvayn-1)</sup>

## Notes
- A trade caravan in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) bore Sadras' name as a form of codename.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCN-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CDN-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LL-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VL-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LCGN-7)</sup>
