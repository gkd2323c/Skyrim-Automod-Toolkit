# Ammunition


## Arrows
Arrows are the required ammunition for [bows](Skyrim_Bow.md). Arrows cannot be enchanted or tempered.

Unless the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on has been installed, arrows cannot be created at [forges](Skyrim_Forge.md). However, with Dawnguard, it becomes possible to craft standard arrows using 1 piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) plus an appropriate metal [ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ingots). The appropriate [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks are necessary to craft each variety of arrow, but arrows still cannot be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). ## Bolts
Bolts (and [crossbows](Skyrim_Crossbow.md)) are only available if the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on has been installed. Bolts are the required ammunition for crossbows. Bolts cannot be enchanted or tempered, but several varieties of bolts can be crafted if you join the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) and complete a [series of side quests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Technology).

## Notes
[![SR-graph-Dragonbone Arrow Damage.gif](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-graph-Dragonbone_Arrow_Damage.gif/200px-SR-graph-Dragonbone_Arrow_Damage.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-graph-Dragonbone_Arrow_Damage.gif) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-graph-Dragonbone_Arrow_Damage.gif) - To the right is a chart of damage per second values for Legendary versions of bows firing Dragonbone arrows. The chart does not include bonus damage from the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill, the [Overdraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Overdraw) perk, or the [Quick Shot](Skyrim_Quick_Shot.md) perk; however, the first two of these would affect all the values equally and would not change the relative order of bows. [Quick Shot](Skyrim_Quick_Shot.md) *does* affect them unequally, due to how bow speed works; see the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) article for a more in-depth analysis and discussion.
- Giving ammunition to a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) will result in that follower making use of them, provided the given ammunition is better than the follower's default ammunition. In addition, no matter how many times your follower uses this gifted ammunition, the number in their inventory will never decrease; and since you can collect missiles that miss their target, as well as some that hit, you can use this strategy as a good source for free ammunition of your choice. - This may not always work. The infinite ammo is typically only possible with the follower bow (the bow not visible in their inventory) and may not work with a bow that was given to them. Some followers may have infinite ammo regardless of whatever bow they are using - for these followers, the infinite ammo will sometimes stop working if they have a better bow. This will sometimes fix itself, and often this goes back and forth between breaking and fixing.
- Installing the [Arcane Archer Pack](Skyrim_Arcane_Archer_Pack.md) Creation can cause enemy archers to come into possession of some of those added arrow types, such as the Forsworn at Lost Valley Redoubt or the mind-controlled bandits in White Ridge Barrow. With this ammunition, they can do a lot of damage to you and make many once-easy areas very hazardous to visit.
