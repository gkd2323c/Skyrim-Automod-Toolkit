The Chronicles of Janisiere *Two known excerpts of the full book detailing the invasion of the Battlespire*
---
I recall the story of the recovery of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) as it was told to me by the son of [Old Chimere](Lore_Chimere_Graegyn.md), these many years ago. How the legions of [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) [Lord Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) overran the spire aided by betrayal. How a [young hero](Lore_Apprentice.md) from obscure origins entered the Battlespire, proving ground and testing facility for the shadow legion, the [imperial guard](Lore_Imperial_Guard.md), and finding his test was more than he bargained for. Thinking this was only a harmless test of his skills he enters the spire through the [pillar of light](Lore_Pillar_of_Light.md) and finds a hell created by the betrayal of the legion and the perfidy of [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md), arch-traitor to [the empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md). And so our story begins...

...and the [Emperor](Lore_Uriel_VII.md) then commanded to bring forth the two champions who had served him so well, who knelt before him and received his blessing. He bid them rise and said to them, 'Let all present know how these two have served their Empire, for had not Jagar Tharn been robbed of the support of the archfiend Mehrunes Dagon and his fell minions, then [Ria Silmane](Lore_Ria_Silmane.md) and [her champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) might never have revealed Tharn's imposture, and long might the rightful emperor have languished imprisoned in Dagon's dark prisons within the [Void](Lore_Void.md).' And he then made them great presents of land and chattels, and raised them up among his counselors, and conferred upon them offices of great importance in his Legions.
