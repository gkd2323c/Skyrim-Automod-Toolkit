# Western Skyrim

—Description of a Westerner<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBBR-1)</sup> Western Skyrim was established after the assassination of High King [Logrolf](Lore_Logrolf.md) in [2E 431](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_431), heralding a major change in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Naturally, the throne would have been passed to the High King's heir, Jarl [Freydis](Lore_Freydis.md) of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md). However, tension arose between the east and west when Jarl [Svartr](Lore_Svartr.md) of Solitude challenged her claim to the High Kingship and called for a moot. Freydis was accepted by the Crown of Verity and named High Queen, though part of the Moot chose Svartr as High King. What followed was a decisive schism that split Skyrim in two, creating distinct Western and [Eastern](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md) Kingdoms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCo F-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MTCHKS-3)</sup> In the west, Svartr and his successors ruled over [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Gt WSH-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WSSO-5)</sup> [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-6)</sup> [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-6)</sup> [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Gt WSHj-7)</sup> and [Karthald](Lore_Karthald.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Gt WSK-8)</sup>

Under High King [Svargrim](Lore_Svargrim.md), Western Skyrim remained neutral during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Western Skyrim came under threat with the reemergence of the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md). [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) traveled to Western Skyrim, where they saved [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) from a [harrowstorm](Lore_Harrowstorm.md). Due to the chaos caused by the Gray Host, Queen Gerhyld was assassinated. Princess [Svana](Lore_Svana.md), Svargrim's daughter, and the Vestige confronted Svargrim after discovering he was a pawn of the Gray Host and managed to defeat him before he could devastate the realm.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOGR-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GR-10)</sup> Western Skyrim was eventually reunified with the rest of Skyrim into a single realm before the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TAH-11)</sup>
