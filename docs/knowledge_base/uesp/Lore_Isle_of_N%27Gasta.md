[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/RG-map-Stros_M%27Kai-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-Stros_M%27Kai-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Stros_M%27Kai-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Stros_M%27Kai-1024x768.png) Map showing the Isle of N'Gasta alongside Stros M'Kai [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/RG-place-Isle_of_N%27Gasta.jpg/200px-RG-place-Isle_of_N%27Gasta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Isle_of_N%27Gasta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Isle_of_N%27Gasta.jpg) The Isle of N'Gasta The Isle of N'Gasta is a small island in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) west of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), named after its infamous resident, the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) necromancer [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md). The isle suffers from frequent storms and is always a gloomy place, inhabited by [bats](Lore_Bat.md) and mysterious creatures with tentacles.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RION-1)</sup>

A chain of islands called [the Spine](Lore_The_Spine.md) extends from Stros M'Kai's northern shore, and at its end a mysterious [skeletal](Lore_Skeleton.md) [boatman](Lore_Boatman.md) once ferried travelers to and from the Necromancer's Isle.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGNGastas Amulet-2)</sup> A large graveyard covers most of the isle, and it was likely once used by the first [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) settlers to bury criminals. This was a tradition brought over from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md), to prevent the criminal's evil spirit from tormenting the living.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3OI-3)</sup> The Necropolis,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-4)</sup> the largest structure in the graveyard, spans the width of the island, barring those who seek the old wizard's tower. This tower, sometimes called N'Gasta's Tower, is situated on an islet at the isle's southern tip.

During the reign of [Thassad II](Lore_Thassad_II.md), N'Gasta came to the isle and made it his, having been granted the shores as "hunting grounds" by [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> After the [Battle of Hunding Bay](Lore_Battle_of_Hunding_Bay.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), he began raising [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md) and [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md), including those who died in the battle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-4)</sup> Later that year, [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) came to the isle and accepted a delivery job from N'Gasta.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGNGastas Amulet-2)</sup> He came back after he learned that his sister, [Iszara](Lore_Iszara.md), was last seen on her way to N'Gasta to ask for assistance. He discovered N'Gasta's treachery and after killing the necromancer, entered the [realm of Clavicus Vile](Lore_Fields_of_Regret.md) to bargain for her soul. With Iszara saved, they headed back to Stros M'Kai together.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
