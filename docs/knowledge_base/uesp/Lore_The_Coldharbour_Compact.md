The Coldharbour Compact *A poem on Sotha Sil's mysterious First Era agreement with the Daedric Princes*
---

- Poor [Gil-Var-Delle](Lore_Gil-Var-Delle.md) we still recall
- The [God of Schemes](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) consumed it all

- To [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) went [Clockwork God](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)
- To bargain on the Princes' sod

- They came to mock at Tribune small
- Came [Schemer](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), [Hunger](Lore_Boethiah.md), [Hunter](Lore_Hircine.md), all

- [Scryer](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), [Pariah](Lore_Malacath.md), [Destroyer](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) came
- And [Raver](Lore_Sheogorath.md) and [Twilight](Lore_Azura.md) just the same

- From dread [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) save us
- From Daedra keep us safe
- Forsake the ones that made us
- To fight a wrongful fate

- They sneered when Clockwork made his demand
- Till Twilight raised a hand

- "And what do you offer in return
- "To keep us from chastising [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md)?"

- Then Clockwork whispered long and low
- And what he said, no mortal can know

- From dread Daedra save us
- From Daedra keep us safe
- Forsake the ones that made us
- To fight a wrongful fate
