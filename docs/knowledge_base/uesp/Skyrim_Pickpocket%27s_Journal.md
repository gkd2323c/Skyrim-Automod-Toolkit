Pickpocket's Journal **by [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket_(person).md)** *The journal of a failed Riften pickpocket*
---
I ain't been having no luck at lifting gold off them rich merchants in the market, and those [thieves guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) s'wits don't want me. Said I'm too clumsy. I'm running out of food and [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md), may need to start killing some of the [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md) down here for meat...

One of the skooma dealers at the docks told me he knew a guy from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) who trained a [nix hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound_(creature).md) to distract his marks - helped him steal a heaping pile of gold, he said. Don't know if I believe a word of it, but what else am I going to do? Might as well give it a try.

Too bad there ain't any nix hounds around here. Plenty of skeevers, though...I'm gonna need to catch some anyway for food, might as well have a go at [training one](Skyrim_Skritch.md) first.
