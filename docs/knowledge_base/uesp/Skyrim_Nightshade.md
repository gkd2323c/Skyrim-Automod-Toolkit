[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-flora-Nightshade.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Nightshade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Nightshade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Nightshade.jpg) A nightshade plant [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-book-Wolfsbane.png/100px-SR-book-Wolfsbane.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Wolfsbane.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Wolfsbane.png) Nightshade as depicted in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* **Nightshade** is a plant commonly found growing in graveyards. While it grows around death, it still needs light, preventing it from growing inside most crypts and barrows.

- *"The name and shape of this plant are known to all; long understood to be one of the more potent components in many poisons, the average Nord keeps his distance from the bright purple flowers nestled among dark leaves. It is exceedingly effective as a pure poison, but can also be combined with other compounds to stiffen joints as well. It is thus favored amongst those who wish to disable their opponents in battle, and can be found coating the blades of many of the more unsavory characters in Skyrim."* —Agneta Falia in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*.

- 3 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

There is a second variety of nightshade in [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup> that has the same alchemical properties as a regular nightshade. Because it's a different ingredient, it will stack separately from regular nightshade, and they can be brewed together to learn all four effects at once. In appearance, this nightshade is distinct in that it has one less leaf along its stem.

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) effect for this ingredient is 4 instead of 5. This is only relevant if [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md) and nightshade are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 20% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Fortify Destruction potion.
