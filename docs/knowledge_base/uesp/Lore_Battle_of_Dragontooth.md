# Battle of Dragontooth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Map of Hammerfell The Battle of Dragontooth<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-1)</sup><sup>:8</sup> was a major battle during the [Camoran Usurper's Invasion](Lore_Camoran_Usurper%27s_Invasion.md) in [3E 253](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_253). It was one of the first battles the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) started in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), after invading western [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) cities, [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) and [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) combined to defend their land,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup> but their defeat led to the Usurper conquering southern Hammerfell.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-1)</sup><sup>:8</sup>

After sacking [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) in Cyrodiil, the Camoran Usurper moved north across the border to invade Hammerfell. Rumors of the Usurper's arrival reached the province and nearby villagers believed that Rihad and Taneth would easily defeat him. Ultimately, both cities were destroyed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup> Their armies were absorbed into the Usurper's greater forces, as they continued their march north into the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). At that time, his [Nightmare Host](Lore_Nightmare_Host.md) took over [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) ahead of his invasion. Only for them to be defeated by Othrok in the [Battle of Wightmoor](Lore_Battle_of_Wightmoor.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFOTU-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTET-4)</sup>

Historically, the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) and [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) of Hammerfell had always been at odds with each other. Because of this, the Crowns of [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md) did not help the Forebears in the Battle of Dragontooth, nor stop the Usurper's northward march. Vice versa, Rihad and Taneth did not assist the eastern Crown cities during the [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md), preferring to watch from afar as the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) took over.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3-5)</sup>

## Notes
- What Dragontooth could refer to is unknown, however, unused resource data from *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* includes the Dragontooth Mountains, which is similarly named to [Dragon's Teeth Mountains](Lore_Dragon%27s_Teeth_Mountains.md) of eastern Hammerfell.
