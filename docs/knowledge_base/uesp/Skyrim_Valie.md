[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-npc-Valie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valie.jpg) Valie **Valie** was a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [thief](Skyrim_CombatThief.md) hired by [Sulla Trebatius](Skyrim_Sulla_Trebatius.md) to be part of his ill-fated expedition into [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) ruins. In Sulla's [expedition manifest](Skyrim_Expedition_Manifest.md), he mentions hiring her specifically because she was a mage not associated with the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). According to [J'zhar's journal](Skyrim_J%27zhar%27s_Journal.md), the expedition group suspected that his brother [J'darr](Skyrim_J%27darr.md) caused Valie's disappearance, though he believed otherwise. Her corpse can be found within the Animonculory zone of the ruin in the main [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) campsite, on the torture table in the bottom room. Sadly, it seems she died after being captured and tortured by the Falmer residing there.

Valie can be found wearing some [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Ragged_Boots.md).

## Notes
- Valie was likely created with [Niranye](Skyrim_Niranye.md) as a base, as she still retains some of the latter's AI packages and factions. This also results in a number of oddities, as seen below. - At some point, the Unofficial Patch also removed the AI packages from her package list, although this isn't noted in the changelog.

## Bugs
- She's part of the Windhelm faction, despite never being found there. Her inclusion could lead to her being eligible to fill an alias in [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. She's been removed from the faction.
- Due to the fact that she's part of the [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill her, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, addresses this issue. She has been removed from the Eastmarch crime faction.
