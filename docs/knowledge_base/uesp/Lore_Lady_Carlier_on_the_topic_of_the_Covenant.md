Lady Carlier on the topic of [the Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) **by [Lady Carlier](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Lady_Carlier)** *Justification for why the Daggerfall Covenant should rule Tamriel*
---
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-Breton.png)
- "It falls to us [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md), of course, to keep the peace. Why, if we can convince the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) to get along, then it is certain we are capable of anything. [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) deserves a strong [Empire](Lore_Empire.md), where each individual has a proper place, and a fair, just Emperor. That is what we demand, and it is abundantly clear that we are the only ones that can restore the Empire's dignity.

- The [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md)? They'll fall apart within a week, I'm certain of it. They lack diplomacy and cooperation, and will need much stronger guidance to contribute positively to society. And the [Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)? It is clear to anyone that they wish to oppress, not mend. I admit, I feel some pity for the poor [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) and [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) —those [High Elves](Lore_Altmer.md) will run them over as soon as they are done using them to capture the Ruby Throne. No, Tamriel needs us to step forward and lead her to a new beginning. We're the only ones who can set things right."
