[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-npc-Bradyn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bradyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bradyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bradyn.jpg) Bradyn **Bradyn** is a dead [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) miner found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md) during the quest [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). He wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1). He carries a copy of the book *[Ahzidal's Descent](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Descent.md)*, a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and other minor items and gold.
