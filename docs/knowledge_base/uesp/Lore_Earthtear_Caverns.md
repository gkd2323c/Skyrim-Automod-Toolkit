[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/ON-crown_store-Earthtear_Cavern.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Earthtear_Cavern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Earthtear_Cavern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Earthtear_Cavern.jpg) Earthtear Cavern circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Earthtear Caverns (or **Earthtear Cavern**) is a network of underground tunnels and chasms in the [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) region of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). At least two entrances exist: one just northeast of [Azra's Crossing](Lore_Azra%27s_Crossing.md), and another in the desert far west of [Belkarth](Lore_Belkarth.md).

## History
Like all natural caves, these tunnels once served as the capillaries of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), pulsing with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Some of the larger caverns are open to the sky and partially flooded.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> The ancient [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) were the original occupants of the caverns, although the Nedic complex was later occupied by the [Yokudans](Lore_Yokudan.md) after their invasion of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808). As such, the architecture has significant [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) influence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The complex was later abandoned and fell into ruin.

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Nedic ruins at Earthtear Caverns were available for purchase as a private lair.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

During the [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md), the invading forces of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) used the caverns as a meeting point. However, one of their numbers was attacked within by [Pergan Asuul](Lore_Pergan_Asuul.md), who summoned vermin to overrun the caverns. Namely, Earthtear became host to [rats](Lore_Rat.md), [spiders](Lore_Spider.md), and [wormmouths](Lore_Wormmouth.md), including a giant [Diamond Spider Queen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Diamond_Spider_Queen). In [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397), the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Hero.md) entered Earthtear to clear it of vermin so that [Skelos Undriel](Lore_Skelos_Undriel.md) could use it as a hiding place. Safe from the agents of [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md), Undriel then used the caverns as a base in his quest to assemble the [Star Teeth](Lore_Star_Teeth.md). Asuul also created a [Shadowgate](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md) within the caves, sealing off a section. With his permission, the Soul of Conflict dissipated this barrier and killed the undead guardians within.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SK-4)</sup>
