[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-place-Serpent_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Serpent_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpent_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Serpent_Stone.jpg) The Serpent Stone **The Serpent Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) scattered across Skyrim.

While the stone is active, the player can use a ranged attack that will [paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) a target for 5 seconds and also do 25 points of damage. Activating the stone will present the message:

"*Once a day, those under the sign of the Serpent can use a ranged paralyzing poison on opponents.*"

## Related Quests
- **[Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md)**: Prove yourself worthy of being a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebel.

## The Serpent Stone
The stone is on **Serpentstone Isle**, directly east of [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) and northeast of [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md). It is guarded by an [ice wraith](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md). The island is fairly empty, apart from a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) on the southeastern shore, a couple of [spiky grass](Skyrim_Grass_Pod.md) plants on the northern shore, and the occasional [snow fox](Skyrim_Snow_Fox.md). There are routes up from both the eastern and northern shores, to a square arch atop a short flight of stairs, with the stone standing beyond.
