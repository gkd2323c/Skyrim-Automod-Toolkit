Beredalmo the Signifier was a historian from the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He was a member of [House Direnni](Lore_House_Direnni.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LSPosts-2)</sup> and was one of the greatest living experts in [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) lore<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-ESOAUA-3)</sup> and mythohistory at the time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SCAS-4)</sup> Much of his writings pertain to his Direnni ancestors (*[Once](Lore_Once.md)* and *[Wayshrines of Tamriel](Lore_Wayshrines_of_Tamriel.md)*) and Ayleid history, especially from the [Ayleid Diaspora](Lore_Ayleid_Diaspora.md) in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). (*[Aurbic Enigma 4: The Elden Tree](Lore_Aurbic_Enigma_4__The_Elden_Tree.md)* and *[Heart of Valenwood](Lore_Heart_of_Valenwood.md)*)

## History
Beredalmo was a member of House Direnni, who hail from the [Isle of Balfiera](Lore_Isle_of_Balfiera.md) in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). Beredalmo lived in the Tourmaline Steeple where he cared for his relative, Heron, and studied Ayleid tomes throughout the day.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup> One of these texts was partially translated and released as *[The Swallowed City](Lore_The_Swallowed_City.md)*, about an Ayleid city that was cast into [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) himself.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSC-5)</sup> Another piece of text was an [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md) reference for phrases and translations that was painstakingly transcribed and interpreted over many years.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AIT-6)</sup><sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_text)</sup> *[The Remnant of Light](Lore_The_Remnant_of_Light.md)* is a translated Ayleid tract about the eponymous artifact found in the [Doomcrag](Lore_Doomcrag.md) and how it found its way to [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TROL-7)</sup>

In preparation for his treatise, *Wayshrines of Tamriel*, Beredalmo went through [Corvus](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md)' old workshop in [Direnni Tower](Lore_Direnni_Tower.md) and read his memoirs.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-WOT-8)</sup> The book, *Once* is an ode to Direnni ancestry, their many accomplishments throughout the years and their greatest failure, solving the mystery of the Zero Stone and opening the Argent Aperture in Direnni Tower.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup>

The *Aurbic Enigma* is a series of stories about [the Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md), mythical structures found across [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). The fourth book revolves around the Elden Tree and describes the story of how King [Anumaril](Lore_Anumaril.md) rooted the Elden Tree in [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) and built the Osseous Orrery.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AE4TET-9)</sup> The contemporary Imperial historian, [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md) released the "Commentaries on Beredalmo" and commented on the author's thoughts on the Orrery.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONLoad Orrery-10)</sup> The *Heart of Valenwood* is a book about the ancient Ayleid city of [Hectahame](Lore_Hectahame.md) and the [Heart of Valenwood](Lore_Heart_of_Valenwood.md) within its innermost sanctum.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-THOV-11)</sup>
