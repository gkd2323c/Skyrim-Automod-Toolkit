[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/DF-map-Koegria.png/200px-DF-map-Koegria.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Koegria.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Koegria.png) Map of Koegria Koegria was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) fiefdom in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) along the northern bank of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). It was bordered by [Bhoriane](Lore_Bhoriane.md) and [Kambria](Lore_Kambria.md) to the west, the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) to the northeast, and [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire_(fiefdom).md) to the southeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> The provincial seat was the city of Koegria. The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline was [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md), and the regional deity was [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Unused texts, cut from the final version of [Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), suggest that [Koeglin Village](Lore_Koeglin_Village.md) corresponds to the city of Koegria.
