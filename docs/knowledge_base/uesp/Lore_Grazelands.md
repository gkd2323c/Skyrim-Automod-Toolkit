[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg) The Grazelands in their prime [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/MW-place-Grazelands.jpg/200px-MW-place-Grazelands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Grazelands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Grazelands.jpg) The Grazelands during The Blight The Grazelands (also called **Sunna Guradan**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> were the pastoral heartland of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). On the northeastern corner of the island, these grasslands extended from the ancient stronghold of Falensarano north to the coast above [Tel Vos](Lore_Tel_Vos.md). To the east, the Grazelands stretched to the coast and the islands of [Zafirbel Bay](Lore_Zafirbel_Bay.md), facing the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). To the west, it was bounded by the forbidding steepness of the mountains of the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md).

The flora of the Grazelands was dominated by [wickwheat](Lore_Wickwheat.md), and this is one of the few areas where it grew. There were trees spread sporadically around the landscape. [Stoneflower](Lore_Stoneflower.md) was the most frequently flowering plant. It was home to wild guar, alit and shalk. The south was dominated by [hackle-lo](Lore_Hackle-Lo.md) shrubs, whose leaves could be found across the island. It was also a region where, due to its scarce population, it was not uncommon to see [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), including [golden saints](Lore_Aureal.md) and [ogrim](Lore_Ogrim.md), just walking around in the open.

Much of Grazelands was uninhabited and thus belonged to the Temple. The population of the Grazelands consisted of mostly nomadic [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md). Other inhabitants included those who lived in Tel Vos and the nearby town of [Vos](Lore_Vos.md), along with Master Aryon in the tower of [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).

Ashlander settlements in the area were the [Ahemmusa Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ahemmusa_Camp) in the north and the [Zainab Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zainab_Camp) to the south. The presence of Tel Vos and the proximity to the Telvanni homeland meant that this House was the most likely to make future claims in the region.
