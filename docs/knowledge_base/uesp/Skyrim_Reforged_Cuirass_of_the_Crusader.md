[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Reforged_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Reforged_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader.png) Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader The Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader is an artifact [cuirass](Skyrim_Cuirass.md). It is acquired through the [Relics of the Crusader](Skyrim_Relics_of_the_Crusader.md) quest, in which you also claim the other relics from the [Bandit Thieves](Skyrim_Bandit_Thief.md) at [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md). Improving the Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader requires a [Corundum Ingot](Skyrim_Corundum_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Relics of the Crusader](Skyrim_Relics_of_the_Crusader.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>: Reclaim the holy relics of Pelinal Whitestrake.

## Notes
- The original Cuirass of the Crusader has previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader) in the [Knights of the Nine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Knights_of_the_Nine) add-on for *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader.md).

## Bugs
- Although the enchant description only mentions affecting damage from undead, the Creation Kit entry and actual mechanics also applies to Daedra. **Mod Notes**: Confirmed by Creation Kit entry.
