[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-creature-Burnt_Spriggan.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Burnt_Spriggan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Burnt_Spriggan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Burnt_Spriggan.jpg) Burnt Spriggan **Burnt Spriggans**, found in the Ash Wastes, are similar to regular [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md), though they lack the poison attacks and ability to call wild animals to their side. They are powerful and can be dangerous to [vampires](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) and low-level players. Instead of [taproots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot), they drop [burnt spriggan wood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Burnt_Spriggan_Wood). They are immune to fire and utilize fire-based magic in combat. Like other spriggans, when reduced to low health they will crouch momentarily, though instead of healing, they release a flaming aura that will damage you and nearby NPCs.

## Notes
- The process through which a normal spriggan becomes a burnt spriggan is unknown, though [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) is conducting experiments on captured species with his apprentice [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md). These spriggans can be found in separate cages in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md), one standard and one burnt.
- The unique burnt spriggan in Tel Mithryn (base ID: `xx039FDA`) has similar stats as others, but is not part of any factions.
- Burnt spriggans release a shockwave of flames when they reach a critically injured state, unlike regular spriggans, which heal a portion of their health when it is lowered a certain amount. This shockwave is particularly damaging, even to high-level characters, and so you should run from the "blast zone" when they reach this stage.
- Burnt Spriggan Wood has the effect of Weakness to Fire, though Burnt Spriggans themselves are completely immune to the element.

## Bugs
- Burnt spriggans don't have the proper keyword set to exclude them from being fed on by werewolves with the Savage Feeding perk. All other spriggans in the game have this keyword. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.5, fixes this bug.
