# Khajiit Traders


The Khajiit caravans can be encountered on the road traveling between their destinations. When arriving at a city, they will establish a camp outside the main entrance to the city. These camps will have tents and a campfire; in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) the camp will have a tanning rack as well. The caravan will stay for a while, then resume traveling. Both setting up and breaking camp will only happen off-screen. Whether at their camp or on the road, they will always be ready to do business. Their merchandise levels with you, so the higher your level, the greater the quality of what they have for sale.

Each caravan has a [merchant chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant_Chest) assigned to it. One of them can be found near a rock formation outside of [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). Another one can be found near a stone wall and a Juniper tree outside of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). The third can be found near a plant formation outside of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Their contents level with you, so the higher your level, the greater the quality. You can loot each chest for free, and all 3 will respawn every 48 hours. Be warned, however, that the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) will cause these chests to be inaccessible.

The [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a number of items that are exclusively available from the Khajiit traders, most of which are rare ingredients with otherwise unobtainable [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) effects. The traders are also the only source of [Aloe Vera Leaves](Skyrim_Aloe_Vera_Leaves.md), added in [Special Edition Patch 1.6.1130](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#1.6.1130).

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Khajiit_Caravans.md).

## Caravans

### Ri'saad's Caravan
[Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad)'s own caravan has a trade route between [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), and includes the fence [Atahbah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atahbah) and the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) target [Ma'randru-jo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27randru-jo). They are protected by the mercenary [Khayla](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Khayla), who is a common [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) trainer. The merchant chest assigned to his caravan is near a stone wall and a Juniper tree outside of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

### Ahkari's Caravan
[Ahkari](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ahkari) is accompanied by the fence [Zaynabi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaynabi) and travels between [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). They are protected by the mercenaries [Dro'marash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dro%27marash), a common [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) trainer, and [Kharjo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kharjo), who becomes a follower after [retrieving his Moon Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_the_Moon). The merchant chest assigned to her caravan is near a rock formation outside of [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

### Ma'dran's Caravan
[Ma'dran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27dran)'s route between [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) has proven to be very profitable during the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md). He is protected by the mercenary [Ra'zhinda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ra%27zhinda) and is also accompanied by [Ma'jhad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27jhad), who is both a fence and an expert trainer in [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md). The merchant chest assigned to his caravan is near a plant formation outside of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

## Notes
- The caravans would have originally been accompanied by horses, which still exist in the files, but go [unused](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unused_Creatures#Horses) in the final game. The caravan's leader would have presumably ridden on them.
- 12 NPCs were created as part of the caravans, but only 11 made it into the final game. The only one that goes unused is, notably, an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) by the name of [Grushnag](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Grushnag). Based on his Form ID, it's known that he was created alongside the caravan Khajiit, and he's also present in the list `Caravans NPCList` which just lists all relevant NPCs. Based on his placement in said list, as well as his equipment, he was in all likelihood a guard for a caravan. However, for which caravan this was, is unknown, as he has no remaining dialogue and he is not mentioned anywhere else; not in the game nor its files.
- If you start "[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)" as a Khajiit, [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) will ask: *"You with one of the trade caravans, Khajiit?"*
- If you start the quest to retrieve Kharjo's Moon Amulet and obtain it, you'll always know where Ahkari's caravan is due to the quest marker, as long as you don't return the amulet to Kharjo.
- Unlike regular general goods merchants, Khajiit traders don't sell arrows. With [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) installed, they will sell [Corkbulb Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corkbulb_Arrow)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup>.

## Bugs
- The Khajiit caravans may fail to set up tents and just stand in place. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Entering the following console commands in sequence can be used to reset bugged caravans. Do this at a location distant from the caravan stop points and save your game beforehand.

Relevant Commands `prid 0010372F` `disable` `prid 001030E2` `disable` `prid 00104AD6` `disable` `prid 00103774` `disable` `prid 0010311B` `disable` `prid 0010332A` `disable` `set Caravan Is Camped A to 0` `set Caravan Is Camped B to 0` `set Caravan Is Camped C to 0` `set Caravan Location A to 0` `set Caravan Location A to 1` `set Caravan Location B to 0` `set Caravan Location B to 1` `set Caravan Location C to 0` `set Caravan Location C to 1` - A bug similar to the above occurs when the tents and campsite are set up at one caravan stop (say, Ri'saad's camp is outside Whiterun), but the Khajiit merchant in question is on the other side of the map at their other stop (in Risaad's case, Markarth), without the camp. Once this bug happens to a particular Khajiit merchant, it will continue for them.
- Investing with [Belethor's General Goods](Skyrim_Belethor%27s_General_Goods.md) also adds gold to Khajiit caravans. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13209))
- Members of a Khajiit caravan will join [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) and [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) in fighting you if they witness the battles, due to faction relationships being set up incorrectly. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
