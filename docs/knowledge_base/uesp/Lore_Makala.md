Makala was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) politician,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ABKF-2)</sup> and a member of the [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) nobility,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE-3)</sup> active in the Second Era, known as the Ever-Virtuous.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup> Various sources ascribe to him the titles of King,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KMEP-4)</sup> and Grandee, respectively.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup>

## History
Grandee Makala was a member of the same line as High King [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md) and King [Ja-Fr](Lore_Ja-Fr.md), and thus carried the blood of the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md). At one point, he married Grandeya Fanesh of [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md), also a descendant of the same dynasty, thus further endowing the line with the Phyllocid blood of the Meritorious [Zizzeen](Lore_Zizzeen.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ABKF-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Fanesh and Makala had only one child,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup> Fahara'jad, the Prince of Antiphyllos,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ABKF-2)</sup> who later seized the throne of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) from [Ramzi the Distrait](Lore_Ramzi_the_Distrait.md) by force.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE-3)</sup>

Makala was an infamous ruler. He was also hard of hearing and often relied on small golden ear pieces to aid his sense of sound.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KMEP-4)</sup>
