# Skinmail

Skinmail is an armor of myth and legend created via decades' worth of attentive work in both [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) and [enchantment](Lore_Enchantment.md) to produce. They are the result of vials of experimental liquids which upon contact with skin, will harden it and produce plate-like coverings to the wearer's liking. Skinmail was created by an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) alchemist, who performed profane experiments on live subjects to reach the results they desired.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
