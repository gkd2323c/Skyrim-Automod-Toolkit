[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/ON-creature-Domihaus_the_Bloody-Horned_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Domihaus_the_Bloody-Horned_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Domihaus_the_Bloody-Horned_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Domihaus_the_Bloody-Horned_02.jpg) Domihaus attacking Jarl Skjoralmor Domihaus the Bloody-Horned was a minotaur warlord who forged an alliance with the [Dreadhorn](Lore_Dreadhorn_Clan.md) clan, a tribe of [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) descended from the [Keptu](Lore_Keptu.md) people. Together, he and the reverent Reachmen planned to wrest control of the clan's ancestral lands in [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md) from the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt C-1)</sup>

[Gherig Bullblood](Lore_Gherig_Bullblood.md), the patriarch of the Dreadhorn clan, believed Domihaus was a favored champion of the clan's patron deity, [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LMIL-2)</sup> Although his lineage is unknown, Domihaus supposedly possessed a pedigree of some significance, claiming that his line was "unbroken".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Domi Dial-3)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Domihaus had a strong connection to the land, and wielded powerful [destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) and [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) magic. He shaped the earth below his hooves and summoned [atronachs](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) and intense blasts of fire in his conquest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Domi Abil-4)</sup> He also possessed the ability to manifest flaming wings.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Domi Appear-5)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) confronted Domihaus at the jarl's manor at [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) after he killed Jarl [Hjurgol Skjoralmor](Lore_Hjurgol_Skjoralmor.md), and ultimately defeated him. His severed head was taken as a trophy.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Notes
- | [^](#note_nb1) | Some scholars believe that all minotaurs are descended from Emperor [Belharza the Man-Bull](Lore_Belharza.md), son of [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> It is therefore possible that Domihaus' claim to an unbroken line refers to his direct relation to these mythical figures and, if true, it would make him the last known direct descendant of Empress Alessia, and therefore a [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md). |
