[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/MW-place-Tel_Fyr.jpg/200px-MW-place-Tel_Fyr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Tel_Fyr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Tel_Fyr.jpg) Tel Fyr Vistha-Kai was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) knight who resided in [Tel Fyr](Lore_Tel_Fyr.md) the tower ruled by the ancient [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) mage [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md). Vistha-Kai was initially a slave until Divayth released all of his slaves. However, Vistha did not wish to leave.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VK-2)</sup> Divayth kept him on as a hireling, eventually making him a partner and the Warden of his [Corprusarium](Lore_Abanabi_Caves.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VK-2)</sup> a sanctuary that Divayth maintained in the caverns beneath Tel Fyr to maintain and care for victims of [Corprus](Lore_Corprus.md), the divine disease wrought by [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md). As warden, Vistha-Kai served as the guardian and peacekeeper of the Corprusarium making sure to ease the suffering of the victim inmates.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VK-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DEF-3)</sup> He also served as the chief of the guard who ensured no harm came to the victims and hunted down thieves who wished to plunder the many treasures found within Tel Fyr.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VK-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AF-4)</sup>

Vistha-Kai also befriended the four daughter- [clones](Lore_Alfe_Fyr.md) of Divayth Fyr.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup> He was self-taught in the ways of martial arts and often trained with Fyr's daughters, who were apt in their own right.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup> Along with his four daughter-wives and his [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) companion [Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), Vistha-Kai was someone dear to Divayth Fyr.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DF-1)</sup>
