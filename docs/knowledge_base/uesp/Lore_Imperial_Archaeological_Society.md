# Imperial Archaeological Society

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/RG-book-No_Trespassing.png/200px-RG-book-No_Trespassing.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-book-No_Trespassing.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-book-No_Trespassing.png) A site notice from the Imperial Archaeological Society The Imperial Archaeological Society is an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution devoted to archaeological study. It was contemporary with the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RG-1)</sup>

In [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), the society was present on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) as part of the [Imperial](Lore_Third_Empire.md) occupation of the island. It had taken possession of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [ruins](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) of [Bthzark](Lore_Bthzark.md) as an archaeological site under the Dwarven Reclamation Act of 2E 864.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
