[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-place-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg) Forgotten Vale Cave **Forgotten Vale Cave** is a small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md) containing [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) and one of the rare [shellbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug). It contains only one zone, *[Forgotten Vale Cave](#Forgotten_Vale_Cave)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The exterior of this cave is the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). The entrance is in the first section of the vale along the east wall of the valley, and is guarded by a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md). A [gleamblossom](Skyrim_Gleamblossom.md) plant is to the left of the entrance.

### Forgotten Vale Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Forgotten_Vale_Cave.jpg) Main chamber The entrance leads into a passage with [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md) hanging from the ceiling. A gated pen to the north contains a [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), a [chaurus hunter](Skyrim_Chaurus_Hunter.md), two unoccupied [chaurus cocoons](Skyrim_Chaurus_Cocoon.md), seven chaurus egg sacs, and five [glowing mushrooms](Skyrim_Glowing_Mushroom.md) on the walls. The path splits to the west, with the left side rising along a ledge to a rope release that opens a concealed door in the northwest wall of the main chamber, and the right side descending past a Falmer [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) to the floor of the chamber. The chamber features six [Falmer hives](Skyrim_Falmer_Hive.md) and five cocoons, but only one of each is occupied by a chaurus hunter.

The passage behind the concealed door on the northwest ledge of the chamber descends into another chamber containing another rope release that operates the door, a few glowing mushrooms, an unoccupied cocoon, and one of the rare [shellbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug). In order to obtain their [chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug_Chitin), these creatures must be [mined](Skyrim_Mining.md) with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe); the material cannot be obtained through killing the creatures by conventional means. Shellbugs are also not detected by the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) spell.

## Bugs
- You may be unable to mine the shellbug after it respawns. Leaving and reentering the cave will enable the shellbug, allowing you to mine it normally. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.
