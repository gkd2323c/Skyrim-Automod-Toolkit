Umana's Journal **by [Umana](Skyrim_Umana.md)** *A record of an ill-fated exploration of Dwemer ruins in Skyrim* *
---
It's been about a week since [Valie](Skyrim_Valie.md) went missing and now [Endrast](Skyrim_Endrast.md) is gone too. We found blood leading over to the barred off doorway, but [Sulla](Skyrim_Sulla_Trebatius.md) seems to think that they found a way through and that they are trying to cut him out of the discovery.

He keeps saying that we need to press on. We've manage *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* to break through into another section of the ruins, an "Animonculory", where the [dwarves](Lore_Dwemer.md) would produce their [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).

We learned the hard way that the metal creatures are still alive in there and it hasn't improved [Yag](Skyrim_Yag_gra-Gortwog.md)'s mood at all. She holds that the [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) brothers aren't involved with the disappearances and has been keeping a hard eye on Sulla.

The rations have all but run out and we are going to have to decide soon whether to brave the storm or try to push further into the ruins. I don't know if the echoes of screams I've heard in my sleep are those of our missing comrades, or my own nightmares.
