## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Helnim Wall was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Zirkan and had a rivalry with [Greenheights](Lore_Greenheights.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena-4)</sup> Helnim was neighbored by several settlements, including [Draloris](Lore_Draloris.md), [Velothis Haven](Lore_Velothis_Haven.md), [Darnim Watch](Lore_Darnim_Watch.md), [Sailen Vulgate](Lore_Sailen_Vulgate.md), and [Greenheights](Lore_Greenheights.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>
