[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero.jpg) Teldryn Sero [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teldryn_Sero_02.jpg) Teldryn Sero without his chitin helmet **Teldryn Sero**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [spellsword](Skyrim_Classes.md#DLC2NPCClass Teldryn), is a mercenary found in [the Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md). He can be hired as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) for 500 gold.

He wears a full set of light [chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin) armor, including the [cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Boots), [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Bracers), and [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Helmet). He carries an [elven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword) and an [elven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger).

Teldryn knows the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) and [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md). His first move in combat is to summon a [flame atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md). He usually alternates between using his sword and his Destruction spells dealing [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage).

He grew up in [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). He claims he once met [Saint Jiub](Lore_Jiub.md), making him over 200 years old. At some point he moved to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)'s Gray Quarter before eventually moving to Raven Rock. According to him, his chitin armor was especially made for him by Grendis Rolovo, one of the finest master armor smiths in Morrowind.

## Dialogue
When you first meet him, he may greet you with one of the following lines:

*"Teldryn Sero, blade for hire. If you have the coin, I'm at your service."* *"The best swordsman in all Morrowind is at your service... for the right price."* *"Don't pass up on this opportunity, outlander. I'm worth every coin."* *"I've got swords, spells and a few other tricks up my sleeve. You'll find I'm full of surprises."* If you don't have enough gold to hire him, he'll reply,

*"Unless you have enough coin on you, I'm not going anywhere."* If you do have enough gold, he'll respond,

*"Then let's be off!"* If you have dismissed him and approach him again, he'll greet you with:

*"I was enjoying our travels together before we parted company. I'd be happy to rejoin you for no charge... if you're interested."* If you choose to rehire him, it will be free and he will tell you,

*"Lead the way."*
## As a Follower
While Teldryn is your active follower, you can ask him how he ended up on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md):

**How did you end up on Solstheim?** *"Long story, but I don't see the harm in sharing it with you. My last patron was a true [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md). He was dressed in animal skins, had tattoos on his face... a real traditional type, if you know what I mean. He had an insatiable bloodlust and was extremely stubborn... one of the toughest employers I've ever had."* **Why did you work for him then?** *"Simple... he paid well. Very well. Anyway, we'd just cleared a ruined fort somewhere outside of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and when we got outside, we saw one of them galloping away on horseback. Well, my boss wasn't about to let him get away, so we pursued... on foot, for three days."* **Until?** *"Until we ended up tracking him to one of the largest bandit encampments I'd ever seen. My patron took one look at me, and made the most menacing grin I'd ever seen. At that moment, I knew I'd never see him again. No amount of money would ever be worth that kind of death."* If you ask him to do something, he'll say, *"What do you have in mind?"* If you talk to him before giving a command, he'll say, *"Did you still want me to help you or not?"* If you tell him yes, he'll say, *"Very well."* If you tell him no, he'll say, *"You're the boss."* When you tell him to do something, he'll say, *"Right away."*, *"That's not going to happen."*, or *"I don't care how much you're paying me, there's no way I'm doing that."* He'll then ask, *"Anything else?"* If you tell him you need to trade some things with him, he'll say, *"If it'll help us get moving quicker, sure."* If you tell him to wait where he is, he'll say, *"You're going ahead without me? Suit yourself..."* While he's waiting, he'll say, *"You better not be gone long."* and *"Make it quick, I don't like this waiting game."* If you tell him you want to part ways, he'll say *"Oh fine, go on... leave me here. When you decide to return to your senses, you know where to find me."* When you exit conversation while he's following you, he'll say, *"Right behind you."* or *"Lead on."*

## Notes
- Teldryn will only equip headwear given to him that does not expose his face and has higher armor rating than his default chitin helmet.
- He is one of the two hirelings that cannot be married, the other being [Erik the Slayer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erik_the_Slayer).
- Teldryn is the only follower in the game that has the [Armsman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armsman) [perk](Skyrim_Perks.md), which makes him more effective with one-handed weapons than the average follower.
- His maximum level is 60, which is the highest of all hirelings in the game, and 2nd highest of all followers in the game (excluding the followers with no level cap).
- Given his inventory, Teldryn presents a rare opportunity to obtain an elven sword and an elven dagger from the start of the game and at level 1, at the cost of 750 gold (250 for traveling to Solstheim and 500 for hiring him).
- Teldryn Sero asks the player if they like his Chitin armor even if he's no longer wearing any of it. - Version 4.0.6 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) removes this audio line if that is the case.

## Bugs
- After being [turned](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md) into a [Blade](Skyrim_Blades.md), Teldryn may lose all dialogue options and can't be a follower. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console, select Teldryn, then enter `addtofaction bcc9a 1`. This will add him back to the hireling faction and allow you to hire him as usual.
- Teldryn may become bugged and start blocking whenever his weapons are out, reducing his speed and usefulness significantly. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Take back any equipment you may have given to Teldryn, dismiss him, open the console, select him, enter `recycleactor` and rehire him to fix this bug.
- When Teldryn has summoned a Flame Atronach, if you even slightly harm Teldryn, the Atronach will become hostile and attack you, even when Teldryn himself doesn't.
