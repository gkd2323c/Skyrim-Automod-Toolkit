## History

### First Era
The [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) settlement of Telepe was abandoned at some point after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) in [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243) and subsequently fell into ruin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-place-Telepe.jpg/200px-ON-place-Telepe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Telepe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Telepe.jpg) Telepe circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582)
### Second Era
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Telepe's interior was sealed and the surface ruins were inhabited by [minotaurs](Lore_Minotaur.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> That year, the Keywright's Gallery in the [Adamantine Tower](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md) opened a portal to Telepe due to a string of high-profile murders that had been carried out by the [Order of the Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md) in their search for the [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md) of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

### Third Era
Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Telepe was occupied by the [Black Bow Bandits](Lore_Black_Bow_Bandits.md), a gang led by the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) Black Brugo. This bandit gang became a menace to County Leyawiin and came to the attention of Count [Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md) of Leyawiin as a result.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Count Caro tasked Mazoga the Orc and the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) with tracking down and killing Black Brugo. Coincidentally, Mazoga had previously run with Brugo and knew that he ventured to his hideout in Telepe each night to pick up his take of the bandits' ill-gotten gains.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> The two ambushed Brugo at Telepe when he arrived, and were made Knights-Errant of the [Knights of the White Stallion](Lore_Knights_of_the_White_Stallion.md) by Count Caro as a reward for their service.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
