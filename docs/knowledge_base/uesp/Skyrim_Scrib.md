[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg) An Ash Hopper **Ash Hoppers** are creatures found in southern [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), similar to the [scrib](Lore_Scrib.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). They appear as moderate sized insects, often seen sifting through the Ashlands in groups of two or three. They will hide themselves in the sand before you enter their ambush ring. When attacking, hoppers will often attempt to circle around behind their prey like wolves. They may carry [droops](Skyrim_Droops.md), a disease which reduces your one-handed and two-handed weapon damage by 15%.

One guaranteed location for Ash hoppers is [Ash Hopper Island](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Ash_Hopper_Island), just south of [The Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) where three Ash Hoppers can be found, along with the bodies of two dead reavers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=27160&y=23006&zoom=16&legacy=true)

## Notes
- The Ash Hopper is identified in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) as "Scrib". There is also unused data for a Scrib ability that [paralyzes](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) foes upon contact, much like [scrib](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Scrib) have in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), as well as an additional [poison damage](Skyrim_Poison_Damage.md) effect.
