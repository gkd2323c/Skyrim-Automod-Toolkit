**Armiger** Rhina was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) member of the [Order of Diagna](Lore_Order_of_Diagna.md) who spent her entire twenty-six years fighting the [Orcs](Lore_Orcs.md) during the Siege of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), where her sacrifice was said to have brought the Redguards victory. She was remembered as one of the people who changed the course of Redguard history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) Tu'whacca's Throne Rhina was born around [1E 954](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_954) within the siege camp among the Redguard soldiers during the Siege of Orsinium. At some point, she became a member of the Order of Diagna. She dedicated her entire twenty-six years to battling the Orcs, with her sacrifice believed to have secured victory for the Redguards. She died during the final days of the siege in [1E 980](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_980) and was commemorated as one of the individuals who altered the trajectory of Redguard history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NWOB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3O-3)</sup>

Following her demise, she was laid to rest in [Tu'whacca's Throne](Lore_Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.md) in Alik'r. According to [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) scholars, alongside Queen [Afsar](Lore_Afsar.md) and Yokeda [Razul](Lore_Razul.md), she was among the most powerful and dangerous [mummies](Lore_Mummy.md) buried there. During the Withered Hand crisis in Alik'r in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she was one of the many risen dead, but she was eventually returned to rest thanks to the efforts of the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) and an adventurer who aided them at their task.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RA-5)</sup>
