[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-npc-Phantom.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Phantom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Phantom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Phantom.jpg) A phantom [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-npc-Phantom_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Phantom_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Phantom_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Phantom_02.jpg) A phantom created by the player **Phantoms** are [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) created by the [Phantom Form](Skyrim_Phantom_Form.md) shout.

Phantoms wield the unique [Phantom Sword](Skyrim_Phantom_Sword.md) and wear a set of [Greybeard's robes](Skyrim_Greybeard%27s_Robes.md), a [Greybeard's hood](Skyrim_Greybeard%27s_Hood.md), and a pair of [Greybeard's boots](Skyrim_Greybeard%27s_Boots.md). The [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) will summon phantoms for you to practice your [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout on during the related quest.

## Related Quests
- **[The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md)**: Meet the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md).

## Notes
- Phantoms are members of the [bandit class](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), which determines the level of their skills.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using [console commands](Skyrim_Console.md), you can unlock the Phantom Form shout. When used, it will create a phantom with the appearance of a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) (Base ID `00072310`).
