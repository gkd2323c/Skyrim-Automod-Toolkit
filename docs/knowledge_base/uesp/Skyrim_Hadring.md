[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-npc-Hadring.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hadring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hadring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hadring.jpg) Hadring **Hadring**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is the proprietor of the [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md). He has a tendency to talk too much, which may prove beneficial during the related quests.

Hadring wears barkeeper [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0005b6a0). He carries some [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a bottle of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md)**: Find and deliver some forged [documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm Cloak_Documents) to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).
- **[Recipe for Disaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Disaster)**: Assume the identity of the famous [Gourmet](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md) in an effort to assassinate the Emperor.

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"Ah, hello there, traveler. Come to the Nightgate for food or lodging?"*
- *"Need a room? Or maybe just some mead?"*
- *"Something I can get for ya?"*
- *"Anything you need, just holler."*

When asked about his inn, he replies:

- **What can you tell me about the inn?**
- *"This old place? Been here forever. Built by my great grandda. Run by him, then all the way up the line to me."*

- **Where can I learn more about magic?**
- *"Seen enough magic in my day to know to stay away from it. I'd stay away from the Jarl's [wizard](Skyrim_Madena.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) too. If you wanna be a damn fool, go to the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) instead."*

- **Do you get a lot of business?**
- *"Nah, not so much. The odd traveler on the road. But mostly just old [Fultheim](Skyrim_Fultheim.md), come to drink away a lifetime of bad memories, I'd wager. Course there's the [Orc](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md). Long-term tenant, that one. For what he pays, I could afford to shut this place down."*
- **Tell me about the Orc.**
- *"Him? Oh... Ah, name's Balablob or Malaclob, one of them funny Orc names. Talks real good, though. Not a savage at all. Said he's a writer. Don't know what kind of job that is, but it must earn him some pretty coin. He's paid up for the next few months. He mostly just hangs about. Goes down to the lake, sometimes samples the stores of wine in the cellar. Man can do whatever he pleases, far as I care."*
