**Fade Other** is an expert level Illusion spell that causes the target to become [invisible](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) for 30 seconds. It cannot be found in the game, and there is no spell tome for it, but it does work.

## Effects
- [Fade Other](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) on target for 30 secs

## Notes
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can give yourself the spell using the console command: `player.addspell 28532`.
- The spell was probably *intended* to be available, as there is dialogue voiced for it when you cast it on your allies, as well as a world interaction where a child can ask you to make them invisible. It is unknown why it was never made available.
