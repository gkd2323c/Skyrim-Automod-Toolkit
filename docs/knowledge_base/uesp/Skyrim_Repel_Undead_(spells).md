- *All affected undead up to level <mag> flee for 30 seconds.*

The **Repel Undead** spells are [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells, which will cause all affected [Undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) enemies to flee from the caster for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Repel Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(effect).md), <mag> pts for 30 secs
- [Turn Undead Push](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 100 pts

## Perks
- [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md), increases magnitude by 25% and duration by 50%.
- [Restoration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Restoration_Dual_Casting.md) increases duration by 120%, but does **not** increase effect level.

The effective levels of undead that can be turned with these spells are:

| Spell | Default | Necromage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Repel Lesser Undead | 8 | 10 |
| Repel Undead | 16 | 20 |

The durations are the same for all spell levels, varying only with perks applying (remember, for dual casting, you need both the perk and to be dual casting it):

| Perks | Duration (seconds) |
| --- | --- |
| None | 30 |
| Necromage | 45 |
| Dual Casting | 66 |
| Dual Casting, Necromage | 99 |

## Notes
- Unlike the [Turn Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(spells).md) spells, these cast a swirling wave projectile that will affect multiple targets. Their range is much shorter, however.
