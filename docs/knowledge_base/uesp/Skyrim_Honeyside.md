[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-place-Honeyside.jpg/200px-SR-place-Honeyside.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Honeyside.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Honeyside.jpg) Honeyside **Honeyside** is a two-story [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) in the northwest corner of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-interior-Honeyside_08.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Honeyside_08.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeyside_08.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeyside_08.jpg) Kitchen and Bedroom Area This house, the second-least expensive home that can be obtained, becomes available for purchase during a quest for the [Jarl of Riften](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md). The quest can be started at the [Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) outside Riften; look for an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) woman, [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md), working inside the Fishery. However, you need not contact her at the Fishery to start the quest since she can be contacted outside work hours to initiate the quest as well.

Note that the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) will not even mention selling the house until you have completed the associated quests [Helping Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helping_Hand), [The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid) and [Supply and Demand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand), and also built a reputation with Riften citizens by doing a few miscellaneous quests for the people of the town (see [I Done Got Thaned!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:I_Done_Got_Thaned!) for details). Honeyside must be purchased from her steward [Anuriel](Skyrim_Anuriel.md) in order to complete the quest and receive the title of [Thane of Riften](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Rift.md).

Alternatively, if you give Riften to the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or conquer it during [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md), Honeyside can be purchased from [Hemming Black-Briar](Skyrim_Hemming_Black-Briar.md) without having to complete or begin the quests for the Jarl.

Honeyside is a small two-story house, with access from within Riften and from the docks just outside the city. The house comes with a basic kitchen, bedroom, basement with housecarl's quarters, and a dock. A [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md), [Iona](Skyrim_Iona.md), will be employed when you have become a Thane of Riften. The garden will regrow after spending 30 in-game days outside of Riften and all its buildings including Honeyside.

## Notes
- Riften does not have a smelter. Since there is no smelter nearby, if you live in Honeyside, you have to travel all the way to [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) to create ingots at the smelter there.
- There are two windows on the outside wall of Honeyside, facing [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md). However, once you enter, no windows are seen, as the space on the wall where the windows supposedly are located is taken up by the fireplace.
- Any outdoor containers (not inside the building) eventually respawn and delete anything that you may have placed in them. This includes the fish barrel on the deck.
- The music that plays inside the house is environmental music, rather than city music.
- Some of the staged objects obtained when purchasing decorations can't be moved by you (such as forks).
- If Wujeeta dies prior to you completing the quest [Helping Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helping_Hand) and starting the quest [The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid), then the only way to purchase the house is if the Imperials occupy Riften.
- This house has a unique back porch entrance that allows you to access the house from outside the city, which can be handy for avoiding guards while you have a price on your head. It's also incredibly helpful if you're encumbered and have a horse. You can hop on your horse, fast travel to the Riften stables, and then ride your [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) right up to the back door for easy loot unloading.
- The three weapon plaques and four weapon racks can all be used to remove quest-marked weapons from your inventory.
- A copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Enchanter's Primer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Primer)* can be found on the table in the enchanting laboratory.
- [Adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) children living here will have unique dialogue comments about Riften: [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-interior-Honeyside_09.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Honeyside_09.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeyside_09.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeyside_09.jpg) Child's Bedroom - *Maramal said I could visit the temple whenever I wanted.* (girls)
- *Mister [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) gave me an apple, Papa! He seems nice.* (girls)
- *There are lots of scary people here, Mama. Promise you'll keep me safe?* (girls)
- *I wanna go swimming, but the guards keep yelling at me. The canal's not that dirty...* (boys)
- *Mister Brynjolf said he could teach me a lot of neat things when I'm older.* (boys)
- If you ask your spouse "How are the kids?" they will reply with one of the following, depending on their [voicetype](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md#Adoption_Dialogue): - *Oh fine, love, if you want to bring up little pickpockets. This place is a terrible influence on a child.* (Arrogant)
- *Just fine, love. The weather here is wonderful, perfect for playing outdoors.* (Neutral)
- *Well enough. Riften's a tough city, but... it's a tough world. Kid has to learn to stand up for themselves sometime.* (Nord)
- *Hmph. This city's crawling with thieves. Cowards. Kid could learn some bad habits here.* (Orc)
- Version 4.3.5 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) prevents the fish barrel on the deck from respawning.

## Bugs
- There are a few bugs which affect all houses, see the [bugs](Skyrim_Houses.md#Bugs) section of the Houses page for details and fixes.
- Jumping onto the bed in the main bedroom while the Housecarl Iona is in the room (but not an active follower) will cause her to say one of her passing phrases, even when you aren't even close to her. Jumping onto anything else doesn't cause this bug, however. <sup>**?**</sup>
- While the purchase price is officially 8,000 gold, the minimum gold requirement is set to the price for Breezehome, so the jarl's steward will accept as little as 5,000 gold. If you have between 5,000 and 8,000 gold, the steward takes whatever you have. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.13, fixes this bug.
- Visiting the house for the first time before becoming a thane of Riften may prevent you from ever being able to be a thane, and you won't get a housecarl. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- Accessing the bookshelves in another home (e.g. [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md)) may cause the bookshelves at Honeyside to appear empty when activated. Any books that were inside the 'empty' shelf may have been deleted. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.

- Books can be rescued by removing them individually.
- Switching the enchanting room in Honeyside for a child's bedroom will cause you to lose any equipment stored on the mannequins. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.1, addresses this issue. Equipment stored on them will now be transferred to the chest near the double bed upstairs.

- Switching back to the enchanting Laboratory, all items remain on the mannequins, weapon racks, and plaques and will be recoverable.
- When you buy the garden upgrade, the rest of the options may be removed from the steward's dialogue, making it impossible to buy any other upgrades. - To avoid this, purchase the garden last when buying upgrades.
- When you purchase the enchanting area upgrade, the upgrade will not take effect, and the option to purchase it will remain in Anuriel's list of upgrades for purchase. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can use the following console commands to fix the issue. Note that these commands do not require you to actually buy the enchanting area upgrade. (use the [Hearthfire prefix](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) for xx)
```
prid xx004258
disable
prid c7f1c
enable
```
- The world interactions - Courier Delivering an Important Message or Taunting Adventurer - may spawn inside Honeyside's bedroom. <sup>**?**</sup>
