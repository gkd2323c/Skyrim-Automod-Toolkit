[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-item-Sword_of_Jyggalag.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sword_of_Jyggalag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sword_of_Jyggalag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sword_of_Jyggalag.jpg) Sword of Jyggalag The Sword of Jyggalag is an artifact associated with [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md), the Daedric Prince of Order. It can be found in the [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md) at the end of the [related quest](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md), and can be given to [Staada](Skyrim_Staada.md) as part of [her quest](Skyrim_Staada%27s_Quest.md). It does not come with its own enchantment, but can be enchanted at an [Arcane Enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md). [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Sword of Jyggalag requires one [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), and the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk makes tempering twice as effective.

## Related Quests
