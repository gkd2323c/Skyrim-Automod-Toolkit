[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-npc-Edda.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Edda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Edda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Edda.jpg) Edda **Edda** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) from [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) who, during the day, can be found near the market stalls, asking for septims. She sleeps in [Beggar's Row](Skyrim_Beggar%27s_Row.md) between midnight and 7am.

Edda wears a beggar outfit consisting of [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) and [ragged boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat and also carries some [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).
