Endare was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) general that was active in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). In life, she was a controversial figure that served in the early years of the First [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). She was the commanding officer of the Jade Dragoons, an elite militia of Altmeri soldiers. She acquired the epithet of the **Jade Butcher** for her involvement in the massacre at [Cormount](Lore_Cormount.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMAC-1)</sup> In general, she was known for being extremely thorough, whether in the battlefield or not.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONBrelor-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/ON-place-Battle_of_Cormount_Memorial.jpg/200px-ON-place-Battle_of_Cormount_Memorial.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Battle_of_Cormount_Memorial.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Battle_of_Cormount_Memorial.jpg) The Battle of Cormount Memorial Endare was a veteran fighter who served in the military before Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md)'s birth.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONEndare-3)</sup> She became the leader of the Jade Dragoons and traveled to the Kingdom of Grahtwood as part of Ayrenn's intervention in the civil war between [Aeradan Camoran](Lore_Aeradan_Camoran.md) and the Blacksap Rebellion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGelthior-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONGorinir-5)</sup> The General led the assault on the rebellion's primary stronghold, Cormount.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMAC-1)</sup>

In the dead of night, they entered the city and were met with hostilities by the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), no matter their allegiance in the war. According to an account of the incident, the Jade Dragoons razed the [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) forest with their torches. Families fled their homes as the cavalry rounded up the children, forcing their parents to watch as they strangled them. The retaliation was led by Camoran Gorinir, who called upon the aid of the Green but only found it poisoned by Endare's mages. Even with Endare's victory over Cormount, the resistance survived underground, awaiting another chance to strike against the Aldmeri Dominion. For her effort in the conflict, Endare was recognized as a hero by the Dominion and its people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMAC-1)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/ON-place-Falinesti_Winter_Site.jpg/200px-ON-place-Falinesti_Winter_Site.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Falinesti_Winter_Site.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Falinesti_Winter_Site.jpg) The Falinesti Winter Site In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Endare and the Jade Dragoons were sent to the port town of [Haven](Lore_Haven.md) to assist the city guard in maintaining order. She arrived at the height of Fleet Day and at the peak of activity in Haven's port.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AD-6)</sup> At some point, she learned about an ancient artifact known as Rajhin's Mantle, which was buried deep in the winter site of [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md). Endare and her troops left Haven for the northwest, leaving it completely defenseless.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AD-6)</sup> At the winter site, they forced the Falinesti Faithful to excavate the area until they discovered some frozen [ayleid ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONBrelor-2)</sup> Once they did, they unearthed several [Frost Trolls](Lore_Frost_Troll.md), who slaughtered most of the Jade Dragoons. Endare and a handful of survivors pressed onward into the ruins.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONCirtor-7)</sup>

The guardian of the ruins, one of Rajhin's Shadows, suspended them in the prison to guard against the invaders, turning them against each other in a cannibalistic frenzy. Endare advanced further into the ruins and met the spirit, Nairume, who was imprisoned there by Rajhin's Shadow. Endare ignored her and traveled into the inner vault where she discovered the mantle. Once she took it, Endare became corrupted by its magic and turned on her own soldiers. An [Agent](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the Dominion tried to pursue her to stand down, but it was in vain and they had to strike down Endare. The Agent left the ruins with the mantle and freed the spirit from her prison. The mantle was purified of its evil magic and it was later used by Ayrenn for her ratification ceremony.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONWinter-8)</sup>
