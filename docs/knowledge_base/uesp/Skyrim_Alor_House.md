## Walkthrough

### Exterior
In front of the entrance is where Fethis Alor generally sells his wares, with a narrow table holding five bottles of [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma) nearby. To the left of the entrance is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and an open box containing two more bottles of sujamma. To the right of the entrance are two ash yam urns, a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), another woodcutter's axe, and two [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants. The entrance has an apprentice-level lock.

### Alor House
The entrance leads into the upper floor of the house. Along the south wall are three straw bales, an ingredient barrel, a fish barrel, two meat barrels, two food barrels, one of which has a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on top, an open box containing a copy of *[Words of the Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Words_of_the_Wind)*, and a table holding two bottles of sujamma, a bottle of [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shein), two bottles of alto wine, three bottles of wine, and a *[note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dearest_Dinya)* to Dreyla. Near the west wall is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand in front of the fire, with two chairs nearby. Along the north wall are three food barrels, a meat barrel, three unusable barrels, three crates, a sideboard holding a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and copies of all seven volumes of *[The Poison Song](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song)*, five sacks, an ash yam urn, and two loose pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) on a wood pile. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with three straw bales, two ash yam urns, and a bench around the sides of the stairs.

At the bottom of the stairs, to the left of a doorway are two unusable barrels. Beyond the doorway is an area with rooms beyond doors to the southeast and northeast and a crafting area ahead in an alcove to the west. In the main area is a tanning rack, three sacks, a meat barrel, many unusable barrels, one of which has a food urn on top, and many crates. In the alcove to the west are four crates, a table holding a bottle of sujamma, a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), an open box containing seven [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot), and a chair.

The room to the southeast is Fethis Alor's bedroom. Along the north wall is an owned double bed with a chest at its foot, and a sideboard holding a bowl of [cabbage potato soup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage_Potato_Soup), a [potion of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Stamina), a bottle of sujamma, and copies of *[The Red Year, Vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year,_Vol._I)* and the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Hallgerd's Tale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hallgerd%27s_Tale)*. Near the east wall are three unusable barrels, two chairs, and a table holding three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox), a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe), and a copy of *[The Axe Man](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Axe_Man)*. Along the south wall is a fish barrel, a set of shelves holding two empty [petty soul gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem) and a large coin purse, with three letters to Fethis Alor titled *[first letter from EEC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:First_Letter_from_EEC)*, *[second letter from EEC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Second_Letter_from_EEC)*, and *[third letter from EEC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Third_Letter_from_EEC)* on the floor in front of the shelves, and two ingredient urns.

The room to the northeast is Dreyla Alor's bedroom. Along the north wall are two unusable barrels and a bench. Near the east wall is a meat barrel, an owned single bed, a table holding a copy of *[Bone, Part I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_I)* and a bottle of shein, and a chair. Along the south wall are two meat barrels, a set of shelves holding a chest, copies of *[The House of Troubles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles)* and all three volumes of *[History of Raven Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock)*, three [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), and two [green apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), and a sack.
