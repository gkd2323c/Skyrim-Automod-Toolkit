## Radiant Options
This is a radiant quest which you can receive from either [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md) or [Brina Merilis](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md), whichever is the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of the Pale. The Jarl lives in [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Befriend the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md).
2. Assist three people of the Pale.
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed if you wish to become [Thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md). In order to receive this quest you must first aid the current [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md). If [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md) is Jarl of the Pale, then normally, you must first complete [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) and then [Kill the Giant](Skyrim_Kill_the_Giant_(Jarl).md) in order to befriend him. If Skald was ousted from [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md), and then reinstated after [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Stormcloaks).md), that will also befriend him. If [Brina](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md) is Jarl of the Pale (whether she was installed via [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial).md) or [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md)), then she is automatically friendly to you.

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the jarl again and he or she will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People). In order to assist the people of the hold, you must befriend three people who are citizens of the Pale. Usually you do this by completing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) on their behalf. All of the favor quests in the Pale are for people in Dawnstar, except for Aeri, who lives at [Anga's Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill). Note that you can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md) perk by investing in their stores.

### Reward
After helping three inhabitants, the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The Blade of the Pale is awarded along with the title. The Blade of the Pale is a randomly-leveled and randomly-enchanted sword. There is a 50% chance that it will be a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), and a 50% chance it will be a [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword). If you have [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, then [Gregor](Skyrim_Gregor.md) will also be assigned to you as a housecarl.

## Notes
- It is possible to become Thane of the Pale and receive a leveled Blade of Pale twice. First from Skald and then from Brina.
