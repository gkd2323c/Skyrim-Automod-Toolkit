[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/LO-map-Summerset_Isle_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Summerset_Isle_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Summerset_Isle_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Summerset_Isle_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Summerset Isles; Calluis Lar is located off the coast of the northern Auridon Calluis Lar is one of the minor islands of the [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) archipelago. It is located in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), off the coast of the northern [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), near the city of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md).

During the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), it was a popular hideout of the pirates. A few days before the revolt of the [Trebbite Monks](Lore_Trebbite_Monks.md) against Queen [Morgiah](Lore_Morgiah.md) the activity of the pirates in the area increased. They had been more brazen and began to attack royal barges in organized raids. [Reman Karoodil](Lore_Reman_Karoodil.md) decided to deliver a crushing blow to the criminals who were using the islet as a base and ordered the greatest part of his militia to invade the island. He led the incursion himself. Although the outcome of the intervention is unclear, he returned to his castle after a few days.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFR-1)</sup>
