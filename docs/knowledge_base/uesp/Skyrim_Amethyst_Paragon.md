[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Paragon.jpg) The Amethyst Paragon The Amethyst Paragon is located on a [frost giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md) northwest of the Shrine of Learning in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), the frost giant is on the east shore at the end of the river, next to the waterfall that leads into the [Darkfall Grotto](Skyrim_Darkfall_Grotto.md) and opposite the path up to the paragon platform. If placed into the gem holder for the [Paragon Platform](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform), it will create an opening to an area above the Darkfall Grotto, with a chest filled with leveled loot.
