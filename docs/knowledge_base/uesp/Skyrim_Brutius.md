He will invite you to join him, but this is all a ruse.

His spells and perks depend on his level, see [this page](Skyrim_Bandit.md#Mages) for details.

## Quest-Related Events

### No Contest
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Contest.jpg) Brutius reveals the ruse When you first approach him:

- **I'm here for the fishing contest. Are you ready to begin?**
- *"Yeah."*
- **You're Brutius? I expected someone younger. You're just a feeble old man.**
- *"I don't have to take that from you!"*
- **Shall we make a wager, then?**
- *"How much?"*

- - **100 gold.**
- **500 gold.**
- **1000 gold!**

- **Where shall we hold the contest?**
- *"Here's where you need to go."*

When you meet at the specified location, he will attack you with his fellow accomplices.
