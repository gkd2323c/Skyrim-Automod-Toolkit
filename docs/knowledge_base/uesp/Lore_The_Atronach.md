# The Atronach

*This one sees … strength. Yes. The clenched fist and walls of stone.*
*This one senses a heart of stone. Or is it ice?*
*Ah, it is the Atronach that guides you. Sturdy, adaptable. Your sign grants you a steady arm and a nimble mind. You can be fire, stone, or ice. Adapt, but remain true to yourself.*
—Baandari fortune-telling for those born under the Atronach<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Atronach (sometimes the **Golem**, known as the **Warmachine** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md))<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> is a constellation of ten stars which is in the night sky during [Sun's Dusk](Lore_Sun%27s_Dusk.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup> It is one of the [Mage](Lore_The_Mage.md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Atronach are thought to be natural sorcerers with deep reserves of magicka, but that cannot generate their own magicka.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

[Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) who followed the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) modeled their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor after the constellations. Each part of their armaments were crafted in honor of different constellation. Maces were linked to the Atronach, "who bludgeons the sky with magical might" and "rolls like juggernaut twixt night and day". Their maces emulated the Atronach's mighty arm and were designed to "smite thy foes even as rocks that fall from ye sky".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CM41-5)</sup>

The constellations are present in ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) poetry.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWC-6)</sup> In one of their songs The Golem is "burning through the night."<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Coyle-7)</sup>

The Dwemer associate the Atronach with the letter r.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

According to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) the charges, or the Lesser Stars (the Atronach among them) are protected by the Guardians in the Sky who stand together against [the Snake in the Stars](Lore_The_Snake_in_the_Stars.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARANA-9)</sup>

According to the [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) fortune tellers, the Atronach is associated with sturdiness and adaptability.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Atronach was also believed to "aid ones with their appointed burden".<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Uriel-10)</sup>

In certain circumstances, the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) attach significant importance to aligning their landmarks with constellations. A prime example of this practice is Ondil, built in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) to house Kinlady Fiorallelle's extensive collection of ceramic figurines. However, the project was abandoned before completion when it was revealed that its alignment with the constellation of the Atronach deviated by seven percent.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Ondil-11)</sup>
