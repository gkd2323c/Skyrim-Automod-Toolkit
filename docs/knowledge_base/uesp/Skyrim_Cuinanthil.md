He wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md). He is the brother of [Ernanthil](Skyrim_Ernanthil.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
When spoken to:

- **Never mind, I mistook you for someone else.**
- *"I understand."*

- **Spare a coin for a beggar?**
- *"Walk away. Now."*

- **Are you new in town? I can help show you around.**
- *"Walk away. Now."*

You can try and inform Cuinanthil about the planned robbery:

- **Someone is trying to rob you. I have proof.**
- *"You expect me to believe that?"*

- - **In this note the thief mentions you by name. (Persuade)**
- *"It would seem so. Now, get out of my way."* (succeed)
- *"That doesn't change anything. Now, get out of my way."* (fail)

- - **On second thought, never mind.**
- *"And now you'll walk away, if you know what's good for you."*

You will then need to return to him later in the quest in a courier's outfit:

- **I've been looking for you.**
- - He doesn't reply.

Next choices of dialogue will appear.

- **I'm a courier and I have an important message to deliver to you.**
- *"You expect me to believe that?"*

- **Greetings, I'm with the courier service.**
- *"You expect me to believe that?"*

- **Got something I'm supposed to deliver - your hands only.**
- *(He turns to his Dunmer body guard and says), "Do you wish to confess something now?'* (This choice of dialogue will cause the bodyguard to start fighting city guards and civilians in his attempt to escape arrest.)
