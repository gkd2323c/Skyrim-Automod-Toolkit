- *Summons a Skeleton Champion that gains +10% attack damage for each nearby enemy (up to 5) for 60 seconds.*

**Conjure Skeleton Champion** is an Adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Effects
- [Conjure Skeleton Champion](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy), Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health with 100.

## Notes
- **Conjure Skeleton Champion** is unaffected by [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md).
- The Skeleton Champion uses a unique version of the Steel Greatsword called the Skeleton Champion Greatsword. Although it cannot be obtained in game, it has the same base damage as a Skyforge Steel Greatsword.
