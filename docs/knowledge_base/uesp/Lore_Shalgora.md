[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/DF-map-Shalgora.png/200px-DF-map-Shalgora.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Shalgora.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Shalgora.png) Map of Shalgora Shalgora was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) county on the southeastern coast of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It shared borders with [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) to the west, the [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md) to the north, [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md) to the northeast, and [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) to the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Its capital was the city of Shalgora. The [Haarvenu](Lore_Haarvenu.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region, and the national deity was [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>
