[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-place-Lady_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lady_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lady_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lady_Stone.jpg) The Lady Stone **The Lady Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) scattered across [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Lady_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lady_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-Lady_Stone_(active).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-Lady_Stone_(active).jpg.md) The Lady Stone, when activated *Those under the sign of [The Lady](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Lady) regenerate [Health](Skyrim_Health.md) and [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) more quickly.*

Activating the Lady Stone provides the player with a 25% boost to both health and stamina regeneration rates.

## The Lady Stone
It can be found in middle of [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md), on an island directly north of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). There are a few upright stones flanking it and many [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), [blue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) and [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) on the island and around the stones. You will also find many [monarch butterfies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monarch_Butterfly) and [blue butterflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly) during the day and [luna moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth) and [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) in the evening and at night. Three [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) linger near the island which will attack if they see you. An [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) can be found on the southwestern end of the island.

## Notes
- The treasure chest for [Treasure Map X](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_X) is located underwater just east and slightly north of the stone.
- The [loading screen](Skyrim_Loading_Screens.md#Standing_Stones) for The Lady Stone implies the stone provides a power rather than a constant effect ability, which is incorrect.
