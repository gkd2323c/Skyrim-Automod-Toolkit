# Levinace

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/OB-creature-Storm_Atronach.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Storm_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Storm_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Storm_Atronach.jpg) A summoned Storm Atronach Levinace is a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). It is the home of the [Storm Atronachs](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOmounts-1)</sup>

It might be assumed that Levinace is a 'collective realm' similar to [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md), created as an extension of its Storm Atronach inhabitants. If so, the realm would be rather mundane and uniform in appearance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FNHQA-2)</sup>

Levinace is well known to all [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) due to the ability to summon subservient atronachs from the realm.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Summoned Storm Atronachs are usually compelled to take a roughly humanoid form, often for combat purposes. Sufficiently skilled conjurers can compel atronachs from this realm to assume any number of exotic forms, such as for use as summoned mounts. [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md) is famed as a pioneer in this field.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOmounts-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CD-6)</sup>
