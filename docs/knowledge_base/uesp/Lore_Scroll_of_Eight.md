Scroll of Eight *A list of the eight sacred aspects of the Ghost Snake*
---

- Serpent
- - Darkness
- Devourer
- Dreamer
- Silence
- Light
- Death
- Rebirth

These are the eight sacred aspects of the [Ghost Snake](Lore_Ghost_Snake.md). Each contains a truth, a lie, and a mystery that entwines upon itself, forever undulating like the coils of the great serpent.

Ghost Snake, accept our humble offering!
