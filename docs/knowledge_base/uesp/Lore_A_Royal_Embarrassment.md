A Royal Embarrassment **by [Aemilianus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Aemilianus_Falto), "A Concerned Citizen"** *A concerned citizen's open letter disparaging the Evermore royal family*
---
Can we be frank, just one citizen to another? It's dismayingly obvious that what we have left for a royal family here in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) is just not going to suffice. Nothing against the late King [Eamond](Lore_Eamond.md), who was a reasonably strong voice from the throne—though it was a shame the way he planned to allow Moneybags [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) to get away with giving nominal rulership over the [Fallen Wastes](Lore_Fallen_Wastes.md) to [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md) (who's never even been there, so far as I know).

However, now that Eamond's gone, we're in deeper trouble. Princess [Elara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elara) acts more like a flighty [Wood Elf](Lore_Bosmer.md) than an honest [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) maid, and Prince Adrien —well, you've heard about his gambling debts, haven't you? And that he's a little too fond of the old spiced [wine](Lore_Wine.md)? All true, from what I've heard. In fact, that story about his half- [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) bastard might even be true too, so far as I know.

And as for Queen [Arzhela](Lore_Arzhela.md), to be charitable, let's just say she isn't playing bowls with a full set of pins. Sewing with her thimble on the wrong finger. Fishing for [mudcrabs](Lore_Mudcrab.md) without a net. You know, waltzing barefoot on [oyster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oyster) -shards with a [duck](Lore_Duck.md) egg, a nose-flute, and a nine-pound hammer. Got me?

So where does that leave us? Dangerously bereft of strong leadership, that's where. And I'm not about to accept another [bird](Lore_Bird.md) -brained, gift-wrapped import from [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), I can tell you.

Where, then, can we turn? Personally, I've got my eye looking east, toward [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The [Empire](Lore_Empire.md)'s a proven source of strong leaders when we Bretons are in the soup, going all the way back to Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md). And they worship the proper [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), too—none of your weird [Tall Papas](Lore_Tall_Papa.md) or [Malacaths](Lore_Malacath.md) for me, thank you very much.

The Empire. Think about it. I'm pretty sure you'll agree.
