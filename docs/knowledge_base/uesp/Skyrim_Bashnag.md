[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-npc-Bashnag.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bashnag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bashnag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bashnag.jpg) Bashnag at an arcane enchanter **Bashnag**, an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Conjurer), is the leader of the [spellsword](Skyrim_Spellsword_(NPC).md) coven residing in [Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum). He cast [Selveni Nethri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Selveni_Nethri) out of his circle of mages, cursed and chased her into a [spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) web, giving you the opportunity to save her as part of a [miscellaneous quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri).

He spends most, if not all, of his time at the [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) in the back of the dungeon. He always wears [ancient Nord armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Armor.md), but the rest of his equipment is leveled. His boots can be [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md), [steel cuffed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots) from level 6, and [steel plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots) from level 18 onward. His gauntlets will always be enchanted with either [Fortify Alchemy](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Alchemy), [Fortify Marksman](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Marksman), [Fortify One-Handed](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_One-Handed), [Fortify Smithing](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Smithing), or [Fortify Two-Handed](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Two-Handed); and they can be [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md), [steel Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets) from level 7, [Dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven_Gauntlets.md) from level 13, [steel plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets) from level 19, [Orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Gauntlets) from level 26, [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony_Gauntlets.md) from level 33, [dragonplate](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Gauntlets.md) from level 41, and finally [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric_Gauntlets.md) from level 49 onward. He also wears two pieces of unenchanted leveled jewelry, specifically a [ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring) and an [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md). He wields a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), which can be [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace), [Orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Mace) from level 9, [Dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven_Mace.md) from level 15, and [elven](Skyrim_Elven_Mace.md) from level 22 onward. He also carries a leveled [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md), three random [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), and some gold.

His spells are taken from the standard [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) spell lists. Technically speaking, he is an unnamed necromancer that will respawn, but has been given the name Bashnag through the related quest. In addition to a name, he's also been given a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Art of War Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Art_of_War_Magic)*, and the [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), and [Conjure Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md) spells. This simply means that when he respawns, he'll have a generic necromancer name, without the book and extra spells.

## Related Quests
- **[The Savior of Selveni Nethri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri)**: Help a necromancer escape from [Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum).

## Notes
- With [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) installed, his equipment can also consist of [Nordic material](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic).
- One internal reference names him "**Hacklecomb**".
- Bashnag almost never leaves the room he is found in under normal circumstances. This applies even to normal combat; if you are having trouble with him, run back the way you came and he very likely won't follow.

## Bugs
- He can be named generically as a necromancer instead of Bashnag when he is the target boss of a radiant quest.
