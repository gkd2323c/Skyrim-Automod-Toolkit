## Walkthrough
The shrine is in the mountains east-southeast of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). It is composed of three levels, with blood stains on the ground and bloody bones and burned corpses around the shrine. If you are level 30 or higher, a number of [Boethiah Cultists](Skyrim_Boethiah_Cultist.md) and a Priestess of Boethiah will be present; speaking to the priestess will start the quest [Boethiah's Calling](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Calling.md) if it has not already begun. If you approach the shrine at level 29 or below, the quest won't start, and no one will be there.

On the lowest level is a makeshift camp with a tent and three bedrolls, as well as a small arena. There are also several crates near the tent, with three bottles of [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md) and a lit lantern on one of them and a small sack leaning against it. A [shovel](Skyrim_Shovel.md) leans against another crate. Two weapon racks are in the arena, with bloody bones scattered throughout. Two [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes are on this level, with one at the camp and the other in the arena. On the middle level is a tent surrounded by cages. On the left side of the tent is a burned corpse with all four [embalming tools](Skyrim_Embalming_Tool.md) next to it. Inside the tent, there is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) to your left and an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) to your right, with two [jugs](Skyrim_Jug.md) in between. On the highest level is the shrine, with a mysterious pillar in front of it. If you have a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) with you inside the circle, you can activate the pillar. Tell your follower to use the pillar, and they will be drawn toward it, causing them to be trapped. This allows you to kill your follower and loot their corpse without consequence (see [here](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Calling.md#The_Sacrifice) for a workaround). A nearby unlit brazier holds two [red apples](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md) and a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md). On the other side is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), with a [weak poison](Skyrim_Weak_Poison.md) and a [weak stamina poison](Skyrim_Weak_Stamina_Poison.md) nearby.

Two [gold ore veins](Skyrim_Gold_Ore_Vein.md) are 300 feet south-southwest of the Sacellum, next to a small animal den. You can also find sixteen snowberry bushes, eight [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), three [purple mountain flowers](Skyrim_Purple_Mountain_Flower.md), an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md), and a [stone quarry](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> on your way up the hill.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/LO-lettering-Sacellum_of_Boethiah_Carvings.jpg/200px-LO-lettering-Sacellum_of_Boethiah_Carvings.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-lettering-Sacellum_of_Boethiah_Carvings.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-lettering-Sacellum_of_Boethiah_Carvings.jpg) The Daedric carvings The magic pillar is surrounded by spiral [Daedric carvings](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) on the ground, with a magical channel that lights up when you enter its zone. The Daedric carvings are mirrored, and must be read from the outside to the center. Translated into the Latin alphabet, the text reads as follows:

- I am alive because that one is dead
I e[x]ist because I have the will to do so

This is a direct quote from the book *[Boethiah's Proving](Skyrim_Boethiah%27s_Proving.md)*.

## Notes
- As you climb the mountain path to the shrine, no matter what time of day it is, the sky will darken rapidly as if it were night. Nevertheless, music that plays during the day will still play here during daytime hours.

## Bugs
- The sky may not darken as you make your way to the shrine. Also, if you go back down the hill during the day to revert the sky to its normal state, then immediately turn around and go back up the hill, the sky may remain bright and not darken again. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The weapons in the arena may be hovering in the air in front of their respective weapon racks. <sup>**?**</sup>
