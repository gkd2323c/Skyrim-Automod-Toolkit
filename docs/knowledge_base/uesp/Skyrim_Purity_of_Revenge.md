## Quick Walkthrough
1. With [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) as a shield brother, infiltrate the [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md)'s refuge.
2. Retrieve the [Fragments of Wuuthrad](Skyrim_Fragments_of_Wuuthrad.md).
3. (Optional) Wipe out the [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md).
4. Return to [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Revenge Served Cold
After the death of [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md), [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) is looking for revenge. He wants to go after the [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) for the attack on Jorrvaskr and the further affront of stealing the [fragments of Wuuthrad](Skyrim_Fragments_of_Wuuthrad.md). With no questions asked, Vilkas will then act as your follower all the way to the Silver Hand hideout at [Driftshade Refuge](Skyrim_Driftshade_Refuge.md), a small fort southeast of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). Upon arrival at the fort, dispose of the two Silver Hand members outside, a guard at the entrance and an archer on the roof, then head inside. Vilkas will equip the helmet of his [Wolf Armor](Skyrim_Wolf_Armor.md) for the duration of the assault.

### Den of the Silver Hand
Not surprisingly, Driftshade Refuge is crawling with hostile Silver Hand members, so take advantage of your follower and kill the opposition one by one. In the two-leveled room with many boxes on the floor, make sure to search for *[An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_An_Explorer%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* which will add a few map markers for later exploration. It is located on a bookshelf beneath an elk head mounted on the wall. The tunnel north of the room contains a locked door, so unlock it and wipe out yet another Silver Hand. In the next room, a set of spears blocks the entrance but the corresponding lever is easily located right next to the spears. Proceed forward and enter Driftshade Cellar.

After the first turn, make sure to loot the hidden treasure chest, then follow the path and make use of the [Spike Wall](Skyrim_Spike_Wall.md) trap to dispose of yet another enemy. Ignore the locked cell with the dead [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolves.md), activate the trap again and run to the area behind it for another treasure chest. Hit the pullbar on the wall to get back out, and head forward to another room with two enemies and, hidden on a shelf, the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill book *[The Rear Guard](Skyrim_The_Rear_Guard.md)*. The cellar now turns into a snowy cave, and the next room is in two levels, with a Silver Hand sniper on the ledge above. Kill him and examine the cages, one of them holds a living werewolf, who will attack you if you pick the lock, the other one contains a dead [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-quest-Purity_of_Revenge_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Purity_of_Revenge_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Purity_of_Revenge_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Purity_of_Revenge_02.jpg) The Fragments of Wuuthrad
### The Fragments of Wuuthrad
After passing through a torture chamber, the next large chamber contains more enemies, an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md), a coin purse, and two potions on a bookshelf behind the fireplace. Take the only available path, leading back to an initially inaccessible part of Driftshade Refuge. The first chamber contains the three remaining members of the Silver Hand, the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill book *[2920, Morning Star, v1](Skyrim_2920,_Morning_Star,_v1.md)*, the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill book *[Warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(book).md)*, a treasure chest with considerable loot and finally, the stolen Fragments of [Wuuthrad](Skyrim_Wuuthrad.md). Use the door to the south for an easy escape route, then return to [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md). As soon as you arrive, [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) will inform you that you should meet up at the Skyforge and pay your respects to the deceased, but now avenged, [Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md). This will end the quest and start the next one, [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md).

## Notes
- If you have a [follower](Skyrim_Follower.md) with you, Vilkas will replace that follower during this quest. Be sure to give any important equipment to your follower before entering Jorrvaskr, as Vilkas won't help if you're overencumbered, and you won't be able to trade things with him yet.
- Kodlak's body cannot be interacted with or searched, as the quest script makes it uninteractable. You can, however, pick up a [steel warhammer](Skyrim_Steel_Warhammer.md) found next to it.
- Vilkas tells you that no one else got hurt in the Jorrvaskr assault if you ask him if anyone else was injured. However, this is clearly incorrect, as [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md) is wounded, and [Ria](Skyrim_Ria.md) is tending to him.
- Don't bother transforming into a werewolf during this mission--thanks to a [bug](Skyrim_Vilkas.md#Bugs), Vilkas will become hostile towards the player upon witnessing them as a werewolf.
- When clearing out Driftshade Refuge, unlike [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) during "[Proving Honor](Skyrim_Proving_Honor.md)" and [Aela the Huntress](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md) during "[The Silver Hand](Skyrim_The_Silver_Hand.md)", Vilkas will make no comments whatsoever, except for his standard combat dialogue. You can, however, still ask him for training in [two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) weapons.
- Version 2.1.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) reserves Driftshade Refuge to block radiant quests until this one is finished. After that point it should switch over to regular bandits and then become available for use in standard radiant questing.

## Bugs
- The shortcut to zoom in on the map point will not work for this quest for some people. The map point is nevertheless visible at the location specified in this article.
- If Driftshade Refuge has already been cleared, this quest cannot be started normally without a workaround. Before you begin Blood's Honor, wait 30 in-game days after you have cleared Driftshade Refuge to allow that dungeon to reset (you will have to revert to a slightly earlier save to do this). This should allow Purity of Revenge to begin normally upon completion of Blood's Honor. An easy way to check if the dungeon has been reset is to check the doors--if they have all been shut, it is reset (e.g., if the formerly barred door is barred again). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The console may aid in fixing this. Click on Vilkas and use the `disable` and then `enable` commands, then use `setstage c05 0` and talk to him.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Another method that has worked is to reset the interior of Driftshade Refuge by typing `Reset Interior Driftshade Sanctuary01` into the console. Then go and visit Driftshade Refuge and confirm that it is reset. It should be populated with Silver Hand fighters. Do this before talking to Vilkas, and when you do speak to him the next quest may start.
- Kodlak's corpse in Jorrvaskr causes him to appear without his Wolf Armor equipped. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- The Silver Hand radiant quests are available until the last stages of Purity of Revenge, in particular if you return to Whiterun before Vilkas and speak to Aela the Huntress to get a radiant quest. Note that you can't exploit this to get a third Silver Hand radiant quest, since Aela will offer such quests only if you previously completed just one radiant quest from the Silver Hand pool. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12948)) Those quests are no longer available once this one begins.
- Fast travelling to [Fort Fellhammer](Skyrim_Fort_Fellhammer.md) during this quest may automatically complete the optional objective to wipe out the Silver Hand members at Driftshade Refuge, though they may not actually be dead when you arrive. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. The bandits at Fort Fellhammer all had a script attached to them when they never needed one.
- If Blood's Honor has already been completed and this quest won't begin because the [Helm of Winterhold](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Jarl_-_Caves).md) miscellaneous quest is active, it is possible to proceed through Driftshade Refuge without killing anyone (use stealth, fear or invisibility as needed) and take the Helm of Winterhold from the chest at the end. Once the Helm is returned to the Jarl of Winterhold to complete that quest, return to [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) and Purity of Revenge will begin. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) A possibility to start this quest is by completing the Helm of Winterhold quest by typing `setstage favor158 20` into the console before talking with Vilkas.
- Should the "Retrieve the Helm of Winterhold" quest pose a repetition problem, simply go to the Jarl Korir, take the quest one more time and enter `setstage favor158 20` on the console. This quest shouldn't be a problem anymore. On the console, proceed by tapping `Reset Interior Driftshade Sanctuary01`. Then return to Vilkas and on the console, enter `setstage c05 0`.
- Even after wiping out the Silver Hand, you may not be able to wait (i.e., to end your run as a werewolf) getting the message there are still enemies nearby. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If Blood's Honor has already been completed and this quest won't begin, and you've tried all other possible solutions, the quest may be skipped entirely to avoid any bugs. Load a game right after accepting Blood's Honor from Kodlak, and while standing in any interior cell, type `setstage c04 200`, then `setstage c05 200`, and finally `setstage c06 00` to start the next quest and attend the funeral bug-free. <sup>**?**</sup>

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage C05 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest C05`.
