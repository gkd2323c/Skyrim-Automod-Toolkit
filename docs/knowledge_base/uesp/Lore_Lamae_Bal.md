*The first vampire came from Molag Bal. She... was not a willing subject. But she was still the first. Molag Bal is a powerful daedric lord, and his will is made reality.*
— [Serana](Lore_Serana.md) Lamae Beolfag (better known as **Lamae Bal** or the **Blood Matron**) was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) priestess of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), who was made the unwilling bride of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), and transformed into the first [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) sometime between the middle and late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vorundil-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FC-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb_1)</sup> She is the progenitor of a strain of vampirism caused by [Noxiphilic Sanguivoria](Lore_Disease.md#Noxiphilic_Sanguivoria).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESONox San-3)</sup> Over time, she discovered that as others fed from her and spread her curse, instead of becoming weaker she became stronger, her power multiplying as the number of her "children" grew.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESORahaja-4)</sup> She is an enemy of both Molag Bal and Arkay.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup>

Through blood and sorcery, Lamae altered her bloodline to create [Blood Scions](Lore_Blood_Scion.md), vampires whose strength almost rivals that of the vampire lords that were turned directly by Molag Bal.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lamae-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Turtle-7)</sup> She embraces her condition, seeking to use it against her hated adversaries.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lamae-6)</sup> She openly encourages vampires to feed freely upon the blood of mortals, and her alterations to the bloodline allow her children to become stronger by consuming blood.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lamae-6)</sup>

## History
Lamae was unfortunate enough to be a priestess of Arkay at a time when Molag Bal, an avid wielder of necromancy, decided to spite Arkay for daring to hold sway over the cycle of life and death.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup> Lamae was brutally raped by Molag Bal in the wilds of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), leaving her viciously mangled. Once Molag Bal finished his assault, he shed a single droplet of his own blood on the gravely injured girl's brow before disappearing back into [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup>

The comatose Lamae was discovered by a coven of nomadic Nedes, who took her in and cared for her wounds.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup> They noticed her injuries unnaturally rapidly heal on their own.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Horrified, the nomads attempted to cremate Lamae, but as the pyre was lit, Lamae awoke in a state of undeath.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Disoriented and enraged, Lamae massacred the nomads and feasted upon them.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> She eviscerated the women and children, and raped the men as savagely as Molag Bal had raped her. Lamae imprecated her foul aspect on Tamriel, begetting many abominations. From the countless brood spawned by Lamae came the first vampires.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OLBTM-8)</sup> Whispers claim that the [Lamia](Lore_Lamia.md) race are descended from the spawn begat by Lamae.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KC:AHC-9)</sup> After finishing her rampage, Lamae began to return to her senses.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Horrified by what she had become, she desperately prayed to Arkay for help.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Despite her efforts, Arkay did not answer.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Embittered by Molag Bal's attack and Arkay's subsequent betrayal, Lamae cursed their names and swore to undermine them wherever she could.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup>

*A handful of greater vampires lay claim to a twisted shape gifted to them by the Defiler. Why should my children not possess such horror-wrought strength? In you, we shall see this form perfected.*
— *Lamae Bal* Lamae later founded a [cult of vampires](Lore_Cult_of_Lamae_Bal.md), its core tenet being hatred for both Arkay and Molag Bal.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> She considered the members her children, who referred to her as **Mother Lamae**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESORahaja-4)</sup> Those infected with Noxiphilic Sanguivoria who found their way to her crypt would be given the option to engage in the cult's initiation ritual, known as the Rite of the Scion.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> During the Rite, initiates would experience visions of Lamae's past and would profane symbols of Arkay and Molag Bal.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> After the first portion of the ritual was completed, the initiate would kneel in a pool of blood. The initiate would then be completely drained of their own blood by the cult's priests. If the initiate was deemed worthy, Lamae Bal herself would appear and fill the initiate with her own blood, enhancing their power and transforming them into a [Blood Scion](Lore_Blood_Scion.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ROTS-10)</sup>

Lamae was seemingly the progenitor of the [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md), [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md), and [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md) vampire bloodlines, which are named after Nedic Wyrd people present at her burning that she devoured.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sot BM-5)</sup> Emblems featuring her visage were spread throughout the Second Era.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ELBal-11)</sup>
