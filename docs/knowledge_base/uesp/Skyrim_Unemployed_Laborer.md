[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-npc-Unemployed_Laborer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Unemployed_Laborer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Unemployed_Laborer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Unemployed_Laborer.jpg) Unemployed Laborer The **Unemployed Laborer** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in the [Frostfruit Inn](Skyrim_Frostfruit_Inn.md) in [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1da) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd) and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and his copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)*.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task. He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.

If he is selected as the contact, then the Night Mother will tell you to *"Speak with the Unemployed Laborer at the Frostfruit Inn, in Rorikstead. Accept his gold, then kill the target. So begins a contract, bound in blood."*
