[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-item-Krosis.jpg/200px-SR-item-Krosis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Krosis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Krosis.jpg) Krosis Krosis is a bronze [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) gained by killing the eponymous [Krosis](Skyrim_Krosis_(creature).md) upon [Shearpoint](Skyrim_Shearpoint.md), a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md). The mask's enchantment improves the user's [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md), [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md), and [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) skills by twenty points. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [iron ingots](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md).

## Notes
- Placing Krosis on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses it gained from tempering.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- Krosis means "Sorrow" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- This is the only dragon priest mask that can be obtained through normal gameplay without the need for dungeon delving. [Volsung](Skyrim_Volsung_(item).md) can also be obtained without dungeon delving, but doing so requires one to climb the side of a mountain.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a [Special Holder](Skyrim_Special_Holder.md) for this item.
