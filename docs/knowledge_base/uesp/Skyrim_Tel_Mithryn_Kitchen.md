It is one of the four mushroom structures in the settlement, northwest of [Tel Mithryn Steward's House](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.md).

## Tel Mithryn Kitchen
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.jpg) Tel Mithryn Kitchen interior The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the east is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand over a fire, four samples of [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), an empty pot, and a cooking spit over a fire. To the south is a table holding another empty pot, six more samples of canis root, a head of [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a piece of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), a piece of [horse meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat), and a piece of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison). To the west are six crates, an open box holding three heads of cabbage, a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and an owned bedroll.
