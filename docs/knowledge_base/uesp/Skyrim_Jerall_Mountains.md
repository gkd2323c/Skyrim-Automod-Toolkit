[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-place-Jerall_Mountains.jpg/200px-SR-place-Jerall_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Jerall_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Jerall_Mountains.jpg) The Jerall Mountains in the far south of Falkreath Hold The **Jerall Mountains** are a mountain range that stretches along the southern border of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

The mountains form the southern border of [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) and [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). Its harsh, cold climate has made the mountains nearly inhospitable, with few inhabitants living there and no major settlements throughout history. Some points of interest include the town of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md), [Bloodlet Throne](Skyrim_Bloodlet_Throne.md), the dragon shrines of [Ancient's Ascent](Skyrim_Ancient%27s_Ascent.md) and [Lost Tongue Overlook](Skyrim_Lost_Tongue_Overlook.md), the Dwemer ruins of [Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund), and the large [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) of [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md).

The [Goblins](Skyrim_Goblins.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a subterranean mountain passage known as [Gromm's Pass](Skyrim_Gromm%27s_Pass.md), although access to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) was recently cut off by a tunnel collapse.

[The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) adds an underground [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin beneath the mountains known as [Rielle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle). It extends into Cyrodiil, but its southern tunnels have been blocked by rubble.
