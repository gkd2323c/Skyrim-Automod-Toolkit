# Sleeping Tree

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-place-Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sleeping_Tree_Camp.jpg) The Sleeping Tree The Sleeping Tree is a strange leafless tree found in [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It grows in a misty pool beside a small cave now named after the tree. A [giant](Lore_Giant.md) [encampment](Lore_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) also named for the tree surrounds it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup> Several legends exist on the origins of the Tree. Some say that a piece of rock was blasted from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) during the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) and landed in Skyrim, and the Tree grew from the resulting crater. Others say that it grew from a spore which fell from a "floating island", referring to [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md), in which case the Tree is one of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) "cousins" of Umbriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRYsolda-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIC-3)</sup>

### Sleeping Tree Sap
[Sleeping Tree Sap](Lore_Sleeping_Tree_Sap.md) is highly valued for its ability to make people feel much healthier for a time, although side effects include temporary slowness and blurry purple vision. A spigot was installed on the Sleeping Tree in [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) to harvest the sap, and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) trading caravans have been known to pay highly for samples.
