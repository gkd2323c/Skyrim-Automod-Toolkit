[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-npc-Snilf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Snilf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snilf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snilf.jpg) Snilf **Snilf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living the hard life of a [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

Like the other beggar in town, [Edda](Skyrim_Edda.md), Snilf has been given a bedroll in the slums of [Beggar's Row](Skyrim_Beggar%27s_Row.md) and sleeps there every night between 10pm and 6am. After waking up, he heads to the market and finds his usual spot near [Marise Aravel](Skyrim_Marise_Aravel.md)'s stall. He then spends the entire day begging the townsfolk for money, at times complaining loudly about his situation and how people tend to ignore him because he is a beggar.

He wears [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) and matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots). He carries a spare set of ragged robes, an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and some [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists).
