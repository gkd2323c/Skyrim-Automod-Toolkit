[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-npc-Thief_%28Dark_Elf_female%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thief_%28Dark_Elf_female%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Thief_(Dark_Elf_female).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Thief_(Dark_Elf_female).jpg.md) A female [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) thief [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Thief.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thief.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thief.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thief.jpg) A male Dark Elf thief **Thieves** are random [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md) that can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Thieve.27s_Guild_Holdup) in the wilderness. There is a 25% chance they will appear as a male [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md), a 25% chance of appearing as a male [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), a 25% chance of appearing as a female Dark Elf and a 25% chance of appearing as a male [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md). They will simply be named "Thief" when you see them in gameplay.

They all wear leveled [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light), a cuirass and a pair of boots with a 50% chance of a pair of gauntlets, all items leveled to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) quality at level 36. To hide their face they will wear either a [mage hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mage_Hood) or a [torturer's hood](Skyrim_Torturer%27s_Hood.md). They will always carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), two leveled [daggers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality at level 46), a level-dependent [gem](Skyrim_Gems.md), and a few gold coins.

They will approach you and demand gold. They can be paid off, bargained with, or, if you are a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), persuaded to walk away. They will show extra respect if you are a [Nightingale](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). Additionally, if you are wearing any piece of [Nightingale armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Armor), the thieves will offer you "your cut". If wearing a piece of regular or improved Thieves Guild armor, they will give some gold or a few lockpicks as a gesture of goodwill.

If you wear a piece of Nightingale armor, they will greet you with deference: *"Hail, Nightingale! Luck has shined down on me and made me a rich man * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *. Here is your cut, as promised."* Likewise, if you wear a piece of Thieves Guild armor, they will say, *"Hail, brother/sister! I saw you walking there and thought you could use this."* Either way, they will leave you with some gold or lockpicks, wishing you well: *"Good luck out there!"*

If you are not wearing Nightingale armor or Thieves Guild armor, they will threaten you: *"All right, hand over your valuables, or I'll gut you like a fish."* You have a variety of possible responses with differing outcomes. If you fail to persuade or intimidate them, they will tell you, *"I'm not going to ask again."* and you can try your remaining options.

**"Here, take it. Just leave me alone."** (Hands them gold) *"Ha, what a coward. Didn't even put up a proper fight."* (They leave you alone) **"I'd rather die!"** *"Then I guess that's your last request, fool!"* (They turn hostile) **"Walk away. Right now. (Intimidate)"** *"Well. I, uh, can see you're not one to be trifled with. Let this be a warning to you!"* (Passed: they leave you alone) *"Nice try, but you don't scare me."* (Failed: they offer you a second chance) **"Do I look like I have any money? (Persuade)"** *"No, I guess not. Well, get out of here before I change my mind."* (Passed: they leave you alone) *"Yes, actually. You look like you're lousy with gold."* (Failed: they offer you a second chance) **"Whoa, brother *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*. Don't you recognize a fellow Guild member?"** (If you carry a piece of Thieves Guild armor) *"Of course! I've seen you at the [Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). My mistake. Good luck out there!"* **"I would think you'd have more respect for a Nightingale."** (If you carry a piece of Nightingale armor) *"A Nightingale? Oh, I had no idea. My apologies. Good luck out there!"* **"I don't have time for this."** *"Don't you walk away from me!"* (Turns hostile)
## Notes
- If you are the Guild Master of the Thieves Guild, they do not have the dialogue responses to recognize you as such, and will treat you with hostility.
- If you are in [beast form](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Form), the thief may still approach you with the usual dialogue.
- If the thief is wearing a Torturer's Hood, you can pick it up in the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md).
- Backing out of conversation will also cause the thief to say "Don't you walk away from me!" and become hostile afterwards.
- Despite saying they have seen you at the Flagon, a thief never appears at the Flagon in-game.
