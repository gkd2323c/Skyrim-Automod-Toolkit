[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/DF-place-Shedungent.png/200px-DF-place-Shedungent.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Shedungent.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Shedungent.png) Shedungent Castle Shedungent is a great castle and laboratory located deep within the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shedungent-1)</sup>

In [3E 403](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_403), [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md) retired to the castle during the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md) between [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFAubk-i-3)</sup> She remained informed of the happenings in Daggerfall by [Skakmat](Lore_Skakmat.md), her dragon familiar.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFC-4)</sup> After the death of her son, [King Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md) of Daggerfall, later that year, Nulfaga was stricken with grief and slowly descended into madness within the confines of Shedungent.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFConcern-5)</sup>

In the events leading up to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Nulfaga was cured of her madness and was responsible for teleporting an [agent](Lore_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) to the [Mantellan Crux](Lore_Mantellan_Crux.md) in [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) to retrieve the [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md). The Mantella was returned to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) at Shedungent, thus beginning the Warp in the West.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Notes
- The doorkeeper of Shedungent that appears as an undead statue was expanded on by developer [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson) for [Loranna's RP](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loranna%27s_RP), where it is explained that the "statue" is actually a [lich](Lore_Lich.md) named Yenearclogh, bound to Nulfaga, who can engage in dialogue.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPHighrock Highjinks-8)</sup>
