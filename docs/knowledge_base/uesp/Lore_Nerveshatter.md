# Nerveshatter

*"You'll be but another notch in my hammer!"*
— [Syl](Lore_Syl.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Syl-1)</sup> Nerveshatter is a warhammer associated with the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) Lady [Syl](Lore_Syl.md), Duchess of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md). It is made from [madness ore](Lore_Madness_Ore.md) and jagged shards of [amber](Lore_Amber.md), and is enchanted to deal shock damage to those it strikes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SI-2)</sup>

When threatened, the eternally paranoid Lady Syl would retreat to the ruined tunnels of Xirethard that run beneath [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md) and would equip herself with [Dark Seducer](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) armor and Nerveshatter.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SI-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> She claimed to have defeated many while wielding the hammer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Syl-1)</sup> Syl was killed by the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) during the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), although it is unknown if she defected to the [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md) or if her death was part of the Ritual of Accession. In any case, Nerveshatter was claimed by the Hero.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SI-2)</sup>

Nerveshatter later found its way to a Bosmer named Gadnor who won the hammer from a coin toss. Over time he became deeply infatuated with it, and researched into its origin, discovering that it was made out of both madness ore and amber. Shortly before his passing, he journeyed to his final resting place in Crystaldrift Cave near [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), and hid Nerveshatter within the cave with a powerful illusion spell. Only those who had walked the Shivering Isles and brought forth to the hammer's altar both madness ore and amber would be able to claim it.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GLWCC-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a manic mage named [Thoron](Lore_Thoron.md) used the [Sword of Jyggalag](Lore_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md) in a ritual within [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md)'s sewers, to magically cut a rift into the Shivering Isles, resulting in a [root](Lore_Root.md) tunnel forming in the sewers, as well as flora, fauna, and objects crossing over into Tamriel<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TJv3-5)</sup> The ritual was stopped by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who killed Thoron and removed the Sword from the heart of the burgeoning Root system.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Some of the Isle's strange objects that crossed over remained however, and would even make it into the inventory sold by some of Tamriel's merchants.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-NNWCC-7)</sup> Among them were amber and madness ore, giving the Last Dragonborn the opportunity to claim Nerveshatter, which they did after hearing of an exotic treasure and its rumored location of Crystaldrift Cave.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
