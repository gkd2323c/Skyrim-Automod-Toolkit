## Quick Walkthrough
1. *(Optional)* Find [Lucan Valerius](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md) at the [Riverwood Trader](Skyrim_Riverwood_Trader.md) and talk to him.
2. Retrieve the [golden claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw) from [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md).
3. Find the secret of Bleak Falls Barrow.
4. Return the claw to Lucan.

**Note**: The events of this quest are closely related to those in the quest [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md), at times taking over important objectives. It can be triggered right after entering Riverwood, but it is recommended to follow the intended path and initiate the Bleak Falls Barrow quest in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). Otherwise, you may miss a few story-related events and details. See [this section](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md#Notes) for details.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Stolen Heirloom
In Riverwood, you may hear concerned townsfolk talk about a recent robbery at Riverwood Trader. When you pay a visit to the store you will find proprietor Lucan Valerius and his sister [Camilla](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md) in the middle of a hefty argument, both of them frustrated with recent events. Talking to Camilla will not help much, while Lucan will be more informative, although puzzled: *"Yes, we did have a bit of a... break-in. But we still have plenty to sell. Robbers were only after one thing. An ornament, solid gold. In the shape of a dragon's claw."* If you offer to help him get the claw back, he will offer a gold reward and will then continue debating with Camilla. She will argue that you may need a pointer in the right direction and will then get up and leave. Follow her outside and she will point out [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md), barely visible in the distance behind [Alvor and Sigrid's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor_and_Sigrid%27s_House), and lead you to the bridge northeast of town. She will provide some sparse directions, but follow her advice and pick the northwestern trail up the mountain. At this point, make sure you have the [concurrent quest](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md) in your journal. If not, you can go to [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and activate it.

If you don't want to go through the introduction at Riverwood Trader, go straight to Bleak Falls Barrow and you will receive the quest when you overhear two bandits in the first room.

### Retrieving the Claw
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Arvel_the_Swift_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arvel_the_Swift_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arvel_the_Swift_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arvel_the_Swift_02.jpg) *"Cut me down before that thing gets us!"* When you arrive at [Bleak Falls Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleak_Falls_Temple), you may overhear two bandits talking about a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) that's gone on ahead. If you did not speak to Valerius in Riverwood, this moment serves as the start of the quest. After killing these two bandits and progressing further into the barrow, you will see another bandit approaching a gate. Upon activating the lever for the gate, the bandit is killed by [poisonous darts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dart_Trap) and the gate remains closed, blocking your progress. Notice the broken piece of rock next to the lever. It has the image of a snake. Notice the [spinning symbols](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spinning_Symbol) behind you to the left. These rocks must be rotated to the correct position to activate the lever. Look up to see the carvings on the wall that denote the correct sequence: *Snake, Snake, Whale*. (The middle rock has fallen off the wall and onto the ground, hence the snake being the right solution.)

Continue through the gate and descend the wooden spiral staircase to your left. Three skeevers will emerge and attack, but they shouldn't pose a problem. As you progress further, you will hear [Arvel the Swift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift) calling out for help. Some webs block your path; cut through them to proceed.

When you find the thief, Arvel the Swift, he will be trapped in a spider web by a [Wounded Frostbite Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wounded_Frostbite_Spider). Kill the spider and talk to Arvel. Arvel claims that he will give you the claw if you free him from the web. Once you free him, Arvel will flee, forcing you to pursue him deeper into the dungeon. Alternatively, if the spider is too strong for you, you can just free him from the web and pursue the fleeing Arvel. You can also retreat out of the room to recover, as the spider is too big to leave. If you overtake Arvel quickly, you can kill him yourself; otherwise, he will soon run into a group of draugr, who will likely eliminate him easily. If you protect him from the draugr, he may be killed by the [spike trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall) up ahead. The spike trap will reset after use, and can be activated again to help deal with the draugr. You can loot the claw and a [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel%27s_Journal) from Arvel's body. The journal hints that the claw is actually a key of some sort.

### Find the Secret of Bleak Falls Barrow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-quest-The_Golden_Claw.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Golden_Claw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Golden_Claw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Golden_Claw.jpg) The palm of your hand Although you have found the golden claw, you cannot return it to Lucan until you find the secret of Bleak Falls Barrow, so continue through the chambers. After a while you will find yourself in what appears to be a burial tomb and you will have to fight your way through a group of draugr. If you have not killed Arvel by the time he reaches this room, the draugr will likely kill him. Continue on until you find a [puzzle door](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Puzzle_Door) with three animal seals on it. The claw is the key, however it will not open the door like a regular key would. Instead, [examine](Skyrim_Controls.md#Change_View) the claw in your inventory and you will see several symbols engraved in it, palm side up. Set each ring to the correct seal and open the door. From the outermost to the innermost ring, the solution is **Bear—Moth—Owl**.

When you enter the door, follow the tunnel until you find yourself in a large, open room with waterfalls. You will see a [Word Wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md). When you approach it, one word will stand out, and your character will learn a Word of Power, *[Fus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fus)*. A level-dependent creature (at low levels a [Draugr Overlord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draugr_Overlord) and at higher levels a [Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md)) will then rise out of a coffin and you will have to kill it. Leave the dungeon and return to the village. To leave the dungeon, climb to the top of the stairs and activate the lever on the pedestal.

There are three chests in this room where you obtain the Word of Power; one centered by the sleeping draugr is quite well seen and unlocked. The second is behind the waterfall on the left. The third has a novice lock and is located to your right behind the waterfall. To find it, go across the bridge but not up the steps leading to the wall, go right along the river path to the bigger waterfall, you will have to jump around on the large stones to the right of the waterfall to gain access to the chest. You will see the chest nesting among the rocks behind the waterfall when in third person view.

### Return the Claw
If you return the Golden Claw, Lucan or Camilla will give you a leveled amount of gold, increase their [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) toward you to "friend" (assuming you previously were not), and return the item to its home on the counter.

| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 400 |
| 10–19 | 600 |
| 20–29 | 800 |
| 30–39 | 1,000 |
| 40+ | 1,200 |

## Notes
- This quest counts towards the [Sideways](Skyrim_Sideways.md) [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) regardless of whether you do the quest for Lucan or Camilla (i.e., both quest stages 100 and 110 qualify).
- You cannot return the claw to Lucan until [this part of the concurrent quest](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md#The_Secret_of_Bleak_Falls_Barrow) is complete.
- [The shop](Skyrim_Riverwood_Trader.md) is open 24 hours if you don't finish this quest.
- While guiding you to the bridge, Camilla frequently gets stuck in place. Walking a few steps exactly behind her in line with the road prevents this, and if already stopped, returning yourself to that exact position will usually make her start walking again. If all fails, just go on and leave her; she will return to Lucan and her directions are not needed for the quest.
- Arvel is immune to any damage before you cut the webs he is trapped into.
- If Camilla is somehow killed Lucan will exclaim how he wishes Camilla was here to see this.
- * If you don't return the golden claw to Lucan, it will remain a weightless quest item in the inventory, but if you display it on the claw displays of additional houses added by the Creation Club, you can remove it without returning it to Lucan. If you return it to Lucan, it will be placed on the counter and taking it will always be a steal. If you steal a golden claw, you can make the stolen tag invisible while the player is holding it or putting it in the box, but if you display it as a physical object, the stolen tag will not disappear in any way. This affects the display of the golden claw, making it fall all the time when the player re-enters the house.

## Bugs
- If the puzzle door does not work, check [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Puzzle_Totems#Bugs) for possible fixes.
- If you kill Arvel before he turns against you, then you cannot check your next objective. However, this can be fixed by reading his journal and skipping to the last step of returning it to Lucan once the dungeon is finished.
- Casting any [frenzy spell](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md#Spells) on the Wounded Frostbite Spider will cause Arvel to run free and begin to fight it. After it dies or the effect wears off, he will stand where he is and engage in the normal dialogue with you. You still have to go cut the webs, and after you do so, he will begin his sprint down the hall like normal.
- If you don't speak to Lucan before going into Bleak Falls Barrow, you may trigger a bug which will prevent you from completing the quest if you attempt to give him the claw after you have obtained it in the dungeon during the [related quest](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md). You can fix this by loading a save from before the beginning of the quest.
- Even if you finish the quest, the chest in the first floor of the Riverwood Trader may still need to be stolen from. <sup>**?**</sup>
