# Boneshaver

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/BL-item-Boneshaver.jpg/200px-BL-item-Boneshaver.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Boneshaver.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Boneshaver.jpg) Boneshaver (Blades) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-item-Boneshaver.jpg/200px-SR-item-Boneshaver.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boneshaver.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boneshaver.jpg) Boneshaver (Skyrim) Boneshaver is a legendary saber named after the warrior god of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md), [Onsi](Lore_Onsi.md) the Boneshaver. Onsi himself is rumored to have gifted the saber to the Ra'gada. Its blade is noted as being sharp enough to be able to cut through any foe.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Boneshaver-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-REA-2)</sup>

The saber's physical features are ornate with dragon imagery. Its pommel is shaped like a dragon's head, and has areas on the saber's hilt that resembles scales. The blade itself features a pattern akin to that of flowing water.

## History
Sometime after the end of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), around [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) and member of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), Azzin, was the wielder of Boneshaver. Azzin tasked the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) to kill a dragon by the name of Shulknunaak. The Warrior came into possession of the blade after refusing to kill the dragon and defeating an angered Azzin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-3)</sup>

At some point before [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the blade was owned by Ismal, a Redguard noble from Sentinel who was working with the Thalmor. He was defeated by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) who dispatched him and a unit of Thalmor due to the [Remnants](Lore_Remnants.md) request. The Last Dragonborn claimed the blade for themselves.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SI-4)</sup>
