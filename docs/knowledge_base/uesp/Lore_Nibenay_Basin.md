[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg/200px-OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) Nibenay Basin [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/OB-map-Nibenay_Basin.jpg/200px-OB-map-Nibenay_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) Map of the Nibenay Basin The Nibenay Basin is subregion of [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) that consists primarily of the eastern side of the valley of the [Niben](Lore_Niben_River.md), and its tributaries. It's mostly an open, agricultural area where the [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenay.md) [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) hail from. The regions of the [Nibenay Valley](Lore_Nibenay_Valley.md) and the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) are sometimes considered as an extension of the Nibenay Basin area, with the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) being located on an [island](Lore_City_Isle.md) at the head of the valley. Beyond the [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) foothills at the eastern end of the basin, the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) form the border with the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). To the south, the [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md) marks the border with [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>

As the drainage basin of the Niben, a number of tributaries and solitary lakes can be found in the region. These include the [Reed](Lore_Reed_River.md), [Corbolo](Lore_Corbolo_River.md) and [Silverfish](Lore_Silverfish_River.md) rivers, and the lakes [Arrius](Lore_Lake_Arrius.md), [Poppad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lake_Poppad) and [Canulus](Lore_Lake_Canulus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>
