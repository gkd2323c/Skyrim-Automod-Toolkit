The Care and Feeding of Trolls **by [Gulzog the Butcher](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Gulzog_the_Butcher)** *A step-by-step guide to caring for nirncrux-infused trolls*
---
By order of Chief Braadoth, who now wants to be known as the Ophidian Exarch of Undulating Destruction for some reason or another, we are to make sure these special [trolls](Lore_Troll.md) are well cared for. Follow these steps and I won't use you as troll chow.

- 1. Bathe the trolls in a mix of water, nirncrux-infused mud, and blood.
- 2. Massage each troll for at least one hour.
- 3. Fill their drinking buckets with [bog-iron](Lore_Iron.md) ale.
- 4. Tenderize captives with sound beatings.
- 5. Toss tenderized captives to trolls.
- 6. Stand back and let them eat!
- 7. Repeat every day.

— Gulzog the Butcher
