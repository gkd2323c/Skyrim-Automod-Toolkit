## Usage
The broom is one of 4 items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a [Staff of the Flame Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Flame_Atronach), [Staff of the Frost Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Frost_Atronach), or [Staff of the Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Storm_Atronach).
