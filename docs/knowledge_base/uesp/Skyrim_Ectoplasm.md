## Ingredients
40 guaranteed samples can be found in 27 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 5 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Forsaken Crypt](Skyrim_Forsaken_Crypt.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [the Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in the [Palace of the Kings Upstairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))
- 2 in [Yngvild Throne Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngvild_Throne_Room) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Ectoplasm is dropped by all varieties of ghosts and other ethereal undead, including [dragon cultists](Skyrim_Dragon_Cultist.md), [sanctuary guardians](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanctuary_Guardian), and [spectral warhounds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spectral_Warhound). It also has a decent chance of being dropped by the inhabitants of the Soul Cairn: the Mistmen, Wrathmen and Bonemen.

## Notes
- Ectoplasm is frequently confused with [glow dust](Skyrim_Glow_Dust.md), which comes from [wispmothers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother), and has completely different alchemical properties.
- 3 bowls of ectoplasm are needed at [grindstones](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) to improve the [Ghostblade](Skyrim_Ghostblade.md) (requires the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) [perk](Skyrim_Perks.md)).
- It is one of 3 items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a [conjurer's elixir](Skyrim_Conjurer%27s_Elixir.md).
- The base magnitude of the [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) effect for this ingredient is 4 instead of 5. This is only relevant if ectoplasm and [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 20% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Fortify Destruction potion.
- Ectoplasm can sometimes be found in chests in the Soul Cairn.
