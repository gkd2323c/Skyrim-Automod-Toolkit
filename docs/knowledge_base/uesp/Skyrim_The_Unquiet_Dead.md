## Quick Walkthrough
1. *(Optional)* Ask an innkeeper for work until you receive the [Restless Spirits bounty letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_-_Restless_Spirits).
2. Travel to Goldenhills Plantation.
3. Investigate the plantation house.
4. Investigate the well.
5. Return to the plantation house.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead.jpg) Rin and the old well You can start this quest by either asking an innkeeper for work until they give you [Bounty - Restless Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty_-_Restless_Spirits), or you can simply head straight to Goldenhills Plantation.

As you arrive at [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md), a ghost will attack you, after dealing with it you can collect the [torn page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torn_Page) next to the corpse of [Urval](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urval). Head inside and go to the top floor to find [Urval's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urval%27s_Journal) which shows that Urval accused his wife of being a witch before attacking her, after which she appears to have poisoned him. The next step is to find her secret laboratory to uncover the truth, head down to the basement and look to the right as you enter, there is a button on the wall by the door, press it and the cupboard will move to reveal the secret lab.

Enter the laboratory and the ghost of [Jonquil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jonquil) will appear and attack you, after dealing with her, read the [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jonquil%27s_Journal) on the apothecary table to discover the truth about what occurred at Goldenhills Plantation. After their son disappeared, Urval and Jonquil killed each other, Jonquil with extract of Deadly Nightshade, Urval with an axe each thinking that the other had murdered their son, Rin. To put their spirts to rest you need to discover what happened to Rin.

Head back upstairs to Rin's room, and look under his bed to find his [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rin%27s_Journal) which mentions that he was going to go to the old well to hunt a wolf. Leave the building and head southeast towards [Gjukar's Monument](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gjukar%27s_Monument), as you are heading towards it, look to the left and you will see the old well surrounded by wolves. Once you have dispatched the wolves, take Rin's toy sword from the child's skeleton.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Unquiet_Dead_03.jpg) Urval, Jonquil and Rin Take the toy sword into Rin's room and place it under the Imperial banner, the ghosts of Urval, Jonquil and Rin will appear behind you. Speak to Rin:

**Your family can rest in peace now.** *"Thanks a lot! Here. Take this. For all your hard work."* The [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Goldenhills_Plantation) to the plantation will be placed in your inventory as a reward. The next quest, [A Farmer's Life For Me](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Farmer%27s_Life_For_Me) will then automatically start when you leave the house.

## Notes
- There's a [Nord War Horn](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Nord_War_Horn) in Rin's room, which is unobtainable otherwise, a unique Alembic in [Jonquil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jonquil)'s secret lab, a [Broken Iron War Axe Head](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md#Broken_Iron_War_Axe_Head) and a [Broken Iron War Axe Handle](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md#Broken_Iron_War_Axe_Handle) in the box on the second floor. They will disappear once the rooms are renovated.

## Bugs
- Quest won't start if the wolves at the old well are killed or have not loaded in before fighting the ghost outside of the house.
- The ghosts of Rin and his parents may not show up after placing the toy sword in Rin's room. This appears to happen after you've completed the quest and then reload a save from before completing it. - Restarting the game and then placing the toy sword should fix the issue. Restarting the game will also make the ghost of Rin's mother appear if she hasn't appeared before.
- Quest won't start if a follower or horse kills the ghost in the beginning. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Quest won't start if Urval's corpse does not appear on site. This happens if the cell containing Urval’s corpse is loaded prematurely, causing his body to despawn after a time. - The sure way of triggering this quest is to confront Urval’s ghost the first time you’re in the vicinity of the plantation.
- The ghosts may not appear if you have a follower with you, especially if you have more than one. - You can avoid this by dismissing them before doing the quest or simply having your follower(s) wait outside the farmhouse.
- Quest won't update past "find out what happened to Rin" if you find his body and take his sword before this point. - To fix this place the sword in a weapon rack, then pick it up again.
- If the player leaves after killing the husband and returns at a later time, there is a chance that the ghosts in the house will not spawn, making the quest impossible to complete. <sup>**?**</sup> - This specifically occurs after reloading a save, regardless of location. It is best to complete the related quest in one sitting after killing the husband.
