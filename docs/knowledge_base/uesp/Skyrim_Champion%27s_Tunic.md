It is only available in the [Nintendo Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) version of the game. The item takes up the armor, gauntlets, and boots slots when worn.

## Notes
- The [Champion's Tunic](https://zeldawiki.wiki/wiki/Champion%27s_Tunic) is an item from the *[The Legend of Zelda](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda)* series. It was introduced in *[The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda:_Breath_of_the_Wild)*.
