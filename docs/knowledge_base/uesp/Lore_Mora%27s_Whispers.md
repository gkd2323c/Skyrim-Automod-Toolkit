# Mora's Whispers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/ON-item-Mora%27s_Whispers_02.jpg/200px-ON-item-Mora%27s_Whispers_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Mora%27s_Whispers_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Mora%27s_Whispers_02.jpg) Mora's Whispers (ESO) Mora's Whispers are pauldron artifacts of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md). With its enchantment, its wearer learns faster. Knowledge literally becomes power, as the more you have accumulated, the more beneficial that it is to you. The shoulderpads are an amalgamation of the body parts of the many [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that inhabit [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). Skin from a [Lurker](Lore_Lurker.md) provides for the armor-sheath, rune-etched tentacles from [Watchers](Lore_Watcher.md) protrude the sides, living eyes are embedded throughout, and sinews taken from [Seekers](Lore_Seeker.md) are sewn into it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWCodex-1)</sup>
