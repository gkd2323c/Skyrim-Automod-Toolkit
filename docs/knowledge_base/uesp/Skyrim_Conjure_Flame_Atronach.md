[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Flame_Atronach.jpg) Flame Atronach *Summons a [Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) for 60 seconds wherever the caster is pointing.* **Conjure Flame Atronach** is an apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a [Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

or - [Conjure Potent Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs (with [Elemental Potency](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Potency) perk)

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Atromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atromancy), extends duration to 120 secs.
- [Elemental Potency](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Potency), summons the more powerful Potent Flame Atronach.
