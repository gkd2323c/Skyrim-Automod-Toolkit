[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-item-Champion%27s_Cudgel.jpg/200px-SR-item-Champion%27s_Cudgel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Champion%27s_Cudgel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Champion%27s_Cudgel.jpg) Champion's Cudgel [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-quest-March_of_the_Dead_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-March_of_the_Dead_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-March_of_the_Dead_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-March_of_the_Dead_02.jpg) Carius wielding the Cudgel The Champion's Cudgel is a [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer) wielded by [General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md) who can be found in [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md). The head of the warhammer is made of steel, with the [Imperial Legion symbol](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) engraved on it. One of the ends of the head is blunt and cleated for smashing; while the other end is curved and pointed, like a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). The shaft of the warhammer is made of wood, with some red cloth secured with [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) wrapped around it below the head for a better grip on the hammer. Its damage is equal to that of a [glass warhammer](Skyrim_Glass_Warhammer.md), but it weighs less. For each element, its enchantment has a 50% chance to deal 25 points of [shock damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) and/or [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md).

## Related Quests
- **[March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead)**: Stop the [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) attacks on [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

## Notes
- This is the only guaranteed item with the [chaos damage](Skyrim_Chaos_Damage.md) enchantment in the entire game, including expansions.
- This item has no tempering recipes and cannot be improved at grindstones. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. It can be improved with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), and the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk doubles improvement.
- The hammer is also the only two-handed weapon with an Imperial Legion theme in the game, making it a good choice for players who wish a complete set of Legion equipment and prefer a two-handed playstyle.
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> and [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
