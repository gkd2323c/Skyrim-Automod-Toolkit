# Anthotis

Anthotis is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with great intellect. The bloodline dominates an area of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) —specifically the regions of [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md), [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md), [Bergama](Lore_Bergama_(barony).md), [Dak'fron](Lore_Dak%27fron.md) and [Tigonus](Lore_Tigonus_(fiefdom).md). They are the enemies of the [Thrafey](Lore_Thrafey.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
