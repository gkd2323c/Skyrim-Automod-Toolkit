Authenticity of the Giovessen Skull **by [Mercator Mannix](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mercator_Mannix), Imperial Historian** *A certification confirming the authenticity of a relic*
---
Honorable Thane Fulstrom,

Though the events of [2920](Lore_2920__The_Last_Year_of_the_First_Era.md) are largely fictional of nature, the seed of truth has borne fruit in the form of the Giovessen Skull. It is indeed the gold-dipped and gem-encrusted cranium of [Tavia](Lore_Tavia.md), famously imprisoned in Castle Giovesse at the behest of Emperor [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md). Whispers that the Giovessen Skull is a false relic appear to have been spread by a previous owner in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), no doubt to discourage the attempted theft of such a valuable piece of history.

I hereby certify the Giovessen Skull as authentic. All rumors claiming the source skull was from a condemned [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) prisoner are just that—rumor.

Mercator Mannix, [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Historian
