[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-npc-Verner_Rock-Chucker.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Verner_Rock-Chucker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Verner_Rock-Chucker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Verner_Rock-Chucker.jpg) Verner Rock-Chucker **Verner Rock-Chucker**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is a miner found in [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md). He was one of the original settlers of the mining camp along with his wife, [Annekke Crag-Jumper](Skyrim_Annekke_Crag-Jumper.md), after she discovered [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md). They own and live in the only [house](Skyrim_Verner_and_Annekke%27s_House.md) located at the camp. He claims to love his wife greatly, but is annoyed by the fact she continuously goes out adventuring rather than helping out in the mine. Their daughter [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md) seems to have inherited some of her wanderlust and works in [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) as a miner.

Verner is worried that the mine will eventually run out of [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md), so he will pay for any that is brought to him.

Verner wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000209aa) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) along with a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Special Delivery
If you're visiting Verner for the [related quest](Skyrim_Special_Delivery_(Sylgja).md) and tell him you have a [package](Skyrim_Sylgja%27s_Satchel.md) for him from Sylgja, he will be happy to hear from his daughter and will also ask you to deliver something for him:

- **I have a package for you from Sylgja.**
- *"It's a shame our daughter couldn't make the journey herself, but at least she still thinks of us. If you wouldn't mind bringing [this](Skyrim_Verner%27s_Satchel.md) back to her for us, I'd appreciate it."*
