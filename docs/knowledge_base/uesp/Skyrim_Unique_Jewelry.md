# Unique Jewelry

**Unique Jewelry** are various accessories available in Skyrim that are generally found in fixed locations, unlike most of the equipment in the game, which are randomly generated or generic equipment (see [Generic Magic Apparel](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md)).

Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md) article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md) article. For a list of the rest of the available jewelry, see the [jewelry category](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Jewelry).
