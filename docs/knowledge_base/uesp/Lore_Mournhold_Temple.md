[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-concept-Tribunal_Temple.png/200px-ON-concept-Tribunal_Temple.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Tribunal_Temple.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Tribunal_Temple.png) Mournhold Temple Mournhold Temple (sometimes called the **Tribunal Temple**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTemple-1)</sup> the **Tribunal Palace**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-APGTM-2)</sup> or the **High Temple**)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Generic Dialogue A-3)</sup> was the home of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md). It is situated in the center of a large and decorative courtyard, which lies at the northern end of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), within the city of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia_(city).md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tribunal-4)</sup>

It served as a center of worship for the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), as well as an administrative center for the [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) itself. The temple is connected to the underground ruins of [Old Mournhold](Lore_Old_Mournhold.md) through the temple sewers. [Barilzar's Mazed Band](Lore_Barilzar%27s_Mazed_Band.md) has the ability to teleport the wearer directly to Mournhold Temple.

During the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), the temple displays were quite multicultural, with [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) relics placed alongside [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the temple was invaded by [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) due to a Maulborn plot to overthrow the Tribunal and return the Dunmer to Daedra worship. With Almalexia powerless to act directly, she depended on the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to use the power of the Tribunal to clear the complex of invaders.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Almalexia disappeared from the temple in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), when she teleported to the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) and was slain by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tribunal-4)</sup>

The temple survived the [Argonian Invasion](Lore_Accession_War.md) of Mournhold, as well as the general population's disillusionment with the Tribunal following the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRY-6)</sup> However, the last vestiges of Almalexia's presence were being removed by the [New Temple](Lore_New_Temple.md) almost two hundred years later.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TMFN-7)</sup> Around [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), they added a new sanctuary.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TMFN-7)</sup> [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) had to erect a large building in [Plaza Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Plaza_Brindisi_Dorom.md) to house the many new priests and pilgrims who had come to visit.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TMFN-7)</sup>
