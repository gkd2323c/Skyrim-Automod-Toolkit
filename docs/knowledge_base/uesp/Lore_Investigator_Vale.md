# Investigator Vale

| Investigator Vale | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) | Gender | Female |

Investigator Vale is a fictional [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) detective and the main protagonist of the eponymous series of mystery novels, which were extremely popular during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Her adventures take place across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and often make reference to the current political or social climate. Some people believed that Investigator Vale was based on Lady [Arabelle Davaux](Lore_Arabelle_Davaux.md), a retired member of the [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) and the [Ring of Daggers](Lore_Ring_of_Daggers.md). The author of the series was officially unknown, but some people claimed it was Arabelle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRIV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTCLA-2)</sup> Arabelle officially denied any connection with the Investigator Vale novels,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTCLA-2)</sup> though she did in fact author them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MTCS-3)</sup> It has been claimed that her adventures are "true-to-life" reimaginings of actual historical events.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mt CA-4)</sup>
