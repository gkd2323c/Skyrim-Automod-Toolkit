## Amren's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Armen%27s_House.jpg) Saffir enjoying her reading time Entering leads directly into a large room with a round wooden table and a couple of chairs with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and cheese on top of the table. Beside this is a flight of wooden stairs against the northeast wall leading up to a partially-floored upper floor. On your right is a cupboard, a [fish barrel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel), and a food sack. On the cupboard's shelves are three bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel). If you have already completed the [quest](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits).md) to recover Amren's [sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amren%27s_Family_Sword), this will also be found on a shelf. On top of the cupboard is a bottle of wine and loaf of bread, while on the fish barrel is a bowl containing a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage).

Under the stairs are more food sacks and a barrel, and in the eastern corner of the room is a small table and chairs with a bottle of wine and [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) on top. Above the table are three [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md) and four bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) hanging from a rack. In the middle of the southeast wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand in front and a bottle of wine on the mantelpiece. Coming out from the middle of the southwest wall is a large double bed with an end table to the left holding a copy of *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)* To the right of the bed is a dresser containing [fine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes) and common [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) and a food sack on top. Climbing the stairs leads to [Braith](Skyrim_Braith.md)'s sleeping area, with a single bed at the end, an end table to the right, and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing leveled items at the foot of the bed. On the end table is a copy of *[The Holds of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim)*.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Amren%27s_House) to which is carried by Amren and Saffir.
- There is [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Danger [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the left of the door near the ground due to Amren's high proficiency using [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [weapons](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md). - Due to the plants added in front of the house in the Special Edition, this shadowmark is no longer visible.
- After [returning Amren's family sword](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits).md#Amren.27s_Family_Sword), all low level items in the house become free to take, and both beds become usable.
