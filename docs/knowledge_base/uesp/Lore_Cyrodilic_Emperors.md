## Notes
1. [^](#cite_ref-Shor-El_15-0) The dates of Shor-El's reign are unknown, but Shor-El is mentioned after Hestra in *[Where were you when the Dragon Broke?](Lore_Where_Were_You_..._Dragon_Broke.md)* as part of the Emperor Oversoul.
2. [^](#cite_ref-Remanada_19-0) The *[Remanada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Remanada)* contains a mythical story of Reman emerging from [Sancre Tor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sancre_Tor) as a child, but this should not be taken as literal truth.
3. [^](#cite_ref-Reman_II_28-0) The exact year of Reman II's accession to the throne is unclear and requires some deduction. Reman II's epitaph states that he was crowned emperor in 1E 2812 and reigned for 39 years, but according to *[Reman II: The Limits of Ambition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_II__The_Limits_of_Ambition)*, Reman II died in 1E 2843. *[Emperor Kastav's Epitaph](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Emperor_Kastav%27s_Epitaph)* states that Kastav died in 1E 2812. *[Annals of the Dragonguard](Lore_Annals_of_the_Dragonguard.md)* states that Kastav was deposed sometime between 1E 2804 and 2806. With all the information combined, the most likely explanation is that Kastav was deposed in 1E 2804, and Reman II was only officially crowned emperor after Kastav's death in 1E 2812.
4. [^](#cite_ref-SCd_37-0) Most sources such as *[The Brothers of Darkness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Brothers_of_Darkness)*, *[Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_3rd_Edition)*, and *[Systres History: Volume 7](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Systres_History__Volume_7)* state that Savirien-Chorak was assassinated in 2E 430, but other sources such as the *[Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition)* and the *[Crafting Motifs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crafting_Motifs)* series state that Savirien-Chorak was assassinated in 2E 431. Since more sources state that Savirien-Chorak was assassinated in 2E 430, this year is presumably the correct year of the assassination.
5. [^](#cite_ref-SCMTo DB_38-0) It is unclear which assassin organization assassinated Savirien-Chorak. *The Brothers of Darkness* states that the Dark Brotherhood assassinated Savirien-Chorak, while *[Sacred Witness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sacred_Witness)* states that the Morag Tong were responsible for the assassination.
6. [^](#cite_ref-Attrebus_40-0) It is unknown when the warlord Attrebus assumed the Imperial Throne during the Interregnum. The *[Pocket Guide to the Empire, 1st Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition_The_Elsweyr_Confederacy)*, establishes that Attrebus' reign occurred prior to the city of Rimmen's secession from Elsweyr in 2E 812, and the book mentions that the Akaviri were expelled from Cyrodiil by Attrebus during his reign and that they later settled in the Rim Territories in Elsweyr. The Rim-men already existed by 2E 582, and since every emperor from 2E 533 to 2E 582 is known, this means that Attrebus could have only reigned sometime during the period from the assassination of Savirien-Chorak in 2E 430 to the accession of Durcorach the Black Drake in 2E 533.

Additionally, the original version of *[The Real Barenziah](Lore_The_Real_Barenziah,_Part_VII.md)* refers to Pelagius as Tiber's son, while the revised [version of the book](Lore_The_Real_Barenziah,_v_4.md) instead refers to him as both Tiber's grandson and son, directly contradicting itself. *[The Real Barenziah](Lore_The_Real_Barenziah,_v_3.md)* suggests that Tiber's wife, simply referred to as the Empress, had multiple children. The likeliest explanations include that Tiber and his wife had female children otherwise unrecorded in history. Other possibilities include that the Empress had children that were not Tiber's children, that the two of them had secret offspring, or simply a mistake on the part of the author. Regardless, the throne ended up with Kintyra I.

[^](#cite_ref-PSIIdod_74-0) *The Third Era Timeline* and *[Biography of the Wolf Queen](Lore_Biography_of_the_Wolf_Queen.md)* both state that Pelagius' reign ended in [3E 99](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_99); this is likely a rounded figure, however, as *[The Wolf Queen](Lore_The_Wolf_Queen,_v3.md)* states that he died on the 15th of [Evening Star](Lore_Calendar.md#Evening_Star), [3E 98](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_98) (Evening Star is the last month in the year). *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)* also places the death of Pelagius in 3E 98.

The year 3E 120 can be assumed to be correct, since more sources state that to be the correct year.
16. [^](#cite_ref-KSII_84-0) There is uncertainty over the date of Kintyra's death. According to *Brief History of the Empire*, Kintyra was imprisoned for two years after her capture and then killed, placing her death in 3E 123. As more sources confirm 3E 123 as the year of Kintyra's death, this can be assumed to be correct.

[^](#cite_ref-Kat D_87-0) Both *[Brief History of the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brief_History_of_the_Empire_v_2)* and *The Madness of Pelagius* put the length of Katariah's reign at forty-six years, and *The Third Era Timeline* puts her death in 3E 200.
