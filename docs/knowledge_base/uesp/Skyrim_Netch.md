# Netch

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-creature-Bull_Netch.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bull_Netch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bull_Netch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bull_Netch.jpg) A bull netch [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-creature-Betty_Netch.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Betty_Netch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Betty_Netch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Betty_Netch.jpg) A betty netch **Netch** are a species of large, tentacled creatures that float several feet above the ground. Netch were often domesticated on the Morrowind mainland, while wild netch were first sighted on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) several decades ago. Variants of netch include the betty netch, bull netch, and netch calf. They are often found in groups of three to six: one bull, one to two betties, and one to three calves. They are docile animals and can be approached without incident, but will defend themselves with powerful strikes of their tentacles if attacked.

You may [randomly](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Netch_Hunting_Party) find a group of four [hunters](Skyrim_Hunter.md) preparing to hunt netch in the wilderness of Solstheim. You can choose to assist them, or wish them luck and be on your way.

## Notes
- Because of their shock damage attack, netch can deal surprising amounts of damage to players without high [shock resistance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Shock).
- Despite their size, netch attack rather rapidly, often attacking with "headbutts", which also deal shock damage.
- They have rather slow turning animations, despite their swift forward-moving speeds.
- However, they also have a waterwalking effect so you can't escape them by swimming away.
- The Netch Calf does not have the Netch keyword assigned to it.

## Locations
- One adult Netch can be hunted by Skaal hunters at a random location in southeast [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
- Two pairs of Netch can be found at the coast near [Tel Mithryn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn).
- At the coast north of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md).
