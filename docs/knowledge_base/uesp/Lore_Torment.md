# Torment

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-item-Torment.jpg/200px-SR-item-Torment.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torment.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Torment.jpg) Torment (Skyrim Creation Club) Torment is a powerful [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) longsword, imbued with an [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) that immolates those struck by the blade. Torment is also more lethal when used against those who are already set afire. The blade is inscribed with a single word: "Putrefaction" in [Daedric script](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Torment-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-The_Consequences-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Vakyn_Methats-3)</sup>

## History
Little is known of the origins of the blade, but as of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), it was in the possession of Valkynaz [Methats](Lore_Methats.md), who, alongside with Valkynaz Gatanas, served as the herald for [Mehrunes Dagon's](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) assault on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-The_Consequences-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup> Methats wielded Torment during his battle against the Last Dragonborn in the realm of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), accessed through the portal within Red Scar Cavern. Following the fall of Valkynaz, Torment was retrieved by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-The_Consequences-2)</sup>
