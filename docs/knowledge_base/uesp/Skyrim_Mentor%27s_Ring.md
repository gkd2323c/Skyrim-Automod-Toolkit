The ring is a rare quality junk item, which has a 3% chance (8% chance with the [Dwarven Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Dwarven_Fishing_Rod.md)) of being caught when no fish is caught. See our [Fishing activity page](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Catch_Chances) for more details.

## Notes
- The Mentor's Ring has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Mentor%27s_Ring)* and *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Mentor's Ring)*.
