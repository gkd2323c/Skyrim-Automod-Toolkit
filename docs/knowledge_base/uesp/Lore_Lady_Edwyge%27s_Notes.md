Lady Edwyge's Notes **by [Lady Edwyge](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Lady_Edwyge)** *A journal recounting an ill-fated attempt to bend time*
---

### First Entry

- This is so frustrating. I'm not asking to recreate the [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md), just to roll back time a few years. Is that too much to ask? But [this book](Lore_Vindication_for_the_Dragon_Break.md) tells us nothing! It's just incoherent [Maruhkati](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md) ravings on the nature of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md).

### Second Entry

- We've had a breakthrough! We managed to turn back time a few hours …! As soon as we figure out how to extend it, we can go back and change history. And I shall be Queen of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md), not that puling wench [Arzhela](Lore_Arzhela.md)!

### Final Entry

- We must have done something terribly wrong. We're caught in a time trap—we keep reliving the same few hours over and over again. Any minute now, someone will come in and kill us all to reclaim the book. Then it will happen all over again ….
