Red Bramman, also known as the **Bandit King**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Leg Car-1)</sup> was an infamous [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) brigand based in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) who gained notoriety due to his piracy against the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). He was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LG-2)</sup> but was documented by [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) historians as one of the first [men](Lore_Men.md) to venture deep into the Marsh.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGT-4)</sup> Bramman earned the nickname "Red" due to his red hair, which he possessed despite being an Argonian.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LG-2)</sup>

Bramman and other brigands used the swamplands of Black Marsh to disappear without recourse after raiding the easily accessible riches of the Empire. Bramman established a bandit kingdom deep within the swamps, near the site of the future city of [Blackrose](Lore_Blackrose.md). It was connected to Topal Bay by a narrow, winding river that emptied into the Bay near [Soulrest](Lore_Soulrest.md) and was shrouded from view by mangroves. According to Imperial accounts, Bramman and similar human brigands were the first contact most Argonians had with Imperial civilization. They raped, pillaged, and enslaved the natives, causing Argonians to become skittish of the Alessian Empire.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGT-4)</sup>

In [1E 1033](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1033), Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md) demanded Red Bramman's head. After many unsuccessful battles in Topal Bay, the [Imperial Navy](Lore_Imperial_Navy.md) discovered Bramman's river and sent a fleet to catch Bramman in his brigand kingdom in what became known as the Raid of Red Bramman.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGT-4)</sup> The crew of the *Dominus Fatum* were responsible for his capture.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SC-5)</sup> His head was brought before the Empress, but more importantly the fleet's expedition resulted in the Empire's first reliable accounts of Argonian culture. The Imperials attempted to settle along the pirate routes and were successful in driving the pirates out of Black Marsh, but the province was largely left alone until its eventual conquest by the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) centuries later.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGT-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The fact that Bramman appears as an Argonian in *Legends* appears to have been a mistake or an intentional retcon, given the source material.
- Red Bramman was believed to own a navigation hourglass made of a heavy petrified wood.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Contraband R-6)</sup>
- The Dreadsails, a group of [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) pirates, adopted a tradition from Red Bramman's crew whereby anyone who had survived swells eight sailors high would tattoo a gust of wind on their cheeks. Such tattooes were even used as a letter of recommendation when signing on to a new crew.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFT-7)</sup>
