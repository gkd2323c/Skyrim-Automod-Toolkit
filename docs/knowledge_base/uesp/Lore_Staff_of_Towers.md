# Staff of Towers

This article is about the artifact. For the book, see [Staff of Towers (book)](Lore_Staff_of_Towers_(book).md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/ON-misc-Staff Of Towers.png/200px-ON-misc-Staff Of Towers.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Staff Of Towers.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Staff Of Towers.png) The Staff of Towers The Staff of Towers is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) crafted by the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) arch-mage [Anumaril](Lore_Anumaril.md) around the time of the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). It is composed of eight segments, each corresponding to one of the metaphysical [Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md) that shape the fabric of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Some of the fragments of the Staff of Towers, such as the Walk-Brass fragment, were somehow created by Anumaril as counterparts to Towers which had not yet been made at the time of its creation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Augur_of_the_Obscure-1)</sup> No sooner was the [Staff](Lore_Staff.md) constructed than it was separated into eight pieces, seven of which were taken to far corners of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and hidden by White-Gold Knights. The eighth, made in the image of [White-Gold](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), Anumaril himself wore as a femur while walking with other Ayleid refugees to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and the Tower of [Green-Sap](Lore_Green-Sap.md). There, he located the Stone of the Tower, the Perchance Acorn, and built an orrery around it, attempting to convert Green-Sap into another White-Gold to restore the Ayleid empire. This attempt failed, due to a fundamental incompatibility between Ayleid and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) magic and belief.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AE4t ET-2)</sup>

Centuries later, Arch-Prelate [Fervidius Tharn](Lore_Fervidius_Tharn.md) of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) dispatched agents to recover all eight pieces and reassemble the Staff.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CATAYQ-3)</sup> It was then used by the radical [Marukhati Selective](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md) in an attempt to excise the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) influences from the Dragon God of Time, [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vft DB-4)</sup> The wild ritual resulted in a massive [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md), and once again disintegrated the Staff into eight pieces. The pieces slowly fell back to Tamriel as stars, and were the only means for those trapped in the Middle Dawn to chart the progress of time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WWYDB-5)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the fragments of the Staff of Towers were located by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md). The pieces were scattered all around Tamriel: the [Adamantine](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md) and White-Gold fragments in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), the [Orichalc](Lore_Orichalc_Tower.md) and [Snow](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md) fragments in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), the [Walk-Brass](Lore_Numidium.md) and [Red](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) fragments in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), the [Crystal](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) fragment in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), and the [Green-Sap](Lore_Green-Sap.md) fragment in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). A member of the Psijic Order, the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), sealed the [time breaches](Lore_Dragon_Break.md) the Staff had created around the continent with the aid of the [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md), which allowed them to locate and retrieve the fragments. The reassembled Staff was subsequently stolen by Josajeh, a member of the Order who wanted to use the staff to [alter time](Lore_Time_Magic.md) to reverse damage done to her family in the past, threatening to envelop Aurbis in a new [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md) or even unravel time entirely. The Vestige tracked Josajeh to the White-Gold Tower's Time-Lost Throne Room, a location detached from regular time and space, where she intended to use an Ayleid ritual that could, when used alongside the Tower and Staff, alter time. The Order's agent bested Josajeh and her [alternate timeline](Lore_Many_Paths.md) selves that had entered the chamber through temporal rifts, and reclaimed the artifact for the Order.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOPsijic-6)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Augur_of_the_Obscure-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOCelarus-7)</sup>

## Notes
- A partially translated Ayleid tract, *[The Remnant of Light](Lore_The_Remnant_of_Light.md)*, may describe the journey of one of the "White-Gold Knights". At a time of blood—possibly the Alessian Rebellion—an Ayleid lord is given an artifact by Anumaril and told to take it to the far northern reaches of Tamriel. However, this could also be referring to the Light of Life, the relic stored in the Doomcrag of Rivenspire which was eventually corrupted into the Lightless Remnant.
- The [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) is another similar artifact that was split into eight pieces and scattered across Tamriel. However, this staff was not created until at least two centuries after the Staff of Towers, and was not disassembled until the Third Era.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TFL-8)</sup> - In early drafts of the [Main Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Main_Quest) for *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*, the Staff of Towers played a key role in the story and was called the Balac, meaning that it was the same artifact as the Staff of Chaos.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-9)</sup> This story was later scrapped and the Staff of Towers initially only appeared in the game by mention in some books, later appearing in the [Summerset](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Summerset_(Chapter)) [Chapter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Chapters) several years after launch.
