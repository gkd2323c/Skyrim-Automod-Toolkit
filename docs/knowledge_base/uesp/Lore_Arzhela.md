Arzhela was the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) Queen of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) during the sixth century of [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was a cousin of the King of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md), whom she often played with when they were children.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONArzhela-1)</sup> After [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), King Emeric arranged for Arzhela to marry Prince [Eamond](Lore_Eamond.md) because his father, King [Blaise](Lore_Blaise_Guimard.md), had aided Emeric during the conflict. In [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568), shortly after the marriage of Eamond and Arzhela, Blaise died, so they were crowned the King and Queen of Evermore.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-2)</sup> The couple had two children, [Elara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elara) and Adrien.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIEGTB-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARE-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Evermore faced concurrent invasions from the [Empire](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md)'s [Seventh Legion](Lore_Seventh_Legion.md), led by Magus-General [Septima Tharn](Lore_Septima_Tharn.md), in the south,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BSo HR-5)</sup> and the Dark Witness [Reachman](Lore_Reachman.md) clan in the north.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAdrien-6)</sup> Eamond was killed in battle against the Reachmen at Northglen, and both Adrien and Elara were presumed dead as well, leaving Arzhela as the ruler of Evermore.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAdrien-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONAntieve-7)</sup>

After Eamond's death, Arzhela and the rest of the royal family were slandered in what was most likely Imperial propaganda.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARE-4)</sup> With Arzhela in mourning, she stepped aside and allowed Eamond's brother, Duke Renchant, to serve as regent, as she believed that he was better suited to handle the city's defense against the Reachman invasion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONArzhela-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONGlarikha-8)</sup> Arzhela eventually removed Renchant from power after his plan to surrender Evermore to the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) was revealed, a strategy he believed would protect the city from the Reachmen. Following the exposure of Renchant's plot, Arzhela assumed leadership of Evermore.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOACi B-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOII-10)</sup>

After Arzhela resumed the throne, the Dark Witnesses were defeated.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOHo E-11)</sup> Following the defeat of the Dark Witnesses, Arzhela attended a parley in a remote valley in central [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) with Septima Tharn. At the parley, Arzhela and her guards were attacked by the Seventh Legion, but the Queen managed to escape safely.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOTP-12)</sup> Arzhela later fought alongside Emeric and the [Knights of Saint Pelin](Lore_Knights_of_Saint_Pelin.md) in the battle that recaptured the [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md) from the Seventh Legion.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOSt G-13)</sup>
