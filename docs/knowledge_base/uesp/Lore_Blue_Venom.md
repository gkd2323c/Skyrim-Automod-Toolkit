# Blue Venom

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/ON-place-Blue_Venom_Kanebar%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-ON-place-Blue_Venom_Kanebar%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blue_Venom_Kanebar%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blue_Venom_Kanebar%27s_Camp.jpg) Camp of one of the Blue Venom pirates The Blue Venom were a notorious pirate crew consisting of the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md), the Sea Elves, active in the waters south of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LS-1)</sup>

## History
The exact origins and history of the Blue Venom pirates are unclear. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Blue Venom pirate crew had only four remaining individuals,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LS-1)</sup> namely swordswoman Hasawen,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Hasawen-2)</sup> [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) Kanebar,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Kanebar-3)</sup> bounty hunter Tildaro,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tildaro-4)</sup> and [martial artist](Lore_Claw-Dances.md) and a fire mage Vindaire.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vindaire-5)</sup>

Around the same year, an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) embarked on a mission to track down and eliminate the last four surviving members of this crew. These elusive pirates were scattered throughout the region of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md) in Elsweyr. With the successful elimination of these individuals, the Blue Venom crew was effectively disbanded.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LS-1)</sup>
