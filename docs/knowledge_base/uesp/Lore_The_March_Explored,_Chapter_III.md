[The March Explored](Lore_The_March_Explored.md), Chapter III **by [Fenlil the Wayfarer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fenlil_the_Wayfarer)** *Part of a guide to the Reaper's March region*
---
The [March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) marks the eastern boundary of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and thus a boundary of the Ancient diaspora. While the rare pile of stones can be found deep into [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), the ruins of the March provide some of the most interesting insights into [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) psychology.
