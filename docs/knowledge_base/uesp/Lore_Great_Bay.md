[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/ON-place-Shipwreck_Cove.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shipwreck_Cove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shipwreck_Cove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shipwreck_Cove.jpg) Waters of the Great Bay Great Bay is the body of water found between the regions of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) and [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), serving as an extension of the waters of the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The northern area is much colder than the warm waters of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PL-2)</sup> A number of settlements are found along the coastline of the bay, most notably [Northpoint](Lore_Northpoint.md), [Old Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md#First_Era), and [Morkul](Lore_Morkul_Clan.md) Stronghold. The Jugular,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Rt O-3)</sup> Grudge-Rock, and Two Rivers all feed into it. The inlet of its southernmost coastline forms the Shipwreck Cove, a harsh series of jagged rocks that many ships have fallen victim to.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It is found on the obverse side of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).

## History
When [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) assembled the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md), many shipyards were built throughout Tamriel to supply the fleet with ships. While the center of manufacturing was set in the distant [Systres](Lore_Systres.md), some of the largest operations were based in Rivenspire.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SH3-5)</sup> One such shipyard was located on the Great Bay, east of the main road between [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) and Northpoint. Over time it had fallen into disrepair is known locally as a haunted site called the [Shipwright's Regret](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shipwright%27s_Regret).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONShipwright-6)</sup> Some believe that this village was perhaps the greatest shipyard in High Rock.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONZa.27ji-7)</sup>
