[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/ON-crown_store-Cold_Flame_Atronach_Wolf.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Cold_Flame_Atronach_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Cold_Flame_Atronach_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Cold_Flame_Atronach_Wolf.jpg) Cold Flame Atronach Wolf, Peregrine's personalized mount Peregrine Direnni was one of the prominent members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup> and the pioneer conjuration mage. She was a student of Arch-Conjurer [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md) and was accredited with the summoning of the first [Flame Atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) [Camel](Lore_Camel.md) in history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAC-2)</sup>

In her youth, she was an apprentice of Corvus Direnni and was mentored by him for the Master Conjurer test. During the test, she received high marks for the summoning of an aforementioned flame atronach camel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAC-2)</sup>

In [1E 907](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_907), she drove entire [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) flotilla back to [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) by merging her very will with the waves of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBMOP-3)</sup>

After years of conjuring, Peregrine Direnni sought a new Daedra to compel to be her mount. She took an immediate liking to the Cold-Flame Atronach, found in the Daedric realm of [Takubar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Takubar). She was known to shape it in the form of a wolf, for an extra flair.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CFAW-4)</sup>
