[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Anger.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Anger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anger.jpg) Anger **Anger** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Imperial) who is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_the_Mad) mind. He only appears in [The Mind of a Madman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_a_Madman) during the related quest. He will be beating up the much smaller [Confidence](Skyrim_Confidence_(NPC).md), and you will be tasked with evening the odds with the help of the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md).

Anger is equipped with [studded Imperial armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Imperial_Armor) along with [Imperial light boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md), [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Bracers.md), and a [helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Helmet.md).
