[Coming of the Learned One](Lore_Coming_of_the_Learned_One.md) Vol. I *A Firstmage account of the Celestial Mage's arrival to the Aetherian Archive*
---
Volume I

The [learned one](Lore_The_Mage.md) has begun work in the upper chambers that promises to be the most scintillating magic I have ever witnessed. It will consume the upper floors of the [Archive](Lore_Aetherian_Archive.md), and I am among the stewards of the upper floors. I cannot think of a better way to die than writhing in the sorcery of the learned one—but it means my time on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) grows brief.

Thus, I spend my final days recounting the learned one's arrival. What follows is a recollection of the Archive's defining moment. Though we built it intending to reach for [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), it was a denizen of Aetherius who found us.
