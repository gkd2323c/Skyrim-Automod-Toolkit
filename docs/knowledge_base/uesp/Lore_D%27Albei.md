# D'Albei

D'Albei was a historical figure who flourished prior to the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Campaign-1)</sup> They were of some importance to the history of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), but little is known about them.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1.](#note_nb1) | All campaign names in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) that are named after individual figures, at the time of D'Albei's inclusion, were those who were linked to Cyrodiil's history in some capacity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Campaign-1)</sup> D'Albei's placement in the files is also next to military heroes and [Akaviri Potentates](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md), suggesting D'Albei possibly possessed one of these ranks.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOStrings-2)</sup> |
