[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Moonlit Cove is an ancient Khajiiti temple found on the azure beaches of the [Quin'rawl Peninsula](Lore_Quin%27rawl_Peninsula.md), in the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). It is known for its luminous caverns that contains a plethora of exotic flora and fauna, the likes of which leave naturalists in awe of its glory.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Cove-1)</sup> The moonlight and the waters from the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) and [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) are harnessed and blended together into the cove to achieve the serene and calm ambiance of the night sky, blocking out the harsh sunlight from outside. Many people come to the temple to soak in the blessed [sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md) waters and its shrines to soothe their mind, body, and [soul](Lore_Soul.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SWOTSS-2)</sup>

## History
The Moonlit Cove was built countless years ago at an unspecified time. It is known to contain a variety of creatures and plants that cannot be found anywhere else, such as the [Moon-Kissed Swamp Jellies](Lore_Swamp_Jelly.md) or the [Prune Morel Mushrooms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Prune_Morel_Mushroom). When the Khajiiti Hero, [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) was killed in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) by the [Betrayer](Lore_Cadwell.md), he was buried in an isolated tomb deep in the confines of the Moonlit Cove.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKhunzar-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RT-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the bandit pride called the [Ruddy Fangs](Lore_Ruddy_Fangs.md) took control of the Moonlit Cove and established a strong [skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) operation throughout the temple. In their hubris, they tainted the sacred waters and sought to use the sacred moon sugar in the shoals of the water to make their product. The leader of the operation was the [Pahmar-raht](Lore_Pahmar-raht.md) bandit lord, Bahzahar the Bull, who believed that the blessed waters could make a more potent stock of sugar.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONZa.27ji-5)</sup> Several labs and distilleries were built in the caves and ruins, all of which were overseen by the bruiser called the Tigershark. Anyone that drank from his skooma were known to turn feral and dangerous.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONAmbient-6)</sup> This skooma operation started to fall apart when Bahzahar was killed in a skirmish with the then reforming [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONUneasy-7)</sup> and the waters were purged by an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONSkooma-8)</sup>

A member of the Dragonguard traveled to the Moonlit Cove and entered the tomb of Khunzar-ri, where they communed with his spirit. From there, they acquired his claw and used it to walk the Jodelight Path to the floating island of [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONNew Moon-9)</sup>
