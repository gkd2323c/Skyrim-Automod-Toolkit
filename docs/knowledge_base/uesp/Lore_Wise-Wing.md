# Wise-Wing

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/ON-crown_store-Jhunal%27s_Owl_Mask.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Jhunal%27s_Owl_Mask.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Jhunal%27s_Owl_Mask.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Jhunal%27s_Owl_Mask.jpg) Jhunal's Owl Mask, mimicking the visage of the Wise-Wing Wise-Wing **the Owl** was a mythical [owl](Lore_Owl.md) companion of [Jhunal](Lore_Jhunal.md) the Rune God,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JOW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BFH-2)</sup> [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) god of knowledge and wisdom. Like her, Jhunal’s symbol was also an owl.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Treasures-3)</sup>

Wise-Wing the Owl is linked to the god Jhunal or [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md), in the northern regions of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). During the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md), it is believed that she serves as a protector, cautioning the unwary to steer clear of graveyards and witches' covens where danger may await. Masks were crafted in Wise-Wing's likeness. It was believed by some that wearing her owl-shaped mask would inspire others to trust in the wearer's wisdom and guidance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JOW-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CSS-4)</sup>

She was commonly depicted as a barred owl. Wearing tail-feathers from a barred owl was considered a sign of devotion to the God of Wisdom.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BFH-2)</sup>
