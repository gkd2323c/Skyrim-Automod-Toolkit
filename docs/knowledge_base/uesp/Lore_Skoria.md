**Valkyn** Skoria is a giant [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) whose clan is based at the Knives of Discord, a region of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md). He serves [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) as a Valkynaz (or 'prince'), a member of the Valkyn, Dagon's personal guard. Skoria rules over Red-Zeal Keep, a Sigil Keep found in the Knives of Discord region. The fact that a Valkynaz is posted away from Dagon's side would imply that the region is of particular importance or interest to the Prince.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

In an attempt to enhance the destructive power of Skoria, Dagon experimented with mutating his [vestige](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) by imbuing it with certain aspects of the [Flame Atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) of [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md). Although successful in this regard, the mutation forces Skoria to remain in close proximity to magma at all times in order to maintain his molten condition, and also means that he eternally suffers the agony of immolation.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LFAYQ-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) cultists opened an Oblivion Gate in [Whisper Grove](Lore_Whisper_Grove.md) in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) that allowed a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) invasion from the Knives of Discord. After the death of the cult leader, Razor Master Erthas, Dagon proposed to seal the gate in return for the potential service of another Bosmer named Gilraen. However, Gilraen's husband Fingaenion reopened the Gate later that year in a failed invasion attempt in order to rescue her. Skoria's minions took the trapped invaders as prisoners, but Fingaenion managed to find a group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) and launch a second rescue mission. The group sliced through the Deadlands and eventually forced Skoria to face them in battle, where he was defeated. The keep's [Sigil Stone](Lore_Sigil_Stone.md) was then taken, permanently severing the link to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and likely causing significant destruction in the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Co A-3)</sup>

Following this demise, Skoria's headgear, shoulder armor, and claymore were brought back to Tamriel by the Undaunted, and his head was mounted as a trophy.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Co A-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOtrophy-4)</sup>

[Lyranth](Lore_Lyranth.md) the [Foolkiller](Lore_Foolkillers_Clan.md) looks down upon Valkyn Skoria, and views him as too weak to serve her master [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LFAYQ-2)</sup> It is unknown if Skoria is a member of the [Dremora Clan](Lore_Dremora_Clan.md).
