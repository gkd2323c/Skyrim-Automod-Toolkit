[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-item-Nightingale_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Bow.jpg) Nightingale Bow The Nightingale Bow is a bow given to you by [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md) as a reward for completing the quest [Blindsighted](Skyrim_Blindsighted.md). Its enchantment causes instantaneous [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) over one second, and [slows](Skyrim_Slow.md) the target down for three seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Nightingale Bow requires one [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Blindsighted](Skyrim_Blindsighted.md)**: Stop [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md).

## Notes
- The bow has a projection which overlaps with the marker in the center of the screen. This can be a distraction while aiming.
- [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.
- This bow appears on a [loading screen](Skyrim_Loading_Screens.md), being drawn by a female [Bosmer](Skyrim_Bosmer.md) in an unused version of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md).

## Bugs
- All Nightingale Bows have their sound set incorrectly to "normal" instead of "silent" unlike all other bows, meaning that using the bow will make you easier to discover when using stealth. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- This weapon is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk.
