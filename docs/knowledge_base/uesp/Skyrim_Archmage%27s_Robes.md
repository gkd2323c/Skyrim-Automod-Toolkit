[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-item-Archmage%27s_Robe_%28hooded%29.jpg/200px-SR-item-Archmage%27s_Robe_%28hooded%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Archmage%27s_Robe_(hooded).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Archmage%27s_Robe_(hooded).jpg) The Archmage's Robes The Archmage's Robes are a set of hooded robes that are awarded to you after completing [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md). They provide boosts to all magic based skills except [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md), plus bonuses to [magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) and [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md)**: Reclaim the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) and contain the Eye.

## Notes
Enchantment from the robes is called "Skein of Magnus" in the CK, but the robes in the game cannot be disenchanted.

- Another set of Archmage's Robes without the hood and the 50 magicka point bonus exists in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) data (form ID `0007c932`, editor ID `Clothes MGRobes Archmage`). This version of the robes can be found on one of the [Heroes of Sovngarde](Skyrim_Hero_of_Sovngarde.md) in the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md), but Heroes of Sovngarde can't be killed or pickpocketed. One is present in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) on a table near the bed, but it is permanently set to 'initially disabled', and thus cannot be obtained. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. 'Initially disabled' tag has been removed.

## Bugs
- Although the Archmage's Robes are considered to be hooded, they occupy only the "body" and "hair" body part slots, but not the "circlet" body part slot, unlike most other hooded robes. This means that they can be worn simultaneously with [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md) or [Dragon Priest Masks](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes you can receive two or even three Archmage's Robes after completing the Eye of Magnus quest.
- Unlike all other mage robes, an NPC follower whose default apparel is clothing or robes (not armor) will equip the Archmage's Robes if given. <sup>**?**</sup> - In order for this to work for J'zargo or Brelyna Maryon, either one must be given a circlet to equip as well (since these hooded robes allow a circlet). The circlet must have a minimal gold value of about 350, as their default hoods are enchanted. Otherwise, they will unequip their default robes but leave the default hood on, yet never equip the Archmage Robes and walk around clothesless. <sup>[*verification needed — Are there other followers that have this bug?*]</sup>
