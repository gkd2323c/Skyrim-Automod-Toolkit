## *[Chapter I](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_I.md)*
Gentle reader, welcome. Welcome to the crystal blue waters and gentle zephyrs of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). Jewel of the [Summerset Archipelago](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) and gateway to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), this fair isle has long been home to the friendliest, the most outward-facing of my [noble race](Lore_Altmer.md).

Herein, I've included an unedited journal of observations. Thoughts jotted down during my most recent trip across the isle. Some of the hidden gems of the isle, some of the most striking locales of this sheltered paradise, will see description in these pages.

From I, your humble guide. Enjoy!

— [Fenlil the Wayfarer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fenlil_the_Wayfarer)

## *[Chapter III](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_III.md)*
Ahh, fair [Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md). A part of the [Buraniim](Lore_Buraniim.md) sea-complex during the time of the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md), and then long used as a shipping and warehouse complex by nearby [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md). Then came the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md), disease, and death.

Even in the aftermath of Skywatch's defiance, Sload forces held the island for decades. When it was finally cleansed by a concerted force, a remnant of the [All-Flags Navy](Lore_All-Flags_Navy.md), it was left to rot and decay.

A shame, as its natural beauty is extreme, and its ruins ... magnificent.

## *[Chapter IV](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_IV.md)*
This [island](Lore_Buraniim_Isle.md) is fascinating. It was clearly once a hub for the [ancient](Lore_Ancients.md) [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), perhaps part of a larger structure that extended out into the sea. Now it's used most often as a retreat from the city life in nearby [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md).

I've found some evidence here that the resurgent [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) cults, the [Worm cultists](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md), are making preparations to construct some kind of dolmen here. I'll have to warn the local authorities in Skywatch.

## *[Chapter V](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_V.md)*
Gentle reader, I feel the need to edit my unedited thoughts about the beautiful, unparalleled [Isle of Contemplation](Lore_Isle_of_Contemplation.md). Without a doubt, one of the most beautiful places on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), your fair explorer has many happy memories of quiet meals ... meaningful looks ... moonlit encounters ...

As I said, my time on the Isle of Contemplation has been magical. I hope yours is the same.

## *[Chapter VII](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_VII.md)*
The ruin known by locals as [Hightide Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hightide_Keep) was once a sprawling complex of defensive fortifications. A relic from the time of the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md), it was painstakingly maintained and manned.

As a result, [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) was able to put up an incredible defense during the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) attack on the city. Powerful magical constructs were crafted and expended. Dozens of Sload warships were destroyed.

The city finally fell when a Sload Warcaster filled his voluminous gullet with volatile [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) reagents and threw himself at the base of the cliffs. The resulting explosion toppled much of the extended rock face, and plunged the ruins into the sea.

## *[Chapter XII](Lore_Auridon_Explored,_Chapter_XII.md)*
I'm often asked when the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md) first arrived in the [Archipelago](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). My researches indicate that it was in the middle of the Early [Merethic Period](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).

The farthest northern tip of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), now called Nine Prows Landing, is by tradition the first place within the Summerset Isles to feel the tread of the Ancients. From Summerset, the Ancients spread to the shores of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), settling almost every region of that great, contentious continent. There they built new societies, and found both glory and destruction.
