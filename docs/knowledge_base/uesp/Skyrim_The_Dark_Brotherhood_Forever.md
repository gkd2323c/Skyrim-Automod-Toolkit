## Quick Walkthrough
1. Approach the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md).
2. Speak with the contact.
3. Kill the target.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Keeping the Faith
After the events of [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md), the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) has new headquarters to improve and maintain. As the Listener, your responsibility is to heed the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) and continue to fulfill contracts. For this recurring quest, approaching the Night Mother will initiate a dialogue: another child has prayed to their mother and performed the [Black Sacrament](Lore_Night_Mother.md), securing another contract for the Brotherhood. Travel to the specified location and speak to the client who will give you a name and location of a generic NPC that he wants eliminated. The client will pay you the money up front. Travel to the location and complete the contract. Return to the Night Mother for another contract as needed or desired.

## Notes
- The targets that you are supposed to murder never sleep, and almost always stay in crowded areas. This can make it very difficult to kill them without incurring a bounty. One possible way to accomplish it is to hide a safe distance away (remain in sneak mode and make sure the eye is totally closed) and then send a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) to kill the target. Run outside the area in question while the crowd is retaliating against your follower, and then fast travel out of the hold. Your follower will fast travel with you.
- All targets have 50 health. Plan your assassination tools (such as poisons and weapon choices) with this in mind.
- If the recently killed target is reanimated and subsequently turned into an ash pile on their "second death", the next time they are randomly chosen as a target, both the NPC and the ash pile will appear. Attempting to search the ash pile will cause the NPC to speak. Sneaking and searching the ash pile qualifies as pickpocketing.
- Sometimes if you reactivate (or talk to) the contact while they are still talking it will cause you to get paid twice.
- Sometimes you can intimidate the target when talking to him. This can cause him to attack you, and the surrounding people will try to kill him. If they kill him, the quest will be complete and you will not have a bounty. The target can also try to flee; in that case, chase him into a secluded area, and then kill him.
- Pickpocketing a target will never work, no matter how high your Sneak and Pickpocket skill.

## Bugs
- Sometimes the contact will simply say, *"Look, I'm busy. Please go bother somebody else."* If you return after a few days, the contact will eventually give you information about the target. It will count as speaking to the contact but you will not receive payment. <sup>**?**</sup>
- [Deceiving the Herd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deceiving_the_Herd) can trigger its kill selection setup before you have been told to actually do anything. This can cause the quest to react to this one's target kills inappropriately. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.1, addresses this issue. Keep in mind though that using a Dark Brotherhood target for Deceiving the Herd is perfectly valid. All the fix does is prevent Deceiving the Herd from registering the kill until you're actually told to make one.
