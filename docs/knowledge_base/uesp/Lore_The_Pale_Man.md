The Pale Man was a folklore legend in [Western Skyrim](Lore_Western_Skyrim.md) during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) that was said to snatch people away from the shadows.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Asda-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TPM-2)</sup> In truth, these stories were based on the actions of a single figure, a [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) responsible for the deaths of several people reaching back decades and perhaps centuries.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Aerolf-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Hautgerd-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Eyfja-5)</sup> Witnesses described the Pale Man as gaunt and taller than a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md), with pale skin like a "cave fish" and would move silently.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Hautgerd-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Aerolf-3)</sup> He was also known to have taken trophies from his victims.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOTPM-6)</sup>

A hunter called Aerolf spent thirty years of his life hunting the Pale Man after the death of his brother. It was in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) that Aerolf and [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) tracked the Pale Man to his current lair along the northern coast of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) and lured him out before slaying him.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Aerolf-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOTPM-6)</sup>
