[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-npc-Agrius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Agrius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agrius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agrius.jpg) Agrius **Agrius** is a dead [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found in the first part of [Halldir's Cairn](Skyrim_Halldir%27s_Cairn.md). His dead body is found along with those of his friends, [Vidgrod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vidgrod) and [Raen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raen), next to the magical cairn. According to [his journal](Skyrim_Agrius%27s_Journal.md), he and his friends were in a bandit gang but were kicked out and took refuge in Halldir's Cairn. He was the last of them to be possessed by [Halldir](Skyrim_Halldir.md).

He wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance he will wear [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers) and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

His weapon of choice is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) or a [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe). He also carries a bit of random loot, food and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or some gold.

Agrius' body, along with his two friends, is consumed by Halldir to make his three elemental copies during your fight with him.

## Bugs
