[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Hewnon_Black-Skeever.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hewnon_Black-Skeever.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hewnon_Black-Skeever.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hewnon_Black-Skeever.jpg) Hewnon Black-Skeever **Hewnon Black-Skeever** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who can be found scheming alongside [Drahff](Skyrim_Drahff.md) in the entrance to the [Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md). Both men will attack you on sight.

Hewnon wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) and a pair of normal [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He is equipped with a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality), and a leveled [heavy shield](Skyrim_Armor.md#Shields_2) (up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) quality). He carries one random leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) or [poison](Skyrim_Poisons.md), a 25% chance of a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), and a leveled amount of gold ranging from 25 to 125 gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md)**: Collect some debts for the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Dialogue
As soon as you enter the Ratway and move down the first tunnel, you will hear Hewnon and Drahff discuss their little scheme, although Hewnon is starting to doubt if the plan will ever pay off:

**Hewnon**: *"I dunno, Drahff. [They'd](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) skin us alive if they knew we were doin' this."*
**Drahff**: *"Why are you always acting like such a big baby? I've gotten us this far."*
**Hewnon**: *"This far? We're livin' in a sewer. You said we'd have a house as big as [the Black-Briars'](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) by now."*
**Drahff**: *"You worry about bashing people's heads in, I'll worry about the Guild. Okay?"*
**Hewnon**: *"Okay, okay."*
**Drahff**: *"I'm going to check the entrance to the Ratway. Be right back."*
## Unused Dialogue
Hewnon and Drahff were not always supposed to attack you on sight. See [this section](Skyrim_Drahff.md#Notes) for details. In the case Hewnon survived his initial encounter with you, he would've gained one new line to use:

*"What are you lookin' at?"*
## Notes
- Although Hewnon's outfit is technically supposed to be unique to him, the [Companion](Skyrim_Companions.md) [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md) also wears a copy of it.
