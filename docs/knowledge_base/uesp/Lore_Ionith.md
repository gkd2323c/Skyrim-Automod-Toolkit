Ionith was a large [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) settlement used as a headquarters by [Emperor Uriel Septim V's](Lore_Uriel_Septim_V.md) invasion of Akavir in the year [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> It was originally a [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) town farther upriver from [Septimia](Lore_Septimia.md), but was found abandoned and was taken over by the invasion force to be re-founded as a colony of the short-lived [Imperial Province of Akavir](Lore_Imperial_Province_of_Akavir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> A road linked the settlement to Septimia.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup>

Due to the terrible, almost unnatural weather suffered leading up to the [Battle of Ionith](Lore_Battle_of_Ionith.md), communications between the two cities was lost as the river dried up and the garrisons along the road were lost to raiders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> The Emperor was besieged, and led a valiant march towards Septimia in order to retreat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> This was the last direct contact from the Expeditionary Force, but survivors of the Battle say that the Tsaesci surrounded them and Uriel V was killed, *"cut down by enemy arrows as he rallied the [Tenth [Legion]](Lore_Tenth_Legion.md)'s shield wall"*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDAI-1)</sup> The settlement was reclaimed by the Tsaesci and has never been revisited by Imperial forces since.
