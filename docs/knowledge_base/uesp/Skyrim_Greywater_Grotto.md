It contains only one zone, *[Greywater Grotto](#Greywater_Grotto)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There is a leveled animal guarding the entrance, which is covered in splattered blood and gore, with a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) lying on the ground among it. There are also a number of [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes in the vicinity and an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) above the entrance, accessed by heading west then ascending back to the east.

### Greywater Grotto
The cave is a long descending corridor following a stream down to a shallow pool. The animals in the cave are leveled, and seem to be entirely random. You are as likely to encounter [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) as you are to encounter [snowy sabre cats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat), [snow bears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear), or [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), depending on your level. The cave itself is entirely natural in appearance, and descends in a series of plateaus with earthen ramps linking them.

Upon entering, there is a dead [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) in front on a ledge, with a ramp down to the left and an iron ore vein beneath it. Partway down, there is a wellspring that has formed a partially frozen pool that feeds a stream flowing to the south, with a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) to the north. To the right, the ground rises, with a couple more animals found at the point before the cave continues to descend, passing a dead [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md) near the west wall. A short way farther south, the stream forms a waterfall, with the path becoming a ledge along the west wall. Near the bottom, the path crosses below the waterfall as a bridge over another partially frozen pool. Three more animals are found at the bottom next to an unlocked wooden [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), with a second dead bandit and a medium coin purse on the ground among more human debris. There is a [gold ore vein](Skyrim_Gold_Ore_Vein.md) against the south wall of the cave. There are no plants or fungi anywhere inside the cave.

## Notes
- Whenever there are different animals present, it is possible you will only find the victors of any fights alive and the carcasses of the losers.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
