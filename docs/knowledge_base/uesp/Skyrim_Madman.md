[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Madman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Madman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madman.jpg) Madman The **Madman** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found in the wilds of Solstheim. He has apparently been driven mad from reading a [Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book_(book).md), and will direct you towards it. He wears [ragged boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots) and [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes), and carries some [scribblings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scribbles_of_a_Madman). He has a chance to spawn at several specific spots all over the island of Solstheim as part of a [world interaction](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Black_Book).

## Related Quests
- **[Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book_(quest).md)**: Retrieve a Black Book from a dungeon.

## Dialogue
Approaching him, he says:

*"I know things. Hidden things. Things you aren't supposed to know."* This presents three options to reply:

**Slow down there. What are you going on about?** *"You don't believe me. No one does. They don't want to. I don't want to either. But I can't help it. They're in my head!"* **Ummm... Are you alright?** *"No! That's what I'm trying to say. The secrets. They're in there. In there deep. Can't get them out. They're in my head."* **Whoa. Not so close** *"Good idea. They might leak out. Don't want them crawling into your head, too. Like me. They're in my head. Right now. Saying things.*"The conversation then follows with more ramblings, after which he becomes hostile and attacks you with his fists:

**What's in your head?** *"Secret hidden things."* **Secrets? What kind of secrets?** *"The secret kind!"* **Alright then. Where did you learn these secrets?** *"The Black Book. It shoved them in there. With black slick fingers. My fingers are too short. I can't get them out."* **Where did you find this Black Book?** *"Oh no. I didn't find it. It found me. Here. I'll show you. See. Right there. But don't go there. You don't want these things in your head. You don't!"* **I can see this is going nowhere. Good luck with all that.** *"Luck won't help. I need something to pry these things out of my head. Why won't anyone listen to me? Listen to me! They're in my head!"* If he is killed, his body can be looted for a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scribbles_of_a_Madman) which begins a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Black_Book_(quest).md) to find the Black Book he was referring to.

## Notes
- He can be encountered on more than one occasion.
- Using a calm spell on him will prevent him from attacking the Dragonborn, the note can be pickpocketed from him.

## Bugs
- If that particular Black Book has already been found, the miscellaneous quest cannot be cleared from your journal.
