## Walkthrough

### Romlyn Dreth's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House.jpg) Romlyn Dreth at home Just to the left of the entrance are two [fish barrels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Barrel) that you are free to take from. It is a single room dwelling, like all the homes all the canal-side with a partial wall separating the living and sleeping quarters. Upon entering you are in the living area that consists of a fireplace in the middle of the west wall, with a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid, a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [frost mirraim](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging above. Located in the northwest corner is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) and a piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md). Situated in front of the fireplace are three chairs and a round wooden table holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak). There is a dresser holding [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) against the partial east wall and hanging above it are three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), two garlic braids and a bunch of elves ear. There are a set of shelves and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) against the south wall. The shelves are holding a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a couple of loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and several pieces of cheese. Barrels and sacks containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) are situated all around this room as well.

The sleeping area consists of a single owned bed in the northwest corner, with a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) under it and on the west wall a wall shelf over the bed. On the shelf is a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). Further along the west wall by the foot of the bed is an end table holding a wooden bowl containing [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and to the right of the bed against the south wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md).
