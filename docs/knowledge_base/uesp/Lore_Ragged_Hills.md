[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/ON-map-Ragged_Hills.jpg/200px-ON-map-Ragged_Hills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ragged_Hills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ragged_Hills.jpg) Map of the Ragged Hills circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Ragged Hills** is a region located in the hold of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), which is a part of the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The Ragged Hills are located at the base of the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md), and consists of the land on the southern end of the [Treva River](Lore_Treva_River.md). The major settlement in the region is the town of [Nimalten](Lore_Nimalten.md), located where [Lake Geir](Lore_Lake_Geir.md) and the Treva River meet each other. The village of [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md) is at the base of the Throat of the World and is the beginning of the pilgrimage on the [Seven Thousand Steps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Seven_Thousand_Steps). Deep in the mountains south of Nimalten is the ruins of [Arcwind Point](Lore_Arcwind_Point.md), and deep underground, west of Nimalten is the ancient ruins of Taarengrav. Traveling southeast in the Ragged Hills would reveal the ruins of Faldar's Tooth and Honrich Tower.
