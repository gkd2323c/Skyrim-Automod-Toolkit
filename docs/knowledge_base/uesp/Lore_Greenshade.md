[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-place-Greenshade.jpg/200px-ON-place-Greenshade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Greenshade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Greenshade.jpg) Greenshade circa 2E 582 Greenshade is a large region which forms southwestern [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The southwest area is known as the [Drowned Coast](Lore_Drowned_Coast.md), which lies west of [Wilderking Court](Lore_Wilderking_Court.md). Northern Greenshade is known as [Green's Marrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Green%27s_Marrow). Many rivers course through the land, and life abounds under its canopy. The towns [Marbruk](Lore_Marbruk.md), [Greenheart](Lore_Greenheart.md), and the capital [Woodhearth](Lore_Woodhearth.md) are located here, along with many smaller settlements. The walking city [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) has been known to spend the spring around the center of the region. Several [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins can still be found in Greenshade. Many [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of Greenshade venerate the Wilderking.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Greenshade is home to many tribal Bosmer, or [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) Bosmer, who are known for being unfriendly to outsiders and for practicing the "Rite of Theft".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AMGTV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOTGPB-3)</sup> Many merchants travel well-armed and stick close to major cities as a result. Old "Merchant Tunnels", which were dug so that legitimate businessmen could transport goods in relative safety, are now generally used instead for black-market smuggling and trading.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AMGTV-2)</sup>

In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) sought to "civilize" the region, but met resistance in eradicating widespread practices, such as [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worship.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> Their construction of the town of [Marbruk](Lore_Marbruk.md) proved controversial due to the damage it caused to the Green of Valenwood.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TDDM-4)</sup>
