[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-npc-Engar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Engar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Engar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Engar.jpg) Engar **Engar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) and a [carriage](Skyrim_Transport.md#Carriage) driver who can be hired at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md).

He wields an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), and wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006C1D8) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f).
