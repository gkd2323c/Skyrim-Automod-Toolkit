Hanin's Wake *A noxious and lethal drink allows for a mass sacrifice*
---
...and upon that year of the Reign of [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) and his Son's, the Magnificence that was [Mordrin Hanin](Lore_Mordrin_Hanin.md) ended in this world. Representative *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* of [Ashalmawia](Lore_Ashalmawia.md), [Maelkashishi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Maelkashishi) and [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md) gathered in a great host at the vastness of [Assurnabitashpi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Assurnabitashpi). Even [Hilbongard](Lore_Hilbongard_Rolamus.md) and [Dorach Gusal](Lore_Dorach_Gusal.md) were lured from their Forge, and for a time the Fires of [Anudnabia](Lore_Anudnabia.md) were silent.

And thus on the Ninth Day of Mourning, many slaves and enemies were sacrificed and the Cup of Passage was mixed according to the direction of Hanin's Formulae:

- 2 Parts Blood of Traitors
- 1 Part [Heart of Daedra](Lore_Daedra_Heart.md)
- 1 Part mixed [Bittergreen Petals](Lore_Bittergreen_Petals.md), [Void Salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void_Salts), [Green Lichens](Lore_Green_Lichen.md) and [Bonemeal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bonemeal)
- 1 Part [Moonsugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md)
- 5 Parts [Flin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Flin)

Combine Blood, Heart, Moonsugar in Large [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) Alembic. Heat fire fed by Bones of Traitors. Condense vapors into a large Ebony flask. For a hot drink, strain contents through [Scamp Skin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scamp_Skin) and mix with Flin in large mug, slowly stirring with a glass rod. For a chilled drink, mix in flask with pure [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) Ice and shake vigorously. Strain through [Winged Twilight](Lore_Winged_Twilight.md) membrane and served in gem encrusted goblet.

The wake was considered a great success as the beverage killed a great many guests and thus Mordrin Hanin was supplied with companions in the next world.
