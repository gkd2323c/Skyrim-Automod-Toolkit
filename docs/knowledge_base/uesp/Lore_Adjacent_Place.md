# Adjacent Place

| Adjacent Place | |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Plane |
| Realm | [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) |

Adjacent Places are realms within the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) that are [accessible by magic](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sermon26-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAW-2)</sup> Some sources say they exist as parallel reflections of the realms they are 'adjacent' of.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKAMA-3)</sup> Severe [magical](Lore_Magic.md) anomalies that disrupt the normal non-cardinal points can open breaches to Adjacent Places.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sermon26-1)</sup> Arch- [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md) [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md) wrote that he would temporarily lodge his [Flame Atronach Wolf](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md#Flame_Atronach_Wolf) in a pocket realm Adjacent Place.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAW-2)</sup>

Though some sources claim that there are multiple Adjacent Places,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAW-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKAMA-3)</sup> others point to one, unique **Adjacent Place**. The [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md) claims to have an alternate form in the Adjacent Place.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md)'s [teachings](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md) tell of the Adjacent Place, describing it as a forbidden realm of apology for absolution and the illusion of the vocal.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sermon27-5)</sup> The teachings tell of a people known as [Grabbers](Lore_Grabber.md) who inhabited the Adjacent Place, having never built a city of their own. The Grabbers tried to conquer [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) at some point by transforming the concept of Ha-Note into a monster named [City-Face](Lore_City-Face.md). The monster was stopped by the [god](Lore_Gods.md) [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md), who showed the Grabbers the error of their false hope.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
