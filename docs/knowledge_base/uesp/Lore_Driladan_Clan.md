# Driladan Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-place-Driladan_Pass.jpg/200px-ON-place-Driladan_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Driladan_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Driladan_Pass.jpg) Driladan Pass [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/ON-npc-Indanas.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Indanas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Indanas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Indanas.jpg) A Driladan ranger The Driladan Clan is a group of [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) that lived in eastern [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md), in the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and the general vicinity of [Marbruk](Lore_Marbruk.md). Before the city was built, the clan lived in the eponymous Driladan Pass<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAnglorn-1)</sup> north of Marbruk and south of [Whisper Grove](Lore_Whisper_Grove.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPass-2)</sup>

## History
The Driladan Clan lived in the region since time immemorial, longer than their elders could remember.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAnglorn-1)</sup> When the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) constructed Marbruk south of the pass, many indigenous Wood Elves were fascinated with its process to completion, unlike city Bosmer that greatly opposed it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MBL-3)</sup> While it is unknown how the Driladan reacted to the city, they stayed in Driladan Pass until their home was attacked by the [undead](Lore_Undead.md). Sometime in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the shade of Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md) resurrected the undead in Driladan Pass<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBodring-4)</sup> and drove them out. The clan fled south into the [Wilderking Court](Lore_Wilderking_Court.md) and settled in the seemingly abandoned Shadows Crawl, a ruined fort northeast of [Greenheart](Lore_Greenheart.md) weeks later.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAnglorn-1)</sup>

When the Driladan Clan made themselves home in the ruins, they were attacked by the [Hollow](Lore_Hollow.md), stone guardians of the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md), who slaughtered dozens, including their Treethane. One clan member, Indanas assembled the remaining warriors and took refuge in Shadows Crawl's [graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md) tree. An [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) who wandered into the ruins became involved in the conflict. They helped the Driladan seal holes the Hollow was sprouting from and while the adventurer went to their refuge west of Shadows Crawl, the clan held a vote at the Clan Holdfast and decided to stay despite the fact they were indeed treading on sacred ground. It is unknown if the adventurer sided with the Hollow and destroyed the clan or if they assisted the clan and banished the Hollow.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONStone-5)</sup>
