[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-npc-Fruki.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fruki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fruki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fruki.jpg) Fruki at work **Fruki** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works in the [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

She carries her [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), her [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Fruki%27s_House), and a random selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor). She wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) with [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0009cca1).

She wakes up every morning at 7am and prepares breakfast using her [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). At 8am she walks the short distance to the neighboring Quicksilver Mine where she spends twelve hours in the dimly lit tunnels, tirelessly mining ore for her boss [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md). At 8pm, she goes to the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) for a bit of socializing with the townsfolk before her bedtime at midnight. The only exception to this routine is when you first enter Dawnstar; she will then be inside Windpeak Inn, discussing the strange [nightmares](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) with [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md), [Irgnir](Skyrim_Irgnir.md), and [Thoring](Skyrim_Thoring.md).

## Dialogue
Before you have completed [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md), Fruki will say:

*"[Lady Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mara) will save us from these nightmares. You'll see."* Like most of the miners in town, Fruki almost exclusively talks about her work. She might also mention the feud between her boss and his ex-wife [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md):

*"Used to be twice as many miners working here before all this trouble started."* *"I work in the mine, just like everyone else."*
### Conversations
When you first enter Dawnstar, you'll find her engaged in a conversation in the Windpeak Inn.

**Irgnir:** *"It's a curse! It has to be! I've got to get out of this town."* **Fruki:** *"Irgnir, get a hold of yourself. They're just dreams. Please tell her, Erandur."* **Erandur:** *"Listen to your friend, Fruki. They are just dreams, my dear. I assure you that it is quite normal."* **Irgnir:** *"It's the same dream over and over again. You think that's normal? It's evil I tell you!"* **Fruki:** *"Erandur, she has a point. You keep telling us no harm will follow these dreams, but they must be an omen."* **Thoring:** *"Give him a chance to speak. He's trying to help us."* **Erandur:** *"Everyone, please. I'm doing what I can to end these nightmares. In the meantime all I ask is you remain strong and put your trust in Lady Mara."* **Irgnir:** *"I... I will. Thank you..."* Even though they work in competing mines, Fruki can often be seen talking to Irgnir at the Windpeak Inn.
