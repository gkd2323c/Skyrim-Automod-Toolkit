## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The house has a field attached, enclosed with wicker fencing and planted with seven bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md). There is a lean-to being used as a chicken coop with two [nests](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) in it, with two [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) and a [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) wandering near the house. To the left of the chicken coop is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and in front of the house by the southwestern corner are a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and a [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md).

### Verner and Annekke's House
The entrance is novice-locked at night, but will be unlocked during the day. Against the west wall there are two [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels in each corner separated by a cupboard holding three bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and two bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md). There is a long wooden table against the north wall with a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), and a bucket containing a few [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md) on it. Above this, hanging from the ceiling, are three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), and a rack that holds three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). To the right of the table are another food barrel and a set of shelves; the shelves hold four more bottles of alto wine and four more bottles of wine. To the right of the shelves is a fireplace with a round wooden table and two chairs in front. On the table are a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, and some [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and cheese. On the hearth are a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) and a kettle containing two [leeks](Skyrim_Leek.md). On the east wall is an owned double bed with an end table to the left and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed. There is a bottle of Nord mead on the end table. To the right of the bed against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a food barrel and dresser to the right. On the dresser are six [corundum ingots](Skyrim_Corundum_Ingot.md), two pieces of [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md), a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), and a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md).
