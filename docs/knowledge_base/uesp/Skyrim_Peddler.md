[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Peddler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Peddler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peddler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peddler.jpg) A female Peddler The female **Peddler** can only be encountered in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md), often in combat against the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

If you help her against her attackers and she survives, she will be grateful and offer her merchant services, saying: *"Thanks for the help. The Reach has been crawling with Forsworn lately. It's a wonder people can get anywhere around here. Maybe I'll avoid this entire hold for awhile. That said... I do have a few things to sell."*

She wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#0004223b). Against her attackers she uses a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to Elven quality at level 25).

She carries all her purchasable items on her person, resulting in quite a catch if you find her dead or successfully pickpocket her. However, the wares are cheap and include three [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) (and a 75% chance of seven additional lockpicks), a 75% chance of some randomly selected common [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and the same chance for some uncommon ingredients. There is also a 75% chance she carries a [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), either empty or filled, a 75% chance of some leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow), a 50% chance of some [ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ore), although you can be unlucky and get a piece of [charcoal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal) instead of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) before level 3. From level 3 to 6 it will be [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md), from level 7 to 10 it will be [orichalcum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore), and from level 10 to 13 you may find [moonstone ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore). Lastly, there is a 25% chance she will carry some ingots, leveled and ranging from cheap [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot) at level 1, to rare and expensive [ebony ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Ingot) from level 27. She always carries a bit of gold for transactions.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-npc-Peddler_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Peddler_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peddler_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peddler_02.jpg) A male peddler The male **Peddler** can be found all over Skyrim, usually battling bandits.

When you have slain his opponents the happy Peddler will say: *"I thought I was a goner just now. Thanks for the help, and if you need any supplies I have a few to sell."*

Like his colleague in the Reach, he wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#0004223b). Against his attackers he uses a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to Elven quality at level 25).

He carries his wares around with him, which includes the gold he has available for barter, along with all the items he sells. There is a 75% chance of up to five uncommon [Ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and a similar chance for up to ten common ingredients. He carries three [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), with a 75% chance of an additional lockpick. He also has a 50% chance of selling the same random minerals as his female colleague above, and a 25% of up to two processed minerals. He also has a 75% chance of carrying a [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), either filled or empty, and also has a 75% of some leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow) (up to Dwarven quality at level 10).

## Notes
- Although both peddlers come with a horse, they never ride it under any circumstances.
