[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/SR-creature-Grok.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Grok.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Grok.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Grok.jpg) Grok near [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) **Grok** is a [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) that is initially non-hostile to you in the quest [A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md).

Grok cannot be interacted with and will become hostile once you steal back [Gleda the Goat](Skyrim_Gleda_the_Goat.md) to finish the quest. It is possible to spare him by sheathing your weapon to yield when he becomes hostile or by doing enough damage to put him in recovery position and then escape. If spared, he will just stay in the area in which you find him.
