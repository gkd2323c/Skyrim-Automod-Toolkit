[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-npc-Kjar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kjar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjar.jpg) Kjar **Kjar**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md), is a boat captain commonly found on his ship, [The North Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_North_Wind), docked at the [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) docks.

With the ship permanently docked, Kjar has no choice but to wander around on deck all day and night, at times pausing to sit down and eat. He wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d). He wields a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) in combat and carries some [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Harsh Master](Skyrim_Harsh_Master.md)**: Take care of a runaway crew member. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Dialogue
When approached:

- *"Sorry, but I'm not looking to hire any more crew right now."*
- *"You don't know what freedom really means until you've lived on the river."*
- *"Been on the river since I could walk. Wouldn't trade it for the world."*
- *"While you're on my boat, you follow my rules. First rule is, don't touch anything that doesn't belong to you."*

His goodbyes:

- *"Right. See you around."*
- *"Aye, 'til next time."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Harsh Master

- **You're the ship captain?**
- *"The North Wind is my mistress, and I'm her master. I don't tolerate any disloyalty. You looking for work? Got a sailor who thought running a gang of thieves would be more money than hauling my cargo. You make an example of that gang, I'll give you a year's worth of pay."*

- - **Not interested.**
- *"Coward."*

- - **They're as good as dead.**
- *"That's the spirit."*

When you have killed the leader of the gang:

- **Your old crew member has been taken care of.**
- *"Fine work. Here's your pay. Don't spend it all on mead."*

## Bugs
- Kjar is in the [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) crime faction, rather than the Eastmarch one. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- Oddly, since the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) is marked as being owned by the Winterhold crime faction, if you help anyone in the hold (including Kjar in Windhelm and ironically enough, anyone from the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md)), you will be able to take things from the longhouse without it being considered theft. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19319)) A new faction was created specifically for the residents of the longhouse in order to set ownership correctly.
