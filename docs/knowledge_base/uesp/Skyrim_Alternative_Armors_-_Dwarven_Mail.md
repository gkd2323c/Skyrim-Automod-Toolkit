## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of light Dwarven Mail armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades!. (Quest "Fan Favorite" starts by reading Arena Fan's Note at a camp southeast of Ivarstead.)*

- The dead [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) [Arena Fan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arena_Fan) featured in the quest is a reference to the [Adoring Fan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Adoring_Fan) from *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
