[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.jpg) The Ancient Helmet of the Unburned (female version) The Ancient Helmet of the Unburned is a unique [Ancient Nord Helmet](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Helmet.md). It is located behind a locked gate in [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md), Tribune. Its enchantment increases [fire resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md) by forty percent. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this helmet requires an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the improvements doubling if the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk is also unlocked.

## Notes
- Quite fittingly, the Ancient Helmet of the Unburned is found hanging from an Ancient Nordic Greatsword with a rare [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) enchantment, which is also stuck into a blazing brazier.
- It is one of two Ancient Nordic helmets with an elemental resistance enchantment; the other is the [Helm of Yngol](Skyrim_Helm_of_Yngol.md).

## Bugs
- The helmet uses the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk despite using an iron ingot. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. The perk is changed to [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing).
