[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Argis_the_Bulwark.jpg) Argis the Bulwark **Argis the Bulwark** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) living and working at [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Once you have purchased the home and [attained](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Reach.md) the rank of [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) by completing quests issued by the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), Argis is appointed to be your personal [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). As such, he is not accessible until Vlindrel Hall has been purchased.

Argis wears a set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets), and a pair of [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots). He is equipped with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield).

Argis can be asked to accompany you as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), a capacity in which he is rather useful. At level 50, Argis has 100 in Block, Heavy Armor, and One-handed, as well as 98 Archery and 72 Two-handed. He is also a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).
