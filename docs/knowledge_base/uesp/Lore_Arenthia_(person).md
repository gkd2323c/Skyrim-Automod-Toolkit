**King** Arenthia was the first ever king of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> Arenthia was a [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) figure as the Bosmer [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) was established at the end of the Merethic Era by [King Eplear](Lore_Eplear.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BTAOM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3V-3)</sup> The eponymous [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) city, [Arenthia](Lore_Arenthia.md) was named in his honor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

## Notes
- It is possible that Arenthia wasn't the precise name of the king, but rather just a similar name given to the city in his honor, comparable to [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md) named for [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md).
