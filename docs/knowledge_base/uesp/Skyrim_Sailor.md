They will patrol their ships, but are not hostile towards you. They are relatively weak, wearing only a random set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and carrying either a [steel](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) or [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).
