## Ingredients
With the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), 2 guaranteed samples can be found in 2 different locations:

- [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- [Volkihar Undercroft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Undercroft)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

Bees are categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, they may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

## Creatures
Hovering bees typically spawn near beehives. Bees can also be harvested from both beehives and vacant beehives—vacant beehives are missing [honeycomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honeycomb), rather than bees. A quick way to harvest bees is by entering a nearby cave or other area with a loading screen; on return to the initial area, any bees will have respawned. In total, there are 27 beehives in 21 different locations, and 13 bee spawning sites (each of which can generate up to 10 bees). Locations with spawning sites and/or multiple hives are:

- 2 spawning sites and 4 hives around [Honeystrand Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honeystrand_Cave) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 spawning sites and 2 hives centered 260 ft NW of [Halldir's Cairn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Halldir%27s_Cairn) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-47000&y=-92553)
- 2 spawning sites and 1 hive centered 520 ft NE of [North Shriekwind Bastion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:North_Shriekwind_Bastion) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-19486&y=-63059)
- 2 hives/spawning sites around [Redoran's Retreat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redoran%27s_Retreat) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md)), one WSW and the other ENE
- 1 spawning site and 1 hive 220 ft WNW of [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=175104&y=-46004)
- 1 spawning site and 1 hive 110 ft NNW of [Lost Knife Hideout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Knife_Hideout) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=94284&y=-26945)
- 2 spawning sites and 1 hive 610 ft E of [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=3614&y=-77811)
- 1 spawning site and 1 hive 440 ft SE of [Bleakwind Basin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleakwind_Basin) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=8255&y=-5322)
