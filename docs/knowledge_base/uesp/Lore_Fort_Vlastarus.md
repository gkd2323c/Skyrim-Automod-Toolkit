[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/ON-place-Vlastarus.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vlastarus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vlastarus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vlastarus.jpg) Vlastarus circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/OB-place-Fort_Vlastarus.jpg/200px-OB-place-Fort_Vlastarus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Vlastarus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Vlastarus.jpg) The ruins of Fort Vlastarus circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Vlastarus was a settlement located in the [Greenmead](Lore_Greenmead.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md), along the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md) east of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md). The town was built around a lone and ancient [Imperial](Lore_Empire.md) watchtower called the **Eye of the Greenmead**, which was built long before the town was ever founded. The tower was once located at the southern edge of the town.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONJurana-1)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Vlastarus was a bustling town. It was largely untouched by the war, attracting refugees from all over Cyrodiil. However, the town was threatened by an increase in the number of undead, [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), and bandits in the surrounding countryside, and the nearby ruins of Vlastarus Towers were occupied by a bandit gang known as the Gray Vipers led by a [hag](Lore_Hag.md) named Fegra. Vlastarus was initially located within the territory held by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), although the town and the nearby [Castle Brindle](Lore_Castle_Brindle.md) changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

At around the same time, the [Vlastarus Cohort](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) was considered one of the best troops in the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) armies, and were present in the Colovian annexation of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). They splintered themselves from the main invasion force that took control of [Arenthia](Lore_Arenthia.md), so that they may defeat the [Vinedusk Rangers](Lore_Vinedusk_Rangers.md) that settled southwest from the city. They built their camp in the [Ayleid ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) east of their village, where their original founder Nendaer the Ironbark was buried.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONOdreth-3)</sup> After they successfully expelled the Colovians from the village,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONOath-4)</sup> they made their way toward their camp and razed the entire area. The Vlastarus Cohort and their leader Festius were defeated.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSkeever-5)</sup>

The [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) later built a fort at Vlastarus, but it was abandoned by the Legion during the relatively peaceful [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), nothing remained of the town other than the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) -infested ruins of Fort Vlastarus.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> At around this time, the fort contained one of the last samples of [Shadowbanish Wine](Lore_Shadowbanish_Wine.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONVenerable-8)</sup>
