[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-item-Miraak.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak.jpg) Miraak Miraak is a mask worn by the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) of the same name. There are six versions available to you. Whether you receive the heavy version or light version depends on which of your corresponding skills, [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) or [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md), is higher. If both skills are the same level you will receive the light version.

## Notes
- At level 60, this helmet has the highest armor rating for both light and heavy armor in the game. The armor rating and weight of the light and heavy armor versions are the same, which is very unusual. In comparison, the lowest leveled heavy armor version is on par with a [Daedric Helmet](Skyrim_Daedric_Helmet.md)'s armor rating, while the lowest leveled light armor version's armor rating exceeds by far even a [Dragonscale Helmet](Skyrim_Dragonscale_Helmet.md), which has an armor rating of 17.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> and [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
- At its highest leveled enchantment, it equals an apparel piece "of Peerless Magicka".

## Bugs
[†](#note_2) Although all other Dragon Priest masks can be tempered, this one cannot. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. The mask can be tempered with an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), and benefits from the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk.
