## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to Moth gro-Bagol.
2. Return with a [Daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Moth gro-Bagol has been ordered to make a special weapon for the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). To do so he needs to dip it in the blood of a [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md). All he needs is a heart though, and would appreciate it if you could bring him one.

He will give you a random base piece of armor in return.

## Notes
- This quest raises his [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you, allowing you to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) him.
