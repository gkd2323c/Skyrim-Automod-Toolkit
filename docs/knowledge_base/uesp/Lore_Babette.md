Babette is a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) assassin and member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md). She is a master [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md). While she looks like a ten-year-old girl, Babette is in fact a three hundred-year-old [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md). She was bitten as a child and, as a result, retained her childlike appearance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRBabette-1)</sup> She uses this to her advantage to complete her contracts; her targets do not perceive her as a threat, and she often plays the part of a scared, lost little girl to gain their trust.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRBabette-1)</sup> Due to her age, it can be inferred that she was born and turned during the reign of Empress [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md).

Babette resided in a Dark Brotherhood sanctuary in the outskirts of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md), where the last known chapter of the Brotherhood was still active in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). That same year, Babette witnessed the arrival of [Cicero](Lore_Cicero.md) and the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md) to the Falkreath Sanctuary and the appointment of a new [Listener](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md). She was absent from the Sanctuary when the chapter was massacred by the [Penitus Oculatus](Lore_Penitus_Oculatus.md), and subsequently moved to the Dawnstar Sanctuary with her surviving Dark Brotherhood family. Conflicting reports suggest that the Falkreath Sanctuary was wiped out without the appointment of a Listener. In any case, Babette remains one of the few known surviving members of the Dark Brotherhood in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
