[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-creature-Dwarven_Horse.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Dwarven_Horse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Horse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Horse.jpg) Dwarven Horse The **Dwarven Horse** is a unique [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) which can be constructed as part of the related quest. It is a [vamidium](Lore_Vamidium.md), a [Dwarven automaton](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) built to resemble a living horse for use as a mount. The parts needed to build it can be found within [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns).

It is unique in that it takes no damage; it cannot be hit, can fall from any height, and cannot even be killed through use of the `kill` [console command](Skyrim_Console_Commands.md). Unlike regular horses, it is capable of sprinting for an indefinite amount of time.

## Related Quests
- **[The Dwarven Horse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dwarven_Horse)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md)</sup>: Reassemble an ancient Dwarven [vamidium](Lore_Vamidium.md).

## Notes
- Dwarven Horses first [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Dwarven_Horse) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
- The Dwarven Horse cannot change saddles or wear Horse Armor
- The Dwarven Horse always stays exactly where you last left it; it doesn't return to any location. However, if you have acquired [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md), it will spawn there.
- As a result of the horse's invulnerability, you can fall from any height while riding it and not take any fall damage.
- The Dwarven Horse has similar stats to [Shadowmere](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere), apart from Shadowmere's [Health Regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Health.md). As the Dwarven Horse is invulnerable to damage, this does not affect gameplay.

## Bugs
- Despite being mechanical, it makes regular horse sound effects; it also can be detected by the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) spell.
- Originally, one of the side effects of the horse's invulnerability is that you were rendered invincible while mounted, making [mounted combat](Skyrim_Combat.md#Horse-Mounted_Combat) quite useful. - This was fixed by an update released alongside the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md).
