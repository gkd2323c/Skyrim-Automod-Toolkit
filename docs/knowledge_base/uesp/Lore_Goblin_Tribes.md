# Goblin Tribes

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/BL-quest-Goblins_United.jpg/200px-BL-quest-Goblins_United.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-Goblins_United.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-Goblins_United.jpg) Tribes of Cyrodiil uniting circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180) *Goblin tribes are notoriously insular and wary of outsiders, not surprising considering their only interactions with people tend to be at the point of swords.*
—Landal Gevont, Scholar of Goblins<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Landal Gevont-1)</sup> [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) usually live in a tribal society, although they are not always part of a named tribe. Those tribes that do have names are notable for their use of symbols, strong leadership, and distinctive traditions.

## Tribes by Province

### Black Marsh

---

#### Gnaw-Root Tribe
The **Gnaw-Root Tribe** are found in the [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) region of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). They were enslaved by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) to work the [iron](Lore_Iron.md) mines at Mud Tree Village. However, the tribe revolted and briefly took control of the mine. The Pact managed to subdue them by taking their tribal totem and planned to continue using the goblins as slaves, but the local [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) protested and wanted to release them into the swamp. The ultimate fate of the tribe is unknown.

#### Bitterfish Tribe
The **Bitterfish Tribe** are a loosely organized tribe found in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). They use [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) as a food source, and ritually carry their [scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scales). They attempted to take over the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), but were driven back by its undead guardians.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup>

#### Blood Skull Tribe
The **Blood Skull Tribe** were a Goblin tribe found in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). When the Sharp Rock Tribe tricked the Shatter Bone Tribe into attacking the Blood Skulls, they quickly found themselves on a three-sided war with both the Shatter Bone and the Gut Blade Tribe. The Sharp Rock Tribe then allied with the Eye Stick Tribe to conquer those three tribes, but the allies turned on each other at the eve of their victory. Survivors of all the five tribes banded together and formed the Rock Stick and Sharp Stick Tribes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GCGT-3)</sup>

#### Bloody Hand Tribe
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Goblin-Bloody_Hand.png) The **Bloody Hand Tribe** inhabit Cracked Wood Cave in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md). Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the tribe had invaded and occupied [Harlun's Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Harlun%27s_Watch) and was aggressively encroaching on the settlement of [Cropsford](Lore_Cropsford.md), where the villagers feared an attack was imminent. The hostility was evidently too much, as Cropsford was later abandoned. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), their totem was stolen by the [Rock Biter Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rock_Biter_Tribe), inciting a Goblin war. The war ended after a group of settlers got external help to stop the constant raiding parties preventing them from building a farm at Cropsford.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup>

#### Eye Stick Tribe
The **Eye Stick Tribe** were found on the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). They battled with the Sharp Rock tribe, but allied with them to conquer the other resident tribes, the Shatter Bones, the Blood Skulls, and the Gut Blades, when these found themselves in a three-sided war. On the eve of their victory, Eye Sticks and Sharp Rocks turned on one another. Survivors of all the five tribes banded together and formed the Rock Stick and Sharp Stick Tribes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GCGT-3)</sup>

#### Gut Blade Tribe
The **Gut Blade Tribe** were a Goblin tribe found in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). #### Rock Stick Tribe
The **Rock Stick Tribe** was a Goblin Tribe of the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) region. They formed when the survivors of a complicated tribal war between the Eye Stick, Sharp Rock, Shatter Bones, Blood Skull, and Gut Blade Tribes banded together (a separate group of survivors of that war founded the Sharp Stick Tribe). Later, they were slaughtered by the Long Claw and Tooth Rock Tribes when they migrated into the Gold Coast from near [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md). The remaining members of the three tribes were then known as the Long Tooth Tribe.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GCGT-3)</sup>

#### Flint-Tooth Tribe
The **Flint-Tooth Tribe** was a tribe of giant Goblins that inhabited Hammerfell before the arrival of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md). [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) rallied the Ra Gada to exterminate the Goblins, but was killed during one of the bloodiest battles of the campaign. The Goblins, led by their Warchief, were eventually slaughtered, and their spirits became bound to Hoon Ding's Watch in the Alik'r. These spirits serve to test pilgrims hoping to understand the trials of the Ra Gada, who came to love the desert's simple beauty during their fight against the Goblins.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONMOTR-8)</sup>

#### Sandgrubber Tribe
The **Sandgrubber Tribe** inhabit Divad's Chagrin Mine, a shallow mine in the Alik'r. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) mage apprentice named Denthis Romori accidentally transformed his brother Dithis into a female Goblin. Dithis was then kidnapped and taken to a nearby Goblin camp by the Shadowsilk chieftain Grimmstal, prompting a rescue.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONAGA-11)</sup>

### Summerset Isles

#### Coral-Splitter Tribe
The **Coral-Splitter Tribe** are found in the [King's Haven Pass](Lore_King%27s_Haven_Pass.md) in the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md). The Coral-Splitter is among the only known tribes on the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), who live in the deepest and loneliest corners of the island.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> The Coral-Splitter have traditionally lived in King's Haven for quite sometime.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-KHSR-13)</sup> Because of the Coral-Splitter's aggressive nature, and the perilous cliffs of King's Haven, the pass would be used by the Cult of [Phynaster](Lore_Phynaster.md) to test the pilgrim's faith.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

#### Toothmaul Tribe
The **Toothmaul Tribe** are found on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Originally slaves from the main [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), the Goblins escaped from captivity and laid claim to a cave they named Toothmaul Gully. They then formed a tribe, led by Chief Koth.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-byz-15)</sup> In return for the ability to recruit tribe members into labor and fighting forces, the Heritance provided the Goblins with reserve arms and armor. After discovering the Goblin occupation of the gully, the First Auridon Marines broke up the alliance and ensured the tribe's survival for their continued cooperation with the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).
