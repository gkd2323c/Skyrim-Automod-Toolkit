# Eleidon

| Knight Eleidon | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) | Gender | Male |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg/200px-MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg) Eleidon's Ward Eleidon was a renowned holy [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [knight](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) of legend.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-1)</sup><sup>:241</sup> He was recorded in Breton history as a highly esteemed individual, admired for his bravery and unwavering commitment to set all wrongs right and fight against any injustices. Many stories exist about Eleidon's gallant and brave deeds, including a particularly well-known tale in which he saved a baron's daughter from certain death at the hands of a warlord. In recognition of his noble acts, the baron expressed his gratitude by commissioning the creation of an enchanted shield, known as [Eleidon's Ward](Lore_Eleidon%27s_Ward.md). This artifact was imbued with magical properties, allowing the wielder to heal wounds.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FAT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TLR-5)</sup>

While Eleidon himself remained a figure of ancient legends, his shield became a well known artifact that has withstood the test of time and has continued to feature prominently in the annals of Tamrielic history.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HW-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWIbar-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tribunal-8)</sup> It was recorded among several other renowned and noteworthy artifacts of Tamrielic history by [Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FAT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TLR-5)</sup>
