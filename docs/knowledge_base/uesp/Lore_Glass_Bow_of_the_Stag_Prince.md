# Glass Bow of the Stag Prince

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Glass_Bow.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Glass_Bow.png) Glass Bow of the Stag Prince (Skyrim) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-npc-Falas_Selvayn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Falas_Selvayn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Falas_Selvayn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Falas_Selvayn.jpg) Falas Selvayn The Glass Bow of the Stag Prince is a bow made of [Malachite](Lore_Malachite.md). Its exact origins are unknown. The [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md) is imbued with an [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) that bestows the Blessing of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) the Stag Prince upon the wielder.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SBOW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup> The boon it grants increases the physical attributes of the wielder, namely their vitality and stamina. The powers granted by the Blessing of Stag Prince grow in potency the more game is hunted with the bow.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SBLE-3)</sup>

It is unknown when the Glass Bow of the Stag Prince was created, but as of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) it was in possession of Falas Selvayn, a [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) trader, who inhabited the isle of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Falas_Selvayn-4)</sup>
