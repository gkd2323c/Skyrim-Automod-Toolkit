## Radiant Options
This is a radiant quest which you can receive in any of the nine [holds](Skyrim_Holds.md) of Skyrim. The name of the objective appearing in your journal will be one of the following, depending upon the hold:

- Assist the people of [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Thane_of_Eastmarch.md)
- Assist the people of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Thane_of_Falkreath.md)
- Assist the people of [Haafingar](Skyrim_Thane_of_Haafingar.md)
- Assist the people of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Thane_of_Hjaalmarch.md)
- Assist the people of [the Pale](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Pale.md)
- Assist the people of [the Reach](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Reach.md)
- Assist the people of [the Rift](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Rift.md) (see [notes](#Notes))
- Assist the people of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Thane_of_Whiterun.md)
- Assist the people of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Thane_of_Winterhold.md)

## Quick Walkthrough
Before starting this quest, you must first befriend the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of the hold.

1. Speak to the jarl to receive the quest.
2. Improve the [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) of three or five people of that hold, usually by doing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) for them.
3. Return to the jarl.

## Detailed Walkthrough
You will need to improve the disposition of three people for the minor holds Falkreath, Hjaalmarch, the Pale, and Winterhold. For the major holds Eastmarch, Haafingar, the Reach, the Rift, and Whiterun, you will need to help five people. The people you help must be ordinary citizens of the hold; members of [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) and Orcs living in [strongholds](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) do not count, and neither does befriending the hold's [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) (as that is a prerequisite to being given the objective).

Disposition can be improved in any of the following ways:

- by performing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) on their behalf
- by completing certain [side quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md)
- investing 500 gold in stores, if you have the [investor perk](Skyrim_Investor.md)
- dropping some armor pieces in front of citizens and [allowing them to keep it](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Reactions)
- [rescuing](Skyrim_Rescue_Mission.md) kidnapped citizens

Citizens that you befriend before starting this quest also count towards your total, and as such, it is possible to complete this objective for a particular hold immediately after starting the Thane quest. Also, citizens that you befriended which then died still count towards your total. (Usually in those cases, you will have received an [Inheritance](Skyrim_Inheritance.md).)

## Notes
- While [the Rift thane quest](Skyrim_I_Done_Got_Thaned!.md) is active, most of its objectives do not show up in your journal. The Rift thane quest uses its own code to replace the functions of this quest, so Objective 20 is never assigned. The requirement to assist citizens of the Rift is replaced by an invisible requirement to complete [miscellaneous objectives in Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md#Miscellaneous), which starts automatically after completing [Supply and Demand](Skyrim_Supply_and_Demand.md). The game will track the number of Riften quests you complete, unlike in other holds where you are required to ask the jarl if there is something you can do to help first. Once you have completed enough miscellaneous quests, you will be given an objective to return to the Jarl of Riften.
- If Balgruuf is Jarl of Whiterun, you cannot receive this quest from him. He will make you Thane as a quest reward for completing [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), which is early in the main quest. Only if you side with the Stormcloaks and remove him from power is this quest available in Whiterun.

## Bugs
- "Assist the people of Haafingar" may not be cleared from your quest log by Solitude's jarl once completed, though you are able to become a thane and gain a housecarl in your home (once purchased). The concern may be complicated by having already helped five people before talking to the jarl for the first time. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Entering the console command `setobjectivecompleted Favor Jarls Make Friends 30 1` after becoming a thane may resolve this.
- Continuing to assist the people of Haafingar can cause the counter to update to 6/5, at which point the objective is completed and cleared from the quest log.
