[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-place-Karth_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Karth_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karth_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karth_River.jpg) Karth River The **Karth River** is a river flowing from [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

The Karth River originates in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) at Lost Valley Redoubt from a waterfall flowing from the mountain range. It is unknown where the river that supplies that waterfall flows from without consulting with another provincial map of a region such as Hammerfell or High Rock. From there it flows north and northeast, temporarily splitting in two around the [Karthspire](Skyrim_Karthspire.md), then passing [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) and [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) before finally reaching the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) near Solitude. This river is fed from at least four different sources, including the waterfalls at [Lost Valley Redoubt](Skyrim_Lost_Valley_Redoubt.md), the stream at [Hag Rock Redoubt](Skyrim_Hag_Rock_Redoubt.md), also known as the [Reachwater River](Lore_Reachwater_River.md), the stream sourced from the three waterfalls that flow through Markarth from the mountains above, and the waterfall just above [Deep Folk Crossing](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md) near the border with High Rock.
