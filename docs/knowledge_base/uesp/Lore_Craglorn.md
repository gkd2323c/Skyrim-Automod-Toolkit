[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/ON-place-Craglorn.jpg/200px-ON-place-Craglorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Craglorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Craglorn.jpg) Craglorn circa 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/ON-concept-Craglorn_Live_02.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Craglorn_Live_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Craglorn_Live_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Craglorn_Live_02.jpg) Varying biomes of Craglorn Craglorn is a region in northeastern Hammerfell, at the foot of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md). It is situated between [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

The southern part of the region, called Lower Craglorn, consists mainly of dry wastelands, with some green areas near the bodies of water. A gate in the western mountains connects the region with [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md). The main settlement is [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md), a city heavily influenced by the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) culture, located in the easternmost part of Lower Craglorn. Elinhir lies very close to the Skyrim town of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md), which is just across the border to the northeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SR-2)</sup> [Belkarth](Lore_Belkarth.md) is located within the south-central portion of Craglorn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

The northern part of the region is called Upper Craglorn and is part of the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Vir Web-3)</sup> Its landscape is rugged, mountainous, and very similar to [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), which is just across the mountains to the north. The main city, [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md), lies in the northwest corner of the region. The east part of this region is called the Valley of Scars, while the west is known as [Skyreach](Lore_Skyreach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

Craglorn was home to many different civilizations in the past. These include [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md), [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) and [Yokudans](Lore_Yokuda.md), and their ruins are common in the region. It was also the last bastion of the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) and the remnants of their civilization can be found here. A Nedic [Bloodroot Forge](Lore_Bloodroot_Forge.md) can be found near the city of Elinhir.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup> The [Iron Orcs](Lore_Iron_Orcs.md) are the oldest remaining civilization in the region. Old [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) ruins are located in the northernmost mountains of Upper Craglorn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

The city of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) was rebuilt between the borders of Hammerfell and Skyrim in the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRLoading-4)</sup>
