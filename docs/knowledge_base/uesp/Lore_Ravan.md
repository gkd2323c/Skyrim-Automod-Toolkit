# Ravan

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |
| Region | [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Ravan [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg/200px-RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) Map of Ravan by Maiko K'Elmar Ravan (also spelled **Raven**) is a large island in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), south of the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls), that is possibly considered a part of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago. The similarly sized island of [Nalonga](Lore_Nalonga.md) neighbors it to the northwest. Between them is [Asil Yelir](Lore_Asil_Yelir.md) and to Ravan's west is [Siuol Yelir](Lore_Siuol_Yelir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The alternate spelling Raven is seen on the [Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard) box art, but the island is spelled Ravan on all other maps.
