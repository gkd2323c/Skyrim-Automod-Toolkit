*We must hold the gate!*
—Galdal Omayn<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GD-1)</sup> Galdal Omayn was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) leader of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She held the position of the Grand Marshal among the Armigers, and was also the Disciple of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/LG-cardart-Ghostgate_Defender.png/200px-LG-cardart-Ghostgate_Defender.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Ghostgate_Defender.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Ghostgate_Defender.png) Galdal Omayn at the Ghostgate Little is known about Galdal Omayn's past. As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), another member of her family, Elynea Omayn, the proud daughter of a [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) councilor, was a prominent Buoyant Armiger. Archcanon [Tarvus Indoril](Lore_Tarvus_Indoril.md) remarked that she was favored by [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) himself, often visiting his palace and undertaking many missions in his name and inspiring all her fellow Armigers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOV-4)</sup> At that time the position of the Grand Marshal was held by Medno Oren.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MTCS-5)</sup>

It is unknown when Galdal was born. Being an Armiger, and a member of the Omayn family with long noted historical ties to House Redoran,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOV-4)</sup> she was most likely a member of House Redoran herself. By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), she already held the rank of Grand Marshal of the Buoyant Armigers and the rank of Disciple within the Tribunal Temple. As of then she was stationed at [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md), where she helped safeguard the rest of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) from the threat contained within the [Ghostfence](Lore_Ghostfence.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-3)</sup> She was known as a person of importance at Ghostgate. Alongside her, [Ordinator](Lore_Ordinators.md) Grand Commander [Ralyn Othravel](Lore_Ralyn_Othravel.md) and Ordinator [Knight](Lore_Knights.md) Commander [Enar Dralor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Enar_Dralor) were stationed there.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-3)</sup>

She held the honor of donning the [Daedric Face of Inspiration](Lore_Daedric_Faces.md#Daedric_Face_of_Inspiration),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GO-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DFOI-6)</sup> a mask associated with [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) and illegal for any but members of the Tribunal Temple to wear.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFTDE-7)</sup>

She donned high quality [malachite](Lore_Malachite.md) armaments and wielded a glass jinkblade and shield. Aside from being an accomplished warrior, Omayn was also a competent [spellcaster](Lore_Magic.md) in her own right, wielding an array of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) school spells.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GO-2)</sup>
