Fahamal, Trader **by [Fahamal](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fahamal)** *A Redguard's thoughts on the Breton use of magic*
---
[![ON-concept-Breton staff.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/29/ON-concept-Breton_staff.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Breton_staff.png)
- "Our [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) allies are comfortable with [magic](Lore_Magic.md), yes. More than comfortable, I would say. I have even seen their youths practice [illusions](Lore_Illusion.md) upon each other in the very streets of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md). No [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) would ever permit his own children to tamper with magic in such an irresponsible fashion. Do they not realize the dangers?

- Magic is powerful, yes, and a mighty force in battle, but the perils are many and the temptation of foul [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) always lurks near. I see more [staves](Lore_Staves.md) here than blades, and I admit, I fear for the safety of our sword-adepts as they stand in battle beside so many mages. King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md) would not ally us with evil, but we must always be vigilant."
