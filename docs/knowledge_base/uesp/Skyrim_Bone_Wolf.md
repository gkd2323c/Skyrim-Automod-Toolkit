It carries items for you and boosts your damage against undead enemies. It was released on October 4, 2018 for 200 Creation Credits.

The quest added by this Creation was made by [Skinnytecboy](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2373072).

## Official Summary
*Despite being raised by necromancers, a Bone Wolf's true loyalty is earned through actions rather than magic. A faithful companion with a bone to pick, this skeletal pet will reward your friendship by carrying items and boosting your damage against the undead. Quest created by Skinnytecboy. (Quest "Let Sleeping Wolves Lie" starts by reading the Letter from Bolgeir Bearclaw delivered by courier after completion of "The Wolf Queen Awakened".)*
## Overview
This Creation adds [Bone Wolves](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf_(creature).md) to the game as generic enemies, as well as a **[quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Let_Sleeping_Wolves_Lie)** to obtain one as a pet. The pet Bone Wolf will not fight for you, but does grant the Bone Wolf's Revenge perk while in your company, increasing your damage against undead and necromancers by 25%. Bone Wolves first [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bonewolf) in the *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)* expansion for *[The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
