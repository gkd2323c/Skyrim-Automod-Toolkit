Mammoths are large woolly [elephants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elephant) that are generally non-hostile unless provoked, they are most commonly found near [giants](Lore_Giant.md) in herds. Their distribution varies, with them being a common sight across the tundras of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md), northern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and the western regions of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-skyrim-2)</sup> Mammoths often have two tusks, though many larger mammoths or bulls will grow four tusks.

## Variants
Typical brown-furred Tundra Mammoths can be found in Skyrim, northern Cyrodiil, and the Wrothgarian Mountains.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The Timber Mammoths of Valenwood often have blue-gray fur,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TM-4)</sup> though they also can be brown like tundra mammoths.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mount-5)</sup>

Pocket Mammoths are a miniature variety of Mammoth that were originally bred by the Orcs of Wrothgar to be more manageable as livestock, however many escaped into the wild.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> They have since diversified into several breeds.

## Uses
They are commonly hunted for their meat and ivory tusks, with the tusks being very valuable. The meat of one mammoth can feed many for a whole month.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> [Powdered mammoth tusk](Lore_Powdered_Mammoth_Tusk.md) can be used in alchemy, but only the giants know how to grind the incredibly hard tusks down into powder, making the ingredient rare.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Quintus-8)</sup> During the mid-Second Era, baby mammoth fur was said to be much sought after in Morrowind, but exporting it from Skyrim was illegal.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONIirila-9)</sup> [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) were known to utilize mammoths in siege warfare and as mounts.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-HTB-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SM-11)</sup>

Bosmer are known to raise timber mammoths for their meat, which is primarily used in [rotmeth](Lore_Rotmeth.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-On Rot-12)</sup> They are also are bred by the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md) as mounts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mount-5)</sup>

### Relationship with Giants
While it's commonly believed that giants herd mammoths,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> scholars have observed that their relationship is based more on mutual respect. They are seemingly able to communicate with mammoths, and protect them fiercely. In return, giants receive companionship and milk, which they process into cheese.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> Mammoths belonging to giants have markings on their tusks. The two have a protective relationship, with giants protecting them from anything that would do harm to the mammoths, and conversely, tamed mammoths will attack anything that threatens the giant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-skyrim-2)</sup>
