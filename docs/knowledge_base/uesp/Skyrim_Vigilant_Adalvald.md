[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-npc-Vigilant_Adalvald.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vigilant_Adalvald.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Adalvald.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Adalvald.jpg) Vigilant Adalvald **Vigilant Adalvald** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md), first mentioned by [Vigilant Tolan](Skyrim_Vigilant_Tolan.md) during the first [Dawnguard quest](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md). He is later found in [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md) on your first mission as a member of [the Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md). When you find him, he is being interrogated by the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) [Lokil](Skyrim_Lokil.md), but dies without revealing anything. By his corpse you can find a segment of [his notes](Skyrim_Notes_on_Dimhollow_Crypt,_Vol._3.md) on the crypt, detailing its connection with Skyrim's ancient vampiric clans.

He wears [ragged trousers](Skyrim_Ragged_Trousers.md), either [steel cuffed boots](Skyrim_Steel_Cuffed_Boots.md) or [steel plate boots](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Boots.md), and an [Amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md). His hands are tied with [prisoner's cuffs](Skyrim_Prisoner%27s_Cuffs.md). He also carries a random book. He knows the spells [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

## Quest-Related Events
When you enter Dimhollow Cavern, you will overhear a conversation between Lokil, Vigilant Adalvald, and another vampire. Getting too close will interrupt the conversation, either way Adalvald will be killed:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-quest-Awakening_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Awakening_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Awakening_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Awakening_04.jpg) *"So go and meet your beloved Stendarr."*
- **Adalvald**: *"I'll never tell you anything, vampire. My oath to [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) is stronger than any suffering you can inflict on me."*
- **Lokil**: *"I believe you, Vigilant. And I don't think you even know what you've found here."*
- **[Lokil draws his weapon and strikes Adalvald]**
- **Adalvald**: *"Peace at last..."*
- **Lokil**: *"So go and meet your beloved Stendarr."*
- **[Lokil sheathes his weapon and walks towards the stone structure, shortly followed by the other vampire]**
- **Vampire**: *"Are you sure that was wise, Lokil? He still might have told us something."*
- **[Lokil will interrupt the other vampire]**
- **Vampire**: *"We haven't gotten anywhere ourselves with..."*
- **Lokil**: *"He knew nothing. He served his purpose by leading us to this place. Now it is up to us to bring Harkon the prize. And we will not return without it. [Vingalmo](Skyrim_Vingalmo.md) and [Orthjolf](Skyrim_Orthjolf.md) will make way for me after this."*
- **Vampire**: *"Yes, of course Lokil. Do not forget who brought you news of the [Vigilants'](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) discovery."*
- **Lokil**: *"I never forget who my friends are. Or my enemies."*

## Notes
- Like [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md), his death is scripted and there is no way to save him. - Even if you attack or kill Lokil before the vampire draws his weapon, Adalvald will just immediately die; despite never being attacked by Lokil or the other vampires.
