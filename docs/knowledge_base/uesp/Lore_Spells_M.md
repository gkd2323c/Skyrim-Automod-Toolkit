## Magelight

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Magelight.md)

## Medusa's Gaze
**Medusa's Gaze** is a variant of [Paralysis](Lore_Paralysis.md) spell of the Arch-Circinate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TKS-2)</sup><sup>:73</sup> Named for its similar effects to the gaze of a [Medusa](Lore_Medusa.md).

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:The_Known_Spellbook#Medusa.27s_Gaze), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Medusa%27s_Gaze), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Medusa%27s_Gaze)

**Spell schools:** [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md), [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md)
