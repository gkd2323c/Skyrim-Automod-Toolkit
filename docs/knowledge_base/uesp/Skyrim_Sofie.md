[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-npc-Sofie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sofie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sofie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sofie.jpg) Sofie selling flowers **Sofie** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who wanders around the streets of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) selling flowers from her [flower basket](Skyrim_Flower_Basket.md). Starting her day at 8am, she sells her flowers for nine hours until 5pm. After that, she finally rests and can usually be found sitting near the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](Skyrim_New_Gnisis_Cornerclub.md). After three hours, she heads back to the barrel at the back of [Niranye's house](Skyrim_Niranye%27s_House.md) and sleeps until 8am. She will then repeat her schedule the following day.

## Dialogue
When she spots you during the day, she will approach you and ask:

- *"Hi Mister/Lady! Would you like to buy some flowers? Please?"*

- - **Sure. What do you have?** (This becomes **What have you got for sale?** when asked later)
- *"Not much. But I hope you like them!"*
- *"Some pretty flowers. I picked them all by myself!"*

The standard barter menu will then open up.

Alternately, you can either politely decline or rudely dismiss her, to which she'll answer:

- **Sorry, not right now.** or **Out of my way, kid.**
- *"Oh, I... okay. Sorry."*

Regardless of your choice, you will have an option to ask her what she's got for sale the next time you talk to her, as well as an option to ask about her parents' whereabouts. If you inquire about her parents, she'll inform you:

- **Who are you? Where are your parents?**
- *"They... they're dead. My mama died when I was little... I... I don't remember her very well. My father was a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) soldier. One day, he left and... didn't come back. I'm all alone... I... I try to sell flowers so I can buy food. It's not much but... what else can I do?"*

You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) her. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) with a child's bed and container.

When near her, Sofie can be heard trying to sell her flowers:

- *"Buy some flowers? Pretty please?"*
- *"Please, won't you buy a flower?"*

However, she can also be heard talking to herself, showcasing her desperation:

- *"I'm... I'm so cold..."*
- *"No one bought any flowers again today. I... I don't know what to do."*

If you do not adopt her, she will exit the conversation with:

- *"Thanks... Thanks for talking to me."*
- *"Stop by again!"*

## Standard Merchandise
Sofie sells the following flowers:

- 1 [Red Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md)
- Up to 2 rare flowers from the list: [Deathbell](Skyrim_Deathbell.md), [Dragon's Tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md), [Lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), [Nightshade](Skyrim_Nightshade.md)
- Up to 4 common flowers from the list: [Blue Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [Purple Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Purple_Mountain_Flower.md), [Red Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md)

With the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk, she also sells [flower baskets](Skyrim_Flower_Basket.md).

## Bugs
- After adopting Sofie when trying to give her a gift all the options may be gray and the message "Item Is Too Heavy To Carry" appears in the top-left corner. This is because she still has all her flower baskets on her from before you adopted her. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.

- If you have the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk unlocked, make sure to purchase all of her flower baskets before adopting her, to keep this problem from occurring in the first place (your only option if you do not have access to the console).
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, simply open the console and select her with the mouse, then use the command `drop 000d955a 500` to force her to drop all the remaining baskets. They stack so only 1 basket will appear, with the total number after the name. If she does not drop any baskets after using this command, make sure the ref ID of your selected target is `xx004031`, as it is common to accidentally click the wrong asset when using click targeting. Note - this command tells her to drop up to 500 baskets. She may have more. If she drops exactly 500, repeat the command, and she will drop the remainder.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you attack Sofie until her health bar appears she will drop her baskets. This will incur a bounty.
- When you tell Sofie to go to bed and relent when she says it's not even dark outside instead of insisting she go to bed anyway, she may say "Thanks, pa", even if you're playing as a female character. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- When you adopt Sofie, she may tell you that she will collect her things and meet you at the house, but then remain in Windhelm and not move, even if you approach her repeatedly. - A possible fix is to have a separate house with a room for a child, and tell Sofie to go there. Find her once again at that separate house, and then tell her to move to your Hearthfire house. She will appear at the manor some time later.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, stand inside your house and use the console command `prid xx004031` to select her, followed by `moveto player` to move her into the house .
- If you've adopted Sofie before capturing Windhelm for the Imperials, she may disappear after you've captured Windhelm. <sup>**?**</sup> - A possible fix is to speak to another family member and have them move to another home. Sofie should appear in this new location.
- Sleeping 48 hours also fixes the problem. It seems Sofie's location is reset to Windhelm during the siege and she needs time to walk back.
- Due to a scripting error, Sofie would end up overloading her merchant inventory with flower baskets. This script polled every 10 seconds while she was loaded and could in theory result in her having enough baskets to overflow the reference counts. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. This script has now been removed, and her AI package has been modified to instead play the idle continuously. In addition, the AI pack script will slowly remove the baskets over time as they are not needed for her schedule to function.
