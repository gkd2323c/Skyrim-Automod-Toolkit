## Quick Walkthrough
1. Investigate the desecration of the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).
2. Clear Reachcliff Cave of [undead](Skyrim_Draugr.md).
3. Bring a [fresh kill](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md) to Namira's Shrine.
4. Do the unthinkable for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### City of Stone
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_04.jpg) *"What are you hiding, priest?"* [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) is the capital city of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md), a place of misfortune and decay, and the denizens rarely talk to strangers about anything but the endless problems with the natives of the Reach, the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). Fortunately, the innkeeper at the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) will happily share a few local rumors when asked, so enter the tavern near the main gate and find [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md) (or [Frabbi](Skyrim_Frabbi.md) if her husband is dead) behind the bar. One of the rumors may reveal problems with the local [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md), which has been closed for unknown reasons. You are told to see [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md) for further information, thus starting the objective that eventually leads to this quest. If Kleppr or Frabbi refuse to talk about the hall, you can find Brother Verulus yourself, who will openly tell you about current events.

Inside [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) you will witness a heated argument between Verulus and [Thongvor Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md):

**Thongvor**: *"What are you hiding, priest?"*
**Verulus**: *"I'm not hiding anything. It's closed for a reason."*
**Thongvor**: *"Typical Imperial lies. First you take away Talos, now you're keeping us from seeing our honored dead? You and the Jarl will answer for any desecration of my ancestors' bodies."*
**Verulus**: *"That's enough, Thongvor. We're done."*

After the conversation, talk to Verulus and he will deny you access to the hall and will provide a standard explanation. You can attempt to bribe, persuade, or intimidate him, although only the first two options will get you anywhere. To succeed at bribery or persuasion, you will need to pass an easy [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options).

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| I could help if you tell me. **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: All right. I was going to suggest the Jarl hire someone to sort this mess out, anyway. | **Passed**: Investigate the Hall of the Dead |
| Would some coin help? **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: Well, the Priesthood of Arkay always accepts generous donations. <br> **Failed**: That's not going to work. | **Passed**: Investigate the Hall of the Dead <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| I don't like being ignored. **(Intimidate)** | **Failed**: Then my answer is the Hall of the Dead is closed. By order of the Jarl. Understand? | **Failed**: Nothing |
| I'll come back later, then. | Arkay keep you and protect you. | Nothing |

He will then explain the macabre situation in the hall and reveal the truth: *"We've discovered that some of the dead have been... eaten. Flesh has been chewed off, bones were snapped to get at the marrow inside."* Verulus then proceeds to ask you to investigate the Hall of the Dead and hands you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Hall_of_the_Dead_Key) to the hall. This is the beginning of the favor quest.

Alternatively, you can completely ignore Verulus and pick the lock to the hall. Entering the hall this way will trigger the main quest; however, unless you persuade or bribe him, you will not be able to befriend him using the favor quest.

### Hall of the Dead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_02.jpg) Eola in the Hall of the Dead Moments after entering, a female voice will echo through the hall, saying, *"Not many would walk blindly into a crypt, smelling of steel and blood, but not fear. I feel the hunger inside of you. Gnawing at you. You see the dead and your mouth grows wet. Your stomach growls. It's all right. I will not shun you for what you are. Stay. I will tell you everything you have forgotten."* After the speech, [Eola](Skyrim_Eola.md) will then run up to you and continue in the same vein, trying to seduce you with suggestions and accusations. When she is finished, three options are available. If you choose the third option, the quest will immediately fail, as she will attack you, with no option to yield. You can still complete the favor quest and receive your [reward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Arkay) from Verulus, but will never get to experience The Taste of Death.

If you instead pick any of the two other options, Eola will continue her seductive behavior, eventually mentioning the Lady of Decay, [Namira](Lore_Namira.md), and a so-called safe haven for cannibals like yourself. Regardless of your answer, the quest will start. If you ask her about the place, she will mark [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave) on your map and reveal the truth about the desecration of the hall. Recently, the cave itself saw the dead rise from their graves, forcing Eola to flee and live in the hall and feast on its more passive inhabitants. She will task you with clearing the cave, and will then cast her [invisibility spell](Skyrim_Invisibility_(spell).md), asking you to meet her at the cave entrance. She will also assure you that the hall will see no further disturbances, and will then leave. Go talk to Brother Verulus and tell him the good news. He will be quite happy, hand you his [Amulet of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Arkay) as a reward and return to his duties inside the hall. This completes the favor quest.

### Cave of the Dead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_05.jpg) *"Bring Verulus here. The Lady of Decay will do the rest."* Reachcliff Cave is located east and slightly south of Markarth and can easily be reached by crossing the bridge to the south from [Karthspire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karthspire). As promised, Eola will be waiting for you near the entrance and will seek you out as you approach. She will reveal that the opposition inside the cave consists of [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) enemies and the whole cave has to be cleared. You can choose to have her as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), so decide whether or not to accept her offer and head inside. Watch out for the higher-leveled draugr in the second chamber and carefully proceed forward. Eola is a powerful follower and will not hesitate to [summon](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) a [flame atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) or revive defeated draugr for extra help. In the catacombs, take a left for a difficult jump to a chest on a small ledge. (Use Whirlwind Sprint for an effortless little zoom across the gap). After a short trek, you will arrive in a room with several draugr, including the boss, so dispose of them all and take a look around; the chamber consists of a huge dinner table, and, in the southern end, the very altar of Namira. Eola will now approach and thank you for reclaiming the Shrine, and will ask you to bring in a fresh kill for your initiation into the coven, specifically a priest filled with the taste of the easy life, [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md). She will then hand you 100 gold to cover expenses and show you the way out. A shortcut to the outside world will now have opened in the room next to the Shrine, so exit the cave and travel to Markarth.

### Champion of Namira
Back in the Hall of the Dead, the unsuspecting priest has picked up his daily routines and spends most of his time sweeping or praying near the Shrine of Arkay, convinced that everything is all right. Talk to him and ask him for assistance during your travels. In order to get him to follow, you need to pass a speech check. As with the initial conversation with him about investigating the hall, only the first two options will get you anywhere. (However, persuading may be impossible, necessitating a bribe—see [Bugs](#Bugs).)

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| There would be treasure to share... **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: Treasure, you say? I suppose the Jarl won't mind if I'm gone for a little while. Lead on. <br> **Failed**: You know, in the Priesthood of Arkay, we have a joke about an empty pocket being better than a full grave. | **Passed**: Return to Namira's Shrine <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| I'm willing to pay. **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: That's no small amount of coin, friend. I suppose I can come with you for a short venture. Lead on. <br> **Failed**: I'm just not up for it, sorry. I'd rather stay here, have a glass of wine, and perform my rites to Arkay in peace. | **Passed**: Return to Namira's Shrine <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| You're coming with me. Now. **(Intimidate)** | **Failed**: I think you need to calm down before I summon the guards. | **Failed**: Nothing |
| Never mind, then. | Arkay watch over you. | Nothing |

Leave the Hall of the Dead and fast travel to the secret back entrance to Reachcliff Cave. When you enter the dining hall, you will find several well-known Markarth citizens seated at the large table, all hungry and ready for the evening feast: [Lisbet](Skyrim_Lisbet.md), [Banning](Skyrim_Banning.md), [Hogni Red-Arm](Skyrim_Hogni_Red-Arm.md), [Sanyon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanyon), and [Nimphaneth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nimphaneth). The table will be set with beverages, bread, cheese and two pieces of [human flesh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Human_Flesh). A confused Brother Verulus will approach the table, stuttering. Eola will immediately be over him and will use her seductive charms to make him forget where he is and feel incredibly tired. She will then proceed to invite him to take a rest on the altar in front of the Shrine of Namira, something he will do right away. Listening to the guests' private conversations makes it quite clear what they're all waiting for: the flesh of Verulus. Head to the altar where Eola will invite you to start carving the sleeping priest in front of you.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Taste_of_Death_03.jpg) Death of a priest At this point, you can choose to kill Eola, which will net you a leveled amount of gold from a grateful Brother Verulus and a fight against all the dinner guests, including the quest-related Lisbet, dog seller Banning and Markarth market merchant Hogni Red-Arm. Killing Eola will cause the quest to fail.

Slay the sleeping priest in cold blood, which will make an excited Eola invite you to have the very first bite. Examine the dead priest and choose the option 'eat'. You will then be treated to an animation of yourself feasting on the still warm body of Verulus, and Namira will finally speak to you from her realm. *"Mortal. I am Namira, the Lady of Decay. Your consumption of the blood and bile of Arkay's own is pleasing to me. I give you my ring. Wear it, and when you feast on the flesh of the dead I will grant you my power."* You will then receive the [Ring of Namira](Skyrim_Ring_of_Namira.md) which increases your stamina by 50 points and allows you to feed on corpses for increased health and health regeneration of 50 points for 5 minutes. Eola will then welcome you to the coven and will be available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) should you ever need her. When all is said and done, leave the cannibals to their feast and head out into Skyrim.

## Notes
- If Thongvor is Jarl of Markarth before you enter Understone Keep for the first time, the argument between him and Verulus does not happen. This can happen if you have completed [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or the Civil War quests (and sided with the Stormcloaks) before coming to Understone Keep. There is no change in Verulus' dialogue if you speak to him in this case.
- After Verulus agrees to follow you to the cave, he will become a follower and will follow you anywhere until you enter the cave. Unlike nearly all other quest followers, if you already have a follower when he joins you, he won't replace them.
- Though Namira makes no visible appearance, she's voiced by an unseen NPC named [Voice of Namira](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Voice_of_Namira) located in an unreachable room east of Namira's shrine.
- If you decide to save Brother Verulus, you can use a Calm spell on the other dinner guests after killing Eola to end the quest, but Banning and Hogni Red-Arm will never leave the feast to return to Markarth. Lisbet will return to her store, but she and Imedhnain will be hostile towards you. Use another calm spell on them and leave the shop. Wait at least a day before returning and Lisbet will be friendly again, acting as though the feast had been successful.
- If you are a werewolf and have decided to save Brother Verulus by killing cannibals, be careful when you go in to beast form as it can cause issues. If you go into beast form, Verulus may wake up early in the middle of the fight and start the conversation with you as if he has already been saved. You will receive the same reward from him, but he may get killed or possibly stuck walking out the door and be unable to return to Markarth. If he sees you in beast form after the fight is over and has already paid you, he will attack you.
- After killing Brother Verulus in Reachcliff Cave, you are able to search his body and get back your earlier bribe.
- After completing the quest you can speak to the other guests, many of whom you might recognize from [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). If you meet the guests later, while they are at work, you can ask about the feast to which they will respond *"Only the guests must know."* You may also encounter [Sigar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigar) in the wilderness, feasting on a fresh corpse.
- If you attack any guest after completing the quest, Eola becomes aggressive. Killing Eola, even after quest completion, causes your journal to show all quest objectives as failed, although you will retain the Ring if you had received it.
- If you are wearing the Ring of Namira and receive the Cursed Ring of Hircine from Sinding during the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md), you will forever be able to feed off of bodies and obtain +50 health and +50% health regeneration.
