[![](https://images.uesp.net/8/86/DF-place-Alik%27ra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Alik%27ra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Alik%27ra.jpg) Alik'ra ca. 3E 405 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/DF-map-Alik%27ra_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-map-Alik%27ra_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Alik%27ra_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Alik%27ra_(city).png) Map of the Alik'r Desert ca. 3E 405 showing the location of Alik'ra Alik'ra is a walled city in the unconquered [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) region and was its capital in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). It is located just north of [Bergama](Lore_Bergama_(barony).md), where the narrow strip of land begins to the west between [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md) and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) to the north, and Bergama to the south. Despite its importance in the area, no ruler claims it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFAlik.27ra-1)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Alik'ra and the surrounding area became a part of the kingdom of Sentinel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWITW-2)</sup>
