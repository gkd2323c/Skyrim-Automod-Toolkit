# Dynamo Cores

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Centurion Dynamo Core.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Centurion Dynamo Core.png) A Centurion Dynamo Core (Skyrim) *Footfalls-in-Snow was up on its chest, slicing apart metal tendrils, and finally plunged an arm through a chest cavity, struggling for a few moments before wrenching out a weighty golden contraption, spherical with a red, pulsing center. He held it aloft, in triumph.*
“Dynamo core!” *
—* [Kyne's Challenge: A Hunter's Companion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Kyne%27s_Challenge:_A_Hunter%27s_Companion) ** Dynamo Cores,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Centurion-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Deshaan-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Construct-3)</sup> referred to formally as **Independently Apportioning Dynamo Cores**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ner-4)</sup> or simply as **Dynamos** or **Dwarven Dynamos**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Trophy-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup> are mechanical reactors created by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) that generate or distribute power for their machines.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Centurion-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ner-4)</sup> Referred to as the "heart" of any Dwemer device,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup> they are powered seemingly by a rotating, pulsing red gem at the center of a framework of [Dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Centurion-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Deshaan-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Kyne C-7)</sup> The red gem at the center may be a form of [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Antiquity-8)</sup> though other variants possess an [Aetherium](Lore_Aetherium.md) core.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Aethrium-9)</sup> While soul gems play an important role in keeping [Dwemer Animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) "charged",<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ner-4)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> they are not their main power source.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Research_Notes-12)</sup> Dynamo Cores serve the purpose of distributing the energy of the soul gems into resonators which create "[harmonic resonances](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md)" which heat the boilers that create the mechanical steam power that allows them to function.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOBedyni-13)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Research_Notes-12)</sup> Dynamos are known for remaining active even after being separated from their host apparatus.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Deshaan-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Trophy-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Centurion-1)</sup>

**Centurion Dynamo Cores** are Dynamos used for the powering of [Dwarven Centurions](Lore_Dwarven_Centurion.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Centurion-1)</sup> though they function in other apparatus such as special actuators as well.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> The Dwemer of [Irkngthand](Lore_Irkngthand.md) were known to have created **Aetherium Dynamo Cores**.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Aethrium-9)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Dynamos used in the powering of Dwemer facilities have been known as **Dwarven Power Cores** or **Dwemer Power Cores**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ner-4)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Raynor-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Kireth-17)</sup> They are known to contain enormous amounts of energy and have been seen to be guarded by Centurions.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Raynor-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Kireth-17)</sup> **Dwarven Power Nodes** are a miniature version of these cores. They are capable of generating an extraordinary amount of energy despite their small size.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ner-4)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup>

Dynamo Cores often serve as a valuable trophy for adventurers who plunge into Dwarven ruins.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Trophy-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Kyne C-7)</sup> Many have used them in experiments with creating or repurposing Dwarven Animunculi.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Juli-6)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Raynor-16)</sup> Centurion Dynamo Cores have been utilized in [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) rituals at the Atronach Forge at the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-AF-19)</sup>

## Animo Cores
*We tossed the place, prying open mnemo-crates and wall alcoves as fast as we could. At last, we found it: one of the Proctor's custom animo cores. Her battery, see? It was a thing of beauty—wrought in heavy brass, covered in copper filigrees, and filled to the brim with geodic energy.*
—Transcribed words of Grimrald Brassbones<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Meet-20)</sup> While the [god](Lore_God.md) [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) utilized a Dynamo Core in the creation of the Precursor, his first [Factotum](Lore_Factotum.md) construct,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Construct-3)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup> he later developed his own iteration: the **Animo Core**.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Antiquity-8)</sup> Animo Cores are used to power the many Factotums and [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricant.md) of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-IOM-22)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-Fabricated-23)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-24)</sup> Sotha Sil is known to have made at least two 'series' of them.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Antiquity-8)</sup> Some [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostle.md), such as [Procter Luciana Pullo](Lore_Luciana_Pullo.md), were known to have custom Animo Cores.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Meet-20)</sup>

Sotha Sil began the developments that led to Animo Cores when he became dissatisfied with the potency of regular soul gems as power sources for his ambitious designs, and sought to improve upon their effectiveness. One such iteration was by utilizing larger animus geodes, but their uses were still limited.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-MPStars-25)</sup> He eventually came up with the Animo Core, which are [brass](Lore_Brass.md) containment cells that utilized animus geodes that have been ground into a slurry-like crystalline residue known as geodic distillate,<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-IOM-22)</sup><sup>[[26]](#cite_note-twitch1-26)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-Fabricated-23)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Meet-20)</sup> noted for sharing similar properties to soul gems.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Antiquity-8)</sup> The large animo cores that power constructs such as the [Imperfect](Lore_Imperfect.md) must be periodically refilled with this geodic distillate.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-IOM-22)</sup>
