[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/DF-map-Bhoriane.png/200px-DF-map-Bhoriane.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Bhoriane.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Bhoriane.png) Map of Bhoriane Bhoriane was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) county on the southern coast of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) stretching along the northern coast of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). Its capital was the city of Bhoriane. Two islands just west of the [Isle of Balfiera](Lore_Isle_of_Balfiera.md) were also part of this region. The mainland shared borders with [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) to the west, [Kambria](Lore_Kambria.md) to the north, and [Koegria](Lore_Koegria.md) to the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup> Bhoriane was briefly mentioned in the *[Charwich-Koniinge Letters](Lore_Charwich-Koniinge_Letters.md)* during the quest for [Azura's Star](Lore_Azura%27s_Star.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CKL-3)</sup>
