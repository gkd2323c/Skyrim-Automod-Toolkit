# Spellmirror

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg/200px-LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg) Statue of Shalidor Spellmirror (or **Shalidor's Mirror**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup> is a [spell](Lore_Magic.md) used to reflect incoming hostile spells back at their caster. It is classified under the school of [Thaumaturgy](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup> and [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup> It is also part of the 7th Circinate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMirror-3)</sup> Spellmirror was forged by the [Arch-Mage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Valaste-4)</sup> Shalidor bore his Spellmirror into battle, possibly during the [Battle](Lore_Battle_of_Rourken-Shalidor.md) of [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) -Shalidor, which also saw the use of the [Spellbreaker](Lore_Spellbreaker.md) shield with similar properties.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Shalidor-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AR-6)</sup>
