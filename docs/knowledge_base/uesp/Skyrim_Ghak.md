[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-npc-Ghak.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghak.jpg) Ghak in the mine **Ghak** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc1H) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She works as a miner in [Dushnikh Mine](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Mine.md), mining [orichalcum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore) along with [Dulug](Skyrim_Dulug.md), [Mahk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mahk), and [Oglub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oglub).

Ghak spends her entire life working in the mine and never even sees the light of day. She divides her 24-hour workday between mining, relaxing, and eating. She never sleeps.

She wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). She carries her [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), and the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md), along with a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).
