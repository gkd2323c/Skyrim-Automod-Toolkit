## Notes
- A [cut Nobility faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Unfinished_Quests#Quests_Only_Known_Due_To_Interviews) questline in *[TES IV: Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* involved the [player character](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) becoming the Duke of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) following the death of the previous duke, [Ormellius Goldwine](Lore_Ormellius_Goldwine.md).<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-18)</sup>
- [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) officers have traditionally been appointed as provincial governors to the human regions of the Empire. <sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1HR-8)</sup>
