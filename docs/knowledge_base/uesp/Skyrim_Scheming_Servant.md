[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-npc-Scheming_Servant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Scheming_Servant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scheming_Servant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scheming_Servant.jpg) Scheming Servant The **Scheming Servant** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000209aa) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)*.

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.
