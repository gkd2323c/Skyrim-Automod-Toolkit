Erden Relvel's Note **by Erden Relvel** *An invitation from a priest of Dagoth Ur*
---
I was told that without the [masks](Lore_Masks_of_the_Tribunal.md), the gate would not open, but the priests underestimate my master's will.

It was [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) who opened the gate, and beckoned me forward. And when I wear [his face](Lore_Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur.md), I speak his truth, and from his words I spawn [the cure](Lore_Corprus.md) that will spread across the world.

My [temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) brother, Arthamis, was the first to receive its blessing. His once melancholy face has been carved into the most delightful shape, and his mouth stitched into a comely smile. He must be quite pleased with the changes, as I have heard no complaints. I feel now that he is truly happy.

You may have already met him - slain him with Her Hand. Perhaps you have met all my master's [little pets](Lore_Ash_Zombie.md) as they descended on Her temple. I knew this would happen. For there must come a time when the Tribunal's champion will challenge my master, as the Nerevarine once did.

The [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) has three heads, and you wear them all. And so, by opening the way, you are worthy of dying by my master's hand.

Come to the graveyard by [Tel Mithryn](Lore_Tel_Mithryn.md). My master's creatures will dine on you there.
