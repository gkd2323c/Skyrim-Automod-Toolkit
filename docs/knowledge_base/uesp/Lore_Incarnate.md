# Incarnate

This article is about experimental Daedra. For the reincarnation of Nerevar, see [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).

Incarnates are an experimental race of elemental [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) beings created by changing the [animus](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) of a Daedra using a machine known as a [cataclyst](Lore_Cataclyst.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TKC-1)</sup> This experience is alien to the immortal Daedra, whose spirits are generally considered to be unchanging and immutable. The process causes the Daedra to lose all sense of individuality and consciousness, becoming an incarnation of destruction.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lyranth-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dothaz-3)</sup> Each Incarnate is a manifestation of a specific kind of [elemental](Lore_Elements.md) destruction or natural disaster.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The Perfected Incarnate was unique in that it embodied both destruction and creation, combining the power of the two forces.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VN-5)</sup> Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), the Daedric Prince of Change, ordered the construction of numerous cataclysts to create an army of Incarnates, although his efforts were ultimately thwarted with the destruction of these machines and the banishing of the few Incarnates that were successfully created.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Notes
- The ability to assume the form of the Tidal Wave Incarnate is possible through the use of Daedric polymorph magic. It is very similar in appearance to the Storm Incarnate.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TDW-7)</sup> This magic can also be used to assume the form of the Solstheimstone Incarnate, an incarnation of a [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) blizzard.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SI-8)</sup>
- The same power and process which was used to create the Incarnates was also used to create the [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md), four mortals imbued with parts of Mehrunes Dagon's [Egonymic](Lore_Nymic.md), granting them immense power that the Waking Flame cult intended to use to merge Nirn with the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOLyranth Key-9)</sup>
- [Maligraphies](Lore_Maligraphy.md) present in the [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) were known to take forms mimicking the Incarnates.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MH-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MU-11)</sup>
