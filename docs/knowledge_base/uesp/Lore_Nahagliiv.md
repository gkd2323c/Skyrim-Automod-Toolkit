Nahagliiv is a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Fury Burn Wither" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

Nahagliiv was among the dragons to die during the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md) era, with his remains being gathered by the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) and buried in a dragon mound west of [Rorikstead](Lore_Rorikstead.md). By [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373), local tales still named him as the dragon buried near the town, and the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) recorded him in their atlas of dragons as being deceased by report.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOD-1)</sup>

Upon his return, Alduin began the process of resurrecting the many dead dragons buried throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in ancient times by the Dragon Cult. Nahagliiv made a reappearance approximately before the reformed Blades unsealed [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Rat-2)</sup> and attempted to terrorize the town of Rorikstead. The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) slew Nahagliiv and absorbed his soul. Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Nahagliiv was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup>
