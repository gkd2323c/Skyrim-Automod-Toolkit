# Kh-Utta

*The Duke of Scamps said, 'I was summoned by Lord Dagon, master of the foul waters and fire, and I have brought the pennants of my seven legions.'*
— *[The Thirty-Six Lessons of Vivec, Sermon Seven](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_7.md)* Kh-Utta, the **Duke of Scamps**, was a duke among [Scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) and a servant to the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of the [House of Troubles](Lore_House_of_Troubles.md). He is spoken of in the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) religious text, *[The 36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*, which take place in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S7-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S14-2)</sup> He is implied to have been a greater being than the Scamps of modern day, and is the reason they were cursed to be lesser.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S14-2)</sup>

## History
*The Duke of Scamps therefore became a lesser thing, as did all his own children. Molag Bal said to them: 'You are the sons of liars, dogs, and wolf-headed women.' They have been useless to summon ever since.*
— *[The Thirty-Six Lessons of Vivec, Sermon Fourteen](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_14.md)* In the narrative of the Sermons, Kh-Utta was summoned by Lord [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) to bring the pennants of his seven legions. He bought them before the Daedric Prince's major domo, whose head was a bubble of foul water and fire, who saw the pennants and the legions they commanded. Said legions appeared to be representations of the major world powers of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). The major domo then told the Duke that while his legions were mighty, they were not enough to destroy [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) or the [Triune](Lore_Tribunal.md) way.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S7-1)</sup>

Later on, during the union of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), known as the Pomegranate Banquet, the Duke of Scamps arrived, and Molag Bal looked on the seven pennants with anger. It is said then that "the King of Rape had become necessary and therefore troubled for the rest of time". Molag Bal's legions and Kh-Utta's would fall into open war, but the recently born [children](Lore_Monster_Children.md) of Molag Bal and Vivec were too elaborate in power and form for the Duke's legions to handle. As a result, the Duke of Scamps was said to have become a lesser thing, as did all his children. It said this is the reason Scamps have been useless to summon ever since.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S14-2)</sup>
