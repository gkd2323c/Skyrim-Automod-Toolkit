Sage Voernet was a renowned [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) sage and author who was active around the start of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He was a keen and masterful manipulator of the magical arts in his time, and was listed alongside [Gyron Vardengroet](Lore_Gyron_Vardengroet.md) and [Druid](Lore_Druid.md) [King](Lore_Druid_King.md) [Kasorayn](Lore_Kasorayn.md) as one of the most powerful Breton mages in history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup> He was mentioned in the earliest written record of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) from around [1E 20](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_20), when he traveled to the Isle of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) to meet with [Ritemaster Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md). At the time, the Psijics were the counsellors of kings and proponents of the [Elder Way](Lore_Mysticism.md), taught to them by the original race that inhabited Tamriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-F:OA-2)</sup> The historical account makes Voernet one of the earliest known Bretons in history.

## Notes
- With his place in the timeline, Voernet would specifically be a Manmer, or in other words, a Half-Elf.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1High-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBMOP-4)</sup> These early Bretons were more elven in appearance than their modern counterparts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBMOP-4)</sup>
