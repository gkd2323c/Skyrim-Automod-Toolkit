[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/ON-map-Daen_Seeth.jpg/200px-ON-map-Daen_Seeth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Daen_Seeth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Daen_Seeth.jpg) Map of Daen Seeth circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). **Daen Seeth** (or **Daen Seth**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IEGT-1)</sup>) is a coastal region in east-central [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), within the larger region of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). It borders the region of [Zabamat](Lore_Zabamat.md) to the west, and the region of [Bal Foyen](Lore_Bal_Foyen.md) to the east. The capital of region is the city of [Davon's Watch](Lore_Davon%27s_Watch.md), which is a [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) port city in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md). [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md) is one of the two volcanoes located in Stonefalls and it houses one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md), Balreth. The town of [Senie](Lore_Senie.md) is located along a [foyada](Lore_Foyada.md) near the volcano, while the [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) settlement of Bal Sunnar can be found high in the mountains to the south. The Starved Plains was a battlefield during the time of the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md).
