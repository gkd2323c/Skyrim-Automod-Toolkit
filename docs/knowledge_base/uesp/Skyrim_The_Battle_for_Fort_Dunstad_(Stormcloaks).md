## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) at the [Pale Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Pale_Stormcloak_Camp.md).
2. Meet the Stormcloaks east of the fort.
3. Take the fort.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Receiving Orders
You will only be assigned this quest if you chose to give [The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) to the Imperial Legion during the quest [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). Go to the [Pale Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Pale_Stormcloak_Camp.md) and talk to [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md). Galmar will tell you to go to [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md) and meet the Stormcloaks preparing for the attack.

### Assaulting the Fort
Head northwest and join up with the men east of the fort. Speak to the leader of this force and he will agree to attack as soon as you are ready. If necessary, take a few moments to prepare and then lead the charge up the southeastern road. All Imperial forces must be eliminated. Your journal will keep track of the percentage of enemies left. While a straightforward battle beginning at the front entrance and working into the fort may be preferable for melee fighters, the fort also has several towers which are ideal for archers and ranged attacks.

### Conquering Hero
Once the enemy is fully vanquished, head back to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). He bestows upon you the title of Bone-Breaker and gives you a leveled weapon in honor of this new rank. You are now also able to purchase a [home](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) in Windhelm. Your next assignment will be the [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md).

## Notes
- Completing this quest will exile [Brina Merilis](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md) and [Horik Halfhand](Skyrim_Horik_Halfhand.md) to the [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), and re-install [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of The Pale.
