[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-place-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg) Uthgerd's House **Uthgerd's House** is the home of [Uthgerd the Unbroken](Skyrim_Uthgerd_the_Unbroken.md). It is located in the Wind District of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), across the street from [Carlotta Valentia's House](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.md).

It is owned by [Uthgerd the Unbroken](Skyrim_Uthgerd_the_Unbroken.md), although she rarely sets foot in the house, spending most of her time inside [The Bannered Mare](Skyrim_The_Bannered_Mare.md). It consists of one zone, *[Uthgerd's House](#Uthgerd.27s_House)*. She will always be in residence during the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md) and may be found there at other times.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is the first house on the right as you enter the Wind District of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) from the stairs nearest the gate. There are three [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) plants around the house which is attached to [Amren's house](Skyrim_Amren%27s_House.md).

### Uthgerd's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Uthgerd%27s_House.jpg) Inside Uthgerd's House The front door opens to the living room, with a ladder leading to the bedroom upstairs. Left of the door sits a small table with a bottle of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md) and a [sweet roll](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll.md) on top. At the foot of the ladder is a dresser with some random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md). Under the ladder are three [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks, two food barrels, a bowl with four [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md), an end table with clutter, and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) with a bit of gold. In the northwestern corner is a dinner table that seats three, laid with a bottle of ale and a piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md). In the adjacent corner is a cupboard with clutter, while the shelves above hold a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) and an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md). At the foot of the cupboard, scattered on the floor, are three [carrots](Skyrim_Carrot.md). A small table in front of the fireplace holds a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), and a [boiled creme treat](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md). The alcove to the south has a small cupboard with clutter with four [tomatoes](Skyrim_Tomato.md) on top. A bookshelf holds several [common books](Skyrim_Books.md).

The ladder leads upstairs where a wardrobe contains some clutter, clothes, and a slim chance of some [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), while an unlocked chest holds a bit of gold. A display case contains two [iron daggers](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). The bedroom has a dresser with random clothing and the end table near the bed contains clutter. At the foot of the table sits a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Boots) and on top of the table is a bottle of alto wine and a bowl with five [red apples](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md).

## Notes
- Once you recruit Uthgerd as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), all items, including the bed, are free to use.
- There is an [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Empty [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the left of the entrance door due to the sparseness of loot; however, the location will no longer be so empty if you are on one of [Vex's](Skyrim_Vex.md) burglary or sweep jobs.
