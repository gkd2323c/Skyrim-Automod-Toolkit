## Quick Walkthrough
1. Obtain a piece of Nordic jewelry and read the [*Certificate of Authenticity*](Skyrim_Certificate_of_Authenticity.md).
2. Travel to [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) and locate [Hugin Ice-Shaper](Skyrim_Hugin_Ice-Shaper.md) west of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
3. Read [*Hugin Ice-Shaper's Notebook*](Skyrim_Hugin_Ice-Shaper%27s_Notebook.md) and craft an item of Nordic jewelry to complete the quest.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Make a Purchase
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Nordic_Circlet.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Nordic_Circlet.png) Nordic circlet [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg) Hugin Ice-Shaper This quest will be added to your journal the first time you obtain a [Nordic Amulet](Skyrim_Nordic_Amulet.md), [Nordic Circlet](Skyrim_Nordic_Circlet.md), or [Nordic Ring](Skyrim_Nordic_Ring.md). A [*Certificate of Authenticity*](Skyrim_Certificate_of_Authenticity.md) will be automatically added to your inventory, and you will be directed to read it to progress the quest.

Nordic jewelry can be found in the world as random loot. Alternatively, all general goods and jewelry merchants will have these items available for sale, so the quickest method may be to purchase a piece from them. For example, in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) you can purchase Nordic jewelry from the [Pawned Prawn](Skyrim_Pawned_Prawn.md) or from [Madesi](Skyrim_Madesi.md)'s stall in Riften Grand Plaza. Note that these items are all relatively pricey given that they're pieces of jewelry.

Once you've obtained your first piece of Nordic jewelry, read the certificate to learn that the creator of the jewelry is a craftsman named [Hugin Ice-Shaper](Skyrim_Hugin_Ice-Shaper.md) who can be found on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).

### Stalhrim Lapidary
Travel to Solstheim and you will be directed to a spot west of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), in the ash wastes just north of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md). There you will find two hostile [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) (a melee fighter and an archer) standing over the body of Hugin. Dispense with them both and loot the melee reaver for three powerful enchanted items: a leveled [Nordic Amulet of Health](Skyrim_Nordic_Amulet_of_Health.md), a leveled [Nordic Circlet of Magicka](Skyrim_Nordic_Circlet_of_Magicka.md), and a leveled [Nordic Ring of Stamina](Skyrim_Nordic_Ring_of_Stamina.md). You can then locate and read [*Hugin Ice-Shaper's Notebook*](Skyrim_Hugin_Ice-Shaper%27s_Notebook.md) to learn how to craft Nordic jewelry.

To complete the quest, you will need to craft a piece of Nordic jewelry yourself. Be sure to grab the required components from the satchel next to the body: three samples of [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) and three [quicksilver ingots](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Ingot.md). Head back into town and find a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) in order to craft the jewelry of your choice and finish the quest.

## Notes
- This quest was originally added to your journal automatically upon installing the [Nordic Jewelry](Skyrim_Nordic_Jewelry.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). You would then be directed to Riften to purchase a Nordic jewelry item, although obtaining the jewelry via any other method would also progress the quest. This was changed with the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update, and the quest now only starts once you have already obtained a Nordic jewelry item.
- The quest can also be started by simply approaching Hugin Ice-Shaper's corpse on Solstheim.
