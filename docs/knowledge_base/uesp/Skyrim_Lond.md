[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-npc-Lond.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lond.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lond.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lond.jpg) Lond at work **Lond** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) and is one of the many workers at the [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

He spends his entire life working the mine and never even pauses to eat or sleep. Between 7am and 7pm he can be found working the [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) outside the mine; the rest of the time, he uses his pickaxe inside.

He wears a pair of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). He carries a selection of [lower class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
Before [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) is complete, every inhabitant of Dawnstar will be kept up by horrible nightmares. Lond also has to deal with this.

*"If I get any rest soon, I'll count myself richer than a king."* He seems to be perfectly happy with his simple lifestyle, implying that he doesn't really fit in anywhere.

*"I mine. Work the smelter. Ain't much else to tell."* *"Been mining all my life. Quicksilver is as much my home as anywhere."*
### Conversations
When working the smelter, he is often accompanied by his boss [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md) who is at war with his wife [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md), the owner of [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md):

**Lond:** *"If you don't mind me saying, Leigelf. You're working too hard. Go home."*
**Leigelf:** *"Never. Not until we turn this mine around, drive Beitild out of business, and force her to apologize to me for everything she's done!"*
**Lond:** *"What is this feud you have with your wife? What started this?"*
**Leigelf:** *"I don't want to talk about it."*
**Lond:** *"You don't want to talk about it, or you don't know?"*
**Leigelf:** *"I don't want to talk about it."* Leigelf can also be heard complimenting Lond on his loyalty:

**Leigelf:** *"Thank you for sticking by me."*
**Lond:** *"Where else would I go? Dawnstar is my home and working here is my job."*
**Leigelf:** *"It's good to hear you say that. I wish my wife was as simple as you."*
**Lond:** *"I'll, uh, just take that as a compliment."*
## Notes
- According to the Creation Kit, his full name is **Lond Northstrider**. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* also refers to him under this name.
- The guide also states that Lond lives with fellow miner [Fruki](Skyrim_Fruki.md) at [her house](Skyrim_Fruki%27s_House.md). While Lond actually is a part of the house's locklist, ensuring he could theoretically enter the building, none of his packages ever make him go there. - The house does actually contain two separate beds, implying that Lond was indeed meant to sleep there.
- He shares his name with two other people in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Lond) and [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Lond).
