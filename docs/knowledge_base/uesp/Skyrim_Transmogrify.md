- *Turns a low level enemy into a harmless creature for 30 seconds or until they take damage*

**Transmogrify** is an apprentice level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes the target to turn into a [chicken](Skyrim_Chicken.md) for 30 seconds. It cannot be found in the game and there is no spell tome for it. The effect is similar to the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md)'s Transmogrify effect.

## Effects
- Transmogrify target for 30 secs

## Notes
- Unlike the Wabbajack, targets cannot turn into a [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) or [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit.md). It was intended for those creatures to be possible transformations, but the script for this effect has not been set up correctly.
- Like the Wabbajack, the Transmogrify effect does not work properly on guards. Using the spell on them will spawn a chicken, but the guard will not vanish. Casting the spell on the chicken will cause it to vanish and the guard to teleport to its position.
