[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/OB-place-Valus_Mountains.jpg/200px-OB-place-Valus_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Valus_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Valus_Mountains.jpg) The Valus Mountains seen from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/OB-map-Valus_Mountains.jpg/200px-OB-map-Valus_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Valus_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Valus_Mountains.jpg) Map of the Valus Mountains in Cyrodiil The Valus Mountains are a mountain range on the border between [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They converge with the [Jerall](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) and [Velothi](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) ranges at [Mount Trolhetta](Lore_Mount_Trolhetta.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONTrolhetta-2)</sup> They are sometimes considered an extension of the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1M-3)</sup> [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) of the region often travel between the provinces through secret passageways and caves under the mountains. Because of this, cultural exchanges have taken place between tribes ever since.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLoad Muck-4)</sup> The [Kavas Rim Pass](Lore_Kavas_Rim_Pass.md) is a road through the provincial border.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-2920v5-5)</sup>

## Cyrodiil
The Hero Hill, a secret shrine to [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), and the ruins of Fort Scinia are some of the many landmarks in the region. The mountains are home to several [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins, such as [Fanacas](Lore_Fanacas.md) and [Kemen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kemen). The city of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) is located on the foothills.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OB-6)</sup>

During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), one of the two bases of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) was located in the Valus Mountains, along with the Scroll Temple of Ghartok.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONC-7)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a rogue [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) wizard [Frathen Drothan](Lore_Frathen_Drothan.md) crossed the Valus Mountains from [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md) in search for the lost Ayleid city of [Varsa Baalim](Lore_Varsa_Baalim.md), hidden beneath the ruins of [Sundercliff Watch](Lore_Sundercliff_Watch.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-OBMR-8)</sup> Circa [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), a village called [Mountain Watch](Lore_Mountain_Watch.md) existed in the Valus Mountains. It was evacuated when the undead forces of [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) crossed the mountains and invaded Cyrodiil.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Lo S-9)</sup> Clawhorn Mountain [Goats](Lore_Goat.md) are native to the region.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CMG-10)</sup>

## Morrowind
The Valus Mountains form the western border of two regions of Morrowind, [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) and [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). The cities of Kragenmoor and [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md) are located on the foothills.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONDS-11)</sup>
