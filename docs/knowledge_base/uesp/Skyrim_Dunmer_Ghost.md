[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Dunmer_Ghost.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dunmer_Ghost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dunmer_Ghost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dunmer_Ghost.jpg) Dunmer Ghost Three **Dunmer Ghosts** can be found when retrieving the Crown Of Barenziah in [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) after finding all 24 [Unusual Gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) (Stones of Barenziah) during the quest [No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md). They are the [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of the ambushed [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) caravan of refugees from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). One of the ghosts appears to be sitting next to her skeleton and was a battle mage prior to being in the caravan as can be learned from the journal where she is sitting. This ghost will at times respond to spells equipped if you have them "unsheathed". The second appears to be wearing full elven armor but does not appear to have any dialogue options. The third ghost is located to the right of the Crown of Barenziah beside a pillar.

## Related Quests
- **[No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md)**: Find the [24 gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) to restore the Crown of Barenziah.
