# Ash Spawn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg) Ash Spawn Ash Spawn are [undead](Lore_Undead.md) [ash creatures](Lore_Ash_Creature.md) that roam the [Ashlands](Lore_Solstheim_Ashlands.md) of southern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They began to appear on the island after the massive eruptions of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) early in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5) and onward covered the southern part of the island in ash. Their spawning from the ash is caused by [heart stones](Lore_Heart_Stone.md), which contain some trace of the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md)'s power and give life to the bones of Solstheim's dead found buried in the wastes, infused with the ash of Red Mountain. They are immune to poison and highly resistant to fire, but have no particular weaknesses.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup>

[Neloth](Lore_Neloth.md) developed a spell that allowed conjuration of Ash Spawn in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
