[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-npc-Wary_Outlaw.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wary_Outlaw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wary_Outlaw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wary_Outlaw.jpg) Wary Outlaw The **Wary Outlaw** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in the [Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He spends all of his time in the Bee and Barb awaiting the arrival of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). He spends much of his time nervously pacing around, but may occasionally sit down to relax or have a quick bite or drink.

He wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), including the [bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md). He also wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and carries a copy of [*A Kiss, Sweet Mother*](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md).

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.
