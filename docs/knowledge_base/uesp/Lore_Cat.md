# Cat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-npc-Finunval_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Finunval_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Finunval_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Finunval_02.jpg) A [High Elf](Lore_Altmer.md) with a feline companion **Cats** are domestic felines that are usually found in populated areas. They are often tasked with catching rodents. The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) share many feline features with cats, particularly the [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md), who are often confused for house cats outside of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).

## Breeds

### Abecean Ratter Cat
A short-haired brown cat famous from [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md) to [Woodhearth](Lore_Woodhearth.md) for its rodent-hunting skills. <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOPet-1)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Abecean_Ratter_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/ON-pet-Abecean_Ratter_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Abecean_Ratter_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Abecean_Ratter_Cat.jpg) Abecean Ratter Cat (ESO)

### Big-Eared Ginger Mouser
A breed of cat known for its mousing skills and affectionate personality. They are favored as barn cats, and are loved by many for their cute, goofy-eared looks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Their kittens make excellent shipboard pets. Their large ears allow them to hear the slightest motions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Big-Eared_Ginger_Mouser)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Mouser.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Mouser.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Mouser.jpg) Big-Eared Ginger Mouser (ESO)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Kitten.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Kitten.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Big-Eared_Ginger_Kitten.jpg) Big-Eared Ginger Kitten (ESO)

### Black Cat
A short-haired black-colored cat.

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Black_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/ON-pet-Black_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Black_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Black_Cat.jpg) Black Cat (ESO)

### Grimalkin
The Grimalkin is a fluffy, prong-eared cat renowned for its excellent hearing. They have four ears, which enhances their sense of hearing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Odd-Eyed grimalkins are heterochromic cats with white fur. They are rumored to have supernatural abilities by some.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The Prong-Eared Forge Mouser is a grimalkin with glossy black fur and amber eyes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Prong-Eared_Grimalkin)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Grimalkin.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Grimalkin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Grimalkin.jpg) Prong-Eared Grimalkin (ESO)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Forge_Mouser.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Forge_Mouser.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Forge_Mouser.jpg) Prong-Eared Forge Mouser (ESO)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Odd-Eyed_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Odd-Eyed_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Prong-Eared_Odd-Eyed_Cat.jpg) Prong-Eared Odd-Eyed Cat (ESO)

### Heartland Coon Cat
An enormous breed of cat from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) with pronged ears. The Heartland Coon Cat is a renowned hunter of pests such as moths and large rodents.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Heartland_Coon_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/ON-pet-Heartland_Coon_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Heartland_Coon_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Heartland_Coon_Cat.jpg) Heartland Coon Cat (ESO)

### Housecat
A short-haired gray tabby cat, said to be a distant relative of the [Alfiq](Lore_Khajiit.md#Alfiq) subspecies of Khajiit.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Housecat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/ON-pet-Housecat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Housecat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Housecat.jpg) Housecat (ESO)

### Mages Guild Sentry Cat
A large-eared cat often seen in Mages Guildhalls. It is said to be able to detect any and all intruders, including [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and those who are magically concealed. <sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mages_Guild_Sentry_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/ON-crown_store-Mages_Guild_Sentry_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Mages_Guild_Sentry_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Mages_Guild_Sentry_Cat.jpg) Mages Guild Sentry Cat found in a Mages guildhall (ESO)

### Milady's Cloud Cat
Milk-white cats that are often kept by the [House of Dibella](Lore_House_of_Dibella.md). They are said to be sacred to [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md) and serve as her representatives in the animal kingdom. A House of Dibella will typically have three or four milk-white cats within its walls.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOPets M-10)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Milady%27s_Cloud_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/ON-crown_store-Milady%27s_White_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Milady%27s_White_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Milady%27s_White_Cat.jpg) Milady's Cloud Cat (ESO)

### Necrom Ghostgazer Cat
The Necrom Ghostgazer is a short-haired sealpoint cat favored by [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) wizards. They can purportedly see demons and spirits.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Necrom_Ghostgazer_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/ON-creature-Sir_Sabre_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Sir_Sabre_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Sir_Sabre_Cat.jpg) Necrom Ghostgazer Cat (ESO)

### Senchal Striped Cat
The Senchal Stripe is a cunning and elegant short-haired breed. It's known for being mischievous and getting into places where it doesn't belong.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Senchal_Striped_Cat)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/ON-pet-Senchal_Striped_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Senchal_Striped_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Senchal_Striped_Cat.jpg) Senchal Striped Cat (ESO)

### Sphynx
The Sphynx, also called the "Sphynx Lynx", is a hairless cat popular with nobles who are allergic to cats.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> The black sphynx cats of the Alik'r, dubbed "Tu'whacca's Sphynx Cats", are said to bring the blessing of [Tu'whacca](Lore_Tu%27whacca.md) to their household. For this reason, they are honored. They have also been observed sleeping with those who near death, giving them a reputation as guides to the [Far Shores](Lore_Far_Shores.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sphynx_Lynx)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/ON-pet-Sphynx_Lynx.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Sphynx_Lynx.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Sphynx_Lynx.jpg) Sphynx Lynx (ESO)
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-pet-Tu%27whacca%27s_Sphynx_Cat.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Tu%27whacca%27s_Sphynx_Cat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Tu%27whacca%27s_Sphynx_Cat.jpg) Tu'whacca's Sphynx Cat (ESO)
