- *Heals the target 75 points, but not undead, atronachs or machines.*

**Heal Other** is an adept level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will instantly [restore the health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) of the target by 75 pts.

## Effects
- [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), 75 pts
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 75 pts; if the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Regeneration), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 112.5.

## Notes
- Despite the description's claim that it will not work on undead, it *does* work on vampires (and notably, [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> and [Heal Undead](Skyrim_Heal_Undead.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> do not). <sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Heal_Other)*]</sup>
