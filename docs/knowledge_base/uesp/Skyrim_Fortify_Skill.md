## Notes
- Fortify Skill is the only type of skill alteration effect that exists in Skyrim. Drain, Damage, and Restore do not exist for skills.
- Fortify Persuasion, Enchanting, Light Armor, and Heavy Armor are the only Fortify Skill effects that actually increase the skill level itself.
