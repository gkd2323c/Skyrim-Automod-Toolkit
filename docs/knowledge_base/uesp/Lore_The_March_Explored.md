## *[Chapter I](Lore_The_March_Explored,_Chapter_I.md)*
Gentle reader, welcome. Welcome to open skies and red earth. To the fields of grass and savage landscape of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). Welcome, reader, to the end of the world. To the [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md).

Herein, find an unedited journal of observations. Thoughts jotted down during my first trip across this stark landscape. Some of the hidden gems of the land, some of the most striking locales, will see description in these pages.

From I, your humble guide. Enjoy!

—Fenlil the Wayfarer

*<The rest of the pages have been torn out.>*

## *[Chapter III](Lore_The_March_Explored,_Chapter_III.md)*
The [March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) marks the eastern boundary of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and thus a boundary of the Ancient diaspora. While the rare pile of stones can be found deep into [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), the ruins of the March provide some of the most interesting insights into [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) psychology.

## *[Chapter VI](Lore_The_March_Explored,_Chapter_VI.md)*
… there's no truth to the rumors of dark cults in the [March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). I can assure you, gentle reader. Bandits may take your goods and threaten your life, but the fell [Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) hold no sway in this savage land.

## *[Chapter VII](Lore_The_March_Explored,_Chapter_VII.md)*
I will say this for the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), their architecture is wholly unique on the continent of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The beauty of their temples is awe-inspiring to behold. The House of the Dance in [Rawl'kha](Lore_Rawl%27kha.md) is a wonder.

It's a shame, then, that so many of their greatest monuments are decayed and ruined. So many fine places fallen to disrepair.

It seems to be a theme across the face of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), does it not reader? Beauty and splendor fallen into ruin over time ….

## *[Chapter IX](Lore_The_March_Explored,_Chapter_IX.md)*
… and I must confess, I don't understand it at all. I've spent six seasons in the [March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md), all told, and the lunar religion of the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) makes just as much sense to me now as it did when I first arrived.

Something about the pull of the moons on the waters of your mind. I don't know. Thank [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md) we [High Elves](Lore_Altmer.md) have a finer, cleaner sensibility to our religious studies ….
