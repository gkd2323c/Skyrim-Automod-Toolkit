Zebiden-jo (or **Zibbiden-jo**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOShazah-1)</sup> was the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) people during the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), before the unification of the Sixteen Kingdoms. Little is known about Zebiden-jo's life. However, during his trials to become the Mane circa [2E 182](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_182), it is known that he faced the spirit of one of his parents, as well as [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) soul reavers. These trials reflect the challenges faced by a Mane before their ascension.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOShazah-1)</sup>

He died at some point prior to the unification of Elsweyr and the formation of the Elsweyr Confederacy in [2E 309](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_309). His remains are preserved in the Tomb of the Manes in [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) Necropolis, a huge tomb complex located southwest of Rimmen itself. He was buried together with two other prominent Manes, [Jinninji-ri](Lore_Jinninji-ri.md) and [Tullar-dra](Lore_Tullar-dra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zebiden-jo-2)</sup> His spirit's rest was briefly interrupted in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) escorted the remains of the ancient Khajiiti ruler Jora-ri to the burial chamber in the Tomb of the Manes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jora-ri-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Although [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) was dubbed the First Mane, there were numerous Manes such as Zebiden-jo who lived chronologically before him.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Other Mane-5)</sup> [Lawrence Schick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Lawrence_Schick) stated that it could be "transcription error, but that would invalidate all these lovely theories" and as such refused to confirm that notion.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LSP-6)</sup> The founding date of the [Pridehome](Lore_Pridehome.md) in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) further confirms the existence of Manes prior to the ascension of Rid-Thar-ri'Datta.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONDoomstone Load-7)</sup>
