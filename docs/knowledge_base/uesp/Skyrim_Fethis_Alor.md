[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-npc-Fethis_Alor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fethis_Alor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fethis_Alor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fethis_Alor.jpg) Fethis Alor **Fethis Alor** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in [his house](Skyrim_Alor_House.md) at [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). He is a general goods merchant who will sell various types of items, and requests that you locate [East Empire pendants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Pendant) for him. He has a daughter named [Dreyla](Skyrim_Dreyla_Alor.md), who helps him run his shop.

Fethis wears a [Dunmer outfit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Outfit) with [Dunmer shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Shoes), and he carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alor_House_Key) to his house, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

He gets up early at 6am to eat a two-hour breakfast with his daughter in his house. Then at 8am, he heads out to his stall and works for twelve hours. At 8pm, he will close the shop and heads to [the Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) to drink for four hours. At midnight, he either heads to the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) to work for six hours, or if the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), he heads to his house to finally go to sleep.

## Dialogue
His greetings are:

*"Want to buy anything, or just laugh at our misfortunes?"* *"I'd be on the first ship off of this island if I had the coin. What a waste."* *"If you're looking to buy, then I'm looking to sell."* *"Yeah, so. What can I get you? Got everything from potions to pitchforks."* *"It's busier now that things are starting to look up. I guess I might stay a bit longer after all."* **(If you've helped Raven Rock get [back](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md) [on its](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead) [feet](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md))** Once he's free from [Miraak's](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) control, he'll say:

*"Being freed from building the shrine... I don't even know how to begin to thank you."* If he is at the Earth Stone when it is cleansed he will say:

*"If I could only get my hands on Miraak. I'd make him pay for playing with my mind."* When spoken to directly he can be asked questions:

**What exactly do you sell here?** *"The question you should be asking is "what don't I sell here" because I have pretty much any supplies you might need. If it's not a weapon, potion or armor, then it's likely that I have it."* **Where did you get all these items?** *"[Gjalund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gjalund_Salt-Sage) over at the docks brings me most of my supplies, and the rest comes from bartering with the people in town. Oh, [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md) gets me a few things once in a while. He usually has the most amazing deals that I can't pass up."* **Are sales that bad out here?** *"Bad? What if I told you that you're the first person that's even considered looking at my wares today. I've half a mind to contact my friends at the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company) and see if they need an extra hand in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)."* **You have friends at the East Empire Company?** *"Oh, absolutely. In fact, I've corresponded with [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) herself more than once. You know, the lady who runs the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)... the biggest warehouse they have in Skyrim."* **Okay.** *"Bah, you're just like the rest of the folks around here. Mark my words... the East Empire Company will return to Raven Rock, and when they do, I'll be at the top of their list."* Once the mines are [reopened](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md):

**How are things now that the mines are open again?** *"Things are picking up... for now. All I'm wondering is how long it'll take for House Redoran to begin ignoring us again if the mine runs out of ore for a second time."* When exiting conversation, he may say:

*"If you need any supplies, you know where to find me."*

## Notes
- In his basement, the [first](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:First_Letter_from_EEC), [second](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Second_Letter_from_EEC), and [third letters from the EEC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Third_Letter_from_EEC) make it clear that this is not quite the friendship he holds it out to be.
- Even if [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md) is completed, Fethis' dialogue about [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) doesn't change, and he still refers to her in the present tense. Additionally, there is no dialogue option for you to inform Fethis about Vittoria's fate.
- Between 8pm and midnight (when he can be found at the Retching Netch), he will not buy and sell goods but he will still trade East Empire pendants for gold.

## Bugs
- Fethis' line during Reluctant Steward should say "You should ask around at The Retching Netch." However, the word "should" is not spoken, making it grammatically unsound. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. The line and its matching subtitle have now been altered to say "Ask around at The Retching Netch.".
- Fethis is unaffected by the [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md) perk. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- Despite it being clear in-game that he is the father of Dreyla, the game data incorrectly has them set up as husband and wife. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.5, fixes this bug.
