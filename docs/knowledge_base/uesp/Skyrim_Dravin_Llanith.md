[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-npc-Dravin_Llanith.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dravin_Llanith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dravin_Llanith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dravin_Llanith.jpg) Dravin Llanith **Dravin Llanith** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Classes.md#Farmer) who works and lives at [Merryfair Farm](Skyrim_Merryfair_Farm.md). He is married to [Synda Llanith](Skyrim_Synda_Llanith.md).

Dravin wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

He gets up early at 5am and starts his day with a one-hour breakfast. He then heads outside to farm for most of the day, only to stop for a one-hour dinner in the farmhouse. At 11pm, he finally calls it a day and goes to bed.

## Dialogue
His greetings can be:

*"Who in the name of Azura are you?"* *"It's not safe around here. There's thieves out there looking to steal everything you own."* *"You look suspicious. You better not be one of those damn thieves."* *"You even think of taking something from my house... and I'll kill you."* His goodbyes:

*"Watch yourself out there or they'll take everything away from you."* *"[Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), thieves and [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md). I should have stayed in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Bow to the Master
**You've had trouble with thieves?** *"Are you kidding me? We're all alone out here except for a few guards who couldn't give a damn about us. We make easy pickings for thieves looking to steal everything we worked so hard for. Not only did they take my bow, but they took all of our coin as well. Nothing's sacred to these people."* **What happened to your bow?** *"A few days ago, some thieves from [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) broke in here and stole it! Can you imagine? Took the only thing of value we had. If you have the guts to head into the [Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) and get my bow back, I'll pay you what I can."* **Sorry, I try and avoid thieves.** *"Fine. I'll wait until someone else with a little more courage comes along."* If you have already found the bow before meeting him, you can say:

**No need. It's right here.** *"That's it! That's my bow!"* Otherwise:

**I'll see what I can do.** *"Thanks. It would be nice to have it back... means a lot to me."* **How do you know the thieves were from Riften?** *"I bumped into them when I was coming back to my farm after buying supplies. I know those lowlifes took it with them back to the Ratway. They left an unmistakable stench behind. It was a family heirloom. I can't even put a price on how much that thing means to me."* As you leave to find the bow he will further tell you:

*"The Ratway is somewhere under Riften, shouldn't be too tough to find."* And upon returning:

*"So, has my bow turned up yet?"* **I found your bow.** *"That's it! You found it!"* Either way, upon returning his bow he will continue with:

*"I don't know how you got it back, and I don't want to know. Frankly, I don't even care... I'm just so glad to see it again. Here, this is all can afford to give you as compensation. Hope it's enough."* After the quest, he'll now greet you with:

*"Thanks again for getting my bow back from those thieves. Next time they come knocking I'll be ready."*

## Notes
- It is best to do Dravin's quest while you are still comparatively low leveled: since there is a wildlife spawning point very close to the farm, and despite the best efforts of the local guards, either or both of the Llaniths may be killed by wildlife, most usually bears. It doesn't help that they have a tendency to rush into combat rather than run away and leave the fight to the guards. They are also vulnerable to potential dragon attacks that spawn just outside Riften.
