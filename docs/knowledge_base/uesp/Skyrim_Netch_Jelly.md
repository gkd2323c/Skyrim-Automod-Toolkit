## Notes
- As Paralysis is its first effect, when you eat a sample to figure out its effect, you will experience the paralysis. Upon it wearing off, the camera enters third-person (if you weren't already) as your character gets back up, similar to when you are knocked down from the Unrelenting Force shout.
