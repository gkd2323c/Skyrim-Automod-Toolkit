### The Ranks of the Blessed
Blessed are the [Bonemen](Skyrim_Boneman.md), for they serve without self in spirit forever.

Blessed are the [Mistmen](Skyrim_Mistman.md), for they blend in the glory of the transcendent spirit.

Blessed are the [Wrathmen](Skyrim_Wrathman.md), for they render their rage unto the ages.

Blessed are the [Masters](Skyrim_Ideal_Master.md), for they bridge the past and span the future.

### The Litany of Service

#### The Boneman's Oath
We die.

We pray.

To live.

We serve.

#### The Master's Voice
You swore.

To Serve.

Your Lord.
