# Haarvenu

Haarvenu is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted in the school of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) magic, particularly elemental attacks. The bloodline dominates an area of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) - specifically the regions of [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md), [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md), and [Shalgora](Lore_Shalgora.md). They are the enemies of the [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
