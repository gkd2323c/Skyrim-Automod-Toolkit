[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Kagrenzel (translated as *"Music City"* in [Dwemeris](Lore_Dwemeris.md))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOCalcelmo-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KJ-2)</sup> is a small [dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) found in the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is found in the region of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md). Kagrenzel was a holdout shrouded in mystery, a *"death trap"* reminiscent of Yokudan tombs; the journal of a seeker after a Dwemer artifact opined that Kagrenzel's *"entire purpose is to kill those who would search it."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IPo M-3)</sup> A vast pitfall leads into a giant chasm, which flows into **Stony Creek Cave**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRKagrenzel-4)</sup> At one point, the stronghold contained the left side of the [Wrathstone](Lore_Wrathstone.md).

## History
Amidst the Aetherian Wars, sometime in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the ruins of Kagrenzel contained the left side of the Wrathstone, an ancient tablet. During a raid on the city-state of [Mzulft](Lore_Mzulft.md), conducted by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md), the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) architect, Mhuvnak had left for Kagrenzel, seeking refuge from the attack, all the while, recovering the Wrathstone. When the traps in Kagrenzel proved to be insufficient to hold half of the tablet, Mhuvnak would travel across Eastmarch and establish his stronghold, the [Vault of Mhuvnak](Lore_Vault_of_Mhuvnak.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IPo M-3)</sup> Here the tablet would be stored for many years, even following the Dwemer's extinction.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONLock-5)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the ruins of Kagrenzel were still present. Many adventurers would come to Kagrenzel, only to succumb to its many traps.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Skyrim-6)</sup> The nearby Stony Creek Cave was known to contain a number of artifacts during this time, such as Finn's Lute (the very first eight course lute ever created), [Ruin's Edge](Lore_Ruin%27s_Edge.md), and a [Stone of Barenziah](Lore_Stone_of_Barenziah.md). The Forgotten Hero would journey to the connected Stony Creek Cave during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md) to retrieve [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s replica of [Sunder](Lore_Sunder.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LRTCCEIC-7)</sup>
