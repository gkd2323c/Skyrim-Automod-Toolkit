Sparsely populated by [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) farmers and fishermen, and far off any major trade routes, it has little strategic or economic importance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle_02.jpg/200px-ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle_02.jpg) Bleakrock Isle [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle.jpg/200px-ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bleakrock_Isle.jpg) Landscape of Bleakrock Isle Archaeological findings indicate that [Auri-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auri-El) was once worshipped on the island, possibly indicating the presence of [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

In the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), the island was ruled over by the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md). The local [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) was named Haldriin, and was entombed in Skyshroud Barrow. At some point, a wealthy family moved to the island and set up a mining operation at Hozzin's Folly. However, the mine was simply a cover for [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worship, and after a disastrous ritual the site was left abandoned. The main settlement on the island was Bleakrock Village, a farming community with a small dock.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md), the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) established a small garrison on the island to keep watch for enemy ships.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Bandits began appearing on Bleakrock around this time, and many citizens begun to carry around alabaster whistles to sound when they required aid.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOCABW-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) targeted the island as the first step to a large-scale invasion of the Pact's territory. A group of bandits were hired to cause havoc and reawaken the Daedric magic within Hozzin's Folly and a necromancer was sent to Skyshroud Barrow to raise the dead. The local garrison learned of the Covenant's activities on the island but were too late to request backup. The village was razed by pyromancers and the few survivors were evacuated to mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

## Notable Places
- Bleakrock Village
- Companions Point
- Hozzin's Folly - Mine in west-central Bleakrock Isle. It was first started by a family from Stonefalls, who were rumored to be daedra worshipers.
- [Orkey's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Orkey) Hollow - Cave in the north of Bleakrock Isle. The locals of the island have a custom of daring each other to spend a night inside the cave.
- Skyshroud Barrow
- Last Rest - Nordic burial tomb south of Bleakrock Village
