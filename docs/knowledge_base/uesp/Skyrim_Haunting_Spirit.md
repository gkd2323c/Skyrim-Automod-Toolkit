[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Haunting_Spirit.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Haunting_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haunting_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haunting_Spirit.jpg) A Haunting Spirit **Haunting Spirits** are undead beings summoned by an Apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell, [Conjure Haunting Spirit](Skyrim_Conjure_Haunting_Spirit.md) added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

As a ghost she is immune to [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md) and [disease](Skyrim_Disease.md), and has 25% resistance to [frost](Skyrim_Frost.md). She can also [walk on water](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Water_Walking). On her left hand she can use the [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) spell to deal health and stamina damage.

She wears a [Silver Sapphire Necklace](Skyrim_Silver.md#Silver_Sapphire_Necklace), [warlock boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Boots), [vampire robes](Skyrim_Vampire_Robes.md), and a haunting spirit hood. She has a [Bound Dagger](Skyrim_Bound_Dagger.md) to attack.

## Notes
- The appearance and utility of the Haunting Spirit was inspired by a *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* card [of the same name](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Haunting_Spirit).
