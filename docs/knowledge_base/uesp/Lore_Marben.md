**Saint** Marben is a saint of Arkay.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAlvaren-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Saint Marben's Fingerbone is a holy relic of Arkay. The Fingerbone was buried at [Sanguine Barrows](Lore_Sanguine_Barrows.md) until it was retrieved from Marben's grave in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
