Valentis was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) prince<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Statue Desc-1)</sup> and the brother of Sorcerer-King [Celemaril](Lore_Celemaril.md). He revolted against his brother and founded the town of [Rivercrest](Lore_Rivercrest.md) at the site of his brother's tomb.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saashi-2)</sup>

## History

### First Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/LO-misc-Blades_Mural.png/200px-LO-misc-Blades_Mural.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Blades_Mural.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Blades_Mural.png) Mural depicting the conflict between Valentis and his brother In the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), together with his undead forces,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saashi-2)</sup> the immortal Celemaril waged war across Tamriel.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Telarendil-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Shulkunaak-4)</sup> His brother Valentis led other Ayleid kings in a rebellion against Celemaril. With [varla stones](Lore_Varla_Stone.md) and an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), Valentis donned Celemaril's crown and spoke a ritual incantation, sealing his brother within a crypt beneath a [marble](Lore_Marble.md) statue bearing his likeness.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Shulkunaak-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saashi-2)</sup> After sealing his brother, Valentis would then erase much of the records of Celemaril and his conquest of Tamriel.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Telarendil-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saashi-2)</sup> Valentis also founded the town of [Rivercrest](Lore_Rivercrest.md) at the site of the statue which sealed Celemaril.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Statue Desc-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saashi-2)</sup>
