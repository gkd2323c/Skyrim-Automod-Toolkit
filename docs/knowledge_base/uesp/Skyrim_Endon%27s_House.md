## Endon's House
The entrance is normally adept-locked. The property has piles of fancy cookware throughout including [bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), [platters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Platter) and [jugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug), etc. Upon entering there is a large room with a couple of shoulder-height partition walls segregating separate areas. On your right against the east wall is a set of shelves holding a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), a sheaf of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) and a copy of *[Chaurus Pie: A Recipe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chaurus_Pie__A_Recipe)*, with a barrel containing bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) to the right, in the corner and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots) on the floor beside it. Further along the wall to the left is a small square table and chair with a [silver amethyst ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Amethyst_Ring) on top and a washbasin to the left, with a bowl on top, containing an empty [lesser soul gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem). There is a food barrel in front of the partition wall to the north.

In the southwest corner of the room is a small raised area with a long stone table against the west wall with two display cabinets on top, they are both adept-locked and contain many different [silver necklaces](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Necklace) and [rings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ring). To the left, against the south wall is a set of shelves holding an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). To the right of the stone table is a small set of shelves with a food barrel to the right of them. In the northwestern corner is a door through into a bedroom, while in the northeastern corner is another raised section. This one has a fireplace on the east wall with a piece of [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) in the flames. To the left is an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), with a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) along the west wall. Against the back of the partition wall is a stone table with a piece of silver ore and an open crate containing four [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on the table and another open crate to the left of the table, on the floor holding five pieces of silver ore.

Through the door into the bedroom there is a wooden bench and stool on the left, against the west wall, with a plate of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and cheese on top of the stool and a bowl of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) on the floor. To the right, against the south wall is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand and against the east wall is a long wooden table with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [pheasant breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast), a portion of grilled leeks, two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) and a cast iron pot containing a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes) and a [carrot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot). Under the table is a basket containing a cabbage, a gourd and two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato).

To the north is a raised section with two small flights of stairs up to it, with a low wall between them holding a platter and gourd. In the center of the north wall is a king-sized bed coming out from the wall with an end table to the right, on top of which is a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bunch of [jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md). In the northeastern corner, along the east wall is a child's single owned bed, with a round wooden table and chair at the foot of the bed with a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton), an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing) and a copy of the non-skill book version of *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)* on top. To the left of the king-sized bed is a dresser with a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md), a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), a [blue butterfly wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Butterfly_Wing) and a [silver sapphire necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Sapphire_Necklace) on top. Against the west wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), a couple of pieces of cheese, a [slaughterfish egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg), three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulbs and a bowl of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust).

## Notes
- In the far side of the house there are samples of garlic, glow dust, sliced goat cheese, and a slaughterfish egg which clearly resemble [Pac-man](Skyrim_Easter_Eggs.md#Pac-Man).
- There are two [shadowmarks](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) found here: - A [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Fence shadowmark is to the left of the entrance door. This is due to the fact that after you complete the [reputation quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Lining) in Markarth, [Endon](Skyrim_Endon.md) becomes a [fence](Skyrim_Fence_(merchant).md) for you and the guild.
- A [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Protected shadowmark is to the bottom left of the door because Endon is a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).
