Windwalker Tamahl *An ode to Gaiden Shinji's loyal aide* *
---
Ever faithful was Tamahl,
Quick of wit and slow to fall,
So his friends remember.

He swung his blade with peerless grace,
A tranquil mask upon his face,
As his enemies remember.

For [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md)'s loyal aide,
No debt of honor went unpaid,
Shall everyone remember.
