# Dark Elvish

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/RG-comic-Page_14-Dark_Elvish.jpg/200px-RG-comic-Page_14-Dark_Elvish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_14-Dark_Elvish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_14-Dark_Elvish.jpg) A Dres overseer [yelling](Lore_Dark_Elf_Script.md) in Dark Elvish Dark Elvish<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARGreetings-1)</sup> (also known as the **Dark Elf language**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AFAt D-2)</sup> **Dunmeri**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ma Lot H-3)</sup> **Dunmeris**,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGFP-4)</sup> or **Chimeric tongue**)<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-UOLMK-5)</sup> is the language spoken by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The language is derived from **Ald Chimeris**, which was spoken by their [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) ancestors from the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Ald Chimeris is similar to [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md) and descended from [Ehlnofex](Lore_Ehlnofex.md).<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-Land War Poetry-6)</sup> The [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md) speak in a dialect called **Ashland**.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Yakub Hair-7)</sup>

The language is usually written in the [Daedric alphabet](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md), but also possesses its own [Dark Elf script](Lore_Dark_Elf_Script.md). See other [Elvish languages](Lore_Linguistics.md#Common_Words) for possible cognates.
