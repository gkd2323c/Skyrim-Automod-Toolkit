[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg/200px-ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg) Bisnensel-By-The-Lake Bisnensel is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md), in the middle of [Halcyon Lake](Lore_Halcyon_Lake.md). It was founded by Ayleid refugees from [Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md) between [1E 375](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_375) and [1E 452](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_452)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-2)</sup> under the rule of [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md). Bisnensel was built partly above and partly below the surface, although only the subterranean parts have survived.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BOAR-3)</sup>

Between [1E 453](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_453) and [1E 460](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_460), a cult of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) formed under the leadership of High Priest [Uluscant](Lore_Uluscant.md), who challenged King Dynar's rule. After being subject to strange visions and murder, the royal family was driven into exile.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BOAR-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ULUSCANT-4)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a conflict emerged between the [nereid](Lore_Nereid.md) -worshipping Rain Disciples and the Hermaeus Mora-venerating Primeval Seekers. The nereid Lorelia wished to prevent Halcyon Lake from eventually desiccating, and to accomplish that she used her Rain Disciples' memories to create a water stone. The Primeval Seekers, on the other hand, tried to stop her and drive out the Rain Disciples from the ruins. It is unknown which side succeeded.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Notes
- The Ayleid name translates to "New Water Halls".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BOAR-3)</sup>
