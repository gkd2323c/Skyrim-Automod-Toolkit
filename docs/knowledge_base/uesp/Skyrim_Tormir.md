[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-npc-Tormir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tormir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tormir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tormir.jpg) Tormir at Darkwater Crossing **Tormir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) and a resident of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) who works at [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md). She is the mother of [Hrefna](Skyrim_Hrefna.md).

She wears merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d), and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223b). She carries a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and some [common gold and loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

She gets up at 7am and eats a one hour breakfast at her campsite. At 8am, she then heads to the mine to get to work, only stopping to eat in the mine at noon. At 4pm, she's done for the day in the mine, so she heads back to the campsite, eating dinner at 6pm. She then goes to bed at 11pm, in her bedroll.

## Dialogue
Tormir will talk about a variety of subjects when spoken to, the most recurring one being her daughter:

- *"Is there something I can help you with?"*
- *"What's your trouble? Dasturn telling tales again?"*
- *"We keep hearing that the [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) could come through here. I try not to worry about it."*
- *"It's tough to keep track of Hrefna. If her father wasn't such a snowback, he'd be around to help out."*
- *"I'm not proud of Hrefna's father. If she knew him, she wouldn't be either."*

She can also be asked about Darkwater Crossing itself:

- **What can you tell me about Darkwater Crossing?**
- *"Not much to it, really. We fish, and keep to ourselves. Try to keep from upsetting the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) or the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), and so far the war's left us alone."*

## Conversations
She can often be seen talking to Hrefna or her fellow miner, [Sondas Drenim](Skyrim_Sondas_Drenim.md):

- **Hrefna**: *"And Meieran thinks that we should go there."*
- **Tormir**: *"I don't want to hear another word about what you're learning from the elf! It's high time you learned to be a Nord, too!"*
- **Hrefna**: *"But mama..."*
- **Tormir**: *"He's a half-decent farmer. Ask him to teach you to farm!"*

- **Tormir**: *"Thanks for looking after Hrefna."*
- **Sondas**: *"Oh, it's no trouble. She's a smart little girl. Might talk your ear off, but she's fun."*
- **Tormir**: *"Well, I know she thinks the world of you."*
- **Sondas**: *"Well, I'll try to be a good influence, then."*

- **Tormir**: *"Sondas, does Hrefna ever mention Meieran to you?"*
- **Sondas**: *"She hardly ever mentions anything else."*
- **Tormir**: *"I'm just worried about what he's teaching her."*
- **Sondas**: *"I wouldn't worry about that. He's a good elf. I trust him."*
- **Tormir**: *"You're probably right."*
