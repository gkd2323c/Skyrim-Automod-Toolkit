Anaxes is a [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md) who serves as an agent of the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) [Kathutet](Lore_Kathutet.md). He bears some relation to another Xivilai named Medrike.

Like Kathutet, Anaxes once served [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) as a torturer and guard. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he and Medrike were sent to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) by Bal to defend a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md). However, they were swiftly banished by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and the Anchor was released.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MB-2)</sup> At that time, Anaxes was known to wield a warhammer known as the Hammer of Anaxes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOA-3)</sup> Sometime after this, Anaxes, Medrike, and Kathutet came into the service of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md).

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Kathutet was ordered by Dagon to serve [Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md) in [Gaiar Alata](Lore_Gaiar_Alata.md). He was charged with keeping order in the Savage Garden, where the souls of "unmortal" [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) members took part in ceaseless combat to prove themselves. Anaxes was appointed Lord of the Savage Garden, and became feared by the cultists as the worst of their tormentors. The cultists eventually succeeded in trapping him in a cave. Although Kathutet regarded the humiliation of his imprisonment as a fitting punishment, Anaxes' shame made a mockery of Kathutet's authority. Seeing the matter as too insignificant for his own attention, Kathutet attempted to bring the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) into his service in order to free the Xivilai. The outcome of this proposition is unknown, and Gaiar Alata was destroyed shortly afterward.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
