Overheard at the Wailing Hag Inn *Two Nords discuss their encounter with a Wamasu*
---
[![ON-concept-wamasu.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-concept-wamasu.png/530px-ON-concept-wamasu.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-wamasu.png)
- *Logodvild*: It was big as a horse, no lie! Right, Ogondar?

- *Ogondar*: Two horses if you ask me, two big stallions—even three!

- *Logodvild*: Aye! [Shor](Lore_Shor.md)'s bones, I'll never forget the fright when it reared up from the mud and took a snap at my leg! All a-crackle, and that roar—enough to chill the souls in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). I never saw a beast so terrible! What'd they call it?

- *Ogondar*: [Wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md)! Should've named it "Death-lizard" if you ask me. If I hadn't been there, you'd be dead for sure. Don't you owe me another round?

- *Logodvild*: Barkeep! More ale!
