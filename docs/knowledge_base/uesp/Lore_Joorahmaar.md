[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg) Joorahmaar together with Paarbahlot and Laatvulon Joorahmaar is a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Mortal Hunter Terror" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shout-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-A-3)</sup>

Little is known of Joorahmaar's past. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), together with [Paarbahlot](Lore_Paarbahlot.md), he ventured to the New Moon Fortress in [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md) to meet with [Laatvulon](Lore_Laatvulon.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Paarbahlot-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Joorahmaar-5)</sup> During the meeting, Laatvulon promised power to both Joorahmaar and Paarbahlot,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OONM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Laatvulon-7)</sup> and ordered the recruits of the [Order of the New Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_New_Moon.md) to offer them their power with the use of the [aeonstone](Lore_Aeonstone.md). The ritual was interrupted by the members of [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md)'s reformed [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md). Following the disruption of the ritual, Paarbahlot, together with Joorahmaar, left the fortress of the New Moon Cult.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OONM-6)</sup>

Although Paarbahlot's allegiances and status as of then were unknown, Joorahmaar was later seen assisting Laatvulon in the [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md). He was ordering the cultists of the New Moon to continue to feed the ritual sites for Laatvulon. [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) wounded the dragon and later a [member](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the Dragonguard killed him.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TDA-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AT-9)</sup> As his soul was not devoured by another dovah, Joorahmaar was not killed permanently and can be raised again.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LAD-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Arngeir-11)</sup>

The data about the dragon Joorahmaar, documented within [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) was shrouded in ambiguity. Various accounts describe conflicting details about the dragon's abilities, with some stating that Joorahmaar breathed fire, while others claim that he emitted clouds of poisonous smoke.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MJ-12)</sup>
