# Chert

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/OB-place-Kingscrest_Cavern.jpg/200px-OB-place-Kingscrest_Cavern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Kingscrest_Cavern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Kingscrest_Cavern.jpg) Kingscrest Cavern Chert is a type of rock. It was mined by [Imperials](Lore_Imperials.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Kingscrest Cavern, located north of Cheydinhal in the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md), was a known source of chert and [flint](Lore_Flint.md), occasionally utilized by the vassals of [House Tharn](Lore_House_Tharn.md). By the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), it had been abandoned and taken over by wild creatures, a state it remained in during [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433). A specific part of the cavern was referred to as the Chert Galleries.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup>
