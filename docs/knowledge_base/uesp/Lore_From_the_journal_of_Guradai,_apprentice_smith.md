From the journal of Guradai, apprentice smith **by [Guradai](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Guradai)** *An Orcish smith's thoughts on apprenticing under Redguards*
---
[![ON-concept-Redguard smithy.png](https://images.uesp.net/8/8d/ON-concept-Redguard_smithy.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Redguard_smithy.png)
- Maybe it wasn't so stupid to come here, after all. Mother thought I was crazy, an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) apprenticing among [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), but I'll prove her wrong. We have so much we can learn from each other, even though we have our differences.

- Both of our cultures have respect for forging fine weapons and armor, we're both proud and stubborn, and even though our styles are different, we value skill in combat. I have already learned new things about forging swords I never even thought of from Master Romasad, and there is much more to learn!
