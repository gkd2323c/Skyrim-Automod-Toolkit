[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-creature-Drokt.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Drokt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drokt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Drokt.jpg) Drokt's skeletal remains and his journal beside him. **Drokt** is a named skeleton that can be found in the [Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md). His [journal](Skyrim_Journal_of_Drokt.md) can be found near his body. It recounts how Drokt became obsessed with the Oculory, and how he died without aligning it correctly. It also aids the reader in solving the Oculory puzzle and obtaining the [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md).
