[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-place-Crowstooth%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Crowstooth%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crowstooth%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crowstooth%27s_Camp.jpg) Crowstooth's Camp **Crowstooth's Camp** is a small [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) encampment southwest of [Darklight Tower](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower.md), and west of the [Ruins of Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund).

It is a bare bones affair. Tents have been set up, but the grass has not been cleared; there is no storage, crafting station, resting place, or furniture. It is occupied by [Crowstooth's](Skyrim_Crowstooth.md) forces, who were separated from her prior to the events of [Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md).

## Walkthrough
The camp is most easily reached by traveling by way of [Darklight Tower](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower.md) from the ground. The exit of the tower opens out onto the same continuous mountainside area as both the camp and the [Ruins of Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund). From here, there is a path to left leading to Rkund and to the right ending at the camp.

On the southeast side of the camp is a fallen tree. Next to the tree is a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) skill book *[Proper Lock Design and Construction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proper_Lock_Design)*.

Once these forces are defeated, Crowstooth's second-in-command, [Linelle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle), must be looted for [her note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linelle%27s_Note), which has information on where Crowstooth has gone.

## Notes
- The enemies here, including Linelle the Hagraven, will respawn after the quest is completed. Linelle's note does not respawn.
- The lockpicking skill book, *Proper Lock Design and Construction*, is the same one as mentioned at [Darklight Tower](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower.md). The camp makes it easier to locate, but won't be present until you start the related quest.
