## Official Summary
*Available now at the Fletcher in Solitude, eight crossbows made from the finest Tamrielic materials! Choose from simple but elegant crossbows of Imperial, Orcish, Silver, or Nordic design, or high grade versions of Glass, Stalhrim, Daedric and Dragonbone! Purchase these finely assembled weapons at the Fletcher, or acquire one and learn to craft them yourself! Created by Borja "Borx25" Franco.*

## Notes
- The [Stalhrim Crossbow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Crossbow) requires the completion of the quest [A New Source of Stalhrim](Skyrim_A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.md) to be crafted.
- The Enhanced versions aren't sold by merchants. They can only be crafted after first purchasing the specific crossbow from the Fletcher shop.
- Unlike the [Crossbows](Skyrim_Weapons.md#Crossbows) added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), these can be crafted at any regular [Forge](Skyrim_Forge.md).
- The Enhanced versions have an additional property: *Attacks with this crossbow ignore 50% of armor.* This effect is not considered an enchantment, so enhanced crossbows can be enchanted like any normal unenchanted weapon, though they will only display the text for this effect and not for any enchantment that you may place on it.
