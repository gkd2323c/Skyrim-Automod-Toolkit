[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-npc-Arnskar_Ember-Master.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arnskar_Ember-Master.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnskar_Ember-Master.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arnskar_Ember-Master.jpg) Arnskar Ember-Master **Arnskar Ember-Master** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) in the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He moves into the area after you complete three special high-profile jobs for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md). Despite his connection with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), he is not a fence. He sells weapons and armor as well as the materials to make them. He focuses mostly on melee weapons over bows or armor and carries 1000 gold which is replenished daily.

There is a usable [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) and [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md), as well as a counter with a few stealable weapons inside his shop.

Arnskar spends all his time serving his store he has set up in the Ragged Flagon. He will spend much of his time working at the various facilities inside his shop. However, he will occasionally take a break and just stand around or will sit down and dip his feet in the water to cool off. The only time he will not offer you his services is when you have offended the Thieves Guild by breaking one of their rules. He will not offer you his services until you have completed the [Reparations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reparations) quest to get back on good terms with the Guild.

Arnskar wears a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff37) along with a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69e). He carries around a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), which can be up to [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality, and a small amount of gold.

His normal greetings:

You found it * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *."* *"Need your weapon repaired? Or could I interest you in a new one?"* When you have offended the guild, he will not want your business and will only remark:

*"You're in it deep... better start shoveling your way out."*
