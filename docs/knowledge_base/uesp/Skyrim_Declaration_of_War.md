Declaration of War **by [General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md)**
---
[Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) Stronghold,

My calls for the unconditional surrender of your forces and an immediate cessation of all hostilities has *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* been ignored numerous times. I therefore have no choice but to assume your purpose on Solstheim is hostile, and to treat Raven Rock Stronghold as an enemy of the Empire. I warn you, any attempt to breach [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md) will be met with an equal level of aggression. I will do everything in my power to wipe you and your forces off the face of Tamriel. There will be no further communications between us.

[General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md)
Garrison Commander, Fort Frostmoth
