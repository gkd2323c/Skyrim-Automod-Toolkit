[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/OBR-place-Harlun%27s_Watch.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Harlun%27s_Watch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Harlun%27s_Watch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Harlun%27s_Watch.jpg) Harlun's Watch circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Harlun's Watch is a settlement located in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) to the south of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), in the [Cheydinhal Foothills](Lore_Cheydinhal_Foothills.md) region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The settlement has existed as a homestead since sometime prior to [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), but by the time of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) it had been abandoned by its residents. At some point in time, freebooters from [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) occupied its ruins before being wiped out by the Dusksabers, an elite mercenary group of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) seeking the same [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) treasures as they were. A guard of Cheydinhal's Great Chapel of Arkay investigated the incident and interviewed one of the survivors before leaving for [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AROTD-3)</sup>

Sometime later, Harlun's Watch was occupied by [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) of the [Bloody Hand Tribe](Lore_Bloody_Hand_Tribe.md), led by their chief known as Wither Tooth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> The nearby military fortification of **Harlun's Outpost** was located to the south of the abandoned settlement. Initially located within the territory held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), the outpost changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), life in the village had been relatively peaceful until the villagers began to see mysterious lights coming from the nearby Swampy Cave. Some villagers went to investigate these lights, but they never returned. The local [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) were hired to investigate, and it was discovered that the villagers had been lured to the cave by [will-o-the-wisps](Lore_Will-o-the-Wisp.md) before being killed by the [swamp trolls](Lore_Swamp_Troll.md) that inhabited it.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Events-5)</sup>
