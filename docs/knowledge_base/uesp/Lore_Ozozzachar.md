*Ozzozachar, my son! I summon you from the skies!*
—Molag Bal<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MB-1)</sup> Ozozzachar is a [Daedric Titan](Lore_Daedric_Titan.md) in service of [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ozozzachar-2)</sup> He is the favored titan and a general in the Prince's armies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Anchor G-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OD-4)</sup> Molag Bal referred to him as his child and son,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MB-1)</sup> but the true nature of their relationship remains unclear, as such bonds for Daedra differ from those of mortals.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ETLR-5)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/ON-place-Alcaire_Dolmen.jpg/200px-ON-place-Alcaire_Dolmen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alcaire_Dolmen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alcaire_Dolmen.jpg) A Dark Anchor Daedric Titans in Molag Bal's service were created as imitations of the [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), specifically [Boziikkodstrun](Lore_Boziikkodstrun.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DDTT-6)</sup> It is unknown whether Ozozzachar's status as Molag Bal's son meant his origins were different from the others.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MB-1)</sup>

During the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), Ozozzachar was Molag Bal's most favored Titan, and served as one of his generals.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Anchor G-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OD-4)</sup> At some point he was recalled to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), and in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he was transported back to Tamriel to protect a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md). Molag Bal summoned him from the skies to vanquish his enemies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MB-1)</sup> However, he was quickly banished by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and the Anchor was lifted.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ozozzachar-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DA-7)</sup> Two weapons were associated with him: Ozzozachar's Claw, a two-handed sword, and Ozzozachar's Tooth, a dagger. Both were recovered after his defeat.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tooth-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Claw-9)</sup> Little is known about what happened to him afterward.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ozozzachar-2)</sup>
