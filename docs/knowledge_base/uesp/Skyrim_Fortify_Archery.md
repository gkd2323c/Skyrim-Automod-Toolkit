*Alchemy description:* **Bows do <mag>% more damage for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Bows do <mag>% more damage.**

**Fortify Archery** (a.k.a. **Fortify Marksman**) increases the damage of [bows and crossbows](Skyrim_Archery.md).

## Alchemy
The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a potion of Fortify Marksman:

- [Angelfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Angelfish)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Canis Root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root)
- [Corkbulb Root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corkbulb_Root)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**1.25** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.27** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))
- [Elves Ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md)
- [Juniper Berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries)
- [Spider Egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg)

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Archery if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Bracers/Gauntlets, Circlets/Helmets, Necklaces, and Rings *[of Archery](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Archery)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Archery*, *of Major Archery*, *of Extreme Archery*, *of Eminent Archery*, and *of Peerless Archery.*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Ancient Shrouded Cowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Shrouded_Cowl)
- [Gauntlets of the Old Gods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets_of_the_Old_Gods)
- [Krosis](Skyrim_Krosis_(item).md)
- [Kyne's Token](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Token)
- [Shrouded Cowl Maskless](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Cowl_Maskless)
- [Shrouded Cowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Cowl)

[Linwe's Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe%27s_Hood) can technically be disenchanted, allowing you to learn a special version of Fortify Archery named **Shadowsight**. However, Shadowsight cannot be used to enchant any items because no enchanting slots are enabled for the effect.

## Items to Enchant
The Fortify Archery effect can be applied to the following items:

- Circlets, Hats, Hoods, and Helmets
- Bracers, Gauntlets, and Gloves
- Necklaces
- Rings

## Blessings
- The [Blessing of Auriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Auriel)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> provides 10% Fortify Marksman for 12 hours.
- The [Elytra's Fierce Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elytra%27s_Fierce_Aura)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup> provides +10 Fortify Marksman for 5 minutes.

## Food
The following food items provide a temporary version of this effect:

- [Apple Dumplings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Dumpling)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> 5 pts for 60 secs

## Notes
- The [Call to Arms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Call_to_Arms) spell includes a secondary Fortify Marksman effect, which directly increases the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill of allies.

## Bugs
- Fortify Archery potions affect all types of weapons and unarmed damage. A possible potion combining Fortify Archery and Fortify One-handed therefore doubles the boost for One-handed weapons. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
