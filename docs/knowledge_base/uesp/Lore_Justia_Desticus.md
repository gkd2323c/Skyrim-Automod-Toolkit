Justia Desticus was the original First Sword of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOJD-1)</sup> a position given to the chief warrior of the order.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBJ-2)</sup> When [Cavor Merula](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md) re-established the Order of the Hour in [2E 432](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_432),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OOTH-3)</sup> she stood by his side and devised the Rigors of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), eight traits to which all members of the Order must aspire. She was considered the second in devotion in Akatosh's eyes.

A statue to Justia was later made in Knightsgrave to her memory as one of the Order's founding members.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KLL-4)</sup> [Lyra Viria](Lore_The_Black_Dragon.md) would later take the mantle of First Sword, and felt a connection to Justia when she traveled to Knightsgrave, curious if she would be proud of her taking up her role.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TBJP2-5)</sup>
