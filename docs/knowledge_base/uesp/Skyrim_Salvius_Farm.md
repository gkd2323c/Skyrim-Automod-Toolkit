[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-place-Salvius_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Salvius_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Salvius_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Salvius_Farm.jpg) Salvius Farm **Salvius Farm** is a small farm east of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). [Vigdis Salvius](Skyrim_Vigdis_Salvius.md) and [Rogatus Salvius](Skyrim_Rogatus_Salvius.md) tend the farm and live in the Farmhouse.

The farm itself contains 17 [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants and three [chicken nests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg). Three [rock warbler eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) can be found around the walls of the farm and many [juniper berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries) can be harvested nearby.

Rogatus will ask you to deliver a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rogatus%27s_Letter) for him, while [Vigdis](Skyrim_Vigdis_Salvius.md) runs the farm and will pay for any harvested potatoes.

## Notes
- Just to the east of the farm by the signpost adjacent to the bridge is the camping site for [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad)'s [caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).
