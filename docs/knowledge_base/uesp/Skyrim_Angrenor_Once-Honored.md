[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Angrenor_Once-Honored.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Angrenor_Once-Honored.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Angrenor_Once-Honored.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Angrenor_Once-Honored.jpg) Angrenor Once-Honored [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Rolff_Stone-Fist_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rolff_Stone-Fist_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rolff_Stone-Fist_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rolff_Stone-Fist_02.jpg) Angrenor and Rolff bullying Suvaris Atheron **Angrenor Once-Honored** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) who was a former member of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). Upon raising his disposition by giving him a coin, he will become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

He can be found relaxing in [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) from 4am to 10am. He then goes to the Stone Quarter, leaning against a wall. At 4pm he patrols Windhelm for twelve hours, then returns to Candlehearth Hall.

Unlike most beggars, he wears a set of prison clothes: a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) with [footwraps](Skyrim_Footwraps.md). He carries an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) and a selection of [lower-class items](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"I was one of the best soldiers in the Stormcloak army, until I took a sword through the chest."*
- *"I once killed six men, single-handed, while rescuing my brothers-in-arms from ambush by an Imperial patrol."*
- *"I'd be out there fighting the Imperials right now if it wasn't for my war-wound."*
- *"Ever since that Imperial craven ran me through from behind, I've had trouble breathing. Don't have any trouble drinking though."*
- *"I'm not too proud to admit that I need a bit of help from time to time."*

### Conversations
Upon entering [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) for the first time, you will witness Angrenor and his counterpart [Rolff Stone-Fist](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rolff_Stone-Fist) harassing a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [woman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron) and accusing her of being an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) spy, making no attempt to conceal their disdain for elves:

- **Rolff**: *"You come here where you're not wanted, you eat our food, you pollute our city with your stink and you refuse to help the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md)."*
- **Suvaris**: *"But we haven't taken a side because it's not our fight."*
- **Angrenor**: *"Hey, maybe the reason these gray-skins don't help in the war is because they're Imperial spies!"*
- **Suvaris**: *"Imperial spies? You can't be serious!"*
- **Rolff**: *"Maybe we'll pay you a visit tonight, little spy. We got ways of finding out what you really are."*

## Unused Dialogue
He was meant to have more dialogue once he moved into the [Aretino Residence](Skyrim_Aretino_Residence.md), which will never be heard due to a [bug](#Bugs). He would have defended his claim on the house to you, saying:

- *"What? Were you hoping to claim this house for yourself? Well, the Aretinos always liked me, so I think I'm family enough to take up here."*

Angrenor also would have referenced Innocence Lost, saying:

- *"Rumor holds that the Aretino boy brought an assassin here. Sounds like nonsense to me."*

He would also have disparaged his fellow Windhelm residents, saying:

- *"The milk drinkers in this town wouldn't last five minutes in a real battle."*

## Notes
- He is the only beggar who is able to be married in the game.

- After completing the quest [Innocence Lost](Skyrim_Innocence_Lost.md) he carries around a key to the [Aretino Residence](Skyrim_Aretino_Residence.md). He should have moved into the house after the quest, but the scripting is incomplete. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- Once married, Angrenor will still beg for money, even if he is living at your home. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- If their conversation with Suvaris is interrupted by combat (e.g. by a dragon or vampire attack), Rolff and Angrenor will be stuck standing at the main gate permanently. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed by using the command `startscene CD91F`.
