# Kamal (place)

| --- | --- |
| Type | Province |
| Continent | [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md) |
| Demonym(s) | Kamal, Kamali |

Kamal (meaning **Snow Hell**) is a nation in northern [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM18AS-1)</sup> It is inhabited by armies of snow demons (also known as [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(race).md)), who thaw out every summer and attempt to invade [Tang Mo](Lore_Tang_Mo_(place).md), but are always successfully repelled by the [monkey-folk](Lore_Tang_Mo_(race).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> [Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ada%27Soom_Dir-Kamal), a king among the demons, once invaded [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), but was defeated circa [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), when [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), warrior-poet god of the Dunmer, flooded the entirety of Morrowind, successfully drowning the invasion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Kamal is known to be icy and snow-covered, with deep snows and northern forests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM18AS-1)</sup>
