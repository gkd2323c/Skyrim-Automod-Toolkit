[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-npc-Gambler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gambler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gambler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gambler.jpg) A Gambler Three **Gamblers** are found in [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md), a cave used as both a [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md) den and an arena for animal fights. All three gamblers wear farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and have a 50% chance of wearing a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md). They all carry between 25 and 125 gold. One gambler is found near the bar, while the other two are near the fighting ring, watching the pit wolves fight. They are hostile to you on sight, and attack with their fists.
