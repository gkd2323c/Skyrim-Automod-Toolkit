[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-npc-Assassin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Assassin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Assassin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Assassin.jpg) A female Khajiit Assassin **Assassins** of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) are occasionally sent to track and subsequently make an attempt on your life throughout the game. The assassin's race and gender will vary between an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) female, a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) male, or a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) female.

The assassins appear as [random encounters](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin_Sent_to_Kill_the_Player) starting at level 5. Unlike [hired thugs](Skyrim_Hired_Thug.md), the assassins appear for no reason and there is no way to avoid encountering them. A [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin%27s_Note) detailing their mission can always be found on their bodies, though it is never revealed who performed the [Black Sacrament](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Sacrament) to order the assassination.

After completing either the [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) or the [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) quest, you are no longer in danger of being attacked by these assassins.

## Notes
- [Shrouded Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Armor) cannot be collected from slain assassins.
