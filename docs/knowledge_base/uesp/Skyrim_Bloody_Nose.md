1. Defeat [Hofgrir Horse-Crusher](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md) in an unarmed [brawl](Skyrim_Brawl.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Meeting Hofgrir
Meet [Holfgrir Horse-Crusher](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md) at the [Riften Stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md), just to the north of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). Talk to him about the dangers of living outside the walls, which will lead to him boasting about being the strongest brawler in Skyrim. Make a 100 gold bet with him that you can beat him. This [brawl](Skyrim_Brawl.md) is like any other, and the usual rules apply: no weapons, and no offensive magic. Beware, as he is much tougher than most other brawlers.

### Claiming your reward
After winning the brawl, talk to Holfgrir and he will give you your 100 gold back, as well as another 100 gold for winning the bet.

## Notes
- Hofgrir brawls like other NPCs, though the one difference is that he does a lot more damage per hit than other characters. This isn't quite as notable on the lesser difficulties (Novice, Apprentice, Adept), but it is very notable on the higher ones (Expert, Master and especially Legendary). If you are a lower level/health or are wearing clothes instead of armor when doing this quest on those higher difficulties, it would be viable to heal during the encounter or you'll be swiftly beaten.

## Bugs
- If you don't have 200 gold when you attempt the brawl the quest may be uncompletable. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed using `setstage Freeform Riften19 200` followed by `resetquest Freeform Riften19`.
