[3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Rotheran is a stronghold on the south coast of [Sheogorad](Lore_Sheogorad.md) island, in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Despite how remote and secluded it is, these ruins have been occupied throughout the eras, and it is a place with an evil reputation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWLalatia-1)</sup> The ruin's [propylon chamber](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) links to [Indoranyon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Indoranyon) and Valenvaryon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWIndoranyon-2)</sup>

Rotheran, like the other Chimer strongholds, was built to defend Morrowind from [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) raiders that predated the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md)' formation. They were ultimately abandoned after the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) and the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) was created.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TMPGOTY-3)</sup><sup>:236</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the prominent noble, Ulasa Redoran, occupied Rotheran.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SBBAON-4)</sup>

In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Rotheran was taken over by a powerful [sorcerer](Lore_Sorcerers.md) and [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) worshipper named Llaren Terano. With his followers, he ran a gladiatorial arena that pitted [slaves](Lore_Slave.md) against [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and animals.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWRotheran-5)</sup> In his possession was the [Ice Blade of the Monarch](Lore_Ice_Blade_of_the_Monarch.md). The [Imperial Cult](Lore_Imperial_Cult.md) sent Adusamsi Assurnarairan to retrieve it, but she was caught and stripped of her belongings before being imprisoned in Rotheran's arena. The cult oracle, Lalatia Varian, sent an [acolyte](Lore_Nerevarine.md) to rescue Adusamsi and take the blade. In the end, Llaren Terano was slain, and the sword was recovered.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWBlade-6)</sup> Whatever happened to Terano's followers and the Rotheran arena is unknown.
