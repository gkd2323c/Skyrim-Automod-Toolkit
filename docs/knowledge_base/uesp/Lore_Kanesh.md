| --- | --- |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Kanesh Kanesh is a large island in the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago, between the [Azurian Sea](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azurian_Sea) and the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls), and the closest of the group to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> Located due east of [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md), it is a harsh volcanic island.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLIOOY-2)</sup> To its south are the islands of [Irranhu](Lore_Irranhu.md) and [Samara](Lore_Samara.md).

In the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), there was a settlement named Lumut on the east side of the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup>
