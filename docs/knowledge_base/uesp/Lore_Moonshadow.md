[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-furnishing-Crimson_Shard_of_Moonshadow.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Crimson_Shard_of_Moonshadow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Crimson_Shard_of_Moonshadow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Crimson_Shard_of_Moonshadow.jpg) A Crimson Shard of Moonshadow *Too much beauty [...] I am half blind. I see flowers and waterfalls, majestic trees, a city of silver, but it is all a blur. The colors run like water. It's raining now, and the wind smells like perfume. This surely is Moonshadow, where Azura dwells.*
—Morian Zenas, from Seif-ij Hidja's *[The Doors of Oblivion](Lore_The_Doors_of_Oblivion.md)* Moonshadow is a realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) created and ruled over by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), Queen of Dawn and Dusk.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDOO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MH-2)</sup> The realm is said to hold "too much beauty", so much so it can render mortal visitors "half-blind". It contains flowers, waterfalls, pink trees,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RF-3)</sup> and a city of silver. Azura herself resides in a rose palace, and is welcoming to mortal travelers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDOO-1)</sup>

To the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), Moonshadow is where [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) tends the Gates of the Crossing—the bridge between Nirni and the afterlife beyond.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSR-5)</sup> It is there where they are guided to the [Sands Behind the Stars](Lore_Sands_Behind_the_Stars.md) if they are worthy.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Book-6)</sup><sup>:152</sup>

According to myth, as Azurah wept in the [Great Darkness](Lore_Void.md) after the death of her [mother](Lore_Fadomai.md), the light from her tears chased away the Darkness and eventually left a "place of moonlight and shadow".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TFDOF-7)</sup> Khajiiti mythology also writes that when [Magrus](Lore_Magrus.md) fled from [Boethra](Lore_Boethra.md) and [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md) after the former had plucked his eye out,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TWS-8)</sup> he fell into the Moonshadow. There, Azurah judged him as too fearful to rule a sphere, and tore out his other eye. The blinded Magrus fled to the [heavens](Lore_Aetherius.md), and Azurah made of his eye a "stone to reflect the [Varliance Gate](Lore_Varliance_Gate.md)".<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSS-9)</sup>

During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) inhabitants of [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md) had come into possession of several Crimson Shards of Moonshadow, large red crystals which glow with a powerful red light.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Rose foundation is made from the pink petals of flowering trees in the realm and imported to Tamriel.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RF-3)</sup> Several pieces of armor of Daedric quality found in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) are thought to have originated from Moonshadow; they are enchanted with a strong magical light enchantment, but are otherwise unremarkable.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBArtifacts-11)</sup>

## History
During [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md) and [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md)'s experiments to travel to Oblivion between [2E 418](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_418) to [2E 431](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_431), they managed to figure out a ritual to open a gate to Oblivion. Zenas decided to try going to Moonshadow as it was a safe option and visited the realm,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-CM:8-12)</sup> but then went on to various other realms before settling in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDOO-1)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-CM:10-13)</sup> He described Moonshadow to his assistant, [Seif-ij Hidja](Lore_Seif-ij_Hidja.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-CM:14-14)</sup>

A rift to Moonshadow opened within Scrivener's Hall on 2nd Hearthfire, [2E 571](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_571), and closed two years later on 25th Evening Star, [2E 573](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_573).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-MRT-15)</sup>

Following [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)'s indecent acts toward Azura at Hogithum Hall in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), Azura had made the regular gateways to the realm inaccessible by [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ICDL-16)</sup>

## Notes
- "Moonshadow" is also another name for Azura.
- Mortals born under the sign of [The Shadow](Lore_The_Shadow.md) are known to have the ability to turn invisible. This ability is called "Moonshadow", although it is unclear if there is any connection to Azura's realm.
- In a speech to the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md), [Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md) incorrectly names [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md)'s [realms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mephala%27s_Realms) the "ten Moonshadows".<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-OBMankar-17)</sup>
