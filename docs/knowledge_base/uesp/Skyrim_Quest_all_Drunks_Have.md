## Walkthrough
If you talk to one of the quest givers while having alcohol in your inventory, you will have the option to share a drink with them. This then makes them your friend, meaning that you can take low value items that belong to them and that they may occasionally give you a gift.

## Notes
- This quest does not have any stages or objectives.
- This quest can be repeated indefinitely.
- Certain expensive drinks will not give you the option to share a drink. - Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) partially fixes this bug.
- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) quest for the quest giver's hold.
- Because this quest is unmarked, completing it won't help with progress towards the "[Hero of the People](Skyrim_Hero_of_the_People.md)" [Achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md).
- Only drunks with the "[Male Drunk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Voice-Male Drunk)" voicetype can be quest givers for this quest.
