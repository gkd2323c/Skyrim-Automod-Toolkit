# Night Knives

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/ON-furnishing-Trifold_Mirror_of_Alternatives.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Trifold_Mirror_of_Alternatives.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Trifold_Mirror_of_Alternatives.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Trifold_Mirror_of_Alternatives.jpg) Trifold Mirror of Alternatives, rimmed with umbral frames The Night Knives were a minor cult of [Namira](Lore_Namira.md) worshipers headquartered in Western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) sometime during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). They were known to have techniques in rimming their tables with umbral frames; shadows made up of coherence energies specific to Namira and/or [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMA-1)</sup>
