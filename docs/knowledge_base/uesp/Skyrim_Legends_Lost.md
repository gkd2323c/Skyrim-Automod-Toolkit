## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to [New Gnisis Cornerclub](Skyrim_New_Gnisis_Cornerclub.md) and read the *[Lost Caravan Guard's Note](Skyrim_Lost_Caravan_Guard%27s_Note.md)*.
2. Locate the lost caravan north of [Bronze Water Cave](Skyrim_Bronze_Water_Cave.md) and read the *[Carriage Driver's Note](Skyrim_Carriage_Driver%27s_Note.md)*.
3. Locate the lost caravan east of [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md) and read the *[Seedy Guard's Note](Skyrim_Seedy_Guard%27s_Note.md)*.
4. Enter [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)'s [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun).md) and read the *[Caravan Captain's Note](Skyrim_Caravan_Captain%27s_Note.md)*.
5. Locate the final caravan northwest of [Peak's Shade Tower](Skyrim_Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.md) and read *[Velyne's Letter](Skyrim_Velyne%27s_Letter.md)*.
6. Loot the Attunement Crystal and journey to [Sightless Pit](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md).
7. Explore Sightless Pit and use the Attunement Crystal to unlock the entrance to the Sightless Vault near the dungeon exit.
8. Solve the steam valve puzzles and defeat [the Messenger](Skyrim_The_Messenger.md) to claim [Sunder](Skyrim_Sunder.md) and [Wraithguard](Skyrim_Wraithguard.md) as your own.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Letters from the Red Year
Upon installing the [Sunder & Wraithguard](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), you will be directed to the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](Skyrim_New_Gnisis_Cornerclub.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) in relation to a trove of old refugee letters from the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) which have recently been uncovered. On the bar top is a *[Lost Caravan Guard's Note](Skyrim_Lost_Caravan_Guard%27s_Note.md)*, which can be freely taken. Reading this note will reveal that a highly secretive smuggling operation had been organized to transport some unknown cargo through Skyrim, using the refugees fleeing [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) as cover. Several decoy caravans were employed to transport this cargo, each with a code name based on the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of Morrowind.

The author of this letter was part of the Telvanni caravan, and was reportedly carrying the cargo towards the [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md). The destroyed remains of this caravan are half-buried in snow along the road between Windhelm and the inn, just north of the entrance to [Bronze Water Cave](Skyrim_Bronze_Water_Cave.md). Several destroyed [Dwarven automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) surround the carriage, along with the skeletons of the unfortunate caravaneers. Unfortunately, the elusive cargo is nowhere to be found, although there is a *[Carriage Driver's Note](Skyrim_Carriage_Driver%27s_Note.md)* in an "Old Caravan Chest" at the wreckage. The author of this note complains of attacks from Dwarven automatons as well as "singing" coming from the cargo. It also reveals that the items were handed off to the Sadras caravan, which was heading for [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

(If [Saints and Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> is also installed, the first caravan is located right next to [Svarig's](Skyrim_Svarig.md) Dark Seducer camp and can be discovered before starting this quest. Reading the Carriage Driver's Note without going to the cornerclub will start the quest and direct you to the next caravan with no context.)

The next ruined caravan is just off the road east of [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md). You will be greeted by a familiar scene of Dwarven machines and skeletal corpses. One such skeleton can be found holding the *[Seedy Guard's Note](Skyrim_Seedy_Guard%27s_Note.md)*, which reveals that the cargo had been handed over to the Indoril caravan and that its captain was planning to stop by the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Whiterun).md) in Whiterun. Be sure to loot the nearby Old Caravan Chest for valuables, then make your way to Whiterun.

### The Plot Thickens
You will need to enter the Whiterun Catacombs once you arrive at the Hall of the Dead. This area is infested with low level undead [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). Down the first set of stairs, you will find the *[Caravan Captain's Note](Skyrim_Caravan_Captain%27s_Note.md)* atop a low-lying table. According to the note, the surviving caravans took a detour towards [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) to avoid any further Dwemer attacks. You will find their remains at a ruined campsite in the woods northwest of [Peak's Shade Tower](Skyrim_Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.md). One of the skeletons is in fact the body of Velyne, a member of the Hlaalu caravan and seemingly one of the masterminds behind this venture. *[Velyne's Letter](Skyrim_Velyne%27s_Letter.md)* at last reveals that the secretive cargo is actually [Sunder](Skyrim_Sunder.md) and [Wraithguard](Skyrim_Wraithguard.md), and that the Dwemer automaton attacks are the result of a fail-safe system implemented by [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md) to prevent his Tools from ever leaving Morrowind. The Dwemer created a Tamriel-wide network of drones which would track down and retrieve the Tools if they were taken out of Morrowind, and would then deposit them in a vault until a Tonal Architect could come and retrieve them. Seeking to put a stop to the attacks, some of the caravan captains betrayed their employers and handed Sunder and Wraithguard over to the drones. Velyne learned of this, and managed to track the items to a vault located in [Sightless Pit](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md). She obtained a Attunement Crystal to unseal this vault, but evidently died before an expedition could ever be organized. Take the crystal from her skeleton and be sure to loot the Old Caravan Chest before you leave.

### Hidden Depository
Sightless Pit is a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) -infested dungeon in the snowy wastes southwest of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). Unlike most dungeons in the game, the entrance to Sightless Pit is one-way, meaning you will need to fight your way through the entire complex in order to escape. See the [dungeon walkthrough](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md#Walkthrough) for more information. Your goal is to reach the final zone, [Abandoned Cave](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md#Abandoned_Cave). A new door has been added by this Creation, to the right of the lift as you enter the zone. Place the Attunement Crystal on the Dwarven Pedestal to unseal the entrance to the Sightless Vault. This is a new area added by the Creation and consists of a large central room with two side areas. Sunder and Wraithguard are locked behind gates in the center of the room and cannot be reached.

To begin unlocking the gates, you must first activate a Vault Release Valve at the back of the room. You must then solve two steam valve puzzles in the two side rooms, which will involve activating each valve several times until the steam travels in the right direction. Your goal is to direct the steam in the direction of the next valve, until you reach the end of the piping and your journal updates. You will need to fight two [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) and two [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) respectively after you solve each room. With this done, the door near the original Vault Release Valve will open and an extremely powerful ebony-colored [Dwarven Centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) named [the Messenger](Skyrim_The_Messenger.md) will emerge.

Once you defeat this final guardian, the gates will swing open and Sunder and Wraithguard are yours to claim. On its own, Sunder does 5 points of frost, fire, and shock damage each and 15 points of damage to stamina and magicka. With Wraithguard equipped, Sunder becomes a powerful weapon, although unlike its [initial appearance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Sunder) in [*Morrowind*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind) it will not kill you if you equip the weapon without wearing Wraithguard. Wraithguard also provides 10% resistance to shock, fire, frost, magic, disease, and poison when worn. If you've already completed [Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md), you will have come into possession of [Keening](Skyrim_Keening.md), another of Kagrenac's Tools. Keening is also empowered if wielded while Wraithguard is equipped, and it can be dual wielded along with Sunder. Before you depart, make sure you loot the chest in the room where the Messenger emerged from.

## Bugs
- The Lost Caravan Guard's Note frequently falls through the top of the bar to the shelf below, making it difficult to find.
- Upon purchasing the Sunder and Wraithguard creation, the quest may not activate causing the Attunement Crystal to not spawn.
- If you read the Carriage Driver's Note then take it, the quest may not advance. You should take it first, then read it from your inventory. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If you leave the Sightless Pit without completing the vault puzzle, you may lose the Attunement Crystal and be unable to complete the quest.
- If you only obtained one item and receive the quest again, [the Messenger](Skyrim_The_Messenger.md) will not respawn even if you perform all the steps necessary to encounter it. The doors to the vault will remain inoperable and leave the quest unable to be completed.
- If you read [Velyne's Letter](Skyrim_Velyne%27s_Letter.md) before picking it up, you will be given the objective to "Read Velyne's Letter" but be unable to complete it.
- If one activates the Messenger release valve and quickly hides in one of the side rooms, the Messenger will not respond to attacks and can be killed easily
