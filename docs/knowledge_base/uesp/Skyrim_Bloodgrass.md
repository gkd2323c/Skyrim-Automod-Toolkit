[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-ingredient-Bloodgrass.png/200px-SR-ingredient-Bloodgrass.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Bloodgrass.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Bloodgrass.png) Bloodgrass **Bloodgrass** is an ingredient added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It can be harvested from **Blood Grass**, a plant native to the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md). It is the only one out of the three species of Deadlands flora that appear in Skyrim which will not cause health damage to the player in self-defense.

## Notes
- Bloodgrass previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Bloodgrass) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
