[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-ingredient-Jarrin_Root.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Jarrin_Root.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Jarrin_Root.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Jarrin_Root.jpg) Jarrin root **Jarrin Root** is given to you by [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) as part of a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest to [assassinate](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md) the Emperor (his body double). The sample from this quest is the only one available in the game.

It can be used for alchemy if you choose to kill the Emperor in some other way.

The most valuable potions in the base game all involve Jarrin Root, but as there is only ever one sample available, it is not considered or placed in lists of valuable potions. There are 19 different poisons that can be made with Jarrin Root and base game ingredients that are all more valuable than the most valuable created with [Giant's Toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md) (the most valuable farmable ingredient). With the addition of [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe) with [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) and [Boar Tusk](Skyrim_Boar_Tusk.md) with [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md), Jarrin Root is relegated to a marginal third place (it retains competitiveness with Boar Tusk through mixes with other add-on ingredients) for most expensive ingredient.

The three most valuable base-game combinations are:

- Jarrin Root + [Glow Dust](Skyrim_Glow_Dust.md) + [Ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md)
- Jarrin Root + Glow Dust + [Nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade)
- Jarrin Root + [Blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md) + [Human Heart](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md)

The first two combinations make the same poison, while the third combination notably makes the only five-effect poison available in the base game. The effects of this poison are [Damage Health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md), [Damage Magicka](Skyrim_Damage_Magicka.md), [Damage Magicka Regen](Skyrim_Damage_Magicka_Regen.md), [Damage Stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md), and [Frenzy](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md). With 100 Alchemy, the damage health effect will have over 1000 points of damage, enough to kill almost everything in the game.

## Combinations
In terms of combinations of two ingredients, Jarrin Root can be combined with 62 other ingredients. Note that these recipes have limited practical use since only one sample of Jarrin Root can be obtained through normal gameplay.

## Notes
- The Jarrin Root cannot be planted in the gardens of [player-owned houses](Skyrim_Houses.md#Hearthfire_Houses HF)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>.
- If you eat the root, you will receive 100-600 points of [poison damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poison_Damage), depending on the game's difficulty.
- With a skill level of 100 in Alchemy, all five levels of the *[Alchemist](Skyrim_Alchemist_(perk).md)* [perk](Skyrim_Perk.md), and the *[Poisoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poisoner)* perk, a Poison of Damage Health made with Jarrin Root will have a magnitude of 3000 damage. Wearing four pieces of apparel each with a 25% [Fortify Alchemy](Skyrim_Fortify_Alchemy.md) [enchantment](Skyrim_Enchanting_Effects.md) will bring this up to 6000 damage.
- If you pickpocket the Jarrin Root from Astrid and turn it into a potion before talking to her, you'll get a second sample. Don't simply pickpocket it from her, though, or it'll disappear off the ground if you drop it when she gives you the "second" root, and if you store it in a container, she won't give you another one at all.
