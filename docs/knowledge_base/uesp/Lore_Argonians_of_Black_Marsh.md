Argonians of Black Marsh *A plaque describing displayed Argonian artifacts* *
---
Although nearly as ancient as the [Mer](Lore_Mer.md), the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) are still a primitive people. Shamanistic thinking and [tree worship](Lore_Hist.md) indicates a sub-mer capacity for critical thinking. The crude craftsmanship in these artifacts further points to a limited appreciation for aesthetics, calling into question their possibility of possessing a [soul](Lore_Souls.md).
