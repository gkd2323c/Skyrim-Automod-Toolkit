[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-npc-Blaise.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Blaise.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blaise.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blaise.jpg) Blaise **Blaise** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who works and lives at [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He sleeps outside on a hay pile in the stables.

## Dialogue
When you first approach him, he may tell you:

*"You probably want someone else. I'm just the stable boy."* *"I take care of the horses. And the goats. And the chickens. And the garden."* *"Talk to [Geimund](Skyrim_Geimund.md) if you want a horse, or [Katla](Skyrim_Katla.md) if you're looking for work. I can't help you."* If spoken to directly he can be asked questions about himself:

**How do you like the farm?** *"I hate it. Everyone feels sorry for me, so they don't treat me too bad, but..."* **Sorry for what?** or **What was your story again?** (if you asked about him before) *"Both my parents were in the Legion. There was... an ambush. Katla said she could feed me if I could make myself useful. I take care of the animals, run errands, that kind of thing. I guess it could be worse. But... I'm sick of sleeping outside with the horses. I want a real home, real parents. Not... this."* You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](Skyrim_Child.md#Adoption) him. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) with a child's bed and container.
