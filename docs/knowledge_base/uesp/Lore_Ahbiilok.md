Ahbiilok is a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Hunter-Blue-Sky" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup>

During the early years of the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), at some point after the founding of the organization<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup> in [1E 2703](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2703),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOFG-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GGB-4)</sup> he was seen throughout the northern parts of the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md). There were numerous recorded attempts to kill him, but not a single one was successful. He was recorded as an active dragon by the chronicler of the Dragonguard, [Brother Mathnan](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Brother_Mathnan), in the *[Atlas of Dragons](Lore_Atlas_of_Dragons.md)*, and as of [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373), he was believed to be lairing somewhere in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup>

According to *[Twin Secrets](Lore_Twin_Secrets.md)*, a renowned [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) [Brarilu Theran](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Brarilu_Theran) found a dragon sheltered in the smoking ruins of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and defeated it in combat. Instead of killing the Dragon, he parlayed with it. He asked the Dragon for its name, but the Dragon refused and instead offered to teach the [mer](Lore_Mer.md) how to defy the Law of Firsts and enchant items with multiple effects.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TS-5)</sup>
