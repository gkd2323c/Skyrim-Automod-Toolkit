# Vakka Stone

Vakka Stones (literally "Sun Stones" in the [Jel](Lore_Jel.md) language)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ST:AJP-1)</sup> are bright yellow crystals used by the ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) to store the raw [magicka](Lore_Magicka.md) that falls from the [Sun](Lore_Sun.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kass-2)</sup> They are only known to be found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). The stones are pieces of cut and enchanted [Meteoric Glass](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md), a rare form of [Aetherial Fragment](Lore_Aetherial_Fragments.md), glowing with arcane power. They are similar to the [Welkynd Stones](Lore_Welkynd_Stone.md) and [Varla Stones](Lore_Varla_Stone.md) that were used by the Ayleids, and rival that of the Kajin-Jat Crystal.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MQI-3)</sup> Prior to [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md), some tribes of ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) were directed by their [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) tree to worship the [sun](Lore_Magnus.md) or even to collect its power.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VBX-4)</sup> Known as the Sun-Blessed, they were eventually betrayed by their [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) -worshipping brethren, and the practice faded into obscurity.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
