[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Fork.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Fork.png) Balbus's Fork Balbus's Fork is a fork carried by [Balbus](Skyrim_Balbus.md). Balbus is a fan of [the Gourmet](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md), and it is with this fork that he tasted one of his dishes for the first time. If you have completed [Recipe for Disaster](Skyrim_Recipe_for_Disaster.md) and possess the [Gourmet's Writ of Passage](Skyrim_Gourmet%27s_Writ_of_Passage.md), he will gift it to you. Its appearance is identical to the weapon [fork](Skyrim_Fork_(weapon).md) variant. Unlike those forks, Balbus's Fork is a miscellaneous item, it weighs nothing, and it is worth nothing.
