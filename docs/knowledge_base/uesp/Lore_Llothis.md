Saint Llothis the Pious is the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) patron [saint](Lore_Temple_Saints.md) of Tailors and Dyers and the patron saint of [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Contemporary and companion of the Tribunals, and the best-loved [Alma Rula](Lore_Alma_Rula.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md), he formulated the central rituals and principles of the New Temple Faith. Saint Llothis is the symbolic mortal bridge between the gods and the faithful, and the archetypal priest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWdialogue-3)</sup> Llothis was canonized by the Tribunal, and shrines to him could be found in Tribunal temples. His [crosier](Lore_Crosier_of_St._Llothis.md) is now a prized relic of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Crosier Q-4)</sup>

While they were alive, saints Llothis and [Olms](Lore_Olms.md) were known to ride through the [Brass Fortress](Lore_Brass_Fortress.md) during the parades on holy days on [fabricant](Lore_Fabricant.md) [Nix-Ox](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) mounts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NX-5)</sup>

When close to death, [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) placed Llothis's soul into a modified black soul gem which was then brought to the Asylum Sanctorium, in the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), and installed in a giant [factotum](Lore_Factotum.md) body to make him immortal.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mercyquest-6)</sup> Unfortunately, the process eventually drove him to madness, which manifested as a deep depression over the seeming loss of his spiritual connection to the Tribunal.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> His soul was released from the factotum body in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AL-8)</sup> and was later transferred into the [Crosier of St. Llothis](Lore_Crosier_of_St._Llothis.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-9)</sup>

All [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) shrine offerings grant cures of common diseases, of blight diseases, and of afflictions of poison. Offerings made to the shrine of Saint Llothis additionally grant Rock of Llothis, for fortifying the will.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-mwshrine-11)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) the Captain of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armiger.md) took the Crosier of St. Llothis into battle against [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s forces on [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). To protect his retreating subordinates, the Captain mounted a last stand near Dagoth Ur's citadel, and the staff was lost.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-9)</sup> The crosier was subsequently recovered from there for the Temple by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Hero.md#Nerevarine).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Crosier Q-4)</sup>

There is a tailor shop called St. Llothis' Garments in the Royal Bazaar of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>
