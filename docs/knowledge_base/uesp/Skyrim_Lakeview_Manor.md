[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_13.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_13.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_13.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_13.jpg) Lakeview Manor **Lakeview Manor** is a piece of property in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md), north of [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md). It is one of three potential homes available with the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_14.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_14.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_14.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lakeview_Manor_14.jpg) Back view of the house After installing Hearthfire, you may purchase this plot of land at the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). If [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) is still the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), speak with his [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md), [Nenya](Skyrim_Nenya.md); if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) have control of the hold, speak with [Tekla](Skyrim_Tekla.md), steward to the new Jarl, [Dengeir of Stuhn](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md). You will have to complete the quest [Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader_(Jarl).md) before being allowed to purchase land if Siddgeir is Jarl. If you are level 9, then a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name)_of_(Jarl%27s_City).md) from the Jarl of Falkreath may arrive; reading it gives you an objective to talk to the Jarl, as a part of the Falkreath version of [Build Your Own Home](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Build_Your_Own_Home). However, if this has not happened yet, then Siddgeir will require you to complete his version of [Rare Gifts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Gifts) before giving the Bandit Leader quest, and the dialogue option to start this quest will not appear if another version of the quest is active. If Dengeir is Jarl, then he will allow you to purchase property out of thanks for your help installing him, whether this was because of events in [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or because you [conquered Falkreath for the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad.md). When you ask the steward if you can buy a house in the hold, you will be told that there are no houses for sale at that time, but a new plot of land is available. It will cost 5,000 gold.

Your new property, Lakeview Manor, will be marked as an undiscovered location on your map, located between Falkreath and [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md). When you arrive on your new plot of land, you will find a drafting table, carpenter's bench, [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and chest directly west of the housebuilding area, sheltered by a small cliff. Follow that same cliff face a few steps north to find the [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry). There is a [clay deposit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit), [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and [log pile](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md) directly west from the carpenter's workbench, on the other side of the dirt path.

You can begin [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) your home immediately, with materials provided in the chest when you first arrive. A unique feature at Lakeview Manor is that you have the option to build an apiary. It is initially stocked with one piece of [honeycomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honeycomb) and five [bees](Skyrim_Bee.md). When the apiary has been emptied, it will eventually be restocked with these items; it is therefore not safe to use as storage. [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md) can be seen from the north end of the property, beyond [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md).

Like the other player-built homes, you will have the option to expand onto your home with a [Main Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall), cellar, and three wing additions. See [this page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Hearthfire-Furnishing_Options) for pages detailing the furnishing options in each part of your home.

Should you become a [Thane of Falkreath](Skyrim_Thane_of_Falkreath.md), [Rayya](Skyrim_Rayya.md) will become your [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). She will move to Lakeview Manor upon construction of the [small house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout) layout.

## Notes
- Compared to [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) and [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md), Lakeview Manor sees more random spawn events, including bandits, giants, and wolves.
- A [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) can be found a short distance behind the house on the north side, down the hill.
- [Adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) children appear to favor Lakeview Manor over [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) and [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md). When spoken to, they sometimes remark, *"I like living here. The lake is so pretty."* (girls) or "*I went fishing at the lake. I almost caught something!"* (boys) - For the reactions given by your spouse, see [this page](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md#Spouse_Dialogue).
- You can find two [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) veins just to the south of the area, between the crafting area and the pond. There is also a [corundum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore) vein up the slope to the southwest, around a large gray rock.
- Down the hill before coming to the warlock is a deceased [wood cutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Cutter) lying next to the unique battleaxe [The Woodsman's Friend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Woodsman%27s_Friend).

## Bugs
- There are a few bugs which affect all houses. See the [Bugs](Skyrim_Houses.md#Bugs) section of the Houses page for details and fixes.
- Completing the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Kill Helvard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Helvard) results in a negative disposition towards you from the Jarl of Falkreath. As such they will not have a high enough disposition towards you in order to offer the dialogue option to purchase the Lakeview estate. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. It corrects this oversight by removing the disposition modifier from the quest.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can set the Jarl's relationship to a high enough level using `player.setrelationshiprank Ref ID 4` (replacing "Ref ID" with [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md)'s or [Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md)'s).
- Changing the jarl through the Civil War questline will automatically give you a high enough disposition with the new jarl in order to buy the property.
- When [Valdimar](Skyrim_Valdimar.md) is assigned as a Housecarl for [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md), he may be found here instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Spouses are scripted to visit the top of the armory porch during the day. If you didn't get this addition, however, they will simply walk to where the stairs would be and stand there until nightfall, occasionally walking in place. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- You may be able to hit the hawks on the roof and have them fall to the ground while at the same time still being airborne because the hawks are linked to enable parents that can prevent the script from processing their deaths properly. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
