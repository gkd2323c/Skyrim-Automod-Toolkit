Note to the Authorities
---
I have heard talk about the [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), but didn't believe it until now. One flew by last week and ever since we have seen him fly back and forth from a nearby mountain peak. My wife thinks we should move away, go somewhere else. I told her that would be nonsense. As long as we don't bother it, it won't bother us. Still, I thought it would be best if we report it to the local authorities. The creature's lair is just south the hill behind our shack near Bear's Cave Mill.

## Notes
- Bear's Cave Mill cannot be found anywhere in Skyrim, with only a small handful of references to the cut location remaining in the game's files.
