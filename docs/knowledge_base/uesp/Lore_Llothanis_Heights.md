The settlements of this area typically herd giant insects.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWElone-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1M-4)</sup>

## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Karththor Heights was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Shurien and had a rivalry with [Greenheights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenheights).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Arena-5)</sup> Karththor Heights was neighbored by several settlements, including nearby [Velothis Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Velothis_Haven), [Helnim Wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helnim_Wall) to the southwest, Firewatch to the west, and Greenheights to the southeast.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap-6)</sup>
