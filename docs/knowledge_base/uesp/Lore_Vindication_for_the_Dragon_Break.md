Vindication for the Dragon Break **by [Fervidius Tharn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Arch-Prelate_Fervidius_Tharn), Arch-Prelate of the [Maruhkati Selective](Lore_Alessian_Order.md)** *A Marukhati edict instructing the expungement of the Aldmeri aspects from Auriel*
---
It is the first of [the Exclusionary Mandates](Lore_The_Exclusionary_Mandates.md) that the Supreme Spirit [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) is of unitary essence, as is inconclusively proven by the monolinearity of Time. And clearly, the Arc of Time provides us with the mortal theater for the act of [Sacred Expungement](Lore_Dragon_Break.md). Thus it is our purpose upon [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) to reverse the error of Sanctus Primus and restore Ak-at-Osh to humanadic purity. To say otherwise is vain and empty persiflage.

Therefore let the [Staff of Towers](Lore_Staff_of_Towers.md) be prepared for the ritual that will cleanse the protean substrate of the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) Taint. All [Selectives](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md) are to initiate [chants of Proper-Life](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Proper-Life__Three_Chants) and maintain them until a state of monothought is achieved. Then each shall Dance, duration-forward then volteface, till the Roll of Time winds withershins.

[Prophet-Most-Simian](Lore_Marukh.md) guide us! Misplaced [Shezarr](Lore_Shezarr.md) bless us! May our Wills in this be Enacted!
