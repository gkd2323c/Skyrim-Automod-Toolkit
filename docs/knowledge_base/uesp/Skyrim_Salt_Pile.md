## Ingredients
350 guaranteed samples can be found in 162 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 14 centered 330 ft S of [Merryfair Farm](Skyrim_Merryfair_Farm.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=167275&y=-95667) (In several barrels on the Riften Docks.)
- 13 in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 9 in [The Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 9 in [Mistveil Keep Barracks](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep_Barracks.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 8 in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 6 in [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 6 in [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 6 in [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 6 in [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))

Nearly all innkeepers and apothecary merchants are guaranteed to sell 1-6 salt piles. In addition, salt can commonly be found in many containers. Fish [barrels](Skyrim_Barrel.md) always contain at least 1 salt pile, as do certain barrels that are internally identified as meat barrels. Standard food barrels and most small [sacks](Skyrim_Sack.md) have a 40% chance of containing 1-3 salt piles.

There is a second variety of salt pile which shares all the same alchemical properties as a regular salt pile and can be used in the same way in potion creation. This salt pile cannot be used for cooking, and is worth one less gold than a normal salt pile. It can only be found inside the [Black-Briar Manor](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), but it cannot be reached without the use of glitches or [console](Skyrim_Console.md) commands.
