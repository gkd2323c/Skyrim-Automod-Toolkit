# Chitin

**Chitin** armor can be crafted with the [Elven Smithing](Skyrim_Elven_Smithing.md) perk and comes in light and heavy armor variants, with the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adding more. All items can be tempered using one piece of [chitin plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md), which can be gathered in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) from [ash hoppers](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper.md) and vendors. [Morag Tong armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong_Armor) is a variant of chitin with more of a brown color.

### Indoril Armor<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>

The set has its own material keyword for the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk, which includes the Ordinator shield.

Its stats are identical to those of [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor), save for the cuirass' value. It also comes with a matching shield.

### Her Hand Armor<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
Added by the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), [Her Hand Armor Set](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor_Set.md) is an equipment set worn by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md)'s personal guardians. Although Almalexia is gone, the armor is still used by members of a cult dedicated to the Tribunal. The set consists entirely of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md). Every piece of the set requires a [chitin plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md) to be improved. The set has its own material keyword for the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk.

The armor can be crafted after killing [Hand Kenro Hlan](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro_Hlan.md) during the [Ghosts of the Tribunal quest](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest).md). The total armor rating matches that of [ebony armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Armor.md), but this set is lighter and costs less. It also lacks a matching shield.
