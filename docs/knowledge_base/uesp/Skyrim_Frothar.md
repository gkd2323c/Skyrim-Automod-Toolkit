[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-npc-Frothar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Frothar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frothar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frothar.jpg) Frothar **Frothar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md). He is the eldest son of [Jarl Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) and brother to [Dagny](Skyrim_Dagny.md) and [Nelkir](Skyrim_Nelkir.md). Of the jarl's three children, Frothar is the only one who isn't rude toward you and won't make any condescending remarks.

Like the jarl's other children, he spends all his time inside Dragonsreach. He wakes up at 6am and stays in the children's room in the Jarl's Quarters talking with his siblings before heading out to play in the main hall at 9am. He spends his whole day wandering in the hall, taking an hour long lunch at 12pm and a two hour long dinner at 5pm. After dinner, he heads back to his room then waits until 10pm to go to sleep for eight hours.

Frothar wears red [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). He carries a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child), and a small amount of gold.

## Conversations
His sister, Dagny, will sometimes complain to him about a new dress:

**Dagny**: *"I'm so angry, I can hardly stand it! Father promised me a new dress days ago, now where is it?"*
**Frothar**: *"It takes a while to make a dress, Dagny. You have to be patient."*
**Dagny**: *"But I don't want to be patient! I want my dress right now! Father promised!"*
**Frothar**: *"All you ever do is complain about what you want. You're a spoiled baby."*
**Dagny**: *"I'm going to tell father you said that. He'll tan your hide for sure!"*

## Notes
- If Whiterun is conquered by the Stormcloaks, then Frothar's father moves to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) but he does not. Also, his dialogue does not change. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, addresses this issue. He will move to the Blue Palace.

## Bugs
- Frothar will try to sleep in his sister [Dagny](Skyrim_Dagny.md)'s bed instead of his own. This behavior is due to him being assigned the wrong sleep package. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- He also can be found in front of the [Whiterun stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables), just standing there no matter the time of day or night, and answering generic child greetings when talked to.
