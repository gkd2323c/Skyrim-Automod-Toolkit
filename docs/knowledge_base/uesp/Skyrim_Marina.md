[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-npc-Marina.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Marina.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Marina.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Marina.jpg) Marina **Marina** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). If she survives the dinner, she can later be found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) at [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), but in fact she will be disabled after walking out of [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md).

She wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md) and [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md). She wields a leveled dagger.

## Related Quests
- **[Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>: Attend a feast.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Before the dinner:

- **Are you here for the dinner party?**
- *"Indeed."*

- **Do you think the food will be any good?**
- *"Of course."*

If you save her from the vampires:

- *"Well. I do suppose thanks are in order. So... my thanks."*
