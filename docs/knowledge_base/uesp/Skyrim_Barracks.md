# Barracks

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar_Barracks.jpg) Dawnstar Barracks This page lists all the **guard barracks** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). Each [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md), with the exception of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md), has its own barracks.

- **[Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md)** — A hold in eastern Skyrim, with its capital in Windhelm.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Palace.png) **[Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md)** — A large castle at the far end of the Valunstrad district in Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Palace+of+the+Kings)
- **[Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)** — A hold in southern Skyrim, with its capital in Falkreath.
- **[Falkreath Barracks](Skyrim_Falkreath_Barracks.md)** — The living quarters for the Falkreath guards and also contains Falkreath Hold's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Falkreath+Barracks)
- **[Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md)** — A relatively small hold in northwest Skyrim, containing the provincial capital Solitude.
- **[Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md)** — A large fortress in Solitude which acts as the headquarters of the Imperial Legion and residence of the Emperor in Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Dour)
- **[Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md)** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Morthal.
- **[Guardhouse](Skyrim_Guardhouse.md)** — The Hjaalmarch barracks on the south side of Morthal. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guardhouse)
- **[Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A large island in the Sea of Ghosts northeast of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Solstheim)
- **[The Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — An imposing stone wall created to protect Raven Rock from ash storms, with the city's garrison and jail both built directly into the wall. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=The+Bulwark)
- **[The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)** — A hold in northern Skyrim, with its capital in Dawnstar.
- **[Dawnstar Barracks](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Barracks.md)** — The living quarters for the Dawnstar guards. It also houses Dawnstar's jail. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dawnstar+Barracks)
- **[The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)** — A hold in western Skyrim, with Markarth as its capital.
- **[Guard Tower](Skyrim_Guard_Tower.md)** — A Dwemer tower in the center of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Tower)
- **[The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)** — A hold in southeast Skyrim, with its capital in Riften.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png) **[Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md)** — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mistveil+Keep)
- **[Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md)** — A hold in central Skyrim, with its capital in Whiterun.
- **[Guard Barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks.md)** — A building in Whiterun where guards live when not on duty. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Barracks)
- **[Guard Barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks_(Gate).md)** — A building near the main gate in Whiterun. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Guard+Barracks)
