## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The cave entrance is unmarked and easily missed. From [Clearspring Tarn](Skyrim_Clearspring_Tarn.md), move a few meters north toward the cliff edge (overlooking [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) to the north). Look down a few feet for the path that tracks across the cliff face. The cave is located at the right end of that path, and its entrance is a great place to enjoy a gorgeous view of Eastmarch.

### Clearspring Cave
The cave itself is located underneath the tarn, whose waters seep through the ceiling. The first half of the chamber is partially flooded with the water pooling on the left, with an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) in front of the pool as you enter. Mushrooms can be found along the path from the entrance, and [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) is abundant and can be harvested around the edges overhanging the pool. There is a [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) floating in the water to the left of the bridge with a leveled one-handed [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and a random [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) at the bottom of the pool beneath him.

The second half of the chamber is slightly larger and full of various flora and fungi, as well as numerous bones, both human and animal. The sole occupant of the cave, a [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md), makes its home here. Toward the back of the chamber is an altar holding the unique [Bow of the Hunt](Skyrim_Bow_of_the_Hunt.md) and a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*. To the right of this is an unlocked wooden [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), with leveled [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and [magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka) in front. There is a second skeleton that can be looted just to the right of the troll's nest, and by the edge of the water, on the right after crossing the earthen bridge a random sword either found among various gory bones or hanging high overhead.

With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, three [tripod spiderfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripod_Spiderfish) and a [direfish](Skyrim_Direfish.md) can be caught by hand in the ponds on either side of the causeway.

## Notes
- The guaranteed troll spawn makes this cave dangerous to low-level characters.
- The cave contains a large amount of alchemy [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (including the [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) from the troll's corpse).
- There is no separate map marker for the cave; however, Clearspring Tarn is marked as cleared once the troll is destroyed.
