# Ring of Azura

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/MW-item-Exquisite_Rings.jpg/200px-MW-item-Exquisite_Rings.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Exquisite_Rings.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Exquisite_Rings.jpg) Ring of Azura *You no longer bear the burden of prophecy. You have achieved your destiny. You are freed. The doomed Dwemer's folly. Lord Dagoth's temptation. The Tribunal's seduction. The god's heart freed. The prophecy fulfilled. All fates sealed and sins redeemed. If you have pity, mourn the lost but let the weeping cease. The blight is gone, and the sun's golden honey gilds the land. Hail savior, Hortator and Nerevarine. Your people look to you for protection. Monsters and villains great and small still threaten the people of Vvardenfell. Enemies and evils abound, yet indomitable will might rid Morrowind of all its ills. For you, our thanks and blessing, our gift and token given. Come, take this thing from the hand of god.*
—Azura<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azura-1)</sup> Ring of Azura is an [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) piece of jewelery associated with [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). It was an exquisite ring with a dark gemstone with ability to restore fatigue to its bearer and grant them the ability to see in the dark.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROA-2)</sup>

## History
In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), shortly after the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) defeated [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) beneath the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), Azura's avatar appeared before them in the form of an ethereal [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) woman. She thanked the Nerevarine for saving [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), warned that enemies of its people still remained, and bestowed her blessing along with a ring as a token of her gratitude.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azura-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-3)</sup><sup>:111</sup> The moment the Nerevarine first took up the artifact, they were blessed and rejuvenated by the Hand of Azura after the great battle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Quest-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HOA-5)</sup>
