[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-npc-Hjaalmarch_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hjaalmarch_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjaalmarch_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjaalmarch_Guard.jpg) The Hjaalmarch Guard The **[Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) Guard** can be found in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md). He guards the entrance to [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) on the eastern side of Stonehills. He stands between the cart and the tree stump beside the entrance.

He wears the standard set of [Hjaalmarch guard's armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Hjaalmarch_Guard.27s_Armor), including a [Hjaalmarch guard's helmet](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch_Guard%27s_Helmet.md) and a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and a [Hjaalmarch guard's shield](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch_Guard%27s_Shield.md). He also carries in his inventory an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and a 75% chance of carrying 20 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). He may also carry, albeit a small chance, an [amulet](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Amulets_of_the_Divines) of one of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), some inn food, and a small amount of gold.

Like other guards, the Hjaalmarch Guard has standard [guard dialogue](Skyrim_Guard_Dialogue.md) and, in all respects, is just a normal [guard](Skyrim_Guards.md).
