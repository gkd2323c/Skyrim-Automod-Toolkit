[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Frorkmar_Banner-Torn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Frorkmar_Banner-Torn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frorkmar_Banner-Torn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frorkmar_Banner-Torn.jpg) Frorkmar Banner-Torn **Frorkmar Banner-Torn** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) and commander of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) who can be found in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). He is usually in [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) or upstairs sleeping in the barracks. If [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) is taken by the Imperials, he will relocate to the [Pale Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Pale_Stormcloak_Camp.md).

He wears a full set of [Stormcloak officer armor](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Armor.md), including matching pairs of [bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md), and the trademark [helmet](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Helmet.md). He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and a leveled [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md), and also has a [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md). He also carries a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) supplemented with 12 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger).

## Dialogue
When you have not joined either the Stormcloaks or the Imperial Legion he can say:

**How does one join the Stormcloaks?** *"[Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s not running a social club. You don't join anything. Either you're a true son of Skyrim, or you're not. If you are, then you make your way to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and talk to the man himself. If you're not, well friend, you'd best walk away before this gets ugly."* **How do you feel about this war?** *"Skyrim is the birthplace of humanity. The birthplace of honor. And those snowbacked Imperials renounced both when they laid down before the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md). Worse still, to pay for the privilege to keep his throne, the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) sells our very gods - [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), no less! - to the damn [elves](Skyrim_High_Elf.md). And what did [Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md) do? He and the [Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) who supported him? They took the Emperor's gold. Sold out their people for a slave master's "[peace treaty](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md)." Ulfric did right killing him. Soon we'll rid Skyrim of elves, their bloody Justiciars, and the Jarls in their pockets. The whole damn lot of them. And we'll cleave through the Legion to get to them."* **Why are you called Stormcloaks?** *"At first they called us that to belittle our cause. But we gladly accept being named for Ulfric Stormcloak, the only true High King of Skyrim. We are the true sons and daughters of Skyrim, and Ulfric... he's the truest of us all. We fight to make our land great once more, to raise up strong, noble [thanes](Skyrim_Thane.md), and lay low false Jarls and the cowards who hide behind them. The sound of our fury is a storm about to break on the heads of our enemies. Our friendship, a cloak of true brotherhood for all who would wear it."*
