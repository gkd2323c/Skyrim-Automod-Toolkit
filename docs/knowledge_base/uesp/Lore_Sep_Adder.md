# Sep Adder

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/ON-pet-Sep_Adder.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Sep_Adder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Sep_Adder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Sep_Adder.jpg) A Sep Adder Sep Adders are venomous winged [snakes](Lore_Snake.md) most commonly seen with brown scales and crimson eyes. While they are commonly seen in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), particularly on the [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) peninsula, they have also been witnessed in northern [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). Despite being winged, the sep adder cannot fly but can use them to glide short distances.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tot SA-1)</sup> They have also been observed to use their wings in threat displays and to shade their heads from the sun.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAIT-2)</sup> The diet of the sep adder is varied as they have been observed to eat both insects and [fish](Lore_Fish.md) as well as small rodents,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tot SA-1)</sup> they are constantly on the hunt and are capable of pouncing on prey from a great distance. However, they are also edible and are described as being easy to skin and cook, akin to a chicken.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAIT-2)</sup>

Sep adders are often described as having a mean temperament and short tempers, like [badgers](Lore_Badger.md) in snake-form. However, this has not stopped people from attempting to tame these reptiles as pets.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sep Adder-3)</sup> Aside from the common sep adder, there are a few known subspecies; the cobalt sep adder is rarely seen in captivity but is prized among the Hammerfell nobility for its rumored ability to be able to detect poison in drinks and food.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cobalt-4)</sup>. Another variety, the silver-scaled infernal sep adder, is known to inhabit the high altitudes regions of Hammerfell.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Infernal-5)</sup> Some [Brassilisk](Lore_Brassilisk.md) [fabricants](Lore_Fabricants.md) from the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) are created in the form of Sep Adders.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TFFOC-6)</sup> [Reef Vipers](Lore_Reef_Viper.md) are a similarly winged species of snake.
