# Veloth (family)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-place-Veloth_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-ON-place-Veloth_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Veloth_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Veloth_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) The Veloth Ancestral Tomb The Veloth Clan is a family of [Dark Elves](Lore_Dark_Elves.md) that live in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The Veloth clan has a long and prominent history on the region of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and is said to be the lineage of the prophet, [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) the Pilgrim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Loading-1)</sup> The **Veloth Ancestral Tomb** is found in the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) of Vvardenfell.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Loading-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind-2)</sup> Their house affiliation is unknown.

## History
The Veloth Clan claim to be the descendants of Veloth the Pilgrim, the former noble of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), who guided the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) to the promised lands of Resdayn in the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Like other families, the Veloth Clan was ruled by a Patriarch or Matriarch, one of which being Matriarch Rathila who was interred in their family tomb.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

One of the earliest members was Llirala Veloth, who was the Matriarch for more than one hundred years. She was known for beauty, and brought peace and prosperity through three marriages. The [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) had graced her with everlasting beauty, leaving a positive impact on the Veloth family. Another was Ondre Veloth, known as the *Master Archer of [Molag Amur](Lore_Molag_Amur.md)*. He was given a mighty bow from [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md). He had defended the pass near [Lake Nabia](Lore_Lake_Nabia.md) for nine days, until he was taken down by the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EP-6)</sup>

One fateful day, a member of the Veloth Clan was killed. His daughter, Valyne Veloth, would seek out revenge for the rest of her life. As time went on, Valyne had infiltrated their ranks and was soon to marry the same person that killed her father. She had secretly poisoned the killer and his entourage, kill everyone and herself in the process. Elms Veloth was a member of the Veloth family during the Year of Great Hunger, feeding both family and retainers of the clan. He would not accept food for himself, and so because of this, the Veloth Clan survived but Elms would pass away in hunger.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EP-6)</sup>

As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), one of the members of the family, Tanusea Veloth was a beloved holy pilgrim and a carrier of the Divine Disease who resided in Vivec City before later relocating to Tel Fyr, having been persuaded by a member of the Tribunal Temple to stop spreading the disease seek a cure for her [Corprus](Lore_Corprus.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DC-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TV-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EL-9)</sup>
