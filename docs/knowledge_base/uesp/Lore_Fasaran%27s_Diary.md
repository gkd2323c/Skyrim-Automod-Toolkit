Fasaran's Diary **by [Fasaran](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fasaran)** *Diary of a lonely lover's thoughts on recent events*
---
Dear Fara,

I watched the sunset, thinking of you again. I've heard rumors of [war](Lore_Wars.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Some are calling it "[The Great War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md)." Alliances are forming and rising up. Mirkalinde told me our [dark cousins](Lore_Dark_Elf.md) have united with the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), their own [slaves](Lore_Slavery.md), and the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md). It must be quite the battle to forge such an [uneasy alliance](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). It's hard to believe as I gaze out upon the beauty of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) that such devastation is happening in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). I hope our people stay out of the conflict.
- - - - - - -

Dear Fara,

Time passes by so slowly without you here. Things seem to be changing swiftly. [Queen Ayrenn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Queen_Ayrenn) has returned to Auridon. No one is certain where she's been, but she's been speaking of this alliance called the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). Would you believe we're now allies with our [woodsy cousins](Lore_Wood_Elf.md) and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md)?

Not all the people here seem happy about it. Sinyon speaks of a group called the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md). It's a shame most of us are so against allying with the outside races.
- - - - - - -

Dear Fara,

I miss you so much. Tenmenye is bustling about the town. Queen Ayrenn is going to stop by [Dawnbreak](Lore_Dawnbreak.md) on her way to [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md). Ten was telling me how much she misses you and your guidance. She's overwhelmed with preparations, but we're all very excited to see her in our small town.
