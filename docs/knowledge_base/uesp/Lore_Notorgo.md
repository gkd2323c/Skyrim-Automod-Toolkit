# Notorgo

[![DF-demo-Nortorgo.png](https://images.uesp.net/7/76/DF-demo-Nortorgo.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-demo-Nortorgo.png) Notorgo (or **Torgo**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is the Messenger God<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup> of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup> He is mentioned in *[King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md)* when Queen Aliera praises Notorgo for bringing her husband [Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md) and son [Edward](Lore_Edward.md) to her safely.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Notorgo lends his name to spells such as Notorgo's Curse and Feet of Notorgo.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup> He is often invoked to request fleetness in the delivery of a message.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Several additional knightly orders existed in earlier versions of Daggerfall that did not appear in the final version of the game, though their strings remain in the final version's data. They are noted in the [Daggerfall Preview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall_Preview), including the templar order of The Order of the Wings, dedicated to Notorgo.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup>
