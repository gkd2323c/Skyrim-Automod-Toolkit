King Karoodil, who also bore the name **Reman**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMorgiahs-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup><sup>[[nb 3]](#intnote_nb3)</sup> was the [High Elven](Lore_Altmer.md) king of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), a city-state in the northern region of [Vafe](Lore_Vafe.md), located on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMorgiahs-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESORegions-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/ON-map-Vafe.jpg/200px-ON-map-Vafe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Vafe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Vafe.jpg) Vafe
### Tragedy
At some point in the past prior to [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Karoodil had a son of his own, but his life was lost.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMorgiahs-1)</sup>

Karoodil also had an elder brother, who was the first in the line of succession to Firsthold; however, his brother was slain by the necromantic forces of the [King of Worms](Lore_Mannimarco.md), who long plagued their land. Circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Karoodil was crowned King of Firsthold in his brother's place. But his brother's son, Aedrin, remained the heir apparent and Crown Prince to the kingdom.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-4)</sup> Additionally, Karoodil had a younger brother who was also next in line for the throne.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-5)</sup>

### Seduction
Being a second-born child and not standing to inherit a kingdom of her own, the Princess [Morgiah](Lore_Morgiah.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) sought to enter into the royalty of Summerset. Morgiah had learned of Karoodil's coronation, and also the fact that he wished to speak with his dead son. Taking advantage of the situation, Morgiah made a contract with the [King of Worms](Lore_King_of_Worms.md), leader of the [Necromancers](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md); in exchange for Morgiah giving the King of Worms "her first," he would "let" Karoodil speak with his dead son, a thing which "only" the King of Worms could do, by "exerting" his influence over the King. Out of gratitude for arranging this, Karoodil agreed to marry Morgiah, making her the Queen of Firsthold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMorgiahs-1)</sup> Such a union would've been forbidden a century ago, but thanks to a significant element of Altmer agitating for social change, new cultures and races have been accepted onto the shores of Summerset, even a [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) queen.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3S-6)</sup> This advocation was especially propagated by a faction of youths called [the Beautiful](Lore_The_Beautiful.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3S-6)</sup>

The engagement was said to have been "quite lengthy," lasting three years,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMorgiahs-1)</sup> but Karoodil and Morgiah were wed sometime between [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) and [3E 413](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_413) (perhaps in [3E 409](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_409)).<sup>[[nb 4]](#intnote_nb4)</sup> With his new queen wife, Karoodil sired a son named Goranthir (born around [3E 413](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_413)), as well as a daughter named Rinnala (born around [3E 416](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_416)).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3S-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-4)</sup><sup>[[nb 4]](#intnote_nb4)</sup> While his children were half-Altmeri and half Dunmeri, they primarily took after their mother in appearance, though Goranthir inherited Karoodil's red-gold hair.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3S-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-4)</sup>

The [Trebbite Monks](Lore_Trebbite_Monks.md) were staunchly opposed to the marriage, their credo had been for only the purest Altmer bloodlines to be on Summerset across all families, especially in royal dynasties.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup> Queen Morgiah saw to it that they had little power within the kingdom, and would have had the Trebbites exterminated, if not for Karoodil arguing against it, citing the good they do for the common folk.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup>

### Revolt in Firsthold
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/ON-place-Firsthold.jpg/200px-ON-place-Firsthold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Firsthold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Firsthold.jpg) Firsthold According to historical fiction called *[The Firsthold Revolt](Lore_The_Firsthold_Revolt.md)*, by [Maveus Cie](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Maveus_Cie), the Princess Gialene of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) (who's father ruled the Kingdom of Skywatch in the [Calambar](Lore_Calambar.md) region to the south) had been in [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md) serving as the chief royal concubine of the King. For some time she had been unsuccessfully attempting to woo King Karoodil into marrying her, the King however, chose to marry Princess Morgiah of Wayrest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup> By [3E 418](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_418),<sup>[[nb 5]](#intnote_nb5)</sup> Gialene was hedging her bets on Karoodil setting aside this marriage after Morgiah's brother failed to secure the throne of Wayrest, and thus their family should have lost all political leverage, but Karoodil did not do this thing.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup>

From behind the scenes, Princess Gialene and her confidant Kael then sponsored and propagated a revolt against Morgiah using her father's financial resources, rallying and arming the Trebbite Monks and the common folk against the Queen; with growing resentment among the peasants of the countryside, Morgiah was named the "Black Queen" as an insult, but Karoodil waved away the concerns as these things were nothing he had not heard before, despite hearing rumors of such talk grow louder and louder over a period of two weeks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup> Karoodil's attentions had been more focused on the pirate activity coming from the small island of [Calluis Lar](Lore_Calluis_Lar.md) off the coast, which had been more brazen of late, attacking royal barges in "organized raids." To eliminate the pirates, Karoodil left his city with a great part of his militia to personally lead an incursion against Calluis Lar (leaving the city less defended).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup>

A few days after the King's departure, a revolutionary army comprised of the Trebbite Monks and mercenary rejects from the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) launched a series of well coordinated attacks against the kingdom, who were led by Friar Lylim of the Trebbites, and pressuring Morgiah to abdicate. Gialene thought she could count on the countrysiders to join her cause, but they were disinterested in actually joining the revolution. After an seemingly successful attempt to coax the plan of city defense out from Queen Morgiah, noting the intended plan of attack, Gialene reported Morgiah's plans to the monks. But Morgiah had known of the intended deception by Gialene, and ordered her troops to fight in an unexpected fashion, which left the army decimated, and all the monks fleeing in disorganized retreat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup>

When Karoodil returned, he had Kael and Gialene tortured until they conveyed their treachery to the King's satisfaction. He had Kael executed, and Princess Gialene sent back to her father at Skywatch. Karoodil then elected not to take on a new royal concubine, breaking from tradition; which only proved to worsen rumors of the Black Queen's influence at court among the common people.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFR-2)</sup>

### Morrowind Civil War
When conflict took place in Morrowind between [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) and [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md) against [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) after newly crowned [King Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md) declared slavery illegal throughout the province circa [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), King Karoodil sent a fleet east to assist Hlaalu Helseth in the war, with Queen Morgiah sailing with the fleet.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBRC-7)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-4)</sup>

### Rise of the Thalmor
After the [Stormcrown Interregnum](Lore_Stormcrown_Interregnum.md) (sometime around/after [4E 22](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_22)), the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) congress seized control of all Summerset, overthrowing the rightful Kings and Queens of the Altmer, as well as launching many pogroms against any who were not "of the blood of the [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md)."<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
