[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg) Miraak absorbing a dragon soul Relonikiv was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Dominate-Enlightenment" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PAR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIL-2)</sup>

Little is known about Relonikiv's past. At a certain point in history, he swore allegiance to [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), though whether he did so willingly or was compelled through Miraak's mastery of [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) is unknown.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASA-3)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) traveled to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) to challenge Miraak. Through the use of the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), they swayed a dragon [Sahrotaar](Lore_Sahrotaar.md) to betray Miraak. He helped them to reach the First Dragonborn on the summit of Apocrypha where the Last Dragonborn confronted Miraak. It was there where he called the mighty dragons Relonikiv and [Kruziikrel](Lore_Kruziikrel.md) to aid him in the battle. During the fight Miraak absorbed Relonikiv's soul to regain vitality, killing him in the process.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Miraak-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PRIMA-5)</sup><sup>:604-605</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Relonikiv was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LAD-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Arngeir-7)</sup>
