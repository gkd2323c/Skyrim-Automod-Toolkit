[Niranye](Skyrim_Niranye.md),

Preparations have been made to secure the [locket](Skyrim_Fjotli%27s_Silver_Locket.md) at [Uttering Hills Cave](Skyrim_Uttering_Hills_Cave.md). [Torsten](Skyrim_Torsten_Cruel-Sea.md) thinks we're responsible for Fjotli's death and we may need it as leverage. I assure you we merely lifted it from her body; the [Shadows](Lore_Summerset_Shadows.md) never murder. I hope you find a buyer soon, I don't like the heat this is bringing.

[Linwe](Skyrim_Linwe.md)
