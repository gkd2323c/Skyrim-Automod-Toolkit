He wears some farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). He wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
