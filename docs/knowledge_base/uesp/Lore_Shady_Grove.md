[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/BL-place-Shady_Grove.jpg/200px-BL-place-Shady_Grove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-place-Shady_Grove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-place-Shady_Grove.jpg) Ruined buildings in Shady Grove circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180) Shady Grove (also called **Shadymarch**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a village found in the Khefrem region of southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), near the waters of [Hubalajad's Bay](Lore_Hubalajad%27s_Bay.md) and the city-state of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup>

The village of Shady Grove has been in existence since the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGMap-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Shadymarch was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Maoler and had a rivalry with [Riverview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverview).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Shadymarch was neighbored by a few settlements, including Lainebon Place to the east, and Gilane to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup> The al-Danobia Tomb can also be found to the east.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> By [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the forests of Shady Grove were overrun by hostile wildlife and had a number of abandoned buildings within.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Blades-5)</sup>

## Notes
- *Shadymarch* was renamed to *Shady Grove* during the development of *[Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard)* as an [Easter egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Easter_Eggs) reference to the area of Shady Grove, where [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda)'s offices at 1370 Piccard Drive, Rockville, Maryland are located.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TIL-6)</sup>
