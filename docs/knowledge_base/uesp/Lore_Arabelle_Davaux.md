Arabelle Davaux of [House Davaux](Lore_House_Davaux.md) was a captain of [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) and a member of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md)'s intelligence branch [Ring of Daggers](Lore_Ring_of_Daggers.md). She was also a member of a humanitarian organization [Society of the Steadfast](Lore_Society_of_the_Steadfast.md) and worked to ensure peace talks between representatives of the three alliances during [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAD-1)</sup> According to some chroniclers she was the author of [Investigator Vale](Lore_Investigator_Vale.md) detective novels or an inspiration for the main heroine of the books that were believed to be "true-to-life" re-imaginings of actual historical events.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRIV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt CA-3)</sup> Arabelle officially denied any connection with Investigator Vale novels,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MTCLA-4)</sup> though she did in fact author them.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MTCS-5)</sup>

## History

### Early Life
Little is known of Arabelle Davaux's youth. She was born into a [noble](Lore_House_Davaux.md) household in [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) in [2E 527](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_527). Since her youngest years, she received the best education High Rock had to offer.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MTCLA-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRIV-2)</sup>

### Career
When Arabelle was fifteen years old, in [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542) she left her home and joined a mercenary group that helped defend High Rock from the [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md)'s invasion of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). She partook in numerous battles across the [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and was eventually recruited into the [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md). Among her achievements was saving the inhabitants of Kerbol's Hollow from numerous [trolls](Lore_Troll.md) single-handedly and protecting [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) from the threat of [harpies](Lore_Harpy.md). She climbed the ranks and by [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560) earned the rank of captain and commanded a premier squad of the Lion Guard. She served as King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md)'s personal bodyguard for a year and was later recruited into [Ring of Daggers](Lore_Ring_of_Daggers.md) as one of its first members soon after the formation of the organization.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MTCLA-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRIV-2)</sup>
