[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/MW-place-Andasreth.jpg/200px-MW-place-Andasreth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Andasreth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Andasreth.jpg) Andasreth ca [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Andasreth is an ancient stronghold in the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) region of Vvardenfell, just north of [Khartag Point](Lore_Khartag_Point.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind-2)</sup>

It is one of the ancient strongholds from a brighter time in [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) h History. It can be found north of [Gnaar Mok](Lore_Gnaar_Mok.md), just over the mountains north of Khartag Point. The stronghold has largely been abandoned for some time; in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), it was the campsite for a tribe of [Red Exiles](Lore_Red_Exiles.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>, and in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) it has been taken over by bandits.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind-2)</sup>

Alongside other nine Dunmeri strongholds Berandas, Falasmaryon, Falensarano, Hlormaren, [Indoranyon](Lore_Indoranyon.md), Marandus, [Rotheran](Lore_Rotheran.md), Telasero, and Valenvaryon, it was connected to the [magical transportation](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) network of Propylon Chambers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PC-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
