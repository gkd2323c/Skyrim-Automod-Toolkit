[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-place-Windstad_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windstad_Manor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windstad_Manor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windstad_Manor.jpg) Windstad Manor **Windstad Manor** is a piece of property in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), north of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) and east of the [Abandoned Shack](Skyrim_Abandoned_Shack.md). It is one of three potential homes available with the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Windstad_Manor_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windstad_Manor_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windstad_Manor_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windstad_Manor_02.jpg) Aerial view of the house with the Alchemy, Enchanting, and Library additions After installing Hearthfire, you may purchase this plot of land at [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). If [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) is still the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), speak with her [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md), [Aslfur](Skyrim_Aslfur.md); if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) have control of the hold, speak with the new jarl, [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) or her husband [Pactur](Skyrim_Pactur.md). You will have to complete the quest [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md) before being allowed to purchase land if Idgrod is still jarl; Sorli will allow you to purchase it out of gratitude for your help installing her, whether that was because of [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or [conquering Hjaalmarch for the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks).md). When you ask if you can buy a house in the hold, you will be told that there are no houses for sale at that time, but a new plot of land is available. It will cost 5,000 gold.

Your new property, Windstad Manor, will be marked as an undiscovered location on your map, located in the northern part of Hjaalmarch on a small hill, south of [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md) and just east of where the land lowers into the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).

When you arrive on your new plot of land, you will find a drafting table, carpenter's bench, [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and chest south of the housebuilding area, just before the treeline. A "[Log Pile](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)" can be found slightly north of the drafting table. A [clay deposit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit) is directly adjacent to the north end of the log pile, while a [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry) is a short distance further north, embedded in the cliff wall on the east side just before two snow-laden trees. Directly west from this point, towards the water, is a second clay deposit; another stone quarry is in the cliff wall just behind it.

You can begin [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) your home immediately, with materials provided in the chest when you first arrive. A unique feature at Windstad Manor is you have the option to build a fish hatchery. Once built, the hatchery appears a distance north of your property, next to the second clay deposit previously mentioned. If you place any [fish](Skyrim_Fish.md) or fish eggs ([Slaughterfish Eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg) or [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe)) in the column at the end of the jetty (only one of each species will be accepted) and return to your property in seven days, the netted area of water will contain several offspring of that fish. The [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) that spawn are tame and will not attack you. The house entrance will face to the south; looking west from there affords an impressive view of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) atop its natural arch.

Like the other player-built homes, you will have the option to expand onto your home with a [Main Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall), cellar, and three wing additions. See [this page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Hearthfire-Furnishing_Options) for pages detailing the furnishing options in each part of your home.

Should you become a [Thane of Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Thane_of_Hjaalmarch.md), [Valdimar](Skyrim_Valdimar.md) will become your [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). He will move to Windstad Manor upon construction of the [small house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout) layout.

## Walkthrough

### Fish Hatchery
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery.jpg) The Fish Hatchery Multiple types of fish may be added at the same time to the hatchery and all will eventually mature. The following can be raised in the hatchery:

- [Abacean Longfin](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Abacean_Longfin)
- [Clam](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Clam) (using [Clam Meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat))
- [Cyrodilic Spadetail](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Cyrodilic_Spadetail)
- [Histcarp](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Histcarp)
- [River Betty](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#River_Betty)
- [Salmon](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Salmon) (using [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>) - Roe can be harvested after killing the salmon with the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout.
- [Silverside Perch](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Silverside_Perch)
- [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) (using [Slaughterfish Egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg)) - The offspring are not hostile to you, and slaughterfish egg nests will appear next to the jetty.

[Dragonflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly) may appear above the water of the fishery as well. [Nordic Barnacle Clusters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle_Cluster) and [Pearl Oysters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> cannot be bred in the hatchery even though they are aquatic creatures.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery_02.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Fish_Hatchery_02.jpg) You can farm many different types of fish with the Hatchery, including those added by [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, all of the new fish can be added to the hatchery. The only exception is [Juvenile Mudcrabs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juvenile_Mudcrab)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>. It is not necessary to deposit an [Angler](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Angler)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> in the hatchery; placing a single [Angler Larvae](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Angler_Larvae)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> in the hatchery will result in respawning stocks of both larval and fully-grown anglers.

## Notes
- There is a group of three [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md) near the hatchery. They may become tame once the hatchery has been constructed (see [bugs](#Bugs)).
- Frequent [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) (dead and undead), [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md), and bandit spawns may occur here.
- One of the possible locations for a rupture to appear as part of [Aftershock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aftershock) is located just beside the house.
- While living in Windstad Manor, [adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) female children will sometimes say, *"This place is scary. There are monsters in the swamp, I just know it."* - For the reactions given by your spouse, see [this page](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md#Spouse_Dialogue).
- The [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) adds furnishings to your home each time you cross the property border. These borders include: - Go straight east from your entrance door, up the hill. The border is between the two trees growing next to the rocks on your left (there is a third tree on your right).
- North of the house. Past the quarried stone deposit and smelter, there are two trees. The border is just between the trees.
- Original surface items do respawn after 10 consecutive days of the house not being visited.
- The nest on the roof of the Main Hall yields a Rock Warbler Egg rather than the Hawk's Egg spawned at the other two Hearthfire houses. - Version 3.0.11 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch) replaces the Rock Warbler nest on the roof with a Hawk's nest.
- The fish hatchery can support Histcarp, Salmon, and Silverside Perch. The combination of Histcarp, Salmon Roe, and Silverside Perch is one of the most valuable in the game that uses only farmable ingredients. This combination is special in that it only needs the fish hatchery, no other farming resource - Angelfish, Juniper Berries, and Salmon Roe is the highest-value potion using only farmable ingredients. Angelfish, Garlic, and Salmon Roe is of equal value as Garlic, Nordic Barnacle, and Salmon Roe while using two farmable ingredients instead of one.
- Without using the Fishing creation, other high-value potions combine Histcarp, Salmon Roe, and one of Creep Cluster, Jazbay Grapes, Juniper Berries, or Wheat.
