## Thistle
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-flora-Thistle.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Thistle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Thistle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Thistle.jpg) Flowering Thistles Thistle is a hearty plant which mostly grow purple flowers, though one variety is known for its blooms which emit glowing light.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Glow Thistle-1)</sup> They are native to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), where they grow throughout the warmer [holds](Lore_Skyrim.md#Holds); they are also found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Some pieces of women's clothing in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) were adorned with imagery of a thistle.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Thistle Shirt-2)</sup>

- See also: [Milk Thistle](Lore_Milk_Thistle.md)

## Thorn Hook

## Tibrol
[Tibrol](Lore_Tibrol.md) is a kind of tree known to grow in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It's known for its longevity and for serving as nesting spots for [birds](Lore_Bird.md). These trees are referenced in *[the 36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*, specifically in *[Sermon 1](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_1.md)*, where [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) encounters [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md). In *Sermon 1*, it is suggested that the number of Fa-Nuit-Hen's blade corresponds to the number of birds that can inhabit an ancient tibrol tree, minus three grams of honest labor. However, in his later years, Vivec discovers a superior method and shares this knowledge with his people.

**Found in:** - [Lore](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_1.md)

## Tomato
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/OB-flora-Tomato_Plant.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Tomato_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Tomato_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Tomato_Plant.jpg) Tomato Plant Tomato plants are long vines with tomatoes flowering from the stem. They are a common crop on farms in Cyrodiil. In Wrothgar, a variant known as "glacial tomatoes" are grown. Orcs believe the cold soil gives them a richer flavor.

## Tundra Cotton

## Turmeric
