[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Madwoman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Madwoman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madwoman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Madwoman.jpg) Madwoman The **Madwoman** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [priestess](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest) found [wandering around](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Ramblings_of_a_madman) in the wilderness. She carries a pair of [tongs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tongs) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). Due to her lack of gold, she wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps) during her maddened wanderings. She also knows the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).

Starting [The Mind of Madness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness) but not finishing it will make it so that she can be encountered in the wild.

## Related Quests
- **[The Mind of Madness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness)**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Mind of Madness
During an encounter with her, she will spout several lines relating to the quest:

*"Don't go to the Blue Palace. The master is on vacation. Don't listen to [Dervenin](Skyrim_Dervenin.md). Don't do it."* *"The master has gone, but Dervenin will find him. Dervenin should not have gone."* *"Vacation is where the head is. That is what Dervenin told us about our master. He's cozy in his [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)."* If you possess the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) she will request that you use it on her:

*"You have it! The blessed Wabbajack! You must use it on me. You must."* If you have completed the quest The Mind of Madness but don't have it with you, she will instead say:

*"You're the one who returned blessed [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md). I would be honored to be Wabbajacked by you, your grace."* If you refuse her or ignore her, she will return to wandering around and mutter:

*"Wabbajack, Wabbajack, Wabbajack, Wabbajack."* Although you still get a bounty for using the Wabbajack on her, just like any other use of the Wabbajack on a non-hostile [NPC](Skyrim_NPCs.md), it is removed immediately afterward.

## Notes
- Her lines are accompanied by script notes stating that they are the *"mutterings of a mad old woman"*.
