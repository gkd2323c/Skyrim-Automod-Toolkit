## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to the [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [Guard Barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks_(Gate).md) and read the [dossier](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Dossier__Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg.md).
2. Enter [Embershard Mine](Skyrim_Embershard_Mine.md).
3. Kill [Yakhtu gra-Orkulg](Skyrim_Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg.md) and obtain [her journal](Skyrim_Yakhtu%27s_Journal.md) from her body.
4. Read the journal.
5. Kill the bandit chief and retrieve the Orcish Plate armor.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Guard Barracks
To start this quest go to the [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [Guard Barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks_(Gate).md) and read the [dossier](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Dossier__Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg.md) which is found on a shelf in the back room.

### Embershard Mine
Head to [Embershard Mine](Skyrim_Embershard_Mine.md) just southwest of Riverwood. Battle on through the numerous bandits (some noticeably wearing the armor) and make it to the final grand waterfall chamber. Here battle some more bandits including a Chief Bandit wearing the full set and the Orc woman [Yakhtu gra-Orkulg](Skyrim_Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg.md). Loot her body to find [Yakhtu's Journal](Skyrim_Yakhtu%27s_Journal.md). Read it to complete the quest.

Finally loot the Bandit Chief to acquire an enchanted full set. Upon leaving the mine you'll have several sets of Orcish Plate Armor including the enchanted one.

## Notes
1. As detailed on the [Embershard Mine](Skyrim_Embershard_Mine.md) page, the actual chief bandit in here is not actually designated a "Bandit Chief" by name, and appears to just a normal "Bandit", until you search his corpse and find out that he's carrying the good stuff.
2. If the cave is already in a "Cleared" state when you enter it, and has not respawned normally, a few bandits including the chief will still be spawned. The chief will still drop his full enchanted set, but there is no guarantee of getting a full unenchanted set of Orcish Plate from the few bandits in the cave if it has not had time for a full respawn.
3. As a result of the cave having a maximum level as well as a minimum, Yakhtu (min level 15, scales up with player) will be considerably tougher than the bandit chief she technically serves (max level 10).
4. Yakhtu has a named key, which opens a locked chest of smithing supplies next to the table in the large room.
