# Order of the Dragon

[![Order of the Dragon](https://images.uesp.net/7/7c/Order Of The Dragon.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Order Of The Dragon.gif) The Order of the Dragon is an order of [knights](Lore_Knights.md) originally founded by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md). Throughout the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md)'s history, there have been seven Champions of Cyrodiil, the highest rank in the order, with the seventh being the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
