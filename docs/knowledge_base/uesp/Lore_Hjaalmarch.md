[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg/200px-SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg) Hjaalmarch highlighted within Skyrim, [4E 182](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_182) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg) Hjaalmarch circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Hjaalmarch or **Hjaalmarch Hold**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOTAM-1)</sup> is a lowland coastal [hold](Lore_Hold.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THOS-2)</sup> Roughly half the hold consists of the [Drajkmyr](Lore_Drajkmyr.md) marsh,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POGG-3)</sup> south of which is the capital and only major settlement [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md) (although [Stonehills](Lore_Stonehills.md) and some farmsteads lie on the tundra of [Hailstone Valley](Lore_Hailstone_Valley.md)).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THOS-2)</sup> The region is veined with waterways that empty into the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), and the [Skyborn Range](Lore_Skyborn_Range.md) further isolates the area from the eastern and southern border.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MOS-4)</sup>

Hjaalmarch is home to the Karth delta,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Aslfur-5)</sup> also called the Mouths of the Karth,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-WMC-6)</sup> where the mighty [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) and the smaller [River Hjaal](Lore_River_Hjaal.md) (whence the hold's name is derived)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GWSH-7)</sup> empty into the Sea of Ghosts through a variety of intricate channels. The [Deathbell](Lore_Deathbell.md) flower is known to be abundant throughout the swamps.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-HGTS-8)</sup> Many ruins dot the hold, including the legendary [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Skyrim-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AMM-10)</sup> Combined with the fog that surrounds the marshes and the creatures that stalk the land ([Wispmothers](Lore_Wispmother.md) purportedly originated here), Hjaalmarch is considered one of the more mysterious holds in Skyrim.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Skyrim-9)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TW-11)</sup>

Hjaalmarch has the lowest taxes of the nine holds, but it is also the poorest hold.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POGG-3)</sup>
