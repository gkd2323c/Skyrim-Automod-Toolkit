# Carvain

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/OB-interior-Castle_Bruma_Great_Hall.jpg/200px-OB-interior-Castle_Bruma_Great_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Castle_Bruma_Great_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Castle_Bruma_Great_Hall.jpg) Castle Bruma, seat of the Carvains The Carvains are a noble [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) family associated with the city of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md). The family are [Nibenean](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nibenean) [Heartlanders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heartlander) and were the rulers of County Bruma at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

## History
Queen [Euraxia Tharn](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md) of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) employed one Captain Carvain as a knight during the [Euraxian](Lore_Euraxians.md) occupation of [Northern Elsweyr](Lore_Northern_Elsweyr.md). He was slain during an assault on Star Haven Adeptorium circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

By the end of the Third Era, Countess [Narina Carvain](Lore_Narina_Carvain.md) served as the ruler of County Bruma. She was noted as a respected ruler and dutiful [chapelgoer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Chapel), but was known as a cunning and ruthless negotiator due to her reputation for sharp dealing and treachery.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> She had a great interest in collecting [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) artifacts, and funded an expedition to the then-lost [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) to recover the [Draconian Madstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Draconian_Madstone).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> She was also the first Cyrodilic ruler to recognize [Martin Septim](Lore_Martin_Septim.md) as Emperor, when he led the Defense of Bruma against the forces of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) during the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) nobleman [Marcius Carvain](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Marcius_Carvain) served as Viscount Bruma. He is known for authoring *[An Explorer's Guide to Skyrim](Lore_An_Explorer%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*, wherein he recounts the many [Stones of Fate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stones_of_Fate) he encountered on his journeys throughout the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Plautis Carvain and his wife Salonia Carvain set out from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) on foot to reach [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) in time for the wedding of [Vittoria Vici](Lore_Vittoria_Vici.md) and Asgeir Snow-Shod. The couple hoped to convince the bride to use her connections with the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) to secure an import deal, with the ultimate goal of gaining an audience with Vittoria's cousin, Emperor [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md). Despite hiring an armed bodyguard, the couple never made it to the wedding, having encountered a myriad of dangers along the road.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
