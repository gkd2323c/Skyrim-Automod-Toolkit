Q'fura **the Pendulous** was a powerful [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md) and ice mage. They possessed great magical abilities, deemed too hazardous for the mages such as [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) Loremaster [Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md) to confront single-handedly. They infiltrated the Dreaming Cave on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) with the intention of unleashing hordes of Oblivion upon the world.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R2-2)</sup>

## History
Little is known about Q'fura, including their past and affiliations. Although their attack on Artaeum occurred around the same time as the actions of the Abyssal Cabal in [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), there is no evidence of their collaboration. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), they appeared in the Dreaming Cave beneath [Ceporah Tower](Lore_Ceporah_Tower.md) on Artaeum. The Psijics themselves were unaware of how such a feat was possible. Loremaster Celarus, uncertain about how Q'fura arrived there, recognized the imminent danger of the renegade mage opening the Void Gate and unleashing [Oblivion's](Lore_Oblivion.md) [hordes](Lore_Daedra.md). To address this threat, Celarus and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) agent [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md) collaborated, successfully thwarting Q'fura's plans and defeating them in "a stunning and rather glutinous explosion."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-R2-2)</sup>
