*Enchanting description:* **Summoned Daedra up to level <mag> are sent back to Oblivion.**

**Banish** sends [conjured](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure) [daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md) back to Oblivion. Simply put, it makes them disappear. Therefore, it either pushes the daedra back or results in a one hit kill.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Banish if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All weapons *[of Annihilating](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#Banish)*
- All weapons *of Banishing*
- All weapons *of Expelling*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Scourge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>

## Spells
The following spells use the Banish group of effects. Following each spell is the specific variant of the effect that it uses:

- [Banish Daedra](Skyrim_Banish_Daedra.md): *Banish* (`0006d22b`; `Banish FFActor50`)
- [Expel Daedra](Skyrim_Expel_Daedra.md): *Banish* (`0006f951`; `Banish FFActor75`)
- [Banish Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banish_Undead)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md)</sup>: *Banish Undead* (`FExxx819`; `cc VSVSSE003_MGEF_Banish Undead`)

## Notes
- This effect is automatically added to all [Bound Weapons](Skyrim_Bound_Weapon.md) if the [Oblivion Binding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Binding) perk has been unlocked.
- For enchanting purposes this effect will yield the most profitable items. It is possible to make an iron dagger with a value greater than 2000 gold with just a petty soul gem and banish.
- Finding merchants that sell items with this effect for disenchantment purposes is only possible when you are level 22 or higher.
- Only the three [elemental atronachs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach) are considered daedra. The three levels of Banish that are buyable as spells and appear on non-custom enchantments are suited to those three daedra; the weakest spell/enchantments can only banish flame atronachs; the middle-tier spell/enchantments can banish flame and frost atronachs, and the strongest can banish all three atronachs. Dremora you can summon [via spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Dremora_Lord) [or staff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanguine_Rose) are able to be dispelled, as well. Dremora conjured through use of the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) cannot be banished; the effect appears when banish is used against them (the purple "Oblivion portal"), but they are not dispelled.
- All conjured creatures (except Ash Guardians) are banished when struck by a banish effect. This also includes familiars.
- While a weak spell won't remove higher level daedra, it will briefly stun them and make them walk backwards, and/or stagger them. It also applies a lingering damage health effect which persists over a few seconds.
- Only *summoned* daedra are affected by this effect. Daedra which appear naturally as regular enemies (which can be corpse-looted after death) cannot be banished, only killed.
