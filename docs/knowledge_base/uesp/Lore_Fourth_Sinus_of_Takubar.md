[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/ON-creature-Flame_Atronach_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Flame_Atronach_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Flame_Atronach_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Flame_Atronach_02.jpg) A Cold-Flame Atronach [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg) A Frost Mare The Fourth Sinus of Takubar (also known as **DOP 9497.15**, called **Takubar** for short) is a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), and the home of the [Cold-Flame Atronachs](Lore_Cold-Flame_Atronach.md). It is a realm of extreme cold, where the material bonds of the bedrock slide apart to flow like lava. It is the inversion of [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md), a realm of extreme heat. Takubar was discovered by the denizens of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) after [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) banished common [Flame Atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) from his realm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> [Frost Mares](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frost_Mare) are theorized to originate from this plane.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md) theorized that the Frost Senche-Panther may be a form of atronach summoned from Fourth Sinus of Takubar.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFS-3)</sup>

It can be assumed that Takubar, like Infernace, is a 'collective realm', a highly organized realm of Oblivion created not by a single Daedra, but as a collective extension of its numerous, less-powerful inhabitants. This type of realm is considered relatively exceptional, although they're also rather mundane and uniform.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
