# Mede Dynasty


As a consequence of the destruction and confusion wrought across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) by the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), Imperial control over the provinces had waned and the Empire began to crumble despite Titus Mede's intervention. Between 4E 1 and [4E 29](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_29), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), and [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) (since renamed Alinor) seceded from the Empire. [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) was devastated by the twin catastrophes of the eruption of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) and an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) invasion, and ceased to be an Imperial province at some point prior to [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DBArano-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:109</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOS-3)</sup><sup>:16</sup> [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) fell to a [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) -backed coup and was absorbed by Alinor to form the third [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RT-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TGW-5)</sup>

Over a century later, the Empire was brought to its knees during the reign of [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md). In [4E 171](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_171), only three years after Titus II was crowned Emperor, the Aldmeri Dominion invaded the Empire and ushered in the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md). When the conflict ended in [4E 175](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_175) under the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), Titus II was forced to renounce [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) as a province because it refused to concede its territory to the Thalmor as the Concordat required.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TGW-5)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Empire was reduced to only [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with the latter embroiled in two rebellions, the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md) in the [Old Holds](Lore_Skyrim.md) and the [Forsworn Rebellion](Lore_Forsworn_Rebellion.md) in [The Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Skyrim-6)</sup>

## Notes
1. | [^](#note_attrebus) | Although it is never stated that Attrebus Mede succeeded his father Titus I as Emperor, it can be assumed that he did since he was Crown Prince as of 4E 48, there was no indication of other heirs to the throne at the time, and the Mede bloodline survived until the time of Titus II. |
| --- | --- |
2. | [^](#note_unknown) | It is unknown how many Mede Emperors reigned between Attrebus and Titus II. Given the gap of 142 years between Attrebus' birth in 4E 26 and Titus II's ascension to the throne in 4E 168, as well as the knowledge that Titus II's father had a sister (Alexia Vici) while Attrebus had no known siblings, Attrebus was most likely not Titus II's father or grandfather. Assuming the former is correct, and assuming no break in the lineage, it can be surmised that at least two or more Medes reigned as emperor between Attrebus and Titus II. |
| --- | --- |
3. | [^](#note_brother) | An unidentified brother of Titus Mede I. As of 4E 48, this brother was kept under surveillance in case he attempted to act against Titus I. |
| --- | --- |
4. | [^](#note_brother2) | Another unidentified brother of Titus Mede I. As of 4E 48, he was deceased, having built a cabin in the township of Ione fifteen years prior, which he had left to his nephew Attrebus Mede. |
| --- | --- |
5. | [^](#note_souls) | At the end of *[Lord of Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Lord_of_Souls)*, Attrebus Mede and Annaïg Hoïnart, who had fallen in love during their adventures, resolved to spend their lives together. Whether this resulted in matrimony or otherwise remains uncertain. |
| --- | --- |
6. | [^](#note_alexia) | Vittoria Vici is said to be the "first cousin" of Titus Mede II, however, it is unknown if Vittoria is his cousin on his mother's side or his father's side. Correspondingly, it is unknown what Alexia Vici's familial connection to the Mede family is—whether or not she's the sister or sister-in-law of Titus II's father. |
| --- | --- |
7. | [^](#note_db) | Depending on player choice, Titus Mede II may have been murdered by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) on the orders of Councilor [Amaund Motierre](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Amaund_Motierre).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> |
| --- | --- |
