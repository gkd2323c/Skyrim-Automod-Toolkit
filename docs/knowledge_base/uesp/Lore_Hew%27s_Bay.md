[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Hubalajad%27s_Bay.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hubalajad%27s_Bay.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hubalajad%27s_Bay.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hubalajad%27s_Bay.jpg) Hew's Bay Hew's Bay<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONNicolas-1)</sup> (formerly known as **Hubalajad's Bay**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mot HBTGAYQ-2)</sup> is the body of water that encompasses south-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), around the eastern coast of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) and the cities of [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md) and [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mot HBTGAYQ-2)</sup> It is named after Prince [Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md), the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) noble that founded the aforementioned region of Hew's Bane, but his efforts throughout the years ended in failure and tarnished his name.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IDo PH-3)</sup>

The bay also contains smaller locations such as the village of [Chaseguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chaseguard), the towns of Lainebon Place and [Roseguard](Lore_Roseguard.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup> as well as landmarks like No Shira Citadel. The bay opens up from the narrow mouth to the south near Abah's Landing, where it meets with the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), further down leads to other major cities like [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), as well as the [Brena River](Lore_Brena_River.md). Hew's Bane north coast is riddled with steep mountains, leaving no room for any harbor or landing on the bay.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONMap-5)</sup> The [Irk River](Lore_Irk_River.md) and the [Tark River](Lore_Tark_River.md) are found on the northern end of the bay.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RGMap-6)</sup> Trading ships of the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md), typically from [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), sail through the bay for passage to the ports of Gilane, Taneth, and Rihad.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EECMap-7)</sup>
