Chim-el Adabal: A Ballad *A poem surrounding the creation of the Chim-el Adabal* *
---
When [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) slew [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md),
He ripped [his heart](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md) right out,
Hurled it across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),
And the heart was heard to shout:

Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
The heart and soul of [Men](Lore_Man.md).
Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
Protect us till the end.

The laughing heart sprayed blood afar,
A gout on [Cyrod](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) fell,
And like a dart shot to its mark
Down in an [Ayleid Well](Lore_Ayleid_Wells.md).

Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
The heart and soul of Men.
Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
Protect us till the end.

[Magicka](Lore_Magic.md) fused the Lorkhan blood
To crystal red and strong
Then [Wild Elves](Lore_Ayleids.md) cut and polished it down
To [Chim-el Adabal](Lore_Chim-el_Adabal.md).

Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
The heart and soul of Men.
Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
Protect us till the end.

When Elves [lost Nirn to Man](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md),
Akatosh gave the stone
To [Saint Alesh](Lore_Alessia.md) in token of
Her right to sit the throne.

Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
The heart and soul of Men.
Red Diamond! Red Diamond!
Protect us till the end.
