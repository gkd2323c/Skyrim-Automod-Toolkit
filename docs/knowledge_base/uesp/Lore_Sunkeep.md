[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/AR-place-Sunkeep.jpg/200px-AR-place-Sunkeep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Sunkeep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Sunkeep.jpg) Sunkeep during the Imperial Simulacrum Sunkeep is a town found in westernmost [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), specifically in the rainforests of [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-1)</sup> in an elevated area near the shorelines of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) and [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap-3)</sup>

The town of Sunkeep has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap-3)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Sunkeep was an active settlement. It was ruled by Prince Alazen and had a rivalry with Lainebon Place.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Sunkeep is neighbored by several settlements, including [Chasetown](Lore_Chasetown.md) to the north and [Dragon Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dragon_Grove) to the southwest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup>
