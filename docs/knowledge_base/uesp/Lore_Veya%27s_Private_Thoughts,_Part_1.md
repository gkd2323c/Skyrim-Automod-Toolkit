Veya's Private Thoughts, Part 1
Leaving Morrowind **by [Veya Releth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Veya_Releth)** *The journal of an exiled House Redoran noble*
---
For the first time in my life, I'm alone. I've felt loneliness before, but what I feel now is a crushing weight that smothers me. It fills my every thought. Clouds my every judgment.

Should I be angry? Sad? Relieved? I'm getting a new start. My mentor, [Naryu](Lore_Naryu_Virian.md), guaranteed that. I'm to report to [a Khajiit](Lore_Razum-dar.md) who I am assured will look after me. Someone who will continue to train me, though in a very different way from the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md). My past will be wiped away.

But is this what I want? Another person to tell me what I can and cannot do? To forget where I came from and the pain I felt from my own family's actions? That's what made me who I am. What shapes me.

Can I really forget all that and leave [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) behind me?
