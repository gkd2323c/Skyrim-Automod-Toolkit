[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-npc-Agent_Lorcalin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Agent_Lorcalin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agent_Lorcalin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agent_Lorcalin.jpg) Agent Lorcalin **Agent Lorcalin** is a [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) [wizard](Skyrim_Thalmor_Wizard_(class).md) that can be found near a [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos) in [White River Gorge](Skyrim_White_River.md), but only after you've approached the shrine. He is carrying out his [orders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lorcalin%27s_Orders) from [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md) to hunt down any "heretics" at the shrine. He and a [Thalmor soldier](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md) will appear at the shrine once you get close to it, but will not be present before then.

He wears the standard [robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Boots), and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Gloves) of a Thalmor wizard, and wields either a steel, elven, or glass [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), depending on [your level](Skyrim_Thalmor.md#Leveled_Equipment). He also carries his orders and a random selection of [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), and gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Elisif's Tribute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elisif%27s_Tribute)**: Take Torygg's war horn to the Shrine of Talos.
