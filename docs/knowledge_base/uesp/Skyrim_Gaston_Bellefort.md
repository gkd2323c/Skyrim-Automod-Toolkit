[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-creature-Gaston_Bellefort.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Gaston_Bellefort.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gaston_Bellefort.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gaston_Bellefort.jpg) Gaston Bellefort's remains The bones of **Gaston Bellefort** lie in a puddle of water within the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), near the pet [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) [Lis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lis). If you search the bones, they contain a [hastily scribbled note](Skyrim_Hastily_Scribbled_Note.md). According to the note, he found the location of the Sanctuary and was determined to find a way inside, so it's likely that once he did, he was caught and fed to Lis. The book *[The Night Mother's Truth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Night_Mother%27s_Truth)* is also credited to him.
