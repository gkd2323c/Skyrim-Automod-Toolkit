*Alchemy description:* **Resist <mag>% of poison for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases Poison Resistance by <mag>%.**

**Resist Poison** increases the target's resistance to damage from [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md). Resist Poison 50% will halve any poison damage the target takes. The Resist Poison effect must be applied before poison damage begins.

Resist Poison is capped at 85%; i.e., you cannot gain poison immunity.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Resist Poison if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- [Necklace of Poison Immunity](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necklace_of_Poison_Immunity)
- [Necklace of Poison Resistance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necklace_of_Poison_Resistance)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

## Powers
- [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) and [Redguards](Skyrim_Redguard.md) both have a constant Resist Poison 50% ability.
- Becoming a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) gives you a constant Resist Poison 100% ability.
- The [Snakeblood](Skyrim_Snakeblood.md) perk gives you a constant Resist Poison 50% ability.

## Food
- [Argonian Bloodwine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Bloodwine) 40 pts for 50 secs

## Notes
- Unlike in previous games, Argonians are not immune to poison.
- The poison gas room in Calcelmo's Laboratory in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) ignores poison resistance; it is not possible to immunize yourself from the gas by wearing Hevnoraak's mask or being a vampire.
