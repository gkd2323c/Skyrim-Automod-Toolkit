# Unstars

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg/200px-MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-birthsign-Serpent.jpg) Four unstars forming the Serpent Unstars, also known as **not-stars**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S33-1)</sup> are [celestial objects](Lore_Astronomy.md) that resemble [stars](Lore_Stars.md). Unlike stars, unstars move across the sky and do not emit [varliance](Lore_Varliance.md), though they still emit [light](Lore_Elements.md#Light). The nature of this light and its effect on mortals remains uncertain.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup>

The [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) of [the Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md), comprised of four unstars,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Redguard-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Birthsigns-4)</sup> is not confined to appearing in the night sky at specific times of the year.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Cosmology-7)</sup> The motions of unstars that form the Serpent are deemed unpredictable. Although they can be forecasted to some extent,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Coyle-8)</sup> sometimes it is deemed impossible.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FTN-9)</sup>

Like stars, unstars are associated with [Magna Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md), though their connection is less clear. [Unala-Se](Lore_Unala-Se.md) taught the stars to be immovable, then "the unstars came, and still she taught them to be undevourable". [Xero-Lyg](Lore_Xero-Lyg.md) was carried among unstars and cataloged events through time and untime.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9C-10)</sup>

## Notes
- [Sermon 33](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_33.md) of the Lessons of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) asserts that the Serpent constellation is "made of not-stars, whose number is thirteen."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S33-1)</sup> From Vivec's [29th Sermon](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_29.md), this number refers to the Constellation's place as the thirteenth constellation, "13. The Serpent", rather than the unstars in general.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-S29-11)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup> The Serpent constellation itself consists of only four unstars.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Redguard-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Birthsigns-4)</sup>
- The [Star-Gazers](Lore_Star-Gazers.md) of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) were known for studying the phenomenon of the unstars under the direction of the High Astrologer [Caecilus Bursio](Lore_Caecilus_Bursio.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup>
