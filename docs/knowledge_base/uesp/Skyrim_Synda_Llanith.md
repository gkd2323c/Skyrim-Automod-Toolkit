[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-npc-Synda_Llanith.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Synda_Llanith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Synda_Llanith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Synda_Llanith.jpg) Synda at work **Synda Llanith** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who works and lives at [Merryfair Farm](Skyrim_Merryfair_Farm.md) with her husband [Dravin](Skyrim_Dravin_Llanith.md). She believes in honest pay for honest work and will [pay you](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md) for harvested [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), and [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd).

She rises early at 5am and always starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. She then heads outside and works the farm all day long, interrupted only by a one-hour dinner at 6pm. At 11pm she heads back inside and goes to bed.

She wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f) and wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 20). She carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

*"After we were robbed, Dravin's become more bitter than ever. He hates this place."* *"I'm afraid we have little to offer you in the way of hospitality."* *"You'll have to forgive my husband's manners. He's not used to pleasantries."* *"The war's been hard on the farm and hard on Dravin. We don't know what to expect from one day to the next."* *"Someday, I hope we can get away from here... perhaps move back to Morrowind. This isn't at all what we expected."* After you complete her husband's [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bow_to_the_Master):

*"Now that you've brought Dravin's bow back, he's back to his crabby old self. For better or for worse, I still thank you."*

## Notes
- It is best to gather Synda's wheat while you are still comparatively low leveled: since there is a wildlife spawning point very close to the farm, and despite the best efforts of the local guards who also patrol the farm, either or both of the Llaniths may be killed by wildlife, most usually bears. It doesn't help that they have a tendency to rush into combat, rather than run away and leave the fight to the guards. They are also vulnerable to potential dragon attacks that spawn just outside Riften.
