Journal **by [Kornalus](Skyrim_Kornalus.md)** *About experiments for infusing elemental energy into Frostbite Spiders* *
---

- **Notes and Results**

- It would seem the [smaller specimens](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) cannot survive the infusion process. Application of the serum results in a short burst of elemental energy, followed by death. I will have to change the formula before trying again.

- The [larger subjects](Skyrim_Giant_Frostbite_Spider.md), however, have taken to the transformation nicely. For now, they are stored away in their pens until I can examine them further.
