[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/OBR-place-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg) Bloodrun Cave circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Bloodrun Cave is a natural cave network found in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), on the steep southern banks of the [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> It is notable for the ancient [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) ruins found within, including a sanctuary and tombs for fallen assassins.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

## History
Like all natural caves, these tunnels once served as the capillaries of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), pulsing with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The cave possesses black rock walls and extensive stalagma.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

Bloodrun Cave was once used as an honorable tomb for fallen members of the Dark Brotherhood, including several well-respected [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscales.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mim-Jasa-4)</sup> The Dark Brotherhood also maintained a sanctuary deep within the cave.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-place-Bloodrun_Cave_08.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bloodrun_Cave_08.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bloodrun_Cave_08.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bloodrun_Cave_08.jpg) The Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/OB-interior-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg/200px-OB-interior-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Bloodrun_Cave.jpg) Ruins in the interior Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) Tumma-Maxath invaded the cave to raise the bodies of the dead entombed there. In particular, he sought out the bodies of several renowned Shadowscales in order to heighten his own necromantic power.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> An Argonian [nisswo](Lore_Nisswo.md) named Mim-Jasa was sent to the cave by the [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) -worshipping [Clutch of Nisswo](Lore_Clutch_of_Nisswo.md) to perform the last rites for several of these Shadowscales, but was chased away by Tumma-Maxath's undead minions. Mim-Jasa enlisted the aid of [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in foiling the necromancer's plans by burning the mortal remains of these assassins and killing Tumma-Maxath.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mim-Jasa-4)</sup> The Vestige also destroyed several ancient caches within the cave which contained old information about the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md); the information contained in these documents was of a sensitive nature, and the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) politician Faves Anthones put out a contract to have the documents destroyed.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

That same year, a group of Brotherhood assassins led by Elam Drals were sent to re-establish the Dark Brotherhood sanctuary within Bloodrun Cave. However, the Brotherhood were then framed for the murder of several [Elder Councilors](Lore_Elder_Council.md) in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) by the [Order of the Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md). The Vestige and Eveli Sharp-Arrow entered the sanctuary as agents of Leyawiin and spoke with Elam Drals, who confirmed that the Brotherhood was not behind these assassinations. However, the Waking Flame chose this moment to attack the sanctuary, teleporting inside using [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [portal magic](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md). The group managed to escape with their lives, but the attack resulted in the deaths of many Dark Brothers at the hands of the cultists.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Elam Drals later helped defend the [Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md) at [Fort Redmane](Lore_Fort_Redmane.md) in order to exact his revenge on the Waking Flame for this attack.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Bloodrun Cave had been taken over by a group of necromancers and their undead minions, as had the nearby Kindred Cave located right across the river.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
