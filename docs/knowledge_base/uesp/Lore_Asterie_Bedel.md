# Asterie Bedel

Asterie Bedel was an accomplished [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [blacksmith](Lore_Smithing.md) of the [Late Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#Late_Merethic_Era). She possessed a great talent that drew from both the techniques of [men](Lore_Man.md) and [mer](Lore_Mer.md) in crafting her exceptional wares. Her unmistakable signature, a mage's knot, is engraved within the heart of the blade, reflecting the earliest traditions of Bretonic culture. Among her remarkable creations is the mighty [Chrysamere](Lore_Chrysamere.md), a legendary two-handed [adamantium](Lore_Adamantium.md) claymore,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAIA-1)</sup> and a potent artifact with offensive capabilities only surpassed by its defenses.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR.2FDF-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TL-4)</sup> The historical account makes Asterie Bedel one of the earliest known Bretons in history.

Asterie Bedel's name endured through the ages and was recorded in the Infinite Archive.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAIA-1)</sup> While it remains uncertain if there is any familial connection, a Breton smith named Huet Bedel resided in [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) during the turbulent times of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HB-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SS-6)</sup>

## Notes
- With her place in the timeline, Asterie Bedel would specifically be a Manmer, or in other words, a Half-Elf.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1High-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TBMOP-8)</sup> These early Bretons were more elven in appearance than their modern counterparts.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TBMOP-8)</sup>
