[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-npc-Erdi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Erdi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erdi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erdi.jpg) Erdi **Erdi** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) maid living in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). She is involved in the quest [The Mind of Madness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness), where she provides you with the key to the [Pelagius Wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing) of the palace.

Erdi wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1da) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). She carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[The Mind of Madness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness)**: Gain access to and solve the mystery of the [Pelagius Wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing).

## Dialogue
When approached, she will greet you with:

*"Oh, hello! Just tidying up."* *"So exciting when new folk come into town. I bet you've had plenty of adventures."* If your character is female, Erdi will talk idly about her aspirations:

*"Maybe you need a partner? Hmm? Just picture it. Two women, the dusty road, strapping men at every tavern, a fortune in gold to be claimed..."* *"Someday, I'll be a gallant heroine. You make it look so.. appealing."* If your character is male, she will keep talking about her dream for a chivalrous hero to arrive and take her away:

*"Are you my gallant hero? Have you come to whisk me away, to a life of adventure and romance?"* *"I made a wish in the town well that a golden knight came to take me to his castle. Oh, does it spoil the wish if I tell?"* *"Someday, a gallant hero will ride up and take me away. Someday..."* Despite this misleading dialogue, she cannot be [married](Skyrim_Marriage.md) or become a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Mind of Madness
Telling her that you need to get into the Pelagius Wing:

**I need to get into the Pelagius Wing.** *"Oh, I'd get in trouble for that. It's not allowed. Besides, it's scary in there!"* **Falk asked me to check it out.** *"Well... I suppose in that case, it's okay to let you in. But only briefly. Be careful, and come right back!"* She will then give you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pelagius_Wing_Key) to the wing.
