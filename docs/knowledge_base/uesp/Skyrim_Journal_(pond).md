Journal *About a woman's fatal trip to her favorite swimming hole* *
---

- Father wouldn't like it if he caught me swimming out here again. But I just can't help myself. [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) sightings be damned, I love the way the cool water feels on my skin. Besides, with the war raging, those savages won't dare leave the safety of their tents. So I think I'll head down to my special spot one more time.
