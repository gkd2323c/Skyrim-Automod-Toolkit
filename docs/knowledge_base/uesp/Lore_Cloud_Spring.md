[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/AR-place-Cloud_Spring.jpg/200px-AR-place-Cloud_Spring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Cloud_Spring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Cloud_Spring.jpg) Cloud Spring Cloud Spring is a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) town located in northeastern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), southeast of [Farrun](Lore_Farrun.md) and southwest of [Jehanna](Lore_Jehanna.md). Cloud Spring has road connections to Farrun, Jehanna, and [Dunlain Falls](Lore_Dunlain_Falls.md). The town has a rivalry with the town of [Raven Spring](Lore_Raven_Spring.md), and is known for its clear waters from the spring.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-A-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPet-2)</sup>

The settlement was active as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), where it was known for a breed of white sheep herded by the doughty shepherds, rumored to have the ability to sense the many dangers nearby and avoid peril.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPet-2)</sup> Its ruler during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) was Prince Edwyval.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-A-1)</sup>
