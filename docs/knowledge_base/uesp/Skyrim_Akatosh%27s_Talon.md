It is acquired through the [Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md) quest. If you are on the Stormcloaks side, it will be used by the [Imperial Champion](Skyrim_Imperial_Champion.md) and can be acquired after defeating him, however, if you are on the Imperial's side it will be given to you to use against the [Stormcloak Champion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Champion).
