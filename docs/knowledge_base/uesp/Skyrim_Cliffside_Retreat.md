[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/SR-place-Cliffside_Retreat.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cliffside_Retreat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cliffside_Retreat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cliffside_Retreat.jpg) Cliffside Retreat **Cliffside Retreat** is a small [shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) south of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md).

The shack is inhabited by a lone bear [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md), who spends all day sitting on the wooden ledge overlooking [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md) and does not sleep. The shack has a bed that is free to use.

## Walkthrough
The small open shack contains an unowned bed with a set of shelves beside it. All the items in and around the shack and on the wooden platform are marked as owned, and taking them is considered stealing. The shelves hold a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Marksmanship Lesson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Marksmanship_Lesson)*, three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), two [carrots](Skyrim_Carrot.md), and a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). Leaning against the foot of the bed are a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) and a quiver of [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). The south wall of the hut is open with a deck attached. On the deck is a table with two [cave bear pelts](Skyrim_Cave_Bear_Pelt.md), a [bear pelt](Skyrim_Bear_Pelt.md), and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) on it. Underneath the table are two [bear traps](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md), and by the corner of the shack is a respawning barrel of foodstuffs.

Behind the shack, to the north, is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), while against the outside northeast wall is an open crate containing two more bear pelts and a cave bear pelt, with a set bear trap to the right. There is an unlit campfire a few paces east of the shack, near the wooden platform, where the [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md) sits. Beside him is an open-topped wooden box containing three red apples. There are several [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) bushes around the site as well.

## Notes
- The only container in the area is the barrel, which [respawns](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and is therefore not safe to store items in.
