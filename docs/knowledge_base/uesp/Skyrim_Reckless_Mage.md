[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-npc-Reckless_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reckless_Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reckless_Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reckless_Mage.jpg) Reckless Mage The **Reckless Mage**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md), can be the target of the recurring quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He spawns inside the [Frozen Hearth](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md).

He will just wander around the taproom, at times sitting down for a drink or a bite to eat.

He wears a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) and a random pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) or [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and knows a few spells, specifically [lightning bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [ice spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md), [fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md) and [healing](Skyrim_Healing.md).
