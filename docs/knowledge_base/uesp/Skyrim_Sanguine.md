[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-npc-Sanguine.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sanguine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sanguine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sanguine.jpg) The Daedric Prince Sanguine **Sanguine** is a [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) whose sphere is hedonistic revelry, debauchery, and passionate indulgences of darker natures. He is initially met under the disguised persona of [Sam Guevenne](Skyrim_Sam_Guevenne.md).

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Sanguine.md).

Sanguine wears [Daedric armor](Skyrim_Daedric_Armor.md) with matching [gauntlets](Skyrim_Daedric_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Daedric_Boots.md), and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md)**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](Skyrim_Sam_Guevenne.md), as you recover from a bad hangover.

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### A Night To Remember

- *"I really just needed something to encourage you to go out into the world and spread merriment. And you did just that! I haven't been so entertained in at least a hundred years."*

- - **So all of this was just a prank?**
- *"Just a prank? Just a prank? The Daedric Lord of Debauchery does not deal in mere "pranks." This may have begun as a minor amusement, but it wasn't long before I realized you'd make a more interesting bearer of my not-quite-holy staff."*

- - **Who are you?**
- *"I am Sanguine, Daedric Prince of Debauchery! I know, I know, how could I lie to you? Well, how could I trust you until we've shared a few drinks? But it wasn't long before I realized you'd make a more interesting bearer of my not-quite-holy staff than this waste of flesh."*

Either leads to:

- **Why did you choose me?**
- *"Let's be honest, here. I don't always think my decisions through. But you... you're going places. Maybe a little influence from your old uncle Sanguine could help adjust your course a bit..."*

- **Thanks, I guess.**
- *"My pleasure. But I think it's time for you to go. No fun keeping you locked up in here with the staff."*

## Unused Dialogue
Regardless of which option you chose in dialogue when first speaking to him; after Sanguine mentioned his "not-quite-holy staff" you were meant to get another reply:

- **But the staff is broken.**
- *"Details, details. The Sanguine Rose breaks all the time and is fixed with but a thought. In fact, here it is now. Think of it - a never-ending stream of minions. And all of the prestige of being Sanguine's chosen."*

However, it seems that this reply was deliberately disabled by the developers, as it is attached to stage 10000 of the quest, which doesn't exist and never seems to have existed in the first place.

## Notes
- Sanguine cannot be pickpocketed.
- He is one of three Daedric Princes to make an appearance in humanoid form, the others being [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md) and [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md).
