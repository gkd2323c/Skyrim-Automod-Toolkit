[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg) Tel Mithryn circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Tel Mithryn is a settlement on the banks of the [Iggnir River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iggnir_River) in southeastern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), made primarily out of four large fungi.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DB-1)</sup> It was planted by Master [Neloth](Lore_Neloth.md) of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) from cuttings of fungi in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), presumably before [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Elynea-2)</sup> The Nordic burial ground of Himmelhost Barrow was once found at the site of the settlement,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BM-3)</sup> but like many of the ruins was later buried by the ash from the eruption of Red Mountain.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jo RS-4)</sup> Relations between Tel Mithryn and the nearby [Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) town of [Raven Rock](Lore_Raven_Rock.md) have been cordial; the two settlements have been content to ignore each other.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Morvayn-5)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Tel Mithryn was plagued by a string of bad luck, with the death of its steward, withering of its fungi and frequent [Ash Spawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ash_Spawn) and [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) attacks. It was later discovered to be the work of [Ildari Sarothril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ildari_Sarothril), who was subsequently killed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> A priest of [Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dagoth_Ur) named Erden Relvel also chose the graveyard at Tel Mithryn as the site of his fated battle against the champion of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ERF-7)</sup> Following a string of [Ash Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ash_Zombie) attacks, the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) followed Erden's summons and defeated him, ending the threat.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
