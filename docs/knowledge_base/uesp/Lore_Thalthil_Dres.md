[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/Lore-people-House_Dres_Elf_Concept.png/200px-Lore-people-House_Dres_Elf_Concept.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Lore-people-House_Dres_Elf_Concept.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Lore-people-House_Dres_Elf_Concept.png) A Dres elf, part of the Great House that Thalthil founded Grandmaster Thalthil Dres was a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) slaver and former Grandmaster of [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md). He is credited as being the one to elevate House Dres to the status of a Great House after his slave-raid on [Thorn](Lore_Thorn.md), which secured the House's position as the primary providers for Morrowind's slave trade.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DGMM-2)</sup>
