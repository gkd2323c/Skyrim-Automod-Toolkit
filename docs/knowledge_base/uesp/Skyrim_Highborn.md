*Regenerate magicka faster for 60 seconds.* **Highborn** is a [Greater Power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Powers) possessed by all [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), which allows them once a day to increase their rate of [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) 25× for 60 seconds.
