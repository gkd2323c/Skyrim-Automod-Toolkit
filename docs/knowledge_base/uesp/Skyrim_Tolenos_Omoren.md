[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/SR-npc-Tolenos_Omoren.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tolenos_Omoren.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tolenos_Omoren.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tolenos_Omoren.jpg) Tolenos Omoren **Tolenos Omoren** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md). He appears after you reopen the mine.

Tolenos wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261C0) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261BD). He carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
