# Black-Briar Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/ON-place-Black-Briar_Lodge.jpg/200px-ON-place-Black-Briar_Lodge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Black-Briar_Lodge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Black-Briar_Lodge.jpg) Black-Briar Lodge circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Black-Briar Clan is an ancient prominent [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) family from [Riften](Lore_Riften.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLeidela-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the matriarch of the Black-Briar family, Leidela Black-Briar, had ties with Krin Ren-dro, a former member of the [Eyes of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLOU-2)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the matriarch of the Black-Briar family, Maven Black-Briar was the owner of Black-Briar Meadery and had ties with both the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) and [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTB-4)</sup>

## Notable Members
- Maven Black-Briar (fl. 4E 201) — Owner of Black-Briar Meadery
