# Pahmar

*When Jode is Full and Jone is New
Then shall be born the Pahmar
Striped warriors who live for battle
Their bloodlust unmatched
You will find them at the forefront of every war
Striking fear in the hearts of those who threaten our kingdoms.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/a/a5/Mfull_Snew.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mfull_Snew.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mfull_Snew.jpg) Pahmar are born when Masser is full and Secunda is new The Pahmar are a [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). The Pahmar are a giant bipedal furstock, who are similar to their larger counterparts, the [Pahmar-raht](Lore_Pahmar-raht.md), and resemble [Tigers](Lore_Tiger.md) or [Senche-Tigers](Lore_Senche-Cat.md#Tigers).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bahzaharthe Bull-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup> Like the Pahmar-raht, the Pahmar, are often employed as bodyguards and warriors, due to their powerful and robust physique.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AOPF-4)</sup> Due to their physique, foreigners will write them off as "big, dumb brutes", though Pahmar themselves can very much be kind and gentle souls.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ember-5)</sup>
