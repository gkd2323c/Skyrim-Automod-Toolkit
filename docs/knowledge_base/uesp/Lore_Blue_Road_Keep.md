[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/OB-map-Blue_Road.jpg/200px-OB-map-Blue_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Blue_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Blue_Road.jpg) Map showing the route of the Blue Road The Blue Road runs east to west between the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The road continues on east past Cheydinhal, into the foothills of the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Borba-1)</sup> It also branches south of Cheydinhal, towards the settlement of [Harlun's Watch](Lore_Harlun%27s_Watch.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

The Blue Road connects to the [Silver Road](Lore_Silver_Road.md) in north-central Cyrodiil via minor dirt paths in and around the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBMap Road-3)</sup> It also extends beyond Cyrodiil's border and into western Morrowind. Northeast past [Fort Farragut](Lore_Fort_Farragut.md) is the Southern Morrowind Gate<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGate-4)</sup> and the first settlement after crossing the border is the city-state; [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARMap MW-6)</sup><sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Gate)</sup>

In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), a military stronghold known as **Blue Road Keep** was located on the Blue Road, among the volcanic hot springs found between Cheydinhal and the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md). During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Blue Road Keep was an expansive and strategically important keep. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was initially located within the territory held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), and its strategic position proved vital for any potential capture of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). The keep changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONKeep-7)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), nothing remained of Blue Road Keep or the surrounding hot springs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup> In [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), when [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) crossed over the Valus Mountains and invaded Cheydinhal. Its people were forced to evacuate and travel down the Blue Road all the way to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LOS-8)</sup><sup>:Pt. 2, Ch. 12</sup>
