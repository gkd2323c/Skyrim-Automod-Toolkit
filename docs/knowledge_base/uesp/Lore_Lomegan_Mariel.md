Lomegan Mariel was Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md)'s Imperial Secretary and Personal Liaison to the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md). During [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s invasion of the Battlespire in [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398), [Sirran Angada](Lore_Sirran_Angada.md), a leechcrafter retainer of the false emperor [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md), used a special Transmorph spell to disguise themselves as Mariel to gain gateway access to the Battlespire, which allowed the forces of [Clan Dagon](Lore_Clan_Dagon.md) to conquer it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSAngada-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTL-2)</sup>

Mariel was struck down and presumably killed after surrendering the Portal Keys of the [Weir Gate](Lore_Weir_Gate.md) to Angada.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mactana-3)</sup>
