*No matter the seed, if the shoot is nurtured with love, will not the flower be beautiful?*
—Sibyl Augustine Viliane<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMAR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-2)</sup> **Lady** Augustine Viliane was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [Sibyl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sibyl) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md)'s chapter of the [House of Dibella](Lore_House_of_Dibella.md). Aside from her role as a high-ranking member of the clergy, she also authored books and letters addressing topics of faith, including *[Dibella's Mysteries and Revelations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella%27s_Mysteries_and_Revelations)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMAR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-2)</sup>

## History
Little is known of Augustine Viliane's past. She joined the House of Dibella and eventually became one of its Sibyls. She resided in Wayrest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SAV-3)</sup> At some point in her life, she lost her sight due to the Great Flu, but when questioned about her blindness, she didn't consider it significant and would express that it didn't impede her ability to dance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMAR-1)</sup>

At one point, on a day in the month of [Second Seed](Lore_Calendar.md), she welcomed several new novices to their vocation in the House of Dibella and answered many of their questions. She recounts that day in her publication *Dibella's Mysteries and Revelations*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMAR-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she answered many questions regarding the faith of Dibella through correspondence. During this process, she received a cryptic message about the supposed location of the [Brush of Truepaint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brush_of_Truepaint) and how it could be obtained. She also addressed many of her correspondents' questions, sharing insights on topics related to the worship of Dibella. Her responses covered the goddess's views on specific aspects of love, the elven lineage within the Breton family of [Dumonte](Lore_Dumonte.md), certain practices of the ancient [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), the extinction of the [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md), and her temple's stance on the [undead](Lore_Undeath.md), among other subjects.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-2)</sup>
