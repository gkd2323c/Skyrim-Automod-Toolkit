#### Dagon, the Demon Cat
**Dagon**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAS-1)</sup> (or **Daegon**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMara.27dahni-2)</sup> **the Demon Cat**, also known as **Merrunz**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAS-1)</sup> **the Fire-Cat**<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONNisuzi-3)</sup> (or **Mehrunnez**)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOGroup Event-4)</sup> is the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) version of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). A child of [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md)'s Second Litter, Merrunz became "destructive and wild", and was exiled by his father, [Ahnurr](Lore_Ahnurr.md). Rather than the Many Paths, he chose to explore the Great Darkness, and there was captured by [Molagh](Lore_Molagh.md) and tortured until the [creation of the World](Lore_Dawn_Era.md#Convention). It is said he was eventually freed by Molagh's wife, and used to assault the [Lunar Lattice](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md). As a result, Merrunz happily became a kinslayer, and as a result is now known as the demon Dagon.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAS-1)</sup>

#### Dagoth Ur, Father of the Mountain
[Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) ([/ˈdeɪˌgɒθ ˈʌɹ/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English) *DAY- **goth** -ERR* Link ⏵ or [/ˈdæ.gə.θəɹ/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English) *DAG- **guh** -therr* Link ⏵), originally **Voryn Dagoth**, also known as the **Sharmat**, as well as **Awakened Lord of the Sixth House**, **Lord Dagoth** and **Father of the Mountain** to his followers, was the immortal Lord High Councilor of [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md). In Tribunal mythology, he is demonized as **the Devil** and **the Enemy**, **False Dreamer**, **Devil Dagoth Ur**, and **Devil of Dagoth**.

#### The Deep Ones
The [Deep Ones](Lore_Deep_Ones.md) are a [race](Lore_Races.md) of beings who dwell deep underground beneath the town of [Hackdirt](Lore_Hackdirt.md), an isolated [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) settlement in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md). They are worshipped as [gods](Lore_Gods.md) by the inhabitants of Hackdirt, who built the Chapel of the Brethren in their honor. Worshippers who have changed the most are known as the Brethren. They possess large eyes and a strong sense of smell, and dwell underground in the Hackdirt Caverns to be closer to the Deep Ones. The other residents descend into the caverns at night for "Gatherings" where they worship the Deep Ones; tunnels were dug connecting each house to the caverns for this purpose.

#### Demon of Fire and Shadow
The entity only known as the [demon of fire and shadow](Lore_Demon_of_Fire_and_Shadow.md) is an obscure being from the ancient [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) mythology predating the [ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) era. [Akha](Lore_Akha.md)'s union with the demon resulted in the birth of [Alkhan](Lore_Alkhan.md), the Firstborn and Scaled Prince of Akha. Following the counsel of his father [Ahnurr](Lore_Ahnurr.md) to seek love, Akha later mated with the [Dune Queen of the West](Lore_Dune_Queen_of_the_West.md), [Mother Mammoth of the North](Lore_Mother_Mammoth_of_the_North.md), and the [Winged Serpent of the East](Lore_Winged_Serpent_of_the_East.md), giving rise to numerous [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) wayward children.

#### Diagna, Orichalc God of the Sideways Blade
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Diagna_Statuette.png) [Diagna](Lore_Diagna.md), (also known as the **Orichalc God of the Sideways Blade**) was an avatar of the [Hoon Ding](Lore_HoonDing.md) (the Yokudan God of Make Way) that achieved permanence as a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) sword-god. His "hoary thuggish cult" originated in [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) during the Twenty Seven Snake Folk Slaughter. He was instrumental to the defeat of the [Lefthanded Elves](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md), as he brought [orichalc](Lore_Orichalcum.md) [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) to the Yokudan people to win the fight. According to one myth, Diagna crashed his fist against the Hoon Ding Gong and swords fell from the walls of the Orichalc Tower.

In [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), he led a very tight-knit group of followers, called the [Order of Diagna](Lore_Order_of_Diagna.md), against the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) during the height of their ancient power, but then faded into partial obscurity. He is described as now being little more than a local power spirit of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) but he is still worshipped as of the [4th Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), being part of the Eight of the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md).

High Kings of Hammerfell are crowned on the Throne of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md) by wearing the [Diadem of Diagna](Lore_Diadem_of_Diagna.md). Warriors of the Order of Diagna carried stone effigies of Diagna during the siege. The [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md)'s alliance of the Orcs and Redguards was alleged by the Unveiled Azadiyeh to have inflamed Diagna's very sword-arm with the Crimson Rash of Betrayal.

#### Dibella, Goddess of Beauty
[Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md), also known as **the Lady**, the **Passion Dancer**, **Our Blessed Lady**, **Lady of Love**, and **Queen of Heaven**, is the **Goddess of Beauty**, Love, Affection, Art, and Music. She is one of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md).

#### Druagaa, Goddess of Flowers
[Druagaa](Lore_Druagaa.md) is the old Goddess of Flowers, worship of whom has all but died out. A festival named [Gardtide](Lore_Gardtide.md) is still held in her honor on the 1st of [Rain's Hand](Lore_Rain%27s_Hand.md) every year by the people of [Tamarilyn Point](Lore_Tamarilyn_Point.md), a coastal region of [Menevia](Lore_Menevia_(county).md) in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

#### Dune Queen of the West
The [Dune Queen of the West](Lore_Dune_Queen_of_the_West.md) is an obscure entity from the ancient [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) mythology predating the [ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) era.

The Dune Queen is one of [Akha](Lore_Akha.md)'s mates. In the earliest days, when [Ahnurr](Lore_Ahnurr.md) and [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md) were deeply in love, Akha ventured across the heavens, his trails forming the Many Paths. His father told him to seek love akin to that of Ahnurr and Fadomai. Akha united with the Dune Queen of the West. He also had other mates, including the [Mother Mammoth of the North](Lore_Mother_Mammoth_of_the_North.md) and the [Winged Serpent of the East](Lore_Winged_Serpent_of_the_East.md).

However, upon venturing to the South Akha never returned. [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md) and his loyal guardians oversee the numerous [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) children of Akha, some of whom were from the union with the Dune Queen of the West, "for they are both terrible and kind".<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>
