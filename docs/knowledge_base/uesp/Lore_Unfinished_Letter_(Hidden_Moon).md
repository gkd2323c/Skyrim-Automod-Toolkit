Unfinished Letter *An unfinished letter warning about an internal threat to the Hidden Moon* *
---
Shando-ri,

Word has reached our small shrine of danger from within our order. A ma'hadaliit from [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md) arrived having fled her cloister to warn us. She says [Arum-Khal](Lore_Arum-Khal.md) has forsaken [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) and sways the brothers and sisters of [our order](Lore_Order_of_the_Hidden_Moon.md) to follow [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md).

Can this be true? How could such a thing happen? We beg you for clarity. As we have no moonclaw-armed adepts here, I will send this letter by courier and hope it reaches you swiftly at Ashen Scar.

Until we hear back, we will pray the wards of our shrine hold against those that seek only to harm. If they do not, know that we will fight until our last to bring Azurah's fallen children back from the dar—
