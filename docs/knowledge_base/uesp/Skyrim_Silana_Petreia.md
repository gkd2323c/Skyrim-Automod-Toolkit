[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-npc-Silana_Petreia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silana_Petreia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silana_Petreia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silana_Petreia.jpg) Silana Petreia [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Rorlund_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rorlund_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rorlund_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rorlund_02.jpg) Rorlund and Silana speaking. **Silana Petreia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [priestess](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest) who lives and works in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) at the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md). She can be found wandering around Solitude or in the Temple. She wears [hooded monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Monk_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000bacd7). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries some [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

Silana starts her day by waking up at 6am and leaving the temple to go on a relaxing stroll through Solitude. She will head to [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md) and stand outside the store for the next three hours to watch the sunrise. At 9am, she will make her way back to the temple where she will perform her duties as a minister. She will be found standing near the various [shrines](Skyrim_Shrines.md) inside the temple for the next five hours. At 2pm, she will leave the temple again, this time to head to the Solitude marketplace to shop at the various market stalls for the next three hours. Once she has finished shopping, she will head back to the temple and will continue to perform her duties until midnight. After a busy day of working at the temple, Silana will head upstairs and sleep in one of the beds for the next six hours. The only exception to this schedule is when she attends [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md)'s execution when you first enter Solitude.

## Dialogue
Silana seems to be very devout, and this can be seen in her greetings:

*"The Divines saved me from the streets, and they can save you from your trials, as well."* *"May the Divines guide and keep you."* *"May you be saved by the Divines, as I once was."* Inside of the temple, she may also greet you by saying:

*"Blessings to you. The Divines are listening."* She can also greet you with prayer lines she shares with her fellow priests and [Greta](Skyrim_Greta.md):

*"Let us walk in the steps of the Divines."* *"Remind us of our place, when we forget."* *"May knowledge of your Divinity carry us through our daily trials."* When exiting dialogue she can say:

*"The Temple of Divines is always open for worshipers."* *"Take care, and go with the blessings of the Eight."* (If the city is under Imperial control) *"Take care, and go with the blessings of the Nine."* (If the city is under Stormcloak control) When Roggvir is executed, she whispers:

*"Talos protect you."*
## Conversations
Inside of the temple, she can often be seen conversing with her fellow faithful:

**[Freir](Skyrim_Freir.md)**: *"Certainly, acolyte. What troubles you?"*
**Silana**: *"I know you and father [Rorlund](Skyrim_Rorlund.md) have many duties to attend to in the temple, but sometimes I feel that there are more important matters to attend to."*
**Freir**: *"What could be more important than hearing the prayers of the faithful, and answering the questions of the dedicated?"*
**Silana**: *"Mistress, the people of Solitude are destitute. Many are starving and desperate for work. We need to be more active, to reach out to them."*
**Freir**: *"We took you in, didn't we? We would be glad to extend our welcome to any other who showed your dedication. What more could we do?"*
**Silana**: *"I know, and I appreciate it dearly. But I can't help but feel we could be doing more..."*
**Freir**: *"[Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) has blessed you with tremendous compassion, Silana. You will find a way, I'm sure of it."* **Rorlund**: *"Silana, you don't seem yourself. Is something the matter?"*

**Silana**: *"I respectfully disagree, sir. [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) and [Zenithar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zenithar) may demand strength from them, but [Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mara) and [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella) demand compassion from us."*
