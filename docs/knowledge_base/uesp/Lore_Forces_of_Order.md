# Forces of Order

The Forces of Order are the minions of [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Order. They are seen as an unstoppable force, who invade the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) at the end of every era in an event known as the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The Forces of Order consist of three parts:

- The [Knights of Order](Lore_Knights_of_Order.md), soulless abominations that invade the Isles via [Obelisks of Order](Lore_Obelisk_of_Order.md);<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIKiliban-3)</sup>
- The [Priests of Order](Lore_Priests_of_Order.md), mortal inhabitants of the Isles who betray [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) and devote themselves to facilitating the invasion;<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
- Jyggalag, whose appearance marks the conclusion of the Greymarch and the desolation of the realm.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TEo O-5)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Greymarch was ended when the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) mantled Sheogorath and defeated Jyggalag in single combat.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TEo O-5)</sup>
