# Unique Items


Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md) article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Other Unique Items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Unique_Items) article. Unique items that are quest-related are listed at [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md). For lists of other available items, see:

- [Skyrim-Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Armor): Category listing of all the armor available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Clothing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Clothing): Category listing of all the clothing available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Jewelry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Jewelry): Category listing of all the jewelry available in the game.
- [Skyrim-Weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Weapons): Category listing of all the weapons available in the game.
