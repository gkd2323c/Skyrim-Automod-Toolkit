[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Pactur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Pactur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pactur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pactur.jpg) Pactur **Pactur** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who, with his wife [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder), runs [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md) for [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md). He'll ask you to [relay a message](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery) to Bryling on their behalf.

With [Gestur Rockbreaker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gestur_Rockbreaker) keeping an eye on the workers in the mine, Pactur spends most of his time inside the [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli%27s_House) with his wife and son. He sleeps between midnight and 7am and only has two errands during the day; at 9am he spends two hours standing outside the house, and at 1pm he pays a three-hour visit to the workers in the mine. At all other times he will relax in the house. He was supposed to change his routine drastically when the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) and he leaves his and Sorli's son [Sirgar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sirgar) in charge at Stonehills, but doesn't. (see [bugs](#Bugs))

He wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd) and has an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) equipped. He carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Sorli%27s_House) to [Sorli's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli%27s_House), a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Payment Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery)**: Tell [Thane Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md) that the late shipment from [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) will be arriving soon.

## Dialogue
When approached, he will greet you with:

*"Sorli and I are just here to make sure the mine is run proper."* *"Wouldn't ever have expected to wind up living in a place like this, but the money's good."* *"Don't bother the miners. They've got work to do."* **You're out in the middle of nowhere, aren't you?** *"Don't have to tell me twice. But then, I'm not the one that buried iron under these mountains."* If Sorli becomes Jarl, Pactur will become her [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) and will greet you with:

*"It's good that Sorli has finally been given some real authority."* *"I'll see to it that Sorli is safe now that she's running Morthal."* *"We left Sirgar in charge back at Stonehills. I hope that wasn't a mistake."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Payment Delivery
**Do you own the mine?** *"No, and before you ask, it's not for sale."* **I wasn't asking.** *"I'm just protecting Bryling's investment. She's the owner, even though we're the ones here doing all the work. She's already come down hard on us for missing shipments. Likely to do it again, since we're late."* **Do you get many offers?** *"Well, no. But that's not the point. Bryling owns the mine, and she's not selling. We make her some decent coin running this place. Keeps her living well in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). At least, when we can get shipments to her. We're late on the last one. I expect her to start breathin' fire any moment now."* **Is there any way I can help?** *"You could take word to her that there'll be a shipment soon. Buy us some time and keep her off our backs. In exchange, I'm willing to let a few ingots go missing. Maybe more than a few."* **Can't you reason with her?** *"Can't do that and keep an eye on things here at the same time. But... You could take word to her in Solitude, let her know it'll be coming soon. I'd make it worth your while. No one would notice a few missing ingots out of the next shipment."* You can then offer to help or decline:

**I'll go see Bryling for you.** *"You're doing me quite a favor. I won't forget it. I'm sure she'll be angry. Brace yourself."* **Maybe some other time.** *"Won't be 'some other time' at this rate."*
## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

*"Pactur's life revolves around Sorli. He assists with running the mine, which means doing all the work Sorli doesn't care to, and he feeds her ambitions in whatever way he can."* - In dialogue, Pactur and Sorli act like they run the mining operation, but this does not match up with the in-game depiction. Sorli never leaves the house and Pactur predominantly works outside of the mine. [Gestur Rockbreaker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gestur_Rockbreaker) seems to be the actual boss. He has dialogue implying so, similar to Pactur, but he's also marked as the boss of all the miners internally, as well as an employee of Bryling. Gestur also has scenes with all his workers, talking about their progress, which neither Pactur nor Sorli have.

## Bugs
- If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) as part of the civil war, Pactur's wife Sorli will be appointed the new [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) and will move to [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). Because the steward alias in the `Dialogue Morthal` quest only provides him a generic sandbox package, Pactur will still uphold his old schedule. Because this schedule expects him to be in Stonehills, he will not move to Morthal alonsgide Sorli and Teeba-Ei, despite his dialogue changing to suggest he is living in Morthal and serving as her steward. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, addresses this issue. The patch makes it so that Pactur inherits [Aslfur](Skyrim_Aslfur.md)'s AI packages, giving him the same schedule as his Imperial-aligned predecessor.

- This bug is fixed by the [Hearthfire add-on](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md).
- With the Unofficial Skyrim Patch, Pactur will wake up at 7am and hang around the hall all day, standing by the fire from 8am to 12pm. He'll then stay in the hall until 7pm when heads to the inn for four hours at which point he'll return to the hall for his bedtime at midnight. This is the same for Hearthfire, although it does not give him an equivalent to Alsfur's inn package, making him stay in the hall all day.
- The Morthal sleep package he was given by the patch, `Morthal Aslfur Sleep0x7`, was set to use the editor location for sleeping, which would cause Pactur to return to Stonehills each night to sleep instead of staying in Morthal as expected when he becomes Sorli's steward. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- At some point, the patch also gave Pactur a new package called `USKPDefault Sandbox Center Marker Highmoon Hall512`, which is an equivalent to Aslfur's sandbox package. Pactur was similarly unable to just reuse Aslfur's, as it would make him return to his editor location, which is in Stonehills.
- Pactur may accuse Teeba-Ei of trespassing in Highmoon Hall because Teeba-Ei was never assigned to the relevant [faction](Skyrim_Morthal_Longhouse_Faction_(Both_Sides).md) like all the other possible occupants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
