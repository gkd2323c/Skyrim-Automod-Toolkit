[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-npc-Ma%27dran.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ma%27dran.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27dran.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27dran.jpg) Ma'dran **Ma'dran** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [pawnbroker](Skyrim_Pawnbroker.md) and the leader of his [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). He travels with [Ma'jhad](Skyrim_Ma%27jhad.md) and [Ra'zhinda](Skyrim_Ra%27zhinda.md) between [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

Ma'dran wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He is equipped with a [steel mace](Skyrim_Steel_Mace.md), and carries a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Rich).

## Dialogue

- **Has the war made your travels difficult?**
- *"Quite the opposite, in fact. The soldiers are some of our best customers, for we offer supplies and wares they cannot obtain by other means. It was [Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md)'s idea to come here now, while the land is in turmoil. Where others saw only danger, he saw opportunity."*

- **Do you work for Risaad *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*?**
- *"He hired the others and paid for my goods. I am allowed to run the caravan, but I pay Ri'saad a percentage of my profits. I say this without bitterness, for it was Ri'saad who saved me from a life of servitude aboard a trading ship. I am grateful to have the life that I do now. It is a far cry from my days as an orphan on the streets of Corinthe."*

- **Who owns the trading caravans?**
- *"It is Ri'saad who owns the three trading caravans that travel the roads of Skyrim. Long ago, I was an orphan on the streets of [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md). I stowed away aboard a trade ship that brought me here. It was Ri'saad who found me. He provided the funds to start this caravan, and put me in charge of it. I owe him more than I could ever hope to repay."*

## Notes
- Although [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) claims to have met him, his stop is not Whiterun and his route does not take him past the city. - It's possible the caravans are meant to be implied to have changing end locations, as the book *[Ghosts in the Storm](Skyrim_Ghosts_in_the_Storm.md)* implies Ri'saad's caravan heading to Windhelm, while the *[Smugglers' Note](Skyrim_Smugglers%27_Note.md)* implies Ahkari's caravan stopping in Whiterun as well. The former book states that the caravans "crisscross Skyrim".
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* also states the following about Ma'dran's caravan: *"Ma'dran's route is unique in that he shares a common stop with Ri'saad's caravan: Windhelm, However, Ma'dran's route runs from Windhelm to Solitude instead of to Markarth."* Ri'saad's caravan does not have a stop at Windhelm.
- Ma'dran's caravan is the smallest, at just three members. The other two caravans have four Khajiit (the leader, two guards, and one other).
