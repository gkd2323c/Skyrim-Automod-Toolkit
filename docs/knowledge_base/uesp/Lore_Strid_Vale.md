[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/OB-place-Strid_Vale.jpg/200px-OB-place-Strid_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Strid_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Strid_Vale.jpg) The Strid Vale The Strid Vale is a sparsely wooded region on the northern bank of the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md), in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The Strid Vale is generally understood to encompass the land between [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) and [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), including the historical town of Ostumir.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESONantharion-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> At its greatest extent, the Strid Vale is said to stretch from Kvatch to [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The region is bisected by the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md), and is home to vineyards and [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) estates.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCalantius-5)</sup>

## History
During the time of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md), the city-state of [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md) was one of the most powerful in western [Cyrod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ald_Cyrod). Miscarcand recognized [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) as Empress following the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), but the city was gradually abandoned by its people. The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) began to depart in small bands, fleeing across the Strid to nearby Valenwood.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Beragon-6)</sup>

Starting around [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), the lands between Kvatch and Skingrad became all but abandoned by the local Imperials after the troubles of the [Colovian Revolt](Lore_Colovian_Revolt.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESONantharion-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md) used the destructive power of corrupted Greenspeaker seeds, known as [Wildburn seeds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn), to replace the Strid Vale's typical vegetation with a jungle. Named Dawnwood by King [Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md), this new forest resembled the [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) on the other side of the Strid and was swiftly colonized by [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) from the city of [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md). The use of Wildburn seeds also had caused the creation of a corrupted area known as "the Wildburn" on its outskirts. King Nantharion oversaw the settlement of Vashabar and [Hoperoot](Lore_Hoperoot.md) and attempted to expand the forest even further. His plan to destroy Skingrad and cover the entire West Weald in jungle was ended when he was killed within the ruins of Miscarcand that same year.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGambit-7)</sup> Following Nantharion's death, Count [Calantius of Skingrad](Lore_Calantius_of_Skingrad.md) entered into talks with [Greenspeaker](Lore_Greenspeakers.md) Darolith of Vashabar. The count hoped to secure a treaty of cooperation, granting Vashabar land rights and trade agreements with Skingrad and the rest of the West Weald. Securing this alliance required the count to persuade certain nobles and landowners to part with abandoned estates they could no longer return to by purchasing their land interest.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCalantius-5)</sup>

The Dawnwood had completely disappeared by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), with the Strid Vale's flora and fauna resembling the rest of the West Weald at the time.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
