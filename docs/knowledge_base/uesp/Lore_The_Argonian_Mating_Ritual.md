The Argonian Mating Ritual **by [Valrendil](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Valrendil) of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md), Research-Appointee to the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md)** *Research notes on Argonian mating habits*
---
I shall endeavor to shed light on these most mysterious creatures, the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), paying particular attention to their mating rituals.

Many speak in whispers of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md), a faceless entity with whom all Argonians claim bonds. According to legend, the Hist lives at the heart of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) in a tree that routinely walks the lands, patrolling its borders.

Some say the wandering Hist is a metaphor for the Argonian condition. Doomed to toil amongst the fetid swamps, they desire a way out of their misery. Somehow the Argonians continue to survive, their numbers neither rising nor falling regardless of adversity.

The lizard-folk view mating as a simple call to procreation by their leader, the Hist. To participate in this annual event, Argonians travel to the town of [Hissmir](Lore_Hissmir.md) and engage in several trials. The trial winners are allowed to mate, while losers must return the following year.

Though I was unable to observe this year's trials, I hope to attend next year in order to learn more about these strange and sub- [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) reptilians.
