# Akaviri Sunderblade

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/BL-concept-Akaviri_Sunderblade.jpg/200px-BL-concept-Akaviri_Sunderblade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-concept-Akaviri_Sunderblade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-concept-Akaviri_Sunderblade.jpg) Sunderblade (Blades) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-weapon-Akaviri Katana.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-weapon-Akaviri Katana.png) Akaviri Sunderblade (Oblivion) The Akaviri Sunderblade, also shortened to just **Sunderblade** is a legendary katana that belonged to the first leader of the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), [Ghelin-Brol](Lore_Ghelin-Brol.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GGB-1)</sup> who were sworn to the protection of the [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sunderblade-2)</sup> It was known for its potent enchantment that could disintegrate weapons and armors of the foes of the wielder.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBL-3)</sup> Numerous superb katanas were fashioned to look like the legendary Sunderblade and were used for centuries by high-ranking [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) before the order was disbanded.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AB-4)</sup>

As of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the legendary blade was available for purchase in the Three Brothers Trade Goods shop in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) from Imperial merchant Sergius Verus. It is unknown whether or not [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) purchased the blade or not, however.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SV-5)</sup>

## Known Owners
- [Ghelin-Brol](Lore_Ghelin-Brol.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sunderblade-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GGB-1)</sup>
- Sergius Verus<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SV-5)</sup>

It is unknown whether [The Warrior](Lore_Hero.md#Warrior) and [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) wielded the Sunderblade or not, but it could have potentially come into their ownership at some point.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sunderblade-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBL-3)</sup>
