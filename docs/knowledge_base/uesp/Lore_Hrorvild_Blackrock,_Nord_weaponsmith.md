Hrorvild Blackrock, Nord weaponsmith **by [Hrorvild Blackrock](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Hrorvild_Blackrock)** *A blacksmith's thoughts on working with ebony*
---
[![ON-concept-Rocks.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/01/ON-concept-Rocks.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Rocks.png)
- "Now that [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), that is a true wonder. I'd love to see what a real [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) smith can do with it, now that we've got access to more. Hard to get much before the [Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) —most of it comes from [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) guard it like a [sabre cat](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md) over her cubs. I heard it's the blood of [a god](Lore_Lorkhan.md); that's what they say, at least.

- Anyway, I've seen Dark Elven smiths make some fine weapons from it. They keep an edge like you wouldn't believe and seem to have just the right weight and balance. Give one of our own Nord smiths enough time with a good quantity, and he'll produce weapons to live in legend forever!"
