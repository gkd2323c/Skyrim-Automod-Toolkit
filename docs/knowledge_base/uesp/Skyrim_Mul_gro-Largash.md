[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-npc-Mul_gro-Largash.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mul_gro-Largash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mul_gro-Largash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mul_gro-Largash.jpg) Mul gro-Largash **Mul gro-Largash** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) found in [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md)'s [Gloombound Mine](Skyrim_Gloombound_Mine.md). He stays in the mine all the time, sometimes sitting to take a break, but never eating or sleeping. Originally from the [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) [Orc Stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md), he left with his brother [Gadba](Skyrim_Gadba_gro-Largash.md) and a lofty scheme.

Mul wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md) and matching [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He carries a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) along with a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md), which can be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality.

## Dialogue

- *"Gadba and me came to work the mine. The plan is to get rich and then go back to Largashbur and take over!"*
- *"Mauhulakh brought us in when he found [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) in the mine. Not enough men in town to work it, so here we are."*
- *"One of these days we need to head back to [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). Get some of that good [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) mead."*

## Conversations
Sometimes he will chat with Gadba:

- **Mul**: *"How much longer do we have to work this backwoods mine?"*
- **Gadba**: *"A bit longer, yet. We need more money if we want to be chiefs of our own."*
- **Mul**: *"But I thought there was only one chief."*
- **Gadba**: *"Well, right. One of us will be a chief."*
- **Mul**: *"What about the other one?"*
- **Gadba**: *"Which other one?"*
- **Mul**: *"The other chief!"*
- **Gadba**: *"There's only one chief!"*

- **Mul**: *"I found a big load of ebony at the bottom pocket in the mine."*
- **Gadba**: *"Did you mark it?"*
- **Mul**: *"No, I don't need to mark it. I remember it."*
- **Gadba**: *"So where is it?"*
- **Mul**: *"You can tell it because it has lots of ebony."*

- **Mul**: *"Do you ever get a weird feeling about those sisters?"*
- **Gadba**: *"Who has a sister?"*
- **Mul**: *"Mauhulakh's aunts. You know... the way they're all around him all the time."*
- **Gadba**: *"That's just how it is with families. When we're chiefs, you'll see how it is."*
- **Mul**: *"Can we both be chiefs?"*
- **Gadba**: *"Not today, we can't."*

- **Gadba**: *"Sometimes I think your head is made of rocks."*
- **Mul**: *"That would be good! I could use it to smash other rocks. Easier to mine, then."*
- **Gadba**: *"Oh, good point. I wish my head were made of rocks."*

[Dushnamub](Skyrim_Dushnamub.md) may express his concern about the brothers to [Chief Mauhulakh](Skyrim_Chief_Mauhulakh.md):

- **Dushnamub**: *"Father, I'm worried about Gadba and Mul."*
- **Mahulakh**: *"What about them?"*
- **Dushnamub**: *"You don't worry about two Orc men coming into your stronghold? That they might challenge you?"*
- **Mahulakh**: *"They're miners. Harmless."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"I've just learned not to trust luck around here."*

## Notes
- Neither Mul nor Gadba ever gets mentioned by anyone in Largashbur. They also don't appear to have any other family or any belongings there at all.
- While Mul is stated to be the smart one of the brothers, in conversations with his brother, their roles often seem reversed.
