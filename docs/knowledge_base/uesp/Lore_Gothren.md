Gothren was a powerful Sorcerer-Lord and a high-ranking and influential member of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md). By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), he had become the Archmagister of the House, and led the Telvanni council from his tower in [Tel Aruhn](Lore_Tel_Aruhn.md) with an iron grip.

## History

### Second Era
Gothren was an influential member of Great House Telvanni. By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he had ascended to the rank of Magister, one rank below the highest rank of Archmagister. Using his position in power, he (and those associated with him) freely expressed his xenophobia, and discriminated against those he saw as being beneath him, such as "lesser" races. He attempted to stop the advancement of an aspiring [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md), [Sun-in-Shadow](Lore_Sun-in-Shadow.md), who was quickly climbing up the social ladder,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONOO-1)</sup> and later kidnapped her for having the audacity to poison his representative. He, like other Telvanni mages, was an opportunist, and he blackmailed Sun-in-Shadow's errand-runner to retrieve an item of importance. To him, even the leader of House Telvanni was not off limits, as he sought the research notes of Archmagister Nelos Otheri relating to [Tonal Architecture](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md), which the errand-runner provided.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMMM-2)</sup> He also personally oversaw the Masters Conclave in Tel Huulen during which Master [Shelreni Baro](Lore_Shelreni_Baro.md) violently announced her desire for ascension and was revealed to cooperate with [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) and [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-COF-3)</sup>

### Third Era
By the late Third Era, Gothren had risen to the rank of Archmagister and become the leader of House Telvanni. He remained highly distrustful of any outsiders, a position which would ultimately lead to his demise. A key event was the rise of Magister [Aryon](Lore_Aryon.md), an ambitious young Telvanni sorcerer with a very progressive ideology which contrasted sharply with that of his more conservative peers. Aryon wished to introduce many reforms to Telvanni ethics and practices, but Gothren became a major roadblock. In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) appeared on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and Aryon became the Nerevarine's most ardent supporter within House Telvanni. He encouraged the Nerevarine to challenge Archmagister Gothren, while frankly admitting that he stood to gain personally from Gothren's death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWAryon-4)</sup> The Nerevarine did so, although whether this was done in regards to their succession to the position of Archmagister<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWAG-5)</sup> or their effort to be named Telvanni Hortator<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWTH-6)</sup> is unclear. In either event, the Nerevarine was victorious, and Gothren was killed.
