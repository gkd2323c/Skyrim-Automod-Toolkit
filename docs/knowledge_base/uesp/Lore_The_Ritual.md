# The Ritual

*This one sees … the arcane. Yes, Magrus dances on light blue paws—tiny flames and the scent of incense ….
This one hears soft chanting, glimpses flickering candles.
You are guided by the Ritual, yes? Allies shall seek you out, for your sign guides you to heal and mend. But beware—not all ills can be repaired.*
—Baandari fortune-telling for those born under the Ritual<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Ritual (known as the **Laboratory** to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md))<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> is a constellation of seven stars which is in the night sky during [Morning Star](Lore_Morning_Star.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup> It is one of the [Mage](Lore_The_Mage.md)'s charges. Those born under the sign of the Ritual are thought to have various abilities depending on the aspects of the moons and planets.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It is consistently depicted by most cultures as taking the shape of the [Aurbic Eye](Lore_Aurbic_Eye.md).

[Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) who followed the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) modeled their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor after the constellations. Each part of their armaments was crafted in honor of a different constellation. Helmets were linked to the Ritual, "who watcheth all with glowing eye in the face of space". They believed that Ritual's eye is seen but the visage is hidden, and their helmets were meant to emulate the Ritual and hide their visages beneath cap and behind the visor, seeing but not seen, unknown until their actions make them known.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CM41-5)</sup>

The [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [Druids](Lore_Druid.md) of old praised the Ritual constellation above all.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONWD-6)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), megaliths devoted to the Ritual<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOTH-7)</sup> and charms devoted to the Constellation of Ritual could be found in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md), among the modern druidic circles. Seven green beads hung in the shape of the ritual constellations were presumably rubbed for good luck.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TRSC-8)</sup>

The constellations are present in ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) poetry.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TWC-9)</sup> In one of their songs The Ritual is "turning through the night."<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Coyle-10)</sup>

The Dwemer associate the Ritual with the letter b.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

According to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) the charges, or the Lesser Stars (the Ritual among them) are protected by the Guardians in the Sky who stand together against [the Snake in the Stars](Lore_The_Snake_in_the_Stars.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ARANA-12)</sup>

According to the [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) fortune tellers, the Ritual is linked to the arcane and [Magrus](Lore_Magrus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Ritual was also believed to "speed ones on their star-patterned path".<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Uriel-13)</sup>
