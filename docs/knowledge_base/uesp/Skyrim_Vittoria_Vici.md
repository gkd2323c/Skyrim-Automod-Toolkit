[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici.jpg) Vittoria Vici [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vittoria_Vici_03.jpg) Vittoria at work **Vittoria Vici** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and the first cousin to [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md). She oversees the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company)'s holdings in Solitude and is influential and wealthy enough to be invited to [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md)'s upper-class party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). She is also engaged to a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), [Asgeir Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md) from [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), and a huge wedding is in the works. Due to the couple's differing political stances, the wedding is considered a precursor to end the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) and is expected and celebrated by many. Unfortunately, the wedding has also attracted the attention of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) who deems it the perfect opportunity to regain their reputation.

Vittoria lives in one of the exclusive mansions in Solitude, neighboring [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md), and she sleeps there every night between midnight and 8am. After waking up she heads down to the dockside and finds her stall near the entrance to the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) where she spends twelve hours. At 8pm she returns to [her house](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md) where she wanders around until midnight. There is one major exception to this daily routine, specifically during the quest [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md). Once initiated, Vittoria Vici will be in the courtyard outside the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) busy being the bride of the much anticipated wedding.

Her everyday attire consists of a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991) along with a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). She wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House) to her house, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich). During her wedding, Vittoria wears a unique [wedding dress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Dress), [wedding sandals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Sandals), [wedding wreath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Wreath), and [her wedding band](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band).

If you meet her on the streets of Solitude she will immediately defend herself: *"Some say I got my position because my cousin is the Emperor. Nonsense. The man's given me nothing. He can't even be bothered to attend my wedding."* She will then tell you: *"Good business for the East Empire Company means all the more gold for the Empire."* She will then hint on her status: *"I don't have time for anyone too lazy or cowardly to make their own way in this world."* Should you approach her while she is at work, she may say: *"Hmm? Sorry, I was lost in thought. I'm planning my wedding, you see. So many details to work out."*

With the civil war roaming across the land, the upcoming marriage between Vittoria Vici, an Imperial with obvious ties to the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), and Asgeir Snow-Shod, whose family staunchly supports the rebellion, is an event known all over Skyrim. Their union is seen as a step toward reconciliation and peace, and the wedding and celebration is expected to go on for days. All over Skyrim, the patrolling guards, both Stormcloaks and Imperials, will happily tell you: *"The Emperor's cousin Vittoria Vici is getting married soon. The reception will be public - anyone in Solitude can attend."* In the Solitude market, local merchant [Evette San](Skyrim_Evette_San.md) and [Silana Petreia](Skyrim_Silana_Petreia.md) will have a joyful conversation about the wedding, with Silana saying: *"Nice to have something happy in the midst of the war."*, and Evette replying: *"Vittoria is practically glowing."* Even at the [party](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) at the Thalmor Embassy, the guests will mention: *"Did you know Vittoria is the Emperor's cousin? Best stay on her good side."* and *"Did you know Vittoria's getting married? They say the Emperor might even come to Skyrim for the wedding!"* In the wilderness, you can encounter two confused travelers from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), [Plautis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plautis_Carvain) and [Salonia Carvain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Carvain), on their way to the wedding ceremony, although they have some problems locating Solitude:

**Plautis**: *"Come along, no more stops, we need to find our way to Solitude."*
**Salonia**: *"Why are we even going to a wedding at this far flung end of the Empire anyway?"*
**Plautis**: *"I told you, it's the wedding of Vittoria Vici, an extremely well connected merchant with the East Empire Company. The Emperor's cousin? Remember? Hopefully these gifts will put us in her good graces, secure that import deal, and lead the way to an audience with the Emperor."*

## Related Quests
- **[Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md)**: Assassinate Vittoria Vici at her own wedding.
- **[Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md)**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).
- **[The Spiced Wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Spiced_Wine)**: Convince Vittoria Vici to hand over some spices.

### Bound Until Death
While Solitude prepares for the celebration, the once feared and dreaded [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) is nothing but a shadow of its former self, and is in desperate need of a way to regain its reputation in and around Skyrim. An [offer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silence_Has_Been_Broken) from the mysterious [Amaund Motierre](Skyrim_Amaund_Motierre.md), who wants [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) assassinated, puts new hope into Brotherhood leader [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md), who quickly reckons the upcoming wedding of the Emperor's cousin the perfect opportunity to get to Titus himself. Once you have talked to [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) to confirm Amaund's identity, you will find that Astrid has already come up with a plan: *"Now then. I hope you have something nice to wear. Because you're going to a wedding."* She will then share her plan: *"Well, more like the public reception. It should be a lovely affair. You'll mingle with the guests, eat some cake... stab the bride. Oh yes. You've got to kill the bride. At her wedding. And they say romance is dead."* You can then choose between three responses, all of which will lead her to elaborate on the macabre task:

**Who's the target?**
*"Her name is Vittoria Vici. She oversees the East Empire Company's business holdings in Solitude. The wedding is being held in that city, at the Temple of the Divines. Her death will cause an uproar, which is exactly what we want. Vici is likely to address her guests frequently, as is the wedding custom. Kill her when she does that, and I promise you a significant bonus. Now go. And give my best to the bride."*

**Enough talk. Just tell me who to kill.**
*"Very well. The target's name is Vittoria Vici. She oversees the East Empire Company's business holdings in Solitude. The wedding is being held in that city, at the Temple of the Divines. Her death will cause an uproar, which is exactly what we want. Remember, we want people to notice. Kill Vici while she's addressing her guests, as is the custom, and I can promise a bonus. Now go. And give the bride a special kiss, from me."*

**(Remain silent)**
*"Your target's name is Vittoria Vici. She oversees the East Empire Company's business holdings in Solitude. The wedding is being held in that city, at the Temple of the Divines. ## Notes
- Killing Vittoria before [The Spiced Wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Spiced_Wine) is started will make it impossible to complete.
- Vittoria was supposed to be involved in two conversations while working on the dockside. The first conversation, ` Dialogue Solitude Docks Scene1`, involves the three Argonian dockworkers, [Deeja](Skyrim_Deeja.md), [Jaree-Ra](Skyrim_Jaree-Ra.md), and [Gulum-Ei](Skyrim_Gulum-Ei.md), although all three of them can respond to Vittoria when she says: *"Big shipment coming in later tonight. I need you three working late."* To which one Argonian will say: *"Oh, we'll make sure it's emptied out by morning. You can trust us!"* and another will continue with: *"Yeah, we'll get the valuables out of there, no problem!"* Due to the extreme conditions of the conversation, it will practically never take place.
The second conversation, ` Dialogue Solitude Docks Scene3`, involves [Greta](Skyrim_Greta.md), who never goes near the docks, so her plea: *"Got any work? I ain't begging. I just need a job. Anything."* And Vittoria's answer: *"Sorry, I have everyone I need at the warehouse. Times are tight."* will never be heard.
