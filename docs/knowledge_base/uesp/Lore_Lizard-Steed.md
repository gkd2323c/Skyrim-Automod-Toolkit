*[Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) and its surrounding lands are home to a seemingly infinite variety of [lizards](Lore_Lizard.md) and [lizardly relations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reptiles), so different and manifold, and yet all so pleasingly moist! Truly, there are no boundaries nor limits to the imagination of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md)!*
—Esqoo of [Dhalmora](Lore_Dhalmora.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-1)</sup> **Lizard-Steeds** (sometimes spelled **Lizard Steeds**) are [Useful Lizards](Lore_Useful_Lizard.md) shaped by the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) to look like large animals commonly used as mounts by the people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The Hist shaped them as such to allow the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) people to travel long distances in the swamps. [Horses](Lore_Horse.md), [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md), and other creatures traditionally used as mounts are not suited for long travel through the deep swamp; these mounts can get stuck in the mud, catch [sloughskin](Lore_Sloughskin.md) from [fleshflies](Lore_Fleshfly.md), or eat poisonous flora and die. Lizard-Steeds do not have such issues.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The origin of the Lizard-Steeds is similar to the Argonians' own origins. The Argonian Parable of Becoming states that the Hist found the humanoid forms of Men and Mer useful, molding the form of the swamp's lizards after them to create Argonians.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LAMQA2-3)</sup>

## Variants
The stablemasters in the Marsh offer a variety of lizard mounts that are well-suited to the swampy conditions. These mounts are both convenient and personable. Some stablemasters trained their lizard-steeds to glide over mud like water over stone.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ukaspa-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WLS-5)</sup> [Wolf](Lore_Wolf.md) -Lizards are among them. Instead of howling, Wolf-Lizard pups are known to emit a hissing sound.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WLS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-WLP-6)</sup> Some of the lizard wolves are venomous, making them an even more impressive and intimidating option for riders looking to make an impression. Their venom glows in the dark.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VWL-7)</sup>

Just like wolves, grown-up Lizard- [Senches](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) are employed as mounts, and their offspring are known to hiss instead of meowing.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SLS-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SLS2-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SLC-10)</sup> Black Fredas Senche-Lizards are an entirely black variant of the Senche-Lizard that attracts superstitions.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BSL-11)</sup> Chilling Senche-Lizard are a quite rare variant of Senche-Lizard, it originates from very deep swamp, from a tribe said to be dedicated to frost spells as a means of living longer by living slower. Allegedly their [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) tree was known to drip with icicles.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-CSL-12)</sup>

Although [guars](Lore_Guar.md) are already a reptilian species, Guar-Lizard Steeds do exist; as such their existence was deemed strangely redundant.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-GLS-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-GLC-14)</sup> They are excellent for traversing swampland.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ukaspa-4)</sup>

The origins of [Camel](Lore_Camel.md) -Lizards are unclear, but it is speculated that they were created to aid adventurous Argonians in traversing arid lands such as [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), or to serve as mounts for daring [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) adventurers exploring the swamps of Black Marsh.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-CLS-15)</sup> Pyrodraconic Camel-Lizards are a variant of the Camel-Lizard created by Magister [Varkenel](Lore_Varkenel.md) as a commission using a combination of Wood-elf and House Telvanni techniques. They are bright red, and are capable of breathing fire.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-PCL-16)</sup>

[Bear](Lore_Bear.md) -Lizards are also used as mounts, though they can be also found in the wilderness. They are known for their intimidating, and even fearsome nature. However, their offspring do not exhibit these characteristics during their adolescence.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-BLS-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-BL-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-BLC-19)</sup> Wormwrithe Bear-Lizards are Bear-Lizards that have been twisted and warped by the power of the souls contained within the Writhing Wall.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-WBL-20)</sup>

[Horse](Lore_Horse.md) -Lizards are commonly used for long-distance travel, with the [Bright-Throat Tribe](Lore_Bright-Throat_Tribe.md) known to breed their own variants of these beasts.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-WHL-21)</sup> Additionally, there are also smaller Pony-Lizard breeds available, and popular among Argonian children.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-HLS-22)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-PL-23)</sup> Another breed of Horse-Lizards, Scorching Horse-Lizards are known for their ability to emit fire.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-SHL-24)</sup>
