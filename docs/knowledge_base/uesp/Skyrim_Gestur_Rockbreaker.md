[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Gestur_Rockbreaker.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gestur_Rockbreaker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gestur_Rockbreaker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gestur_Rockbreaker.jpg) Gestur Rockbreaker **Gestur Rockbreaker** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) working in [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md). He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b), and defends himself with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). He also carries [a selection of common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). Gestur will buy any [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) you have for 7 gold each.

Gestur sleeps at the miner camp from 1am to 6am. When he wakes up, he immediately goes to work in the mine for a grand 15 hours before returning to wander around the camp until his usual bedtime.

## Dialogue
If spoken to, he may tell you about the mine and the owner:

- *"If you're here about the mine -- it's already got an owner, and she's not selling. If not, then please stay out of my workers' way."*
- *"[Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md) owns this mine. I wouldn't have much to live for if she hadn't offered me a job."*
- *"I spend every waking moment making sure the mine is put to good use."*
- *"My job is my life. Whatever happened before doesn't matter."*
- *"You get used to the cold after a while. The harder you work, the warmer you stay."*

### Conversations
Gestur may also have a brief conversation with one of his employees, either [Swanhvir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swanhvir), [Jesper](Skyrim_Jesper.md) or [Teeba-Ei](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Teeba-Ei):

- **Gestur**: *"Let's make sure we're just as productive this month as we were last. If Bryling's happy, we're happy."*
- **Swanhvir**: *"Yes sir, of course."*
- **Gestur**: *"All right. Keep up the good work."*

- **Gestur**: *"Everything well, Jesper?"*
- **Jesper**: *"Yes sir, fine sir."*
- **Gestur**: *"Good, good. Keep up the hard work. It'll all pay off."*

- **Gestur**: *"Is everything well with you, my scaly friend?"*
- **Teeba**: *"Teeba-Ei is cold, but Teeba-Ei works hard as always."*
- **Gestur**: *"Yes, well... That's all we ask of you. Carry on."*
- **Teeba**: *"Yes sir."*

### Unused Conversations
Gestur was also supposed to have a Redguard son, [Talib](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Talib), but he does not appear in the final game. As such, you are unable to hear the unique conversation the two were supposed to have:

- **Gestur**: *"How goes it, son?"*
- **Talib**: *"I'm fine, father. Work is fine."*
- **Gestur**: *"Good, good. It's honest work, son. No shame in that."*
- **Talib**: *"Of course not. I never thought otherwise."*
- **Gestur**: *"Right. Well, you let me know if you need anything."*

## Notes
- Gestur was supposed to have a house located at Stonehills; the [related key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Gestur%27s_House) and locklist still exist but go unused in the final game.
- In dialogue, [Pactur](Skyrim_Pactur.md) and [Sorli](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) act like they run the mining operation, but this does not match up with the in-game depiction. Sorli never leaves the house and Pactur predominantly works outside of the mine. Gestur seems to be the actual boss. He has dialogue implying so, similar to Pactur, but he's also marked as the boss of all the miners internally, as well as an employee of Bryling. Gestur also has scenes with all his workers, talking about their progress, which neither Pactur nor Sorli have.
