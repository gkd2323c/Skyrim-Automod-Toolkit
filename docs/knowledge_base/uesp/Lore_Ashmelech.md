[3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Ashmelech is an isolated catacomb in the archipelago region of [Sheogorad](Lore_Sheogorad.md), in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It is built on a small island between the main island near the Velothi tower, Shara, and the mainland near the Chimer stronghold, Valenvaryon. It is the home of the [Aundae Clan](Lore_Aundae_Clan.md), one of the three [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) clans of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWAshmelech-1)</sup> The clan uses an enchanted extravagant [ruby](Lore_Ruby.md) amulet to [magically transport](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) members back to Ashmelech.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWAmulet-2)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), a vampire hunter named Ano Vando lurked around Ashmelech seeking the Aundae Clan. Taking great offense to this, the clan's matron, [Dhaunayne Aundae](Lore_Dhaunayne_Aundae.md) sent one of their [members](Lore_Nerevarine.md) to kill him in broad daylight in [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWAundae-3)</sup>
