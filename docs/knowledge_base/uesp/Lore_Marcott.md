# Marcott

The Marcotts are a fallen [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble family<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> based in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md). During [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) they were loyal to [King Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md), and in the aftermath, [High King Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) stripped them of their power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Those who had ties with the Marcotts severed them, wishing to no longer be associated with them or their political beliefs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> A husk of their former selves, the Marcotts decided to go underground, pursuing criminal activities to stay afloat.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Vetitia Marcott attempted to regain some of her family's influence. A [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) swindler by the name of Kiv Lindres stole the signet ring of the [Veloise](Lore_Veloise.md) merchant family from [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), and traded access to the merchant houses' funds to pay off their debt. They hired [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) from the Oathbound Clan as muscle, and planned to collect their ill-gotten gains from the Veloise by force.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> However, Vetitia's plans were stopped, and she was slain in the process.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

[Harcourt Marcott](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Harcourt_Marcott) was a scholar and an author. He penned *[Planar Exploration Vol. 14: Darkreave Curators](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planar_Exploration_Vol._14__Darkreave_Curators)*, a text devoted to the Darkreave Curators of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PEV14-7)</sup>
