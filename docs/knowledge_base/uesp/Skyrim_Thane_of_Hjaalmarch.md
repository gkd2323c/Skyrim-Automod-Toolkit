## Radiant Options
This is a radiant quest which you can receive from either [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) or [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder), whichever is the Jarl of Hjaalmarch. The jarl lives in [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). You must assist three people.

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Befriend the Jarl of Hjaalmarch.
2. Assist three people of Hjaalmarch.
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an umbrella quest for several other quests which must be completed if you wish to become Thane of Hjaalmarch. In order to receive this quest, you must first aid the current jarl. You must usually complete [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md) first if the jarl is Idgrod; however, you can begin the quest if she was ousted from Morthal during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and reinstated following [The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Imperial).md). You can also begin the quest if the jarl is Sorli, whether she was installed via [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or [The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks).md).

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the jarl again and she will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People). You must assist three people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests . If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md) perk by investing in their stores.

### Reward
After helping three inhabitants, the journal tells you to return to the jarl. Note that Idgrod in this context refers to [Jarl Idgrod (The Elder)](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md), not her daughter [Idgrod the Younger](Skyrim_Idgrod_the_Younger.md). The reward, the [Blade of Hjaalmarch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Hjaalmarch), is a random, leveled, enchanted sword. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword (two-handed). Due to a [bug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Bugs), however, maces are also occasionally awarded. You also gain the title of Thane of Hjaalmarch and, if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed, a personal housecarl, [Valdimar](Skyrim_Valdimar.md).
