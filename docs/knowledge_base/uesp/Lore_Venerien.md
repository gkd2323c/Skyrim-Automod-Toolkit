Against their determination, Kinlady Iniel had one last ploy—she sent her most powerful necromancer, Wraithmaster Venerien, to slay King Xakhwan. He was the most powerful [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) of his clan in his time and the assassin of King [Xakhwan](Lore_Xakhwan.md) of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/ON-npc-King_Xakhwan_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-King_Xakhwan_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Xakhwan_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King_Xakhwan_02.jpg) Xakhwan's corpse animated by Venerien [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kinlady_Iniel.jpg) Kinlady Iniel Little is known of Venerien's past, except that he was the most powerful necromancer in Kinlady [Iniel Corelanya](Lore_Iniel_Corelanya.md)'s employ.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup> During the battle of Ash'abah Pass in [1E 853](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_853) Iniel sent Venerien to assassinate King Xakhwan prior to the battle, while she engaged his sons, Princes Haqmir and Sameer, and their forces on an open field.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KI-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TI-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VY-4)</sup>

Venerien displayed traits of [vampirism](Lore_Vampire.md) and fought the princes while animating the corpse of their father. He was ultimately defeated by the forces of the Ra Gada along with the rest of the Corelanya.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TI-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WV-5)</sup>

The events of the battle were witnessed in a vision by an adventurer in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), who sought to join the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md). Venerien was also mentioned by Regent Karinwe, a leader of the remaining Corelanya of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice.md), in her research on the history of her clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC-1)</sup>
