# Carnaval

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Celebrant.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Celebrant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Celebrant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Celebrant.jpg) Carnaval celebrants Carnaval is a holiday associated with [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md), whose exact date is unknown, save for the fact that it is a party thrown by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po DV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCarnaval-2)</sup> It is a portmanteau of [carnal](Lore_Sex.md) and carnival. Indeed, people participating in Carnaval honor Sanguine with unrestrained celebration, boundless enthusiasm, and surrender their mind, body, and spirit to the rhythm of everlasting revelry.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONCC-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONFD-4)</sup> [Tamrielian](Lore_Tamriel.md) customs for Carnaval can vary depending on region. For example, participants from the [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) region decorate themselves in elaborate swirling and twirling body markings believed to draw Sanguine's gaze.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONMarks-5)</sup> If a reveler is not careful, there is a danger that they may not leave with their soul intact.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOCarnaval-2)</sup> Some celebrants will buy each other roses during the festival.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-6)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/ON-Misc-Carnaval_Cosmetics.png/200px-ON-Misc-Carnaval_Cosmetics.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-Misc-Carnaval_Cosmetics.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-Misc-Carnaval_Cosmetics.png) Carnaval cosmetics as worn by a Khajiit, Argonian and Human model heads Participants often wear eye-catching attire and cosmetics. Dancers whirl through the festivities clad in scarlet finery, vibrant hues, and plush feathers that seem to lift them from the floor, their movements fueled by excess and abandon.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONCFD-7)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONFan-8)</sup> Some were of the opinion that the first-day-of-Carnival dance involved a great deal of movement and spectacle, but little real payoff.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LATSFAS-9)</sup> Towering feathered headdresses, gleaming tiaras, and jeweled earrings are often used, proclaiming that modesty has no place in the night’s indulgence.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONCFH-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONCDT-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONCEE-12)</sup> Beaded veils and masks add an air of mystique, hiding identities and shielding the wearer from shame.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONCBV-13)</sup> Beneath these disguises may be makeup such as alluring, gold-painted designs that burn with passion and invitation, or vibrant feathers painted around one’s eyes.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONCAE-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ONCFG-15)</sup> Many wear the Golden Eye Gaze, a shimmering effect that colors the eyes gold, emulating the glint of treasure and drawing a “greedy” glance from onlookers.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-GEG-16)</sup> An ornate hairpiece may complete one's transformation as their crowning glory.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ONCSP-17)</sup>

Creatures are carefully fed, bred, groomed, outfitted, and trained throughout Tamriel for the purpose of being entered into the festival. [Guar](Lore_Guar.md) are nourished on a rich diet to bring out the vibrancy of their feathers, making them the stars of Carnaval parades.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONGuar-18)</sup> Carnaval [indriks](Lore_Indrik.md), with their dazzling plumage and gilded barding, turn heads wherever they go and may even draw the curious gaze of Sanguine.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONCI-19)</sup> Carnaval [jaguars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jaguar) glide through the festivities and are skilled at keeping silent,<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ONCJ-20)</sup> while Carnaval [welwas](Lore_Welwa.md) are fearsome and showstopping mounts that demand careful handling because a slip in control may leave the outcome entirely in the hands of Sanguine.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ONCW-21)</sup> Even the most dourest of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) have been tempted to celebrate, with some breeders from [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) transforming [nix-oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) into vibrant, ornate wonders.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-ONCN-22)</sup>

To the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of the pre-ri'Datta faith, Sanguine was known as Sangiin, a tempter who draws them toward blood and pleasure without purpose, a force that must be resisted.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-TWS-23)</sup> One tale tells of the Khajiit S'razar, a Carnaval participant, mesmerized by flame wisps. The blood on his claws tasted like wine as the Daedric Prince called to him. Rose patterns etched across his fur mirrored his surrender, each marking a moment he gave in to his impulses. From that point, nothing mattered except the drink and the blood he would spill for it.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-ONSIFM-24)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ONSIBM-25)</sup>
