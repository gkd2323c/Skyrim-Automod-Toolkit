# Mimic Stone

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-store-Crown_Mimic_Stone.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-store-Crown_Mimic_Stone.png) A Mimic Stone Mimic Stones are crystals of an orange-to-yellow hue. They were used by craftsmen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CMS-1)</sup> often as a substitute for other materials such as goldscale or [malachite](Lore_Malachite.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CMS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CSS-2)</sup>

They were available for purchase in quantities to suit any budget.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CSS-2)</sup>

## Notes
- There is no known connection between Mimic Stones and [Mimics](Lore_Mimic.md), the winged creatures of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DIF-3)</sup> or [Mimic Hist](Lore_Mimic_Hist.md) trees of the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MHT-4)</sup>
