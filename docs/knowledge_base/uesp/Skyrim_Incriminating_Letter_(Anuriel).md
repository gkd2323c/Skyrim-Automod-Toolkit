Incriminating Letter **by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)** *A letter exposing Anuriel's collusion with Maven Black-Briar* *
---
Dearest [Anuriel](Skyrim_Anuriel.md),

The [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) has shown great wisdom in choosing you as her steward. A position well befitting your character, as it demands great cunning, grace, and, shall we say, a talent for performing one's duties tactfully and discreetly.

You have become an important asset to our operation. I trust you found the dress accompanying this letter to your satisfaction. Remember, as I flourish, so too will my friends.

However, I wish to remind you of your pledge to prevent [Honningbrew](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Meadery.md) deliveries from entering the city. I am also still waiting for the detachment of city guards to escort my merchandise. I understand it can take time navigating around the delicate sensibilities of your mistress, the great [Lalia Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*, but really, I've waited long enough.

Provided you continue to serve my interests, I will continue to look after yours.

I trust I'll be hearing from you soon.

-- [Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)
