*Truly the Divines have shown me the answer. For has not the greatest Forebear Grandee, Ebrahim of Sentinel, a fair and lissome daughter, Fereshtah? And has not the greatest Crown Grandee, Murahd of Rihad, a fierce and clever daughter, Arlimahera?*
— [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup> **Grandee** Murahd of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) was a prominent [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) nobleman active during the mid– [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He was a contemporary of High King [Ja-Fr](Lore_Ja-Fr.md) and his successor, [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), and the greatest Crown Grandee of his time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/AR-place-Rihad.jpg/200px-AR-place-Rihad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Rihad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Rihad.jpg) Rihad in the Third Era Little is known of Murahd's life. He hailed from Rihad, and by the time of High King Ja-Fr's death and Ar-Azal's accession to the throne, he was regarded as the greatest Grandee among the Crowns. He also had a daughter, [Arlimahera](Lore_Arlimahera.md), noted for her fierce intellect and cunning,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup> who would later become known as the infamous Blood Queen.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBOD-2)</sup>

When Ar-Azal became king, he enjoyed little support from either faction: the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) distrusted him because he was a Crown, while the Crowns resented him because his father had failed to destroy the Forebears. Guided by a vision, Ar-Azal sought to mend the divide by [marrying](Lore_Marriage.md) both [Fereshtah](Lore_Fereshtah.md), daughter of [Ebrahim](Lore_Ebrahim.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), the greatest Grandee of the Forebears and Arlimahera, daughter of Grandee Murahd of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), the greatest Grandee of the Crowns, thus bringing Hammerfell closer to unification.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>
