# Knights of the Eight

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/ON-npc-Knight_of_the_Eight.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Knight_of_the_Eight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Knight_of_the_Eight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Knight_of_the_Eight.jpg) A Knight of the Eight The Knights of the Eight were an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) knightly order based in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They trained to guard the [Abbey of the Eight](Lore_Abbey_of_the_Eight.md) and the initiates there. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the knights were neutral in the ongoing [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The [Knights of the Nine](Lore_Knights_of_the_Nine.md), first founded in [3E 111](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_111),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> were seemingly the spiritual successors of the order.
