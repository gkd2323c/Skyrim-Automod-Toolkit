The Aetherial Staff can summon [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) or [Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md), although there is a chance there will be a mishap in the summoning with interesting effects. The specific chances associated with each outcome and the specific details of those outcomes are captured in the tables below.

| PC Level | Mishap <br> Chance | Chance for <br> Creature #1 | Creature #1 | Chance for <br> Creature #2 | Creature #2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 - 14 | 30% | 54% | [Dwarven Spider Worker](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider_Worker.md) | 16% | [Dwarven Sphere](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) |
| 15 - 29 | 15% | 42% | [Dwarven Spider Worker](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider_Worker.md) | 43% | [Dwarven Sphere](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) |
| 30 - 44 | 15% | 42% | [Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) | 43% | [Dwarven Sphere](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md) |
| 45+ | 15% | 42% | [Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) | 43% | [Dwarven Sphere Guardian](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere_Guardian.md) |

When a mishap occurs, the mishap can be any of three outcomes according to the following table:

| Chance | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| 20% | A [Broken Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Broken_Dwarven_Spider.md) spawns, and then dies instantly. |
| 20% | A [Broken Dwarven Sphere](Skyrim_Broken_Dwarven_Sphere.md) spawns, and then dies instantly. |
| 60% | A [Broken Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Broken_Dwarven_Spider.md) spawns, and then dies and transforms into a pile of [Dwarven metal scraps](Skyrim_Dwemer_Gyro.md#Dwemer_Metal_.28Smeltable.29). |

## Related Quests
- **[Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages)**: Search for the Dwemer's [Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge).

## Notes
- The summoned spider will replace any dead thralls. Even with the [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls) perk you can only have one spider at a time.
- The staff is not affected by [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell cost reduction and the summoned creatures aren't affected by any of the Conjuration perks, however the duration of the Sphere Guardian is affected by [Fortify Conjuration](Skyrim_Fortify_Conjuration.md) potions.
- Through the chance for the third mishap on the above table, the Aetherial Staff is an excellent tool for gathering lots of dwarven scrap, of which one or more are guaranteed to be smeltable. You can gather a lot of scraps for ingots this way, or unsmeltable scraps for selling for gold. All you need are some filled soul gems to recharge the staff as it is used.
- Summoning automatons with the staff takes slightly longer than "traditional" staves that summon creatures. Upon using it, there is a muted "roar" sound effect and a wavering column of light, then the portal opens and the spider or sphere comes out. The portal is also a light blue (not deep purple), of which like is seen when summoning non-daedric entities, such as [Arvak](Skyrim_Arvak.md), [Lucien Lachance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lucien_Lachance), etc.
- The Aetherial Staff is arguably the least powerful of the three possible rewards for the quest [Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages). It is the only reward with a negative side effect, and its summon effect, while unique, is functionally the same as any other Staff of Conjuring. It is also the only of the rewards that is leveled, meaning it is significantly weaker if obtained early. - The lowest level version has a 30% change to summon a Broken Dwarven Spider, which in turn has a 60% to drop Dwarven metal scraps, meaning the staff could potentially be used as source of infinite Dwarven metal, although this is of limited benefit.

## Bugs
- The Sphere Guardian is affected by fortify conjuration, but none of the other spheres or spiders are.
