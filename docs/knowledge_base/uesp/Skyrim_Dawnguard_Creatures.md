# Dawnguard Creatures


## New Creature Types
- **[Ancestor Moth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth)** — New passive creature which can be caught to yield [Ancestor Moth Wings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestor_Moth_Wing).
- **[Frost Giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md)** — Found in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). Each one possesses a jewel paragon.
- **[Gargoyle](Skyrim_Gargoyle.md)** — New creatures which guard the undead. Can be summoned to fight for the player. - **[Gargoyle Brute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gargoyle_Brute)** — A stronger version of the gargoyle.
- **[Gargoyle Sentinel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gargoyle_Sentinel)** — The strongest of the gargoyle variants.
- **[Shellbug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug)** — New creatures which yield [chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug_Chitin) when "[mined](Skyrim_Mining.md)" by a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe).

## New Named Creatures
- **[Arvak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvak)** — A skeletal horse which can be summoned as a mount after completing the [related quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Cairn_Horse_Quest).
- **[Bran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bran)** — An armored [husky](Skyrim_Husky.md) in [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md), available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md#Creatures) by members of [the Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).
- **[Cu Sith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cu Sith)** — A [death hound](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md) in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md), available as a follower to those siding with the vampires during the add-on's [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests).
- **[Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md)** — A [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) that inhabits the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md), he can teach the words of the [Soul Tear](Skyrim_Soul_Tear.md) shout.
- **[The Forgemaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forgemaster)** — A powerful [Dwarven Automaton](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) guarding the [Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge).
- **[Garmr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garmr)** — A death hound in Volkihar Keep, available as a follower to those siding with the vampires during the add-on's main questline.
- **[Naaslaarum](Skyrim_Naaslaarum.md)** — The twin dragon of Voslaarum. Guards a [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) for the [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md) shout in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).
- **[Reaper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reaper)** — The shadowy figure of an executioner with burning red eyes. Found in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).
- **[Sceolang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sceolang)** — An armored husky in Fort Dawnguard, available as a follower by members of the Dawnguard.
- **[Voslaarum](Skyrim_Voslaarum.md)** — The twin dragon of Naaslaarum. Guards a word wall for the Drain Vitality shout in the Forgotten Vale.

- **[Whispering Spirit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whispering_Spirit)** — Found flying around the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

### Undead
These new undead creatures are known as the [Soul Guard](Lore_Soul_Guard.md), guardians of the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

- **[Boneman](Skyrim_Boneman.md)** — [Skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) variant encountered in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).
- **[Keeper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Keeper)** — Undead warrior equipped with [Dragonplate armor](Skyrim_Dragon_Armor.md) and [Dragonbone weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Weapons) encountered in the Soul Cairn.
- **[Mistman](Skyrim_Mistman.md)** — Skeleton variant found in the Soul Cairn.
- **[Wrathman](Skyrim_Wrathman.md)** — [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) -like enemy encountered in the Soul Cairn.

## Unused Creatures
*Main article: [Unused Creatures](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unused_Creatures)*

- **[Load Dummy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Load_Dummy)** — Has the same race as the [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) version of [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) in game-data (may be used for his voice in that form). Cannot be reached in normal gameplay, PC players can use `tcl` command in console to find it in a tiny room far beneath the main hall of [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).
- **[Mist Man](Skyrim_Mistman.md)** — Skeleton variant, similar to the in-game Mistman. Doesn't exist and isn't used in game, but existing game-data suggests it was meant to be summonable.

## Notes
- Although there are no ***new* [Werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) types** added by the add-on, the game data for [race](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mod_File_Format_RACE) files has been updated for werewolf variants, along with some loot and outfit changes. In addition, werewolves can now be encountered in the wild and along roads, instead of just in [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) bases.
- A second **[Wounded Frostbite Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wounded_Frostbite_Spider)** can be found in [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md), previously a unique creature in the original version of the game.
