[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-place-Solitude_Stables.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Stables.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Stables.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Stables.jpg) Solitude Stables **Solitude Stables** is the [stable](Skyrim_Stables.md) serving the city of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Located at [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md), a [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) can be bought from [Geimund](Skyrim_Geimund.md) for 1000 gold. If Geimund dies, [Horm](Skyrim_Horm.md) will instead sell you the stables' horses.

## Related Quests
- **[Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md)**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).
- **[Horse Whisperer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Whisperer)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Wild_Horses.md)</sup>: Purchase a map that lets you find wild horses.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Exterior
The stables use the north end of the yard shared with [Katla's farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md). There are three steps up to the front door, which isn't locked during the day. Most of the plants and mushrooms can be found against the walls of either the building or stables. There are many barrels of food in the yard.

## Solitude Stables
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-interior-Solitude_Stables.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Stables.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Stables.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Stables.jpg) Solitude Stables On your left when entering is an open crate against the southeast wall containing containing five [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and two [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). Above this is a rack holding two [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). There is a table against the southwest wall holding two potatoes, a haunch of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), several cheese wheels and wedges, a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [pheasant breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast) and several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). Under the table is an open crate containing a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg), and an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing). To the right is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front. On the hearth is a piece of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), a haunch of venison and a cabbage in a cast iron pot.

There is a round wooden table and two chairs in the middle of the room holding a loaf of bread. There are two racks hanging from the ceiling holding four [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). To the right of the fireplace is a cupboard holding four [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), two [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), several loaves of bread a haunch of venison, a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a couple of cheese wheels, and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). To the northwest is a doorway to a bedroom, with a stack of three barrels between the cupboard and doorway.

Against the northeast wall is a small unit holding a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) and a wall shelf holding various [cups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup), [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet) and [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate). Also against this wall is the staircase leading to the top floor. Under the stairs is a cupboard holding an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) and a couple of sacks. To the left is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, three cabbages, three potatoes and a [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration). To the left of this is a second bedroom containing an unowned single bed in the northern corner, with an unlocked chest at the foot of the bed. To the left of the bed is an end table holding a common book. In the southern corner of the room is a stack of three more barrels. The first bedroom contains a single bed in the western corner with an end table to the right.

At the top of the stairs, against the northwest wall, is a small unit holding a selection of [iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), and [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) and a sack. To the left is a small table and two chairs. On the table is the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger) and a level-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) containing a considerable amount of gold and a steel ingot. The upstairs only extends halfway across the building, with a railing around the edge. Against the railing is an unowned single bed with an end table to the right and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of the bed.

## Notes

## Bugs
- If you buy a horse from here and the horse is killed, attempting to purchase a new horse from the same stable results in 1000 gold being deducted, and [Geimund](Skyrim_Geimund.md) stating that your horse is "the one with the saddle", despite no saddled horse appearing at the stables here. Instead, your new horse spawns at the [Riften Stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- The horses here are able to be taken for free if you befriend anyone in the [Solitude Stables Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude Stables Faction), due to the horses being owned by the stables faction specifically. The problem is that horses cost 1,000 gold, making them way too valuable to let stablemasters let you ride them for free after completing a small favor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19062)) The ownership of the horses was changed to just Geimund, rather than the faction, preventing this issue.
