# Sethiete

**Sethiete** (also called **Seth**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARGideon-1)</sup> is a deity that is worshipped across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The nature of Sethiete is unknown, but what is known is his influence. His places of worship are known as the [Temples of Sethiete](Lore_Temple_of_Sethiete.md), and one of his temples is a landmark in the city of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) that anchors the main street with the Royal Palace.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOT1-2)</sup> Seth's followers held vast control over the city of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) during the Imperial Simulacrum.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARGideon-1)</sup> There is the exclamation, *"By Sethiete"*, which is used in the Kingdom of Camlorn.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MOT2-3)</sup>

[Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) was a follower of Sethiete and a member of the temple in Camlorn, four years before he impersonated Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) and started the decade of conflict known as the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MOT4-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Dark Elf name generator](Lore_Dunmer_Names.md#Arena_and_Daggerfall) in *Arena* and *Daggerfall* features "-seth" as an ending particle for male forenames, seen in instances such as [Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md).
