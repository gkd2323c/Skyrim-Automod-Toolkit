## Mahogany
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Twisted_Mountain_Mahogany.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Twisted_Mountain_Mahogany.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Twisted_Mountain_Mahogany.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Twisted_Mountain_Mahogany.jpg) Mahogany Mahogany is a variety of tree that can be found around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and is commonly used as material for a variety of objects such as furnishings and gear. ## Mammoth Ear
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/ON-furnishing-Plant%2C_Tall_Mammoth_Ear.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Plant%2C_Tall_Mammoth_Ear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Plant,_Tall_Mammoth_Ear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Plant,_Tall_Mammoth_Ear.jpg) Mammoth Ear Mammoth Ears are a broadleaf tropical plant found in temperate climates. ## Mana Bloom
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/OBR-place-Paradise_Savage_Garden.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Paradise_Savage_Garden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Paradise_Savage_Garden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Paradise_Savage_Garden.jpg) Mana Blooms Mana Blooms are rare flowering plants found almost exclusively on the plane of [Gaiar Alata](Lore_Gaiar_Alata.md), but some mages were also captable of growing the plants. ## Mangrove
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Young_Mangrove.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Tree%2C_Young_Mangrove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Young_Mangrove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Tree,_Young_Mangrove.jpg) Mangrove Mangroves are a wetland tree found throughout Tamriel, with many mangrove swamps that can be found along the coasts of Valenwood, as well as in parts of western Cyrodiil.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Po T-3)</sup> Mangrove trees thrive in the swamps of Black Marsh, particularly along the shores of the [Oliis Bay](Lore_Oliis_Bay.md), where they form a nigh-impenetrable wall. An [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) folktale claims that these mangroves were once giant spiders that earned the Hist's wrath in an ancient conflict and were transformed into trees.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TIC-4)</sup><sup>:52</sup> The pirate [Red Bramman](Lore_Red_Bramman.md) took advantage of a dense thicket of mangroves hiding a narrow river near [Soulrest](Lore_Soulrest.md) to escape capture from the [Imperial Navy](Lore_Imperial_Navy.md) before later being found.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3-5)</sup>

Mangroves can also be found on islands including [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) and [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Conservatory_Furnishings/Trees)

## Marshmerrow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/MW-flora-Marshmerrow.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Marshmerrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Marshmerrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Marshmerrow.jpg) Marshmerrow Marshmerrow is a common type of cane that grows abundantly anywhere there's water in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), harvested for its sweet pulp. It has modest magical properties when eaten fresh or prepared,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> and is among the most common and cheap [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) ingredients in the province. It is a very important crop in the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), but it grows in the wild in other similar regions, such as [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md) and the [Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAF-8)</sup> While it has no effect on the breeding habits of the [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md), it can make for a good feed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-KBRN-9)</sup> Marshmerrow cakes are sweet treats which can be dusted with powdered [pearl](Lore_Pearl.md) and sugar.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONQuest-10)</sup> The Marshmerrow shares its name with the Dunmeri martial art practiced by the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWDeesei-11)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Marshmerrow)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Marshmerrow](Lore_Marshmerrow_(alchemy).md)

## Mushroom Tree Sapling
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SI-flora-Mushroom_Tree_Sapling.jpg/200px-SI-flora-Mushroom_Tree_Sapling.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-flora-Mushroom_Tree_Sapling.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-flora-Mushroom_Tree_Sapling.jpg) Mushroom Tree Sapling (Mania variant) [Mushroom Tree](Lore_Mushroom_Tree.md) saplings are found across the Shivering Isles. The Dementia sapling produces the equally dangerous [Black Tar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Tar), a toxin in both solid and liquid form that is collected from the tree itself or from the strings of tar that hang from its limbs.

**Found in:** - [Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)
