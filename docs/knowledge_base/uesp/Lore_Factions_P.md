## The Pale Watch
The [Pale Watch](Lore_Pale_Watch.md) was a knightly order from [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Pledging fealty to the monarch of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), they were fierce adherents to their oath of vigil, but eventually descended into banditry.

## Penitus Oculatus
[Penitus Oculatus](Lore_Penitus_Oculatus.md) means "The Inner Eyes." As their name suggests, they are the eyes, the sword, and the shield of the [Mede Dynasty](Lore_Mede_Dynasty.md).

They seemingly took up the duties of the [Imperial Order of Blades](Lore_Blades.md) and [Imperial Guard](Lore_Imperial_Guard.md) after the fall of the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md).

## Peryite's Chosen
[Peryite's Chosen](Lore_Peryite%27s_Chosen.md) is a cult of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) worshipers that operated in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) around the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). The group was led by an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) named Yamighu and looked into establishing a headquarters in the Black Kiergo district of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md). Hunting down a [skeever](Lore_Skeever.md) and fashioning its pelt into a hat is considered a rite of initiation by the group. Followers of Peryite can wake up to find their skeevers transformed by his blessing, which was documented as occurring with the group's leader, Yamighu. They were also known to raise swamp skeever pups into enormous beasts, riding the plaguewater skeevers and pestilent [guars](Lore_Guar.md) as mounts.

## Phyllocid Dynasty
The [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md) was the royal family of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) that governed the province from the ancient capital of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md) and later in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) during its time under the regime of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) and the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). Each monarch is adorned with the Diadem of [Diagna](Lore_Diagna.md) when graced with the title of the High King.

## Plessington Clan
The [Plessington Clan](Lore_Plessington_Clan.md) was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble family hailing from [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md). They were known to led a [knightly order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) called the [Order of the Cup](Lore_Order_of_the_Cup.md), which also served as the protectors of the family. In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Lord Plessington led the order and was very eager to help in all the affairs of High Rock. He was known to send them off to right a wrong and come back with a story or two, with no other, greater purpose, beyond the charity.

The events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) led to the incorporation of the fiefdom of Ykalon into the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). It also caused most of the forests in Ykalon to burn down. It is unknown how this affected the members of the Plessington Clan, however.

## Predecessors
The [Predecessors](Lore_Predecessors.md) were the mortal inhabitants of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) in bygone eras, whose civilizations were wiped out by the cycle of the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md). They are responsible for the many ancient ruins that dot the realm.

## Priests of Order
The [Priests of Order](Lore_Priests_of_Order.md) are mortal inhabitants of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) who have betrayed their liege, [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), to join [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md) and his [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md). With their link to Jyggalag giving them power, these priests perform rituals at the obelisks to summon [Knights of Order](Lore_Knights_of_Order.md) to the realm. As long as their obelisk is active, they cannot truly be killed. The only way to break the connection between a Priest of Order and their obelisk is to place three Hearts of Order into the obelisk, causing it to overload, then kill the priest before they can repair the damage.

## Prostitutes Guild
The [Prostitutes Guild](Lore_Prostitutes_Guild.md) was sanctioned in [2E 321](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_321) by Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) with the approval of the [Guilds Act](Lore_Guild_Act.md). Each local chapter of the Prostitutes Guild has a Guildmistress who handles appointments with clients and oversees the safety of the guild's [prostitutes](Lore_Prostitution.md).

## Psijic Order
The [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) (rarely spelled **Psijiic Order** and **the Order of Psijics**) or the **Order of PSJJJJ** is the oldest monastic group of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), devoted to the study and practice of [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md), which they call the "Old Ways" or "Elder Way". Though these terms are also used to refer to the religion and customs of the order, related to ideas of meditation and achievable higher states of being, and described as the combination of Mysticism, the Eleven Forces, the divine laws that bind and define the nature of reality, and a deep understanding of the [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md), those [Aedric](Lore_Aedra.md) spirits which established the foundations of life and the world, into a philosophy and way of life. They make their home on the Isle of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). They have often been criticized for hoarding their knowledge and in turn mocking other scholars for not seeing their way. Some sources claim that the Psijics were not the original practicioners of the Elder Way, but instead learned it from the original inhabitants of Nirn, the Ehlnofey.
