# Peryite's Chosen

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/ON-card-Skinned_Skeever_Cap.png/200px-ON-card-Skinned_Skeever_Cap.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-card-Skinned_Skeever_Cap.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-card-Skinned_Skeever_Cap.png) A Skinned Skeever Cap, worn by some members Peryite's Chosen is a cult of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) worshipers that operated in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) around the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). The group was led by an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) named Yamighu and looked into establishing a headquarters in the Black Kiergo district of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BKPp P-1)</sup> Hunting down a [skeever](Lore_Skeever.md) and fashioning its pelt into a hat is considered a rite of initiation by the group.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SSC-2)</sup> Followers of Peryite can wake up to find their skeevers transformed by his blessing, which was documented as occurring with the group's leader, Yamighu.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blightlord Skeever-3)</sup> They were also known to raise swamp skeever pups into enormous beasts,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SSP-4)</sup> riding the plaguewater skeevers and pestilent [guars](Lore_Guar.md) as mounts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PSS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PG-6)</sup>
