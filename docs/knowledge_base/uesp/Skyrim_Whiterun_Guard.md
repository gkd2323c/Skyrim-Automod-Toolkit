[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-npc-Whiterun_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Whiterun_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Whiterun_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Whiterun_Guard.jpg) A guard from Whiterun **Whiterun Guards** are [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md).

These guards serve under [Jarl Balgruuf](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), and will defend the city during the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md). Should the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) forces gain control of the city, they will be replaced by [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) under the service of [Jarl Vignar](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md).

Whiterun Guards, like all guards, wear a set of their city's armor, shield and helm, but in yellow color. In addition they wear fur boots but no gauntlets. Whiterun's guards are armed with an Imperial sword, an Imperial bow, a random number from twelve to twenty steel arrows and a steel dagger. They all carry a set of common clothes. In addition they may carry two or all of the following: a torch, a beverage, and a small amount of gold.

They can be found patroling the streets of Whiterun at any times and they sleep in the [Guard Barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks.md). Three Guards are stationed at [Whitewatch Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whitewatch_Tower) and may be attacked by bandits, although the guards will defeat them easily. After [Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md), you can witness [Irileth](Skyrim_Irileth.md) sending three Guards to [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md) and you can follow them to their destination where they will stay for the rest of the game. At any other points in the game, there is roughly thirty Whiterun Guards among the streets, halls, and posts of the city.

Their primary skills are one-handed, two-handed, archery and block. Their secondary skills are heavy armor and light armor.

## Notes
- Despite being initially neutral in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), they still wield Imperial weaponry, and even after the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md) is completed and they formally take a side, they will still make comments indicating their neutrality. On the other hand, even if Battle for Whiterun isn't completed yet, they will comment positively if you wear Imperial armor, and they will engage Stormcloak soldiers in combat.
