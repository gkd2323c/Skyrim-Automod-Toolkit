*Skingrad's wines are the best in Tamriel. I invite you... try them for yourself. Here, it is perfect for grapes, and perfect for wine. The sun and soil here are like my native Hammerfell, but the climate is milder, and the rainfall more reliable.*
—Tamika<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup> Tamika was a famous and renowned [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) vintner whose [wines](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md) were celebrated throughout [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in the late years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHWC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BHCUL-3)</sup> Originally from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), she became a resident of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup> She was also the sister of Talan, a fellow vintner and an [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md) to whom she taught her craft.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHWC-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Talan-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/OBR-misc-Winepress.jpg/200px-OBR-misc-Winepress.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-misc-Winepress.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-misc-Winepress.jpg) Tamika's winepresses Tamika's Dark Rum was a beverage already known in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CK-5)</sup> as was the brand of her rivals, the [Surilie](Lore_Surilie.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Maltholbor-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOAMA-7)</sup> Tamika herself, however, was born much later, in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She hailed from the province of Hammerfell, but eventually moved to Skingrad and established her business there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup> She resided in an opulent three-floor townhouse with a basement,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OBTH-8)</sup> which had once served as an office of Count [Calantius](Lore_Calantius_of_Skingrad.md) centuries earlier.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONCCO-9)</sup>

She considered Skingrad's wines to be the best in all of Tamriel, and its climate perfect for growing grapes and producing fine wine. The sun and soil reminded her of her native Hammerfell, though the climate was milder and the rainfall more reliable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup> By some point before 3E 399, she had already overseen the production of several vintages of her wines,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TV399-10)</sup> and she had come to be known as Tamika of Skingrad.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHWC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BHCUL-3)</sup> She taught her craft to her brother Talan,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHWC-2)</sup> who had once been a member of the prestigious [Imperial Alchemy Symposium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Alchemy_Symposium), though he was later expelled.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BHCUL-3)</sup>

By 3E 433, Tamika had achieved widespread fame. Her skill was considered among the finest,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHWC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BHCUL-3)</sup> and her wines were served in numerous prestigious establishments throughout Cyrodiil. Her wines reached the tables and cellars of many powerful and influential individuals. Among them were individuals such as collector [Umbacano](Lore_Umbacano.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-UM-11)</sup> Francois Motierre of the distinguished [Motierre](Lore_Motierre.md) family,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-FM-12)</sup> [Jakben](Lore_Jakben.md), the Earl of [Imbel](Lore_Imbel.md),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-JEOI-13)</sup> a famous painter [Rythe Lythandas](Lore_Rythe_Lythandas.md),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-RLH-14)</sup> and [Herminia Cinna](Lore_Herminia_Cinna.md), a scholar of the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-HCH-15)</sup>

It was also served at many courts of Cyrodiil's counts and countesses, including [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) Castle, the seat of Count [Ormellius Goldwine](Lore_Ormellius_Goldwine.md),<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Kvatch_Castle-16)</sup> Castle [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), the seat of Count [Regulus Terentius](Lore_Regulus_Terentius.md),<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-CB-17)</sup> [Anvil Castle](Lore_Anvil_Castle.md), the seat of [Millona Umbranox](Lore_Millona_Umbranox.md),<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-AC-18)</sup> [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) Castle, the seat of Count [Andel Indarys](Lore_Andel_Indarys.md),<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-CHC-19)</sup> Castle [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), the seat of Countess [Arriana Valga](Lore_Arriana_Valga.md),<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-CHEC-20)</sup> [Castle Leyawiin](Lore_Castle_Leyawiin.md), the seat of Count [Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md),<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-CLeyawiin-21)</sup> Castle [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), the seat of Count [Janus Hassildor](Lore_Janus_Hassildor.md),<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Castle Skingrad-22)</sup> and even at the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-IP-23)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/OBR-place-Tamika%27s_House.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Tamika%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Tamika%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Tamika%27s_House.jpg) Tamika's House Tamika's alcoholic beverages could be found in [Frostcrag Spire](Lore_Frostcrag_Spire.md),<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-FS-24)</sup> and at the [Blades'](Lore_Blades.md) stronghold of [Cloud Ruler Temple](Lore_Cloud_Ruler_Temple.md).<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-CRT-25)</sup> Tamika's products could also be found in [Weatherleah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Weatherleah),<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Weatherleah-26)</sup> and the Bloated Float Inn in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-TBFI-27)</sup> Her wine was also found at various sites of religious significance, including [Weynon Priory](Lore_Weynon_Priory.md),<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-WP-28)</sup> and chapels throughout Cyrodiil dedicated to [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md),<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-COS-29)</sup> [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md),<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-TGCOJ-30)</sup> [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md),<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-TGCOZ-31)</sup> [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md),<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-TGCOA-32)</sup> [Talos](Lore_Talos.md),<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-GCOT-33)</sup> [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md),<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-COD-34)</sup> and [Mara](Lore_Mara.md).<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-COM-35)</sup>

The wine was also served within the halls of various institutions such as [Office of Imperial Commerce](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Office_of_Imperial_Commerce),<sup>[[35]](#cite_note-OOIC-36)</sup> and [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) Sanctuary.<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-DBS-37)</sup> It could also be found in [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) chapters in Cheydinhal,<sup>[[37]](#cite_note-CFG-38)</sup> Chorrol,<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-FGChorrol-39)</sup> [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md),<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-FGAnvil-40)</sup> [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md),<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-FGBruma-41)</sup> Skingrad,<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-FGSkingrad-42)</sup> [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md),<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-FGLeyawiin-43)</sup> and Bravil,<sup>[[43]](#cite_note-FGBravil-44)</sup> as well as in [Mages Guilds](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) chapters in Cheydinhal,<sup>[[44]](#cite_note-CMG-45)</sup> and Chorrol,<sup>[[45]](#cite_note-Chorrol MG-46)</sup> and at the [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md).<sup>[[46]](#cite_note-AU-47)</sup> Her wine was even known to have reached the tables of some residents of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) in [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[47]](#cite_note-Shivering-48)</sup>

Among the connoisseurs of her wine was the grand [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md) [Sinderion](Lore_Sinderion.md), who refused to reveal his craft's secrets unless he was given a gift of the 399 vintage from both Tamika and the Surilie Brothers.<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-Sinderion-49)</sup>

The people of Skingrad regarded Tamika as a fine and pleasant individual,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup> and her wines were celebrated for their grand quality. Tamika's wines were seen as a fine choice for any meal,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tamika-1)</sup><sup>[[49]](#cite_note-SR-50)</sup> and often compared to the Surilie Brothers' offerings. They were also reasonably priced,<sup>[[49]](#cite_note-SR-50)</sup> though Surilie Brothers claimed that their products were more affordable. Tamika and the Surilies shared a cordial rivalry. The Surilies never spoke poorly of her wines,<sup>[[50]](#cite_note-DS-51)</sup> and although many considered the two brands equal in quality,<sup>[[49]](#cite_note-SR-50)</sup> Davide Surilie himself conceded that their vintages were no finer than Tamika's.<sup>[[50]](#cite_note-DS-51)</sup>

Her employees, Bernadette Peneles and Shameer, worked tirelessly to keep her business running smoothly.<sup>[[49]](#cite_note-SR-50)</sup><sup>[[51]](#cite_note-Shameer-52)</sup><sup>[[52]](#cite_note-BP-53)</sup>

## Products
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/OB-item-Tamika%27s_West_Weald_Wine.png/200px-OB-item-Tamika%27s_West_Weald_Wine.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Tamika%27s_West_Weald_Wine.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Tamika%27s_West_Weald_Wine.png) Tamika's West Weald Wine *Wonderful thing, wine. It's all about the balance. The flavor, the bouquet, the consistency... just wonderful. I'm sorry, what? Training... right. I'll make you a deal: you fetch me a bottle of each of the best wines of Skingrad, and I'll think about it. Both vineyards, Tamika's and Surilie Brothers. Vintage 399. Oh, what a good year that was...*
— [Sinderion](Lore_Sinderion.md)<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-Sinderion-49)</sup> At her winery, Tamika produced Tamika's West Weald Wine,<sup>[[53]](#cite_note-TWWW-54)</sup> among whose recorded vintages were 399 and 415.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TV399-10)</sup><sup>[[54]](#cite_note-TV415-55)</sup> Tamika's Dark Rum, which shared her name, actually predated her and was not of her own making.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CK-5)</sup>

### Tamika's West Weald Wine
Tamika's West Weald Wine was an alcoholic beverage produced at Tamika's Wineyards.<sup>[[53]](#cite_note-TWWW-54)</sup> Gaston Surilie of the Surilie Brothers considered their 415 vintage particularly good, while the 399 vintage was regarded as a once-in-a-lifetime experience.<sup>[[55]](#cite_note-Gaston_Surilie-56)</sup> Master Alchemist Sinderion himself agreed that the year 3E 399 was exceptionally good for both Tamika and Surilie's wines.<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-Sinderion-49)</sup> This was reflected in the exceptional quality of both Tamika's and Surilie's products.<sup>[[56]](#cite_note-Surilie-57)</sup> The 399 vintages were known to be more potently fortifying and restorative than those of 415, and both surpassed the usual standard.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TV399-10)</sup><sup>[[54]](#cite_note-TV415-55)</sup><sup>[[53]](#cite_note-TWWW-54)</sup> Overall, however, Tamika's products were marginally more potent.<sup>[[56]](#cite_note-Surilie-57)</sup><sup>[[57]](#cite_note-All-58)</sup>
