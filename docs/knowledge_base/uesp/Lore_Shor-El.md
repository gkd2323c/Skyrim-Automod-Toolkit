Shor-El was an [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WWY-1)</sup> during the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Like other emperors, they became a part of the oversoul of the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md). According to Shor-El, "Cyrodiil became an egg" during the [Dragon Break](Lore_Middle_Dawn.md) caused by [Marukhati Selectives](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WWY-1)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1.](#note_nb1) | The dates of Shor-El's reign are unknown, but they are mentioned after Hestra in *[Where were you when the Dragon Broke?](Lore_Where_Were_You_..._Dragon_Broke.md)* as part of the oversoul of emperors. As all five rulers of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) are known, and no legitimate emperors arose between then and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-General-2)</sup> Shor-El must have ruled during the Alessian Empire. An unknown emperor reigned during [Fervidius Tharn](Lore_Fervidius_Tharn.md)'s tenure as Arch-Prelate of the Maruhkati Selective,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CATAYQ-3)</sup> starting in [1E 1188](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1188).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HTo N-4)</sup> It is unknown if this was Shor-El or a different figure entirely. |
