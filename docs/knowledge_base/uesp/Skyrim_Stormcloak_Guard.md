[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Stormcloak_Guard_%28Stonehills%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Stormcloak_Guard_%28Stonehills%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Guard_(Stonehills).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stormcloak_Guard_(Stonehills).jpg) Stormcloak Guard **Stormcloak Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) that can be found in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) and [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) when the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over their respective Hold and they act as the military and police force throughout that Hold.

Each guard wears a set of [Stormcloak armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Stormcloak_Equipment), a helm (either Stormcloak, hide or scaled), fur gauntlets, fur boots and a steel (or hide) shield. Their melee weapon ranges from one that is iron and one-handed to one that is steel and two-handed. They have a hunting bow, a random number from ten to twenty steel arrows and a steel dagger. They all carry a set of common clothes. In addition they may carry from none to all of the following: a torch, a food item, a small amount of gold, and an extra helm.

Their primary skills are one-handed, two-handed, archery and block. Their secondary skills are heavy armor and light armor.

The guard pictured can be found at [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md) when the Stormcloaks take over [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md).
