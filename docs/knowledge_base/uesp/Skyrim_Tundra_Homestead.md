This Creation was made by [Elianora](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3231858), the author of many Skyrim housing mods.

## Official Summary
*Tundra Homestead provides a cozy cottage just outside Whiterun. Complete with crafting stations and fully furnished, this home is a quiet getaway from adventuring. Full support for spouses and adopted children included. Created by Elianora. (House outside the city is purchased from the Jarl's Steward in Dragonsreach.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds **[Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)** east of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). The cottage is initially locked, and there is a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Homestead_For_Sale) outside that tells you to buy it from the city's steward. Buying the property gives you the typical objective to visit your new home.

Tundra Homestead comes fully furnished, unlike all other houses except [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). It is a small house with two floors and an exterior estate. There is no prerequisite quest, making it the simplest house to obtain, though at 7,500 gold it is slightly more expensive than a fully furnished [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md) at 6,800 gold. The property contains many [crafting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crafting) stations, including a "Baking Surface" which acts as an [oven](Skyrim_Oven.md) for access to the [baking](Skyrim_Baking.md) recipes added by [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md). It also possesses a large display room in the cellar, with places to store unique items such as [dragon priest masks](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) and [bugs in a jar](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Bugs_in_a_Jar).

## Notes
- This Creation uses an `.esm` file rather than `.esl` to store its data.
- Speak with the current steward of Dragonsreach with 7500 gold in your inventory. Choose the option to purchase the home. Quickly back out of the dialogue and activate any nearby container (or follower) and store all your gold within it before the house key is given. Once the dialogue ends go back in and take your gold back. The key to Tundra Homestead will now be in your possession free of charge.
