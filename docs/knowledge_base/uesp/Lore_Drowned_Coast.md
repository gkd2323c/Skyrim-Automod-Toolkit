[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/ON-map-Drowned_Coast.jpg/200px-ON-map-Drowned_Coast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Drowned_Coast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Drowned_Coast.jpg) Map of Drowned Coast circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). The **Drowned Coast** is the westernmost region of the greater [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md) region. The Drowned Coast is the home of the city of [Woodhearth](Lore_Woodhearth.md), the capital of Greenshade.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Woodhearth-1)</sup> The northern area is mountainous with several [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins scattered about, including [Rulanyil's Fall](Lore_Rulanyil%27s_Fall.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Rulanyil.27s_Fall-2)</sup> On the other side of the mountains is the fortress known as the [Seaside Sanctuary](Lore_Seaside_Sanctuary.md), which safeguards the western shores of Valenwood.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Seaside_Sanctuary-3)</sup> The southern area is riddled with swamps and is the home of the [Keerilth Clan](Lore_Keerilth.md) of [Vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), who haunt the nearby township of [Longhaven](Lore_Longhaven.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Longhaven-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MAS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Drowned_Coast-6)</sup>

There also around four islands located in the ocean south of the coastline, the easternmost isle being the largest. Though they often appear on maps of Tamriel, little is known about them.
