[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/ON-place-Zenithar%27s_Abbey.jpg/200px-ON-place-Zenithar%27s_Abbey.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zenithar%27s_Abbey.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zenithar%27s_Abbey.jpg) Zenithar's Abbey Zenithar's Abbey is an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) ruin north of [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md) in central [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOMap-1)</sup>. It once was a thriving bazaar that encouraged not just the trade of items, but of ideas. The divine relic, the Golden Anvil, resided within. Trades in the Abbey were organized by a group of trade-ministers. When Gavos Douar lead the Abbey as Arbitrator, these trade-ministers became unhappy with his regulations on trade, and lead an uprising against him. The rebellion ended with Gavos locked within the chapel, along with the Golden Anvil. The Abbey fell into complete ruin afterwards, due to the trade-ministers fighting among each other.

In 2E 582, the [Vestige](Lore_Vestige.md) and the Resolute of Zenithar priest Daluion ventured within the Abbey, uncovering its past and eventually recovering the Golden Anvil and freeing Gavos Douar.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAnvil-2)</sup>
