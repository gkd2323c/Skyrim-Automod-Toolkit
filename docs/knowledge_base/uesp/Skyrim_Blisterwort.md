## Ingredients
24 guaranteed samples can be found in 17 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 3 in [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 in [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 3-7 guaranteed samples sold by [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md)
- 3-7 guaranteed samples sold by [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md)
- 3-6 guaranteed samples sold by the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md)

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) effect for this ingredient is 3 instead of 5. This is only relevant if blisterwort and [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 40% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Restore Health potion.
