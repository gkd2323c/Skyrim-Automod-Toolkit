[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg) Death Hound **Death Hounds** are creatures added to Skyrim with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md). They are guaranteed to carry a [death hound collar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Hound_Collar) and [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat), as well as having a 10% chance of carrying minor loot, including leveled [rings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring), [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), and gold.

They can be found both in the wilderness, as well as interior locations such as [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) and the [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md). In any location that they are found in, there are always [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) nearby. Generally they fill the guard-dog role, often patrolling around hallways or around doorways.

They do [frost damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage) with their bite (10pt Frost, 50pt [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md), referred to by the game as "Death Hound Attack") as well as having a minor frost cloak effect (1pt/sec, seen in the Active Effects menu as "Ice Cloak Freeze") while in combat with them; the game [describes](Skyrim_Loading_Screens.md#Creatures) them as having a bite "as cold as the grave".

## Notes
- Two Death Hounds, [Cu Sith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cu Sith) and [Garmr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garmr), can be used as [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md#Creatures) if you side with the [Volkihar vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) in Dawnguard's [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests). If you side with the Dawnguard, however, they will be a part of the first wave of enemies, outside the castle.
- Death Hounds will flee if the Dragonborn is in [Beast Form](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beast_Form), even if the howl of terror/howl of fear is not used.
- Death hounds have a black, misty aura that surrounds them when they are aware of you, or suspicious of your (or followers') presence. If they are unaware of you, the aura vanishes.
