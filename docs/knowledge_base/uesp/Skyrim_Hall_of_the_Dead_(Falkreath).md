[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).jpg.md) The Hall of the Dead The **Hall of the Dead** is both [Falkreath's](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) only temple and home for both [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md), the priest of Arkay, and [Kust](Skyrim_Kust.md), who is responsible for looking after Falkreath's cemetery.

The living quarters are at the northern end of the house, while the southern end is given over for a shrine and for devotions. It is found at the far western end of Falkreath with cemeteries all around.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There is a [shrine of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay) on either side of the front porch entrance, as well as two tall posts with banners of Arkay hanging from them. Falkreath has been the site of many battles, helping to feed its massive cemetery. Many of the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) see it as a grand honor to be buried there among the ancestors and heroes. The first time you visit Falkreath, as you approach the Hall, you will witness a funeral taking place for the daughter of [Mathies](Skyrim_Mathies.md) and his wife [Indara Caerellia](Skyrim_Indara_Caerellia.md). Asking about will reveal their daughter was brutally murdered. [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md), as the priest to [Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arkay), will be performing the service, while [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md), the culprit, is in [Falkreath jail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Jail). Visiting and speaking to Sinding gives you the option to initiate the related [quest](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md). There are several [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plants and fungi growing among the gravestones around the hall.

### Hall of the Dead (Falkreath)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).jpg.md) The living area [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Falkreath%29_02.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath)_02.jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath)_02.jpg.md) The shrine of Arkay It is a single-room house. In the middle of the south wall there is a shrine of Arkay, with a wooden bench and a couple of chairs in front and a couple of barrels in the corners. Opposite the door is a long wooden table and bench, the table holds a [chicken's egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bucket containing three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). Above the table is a rack with three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging from it and three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging from the ceiling beside it. To the right of the table is a fireplace with a kettle containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) on the hearth and a round wooden table and two chairs in front of the fire.

This table holds a bottle of alto wine, one of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). Coming out from the north wall are two owned single beds with an end table between them and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot at the foot of the left-hand one. On the end table is a bottle of Nord mead and a copy of *[Aedra and Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aedra_and_Daedra)*. Against the east wall is a wardrobe containing common clothes and having a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace) on top, a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel and a dresser with more clothes in. On the dresser are two more pine thrush eggs, four bulbs of garlic, two bunches of frost mirriam, two bunches of elves ear and a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead), with three garlic braids and a bunch of each frost mirriam and elves ear hanging from the ceiling above the dresser.
