[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-place-Shrouded_Vale.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shrouded_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shrouded_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shrouded_Vale.jpg) The Shrouded Vale while on Tamriel The forest realm is a mysterious realm of existence. It was created by the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md) as part of a pact with the denizens of the [Shrouded Vale](Lore_Shrouded_Vale.md). Every one hundred years or so, a caretaker is left alone on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) to provide for the [Fading Tree](Lore_Fading_Tree.md). In return, the Wilderking provides a safe haven for Shrouded Hollow, causing the village to disappear, only coming back to choose a new caretaker.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOHenodras-1)</sup>

When the village disappears, it is transported to an unknown place referred to as the forest realm.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> This realm is described as a place totally unlike [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), without hunger or suffering, where the denizens of Shrouded Hollow live peacefully and without fear.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Even the light in this place is different in comparison to Tamriel, which the inhabitants of the Vale refer to as the "mundane world".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
