[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-place-Karthmad.jpg/200px-ON-place-Karthmad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Karthmad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Karthmad.jpg) Karthmad circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Karthmad is a mountain in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) that stands at the head of the valley of the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md). It is better known as the site of the city of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), or [Nchuand-Zel](Lore_Nchuand-Zel.md) as it was called by its original builders, the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md). The stronghold of Nchuand-Zel was carved by the Dwemer into the subterranean heart of Karthmad and then expanded upwards until its structures penetrated above the surface.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOMASIS-1)</sup>
