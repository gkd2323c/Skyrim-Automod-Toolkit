# Storage Room


The Storage Room, like its counterparts, the [Bedrooms](Skyrim_Bedrooms.md) and [Armory](Skyrim_Armory.md), has a patio area on its roof when completed. If the Storage Room is built along with either or both of the other additions, these patio areas have walkways between them.

The following is a list of possible furniture items and decorations which can be added by using the carpenter's workbench in the Storage Room. It is not necessary to purchase or build all furnishing options; you can pick and choose to better dictate style. However, all furnishing options are in a pre-arranged location and only ingredients and standard items can be moved. Oftentimes, the name of the furnishing option only indicates the primary piece of furniture, and actually includes various additional [containers](Skyrim_Containers.md) and accessories.

## Notes
- Sometimes, the exterior door for the patio cannot be accessed due to a wall glitch when trying to come back into the house.
- When adding exterior furniture, items may not appear. It's recommended you save before each piece you create.

## Bugs
- Multiple exterior items may refuse to generate properly. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Sometimes you may build an item, have it placed, and have it reappear as an item you can build again in the worktable. When selecting this item the second time, your resources are used, but no item is made.
- The patio door will sometimes be impossible to open without specifically targeting the handle, instead of any other part of the door.
- The door, foundation, and floor may not appear when built, making it impossible to complete the addition.
