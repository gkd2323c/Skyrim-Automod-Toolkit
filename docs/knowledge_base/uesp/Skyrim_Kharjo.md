[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-npc-Kharjo.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kharjo.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kharjo.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kharjo.jpg) Kharjo **Kharjo** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who guards and travels with the rest of the [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) led by [Ahkari](Skyrim_Ahkari.md). He can be found outside either [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) or [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), and walks the route in between.

Kharjo wears a set of [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor), including matching [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Gauntlets) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Boots). He is equipped with a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). After you return his amulet, Kharjo will wear it as well. You can take it back from his inventory when he is an active follower.

Kharjo can also be [recruited](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md) into the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md). When told to depart, or after waiting for too long, he will return to his original caravan.

## Quest-Related Events

### Amulet of the Moon
Upon meeting him, he will say:

- *"[Bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) harass the caravans at every step, but Khajiit usually scare them off."*
- **Bandits giving you trouble?**
- *"A few days ago, we were ambushed. A nuisance, really, but many of the marauders had quick fingers. One of them stole my [Moon Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Amulet), given to me by my mother when I was just a cub. It is my only memory of home in this cold land."*

- - **I could get your amulet back.**
- *"You are kind to offer. We believe the bandits are a part of a group headquartered here. Be careful. I miss my amulet, but a life cannot be replaced as easily."*

- - **Sorry to hear that.**
- *"We are used to the cycle of gain and loss. It is no trouble."*

Upon returning the amulet to him, he will express his thanks:

- **I have your amulet.**
- *"There it is. Ah.... Home.... If you ever need the skills of a Khajiit warrior by your side, I would be honored to travel with you. Until then, here. This is for you, in thanks."*

He can now be recruited as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).

## Dialogue
When spoken to directly you can ask him a couple of questions:

- **You guard the caravan?**
- *"Yes, I was hired to protect the others as we walk the roads of Skyrim. It is a thankless task and I would rather be back home in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), but I have little choice. Ahkari freed me from a prison in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and now I must repay my debt to him * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *. A word of advice, my friend -- do not mix gambling and drink. Taken together, they will empty your pockets of every septim."*

- **What dangers does the caravan face?**
- *"Skyrim is filled with all manner of dangerous beasts. [Wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md), [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md), [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) and [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) would all like to make a meal of us. But the [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) are the most fearsome. Lucky for us, they don't seem to hunt along the roads."*

## Notes
- Kharjo is one of only two Khajiit followers available in the game, the other being [J'zargo](Skyrim_J%27zargo.md).
- Kharjo may have been depicted, albeit with some changes, in the card [Caravan Enforcer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Caravan Enforcer) in the [Heroes of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Heroes_of_Skyrim) card expansion for *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*.
- The Editor ID for his dialogue topics in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) mistakenly refers to him as **Khorja**.
- The base game version of Oblivion's *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV:_Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions "Kharjo" under the male Khajiit "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".
