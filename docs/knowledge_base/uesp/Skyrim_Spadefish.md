## Sources
Spadefish can be caught via [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md) in temperate streams when raining, like in Solitude, or in guaranteed locations without fishing in groups of 3:

- 6 within [Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md)
- [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md)
- [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md)
- Under the waterfall just south of [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md)
- Under the waterfall in front of [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md)
