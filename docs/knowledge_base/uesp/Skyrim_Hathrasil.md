[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-npc-Hathrasil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hathrasil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hathrasil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hathrasil.jpg) Hathrasil **Hathrasil** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who lives in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He spends most of the day working at the silver smelter alongside [Omluag](Skyrim_Omluag.md). Like many other workers, he sleeps in the [Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md) at night.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a copy of the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Omluag%27s_Room) to Omluag's room, as well as some [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

He lives a simple life, starting his day at 8am, and heading to the smelter to begin his work. At 7pm, after working for eleven hours, he heads to the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) to drink for three hours. He then heads down to his room in the Warrens and hangs out there until his bedtime at midnight.

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

## Dialogue
He is a man of few words, as can be seen in his brief greetings while he's at the smelter:

*"They don't like us talking to people when we work."* *"Got metal to shape."* *"Busy with the smelter."* When he's at the Silver-Blood Inn:

*"Just here to drink."* If spoken to while he's working:

**Who's in charge of the smelter?** *"[Mulush](Skyrim_Mulush_gro-Shugurz.md). Big [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) fella. Can't miss him."*
## Conversations
Hathrasil may be seen having random conversations with Omluag or Mulush while working at the smelter:

**Omluag**: *"First [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), and now [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md). Everywhere they go, the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) bring nothing but ruin."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Ain't nothing for it. They're still in charge."*

**Omluag**: *"Divines, I'm tired. This is too much work."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Just keep at it, or Mulush will beat you."*

**Hathrasil**: *"Careful of the molten metal. That gets on you, kiss your fingers goodbye."*
**Omluag**: *"I know what I'm doing. Stop telling me."*

**Hathrasil**: *"Surprised to see you working. Mulush almost broke your arm yesterday."*
**Omluag**: *"Does it look like I can afford losing a day's wage? That damn Orc isn't getting the best of me."*

**Mulush**: *"I could smelt metal in half the time as you weak-willed pink-skins. Do I have to do everything?"*
**Hathrasil**: *"No Mulush. I'll work harder."*

**Mulush**: *"I don't want any of you thieving workers even thinking about stealing any silver. If I see you sneaking off with even one speck of silver, I'll break your leg and throw you into the river."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Yes, Mulush."*

He can also be seen having the following conversation with Omluag at the inn:

**Omluag**: *"Another foul day at the smelter."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Mmmhmm."*
**Omluag**: *"'Mmmhmm'? I've been working with you for over 10 years, and all you have to say is 'mmmhmm'?"*
**Hathrasil**: *"Mmmhmm."*
**Omluag**: *"Sometimes I think we deserve these miserable lives of ours."*

## Unused Dialogue
Befitting his character, Hathrasil has a very brief unused interaction he was meant to have with you in the [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) **MS01B**. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

During the quest, you would first come across Omluag and Hathrasil talking to each other, although the exact contents of their conversation are unknown, it's likely that Omluag would've tried convincing Hathrasil to help him aid the Forsworn by sabotaging the smelter.

After this, you could ask him what they were talking about:

**What were you two talking about?** *"Talk to Omluag. His problem, not mine."*
