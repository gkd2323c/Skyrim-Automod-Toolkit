Kinlady Heinarwe was an [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lindafwe-1)</sup> noblewoman who ruled over a castle to the northeast of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md). She was said to have had an affair with the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) King [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md). When the affair came to light, she was so ashamed that she forsook her name, title, and clan, and banished all family and servants from the hall of her place. She thereafter withdrew into her vaults to live alone for the remainder of her life, which became known as the Vaults of Heinarwe.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo H-2)</sup> An altar was made in the halls to commemorate her relationship with Orgnum.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lindafwe-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>
