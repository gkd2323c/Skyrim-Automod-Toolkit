[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-creature-Rahgot.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Rahgot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Rahgot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Rahgot.jpg) Rahgot **Rahgot** is a high-ranked [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) whose sarcophagus is in [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md), on the mountain peak south-southeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He wields a [dragon priest staff](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Staff_(Rahgot).md), and carries his eponymous [mask](Skyrim_Rahgot_(item).md).

Rahgot led the last remnants of the [Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Dragon_Cult.md) until a legion of King Harald's soldiers discovered his hidden stronghold.

For historical information, see the [lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rahgot).

Rahgot knows the following spells: [Greater Ward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Ward), [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Ebonyflesh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebonyflesh), [Command Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Command_Daedra), [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md), and [Flame Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Cloak).

## Related Quests
