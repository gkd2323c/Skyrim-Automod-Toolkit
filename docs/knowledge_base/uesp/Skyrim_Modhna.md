[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-npc-Modhna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Modhna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Modhna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Modhna.jpg) Modhna **Modhna** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [warrior](Skyrim_TrainerTwoHandedMaster.md) and [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) living in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).

She is a low ranking member of [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)'s court and can usually be found feeding on a human cattle. She says that she doesn't care about politics and she only serves the lord of the castle. She often tells you to leave her alone. Her attitude doesn't change even if you become the leader of the Volkihar vampires.

Modhna wears a set of red colored [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). She is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. She carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), and has a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

If you sided with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md), she will be one of the Vampires outside the front gate during the quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md).

## Dialogue
She uses one of the following greetings when spoken to:

- *"I leave politics to others. I serve the lord of the castle."*
- *"I have everything I need right here."*

When sitting in the main dining hall of Volkihar Keep, she may tell you:

- *"Find your own thrall. This one's mine."*

She may simply dismiss you, wanting to be alone:

- *"Leave me in peace."*
