[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-creature-Chicken.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Chicken.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Chicken.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Chicken.jpg) A Chicken **Chickens** are docile, domesticated birds common throughout Skyrim, usually within cities and towns. [Attacking NPC-owned chickens](Skyrim_Crime.md) will earn a [bounty](Skyrim_Crime.md#Bounties), but you can legally harvest [eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) from their nests for use in [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md). Chickens are mainly encountered in settlements without a wall, such as [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md).

With [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, your [steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) can buy you up to three chickens for your homestead if you add an animal pen, though you only get one nest for all three chickens.

## Notes
- Chickens are confusingly labeled as "rabbits" by the game data (with an editor ID of `Chicken Race`). The race stats were obviously copied from the rabbits race, but not fully changed.
- [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) was the only house in the base game where you could have chickens; two come with the Garden improvement.
