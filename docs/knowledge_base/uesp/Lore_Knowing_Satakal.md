Knowing Satakal *Seven Redguard Maxims*

"To deny that the [world](Lore_Nirn.md) must end is to deny that it [began](Lore_Kalpa.md)."

"For the world is the egg that Satakal laid, and the egg that in time Satakal shall eat."

"To know Satakal, consider a river. As a [snake](Lore_Snake.md) sheds its skin and lives on, so a river sheds its water into the sea, yet is reborn at the source."

"To be the Worldskin is to be everything, and to be everything is to be nothing."

"Fear not the unbelievers, for believer and unbeliever alike shall be eaten by the Serpent God."

"Does not the [serpent made of sky above](Lore_The_Serpent.md) reflect the [serpent made of the sea](Lore_Sea_Serpent.md) below? Yea, it is so."
