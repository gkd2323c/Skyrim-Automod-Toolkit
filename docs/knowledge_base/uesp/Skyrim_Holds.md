# Holds


In the absence of a High King, the holds have effectively split from one another, with some even at war with each other. As a consequence, [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) in each hold only care about [crimes](Skyrim_Crime.md) you have committed within that hold, with any bounties you acquire tied to a specific hold. In addition, the [Tribal Orcs](Skyrim_Tribal_Orcs.md) faction and [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> track their crime separately from any of the Nord holds.

Many of the holds have distinct climates, such as the Rift being an aspen forest, Falkreath Hold being a pine forest, and Eastmarch being tundra. Snowy mountain environments are also found in most holds.
