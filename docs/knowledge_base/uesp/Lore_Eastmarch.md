[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-place-Eastmarch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Eastmarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eastmarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Eastmarch.jpg) A view of Eastmarch, looking southwest towards the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md), circa 4E 201 Eastmarch is the eastern [hold](Lore_Hold.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and one of the four known collectively as the [Old Holds](Lore_Old_Holds.md). As its name suggests, it lies against the province's eastern border, close to the [Dunmeth Pass](Lore_Dunmeth_Pass.md), the only passage to and from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) in the area due to the high and rugged [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md).

[Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) is the only sizable city in the otherwise rural province due to the expansive hot springs which dominate the hold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1S-1)</sup> According to legend, these hot springs were created when the hero [Wuunding](Lore_Wuunding.md) fought a mighty Daedra and defeated it with a flaming hammer. The hammer's blows melted the Daedra, leaving behind the numerous hot springs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AMOD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WAT-3)</sup>

[Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md) winds north from Lake Geir into this treacherous hot spot, where it meets the [White River](Lore_White_River.md) which then drains into the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) as it passes Windhelm. [River Yorgrim](Lore_River_Yorgrim.md) also joins with the White River before it ends at the Sea of Ghosts. It drains [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md), which lies west in the hold known as [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup>

## Notable Places
[Amol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Amol) An Imperial fort that was once home to the settlement of Amol. Ansilvund A Nordic tomb built by Holgeir in memory of Fjori, as described in the book [Of Fjori and Holgeir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir). Bonestrewn Crest A [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) tomb. [Darkwater Crossing](Lore_Darkwater_Crossing.md) A village located along the banks of the Darkwater River. [Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md) A river connecting [Lake Geir](Lore_Lake_Geir.md) to the White River. [Eldergleam Sanctuary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eldergleam_Sanctuary) Underground grove and worship site of followers of Kynareth. [Kagrenzel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kagrenzel) (Music City) A [dwarven ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwemer_Ruins) in the Velothi Mountains. [Kynesgrove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kynesgrove) A village located south of Windhelm. [Mistwatch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mistwatch) Region in southern Eastmarch.
