A Letter to Selina II **by [Maximian Axius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Maximian_Axius)**
---
My dearest Selina,

Euphemius was killed this morning by one of those awful [Rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md). We were escorting a supply wagon from [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md) to [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) when a war party of those bastards ambushed us from the cliffs. We fought them off, but poor Euphemius was impaled by one of their spears. The healers at the fort couldn't do anything for him, and I watched him slip away as I held his hand. I don't know how much longer I can stand being here. My loyalty to the Empire, and the strong words [General Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md) delivers to us at each morning muster are the only things keeping me going. The supply ship should arrive tomorrow, and I promise to give the quartermaster my letters so they can finally be sent home. Give my love to the children.

Yours always,

[Maximian Axius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Maximian_Axius)
11 Morning Star 4E 05
