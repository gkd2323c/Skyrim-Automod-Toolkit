## Walkthrough
The fallen tower is atop [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md)'s western mountains a short distance south of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). Two leveled snow animals or creatures (which may include [frost trolls](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md), [ice wolves](Skyrim_Ice_Wolf.md), [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), or [snow bears](Skyrim_Snow_Bear.md)) guard the tower. There is a lit campfire with a pot hanging over it; inside the pot is a bowl of [venison stew](Skyrim_Venison_Stew.md). Behind the fire are three pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md). Across the ramp near the lit campfire is a short flight of stairs. At the end of the stairs is an apprentice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing [leveled loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md). In front of the chest is a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) and a random one-handed [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md).
