# Scuttlebloom

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom.jpg) An active Scuttlebloom [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom_Hidden.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom_Hidden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom_Hidden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Scuttle_Bloom_Hidden.jpg) A camouflaging Scuttlebloom Scuttlebloom, also known as **Scuttle Bloom** is a type of [plant](Lore_Flora.md) native to the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Easily startled, scuttleblooms will retract their leg-like appendages and burrow into the ground when they sense danger, using this behavior as a means of protection. They produce a honey-like substance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Scuttlebloom-2)</sup> When harvested, it can briefly enhance one's mobility.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SS-3)</sup> Some wild scuttleblooms, once captured, can gradually become friendly companions and cease to panic when approached.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Scuttlebloom-2)</sup>

Some scuttleblooms have been kept in terrariums for display. Specimens in such a state have been encountered in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FSB-4)</sup>
