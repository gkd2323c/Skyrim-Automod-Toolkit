## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Deor Woodcutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter) in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).
2. Find [Baldor Iron-Shaper](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md) at the [abandoned lodge](Skyrim_Abandoned_Lodge.md).
3. Retrieve the [map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Source_Map) from [Ancarion](Skyrim_Ancarion.md) at [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md).
4. Return to Baldor in Skaal Village.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Finding Baldor
If you return to [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) after the quest [The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md), you will overhear a conversation between [Fanari](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice) and [Deor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter):

**NOTE: Do not talk to anyone until they finish their conversation—see [bugs](#Bugs).** [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-quest-A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.jpg) *"Something isn't right, Fanari."* **Fanari**: *"Deor, you must be calm. I'm sure there's a good reason Baldor left us."*
**Deor**: *"Something isn't right, Fanari. I feel it in my bones and I smell it in the air. Baldor wouldn't leave without telling someone."*
**Fanari**: *"Don't worry yourself over nothing, Deor. Do you want a head full of gray hairs before you've seen forty winters?"*
**Deor**: *"It makes my heart heavy that you don't believe me, Fanari. Baldor could be in danger, and you will do nothing. If you won't help me, then I'll ask the All-Maker to send someone else who can."*

If you ask Fanari about the missing person, she will just brush it off as another of Deor's crazy ideas, but Deor is quite serious. He will tell you that local blacksmith [Baldor Iron-Shaper](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md) went missing in the middle of the night. Deor suspects a kidnapping motivated by Baldor's valuable expertise in [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) smithing. On the night of his disappearance Deor also saw two elves dragging something away to the *"south and west"*, so start your search in that direction.

### Abandoned Lodge
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-npc-Baldor_Iron-Shaper_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Baldor_Iron-Shaper_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Baldor_Iron-Shaper_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Baldor_Iron-Shaper_02.jpg) The abducted [blacksmith](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md) While Deor's directions could lead you more-or-less anywhere on Solstheim, your compass objective marker will point you directly to an [Abandoned Lodge](Skyrim_Abandoned_Lodge.md) southwest of the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md). The lodge is normally empty, but once you arrive, you'll be attacked by four hostile members of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md). Dispose of them and loot their bodies for a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hand-written_Note) from their commander and the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Abandoned_Lodge) to the lodge. Inside, head into the basement where you will find the scared, tied-up smith in a corner, weeping: *"Help! Somebody! Please, someone, help me!"* and *"I have been taken against my will! I am in need of rescue!"*

When you talk to Baldor, he will remember you as the hero who rescued the people of Skaal Village. Despite his wounds, he'll claim he's not hurt. When you ask why the Thalmor abducted him, he'll reply: *"I do not know this word Thalmor, but if you mean the elves, they were trying to learn the secrets of forging Stalhrim. Their leader, an elf named Ancarion, has a map. He says that it shows the location of a hidden source of Stalhrim."* He will then explain what stalhrim is: *"Enchanted ice as hard as iron and cold as death. Stalhrim can be forged into deadly weapons, but the art is known only to smiths of the Skaal."* He will then point you towards a Thalmor ship at the northwestern coast where [Ancarion](Skyrim_Ancarion.md) resides. He asks you to retrieve the [map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Source_Map) as it belongs to the Skaal people. Leave the wounded smith behind and make your way northwest to [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md).

### Northshore Landing
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg) Ancarion's ship at the dock. When you arrive, a Thalmor soldier will warn you to be careful. Make your way to the ship, and talk to Ancarion.

**I'm here about the Stalhrim map.** *"So you know my purpose here, then? I suppose you must have found that dullard of a blacksmith. Unfortunately for you, my mission here is also a secret. To protect it, I have no choice but to silence you. Now, give me one good reason that I should not kill you where you stand."* Three dialogue options will now be available. Successfully completing the third option will require a [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill of at least 75:

If the Thalmor soldiers at the dock engage you in combat, you'll need to kill Ancarion and loot the map from his corpse. Regardless if he gave you the map, if you pickpocketed it from him, or if you killed him and looted it from his corpse, your final step is to return to Skaal Village and give it to Baldor. He will be quite pleased, and will teach you how to smith Stalhrim. This quest is now complete, and the next two will start when you return to Skaal Village.

## Notes
- The [stalhrim source](Skyrim_Stalhrim_Source.md) that the Thalmor are seeking is in the mountains to the south of Northshore Landing, and its marker will be added to your game map if you read the [Stalhrim Source Map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Source_Map), or upon finishing the quest if you don't. There are another two stalhrim deposits just east of the ship, on a small [unmarked island](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Horker_Stalhrim_Island) surrounded by [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md).
- If [Deor Woodcutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter) or [Fanari Strong-Voice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice) are killed prior to completing the quest, you will never be able to learn to forge stalhrim yourself. In addition, you will have to discover the Stalhrim Source's location on your own.
- If you have completed [The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md), all the villagers including Baldor will be "busy" until you complete [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md). Make sure to talk to Baldor before you talk to Storn if you wish to complete this first.
- Although you must finish this quest before you can craft stalhrim, there is another requirement that must be met: your [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill must be at least level 80, and the Ebony Smithing perk must be active.
- After you finish the quest, Ancarion will remain docked at Northshore Landing if you successfully made a deal to sell him stalhrim equipment. You cannot invest with Ancarion, though if the Merchant perk is active, you'll also be able to sell him weapons and armor that aren't made out of stahrim. - With version 2.0.3 of the [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), Ancarion's shop will benefit from the [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md) perk as well.
- After you finish the quest, the Thalmor soldiers you encountered outside the Abandoned Lodge will eventually respawn, though the new ones won't be hostile.

## Bugs
- If you talk to the two Skaal villagers discussing the disappearance of the village blacksmith before they have finished talking, you will be unable to interact with the male villager, Deor Woodcutter, thus making the quest unattainable. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If the quest fails to start, use `Setstage DLC2SV02 10` to kick it off.
- There is a chance that the Thalmor Soldiers at the Abandoned Lodge will not be named, rendering them unlootable, thus making it impossible to obtain the key. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.

- This can be fixed by typing `player.additem xx027e00 1` into the console where "xx" is the load order number of the Dragonborn add-on.
- There is a chance that after you speak with Deor your compass marker will point you to the furthest southwest point on the map, rather than the abandoned lodge. - This can be fixed by waiting 48 hours outside of the abandoned lodge. The compass marker will move to the correct location and Baldor will spawn inside the lodge.
- If you successfully make a deal to sell Ancarion stalhrim equipment, he may eventually run out of gold, leaving him with nothing. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Since Deor and Fanari are only marked as Protected, they can be killed by the player (for example, accidentally during the battle with the Lurker after the Wind Stone is freed). This will prevent the quest from starting. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. It resurrects them if necessary, and marks them as Essential until the quest has started.

- You can force the quest to start by using `Setstage DLC2SV02 10`.<sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>
