# Men-of-ket

Men-of-ket were an ancient a tribe of [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LCL-1)</sup> They lived in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) and early [First](Lore_First_Era.md) eras and were among the men herded as slaves from across the [Niben](Lore_Niben.md) by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup>

## Notes
- [Keptu](Lore_Keptu.md), another group of Nedic people were often referred to as Ket Keptu.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM12:B-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KKBody-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KKFace-5)</sup>
