The Augur was once a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) mage from [Dunlain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dunlain_Falls) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and a well-respected, brilliant student of the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md). He delved into [magic](Lore_Magic.md) in a way no one had seen before, and showed a particular talent for the [School of Restoration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Restoration). However, during an ambitious, unapproved magical experiment in the Midden, he lost his corporeal form and wound up fused to the magical energies that flow through the College.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:172</sup> He thereafter appeared instead as a large sphere of blue-white light, described as an incorporeal "maelstrom of writhing oddness and flame".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Quaranir-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Colette_Marence-3)</sup> He has sequestered himself in the Midden, the catacombs beneath the College, ever since, but keeps tabs on everything going on in the College.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> He possesses a unique wisdom which the College has relied on in times of need.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tolfdir-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), he guided the College through the potentially disastrous crisis they faced following their excavation of [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md) and the discovery of the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRGood-5)</sup>
