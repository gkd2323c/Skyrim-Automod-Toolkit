[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_(Anthology).jpg) Map of the islands Port Telvannis is found on [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/ON-crown_store-Port_Telvannis_Bantam_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Port_Telvannis_Bantam_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Port_Telvannis_Bantam_Guar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Port_Telvannis_Bantam_Guar.jpg) A Port Telvannis Bantam Guar on [Gorne](Lore_Gorne.md) Port Telvannis (also spelled **Port Telvanis**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup> or **Port Telvanni**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-XG-2)</sup> is a city located on an [archipelago](Lore_Northeast_Archipelago.md) in the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md), northeast of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It is described as an island palace under the control of [Great House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and was their capital during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Concept Map-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The Parliament of Bugs, the High Telvanni wizards who preside over the entire house, are headquartered here.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup>

The Port Telvannis Bantam Guar is a variety of green [bantam guar](Lore_Bantam_Guar.md) found in the area. Their mottled coloring helps them blend in with the foliage of Port Telvannis.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PTBG-6)</sup>

One of the more prominent citizens coming from Port Telvannis in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) was Xal, a human [Marukhati](Lore_Marukhati_Selective.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup> who issued correspondence from the city.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-XG-2)</sup>
