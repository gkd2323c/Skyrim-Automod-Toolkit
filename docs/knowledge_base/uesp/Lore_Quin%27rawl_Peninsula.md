[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/ON-map-Southern_Elsweyr.jpg/200px-ON-map-Southern_Elsweyr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Southern_Elsweyr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Southern_Elsweyr.jpg) The Quin'rawl Peninsula in southern Elsweyr The Quin'rawl Peninsula (sometimes simply called **Quin'rawl**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ADi M-1)</sup> is a region found in the southeast of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) between the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) and the [Topal Sea](Lore_Topal_Sea.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Fot N-3)</sup> It is part of the greater kingdom of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md), specifically where the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md) slowly opens to a region of perilous highlands across the north, the sandy beaches in the southeast, and the marshes of the southwest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMap-4)</sup> Quin'rawl is heavily centered around [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup> a major city in Elsweyr that also serves as Pellitine's capital since the upheavals of the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) in [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ho SAO-5)</sup> Several islands are found off the region's coast, such as [Tideholm](Lore_Tideholm.md) and [Topal Island](Lore_Topal_Island.md) to the east, and both [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) and [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md) to the south.

## Notable Locations

[Black Heights](Lore_Black_Heights.md)
- A sleepy mountain-village in the northwest, known for its artistic roots and ancient murals. It is primarily populated by [Dagi-raht](Lore_Dagi-raht.md).
Forsaken Citadel
- Formerly the Topal Legionary Academy, it is an abandoned Imperial stronghold in the north end precipices.
[Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md)
- An ancient ruin whose origins are shrouded in mystery. It has appeared long before history has been recorded and leaving no indication of its conception.
[Moonlit Cove](Lore_Moonlit_Cove.md)
- A former religious site-turned smuggling den was known for its sacred waters and diverse flora and fungi.
Potentate's Retreat
- The estate of Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md)
[Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md)
- A major, albeit infamous harbor-city found on the eastern coastal region, known for its expansive and open-aired markets and bazaars.
