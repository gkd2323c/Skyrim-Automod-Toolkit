# Diadem of Diagna

The Diadem of Diagna is a symbol of office for the [High King](Lore_Hammerfell.md#Rulers) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), named for [Diagna](Lore_Diagna.md). Each monarch of the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md) is adorned with the Diadem when graced with the title of the High King, such as [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md) did during his coronation upon the Throne of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAHD-1)</sup>
