Dwarven Ruin Explorer's Journal *A fragment of a journal recounting an ill fated journey into a Dwemer ruin*
---
… with some success. Beem-Tei discovered the hidden entrance to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) underworks; may her spirit find repose in the waters of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). The [automatons](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) guard their masters' secrets well.

The [spider](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md) and [sphere](Lore_Dwarven_Sphere.md) guardians swarmed over us, killing Beem-Tei and Reekatul before we fought them off. The outer chamber was ours, for a time. The central heating plant and other machinery were fascinating but Ukka-Malz insisted we press farther in, searching for relics we could remove.

The inner chamber was our undoing. Spheres and spiders were the least of our worries. Our intrusion awakened a [Dwemer Centurion](Lore_Dwarven_Centurion.md). From the inscriptions on the walls, we were able to learn its name just before we were overwhelmed: Thzallek Eft. Ukka-Malz and the others died under its relentless assault. Only I lived to reach the exit. Thzallek Eft. The name will haunt my sleep.

But I cannot get out. The doors are locked by an unseen mechanism. Our food and water are back in the outer chamber. I'll venture there only if desperate. Perhaps other researchers will open the doors before I reach that point.
