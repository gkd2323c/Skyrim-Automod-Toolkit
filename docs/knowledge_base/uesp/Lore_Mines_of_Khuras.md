[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-place-Mines_of_Khuras.jpg/200px-ON-place-Mines_of_Khuras.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mines_of_Khuras.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mines_of_Khuras.jpg) The Mines of Khuras circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Mines of Khuras (or simply **Khuras**) are an extensive abandoned mine complex located in the [Cambray Hills](Lore_Cambray_Hills.md) region of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The entrance lies on the coast east of [Aldcroft](Lore_Aldcroft.md), and the lower levels are flooded with lava.

## History
In the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the mines were famous as a source of crystals and gems until a ceiling collapse rendered most of the tunnels inaccessible in [1E 2906](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2906).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoading-1)</sup> The mines were subsequently abandoned. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the mines were taken over by the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md), who sought a certain type of gem for use in corrupting the [Wyrd Tree](Lore_Wyrd_Tree.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BO-2)</sup> Their leader, Lilou, was later killed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMines_of_Khuras-3)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Oracle Marieve of the [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md) was targeted by the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) so that the cult could use her to discern the location of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). The oracle fled to an old Wyrd site within the Mines of Khuras, but was tracked down<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> by [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and captured. She managed to leave a cryptic note for [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) with instructions on how to perform a ritual to contact her. The ritual was completed by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and the oracle was ultimately rescued from the clutches of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) before she was forced to reveal Artaeum's whereabouts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), a junior member of the [Brotherhood of Seth](Lore_Brotherhood_of_Seth.md) from [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) named Brother Barnabas seemingly went mad after learning of [Emperor Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md)'s imprisonment. Taking it upon himself to restore the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) and rescue the emperor, Barnabas took a map fragment that showed the location of the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md) and fled to Khuras. He was then killed by the monsters inhabiting the mines. When the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md) came in search of the staff piece hidden in the Crypt of Hearts, the Brotherhood revealed the fate of the map. The Champion then entered the mines and retrieved it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARCrypt-6)</sup>
