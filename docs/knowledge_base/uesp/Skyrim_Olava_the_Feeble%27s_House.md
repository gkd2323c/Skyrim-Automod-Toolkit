It is located right behind [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md).

It is the residence of elderly seer [Olava the Feeble](Skyrim_Olava_the_Feeble.md) and consists of one area only, *[Olava the Feeble's House](#Olava_the_Feeble.27s_House)*.

## Olava the Feeble's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Olava_the_Feeble%27s_House.jpg) Olava's House The house is among the smallest houses in Whiterun and contains very little of value. The front door opens to a huge open fire with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) and some hanging [Dried Elves Ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md). The table on the left holds a [petty soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) while the single shelf on the wall holds two [soul gem fragments](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment) and the end table contains a few pieces of gold.

To the right of the front door is Olava's owned bed and a cupboard with clutter. On the shelves above sits some food and, a coin purse, a book *[The Holds of Skyrim](Lore_The_Holds_of_Skyrim.md)*, and a sack with food or [Salt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile). The small alcove to the west has a chest with some gold and pelts, an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) and a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese). On the adjacent shelves, above the two food barrels, sits a sample of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton), a [potion of enhanced stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina), a [potion of brief invisibility](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Brief_Invisibility) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine).
