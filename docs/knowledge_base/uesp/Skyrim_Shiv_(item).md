[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-item-Shiv.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shiv.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shiv.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shiv.jpg) A Shiv A Shiv is an improvised dagger-like weapon, commonly made and used by the prisoners of [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md). These sharpened utensils are the only handily available weapon in the prison, excluding fists and magic. Due to its improvised nature, a shiv cannot be [improved](Skyrim_Grindstone.md).

## Notes
- [Grisvar the Unlucky](Skyrim_Grisvar_the_Unlucky.md) and [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) can both give you shivs if you ask them. Click on their respective pages for details.
- Version 1.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) adds the ability to temper the weapon using an [Iron Ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) but without the benefit of any smithing perks.
