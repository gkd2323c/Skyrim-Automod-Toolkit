[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/LO-map-Morrowind_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Morrowind_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Anthology).jpg) Map of Morrowind Noormoc is a location on mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), known as the home of the "Red Wives of [Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)", who paid well for the mechanical wonders of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) —offering performative "[belly-magic](Lore_Sex.md)" as their form of [currency](Lore_Currency.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV5-1)</sup>

## Notes
- "Noormoc" shares a phonetic similarity to "[Gnaar Mok](Lore_Gnaar_Mok.md)", a location on the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). It is unknown if there is any connection between the two locations, in the narrative of the Sermons, Noormoc was in existence prior to the alleged splitting of Vvardenfell from the mainland.
