Yngol and the Sea-Ghosts *A tale of Ysgramor's first adventure in Tamriel* *
---
[![M](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/M_letter.png/73px-M_letter.png)
asser](Lore_Masser.md) and [Secunda](Lore_Secunda.md) passed over [Ysgramor](Skyrim_Ysgramor.md)'s people as their fellowship landed in long-boats upon the rocky shores of [Hsaarik Head](Lore_Hsaarik_Head.md) on their journey from [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md) to [Mereth](Lore_Skyrim.md). Boats littered the coast, but Ysgramor did not count his kin, [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md)'s among them.

Ysgramor commanded the sea-ghosts to surrender his kin, and a great gale darkened the sky. The seas thrashed and churned, and a wrathful storm appeared. Ysgramor took up the oars and rowed into the storm alone.

Upon the [Sea](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), Ysgramor wrestled the sea-ghosts, and the storm carried him along the jagged coast. Two fortnights passed without relief, until finally the storm broke. Come the next dawn, Yngol's long-boat was found in the icy surf, but the vengeful sea-ghosts had already taken Yngol and his clansmen.

In his terrible grief, Ysgramor slew a dozen dozen beasts and burned them in honor of his fallen kinsman. A [barrow-hill](Skyrim_Yngol_Barrow.md) was dug in the Atmoran tradition, and Yngol was laid to rest with rites and honors among his clansmen far below the rocky face of Hsaarik Head, the first [Children of the Sky](Skyrim_Nord.md) to perish in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).
