Jonas Coventine served as the Primate of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) and the leader of the [Akatosh Chantry](Lore_Akatosh_Chantry.md) in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Circa [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542), during his annual visit to the Temple of the Divines, Jonas met a young penitent named [Artorius Ancrus](Lore_Artorius_Ponticus.md) and immediately took a liking to him. After leading the temple in worship, the Primate invited Artorius to a private meeting. The details of the meeting are unknown, but shortly after, Artorius pledged himself to the Divines.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TP2-1)</sup>

After Artorius, who had become a priest of Akatosh, performed a reputed miracle known as "the Miracle of the First Shrine", word spread throughout the Empire, quickly reaching Jonas. Jonas decided that if Akatosh was truly performing miracles through one of his priests, then that priest needed to be at the Cathedral of Akatosh in Kvatch. Soon, Artorius was serving under Jonas in the Cathedral of Akatosh.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP3-2)</sup> Jonas became Artorius' mentor, and he was never far from the Primate's side.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLV-3)</sup>

Artorius rose through the hierarchy of the Cathedral, eventually becoming Grand Sermonizer of Akatosh, second only to the Primate himself. However, at some point after the outbreak of the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) in [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560), the relationship between the Primate and Artorius became strained. Artorius wanted the clergy to become more involved in the growing political unrest spreading throughout the Empire and with the increasing threat of the Knahaten Flu, but Jonas wanted the Cathedral to remain distant from secular issues. The two argued constantly about the subject, reaching the point where Jonas was prepared to put an end to the disagreement through any means at his disposal—including sending Artorius to a distant posting.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP3-2)</sup>

Before Jonas could carry out his threat, Artorius performed the [Black Sacrament](Lore_Black_Sacrament.md) to contract the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) to kill him so that he could become the Primate of Akatosh.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONPA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONST-5)</sup> The Dark Brotherhood accepted and sent an assassin to Kvatch to carry out the contract. The Brotherhood assassin [Lyra Viria](Lore_Lyra_Viria.md) killed Jonas in the Cathedral of Akatosh.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLV-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONGFTP-6)</sup>

Although it was claimed that Jonas died of natural causes, rumors spread throughout Kvatch that the Primate was assassinated by the Dark Brotherhood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP3-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONGVT-7)</sup> Regardless of the rumors, Jonas' death paved the way for Artorius to advance to the top of the Akatosh Chantry's hierarchy, later being elected the Primate of Kvatch.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP3-2)</sup>
