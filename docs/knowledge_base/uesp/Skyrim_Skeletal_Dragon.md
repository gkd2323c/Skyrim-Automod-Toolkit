[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-creature-Skeletal_Dragon_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Skeletal_Dragon_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeletal_Dragon_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeletal_Dragon_02.jpg) Skeletal Dragon The **Skeletal Dragon** is a unique undead [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) found within [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) during the quest [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md). When killed, it provides a large number of [dragon bones](Skyrim_Dragon_Bone.md) but no [dragon scales](Skyrim_Dragon_Scales.md), and it will not provide [dragon heartscales](Skyrim_Dragon_Heartscales.md) when struck with [Kahvozein's Fang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kahvozein%27s_Fang). It does not have a dragon soul.

The Skeletal Dragon is an undead creature, and thus is vulnerable to [sun damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Damage). It also has weakness to [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage). Unlike other dragons, the skeletal variety doesn't possess blanket immunity to [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spells, however the *[Master of the Mind](Skyrim_Master_of_the_Mind.md)* perk is required to make Illusion magic effective against it.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- The Skeletal Dragon cannot be mounted with the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) shout.<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- If you disengage from combat with the Skeletal Dragon and are no longer detected, its health will instantly regenerate. This is because its health regenerates more than 20x faster than a regular dragon outside of combat.
- A Skeletal Dragon also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Skeletal_Dragon) in the [Heroes of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Heroes_of_Skyrim) card expansion for *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*. [Bone Dragons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bone_Dragon) appear in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), while [Undead Dragons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Undead_Dragon) appear in *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.
- The Skeletal Dragon in Labyrinthian is one of the only three instances the player can interact with and fight a dragon prior to completing [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) with the others being [Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md) and [Vulthuryol](Skyrim_Vulthuryol.md)
