[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-creature-Steadfast_Dwarven_Sphere.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Steadfast_Dwarven_Sphere.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Steadfast_Dwarven_Sphere.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Steadfast_Dwarven_Sphere.jpg) Steadfast Dwarven Sphere The **Steadfast Dwarven Sphere** is a [Dwarven Automaton](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) that can be found in [Kagrumez](Skyrim_Kagrumez.md). After completing the second stage of the resonance gem puzzle there, the door within the room containing the [Steadfast Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Steadfast_Dwarven_Spider.md) will unlock and you can proceed to the next chamber. After hitting the lever, the wall lowers, and you will find the Steadfast Dwarven Sphere. Like the spider, it won't attack you. Talk to it and you can make it your follower. Since it is a creature rather than an NPC, it cannot carry items for you as a follower, though it follows basic commands (wait here, follow me, and return home).

## Notes
- It might be preferable to enter Kagrumez at a later level (24+), as the Steadfast Dwarven Sphere follows the standard leveling of regular, hostile Dwarven Spheres where the base version (found at player character levels of 16 and below) has no ranged attack, even though it has a mounted crossbow. The Dwarven Spheres (and thus the Steadfast, "follower" version of the creature) of levels 24+ *have* a ranged attack, making it far better for combat purposes. At its highest level, its health can out-range most human followers. - At its highest level, the crossbow bolts will deal fire damage, identical to those of enemy Spheres.
- When not actively fighting - or you do not have weapons/spells unsheathed - it follows you around as a closed-up sphere. If you do have weapons out or are engaging hostiles, it will follow in its unfolded form. It also has a movement speed that is faster than most NPC followers.

## Bugs
- If the Steadfast Dwarven Sphere is hit with [magelight](Skyrim_Magelight.md), the game might freeze when magelight is supposed to end because it is "stuck" to the Dwarven Sphere.
