## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There are steps leading up to a porch at the front of the house. To the right of the door are two barrels containing [food](Skyrim_Food.md). There are several piles of firewood on the left side of the porch, and a wooden bench on the right. The house faces the bay and is protected by a novice lock.

### Beitild's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-interior-Beitild%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Beitild%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Beitild%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Beitild%27s_House.jpg) Beitild's house The house is a simple single-room dwelling where [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md) sleeps at night. She carries a copy of the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Beitild%27s_House) to the house. It contains typical furnishings. There is a square table with a chair in the western corner of the house with two medium [coin purses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md) placed on top. Coming out of the middle of the northwest wall is a large double bed, with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the end containing leveled items. To the right of the bed is a round wooden table holding a basket containing three [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg).

In the middle of the northeast wall is a fireplace and [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md), a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantlepiece, and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes) in front of the hearth. To the left of the fireplace, against the northeast wall, is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). To the right is a long wooden table and bench. On the table is a bottle of alto wine, a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), and an [Eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). Under the table is a [potion of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor).

Opposite the table, against the southwest wall, is a set of shelves holding two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, three potatoes, four bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine, and several pieces of cheese on top. Above the shelves are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the left is a basket on the floor containing two leeks, and to the right is a food barrel.

## Notes
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* describes the building as follows:

*"Beitild Iron-Breaker manages Iron-Breaker Mine. She's in constant competition with Leigelf Quicksilver, the owner of Quicksilver Mine and her former husband. Beitild lives in the house she used to share with her husband. She refuses to do much else but tend to her mine, eat, and sleep."* - Other than some barrels and sacks, all containers in the area are non-respawning and safe to store items in if you wish.
