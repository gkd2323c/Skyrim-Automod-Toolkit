# Reefborn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-crown_store-Reefborn_Wolf.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Reefborn_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Reefborn_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Reefborn_Wolf.jpg) A Reefborn Wolf Reefborn creatures,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bear-1)</sup> also referred to as **Coraldrift**<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> (or **Coral Drift**)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Coral_Drift-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Coral_Drift2-4)</sup> creatures are [artificial](Lore_Golem.md) [coral](Lore_Coral.md) beasts that imitate the appearance of [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) land animals. They can be found on the isles of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md). Among the known reefborn creatures are those that resemble [bears](Lore_Bear.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bear-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> [senche](Lore_Senche.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Senche-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SEN-7)</sup> [haj mota](Lore_Haj_Mota.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Coral_Drift-3)</sup> [horses](Lore_Horse.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Horse-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SC-9)</sup> [guars](Lore_Guar.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Guar-10)</sup> [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Wolf-11)</sup> and possibly even [dwarven spiders](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Spider-12)</sup>

The [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) scholars speculated that the creation of Reefborn creatures involves the use of magic. Some animal specialists have theorized that [druids](Lore_Druids.md) might be responsible for their creation.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Senche-6)</sup> The druids of the [Eldertide Circle](Lore_Eldertide_Circle.md) were indeed capable of crafting these creatures.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOCC-13)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> Practices of Eldertide Druids of [Coral Cliffs](Lore_Coral_Cliffs.md) involved live sacrifices, including feeding living people to coral,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-C-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SB-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-FMV-16)</sup> as a punishment for harming nature.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

Although the reefborn creatures share some characteristics with normal animals, their diet is different. Equine reefborn feed on algal blooms.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Horse-8)</sup> While guars are a rare sight in the region of the Abecean Sea, there are Reefborn creatures resembling guars that inhabit its shallow waters.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Guar-10)</sup> During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), reefborn wolves were considered prestigious in this area, especially among the Druids.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Wolf-11)</sup> Coral creatures resembling [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) spiders have been observed, but it is unclear whether they are mimicking the Dwarven spiders or if the coral has simply grown over discarded [animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Spider-12)</sup>

Additionally, the reefborn creatures can be found wild on [Coral Aerie](Lore_Coral_Aerie.md), an island located off the northern coast of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md), in the Dreadsail Reef, commanded by the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md), and on the shores of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Coral_Drift-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Coral_Drift2-4)</sup>
