[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) The Sapphire Paragon The Sapphire Paragon is located in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). To find it, go to the area where the [Amethyst Paragon](Skyrim_Amethyst_Paragon.md) was found, northwest of the Shrine of Learning, and there is a path up the mountain. At the top, turn right towards the waterfall and you should see both the [frost giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md) with the Sapphire Paragon and the gem platform directly across the river from him. If placed into the gem holder of the [Paragon Platform](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform), it will create an opening to the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md), with an abundance of leveled gems, several pieces of leveled elven gear, and three chests of leveled loot.
