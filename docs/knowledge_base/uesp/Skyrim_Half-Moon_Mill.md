[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg/200px-SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg) Half-Moon Mill **Half-Moon Mill** is a lumber mill that supplies [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). It is operated by [Hert](Skyrim_Hert.md) and [Hern](Skyrim_Hern.md), who live in a small cabin across from the mill itself. It is located on the shore of [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md), west of [The Lady Stone](Skyrim_The_Lady_Stone.md), east of [Hunter's Rest](Skyrim_Hunter%27s_Rest.md), beside the road that leads north from Falkreath towards [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Half-Moon_Mill_02.jpg) The cabin and chicken coop The property is adjacent to the road, beside a stone bridge that crosses a stream that is filled with tree trunks and branches as it flows from [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md). The stream splits the property and is rich in [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md). The [wood mill](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md) and a butcher shack are on the east side of the stream, with a wooden bridge leading to the main cabin to the west. The butcher shack contains a table holding three haunches of raw [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison) and two joints of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef). Hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). Under the table are gory human remains, a possible clue as to the [true nature](Skyrim_Vampire.md) of the mill's inhabitants. A [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull) is found on the ground as well.

The cabin is a single-story building with a small henhouse containing two chicken nests between the road and the cabin. In front of the cabin, near the stream, is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and opposite the henhouse are a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). There are several tree stumps around the property, harvested by the mill. At the rear of the property is a large [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) stuck in the ground beside it, and not far to the north is another iron ore vein. Near this is a tree stump containing a dead rabbit, a fork, and a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). Numerous plants and flowers may be found around the property. If the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) is installed, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) can be found near the shore, along with a salmon rack and two [baskets](Skyrim_Basket.md). Fish from [Temperate Lakes](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Biomes) can be caught here.

When speaking with [Hert](Skyrim_Hert.md), she will instruct you to grab an [axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), as she will pay you for [chopping wood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md).

### Half-Moon Mill
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Half-Moon_Mill.jpg) The interior of the cabin The cabin itself is a single-room dwelling. Upon entry there is a chair and barrels on the left, against the southeast wall. Against the southwest wall is a cupboard holding only clutter, with more [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels next to it, mostly containing raw meats. A long wooden table and bench along the northwest wall holds a haunch of raw venison, a joint of raw beef, a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), and a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), with two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) under the table. Hanging above the table are three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the right is a hanging rack holding three rabbits and three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). To the right of the table is a kettle containing three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) and another food barrel. In the middle of the northwest wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a kettle on the hearth containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). In front of the fireplace is a round wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a bottle of wine, a bottle of alto wine, and two [pheasant breasts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast). To the right of the fireplace, against the northwest wall, is a large owned double bed with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of it. Under the bed on the right-hand side is a copy of *[Physicalities of Werewolves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves)*. In the northern corner is an end table holding a bottle of Nord mead. Against the southeast wall is a wardrobe and dresser, both containing common [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a food barrel between them.

## Notes
- You can buy [sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> here after installing [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) by speaking to Hert. You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so.
- When [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) a home with the Hearthfire add-on, your map marker may send you here to buy sawn logs. If Hert is dead, you may simply go to [another wood mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Mills) to purchase lumber to clear the objective.
- To the northwest is a memorial pillar with a flag and the corpse of a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf_(dead).md) lying beside it.
- In the northwest corner of Lake Ilinalta, north from Half-Moon Mill, is an unmarked fisherman's camp with a copy of the [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Fire and Darkness](Skyrim_Fire_and_Darkness.md)*.
- Although the owners here are vampires themselves, they will still become hostile towards you if you are a [blood-starved vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md#Stages_of_Vampirism).
