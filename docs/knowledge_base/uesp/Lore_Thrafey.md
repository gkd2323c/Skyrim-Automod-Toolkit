# Thrafey

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/ON-costume-Thrafey_Debutante_Gown_%28female%29.jpg/200px-ON-costume-Thrafey_Debutante_Gown_%28female%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-costume-Thrafey_Debutante_Gown_(female).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-costume-Thrafey_Debutante_Gown_(female).jpg) A Thrafey debutante gown Thrafey is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with the ability to restore damaged tissue. The bloodline dominates an area of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) - specifically the regions of [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md), [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md), [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) and [Urvaius](Lore_Urvaius.md). They are the enemies of the [Anthotis](Lore_Anthotis.md) bloodline, but have made a rare alliance with the [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Archblood Delyna of the Thrafey was known for promoting the value of light and fashion among vampires, particularly for the purpose of attending aristocratic debutantes in search of easy prey.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Thrafey were known for accession gowns that were embedded with scales, typically worn upon joining the ranks of the bloodline. Fragments of these garments were discovered within the Silent Halls, a ruined [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) xanmeer found deep in the bogs of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDG-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCS-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The Thrafey were once intended to appear as enemies in *[ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)* early on, but were ultimately cut. They had ranks such as Bonecaller, Dreadmage, Lurker, Shadow Walker, and Vigilant.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
