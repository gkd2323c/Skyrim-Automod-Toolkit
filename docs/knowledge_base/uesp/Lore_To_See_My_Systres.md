To See My Systres *A song about the Systres* *
---
1.
I hugged the coast on the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md)
And jumped at every whisper
May [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s [fair kin](Lore_Nord.md) get kissed 't the end,
But I'd rather see my [Systres](Lore_Systres.md)

CHORUS:
Way-hey, over and down,
As the sunlight glisters
Sailing 'cross the bright blue sea,
Sailing for my Systres

2.
So guide all hands through [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) lands
And sing [Saxhleel](Lore_Argonian.md) tongue twisters
Go drink and sin in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)
I'd rather see my Systres

3.
We'll sail on in to [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md)
And meet fine [maids and misters](Lore_Imperial.md)
Let my anchor weigh from [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md)
And we'll sail to see my Systres

4.
[Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) lands have [warmer sands](Lore_Elsweyr.md)
More kind to tails and whiskers
But feline friends purr till the end
For the love of my sweet Systres

5.
My boat was paid by [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) maid
Who hoped I might assist her
I'd sail all night if the price was right
And it means I'd see my Systres

6.
Bedded a bard in [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md)
Who knew I can't resist her,
If the sun should set on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md)
It'll rise upon my Systres

7.
No tale more sad than a land-locked lad
Who walks the world in blisters
But he'll sail with cheer from [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md)
And meet my blessed Systres

CHORUS:
Way-hey, over and down,
As the sunlight glisters
Sailing 'cross the bright blue sea,
Sailing for my Systres

8.
Met a clan of [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) at Stonetooth Fort
With tusks and scraggly whiskers
We'll war no more on far-off shores
We'll truce for my dear Systres

CHORUS:
Way-hey, over and down,
As the sunlight glisters
Sailing 'cross the bright blue sea,
Sailing for my Systres

9.
Life's as long as a sailor's song,
Death's nothing I'd consider,
And I'll ne'er abate the tides of fate
May I drift back to my Systres
May we drift back to our Systres
