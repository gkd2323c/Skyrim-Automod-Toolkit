[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-npc-Little_Pelagius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Little_Pelagius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Little_Pelagius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Little_Pelagius.jpg) Little Pelagius **Little Pelagius** is [Pelagius Septim III](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Mad.md) as a [child](Skyrim_Child.md). He is seen during the Night Terrors part of the quest [The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md), when you use the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) on a bandit.

He wears red colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8).
