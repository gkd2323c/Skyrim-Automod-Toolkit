[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/SR-item-Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.jpg) Gray Cowl of Nocturnal The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) artifact [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md) which once belonged to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) before being stolen. It is acquired through the quest [The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal).

While equipped, the living will be colored depending on their intent, with non-hostiles being shaded blue and hostiles emitting a red aura. You will be attacked by [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) while wearing the cowl, whereas followers are free to wear it without facing their wrath.

## Notes
- The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal has previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal.md).
- Unlike its previous appearance in *Oblivion*, the blue inscription down the middle reads "Shadow hide you" without omitting the letter Yahkem (Y), as is common in the [Daedric Alphabet](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md).
- Similarly, it is also now considered a piece of Light Armor, with an armor rating equivalent to an [elven helmet](Skyrim_Elven_Helmet.md), though it cannot be tempered.
- This is the only item in the game with a [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md) enchantment, making it especially useful since it's a constant effect as opposed to a spell. Detect Life was generally available as an enchantment in *Oblivion*, where this item originated.
