Pirate's Note *A mysterious note from a pirate*
---
They say a pirate's home is the sea, but the bottom of the ocean is no place to breathe, let alone live.

But word is you found a [right ol' ship](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place).md) on [an isle far off the coast](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md). If you can't sail it out to open waters, it may make a good home.

Just a little advice from a [restless](Lore_Cyrus.md) soul.
