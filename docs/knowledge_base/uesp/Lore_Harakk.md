# Harakk

**Harakk** is one of the two favored [stars](Lore_Stars.md) in the [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) sky. The ship of [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md), of the original [Companions](Lore_Companions.md), was named after it, while the ship of his brother [Ylgar](Lore_Ylgar.md) was named after [Darumzu](Lore_Darumzu.md), another of the stars favored by the Atmorans.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The crew of the *Harakk* perished in the Storm of Separation during the crossing of the Atmoran Strait.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOR5-3)</sup>
