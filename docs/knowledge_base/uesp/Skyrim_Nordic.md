# Nordic

**Nordic** armor and weapons can be crafted with the [Advanced Armors](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors.md) perk. All items, except arrows, can be tempered with a [quicksilver ingot](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Ingot.md).

The [Nordic Jewelry](Skyrim_Nordic_Jewelry.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds jewelry items in the same style, which can be crafted after completing the [related quest](Skyrim_Nordic_Jewelry_(quest).md). See **[this page](Skyrim_Nordic_Jewelry_Items.md)** for the full list.

## Nordic Carved Armor
Nordic carved armor is medium-quality heavy armor. The cuirass is comparable to [ebony armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Armor.md) in strength but the boots, gauntlets and helmet are only of [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish_Armor.md) quality. The Nordic Shield is comparatively weak, being only slightly stronger than its [Dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven.md) counterpart. A full set of Nordic carved armor (shield excluded) will offer slightly better protection than Orcish armor but weigh more, whereas including the shield makes it weaker than a full set of Orcish armor, but slightly lighter.

## Notes
- Although added by Dragonborn, Nordic equipment is part of generic leveled lists, meaning it can be encountered outside of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). However, you can only find it in enemies' inventories (specifically, high-leveled [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md)) and the weapons and armor are not for sale at mainland merchants. Merchants at [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) and the blacksmith at the [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) commonly sell Nordic armor and weapons, including pre-enchanted and unenchanted versions.

The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- Nordic Arrows were never added to the bandit leveled lists with the Dragonborn add-on even though Nordic Bows were. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
