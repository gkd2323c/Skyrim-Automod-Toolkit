[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall.jpg) Heljarchen Hall **Heljarchen Hall** is a piece of property in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md), north of [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md) and south of [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md). It is one of three potential homes available with the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heljarchen_Hall_02.jpg) Heljarchen Hall as seen from the west After installing Hearthfire, you may purchase this plot of land at [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). If [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md) is still the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), speak with him; if Skald won't sell you the land, speak to [Bulfrek](Skyrim_Bulfrek.md); if the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) has control of the hold, speak with the new jarl, [Brina Merilis](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md). You will have to complete the quests [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) and [Kill the Giant](Skyrim_Kill_the_Giant_(Jarl).md) before being allowed to purchase land if Skald is jarl; the latter quest is not made available until level 22. Brina, however, will allow you to purchase land out of gratitude for your help in installing her. When you ask if you can buy a house in the hold, you will be told that there are no houses for sale at that time, but a new plot of land is available. It will cost 5,000 gold.

Your new property, Heljarchen Hall, will be marked as an undiscovered location on your map, located on the southern border of The Pale, where the northern mountains give way to the great plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). The property is southeast of the [Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md); a short distance to the east is the giant camp [Blizzard Rest](Skyrim_Blizzard_Rest.md), and you can see [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) rising behind Loreius Farm to the south. It is also located almost directly over the central ruin in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md).

When you arrive on your new plot of land, you will find a drafting table, carpenter's bench, [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and chest on the outcropping on the south edge of the property; a [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry) is a few feet beyond the chest to the south, in the outcropping itself. The "[Log Pile](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)" is on the western border, and there are two [clay deposits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit); one is south of the log pile, next to the wood chopping block, and the other is just a few feet south of the stone quarry.

You can begin [building](Skyrim_Construction.md) your home immediately, with materials provided in the chest when you first arrive. A unique feature at Heljarchen Hall is you have the option to build a [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md). Once built, the grain mill will appear along the house's western wall. It can be used to convert [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) into a [sack of flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md). The hall's entrance will face Dragonsreach once construction begins, and standing on its steps will offer an impressive view of the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md).

Like the other player-built homes, you will have the option to expand onto your home with a [Main Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall), cellar, and three wing additions. See [this page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Hearthfire-Furnishing_Options) for a list of pages detailing the furnishing options in each part of your home.

Should you become a [Thane of the Pale](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Pale.md), [Gregor](Skyrim_Gregor.md) will become your [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). He will move to Heljarchen Hall upon construction of the [small house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout) layout.

## Notes
- Heljarchen Hall may be named after [Helarchen Creek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Helarchen_Creek), a settlement located roughly in the area, which appeared in [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena). - A village named Heljarchen, located in The Pale, was originally planned to be in the game but only [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md) made it into in the finished game.
- [Adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) children sometimes remark *"Look at all the snow! I want to go sledding! Or skating!"* (girls) or "*It's so c-c-cold here! If I play outside for too long, I'm going to freeze solid!"* (boys) - For the reactions given by your spouse, see [this page](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md#Spouse_Dialogue).
