# Saturalia

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-item-Saturalia_Clothing.jpg/200px-SR-item-Saturalia_Clothing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Saturalia_Clothing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Saturalia_Clothing.jpg) Traditional Saturalia clothing Saturalia is a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) celebration that heralds the [New Life Festival](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md), and is held on the 25th of Evening Star.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BLST-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NLFI-2)</sup> Originally a holiday for a "long forgotten god of debauchery", it has become a time of gift giving, parties, and parading. Visitors are encouraged to participate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SDF-3)</sup> [Reindeer](Lore_Reindeer.md) are traditionally associated with the holiday.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

A tale covers a miraculous rescue by [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md). During a winter storm, a lost and abandoned mercenary prayed for rescue, and Sanguine answered it by [creating](Lore_Golem.md) a frozen steed for her, which allowed her to take vengeance against the lord that betrayed her on Saturalia morning.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SIS-5)</sup> Saturalia Bitter Mulled Wine is an alcoholic drink enjoyed during this day.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Saturalia and New Life Overlap
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/CT-Events-Winter_Festival_%28Large%29.png/200px-CT-Events-Winter_Festival_%28Large%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Winter_Festival_(Large).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Winter_Festival_(Large).png) A castle decorated for the coming Saturalia and New Life Festival (Castles) Saturalia shares some elements with the cross-continental New Life Festival, with it managing to become connected (or at some points synonymous with) the festival. This is thanks to the efforts of merchants from the trade hub that is [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) capitalizing on the holiday through consumer goods. Widespread adoption of Saturalia-themed trees, decorations, and more across Tamriel for usage in the New Life Festival make for a booming market during this time of year, touching even Tamrielans that grew up with their own New Life traditions.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LATH-7)</sup> This convergence of these Breton-influenced aesthetics and practices were started during the time of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LATH-7)</sup> and has carried onto the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SASL-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

A shared tradition involves converting evergreen trees via makeover into "saturalia trees" with elaborate decorations, which are topped with a shining light ornament on its peak. Evergreen trees are chosen because they are associated with resilience and rebirth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BLST-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SASL-8)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> However, for the Bretons, decorating with saturalia trees is a longstanding tradition.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Gift giving is another thing shared among these holidays, where "giving gifts" are placed under saturalia trees.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-CWCFP-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> Among the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), the Saturalia celebration and sharing of gifts is known as the "Saturalia [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md) gift swap".<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TDMJ-14)</sup> The people of [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md) celebrate the tradition of "Secret Sanguine" during New Life, where the identity of a gift giver is a mystery from the intended recipient. The gifts are promiscuous in nature, and are intended to dare the recipient into naughty acts.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NLFI-2)</sup> In both holidays, shades of cerulean are used as the color of clothing for these holidays.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

Despite many Saturalian traditions being incorporated into New Life, the latter retains its unique customs, which each participating Tamrielic race practices in their own way—even among the Bretons, who celebrate both Saturalia and the New Life Festival.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONBreda-17)</sup>

## Notes
- The name of the holiday is nearly identical to the ancient Roman [Saturnalia](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saturnalia), which ran from December 17–23 on the Julian calendar and involved feasting, partying and gift-giving. There was also a loosening of certain social mores, though it fell short of outright debauchery.
