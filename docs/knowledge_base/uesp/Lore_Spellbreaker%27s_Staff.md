# Spellbreaker's Staff

Spellbreaker's Staff was an ancient [staff](Lore_Staff.md) associated with the element of ice, [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) and wielded by the Arch-Mage [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SS-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/ON-npc-Arch-Mage_Shalidor.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Arch-Mage_Shalidor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Arch-Mage_Shalidor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Arch-Mage_Shalidor.jpg) Arch-Mage Shalidor
### First Era
Spellbreaker's Staff was enchanted by Shalidor following the [Battle of Rourken-Shalidor](Lore_Battle_of_Rourken-Shalidor.md) in the First Era. Little is known about how Shalidor used it afterward.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SS-1)</sup> Although the legendary shield [Spellbreaker](Lore_Spellbreaker.md) was wielded and subsequently lost by Shalidor's foes of the [Rourken Clan](Lore_Rourken.md) in that same battle,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARTIL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TL-3)</sup> it did not appear to be connected to the staff.

### Second Era
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the events surrounding Eyevea's return to Tamriel, the staff seemingly was in the possession of [Valaste](Lore_Valaste.md), Master of Incunabula of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). It is unclear whether she was trapped in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) or freed from the curse of madness by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and the spirit of Shalidor. Accounts that claim that she was freed, state that she would offer her thanks to them and with it, the Spellbreaker's Staff.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Valaste-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMGB-5)</sup>
