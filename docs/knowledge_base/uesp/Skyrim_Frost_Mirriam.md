It is not possible to harvest fresh frost mirriam directly from plants. However, like plants, harvested samples will respawn after a given length of time.

## Ingredients
37 guaranteed samples can be found in 30 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 2 in [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Fellglow Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellglow_Keep) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 2 in [Lemkil's Farmhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lemkil%27s_Farmhouse) ([Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md))
- 2 in [Odfel's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Odfel%27s_House) ([Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md))
- 2 in [Sven and Hilde's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sven_and_Hilde%27s_House) ([Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

## Plants
260 dried frost mirriam branches can be found in 162 different locations, including nearly all homes and inns. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 5 in [Black-Briar Lodge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Lodge) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 4 in [the Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 4 in [Burguk's Longhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Burguk%27s_Longhouse) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 4 in the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 4 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

## Notes
- Frost Mirriam is one of 4 items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a *[Spell Tome: Conjure Frost Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Frost_Atronach)*.
- Frost Mirriam's appearance seems to be based on the real-world Parsley or Coriander, culinary plants and herbs, which explains Frost Mirriam's presence in kitchens in Skyrim.
