[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-place-Pawned_Prawn.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pawned_Prawn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pawned_Prawn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pawned_Prawn.jpg) The Pawned Prawn The **Pawned Prawn** is a general goods store in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). The store is owned and run by [Bersi Honey-Hand](Skyrim_Bersi_Honey-Hand.md), although his wife [Drifa](Skyrim_Drifa.md) will take over running the store if anything happens to Bersi. It is located next door to [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md), to the west of [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) near the center of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

## Walkthrough

### Pawned Prawn
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pawned_Prawn.jpg) The main shop Upon entering the shop, you will find that the room is also their main living room, where they cook and dine. There is a fireplace opposite the door on the west wall, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front of it. Against the south wall is a cupboard holding [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise), including some [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), and to the left is a round wooden table holding a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and two chairs.

The counter is L-shaped and comes out from the north wall, and holds various displayed merchandise, including a couple [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot) and an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). Also on the counter are three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) and a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:An_Apology) from [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), who refuses to go into [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md). This note is here regardless of whether you have cleared the barrow or not.

There is a set of shelves in front of the counter against the north wall that holds only display merchandise and clutter. To the right, against the west wall is a dresser with a Dwarven Urn on top that can be used as a bargaining piece when trying to complete the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) [quest](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md).

Behind the counter, against the west wall is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot, with a wall shelf above holding displayed merchandise, with two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) between the fireplace and chest. Above the counter is a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), all hanging from the ceiling.

### Cellar
There is a door to the north that leads to the residential area. Beyond the door are three [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels, with a fourth over a flight of stairs leading down to the cellar. Under the stairs is a pile of firewood and a couple straw bales, with a dresser to the left, against the west wall, holding a bottle of wine. Another food barrel is in the southwestern corner, with a set of shelves to the left, against the south wall. The shelves hold five [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a couple loaves of bread, and several pieces of cheese, with an adept-locked [safe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Safe) to the left. The safe contains minor loot, plus several copies of a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:To_A_Concerned_Citizen) that doesn't seem to have been used in a quest. Beside the safe is a doorway to the south that leads into [Bersi's](Skyrim_Bersi_Honey-Hand.md) and [Drifa's](Skyrim_Drifa.md) bedroom. It contains a double bed coming out of the middle of the south wall, with an end table to the left which holds the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger), and a wall shelf above holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). At the foot of the bed is a dresser with an apprentice-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) on top. A chair is against the north wall, and a wardrobe containing [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes) is in the northwestern corner.
