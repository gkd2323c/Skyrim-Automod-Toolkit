# Bloodfire Cult

The Bloodfire Cult was a cult of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) worshipers, mainly members of the nobility, that attempted to destroy the city of [Ravenwatch](Lore_Ravenwatch.md) around [2E 244](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_244). They were in operation since [2E 243](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_243). Every member of the Bloodfire cult was captured and sentenced to death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dtt A-1)</sup>
