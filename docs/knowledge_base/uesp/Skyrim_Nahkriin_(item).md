[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-item-Nahkriin.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nahkriin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nahkriin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nahkriin.jpg) Nahkriin Nahkriin is an ebony [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) obtained by killing the eponymous [Nahkriin](Skyrim_Nahkriin_(creature).md), who is encountered during the [main quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md) at [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md). It is imbued with an enchantment that grants the wearer a [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) effect that gives the wearer an additional fifty points of [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md). The enchantment also decreases the cost of spells in the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) and [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) schools by twenty percent, due to its [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) and [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) effect. It can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [ebony ingots](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The World-Eater's Eyrie](Skyrim_The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie.md)**: Battle through [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md) to reach [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md)'s portal to [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).

## Notes
- Nahkriin can only be obtained when in [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn_Temple.md) as part of the main quest. If you fail to retrieve it, you will not get a second chance to obtain it.
- Placing Nahkriin on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses it gained from tempering.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- Nahkriin means "Vengeance" in the [dragon tongue](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
