Antus Pinder was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) warrior of the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovian_West.md), known for his unwavering defense of the city of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) against a vastly superior threat. Despite suffering a grave defeat, the city honored his bravery and spirit by erecting a [statue](Lore_Statues.md) in his likeness at the main square, just outside the castle gate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion Loading-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion Kvatch-2)</sup>

The identity of the superior invading force remains unclear. However, it is known that the statue was erected sometime between the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup> and the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) of the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion Kvatch-2)</sup>

## Notes
- One of the most significant conflicts Kvatch faced during this period occurred in [3E 249](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_249) when it endured the invasion of western [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) led by the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md), resulting in the city's brutal sacking.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TR-4)</sup>
