*I didn't think there could be anyone more idiotic than a lizardman, then I met with you.*
—An annoyed blacksmith<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Lizard Men (or **lizardmen**) are bipedal [lizard](Lore_Lizard.md) -like creatures, though once thought to be distant cousins of the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), it was soon evident they had nothing "human" within them whatsoever. They are described as a race of carnivorous [reptiles](Lore_Reptile.md), whose use of language stretches only far enough to communicate the location of their prey to the rest of their hunting party, which then move in to attack and feed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMLM-2)</sup> These creatures are easily identified as cold-blooded reptiles due their distinctive eyes, claws, spiked tails, fangs, and scales which glisten in the light. They have been identified with various descriptors such as "reptilian humanoids", "man-beasts", and have been described as "a [hybrid](Lore_Multiraciality.md) of a [man](Lore_Human.md) and a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md)".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They are known to posses the ability to paralyze their opponents, and despite their bestial nature, they are capable of wielding axes and shields which they use to attack with and defend themselves.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Enemy-4)</sup>

Various factions and forces were known to utilize lizard men as foot soldiers or guards. Lizard men were found all throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Enemy-4)</sup> and were known to be among [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md)'s legions.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) were known to use lizard men to patrol their guild halls across Tamriel during night hours.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Enemy-4)</sup> Various criminal groups such as the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARFaction DB-6)</sup> [the Necromancers](Lore_Worm_Cult.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARFaction NC-7)</sup> and the [Wharf Rats](Lore_Wharf_Rats.md) were known to utilize the aid of lizard men to deal with their enemies.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ARFaction WR-8)</sup> The [Underking](Lore_Underking.md) was known to use lizard men as minions,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARFaction UK-9)</sup> with them also being among the beasts that infested [Stonekeep](Lore_Stonekeep.md) during its occupation by [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) working for [Golthog the Dark](Lore_Golthog_the_Dark.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup> One lizard man was among the specimens contained in the Bestiary of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CTArena-11)</sup>

Despite the apparent lack of relation, "lizard men" is a pejorative term that is often synonymous with Argonians.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-PGE1-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Ts-13)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-OMa M-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-15)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

## Notes
- | [^](#note_nb1) | At the time of the introduction of Lizard Men and Argonians in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)*, the Argonians were much more humanoid than lizardlike in appearance, having grey skin, abdominal muscles with a linea alba, nipples, human-like toes and fingers, and lacking tails altogether, making their differences from lizard men much clearer at the time as Argonians were initially intended to be [dragonlike](Lore_Dragon.md) humanoids in appearance.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Vijay Making Of-16)</sup> From *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* onwards, Argonians were given a more lizardlike appearance, having green skin, lizard heads, large tails, scaly underbellies without a linea alba, and no longer having mammalian nipples; *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* going even further by giving them clawed feet, and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) giving them tail spikes, further blurring the line between the two species. Coinciding with this, lizard men have never been directly mentioned in any of the games since *Arena*, with the meaning of "lizard men" itself having been supplanted as a term for Argonians. There has been no direct explanation given for the link/difference between since *Arena*, though a sketch of a hunched toll-town Argonian similar to the lizard men appears in the *[Pocket Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition_The_Wild_Region)*, and [hist sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md) is known to have bestowed Argonians their intelligence,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Lo S-17)</sup> though lizard men being Argonians would directly contradict their description in *Arena*. |
