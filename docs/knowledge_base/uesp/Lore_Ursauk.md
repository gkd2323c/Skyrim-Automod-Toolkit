Ursauks are [bear](Lore_Bear.md) -like [mammals](Lore_Mammal.md) with strong front legs native to the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md), and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IU-1)</sup> Like bears, they are often used as mounts. Though fearsome in appearance, ursauks are capable of making staunch companions when properly socialized.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Atmora-2)</sup> Some [druids](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md) are capable of transforming into ursauks,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> while others use them as guardians.

Like bears,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> they likely orginate from the frozen continent of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Atmora-2)</sup> Atmoran Snow Ursuaks are a thick furred species that are able to thrive in Tamriel's northern reaches.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Atmora-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Ursauks bear several similarities to the extinct real-world carnivores known as *[Amphicyon](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amphicyon)*, also known as "bear-dogs".
