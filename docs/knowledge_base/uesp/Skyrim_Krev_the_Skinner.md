[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-npc-Krev_the_Skinner.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Krev_the_Skinner.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Krev_the_Skinner.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Krev_the_Skinner.jpg) Krev the Skinner (female) **Krev the Skinner** is the leader of the group of [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) who make [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md) their home. Krev only appears once the quest [The Silver Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silver_Hand) has been started.

Krev's gender and race are randomly chosen.

## Related Quests
- **[The Silver Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silver_Hand)**: Join the ranks of [the Circle](Skyrim_The_Circle.md) and clear [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md) of werewolf hunters.
- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md)**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Bugs
- Krev respawns with the rest of the dungeon. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, addresses this issue. A generic boss will spawn instead.
- Krev may fail to spawn. This can cause the quest to become stuck. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed by typing into the console: `prid 0002516D` then in the next line `resurrect`. If Krev is still invisible, re-open the console and type `prid 0002516D`, then `kill`. This will emulate Krev being killed and will allow you to progress in this quest-line.
