[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-npc-Ogmund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ogmund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ogmund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ogmund.jpg) Ogmund the Skald **Ogmund** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) and the resident [bard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Speechcraft Expert) (or as he puts it, "old skald") at the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). While most of his time is spent at the inn, he also spends some time at [his home](Skyrim_Ogmund%27s_House.md) nearby. He claims to have been very influential among Skyrim's bards, as his styles and songs are still passed down by teachers to their students at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md). He will [perform](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) *[The Age of Aggression](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md#The_Age_of_Aggression)* (or *[The Age of Oppression](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md#The_Age_of_Oppression)* if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) control the city), *[Tale of the Tongues](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md#Tale_of_the_Tongues)* or *[The Dragonborn Comes](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md#The_Dragonborn_Comes)* upon request. Also, he is an expert [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md). He leaves the Silver-Blood Inn around 8pm to head home and go to sleep.

Ogmund wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), including matching pairs of [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Ogmund%27s_House) to his house and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached he can say:

*"I've seen every corner of Skyrim, but nothing is as beautiful as the battlefield."* *"Songs are just old memories that help the mead go down quicker."* *"Stormcloaks took Markarth. It is good to see Talos worshipped openly again."* **(If the Stormcloaks control Markarth)** After Search and Seizure he will only say:

*"Damn Elves! You'll pay for this!"* *"Praise Talos!"* When spoken to directly:

*"What can an old skald do for you, huh?"* **A skald? Is that some kind of bard?** *"That's about the truth of it. Write tales about the sights I've seen. The battles I've fought. Earned quite a name for myself when I was younger. The Bards College still teaches some of my work to their students."* **What do you do around here?** *"I'm a bard, trained at the Bards College in Solitude. Wanderers like you should think about applying."* **The Bards College? What's that?** *"It's a school that teaches music, poetry, that sort of thing. Most bards in Skyrim have been to the college at some point. If you're thinking of applying, talk to Viarmo in Solitude."*
## Conversations
Ogmund may be seen talking with Kleppr about his performance in [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md):

**Kleppr**: *"Playing tonight, Ogmund? Everyone here is anxious to hear the old ballads performed by the True Skald of the Reach."* **Ogmund**: *"Son, I know thousands of the old ballads. If I played all of them, everyone in this inn would be as grey as I am before I finished."* **Kleppr**: *"Uh, right. Maybe just one of the popular songs, like "The Slaying of the Falmer Princes"?"* **Ogmund**: *"That's a sensible lad. Now fetch me drink."*
## Notes
- If Search and Seizure is completed first but Stormcloaks later take over Markarth, there is no indication that Ogmund will be freed despite Talos worship is no longer banned under Stormcloak rule.
- Ogmund is a potential target in the radiant Dawnguard quest [Deceiving the Herd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deceiving_the_Herd).
