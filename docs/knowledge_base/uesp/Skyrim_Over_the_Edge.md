## Quick Walkthrough
1. Look for a [note](Skyrim_Suicide_at_Dragon_Bridge.md) at the Four Shields Tavern.
2. Search downriver for the [body](Skyrim_Guard_(Alternative_Armors_-_Steel_Soldier).md).
3. Read [the guard's note](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Note_on_Aldepius.md).
4. Confront [Aldepius](Skyrim_Aldepius.md).
5. Meet [Peladius](Skyrim_Peladius.md) on the Dragon Bridge.
6. Defeat Peladius.
7. Retrieve the armor.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Suicide in Haafingar
Upon installing the [Alternative Armors - Steel Soldier](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Steel_Soldier.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), you will be directed to read a [note](Skyrim_Suicide_at_Dragon_Bridge.md) located in the [Four Shields Tavern](Skyrim_Four_Shields_Tavern.md) in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). Reading it, you will learn of an apparent suicide which took place at the town's eponymous Dragon Bridge. A new recruit guardsman had gone missing but a fellow guard named [Aldepius](Skyrim_Aldepius.md) claims to have seen him leap from the bridge.

You will find the [guard](Skyrim_Guard_(Alternative_Armors_-_Steel_Soldier).md)'s body quite a ways downstream to the northeast of the bridge (though on the south bank of the river). It is just before the river meets another on their way to the bay at Solitude; about half-way between Dragon Bridge and the Solitude Sawmill. On the guard's body, you will find [a note](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Note_on_Aldepius.md) that shows the dead guard suspected Aldepius of being a bandit informant rather than a stationed member of the Dragon Bridge garrison. You will need to return to town to confront Aldepius.

### Duel at the Dragon Bridge
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-quest-Over_the_Edge_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Over_the_Edge_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Over_the_Edge_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Over_the_Edge_02.jpg) Face Peladius on the bridge Upon re-entering the Four Shields Tavern, you will find that Aldepius is nowhere to be found. Instead, he has left a [confession](Skyrim_Aldepius%27_Confession.md) at one of the tables, inviting you to face him in honorable combat at the Dragon Bridge. In it he admits that his real name is [Peladius](Skyrim_Peladius.md) and that he is a former member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) bandit gang.

Peladius will be waiting for you on the bridge and will attack on sight, so be prepared to fight to the death. Grant him the honorable death he wishes for, and loot his body for a full set of [enchanted steel soldier armor](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Steel_Soldier_Items.md). This will complete the quest and will grant you the ability to craft this new armor for yourself.

## Notes
- If the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) still holds [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), the guards involved in this quest will be wearing Solitude colors. If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) have taken the city, the guards will be wearing Stormcloak armor instead.
