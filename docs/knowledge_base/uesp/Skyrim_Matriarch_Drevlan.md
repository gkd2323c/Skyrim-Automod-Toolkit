[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Matriarch_Drevlan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Matriarch_Drevlan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matriarch_Drevlan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Matriarch_Drevlan.jpg) Matriarch Drevlan **Matriarch Drevlan** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) priestess who leads the last worshippers of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), the powerful demigods who once ruled over [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) before being killed or mysteriously vanishing from Mundus.

She and the other members of the [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md) have started building a new temple location in Ashfall's Tear, just north of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). After gaining access to the inner sanctum of Ashfall's Tear through the [hints](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro%27s_Note.md) from [Hand Kenro Hlan](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro_Hlan.md), you can choose to kill her and the other "heretics" or spare her life and join the temple. If spared, she will give several quests to obtain sacred Tribunal artifacts, spread the word about the temple, and save Solstheim from the threat of Dagoth Ur's last devotee.
