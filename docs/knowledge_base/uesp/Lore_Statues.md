# Statues


Statuary and sculptures allow some to gain a deeper understanding of their history and heritage and connect with their ancestors in a tangible way. This is especially true for [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), who maintain a connection with their original homeland of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) through the numerous forms of art. Statues and sculptures can also serve as a symbol of hope, strength, and resilience. For the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) they can inspire individuals to strive towards greatness and can act as a reminder of the accomplishments and contributions of the people who came before. Statues of great leaders and heroes, for instance, encourage the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) to emulate their qualities and work towards achieving their greatness. Moreover, statues and sculptures can also serve an important role in the various religions on Nirn, from the worship of the [Aedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aedra), the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md), the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) and many others. These can be a source of pride for communities and help to create a sense of identity and belonging, and act as a reminder of the unique qualities and characteristics of a particular region or culture. Many statues can be animated to serve as [golems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Golem).
