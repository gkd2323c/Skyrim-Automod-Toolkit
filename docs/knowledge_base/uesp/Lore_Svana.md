Svana was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) ruler of [Western Skyrim](Lore_Western_Skyrim.md). Born a princess to High King [Svargrim](Lore_Svargrim.md) and Queen Gerhyld in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt C:PS-1)</sup> Svana was not fond of her role as heir, which she referred to as a role that "she didn't ask for". She was a descendant of High King [Svartr](Lore_Svartr.md), whose opposition to Queen [Freydis](Lore_Freydis.md) had been responsible for Skyrim's split into [Eastern](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md) and Western Kingdoms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Western Skyrim would come under threat with the reemergence of the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md), resulting in the assassination of Queen Gerhyld. Svana and [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) confronted Svargrim after discovering he was a pawn of the Gray Host and managed to defeat him before he could devastate the realm.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOGR-3)</sup> Princess Svana was able to persuade both Prince [Irnskar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Irnskar) and [Jorunn the Skald-King](Lore_Jorunn.md) to offer their support to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachmen.md), who also had suffered greatly from the actions of the Gray Host. Svana, now Jarl, attended the meeting of local rulers in [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), which was called by the [Ard Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md), the leader of the Reachfolk. Jarl Svana, the Skald-King, and Prince Irnskar together with the Ard signed a peace treaty between Western Skyrim, [Eastern Skyrim](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md), and [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md). After the declaration of peace, Svana and Prince Irnskar worked together to establish the terms of the new accord.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-S-5)</sup>
