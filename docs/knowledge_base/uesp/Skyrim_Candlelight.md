**Candlelight** is a novice level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell that creates a floating ball of [light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light) that hovers around the caster for 60 seconds.

## Effects
- [Candlelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light), 5 pts for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Stability](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stability), raises duration to 90 secs
- [Alteration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Alteration_Dual_Casting.md), increases duration to 132 secs, if cast with both hands

## Notes
- *Stability* and *Alteration Dual Casting* perk effects stack rendering its duration to 198 seconds, if cast with both hands.
- Understandably not recommended for stealth. [Magelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Magelight) may be preferable in these situations as it doesn't constantly reveal your location.
- Interestingly, in the *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* add-on, when entering some Black Books where dark areas can inflict damage, this spell, and Magelight by extension, has the same effect as using a torch in that it can protect you, so long as it is maintained.
