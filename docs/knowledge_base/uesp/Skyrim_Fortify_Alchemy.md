*Enchanting description:* **Created potions are <mag>% more powerful.**
**Fortify Alchemy** increases the strength of potions created using [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Alchemy if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Bracers/Gauntlets, Helmets, and Necklaces *[of Alchemy](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Alchemy)* - Includes the enchantments *of [Minor/Major/Extreme/Eminent/Peerless] Alchemy*
- [Muiri's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Muiri%27s_Ring)
- [Ring of Pure Mixtures](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Pure_Mixtures) - This item cannot be disenchanted while the [related quest](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Caves).md) is active, but can at other times.

Items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Krosis](Skyrim_Krosis_(item).md)

## Items to Enchant
The Fortify Alchemy effect can be applied to the following items:

- Circlets, Hats, Hoods, and Helmets
- Gauntlets and Gloves
- Necklaces
- Rings

## Notes
- The book *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim)* lists the ingredient [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) as *"effective in mixtures for boosting one['s] Alchemy skill."* This is not true, as there are no ingredients with the fortify alchemy effect.
- [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md) in Solitude is a great place to look as it is frequently restocked with several enchanted items.
- The [Seeker of Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seeker_of_Shadows) power from the [Dragonborn add-on](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) fortifies your alchemy along with all other stealth skills by 10%.

## Bugs
- Falmer and Penitus Oculatus helmets can be worn together with circlets or [Krosis](Skyrim_Krosis_(item).md). When enchanted with Fortify Alchemy, this allows you to equip five Alchemy-boosting items at once, instead of four. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- The Seeker of Shadows reward acquired from the Black Book *[The Sallow Regent](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Sallow_Regent_(book).md)* only gives a 9% bonus to prices instead of the advertised 10%. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Due to a bug, the [Dwarven Helmet of Eminent Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Helmet_of_Eminent_Alteration) also has an alchemy enchantment. <sup>**?**</sup>
