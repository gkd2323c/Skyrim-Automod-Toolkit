[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-npc-Indolent_Farmer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Indolent_Farmer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indolent_Farmer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indolent_Farmer.jpg) Indolent Farmer The **Indolent Farmer** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear inside [Vilemyr Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000330b3) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). He carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)* and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
