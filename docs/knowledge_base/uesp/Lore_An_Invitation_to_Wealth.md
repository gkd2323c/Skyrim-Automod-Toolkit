An Invitation to Wealth *An Ebonheart trader appeals to peoples' pockets* *
---
Many have spoken of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) as a force for peace between our peoples. A force for war against the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), and other such upstart powers.

Today, I ask you to think of the Ebonheart Pact as a force for wealth!

Until now, [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) has remained largely closed to trade, other than that in [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) slaves. The inhabitants have never been willing to deal with their enemies. However, [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) is slightly more developed than the rest of the Marsh, and it is Shadowfen that is the gateway to riches!

For only a small commission, I can take your goods to Shadowfen and sell them to the Argonians. They are a people with relatively few fine goods, yet they are no duller or less covetous than your [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) customers. The Argonians will be lining up for our silks and steels. The potential demand is almost impossible to calculate.

Give me your goods today, and I will bring you gold tomorrow!
