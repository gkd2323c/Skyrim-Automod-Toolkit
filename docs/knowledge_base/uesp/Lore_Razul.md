**Yokeda** Razul was a powerful [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) warrior who fought alongside [Divad Hunding](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md) and played a significant role among the leaders of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) forces during the conquest of [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md). Some sources also regard him as the first of the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) Tu'whacca's Throne Little is known about Razul's life. He was born in [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) and fought alongside Divad Hunding, guiding the Ra Gada in their conquest of Alik'r. Subsequently, upon the arrival of the [Na-Totambu](Lore_Na-Totambu.md) and the Yokudan nobility, Razul asserted himself as Yokeda to solidify the Ra Gada's authority, earning him the title of the first Forebear by some accounts. He was an extremely powerful warrior known for wielding dual swords. Razul was remembered as one of the figures who changed the course of Redguard history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-YR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RA-3)</sup>

His remains were laid to rest in [Tu'whacca's Throne](Lore_Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.md) in Alik'r. According to [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) scholars, alongside Armiger [Rhina](Lore_Rhina.md) and Queen [Afsar](Lore_Afsar.md), he was considered among the most powerful and dangerous [mummies](Lore_Mummy.md) buried there. During the Withered Hand crisis in Alik'r in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he was among the many risen dead, but he was eventually returned to rest thanks to the efforts of the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) and an adventurer who aided them at their task.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-YR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RA-3)</sup>
