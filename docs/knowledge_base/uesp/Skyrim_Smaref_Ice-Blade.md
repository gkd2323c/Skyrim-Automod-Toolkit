[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Smaref_Ice-Blade.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Smaref_Ice-Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Smaref_Ice-Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Smaref_Ice-Blade.jpg) Smaref Ice-Blade **Smaref Ice-Blade** is an orphaned [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) living in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He became the true heir of Clan Ice-Blade after his mother was assassinated as part of a plot led by [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md) and [Gisli](Skyrim_Gisli.md). Smaref was then used as a political puppet to transfer away the clan's land holdings east of [Jehanna](Lore_Jehanna.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), before being sent to live comfortably in Riften in ignorance of what had transpired.

You will only encounter Smaref at the end of the related quest, when his uncle [Pjofr Ice-Blade](Skyrim_Pjofr_Ice-Blade.md) comes to Riften to bring Smaref back to High Rock and restore the clan's titles.
