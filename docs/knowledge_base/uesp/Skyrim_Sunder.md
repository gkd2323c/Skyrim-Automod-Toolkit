[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-item-Sunder.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sunder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sunder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sunder.jpg) Sunder Sunder is a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) artifact created by [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md), one of the three [tools](Lore_Kagrenac%27s_Tools.md) used to siphon the power of the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md). Together with [Wraithguard](Skyrim_Wraithguard.md), it is acquired through the [Legends Lost](Skyrim_Legends_Lost.md) quest which has you claim them from the [Sightless Vault](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md). Tempering Sunder requires [Dwarven Metal Ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

If it is wielded with Wraithguard equipped, damage of [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) weapons is increased by 15% and [stamina regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Stamina.md) by 50%.

## Related Quests
- **[Legends Lost](Skyrim_Legends_Lost.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md)</sup>: Track down Kagrenac's missing Tools.

## Notes
- Sunder has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Sunder)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Kagrenac%27s_Tools.md).

## Bugs
- Despite being a Mace and receiving the bonuses from the [Bone Breaker](Skyrim_Bone_Breaker.md) perk, Sunder has game data (including swing speed, stagger chance, and animations) of a war axe.
- Sunder's Fury effect may be erroneously tagged as fire damage. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
