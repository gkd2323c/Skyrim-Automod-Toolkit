[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-interior-War_Quarters.jpg/200px-SR-interior-War_Quarters.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-War_Quarters.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-War_Quarters.jpg) First room of the War Quarters The **War Quarters** is a small [Dwarven building](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md). It is set into the southeastern wall of the cavern, northeast of the [Great Lift at Raldbthar](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Raldbthar.md). The building is devoid of any [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) or [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).

## War Quarters
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg/200px-SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-War_Quarters_Sleep_Chamber.jpg) The sleeping chamber It is a typical deserted Dwemer building. Upon entering, the first room has a balcony at the rear with a door accessing it, and a doorway in front of the balcony to the south. The southern doorway leads into another empty room, with an unlocked door to the east that opens into a T-junction. To the south is a sleeping chamber, with seven beds arranged in a fan formation. To the north is another empty room, with a stone double bed in an alcove to the north and a door to the west. This door opens onto the balcony at the rear of the first room, which has two unlocked Dwemer [chests](Skyrim_Chest.md) along its edge.

## Notes
- One copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Mid Year, v6](Skyrim_2920,_MidYear,_v6.md)* can be found on a table at the top of the stands overlooking the arena-like ring just west of the War Quarters.
