# Rare Curios Items

This page documents the **new items** added by the [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). Most can only be obtained from [Khajiit caravan traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).

## Ammunition
All of these items are crafted at a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) with [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) and either the appropriate metal [ingot](Skyrim_Ingots.md) or the noted item.

### Bolts
Unlike steel and Dwarven bolts, these can be forged anywhere if you have joined the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). They can also be purchased from [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) after the quest [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md). All bolts can still only be crafted by being a member of the Dawnguard faction and speaking with Sorine after A New Order.

## Food
Both of these items are created by [cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md) them in a pot. Chokeberries can be purchased from Babette. Placing a poisoned apple in an NPC's inventory and waiting results in the NPC's death. Waiting time varies and occasionally poison fumes become visible. This does not require the [Poisoned](Skyrim_Poisoned.md) Pickpocketing perk.
