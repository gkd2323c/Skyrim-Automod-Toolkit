[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-npc-Frodnar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Frodnar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frodnar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frodnar.jpg) Frodnar on the bench outside of the Sleeping Giant Inn **Frodnar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md). He enjoys playing pranks on people and can sometimes be found talking to his friend [Dorthe](Skyrim_Dorthe.md) about pulling a prank. He is often accompanied by a [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) named [Stump](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stump) as well.

Frodnar is the son of [Gerdur](Skyrim_Gerdur.md) and [Hod](Skyrim_Hod.md), who own the local mill. Like his parents, Frodnar is a supporter of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), and has a great deal of respect for his uncle [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md).

Frodnar wears [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). He carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Gerdur%27s_House.md) to [the family's house](Skyrim_Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.md), a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child), and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
He thinks very highly of himself and will sometimes say:

- *"You're new around here, so I'll go easy on ya, but don't get on my bad side."*
- *"Get me mad, and I'll prank ya. And not a little. A lot."*
- *"Ah, everybody around here's so serious."*
- *"So I like to have a little fun, so what."*
- *"My friend Dorthe doesn't like my pranking. "You're being mean, Frodnar!" So what if I am?"*
- *"Mother wants me to work at the mill, but I ain't nobody's slave."*
- *"You gotta have better things to do than talk to me all day..."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Before the Storm
Should you choose to accompany Ralof to Riverwood after [escaping from Helgen](Skyrim_Unbound.md), a scene will occur in which Frodnar eagerly asks about the status of the war and professes his desire to join the Stormcloaks:

**Frodnar**: *"Uncle Ralof! Can I see your axe? How many Imperials have you killed? Do you really know [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)?"*
**Gerdur**: *"Hush, Frodnar. This is no time for your games."*
**Frodnar**: *"Aw, mama, I want to stay and talk with Uncle Ralof!"*
**Ralof**: *"Look at you, almost a grown man! Won't be long before you'll be joining the fight yourself."*
**Gerdur**: *"Go and watch the south road. Come find us if you see any [Imperial soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md) coming."*
**Frodnar**: *"That's right! Don't worry, Uncle Ralof, I won't let those soldiers sneak up on you."*

## Conversations
Frodnar is usually seen together with Dorthe and Stump, and can be heard having a conversation about a prank involving Stump:

**Dorthe**: *"Wait, so... a costume? You're not serious..."*
**Frodnar**: *"Sure I am. We get some furs and tree branches, paint them white, and then tie them to Stump. Instant [Frostbite Spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md)!"*
**Dorthe**: *"Frodnar... nobody is going to believe that your dog is a... Frostbite Spider. And if they do... they'll kill him. That's not much of a prank."*
**Frodnar**: *"Oh yeah, and what would you rather do?"*
**Dorthe**: *"How about... we play tag! You're it!"*
**Frodnar**: *"Hey, no fair! Get her, Stump!"*

Sometimes Frodnar and his father Hod can be seen spending some quality time together. When they have a conversation, Hod will usually ask his son about the recent pranks he did, and also encourages him. Frodnar will pick between three random lines and Hod will do the same:

## Notes

## Bugs
- Frodnar may be stuck at times, standing idly alone at the center of Riverwood, no matter the time of the day, how long you wait, or how much time you leave Riverwood.
A possible fix is entering and leaving the house (Alvor's or Hod & Gerdur's) where his dog Stump is. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If Frodnar is orphaned and you [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) him, Stump will continue to follow Frodnar, causing him to travel from Riverwood to your house and back every day. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- Frodnar's bed isn't a child's bed, causing him to either sleep in his parents' bed or stay up all night. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. His bed wasn't marked as a child's bed, making it impossible for him to use it.
