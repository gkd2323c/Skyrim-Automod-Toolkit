[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bristleback.jpg) A Bristleback **Bristlebacks** are large tusked boars that are found on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) near the [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) and [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md). They will become hostile if approached too closely or provoked. You may loot the corpses for their [meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boar_Meat) and [tusks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boar_Tusk). They are often used by [Rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md) as mounts.

## Notes
- There is a unique bristleback named [Bilgemuck](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bilgemuck) which is a part of the quest [The Chief of Thirsk Hall](Skyrim_The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall.md).
- The gold dropped by bristlebacks is affected by both the [Imperial Luck](Skyrim_Imperial_Luck.md) racial ability and the [Golden Touch](Skyrim_Golden_Touch_(perk).md) perk.
- The meat dropped by bristlebacks cannot be cooked, even though there is a [cooked variety](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Boar_Meat) available. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, addresses this issue. The meat can be cooked.

## Bugs
- Bristlebacks fail to respawn due to missing respawn flags in the game data. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
