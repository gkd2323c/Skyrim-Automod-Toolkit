[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Khajiit_Bandit.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Khajiit_Bandit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Khajiit_Bandit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Khajiit_Bandit.jpg) Khajiit Bandit This **Bandit** is a male [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) found in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md) in one of the booths set up for enjoying [Redwater Skooma](Skyrim_Redwater_Skooma.md). He is lying on a bedroll coughing, surrounded by [empty skooma bottles](Skyrim_Empty_Skooma_Bottle.md) and will never move from it. If you attempt to interact with him, you will receive the message "This person is busy." He wears leveled [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor), leveled [light boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor), and has a 50% chance of wearing leveled [light bracers](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor).

## Notes
- This NPC's displayed name may be different for different players. He can appear as "Bandit", "Bandit Outlaw", "Bandit Thug", "Bandit Highwayman", "Bandit Plunderer", or "Bandit Marauder".
