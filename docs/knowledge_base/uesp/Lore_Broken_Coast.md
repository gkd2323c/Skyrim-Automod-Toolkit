[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/ON-map-Broken_Coast.jpg/200px-ON-map-Broken_Coast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Broken_Coast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Broken_Coast.jpg) Map of Broken Coast circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Broken Coast**, also called the **Strident Coast**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTValenwood-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> is the westernmost region of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md), consisting of the outer coast of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). The city-state of [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) has the seasonal summer site located deep into the Broken Coast mainland, on the road in between the Broken Coast and the rest of Malabal Tor. The township of [Velyn Harbor](Lore_Velyn_Harbor.md) is the second largest settlement in Malabal Tor, due to its convenient location. The [Wood Orc](Lore_Wood_Orc.md) stronghold of Dra'bul is located on the eastern end of the Broken Coast, in an open area just before the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md) Estuary.
