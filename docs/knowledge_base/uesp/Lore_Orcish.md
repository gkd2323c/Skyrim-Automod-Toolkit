**Orcish** is the language of the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), and a descendant of the language known as [Old Orcish](Lore_Old_Orcish.md). While it is unknown precisely when the transition from Old Orcish to modern Orcish took place, it could not have evolved any later than [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), by the time of which it was well established by the Orcs of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> However, seeing that there is evidence for the Old Orcish language having been largely forgotten by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKharsthun-2)</sup> it's impossible to know for certain when this transition did in fact occur.

## Language Family and Genetic Relationship
Orcish is likely a direct descendant of [Old Orcish](Lore_Old_Orcish.md).

## Spread of Language
Due to its age compared to Old Orcish, modern Orcish is spoken more widely by society. Circa [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), Orcish language lessons were provided by various [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) trainers and [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) patricians. At the time, Orcish was a required skill in the Fighters Guild's High Rock and Hammerfell branches.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup>
