[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Cosnach.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Cosnach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cosnach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cosnach.jpg) Cosnach **Cosnach** is a drunken [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who is usually found at the bar in the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md). He is a porter for the [Arnleif and Sons Trading Company](Skyrim_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company.md), which can be found directly across the path from the Silver-Blood Inn. Cosnach thinks himself a good worker, but his employer, [Lisbet](Skyrim_Lisbet.md), complains about his constant drinking and says the only reason she doesn't fire him is because his cousin [Imedhnain](Skyrim_Imedhnain.md) is her assistant. He claims that the reason he spends all his time drinking is the multiple [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) raids on supply caravans, hurting business.

Cosnach gets up at 8am, and proceeds straight to the Silver-Blood Inn, where he spends the next 16 hours drinking. At midnight he will finally stop drinking and walk to his bed in [the Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md) to sleep; before repeating his schedule the following day.

You can offer him an alcoholic beverage for which he will be grateful. If you either offer him an alcoholic beverage or accept his offer of a brawl and win, he will become an available [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). With that he becomes a potential [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) partner, and can be recruited into the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md). You may also recruit him as a [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) if the [Hearthfire DLC](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.

Cosnach wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) and a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Cosnach%27s_Room) to his room as well as a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Quest-Related Events

### Fight! Fight!
If you talk to him about his mentioned bet:

**You say something about a bet?** *"I'm drunk, and it's been a while since I had a good fight. A hundred septims says I can take ya, bare-handed."* **I've got better things to do.** *"Milk drinker."* **You're on. (Brawl - 100 gold)** *"That's the spirit! Let's go."* Should you accept his bet without 100 gold in your possession:

*"Hey, I may have had a few, but I can still count. You don't have a hundred septims on you."* When you have defeated him in hand-to-hand, he will get up and say:

*"Wait, I can still fight. No, no I can't."* When he hands you the prize, he will dryly state:

*"Fair fight. Here you are."* He is now available as a follower and is a potential candidate for marriage.

### The Forsworn Conspiracy
If you ask him about [Margret](Skyrim_Margret.md) while he is in the Silver-Blood Inn during [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md):

**Does a woman named Margret stay here?** (If she is alive) *"Oh yeah. Pretty little thing. Been sitting by the fire all day."* **Did a woman named Margret stay here?** (If she is dead) *"Oh yeah. Pretty little thing. Had a room here I think. Ask the innkeeper."*
## Dialogue
When approached he can say:

*""Check the shipments" Lisbet says. "There are no shipments" I says."* *"There's no shipments coming in, so I do nothing. Just drink."* *"No breaks for a good worker out here."* When spoken to directly:

**So where do you work?** *"Arnleif and Sons Trading Company. I'm a porter. Just some muscle hired to move heavy packages. But we don't get any shipments in. Forsworn attack every caravan. So all I do is sit here and drink."* When greeting you, he might ask:

*"You a fighting man/girl? How about a little bet?"* He can exit conversations with:

*"I think I'm going to get another drink."* *"Bye."*

## Notes
- Although his default gear is light armor, Cosnach is actually more proficient in heavy armor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. His class was changed to [Combat Barbarian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Barbarian).
- As is the case with all members of the `Favor Jobs Drunks Faction` faction, the option to give Cosnach an alcoholic drink never goes away. Even if he is an active follower, you can give him a drink any time during your travels. The drink(s) can be taken from his inventory later if desired.

## Bugs
- Cosnach will still have the "So where do you work?" dialogue option even if he is married and living with you, and obviously no longer working for Lisbet.
