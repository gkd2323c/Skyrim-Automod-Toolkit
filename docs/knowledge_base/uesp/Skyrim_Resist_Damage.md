# Resist Damage

| --- | --- |
| Type | Defensive |

**Resist Damage** reduces the amount of damage taken from a physical attack as though the creature was wearing armor of the specified number of points. This effect is originally used for abilities for [Ash Guardians](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md), [Chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), [Lurkers](Skyrim_Lurker.md), [Seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md) and [Karstaag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karstaag), and not available to the player. However, if [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) is installed, you will be given Resist Damage as part of the Werewolf Claws effect when you are in [Beast Form](Skyrim_Powers.md#Beast_Form). See [Lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md).

## Notes
- There is a reference to this effect in association with the giant at [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md), but it isn't actually used on the giant.
