[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) Drear on a map of west Tamriel Drear is a location that existed during the time of the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), nestled within the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) near the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> Drear is neighbored by several settlements, including [Heldorn Mount](Lore_Heldorn_Mount.md) to the east,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md) to the northeast, and [Skaven](Lore_Skaven.md) to the south.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> Following the [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) alliance with [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), Drear was one of the first locations captured by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) during the war.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGIntro-3)</sup>
