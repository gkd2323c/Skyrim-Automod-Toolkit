Skylamps (or **Sky Lamps**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-1)</sup> are flying creatures native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAo M-2)</sup> described as big gas bags with spears for feet.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWADE-3)</sup> Skylamps can be seen flying alongside [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAo M-2)</sup> They are said to have preyed on [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), running them out of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Po M-4)</sup> However, some claim it was the [cliff racers](Lore_Cliff_Racer.md) who drove out the dragons instead, overwhelming their territory with numbers.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-GN-5)</sup>

The spell "Skylamp's Shadow" is named after them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Notes
- Skylamps as creatures were originally intended to appear in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* but were cut at some point in development,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-1)</sup> possibly being replaced in role by cliff racers.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-GN-5)</sup>
