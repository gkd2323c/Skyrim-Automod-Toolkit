Vault Twelve: Rare Book Collection

Access FORBIDDEN

Items:

"The Athedorix Conundrums" by [Mymophonus](Lore_Mymophonus.md)

- - — Nine portentous questions, with answers known to drive readers insane.

"The Hosiric Lays" by Unknown (poss. [Calisos](Lore_Callisos.md) or [Morachellis](Lore_Morachellis.md))

- - — Tales in epic verse recounting the [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) folklore of the [Cyro-Nords](Lore_Imperial.md).

"The Gwylim Praxis" by the [Aureate Serpent](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Aureate_Serpent)

- - — Methods of draining [magicka](Lore_Magicka.md) from intelligent creatures at the moment of death.

"The Third Scroll of [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md)" by [Arkan](Lore_Arkan.md)

- - — How the great thief stole the "[37th Lesson](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_37.md)" from [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) —before he could write it.

"The Letters of [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) and [Belharza](Lore_Belharza.md)"

- - — Intimate correspondence between the First Empress and the Man-Bull.

"Eleven OTHER Edicts of the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md)" by the Heretic of Lindai

- - — A parody of the classic "Eleven Edicts" supposing the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md) had worshiped [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md) rather than [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).

"[Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) Blueprints" by [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md) the Engineer

- - — ALL SCROLLS MISSING—REWARD FOR RECOVERY

"Conversations with the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md)" by [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md)

- - — Ruminations on the nature of the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md). Warning: Probably apocryphal.

"The Southern Coast as Far as the Eastern Sea" by [Topal the Pilot](Lore_Topal_the_Pilot.md)

- - — Sea logs of the great [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorer.

"[Insights](Lore_Shalidor%27s_Insights.md)" (pristine copy) by [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) the Arch-Mage

- - — A disquisition on the origin and nature of [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md).

"The [Alessian Doctrines](Lore_Alessian_Doctrines.md): Original Manuscript" by the Monkey Prophet [Maruhk](Lore_Maruhk.md)

- - — Screed defining the dogma of the non-Elven nature of Akatosh.

"Studies in Aprocrypha *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*" by [Morian Zenas](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Morian_Zenas)

- - — A summary of truths learned in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) that Zenas refuses to believe. In fourteen volumes.

"[Epistolary Acumen](Lore_Black_Book__Epistolary_Acumen.md)" by [The Transparent One](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Transparent_One)

- - — Forbidden invocations of inimical Daedra.

"Grimoire" by [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Corvus_Direnni)

- - — Spell secrets of the mighty conjurer.

"The Tract of Merid-Nunda" (complete) by Anonymous

- - — Revelations on the nature of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) and the mistake of conflating her with the [Star-Orphans](Lore_Magna_Ge.md).

"[The Lusty Argonian Maid](Lore_The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid.md)" (complete play)—Traditional

- - — Copy out for repair—accidentally damaged by Librarian.

"The Art of Love and Swordplay" by [Fjokki the Bard](Lore_Fjokki_the_Bard.md)

- - — The popular autobiography of the frisky [Nord](Lore_Nord.md), Fjokki, whose exploits are unbelievable but strangely compelling. Not rare, but a perennial favorite here at the Library.
