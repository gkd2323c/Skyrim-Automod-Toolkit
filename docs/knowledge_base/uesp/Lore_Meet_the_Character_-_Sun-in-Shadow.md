[![ON-render-Sun-in-Shadow.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-render-Sun-in-Shadow.jpg/300px-ON-render-Sun-in-Shadow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Sun-in-Shadow.jpg)
- Eraven,

- I received your talent assessment just hours ago, Mouth. Your list of potential hirelings, while impeccably written, suffers from one glaring omission: the [lizard](Lore_Argonian.md) slave, [Sun-in-Shadow](Lore_Sun-in-Shadow.md).

- These other mages bore me. According to my spies, Saduro does nothing but tinker away in Magister [Otheri](Lore_Nelos_Otheri.md)'s laboratory, like some [blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) -faced, maladjusted [Dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md). And Teris Saryon? Two-hundred years old and still suckling at her uncle [Gothren](Lore_Gothren.md)'s teat. Pathetic.

- This Sun-in-Shadow, though; she intrigues me. What a novelty! An Argonian who can weave spells as well as she pushes a broom? It's just beyond belief. And self-taught, as well? My contacts inside [Tel Naga](Lore_Tel_Naga.md) tell me that she reads Nomu's Tome of Unchained Arcana when no one is looking. The Unchained Arcana! I dare say that you still struggle with Nomu's work, Eraven. Honestly, the fact that she can read at all boggles the mind.

- Her talent is raw, undoubtedly. Like most novices, she breaks more things than she mends. Otheri tells me that she nearly burned down Tel Naga, casting spells of conflagration to warm soup in the scullery. She also played a role in that unfortunate [nix-ox](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) transmutation a few weeks back. But any true mage can see that these blunders are the result of audacity. Ambition. A [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) mage needs both in ample measure. I have no patience for the meek. Cowardly mages are like capless mushrooms—unsightly and worthless.

- I doubt we have much to worry about when it comes to mixed loyalties. Rumor has it that she is widely disliked by the other slaves. They call her "Lukiul," "smoke-scale," and "[Elf](Lore_Mer.md) -heart." Reinforce this sentiment whenever possible. She must feel isolated. Isolation breeds contempt, and contempt drives excellence. She needs a heart of cold [obsidian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Obsidian) if she's to be of any use to us.

- Now, there is the issue of her [racial](Lore_Races.md) handicap. You needn't remind me of her limitations. I've known ten generations of Argonians, and even the brightest were little better than well-trained [guars](Lore_Guar.md). With time and effort, though, she might ascend to retainer. Perhaps even oathman if she fully commits to the craft. Who knows? I'm anxious to find out how far this lizard is willing to go.

- Keep an eye on her. She has the magical aptitude, certainly. Whether she has the stomach for Telvanni politics, well, that remains to be seen.

- Magister [Therana](Lore_Therana.md)

- Wizard-Lord of the Great House Telvanni

- Master of [Tel Branora](Lore_Tel_Branora.md)
