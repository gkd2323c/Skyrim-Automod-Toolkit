Mastery of Sacrifice **by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frandar_Hunding)** *Frandar Hunding's insights on achieving mastery*
---
In the season of life in which I shivered from the [Frost's Fall](Lore_Frostfall.md), so did I find myself [called upon](Lore_War_of_the_Singers.md) to cast aside the notions of my destiny that I had deemed true. I left the [land of my home](Lore_Yokuda.md) to which I did not return for all my future days.

Under [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md)'s ever-changing influence, the [sword-singer](Lore_Sword-singer.md) must do the same to achieve Mastery of Sacrifice.
