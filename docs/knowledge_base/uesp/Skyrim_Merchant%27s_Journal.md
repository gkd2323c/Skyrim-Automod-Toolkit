Merchant's Journal
---
I begged my husband not to go, but he just wouldn't listen. He said if he delayed the shipment even another week, the [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) would find someone else to take it south.

Let them keep their blasted money. The road to [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) is dangerous these days, everyone knows it. So many caravans have been attacked, so many travellers vanished into thin air. Some say it's only bandits, but there are rumors of worse. Goblins. Ghosts. Witches.

Twenty years we've been married now, and I can't bear the thought of losing him. I'm not letting him go alone, not this time. We live or die together. Mara preserve us.
