[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-npc-Subjugated_Ghost.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Subjugated_Ghost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Subjugated_Ghost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Subjugated_Ghost.jpg) A Subjugated Ghost with his sword drawn **Subjugated Ghosts** are [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), and are the [ghostly](Skyrim_Ghost.md) appearances outside of [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md). They are accompanied by a [Ghost Adventurer](Skyrim_Ghost_Adventurer.md) and are forced to attack you against their will.

They all wear a set of randomly determined [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md) which includes a pair of boots and a 50% chance of a pair of gauntlets. Their outfit may not be a matching set, as it can be a mixture of [fur](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), [hide](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), [studded](Skyrim_Studded_Armor.md), [leather](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) or [scaled](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) versions, depending on your level.

There are two different versions of the Subjugated Ghost. One version (`Base ID 3481A`) wields a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) of any type, and a 20% chance of wielding a leveled [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md), both of which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 22. The other version (`Base ID 515F6`) wields a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md) of any type, and similarly can be up to elven quality at level 22.

They also have a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold and a 25% chance of a lockpick, as well as random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md).

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
Like the Ghost Adventurer forced to protect the Rannveig's Fast entrance, he will apologize for his attacks:

*"Stay back or I'll be forced to attack you!"*
*"Is someone there? Stay back! I don't want to attack you."*
*"Stay away!"*
*"I'm sorry! This isn't what I want!"*
*"I don't want to do this!"*
*"I don't have any choice! I'm sorry!"*

## Notes
- The Subjugated Ghost was intended to be summoned with the unique [Sild's Staff](Skyrim_Sild%27s_Staff.md). However the staff uses the wrong enchantment, and is ultimately not used in the game.
