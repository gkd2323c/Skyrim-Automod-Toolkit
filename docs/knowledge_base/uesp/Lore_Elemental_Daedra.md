# Elemental Daedra

This article is about lesser Daedra. For the artificial constructs also known as Atronachs, see [Golem](Lore_Golem.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-quest-Elemental_Army_03.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Elemental_Army_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Elemental_Army_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Elemental_Army_03.jpg) The Frost, Fire, and Storm Monarchs [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-mount-Atronach_Mounts.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Atronach_Mounts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Atronach_Mounts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Atronach_Mounts.jpg) Several atronachs bound in exotic forms Elemental Daedra, commonly known as **Atronachs**, are lesser [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) composed of various [elements](Lore_Elements.md), and can seemingly range from mindless to highly intelligent. They inhabit many planes of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), including their home realms which are also elemental in nature, and are often summoned by conjurers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Friend-3)</sup> The art of conjuring Atronachs is known as [Atromancy](Lore_Atromancy.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Atromancy-4)</sup> and those who practice it are called Atromancers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ex A-6)</sup> A more powerful version of the Atronach is the **Monarch**.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-EA-8)</sup>

It has been claimed that most individual Atronachs do not possess much in the way of thoughts,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Izzmothar-9)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-EA-8)</sup> with some Daedric Princes considering them to be "lowly animals".<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWVamp-10)</sup> It seems their intelligence may vary.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Other sources claim that Atronachs are highly intelligent, but that not all varieties may be social or have the power of speech.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> The Flame, Frost, and Storm Monarchs of Craglorn were bestowed thought by the [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) [Mage](Lore_The_Mage.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-EA-8)</sup> The more humanoid Fire and Frost Daedra are known to possess individual intelligence and are capable of speech.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-FFDD-13)</sup>

Most Elemental Daedra have no kinship or loyalties with any of the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md), although the Frost Daedra are known to have served [Xivilai Moath](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md), as have the Fire Daedra served [Faydra Shardai](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md). Frost Daedra and Fire Daedra have a deep mutual hatred for one another, but were forced to work together to claim the [Shade Perilous](Lore_Shade_Perilous.md) for [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) during the invasion of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-FFDD-13)</sup>

There are several varieties of Elemental Daedra, including:

- [Air Atronach](Lore_Air_Atronach.md), Air Monarch
- [Glass Atronach](Lore_Glass_Atronach.md), Crystal Atronach, Glass Fragment
- [Fire Daedra](Lore_Fire_Daedra.md)
- [Flame Atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md), Fire Atronach, Potent Flame Atronach, Flame Pixie, Flame Thrall, Fire Behemoth
- [Frost Atronach](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md), Ice Atronach, Dire Frost Atronach, Potent Frost Atronach, Frost Thrall, Ancient Frost Atronach, Frost Monarch, Water Atronach
- [Frost Daedra](Lore_Frost_Daedra.md)
- [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md), Earth Atronach
- [Shadow Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shadow_Atronach)
- [Stone Atronach](Lore_Stone_Atronach.md), Foundation Stone Atronach
- [Storm Atronach](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md), Potent Storm Atronach, Storm Thrall
- [Cold-Flame Atronach](Lore_Cold-Flame_Atronach.md), Lava Atronach

Banished Atronachs are often harvested for the magical materials they leave behind. [Fire Salts](Lore_Fire_Salts.md), [Frost Salts](Lore_Frost_Salts.md), and [Void Salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void_Salts) are taken from Flame, Frost, and Storm Atronachs respectively, and are useful ingredients for [alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> [Atronite](Lore_Atronite.md) is a type of crystal that is rarely left behind by only the most powerful of Atronachs.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
