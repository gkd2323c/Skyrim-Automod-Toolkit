## Notes
- For players that had not purchased Saints & Seducers, Thorn Hook was an [unobtainable ingredient](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Ingredients) added to `Update.esm` by [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) [Patch 1.5.97](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#Version_1.5.97). For those that did purchase Saints & Seducers, it was available and obtainable with the same patch. Later, Saints & Seducers became free content for all players of Skyrim Special Edition.
- Thorn Hook previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Thorn_Hook) in the *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* expansion for *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
