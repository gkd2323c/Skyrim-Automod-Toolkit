Telekinesis Arrows Research Notes *Research notes about a spell tome for telekinesis arrows* *
---
This [Spell Tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Telekinesis_Arrows.md) will teach its reader the unique art of the [Telekinesis Arrow](Skyrim_Telekinesis_Arrow.md), giving both a [Lesser Power](Skyrim_Telekinesis_Arrows.md), and the knowledge to craft these special arrows.

Upon firing the arrow, one will find it to be suspended - frozen in the air until the Telekinesis Arrow Lesser Power is cast, upon which the volley will be released.

It appears that only a certain number of arrows can be suspended in the air at a time. Attempting to fire any more than ten, they are simply returned to the archer's quiver. While suspended, one can pluck them from the air, however.

Upon making contact, these arrows lose their magical power, and cannot be retrieved. As they contain magical properties of their own, they appear to me *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* impervious to enchantments from the bow they are fired from.
