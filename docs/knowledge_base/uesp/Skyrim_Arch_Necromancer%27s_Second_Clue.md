Arch Necromancer's Second Clue *The necromancer tells you how to find the dream*
---
A man dreams his empty coffers are full of gems.

A necromancer dreams a similar dream, but his coffers are a different kind, and hold different gems and treasures inside.

But to see this dream and retrieve these gems, you must first pray to a Prince for a door.
