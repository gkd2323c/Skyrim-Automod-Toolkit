Speaking to them will open the trade screen.

Quartermasters wear a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69f) and a pair [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69e). They are equipped with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) supplemented by up to 13 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow).
