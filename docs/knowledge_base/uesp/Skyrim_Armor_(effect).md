# Armor (effect)

| [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-spell-Alteration.png) | Armor |
| --- | --- |
| School | [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) |
| Type | Defensive |

This article is about the magic effect. For the apparel, see [Armor](Skyrim_Armor.md).

**Armor** spells increase the spellcaster's [Armor Rating](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md). The effectiveness of Armor spells is improved by the [Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md) perk.

Shields do not interfere with the bonus granted by the armor spells.

## Spells
The following spells use this group of effects. Following each spell is the specific variant of the effect that it uses:

- [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md): *Armor - Oak* (`00051b15`; `Armor FFSelf0`)
- [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md): *Armor - Stone* (`00059b7a`; `Armor FFSelf25`)
- [Ironflesh](Skyrim_Ironflesh.md): *Armor - Iron* (`00059b7b`; `Armor FFSelf50`)
- [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md): *Armor - Ebony* (`00059b7c`; `Armor FFSelf75`)
- [Dragonhide](Skyrim_Dragonhide.md): *Armor - Dragonhide* (`000cdb75`; `Armor FFSelf100`)

If the [Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md) perk has been unlocked, the following effects are added to the spells

- At rank 1: *Armor 30 Perk bonus* (`00104ab5`; `Perk Armor FFSelf30`)
- At rank 2: *Armor 50 Perk bonus* (`00104aba`; `Perk Armor FFSelf50`)
- At rank 3: *Armor 70 Perk bonus* (`00104ab9`; `Perk Armor FFSelf70`)

## Notes
- This group of magic effects is the most comparable to [Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Shield) from Oblivion. Both are [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) effects that grant the caster an armor bonus that stacks with the existing bonus from any equipped armor or shields.
