*We dance the Two Moons Dance in every hour. It is our delight.*
—Clan Mother Ahnissi<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup> **Clan Mother** Ahnissi was a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) community leader and theologian, as well as an influential and prolific author. She penned *[Words of Clan Mother Ahnissi](Lore_Words_of_Clan_Mother_Ahnissi.md)*. The authorship of *[The Moon Cats and their Dance](Lore_The_Moon_Cats_and_their_Dance.md)* was also attributed to her.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup>

## History
*In the beginning there were two littermates, Ahnurr and Fadomai. After many phases, Fadomai said to Ahnurr, "Let us wed and make children to share our happiness."
And they gave birth to Alkosh, the First Cat. And Ahnurr said, "Alkosh, we give you Time, for what is as fast or as slow as a cat?"
And they gave birth to Khenarthi, the Winds. "Khenarthi, to you we give the sky, for what can fly higher than the wind?"
And they gave birth to Magrus, the Cat's Eye. "Magrus, to you we give the sun, for what is brighter than the eye of a cat?"
And they gave birth to Mara, the Mother Cat. "Mara, you are love, for what is more loving than a mother?"
And they gave birth to S'rendarr, the Runt. "S'rendarr, we give you mercy, for how does a runt survive, except by mercy?"
And many phases passed and Ahnurr and Fadomai were happy.*
—Clan Mother Ahnissi<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup> It is unknown when exactly Ahnissi was alive, but her writings cannot predate the Riddle'Thar Epiphany of [2E 311](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_311),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RT-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VOF-4)</sup> as they mention [Riddle'Thar the Two Moons Dance](Lore_Riddle%27Thar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup> Her works were already widespread by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup> placing her life sometime between the fourth and sixth centuries of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). As a Clan Mother, she held great influence within her community.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Zansora-5)</sup>

Her texts focused primarily on theology: *The Words of Clan Mother Ahnissi* detail the many litters of [Ahnurr](Lore_Ahnurr.md) and [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md), with clear differences from older texts predating the Epiphany, such as those authored by the Silent Priest [Amun-dro](Lore_Amun-dro.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SOAD-6)</sup> Ahnissi's words are further removed from original Khajiiti beliefs, as they were written after multiple conquests of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGP-7)</sup>

*The Moon Cats and their Dance* presents a story of Ahnissi and her daughter speaking with a foreign scholar who seeks to understand Khajiiti culture and faith, and [their connection](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md) to the [moons](Lore_Moons.md). She expresses sadness that scholar's eyes cannot perceive truths that are obvious to her. In the text, Ahnissi also asserts that she is an [Ohmes-raht](Lore_Ohmes-raht.md), though the narrative's nature made this claim unreliable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup> She also presented certain beliefs regarding other Khajiiti divinities, such as [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md). According to her words Rajhin the Purring Liar cast so many shadows that some gained lives of their own, becoming [black Senche-panthers](Lore_Panther.md) roaming across Elsweyr.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BSPK-8)</sup>

## Legacy
Her works were widespread during the Second, [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md), and [Fourth Eras](Lore_Fourth_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOCMA-2)</sup> and continued to influence and inspire the Khajiit.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-JI-9)</sup> Some Khajiiti rulers were known to celebrate the [Old Life Festival](Lore_Old_Life_Festival.md) by commissioning performances of the *Words of Clan Mother Ahnissi*.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Castles-10)</sup>
