# Mehrunes Dagon's Axe

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/OB-creature-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg) Mehrunes Dagon wielding his axe (Oblivion) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/ON-trailer-Gates_of_Oblivion-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Gates_of_Oblivion-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Gates_of_Oblivion-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Gates_of_Oblivion-Mehrunes_Dagon.jpg) Mehrunes Dagon wielding his axe (ESO) *A gout of fire erupted to her right. There she laid eyes upon her sibling Merrunz for the first time in eternity. The blood of a god dripped from his axe, and his fanged smile belied the story of a kinslayer. He slammed his axe against the Lattice, and though nothing before this had ever done so, the Lattice shook and cracked under its weight.*
— *[The Bladesongs of Boethra](Lore_The_Bladesongs_of_Boethra.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOB-1)</sup> Mehrunes Dagon's Axe is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) associated with [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Destruction [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOB-1)</sup> It is a large and fiery double-bitted axe.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOD-2)</sup> It is said that no mortal has earned enough favor to be entrusted with Dagon's own axe, or to know its name.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Mehrunes Dagon was known to wield his axe on numerous occasions,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup> including his attempts to invade [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PBD-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Last Year-6)</sup> [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup> and [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Oblivion-8)</sup> as well as during his assault on the [Lunar Lattice](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md) in the ancient past.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOB-1)</sup> Numerous [statues](Lore_Statues.md) also depict him wielding the weapon,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOStatuette-9)</sup> including—but not limited to—those found in the Daedric ruins of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md), Ebernanit, and [Ularradallaku](Lore_Ularradallaku.md) on the isle of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Morrowind-10)</sup> as well as in the mountains of [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md) in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Skyrim-11)</sup> The statue in [Plaza Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Plaza_Brindisi_Dorom.md) in the southern part of the city of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), commemorating [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md)'s victory over Mehrunes Dagon, also depicts him wielding an axe during their battle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PBD-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Last Year-6)</sup>

The axe is considered a blessed weapon of Mehrunes Dagon, believed by some of his followers to be forged solely for the purpose of destruction.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-CM1-12)</sup> Axe bearers were known to have a proud history in Dagon's service.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-CM2-13)</sup>
