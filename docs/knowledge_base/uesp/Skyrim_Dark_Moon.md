*Enchanting description:* **Increases your Magicka by <mag> points. Health regeneration decreased by <30>%.**
**Dark Moon** increases the target's maximum [Magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) based on the effect strength. It also decreases their [Healing Rate](Skyrim_Damage_Health_Regeneration.md) by 30%.

Note: Enchanting IDs include both FE [xxx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md#Creation_Club) 83f and FE [xxx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md#Creation_Club) 879 with base costs of 7 and 0, respectively.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to [enchant](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) custom items with this effect if you find one of the following items and disenchant it.

- [Elite](Skyrim_Elite_Necromancer_Hood.md) and [Ascendant](Skyrim_Ascendant_Necromancer_Hood.md) Necromancer Hoods

While the increased maximum magicka applies only at night (7pm-5am) for the listed items, enchanting a custom item with this effect does not have the same restriction.
