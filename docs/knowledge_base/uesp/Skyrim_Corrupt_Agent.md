[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-npc-Corrupt_Agent.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Corrupt_Agent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Corrupt_Agent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Corrupt_Agent.jpg) Corrupt Agent The **Corrupt Agent** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.md) in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

The Corrupt Agent wears a set of [Penitus Oculatus armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Armor) with a matching [helmet](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Helmet.md), pair of [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Bracers), and pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Boots). He wields an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)*.

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.
