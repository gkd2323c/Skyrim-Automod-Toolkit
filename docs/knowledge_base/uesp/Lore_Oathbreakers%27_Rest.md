Oathbreakers' Rest *Notes on the Bosmer who disagree with the Green Pact*
---
Being divided into two parts, the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) who accepted the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) were blessed by [Y'frre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) and dwelt in the forests—but those who rejected the Pact were cursed. Their [ever-changing forms](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) were stripped of life and tossed into the great tar-pits of [Ouze](Lore_Ouze.md), where they sank into eternal slumber.

The spirits of Ouze are restless, however, and the [Oathbreakers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oathbreakers) may return one day. They may return to seek vengeance on their brethren, but our eternal hope is that they will eventually accept the Green Pact, returning to Y'frre's favor and the arms of their people.
