[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Roggi_Knot-Beard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Roggi_Knot-Beard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Roggi_Knot-Beard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Roggi_Knot-Beard.jpg) Roggi Knot-Beard **Roggi Knot-Beard** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) living in [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md), where he works in [Steamscorch Mine](Skyrim_Steamscorch_Mine.md). When not mining, he can be found inside [Braidwood Inn](Skyrim_Braidwood_Inn.md) or in the mining camp outside the inn. He's an expert on mead after "a lifetime of study," and believes that the best mead in Skyrim can be found in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), which he visited when younger. This love for mead had led him to owe rather a large tab to [Iddra](Skyrim_Iddra.md), the barkeep in the local inn. After helping him by finding his ancestor's shield, Roggi becomes available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) and a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md). He's a friendly sort who will happily greet the player and share stories about his family and, of course, mead.

Roggi Knot-Beard wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) along with some [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c1) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). He carries three bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) along with a [flagon](Skyrim_Flagon.md) and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). He also wields a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md). Roggi is friends with [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](Skyrim_Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver.md), [Ganna Uriel](Skyrim_Ganna_Uriel.md), and [Kjeld](Skyrim_Kjeld.md). He is an acquaintance of [Iddra](Skyrim_Iddra.md).

## Dialogue
He will cheerfully greet you in one of several ways:

- *"If you bring mead, you're welcome here! Even if you don't, you're still welcome!"*

- *"I visited the meaderies of Riften once, as a lad. Wish I had their skill."*

- *"You've got a fine look about you. Come have a visit at the Braidwood Inn. Better than home!"*

You can ask him a few questions about himself, including why he knows so much about mead.

- **How do you know so much about mead?**
- *"A lifetime of study, you might say. The best comes from Riften, but I haven't been there in years. Ever tried making your own? It's... not easy. Trust me."*

After [A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md) has been completed, Roggi may comment on the rumors of [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md):

- *"All this talk of dragons is making people antsy. Nonsense!"*

Roggi will occasionally mention Dravynea, claiming that she keeps the mine running.

- *"Working our mine is dangerous work, but Dravynea keeps it going. Well, Dravynea and the promise of more mead."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Dungeon Delving
You can ask him if he's from Kynesgrove, which starts you off on a chain of dialogue to start the quest.

- **Are you from Kynesgrove?**
- *"Yes. I've wandered a bit, but I always come back to Kynesgrove. My clan was one of the first to settle here. Legend says that my forefather Lenne discovered the mines after he stumbled into a dark cave to relieve himself. Too much mead. Of course, there's another story of Lenne losing the family shield in a different cave, so it depends on who you believe."*
- **That's a terrible story.**
- *"Well, I never claimed otherwise."*
- **Your ancestral shield is lost in a cave?**
- *"According to my grandfather at least. He swore that version of the legend was true, even found the cave old Lenne stumbled into. Hey, if you find it, I could show you some of the clan's favorite fighting moves. They're all shield techniques."*

If you ask him if his ancestral shield is lost in a cave, he'll reiterate it and mark the cave on your map. After you exit the conversation, he may say:

- *"Hopefully, that story about Lenne's Shield is true."*

When you find the shield, you should return to Roggi and give him the shield.

- **I found your shield.**
- *"My clan's shield. So Lenne really did stumble into that cave and was eaten by monsters. In honor of his memory, let me show you a few shield techniques. And tell me if you're leaving town. Be good to travel again."*

### A Few Words with You
During the quest, Iddra will ask you to tell Roggi that she doesn't care about his tab. She says he owes her a lot of money for drinks, and she always tells him not to worry about it, but he beats himself up over it anyway. Then he gets thirsty, and the cycle repeats. Talk to Roggi.

- **Iddra wants you to forget about all that coin you owe.**
- *"I guess if you're telling me to stop worrying about it, maybe I should just let it go. Thank you, friend."*
- *"What do you think I am, a beggar? My family has always paid back every single coin we owed, going back to Tiber Septim's time."*
