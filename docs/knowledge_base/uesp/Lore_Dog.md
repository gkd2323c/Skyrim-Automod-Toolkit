# Dog

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/ON-prerelease-Gifting_02.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Gifting_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Gifting_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Gifting_02.jpg) Various dogs in a home **Dogs** are domestic animals found across Tamriel. They are often held as pets, guarding and protecting their owner's property. Bandits will often train their dogs to protect camps and attack intruders. Hunters may bring a dog along to protect them while they retrieve their kills.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Biscuit ESO-1)</sup> Dog meat is edible.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dog Meat-2)</sup> Dogs attack primarily by biting, and can carry [Witbane](Lore_Witbane.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBDog-3)</sup>

Domesticated dogs are related to [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md), and the two can be [bred](Lore_Sex.md#Fauna) to produce [wolfdogs](Lore_Wolfdog.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBHjalti-4)</sup>

### Anthorbred Avalanche Dog
Large, loyal dogs with very thick coats that help them brave the cold mountains of Skyrim. They are trained to help mortals navigate through the most treacherous of avalanches in the Pale Pass, and are capable of pulling fallen climbers out from under large piles of fallen snow and detritus.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Anthorbred_Avalanche_Dog)

### Bear-Dog
The Bear-Dog is a fierce, thick-furred breed used to hunt [bears](Lore_Bear.md) in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They track, harry and corner the bear until the hunters arrive with spears.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Black Mask Bear-Dogs were popular with the [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) [Watch](Lore_Markarth_Watch.md) (and the outlaws who hoped to evade them) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Copperback Bear-Dogs are a mischievous breed known for its responsiveness to training. Copperback Bear-Dogs are heterochromic.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Karth Winter Hounds are Bear-Dogs whose coats are white year-round for hunting in Skyrim's snowy peaks. Some Reach clans only allow them to be owned by chieftains.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> The Bear-Dogs of Morthal have spotted coats and playful dispositions.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> The Biscuit Bear-Dog possesses golden fur, serving as a guard and loyal companion to hunters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Biscuit ESO-1)</sup>

### [Death Hound](Lore_Death_Hound.md)
Death Hounds are monstrous, undead canines who can often be found in the company of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup> They have a bite as cold as the grave, which freezes its prey's flesh.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup> Their meat is edible, and they often wear valuable collars.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SRDH-16)</sup> Death hounds are voracious, indiscriminate predators that raze ecosystems where they are present.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup> They eviscerate and consume prey, not for survival, but due to their temperament.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup> Death hounds will leave nothing left alive if their populations are left unchecked.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup> Despite how harmful they can be to the environment, their presence can be vital to the ecology of the area they inhabit.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EOTDH-15)</sup>

- **Found in:** [Skyrim](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Death_Hound)

### Gonfalon Sea Cur
The Gonfalon Sea Cur is a breed of dark-furred mutt often seen working among sailors on docked ships in [Gonfalon Bay](Lore_Gonfalon_Bay.md). During the Interregnum, Gonfalon Bay nobles participated in a trend that involved purchasing hats for these shipyard dogs, as well as paying people to feed and care for them. This breed is also referred to as the "Dog of the Watch".<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

They were adopted by the settlement of [Greenhill](Lore_Greenhill.md), where they became known as fierce, loyal defenders of the villagers' grain and livestock, protecting the former from rats and the latter from predation.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup>

### Mastiff
Mastiffs are dogs with droopy jowls that make loyal companions and excellent guard dogs. During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), mastiffs were bred by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), and [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). They wore armor specially forged for them, and often served as aides to the military police.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-25)</sup> The black mastiffs of [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md) are popular with mages and alchemists during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). while tan mastiffs were popular with scouts, hunters, and trackers.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-26)</sup><sup>[[27]](#cite_note-27)</sup> During this same time period, Morthal's gray mastiffs were popular with rogues, thieves, and, oddly, priests. Morthal's titular mastiffs are considered to be fearless and tireless.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-28)</sup> White mastiffs are commonly used by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) in battle. The white mastiff is the mascot of the [Second Legion](Lore_Second_Legion.md), which is headquartered near [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md) in peacetime.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-29)</sup> Mastiffs can be outfitted in a variety of fashionable and practical gear.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-30)</sup>

### Waxing Terrier
The Waxing Terrier is a breed conceived in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by acclaimed [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) dog breeder, Kinlady Ilunsare. It took sixteen generations of careful selective breeding to distinguish the Waxing Terrier from its "parent" breed, the common Glenumbran Terrier.<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-ESOIlunsare-39)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Eustasia)

### Wolfdog
Wolfdogs are a crossbreed produced by mating a dog with a wolf. They closely resemble wolves, but exhibit doglike behavior and are less resistant to frost. Like dogs, they can also carry Witbane.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBHjalti-4)</sup>

- **Found in:** [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Hjalti)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/OB-creature-Hjalti.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Hjalti.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Hjalti.jpg) A wolfdog (Oblivion)

### Wolfhound
Wolfhounds are a shaggy breed used as hunting companions by the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of Skyrim. Whiterun Wolfhounds have gray fur, are reportedly brave enough to face [sabre cats](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md) for their masters.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-40)</sup> [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) Wolfhounds have tawny coats and are said to be descended from royal hunting dogs.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-41)</sup> Winterhold Wolfhounds have lighter, fluffier coats, as they live at high elevations where the snow never melts. They are used to hunt wolves, and their howls resemble that of their canine quarry.<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-42)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Whiterun_Wolfhound)
