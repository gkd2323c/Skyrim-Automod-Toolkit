[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane.jpg) Eorlund Gray-Mane **Eorlund Gray-Mane** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) in charge of forging weapons and armor for the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) at the [Skyforge](Skyrim_Skyforge.md), located behind [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md). He is married to [Fralia Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Fralia_Gray-Mane.md), with whom he has three children: [Avulstein](Skyrim_Avulstein_Gray-Mane.md), [Olfina](Skyrim_Olfina_Gray-Mane.md), and [Thorald](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md). He is also the brother of [Vignar Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md). Though Eorlund works closely with the Companions, he does not consider himself one of them, as his main concern is continuing his family's tradition of working the Skyforge. Nevertheless, he is very knowledgeable about their traditions and customs, and provides advice and master [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) training to new members and veterans alike. During the initiation quest [Take Up Arms](Skyrim_Take_Up_Arms.md), Eorlund will ask you for a favor, he will reward you after [Proving Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proving_Honor), and his expertise will prove invaluable during [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md).

Like the majority of Whiterun citizens, Eorlund has an opinion on the ongoing [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), and like his family, he supports the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), though not as much as the other members of [clan Gray-Mane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane).

Eorlund works the Skyforge every day from 8am to 9pm, either forging or using the grindstone. He spends the rest of his time inside [House Gray-Mane](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md) with his family, either wandering around, eating, relaxing or sleeping in his room on the second floor.

Eorlund wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots). He is equipped with a [Skyforge steel sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Sword), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Gray-Mane) to House Gray-Mane, as well as a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Related Quests
- **[Take Up Arms](Skyrim_Take_Up_Arms.md)**: Prove you are worthy to join the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).
- **[Proving Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proving_Honor)**: Claim a [Fragment of Wuuthrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fragment_of_Wuuthrad) from [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md).
- **[Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md)**: Cure [Kodlak](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md)'s spirit and become Harbinger of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).

**Are you glad the Stormcloaks are in charge?** *"I'm sure my brother Vignar is, since they made him Jarl after throwing Balgruuf out. Comes the end of the day, Imperials and Stormcloaks ain't much different. Both sides want to tell you how you should live your life. Guess I'd rather hear it from a real Nord than some Emperor down south."* His goodbyes:

*"Hm hmm."* *"We done?"*

### Take Up Arms
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_04.jpg) *"My name is Eorlund Gray-Mane. I work the Skyforge."* During the quest [Take Up Arms](Skyrim_Take_Up_Arms.md), you will find yourself as an errand runner for the Companions. When you locate Eorlund, he will ask:

*"What brings you here?"* **Vilkas sent me with his sword.** *"I'm guessing you're the newcomer then?"* **Does Vilkas always send newcomers on errands?** *"Oh, don't worry too much about it. They were all whelps once. They just might not like to talk about it. And don't always just do what you're told. Nobody rules anybody in the Companions."* **I'm just doing what I'm asked.** *"That attitude would get you far, if you were some stuffy merchant or a Jarl's footstool. Around here, you'll want to learn to live your own life. Remember, nobody rules anybody in the Companions."* Either will lead to:

**Someone has to be in charge, though.** *"Well, I'm not sure how they've managed it, but they have. No leaders since Ysgramor. Kodlak is the Harbinger, and he's a sort of advisor for the whole group, but every man is his own. Every woman, her own."* **Are you a Companion?** *"Not actually a Companion myself, but none of them know how to work a forge properly, and I'm honored to serve them. My name is Eorlund Gray-Mane. I work the Skyforge. Best steel in all of Skyrim. All of Tamriel."* Lastly, he will grab the opportunity and use your low rank to his benefit:

*"I have a favor to ask."* **What is it?** *"I've been working on a shield for Aela. My wife is in mourning and I need to get back to her soon. I'd be much obliged if you could take this to Aela for me."* **Didn't you just tell me not to be a servant?** *"This isn't a command. Just decency. Help out an old blacksmith. I've got to get back to my work."* **I'm happy to lend a hand.** *"That's a good man."* He will then return to his duties. Once the quest is completed, Eorlund will offer master training in [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) with the words:

*"It's an honorable craft. I'll show you what I can."* You can ask him again about the Companions, although he will be a bit insulted:

**** *"By Ysmir's beard, I ain't one for humor. You know who the Companions are. So quit your asking."*

### Glory of the Dead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_02.jpg) *"We take our leave."* When you have started [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md) and returned to Whiterun, you'll find several citizens gathered around the Skyforge to pay their respects to deceased Harbinger [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md). Naturally, Eorlund is present as well, standing next to Kodlak's body on top of the forge itself. When you arrive, you'll witness the ritual, with Eorlund asking the first question:

**Eorlund:** *"Who will start?"* **Aela:** *"I'll do it. Before the ancient flame..."* **Everyone:** *"We grieve."* **Eorlund:** *"At this loss..."* **Everyone:** *"We weep."* **Vilkas:** *"For the fallen..."* **Everyone:** *"We shout."* **Farkas:** *"And for ourselves..."* **Everyone:** *"We take our leave."* After the ritual, Eorlund will watch as Aela ignites the fire under Kodlak, and people will then start to go their separate ways. Aela will gather what is left of [The Circle](Skyrim_The_Circle.md), while Eorlund will seek you out and ask:

*"Do you have the fragments of Wuuthrad, still? I'll need to prepare them for mounting again."* **I return them with honor. (Give fragments)** *"Don't get too flowery on me, lad/lass."* **Here, take them. (Give fragments)** *"Careful with those. Don't want even more fragments, do we?"* He will then make a special request:

*"Of course, I have a small favor to ask of you. There's another piece, that Kodlak always kept close to himself. Would you go to his chambers and bring it back for me? I'm not sure I'm the best one to go through his things."* *"Thank you. I'll be here."* *"I appreciate it."* He will then return to his daily routines while you go through Kodlak's end table for the fragment. When you return to Eorlund, he will say:

*"You're back."* *"Thank you. Your Shield-Siblings have withdrawn to the Underforge. I think they're waiting for you."* He will then get to work, regardless of the hour, and keep his plans secret for the time being.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eorlund_Gray-Mane_03.jpg) *"Just because something is, doesn't mean it must be."* In the Underforge, the members of the Circle will be gathered around the fountain, all of them frustrated and discouraged. They all know that Kodlak died without being granted his only wish, namely to be freed of his lycanthropy so he could spend his afterlife in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). While they know of a way to cleanse his soul, it requires them to enter the [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) and perform a ceremony:

**Vilkas**: *"The old man had one wish before he died. And he didn't get it. It's as simple as that."*
**Aela**: *"Being moon-born is not so much of a curse as you might think, Vilkas."*
**Vilkas**: *"That's fine for you. But he wanted to be clean. He wanted to meet Ysgramor and know the glories of Sovngarde. But all that was taken from him."*
**Aela**: *"And you avenged him."*
**Farkas**: *"Kodlak did not care for vengeance."*
**Vilkas**: *"No, Farkas, he didn't. And that's not what this is about. We should be honoring Kodlak, no matter our own thoughts on the blood."*
**Aela**: *"You're right. It's what he wanted, and he deserved to have it."*
**Vilkas**: *"Kodlak used to speak of a way to cleanse his soul, even in death. You know the legends of the Tomb of Ysgramor."*
**Aela**: *"There the souls of the Harbingers will heed the call of northern steel. We can't even enter the tomb without Wuuthrad, and it's in pieces, like it has been for a thousand years."*

At this point, Eorlund Gray-Mane will enter and say:

**Eorlund**: *"And dragons were just stories. And the elves once ruled Skyrim. Just because something is, doesn't mean it must be. The blade is a weapon. A tool. Tools are meant to be broken. And repaired."*
**Vilkas**: *"Is that? Did you repair the blade?"*
**Eorlund**: *"This is the first time I've had all the pieces, thanks to our Shield-Brother/Shield-Sister here. "The flames of a hero can reforge the shattered." The flames of Kodlak shall fuel the rebirth of Wuuthrad. And now it will take you to meet him once more."*

He will then address you personally:

*"As the one who bore the fragments, I think you should be the one to carry Wuuthrad into battle."* He will then hand you the weapon and speak to the other members:

*"The rest of you, prepare to journey to the Tomb of Ysgramor. For Kodlak."* He will then turn around and leave the Underforge.

When you have completed Glory of the Dead and Kodlak enjoys his afterlife in Sovngarde, a trip up to the Skyforge and Eorlund will make him tell about a strange change he has observed with the forge:

*"You know, since Kodlak's funeral the Skyforge feels more... awake. It's always been said that the souls of the heroes of old are what give Skyforge steel its strength. But I think the forge knows the greatness of Kodlak's soul. I can't really explain, but it feels like it's... young. I'll wager it could now forge metal the likes of which hasn't been seen since eras long forgotten. I'm eager to try."* After completing the full Companions questline, standard "Ancient Nord" weapons from draugr can be converted into "Nord Hero" weapons by speaking to Eorlund at the Skyforge.

## Rumors
Eorlund's smithing skills are known all over Skyrim, although you will hear Whiterun citizens talk about them the most. At the local weaponry shop, [Warmaiden's](Skyrim_Warmaiden%27s.md), [Adrianne Avenicci](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adrianne_Avenicci) will tell you: *"I don't claim to be the best blacksmith in Whiterun. Eorlund Gray-Mane's got that honor. Man's steel is legendary. All I ask is a fair chance."* When giving you her [favor quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword_for_a_Great_Man), she will mention Eorlund once again: *"Aye, that I do. I've got to, if I hope to be as good as Eorlund Gray-Mane some day. In fact, I just finished my best piece of work."* Naturally, Fralia Gray-Mane thinks highly of her husband and will tell you: *"My husband Eorlund tends the Skyforge up at Jorrvaskr, the Companions mead hall. There's no finer smith in the nine holds. You should ask him about his work. It's the only subject that'll get him talking. Well, that and maybe those stuck-up, dung-sniffing Battle-Borns."* When trying to sell her wares at the market, she will shout: *"Shiny trinkets for your good lady! All crafted by the great Eorlund Gray-Mane himself!"* She can also be seen telling [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) why she doesn't need to trade with the caravans: *"Plenty of people here that need Eorlund's craftsmanship, my dear. We've never had any reason to deal with those caravans of theirs."* She will also tell [Lillith Maiden-Loom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lillith_Maiden-Loom) that Eorlund will craft a new locket in polished silver: *"Ahhh, I see. I think I know just what you need. I don't have anything like that here now, but my Eorlund - he can make you one right quick. When I get home, I'll have him start on it right away."* In Jorrvaskr, [Njada Stonearm](Skyrim_Njada_Stonearm.md) will tell [Skjor](Skyrim_Skjor.md) how to hold his shield: *"If Eorlund made it, it's more likely you're gripping it wrong. Find me tomorrow and we can go over it."*

When you first enter Whiterun you can overhear Adrianne Avenicci trying to convince [Idolaf Battle-Born](Skyrim_Idolaf_Battle-Born.md) about asking Eorlund for help: *"Why don't you swallow that stubborn pride of yours and ask Eorlund Gray-Mane for help?"*, to which Idolaf will hatefully reply: *"Ha! I'd sooner bend my knee to Ulfric Stormcloak."* Due to the feud between the two clans, another Battle-Born, namely [Olfrid](Skyrim_Olfrid_Battle-Born.md) will say: *"Eorlund Gray-Mane's a stubborn fool. Our friendship ended when he turned his back on the Emperor."* However, most people outside Whiterun recognize Eorlund's skills, even young [Dorthe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dorthe) from [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), who will ask her [father](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alvor): *"You really think I can be a blacksmith someday, papa? As good as Eorlund Gray-Mane?"* In [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), local blacksmith [Oengul War-Anvil](Skyrim_Oengul_War-Anvil.md) will prove more bitter, saying: *"Everyone says Eorlund Gray-Mane is the best smith in Skyrim. I plan to change a few minds about that."* and add: *"I respect Eorlund, but he has the good fortune to work the Skyforge. Something about the fires... their steel just holds tighter."* The most prominent rumor about Eorlund occurs during the [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) quest, when most of Skyrim's upper class is gathered for a party inside the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). At that occasion, the party guests will discuss Eorlund. One guest will comment: *"I'm not sure that it's news, but lately it seems like every blacksmith in Skyrim is out to prove that he's the equal of Eorlund Gray-Mane."* The other guest will then reply: *"It's not really a fair comparison, if you ask me. After all, Eorlund works the Skyforge. How is any smith going to compete with that?"*
