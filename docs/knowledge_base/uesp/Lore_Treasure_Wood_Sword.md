# Treasure Wood Sword

The Treasure Wood Sword was one of the eight [monster children](Lore_Monster_Children.md) from the unholy union of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), who initially escaped being slain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV12-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOV18-2)</sup> The Treasure Wood Sword found itself in the possession of the [Clan Ra'athim](Lore_Ra%27athim.md) of the Chimer [House Mora](Lore_House_Mora.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV22-3)</sup> Vivec demanded that the house return the Treasure Wood Sword to him lest they face his wrath, to which a [Bonewalker](Lore_Bonewalker.md) of House Mora stated they would not return the sword, in part due to a bargain with the Daedric Prince [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV22-3)</sup> Vivec, unpleased with this, employed the services of the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md). They proceeded to, per the Sermons, destroy the House and Clan in its entirety.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV22-3)</sup> The King of Assassins of the Morag Tong reported the assassination of the Prince of House Mora himself to Vivec and presented the Warrior-Poet with the Treasure Wood Sword.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV22-3)</sup>
