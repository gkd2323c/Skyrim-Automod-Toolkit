# Asil Yelir

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Asil Yelir [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg/200px-RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) Map showing Asil Yelir by Maiko K'Elmar Asil Yelir is an island in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), south of the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls), that is possibly considered a part of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago. The two large islands [Nalonga](Lore_Nalonga.md) and [Ravan](Lore_Ravan.md) neighbor it to the northwest and southeast, respectively. It shares the word "Yelir" with the similarly sized [Siuol Yelir](Lore_Siuol_Yelir.md) to its southwest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
