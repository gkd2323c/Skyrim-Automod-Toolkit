[3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389) Amber Guard was a village located in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), southwest of [Snowhawk](Lore_Snowhawk.md). It had road connections to [Karthwasten Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Karthwasten_Hall) and Lainalten.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Amber Guard was an active settlement, being ruled by Lord Jurger. It had a rivalry with Lainalten and [Markarth Side](Lore_Markarth_Side.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>
