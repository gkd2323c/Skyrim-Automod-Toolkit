# Fenlords

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/ON-npc-Fenlord.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Fenlord.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Fenlord.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Fenlord.jpg) A Fenlord Fenlords, also called **Mummy Lords**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> are undead [Barsaebic Ayleid](Lore_Barsaebic_Ayleids.md) [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) who transformed themselves into [mummies](Lore_Mummy.md). These revenants once ruled over parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) as necromancer kings in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOZahra-2)</sup>

The Barsaebics were [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) worshippers, driven from the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) into exile in Black Marsh following the [Narfinsel Schism](Lore_Narfinsel_Schism.md). The Barsaebics continued their practice of [slavery](Lore_Slavery.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Captured Time-3)</sup> and the Fenlords were no different in that regard. When their slaves rose against them, the Fenlords had them executed and revived as undead. Even in undeath, the Fenlords mistrusted their servants. To guard against rebellion, they inserted [magical](Lore_Magic.md) control icons into the heads of their undead slaves.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOZahra-2)</sup> These mummified servants can still be found guarding their tombs in the [Ayleid ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md#Black_Marsh) of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The Fenlords eventually disappeared, possibly as a result of [Alessian](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) persecution. Some instead believe that the Fenlords used the [Crown of Ascension](Lore_Crown_of_Ascension.md) to leave [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) forever.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOZahra-2)</sup> In truth, some Fenlords continue to dwell in the depths of Barsaebic ruins, ruling over their undead servants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOFenlord-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOZahra-2)</sup>
