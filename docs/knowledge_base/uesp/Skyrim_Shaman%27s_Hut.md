## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance is a bench. The entrance is unlocked.

### Shaman's Hut
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shaman%27s_Hut.jpg) Shaman's Hut interior The entrance leads into a room with a well-dressed dinner table to the left and three respawning barrels and a non-respawning sack to the right, all of them with food. Next to the barrels are a set of shelves with two bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), an [ash yam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam) and one bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) along with a respawning food sack. The dinner table holds three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) in a bowl, a [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md), two pieces of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a piece of bread, a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). On a shelf above are two [salt piles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) on a plate and a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute).

Opposite the table is the entrance to the room with an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md). A non-respawning dresser holds various clothing and a bowl with a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) sits on top, next to a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6). To the left of the enchanter is a copy of the book *[Children of the All-Maker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Children_of_the_All-Maker)* and in a wooden bowl are a [Nordic barnacle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle), a [pearl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl), and a rare [small pearl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Pearl). Also on the table there's a filled [lesser soul gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk), a salt pile, and a plate with another Nordic barnacle and a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg). Above the enchanter are two non-respawning food sacks, some [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), and a bit of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) on a shelf. On the floor to the left of the table is an open box containing a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato).

A huge fireplace covers most of the floor in the central room with two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) and two ash yams on the side. On the roof supports above is a single bottle of ale. There is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) in the southwestern corner, with a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) in a bowl and a freshly caught [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and some [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging above. The non-respawning cupboard to the left contains clutter and the respawning barrel contains food. The shelves above the cupboard hold a head of [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a fillet of [horker meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat), and another salt pile. A bottle of alto wine and a bottle of ale can be found on the top shelves along with a respawning [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) containing uncommon ingredients.

Another respawning apothecary's satchel on a ledge, this time containing rare ingredients, can be found beneath the mudcrab trophy on the wall, along with some wheat and a bucket containing a sample of [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw), a sample of [trama root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root), and a [mudcrab chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Chitin). Some shelves near the bedroom door hold a [deer hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd), and two non-respawning food sacks. The bedroom contains two owned beds and a set of shelves with three pearls and a horker tusk.
