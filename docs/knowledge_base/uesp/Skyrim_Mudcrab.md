## Named Mudcrabs
There are several named mudcrabs in the game:

- [Old Salty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Salty)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Pincer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pincer)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, who can be brought in as a pet by your [child](Skyrim_Adoption.md)
- [Snippy](Skyrim_Snippy.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg) The unused [Solstheim Mudcrab](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim_Mudcrab)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> - Arrows shot at mudcrabs will bounce back toward you, just like they do when fired at [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) and [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).
- [Solstheim Mudcrabs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solstheim_Mudcrab)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> are a unique ash-covered variety of mudcrab. They don't appear in-game under normal circumstances; you can find a single one in [Editor Smoke Test Cell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Test_Cells#Editor_Smoke_Test_Cell).
- A giant mudcrab shell lies in a pool of water southeast of [Gjukar's Monument](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gjukar%27s_Monument), which is also the location of the [Mudcrab Guardian Spirit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit) during [Kyne's Sacred Trials](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials).
- The [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a unique [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md), which can be purchased as a pet from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md).

## Bugs
- Mudcrab legs<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> have a duplication bug. The model of the single item in your inventory is displayed as a pair of legs, and if you drop a single instance of the item in your inventory, the legs will be dropped as two separated legs instead of a single paired item. If one leg is picked up, you will receive another pair of mudcrab legs that can be dropped again to become two legs. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Sometimes, mudcrab bodies will sink below the surface of the ground after you've killed them, with only their pincers stuck above ground. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Two mudcrab spawn points (one east of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) and one near [Bleakwind Bluff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleakwind_Bluff)) are unnaturally too far away from any water source, which obviously doesn't match their natural habitat. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
