# Xero-Lyg

*… were known as the Nine Coruscations, who followed the parabolas that led away from Magnus. Merid-Nunda was of these Sisters, as was Mnemo-Li, as was Xero-Lyg, as was ….*
— *[Exegesis of Merid-Nunda](Lore_Exegesis_of_Merid-Nunda.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EOMN-1)</sup> Xero-Lyg, ** the Black Star**, **the Orphan Opposite** is one of the lesser-known [Magna Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md), one of the Star-Orphans and an [Anuic](Lore_Anu.md) ur-entity. She was one of [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md)'s daughters and the sister of [Merid-Nunda](Lore_Merid-Nunda.md), [Mnemo-Li](Lore_Mnemo-Li.md), [Iana-Lor](Lore_Iana-Lor.md), [Unala-Se](Lore_Unala-Se.md), the [Prime Archon](Lore_Ithelia.md), [Londa-Vera](Lore_Londa-Vera.md), [Sheza-Rana](Lore_Sheza-Rana.md), and [Valia-Sha](Lore_Valia-Sha.md). Alongside her sisters, she was part of the Nine Coruscations, who followed the parabolas that led away from Magnus and separated from him when he withdrew from the creation of the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-9C-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EOMN-1)</sup> Similarly to other Magna Ge, she is now synonymous with the [star](Lore_Stars.md) that she created.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EOMN-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WOT-3)</sup>

Xero-Lyg is associated with the [element of Flesh](Lore_Elements.md#Flesh).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-9C-2)</sup> She ventured into the [adjacent space](Lore_Adjacent_Place.md), where she fought side by side with [Lorkh](Lore_Lorkh.md), the Sower of Flesh, a [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) interpretation of the Missing God [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md). They battled within alternate worlds unto endless possibilities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-9C-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GSOTR-4)</sup>

According to [Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md), the Magna Ge as a whole were responsible for the creation of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) in "the bowels of [Lyg](Lore_Lyg.md)".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MDC4-5)</sup> According to Star-Queen Varalias, the "[King of Dreugh](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) fell to Mehrunes the Razor … was forced to … the next [kalpa](Lore_Kalpa.md) … to spiral ever-out and see the land and sky preferred to sea". Following that fall, "she was left to wander beside [the serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md), so dark as to not be at all". She was said to be carried by the [unstars](Lore_Unstars.md) and cataloged events through time and untime, finding paths yet unseen to those among the stars. She "looked within [the wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Wheel) and the center was gone". What she saw was unknown, known only to her and other unknown entities, who had to carry this burden alone.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-9C-2)</sup>
