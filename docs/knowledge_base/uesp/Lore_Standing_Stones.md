# Standing Stones


Throughout the wilderness of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) lie various types of **standing stones**, [stones](Lore_Statues.md) of power which are often attuned to the [heavens](Lore_Astronomy.md). The origins of these stones are a mystery for the most part, but they have been known to grant great power to those deemed worthy enough.

## All-Maker Stones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/BM-place-Sun_Stone.jpg/200px-BM-place-Sun_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Sun_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Sun_Stone.jpg) An All-Maker Stone **All-Maker Stones** are a type of standing stone found on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were created by the [All-Maker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:All-Maker), and maintain the balance of nature on the island. There are six stones: Beast, Earth, Sun, Tree, Water, and Wind. The [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) keep the stones free of corruption to empower their nature magic. The Stones themselves can grant powerful blessings. In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md) attempted to harness the power of the Stones to free himself from [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md), but the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) used the power of the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) to cleanse them.

## Doomstones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/OBR-place-The_Tower_Stone.jpg/200px-OBR-place-The_Tower_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-The_Tower_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-The_Tower_Stone.jpg) A Doomstone **Doomstones** (or **Doom Stones**) are a type of [runestone](#Runestones) found throughout [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). They are marked by red glowing runes, and often surrounded by crude stone circles. Each Doomstone is attuned to a specific celestial object, such as the major [constellations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Birthsigns) (**Birthsign Doomstones**), as well as other astronomical bodies such as the moons, the [Void](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void), [Aetherius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherius), [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md), and [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) (**Heavenly Doomstones**).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lectures-1)</sup> The Doomstones grant great power to doom-driven [heroes](Lore_Hero.md) of significant renown, but appear inert to common mortals. As such, their apparent magical nature was of great interest to the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). It is commonly believed by scholars that the Doomstones were sites of ancient sky-worshipping cults of [beastfolk](Lore_Beastfolk.md) before the ages of [man](Lore_Man.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## Mundus Stones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-place-The_Warrior_%28Cyrodiil%29.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Warrior_%28Cyrodiil%29.jpg)](File_ON-place-The_Warrior_(Cyrodiil).jpg.md) [](File_ON-place-The_Warrior_(Cyrodiil).jpg.md) A Mundus Stone **Mundus Stones** are a common type of standing stone found throughout Tamriel. They are attuned with the thirteen [birthsigns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Birthsigns), and grant powerful blessings. The magical resonance of the stones comes from strong Aetherial currents filled with unrealized potential,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Azandar Dialogue-3)</sup> which flow directly from Aetherius through the stars and radiate outwards to form a continental web. The origins or purpose of these stones are unknown,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> although it has been speculated that they were erected by ancient star-worshipping [Nedes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede) who imbued them with [nirncrux](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nirncrux) to attune them with the power of the stars. With sufficient skill, [sapphires](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sapphire) can be integrated into pieces of clothing and armor; these items are then blessed by the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), and can increase the power gained from a Mundus Stone when worn.

## Runestones
*This section is about a type of standing stone. For information on the runes used in enchanting, see [Rune-Language](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rune-Language) and [Runic Alphabet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Runic_Alphabet).* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/OBR-place-Sidri-Ashak_Runestone.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Sidri-Ashak_Runestone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Sidri-Ashak_Runestone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Sidri-Ashak_Runestone.jpg) A Runestone **Runestones** (or **Rune Stones**) are a type of standing stone found throughout Cyrodiil. They are marked by green glowing runes. There are four types of Runestones: **Hestra Runestones**, **Reman Runestones**, **Sidri-Ashak Runestones**, and **Fort Runestones**. The former three are named after [Empress Hestra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hestra), [Emperor Reman](Lore_Reman.md), and [Potentate Sidri-Ashak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sidri-Ashak) respectively, and regional scholars say they are mythically aligned with former Emperors.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Azandar Dialogue-3)</sup> Fort Runestones are found in ancient [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) ruins such as [Sancre Tor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sancre_Tor). [Doomstones](#Doomstones) are also considered a type of Runestone. It is theorised that the Runestones are related to [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) and date from the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), but competing theories say they originated in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) or early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). In any case, it is believed that they were then moved to their current positions by the [First](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) and [Second Empires](Lore_Second_Empire.md) to serve as magical beacons. This is supported by the fact that all three Emperors associated with the Runestones were involved with improving Cyrodiil's roads.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lectures-1)</sup> The Runestones grant [bound](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bound_Items) Daedric weapons and armor to doom-driven heroes, but appear inert to common mortals. As such, their apparent magical nature was of great interest to the Mages Guild.

## Standing Stones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-place-The_Lord_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Lord_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lord_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lord_Stone.jpg) A Standing Stone Also known as **Stones of Fate**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> **Standing Stones** can be found throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and in Northern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). They are attuned with the thirteen birthsigns, and grant powerful blessings. The three **Guardian Stones** are found together, while the others are scattered throughout the province. They are well-known to the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of Skyrim and legends speak of them allowing heroes of old to rewrite their fate.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> [Aetherium](Lore_Aetherium.md) can be used to harness the power of the stones.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skyrim-7)</sup> The stones can also be used to create powerful scrolls due to them emitting large amounts of magicka, enhancing the scrolls' powers.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TSS-8)</sup>
