# Sheep

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/OB-creature-Sheep.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Sheep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Sheep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Sheep.jpg) A sheep (Oblivion) Sheep are domestic livestock found on farms and sheepfolds. They are very docile and produce wool. Male sheep are called **rams**, and have horns. People who farm sheep are called shepherds. Female sheep are sometimes known as **ewes**. Sheep are easy prey for [bears](Lore_Bear.md) and other predators. Occasionally, shepherds need to take precautions to protect their flock.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBTA-1)</sup> Shepherds are known for making [cheese](Lore_Cheese.md) out of sheep's milk. Sheep cheese is described as having a sharp, startling odor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTS-2)</sup> They can also be raised for [mutton](Lore_Mutton.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBSheep-3)</sup>

Their wool can be used to make a variety of products, including blankets,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOWool Blank-4)</sup> carpets,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOWool Carp-5)</sup> flags,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOWool Flag-6)</sup> and clothes.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOWool Scarf-7)</sup> Wool is an insulator, making it an excellent material for products that allow citizens to handle hot food and utensils.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOWool Mitt-8)</sup> It is also soft, making it excellent for filling soft pillows.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOPillow Wool-9)</sup> The [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) use wool in their clothing, as this is in compliance with the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOSpinner Costume-10)</sup> [Harpy](Lore_Harpy.md) down can be spun with fine wool to make a sturdy material for mages' robes.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOHarpy Shirt-11)</sup>

During an annual competition in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), horse-sized rams from the [Yorgrim River](Lore_Yorgrim_River.md) are ridden by contestants up and then down a mountain at top speeds, bareback and without falling off. The rams bred for this purpose are massive, and often toss riders off their backs purposefully during the competition. Many believe that the rams choose their riders because of this.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-YRR-12)</sup> The ancient kingdom of [Helkarn](Lore_Helkarn.md) in central [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) was known for its expert shepherds and herders.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TSK-13)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a group of pirates ran a sheep smuggling operation out of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) before being forced to relocate to [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-IC-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-NFFMF-15)</sup>
