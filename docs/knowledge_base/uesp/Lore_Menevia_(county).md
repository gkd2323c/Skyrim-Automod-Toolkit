[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Menevia was found Menevia was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) county on the southern coast of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It was bordered by [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire_(fiefdom).md) to the northwest, the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) to the north, and the [Orsinium Area](Lore_Orsinium_Area.md) and [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) to the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> Its capital was the city of Menevia. The [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region, and the regional deity was [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Menevia became a part of a greatly enlarged Wayrest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWit W-3)</sup>
