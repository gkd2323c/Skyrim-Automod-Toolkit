Some of these ingredients have new effects from the previous games. The Creation also includes poisoned apples, Ayleid treasures, and new arrows and bolts.

It was released on August 29, 2018 for 100 Creation Credits, and was later included as part of the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) Creation. Both Creations were made available for free to all [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) players with the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) on November 11, 2021.

## Overview
This Creation adds a huge number of new **[items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Curios_Items)**, the majority of which can only be obtained from [Khajiit caravan traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). True to name, these items are quite rare and difficult to obtain, as the traders will only have a limited selection of items available at any given time. Many of the new items are [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), some with effects which are otherwise unavailable through [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) (for example, [Cure Poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison), [Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light), [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md), [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md)).

The Creation also adds new crafting recipes. The new [Chokeberry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chokeberry) ingredient can be used to [cook](Skyrim_Cooking.md) powerful [poisoned apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Curios_Items#Food), and several new types of [ammunition](Skyrim_Ammunition.md) can now be [forged](Skyrim_Forge.md).

In addition to Khajiit caravans, chokeberries can be purchased from [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md), the new ammunition can be purchased from archery shops, and the Ayleid stones can be found as loot in boss chests. If Saints & Seducers is installed, many of the enemies in the questline drop Shivering Isles-related ingredients, and several can be found as loot or growing in the [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md).

## Notes
- The new bolts are made of specific materials because they are recreations of the types of [bolts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Marksman#Crossbows_and_Bolts) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
- There are now new ways to fall from any height. Originally the ingredient [Netch Jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly) was a life-saver when eaten mid-fall, unlike the [Become Ethereal](Skyrim_Become_Ethereal.md) shout that cannot be activated mid-fall. The Rare Curios Creation adds a couple of new ingredients with a similar effect: [Corkbulb Root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corkbulb_Root) and [Gold Kanet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Kanet), with Corkbulb Roots being a lot more common. As all of these ingredients cause [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) when eaten, they can be eaten shortly before impact to negate the [fall damage](Skyrim_Damage.md#Fall_Damage). - Similarly, these ingredients can be used for "jelly clipping" - paralyzing yourself during certain types of third-person power attack to clip through walls.
