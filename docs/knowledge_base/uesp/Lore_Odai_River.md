[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/MW-place-Odai_River.jpg/200px-MW-place-Odai_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Odai_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Odai_River.jpg) The Odai River The Odai River, also called **River Odai**, was a river running north to south on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It began in the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) region just north of [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) and flowed placidly through the center of the city to the Odai Plateau. It then passed through the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) region before emptying into the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) at the eastern edge of the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md) near the town of [Hla Oad](Lore_Hla_Oad.md). The terrain surrounding much of the river was mountainous and dotted with mines.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> A rough track along the western shore of the river connected Hla Oad with Balmora.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GTB-3)</sup> Water from Odai River was considered fine for brewing, as lava runoff gave it a charred taste.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ciirillie-4)</sup> It is unknown how the river was affected by the cataclysms of the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md).
