# Purified

The Purified are beings that have been lustrated for purification by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md). For mortals, this usually involves being stripped of their free will and bestowed with the gift of immortality for the purpose of serving her for all eternity. The Purified are imbued with a golden radiance within their bodies that causes them to emit a glow from beneath their skin. Beings who join the ranks of the Purified include those who willingly pledge themselves to Meridia's service, as well the unwilling,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTharayya-1)</sup> such as those who defy her.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTharayya-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKing Narilmor-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMeridian Purified-3)</sup> They are also referred to as **Lustrants** by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) minions,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLO-4)</sup> though this is sometimes used as a catch-all term to refer to Meridia's followers.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive AUA-5)</sup>

A notable example of a willing convert was the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [King Narilmor](Lore_Narilmor.md), who guarded one half of the ancient relic known as the [Wrathstone](Lore_Wrathstone.md) in the Ayleid ruins of [Garlas Malatar](Lore_Garlas_Malatar.md) for three thousand years. An entire Imperial expedition was forcibly converted when they uncovered the power hidden within Garlas Malatar, serving as unwilling protectors of the Wrathstone.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FMMWTDOM-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKing Narilmor-2)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the treasure hunter Tharayya, accompanied by the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md), uncovered the horrors that took place in Garlas Malatar. The expedition fought off hordes of the Purified who attempted to convert them, including King Narilmor. Despite the resistance, the Undaunted were ultimately successful in retrieving the right half of the Wrathstone. In certain circumstances, the Purified may be turned into [Blessed Sentinels](Lore_Blessed_Sentinel.md), such as when Narilmor was transformed into one.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TGL-7)</sup>

Beasts are also capable of becoming Purified. An example of beasts becoming Purified are creatures "elevated" from [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), such as Sabre Cats. These beasts are rumored to dwell in Meridia's realm, the [Colored Rooms](Lore_Colored_Rooms.md), in a menagerie.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONMeridian_Sabre_Cat-8)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONMeridian Cub-9)</sup> One instance of Daedra becoming Purified is when the Daedric Prince [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) invaded Meridia's plane, allowing [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md) [Daedrats](Lore_Daedrat.md) to cross into the realm. After the failed invasion, the Daedrats were rounded up, purified, and then banished to Nirn as Lustrated Daedrats.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONLustrated_Daedrat-10)</sup> In [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), lustrants are specifically imprisoned in the Lightless Oubliette when captured by the minions of Molag Bal.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLO-4)</sup>
