# Cavilstyr Rock

Cavilstyr Rock is a promontory facing the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), located directly outside of the walls of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md). Just behind the castle, a path goes directly behind the walls. Every year on the [Festival of Flowers](Lore_Holidays.md), a crowd of spectators gathers around a large circle of unlit torches, and covered baskets are arranged with dancers inside the circle, facing the Eltheric Ocean. <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MOT1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOT2-2)</sup>
