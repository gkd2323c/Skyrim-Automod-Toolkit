Arlimahera of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBOD-1)</sup> was the High Queen of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). In life, she was one of two wives of High King [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), whose ascension to the throne brought peace between the rival parties, the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) and [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWAAHD-2)</sup> In her time as monarch, she acquired the sobriquet of the **Blood Queen**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBOD-1)</sup> The descendants of this union of three held positions of power throughout Hammerfell for generations.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRLo S-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TABKF-4)</sup>

## History
Arlimahera was born to a royal family of [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md), as the fierce and clever daughter of [Murahd](Lore_Murahd.md), the Crown Grandee of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) in southern Hammerfell. She was married to the then newly-named High King of Hammerfell, [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), alongside with [Fereshtah](Lore_Fereshtah.md), the daughter of the Forebears Grandee of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), [Ebrahim](Lore_Ebrahim.md). The three together had split the rift between the ancient groups and had created a more unified Hammerfell under the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWAAHD-2)</sup>

Arlimahera was remembered as a ruthless leader and conqueror, often speaking of slaying her enemies with her own hand. But according to her old journal written in [2E 412](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_412), she would employ the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) and their leader, the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md), the quote, "secret arsenal my family has employed since my grandfather's time." This was the first mention of the Brotherhood in history and scholars have theorized that they have been active since at least [2E 360](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_360), if her grandfather had really used them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBOD-1)</sup>

At one point in time, Arlimahera traveled to the hinterlands of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) and built a massive fortress on the south shores of [Viridian Lake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Viridian_Lake) aptly named Arlimahera's Grip.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONArlimahera-5)</sup> Deep inside the ruins is a device called Arlimahera's Altar, a secret [teleportation](Lore_Teleportation.md) device supposedly created by the Queen herself as an escape in case the fort had fallen. It required two [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) rune stones.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONSkyward-6)</sup>
