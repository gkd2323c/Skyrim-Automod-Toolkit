# Star-Gazers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/ON-place-Star-Gazers%27_Observatory.jpg/200px-ON-place-Star-Gazers%27_Observatory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Star-Gazers%27_Observatory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Star-Gazers%27_Observatory.jpg) The Star-Gazers' Observatory The Star-Gazers, once known as the **Esteemed Order of the Observers of the Celestial Motions and Portents**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-basics1-1)</sup> are an order of scholars devoted to the study of the [stars](Lore_Astronomy.md) and the meaning of their motions. They are based in [Belkarth](Lore_Belkarth.md), a major settlement in the [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The order has been active since at least the early Second Era.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-basics1-1)</sup>
