*Alchemy description:* **Drain the target's Stamina by <mag> points.**
*Enchanting description:* **Does <mag> points of Stamina damage.**
**Damage Stamina** reduces the value of the target's [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md). Damage Stamina causes a total of <mag> points of damage, instantaneously. There are two additional variants of this effect, documented on their own effect pages: [Ravage Stamina](Skyrim_Ravage_Stamina.md) and [Lingering Damage Stamina](Skyrim_Lingering_Damage_Stamina.md). All three of these effects are available from poisons but only Damage Stamina is available as an enchantment.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Damage Stamina if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All weapons *[of Fatigue](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Fatigue)*
- All weapons *[of Weariness](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Weariness)*
- All weapons *[of Torpor](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Torpor)*
- All weapons *[of Debilitation](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Debilitation)*
- All weapons *[of Lethargy](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Lethargy)*
- All weapons *[of Exhaustion](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#of_Exhaustion)*
- [Froki's Bow](Skyrim_Froki%27s_Bow.md)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Bolar's Oathblade](Skyrim_Bolar%27s_Oathblade.md)
- [Mace of Molag Bal](Skyrim_Mace_of_Molag_Bal.md)
- [Skull Crusher](Skyrim_Skull_Crusher.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
- [The Rueful Axe](Skyrim_The_Rueful_Axe.md)

## Notes
- The name of the enchanting version of this effect is actually "Stamina Damage" instead of "Damage Stamina"
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Hunger](Skyrim_Hunger.md) levels will damage your maximum stamina.

## Bugs
- The magnitudes of all Stamina Damage enchantments (both custom and generic) are increased by the [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) perk, because the Stamina Damage effect is incorrectly tagged with the `Magic Damage Fire` keyword. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12439))
