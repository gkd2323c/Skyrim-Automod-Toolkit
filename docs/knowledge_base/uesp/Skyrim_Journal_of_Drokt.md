Journal of Drokt **by [Drokt](Skyrim_Drokt.md)**
---
I weren't never one for writing about my life. The [king-priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) of old deserve their stories told, but [Drokt](Skyrim_Drokt.md) is a simple man. So this isn't no journal and I'm not telling any stories here. But this infernal machine done worked its way into my brainspace and I won't leave till it's figured out.

The whole contraption just sets like a dead [horker](Skyrim_Horker.md) if it doesn't have the box. Putting the box in the hole made it all come to life.

Five rings, but only four buttons? Most of of *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* 'em don't work most of the time anyhows. When the lights line up, more open, but they don't seem to help. Just make another damned thing move.

The old fleabag [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) what sold me this cube said something about "the light through the knowledge through the machine rests on the cube." I remember because he made me say it back at him till I wanted to throttle him.

So I did.

Tried to leave, but [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) to the top and [them eyeless freaks](Skyrim_Falmer.md) outside the tower. Gotta stay here till I get it right.

And I will get it right.
