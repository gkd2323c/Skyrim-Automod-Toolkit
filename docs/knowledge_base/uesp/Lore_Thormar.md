Thormar is a rather insignificant ruin, but it is the gathering place for Baan Dar's Boast, a celebration between the [Wood Elves](Lore_Wood_Elves.md) and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), where both factions prank and pull practical jokes with each other for the chance to partake in [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md)'s [Five Fingers Dance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Five_Fingers_Dance).

## History
Thormar was constructed by the Ayleids, but like the rest of their settlements, it was abandoned as time went on. The land around it was claimed by both Wood Elves and Khajiit. A conflict ensued in the fields around Thormar between these two races. The Khajiit were led by Yenamu Frost-Fist, a powerful mage. Eventually, the Bosmer overwhelmed the Khajiit, leaving only Yenamu standing. She and the remaining warriors held of the Wood Elves for an hour straight until only Yenamu remained, and she was struck down by a stray arrow that pierced her eye.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Ever since [2E 557](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_557), the Wood Elves, and the Khajiit gather at Thormar to partake in Baan Dar's Boast, a celebration of the ideals of Baan Dar, the Bandit God of the provinces of Valenwood and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Baan Dar's Boast is an honest celebration with the sole purpose of proving oneself to Baan Dar and to join his [Five Fingers Dance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Five_Fingers_Dance).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BDB-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BDB2-5)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Baan Dar's Boast was held in Thormar for the twenty-fifth consecutive year. [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) happened onto Thormar during this time and was asked by Aniel (a disguised daughter of Baan Dar) to help with her pranks so they could be invited to a "party".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BDB-4)</sup> Upon assisting her, they were able to enter the Five Fingers Dance for further celebrations.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BDB2-5)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Thormar Keep was an active settlement, being under the rule of Count Karocher. It had a rivalry with [Karthdar Square](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Karthdar_Square).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Arenthia](Lore_Arenthia.md) in the northeast, [Ebon Ro](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ebon_Ro) in the west, and Stone Fell in the southwest.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Map Arena-6)</sup>
