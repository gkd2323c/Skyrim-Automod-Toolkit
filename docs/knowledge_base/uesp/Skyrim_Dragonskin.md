*Absorb 50% of the magicka from hostile spells for 60 seconds.* **Dragonskin** is a [greater power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Powers) possessed by all [Bretons](Skyrim_Breton.md). The power actually gives a 50% chance to fully block a hostile spell, and gaining magicka equal to the base cost of the spell, the same as any other method of spell absorption.

## Notes
- Without the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on, only a Breton can gain temporary 100% spell absorption without resorting to minor exploitation of how Standing Stone powers are implemented, specifically [the Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md).
- Dragonskin will **not** absorb magicka from spells deflected by wards.
