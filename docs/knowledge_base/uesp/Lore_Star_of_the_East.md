# Star of the East

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg/200px-ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Hunter_Plaque.jpg) The Thief, associated with the Stars of the North, and the cardinal direction of the East The Star of the East is a [star](Lore_Stars.md). It was incorrectly speculated by some that the star could be linked to either the [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) of [the Lord](Lore_The_Lord.md) or [the Lady](Lore_The_Lady.md), but neither of them were connected to the east or west during their respective seasons.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JODT-1)</sup>

One of the [Dwemeri](Lore_Dwemer.md) puzzles in [Nchuand-Zel](Lore_Nchuand-Zel.md) involved the constellations. Although Dionus Trutor linked [The Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md) to the north, because of its connection to the [Stars of the North](Lore_Stars_of_the_North.md), it was associated with the cardinal direction of the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JODT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AFOS-2)</sup>
