# Factions


## Joinable Factions
There are four primary factions that you can join.

- **[Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md)** — A guild of mercenaries, dedicated to combat, based in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). They are similar to the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) in previous games. - *[The Circle](Skyrim_The_Circle.md)* — The most trusted and accomplished members of the Companions.
- **[College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md)** — A guild dedicated to the study and practice of magic based in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). They are similar to the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) of previous games.
- **[Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)** — A secret guild dedicated to professional thievery, based in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). - *[Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md)* — A cabal of the Thieves Guild's top members that serves [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md) and protects her shrines in Skyrim.
- **[Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)** — A secret guild made up of professional assassins, the same as from previous games.

You may also take part in the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) by joining one of these two factions.

- **[Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)** — The military power of the empire, they seek to maintain Imperial control of Skyrim from their base in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
- **[Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md)** — The main organization of rebels in Skyrim's civil war, they seek Skyrim's independence from the Empire. They are based in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

You can join these factions, although they only have a short quest chain.

- **[Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md)** — A guild of musicians, vocalists and story tellers based in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). They have a single quest to join and a few side quests for members.
- **[Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>** — A [Dark Elven](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) cult that still worships the old gods of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md). You can choose to side with them while completing the related quest chain.

Two new factions become available with the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md).

- **[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md)** — An order of [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunters.
- **[Volkihar Vampire Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md)** — A clan of vampires trying to blot out the sun.

## Noteworthy NPC Factions
- **[Blackblood Marauders](Skyrim_Blackblood_Marauder.md)** — A group of bandits based out of [Broken Oar Grotto](Skyrim_Broken_Oar_Grotto.md).
- **[Blackwater Brigands](Skyrim_Blackwater_Brigands.md)** — A gang of wannabe pirates found at [Orphan's Tear](Skyrim_Orphan%27s_Tear.md).<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup>
- **[Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md)** — An order of warriors that once served the Emperor before being disbanded and nearly exterminated by the Thalmor.
- **[Blood Horkers](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md)** — A group of pirates based out of [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md).
- **[Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md)** — A former bandit crew from Cyrodiil.<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup>
- **[Cultists](Skyrim_Cultist.md)** — Worshippers of the fallen [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md), also known as the First Dragonborn.
- **[Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md)** — [Bretons](Skyrim_Breton.md) native to [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) who want to overthrow the region's Nord rulers.
- **[Frostmoon Pack](Skyrim_Werewolf.md#Frostmoon_Pack)** — A pack of [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md) based at [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md).
- **[Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md)** — A monastic order dedicated to studying and preserving the Way of the Voice.
- **[Morag Tong](Skyrim_Morag_Tong.md)** — A professional guild of [assassins](Skyrim_Morag_Tong_Assassin.md) lawfully sanctioned in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).
- **[Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md)** — An Imperial espionage organization responsible for protecting the Emperor; it rose to prominence after the disbandment of the Blades.
- **[Pirates](Skyrim_Pirate.md)** — Criminals who prowl the shores of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
- **[Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md)** — An ancient and mysterious monastic order from the Summerset Isles known to practice an arcane form of magic known as Mysticism.
- **[Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md)** — Werewolf hunters that oppose the Companions.
- **[Skaal](Skyrim_Skaal_Village_People.md)** — An offshoot tribe of [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) who inhabit [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).
- **[Summerset Shadows](Skyrim_Summerset_Shadows.md)** — A group of thieves based in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) that steal valuables from the corpses of the dead.
- **[Synod](Skyrim_Synod.md)** — One of two competing mage organizations in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) which formed when the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) splintered.
- **[Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md)** — The ruling faction of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), it seeks to establish elven supremacy over the race of men.
- **[Thirsk](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall_People.md)** — A group of former Skaal based out of [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md).
- **[Vigil of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md)** — A militant holy order of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) adherents founded after the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), its members are dedicated to hunting [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).

## Group Combat Reaction
The combat reaction value together with the [aggression](Skyrim_Aggression.md) and [assistance](Skyrim_Assistance.md) values of an NPC determine how members of a faction behave in combat<sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>. See the [CK Wiki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Faction) for more.
