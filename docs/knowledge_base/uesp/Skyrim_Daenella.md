[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-npc-Daenella.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Daenella.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daenella.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daenella.jpg) Daenella Daenella is a dead [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) who was a witch of the [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md). Her body is found at [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) during [Crypt of the Heart](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md), on the rock where normally the dead [Giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) lies.

She wears [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). A random selection of gold based on your level can be found on her body, and possibly a [gem](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Leveled_Gems) and/or an [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). Additionally, she knows the spell [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md).

## Notes
- Her body cannot be [reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md).

## Bugs
- Daenella's body may be found before the start of the related quest. A sacrificed [Giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) will be lying on top of her corpse.
