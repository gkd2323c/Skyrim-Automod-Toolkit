[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-item-Ring_of_the_Erudite.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_the_Erudite.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Erudite.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Erudite.jpg) Ring of the Erudite The Ring of the Erudite is a [ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring) retrieved as part of the [Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest. The ring is made of three bands of bone, with two green jewels set into it. Its enchantment gives you 100 more [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) and causes you to [regenerate magicka faster](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md). Unlike most other rings, its bonuses will still apply when you are in [Vampire Lord form](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).

## Related Quests

## Notes
- Unlike other magicka regeneration enchantments, this ring's enchantment is not a multiplier upon your magicka regeneration rate, but a flat value of 2.00 added to your base regeneration rate (default 3.00). This makes this ring function like a 2/3 bonus to your magicka regeneration rate when not coupled with other bonuses. This ring's effect combines multiplicatively when mixed with normal magicka regeneration enchantments as follows: - Normal magicka Regeneration (100%): (3.00) x 2 = 6.00
- Ring of the Erudite: (3.00 + 2.00) = 5.00
- Normal magicka Regeneration (100%) with Ring of the Erudite: (3.00 + 2.00) x 2 = 10.00
