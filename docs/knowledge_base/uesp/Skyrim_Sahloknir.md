[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-creature-Sahloknir.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Sahloknir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sahloknir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sahloknir.jpg) Sahloknir **Sahloknir** is a leveled [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) brought back from the grave by [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) east of [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md). Depending on what type of dragon he is, he can breathe either [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md) or [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md).

Sah Lok Nir translates to "Phantom Sky Hunt".

## Related Quests
- **[A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md)**: Prove you are Dragonborn to [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md) by slaying the dragon Sahloknir.

## Dialogue
When you fight him during the quest [A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md) he will use the following lines in combat:

- *"I am Sahloknir! Hear my Voice and despair!"*

- *"Thurri * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *du hin sille ko Sovngarde!"*

- *"Your Voice is strong ... for a mortal."*

- *"Dohaviin * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, your Voice is no match for mine!"*

- *"My lord Alduin requires your death. I am glad to oblige him."*

- *"My Voice has been silent for too long!"*

- *"I see that mortals have become arrogant while I slept."*

- *"It's to be a real fight, then. Good!"* (if health is low)

- *"Hiding will not save you!"* (if you are trying to hide)

If spoken to, he uses the same line of dialogue as other named dragons who have yet to be resurrected from a burial mound by [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md): *"Speak, if you have aught to say worth the listening."*

## Notes
- He is unaffected by the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) shout and cannot be [ridden](Skyrim_Dragon_Riding.md).
