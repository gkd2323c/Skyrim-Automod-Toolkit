[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/SR-npc-Arniel%27s_Shade.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arniel%27s_Shade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arniel%27s_Shade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arniel%27s_Shade.jpg) Arniel's Shade **Arniel's Shade**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [sorcerer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Sorcerer), is the [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of [Arniel Gane](Skyrim_Arniel_Gane.md) created when you use the spell [Summon Arniel's Shade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arniel%27s_Shade). He has the same dialogue as reanimated [zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md). Arniel favors [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic, and may cast the following spells in combat: [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Banish Daedra](Skyrim_Banish_Daedra.md), and [Heal Other](Skyrim_Heal_Other.md).

He wears [adept robes of Alteration](Skyrim_Adept_Robes_of_Alteration.md) along with an [adept hood](Skyrim_Adept_Hood.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c).

Arniel's Shade has some high-level fixed skill levels. He has 100 in Destruction and [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md), 97 in [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) and [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md), and 66 in [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md). His skill in One-handed and Heavy Armor are pointless, as he never equips a weapon or any armor. His magical skills differ significantly from when he was alive. Notably, his skill in [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) has fallen from 84 to 25, and his Restoration has fallen from 84 to 66. He has positively gained in Destruction and Illusion (going from 15 and 20 respectively, to 100).

He has the exact same schedule as his living counterpart (sleep from 4am to noon, visit [Phinis Gestor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Phinis_Gestor) for three hours from 8pm, and idle in his room), which he may follow if you summon him within the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) grounds, but will be ignored outside the college.
