*That's a shame about Odar, but his dynasty must endure.*
—Cassius the Elder, after Odar's assasination Odar the Brave was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) king of unspecified origin, who was renowned for defeating an army of [Ogres](Lore_Ogre.md), led by an Ogre king,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Castles Rulings-1)</sup> who plagued his land before being raised to kingship.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles Event-2)</sup> Other great feats were ascribed to him, such as supposedly killing a monster with a spoon.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/CT-scene-Ogres.png/200px-CT-scene-Ogres.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-scene-Ogres.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-scene-Ogres.png) Odar defeating the Ogres Odar the Brave was a Nordic adventurer who courageously defeated the army of an Ogre king that plagued his land.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles Event-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Castles Rulings-1)</sup> For this momentous act, Odar was made the ruler of a small kingdom by the current [Emperor](Lore_Emperor.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and founded his own dynasty, leaving adventuring behind.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles Event-2)</sup> His kingdom neighbored the [Bloodfall Kingdom](Lore_Bloodfall.md) in Cyrodiil, as well as several [goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) settlements and [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Castles Rulings-1)</sup>

After serving as king for a time, at the age of 50, Odar was eventually killed by an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) assassin, his successive heir was selected from his [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) warrior spouse, Queen [Ysabel](Lore_Ysabel.md) of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md), his [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) mistress, Alassi, his direct heir, Jofarr, or the heroic [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) champion, Rahim al-Elinhir, who was Jofarr's cousin and Odar had mentored. It is ultimately uncertain who was selected to carry on Odar's dynasty.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Castles Event-2)</sup>

Odar was remembered by his kingdom's subjects for years to come. Full-size [steel](Lore_Steel.md) replicas of his armor would be created as decorations for the castle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Some would even sing songs about Odar and Ysabel on stage,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> while children would sing nursery rhymes about "Old King Odar".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
