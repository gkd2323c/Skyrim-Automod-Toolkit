Topal the Pilot (also known as **Topal the Explorer**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-At UQ-1)</sup> and **Torval the Pilot**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Opsius-2)</sup> was an [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorer and poet. He was the first known Aldmer to explore Tamriel, charting Tamriel's sea lanes in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) on his ship the *Niben*, after which the [Niben River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Niben_River) was named.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTBlack Marsh-3)</sup> Topal sailed into [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) (which was subsequently named after him) and up through the Niben River until he was deep in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Here he taught the native [Bird Men](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bird_Men) how to read and write in exchange for the [Eight Islands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eight_Islands), the future site of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fot N-4)</sup>

He made wide-ranging voyages, penetrating even into hostile environments such as Black Marsh, where he made some of the first recorded observations of Argonians: "manlike reptiles, fleet of foot and running the length of this great mire".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTBlack Marsh-3)</sup> The *[Udhendra Nibenu](Lore_Udhendra_Nibenu.md)* ([Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris_(language).md) for *"Father of the Niben"*) is an epic describing Topal's voyages, but only fragments of the poem have survived.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fot N-4)</sup> He supposedly also kept a record of his voyages named *The Southern Coast as Far as the Eastern Sea*, which were later kept in the Library of Dusk in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LOD-5)</sup> By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) he was recognized as the [patron saint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Saints) of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md), where [a statue of him](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Statues_Notable_Figures#Topal_the_Pilot) could be found in the southwest corner of town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Opsius-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TESIVOGGTopal-6)</sup><sup>:363</sup>

## Notes
- Topal is the namesake of [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fot N-4)</sup> and therefore also [Topal Island](Lore_Topal_Island.md) and [Grand Topal Hideaway](Lore_Grand_Topal_Hideaway.md). - As Topal is also known as Torval,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Opsius-2)</sup> it is possible that the [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) city of [Torval](Lore_Torval.md) is named after him. Indeed, Topal once documented so-called "cat demons" in his journal, which are speculated to be the ancient ancestors of the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), the modern-day inhabitants of Elsweyr.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fot N-4)</sup>
