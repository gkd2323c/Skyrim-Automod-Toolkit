[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-npc-Jordis_the_Sword-Maiden.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jordis_the_Sword-Maiden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jordis_the_Sword-Maiden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jordis_the_Sword-Maiden.jpg) Jordis inside Proudspire Manor **Jordis the Sword-Maiden**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is a [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) assigned to you upon becoming a [thane](Skyrim_Thane_of_Haafingar.md) of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Jordis is eligible to become a member of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), and is also a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

Jordis is clad in steel quality equipment; she wears a set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), a pair of [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets) and a pair of [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots). She uses a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) to attack her enemies and blocks with a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield).

## Bugs
- When you become a thane of Solitude, she may still not spawn, but using the console command `player.moveto` will place you next to the bench in the living room area. This indicates that she does exist but is not displayed, nor can she be interacted with. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed by entering the console and typing `prid A2C95` then `disable` and `enable`. She will then appear next to you.
