[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/MW-place-Pelagiad.jpg/200px-MW-place-Pelagiad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Pelagiad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Pelagiad.jpg) Pelagiad circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Pelagiad was a small settlement in the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) region of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a Pelagiad Garrison was maintained by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> although no town or fort existed by that name in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) at the time.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> The opulent Amaya Lake Lodge was built upon the shores of Lake Amaya during an earlier era, near the future site of Pelagiad.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-eso-3)</sup> After Vvardenfell was opened by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) for settlement in [3E 414](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_414), Fort Pelagiad was built by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) to guard the roads between [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) and [Seyda Neen](Lore_Seyda_Neen.md). Legion veterans began to build farms around the fort, and several shops were set up as the village grew. The town was officially chartered, and noted for its Western architecture resembling that of a [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) village.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the settlement boasted a tavern, tradehouse, blacksmith and [Imperial Cult](Lore_Imperial_Cult.md) services.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Following the destruction caused during the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TR-6)</sup> Pelagiad's fate is uncertain.
