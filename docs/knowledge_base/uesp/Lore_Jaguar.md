Jaguars are a rare type of large, predatory [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in jungled regions in southern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Carn-1)</sup><sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> They are known for their distinctive rosette fur patterns.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONJaguar Rosettes-3)</sup>

Many of the cats known as jaguars in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Jaguar**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONSenche Jaguar-4)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup>

Many [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have jaguar-like fur patterns,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONJaguar Rosettes-3)</sup> with Khajiit of the [Cathay-raht](Lore_Cathay-raht.md) furstock being nicknamed as "jaguar-men" by the [Imperials](Lore_Imperials.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1E-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Survival G-7)</sup> though many do not actually resemble jaguars.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Survival G-7)</sup> The ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of Naj-Caldeesh were said to learned how to hunt from "jaguar-kin", leading to speculation that they must have hunted alongside a Khajiit furstock.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
Jaguars have been trained for use as stately and suave mounts, known for being silent as the shadows. These mount are sometimes outfitted for use in [Carnaval](Lore_Carnaval.md) celebrations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Carn-1)</sup> Compacts depicting running jaguars chasing people have seen use in Elsweyr.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

One summoner had attempted to compel a [Flame Atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) into the shape a fully grown senche-jaguar, however the result was it taking the shape of a kitten, which the summoner nonetheless found adorable and grew attached to.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONSenche Jaguar-4)</sup>
