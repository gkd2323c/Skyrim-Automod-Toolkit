[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-place-Dragon_Mound_Yorgrim_Resurrection.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dragon_Mound_Yorgrim_Resurrection.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragon_Mound_Yorgrim_Resurrection.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragon_Mound_Yorgrim_Resurrection.jpg) Viinturuth's Dragon Mound Viinturuth was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Shine-Hammer-Rage" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Alduin-2)</sup>

He was recorded as a deceased dragon by the chronicler of the Dragonguard, [Brother Mathnan](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Brother_Mathnan), in the *[Atlas of Dragons](Lore_Atlas_of_Dragons.md)* in [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373). According to the documents recovered from [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) temples he died during the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md) and was buried near the [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AOD-3)</sup>

Upon his return in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) began the process of resurrecting the many dead dragons buried throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) by the Dragon Cult in ancient times. Viinturuth was among the dragons to be brought back to life, where his resurrection was witnessed by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md). Upon the resurrection Viinturuth pledged allegiance to Alduin and fought against the Last Dragonborn, but was defeated and killed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Alduin-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Viinturuth-4)</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Viinturuth was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAD-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Arngeir-6)</sup>
