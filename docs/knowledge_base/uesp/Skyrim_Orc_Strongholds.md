## Blood-Kin
Only [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) characters have immediate access to the strongholds unchallenged. Characters of any other race must first become [Blood-Kin of the Orcs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood-Kin_of_the_Orcs) before being welcome in the strongholds. You can enter a stronghold without being Blood-Kin, but the residents will continuously tell you that you are not welcome. If the stronghold includes [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md), [trainers](Skyrim_Trainers.md), or potential [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md), their services will be unavailable until you comply.

Several quests fulfill this requirement. Members of any stronghold you approach may give you the quest [The Forgemaster's Fingers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Forgemaster%27s_Fingers) if you have not become Blood-Kin already. Alternatively, completing any quest listed below will make you Blood-Kin. Upon first completing one of these quests, the grateful Orc will say, *"For your help, I'm sending word to the Orc strongholds that you're to be trusted. You should be welcome there now."*, signifying that you are now Blood-Kin. In addition, each associated Orc's [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you will increase by 1 upon completing the quests below, with two possible exceptions.

## Notes
- If you start "[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)" as an Orc, [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) will say: *"You from one of the strongholds, Orc? How did you end up here?"*
- If you become Blood-Kin and later bring your [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) to the Orc strongholds, the residents will treat your follower as an extra ally as long as you and your follower are together.
- Another Orc who would've made you Blood-Kin, named [Makhel Abbas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Makhel_Abbas), was meant to play a role in a Civil War quest, but this quest didn't make it into the final game.

## Bugs
- [Atub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atub) and [Garakh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garakh) at [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) were apparently intended to be merchants (apothecary and blacksmith respectively), but do not function as merchants themselves. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. They were assigned the wrong settings. Once fixed, they will be affected by [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md), but not [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md).
- Even after becoming Blood-Kin to the Orcs, you may still be warned about trespassing in the longhouses at the Orc strongholds. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.5, fixes this bug.
