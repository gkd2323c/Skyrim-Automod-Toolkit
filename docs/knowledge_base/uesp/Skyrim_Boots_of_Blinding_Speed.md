[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-item-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed.jpg/200px-SR-item-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed.jpg) The Boots of Blinding Speed [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-misc-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed_effect_with_Night_Eye.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed_effect_with_Night_Eye.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed_effect_with_Night_Eye.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Boots_of_Blinding_Speed_effect_with_Night_Eye.jpg) Blinding effect of the Boots with [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md) The Boots of Blinding Speed are a unique set of [Netch leather](Skyrim_Netch_Leather.md) boots of unknown origin. They can be obtained during a miscellaneous quest that is received by reading the [Crafting with Netch Leather](Skyrim_Crafting_with_Netch_Leather.md) book, acquired through the quest [More Than You Can Chew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:More_Than_You_Can_Chew).

## Related Quests
- **[More Than You Can Chew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:More_Than_You_Can_Chew)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md)</sup>: Obtain the secrets of crafting with [netch leather](Skyrim_Netch_Leather.md) from a tribe of [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md).

## Notes
- The blinding effect can be somewhat negated through the use of [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md), [Vision of the Tenth Eye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vision_of_the_Tenth_Eye), and [Orum's Aquatic Escape](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orum%27s_Aquatic_Escape). The most effective way to negate the blinding effect is to use the [Vampire's Sight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Sight) [lesser power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Power) combined with Vision of the Tenth Eye. Some spells like [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md) can briefly negate the effect on impact.
- The Boots of Blinding Speed have previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Boots_of_Blinding_Speed) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*. For more information, see the [lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boots_of_Blinding_Speed).
