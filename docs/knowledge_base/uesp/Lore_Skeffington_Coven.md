# Skeffington Coven

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/DF-place-Skeffington_Coven.png/200px-DF-place-Skeffington_Coven.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Skeffington_Coven.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Skeffington_Coven.png) The Skeffington Coven in 3E 405. The Skeffington Coven is a notable group of [witches](Lore_Witches.md) that live in the Barony of [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md), within the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The Skeffington Coven has been active as far as the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), all the way into [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417). The Skeffington operates at **Old Barbyn's Farm**, deep in the wilderness of Phrygias, somewhere near the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920HF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFSkeffington-2)</sup>

There is an area known as the *Skeffington Wood* around Camlorn and the [Ebon Wastes](Lore_Ebon_Wastes.md) which is presumably linked to the Coven.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap West Tamriel-3)</sup>

## History
In [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920), the Skeffington Coven had taken in a woman named [Turala](Lore_Turala.md), who was once the lover of the Duke of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), [Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Brindisi_Dorom.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920HF-1)</sup> However, after a trip to [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), the Skeffington Coven was killed in a massacre caused by an [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) raiding party, supposedly sent by Brindisi Dorom himself. Enraged, Turala had called forth [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) to burn Mournhold into the ground, and so he did.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-2920V10-4)</sup>

Whatever happened to the remaining witches is unknown, but by [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Skeffington Coven was active, working out of Old Barbyn's Farm.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFSkeffington-2)</sup> In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), an Agent of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), known as the "*Graylady*" was undercover in the Skeffington Coven when the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), on their way to the city of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md), the group was interrupted by a three-way clash between [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), Wayrest, and [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), which left an inferno as far as [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md) and [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon_(fiefdom).md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The coven acknowledged Kynareth. Members of the coven also acknowledged three air spirits: Amaro, Pina, and Tallatha, who were believed to be "[the fingers of Kynareth](Lore_The_Fingers_of_Kynareth.md)".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920HF-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Skeffington Coven are one of many [Witch Covens in High Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Witch_Covens#High_Rock) featured in *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)*, though it is unknown if they are considered a [Wyrd Coven](Lore_Wyrd_Covens.md) in the modern context.
