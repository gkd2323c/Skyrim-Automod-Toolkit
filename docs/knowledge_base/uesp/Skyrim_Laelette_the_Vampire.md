She is the wife of [Thonnir](Skyrim_Thonnir.md) and mother of [Virkmund](Skyrim_Virkmund.md). She is said to have left her family to go join the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). Her son is determined to wait for her return, as it's all he talks about.

## Related Quests
- **[Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md)**: Investigate a tragic house fire, and discover an insidious plot against the town.

## Quest-Related Events
After [Jarl Idgrod](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) sends you to inspect the burned down house during [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md), you'll find [Helgi's Ghost](Skyrim_Helgi%27s_Ghost.md) who's willing to help you if you play hide and seek with her and "the other one". When you inquire further on this person, she'll only reveal: *"I can't tell you. She might hear me. She's so close."*

When you enter the small graveyard and approach Helgi's coffin, you may hear Helgi's voice: *"Make Laelette go away!"*

Laelette will come running up to you and immediately attack. Her stats and inventory are taken from the [standard vampire stats](Skyrim_Vampire.md#Vampire_Types).

When you discover [Alva's Journal](Skyrim_Alva%27s_Journal.md), you'll learn that Alva turned Laelette into a vampire and kept her as her handmaiden. It was Alva that spread the rumor that Laelette had left to join the Stormcloaks, to cover for her disappearance. She told Laelette to kill [Hroggar](Skyrim_Hroggar.md)'s family and make it look like an accident. To Alva's dismay, Laelette set fire to Hroggar's house with the family still alive inside. Laelette, remorsefully, tried to turn Helgi into a vampire but was too late, as the girl was severely injured and soon died. The journal states Laelette became obsessed with the girl after the incident, thinking she could be brought back as a companion.

## Notes
- During the quest, it's possible to encounter Laelette non-hostile up the slope from the graveyard near the rocks, invisible and unable to be harmed by melee attacks. She will turn hostile when using spells and may turn visible, and can be killed. This does not change the course of the quest. - She can even be visible and invulnerable to any kind of damage, although she can turn hostile if targeted by magic or arrows, at least in the Special Edition.
- Her Ref ID's difficulty modifier is set to "Easy", so for her to be, for example, level 48, you need to be at least three times that, level 144.
