[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/RG-place-Saintsport.jpg/200px-RG-place-Saintsport.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Saintsport.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Saintsport.jpg) Saintsport circa [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/ON-place-Saintsport.jpg/200px-ON-place-Saintsport.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Saintsport.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Saintsport.jpg) Saintsport circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Saintsport is a small settlement located on the southern tip of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md). It contains a port, a lighthouse, and the Square of Leki, a large shrine dedicated to [Leki](Lore_Leki.md). The town was named in honor of Leki, Saint of the Spirit Sword.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the town was captured by the Sea Drakes, a band of pirates led by Captain Helane. Their presence on the island contested the rule of Headman Bhosek and his Bloody Fist thugs in [Port Hunding](Lore_Port_Hunding.md). The pirates were eventually driven off after a rescue mission was mounted to save the kidnapped crew of the *Maiden's Breath*.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

By [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), Saintsport had been reduced to a ghost town. The lighthouse was used by the [Restless League](Lore_Restless_League.md) to signal when it was safe to come ashore. It was here that [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) first made contact with the League.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> He also discovered the location of the [Flask of Lillandril](Lore_Flask_of_Lillandril.md) buried in the sand west of Saintsport. The map he pieced together began its path at the shrine in the center of town.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
