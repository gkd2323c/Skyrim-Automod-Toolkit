Its ruins can be found at the end of the [Blue Road](Lore_Blue_Road.md), west of the city of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md).

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the entrance to Belda was sealed. The necromancer Goren Veer took up residence at the ruins and began summoning Daedra, while various bloodfiends also inhabited the ruins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Belda-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GV-2)</sup> A [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) scout camped out nearby noted that there were strange sounds coming from the ruins.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BRSN-3)</sup> The nearby military strongholds of Arrius Keep and [Blue Road Keep](Lore_Blue_Road_Keep.md) were located just to the north and south of the ruins respectively, and were the site of much contention during the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Belda-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BRK-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AK-5)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the ruins had been opened, but its subterranean halls were once again occupied by conjurers and Daedra.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OB-6)</sup>
