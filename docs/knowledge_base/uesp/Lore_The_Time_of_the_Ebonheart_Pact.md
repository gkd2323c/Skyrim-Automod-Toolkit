The Time of the Ebonheart Pact **by [Alla Llaleth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Alla_Llaleth)** *An essay on the downfall of mankind and a rallying call to take Cyrodiil*
---
The Wheel of Time turns: in [the dawn](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), it was the day of the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md), and then came the time of [Men](Lore_Man.md) —but now, as the decay and chaos in the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) makes clear, the time of Men has passed. Of the Men of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), only the Eastern [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) had the strength of character to join [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) and [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) in repelling the recent invasion of [Snake-Men](Lore_Akaviri.md) from [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md), thereby showing that of all Men, only they may be trusted.

Time and again Men have shown that without proper guidance, their meddling with [External Powers](Lore_Gods.md) leads to disaster. Men's reckless dabbling with beings beyond [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) must stop forever. Now it is the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), which shall and must become the Tamriel Pact. Within the Pact, the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md), [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), and [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) are all revered … from an appropriate distance. Within the Pact is the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), three Living Gods who abide among us here on Nirn and whose interest therefore coincides with that of all residents of Nirn. Only they have shown how to treat successfully with powers beyond Nirn.

The rash actions of those who sit on the [Ruby Throne](Lore_Ruby_Throne.md), or who pull their strings, have brought Tamriel to the verge of irretrievable doom. They must be scoured from the face of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and the decaying remains of the Empire of Men must be swept away. It shall be replaced by the Pact, which will enforce peace across the continent and strictly regulate all involvement in dangerous magical pursuits.

Onward, warriors of the Pact! We will never know peace and freedom until we occupy [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md)!
