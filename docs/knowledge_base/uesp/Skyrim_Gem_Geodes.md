Geodes are smaller than typical ore deposits. They will be exhausted in just 3 strikes of your pickaxe instead of the usual 9. They will always produce one gem of the given type, with a 10% chance of an additional "flawless" gem of the same type.

### Amethyst Geode
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-item-Amethyst_Geode.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amethyst_Geode.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Geode.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amethyst_Geode.jpg) Amethyst Geode There is only 1 Amethyst Geode that can be found in Solstheim. When mined you will get an [Amethyst](Skyrim_Amethyst.md) and sometimes a [Flawless Amethyst](Skyrim_Flawless_Amethyst.md).
