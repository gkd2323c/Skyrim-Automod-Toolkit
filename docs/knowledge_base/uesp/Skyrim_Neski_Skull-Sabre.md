She carries the [Bittercup](Skyrim_Bittercup.md), the [Grand Champion's Sword](Skyrim_Grand_Champion%27s_Sword.md), and the [Grand Champion's Helm](Skyrim_Grand_Champion%27s_Helm.md). She also carries the note *[On the Bittercup](Skyrim_On_the_Bittercup.md)*.

## Notes
- Neski can be the target of The Companions random quest Hired Muscle. It's then impossible to finish this task, because Neski only appears during The Pit quest when you choose the Path of Power option.
