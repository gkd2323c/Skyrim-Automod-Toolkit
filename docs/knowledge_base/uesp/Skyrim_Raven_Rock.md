[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-place-Raven_Rock.jpg/200px-SR-place-Raven_Rock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Raven_Rock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Raven_Rock.jpg) Raven Rock **Raven Rock** is a [city](Skyrim_Dragonborn_Places.md#Cities) on the southwest coast of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). It is the first place discovered in the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion. Once an [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company) mining outpost, it is now a [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) colony. A far cry from the prosperous mining town of the past, Raven Rock has become a mostly forgotten settlement, its ebony mine depleted.

The city consists of a single thoroughfare running from the entrance through [the Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md) in the east to the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) in the west, following the line of the small adjacent bay. The jail and the Redoran guards' garrison are built into the Bulwark, which is the city's main defense against the ash from the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md).

A curved wooden boardwalk runs along the bay, with a short pier near the center and a longer pier to the east. The [Northern Maiden](Skyrim_Northern_Maiden.md), which plies its trade between Solstheim and the city of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), is docked at the long pier, and three rowboats are docked elsewhere in the bay. The various shops and services of Raven Rock can be found in the central marketplace around the city's well north of the bay, while the [Temple](Skyrim_Temple.md) is near the Bulwark to the east. There are many freely available crafting stations outside in the city, including three [wood chopping blocks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), two [tanning racks](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), two [smelters](Skyrim_Smelter.md), a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md), a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), and an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). These stations are outside [Alor House](Skyrim_Alor_House.md), [Glover Mallory's House](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md), [Ienth Farm](Skyrim_Ienth_Farm.md), and [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md), as well as on the long pier.

It is possible to acquire a home here, [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md), after completing the quest [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md).

For historical information on Raven Rock and House Redoran, see the respective lore articles on [Raven Rock](Lore_Raven_Rock.md) and [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 4.3.1, fixes this bug.
