**Flaming Familiar** is an apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a Flaming Thrall which will rush towards your enemies and explode. The Flaming Thrall appears as a fiery wolf, and uses melee attacks for the brief period before it explodes.

## Effects
- [Conjure Flaming Familiar](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 6 secs

**Explosion has the following effect:**

- [Fire Damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 50 pts in 15 ft for 3 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](Skyrim_Summoner.md): increase range to 200 ft at first rank or 300 ft at second rank.
- [Conjuration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Conjuration_Dual_Casting.md): multiply spell cost by 2.8 in exchange for increasing the duration to 13.2 seconds.
- [Twin Souls](Skyrim_Twin_Souls.md): two flaming familiars can be summoned at one time.

## Notes
- Dual-casting this spell increases its duration but not its damage or area.
- The familiar will explode upon death, or when the duration of the spell expires. Conjuring another creature (without the Twin Souls perk) will dispel the first. This causes it to play a short death animation. It will not explode during this animation, however if the spell expires while the body is still ragdolled, the explosion will occur.
- Despite being on fire, the familiar is not immune to fire damage. If one familiar explodes and another is in the radius, it will kill the other and trigger its explosion.
- The explosion does damage to friends as well as foes, including the caster, so try to be out of range when it blows. If no foes are close enough, it will seek out the caster, much like any summoned creature or raised undead. Because of its short timer, it will tend to explode just before or right when it reaches you.
- Data in the Creation Kit indicates that this spell was originally intended to be a flaming [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). In fact, unlike the actual [familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md), the flaming familiar has neither the keyword to make it a familiar nor the keyword to make it a daedra. - This also means it spawns with an [Ancient Nord Sword](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md) or an [Ancient Nord War Axe](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_War_Axe.md); it can equip this, as it has a right hand, and it may choose to do so, as it has 15 ranks in [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) and uses the Draugr combat style, not the Familiar combat style.
- Because it is not considered either a daedra or a familiar, it cannot be turned against you by enemy Conjurers or Dragon Priests.
