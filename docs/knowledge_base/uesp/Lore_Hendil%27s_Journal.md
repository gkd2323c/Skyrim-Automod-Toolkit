Hendil's Journal **by [Hendil](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Hendil)** *The journal of a desperate father*
---
My son's gotten worse. I'm finding it harder to keep him sedated. His [illness](Lore_Vampirism.md) is insatiable. And those things! I have no idea what to do about the ones he's infected.

The [aloe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salloweed) is my only chance. I'm the first to uncover its true properties as a powerful topical sedative. If these were more peaceful times, I would have submitted my research to the College. I could have won the esteem of my peers.

Now I live all these waking hours in fear of those around me. At any moment they might uncover the truth about Tancano. What that could mean—
