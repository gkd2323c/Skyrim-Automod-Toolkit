Welwa are great four-eyed pack hunting beasts, with thick pelts, tails, and savage teeth. They often serve as war mounts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

According to tapestries in the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md), the early [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) settlers of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) encountered welwa, depicted as strange, holy ravagers, and saviors of the land.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup> Led by [Torinaan](Lore_Torinaan.md), the Aldmer of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) drove them back using ancestral magics,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and eventually the welwa of Summerset were driven to extinction.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup> In [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) they were considered myths, and described in their ancient texts as having "the jaws of a bear, and the charging strength of a boar".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AC-6)</sup>

Welwa continued to inhabit the wilds of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) on mainland [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), as well as parts of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) that border the region. They are often domesticated and armored by the [Iron Orcs](Lore_Iron_Orcs.md) of the region, who use them as war beasts. History indicates that every pastoral society in Craglorn's long history: Nedes, Yokudans, Goblins, and Orcs has domesticated them at some point.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> They also have been found in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md)

In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the [Sapiarchs](Lore_Sapiarchs.md) of the Crystal Tower reintroduced welwa to [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) by bringing in mated pairs from Craglorn. These welwa quickly spread across the wilderness of Summerset, although many inhabitants of the Isle were displeased with their reintroduction.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup>

Welwa can be transformed into reptilian forms through [Naga](Lore_Naga.md) [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) rituals or exposure to their special [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) sap. The green-skinned Nagahide Welwa is thought to have been created by the blue Hist of [Dead-Water Village](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dead-Water_Village).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> They were utilized by cultists of the [Order of the Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TESOMWFT-9)</sup> The Sul-Xan Fleshripper is a fiery breed imbued by [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s chaos through [Sul-Xan](Lore_Sul-Xan.md) ritual.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Other reptilian breeds include the Duskfall Welwa, Speckled Welwa, and the Azuredoom Wrathsteed.

[Namira](Lore_Namira.md) has also been known to alter welwa for her own needs. The so-called [Voidmother](Lore_Voidmother.md)'s Welwa is a dark breed infused with [Void](Lore_Void.md) energy, given as a reward to those who serve her.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Notes
- The welwa can trace its origins back to [Loranna's RP](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loranna%27s_RP) and user Allerleirauh, being introduced in the [*Twisted Tower*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loranna%27s_RP#Campaign_4:_Twisted_Tower) campaign as a nod to real world Romanian [folklore](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Fairy_Aurora).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Twisted Tower-12)</sup> Welwas were later canonized in the [Summerset section](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_3rd_Edition_Summerset) of *[A Pocket Guide to the Empire: Third Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire:_3rd_Edition)*.
