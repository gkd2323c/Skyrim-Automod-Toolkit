# Mudcrab

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-creature-Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mudcrab.jpg) A Mudcrab **Mudcrabs** are small hostile creatures primarily found near water. Their corpses may be looted for [Mudcrab Chitin](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Chitin.md), [Mudcrab Legs](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Legs.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, and [Crab Meat](Skyrim_Crab_Meat.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>. Skyrim mudcrabs are hardier than their Cyrodilic cousins, having nearly triple their health. Furthermore, they are larger, and capable of camouflaging themselves as small rocks, resembling their Vvardenfell cousins. They are poor swimmers, which results in their being swept down swift rivers, and prevents them from properly attacking and defending themselves against [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), their natural enemies.

[Juvenile Mudcrabs](Skyrim_Juvenile_Mudcrab.md) can sometimes be caught while [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Solstheim_Mudcrab.jpg) The unused [Solstheim Mudcrab](Skyrim_Solstheim_Mudcrab.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> - Arrows shot at mudcrabs will bounce back toward you, just like they do when fired at [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) and [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).
- [Solstheim Mudcrabs](Skyrim_Solstheim_Mudcrab.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> are a unique ash-covered variety of mudcrab. They don't appear in-game under normal circumstances; you can find a single one in [Editor Smoke Test Cell](Skyrim_Test_Cells.md#Editor_Smoke_Test_Cell).
- A giant mudcrab shell lies in a pool of water southeast of [Gjukar's Monument](Skyrim_Gjukar%27s_Monument.md), which is also the location of the [Mudcrab Guardian Spirit](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit.md) during [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md).
- The [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a unique [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md), which can be purchased as a pet from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md).

## Bugs
- Mudcrab legs<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> have a duplication bug. The model of the single item in your inventory is displayed as a pair of legs, and if you drop a single instance of the item in your inventory, the legs will be dropped as two separated legs instead of a single paired item. If one leg is picked up, you will receive another pair of mudcrab legs that can be dropped again to become two legs. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Sometimes, mudcrab bodies will sink below the surface of the ground after you've killed them, with only their pincers stuck above ground. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Two mudcrab spawn points (one east of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) and one near [Bleakwind Bluff](Skyrim_Bleakwind_Bluff.md)) are unnaturally too far away from any water source, which obviously doesn't match their natural habitat. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
