Esbern is one of the few surviving [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) and one of the last known loremasters of the order after it was reformed during [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md)'s return.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>

Esbern was one of the experts in the dragonlore of the Blades. A high-priority target of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) during the Great War, for both operational and political reasons, he was directly involved in several of the most damaging operations carried out by the Blades within the Dominion, including the [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md) Incident and the breach of the [Blue River Prison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blue_River_Prison).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDD-2)</sup> He went into hiding in [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), evading the Thalmor for over decades.

When the dragon [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) returned in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Delphine](Lore_Delphine.md) enlisted the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) to retrieve Esbern from the Ratway of Riften before the Thalmor located him. Together they reoccupied [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md), reforming the order of the Blades to their original purpose as dragonslayers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>
