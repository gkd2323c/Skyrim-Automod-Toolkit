[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-creature-Petra.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Petra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Petra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Petra.jpg) Petra **Petra** is a hostile [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) living in [Blind Cliff Bastion](Skyrim_Blind_Cliff_Bastion.md). She stole the tower from another hagraven, [Melka](Skyrim_Melka.md), who she has imprisoned in a cage. Melka will ask you to help kill her in return for a [unique staff](Skyrim_Eye_of_Melka.md).
