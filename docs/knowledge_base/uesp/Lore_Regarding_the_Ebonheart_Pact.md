Regarding the Ebonheart Pact **by [Aicantar of Shimmerene](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Aicantar_of_Shimmerene), Sapiarch of Indoctrination** *Condemnation against the Dunmer and their allegiance to the Ebonheart Pact*
---
Our cousins the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) have been duly punished for the betrayal of their ancestry: because they have embraced, and then attempted to emulate, the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), a curse has disfigured their bodies and blasted [their land](Lore_Morrowind.md). That lesson was apparently insufficient, for they have now left the boundaries of their cursed domain, and seek to infect the rest of [the mainland](Lore_Tamriel.md) with their heresies. The [trio of wily demonspawn](Lore_Tribunal.md) who rule them have duped the simple [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and the slave-lizard [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) into joining this mad escapade.

To allow the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) to rule in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) would be just as foolish as to leave the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) to one or another of the tribes of [Men](Lore_Man.md). The Tribunal is ultimately an even greater danger to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) than the heedless and impetuous human nations. They must be driven back into their haunted corner of the continent with their power broken … forever.
