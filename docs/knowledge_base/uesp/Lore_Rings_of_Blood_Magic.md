# Rings of Blood Magic

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-quest-Rings_of_Blood_Magic.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Rings_of_Blood_Magic.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Rings_of_Blood_Magic.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Rings_of_Blood_Magic.jpg) The Rings of Blood Magic The Rings of Blood Magic are a set of two ancient [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) rings which enhance the wearer's [Blood Magic](Lore_Blood_Magic.md) and [vampiric](Lore_Vampirism.md) powers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSadri-1)</sup> The **Ring of the Erudite** fortifies the wearer's magicka,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRErudite-2)</sup> while the **Ring of the Beast** fortifies the wearer's health and increases the power of a [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md)'s claws.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRBeast-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LGAsia-4)</sup> Both rings are triple-banded with two gemstone insets; the Ring of the Erudite is brassy and possesses green gems, while the Ring of the Beast is golden with red gems.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRErudite-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRBeast-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LGAsia-4)</sup>

According to myth, [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) himself gave these rings to ancient and powerful Vampire Lords.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSadri-1)</sup>

## History
Lord [Harkon](Lore_Harkon.md) of the [Volkihar](Lore_Volkihar.md) clan of vampires is said to have disbelieved in their existence, though some among his court speculated that this may have been a feigned indifference and that Harkon felt slighted that the rings were not already in his possession.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSadri-1)</sup> However, the Volkihar were believed to have discovered the whereabouts of both rings circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). Both were located somewhere in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and were recovered at the request of the court's resident conjurer, Feran Sadri.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
