[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg/200px-ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-The_Dreaming_Cave.jpg) The Dreaming Cave Nematigh was a notable planar traveller. They were listed alongside Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md) and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) as a person who used [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md)'s [Dreaming Cave](Lore_Dreaming_Cave.md) to enter [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). They are mentioned in [Seif-ij Hidja](Lore_Seif-ij_Hidja.md)'s *[The Doors of Oblivion](Lore_The_Doors_of_Oblivion.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDOO-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Nematigh's exact relationship to the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) of Artaeum is unknown, though they are listed alongside other people with ties to the order.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDOO-1)</sup>
