[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg) Wind Scour Temple Ettiene Volusus was one of the leaders of the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), who was known for being the one who chronicled the history of the Order.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GEV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSai-2)</sup> She was remembered as one of the four greatest Grandmasters the Dragonguard have ever known,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSai-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONThe Dragonguard-3)</sup> and as the last one honored in the [Wind Scour Temple](Lore_Wind_Scour_Temple.md). She served as the Grandmaster from [1E 2879](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2879) to [1E 2902](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2902). She was known to be modest, honest, and astute.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GEV-1)</sup>

After her death, she was buried in Wind Scour Temple in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), and honored with a [statue](Lore_Statues.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WST-4)</sup> prayer, faith and brazier's blaze.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GEV-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Based on her name, she may have been an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md).
