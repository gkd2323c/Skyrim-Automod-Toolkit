Letter from Maven **by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)**
---
Christophe,

Upon reading your [last letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Christophe.md), I was deeply touched. You're right, I was angry, but now I realize my anger was misplaced. It's time to leave our disagreements in the past and rekindle our friendship. I'll be sending someone to fetch you and bring you to [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). I wish to avoid a scene, so he'll be calling at night. Make sure you pack all your belongings securely, as the journey could be rather treacherous.

[Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)
