Your majesties,

We received your formal letters of inquiry at the palace in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md). At the request of my father, King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md), and with the aid of the [Ring of Daggers](Lore_Ring_of_Daggers.md), I have compiled what little information we could find on [Bahtra at-Hunding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bahtra_at-Hunding). Though she is an adventurer of some repute, she is also an enigma—as you will see.

In the interests of further peaceful collaboration between the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), and the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), I have made this dossier available to you by courier at [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md), and [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). I hope it finds you hale and hearty.

This Bahtra is a traveler of impressive skill and a woman of action, but despite your (quite reasonable) concerns, she exhibits no real threat that I can ascertain.

Respectfully, Prince Azah

* * *

*Letter from the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) Talia at-Marimah to King Fahara'jad.*

My King,

Bahtra at-Hunding left a lasting impression on the Ash'abah. As, it seems, she does to all who cross her path. While she was forthright in her study of the [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) arts, she refrained from using them a single time during her stay at Morwha's Bounty. "Respect for your traditions is the least I can offer you in compensation," she said. This restraint was coupled with a keen mind and insightful questions, and so my people were happy to tutor her in combating the [undead](Lore_Undead.md) plague.

She does honor to her [ancestral name](Lore_Hunding.md), my king. I believe it to be truth. And if she crosses your threshold, I would give her every courtesy due to a descendant of a [sword saint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sword_Saints).

Faithfully, Talia at-Marimah

* * *

*Excerpt from a letter from [Naryu Virian](Lore_Naryu_Virian.md) to [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md).*

This is just another way I disagree with your methods, agent. If you'd had the stomach for—well. I'll keep my eyes open for your wayward [cultists](Lore_Worm_Cult.md). Just remember, you'll owe me, right?

One more thing. I finally met that wanderer you mentioned, Bahtra. I'm not sure she's quite as impressive as you made her out to be. Your weakness for strong women shows again. But she certainly knows which way to hold a dagger, and I can tell how closely you two trained together. We spent some time on the fine art of [assassination magic](Lore_Nightblades.md) and I confirmed she drinks like a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md). Pleasant company. Thankfully not nearly as talkative as you. And her talent with our technique is impressive for one not as dedicated to the art as we are.

Until next month. Your turn to pick the inn.

*Excerpt from correspondence between [Gwaering](Lore_Gwaering.md) (the [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md)) and King [Camoran Aeradan](Lore_Camoran_Aeradan.md).*

The spirit of our people still stands divided. [Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md)'s [movement](Lore_Dawnway.md) still lingers, in some corners. Be careful, my king.

One final thing, Camoran dear. I've had something of an apprentice here in the grove with me the last few days. Bahtra at-Hunding, a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) of considerable potential. She is studying the ways of the [warden](Lore_Warden.md) and sought me out to discuss the intricacies of [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md) from "the deep knowledge of the living wilds." Her words, not mine. She's sharp as rose thorns, that one. Do give her your time if she ends up at Elden Root.

*From the notes of [Martina Abor](Lore_Martina_Abor.md), [Arcanist](Lore_Arcanist.md) Scholar.*

Underweave Expedition 4: Accompanying me on my journey to this nexus of fated potential is a student, a newly minted Arcanist named Bahtra at-Hunding. She speaks plainly and infrequently, which is to my liking. When she does speak, it is to ask a direct question that cuts to the heart of the logographic magic I have spent my life perfecting. As ever, the heuristic metamagical substrate that defines arcanist spellwork is difficult to convey, even to another wielder of the art. But Bahtra seems keen to learn not just how to use arcanoforms, but to convey the concepts around the art as well. A pleasant enough companion on these shadowed roads. I have no qualms recommending her, should anyone ask.
