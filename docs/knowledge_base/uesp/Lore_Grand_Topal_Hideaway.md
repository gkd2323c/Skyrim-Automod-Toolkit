It mostly consists of sandy beaches with shallow waters, a variety of tropical and deciduous trees, and a large active volcano.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Topal-1)</sup> Due to the volcano's constant trembling, it is believed that an eruption could destroy the entire island.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Homestead Int-2)</sup>

## History
Eroded ruins of [xanmeer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xanmeer) structures dot the island, suggesting that there was once an ancient [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) civilization that inhabited it before [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONTopal-3)</sup> Its location near the mouth of the storied [Niben River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Niben_River) has likely resulted in an interesting history on the island, although there is little documentation as to the history or provenance of the property.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Homestead Int-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Architects Guild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Architects_Guild) put the Grand Topal Hideaway on the market as a private residence. The guild was in correspondence with the office of a reputable attorney from [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md) for its sale, implying that the previous owner was a [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) pirate. The guild had no information on the hideaway or the islet's history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Homestead Int-2)</sup> Transport to the island was provided by a ship from Fisherman's Isle, southeast of [Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Haven).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONTopal-3)</sup>
