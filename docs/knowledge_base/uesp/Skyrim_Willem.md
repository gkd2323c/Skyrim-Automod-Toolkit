[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Willem.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Willem.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Willem.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Willem.jpg) Willem **Willem** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in the [Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) and works in the nearby [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine).

Willem will spend all of his time mining, never breaking from his duties to eat or sleep. He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks) to the barracks, as well as some [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
