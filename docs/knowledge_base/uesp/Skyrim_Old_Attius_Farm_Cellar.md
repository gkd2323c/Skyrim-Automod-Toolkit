[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar.jpg/200px-SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar.jpg) Old Attius Farm Cellar **Old Attius Farm Cellar** is a cellar located underneath the ruined house at [Old Attius Farm](Skyrim_Old_Attius_Farm.md). It can be accessed via a trapdoor hidden within the ruined wooden farmhouse.

You will follow a suspicious [Redoran Guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) to this location during the related quest. He will enter the cellar to meet with the [Buyer](Skyrim_Buyer.md), a mysterious individual seeking to purchase the [Mask of Vivec](Skyrim_Mask_of_Vivec.md).
