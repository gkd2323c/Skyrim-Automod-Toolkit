[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg/200px-ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg) The Aetherian Archive The Aetherian Archive is a ruined tower in western [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md), overlooking [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md) to the south.

The tower was built by the Firstmages in ancient times<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTLO1-1)</sup> and predates the nearby towers in Elinhir. According to local legend, the three towers at Elinhir were constructed in homage to the Archive.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMor-2)</sup> It served as the home of the [Firstmages](Lore_Firstmages.md), a group of [elven](Lore_Mer.md) mages completely dedicated to studying Aetherius and achieving magical power. The mages assembled a great library in order to study [magic](Lore_Magic.md) and [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), before casting impenetrable wards to eternally seal themselves inside.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOEar-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOAnir-4)</sup> The mages found a magicka-infused anomaly called the Great Source in the Archive during the First Era. It was the source of their magic, and granted the Firstmages long lifespans. This allowed them to study indefinitely.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOArn-5)</sup>

The Archive remained sealed for several thousand years. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mage Quest-6)</sup> the constellations fell from the sky above Craglorn and took on tangible forms. The Mage [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) was then targeted by [the Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md), who corrupted her Apex Stone and split her Celestial into many aspects.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mage Quest-6)</sup> This allowed the Serpent to take control of the aspects one by one, although this process was stopped by [the Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md), who guided the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in gathering the remaining aspects before they could be corrupted. After weakening the corrupted Lost One, the aspects joined themselves to her in an attempt to resist the Serpent's corruption,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mage Quest-6)</sup> and the confused Celestial Mage subsequently fled to the Aetherian Archive, shattering the wards to enter the tower.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-COTLO2-7)</sup>

Once inside the tower, the Mage's uncontrolled power caused the interior to warp, transforming it into an impossible landscape of floating islands.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TCM-8)</sup> Her entry shattered the Great Source.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOAnir-4)</sup> She sequestered herself away in the tower's gardens, summoning powerful [atronachs](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) to keep intruders from interfering in her work.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TCM-8)</sup> Many of the Firstmages entered her service as guardians, although a small minority avoided the upper floors in fear of their lives.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOAnir-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOArn-5)</sup> The [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) were then quickly made aware of the opening of the Archive, and flocked to the tower in an attempt to be the first to scale it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMor-2)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TMT-9)</sup> The Undaunted ascended the tower and fought through the Mage's guardians before defeating the Celestial herself in battle. Freed from the Serpent's influence, the Mage departed the Archive to help the Thief contain the Serpent's growing influence.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TMT-9)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_%28Hero%27s_Return%29.jpg/200px-ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_%28Hero%27s_Return%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_(Hero%27s_Return).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_(Hero%27s_Return).jpg) Within the Aetherian Archive At some point, the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) had placed arcane wards around the Aetherian Archive, the remnant of the "once-Great Source of knowledge and power", guarded by the fractured fraternity of the Firstmages, in order to prevent its misuse. In 2E 582, a rogue Mages Guild wizard that had been taught all he knew by Galerion himself, Brulo Rlane, cracked the wards the guild had placed around the Archive, and entered the tower hoping to claim the Great Source within for himself, thus threatening all of Tamriel. One of the Firstmages, Arnure, reached out to Galerion for aid in preventing Brulo from claiming the Great Source, something that would've caused a terrible calamity. Seeking to impede Galerion, Brulo opened planar breaches that unleashed a force to obstruct him, [shadowy](Lore_Shadow.md) [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) beings intended to keep him the Arch-Mage off balance. Galerion went to work on sealing the planar breaches, and cast a summoning spell that teleported the Vestige to the Archive, asking them to deal with the shadowy Daedra while he closed the rifts and confronted Brulo himself. The Vestige fought the shadow Daedra while Galerion closed the breaches and engaged Brulo in a magical duel. Eventually the Vestige defeated the conjured beings and the powerful [Gloam Knight](Lore_Gloam_Knight.md) that was Brulo's final defense, while Galerion defeated his rogue apprentice. The victorious Galerion remarked that Nirn could sleep safe, not knowing the danger it faced that day. The pair than quickly departed from the tower, as the power of the Great-Source guarded by the Firstmages was too much for either the Vestige or Galerion to linger around long.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Galerion Rogue-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Rogue-11)</sup>
