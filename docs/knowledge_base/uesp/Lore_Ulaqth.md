[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg) Lilmoth circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Warchief ** Ulaqth was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) ruler of the city of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) during the reign of [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md), in the last years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He was known for his distrust towards the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), due to the slave-raids on his lands organized by the Duke of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup>

He took part in the [Four Score War](Lore_Four_Score_War.md) in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) and commanded the right flank of the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) army during the Battle of Bodrum on the 20th of First Seed, when Queen Naghea of [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md) led the left flank and Lord Storig of [Farrun](Lore_Farrun.md) charged with the vanguard. During that battle, the forces led by the member of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) ambushed the Cyrodilic army and almost completely destroyed it. Ulaqth's fate is unknown, and whether or not he perished in this battle is unclear.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup>
