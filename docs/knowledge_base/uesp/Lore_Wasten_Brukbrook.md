During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Wasten Brukbrook is an active settlement. It was ruled by Princess Physandra and had a rivalry with Eagle Vale.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Eldenroot](Lore_Eldenroot.md) to the far northwest, [Green Hall](Lore_Green_Hall.md) to the south, and [Lynpar March](Lore_Lynpar_March.md) to the north.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Wasten Brukbrook was set to appear in *[ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)* as the town of **Brukbook** but it was later cut from the game. It was set to appear north of Elden Root, in the region of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md). The Blacksap Rebellion would have attacked the town and send its survivors to their occupied settlement, [Ebon Ro](Lore_Ebon_Ro.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ONCut-3)</sup>
