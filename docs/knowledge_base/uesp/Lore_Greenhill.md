[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Greenhill (formerly called **Thibaut's Rest**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCai H-1)</sup> is a township found on the borderlands of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md), in the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Greenhill is a traditional [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) settlement that was built by the [Legion cohort](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) that settled in the march after the death of General Thibaut of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCai H-1)</sup> It has since been a diverse community of Colovians descended from the cohort, and the local [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of the march.

## History
General Thibalt of Kvatch and the [Hastrel cohort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Hastrel) descended from the Colovian West into the southern savanna, much like other incursions throughout the years. When the general died from unknown circumstances, his trusted cohort would inter him in a crypt not far from the [Falinesti Autumn Site](Lore_Falinesti_Autumn_Site.md). They then settled not far north from it. The General's wife, Tertcia manipulated the local Wood Elves and Khajiit to put them against each other to build the crypt where he would be buried and the town they would live in. The town they built was Thibaut's Rest, which later became Greenhill.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCai H-1)</sup>

At the center of town, they built a temple of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) with a traditional catacomb underneath, but as the local races moved in, they expanded them for themselves.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoad Greenhill-2)</sup> Sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), a greenshaper came into town and used her magic to burrow roots and flora into the catacombs. At this point, the town is governed by a Treethane.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONBowenas-3)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Legion of the West Weald, led by [Javad Tharn](Lore_Javad_Tharn.md) and the Stonefire Cult launched an invasion over Reaper's March to annex the region into the [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md). The cult, under Balras Arenim, blackmailed a local Khajiit and had her place amplification crystals across town to possess the people and turn them into [Dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAG-5)</sup> However, the intervention of an [adventurer](Lore_Soulless_One.md) had foiled their plans and liberated the town from their clutches.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESODoorstep-6)</sup>
