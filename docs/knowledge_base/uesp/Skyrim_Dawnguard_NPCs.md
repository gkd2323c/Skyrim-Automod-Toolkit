# Dawnguard NPCs


## Key Players
There are a few NPCs who play a major role in the adventures you embark on during the add-on's [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests). You will encounter them throughout the storyline regardless of your choices in gameplay.

- **[Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran)** — Leader of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) vampire hunters. Wishes to wipe out the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) menace in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).
- **[Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)** — Leader of the [Volkihar vampire clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md). Plans to use [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) in a ritual to blot out the sun.
- **[Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md)** — The daughter of Lord Harkon. She is sought by Harkon for use as a sacrifice in his ritual.
- **[Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md)** — A [moth priest](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) who reads an [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll_(Sun).md) for whichever faction you ally yourself with.
- **[Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica)** — The estranged wife of Harkon and mother of Serana. She is trying to protect her daughter and keep Harkon from succeeding in his plan.
- **[Knight-Paladin Gelebor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knight-Paladin_Gelebor)** — A [Snow Elf](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md) who guards the wayshrine at the Chantry of [Auriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auriel). He will offer you [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) in exchange for a favor.
- **[Arch-Curate Vyrthur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arch-Curate_Vyrthur)** — The brother of Knight-Paladin Gelebor. He is responsible for creating the prophecy Harkon wishes to fulfill.
