[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-place-River_Yorgrim.jpg/200px-SR-place-River_Yorgrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-River_Yorgrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-River_Yorgrim.jpg) River Yorgrim The **River Yorgrim** is a river flowing from [Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md) to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

[Anga's Mill](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill.md) is located along the river. A bridge is located near the mill, and is the only crossing point on the river. It joins the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) at Windhelm.
