Shelreni Baro was a rogue [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) who served as a Master of the house during the time of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md). She did not get along well with [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md), who referred to her and the other magisters as a dangerous, self-selecting nest of snakes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dialogue HT-2)</sup>

Meln Rendys was once her mentor, but upon recognizing a "darkness" in her in that she was reckless and dangerous (not the usual Telvanni traits), he refused to sponsor her advancement beyond the rank of master. Shelreni coveted his Black Book and ultimately murdered Meln.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt M-3)</sup>
