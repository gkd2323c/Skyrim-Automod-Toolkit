**Conjure Seeker** is an adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons one of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s [Seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md) to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure Seeker](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Notes
- This spell is not affected by the [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls) perk.
- This spell radiates with the wrong color for conjuring spells, being white instead of purple.
- The stats of a summoned Seeker are:
