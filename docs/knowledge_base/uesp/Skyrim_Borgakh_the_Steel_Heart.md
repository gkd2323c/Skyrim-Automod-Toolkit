[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-npc-Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.jpg) Borgakh the Steel Heart **Borgakh the Steel Heart** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc1H) and potential [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) found in [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). During the day, she can be found using the training dummy in front of [Larak's Longhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse), and at night, sleeping inside. Although she is proud of Orc traditions and willing to abide by them, she seems uneasy about what her future may hold.

She wears [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Armor_2), including [heavy boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots_2) and [heavy gauntlets](Skyrim_Armor.md#Gauntlets_2), and she keeps a set of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) for sleeping. She carries a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) and [shield](Skyrim_Armor.md#Shields_2), a [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with 20 [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrow), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse) to the longhouse, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). Her armor and weapons are leveled, and can be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality.

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"I envy you, outlander. You can choose to leave if you wish."* *"Soon, I will be of age. The taste is bitter."* *"Perhaps I'll join the Legion one day, as my father had done."* When spoken to:

**What do you do for the stronghold?** *"I am [Larak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak)'s daughter. Soon, I'll be of age and will be sent to marry an Orc chief in some distant stronghold. Until then, I train. My new home will need strength, and my new sisters will know I'm not weak when I arrive."* **You don't know who you'll marry?** *"No. A chief's daughter marries who her chief wishes. It ensures the stronghold receives a fair trade in goods for my absence. I used to look forward to the day when I was of age, to see new places and people, but now it feels like a cage."* **You want to leave the stronghold?** *"I couldn't. ## Unused Dialogue
The game's Dialogue Views files include an early iteration of her response to you persuading her. Said line notably includes the term **Mauloch**, instead of Malacath. Both names are in use by the Daedric Prince, but Malacath is much more prominently used:

*"You're right. Mauloch teaches us to meet our fate. Perhaps this is mine."*
## Notes
- Once she is following you, you can simply take back the gold you gave her for the dowry if you are in need of it.

## Bugs
- When married, she lacks most of the store dialogue, leaving it unvoiced.
- If you marry Borgakh and [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) a [child](Skyrim_Child.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, she cannot be asked how the children are doing as she has no recorded dialogue for it.
- Borgakh's confidence level is so high, you cannot intimidate her no matter how high your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill is. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Her class, `Guard Orc1H` incorrectly levels her Magicka instead of Stamina. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 2.3.1, addresses this issue. Changing it to level her and other Orc Warriors' Stamina rather than Magicka.
