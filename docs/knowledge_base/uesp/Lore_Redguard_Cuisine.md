— *[Sentinel, the Jewel of Alik'r](Lore_Sentinel,_the_Jewel_of_Alik%27r.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SJA-1)</sup> The [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) live upon the arid land of the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), which makes farming an arduous task. They allow domesticated livestock (such as goats) to graze, and their crops need to be able to resist drought. They often dehydrate or salt their food for storage and trade. Everyday meals, on the other hand, are cooked using methods that don't exacerbate the intense heat of the sun during the day: flatbreads are baked within seconds on searing hot stones instead of ovens, and pots of stew are placed over coals to cook over long periods of time. Their port cities bring in trade that adds variety to their diet, and the hot peppers that grow in their homeland are prized both by them and cooks overseas.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOC-2)</sup><sup>:10</sup>

## Utensils and Tableware
Redguard cutlery is high quality and known for its expert craftsmanship by discerning chefs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) cutlery was much the same<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and modern imitations of that style are still produced years later.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Many spices from the Alik'r are rare and expensive and may be stored in special decanters<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> or handmade jars.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Intricate spice grinders are produced in the Alik'r as well.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

Clay sipping cups are made for use by young children.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Fine desert crystal is used to make wine glasses<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> and fine goblets are crafted from glass.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

The Goddess [Morhwa](Lore_Morwha.md) is sometimes honored or depicted with certain dishes such as ornate silver teaspoons that are made in sets of four, one spoon for each of her breasts<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> and on milk pitchers<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> and silver plates.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>
