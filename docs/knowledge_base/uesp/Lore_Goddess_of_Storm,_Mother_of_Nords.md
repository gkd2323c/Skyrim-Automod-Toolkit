Goddess of Storm, Mother of Nords *Details on Kyne, the Nordic goddess of the storm*
---
[Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md), one of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md) of the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) pantheon, is considered by some to lead those Divines. She is one of the Hearth Gods, watching over the present cycle of the world. Her titles are numerous, revealing much about the character of Kyne.

Kyne is called the Kiss at the End, for most Nords agree that Kyne leads the dead to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). She is revered as the Goddess of Storm, called upon to bring rain and snow in dry times. She protects her faithful from the raging gales and blizzards that regularly sweep across the [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) expanse. Other names applied to Kyne include Widow of [Shor](Lore_Shor.md) and the Mother of Nords.

Warriors favor Kyne, as they call upon her for strength in battle and victory in conflict.
