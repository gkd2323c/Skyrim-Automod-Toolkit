# Spellstone

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Dwemer_Puzzle_Cube.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-stolen-Dwemer_Puzzle_Cube.png) Inert Daedric Spellstone Spellstone is a magical alloy created by the great mage [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md). It is said that Shalidor traveled to multiple [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) [planes](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md), collecting [crystals](Lore_Crystals.md) from at least five different realms. Through a highly perilous transmutation spell, he fused these crystals together to create an alloyed spell-focus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IDS-1)</sup>

Around [2E 282](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_282), a member of the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) discovered a spellstone on the isle of [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md). Regrettably, centuries of neglect had left this particular spellstone in an inert state. Nevertheless, despite the passage of many years without use, it still retained its status as one of the most concentrated collections of [Padomaic](Lore_Padomay.md) matter.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IDS-1)</sup>
