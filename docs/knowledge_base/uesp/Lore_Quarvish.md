# Quarvish

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/ON-statue-Trinimac.jpg/200px-ON-statue-Trinimac.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Trinimac.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Trinimac.jpg) Trinimac the Golden Champion, the enemy of Quarvish [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/ON-npc-Priest_Uugus.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Priest_Uugus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Priest_Uugus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Priest_Uugus.jpg) Uugus, the author of the text mentioning Quarvish Quarvish is an obscure entity present in the faith of the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) branch of the Cult of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md). In one of the texts, authored by [Venerable Uugus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Venerable_Uugus), a member of the cult active in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Quarvish is described as [man](Lore_Man.md) -demon. Trinimac, the Golden Champion rode the great [eagle](Lore_Eagle.md) [Zylvara](Lore_Zylvara.md) to his reckoning with Quarvish.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM22-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Although both Quarvish and [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) fought against Trinimac and were associated with mannish cultures, Lorkhan and Quarvish are believed to be separate entities as Uugus' text makes a distinction between the two, and associates them with different events.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM22-1)</sup>
