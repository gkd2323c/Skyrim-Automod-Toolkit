# Twilights

Twilight is the name of two types of related winged [lesser Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).

## Winged Twilight
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/ON-creature-Winged_Twilight.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Winged_Twilight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Winged_Twilight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Winged_Twilight.jpg) A Winged Twilight [Winged Twilights](Lore_Winged_Twilight.md) are intelligent winged [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) with claw-like limbs, black horns, and spiked tails. They can have blue or purple skin, and their eyes can be red or blue. Their bodies are dappled with scales. They are known to operate as servants and messengers of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). In appearance they have a beautiful feminine form similar to that of [Harpies](Lore_Harpy.md) with [bat](Lore_Bat.md) -like wings, though the feminine aspects of the winged twilights are more "ravishing", and their long, sharp, hooked tails are immeasurably more deadly. When working together, they follow a matriarch.

## Grievous Twilight
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg) A Grievous Twilight [Grievous Twilights](Lore_Grievous_Twilight.md) are large, intelligent winged [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that are skilled in magic and melee combat. They have clawed limbs, black horns, bluish-purple skin, glowing blue eyes, spiked tails, and possess the ability to teleport, making them formidable foes. They are believed to be a corruption of the [winged](Lore_Winged_Twilight.md) twilights twisted into a more frightening creature by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). They have a brutish masculine form with a skeletal visage more so in the image of Bal, and unlike winged twilights, their wings are separate limbs from their arms. They are often associated with and found serving Molag Bal, but they can also be found under [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md).
