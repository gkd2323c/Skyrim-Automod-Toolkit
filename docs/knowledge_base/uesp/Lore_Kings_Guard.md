[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/ON-place-King%27s_Guard.jpg/200px-ON-place-King%27s_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-King%27s_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-King%27s_Guard.jpg) King's Guard circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) King's Guard (or **Kings Guard**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup>) is a region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) in northern [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md), to the north of [Cambray Hills](Lore_Cambray_Hills.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup> Due to the trade that regularly passes through the area, the region is a melting pot of the dress, food, and culture of the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) city-states.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GP-3)</sup>

The region was named after the mountain range separating Glenumbra, [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) and [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GP-3)</sup> It is home to many crypts and burial grounds, such as the great cemetery of [Cath Bedraud](Lore_Cath_Bedraud.md) and [Balefire Island](Lore_Balefire_Island.md). The only settlement of note is the mining town of Crosswych. The mountain pass linking Glenumbra with Stormhaven is also located here.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a town called Kings Guard was present in the region. It was ruled by Baron Rodoryan, and had a rivalry with the towns of [Ilessen Hills](Lore_Ilessen_Hills.md) and [Meir Darguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Meir_Darguard).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Eagle Brook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eagle_Brook) in the north, [Moonguard](Lore_Moonguard.md) in the southwest, and [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), in the southeast.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Map Arena-4)</sup>

The Red Rook Bandits, a large bandit clan from the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) period, originated in the mountain passes of the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GP-3)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the region suffered heavily from the necromantic activities of the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md), requiring military intervention from the [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup>
