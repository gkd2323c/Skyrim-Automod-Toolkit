[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-npc-Rakel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rakel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rakel.jpg) Rakel keeping watch **Rakel** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [werewolf](Skyrim_EncClassWerewolf.md) living under [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md). She is a member of the Frostmoon Pack.

Rakel wears [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) along with a pair of [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) and a pair of [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md). She wields a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md) or a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) in combat.

## Dialogue
During your first visit there, she will come up to you and keep you away from their pack:

- *"Hold, traveler. You have no business here."*

If you are not a werewolf:

- *"We mean you no harm, but... be on your way."*

If you are a werewolf:

- *"...Wait. You... you're one of us, aren't you? A werewolf? Then perhaps you do have business here... Forgive me. I'm Rakel, of the Frostmoon Pack. Welcome, brother/sister."*
- **Do you live out here?**
- *"Since I was born. My parents were members of the pack, back when Majni's father was Alpha. After they died, Majni and Akar took care of me, taught me to hunt. I'm not as good as they are, but... I do my best."*
- **Have you ever thought about moving to a city?**
- *"Why? Have you seen how they live? They cower behind their walls, afraid of every [ash hopper](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper.md), every [netch](Skyrim_Netch.md)! No, I'd rather be here. Free to hunt, free to live my life as I choose."*

Her goodbyes:

- *"Hircine go with you."*
- *"Good hunting."*
