It contains only one zone, *[Goldenrock Mine](#Goldenrock_Mine)*.

It was discovered and established by the adventurer [Annekke Crag-Jumper](Skyrim_Annekke_Crag-Jumper.md). [Verner Rock-Chucker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker) will [buy corundum ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) from you.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine.jpg) Mine treasure Around the entrance to the mine are stacks of crates and food sacks that are marked as owned. Among these are four pieces of owned [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md). The [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) is a few paces away, with three owned [corundum ingots](Skyrim_Corundum_Ingot.md) on the ground beside it.

The miners' camp is just outside and to the right of the mine entrance. It consists of a central campfire surrounded by four small tents, three of which contain bedrolls, while the fourth contains a hay pile. Once you are on friendly terms with the settlement, you can use these freely. Eight whole [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) hang on fishing racks behind the tents.

### Goldenrock Mine
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Goldenrock_Mine_02.jpg) Mine interior The mine has many [pickaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and shovels lying around. The entrance tunnel descends to the southeast to a T-junction with a corundum ore vein on the ground at the junction and a dead end to the right with a second ore vein. The tunnel continues descending ahead. At the bottom there is a braced section with a bedroll under it along with a couple food barrels; on top of one of the barrels is a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Chimarvamidium](Skyrim_Chimarvamidium.md)*. The tunnel turns to the northeast and opens into a larger cavern with wooden scaffolding around a central rock pillar allowing access to two more ore veins. At the bottom on the left are several food sacks and a random [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) on a stack of crates. Ascending the ramp leads to the two ore veins on the walls, a small round wooden table with an [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md) and a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on top, and an open box holding three bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead).
