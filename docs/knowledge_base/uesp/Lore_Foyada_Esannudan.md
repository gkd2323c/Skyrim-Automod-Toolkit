[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/MW-place-Foyada_Esannudan.jpg/200px-MW-place-Foyada_Esannudan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Esannudan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Esannudan.jpg) Foyada Esannudan Foyada Esannudan is one of the several [foyadas](Lore_Foyada.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the island district of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The volcanic ravines (or "fire-rivers") are valleys formed by lava flows from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), functioning as drainage basins for lava when the volcano is active, or alternatively forming pathways to travel on when dormant and devoid of lava.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen F-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWAjira-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup>

The foyada branches off from Foyada Mamaea close to the caldera of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) and travels south through the Ashlands.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWMap-4)</sup> Aryon Ancestral Tomb is found at the end, and Dunirai Caverns is found between Foyada Esannudan and [Foyada Ashur-Dan](Lore_Foyada_Ashur-Dan.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWSujamma Dunirai-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWHrundi-6)</sup> Foyada Esannudan was filled with lava at the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AMap-7)</sup> The foyada had dried up by the time of [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), and though the [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md) went through it, House Dagoth set up operations within Sharapli on the exterior.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWSharapli-8)</sup>
