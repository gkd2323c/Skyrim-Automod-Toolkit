Krosis was a high ranking member of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md), and a [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-The_Dragon_War-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDOM-2)</sup> After his demise, Krosis was entombed atop [Shearpoint](Lore_Shearpoint.md), and was reawakened from his slumber in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRKrosis-3)</sup> As a Dragon Priest, he was a powerful mage, also able to wield the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) in battle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGATT-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-item-Krosis.jpg/200px-SR-item-Krosis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Krosis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Krosis.jpg) Krosis's mask (Skyrim) Little is known of the past of Krosis. During the Merethic Era, Krosis ascended through the ranks of the Dragon Cult and achieved a high-ranking position within the organization, attaining the esteemed status of a Dragon Priest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRKrosis-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-The_Dragon_War-1)</sup> Like his fellow priests, he achieved [lichdom](Lore_Lich.md) by receiving eternal worship from his [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) servants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ATD-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRValdar-6)</sup> It is said that due to his high rank, his dragon overlord bestowed upon him a magical [iron mask](Lore_Dragon_Priest_Masks.md#Krosis),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRLoad-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> which bestowed enhanced abilities in alchemy, archery, and lockpicking upon its wearer.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Krosis Mask-9)</sup>

During the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), the ancient Nords rebelled against the dragons and successfully defeated the Dragon Cult. The specific details of Krosis' involvement in the war remain unknown, but he was eventually entombed at the peak of [Shearpoint](Lore_Shearpoint.md), a mountain located in the [Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md) hold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-The_Dragon_War-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDOM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRKrosis-3)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) journeyed to the summit of Shearpoint. There, they encountered awakened Krosis, engaging in a fierce battle that ultimately led to the dragon priest's defeat and demise. The Dragonborn emerged victorious, claiming the mask as their own.
