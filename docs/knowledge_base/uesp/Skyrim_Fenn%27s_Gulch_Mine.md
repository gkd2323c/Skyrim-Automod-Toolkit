It contains only one zone, *[Fenn's Gulch Mine](#Fenn.27s_Gulch_Mine)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md), two [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), a piece of [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md), and a [Juniper](Skyrim_Juniper_Berries.md) tree are found outside the mine entrance.

### Fenn's Gulch Mine
The mine only boasts a single silver ore vein. The rest of the mine contains a variety of typical mine clutter: [pickaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), shovels, a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), and many barrels containing [food](Skyrim_Food.md). When you enter the mine, you have a choice of going left or right. If you go right from the entrance, you will come upon the silver ore vein. If you go right from the entrance then left, you will come across a barred area. If you go left from the entrance, you will ascend a ramp and cross a bridge to a platform. The platform holds a bench, a table with a pickaxe, and another pickaxe at the end next to two barrels.
