[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-npc-Rhorlak.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rhorlak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rhorlak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rhorlak.jpg) Rhorlak **Rhorlak**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is a compatriot of [Rigel Strong-Arm](Skyrim_Rigel_Strong-Arm.md) living in [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md). He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b). He wields a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) that can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality and carries a small amount of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Silver Lining](Skyrim_Silver_Lining.md)**: Recover a stolen [silver mold](Skyrim_Curious_Silver_Mold.md) for a [silversmith](Skyrim_Endon.md).

## Quest-Related Events
During the quest [Silver Lining](Skyrim_Silver_Lining.md), [Endon](Skyrim_Endon.md) will send you to Pinewatch to recover a silver mold. When you enter Pinewatch, you will initially find Rhorlak alone. You can then tell him you are looking for Endon's mold:

- **I'm looking for Endon's mold.**
- *"Endon's mold? I'm afraid you have the wrong place, my friend. You'll find no silversmiths here. I assure you, I have nothing of value. I'm nothing but a poor woodcutter just trying to make ends meet."*
- **I never said it was a silver mold.**
- *"Well uh, Endon is a silversmith in Markarth, so I just assumed..."*

You now have three options. He will attack if the first option is chosen.

- **Tell me where it is, or else.**
- *"So that's how it's going to be, huh? Fine. Take your best shot!"*
- **I see. I must have the wrong place.**
- *"No harm done. Tell this Endon fellow I hope he finds what he's looking for."*
- **Remember now? *(Bribe)***
- *"Well, can't say exactly where the mold is, but something does spring to mind. It seems there's this strange button on the wall opposite the fireplace in my house. Always wondered what it was for. Think I'm going to head out now. Take a long vacation from woodcutting, you know? Good luck."*
- *"Come on, what are trying to do? Insult me?"* (If you do not have the gold)

If you ask him about the mold again, he continues to deny knowing anything.

- **Let's try again. Where is the silver mold?**
- *"I thought I was clear when I said I didn't know."*

## Notes
- There is a [note](Skyrim_Note_to_Rhorlak.md) addressed to Rhorlak from Rigel Strong-Arm downstairs in the cottage.
- Rhorlak is not present at Pinewatch before the related quest.
- If you want to spare Rhorlak, but do not want to bribe him, either, with a high enough Sneak and possibly also Invisibility, you can hide from him as soon as he turns hostile (whether through the conversation options or from pressing the button without speaking to him). He calms relatively quickly and will move to leave the house (he may be stuck at the front door during which he will still be hostile, so you may need to leave quickly.). At certain times of the day/night he also sleeps in the bed in the basement, so you can sneak by him then.
