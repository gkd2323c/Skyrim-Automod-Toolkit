[Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md) will give it to you as a reward for completing the quest [Spirits Away](Skyrim_Spirits_Away.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Spirits Away](Skyrim_Spirits_Away.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Help a [Khajiit trader](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) recover his shipment of [aged flin](Skyrim_Aged_Flin.md).

## Notes
- The Ring of Khajiit has previously appeared in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Ring_of_Khajiit)*, *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Ring_of_Khajiiti)*, *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Ring_of_Khajiit)* and *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Ring_of_Khajiiti)*.
- The ring's [model and texture](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#BSA-only_Items) were included in Skyrim's game data since release, although in older versions of Skyrim it does not exist as an item. The item was added by the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Bugs
- Upon putting [Ildari Sarothril](Skyrim_Ildari_Sarothril.md) into bleedout, you are given a perk that adds a multiplier of 0.01 to the magnitude of [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) effects on you. This persists until the perk is removed upon killing Ildari. This notably makes the Ring of Khajiit and [Ring of the Wind](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Wind.md) very weak. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The perk can be removed using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `player.removeperk 0403d597`; it will be reacquired when Ildari is next put into Bleedout.
- If the ring is equipped while you have the [Kindred Mage](Skyrim_Kindred_Mage.md) perk, the movement speed buff will be increased by 10%. The enchantment's strength will not update in the inventory, but the Active Effects menu will show the correct magnitude.
