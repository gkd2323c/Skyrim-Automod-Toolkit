[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Ralof.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ralof.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ralof.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ralof.jpg) Ralof **Ralof** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [barbarian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Barbarian), a member of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), and an early companion. Captured alongside you, he escapes [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody when the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) attacks [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). If you follow him into [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) for shelter from the attack, he frees you from your bindings so you can assist him against the [Imperial soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md).

Ralof’s dialogue introduces the player to the world of Skyrim, its culture and its conflicts in 4E 201.

After escaping from Helgen, he takes you to [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), where his sister [Gerdur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gerdur) lives. He will also invite you to join the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). Once you complete the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), you and Ralof will split up. However, if you decide to join the Stormcloaks, he will fight alongside you and your fellow Stormcloaks in the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md).

You will meet him again when you are tasked with [rescuing prisoners](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Neugrad.md) from [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md), where you learn that Ralof has been promoted and is commanding the troops that will be aiding you in this quest. You will also see Ralof during the quest [Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Stormcloaks).md), when you and his soldiers attack an Imperial cart filled with silver and weapons. In battle, Ralof uses his [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe) and [longbow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) with [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) during Unbound, and later on an [iron warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer).

Ralof's sister [Gerdur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gerdur) is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) in Riverwood, and runs the sawmill with her husband [Hod](Skyrim_Hod.md). Gerdur and Ralof's family founded Riverwood, and has lived there for many generations. Gerdur and Hod have a son, [Frodnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frodnar), and a dog, [Stump](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stump). Like Ralof, the entire family staunchly supports the Stormcloak cause.

After the war is over, if you chose to escape Helgen with Ralof and sided with the Stormcloaks, he can be found living in Riverwood at the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md).

### Aftermath
After the Stormcloaks win the war and Ralof moves into the Sleeping Giant Inn, he will only greet you with generic dialogue, along with his own lines, and cannot be interacted with further.

## Notes
- He is the first NPC in the game to speak, and his "Hey you, you're finally awake" line in the opening scene of the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md) has become iconic.
- Before you escape Helgen, Ralof will not retaliate if you attack him.
- If you followed Hadvar out of Helgen, but you joined the Stormcloaks, Ralof will not be present during the Civil War quests. Instead, he is disabled from the game.
