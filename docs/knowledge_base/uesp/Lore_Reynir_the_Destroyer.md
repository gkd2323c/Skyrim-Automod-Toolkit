Reynir the Destroyer *A warning regarding a powerful draugr buried in the crypts beneath Windhelm*
---
Those who would brave the crypts beneath [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), be warned.

Herein dwells the [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) remnants of the great warrior [Reynir](Lore_Reynir.md) of [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), who claimed to have survived the [Night of Tears](Lore_Night_of_Tears.md), and sailed to [Mereth](Lore_Skyrim.md) with the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Companions.md) on the ship commanded by [Ylgar](Lore_Ylgar.md), son of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md).

The draugr retains some semblance of Reynir's intellect, but his humanity has been subsumed by the madness of undying. The creature that remains is no proud [Nord](Lore_Nord.md). He is an undead abomination who delights in the suffering of others.

He knows only hate and vengeance. Do not disturb his slumber.
