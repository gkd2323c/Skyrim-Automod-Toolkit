## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md).
2. Make your way through [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md).
3. *(Optional):* Free [Orthorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthorn).
4. Find the three stolen books.
5. Return the books to the College.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Arcanaeum
After the discovery of the mysterious Orb in the depths of [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md), a puzzled [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) decides to send you back alone to alert Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md). Aren turns out to be quite uninterested in the whole affair. You will then be given the quest and prompted to go speak to [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md), who then sends you along to librarian [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) to research the subject. Locate Urag, who can usually be found behind his desk inside the [Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum) where he functions as the librarian and keeps a close eye on the College's huge collection of ancient tomes. After warning you about touching any of the books in the library, the old Orc will mention that there may be relevant information in one of three tomes which are no longer in his possession. They were 'borrowed' by an Apprentice named [Orthorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthorn) who recently, due to a dispute with the College, joined a group of renegade mages in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md). Urag can do nothing for you without the books, and says you'll have to find Orthorn to retrieve the books so the research of the Orb can begin.

On your way out the door you may be approached by [Faralda](Skyrim_Faralda.md) who tells you that the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) visitor, [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md), wants a word with you. If you do not see Faralda, Ancano will actively seek you out and ask you questions about what was found in Saarthal. During this discussion you can choose to provoke him or not, but he will always get the answer he seeks. Proceed out the door and prepare for Fellglow Keep.

### The Dungeons
Fellglow Keep is a large fort located between [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). When you arrive it becomes clear that the residing mages are far from friendly as you will be attacked by two elemental mages and a [flame atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md). Dispose of them and locate the entrance to [Fellglow Keep Dungeons](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep_Dungeons.md).

Upon entering, head down the stairs and follow the path as it twists and turns. The second flooded room is guarded by another hostile mage who has been experimenting with [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) and will send them after you with the words: *"Ah, a test subject! Go, my pets! Attack!"* Fight the spiders and the mage, then proceed southeast and beware of the [pressure plate](Skyrim_Traps.md#Pressure_Plate) triggering a [dart trap](Skyrim_Traps.md#Dart_Trap) along the way. Before long, you will arrive in a room filled with cages containing some very unusual prisoners, specifically leveled [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). You can use the four handles to release them, or pick their cell locks, and they will cause havoc in the next room before leaving the dungeon peacefully. The next room reveals that several experiments were conducted on vampires as bodies are everywhere. Locate the northeastern tunnel and listen to the sound of [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) howling. In the next room are more cages and another hostile mage, seemingly another experiment chamber. Dispose of the mage and inspect the cages. Apart from the wolves, you will also find the renegade mage [Orthorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthorn), seemingly imprisoned by the mages he thought of as friends.

### Orthorn
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_03.jpg) [Orthorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthorn) in action As it turns out, Orthorn was imprisoned as yet another test subject by the mages, despite the fact that he came to Fellglow Keep to help them. You can choose to comfort him by saying Savos Aren sent you to rescue him, or you can simply be honest and say you are there solely for the books. In both cases, Orthorn will offer to help clear out the dungeon and find the missing books, so decide if you want him to tag along. Pull the lever in the middle and he will be released from the cage. He will proceed to explain that the books were taken by someone known as [The Caller](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Caller). If you accept his help you will have to put up with his comments for the rest of a very long trip, although he is a powerful [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) and wields a [lightning bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md) spell. If you decline, he will head out of the dungeon peacefully. Regardless of your choice, he is disabled later on and disappears from the game.

Unfortunately, the nearby door leading to [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) is locked (key required), so you will have to take the long path. Go through the wooden door and make your way through the trashed tunnels. The next chamber has three fire mages training destruction spells on a living [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) in a cage, so be on your guard when they notice you. You can find a copy of [The Legend of Red Eagle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle) on the table in the middle of the room. Take the northern path and you will reach the undercroft, which is guarded by [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md). There is a pedestal with a copy of *[Spell Tome: Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Raise_Zombie)* just before the stairs leading to the next zone, Fellglow Keep.

### Fellglow Keep
There is a small table at the end of the hallway before the stairs that has a coin purse and an easily missed [Black Soul Gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) behind a book. The first chamber of Fellglow Keep is guarded by a [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) and an ice mage and has the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill book *[The Doors of Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Doors_of_Oblivion)* on a pedestal. Open the eastern door and fight the patrolling necromancer, then proceed through the chambers and fight all the enemies in the room with multiple paths. A locked door (key required) leads outside while the eastern door leads to the sleeping quarters and more enemies. Check all the shelves for rare books, then head up the stairs. The room to the west holds an [Unusual Gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) so make sure to pick it up. Take the adjacent path and head upstairs to a room filled with shelves and the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[A Hypothetical Treachery](Skyrim_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md)* on a table. Proceed forward and make sure to check behind the closed doors for some extra loot. One of the doors is locked but pick the lock and find a [shrine](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Julianos) to [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md) and a wealth of ingredients. Open the wooden door to the north and start climbing the tower. Before long you will reach the Fellglow Ritual Chamber and an encounter with [the Caller](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Caller).

### The Caller
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hitting_the_Books_02.jpg) The Caller waiting in the ritual chamber You arrive in the ritual chamber right away and the Caller awaits you, right in the center. She will, however, remain calm and greet you: *"So, you're the one who barged into my home and laid waste to my projects. How nice to meet you."* You will have two dialogue options to choose from; either ask her who she is or ask for the three books. The first option will make her respond, *"Names no longer matter. You may refer to me as The Caller. Now, do you have a reason for making such a mess?"* When you ask her for the books she will be disappointed: *"So you're just one of Aren's lackeys? That's disappointing. You show real promise. You come here, kill my assistants, disrupt my work... You've annoyed me, so I don't think I'll be giving you anything."*

At this point it is actually possible to persuade her to let you have the books. Alternatively, if you brought Orthorn along with you, you can negotiate with her for the books. She will then say, *"An arrangement? After you've destroyed so much? Fine. Leave me the elf. You may go with your books."* You have to walk up to the Caller and start a conversation. Agree to her conditions, then pick up the book. If you simply pick up a book without doing this she will attack you. If you didn't bring Orthorn, she will say, *"I'm afraid you don't have anything worth trading."* Dispose of her and claim the three books (two of the books are behind the Caller on pedestals; lifting either will spawn a Flame or Frost Atronach, depending on your level). If Orthorn is still there, you can have a brief conversation with him which he ends with: *"You'll be going back to the College, then? I, uhh... I think perhaps I'll wait a bit before venturing there myself. Let this whole thing blow over, you know. Give certain parties a chance to forget what I may have done. Still, you've gotten me out of that cage, and I won't forget it. Thank you again, and good luck to you."* Find the exit and leave Fellglow Keep. You may still need to kill the Caller to obtain the key to the exit.

### Night of Tears
When you arrive back at the College, you will find that [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) has returned from Saarthal and placed the Orb in the [Hall of the Elements](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Elements.md). Head into the Arcanaeum and locate Urag gro-Shub. Out of the three books, *[Night of Tears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Night_of_Tears)*, *[The Last King of the Ayleids](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Last_King_of_the_Ayleids)*, and *[Fragment: On Artaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fragment__On_Artaeum)*, he will be particularly interested in the first one, as it has some interesting implications. The quest ends with him handing you a bunch of books as reward (see [this section](#Rewards)) and asks you to speak to Tolfdir. The next quest, [Good Intentions](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md), will then start.

## Rewards
Upon returning the books to Urag gro-Shub, he will give you several [skill books](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md), one for each school of magic. The books are:

- [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md): *[Daughter of the Niben](Skyrim_Daughter_of_the_Niben.md)*
- [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md): *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](Skyrim_2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9.md)*
- [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md): *[Response to Bero's Speech](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Response_to_Bero%27s_Speech)*
- [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md): *[Catalogue of Weapon Enchantments](Skyrim_Catalogue_of_Weapon_Enchantments.md)*
- [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md): *[The Black Arts On Trial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arts_On_Trial)*
- [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md): *[Racial Phylogeny](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Racial_Phylogeny)*

## Notes
- At stage 60 of this quest, the Eye of Magnus is invisibly moved from [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md) to the [Hall of the Elements](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Elements.md). [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) also returns to the College at this time, allowing you to start [his quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir_the_Absent-Minded).
- There is another book called *[Last King of the Ayleids](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Last_King_of_the_Ayleids)*. You need to find *[<u>**The**</u> Last King of the Ayleids](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Last_King_of_the_Ayleids)*. The text is the same, but the other book will not work for this quest.
- Even if you didn't kill anyone by sneaking through the dungeons, the Caller will still blame you with the same dialogue.
- While the Caller will not accept a dead Orthorn in exchange for the books, it is possible to trick her into accepting the deal even if he dies. If Orthorn's corpse is reanimated prior to entering the Ritual Chamber, he will follow the player in as normal, and the quest can proceed as if he is still alive.
- Using the "Persuade" option when speaking with the Caller while Orthorn is accompanying you results in the same outcome as trading him for the books. He will return to his cell, and approaching or interacting with him will be met with "How could you just leave me here?"

## Bugs
- [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md) was originally supposed to play a part in this quest, at stage 10 and 20 specifically. For unknown reasons, she was skipped and Tolfdir now asks you to go directly to Savos Aren from Saarthal. However, the in-game text still describes the original journal intro including Mirabelle Ervine. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- The College students are supposed to gather next to the Eye of Magnus and play out a scene. Unfortunately, due to issues with the scene trigger, this would never happen because the trigger would be encountered prior to this portion of the quest being reached since the trigger box has no conditions and would become disabled upon first entering the Hall of the Elements. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.9, fixes this bug.
- Once you clear the fort, trying to enter it again will cause a crash. This can happen even during the quest if you try to backtrack from the Ritual Chamber. If you are on the quest just leave by the exit to the exterior in the chamber. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To avoid this, enter `coc Fellglow Keep03` in the console to move to the Ritual Chamber, complete the quest, and use the console again to exit the keep (`coc Winterhold Exterior01`).
- There is a possibility that the stairway leading up to the Caller's room in Fellglow Keep is blocked off with boulders, and the door leading up to her room is removed. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Enter `coc Fellglow Keep03` in the console to bypass the boulders.
- If you collect the books before talking to Orthorn he may not recognize it when you free him. Messing with the levers can eventually trigger a quest update for him being freed.
- If Orthorn is killed before reaching the Caller and she accepts your "Persuade" offer, the door to the exit may remain locked with a "Requires Key" status. - Killing the Caller will allow you to obtain the key from her corpse.
