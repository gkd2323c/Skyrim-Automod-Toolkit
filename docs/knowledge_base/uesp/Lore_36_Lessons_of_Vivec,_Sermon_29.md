[The Thirty-Six Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md), Sermon Twenty-Nine **by [Vivec](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vivec)** *Book 29 of a series of words of wisdom relating to Vivec*
---
*Note: If you associate each of the thirty-six listed numbers with a word in its respective sermon, a hidden message is revealed:* He was not born a god. His destiny did not lead him to this crime. He chose this path of his own free will. He stole the godhood and murdered the Hortator. Vivec wrote this.
