Monument Lighthouse *A plaque describing Monument Lighthouse's relationship with the All Flags Navy* *
---
This lighthouse guided ships to [All Flags Islet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:All_Flags_Islet) during the period of fleet construction leading up to the assault on [Thras](Lore_Thras.md). Afterward, it was restored and turned into a museum as part of the memorial to the memory of the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md).
