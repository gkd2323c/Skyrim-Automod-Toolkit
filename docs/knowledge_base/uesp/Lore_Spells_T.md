## Telekinesis
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/ON-misc-Absurd_Reading.png/200px-ON-misc-Absurd_Reading.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Absurd_Reading.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Absurd_Reading.png) A priest absurdly using telekinesis to read multiple books at the same time [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/SR-spell-Telekinesis.jpg/200px-SR-spell-Telekinesis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Telekinesis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Telekinesis.jpg) Telekinesis **Telekinesis** is an adept level [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) spell that allows the caster to pick up, drop, and throw items from a distance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Telekinesis.md)

**Spell schools:** [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md)
## Transmute
**Transmute** is an spell that can transmute unrefined Iron ore to Silver, or Silver ore to Gold.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Transmute.md)

**Spell schools:** [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md)
## Transmute Fog
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-skill-Elusive_Mist.jpg/200px-ON-skill-Elusive_Mist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Elusive_Mist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Elusive_Mist.jpg) Vampires' mist form **Transmute Fog** is an spell that can transmute matter to fog. Eboric Direfrost of the [Direfrost Clan](Lore_Direfrost_Keep.md) used it on himself to bypass locked doors.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Some vampires are able to turn into mist, achieving a similar effect to the Transmute Fog spell.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Eboric_Direfrost)
