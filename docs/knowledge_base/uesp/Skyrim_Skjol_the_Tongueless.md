[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SR-npc-Skjol_the_Tongueless.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Skjol_the_Tongueless.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skjol_the_Tongueless.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skjol_the_Tongueless.jpg) Skjol the Tongueless Skjol the Tongueless is a hostile [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior. He is a mute due to his lack of a tongue. He is also the author of *[Skjol's Journal](Skyrim_Skjol%27s_Journal.md)* and is responsible for the murder of an innocent [Skaal villager](Skyrim_Skaal_Villager.md). He has fled to an [unnamed island](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Horker_Stalhrim_Island) northeast of [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) in search of [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md), and you will need to kill him during the related quest in order to claim a set of Stalhrim Fur armor located within a sarcophagus on the island.

Skjol wears a full set of [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) with matching [helmet](Skyrim_Scaled_Helmet.md), [boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md), and [bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). In combat he wields a fearsome [Stalhrim Battleaxe of Freezing](Skyrim_Stalhrim_Battleaxe_of_Freezing.md).
