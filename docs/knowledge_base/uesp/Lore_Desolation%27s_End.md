[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/ON-quest-Cutting_Off_the_Source.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Cutting_Off_the_Source.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Cutting_Off_the_Source.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Cutting_Off_the_Source.jpg) Desolation's End Desolation's End was a small pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), created in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by a member of the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md) called Lord Velian.

It was created through [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) ritual after the villagers of [Eagle's Brook](Lore_Eagle%27s_Brook.md), in the [Cambray Hills](Lore_Cambray_Hills.md) region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), were turned into [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md). The realm resembled a nightmarish [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) covered in glowing red glyphs and surrounded by lava. The ruin's only inhabitants were [banekin](Lore_Banekin.md) and Velian's shade.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The local mage, Merien Sellan, learned of Velian's ritual and entered the pocket realm to stop him. He allowed himself to be willingly captured in order to collapse the plane, killing himself in the process. His son Tamien asked [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to enter the realm and kill Velian, allowing Merien to escape before the collapse but also allowing the cultists to leave with the zombie army by ship. It is unknown if Merien was rescued, although the realm was collapsed regardless.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
