The Dark Adept, Arum-Khal, was a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) [lich](Lore_Lich.md) who fell into [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md)'s grasp in ages long past. Originally an adept with the [Order of the Hidden Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_Hidden_Moon.md), he got his start redeeming [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) in [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md)'s name. He was curious, always asking questions. He was also described as impatient. These qualities in part lead to his downfall. He was a powerful member of the Order, and very proud of that fact. He was considered as a candidate to wield the [Moonlight Blade](Lore_Moonlight_Blade.md), a relic given as a gift to the Order by Azurah.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMESO-1)</sup>

When the time came for a new blade-bearer to be selected, Arum-Khal's friend and rival, Shando-ri, was picked over him. Arum-Khal became bitter, sending him over the edge. He renounced his faith and fell to the Darkness, bringing many of the Order's adepts with him. He incited a civil war, which destroyed the Order of the Hidden Moon. The remaining members of the original Order managed to weaken Arum-Khal, but they could not kill him. He hid fragments of his spirit in his phylacteries as a last resort, allowing the Order to trap him within using wards, which faded over time. Arum-Khal survived within the phylacteries, which connected to a [realm of darkness](Lore_Arum-Khal%27s_Realm.md) of his own design.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMESO-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) arrived at the ruins of the Temple of the Hidden Moon, where they investigated strange instances of the dead rising from their rest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The Vestige managed to pass the Order of the Hidden Moon's trials and became the last bearer of the Moonlight Blade.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They then vanquished Arum-Khal once and for all in the Order's crypts, entering his realm using the Moonlight Blade and destroying his spirit.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The necromancer [Vastarie](Lore_Vastarie.md) then performed a ritual to draw every speck of light from Arum-Khal's realm, inadvertently retriving the remaining kernel of light from Arum-Khal's sundered soul. Fortunately, this redeemed version of him was no threat to Tamriel and was able to pass on to the Sands Behind the Stars alongside his fellow adepts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vastarie-5)</sup>
