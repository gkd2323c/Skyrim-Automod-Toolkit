## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Azadi](Skyrim_Azadi.md) in [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md).
2. Read [Azadi's Note](Skyrim_Azadi%27s_Note.md) and wait for a courier to deliver another [letter](Skyrim_Fijeh%27s_Letter_to_(Player).md) to you.
3. Travel to [Purewater Run](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purewater_Run) and speak to [Fijeh](Skyrim_Fijeh.md).
4. Read [Fijeh's instructions](Skyrim_Fijeh%27s_Instructions.md).
5. Head to the Vilemyr Inn in Ivarstead, and speak to [Sirayar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sirayar).
6. Return to Fijeh at Purewater Run.
7. Travel to Sunderstone Gorge where you can either attack the caravan on your own or meetup with the Remnants.
8. Head back to Purewater Run and speak to Rakeed and Fijeh.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Azadi
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-npc-Azadi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Azadi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azadi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azadi.jpg) Azadi To begin this quest, speak to Azadi in [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) where you can either brawl him or speak him down:

- *"Well, look what we have here."*
- **Looking for a fight?**
- *"Yes."*

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Alright, which side of your face would you like rearranged? (Brawl)** | *"I'll teach you to talk to me that way!"* | Brawl begins |
| **You don't frighten me. All you're doing is being insufferable. Leave. (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"Okay, okay. You've talked me into it. Thank you. Here, this is for you."* <br> **Failed**: *"I'll teach you to talk to me that way."* | **Passed**: Gives [note](Skyrim_Azadi%27s_Note.md) and warps away <br> **Failed**: Brawl begins |
| **I don't have time for this right now.** | *"I can smell your shame!"* | Nothing |

After defeating him in a brawl:

- *"You're tougher than you look. You got me, fair and square. Thank you. Here this is for you."*

He will then hand you a [note](Skyrim_Azadi%27s_Note.md) and disappear. Read the note and wait for a courier to deliver another [letter](Skyrim_Fijeh%27s_Letter_to_(Player).md) to you.

### Purewater Run
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-quest-Interception_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Interception_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_02.jpg) Fijeh awaits your arrival Once you have read the courier's letter, you will need to travel to [Purewater Run](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purewater_Run). Once you arrive, speak to [Fijeh](Skyrim_Fijeh.md):

- *"Out with it."*
- **You must be Fijeh.**
- *"Correct."*

- - **I'd like to help your missing Remnant, Rakeed. What comes next?**
- *"It's all in this note."*

- - **I don't work for free. Before this goes further, we need to talk about payment.**
- *"A little coin always greases the wheels. How much?"*

| Option | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **400 gold seems reasonable.** | *"For that price? Sure."* |
| **I wouldn't do this for any less than 800 gold. (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"How can I argue with that?"* <br> **Failed**: *"Nah, I don't think so."* |
| **There's other contracts I could be working right now. 1600 gold. (Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"How can I argue with that?"* <br> **Failed**: *"You expect me to believe that?"* |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-npc-Josla.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Josla.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Josla.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Josla.jpg) Josla [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-npc-Sirayar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sirayar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirayar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirayar.jpg) Sirayar All three options will lead to the following dialogue:

- **It's settled then. What's next?**
- *"It's all in this note. Now... this conversation never happened. Understood?"*

- - **I'll return soon.**
- *"Good luck out there."*

- - **I hope Rakeed is worth all of this trouble.**
- *"Good luck out there."*

Alternatively, you can tell him:

- **Actually, I don't have time to deal with this right now.**

He will then hand you [instructions](Skyrim_Fijeh%27s_Instructions.md) which explain that the Remnants have agents that have lived in Skyrim for over 20 years, that one lives in the Miner's Barracks in Karthwasten and the other should be waiting for you in front of Vilemyr Inn in Ivarstead.

### Karthwasten
Head to the [Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) in Karthwasten, where you will find [Josla](Skyrim_Josla.md) sitting behind the barracks. Speak to her:

- **"The ravens take flight once more."**
- *"Of course. Let's both get on with our lives now, hmm?"*

She will then hand you a [note](Skyrim_Josla%27s_Note.md) which states that she overheard that a Hammerfell noble from Sentinel would be crossing the border in the next few days and paid a hefty coin purse to the Thalmor to ensure safe passage to [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md). She suspects that the noble is [Ismal](Skyrim_Ismal.md) and that Rakeed would most likely be with him.

### Ivarstead
Head to the Vilemyr Inn in Ivarstead, where you will find [Sirayar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sirayar):

- **"The ravens take flight once more."**
- *"I understand. Anyway, it's done. Time to move on."*

He will then hand you a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sirayar%27s_Note) which states that the wagon will be passing by [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md).

### Ambush the Thalmor
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-quest-Interception_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Interception_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_03.jpg) The Remnants await you. Return to Fijeh at Purewater Run:

- **Ismal has been traveling with a caravan of Thalmor to Morrowind.**
- *"You've done well."*
- **They will enter Skyrim from the southwest, passing by Sunderstone Gorge.**
- *"It just so happens I've got some scouts along the road. Meet up with them, and together see if you can't overpower the caravan."*

- - - **Sounds good.**
- *"Here. This is for you."*

- - - **I prefer to work alone.**
- *"I understand. Go in quiet, break our man out, then get out of there. Don't risk combat if you can avoid it. If you can't, then give 'em hell. Here. This is for you."*

- - - - **I'll make sure your man gets out safely.**
- *"Stay sharp out there."*

- - - - **I only want to kill Thalmor. I can't promise anything else.**
- *"It can't be helped."*

He will then hand you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Supply_Chest_Key) to the remnant supply chest that can be found near him. Unlock the chest and it contains:

- [Remnant Agent Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Armor)
- [Remnant Agent Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Boots)
- [Remnant Agent Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Gloves)
- [Remnant Agent Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Agent_Hood)
- [Remnant Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Shield)
- [Remnant Scimitar of Fire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remnant_Scimitar_of_Fire) x 2
- [Potion of Extended Invisibility](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extended_Invisibility) x 2
- [The Ebon Arm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebon_Arm)

Travel to Sunderstone Gorge where you can either attack the caravan on your own or meetup with the Remnants.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-quest-Interception_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Interception_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Interception_04.jpg) Rakeed and the fight. Meet up with the three Remnants and they will charge to the caravan alongside you. You will have to fight four Thalmor soldiers and [Ismal](Skyrim_Ismal.md), who wields [Boneshaver](Skyrim_Boneshaver.md).

Once the Thalmor and Ismal have been dispatched, rescue Rakeed:

- *"Please! Have mercy... I beg you..."*

- - **Wait Here. I'll be back.**
- *"This doesn't look good."*

- - **(Cut Rakeed's Bonds)**
- *"Thanks a lot."*

When freed he will then turn himself invisible and disappear. Additionally before leaving the three remaining Remnant Agents can be pickpocketed from or killed with no consequence to gain even more enchanted Remnant sets of armor and weapons. Finally head back to Purewater Run where Rakeed and Fijeh are waiting:

- *"You made it! I'm impressed. It was a gamble, but turned out well."*

- - **Good luck out there. I hope we meet again someday.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you. May the gods keep you."*

- - **More than deserving of some payment, I'd say.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you. May the gods keep you."*

You will receive the gold you and Fijeh agreed on, and both Rakeed and Fijeh will vanish with the casting of Invisibility spells. This ends the quest.

In addition to the rewards from the chest, you will now be able to smith the Remnant Armor, Boots, Gloves, Hood, Shield and Scimitars. These are the unenchanted versions of the Remnant Agent armor pieces and scimitars that you picked from the chest (all of which are enchanted except for the shield): they are to be found in the Steel menu, but require the Advanced Armors perk (on the left side of the Smithing tree) to forge, or to temper properly.

## Notes
- When Rakeed is rescued he is wearing a white variant of the Execution Hood which was always in the game files yet unobtainable and marked as unplayable.

## Bugs
- Both Josla and Sirayar can have the code phrase repeated to them indefinitely resulting in an unlimited number of their notes. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Azadi may not initiate dialogue when speaking to him and will only respond in generic dialogue such as "Need something?" and "Huh?"
- When you talk to Azadi and choose the brawl option, he may not initiate combat. This will give you a bounty for assault if you attack him.
- When you win the brawl with Azadi, he may only say "You're tougher than you look. You got me, fair and square." and not say the second line "Thank you. Here, this is for you.", so you will not get his note. Talking to him again will only result in "Need something?" and "Huh?" <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, use the console command `setstage cc EDHSSE003_Quest 22`. Afterwards talk to Azadi and he will have the proper dialogue and give you the note.
