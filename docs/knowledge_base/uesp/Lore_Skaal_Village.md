[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) Skaal Village circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/BM-place-Skaal_Village.jpg/200px-BM-place-Skaal_Village.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) Skaal Village circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Skaal Village is home to the [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md), a tribe of [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) who strive to live in harmony with nature in accordance with their worship of the [All-Maker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:All-Maker).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTAM-1)</sup> The village is located in the [Felsaad Coast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Felsaad_Coast) region of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), north of the Mead Hall of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md), and is the northernmost settlement in Tamriel. It consists of small, sturdy wooden dwellings, arranged around a central Greathall which hosts feasts and other gatherings.

The Skaal are traditionally not very friendly to outsiders.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-STOL-2)</sup> Clad in traditional Nordic mail armor, Skaal Honor Guards protect the village from intruders, man and beast alike.

## History
The term "Skaal" first appeared as the name of one of the longships used by the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md) to cross the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) from [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), with the *Skaal* sailing south-southeast from Atmora through the bitter winds of the Tidal Woe.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sot R7-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-T500MC-4)</sup> Visuals carved onto horker scrimshaws depict the early ancestors of the Skaal people running their boats aground on the land of Solstheim.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCHT-5)</sup>

Early in the [third century](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_300) of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a group of Skaal left the village, hoping to return more to their roots. These Skaal were led by [Hrothmund the Red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hrothmund_the_Red), and founded [Thirsk Mead Hall](Lore_Thirsk.md), a settlement on the southern shore of Lake Fjalding<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAH-6)</sup> (though some local Nords believe the history of the hall dates back to the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Thirsk-7)</sup>). There, they lived like the Nords of old, hunting and fighting without restraint, with the mightiest warrior rising to leadership.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DBKuvar-8)</sup>

Following the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), the ashfall from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) gradually worsened, poisoning the land and water. This causes animals to have less to eat and become desperate, driven north in search of food, into Skaal lands.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WWB-9)</sup>
