*When Sir Byric's flames razed the ground outside our walls and the Reachfolk hurled stones at our walls. I stood tall at my post, knowing that our smallest knights padded the ramparts alongside me.*
—Sir Hughes<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pet-1)</sup> **Sir** Byric **of the Flame** was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [knight](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md). In an act of valor, Sir Byric set the fields surrounding Alcaire Castle ablaze to repel [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) invaders in [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542). This heroic feat led to the renaming of the [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire.md) Knights to the [Knights of the Flame](Lore_Knights_of_the_Flame.md), a tribute to Sir Byric's courageous and determined actions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pet-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOSB-2)</sup> A [monument](Lore_Statues.md) stood behind Alcaire Castle, honoring his heroic actions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOSB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AC-3)</sup>
