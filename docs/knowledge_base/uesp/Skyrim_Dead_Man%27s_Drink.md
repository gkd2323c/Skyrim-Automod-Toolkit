[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-place-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg) Dead Man's Drink Inn, Falkreath **Dead Man's Drink** is an [inn](Skyrim_Inns.md) in the town of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). It is owned by [Valga Vinicia](Skyrim_Valga_Vinicia.md). It is located near the center of town, opposite [Gray Pine Goods](Skyrim_Gray_Pine_Goods.md), next door to [Corpselight Farm](Skyrim_Corpselight_Farm.md). [Narri](Skyrim_Narri.md) waits tables, although she can regularly be found outside leaning against the porch fence, while [Delacourt](Skyrim_Delacourt.md) is the inn's resident [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md). It contains only one interior zone, *[Dead Man's Drink](#Dead_Man.27s_Drink)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is a single story building with a raised porch at the front, wooden stairs lead up to the entrance and a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel is in the corner. By the southern corner of the building is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and a couple of [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch) that can be harvested nearby. Many of Falkreath's citizens congregate in the inn throughout the day and night.

### Dead Man's Drink
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Drink.jpg) Inside Dead Man's Drink It is a typical Nord inn, with a firepit down the middle of the common room. The counter is at the southwest end, with five long wooden tables along the southeast and northwest walls. There are three doorways leading to bedrooms, two on the southeast side and a larger one on the northwest side. There is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) in the eastern corner and several barrels around the room. Behind the counter are two large vats with racks hanging from the ceiling in front of them. These hold nine [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and six [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), also hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). These are in front of a set of shelves holding bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). On the counter are several pieces of cheese, a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine. Under the counter is a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Dance in Fire, v6](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v6)*, five loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md), a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe) and several more bottles of wine and alto wine.

Either side of the doorway into the larger bedroom are a set of shelves holding several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), six bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine, seven bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and three bunches of elves ear. The long wooden table against the walls typically hold bread and a selection of wine and alto wine. However the middle table against the northwest wall also holds two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) and the table on your right as you enter, next to the alchemy lab, also holds a selection of cheeses and two salmon steaks. There is a basket containing three pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) beside the firepit.

The larger bedroom is the room you will be shown to if renting a room for the night. It consists of a large double bed coming out from the northwest wall, with end tables holding a common book ([Mysterious Altar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mysterious_Altar) with the [Bittercup](Skyrim_Bittercup.md) Creation installed) and bottle of wine either side. At the foot of the bed is an unlocked non-respawning wooden [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing gold, and against the northeast wall is a long unlocked non-respawning empty chest. There is also a small square wooden table and chair against the southeast wall holding a medium coin purse and four loose septims.

The two smaller bedrooms are opposite the larger bedroom. To the left of the left-hand doorway is a flower basket containing three [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). The left-hand bedroom contains a single owned bed in the eastern corner with an unlocked non-respawning chest and wardrobe at the foot of the bed and a small round wooden table and chair beside the bed. On the table is a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead). The right-hand bedroom has a single owned bed in the southern corner, with an unlocked non-respawning chest and dresser at the foot of the bed and a small round wooden table and chair beside it. Both these chests contain only gold as well.
