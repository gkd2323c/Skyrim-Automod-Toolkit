Daring Waterfront Raid Fails! *A newspaper article on Captain Hieronymus Lex's attempt to capture the Gray Fox* *
---
Stymied in his attempts to capture the legendary thief, the [Gray Fox](Lore_Gray_Fox.md), Captain Hieronymus Lex of the [Imperial Watch](Lore_Imperial_Watch.md) raided the [Waterfront](Lore_Waterfront_District.md). Extra Watchmen were pulled from duty in other parts of the city to search the slums of the Waterfront. A small amount of contraband was confiscated, but the Gray Fox escaped.

The [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md) has filed a formal complaint against Captain Hieronymus Lex for dereliction of duty. The guards normally posted at the Arcane University were sent to the Waterfront during the raid. An attempted break-in at the University was foiled. University spokesmen insist that nothing was taken. They scoffed at the idea that any mere thief could make off with one of their treasures.
