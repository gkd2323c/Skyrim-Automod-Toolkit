*Enchanting description:* **Increases Disease Resistance by <mag>%.**

**Resist Disease** decreases by <mag>% your chances of contracting a [disease](Skyrim_Disease.md).

Resist Disease 100% provides disease immunity (though see [bugs](#Bugs)); values above 100% provide no additional benefit.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Resist Disease if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- [Necklace of Disease Immunity](Skyrim_Necklace_of_Disease_Immunity.md)
- [Necklace of Disease Resistance](Skyrim_Necklace_of_Disease_Resistance.md)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Gauntlets of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader) and [Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>
- [Hevnoraak](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(item).md)
- [Wraithguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wraithguard)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md)</sup>

## Powers
- [Argonians](Skyrim_Argonian.md) and [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) both have a constant Resist Disease 50% ability.
- Becoming a [werewolf](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) or a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) gives you a constant Resist Disease 100% ability.

## Notes
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md) levels will reduce resistance to diseases.

## Bugs
- Resist Disease 100% does not prevent diseases from traps due to scripting oversights. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.5, fixes this bug.
