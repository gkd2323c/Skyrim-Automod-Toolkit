[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/AR-place-Chaseguard.jpg/200px-AR-place-Chaseguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Chaseguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Chaseguard.jpg) Chaseguard (Arena) Chaseguard (also called **Chasegard**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> is a town found in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), on the eastern shores of [Hew's Bay](Lore_Hew%27s_Bay.md) between the city-state of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) and [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md).

The town has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Chaseguard was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Shraj and had a rivalry with [Cartmont](Lore_Cartmont.md). Taverns in the village included the Black Sailor's, Devil's Saber, Dirty Mug, Rusty Ship, and Unfortunate Crow's Nest inns.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Chaseguard is neighbored by several settlements, including Rihad to the southeast, [Roseguard](Lore_Roseguard.md) to the north, and [Stonemoor](Lore_Stonemoor.md) to the east.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
