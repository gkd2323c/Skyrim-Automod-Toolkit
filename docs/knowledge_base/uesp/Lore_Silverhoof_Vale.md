[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/ON-place-Silverhoof_Vale.jpg/200px-ON-place-Silverhoof_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silverhoof_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silverhoof_Vale.jpg) Silverhoof Vale circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Silverhoof Vale, also called the Vale of Silverhoof, is a pastoral basin located on the northwest coast of the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) region of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md), some leagues west of the city of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md). It is inhabited by the [Silverhoof Horsemen](Lore_Silverhoof_Horsemen.md), [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) nomads known for breeding horses.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Horse Folk-1)</sup> The inhabitants of Silverhoof Vale live in caves in the side of the mountain. This keeps them from having to build shelter, and gives them protection against beasts such as [Harpies](Lore_Harpy.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bumnog-2)</sup>

## History
In the early sixth century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), during a period of upheaval in High Rock, the Horsemen arrived on Silverhoof Vale from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) and established themselves before they could be driven out or absorbed by the native peoples.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Horse Folk-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Horsemen's horses were attacked by harpies, causing them to hire a band of traveling fighters to deal with them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Elder-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RD-4)</sup>
