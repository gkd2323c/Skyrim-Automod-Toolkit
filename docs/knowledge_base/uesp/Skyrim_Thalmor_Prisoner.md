[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-npc-Thalmor_Prisoner.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thalmor_Prisoner.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thalmor_Prisoner.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thalmor_Prisoner.jpg) Thalmor Prisoner The **Thalmor Prisoner** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) who has been captured by [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md) soldiers. He is being escorted by a [Justiciar](Skyrim_Justiciar.md) and two [Thalmor soldiers](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md).

You have the option to free them, and even share items after they are freed. If they are freed, you can escort them back to Windhelm, where he waits inside the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md).

He wears a [roughspun tunic](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003c9fe) and carries nothing.

## Dialogue
They have one line of dialogue, no matter if you free him or not:

- *"If you know any true sons and daughters of Skyrim, tell them to head to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) wants to see them."*
