The Exclusionary Mandates *The dictates of the Marukhati Selective*
---
The Exclusionary Mandates of [Maruhkite Selection](Lore_Maruhkati_Selective.md): All Are Equal

1: That the Supreme Spirit [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) is of unitary essence, as proven by the monolinearity of Time.

1: That [Shezarr](Lore_Shezarr.md) the missing sibling is Singularly Misplaced and therefore Doubly Venerated.

1: That the protean substrate that informs all denial of (1) is the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) Taint.

1: That the [Prophet Most Simian](Lore_Marukh.md) demonstrated that monothought begets Proper-Life.

1: That the purpose of Proper-Life is the Expungement of the Taint.

1: That the Arc of Time provides the mortal theater for the [Sacred Expungement](Lore_Dragon_Break.md).

1: That Akatosh is Time is Proper-Life is Taint-Death.
