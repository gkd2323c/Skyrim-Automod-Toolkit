## Catapult Cabbage

When it feels threatened, it will lash out with great force using its petals. The source of the ability comes from the animal-like structure hidden by the petals, which resembles a praying mantis with serrated limbs. The Catapult Cabbage is capable of knocking a full-grown [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) off their feet.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
