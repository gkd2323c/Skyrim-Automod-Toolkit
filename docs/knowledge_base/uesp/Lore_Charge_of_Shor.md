Charge of Shor **by [Frogirde](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frogirde)** *The onus of those of Shor's Stand*
---
When the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) formed our [realms](Lore_Mundus.md) from creatia, did [Shor](Lore_Shor.md) abandon his creations? No.
When the foreign [gods](Lore_Gods.md) doomed Shor to the underworld, did he go quietly? No.
When the [sun](Lore_Sun.md) threatened to still beyond the horizon, did Shor leave us in the darkness? No.

When all seems dark, spark a fire.
When others seek to decide your fate, stand firm.
When all else fails, do not go to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md) without a fight.
This is Shor's charge. This is what makes Shor's Stand.

Frogirde
