Coral Aerie Temporal Tome **by [Varallion](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Varallion)** *A pamphlet from an alternate reality where the Ascendant Order won*
---
Hail now the [Ascendant Lord](Lore_Ascendant_Lord.md), eternal ruler of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)!

Citizens of all nations, you have seen the glory that is our Ascendant Lord in all his finery. [The Dream of Kasorayn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Dream_of_Kasorayn) made manifest, as the furious power of fire and brimstone smote the false kings and queens in their castles.

As volcanoes continue to belch forth their retribution, I know the skies might darken and the days grow cold. But stoke the fire of hope in your heart, for the Ascendant Lord now stands triumphant. You will be borne up by his glorious hand to a better tomorrow.

Even now the Knights of the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md), the faithful [Druids of Galen](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md), and the [Society of the Steadfast](Lore_Society_of_the_Steadfast.md) emissaries spread across the continent to bring aid and succor to those displaced by the firestorms. All who have need will receive help from these forces. And the Lord himself will soon follow! I have spoken with him in his new domain atop [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md), seat of the Ivy Throne.

Your Lord and Master knows that this time of change and turmoil has been hard on the citizens of Tamriel. He wishes to spread a clear message: Your day has come. Hardship now, yes. Challenges in the days ahead. Certainly. But no longer will tyrants kneel upon your necks. No longer will armies smash through townships. No longer will distant trumpets herald death and carnage.

The day of the [druid](Lore_Druids.md), of the Ascendant Lord, has come. And it will be a glorious day for all. Keep hope alive and your Lord will lift you up. And I will be there by his side.

Faithfully,
Varallion
