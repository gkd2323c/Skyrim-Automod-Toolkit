# Elden Accord

—Aicantar of Shimmerene<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTNAI-1)</sup> The Elden Accord was a treaty signed by [Queen Ayrenn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayrenn) of the [Kingdom of Alinor](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md), [King Aeradan Camoran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aeradan_Camoran) of the [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md), and Lord Gharesh-ri of the [Elsweyr Confederacy](Lore_Elsweyr.md), which resulted in the formation of the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ayrenn-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shazah-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TCC-4)</sup>

In the midst of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), Queen Ayrenn was aware of the dire threats menacing the world and saw it as the proper duty of the Elves to put things right.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AUQ-5)</sup> At the same time, the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) were in the midst of a civil war and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) were facing the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md). Both petitioned the Kingdom of Alinor for aid, and their call was answered. In return Queen Ayrenn asked the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) to stand by her side as invading armies moved towards southwest Tamriel, to which they agreed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TEATC-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Gelthior-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Gharesh-ri-8)</sup>
