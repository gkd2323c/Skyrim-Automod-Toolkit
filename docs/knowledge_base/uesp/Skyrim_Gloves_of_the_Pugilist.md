[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-item-Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.jpg) Gloves of the Pugilist The Gloves of the Pugilist are unique gloves located inside of the sewer tunnels beneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) known as [The Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md). Specifically, the Gloves of the Pugilist are found on the appropriately named [Gian the Fist](Skyrim_Gian_the_Fist.md), who uses them for their unique [fortify unarmed damage](Skyrim_Fortify_Unarmed_Damage.md) enchantment, which increases the damage unarmed strikes do by ten points. The gloves themselves share their appearance with [fur gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Gauntlets), along with its base stats. Unlike fur gauntlets, the improvements available through the use of a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) require two [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md), and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, versus just a single piece of leather.

## Notes
- Unlike most unique items, the Gloves of the Pugilist can be disenchanted.
- The Gloves of the Pugilist also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Legendary_Encounters) in [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Skyrim_-_The_Adventure_Game).
