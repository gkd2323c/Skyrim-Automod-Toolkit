[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Karth River Forest is a pine forest region in the southern area of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is one of the three regions of the Hold, surrounded primarily by the [Haafingar Mountains](Lore_Haafingar_Mountains.md), and a small part meets the [Giant's Coast](Lore_Giant%27s_Coast.md) north of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). It also makes up the northwest corner of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> where it borders the [Tundra Plain](Lore_Tundra_Plain.md) to the south, [Hailstone Valley](Lore_Hailstone_Valley.md) to the east, and [Drajkmyr Marsh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Drajkmyr_Marsh) to the northeast.
