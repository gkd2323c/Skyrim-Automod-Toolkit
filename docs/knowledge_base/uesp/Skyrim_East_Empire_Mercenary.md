[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-npc-East_Empire_Mercenary.jpg/200px-SR-npc-East_Empire_Mercenary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Mercenary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Mercenary.jpg) An East Empire Mercenary **East Empire Mercenaries** are the four [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldiers](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) found on the ship you take to [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md) during the quest [Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md). After you have helped [Orthus Endario](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario) by finding information on pirate attacks which have affected the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md)'s business, you'll be approached by [Adelaisa Vendicci](Skyrim_Adelaisa_Vendicci.md), the captain of the East Empire mercenaries. She will request that you accompany the crew to Japhet's Folly to help defeat the pirates.

## Quest-Related Events

### Rise in the East
When you come to the docks for the voyage, one of the East Empire mercenaries will ask you:

- *"You're the one who's coming to Japhet's Folly with us, right? You ready to go?"*

- - **Let's go.**
- *"All aboard!"*

- - **Not just yet.**
- *"Well, hurry it up!"*
