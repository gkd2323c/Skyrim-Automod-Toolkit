## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to [the Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum) and read [*Soran's Journal*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal).
2. Locate and [tame](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC) the [Unicorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn) just west of [Lost Prospect Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Prospect_Mine).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### First Named, Last Tamed
Upon installing the [Wild Horses](Skyrim_Wild_Horses.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), you will be directed to [the Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum) in the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) to learn more about reports of a recent [Unicorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn) sighting. Once there, you will find [*Soran's Journal*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal) sitting on [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md)'s desk. It details the research on Unicorns carried out by a wildlife scholar named Soran Hariksen, who sighted a Unicorn in the forests of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). The creature is supposedly from the distant past and was brought forward in time by the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) after the [last living Unicorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Unicorn) was sacrificed to [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) during his [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Hircine) in [*Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion).

Once you have read the journal, you will be directed to an area just west of the entrance to [Lost Prospect Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Prospect_Mine). The Unicorn can be found right next to the nearby pond. It is a [wild horse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse) and will run away from you if approached. It must be [tamed](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC), which is a mechanic added by the Creation. You must mount the Unicorn until it stops bucking in order to claim it as your own. You may be thrown off when it bucks, which will necessitate chasing it down again once you get back on your feet.

### Reward
Once tamed, the Unicorn will become your mount. If you decide to switch to a different mount, it will make its way to the College of Winterhold courtyard and wait for you there.

## Notes
- Like other horses, the Unicorn can be [named](Skyrim_Horses.md#Horse_Naming CC) and [saddled](Skyrim_Horses.md#Saddles CC), which are two other mechanics added by this Creation. To do this, you must visit a [stable](Skyrim_Stables.md) with the Unicorn as your active mount.
