Space space. Me, I call me treasure.*
—A Space Core<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Him-1)</sup> Space Cores, also known as **Space Spheres** or **Spheriphem**, are [moonstone](Lore_Moonstone.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AF-2)</sup> [constructs](Lore_Golem.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Summary-3)</sup> native to [space](Lore_Astronomy.md), the void above [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Load-4)</sup> They are capable of speech and possess a single eye.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Him-1)</sup> Similar to [Aetherial fragments](Lore_Aetherial_Fragments.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Space Cores occasionally fall to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), although the experience is apparently unsettling for them. Fallen Spheriphem will therefore attempt to cajole passing adventurers into helping them return to the comforting void.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Load-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Him-1)</sup> Space Cores can be used in [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) rituals at the Atronach Forge in place of a [Sigil Stone](Lore_Sigil_Stone.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AF-2)</sup>

## History
*Okay, okay, Spaceborn. Yeah yeah I am the Spaceborn. As [foretold](Lore_Sight.md)... in space!*
—A Space Core speaking to the Last Dragonborn<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Him-1)</sup> Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) a Spheriphem allegedly fell to Tamriel, landing in dilapidated structure in [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). This Space Sphere was found by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), whom the Sphere would seemingly try to convince to return him to space. The Space Core mostly babbled insanely and endlessly about space, [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), and celestial bodies and organizations, many of which do not actually appear to exist in the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Him-1)</sup> It is unknown if the Last Dragonborn returned the being to its comforting void.

## Notes
- The canonicity of Space Cores can be debated, as they are actually a [*Portal 2*](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Portal_2) cameo featured in the mod [*Fall of the Space Core, Vol. 1*](Skyrim_Fall_of_the_Space_Core,_Vol._1.md) for [*The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim*](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). Although officially endorsed by [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda), the mod was co-created by [Valve](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Valve_Corporation).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Summary-3)</sup> According to [Cartogriffi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Andrew_Griffiths), a Community Content Manager at Bethesda, promotional items from other games are "sort of a category of their own" in terms of canonicity.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
