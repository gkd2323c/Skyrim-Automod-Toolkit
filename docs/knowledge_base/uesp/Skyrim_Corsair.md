# Corsair

**Corsairs** are hostile NPCs unique to the [Dainty Sload](Skyrim_Dainty_Sload.md), a pirate ship docked near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). They are under the command of the elusive Captain Volf, who is never encountered in-game.

They each carry a [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar) and a small amount of gold. They wear [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers), [Redguard boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Boots), and [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md). One sits at a table in the downstairs kitchen-like room with the corsair in the section above. The other is at the top, guarding the entrance to the interior of the ship.
