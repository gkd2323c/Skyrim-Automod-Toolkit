# Withershin

Withershins (from [Old Cyrodilic](Lore_Old_Cyrodilic.md) **withersynes**, meaning backwards) is a concept in [Cyrodilic](Lore_Imperial.md) tradition associated with reversed directionality.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VFTDB-2)</sup> An incantatory practice called **Withershin Therapy** is the art of reversing the direction of things in order to gain access to the spirit world to break curses, cure vampirism, or trigger any manner of apotropaic healing.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WS-1)</sup> It is possible to become a master of the withershin.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Loranna-3)</sup> Certain withershin spells, when cast, appear as burning sigils in the air, drawn in a backwards motion by the caster.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Loranna-3)</sup>
