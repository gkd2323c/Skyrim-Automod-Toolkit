[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-npc-Jonquil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jonquil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jonquil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jonquil.jpg) Jonquil [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/SR-npc-Jonquil_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jonquil_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jonquil_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jonquil_02.jpg) Jonquil's ghost **Jonquil** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) added by the [Farming](Skyrim_Farming.md) creation. She is found dead in the secret basement of the main house of [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md), with the Woodcutter's Axe that killed her still embedded in her chest. [Her journal](Skyrim_Jonquil%27s_Journal.md) on the alchemy table details the events leading up to her death, and her falling out with her husband, [Urval](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urval), which led them to kill one another. Her [ghost](Skyrim_Undead.md#Ghost) will be encountered soon after you discover her body, and must be defeated.

She wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) and her body holds an iron dagger on it.

At the end of the quest, her ghost will appear along with the ghosts of Urval and their son Rin, the family reunited and reconciled at last. After thanking you, they will pass on to the afterlife and leave the plantation to you. When the quest is complete, Jonquil's body will despawn.
