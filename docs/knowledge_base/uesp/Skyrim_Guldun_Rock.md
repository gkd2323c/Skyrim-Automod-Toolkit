## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Guldun_Rock_02.jpg) Two mammoth cheese bowls The camp is off the road that follows the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) east from Whiterun to [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md), directly south of [Valtheim Towers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valtheim_Towers). A path leading from the road is marked by a stone cairn surrounded by two [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) shrubs and several [blue](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [purple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), and [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md). Near the first bend in the path is a leveled [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) that will normally be alerted to your passing. The path leads to a wide flight of stairs up to the plateau with the camp.

Two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) occupy the camp. Several painted stones are on your right as you approach the camp; between two of these is a fire-hardened stake with bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) hanging from it. Nine [mammoth cheese bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl) and many bones and skulls are scattered around the camp. The campfire is north of *Guldun Rock Cave* up a flight of stone stairs on top of a ruined stone building. It provides a good vantage point to view quite a distance to the north. North of the campfire, near two [orichalcum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein), is a leveled animal.

### Guldun Rock Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guldun_Rock_Cave.jpg) Guldun Rock Cave The cave is typically unoccupied. Two patches of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) mushrooms grow within the cave. As you enter, there is a central rock pillar with painted stones and a mammoth cheese bowl around it. On a flat rock to the west is a dead [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) with three [charred skeever hides](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) under it and another mammoth cheese bowl beside it. A large campfire is on the other side of the central pillar with a dead [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) to the west, while to the south is another flat rock with two [coin purses](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) (one large, one medium), a random filled [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), six loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Knights of the Nine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Knights_of_the_Nine)* on top and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) behind. Northeast of the central pillar is a second fire built from carefully stacked wood of the type usually used by giants to cook larger animals like [horses](Skyrim_Horses.md) and cows.
