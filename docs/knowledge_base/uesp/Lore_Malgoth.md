General Malgoth was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) who led the Blackroot Clan and attempted to unite all the Bosmer under his leadership and conquer [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOIndaenir-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Malgoth-2)</sup> Malgoth traveled throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and saw the greatness that came to those who conquered through military might, a status that he hoped to achieve.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOIndaenir-1)</sup> Malgoth led the Blackroot Clan during the Bosmer tribal wars. Malgoth and his clan eventually set their sights on the settlement of [Vullain](Lore_Dread_Vullain.md), intending to make an example of them for the other villages, with the goal of uniting the Bosmer to strengthen them against their enemies, the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOIndaenir-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Malgoth-2)</sup>

Word eventually reached the inhabitants of Vullain that Malgoth intended to march on Vullain. With no way to match the military might of the Blackroot Clan, the villagers turned to the local [nature guardians](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md), the [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md). Knowing that Malgoth and his clan would adhere to the [Meat Mandate](Lore_Green_Pact.md), the villagers convinced the Nereids to provide them with elixirs to poison their bodies to cause the deaths of the Blackroot warriors when they consumed their corpses.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Elphirion-3)</sup>

As expected, Malgoth and his army easily defeated the inhabitants of Vullain and consumed their remains per the Meat Mandate. Eventually realizing the trap they had fallen into, Malgoth himself pleaded with the Nereids for a cure to the poison, but they ignored him.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Malgoth-2)</sup> Malgoth and the Blackroot Clan soon succumbed to the poison.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOIndaenir-1)</sup> Following their deaths, Malgoth and his people became bound to the [Ooze](Lore_Ooze.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOMalgoth-4)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the site of Dread Vullain was visited by the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md). One of the servants of the Shade of Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md) resurrected the Blackroot Clan, forcing them to serve him through the [Rite of Theft](Lore_Bosmer.md#The_Rite_of_Theft) by stealing Malgoth's bow. An [agent](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), along with the mage [Indaenir](Lore_Indaenir.md), traversed Dread Vullain and uncovered the mystery surrounding the site. Once the two found Malgoth's remains, they were brought to the [Shadow Wood](Lore_Shadow_Wood.md), a corrupted form of Valenwood created by the Shade of Naemon. It was there that the two acquired the bow of Malgoth. The agent invoked the Rite of Theft, putting Malgoth's forces at their command. Whether the agent chose to use the clan in the battle against Naemon or released them is unknown; regardless, Dread Vullain was liberated from Naemon.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
