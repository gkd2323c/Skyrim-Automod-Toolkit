[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-npc-Leifnarr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Leifnarr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leifnarr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leifnarr.jpg) Leifnarr **Leifnarr** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who can be found dead in [Broken Helm Hollow](Skyrim_Broken_Helm_Hollow.md). [Grosta](Skyrim_Grosta.md) at [Heartwood Mill](Skyrim_Heartwood_Mill.md) will ask you to find him.

Leifnarr wears randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance he may carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Flight or Fight](Skyrim_Flight_or_Fight.md)**: Help [Grosta](Skyrim_Grosta.md) at [Heartwood Mill](Skyrim_Heartwood_Mill.md) locate her missing husband.

## Bugs
- If you find Leifnarr without getting the quest first, it may not update when you speak to Grosta, making it unable to be completed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can fix it by using console command `setstage e3ea5 30` .
- Despite being named Leifnarr by his wife and the related quest, his corpse will be labeled as an unnamed "Nord". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
