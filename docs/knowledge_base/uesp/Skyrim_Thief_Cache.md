# Thief Cache

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-misc-Thief_Cache.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Thief_Cache.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Thief_Cache.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Thief_Cache.jpg) Thief Cache outside [Mistveil Keep Barracks](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep_Barracks.md) **Thief caches** are barrels that appear in various places around Tamriel as you progress in the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest line. They often appear near other barrels, but can easily be distinguished from them by the Thieves' Cache [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) ([](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-shadowmark-Cache.png)) on their side. The first three appear in Riften after you first join the Thieves Guild. After that, three barrels appear in each of the appropriate cities as you complete the various [reputation quests](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Reputation_quests).

The barrels contain significant loot and will respawn if the player leaves the town for at least 10 days completely. If entered within that period, the timer will revert to zero. This includes the one in Glover Mallory's house.

An additional barrel is available with the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on.

## Locations

### [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)
1. In the northwest corner of the garden behind [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md).
2. In the northwest corner on the walkway outside the second floor of [Mistveil Keep Barracks](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep_Barracks.md).
3. West of [Valindor's House](Skyrim_Valindor%27s_House.md), across the canal.

### [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md)
1. Beside a pillar down the stairs between [Nepos's House](Skyrim_Nepos%27s_House.md) and [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md).
2. At the base of the northernmost waterfall below [Ghorza](Skyrim_Ghorza_gra-Bagol.md)'s smithy. Only the top is visible; the rest is submerged.
3. Immediately outside [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md).

### [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)
1. Just off the pathway behind [Vittoria Vici's House](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md).
2. Behind [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), to the north.
3. Standing upright beside the other barrels next to the door to [Erikur's House](Skyrim_Erikur%27s_House.md).

### [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)
1. Behind [Carlotta Valentia's House](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia%27s_House.md).
2. In the farmyard next to [House Gray-Mane](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md).
3. Behind [The Bannered Mare](Skyrim_The_Bannered_Mare.md).

### [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)
1. Outside [Niranye's House](Skyrim_Niranye%27s_House.md).
2. Beside the door to [Sadri's Used Wares](Skyrim_Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.md).
3. In the alcove north of the door to [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md).

### [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
1. In the basement of [Glover Mallory's House](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md) at the end of the [Paid in Full](Skyrim_Paid_in_Full.md) quest.

## Notes
- Once your [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Prowler%27s_Profit.md) ability is active, all of the caches will now be likely to include a considerable number of precious gems.
- The contents of the caches are leveled, so the higher your level, the greater the quality.
- Looting the caches and selling their contents can be quite profitable, as all fences have 4,000 gold each, (except for [Enthir](Skyrim_Enthir.md), who only has 500), meaning there is a substantial amount of money to be made.
- You can change the contents of the caches by saving before visiting any of the towns listed above, as the game will have the items fixed once you enter. By doing so, you can potentially gain access to rare gems and/or powerful enchanted daggers.
- The Shadowmark for a "Thieves' Cache" exists in [Irkngthand](Skyrim_Irkngthand.md) on an alternative pipe route in the Falmer Slave Pens on the stone wall you need to jump down from, though only a leveled healing potion and a leveled fortify carry weight potion will be there.
- Another "Thieves' Cache" sign can be found on the right side of [Ysolda's House](Skyrim_Ysolda%27s_House.md) behind 2 barrels and a pile of firewood, but the game doesn't consider it a real "Cache".
- Unlike all other thief caches, the one in Glover Mallory's basement doesn't include a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md).
