[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Skjoldr Wolf-Runner was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) in the fifth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

He became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying the wizard Griss the Yellow and presenting his head as a battle trophy. He was chieftain for three years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAHR-2)</sup>

He was one of the victims of the Mead Hall Massacre when the [Udyrfrykte](Lore_Udefrykte.md) attacked the mead hall. He was then succeeded by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) who slew the troll, brought its heart as a trophy, and restored the mead hall with the help of Svenja Snow-Song.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
