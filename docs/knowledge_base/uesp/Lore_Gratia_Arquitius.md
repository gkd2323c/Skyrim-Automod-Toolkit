# Gratia Arquitius

| --- | --- | --- | --- |

Gratia Arquitius was a legendary chef that lived during the time of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) known for her Colovian War Torte recipe, a robust yet lavish dessert designed to facilitate camaraderie among [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) during their war marches.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-1)</sup> Known ingredients include [nutmeg](Lore_Nutmeg.md), citrus,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-1)</sup> but flour, [carrots](Lore_Carrot.md), [frost mirriam](Lore_Frost_Mirriam.md), and [honey](Lore_Honey.md) have also been known to be used.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONCWT-2)</sup> She elevated the dessert into the Molten War Torte by adding Rubyblossom Extract, a rare and robust sweetener once cultivated by [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenese.md) [alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMWT-3)</sup> The final evolution to her confectionary creation was the White-Gold War Torte, achieved by adding rare Mourning Dew on a Molten War Torte.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONWWT-4)</sup>

Her torte recipe was misplaced by her in the wilds of Cyrodiil, and subsequently utilized by the alliances participating in the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONCWT-2)</sup> The legacy of Gratia Arquitiu lasts much longer than the Interregnum of the Second Era. [Urzhag-gro Larak](Lore_Urzhag_gro-Larak.md), apprentice to the famous Gourmet, [Balagog gro-Nolob](Lore_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md), recounts in his book *Tastes and Tales from Tamriel* how sharing a Colovian War Torte helped connect him with two total strangers. Stuck in a cave in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) duiring a blizzard, Urzhag sharing his torte with the strangers led to them joking and conversing like they had known each other for years, when earlier they had been suspicious of each other.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-1)</sup>
