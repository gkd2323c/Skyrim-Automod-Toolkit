[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Kyne%27s Token.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Kyne%27s Token.png) Kyne's Token Kyne's Token is an [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md) worn by [Froki Whetted-Blade](Skyrim_Froki_Whetted-Blade.md). It can be obtained either as a reward for his quest, [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md), or by killing or pickpocketing him. In appearance, this amulet is identical to an [ancient Nord amulet](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Amulet.md). The token's enchantment reduces the damage you take from creatures (including Daedra, undead, etc) by 10%, while also [increasing your bow damage](Skyrim_Fortify_Archery.md) by 5%.

## Related Quests
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

## Notes
- if you use a [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) potion (found or custom-made) before equipping the necklace, only the Fortify Archery effect is boosted; the reduction in damage from animals is not, staying at %10.
