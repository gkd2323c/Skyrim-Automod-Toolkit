Statue of Justia Desticus *A plaque honoring an Order of the Hour knight*
---
The original First Sword of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md), [Justia Desticus](Lore_Justia_Desticus.md) stood by [Cavor](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md)'s side and devised the Rigors of Akatosh, eight traits to which all members must aspire. She is considered the second in devotion in [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md)'s eyes.
