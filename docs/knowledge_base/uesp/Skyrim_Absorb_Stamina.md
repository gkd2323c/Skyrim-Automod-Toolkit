*Enchanting description:* **Absorb <mag> points of stamina.**

**Absorb Stamina** (a.k.a. **Drain Stamina**) transfers <mag> points of [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) from the target to the person wielding the weapon. On the target, the effect is comparable to [Damage Stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md); on the attacker, the effect is comparable to [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md).

Two versions of Absorb Stamina are actually available for enchanting. The stats above are for the most common version of the effect. A second version is only available from one item: the [Drainheart Sword](Skyrim_Drainheart_Sword.md). The sword can be disenchanted, meaning that its effect can be used for custom enchantments. The stats for this second version of the effect are:

- ID: 0010f21f
- Cost: 1.2

## Notes
- In Skyrim, there appears to be no distinction between "Drain Stamina" and "Absorb Stamina".
- Even a single point of stamina drain on a weapon allows unlimited power attacks, because of how stamina works in Skyrim.
- Absorb Stamina does not work when attacking [Dwarven automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).
