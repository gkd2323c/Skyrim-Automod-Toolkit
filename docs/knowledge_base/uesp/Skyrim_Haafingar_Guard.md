[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-npc-Haafingar_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Haafingar_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haafingar_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haafingar_Guard.jpg) Haafingar Guard **Haafingar Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) that can be found patrolling the region of [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md). They can be found on [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) and [Dragon's Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md).

Haafingar Guards wear [Solitude Guard's Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Guard%27s_Armor), Solitude Guard's Helmet, [Imperial Boots](Skyrim_Steel.md#Imperial_Armor), and Imperial Bracers. They carry a Solitude Guard's Shield, an [Imperial Bow](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Imperial_Soldier_Weapons), and an Imperial Sword.

## Notes
Haafingar Guards carry the same equipment as [Solitude Guards](Skyrim_Solitude_Guard.md), as [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) is in the Haafingar region.
