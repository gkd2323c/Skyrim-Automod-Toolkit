Vald's Debt **by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)** *A loan agreement* *
---
This document absolves [Vald](Skyrim_Vald.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) (borrower) from all debts and accounts owed to [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) of Riften (lender). This document should be held in a secured location until such time as the lender considers the debt satisfied. In the event of the borrower's death, the debt will be collected from any remaining assets or property owned as determined by the acting Steward of Riften.
