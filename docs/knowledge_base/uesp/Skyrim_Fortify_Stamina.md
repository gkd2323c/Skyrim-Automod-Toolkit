*Alchemy description:* **Stamina is increased by <mag> points for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases your stamina by <mag> points.**
**Fortify Stamina** increases the target's total [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md). However, this does not alter the target's [carry weight](Skyrim_Carry_Weight.md).

The rare built-in [Potions "of Enhanced Stamina"](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina) increase stamina for 5 minutes (300 seconds). Most Alchemy potions last 1 minute (60 seconds).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Stamina if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Boots, Necklaces, and Rings *[of Stamina](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Stamina)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Stamina*, *of Major Stamina*, *of Extreme Stamina*, *of Eminent Stamina*, and *of Peerless Stamina.*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Amulet of Kynareth](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Kynareth.md)
- [Gauldur Amulet Fragment](Skyrim_Gauldur_Amulet_Fragment.md)
- [Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw](Skyrim_Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md)</sup>
- [Her Hand Boots](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Boots.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
- [Locket of Saint Jiub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locket_of_Saint_Jiub)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>
- [Lynea's Amulet of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynea%27s_Amulet_of_Dibella)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

[Linwe's Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Linwe%27s_Armor) can technically be disenchanted, allowing you to learn a special version of Fortify Stamina named **Shadowthrive**. However, Shadowthrive cannot be used to enchant any items because no enchanting slots are enabled for the effect.

## Blessings
- The [Blessing of Kynareth](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Kynareth.md) provides 25 points of additional Stamina for 8 hours.
- The [Blessing of the Stag Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_the_Stag_Prince) provides 5 points of Stamina while the [Glass Bow of the Stag Prince](Skyrim_Glass_Bow_of_the_Stag_Prince.md) is equipped, as well as an additional 5 points for every 20 animals killed with the bow (up to a maximum of 25 points).<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- The [Elytra's Uplifting Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elytra%27s_Uplifting_Aura) provides 10 points of Stamina for 5 minutes.<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>

## Food
The following food items provide a temporary version of this effect:

- [Beef Stew](Skyrim_Beef_Stew.md) 25 pts for 720 seconds
- [Creamy Crab Bisque](Skyrim_Creamy_Crab_Bisque.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> 25 pts for 720 seconds

## Notes
- The various [Rally](Skyrim_Rally_(effect).md) spells ([Call to Arms](Skyrim_Call_to_Arms.md), [Courage](Skyrim_Courage.md), and [Rally](Skyrim_Rally_(spell).md)) include a secondary Fortify Stamina effect.
