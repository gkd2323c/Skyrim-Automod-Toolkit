# Vlindrel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/ON-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg) Vlindrel Hall in 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Vlindrel_Hall.jpg) Vlindrel Hall in 4E 201 The Vlindrels are a great and ancient noble merchant family of [Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md) origin, active in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TKON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lathon-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:502</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/OB-interior-Underpall_Cave.jpg/200px-OB-interior-Underpall_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Underpall_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Underpall_Cave.jpg) Underpall Keep [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/OB-creature-Lord_Vlindrel.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Lord_Vlindrel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Lord_Vlindrel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Lord_Vlindrel.jpg) Lord Vlindrel's spirit The precise origins of the Vlindrels remain unknown, but the name Vlindrel had already been used by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), associated with a notable estate in the city of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), the Vlindrel Hall. During that time, the estate was inhabited by a wealthy and influential [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) family, including Deantenna, who was known for acquiring luxurious goods from various regions of Tamriel. The connection between these Reachfolk and the Colovian Vlindrels is unknown.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VB-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VHO-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Deantenna-6)</sup>

The noble family was active during the early Third Era, around the time of the establishment of the [Knights of the Nine](Lore_Knights_of_the_Nine.md) in [3E 111](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_111). Throughout the Third Era, the [Underpall Keep](Lore_Underpall_Cave.md) served as the ancestral tomb for the Vlindrels,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-UC-7)</sup> where Lord Berich Vlindrel, a tyrannical ruler and former Knight of the Nine, was laid to rest alongside the stolen [Greaves and Sword of the Crusader](Lore_Crusader%27s_Relics.md), revered artifacts of [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SAJ-8)</sup> However, Lord Vlindrel's malevolent spirit, wielding the corrupted Sword of the Crusader, haunted the [Orange Road](Lore_Orange_Road.md), unable to join his fellow Knights at the [Priory of the Nine](Lore_Priory_of_the_Nine.md). In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) eventually put his spirit to rest by recovering the Sword of the Crusader and restoring its sanctity at the Great Chapel of Arkay in Cheydinhal, thereby ending his tormented existence,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lathon-2)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Quest-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SB-10)</sup> allowing him to join his fellow knights.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SC-11)</sup>

During the late years of the Third Era, Lord Vlindrel's Tower, which had once stood southwest of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), lay in ruins, its original purpose was unknown.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Lord_Vlindrel.27s_Tower-12)</sup>

Following the [Reachmen Rebellion](Lore_Forsworn_Rebellion.md) in [4E 174](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_174), the Vlindrels of Markarth were forcibly expelled, and their once illustrious mansion has remained vacant ever since.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:502</sup> However, a few years later, in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the opportunity to acquire the property arose for those who could demonstrate loyalty to the Jarl of Markarth.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SVH-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-G-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Quests-15)</sup>
