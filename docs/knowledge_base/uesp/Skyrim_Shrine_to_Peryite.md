[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-place-Shrine_To_Peryite.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrine_To_Peryite.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_To_Peryite.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrine_To_Peryite.jpg) Shrine to Peryite The **Shrine to Peryite** is the [shrine](Skyrim_Daedric_Shrines.md) to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Only Cure](Skyrim_The_Only_Cure.md)**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](Skyrim_Orchendor.md) for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

## Walkthrough
The shrine is northeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), west of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md). Anyone traveling to the shrine from Markarth would be advised to take the path to the north of [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md), then take the right fork near [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md), as there is no direct route from Karthwasten to the shrine. A [silver ore vein](Skyrim_Silver_Ore_Vein.md) is down the hill to the west, and an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is near the ancient totem to the southeast, just below and east of the nearby [juniper](Skyrim_Juniper.md) tree at the base of the small outcropping. Farther east toward Karthwasten, near the edge of the hill above, is another iron ore vein.

The shrine is relatively humble. There is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) next to a large rock, with a long wooden table to the right, and a campfire to the right of the table. A [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) is hanging over the fire, as is an unused cooking pot that contains an [alchemy ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). On the table are a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Buying Game](Skyrim_The_Buying_Game.md)*, a [scroll of Heal Other](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Heal_Other.md), and a metal pot containing a [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md), an [abecean longfin](Skyrim_Abecean_Longfin.md), a [silverside perch](Skyrim_Silverside_Perch.md), and a filled [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). There is also an odd-looking golden cauldron in front of a withered tree to the south; the tree is wrapped in sickly green roots, with three clusters of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) fungus along with several human and animal skulls around the trunk. The cauldron is used in the ceremony to contact Peryite during the related quest.

Whereas most Daedric Princes have a statue, the shrine to Peryite is the tree, which is flanked by three large upright stones. The only worshipper of Peryite at the shrine is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) named [Kesh the Clean](Skyrim_Kesh_the_Clean.md). Kesh owns all of the items on the table, and will be displeased if he sees you taking any of the items even after you complete the [quest](Skyrim_The_Only_Cure.md) for his master. Kesh spends all of his time either cooking or working at the alchemy lab. He doesn't seem to sleep, sit, or even eat, as there is no furniture, bedding, or [food](Skyrim_Food.md) in the area.

The shrine is positioned on the top of a cliff above Karthwasten. At the base of the cliff itself, beneath a withered tree to the southeast, lies a natural recess with an interesting [easter egg](Skyrim_Easter_Eggs.md#William_Shakespeare) and some good loot. This is an unmarked location. Two random [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), a random enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md), a [diamond](Skyrim_Diamond.md), and two [salt piles](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md) lie at the base of an ancient stone totem. The top of the totem has two carved human faces facing east and west, and two dragons with straightened tails facing north and south. Shackles hang from the tail of the northern dragon. Given its proximity to the shrine and the fact that Peryite's symbol is a dragon, it can be inferred that the ancient totem has something to do with the Daedric Prince. A [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) corpse and some bloody human body parts lie beneath the totem. There is also an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) on the south side of the totem containing nice leveled loot, which seems to always include a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skill book *[Incident at Necrom](Skyrim_Incident_at_Necrom.md)*. Farther south, just up the hill and behind the rocks, lies a lootable [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) (easily accessible when descending from the shrine). A few paces westward up the hill from the skeleton in a rocky crevice is another unmarked location, comprising a dead sabre cat on a patch of blood-splattered ground surrounded by a leveled weapon, a leveled enchanted [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), four arrows, and a knapsack containing some loot. A juniper tree lies just east of the totem, toward Karthwasten. A wild animal is prowling nearby, just to the north.
