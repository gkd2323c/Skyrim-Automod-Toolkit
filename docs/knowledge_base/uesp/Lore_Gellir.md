Gellir was a High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Little is known about him or his reign, except that he ruled at least several decades after [King Harald](Lore_Harald.md), whose reign ended in [1E 221](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_221). He led the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) to conquer several [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) city-states, including [Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mzulft), though the Dwemer would retake them about a century later.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IPo M-2)</sup>

Nordic chroniclers have ascribed his surprising success to inspired tactics and the blessings of [Shor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shor). Others assert the Dwemer were in a weakened state due to an ongoing civil war, which paved the way for Gellir's subsequent conquests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAW-1)</sup>
