#### Quarvish
[Quarvish](Lore_Quarvish.md) is an obscure entity present in the faith of the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) branch of the Cult of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md). In one of the texts, authored by [Venerable Uugus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Venerable_Uugus), a member of the cult active in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Quarvish is described as [man](Lore_Man.md) -demon. Trinimac, the Golden Champion rode the great [eagle](Lore_Eagle.md) [Zylvara](Lore_Zylvara.md) to his reckoning with Quarvish.
