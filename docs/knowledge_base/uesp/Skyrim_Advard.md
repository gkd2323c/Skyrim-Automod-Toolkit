[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-creature-Advard.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Advard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Advard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Advard.jpg) Advard **Advard** is a skeleton that can be found underwater, near a capsized rowboat just north of the [Wreck of the Winter War](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War). He is next to [Skeggr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeggr).

To the west of the capsized boat, you can find their small camp. On one of the crates there is a [fisherman's journal](Skyrim_Fisherman%27s_Journal_(camp).md).
