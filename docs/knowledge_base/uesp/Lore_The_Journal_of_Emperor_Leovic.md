The Journal of Emperor Leovic **by [Emperor Leovic](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Emperor_Leovic)** *The journal of Emperor Leovic from his final days*
---
My [father](Lore_Moricar.md) and my [grandfather](Lore_Durcorach.md) before me started all this, but I shall be the one to end it and reap the reward. If the bastard [Varen](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) doesn't show up and [kill me](Lore_Colovian_Revolt.md) first.

My father had this annex built beneath [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) to house the last of our secrets. A safe haven for others to find so they can carry out the work. If worse comes to worse, you understand.

Here are all the people who have at least a part of the puzzle, though only my steward and the leader of the [Order of the Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md) know the entire truth—and even they aren't privy to the changes I needed to make as the forces of the traitor Varen draw near.

The [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md) aren't ready yet. If they were, I'd use them to destroy Varen and save my [legacy](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md). But the time of the Ambitions is still a few years away.

I have one last trick up my sleeve. I'll use the shrine in this annex, the one my father personally dedicated to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), to appeal to the Prince of Destruction and seek his help again, as my grandfather did when he conquered the [Empire](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md). If I fail, and if I fall to the traitor Varen, then you, as the [High Priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Councilor_Vandacia) of the Order of the Waking Flame, will have to deal with the others and determine where I moved the Ambitions. I could not let them fall into Varen's hands.

Steward Farrul Lupus
