# Torchbug

Torchbugs (also known as **fireflies**, **glowflies**, or **torchflies**) are small, nocturnal, glowing [beetles](Lore_Beetle.md) that are found all over Tamriel. They are prized for their [usefulness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Torchbug_Thorax) in crafting potions. Torchbugs are attracted to the nocturnal flowers that feed from [graht-oaks](Lore_Graht-oak.md), and often build their hives near those flowers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AA-1)</sup> Some [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) also believe that being followed by a friendly torchbug is a sign of good fortune.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DT-2)</sup>

The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) believe that crimson torchbugs are the light of [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) given form, and that they bless [moon-sugar](Lore_Moon-sugar.md) crops as they flit around them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CT-3)</sup> These crimson torchbugs are also known to thrive in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Purple torchbugs are often associated with [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), with some claiming that madness follows the purple torchbug, whereas others believe that those who once held Sheogorath's attention attract these insects.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Torchbugs are often used as bait for fishing in rivers.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Bosmer catch torchbugs and dry them out to be smoked in pipes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DT-2)</sup> Torchbugs can be encased in [amber](Lore_Amber.md) and enchanted to create a handy light source that fits in one's pocket.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> The glowing liquid from torchbugs is sometimes distilled to make hair tonics.
