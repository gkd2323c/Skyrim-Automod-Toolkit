# Imperial University

The **Imperial University** was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution based in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). It was active during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) and later during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOPO-1)</sup>

## Known Publications
- ***[A Scholar's Guide to Nymphs](Lore_A_Scholar%27s_Guide_to_Nymphs.md)*** by [Vondham Barres](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vondham_Barres) — One man's experience with Nymphs
- ***[Cherim's Heart of Anequina](Lore_Cherim%27s_Heart_of_Anequina.md)*** by [Livillus Perus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Livillus_Perus), Professor at the Imperial University — Interview with Cherim, a master tapestry weaver
- ***[Notes For Redguard History](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Notes_For_Redguard_History)*** by [Destri Melarg](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Destri_Melarg) — An unmailed letter from an author to his publisher
- ***[Notes on Racial Phylogeny](Lore_Notes_on_Racial_Phylogeny.md)*** by [the Council of Healers](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Council_of_Healers), Imperial University — About the similarities and differences between the races of Tamriel
- ***[Overview of Gods and Worship](Lore_Overview_of_Gods_and_Worship.md)*** by [Brother Hetchfield](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Brother_Hetchfeld) — Speculation concerning the worship of gods and their benefit from such
