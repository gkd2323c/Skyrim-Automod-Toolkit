# Fetcherfly

Fetcherflies are insects found on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and in the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Lone fetcherflies are harmless. However, when something approaches their nests, they will swarm and attack whatever they perceive as a threat. They emit a moderate amount of heat, singeing fur or hair on contact, and due to this heat, their nests often glow and have pores emitting smoke<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-flybook-2)</sup>. When the creatures swarm, their queen can tap her magma source to transform the nest into a hive [golem](Lore_Golem.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> which can walk around independently. A hive golem's purpose is to relocate the hive to new surroundings.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VFAF-4)</sup> These golems are attracted to the destruction of regular hives, and will seek out those responsible.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-flybook-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Fetcherflies are used by some [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) anglers as a lure.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Fetcherfly husks stuffed with sea sponge make passable pin cushions,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> and a puree of crushed fetcherflies can be used as an unorthodox ingredient in lotion.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
