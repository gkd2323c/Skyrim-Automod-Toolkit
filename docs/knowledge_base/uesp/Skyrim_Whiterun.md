[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg) Whiterun **Whiterun** is a major city in the center of Skyrim, and the capital of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). Its central location makes it the province's major commercial hub, as well as a crucial strategic point in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) between the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) loyalists and [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebels, as control of Whiterun Hold grants access to all surrounding lands. Whiterun is initially nominally aligned with the Empire, but [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) seems to care more about the people of Whiterun than either side of the conflict, rendering the hold effectively neutral.

Whiterun comprises three districts arranged in tiers and connected by stairways. The Plains District is the lowest and contains the city's marketplace and shops, inns, a few homes, and the city gates. The Wind District is the main residential area; it features the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md), [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md), the ancient mead hall and headquarters of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md), and the [Skyforge](Skyrim_Skyforge.md), worked by the greatest blacksmith in Skyrim, [Eorlund Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Eorlund_Gray-Mane.md). The Cloud District is dominated by the government seat [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) and its [dungeon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach_Dungeon), and is the highest point in Whiterun. Local businesses and services include the smithy and weapons shop [Warmaiden's](Skyrim_Warmaiden%27s.md), the tavern and hunting supply store [The Drunken Huntsman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman), [Belethor's General Goods](Skyrim_Belethor%27s_General_Goods.md), the alchemy shop [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md), and [The Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) inn. The jarl's court wizard [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md) has spells and enchanting supplies for sale and can teach you the basics of enchanting.

For more information on Whiterun, see the [lore article](Lore_Whiterun.md).

- **[Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md)**: Inform the [Jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of Whiterun about the dragon attack on [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md).
- **[Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md)**: Retrieve the [Dragonstone](Skyrim_Dragonstone.md).
- **[Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md)**: Investigate a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) sighting near Whiterun.
- **[The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md)**: Meet the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md).

#### [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md)
- See [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md#Quests).

#### [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md)
- **[The Whispering Door](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Whispering_Door)**: Investigate [Nelkir](Skyrim_Nelkir.md)'s story of a Whispering Lady behind a locked door.

#### [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md)
- **[In My Time Of Need](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:In_My_Time_Of_Need)**: Find a [Redguard woman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saadia) who is on the run from [her past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Taneth).
- **[Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md)**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md) from the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).
- **[The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen) for [Danica Pure-Spring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Danica_Pure-Spring).

#### [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)
- **[Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md)</sup>: A battle between two [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) champions is about to begin.
- **[Beyond the Grave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beyond_the_Grave)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Plate.md)</sup>: Follow the trail of a set of valuable [Daedric Plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Plate_Armor) armor.
- **[In the Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:In_the_Shadows)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bow_of_Shadows.md)</sup>: Save the Jarl of Whiterun from an assassination plot.
- **[Smith 'N Slash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Smith_%27N_Slash)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.md)</sup>: Defeat an [Orc blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg) and her bandits to obtain an enchanted set of armor.
- **[When the Cat's Away](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)</sup>: Find a stash of [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) armor.

## Notes
- [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md) is a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) available for purchase after completion of the quest [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md). It may be purchased from [Proventus Avenicci](Skyrim_Proventus_Avenicci.md) for 5,000 gold, as a reward for retrieving the [Dragonstone](Skyrim_Dragonstone.md) for [Jarl Balgruuf's](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) court mage, [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md). If it is not purchased at this point, and Whiterun is conquered by the Stormcloaks, it may be purchased from [Brill](Skyrim_Brill.md) as a reward for [helping the Stormcloaks conquer Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md).
- Unused data in the game files indicates at least six buildings were removed from Whiterun: Maiden-Loom Manor, Pathway Guard Tower, Shrine of Talos, Ulfberth's House, Whiterun Jail, and Wintersand Manor.
- Approximately 220 alchemy [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) are freely available in Whiterun, with a respawn time of 30 days. These include 71 purple mountain flower, 41 blue mountain flower, 30 lavender, 23 tundra cotton, and 22 red mountain flower found throughout the city, as well as 14 nightshade in the graveyard outside the Hall of the Dead and 7 Nordic barnacle in the pool outside Dragonsreach.
- Whiterun also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Whiterun) in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)*.

## Bugs
- If you sprint as soon as you fast travel to Whiterun, the game may freeze.
- [Louis Letrush](Skyrim_Louis_Letrush.md) may be found partially embedded into the ground just outside the city. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.8, fixes this bug.
- If you finish [In My Time Of Need](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:In_My_Time_Of_Need), the two [Alik'r Warriors](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Warrior.md) who started it will return to the city. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- If the Stormcloaks take Whiterun, two guards will spawn on the left side of the main gate instead of one on either side. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- Some of the items at the vendors in the marketplace aren't owned by them, allowing you to pick them up for free. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.1, fixes this bug.
- A dialogue line referring to the Gildergreen here can be spoken by [children](Skyrim_Child.md) in any city due to a missing condition check. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- You can leave the city without using the main gate by jumping on a barrel and over a wall near the gate. You can also do it by using the red plant formation on the north corner of House Gray-Mane.
