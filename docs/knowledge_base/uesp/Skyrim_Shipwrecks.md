- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Shipwreck.png) **[Pilgrim's Trench](Skyrim_Pilgrim%27s_Trench.md)** — A deep underwater ravine northwest of Winterhold consisting of four submerged shipwrecks. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Pilgrim%27s+Trench)
