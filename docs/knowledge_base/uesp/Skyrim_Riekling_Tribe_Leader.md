[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-creature-Riekling_Tribe_Leader.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Riekling_Tribe_Leader.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Riekling_Tribe_Leader.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Riekling_Tribe_Leader.jpg) The Riekling Tribe Leader The **Riekling Tribe Leader** is the chieftain of a tribe of hungry [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md) added by the [Netch Leather Armor](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). He and his tribe are non-hostile and can be found on an unmarked island north of [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md).

## Related Quests
- **[More Than You Can Chew](Skyrim_More_Than_You_Can_Chew.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md)</sup>: Obtain the secrets of crafting with [netch leather](Skyrim_Netch_Leather.md) from a tribe of [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md).

## Dialogue
The tribe leader is a riekling of few words, leaving you to do most of the talking:

I understand you're in need of leather.

At this point he will hand you a [rough sketch](Skyrim_Riekling_Hunting_Party_Sketch.md) of his hunting party which has been sent to kill a Giant Bull Netch.

You will then have three options for how to deal with his request:

Eating netch leather will kill you. You should give me your cookbook. (Persuade)
- Failed: *"Joooo fah!"*
- Passed: [<sup>(?)</sup>](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Pages_Missing_Data)
I've brought back your [netch leather](Skyrim_Giant_Bull_Netch_Leather.md).
- *"Waaaah!"*
This [purple leather](Skyrim_Imitation_Netch_Leather.md) is from a giant netch. I'll trade it for the cookbook. (Lie)

Once you have supplied him with leather, he will give you a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Riekling_Chest.md) to the nearby chest, allowing you to complete the related quest.
