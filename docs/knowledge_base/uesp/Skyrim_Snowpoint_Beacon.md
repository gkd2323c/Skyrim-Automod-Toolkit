## Related Quests
- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The main path to the tower approaches from the north, with stone steps leading up to a doorway in the lowest of the tower's three floors. It is also possible to climb the mountain behind [Fort Fellhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Fellhammer) to reach the tower from the southwest; from this direction, you will reach the middle floor first.

There is nothing of interest on the lowest floor, which features only a spiral stone staircase leading up to the middle floor. At the top of the stairs is a large hole in the wall leading out onto a wooden platform. A bandit wielding a [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) guards this level. Inside against an internal wall is a novice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot with a mounted wolf head above it. To the left is a stack of crates with a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and two [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on top. The internal staircase continues to the top floor, where a bandit chief stands looking out to the north, allowing you to attack from behind. A quiver of eighteen random [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows) rests on a table. To the right of the table is a lean-to with an unowned bedroll under it, and farther to the right are some barrels containing [food](Skyrim_Food.md).

## Notes
- Two [corundum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) are east of the tower on a small plateau on the side of the mountain.
