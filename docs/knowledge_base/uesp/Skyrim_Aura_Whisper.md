**Aura Whisper** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) that reveals nearby mobile entities, similar to the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md) spell effect. Aura Whisper will reveal both NPCs and creatures. Unlike the Detect Life spell, it does not show if the entities detected are friendly or hostile. Although this shout is much quieter than the others, it is not silent and *can* alert enemies to your presence, unless you have the Illusion perk [Quiet Casting](Skyrim_Quiet_Casting.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Evil in Waiting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Evil_in_Waiting)**: Seal away an [ancient evil](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md).
- **[Silenced Tongues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silenced_Tongues)**: Unlock the burial chamber of the Tongue Chieftain [Kvenel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kvenel_the_Tongue).

## Possible Uses
- Detecting [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md), [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md), and [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>, which are not visible with either the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) or [Detect Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Detect_Dead) spells (some Ash Spawn are said to be detectable with Detect Dead).
- Determining whether bodies in a crypt are [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) waiting for unwary adventurers, or just harmless mummified remains.
- Hunting animals in the fields of Skyrim.

## Notes
- Aura Whisper has a range of 300 ft indoors and 500 ft outdoors.

## Bugs
- If you learn the first word of the shout at [Northwind Summit](Skyrim_Northwind_Summit.md) or [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) and then go to [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md), you'll learn the *third* word next, but it won't appear in the shouts list because the second word hasn't been learned. After this, the word wall for the second word in the remaining location won't work at all (the word wall sound effects are played, but nothing happens when it is approached).

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can learn the missing second word using the following console command: `player.teachword 60295`. The associated word wall will remain bugged, though, and continues to play the sound effects.

- Sometimes after using the Aura Whisper shout, various visual effects may be permanently applied to your character, such as draugr eyes. Text saying *"was equipped"* may accompany the adding of the effects. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.

- This may be fixed by transforming into a [Werewolf](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) or [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md). Note that the effects may reappear at any time and while they vanish after reverting back to your regular form, the extra visual effects (mainly draugr eyes) can re-equip themselves after a certain length of time and so this is not a permanent fix.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can fix your character's eyes by opening the console and using the following command: `player.addspell 000f71d1`. Close the console, then reopen the console and enter the following command: `player.removespell 000f71d1`. Note that this fix may not work in third person view, so try to do it in first person.

- This shout may trigger confrontations with Guards and locations of word walls even though it's silent to everyone else. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
