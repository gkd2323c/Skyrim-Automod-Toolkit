[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/ON-map-Wilderking_Court.jpg/200px-ON-map-Wilderking_Court.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Wilderking_Court.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Wilderking_Court.jpg) Map of Wilderking Court circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Wilderking Court (also called the **Court of the Wilderking**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> or simply, **The Court**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Legend-2)</sup> is a subregion of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md), the southwestern region of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The cities of [Greenheart](Lore_Greenheart.md) and [Marbruk](Lore_Marbruk.md) are anchored on each side of the region, with the latter found on the crossroad towards [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md). The name earns its name through the presence of the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md), a god worshipped by the tribal [Wood Elves](Lore_Wood_Elves.md) that live in the region, such as the ones that live in [Bramblebreach](Lore_Bramblebreach.md) and the [Shrouded Vale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shrouded_Vale). The Wilderking is worshipped across the land, and his will is enforced through the [Hollow](Lore_Hollow.md), stone constructs that live in the land, in ruins such as Shadows Crawl and the Labyrinth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Legend-2)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#First_Dominion) built the city of Marbruk on the northern edge of the Wilderking Court, which became their foothold in the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> At this point, the tribal clans of the Court were unaligned with the Aldmeri Dominion, unlike the rest of Valenwood. They only served the Wilderking. An [Agent](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the Dominion was sent on a mission to gain an audience with the Wilderking, and ultimately uncovered a plot conceived by the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md) to kill the Wilderking. In the end, the Wilderking died and was replaced by [Aranias](Lore_Aranias.md), who would be named the Wilderqueen. The queen, along with the rest of the Wilderking Court, pledged allegiance to the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
