[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/ON-place-Ebonheart.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ebonheart.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ebonheart.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ebonheart.jpg) Ebonheart Cruethys was a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md)<sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Chimer)</sup> noble from the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). In life, he was the King of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md)<sup>[[Note 2]](#intnote_King)</sup> and a member of the [Ra'athim Clan](Lore_Ra%27athim.md). He was succeeded by his younger brother, [Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KEXII-2)</sup> He is featured in the historical fiction novel series *[King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md)*.

## History
Cruethys was born sometime in the early First Era by his father [Kronin](Lore_Kronin.md) of Ebonheart and his mother. His half-brother was [S'ephen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:S%27ephen)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KEXII-2)</sup> of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRB4-3)</sup> and his younger brother was Moraelyn.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KEIV-4)</sup> Cruethys' house allegiance is never explicitly stated but the Ra'athim clan's ruling house was [House Mora](Lore_House_Mora.md) before it was adopted into [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) at an unknown time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRB5-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-IWTB-6)</sup> When the [Skyrim Conquests](Lore_Skyrim_Conquests.md) took place in the third century of the First Era, the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) invaded ancient Morrowind and took over an abundance of land, including Ebonheart. Since then, Cruethys and his family became raiders across occupied Morrowind but returned home after the land was liberated<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup> in [1E 416](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_416).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1-7)</sup>

Cruethys assumed the title of King of Ebonheart sometime later. At some point, he paid a visit to the King of [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md) and brought Moraelyn along with him. He gave him a pouch of money and his knife to have it mended by a [blacksmith](Lore_Smithing.md). Through circumstance, Moraelyn met his longtime friend, [Mith](Lore_Mith.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KEIV-4)</sup> Cruethys and his mother died when the Ebonheart council chamber collapsed onto them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KEXII-2)</sup> With his death, Moraelyn became his successor and his clan continued to rule for years.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KEXII-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRB5-5)</sup>
