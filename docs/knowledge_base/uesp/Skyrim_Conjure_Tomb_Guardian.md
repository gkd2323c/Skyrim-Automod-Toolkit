- *Summons a Tomb Guardian for 60 seconds that conjures a Shade when it kills an enemy.*

**Conjure Tomb Guardian** is an Adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

The Tomb Guardian uses the same dialogue as some varieties of [Ghost](Skyrim_Undead.md#Ghost), specifically the lines used by [Companion Ghosts](Skyrim_Companion_Ghost.md) and [Nightingale Sentinels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Sentinel).

## Effects
- [Conjure Tomb Guardian](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy), Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health by 100.

## Notes
- **Conjure Tomb Guardian** is unaffected by [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md).
- The Tomb Guardian lacks a Resist Paralyze.

## Bugs
- Summoned spirits may despawn immediately or a few seconds after spawning. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Certain types of summoned spirits (e.g. [Bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md)) may be invisible after spawning. <sup>**?**</sup> - They will still be somewhat visible due to the blue smoke surrounding them.
- Tomb Guardian may use standard NPC [generic dialogue](Skyrim_Generic_Dialogue.md), such as "That spell looks dangerous... keep your distance." <sup>**?**</sup>
