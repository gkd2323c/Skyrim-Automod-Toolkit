## Walkthrough
The camp is at the end of a ravine, with the easiest approach from the east. If *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* is installed and [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) has been built, a short distance uphill west of the hall is a path leading west into the ravine. A [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) guards the ravine. The path features the painted stones, [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md), and mammoth ribs and skulls typical of giant camps. As you follow the ravine into the camp, you will find a patch of [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) near a patch of [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md), with two more white caps in the camp itself.

Two giants (including the guard in the ravine) occupy the camp, which is in a basin surrounded by large rock formations. There are two large campfires with a total of six [charred skeevers](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) cooking on fire-hardened stakes. Seven [mammoth cheese bowls](Skyrim_Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl.md) and two unlocked [chests](Skyrim_Chest.md) are found in the camp, with one chest at the northern end of the basin and the other against the rocks to the east.
