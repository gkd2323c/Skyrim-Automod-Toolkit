[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg) Rustleif's House **Rustleif's House** is a smithy near [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md)'s shoreline. [Rustleif](Skyrim_Rustleif.md) has his smithy and home set up facing the bay. He is a renowned weaponsmith, and he is often called upon to arm and outfit the jarl's men. His Redguard wife, [Seren](Skyrim_Seren.md), assists him and is pregnant. Both Rustleif and Seren provide merchant services.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rustleif%27s_House_02.jpg) Blacksmithing There is a raised, covered porch in front and to the left of the door, with a couple of food barrels to the right and wooden benches to the left of the door. There are two sets of steps leading up to the porch and a snowberry bush beside one of them. On the side of the house the [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) setup is rather minimal, consisting of only a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and where they buy and sell their goods, although two [smelters](Skyrim_Smelter.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) can be found elsewhere in town. The front door is novice-locked during the day when they are both around the forge working and at night.

### Rustleif's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rustleif%27s_House.jpg) Rustleif's house The house is a single-room dwelling that is a little spartan inside. Upon entering, on your left is a long wooden table and bench holding a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). To the left of the table, on the floor is a basket containing four [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) and to the right is a basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). Further to the right against the north wall is a set of shelves holding two leeks, a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three potatoes, with several pieces of cheese on top. Above these shelves are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging from the ceiling. There is a food barrel in the northeastern corner.

Against the east wall is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot, beside a large double bed with a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes) by the foot of it and a basket containing two [potions of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor) to the right and a round wooden table beside this holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead). Next to this against the south wall is a tall wardrobe with a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron) on top and pile of firewood, with a hang rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) between them. Opposite the door, on the south wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantlepiece. In front of the hearth is an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md). To the right of the fireplace are several barrels and against the west wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md).
