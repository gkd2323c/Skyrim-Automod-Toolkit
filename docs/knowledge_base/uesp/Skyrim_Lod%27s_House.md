[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-place-Lod%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lod%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lod%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lod%27s_House_02.jpg) Lod the blacksmith [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lod%27s_House.jpg) Lod's house It is almost always novice-locked and will require [stealth](Skyrim_Stealth.md) for entry. It's made up of two rooms: a living area and a basement. It is found near the center of Falkreath, between *[Gray Pine Goods](Skyrim_Gray_Pine_Goods.md)* and *[Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md)*.

### Exterior
There is a raised, covered porch along the length of the front of the house with steps leading up to it. To the left of the house is a larger cover area where Lod plies his craft at his [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md). There are also a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). There are two more tanning racks in front, not under cover. Beside these is an open crate containing four [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips). Around the house there are four [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) and one [purple mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) and a [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch). Unlike most merchants Lod doesn't display any of his wares.

### Lod's House
Upon entering there is a long wooden table on the left against the north wall. On the table is a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), and two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). A basket on the floor to the right contains two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), with a set of shelves to the right of that. These hold two more leeks, a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), with two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) and several pieces of cheese on top and two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging above. There is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel in the northeast corner, above the stairs leading down to the basement.

The stairs run along the east wall. Opposite the table on the south wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantel. There is an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) on the hearth. To the left, hanging from the ceiling, is a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), and to the right is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Next to the wardrobe is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead), and a [quest](Skyrim_Some_Light_Theft.md) -related [private letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Letter) is lying on the table next to his bed. In the southwestern corner and running along the west wall is a single owned bed, with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot. To the right of the bed, against the west wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are five [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse).

In the basement, along the north wall are two long wooden tables. The first holds a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) and three [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow), while the second has a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two bottles of Nord mead, an iron dagger, an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), a set of [iron armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor) and a pair of [iron gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets), with a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) underneath. To the left is an end table and chair. On top of the end table are two [blacksmith's potions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion) and a copy of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Light Armor Forging](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Armor_Forging)*. Against the west wall is an unlocked chest, a wardrobe containing more clothes, and a couple of food barrels. Along the south wall is a wooden block with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on top and a set of shelves holding nothing but clutter. To the left is a round wooden table and chair, with a loaf of bread on the table and a garlic braid, a bunch of elves ear and a bunch of frost mirriam hanging above.
