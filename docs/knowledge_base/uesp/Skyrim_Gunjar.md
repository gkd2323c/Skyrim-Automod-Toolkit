[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Gunjar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gunjar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunjar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunjar.jpg) Gunjar **Gunjar** was a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) soldier and a friend of [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md). He was to be executed by the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) but escaped when [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) attacked. Once you enter [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md), you will find him dead and can take his equipment. He wears a [Stormcloak cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Cuirass) and a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md), and carries an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md).

If you enter the keep with Ralof, he will run towards Gunjar and, after realizing that he is dead, tell him, *"We'll meet again in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md), brother."* He will then address you: *"There you go. You may as well take Gunjar's gear. He won't be needing it any more."*

If you enter with [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md), you will still encounter Gunjar's body shortly after entering the keep, but Hadvar will not teach you that you can take his things.

After this, Gunjar is never encountered or mentioned again.
