**Freeze** is an apprentice level destruction spell that deals [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to health and stamina, and slows targets' movement for 15 seconds.

## Effects
- [Freeze](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 20 pts
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md), 50 pts for 15 secs
- [Deep Freeze Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 3 secs; if the [Deep Freeze](Skyrim_Deep_Freeze.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Augmented_Frost.md), raises damage to 25 at first rank or 30 at second rank

## Notes
- Freeze is very similar to [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) and [Icy Spear](Skyrim_Icy_Spear.md) in operation, though at a lower cost than either of them. It does less damage as well, but slows targets for much longer. This makes it ideally suited for tougher opponents (who won't be dead in 3 seconds). Cast it once at the start of a fight and then use other means to actually kill them. Like [Ignite](Skyrim_Ignite.md), you must have [Ahzidal's Ring of Arcana](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Ring_of_Arcana.md) equipped in order to use it.
- Like most Frost spells, it can be used to set off [Frost Runes](Skyrim_Frost_Rune.md) from a safe distance.
- Freeze spikes which hit enemies will continue to exist after they die until you leave the area. This makes the corpses easy to spot from a distance for looting.
