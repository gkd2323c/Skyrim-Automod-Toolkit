# Amber Plasm

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/ON-trap-Blistering_Amber.jpg/200px-ON-trap-Blistering_Amber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trap-Blistering_Amber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trap-Blistering_Amber.jpg) A pool of Amber Plasm Amber Plasm is an orange sap-like substance produced by the mad [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) of [Mazzatun](Lore_Mazzatun.md), [Tsono-Xuhil](Lore_Tsono-Xuhil.md). Despite its appearance, this material is not like normal [Hist Sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md), and appears to be a form of [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) which leaks into [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) through the Hist like blood from a wound. Amber Plasm is usually associated with [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NKJournal-1)</sup> Drinking it or bathing in it causes hallucinations and serious blistering that can result in death. These effects can drive an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) mad.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Amber Plasm has even been utilized to create "Amber Projections", phantoms of the recently deceased who are said to be "preserved in amber". These phantom forms are not exclusive to Argonians, as even dead [haj mota](Lore_Haj_Mota.md) have been summoned in this manner.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Tree-Minder of the [Xit-Xaht](Lore_Xit-Xaht.md) tribe once attempted to harness Amber Plasm to achieve great power and order. Through alchemical study and the Tsono-Xuhil's guidance, Tree-Minder [Na-Kesh](Lore_Tree-Minder_Na-Kesh.md) of the [Xit-Xaht](Lore_Xit-Xaht.md) learned to harness the power of the substance. When properly manipulated, amber plasm can grant a Saxhleel great speed and strength. She performed horrific alchemical experiments on the substance, and planned to use it to uplift her people and return [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) to the pre- [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md) way of life before conquering all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NKJournal-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> This dangerous alchemy was stopped by a group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), who killed Na-Kesh and liberated the enslaved Su-Zahleel tribe from Mazzatun. The surviving Su-Zahleel elders then put the mad Hist into a deep slumber, hoping to alleviate its madness.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
