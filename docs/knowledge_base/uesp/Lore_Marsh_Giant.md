[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) Blackwood, a known Marsh Giant habitat Marsh Giants are a [giant](Lore_Giant.md) -like [race](Lore_Race.md) native to [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). These manifestations of nature are a form of [woodland spirit](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md) considered "cousins" to [spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md). They are not related to the common giants of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but they do share their size and pastoral activities. Their exact level of intelligence is unknown due to limited interactions. However, the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) apparently have a long and acrimonious history with the Marsh Giants, and urge the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) to avoid the creatures if they can.

They play an important role in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md)'s ecosystem by knocking down dead trees, turning the soil and making room for younger vegetation to grow. This encourages the growth of mosses and suppresses the development of invasive fungi. Marsh giants also hinder loggers, contribute to [avian](Lore_Birds.md) diversity and ward off certain predators, such as feral [cats](Lore_Cat.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OMGAS-1)</sup>

Argonians sometimes harvest the blood of fallen marsh giants for use in paints and inks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
