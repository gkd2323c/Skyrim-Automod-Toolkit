*Alchemy description:* **Blocking absorbs <mag>% more damage for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Block <mag>% more damage with your shield.**

**Fortify Block** grants damage reduction while [blocking](Skyrim_Block.md).

The block cap is 85%, so past a certain point extra fortify block effects will not help. What that point is depends on the shield, your block skill, and the Shield Wall perk; see [here](Skyrim_Block.md#Damage_Reduction_from_Blocking) for details of the formula.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Block if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of <item> *[of Blocking](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Block)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Blocking*, *of Major Blocking*, *of Extreme Blocking*, *of Eminent Blocking*, and *of Peerless Blocking.*
- [Shield of Solitude](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shield_of_Solitude)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md)
- [Fists of Randagulf](Skyrim_Fists_of_Randagulf.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>
- [Her Hand Helmet](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Helmet.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>
