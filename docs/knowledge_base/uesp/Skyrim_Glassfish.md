From Fishing Mastery, v1:

Caught in Temperate Lakes Small in Size commonly Caught Seen in Fair Weather

Glassfish are a common fish one can see through like a window! They are not so different than us on the inside. The best time to catch them is on a clear day, but if you are lucky, you can catch them on a rainy day as well.

While their natural habitats are deep subterranean waterways, glassfish can be caught in the open waters of warm, clear lakes when they swim above ground for spawning.

They say that if you eat a glassfish, you'll become clear as glass yourself.

## Related Quests
- [Skyrim:Clear-Headed](Skyrim_Clear-Headed.md): Help a guard overcome his skooma addiction.

## Notes
- Up to three Glassfish can be found and picked up by hand in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md).
- Since the fourth effect, "Fortify Persuasion", is not possessed by any other alchemy ingredient in the game, it can only be learned by eating a glassfish while you have all three perks in "Experimenter", and it can never be made into a potion either.
