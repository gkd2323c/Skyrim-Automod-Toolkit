[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Sword.jpg) A Lunar Steel Sword The Lunar Steel Sword is a special [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) that can be occasionally found at [Silent Moons Camp](Skyrim_Silent_Moons_Camp.md). Its enchantment causes [light damage](Skyrim_Light_Damage.md) over one second on whoever is struck by the sword. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Lunar Steel Sword requires one [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the tempering being twice as effective if you have gained the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk.
