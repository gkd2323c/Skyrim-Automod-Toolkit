[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-npc-Markarth_City_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Markarth_City_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Markarth_City_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Markarth_City_Guard.jpg) Markarth City Guard **Markarth City Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md). Should the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) forces gain control of Markarth, they will be replaced by [Stormcloak Guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Guard) under the service of [Jarl Thongvor](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md). They wear a green version of the guard armor.

## Quest-Related Events

### The Forsworn Conspiracy
After the incident inside the [Treasury House](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md), or your encounter with Nepos the Nose, the plot thickens, although Eltrys will never find out what was really going on. When you enter the Shrine of Talos you will find Eltrys dead on the floor, and three Markarth City Guards waiting for you. *"We warned you, but you just had to go and cause trouble. Now we have to pin all these recent murders on you. Silence witnesses. Work. Work. Work."*

**What did you do to Eltrys?** *"Same thing we do with all the other natives who want to change things around here. We had a nice little deal going between Thonar and Madanach until you and Eltrys started snooping around. Well, you wanted to find the man responsible for those killings? You'll have plenty of time with the King in Rags when you're in Cidhna mine * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *."* **You're corrupt. Thonar is paying you.** *"We have a nice arrangement in this city, and we're not letting you get in the way. You have a problem with that? Take it up with Madanach. I'm sure the King in rags and his Forsworn would love to meet you. Now you're coming with us. It's a life sentence in Cidhna Mine for you."* **Madanach is behind these killings!** *"You think you're the only one who knows that? We had a nice little deal going between Thonar and Madanach until you showed up. You'll have plenty of time to take it up with the King in Rags while you're serving a life sentence in Cidhna Mine."* The guards there will now attempt to [arrest](Skyrim_Crime.md#Getting_Arrested) you. You can try to resist arrest or talk your way out of it; however, you must submit to being jailed in order to advance the quest to [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md).

If you are already a thane before starting this quest, and you pickpocketed Nepos's journal to avoid conflict; you may be able to avoid arrest and/or infamy related to finishing the quest by the following:

1. Go back to the shrine of Talos to meet Eltrys.
2. Allow the main guard there to initiate the dialogue: 1. **Guard**: "We warned you... ...Work. Work. Work."
3. Exit the chat before responding [press B etc].
4. The guard will immediately re-engage you. 1. **Guard**: "You have committed crimes against the Jarl... how do you respond?"" (Or similar dialogue depending on version).
2. **Player**: "I am the thane. I demand you release me at once"", to which the guard will apologize, and give the standard response to using thaneship to avoid crimes.
5. You must then *immediately* exit the shrine of Talos. 1. If you do not immediately exit the shrine the guard (Presumably realizing his error) tries to re-engage you once again, back down the quest related options of dialogue: jail or resist only, and will immediately attack if you try to exit the dialogue any further.
6. Once exiting, the main guard will follow outside, but will have apparently forgotten about the whole thing once leaving the shrine; as will all of the guards around who will treat you as before the quest started /no bounty or arrest warrant.

You can also re-enter the shrine of Talos with this method to loot Eltrys's body after, which will remain in place alongside the other two guards (who will have normal dialogue with you instead of trying to arrest or attack).

This route will complete [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md) quest without starting [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md) while keeping you free from the repercussions of finishing The Forsworn Conspiracy quest line. Using this method you will not be able to start the No One Escapes Cidhna Mine quest line.

**Confronted by a guard after returning to the [Shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Talos.md):**
*"Halt. You're under arrest for murder and conspiracy against the city of Markarth!"*

**What's the problem?** *"The "problem" is your disrespect for the laws of this land. It's time to face the Jarl's justice."* **I'm with the Guild. How about you look the other way?** (Only available after you joined the Thieves Guild) *"I don't care who you're with. After what you did, you're lucky I'm not cutting you down where you stand!"* **How about I pay you some gold and you look the other way?** *"Not a chance. Not for this. You're going to the Cidhna Mine for the rest of your days."* **I'm the Jarl's Thane. I demand you let me go at once.** *"No one in the Jarl's court is above the charge of conspiring against the city. You're coming with us."* **Any chance I could talk you into overlooking this? (Persuade)** *"You're not sweet-talking your way out of this. Not after what you did. You're headed to Cidhna Mine, and that's that."* **You caught me. I'll pay off my bounty.** *"You aren't fooling me. Trusted witnesses have seen you commit several murders. And there are accounts of your suspicious behavior throughout the city. Now come quietly."* **I submit. Take me to jail.** *"You'll never see the sun again. No one escapes Cidhna Mine. No one."* **I'd rather die than go to prison!** (Guards turn hostile) *"Then pay with your blood."*
*"That can be arranged."*
*"So be it."*
