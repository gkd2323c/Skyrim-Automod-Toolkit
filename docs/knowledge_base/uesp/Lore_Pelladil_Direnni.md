[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/ON-load-Blackrose_Prison.jpg/200px-ON-load-Blackrose_Prison.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Blackrose_Prison.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Blackrose_Prison.jpg) Blackrose Prison Pelladil Direnni was one of the prominent members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup> and the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md) in service of the [Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md). He was one of the supporters of the ratification of the [Guild Act](Lore_Guild_Act.md), without which numerous professional organizations, such as the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), would not have been established.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DRD-2)</sup>

In the early years of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), by the order of the Potentate, Pelladil Direnni built the [Blackrose Prison](Lore_Blackrose_Prison.md) from the scattered rubble of [Lilmothiit](Lore_Lilmothiit.md) ruins by summoning an army of [Stone Atronachs](Lore_Stone_Atronach.md) in a single day.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FTSMBP-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-U20P-4)</sup> Validity of the claim that the prison was constructed within such short time frame was considered doubtful by some historians, but the fact that he partook in the construction of the facility is uncontested.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AHOBR-5)</sup>

He was a descendant and an ancestor of several other famous figures. Famous witch, [Raven Direnni](Lore_Raven_Direnni.md) was his distant ancestor. He was a great great grandfather of [Jovron Direnni](Lore_Jovron_Direnni.md) and a great great great great grandfather of [Medora Direnni](Lore_Medora_Direnni.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DRD-2)</sup>

Little is known of Pelladil's fate following the fall of the Akaviri Potentate, but it is known that as of the [2E 465](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_465) the position of the Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel was held by [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Cot FC4-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-IP-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AT2-8)</sup>
