# Ebony Mail

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ebony_Mail.jpg) The Ebony Mail The Ebony Mail is a Daedric [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md). It may appear as an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) breastplate or occasionally a fuller suit of armor. It grants its wearer resistance to fire and [magic](Lore_Magic.md), as well as magical shielding from physical blows, though in some instances it provides for quieter movements and the ability to poison nearby enemies. According to legend, the artifact was created before recorded history by Boethiah, and it is Boethiah alone who determines who should possess the Mail and for how long a time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup>

## History
Legend holds that after the defeat of [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) in the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md) could not destroy his [Heart](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md), and so lashed it to an arrow and shot it with his [bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) across Tamriel and into the sea.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TM-3)</sup> The blood that poured from the Heart of Lorkhan became ebony,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CM22T-4)</sup> the substance the Ebony Mail is made from. [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) lore predating the [Riddle'Thar Epiphany](Lore_Riddle%27Thar_Epiphany.md) refers to the "death-shroud of [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md)", which [Boethra](Lore_Boethra.md) wears on nights of the [Ghost Moon](Lore_Ghost_Moon.md) and "wages war beyond the [Lattice](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md)".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWS-5)</sup>

[Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), at one point befriended the sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWYB-6)</sup> At Fyr’s behest, Yagrum Bagarn wrote and compiled centuries of personal notes into a book that would become known as *[Tamrielic Lore](Lore_Tamrielic_Lore.md)*,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-7)</sup><sup>:241</sup> listing the Ebony Mail among the artifacts of "unimaginable significance". By the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), copies had spread across Tamriel, offering readers rare insights into these legendary items,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TA-8)</sup> and Fyr himself hoped to utilize this knowledge in his search for the Ebony Mail and the other artifacts detailed in the book.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-MWFR-9)</sup>

During the play [*A Hypothetical Treachery*](Lore_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md), a group of adventurers (Malvasian, Inzoliah, Dolcettus and Schiavas) recover the Ebony Mail from the legendary [Elden Grove](Lore_Elden_Grove.md) of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). In the end, Inzoliah alone is left alive and sells the artifact to the King of [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AHT-10)</sup>

At an unspecified point in time prior to the events of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a warrior named [Helath Stormbinder](Lore_Helath_Stormbinder.md) was Boethiah's champion and possessed the Ebony Mail. He eventually lost Boethiah's favor, however, and the Mail was taken from him by Boethiah. The Ebony Mail would remain hidden until its eventual recovery during the Imperial Simulacrum.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup>

### Third Era
During the Imperial Simulacrum from [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389) to [3E 399](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_399), rumors circulated around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) of the return of the Ebony Mail—the "lost armor of the Drow"<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_AR)</sup> —not seen since the days of Helath Stormbinder, whose loss of the Mail was compared to the legend of [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md)' loss of the [Lord's Mail](Lore_Lord%27s_Mail.md) due to his arrogance. The legends made the Ebony Mail highly sought after in these turbulent times by people believing it would make them not only "tougher than a troll", but stronger and faster as well. Following these rumors, the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md) bartered for information on an unpleasant dungeon in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), which the Eternal Champion braved to find a map. The map led to another dungeon elsewhere on Tamriel, where the Mail was finally recovered and returned to the world of Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTIL-1)</sup>

Circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) - [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), prior to the events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), an [unknown agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) in search of power summoned Boethiah in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). In return for killing an innocent spellsword who had displeased the Prince, the agent was gifted with the Ebony Mail from one of her worshippers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-DFBoethiah-11)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was sent by the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) at the request of [Archcanon Tholer Saryoni](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Tholer_Saryoni.2C_Archcanon) to make a pilgrimage to [Mount Assarnibibi](Lore_Mount_Assarnibibi.md), where [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) oversaw the ninety-nine lovers of Boethiah, who gave birth to [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md). Upon completing the pilgrimage, the Nerevarine received the Ebony Mail and brought it to Tholer Saryoni. The archcanon refused to accept it, saying it would be put to better use by the Nerevarine.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWB-12)</sup> The Nerevarine later sold it to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md), who put it on display in the [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) Museum of Artifacts.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Tribunal-13)</sup>

### Fourth Era
Sometime after the end of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) came across the Ebony Mail in their journey. The armor is thought to have been looted from an extravagant chest.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Boethiah tasked the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) with assassinating the Prince's previous champion and his group of bandits. The Dragonborn retrieved the Ebony Mail from the corpse of the ex-champion.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Skyrim-15)</sup>
