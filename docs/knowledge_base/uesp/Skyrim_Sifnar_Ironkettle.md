[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Sifnar_Ironkettle.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sifnar_Ironkettle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sifnar_Ironkettle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sifnar_Ironkettle.jpg) Sifnar Ironkettle **Sifnar Ironkettle** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) servant found in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) in the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md).

He wakes up at 8am and spends the day down in the lower area of the palace before heading off to bed [upstairs](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs.md) at midnight.

Sifnar wears a set of [farm clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) along with some [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
He does not have much to say about himself and he doesn't seem to mind telling you this when he remarks,

- *"I do the cookin' and cleanin' round the palace."*
- *"Don't need to pay me no mind. I'm the least important person in here."*

He also admits that his job isn't particularly easy or rewarding when he says,

- *"Keepin' the palace clean an' the lords fed ain't easy work, but it's honest work."*

## Notes
- Unused markers, AI packages, and scripting refer to a "Sifnar" that was to accompany you during an earlier iteration of [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md). It's unknown if Sifnar Ironkettle and the aforementioned NPC were once meant to be one and the same, although this seems unlikely. The removed Sifnar was to be a field commander-type character, as a Stormcloak equivalent to the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)'s [Captain Metilius](Skyrim_Captain_Metilius.md). In the Stormcloak version of the quest, you and Sifnar would go out to investigate a dragon sighting near [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md). For the Legion, this would be you and Metilius investigating [Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_Fort_Hraggstad.md).
