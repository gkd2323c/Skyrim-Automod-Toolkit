[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Reforged_Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Reforged_Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader.png) Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader The Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader are artifact [gauntlets](Skyrim_Gauntlets.md). They are acquired through the [Relics of the Crusader](Skyrim_Relics_of_the_Crusader.md) quest, in which you also claim the other relics from the [Bandit Thieves](Skyrim_Bandit_Thief.md) at [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md). Improving the Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader requires a [Corundum Ingot](Skyrim_Corundum_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
