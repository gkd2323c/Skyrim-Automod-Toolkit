Fenrik's Welcome is an expert level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes the caster to become [invisible](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) for 5 seconds and allows them to unlock expert and below locks.

## Effects
- [Fenrik's Welcome](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md), for 5 secs

## Perks
- [Illusion Dual Casting](Skyrim_Illusion_Dual_Casting.md), multiplies duration by 2.2 (rounded down)

## Notes
- This spell is silent.
