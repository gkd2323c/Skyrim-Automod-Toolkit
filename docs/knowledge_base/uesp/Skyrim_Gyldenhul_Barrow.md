[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/SR-place-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-place-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg) Gyldenhul Barrow **Gyldenhul Barrow** is a small [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) east of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) containing the spirit and treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand). It contains only one zone, *[Gyldenhul Barrow](#Gyldenhul_Barrow)*.

The ruin only becomes accessible near the end of the quest [Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md)**: Find the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The entrance is on the northwestern shore of [Horker Island](Skyrim_Horker_Island.md). Two small rowboats are drawn up on the nearby beach, and two leveled [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) guarding the entrance will attack on sight. There are four [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod) plants on the shore, a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush and a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein) to the left of the entrance, and several standing stones around the entrance. The entrance is through an iron door requiring the [Gyldenhul Barrow key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gyldenhul_Barrow_Key), which is found upon collecting the fourth piece of the [Deathbrand armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_Armor) treasure.

### Gyldenhul Barrow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow.jpg) A stalhrim deposit Upon entering, you will be in a small catacomb area. A dead adventurer is sitting against a wall in front of you. A pickaxe is beside him, and he is carrying a [torn note](Skyrim_Torn_Note_(Dragonborn).md). Two upright sarcophagi to the east and west contain [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) deposits, as does an upright sarcophagus in front of you with a [dead draugr](Skyrim_Containers.md#Draugr) spilling out (although this small northern deposit cannot be mined). After you mine the stalhrim to the west, pass through the now unblocked sarcophagus to enter a wide corridor with stairs descending on your left to a key-locked iron door. The same key that opened the outside door opens this one.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-quest-Deathbrand_%28quest%29_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Deathbrand_%28quest%29_05.jpg)](File_SR-quest-Deathbrand_(quest)_05.jpg.md) [](File_SR-quest-Deathbrand_(quest)_05.jpg.md) The treasure of Haknir Death-Brand Through the door is a wide corridor leading to another locked iron door that uses the same key. The corridor is full of treasure, some of which is trapped, causing a gate to drop behind you and sealing you in when you take it, and forcing you to explore further into the barrow to escape. At the far end on the left is a master-locked chest, and at the near end on the right is an expert-locked chest with two [burial urns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn) beside it. In the middle of the corridor is a large [urn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urn). Found in the corridor are a quiver of [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows), a [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), a [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), a [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), a [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword), a [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe), a pair of [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets)/bracers, a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md), a set of [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots), a [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet), a [healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md), a [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) potion, four additional potions, an empty [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), and a filled soul gem, all of which are random and set the first time you enter the barrow even if you didn't mine the stalhrim deposit. In addition, there is a [potion of ultimate healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Healing), a [potion of ultimate magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Magicka), three [bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), four [cups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup), a [diamond](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Diamond), two [emeralds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald), a [garnet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet), seven [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), two [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), a [ruby](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby), an [amethyst](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst) (this is almost under the front of the master-locked chest and can be easily missed), and 6400-9600 [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md). Both chests are opened with the same key used for the doors. The only way you can go is forward through the second iron door.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gyldenhul_Barrow_02.jpg) The King of Ghosts' skeleton Upon opening the second door, you find yourself at the top of several flights of stairs leading down to the southeast. At the bottom of the stairs is a doorway into a huge room, featuring arches on either side and tall pillars rising to a suspended ceiling. Ahead is a wide flight of stairs leading up to the south-southeast. At the top is an altar upon which the skeleton of Haknir Death-Brand is laid out. Beside him is [Bloodscythe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodscythe), a unique [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar). Upon taking the weapon, the whole barrow will shake, and [Haknir's ghost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand) will rise behind you, down on the main floor of the room. In addition to fighting his ghost, you also must face the ghosts of his quartermaster [Garuk Windrime](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garuk_Windrime), his helmsman [Thalin Ebonhand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalin_Ebonhand), and [the ghosts of his former crew](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Crew.md), making this quite a long and potentially difficult fight.

Once they are all vanquished, you will complete the quest Deathbrand, and can loot his skeleton for some gold and Bloodscythe's bladed companion, the unique scimitar [Soulrender](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soulrender). There are short flights of ascending stairs on either side of the altar, one to the northwest with a large urn at the top, and the other to the southeast leading to the boss chest.

## Notes
- Gyldenhul Barrow also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Gyldenhul_Barrow) in *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)*.

## Bugs
- Gyldenhul Barrow's entrance door is locked by a master-level lock and technically requires the Gyldenhul Barrow key to open. However, this can be circumvented by attempting to pick the lock while having the [Wax Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wax_Key) perk, which gives you a copy of the required key. Entering through this method and proceeding to fight Haknir will break the quest and may cause it to remain permanently in your quest log, as the correct quest stages are skipped. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=11965)) The entrance door is now marked as requiring the key, such that you can't attempt to lockpick it and thus can't receive a duplicate key.
