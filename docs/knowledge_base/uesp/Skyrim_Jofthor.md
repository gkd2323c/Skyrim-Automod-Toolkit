[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-npc-Jofthor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jofthor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor.jpg) Jofthor at his farm [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-npc-Jofthor_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jofthor_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor_02.jpg) Jofthor berating his daughter [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/SR-npc-Jofthor_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jofthor_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jofthor_03.jpg) Jofthor and Boti discussing their daughter's future **Jofthor** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), where he owns [Fellstar Farm](Skyrim_Fellstar_Farm.md) with his wife [Boti](Skyrim_Boti.md) and daughter [Fastred](Skyrim_Fastred.md). He is rather overprotective of his daughter who wishes to become an adventurer. When speaking to his wife, he admits to her that he is handling their daughter a bit hard and is simply afraid that he'll lose his only daughter.

Jofthor forbids Fastred from eloping with [Bassianus Axius](Skyrim_Bassianus_Axius.md), believing that her lovestruck attitude will pass. In addition, with Ivarstead's youth mostly gone in search of better opportunities, he is very reluctant to let her go.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellstar_Farm_Key) to the farmhouse, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"You a pilgrim or just passing through?"*
- *"My daughter's driving me crazy, so forgive me if I'm a bit on edge."*
- *"If you ever settle down to have children, think twice before you do it."*
- *"Any idea how I can get my daughter's head out of the clouds?"*
- *"Watch yourself on High Hrothgar, I hear wolves prowl the steps."*
- *"I can't believe Fastred ran off to Riften with that... that bastard."*
- *"I can't believe Fastred's gone. I... I should have been nicer to her."*

When spoken to:

- **Anything you can tell me about High Hrothgar?**
- *"They call the path to the monastery the 7,000 Steps. Can you imagine? I'm not certain if I could even make it to the top without collapsing from exhaustion."*

His goodbyes:

- *"Just get out of town as fast as you can. Nothing for anyone here."*

### Conversations

- **Boti**: *"You have to let Fastred follow her heart. You can't tell her what to do."*
- **Jofthor**: *"She's our only child, Boti. Am I to throw her to the wolves?"*
- **Boti**: *"You're hardly throwing her to the wolves. She just wants to see the world... just like I did when I was her age."*
- **Jofthor**: *"And if she decides to leave Ivarstead, what then? No, no... I can't bear the thought."*

- **Jofthor:** *"Fastred, I wanted to talk with you."*
- **Fastred:** *"What is it now?"*
- **Jofthor:** *"Wilhelm said he saw you speaking to one of the pilgrims on the 7,000 Steps. I told you that I didn't want you to go up there."*
- **Fastred:** *"I know what you said, but I only wanted to speak to him for a moment."*
- **Jofthor:** *"Well it stops now, and that's final!"*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Book of Love

- **Your daughter asked me to speak to you.**
- *"I'm sure I know what this is about. She thinks she's in love with Bassianus and wants to leave town. With him. Let me tell you how I see it. Until a few months ago, the girl was head over heels for Klimmek. Wouldn't stop talking about him. Now if I thought she was really in love with Bassianus, that would be one thing. But she's a child. It will pass."*
- **Why do you want her to stay so badly?**
- *"Look around here. There's not much left. Used to be a good-sized town, but folks have been moving to Riften for a while now. If all the young people leave, what happens to Ivarstead?"*
- **You think she'll get over it?**
- *"You know how children are. Her fancies change with the moon. And like always, the boy lacks any kind of spine. He'll need some convincing. A little push and this would all be simpler."*
