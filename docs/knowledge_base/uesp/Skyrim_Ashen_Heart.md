## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read the Matriarch's note
2. Read [Priest Arthamis' Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Arthamis%27_Note)
3. Use the mannequins to solve the armory room puzzle. The solution (left to right) is: Mask of Sotha Sil, Mask of Almalexia, Mask of Vivec.
4. Kill [Erden Relvel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel) and retrieve [Mask of Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur).

## Detailed Walkthrough
After dealing with the ash zombie attack, speak to the Matriarch:

**Is there anything I can do to stop these attacks?** *"Of course. It's all in this note."* She will then hand you a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan%27s_Note). After reading it you will have to head to the armory and pick up and read [Priest Arthamis' Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Arthamis%27_Note).

### Armory Puzzle
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-quest-Ashen_Heart_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Ashen_Heart_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Ashen_Heart_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Ashen_Heart_02.jpg) Correct order The solution for the armory puzzle can be deduced from the banner above the locked gate. The solution (left to right) is: Mask of Sotha Sil, Mask of Almalexia, Mask of Vivec. The other mannequins are irrelevant.

Open the chest to find [Hopesfire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hopesfire) and [Erden Relvel's note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel%27s_Note), read the note and you will have to then travel to the graveyard of [Tel Mithryn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn).

You will have to kill the ash zombies and Erden Relvel, you can then loot his body for the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur), the [Robe of the Lich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Robe_of_the_Lich) and a [Fahlbtharz Forge Gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fahlbtharz_Forge_Gem).

## Bugs
- Just adding the masks to the mannequins may not result in the door opening. In this case, removing the other armor from one or more of the mannequins may trigger the door to open.

- The mannequin might spawn with 2 set of identical armor where one is invisible this can cause the corresponding mask and armor to be marked as Quest item (if removed) and unable to re-equip on the mannequin ( You can drop the item but you can't equip it and it somehow prevent the right mask to be equipped on the mannequin , On "MAKE A QUICK SAVE BEFORE INTERACTING WITH ANY MANNEQUIN " and open console and select the mannequin and perform a "resetinventory" then equip the mask on the mannequin you shouldn't need to do this for the one in the middle just equip the mask on the display case on it but do the same if it's doesn't work.
