[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hjaalmarch.jpg) The salt marshes of Hjaalmarch **Hjaalmarch** is a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in northern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with its capital in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). It is initially affiliated with the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), though the [Jarl](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) laments the war and reportedly co-operates with the Empire only as far as their goals align. It can, however be captured by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) after completing [A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).md) and [The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks).md).

Despite its position just east of the capital of Skyrim, [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), Hjaalmarch is remote and isolated. The enormous, festering swamp dominating the northern part of the hold could be a factor, as could be the tall, snowy mountains in the south, and the ancient, cursed city of [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) blocking a possible trade route to [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md).

Hjaalmarch is one of the poorest holds in Skyrim, second only to [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md), and is sustained by the Morthal lumber mill and the mining town of [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md). [Jarl Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) takes a hands-off approach to leadership; her people are unhappy with her governance and several will complain openly.

## Topographical Overview
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Swamp_Fungal_Pod.jpg) Swamp fungal pods are endemic to Hjaalmarch Hjaalmarch is well known for the salt marshes which dominate the north and central areas of the hold, stretching from [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) in the west to [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) in the east, formed by the River Hjaal breaking into many tiny streams. Much of the rest of the hold more resembles neighboring holds, for example, in the south and east of the hold, the terrain is more resemblant of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) along the border with that hold - snowy pine forest and plains broken up by crags around [the Stormcloak camp](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp.md) and Ustengrav. The southern half is mountainous terrain above the snow line, although in the west, the mountains give way to the Hjaal river valley, where the climate is more resemblant of neighboring [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). Beyond Fort Snowhawk, in the hold's western extremities lie pine forests and meadows, much like those found just across the [Karth River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River) in [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md).

### Flora and Fauna
The swamp boasts the highest numbers of wild [deathbell plants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) in Skyrim, as well as being the only place to find [swamp fungal pods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod) and [giant lichen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen). These ingredients all boast powerful poison effects for [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md), making Hjaalmarch an ideal place to visit for assassins wishing to brew deadly poisons. Other plants include [canis roots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella), [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass) and a few scattered [nightshade plants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade). The snowy areas have few usable plants besides the humble [snowberry bush](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry), but there are some [blue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) and [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) to be found, with the blue variant especially abundant around Morthal.

As for fauna, all manner of beasts roam the swamp; even [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) might ambush you at night, but more commonly [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) and [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md) lurk between the trees. Of course, [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) are always in abundance, from the swamp to the banks of the River Hjaal. Your typical predators, [bears](Skyrim_Bear.md) and [sabre cats](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md), and their prey, [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) and [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md), roam the south, but there are also more trolls to watch out for. Passive creatures include large amounts of [fish](Skyrim_Fish.md) in the shallow waters with [dragonflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonfly) circling above.

### Routes and Pathways
A single paved road runs from the west near [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md), past [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) (where [the residents](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) may attack you even if you stay on the path) and [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), curving north around the [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md) area before entering the Pale.

The most notable pathway starts southwest of Stonehills and ends in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) between [Hamvir's Rest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hamvir%27s_Rest) and [Silent Moons Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Moons_Camp), passing through the ruins of Labyrinthian along the way. Other paths run from the road east of Fort Snowhawk south to [Dead Men's Respite](Skyrim_Dead_Men%27s_Respite.md), and north from Morthal to [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md). Beyond that the swamp is pathless, however, so beware of getting lost.

### [Daedric Quests](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md)

### [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)
- **[Kill Lurbuk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Lurbuk)**: Silence a [terrible bard](Skyrim_Lurbuk.md), permanently.
- **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md)**: Repay your debt to [the Family](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), and show you are willing to kill on command.
- **[Kill Maluril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Maluril)**: Kill [Maluril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Maluril) in [Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md).

### [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md)
- **[Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md)**: Unveil the truth about a deceitful [King](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_One-Eye) and become a member of the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md).

### [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md)
