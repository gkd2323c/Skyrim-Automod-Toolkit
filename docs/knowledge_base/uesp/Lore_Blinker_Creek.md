Blinker Creek is a body of water where the eponymous Battle of Blinker Creek occurred.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup> At some point between [4E 26](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_26) and [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), Crown Prince [Attrebus Mede](Lore_Attrebus_Mede.md) took part in the Battle of Blinker Creek. Similarly to his many other battles before [Umbriel Crisis](Lore_Umbriel_Crisis.md), he did not lose more than three of his personal guard in a single battle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:126</sup>
