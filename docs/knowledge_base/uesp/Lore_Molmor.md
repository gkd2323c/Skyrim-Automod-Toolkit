# Molmor

The Molmor was the most powerful of the numerous colossal sea creatures unleashed by the [Sloads](Lore_Sload.md) upon the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md) during the Thrassian War, a fierce conflict between the [Sloads](Lore_Sload.md) of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) and the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md). Tales describe the Molmor in various ways, recounting that it resembled a massive [whale](Lore_Whale.md), an [octopus](Lore_Octopus.md) with grasping tentacles, or even "veritable forests of sharp spines, each longer than the lance of a knight".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BATM-1)</sup>

## History
Molmor wreaked havoc on [Galen](Lore_Galen.md)'s coasts for seven years. Countless attempts by the All Flags Navy to defeat it ended in failure. Eventually, Baron-Admiral [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) acknowledged the necessity of external aid and reached out for assistance, enticing individuals with a reward for subduing the creature. At some point between [1E 2250](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2250) and [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260),<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> [Belaigh](Lore_Belaigh.md), a young druid of the [Eldertide Circle](Lore_Eldertide_Circle.md), accepted the challenge. She gathered elements from Galen's land and sea, confronting the Molmor in the western bay. With a powerful invocation, she cast a mixture into its maw, summoning vines from within. The Molmor thrashed and fell back into the sea, transforming into a rocky structure adorned with seaweed and a small forest. Its remains became [Molmor Islet](Lore_Molmor_Islet.md), a small island off Galen's western shore. Belaigh's triumph compelled Bendu Olo to honor her request to safeguard the trees and stones of Galen, thus preserving the island from further exploitation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BATM-1)</sup>
