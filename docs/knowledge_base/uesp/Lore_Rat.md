# Rat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/ON-creature-Rat.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Rat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Rat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Rat.jpg) A rat (ESO) *Regular urban pests are not worth mentioning. They are usually more frightened of adventurers than vice versa. There exist, however, giant varieties that are the sharks of their swamps and sewers. Their strength lies in their ferocity, the size of their packs, and the virulence of the disease transmitted by their bites. A seasoned, well-armored adventurer can pass through swarms of the little beasts in relative safety, but underestimating their brute cunning can be the last mistake that the less experienced will ever make.*
— *[Daggerfall User's Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Daggerfall_User%27s_Guide)* Rats are rodents common to all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), closely related to [mice](Lore_Mice.md). They can be found both in city sewers and the wilderness, and are known to carry a variety of [diseases](Lore_Disease.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skeevara-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BAJ-2)</sup> They also can vary in size greatly. In addition to their standard diet, rats have been documented to consume the flesh of [men](Lore_Man.md) and [mer](Lore_Mer.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSRats-3)</sup> [Skeevers](Lore_Skeever.md) are similar large rodents found commonly in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skeevara-1)</sup>

The [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) rat is seemingly not as aggressive as its other provincial counterparts, but they are widely prevalent in Cyrodiil, equally in basement dwellings, plains, caves, and ruins.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BAJ-2)</sup> [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) is home to several species of giant rat, such as waste rats,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> rust rats, and cave rats.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BGN-5)</sup> The rats of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) are known to prey upon [swamp leviathans](Lore_Swamp_Leviathan.md), overwhelming them by the hundreds and eating the massive creatures inside out.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AAB4-6)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
Rats are often kept by [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) for food.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OBPrima-7)</sup><sup>:53</sup> Packrats (or pack rats) from [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) are rats trained to carry or drag items and sold as cheap pack animals. Their meat purportedly can also be used to make a good stew in an emergency.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Rerlas-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWGen Alit-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

There is an obscure [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) deity known as the [Rat-god](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rat-god),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> whose followers can be found at Alizahad.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGFP-12)</sup>
