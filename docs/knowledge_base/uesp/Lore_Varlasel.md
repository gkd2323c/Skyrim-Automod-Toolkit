# Varlasel

**Varlasel** (meaning Hall of Stars in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md)) may refer to four things:

- Varlasel, one of the ruins of [Erokii](Lore_Erokii.md)
- [Kemen Varlasel](Lore_Kemen.md), a subsection of the Valus Mountains ruins of Kemen
- [Nornal Varlasel](Lore_Nornal.md), a subsection of the Heartlands ruins of Nornal
- [Varondo Varlasel](Lore_Varondo.md), a subsection of the Colovian Highlands ruins of Varondo
