The Summoner *Poem on Faindor the Prodigy*
---
Faindor the Prodigy,
Used his deficient [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md),
To summon [Scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) and [Vermai](Lore_Vermai.md),
For the whims of the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md)

He was young, his face so fresh,
His mistake was what he deigned to say,
The summoned now torment his flesh,
To the horror of Alanwe
