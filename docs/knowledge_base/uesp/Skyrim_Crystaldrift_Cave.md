[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-place-Crystaldrift_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Crystaldrift_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crystaldrift_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Crystaldrift_Cave.jpg) Crystaldrift Cave **Crystaldrift Cave** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md). It contains only one zone, *[Crystaldrift Cave](#Crystaldrift_Cave)*.

It serves as the final resting place for an insane [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Gadnor.md), with a shrine to [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) built to watch over him and bring peace to weary travelers.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The path to the cave is a short way past [Snow-Shod Farm](Skyrim_Snow-Shod_Farm.md) coming from Riften. It winds its way around before reaching a fork in the path: taking the right-hand path takes you past the cave entrance. It then loops around the rock outcrop that the cave is formed from, passing a path to [Lost Tongue Overlook](Skyrim_Lost_Tongue_Overlook.md) and a [dragon mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md) before completing the loop along the left-hand path. As you approach the cave, there are up to two leveled animals standing guard by a dead [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md). Blood is splattered on the ground around the cave entrance. Following the loop past the cave, to the south, you come to a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) on the right after a short distance.

### Crystaldrift Cave
As you enter the cave, there will be a random grouping of animals which may attack each other first. After descending an earthen ramp and entering the cave proper, there is a pile of eight portions of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md) that have been knocked out of a long open box in front of you. To the right, on a large rock against the northwest wall is a [shrine of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Kynareth), with a random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a [plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate) holding five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), and several lit candles around it. With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, you can also find the journal *[Gadnor's Last Wishes](Skyrim_Gadnor%27s_Last_Wishes.md)* on the shrine, which gives instructions on how to obtain the artifact [Nerveshatter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nerveshatter). In the western quarter of the cave is a camp consisting of a campfire, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) hanging over it and a couple of pots holding three [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and three [carrots](Skyrim_Carrot.md). There is a hay pile against the west wall, with a rack to the right holding two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md). To the left of the hay pile, against the southwest wall is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing minor loot. There is also a barrel containing [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) with an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) on top near the fire.

On the other side of the fire next to a rock pillar is a crate with a basket holding two [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md) and two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and a few more loose vegetables on top. In the center of the cave is a large flat-topped rock. Resting on top of the slab are the remains of a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) adventurer named [Gadnor](Skyrim_Gadnor.md) with his unique [Staff of Charming](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor%27s_Staff_of_Charming) lying next to him. He carries a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2)*. Around the rock are three [dragon's tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md) plants, with several fungi found throughout the cave, including five [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), five [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md), and one [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md). To the northeast, near a shallow pool of water, is an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md). At the rear of the cave, a short way up an earthen ramp against the south wall, is a [moonstone ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein).
