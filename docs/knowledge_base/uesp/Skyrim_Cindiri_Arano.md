[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-npc-Cindiri_Arano.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Cindiri_Arano.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cindiri_Arano.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cindiri_Arano.jpg) Cindiri Arano **Cindiri Arano** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) who lives in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). She is the wife of [Adril](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md), the Second Councilor of Raven Rock. She is originally from [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md), though she hasn't seen the city in fifty years, and has spent some time in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). She desperately wants to return to Blacklight, but she can't convince Adril to leave [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md)'s side. She's concerned for her husband's safety, believing his duties put him in harm's way. She's lost an important [folio](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lusty_Argonian_Maid_Folio) and is looking for someone to retrieve it.

At 6am, she heads to [Morvayn Manor](Skyrim_Morvayn_Manor.md) to have a two-hour breakfast with Councilor Morvayn and her husband. She then hangs around the manor until 2pm, when she goes to [the Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md). She leaves at 4pm and returns to Morvayn Manor. She leaves at 8pm with her husband, and they have dinner at the Retching Netch until midnight.

Cindiri wears [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991) with [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993), and she carries an [elven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morvayn_Manor_Key) to Morvayn Manor, and a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rick).

## Dialogue
Her potential greetings are:

*"As the wife to the Second Councilor of Raven Rock, it would be my pleasure to help you any way that I can."* *"I can't believe Miraak made us build those shrines. To take our free will like that... just horrible."* *"Ugh, I can't believe I was forced to do manual labor at those shrines. It was horrifying..."* *"You've brought Raven Rock back from the brink, traveler. I don't know how to thank you."* *"Your efforts in Raven Rock have proven most invigorating for my husband. I haven't seen him this pleased in many years."* *"You aren't here with bad news regarding my husband, are you? I couldn't bear it."* *"It's been a long time since I walked the ashlands of Vvardenfell. I miss it terribly."* *"Perhaps our town isn't as grand as Mournhold, but it's our home and we must make the most of it."* After she is [freed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md) from [Miraak's](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) influence, she may remark:

*"I feel, different somehow. More myself."* *"I can't tell you how relieved I am that Miraak's gone from my mind. I appreciate what you've done."* When spoken to directly:

**Where are you from originally?** *"Adril and I lived in Blacklight, the capital city of Morrowind. House Redoran has improved Blacklight substantially over the last thousand years... its grandeur rivals that of Mournhold itself. The Rootspire where the Council of Great Houses meet is something to behold."* **It sounds like quite a sight.** *"I haven't seen the city in over fifty years. Even for a Dunmer, that seems like an eternity."* **Doesn't sound that impressive.** *"Hmph. Well, beauty's in the eye of the beholder, I suppose."* **Who's your husband?** *"Adril Arano, Second Councilor of Raven Rock. How I hate that title... damn fool's going to get himself killed over it someday."* **You worry much on your husband's behalf.** *"He's in grave danger, and yet he continues to place himself in harm's way. When I fall asleep at night, all I can do is hope he'll still be there when I awaken. This island was never meant for us. There's too much death here."* **Perhaps you should return to Morrowind.** *"I assure you, if I could convince Adril to do that, I'd be on the next ship back to Blacklight. Unfortunately, Adril and Councilor Morvayn are very close friends, and they will never abandon one another... despite my feelings."* **You haven't told him how you feel?** *"I'm the wife of a Second Councilor and certain things are expected of me. The most important is that I stand by Adril, no matter how I feel. And I intend to do that until we get off this miserable rock."* When exiting conversation she will say:

*"Good journey, traveler."*
