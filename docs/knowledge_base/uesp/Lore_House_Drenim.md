# House Drenim

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/MW-npc-Mavon_Drenim.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Mavon_Drenim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Mavon_Drenim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Mavon_Drenim.jpg) Mavon Drenim, the ranking Telvanni official of Vivec City [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/MW-npc-Galas_Drenim.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Galas_Drenim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Galas_Drenim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Galas_Drenim.jpg) Galas Drenim, Telvanni representative of the Vvardenfell Grand Council House Drenim is a minor house of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) based in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They are featured in the traditional ballad called *[The Battle of Molag Beran](Lore_The_Battle_of_Molag_Beran.md)*, in which they defeated House Retheran on the hills of Molag Beran.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBOMB-1)</sup> Many House Dunmer with this name served in [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), such as Galas Drenim, who represented House Telvanni on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)'s Grand Council,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGalas-2)</sup> and Mavon Drenim, the highest-ranking Telvanni noble that lived in [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWMavon-3)</sup>

*The Battle of Molag Beran* is a classic Dunmer tale, one that everyone knows by heart, and a favorite with locals.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWDulnea-4)</sup> But information surrounding it, such as the date of its occurrence or its authenticity is unknown. Sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Master Drenim was a House Telvanni mage with a middling reputation.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONMaster-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONGilan-6)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Alberic Drenim was a noble who lived in Vivec City and frequented the St. Olms Waistworks. Ethyan Drenim lived in [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md) when the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) occurred.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONEthyan-7)</sup> After a fissure known as the Sundering opened through Chorrol, Ethyan and another person named Larian were sent to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) learn more about what happened. Having got as far as the city bridges, they returned to explain the [Soulburst](Lore_Soulburst.md) and disappearance of Emperor [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TCC-8)</sup> Rilorns Drenim was a beggar in the city of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONRilorns-9)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), several Drenims held high positions in House Telvanni, such as Endar Drenim, a spellwright in [Tel Naga](Lore_Tel_Naga.md), Galas Drenim, the Great House's representative on the Vvardenfell Grand Council based in [Castle Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGalas-2)</sup> and Mavon Drenim, another spellwright and ranking Telvanni noble in Vivec City.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWMavon-3)</sup> Mavon had a writ on them from the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) and prepared an ambush for their arrival in the Telvanni Canton. In the end, however, they were slain and the contract was fulfilled.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWWrit-10)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Sondas Drenim lived in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and worked as a miner all their life. At this time, he lived in [Darkwater Crossing](Lore_Darkwater_Crossing.md), a recently established mining settlement.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SRSondas-11)</sup>
