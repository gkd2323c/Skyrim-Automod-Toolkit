[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-item-Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrows.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrows.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrows.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrows.jpg) A quiver of Bloodcursed Elven Arrows A Bloodcursed Elven Arrow is an [elven arrow](Skyrim_Elven_Arrow.md) that has been tainted with the blood of a Daughter of Coldharbour, a female vampire created by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) himself. The arrows are visually similar to the elven arrows that are needed to create them, with a gray shaft instead of a golden one, with the fletchings being alternating shades of gray. The quiver is similar, being a more bleak color compared to the strong golden one of the normal arrows. When fired at the sun during the day with [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md), it will block out its light for the rest of the day, negating the [damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Damage) [vampires](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) suffer from it. It is the counterpart of the [Sunhallowed Elven Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrow).
