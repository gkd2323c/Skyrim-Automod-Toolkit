## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to the residents of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md).
2. Travel to [Darkwater Pass](Skyrim_Darkwater_Pass.md).
3. Search the cave for [Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md).
4. Free Derkeethus from his prison.
5. Escort Derkeethus out of the cave.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Darkwater Crossing
If you talk to the residents of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) (for example [Sondas Drenim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sondas_Drenim), a miner in nearby [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md)), you will soon hear of an Argonian named [Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md) who recently went to explore the pools around Darkwater - and who hasn't been seen in a long time. The residents will not be able to say for sure where Derkeethus is, but he has actually run into trouble inside [Darkwater Pass](Skyrim_Darkwater_Pass.md).

Locating Darkwater Pass can be difficult. The easiest route from Darkwater Crossing is to cross the bridge that spans the river to the southwest of the settlement. At the crossing, take the right path. Just before you reach the second bridge and [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md), a stone marker to the left of the road will indicate a dusty path heading west up the mountainside. Follow the path as it curves sharply south, and onward through the trees along the cliff's edge. Eventually you will come to a large pool fed by three large waterfalls. Drop into the pool and turn directly around to the north - an iron door in the cliff wall in front of you leads to Darkwater Pass.

### Darkwater Pass
Follow the low tunnel past an unlocked chest, and you will reach a small cavern. Watch out - a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) will drop out of a [Falmer hive](Skyrim_Falmer_Hive.md) in the wall immediately to your right as you enter. Follow the path up into a larger cavern. A Falmer waits immediately ahead outside a tent, and yet another may drop from a hive above and to the left. In the north wall is an opening into a pillared chamber where three [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) reside. Head past this chamber and up the stairs to reach a small room with a trap door in the center. Look down through the bars to see [Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md) pacing about in his cell below. Be mindful of the Falmer in the adjoining room with an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md) (step in here and two more Falmer will drop from the hives up near the ceiling).

Northeast from the center of the trap door, there is a lever on the wall. Pulling this lever lowers a section of wall, revealing a spiral staircase heading down. A corridor in the west wall leads south to a small circular room called Darkwater Pit. As soon as you approach, Derkeethus will engage you in conversation and ask you to help him escape. The [Darkwater Pit Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_Pit_Key) is in the left [Burial Urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md) just inside the gate, but the gate is the standard barred prison cell door with sufficient gaps to allow you to reach through and take the key from the burial urn. If he has lockpicks in his inventory, Derkeethus may have already escaped his cell and be waiting somewhere in the corridor. If this has happened, it may alert the Falmer in the arcane enchanter room.

Although there is still more of Darkwater Pass to explore, the easiest way to get Derkeethus out is to head straight back the way you came - particularly as he will not crouch into a sneaking stance when you do during this quest, and there are some nasty traps and Falmer further up the cave.

Once you have led Derkeethus to safety, he will thank you for saving him. You can now recruit him as a follower if you wish. If not, you can recruit him at any time from Darkwater Crossing.

## Notes
- Derkeethus will only continue to follow you if you remain close to him. If you move too far away he will turn around and return to the pit he started in.
- On rare occasions Derkeethus may appear on the trail between the upper and lower entrances to Darkwater Pass, begging for help escaping the cave. Just lead him to the lower entrance. He will thank you and promptly vanish. He may then be found at Darkwater Crossing.
- This quest can sometimes clash with a [Companions quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue_Mission) where an NPC needs to be rescued, potentially giving you two temporary followers.
