Reynir, also known as **Reynir the Destroyer**, was a great Atmoran warrior who lived in [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md) around the time of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) in the Merethic Era. He was said to have survived the [Night of Tears](Lore_Night_of_Tears.md) and sailed back to Skyrim with the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md) on the ship commanded by [Ylgar](Lore_Ylgar.md), son of Ysgramor. After he died, he was interred in the Hall of the Dead beneath [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Rt D-1)</sup>

During the mid-Second Era, the priests who tended to his crypt noted that he had risen as a powerful Draugr and retained some semblance of his intellect in life, but his humanity has been subsumed by the madness. As such he was no less than a hateful, undead creature who delighted in the suffering of others. As such, a warning was announced to not disturb his slumber.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Rt D-1)</sup> Despite this he was briefly awoken when the Vestige ventured into the crypts in search of Lodur's Crown, but was ultimately defeated.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLost Crown-2)</sup>
