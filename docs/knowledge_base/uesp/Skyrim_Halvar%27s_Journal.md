Halvar's Journal **by [Halvar](Skyrim_Halvar.md)** *Personal diary of a traveling farmhand*
---
Came into town a few days ago looking for work. The folks here are nice enough, and there's plenty of wheat and potatoes needing to be harvested, so I've got a steady supply of coin coming in.

I don't think I'm going to need my old pack goat [Hilda](Skyrim_Hilda.md) for much longer...all my bones are starting to creak, and my adventuring days are long over. Now I just need to find someone willing to take care of her. Maybe get a few coins for her too - there's strength in those old bones of hers yet.

Ah, how I'm going to miss wandering the wilds of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)! I saw so many strange and wonderful things out there - pine trees as tall as mountains, old [nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) tombs, I once even stumbled across a wandering spirit made of pure ice!

Even ran across a few folks trying their hand at taming different types of creatures. Don't know why they'd want anything but a loyal dog or trusty goat like my Hilda though - some of them beasts out in the wild just aren't meant to be tamed.

Ran across an alchemist with a [pet rabbit](Skyrim_Thistle_(creature).md), and almost got robbed by a thief with a [tame fox](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll_(creature).md), but I suppose a rabbit or a fox could be docile enough. But that pickpocket trying to train a [skeever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skritch) must have been as mad as old [Sheo'](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md). And I don't want to think too hard about the [vampire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Naturalist) that almost made a meal out of me...could have sworn it had a trained [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Arachnia.md)! Gives me the shivers just thinking about it.
