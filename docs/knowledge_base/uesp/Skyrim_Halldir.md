[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-creature-Halldir.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Halldir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Halldir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Halldir.jpg) Halldir as he appears after emerging from the energy beam in Halldir's Cairn **Halldir** is a powerful [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) who can be found in [Halldir's Cairn](Skyrim_Halldir%27s_Cairn.md). He is a powerful mage and has been draining the life from anyone unfortunate enough to stray into his lair in order to preserve and eventually restore himself to full life. He carries a unique weapon, [Halldir's Staff](Skyrim_Halldir%27s_Staff.md#Halldir.27s_Staff).

During battle, when he loses a third of his health, he will disappear and split into three different elemental versions of himself. Once these versions are killed, his [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) materializes and can be defeated.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- The clones of Halldir disappear quickly: after around 5-10 seconds. If you're quick enough, you can loot them before they vanish for an Ancient Nordic weapon of [Frost Damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) each, standard Draugr loot, and a high chance for a quantity of Ancient Nordic arrows, despite the clones being spellswords and not archers.
- Halldir's clones are one of two instances of Draugr in the game that use all elements (fire, frost and shock) against you; the other is [Jyrik Gauldurson](Skyrim_Jyrik_Gauldurson.md), found in [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md). All other spellsword Draugr use frost magic exclusively.
