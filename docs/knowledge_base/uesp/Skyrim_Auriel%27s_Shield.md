[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg/200px-SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) Auriel's Shield Auriel's Shield is a powerful shield allegedly created and used by the elven god [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md). It can be found wielded by a [Falmer Warmonger](Skyrim_Falmer_Warmonger.md) in the [Forgotten Vale Forest](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale_Forest.md) after using the [Ruby Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby_Paragon) on the paragon portal. After 5 successful blocks, the shield will start glowing and a shockwave can be released by power bashing, which becomes stronger with each 5 blocked attacks. After 15 blocked attacks, the shield is fully charged and the shockwave is similar to the effect of the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout.

## Notes
- Unlike normal power bashes, using the fully charged shield to bash the opponent during a [brawl](Skyrim_Brawl.md) violates the rules of the brawl and will give you an assault bounty.
- [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup> and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- If you are a werewolf and use the maximum power bash, a message will appear informing you that you consumed a heart and the werewolf perk has increased. <sup>**?**</sup>
- When you take the shield partially charged from the Falmer it may not release the power. <sup>**?**</sup> - To fix this you can drop it on the ground and ask your follower to take it. Now get it back from their inventory and it should be working.
