Epitaph of Bjarfrud Skjoralmor *An epitaph to the founder of Falkreath*
---

- Here lies the stone upon which [this city](Lore_Falkreath.md) was found,
- Took the shape of [a man](Lore_Bjarfrud_Skjoralmor.md) whose deeds saw him crowned.
- Thrice he drove the beastly host from their heathen wood,
- So he hewed it down and planted this town,
- On the very spot that he stood.
