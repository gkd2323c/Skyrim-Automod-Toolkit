[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Kambria was found Kambria was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) march in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) located in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It shared borders with the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) to the north, [Koegria](Lore_Koegria.md) to the east, [Bhoriane](Lore_Bhoriane.md) to the south, and [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md) to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMap-1)</sup> The provincial seat was the city of Kambria.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup> The province rose from lowlands in the south to a windswept plateau in the north, where towns were situated in valleys for shelter. The tops of the hills in the area were once covered with forts, but these were demolished by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) in order to reduce potential points of resistance.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1High Rock-3)</sup> The regional deity was [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md), and the dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region was [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

The ruler of Kambria was allied with the Baron of Dwynnen during the war against the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) and helped turn the tide of the war against the Usurper.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TFot U-4)</sup> The city of Kambria is known to possess several libraries, and has been described as picturesque.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CK1-5)</sup>
