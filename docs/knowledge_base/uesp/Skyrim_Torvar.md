[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-npc-Torvar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torvar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torvar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torvar.jpg) Torvar **Torvar**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is the drunken misfit of the [Companions](Skyrim_The_Companions.md) of [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md). Initially of a friendly disposition, his attitude towards you seems to sour as you rise through the ranks, but he still becomes a potential [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) after the Companions questline is completed. It's also possible to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Torvar once the Harbinger rank is achieved.

He wears a simple set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) with matching [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots). In combat, he relies on a [Skyforge steel sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Sword) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield). His only other possession is a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Notes
- Although his default gear is governed by the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill, Torvar is actually more proficient in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md).
