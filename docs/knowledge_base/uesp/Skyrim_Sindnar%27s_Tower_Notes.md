Sindnar's Tower Notes **by Sindnar** *Notes on the mage tower called Myrwatch* *
---
Deep in the swamps of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), where the branches are gnarled and the mist is thick, lies a tower of unknown provenance.

Few in the province dare speak the name [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md), and those that do couch their words with apprehension and fear. Yet the mystery of the tower only piques my interest further, and so I have made it my life's work to discover its secrets.

What I can deduce of its past comes from its present. From the cut of the stone to the wards that surround it, every piece is a clue, a signature that can be traced back to a pen and hand.

The stone seal that guards the entrance is none other than the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Aurbic_Eye.md), a symbol born from the legacy of [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md). Its burning gaze can be found not just in Myrwatch, but outside the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md).

Could it be the mages here were cast out from the college, and set out to carve their own path? This is but a theory, and yet, one I feel has merit.

Nevertheless, without access to the mind of its occupants, true insight will remain frustratingly out of grasp.
