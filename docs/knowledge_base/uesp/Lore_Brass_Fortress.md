[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/ON-place-Brass_Fortress_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Brass_Fortress_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brass_Fortress_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brass_Fortress_02.jpg) The Brass Fortress, circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Brass Fortress is near the center of [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), and provides safety for people from the dangers of [the Radius](Lore_The_Radius.md). Both are inhabited by [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricants.md), [Factotums](Lore_Factotum.md), and people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup>

Upon entering the Brass Fortress, one is greeted by a towering statue of Sotha Sil as well as a towering skyline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup> The central plaza surrounding the statue contains many of the goods and services to the people of the fortress.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup> The northern section of the city lies the Clockwork Basilica, where the Clockwork Apostles conduct their research.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup> To the east side of the city lies the slums of Slag Town and houses the Tarnished and east of that lies the Machine District which contains the Fabricant production plant known as the Incarnatorium.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup> To the west of the Fortress is the Reactor District.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup> It contains the Asylum Sanctorum, used to seal the Dunmer Saints Felms, Llothis, and Olms as their clockwork vessels caused them to turn mad overtime.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BFESO-1)</sup>
