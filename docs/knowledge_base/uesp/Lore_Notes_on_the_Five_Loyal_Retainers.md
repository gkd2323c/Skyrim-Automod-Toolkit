Notes on the Five Loyal Retainers **by [Narsis Dren](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Narsis_Dren)** *A description of the five loyal retainers of King Dynar*
---
The [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) collapsed in the face of the [Alessian Slave Revolt](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), its Sorcerer-Kings crushed by the rising power of the [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) rebels. Not all the ancient [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) rulers were overthrown, however, and some city-states threw in with the former slaves, either out of self-preservation or because they felt the Nedic cause was just.

[Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md) was one of these states, under the rulership of [King Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md). As a result, his kingdom survived the fall of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) and continued as a vassal state to the new Alessian rulership. Over time, these vassal states were absorbed into the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). Ultimately, Emperor Amiel <Note in the margin: "Or is it [Ami-El](Lore_Ami-El.md)?"> issued an ultimatum to expel the surviving Ayleids and take over their kingdoms. King Dynar fled before the Alessian armies, and in the confusions *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* the symbols of his office— [Crown](Lore_Crown_of_Nenalata.md), [Scepter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Nenalata), and Emblem —were lost. <Note in the margin: "Check with Elfbetta about the year. Original sources are unclear.>

Dynar had five loyal retainers in his service. He entrusted the Emblem to them, thereafter known as the Emblem of the Last King. The loyal retainers we know were:

Lor-Emero, a noble knight in the service of the king.
Parhaelia, also known as the Terrible Fist.
Ballepelin the Boisterous, a noted adventurer of great humor and girth.
Yondvarla, also known as the Oracle.

These four were all Ayleids. The last retainer, whose name has been lost to history <Note in the margin: "The One Unnamed?"> was apparently Nedic, though his origin, along with his name, remain unknown. To those Five Loyal Retainers Dynar entrusted his Emblem, and they retreated to a haven that I believe is now called the ruins of [Wendir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wendir). The ruins were also rumored to hold one of the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md) —ancient statues from the Fall of the Empire, but I have yet to confirm this. This may be why this location was chosen.

The legends I (well, Elfbetta) tracked down indicated that the One Unnamed was the last to arrive, and this Nedic retainer sealed the doors to the underground crypts beneath Wendir, though whether physically or by [magic](Lore_Magic.md) is unclear. The One Unnamed may have been a great spellcaster, for the legends say that the Five Loyals protect the Emblem to the present day.
