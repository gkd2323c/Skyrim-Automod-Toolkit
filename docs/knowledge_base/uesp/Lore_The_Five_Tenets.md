The Five Tenets *The honorable code of the Dark Brotherhood*
---
*Note: The following text appears in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) on a wall of the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), though no book actually appears in-game.*

---
Tenet 1: Never dishonor the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md). To do so is to invoke the [Wrath](Lore_Wrath_of_Sithis.md) of [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md).

Tenet 2: Never betray the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) or its secrets. To do so is to invoke the Wrath of Sithis.

Tenet 3: Never disobey or refuse to carry out an order from a Dark Brotherhood superior. To do so is to invoke the Wrath of Sithis.

Tenet 4: Never steal the possessions of a Dark Brother or Dark Sister. To do so is to invoke the Wrath of Sithis.

Tenet 5: Never kill a Dark Brother or Dark Sister. To do so is to invoke the Wrath of Sithis.
