[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-npc-Annekke_Crag-Jumper.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Annekke_Crag-Jumper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Annekke_Crag-Jumper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Annekke_Crag-Jumper.jpg) Annekke Crag-Jumper **Annekke Crag-Jumper** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) and former adventurer who lives in [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) with her husband [Verner Rock-Chucker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker). The couple has put their exploration days behind them, but while Verner is seemingly happy with the more relaxed lifestyle, Annekke has a hard time settling down and will ask you to do some adventuring for her during the [related quest](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md). Once you do this favor for her she is available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).

She claims to be the one who discovered [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md), a likely claim since [her house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_and_Annekke%27s_House) is the only building in town. She sleeps in the house every night between midnight and 6am and always starts the day with a one-hour meal, followed by yet another hour in bed. At 8am she ventures outside and spends three hours wandering restlessly around town until her workday in the mine begins at 11am. After five hours of mining, she leaves the mine at 4pm. She then spends the rest of the day wandering around town interrupted only by a one-hour dinner at 6pm.

Annekke wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) with [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699) and a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#_000261c1). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) with twelve [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Events

### Kill the Bandit Leader
When asked if she used to be an adventurer, Annekke will immediately start to tell stories:

- **You were an adventurer?**
- *"That's right. Used to explore all over. I'm the one who originally found the mine Darkwater Crossing was built around. I was actually scouting a bit the other day, old times sake. Spied a brigand heading back to his camp. Up for a little hunting? My husband is being a sad stick, so I can't go myself."*

- **Forget it.**
- *"Fair enough."*

- **I'll do it.**
- *"This brings back memories. Come back when you're done and maybe I'll be up to showing you a few tricks."*

When exiting conversation she will remind you:

- *"Bandits are cowards. Don't strain yourself chasing them all down."*

When you return to her after killing the bandit leader, Annekke will be impressed:

- *"You remind me of myself a few years ago. Let me show you a little bit about wearing light armor. It's helped me out whenever I was scouting."*

She will then raise your [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill by one point and add:

- *"Now I've got the itch to travel again. If you're leaving town, I wouldn't mind coming with for a while."*

## Dialogue
When you approach her she can say:

- *"A traveler? I'll be * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * you've seen some sights. We should swap tales sometime."*
- *"There's a gorgeous [tree](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md) that grows right up through a cave to the northeast of here."*
- *"If you're heading to [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md), stick to the road. Some pretty dangerous caves between here and there."*
- *"Verner used to love exploring before we got married. Now he's become a bit of a milk drinker. Likes to stay home."*
- *"If you head straight west from where the White River splits towards Whiterun, you can get a look at the [Cradlecrush](Skyrim_Cradlecrush_Rock.md) [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md)."*

While Verner is perfectly happy with his new life as a miner, Annekke has a hard time forgetting the excitement of adventuring and their private talks often center on that very topic:

## Notes
- Although you can't [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Annekke, you can marry her daughter, [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md) —making her both your follower and mother-in-law.
- Despite them being close family, there is no special dialogue when you visit Sylgia with Annekke as a follower.
- Although listed as a Ranger class, unlike [Faendal](Skyrim_Faendal.md) and [Jenassa](Skyrim_Jenassa.md) she cannot dual wield most weapons properly. She will dual wield Forsworn weapons. Also, she can do so if you give her a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), just like [Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md), and will use the pickaxe as an offhand weapon.
- As with all [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quests that have multiple quest-givers, you can only have one of them active at a time. If you want to start Annekke's quest and you already have another version of [Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md) active, you need to complete it before you can start this one.
- Annekke incorrectly states that Eldergleam Sanctuary is northeast of Darkwater Crossing. It is directly north of there.
- Her husband, Verner, has different interests than his wife and will say:

- - *"Annekke used to help more with the mining, but nowadays she's a bit of a bounder."*
- *"I do love my wife. Just wish she'd get her head out of the clouds."*
- *"If you want some information about the area, Annekke knows it better than anyone."*
- *"Sometimes I feel like Annekke cares more about finding new rocks than being with me."*

## Bugs
- It appears that she may attack without provocation either when in the mine or out. - This can be resolved by leaving, waiting, and then returning at a later date.
- Another way to solve this is to get a bounty in Eastmarch and pay it off.
- Her radiant quest may conflict with [a Thieves Guild quest](Skyrim_Summerset_Shadows.md) depending on the radiant options. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- Annekke may not give her radiant quest, ever, even if you have no other active quests that could possibly conflict with it. The other three NPCs ([Ahtar](Skyrim_Ahtar.md), [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md) or [Fanari Strong-Voice](Skyrim_Fanari_Strong-Voice.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>) that can give a version of the radiant quest appear unaffected, even while Annekke is glitched. This makes it impossible to acquire Annekke as a follower, and she will only give ambient dialogue instead of entering into a conversation tree. - Rescuing Derkeethus from Darkwater Pass, having him as an active follower or if you had recently had him as a follower might fix this.(Dismiss your active follower, take Derkeethus as follower, close the dialogue, open up again and dismiss him. Your original follower shouldn't have gone far away and can be asked to follow again even before finding and talking to Annekke.)
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to change her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) to Friend can fix this, allowing her to become your follower.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Annekke_Crag-Jumper)*]</sup>
