The Silvenar Vale (also known as the **Silvenar Grove**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup> is the northernmost region of the [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md) region, closer inland to the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovian_West.md) than the rest of Malabal Tor. The region is long, beginning towards the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md), all the way to the [Xylo River Basin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xylo_River_Basin). The region is named after the regional capital, the city-state of [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md), which serves as the seat of power for [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md). Most Bosmeri settlements exist in the area ranging from Valeguard and the landmark known as [Ouze](Lore_Ouze.md). The [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) township called the [Baandari Trading Post](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Baandari_Trading_Post) is on the border of the [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md), which is evident with the Colovian architecture in the Trading Post.
