— [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup> Bhag (also stylized as **BHAG**), known as **the Two-Tongued**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup> ** the Great Debater** and **the Counterargument**,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-500MC-2)</sup> was a powerful [Tongue](Lore_Tongue.md) and one of the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) war chiefs who took part in the [Conquest](Lore_Conquest_of_Morrowind.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) by the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

## History

### First Era
Little is known of Bhag's early life. The precise date of his birth is unknown, although he was alive during the lifespan of the Nordic Empire, in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and the Conquest of Morrowind.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGEMW-3)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1ES-4)</sup> He was one of several war leaders known to wield the Voice vilified by Dunmer historical accounts as a "demon chieftain". According to the Vivec, he looked like a great bearded king and wielded powers of Surety and Form Change. Alhtough his raiders were not numerous they ran amok in the west hinterlands defeating numerous Velothi scouts and trappers. Vivec described his duel with Bhag as a profound debate that ended in Bhag's defeat, noting that only the warrior-poet could comprehend the Nord's two-layered speech. Even though the ALMSIVI was forced to remain invisible throughout the exchange.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

### Legacy
Bhag was named by Vivec alongside [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md), the Mouth of Mud, [Chemua](Lore_Chemua.md), the Running Hunger, [Barfok](Lore_Barfok.md), Maid of Planes, and [Ysmir, Dragon of the North Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md), as one of the five greatest and most dangerous chieftains of the conquering Nords in his *[Sermons](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>
