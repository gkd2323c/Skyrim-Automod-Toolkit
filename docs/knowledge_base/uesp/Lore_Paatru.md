# Paatru

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) Black Marsh, the homeland of the Paatru Paatru are a breed and tribe of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) that live in the inner parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), close to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> Paatru have some distinct differences in their appearance compared to the Argonians most commonly seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> as they are described to be [toadlike](Lore_Toad.md). They and several of the other [Middle Argonia](Lore_Middle_Argonia.md) tribes are reputed to kill outsiders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Two toad-like Argonians, Peel and Footsteps-Pad, were introduced into [*The Blue Dawn*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loranna%27s_RP/The_Blue_Dawn) role-playing campaign by [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson). As this RP was also the debut of the [Naga](Lore_Naga.md) Argonians, it is likely these were two were meant to be of (or inspired) the Paatru. In the RP, Ted Peterson describes Peel as having a long tongue and often hopping around, and states he never left Black Marsh before. The other, Footsteps-Pad, is described as six feet tall and muscular.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPThe Blue Dawn-3)</sup>
