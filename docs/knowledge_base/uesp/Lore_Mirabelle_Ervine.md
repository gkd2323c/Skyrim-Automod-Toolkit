Mirabelle Ervine was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) mage who served as a Master Wizard of the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md), reporting directly to Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Lore_Savos_Aren.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). She was commonly acknowledged by other college mages as running day-to-day operations for him <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRFaralda-1)</sup> .

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), when the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) joined the college, Mirabelle gave them their first tour and directed them to their first lessons with teacher [Tolfdir](Lore_Tolfdir.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FL-2)</sup> After the Dragonborn and Tolfdir discovered the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md) within [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), Mirabelle tasked the Dragonborn with venturing to the [dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins of [Mzulft](Lore_Mzulft.md) to learn the location of the [Staff of Magnus](Lore_Staff_of_Magnus.md). Sometime later inside the Hall of Elements, Mirabelle, Savos, and the Dragonborn confronted the college's [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) advisor [Ancano](Lore_Ancano.md), where he was abusing the power of the Eye of Magnus. A subsequent magical explosion blasted them all away from Ancano, and Mirabelle was gravely injured. In the immediate aftermath, she asked the Dragonborn to find Savos, who was discovered dead in the college's courtyard. Mirabelle then tasked the Dragonborn with journeying to [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md) to retrieve the Staff of Magnus. When the Dragonborn returned with the Staff, Tolfdir informed the Dragonborn that Mirabelle had died while staying behind trying to make sure the other mages escaped. She was avenged shortly afterward when the Dragonborn defeated Ancano, saving the college. Tolfdir would succeed her as Master Wizard.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RTU-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRCQ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSOM-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TEOM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-7)</sup>
