[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha_03.jpg) Miraak absorbing a dragon soul Kruziikrel was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Dominate-Enlightenment" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PAR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DLMNM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOS-3)</sup>

Little is known about Kruziikrel's past. At a certain point in history, he swore allegiance to [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), though whether he did so willingly or was compelled through Miraak's mastery of [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) is unknown.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ASA-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) traveled to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) to challenge Miraak. Through the use of the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), they swayed a dragon [Sahrotaar](Lore_Sahrotaar.md) to betray Miraak. He helped them to reach the First Dragonborn on the summit of Apocrypha where the Last Dragonborn confronted Miraak. It was there where he called the mighty dragons [Relonikiv](Lore_Relonikiv.md) and Kruziikrel to aid him in the battle. During the fight Miraak absorbed Kruziikrel's soul to regain vitality, killing him in the process.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ASA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Miraak-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PRIMA-6)</sup><sup>:604-605</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Kruziikrel was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAD-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Arngeir-8)</sup>
