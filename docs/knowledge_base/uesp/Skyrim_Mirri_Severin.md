[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-npc-Mirri_Severin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mirri_Severin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirri_Severin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mirri_Severin.jpg) Mirri Severin **Mirri Severin** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) living in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). She is known as the daughter of [Vendil](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md) and [Tilisu Severin](Skyrim_Tilisu_Severin.md). Her family arrived in Raven Rock about ten years ago and have worked their way up to be very rich and respected members of the community.

Mirri wears a blue [Dunmer outfit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Outfit) with matching [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Shoes). She carries a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) up to [daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality, along with the [key to the manor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor_Key) and a [selection of upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

She sleeps from midnight to 6am, after which she wanders around the manor for 3 hours before going to the marketplace. After 4 hours of browsing, Mirri heads back home for some relaxation until 6pm, when she heads out again for 4 hours of drinking at [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) until it is midnight, when she heads back home to rest.

## Related Quests
- **[Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md)**: Save [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) from an assassination plot.
- **[It's All In The Taste](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:It%27s_All_In_The_Taste)**: Distribute [Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md)'s [Sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Sujamma) to the citizens of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

## Dialogue
If you are male she has some unique geetings:

*"Mmm. Always a pleasure to watch when a man like you walks by."* *"It gets lonely out here you know, on this remote island."* The next set of greetings will appear regardless of your sex:

*"Oh, hello. You looking for a handout or something?"* *"My father and mother are the richest on the island... and that includes Councilor Morvayn."* *"If you ask me, my father knows how to run things better than the Councilor."* Should a beggar approach her in the market, she will be disgusted, *"Ugh, get away from me."* When exiting conversation with Mirri, she will either flirt and say, *"Don't stay away for too long."* if you are male, or give you a standard *"Next time, then."* if you are female.

## Conversations
When in Severin Manor, Mirri will talk with the rest of her "family", revealing that their family is just a front for an unspecified [job](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md) and that she and "her father" are actually lovers:

**Vendil**: *"I'm worried. I think she knows."*
**Mirri**: *"She's jealous. Can't say that I blame her."*
**Vendil**: *"Have you taken leave of your senses? We could be exposed... ruined. Ten years of planning all for nothing!"*
**Mirri**: *"It's your choice, "father". Anytime you want to end it, you just give the word."*

**Mirri**: *"This town bores me. How much longer must we stay in this wretched place?"*
**Tilisu**: *"If everything goes well, we won't have to stay here much longer. For now, we wait."*
**Mirri**: *"What am I supposed to do until then?"*
**Tilisu**: *"I don't know, look for someone else to hop into bed with perhaps!"*

### Served Cold
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg) *"Watch it Tilisu, I think we might have a visitor!"* After your investigation has been reported to the Second Councilor you will be sent to Severin Manor to gather [incriminating evidence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ulen_Matter). Once you're inside, you will notice that you're not alone. Tilisu and Mirri are both located within and should you get detected by Mirri, she will call out to her companion:

*"Watch it Tilisu, I think we might have a visitor!"* *"Stop hiding, coward. I'll make it quick, I promise."* Should you get into a fight with Mirri here, she will angrily yell:

*"For House Hlaalu! For Vilur Ulen!"* Once you have collected your evidence, and returned it to Second Councilor Arano, he will send you to the final location, namely [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md). Mirri will only appear here if she was spared earlier while searching through the manor. Together with several [Morag Tong assassins](Skyrim_Morag_Tong_Assassin.md), she will attempt to make a last stand in the citadel. This time Mirri is clad in a full set of [Morag Tong armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong_Armor). While attacking you, she will have some taunts that she shares with Vendil and Tilisu:

*"For House Hlaalu!"* *"Morvayn will pay for this!"* *"Redoran-following dog!"'* *"I'll scatter your ashes myself!"* *"Morvayn's reign is over!"* When you deliver the killing the blow, she will fall to the ground with her last words being:

*"No, it's not my time..."* Upon her death, you will be able to loot the [Severin Safe Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Safe_Key) from her corpse.
