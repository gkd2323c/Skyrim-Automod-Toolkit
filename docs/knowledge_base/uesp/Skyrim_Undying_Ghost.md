[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Undying_Ghost.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Undying_Ghost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Undying_Ghost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Undying_Ghost.jpg) An Undying Ghost **Undying Ghost** are undead beings summoned by a Novice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell, [Conjure Undying Ghost](Skyrim_Conjure_Undying_Ghost.md) added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

As a ghost they are immune to [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md) and [disease](Skyrim_Disease.md), and have a 25% resistance to [frost](Skyrim_Frost.md). They can also [walk on water](Skyrim_Water_Walking.md). They also have the [Light Foot](Skyrim_Light_Foot.md) perk.

They are equipped with an [Undying Ghost Sword](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Undying_Ghost_Sword) for combat. To defend themselves, they wear Ancient Nord [Boots](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Boots.md), [Armor](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Ancient_Nord_Armor), [Gauntlets](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Ancient_Nord_Gauntlets), and a [Helmet](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Helmet).
