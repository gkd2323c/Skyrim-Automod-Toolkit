Khamira was Queen of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), the northern kingdom of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), from [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Born in [2E 563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563) to King [Hemakar](Lore_Hemakar.md) and Queen [Numara](Lore_Numara.md) of Anequina,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Khamira2Q-1)</sup> she was presumed dead with the rest of her family after [Euraxia Tharn](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md) overthrew her father in the [Frostfall Coup](Lore_Frostfall_Coup.md) of [2E 576](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_576).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFC-2)</sup> She had in fact survived, and covertly took up arms in a resistance movement against Euraxia's rule known as the Northern Elsweyr Defense Force.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gharesh Ri-3)</sup> After unsuccessfully attempting to stop the unwitting release of [Kaalgrontiid](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his rage of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) by [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) in 2E 582,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-THo C-4)</sup> she subsequently helped to defend the city of [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md) from the combined assault of Euraxia's forces and the Dragon Bahlokdaan, and revealed her true identity there after their defeat.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TBf R-5)</sup> After Euraxia's deposition she banished Kaalgrontiid with the help of Abnur Tharn and [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), and was crowned queen soon afterwards.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-JC-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-THo A-7)</sup> As monarch she sought to restore the Elsweyr Confederacy between Anequina and [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Khamira DA-8)</sup>
