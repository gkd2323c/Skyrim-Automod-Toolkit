They have [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md) as their first effect, making them as effective as a Cure Disease potion as the effect is not magnitude-dependent. They are a lightweight and cheap alternative to the potion, which is probably why [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) members frequently carry them.

## Ingredients
Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 1 in [Alftand Animonculory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alftand_Animonculory) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 1 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))
- 1 either on the ground behind the [Small House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout) or on the roof of the [Main Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall) of a player-built home <sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- 2 on a barrel in the garden of the [Alchemist's Shack](Skyrim_Alchemist%27s_Shack.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 4 in the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) village before the Wayshrine of Radiance, in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md)
- 1 in [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- 2 in [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- 1 in [Benkongerike](Skyrim_Benkongerike.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon and rare [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel). Other possible locations for already harvested samples include:

- 75% chance at 1-3 hawk feathers at 9% each, or 9% chance at 1 hawk feather being carried by any [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) members.

## Creatures
[Hawks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk) can be seen flying in the sky or found near their nests. When shot down, each hawk can be looted for three hawk feathers and one [hawk beak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Beak). There are 67 nests where hawks can spawn, in 17 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 17 around the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 10 centered 300 ft ENE of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-58956&y=106667)
- 4 in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 4 around the [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 centered 280 ft SE of [Autumnwatch Tower](Skyrim_Autumnwatch_Tower.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=87391&y=-104395)
- 3 centered 300 ft NNW of [Froki's Shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Froki%27s_Shack) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=93532&y=-95657)
- 3 around [Hela's Folly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hela%27s_Folly) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md) effect for this ingredient is 2 instead of 5. The only real implication of this reduced magnitude is that if [charred skeever hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide) and hawk feathers are combined, the resulting potion will cost 64% less than a typical custom Cure Disease potion.
- With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, [hawk eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk%27s_Egg) in a hawk's nest can be found next to Hawk Feathers at [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md) and [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md). You can build a tower add-on, jump from a nearby rock formation, or use [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) to get to them. - Version 3.0.11 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch) adds a third hawk's nest at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md), replacing the [Rock Warbler](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) nest there. You can get to it using the 3 methods mentioned above.
