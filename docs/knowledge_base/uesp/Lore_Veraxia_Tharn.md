[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) Veraxia's husband Durcorach Veraxia Tharn was a member of the prominent Nibenese [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHot LE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTFC4-2)</sup>

During her lifetime the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) led by [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md) the Black Drake conquered the [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and founded the [Longhouse Dynasty](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) in [2E 533](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_533).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ho M:ASo S-3)</sup>

One year after the conquest, in [2E 534](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_534) she married the Emperor [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHot LE-1)</sup> The new Emperor decided to marry her for political gain, in an attempt to merge Reach sensibilities with Imperial culture in order to reinforce his connection and commitment to the Empire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTFC4-2)</sup>

Her fate following the death of her husband in [2E 541](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_541)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TATOAM-4)</sup> or [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-5)</sup> and the fall of the Longhouse Dynasty in [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTFC4-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EFEV-6)</sup> is unknown.

## Notes
- Although Veraxia married Durcorach, she was not the mother of his son and inheritor [Moricar](Lore_Moricar.md), who was a teenager when his father and Veraxia married.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHot LE-1)</sup>
