[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Nilheim.jpg/200px-SR-place-Nilheim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Nilheim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Nilheim.jpg) Nilheim on the edge of the mountain Ysgramor's Teeth<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOTR49-1)</sup> is a mountain range in southeast [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) between the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md) and the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md). This range marks the border between [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) and [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), and it is because of this range that the Rift can maintain its temperate climate and avoid the cold winds of the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoad Rift-2)</sup> The main road runs along the base of the mountain and branches separately into either a pass that leads into [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md), or a sharp incline past Snapleg Cave and toward [Lake Geir](Lore_Lake_Geir.md). There is a series of uncharted dirt roads on the western alps that come together at the north end of [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRMountains-3)</sup>

## History
The mountains were first discovered by the original [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) of the [Fallowfire](Lore_Fallowfire.md), who also coined the name. They spent quite some time trying to get over it and when they did, they discovered the Rift. When their war against the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elves.md) finished, they built a mead hall and mortuary for the fallen called [Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOTR49-1)</sup> Over time, the Ancient [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) settled throughout the mountain and exploited resources underneath it. High on Ysgramor's Teeth is an Ancient Nordic monastery called [Mistwatch](Lore_Mistwatch.md), the home of the [Ternion Monks](Lore_Ternion_Monks.md), monks who continued to worship the Three Old Gods of the Nord's ancient totemic religion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTernion-5)</sup>

At some point in time, the Ancient Nords also built a monument on Northwind Summit but whatever it was dedicated to is unclear.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRNorthwind-6)</sup> They also built a temple in the mountain and near the waterfall that drains into [Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md). After it was abandoned, it was taken over by the [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRDarkwater-7)</sup> There is an isolated tower called Nilheim found on the western main road, between the cliffs of Ysgramor's Teeth and the northeast shore of Lake Geir.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRNilheim-8)</sup>

Ragnor Stormcrag opened a series of mines throughout southeast Eastmarch and turned one of them at the base of Ysgramor's Teeth into his family crypt. It was a grand project that he could not live to see completed. He was buried in its deepest confines by his son, Agenor, who saw it finished.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSFC-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONStormcrag-10)</sup> Sometime ago, there was prospector named Sord Pot-Helm who singlehandedly dug out a cave bearing his name until his death at the age of 103. He believed that if he dug any deeper, it would reveal a rich [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) lode.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONLoad Sord-11)</sup>

Striped Fawn from Clearspring Tarn, a spring on the mountain, migrated south across the provinces and found a home with the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md). They can be spotted anywhere but retain their original name.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONFawn-12)</sup>

## Notable Locations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg) Fallowstone Hall
Darkwater Pass
- An underground Nordic temple built around the waterfall that now contains a Falmer hive
[Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md)
- The former headquarters of the Companions, built from the remains of the Fallowfire ship
[Mistwatch](Lore_Mistwatch.md)
- An old monastery formerly occupied by the Ternion Monks, but now a military fort
Nilheim
- A tower and outpost that overlooks both the mountain range and Lake Geir.
[Northwind Summit](Lore_Northwind_Summit.md)
- The highest mountain peak in the range, located northwest of Shor's Stone
Stormcrag Crypt
- The family tomb of the Stormcrag Clan, a powerful and influential family throughout Eastmarch
