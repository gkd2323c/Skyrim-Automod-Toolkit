*A good soup is like a lake. The surface may be rife with activity, but the true greatness lies underneath. The surface is just the introduction.*
—Makes-Many-Soups<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup> Makes-Many-Soups was a highly respected cook of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was credited with numerous recipes that were celebrated throughout [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) as late as the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/ON-npc-Makes-Many-Soups_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Makes-Many-Soups_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Makes-Many-Soups_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Makes-Many-Soups_02.jpg) Makes-Many-Soups in Windhelm *My meal with Makes-Many-Soups was surprisingly pleasant. We decided to partake of the fare at the Sober Nord, and the food was delicious. Makes was impressed--and when it comes to food, she's very hard to please.*
—Veesk-Olan<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VO-3)</sup> Little is known of Makes-Many-Soups' past, but by her own account she was interested in culinary pursuits as a child. When she was little, she used to pretend to be a cook and make soup in mud puddles with the wings of [flies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fly). Since then, she has grown considerably as a cook.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) she was a cook in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) and Jorunn's Stand. She attended the [Konunleikar](Lore_Konunleikar.md) celebrations that year, and also served meals to members of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) at the army camp of Journn's Stand.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup> During her stay at the camp, she crossed paths with [Veesk-Olan](Lore_Veesk-Olan.md), another Argonian who would later become a renowned author. She admitted to him that she harbored a secret love for steamy tales of romance and adventure, with a particular fondness for *[The Lusty Argonian Maid](Lore_The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid.md)*. Their brief romantic involvement inspired Veesk-Olan to create a heroine named Makes-Much-Love, based on her, in his novel *[The Green Hood and the Dres Slavers](Lore_Mentioned_Texts.md#The_Green_Hood_and_the_Dres_Slavers)*.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VO-3)</sup>

Chef [Donolon](Lore_Donolon.md)'s Jubilee Cakes served as inspiration for several of her dishes, including the popular Jubilee Soup. The celebrations surrounding the Jubilee also prompted her to develop six new recipes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup>

## Legacy
*Makes-Many-Soups has many recipes credited to their person: Jubilee soups, soups made with mead, and soups that are celebrated all over Nirn.*
—Urzhag gro-Larak<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>
### Second Era
Makes-Many-Soups served as the inspiration for and was immortalized as the heroic character Makes-Much-Love in Veesk-Olan's popular adventure novel *The Green Hood and the Dres Slavers*.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VO-3)</sup>

### Fourth Era
The legacy of Makes-Many-Soups lasts much longer than the Interregnum of the Second Era. She is also celebrated among other renowned chefs in *Tastes and Tales from Tamriel* by [Urzhag-gro Larak](Lore_Urzhag_gro-Larak.md), apprentice to the famous Gourmet, [Balagog gro-Nolob](Lore_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md). In his book, Urzhag refers to her as a well-respected chef of the Second Era, whose numerous recipes continue to be celebrated across Nirn. One such recipe, the Argonian Pumpkin Stew, is credited as one of her original creations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>

## Recipes
*My soups are works of art. They aren't meant for the hungry or the curious. Such masterpieces are destined for true connoisseurs.*
—Makes-Many-Soups<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup>
### Argonian Pumpkin Stew
Although Makes-Many-Soups claimed not to make stews and was adamant about distinguishing between stews and soups,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup> one of her most famous recipes was, in fact, a stew. It was described as rich and flavorful dish.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup> The Argonian Pumpkin Stew featured ingredients such as pumpkin, fish, and saltrice.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-APS-4)</sup> It was among the favorites of Urzhag gro-Larak and recorded in his book.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>

### Jubilee Soup
Little is known about Jubilee Soup, except that it was inspired by Donolon's Jubilee Cake,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup> and remained recognized as late as the Fourth Era.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>

### Soup With Mead
Among the many dishes created by Makes-Many-Soups was a soup made with mead. She once met a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) who requested such a dish, and though she was initially dismissive, she perfected the recipe, which turned out to be delicious.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MMS-1)</sup> Mentions of this recipe have also survived into the Fourth Era.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-2)</sup>
