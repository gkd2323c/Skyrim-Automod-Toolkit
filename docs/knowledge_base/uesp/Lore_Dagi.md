# Dagi

*When Jode is waning and Jone is new
Then shall be born the Dagi
Tree-dwellers of Tenmar
Trickery and deception is their forte
Masters of Illusory Rites
To hide what Others must never know.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/5/51/Mwaning_Snew.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Snew.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Snew.jpg) Dagi are born under Masser waning and Secunda new<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Dagi are a less common [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), living in the southern marshes and jungle regions of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), as well as the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> Physically, they have facial features that are comparable to that of [lynxes](Lore_Lynx.md) and are short in stature, making them among the smallest of the furstocks.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSamari-4)</sup> Due to their light weight, they have been reported as being able to dwell in higher branches of trees that cannot hold a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MUT-5)</sup> There are said to be entire communities of Dagi that live on branches.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup> With their inclination to climb trees, they have been compared to [monkeys](Lore_Monkey.md), though this is considered insulting.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSamari-4)</sup>

However, some folklore claims that a mummified Dagi paw can grant wishes at a price, mirroring the stories of Monkey paws among the Bosmer.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MDP-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MMP-7)</sup> Dagi are said to be skilled spellcasters, similar to the [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MUT-5)</sup> They are said to be especially knowledgeable of [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup>
