# Vitrine

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/ON-hat-Radius_Mask.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Radius_Mask.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Radius_Mask.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Radius_Mask.jpg) A mask made of wool and vitrine [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-crown_store-Vitrine_Dwarven_Wolf.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Vitrine_Dwarven_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Vitrine_Dwarven_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Vitrine_Dwarven_Wolf.jpg) A vamidium made of vitrine Vitrine, also known as **Dwemeri Glass**, is a kind of [glass](Lore_Malachite.md) used by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) and the [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostles.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VDW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RM-2)</sup>

The Dwemer mined raw [Malachite](Lore_Malachite.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They used vitrine, along with other materials, such as [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), [adamant](Lore_Adamant.md) and [dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md), for constructing certain [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VDW-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VDS-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VDH-5)</sup>

The Clockwork Apostles of [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) used vitrine as well. It was used together with synthetic wool to create masks used to protect their eyes and faces during their travels through [the Radius](Lore_The_Radius.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RM-2)</sup>
