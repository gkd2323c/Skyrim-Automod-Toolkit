# Pygmy

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SI-item-Pygmy_Skull.jpg/200px-SI-item-Pygmy_Skull.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-item-Pygmy_Skull.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-item-Pygmy_Skull.jpg) A supposed Pygmy skull *Look closely and you'll see a tiny skull. These pygmys used to live in the Shivering Isles until they were all eaten by Grummites, poor dears!*
—Una Armina giving a tour of the Museum of Oddities<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Una-1)</sup> Pygmies are a diminutive humanoid group,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Una-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shivering-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup> some of which supposedly inhabited the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Una-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skull-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shivering-2)</sup> Though the pygmies of the Isles are said to have all been eaten by the [Grummites](Lore_Grummite.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Una-1)</sup> Pygmies are known to the inhabitants of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, and referred to in direct contrast with [giants](Lore_Giant.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup>

The skeletal remains of pygmies can be found scattered throughout the Shivering Isles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shivering-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skull-4)</sup> The skull of a pygmy was one of the objects on display at the Museum of Oddities in [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Una-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skull-4)</sup> The word pygmy is used in Tamriel to describe comparatively small creatures, such as with the [pygmy sunfish](Lore_Sunfish.md) and [weeping pygmy shark](Lore_Shark.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Shark-7)</sup>
