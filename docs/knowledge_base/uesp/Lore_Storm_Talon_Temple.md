[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-load-Storm_Talon_Temple.jpg/200px-ON-load-Storm_Talon_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Storm_Talon_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Storm_Talon_Temple.jpg) Storm Talon Temple Storm Talon Temple is a secret fortress located in the [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Hidden beneath a ruined fort known as [Aphren's Hold](Lore_Aphren%27s_Hold.md), it was established as a sanctuary by the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) in ancient times and also was known to house a vast archive of accumulated knowledge.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRFB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSai-3)</sup> On his search for the fabled [Horn of Ja'darri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Horn_of_Ja%27darri), [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) briefly visited the sanctuary circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODL-2)</sup>
