# Spider

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/LG-cardart-Cavern_Spinner.png/200px-LG-cardart-Cavern_Spinner.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Cavern_Spinner.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Cavern_Spinner.png) A spider Spiders are multi-legged arachnids that come in a myriad of sizes. Giant spiders are dangerous foes, being incredibly aggressive carnivores.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-APG-1)</sup> Most can move very quickly, and deal mortal damage with their legs and fangs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-APG-1)</sup> Through their abdomen, spiders can excrete a sticky, thin substance called spider silk. With it, spiders perfect their dwellings, sealing unwanted exits/entrances and constructing a web, which they can use to quickly travel to any space within their abode. They are known to encase their victims in silken cocoons,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> so that they can feed on them later. Most species are venomous, their venom having a variety of debilitating effects depending on the species or individual. Often it has a paralyzing effect.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-APG-1)</sup>

They tend to inhabit underground caves where the moisture keeps their chitinous skin pliable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-APG-1)</sup> Their broods operate under a matriarchy with a brood mother or spider queen.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMendil-3)</sup> Some species are said to live upwards of 1,000 years.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMendil-3)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-render-Selene_02.jpg/200px-ON-render-Selene_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Selene_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Selene_02.jpg) Selene, High Priestess of Shagrath The [venom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spider%27s_venom) of various spider species can be used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) for potions or poisons. The venom of the floor spiders of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) is known as "spider's milk", and is used as an alchemical solvent.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> [Spider eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spider_Egg) are commonly used as alchemy reagents. Their "[hearts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spider_Heart)" are sometimes harvested in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Various groups and cultures have been known to tame spiders. The [Shadowsilk Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shadowsilk_Tribe) of [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) found in [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) managed to tame giant spiders for use as war beasts.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OJKO-7)</sup>

Spiders are associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Mt CS-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> as well as [Shagrath](Lore_Shagrath.md), the [God](Lore_Gods.md) of Spiders.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Shagrath's High Priestess, [Selene](Lore_Selene.md), is a [Changeling](Lore_Changeling.md) who possesses the ability to transform into a giant spider.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Some hypothesize that Selene could be connected to Mephala.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Mt CS-8)</sup> Some [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), especially Mephala and those in service to her, have spider-like features, such as [Skein Spiders](Lore_Skein_Spider.md), [Spider Daedra](Lore_Spider_Daedra.md), and [Spiderkith](Lore_Spiderkith.md).

The [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) constructed [automatons](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) evocative of spiders called [Dwarven Spiders](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md). Likewise, some [fabricants](Lore_Fabricants.md) of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) are spider-like, such as the [firepot spiders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Firepot_Spider) or dancing spiders.

## Variants

### Albino Spider
Albino Spiders (or Solstheim Spiders)<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-12)</sup> are a medium-sized species of spider found on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). Prolonged exposure to certain minerals (particularly gemstones) causes these spiders to take on new and volatile forms.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-12)</sup><sup>:190</sup> As such, they are fundamental to **[Spider Crafting](Lore_Spider_Crafting.md)**, the art of imbuing albino spider pods with magical properties to create volatile artificial spiders.

### Floor Spider
Floor Spiders are a species of highly deadly spider found in the Goblin Caverns of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md). They hide underground, waiting to strike with their venomous claws and inject deadly "spider's milk" into those who pass by their holes.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-KJ-16)</sup> Their venom is used as an alchemical solvent.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

### [Frostbite Spider](Lore_Frostbite_Spider.md)
[Frostbite Spiders](Lore_Frostbite_Spider.md) are giant spiders native to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and its surrounding islands, such as [Bleakrock Isle](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). As their name implies, they are largely immune to the cold. Frostbite spiders most often make their lairs in caves, covering their nests in large webs, and preying upon creatures who venture into their caverns seeking shelter. When food is especially scarce, they venture out to hunt for prey in the wilderness. They spit poisonous frostbite venom at their prey from a distance, and leap at them to attack with pincers if they get too close. These spiders usually take on a brown and grey coloration, although they can also be blue.

Frostbite spiders have some level of intelligence, as such, they are capable of being taught to obey certain orders. The cave-dwelling [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) are known to tame them in addition to [Chaurus](Lore_Chaurus.md). Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) member of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) named Hlokela had taken to breeding and training frostbite spiders as hunting companions. However whenever she tried to leave [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), someone would kill the spiders accompanying her, mistaking them for pests. Eventually she moved out of the city and began giving out her trained spiders to adventurers as pets. Gabriella, a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md), kept a frostbite spider as a [familiar](Lore_Familiar.md).

- **Found in:** [Skyrim](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Frostbite_Spider), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Frostbite Spider)

Farmers in southern Cyrodiil carry an antidote for their venom since it is not uncommon to accidentally rake through one of their nests and be bitten by thousands of their tiny hatchlings. Despite how dangerous they can be, they can make good pets if raised correctly.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

### [Web Mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Web_Mother)
[Web Mothers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Web_Mother) are a monstrous type of queen spider that can be found rarely in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are distinguished by their red and black coloration and the numerous spider eggs attached to their abdomen. These queen spiders are usually found in the depths of the nest, where they can reproduce and protect their eggs.

Web Mothers have been known to defend themselves by launching their own young at foes. These resemble a variety of [exploding poison spider](Lore_Spider_Crafting.md#Poison_Spiders).

A town in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) once kept giant spiders as livestock to harvest their silk. The farmers used specific scents to trick the nest's Web Mother into thinking they were friendly. Circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), this trick stopped working, and the town was soon overrun with spiders. [The Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md) arrived and killed the spiders, destroying the eggs that were laid in the town. The Hero then slew the Web Mother, who had grown too large to leave the nest.

- **Found in:** [Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Web_Mother)<sup>([CC](Skyrim_The_Contest.md))</sup>, [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Web_Mothers)
