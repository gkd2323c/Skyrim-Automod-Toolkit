## Ingredients
50 guaranteed samples found in 38 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in the [Palace of the Kings Upstairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 3 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in the [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in the [Thalmor Embassy, Elenwen's Solar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Embassy,_Elenwen%27s_Solar) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 2 in [Vittoria Vici's House](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Glow dust can be looted from the remaining ash piles of dead [wispmothers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother), including the named wispmother [the Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady_(creature).md). The wisp cores left behind after killing the spawned [wisps](Skyrim_Wisp.md) also contain one to three glow dust each.

## Notes
- Glow dust is frequently confused with [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md), which comes from [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) and has completely different alchemical properties.
