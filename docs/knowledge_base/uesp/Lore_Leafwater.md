[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/ON-map-Leafwater.jpg/200px-ON-map-Leafwater.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Leafwater.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Leafwater.jpg) Map of Leafwater circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/LG-cardart-Leafwater_Blessing.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Leafwater_Blessing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Leafwater_Blessing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Leafwater_Blessing.jpg) Leafwater Blessing **Leafwater** is a riverside region in the southeastern section of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md). It borders the northern region of [Reticulated Spine](Lore_Reticulated_Spine.md) and the western region of [Venomous Fens](Lore_Venomous_Fens.md). The town of [Alten Corimont](Lore_Alten_Corimont.md) is the most populated settlement in the region and was built from the remains of a pirate ship. Many [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) eggs are hatched under the large [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) tree at the [Hatching Pools](Lore_Hatching_Pools.md). There are other Argonian settlements located in Leafwater such as the Percolating Mire and the Forsaken Hamlet.
