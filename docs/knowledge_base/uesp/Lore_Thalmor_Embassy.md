[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/LG-cardart-Thalmor_Embassy.png/200px-LG-cardart-Thalmor_Embassy.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Thalmor_Embassy.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Thalmor_Embassy.png) The Thalmor Embassy as seen in *Legends* The Thalmor Embassy is the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)'s diplomatic mission to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), located north of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), which serves as the center of [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) activity in Skyrim.

The embassy is a gated complex. [Elenwen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elenwen) is the resident ambassador, known for hosting the province's most powerful [men](Lore_Men.md) and [mer](Lore_Mer.md) at lavish ambassadorial parties.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-1)</sup>

## History
In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Elenwen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elenwen) hosted a lavish party. During the party, she met a [guest](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who unbeknownst to her was the Last Dragonborn. Within the embassy already was the informat [Malborn](Lore_Malborn.md), who was working as a server. The Dragonborn infiltrated the Embassy on the behest of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), to search for any information regarding the [return of the dragons](Lore_Return_of_the_Dragons.md). There they discovered information on [Esbern](Lore_Esbern.md), a surviving Blades agent. <sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-2)</sup>
