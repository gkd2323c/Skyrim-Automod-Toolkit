## History
The area known as Normar Heights was occupied by a tribe of [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), sometime before the founding of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). The Orcish champion, [Emmeg Gro-Kayra](Lore_Emmeg_Gro-Kayra.md), was born in the Normar Heights as a bastard son. He was raised by the local shaman, the Grilikamaug. Kayra had garnered a reputation throughout High Rock, performing noble acts and enticing fear onto the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md). On one fateful day, Emmeg came across the blade known as the [Neb-Crescen](Lore_Neb-Crescen.md), and in his blind rage, killed an Orcish woman. This resulted in [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)'s intervention, which ended in Emmeg's death. The Orcish warrior was then sentenced to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Normar Heights was an active settlement, being ruled by Lady Lysyssa. It had a rivalry with Thorkan Park.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Old Gate](Lore_Old_Gate.md) to the southwest, and [North Point](Lore_Northpoint.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup>
