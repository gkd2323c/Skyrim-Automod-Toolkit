[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) Blue Palace Temylda was a High King of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) during the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Temylda was a senior advisor to [Irthvyd the Impassive](Lore_Irthvyd.md), the previous High King of Solitude. Irthvyd refused both a spouse and the possibility of heirs, so Temylda eventually became the High King after defeating several jarls in single combat. Little is known of Temylda's reign. Temylda's reign ended forty years before a new High King named [Alldimar the Ghostmaker](Lore_Alldimar.md) took the throne.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>

Tales of Temylda's reign were compiled by [Erisa Sylbenitte](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Erisa_Sylbenitte) of the [Coterie of Organized Scholars](Lore_Coterie_of_Organized_Scholars.md) at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>
