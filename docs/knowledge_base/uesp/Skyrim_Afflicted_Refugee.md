They have unusually red skin and the ability to [vomit poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vile_Vapor) at close range.

Their inventories are fully leveled, but they usually wear [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure)**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor) for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

## Dialogue
When approached they will say:

- *"Are you finished ogling the grotesque? I suppose I should be grateful you didn't simply attack me."*
- **Your skin... Are you ill?**
- *"I am one of the Afflicted. I'd have been dead from this plague a year ago if not for [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md)'s protection."*
- **Where are you going?**
- *"Returning to [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Our shepherd lost his way, and I fear Peryite's wrath may consume those who remain with him. Kesh could tell you more. I just want out of Skyrim as quickly as possible."*

This will start a miscellaneous objective: Find Kesh at the Peryite Shrine.

If spoken to again:

- **I have more questions.**
- *"And I have no more time for talking. Go speak with Kesh if you're so curious."*
