Shrine to Derik Hallin *A plaque dedicated to Derik Hallin, last of the Ansei*
---
In memory of the hero [Derik Hallin](Lore_Derik_Hallin.md). Long after the time of [Frandar](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) and [Divad](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md), Derik quested for and recovered the five mighty swords.

He and the [sword-saints](Lore_Sword-singer.md) wielded the swords in battle to forever end the [beastmen](Lore_Goblin.md) threat against [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).
