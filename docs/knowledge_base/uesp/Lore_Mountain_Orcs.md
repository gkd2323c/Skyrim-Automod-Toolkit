# Mountain Orcs

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/ON-place-Malak%27s_Maw.jpg/200px-ON-place-Malak%27s_Maw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Malak%27s_Maw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Malak%27s_Maw.jpg) Malak's Maw, an Orcish stronghold in Morrowind Mountain Orcs were an [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) civilization considered local by the people of the [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They were considered enemies of the native [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOT-1)</sup> A group known as the **Malahk-Orcs** was an isolated Orcish civilization known to inhabit the northern hills of Morrowind. Little is known about them, but they were described as tougher in comparison to the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) of the [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). They were active as of [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and occasionally entered conflicts with the House [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWADE-2)</sup>

According to *[The True Nature of Orcs](Lore_The_True_Nature_of_Orcs.md)*, the early Orcs warred against the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTNOO-3)</sup> During the [War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md), during the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), both the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) and members of the [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md) invited Orc and Nord clans as allies and held northwest Morrowind,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRN-4)</sup> an area where Malahk-Orcs were known to live.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWADE-2)</sup> The second song of King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) talks about his various deeds to the old Nordic gods, such as his victory over the eastern Orcs and [shouting](Lore_Thu%27um.md) their chieftains to [hell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hell).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FSOKW-5)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the King of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), [Kurog gro-Bagrakh](Lore_Kurog_gro-Bagrakh.md) absolved the Oathbound Clan of their crimes and leased them Malak's Maw, a mountain valley in the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) and west of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md) for one year.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-KKP-6)</sup>

Several [Daedric Ruins](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), such as Ashurnibibi or [Zergonipal](Lore_Zergonipal.md) were inhabited by Orcs as of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Ashurnibibi-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Zergonipal-8)</sup> Ashurnibibi also had Orc presence in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ashurnibibi ON-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-FTP-10)</sup>

## Notes
- Although the Orcs of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) are also mountain dwellers, they were officially known as the [Wrothgarian Orcs](Lore_Wrothgarian_Orcs.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Orzorga-11)</sup>
- [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md), also known as the *Mountain Fart*,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-VOF-12)</sup> was associated with [Velothi Mountains.](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Xan-13)</sup> Mauloch troubled the heirs of King Harald for a long time. Fleeing east after his defeat at the Battle of Dragon Wall, ca. 1E 660, his rage was said to fill the sky with his sulfurous hatred, earning that year the sobriquet "Year of Winter in Summer."<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-VOF-12)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Xan-13)</sup>
