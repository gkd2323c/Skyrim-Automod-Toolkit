Resurrection and Revelation **by [Mezamma](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mezamma)** *On the reemergence of the Order of the Black Worm*
---
They thought us dead. But what is death to the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) or to a [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) such as I? It is no ending, no blockade. Merely a temporary state during which one can hoard power. We all know the dead contain energies to be harnessed. The order was no different. We gathered our strength and emerged from the ground invigorated, hungry for revenge.

We have worked in darkness and silence. Gathered our resources and built new tools. Now our time has come to emerge from hiding, deploy the soul reapers, and siphon every [soul](Lore_Soul.md) from across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). To restart our great and terrible work. To prove our loyalty to Lord [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) so that we may gain his favor for our plans.

Mezamma
