[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-npc-Itinerant_Lumberjack.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Itinerant_Lumberjack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Itinerant_Lumberjack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Itinerant_Lumberjack.jpg) Itinerant Lumberjack The **Itinerant Lumberjack** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Warrior2H) found in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). He is a possible victim of the radiant [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md) and only spawns during that quest.

The lumberjack is most often found sharpening a weapon at the [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) across from [Jorgen and Lami's House](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md), on the side of the bridge in Morthal that [guards](Skyrim_Morthal_Guard.md) hardly even pass through. Directly behind him are some bushes perfect for hiding in to ensure there are no witnesses, making for a perfect opportunity to perform a long range sneak attack from his back.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699). He wields an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md) in combat.
