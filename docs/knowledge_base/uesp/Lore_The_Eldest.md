# The Eldest

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-creature-The_Eldest.jpg/200px-ON-creature-The_Eldest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Eldest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Eldest.jpg) The Eldest The Eldest is an ancient [Strangler](Lore_Strangler.md) which has been in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) since creation itself. It was the lifeblood, the very essence of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md) and the state of its being reflected upon the forest around it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Indae Dia-1)</sup>

There are many tales surrounding the Eldest,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Amalien-2)</sup> most of which have never been put to writing. Some speak of a bloodthirsty army which vowed to never make war again upon visiting the Eldest. Others speak of mischievous pranks played upon pilgrims. There's also the tale of the first pilgrims, a couple who watched the Eldest as if it was their own child. These pilgrims eventually became the first [Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md) and [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md). During springtime, Bosmer devotees travel to the Eldest's den, offering thanks to [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) but to also read the stories of their home in the strangler's branches. This is followed by a mighty celebration where drinks are had and the aforementioned tales are shared.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TEAPT-3)</sup> Another tale tells of a mother and her young daughter who seek refuge in the Den of the Eldest. In keeping with an ancient promise, the mother offered her firstborn to the Eldest in exchange for wisdom. The story goes that the daughter remains there for many years, until she grows into adulthood. Before leaving, she buried her childhood shoes in the soil, hoping they will help the Eldest grow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Amalien-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md), the Eldest was tainted by the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md) in an attempt to corrupt Valenwood, using the strangler's innate connection to [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md) to spread taint throughout the forest. Flora turned to a state of undeath, water became tainted and even Bosmer turned feral. To save the wilds, [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was forced to put down the ancient being and carve out its heart, being presented then with two choices- to use the Eldest's heart to cleanse the forest, or to find another way and use it to grow a replacement Eldest. It's unknown which path the hero took, but whichever one was chosen resulted in most of the taint being cleansed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Indae Dia-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Bo TB-4)</sup>
