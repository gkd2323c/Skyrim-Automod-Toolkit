**Dwarven Spheres** (also known as **Centurion Spheres**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWSphere-1)</sup> **Sphere Bots**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> **Dwarven Centurion Robots**, and simply **Dwarven Centurions**)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGSphere-3)</sup> are a common form of [Dwemer animunculus](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) that have proven to be agile fighters, and are much stronger than the smaller [Dwarven Spider](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md). Dwarven Spheres patrol ruins in a spherical form until they encounter an opponent. At this point, they will emerge from their "shells" to attack.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRSphere-4)</sup>

Dwarven Spheres can be armed with different tools. Some are armed with an extendable sword and a shield, while others, such as the Centurion Archers of [Bamz-Amschend](Lore_Bamz-Amschend.md), are equipped with crossbows.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRSphere-5)</sup> A specialized version of the sphere known as the Dwarven Arquebus has a lightning cannon in place of blades.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSphere-6)</sup>
