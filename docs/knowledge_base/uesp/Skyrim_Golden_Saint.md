# Golden Saint


They are added to [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) by the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), and they only appear as summoned Daedra.

## Golden Saint Types
There are two types of Golden Saints, based on their preferred fighting style (melee fighters and archers).

### Melee Fighters
These Golden Saints belong to the [Combat Warrior1H](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) NPC [class](Skyrim_Classes.md) and use one-handed weapons in battle. They wear a unique version of [Golden Saint Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Saint_Armor) available only to them and wield a [Golden Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Mace), [Golden Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Sword), or [Golden War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_War_Axe).

### Archers
These Golden Saints have the [Ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) NPC class and wield bows to fight from a distance. They wear the same unique Golden Saint armor as their melee counterparts and use a [Golden Bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Bow).

They also carry a [Golden Dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Dagger), and a quiver of 40 [Golden Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Arrow).
