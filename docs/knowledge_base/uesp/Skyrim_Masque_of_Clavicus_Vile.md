[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-item-Masque_Of_Clavicus_Vile.jpg/200px-SR-item-Masque_Of_Clavicus_Vile.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Masque_Of_Clavicus_Vile.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Masque_Of_Clavicus_Vile.jpg) Masque of Clavicus Vile The Masque of Clavicus Vile is a Daedric artifact created by [Clavicus Vile](Skyrim_Clavicus_Vile.md) that will gain you the respect and admiration of everyone who meets you. It is given as a reward for returning [the Rueful Axe](Skyrim_The_Rueful_Axe.md) to Clavicus Vile during the quest [A Daedra's Best Friend](Skyrim_A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend.md).

## Notes
- The Rueful Axe does not count as a Daedric artifact, while the Masque of Clavicus Vile does. If you wish to obtain the [Oblivion Walker](Skyrim_Oblivion_Walker.md) [Achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) you should exchange the Axe for the Masque of Clavicus Vile during the quest A Daedra's Best Friend.
- The Masque provides the biggest benefit at vendors of any headgear in the game, with the exception of the [Blackguard's Hood](Skyrim_Blackguard%27s_Hood.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>. This is because the 10-point [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) boost given by [Fortify Persuasion](Skyrim_Fortify_Persuasion.md) provides its own discount, which the [Fortify Barter](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md) effect compounds. - Since Fortify Persuasion cannot bring Speech above 100, the combined discount from the Masque's enchantments never quite matches the flat 25% Barter effect of the Hood; at best, it attains a discount of 24.88%, when the player's Speech level is 90. For reference, the Masque provides a discount of 23.28% when the player's Speech level is 10, or a discount of 23.92% when their Speech level is 50.

## Bugs
- [![On Xbox](https://images.uesp.net/3/33/Xbox.svg)](Skyrim_Xbox.md) Equipping the [Guild Master's Hood](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Hood.md) will let you equip the Masque of Clavicus Vile, allowing you to benefit from both Fortify Barter enchantments at once. <sup>**?**</sup>
- [†](#note_Tempering) This item cannot be tempered. In addition, it has the ebony material keyword, meaning you'll get a bonus with the [Matching Set perk](Skyrim_Matching_Set.md) for wearing it with other ebony pieces, but not daedric. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0.0, addresses this issue. It enables tempering using an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk. The Masque's material is set to be Daedric instead.
- Equipping this helmet along with the [Ebony Mail](Skyrim_Ebony_Mail.md) can cause your head to disappear. <sup>**?**</sup>
