# Black Horse Courier

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/OB-book-The_Black_Horse_Courier_header.png/450px-OB-book-The_Black_Horse_Courier_header.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-book-The_Black_Horse_Courier_header.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-book-The_Black_Horse_Courier_header.png) Black Horse Courier *We organize, we deliver. The news never stops!*
—Urjabhi, a proprietor of the Black Horse Courier<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Urjabhi-1)</sup> The Black Horse Courier was the Imperial news service, chartered by the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) and operating out of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Cyrodiil Province](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ra.27jiradh-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hassiri-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BHCOB-4)</sup> Since the organization was funded by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), their broadsheets were always free.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Urjabhi-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hassiri-3)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the Black Horse Courier acted as a courier service.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> During this time their [horses](Lore_Horse.md) wore black leather bridles with the insignia of the organization etched into them.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Bridle-6)</sup>

### Third Era
During the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Courier was run by three [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) brothers; Ra'jiradh, Urjabhi, and Hassiri,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BHCOB-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ra.27jiradh-2)</sup> though the contents of their broadsheets were written by others.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Urjabhi-1)</sup> The sheets were also distributed throughout Cyrodiil by couriers riding black horses.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Courier-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Originally in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, the Black Horse Courier was going to be called the "Imperial Express" or "Eight Cities Couriers", and would have had you make various deliveries to clients around Cyrodiil, but these quests were cut.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CS-8)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Additionally, the faction would have appeared as a part of [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)'s cut [Horse Racing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Horse_Racing) minigame, where they would have been actual couriers.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
- *The Tamriel Crier* was another newspaper of note around the same time in the late Third Era.<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-RFM-11)</sup>
- Couriers of the Black Horse Courier are noted as always being female [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) in *Oblivion*.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TESIVOOGG-12)</sup><sup>:304</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Courier-7)</sup>
