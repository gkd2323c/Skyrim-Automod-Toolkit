# Octavien

| Saint Octavien | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) | Gender | Male |

**Saint** Octavien was a priest of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md). Little is known about his life, but more is known about his death and legacy. He was killed by [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worshippers with a scythe.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-T-1)</sup> His death is remembered by the Bretons, and the scythe became a holy relic of Arkay. The damaged scythe was preserved in the Lorkrata Hills, and a piece of it was retrieved by the [knights of Arkay](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) to combat the [undead](Lore_Undead.md) armies that assaulted [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-A-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Cesabelle-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup>

Saint Octavien was depicted on bronze talismans.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-T-1)</sup>
