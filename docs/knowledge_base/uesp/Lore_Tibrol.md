# Tibrol

Tibrol is a kind of tree known to grow in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It's known for its longevity and for serving as nesting spots for [birds](Lore_Bird.md). These trees are referenced in *[the 36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*, specifically in *[Sermon 1](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_1.md)*, where [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) encounters [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md). In *Sermon 1*, it is suggested that the number of Fa-Nuit-Hen's blade corresponds to the number of birds that can inhabit an ancient tibrol tree, minus three grams of honest labor. However, in his later years, Vivec discovers a superior method and shares this knowledge with his people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VS-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Some unofficial sources speak of resources associated with tibrol trees, such as tibrol-oil,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Oil-2)</sup> tibrol's tree fruits,<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Fruit-3)</sup> and tibrol-based wasabi.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-Wasabi-4)</sup>
- Tibrol is also mentioned in other unofficial texts such as *[Loveletter From the Fifth Era](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Loveletter_From_the_Fifth_Era)*, and *[Introduction to Pocket Guide to the Empire, Second Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_Second_Edition/Introduction).*
