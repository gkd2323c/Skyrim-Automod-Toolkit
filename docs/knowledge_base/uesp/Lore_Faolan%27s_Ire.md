## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-place-Faolan%27s_Ire.jpg/200px-SR-place-Faolan%27s_Ire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Faolan%27s_Ire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Faolan%27s_Ire.jpg) Faolan's Ire ca. [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The area was named after ancient [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) king [Faolan](Lore_Faolan.md) and was once a site of the battlefield between two warring clans: [Eagleseer Clan](Lore_Eagleseer_Clan.md) and the [Six-Ford Clan](Lore_Six-Ford_Clan.md). Since times long forgotten, those two clans fought against each other.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBOKL-2)</sup> The battlefield was marked with a cairn to commemorate the grim event.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONEslyn-3)</sup> During the battle, one of the clans lost a piece of an ancient relic known as the Lover's Coil, a totem that is tied to the history of the Six-Ford and Eagleseer clans, as well as the source of their ancient rivalry. The second part was lost during the more known event, the Battle of [Karthspire Lea](Lore_Karthspire_Lea.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBarth-4)</sup>

Both parts were recovered in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by an [interloper](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in an affair that involved both the Eagleseers and Six-Fords. They found the part of the relic in the Faolan's Ire,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLC1-5)</sup> which became the site of a [giant](Lore_Giant.md)'s camp.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONEslyn-3)</sup> The ancient relic was reassembled not long after both pieces were found.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONOaths-1)</sup>

As of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), during the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) occupation of the Reach, the location is desolated and no longer inhabited by Giants.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Skyrim-6)</sup>
