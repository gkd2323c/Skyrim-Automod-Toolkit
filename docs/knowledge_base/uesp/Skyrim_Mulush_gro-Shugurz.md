[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-npc-Mulush_gro-Shugurz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mulush_gro-Shugurz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mulush_gro-Shugurz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mulush_gro-Shugurz.jpg) Mulush gro-Shugurz **Mulush gro-Shugurz**, an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is the smelter overseer in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He lives in the [Smelter Overseer's House](Skyrim_Smelter_Overseer%27s_House.md). He's unpopular with the workers for being too strict. [Urzoga gra-Shugurz](Skyrim_Urzoga_gra-Shugurz.md) is his sister.

He gets up at 6am and has a one-hour breakfast. He then heads outside to the smelter to oversee and bark at the workers for the next 12 hours, taking one-hour breaks at noon and 5pm to return home and eat meals. At 7pm, he heads home for the day and relaxes until he goes to bed at midnight.

He wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He wields an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace) and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Overseer%27s_House) to his house and some [common gold and loot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Unused Dialogue
Mulush has unused dialogue tied to a [quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) he was once meant to partake in. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

### Freeform Markarth M1
This quest, despite its name, is actually related to The Forsworn Conspiracy. Originally, Eltrys was meant to give you the names of three people he suspected of having Forsworn ties, and Omluag would be among them:

*"Omluag, [Uaile](Skyrim_Uaile.md), and [Ildene](Skyrim_Nana_Ildene.md) are all involved. They must be waiting for the Forsworn to come so they help somehow. Figure out what they're up to. I'll pay you for any information you can find."* All three of these people could then be investigated. If you went to investigate Omluag, you would come across a note in his room in the Warrens, which contained a plan to blow up the smelter at which he works. One of your options would've been to bring the note to Mulush:

**You might be interested in this note I found in Omluag's room.** *"What's this? Why that rotten, lazy Reachman. I'll have his legs for this."* It's unknown what the consequences of doing so would've been.

## Notes
- Mulush belongs to the Town Markarth Faction in the game files, and will attack the Forsworn prisoners if you side with them in [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md). Unfortunately, since Mulush doesn't wear any armor, there is a very good chance he will perish.
