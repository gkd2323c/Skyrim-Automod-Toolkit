It is home to a small band of [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) who have established a camp around the wreck.

## Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos
On the western side of the wreck is a small camp, with two [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) standing guard. There is a central campfire, with a [cooking spit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) hanging over it. To the west of the campfire are two lean-tos, with an unowned bedroll under each of them. To the right is a novice-locked chest, and to the left a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md). On the other side of the campfire is a rack holding five [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). On the eastern side of the wreck is a small island, with a second campfire with some crates and barrels nearby.

The ship itself is split in two, with a flight of stairs on the eastern side of the large piece leading up to the cabin, where a bandit chief lives. There are several barrels and crates around the room, along with an unlocked chest with a [bear trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap) set directly in front of it and an unowned bedroll on the floor to the right. Against the wall is a table, with a random [magicka potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka) and several [empty wine bottles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle) around it. There are stairs leading down to the partially flooded mid-deck, with several crates and a [Nordic barnacle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle) cluster on the floor. The lowest deck can only be reached from outside where the ship's hull has separated. Here you can find a second Nordic barnacle cluster, and at the waterline you can find a waterlogged chest containing the quest item *[Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lymdrenn_Tenvanni%27s_Journal)*. There is nothing of interest on the southern part of the ship. A search of both sides of the island that the camps are on will locate some [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod), two [iron ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein), one [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein), and one [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein).

## Notes
- During a random conversation with a [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) guard, the wreck is referred to as "The Eye of Talos". The map marker is still added correctly, however.
- The ship is named after [Tel Vos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tel_Vos), a [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) settlement in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).
- There is an island to the southeast of the ship that has two spiky grass, one mountain flower, a mammoth rib cage, and leveled animals. Also on this island is a log, and underneath this log's southern end is a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Chimarvamidium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chimarvamidium)* and a leveled piece of enchanted armor.
