[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Wind_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wind_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wind_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wind_Stone.jpg) The Wind Stone The **Wind Stone** is an [All-Maker Stone](Skyrim_All-Maker_Stones.md) west-northwest of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).

Once [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), this stone gives you the power to cast an [area spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:North_Wind) that deals 20 points of frost damage for 10 seconds, plus stamina damage. Once you use this power, it disappears from the Powers menu, and you must return to the Wind Stone to obtain it again.

## Related Quests
- **[The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md)**: Find a way to free the citizens of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-place-Wind_Stone_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wind_Stone_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wind_Stone_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wind_Stone_02.jpg) Lurker attacking The stone shows the usual signs of construction, with wooden scaffolding on the northwest side, and [pickaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), [shovels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel), hammers, and carts in the vicinity. Several [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plants and [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes grow around the stone. A path leads south-southwest to the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md) and the [Beast Stone](Skyrim_Beast_Stone.md), with a branching path leading east to Skaal Village.

Before you cleanse the stone, [Baldor Iron-Shaper](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md), [Deor Woodcutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter), [Edla](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Edla), [Fanari Strong-Voice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice), [Morwen](Skyrim_Morwen.md), [Tharstan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tharstan), and [Wulf Wild-Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood) will be working on and bowing to the stone. When you cleanse the stone, a leveled [lurker](Skyrim_Lurker.md) will rise from the water around the base of the stone and attack you and the workers. After the lurker is defeated, the workers will express their thanks and return to Skaal Village.
