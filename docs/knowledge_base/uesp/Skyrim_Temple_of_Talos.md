[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-place-Temple_of_Talos.jpg/200px-SR-place-Temple_of_Talos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Temple_of_Talos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Temple_of_Talos.jpg) The Temple of Talos in the city of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) The **Temple of Talos** is located just northwest of [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

After the Empire signed the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md) with the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md), the worship of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) was outlawed. However under the protection of [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), it is now the only formal Temple of Talos in Skyrim.

## Temple of Talos
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-interior-Temple_of_Talos_%28Windhelm%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Temple_of_Talos_%28Windhelm%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_of_Talos_(Windhelm).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_of_Talos_(Windhelm).jpg) Interior It contains a [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Talos.md) in front of the statue of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) in the main room with a row of five wooden benches facing it. It is tended by [Lortheim](Skyrim_Lortheim.md) and [Jora](Skyrim_Jora.md), who also reside in the single sleeping quarters behind the statue. There are arched doorways on either side of the statue that lead to the sleeping quarters. They consist of an owned double bed coming out from the south wall with a set of shelves against the east wall holding various clutter, a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). Opposite, against the west wall are a series of three wall shelves holding copies of *[Aedra and Daedra](Skyrim_Aedra_and_Daedra.md)*, *[The Song of Pelinal, v1](Skyrim_The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v1.md)*, *[v4](Skyrim_The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v4.md)* and *[v5](Skyrim_The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v5.md)*, *[The Amulet of Kings](Skyrim_The_Amulet_of_Kings.md)*, *[The Monomyth](Skyrim_The_Monomyth.md)*, *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)* and two copies of *[A Children's Anuad](Skyrim_A_Children%27s_Anuad.md)*. There is also a chair under the shelves.

Jora will happily reveal that the Shatter-Shields, [Hillevi Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Hillevi_Cruel-Sea.md) and Ulfric Stormcloak frequent the temple.

## Notes
- The temple will remain open for Talos worship, even after the [Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md).
