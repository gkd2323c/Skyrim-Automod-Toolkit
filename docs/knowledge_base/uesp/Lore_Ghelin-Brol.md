[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple_02.jpg) Wind Scour Temple Ghelin-Brol was an [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) warrior who helped [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman_Cyrodiil.md) establish the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) and the first Grandmaster of the order. He was remembered as one of the four greatest Grandmasters the Dragonguard have ever known.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SS-1)</sup> He served as the Grandmaster from [1E 2703](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2703) to [1E 2734](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2734).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GGB-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg/200px-BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg) Sunderblade (Blades) Ghelin-Brol was among the organized armada of [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md), who attacked [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in [1E 2703](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2703). As soon as they heard Reman's voice at [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md), they knelt before him and proclaimed him Dragonborn, claiming he was what they had come to seek.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRAFOTB-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LOTD-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGTE3-5)</sup> In the same year he became the first leader of the newly founded Akaviri Dragonguard,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SS-1)</sup> appointed by the Son of the West, Reman. He was the first of the Grandmasters, laid to rest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GGB-2)</sup> He was succeeded by [Vashu-Pir](Lore_Vashu-Pir.md) in [1E 2734](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2734).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GVP-6)</sup>

After his death, he was buried in [Wind Scour Temple](Lore_Wind_Scour_Temple.md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), and honored with a [statue](Lore_Statues.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-WST-7)</sup> and "brazier bright".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GGB-2)</sup>

In life, he wielded the legendary katana [Sunderblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sunderblade).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Sunderblade-8)</sup> It was known for its potent enchantment that could disintegrate weapons and armors of the foes of the wielder.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBL-9)</sup> Numerous superb katanas were fashioned to look like the legendary Sunderblade and were used for centuries by high-ranking [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) before the order was disbanded.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AB-10)</sup>
