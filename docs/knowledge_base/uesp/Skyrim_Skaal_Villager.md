He carries a leveled one-handed weapon up to elven quality, some [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), and wears [Skaal boots](Skyrim_Skaal_Boots.md), and a [Skaal coat](Skyrim_Skaal_Coat.md) and [hat](Skyrim_Skaal_Hat.md).
