[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg/200px-ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg) Solstice Solstice (or **Oztet-Ta** *"Sprouting Root"* in [Jel](Lore_Jel.md))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAECS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SST-2)</sup> is an isolated tropical island within the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Justice The Fallen-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jubilee2025-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ta S-5)</sup> located south of [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESODirect2025-6)</sup> The island has a sizeable [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) population who largely descended from settlers that arrived with the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ho CC-7)</sup> The [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) that inhabit the island came before the [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md) period occurred.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-8)</sup> It has been described as a "paradise".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jubilee2025-4)</sup>

In the aftermath of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), the [Worm Cult](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Worm_Cult) was reported to be amassing forces on the island of Solstice in preparation for a massive attack. Though its location was unknown to the population of Tamriel at the time, the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) vowed to locate the island and stop them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Justice The Fallen-3)</sup>

## Fauna
Solstice [sea urchins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_Urchin) are sometimes used to make rattlers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PSR-9)</sup>
