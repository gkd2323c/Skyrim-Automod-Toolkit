# Jadeflint

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png/200px-ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png) Argonian armor partially made of Jadeflint Jadeflint is a green-tinted stone found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), commonly used by [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [smiths](Lore_Smithing.md) in crafting light armor. These armors are typically made from natural materials such as jadeflint, along with [obverdian](Lore_Obverdian.md), [bog iron](Lore_Iron.md), and [steel](Lore_Steel.md), often accented with rough [copper](Lore_Copper.md) alloy edges.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup>
