# Bismite

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-lead-Harmonic_Bismite.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-lead-Harmonic_Bismite.png) A piece of harmonic bismite Bismite is an iridescent mineral made from [bismuth](Lore_Bismuth.md). Pure bismuth can be extracted from it. Bismite has harmonic properties, and similarly to bismuth it occurs naturally in large quantities on the isle of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md) south of the mainland [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Its hollow nature gives it [musical](Lore_Music.md) qualities, so the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of the isle used it, or the bismuth obtained from it, to make music boxes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Harm Bismite-1)</sup>
