# Sun Birds of Alinor

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/ON-furnishing-Prismatic_Sunbird_Feather_02.png/200px-ON-furnishing-Prismatic_Sunbird_Feather_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Prismatic_Sunbird_Feather_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Prismatic_Sunbird_Feather_02.png) A Prismatic Sunbird Feather The Sun Birds of Alinor were a mythical order of [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorers from the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). According to legend, they managed to pierce the veil between [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) and [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) using raw magic of the [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Furn Sunbird-1)</sup> Their expeditions to Aetherius are said to be among the most famous attempts in history, alongside similar endeavors undertaken by the [Royal Imperial Mananauts](Lore_Royal_Imperial_Mananauts.md) of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) during the time of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3AS-2)</sup> but some scholars believe that actual proof of a successful journey is nonexistent.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Furn Sunbird-1)</sup>

The order was eventually dissolved due to the vast expenditures of magicka required to travel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3AS-2)</sup> Few remaining legacies exist of these fabled travelers. Possibly the most notable example is the [Great Orrery](Lore_Great_Orrery.md) at [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), whose spheres are made up of genuine celestial mineral gathered by travelers during the Merethic Era.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3AS-2)</sup>

Lastly, during the mid-Second Era, a prismatic Sun Bird feather was unearthed by the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md)'s Antiquarian Circle. While its exact function remains unknown, scholars speculated it may have been an orienteering tool of some sort.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Furn Sunbird-1)</sup>

Their [vessels](Lore_Air_Transportation.md#Sunbirds) have been described as "[birds](Lore_Bird.md) made out of the [sun](Lore_Sun.md)".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-3)</sup>
