The Isle of Craghold is an island within the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It was inhabited during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NWOB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jokes-2)</sup>

At some point during the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md) between years [3E 402](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_402) and [3E 403](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_403),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFC-3)</sup> Craghold was besieged by the forces of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) for several days. The siege was broken by the forces of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) forces under Lord [Bridwell](Lore_Bridwell.md), who liberated the island. Siege of Craghold and Battle of the Bluffs were both victories of the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NWOB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jokes-2)</sup>
