# Saltrice

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/ON-flora-Saltrice.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Saltrice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Saltrice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Saltrice.jpg) Saltrice (Online) Saltrice is an abundant, nutritious [plant](Lore_Flora.md) and crop that grows in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWSaltrice-1)</sup> and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Po T-2)</sup> The commonality of Saltrice plays importance to the culture of Morrowind, and even the history of the [Great House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md), who live in the southeast region around their capital city, [Tear](Lore_Tear.md). They built their expansive fortune from years of harvesting this plant and the slave industry that they currently spearhead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-UHD-3)</sup> Fermented saltrice is brewed into the popular drink, [Mazte](Lore_Mazte.md), which has a negative effect on one's wit and judgment.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWMazte-4)</sup>

There is a market for Saltrice across the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md), exported by the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BMCompany-5)</sup> Normally, it comes from the southeastern region, but by the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), there have been several more plantations growing from the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAM-6)</sup> Saltrice also grows in abundance in the fertile valleys around [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EGTMW-7)</sup> The plant shares its name with the Dunmeri martial art, Salt Rice, which is practiced by the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWDeesei-8)</sup> Saltrice is commonly used as [kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) feed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
