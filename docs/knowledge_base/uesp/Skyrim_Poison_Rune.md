**Poison Rune** is an adept level Restoration spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, causing them to take 3 damage per second for 30 seconds. When you press the cast button, the cross-hair will let you know if you are pointing at a valid surface within range. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- [Poison Rune](Skyrim_Poison_Damage.md), 3 pts for 30 seconds (total of 90 damage)

## Perks
- [Rune Master](Skyrim_Rune_Master.md) increases range to 100 feet.
- [Restoration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Restoration_Dual_Casting.md) increases damage to 220% per rune. Duration remains the same.

## Notes
- You can set off poison runes (your own or others) using any Restoration projectile spell from a distance.
- Some enemies are immune to this spell, notably all [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md), [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md), [automata](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md), and summoned or reanimated enemies of any kind. Note that though they may not be poisoned, they *will* still set off the rune if they touch it.
- The poison effects stack if the target is hit by a second poison rune before the effects of the first rune wears off.
- [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md#Alchemy) potions increase the damage of the spell. Duration unchanged.
- [Weakness to Poison](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Poison.md) and [Resist Poison](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md) effects on enemies does not affect the spell in any way. Some enemies are hard coded to be immune to the spell using an Is Type command.
- [Weakness to Magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md) and [Resist Magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) effects on enemies alters the damage of the spell. Duration unchanged.
