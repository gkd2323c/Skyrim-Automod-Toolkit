[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-place-Whiterun_Imperial_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_Imperial_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Imperial_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Imperial_Camp.jpg) Whiterun Imperial Camp **Whiterun Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) [camp](Skyrim_Imperial_Camps.md) in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md), located east of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) and far west of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-npc-Legate_Quentin_Cipius_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Legate_Quentin_Cipius_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Quentin_Cipius_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Quentin_Cipius_04.jpg) A bugged Legate Cipius, alone in the wilderness where the camp should be The commanding officer of this camp is [Legate Quentin Cipius](Skyrim_Legate_Quentin_Cipius.md). Unlike other military camps, you will never be welcome here, as it will only exist if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) have taken [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) in battle, by which time the player has irreversibly sided with the Stormcloaks. The soldiers will draw their [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and tell you to leave when you approach the camp. If you linger for too long, they will attack you. Like similar Imperial camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has neither a [quartermaster](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md) or any [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md).

## Whiterun Imperial Camp
There are six soldiers in addition to the Legate and wounded. They are all armed with [imperial bows](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) and [swords](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md), and all wear complete sets of [imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md). The camp is situated around a central campfire on the banks of a shallow pond. There is a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) over the campfire, with an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) beside the fire. To the north is the command tent, containing an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of his time. There is a row of three small tents to the right of the command tent, each containing a bedroll. South of these tents is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the right of the tent is a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), and farther to the right is a hospital tent, containing three bedrolls occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding two random potions of healing. Outside to the right is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), and behind the table are some barrels.

## Notes
- There is a [shrine of Zenithar](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Zenithar.md) east of the camp.
- Two ore veins are near the camp: a [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md) vein east-northeast of the camp, and an [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) vein south-southwest of the camp.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map only displays the camp's location; the previous one displayed the camp as well.

## Bugs
- At the very beginning of the game, Quentin Cipius spawns where the camp is supposed to be, standing in the wilderness. If you join the Stormcloaks and take over Whiterun, the camp will be set up, and Quentin will find a purpose in the wilderness. However, if you side with the Imperials and defend Whiterun, Quentin will show up in Dragonsreach, and the camp will never be built. Oddly, a duplicate of Quentin will still exist in the wilderness, even if Quentin is in Dragonsreach. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
