[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-creature-Ulfnir_Bone-Skin.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ulfnir_Bone-Skin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ulfnir_Bone-Skin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ulfnir_Bone-Skin.jpg) Ulfnir Bone-Skin **Ulfnir Bone-Skin** is an undead [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) warrior found at [Yorgrim's Overlook](Skyrim_Yorgrim%27s_Overlook.md).

According to *[The Crimson Dirks, v4](Skyrim_The_Crimson_Dirks,_v4.md)*, Ulfnir slew the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Vithrelnaak](Lore_Vithrelnaak.md) and fashioned a set of [studded Dragonscale armor](Skyrim_Studded_Dragonscale_Armor.md) from its remains. Rather than pass it on to his pupils, Ulfnir ordered that he be buried with the armor. However, his corpse now haunts the barrow, preventing any from recovering it.

You will be tasked with defeating Ulfnir and looting the [Overlook Key](Skyrim_Overlook_Key.md) from his remains as part of the related quest. This can be used to open the chest within the barrow containing his armor. He can also be looted for a sample of [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) and some [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). In combat, he wields an [ancient Nord greatsword](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Greatsword.md). He also wears some ancient Nord armor that cannot be looted from his remains.
