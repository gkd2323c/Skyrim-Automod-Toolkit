# Plessington Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/e/e0/DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_%28face%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_(face).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_Plessington_(face).png) Lord Plessington (Daggerfall) The Plessington Clan was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble family hailing from [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md). They were known to led a [knightly order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) called the [Order of the Cup](Lore_Order_of_the_Cup.md), which also served as the protectors of the family. In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Lord Plessington led the order and was very eager to help in all the affairs of High Rock. He was known to send them off to right a wrong and come back with a story or two, with no other, greater purpose, beyond the charity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-D1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-D2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DC-3)</sup><sup>:101</sup>

The events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) led to the incorporation of the fiefdom of Ykalon into the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). It also caused most of the forests in Ykalon to burn down.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WITW-4)</sup> It is unknown how this affected the members of the Plessington Clan, however.
