# Word Wall


On each word wall one of these words may be highlighted. A highlighted word is a [word of power](Skyrim_Word_of_Power.md), that allows you to unlock one word in one of the [Dragon Shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md). Once you have found a word of power you must absorb a [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) soul to use the associated Dragon Shout.

## Locations
Most of the word walls are located in [Dragon Lairs](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) and [Nordic Ruins](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md). There are two quests that help you discover new word walls:

- **[The Words of Power](Skyrim_The_Words_of_Power.md)**: Follow the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md)' advice to learn a [Word of Power](Skyrim_Word_of_Power.md) from a word wall.
- **[Shout - learn about word wall](Skyrim_Shout_-_learn_about_word_wall.md)**: Explore a location and learn a [Word of Power](Skyrim_Word_of_Power.md).

You can only obtain one location at a time from each method, but you can use both methods at once. Neither source will ever direct you to a location that is potentially quest-locked (even if the quest is active), or to locations that are missing from their list ([Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md) and [Shalidor's Maze](Skyrim_Shalidor%27s_Maze.md)).

## Word Wall Translations
The text shown on the word wall depends on how many words you've learned for that shout. For example, if you have already learned the first word in the shout [Aura Whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) in [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md), then if you visit either [Northwind Summit](Skyrim_Northwind_Summit.md) or [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md), the word wall will be for the second word. This means that the word wall's text changes, depending on which Word(s) you've already learned; the two exceptions are [Throw Voice](Skyrim_Throw_Voice.md) ([Shearpoint](Skyrim_Shearpoint.md)) and [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) ([Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md)).

## Notes
- The choral part of the "Word Learned" sound effect is considered to be music and will not be heard if your music volume is set to zero.
- There are two ruined word walls that you are unable to learn from; one in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md) and one in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>
- The "word wall" for Drain Vitality located in the Forgotten Vale is not a traditional ancient Nordic structure, but rather a large boulder with a single word carved into it.<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>

## Bugs
- Occasionally, when you approach a word wall from which you had previously learned a word, you will hear the chanting and sizzling sound that you normally hear from a word wall you've never visited.
