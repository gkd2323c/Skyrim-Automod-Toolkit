# Orgak Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-item-Scepter_of_the_King-Chief_%28museum%29.jpg/200px-ON-item-Scepter_of_the_King-Chief_%28museum%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Scepter_of_the_King-Chief_(museum).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Scepter_of_the_King-Chief_(museum).jpg) Scepter of the King-Chief The Orgak Clan were one of the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) clans of the northern [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). They were founded by Kloth, and were known for their Scepter of the Riekr King-Chief.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Scepter-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/ON-npc-Marzul_gro-Orgak.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Marzul_gro-Orgak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Marzul_gro-Orgak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Marzul_gro-Orgak.jpg) Marzul gro-Orgak Orgak Clan was established by Kloth at some point long before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) in northern Wrothgar. Kloth crafted an ancient scepter, later known as the Scepter of the Riekr King-Chief, symbolizing power and authority for the Orc clan. The scepter eventually ended up in the hands of a powerful [Riekr](Lore_Riekr.md) Edu who claimed the title "the King-Chief" and terrorized the land northeast of Orsinium.

During King [Kurog's](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md) reign in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the House of Orsimer Glories was on a quest to acquire culturally significant artifacts for their museum. Among the many relics they sought was Scepter of the Riekr King-Chief.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Scepter-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CLOS-3)</sup> It was eventually recovered by an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) hired for the job, who found it and delivered to the curator. The scepter was safely delivered and displayed in the museum.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HOOG-4)</sup>

As of 2E 582, the status of the Orgak Clan is not well-documented. Although the clan was said to have flourished in the past, there was no reference to their current status. However, Orcs bearing the clan name of gro-Orgak remained active and prominent, with Marzul gro-Orgak serving as the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) hall steward in [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MGO-5)</sup>

## Notes
- An Orc named Orgak served as a Recruitment Officer in the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) military. His connection to the clan is unknown.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ROO-6)</sup>
- Duzag gra-Orgak appears in the [cut content](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Cut_Content#For_King_and_Glory) of ESO.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOCUT-7)</sup>
