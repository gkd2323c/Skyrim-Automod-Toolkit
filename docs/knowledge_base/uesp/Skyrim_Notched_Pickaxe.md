[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-item-Notched_Pickaxe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Notched_Pickaxe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Notched_Pickaxe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Notched_Pickaxe.jpg) Notched Pickaxe [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Throat_of_the_World_03.jpg) The Notched Pickaxe at the Throat of the World The Notched Pickaxe is a special [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) found stuck into the peak of the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md). It has the unique enchantment [Smithing Expertise](Skyrim_Smithing_Expertise.md), which boosts the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill while it is equipped, and does five points of [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) when it is used as a weapon. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Notched Pickaxe requires an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Notes
- Strangely, the editor ID for this item is "dun Volunruud Pickaxe", suggesting it originally had some connection to [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md).
- This pickaxe was named after the creator of [Minecraft](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minecraft), [Markus "Notch" Persson](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Markus_Persson).
- There are two ebony and two malachite ore veins nearby the pickaxe.

## Bugs
- Despite being a unique item, the Notched Pickaxe can be disenchanted. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- The enchantment on the Notched Pickaxe restores damage to your Smithing skill rather than enhancing it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- For unknown reasons, the pickaxe may be missing when you reach the peak of the Throat of the World. - It might have fallen down from its spawning place and down the mountain, in which case it will most likely never be able to be recovered.
