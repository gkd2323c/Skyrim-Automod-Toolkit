# Witch Tree

[![](https://images.uesp.net/3/38/SK-narrative-GP01.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-narrative-GP01.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-narrative-GP01.png) Witch Tree [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SK-quest-Witch_Tree.jpg/200px-SK-quest-Witch_Tree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-quest-Witch_Tree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-quest-Witch_Tree.jpg) The Witch Tree The Witch Tree is a mysterious sentient tree found in Ghast's Pass, a mountain pass in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md) region of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), north of [Azra's Crossing](Lore_Azra%27s_Crossing.md). It can speak cryptically through the rustling of leaves, and can also move its roots to communicate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Witch Tree-1)</sup>

Some time after the school at the [Agea Relle](Lore_Agea_Relle.md) in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) was abandoned to undead and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) in the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-STVI-2)</sup> the seven [Star Teeth](Lore_Star_Teeth.md) became dispersed throughout the region. One of the crystals ended up in Ghast's Pass, where the Witch Tree grew around it.

In [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397), the shadowmage [Skelos Undriel](Lore_Skelos_Undriel.md) sent the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Hero.md) on a quest to recover the Star Teeth, and suggested that the hero speak to the Witch Tree. Sympathizing with the hero's "struggle with [shadow](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md)", the tree tasked the hero with helping nearby people before finally sending the hero to face [Pergan Asuul](Lore_Pergan_Asuul.md) and recover a [Shadowkey](Lore_Shadowkey.md). When the hero subsequently presented the key, the tree's bark cracked due to a fierce inner light before peeling back to reveal the Star Tooth.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> It is unknown what became of the tree after the Star Tooth was removed.

## Notes
- The source of the Witch Tree's sentience is unknown. It could have been animated by the Star Tooth as it becomes silent after it was taken. The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) are also a species of sentient tree, who have also been known to communicate through the wind.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Additionally certain [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md) are sentient trees. [Witches](Lore_Witches.md) of the [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md) are known to congregate around the eponymous [Beldama Wyrd Tree](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd_Tree.md), a gargantuan oak tree that serves as the heart of the coven's mystical crafts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-5)</sup> [Brackenleaf](Lore_Brackenleaf_(tree).md) is another sentient tree, planted by [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md).
- A script of some sort is featured at the bottom of the Witch Tree's trunk.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Witch Tree-1)</sup> On a possibly unrelated note, the trees of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) have [Daedric Script](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) written upon them.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
