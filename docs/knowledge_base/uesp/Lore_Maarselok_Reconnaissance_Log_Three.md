Maarselok Reconnaissance Log Three *A Stag of Zen's log of a dragon hunt in the Tenmar mountains* *
---
We spotted him! The [Dragon](Lore_Maarselok.md) finally left its roost. It looked like he was searching for [something](Lore_Selene.md). Think I held my breath out of fear he caught wind of our attack force, but he eventually landed up near the summit and settled in. Now we know where the big blue bastard lives.

* * *
I never heard of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) making nests, but I think this one might be part [magpie](Lore_Magpie.md). Or [skeever](Lore_Skeever.md). The mountain up by the summit has gone [blue](Lore_Azure_Blight.md) as he is. Glows bright as can be at night. It'll be our guiding [star](Lore_Stars.md) as we advance up the mountain in the middle of the night.
