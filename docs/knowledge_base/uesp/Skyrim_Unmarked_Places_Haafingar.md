### Clam Digger's Camp
[![SR-place-Clam Digger's Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp.jpg) [![SR-place-Clam Digger's Camp 03.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Clam_Digger%27s_Camp_03.jpg) - POINorthern Coast12

Southwest of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md) is an abandoned camp with a tent containing an unowned bedroll and a wooden bowl holding a piece of [clam meat](Skyrim_Clam_Meat.md). There are three food sacks and one barrel either side of the tent and a campfire in front, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging over it. Beside the fire is an open crate containing sixteen pieces of clam meat. On a rock near the camp are nineteen [clams](Skyrim_Clam.md), with a couple more underwater nearby. Around the camp there is a tree stump with [mora tapinella](Skyrim_Mora_Tapinella.md) growing on it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-153096&y=111800&zoom=17&legacy=true)

The quest [Brothers in Irons](Skyrim_Brothers_in_Irons.md) takes place here, where the camp will be occupied by a Thalmor wizard and two Thalmor soldiers, along with the corpse of [Pale-Eyes](Skyrim_Pale-Eyes.md).

### Forsworn Ambush Camp
[![SR-place-Forsworn Ambush Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp.jpg) [![SR-place-Forsworn Ambush Camp 03.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Ambush_Camp_03.jpg) - POIReach10

Located just south of [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md), two Forsworn have set up three bear traps in a gully. There is a dead Nord in the gully.

There is a Forsworn tent at the eastern end of the gully, with it having two hay beds, a novice-locked chest, a coin purse, raw beef, two wooden bowls, and an ale. There are two closed bear traps next to the tent. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-132736&y=85696&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Haafingar Sabre Cat's Lair
[![SR-place-Haafingar Sabre Cat's Lair.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-place-Haafingar_Sabre_Cat%27s_Lair.jpg/200px-SR-place-Haafingar_Sabre_Cat%27s_Lair.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haafingar_Sabre_Cat%27s_Lair.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haafingar_Sabre_Cat%27s_Lair.jpg) - POINorthern Coast08

East-northeast of the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md), two [snowy sabre cats](Skyrim_Snowy_Sabre_Cat.md) have established a lair under an overhanging rock. Two of their victims can be found here: an [elk](Skyrim_Elk.md) and a dead [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian_(NPC).md) with a coin purse lying next to him. Looting the body of the Argonian will net you a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill book *[Guide to Better Thieving](Skyrim_Guide_to_Better_Thieving.md)*.

Climbing the rocks to the right of the lair will lead to another unmarked location: Pincushion Peter. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-70507.2891&y=119407.6250&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Howling Wolf's Folly
[![SR-place-Howling Wolf's Folly.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-place-Howling_Wolf%27s_Folly.jpg/200px-SR-place-Howling_Wolf%27s_Folly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Howling_Wolf%27s_Folly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Howling_Wolf%27s_Folly.jpg) - POINorthern Coast11

Located along the rough snow path that winds from Lost Echo Cave toward Steepfall Burrow is a ruined tower that is now the den of a frost troll or wolf. It has killed two deer and one Stormcloak soldier. On the body of the soldier is the skill book *[Proper Lock Design](Skyrim_Proper_Lock_Design.md)*.

Next to the main road, a deer corpse and a blood trail lead there. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-125701&y=111526&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Pincushion Peter
[![SR-place-Pincushion Peter.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-place-Pincushion_Peter.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pincushion_Peter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pincushion_Peter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pincushion_Peter.jpg) - POINorthern Coast04

Located east of the Thalmor Embassy, above the Haafingar Sabre Cat Lair. At the top of the peak is a skeleton with five different weapons pinning it to the slope. These weapons are a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a leveled [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md), a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), and a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md). Next to the skeletal remains lies a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill book *[The Rear Guard](Skyrim_The_Rear_Guard.md)*. A [Staff of Fireballs](Skyrim_Staff_of_Fireballs.md) and a knapsack can be found leaning up against a tree nearby. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-67322.8&y=117734&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Haafingar Nordic Burial Ruins
