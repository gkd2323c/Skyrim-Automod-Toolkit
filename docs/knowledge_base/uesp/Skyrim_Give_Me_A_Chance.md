Give Me A Chance **by [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md)**
---
[Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md),

Give an old man a chance, why don't you? I know I may have a few years under my belt, but that doesn't mean we can't have some fun. Believe me, I can show you a thing or two. Quit playing hard to get... I saw how you blushed when [Dirge](Skyrim_Dirge.md) started reading from the [Lusty Argonian Maid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid). You're a firebrand, and we all know it. Take me up on my offer and stop playing the tough girl. We all know the toughest ones are the softest on the inside.

[Delvin](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md)
