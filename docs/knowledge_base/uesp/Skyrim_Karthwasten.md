[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-place-Karthwasten.jpg/200px-SR-place-Karthwasten.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karthwasten.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karthwasten.jpg) Karthwasten **Karthwasten** is a mining [town](Skyrim_Towns.md) in the northern [Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md).

[Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md) is the owner of the whole town including the two surrounding mines. The mines at Karthwasten are two of the few mines in the Reach that are not owned by a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md).

When you first enter Karthwasten, you will see the townsfolk squaring off against a group of mercenaries sent by the [Silver-Blood family](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md#Silver-Blood_Family) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to stop them from working [Sanuarach Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sanuarach_Mine) until Ainethach agrees to sell the land to them.

The town consists of three buildings in a semicircle: [Karthwasten Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Hall), where Ainethach lives, [Enmon's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon%27s_House), where [Enmon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon) lives with his wife [Mena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mena) and daughter [Fjotra](Skyrim_Fjotra.md), and the [miner's barracks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks), where [Belchimac](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belchimac), [Lash gra-Dushnikh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh), and [Ragnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnar) live. There is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) in front of Karthwasten Hall and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) on the deck in front of the miner's barracks.

Sanuarach Mine is the main [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) mine. Its only entrance is north of Karthwasten Hall. In front of the entrance is a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md), three pieces of [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) in a cart opposite, and a [Juniper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries) tree on a rock. The entrance to [Fenn's Gulch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fenn%27s_Gulch_Mine) is at the northwestern end of town and has another smelter, two [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), and a piece of silver ore to the left of the entrance, with another Juniper tree to the right of the entrance.

## Notes
- Northeast of Karthwasten, there is a small bloodied altar holding a [black soul gem](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) and a piece of bloodied cheese.
- East-northeast of Karthwasten, on a hill facing down to the river next to a circle of ancient stones, there is a [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein).
- West of Karthwasten, below the hill with the [Shrine to Peryite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_to_Peryite), there is an unmarked stone totem with a dead body and some loot.
- Karthwasten appears in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* as [Karthwasten Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Karthwasten_Hall), and also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Karthwasten) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
