The Watcher's Pledge *The Pale Watch's oath of honor*
---
By the sword of my fathers, I pledge fealty to [the Watch](Lore_Pale_Watch.md) and to the rightful heirs of King [Maxevian](Lore_Maxevian.md). I do hereby bind myself to the laws and regulations of the Watch, and shall faithfully execute any orders given to me by the Watchmaster or her surrogates, without question or hesitation. I shall not suffer an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) to [stack stones](Lore_Orsinium.md), and I will defend the Hold against all enemies, be they [men](Lore_Men.md), [mer](Lore_Mer.md), or [beast](Lore_Beastfolk.md).

I take this vow without reservation, and shall hold to it unto death.
