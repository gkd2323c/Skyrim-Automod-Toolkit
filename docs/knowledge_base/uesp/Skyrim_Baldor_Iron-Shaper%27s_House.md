## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The building has two entrances, both on the north side, with one to the east and one to the west. To the east is a complete blacksmithing area, complete with a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelting.md), a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), with an open box on a crate between the tanning rack and workbench containing two pieces of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) and a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips). Between the two entrances is another tanning rack, a fish barrel, a crate with a [snow fox pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt) on top, and another workbench. To the right of the west entrance is a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush. The east entrance is unlocked, and the west entrance has a novice lock.

### Baldor Iron-Shaper's House
The entrance leads into a large room with a large mezzanine above. On your left is a small round table and two chair. On the table is a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) and two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). Against the south wall is the flight of stairs to the top floor and under the stairs is a barrel and four sacks. In the southwest corner of the room is a small square table and two chairs, a bottle of wine, a bowl of [horker stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Stew) and a plate with a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) are on the table. Above the table is a large hanging rack with three [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids hanging from it. There is a wall shelf by the table on the west wall with a shield plaque holding a [banded iron shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield) and two crossed [iron greatswords](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md). To the right of this are two wall shelves with a sack on and a wood pile and [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) underneath. Beside this is a bucket containing with quivers of [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) and then a fireplace. There is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) in front of the fireplace and a shelf above with a bottle of wine on it. There is a stool to the right of the fire. There is a crate with an long open crate to the right inside the crate are six pieces of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md), three pieces of [corundum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore) and a piece of [malachite ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malachite_Ore). Above these is a wall shelf containing a [blacksmith's potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion), a bottle of wine and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and beside the crate is a small open crate containing nine [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot). The room continues into a small bedroom area along the west wall. In the small bedroom area is a single bed along the northern wall with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) and a copy of *[The Lusty Argonian Maid, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v2)*.

To the right of the entrance is a cupboard, on it's shelves are two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) and [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel). To the left is a fish barrel with a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) on top and a sack on the floor beside it. Around the corner against the east wall is a pair of weapon racks separated by an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md). The weapon rack on the right has a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) and the one on the left has an [iron warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer). Next to this is an open crate with five iron and four [steel ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot). Around the corner again is a small round table and chair with a bottle of ale on the table. To the left of this is a second entrance. To the left of this entrance is a dresser with a [fox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fox_Pelt) and [deer pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt) on top and in the northeast corner is a bucket with three pieces of large [charcoal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal). Against the north wall is a second flight of stairs up to the top floor. Under these stairs are two barrels-one of which has four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) on, a crate, four sacks and a dresser. There is a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) on top of the dresser. Between this and the small bedroom area is a weapon rack holding a [steel battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe).

Climbing the stairs by the south wall there is a barrel and sack at the top. Following the floor to the north you pass a narrow table on your right with a copy of *[Forge, Hammer and Anvil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forge,_Hammer_and_Anvil)* and an eidar cheese wedge. Opposite this is an open crate with two iron ore pieces and three [iron daggers](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). Next to this are two crates with a leather strip and [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) on top. On the right is the main mezzanine area with a crate with an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) and [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) and a leather strip on. To the left of this is an open crate with a leather strip and two more large pieces of charcoal. Against the west wall is a low table with a display case on containing two iron daggers with a chair to the right of it. At the end by the stairs up from downstairs is a cupboard, on the shelves is a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron), a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes), a steel ingot and two iron.
