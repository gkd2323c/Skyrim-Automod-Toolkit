[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/OB-place-Vindasel.jpg/200px-OB-place-Vindasel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Vindasel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Vindasel.jpg) Vindasel circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/ON-place-Vindasel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vindasel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vindasel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vindasel.jpg) Vindasel, circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Vindasel is a small [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup> However, it was once a settlement infamous for brutalizing slaves; legend speaks of [flesh sculptures](Lore_Flesh_Sculpture.md) known as the "Wailing Wheels of Vindasel", where the Ayleids derived strange pleasure by subjecting their slaves to this "art-torture",<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAyleid N-3)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md) sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) to the ruin to collect the legendary sword [Umbra](Lore_Umbra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup>
