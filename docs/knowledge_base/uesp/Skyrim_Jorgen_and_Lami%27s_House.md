## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House_02.jpg) The sawmill that supports Jorgen and Lami's lives Their house is separated from the rest of the town of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) as their house is across a bridge from the rest of the buildings. There are a few steps leading up to the porch at the front of the house, the door is normally novice-locked. Opposite the house is the saw mill with two [wood chopping blocks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) beside it. There are two [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) plants near where the sawn logs stack once cut and a [swamp fungal pod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod) slightly uphill to the north.

### Jorgen and Lami's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.jpg) Inside the house They live rather simply in a one room home. To the left as you enter is a set of shelves holding two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb and three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). Above these shelves are two garlic braids, three [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) all hanging from the ceiling. To the left of the shelves is a basket containing two leeks and to the right of the shelves is a barrel of [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md), with a bucket on top hiding a copy of the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Morning Star, v1](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Morning_Star,_v1)*. Behind the barrel is a unique item, a bottle of [Jessica's Wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jessica%27s_Wine). Classed as a [beverage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beverages), it restores five points of [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md).

In the middle of the south wall is a queen sized bed, at the end of the bed is an adept-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), which is identified as Jorgen's chest. To the right of the bed is a wooden table holding a copy of *[Songs of the Return, Vol 56](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_the_Return,_Vol_56)* and two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead). Next to this, against the west wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). In the middle of the west wall is a fireplace, with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantlepiece, a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front of the fire and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes) in front of the hearth. Hanging from the ceiling to the left of the fireplace is a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

In the northwestern corner, still against the west wall is a long wooden table and bench. On the table are two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). Under the table is a [potion of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor). In the northeastern corner is a square wooden table with two chairs, holding a [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing), a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), a [potion of enhanced stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Enhanced_Stamina), a [taproot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot), a [creep cluster](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md), a [torchbug thorax](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax), a bowl of [spriggan sap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan_Sap), a bowl of [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm), a sprig of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a [spider egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Egg). To the left in the middle of the north wall is a food barrel.

## Notes
- If you have started the relevant [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past) to retrieve the parts of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor), speaking to Jorgen can result in him giving you his [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House) and [chest key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen%27s_Chest). The [hilt of the razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hilt_of_Mehrunes%27_Razor) can be found in Jorgen's chest.
