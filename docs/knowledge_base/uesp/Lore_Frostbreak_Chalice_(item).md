# Frostbreak Chalice (item)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-item-Frostbreak_Chalice_%28museum%29.jpg/200px-ON-item-Frostbreak_Chalice_%28museum%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Frostbreak_Chalice_(museum).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Frostbreak_Chalice_(museum).jpg) Frostbreak Chalice The Frostbreak Chalice is a goblet emblazoned with the seal of [Frostbreak Fortress](Lore_Frostbreak_Fortress.md). It was believed by some that the chalice possessed the ability to infuse any beverage consumed from it with restorative qualities.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## History
Frostbreak Chalice originally belonged to the last commander of the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) fort known as the Frostbreak Fortress. It later went into Orcish hands after Chief Ghastzog of the [Marag Clan](Lore_Marag_Clan.md) took over the fort, and legends grew around it giving restorative properties to everything drunk from the cup, and an Orcish tradition grew from it. During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), it changed hands once again and was claimed by the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [Briarheart](Lore_Briarheart.md) [Urfon Ice-Heart](Lore_Urfon_Ice-Heart.md), the leader of the [Winterborn Clan](Lore_Winterborn.md) who disposed of the cup for ruining the taste of his wine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FC-1)</sup> It was later rumored to have been reclaimed by one of his underlings and hidden in one of the fortress's towers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOOG-3)</sup>

During King [Kurog's](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md) reign in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the House of Orsimer Glories was on a quest to acquire culturally significant artifacts for their museum. Among the many relics they sought was Frostbreak Chalice.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CLOS-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FC-1)</sup> It was eventually recovered by an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) hired for the job, who found it in the southeastern tower of the Frostbreak Fortress. The chalice was safely delivered and displayed in the museum in [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOOG-3)</sup>
