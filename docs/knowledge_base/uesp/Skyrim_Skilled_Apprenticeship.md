## Detailed Walkthrough
When visiting her shop during business hours, you can hear [Ghorza gra-Bagol](Skyrim_Ghorza_gra-Bagol.md) berating the smithing skills of her hapless apprentice, [Tacitus Sallustius](Skyrim_Tacitus_Sallustius.md). She mentions a skill book, *[Last Scabbard of Akrash](Skyrim_Last_Scabbard_of_Akrash.md)*, that might teach him something.

The marker points to [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md), which is south of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). The fort is well guarded by [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) and the book is in [Fort Sungard Muster](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard_Muster.md). However, you do not need to retrieve this exact copy of the book; any copy will do. The other two locations are [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md) and an unmarked area south-southeast of [Black-Briar Lodge](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Lodge.md). With the [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, a copy can also be found on a shelf to the right of the [Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)'s smithing area.

For retrieving the book, she will reward you with a free smithing lesson and will tell you that she is advising the [Orc Strongholds](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) that you can be trusted and that you should now be welcome in them.
