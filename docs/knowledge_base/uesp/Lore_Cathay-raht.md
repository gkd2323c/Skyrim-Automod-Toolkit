# Cathay-raht

*When Jode is waxing and Jone is waxing
Then shall be born the Cathay-Raht
Great spotted warriors feared by Others
With fur like armor and claws like knives
Fearless, independent, and bold
Weapons of war but never tools
Defenders to inspire us all.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/8/80/Mwaxing_Swaxing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Swaxing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Swaxing.jpg) Cathay-raht are born when Masser and Secunda are waxing<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Cathay-raht are a bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Khajiit of this furstock were nicknamed as "[jaguar](Lore_Senche-Cat.md#Jaguars) -men" by the [Imperials](Lore_Imperials.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup> though many do not actually resemble jaguars.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup> Khajiit of this furstock naturally resemble [Cathay](Lore_Cathay.md), albeit being larger.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tori AMA-6)</sup> They are said to be more agile than a [werewolf](Lore_Lycanthropy.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ADIF-7)</sup> and make great warriors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> They possess phallic barbs.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TRBP3-8)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-DGFP-9)</sup>

During the [Five Year War](Lore_Five_Year_War.md), the Cathay-raht were deployed as powerful front-line infantry against the forces of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MUT-10)</sup>
