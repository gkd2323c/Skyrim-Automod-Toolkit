## Official Summary
*From Iron to Dragonplate, this creation offers 15 gauntlet variants that can be crafted, purchased or found in the world. Each pair has been affixed with spikes, blades or horns to give impact to your unarmed attacks. Mix and match with existing armors to diversify your look! Created by Hothtrooper44. (Items appear at vendors, in chests and can be crafted at any forge.)*

## Notes
- The [Brawler's Fur Bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brawler%27s_Fur_Bracers) cannot be crafted.
- Items crafted at a forge are unenchanted. Enchanted versions can only be found as loot in the world or purchased from vendors.
- The [Fortify Unarmed Damage](Skyrim_Fortify_Unarmed_Damage.md) enchantment was previously only found on the unique [Gloves of the Pugilist](Skyrim_Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.md).
