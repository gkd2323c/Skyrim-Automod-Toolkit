[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/ON-place-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg/200px-ON-place-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg) The Halls of Colossus ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/AR-concept-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg/200px-AR-concept-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-concept-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-concept-Halls_of_Colossus.jpg) Halls of Colossus (Arena) The Halls of Colossus (or **Colossus Halls**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tiber Meeting-1)</sup> is an ancient set of ruins found in the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), deep in the reaches of the region of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md). It overlooks the [Great Divide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Great_Divide), a stretch of road that runs along the [Quin'rawl Peninsula](Lore_Quin%27rawl_Peninsula.md). These ancient ruins have existed in the area long before recorded history, with no indication of who built them, or why.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-2)</sup> But as the eras go by, the Halls of Colossus was used to contain ancient [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md), from [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md), to a piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md).

## History
The Halls of Colossus was constructed long before recorded history, leaving the people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) wondering who built them or why. Because of their obscurity, many people believe that the ruins are nothing but a myth, that contains an ancient weapon.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-2)</sup> While no one knows the origins of the ruins, it does not mean it did not leave people to speculate. A largely agreed upon theory is that the Halls of Colossus was built to honor an ancient race of Giants, even though they have not been seen for ages. A riddle found in the Halls of Colossus explains that the ruins were constructed by an architect known as [Theodorus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Theodorus).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup> Some people believe that [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md), the Brass God of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), is stored inside the halls,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-4)</sup> or that the halls were even built by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) to test the Numidium.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Po M-5)</sup>

During the time of the sixteen kingdoms, the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) hero, [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md), and his band of heroes, sealed a group of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) in the Halls of Colossus when they threatened to conquer all of Elsweyr, and consume the lunar energy of the moons.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kat D-6)</sup> These Dragons were sealed into the Halls of Colossus for many years and became known in legend as the *Demon Weapon*. The location of the Halls of Colossus was inscribed onto a stone tablet known as the [Wrathstone](Lore_Wrathstone.md), and three triangulates were needed to fully decipher it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESODemon-7)</sup> The stone was split in half and lost in history. The Wrathstone was needed to release the Dragons from their imprisonment.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOColossus-8)</sup>

At some point, the Khajiit built a temple in [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) known as the Hall of the Lunar Champion as a tribute to Khunzar-ri. Within, they established portals to several sites associated with Khunzar-ri's great deeds, activated only by means of placing stone tablets documenting his deeds. One such portal connected to an overlook high above the Halls of Colossus. Elsweyr's greatest champions were permitted to assume stewardship of the hall.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOHall-9)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md), Grand Chancellor of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md), and his half-sister, [Euraxia Tharn](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md), Queen of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) learned about the Demon Weapon, and the Wrathstone, both wanting to use them for their own purpose. Abnur Tharn employed the treasure hunter, Tharayya to find the two pieces of the Wrathstone, all the while, Euraxia's militia located the Halls of Colossus, and waited to ambush Abnur for the Wrathstone tablet.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESODemon-7)</sup> After finding the triangulates, Abnur Tharn, [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md), who was an Agent of Gharesh-ri, and the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), located the Halls of Colossus, and fight their way through the [Euraxians](Lore_Euraxians.md). In the end, Abnur Tharn unknowingly released the seal that trapped the Dragons, and unleashed them across Elsweyr.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOColossus-8)</sup> The Vestige later assumed stewardship of the Hall of the Lunar Champion and reestablished the portal to the Halls of Colossus.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOHall-9)</sup>

Years later, amidst the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) was gifted Numidium, the Brass God of the Dwemer from the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The Halls of Colossus and the city of Rimmen were used as a testing ground for Numidium before Tiber Septim used it to demolish the [Second Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#Second_Aldmeri_Dominion).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-WWYDB-10)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Po M-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tiber Meeting-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the ruins of the Halls of Colossus were used to contain the fourth piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md). After acquiring it from the depths of the [Elden Grove](Lore_Elden_Grove.md), the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) traveled to Elsweyr, to uncover the location of the Halls of Colossus. Their journey took them to [Corinth](Lore_Corinth.md), where they retrieved a tablet from the [Temple of Agamanus](Lore_Temple_of_Agamanus.md), in exchange for help from Turamane ap' Kolthis. In the end, Kolthis revealed the way to the Halls of Colossus, where the Eternal Champion obtained the piece of the Staff of Chaos. Afterwards, they left for the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup> The Halls of Colossus later became a pilgrimage spot for Imperial loyalists wishing to re-trace the steps of the Eternal Champion.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AMM-11)</sup>
