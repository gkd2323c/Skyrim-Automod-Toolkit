[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg) Ghostblade Ghostblade is a translucent [Ancient Nord Sword](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md) given to you by the spirits of [Fjori](Skyrim_Fjori.md) and [Holgeir](Skyrim_Holgeir.md) as a reward for freeing them from the control of [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven), as part of the quest [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md). The weapon is enchanted to do three extra points of damage that ignore the target's armor, without needing to be charged through [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). It is also extremely lightweight, and thus requires very little [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) for power attacks. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Ghostblade requires three samples of [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md), as well as the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md)**: Kill [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven) in [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund.md).

## Notes
- Ghostblade shares its appearance with [drainheart swords](Skyrim_Drainheart_Sword.md).
