[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-npc-Lemkil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lemkil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lemkil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lemkil.jpg) Lemkil **Lemkil** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) working to make a living in the small farming community of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). He is the widowed father of twin daughters, [Sissel](Skyrim_Sissel.md) and [Britte](Skyrim_Britte.md), and takes out his bitterness on his children with insults, beatings, and neglect. His daughter Britte takes after her father, beating Sissel as well. Sissel takes secret magic lessons to escape her home life, and complains that she gets beaten by both father and sister. Lemkil will [pay you](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md) for harvested [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), [leeks](Skyrim_Leek.md), and [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md).

All of Lemkil's dialogue centers around his dislike of his daughters, and every interaction with other characters shows his poor reputation due to his mistreatment of them.

He sleeps in his [farmhouse](Skyrim_Lemkil%27s_Farmhouse.md) every night between 10pm and 6am. After waking up he goes straight to work in the field behind his house. At 6pm he enters [Frostfruit Inn](Skyrim_Frostfruit_Inn.md) before he returns home and goes to bed at 10pm.

He wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a1) with a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a0), and carries a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), the [farmhouse key](Skyrim_Lemkil%27s_House_Key.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and an assortment of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings can be:

- *"Do yourself a favor and don't have children. They're good for nothing at all."*
- *"I got enough troubles, so don't go adding to them."*
- *"I spend every day tending the fields, and what do my daughters do? Nothing! Complain and caterwaul! Sleep and eat! Useless!"*
- *"Curse my good-for-nothing daughters! My wife was twice the woman they'll ever grow to be. She gave her life birthing them. What a waste."*

## Conversations
The townsfolk of Rorikstead are all concerned about his unhealthy influence on the children, and a group of citizens will try to calm him down:

- **[Jouane Manette](Skyrim_Jouane_Manette.md)**: *"Good day, Lemkil. I saw some nice dresses for your little girls while I was in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). I'd be happy to purchase them, if you like."*
- **Lemkil**: *"We don't need your charity, [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md). If my girls deserve something nice, it'll be their father who buys it."*

- **[Rorik](Skyrim_Rorik.md)**: *"Those little girls of yours are growing like weeds, Lemkil."*
- **Lemkil**: *"Yes, and just like weeds, they're nothin' but trouble."*

- **[Mralki](Skyrim_Mralki.md)**: *"Your daughters are growing up fast, Lemkil. Seems like only yesterday they were newborn babes."*
- **Lemkil**: *"They've grown, all right - lazier and more stupid."*
- **Mralki**: *"There's times I think you're the coldest man in Skyrim. This is your own flesh and blood you're talking about."*

- **[Erik](Skyrim_Erik.md)**: *"I'm not saying it's my business, but I have to ask. Why are you so cruel to your little girls?"*
- **Lemkil**: *"You're right, it's none of your business. How about you keep your nose out of it?"*
- **Erik**: *"You keep on like you are, you'll end up with two daughters who hate you."*
- **Lemkil**: *"Hah! And what would a witless oaf like you know about raising children?"*
