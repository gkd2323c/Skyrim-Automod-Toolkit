[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-ingredient-Human_Flesh.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Human_Flesh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Human_Flesh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Human_Flesh.jpg) Human flesh **Human Flesh** can be found in locations where people have performed the Black Sacrament ritual to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) (along with [human hearts](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md)), and in a few necromancer dungeons. Flesh cannot be taken from dead humans. It is easily overlooked because it looks like [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef). Can also be found on werewolves, along with a wolf pelt.

- 1 in [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only other ways to obtain human flesh all have relatively low probabilities. The best bets are:

- Rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) have a 6% chance of containing one.
- [Falmer magic casters](Skyrim_Falmer.md#Shaman), [warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md), and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) have a 0.7% chance of carrying one.
- With the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on, dead [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) will always carry one sample of human flesh, as well as one wolf pelt.
- With the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on, dead [werebears](Skyrim_Werebear.md) typically carry one sample of human flesh.
- Random reward from quest [The Straw that Broke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Straw_that_Broke).

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants.
