[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-item-Nerveshatter.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nerveshatter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nerveshatter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nerveshatter.jpg) Nerveshatter Nerveshatter is a Daedric artifact composed of [Madness Ore](Skyrim_Madness_Ore.md) and [Amber](Skyrim_Amber.md) found in [Crystaldrift Cave](Skyrim_Crystaldrift_Cave.md) as part of the [Nerveshatter](Skyrim_Nerveshatter_(quest).md) quest. Like generic weapons "of Storms", it does 30 points of [Shock Damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) to its targets. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Nerveshatter requires [Refined Amber](Skyrim_Refined_Amber.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, and the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk makes tempering twice as effective. It cannot be broken down for its enchantments.
