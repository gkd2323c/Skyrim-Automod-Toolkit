The Crown of Bones is a cursed artifact associated with [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and the Lost Fleet in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). The crown can curse items such as treasure, which will transform anyone who touches it into an [undead](Lore_Undead.md) [skeleton](Lore_Skeleton.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Jalan ESO-1)</sup>. The wearer of the Crown of Bones will then be able to control all the skeletons created by it, though they will be transformed as well.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASESO-3)</sup>

The control the crown has over an individual varies, some may try and resist the user's commands or are considered too willful for the crown to work.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASESO-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOBosune Bones-4)</sup> Additionally, some forms of magic have been demonstrated to be able to break the crown's hold on its thralls.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-One Eye ESO-5)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/ON-npc-Lost_Fleet_Admiral.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Lost_Fleet_Admiral.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Lost_Fleet_Admiral.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Lost_Fleet_Admiral.jpg) The Lost Fleet Admiral The first known bearer of the Crown of Bones was the captain of *the Golden Era*, a ship of the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md). When the [Coral Tower](Lore_Coral_Tower_(Thras).md) was destroyed, the ship and its crew were one of the unfortunates that were drawn into the newly formed Maelstrom of Bal that dumped them in Coldharbour.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTE-2)</sup> Molag Bal then approached the captain and made a pact with him, gifting him the Crown of Bones. He then named himself the Admiral of the Lost Fleet and plunged his remaining crew into undeath and placed them under his control.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTE-2)</sup> In the centuries after donning the crown, the admiral had his Lost Fleet [rebuilding the Coral Tower](Lore_Coral_Tower_(Coldharbour).md) to reach the portal hanging within the sky to return to Nirn.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASESO-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTE-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) during the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), more ships were pulled into the maelstrom and deposited in Coldharbour. These included the Bonnie Spriggan and her captain, the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) One-Eye. Several of her crew were transformed by cursed treasure and placed under the Admiral's control to attack her.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AGo S-6)</sup> One-Eye was then approached by Tsona-Ei, the former first mate of *the Golden Era* who provided her and [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) the means to reach the Admiral and bring him down. In the aftermath, it is unknown whether the Crown of Bones was destroyed or if Captain One-Eye became its next wielder, using her magical skills to break the crown's hold on each soul individually.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BBa B-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-One Eye ESO-5)</sup>
