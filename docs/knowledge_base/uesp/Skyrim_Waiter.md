[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-npc-Waiter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Waiter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Waiter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Waiter.jpg) Waiter The **Waiter** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md). During the quest [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md), he will give you a [dinner menu](Skyrim_Dinner_Menu.md), then he will attack the assembled guests after you [read a note](Skyrim_Please_Read_Aloud.md) at the dinner table. If you are not a vampire, you will have to kill him during the attack. If you are a vampire, he will formally turn the lair over to you once his work is completed.

He wears leveled vampiric attire and [vampire boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>: Attend a feast.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Speaking to the Waiter at the start of the party will allow you to ask him to perform some basic tasks for you, which will always elicit a short response:

- **Can you show me to my table?**
- *Of course.*
- **I'm starving, let's eat already..**
- *I understand.*
- **I'd like to see the menu, please.**
- *Of course*
- **I'd like to be seated. Now.**
- *I understand.*

After killing the guests and the Dawnguard ambush as a vampire, you will need to speak to the Waiter to proceed further:

- **The Dawnguard and the nobles are dead.**
- *It would seem so.*
- **The note seemed to imply the last survivor would get the house.**
- *I'm so glad you succeeded. Here. Please take this for your trouble.*

He will then hand you the [Bloodchill Manor Key](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor_Key.md).
