[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-item-Her_Hand_Gauntlets.jpg/200px-SR-item-Her_Hand_Gauntlets.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Gauntlets.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Gauntlets.jpg) Her Hand Gauntlets Her Hand Gauntlets are the part of the [set of armor](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor_Set.md) used by the members of Almalexia's personal guard to protect their own hands. Alongside these gauntlets, this set of equipment also includes [boots](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Boots.md), [cuirass](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor.md), and a [helmet](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Helmet.md). The armor has been enchanted to [fortify one-handed](Skyrim_Fortify_One-Handed.md) attacks, making Almalexia's ordinators deadlier in combat. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) these gauntlets requires a piece of [Chitin Plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

There is also a version of this armor that does not have an enchantment already placed upon it, leaving its magical effects up to you.
