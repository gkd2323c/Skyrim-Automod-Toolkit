## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md).
2. Meet the men preparing for the attack.
3. Defeat the Imperials.
4. Return to [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) will tell you to meet the Stormcloak soldiers southwest of [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) and to help them attack the fort. When you arrive, the soldiers will begin to storm the fort and your journal will tell you to kill all the Imperials and keep track of the percentage left. There will be some wooden barricades blocking the entrance to the fort—three hits from a weapon or Destruction spell will destroy them. Fight alongside your Stormcloak brothers and finish off the Imperial soldiers. After the Imperials have been defeated, the quest will end and you will have to head back to [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) for the next part of [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md).

## Notes
- Completing this quest will exile [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md), her family, and courtiers to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md), and install [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of Hjaalmarch.
- Completing this quest will also complete the quest [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and advance the quest [The Fallen](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md) to the objective "Learn Shout to call Odahviing".
- If [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) is given to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) then this quest will not be given to you.
