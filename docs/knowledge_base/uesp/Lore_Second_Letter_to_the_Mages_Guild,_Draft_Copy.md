Second Letter to the Mages Guild, Draft Copy **by [Adept Irnard Rirnil](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Adept_Irnard_Rirnil)**
---
To: Grivier Adrad, Second Secretary to Archmagister Emeritus [Vanus Galarion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md)
From: Irnard Rirnil, Adept
Regarding: Work at [the Scholarium](Lore_The_Scholarium.md)

Greetings to the secretary and the archimagister!

Thank you for the tomes included in the last shipment. They have been most useful in helping us translate and cross-check many of the editions we have already uncovered. We have found tomes in Old [Aldermeris](Lore_Aldmeris_(language).md), [Atmoran](Lore_Nordic.md#Atmoran_Dictionary), [Yokudan](Lore_Yoku.md), the [Nedic](Lore_Old_Nedic.md) [languages](Lore_Language.md), and even [variations](Lore_Old_Orcish.md) of [Orcish](Lore_Orcish.md). We've even uncovered metal plates covered with [Dwemer runes](Lore_Dwemeris.md), but confirming this and deciphering them will require extensive research.

Work proceeds slowly on the cataloging and translation of the works. Part of it is due the environment— [the Scholarium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:The_Scholarium) itself has been plagued with high humidity and water damage, and many of the more ancient volumes are in perilous condition, suffering from decay.

In addition, there was a plague of booklice and paper weevils in the western wing that has damaged some irreplaceable volumes. This is no doubt a function of the lengthy period the Scholarium spent in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md)! I've attached (in a separate message) a new copy of my treatise on Daedric realms and their impact on Nirnian pest creatures. I'm sure you'll find it a most enjoyable read!

I have also passed along a request (sent by separate messenger) for a variety of cleansers and preservation supplies. Arresting the immediate damage is only part of the current challenges, as even the most pristine books are in a delicate condition, and we must take utmost care in their protection.

Thank you for your time and attention in this matter. May [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md) watch over us.

Irnard Rirnil, Adept
