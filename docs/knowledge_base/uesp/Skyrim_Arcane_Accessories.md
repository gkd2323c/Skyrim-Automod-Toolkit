It was released on October 4, 2017 for 100 Creation Credits. Many of the spells originally appeared in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*'s *[Spell Tomes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes)* [add-on](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads).

## Official Summary
*From robes that grant spellcasting bonuses, to ancient tomes holding powerful spells like Paralysis Rune and Unbounded Frost, this pack will increase any mage's effectiveness. (Robes and Spells can be purchased at vendors and appear in containers.)*
## Overview
The items added by this Creation can be encountered through normal gameplay, such as from vendors and loot chests. You can also locate a chest in [Hob's Fall Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hob%27s_Fall_Cave) that contains spell tomes for every new spell.

Note that the new robes can be very powerful, and the spells use far less [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) than anything available in the base game with the same effect.

## Notes
- Originally, after you installed this Creation, you would receive a set of leveled Robes of Destruction & Alteration and leveled Robes of Conjuration & Illusion in your inventory. The quest [The Lost Library](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Library) would also be added to your journal, directing you to the chest in Hob's Fall Cave. This quest was removed as part of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update, and the robes are no longer automatically added to your inventory.
- The leveled robes can be purchased at [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md).
- The [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill can be quickly raised to 100 by casting one of the unbounded element spells and fast travelling whilst the spell is being cast. - Fortify Destruction is not necessary for this to work, unlike with the Alteration spell Telekinesis.
- Unlocking perks for [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Destruction.md#Augmented_Frost), [Shock](Skyrim_Destruction.md#Augmented_Shock), or [Flames](Skyrim_Destruction.md#Augmented_Flames) will all strengthen the elemental projectile spells.
