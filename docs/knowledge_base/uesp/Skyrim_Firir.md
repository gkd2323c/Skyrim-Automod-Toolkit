[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Firir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Firir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Firir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Firir.jpg) Firir's body in a Falmer cage **Firir** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) found dead in [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md) after chasing a lead on a brutal assault on a merchant caravan. He wears only a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) with no shoes, and carries at least 25 gold.

## Notes
- A [torn note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torn_Note) is found on his body in the cave, apologizing for his death to his love, "Eydis". It is unclear if this is the [Eydis](Skyrim_Eydis.md) at [Old Hroldan Inn](Skyrim_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md), as her "missing" husband is claimed to be a soldier and not named at any point. Additionally, bringing the note to Eydis has no effect.

## Bugs
- Firir has the `Female Commoner` voice type. This results in a female voice if the body is [resurrected](Skyrim_Reanimate.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
