[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg/200px-ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg) Central Highlands circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Padomaic Crest (or **Central Highlands**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CD:TP-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-2)</sup> is a coastal region in east-central [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VG:TP-3)</sup> presently within the [Indoril District](Lore_House_Indoril.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GHOM-4)</sup> It borders the region of [Fungal Lowlands](Lore_Fungal_Lowlands.md) to the west, and the region of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) to the south. Together with the Fungal Lowlands, it was considered one of the two regions making up the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). The region is home to the city of [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md), as well as the isle of [Gorne](Lore_Gorne.md) off the coast. The [Dres](Lore_Dres.md) town of [Alavelis](Lore_Alavelis.md) is located along the south near a volcano, and is home to a [glass](Lore_Malachite.md) mine.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VG:TP-3)</sup>

The high uplands and treacherous cliff coastline gives way to craggy rocks along the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-POT-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Fot N-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NSH-7)</sup> [Topal the Pilot](Lore_Topal_the_Pilot.md) described seeing large "[bat lizards](Lore_Bat_Lizard.md)" in the area, which are believed to be the ancestors of modern [cliff racers](Lore_Cliff_Racer.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Fot N-6)</sup> Tiny tribes of [ogres](Lore_Ogre.md) and [trolls](Lore_Troll.md) also patrol the highlands.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CD:TP-1)</sup>
