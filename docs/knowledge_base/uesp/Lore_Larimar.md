# Larimar

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fragment-Larimar_Gems.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fragment-Larimar_Gems.png) Larimar Gems [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/ON-render-Tales_of_Tribute_03.jpg/200px-ON-render-Tales_of_Tribute_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Tales_of_Tribute_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Tales_of_Tribute_03.jpg) Larimar was used to make Patron tokens in Tales of Tribute. Larimar, also referred to as **sea-stone** by merchants in [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), is a valuable milky-blue gemstone. This precious stone is exclusive to the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) archipelago, making it extremely rare and expensive. The stone's hue is reminiscent of turquoise, albeit brighter and creamier. Some have compared its color to that of the [Topal Sea](Lore_Topal_Sea.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OR-1)</sup>

During the [Reman Empire](Lore_Reman_Empire.md)'s reign, larimar mining became a prominent industry in the Systres Archipelago due to the stone's rarity and high value, thanks to its exclusivity to the region. In [2E 11](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_11), the newly empowered [Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) established a penal colony on [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md), one of the islands of the archipelago, where political prisoners were forced to work in the larimar and [ammonite](Lore_Ammonite.md) mines.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SH-2)</sup>

It was also used by the [Druids](Lore_Druids.md) to create artictacts, such as the Oakensoul Ring. Ring's larimar gems were engraved with druidic runes and symbolized the [Eldertide Circle](Lore_Eldertide_Circle.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OR-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OR2-3)</sup>

Larimar had a broader range of applications. It was also utilized to create tokens for the widely popular game [Tales of Tribute](Lore_Tales_of_Tribute.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOT-4)</sup> as well as to create prosthetic eyes.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LFE-5)</sup>
