[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-ingredient-Lyretail_Anthias.png/200px-SR-ingredient-Lyretail_Anthias.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Lyretail_Anthias.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Lyretail_Anthias.png) Lyretail Anthias **Lyretail Anthias** is an ingredient added by the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). A school of three can sometimes be caught only by hand (without fishing) at [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md). Otherwise, it is considered a rare fish that only be caught using an Alik'ri fishing rod. The fish lives only in the 24 streams shown at the end of the [fishing spots list.](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Fishing_Locations)
