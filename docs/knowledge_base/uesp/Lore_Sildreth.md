# Sildreth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/ON-npc-Tilenra_Sildreth.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Tilenra_Sildreth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Tilenra_Sildreth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Tilenra_Sildreth.jpg) Tilenra Sildreth Sildreth family is an ancient [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) lineage that lived in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They were affiliated with the [Great House](Lore_Great_Houses.md) [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WRTP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TS-2)</sup> They had ties to [Suran](Lore_Suran.md), and some family members were also known to inhabit the settlement.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TS-2)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
At some point during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) at least one member of the Sildreth family had already ascended to the rank of Magister. A Telvanni Magister from the Sildreth family was among the twelve influential members of House Telvanni who signed the official refusal to join the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WRTP-1)</sup>

[Tilenra Sildreth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Tilenra_Sildreth) was a high-ranking member of the Telvanni, as well as a naturalist and alchemist who ran a healing business in Suran. She was also an author, having written *[Vvardenfell Flora and Fauna](Lore_Vvardenfell_Flora_and_Fauna.md)*, a detailed guide to the various [species](Lore_Bestiary.md) of her homeland, and *[Teas and Tisanes for Aches and Pains](Lore_Teas_and_Tisanes_for_Aches_and_Pains.md)*, a collection of recipes for medicinal infusions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAT-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VFF-4)</sup> Around 2E 582, Tilenra Sildreth had a rivalry with Menaldinion, an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) healer from the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) who recently opened a clinic in Suran. Despite their initial tension, the two eventually set aside their differences and began cooperating.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NTSA-5)</sup>
