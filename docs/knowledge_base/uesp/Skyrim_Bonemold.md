# Bonemold

**Bonemold** armor is heavy armor which can be crafted from [iron ingots](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md), [netch leather](Skyrim_Netch_Leather.md), and [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) with the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk. All items can be tempered using two samples of bone meal. According to [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md), it is crafted from actual bone which is reinforced with a resin-like material and shaped into armor pieces. Bonemold Bolts are only available with the [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed.

## Improved Bonemold Armor
After finding the [bonemold formula](Skyrim_Bonemold_Formula.md), improved bonemold armor can be crafted at any [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md). Additionally [netch jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly), [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md), and [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) will be needed. Its armor rating is slightly better than [Dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armor.md), and it weighs less.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Female.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Female.jpg) Female Bonemold Guard Armor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Male.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Male.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Male.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bonemold_Guard_Armor_Male.jpg) Male Bonemold Guard Armor - The bonemold guard armor variant is worn by [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md), while the pauldron-less version is worn commonly by [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md). The version with pauldrons is worn by [Redoran guards](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md).
- Unlike in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), there are no variants of the helmet or shield.
- Only the pauldron armor variant has an improved version.

## Bugs
- Bonemold Gauntlets and Improved Bonemold Gauntlets don't benefit from the Fists of Steel perk. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.7, fixes this bug.
