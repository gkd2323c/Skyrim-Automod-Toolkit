Red Eagle's Rite *A rite to raise the legendary Red Eagle from death*
---
Having bathed the blade in human blood, present it at [Rebel's Cairn](Skyrim_Rebel%27s_Cairn.md) together with your sacrifice and intone:

Lord [Red Eagle](Skyrim_Red_Eagle.md), ancient one, first and foremost among [Reachmen](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), heed the call of your people! Still we fight for freedom! Still our blades are dark with blood! Turn your gaze upon us, and grant us your blessing anew!

I renew the ancient covenant: When at last our lands are free, we shall return, your sword of victory in hand. Then arise, O great one, from your honored tomb! Reclaim thy stolen throne! Rule over us, High Lord of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md), forevermore!
