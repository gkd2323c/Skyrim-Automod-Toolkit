# Fabricants

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/LG-cardart-Fabricate.png/200px-LG-cardart-Fabricate.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Fabricate.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Fabricate.png) A Fabricant being constructed [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/TR-place-Dome_of_Serlyn.jpg/200px-TR-place-Dome_of_Serlyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Dome_of_Serlyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Dome_of_Serlyn.jpg) Fabricant constructor in the Dome of Serlyn Fabricants are the biomechanical<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMP-1)</sup><sup>:316</sup> (part organic, part inorganic) constructs created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) and are designed to emulate the form and behavior of their [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) counterparts. They are constructed from an amalgam of grown flesh and metallic grafts and are powered by "soul gem matrices",<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PLJ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDivayth NPC-3)</sup> in factories known as Incarnatoriums. They are then stored in fabrication tanks from which they will eventually emerge. They usually dwell in the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), and can resemble the creatures of the land, air, and sea. The majority of Fabricants were created to emulate predatory animals since the synthetic realm was inhospitable to most forms of organic plant life prior to introduction of the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md). Sometimes they might not resemble anything known to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), such as the [Verminous Fabricant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verminous_Fabricant)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ASo F-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TFFOCC-5)</sup> and the [Hulking Fabricant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hulking_Fabricant). Early fabricants had issues with the deterioration of their mechanical parts. They were made capable of self-repair, which was how the early fabricants created in [Kemel-Ze](Lore_Kemel-Ze.md) were able to persist centuries after their creation.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSotha-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Protus-7)</sup>

Fabricants are rumored to have existed since before the disappearance of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md)<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TSLT-8)</sup> and survived past the demise of Sotha Sil.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TESL-9)</sup> To the [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostles.md), they are also known as the **Fabri'siraynosim** ("the merged-ones")<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> and are rumored to be the first step towards the convergence of nature and engineering,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Wit CC-11)</sup> and the creation of the perfect lifeform.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-LRTCCD-12)</sup>

At an unknown time, fabricants that resemble birds were created. Their creation was likely sometime after the Vestige's journey in the Clockwork city in the [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). During this journey, [crow daedra](Lore_Crow_Daedra.md) accompanied [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md)'s invasion of the Clockwork City. The crows surprised the people of the Brass Fortress, as the winged creatures seemed foreign to them. No living birds up to that point are believed to have set foot in Clockwork City.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOPVA-13)</sup>

[Abominations](Lore_Abomination.md) are similar to fabricants, but are a perversion of Sotha Sil's goal of creating a perfect lifeform. They were created by the Clockwork Apostle, [Mecinar](Lore_Mecinar.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-LRTCCD-12)</sup>

Similar to fabricants are the constructs Sotha Sil created known as [factotums](Lore_Factotum.md), but they differ in that they are completely synthetic and are usually more humanoid in appearance.

It is unknown if Clockwork Dreugh are fabricants, but they follow a similar naming pattern to other fabricants. They were constructs created by Sotha Sil before the disappearance of the Dwemer. It is said that [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) asked Sotha Sil to come up with some plan to help stop the Dwemer's Automatons from slaughtering the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) during the [Battle of Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MPStars-14)</sup> Out of necessity, Sotha Sil created an army of Clockwork Dreugh that were inspired by Dwemeri war machines, to be used against the Dwemer. They were said to have risen up from the seas and took their counterparts to the water beneath to be swallowed by the sea. <sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TSLT-8)</sup>

## Varieties
There are many different kinds of fabricants. Reptiles, aquatic animals, mammals and more are all fabricated within the Clockwork City. Fabricants are diverse, and while many resemble creatures commonly seen on Nirn, more still are completely alien.

### Beetle Fabricant

Their legs are metal limbs, and it uses strong lightning attacks. It is aggressive and will attack on sight. It is not to be confused with similar fabricants such as the shalk fabricant.

### Brass Mudcrab
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/ON-pet-Prodigious_Brass_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Prodigious_Brass_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Prodigious_Brass_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Prodigious_Brass_Mudcrab.jpg) Brass Mudcrab **Brass Mudcrabs** (or **Crab Fabricants**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Loranna-21)</sup> are fabricants resembling [Mudcrabs](Lore_Mudcrab.md). They have a brass rust-resistant coating and can enter into water but are likely to sink.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Prodigious_Brass_Mudcrab)

### [Brassilisk](Lore_Brassilisk.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/ON-creature-Brassilisk.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Brassilisk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Brassilisk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Brassilisk.jpg) Brassilisk [Brassilisks](Lore_Brassilisk.md) are fabricants resembling [snakes](Lore_Snake.md). They are created from interconnected metallic squares, with exposed copper wiring that allow for their snakelike movements. Some are created with wings to mimic the [Sep Adder](Lore_Sep_Adder.md). They are a popular choice as pets among the denizens of the Clockwork City. They can be powered by either soul gems, animo cores, or steam power.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Brassilisk), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Brassilisk)

### [Firepot Spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Firepot_Spider)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/ON-creature-Firepot_Spider.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Firepot_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Firepot_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Firepot_Spider.jpg) Firepot Spider **[Firepot Spiders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Firepot_Spider)** are fabricants that resemble [spiders](Lore_Spider.md). They are usually hostile but can be programmed to be passive and repurposed to be used as a walking barbecue by chefs.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-ONFirepet Spider-23)</sup> Firepot spiders are known to attack by lunging at a target and exploding the pot on their back, losing it as their shell and becoming a "dancing spider".

Early prototypes of the firepot spiders contained the same flammable liquid usually contained within the creatures, but their propulsion method differed from the final version. They were also significantly larger than the final versions, and could not reliably pull off the kinds of acrobatic maneuvers that the final version can.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Firepot_Spider), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Firepot Spider)

### [Hulking Fabricant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hulking_Fabricant)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/TR-creature-Hulking_Fabricant.jpg/200px-TR-creature-Hulking_Fabricant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-creature-Hulking_Fabricant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-creature-Hulking_Fabricant.jpg) Hulking Fabricants [Hulking Fabricants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hulking_Fabricant) are [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricant.md) created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) that resemble a cross between an undersized [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md), a [scorpion](Lore_Scorpion.md), and a [chaurus](Lore_Chaurus.md). They have wide jaws, a crest of horns or growths on their heads, two organic forelimbs, four to six metal legs, and a metal scorpion-like tail. Unlike most other Fabricants, they do not appear to directly resemble any extant creatures of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Hulking Fabricants dwell in [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), but have in occasion made it to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

**Found in:** - [Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Hulking_Fabricant), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Hulking Fabricant)

### Nix-Ox Fabricant
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-crown_store-Nix-Ox_Fabricant_Steed.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Nix-Ox_Fabricant_Steed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Nix-Ox_Fabricant_Steed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Nix-Ox_Fabricant_Steed.jpg) Nix-Ox Fabricant **Nix-Ox Fabricants** are programmed for obedience and designed for endurance. Similar to organic [Nix-Oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md), they are used as mounts in the Brass Fortress and in the Radius.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-25)</sup>

The Lustrous Nix-Ox Fabricant is a variety of this construct, noted for its darker coloration in contrast with the usual brassy look. Before [Saints Llothis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Saint_Llothis_the_Pious) and [Saint Olms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Saint_Olms_the_Just) had to be confined in the Asylum Sanctorium, they used to ride through the [Brass Fortress](Lore_Brass_Fortress.md) on holy days on these mounts. However, these parades eventually came to an end with the deterioration of the saints' sanity, and the Lustrous Nix-Ox Fabricant was repurposed.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-26)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Nix-Ox_Fabricant_Steed_(creature))

### Seht's Dovah-Fly
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/ON-pet-Seht%27s_Dovah-Fly.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Seht%27s_Dovah-Fly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Seht%27s_Dovah-Fly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Seht%27s_Dovah-Fly.jpg) Seht's Dovah-Fly **Seht's Dovah-Flies** are fabricants that were created to imitate dovah-flies (more commonly known as [dragonflies](Lore_Dragonfly.md)). They are harmless, and are known to make loyal pets.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-28)</sup> A variant known as the Scintillant Dovah-Fly is black with gold trimmings and is a common sight in the oil-slick ravines of the Clockwork City.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-29)</sup> They are rumored to have been a collaboration between two ill-fated lovers. These mechanical dovah-flies were created to serve as a messenger, delivering communications between them. So impressed were their masters by this creation that they set their feud aside and allowed the apprentices to collaborate on future projects.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-TFFo C-30)</sup>

### Shalk Fabricant

### [Skeevaton](Lore_Skeevaton.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/ON-creature-Skeevaton.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Skeevaton.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Skeevaton.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Skeevaton.jpg) Skeevaton [Skeevatons](Lore_Skeevaton.md) are [fabricant](Lore_Fabricant.md) creatures that, despite their name, resemble [mice](Lore_Mice.md) or small [rats](Lore_Rat.md) moreso than [skeevers](Lore_Skeever.md). They can be found all across the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) and inside the [Brass Fortress](Lore_Brass_Fortress.md). They were created to serve as couriers of information to and from the archives, as well as for probing tiny, inaccessible areas of the Clockwork City. To this end, they contain a sensory tool that sends out pulses of detection magic to survey objects of interest and search within specific parameters. At some point, skeevatons were over-produced, and the denizens of the Brass Fortress had a skeevaton infestation on their hands. The series of events that followed the infestation was a mess that ended in many fabricants becoming scrap. Wind-up Bears and Motorized Tigers were produced to stop the Clockwork Hounds, which were fabricated to stop the Clockwork Cats that were created to stop the mass-produced skeevatons. Sotha Sil destroyed all these fabricants, with the execption of the excess Skeevatons, which one of his followers chalked up to the God's sense of humor. The skeevaton, resembling a copper mouse, lacks legs and moves using a rotating ball housed within its center. Legend has it that its inventor designed the skeevaton to clandestinely observe fellow scholars.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Skeevaton), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Skeevaton)

### [Verminous Fabricant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verminous_Fabricant)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg) Verminous Fabricant [Verminous Fabricants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verminous_Fabricant) are [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricant.md) created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) that resemble lean bipedal [reptiles](Lore_Reptile.md). They have long necks, long tails, a horn on their noses, and various metallic limbs and spikes, which they utilize to attack their prey. Sotha Sil created them as imitations of [extinct creatures](Lore_Verminous.md) of the same name that once roamed across [Velothi](Lore_Velothi.md) [lands](Lore_Morrowind.md), to serve as a reminder of a thing that once was. He sought to perfect them (in his eyes) with an amalgam of flesh and metal. Aside from being quick, agile, and being able to fit into small spaces, they also are capable of spinning into their target, generating shockwaves, and as a last resort, self-destructing. They are powered by an [animo core](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Animo_Cores), and can be contained within fabrication tanks. Verminous Fabricants dwell in [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), but have in occasion made it to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

**Found in:** - [Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Verminous_Fabricant), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Verminous_Fabricant), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Verminous Fabricant)
