[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-npc-J%27Kier.jpg/200px-SR-npc-J%27Kier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-J%27Kier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-J%27Kier.jpg) J'Kier **J'Kier**, a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

J'Kier wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots). He carries an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a small amount of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md)**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md)'s Great Hunt.

## Quest-Related Events

### Ill Met By Moonlight
When you arrive, he is wounded at the first campfire. If you speak to him, he will ask weakly:

- *"Has the [Bloodmoon](Lore_Bloodmoon.md) called you, fellow Hunter?"*
- **What happened here?**
- *"The prey is strong. Stronger than the hunters. But more will come. Bring him down, for the glories of [Lord Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)."*

Once he finishes, he succumbs to his wounds and dies.

## Notes
- It seems that, at one point, J'Kier was meant to be alive longer, as he has dialogue for accompanying you during the hunt. This dialogue never gets used due to his scripted death early in the quest.

- *"May Hircine guide our blades."*
- *"We follow you, hunt-master."*
