Unmarked Pages *Journal entries by an unknown Scaled Courtier* *
---
2nd [First Seed](Lore_First_Seed.md)

We're uncertain if it's possible, but what the [master](Lore_The_Serpent.md) wants, the master gets. Our presence in [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) intensifies by the day, but he remains … inconsolable. I understand his consternation—the highlands would be completely under our control if he could roam free.

The combination of [troll](Lore_Troll.md) and [Nedic](Lore_Nedic.md) ritual that finally cracked his prison can make no further progress on it. Even the thralled [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) Guardians have failed. I don't know what options remain to us.

The Regents will do their work in his stead, of course. The master chose them for a reason.

4th First Seed

Perhaps the answer has been staring me in the face. We have more [nirncrux](Lore_Nirncrux.md) stored at Sanctum Ophidia than we could ever hope to deplete. We could fuel a war for years with our supply.

And as a primordial dust, nirncrux channels energy—transfers it. The master is composed entirely of Celestial energy. Perhaps that is the key.

5th [Second Seed](Lore_Second_Seed.md)

The master possessed a ritual that required just a little blood from our [Courtlings](Lore_Scaled_Court.md) (unfortunately, we lost the volunteers, but footsoldiers can always be replaced) and a smattering of nirncrux dust. We have earned some gratitude from the master—some patience. It will be a while yet before he will walk from this cavern on his own power, but the vessel, at least, is complete.

And may whatever god they believe in help those who stand in its path.
