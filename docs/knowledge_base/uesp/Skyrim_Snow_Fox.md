# Fox

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-creature-Fox.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Fox.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fox.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Fox.jpg) A Fox [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-creature-Snow_Fox.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Snow_Fox.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snow_Fox.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snow_Fox.jpg) A Snow Fox **Foxes** are small, non-aggressive creatures found in the wilderness of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md), often chasing [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit.md). Red foxes will leave [pelts](Skyrim_Fox_Pelt.md) behind upon death, which can be used for [tanning](Skyrim_Tanning.md#Fox_Pelt) purposes. When approached, foxes will flee. They will generally only become hostile if they are under the influence of a [Spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md). There are four unique foxes, two of which are added by *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)*.

- Unique Foxes: [Kit](Skyrim_Kit.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, [Pumpkin](Skyrim_Pumpkin.md), [Sweet Roll](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll_(creature).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim.md)</sup>, [Vix](Skyrim_Vix.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>

## Notes
- With the *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md), your [adopted children](Skyrim_Adoption.md) can bring home either variant as [pets](Skyrim_Pets.md).
- As a pet, the fox may have unpleasantly obtrusive sound effects for its breathing.

## Bugs
- Both variations of foxes fail to respawn due to missing respawn flags in the game data. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
