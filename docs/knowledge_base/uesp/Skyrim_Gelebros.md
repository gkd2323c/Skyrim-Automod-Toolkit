[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-npc-Gelebros.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gelebros.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gelebros.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gelebros.jpg) Gelebros **Gelebros**, a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [sorcerer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Sorcerer), is a member of the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) who arrives at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) along with [Quaranir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir) and [Tandil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tandil) to secure [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md).

Gelebros wears [Psiijic robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Psiijic_Robes) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Psiijic_Boots), [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Psiijic_Gloves), and [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Psiijic_Hood).

If you attempt to talk to him, he will only say, *"Our order will not forget your deeds."*
