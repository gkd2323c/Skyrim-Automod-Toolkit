Septimia was the first colony of the short-lived [Imperial Province of Akavir](Lore_Imperial_Province_of_Akavir.md). It was originally a [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) port at the mouth of a large river, but was abandoned by its inhabitants and captured by [Emperor Uriel Septim V](Lore_Uriel_Septim_V.md) in the year [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288). It was located in a fertile river valley, and provided easy access to the interior of the continent. It was used as the main port of the Far East Fleet, and was connected by road to the invasion headquarters of [Ionith](Lore_Ionith.md). However, the many reefs, islands and sandbars made entering the harbor difficult.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDI-1)</sup>

During the [Battle of Ionith](Lore_Battle_of_Ionith.md), the town was besieged by Tsaesci warriors and suffered terrible, almost unnatural weather. The few survivors of the invasion were rescued here. The settlement was reclaimed by the Tsaesci and has never been revisited by Imperial forces since.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDI-1)</sup>
