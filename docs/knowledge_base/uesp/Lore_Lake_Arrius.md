[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/OB-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg/200px-OB-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg) Lake Arrius circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/ON-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Arrius.jpg) Lake Arrius circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lake Arrius is a lake north of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md). It can be found right where the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) region meets the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup> A small community of worshippers lives around a shrine to [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) northwest of the lake.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MH-2)</sup>

In the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), it was the site of Arrius Keep, a fort in the center of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md)'s claimed territory.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Online-3)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) famously infiltrated a [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) lair near the lake and took the [Mysterium Xarxes](Lore_Mysterium_Xarxes.md) as part of the effort to end the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOC-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Though *[Modern Heretics](Lore_Modern_Heretics.md)* says the shrine to Azura is northwest of the lake, in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, the shrine is almost directly north of the lake.
