## Official Summary
*Perched high in the mountains to the north. This unique player home features several multi-level living quarters, including a dungeon area and bed coffins perfect for warden of the night. Created by Elianora. Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Guests for Dinner" starts by reading the Dinner Invitation delivered by courier upon reaching level 12.)*

## Notes
- The Vampire Leather Hood is the same type of hood Serana wears while in exterior locations.
- Both the Dinner Invitation and the Dinner Menu have two variants based on whether your character is a vampire or not.
- The "Possible Vampire Cave" note can be pickpocketed off Durian before the feast begins
- This location has the Vampire Gauntlets but not the Vampire Gloves
