# Imperial Province of Akavir

| --- | --- |
| Type | Province |
| Continent | [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md) |

The Imperial Province of Akavir was a short-lived province created in [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288) during [Emperor Uriel Septim V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_V)'s invasion of [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(place).md), [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). [Septimia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Septimia), a [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) port town, was the first colony of the province. The settlement of [Ionith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ionith) was the invasion's headquarters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDI-1)</sup>

The province encompassed the lower stage of a large river, along which both of the settlements were located. The surrounding land consisted of rich, well-watered fields. The four [legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) present during the invasion constructed a road between Septimia and Ionith.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDI-1)</sup>

The province was abandoned in [3E 289](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_289) due to extreme weather conditions and Tsaesci attacks. Uriel V died in battle during the retreat. The area was retaken by the natives and has never been revisited by Imperial forces since.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDI-1)</sup>
