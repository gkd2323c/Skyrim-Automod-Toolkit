# Landmarks

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg) Ruins of Bthalft This page lists all the **landmarks** (mountains, clearings, groves, and other geographical features) in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). [Bodies of water](Skyrim_Bodies_of_Water.md) are listed on a separate page.

### [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) General Landmarks

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Altar of Thrond](Skyrim_Altar_of_Thrond.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A landmark west of the Temple of Miraak next to a small cave containing hagravens. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Altar+of+Thrond)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Bard's Leap Summit](Skyrim_Bard%27s_Leap_Summit.md)** — A wooden plank at the very top of Lost Valley Redoubt. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bard%27s+Leap+Summit)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Blackbone Isle](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup> — A haunted island in the Sea of Ghosts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blackbone+Isle+%28CC%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Brodir Grove](Skyrim_Brodir_Grove.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A small circle of stones northeast of Raven Rock containing reavers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Brodir+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Deep Folk Crossing](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md)** — An ancient Dwarven bridge over a weir with structures at either end in the northern part of the Reach. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Deep+Folk+Crossing)
- **[Druadach Mountains](Skyrim_Druadach_Mountains.md)** — A mountain range in western Skyrim serving as a natural barrier to separate Skyrim from High Rock and Hammerfell. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Druadach+Mountains)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Frykte Peak](Skyrim_Frykte_Peak.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — One of the highest points on Solstheim, near Saering's Watch. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Frykte+Peak)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Giant's Tooth](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup> — A small island in the Sea of Ghosts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Giant%27s+Tooth+%28CC%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Gjukar's Monument](Skyrim_Gjukar%27s_Monument.md)** — A stone monument commemorating an ancient battle southeast of Rorikstead. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Gjukar%27s+Monument)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Horker Island](Skyrim_Horker_Island.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A large island east of Skaal Village occupied by horkers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Horker+Island)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Hvitkald Peak‎](Skyrim_Hvitkald_Peak.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — One of the highest points on Solstheim, west of the Abandoned Lodge. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Hvitkald+Peak)
- **[Jerall Mountains](Skyrim_Jerall_Mountains.md)** — A mountain range that stretches along the southern border of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Jerall+Mountains)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Mortrag Peak](Skyrim_Mortrag_Peak.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — One of the highest points on Solstheim, south of Castle Karstaag Ruins. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Mortrag+Peak)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Mount Moesring](Skyrim_Mount_Moesring.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — One of the highest points on Solstheim, south of Moesring Pass. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Mount+Moesring)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Orphan Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan_Rock)** — A wooded valley with a central rock plateau northeast of Helgen containing witches or hags led by a hagraven. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Orphan+Rock)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Reachwind Eyrie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwind_Eyrie)** — A small Dwarven tower east-southeast of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Reachwind+Eyrie)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Ruins of Bthalft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft)** — A small Dwarven ruin south of Ivarstead containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ruins+of+Bthalft)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Ruins of Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund)** — A small Dwarven ruin in the mountains southwest of Riften containing a wispmother and wisps. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ruins+of+Rkund)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md)** — A large mountaintop fort that served as the major Blades temple and outpost in Skyrim, now long abandoned and in Forsworn territory. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sky+Haven+Temple)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Stalhrim Source](Skyrim_Stalhrim_Source.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A small outdoor Nordic ruin east of Broken Tusk Mine and southwest of Mortrag Peak. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Stalhrim+Source)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md)** — The highest mountain in Tamriel, located southeast of Whiterun. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Throat+of+the+World)
- **[Velothi Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Velothi_Mountains)** — A mountain range that stretches along the eastern border of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Velothi+Mountains)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Weynon Stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weynon_Stones)** — A landmark consisting of a circle of stones. It is located between Korvanjund and Fort Dunstad, just south of the path. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Weynon+Stones)

### [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) Clearings

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Autumnshade Clearing](Skyrim_Autumnshade_Clearing.md)** — A circular glade inhabited by a spriggan and its attending animals. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Autumnshade+Clearing)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Clearpine Pond](Skyrim_Clearpine_Pond.md)** — A pond with a small island, located west-southwest of Solitude, just to the northeast of Pinemoon Cave. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Clearpine+Pond)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Clearspring Tarn](Skyrim_Clearspring_Tarn.md)** — A small mountain pool on the cliff overlooking Eastmarch, due east of Snapleg Cave and west of Shor's Stone. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Clearspring+Tarn)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Headwaters of Harstrad](Skyrim_Headwaters_of_Harstrad.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — The source of the Harstrad River. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Headwaters+of+Harstrad)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Mara's Eye Pond](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Eye_Pond.md)** — A small lake due east of Gallows Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mara%27s+Eye+Pond)

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small glade in a cavern east-southeast of Falkreath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ancestor+Glade+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md)** — A grove of trees concealing a small pond fed by a rocky stream, just southwest of Half-Moon Mill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Evergreen+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Giant's Grove](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Grove.md)** — A grove accessed through Fallowstone Cave containing a giant whose tribe has defiled a shrine of Malacath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Giant%27s+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Greenspring Hollow](Skyrim_Greenspring_Hollow.md)** — A shallow outdoor cave west-northwest of Whiterun containing a leveled animal. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Greenspring+Hollow)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Shrouded Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Grove)** — A grove with a small cave located in the Pale. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Shrouded+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Witchmist Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchmist_Grove)** — A small shack north of Bonestrewn Crest and south of Kynesgrove. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Witchmist+Grove)

### [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) Passes

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Brittleshin Pass](Skyrim_Brittleshin_Pass.md)** — A small passage through the mountains between Whiterun Hold and Falkreath Hold containing a necromancer and skeletons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Brittleshin+Pass)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) **[Cold Rock Pass](Skyrim_Cold_Rock_Pass.md)** — A small passage through a mountain south-southwest of Morthal containing a frost troll. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Cold+Rock+Pass)
- **[Dunmeth Pass](Skyrim_Dunmeth_Pass.md)** — A mountain pass and one of the few traversable roads through the Velothi Mountains between northwest Morrowind and northeast Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dunmeth+Pass)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) **[Moesring Pass](Skyrim_Moesring_Pass.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A mountain pass due northwest of the Temple of Miraak, inhabited by rieklings guarding the nearby Strange Vessel. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Moesring+Pass)
- **[Skybound Watch Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skybound_Watch_Pass)** — A small Nordic tower and ruin northeast of Helgen containing bandits and a giant frostbite spider. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Skybound+Watch+Pass)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) **[Wayward Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wayward_Pass)** — A pass through the mountains in the southwest of Winterhold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Wayward+Pass)
