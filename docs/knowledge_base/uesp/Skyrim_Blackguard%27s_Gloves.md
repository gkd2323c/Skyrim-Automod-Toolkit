[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Gloves.jpg) Blackguard's Gloves The Blackguard's Gloves are the gauntlets of a set of armor that also includes a [chestpiece](Skyrim_Blackguard%27s_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Blackguard%27s_Boots.md) and a [hood](Skyrim_Blackguard%27s_Hood.md). They can be found along with the rest of the set in the basement of [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md)'s [house](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md), however Mallory will only give you the key to his basement after you complete [Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full). The gloves are also only available to [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) members, as you must complete [Meet the Family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Meet_the_Family) in order to receive Paid in Full. In appearance, they are identical to the unobtainable [Thieves Guild Variant Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Gloves), though these gloves provide three more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md). Their enchantment functionally serves as an upgrade to the base [Thieves Guild Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Gloves), [increasing your lockpicking skill](Skyrim_Fortify_Lockpicking.md) by 40 points, a bonus five points greater than that of the [Guild Master's Gloves](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.md) (but with 3 fewer points of protection) without having to complete the entire Thieves Guild questline to obtain them. They can be tempered with a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full)**: Recover the [bonemold formula](Skyrim_Bonemold_Formula.md) for [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md).

## Bugs
- All the Blackguard's Armor items will respawn when Glover Mallory's basement resets, allowing multiple copies of the set to be obtained. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The Blackguard's Armor pieces don't have an Armor Material keyword so the full set doesn't count toward the *Matching Set* perk. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
- You can get the gloves without joining the Thieves Guild by taking a wooden plate, holding it in front of you, and running at door to glitch through. You'll need another plate to get out of the room if you wish to attempt this. It may take a few tries to work.
