## Jackal
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/ON-creature-Jackal.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Jackal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jackal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jackal.jpg) A Jackal Jackals are wild [dogs](Lore_Dog.md) usually found in arid climates. They are also known as the "thieves' dog". They are quick to sniff out sources of food, and clever enough to figure out how to get at them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Jackal-1)</sup> The Hallin stand jackal has become a popular pet in Evermore. Some [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) have successfully bred local jackals into mounts. Jackals are known to travel in small packs. Their meat is gamey.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mf S-2)</sup> Smugglers often sell their pelts and skulls to buyers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BAL-3)</sup> The [Order of the Lamp](Lore_Order_of_the_Lamp.md) uses large jackals as mounts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

- See also: [Coyote](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Coyote) and [Wolf](Lore_Wolf.md)

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Jackal), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Slinking Jackal)

## [Jaguar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jaguar)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Jaguar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Jaguar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Jaguar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Carnaval_Jaguar.jpg) Carnaval Jaguar [Jaguars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jaguar) are a rare type of large, predatory [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in jungled regions in southern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are known for their distinctive rosette fur patterns.

Many of the cats known as jaguars in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Jaguar**, differentiated by their larger size. The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.

Many [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have jaguar-like fur patterns, with Khajiit of the [Cathay-raht](Lore_Cathay-raht.md) furstock being nicknamed as "jaguar-men" by the [Imperials](Lore_Imperials.md), though many do not actually resemble jaguars. The ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of Naj-Caldeesh were said to learned how to hunt from "jaguar-kin", leading to speculation that they must have hunted alongside a Khajiit furstock.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Carnaval_Jaguar)

## Jellyfish
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-creature-Jellyfish.png/200px-ON-creature-Jellyfish.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jellyfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jellyfish.png) Jellyfish Jellyfish are a type of aquatic invertebrate that are sometimes kept as decorative pets.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> [Swamp jellies](Lore_Swamp_Jelly.md) are said to have a disquieting resemblance to them.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONJelly-7)</sup>

A mysterious and immortal entity called the [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md), who was known to lie when not asked a direct question, claimed that the water brought in by the tide is filled with brain-billies, tiny [spirit](Lore_Spirit.md) -jellies that sting and disrupt people's memories.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AOTO-8)</sup>

## Jerboa
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/ON-creature-Jerboa.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Jerboa.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jerboa.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Jerboa.jpg) A Jerboa Jerboas are small hopping rodents with long ears and legs native to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). They are compared to both [mice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mouse) and [bunnies](Lore_Rabbit.md) They breed quickly, and their meat is said to taste sweet.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AAIAT-9)</sup>

## Jill
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-creature-The_Dragon.jpg/200px-ON-creature-The_Dragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Dragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_Dragon.jpg) A Luminary in the form of a female Dragon Jills are mythical beings, said to fix the world during [Dragon Breaks](Lore_Dragon_Break.md).<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> For this reason, they are known as "minute-menders".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Nu Hat-11)</sup> Some sources describe them as female [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), servants of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Nu Hat-11)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-NMI-12)</sup> Female Dragons are also mentioned in the historical-fiction, [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-KE-13)</sup> [The Dragon](Lore_The_Dragon.md), a [Luminary of Magic](Lore_Luminary.md), took the form of a female Dragon.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Lum D-14)</sup> Legends mention several varieties of Jill, such as Aether-Jills, Earth-Jills, Magne-Jills, Tear-Jills, Thunder-Scaled Jills, and Void-Jills.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-FHMC-15)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:A_Child%27s_Tamriel_Bestiary_(French_translation))

## Joughat
The joughat is an animal that is known to have been kept as a household pet by nobility. Circa [3E 110](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_110), one was owned by the young [Uriel Septim III](Lore_Uriel_Septim_III.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TWQV4-16)</sup>
