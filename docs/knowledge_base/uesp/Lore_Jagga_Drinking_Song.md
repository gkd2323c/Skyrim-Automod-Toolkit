Jagga Drinking Song *A Bosmer song about how great Jagga is* *
---
One thing a [Wood Elf](Lore_Wood_Elf.md) would never pass up
A fresh pint of [jagga](Lore_Jagga.md) poured straight in their cup.
Sweeter than [bloodfroth](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md#Bloodfroth) and smoother than [honey](Lore_Honey.md)
A full mug of jagga makes everything sunny.

And what makes this drink so wanted by all?
The stench of [pig](Lore_Pig.md)'s milk, right from the stall.
Boil it and keep the [cats](Lore_Cat.md) away from the pan
Wait till the curds form and share with the clan.

Wood Elves and jagga go well from the start
The creamy sweet brew is good for the heart.
[You-know-what whiskey](Lore_Bosmer_Cuisine.md#You-know-what_Whiskey) does not compare
To the ripe taste of jagga, or so we declare!

Forget about [wine](Lore_Wine.md) and [beer](Lore_Beer.md) from hops
The [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) gives plenty of drops.
Of ale made from mold and Arenthian brandy
Compared to them all, jagga's just dandy.

One thing a Wood Elf would never pass up
A fresh pint of jagga poured straight in their cup.
Sweeter than bloodfroth and smoother than honey
A full mug of jagga makes everything sunny!
