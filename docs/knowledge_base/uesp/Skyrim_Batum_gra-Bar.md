[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-npc-Batum_gra-Bar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Batum_gra-Bar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Batum_gra-Bar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Batum_gra-Bar.jpg) Batum gra-Bar **Batum gra-Bar** is a dead [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), and one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md). When you arrive, she is dead at the first campfire, presumably from wounds sustained fighting Sinding. She only spawns during the [related quest](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md).

She wears [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), a [hide helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md) and [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). She wields a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) and a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md), all of which may be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality at level 22. She also carries at least 25 gold.
