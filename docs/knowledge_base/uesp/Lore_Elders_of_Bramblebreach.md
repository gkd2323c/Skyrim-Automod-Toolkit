Elders of Bramblebreach *Poem on the Bramblebreach Clan's ancestor spirits*
---
The [spinners](Lore_Spinners.md) tell of spirits
to whom this [land](Lore_Wilderking_Court.md) belongs
and who belong to this land
if they belong to anything.

They are our ancestors
and our governors
our conscience when conscience fails;
wisdom for foolish [Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md).

We may pretend to live apart from them
but it is only a child's tale, made of childish dreams.
