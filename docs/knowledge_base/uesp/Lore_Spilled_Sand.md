[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/ON-place-Spilling_Sands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Spilling_Sands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Spilling_Sands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Spilling_Sands.jpg) The Spilled Sand The Spilled Sand is an obscure realm speculated to be the realm of [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md) that exists beyond even the [Sands Behind the Stars](Lore_Sands_Behind_the_Stars.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESONuziwa-1)</sup> It is not a physical place, but a story which is simultaneously part of the tapestry of time while also outside of it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOJD-2)</sup> Two [moons](Lore_Moons.md) are visible from the realm, indicating that it may reside in the [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). The realm consists of an endless looping expanse of sands; a giant golden [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md), either deep in slumber or possibly dead, can be seen partially buried in the sand. In the center of the realm lies a golden oasis with Alkosh's Hourglass,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAH-3)</sup> the symbol of the Dragon God of Time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The Spilled Sand was accessed by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in the [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) in pursuit of the [Mask of Alkosh](Lore_Mask_of_Alkosh.md). The dragon [Nahfahlaar](Lore_Nahfahlaar.md) described the realm as a myth made manifest that was simply a "trick of the mask".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESONah-5)</sup> The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) hero [Ja'darri](Lore_Ja%27darri.md) described it as not a place, but rather a story being told again and again, though when pressed would not say outright if it was the realm of Alkosh.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOJD-2)</sup> Under the guidance of Ja'darri's spirit, the Vestige and Nahfahlaar performed a ritual through Alkosh that imbued the mask with the power to take on the dragon [Laatvulon](Lore_Laatvulon.md), and later [Kaalgrontiid](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Dragonguard-6)</sup>
