# Dwarven Sentry

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/ON-creature-Dwarven_Sentry.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Dwarven_Sentry.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Dwarven_Sentry.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Dwarven_Sentry.jpg) A Dwarven Sentry **Dwarven Sentries** (also referred to as **Dwarven Theodolites**) are spherical [animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) found in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md). Sentries patrol ruins in a similar fashion to [Dwarven spheres](Lore_Dwarven_Sphere.md), extending their necks to emit an electrical shock designed to incapacitate intruders. They were primarily used as an automated measuring device designed to explore and survey subterranean areas.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDwarven Theodolite-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONCarnelian Theodolite-2)</sup> They can also generate a barrier to protect nearby animunculi.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Sentries have been observed to lock themselves into mechanical frames to become fixed long-range attackers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Although they are robotic in nature, they have been observed to show intelligent movement, as if reacting with emotions.<sup>[[*citation needed*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Template:Fact)]</sup>
