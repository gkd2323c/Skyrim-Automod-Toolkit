# Alik'r Coterie

The Alik'r Coterie,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVPrima-2)</sup><sup>:442</sup> or simply the **Alik'r**, are a faction of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) mercenaries active in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), seemingly from the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVPrima-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SR-3)</sup> The Coterie is said by its warriors to be sponsored by noble houses of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) as agents against the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRKematu-4)</sup> but others claim that the Coterie is simply a mercenary front working on behalf of the Dominion itself.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRSaadia-5)</sup> The exact truth of this is unknown.

Warriors of the Coterie are known for their curved swords,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Guard Dia-6)</sup> called scimitars.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Scim-7)</sup>

## Notes
- The "Alik'r Coterie" faction was originally called the "Sicarius", according to Skyrim's Dialogue Views files (specifically 0007037A.xml) which released with the *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)* [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Creation Kit Files-8)</sup> They seemed to have originally been named for the [Sicarius](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sicarius) from real world history.
