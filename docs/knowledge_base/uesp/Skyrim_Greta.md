[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-npc-Greta.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Greta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Greta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Greta.jpg) Greta **Greta** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). She is the sister of the recently executed [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md), as well as the wife of [Addvar](Skyrim_Addvar.md), and mother of [Svari](Skyrim_Svari.md). If Addvar dies, she takes over running his fish stall in Solitude's marketplace. She is a devout worshipper of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), and will [ask you](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Return_to_Grace) to retrieve Roggvir's [amulet of Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md).

Greta wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) along with a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Addvar%27s_House) to [Addvar's house](Skyrim_Addvar%27s_House.md) along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[Return to Grace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Return_to_Grace)**: Convince a mourning mother to go to the [local temple](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md).

## Dialogue
When she is at Roggvir's execution:

*"That's my brother up there."* *"Roggvir... Why did it have to be like this?"* *"It shouldn't have been like this."* *"Roggvir. You damn fool."* Her normal greetings:

*"I'm not looking for conversation."* *"If you want to talk about my brother, I'm not interested."* *"I'm in mourning. Can't you see that?"* *"Are you here to talk about my brother? Do you think his execution was justified?"* When spoken to:

**Who was your brother?** *"Roggvir. He opened a gate and they executed him for it. He opened a gate and the wrong man rode out of it. If that man hadn't been [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). If Ulfric hadn't killed [High King Torygg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torygg)... But it was Ulfric. And he did kill the High King, in honorable combat. My brother refused to allow the the * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *[Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) to take revenge for the deed. So now he's dead."* **Sounds like your brother was a traitor / Yes. I think your brother was a traitor.** *"Then you didn't know Roggvir, and you have no idea what it means to be a Nord of Skyrim! He did the honorable thing, even when he knew it would cost him everything. My brother was the best man in Solitude."* **No. He did what he thought was right.** *"Then like Roggvir, you're a Nord at heart. It's hard for many of the people here to understand. I think [Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md) understands. I don't think I could have stayed here if anyone else had..."* She'll end by saying:

*"I... I think I need to stop talking now..."* Her goodbyes:

*"I should make sure Svari is okay."* *"Addvar won't sell anything if I don't go and check up on him."*
## Quest-Related Events
Svari is worried about her mother, and will ask you to talk to Greta:

**Hi. Svari asked me to talk to you.** *"She did, did she? She's going to get a sit down later."* **She wants you to return to Temple.** *"Well. I wish it were that simple. Maybe it is. Look, I want to go back but I won't feel right in there without a tie to Talos. My brother Roggvir wore a symbol of Talos. He kept it hidden on his person. If I had that... I can't go near his body. Would you get it back for me?"* **I'll do what I can.** *"Thanks."* **Actually *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* I've got it here.** *"Why did you... nevermind, it's a sign. I'll return to temple. Here's something for your help."* If you bring the amulet to her later:

**I've got the Amulet *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*** *"Thanks so much. I'll return to temple. Here's something for your time and effort."* Either way she will reward you with some leveled gold.

Once Greta starts visiting the Temple again, she can be found chanting in prayer:

*"Let us walk in the steps of the Divines."* *"Remind us of our place, when we forget."* *"May knowledge of your Divinity carry us through our daily trials."* She shares this dialogue with [Silana Petreia](Skyrim_Silana_Petreia.md), [Rorlund](Skyrim_Rorlund.md), and [Freir](Skyrim_Freir.md).
