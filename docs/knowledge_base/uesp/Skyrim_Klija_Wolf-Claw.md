[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw.jpg) Klija Wolf-Claw [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Klija_Wolf-Claw_02.jpg) Klija at Kynesgrove **Klija Wolf-Claw** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior from [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOC-1)</sup> who wishes to fight for the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) as their champion.

If you begin a new character with the [Civil War Champions](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed (or if your character has not yet joined the Stormcloaks or the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) when the Creation is loaded), Klija can be found at the [Steamscorch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine) camp at [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md). You will be directed to her as part of the related quest if you choose to side with the Stormcloaks, as an alternative to gathering five [snow bear pelts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear_Pelt). She carries a unique item called [Klija's Snow Bear Pelts](Skyrim_Klija%27s_Snow_Bear_Pelts.md) which can be stolen from her and presented to [Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced) to become the Stormcloak champion. The pelts can also be taken from her corpse if you so choose, although you will get a bounty for murdering her in cold blood.

If you decide to side with the Imperial Legion, Klija will appear at the battle location on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) alongside several [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md). She will act as the Stormcloak champion and you will need to kill her and loot everything from her corpse in order to complete the quest. You can speak to her briefly before the battle commences.

At Kynesgrove, Klija will be wearing [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and carrying [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), an [iron battleaxe](Skyrim_Iron_Battleaxe.md), a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [silver garnet ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Garnet_Ring), a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), a bottle of [Alto Wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), the snow bear pelts, and some gold. At the battle location, she will instead be wearing [Storm-Bear armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Boots), [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Gauntlets), and [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Helmet). She wields [Tsun's Judgement](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tsun%27s_Judgement) in combat and also carries the [Storm-Bear sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Sword) and [shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Shield).

## Notes
- Her Ref ID in the battlegrounds is FE [xxx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md#Creation_Club) 95E with a base ID of FE [xxx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md#Creation_Club) 95D.
- If you install Civil War Champions on a character who has already chosen a side in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), Klija will not appear at Kynesgrove.
- If you steal Klija's pelts but then side with the Legion anyway, you will instead face a generic [Stormcloak Champion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Champion) in battle and Klija will remain at Kynesgrove.

## Bugs
- If Klija appears at Kynesgrove and you side with the Legion, two versions of her will exist at once. Killing her at the battle site will not disable the version at Kynesgrove.
