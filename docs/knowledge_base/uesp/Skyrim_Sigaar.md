[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Sigaar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sigaar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sigaar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sigaar.jpg) Sigaar **Sigaar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), and the [carriage driver](Skyrim_Carriage.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). For a fee, he can transport you to any of the other [major cities](Skyrim_Cities.md) in Skyrim.

Sigaar wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1da) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). He is equipped with an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe).
