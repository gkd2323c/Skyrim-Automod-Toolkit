[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/AR-place-North_Keep.jpg/200px-AR-place-North_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-North_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-North_Keep.jpg) North Keep North Keep was a village located on the road between [Granitehall](Lore_Granitehall.md) to the north and [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of North Keep was an active settlement, being ruled by Lord Alan. It had a rivalry with [Neugrad Watch](Lore_Neugrad_Watch.md) and [Black Moor](Lore_Black_Moor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> It was neighbored by [Oakwood](Lore_Oakwood.md) to the east.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The town of [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md) shares a similar name to the town, with its aliases of the "Gateway to the North" and "Helgen Keep". It is unclear if they are intended to be the same town. According to the unofficial text, [Skyrim In the New Era](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Skyrim_In_the_New_Era), Helgen has gone by several names.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-SINE-3)</sup>
