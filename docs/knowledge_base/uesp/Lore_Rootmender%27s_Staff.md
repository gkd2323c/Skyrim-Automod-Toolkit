# Rootmender's Staff

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/ON-item-weapon-Rootmender%27s_Staff.jpg/200px-ON-item-weapon-Rootmender%27s_Staff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-weapon-Rootmender%27s_Staff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-weapon-Rootmender%27s_Staff.jpg) The Rootmender's Staff The Rootmender's Staff, formerly known as the Sap-Speaker's Staff, was a [staff](Lore_Staff.md) artifact entrusted to [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) during their expedition in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) by [Sap-Speaker](Lore_Sap-Speakers.md) Kuzei of the former [Root-Whisper Tribe](Lore_Root-Whisper_Tribe.md). It was materialized out of a lifelike vision of the past into reality. It was quickly snatched by the traitorous Kassandra with the intent of using it to locate another artifact known as the [Remnant of Argon](Lore_Remnant_of_Argon.md), which contained the souls of the former [Root Whisper Tribe](Lore_Root-Whisper_Tribe.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Da DESO-1)</sup>

With the Rootmender's Staff, Kassandra attempted to delay the Vestige by concentrating powerful [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) energy beams towards them, but that was not enough to fend them off, so they fled deeper into the [xanmeer](Lore_Xanmeer.md). She found the Remnant of Argon further in the Vakka-Bok xanmeer, creating a [Miregaunt](Lore_Miregaunt.md) around herself. She took control of it and used it against the Vestige, who had to put her down.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ro AESO-2)</sup> After Celebrations of their victory over the [Blackguards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blackguards), both artifacts retrieved, reformation of the Root-Whisper Tribe, the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of Murkmire granted the Vestige the Rootmender's staff along with the title of Rootmender.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BRa RESO-3)</sup>
