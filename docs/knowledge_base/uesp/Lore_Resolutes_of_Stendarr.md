# Resolutes of Stendarr

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/ON-npc-Resolute_Overseer.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Resolute_Overseer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Resolute_Overseer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Resolute_Overseer.jpg) A member of the Resolutes of Stendarr The Resolutes of Stendarr were a group of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) devotees who were sworn to help the sick and protect the weak. The Resolutes consisted of holy warriors and individuals who were talented in healing magicks. Their healers were encouraged by their leaders to use those magicks unsparingly. The Resolutes of Stendarr wore silver pins to identify themselves as members of the organization.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The Resolutes sought to hunt what they call the Four Abominations: [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), [manbeasts](Lore_Lycanthropy.md), [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) and [risen corpses](Lore_Necromancy.md). The Resolutes held that these abominations are unnatural, and sought to drive them from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in Stendarr's name.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFA-2)</sup> The Four Abominations abhor the light; thus the Resolutes believed that Stendarr gifted those who invoke his name with the ability to clothe themselves in an aura of blessed light. The Resolutes held that Stendarr's faithful have adapted the gift of light to smite their enemies, as well as defend and heal their allies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AOR-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MC-4)</sup>

It is believed that the magic devised by the Resolutes of Stendarr was used by the [Templars](Lore_Templars.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-5)</sup> Certain texts, authored by members of the Resolutes of Stendarr,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AOR-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Templar Book2-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Templar Book3-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Templar Book5-8)</sup> were linked to the Templars.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Skill Books-9)</sup>

## Notes
- The Resolutes of Stendarr are quite similar to the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) organization known as the [Vigil of Stendarr](Lore_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md).
