## Almalexia's Grace
**Almalexia's Grace** is a variation of a [Dispel](Lore_Dispel.md) associated with [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), a spell that removes magic effects from the caster.

**Found in:** - [Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Almalexia%27s_Grace)

**Spell schools:** [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md)
## Almalexia's Wrath
**Almalexia's Wrath** is a spell associated with [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), that simultaneously absorbs target's health, inflicts elemental fire damage and increases susceptibility of the target to fire damage. The only known user of this spell was Almalexia.

**Found in:** - [Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Almalexia%27s_Wrath)

## Almsivi Intervention
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-effect-Almsivi_Intervention.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-effect-Almsivi_Intervention.jpg) Almsivi Intervention (Morrowind) **Almsivi Intervention** is a [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md) spell that allowed its user to [teleport](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) to the nearest [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWAlmsivi-1)</sup> It is named after the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) gods themselves, who go by the acronym: ALMSIVI.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-36LOVS13-2)</sup> Temple pilgrims that sought out the Shrines of Seven Graces on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) were advised by the temple to carry a scroll of Almsivi Intervention while they were in the Puzzle Canal underneath [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)'s palace.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup> What became of this spell after the Temple's dissolution in the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) is unknown.

A similar effect is seen with Mother Stones, dark crystals created by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) that, once attuned, could take their user back to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), regardless of their circumstance. It is powerful enough to transport someone from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSelveni-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONMother-5)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Almsivi_Intervention)

**Spell schools:** [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md)

## Ash Shell
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-spell-Ash_Rune.jpg/200px-SR-spell-Ash_Rune.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Ash_Rune.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Ash_Rune.jpg) Ash rune [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-spell-Ash_Shell.jpg/200px-SR-spell-Ash_Shell.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Ash_Shell.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-spell-Ash_Shell.jpg) Target encased in hardened ash **Ash Shell** is an adept level Alteration spell that causes a target to be encased in hardened ash.

## Azra's Sustenance
**Azra's Sustenance** is a spell associated with [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md), it restores the caster's health every second.

**Found in:** - [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Azra%27s_Sustenance)

## Azra's Wrath
**Azra's Wrath** is a spell associated with [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md), it damages all enemies within a radius around caster.

**Found in:** - [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Azra%27s_Wrath)
