# Rontha

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/ON-npc-Warchief_Ornskar.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Warchief_Ornskar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Warchief_Ornskar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Warchief_Ornskar.jpg) Warchief Ornskar [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/ON-npc-Nede_Warrior.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Nede_Warrior.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nede_Warrior.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nede_Warrior.jpg) One of the Nedic warriors who partook in the invasion of Stonefalls Rontha were an ancient tribe of [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people. The Rontha were nomadic and particularly warlike. They were the largest of the Nedic tribes who invaded the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Nedic Wineskin-1)</sup> in the early years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SV-2)</sup> They were known for branding both people and items captured in battle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Nedic Wineskin-1)</sup>

According to some sources, these Nedic invaders of Morrowind were ancestors of contemporary [Nords](Lore_Nord.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SV-2)</sup>

## History
At some point in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FC-3)</sup> and early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SV-2)</sup> Nedic tribes had a presence in Stonefalls. The tribe of Rontha was the largest of the tribes who partook in the invasion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Nedic Wineskin-1)</sup> The Nedes continued their conquest over Stonefalls, controlling most of the modern-day region within the span of a few short years, executing many of the population. They were led by Warchief (also known as General)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SQI-4)</sup> Ornskar.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WO-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SN-6)</sup> Leading the battle against the Nedes were the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) brothers, [General Balreth](Lore_Balreth.md) and [Legate Sadal](Lore_Sadal.md), considered the greatest Chimer warriors of their time. At first, the brothers and their Chimer armies attempted to push back the Nedes, but as they were slowly overwhelmed, their conflicts focused on defense, until they were left with remnants of their armies in a ruined Daedric temple of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) hidden in a valley.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SV-2)</sup> In a last-ditch effort, Balreth and Sadal underwent a ritual to turn into what is now known as the, [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md), powerful entities that turned the ash red with the blood of both the Nedes and the Chimeri. Hearts of the invading Nedes, including Warchief Ornskar, were used to power the ritual.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ST-7)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SQI-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOBrothers-8)</sup> Eventually, both brothers were sealed into [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md) and the [Tormented Spire](Lore_Tormented_Spire.md) respectively, by a group of Chimer mages from Vvardenfell led by [Mavos Siloreth](Lore_Mavos_Siloreth.md). The Brothers of Strife have been the source of legend and myth throughout Stonefalls ever since.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TBOS-9)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOBrothers-8)</sup>
