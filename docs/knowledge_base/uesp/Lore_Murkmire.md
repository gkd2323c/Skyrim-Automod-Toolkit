[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/ON-place-Murkmire.jpg/200px-ON-place-Murkmire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murkmire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murkmire.jpg) Murkmire ca. 2E 582 Murkmire is a coastal region in southern [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) with access to [Oliis Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oliis_Bay). The port city of [Lilmoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lilmoth) is located there. Murkmire is inhabited by both [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Naga](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Naga) tribes.

The Argonian tribes of Murkmire were claimed to be one of the three regions of Black Marsh that joined the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) at its founding in [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572) by [Heita-Meen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heita-Meen)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> In truth, however, the remarks were more aspirational than descriptive, and though Pact envoys in Murkmire worked to add the region to the alliance, it remained autonomous as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Murkmire's lack of a central authority likewise complicated the matter.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

In [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48) the floating city of [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) passed over the region after being summoned by the [An-Xileel](Lore_An-Xileel.md) of Lilmoth, killing all inhabitants below and resurrecting them as undead soldiers under Umbriel's command.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIC-3)</sup>

Murkmire is home to the [Keel-Sakka River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Keel-Sakka_River).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Datamined information relating to the planned [Murkmire](Online_Murkmire_(DLC).md) zone for *[Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)* and in-universe developer statements originally placed the region of Murkmire in western Black Marsh, adjacent to [Shadowfen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shadowfen) and encompassing the city of [Gideon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gideon).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The zone was delayed and redesigned, resulting in Murkmire being moved to southern Black Marsh and [Lilmoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lilmoth) replacing Gideon as the region's main settlement.
