## [Echatere](Lore_Echatere.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-creature-Echatere.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Echatere.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Echatere.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Echatere.jpg) An echatere [Echateres](Lore_Echatere.md) are large herbivorous mammals with tusks, pointed hooves, and large antlers. Found mainly in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), they serve the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) as livestock and beasts of burden. They provide meat, fur, vellum, and milk for the Orcs. Echateres are stubborn and ornery.

## Elephant
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/ON-crown_store-Na-Totambu_War_Elephant.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Na-Totambu_War_Elephant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Na-Totambu_War_Elephant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Na-Totambu_War_Elephant.jpg) A Na-Totambu War Elephant Elephants are a type of large [mammal](Lore_Mammal.md) known for toughness. Elephants possess four prominent tusks and a highly versatile prehensile trunk. Elephants use their trunks to produce iconic trumpeting calls and to spray cooling water across their backs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NTWE-2)</sup> [Mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) are a type of woolly elephant.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Pocket elephants are a diminutive form whose smaller size and gentle temperament make them ideal family pets.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NTPE-4)</sup> Pocket mammoths also exist, having originally been bred small in order to make them more manageable as livestock.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The Na-Totambu (the ruling class of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md)) trained and armored elephants for use as warbeasts, a tradition which was continued by their [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) descendants. The [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) use locally-sourced [metals](Lore_Metals.md) to produce protective armor for these massive war mounts, and paint their skin with glowing battle sigils.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NTWE-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NTPE-4)</sup> Yokudan [war smiths](Lore_Smithing.md) also once made use of pocket elephants to test armor designs for their larger cousins. Pet pocket elephants are still painted with the same battle sigils; an echo of this legacy.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NTPE-4)</sup>

Elephants are the namesake of the Cuirass of the Elephant, an enchanted piece of fur armor found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Curaiss-6)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Na-Totambu_War_Elephant)

## Elk
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-creature-Elk.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Elk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Elk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Elk.jpg) An elk Elk are a species of large, ungulate mammals found in parts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). Like other [deer](Lore_Deer.md), they are very skittish and will quickly flee from potential danger. The elks of [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) Skyrim are known for their distinctly large antlers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Certain [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) clans worship sacred elk [spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GSOTR-8)</sup> Sacred elk leather was used to craft armor pieces considered true Reach masterpieces.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-RWJ-9)</sup> The [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) is often depicted with the head or skull of an elk, which [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) myths say belonged to [Y'ffer](Lore_Y%27ffer.md)'s champion, the [Graht-Elk](Lore_Graht-Elk.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TWS-10)</sup>

Elks are often tamed as mounts by certain cultures. It is said the [Elves](Lore_Elf.md) tamed Elks so far back in their distant past that they themselves have only a dim understanding of when they became companion animals to their people.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOMT1-11)</sup> The majority of Elk mounts are reared by the [druids](Lore_Druids.md) of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The druids the favor raising elk as mounts since the animals remain calm around the magic used in their rituals and spellcasting. [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) rear elk and deer as mounts due to need and availability.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOMT8-12)</sup>

- See also: [Moose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moose) and [Reindeer](Lore_Reindeer.md)

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Elk.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Elk), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Wilds_Incarnate), Betrayal of the Second Era

## [Elytra](Lore_Elytra.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SI-creature-Elytra_%28Mania%29.jpg/200px-SI-creature-Elytra_%28Mania%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-creature-Elytra_(Mania).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-creature-Elytra_(Mania).jpg) An elytra native to Mania **Elytra** are large [mantis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mantis) -like arthropods indigenous to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Like many of the native creatures of the realm, there are color differences between the Elytra of [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) and [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), but they are otherwise identical. They are found in high concentrations in [root tunnel systems](Lore_Root.md), but inhabit areas throughout the entire Isles. They were also found within [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md)'s sewers, where a root tunnel system manifested as a result of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s influence, and a [madman's](Lore_Thoron.md) experiments with the [Sword of Jyggalag](Lore_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md). Some Elytra were smuggled out into the rest of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in hopes that they could be sold for a significant profit. Some Elytra secrete a glowing green substance known as [Felldew](Lore_Felldew.md), prized as a powerful, addictive drug.

**Found in:** - [Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Elytra), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Elytra_Nymph.md)<sup>([CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md))</sup>, [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Elytra)

## [Emperor Crab](Lore_Emperor_Crab.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png/200px-SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png) An Emperor Crab [Emperor Crabs](Lore_Emperor_Crab.md) are gigantic semi-aquatic [crabs](Lore_Crab.md) who were once native to the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), contemporary with the early settled [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md). The species died out long before [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), with [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) claiming to be the ones who hunted them to extinction. They are described as being as large as cathedrals and some naturalists believe they are related to the [mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md). One source claims that they devolved into the modern mudcrab after abandoning divine pursuits in favor of mundane satisfactions.

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Manor_District), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.md)<sup>([CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md))</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Skar)
