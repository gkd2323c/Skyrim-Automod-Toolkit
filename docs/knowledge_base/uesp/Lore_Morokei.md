Morokei, also known as **the Deathless**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> was a [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) who inhabited [Bromjunaar](Lore_Bromjunaar.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), when the city served as the capital of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md). Like many of his fellow priests, he managed to achieve a state of [lichdom](Lore_Lich.md) through the eternal worship of his [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) servants.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRValdar-3)</sup> By virtue of his high rank, he was gifted a magical [moonstone mask](Lore_Dragon_Priest_Masks.md#Morokei) by his [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) overlord.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRLoad-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The power given to him by the dragons was such that even in undeath, he could not be killed by anything other than the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) of a dragon.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TDo M-6)</sup> As a result, he continued to dwell within the crypts of Bromjunaar's ruins, later known as [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> until his destruction circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRQuest-8)</sup> As a Dragon Priest, he was a powerful mage, also able to wield the Thu'um in battle.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TGATT-9)</sup>

## Biography
In life, Morokei was a Nord who dwelt in Bromjunaar, the seat of power for the Dragon Cult who ruled over [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the Merethic. During the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), the ancient Nords rebelled against the dragons and defeated the Dragon Cult. Morokei and his fellow priests were subsequently entombed, but many refused to remain dead. After Morokei's defeat, the full extent of the power gifted to him by the dragons became clear when it was discovered that he could only be killed by a dragon. As a result, four sacred fires were lit at the ruins of Labyrinthian by priests of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md) to magically seal Morokei within the ruins. It was feared that, should he escape his magical bindings, he would use his terrible power to seek vengeance upon the people of Skyrim.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TDo M-6)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) named Dixulti the Moldering entered Labyrinthian with the goal of unsealing Morokei's tomb in order to steal his power. To do this, he killed Keldnyr, an elderly priest of Kyne who had come to the ruins to tend to the sacred fires keeping Morokei bound. This caused Keldnyr's granddaughter, Yrsild, to come to the ruins in search of him. Although she was a priestess of Kyne, Yrsild was unaware of her grandfather's sacred duty, believing his regular visits to Labyrinthian were academic in nature. With the aid of [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Yrsild discovered the danger posed by Morokei and succeeded in relighting the sacred fires. The Vestige confronted and killed Dixulti, and Yrsild took up the mantle of ensuring Morokei would never escape his tomb.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

At some point in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), Morokei awakened from his ancient slumber<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRLoad-4)</sup> and somehow obtained the [Staff of Magnus](Lore_Staff_of_Magnus.md). As a result, a group of mages from the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) undertook an expedition to Labyrinthian in search of the Staff. The party encountered Morokei within the ruins, and the future Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Lore_Savos_Aren.md) was the only member of the group to survive the encounter. Aren sacrificed two of his companions to eternally contain Morokei, and sealed the ruins.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SRSavos Aren-11)</sup> Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), following the activation of the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) was sent to Labyrinthian by the College to retrieve the Staff.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRQuest-8)</sup> The Dragonborn confronted and destroyed Morokei (a feat seemingly only made possible by possessing the [soul](Lore_Dragon_Souls.md) of a dragon)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TDo M-6)</sup> and claimed the Staff as well as Morokei's mask.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRQuest-8)</sup>
