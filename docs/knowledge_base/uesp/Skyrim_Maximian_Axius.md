[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-creature-Maximian_Axius.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Maximian_Axius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Maximian_Axius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Maximian_Axius.jpg) Maximian Axius' remains **Maximian Axius** was a soldier serving under [General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md) at [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md) just before the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) erupted. Trapped in the fort by debris, his skeleton can be found with his knapsack and [a](Skyrim_A_Letter_to_Selina_I.md) [series](Skyrim_A_Letter_to_Selina_II.md) [of](Skyrim_A_Letter_to_Selina_III.md) [letters](Skyrim_A_Letter_to_Selina_IV.md) to his wife, Selina. He had two children, Siricus and Atia.
