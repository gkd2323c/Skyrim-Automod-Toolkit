Sunk into Ouze *A solution to dealing with the defeated Oathbreakers of the Green Pact*
---
… never rise again. But what to do with them? Obliterating their essence and burning their bones was too much. They are still members of the chosen race, still our brothers and sisters. No, they must be contained, but not punished.

We will sink them into [Ouze](Lore_Ouze.md). The ground here is soft and warm and will make a good resting place for their bones. Their spirits will slumber evermore, or until the [spinners](Lore_Spinners.md) tell the story of their release. If they resist, we shall ….
