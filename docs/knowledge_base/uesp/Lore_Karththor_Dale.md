## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Karththor Dale was an active settlement. It was ruled by Princess Demiah and had a rivalry with [Stoneforest](Lore_Stoneforest.md) and [Silgrad Tower](Lore_Silgrad_Tower.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Karththor Dale was neighbored by several settlements, including connections to [Verarchen Hall](Lore_Verarchen_Hall.md) to the northwest, [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls_(settlement).md) to the east, and [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>

## Notes
