Note from Agna **by [Agna](Skyrim_Agna.md)** *A note from Agna to Golldir about taking on Vals Veran*
---
[Golldir](Skyrim_Golldir.md), don't be such a milk drinker. I know you've been scared of [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md) since your brother *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* locked you in there when you were children *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*, but we can't let [Vals Veran](Skyrim_Vals_Veran.md) get away with this!

Hillgrund and our other ancestors need us to protect their remains, and I'm going in, with or without you. I'm sure one [dark elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) is nothing to worry about, but if something should happen, don't forget to check the secret passage in case he barricaded himself in the deeper chambers.

-Aunt Agna
