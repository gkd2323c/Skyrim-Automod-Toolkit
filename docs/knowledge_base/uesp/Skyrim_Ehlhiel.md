[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-npc-Ehlhiel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ehlhiel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ehlhiel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ehlhiel.jpg) Ehlhiel **Ehlhiel** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) who may be encountered during the quest [Double-Edged](Skyrim_Double-Edged.md) if you decide to help [Zaharia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia) kill him.

Ehlhiel wears a set of [monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monk_Robes) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000bacd7). He has a leveled one-handed weapon and a [Potion of Plentiful Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing). He knows some leveled spells: one or two frost spells, one or two healing spells, a [ward](Skyrim_Ward_(spells).md) spell, an [armor](Skyrim_Armor_(spells).md) spell, and one or two [reanimation](Skyrim_Reanimate_(spells).md) spells, and may know a [turn undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(spells).md) spell.
