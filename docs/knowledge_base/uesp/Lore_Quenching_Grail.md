# Quenching Grail

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/ON-load-Pantherfang_Chapel.png/200px-ON-load-Pantherfang_Chapel.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Pantherfang_Chapel.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Pantherfang_Chapel.png) Pantherfang Chapel circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Quenching Grail was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) religious order in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). They were devoted to worship of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup>

At some point in the First Era, the embattled clergy of the Quenching Grail constructed the citadel of Pantherfang Chapel along the banks of the [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md) in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). This fortress served as a bastion of faith, and within its walls the Quenching Grail built a grand cathedral. Although the order was eventually disbanded, Pantherfang Chapel continued to stand for many centuries.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Pantherfang Chapel was available for purchase as a private residence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), all that remained at the site of the former cathedral were the ruins of an abandoned [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) fort known as Fort Redwater.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
