[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Bleak Falls Barrow is an old Nordic temple ruin in the snow-laden [Brittleshin Hills](Lore_Brittleshin_Hills.md) that divides [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) to the north from [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> The ruin stands out prominently above the town of [Riverwood](Lore_Riverwood.md), the nearest settlement, and has inspired some fear in the local citizens.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) discovered a [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) and the [Dragonstone](Lore_Dragonstone.md) deep in the sanctum of the barrow, a stone tablet depicting a map of [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) burial mounds.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRBleak-4)</sup> The Dragonborn also recovered a golden claw from a thief trying to access the inner sanctum.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The claw, which acted as a key to the sanctum, was passed down as an heirloom in the Valerius family of Riverwood.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
