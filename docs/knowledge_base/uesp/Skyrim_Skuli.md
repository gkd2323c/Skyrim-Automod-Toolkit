[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-npc-Skuli.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Skuli.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skuli.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skuli.jpg) Skuli inside the Old Hroldan Inn **Skuli** lives in the [Old Hroldan Inn](Skyrim_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md) and is the son of [Eydis](Skyrim_Eydis.md). He works in his mother's inn where he serves food and drinks and makes up the beds. He isn't aware that his father is missing, despite catching his mother in lies about his whereabouts.

If Eydis dies he will inherit the inn, but [Leontius Salvius](Skyrim_Leontius_Salvius.md) will take over as publican.

Skuli wears red colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). He carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md) to his mother's inn, a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child), and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
He will sometimes greet you with

- *"This is the Old Hroldan Inn. Clean beds and strong drink."*
- *"If you drink too much, you're out. No drunks."*
- *"My mother's dead, but I won't close the inn. I've hired Leontius to run things."* **(If Eydis is dead)**

When spoken to:

- **You're rather...mature for your age.**
- *"Watch your tongue, stranger. We don't take backtalk in this inn."*

- **Where can I learn more about magic?**
- *"Magic? That's dangerous. You want to learn that, go to that [College in Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). They teach that stuff there. Hey! I know. The Jarl's [wizard](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) could teach ya. He's lots closer."*

## Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-npc-Eydis_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eydis_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eydis_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eydis_02.jpg) Eydis with her son, Skuli
- **Skuli**: *"Mama, where did papa go?"*
- **Eydis**: *"He's off fighting the war, dear. For the Stormcloaks."*
- **Skuli**: *"Last time you said he was fighting for the Empire."*
- **Eydis**: *"Yes. I'm sure he's doing well there."*

They will also have a brief dialogue in which Eydis questions him about the current status of one of his chores:

- **Eydis**: *"Did you clean the linens, yet?*
- **Skuli**: *"I did. Those mead stains are always tricky."*

## Notes
- Even though he is depicted as mature for his age, he uses the default children farewell dialogue inherent of his voice type.
- Should both Leontius and [Eydis](Skyrim_Eydis.md) die, Skuli will be sent to Honorhall Orphanage and be available for adoption if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.
