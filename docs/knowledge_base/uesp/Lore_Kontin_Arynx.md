# Kontin Arynx

Kontin Arynx was the consort of the [Dragonborn Empress](Lore_Dragonborn.md) [Kintyra Septim II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md), who died defending the Imperial Forts on the [islands](Lore_Northeast_Archipelago.md) of easternmost [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) during the [War of the Red Diamond](Lore_War_of_the_Red_Diamond.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

## Notes
- According to the historical fiction, [*The Wolf Queen*](Lore_The_Wolf_Queen.md) by [Waughin Jarth](Lore_Waughin_Jarth.md), Lord Modellus of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) was the fiancé of Kintrya II.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWQ-2)</sup> The relationship between Modellus and Arynx is unknown, but they are seemingly different persons that were active in the early 3E 120s.
