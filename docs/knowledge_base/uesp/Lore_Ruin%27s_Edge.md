# Ruin's Edge

*May your enemies fear you and never know from where or with what enchantment you shall strike.*
— [Syl](Lore_Syl.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Ruin's Edge is a [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md) dark bow associated with [Syl](Lore_Syl.md), Duchess of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) at the beginning of the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SI-2)</sup> The fabled bow applies a random enchantment to each arrow; it has been known to cause paralysis, silence, demoralization, frenzy, burden,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and frost damage, and to drain magicka. The bow itself is ribbed [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) inlaid with a moving eyeball.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## History
In 3E 433, [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to Syl's court to become a courtier of the House of Dementia. After revealing a conspiracy in [Crucible](Lore_Crucible.md) to assassinate the Duchess, Syl rewarded the Hero with Ruin's Edge.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SI-2)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the bow had left the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) and had come into the possession of a bandit chief who may have been a follower of Sheogorath, given their large collection of [cheese](Lore_Cheese.md) and pet [bliss bug](Lore_Bliss_Bug.md). It was recovered from the bandit by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) in Stony Creek Cave, a small natural cave in the [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) region of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
