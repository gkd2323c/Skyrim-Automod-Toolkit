[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-item-Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band.jpg/200px-SR-item-Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band.jpg) Vittoria's Wedding Band Vittoria's Wedding Band is the wedding ring of [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md), worn during [her wedding reception](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md). Unfortunately, you will be attending the wedding reception to kill Vittoria, and so will likely be taking the ring off her dead body, though it can also be pickpocketed from her if you have the [Perfect Touch perk](Skyrim_Perfect_Touch.md). This ring is identical in appearance to a [gold ring](Skyrim_Gold_Ring.md) and does not possess an enchantment.
