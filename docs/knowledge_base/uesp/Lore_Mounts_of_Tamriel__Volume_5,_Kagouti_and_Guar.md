Mounts of Tamriel: Volume 5, Kagouti and Guar **by [Fadus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fadus_Falto) of Skingrad** *A discussion with a Telvanni Magister regarding the domestication of Kagouti and Guar* *
---
I had the pleasure of discussing the stewardship of the strange bipedal creatures of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) at a lavishly appointed cornerclub near [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md). My host, a well-spoken [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) magister, took great pleasure in discussing the many and varied ways these lizard-beasts are husbanded across [the region](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). She personally prefers the sturdy [kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md) over the more prosaic work-a-day [guar](Lore_Guar.md). More recently domesticated, more future potential, even potentially magically more potent as some recent experiments have proven.

I demurred from offering an opinion, a visitor to these lands, happy to sip my Kaveh and listen rather than speak. I find the bipedal nature of the kagouti and the guar their most fascinating attributes, and when my host wound down and I was able to ask after a few details I found her responses oddly cold and evasive. Where as just moments ago she'd been excited, even overjoyed to share her thoughts, asking after this fundamental descriptor caused her to grow distant.

The only thing I could get from her is that the magically adaptable nature of the guar and kagouti is, to use her words, "not an accident." I've asked for access the esteemed [Magister Varkenel](Lore_Varkenel.md)'s notes on the development of the [Vvardvark](Lore_Vvardvark.md). Purely for academic reasons, you understand! But I was soundly rejected based on "secrets vital to the House."

Make of that what you will.
