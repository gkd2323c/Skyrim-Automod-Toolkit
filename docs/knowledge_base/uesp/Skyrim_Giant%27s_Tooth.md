[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-place-Giant%27s_Tooth.jpg/200px-SR-place-Giant%27s_Tooth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant%27s_Tooth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Giant%27s_Tooth.jpg) Giant's Tooth **Giant's Tooth** is a small island in the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). It can only be reached by speaking to the [ferryman](Skyrim_Ferryman.md) [Harlaug](Skyrim_Harlaug.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) during the quest [A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md).

The island consists of a small [giant camp](Skyrim_Giant_Camps.md) and the entrance to [Iron Tusk Cave](Skyrim_Iron_Tusk_Cave.md). You will be sent here to retrieve an [Ironwood Fruit](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md) during the related quest, although these six rare trees only grow in the conditions inside the cave.

The camp is populated by two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and contains two dead [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md). [Snow foxes](Skyrim_Fox.md) will also spawn around the camp and inside the cave. Upon approaching the Ironwood Fruit trees inside the cave, three [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) will awaken and attack you. Live [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit.md) can be found on the island's shoreline. When you exit the cave after retrieving the Ironwood Fruit, three [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md) will appear and attack you. A *[Fisherman's Note](Skyrim_Fisherman%27s_Note.md)* can be found on a chopping block in the camp.

## Related Quests
- **[A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup>: Make a bitter choice.

## Notes
- Although this location has a [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) map marker, it cannot be fast traveled to from mainland [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md).
- It is not possible to swim around the island or climb over the mountains to the other side. Using TCL shows that the backside of the island is unfinished in design.
- [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) can be found on the outskirts of the location by the western shoreline and inside the cave.
- An [Iron Ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Iron_Ore) vein can be found on the north side of the cave entrance.
- Two [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) can be found in the raft right next to Harlaug's.
