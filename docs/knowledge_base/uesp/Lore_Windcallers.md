# Windcallers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/ON-crown_store-Windcaller_Garb.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Windcaller_Garb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Windcaller_Garb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Windcaller_Garb.jpg) A Windcaller The Windcallers, also known as the **Blessed of the All-Maker** are [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) [witches](Lore_Witch.md) who adeptly invoke the [winds](Lore_Weather_Magic.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Costume-1)</sup> They are distinguished by delicate markings on their faces and are renowned for their creature companions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Face-2)</sup> Circlets worn by them feature [stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md) nestled between [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md) embellishments, symbolizing their connection to the winds and their trained wolf guardians.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Circlet-3)</sup> The flowing body art of a [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) Windcaller reflects the breezes and gusts of air accompanying them on their journeys, with some believing these marks tell a tale of self-discovery along the path set by the [All-Maker](Lore_All-Maker.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Body-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The term "windcaller" is sometimes used to denote practitioners of storm magic across Tamriel.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Windcaller.27s_Sash-6)</sup>
- [Jurgen Windcaller](Lore_Jurgen_Windcaller.md), a powerful [Tongue](Lore_Tongues.md) from the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), is seemingly unrelated to the Windcallers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1S-7)</sup>
