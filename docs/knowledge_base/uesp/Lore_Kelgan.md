**Councilor** Kelgan the **Taker of Teeth**, also known as the **Troll-Breaker** was a member of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) during the reign of [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md), the founder of the [Longhouse Dynasty](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IDR-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Durcorach.jpg) Emperor Durcorach Little is known of Kelgan's past, except that he participated in [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md)'s conquest of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in [2E 533](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_533).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho M:ASo S-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> He was known for his great enthusiasm for carrying out Durcorach's will and for his deeds in many battles of the Black Drake's campaigns.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IDR-1)</sup>

In [2E 534](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_534), on 1st of the Sun's Dawn, he was appointed by the recently crowned Emperor Durcorach as a representative at large, together with Lord Pheomas Lucasta of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), as a token of his deeds to the first Longhouse Emperor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IDR-1)</sup>

His fate following the fall of the Longhouse dynasty in [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577) is unknown.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SHot LE-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-COTFC4-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EFEV-5)</sup> Many Reachfolk retreated from Cyrodiil following the death of Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md), but it is unknown whether or not he was among them.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ROTDOM-6)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the reign of the Empress Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), he was no longer a member of the Elder Council.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MOTE-7)</sup>
