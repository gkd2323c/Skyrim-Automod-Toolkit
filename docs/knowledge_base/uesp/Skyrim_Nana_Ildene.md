[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-npc-Nana_Ildene.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nana_Ildene.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nana_Ildene.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nana_Ildene.jpg) Nana Ildene **Nana Ildene** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) who works and lives in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) at [The Treasury House](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md). She works as a cleaner and has done so for twenty years. Her [true colors](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) are revealed during [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md).

Nana Ildene spends all of her time sweeping the floor at the foot of the southwestern stairs, just behind the chair where [Betrid Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Betrid_Silver-Blood.md) sits with her book. During the related quest, should you choose to question [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md), Nana Ildene will immediately attack Betrid, who will suffer a scripted death. You will then have to fight her, with the inevitable result. If Nana survives the quest (see [notes](#Notes)) she will sweep the floor indefinitely.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5), and is equipped with a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger). She carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Markarth_Treasury_House) to the Treasury House and a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor). She knows several spells, specifically [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), [Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Forsworn Conspiracy
After questioning Thonar she will yell out:

*"For the Forsworn!"* After this, Ildene will kill Betrid in cold blood and she, along with [Donnel](Skyrim_Donnel.md), will then proceed to attack you and anyone else in the Treasury House.

## Dialogue
Ildene will greet you with:

*"You just let old nana know if you need anything, all right dear?"* *"I've worked in the Treasury House for almost twenty years."* *"There isn't an inch of this building that I haven't cleaned up."* When exiting conversation with her:

*"Bye now, dearie."* *"Take care, dearie."*
## Conversations
If she runs into Thonar or front desk manager [Rhiada](Skyrim_Rhiada.md), she will appear almost frightening in her dialogue:

**Thonar**: *"Ah, Nana Ildene, whatever would my family do without you?"* **Ildene**: *"Ah, Thonar, don't you worry. Nana Ildene isn't going anywhere."* **Thonar**: *"Um, good. We reward loyalty, you know."* **Ildene**: *"Oh, I know, Thonar. Only a mad woman would ever betray the Silver-Blood family."* **Thonar**: *"Uh, yes. Quite."* **Rhiada**: *"You have worked here in the Treasury House for a long time, haven't you Ildene?"* **Ildene**: *"Yes, child. Nana Ildene has taken care of the Silver-Bloods for many years...."* **Rhiada**: *"It's just... aren't you worried about the Forsworn? Maybe it's not safe here."* **Ildene**: *"Don't you worry Rhiada. If any Forsworn coming knocking on our door, Nana will let you know...."* **Rhiada**: *"Thanks Nana. I feel better now."*

## Notes
- Her nickname of "Nana" seems to come from her earlier role as "nanny" for the Silver-Bloods. This is mentioned by Eltrys in early dialogue, as well as when she states she helped care for "their boy". The Silver-Bloods don't have any children or younger members in the final game, but they may have once had a son named [Kolgrim](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md#Notes).
- Ildene's death (and her attack on Betrid) can be avoided by pickpocketing and reading [Thonar's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar%27s_Journal) or [Nepos's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos%27s_Journal) journals instead of questioning them. - Alternatively, it can be avoided by going directly to [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md) after questioning [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md).
