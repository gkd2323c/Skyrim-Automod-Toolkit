## History
Morrowind's defense against the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) in the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md) was built across the northwest of the province in areas such as Cormaris View and Blacklight, as well as, [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md) and [Silgrad Tower](Lore_Silgrad_Tower.md). The defense was a motley crew of warriors from various aspects of the province, ranging from mercenaries of the Great [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md), [Temple Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md), house noble militias, and the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md). As time went on, Morrowind's defense dwindled, leaving only House Redoran to face this expansive empire. Cormaris View and the rest of the province was claimed by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OM-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Cormar View was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Niskan, and it had a rivalry with [Reich Parkeep](Lore_Reich_Parkeep.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> Cormar View was neighbored by several settlements including Blacklight to the east, Reich Parkeep to the south and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to the west, towards [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap Morrowind-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap Skyrim-5)</sup>
