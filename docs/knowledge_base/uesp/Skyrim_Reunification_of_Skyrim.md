## Quick Walkthrough
Regain each of Skyrim's [Holds](Skyrim_Holds.md) (depending upon your choices during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md)):

1. Regain [The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md).
2. Regain [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).
3. Regain [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md).
4. Regain [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md).
5. Regain [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md).
6. Regain [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md).
7. Regain [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
After defending [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) from the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) during the previous quest, [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md), the [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) will finally side with the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). Go go to [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) to report your success to [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md). He will award you your first promotion, the rank of [Quaestor](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks), along with a [leveled enchanted weapon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Enchanted_Weapons) and order you to meet [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at one of many [Imperial Military Camp](Skyrim_Military_Camps.md#Imperial_Camps) for your next assignment; depending on your progress through the [Main Quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md) the Camp you will be sent to will change as follows.

### Quest Options
This quest governs the overall progress of the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) for the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). It serves as an "umbrella quest", taking you through a series of individual quests that you receive as it progresses.

The overall goal of the quest is to conquer, for the Empire, each [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) of Skyrim that is currently under Stormcloak control. You will accomplish this one hold at a time, by taking control of the [military fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) in each, ending with one final siege on the enemy's capital, [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), in the order of the following table. The holds to be conquered can be altered by negotiation during the optional quest [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and therefore only a subset of the following quests needs to be completed.

- Without doing Season Unending, only the Stormcloak-controlled holds in the following table need to be conquered.
- By doing Season Unending: - The initially allied-controlled holds only need to be reconquered if given to the enemy during negotiation.
- The initially enemy-controlled holds will not need to be conquered if taken from the enemy during negotiation.
- The initially enemy-controlled holds will need to be conquered if not taken during negotiation.
- If you progress it to the point of a truce, any active quest in the series will auto-complete and cannot be restarted. If that quest involves a Military Fort, the related hold is transferred to allied control.
- This quest will be put on hold, until you complete [Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md).

### Reporting for duty
Between each hold conquest, you will be prompted to report to your faction leader for a reward and the next hold assignment. Then, to get your orders, you need to speak to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at the Military Camp you are assigned to with the *"Reporting for duty."* option.

### The Pale
*The Pale does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Pale Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Imperial_Camp).

- **[A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md)**: Find and deliver some forged [documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm Cloak_Documents) to the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).
- **[The Battle for Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial).md)**: Take [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

**Reward**: [Praefect](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) rank with leveled sword (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

### The Rift
*The Rift does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Rift Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp).

- **[Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial).md)**: Use a [corrupt steward](Skyrim_Anuriel.md) to gain intelligence on the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[The Battle for Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial).md)**: Take [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md)) ([![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/18px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) 10 pts/Bronze)

**Reward**: [Tribune](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) rank with leveled shield (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

### Winterhold
*Winterhold does not need to be regained if it was given to the Imperials during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Winterhold Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Winterhold_Imperial_Camp).

- **[Rescue from Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav.md)**: Take [Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Fort_Kastav.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and rescue the prisoners within. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

**Reward**: [Legate](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) rank with leveled armor.

### Hjaalmarch
*Hjaalmarch only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Imperial_Camp).

- **[The Battle for Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Imperial).md)**: Retake [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

**Reward**: leveled sword.

### Falkreath Hold
*Falkreath Hold only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Falkreath Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Imperial_Camp).

- **[The Battle for Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Neugrad.md)**: Retake [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

**Reward**: leveled sword.

### The Reach
*The Reach only needs to be regained if it was given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Reach Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reach_Imperial_Camp).

- **[The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Imperial).md)**: Retake [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md)) ([![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/18px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) 10 pts/Bronze — only if [The Battle for Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial).md) did not need to be completed)

**Reward**: leveled sword.

### Eastmarch
*Eastmarch always needs to be regained as it hosts the enemy's capital* Report to Legate Rikke at the [Eastmarch Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eastmarch_Imperial_Camp).

- **[The Battle for Fort Amol](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Amol.md)**: Prepare for the final assault on [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) by taking [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md)**: Conquer the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [capital](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and bring [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) to justice! ([![SR-achievement-Hero of Skyrim.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-achievement-Hero_of_Skyrim.png/18px-SR-achievement-Hero_of_Skyrim.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Hero_of_Skyrim.png) 30 pts/Silver)

**Reward**: leveled enchanted sword.

After completing [The Battle for Fort Amol](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Amol.md), return to Rikke who will tell you *"We're taking the city. Meet the soldiers gathering for the attack. Move it!"*. This will automatically start [Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md). As part of that quest, [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) will have a short dialogue with [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), who will request the Dragonborn to be the one who kills him, as it will "make for a better song." General Tullius won't care whether you kill Ulfric or not, just as long as he's dead. If you choose to kill Ulfric, General Tullius will tell you to use his sword (which is a leveled enchanteed sword) to do the deed (this is not required though) and Battle for Windhelm will be complete once you deliver the final blow. If you choose to allow Tullius to do it, Battle for Windhelm will complete after that, when General will reward you with his leveled enchanted sword. Either way, Reunification of Skyrim will now be complete and you can join Rikke and Tullius outside to hear him delivering the final speech of the Civil War, promising increased pay and freedom for them to return to their families while giving compensation to families who have lost loved ones. Then, you're allowed to talk to Legate Rikke and General Tullius on what will happen next. Congratulations, [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) is now part of the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) once again and peace is restored.

## Notes
- This quest is mainly used to move you between the other Imperial quests.
- This quest and [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md) are different radiant versions of the same quest.
- Season Unending can be started anytime from prior to starting the Civil War Quest-line to when there are at least 2 holds left for you to conquer for your allied side.
- If, after [Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md), depending on when the truce halted this quest, there is no quest mark, to resume it, you may need to speak to your faction leader.
- It is not possible to receive and complete all the potential quests in the series, as negotiation during Season Unending is limited.
- Trading the wrong holds during Season Unending can prevent obtaining some unique radiant quests from the series, [ranks](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) and [War Hero achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md#War_Hero).
- Ranks are not automatic, they are rewarded if the conditions are met: successful attack (not fleeing) during the related quest and not obtaining the hold by negotiation during Season Unending.
- Ranks are progressive but not required: if you miss one, you can obtain the following one by completing the relative quest. So it is actually necessary to complete only [Rescue from Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav.md) to obtain the final rank of Legate.
- Each rank comes with its leveled item reward.
- To check your rank you can spam-speak to any allied soldier until he greets you by your current rank.
- When a hold switches ownership, deposed [Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) and court/family are exiled, usually to their allegiance side's capital, and replaced by opposing ones, while related [Civil War Forts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forts#Civil_War_Forts) are re-garrisoned by the new owner's soldiers.
- At any point during this quest, enemy-controlled Civil War Forts are always garrisoned by enemy soldiers and will be garrisoned by allied ones when conquered. However initially allied-controlled ones remain inhabited by generic enemies, needing individual clearing.
- Any allied military camp located within an enemy hold disappears upon conquest of that specific hold.
- Legate Rikke will replace the Officer at any military camp for the duration of the hold assignment. If she is not there but the quest marker is upon an empty spot wait a few hours for her to arrive.
- After every quest of this series (except for [Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md)), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards); this is not always recommended though, as this is a cause for the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage CWObj stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest CWObj`.
