[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-place-Brina%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brina%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brina%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brina%27s_House.jpg) Brina's House **Brina's House** is a small house in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), located next to [Leigelf's House](Skyrim_Leigelf%27s_House.md), opposite [The Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md).

It is the home of [Brina Merilis](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md) and her guard, [Horik Halfhand](Skyrim_Horik_Halfhand.md). It consists of one area only: *[Brina's House](#Brina.27s_House)*.

This house will be permanently abandoned if [The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) is given to the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) in [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and subsequently retaken by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The house faces the bay, with a decking out front. There is a pile of firewood on the left side of the decking, and a wooden bench on the right. The house is protected by a novice lock when neither of its inhabitants are home; [Horik Halfhand](Skyrim_Horik_Halfhand.md) acts as [Brina Merilis's](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md) [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) or bodyguard, and as such travels around the town with her at all times.

### Brina's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brina%27s_House.jpg) Brina's home The house is a typical one-room dwelling. On the left as you enter, there is a chair and [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel in the corner. Next to them, against the east wall, is a cupboard holding four bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). There is another collection of barrels in the southeastern corner. Against the south wall, there is a long wooden table with two bottles of alto wine and a single bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top. Hanging above the table are three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), and a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Further along on the right is a food barrel and a set of shelves holding four more bottles of alto wine and four bottles of wine. To the right of this, still on the south wall, lies a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) and a kettle containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) placed on the hearth. To the right of the fireplace is a pile of firewood and a basket containing a copy of the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Importance of Where](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where)*.

Coming out of the west wall are two owned single beds, with an end table holding a bottle of Nord mead and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of each bed, both of which contain leveled items. Against the north wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace) placed on top. To the right of the wardrobe, next to the door, is a small round wooden table and two chairs. On the table is a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine and two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread).

## Notes
- With the exception of most barrels and sacks, all containers in the house are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
