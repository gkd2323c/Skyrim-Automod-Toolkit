[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/SR-npc-Severio_Pelagia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Severio_Pelagia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Severio_Pelagia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Severio_Pelagia.jpg) Severio Pelagia **Severio Pelagia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who owns [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md) just outside [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). He will pay you for any [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) you bring him.

While his farmhands [Nimriel](Skyrim_Nimriel.md) and [Gloth](Skyrim_Gloth.md) live at the farm, Severio owns a [house](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia%27s_House.md) in the center of Whiterun and sleeps there every night between 11pm and 8am. After waking up, he heads down to the farm and helps Nimriel tend the crops for twelve hours. At 8pm he was supposed to spend three hours at [the Drunken Huntsman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman), but doesn't due to a [bug](#Bugs). Instead he keeps tending the farm until 11pm when he finally heads home to bed.

Severio wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Severio_Pelagia%27s_House) to his house.

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
When greeting you, he may talk about himself, or about his farm:

*"There's a nip in the air. Cold won't be good for my crops. Never is."* *"I own the Pelagia Farm, just outside the city walls. Nimriel takes good care of things for me."* *"I had my years of war and fighting. Senseless, all of it. All I want now is a rake and a patch of good soil."* *"You should buy some produce from [Carlotta](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia.md)'s stall. Most of it comes from my farm."* *"You should stop by my farm if you get a chance. See what life's really about."*
## Notes
- During the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), regardless of the path chosen, Severio will always be killed off-screen and his house will get destroyed.
- His relationship rank with his farmhand Nimriel is 4, indicating they are lovers, but neither party ever makes reference to this.
