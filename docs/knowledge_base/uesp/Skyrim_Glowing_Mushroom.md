[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-flora-Glowing_Mushroom.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Glowing_Mushroom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Glowing_Mushroom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Glowing_Mushroom.jpg) A cluster of glowing mushrooms **Glowing Mushrooms** are a variety of fungus found growing on the walls of some [caves](Skyrim_Caves.md), providing a source of light. They grow in clusters of 5-7 mushrooms which can be harvested for 1 sample each. Several clusters may grow close together in a single area.

## Ingredients
40 guaranteed, already harvested samples can be found in 14 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 10 in [Lost Echo Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Echo_Cave) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 10 in [Tolvald's Gap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Gap) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 4 in [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss_(place).md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 in [Irkngthand Grand Cavern](Skyrim_Irkngthand_Grand_Cavern.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in the [Dwemer Museum](Skyrim_Dwemer_Museum.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

Glowing mushrooms are categorized as uncommon ingredients, meaning they may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). They do *not* appear in the inventories of apothecary merchants (even if the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk has been unlocked); however a single sample can be purchased from either [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md) or [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md). Unlike several other ingredients that are not stocked by apothecary shops, apothecary merchants will buy glowing mushrooms.

## Notes
- Despite their rather large size, the "hitbox" to pick the mushrooms only covers a very small portion of the model. This can make them very tedious to harvest, and some plants do not have a hitbox at all, rendering them unable to be harvested.

- In [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md), there are several gigantic glowing mushrooms, larger than a human. However, these serve as little more than scenery and cannot be harvested or interacted with in any way.
