## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read the [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mysterious_Altar) in [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md).
2. Travel to the [Roadside Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Ruins).
3. Choose a cup (Fortune, Power, Nothing).
4. If you chose Fortune, find a bed and sleep. This quest will end and [Fortunate Son](Skyrim_Fortunate_Son.md) will begin.
If you chose Power, find a bed and sleep. This quest will end and [The Pit](Skyrim_The_Pit_(quest).md) will begin.
If you chose Nothing, go back to Falkreath. The quest will continue.
5. Locate a Ferryman to take you to Giant's Tooth.
6. Recover the Iron Wood Fruit.
7. Return to where you left from.
8. Cook soup from Ironwood Fruit (Ironwood Fruit, Charred Skeever Hide, Salt Pile).
9. Travel to Temple of Kynareth in Whiterun.
10. Give soup to priestess.
11. Wait a day to speak with Rulnik.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Choice
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish.jpg) Mysterious Altar note In [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), go to the [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md) and into the owner's room where you will find a note entitled *[Mysterious Altar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mysterious_Altar)*. Read it to start the quest. Once read, you will be sent on to the [Roadside Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Ruins). There, you will find several dead bandits and a [Spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) waiting for you. Deal with the Spriggan and in the center of the ruins there is a leveled chest and the strange altar with three cups on it.

- If you chose Fortune, find a bed and sleep. This quest will end and [Fortunate Son](Skyrim_Fortunate_Son.md) will begin.

- If you chose Power, this quest will end and [The Pit](Skyrim_The_Pit_(quest).md) will begin.

- If you choose Nothing, this quest will continue and you need to head back to town.

### Path of Nothing
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg) Find an Ironwood tree Upon returning to civilization, you will be approached by a courier with a *[Note from the Temple of Kynareth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_from_the_Temple_of_Kynareth)*. The note instructs you to find a ferryman to take you to the location of the Iron Wood Tree to get the fruit, [Giant's Tooth](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md). Only [Harlaug](Skyrim_Harlaug.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) will agree to bring you.

On arrival, there is a giant camp with two giants. You can sneak by them to the path behind the camp or kill them. Once done with the giants, follow the path to the [Iron Tusk Cave](Skyrim_Iron_Tusk_Cave.md). Find the Iron Wood tree and harvest the fruit from it. You will then be attacked by several Spriggans. Defeat them and head back the way you came. Three [Ice Wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md) will spawn in the giant camp when you emerge and you will need to fight your way back to the boat to return to Dawnstar.

Arriving back at Dawnstar, you will need to prepare a bowl of [Ironwood Soup](Skyrim_Ironwood_Soup.md) using three ingredients: the ironwood fruit, [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), and a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile). The nearest cooking station is in [Silus Vesuius's house](Skyrim_Silus_Vesuius%27s_House.md). You will already have been given a charred skeever hide when you received the letter from the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md), so all you need to find is some salt. Look in the Fish Barrels outside the cave on Giant's Tooth, and you should find some. Once you have the soup, head for [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and the Temple of Kynareth.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_Dying_Wish_02.jpg) Acolyte Aldren and Rulnik At the temple, give the soup to [Acolyte Aldren](Skyrim_Acolyte_Aldren.md) who sent you the note. If you demand payment, you receive 75 gold. She will give the soup to Rulnik and you will be told to wait a full day before checking in on him. Wait a day and come back. Talk to Rulnik and you will receive your rewards: [Rulnik's Dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rulnik%27s_Dagger), the [Bittercup](Skyrim_Bittercup_(item).md), and a note titled *[On the Bittercup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:On_the_Bittercup)*. The Bittercup acts as a [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) and will raise your highest [attribute](Skyrim_Attributes.md) by 20 points at the cost of 20 points from your lowest. For choosing the path of nothing, you will also gain Rulnik as a follower. He is a powerful mage with a level cap of 75, and he can also be appointed as a [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md). Rulnik's Dagger does the same damage as an Elven dagger, but weighs slightly less. It can be improved with a quicksilver ingot.

## Notes
- After quest completion, you can return to Giant's Tooth island via the same ferryman and go back in the Iron Tusk Cave where the fruit will respawn. If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, you'll be able to make Hot Ironwood Soup as well. No matter which path is chosen, Ironwood Fruit will become available from [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).
