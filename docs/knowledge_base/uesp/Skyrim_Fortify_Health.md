*Alchemy description:* **Health is increased by <mag> points for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases your health by <mag> points.**
**Fortify Health** boosts the target's [health](Skyrim_Health.md) by <mag> points. When the effect expires, the target loses <mag> points of health unless that would reduce the target's health to 0 or less (the target is left with at least 1 health point when the effect expires).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Health if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor *[of Health](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Health)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Health*, *of Major Health*, *of Extreme Health*, *of Eminent Health*, and *of Peerless Health.*
- [Enchanted Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanted_Ring)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Amulet of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Arkay)
- [Cuirass of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cuirass_of_the_Crusader) and [Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>
- [The Gauldur Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gauldur_Amulet)
- [Gauldur Amulet Fragment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauldur_Amulet_Fragment)
- [Shield of Ysgramor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shield_of_Ysgramor)
