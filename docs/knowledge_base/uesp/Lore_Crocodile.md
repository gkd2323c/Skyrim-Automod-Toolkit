Crocodiles, are large, semi-aquatic, carnivorous [reptiles](Lore_Reptile.md) with thick hides that can be found in swamps and near rivers throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Croc-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kyne C-2)</sup> Crocodiles are described as having "ancient skin", with one Argonian claiming they roamed [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) before his people did.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kyne C-2)</sup> Similar reptiles to them include [alligators](Lore_Alligator.md) and [caimans](Lore_Caiman.md).

In the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tongue of [Jel](Lore_Jel.md), they are known as **rormasu**, which translates to "big-mouthed reptile that blinks above water". Redguards colloquially know them as **marsh [dunerippers](Lore_Duneripper.md)**, while some [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) apparently known them as **four-clawed [daedroths](Lore_Daedroth.md)**, though the humor in this title is thought to be lost in the translation from [Ta'agra](Lore_Ta%27agra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kyne C-2)</sup>

## Variants
Crocodiles are known to grow throughout their lives, as such, some have been known to grow to massive sizes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GSkull-3)</sup> An especially large breed native to the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice.md) can be identified by the fine scale pattern near the tip of their tail.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTail-4)</sup> The [Helstrom](Lore_Helstrom.md) crocodile was another incredibly large variant that went extinct during the Great Burn of [1E 2828](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2828) in the [Blackwater War](Lore_Blackwater_War.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAnti Skull-5)</sup> White crocodiles are known to be extremely rare.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-White-6)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
There are many uses for the parts harvested from a crocodile. Necklaces made from their teeth may be worn by young [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) during their maturation trial.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Crocodile hide makes for both sturdy and attractive armor,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> as well as travel bags,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-White-6)</sup> while baked crocodile tongue is a dish commonly found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ogzar-9)</sup>

The skulls of the extinct Helstom crocodiles were used by the [Dead-Water Tribe](Lore_Dead-Water_Tribe.md) as a trap for either animals or people.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAnti Skull-5)</sup> They also use their leather in making their armor. Skeletal crocodile tails were popularly sold by street vendors in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) in the third century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTail-4)</sup>

Crocodiles have been domesticated by Argonians, for instance the Dead-Water tribe takes crocodile hatchlings and raises them to appear strong and spark fear, though some secretly find them cute to keep as pets.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Among certain sects of the Argonians of Solstice, such as the Death-Dancers, the crocodile was seen as a guardian of the Unmaker, [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md). They were known to husband the gigantic reptiles as temple guardians or mounts that could seat multiple.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTail-4)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-GRibs-11)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GSkull-3)</sup>
