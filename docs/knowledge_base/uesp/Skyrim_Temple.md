[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Temple.jpg/200px-SR-place-Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Temple.jpg) Temple The **Temple** is a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) place of worship in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

It is up a flight of stairs opposite the pier between [Morvayn Manor](Skyrim_Morvayn_Manor.md) and [Caerellius House](Skyrim_Caerellius_House.md), above the [Morvayn](Skyrim_Morvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.md) and [Ulen](Skyrim_Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.md) Ancestral Tombs.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
At the top of the stairs, to the northwest is a circular ash pile garden plot growing five [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants, and to the southeast are three food urns and three ash yam urns near another flight of stairs up to the top of [the Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md). To the left of the entrance are three more ash yam urns and a lit brazier. To the right of the entrance is another ash yam urn and a lit brazier. A [Redoran guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) is posted outside the entrance, which is unlocked.

### Temple
The entrance leads into the upper floor of the temple. Along the east wall is an open box containing six ash yams and a bench. Along the west wall is another bench opposite the first. Along the north wall between the benches is an open doorway to the main worship area. In the middle of the room, stairs lead down to the lower floor, with four lit braziers and seven ash yam urns around the sides of the stairs.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-interior-Temple_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Temple_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_05.jpg) Shrine of Boethiah In the main worship area, pairs of benches flank the entrance to the south and each of the three shrine alcoves to the west, north, and east. The west alcove contains the Shrine of Boethiah, which bestows the [Blessing of Boethiah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Boethiah). The shrine is in the center of a circular ash pile plot growing an ash yam and three scathecraw plants, with two baskets and an open box around the plot containing a total of ten more ash yams and a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), and two more small coin purses, a large coin purse, and a copy of *[The Reclamations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Reclamations)* on the floor around the box. The north alcove contains the Shrine of Mephala, which bestows the [Blessing of Mephala](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Mephala). The shrine is in the center of another circular ash pile plot growing two scathecraw plants, with an open box in front of the plot containing four ash yams, a loose [coin](Skyrim_Gold.md), and an [amethyst](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst), and a small coin purse, a medium coin purse, eight loose coins, two [garnets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet), and an ash yam on the edge of the plot and on the floor next to the box. The east alcove contains the Shrine of Azura, which bestows the [Blessing of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Azura). The shrine is in the center of another circular ash pile plot growing two ash yams and two scathecraw plants, with an open box in front of the plot containing three more ash yams, a medium coin purse, and a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), and two small coin purses, two [emeralds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald), and four ash yams on the edge of the plot and on the floor next to the box. In the middle of the area is a fourth circular ash pile littered with bones and surrounded by eight lit braziers.

At the bottom of the stairs, on which lies a copy of *[History of Raven Rock, Vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._I)*, is a corridor with rooms beyond doors to the west and east and a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Tomb_Key) -locked door ahead to the north leading to the [Temple Ancestral Tomb](Skyrim_Temple_Ancestral_Tomb.md). The key can be obtained as part of the quest [Clean Sweep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clean_Sweep).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-interior-Temple.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple.jpg) Elder Othreloth's room The room to the west is Elder Othreloth's bedroom. Along the south wall is a set of shelves holding seven bowls of [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal) and copies of both volumes of *[The Red Year](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year)*, with two food barrels to the right of the set of shelves. Along the north wall are two chairs and a small table holding two bottles of [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma), a bottle of [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shein), copies of *The Reclamations* and both volumes of *[Bone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone)*, and an expert-locked [strong box](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox). Near the west wall is a chair, a single owned bed, and two unusable barrels, one of which has a copy of *[The Five Far Stars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Five_Far_Stars)* on top.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-interior-Temple_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Temple_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Temple_02.jpg) Galdrus Hlervu's room The room to the east is Galdrus Hlervu's bedroom. Along the north wall is an ingredient barrel with two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on top, and a chair and a small round table holding a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of shein, and a copy of *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*, with another bottle of wine on the floor next to the table. Along the south wall is a chest and a set of shelves holding copies of all three volumes of *[History of Raven Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock)*, *[The House of Troubles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles)*, and *[The Doors of the Spirit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Doors_of_the_Spirit)*. Near the east wall is a single owned bed.
