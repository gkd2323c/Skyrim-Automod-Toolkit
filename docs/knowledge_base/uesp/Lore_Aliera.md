Aliera (*uh- **lee** -ruh*), also unknown as the **Runaway Queen**, the **Thief**, the **Armsdealer**, and the **Uniter of Tamriel**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Jindra-2)</sup> is written in myth to have been the Princess of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md),<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> and is described as having been active sometime in the centuries after the fall of the [Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) (circa [1E 420](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_420)) but before the arrival of the Redguards (circa [1E 792](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_792)).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup> Aliera is described as the mother of [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), though accounts differ on if King Corcyr of Daggerfall was the father or [King Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md) of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Jindra-2)</sup>

## History

### Queen of Daggerfall
Aliera married King Coycyr of Daggerfall as arranged of her parents at Wayrest per the custom of the culture at the time, and she tried to make the marriage work, but her husband shunned her and Aliera disengaged from her marriage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup> Later, King Moraelyn R'aathim of Ebonheart came to High Rock wanting to mine for ebony in the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md). The land had been in the Aliera's dowry from her marriage to Corcyr, and so Moraelyn had come to Daggerfall. Corcyr despised Dark Elves, so Aliera was made to negotiate the deal.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup> This later led to Aliera and Moraelyn committing an affair, and subsequently running off together.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup>

### Queen of Ebonheart
Aliera became a core member of [Moraelyn's Companions](Lore_Moraelyn%27s_Companions.md), a group of heroes and adventurers, and later married Moraelyn himself and became his Queen consort, taking on the name **Aliera R'aathim**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KE-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Jindra-2)</sup> Additionally, Aliera as an anti-hero figure, engaging in arms dealing and committing sins comparable to any other great tyrant the kings of the Iliac Bay are known to do.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Jindra-2)</sup> In her later life, Aliera is described as having united Tamriel and founded an empire with her husband and son.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Jindra-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>
