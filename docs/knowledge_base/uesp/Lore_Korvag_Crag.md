Korvag Crag was a location in ancient [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It was the dwelling of [trolls](Lore_Troll.md) who defeated and devoured all the members of the Sisterhood of the White Rose. A [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) was erected to commemorate the bitter defeat of the Sisterhood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Disarm-2)</sup>
