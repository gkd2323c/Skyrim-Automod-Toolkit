[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/SR-npc-Istar_Cairn-Breaker.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Istar_Cairn-Breaker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Istar_Cairn-Breaker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Istar_Cairn-Breaker.jpg) Istar Cairn-Breaker **Istar Cairn-Breaker** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) and the commanding officer of the [Haafingar Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Haafingar_Stormcloak_Camp.md). His tent is larger than the small one man tents the regular [Stormcloak Soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) have and contains a chest and an end table. He has no unique dialogue and spends the day wandering the camp with the rest of the soldiers. Should the Stormcloaks win the [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) by taking [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and killing [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md), Istar will take up the place of [Legate Adventus Caesennius](Skyrim_Legate_Adventus_Caesennius.md) at [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md).

Istar wears [Stormcloak officer armor](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Armor.md) along with a pair of matching [bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md). He either wields a one-handed weapon and a shield or a two-handed weapon, both of which can be up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) in quality. The shield can also be up to steel quality. He carries a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) which he supplements with 12 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) and has a spare [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
When you have not joined either the Stormcloaks or the Imperial Legion he can say:

- **How does one join the Stormcloaks?**
- *"[Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s not running a social club. You don't join anything. Either you're a true son of Skyrim, or you're not. If you are, then you make your way to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and talk to the man himself. If you're not, well friend, you'd best walk away before this gets ugly."*

- **How do you feel about this war?**
- *"Skyrim is the birthplace of humanity. The birthplace of honor. And those snowbacked Imperials renounced both when they laid down before the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md). Worse still, to pay for the privilege to keep his throne, the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) sells our very gods - [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), no less! - to the damn [elves](Skyrim_High_Elf.md). And what did [Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md) do? He and the [Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) who supported him? They took the Emperor's gold. Sold out their people for a slave master's "[peace treaty](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md)." Ulfric did right killing him. Soon we'll rid Skyrim of elves, their bloody Justiciars, and the Jarls in their pockets. The whole damn lot of them. And we'll cleave through the Legion to get to them."*

- **Why are you called Stormcloaks?**
- *"At first they called us that to belittle our cause. But we gladly accept being named for Ulfric Stormcloak, the only true High King of Skyrim. We are the true sons and daughters of Skyrim, and Ulfric... he's the truest of us all. We fight to make our land great once more, to raise up strong, noble [thanes](Skyrim_Thane.md), and lay low false Jarls and the cowards who hide behind them. The sound of our fury is a storm about to break on the heads of our enemies. Our friendship, a cloak of true brotherhood for all who would wear it."*
