### Mixwater Mill
Along with the sawmill itself is [Gilfre's House](Skyrim_Gilfre%27s_House.md) and the now empty [Worker's House](Skyrim_Worker%27s_House.md). Gilfre's residence holds a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Marksmanship Lesson](Skyrim_The_Marksmanship_Lesson.md)* (for full details, see the dedicated [house page](Skyrim_Gilfre%27s_House.md)). All items inside the worker's house can be freely taken (for full details, see the dedicated [house page](Skyrim_Worker%27s_House.md)). The [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) is next to the sawmill, and the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) is in front of the fireplace inside Gilfre's house.

The mill can serve as a useful free home if you marry Gilfre. However, the drying salmon and containers outside Gilfre's house do not become player-owned, even after marriage, and looting them will be considered stealing, though the [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) remains free to take. You can also freely sleep in the worker's house.

With [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, you can buy [sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> by speaking to Gilfre. You can also cut your own logs for free if she has given you permission to do so or if you have married her. A [clay deposit](Skyrim_Clay_Deposit.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> can be found here as well.

With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation installed, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) can be found by the water, along with a small box containing five [slaughterfish eggs](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Egg.md) and some [slaughterfish scales](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Scales.md).
