## Penitus Oculatus Outpost
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.jpg) Penitus Oculatus Outpost interior Immediately to the left upon entering is a respawning food barrel next to a table holding four loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), four bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), four pieces of [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), one [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), and two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). Above the table are several hanging ingredients, including three portions of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), four portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. To the right of the table is a woven basket containing eight [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and a respawning meat barrel with one [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and two [potions of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina) resting atop it. Above the basket and barrel are two hanging racks, one of which holds three freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) while the other holds two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) and another rabbit.

In the northwest corner is a single bed with a small non-respawning chest at its foot. A basket containing three [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) sits on the floor to the right of the bed, along with another respawning food barrel holding three bottles of Nord mead on top. Another single bed with a small non-respawning chest at its foot is situated next to this food barrel, and a copy of the book *[The Code of Malacath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath)* is stashed beneath the bed. Next to this bed, directly across from the front door, is the fireplace, where four bottles of Nord mead and one bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) are found on the mantel. The base of the fireplace holds a kettle containing five [pheasant breasts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast) on the left, and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) on the right. In front of the fireplace is a small table holding an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), five loose gold coins, a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), and three bottles of Nord mead. To the right of the fireplace is a non-respawning end table. A small non-respawning chest rests on the floor at the center of the east wall, flanked by a single bed on each side.

## Notes
- The porch of the outpost hosts five respawning food barrels, a respawning sack, a workbench, a tanning rack, and a table holding a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and a copy of a random common book, such as *[Palla, volume 1](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palla,_volume_1)* or *[2920, Sun's Dawn, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Sun%27s_Dawn,_v2)*.
- All four beds here belong to the [Penitus Oculatus Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus Oculatus Faction).
- Dialogue between [Commander Maro](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Commander_Maro) and a Penitus Oculatus Agent during the quest [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md) indicates that the outpost is supposed to be abandoned once the Emperor leaves Skyrim, though this never occurs in-game. (See [below](#Bugs) for a workaround)

## Bugs
- If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) capture Solitude, this outpost will remain active in Dragon Bridge, which can result in Penitus Oculatus agents and [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) engaging each other in combat. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, addresses this issue. If [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) questline and the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) questline for the Stormcloaks are finished, the outpost will be cleared out.
