The Exalted Viper was a short-lived [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) created in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). She was intended to replace [the Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md), and thus shared identical aspects.

As a mortal, the Exalted Viper was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) mage named **Cassipia**. When the [constellations](Lore_Constellations.md) fell from the sky above [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) circa [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arnure-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOE-2)</sup> the Serpent took form as a Celestial and began a cult called the [Scaled Court](Lore_Scaled_Court.md). Attracted by his rhetorical goals of shedding corruption from the world, Cassipia left the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and was appointed Regent of Serpentine Stratagems within the Court. However, she soon discovered that the Serpent's plans involved destroying civilization to restore [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) to a primal state. Along with her [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) companion Little Leaf, Cassipia secretly defected from the Scaled Court and began plotting to usurp the Serpent's place in the sky by becoming a Celestial of equal power.

Aided by Little Leaf and the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Regent Cassipia used her authority to steal and disrupt the Court's research on [nirncrux](Lore_Nirncrux.md). She learned how the ancient [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) first created Celestials and assisted in sabotaging the creation of [mantikoras](Lore_Mantikora.md), as well as the [Iron Orcs](Lore_Iron_Orcs.md) imbuing iron and flesh with nirncrux. Her efforts crippled the Serpent's attempt at raising an army in Upper Craglorn in the process, although her treachery went undiscovered and she retreated to Skyreach Temple with her followers to begin the transformation.

Kelmen Locke, an old acquaintance of Cassipia's, attempted to dissuade her from performing the ritual, but instead was killed. His death brought [the Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md)'s attention to her plans, and she journeyed to Skyreach Temple with the Vestige to prevent the creation of the Exalted Viper. Although the ritual had been interrupted, the death of Little Leaf triggered Cassipia's transformation, and the Exalted Viper was born in battle. However, she was promptly slain in her mortal form.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
