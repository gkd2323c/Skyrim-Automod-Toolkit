— *[A Game at Dinner](Lore_A_Game_at_Dinner.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AGAD-1)</sup> The [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) have learned over the ages to live in harmony with the inhospitable landscape of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), which is largely unsuited to agriculture. The dark volcanic terrain makes farming and hunting difficult. They gather [kwama eggs](Lore_Kwama_Egg.md), cultivate crops of [ash yams](Lore_Ash_Yam.md), and maintain herds of domesticated [guar](Lore_Guar.md), from which they harvest meat and hides. A wide variety of drinks also feature in Dunmer cuisine, made from saltrice and native fruits.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOC-2)</sup><sup>:13</sup>

## Utensils and Tableware
The Dunmer make use of a variety of dishes and utensils. The great houses stamp items with their seals such as ornate glassware from [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and carving knives and forks from [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Wax ladles are used to stir scrib jelly, as wax is one of the few things it wont stick to.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Specialty kwama egg spoons are used to slice the tops off of the eggs so that the soft contents can be scooped out and eaten.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Some tableware sets include thirty-six pieces of silverware, one for each of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)'s sermons.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Disposable dining plates are made from pressed ash and [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) ichor.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Earthenware tajines are used to prepare heavily stewed dishes.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Drinkware is made from different materials such as bonemold which is light and sturdy<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> and volcanic glass that lends a swirling black finish to the product.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Beautiful crystal greef decanters are also produced.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> Gilded mazte glasses made from greenglass are used in some cornerclubs.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> Dunmeri tea service sets date back to the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and some are family heirlooms.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Some Serving plates have been made out of the scales of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

Other culinary tools include spice jars crafted by [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md)<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> and spice shakers modeled after Morrowind's landmarks such as the [Brothers of Strife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brothers_of_Strife).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup> Cutting boards are at times made out of goblin-wood.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup> Sundials fashioned from [shalk](Lore_Shalk.md) resin are used to keep track of cooking times in more complicated Dunmer recipes.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup>
