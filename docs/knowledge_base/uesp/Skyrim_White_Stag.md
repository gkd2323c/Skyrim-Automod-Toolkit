[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-creature-White_Stag.jpg/200px-SR-creature-White_Stag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-White_Stag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-White_Stag.jpg) The White Stag in Falkreath Hold The **White Stag** is a unique [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) which appears during the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md). It's much larger than a normal [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md), and its fur is much lighter as well.

According to [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md), the stag roams [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) while [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md) awaits a hunter skilled enough to kill it. However, the stag does not appear until the related quest is started.

After killing it, the [Aspect of Hircine](Skyrim_Aspect_of_Hircine.md) will appear over its corpse. You must speak with it to continue the quest.

## Related Quests
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md)**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md)'s Great Hunt.

## Notes
- Despite being an incarnate form of Hircine, you can [soul trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(spell).md) it.
- During the quest, the White Stag is referred to in your journal as the "great beast".
- Unlike regular elk, the White Stag regenerates health quickly, which can make killing it tough.
- Despite its unique nature, it drops standard elk fare: venison, hide, and a chance at large deer antlers. Both the antlers and the hide are also of the standard color, not white.

## Bugs
- The body of the White Stag doesn't seem to disappear. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- The White Stag may occasionally run past the map boundaries and become unreachable. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be resolved by moving the White Stag using Console Commands.
