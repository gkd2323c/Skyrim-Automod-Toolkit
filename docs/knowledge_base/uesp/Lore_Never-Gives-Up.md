# Never-Gives-Up

| Never-Gives-Up | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) | Gender | Unknown |

Never-Gives-Up was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) war hero of some renown, who perished at some point before or during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup>

Little is known about Never-Gives-Up's life, but as of 2E 582 several mounted, [mummified](Lore_Mummy.md) Argonian tails with attached engraved plaques could be found all across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Engravings on each of those plaques claimed that the tail they were attached to was real and once belonged to this war hero.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup> They were among various oddities sought by the collectors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CVC-3)</sup>
