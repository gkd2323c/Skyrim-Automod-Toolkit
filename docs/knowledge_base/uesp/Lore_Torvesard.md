- *"When dealing with matters of fate, meetings such as this meeting are inevitable. Know that I am Torvesard. And know that we need not be adversaries in these matters. I simply seek an ancient dream, barely remembered. Lost knowledge, nothing more."* —Torvesard<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDialogue-1)</sup>

Torvesard was a [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) who began his service to [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md) long ago. When being wiped from history, Ithelia saved Torvesard and preserved just enough of his memory so that he could one day save her in return.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONTorvesard-2)</sup> He bore unique tattoos on his body, wielded a maul, and had [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) -based duplication abilities, as well as the odd skill of being nearly undetectable in his movements.
