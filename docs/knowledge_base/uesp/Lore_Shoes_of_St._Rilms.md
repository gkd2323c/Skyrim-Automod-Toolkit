# Shoes of St. Rilms

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/MW-item-Shoes_of_St._Rilms.jpg/200px-MW-item-Shoes_of_St._Rilms.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Shoes_of_St._Rilms.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Shoes_of_St._Rilms.jpg) The Shoes of St. Rilms (Morrowind) The Shoes of St. Rilms the Barefooted are [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md) sacred to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md). They are magically enchanted to enhance endurance and make the wearer more personable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOSRA-1)</sup> [Saint Rilms](Lore_Rilms.md) gave away the shoes and did her best to appear as a beggar in order to better acquaint herself with the poor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOTS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Saint Rilms-3)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), a priest of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) has a vision in a dream about the Shoes of St. Rilms lying neglected in the depths of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md). The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was sent by the Temple to verify if the vision was true, and to retrieve the shoes from the ancient [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) ruins there. They discovered the shoes and brought them to the Temple for safekeeping. They later received the shoes as a reward for various services to the Temple.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOSR-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EL-5)</sup>
