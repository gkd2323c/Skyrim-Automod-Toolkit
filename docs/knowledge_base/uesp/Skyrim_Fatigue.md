# Fatigue


**Fatigue** is an attribute added in [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md). High fatigue decreases your total available [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md), represented by a dark region inside the magicka bar. As you become more fatigued, the effectiveness of [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) will begin to decline, and your ability to recover magicka and stamina will begin to suffer. You will need to periodically sleep in a bed in order to restore your fatigue. In Survival, the [Rested](Skyrim_Rested.md) or [Well Rested](Skyrim_Well_Rested.md) bonus is no longer granted automatically after [sleeping](Skyrim_Sleeping.md). If you sleep outside, you will not be able to get a restful night's sleep, waking up Drained at best. If you sleep in a bed indoors, you will receive the Rested or Well Rested bonus as usual.

Fatigue increases by 9 points every 0.48 in-game hours, up to a maximum of 960. [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) instead have their Fatigue increased by 7.65 and elven races have it increased by 6.75 every 0.48 hours.

Sleeping restores fatigue at a rate of 120 per hour. Sleeping outdoors cannot reduce fatigue below 200. Becoming Rested requires you to sleep until fatigue is 0, and the Rested status is lost when fatigue reaches 80.

The player's maximum magicka after the penalty from Fatigue is (Base Magicka)*(960-Fatigue)/801.

The magicka penalty from Fatigue begins once the player becomes Drained(160 Fatigue).

Exhaustion may lead to becoming [Addled](Skyrim_Disease.md#Addled).

While overencumbered, Stamina is drained by 2 points per second, and fatigue accrues at 4 times normal rate.

## Effects
| Fatigue <br> Value | Level | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 0–159 | Refreshed | No effect. |
| 160–339 | Drained | Total magicka is reduced. Magicka and stamina regenerate 25% slower. Beneficial potions are 20% less effective. |
| 340–559 | Tired | Total magicka is reduced. Magicka and stamina regenerate 50% slower. Beneficial potions are 40% less effective. You are 10% less resistant to [disease](Skyrim_Disease.md). |
| 560–799 | Weary | Total magicka is reduced. Magicka and stamina regenerate 75% slower. Beneficial potions are 60% less effective. You are 20% less resistant to disease. |
| 800-960 | Debilitated | Total magicka is reduced. Magicka and stamina no longer recover naturally. Beneficial potions are 80% less effective. You are 40% less resistant to disease. |

## Notes
- While in the [Deadlands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deadlands)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>, you will be immune to the effects of Fatigue.
