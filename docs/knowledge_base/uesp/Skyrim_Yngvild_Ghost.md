[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-creature-Yngvild_Ghost.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Yngvild_Ghost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Yngvild_Ghost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Yngvild_Ghost.jpg) One of the Ghosts of Yngvild **Yngvild Ghosts** are the result of continuous experimentation by the necromancer [Arondil](Skyrim_Arondil.md). They are the [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of young women captured by Arondil's undead minions, and are only found in [Yngvild](Skyrim_Yngvild.md). Devoid of free will, they are a purely female ensemble created to serve Arondil's every desire.

## Notes
- In the room behind Arondil there is an Yngvild Ghost sleeping in a bed. It is possible for [vampires](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) to feed from her despite her being a ghost.
