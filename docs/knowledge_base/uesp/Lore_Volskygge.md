[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Volskygge (also called **Lendoran Ruin**) is an ancient [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) barrow with a [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) found in the mountains of western [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). This ancient tomb is known for being the citadel of the [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) Volsung.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Volskygge Quest-2)</sup> The people of Solitude are cautious of the ruins and know it as a cursed place, a tomb where the dead still live.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Guard Dialogue-3)</sup>
