[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-npc-Vampire_Naturalist.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vampire_Naturalist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Naturalist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Naturalist.jpg) Vampire Naturalist The Vampire Naturalist is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) who can be found within the secret vampire lair in [Cronvangr Cave](Skyrim_Cronvangr_Cave.md). She is responsible for taming [Arachnia](Skyrim_Arachnia.md), a blue [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) she keeps locked in a nearby cage, whom she uses to harvest [frostbite venom](Skyrim_Potent_Frostbite_Venom.md) to torture her victims before drinking their blood.

She wields a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) or [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality in combat and wears [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) or [vampire robes](Skyrim_Vampire_Robes.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). She carries the [Spider Cage Key](Skyrim_Spider_Cage_Key.md) and [her journal](Skyrim_Vampire_Naturalist%27s_Journal.md), along with a [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), some [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). She drops a sample of [vampire dust](Skyrim_Vampire_Dust.md) upon death.

She spends her time wandering around the crafting area in the vampire lair, pausing to use the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) or stand over the desk next to Arachnia. She is hostile and you will need to kill her to obtain her key during the [related quest](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim_(quest).md).
