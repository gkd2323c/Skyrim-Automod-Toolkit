# Ring of Namira

The Ring of Namira is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) bestowed by [Namira](Lore_Namira.md) upon those who do her bidding.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup> The ring has been known to, at times, reflect damage back onto an attacker,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFRing-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBRing-5)</sup> while at other times being able to increase a person's stamina and restore their health if the wearer cannibalizes a corpse.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRRing-6)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), an [agent of the Blades](Lore_Agent.md) was awarded the ring by Namira in return for killing a [vampire ancient](Lore_Vampire.md) that had fallen out of favor with the Prince.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) was tasked by Namira to kill a group of priests of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) who were attempting to convert her followers in the ancient [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin of [Anga](Lore_Anga.md); once the task was complete, Namira awarded the Hero her ring.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) was awarded the ring for bringing a priest of Arkay to Namira's shrine in Reachcliff Cave, and then killing and eating him.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup>
