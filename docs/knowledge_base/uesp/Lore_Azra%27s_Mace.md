Azra's Mace is a powerful [mace](Lore_Weapons.md) created by [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md) that could inflict more damage than weapons of even [Daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) quality. In appearance, it is a dark metal mace engraved with a runic 'A', and a runic 'N', which are the initials of Azra's name. It was found by the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Soul_of_Conflict.md) in the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md) circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKAzra Mace-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Image-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-COH2-3)</sup>
