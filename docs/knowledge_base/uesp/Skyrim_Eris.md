[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Eris.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eris.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eris.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eris.jpg) Eris in the Red Wave **Eris** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) and a pirate aboard the [Red Wave](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md), under the command of [Safia](Skyrim_Safia.md).

Eris wears [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12). She carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), a leveled piece of jewelry, a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common), and an additional small amount of gold. She wields a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), which can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) in quality and knows the following spells: [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).
