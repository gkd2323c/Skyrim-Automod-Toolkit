[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Witch_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Witch_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Witch_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Witch_02.jpg) Witch (Frost) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-npc-Witch_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Witch_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Witch_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Witch_03.jpg) Witch (Shock) **Witches** are generic, hostile female NPCs that can be randomly encountered in two [similar](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Atronach_mis-summoned) [situations](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Witch_fighting_Atronach). There are three versions of witches who differ in their magic specialization and in terms of possible races:

| - The first version (`00074F74`) specializes in [Fire Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), and may be [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md), [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), or [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md). <br> - The second version (`00074F75`) specializes in [Frost Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage), and may be Altmer, Breton, Imperial, or [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md). <br> - The third version (`00074F76`) specializes in [Shock Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), and may be Altmer, Breton, Dunmer, or Imperial. |
