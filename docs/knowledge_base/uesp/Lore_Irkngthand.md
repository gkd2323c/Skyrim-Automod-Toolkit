[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Irkngthand_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Irkngthand_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Irkngthand_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Irkngthand_03.jpg) Irkngthand circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Irkngthand is a [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) tunneling beneath [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md) in [The Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md) hold of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Irkngthand is home to the only known depiction of the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) before the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) corrupted them into the modern [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRLoading-1)</sup> [The Great Statue of Irkngthand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Statues_Other_Statues#Great_Statue_of_Irkngthand) was built in secret by the Falmeri slaves and adorned by the two Eyes of the Falmer, which are giant gems of enormous value said burn into oneself upon viewing their magnificence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLW-2)</sup>

Irkngthand is theorized to be associated with the [Clan Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md), and was built in the Hoagen Kultorra style like many of the other strongholds of the Northern clans, featuring an Arcanex and a Deep Venue layout in its grand cavern.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DI:V2-3)</sup> It is rumored to be one of four Dwemer cities with an entrance into the underground cavern known as [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md) (found just across from [Raldbthar](Lore_Raldbthar.md) and its Great Lift), however no evidence of this entrance has been found, if it ever existed at all.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DI:V3-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Skyrim-5)</sup> Large portions of this ruin were flooded following [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) activity in the area in the year [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
