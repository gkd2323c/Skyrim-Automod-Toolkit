The Werewolf's Hide **by an Unknown Packleader** *A werewolf's greatest asset*
---
Some will tell you that [our](Lore_Werewolf.md) greatest strength is our hunger, or our numbers, or our rage, or our claws, or our fangs. These are fools. The gift of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) is not simply about weapons, but about defense.

To hunt in the great [hunting grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md) of our master, we must be impervious to pain, masters of our own bodies.

Many a werewolf hunter will seek your hide for this reason. They will try to wear it, or else burn it. Either way, it is your greatest prize and you should take care not to let it fall to filth and disrepair.

A wolf's coat is the marker of his status. It bears his scars and protects his body against harm. Treat it as a nobleman treats his finery, for you are a servant of Hircine.

Too often have I encountered feral wolves with mangy coats. You are not wild [dogs](Lore_Dog.md)! You are not senseless [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md) with no choice but to wander the woods as animals! You are kings among hunters!

When you are beset by enemies, when the mob comes for you, and you transform into your true self to face sword and sickle, pitchfork and pike, you will thank me. Your coat will gleam and terrify, and no blow will harm you.
