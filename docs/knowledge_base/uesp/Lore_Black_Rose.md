# Black Rose

This article is about the flower. For the city, see [Blackrose](Lore_Blackrose.md).

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-ingredient-Black_rose.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-ingredient-Black_rose.png) Black Rose *Thy race is as deadly as the thorns of
the black rose which blooms only
in thy mother's breast. Thou hast all that
is graceful in thy brothers of day,
yet thy mother is the moon,
and thou art her children of the night...*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDBA-1)</sup> The **Black Rose** is a flower with known prominence on the [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) mainland<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB-2)</sup> and the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TES2Rose-3)</sup> It is late-blooming and said to possess a sweet spicy smell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB-2)</sup> Within Morrowind, it is known to grow in every shadowy nook and crevice of the highlands, finding nourishment even in the stoniest banks and ridges.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB-2)</sup>

## Culture and Symbology
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/DF-quest-Azura%27s_Quest.gif/200px-DF-quest-Azura%27s_Quest.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Azura%27s_Quest.gif) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Azura%27s_Quest.gif) Azura, holding a black rose
### Morrowind
The flower is of some mythic significance to the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dark_Elf.md), their race is said to be as: "deadly as the thorns of the black rose which blooms only in thy mother's breast," with said mother being "the moon," and the Dark Elven folk being her "children of the night."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDBA-1)</sup> Subsequently, the Black Rose of Morrrowind is associated in the symbologies of [House Mora](Lore_House_Mora.md) and [Clan Ra'athim](Lore_Ra%27athim.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Mara Wasserman-4)</sup> who outfitted themselves in adornments resembling garlands, roses, thorns, and leaves.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KE-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-36LOVS22-6)</sup> [Urili Vox](Lore_Urili_Vox.md), the Magistrix of Morals for [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), once induced an Imperial painter and chronicler [Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md), into a "vision of the Fore Times," wherein he witnessed a vision of a Black Rose hovering before him, petals falling from a withering vine.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGT-7)</sup>

### High Rock
Black Roses are associated with "Daedric influence" and "dark nature magic."<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOB2-8)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOB1-9)</sup> And have some association with the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

## Notes
- [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) is seen holding a black rose during [her appearance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Azura) in [*Daggerfall*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-DF-11)</sup> one of her titles is Mother of the Rose<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TBOD-12)</sup> or Mother of the Roses.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AI-13)</sup>
- A [Black Rose item set](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Black_Rose) exists in [*ESO*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
