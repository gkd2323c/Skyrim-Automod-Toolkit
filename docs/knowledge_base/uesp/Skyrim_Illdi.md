[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-npc-Illdi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Illdi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Illdi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Illdi.jpg) Illdi **Illdi** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md) studying at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md). Although she respects her teacher, [Inge Six Fingers](Skyrim_Inge_Six_Fingers.md), and refers to the College as a *musical museum*, she is very insecure and claims that she is not very well liked by anyone.

Illdi wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bards_College) to the College along with a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"You're new here, right? Welcome to the Bards College. It's like a... musical museum."* *"You know, I don't get the impression that I'm very well liked at the College..."* *"I'm so glad you got back from [Olaf's Tomb](Skyrim_Dead_Men%27s_Respite.md) okay. They gave you a tougher task then they gave me."* **(After completing [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md))** *"Dean Inge says a bard can never have enough practice. So, would you like a tune?"* If spoken to:

**You play well.** *"Thank you. I'm still at the beginning of my lessons but Inge... Dean Six Fingers... says I'm quite good with the flute."* Goodbye:

*"Okay, I'll go now."* *"Thanks for taking the time. People never want to talk to me."*
### Conversations
**Inge**: *"Your solo performance is about a week away. I expect to hear your early rendition tomorrow, understand?"* **Illdi**: *"Oh, thank you! I've been working on an original piece. I hope it can stand up to the classics!"*
**Inge**: *"I'll be the judge of that. Now, back to your practice."* **Evette**: *"A bit of warming wine, Illdi. You know you can't resist."* **Illdi**: *"Not fair. You're well aware I can't seem to get warm here."* **or** *"Do you know I look forward to Burning of King Olaf each year just because of the fire?"* **Giraud**: *"Pardon me, but I trust you've been working on your poem, on the classical heroes of Skyrim, yes?"* **Illdi**: *"Why do we always focus on retelling the old stories? There are so many new stories being made, right now..."* **Giraud**: *"Patience, Illdi. We study the heroes of the past so we can have better understanding of the heroes of today... and tomorrow."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Diplomatic Immunity
Illdi can be seen at the party playing the flute, if you attempt to speak to her she can say:

*"I hope you're enjoying the music, sir/ma'am."* *"These parties are a great way to earn a little extra money."* *"It's good practice. Most people barely notice what I'm playing."*

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
