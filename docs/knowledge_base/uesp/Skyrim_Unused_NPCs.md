[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-npc-Captain_Metilius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Captain_Metilius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Metilius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Metilius.jpg) Captain Metilius **Captain Metilius** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [Imperial Soldier](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier_(class).md). He wears an [Imperial soldier outfit](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Imperial_Soldier_Armor) without a helmet, and carries an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow), 10 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), and a [steel greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword).

If both [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md) and [The Jagged Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Jagged_Crown) (either faction) are in your quest log, he stands near the war room in [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). He sleeps for eight hours at midnight, and wanders around Castle Dour from 8am to 8pm. The rest of the time he wanders near or around the castle.

Captain Metilius was meant to aid you in an earlier version of the quest [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md). In this quest's Legion variation (as there was a Stormcloak equivalent as well) [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) would've sent you alongside Metilius and a squad of generic friendly soldiers to investigate reports of a dragon at [Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_Fort_Hraggstad.md). Once there, you would find the fort under active attack by the dragon, and Metilius and the soldiers would help you defeat it, similar to [Irileth](Skyrim_Irileth.md) and the Whiterun guards in the final version of the quest. It seems that after this quest, he would not get sent out with you any longer, as there are no other relevant leftovers pointing to this.

I take good care of my land."* *"Do the bards still sing of my adventures?"* *"Time was, the name Eyldi the Bear could make brigands flee for miles around."* *"The world has changed since I was young. Let there be new heroes. I’ve earned a rest."* Due to her heroic feats, Stormcloak-aligned guards would have been all too familiar with Eyldi and could have said:

*"I grew up hearing songs about Eyldi the Bear! She wrestled a storm out of the sky!"* Imperial-aligned guards did not see Eyldi in the same light, however:

*"Just my luck, stuck in the sticks with this crazy old woman."*

**Ysleif** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and the [ferryman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ferryman) for [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). He wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1da), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd), and carries an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe).

In the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit), his Editor ID is `cc KRTSSE001_Ferryman Dawnstar BACKUP`, implying he was meant to be a backup in case [Harlaug](Skyrim_Harlaug.md) was dead. However, Harlaug cannot be killed under normal conditions as he is essential and is also set to respawn.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-npc-Voice_of_Namira.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Voice_of_Namira.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voice_of_Namira.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voice_of_Namira.jpg) Voice of Namira The **Voice of Namira** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade) who exists in a cell to the east of the dining room in [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave). She can only be seen by using either the Aura Whisper shout or Detect Life spell. Although she cannot be killed, it is possible to hit her with the Chain Lightning spell if you are pressed against the dining room's eastern wall, thus causing you to be charged with an assault. She wears no clothes, carries no items and cannot otherwise be interacted with.

If you do manage to get to her cell, you can attack her like other NPCs, but not kill her. Doing so will result in a bounty for [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). It is possible to reach her via the [Whirlwind Sprint exploit](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md#Notes) at the rightmost sarcophagus on the eastern wall of the dining hall in Reachcliff Cave.
