[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Ra%27zhinda.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ra%27zhinda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ra%27zhinda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ra%27zhinda.jpg) Ra'zhinda **Ra'zhinda** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](Skyrim_TrainerLockpickExpert.md) who travels with the [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) with [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md) and [Ma'jhad](Skyrim_Ma%27jhad.md), between [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

Ra'zhinda wears a set of [steel armor](Skyrim_Steel_Armor.md), including a pair of steel gauntlets (two variants), and a pair of steel boots (two variants). She is equipped with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and a [steel shield](Skyrim_Steel_Shield.md), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Notes
- Her ref actually bears the name **Ra'zhin-Dar**, implying she was at one point meant to be a male with said name. - The base game version of Oblivion's *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV:_Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions "Ra'zhin-Dar" under the male Khajiit "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".
- Her factions and class name seem to suggest Ra'zhinda was at one point meant to be an Expert Lockpicking trainer. However, no dialogue is present for her to offer any training. It seems that this role was instead given to one of her traveling partners, [Ma'jhad](Skyrim_Ma%27jhad.md).
