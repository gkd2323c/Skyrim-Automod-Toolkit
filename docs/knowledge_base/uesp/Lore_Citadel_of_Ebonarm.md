# Citadel of Ebonarm

[![](https://images.uesp.net/2/2d/DF-demo-Ebonarm.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-demo-Ebonarm.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-demo-Ebonarm.png) Ebonarm's emblem The Citadel of Ebonarm is the religious order devoted to worshipping [Ebonarm](Lore_Ebonarm.md), the God of War. They are allied with the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and they are enemies of the [Temple of Stendarr](Lore_Temple_of_Stendarr.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COE-1)</sup> They lead a Templar Knightly Order called the Battlelords.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Battlelords-2)</sup> Fighters Guild guildhalls in the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) region of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) are used as places of worship by the Citadel, and are considered holy ground.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COE-1)</sup>
