[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Shearpoint (also called **Shearpoint Mountain**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONDS-1)</sup> is a mountain found in the region known as [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The mountain marks a point where the borders of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), the Pale, and [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) all meet at once. There are several [Dwemer ruins](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) found on the mountain's north face, such as the former city-state of [Raldbthar](Lore_Raldbthar.md). The [White River](Lore_White_River.md) forms a valley that separates Shearpoint and the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md), while the snowfields surrounding [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md) separate the mountain from [Mount Anthor](Lore_Mount_Anthor.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SR-2)</sup>

## Geography
Shearpoint is part of the greater **Pale Mountains** that line the borders of the Hold, also known as the **Shearpoint Range**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:454</sup>

## History
Underneath the mountain was once a large deposit of [Aetherium](Lore_Aetherium.md) that was heavily extracted by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) clans of Skyrim in their prime. The city-state [Raldbthar](Lore_Raldbthar.md) was the main source for the blue crystal.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KJ-4)</sup> At the summit of Shearpoint is the burial ground of the [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md), [Krosis](Lore_Krosis.md), which later became a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) lair.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SR-2)</sup> A stone [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) was also built on the summit, dedicated to Oskar the Fool, whose [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) was not as strong as his clan's signature voice. It was built by his brother, [Modir the Far](Lore_Modir_the_Far.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRThrow Voice-5)</sup>
