[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/SR-npc-Lodi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lodi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lodi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lodi.jpg) Lodi **Lodi** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) and educated bard performing at [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md)'s wedding during the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md).

He only exists during the quest and can be found playing his flute in the courtyard outside the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b).

## Related Quests
- **[Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md)**: Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) at her own wedding.

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Bound Until Death
If you approach him for conversation he will brush you off with:

- *"Sorry, no time to talk. I need to keep playing."*

If you manage to catch him during the aftermath of the murder, he will comment:

- *"I never should have taken this job!"*
