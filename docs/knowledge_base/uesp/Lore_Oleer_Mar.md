Oleer Mar is a settlement hosting hunters on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), in the vicinity of the [Ensleth Valley](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ensleth_Valley).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:150</sup>

## History
In [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), [Attrebus Mede](Lore_Attrebus_Mede.md) and [Sul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels/Sul) arrived in the settlement of [Sathil](Lore_Sathil.md). The owner of the Char Bucket inn asked Attrebus and Sul if they came from Oleer Mar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:150</sup>
