[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg/200px-ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg) Seat of Detritus Detritus is a ghastly demi-plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) ruled over by [Hollowjack](Lore_Hollowjack.md), the Lord of Mortal Fears. It has also been described as a "half-world", and is a realm filled with untold horrors. Every year, during the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md) on the 13<sup>th</sup> of Frost Fall, the portals between [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and Detritus open and gruesome items from this realm flood the markets of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> According to an enigmatic and immortal entity known as [the Impresario](Lore_The_Impresario.md), Detritus is a "carving" from the shell of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LATH-2)</sup>

Hollowjack derives his supernatural power from metaphysically feeding on the fear of mortals, and his realm is dedicated to this singular purpose. Detritus is described as a claustrophobic series of small spaces cluttered and jammed with piles of mortals' lost and broken personal items. Hollowjack frivols there among his collection of shattered memories, categorizing and classing and replaying them to find new vulnerabilities in the mortal mind. There is no outside on Detritus, only room after room of recurring nightmares and internal torments. According to Hollowjack, this is because "mortals fear most what is inside themselves".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LHDR-3)</sup>

Relics commonly taken from this realm include [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -Skulls, Flame-Skulls, and [Wraith](Lore_Wraith.md) -Lanterns.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Eerie [horses](Lore_Horse.md), [senche](Lore_Senche.md), and [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md) are sometimes summoned from this realm to be used as mounts, although some maintain that these creatures are simply the result of [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) magic.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Hollowjack-style armor and weapons are crafted using [pumpkin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pumpkin), black hawthorn, and [amber marble](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Amber_Marble).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CM42-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
