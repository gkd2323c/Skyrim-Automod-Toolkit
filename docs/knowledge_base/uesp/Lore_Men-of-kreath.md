# Men-of-kreath

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/ON-npc-The_Forest_King.jpg/200px-ON-npc-The_Forest_King.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Forest_King.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Forest_King.jpg) [Kestic, The Forest King](Lore_Kestic.md) The Men-of-kreath (also written as **men-of-'kreath**) were an ancient tribe of [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people. They lived in the northern parts of the [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) and early [First](Lore_First_Era.md) eras and were among the men herded as [slaves](Lore_Slavery.md) from across the [Niben](Lore_Niben.md) by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md). They were later known to be imported from the North.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TA-1)</sup> During the times of [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) the northern holdings retaken from the Ayleids were consolidated for the Men-of-kreath after the first Pogrom.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOP-2)</sup> Based off their name and inhabitation of "the North", they may have originated from the region of [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) (though it was sometimes considered part of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) in earlier history),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-R-3)</sup> or another location such as Dunkreath.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ST-4)</sup> As they are not described as being [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), the men-of-'kreath seem to have been a distinct group of [humans](Lore_Man.md) that were indigenous to the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOP-2)</sup>

## Notes
- It is possible that at some point in history they were led by [Kestic](Lore_Kestic.md), who was the ruler of the northern tribes of Nedes.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SD-5)</sup>
- The name "Falkreath" derives from [Elvish](Lore_Elvish.md), and by extension so does the name "Men-of-kreath", though its meaning is unknown.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-6)</sup><sup>:573</sup>
