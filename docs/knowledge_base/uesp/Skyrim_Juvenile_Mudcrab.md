[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-ingredient-Juvenile_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Juvenile_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Juvenile_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Juvenile_Mudcrab.jpg) Juvenile Mudcrab **Juvenile Mudcrab** is an ingredient added by the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It can be caught by [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md) or picked up by hand near [Mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) areas.

During the related quest, you will be directed to a fishing spot west of [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) to obtain a Juvenile Mudcrab. However, they can also be caught elsewhere.

## Related Quests
- **[In A Pinch](Skyrim_In_A_Pinch.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Obtain a juvenile mudcrab for research.

## Locations Found
- [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) - In the marshy areas surrounding Whiterun, anywhere that there are larger mudcrabs is a possible location for juvenile mudcrabs
- [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md) - 1
- [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) - 1
- [Sea Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sea_Cave) - 1
- [Wreck of the Winter War](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Winter_War) - 1 (With Alik'ri Fishing Rod equipped)
