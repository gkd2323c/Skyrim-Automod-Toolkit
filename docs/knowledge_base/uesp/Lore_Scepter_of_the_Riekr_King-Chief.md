Scepter of the Riekr King-Chief *Notes on an ancient Orcish artifact*
---
A powerful [Riekr](Lore_Riekr.md) who refers to himself as "the King-Chief" currently terrorizes the land to the northeast of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). He wields an ancient scepter that the museum believes was crafted by Kloth, first chief of the [Orgak](Lore_Orgak_Clan.md) Orc clan that once flourished in northern [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). It was a symbol of power and authority for the Orc clan and appears to be used in similar fashion by the self-styled king-chief.

No [Orsimer](Lore_Orc.md) can rest while this historic relic remains in the hands of a lowly Riekr—no matter how powerful that lowly Riekr may be.
