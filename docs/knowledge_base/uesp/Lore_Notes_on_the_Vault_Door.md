Notes on the Vault Door *Notes found on the vault door of Razak* *
---
The door to Razak's vault is one of the most impressive feats of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) engineering I've seen. It resists every unlock spell I know, and shrugs off even the strongest [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) spells.

Perhaps the most fascinating part of the door is the series of pictograms etched into the door itself. They seem to form a narrative. And if my admittedly limited knowledge of the language serves me, it's the classic [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) cosmogony:

First there was [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md), the formless chaos from which the universe was formed. Then [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) convinced the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) to sacrifice themselves to create the world, [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). Then finally, at the birth of the world, [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md) opened a hole to [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md), creating the sun and letting [magic](Lore_Magic.md) flow into [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).

This trinary comes up frequently in Razak texts. Chaos, earth, and magic. Perhaps it's central to his understanding of creation, not just of the world, but of constructs as well?

I will have to ruminate on this later. For now I'll try my hand at picking the lock. Perhaps I'll have success with the mundane where magic failed.
