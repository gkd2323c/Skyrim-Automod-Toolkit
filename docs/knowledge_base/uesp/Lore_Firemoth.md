[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/MW-place-Firemoth_Region.jpg/200px-MW-place-Firemoth_Region.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Firemoth_Region.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Firemoth_Region.jpg) Firemoth region circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Firemoth (also known as **Firemoth Island** or the **Firemoth Region**) is a region encompassing a ring of barren islands located in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md). The islands often suffer heavy storms, and the bare volcanic rock provides little nourishment for flora. Hardy [trama shrubs](Lore_Trama.md) and stark, leafless trees can survive the ashen, bitter soil, and [marshmerrow reeds](Lore_Marshmerrow.md) flourish along the sandy beaches, but otherwise the islands are devoid of life.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/ON-place-Firemoth_Island_Docks_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Firemoth_Island_Docks_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Firemoth_Island_Docks_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Firemoth_Island_Docks_02.jpg) Firemoth circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/ON-load-Firemoth_Island.jpg/200px-ON-load-Firemoth_Island.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Firemoth_Island.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Firemoth_Island.jpg) View from the main island In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the region's climate was milder and Firemoth was much more lush with vegetation, resembling the nearby swamps of the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md). It was home to a large array of flora and fauna, including Netch Shields, Stinkcaps, Milk Caps, Spongecaps, [mudcrabs](Lore_Mudcrab.md), [shroom beetles](Lore_Shroom_Beetle.md), and even [alits](Lore_Alit.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMW-2)</sup> The island was also home to a unique flame-hued variant of [nix-ox](Lore_Nix-Ox.md). Known as the [Firemoth Nix-Ox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Firemoth_Nix-Ox), these beasts have a fiery temperament and are well suited as combat steeds.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nix-3)</sup> At some point, [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) maintained a fortification on the island known as Fort Firemoth. However, the Redoran allowed the fort to go fallow, and [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) exploited this abandonment to begin encroaching upon ancestral Redoran territory on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> In time, the Hlaalu were successful in driving the Redoran out of major regional settlements such as [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) and [Suran](Lore_Suran.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a band of slavers established themselves on the island, making use of the Redoran compound and docks. After arriving on the island, the slavers found and trained the Firemoth Nix-Ox for use as mounts.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nix-3)</sup> They worked in collaboration with Governor Omellian of [Seyda Neen](Lore_Seyda_Neen.md)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CN-6)</sup> to use the Grand Pharos lighthouse in Seyda Neen to capsize passing ships and sell the captives into slavery, which was illegal in [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) territory. This plot was ended when Deputy Governor Deminah Salvi learned of Omellian's treachery and sent soldiers to clear out the slavers and liberate their captives.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOBTTF-7)</sup> Simultaneously, an agent of the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) named [Naryu Virian](Lore_Naryu_Virian.md) arrived at Firemoth with a writ to assassinate the leader of the slavers, Captain Svadstar. She broke into the compound and helped the captives overrun the island, before boarding Svadstar's ship and assassinating him while he was engaged with the forces from Seyda Neen.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOBB-8)</sup>

### Third Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/MW-place-Fort_Firemoth.jpg/200px-MW-place-Fort_Firemoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Fort_Firemoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Fort_Firemoth.jpg) Fort Firemoth By the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the archipelago had turned barren. The [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) built a new Fort Firemoth made of stone, housed on the main island. Once a strategic strong point guarding the Inner Sea from smugglers, it was overrun when an undead army led by the [lich](Lore_Lich.md) [Grurn](Lore_Grurn.md) marched out of the ocean and captured the garrison, leaving few survivors.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWSellus-9)</sup> While still technically Imperial territory, the islands remained occupied by the skeleton army and were never reclaimed. The fort was built on top of a rich [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) mine, which broke through into flooded natural caverns and an ancient Velothi tomb.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) a band of adventurers hired by the Imperial Legion set sail for Firemoth from Seyda Neen, infiltrated the ruins of Fort Firemoth, slew Grurn, and retrieved the [Ward of Akavir](Lore_Ward_of_Akavir.md), a precious enchanted shield.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWSAF-10)</sup>
