[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-creature-Frenzied_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Frenzied_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frenzied_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frenzied_Mudcrab.jpg) A Frenzied Mudcrab holding a Flaming Pot **Frenzied Mudcrabs** are hostile [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) encountered emerging from the sea near a fisherman's shack west of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

[Viriya](Skyrim_Viriya.md) will send you to deal with several waves of these ferocious crabs during the quest [Crustacean Extermination](Skyrim_Crustacean_Extermination.md). The crabs will return in even greater numbers during the subsequent quest [Wave Breaker](Skyrim_Wave_Breaker.md). After defeating enough of them, the [Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit](Skyrim_Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.md) will emerge from the ocean and attack. Some of the frenzied mudcrabs will be holding flaming pots, which you will need to loot to defeat the Emperor Crab.
