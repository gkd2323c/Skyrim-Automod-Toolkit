It was released on October 4, 2017 for 200 Creation Credits. The hammer first [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Stendarr%27s_Hammer) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*'s *[Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Tribunal)* [expansion pack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Expansion_Packs).

## Official Summary
*Rumored to have been wielded by Stendarr, the God of Righteous Might and Merciful Forbearance himself, this two-handed hammer will deliver devastation to any foe. (Weapon can be stolen from the Dwemer Museum in Markarth.)*
## Overview
Travel to the [Dwemer Museum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to steal Stendarr's Hammer. Note that without a key and [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md)'s permission, entry to the Museum is illegal and the guards inside will attempt to arrest you.

The hammer can be found inside the museum in the Southwest corner and taking it isn't considered theft.

Stendarr's Hammer is a very heavy [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer) with a [damage health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md) enchantment which drains your [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) with each hit.
