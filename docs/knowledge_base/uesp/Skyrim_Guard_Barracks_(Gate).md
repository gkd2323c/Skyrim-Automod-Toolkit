## Guard Barracks
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_%28Gate%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_%28Gate%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_(Gate).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_(Gate).jpg.md) Guard Barracks interior The barracks consists of two cramped rooms, a dining room often used by a single [guard](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md), and a sleeping area with four owned beds. The dining room contains two chairs by a table, a minor loot chest, a food barrel, and four food sacks. The small table seats two and has some food on display, two [baked potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). A set of shelves contains a chunk of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef), the loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), and [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese).

The sleeping area contains four beds squeezed head-to-toe between the north and south walls with an end table with clutter between them. The end table to the east has a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and two [honey nut treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat) on top and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) on the floor below. A copy of *[Beggar Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beggar_Prince)* is carelessly thrown on the floor in the northeastern corner. The shelves along the northern wall hold two bottles of wine, two bottles of ale, and a head of [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). The cupboard under the shelves holds clutter. Under the cupboard sits a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) and, placed on a stool, a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat).

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Danger [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) around the right corner from the door and double marked low down on the wall and a wooden support.
- If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) takes over Whiterun during [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md), nobody will live in the barracks and all items will be free to take.
- There are four food barrels on the roof of the barracks.
