# Reman Purges

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-place-Fang_Lair_01.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fang_Lair_01.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fang_Lair_01.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fang_Lair_01.jpg) Guillotine made for the purges The Reman Purges (ca. [2E 4](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_4) - [2E 320](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_320))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot FG-1)</sup> was a period in the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s history after the assassination of [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md) and the fall of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md). After the Second Empire's conquest of the most of the continent, [Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) began persecuting people who opposed his rule and were loyal to the Reman Dynasty, even after Reman III's death, across Tamriel.

In order to suppress armies remaining loyal to the Remans, as well as the armies belonging to individual kingdoms seeking greater autonomy, Versidue-Shaie was forced to engage in a relentless series of campaigns that, by the year [2E 320](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_320), finally resulted in victory for his forces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot FG-1)</sup> The cost of the campaigns was immense, resulting in the impoverishment of both the empire and its constituent kingdoms and in an effort to maintain order without the expense of a standing army, in [2E 321](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_321) Versidue-Shaie agreed to a proposal from his kinsman Dinieras-Ves that established what was to become known as the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FGC-2)</sup> Numerous dubious tales of his campaigns have been told over the years, most involving various cavalries.

## History
In [2E 4](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_4) the newly elected Potentate established wolf riders of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) to start killing and hunting Reman loyalists.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Pet Wolf-3)</sup> At one point they caused a panic in [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mount Wolf-4)</sup> Next year, [2E 5](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_5), various companies across the continent got the same orders to hunt loyalists.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Pet Charger-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mount Charger-6)</sup> [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) was visited by the Potentate sabre cat mounted regiment in [2E 7](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_7).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Mount Sabre Cat-7)</sup> The sabre cats were, however, brought in from another province.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Pet Sabre Cat-8)</sup>

In [2E 11](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_11), the Potentate converted the isle of [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) into a prison colony for Reman Dynasty loyalists. The prisoners were sent to the island's [larimar](Lore_Larimar.md) and [ammonite](Lore_Ammonite.md) mines.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Systres-9)</sup> [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) under the Reman Dynasty was treated as a prison state, and the Potentate continued in this treatment.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1Arg-10)</sup> In addition to Amenos, the Potentate ordered the construction of the infamous [Blackrose Prison](Lore_Blackrose_Prison.md) for everyone accused of treason, by his mage advisor [Pelladil Direnni](Lore_Pelladil_Direnni.md), specifically chosen for its location.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AHOBR-11)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Systres-9)</sup>

The Imperial Bear Cavalry was established in [2E 23](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_23) in and around the [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) region.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Pet Bear-12)</sup> According to some sources, they performed weekly parades around the city and were used by the [10th Legion](Lore_Tenth_Legion.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Mount Bear-13)</sup> In [2E 25](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_25) the Reman desert pursuit occurred in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), where several herds of camels were used in loyalist hunting activities.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Mount Camel-14)</sup> To hunt Reman loyalists in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), the Potentate hired [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) assassins on trained [guars](Lore_Guar.md) in [2E 27](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_27).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Mount Guar-15)</sup> The guar army was deployed on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), but the guars themselves were allegedly trained in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Pet Guar-16)</sup>

In the year [2E 30](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_30) the first seemingly-spectral Cloudracer steeds were deployed, presumably named so for their speed.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Mount Charger Apex-17)</sup> The ephemeral and ghostly Halcyon Senche was first ridden in either [2E 29](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_29) or [2E 35](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_35).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Mount Sabre Cat Apex-18)</sup> [2E 42](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_42) was the first year that stealth rituals were noted to be used to track down traitors of the state.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Mount Wolf Apex-19)</sup> The Akaviri Potentates are thought to have invented guillotines during the Purges to cut off the heads of loyalists.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ONBlackmarrow-20)</sup>
