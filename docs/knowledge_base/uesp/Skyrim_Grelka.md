[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Grelka.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grelka.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelka.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelka.jpg) Grelka **Grelka** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) merchant who resides in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She is the owner of one of the stalls in the center of town and will sell you weapons, apparel and various other miscellaneous items. She has a starting amount of 750 gold to barter with. She also offers expert [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md).

Grelka currently lives in [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md). She starts off her day by waking up at 7am and has breakfast at [the Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) for an hour. After that, she will work at her market stall at Riften Grand Plaza for a full twelve hours until 8pm. After selling items all day, she will return to the bunkhouse to have her dinner, and she has the rest of the evening free. At 1am, she goes to sleep for six hours and repeats her schedule the following day.

She wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), with matching pairs of [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots), excluding the helmet. She carries a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grelka%27s_Market_Key) to her market stall, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
While she runs her stall, she will try to advertise her goods:

*"Looking to stay alive? Why take a chance?"* *"Buy some armor and live to tell about it!"* *"Protect yourself right... buy armor from Grelka."* She will notice if you approach, though her tone will sour:

*"You going to buy something or just here for training? Otherwise, move on."* *"You gonna stare all day or buy some armor?"* *"Come on, come on. The sooner you buy, the sooner I can get drunk."* Seeing her outside her stall will net you the same surliness:

*"Can't you bother me at my armor stand in the market? Or are you that desperate for training?"* When spoken to:

**You treat all your customers like this?** *"Look, I've moved all over Skyrim selling these bits of junk and I'm barely scraping by. I'm not out to win friends and I could care less if you're happy about your purchase or not. I just need the coin. The sooner I get out of Skyrim the better."* **Why would you want to leave Skyrim?** *"Are you serious? The [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) is tearing the land apart, the [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) have returned seeking who knows what... Skyrim is going nowhere fast. If I were you, I'd pack up and get out of here before you end up in one of those burial cairns."* **I need training in Light Armor.** *"I'll teach you how to use it effectively, and keep you quick on your feet."* **What's the Ratway?** *"There are ruined sewer tunnels beneath the city. The only way I know how to get in there is through a door off of the canal. If I were you, I'd keep my feet dry and stay up here."* **Tell me about the Thieves Guild.** *"They'd stop at nothing to take your last bit of food if that's all you had. The Ratway's too good of a place for that scum."* When you end your conversation with her:

*"Come back when you're ready to spend more gold... goodness knows I could use it."*

## Unused Conversations
Grelka originally had the role that [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md) fills in the final game. She was to be the sole female miner living in [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md), as well as the daughter of [Verner Rock-Chucker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker) and [Annekke Crag-Jumper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Annekke_Crag-Jumper) from [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md). One of the biggest leftovers of this role are these three scenes that were meant to play out between Grelka and the other miners in town. Sylgja does not share any unique scenes with them in the final game, likely as a result of this role change.

**[Odfel](Skyrim_Odfel.md)**: *"Grelka, do you fancy a walk tomorrow?"* **Grelka**: *"Odfel, I've told you time and time again. I'm not interested in a relationship with you."* **Odfel**: *"And why not? I'm handsome, strong and smart. What else could you possibly need?"* **Grelka**: *"I need someone that cares about me more than they care about themselves. That person isn't you."* **[Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md)**: *"How are your parents faring, Grelka? Doing better than we are I would guess."* **Grelka**: *"It's no feast in their neck of the woods either. Last time I was up there, father said they were barely scraping by."* **Filnjar**: *"This civil war is taking its toll on all of us. Miners are some of the hardest hit."* **Grelka**: *"That's what scares me. I'm afraid I'll travel to Darkwater Crossing and find it burned to the ground."* **[Grogmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grogmar_gro-Burzag)**: *"I've been watching the way you mine and I have to say, I'm very impressed."* **Grelka**: *"I owe it all to my father who started me swinging the pickaxe when I was a barefoot little girl."* **Grogmar**: *"Well, it shows. Your skill rivals some of the greatest miners I've known."* **Grelka**: *"Thank you. It's nice to know someone admires me for more than just my appearance."*

## Notes
- Strangely, the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* notes the following in the section about [Gray Pine Goods](Skyrim_Gray_Pine_Goods.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md):

*"[Solaf](Skyrim_Solaf.md) and [Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md)'s sister Grelka left them for Riften a while ago, sick of their attitudes. Solaf and Bolund don't particularly miss her."*
This information is either obsolete or incorrect, as there are no indicators to be found of this in the final game or its files. Grelka originally filled Sylgja's role (see [above](#Unused_Conversations)) and was then moved over to Riften. There is no known connection between her and the brothers. - Like Sylgja in the final game, Grelka was originally supposed to give out the quest [Special Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Special_Delivery). While she doesn't have any leftover dialogue, two unused miscellaneous items called [Package for Grelka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Package_for_Grelka) and [Package for Verner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Package_for_Verner) still exist and would've been used originally.
- Grelka is in the `Potential Marriage Faction` but has no way to raise her disposition through normal means. She shares this distinction with Odfel, her ex-love interest. - It's likely that this is also a result of her role change with Sylgja, as her involvement in Special Delivery was probably meant to make her available to marry, though the faction wasn't removed from her after she got her quest ties removed.
- In the scene she has with Tythis, Grelka asks him to bring his boots over to "her house", but Grelka does not own a house in town. Both her and Tythis reside at Haelga's Bunkhouse.
