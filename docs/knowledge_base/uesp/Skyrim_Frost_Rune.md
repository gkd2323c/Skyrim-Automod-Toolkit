**Frost Rune** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, causing [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to health and stamina. You can't cast it just anywhere. When you press the cast button, the crosshair will let you know if you are pointing at a valid surface within range. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- [Frost Rune](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 50 pts for 1 sec

## Perks
- [Rune Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rune_Master) increases range to 100 feet.

## Notes
- You can set off frost runes (your own or others) using any other Destruction spell from a distance.
