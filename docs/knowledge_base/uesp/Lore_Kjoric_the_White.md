Kjoric the White was the High King/High Chieftain of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), after the reign of High King [Olaf One-Eye](Lore_Olaf_One-Eye.md) and before his son, [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md). In his lifetime, he was a soft-spoken leader and attained power across the province when the [Crown of Verity](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crown_of_Verity) chose him over the [moot](Lore_Moot.md) -chosen warrior. Ever since then, no one argued the authority of the Crown.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo F-1)</sup>

## History
Following the end of Olaf One-Eye's reign, the moot was held to chose the next High King. Kjoric the White was chosen to represent his hold, much like other members of the meeting. In the end, they decided on the tribal chieftain, Asurn Ice-Breaker, but the Crown of Verity had rejected him, and his rage, summoned his loyalist and threatened to kill every member of the moot. Just at that moment, Kjoric challenged him and came out victorious. When he took the crown, it sat perfectly on his head and he was proclaimed the High King.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo F-1)</sup>

His prestige as the High King brought him to the coronation of Emperor [Gorieus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorieus) of the [First Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in [1E 461](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_461), alongside his son, Hoag and other notable figures, such as [Darloc Brae](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Darloc_Brae) of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), and the two leaders of the [First Council](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:First_Council), [Indoril Nerevar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Indoril_Nerevar) and [Dumac Dwarfking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dumac_Dwarfking). But despite this, the High King and the Emperor's acquaintanceship ended sometime later when Kjoric's forces faced the Empire at [Sungard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sungard). Kjoric was stricken down in his homeland, in the center of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:574</sup> and the moot commenced once again to determine the next in line. His son, Hoag Merkiller, was chosen as the next High King of Skyrim and continued his father's fight against the First Empire.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RTR-3)</sup>
