[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-creature-Torom.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Torom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Torom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Torom.jpg) Torom **Torom** is [Erith](Skyrim_Erith.md)'s pet [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md). He can be found in [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine), just outside [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). His owner Erith will often say *"Have you met Torom? He's the best dog in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md)."* If you kill Torom, Erith and [Daighre](Skyrim_Daighre.md) will be upset.

If Erith becomes orphaned and you [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) her, Torom will come with her to your [house](Skyrim_Houses.md).
