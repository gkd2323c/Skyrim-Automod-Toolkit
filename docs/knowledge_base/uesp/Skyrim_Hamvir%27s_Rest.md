[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-place-Hamvir%27s_Rest.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hamvir%27s_Rest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hamvir%27s_Rest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hamvir%27s_Rest.jpg) Hamvirs Rest **Hamvir's Rest** is a small outdoor graveyard and [Nordic ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings) northwest of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) containing [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) and a [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md).

## Walkthrough
The graveyard is just north of [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md). Three skeletons patrol the area, and a draugr sleeps in the upright sarcophagus. A master-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) is beside the sarcophagus, with a random enchanted [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md) behind the grates to its left. Eight [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plants, two [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), and two [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) plants grow among the tombstones and in the immediate area.

This is the final destination of the [Headless Horseman](Skyrim_Headless_Horseman.md) on his nightly travels; once he reaches this area, he will disappear regardless of the time of day.

## Notes
- An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is west-southwest of the ruin.
