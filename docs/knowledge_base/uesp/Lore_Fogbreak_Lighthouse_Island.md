[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-place-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.jpg) Fogbreak Lighthouse circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Fogbreak Lighthouse Island is an island located within the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md), named for **Fogbreak Lighthouse** which can be found atop its highest point.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> It lies north of the [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), southeast of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md), southwest of [Y'ffelon](Lore_Y%27ffelon.md), and west of [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SSB-2)</sup> It is surrounded by a ring of smaller rocks and islets. The flora on the island consists of gorse, beech, cypress, pine, dogwood, sea daffodil, and lizard tail. Additionally, [coral](Lore_Coral.md) formations can be found along the shore.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/ON-load-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.png/200px-ON-load-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Fogbreak_Lighthouse.png) The inside of Fogbreak Lighthouse Little is known of the island's history, but the presence of the [drudic](Lore_Druid.md) architecture as well as megalith constructions such as the "tale pillars" of the [Stonelore Circle](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md) is proof that Fogbreak Lighthouse Island was once inhabited by the druids, most likely of the Stonelore Circle. The presence of druidic buildings, carvings, and cultural objects, as well as more modern [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) architecture in the form of Fogbreak Lighthouse, suggest that the island's ownership changed at some point during its history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the property on the isle was available for purchase as a private residence for those who were interested in the cozy life of a lighthouse keeper. As of then, the main property on the island was Fogbreak Lighthouse, also referred to simply as Fogbreak, which included a large druidic undercroft, a beacon to help ships navigate the dangerous waters, and plenty of outdoor space. It was known for its stunning views of the sea.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Fogb-3)</sup>
