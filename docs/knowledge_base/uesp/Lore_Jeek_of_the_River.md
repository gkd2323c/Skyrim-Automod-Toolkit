Jeek of the River was Captain of the [Jorrvaskr](Lore_Jorrvaskr.md) during [the Return](Lore_The_Return.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> He and [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) had grown up together, and the twenty-one [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) who composed his crew were Ysgramor's closest friends.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOTR7-2)</sup> Ysgramor himself gave him his name.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOTR7-2)</sup> He was the discoverer of the [Skyforge](Lore_Skyforge.md), founder of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), and keeper of the original oath of the Companions, which was lost to time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> While other crews sought glory in conquest, his was the first to settle and serve as protector for the settlers who followed them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Emp-3)</sup>
