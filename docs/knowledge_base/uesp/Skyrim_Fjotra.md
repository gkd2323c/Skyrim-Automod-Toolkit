[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-npc-Fjotra.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fjotra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fjotra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fjotra.jpg) Fjotra **Fjotra** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who is initially found in [Broken Tower Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt). She has been kidnapped by a group of [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) and imprisoned in their redoubt. She is the daughter of [Enmon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enmon) and [Mena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mena) from [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md).

She is the new sybil of Dibella, and you will be tasked with bringing her back to the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

Fjotra wears red colored [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8).

## Related Quests
- **[The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md)**: Find a new Sybil for the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Heart of Dibella
When first spoken to:

*"Are you one of them? Please don't hurt me."* **I'm here to bring you to the Temple in Markarth.** *"Then it is true what they said? I am touched by the gods?"* **You're going to be the Sybil of Dibella.** *"I've heard stories about the wonders of the great Temple in Markarth. But I never dreamed that I would even get to see it. I am honored to be called for this duty. Please, lead on."* When spoken to while she's following you:

*"Please, let's hurry to the Temple before the Forsworn find me again."* *"I can't wait to learn from the priestesses."* *"I hope we reach Markarth soon."*
## Conversations
If Enmon is with you and you go to Karthwasten with Fjotra, this conversation will occur:

**Fjotra**: *"Father!"* **Enmon**: *"Fjotra, dear. Did they hurt you?"* **Fjotra**: *"Of course not. My spirit remains strong."* **Enmon**: *"My little girl. Always strong. Do you know where you have to go?"* **Fjotra**: *"I'm to go to the temple in Markarth. I... I'll have to leave you and mother."* **Enmon**: *"It's our honor to have you there. This man is going to take you to Markarth. Are you ready?"* **Fjotra**: *"Of course."*
## Notes
- Although both her mother and father are [Bretons](Skyrim_Breton.md), Fjotra is a Nord, suggesting that she was adopted. According to the [Prima Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide) she is a Reachwoman.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:332</sup>
- Fjotra is the only child [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) in the game. As a child, she is invulnerable to attacks, making her useful as bait in a fight.
