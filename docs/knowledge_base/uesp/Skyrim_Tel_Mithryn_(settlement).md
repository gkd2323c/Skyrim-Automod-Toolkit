[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg/200px-SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Tel_Mithryn.jpg) Tel Mithryn **Tel Mithryn** is a Telvanni tower [settlement](Skyrim_Settlements.md) east of [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).

The settlement is home to [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) Master Wizard [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md). Its four mushroom structures were grown near the beginning of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) out of special fungal spores from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The structures include the [main tower](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md), the [apothecary](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.md), the [kitchen](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.md), and the [steward's house](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_House.md). Master Neloth has a dubious reputation on the island, but is instrumental in progressing the *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* questline and offers other [unique services](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md) to the enterprising adventurer.

Each of the structures is accessible by a long organic ramp ascending to a round wooden door. A [heart stone deposit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone_Deposit) and a convenient [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) are behind the kitchen, with two more deposits on the cliffs near the coast to the southeast, while a few [bleeding crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown) and [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) fungi and [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants grow near the ramps. Other than the unique exterior architecture, most items of interest are inside the buildings.
