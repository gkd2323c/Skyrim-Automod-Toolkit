[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-npc-Bjartur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bjartur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjartur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjartur.jpg) Bjartur, in the Castle Dour Dungeon **Bjartur** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [prisoner](Skyrim_Prisoner_(class).md) found in [Castle Dour Dungeon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Castle_Dour_Dungeon) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), apparently jailed for being a staunch supporter of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).

She wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

Without anything to do inside her jail cell, her daily routine involves standing in one place, at times conversing with the patrolling jailor and headsman [Ahtar](Skyrim_Ahtar.md) through the bars. She sleeps from midnight to 8am.

## Dialogue
While you are in the dungeon during her waking hours, you were meant to hear her loudly exclaim the following lines, which can't play due to a [bug](#Bugs):

*"You won't get anything out of me, you filthy sons o' horkers."* *"I stand with the Stormcloaks. You Imperial dogs will never break my will. Never!"* If you attempt to talk to her through the bars she will defiantly say:

*"You here for my next interrogation? I got nothing but time..."* *"Leave me be, Imperial. I haven't got anything to say to you."* *"Don't think I don't know what's coming."* *"It's only a matter of time before Ahtar's blade finds my neck..."*
## Unused Dialogue
Because it is impossible to engage in conversation with her, these two farewells will never be heard:

*"Tell these Horkers to set me free."* *"Leave me be. I'm no harm here."*

## Bugs
- Her two idle yelling lines have a condition attached that they only play while on the package `Solitude Blacksmith Work Day8x14`. However, seeing as Bjartur doesn't use said package at any point (as she doesn't work at the [Solitude Blacksmith](Skyrim_Solitude_Blacksmith.md) and has no ties to it at all), they go unused. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

## Notes
- Bjartur will never get released, not even if the Stormcloaks take over Solitude. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
