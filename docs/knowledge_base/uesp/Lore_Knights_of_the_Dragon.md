# Knights of the Dragon

This article is about the faction. For the class of warriors, see [Dragon Knights](Lore_Dragon_Knights.md). For the book, see [Knights of the Dragon (book)](Lore_Knights_of_the_Dragon_(book).md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/ON-place-Daggerfall.jpg/200px-ON-place-Daggerfall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Daggerfall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Daggerfall.jpg) Daggerfall (ESO) *I vow to serve the Dragon. I lay down my life, my gold, and my sacred honor for my King. His word is my order, my sword is his arm. Our lives are one.*
—Oath of the Knightly Order, *"[*Knights of the Dragon*](Lore_Knights_of_the_Dragon_(book).md)*"The Knights of the Dragon (also called the **Knights of Daggerfall**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Toa M3-1)</sup> is the [knightly order](Lore_Knights.md) of the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The Knights serve the purpose of protecting the royal family of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), as well as their domain. The highest rank in the Knights of the Dragon is the Paladin. Like many other knightly orders in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), there is a set of skills required to elevate one's rank in the faction. Among these include the Long Blade, the [Dragonish](Lore_Dragonish.md) language, Archery and Medical knowledge.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFRanks-2)</sup>

## History
The Knights of the Dragon can date their origins to sometime around [1E 500](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_500), when the early [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) liberated themselves from the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md), and established what later became known as the province of High Rock. Other petty kingdoms and duchies created their own knightly order, and the Knights of the Dragon became Daggerfall's militia. This continued all the way into modern times.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1E-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TKOo HR-4)</sup>

The Knights of the Dragon, under the command of Lord Trystan, was present in the thirty-year long [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md). King [Joile](Lore_Joile.md) of Daggerfall had launched an invasion against the Kingdom of the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), the First [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). Lord Trystan was friends with [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md), champion of the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). The Knights of the Dragon held vast respect for the [Order of Diagna](Lore_Order_of_Diagna.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOTrystan-5)</sup> When General [Mercedene](Lore_Mercedene.md) had interrupted a duel between Gaiden Shinji, and [Baloth Bloodtusk](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md), Commander Reliana confronted Mercedene and defeat her in battle, avenging the duel.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOAshes-6)</sup> The Siege of Orsinium ended in [1E 980](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_980).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-WJot B-7)</sup>

Several years later, in [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542), [Durcorach the Black Drake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Durcorach_the_Black_Drake) invaded the province of High Rock, and make his way towards Daggerfall following a failed siege against [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md). King [Bergamot](Lore_Bergamot_(person).md) led his forces, consisting of the Knights of the Dragon, to face the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md). When Earl [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) had arrived with reinforcements, Durcorach was slain. The Kingdom of Daggerfall was brought into the first [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Toa M3-1)</sup> When the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) began in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Knights of the Dragon became a separate militia from the Covenant, per the request of King Casimir Deleyn.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> But the Knights of the Dragon later took part in the defense for [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) against the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOLabyrinth-9)</sup>

Many years later in [3E 402](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_402), the Knights of the Dragon were under the command of Lord [Bridwell](Lore_Bridwell.md). Lord Bridwell was intensely devoted to the royal family, and the King of Daggerfall, [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md). Lord Bridwell served as the Captain of Daggerfall's army during the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md), paving the way for victory at the Battle of the Bluffs, and the Siege of Craghold.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-NWOB-10)</sup> Lord Bridwell returned as a war hero. By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Lord Bridwell continued to control the Knights, and serve as a member of the Court of Daggerfall. Mobar was the second-in-command of the Knights of the Dragon.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DFKnights-11)</sup> However, Mobar had been questioning the royal family and has considered creating a conflict and revolting.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TDC-12)</sup>

Sometime before the War of Betony, Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) sent a [fateful letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Parchment#Orcish_Emancipation) to [an embedded Blades agent](Lore_Brisienna_Magnessen.md) in Daggerfall Court through the use of a Knights of the Dragon courier, However, the matter soon took a back seat to the quickly brewing conflict, and the letter's arrival was delayed until its end, never reaching its intended destination.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-13)</sup>
