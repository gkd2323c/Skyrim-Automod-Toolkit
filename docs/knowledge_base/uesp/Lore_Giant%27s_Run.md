[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/ON-map-Giant%27s_Run.jpg/200px-ON-map-Giant%27s_Run.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Giant%27s_Run.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Giant%27s_Run.jpg) Map of Giant's Run circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Giant's Run** is a region located in the hold of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), which is a part of the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The capital of Giant's Run and Eastmarch is the city-state of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), located along the [Yorgrim River](Lore_Yorgrim_River.md) towards the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). The holy forest of [Kynesgrove](Lore_Kynesgrove.md) takes up the southern portion of the region towards the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md). The township of the Lower Yorgrim occupies the other side of the Yorgrim Estuary from Windhelm, hence the name. Towards the mainland are several strongholds including [Morvunskar](Lore_Morvunskar.md), where the royal family of Windhelm is buried, among other warriors of Ancient Skyrim. And then there was the Ancient Orcish stronghold of [Cradlecrush](Lore_Cradlecrush.md), which is further inland than the rest of Giant's Run.

The mouth of the [White River](Lore_White_River.md), known as Slaughterfish Bay,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> was once considered part of Giant's Run during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> but by the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) was in the borders of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md)'s [Broken Cape](Lore_Broken_Cape.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup>
