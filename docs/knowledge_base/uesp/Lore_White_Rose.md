# White Rose

**White Rose** may refer to one of five different subjects:

- [Knights of the Rose](Lore_Knights_of_the_Rose.md), a knightly order of the kingdom of Wayrest that was also known as the "Knights of the White Rose"
- [White Rose (Black Marsh)](Lore_White_Rose_(Black_Marsh).md), an area in Black Marsh that is home to a toxic breed of black cockerel and a plum-like fruit known to the Argonians as the lizardfruit
- White Rose Prison, an Imperial prison built in the Shadowfen region of Black Marsh that was abandoned during the Interregnum
- [White Rose River](Lore_White_Rose_River.md), a river in central Cyrodiil that is connected to Lake Rumare
- [White rose](Lore_White_Rose.md), a flower used as an ingredient in alchemy in *The Elder Scrolls II: Daggerfall*
