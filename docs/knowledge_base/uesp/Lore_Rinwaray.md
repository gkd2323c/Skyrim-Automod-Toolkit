High Magistrate Rinwaray of the [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) (posing under the identity of **Penewen** with an enchanted jeweled brooch) manipulated [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md) to use his teleportation device, the [Hyperagonal Locational Determinator](Lore_Hyperagonal_Locational_Determinator.md), to teleport her into the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). There she planned to find [Seht's Affect Inducer](Lore_Seht%27s_Affect_Inducer.md), a magical device originally entrusted to [Barilzar](Lore_Barilzar.md) that can manipulate a person's emotions. She planned to use it on [Proxy Queen Alwinarwe](Lore_Proxy_Queen_Alwinarwe.md) for her own political gain and to revert the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) to isolationism. Her efforts were stopped when [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md) caught wind of her plot and followed her tracks through the Clockwork City, the [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md), [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) (accidentally), and eventually caught her in [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) where he managed to subdue her and now awaits the Queen's judgment.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
