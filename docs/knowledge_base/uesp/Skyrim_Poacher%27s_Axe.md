[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-item-Woodcutter%27s_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Woodcutter%27s_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Woodcutter%27s_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Woodcutter%27s_Axe.jpg) A Woodcutter's Axe The Poacher's Axe is a special [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) that is found within [Halted Stream Camp](Skyrim_Halted_Stream_Camp.md). It has the unique enchantment [Huntsman's Prowess](Skyrim_Huntsman%27s_Prowess.md), which does an additional three points of damage to animals (see [Bugs](#Bugs)). [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Poacher's Axe requires a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md). While it has a unique enchantment and name, the Poacher's Axe can still be used to chop wood like a normal woodcutter's axe.

## Notes
- The Poacher's Axe is the only Woodcutter's Axe that can be improved at a grindstone, though the Arcane Blacksmith perk must be unlocked first. Unfortunately, it doesn't benefit from any other smithing perks.
- Unlike most axes, the Poacher's Axe has a swing speed of 1.3, the same speed as daggers. However, unlike the regular woodcutter's axe, this one benefits from one-handed perks.
- If you don't pick up the Poacher's Axe during your first visit to the mine, it will respawn with the rest of the dungeon.
