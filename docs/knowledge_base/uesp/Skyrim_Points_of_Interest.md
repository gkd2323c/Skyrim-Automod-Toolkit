This article describes places of interest in Skyrim that are not marked on your map, are not represented by icons on your compass, and aren't directly related to any quests. Some of the places on this list do not have official names or titles, in which case unofficial names have been used to describe the places.

## Bandit Camps and Bandit Ambushes
- On the road between [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), just south of the [Guardian Stones](Skyrim_Guardian_Stones.md) and east of a Shrine of Talos, there is a camp inhabited by three leveled [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). It contains a chest, [Treasure Map I](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_I.md), and a copy of the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill book *[Night Falls on Sentinel](Skyrim_Night_Falls_on_Sentinel.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=3760&y=-65659&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- Between [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and [Pale Imperial Camp](Skyrim_Pale_Imperial_Camp.md), there is a camp inhabited by three leveled bandits. It has a chest, food barrels and a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill book *[Death Blow of Abernanit](Skyrim_Death_Blow_of_Abernanit.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=10657&y=99519&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- At the northern cliff of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), there is a recess used as a bandit camp. It is filled with various food sacks, food barrels, crates, a rack containing a few potions, alchemy ingredients, and several bottles of skooma, and an adept-locked chest. Three more bandits will ambush you upon opening the chest. This site contains a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill book *[Aevar Stone-Singer](Skyrim_Aevar_Stone-Singer.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=22459&y=5400&zoom=7.548&legacy=true)
- On the road between Helgen and [Haemar's Shame](Skyrim_Haemar%27s_Shame.md) is a wrecked Khajiit caravan, complete with two carts, seven dead Khajiit and two dead horses. In the furthest cart is some sacks and a chest, as well as a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill book, *[The Buying Game](Skyrim_The_Buying_Game.md)*. When you approach from Helgen, multiple bandits will ambush you, usually consisting of one or two melee fighters and two archers. If you head up the road over the hill and then turn around and come back, another three-four bandits will spawn around the wreckage and attack. Note that this ambush only spawns if you head directly there after exiting Helgen. If you went through Helgen Keep with Hadvar, you can go to Riverwood, recruit Faendal, and have him help you battle the bandits and collect the skill book for you, but only if you do not enter Alvor's house. If you went through Helgen Keep with Ralof, the bandits and caravan will disappear as soon as you enter Riverwood or another village or city. If you've already spawned the ambush previously, by the time the nearby Stormcloak camp spawns (which happens only after visiting a major city), the caravan remains will have been cleared up and all the bandits will respawn, sitting around the stump just off the road to the right. They will be non-hostile unless spoken to, or outright attacked.

## Caravan Remains and Shipwrecks
- On the side of the road southeast of Dragon Bridge is a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a knapsack. Next to it are the corpses of a horse, a Nord man, and a Redguard woman, the latter carrying the *[Merchant's Journal](Skyrim_Merchant%27s_Journal.md)*. [Falmer arrows](Skyrim_Falmer_Arrow.md) stuck in the horse, other Falmer arrows around the location, and a [Falmer ear](Skyrim_Falmer_Ear.md) suggest a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) attack, possibly related to the nearby [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-92152&y=67427&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- South of [The Lady Stone](Skyrim_The_Lady_Stone.md) is a shipwreck beneath the waters of Lake Ilinalta. The shipwreck contains a chest and a leveled weapon. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-32600&y=-59576&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northeast of the [Wreck of the Winter War](Skyrim_Wreck_of_the_Winter_War.md) are the sparse remains of ship. Among the oars and wood are three barrels. A short distance to the north is a birds' nest with a [pine thrush egg](Skyrim_Pine_Thrush_Egg.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=184352&y=74240&zoom=15&legacy=true)
- East of [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md) and west of the Evergreen Woods dragon mound, there are remnants of a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a chest. Next to it are the corpses of a horse and two burned humanoids. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-62808&y=-70289&zoom=7.964&legacy=true)

## Creature Lairs
- Southeast of [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md) is a bugling elk near a stony Nordic bust. A hostile animal often wanders into the area. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-46997&y=-70386&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southwest of [Swindler's Den](Skyrim_Swindler%27s_Den.md) and northeast of [Gjukar's Monument](Skyrim_Gjukar%27s_Monument.md) is a dead bandit next to a [nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) presumably killed by a hostile animal. The bandit carries a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill book *[Beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(book).md)* [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-52880&y=-2976&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northeast of Swindler's Den and southwest of [Greenspring Hollow](Skyrim_Greenspring_Hollow.md) there is a river isle with hostile [Mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md), a helm on a skull, an iron ore deposit, and a mammoth skeleton. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-40981&y=8574&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- On the coast between [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and the [Wreck Of The Brinehammer](Skyrim_Wreck_Of_The_Brinehammer.md), there is an upturned skiff inhabited by two mudcrabs and containing a skeleton hand, a knapsack, a scimitar, two pieces of mudcrab chitin, and a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) skill book *[Daughter of the Niben](Skyrim_Daughter_of_the_Niben.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=13463&y=108099&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- One or two bears lair WNW of Halldir's Cairn under a rocky overhang. The corpses of two hunters and an elk are found on the site, and beehives can be found in the trees nearby.
- Underwater in the east end of Lake Ilinalta, southwest of Anise's Cabin, there is a sunken ruin where two slaughterfish reside. The ruins also contain a leveled mace, two soul gems in a stone bowl, and an unlocked chest under the stairs.

## Houses
- Northwest of the [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md) lies a ruined shack, with a usable bed. There is a hidden chest under some broken floorboards, between the bed and ruined fireplace. This chest is bugged and will not display as "Empty" even if it contains nothing. If you have [Fall of the Space Core, Vol 1](Skyrim_Fall_of_the_Space_Core,_Vol_1.md) installed, the [Space Core](Skyrim_Space_Core.md) will crash-land in this shack. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-4772&y=-8420&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northeast of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) and north of [Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp.md), there is a ruined, burning shack. Fire continuously burns the previous occupant and remaining furniture. Before dragons are activated, this shack is home to a single bandit. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-13853&y=92090&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- In a valley south of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) there is another burning shack where fire continuously burns the previous occupant and remaining furniture. A scroll of [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md) can be found here, indicating a possible cause of the fire. Some potatoes and leeks may be harvested from the untouched garden. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=189636&y=-122036&zoom=6.288&legacy=true)
- With [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) installed, there is a hut on the coast west of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) which contains a bed, various furniture, fishing supplies, and other supplies. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=12462&y=109512&zoom=8&legacy=true)

## Hunter's Camps
- There is a downhill path west of the [Guardian Stones](Skyrim_Guardian_Stones.md) which leads to a fisherman's camp. With [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) installed, this site becomes a [fishing spot](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-2900&y=-61566&zoom=7.801&legacy=true)
- On a small island in the eastern part of [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md), there is another fisherman's camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-17343&y=-60775&zoom=7.801&legacy=true)
- On the northwestern part of Lake Ilinalta, there is another camp belonging to a fisherman. This camp contains a copy of the One-handed skill book *[Fire and Darkness](Skyrim_Fire_and_Darkness.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-42436&y=-47165&zoom=7.731&legacy=true)
- West of [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) lies a hunter's camp. It consists of a chest, a sack, a small tent with a bedroll, and a hunter's bow with 12 iron arrows. One hunter lives here. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-63074&y=-40210&zoom=8&legacy=true)
- Southeast of [Autumnshade Clearing](Skyrim_Autumnshade_Clearing.md) is a camp occupied by a pair of hunters. One of their tents contains a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill book *[The Gold Ribbon of Merit](Skyrim_The_Gold_Ribbon_of_Merit.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=158381&y=-83993&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Due south of [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) in Dawnstar and southeast of the [Shrine of Dibella](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Watching_Dawnstar) is a small camp occupied by two hunters. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=35330&y=83811&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- 290 ft N of [North Cold Rock Pass](Skyrim_North_Cold_Rock_Pass.md), is an unmarked hunter encampment, occupied by a single hunter. Under the tent is a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill book *The Gold Ribbon of Merit*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-47237&y=43306&zoom=17&legacy=true)

## Warlock Altars
- Halfway between [Cracked Tusk Keep](Skyrim_Cracked_Tusk_Keep.md) and [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md) is a Nordic altar occupied by a [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md). The altar holds two [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md) and a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Frostfall, v10](Skyrim_2920,_Frostfall,_v10.md)*.
- East of [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md), there is a Nordic altar with the corpses of two necromancers, one lying on the altar and one on the ground nearby. The necromancer on the altar carries a copy of the Conjuration skill book *[Liminal Bridges](Skyrim_Liminal_Bridges.md)*. Two skeletons are stationed by the altar and a hooded skeleton patrols the area east.
- North of [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md), there is a Nordic altar occupied by a necromancer. This site contains embalming tools, a sword, a burial urn, and the Conjuration skill book *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](Skyrim_2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-7964&y=-66405&zoom=7.512&legacy=true)

## Smaller Places of Interest
- South of [South Brittleshin Pass](Skyrim_South_Brittleshin_Pass.md) is an empty dock. With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation, some [fishing supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) can be found here. Fish from [Temperate Lakes](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Biomes) can be caught here. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-18816&y=-54387&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northwest of [Fallowstone Cave](Skyrim_Fallowstone_Cave.md), partway up the mountain slope, there is an unmarked clearing with a [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) vein and an [orichalcum ore](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ore.md) vein. A copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skill book *[Incident at Necrom](Skyrim_Incident_at_Necrom.md)* is well-hidden at the base of one of the trees on that slope. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=189522&y=-79771&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- West-southwest of [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md), if you climb the ridge to the top, you'll find a skeleton with a blood-spattered knapsack and leveled bow lying next to it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-72907&y=-69524&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Between [Reachwater Rock](Skyrim_Reachwater_Rock.md) and [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md) there's an abandoned rowboat at a bend in the river. In the boat are some bottles, an apothecary's satchel, an adept-locked strongbox, and one each of the following gems: an emerald, a garnet, a flawless garnet, a ruby, a flawless ruby, and a flawless diamond. In the river next to the boat is a chest and two mead barrels. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-145818&y=4291&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- West of [Whitewatch Tower](Skyrim_Whitewatch_Tower.md) is a novice-locked strongbox in the midst of a few fallen stone walls. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=25512&y=15632&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southeast of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md), at a point roughly between [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md) and [Sundered Towers](Skyrim_Sundered_Towers.md), there is a locked strongbox with a leveled dagger leaned up against it hidden in the center of a pile of driftwood. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-128320&y=31744&zoom=17&legacy=true)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-place-Flag_and_Skeleton.jpg/200px-SR-place-Flag_and_Skeleton.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Flag_and_Skeleton.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Flag_and_Skeleton.jpg) A tattered flag and an unfortunate soul - In the mountains almost due east of [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md), between it and [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md), a dead [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) bandit can be found draped over a novice-locked chest containing minor loot. A random one-handed weapon is beside it, and another one is carried by the Orc. Two dead [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) next to him hint at his cause of death. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-166352&y=30998&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- East of [Haemar's Shame](Skyrim_Haemar%27s_Shame.md), on the east side of the mountain pass between [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and the western lands of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), a pile of stones marks a steep, narrow path heading east from the road up through a small notch in the rock walls of the pass. A [quicksilver](Skyrim_Quicksilver.md) ore deposit and a copy of the Alteration skill book *[Daughter of the Niben](Skyrim_Daughter_of_the_Niben.md)* can be found at the end of this path. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=63028.8&y=-80338.8&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- On an island west of [Broken Oar Grotto](Skyrim_Broken_Oar_Grotto.md) there is a flag on a pole, at the base of which is an unlootable skeleton. There are also two [gold ore veins](Skyrim_Gold_Ore_Vein.md) on the island. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-85248&y=143232&zoom=16&legacy=true) [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)'s developers have stated that this island appears in ESO as [Kyne's Aegis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Kyne%27s_Aegis).
- West-northwest of Haemar's Shame and east of [Falkreath Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Falkreath_Stormcloak_Camp.md) is an overhanging rock face. Underneath lies a skeleton with a coin purse, along with a few animal bones. There are two [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes here as well. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=43901&y=-74187&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Due west of [Graywinter Watch](Skyrim_Graywinter_Watch.md), on a spit of land sticking out into the middle of the river, is a novice-locked chest hidden among some driftwood. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=43288&y=-3232&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Up along the mountainside due east of [Wolfskull Cave](Skyrim_Wolfskull_Cave.md) and due south of [the Steed Stone](Skyrim_The_Steed_Stone.md) is a dead female bandit with two leveled potions and a sack of food, right next to a tree. To find them, walk along the road east from the cave until you come to a broken tree trunk on the left; she is above that. Just past this is the stump of a tree which marks a place where you can climb high enough to jump up to the ledge she is on. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-95842&y=106922&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- East of [Bruca's Leap Redoubt](Skyrim_Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.md) and southwest of [Dragon Bridge Overlook](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge_Overlook.md) at the bottom of the middle waterfall is an apprentice-locked [dungeon boss chest](Skyrim_Containers.md#Chest). It's near a fallen tree crossing the river. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-118397&y=64158&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southeast of [Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde.md) and northwest of [Avanchnzel](Skyrim_Avanchnzel.md) is a lean-to sheltering an apprentice-locked strongbox, two lockpicks, and two leveled potions (healing and invisibility). Two canis roots grow next to it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=116739&y=-102370&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northwest of [Orotheim](Skyrim_Orotheim.md) on the river shore in a tiny alcove is a lootable skeleton next to a novice-locked common chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-70629&y=38086&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southwest, next to [Orphan Rock](Skyrim_Orphan_Rock.md) is a lootable skeleton, helm, and coin purse in a hollow fallen tree. Mora tapinella grows on the tree. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=35387&y=-71170&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- North of [North Brittleshin Pass](Skyrim_North_Brittleshin_Pass.md) in the valley leading to it is an apprentice-locked strongbox in a hollow fallen tree. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-24309&y=-30074&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- North of North Brittleshin Pass just on the other side of the road is an apprentice-locked strongbox inside a circle of small dead trees and bloodied grasses. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-24693&y=-25426&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northeast of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and south of [Snow Veil Sanctum](Skyrim_Snow_Veil_Sanctum.md) is a coin bag sitting on the Lockpicking skill book *[The Wolf Queen, v1](Skyrim_The_Wolf_Queen,_v1.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=145056&y=48128&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southeast of the [wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md) on an island is a helm inside the trunk of a hollow fallen tree along with the Heavy Armor skill book *[Chimarvamidium](Skyrim_Chimarvamidium.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=142307&y=100264&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Northeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) and southwest of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) on the southern shore of a river is a dungeon chest. That section of the river has a keyhole shape and is at the base of a waterfall almost due south of and very close to the location of *Aevar Stone-Singer* (as described above). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=100975&y=-51434&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- West of [Redoran's Retreat](Skyrim_Redoran%27s_Retreat.md) and due south of [Greenspring Hollow](Skyrim_Greenspring_Hollow.md) is a rock pile with an iron helm in the mountain flowers at the base. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-26573&y=5158&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Southeast from [South Skybound Watch](Skyrim_South_Skybound_Watch.md), at the base of a large tree surrounded by boulders and rocks is a skull with a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) in its mouth, perched atop an apothecary's satchel.
