[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-item-Her_Hand_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Her_Hand_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Armor.jpg) Her Hand Armor Her Hand Armor serves as the chest piece for the [set of armor](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor_Set.md) used by the members of Almalexia's personal guard. Alongside this armor, this set of equipment also includes [boots](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Gauntlets.md), and a [helmet](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Helmet.md). The armor has been enchanted to [fortify heavy armor](Skyrim_Fortify_Heavy_Armor.md) by 25 points, improving the defense provided by armor like this set. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this armor requires a piece of [Chitin Plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

There is also a version of this armor that does not have an enchantment already placed upon it, leaving its magical effects up to you.
