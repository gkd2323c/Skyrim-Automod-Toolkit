[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/SR-npc-Unbound_Dremora.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Unbound_Dremora.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Unbound_Dremora.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Unbound_Dremora.jpg) Unbound Dremora The **Unbound Dremora** is a special summon you get from [Phinis Gestor](Skyrim_Phinis_Gestor.md) during the [Conjuration Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Conjuration_Ritual_Spell.md) quest. A hostile Unbound Dremora can also be summoned using the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) in [the Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) after the quest is complete.

The Unbound Dremora wears a set of [Dremora armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Dremora_Equipment) and carries a random leveled weapon with a [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) enchantment.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- The spell can only be cast on top of the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). It can be "cast" in other locations, but will yield no results.
- Other summons will not be dispelled when you summon the Unbound Dremora.
- After the Conjuration Ritual Quest the Unbound Dremora becomes immune to any type of attack or damage. You will no longer be able to interact with him but he will continue wander about the College grounds, attacking any enemies in the area. If a dragon is killed nearby, the Unbound Dremora may go and observe like other members of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md), but will not say anything.
- It is possible to exit the conversation during the 1st and 2nd dialogue and get behind it to pickpocket its weapon, if you have the [Misdirection](Skyrim_Misdirection.md) perk unlocked.
- The Unbound Dremora carries an [Iron Dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Bugs
- The Unbound Dremora is never removed after the [Conjuration Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Conjuration_Ritual_Spell.md) quest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- It is possible to loot his weapon just before he disappears.
- The Unbound Dremora is hostile to [Barbas](Skyrim_Barbas.md) as well as the Unicorn mount (returns to College courtyard if not using), and vice versa (Barbas is hostile to the Dremora). This can result in an endless battle (as both are immune to damage), and any College members who sight the battle will join in the fight against Barbas (but not against you or other followers, unless you intervene). <sup>**?**</sup>

To manually remove the Unbound Dremora with console commands, select the Unbound Dremora, type in "disable" and hit enter, then type in "markfordelete" and hit enter.
