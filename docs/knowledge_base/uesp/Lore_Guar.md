Guar ([/ɡ w ɑː r/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Archive-1)</sup> are large bipedal omnivorous [reptiles](Lore_Reptile.md) native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-2)</sup> They are the primary domesticated herd animal of these provinces, and are not usually aggressive. Characterized by their endurance and intelligence, guar are used mostly as pack animals and beasts of burden, although certain breeds are used as mounts. Although usually associated with the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), guar have a special relationship with [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) and the Dunmer traditionally employ [Saxhleel](Lore_Saxhleel.md) slaves as guar-herds and handlers. A young guar is called a calf or guarling.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Veru-3)</sup>

The guar is related to the [alit](Lore_Alit.md), [kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md), and [wormmouth](Lore_Wormmouth.md), and some herders argue that the guar is a distant cousin of the Argonian.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-2)</sup> Argonian historians have speculated that guar were originally native to Black Marsh, and were introduced to Morrowind by Dunmer slavers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> However, other sources claim that Morrowind's unique fauna developed in geographic isolation from the rest of Tamriel.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Po M-5)</sup>

Despite the name, [bantam guar](Lore_Bantam_Guar.md) are actually a member of the [scuttler](Lore_Scuttler.md) family, more closely related to [cliff racers](Lore_Cliff_Racer.md) than guar. Domesticated guar are regularly seen outside their native lands, and have been imported into [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) as beasts of burden for hundreds of years.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MF1-6)</sup> Guar are often raised or hunted for their [hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Guar_Hide), eggs, and meat. Wild guar are usually docile, but may become aggressive and attack travelers. Domesticated guar that are used to carry cargo are known as pack guar.

Guar provide milk,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Archive-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Gavin Gavonne-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Bfo B-8)</sup> meat, leather, and transport for Vvardenfell's settled Velothi ranchers and Ashland nomads. In its natural state, the aggressive and territorial wild guar is hunted for meat and hides.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Archive-1)</sup> Guar are known to molt,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Guar Brush-9)</sup> and some are also said to be feathered.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TBS-10)</sup>

[Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) apparently developed a particular fondness for guar during one of his various tours of Morrowind. Septim struggled with the [Dunmer language](Lore_Dunmer_Language.md), and so instead took to calling them "[tigers](Lore_Senche-Tiger.md)"—another animal he was fond of from the storybooks of his youth. Many Imperial Legionnaires and even [Dres](Lore_Dres.md) slavers still refer to their cattle guar as tigers in honor of this.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-11)</sup>

## Breeds
Guar come in many different breeds, listed below:

### Albino Guar
Albino Guar, also called White Guar,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-12)</sup> are a rare type of guar. They often feature in [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) prophecies, and bezoars taken from their stomachs can be used in rituals.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/MW-creature-White_Guar.jpg/200px-MW-creature-White_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-creature-White_Guar.jpg) An albino guar (Morrowind)

### Banded Guar Charger
The Banded Guar Charger is a breed of striped guar used as a war mount. They are related to the feral [tiger guar](#Tiger_Guar).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-2)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/ON-mount-Banded_Guar_Charger.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Banded_Guar_Charger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Banded_Guar_Charger.jpg) Banded Guar Charger

### Common Vvardenfell Guar
The Common Vvardenfell Guar,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MF1-6)</sup> or Freckled Guar,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Freckle-14)</sup> is the most common breed of guar. Despite the name, the breed is not limited to the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Freckled Guar are considered to be the most intelligent breed of guar. They can be taught to come when called, and some can even be house-trained. As such, younger ones are regularly kept as pets in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Freckle-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCSS-15)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/ON-creature-Guar.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Guar.jpg) A common guar
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/ON-pet-Freckled_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Freckled_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Freckled_Guar.jpg) A young Freckled Guar
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/MW-creature-Rollie_the_Guar.jpg/200px-MW-creature-Rollie_the_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-creature-Rollie_the_Guar.jpg) A pack guar (Morrowind)

### Deadlands Guar
The Deadlands Guar is a breed of Guar said to have been born and shaped in the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md). They have a blazing orange hide that reflects their origin and temperament.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-DGWD-16)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/ON-mount-Deadlands_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Deadlands_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Deadlands_Guar.jpg) A Deadlands Guar

### Frostbane Guar
Frostbane Guar is a strange breed of guar found in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It has a shaggy white coat, adorned with symbolic hair-knots and hide strips meant to protect it from the frigid climate. The practice of adorning mounts with these dangling "Frostbane talismans" is an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) tradition dating back to [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s arrival in Skyrim. The ancient [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) worshipped totem animals as [gods](Lore_Gods.md), and Frostbane talismans continue to be used into modern times. Since the guar is native to warmer climates, the existence of Frostbane Guar suggest that these wards do in fact protect against the cold.

The Frostbane Pony Guar is a smaller breed which has long been popular among the more traditional aristocracy of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), who enjoy dressing them in Frostbane garb and keeping them as pets.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-crown_store-Frostbane_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Frostbane_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Frostbane_Guar.jpg) Frostbane Guar
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/ON-pet-Frostbane_Pony_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Frostbane_Pony_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Frostbane_Pony_Guar.jpg) Frostbane Pony Guar

### Glowgill Guar
The **Glowgill Guar** (also called the **Lambent Guar** or **Gill Guar**)<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ONLambent-17)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONTawny-18)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONAmber-19)</sup> is an exotic breed of guar that is distinguished by its glowing eyes and stripes. They trace their origins to a coastal Argonian tribe near [Archon](Lore_Archon.md), where they have dark navy skin and bright blue eyes, and bioluminescent stripes. Rumors suggest that they can breathe underwater, but it's unknown if they can.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ONGlowgill-20)</sup> The glowgill guar from [Gideon](Lore_Gideon.md)<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONAmber-19)</sup> and [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md)<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONTawny-18)</sup> are distinguished by their golden colors, hence why they are called the Ambergill Guar and not just the Gideon Glowgill Guar.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONAmber-19)</sup> Locals that live in Black Marsh's swamps believe that nature's energy feeds the guar's light, but scholars believe that the light is only an [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) reaction.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONTawny-18)</sup> Tattoo artists in Murkmire siphon the guar's blood to create glowing yellow skin markings and say that the blessing of the land keeps them lit.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ONLambent-17)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/ON-mount-Glowgill_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Glowgill_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Glowgill_Guar.jpg) Glowgill Guar
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-mount-Ambergill_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Ambergill_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Ambergill_Guar.jpg) Ambergill Guar
- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/ON-pet-Tawny_Lambent_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Tawny_Lambent_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Tawny_Lambent_Guar.jpg) Tawny Lambent Guar

### Golden Eye Guar
Golden Eye Guar (or "golden-eyes") are a breed of guar used as a mount. It is a cross between a [Banded Guar Charger](#Banded_Guar_Charger) and a wild guar, known for its difficulty to control and its distinctive golden eyes. Despite the difficulty involved in training one, Golden Eyes are fearsome, loyal, and intelligent.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCSS-15)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/ON-mount-Golden_Eye_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Golden_Eye_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Golden_Eye_Guar.jpg) Golden Eye Guar

### Green Narsis Guar
The Green Narsis Guar is a robust breed, used as the standard mount and dray-beast of central Morrowind.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ONNarsis-21)</sup> It's named after the city of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md).

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/ON-mount-Green_Narsis_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Green_Narsis_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Green_Narsis_Guar.jpg) Green Narsis Guar

### Hist Guar
The Hist Guar, known in Black Marsh as Scales-Seem-Daubed-with-Hist-Sap, is a subspecies of guar which appears as if it has splotches of glowing [Hist Sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md) splattered across its green skin. It is unknown if the breed actually has any connection to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) or to [amber plasm](Lore_Amber_Plasm.md). The Hist Guar is used as a mount, and is the only subspecies known to enjoy music.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCSS-15)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/ON-mount-Hist_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Hist_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Hist_Guar.jpg) Hist Guar

### Hollowjack Rider Guar
Hollowjack Rider Guar are a mysterious breed usually only sighted on the night of the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md). These sightings are the result of Jaque o' the Hollow, a malevolent [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) spirit that curses riders all across Tamriel to ride headless through the night. Guar riders stricken by this curse can be sighted riding headless atop jet black guar mounts with red eyes. Hollowjack Rider Guar appear to be native to the realm of [Detritus](Lore_Detritus.md) and can be summoned from that realm.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCSS-15)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-mount-Hollowjack_Rider_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Hollowjack_Rider_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Hollowjack_Rider_Guar.jpg) Hollowjack Rider Guar

### Pale Velothi Guar
The Pale Velothi Guar is a breed that hails from the ashlands downwind of the volcanic [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md). They are used as mounts, and are renowned as hardy creatures capable of enduring especially harsh conditions.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCSS-15)</sup>

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/ON-mount-Pale_Velothi_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Pale_Velothi_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Pale_Velothi_Guar.jpg) Pale Velothi Guar

### Pony Guar
Pony Guar are a smaller species of guar that are rarely exported since they lack the physical strength of their larger cousins. Not well known outside of southern Morrowind, pony guar are usually bred as a curiosity, although some are raised for their meat and skins.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MF1-6)</sup> Many guar breeds or variants possess a "pony" equivalent.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/ON-creature-Pony_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Pony_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Pony_Guar.jpg) A pony guar

### Psijic Guar Exemplar
Psijic Guar Exemplars are a breed of guar created by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md). Like all Psijic Exemplars, this breed theoretically represents the ideal form a guar can assume, at which point the creature transcends the physical and incarnates the mystical. As such, Guar Exemplars take on a yellow ghostly form. They are used as mounts by Psijic monks when traversing the marshy terrain of Black Marsh or southern Morrowind, and are unequaled in this task. The first guar came to [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) when a Psijic advisor to the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) returned from [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) with a [Pony Guar](#Pony_Guar) in the middle of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md),<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup>, so the creation of Guar Exemplars presumably began sometime after this.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/ON-mount-Psijic_Guar_Exemplar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Psijic_Guar_Exemplar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Psijic_Guar_Exemplar.jpg) A Psijic Guar Exemplar

### Psijic Mascot Guar
Psijic Mascot Guar are a breed of guar created by the Psijic Order for use as pets. Like all [Psijic Mascots](Lore_Psijic_Mascot.md), it is unknown if these guar are real animals with an enchanted appearance, or illusory constructs created from Magicka to be used as beast companions and portable storage batteries of Magicka. The Psijics refuse to clarify the true nature of these creatures, although they have dismissed the theory that Mascots are simply portable batteries. In appearance, the Psijic Mascot Guar is a black guar which glows with Magicka through the mystic blue runes that embellish its possibly-illusory epidermis. Guar have been favorites of the Psijics ever since the first one was brought to Artaeum in the middle of the First Era, and Mascot guar calves are very popular as pets.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/ON-furnishing-Psijic_Mascot_Guar_Calf.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Psijic_Mascot_Guar_Calf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Psijic_Mascot_Guar_Calf.jpg) Psijic Mascot Guar Calf

### Shadowghost Guar
Shadowghost Guar are a mottled black and white breed of guar and are the only known nocturnal breed of the species. Due to their nocturnal habits and the fact that their patterned hides blend in with the shifting gray ash clouds on the slopes of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), the breed is rarely seen even by the native Dunmer. Nevertheless, Shadowghost Guar are sometimes used as mounts.

Shadowghost Pony Guar are a smaller breed also native to the slopes of Red Mountain. They are sometimes kept as pets.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/ON-creature-Shadowghost_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Shadowghost_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Shadowghost_Guar.jpg) Shadowghost Guar

### Skyfire Guar
Skyfire Guar are often mistaken as being poisonous due to their colorful pattern. Patience is needed to bring this breed to heed.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4b/ON-mount-Skyfire_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Skyfire_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Skyfire_Guar.jpg) A Skyfire Guar

### Striated Pony Guar
The Striated Pony Guar is a breed of [pony guar](#Pony_Guar) kept as household pets in Morrowind.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/ON-pet-Striated_Pony_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Striated_Pony_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Striated_Pony_Guar.jpg) Striated Pony Guar

### Tessellated Guar
The Tessellated Guar is a multi-purpose guar mount, at home anywhere from swamps to ashlands.

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-mount-Tessellated_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Tessellated_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Tessellated_Guar.jpg) Tessellated Guar

### Tiger Guar
Tiger Guar are a legendary breed of large wild guar, and are a highly prized game animal for noble huntsmen.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Archive-1)</sup> Found only on the [Deshaan Plain](Lore_Deshaan_Plain.md), their stripes have been compared to those of a [tiger](Lore_Tiger.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-11)</sup> The [banded guar charger](#Banded_Guar_Charger) is a breed descended from the tiger guar.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-2)</sup>

## Notes
- A Guar was originally called a "Rotumbo" during the early development of [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Sons-23)</sup>
- Some [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md) were constructed in the shape of a guar.
- Conjurers are able to compel [Atronachs](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) to adopt the form of a guar.
- The Hist have shaped the native [lizards](Lore_Ancestor_Lizard.md) of Black Marsh to create Guar-Lizards for use as steeds by the Argonians.
