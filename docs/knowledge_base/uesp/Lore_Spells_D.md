## Dispel
**Dispel** (short for **Dispel Magic** or **Dispel Magicka**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is a spell associated with the Schools of [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md) and [Thaumaturgy](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md). When cast, it negates the magical effects of another spell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Dispel can also be used to banish [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and the [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

- See also: [Dark Magic](Lore_Dark_Magic.md)

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Mysticism_Spells), [Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Dispel_Magic), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Dispel), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Dispel)

**Spell schools:** [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md), [Thaumaturgy](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md)
