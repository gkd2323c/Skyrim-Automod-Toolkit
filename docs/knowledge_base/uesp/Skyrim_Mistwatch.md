[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-place-Mistwatch.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mistwatch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistwatch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistwatch.jpg) Mistwatch **Mistwatch** is a medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) east of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) containing the [Mistwatch Bandits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch_Bandit) and their [leader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Leader). It contains three zones: *[Mistwatch North Tower](#Mistwatch_North_Tower)*, *[Mistwatch West Tower](#Mistwatch_West_Tower)*, and *[Mistwatch East Tower](#Mistwatch_East_Tower)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch_(quest).md)**: Help [Christer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Christer) locate his kidnapped wife [Fjola](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fjola).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To reach the fort, travel southeast along the road from [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md). After it crosses the bridge over the waterfalls south of Darkwater Crossing, the road starts to climb to the east. Partway up is a path to the north, with an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) on the ground to the left. The northern path will lead you to the entrance to the courtyard, though beware leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) and [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) on the path.

As you approach the fort, unless you are successfully [sneaking](Skyrim_Sneak.md), you will be warned to stay away by the four leveled Mistwatch Bandits that patrol the outer walls of the fort. To reach the courtyard, you can approach from the northeast or southwest; both approaches pass under arches. To the left of the northeastern arch there is a flight of stone stairs leading up to the top of the outer wall. At the top, beside the ruined tower, is a wooden table with a random [arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows) on it. North of the ruined tower is an intact tower with two doorways, one facing south and the other west. Inside the tower is a circular room containing several long wooden tables and benches with a total of four bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) and three bottles of [Honningbrew mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead) on and around the tables. The rest of the walls are empty with the exception of a lever to the left of a wooden bridge section, which will drop both halves of a wooden drawbridge that was built to replace a collapsed stone bridge. Dropping the bridge will allow access to the interior of the fort, comprising a set of sequential towers built into the mountainside.

### Mistwatch North Tower
Entering the first tower leads into a wide, curved corridor with a wooden door at the end and a doorway on the left. There is a small table on the right holding a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). As you approach the doorway, you will hear a farmer by the name of [Christer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Christer). If you talk to him, he will ask you to help him by searching for his wife [Fjola](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Leader). If you agree, the miscellaneous quest [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch_(quest).md) will start, and he will give you a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch_Key) that opens all the doors in the fort. There is an apprentice-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) in the same room as him, along with a couple food barrels and a set of shelves holding an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet), two random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), [magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), or [stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), an empty random [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), a pair of random [boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots), and a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sack. The wooden door at the end of the corridor is locked and requires the key Christer gives you to open.

Through the door is a large circular room with an opening to the north. In the room are two unowned bedrolls and a dresser containing random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) near the west wall, while opposite is a long wooden table with three [pheasant breasts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast), a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) on it, with five more pheasants hanging from racks above the table. Beyond the opening there is an empty cart on your right and a stairwell curving up around the inside of the tower. There are a couple bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on the stairs as you climb. On the landing partway up are three [bear traps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap). A bandit patrols the top half of these stairs, and a magic-using bandit can be found in another circular room at the top. This room contains a pair of cupboards holding a random enchanted [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet), a random potion of stamina, a random poison, a bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), and an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel). To the right of these is a long wooden table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) and a kettle containing two [rock warbler eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg). There is a hanging rack above the table holding two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). To the west is a level-locked gate to a cell that contains two hay piles and some ragged robes. The lock to the cell isn't opened by the key received from Christer. To the right of the cell is an unowned single bed, with an unlocked chest against the northeast wall. Between the bed and chest is an alcove with a ladder up to a level-locked trapdoor that can be unlocked with the key given to you by Christer, leading to *[Mistwatch Lower Balcony](#Exterior:_Mistwatch_Lower_Balcony)*.

### Exterior: Mistwatch Lower Balcony
Outside is a lean-to with an unowned bedroll under it and a campfire with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging over it. A path leads to a level-locked door into *[Mistwatch West Tower](#Mistwatch_West_Tower)*; however, before entering, continue past the door to find an adept-locked chest.

### Mistwatch West Tower
As you enter this tower, there is another curved corridor with a set of shelves on the right holding a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) and a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). To the left are several unowned single beds against the walls, with two bandits roaming this level. There are also three tables holding a total of five bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and a [long taffy treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Taffy_Treat). There is an unlocked cell along the left wall, with a [dead Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_(dead).md) lying on the ground inside and shackles on the rear wall with blood splattered on the wall below, suggesting how the prisoner met her end. To the left of the cell is an unlocked chest, while to the right on a small square table is a [note](Skyrim_Note_(Mistwatch).md) which gives more details about how she died. Opposite the cell is a set of shelves holding a [satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel), an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace), a couple bulbs of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), and several pieces of cheese. Farther along the corridor to the west is another curving flight of stairs leading up to the next floor. There are two more bandits as you climb, one partway up and the other at the top. The corridor continues circling the tower, passing a long wooden table against the inside wall holding a quiver of random arrows, a couple bulbs of garlic, two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), and a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum), with a second drum under the table. Continuing around, before the door at the end is another table with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top. The door is level-locked and leads out onto *[Mistwatch Higher Balcony](#Exterior:_Mistwatch_Higher_Balcony)*.

### Exterior: Mistwatch Higher Balcony
Apart from the trapdoor leading out of *Mistwatch West Tower* and a path across to the door into *[Mistwatch East Tower](#Mistwatch_East_Tower)*, there is nothing else of interest.

### Mistwatch East Tower
The east tower is the highest tower and home to Fjola, the bandit leader and wife of Christer. The door to this tower can only be unlocked if you have the key given to you by Christer. Entering leads into another typical tower, with a corridor curving off to the right and a set of shelves opposite holding three [common books](Skyrim_Books.md), a [leather helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Helmet), some random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots). To the right is a second set of shelves against the inner wall holding a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), an iron helmet, a pair of [Stormcloak officer boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md), a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots), four loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), and seven common books. As you approach the end of the corridor, you will encounter the friendly bandit leader, Fjola (see the [quest page](Skyrim_Mistwatch_(quest).md) for details regarding all options available at this point).

Past Fjola, the corridor leads into a central circular room with two cells and a room to the west. Both cells are empty and contain only unowned bedrolls. There is a long wooden table in the main room with a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), and four loose coins on top. The western room has an unowned single bed against the north wall, while opposite is a dresser containing random clothes and a row of three weapon racks holding a random [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer) and a random [enchanted](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md) [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe). On the dresser is a random potion of stamina, a common book, and a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Hallgerd's Tale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hallgerd%27s_Tale)*. Finally, hanging from a rack in the southwestern corner of the bedroom area are two rabbits.

There is no shortcut back to Christer, except down the side of the mountain. Fast traveling to Mistwatch places you at the lowest level, so this may be helpful in getting back to him.

## Notes
- The Mistwatch Bandits wear better quality [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) and [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and carry far more gold than typical [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md), lending weight to Fjola's claim that *"Now every free blade from here to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) wants to sign on for a piece."*
- It is possible to explore the rest of *Mistwatch North Tower* without starting the related quest if you drop down onto Mistwatch from the mountains behind it and enter through the trapdoor at the top of the tower. However, you will not be able to make it past the locked gate, and will therefore need to go back out through the trapdoor.
- You can enter and speak with the bandit boss without starting the related quest if you have the [Wax Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wax_Key) [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) perk. Unlocking the master-locked door before the final tower gives you the Mistwach key, which then can be used to open and enter the last tower. Doing this will make all bandits inside and outside of the tower friendly.
- Mistwatch is marked as cleared when the related quest is completed, regardless of how you choose to complete the quest. Therefore, clearing this location can either be triggered by killing its boss enemy (i.e., the bandit leader), or by talking to her, convincing Christer to leave, and then returning to tell her that he is gone.
- The doors from within the tower to the outside have custom names, such as "Mistwatch Courtyard", "Mistwatch Lower Balcony", and "Mistwatch Higher Balcony", instead of the usual generic "Skyrim" name given to doors leading back outside.
- The towers offer excellent views of [Bonestrewn Crest](Skyrim_Bonestrewn_Crest.md) to the north. A [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) circling the misty hill is a common sight.
- To the north is a ruined building covered in sulfur. Three [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) will rise here as you approach, but there is nothing else of note.
- A [frightened woman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frightened_Woman) who escaped the Mistwatch Bandits may be [encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Mistwatch_Escapee) in the wild. She will mark Mistwatch on the map for you if you speak to her.
- Mistwatch also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mistwatch) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).

## Bugs
- One of the bandits on the lower level of *Mistwatch West Tower* may not be tagged as a Mistwatch Bandit, but just as a normal leveled [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Clearing this location does not increment the number of "Dungeons Cleared" displayed in your journal, and therefore does not contribute to the [Delver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Delver) achievement. **Mod Notes**: The script for `dun Mistwatch QST` (stage 100) uses the command `Mistwatch Location.Set Cleared()`, which evidently only marks the place as cleared, but does not increment the other counters.
