*Ah yes, from the exotic canah bird--a favorite of mine for they are bred on Summerset Isle. Bred for their beauty I might add. They make an excellent gift for 10 gold.*
—Gerrick on Canah Feathers<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ger-1)</sup> The **Canah Bird**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ger-1)</sup> or simply **Canah**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGFeather-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-3)</sup><sup>:39</sup> is a colorful exotic [bird](Lore_Bird.md) species of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) that are bred for their beauty.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ger-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGFeather-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-3)</sup><sup>:36</sup> Their multicolored feathers are thought of as both beautiful and highly useful, as it is said the [goddess](Lore_Gods.md) [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md)'s light shines bright for them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-3)</sup><sup>:36</sup> Kynareth has been known to bless individuals with the ability to temporarily transform into a canah if an appropriate offering is given at her shrines on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), namely one of their feathers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Shrine-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-3)</sup><sup>:39</sup>

In [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), the [Altmer](Lore_High_Elf.md) trader Gerrick sold canah feathers to [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ger-1)</sup> Cyrus was able to quickly fly between shrines of Kynareth across the island by using the feathers to temporarily transform into a canah.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Shrine-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGCompanion-3)</sup>
