[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-npc-Nikulas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nikulas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nikulas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nikulas.jpg) Nikulas **Nikulas** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He and four companions embarked on a quest to find [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). However, Nikulas, [Erlendr](Skyrim_Erlendr.md), and [Hunroor](Skyrim_Hunroor.md) were betrayed by the wizard [Grimkell](Lore_Grimkell.md), who turned them into stone pillars at Brodir Grove on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were avenged by [Ulfgar the Unending](Skyrim_Ulfgar_the_Unending.md), who killed the wizard. All of the friends eventually did find their way to Sovngarde. Nikulas can be found in the west wing of the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md).

## Notes
- Nikulas is first mentioned in the quest [Betrayal at Brodir Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Betrayal_at_Brodir_Grove) in [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon).
