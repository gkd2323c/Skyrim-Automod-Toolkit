[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-npc-Legate_Sevan_Telendas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Legate_Sevan_Telendas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Sevan_Telendas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Sevan_Telendas.jpg) Legate Sevan Telendas at the Winterhold Imperial Camp **Legate Sevan Telendas** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) and the field commander of [Winterhold Imperial Camp](Skyrim_Winterhold_Imperial_Camp.md). He can be found either in his tent, or planning the war effort in a tent with a map of Skyrim. Should the Legion claim [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), he can be found in the Winterhold [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md).

Sevan is clad in a set of Imperial heavy armor. This includes an [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md), a pair of [Imperial bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Bracers.md), and a pair of [Imperial boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Boots.md). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) for close-range combat, and uses an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) supplemented with 13 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md) when the need arises. He also carries a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
When you have not joined either the Imperial Legion or the Stormcloaks he can say:

- **How do you feel about this war?**
- *"You mean [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s so-called "[Stormcloak rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md)"? The Nord kings - oh, excuse me, "[Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md)" - they can't seem to control their own people. So the Legion has stepped in to keep order. Sadly, the Empire's stretched a little thin these days, and we've gotten very few reinforcements. So we've been forced to recruit locally. But rest assured, citizen. We'll put an end to this uprising... and things will get back to normal soon enough."*

- **What's the Imperial Legion doing in Skyrim?**
- *"Not sure I understand the question. The Legion's always been in Skyrim. We have garrisons throughout all the provinces of the Empire. Our mission is to serve the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md), uphold the law, and protect the citizenry. Skyrim's days are darkening, and the Legion will soon be called into service like never before. But we stand ready, as always, to meet that call."*

- **How do I join?**
- *"Think you've got the mettle, eh? Honestly, the Legion can use all the able bodies it can muster. We have our hands full. A [dead king](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md), a rebellion, and now [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md)? Skyrim's a mess. If you're serious, make your way to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Our headquarters is [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). You find [General Tulius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md)* [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * there, commanding our forces in Skyrim. But talk to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md). She handles the local recruiting."*
