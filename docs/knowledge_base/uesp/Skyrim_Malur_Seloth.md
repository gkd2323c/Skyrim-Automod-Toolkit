[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Malur_Seloth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Malur_Seloth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Malur_Seloth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Malur_Seloth.jpg) Malur Seloth **Malur Seloth**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [witchblade](Skyrim_Witchblade.md), is the [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). He can be found in the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) or on a bench in front of [The Frozen Hearth](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md). Because of his race, the townsfolk assume that he has connections at the [college](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). As such, they treat him with fear and respect. To keep up appearances, Malur wants to get his hands on the [Staff of Arcane Authority](Skyrim_Staff_of_Arcane_Authority.md), which belongs to [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md). He will ask you to steal it for him during the related quest and will reward you with speech training.

Malur wears a modest set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a similar pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) with him to defend himself from the many threats present in Skyrim. Besides those items, he also carries a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

He spends most of his time wandering around the Jarl's longhouse, rarely doing anything else. His first deviation from his duties at court will come at 10am, at which time he will head outside to rest on a bench outside of The Frozen Hearth. He will remain outside until high noon, at which point he will return to the longhouse for lunch. Once he finished eating at 1pm, he will continue to do random tasks around the longhouse until 3pm. At 3pm he will head outside to exterior of the inn once more to spend another two hours. He will return inside the longhouse for the final time for the day at 5pm. Malur will wait around for an hour inside the court until 6pm to have his dinner. After eating, he will continue to perform various activities until 10am the next day, when he will head outside to The Frozen Hearth to repeat his schedule.

## Dialogue
Malur Seloth will provide numerous comments about his lifestyle of avoiding trouble and about how people think he has some connection to the College of Winterhold should he be approached:

- *"My job is to serve the Jarl, not waste time talking with travelers."*
- *"I don't really know anyone at the College. I guess everyone just assumes I do because I'm a Dunmer."*
- *"It turns out that if people think you're some sort of secret wizard, they'll let you get away with a lot."*
- *"I'm trying to do as little as possible. You're getting in the way of that."*

If Winterhold is controlled by the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), Malur will also provide a comment about the new [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md), [Thonjolf](Skyrim_Thonjolf.md):

- *"Thonjolf is convinced I've got some connections at the College, so he goes easy on me."*

### Conversations
Thonjolf will also converse with Malur Seloth about a task he needs done, only for Malur to deny him almost immediately.

- **Thonjolf**: *"Malur, I need you to take care of something."*
- **Malur**: *"No, I don't think so. Not today."*
- **Thonjolf**: *"I haven't even said what it is."*
- **Malur**: *"Doesn't really matter. Thanks, but no."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Some Light Theft
His favor quest can be initiated by asking him about what he does around Winterhold. He will reply to the query about his job by saying:

- **What do you do around here?**
- *"Me? Nothing. And I hope to keep it that way. The Jarl is convinced I have some connection with the College, so he goes easy on me. Maybe you'd like to help me keep up appearances? Old Nelacar has a mage's staff in his room in the Frozen Hearth. It'd be good to have that staff in case the Jarl starts wondering if I actually have a real connection to the College."*

- - **I'll get it for you.**
- *"Thanks. I'll be waiting for it."*

- - **Forget it.**
- *"Daedra take you, then."*

Once you return with the requested staff he will thank you for your service and raise your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill one level:

- **Here's Nelacar's staff.**
- *"This should come in handy. Here. Let me show you a few tricks I've learned about buttering up royalty."*

## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

- *"A slouch with few marketable skills, Melur * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * has landed himself in what he perceives as a wonderful position. Korir is convinced that he's in cahoots with the mages of the College of Winterhold and therefore expects little of Malur in the way of servant's duties. Malur has done nothing to dissuade this misunderstanding; in fact, he's cultivating it to get away with as little work as possible. Naturally, he survives if the balance of power shifts to the Imperials."*

- It seems that Malur was originally created to serve under the initial incumbent jarl, Kraldar, rather than Korir. Kraldar would see value in Malur for having ties with the College, and wants to have a good relationship with the mages there, while Korir certainly wouldn't. His line about Thonjolf and related scene were in all likelihood also created for this purpose, as Malur never has any sort of interaction with anyone on Korir's court, and the scene is numbered in the same way as Kraldar and Thonjolf's initial scenes. Kraldar is also the only person with whom Malur has any relationship markers set up.
- Malur is the only member of the Jarl's Court in Winterhold that will never be replaced, whether or not the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) or the Imperial Legion are in power.
- He shares a surname with [Evul Seloth](Skyrim_Evul_Seloth.md), who lives in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), but there is no indication that they are related.
