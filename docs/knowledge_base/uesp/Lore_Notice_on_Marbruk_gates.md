Notice on Marbruk gates *A warning to travelers, and to the worshipers of Hircine*
---
[![ON-concept-Hircine statue.png](https://images.uesp.net/8/88/ON-concept-Hircine_statue.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Hircine_statue.png)
- ATTENTION TRAVELERS

- Worshipers of the foul [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) have been seizing the unwary. You are advised to travel only in large groups and only if necessary until the cultists are eradicated. Attacks are often prefaced by unnatural howling and hunting horn blasts.

- REMINDER: The worship of Hircine will not be tolerated by [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) authorities in any shape or form, and no excuse will delay justice for those who engage in such depraved behavior.
