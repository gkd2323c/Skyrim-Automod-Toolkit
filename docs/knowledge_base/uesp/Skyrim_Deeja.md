[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Deeja.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Deeja.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Deeja.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Deeja.jpg) Deeja **Deeja** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Assassin). Prior to the related quest, she can be found at the docks of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). [Jaree-Ra](Skyrim_Jaree-Ra.md) and [Gulum-Ei](Skyrim_Gulum-Ei.md) are her brothers.

Until the related quest has been started, Deeja will wander around the dock area all day, and may be found aboard the *[Red Wave](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md)* at night.

Deeja wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) and a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots). She wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality) in combat, and carries [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor), as well as a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Related Quests
- **[Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!)**: Help run a [cargo ship](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Icerunner) aground.

## Dialogue
She is extremely rude to you and will completely dismiss your attempts at conversation.

*"You, talk to egg-brothers. Deeja has no words for you."* *"You have no knowledge of who you speak to. Go away."* *"You want knives? You want them in your belly? No? Then you stop talking to Deeja."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Lights Out!
During [Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!), you will encounter Deeja at the *[Wreck of the Icerunner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wreck_of_the_Icerunner)*. You will need to kill her shortly after talking with her.

Before becoming hostile, she will initiate conversation.

*"My brother told me you were coming. I'm supposed to give you what you've earned."* **So we're splitting the loot, then?** *"Ha! I'm afraid the loot's already been moved. You've been useful to the Blackbloods, though. You've earned a quick death."* She will then attack you.

## Notes
- Lights Out! originally had a scenario where you could help [Ma'zaka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27zaka) and [Captain Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md) get rid of the Blackblood Marauders. In this scenario, Aldis would help you defeat the marauders at the wreck of the Icerunner. The dialogue branch `MS07Deeja Bleedout` and related dialogue for Aldis show that Deeja could be killed, but also spared. If she was spared, Aldis would make sure she got locked up. It's unknown what would've transpired afterwards.
- The cell "Riften Plankside01" (where [From-Deepest-Fathoms](Skyrim_From-Deepest-Fathoms.md) usually wanders outside the Riften Fishery), contains a multitude of markers that all refer to Deeja: `MS04Deeja Docks Walk2`, `MS04Deeja Docks Wander4`, and `Deeja Docks Walk`. These markers imply that either Deeja was at one point involved in the quest [Unfathomable Depths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths), possibly instead of Fathoms, or that Fathoms originally carried Deeja's name. Besides these markers there's no evidence that shows that Deeja was in any way related to the quest.
