## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) after clearing out [Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_Fort_Hraggstad.md).
2. Go to [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md).
3. Pick Heavy, Medium, or Light Imperial armor.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### You're In the Army Now
After successfully clearing the [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) from [Fort Hraggstad](Skyrim_Fort_Hraggstad.md), your usefulness has been proven to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md). Your oath has been sworn; you are officially an Imperial soldier and protector of the Empire. It's time you look the part. [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) tells you to go to [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md), the [smith](Skyrim_Solitude_Blacksmith.md), to get outfitted with your own [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md). Talk to Beirand and choose your preferred weight:

| Armor Type | Helmet | Boots | Shield | Bracers | Armor |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Heavy | Imperial Helmet | Imperial Boots | Imperial Shield | Imperial Bracers | Imperial Armor |
| Medium | Imperial Light Helmet | Imperial Light Boots | Imperial Shield | Imperial Light Bracers | Studded Imperial Armor |
| Light | Imperial Light Helmet | Imperial Light Boots | Imperial Light Shield | Imperial Light Bracers | Imperial Light Armor |

When you are properly outfitted, Beirand will admonish you: *"Now, that's it. One free outfit per soldier. You lose that, or get it banged up, you'll need to pay like everyone else."*

## Notes
- It is not possible to do this quest if you first join the legion by giving the [Jagged Crown](Skyrim_Jagged_Crown.md) to General Tullius.
- Note that the Medium option contains one heavy armor piece (shield) and the rest are light armor pieces.
- Although Beirand tells you that you'll need to pay if you lose or damage the Imperial armor set he gave you, you can forge a new set yourself. - [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md) 1.9 makes it possible to forge Imperial Light Armor.
- After the completion of this quest, Beirand is no longer [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md#Beirund).
- Beirand's inventory will carry less items after this quest.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Get_Outfitted)*]</sup>

## Bugs
- If you join the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) (either [initially](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md) or by defecting after [The Jagged Crown](Skyrim_The_Jagged_Crown_(Imperial).md)), this quest is never shut down which leaves Beirand in a perpetual essential alias. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
