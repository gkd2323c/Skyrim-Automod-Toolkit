[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-place-Journeyman%27s_Nook.jpg/200px-SR-place-Journeyman%27s_Nook.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Journeyman%27s_Nook.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Journeyman%27s_Nook.jpg) Journeyman's Nook **Journeyman's Nook** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings) southeast of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) containing a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md).

## Walkthrough
The Nook is made of two concentric circles, with the living quarters in the innermost section. When you first arrive, a leveled bandit will be residing there. Entering the outer circle of the Nook, you will see windows around the perimeter of the inner wall. The entrance is toward the rear of the shelter on the north side. The Nook itself is cozy, with the exception of a slain college apprentice named [Borvir](Skyrim_Borvir.md) on the floor. An [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) is across the room, with a [potion of illusion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Illusion) and a [potion of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Strength). Borvir's body is on the floor by the lab, with three [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md) by his back and his [dagger](Skyrim_Borvir%27s_Dagger.md) just by his hand. Two barrels hold the usual clutter, but the crate on the east wall acting as a table has [frostbite venom](Skyrim_Frostbite_Venom.md), a [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka), a [flute](Skyrim_Flute.md), and an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Next to the satchel is copy of the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*. A bedroll is laid out by the fire, and a small table on the south wall, by the entrance, holds a [leg of goat roast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat_Roast) and a medium coin purse. A [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) is tucked away behind the table. Opening it reveals a [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing), a piece of jewelry, and [Treasure Map II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_II).

## Notes
- A small abandoned camp can be found northwest of Journeyman's Nook, past the leaning pillar. The camp contains two tents. One of the tents is occupied by a dead skeleton, while the other contains an adept-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=125603&y=86720&zoom=17&legacy=true).
- A steep, narrow trail north-northeast of Journeyman's Nook leads down to the sea. At the base of the trail, two [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md) sit in front of a small alcove in the cliff wall, which contains a novice-locked chest and a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](Skyrim_2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4.md)* [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=135134&y=87629&zoom=17&legacy=true). There is also a [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) on the ground in front of said alcove, as well as an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) on your left just before reaching the coast. A second iron ore vein is also nearby on the northwest side of the island, seen just a few yards across the coast.
- Borvir's Dagger respawns, along with the knapsack and apothecary's satchel.

## Bugs
- Borvir's Dagger might clip beneath the floor, making it unreachable without use of the [console](Skyrim_Console.md).
