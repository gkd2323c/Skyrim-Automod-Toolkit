Rogatus's Letter **by [Rogatus Salvius](Skyrim_Rogatus_Salvius.md)** *About coming home once in a while* *

[Leonitus](Skyrim_Leontius_Salvius.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*,

You idiot. When were you thinking of visiting your family? When [your mother](Skyrim_Vigdis_Salvius.md) was buried in the ground, or after some damned wizard roasts all my crops?

You make plans to come back to the [farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farmhouse.md) this season, or I'll put all your old things in a pile and use them as [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md).

Your Father,
[Rogatus](Skyrim_Rogatus_Salvius.md)
