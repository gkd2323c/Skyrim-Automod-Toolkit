[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-npc-Hert_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hert_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hert_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hert_02.jpg) Hert **Hert**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack), is a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) that owns and runs the [Half-Moon Mill](Skyrim_Half-Moon_Mill.md) with her husband [Hern](Skyrim_Hern.md). She is exceedingly warm with her greetings to strangers, but shows some aggravation towards the war's effect on her mill and the high frequency of passing patrols. She will buy firewood, and will also sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md) if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed. If her disposition is raised high enough, she will allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

Hert wakes up early at 7am and immediately starts chopping wood outside. After an hour of chopping wood she heads to the mill to work until 7pm, only having a small break to eat at 6pm. The remaining hours of the day she will spend just wandering around the mill until midnight, when she will have a last meal before going to bed.

She wears a set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). She also carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a [key to the millhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Half_Moon_Mill) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

- *"A guest, oh my!"*
- *"Why don't you stay a while?"*
- *"The patrols never seem to give us time to be alone, the nosy pests."*

- **What locations does this mill supply?**
- *"We supply [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) to the south. Until recently we supplied [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). Falkreath trades with the surrounding holds and without our wood they would have trouble keeping their prices low."*

- **Do you get many guests?**
- *"We have guests as often as we can but there aren't a lot of travellers these days. [The war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) keeps people off the roads."*

Once Hern is enabled, she gets a bit more dialogue and may now greet you with:

- *"Hern and I do so adore guests! These guards are such a bore."*

You now also have the option to ask if she likes living so far away from everything:

- **Do you like it out here, away from everything?**
- *"Other people just make things complex. Out here, Hern and I can get everything we need from the occasional traveler."*

## Notes
- With the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, Hert's appearance changes slightly, getting a new nose shape, glowing vampiric eyes instead of normal hazel brown ones, and the "Female Orc Vampire" complexion, rather than the default one.
- Even though Hert is a vampire herself, she will still become hostile towards you if you are a [blood-starved vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md#Stages_of_Vampirism).
- Hert often complains about the local guards, even though there are none to be found. There does exist guard dialogue talking about Hert, however. [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) guards would ask you, *"Is Hert his sister or his wife? Or both?"* While [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) guards would be just as confused, *"Hert keeps giving me this look. I don't know if she wants me or if she wants to eat me."*

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.5, addresses this issue. The AI marker was not placed close enough to the mill.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
- Hert has both her own plus Hern's work schedules, so they interfere trying to run the sawmill. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.8, addresses this issue. While Hert's Sawmill packages have a "Hern Sawmill" name, only Hert has the ability to buy and sell, so Hert now exclusively runs the sawmill, while Hern receives the Hert Chopping package, so that both of the occupants have something to do.
