# Banekin

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/ON-creature-Banekin.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Banekin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Banekin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Banekin.jpg) A Banekin Banekin are a small lesser [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) resembling a winged humanoid with mottled gray skin, a tail full of ridged spikes, pointed ears and horns on their heads. Banekin are associated with [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and use powerful shock spells in combat. They can speak Tamrielic, and are sometimes used as [familiars](Lore_Familiar.md) by powerful wizards,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOVal Fam-1)</sup> though they are unreliable due to their forgetfulness, as well as their inclination for cruelty and destruction.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DTTG-2)</sup> Banekin are agile daedra. Aside from being able to use their claws and tails full of ridged spines, they can use spells to weaken their foes as well as make them nauseous.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KCAHC-3)</sup>

They are not well treated by larger daedra, such as [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md), who torture loose banekin,<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Schick2015-4)</sup> and [Daedroths](Lore_Daedroth.md), who are known to eat them alive.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-G-Nazey-5)</sup> There has been one documented case of a Dremora in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) keeping a banekin in a crib, akin to a baby.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Foshaz-6)</sup>
