[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-npc-Derkeethus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Derkeethus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Derkeethus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Derkeethus.jpg) Derkeethus [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-quest-Extracting_an_Argonian.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Extracting_an_Argonian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Extracting_an_Argonian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Extracting_an_Argonian.jpg) Derkeethus at Darkwater Pass **Derkeethus** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) and a miner of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). Several residents of Darkwater Crossing may tell you he has gone missing before you find him in [Darkwater Pass](Skyrim_Darkwater_Pass.md), being held captive by [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md). Rescuing him will increase his [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you.

Derkeethus can become your [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) after rescue, as well as a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) and for joining the [Blades](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md). He is trained in [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) and [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) and tends to dual-wield weapons when not using a bow. However, like [Annekke](Skyrim_Annekke_Crag-Jumper.md), he equips a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) as an offhand weapon, and will not properly dual wield like [Jenassa](Skyrim_Jenassa.md).

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) supplemented by twelve [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). He also carries some [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

Once he is back in Darkwater Crossing, he will assume his normal schedule: he sleeps on a camp bed from midnight to 8am, and starts his day with a two-hour breakfast. He goes to work in the local [mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md) until 7pm, when he spends the evening relaxing by the campfire.

## Dialogue
Only two people back in Darkwater Crossing will tell you that he is missing. [Hrefna](Skyrim_Hrefna.md) will tell you, *"Derkeethus used to take me diving way down deep in the river. I miss him,"* while Derkeethus' friend, [Sondas Drenim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sondas_Drenim), has this to say about the crossing: *"Those falls... dangerous place. Derkeethus thought he could find some good fish in the pools. Haven't heard from him in a long while."*

If you manage to reach him, he will run up to you, giving you the option to ask who he is.

- **Who are you?**
- *"Derkeethus, from Darkwater Crossing. Should have known better than to swim all the way to the falls. Please, get me out of here!"*

He will follow you until you get him out of the cave, when he will exclaim;

- *"Thanks! I'm out!"*

After which he heads home.

## Notes
- Derkeethus is one of only three Argonians in the game that can be married, the others being [Scouts-Many-Marshes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scouts-Many-Marshes) and [Shahvee](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shahvee).
- He is also the only Argonian follower available in Skyrim.
- His mood is set to "afraid". As a result, his facial expression tends to show this.
- Before Derkeethus gets rescued, Sondas and Hrefna will mention him, but after he returns home, neither person will have anything to say about it. Derkeethus does not have any unique scenes set up with anyone in town, either.

## Bugs
- The quest to rescue Derkeethus would not properly shut down when Derkeethus arrived in Darkwater Crossing because the AI package for his alias was not marked as owned by the quest and thus would never trigger the shutdown command. This would lead to his AI being permanently stuck trying to return home, making it impossible to marry him and stay at [home](Skyrim_Houses.md), or recruit him as a [Blade](Skyrim_Blades.md) and have him stay at [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md). This would also result in him being permanently essential. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Derkeethus may occasionally follow you to the door of the cave but refuse to exit. Reloading older save points may not fix this issue. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12066))

- Waiting for the dungeon contents to respawn may fix this.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) A workaround for this problem is to go outside the cave, open the console and type `prid 0001B08D` and `moveto player` and then talk to him, this will finish the quest.
- Another workaround is to try to see if he will follow you through the second door out; both lead to the same entrance, but only one leads out.
- Derkeethus may be found in Darkwater Crossing. He will talk to you as if you're in the cave and thus initiate the quest. He can be stopped from following you by completing the quest through the "setstage" console command. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=12477))
- Derkeethus will occasionally be out of his cage, especially if you rest in the area above it. This makes it much easier to take him out of the Falmer infested area.
- If you marry Derkeethus and [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) a [child](Skyrim_Child.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, he cannot be asked how the children are doing as he has no recorded dialogue for it.
