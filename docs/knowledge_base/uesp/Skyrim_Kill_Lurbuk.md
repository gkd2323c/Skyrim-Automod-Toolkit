## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to [Moorside Inn](Skyrim_Moorside_Inn.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md).
2. Locate and kill [Lurbuk](Skyrim_Lurbuk.md).
3. Return to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is a very simple assassination. Lurbuk is not known to ever leave the [Moorside Inn](Skyrim_Moorside_Inn.md) and can easily be picked off with an arrow when he isn't performing. The difficulty lies in the fact that whatever you tell him, he will never turn hostile and attack, which would potentially increase the chances of earning a bounty, no matter what you say to him.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-quest-Kill_Lurbuk_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Kill_Lurbuk_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Lurbuk_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Lurbuk_02.jpg) Silence the merrymaker If you are having difficulty killing Lurbuk in the common room, you can wait until he either sits in the corner of his room or goes to sleep late at night. There should be no one else in the room, so a single sneak attack should be enough to eliminate him silently. It is also possible to wait until he's sitting in the common room, and then sneak into the room across from him, facing his back. From this position it can be safe to shoot him with an arrow, and as long as the arrow used can take him out with one blow, it will still be a stealthy kill. You can also rent a bed from the innkeeper. When she leaves to show you to your room, you can kill Lurbuk without her witnessing the kill. A creative, albeit risky, way to get the job done is to cast a Frenzy spell on him. Provided that you are not seen or heard, the effect will cause everyone in the inn to see Lurbuk as a hostile, attacking and killing him for you.

Another way to kill him is similar to the bow and arrow method above. Sneak into the room across from him and wait until you are hidden. Once hidden, release an offensive Shout (fire breath, frost breath, etc.) towards Lurbuk. This should do the trick since one Shout should be sufficient in killing him.

Note: invisibility helps with sneaking and hiding in the room across from Lurbuk.

## Notes
- If you have started but not yet completed the quest [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md), Lurbuk may still be standing outside of Movarth's Lair. For this reason, it is recommended that you either dismiss the townspeople or finish Laid to Rest. Otherwise, you will be spotted when killing Lurbuk and thus incur a bounty.
- If you ask Nazir for additional information, he will tell you that Lurbuk is so hated that he had multiple Brotherhood contracts out on him and [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) had to hold a lottery to decide who was able to put in the contract.
- It's quite possible for Lurbuk to have already died by the time you would normally get this contract quest. If this is the case, then the contract will just be skipped entirely.
