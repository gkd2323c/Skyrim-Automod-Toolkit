# Folium Discognitum

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Book_01.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Book_01.png) Folium Discognitum The Folium Discognitum is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). It is a tome of knowledge, filled with insights compiled from the ramblings of madmen. The pages bear near incomprehensible scrawls that move to avoid your gaze when read.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FG-1)</sup> The power obtained from reading these insights is the equivalent of absorbing at least six [skyshards](Lore_Skyshard.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sheo-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AMS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TMGB-4)</sup>

Sheogorath has been known to offer the tome to mortals as part of a deal. [Arch-Mage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md), ever hungry for knowledge, wagered the island of [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md) for the tome in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), and lost. He went on to regret the deal, and in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) he returned to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) to win back Eyevea from Sheogorath. [Valaste](Lore_Valaste.md), the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md)'s Master of Incunabula who aided Shalidor in his attempt, was slowly driven mad by Sheogorath. When the time came to reclaim the island, the Mad God gave an [adept](Lore_Hero.md) of the guild the choice to either restore Valaste's sanity, or take the Folium and allow Valaste to remain in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) for eternity. It is unknown which option was taken.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TMGB-4)</sup>
