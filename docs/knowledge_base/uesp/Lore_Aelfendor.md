Aelfendor is a kingdom and castle featured in the epic tale *[Song of Hrormir](Lore_Song_of_Hrormir.md)*, which was originally governed by the Sovereignty of three Dark Kings who were under the thumb of the Daedric Prince [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOH-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup> In the poem the hero mage Hrormir ventures into a conflict against Aelfendor on behalf of King Vjindak Evensnow, in most part to track down his battle buddy Darfang the Blade, who has fell in with the three Dark Kings, and to turn him back back to the side of good. Hrormir discovers that the Dark Kings and Darfang were under the control of Nocturnal, and using tickeries Hrormir forces her into releasing his friend and himself from servitude.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOH-2)</sup>
