# Dwemer

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Ghost_%28Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghost_%28Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).jpg) A Dwarven ghost The **Dwemer** (called **Dwarves** by men) are an extinct race of elves known for their technologically advanced society. Dwarven society began in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and spread to [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md), [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), and beyond. They disappeared suddenly in [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700), leaving behind [Dwarven ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) inhabited by their still-functioning [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).

For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Dwemer.md).

Only one Dwemer appears in Skyrim, added by the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation.md): the briefly-seen [Ghost](Skyrim_Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).md) encountered within [Fahlbtharz Forge](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz_Forge.md) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). Technically this ghost's race is [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), as the Dwemer do not exist as a distinct [race](Skyrim_Races.md) in *Skyrim*.
