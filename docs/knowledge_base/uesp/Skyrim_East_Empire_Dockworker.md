[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockworker_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockworker_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockworker_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Dockworker_02.jpg) Nord Dockworker The **East Empire Dockworkers** are employees found at the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md). The [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) dockworker wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). The [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) dockworker wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). Neither of the dockworkers carry anything in their inventory.

## Notes
- The dockworkers are replaced by [East Empire wardens](Skyrim_East_Empire_Warden.md) when the quest [Scoundrel's Folly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scoundrel%27s_Folly) is started.
- Both of the dockworkers are members of the [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) class, which determines the level of their skills.
- The Breton dockworker does not have a voice type.
- According to their respective refs, the Nord dockworker's name is **Slading** and the Breton's is **Jonne**.
- Neither of the dockworkers have any AI packages, resulting in them just standing in place unless provoked.
