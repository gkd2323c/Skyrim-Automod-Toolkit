[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-npc-Kibell.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kibell.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kibell.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kibell.jpg) Kibell **Kibell** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who drives the [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) [carriage](Skyrim_Carriage.md).

He will charge you a fare for traveling to a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) [capital](Skyrim_Cities.md). Going to the other four major hold capitals of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), and [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) costs 20 gold. Going to the four minor hold capitals of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), and [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) costs 50 gold. Carriages do not depart from the four minor hold capitals and cannot operate when you are overencumbered.

Kibell wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). He also carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
