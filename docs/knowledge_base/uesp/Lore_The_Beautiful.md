# The Beautiful

The Beautiful are a violent group of militants that appeared in [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). These radicals sought to destroy significant monuments and assassinate traditional leaders in an attempt to further modernity and reject their [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) heritage. Their assassination of the daughter of the King of [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md) horrified and outraged the public.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3-1)</sup>
