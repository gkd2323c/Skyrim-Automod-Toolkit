[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Nura_Snow-Shod.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nura_Snow-Shod.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nura_Snow-Shod.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nura_Snow-Shod.jpg) Nura Snow-Shod **Nura Snow-Shod** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [priestess](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest) of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), and wife to [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Vulwulf_Snow-Shod.md). She has two sons, [Asgeir](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md) and [Unmid Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Unmid_Snow-Shod.md). She also had a daughter, Lilija, whom she trained to replace her as both healer and priestess. However, Lilija joined the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) and was killed. Her daughter's death has increased Nura's strong feelings towards both Talos and Skyrim. She spends most nights and early mornings praying to the statue of Talos in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

Nura wakes up at 8am for a two-hour breakfast with Vulwulf and Asgeir. After this, she goes to the statue of Talos and tends it until 6pm to take a break for a two-hour dinner at her house. She then returns to the statue for the rest of the day. She is supposed to sleep at 12am, but does not due to a [bug](#Bugs). If the Imperial Legion takes over Riften, the ban on Talos worship will be enforced, causing her to stay home all day.

She wears [hooded monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Monk_Robes) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000bacd7). She carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow-Shod_House_Key) to her house, a leveled dagger, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), some [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), a fairly valuable staff (either a [Staff of the Flame Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Flame_Atronach) or a [Staff of Reanimation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Reanimation)), and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). She also carries some potions: a [potion of cure disease](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease), a [cure poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cure_Poison), and two leveled restore attribute potions. She knows the spells [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"How can a humble servant of Talos help you today?"* *"Talos bless you, my child."* *"Blessings of Talos upon you."* *"Blessings upon you, traveler."* Speaking to her:

**What can you tell me about Talos?** *"He's the protector of Man, the Dragon of the North, the patron of all Nords. And despite the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and their treaty, he will always be one of the Nine Divines. A devout Nord would rather fall in battle and have his blood spill upon the earth than allow the Empire to strike his name from the divinity."* **Why all the hatred for the Empire?** *"Talos is part of our culture. He's part of our heritage and our tradition. He's Skyrim personified. To suddenly have all that wrenched away is like losing someone you hold dearly in your heart. The Empire already took my daughter from us, but by Talos they will not take our homeland!"* **What happened to your daughter?** *"I trained our daughter Lilija as a healer... to one day tend the Shrine of Talos and replace me as a Priestess of Talos. When the fighting began over the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), she ran off to join the Stormcloaks. She had her father's fire, how could I say no? She died out there, only having tended to the wounded soldiers for a few months. We never even found her body... the Empire burned all the dead."* When you end conversation with her:

*"Talos preserve you."* *"Talos guide you."* *"Farewell."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Spread the Love
Despite her being a priest, if you [give](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love) her a [religious pamphlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara), she will be disgusted:

**Here, sister. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"Ugh. Get this awful paper away from me, by Talos!"*
### Bound Until Death
During this quest, Nura and Vulwulf will be found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) attending Asgeir's wedding to [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md), an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md). Nura may comment with:

*"If my Asgeir is happy, then I'm happy. Really."* *"To think, my little boy is married. And to an Imperial. Oh, they grow up so fast."* *"Vittoria may be pretty, but does she have the right hips for the job? I want grandchildren, and lots of them."* **Having a good time?** *"Oh. Why... why certainly. I keep telling myself I'm not losing a son, I'm gaining an Imperial. Daughter. An Imperial daughter..."* As Vittoria Vici lies dead, Nura can be found yelling:

*"By Ysmir, this is horrible! I never wanted the girl dead, I swear!"*

## Notes
- If the Imperial Legion takes over Riften, you are supposed to be able to ask Nura what happened to the shrine of Talos. She would have bitterly told you, *"Now that the Imperial blight has poisoned Riften, the worship of Talos is strictly forbidden by the terms of the White-Gold Concordat. They can defile our shrine, they can arrest our people and they can silence our voices, but the Empire will never destroy our spirit. As long as we keep Talos in our heart, his legacy will never die."* However, since she stays home all the time, you will have to trespass to enter her house and speak to her, causing all her dialogue to be replaced with generic lines telling you to leave.
