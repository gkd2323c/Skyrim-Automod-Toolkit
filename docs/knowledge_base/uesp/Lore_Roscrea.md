# Roscrea

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-symbol-Solitude.png/200px-SR-symbol-Solitude.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-symbol-Solitude.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-symbol-Solitude.png) Symbol of Solitude **Roscrea** is an island that was conquered by [Uriel Septim V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_V) in [3E 271](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_271) during his travel between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3SK-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Late in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), one of the richest and most influential counties in Skyrim, started to expand its influence by annexing several former [Imperial](Lore_Empire.md) territories, including Roscrea.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3SK-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Roscrea shares its name with the real-life [Roscrea](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roscrea) of [Ireland](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ireland). Whether this was intentional remains unknown.
