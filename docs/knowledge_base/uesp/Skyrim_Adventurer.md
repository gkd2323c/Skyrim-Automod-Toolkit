[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-npc-Adventurer_%28Harmugstahl%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Adventurer_%28Harmugstahl%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adventurer_(Harmugstahl).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adventurer_(Harmugstahl).jpg) Harmugstahl This **Adventurer** is alive and can be initially encountered in [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md) fighting an enchanted [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). When he exits Harmugstahl he disappears from the game.

He wears a set of regular [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), while his gauntlets and boots are leveled [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor) of the best possible quality. He wields a randomly determined weapon (up to elven quality at level 22), either a [battle axe](Skyrim_Battle_Axe.md), a [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md), or a [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer). Additionally, he carries a small amount of gold.

### Dialogue
After the battle he will sheath his weapon and say:

- *"That was a close one!"*
- **What was going on with that spider?**
- *"I don't know, but this place is full of them. Every one I've encountered has been enchanted or the like. Look, I'm getting out of here. Normally, spiders make my skin crawl, but magic spiders? Blech."*

He will then head for the entrance, but will add:

- *"Is there anything more revolting than spiders? Too many legs..."*

### Notes
- This adventurer has no crime faction assigned and will therefore talk to [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) and [Vampire Lords](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.

He carries a copy of the book *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*, as well as a [note](Skyrim_Torn_Note_(Dragonborn).md) which reveals that he came to the ruin in search of [treasure](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md#A_Cursed_Trove). He couldn't find anything, but noticed a draft in the room which led him to believe there may be a secret passage. He locked himself in the ruin to continue searching while waiting for bandits outside to clear the area.

He wears [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers). He also carries a small amount of gold.
