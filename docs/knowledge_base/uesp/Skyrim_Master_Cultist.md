# Cultist

**Cultists** are mysterious followers of [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md), the first [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md), who live in his [temple](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md) on the island of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) and carry out his wishes. Once you have been recognized as Dragonborn during the quest [The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md), a pair of cultists will be sent to [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) to hunt you and kill you for being a "false" Dragonborn. All cultists are powerful mages, relying on summoning and elemental magic spells in combat. The type of cultist you encounter depends on your level, and you will continue to meet low-level cultists even at high levels.

### Cultists
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Cultist_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Cultist_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cultist_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cultist_02.jpg) A Cultist firing a shock spell Cultists are members of the [Conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) NPC class and use [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells in battle. All cultists of Miraak wear a uniform of [robes](Skyrim_Cultist_Robes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Cultist_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Cultist_Gloves.md), and an eerie bone [mask](Skyrim_Cultist_Mask.md). Arch Cultists wear a standard [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) outfit instead, consisting of a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000c5d12) along with [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md), [hooded black mage robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes.md), robes of [Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka_Rate.md).

Cultists also carry a leveled dagger ([iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), [steel](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), [orcish](Skyrim_Orcish_Dagger.md), [dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md), [elven](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md), [glass](Skyrim_Glass_Dagger.md), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) or Destruction staff.

In addition to the gear listed above, cultists have a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). If the quest [Conjuration Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Conjuration_Ritual_Spell.md) or [Good Intentions](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md) was completed, they are also carrying up to two [atronach forge recipes](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge_Recipe.md) in their inventories - one at 2% chance for each completed quest.

On death, cultists have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis.md)
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie.md)
- **Destruction:** [Scroll of Blizzard](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Blizzard.md), [Scroll of Fire Storm](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Fire_Storm.md)
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms.md), [Scroll of Harmony](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Harmony.md), [Scroll of Hysteria](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Hysteria.md), [Scroll of Mayhem](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Mayhem.md)
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead.md), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle.md)

Master Cultists and Arch Cultists have the same miscellaneous items and daggers as other cultists, and have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Staff_of_Chain_Lightning.md), [Staff of Fireballs](Skyrim_Staff_of_Fireballs.md), or a [Staff of Ice Storms](Skyrim_Staff_of_Ice_Storms.md) and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

### Summoner Cultists
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-npc-Cultist_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Cultist_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cultist_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Cultist_03.jpg) A Cultist with a flame atronach ally These cultists have the ability to use Destruction magic as well, but prefer to summon [flame atronachs](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) and [seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md) to fight alongside them. They wear the same cultist's outfit as the cultists listed above, except Master Cultists and Arch Cultists, who wear the warlock outfit instead. Otherwise, their weapons, staves, and miscellaneous inventory items are the same as non-summoner cultists.

### Dragonborn
Once the main quest [The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md) is finished, you will be approached by a pair of hostile cultists at the first city or town you enter. These cultists will question you with *"You there! You're the one they call Dragonborn?"* You have several dialogue options with which to respond:

| Player Response | Cultists' Response |
| --- | --- |
| **"Yes, I am Dragonborn."** | *"Your lies fall on deaf ears, Deceiver. The True Dragonborn comes... You are but his shadow."* |
| **"The Greybeards seem to think so."** | *"Then it is too late. The lie has already taken root in the hearts of men. So we shall expose them to the falseness in their hearts by tearing out yours, Deceiver!"* |
| **"I don't know what you're talking about."** | *"Your lies fall on deaf ears, Deceiver! We know you are the False Dragonborn! You shall not stand in the way of the true Dragonborn's return. He comes soon, and we shall offer him your heart!"* |

Each response will eventually lead to them saying: *"When Lord Miraak appears all shall bear witness. None shall stand to oppose him!"* They will turn hostile and engage you in combat immediately thereafter.

You can also exit conversation with them, though they will still turn hostile immediately:
*"You cannot escape the truth. You cannot escape the True Dragonborn! When Lord Miraak appears all shall bear witness. None will stand to oppose him!"*
