They wear a full set of [Imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md) and are armed with an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) with fifteen [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). If engaged in melee, they will fall back to defending themselves with a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and an [Imperial shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Shield.md).
