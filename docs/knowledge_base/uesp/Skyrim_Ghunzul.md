[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Ghunzul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghunzul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghunzul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghunzul.jpg) Ghunzul **Ghunzul**, an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is the leader of the bandits in [Cracked Tusk Keep](Skyrim_Cracked_Tusk_Keep.md). He is the 8th of his family line to bear the [blade shards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shards_of_Mehrunes%27_Razor) of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor).

He wears a leveled set of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md), which may be up to [steel plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate) or [Nordic Carved](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Carved_Armor) in quality, and is armed with a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md), which may be up to [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) in quality. He also carries a spare leveled dagger that may be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality, and a leveled amount of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[Pieces of the Past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past)**: Retrieve the scattered pieces of [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor) and reforge this deadly blade.
