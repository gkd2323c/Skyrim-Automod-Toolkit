## Ramshackle Trading Post
As the name suggests, the building is derelict, with the doors, windows, and part of the roof missing, and large holes in the walls. The shack is against a cliff facing a valley down to the sea. In the northeast corner of the interior is a small round table with two overturned chairs and an upright chair, which has clearly been used by someone drinking [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), judging by the three [empty wine bottles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle) on the floor and the full wine bottle on the table. In the northwest corner is a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Note) pinned to the wall by a leveled [arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows), which reads *Meet me here at midnight.* Outside the shack, against the west wall is a crate holding an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox). In front of the shack are two [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants and an empty barrel.

## Notes
- If you arrive between midnight 12am and 4am, you will find [Falas Selvayn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falas_Selvayn) here, sitting at the table and willing to trade with you. However, arriving before midnight and waiting will not cause him to appear.
