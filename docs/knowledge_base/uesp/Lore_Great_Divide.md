[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/AR-map-Elsweyr.jpg/200px-AR-map-Elsweyr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Elsweyr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Elsweyr.jpg) Map showing the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md), which overlooks the Great Divide The Great Divide is a large stretch of road found along the southern coast of the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). It is anchored by the city-state of [Torval](Lore_Torval.md) in the west, and the town of Darvulk Haven in the east. The [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md) overlooks the Great Divide.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Great Divide is only mentioned in passing in [*TES I: Arena*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena), and does not actually appear in game.
