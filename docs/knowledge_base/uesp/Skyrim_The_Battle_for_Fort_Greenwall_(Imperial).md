## Quick Walkthrough
1. Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at the [Rift Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp).
2. Join the Legion soldiers staging for the attack.
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Report for Duty
Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at the [Rift Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp), where she will give you orders to rendezvous with a group of Imperial soldiers preparing to attack [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md) and to assist in the assault.

### Join the Soldiers Preparing the Assault
Make your way to Fort Greenwall. The fort is just north of Riften, and the soldiers are waiting just southeast of the fort. As you approach the group, they will storm the southeast entrance to the fort.

### Take the Fort
Assist your fellow soldiers in clearing the fort of Stormcloak rebels while the game updates you with the percentage of enemies remaining. The battle takes place in the exterior parts of the fort only, in the courtyard and on the walls. The entrance is blocked by wooden barricades, but these can be easily brought down by melee attacks.

Once you have killed all Stormcloak rebels, the quest will be completed.

### Report to General Tullius
Your objective then becomes to report to General Tullius at Castle Dour to receive your reward, but doing so technically occurs under the overarching [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md) quest. After you have taken the fort, The Battle for Fort Greenwall will clear from your journal. If you didn't flee the battle, Tullius will promote you to [Tribune](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) along with a leveled sword (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

### Achievements
One [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) is unlocked at the end of this quest:

- [![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/30px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) **[War Hero](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Hero)** (10 points/Bronze) — Capture [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) or [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md)

## Notes
- Corpses will disappear shortly after death, so you may wish to loot them as you go to avoid missing out.
- If [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) is given to the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) then this quest will not be given to you.
- Even after you are told that 0% of the defenders remain and that you have won, there may still be several Stormcloak survivors who flee northeast along the road in the direction of the mining settlement of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md). If you decide to pursue them, be careful of who you target, especially in the dark, since you can easily kill some of the local guards of Shor's Stone by mistake and incur a bounty in the Rift.
- Once the battle begins at the fort, Rikke leaves The Rift Imperial Camp and it is instead commanded by [Legate Fasendil](Skyrim_Legate_Fasendil.md). After the battle is finished and the quest clears in the journal, the Rift Imperial Camp will be packed up and abandoned.
- Completing this quest will exile [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) and her courtiers to the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md), and install [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of The Rift.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md#Bugs) bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.

## Bugs
- After [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial).md), if General Tullius will promote you to Praefect rank you are actually promoted to Tribune with related leveled shield. It is not possible to get the actual Praefect rank. Since you already are, this will also "skip" the actual promotion to Tribune after The Battle for Fort Greenwall. <sup>**?**</sup> **Mod Notes**: mistake in the script TIF__000CE0E8: there is "3", should be "2".
