**King** Ja-Fr was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) ruler of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). He was a [Crown](Lore_Crown.md) of the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md) and governed from [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRLo S-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/AR-place-Hegathe.jpg/200px-AR-place-Hegathe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Hegathe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Hegathe.jpg) Hegathe, Ja-Fr's seat of power Little is known about Ja-Fr's reign or the events of his rule. As the King, he wore the [Diadem of Diagna](Lore_Diadem_of_Diagna.md) and sat upon the Throne of Hegathe. He was remembered as an estimable man and father. However, he failed to decisively suppress the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) during his reign. He later died and was succeeded by his son, [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md). His actions as ruler, particularly his failure to defeat the Forebears, contributed to the Crowns' distrust of Ar-Azal upon his accession to the Throne of Hegathe.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

Ja-Fr's descendants remained influential in Hammerfell for generations, holding positions of power through the Second Era.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRLo S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TABKF-3)</sup>
