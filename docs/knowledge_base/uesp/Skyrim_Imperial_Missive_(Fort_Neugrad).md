[Legate](Skyrim_Legate_Skulnar.md),

As ordered, I have assumed command of [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md). Morale is low, and the ongoing chaos in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) has left our supply lines dangerously vulnerable. [Pale Pass](Skyrim_Pale_Pass.md) is all but closed due to avalanches in the mountains. We need more support, or our garrison will not withstand another attack.
