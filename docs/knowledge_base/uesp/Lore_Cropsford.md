[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/OB-place-Cropsford.jpg/200px-OB-place-Cropsford.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Cropsford.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Cropsford.jpg) Cropsford circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Cropsford is a settlement located along the [Yellow Road](Lore_Yellow_Road.md) in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The land is in the center of [goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) territory, which has historically caused trouble for settlers.

## History
The early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) master vase painter Martina Russus hailed from Cropsford. She specialized in black figure pottery and created a relic of [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) worship.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PSR-1)</sup>

During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a large town was located at Cropsford. It was primarily a farming settlement, and had a shrine dedicated to [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). Settlement was strongly encouraged by the townsfolk, many of whom were not natives of Cyrodiil and decided to stay after passing through. The town was constantly plagued by the undead from [Culotte](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Culotte), and the [Bloody Hand Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bloody_Hand_Tribe) of [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md). Cropsford was initially located within the territory held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), although the town and the nearby [Drakelowe Keep](Lore_Drakelowe_Keep.md) changed hands many times over the course of the war. It was feared at the time that the encroaching goblins planned a full-scale attack.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The town's troubles were clearly too great to overcome, as the settlement was later abandoned. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the land was offered cheaply by the government, and a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) family from [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) purchased it. Their plans to build a farm at Cropsford were dashed when they arrived to discover that the land was the center of an ongoing goblin war between the Bloody Hand Tribe and the [Rock Biter Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rock_Biter_Tribe) over a stolen totem. The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) put an end to the war, allowing construction to begin. It was hoped that Cropsford would one day become a thriving town.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The Cropsford Hen is a beloved breed of [chicken](Lore_Chicken.md) that originated in Cropsford. The breed is commonly found on farms in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) due to the staunchly protective nature of the hens; they have been known to herd the rest of the flock away from dangers such as circling [hawks](Lore_Hawk.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
