Rayelle was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) princess from the Kingdom of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md). She was the daughter of King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md), the last monarch of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) from [House Branquette](Lore_House_Branquette.md). Rayelle was King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md)'s first choice for his royal bride, but before the marriage contracts were worked out, he chose instead to marry Princess [Maraya](Lore_Maraya.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md). Emeric not marrying Rayelle enraged Ranser, sparking [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), a civil war between Rivenspire and the rest of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOKR-1)</sup>

## History
Much of what is known about Rayelle comes from a piece of propaganda written by [House Tamrith](Lore_House_Tamrith.md) called *[The Story of Princess Eselde](Lore_The_Story_of_Princess_Eselde.md)*. She was the daughter of King Ranser (known in the book as King Lancer) and his queen, whom the book calls Ignort of House Dull.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOPE-2)</sup> Rayelle was their only known child.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOKR-1)</sup> On her Naming Day, nobles from all over Rivenspire came and laid presents at her cradle. According to the book, Rayelle grew up to be as beautiful as a rose but cross-grained and petulant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOPE-2)</sup>

When Emeric of Cumberland became the King of Wayrest in [2E 563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563), he and his advisors quickly began considering who he should select as his queen consort. Emeric was often offered Rayelle's hand in marriage by Ranser.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM6-3)</sup> Emeric almost decided to marry Rayelle,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM6-3)</sup> who wrote to Emeric with half-hearted and unenthusiastic letters at her father's request.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KHL-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONCP-5)</sup> However, Emeric decided instead to marry Maraya of Sentinel, a strategic move to strengthen trade between High Rock and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), which infuriated Rayelle's father, leading to the start of Ranser's War soon after Emeric's marriage in [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM6-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOKR-1)</sup>

After Ranser's death and the conclusion of the war in 2E 567, Rayelle disappeared.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOPE-2)</sup> After the conflict, a rumor spread throughout [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) that Ranser had engaged in incestuous relationships with several siblings in the royal line, resulting in Rayelle's birth; however, scholars dismiss this claim.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LABAHI-6)</sup> By 2E 582, her love letters were kept in storage in the Shornhelm [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and eventually stolen by the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) of [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KHL-4)</sup>

Shortly before the outbreak of Ranser's War, she is said to have become pregnant and later gave birth to [Uthard Scoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uthard_Scoth), whose father was unknown. He was never acknowledged.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HRGazetteer-7)</sup><sup>:19-20</sup>
