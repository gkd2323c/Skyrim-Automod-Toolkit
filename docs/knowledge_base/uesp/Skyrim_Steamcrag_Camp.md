## Walkthrough
One [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) and up to two [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) occupy the camp. An [official warning](Skyrim_Official_warning.md) posted on the nearby signpost grants the giant permission to camp there and warns citizens not to interfere with him. In the center of the camp is a large campfire with two [horse haunches](Skyrim_Horse_Haunch.md) and a [charred skeever](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Meat.md) cooking beside it. A [giant club](Skyrim_Giant_Club.md) is near the campfire (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Southwest of the fire is a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md) and an [ice wolf pelt](Skyrim_Ice_Wolf_Pelt.md), while to the north is a [dead Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit_(dead).md). To the northwest near a large rock is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) and two [mammoth cheese bowls](Skyrim_Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl.md); taking the cheese bowls counts as theft. Two haunches of raw [horse meat](Skyrim_Horse_Meat.md) and an unowned mammoth cheese bowl sit on a rock to the right. On the rocks above is a fire with a dead [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md) and a dead [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) cooking on top. The [wolf's pelt](Skyrim_Wolf_Pelt.md) can be looted from its body, while the [horse's hide](Skyrim_Horse_Hide.md) lies beside the fire. [Jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md) and [dragon's tongue](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Tongue.md) grow in the vicinity.

To the southeast, near the road and the signpost, is a destroyed carriage with another dead Khajiit, a dead horse, various common books strewn about, an adept-locked chest, a barrel containing ingredients, several food sacks, and a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md). The dead Khajiit near the campfire may have come from this caravan to attempt to barter with the giant.

## Notes
- Due to the camp's proximity to the dragon lair at [Bonestrewn Crest](Skyrim_Bonestrewn_Crest.md), you may attract the attention of the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) while dealing with the giant and mammoths. This may be either helpful or harmful depending on whether the parties attack you or each other.
- South-southeast of the camp, atop a large rocky hill, is a [shrine of Akatosh](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Akatosh) on a stone bier. The three leveled [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) near the surrounding pillars will rise and attack when you approach the bier. A leveled war axe and a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Breathing Water](Skyrim_Breathing_Water.md)* are next to the shrine.

## Bugs
- The mammoth cheese containers here are erroneously marked as owned by the giants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- The giant club cannot be used by you, but can be picked up by a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) if you order them to do so. However, it will then be impossible to remove it from their inventory. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
