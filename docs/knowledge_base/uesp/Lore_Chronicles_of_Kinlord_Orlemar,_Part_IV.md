An account of the second [Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) kinlord to reign in [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md).

[1E 1089](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1089): The Studies of the Kinlord

[Kinlord Orlemar](Lore_Orlemar_Corelanya.md) studied [Lirunalion](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Lirunalion)'s [accounts](Lore_The_Guardians_of_Mor_Naril.md) of [Kinheir Iniel](Lore_Iniel_Corelanya.md)'s earlier expeditions to Mor Naril at length and spent some time retracing her steps. Mindful of the island [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md)' superstitions regarding the [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md), Orlemar left the central temple undisturbed. However, he brought away many curious relics, including crystal eyes from the large Argonian statues that stand watch over the ruins. It is said the statues once [came to life](Lore_Voskrona_Guardian.md) to battle whatever inhabited the temple, but the eyes are mere curiosities now.

[1E 1107](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1107): Vaedinhill Manor
The kinlord established his new countryside home at Vaedinhill, building a fine manor overlooking the forests of eastern [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md) that was far from the politics of Sunport. Here he arranged his library and collection of mystic treasures and relics, making it the wonder of all Solstice. Many of the more valuable relics, such as the Eyes of the Colossal, Kinlord Orlemar secured in a great vault beneath Vaedinhill Manor. That vault, said to be impenetrable, held his treasures until such time as he decided to display them to scholars, nobles, and other interested parties.
