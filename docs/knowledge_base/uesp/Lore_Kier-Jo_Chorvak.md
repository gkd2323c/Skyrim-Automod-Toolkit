# Kier-Jo Chorvak

| Kier-Jo Chorvak | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) | Gender | Male |

Kier-Jo Chorvak<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWEM-1)</sup> (sometimes **Kiergo Chorvak**<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWED-2)</sup> or **Kier-Jo the Chorvak**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dgposts-3)</sup> was a descendant of the famed [King Keirgo](Lore_Keirgo.md) of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) and a published scholar of the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWEM-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dgposts-3)</sup> Additionally, Kier-Jo held the title of "Knight of [the Mane](Lore_Mane.md)", sometimes stylized as "Western Knight of the Sandy Mane."<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TDM-4)</sup> Kier-Jo was fond of [elves](Lore_Elf.md) and [nymphs](Lore_Nymph.md), and had a weakness for "elven beauty."<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dgposts-3)</sup> He authored a scholarly work on the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) called *[The Wild Elves](Lore_The_Wild_Elves.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWEM-1)</sup> Despite his scholarly status, Kier-Jo is often described as a liar.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TDM-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dgposts-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The name of this character was altered slightly between *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* to *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, with the spelling being modified from "Kiergo" to "Kier-Jo."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWED-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWEM-1)</sup> *[Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard)* materials later seem to reference this earlier spelling, with [Black Kiergo](Lore_Black_Keirgo.md) and King Keirgo being coined.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1ETEC-5)</sup> Designer [Douglas Goodall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Douglas_Goodall) reckoned that King Keirgo was Kier-Jo's ancestor in explanation, despite the small difference in spelling.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Dgposts-3)</sup>
