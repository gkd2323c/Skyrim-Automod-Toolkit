Mansel Sesnit was an Elden Yokeda of the [Yokudan Empire](Lore_Yokudan_Empire.md). He became the Elden Yokeda, or military dictator, of old [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) in [1E 609](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_609) and succeeded for eight years in gaining control of almost the whole empire. [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md)'s grandfather was a retainer of Sesnit and led many of the battles of unification during his reign. He was assassinated in [1E 617](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_617) and replaced by a commoner, [Randic Torn](Lore_Randic_Torn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTHATH-1)</sup> He is noted for his brief but eventful governance, which laid much of the structural groundwork that later [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) rulers would utilize.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOOrthuna-2)</sup>

His crown was brought to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) after the sinking of Yokuda, where it eventually ended up on display in [Rulanyil's Fall](Lore_Rulanyil%27s_Fall.md) during the mid-Second Era.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMC-3)</sup> A number of his paperweights were also found around this time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cont Paper-4)</sup>
