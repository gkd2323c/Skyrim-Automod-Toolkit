Notice of Sale - Dwarven Mudcrab **by [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md)** *A letter informing you of a potential purchase*
---
During my excavations, I came across a quite peculiar little fellow - a [Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md), clad in plated Dwarven armor. He's quite friendly, but with all of my studies I have no time to take care of such a thing.

From what I have seen, you seem to be quite trustworthy. Would you be interested in purchasing him? If so, please meet with me in my laboratory in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md).

- [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md)
