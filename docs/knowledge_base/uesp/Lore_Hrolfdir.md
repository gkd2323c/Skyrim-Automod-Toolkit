Hrolfdir was the Jarl of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) and the father of [Igmund](Lore_Igmund.md). In 4E 174, the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md), led by [Madanach](Lore_Madanach.md), took advantage of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md)'s preoccupation with the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) to overthrow Hrolfdir and declare [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) to be an independent kingdom.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBo M-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup> By [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176), Hrolfdir was desperate to retake the Reach, so he turned to a Nord militia led by [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) and promised them that he would permit the free worship of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) in Markarth, which was outlawed under the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md) that ended the Great War, in exchange for their help retaking the hold.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBo M-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup> In a short and bloody war, Ulfric and the Nord militia laid siege to Markarth, and when they breached the city gates, they drove the Reachman rebels from the city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBo M-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup> The surviving Reachman rebels fled into the hills and continued to fight the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) in the [Forsworn Rebellion](Lore_Forsworn_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBo M-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sr Kd-4)</sup>

As a condition to return to Markarth, Hrolfdir, his son Igmund, and his steward Raerek swore oaths to the Empire to abandon the worship of Talos.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sr Rd-5)</sup> Following the retaking Markarth, Hrolfdir reportedly ordered the executions of Reachmen with suspected ties to the rebels.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Sr Bd-6)</sup> After Ulfric and his militia retook Markarth, Hrolfdir allowed them to worship Talos in the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup> Shortly after the retaking of Markarth, the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) found out about the promise of legal Talos worship.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup> Faced with the threat of a second war with the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), Hrolfdir helped the Imperial Legion arrest Ulfric Stormcloak and much of the Nord militia in an event called the Markarth Incident.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:501</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Sr Ced-7)</sup>

After the Markarth Incident, Hrolfdir attempted to negotiate peace with the remaining Reachman rebels, who were now calling themselves the Forsworn.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup> While Hrolfdir was attempting to negotiate with the Forsworn, they killed him and took his shield.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:504</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Sr Fd-8)</sup> Eventually, the Forsworn gave his shield to one of their [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) matrons as a trophy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup> Igmund succeeded him as Jarl of Markarth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESVSPOGG-3)</sup><sup>:504</sup> He was remembered as an active leader, one who did not sit idly by while evil men took over his lands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sr JId-2)</sup>
