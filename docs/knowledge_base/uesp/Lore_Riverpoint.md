[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/AR-place-Riverpoint.jpg/200px-AR-place-Riverpoint.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverpoint.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverpoint.jpg) Riverpoint during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Riverpoint (also called **River Point**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> is a town found in central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), north of the city-states of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) and [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md).

The town of River Point has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Riverpoint was an active settlement. It was ruled by Prince Khajun and had a rivalry with [Roseguard](Lore_Roseguard.md) and [Helland](Lore_Helland.md). Taverns in the village included the Blue Ogre, Gold Goblin, Haunted Huntsman, and Howling Djinn inns, and religious groups of the [Conclave of the One](Lore_The_One.md) and Order of the Golden Tomb had temples in the town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Riverpoint is neighbored by several settlements, including [Carmont](Lore_Cartmont.md) to the south, [Karnver Falls](Lore_Karnver_Falls.md) to the west, Vulkneu Town to the east, and [Verkarth City](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md) to the northwest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
