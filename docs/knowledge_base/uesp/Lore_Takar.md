Takar was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) warrior from the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). He was a general of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and one of Emperor [Titus Mede I](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md)'s most trusted generals. Before he joined the Emperor, he fought against the Empire. It was rumored that Titus Mede won him over through personal combat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:250</sup>

During the [Umbriel Crisis](Lore_Umbriel_Crisis.md) in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), in an attempt to stop [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) from reaching the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), General Takar led five thousand soldiers to confront the flying city's undead soldiers near the [Blue Road](Lore_Blue_Road.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:250-252, 254</sup> His force mostly consisted of mounted infantry and [battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md), and they brought eight large wagons that were possibly siege weapons.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:250</sup> Once they encountered Umbriel's army, [Synod](Lore_Synod.md) mages used spells to lift almost three thousand soldiers toward the flying city, but [soul-spinners](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Soul-spinner) killed them, so Takar and the remaining troops retreated.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:250-252, 254</sup>
