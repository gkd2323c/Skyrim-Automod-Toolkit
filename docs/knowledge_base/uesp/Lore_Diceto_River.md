[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/ON-place-Diceto_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Diceto_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Diceto_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Diceto_River.jpg) Diceto River The Diceto River is a waterway flowing from the mountains south of the city of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), through the city to the waters of the [Cape of the Blue Divide](Lore_Cape_of_the_Blue_Divide.md) and to the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md). It is the great river that passes through the royal parks of Firsthold. The Diceto River flows through the entire city in a Y shape, and [kanthleaf](Lore_Kanthleaf.md) is found all over the riverbank.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-W-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSM-2)</sup>
