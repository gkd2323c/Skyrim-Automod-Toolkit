[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/BT-concept-Deslandra_Vampiric.png/200px-BT-concept-Deslandra_Vampiric.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-concept-Deslandra_Vampiric.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-concept-Deslandra_Vampiric.png) Vampiric Deslandra in Skyrim Pale Fingers was a [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) from [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md) who served as an agent of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) mage [Deslandra](Lore_Deslandra.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOTSEAB-1)</sup><sup>:60</sup> and the champion of a group of Falmer raiders.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:34</sup> Deslandra had made an alliance with the Falmer and several of the other inhabitants of Blackreach in exchange for their little-understood magic.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:52</sup> Pale Fingers and the Falmer weren't so much controlled by Deslandra as they were "let loose". Pale Fingers would wreak his particular brand of havoc on the settlements of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) while Deslandra executed her plans underground.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOTSEAB-1)</sup><sup>:60</sup>

At some point circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), these Falmer launched multiple savage raids on cities and settlements in [Eastern Skyrim](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Card-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:34</sup> These raids made the existence of the Falmer, previously assumed by many Nords to have simply been fairy tales,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mo NF-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ST-5)</sup> much more well-known.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Card-3)</sup> Pale Fingers in particular soon became legendary for his frightening visage and proficiency in city sieges. Whisperings of his presence made people more afraid of exploring Skyrim's caves.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Card-3)</sup> While Pale Fingers was participating in a raid on the city of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), the Outer Watch became aware of the strange Falmer behavior in Skyrim and came to suspect their former member, Deslandra, was responsible. The Outer Watch would arrive to provide aid to Windhelm, possibly coming into confrontation with Pale Fingers himself.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:34</sup> Afterwards, they would learn the truth of Deslandra's alliance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:52</sup> Pale Fingers is believed to have perished protecting Deslandra's base of operations in Skyrim, [Greymoor Keep](Lore_Greymoor_Keep.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:56</sup>
