[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-place-Left_Hand_Mine_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-place-Left_Hand_Mine_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Left_Hand_Mine_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Left_Hand_Mine_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg) Miner's Barracks **Left Hand Miner's Barracks** is the collective housing area for several miners who work in the [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine).

It is the home of three of the miners from [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine), [Adeber](Skyrim_Adeber.md), [Ennoc](Skyrim_Ennoc.md) and [Willem](Skyrim_Willem.md). It consists of one area only, *[Miner's Barracks](#Miner.27s_Barracks)*

Before the quest [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest).md) is completed, two refugees will also live in the barracks, namely [Pavo Attius](Skyrim_Pavo_Attius.md) and [Gat gro-Shargakh](Skyrim_Gat_gro-Shargakh.md).

## Miner's Barracks
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.jpg) The Barracks Interior The barracks are obviously only used for sleeping and are pretty much empty, save for a table with three bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and a basket with a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel) and some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese). There are two bedrolls on the floor and, along the western wall, a bed with a belongings chest with minor loot and a bucket with three samples of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md). All items are free to take and the beds are free to sleep in.
