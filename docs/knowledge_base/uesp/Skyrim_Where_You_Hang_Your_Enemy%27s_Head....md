## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) in the new [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).
2. Speak to [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md).
3. Purchase the upgrades.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) will instruct you to go and speak to [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) in [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) about restoring the newly reacquired [Dawnstar Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md). Travel to The Ragged Flagon and speak to Delvin. He is more than happy to restore anything you want for the prices listed below.

After buying all the upgrades, talking to Delvin Mallory once more about the Dawnstar Sanctuary will complete the quest. You will have just 1,000 gold left of your previous reward after purchasing all of the upgrades.

## Bugs
- Even after you have fully upgraded the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md), your quest log may not update and still tell you to "Employ Delvin Mallory's services". - To fix this, visit the sanctuary and then return to Delvin, and ask to upgrade the Sanctuary more, at which point he will tell you there is nothing else he can do and you will complete the quest.
