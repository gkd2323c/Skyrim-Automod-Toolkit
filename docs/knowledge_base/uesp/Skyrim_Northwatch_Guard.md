[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-npc-Northwatch_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Northwatch_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Northwatch_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Northwatch_Guard.jpg) Northwatch Guard The **Northwatch Guard** is encountered at [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md), he will warn you to keep your distance and not interfere with [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) business. He is equipped with a standard [Thalmor Soldier](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md)'s equipment.

## Related Quests
- **[Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md)**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md) from the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).

## Dialogue
If you do get too close then he will call for you:

*"Halt!"* It will be up to you to initiate dialogue, to which his standard response is:

*"You have no business here, so keep walking."* **What is this place?** *"That's privileged information. Now move along."*
## Quest-Related Events
If you have progressed the quest [Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md) far enough:

**I'm here for Thorald Gray-Mane."** *"Are you joking? Even if we had a prisoner by that name, the only way he'd be released is by order of the Imperial Legion. Now I suggest you turn around and go back the way you came."*
