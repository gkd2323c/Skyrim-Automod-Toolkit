## Quick Walkthrough
**Note:** This quest does not appear in your journal.

1. Follow [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md) to his destination at 1am.
2. Ask Falion what he is doing at the summoning circle.
3. Report Falion to the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Guardian
Falion is a master in [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md). While he enjoys the town of Morthal enough to set up a home and business, he seems ever wary of a lurking danger. His academic interests include vampirism and the undead; perhaps he is aware of more than he is letting on.

### The Suspicion
If you inquire around [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) about [the wizard in town](Skyrim_Falion.md), you are told that he seems to be up to something; specifically, that he goes out into the marsh in the middle of the night. A conversation with [Agni](Skyrim_Agni.md), his ward and apprentice, reveals that she had a "bad dream" in which Falion went away and made "monsters" while she slept. Falion dismisses this as only a dream, and insists his talents are needed to "keep the town safe".

### Late Night Wanderings
If you wait near Falion's house until 1am, you can follow him as he heads out of town, past the mill, and northwest into the marshes. He journeys to what appears to be a summoning circle. This is the same summoning circle where you must meet him to cure your vampirism in [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md). Upon confronting him, he will demand that you leave him to his "work" and never speak to anyone about what you observed that night, although he won't reveal precisely what his work is. You can agree to keep quiet, or blackmail him for 200 gold. Regardless of your choice, you may then report him to the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) if you so desire:

### Reporting to the Jarl
After reporting Falion to the jarl, she will immediately find and confront him, threatening dire consequences for Falion if he continues his actions. (See [Bugs](#Bugs).) The scene differs depending on the current jarl - [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) or [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md).

## Notes
- Despite the warning, Falion will continue to visit the summoning circle each night as usual, and the jarl will remain oblivious to his continued behavior.
