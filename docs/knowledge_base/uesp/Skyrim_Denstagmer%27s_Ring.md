It can be found while [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md).

The ring is a rare quality junk item, which has a 3% chance (8% chance with the [Dwarven Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Dwarven_Fishing_Rod.md)) of being caught when no fish is caught.

## Notes
- The ring previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Denstagmer%27s_Ring) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
