# Suthay

*When Jode is New and Jone is New
Then shall be born the Suthay
Their time is yet to come
Though they find purchase where need is most
Before the end and clarion call
It is the Suthay who will have prepared the way.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/d/df/Mnew_Snew.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Snew.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Snew.jpg) Suthay are born when Masser and Secunda are new<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMCATD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOPF-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IWTB-3)</sup> The Suthay are a [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). The Suthay are similar to the [Suthay-raht](Lore_Suthay-raht.md), save that they are of lesser stature.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Survival G-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IWTB-3)</sup> Like Suthay-raht, they are evidently bipedal and have digitigrade legs.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSuthay-5)</sup> In early literature, the Suthay's role in Khajiit society was believed to involve being prepared for the end of time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOPF-2)</sup>
