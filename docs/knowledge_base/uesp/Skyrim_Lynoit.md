[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Lynoit.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lynoit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lynoit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lynoit.jpg) Lynoit **Lynoit** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) Dawnguard member whose body can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Vigilant_Remains) after completing either [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) for the [vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) or [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) for the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). He carries a [key](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Cache_Key.md) to Dawnguard caches and his [orders](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Orders_-_Lynoit.md).

He is found with the remains of three [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) around him. He wears [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md) armor, boots and gauntlets, with a 75% chance of wearing a helmet, any of which can be the light or heavy versions. He wields either a Dawnguard [war axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md) or [warhammer](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Warhammer.md). Lynoit can commonly be found due West of Gloomreach, and east of a small unmarked forsworn camp, and unmarked dwemer ruins.

## Notes
- There are three caches spread around [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md) and [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md):

- - On the small island south of [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md) inside a hollowed out tree stump.
- Due east of the entrance to the ruin [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md), past stone dome structure with skeleton inside, near large fallen tree at limb.
- On the east side of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) Bridge, near city direction sign, behind tall tree.

- Lynoit does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md), and many other generic NPCs.
