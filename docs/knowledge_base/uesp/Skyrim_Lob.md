[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-npc-Lob.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lob.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lob.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lob.jpg) Lob **Lob** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) living in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md), the [Orc Stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). He is usually found tending to the livestock or other maintenance during the day, and at night in the [longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md).

Should the [curse of Malacath](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md) be lifted from Largashbur, he can be asked to become a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).

Lob wears a set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) and a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He wields a [steel war axe](Skyrim_Steel_War_Axe.md) in combat.
