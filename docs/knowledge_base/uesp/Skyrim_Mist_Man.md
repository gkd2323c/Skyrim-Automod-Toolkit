They are armed with a leveled weapon commonly used by [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). Unlike the other undead creatures of Soul Cairn, mist men will taunt you with a ghostly voice, and drop [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md) upon death.
