## Hod and Gerdur's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House_02.jpg) Cooking and dining area Hod and Gerdur's house is laid out in an L-shape, with the front door entering to an open cooking and dining area along one side of the "L", and a bar and the bedroom areas situated on the other. None of the areas are separated by walls or doors. To the left of the door as you come in is a dining table set with two loaves and one piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). North of the table, a respawning food barrel sits in the corner. A [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) sits atop another respawning food barrel to the left of the fireplace, which is directly across from the front door. The fireplace mantel holds three gold coins, while a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) sits at its base. A kettle containing four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) rests on the floor to the right, next to a large table holding two more potatoes, a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, three servings of [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), three loaves of bread, three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), and a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). Above the table are some hanging ingredients, including dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two garlic braids, and two portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). Next to these hanging ingredients are two hanging racks, one of which holds two freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) while the other holds two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Two bottles of alto wine and three bottles of wine can be found on the bottom of a set of shelves to the right. A non-respawning cupboard across from the food table holds various kitchenware on its shelves, along with a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) and one sample of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) at the top.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.jpg) Bar and bedroom area The first bedroom area of the home starts with a single bed in the southwest corner, resting between a non-respawning dresser on one side and a non-respawning end table on the other. A respawning chest sits atop the dresser, along with a copy of *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)*, and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) rests on the end table. The next area of the home includes the bar, atop which sit one bottle each of wine and alto wine, four bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two [wedges of goat cheese](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), and some sliced goat cheese. Behind the bar is a respawning food barrel, as well as an empty [petty soul gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem) on the bottom shelf. Across from the bar is a small round table with two bottles of Nord mead. The final bedroom area can be found past the bar, in the northwest part of the home. A double bed sits at the middle of the wall, flanked by a non-respawning end table on either side and with a non-respawning chest at the foot of the bed. The left end table holds a farm [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000330b3), a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling), and a copy of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir)*, while the right one holds a basket and four gold coins. A pair of farm [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b) sits on the floor in the left corner, in front of a medium-sized [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). An [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md) is stashed beneath the bed on the left side. Along the left wall is a non-respawning wardrobe, atop which lie a [sabre cat pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt) and a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), and along the right wall is a single respawning food barrel.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice-level lock, the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Gerdur%27s_House.md) to which is carried by Frodnar, Gerdur, and Hod. You will obtain a copy of the key if you followed [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) during [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md).
