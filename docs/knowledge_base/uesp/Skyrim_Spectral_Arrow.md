- *Fires a Spectral Arrow at the target doing 30 points of damage and staggering the target.*

**Spectral Arrow** is a [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that deals 30 damage to Health and causes targets to stagger when hit. It is especially effective against a single target, as you can hit enemies repeatedly before they have a chance to recover and fight back. The damage is non-elemental, it is only affected by magic resistance.

This spell is [cut content](Skyrim_Unused_Content.md) and cannot be legitimately obtained in-game.

## Effects
- Spectral Arrow, 30 pts
- [Stagger Push](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 25 pts

## Notes
- This is the only direct-damage spell outside of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) school (with the exception of a few [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md), which only work on the undead, and [Poison Rune](Skyrim_Poison_Rune.md) which was added by [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)).
- This spell is cast silently, even without the [Silent Casting](Skyrim_Silent_Casting.md) perk, making it well suited for stealth.
- The projectile is also used for another unused item, the Ancient Nord Magic Arrow, which was apparently intended to be used by [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md).
