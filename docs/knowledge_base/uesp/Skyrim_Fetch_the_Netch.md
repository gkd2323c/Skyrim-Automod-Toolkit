## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md).
2. Obtain five samples of [netch jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly).
3. Return to Milore Ienth for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Hunt for Jelly
The residing apothecary and [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) trainer in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md), sells a wide variety of exotic ingredients, all of which she grows and harvests herself. However, due to the harsh terrain and the dangers outside the [Bulwark](Skyrim_The_Bulwark.md), she has problems gathering [netch jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly). Tell her you are willing to help and she will immediately promise you a reward if you bring back five samples.

The netch jelly samples are dropped by the peaceful, but potentially dangerous, [netch](Skyrim_Netch.md) that can be found all over [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). If you have started (or completed) the quest [The Final Descent](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md) you can gather the only two harvested samples of jelly in Solstheim, specifically deep within [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md), on an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). However, you will either need to visit an apothecary or go netch-hunting for the rest of the samples. The table below shows all fixed locations of the netch, which often reside in familial groups:

Once you have gathered five samples of netch jelly, return to Milore and hand them over. She will be impressed and say, *"Goodness, it must have been quite difficult tracking those creatures down out there. Here, take these. They should help you during your journeys."* She will then hand you two [Potions of Ultimate Well-Being](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Well-being), ending the quest.

## Notes
- If you already have five samples of netch jelly in your inventory when starting the quest, Milore will say, *"My goodness, you're quite resourceful aren't you? This is all I can offer in return for this. I hope it's enough."* This ends the quest right away.
