# Eye of Argonia

The Eye of Argonia is a treasured artifact described as the "priceless king's jewel of ancient [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Little is known of the eye, but it has captured the imagination of treasure hunter's throughout all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) as to what it is capable of.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:21</sup> Some theories included this invaluable gem being a key the "lost city of Black Marsh",<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Origin-3)</sup> or that it could serve as a gateway to undiscovered lands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:21</sup>

At some point during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) Lark of [Rosgard](Lore_Rosgard.md), a mysterious masked thief sought to obtain the Eye.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGazetteer-2)</sup><sup>:21</sup> Years later [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) and the Cyro-Nordic captain [Tobias](Lore_Tobias.md) both wished to attain the prized artifact as well, although it's unknown if either of them ever succeeded in doing so.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Origin-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> An archeologist and author by the name of Rolard Nordssen aspired to claim the fabled eye, a potential accomplishment which he considered even greater than his prior feats.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ROKZ-5)</sup> Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) mentioned the legendary jewel as a means to cover for his true intentions to infiltrate the chambers of the Common Tongue press printers.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TRTrels-6)</sup> There exists dubious tales about the jewel's discovery.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Tiber Meeting-8)</sup>

## Notes
- ***[The Elder Scrolls Adventures 2: Eye of Argonia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Eye_of_Argonia)*** was a planned sequel to [*Redguard*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard). Like its predecessor, it was an action-adventure game with few of the roleplaying elements of other [Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls) titles. The project was ultimately canceled.
