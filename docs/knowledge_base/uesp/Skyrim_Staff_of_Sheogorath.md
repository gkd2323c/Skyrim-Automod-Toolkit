[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-menu-Staff_of_Sheogorath.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Staff_of_Sheogorath.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Staff_of_Sheogorath.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Staff_of_Sheogorath.jpg) Staff of Sheogorath **Staff of Sheogorath** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds the eponymous [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath_(item).md), as well as the [Fork of Horripilation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fork_of_Horripilation) weapon. It was released on October 4, 2017 for 250 Creation Credits.

## Official Summary
*Symbol of office for the Madgod Sheogorath, this grotesque staff has the power to stop opponents in their tracks. Also included is a bonus artifact, the Fork of Horripilation. (Quest "Put a Fork in it" starts by reading a "Mysterious Note" at The Retching Netch in Raven Rock.)*
## Overview
To start the quest **[Put A Fork In It](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Put_A_Fork_In_It)**, travel to [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), and read a [mysterious note](Skyrim_Mysterious_Note_(Staff_of_Sheogorath).md) from [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), which can be found there along with the [Fork of Horripilation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fork_of_Horripilation) weapon. The note is pinned to a barrel on the lefthand side of the upstairs area, just as you enter the building. You will need to track down two [bull netches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bull_Netch) on Solstheim and kill them with the fork in order to retrieve the necessary components to craft the [Staff of Sheogorath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath_(item).md), a powerful [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) staff.

Note that crafting the staff requires access to a [Staff Enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md), meaning the [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md) quest is a prerequisite if you don't have access to one through another Creation.
