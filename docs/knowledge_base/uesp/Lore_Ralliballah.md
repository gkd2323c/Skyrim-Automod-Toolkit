# Ralliballah

| Ralliballah | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Unknown |

*Recommended examples of the conjurational charter may be found in Therion's Book of Most Arcane Covenants or Ralliballah's Eleven Ritual Forms.*
— *[Liminal Bridges](Lore_Liminal_Bridges.md)* by [Camilonwe of Alinor](Lore_Camilonwe_of_Alinor.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LB-1)</sup> Ralliballah was a [mage](Lore_Magic.md) and the author of *Eleven Ritual Forms*, regarded as an authority in the field of [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) magic,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LB-1)</sup> whose works were cited and studied by prominent scholars across the eras.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ADDOE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/ON-npc-Morian_Zenas.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Morian_Zenas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Morian_Zenas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Morian_Zenas.jpg) Morian Zenas, one of the students of Ralliballah's works [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4b/ON-npc-Gabrielle_Benele_05.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Gabrielle_Benele_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Gabrielle_Benele_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Gabrielle_Benele_05.jpg) Gabrielle Benele, one of the students of Ralliballah's works Little is known of Ralliballah's life, though it is likely that they lived in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) or earlier, as their name and *Eleven Ritual Forms* are mentioned in texts describing events that took place during the First Era.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PH-4)</sup>

Their book was studied by several prominent scholars including [Gabrielle Benele](Lore_Gabrielle_Benele.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ADDOE-2)</sup> the Acting Guildmaster of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MTCW-5)</sup> and the possibly semi-legendary Hafzifeh al-Yath, Petty Vizier to Prince [Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md) of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PH-4)</sup> The former member of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) and one of the most powerful wizards of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MTCDF-6)</sup> and [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md), Professor of Transliminal Studies at the [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CMA-7)</sup> were also known to discuss Ralliballah's works, comparing the *Eleven Ritual Forms* to the *Book of Most Arcane Covenants*.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM-3)</sup> [Camilonwe of Alinor](Lore_Camilonwe_of_Alinor.md), [Sapiarch](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) of Transliminal Arts at the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TGS-8)</sup> suggested that both Therion's *Book of Most Arcane Covenants* and Ralliballah's *Eleven Ritual Forms* contained recommended examples of conjurational charters for summoning [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LB-1)</sup> Doctor [Alfidia Lupus](Lore_Alfidia_Lupus.md), [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Ethnographer for [Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md), was also aware of Ralliballah's works.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM-3)</sup>
