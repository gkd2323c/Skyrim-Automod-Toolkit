[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/SR-npc-Garuk_Windrime.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Garuk_Windrime.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garuk_Windrime.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garuk_Windrime.jpg) Garuk Windrime **Garuk Windrime**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [archer](Skyrim_Bandit_Archer.md), was the ship's quartermaster serving as part of the crew under the legendary pirate king [Haknir Death-Brand](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md). Upon entering the final chamber of [Gyldenhul Barrow](Skyrim_Gyldenhul_Barrow.md) during the [related quest](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md), his spirit appears to fight you.

According to the book *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*, Haknir put a curse on his [armor](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Armor.md) and gave Garuk a [map](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Treasure_Map.md) marked with four locations where each piece was to be buried before he was sealed away in his tomb. Garuk went with three [pirates](Skyrim_Pirate.md) from the crew to what later came to be known as [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md) to follow Haknir's orders, but succumbed to greed and temptation. He opened the chest containing Haknir's [helm](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Helm.md) and put it on his head, hoping to become the next pirate king of legend. When you arrive at Gyldenhul Barrow, it becomes clear that Garuk was affected by Haknir's curse, as his spirit attacks you along with Haknir and his other [crewmembers](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Crew.md).
