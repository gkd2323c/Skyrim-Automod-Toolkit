Mendel Eves was a [Buoyant Armiger](Lore_Buoyant_Armiger.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) operating in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Despite being a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) in an overwhelmingly [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) order,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWMendel-1)</sup> Mendel Eves was a cherished member of the Armigers, seen as brave and honorable.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-2)</sup> Mendel wielded the [Cleaver of St. Felms](Lore_Cleaver_of_St._Felms.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-2)</sup> an ironic weapon for the Armiger to carry given the [eponymous saint](Lore_Felms.md) the axe was named for was famously a scourge on the Nordic people.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOTS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWdialogue-4)</sup> Mendel died fighting against the dreaded [Sixth House](Lore_Sixth_House.md) in Tureynulal, the former [Dwemer tower](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) turned House Dagoth citadel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWUvoo-2)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) plundered Tureynulal and retrieved the Cleaver of St. Felms from Mendel's corpse. The Hero returned the holy relic to the Temple, before later being personally entrusted with the artifact.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Cleaver Q-5)</sup>
