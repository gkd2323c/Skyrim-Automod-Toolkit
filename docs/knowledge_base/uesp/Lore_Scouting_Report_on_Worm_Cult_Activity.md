Scouting Report on Worm Cult Activity **by [Scout Zirpar Faram](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Scout_Zirpar_Faram)** *Report on Worm Cult forces in Eastern Solstice* *
---
Report to Seline Jerick of the Stirk Fellowship, from Scout Zirpar Faram

As Stirk Fellowship reinforcements start to move through the main pass and into eastern [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) forces appear to be in disarray. Some units are still positioned in key locations and continue patrols, while others have been scattered to the four winds. Major forces have pulled back to the major encampment at Mor Naril, while others have created fortified encampments elsewhere in the wilderness. Such units include and *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* and are often lead by [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). Captured cultists remain defiant and adamant in their belief that [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) will save them and lead them to victory.

Some activity of concern has been observed involving setting up new devices throughout the eastern half of the island. These resemble the soul reapers deployed by the Worm Cult earlier, but are larger and seem more menacing somehow. They are being assembled in parts and built in abandoned Stone-Nest villages, presumably for some form of testing or establishing a defensive line. I will provide more information when I learn more.
