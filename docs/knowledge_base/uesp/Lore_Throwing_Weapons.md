# Throwing Weapons

Throwing Weapons such as **throwing stars**, **throwing knives**, **darts** and others are frequently used across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and beyond.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup>

They are recognized as weapons for marksmen, renowned for their swift and rapid attacks.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Marksman-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Marksman_Weapons-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup>

Assassins, such as the members of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup> and [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TBG-6)</sup> often utilize thrown weapons such as throwing stars and throwing knives for their stealthy and surprising attacks.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWGDM-7)</sup> [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) value agility, preferring swift combat in light armor engaging from a distance with ranged weapons when confrontation was inevitable.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GF-8)</sup> Apart from them, marksmanship abilities are valued among [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) tribes, the [vampiric](Lore_Vampire.md) [Berne Clan](Lore_Berne_Clan.md), [the Blades](Lore_Blades.md), [Census and Excise](Lore_Census_and_Excise.md), [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md), and the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Mark Class-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-HAT-10)</sup>

## Varieties of thrown arms
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/ON-skill-Caltrops.jpg/200px-ON-skill-Caltrops.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Caltrops.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Caltrops.jpg) Caltrops
### Bolas
A bola functions as a throwing weapon. [Rieklings](Lore_Riekling.md) of the [Coldsnap Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Coldsnap_Tribe) from [Vault of Mhuvnak](Lore_Vault_of_Mhuvnak.md) are known to use them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-WT-11)</sup>

### Caltrops
Caltrops are projectiles known for their anti-cavalry properties. They were widely used during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Caltrops-12)</sup>

### Chakrams
Chakrams are thrown weapons. Certain [Arcanist](Lore_Arcanists.md) spells were named after chakrams, including the Chakram of Destiny, Tidal Chakram, and Chakram Shields. These spells were linked to apocryphal beings like the [Blind Man](Lore_Blind_Man.md), [Fate Crone](Lore_Fate_Crone.md), and the [Baron of Breakers](Lore_Baron_of_Breakers.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Chakram_Shields-13)</sup> Additionally, some [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) were known to wield chakrams.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Concept-14)</sup>

### Darts
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/MW-item-Darts.jpg/150px-MW-item-Darts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Darts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Darts.jpg) Darts Darts are crafted from various materials, including [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), [silver](Lore_Silver.md), and [steel](Lore_Steel.md). [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> [Dwarven](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md) and spring darts were also in use.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Tribunal-15)</sup> The Carved Ebony Dart is a particularly valuable type favored by Dark Brotherhood assassins.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-DBA-16)</sup>

Among the most lethal and valuable are the Darts of Judgment, exceptionally high-quality [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) Daedric darts crafted in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DOJ-17)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-TDOJ-18)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ATJ-19)</sup>

Although darts are frequently launched without supporting tools, they can be assisted by specialized projectile weapons.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> An [atlatl](Lore_Atlatl.md) is a weapon specifically designed for hurling darts and similar projectiles.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TC-20)</sup>

For the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) following the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md), [the Lady](Lore_The_Lady.md) [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) was associated with marksmanship, covering both bows and darts.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-CM-21)</sup>

[Black Dart Gang](Lore_Factions_B.md#Black_Dart_Gang) is named for their adept use of very powerful throwing darts.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-23)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

### Stones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/ON-creature-Stone_Atronach_05.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Stone_Atronach_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Stone_Atronach_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Stone_Atronach_05.jpg) Atronach using Stone Gaze Stones are occasionally used as simple projectiles.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-VAB-25)</sup><sup>[[25]](#cite_note-TRT-26)</sup> A [sling](Lore_Sling.md) is a weapon specifically designed for hurling stones and similar projectiles.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-CA-27)</sup><sup>[[27]](#cite_note-TSOC-28)</sup> [Stone Atronachs](Lore_Stone_Atronach.md) use a "Stone Gaze" ability to fire rocks at their targets.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-Stone Atronach-29)</sup> Xit-Xaht Stoneshapers use a form of magic to fire stones at targets.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-XXS-30)</sup>

### Throwing Axes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-quest-Victory_Through_Strife_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Victory_Through_Strife_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Victory_Through_Strife_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Victory_Through_Strife_02.jpg) A thrown axe in midair Axes are utilized as thrown weapons in Tamriel,<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-AC-31)</sup><sup>[[31]](#cite_note-KB-32)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Lorana-33)</sup> particularly among the Bosmer and [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md).<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-AC-31)</sup><sup>[[31]](#cite_note-KB-32)</sup>

### Throwing Knives
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/MW-item-Throwing_Knives.jpg/150px-MW-item-Throwing_Knives.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Throwing_Knives.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Throwing_Knives.jpg) Throwing Knives Small thrown weapons, especially throwing knives or daggers are highly esteemed among the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup><sup>[[32]](#cite_note-Baan-34)</sup> known for their preference for agility over heavy weaponry and armor. This choice reflects their reliance on stealth and maneuverability in personal and clan combat situations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup> Throwing knives were also part of the arsenal used by the [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md).<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-TTB-35)</sup> Materials employed in their crafting include [malachite](Lore_Malachite.md), [iron](Lore_Iron.md), steel,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> silver, and ebony. [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) throwing knives also existed.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-36)</sup> They are occasionally enchanted, with known enchantments including those that enhance the marksmanship abilities of the owner and those that inflict poison on the target.<sup>[[35]](#cite_note-GTW-37)</sup> Additionally, there are throwing knives associated with the obscure art of the [shadow magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md) known as penumbric and shadow throwing knives.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-36)</sup> Some penumbric knives are renowned for their exceptional balance and craftsmanship.<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-Shadow Gate-38)</sup>

Additionally, throwing knives are favored within certain criminal circles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup>

### Throwing Stars
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/MW-item-Throwing_Stars.jpg/150px-MW-item-Throwing_Stars.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Throwing_Stars.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Throwing_Stars.jpg) Throwing Stars The throwing star is deemed an exotic weapon in Tamriel, and originated from the martial arts techniques of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-G-2)</sup> They were also relatively popular in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Crafted from materials such as chitin, ebony, malachite, silver, and steel,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> these are were frequently [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) with diverse effects, including poison and elemental qualities like ice, fire and lightning.<sup>[[35]](#cite_note-GTW-37)</sup>

[Azura's Star](Lore_Azura%27s_Star.md), while resembling a throwing star in appearance, is not intended for throwing.<sup>[[37]](#cite_note-AS-39)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>
