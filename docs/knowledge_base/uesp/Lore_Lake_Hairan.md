[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/MW-place-Lake_Hairan.jpg/200px-MW-place-Lake_Hairan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Hairan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Hairan.jpg) Lake Hairan, from the west Lake Hairan was once a small lake just north of the city of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), between that city and [Pelagiad](Lore_Pelagiad.md), in the heart of the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md). It was surrounded by farms and rich plantations under [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) control. At its northeastern end, the lake narrowed into a river channel that connected it to [Lake Masobi](Lore_Lake_Masobi.md). To the south, the lake merged with the vast lagoon encompassing Vivec.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> It's unclear whether any semblance of the lake survived the cataclysms of the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRY-3)</sup>
