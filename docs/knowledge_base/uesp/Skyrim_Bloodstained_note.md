Bloodstained note **by [Sudi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi)** *A note detailing Sudi's last moments before killing herself while being held captive by Falmer*
---
They took [Father](Skyrim_Habd.md).
There had been some others they brought down but they led them off and all we heard were the screams and now Father is gone too. Oh gods why didn't I listen to [Mani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani)? Father's fever was getting worse and kept mumbling about [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). I don't know where he was hiding it, but I think I know why he left me this [dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
