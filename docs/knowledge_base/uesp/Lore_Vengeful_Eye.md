# Vengeful Eye

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-minor_adornment-Pendant.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-minor_adornment-Pendant.png) The Vengeful Eye The Vengeful Eye is an amulet associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md); it is a relic sacred to both the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) and [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) of the [Winterborn](Lore_Winterborn.md) clan. It is a plain amulet carved with the symbol of an eye. Dried blood appears to be caked inside the etching.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOORS-1)</sup> Those who have it in their possession are said to carry Malacath's favor. It was first given by Malacath to his mortal followers, and this artifact would be passed down by combat, the one who slew the bearer claiming the artifact. It is capable of influencing those who have it in their possession. Two Orcish brothers once attempted to destroy the relic, only for it to whisper to one of them, and influence one brother to betray the other. Soon after, the relic fell into the possession of [Mercedene](Lore_Mercedene.md), one of the Winterborn and Beast of the Reach.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dagarha-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Kyrtos-3)</sup>

It would eventually be buried within the tomb of [Honor's Rest](Lore_Honor%27s_Rest.md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Winterborn attempted to retrieve the relic from the tomb, so they could march upon Orsinium and the Orcish Strongholds, in what was referred to as the Vengeful March.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Kyrtos-3)</sup> Due to the intervention of one of the Winterborn, [Kyrtos](Lore_Kyrtos.md) and his ally, [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) it would be secured before that could happen. Its unclear if the relic was given to the Priests of Malacath in Orsinium, destroyed, or if the Vestige took it to a shrine of Malacath and received Malacath's favor and blessing to keep the amulet.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTF-4)</sup>
