Sinderion was a famous [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) master alchemist known for his research into the [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nirnroot) plant.

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/OBR-npc-Sinderion_02.jpg/200px-OBR-npc-Sinderion_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Sinderion_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Sinderion_02.jpg) Sinderion (Oblivion Remastered) For most of his life, he lived in the basement of the West Weald Inn in the [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) city of [Skingrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skingrad). He was an honorary member of the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md). He collaborated with [Arch-Mage Taris Rendil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Taris_Rendil) to develop the Frostcrag Apparatus Table, an alchemy lab in [Frostcrag Spire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frostcrag_Spire).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FSM-1)</sup> Avrusa Sarethi, an alchemist from [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), came to Skingrad to train under Sinderion. He was also a connoisseur of wines, and was known to provide alchemy training in return for the local vintage wine from [3E 399](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_399). Sinderion's interest in the Nirnroot began when he purchased a dusty alchemical tome from an adventurer. Most of the recipes within were worthless, but one detailed a potion called the "Elixir of Exploration". This potion brought out the huge magical potential of the rare Nirnroot. He [presented](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Nirnroot_Missive) his findings at the Alchemical Symposium in the hopes of funding,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-E-2)</sup> but was turned down. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), he hired the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to collect samples of Nirnroot for him. The Hero provided enough samples to complete Sinderion's research and create the strongest version of the elixir.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oblivion-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Oblivion2-4)</sup> Sinderion again presented his speech to the Alchemical Symposium in Rain's Hand of [4E 2](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_2).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TNM-5)</sup>

Over fifty years later, an adventurer named Obeth Arnesian brought a sample of [Crimson Nirnroot](Lore_Crimson_Nirnroot.md) to Sinderion. This variant of the common Nirnroot grows only in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), a gigantic cavern beneath the surface of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). A year later he sold his basement workshop in Skingrad and traveled to Skyrim, eventually coming to the Sarethi Farm outside [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). Avrusa Sarethi had moved here after [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) was ruined during the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SFJ-6)</sup> He passed on all his knowledge of the Nirnroot to Avrusa, who learned how to cultivate them. After a few months of preparation, Sinderion entered Blackreach using the runed lexicon provided by Obeth and set up a field laboratory in one of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) houses found within the cavern. He began researching the Crimson Nirnroot, but was killed circa [4E 58](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_58) in his laboratory by the denizens of Blackreach. In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) came upon Sinderion's remains and completed his research by gathering samples of Crimson Nirnroot and bringing them to Avrusa.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skyrim-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Sinderion possessed a magical [staff](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff) named Emberroot, which was said to blaze with an untamed fire reflective of Sinderion's fervor for his pursuits.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> He also owned a set of robes named Nimblegarb, which harnessed Nirnroot's elusive nature to grant the wearer lightning-fast speed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
- There is a discrepancy in the lore around Sinderion: in his journal, he states he found out about Avrusa Sarethi through his membership with the College of Winterhold, and bargained his months long stay with her for Alchemy knowledge. However, according to Avrusa herself, she'd known him from her days as his alchemy apprentice in Skingrad, and he "just showed up" one day at her doorstep and stayed with her and her sister for decades and became "an uncle of sorts" for her sister Aduri before he headed to Blackreach.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SFJ-6)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Avrusa_Sarethi-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Aduri_Sarethi-11)</sup>
