[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-creature-Cursed_Spectre.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Cursed_Spectre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Cursed_Spectre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Cursed_Spectre.jpg) Cursed Spectre **Cursed Spectres** are undead beings summoned by an Expert level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell, [Conjure Cursed Spectre](Skyrim_Conjure_Cursed_Spectre.md) added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

It has access to a spell that allows it absorb health and magicka, and a poison cloak that will injure enemies that enter combat with it.
