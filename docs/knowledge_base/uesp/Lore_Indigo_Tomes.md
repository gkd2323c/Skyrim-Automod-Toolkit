# Indigo Tomes

The Indigo Tomes (or **Indigo Scrolls**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GEN-1)</sup> are a mysterious collection of tomes found somewhere in the vaults of [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md). They are very difficult to access, though according to rumor, Princess [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) managed to read them by sneaking into the vaults disguised as a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup> The existence of the Indigo Tomes was a closely held secret for centuries by the [Keepers of the Dead](Lore_Keepers_of_the_Dead.md), however [Headmaster Tanion](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Headmaster_Tanion)'s offhand mention of the tomes in his book *[Ayrenn: The Unforeseen Queen](Lore_Ayrenn__The_Unforeseen_Queen.md)* caused the Keepers a small amount of grief, according to [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LA-3)</sup>
