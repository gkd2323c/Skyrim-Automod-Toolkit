[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Lucky_Lorenz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lucky_Lorenz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucky_Lorenz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucky_Lorenz.jpg) Lucky Lorenz's corpse **Lucky Lorenz** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who can be found inside his destroyed house, on the bank of the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) directly across from the [Abandoned Prison](Skyrim_Abandoned_Prison.md). Contrary to his name, Lorenz was crushed by an ill-timed tree falling on him while he was sitting at the dinner table. His corpse contains a variety of loot, including [Treasure Map IX](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_IX.md), while a copy of *[The Lusty Argonian Maid, v1](Skyrim_The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v1.md)* can be found near his body.

There is a [shrine of Dibella](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Dibella.md) in the western corner of his shack, while the riverbank outside is strewn with various food items and ingredients fallen from a half-buried shelf. There is also a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Rislav The Righteous](Skyrim_Rislav_The_Righteous.md)* to be found lying inside the shelf, and two wooden barrels containing more ingredients nearby.
