# Festival of Blades

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/ON-place-Alik%27r_Desert.jpg/200px-ON-place-Alik%27r_Desert.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alik%27r_Desert.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alik%27r_Desert.jpg) The Alik'r Desert [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/DF-map-Alik%27r_Desert.png/200px-DF-map-Alik%27r_Desert.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Alik%27r_Desert.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Alik%27r_Desert.png) The Alik'r Desert ca. 3E 405 The Festival of Blades is a holiday held on the 26th of [First Seed](Lore_First_Seed.md). It is created in honor of the first [Redguard's](Lore_Redguard.md) victory over [Malooc](Lore_Malooc.md)'s Horde, an army of giant [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) that lived in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFHoliday-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-T26OFSIU-2)</sup> Although modern scholars believe that the story is a myth, the holiday is still popular across the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) of the southern [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFHoliday-1)</sup>

When [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) and the original [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) first arrived in Hammerfell after their departure around [1E 780](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_780),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFarvad-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RTHATH-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DTS-5)</sup> they found it occupied by a race of giant goblins.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFarvad-3)</sup> Frandar assembled the [sword-singers](Lore_Sword-singers.md) and waged war with the goblins, known collectively as Malooc's Horde.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-T26OFSIU-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFarvad-3)</sup> Frandar had great initial success, causing the God who led the Goblins, Malooc, to flee east when his army of the overtook his hordes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VOF-6)</sup> Despite this, in one of the bloodiest battles, Frandar's life was taken and his son, [Divad](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md) assumed his mantle to eventually finish the fight. With a band of sword-singers, Divad forged five magical swords and vanquished the giant goblins.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFarvad-3)</sup>

The Festival of Blades is observed in the Alik'r Desert region of Hammerfell, as well as its major population centers, such as [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md). In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the Sisters of the Sand inn prepared a [five-drake prix fixe dinner](Lore_Redguard_Cuisine.md) which encompassed Scuttle Fondue, [Horker](Lore_Horker.md) Loaf and Caramelized [Goat](Lore_Goat.md). In addition, porcelain bowls could be found that depicted a Redguard fighting against a race of giant Goblins.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Contra Bowl-7)</sup> The people hold a ceremony on the streets called the Effigy Dismemberment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-T26OFSIU-2)</sup> The Redguards of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) and [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) to the south also acknowledge it.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PHATTLR-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-9)</sup><sup>:29</sup>
