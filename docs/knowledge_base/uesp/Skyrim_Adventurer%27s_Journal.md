Adventurer's Journal *About investigating attacks on caravans by the Falmer*
---
There's something unsettling about these caravan attacks. I expected [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md), but the remains we saw were literally torn apart, rent limb from limb. Yet no mere [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) could coordinate an attack on a full-sized merchant train. And [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) don't take prisoners.

We've found a trail leading up into the hills. We'll make camp here for the night, then set out at dawn. Whoever- whatever- murdered those people, we'll make sure it never happens again.
