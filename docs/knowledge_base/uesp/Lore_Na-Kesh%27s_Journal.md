Na-Kesh's Journal **by [Tree-Minder Na-Kesh](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Tree-Minder_Na-Kesh)** *Notes on the origin and properties of Amber Plasm*
---
Sap. That is what these thin-shelled fools call it. As if such a substance could ever be compared to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) -tainted resin. No, it is much more than sap.

"[Amber Plasm](Lore_Amber_Plasm.md)." That is what one of the scholars called it before I fed him to Mighty Chudan. He said that it was like the [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) —leaking into [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) through our [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) like blood from a wound. These dry-tongues and their airs—as if anyone could know the secrets of the Hist better than I!

The plasm has accumulated at the roots of the [Tsono-Xuhil](Lore_Tsono-Xuhil.md) since before the [Saxhleel](Lore_Saxhleel.md) could lift a spade, but only I have discovered its secrets. Drinking it or bathing in it can drive a Saxhleel mad. But through alchemical study, and the Hist's guidance, I have learned to harness its power. This was no small feat. The creatia is a thing of chaos. The call to [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) is in its nature. But we must remain deaf to his commands. Those Saxhleel who call him father languish in mud huts and eat old fish from wooden bowls. The [Xit-Xaht](Lore_Xit-Xaht.md) hear the call to order, and shall dwell in a shining city forever more!

If properly manipulated, the Amber Plasm can grant a worthy Saxhleel great speed and strength. I am proof of its efficacy. Soon, my brew will be ready to distribute to all Xit-Xaht sons and daughters. Then [Mazzatun](Lore_Mazzatun.md) will be a rival even to the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)! The People of the Root will rule [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) first, then all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)!
