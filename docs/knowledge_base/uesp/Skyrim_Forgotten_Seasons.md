[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_05.jpg/200px-SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_05.jpg) Forgotten Seasons [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_11.jpg/200px-SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_11.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_11.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Forgotten_Seasons_11.jpg) The exterior of Vardnknd, and the vamidium horse **Forgotten Seasons** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns), a large [Dwemer ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Ruins) full of puzzles. It was released on December 6, 2018 for 1200 Creation Credits.

The ruins feature sections themed after the four seasons: Spring's Symphony, Winter's Chimes, Summer's Chords and Autumn's Bells. Within Vardnknd are powerful artifacts, such as a Dwarven horse (also called a [vamidium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vamidium)), an arcane crown, a Dwarven drone fly and others whose enchantments are based on the seasons.

This Creation was primarily made by [Trainwiz](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/370317), the author of many Skyrim quest mods. Other contributors include [Private Eye](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/887024), [Elianora](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3231858), [fadingsignal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2546964) and [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Overview
This Creation adds a large new dungeon named [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns), located in the [Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). Within, you will find the extensive Dwemer ruins of Vardnknd, filled with all manner of enemies. Vardnknd is a mechanics-heavy dungeon themed after the four seasons, and you will be required to travel through its entirety in order to shut down a malfunctioning Dwemer weather machine.

Forgotten Seasons is one of the largest Creations to date and contains a significant amount of new content, including new items, books, powers, and enemy types. It features a total of three quests, which will require a good deal of exploration in order to complete. The rewards for doing so include a new mount, a robotic Dwemer horse as previously seen in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).

## Notes
- Despite the other three seasons receiving one, there is no Dwarven Summer Visage.
- The four Karstangz items listed above are [unobtainable](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md).
- Multiples of each of the six wheat variants within the Autumn's Bells zone can be collected and kept.
- Sometimes the Dwarven Crown will not spawn on Elberon's body. The only known fix is to spawn it in with console commands
