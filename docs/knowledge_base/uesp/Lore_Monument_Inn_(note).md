Monument Inn *A plaque describing Monument Inn's relationship with the All Flags Navy* *
---
During the long build up and construction phase of the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md), this inn housed captains and other officers, including the famed Baron-Admiral [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md). Afterward, it was dedicated as part of the memorial on [Monument Islet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:All_Flags_Islet).
