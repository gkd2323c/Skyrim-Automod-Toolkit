# Hunding (region)

| Hunding | |

The region of Hunding encompassed the far desert marches of [High Desert](Lore_High_Desert.md) province on [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTHATH-1)</sup> before most of the continent sank in [1E 792](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_792).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-2)</sup> The famous [Frandar do Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) was born in the marches in [1E 720](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_720), and his last name was taken from his region of birth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTHATH-1)</sup>
