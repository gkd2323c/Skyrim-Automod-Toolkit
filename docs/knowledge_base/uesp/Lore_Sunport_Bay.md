[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/ON-place-Western_Solstice_09.jpg/200px-ON-place-Western_Solstice_09.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice_09.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice_09.jpg) Sunport Bay Sunport Bay is a body of saltwater found in northwestern [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), with the mouth of the bay leading out to the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md). The forest to the west of the bay was named after it, being known as the Sunport Bay Forest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LV-1)</sup> The origin of the bay's name comes from the city of [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md), which was established by [Clan Corelanya](Lore_Clan_Corelanya.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) on the eastern shore of the bay.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HCC-2)</sup>

There is a land bridge at the southern end of Sunport Bay which separates it from the Western Bay to the south.
