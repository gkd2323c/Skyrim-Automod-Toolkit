[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of the Azurian Sea The Azurian Sea is the sea north of the [Yokudan archipelago](Lore_Yokuda.md), far west of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It is part of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) and meets the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md) in the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> On some maps, an unnamed landmass appears to be depicted far north of Yokuda within the Azurian Sea.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-All Flags Map-2)</sup>

## History
[Hazadiyya Sea-Queen](Lore_Hazadiyya_Sea-Queen.md) once set out from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) to explore the Azurian Sea after growing restless on [Kanesh](Lore_Kanesh.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLIo OY-3)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) sea captain Angarthal was considered the cruelest slaver in the Azurian.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAlbert-4)</sup> He later moved on to the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) before he was killed on the outskirts of [Koeglin Village](Lore_Koeglin_Village.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONCaptive-5)</sup>

## Fauna
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Azurian_Moth.jpg) An Azurian Moth The waters of the Azurian Sea are home to migratory water- [moths](Lore_Moth.md). Known as the [Azurian Moth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azurian_Moth), these translucent insects appear to be made entirely of [water](Lore_Water.md). They sometimes land on passing ships, and Tamrielic sailors have been known to keep them as pets.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The [Azurian Sturgeon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azurian_Sturgeon) is a prized fish named after the sea. Their caviar was a personal favorite of King [Kurog gro-](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md) [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONContraband-7)</sup>
