**Dragon Cultists** are the restless spirits of members of the ancient [Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Dragon_Cult.md) who chose to take their own lives rather than fall to King Harald's army. They only appear to attack invaders in [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md).

Dragon Cultists make use of [ancient Nord](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord.md) weapons and armor, and may also carry a [dragon priest dagger](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Dagger.md). Some Cultists are capable of using [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic.

## Related Quests
- **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult.md)**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) and defeat [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md) to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](Skyrim_Captain_Valmir.md).

## Notes
- Dragon Cultists make the sounds of [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), even though normally ghosts don't show that behavior.
