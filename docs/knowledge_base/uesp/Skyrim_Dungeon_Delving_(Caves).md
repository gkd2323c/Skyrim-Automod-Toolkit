## Quick Walkthrough
1. Fight or sneak your way through a dungeon.
2. Retrieve an item.
3. Return the item.

## Notes
- Once you've turned in these quests, the items you were meant to retrieve can then be either pickpocketed from the quest-giver, looted off their corpse, or purchased from them if they are a merchant. The most valuable item to take is the Ring of Pure Mixtures, which is one of only two guaranteed [Fortify Alchemy](Skyrim_Fortify_Alchemy.md) enchantments in the game that can be disenchanted (and the only one that does not require you to join a faction or complete an optional leg of a particular quest). It can be purchased from Frida once you deliver it, but only with the Merchant perk.
- If you make Roggi Knot-Beard a follower, you may be able to take the shield from his inventory.
- Only one radiant quest of a similar type can be assigned at any given time. You must clear the radiant quest from your miscellaneous quests in order to be eligible to receive another of the same type.
- Because this is a favor quest which affects the quest giver's disposition, it will count towards the "Help the People" part of the [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) quest for the quest giver's hold.
- Frida and Oengul will only have the relevant dialogue options during their business hours.

## Bugs
- [Shrouded Grove](Skyrim_Shrouded_Grove.md) should also have been available for Frida's quest, but the location wasn't marked as a cave. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- For the skill-increase rewards, the quest giver may say that they are giving you the training, but the skill does not actually increase, and you don't get a notification that it has increased. <sup>**?**</sup>

- - This may happen when that skill was very close to leveling up anyway when you returned the item to the quest giver. One possible solution is to reload a save from before you returned the item, level up that skill by other means (using it, hiring a [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md), or reading a [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md)), and then turn the item in.
