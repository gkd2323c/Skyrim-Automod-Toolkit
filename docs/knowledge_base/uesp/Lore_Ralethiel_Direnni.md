[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/ON-place-Isle_of_Balfiera.jpg/200px-ON-place-Isle_of_Balfiera.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Isle_of_Balfiera.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Isle_of_Balfiera.jpg) Isle of Balfiera **King** Ralethiel of Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) was the ruler of [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) -controlled [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), during the times of the conflict between Clan Direnni and the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AG-1)</sup>

With the rise of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) in Cyrodiil in [1E 361](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_361), many Ayleids fleeing Cyrodiil migrated to High Rock to escape the order in an exodus known as the [Ayleid Diaspora](Lore_Ayleid_Diaspora.md), which may have strengthened Clan Direnni as these immigrants were absorbed into their hegemony.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> When [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md) and his people arrived at the Bjoulsae in [1E 375](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_375), they were welcomed by the Direnni and given a place to settle.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ASi V-4)</sup> During the reign of Ralethiel, many Ayleids hoped to aid the Direnni against the Alessians, hoping to get lands from the king as a reward.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AG-1)</sup> One such Ayleid was betrayed by his brothers and prevented from participating in the war. He was bound in the Dresan Keep in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AG-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AMC-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FADS-6)</sup> and was not able to witness any of the Direnni victories,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AG-1)</sup> notably the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TFL-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DRD-8)</sup>

The ghost of the Ralethiel's supporter was ultimately freed in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md), but it is unknown whether he passed to [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) or remained in [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AG-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AMC-5)</sup>
