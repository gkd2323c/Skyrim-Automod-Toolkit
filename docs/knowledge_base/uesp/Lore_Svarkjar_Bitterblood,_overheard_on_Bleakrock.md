Svarkjar Bitterblood, overheard on Bleakrock **by [Svarkjar Bitterblood](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Svarkjar_Bitterblood)** *A Nord's complaints on dealing with Dunmer on Bleakrock Isle*
---
[![ON-concept-House Banner.png](https://images.uesp.net/3/3e/ON-concept-House_Banner.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-House_Banner.png)
- "If I didn't know better, I'd think the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) just do it to frustrate us. [Great House](Lore_Great_Houses.md) this and that and all the running around to get permission to do anything. I don't know who to go to for what half the time! [Back home](Lore_Skyrim.md), you have your Jarl, and that's that; you know who the boss is and who makes the decisions. Here, you're in trouble for looking the wrong way at the wrong Dark Elf!

- They're completely humorless! Just one little joke and all of the sudden, the only trade route you can get authorization for is to [this damned rock](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md). I thought we'd profit nicely off the trade opened by [the Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), but I'm not so sure about it now. If one more snotty Elf looks down his nose at me, I swear I'm going to break it!"
