Sir Roderic of Wayrest was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [knight](Lore_Knight.md) from [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) who was a veteran of the [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md), where he fought for [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) alongside [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) against invading [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). He was said to have won many battles, and was allegedly famous in High Rock.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBLathon-1)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) he had made his way to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where he and his squire Lathon were performing a pilgrimage to the [Wayshrines of the Nine](Lore_Wayshrines.md). He ultimately met his end in [Underpall Cave](Lore_Underpall_Cave.md) trying to recover the [Greaves](Lore_Greaves_of_the_Crusader.md) and [Sword of the Crusader](Lore_Sword_of_the_Crusader.md) from the [wraith](Lore_Wraith.md) of Lord Berich Vlindrel. His squire Lathon managed to escape with the greaves and brought them to the [Priory of the Nine](Lore_Priory_of_the_Nine.md). A [pilgrim](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) searching for the artifacts accepted them from him, and avenged Sir Roderic's death by freeing Lord Vlindrel of the evil which had ensnared his soul.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NOTN-2)</sup>
