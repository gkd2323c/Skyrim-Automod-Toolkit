[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-item-Shrouded_Shoes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Shoes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Shoes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Shoes.jpg) Shrouded Shoes Shrouded Shoes are the footwear specially designed for use by the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). These loafers are specifically a part of a set of equipment that also includes a pair of [hand wraps](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.md), a [hood](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hood.md), and a set of [robes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Robes.md). The boots have been enchanted to help assassins on missions by making them [muffle](Skyrim_Muffle_(effect).md) sound created by the wearer by half, allowing for stealthy approaches and quiet movement when it is most critical to remain undetected.
