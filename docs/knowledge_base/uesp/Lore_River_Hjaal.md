[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-place-River_Hjaal.jpg/200px-SR-place-River_Hjaal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-River_Hjaal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-River_Hjaal.jpg) River Hjaal The River Hjaal is the main river that flows through the center of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Its headwaters are located in northwestern [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md), in the highlands south of [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md). After dropping down a series of waterfalls and entering Hjaalmarch, the river passes northeast through Morthal, then spreads out and drains into the [Karth delta](Lore_Karth_River.md), the marshy area where the River Hjaal and the larger Karth River empty into the icy [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) through a variety of intricate channels.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>

After entering Hjaalmarch, a separate stream from the headwaters also breaks northwest and drops down some waterfalls, eventually flowing into the Karth River just north of [Dragon Bridge](Lore_Dragon_Bridge.md). This divergence essentially makes an island out of a large portion of Hjaalmarch wherein [Fort Snowhawk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Snowhawk) and several ruins can be found.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>

Fishermen can collect a wide range of fish from the river, including [Abecean Longfin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Abecean_Longfin), [Cyrodilic Spadetail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cyrodilic_Spadetail), [Histcarp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Histcarp), [River Betty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:River_Betty), [Silverside Perch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silverside_Perch), and [Salmon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salmon). People have also been known to make a living hunting for [mudcrabs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mudcrab) on its banks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>.
