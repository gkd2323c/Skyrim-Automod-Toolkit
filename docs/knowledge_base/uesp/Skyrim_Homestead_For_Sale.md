Homestead For Sale *A note directing potential buyers to the steward*
---
<center>HOMESTEAD FOR SALE</center> This home, and its surrounding environs, is available for purchase.

I have had many cherished adventures in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but now I am afraid I must leave it behind. The war has made life difficult on all of us, and I fear that the worst is yet to come.

I have entrusted the steward in [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) with managing the sale of my homestead. If interested, please seek him out.
