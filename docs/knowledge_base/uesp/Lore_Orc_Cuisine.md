Orc cooking can be brutal for folks used to lighter fare.*
—Chef Obgurob<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COD-1)</sup> Because of their tight-knit tribal communities, the [Orsimer](Lore_Orc.md) cook many communal meals where a large central dish is shared among many individuals. <sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOC-2)</sup><sup>:127</sup> While some live in Orsinium, most live in strongholds scattered across the continent. Each stronghold needs to be self-sufficient, so their diet is predominantly practical fare that can be foraged, hunted, or easily grown, then stored. Stronghold Orcs don't have much room for ornamentation, but they make use of a wide variety of spices and seasonings, which they acquire through trade. The fires of their forges are often used to roast skewers of meat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOC-2)</sup><sup>:12</sup> Cooking is usually seen as women's work among Orcs but some Orc men who enjoy cooking do take up the vocation as well.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COD-1)</sup> A resourceful people, Orcish fungus-farmers developed techniques for cultivating food from carefully managed waste, a practice that sustained them through the [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGSOO-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RTO-4)</sup>

## Utensils and Tableware
Metal and stone dishes are the Orcish tradition.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The [Shatul Clan](Lore_Shatul_Clan.md) is a producer of polished brass mess kits<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and other smiths produce sturdy mess kits made from burnished steel.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> The [Morkul clan](Lore_Morkul_Clan.md) forges absurdly heavy tea kettles with sharp edges.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> There are exceptions to this harsh style such as beautiful and delicate crystal wine decanters.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Orc crafted porcelain teacups can also be quite delicate.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Some Orc chefs use spice racks crafted from finely filigreed copper.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

Orcish drinkware and eating utensils are often times crafted from stone or horn. Finer drinking horns may be made from [Silver](Lore_Silver.md)<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> and some drinkware made from cut glass.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-OSSIC-13)</sup> Gift worthy spoons are crafted from Snow- [Ogre](Lore_Ogre.md) fingernails and have scrimshaw handles.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> A chief may use a mighty toothpick crafted from whale bone.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Some Orcish forks are single-tined, resembling a skewer more than a typical fork.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
