Worm Cult Mine Orders **by The Court of Mannimarco at Mor Naril** *A set of orders regarding the creation and testing of Soul Flayers* *
---
To: Overseer Bazgrum, Kaz-Neesak Mine
From: The Court of [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) at Mor Naril

Bazgrum,

Attached are the new goals for perfect crystal production for your mines. These perfect crystals are key to our creation of new power cores for the soul flayers. We need as many as you can produce, as quickly as possible. Run the mines continually. Use [undead](Lore_Undead.md) if necessary. When completed, install the dormant soul flayers at the sites indicated on the attached map. We will send someone to activate the flayers at the prescribed time. These models emit but a single pulse, eliminating all life in the area to harvest their [souls](Lore_Soul.md). If this test proves successful, we will set future flayers for continuous pulse emissions.

See that you complete this task quickly. Mannimarco will not tolerate failure or delay.

Also, a prisoner will pass through your encampment on the way to his new cell. Give his tenders whatever aid they require then send them on their way. Mannimarco has special plans for this one and will meet him at the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) site to personally oversee that operation. Using the [prisoner](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md)'s power, Mannimarco will raise an army of undead from the ancient necropolis.
