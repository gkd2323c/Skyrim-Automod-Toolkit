Blaise Guimard was the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) King of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) during the mid-sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and the first monarch from [House Guimard](Lore_Guimard.md). After [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md) the Black Drake and his horde of [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) sacked Evermore in [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542), Duke Guimard led the [Knights of Saint Pelin](Lore_Order_of_Saint_Pelin.md) in the charge that retook the city from them. The King of Evermore, [Heseph](Lore_Heseph.md), died heirless when the Reachmen sacked the city, so after Duke Guimard retook it, the nobles of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) elected him to the throne.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-1)</sup> A fortnight after Durcorach and his horde were defeated in a battle outside [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), King Blaise and the kings of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md), [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md), and [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), realizing how weak their realms were individually, signed the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), a pact promising to defend each other's kingdoms and unite as one against all foreign foes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTTDC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IEGt THR-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOAM3-4)</sup>

In [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md) of Shornhelm declared war on Wayrest after King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) married Princess [Maraya](Lore_Maraya.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) instead of his daughter [Rayelle](Lore_Rayelle.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTTDC-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TFOKR-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOAM6-6)</sup> Ranser and his army besieged Wayrest, but Blaise, the kings of Camlorn and Daggerfall, and the [Redguards](Lore_Redguards.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) sided with Emeric, breaking the siege in the spring of [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTTDC-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TFOKR-5)</sup> After Ranser's defeat in 2E 567, the Breton kingdoms, the Redguards of Sentinel, and the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) under [Kurog gro-Bagrakh](Lore_Kurog_gro-Bagrakh.md) came together to form the second or greater Daggerfall Covenant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTTDC-2)</sup>

Because Blaise aided Emeric during [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), his son, Prince [Eamond](Lore_Eamond.md), was married to one of Emeric's cousins, [Arzhela](Lore_Arzhela.md), as a reward. In [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568), shortly after the marriage of Eamond and Arzhela, Blaise died, and Eamond succeeded him as King of Evermore.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-1)</sup>
