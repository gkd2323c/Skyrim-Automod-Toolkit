[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-place-Champion%27s_Rest.jpg/200px-SR-place-Champion%27s_Rest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Champion%27s_Rest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Champion%27s_Rest.jpg) Champion's Rest **Champion's Rest** is a medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) located in [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), east of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md). It was once used as a gladiatorial arena but now serves as a crypt for fallen warriors. Once lost to time, miners from Shor's Stone have recently broken through into the ruins while mining for [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md).

It consists of two zones: [Champion's Rest](#Champion.27s_Rest) and [Champion's Rest Amphitheater](#Champion.27s_Rest_Amphitheater).
