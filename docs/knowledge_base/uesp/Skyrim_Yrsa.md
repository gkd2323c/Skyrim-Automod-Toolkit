[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Yrsa.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Yrsa.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yrsa.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yrsa.jpg) Yrsa **Yrsa** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md). She lives with her husband, [Deor Woodcutter](Skyrim_Deor_Woodcutter.md) in their [house](Skyrim_Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.md). You may find her laboring at the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md) with [Oslaf](Skyrim_Oslaf.md) during the quest [The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md). She will be free afterwards and return to her village.

She wears a [Skaal coat](Skyrim_Skaal_Coat.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Skaal_Boots.md) and [hat](Skyrim_Skaal_Hat.md). She carries a [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), her [house key](Skyrim_Deor%27s_House_Key.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md)**: Uncover the source of [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s power in the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md).

## Dialogue
Yrsa may greet you with:

- *"I fear what would have happened if you hadn't freed us."*
- *"This * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *[Dark Elves](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) keep to their city, and we Skaal keep to our village."*
- *"[Fanari](Skyrim_Fanari_Strong-Voice.md) speaks her mind, perhaps more than she should."*
- *"The Skaal and the land are one, joined by the All-Maker."*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Gardener of Men
Yrsa will witness Storn's death and approach the body to pay her respects.

- **Yrsa:** *"Oh... so terrible. When will these dark times be over?"*

If you speak to her, she says:

- *"Leave us to grieve."*

## Conversations
Yrsa may have a short conversation with Oslaf about her snowberry pies:

**Oslaf**: *"When will you make another snowberry pie, Yrsa? It's been a long time."*
**Yrsa**: *"The snowberries are not yet sweet enough. I tasted one just this morning."*
**Oslaf**: *"Just make sure that when the berries ripen, you make more pies than you did last time."*
**Yrsa**: *"Yes, I remember that you ate one of them all by yourself. Maybe this time, you will leave some for the rest of us!"*
**Oslaf**: *"Not if I can help it!"*

She may also talk with Deor about something under their bed:

**Yrsa**: *"Husband, do you know why there's a pouch of spiced tea leaves under the bed?"*
**Deor**: *"What?! That was supposed to be a gift! I was hoping to surprise you."*
**Yrsa**: *"That is sweet of you, Deor, but you should find a better hiding place next time."*
**Deor**: *"If I'd known that I was married to a fox who digs under beds to look for treats, then perhaps I would have."*
