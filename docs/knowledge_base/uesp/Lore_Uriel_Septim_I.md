Uriel Septim I was the fourth [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for eleven years, from [3E 53](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_53) to [3E 64](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_64). He was preceded by his mother, [Kintyra I](Lore_Kintyra_I.md), and was succeeded by his son, [Uriel II](Lore_Uriel_II.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

## Biography
Uriel Septim was the son of [Kintyra Septim](Lore_Kintyra_I.md) (later Empress Kintyra I).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> At some point, he had a son, [Uriel](Lore_Uriel_II.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> In [3E 53](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_53), Kintyra I died, so Uriel was crowned Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BA-3)</sup><sup>:16</sup>

Uriel I was the great lawmaker of the Septim Dynasty, and a promoter of independent organizations and guilds. The [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) increased in prominence throughout Tamriel during his reign. During his reign, Uriel I became known as a kind and tender ruler but also a firm one.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> He was the first of seven emperors to use the name Uriel, a practice that ended with [Uriel VII](Lore_Uriel_VII.md)'s death in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTET-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BA-3)</sup><sup>:16</sup> In [3E 64](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_64), Uriel I died and was succeeded by his son, Uriel II.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTET-4)</sup> His son's reign witnessed the beginning of the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md)'s disunification, and it would not return to the level of unity seen during Uriel I's reign until the days of [Pelagius IV](Lore_Pelagius_IV.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-5)</sup>
