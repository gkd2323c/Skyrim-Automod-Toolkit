# Fearstruck

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/ON-crown_store-Fearstruck.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Fearstruck.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Fearstruck.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Fearstruck.jpg) Second Era replica of Fearstruck Fearstruck was a Daedric [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md). It took the form of a shield. It belonged to [Lyrisius](Lore_Lyrisius.md), a hero from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) famous for leading his army against the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) [slavetraders](Lore_Slavery.md) in their homeland of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). The shield was utterly destroyed in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) by the fiery breath of a [wyrm](Lore_Dragon.md) who Lyrisius encountered after his army was routed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Despite the destruction of the artifact many centuries prior, replica shields styled after Fearstruck were sighted circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). These replicas depicted Fearstruck as an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) shield emblazoned with Boethiah's symbol of a fist surrounded by writhing golden [snakes](Lore_Snake.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
