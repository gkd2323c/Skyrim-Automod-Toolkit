[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-menu-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns%21.jpg/200px-SR-menu-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns%21.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.jpg) The Gray Cowl Returns! **The Gray Cowl Returns!** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds the [Gray Cowl of Nocturnal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gray_Cowl_of_Nocturnal), a Daedric artifact associated with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md). The Gray Cowl was last seen in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.

## Official Summary
Creation Club *The legend of the Gray Fox is known throughout history, yet the ones who wear the cowl remain nameless. To become the next Gray Fox, you must first restore the identity of the Cowl's bearer, and only then can your crimes be bereft of a name. Quest created by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "The Gray Cowl of Nocturnal" starts by confronting a thief in the Riften graveyard.)* Creation Club Anniversary Sale - August 2019 *We have two upcoming adventures. Skulk across Skyrim in the hopes of obtaining the fabled Gray Cowl of Nocturnal* -

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-prerelease-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns%21.jpg/200px-SR-prerelease-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns%21.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.jpg) The original cover from 2017 - Promotional images on the [Creation Club website](https://creationclub.bethesda.net/en) and the [*Creation Club for Fallout 4 & Skyrim Special Edition – E3 Announce Trailer*](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XRkrasc T_i M) showed this Creation back in 2017. Its release was pushed back due to the community being very vocal about desiring more in-depth quests.
- The Antique Nordic Doll is just a Child's Doll marked as a quest item taken from Nivenor's Strongbox. The quest must be completed to remove its quest item status.
- There are two variants of the Stranger's Final Instructions depending on the method used to acquire the Land Title And Deed off Gisli. Pickpocketing it leads to a variation.
