# Order of Arkay

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/OB-Window-Arkay.jpg/165px-OB-Window-Arkay.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-Window-Arkay.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-Window-Arkay.jpg) Arkay [![](https://images.uesp.net/a/a3/DF-sign-Order_of_Arkay.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-sign-Order_of_Arkay.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-sign-Order_of_Arkay.png) Order of Arkay *We are the Order of Arkay, the God of Birth and Death. Arkay is the great spirit who brings every man and woman, human, elf, khajiit, and Argonian into this world, and when the time is true, ends their circle. Arkay is fertility and blight, joy and sorrow. At the Order, we celebrate the Blessed Neutrality that Arkay stands for. While we do not offer strangers blessings, for to give favor or curse would be to upset the eternal balance, the Order offers much to its members. Our libraries and sages are some of the finest in Tamriel. Of course, few are worthy to be initiated into the mysteries of Birth and Death.* The Order of Arkay (also called the **Temple of Arkay**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFDesecrated-1)</sup> is the temple dedicated to [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) the Mortals' God of Birth and Death. They are generally the place of worship for him. The order reveres the neutrality of Arkay; it is absolute to them and, because of this, they will not offer strangers their blessings. To the order, to give favor or curse to another person will upset the eternal balance. Their expansive collection of libraries and repertoire of sages is considered some of the finest in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), however, the order is rather private and does not take applications lightly.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFDesc-2)</sup> The [Knights of the Circle](Lore_Knights_of_the_Circle.md) is their militant arm and the [House of Dibella](Lore_House_of_Dibella.md) is their enemy. In the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), the order has many chapels, some of which include the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), and the [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md) and [Shalgora](Lore_Shalgora.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFOrder-3)</sup>

## Further Information
From the earliest rank of the novice, members of the order are tasked with protecting the souls of the living and destroying those that would temper with the soul such as necromancers and even Daedra. The order follows their own code of conduct, [Arkay's three consecrations](Lore_Consecrations_of_Arkay.md). *Arkay's Grace* is a blessing at birth, to protect the young until they are old enough to make their own choices. *Arkay's Blessing* is given at death to secure their souls from manipulation, and finally *Arkay's Law* which is given to the dead so that their body is not resurrected into unwilling servitude.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TCo A-4)</sup> Like other divine temples, the Order of Arkay focuses on several skills that determine a member's ranking in the temple, among these include the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) language, the school of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) magic, and medical knowledge. The highest-known ranking in the order is the Patriarch/Matriarch,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFOrder-3)</sup> or the Primate.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBNPC-5)</sup> Sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), amidst the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), Countess [Eselde Tamrith](Lore_Eselde_Tamrith.md) of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) studied at the Order of Arkay south of her home of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSo PE-6)</sup>

During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), [Errandil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Errandil), a [Living Saint](Lore_Living_Saints.md) of Arkay, was present at the great chapel of Arkay in [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), and was also one of the most outspoken crusaders against the practice of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) in the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Gt C-7)</sup> He was among the supporters of Arch-Mage [Hannibal Traven](Lore_Hannibal_Traven.md)'s ban on the black arts.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-OBErrandil-8)</sup> At around the same time, the [Aurorans](Lore_Auroran.md) attacked the chapel and slaughtered some of the temple's officials. The [Divine Crusader](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) defeated the invaders and purified the [Sword of the Crusader](Lore_Sword_of_the_Crusader.md) in the temple's altar to the Nine.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBSword-9)</sup>

## Knights of the Circle
The **Knights of the Circle** is the militant arm of the Order of Arkay. Their sole duty is to fight for the temple as their devotion to both the order and their patron deity is absolute. Only the strongest warriors and loyal devouts of Arkay are considered for the knightly order.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-DFCircle-10)</sup>

## Notes
- Rivenspire has knights of Arkay, though it is unconfirmed if they are the same as the Knights of the Circle.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESORelics-11)</sup>
