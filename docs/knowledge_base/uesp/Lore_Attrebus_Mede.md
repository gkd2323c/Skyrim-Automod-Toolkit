Attrebus Mede was a [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) crown prince of the [Mede Dynasty](Lore_Mede_Dynasty.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Emperor)</sup>

The son and heir apparent of [Emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) [Titus Mede I](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md), he was one of several heroes involved in the banishment of [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48). Bards used to make songs about his triumphs, and how he managed to win battles against all odds without taking casualties, never losing more than three of his personal guard in one battle anywhere, including the Battle of [Blinker Creek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blinker_Creek).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup> Attrebus wielded a sword called Flashing and was famous for his six-edge attack.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 2, Chapter 6</sup>

He had blond hair and a slight bend in his nose much like his mother, and green eyes, a strong chin, and a lean face like his father.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 2, Chapter 1</sup>

## Notes
- | [^](#note_Emperor) | Although it is never stated that Attrebus Mede succeeded his father as emperor, it can be assumed that he did as he was the crown prince as of 4E 48; there was no indication of any other heirs to the throne at the time, and the Mede Dynasty remained the ruling Imperial dynasty until at least the end of [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md)'s reign. |
