[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Wilmuth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wilmuth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilmuth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wilmuth.jpg) Wilmuth at Chillfurrow Farm **Wilmuth** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) running [Chillfurrow Farm](Skyrim_Chillfurrow_Farm.md) east of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

While the owner of the farmhouse, [Nazeem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem), seems more interested in Whiterun and his limited influence on the [local jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), Wilmuth single-handedly maintains the farm, working for twelve solid hours starting at 8am while sleeping for only four hours between midnight and 4am. At all other times he wanders restlessly around inside.

He wears a simple set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f), and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). He only carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Chillfurrow_Farmhouse) to the farmhouse and a small selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
