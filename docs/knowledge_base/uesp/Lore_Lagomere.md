[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/ON-place-Lagomere.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lagomere.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lagomere.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lagomere.jpg) Lagomere [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-map-Lagomere.jpg/200px-ON-map-Lagomere.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Lagomere.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Lagomere.jpg) Map of Lagomere circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lagomere is a region that consists of the central portion of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Lagomere is home to the capital of [Great House](Lore_Great_House.md) [Indoril](Lore_Indoril.md), the city-state of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> located within the greater city of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia_(city).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The region also contains [Lake Amaya](Lore_Lake_Amaya_(Deshaan).md) and several [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins, including Bthanual and Mzithumz. The illustrious school of magic, [Shad Astula](Lore_Shad_Astula.md), was located in the region's northernmost reaches. Lagomere borders the regions of [Redolent Loam](Lore_Redolent_Loam.md) to the west, [Siltreen](Lore_Siltreen.md) to the east, and the [Reticulated Spine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reticulated_Spine) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>
