[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The Great Arch (also called the **Cliffs of Solitude**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARExclamations-1)</sup> or **Solitude**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DVN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LL-3)</sup> is a natural landmark that hovers over the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) estuary, in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The natural arch is an extension of [Mount Kilkreath](Lore_Mount_Kilkreath.md) in [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) that goes over the Karth River and stretches southeast to the edge of the [Drajkmyr Marsh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Drajkmyr_Marsh), which is already part of the neighboring hold, [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md). At the base of the arch, on the east side is the ancient barrow, [Folgunthur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Folgunthur), the west side has a road pass that reaches around the mountain and a large cave used as a warehouse by the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company). But on the top of the landmark is the Nordic city, [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md).

The Great Arch was more than an interesting landmark, but a windbreaker for Solitude's port yard and easily-defensible position, making it ideal for a grand city. The city's construction extended across the arch, all of it culminated to the Avenues District and the [Blue Palace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blue_Palace), which sits right on the edge of the landmark.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Wt WXI-4)</sup>
