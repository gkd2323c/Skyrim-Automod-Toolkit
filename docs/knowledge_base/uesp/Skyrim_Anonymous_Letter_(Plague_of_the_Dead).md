Anonymous Letter *A letter pleading for help in stopping a ritual summoning* *
---
Someone, please help! A group of [Necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) are experimenting with conjuring [Zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md) using ancient, dangerous magic, which I fear will have unintended and irreversible consequences. I was expelled from their studies as their ambitions grew into folly, against my warnings.

They're performing a ritual to summon a large group of these foul, uncontrollable creatures as we speak in the southern-most *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* area of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and must be stopped before it's too late!
