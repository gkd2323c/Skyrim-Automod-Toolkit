# Shadowscales

*Hail to Sithis, blood's drop on blade's edge.
Before you, nothing. Behind you, the Void.
Catalyst, agitator, many-fanged maw.
Whisper your need to the Scales.*
— [Inscription found on an Argonian blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Inscription_found_on_an_Argonian_blade) The Shadowscales are an order of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) assassins originating in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Argonians born under the sign of [the Shadow](Lore_The_Shadow.md) are taken at birth and offered to the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) where they are trained in the arts of stealth and assassination. Shadowscales can identify other members of the order by special medallions. They are typically made of brass and inscribed with the likeness of Sithis, the Dread Father.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Contraband S-1)</sup> Any Shadowscale who lives to come of age is accepted into the Dark Brotherhood as a full member of the family.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BTS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBTeinaava-3)</sup> They may also leave the Brotherhood to serve the Argonian people in Black Marsh as ku-vastei, the agents of needed change and arbiters.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sulahkeesh-4)</sup> They act with impunity there, and some are said to serve as personal assassins to the King of Black Marsh<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRVeezara-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRAOV-6)</sup> and as protectors of the ancient members of the royalty.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Antiquity-7)</sup> However, no such king is thought to have ruled since the height of Argonian civilization in ages past—if at all—and the monarch's role as commander of the Shadowscales is said to be overstated.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-IWTAS-8)</sup>

Shadowscales follow the same [five tenets](Lore_The_Five_Tenets.md) traditionally followed by members of the Brotherhood,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BTS-2)</sup> although they have a distinct set of rules. For example, a Shadowscale cannot kill a fellow Shadowscale even if they are not members of the Brotherhood. However, breaking a tenet of the Brotherhood invokes the [Wrath of Sithis](Lore_Wrath_of_Sithis.md), and forgiveness is possible if the family member survives the encounter. If a Shadowscale breaks a tenet, it is viewed as treason and the perpetrator will be put to death. This is usually carried out by the [Argonian Royal Court](Lore_Kingdom_of_Black_Marsh.md), who will send an unaffiliated assassin to carry out the execution.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> During contracts, Shadowscales seek to kill their targets swiftly. Prolonged and torturous kills are generally frowned upon.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Among common Argonians, especially the Black-Tongues, the Shadowscales are deeply respected.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-To M-11)</sup> While all tribes of the [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) participate in the Shadowscale tradition, the Black-Tongue tribe take it the most seriously and spend a large amount of effort towards producing Shadowscales.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-To M-11)</sup>

Some Shadowscales were opposed to the formation of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), with some even turning traitor and fleeing Black Marsh.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> However, some Shadowscales fought on the Pact's side during the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) invasion of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by liberating the town of Murkwater.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

With the closure of the training facility in [Archon](Lore_Archon.md), and the death of the [last known living Shadowscale](Lore_Veezara.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), it is possible that the Shadowscales have become extinct.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Skyrim-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-CJ1-15)</sup>
