Bone Hawks are [undead](Lore_Undead.md) [skeletal](Lore_Skeleton.md) [birds](Lore_Bird.md), similar in appearance to a regular [hawk](Lore_Hawk.md) but with black feathers and an exposed skull.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim Bone-1)</sup> They are sometimes hunted and used to create rings and amulets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Bone Hawks are associated with [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), and can be found circling [Castle Volkihar](Lore_Castle_Volkihar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim Bone-1)</sup>
