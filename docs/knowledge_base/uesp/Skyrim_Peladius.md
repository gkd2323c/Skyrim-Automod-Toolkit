[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/SR-npc-Peladius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Peladius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peladius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Peladius.jpg) Peladius **Peladius** is the real identity of [Aldepius](Skyrim_Aldepius.md). He is a bandit and a former member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) bandit gang. He can be found on the bridge of Dragon Bridge after finding his [confession](Skyrim_Aldepius%27_Confession.md), where he will have to be fought to the death.

He wears a full set of enchanted [steel soldier armor](Skyrim_Steel_Soldier_Armor.md). He can use a [long bow](Skyrim_Long_Bow.md) and a [steel sword of embers](Skyrim_Steel_Sword_of_Embers.md) to attack you, he also carries 30 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md) and a [potion of plentiful healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing.md).
