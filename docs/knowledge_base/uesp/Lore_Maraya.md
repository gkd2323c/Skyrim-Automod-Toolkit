Queen Maraya, dubbed the Jewel of [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md), was the eldest daughter of King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md) and [Za-Rifah](Lore_Za-Rifah.md), and sister to Lakana and Crown Prince [Azah](Lore_Azah.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLOS-1)</sup> She was wed to King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) to cement a trade agreement between [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), bringing great prosperity to the two states. However, the move drew the ire of King Ranser, who felt betrayed as Emeric had planned to marry his daughter [Rayelle](Lore_Rayelle.md). Maraya and Emeric's wedding took place in the spring of [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), followed by Ranser's surprise attack on [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) that culminated in [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md). He was ultimately defeated and the Greater [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) formed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFOKR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOAM-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTTDC-4)</sup>
