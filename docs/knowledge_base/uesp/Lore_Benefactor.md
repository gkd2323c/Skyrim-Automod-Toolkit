Here lies the spirit of Benefactor, though his body still guards the way.
—The inscription on the Benefactor's grave Benefactor (or the Benefactor) is a [spirit](Lore_Ghost.md) bound to their grave in the [Mantellan Crux](Lore_Mantellan_Crux.md). However, the Benefactor's body still "guards the Way" further into the crux, granting passage into the Great Pyramid there to those that can discover his name. When [the Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) travelled through the Mantellan Crux, they had to speak the Benefactor's name to him to be granted passage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Jt A-1)</sup>
