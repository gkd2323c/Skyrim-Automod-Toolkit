# Golden Scab

The Golden Scab is a curio associated with [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), it was last known to be in the possession of Gazmod the Collector, a [watcher](Lore_Watcher.md) residing in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md), alongside the [Toxicruciform](Lore_Toxicruciform.md) and [Bilious Censer](Lore_Bilious_Censer.md). Eorda, a worshipper of Peryite, believed that she could gain Peryite's favor by somehow recovering these artifacts for her lord.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Eorda-1)</sup>
