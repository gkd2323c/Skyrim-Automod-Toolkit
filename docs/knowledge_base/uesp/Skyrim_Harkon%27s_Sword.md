[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-item-Harkon%27s_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Harkon%27s_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Harkon%27s_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Harkon%27s_Sword.jpg) Harkon's Sword Harkon's Sword is a [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) sword carried by [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md). It can be acquired through either pickpocketing or by killing him when completing the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on's [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests). It is similar in appearance to a [Blades katana](Skyrim_Blades_Sword.md), except that its sword guard is decorated to resemble [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), and the handle is black. Its effect is unique in that its enchantment will only take effect if wielded by a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md).

## Notes
- It can be pickpocketed from Harkon's inventory without the [Misdirection](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Misdirection) perk, as he does not have it equipped.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> each contain a special holder for this item.
- The [Dwarven Black Bow of Fate](Skyrim_Dwarven_Black_Bow_of_Fate.md) is one other weapon in the game with a similar enchantment. However, it differs from Harkon's Sword by the fact that it has a chance to absorb one or more attributes (or none at all), and the wielder does not have to be a vampire in order for the enchantment to activate.

## Bugs
- Lord Harkon never has the sword equipped due to a faulty setting on his combat style. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. His essential instance (the one you never actually fight with) now uses a combat style that will make him equip it.
