[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Bor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bor.jpg) Bor **Bor** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives and works in [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md) at [Gloombound Mine](Skyrim_Gloombound_Mine.md). She spends her time mining the [ebony ore](Skyrim_Ebony_Ore.md) there.

Like her fellow workers, Bor spends all her time inside Gloombound Mine, mining the ore inside. She may occasionally stop to relax, drink, or eat.

Bor wears a set of [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) along with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots). She wields a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) which can be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality and carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe).
