[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Hulda.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hulda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hulda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hulda.jpg) Hulda, owner of The Bannered Mare **Hulda** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [innkeeper](Skyrim_Inns.md) who owns the [Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). She plans on retiring soon and selling the inn to [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md), who is trying to save up the money to buy the inn, but this never actually happens. If Hulda is killed, Ysolda will immediately take over the Bannered Mare, but will still speak of buying the inn from Hulda.

She does not go to bed, instead staying behind the counter almost all of the time. Like all innkeepers, she will sell some food and ingredients, and offers a bed for rent at a rate of 10 gold. She will also pass on rumors about quests and give [bounties](Skyrim_Bounty_Quests.md) to complete for Whiterun's jarl. Additionally, she will [buy firewood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) for 5 gold each, which increases her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) and allows you to take low value items from the inn.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Greetings while she is working at the inn:

- *"I enjoy this work well enough, but I'm ready to retire. I've been thinking of selling the inn to Ysolda. Anyway, what do you need?"*
- *"What can I get you?"*
- *"Hungry, tired, or just plain thirsty?"*
- *"If it's work you need, how about chopping up some wood for the fires?"*
- *"Here for work? Get an axe and bring me all the wood you can chop."*

Greetings outside the Bannered Mare:

- *"I'm just out and about, love. The Bannered Mare can do without me for a bit, I suspect."*
- *"I don't get much time time off from the tavern, but I enjoy it when I do."*
- *"I'll get back to the Bannered Mare soon enough. Just need to stretch my legs a bit..."*

While at the inn, if you sit in a chair or enter the kitchen, Hulda will say:

- *"[Saadia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saadia), wake up dear!"* Saadia will reply, *"Yes, mum!"* and approach you to offer you a drink. Hulda will do this even if you sit on the bar stool right in front of her.

- **Where can I learn more about magic?**
- *"Magic is for the weak. Elves, I mean. They keep to that [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). They know their place. Of course there is [Farengar](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md). He might be the Jarl's wizard, but at least he's a Nord."*

If you are either a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), or [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md), she will be even more annoyed:

- *"Pfft, I should've guessed as much. Your kind sticks to that College in Winterhold, as well they should. You'd never think to learn from a Nord like the Jarl's wizard."*

You can also ask her for gossip and rumors. She may tell you about a job opportunity:

- *"The [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md)? In [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md)? They're recruiting new members, from what I hear."*
- *"I assume you saw the Gildergreen? Big tree, all burnt up, in the middle of the Wind District. It's part of the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md). People want [Danica](Skyrim_Danica_Pure-Spring.md) to do something about it, but I don't know what they expect."*
- *"Been hearing some strange tales of the Jarl's children. Say the one's turning wicked, and the others have an ill-favored look to them. Best to keep clear."*

When Whiterun is under control of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), you can ask her about her life under Stormcloak rule:

- **How's life under Stormcloak rule?**
- *"Well, the Stormcloak guards drink a lot more than the Imperials did, so I've been making more money lately. On the other hand, they're a violent bunch. Every night a fight breaks out and I end up with a smashed chair or a broken table. So, not much has changed, really."*

Upon ending conversation with her:

- *"Need anything else, just let me know."*
- *"I think I've got some mugs need scrubbin'."*

## Notes
- If Hulda dies before you receive the rumor for the [Whispering Door](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Whispering_Door), you may be unable to start the quest, due to a [bug](Skyrim_Ysolda.md#Bugs) with Ysolda.
- As part of an [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md), you could have delivered a case of Frost River mead to either Saadia or Hulda, who will reply, *"Ah, good. I've been waiting on that. Here's the gold for it. Thanks."*
- Like the other residents, she will never mention that Saadia is gone as a possible result of your choices in [her quest](Skyrim_In_My_Time_Of_Need.md).
- Hulda's line *"Saadia, wake up dear!"* is kept under the quest `WITavern`, which has both generic dialogue recorded for all innkeeper voice types, as well as unique lines like this one. However, likely due to an oversight, this line was also recorded in all possible innkeeper voice types, despite not ever getting used.
- Hulda sells almost all food and drink on display in the inn as well as her "normal" merchandise, making her stock much larger than other innkeepers'.
