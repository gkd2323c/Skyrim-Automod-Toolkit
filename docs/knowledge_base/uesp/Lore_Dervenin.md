Dervenin (or **Dervenin the Mad**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> is a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) and the former High Priest of [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). He preached the Manic beliefs of [Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md) in the Sacellum of [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md). His rival was [Arctus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arctus), the Priest of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md). Dervenin came to power within the Sacellum at the whim of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md); he could only preach his Manic teachings if the [Cold Flame of Agnon](Lore_Cold_Flame_of_Agnon.md) was lit for Mania. He was responsible for aiding anyone trying to replace the Duke of Mania, which requires a sacrifice of the current duke's [Greenmote](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenmote) -tainted blood to Arden-Sul.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shivering-2)</sup>

Eventually, he no longer held his position as High Priest of Mania.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CT-3)</sup> He instead became more of a personal servant and plaything for his master, who would play cruel jokes on him.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skeever-4)</sup>

## History
As High Priest, Dervenin helped [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) on several occasions, working to halt the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shivering-2)</sup>

When Sheogorath abandoned the Shivering Isles, Dervenin was brought to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) with him. By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Dervenin was living as a beggar in the [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) city of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). Here, he tricked the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) into entering the mind of the long-dead [Pelagius Septim III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md), where Sheogorath had taken up residence. After the Last Dragonborn cleansed the late emperor's mind of the madness which had plagued it in life, Dervenin confronted Sheogorath and pleaded with him to return to the Shivering Isles, eventually persuading him.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Skyrim-5)</sup>
