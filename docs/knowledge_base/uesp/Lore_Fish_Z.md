## Zander
The **Zander** is a type of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md).

- The **Colovian Zander** can found in the lakes of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).
- The **Rainbow Zander** can be found in the lakes of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).
- The **Red Zander** can be found in the lakes of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
