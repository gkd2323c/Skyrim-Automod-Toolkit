[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/ON-place-Vivec%27s_Antlers_Land-Coral.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vivec%27s_Antlers_Land-Coral.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vivec%27s_Antlers_Land-Coral.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vivec%27s_Antlers_Land-Coral.jpg) Vivec's Antlers circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Vivec's Antlers is a historical battlefield found in [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md), a region of the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Not only have the fields of Vivec's Antlers been the site of several battles in Morrowind's history, but it contains the best-known example of a bizarre form of [Land Coral](Lore_Coral.md#Land_Coral) along the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md). The local [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) claim that the Land Coral is both self-aware and thoughtful. Vivec's Antlers is the home of the [Coral Heart](Lore_Coral_Heart.md).

## History
During the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md) of [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), the fields of Vivec's Antlers served as the setting of the final battle involving the [Snow Demons](Lore_Kamal_(race).md) of [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(place).md) and their invasion of the province of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Akaviri-1)</sup> The battle had begun between the Akaviri, led by [Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal](Lore_Ada%27Soom_Dir-Kamal.md), and the Dark Elves under the command of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) and her leading general, Grandmaster [Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md). Despite the assistance of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), whose drowning of the Akaviri in Vivec's Antlers is referenced in Dunmeri culture, the Akaviri had the upper hand and had forced the Dark Elves east into prepared defenses.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> As the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) under [Jorunn the Skald-King](Lore_Jorunn.md) and [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) landed and fell upon the Akaviri rear, the tide of battle turned. However, the Kamali lines remained intact as they retreated to the Inner Sea, where they desperately tried to hold out for evacuation. It wasn't until the surprise arrival of the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) under the command of [Heita-Meen](Lore_Heita-Meen.md) that the Akaviri were ultimately defeated. The combined forces of these [races](Lore_Races.md) would go on to establish the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Akaviri-1)</sup>

Vivec's Antlers would be the site of another battle ten years later during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) between the Ebonheart Pact, the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), and even the local [dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) of Vivec's Antlers. The Covenant general Alexandra Conele had staged her camp on the shores of Vivec's Antlers, seeking to pillage the site and find the Coral Heart in the hope of summoning [Sadal](Lore_Sadal.md), one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md), to lay waste against the region of Stonefalls. However, Conele's operation was halted, and the Coral Heart was acquired by the Ebonheart Pact. The Coral Heart would later be used in Sadal's summoning by Tanval Indoril after the fateful battle at [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
