# Predator's Grace

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg/200px-SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg) Predator's Grace (Skyrim) Predator's Grace is a pair of unique [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) hide shoes,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:817</sup> imbued with a powerful [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) that causes its wearer to be perpetually muffled and to move in near-total silence, while also slightly hastening the natural regeneration of their stamina.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG-1)</sup> This pair of shoes also restored stamina at an accelerated rate whenever the wearer was wounded.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAG-4)</sup>

## History
At some point between [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176) and [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the shoes were found in a proximity of a group of [Dwarven Centurions](Lore_Dwarven_Centurion.md) and were possibly recovered by a member of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAG-4)</sup> Eventually, however, by circa 4E 201, they had come into the possession of the [Hags](Lore_Hag.md) and [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md) of Hag's End, located within Deepwood Vale and accessed through Deepwood Redoubt, a stronghold associated with the [Forsworn](Lore_Forsworn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HE-5)</sup> They were later possibly found by an [adventurer](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who subsequently made use of them throughout their travels and exploits.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HE-5)</sup>
