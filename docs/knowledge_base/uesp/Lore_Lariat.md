# Lariat Family

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/LO-misc-Wayrest.jpg/200px-LO-misc-Wayrest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Wayrest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Wayrest.jpg) Wayrest The Lariat Family (also spelled **Lariate**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMOP-1)</sup> was an influential [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) family of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), active in [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). They were distant cousins of the [Septims](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), and were known for their high position within the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE-2)</sup>

[Gallivere Lariat](Lore_Gallivere_Lariat.md) was consort of [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md) after the death of [Pelagius Septim III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md). With her, he was the father of [Uriel Septim IV](Lore_Uriel_Septim_IV.md), also known as Uriel Lariat. At some point Uriel Lariat became King of Wayrest, and was adopted into Septim Dynasty by Emperor [Cassynder](Lore_Cassynder.md). Cassynder was succeeded by his adopted half-brother as an Emperor. Despite the adoption and distant relation between Lariats and Septims, Uriel's lack of a close connection to the Septim bloodline led to lingering sedition against his rule. Few of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) could be persuaded to accept him fully as a blood descendant of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMOP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE-2)</sup>
