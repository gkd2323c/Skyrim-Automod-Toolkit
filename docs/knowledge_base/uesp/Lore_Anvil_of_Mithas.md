# Anvil of Mithas

The Anvil of Mithas is an ancient anvil created by [Mithas](Lore_Mithas.md), the greatest [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [smith](Lore_Smithing.md). It is twinned with the [Hammer of Gharen](Lore_Hammer_of_Gharen.md), which is the only object strong enough not to shatter when used to shape metal upon the Anvil. When the Hammer strikes the Anvil, it produces a ringing which reveals the location of the entrance to the Dwarven mines beneath [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup>

By the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), the Anvil had come into the possession of King Casik of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md). The King had the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md) bring him the Hammer, which had been lost in the dungeon known as [Black Gate](Lore_Black_Gate.md), and used the artifacts to craft the finest armor and weapons in the realm. The Anvil has never reportedly left the city's palace since.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup>
