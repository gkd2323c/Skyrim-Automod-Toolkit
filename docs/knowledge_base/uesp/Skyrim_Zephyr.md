[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg) Zephyr Zephyr is a unique [Dwarven bow](Skyrim_Dwarven_Bow.md) that belonged to [Katria](Skyrim_Katria.md) before she fell down a cliff to her death inside of [Arkngthamz](Skyrim_Arkngthamz.md). The bow itself rests at the top of a ravine, precariously perched on the side of a fallen tree. Zephyr's unique enchantment gives it a much higher [draw speed](Skyrim_Archery.md#Draw_Speed) than regular Dwarven bows, making it one of the quickest bows available, alongside [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) and the [long bow](Skyrim_Long_Bow.md). Zephyr can be further [improved](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) with a [Dwarven metal ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md) if the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk is unlocked, with the benefits of these alterations being doubled if the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk is also unlocked.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- The text "Fires 30% faster than a standard bow" is slightly misleading. Each different type of bow has its own [weapon speed](Skyrim_Archery.md#Detailed_Bow_Comparison). So while the Zephyr may fire faster than a standard Dwarven Bow, it has the same speed as a Long Bow, and is 100% faster than a Daedric Bow. Additionally, the increase in speed is from 0.75 to 1.0, which is 33.33% faster, as opposed to the listed 30%.
- Zephyr is a [permanent item](Skyrim_Permanent_Items.md), and will always remain even if placed in a respawning container.
- Zephyr cannot be disenchanted.
- [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.
- Zephyr also appeared as [a card](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Zephyr) in *[Legends Asia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends_Asia)* and as [a weapon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Zephyr) in *[Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Castles)*.

- Activating a weapon rack with Zephyr equipped may cause it to disappear, forcing you to reload a previous save to recover it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.

The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.0, fixes this bug.
