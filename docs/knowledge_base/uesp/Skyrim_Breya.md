[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-npc-Breya.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Breya.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Breya.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Breya.jpg) Breya's ghost **Breya** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) in [Avanchnzel](Skyrim_Avanchnzel.md). She appears to be generated when given the quest [Unfathomable Depths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths) as her Ref ID will change.

Breya's corpse can be found near the pedestal in the final chamber of Avanchnzel, next to a dead [centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md). On her corpse is a set of [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md) with matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md), an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Unfathomable Depths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths)**: Uncover the [memories](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lexicon) of the Dwemer.

## Quest-Related Events

### Unfathomable Depths
As you first enter Avanchnzel you will see the ghost of Breya speaking to the other ghosts:

- **Drennen**: *"I don't like this place. It feels like we're being... watched."*
- **Breya**: *"It's a simple job, Drennen. We get in, we steal the Lexicon, we leave. Don't get jumpy."*
- **Fathoms**: *"If you two cannot handle this, we can find others to hire."*
- **Roots**: *"Enough. Their services will be more than adequate. Let us continue."*

Just before the doors to Avanchnzel Animoncultory:

- **Breya**: *"Why are all these metal... things... ignoring us?"*
- **Roots**: *"Anavchnzel * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * is waiting."*
- **Breya**: *"Waiting for what?"*
- **Roots**: *"No one seems to know. Perhaps the return of the Dwemer. Perhaps the ending of the world."*
- **Breya**: *"Best get rid of the ones we see. Just in case."*

In the first chamber of the Animoncultory, you will yet again encounter the ghosts, this time trying to break into one of the many hidden chambers of Avanchnzel. At this point, it is clear the place is starting to get to them:

- **Breya**: *"I told you we should have hired a thief."*
- **Fathoms**: *"We are thieves, you fool. We're stealing the cube."*
- **Breya**: *"And yet none of us can pick a damned lock."*

In Avanchnzel Animoncultory at the campsite:

- **Drennen**: *"This place is huge."*
- **Roots**: *"It is large, indeed. I did not anticipate Avanchnzel's size or the time needed to uncover the entrance. A few hours sleep and we should be ready to continue through."*
- **Drennen**: *"Maybe we should turn back. I don't want to sleep here."*
- **Roots**: *"Drennen, you do understand that the Lexicon at the bottom of this place holds the accumulated memories of centuries of Dwemer."*
- **Breya**: *"Not to mention our pay."*
- **Drennen**: *"So?"*
- **Fathoms**: *"So we're not turning back, you fool."*
- **Breya**: *"Besides, you're not afraid of a few sleeping metal men. Are you, Drennen?"*

Further in the Animoncultory in a storage room:

- **Breya**: *"I thought you said these things were "sleeping"?"*
- **Roots**: *"You said they were sleeping. I said they were waiting. It seems a few of them were "waiting" for someone to try and take the Lexicon."*
- **Breya**: *"Great."*

Just before the doors to the Avanchnzel Boilery:

- **Drennen**: *"I can't do this. We shouldn't be here. I'm sorry."*
- **Breya**: *"Drennen!"*
- **Fathoms**: *"Let the field mouse run. Your pay will be double."*
- **Breya**: *"My pay will be nothing if we die in here!"*
- **Roots**: *"Calm yourself, Breya. We are closing in on the Lexicon. I can feel it calling. We'll continue without Drennen."*

As you enter the Boilery:

- **Roots**: *"We are close now. Can you feel the Lexicon calling out?"*
- **Fathoms**: *"Sure."*
- **Breya**: *"So we get it and get out, right?"*
- **Roots**: *"Once we have the Lexicon, we'll need to take it to its podium. I know what to do from there. Soon the Lexicon's knowledge will be mine."*
- **Fathoms**: *"Ours. Soon the Lexicon's knowledge will be ours."*
- **Roots**: *"Of course."*

Just before the trapped room where you find Watches-The-Roots' body:

- **Roots**: *"We're almost there. I can hear the Lexicon. Come on, quickly!"*
- **Breya**: *"Something doesn't feel right. Wait!"*
- **Roots**: *"Aaaaaaaaaaa...."*

## Notes
- There are 13 different dialogue scenes set up for the quest Unfathomable Depths, but not all of them contain dialogue. The first scene **MS04Vision01Scene**, appears to have been a conversation between Fathoms and Breya, but its contents are unknown. Scene 7 **MS04Vision07Scene** also has a single entry for Breya alone, but no accompanying dialogue.
- Unused scripting in the quest would've given every member of the party a full set of Dwarven armor, although this never happens, and it's unknown what purpose this would've served.

- Due to a spelling error in the game code, the final scene near the Centurion will never play as intended. It was meant to describe the death of Breya and how Fathoms escaped:

- **Fathoms**: *"I have the Lexicon! Where's the podium?"*
- **Breya**: *"I can't hold these things! We need to leave!"*
- **Fathoms**: *"I did not come all the way here just to walk out empty-handed."*
- **Breya**: *"The way out is right there! Run, damn it!"*

- This scene was originally fixed by version 1.3.3. of the Unofficial Skyrim Patch, however, this was reverted by version 2.0.0., due to the changes actually breaking the scene further.
