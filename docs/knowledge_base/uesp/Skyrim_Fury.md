*Creatures and people up to level 6 will attack anything nearby for 30 seconds.* **Fury** is a novice level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes a creature or person up to level 6 to attack anyone in sight for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Fury](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md), 6 pts for 30 secs
- [Courage Secondary](Skyrim_Rally_(effect).md), 6 pts for 30 secs

## Perks
- [Illusion Dual Casting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Dual_Casting), multiplies effective level by 2.2 (rounded down).
- [Animage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animage), animals 8 levels higher can be affected.
- [Kindred Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kindred_Mage), people 10 levels higher can be affected.
- [Rage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rage), all targets 12 levels higher can be affected
- [Master of the Mind](Skyrim_Master_of_the_Mind.md), allows spell to work on undead, daedra, and automata.

Note that most of these perks will stack, so the overall effective values for the spell with perks are as follows:

| | Max Lvl | |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Single-cast | Dual-cast | |
| Default | 6 | 13 |
| Animage | 14 | 30 |
| Kindred Mage | 16 | 35 |
| Rage | 18 | 39 |
| Animage + Rage | 26 | 57 |
| Kindred Mage + Rage | 28 | 61 |

*Master of the Mind* will *not* stack with *Kindred Mage* or *Animage*, which is primarily of interest when attempting to cast spells on [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md), when it will come up that Kindred Mage does not apply, even though vampires have black souls. [Death Hounds](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md) are not affected by *Animage*, but they are not animals to begin with; more generally, the perks will not combine when fighting people or animals raised by necromancers.

## Notes
- Unlike previous games, casting this spell on a non-hostile target in public is considered an assault, so you can't use it to easily murder someone in front of witnesses. However, if you are hidden when you cast the spell and nobody sees or hears you, you can still get away with it.
