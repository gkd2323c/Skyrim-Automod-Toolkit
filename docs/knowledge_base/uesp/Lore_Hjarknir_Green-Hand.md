[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Hjarknir Green-Hand was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) who lived in the second century of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). He became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying an entire grove of [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md) and presenting their [Taproot](Lore_Taproot.md) hearts as a battle trophy. He was chieftain for twenty-seven years, the longest known chieftainship in Thirsk's history, surpassing [Beinir White-Beard](Lore_Beinir_White-Beard.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
