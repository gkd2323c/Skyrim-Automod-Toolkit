Viti's Notes: Song of Binding **by [High Cantor Viti](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#High_Cantor_Viti)** *A translation of the Song of Binding* *
---
Moon-Singer Talbira speaks through my sahida, Cantor Krin'ze. She has taught her a song to bind a Rajaska, a powerful [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) made from the souls of [fallen singers](Lore_Moon-Singers.md).

The words of her song of binding Rajaska are thus:

Morna rithantii
seribani ah'vero masmandi
jah'zhet [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) traji venya:
besha, besha, besha.

The [dialect](Lore_Ta%27agra.md) is archaic, but I will translate the holy words to [Cyrodiilic](Lore_Tamrielic.md) as best as I can:

Dark singer,
shackled by one thousand (countless?) sorrows,
brilliant/mother (?) Azurah compels you:
forgive, forgive, forgive.
