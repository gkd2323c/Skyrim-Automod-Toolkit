- *Teleports <pet name>.*

**Teleport Pet: *<Pet Name>*** are novice level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [spells](Skyrim_Alteration_Spells.md) that teleport their associated pet to your location. Their individual names vary according to the pet they summon.

## Effects
- [Teleport Pet](Skyrim_Teleport_Pet_(effect).md) - As noted on the page for the magic effect, each Teleport Pet spell uses its own variation of the Teleport Pet effect. They all work identically, however.

## Notes
- These spells are free to cast and do not raise your Alteration skill.

## Bugs
- In Skyrim [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), Teleport Pet: Dwarven Mudcrab will not work due to the removal of the [My Pet Mudcrab](Skyrim_My_Pet_Mudcrab.md) quest, which needs to be completed to mark the mudcrab as owned by the player. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.629.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Forcing the quest as complete using the `Completequest cc BGSSSE010_Pet Purchase Quest` command will fix this problem.
