The Mudcrab (or **Mud Crab**) is a common variety of [crab](Lore_Crab.md) capable of camouflaging itself as a small rock. They can be found along every shore and waterway of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and are also a common sight underground.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> They are not typically aggressive unless cornered or provoked. However, if a mudcrab ever gets a taste of meat it becomes a hunter, and may be a threat to livestock.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Neminda-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Mudcrabs are usually not dangerous unless they swarm, as they rely solely on their pincers and burrowing abilities in combat.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Mudcrabs are capable of stripping a corpse down to its bones in days, which usually takes weeks for lesser crabs.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Some mudcrabs can grow to enormous sizes, and rare albino variants are known to exist.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TPo T-6)</sup>

Although they are inquisitive by nature and can be kept as pets,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESORufous-7)</sup> most citizens of Tamriel regard mudcrabs as disgusting vermin.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> Mudcrabs have been characterized by certain [fish](Lore_Fish.md) enthusiasts as unhappy creatures, "dour and sunless like the soil in which they sleep".<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Mudcrabs are prized for their sweet crab meat and legs, and their chitin is sometimes used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWMeat-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SRChitin-10)</sup>

The Ciphers of the Eye made several observations of mudcrabs kept in the Feral Gallery in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). Due to their nature as social creatures, the Ciphers kept many mudcrabs together, and noted strange behaviors in the group in captivity. They noted a "strange form of dance display wherein they gather in a circle and sway rhythmically to either side". The observed mudcrabs would practice this dance several times a day. Their dancing would reduce observers to "incomprehensible raving messes".<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

There have been cases where mudcrabs have shown humanoid levels of intelligence and the ability to speak,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> and they were rumored to be in control of the town of [Pelagiad](Lore_Pelagiad.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

## Variants
Many different varieties of mudcrab exist; two known species are the [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) Mudcrab<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> and the Rufous Mudcrab.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESORufous-7)</sup> [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) is home to a rare species of red or pink mudcrab that has slightly larger pincers and sweeter flesh than the gray northern mudcrab breeds. This species was considered protected by [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Kyne C-16)</sup><sup>:100-111</sup> Mudcrabs also have larger and more dangerous cousins, namely king mudcrabs and mudcrab titans, which can pose a danger to travelers.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

### Dwarven Armored Mudcrab
Dwarven Armored Mudcrabs are a variant of Mudcrabs which have been specially outfitted with and fused to [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) metals. Only one is confirmed to have existed, having been found deep in Dwarven ruins in [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), which suggests that the Mudcrab had survived for some years within the ruins.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Dw Ar Mu-18)</sup>

### Mudcrab of Eternal Doom
Mudcrabs of Eternal Doom are scuttling horrors which superficially resemble the mudcrabs of Tamriel, but which seemingly thrive in the scorching heat and relentless desolation of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup> They have an embering, rocky shell, and are sometimes summoned to Tamriel.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup> It is unknown whether or not these crabs are [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).

### Spectral Mud Crab
Spectral Mud Crabs (or Spectral Mudcrabs) are ghostly mudcrabs. It is unknown if they are truly [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md), as they are said be very gentle compared to most hauntings.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup> They are known to hoard enchanted items. In addition to being found on Tamriel, Spectral Mudcrabs were once present in [Gaiar Alata](Lore_Gaiar_Alata.md), a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) that was destroyed in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>

### Three-Clawed Mudcrab
The Three-Clawed Mudcrab is a rare species of mudcrab found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

### Wasten Coraldale Mudcrab
The Wasten Coraldale Mudcrab is an iridescent, blue species of mudcrab found on the island of [Wasten Coraldale](Lore_Wasten_Coraldale.md). They regard everything as a threat (even other mudcrabs), and were once considered a delicacy by the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup>
