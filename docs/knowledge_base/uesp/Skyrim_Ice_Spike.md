- *A spike of ice that does 25 points of frost damage to Health and Stamina.*

**Ice Spike** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to health and stamina, and slows targets' movement for 3 seconds.

## Effects
- [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 25 pts
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md), 50 pts for 3 secs
- [Deep Freeze Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 3 secs; if the [Deep Freeze](Skyrim_Deep_Freeze.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Augmented_Frost.md), raises damage to 31.25 at first rank or 37.5 at second rank

## Notes
- Like most Frost spells, it can be used to set off [Frost Runes](Skyrim_Frost_Rune.md) from a safe distance.
- Ice Spikes which hit enemies will continue to exist after they die until you leave the area. This makes the corpses easy to spot from a distance for looting.
