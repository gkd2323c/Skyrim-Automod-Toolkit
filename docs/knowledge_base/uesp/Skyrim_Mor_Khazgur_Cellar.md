## Mor Khazgur Cellar
Upon entering via the novice-locked trapdoor, you look down a sloping passage that appears as a natural cave rather than a normal cellar. On the left-hand side of this passage are three [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) mushrooms. The passage opens into a room with a central pillar. To the left is a wicker fence enclosing a mushroom patch of two [blisterworts](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), four [bleeding crowns](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md), two [Namira's rot](Skyrim_Namira%27s_Rot.md), and two more fly amanitas. Next is an iron drying rack from which hang two braids of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) and one [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md). To the right of this are three food barrels and three covered baskets. To the right again is another iron drying rack from which hang a dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and another dried elves ear. Next is an adept-locked jail door to a small room with a single shelving unit. The top shelf holds a [potion of the healer](Skyrim_Potion_of_the_Healer.md), a [potion of minor magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), and a random skill potion. The second shelf has a [minor healing potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md), a leveled [strength potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Strength.md), and a random leveled skill potion. To the right of the jail door are some [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) and a chest containing some minor loot. Next on the east wall is another shelving unit with three bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) on the top shelf and three bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) on the bottom.
