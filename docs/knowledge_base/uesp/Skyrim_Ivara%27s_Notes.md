Ivara's Notes **by [Ivara of Olenveld](Skyrim_Ivara_of_Olenveld.md)** *Journal of a necromancer hoping to gain power from the Ideal Masters*
---
The journey from [Olenveld](Lore_Olenveld.md) was long but worth the trip. We'll have a far easier time finding what we need to open the portal to the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

The [pass](Skyrim_Brittleshin_Pass.md) is remote and yet there are no shortage of travelers looking for a shortcut across the mountains. As such, we'll have plenty of blood and [bonemeal](Skyrim_Bonemeal.md) at our disposal, but we'll need [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and [soul gem fragments](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment) to complete the ritual.

[Lushak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lushak_gra-Ragdam) suggested we contact the local smugglers for help with the latter. She'd been in contact with one about the [Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_Staff_of_Hasedoki_(item).md). If the wizard truly bonded his soul to the [stave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff) as the legends suggest, releasing him might provide a worthy offering to the [Ideal Masters](Lore_Ideal_Masters.md).

In any case, adding the ingredients to the list should be an easy task.
