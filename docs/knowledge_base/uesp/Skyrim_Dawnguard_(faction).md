# Dawnguard (faction)


[![SR-banner-Dawnguard.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-banner-Dawnguard.png/100px-SR-banner-Dawnguard.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-banner-Dawnguard.png) The **Dawnguard** is an order of [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunters living and operating from [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md). You may join them to fight against [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) and his plot to blot out the sun. The Dawnguard was re-formed by [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran) in order to combat the growing vampire menace in Skyrim, and initially only consists of him, [Celann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Celann), and [Durak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Durak) when you arrive. As time passes (regardless of which faction you join), more people join the Dawnguard. The Dawnguard also keeps two armored huskies as war dogs, three armored trolls (tamed by [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar)), and partially shelters several refugees who fled there for relief from the vampire scourge. However, the latter are not permitted to enter the fort itself.

## Fort Dawnguard
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg) Fort Dawnguard The Dawnguard's base of operations is the ancient Fort Dawnguard in [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md) in the extreme southeast of Skyrim. Built in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by the Jarl of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), the castle served to contain the Jarl's son after he became a vampire. To prevent his son from leaving the castle, the Jarl hired a group of mercenaries known as the Dawnguard, who extended their purpose to root out any vampiric presence in Skyrim. However, the group eventually had to kill their charge, forcing the Jarl to disband them and leave the fort abandoned. At some point in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), Isran, a former [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md), rediscovered and repaired the fort and reformed the ancient order.

## Membership

### Joining
To join the Dawnguard, you must speak with [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran) at Fort Dawnguard. You can learn how to reach the fort by hearing about the vampire hunters as part of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md) quest. Once there, Isran will ask you to investigate [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md) in the next quest, [Awakening](Skyrim_Awakening.md).

After completing Awakening, you will become a member of the Dawnguard in the quest [Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md) by rejecting [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)'s offer to become a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).

### Benefits
These benefits should be compared to those offered by the [Volkihar](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md).

- Master-level trainers in [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) ([Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard)), [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) (Isran), [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) ([Florentius Baenius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius)), and [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) ([Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar)).
- Access to an [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant) (Florentius Baenus), [blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant) (Gunmar), and [general goods merchant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant) (Sorine Jurard).
- Access to an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md) and [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md).
- Access to [Gunmar's Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forges#Gunmar.27s_Forge), where more powerful [crossbows](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) and [bolts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolt) can be forged.
- Can take on the [Ancient Technology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Technology) quests, which are the only way to gain access to Enhanced and Dwarven crossbows and Exploding bolts.
- Can acquire the powerful [Dawnguard Rune Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Axe), [Hammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Hammer), and [Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dawnguard_Rune_Shield).
- Only way to purchase Restoration spells with the rare [sun damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Damage) effect, aside from using an alternate method; see [Banishment](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md#Banishment).
- Multiple possible [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) as well as [Bran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bran) and [Sceolang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sceolang), two [huskies](Skyrim_Husky.md).
- [Celann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Celann), [Durak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Durak), and [Ingjard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ingjard) make up the majority of followers without a [level cap](Skyrim_Followers.md#Follower_Stats).
- Able to hire [armored trolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armored_Troll) for 500 gold from [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar).
- [Random encounters](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) where vampires attack the player or settlements are less likely to occur after completing the Dawnguard questline.
- Endless supply of [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quests.

### Suspension
If you have [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) but rejected Harkon's offer, members of the Dawnguard will not offer their services to you and will have very limited dialogue until you are cured. Most members of the Dawnguard will suggest you [speak with Falion](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), but contracting [lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) is another option.

### Banishment
If you become a member of the [Volkihar Vampire Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) by accepting Harkon's offer, you will be labeled an enemy of the Dawnguard. You will not be able to use any of the Dawnguard's services and will be attacked on sight by members even if you choose to cure your vampirism. This can be worked around by casting high level [Calm](Skyrim_Calm.md) spells if you still wish to access the Dawnguard's merchants and trainers. This can be remedied somewhat by [destroying the Dawnguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Destroying_the_Dawnguard).

## Quests
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-qico-Dawnguard_Dawnguard.png/200px-SR-qico-Dawnguard_Dawnguard.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-qico-Dawnguard_Dawnguard.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-qico-Dawnguard_Dawnguard.png) Dawnguard knotwork as it appears in the [Journal](Skyrim_Quests.md)
### Primary Quests
The primary Dawnguard questline consists of twelve required quests and one optional quest. Three [achievements](Skyrim_Achievements.md) (80 points; 2 Bronze and 1 Silver) are unlocked by Dawnguard quests.

- **[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md)**: Speak with the leader of the Dawnguard.
- **[Awakening](Skyrim_Awakening.md)**: Find out what the [vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) are looking for. ([![Awakening](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-achievement-Awakening.png/20px-SR-achievement-Awakening.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Awakening.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
- **[Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md)**: Choose your side!
- **[A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md)**: Recruit [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran)'s old allies for the Dawnguard. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md)**: Find and rescue [a Moth Priest](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) from the [vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md). - **[Bolstering the Ranks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolstering_the_Ranks)**: Find [Florentius Baenius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius) and recruit him into the Dawnguard.
- **[Seeking Disclosure](Skyrim_Seeking_Disclosure.md)**: Bring the Elder Scrolls to [Dexion](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md).
- **[Chasing Echoes](Skyrim_Chasing_Echoes.md)**: Explore [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md).
- **[Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md)**: Search for [Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica) in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). ([![Beyond Death](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png/20px-SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
- **[Scroll Scouting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_Scouting)**: Obtain information on the Dragon Elder Scroll.
- **[Unseen Visions](Skyrim_Unseen_Visions.md)**: Perform a ceremony to read the Elder Scroll.
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).
- **[Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md)**: Confront [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md). ([![Kindred Judgement](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png/20px-SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png) 40 pts/Silver)

### [Radiant Quests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Radiant_Quests)
*These quests are available after completing [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) and are repeatable:*

- **Given by [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar):** - **[Cleansing Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_Light)**: Kill the boss of a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) lair.
- **[Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md)**: Kill a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) masquerading as a citizen.
- **[Hunting the Monster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_the_Monster)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) target.
- **[Preemptive Strike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Preemptive_Strike)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) before it turns its allies.
- **Given by [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran):** - **[A Jarl's Justice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Jarl%27s_Justice)**: Kill a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) masquerading as a [Visiting Advisor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Visiting_Advisor).
- **Given by [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard):** - **[Ancient Technology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Technology)**: Retrieve Dwemer schematics to improve technology for the Dawnguard. *(This quest can only be done six times, after which you can buy or create [Enhanced Dwarven Crossbows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enhanced_Dwarven_Crossbow))*

*These quests are available after completing [Bolstering the Ranks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolstering_the_Ranks) and are repeatable:*

- **Given by [Florentius Baenius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius):** - **[Lost Relic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Relic)**: Retrieve a relic of the original Dawnguard. *(This quest can only be done three times, after which all three Dawnguard [artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) will have been found)*
- **[Rescue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue)**: Rescue a victim from [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md).

### Other Quests
- **[Destroying the Dawnguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Destroying_the_Dawnguard)**: Kill the Dawnguard leaders.
