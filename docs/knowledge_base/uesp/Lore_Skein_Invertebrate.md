**Skein invertebrates** are [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) invertebrates that inhabit the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md). They were created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) as "improved" versions of [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) invertebrates.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Wpet-1)</sup> Uncommonly for Daedric creatures, they are capable of conventional reproduction.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skein Wasp-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skein Spider Hatch-3)</sup>

**Skein Spiders** are imitations of [Nirn's](Lore_Nirn.md) [spiders](Lore_Spider.md). They are the most common denizen of the Spiral Skein and can be fiercely loyal pets,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSkein_Spider-4)</sup> often being summoned by [Spiderkith](Lore_Spiderkith.md). Bookbinder Spiders found in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) share a similar appearance. **Skein Beetles** (also known as Skein [Shalks](Lore_Shalk.md)) are imitations of Nirn's [beetles](Lore_Beetle.md). They are said to use their pointed proboscis to drain a victim of their blood.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSkein_Beetle-5)</sup> **Skein Scorpions** are imitations of Nirn's [giant scorpions](Lore_Giant_Scorpion.md). They are the preferred companion of Deathweavers, the [Knights of the Spider Cult](Lore_Knights_of_the_Spider_Cult.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONSkein_Scorpion-6)</sup> **Skein Wasps** are imitations of Nirn's [giant wasps](Lore_Giant_Wasp.md). They are deemed to be some of the finest of Mephala's creations, in terms of the fear they instill.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Wpet-1)</sup>
