Harvest Dwarven Centurions are a powerful type of [Dwarven Centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md#Dwarven_Centurion) added by the [Forgotten Seasons](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md) creation. Three of them patrol the paths and fields of [Autumn's Bells](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Autumn.27s_Bells) within [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns). In addition to the standard loot of other Centurions (minus the arrows), they also drop 10-15 units of [Wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) after being killed.

They are extremely dangerous, armed with both steam breath and dual-axe arms that will quickly deplete your health and melee strikes that can one-hit kill a player with a low health stat. Their power is comparable to the strongest variants of Dwarven Centurion found in other [Dwarven ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md). Their attacks are most similar to [Dwarven Centurion Masters](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md#Dwarven_Centurion_Master). Strong followers and/or a high Sneak skill are good strategies to prepare in advance; also bring healing items to combat the steam blasts. Harvest Dwarven Centurions tend to focus on one target at a time during melee, which will help you score more hits while taking less damage if your follower engages the Centurion at close range.

Although the three centurions are spaced far apart (one patrolling the western third of the room, another the central, and the final in the eastern third), you may be detected by them as you gather the multicolored wheat for the [main quest](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons_(quest).md) of the creation. You will also have to fight many [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md#Dwarven_Sphere) and [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md#Dwarven_Spider) of varying levels, and the commotion of combat with these weaker automatons may catch the Harvest Centurions' attention that way.
