War of the First Council **by [Agrippa Fundilius](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Agrippa_Fundilius)** *A brief account of a First Age religious conflict* *
---
This account by the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) scholar Agrippa Fundilius is based on various Imperial and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) sources, and written for Western readers.

![T](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/T_letter.png/44px-T_letter.png)
he [War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md) was a [First Age](Lore_First_Era.md) religious conflict between the secular [Dunmer Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) and [Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md) and the orthodox Dunmer Houses [Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md), [Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md), [Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md), [Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md), and [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md). The First Council was the first pan-Dunmer governing body, which collapsed over disputes about sorceries and enchantments practiced by the Dwemer and declared profane by the other Houses.

The Secular Houses, less numerous, but politically and magically more advanced, and aided by [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) and [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) clans drawn by promise of land and booty, initially campaigned with great success in the north of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and occupied much of the land now comprising Redoran, [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and Telvanni District. The Orthodox Houses, widely dispersed and poorly organized, suffered defeat after defeat until [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) was made general of all House troops and levies.

Nerevar secured the aid of nomad barbarian tribesmen, and contrived to force a major battle at the Secular stronghold of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) on Vvardenfell. The Secular forces were outmaneuvered and defeated with the help of [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) scouts, and the survivors forced to take refuge in the Dwemer stronghold at Red Mountain.

After a brief siege, treason permitted Nerevar and his troops to enter the stronghold, where the Secular leaders were slain, and Nerevar mortally wounded. General slaughter followed, and Houses Dwemer and Dagoth were exterminated. Nerevar died shortly thereafter of his wounds.

Three of Nerevar's associates among the Orthodox Houses, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md), [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), succeeded to control of the re-created First Council, re-named the Grand Council of Morrowind, and went on to be come the god-kings and immortal rulers of Morrowind known as the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), or Almsivi.
