[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-place-Ephesus.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ephesus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ephesus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ephesus.jpg) Ephesus circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-map-Ephesus.jpg/200px-ON-map-Ephesus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ephesus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ephesus.jpg) A map of Ephesus Ephesus is a region in southeastern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), within the [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) region, to the south of the city of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md). It borders the [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth.md) region to the north and the [Fallen Wastes](Lore_Fallen_Wastes.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) to the south. The region contains the Pelin Graveyard and the famous [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Pass.md), on the border with Hammerfell. Apart from the Garrison, access to Hammerfell is also possible through the lesser-known Sunken Road. Ephesus is also home to the ruins of Arlimahera's Grip.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ephesus-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ED-2)</sup> It was known for is freeborn militia.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSOHR-3)</sup>
