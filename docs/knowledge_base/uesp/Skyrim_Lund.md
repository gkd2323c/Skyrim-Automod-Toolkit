[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Lund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lund.jpg) Lund's corpse **Lund** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in a [hut](Skyrim_Lund%27s_Hut.md) on top of the hill northwest of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). He died of unknown causes. His corpse is still lying in his bed and the house is overrun by [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md).

Lund wears randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variations) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variations), with a 50% chance for a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) (3 variations). Additionally there is a slim chance that he may carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.

## Notes
- There are three plates with meat on the floor, implying the skeevers may have been kept as pets.
- A bottle of poison sits on the table opposite the bed, and a silver ring is atop the grave outside his house. This might indicate that Lund killed himself over the loss of his wife.
- Plates of food for the skeevers and poison may alternatively indicate he tried to introduce some pest control (and accidentally poisoned himself in the process).
- Sometimes Lund won't be wearing his clothing when you first enter but he still has it in his inventory.
