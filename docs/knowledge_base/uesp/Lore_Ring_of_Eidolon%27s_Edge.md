# Ring of Eidolon's Edge

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/OB-item-Ring_of_Eidolon%27s_Edge.jpg/200px-OB-item-Ring_of_Eidolon%27s_Edge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Ring_of_Eidolon%27s_Edge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Ring_of_Eidolon%27s_Edge.jpg) The Ring of Eidolon's Edge The Ring of Eidolon's Edge is a minor [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md). It is an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) ring made in the Daedric style, likely enchanted with the soul of a lesser [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). The ring magically increases the wearer's proficiency at using blades and shields.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROEE-2)</sup> The identity of Eidolon and the origins of the ring are unknown, although a Daedric ruin named Eidolon's Hollow exists in the [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) region of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The ring came into the hands of a merchant in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Rosentia Gallenus, a young [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) woman with a passion for collecting unusual arcane craftwork, purchased the ring for a lover, although it returned to her possession when he left her for someone else. Rosentia later married a wealthy man in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md), and was left with his fortune and large manor when he died. She used her wealth to expand her collection, and in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) she came into contact with the [Staff of the Everscamp](Lore_Staff_of_the_Everscamp.md). In return for helping her to dispose of the cursed Staff, she gave the Ring of Eidolon's Edge to the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROEE-2)</sup>

## Notes
- An [eidolon](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eidolon) is the spirit-image of a living or dead person in ancient Greek literature.
