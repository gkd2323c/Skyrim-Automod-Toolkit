*I am endlessly fascinated by this gigantic, murderous frog. And my research has only just begun! I can only imagine what more I might learn about the death hopper. But first, I must get closer!*
— *[Terror of the Death Hopper](Lore_Terror_of_the_Death_Hopper.md)* by Remius Volsonus<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTDH-1)</sup> Death Hoppers are giant, carnivorous [frogs](Lore_Frog.md) that can be found in the swamps of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) and the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DH-2)</sup> They are typically green, but can also be brown. Death hoppers are generally found in packs. They hunt by lurking beneath the surface of water, waiting for their prey to wander above them. They can swallow large prey whole.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTDH-1)</sup> Death hoppers are extremely poisonous, and can spit globs of toxic matter over great distances.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTDH-1)</sup> They are known to spawn in the foul waters of the West Weald: their tadpoles are referred to as nymphs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOFish-3)</sup>

Plague Hoppers are a [diseased](Lore_Disease.md) brown variety of Death Hoppers which are born in the [Pits](Lore_Pits.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NAT-4)</sup> They could also be found in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) as a result of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md)'s infiltration of the realm.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Plague Hopper-5)</sup> A tale about the Viridescent [Dragon Frog](Lore_Dragon_Frog.md) says that the green frogs once grew as large as [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), somewhat similar to Death Hoppers, but envied the Dragons their wings. Upon making a pact with [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md), they gained their wings but lost their size, becoming dragon frogs.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VDF-6)</sup>

The [Sul-Xan](Lore_Argonian.md#Tribes) tribe is known to use death hopper vocal sacs when making armor.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
