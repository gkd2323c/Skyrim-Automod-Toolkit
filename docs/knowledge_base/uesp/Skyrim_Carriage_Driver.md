# Carriage Driver

**Carriage Drivers** only appear during the quest [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md); one of them acts as your means of transportation from [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) to the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md), where the other two drivers will be parked in the courtyard. All three drivers are [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warriors](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and will disappear from the game upon completion of the quest.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-npc-Carriage_Driver.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Carriage_Driver.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Carriage_Driver.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Carriage_Driver.jpg) Carriage Driver This driver appears just outside [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), specifically at [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md), and will transport you to the party at the Thalmor Embassy after you have spoken to [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md). Before departure, the driver will say, *"The lady's already paid the fee. We leave when she gives the word."* When parked in the embassy courtyard, he will say, *"I'm paid to wait to take you home. Enjoy yourself."*

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) with [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd) and a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c1) and wields an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md).

## Bugs
- According to the game files, the two drivers situated in the embassy courtyard were supposed to share the same two lines of dialogue. Due to a programming error, all dialogue options were attached to the driver near the gate, leaving the other one with nothing but generic greetings. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
