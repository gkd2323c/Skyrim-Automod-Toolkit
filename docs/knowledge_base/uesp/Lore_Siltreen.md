[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-map-Siltreen.jpg/200px-ON-map-Siltreen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Siltreen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Siltreen.jpg) Map of Siltreen circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). **Siltreen** is a region that consists of the eastern portion of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md). Siltreen is the closest to the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) and is the home of several plantations and other settlements. The [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) farming settlement of [Silent Mire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silent_Mire) can be found along the coast. The [Shrine of Saint Veloth](Lore_Shrine_of_Saint_Veloth.md) is the burial grounds of the prophet, [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md), who guided the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) to Morrowind. Tal'Deic Fortress is a [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) stronghold that was once occupied by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). South of Tal'Deic is [Darkshade Caverns](Lore_Darkshade_Caverns.md), one of the most successful kwama mines in Morrowind. Siltreen is bordered by [Lagomere](Lore_Lagomere.md) to the west and the [Padomaic Crest](Lore_Padomaic_Crest.md) to the northeast.
