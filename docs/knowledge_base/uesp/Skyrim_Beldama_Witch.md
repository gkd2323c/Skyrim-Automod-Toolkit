They wear [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). One of them carries *[Beldama Witch's Note](Skyrim_Beldama_Witch%27s_Note.md)*. Their appearance may differ more than two types shown in the picture.
