Peakstar was an [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) woman who washed ashore near [Ald Redaynia](Lore_Ald_Redaynia.md) as a baby. She was found and raised by the [Urshilaku Tribe](Lore_Urshilaku_Tribe.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWAshlanders-1)</sup> and became a figure of legend among the Ashlanders around [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397). Many believed this mysterious "girl child" was the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NFH-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWSharn-3)</sup> Though she survived [The Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md), she did not master the arts of war, and died while fighting an [Ash Vampire](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md). She became the last known [failed Incarnate](Lore_Failed_Incarnates.md), and her spirit went to the Cavern of the Incarnate with the others.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWPeakstar-4)</sup> The [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) searched for her unsuccessfully for many years, and finally, shortly before the fulfillment of the [Nerevarine Prophecies](Lore_Nerevarine_Prophecies.md) around [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), they announced she was dead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWSharn-3)</sup> Ashlanders were under the belief that she had been found and captured at some point.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWAshlanders-1)</sup> Rumors persisted that she was still alive, as the Temple could not produce a body.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWSharn-3)</sup> Her spirit helped guide the Nerevarine during the latter's campaign against [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md).
