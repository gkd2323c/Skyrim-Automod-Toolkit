# Spirit

**Spirit** is a term that may refer multiple types of beings:

- [Ghost](Lore_Ghost.md), disembodied souls of formerly living beings
- [Nature Spirit](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md), embodiments of nature itself
- [Gods](Lore_Gods.md), various greater beings including the et'Ada, "original spirits"
