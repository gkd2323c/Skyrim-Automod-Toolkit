He is lying on top of a tree stump at a bandit camp just off the road to the southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).

He wears a set of farmer [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and [bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md). There is a slim chance that he carries a small amount of gold and/or a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Wood_Cutter_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wood_Cutter_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wood_Cutter_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wood_Cutter_02.jpg) Wood Cutter **Wood Cutter** is the corpse of a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md). He is located in the wilderness to the northwest of [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md), under the small cliff.

He wears a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) and randomly determined, leveled footwear; at all levels, there is always a 50% chance he will wear a pair of [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md). He always carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a few random potions, and has a slim chance of carrying a bit of gold and/or a single [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).

Additionally he also knows a variety of spells, even if he never gets to use them—specifically [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), and the three [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md), and [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md). He also has a constant effect of Recover Magicka.

## Notes
- A few feet away from the second wood cutter's body, the unique axe [The Woodsman's Friend](Skyrim_The_Woodsman%27s_Friend.md) can be found in the forest floor. It's possible to find [The Woodsman's Friend](Skyrim_The_Woodsman%27s_Friend.md) with the wood cutter's body nowhere around.
- Both the the corpse of the second wood cutter, as well as his axe bear internal names referring to a location known as "Pinewood Grove". Pinewood Grove is not an actual location, although it might have once been intended to be a minor dungeon.
- It's possible that the first wood cutter was created with [Bassianus Axius](Skyrim_Bassianus_Axius.md) as a base, as they share the same voice type and it would explain his otherwise random faction inclusions.

## Bugs
- Due to the fact that the first wood cutter is part of the [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, addresses this issue. He has been removed from the crime faction.
