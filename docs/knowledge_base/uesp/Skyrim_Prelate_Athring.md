[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-npc-Prelate_Athring.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Prelate_Athring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Prelate_Athring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Prelate_Athring.jpg) Prelate Athring **Prelate Athring** is a [Snow Elf](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md) [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) who can be found next to the Wayshrine of Sight in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).

He wears [monk robes](Skyrim_Monk_Robes.md#xx01570c) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#xx01570b). He carries an [elven dagger](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Touching the Sky
He will greet you with:

- *"Welcome, Initiate. This is the Wayshrine of Sight. Are you prepared to honor the mantras of Auri-El and fill your vessel with His enlightenment?"*
- **Yes.**
- *"Then behold Auri-El's gift, my child. May it speed your journey to the Inner Sanctum."*

He will occasionally bless you with:

- *"May Auri-El's light guide you in your darkest hours."*

## Notes
- In the game files, he is part of a newly created race known as `Snow Elf Race`. It is largely based off of the [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) race, and even bears the name "High Elf", but for all purposes Prelate Athring can be considered to be a Snow Elf.
