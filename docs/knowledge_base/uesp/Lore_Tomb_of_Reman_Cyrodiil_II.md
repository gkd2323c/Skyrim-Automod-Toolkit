Tomb of Reman Cyrodiil II *The sarcophagus inscription of Reman II*
---
Here lies [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), crowned [Emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the year [2812](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2812). He fell in battle against the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md), in the fifty-seventh year of his age, after a reign of thirty-nine years and eight months wanting a day.
