[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/ON-place-Feywatch_Isle_Volcanic_Vent.jpg/200px-ON-place-Feywatch_Isle_Volcanic_Vent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Feywatch_Isle_Volcanic_Vent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Feywatch_Isle_Volcanic_Vent.jpg) Feywatch Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Feywatch Isle is a lake island situated on the northeastern coast of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) within a lake with an unknown name. It harbored a volcanic vent. It is positioned to the north of [Tor Draioch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tor_Draioch) and to the south of the Duford Shipyards.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FIVV-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the tranquility of the archipelago was disrupted as not only Feywatch Isle's volcanic vent but several others across the archipelago erupted. The island's normally placid surroundings were thrown into chaos as a volcanic vent on Feywatch Isle became increasingly active. This unexpected eruption was attributed to a mysterious disturbance in the local environment. It was one of many vents in the region that were calmed by the [Druids](Lore_Druids.md) of the [Stonelore Circle](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md) and [their associates](Lore_The_Vestige.md) during that period.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DP-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VTT-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DO-4)</sup>

At that time a [skyshard](Lore_Skyshard.md), a shard of [meteoric glass](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md) imbued with mystical energy, was positioned between Feywatch Isle and the towering structure of Tor Draioch, overlooking the isle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HISH-5)</sup>
