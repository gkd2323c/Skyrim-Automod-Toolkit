# Hawk

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-creature-Hawk.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Hawk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Hawk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Hawk.jpg) A hawk Hawks are [birds](Lore_Bird.md) found throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are hunted for their [beaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hawk_Beak), [feathers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hawk_Feathers) and [eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hawk%27s_Egg), which can be used as [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) ingredients. They are territorial animals who rarely venture far from their nests. Though predatory, they only hunt smaller birds and vermin. Trained hawks have been used to convey orders during battle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTR-1)</sup>

Alongside several other animal totems, the ancient [Atmorans](Lore_Atmora.md) worshiped the hawk too.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDW-2)</sup> [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) often associate the goddess [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md) with the hawk,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRFeldir-3)</sup> and are known to use stylized motifs of the bird in their architecture, crafts, and clothing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RM4-4)</sup> Similarly, the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) goddess [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md) is sometimes represented as a great hawk.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONload-5)</sup> The city of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) was founded on the site of the [Skyforge](Lore_Skyforge.md), an ancient forge with a stone hawk edifice which predated the arrival of the Nords and elves.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Sot R7-6)</sup>

During the [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) exodus to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), Saint [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) was supposedly guided by a great hawk in the sky.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eot J-7)</sup> The redtail hawk is considered sacred to [Tava](Lore_Tava.md), as is the [goshawk](Lore_Goshawk.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONWriting Quill-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The hawk's keen sight, accuracy, and rending claws inspired the sword-songs of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md), and they were known to engrave their blades with the wings of the hawk.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GT-10)</sup> [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) of the [Wayward Guardians](Lore_Wayward_Guardians.md) adorn their armors with hawk skulls.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-WGS-11)</sup>

In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), there was a city-state called [Snowhawk](Lore_Snowhawk.md) in northwestern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and it drew inspiration from the animal.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AR-12)</sup> Around the same time, the county of [Santaki](Lore_Santaki_(county).md) was home to the [Knights of the Hawk](Lore_Knights_of_the_Hawk.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-DFHawk-13)</sup>
