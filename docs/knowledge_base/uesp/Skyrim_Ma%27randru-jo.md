[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-npc-Ma%27randru-jo.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ma%27randru-jo.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27randru-jo.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27randru-jo.jpg) Ma'randru-jo **Ma'randru-jo** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [elemental mage](Skyrim_CombatMageElemental.md) who travels around [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) with a [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md), which includes [Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md), [Atahbah](Skyrim_Atahbah.md) and [Khayla](Skyrim_Khayla.md). When the caravan is camped, he can be found wandering around the campsite. He is also a target of a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) [assassination contract](Skyrim_Kill_Ma%27randru-jo.md) given by [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md).

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f). He is equipped with an [elven sword](Skyrim_Elven_Sword.md), and carries a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). He knows the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md) and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Kill Ma'randru-jo](Skyrim_Kill_Ma%27randru-jo.md)**: Assassinate Ma'randru-jo to complete a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) contract.

## Dialogue

- *"Purchase something or leave. Those are your options."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Like with most Dark Brotherhood targets, you can talk to Ma'randru-jo before you kill him. You will be given three options:

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **I'm going to beat you like a house cat.** (if the player is a non-Khajiit) <br> **You are a disgrace to our race! I spit on your fur!** (if the player is a Khajiit) | *"What? You dare challenge Ma'randru-jo? You will suffer for your insolence!"* | Becomes hostile and attacks |
| **You're one hard mage to track down, Ma'randru-jo.** | *"And why would you be looking for me, hmm? I'm not sure I appreciate your presence here. Friend."* | Walks away |
| **(Remain silent)** | *"Now aren't you a strange one. Well, if you're here to shop, shop. If not, find some other caravan to stalk."* | Walks away |

## Notes
- **"Ma"** means "apprentice", and **"Jo"** which means "wizard" in the Khajiiti Language, [Ta'agra](Lore_Ta%27agra.md).
