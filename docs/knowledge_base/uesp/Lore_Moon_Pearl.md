# Pearl

[![](https://images.uesp.net/7/7b/ON-furnishing-Pearl%2C_Large.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Pearl,_Large.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Pearl,_Large.jpg) A large pearl Pearls are smooth, round, and lustrous beads that are produced primarily by bivalve [mollusks](Lore_Mollusk.md). These are highly valued gemstones, either for their aesthetics<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWPearl-1)</sup> or their magical properties. Their scarcity makes them a valuable commodity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWPearl-1)</sup> Because they are a product of a living organism, pearls are unique in the realm of [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAnti Great-2)</sup> The [Elves](Lore_Elves.md) in particular have an immense fascination with pearls, not only for their healing properties but their augmentative powers, as illustrated in Fandamorien's "Treatise on Vigor".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONAnti Nilumin-3)</sup> Pearls are symbolically associated with the hard shell of clams, which Verita Numida of the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) believed represented a defensive magical element.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAnti Ward-4)</sup>

In [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), pearls can be harvested from [kollops](Lore_Kollop.md) and in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), they can be found in mass across both [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md) and [Sheogorad](Lore_Sheogorad.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWPearl-1)</sup> The [Grummites](Lore_Grummite.md) of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) make unique yellow pearls that are valuable in Tamriel. There was a mage in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) that tried to bring grummites to Tamriel and harvest the pearls but his tower was burned to the ground by locals. Some of these pearls were unearthed on the island of [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md) after its extraction from the Shivering Isles.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAnti Grummite-5)</sup>

## Culture and Uses
Pearls are sometimes grounded and used for a variety of reasons. The [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) use pearl sand to create their weapons and armors.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONPearl Sand-6)</sup> Pearlwater gives a glossy finish to anything it is applied to. Seafarers use it and other types of oceanic byproduct. Pearlwater can be used in protective magics, most notably in an artifact called Syrabane's Ward. The result of the eponymous [arch-mage](Lore_Syrabane.md)'s work in perfecting the art of [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAnti Ward-4)</sup>

Many nobles in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)'s [Nobles District](Lore_Nobles_District.md) like to wear pearls during one of their many soirees.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAnti Great-2)</sup> Sometime during Emperor [Brazollus Dor](Lore_Brazollus_Dor.md)'s reign in the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md), he hosted the feast of Saint [Coellicia](Lore_Coellicia.md) and during the fourth course, the people were served a dish called Brazollus' Suprises, simple bread rolls served with butter and cream, and with a mysterious filling different for each guest. As the story goes, one minor squire found his bread filled with pearls the size of [grapes](Lore_Grapes.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TFOSC3-7)</sup>

## Eltheric Pearls
Pearls have more history and cultural significance throughout the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), and even a portion of it, the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md) is indirectly named after them.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONNautilis-8)</sup> The [Great Eltheric Clam](Lore_Clam.md) is a creature that can be found near the [Thrassian](Lore_Thras.md) reef and as flashy as its name is, contains a **Great Eltheric Pearl**. Minstrels and troubadours will sing songs about these clams and sea-dwelling [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md) with hearts made of pearls. In the Antiquarian Circle of the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the scholar, Reginus Buca believed their mythical origin is a metaphor but his contemporary, [Amalien](Lore_Amalien.md) believed they held genuine [aurbic](Lore_Aurbis.md) power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAnti Great-2)</sup>

**Black Eltheric Pearls** can be found deep in the ocean and can either occur naturally or come from [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) pearl farms, where surfpeelers will insert [snake](Lore_Snake.md) scales into [Pyandonean Snap-Mussels](Lore_Mussel.md). Elven myth purports that they were once one of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s beady eyes, cut off by [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md) who scattered them across the ocean. These pearls can be made into a unique and powerful necklace called the Sea-Serpent's Coil.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONAnti Black-9)</sup>

## Nilumin River Pearls
**Nilumin River Pearls** (known by [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) divers as **Tears of the Ehlnofey**) are special freshwater pearls used by ancient alchemists to ground into powder remedies. Aldhessia healers would wear them as talismans. These pearls were fashioned into a powerful artifact called the Pearls of Ehlnofey, whose pearls shined brightly like sunlight breaking into calm water.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONAnti Nilumin-3)</sup>

## Abyssal Pearls
**Abyssal Pearls** are large, magically infused pearls fueled by the [soul magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md)<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PRNL3-10)</sup> of the [Sea Sloads](Lore_Sea_Sload.md), who hail from the underwater kingdom of [Ul'vor Kus](Lore_Ul%27vor_Kus.md). The [Yaghra](Lore_Yaghra.md), creatures from deep underwater are attracted to these pearls.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PRNL3-10)</sup> The [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) used these pearls to create [abyssal geysers](Lore_Abyssal_Geysers.md), a magical phenomenon that spurted sea water and summoned creatures from the deep.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-IJA-11)</sup>

## Moon Pearls
The **Moon Pearls** of [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) are sacred to the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and are often put up for display in the greatest [Temples of Two-Moons Dance](Lore_Temple_of_Two-Moons_Dance.md) across the province. A pair of them, which were blessed by the first [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) were kept in the Temple of Seven Riddles, located in the southern hills of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). They were lost in an attack on the temple in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) and considered highly valuable.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONMoon-12)</sup>
