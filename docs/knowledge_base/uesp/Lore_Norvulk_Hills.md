[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/AR-place-Norvulk_Hills.jpg/200px-AR-place-Norvulk_Hills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Norvulk_Hills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Norvulk_Hills.jpg) Norvulk Hills (Arena) The Norvulk Hills is a settlement found on the rolling hills of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md), in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It is built from the ruins of the same name, which was named after Norvulk Tome-Hoarder, an eccentric wizard from the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad-1)</sup>

## History
The Norvulk Ruins have lost their original name as the eras kept going, but it garnered its name from its inhabitant, the wizard, Norvulk Tome-Hoarder, who was active in the Second Empire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad-1)</sup> Sometime later in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), it was redoubt used by the [Supernal Dreamers](Lore_Supernal_Dreamers.md) to plunder its riches and secrets, But it became a base for the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md), and the King of Worms, [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) used it as a place to store his important documents.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOTharn-2)</sup> [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) wandered across the halls and kept cursed artifacts that the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) had later purified.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBauble-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Norvulk Hills was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lady Chrystorya and had a rivalry with [Wind Keep](Lore_Wind_Keep.md). The only temple it had at the time was the [Brotherhood of Seth](Lore_Brotherhood_of_Seth.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> The Norvulk Hills were neighbored by several settlements, including [Portdun Creek](Lore_Portdun_Creek.md) to the north and Vermeir Wastes to the south.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup>
