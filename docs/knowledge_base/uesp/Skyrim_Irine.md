[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-npc-Irine.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irine.jpg) Irine **Irine** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) traveler who can be found wandering around [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), close to the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md). She is named in *[Azarain's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azarain%27s_Journal)* as a possible member of a Daedric cult due to the fact that she seems to know more about [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) than the proprietor of [The Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md).

She carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and a selection of [poor loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

You will be tasked with [pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) or following Irine during the related quest to see if she will lead you to the Daedric cult. However, she is not a member of the cult, and you will need to follow [Harald](Skyrim_Harald.md) instead to progress the quest. If you do decide to follow her at night, she will be seen praying to an [altar to Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Dibella#Watching_Dawnstar) south of Dawnstar. Irine disappears at the conclusion of the quest.
