[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-npc-Nagrub.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nagrub.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nagrub.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nagrub.jpg) Nagrub patrolling the walls **Nagrub** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) and the son of [Chief Burguk](Skyrim_Chief_Burguk.md), the leader of Orc Stronghold [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

Nagrub's mother, [Arob](Skyrim_Arob.md), is the hunts-wife of Burguk and, as such, patrols back and forth the hold all day and night. Nagrub has no other choice but to follow her example, so mother and son spend their entire existence wandering back and forth the courtyard, at times scouting for enemies in the surrounding wilderness.

He wears [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), and a set of [Stormcloak officer bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md). He never sleeps but still carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md). He wields leveled weapons; at level 1 he uses a [steel war axe](Skyrim_Steel_War_Axe.md) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md); at level 6 he uses an [Orcish war axe](Skyrim_Orcish_War_Axe.md) and an [Orcish bow](Skyrim_Orcish_Bow.md). He carries at least twenty leveled arrows, along with a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue

- *"I help my mother Arob with the hunt. We bring much meat and skins to the stronghold."*
- *"Brother Umurn is a strong warrior. I look forward to fighting him for chiefdom one day."*
- *"One day, I intend to be chief. I will fight my father and my brother for it, as is tradition."*

## Conversations
During the long days and nights, Arob and Nagrub will occasionally share their happiness:

**Nagrub**: *"Ah, the air is good today. I welcome the smell of blood from all the kills I will make."*
**Arob**: *"Patience, my son. The hunter days * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * not welcome the blood of his prey, he waits for it."*

**Arob**: *"The air has the smell of beasts. The hunt is good today."*
**Nagrub**: *"Yes, mother. I'm eager to prove my skills today."*
