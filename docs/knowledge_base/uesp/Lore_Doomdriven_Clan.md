# Doomdriven Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/ON-npc-Dremora_Kynlurker.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Dremora_Kynlurker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Dremora_Kynlurker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Dremora_Kynlurker.jpg) A Dremora in service to Malacath, guarding Coldwind's Den The Doomdriven Clan is a [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) clan in service to [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) based in [Ashpit](Lore_Ashpit.md).

The Doomdriven are most commonly found as marshals and commanders to the [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) spirit-legions of the Ashpit. They also, occassionaly perfrom the duties of emmissaries of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) Malacath, and send his word to his followers in [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ODC-1)</sup>
