[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-npc-Svana_Far-Shield.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Svana_Far-Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Svana_Far-Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Svana_Far-Shield.jpg) Svana Far-Shield **Svana Far-Shield** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living and working in [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

Svana's supposed to sleep from midnight to 6am, but her work package overrides this (see [bugs section](#Bugs)). She eats breakfast from 6am to 7am. Then she works at the bunkhouse only to stop and eat at 1pm and 5pm both for one hour.

She wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff38) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0). She wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse_Key) to the bunkhouse, along with a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[Caught Red Handed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Caught_Red_Handed)**: Gather three marks of Dibella to help Svana Far-Shield against [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Caught Red Handed
**Why do you hate Haelga so much?** *"It's not just the work, it's everything about her. She's disgusting. I think she takes her worship of [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella) a bit too seriously. Do you know she's slept with three different men in the last month alone? What kind of a woman would do such a thing? Just for once, I'd like to see her squirm... to rub her nose in it."* **There must be a way.** *"Actually, there is, but I don't think I could get away with doing it. She'd kill me if she found out. You see, I happen to know after she makes love, she gives her partner a token of her affection called a [Mark of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mark_of_Dibella). If you confront her with three of the Marks, she'd be so embarrassed... well, I don't know what she'd do!"* **Sure, I'll help you.** *"Oh, this is going to be great! You need to get the Marks of Dibella from [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md), [Hofgrir](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md) and [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md). Not sure how you're going to do that, but try your best. Then just confront Haelga with them and the rest works itself out."* **Maybe another time.** *"Yeah, you're right. Maybe it's not the best idea."* When you have yet to deliver the marks, she might greet you with:

*"Any luck getting the marks from those men?"* And she'll leave conversations with:

*"Try and get those marks without violence please. I don't want to be responsible for their deaths... or yours."* After you have confronted Haelga with the marks:

**I gave the marks to Haelga.** *"Oh I know. Isn't it wonderful? I bet she squirmed like a [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) when you pulled them out of your pocket. I think things are going to be a lot different around here from now on and I have you to thank for it. Here, I want you to have this. It was my father's but I'm certain you'll put it to good use."*
### Spread the Love
During the quest [Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love), you can give one [religious missive](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara) from the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) to Svana, who will confusedly accept your offer:

*"Thank you. Umm, blessings upon you too."*
## Dialogue
When greeting you:

*"Sorry about the mess. Keeping this place clean is quite a chore."* *"I'd appreciate it if you'd keep your hands to yourself."* *"The Bunkhouse isn't an inn. I'd recommend the [Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md)."* *"Hey! Good to see you again."* **(After completion of Caught Red Handed)** **Do you work here?** *"Work? I suppose you can call it that. I call it slavery. I work my fingers to the bone keeping this disgusting place clean. Ever since my parents died and [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md) took me in, it's been a nightmare. How was I to know she was such a wretched woman? So now I'm stuck living here while those pigs she calls customers grope me and say the most awful things."* When exiting conversation, she may bitterly say:

*"Sure, leave. Not like you care anyway."*
