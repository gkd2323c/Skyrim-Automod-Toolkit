Vundeheim was a village in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) that was destroyed in ancient times. A [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) was erected to commemorate the doomed villagers that were burned alive by the Lightning of Unending Sorrow.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SC-2)</sup>

## Word Wall
*(This) stone commemorates (the) villagers
of doomed Vundeheim, burned
alive by (the) Lightning of
Unending Sorrow.*
—Word Wall's translation<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SC-2)</sup> *QETHSEGOL V4RUKIV S4SUN1R
DO D1NIK VUNDEH2M AG
N4L1S N1L QO DO
UNSL1D KROSIS.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SC-2)</sup>
