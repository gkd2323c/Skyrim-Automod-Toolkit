— *[Protocol and Propriety in Summerset](Lore_Protocol_and_Propriety_in_Summerset.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PPS-1)</sup> Being isolated in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) has allowed the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) to perfect their own cuisine without much interference from the outside races. When they do open their shores to the other races, they will embrace their culinary contributions. Among themselves, Altmer culinary tastes may differ depending on where they are from as those from one area may not agree with those from another on who has the best take on their cuisine.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CCV1-2)</sup>

The Altmer have strict rules when it comes to table manners and deportment<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TESOC-3)</sup> with several details and rules about how to behave at the dinner table. Some of these rules include refilling the glass of the diner to your left and never refilling your own, never resting utensils on the edge of any vessel, not eating more than the most prestigious attendee at any function, always allowing the head of your table to lead the discussion, never watching another while they chew, and never using the wrong utensil as it is considered barbaric. They believe it is better to watch and follow the example of the other guests than to make such a mistake.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PPS-1)</sup> Rules around food and drink in Summerset can be similarly strict as well. Altmeri beverages must all be brewed according to the "rules of conformance"<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and being caught with any drinks that don't follow the laws can cause one to run afoul of the [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) and lead to possible imprisonment.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Seafood is commonplace on Summerset Isle, which is surrounded by sparkling seas. There is no aversion to raw meat and several Altmeri dishes contain raw [fish](Lore_Fish.md), however Altmer will never consume anything that is alive and there are certain animals that are not to be eaten such as rodents.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CCV1-2)</sup>

## Utensils and Tableware
Altmer dishes and utensils are crafted from a wide variety of materials including but not limited to wood, crystal, [silver](Lore_Silver.md), pearl, and [coral](Lore_Coral.md) from Summerset's coasts. No matter what material is used the same obsession with quality and craftsmanship is present in their creation. General cutlery is noted for its fineness<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and even frontline soldier's mess kits include well polished and refined cookware.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Spoons are crafted from deeply hued cherrywood,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> and milky green pearl-glass is used to make simple utensils like knives into works of art.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Other utensils are treasured heirlooms that get passed down through generations such as [golden](Lore_Gold.md) spoons of [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Seafood forks are fashioned from a combination of silver and pearl.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Altmeri forks sometimes have up to eight tines, an amount that is traditionally believed to improve any meal.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Altmer royalty may eat with up to fourteen different forks during a meal.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

Plates are made from porcelain<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> and bowls from transparent crystal so that the contents can be seen from all angles.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Serving trays are fashioned to look like descending eagles.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup> Exquisitely crafted gravy boats are crafted from silver.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

Drinkware is crafted from a variety of materials with the simplest being made from pewter or wood.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup> Even simple wood canteens are known to display high quaility craftsmanship.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup> [Oystershell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oyster) is used to give goblets an air of rustic sophistication<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup> and sanded coral is used for the same purpose but may be inlaid with gold to create a goblet fit for royalty.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup> Amberware is used to craft mugs<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup> and delicate pitchers.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup> Exquisite glass decanters are made in a style known as swan-feather style.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-24)</sup> Tea cozies have been patterned with the golden [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) eagle.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-25)</sup> There exists Summerset wine pourers that use scales to measure the weight of a glass and then pour the difference.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-26)</sup>

Altmer tables may be decorated with tapestries, some of which are large and elaborately decorated.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-27)</sup> Sliced, dried, and polished coral is used to make placemats for table setting.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-28)</sup> Copper coffee presses are used.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-29)</sup> Spice shakers are crafted from painted ceramic.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-30)</sup>
