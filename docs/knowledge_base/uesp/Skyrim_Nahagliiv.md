[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-creature-Nahagliiv_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Nahagliiv_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nahagliiv_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nahagliiv_02.jpg) Nahagliiv near its dragon mound [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-creature-Nahagliiv.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Nahagliiv.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nahagliiv.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nahagliiv.jpg) Nahagliiv attacking Rorikstead **Nahagliiv** is a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) buried in a [dragon mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md) atop a hill, just southwest of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). He is one of several named dragons that [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) resurrects throughout the course of the [main quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md).

He will be resurrected by Alduin and appear in the skies near Rorikstead once the player reaches the stage 60 of [Alduin's Wall](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Wall.md), but will not attack the town until you arrive to face him.

Nahagliiv's name translates to "Fury Burn Wither."
