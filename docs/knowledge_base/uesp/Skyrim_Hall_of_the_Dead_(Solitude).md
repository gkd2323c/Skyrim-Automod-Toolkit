[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-place-Solitude_Hall_of_the_Dead.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Hall_of_the_Dead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Hall_of_the_Dead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Hall_of_the_Dead.jpg) The Solitude Hall of the Dead The **Hall of the Dead** is where [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s dead are brought to be prepared for burial.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude).jpg.md) The interior of Solitude's Hall of the Dead Solitude's Hall of the Dead is tended by [Styrr](Skyrim_Styrr.md), a priest of Arkay. There is a [shrine of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay) in front room, which will grant you the [Blessing of Arkay](Skyrim_Blessings.md#Blessing_of_Arkay) for eight hours. The dead themselves are buried in [Solitude Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Catacombs), which can be accessed through the door in the basement.

### Solitude's Hall of the Dead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Solitude%29_02.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude)_02.jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Solitude)_02.jpg.md) Shrine of Arkay Upon entering you are in a small room with a small pillar, opposite is an unlocked door. In the right half of the room is a firepit with a stone wall around it. In the eastern corner there is a wooden bench and narrow table against the walls with a low table in front of them. On the tables are; a plate of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and cheese, a [goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), a common book, an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) in a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl). In the northern corner there is a small unit with an [iron warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Warhammer) and the shrine to Arkay.

Around the central pillar there are several [skulls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skull), including one [troll skull](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Skull) on a table. The left half of the room is dominated by a table and chairs in the center. The table is set for two, plates containing a [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish) and two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), as well as two goblets, a [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration), bread and cheese. Beside the table is an open crate containing a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) and an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing). Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves holding several random [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md), including two [bows](Skyrim_Bow.md), a [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword), two [battleaxes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe) and a [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe). To the right of this against the southwest wall is a random [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) on a unit. In the western corner there is a barrel of [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) and a set of shelves against the northwest wall holding a potion of regeneration, a [potion of resist fire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Fire), two [potions of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and one of [minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), several wheels of cheese, a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and six [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). To the left of the internal door is a small set of shelves holding an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace) and three [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow).

Through the door, on your right is a small wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a wall shelf above with a [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) flower and common book on it. In the eastern corner is a round table with a goblet on top. Opposite the door, against the northwest wall is a narrow table holding a bowl of bread, a plate of [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a sack underneath. To the southwest are flights of stairs leading up and down. At the bottom of the stairs there are a pile of sacks containing foodstuffs and a corridor flanked by rows of lit braziers leading to a closed door that gives access to **Solitude's catacombs**.

Climbing the stairs to the top floor, you come to a landing with a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush in a planter at the top of the stairs, a wardrobe containing clothes against the northwest wall with a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) on top. Opposite the top of the stairs on the northeast wall is the door to [Styrr's](Skyrim_Styrr.md) bedroom. Against the southeast wall is a large round wooden table holding three common books, a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a [cup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup) and a [solution of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solution_of_Strength). Next to this against the northeast wall is another narrow table holding bread and cheese in a bowl and on a plate respectively and a sack underneath. Above the narrow table is a wall shelf holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead).

Styrr's bedroom consists of a double owned bed in the northern corner and an open crate at the foot containing a potion of regeneration and [draught of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Strength). To the right against the northeast wall is a tall wardrobe containing clothes. There is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) behind the door in the western corner to the left of a cupboard. Against the southwest wall there is a long unlocked chest, with a wall shelf above it holding a [potion of plentiful healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Healing) and a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md). Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves with two [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales), two bottles of [frostbite venom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom), a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling), a bowl of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust), a bowl of [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and a basket containing two bunches of lavender. To the left of the unit is a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots), with a couple of sacks to the left of these. In the eastern corner in front of another cupboard is a low table and chair. On the table is a plate of bread and cheese, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a goblet.
