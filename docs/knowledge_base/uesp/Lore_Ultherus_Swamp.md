Little is known about this region of Argonia. The beautiful, but deadly [Dragon's-Tongue](Lore_Dragon%27s_Tongue_Plant.md) plants are especially common around the area of the swamp.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MW-2)</sup>
