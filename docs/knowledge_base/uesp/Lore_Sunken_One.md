The Sunken One, also known as **He Who Shakes The Ground**, is a [Storm Atronach](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md) that dwells in the depths of Sandstone Cavern, a system of natural caverns located in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md), northeast of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) and west of [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SJournal1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>

In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Sunken One was worshipped as an all-powerful [god](Lore_Gods.md) by the Seringi family, local [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) farmers who lived at the nearby Shetcombe Farm. The atronach supposedly revealed His teachings to these farmers, demanding that they spread His word and follow His laws in return.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SJournal1-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SJournal2-3)</sup> The farmers believed that he sat below the surface and passed sentence upon the mortal world without pity or mercy. The only way to appease the Sunken One was by braving the depths of Sandstone Cavern to leave him "the Offering", consisting of gold and gems.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SJournal1-1)</sup>

By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Shetcombe Farm's sole inhabitant was Slythe Seringi, a Dunmer man who was devoted to the teachings of the Sunken One, as was his father before him. Following the sacking of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) by the forces of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), Slythe flew into a panic, believing the burning of the nearby city to have been the work of the Sunken One, angered by the growing corruption of the world of man. He believed that the atronach in His hunger would destroy more Cyrodilic cities if he did not enter Sandstone Cavern and deliver the Offering to appease Him, as his father once had.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SJournal1-1)</sup> Slythe entered the caverns without weapons or armor, relying only on his faith, and managed to descend to the Sandstone Big Rooms, the lowest depths of the cave network. However, the caverns were home to dangerous monsters and wild animals,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> one of which attacked Slythe and mortally wounded him. He died within the caverns without ever delivering his offering.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SJournal3-5)</sup>

The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) subsequently came upon Slythe's recently abandoned farmhouse at Shetcombe and followed him into the depths of Sandstone Cavern. Upon discovering Slythe's corpse in the cave's depths, the Sunken One appeared and attacked the Hero.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Although defeated by the Hero, the Sunken One was not permanently banished to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), and would always eventually reappear within the caverns.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup>
