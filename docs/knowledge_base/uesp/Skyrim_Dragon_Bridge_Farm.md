[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-place-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg) Lylvieve Family's House **Lylvieve Family's House** is a small farmhouse on the northwestern side of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md), located next to [Horgeir's House](Skyrim_Horgeir%27s_House.md).

It is home to [Azzada Lylvieve](Skyrim_Azzada_Lylvieve.md), his wife [Michel](Skyrim_Michel_Lylvieve.md), and their children, [Julienne](Skyrim_Julienne_Lylvieve.md) and [Clinton](Skyrim_Clinton_Lylvieve.md). It consists of one zone, *[Lylvieve Family's House](#Lylvieve_Family.27s_House)*.

### Lylvieve Family's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House.jpg) Ground floor There are two levels to this home, including the ground floor and the basement. Upon entering, the right side holds a bedroom area, along the east wall. The double bed (belonging to the [Lylvieve Family House Occupants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family_House_Occupants) faction) is flanked by a small table to the right and a non-respawning dresser to the left. Atop the table are five loose gold coins and a basket containing four [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md). The dresser holds a [common soul gem](Skyrim_Common_Soul_Gem.md) (filled), two [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), two [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), a [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka), and a [potion of plentiful stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina). Along the north wall is a set of shelves holding four bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and one bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) on the top shelf, along with three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), three [green apples](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md), two [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), a [boiled creme treat](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md), and two [goat cheese wheels](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) on the middle shelf. On the floor to the right of these shelves is a woven basket containing three [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). Above the shelves are some hanging ingredients, including two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, one dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), and two portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). At the center of the room is a large dining table which holds a messy arrangement of food, including two [goat cheese wedges](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), one [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), three loaves of bread, two chicken's eggs, two bottles of alto wine, and two pieces of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat). In the southwest corner of the room, there are two respawning meat barrels.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Lylvieve_Family%27s_House_02.jpg) Basement Proceeding down the stairs to the basement, the children's bedroom area is along the east wall, while the rest of the room is used for general storage. A child's bed owned by [Clinton](Skyrim_Clinton_Lylvieve.md) is in the northeast corner, while a single bed owned by [Julienne](Skyrim_Julienne_Lylvieve.md) is in the southeast corner. At the foot of each bed is a small non-respawning chest. Along the north wall, there is a small round table holding one goat cheese wedge, one loaf of bread, and two boiled creme treats. To the left of the table is a respawning food barrel, and between them are four freshly caught [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) hanging above. Another respawning food barrel can be found below the stairs, among various clutter.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_House_Key) to which is carried by all four members of the Lylvieve family.
- A [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md) can be found behind the house, which can be used to produce [flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> from [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.
