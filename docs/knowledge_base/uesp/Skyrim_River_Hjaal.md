The **River Hjaal** is the main river that flows through the center of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md).

This river's headwaters are located in the highlands south of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), along the border between Hjaalmarch and [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). After dropping down a series of waterfalls, the river passes through Morthal, then spreads out into a wide marshy region that eventually drains into the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md), which flows into the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).

The river is inhabited by many species of fish, such as [Abecean Longfin](Skyrim_Abecean_Longfin.md), [Cyrodilic Spadetail](Skyrim_Cyrodilic_Spadetail.md), [Histcarp](Skyrim_Histcarp.md), [River Betty](Skyrim_River_Betty.md), [Silverside Perch](Skyrim_Silverside_Perch.md), and [Salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md).
