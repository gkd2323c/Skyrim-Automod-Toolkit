[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-place-Guard_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-place-Guard_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Guard_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Guard_Barracks.jpg) Guard's Barracks exterior The **Guard Barracks** is a building in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) where guards live when not on duty. It is located behind [the Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) in the Plains District.

It is the main barracks of Whiterun and it consists of only one area, *[Guard Barracks](#Guard_Barracks)*. There is another [barracks](Skyrim_Guard_Barracks_(Gate).md) by the main gate to the city, for use by the guards who guard the gate.

The building itself is quite hard to spot for the casual user of Whiterun's services. The path to the building starts between the Bannered Mare and [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md), it runs south, then follows a loop to the east, and then back northwards and straight to the door.

## Guard Barracks
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Guard_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guard_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guard_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guard_Barracks.jpg) The Entrance to the Guard Barracks [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Guard_Barracks_02.jpg) The Sleeping Quarters The interior of the building is split into four rooms with a hallway in the middle of the building connecting the four rooms. Upon entering, the first room contains a dining area along the western wall while the eastern wall is used for storage. A large sack with food sits next to the front door and another large sack rests underneath a locked display case with a [steel battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe) on display. The rest of the area contains two food barrels, a cupboard with clutter and an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel) and a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel) on the shelves above, and two sacks with food. Along the western wall is a small table with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bowl with four [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie). A set of shelves holds five bottles of Nord mead and some regular books and two wheels of goat cheese. The dinner table is set for five and contains a piece of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) and a coin purse.

The hallway in the center contains a wooden table with an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) and underneath the table, a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots). It also contains three food barrels and four bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), along with a dresser with normal clothing. The western room contains three food barrels and the secret entrance to [Dragonsreach Dungeon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach_Dungeon) while the eastern room has four large food sacks, two food barrels, a wardrobe with clothes and a bit of gold and two bottles of wine.

The last room is a sleeping area where the guards relax in shifts. It contains eight owned beds and opens up to the sight of a small dining table with a [honey nut treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), three [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) and a bowl with eight [baked potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes). Behind the entrance door is a set of shelves with a pair of [iron gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets). To the west are four of the beds, an end table with clutter and some more cheese on top, a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) and some [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). A pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) is on the floor next to a huge wardrobe with minor valuables. A large sack lean against another bed, a minor chest and another end table with clutter can be found between two beds, with a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and four wedges of goat cheese. The eastern part of the sleeping area contains the rest of the beds. Going clockwise, you will stumble upon a dresser with clothing, with a pair of shoes on the floor and some clothes on top, right next to a coin purse. An end table sits between the next two beds, with a coin purse on top. Between the last two beds are a pair of shoes on the floor and a small table with some food and drink, two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine).

## Notes
- The guards here will not be immediately hostile to you if you have escaped from jail, they will only say *"Wait! I know you..."*, when you get close to them, but will not try to arrest you, or attack you.
