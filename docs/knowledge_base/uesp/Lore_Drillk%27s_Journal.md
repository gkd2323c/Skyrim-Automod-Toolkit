Drillk's Journal **by [Drillk](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Drillk)** *A journal fragment of the last Kothringi of Zuuk*
---

#### — 15th of Morning Star, [2E 561](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_561)
A week ago [Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(place).md) flourished. Today, only I remain.

I watched helplessly as family and friends wither away from this [terrible plague](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md). Am I immune? Is it only a matter of time before I succumb? I'm giving up this journal.

#### — Date uncertain. How many years? Dismayed and angry.
[Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) troops moved into the nearby ruins and have disturbed the ashes of [my people](Lore_Kothringi.md)! I must confront them. I'll demand they respect the dead. Whatever they seek, they won't find it in the remains of Zuuk. May the plague strike them down for their trespass!
