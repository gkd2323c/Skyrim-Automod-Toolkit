[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-npc-Morven.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Morven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Morven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Morven.jpg) Morven **Morven** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) living in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) at [Nepos's house](Skyrim_Nepos%27s_House.md) where he works as a servant.

He is an undercover [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) agent and bodyguard of [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md), one of the most influential citizens in Markarth. He spends all of his time cleaning the house, without ever eating or sleeping. Morven becomes hostile and must be killed during your investigation into the [recent Forsworn attacks](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md).

He wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Nepos%27s_House) to Nepos's house, as well as a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests

## Notes
- Morven's death can be avoided by pickpocketing and reading [Thonar's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thonar%27s_Journal) or [Nepos's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nepos%27s_Journal) instead of questioning them, then going directly to [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md). - Alternatively, it can be avoided by going directly to Eltrys after questioning [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md).
