The Song of Gods *A song extolling the living Nordic Gods*
---
[Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md), the Kiss at the End,
Called the Mother of [Men](Lore_Men.md).
Dance in her storm's divine,
As [hawk](Lore_Hawk.md) she flies, our Kyne.

[Mara](Lore_Mara.md), Goddess of Love,
Whose grace lifts us high above.
She blesses our sacred flora,
Does our beloved [she-wolf](Lore_Wolf.md), Mara.

[Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md), the artist's muse,
Goddess of Beauty, that's no ruse.
As [silver](Lore_Silver.md) [moth](Lore_Moth.md), there is no flaw,
We see only the fair Dibella.

[Stuhn](Lore_Stuhn.md), [whale](Lore_Whale.md) God of Ransom,
War captives seek his calm.
With shield and horn his boon,
Stands with us, does fair Stuhn.

[Jhunal](Lore_Jhunal.md), sage God of Runes,
Flies free beneath the glistening moons.
A night [owl](Lore_Owl.md) who's so droll,
Soars with us, the wise Jhunal.

[Orkey](Lore_Orkey.md) tests all mortals,
He likes to start all quarrels.
A [snake](Lore_Snake.md) no one can flee,
None may fool wary Orkey.

[Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md), the dread World-Eater,
Does much that we might fear.
Known as the First [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md),
None dare worship Alduin.
