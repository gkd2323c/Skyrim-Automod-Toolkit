## Quick Walkthrough
1. Attend the wedding at the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
2. Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md). For a bonus, kill her during her speech.
3. Return to [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Let the Festivities Begin
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_02.jpg) Vittoria and Asgeir at the wedding After the [Silence Has Been Broken](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silence_Has_Been_Broken) quest, the first part of the Dark Brotherhood's biggest contract is set in motion. At Astrid's behest, travel to the courtyard of the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Here you will find the small wedding reception you were directed to attend. Your target is the bride, [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md). If you approach Vittoria before she addresses the reception, she will exclaim: *"What an amazing day this has been. I hope you're enjoying the festivities. I'm... I'm just so overwhelmed. Such kindness... for me."* You can choose one of three dialogue options in response:

**The Stormcloaks say hello!** *"Oh! Well, you tell your Stormcloak friends "hello" right back at them! This is a day of healing! Legion, Stormcloaks - all are welcome here!"* **Hail the Imperial Legion!** *"Ha ha ha ha! Yes, yes indeed! Hail the Imperial Legion! Hail the Stormcloaks! Hail Solitude and the Eight Divines! On this glorious day, we honor everyone! Ha ha ha ha!"* **(Remain silent)** *"Oh, no need to be shy! My day is your day! Please - eat, drink, make merry! We're all friends here!"* During the reception, she will also tell the guests: *"Please, enjoy the festivities! This is a day of peace and happiness, when Imperials and Stormcloaks join together, as equals!"* or *"Please, enjoy yourself!"* She will then stand up with her fiancé, [Asgeir Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md), and enter a door to reach the balcony, where she will begin addressing her guests. Assassinate her while she speaks to earn the bonus reward. There are several ways you can choose to accomplish the goal (see [this section](#Strategies) for more details).

#### Strategies
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_03.jpg) A loose gargoyle hanging over the balcony [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg) A sniping spot opposite the balcony There are many strategies you can use to kill Vittoria at her wedding. If you chose to speak to some of your fellow family members, they will give you advice on ways to kill her and what may happen afterwards:

- If you speak to [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md), she will tell you about a loose gargoyle statue above the balcony: *"It's no fair. I love weddings. [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) should have given this contract to me. Instead, she's got me visiting cities, assessing security. How boring. But listen, I think I can help you. The wedding reception is outside the Temple of the Divines, right? In Solitude? If so, the bride will probably address the crowd from the balcony. It overlooks the courtyard. Well, there's an old statue there. I've seen it. Hanging right over where she'll be standing. Old means weak. Weak means it could fall. Hmmm?"* If you travel to the wall above the balcony, you can push a loose gargoyle statue when Vittoria makes her address to the wedding guests. To access this balcony, enter the Temple of the Divines, then go up the stairs to the right and through the door at the end of the corridor. You can also access this balcony by going up the stairs located to the right of [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). The gargoyle is there whether you talk to Babette or not.
- If you speak to [Gabriella](Skyrim_Gabriella.md), she will tell you: *"I hope you don't mind, but I took the liberty of surveying the site of the reception. There's a small parapet, just opposite the balcony, that would prove an excellent spot for a long-range kill. I left something there for you. If you're inclined to take that approach."* If you take the door to the northwest of the temple entrance, you will end up on a ledge located opposite the balcony where Vittoria addresses the reception. On the ledge are six arrows, an [Elixir of True Shot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elixir_of_True_Shot), and the unique magical bow [Firiniel's End](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Firiniel%27s_End). You can simply shoot the bride from this spot; she is easily killed with a single sneak attack from the magic bow you are given. It is best to use these free items now to be sure she is killed immediately. This is especially important if you have a low [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill and are planning a ranged attack. Like the gargoyle, the bow is there even if you haven't talked to Gabriella.
- [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) will warn you about the possible outcome of murdering Vittoria: *"Slaughtering the Emperor's cousin? At her wedding? Those guards will be out for blood. Wouldn't surprise me if the crowd jumped in too."*
- [Veezara](Skyrim_Veezara.md) will advise you to plan your escape route beforehand: *"Your contract sounds simple, which means it certainly will not be. Getting into that reception will be easy, but have you thought about getting out?"*

Other strategies for killing Vittoria include the following:

- Pickpocket a [Frenzy](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md) poison into [the groom](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md)'s inventory as Vittoria addresses the crowd. He will immediately drink it, turn, and kill her, provided that the poison is of sufficient strength. Casting a spell of Frenzy will achieve the same effect, though the [Quiet Casting](Skyrim_Quiet_Casting.md) perk may be needed to avoid detection. To kill with a particular poison, you will need a poison of better than Weak strength and the [Poisoned](Skyrim_Poisoned.md) perk.
- Turn into a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) or a [vampire lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> (without being seen and therefore incurring no bounty), and then use the door to the overhead balcony where she is located and kill her. A good spot to transform while Vittoria is speaking is the small nook behind the loose gargoyle. By crouching (sneaking) inside this nook, you can avoid incurring a bounty for transforming, and you can swiftly jump down on the bride before the crowd notices what's happening.
- Follow the happy couple to the balcony where the bride gives her speech. If you crouch down behind the couple you will not be seen by them or the guests. You can kill the bride and, by killing the groom as your last witness, you will get rid of the bounty as both are joined in death.
- Drop onto the balcony, crouch, and use Unrelenting Force.
- Purchase an invisibility potion and use it when killing Vittoria, guards shouldn't know that you did it.

### 'Til Death Do Us Part
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_05.jpg) Veezara coming to your aid Once Vittoria is killed, pandemonium will break loose. Veezara will then appear, sent by Astrid to offer a distraction while you escape. Even if you weren't detected and got away with the assassination undiscovered, Veezara will still charge in and initiate combat with everyone, drawing attention away from you so you can simply walk out. Sometimes, the guests will still give chase, in which case you can escape the city quickly by using a stairwell under the [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) tower bridge, near the market. One way of avoiding a bounty is to fast travel to the Sanctuary to collect your reward as soon as Vittoria is killed and before the guests turn hostile.

### Summoning a Legend of the Brotherhood
Upon your arrival back to the [Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), Astrid will congratulate you on your success: *"With Vici's murder, you've started us down a path the Dark Brotherhood hasn't traveled in centuries. The assassination of an Emperor. And now, your reward. A [unique spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Spectral_Assassin) to summon a legend of the Dark Brotherhood. His soul serves us now in death, as his body once did in life"*. This spell will allow you to summon the powerful spirit of an assassin to fight by your side. If you fulfilled the bonus requirements, she will also say *"Ah, and of course, a bonus, for killing Vici while she addressed her guests, as instructed. Gold... simple and pure. Spend it as you will."*, and give you a leveled amount of gold. Astrid will then carry on: *"Now then. Time to proceed to the next stage of the plan. Go and speak with Gabriella. She's been helping me arrange your next contract."* When you approach her again, she will tell you: *"As I said, go speak with Gabriella. She's been assisting me with your next contract. This Emperor business, well... it does involve everyone."* The next quest, [Breaching Security](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Breaching_Security), will now begin.

## Notes
- Completion of this quest will make another minor [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Spiced_Wine) unavailable, unless you completed that one first.
- Some of the guests at the wedding include [Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md), [Alexia Vici](Skyrim_Alexia_Vici.md), [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Vulwulf_Snow-Shod.md), [Nura Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Nura_Snow-Shod.md), [Vivienne Onis](Skyrim_Vivienne_Onis.md), [Kayd](Skyrim_Kayd.md), [Rorlund](Skyrim_Rorlund.md), [Pantea Ateia](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md), [Lodi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lodi), [Noster Eagle-Eye](Skyrim_Noster_Eagle-Eye.md), and a [Penitus Oculatus agent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Agent).
- After Veezara appears, if you are not being pursued, you can talk to all of the wedding guests to hear their individual reactions to Vittoria's murder.
- Even if you are not detected when killing the bride, the guests will still turn hostile, and attacking them will incur a bounty. Raising and lowering arms will count as yielding and will not earn you a bounty.
- [Plautis Carvain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plautis_Carvain) and [his wife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salonia_Carvain) can be found traveling in the wilderness during a random encounter, and claim to be heading towards the wedding, although they will never appear there and are not mentioned by any of the other guests. After completing the quest, you may once more run into Plautis and Salonia, where they explain that they were robbed by bandits and missed the wedding.
- Using an [Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) potion, power, or spell may help you avoid a bounty if it is used right after the murder, although the guards may still locate and attack you, causing the bounty to be incurred.
- Vittoria can be looted for her unique [wedding dress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Dress), [wedding sandals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Sandals), [wedding wreath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wedding_Wreath), and [her wedding band](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vittoria%27s_Wedding_Band) before it disappears. If the body is gone, you can find Vittoria's remains in the [Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Catacombs), in a coffin on the northeastern side of the longest tunnel.
- Killing Vittoria by casting a Frenzy spell at her can cause her to leave the balcony, which would cause your bonus to be forfeit as she is no longer considered "speaking to the guests".

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=7300))
- Any of the guests can be moved to the courtyard once the wedding scene is set up, regardless of whether they're alive or not. If they have died at some point, then their emptied bodies will appear instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
- [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md) and [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md) are set to lose their essential status once this quest is done, but they both have other quests they're involved in. If Erikur is dead but [The Dainty Sload](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dainty_Sload) is not finished yet, the entire [Thieves Guild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild) questline will halt. He will be resurrected if this has happened. If Bryling dies before [Payment Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery) is finished, that quest will be marked as failed in your journal and shut down. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Lodi the bard may not be able to play his flute because his 3D doesn't enable fast enough for the bard song scripts to catch up. Also, the two wedding guests coming in from outside Solitude need to be manually checked to make sure they're not dead and then moved into the city. Their AI will not get them to Solitude in time if you head there shortly after getting the quest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=15112))
- Lodi may continue to play after Vittoria is killed, despite the vocal reaction he gives. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=16072))
- Once Vittoria is dead and you reach the *Report back to Astrid* quest stage, the quest marker may erroneously point you to the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in Solitude. Once you enter the temple, the quest marker is updated to correctly point you to the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md).
- Dropping the gargoyle statue may make Vittoria's body clip inside the balcony, preventing her body from being easily looted.
- Astrid sometimes doesn't give you the bonus reward even if you killed Vittoria while she was on the balcony speaking. This may occur if you're using the loose gargoyle method or by assassinating her at night. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This bug can be fixed by entering `setpqv DB05 Bonus_var 1` in the console after killing the target.
- Vittoria sometimes may not start her speech and will only get up and walk to the tower entrance, turn around and then sit back down repeatedly. <sup>**?**</sup> - Reloading the game may fix this.
- If you kill her via an arrow shot without being detected (invisibility and line of sight breaking cover), you can escape the town without Veezara appearing. In this case, you will be attacked by guards the next time you visit Solitude and walk by the courtyard. <sup>**?**</sup> - To avoid this, double back and trigger Veezara before leaving town.
