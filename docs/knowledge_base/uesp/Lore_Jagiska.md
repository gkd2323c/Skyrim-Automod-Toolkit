—Jagiska<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MJ-1)</sup> **Matron** Jagiska was a [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [witch](Lore_Witches.md) and a prominent member of the [Icereach Coven](Lore_Icereach_Coven.md). She was a powerful [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md) responsible for conducting many [Harrowstorm](Lore_Harrowstorm.md) rituals across the lands of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), and the Holds of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), and [Karthald](Lore_Karthald.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and the tunnels of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md) below during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) and the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) crisis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MJ-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/ON-npc-Nelldena.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Nelldena.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nelldena.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nelldena.jpg) Nelldena, a Reachfolk agent of the Stonehands trying to prevent the Harrowstorms [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/ON-npc-Swordthane_Jylta.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Swordthane_Jylta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Swordthane_Jylta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Swordthane_Jylta.jpg) Swordthane Jylta, a Nord agent of the Swords of Solitude trying to prevent the Harrowstorms Little is known about Jagiska's past. As a member of the Icereach Coven, she came into contact with the Gray Host and began cooperating with them. At some point between [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577) and [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Coven was tasked by [Rada al-Saran](Lore_Rada_al-Saran.md) with mastering the Harrowstorm ritual.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LTTIC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOODialogue-3)</sup> By 2E 582, they began terrorizing the populations of Western Skyrim and the Reach. They performed tempest rituals known as Harrowstorms, which they used to resurrect members of the Gray Host who had died and were sent to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LTTIC-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-On Har-4)</sup>

Jagiska was responsible for many of the Harrowstorm attacks, particularly at the ritual sites at Black Morass, Chilblain Peak, [Giant's Coast](Lore_Giant%27s_Coast.md), Hailstone Valley, Northern Watch, and Old Karth within the region of Skyrim, and underground in Blackreach at the sites of [Dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md)'s Bane, Gloomforest, Miner's Lament, and Nightstone.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Harrowstorms-5)</sup> Many of the rituals within the region were stopped by the representatives of the Swords of Solitude in orer to protect local [Nords](Lore_Nord.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HTH-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SJ-7)</sup>

She also performed the rituals at the sites of Harrowed Haunt, [Ragnvald](Lore_Ragnvald.md), Reachwind, and Witchborne within the Reach.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Harrowstorms-5)</sup> Under the command of Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md) of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), the agents of [Stonehands](Lore_Stonehands.md) intervened to stop many of those rituals to protect the Reachfolk.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-HTH2-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Nelldena-9)</sup>

Her actions led to the demise of many travelers,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Travel-10)</sup> and the [souls](Lore_Souls.md) she absorbed were used to bring fallen Gray Host members back to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MJ-1)</sup> However, her efforts were ultimately thwarted, and the Icereach Coven,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TFI-11)</sup> along with their vampire allies, were defeated, both in Skyrim and in the Reach.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DOW-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SC-13)</sup> What happened to Jagiska herself, however, is unknown.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MJ-1)</sup>
