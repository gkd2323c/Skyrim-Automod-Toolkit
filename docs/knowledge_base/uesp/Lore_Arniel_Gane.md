[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/CT-Events-Dwemer_3_%28Large%29.png/200px-CT-Events-Dwemer_3_%28Large%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Dwemer_3_(Large).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:CT-Events-Dwemer_3_(Large).png) Arniel Gane researching Dwemer secrets Arniel Gane was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) researcher and [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) mage at the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). Arniel studied the ruins of [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), and had a particular interest in [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) artifacts. He was both passionate about his work and paranoid that it would be stolen or compromised. He was obssesed with arcane mysteries and relentlessly pursued Dwarven secrets, navigating forgotten ruins on the edge of discovery and madness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

He endeavored to discover the secret behind one of the greatest mysteries of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md): the disappearance of the Dwemer. In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), his work came to a head when he finally succeeded in having the dagger [Keening](Lore_Keening.md) brought to the College from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). He used [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md)'s tool in an experiment he had devised to try to recreate the event that caused the Dwemer's disappearance. Sadly, he did not have the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md), and unfortunately, Arniel's recreation proved to be too accurate, and he has not been seen since.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRArniels-2)</sup>
