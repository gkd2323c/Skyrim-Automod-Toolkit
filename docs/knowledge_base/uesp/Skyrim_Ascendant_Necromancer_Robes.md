[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-item-Ascendant_Necromancer_Robes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ascendant_Necromancer_Robes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ascendant_Necromancer_Robes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ascendant_Necromancer_Robes.jpg) Ascendant Necromancer Robes The Ascendant Necromancer Robes are robes that make conjuration spells last 25% longer and increase the summon limit by 1 for lesser conjured or reanimated undead.

The robes will start dropping after level 40 and can be dropped by Arch Necromancers and Necromancer bosses, such as the one in [Boulderfall Cave](Skyrim_Boulderfall_Cave.md). [Arondil](Skyrim_Arondil.md) in [Yngvild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngvild) may also wear these robes.

## Notes
- This item can be disenchanted and is listed as Empowered Necromancy in an [Arcane Enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md).
- The enchantment can only be placed on the body slot.
- The increase of the summon limit is always 1, even if you have the enchantment active more than once.
- You can summon one additional undead creature as long as it is not conjured by a master or expert level spell.
- The extra summon remains even after you stop wearing the robes.
