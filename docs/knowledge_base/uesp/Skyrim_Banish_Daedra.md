*Weaker/Powerful summoned [daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md) are sent back to Oblivion.* **Banish Daedra** and **Expel Daedra** are conjuration spells that send a targeted [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md) back to Oblivion - essentially resulting in a one-hit kill. Only summoned [Atronachs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach) and [Familiars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Familiar) (but not [Flaming Familiars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flaming_Familiar)) are affected; [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) are immune. Daedra which are too high level for the spell you are casting are instead pushed back and damaged by the spell, so it's not useless even if fighting higher level atronachs.
