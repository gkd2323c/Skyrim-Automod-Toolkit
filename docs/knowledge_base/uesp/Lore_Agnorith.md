# Agnorith

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Male |
| Floruit | [2E 900s](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_900s) | | |

Agnorith was the brother of [Emperor Tiber Septim I](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> Agnorith had a daughter named [Kintyra](Lore_Kintyra_I.md), who was the Queen of [Silvenar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silvenar), and became Empress Kintyra Septim I after her [first cousin once removed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pelagius_Septim_I) was assasinated at the [Temple of the One](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Temple_of_the_One) by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> Thereafter, Agnorith's descendants continued the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), ushering in the beloved line of [Emperors Uriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Emperors_Uriel).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-2)</sup><sup>:15</sup>

## Notes
- When presented with two options of Agnorith being a real figure (with Kintryra's descendants somehow being [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md) anyway despite not directly descending from Tiber) or being a fictional figure invented by Kintryra to support her claim to the throne (with Kintryra being an illegitimate child/descendant of Tiber and thereby inheriting his dragonblood), designer [Ted Peterson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ted_Peterson) recommended that one should assume the latter to keep with [Skyrim's lore concepts about the Dragonborn](Skyrim_The_Book_of_the_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPosts-3)</sup>
