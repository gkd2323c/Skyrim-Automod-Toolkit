[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/MW-book-Provinces_of_Tamriel.jpeg/200px-MW-book-Provinces_of_Tamriel.jpeg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-book-Provinces_of_Tamriel.jpeg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-book-Provinces_of_Tamriel.jpeg) Map of Tamriel (*Morrowind*) The Pryai River (sometimes called the **Prytasus River**)<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> is a meandering waterway that flows at [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)'s border to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where the flora of Cyrodiil met the flora of Morrowind.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup> Its entire length and prominence across the land is unknown.

## History

### Four-Score War
During the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md), Morrowind marched their armies to [Bodrum](Lore_Bodrum.md), a hamlet located on steep bluff just above the Pryai River. The Morrowind commanders dammed the river, flooding the plains beyond and forcing their [Cyrodilic enemies](Lore_Second_Empire.md) to trudge through mud into battle, before the Morrowind forces released the dam, and flooded their enemies out completely.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup>
