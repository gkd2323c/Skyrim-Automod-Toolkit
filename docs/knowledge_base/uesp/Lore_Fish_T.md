## Tench
**Tench** are a species of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).

- **Lost Valley Tench** can be found in the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), as well as in the underground rivers of Arkthzand Caverns in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).
- **Ruby Tench** can be found in the rivers of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Tetra
**Tetra** are a species of fish.

- **Elsweyr Moon Tetra** can be found in the rivers of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md).
- **Hypogean Tetra** can be found in the foul waters of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).
- **Tidebarrow Tetra** can be found in the lakes of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Toadfish
**Toadfish** are a species of fish that can be found in the saltwater of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md).

- **Ouze Toadfish** can be found in the foul waters of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Tilapia
**Tilapia** are a species of fish.

- **Pellitine Tilapia** can be found in foul water within [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).
- **Toadstool Tilapia** can be found in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Trodh
**Trodh** are a common species of fish that can be found in foul waters across Tamriel. They are very small and crunchy. Dead, cooked trodh are poisonous, thus they are best eaten live.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA-1)</sup> Fish roe and crawlers can be used as bait to catch them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AGt FT-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Common_Fish)

## Trout
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/ON-furnishing-Fish%2C_Trout.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Fish%2C_Trout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Fish,_Trout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Fish,_Trout.jpg) A dead trout **Trout** are a species of fish.

- **Bone-Trout** are a type of trout that can be found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They aren't very smart. The native tribes of Murkmire may say "[that person] has a brain like a bone-trout" when describing someone who isn't very bright.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
- **Brook Trout** can be found in the rivers of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).
- **Brown Trout** can be found in the lakes of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md).
- **Cutthroat Trout** can be found in the rivers of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).
- **Deadlands Trout** can found in the foul waters of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).
- **Ilinalta Trout**, named for [Lake Ilinalta](Lore_Lake_Ilinalta.md), can be found in the lakes of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).
- **Marble Trout** can be found in the rivers of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).
- **Nchuand Cavetrout** can be found in the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), as well as in the underground rivers of Arkthzand Caverns in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).
- **Nibenay Trout** can be found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).
- **Rivenspire Trout** can be found in the lakes of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).
- **Spotted Seatrout** can be found in the ocean around [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md)

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Creatures#Fish)<sup>([OBR](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion_Remastered))</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
