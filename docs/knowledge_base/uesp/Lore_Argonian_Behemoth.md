# Argonian Behemoth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/ON-creature-Xal-Nur_the_Slaver.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Xal-Nur_the_Slaver.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Xal-Nur_the_Slaver.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Xal-Nur_the_Slaver.jpg) Argonian Behemoth Argonian Behemoths,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSTAJP-2)</sup> also known as **Xal-Krona** ([Jel](Lore_Jel.md): *Sacred Behemoth*),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBritta-3)</sup> are hulking [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) most often found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Argonians can become Xal-Krona through the consumption of [Hist sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sali-Raniur-4)</sup> They possess immense physical strength and can spew corrosive sludge.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

Considered great warriors, their ample size and strength is bestowed upon them by the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DOS-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONKishi-7)</sup> They are often created for a reason: the [Dead-Water](Lore_Naga-Kur.md) Hist provided its tribe with a behemoth to test young warriors during their rite of passage.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONKishi-7)</sup> It is easy for one to underestimate the Xal-Krona's intelligence,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOHeem-8)</sup> but assuming their sheer size and brute force means they lack wit is a fatal mistake.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The Xal-Krona are not mindless monsters: they are more than capable of being sentimental or proud, and respect their tribes' traditions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBritta-3)</sup> They are capable of speech, but unlike regular Argonians, they usually form simplistic sentences and refer to themselves in the third person.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-XNXTT-10)</sup>

There are some variation present in Behemoths, such as a crimson red-scaled variety found in service to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), believed to have been corrupted and altered by [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) magic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DOS-1)</sup> Some behemoths have also been turned into [Soul Shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SSB-11)</sup> [Vahath Behemoths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vahath_Behemoth) are similar creatures of different origins.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SNTB-12)</sup>

An Argonian behemoth was rumored to be the captain of a pirate ship during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>
