# Shalidor's Insights (unused pages)

Shalidor's Insights **by [Shalidor](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Shalidor)** *A disquisition on the origin and nature of Dragons* *
---
Note:

The first split paragraph is the third song of the [Five Songs of King Wulfharth](Lore_Five_Songs_of_King_Wulfharth.md).

The second page seems to be a design document about the layout of Septimus Signus's Outpost, with three giant letters in the Magic Script reading "O L F" and the star of Lakshmi diagram containing a Q at the center, encircled clockwise (starting at the top) with the characters 2, 1, Z, X, Y, W, V and U.

The third page seems to be written in Portuguese (*"conduziu o aqui a este"*, meaning *"led here to this"*) and also talks about Septimus Signus (*"Skyrim. Septimus vive em"* meaning *"Skyrim. Septimus lives in"*), and might be a shorter excerpt of the previous design document. It also has a spiral showcasing the golden ratio, containing the letters S, T U, V and W (starting from the outside of the spiral). The text at the bottom remains undeciphered.

The fourth page is a diagram with three letters in the Magic Script (F, R and P) and yet undeciphered text all around.

The fifth page contains three texts, the first of which *"Finally, one dragon (that we know of) decided to join the human side — this was Paarthurnax. He's the Prometheus to the proto-Nords — he taught them to use the Voice. This turned the tide, and the proto-Nords continually defeated Alduin and the dragons and"* reads like a design document about Paarthurnax, followed by a text in the Magic Script saying *"Alduin's civilizaton was the Dragon Cult of Atmora. He's basically the Dragon God on earth. The dragon high priests are his acolytes and everything is ordered."* and a final text *"everything else. Alduin was the creator of dragon civi[liz]ation — the Firstborn and the smar[test.] [D]ragons were attracted to Human oppo(?) [b]ecause they shared the Human (?) of the world as a gift o[f] gods (even Alduin knows Akatosh created Mundus). (Or humans learned their"* which seems to be a continuation of the deciphered text from the second used page of the [Shalidor's Insights](Lore_Shalidor%27s_Insights.md) and to explain that either dragons found themselves ruling over humans because they shared a similar worldview, or that humans learned that worldview from dragons.

All of these read less like in-game texts meant to be read by the player and more like design documents used during game development and later repurposed as filler-words for obscure ancient texts with unreadable fonts that we were never really supposed to decipher.
