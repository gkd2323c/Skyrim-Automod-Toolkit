[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/AR-place-Ebon_Ro.png/200px-AR-place-Ebon_Ro.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Ebon_Ro.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Ebon_Ro.png) Ebon Ro (Arena) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/AR-map-Valenwood_towns.png/200px-AR-map-Valenwood_towns.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Valenwood_towns.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Valenwood_towns.png) Map of Valenwood Ebon Ro is a settlement located in the northern area of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), specifically northeast of [Vulkwasten Wood](Lore_Vulkwasten.md) and [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md) and west of [Thormar Keep](Lore_Thormar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Ebon Ro was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Lidell and had a rivalry with Stone Fell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, and had road connections to Silvenar, Vulkwasten Wood, and Thormar Keep.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Ebon Ro was set to appear in *[ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)* but it was later cut from the game. It was set to appear in the region of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md) as a settlement occupied by the Blacksap Rebellion.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ONCut-3)</sup>
