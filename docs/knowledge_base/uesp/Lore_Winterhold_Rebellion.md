# Winterhold Rebellion

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-place-Sky_Haven_Temple_Exterior.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sky_Haven_Temple_Exterior.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sky_Haven_Temple_Exterior.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sky_Haven_Temple_Exterior.jpg) Sky Haven Temple The Winterhold Rebellion (ca. [1E 2804](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2804) - [1E 2806](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2806))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ann DG-1)</sup> was a rebellion within the [Reman Empire](Lore_Reman_Empire.md).

## History
The primary cause of the rebellion was the taking of hostages from the [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) cities of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) and [Hroldan](Lore_Old_Hroldan.md) at the orders of the [Emperor Kastav](Lore_Kastav.md) in the year [1E 2801](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2801). The Winterhold Rebellion would start in [1E 2804](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2804). The Master of the [Akaviri Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) within [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) refused to aid Kastav in suppression of the rebellion, a choice praised by the Grandmaster of the Dragonguard, who agreed that obeying the Emperor would break their Oath of Allegiance. However the result of this choice was Emperor Kasav cutting off supplies for Sky Haven Temple.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ann DG-1)</sup>

Soon Sky Haven Temple found itself under siege from angered Nordic forces in the year [1E 2805](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2805), as a result of a non-dragonguard [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) by the name of [Kalien](Lore_Kalien.md) sacking the city of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold.md). This led to unrest and anger among the Dragonguard, who had been intentionally building trust with the people of Skyrim for years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ann DG-1)</sup>

The Rebellion and siege of Sky Haven Temple soon came to an end in the year [1E 2806](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2806), when [Reman II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reman_Cyrodiil_II) ascended to the throne, and perfomed his first notable act as Emperor, a swift and bloodless negotiation that ended all hostilities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTLOA-2)</sup>
