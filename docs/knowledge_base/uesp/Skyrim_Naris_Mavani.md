[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Naris_Mavani.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Naris_Mavani.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Naris_Mavani.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Naris_Mavani.jpg) Naris Mavani **Naris Mavani** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md). He appears after you reopen the mine.

Naris wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He carries his [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
