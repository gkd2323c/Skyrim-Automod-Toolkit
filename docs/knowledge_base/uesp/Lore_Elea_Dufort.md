Elea Dufort was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) Duchess of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) and the leader of [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was the daughter of Donoven Dufort, who was the first Duke of the Systres from House Dufort.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EJ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mt CSSM-2)</sup> She was the sister of [Count Damard Dufort](Lore_Damard_Dufort.md), who acted as her court mage.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CDD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Arabelle-4)</sup> Her residence was Castle Navire.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VGt HI-5)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md) emerged, Elea investigated and worked to combat it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elea-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DEIN-7)</sup> [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) traveled to Castle Navire to investigate the Order and questioned Elea.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-POI-8)</sup> Elea expressed suspicion that [House Mornard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mornard) was involved with the Ascendant Order because of the animosity between the Dufort and Mornard families.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elea-6)</sup> While inspecting her new flagship, the Silver Swan, Elea's steward, Hercian Stalo, locked her in her cabin aboard the ship on the orders of Damard, who was the Ascendant Magus, the second-in-command of the Ascendant Order.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elea-6)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAMC-9)</sup> Afterward, the Vestige rescued her.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TCAM-10)</sup> The Vestige later killed Damard, and Elea expressed regret over his death, but she realized there was no other way.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elea-6)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ACFP-11)</sup>

After Lady [Arabelle Davaux](Lore_Arabelle_Davaux.md) was poisoned by gifted wine, the Vestige learned that the order of wine was placed by Elea's seneschal; however, it was actually from the Ascendant Order.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-THL-12)</sup> Elea also received poisoned wine and a letter from the Ascendant Order, but Knight Commander Jourvel intervened, insisting that the gift be tested before consumption.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Elea-6)</sup> The Vestige later investigated the wine and letter and questioned Elea about them.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-THL-12)</sup> After Lord [Bacaro Volorus](Lore_Bacaro_Volorus.md) was revealed to be the Ascendant Lord and killed, Elea was present for the peace talks between the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), and [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) on All Flags Islet.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ANPP-13)</sup>
