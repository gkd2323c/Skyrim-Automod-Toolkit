I'd consider it a personal favor if I could once again utilize [Cynric](Skyrim_Cynric_Endell.md)'s unique skills for a delicate situation in the [Cidha Mines](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*. A close friend has been incarcerated in that horrible place, and I should like him freed as soon as possible. I'd recommend Cynric avoid the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) element that's prevalent within the mine and stick to as simple an escape plan as possible.
