[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-place-Saarthal.jpg/200px-SR-place-Saarthal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Saarthal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Saarthal.jpg) Saarthal [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg) An Ancient Frost Atronach Serenarth was an ancient [Snow Elf](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) wizard. He participated in the infamous [Night of Tears](Lore_Night_of_Tears.md) where the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elves.md) and [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) fought over the razing of [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTSkyrim-1)</sup>

According to the tales, [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md), with swift and accurate aim, fell Serenarth with his legendary bow [Long-Launcher](Lore_Long-Launcher.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTSkyrim-1)</sup>

As Serenarth lay dying, he made a bargain with unknown but malevolent forces to swap his spirit with that of a [Frost Atronach](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md). His host form still persists to this day, encased in ice within a glacier close to where his frozen corpse still lies with the threat of thawing out and exacting vengeance upon Ysgramor's heirs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTSkyrim-1)</sup>
