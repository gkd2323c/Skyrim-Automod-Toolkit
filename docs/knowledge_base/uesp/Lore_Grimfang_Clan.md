# Grimfang Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/ON-crown_store-Druadach_Mountain_Bear.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Druadach_Mountain_Bear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Druadach_Mountain_Bear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Druadach_Mountain_Bear.jpg) Druadach Mountain Bear [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg/200px-SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Druadach_Mountains.jpg) Druadach Mountains Grimfang Clan was one of numerous [clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). They were an ancient clan that inhabited the region of [Druadach Mountains](Lore_Druadach_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMB-1)</sup>

Grimfangs were known for taming and breeding Druadach Mountain [Bears](Lore_Bear.md) as mounts. Their bears were known for inherent docility but also were swiftly angered when threatened.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMB-1)</sup>

Grimfang Clan feuded with [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and endured numerous raids from their neighbors. The breed of bear they were famous for became popular among their enemies, who were known to capture them during their attacks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMB-1)</sup>

At some point in history, during the reign of the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) one of the Druadach Mountain Bears saved the life of Tirudilmo, an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) child, whose family moved into [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md). The same Altmer, in adulthood, kept a store in the city and was known as the Bear-Blessed among the locals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tirudilmo-2)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the Grimfang Clan was already considered ancient. Their status was unknown, but their Druadach Mountain Bears were popularized across the [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and available for purchase.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DMB-1)</sup>
