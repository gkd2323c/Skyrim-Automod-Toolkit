[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/ON-map-Vafe.jpg/200px-ON-map-Vafe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Vafe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Vafe.jpg) Map of Vafe circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Vafe** is the northernmost region of the island of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Vafe is the highest from sea level, and it is more evident as one ascends toward the capital of Auridon, [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md). The [Diceto River](Lore_Diceto_River.md) flows throughout Firsthold and empties into the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). The town of [Greenwater Cove](Lore_Greenwater_Cove.md) is a large port equal in size to the city of [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md). The College of Aldmeri Propriety is where the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) study to become fully-fledged members of the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), although it is mainly used for [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), and the lower class of the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).
