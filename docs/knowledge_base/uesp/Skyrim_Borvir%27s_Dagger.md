[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/SR-item-Elven_Dagger.jpg/200px-SR-item-Elven_Dagger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Elven_Dagger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Elven_Dagger.jpg) An Elven Dagger [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-npc-Borvir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Borvir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borvir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Borvir.jpg) Borvir and his dagger Borvir's Dagger is the weapon found beside its late eponymous owner, [Borvir](Skyrim_Borvir.md), inside [Journeyman's Nook](Skyrim_Journeyman%27s_Nook.md). Its appearance and stats are identical to those of an [elven dagger](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md), only being notable due to its unique name and owner. As with all elven daggers, it requires one [refined moonstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone) to [temper](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), with the benefits of the tempering being doubled with the [Elven Smithing](Skyrim_Elven_Smithing.md) perk.

The dagger's owner is one of the missing apprentices from the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). After going off on a private expedition to try to create a spectacular recipe for mead that could compete against the popular [Honningbrew Mead](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Mead.md), Borvir was killed by a volley of arrows inside Journeyman's Nook, having failed to complete his task.

## Notes
- This weapon was originally a quest item in the unused College of Winterhold quest, [The Missing Apprentices](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#The_Missing_Apprentices). For each of the missing apprentices, an item that would be identified as belonging to them (such as this dagger) would be taken to [Phinis Gestor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Phinis_Gestor), confirming their grim fate. Despite their quest not making it into the final game, the dagger and Borvir can still be found.
