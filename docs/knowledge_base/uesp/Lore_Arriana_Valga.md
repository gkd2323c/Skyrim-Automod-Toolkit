Arriana Valga was the Countess of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She married the hereditary Count of Chorrol, [Charus Valga](Lore_Charus_Valga.md), and they had one daughter, [Alessia](Lore_Alessia_Caro.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ob LS-3)</sup> Count Valga was killed in battle against [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) clansmen of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and his death was greatly lamented by the Countess and the people of Chorrol.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup> After Charus' death, Arriana became the ruling Countess of Chorrol.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ob LS-3)</sup> The Count's death greatly affected her, as on many a lonely night she would speak to the portrait of him that hung in her bedchamber.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CAV-4)</sup>

During her reign, Valga was regarded as a dignified, righteous, and reliable ruler.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Obrs-5)</sup> She was known as a devout follower of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) and frequently attended the [Chapel of Stendarr](Lore_Temple_of_Stendarr.md) in Chorrol.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup> Around [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), Alessia married the Count of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md), [Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md), and she dutifully visited Arriana every month.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE3C-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HM-7)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Chorrol's court mage, Chanel, stole the portrait of Count Valga from the Countess' bedchamber.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CAV-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-C-8)</sup> Valga was devastated by the loss of the portrait and launched an investigation into the theft.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CAV-4)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ct C-9)</sup> It is unknown if the painting was ever recovered. During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), Valga, unlike the other counts and countesses of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), did not have a strong opinion of High Chancellor [Ocato](Lore_Ocato_of_Firsthold.md)'s handling of it.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CAV-4)</sup> After an Oblivion Gate opened outside of Chorrol's south gate, she warned the people of Chorrol to stay inside the walls, and she put the city guard on full alert.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CAV-4)</sup> However, some of Chorrol's residents felt that the Countess was more worried about finding the portrait of Count Valga than the security of the city.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Bt CB-10)</sup> When the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) closed the Oblivion Gate outside of Chorrol, Valga provided military aid for the defense of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Af B-11)</sup>
