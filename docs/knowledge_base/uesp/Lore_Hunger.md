# Hunger

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SI-creature-Ravenous_Hunger.jpg/200px-SI-creature-Ravenous_Hunger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-creature-Ravenous_Hunger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-creature-Ravenous_Hunger.jpg) A Hunger (Shivering Isles) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/ON-load-Depths_of_Malatar.jpg/200px-ON-load-Depths_of_Malatar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Depths_of_Malatar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Depths_of_Malatar.jpg) A Hunger in Malathar Hungers are ferocious [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) known for their long tongues, demonic appearance, and their taste for mortal flesh.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTirvina-1)</sup> The Hunger uses its long tongue to siphon the life out of its prey.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOHunger-2)</sup> It also makes use of its razor-sharp claws and can spew poisonous bile.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWHunger-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOHunger-2)</sup> They possess a paralyzing touch and have the uncanny ability to quickly destroy their opponents' armor and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWHunger-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DD-4)</sup> Hungers can also teleport.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOHunger-2)</sup> In Morrowind, they are typically associated with the Daedric Prince [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTirvina-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DD-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> who is also known as *Hunger* by the Dunmer.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TCC-6)</sup> They are also known to roam the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) as servitors and guards.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TSB-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Shiv Hunger-8)</sup> They are described by some as perfect representation of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s darker side.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TSB-7)</sup> They are also found in the realms of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) and [the Gorge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorge).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Hungers have been observed to be more ferocious when in the presence of a warm body which is described as a territorial hunting imperative.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

Their [tongues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hunger_Tongue) can be used by alchemists as an ingredient in potions to cure diseases and aid those who are afflicted by poison.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> Their tongues are also used by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) as hair combs.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> The claws of hungers have been known to be used to make filleting knives.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
