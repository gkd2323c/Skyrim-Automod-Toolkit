Thules the Gibbering was a [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenay.md) [witch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Witch) -warrior who assumed control of the weakened [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) at some point during the [Stormcrown Interregnum](Lore_Stormcrown_Interregnum.md) in the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:96</sup>

He was not a well-liked ruler, and he was known for having various perversions. [Hierem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hierem), who came from an old and well-connected Nibenese family, served as a minister to Thules.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:96</sup> Circa [4E 22](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_22), the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) warlord [Titus Mede](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md) captured the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) with a force of less than a thousand men, and he deposed Thules.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:96</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup><sup>:109</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RT4-3)</sup> Although Thules was not a well-liked ruler, since he was Nibenese, the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) favored him over a Colovian usurper. Hierem eventually convinced the Elder Council to accept Titus Mede as a liberator rather than a conqueror, and he was crowned [Emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>:96</sup>
