## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The exterior of the huge mead hall is Sovngarde. The whalebone bridge leading to the hall is guarded by Tsun. You must battle him to prove your worth and be allowed to proceed across the bridge. On the far side, a paved terrace features two curved flights of stairs ascending to a row of four doors that all lead into the hall.

### Hall of Valor
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_East_Wing.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_East_Wing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_East_Wing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_East_Wing.jpg) East Feasting Wing [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_Central_Area.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_Central_Area.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_Central_Area.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_Central_Area.jpg) Central Feasting Area [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_West_Wing.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_West_Wing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_West_Wing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hall_of_Valor_West_Wing.jpg) West Sparring Wing Although the interior of the Hall consists of only one zone, it can be visually divided into three areas. The entrance leads into the central area, with huge chambers to the west and east. Throughout the hall are many [Heroes of Sovngarde](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hero_of_Sovngarde), as well as several important people from Skyrim's history. When you first enter, [Ysgramor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ysgramor) will approach and greet you, stating that three heroes have awaited your arrival to aid you in the battle with Alduin. These are the three you saw defeat Alduin in a vision from the past when using the [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll_(Dragon).md): [Felldir the Old](Skyrim_Felldir_the_Old.md), [Gormlaith Golden-Hilt](Skyrim_Gormlaith_Golden-Hilt.md), and [Hakon One-Eye](Skyrim_Hakon_One-Eye.md).

The central area is mainly for feasting. A huge raised fire pit is in the middle of the area, with two whole oxen turning on a spit. A long table is on the south side of the fire pit near the entrance, while another is on the north side on a raised section in front of Shor's empty throne. Both tables are laid with golden plates, platters, and goblets; the [food](Skyrim_Food.md) and drink found on these tables and elsewhere in the hall is listed below. Many small empty barrels and three large mead kegs line the walls. Heroes come here to feast and enjoy the music of the [Hero Bard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hero_of_Sovngarde), who mainly plays the [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) and [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute).

The west wing is a vast bare chamber reserved for sparring matches. Heroes who belong to the [Sovngarde Sparring Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:MQSovngarde Sparring Faction) come here to quench their thirst for battle, uncertain why Shor has forbidden engaging Alduin in combat. Raised sections along the north and south walls with tables and rows of benches allow spectators to watch the matches. Stairs to the west allow access to the raised sections, and two large mead kegs are against the west wall.

The east wing is also for feasting, with large mead kegs against the walls and two small tables and a horseshoe of longer tables in the middle. All of the tables are laid with more golden crockery. Heroes come here to feast and enjoy the music of the [Hero Drummer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hero_of_Sovngarde), who prefers this hall for his performances.

## Notes
- If you attempt to cross the bridge to the hall without dueling Tsun, you will be killed by lightning strikes on the bridge, even in [god mode](Skyrim_Console.md). You will also automatically die if you fall into the chasm beneath the bridge, though not in god mode.
- Inside the hall, you are unable to sneak, wait, or draw or equip your weapon. (Unless you use `coc Sovngarde01` to teleport there, since the effect is triggered by interacting with the door.)
- Inside the hall, none of the golden crockery can be taken.
- After slaying Alduin, the hall will be locked, and you cannot reenter.

## Bugs
