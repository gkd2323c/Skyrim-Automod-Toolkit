# Orgnium

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/ON-item-armor-Pyandonean_Heavy_Female.jpg/200px-ON-item-armor-Pyandonean_Heavy_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Pyandonean_Heavy_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Pyandonean_Heavy_Female.jpg) A set of Pyandonean heavy armor Orgnium is a mysterious metal used by the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md). Easily recognizable for its light weight and teal color, it is used extensively in their heavier [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor; notable examples of which include cuirasses and shoulder armor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM64P-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Its name is likely attributed to King [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md), ruler of the Pyandonea.
- In many ways, it can be seen as the Maormeri alternative to Altmeri Elven Armor.
