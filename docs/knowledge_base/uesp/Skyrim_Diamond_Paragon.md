[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg/200px-SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Diamond_Paragon.jpg) The Diamond Paragon The Diamond Paragon is found in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md) on a [frost giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md) after traversing the normally accessible Glacial Crevice and before the Shrine of Radiance. Turn left up a path into mountains immediately upon departure from Glacial Crevice and before running into the second part of the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md)'s mountain homesteads. Two leveled Falmer must be killed and past their little outpost should be the frost giant, wandering about among several dead Falmer. The paragon will create an opening to an area above the Glacial Crevice if placed into the gem holder for the [Paragon Portal](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform), and it will have a single chest with leveled loot.
