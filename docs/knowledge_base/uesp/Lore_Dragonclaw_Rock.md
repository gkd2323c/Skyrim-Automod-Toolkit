[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/ON-place-Dragonclaw_Rock_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Dragonclaw_Rock_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dragonclaw_Rock_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dragonclaw_Rock_02.jpg) Dragonclaw Rock circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Dragonclaw Rock is a landmark found in the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is a rock formation resembling a giant [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md)'s claw. It is said that the [Way of the Voice](Lore_Way_of_the_Voice.md) is exceptionally strong here.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGDragon-1)</sup>

The landmark was used as a navigational tool by the invading [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) army when they were headquartered at [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) during the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) invasion of [1E 2703](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2703).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), an expansive and strategically important fortress known as **Fort Dragonclaw** stood at the grounds. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was initially located within the territory held by the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), although the keep changed hands many times over the course of the war. When it was still active, the fort was part of the [Transitus Network](Lore_Transitus_Network.md), which connected it across other strongholds in central Cyrodiil.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), knowing of his impending death, the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) god [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) journeyed to Dragonclaw Rock to use it as a hiding place for [Keening II](Lore_Keening_II.md), a replica of the ancient blade [Keening](Lore_Keening.md) which he had created to manipulate the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LGThrone-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGDragon-1)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), nothing remained of the keep. Having come into the possession of an ancient Akaviri messenger's diary, Countess [Narina Carvain](Lore_Narina_Carvain.md) of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md) enlisted the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) in retracing the messenger's steps to confirm her belief that she had rediscovered the location of Pale Pass. Her theory was proven correct when the Hero made use of local landmarks, including Dragonclaw Rock, to reach Pale Pass.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Oblivion-5)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) used a system of portals within [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) known as the Doors of Egress to track down Sotha Sil's replica tools.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LGThrone-4)</sup> However, when the Hero arrived at Dragonclaw Rock in search of Keening II, a dragon attacked. Although this Stoneclaw Dragon was a master of the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) who had been trained in ages past by [Paarthurnax](Lore_Paarthurnax.md) himself, it quickly fell, allowing the Hero to locate Sotha Sil's hiding place.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGDragon-1)</sup>
