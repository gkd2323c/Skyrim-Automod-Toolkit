[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Keerava.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Keerava.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Keerava.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Keerava.jpg) Keerava in the Bee and Barb **Keerava** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) innkeeper in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She tends [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) alongside [Talen-Jei](Skyrim_Talen-Jei.md).

Although she has a bed and a room in the inn, she never sleeps and is found behind the bar almost every moment of every day.

She wears some barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff38) and some [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e). She wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bee_and_Barb_Key) to her inn, and her own [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Keerava%27s_Key).

## Related Quests
- **[A Cornered Rat](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md)**: Locate [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md).
- **[Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love)**: Distribute religious [literature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara) to the citizens of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).
- **[Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md)**: Collect some debts for the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"I can't stand this city. It's impossible to make a decent living here."* *"If you've got the coin, you're welcome here. Otherwise, hit the road."* *"If you've got the coin, you've come to the right place. Pull up a seat."* If you've muscled her into paying Brynjolf's debt, she'll greet you with:

*"Oh, it's you again. What in blazes do you want?"* She often checks up on you.

*"What can I get you? Mead? Wine?"* *"Looking for a place to stay?"* If you ask her where you can learn more about magic, she'll point you in the right direction.

**Where can I learn more about magic? ** *"Head north to [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). There's a [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) there, they teach magic. Most Nords don't care for them.
Else you could go see the Jarl's [wizard](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md). Might be able to help you."* **What's the word around town?** *"Riften's biggest exports used to be fishing and honey, until the Black-Briar family moved in. Now the meadery is the biggest game in town."* *"The city guard has been tearing Riften apart looking for the source of our [skooma problem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand). I'd hate to be in their shoes if they get discovered."* *"[Sibbi Black-Briar](Skyrim_Sibbi_Black-Briar.md) is furious that his [ex-lover](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynly_Star-Sung) tried to murder him. He's leaving no stone unturned [searching for her](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lover%27s_Requital)."* *"[Mjoll the Lioness](Skyrim_Mjoll_the_Lioness.md) used to adventure across Skyrim before she settled down in Riften. I hear it all ended when she lost her [enchanted blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grimsever)."* *"If [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) keeps raising prices on mead, I'll have to resort to watering it down just to make ends meet."* *"If it's true that the dragons have returned, what will it mean for all of us? Is this how it all ends?"* If the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) is still on hard times, she'll say

*"The Thieves Guild has been pushing hard to regain a foothold in this city. Rumor has it they're falling apart from the inside."* If they're back on their feet, she'll say

*"Crime is at an all-time high in this city thanks to the Thieves Guild. Don't we have enough problems with the [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) going on?"* Exiting conversation:

*"Come back and see us again."* Exiting conversation if you muscled her debt to Brynjolf out of her:

*"Door's over there. Why don't you use it?"* Occasionally, she'll call out to Talen-Jei.

*"Keep your scales on."* Sometimes, she'll call him over to serve a customer.

*"Hey, Talen... get off your lazy tail and take care of the customer!"*
### Taking Care of Business
When you approach her for coin, she'll have some different greetings.

*"Here for a room or something to drink? Show me some coin first."* *"You want something or not?"* **I have a message for you from Brynjolf.** *"I'll * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * already told that buffoon I'm not paying you people a single coin!"* **It was never a request.** *"Look around you, I'm barely keeping this rathole together. The war's seen to that."* **It's out of my hands.** *"Then go back to your boss and tell him what I've already told him so many times, I lose count.
His request is denied... I'm not paying him any more gold. This madness has to end."* You can give up.

**I'm finished wasting my time talking to you.** *"Good, and take this little message back to your bosses.
I'm not paying any of you a single septim... ever! Now get out of my inn!"* You can brawl to get the money out of her.

**Don't say I didn't warn you. (Brawl)** *"Come on, give it your best shot!"* When you beat her she'll plead:

*"Please, stop! I'll pay! Take it!"* If you take the route suggested by Brynjolf and talk to Talen-Jei first:

**Maybe I should visit that farm in Morrowind.** *"How could you possibly know about... Please. My family means too much to me. Don't hurt them."* **Then pay up and I'll forget about it.** *"Very well. Here. Take this back to Brynjolf and tell him he'll have no more trouble from me."* If you have already visited the [other](Skyrim_Haelga.md) [two](Skyrim_Bersi_Honey-Hand.md) merchants, she'll immediately hand over the cash, saying

*"Look, everything was all just a misunderstanding. I didn't mean to tell Brynjolf to go jump off the pier. You'll tell him I said sorry, yes?
Take this. Every single coin I owe is there, I swear it."* If you exit conversation before taking the money and re-enter conversation, she says:

*"I'm not paying you or Brynjolf a single coin! Now get out of my tavern!"* *"I said get out! I'm not paying a single coin!"*
### A Cornered Rat
You can ask her about [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md).

**I'm looking for an old guy, hiding out somewhere in Riften.** *"Never heard of anybody like that. But if you want to hide out in Riften, the Ratway's where you'd be.
There's a sort of tavern down there, [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). Where all the lowlifes in Riften get drunk and knife each other.
You might try there, if you can get through the Ratway alive."*
### Spread the Love
You can give her a [pamphlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara).

**Here, sister. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"Look, the only religion I believe in is the good old septim. Everything else is just a waste."*
## Conversations
When you first enter The Bee and Barb, she can be seen having the following argument with [Maramal](Skyrim_Maramal.md):

**Maramal:** *"People of Riften, heed my words. The return of the dragons is not mere coincidence."* **Maramal:** *"This is one of the signs. The signs that Lady Mara is displeased with your constant inebriation."* **Maramal:** *"Put down your flagons filled with your vile liquids and embrace the teachings of the handmaiden of Kyne."* **Keerava:** *"No, no... Maramal... we talked about this. Talen..."* **Maramal:** *"Keerava, certainly we can come to an understanding. These people must be made aware of the chaos they've sown."* **Talen-Jei:** *"Enough, Maramal. We've all heard of the dragons and their return. There's no need to use them as an excuse to harass our customers."* **Maramal:** *"Very well, Talen. I'll remove myself from this den of iniquity."* **Talen-Jei:** *"We're not kicking you out, just keep the sermons at the temple and let us all sin in peace."* Keerava can often be seen conversing with Talen-Jei or her customers:

**Talen-Jei:** *"Keerava, we're running low on Black-Briar Mead."* **Keerava:** *"If only Maven would stop raising the price on it maybe I could afford to stock more cases."* **Talen-Jei:** *"We could always look for another brand."* **Keerava:** *"That isn't an option. Maven sees me serve a competitor's brand here and we may as well dig our own graves."* They talk about their finances.

**Keerava:** *"Don't worry, the time will come when we can walk away from here."* **Talen-Jei:** *"I hope so. I can't stand this city anymore."* **Keerava:** *"Damn it, Talen. We still don't have enough this year to get out of Riften."* They'll also talk about the [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md).

**Keerava:** *"Talen, do you believe what they're saying? Do you really think the dragons have returned?"* **Talen-Jei:** *"I don't know what to believe. If it's true, then dark times may be upon us."* **Keerava:** *"What should we do?"* **Talen-Jei:** *"We hold each other close and hope that all of this ends well."* She can be seen having a conversation with [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md).

**Indaryn:** *"Why don't they listen to me, Keerava? Why won't they do as I say?"* **Keerava:** *"Maybe they're afraid of you. Maybe it's your breath. How should I know?"* **Indaryn:** *"They always try to take advantage of me. Give them an inch and they'll take a mile."* **Keerava:** *"I'll tell you one thing, if you don't make peace with them soon you may get a walkout on your hands. Then we're both in trouble."* She can also be seen speaking to [Romlyn Dreth](Skyrim_Romlyn_Dreth.md).

**Romlyn:** *"Have I told you about [Valen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Valen_Dreth), my dear?"* **Keerava:** *"I love when you spin that yarn. Gets better every time you add something new."* **Romlyn:** *"He killed six Imperial Guards before they dragged him into the prisons."* **Keerava:** *"Was this before or after the Mythic Dawn attacked the Emperor and Valen single-handedly fought them off? Just shut up and drink, Romlyn."*
