[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/ON-place-Sword%27s_Rest_Isle.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sword%27s_Rest_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sword%27s_Rest_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sword%27s_Rest_Isle.jpg) Sword's Rest Isle ca. 2E 582 Sword's Rest Isle (or simply **Sword's Rest**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valessea-1)</sup> is an island located off the coast of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), under the government of the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). During the rule of High King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) in [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) it served as a getaway for the monarch whenever he wanted to escape the daily pressures of running the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) alliance. [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) unit was stationed on the island to provide protection. It contains a luxurious complex of facilities designed to ensure the comfort of the monarch during their stay on the island.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRI-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GSV-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOJAKARN-4)</sup> King's chambers were additionally protected by magical wards.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAC-5)</sup>

## History
During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the isle was infiltrated by the members of the anti-monarchist faction [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md) who conspired against the leaders of the alliances, including king Emeric.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ACNO-6)</sup> They attempted to assassinate the king during his stay on the island. Their efforts were stopped by [Arabelle Davaux](Lore_Arabelle_Davaux.md) and Jakarn of the [Society of the Steadfast](Lore_Society_of_the_Steadfast.md) aided by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who killed the Ascendant Order Champion and her subordinates before they could reach the monarch.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AKR-7)</sup>
