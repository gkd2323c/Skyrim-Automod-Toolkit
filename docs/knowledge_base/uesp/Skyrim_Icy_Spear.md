- *A spear of ice that does 60 points of frost damage to Health and Stamina.*

**Icy Spear** is an expert level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to health and stamina, and slows targets' movement for 3 seconds.

## Effects
- [Icy Spear](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 60 pts
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md), 50 pts for 3 secs
- [Deep Freeze Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 3 secs; if the [Deep Freeze](Skyrim_Deep_Freeze.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Augmented_Frost.md), raises damage to 75 at first rank or 90 at second rank

## Notes
- Icy Spear is essentially just a much more powerful (and expensive) version of [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md). It also comes with a swirl of frost behind the projectile, and if cast in first-person, it briefly lightens the screen with a pale bluish white glow. It is also the only Expert-level Destruction spell that does not cause the screen to shake when cast.
- Like most Frost spells, it can be used to set off [Frost Runes](Skyrim_Frost_Rune.md) from a safe distance.
- Icy Spears which hit enemies will continue to exist after they die until you leave the area. This makes the corpses easy to spot from a distance for looting. The only exceptions to this are enemies which dissolve or break apart when they die, such as [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md), [storm atronachs](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md) and those raised with most [reanimation spells](Skyrim_Reanimate.md).
