Mountain Lions (also known as **pumas** or **cougars**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ABig Catof Some Kind-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> are a type of large, predatory [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in the wilderness of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and parts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITLD-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-2)</sup> They are sometimes referred to in shorthand as simply **lions**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Pelt-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Molag-7)</sup> but they are not to be confused with actual [lions](Lore_Lion.md) or [senche-lions](Lore_Senche-Lion.md).

Many of the cats known as cougars in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Cougar**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-2)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-8)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-8)</sup> Senche-Cougars dwell specifically in east central Elsweyr, where they prowl in the cliffs and escarpments for [goats](Lore_Goat.md) and [marmots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Marmot).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-2)</sup> Many [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have puma-like fur patterns.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ABig Catof Some Kind-1)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/OBR-item-Lion_Pelt.jpg/200px-OBR-item-Lion_Pelt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Lion_Pelt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Lion_Pelt.jpg) Mountain Lion Pelt
(Oblivion Remastered) Mountain lion pelts are fairly valuable,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Pelt-6)</sup> and can be used in rituals to summon [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Molag-7)</sup> Mountain lions are often hunted in [County Anvil](Lore_County_Anvil.md), where they are often found in large numbers.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Mountain lions are an occasional subject of taxidermy; a stuffed mountain lion was one of the trophies displayed in [Battlehorn Castle](Lore_Battlehorn_Castle.md) after its restoration.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

The valley [Lion's Den](Lore_Lion%27s_Den.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) was a historical breeding ground for mountain lions, which the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) of the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) often hunted for pelts. Over the years, locals avoided the area in favor of harvesting furs from domestic livestock.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITLD-5)</sup>

Senche-Cougars have been domesticated by the Khajiit and are used as mounts.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-2)</sup> Mountain lions are often captured for use in gladiatorial arenas, such as in the famous [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) [Arena](Lore_Imperial_City.md#Arena_District).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-12)</sup>
