## Walkthrough
Two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and two [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) occupy the camp and the nearby area, with one giant in the camp and the other giant herding the mammoths to the northeast. A large campfire is in the middle of the camp, with two [charred skeevers](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) on fire-hardened stakes cooking over it. Typical of giant camps, many painted stones, [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md), and mammoth ribs and skulls are found throughout the area. North of the campfire is an empty stone bowl with a [leather strip](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md), a random [healing potion](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Minor_Healing), and a random [fortify skill potion](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Alteration) beside it. To the left is another stone bowl holding many [soul gem fragments](Skyrim_Soul_Gem_Fragment.md) and a random [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). Nearby to the north is a tree stump with a [mora tapinella](Skyrim_Mora_Tapinella.md) fungus and a [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). To the left of the chest is a large painted stone with several giant clubs, a few leather strips, a random [warhammer](Skyrim_Warhammer.md), and a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) displayed on it. On the other side of the display is a [mammoth cheese bowl](Skyrim_Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl.md).

South of the display are a couple of drying racks with a bunch of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md) hanging from them. A large mammoth cheese bowl is between the racks. East of the drying racks and past the path to the south are another two racks. Behind them is a tree, and behind that is a high ledge where two [iron ore veins](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) can be found. Farther east from the racks is another rack with a bunch of dried elves ear and another mammoth cheese bowl nearby. Beyond this is a large rock formation, with a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) on a low ledge to the northeast. Beneath the rock is a pool of water fed by a wellspring. Submerged in front of the wellspring is a random [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md), a [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), and an apprentice-locked chest.

## Notes
- There is a high chance of the camp being attacked by [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). If any survive the initial attack, they will flee toward [Orotheim](Skyrim_Orotheim.md).
- Talking Stone Camp has more insect spawn points than any other location, with twenty-two total, allowing you to collect many [butterfly wings](Skyrim_Butterfly_Wing.md) and [blue butterfly wings](Skyrim_Blue_Butterfly_Wing.md) during the day and [luna moth wings](Skyrim_Luna_Moth_Wing.md) and [torchbug thoraxes](Skyrim_Torchbug_Thorax.md) at night. Twenty-three [tundra cotton](Skyrim_Tundra_Cotton.md) plants also grow near the camp.
- A stream flowing east from the pool leads to a waterfall above an inlet to [River Hjaal](Skyrim_River_Hjaal.md). Just above the waterfall is another iron ore vein. The corpse of an [alchemist](Skyrim_Alchemist.md) lies on an island in the middle of the inlet.
- A dirt path leading west from the campfire leads to a small stream. On the other side of the stream is another iron ore vein.
