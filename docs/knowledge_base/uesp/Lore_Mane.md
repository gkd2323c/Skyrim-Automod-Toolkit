*Under the Hidden Moon
Then shall be born the Mane
Lion Lords and natural leaders
They carry the burden of all
Only one will be chosen
To wear the Crown of Braids
But though forgotten or remembered
Each Mane will forever alter
The Fate of All Khajiit.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:All_Our_Perfect_Forms)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/LO-book-The_Mane.jpg/200px-LO-book-The_Mane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-book-The_Mane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-book-The_Mane.jpg) A Mane The Mane is a unique type of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) designated as the spiritual leader and temporal ruler of the Khajiit people, who lives with his tribe in the stately and exotic palaces of [Torval](Lore_Torval.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1EC-1)</sup> Khajiit tradition holds that only one Mane can be alive at one time and, more specifically, believe that there is actually only one Mane who is reborn again and again in different bodies. There has been no recorded incident of more than one Mane contending for power, although whether due to the truth in the Khajiit belief or whether the ruling Mane takes care of any potential rivals is unknown. Manes can only be born under a rare alignment of the moons [Masser](Lore_Masser.md) and [Secunda](Lore_Secunda.md) when, according to legend, a third moon actually appears. In older days the Khajiit would shave off their manes in deference to the Mane, braiding them into locks which the Mane would incorporate into its own mane/headdress.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONKhali-2)</sup> The Mane is so weighted down by the hair that movement is difficult without aid and they often travel the countryside by means of a palanquin. As the population grew, however, this became impractical.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONKhali-2)</sup> As a result, the headdress now only includes hair of the Mane's tribe and [Warrior Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Warrior_Guard) attached to it, which includes several hundred in number.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1EC-1)</sup>

The Moon Hallowed is a champion blessed by past Manes who arrive in times of peril. They guide the future Manes through their initiation<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONKhali-2)</sup> which is known as the Paths. It ends with the final path in the Temple of Two Moons Path, in the city of [Dune](Lore_Dune.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKauzan-3)</sup>

Those born under the sign of the eclipse not chosen to be the Mane would instead be known as Forgotten Manes. Most of these Khajiit would be given to the Pride of Alkosh in [Pridehome](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pridehome) and be raised as warriors, ready to serve [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Alkosh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alkosh) in times of great need.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TPo A-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-P:APOT-5)</sup> In [2E582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E582), a Forgotten Mane by the name of Ra'khajin left the temple in order to serve the dragon [Laatvulon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Laatvulon) as his [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md), and would later return to raze his former home to the ground. <sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TPo Aq-6)</sup>

Those born under the eclipse are more subjectable to the moons, and thus the [Dark Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dark_Moon). The [Dark Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dark_Heart) consequently calls to them to the Bent Dance that threatens to turn them to [dro-m'Athra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dro-m%27Athra), as with the case of the Forgotten Mane [Ja'darri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ja%27darri),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONZa.27ji-7)</sup> who managed to ward off the corruption.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONJa.27darri-8)</sup> Others are unsuccessful, with the Dark Mane being a prime example. He listened "too much to the Darks", and thus his soul was claimed by [Namiira](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Namiira).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MBHAYQ-9)</sup> Tainted, he was no longer fit to lead the Khajiit as a spiritual leader, and was "cast out of the Moon's light, to walk forever in darkness."<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONAkkhuz-ri-10)</sup>

In or around [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48) the Mane was assassinated by the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md), sinking the south of Elsweyr into chaos.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TIC-11)</sup><sup>:179</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup>

## Previous Manes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/LO-people-Nhad-hatta.png/150px-LO-people-Nhad-hatta.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-people-Nhad-hatta.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-people-Nhad-hatta.png) Mane Nhad-hatta, wearing the braids and hair-locks of his entire tribe - [Tullar-dra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tullar-dra): Unspecified. Reigned in ancient times when the Golden Claw was established as a holy relic in Do'Krin Monastery.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONAzahrr-13)</sup>
- [Jinninji-ri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jinninji-ri): ca. [1E 2902](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2902)<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONShazah-14)</sup>
- [Zebiden-jo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zebiden-jo): ca. [2E 182](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_182)<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONShazah-14)</sup>
- [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta), the First Mane of Elsweyr: ca. [2E 309](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_309)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1EC-1)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-PGE3E-15)</sup>
- Dark Mane: Unspecified. Reigned in ancient times over the Khajiiti people until he fell into the Bent Dance.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONAkkhuz-ri-10)</sup>
- Akkhuz-ri: ca. [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582)<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONAkkhuz-ri-10)</sup>
- The Lunar Champion: [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582)<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>
- [Nhad-hatta](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nhad-hatta): ca. [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1EC-1)</sup>
- Unnamed Mane: ca. [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432). Reigned in the late Third Era and negotiated with Count [Marius Caro](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Marius_Caro) of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) on the long disputed border between Elsweyr and Cyrodiil. The Mane's actions were opposed by the [Renrijra Krin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Renrijra_Krin).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-PGE3E-15)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-AT-16)</sup>
- Unnamed Mane: [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). Reigned in the Fourth Era after the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) and was assassinated.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TIC-11)</sup><sup>:Pt. 2, Ch. 9</sup>

## Notes

- [Michael Kirkbride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Kirkbride) described the original ideas for the Mane as a cat sitting on a tall, wheeled platform hidden by the countless hair-locks, with each Mane varying and being "absolutely outlandish and unique". This could consist of "overwhelming hair-locks, mobile beds, clouds of moon sugar blowing out when they speak, and then go nuts with individual ornamentation".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-12)</sup>
