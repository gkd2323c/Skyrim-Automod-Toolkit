## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read the *[Letter on Mudcrab Attacks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_on_Mudcrab_Attacks)*.
2. Travel to the shack west of Dawnstar.
3. Kill the 12 Frenzied Mudcrabs.
4. Search [Seviur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seviur) for his [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seviur%27s_Journal) and read it.
5. Return to Viriya.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-quest-Crustacean_Extermination_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crustacean_Extermination_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crustacean_Extermination_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crustacean_Extermination_02.jpg) Swarm of frenzied mudcrabs This quest can be started after [No Contest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:No_Contest) has been completed by talking to Viriya:

- **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
- *"Maybe you can help me. You're headed to Dawnstar. It's all in this note. This won't be easy, but that's why I'm sending you. Think you can handle it?"*
- **I'm always up for one of your crab dilemmas.** / **Just point me toward something with claws, and I'll make it dead.**
- *"Thanks."*

Either option leads to the same outcome. Viriya will hand you a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_on_Mudcrab_Attacks) that mentions multiple accounts of fishermen being attacked near a small shack on the shore west of Dawnstar by a swarm of mudcrabs. Travel to the shack, west of Dawnstar to investigate. Once you arrive at the shack [frenzied mudcrabs](Skyrim_Frenzied_Mudcrab.md) will begin to swarm you, kill all twelve of them.

Once you have killed all the mudcrabs, investigate the body of [Seviur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seviur) to find his [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seviur%27s_Journal). Read the journal and you will discover that Seviur was using pacification magic and necromancy on mudcrabs which started to fail, until eventually the mudcrabs turned on him in a frenzy. You can then return to Viriya to speak to her:

- **The mudcrabs have been slain. They were driven into a fury by a necromancer.**
- *"We sure show them, eh? Thanks for your part in all that."*
- **Seviur drew them to the coast using magic, but he lost control over them.**
- *"I understand. I've come to rely on you. There's something I want you to have. It was my father's. It would be my honor if you carried it. May the gods keep you."*
- **This Alik'ri rod is fit for a master fisherman. Thank you, Viriya.**
- *"Stay sharp out there."*

You will be rewarded with an [Alik'ri Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Alik%27ri_Fishing_Rod.md) and the ability to craft the rod.
