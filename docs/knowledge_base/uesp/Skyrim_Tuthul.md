[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-npc-Tuthul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tuthul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tuthul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tuthul.jpg) Tuthul warming himself by the fire **Tuthul** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) located in the [Miner's House](Skyrim_Miner%27s_House.md) right by [Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole.md), in the eastern part of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md).

He wakes up at 7am and always starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. If the mine's still closed, he hangs around his house all day, eating one-hour meals at noon and 5pm, and goes to bed at midnight. If the mine has been cleared of draugr, he will head in there at 8am and work there for twelve hours, interrupted only by his usual meals back in the house.

He wears some [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080697) and some [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699). He wields a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), and carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Soljund%27s_Miner%27s_House.md) to his house as well as some [lower-class items and a bit of gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole_(quest).md)**: Defeat the [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) in [Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole.md).

## Dialogue
Before the mines are reopened, he'll talk about the [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) in the mine, saying:

- *"We tunneled into an old crypt. We're warning strangers to keep out."*
- *"I'm not going into the mine with draugr lurking down there."*
- *"Guards are useless. They just stand around waiting for a full regiment to come and help."*

When the mine is reopened, he'll still be rife with worry about the draugr, saying:

- *"Any day now more draugr are going to come out of that crypt."*
- *"This mine will be our grave yet. I just know it."*
- *"It's crazy to still be working here. What if there's more draugr down there?"*

## Conversations
At the Miner's House, he can often be seen having conversations with his boss, [Perth](Skyrim_Perth.md), worrying about the draugr:

- **Tuthul**: *"Those draugr are going to be back. I can feel it."*
- **Perth**: *"Don't be such a coward. We haven't seen any draugr since the mine was cleared. Be grateful."*

- **Tuthul**: *"I don't think it's safe mining a cave on top of that crypt."*
- **Perth**: *"There's a war on. We need every scrap of ore we can get, so we're going to keep digging. Understand?"*
- **Tuthul**: *"Fine, but don't say I didn't warn you."*

When the mines are reopened, he can also be heard having the following conversations in the mine:

- **Perth**: *"Feels good to be mining again."*
- **Tuthul**: *"How can you say that? This mine was filled with draugr not too long ago."*

- **Tuthul**: *"I feel like any moment, a draugr is going to come and chop my head off."*
- **Perth**: *"Steel your nerves. The draugr are gone. Focus on your mining."*
