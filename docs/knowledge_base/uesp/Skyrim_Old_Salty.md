[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-creature-Old_Salty.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Old_Salty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Old_Salty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Old_Salty.jpg) Old Salty **Old Salty** is a unique [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) found at [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md). In addition to the standard [mudcrab chitin](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Chitin.md) and [mudcrab legs](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Legs.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, it carries a few odd items: a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md#0005b69e), a [tankard](Skyrim_Tankard.md), 35 gold coins, and a random [gem](Skyrim_Gems.md).

## Notes
- Old Salty also appears in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*, as the card [Old Salty's Assault](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Old Salty's Assault).
- Old Salty has similarities to the [Mudcrab Merchant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Mudcrab_Merchant) from *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* due to the fact that it has a tankard and gold and in *Morrowind*, the Mudcrab Merchant sold alcoholic drinks.
- Old Salty may no longer be present at Northshore Landing once Ancarion and his men arrive at the dock during the quest, especially if you convince them to become merchants for Stalhrim equipment.
