*Absorb <mag> points of health per second from the target.* **Vampiric Drain** is a [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell possessed by all [vampires](Skyrim_Vampirism.md), which [drains the health](Skyrim_Drain_Health.md) of a target and heals the caster.

## Effects
- [Drain Health](Skyrim_Drain_Health.md), <mag> pts for 1 sec

## Notes
- The magnitude and cost vary based on your stage of vampirism.
- Using the spell does not level your Destruction skill.
- Vampiric Drain after Stage Three vampirism counts towards the Spells Learned stat. Even if you get cured of vampirism, losing the ability to cast the spell, the stat won't decrease. In addition, if you equip the spell before [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md) cures you in [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md), it will remain equipped, allowing you to still use it.
- In [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> form, killing [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit.md), [foxes](Skyrim_Fox.md), [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md), [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), and specific undead (such as those in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md) or zombies raised with reanimation spells) with this spell will not give vampiric experience.
