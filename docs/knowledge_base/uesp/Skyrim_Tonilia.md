[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Tonilia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tonilia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tonilia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tonilia.jpg) Tonilia in the Ragged Flagon **Tonilia** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [pawnbroker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker), [fence](Skyrim_Fence_(merchant).md), and lookout for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md). As you progress in the guild, you will be able to gain access to three more fences by handling a [moon sugar delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest) for her. Although she isn't officially a member of the guild, she also handles the supply and upgrading of [Thieves Guild armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Armor). She is in a relationship with [Vekel the Man](Skyrim_Vekel_the_Man.md), but she refuses to marry him, perhaps because of a rumored affair with [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md).

Tonilia will always be found in the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) near her lover Vekel and her friend [Dirge](Skyrim_Dirge.md). She will always offer her services while pacing the tavern or sitting at a table. While not a member, she still wears the standard [Thieves Guild armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Armor_Alt) and [boots](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Boots) as well as a leveled necklace. She also carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), a leveled [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), and up to 54 gold coins. She is armed with two leveled [daggers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven quality](Skyrim_Elven.md)).

**Brynjolf sent me.** *"So, you're the new recruit, eh? Well, looks like you and I are going to have to get very well acquainted."* **Why is that?** *"I'm the fence down here. You come by anything you don't exactly own and I'll pay you some coin for it. Minus a little slice for the Guild, of course. I can also provide a few supplies useful to our trade now and again, for a small fee."* **Is there anything you don't charge for?** *"Sure, how about I get Dirge to knock you over your head and dump you into the cistern? Look, I've been in this business a long time and I've seen all types. You can play it tough, you can play it smart... whatever. At the end of the day you'll find all we care about down here is how much gold you can make us. Good. Then there isn't much more to say. Here's your armor, just make sure you put it to good use."* **Understood.** *"Good. Then there isn't much more to say. Here's your armor, just make sure you put it to good use."* **Does the Guild provide other services?** *"Well, you could always speak to Delvin or Vex if you're looking for extra work. Or if you're looking for training, we've got plenty of it down here. Delvin, Vex, [Niruin](Skyrim_Niruin.md) and Vipir can give you a leg up on that."*

### Deliver Moon Sugar to Ri'saad
After you've completed three special job quests, Tonilia will offer you a side job: "*Before we get down to business, I've got something I need you to do. Well, as you know, the Guild's growing and things are looking up around here. The only thing we're lacking is a reliable way to transport our merchandise across Skyrim. I'm not sure if you've noticed, but there are several Khajiit Caravans that travel across the realm. They're shrewd traders and don't mind getting their hands dirty. I've bartered with their leader, Ri'saad, on more than one occasion. Actually, I want you to bring him something. You see, the caravans are notorious for transporting illegal substances. Present Ri'saad with this satchel of Moon Sugar, and I bet my last septim he'll make a deal.*" She'll also warn you: "*You'll find Ri'saad with his caravan group. Just don't do anything stupid when you get there.*"

If you inquire further about the caravans, she'll explain: "*Ri'saad is the head of a small syndicate of independent merchant families. Each caravan is obliged to pay dues, but in return a small portion of the profits are shared between them and the leader parcels out the routes. If we could make contact with them, they could provide an excellent way to inconspicuously transport goods for us.*" If you ask her what Moon Sugar is, she'll happily divulge: "*It's an illegal substance highly favored by the Khajiit. Very difficult to come by. If Moon Sugar is refined properly, it can be used to make skooma, another substance that will knock you over after a single draught.*"

Once you've struck the deal, she'll be pleased and reward you with a leveled amount of gold: "*Good, I'll contact some of my people and have them prepare some shipments right away. Good job. Here, let me give you something for all the leg work.*"

## Notes
- When the quest [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md) becomes available, Tonilia, unlike the others, will remain at the Ragged Flagon, but won't provide her services. Instead, she'll mention that you should go talk to Brynjolf.
- Tonilia sometimes has the "What'll you give me for these?" dialogue option, and other times has the "What have you got for sale?" dialogue option.

## Bugs
- As long as you are eligible for upgraded Guild armor, Tonilia will not offer any other dialogue. See [Scoundrel's Folly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scoundrel%27s_Folly#Bugs) for details and fixes.
- Despite being a fence, there is a chance that Tonilia may eventually stop accepting stolen goods. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
- There is a chance that Tonilia may not give you Guild Leader Armor. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.4, fixes this bug.
