[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-item-Shrouded_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Armor.jpg) Shrouded Armor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-item-Shrouded_Armor_%28sleeveless%29.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Armor_%28sleeveless%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Armor_(sleeveless).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Armor_(sleeveless).jpg) The sleeveless version of this armor Shrouded Armor is the chest piece of a set of armor used by the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). Alongside this piece, this set of armor also includes [boots](Skyrim_Shrouded_Boots.md), a [cowl](Skyrim_Shrouded_Cowl.md), and [gloves](Skyrim_Shrouded_Gloves.md). The armor provides fifty points of [poison resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md). [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) Shrouded Armor requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
