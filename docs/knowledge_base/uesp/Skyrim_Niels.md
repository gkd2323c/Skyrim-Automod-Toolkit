[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-npc-Niels.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Niels.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niels.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niels.jpg) Niels' corpse **Niels**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), was part of a [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) -based hunting party led by [Valdr](Skyrim_Valdr.md). The group recently disappeared when they chased a [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) sow into [Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern.md). You will find out what happened during the [related quest](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md).

You will find Niels halfway through the cave where he was ripped to shreds by [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md), although the bear may have participated as well.

Niels wore randomly determined [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md); at all levels, there is a 50% chance it will be [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) or [studded armor](Skyrim_Studded_Armor.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, there is a 50% chance of him wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5 there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md), and at level 19 it can be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of him carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12. His weapon of choice is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), or a [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md). He also carried a bit of random loot, food and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or some gold.

The only survivor of the group is Valdr, who will be sitting outside the cave, heavily wounded. He will tell you about the fate of the hunters: *"We tracked a bear to this den. Good coin for those pelts. We had the big sow cornered when they showed up. Three of them, out of nowhere. Spriggans. Niels went down before we even knew to run. [Ari](Skyrim_Ari.md) died just inside."* Once you have healed him, he will ask you to help him avenge the deaths of his fallen comrades: *"But now what? I can't just walk away. Not with my friends' bodies in there, being torn apart by those beasts."* Once the cave is clear, he will do one last thing for Niels and Ari: *"I'll give them a proper burial, then head back."* Niels' body will then disappear from the game.
