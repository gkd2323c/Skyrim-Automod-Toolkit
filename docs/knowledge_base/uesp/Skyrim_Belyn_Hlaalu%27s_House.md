It is the first house on the left when entering the Quarter from [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md).

It is the home of [Belyn Hlaalu](Skyrim_Belyn_Hlaalu.md), the owner of [Hlaalu Farm](Skyrim_Hlaalu_Farm.md), who lives here alone. It consists of one area, *[Belyn Hlaalu's House](#Belyn_Hlaalu.27s_House)*.

## Belyn Hlaalu's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Belyn_Hlaalu%27s_House.jpg) Inside Belyn Hlaalu's House The front door opens to the sight of an old cart stashed underneath a ladder leading upstairs. Along the eastern wall sits an end table containing clutter, with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on top. At the foot of the ladder is a dresser with [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md). An entrance to the west leads to the dining area; the southeastern corner has seven food sacks, while the nearby set of shelves holds a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a [honey nut treat](Skyrim_Honey_Nut_Treat.md), three heads of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a sample of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), a bouquet of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), and a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). Leaning against the shelves is another pickaxe. The obligatory fireplace can be found along the southern wall, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) above the hot embers, and a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md) leaning against the wall. The table in the southwestern corner holds three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a wedge of [goat cheese](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), and a wedge of [Eidar cheese](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md). The dinner table only seats one, and has more food, including a slice of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [boiled creme treat](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md), some [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), and a bottle of regular [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). Lastly, the shelves in the northeastern corner have two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of regular wine, and a Nord mead on display.

The upstairs area is spacious, but also rather bare. At the top of the stairs is a small table with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). In the bedroom, two food barrels decorate the southeastern corner, one of them located right next to Belyn's owned bed. In the adjacent corner is an end table with clutter, a copy of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](Skyrim_Dunmer_of_Skyrim.md)*, and a respawning [knapsack](Skyrim_Containers.md#Knapsack) with leveled items. The chest contains random items, while the dresser contains random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md). More clothes are scattered around the dresser: a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Boots), a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Hats), and a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md). Hidden behind the dresser is a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Frostfall, v10](Skyrim_2920,_Frostfall,_v10.md)*.

## Notes
- With the exception of some barrels, sacks, and the knapsack, all containers in the area are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
