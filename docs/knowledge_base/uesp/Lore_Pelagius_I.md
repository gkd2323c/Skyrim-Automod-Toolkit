Pelagius Septim I was the second [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for less than three years, from [3E 38](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_38) to [3E 41](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_41). He was preceded by his grandfather, [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), and was succeeded by his first cousin once removed, [Kintyra I](Lore_Kintyra_I.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

## Biography
Pelagius Septim's parentage is unknown, but he was the grandson of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_PSIRt TS)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3AEM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAH-3)</sup> [Barenziah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barenziah) came to live with the imperial family until she turned eighteen, and Pelagius reportedly came to love her like a sister.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BOB2-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRBv3-5)</sup> On his deathbed in [3E 38](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_38), Tiber named Pelagius his successor, and after Tiber's death, he was crowned Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3AEM-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TDC-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTET-7)</sup>

According to one source, Pelagius I was initially nervous about managing all the provinces, so the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md) approached the Emperor and offered to serve as an advisor.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAH-3)</sup> After his accession, Pelagius I reportedly viewed Barenziah with hostility; however, he had absolute trust in Barenziah's prime minister, [Symmachus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Symmachus), who was a friend and general of his grandfather.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TRBv4-8)</sup>

Pelagius I was as strong and resolute as Tiber, and it was believed that his reign could be a continuation of the golden age that Tamriel experienced during his grandfather's reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> However, less than three years into his reign, in [3E 41](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_41), Pelagius I was killed by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) as he knelt at prayer in the [Temple of the One](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Temple_of_the_One) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTET-7)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SRA-9)</sup> An unknown individual hired the Dark Brotherhood to assassinate Pelagius I in order to bring about a change in imperial policy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SRA-9)</sup> Pelagius had no living children, so the [Ruby Throne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ruby_Throne) passed to his first cousin once removed, [Kintyra](Lore_Kintyra_I.md), the daughter of Tiber's brother [Agnorith](Lore_Agnorith.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTET-7)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1](#note_PSIRt TS) | There is some disagreement regarding Pelagius' relationship with Tiber Septim. A version of *[Brief History of the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brief_History_of_the_Empire,_Part_I)* and the *[Biography of Barenziah](Lore_Biography_of_Barenziah,_v_2.md)* refer to Pelagius as Tiber's son, but a revised version of *[Brief History of the Empire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brief_History_of_the_Empire_v_1)*, *[Pocket Guide to the Empire, 3rd Edition](Lore_Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_3rd_Edition_All_the_Eras_of_Man.md)*, and *[The Arcturian Heresy](Lore_The_Arcturian_Heresy.md)* refer to Pelagius as Tiber's grandson. Additionally, the original version of *[The Real Barenziah](Lore_The_Real_Barenziah,_Part_VII.md)* refers to Pelagius as Tiber's son, while the revised [version of the book](Lore_The_Real_Barenziah,_v_4.md) instead refers to him as both Tiber's grandson and son, directly contradicting itself. |
