[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-place-The_Treasury_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Treasury_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Treasury_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Treasury_House.jpg) The Treasury House **The Treasury House** is the main financial institution for [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) and [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). It is on the first terrace above and in the northern area of the city. It provides financial support and services for the landowners of the Reach, notably the Silver-Blood family and its patrons. All the miners, farmers, and day-laborers of the Reach get their wages through the Treasury House. [Rhiada](Skyrim_Rhiada.md) runs the front desk, where the actual treasury is kept behind an expert-locked gate. [Nana Ildene](Skyrim_Nana_Ildene.md) (who has worked there for over twenty years) and [Donnel](Skyrim_Donnel.md) keep things tidy. [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md), who handles all of his family's business, effectively controls the establishment. [Betrid](Skyrim_Betrid_Silver-Blood.md), Thonar's wife and an admitted and unrepentant gold-digger, can also be found here.

## The Treasury House
It is a large building, like all in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) is built into the hillside. From the entrance there is a short corridor to the main room with a large counter in front of you and raised areas in the southeastern and southwestern corners. There are stairs along the east and west wall leading to [Thonar](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md)'s quarters and a pair of twin bedrooms used by the employees. By the bottom of the stairs are stone tables and chairs, with numerous [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and sacks around the building. Most of the cookware in the building is high quality, including [plates](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate), [bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl), [jugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug), [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), etc. [Rhiada](Skyrim_Rhiada.md) will be stood behind the counter and make you unwelcome with comments about it only being for the employees of the Silver-Blood family.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-interior-The_Treasury_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Treasury_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Treasury_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Treasury_House.jpg) Inside the Treasury House On the counter is a wooden bowl containing a [dragon's tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue) flower, two [red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple) on a wooden plate, a bowl containing a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) and a plate with two more dragon's tongue flowers on it. While under the counter is a collection of cookware, a bunch of [juniper berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries), a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) and a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute). to the rear of the counter is a flight of stairs up to an expert-locked gate concealing an expert-locked [safe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Safe) with three [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on each side of it.

On the raised section in the southeast corner is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on a barrel, a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) on a crate and a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) on the floor. The table by the stairs holds the same as the other one, except it has a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) instead of alto wine. At the top of the stairs is a junction with a twin bedroom to the east and west. The one to the west consists of two owned single beds, one against a ledge to the south and the other against a ledge to the north. On the first ledge is a bottle of wine, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), two red and a green apple, while on the second ledge is a copy of *[Scourge of the Gray Quarter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge_of_the_Gray_Quarter)* and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger). Against the west wall is a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and with a piece of [silver ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md) and bottle of alto wine on top. The bedroom to the east consists of another two owned single beds, one against a ledge to the north and the other against a ledge to the south. The ledge to the north has a copy of *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)*, a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and two loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on top and the ledge to the south has two bottles of Nord mead on top. To the east is a tall wardrobe with a bottle of wine and a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese) on a crate to the right.

By the raised section to the southwest is a kettle containing two cabbages and a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), while on the raised section is a round wooden table with a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine on top. Along the west wall is a dresser, with a small coin purse on a barrel to the right and further right is a stone ledge with a red apple in front of a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*. The stone table by these stairs has two wooden chairs and has a bottle of alto wine, a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a red apple, a loaf of bread and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) on top. [Betrid Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Betrid_Silver-Blood.md) spend all her time sat at this table. In the northwest corner, up the stairs is the door to Thonar's quarters which is apprentice-locked.

His quarters consist of two rooms, the first has a central stone table with two chairs. It is laid with plenty of cookware as well as a bottle of wine, a baked potato, two [salmon steaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), two portions of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a rack of [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat), three loaves of bread and several pieces of cheese. There is an alcove to the west with an owned single bed in against the west wall with a ledge running along the wall holding a copy of *[The Cake and The Diamond](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cake_and_The_Diamond)*. To the left of the alcove is a set of shelves holding two loaves of bread, a cabbage, a potato in a cast iron pot and several pieces of cheese, while to the right of the alcove is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot. Against the east wall is a large fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a pot containing a cabbage and a wooden bowl containing two red and a green apple on either side. To the left of the fireplace is a food barrel with a goat cheese wedge on top and a basket on the floor containing a gourd and two cabbages, with a rack hanging from the ceiling above it holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), a garlic braid and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md).

To the north is a doorway into a small bedroom most of which is taken up with a large double bed coming out from the north wall. To the right of the bed, on a stone ledge is an [unusual gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) with a long end table against the west wall. On top of which is a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton), a [silver jeweled necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Jeweled_Necklace), a large coin purse and a bowl containing a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) and three bunches of juniper berries, while against the east wall is a tall wardrobe.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom left of the entrance door.
- During the Forsworn Conspiracy quest all or most of the occupants will be killed unless pickpocketing is used to acquire Thonar's Journal.
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions a character by the name of **Kolgrim** living here, although no such person exists, and is never mentioned in-game. No trace of this NPC can be found in the game data.
