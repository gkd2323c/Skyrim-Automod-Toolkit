[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg/100px-LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Bjoulsae_River_Tribes.jpg) River Horse Bretons Ain-Kolur was a battlefield where the Battle of Ain-Kolur took place. During this battle one of the most renowned [River Horse](Lore_River_Horse_Bretons.md) [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) Mindothrax, who was a master of defensive combat perished against a rival tribe led by Chief Iymbez, who brought in reinforcements from the islands to the south. A camp of Mindothrax's tribe was positioned in a high-walled garden of an old burial ground, adorned by springtide blossoms.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-The_Mirror-1)</sup>
