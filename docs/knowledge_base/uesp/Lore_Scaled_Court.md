# Scaled Court

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/ON-misc-Scaled_Court_Banner.jpg/200px-ON-misc-Scaled_Court_Banner.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Scaled_Court_Banner.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Scaled_Court_Banner.jpg) The banner of the Scaled Court The Scaled Court was a group of fanatics active in [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) and Western [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). They follow the teachings of the [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md) body [The Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md).

The were initially led by the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) warlord, Milvern One-Sword, and emerged at the mysterious disappearance of the Celestial Guardians in the night sky in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They were driven from Cyrodiil by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md), but they were still active in Craglorn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oot SC-1)</sup>

They were disbanded after being defeated by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Online-2)</sup>

## Ranks
- **Vicious Ophidians**: The Serpent's elite guards. They wore face-concealing masks.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
- **Regents**: The Court's mortal leaders. Each possessed a grandiose title and was in charge of a specific operation that would contribute to the Serpent's cause.
