# Bull-Heart Warriors

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/ON-hat-Bull-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Bull-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Bull-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Bull-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) Bull-Heart Skull Sallet Bull-Heart Warriors were one of the numerous [groups](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They were active in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONBull Sallet-1)</sup>

Bull-Heart Warriors were known to wear sallets that were partially made of bull skulls during their Horned Triumph ritual.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONBull Sallet-1)</sup> They shared the usage of the bull imagery with another group of Reachfolk, the [Dreadhorn Clan](Lore_Dreadhorn_Clan.md), who were known for their alliance with [Minotaurs](Lore_Minotaur.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM-2)</sup>
