[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-npc-Curate_Melita.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Curate_Melita.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Curate_Melita.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Curate_Melita.jpg) Curate Melita **Curate Melita** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) Curate of the Tribunal Temple. She can be rescued during [Careless Curation](Skyrim_Careless_Curation.md), and will relocate herself to [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) as an Apothecary vendor for the [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md). Alternatively, you can choose to kill her.

## Related Quests
- **[Careless Curation](Skyrim_Careless_Curation.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>: Find the missing Curate to retrieve the [Mask of Sotha Sil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Sotha_Sil).

## Quest-Related Events

### Careless Curation

- *"Maybe you can help me."*

- - **Actually, I'm going to kill you and take the mask.**
- *"Don't hurt me. You win."*

- - **Do you have the mask?**
- *"Uh... yeah."*

- - **I'm here to rescue you on behalf of the Goddess.**
- *"Oh. Good job. Here... My way of saying thanks."*

If talked with after choosing the first option:

- **Let me untie your bonds.**
- *"Thanks!"*
