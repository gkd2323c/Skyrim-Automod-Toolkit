# Concordat of Fraternity


After signing the Concordat, the remaining captains and crews of the All Flags Navy sailed back to their homelands, save for a few from [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md). Those who stayed behind were mostly engineers and laborers tasked with the construction of the All Flags monument to commemorate the fleet's triumph over the Sload. The people of Tamriel fell back into squabbles soon after, most notably in the [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SH-1)</sup>
