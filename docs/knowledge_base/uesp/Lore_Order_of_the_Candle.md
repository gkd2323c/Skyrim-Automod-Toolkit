# Order of the Candle

The Order of the Candle, which replaced [Knights of the Moon](Lore_Knights_of_the_Moon.md) as the official protectors and warriors of the Kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), was a knightly order founded early in the Fifth Century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). The Order was charged with protecting the royal family and kingdom of Sentinel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFOrder Dia-1)</sup> The Order was treated with high respect in Sentinel, and only skilled men and women with high loyalty to the royal family of Sentinel were able to join the Order. It was initially led by Lord Vhosek and Lord [K'avar](Lore_K%27avar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFOrder Dia-1)</sup> They were known for their affinity for espionage and worn numerous different uniforms whenever they needed to go into combat incognito.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFGuide-2)</sup>
