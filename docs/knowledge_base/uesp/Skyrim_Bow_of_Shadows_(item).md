[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-item-Bow_of_Shadows.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bow_of_Shadows.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bow_of_Shadows.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bow_of_Shadows.jpg) Bow of Shadows The Bow of Shadows is an artifact of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md). It is acquired through the [In the Shadows](Skyrim_In_the_Shadows.md) quest. Tempering the Bow of Shadows requires an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[In the Shadows](Skyrim_In_the_Shadows.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bow_of_Shadows.md)</sup>: Save the Jarl of Whiterun from an assassination plot.

## Notes
- The Bow of Shadows has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Bow_of_Shadows)*. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Bow_of_Shadows.md).

## Bugs
- The Bow of Shadows’ Quick Shot perk does not account for the Quick Shot Archery perk, causing their effects to stack additively and resulting in a Weapon Speed Mult of 2.5 when both are active. It also does not account for Survival Mode hunger debuffs. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The [Unofficial Skyrim Creation Club Content Patches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Creation_Club_Content_Patches), version 6.4, fixes this bug.
- The Bow of Shadows has no material keywords assigned, which results in no smithing specializations applying to it. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The [Unofficial Skyrim Creation Club Content Patches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Creation_Club_Content_Patches), version 6.4, fixes this bug.
- The bow can suddenly stop making you invisible. In some cases, this may be caused by taking the [Quick Shot](Skyrim_Quick_Shot.md) perk, but it can occur without that perk as well. - If you've taken the Quick Shot perk, leveling your Archery skill to 100 and setting it Legendary may fix it.
- Dropping it on the ground and picking it back up may fix the issue.
- If you have the Gray Cowl, making that a favorite and then equipping it from there may fix it.
- Saving and reloading may restore invisibility on a one-shot basis, but the problem will continue after that.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md), enter `player.removespell FE [xxx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md#Creation_Club) 80B` followed by `player.addspell FExxx80B`.
- Using any door with the bow drawn can fix this bug.
