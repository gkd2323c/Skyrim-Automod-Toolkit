**Haknir's Crew** were [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) pirates who served under the legendary pirate king [Haknir Death-Brand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand). Upon entering the final chamber of [Gyldenhul Barrow](Skyrim_Gyldenhul_Barrow.md) during the [related quest](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md), their spirits appear with Haknir to fight you.

## Related Quests
- **[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md)**: Find the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand).
