Blessed of [Onsi](Lore_Onsi.md), [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md): where warriors are bravest in all [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md).

Blessed of [Tava](Lore_Tava.md), Satakalaam: where [goshawk](Lore_Goshawk.md) nests atop High Temple tower.

Blessed of [Morwha](Lore_Morwha.md), Satakalaam: where [bees](Lore_Bee.md) carry pollen to [pomegranate](Lore_Pomegranate.md) and [fig](Lore_Fig.md).

Blessed of [Zeht](Lore_Zeht.md), Satakalaam: where water from deep rock fills fountain and jug.

Blessed of [Tu'whacca](Lore_Tu%27whacca.md), Satakalaam: where Motalion guards ancestors from eras before.

Blessed of [Ruptga](Lore_Ruptga.md), Satakalaam: where stars shine to guide us upon Walkabout.

Blessed of [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md), Satakalaam: where we shall keep true faith till called to World's End.
