## Explicit Instructions
- Start by facing the entrance to the windmill on [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md).
- Move south, and start bearing to the right (west) slightly.
- As you go up the mountain keep turning to the west, you will be funneled into a path.
- The treasure chest is under some bushes at the top of a rise on this path, not far above the snowline. - A horse at full gallop takes approximately 50 seconds to get from the windmill to the treasure.

## Bugs
- The chest may not be there at all. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.3, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The chest can be spawned to the hidden spot by using the console command `"f4a03".placeatme 10e05e`.
