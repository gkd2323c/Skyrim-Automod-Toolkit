Alisanne Dupre was a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) who had become the Listener of the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) by the year [4E 187](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_187). Her father, Uther Nere, had murdered her mother when she was a young woman, and she fled to join the Brotherhood, vowing to take revenge as an assassin.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LG-1)</sup>

Dupre maintained a private residence in [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), from which she commuted to the Brotherhood's last Cyrodilic sanctuary in [Cheydinhal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cheydinhal). While there, she discussed the state of the Brotherhood with Rasha. Among the topics they discussed was the destruction of the [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) sanctuary, and the potential re-opening of the [Shadowscale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shadowscales) training facility in [Archon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Archon), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), a plan that was ultimately abandoned due to lack of resources.

When the feud between Bravil's two largest [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skooma) traffickers turned violent, Dupre was forced to hire a battalion of sellswords to protect her and her property. Ultimately the effort proved futile, as she was tricked by her father, and incinerated by his fiery spell. Dupre was killed while attempting in vain to guard the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md)'s crypt from desecration. During the fight, an assassin named Garnag managed to escape with the Night Mother's body, saving it from destruction and ruining Uther Nere's plans to destroy the Dark Brotherhood entirely.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CJ-2)</sup>
