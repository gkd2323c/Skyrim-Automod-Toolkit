# Shadefeather Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp.jpg) Shadefeather Clan Camp ca. 2E 582 The Shadefeather Clan was a prominent [clan](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), a region in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They were not numerous, but a notorious nomadic clan, known for their violence to both Reachfolk and outsiders alike. The clan was under the sway of a strong coven of [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SH-2)</sup> They were known to waylay travelers throughout the Reach and murdering their captives in dark rituals. Other Reachfolk clans were often targets of their attacks. They frequently shifted camps and killed anyone unfortunate enough to stumble across them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Drisbellairn-3)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) their camp was located to the northwest of [Hroldan Ring](Lore_Hroldan_Ring.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCC-4)</sup>

The Reachfolk of the Shadefeather Clan dedicated themselves to the two of the [Great Spirits of the Reach](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) and [Namira](Lore_Namira.md). They erected shrines to both of the spirits. The shrine dedicated to Nocturnal was known as the Shadefeather Ritual Stone and was located in the center of the camp. The shrine to Namira was placed in a less central spot, at the camp's eastern entrance.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCC-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/ON-creature-Shadefeather_Hagraven.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Shadefeather_Hagraven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Shadefeather_Hagraven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Shadefeather_Hagraven.jpg) Shadefeather Clan Hagraven ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadefeather_Clan_Camp_02.jpg) Members of the Shadefeather Clan praying to Nocturnal ca. 2E 582 The Shadefeather Clan was considered one of the more prominent clans of the Reach as of [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTCOTR-1)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) members of the Shadefeather Clan were hostile to both outsiders and their Reachfolk neighbors,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCC-4)</sup> making them common enemy of the majority of native Reachfolk.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Drisbellairn-3)</sup> Their camp was located just west of [Gloomreach](Lore_Gloomreach.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SCC-4)</sup>

Shadefeathers were known to occasionally disrupt the trade and travel between the city of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) and the rest of the world. Due to the fact that this behavior directly threatened the interests of the City of Stone, Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md) intervened and was known to move his forces against the clan.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-POTR-5)</sup>
