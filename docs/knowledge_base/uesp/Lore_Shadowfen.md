[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-place-Shadowfen.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shadowfen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadowfen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shadowfen.jpg) Shadowfen ca [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Shadowfen is the northwest region of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). The region borders [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) to the north, and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to the west.

It is a fetid mire, often fought over by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) slavers. The cities of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md) and [Alten Corimont](Lore_Alten_Corimont.md) are located here. It is also home to the [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscales.md), those [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) born under the sign of [the Shadow](Lore_The_Shadow.md), and thus destined for a life in the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md). The Argonians of Shadowfen are traditionally born beneath the great Hist tree at the [Hatching Pools](Lore_Hatching_Pools.md), tended to by the Keepers of the Shell.

Shadowfen was the source of the devastating (to non-Argonians) [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md). Local creatures include giant snakes, [fleshflies](Lore_Fleshflies.md) and [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md), and diseases such as [blood rot](Lore_Blood_Rot.md), the [droops](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Droops), and [swamp fever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Swamp_Fever) are quite common.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFBMT-1)</sup> [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) trees and ruined temples can also be found along the winding channels and dank foliage.
