[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/RG-comic-Page_13-Tear_Swamp.jpg/200px-RG-comic-Page_13-Tear_Swamp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_13-Tear_Swamp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_13-Tear_Swamp.jpg) The Thornmarsh swamp [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-map-Ebonheart_Pact.jpg/200px-ON-map-Ebonheart_Pact.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ebonheart_Pact.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Ebonheart_Pact.jpg) A map of the Pact territory and surrounding areas Thornmarsh, known as the **[Jewel of the East](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jewel_of_the_East)**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBBR-1)</sup> is a coastal region in northeastern [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Co T-2)</sup> It is bordered by [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) to the west, [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) to the north, and the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) to the east. The region contains the city of [Thorn](Lore_Thorn.md). Like the rest of northern Black Marsh, the area has more temperate grasslands compared to the swampier areas in the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-3)</sup>

It borders Morrowind to the north around [Tear](Lore_Tear.md), a coastal plain part of the [Dres District](Lore_House_Dres.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWElone-4)</sup>

The [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tribes of Thornmarsh were one of three regions of Black Marsh to join the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) at its founding in [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The Four-Spike Wattle is an arrangement of throat spines particularly popular on the coastal shores of Thornmarsh.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FSW-6)</sup>

## Notes
- According to various files and maps dating back to the alpha state of [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), Thornmarsh is the name for the planned zone also known as Southern Marsh, which is displayed as encompassing the [Tear](Lore_Tear.md) area of Morrowind and goes down to [Brachmont](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Branchmont) in Black Marsh. While the outlines of the zone can be found in-game, the zone itself has yet to appear.
