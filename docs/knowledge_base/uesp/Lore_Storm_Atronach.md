# Storm Atronach

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/LG-cardart-Storm_Atronach.png/200px-LG-cardart-Storm_Atronach.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Storm_Atronach.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Storm_Atronach.png) Storm Atronach (Legends) Storm Atronachs (also called **Storm Daedra** or **Thundernachs**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOP4-2)</sup> are [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) native to [Levinace](Lore_Levinace.md). Seemingly made entirely of lightning and rocky debris, they can be found unbound in the wild or can be conjured by those with magical aptitude to serve as guards, laborers, or otherwise obey their conjurers. Summoning of storm Atronachs is usually performed through [Koron's](Lore_Koron.md) [Peremptory Summons](Lore_Koron%27s_Peremptory_Summons.md), a spell which usually summons storm Atronachs in their conventional forms but which can be altered by conjurers to alter the minutiae of the summons (such as plane to summon from, daedra to summon, and form in which the daedra can be conjured).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO_CSCCSC-3)</sup> They are conventionally summoned and found in the form of bulky humanoids; however they do appear in other forms. Their summoning was first perfected by the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md), especially [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md), who discovered that they could be bound into different forms by their conjurers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSCC-4)</sup>

They are a source of [Void Salts](Lore_Alchemy_V.md#Void_Salts), an alchemical ingredient which has varied utility, and are a source of [Atronite](Lore_Atronite.md), valuable stones left behind when an atronach is dispelled or defeated. Storm Atronachs are found in many [Daedric Realms](Lore_Oblivion.md), including those of prominent princes and minor planes. While possessing some intelligence, storm Atronachs are largely an unorganized and chaotic group, with individuals being largely indistinguishable from one another.

While they primarily create lightning and electricy, Storm Atronachs are capable of created controlled amounts of rainfall. This ability has been used by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md), who have Storm Atronachs workers to water crops.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Variants

## Levinace
[Levinace](Lore_Levinace.md) is a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). It is the home of the Storm Atronachs.

It might be assumed that Levinace is a 'collective realm' similar to [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md), created as an extension of its Storm Atronach inhabitants. If so, the realm would be rather mundane and uniform in appearance.

Levinace is well known to all [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) due to the ability to summon subservient atronachs from the realm. Summoned Storm Atronachs are usually compelled to take a roughly humanoid form, often for combat purposes. Sufficiently skilled conjurers can compel atronachs from this realm to assume any number of exotic forms, such as for use as summoned mounts. [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md) is famed as a pioneer in this field.
