## Notes
- Light of Day has previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Light_of_Day)*.
- If the player is a vampire, when equipped the Light of Day deals 15 points of [Sun Damage](Skyrim_Sun_Damage.md) to the player. This also applies to NPCs who attempt to wield it such as [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md).

## Bugs
- Along with [Magebane](Skyrim_Magebane.md) and [Skull Crusher](Skyrim_Skull_Crusher.md), Light of Day respawns.
