[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Grim_Shieldmaiden.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grim_Shieldmaiden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grim_Shieldmaiden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grim_Shieldmaiden.jpg) Grim Shieldmaiden **Grim Shieldmaiden**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md), can be the target of the recurring quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). She walks a circular path around the waterwheel-powered smithy in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

She wears a random set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor) with steel boots and gauntlets. She wields a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and blocks with an [imperial shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Shield.md).

When you approach her she will only say: *"Did you want something? I'm quite busy."*
