[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-item-Headman%27s_Cleaver.jpg/200px-SR-item-Headman%27s_Cleaver.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Headman%27s_Cleaver.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Headman%27s_Cleaver.jpg) Headman's Cleaver Headman's Cleaver is a [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md) artifact. It is acquired through the [Blood in the Water](Skyrim_Blood_in_the_Water.md) quest. Tempering Headman's Cleaver requires an [Orichalcum Ingot](Skyrim_Orcish.md#Orichalcum_Ingot), and having the [Orcish Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md#Orcish_Smithing) perk doubles the improvement. Despite the material and perk, it does as much damage as a [Daedric Battleaxe](Skyrim_Daedric.md#Daedric_Battleaxe) while weighing less, making it the most powerful Orcish weapon in the game.

## Related Quests
- **[Blood in the Water](Skyrim_Blood_in_the_Water.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Headman%27s_Cleaver.md)</sup>: Deliver vengeance upon the enemies of the [Bloodfall Queen](Lore_Bloodfall_Queen.md).

## Notes
- Headman's Cleaver has previously appeared in *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Headman%27s_Cleaver)*.
- An enchantment for this weapon exists in the game data, but goes unused. The enchantment deals 20 points of [stamina damage](Skyrim_Stamina_Damage.md), and 2 points of [bleeding damage](Skyrim_Bleeding_Damage.md) for 5 seconds.

## Bugs
- The weapon is set to disallow enchanting, and 2000 enchantment charges are set. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.629.0, fixes this bug.
