# Dragon's Blight

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/BL-artifact-Dragon%27s_Blight.jpg/200px-BL-artifact-Dragon%27s_Blight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-artifact-Dragon%27s_Blight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-artifact-Dragon%27s_Blight.jpg) Dragon's Blight (Blades) The Dragon's Blight is a legendary battle axe imbued with power that lets it bypass the physical defenses of those it strikes. It was also a potent [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md) against the [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md). According to the legend, the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) themselves forged this devastating weapon at the end of the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), but the exact reason why has been lost to time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dragon.27s_Blight-1)</sup>

It is unknown whether the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md#Warrior) wielded the Dragon's Blight, but as of [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the legendary battle axe was in use.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Blades-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Dragon's Blight is also a term used to describe the corruption linked to the Dragon [Maarselok](Lore_Maarselok.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
