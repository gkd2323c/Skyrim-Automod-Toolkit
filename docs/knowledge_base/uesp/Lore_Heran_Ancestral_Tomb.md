# Heran

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/MW-place-Heran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-MW-place-Heran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Heran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Heran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) The Heran Ancestral Tomb [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/ON-npc-Idras_Heran.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Idras_Heran.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Idras_Heran.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Idras_Heran.jpg) Idras Heran (Online) The Heran clan is an ancient family of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) that lives in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The Heran Clan is one of the thirty revered families of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATOTTRF-1)</sup> and a sub-faction of the Great [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONHeran-2)</sup> The **Heran Ancestral Tomb** is found on the waters of the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md), off the road between the [Odai River](Lore_Odai_River.md) and the shanty-village of [Hla Oad](Lore_Hla_Oad.md). It is known for containing hundreds of years' worth of family treasure, safeguarded by snap-wire traps and deadfalls.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup>

## Further Information
The Heran clan and the other twenty-nine revered families hid the location of the [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md), the old repository of knowledge for the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md), and the clues to its whereabouts are hidden in each of their tombs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBradyn-4)</sup> For the longest time, the library's location was lost to time until in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Librarian Bradyn of [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) sent an [Outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to retrieve these clues and find the ruins. In time, they were able to recover all of the clues, and the Library of Andule was discovered.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAncestral-5)</sup>

The fictional story, [Chance's Folly](Lore_Chance%27s_Folly.md) is centered around a heist on the Heran Tomb and its treasures. It revolves around a notorious thief and lockpicker that attempts to betray her accomplice until she was outplayed and left to her own demise. The treasures were later taken by her former partner.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup>

There have been very few noted members of the Heran clan throughout history. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Idras Heran was among the very few to be accepted into the [Shad Astula](Lore_Shad_Astula.md) academy in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), to become a great leader in the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONIdras-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONInvitation-7)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Galviso Heran was a Redoran lawman that lived in the Redoran Canton of Vivec. At the same time, Brevasu Heran was a bandit under the service of Rels Tenim, whose bandit clan was hunted down by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) in their hideout, Shallit.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWBrevasu-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWTenim-9)</sup>
