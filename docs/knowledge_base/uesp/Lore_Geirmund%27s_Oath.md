Geirmund's Oath *An oath dedicated to the sacrifice of Archmage Geirmund*
---
Lord [Geirmund](Lore_Geirmund.md), great battlemage,
saved [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in [Harald](Lore_Harald.md)'s age.

He slew the wicked [Gauldur](Lore_Gauldur.md) three,
and set their father's spirit free.

His tomb was built upon [this lake](Lore_Lake_Geir.md),
and in his name this oath I take.

Should evil come, should night descend,
I swear the Rift I will defend.
