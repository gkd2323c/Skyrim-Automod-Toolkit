Nepos's Journal **by [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md)** *The regretful musings of an elderly Forsworn agent*
---
I grow guilt-ridden in my old age. So many of the young sent to their deaths. All in the name of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). All in the name of [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md).

My king. Who watches us from behind the iron bars of [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md). How long have I served you? Since the uprising against the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md)? Was there ever a time when all that violence hasn't over-shadowed our destinies?

What choice do I have but to do as I am instructed?
