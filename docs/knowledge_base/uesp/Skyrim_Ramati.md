[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-npc-Ramati.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ramati.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ramati.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ramati.jpg) Ramati **Ramati** was a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) whose family got more than they bargained for when they bought [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md). She wore a set of blue farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f), and carried her husband's [journal](Skyrim_Habd%27s_Journal.md). Her body is pierced by a [Falmer war axe](Skyrim_Falmer_War_Axe.md). [Her journal](Skyrim_Ramati%27s_Journal.md) is located in her room, and gives more detail on the family before their deaths.

Her husband [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md) saved for years before finally being able to live there with their children [Sudi](Skyrim_Sudi.md) and [Mani](Skyrim_Mani.md). For Ramati and Habd it was a dream come true, but their children weren't coping well with the move. She thought it was just her children messing with her when Sudi kept complaining of hearing scratching coming from the cellar. Ramati was the first to die when the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) finished tunneling through the walls. Her husband discovered her body upon returning from a trip to town and took off into [Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss_(place).md) in search of his missing children.

## Related Quests
- **[Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss.md)**: Uncover the tragic events inside [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md).

## Notes
- Ramati does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female Redguard face. She shares this quirk with her daughter, [Sudi](Skyrim_Sudi.md), oddly making them look identical.
