# Talin

Talin was a [forename](Lore_Names.md) of significance during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md). It can refer to either the **[Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md)**,<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> and/or the Champion's parental guardian, General **[Talin Warhaft](Lore_Talin_Warhaft.md)**.<sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1](#note_nb1) | |
| --- | --- |

The [introduction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Arena_Player%27s_Guide/Introduction) to *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)'* s [official game manual](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Arena_Player%27s_Guide) refers to the Eternal Champion as a male named Talin.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARManual-1)</sup> Additionally, a pre-release screenshot depicts the Eternal Champion as a [Breton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Breton) male named Talin.<sup>[[nb 3]](#intnote_nb4)</sup> The use of this name in the screenshot may have been used as a placeholder, as various other names were also featured in other screenshots.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARScreenshots-2)</sup> Regardless, it later became a policy of [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda) to avoid canonizing the identities of [player characters](Lore_Hero.md), so as to not cheapen the player's own personal experiences in [the games](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls#Games).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGI-3)</sup>
- | [^2](#note_nb2) | Refer to the [notes section](Lore_Talin_Warhaft.md#Notes) of the Talin Warhaft lore page for a detailed explanation regarding Warhaft's relation of the Eternal Champion. |
