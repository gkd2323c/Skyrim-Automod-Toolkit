Vampire Nest in the City! *A newspaper article on the discovery of a vampire lair in the heart of the Imperial City*
---
A nest of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) was recently discovered in the home of the Earl of Imbel. The Courier is shocked to learn that Earl Jakben, a local noble of previously unblemished reputation, is revealed to have *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* one of these vampires!

Responding to a tip by the Earl's servant, the [Imperial Watch](Lore_Imperial_Watch.md) raided the Imbel estate and slew all of the foul creatures. Captain Quintilius has categorically denied the rumors that most of the terrible creatures of the night were already dead by the time the Watch arrived.
