[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-item-Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede.png/200px-SR-item-Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede.png) Staff of Ehlno Ede The Staff of Ehlno Ede is an ancient Ayleid staff bearing a unique enchantment that attacks health, magicka, and stamina simultaneously. It is wielded by the undead Ayleid Lich [Norion the Undying](Skyrim_Norion_the_Undying.md), and he uses it against you with impunity after you disturb his rest in [Rielle Crypt](Skyrim_Rielle.md#Rielle_Crypt) during the quest [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause_(quest).md). Once he is defeated, the staff is yours to claim.

## Notes
- Ehlno Ede means Mortal End in the [Ayleid language](Lore_Ayleidoon.md).
