*As the Sub-Sapiarch of Domestic Livestock, I was assigned by Larnatille to breed an appropriate mount for our sages to ride upon when working in the field. I proudly present my result: the loyal and majestic Senche-Serval!*
—Corderil of [Sunhold](Lore_Sunhold.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSapiarchic Senche Serval-1)</sup> Servals are predatory [felines](Lore_Cat.md) found mainly in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSapiarchic Senche Serval-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ashgray-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Piebald-3)</sup> [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKhefrem Serval-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Piebald-3)</sup> and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dunecoat-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Oleander-6)</sup> Most are known for their distinctive spot patterns.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLight Serval Spots-7)</sup>

Many of the cats known as servals in Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Serval**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSapiarchic Senche Serval-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-kitten-8)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-9)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-9)</sup> Senche-Servals are known to have been bred on the Summerset Isles,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSapiarchic Senche Serval-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-kitten-8)</sup> but these breeds have since spread to the mainland.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ashgray-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Piebald-3)</sup> Khajiit can have fur patterns that resemble those of servals.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLight Serval Spots-7)</sup>

## Variants
Dune-Coat Senche-Servals are a striped breed found in the arid canyons of [Northern Elsweyr](Lore_Northern_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dunecoat-5)</sup> Piebald Senche-Servals are a grey breed with white fur on their paws, they are known for their love of the heat.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Piebald-3)</sup> Ashgray Senche-Servals posses a blueish grey coloration and have green eyes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ashgray-2)</sup> Snowclaw Senche-Servals live in snowy environments and possess an all white coloration.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Snowclaw-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Snow Kitt-11)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
Many Senche-Servals are bred for use as mounts. The Sapiarchic Senche Serval are a breed created by the [Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) of Summerset for the sages of their ranks to ride on.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSapiarchic Senche Serval-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-kitten-8)</sup> [House Mornard](Lore_House_Mornard.md) was rumored to have originally tamed Ashgray Senche-Servals on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md). For a time, only the elder nobles of that house rode this breed, after a downturn in Mornard fortunes the breed can be found throughout Tamriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ashgray-2)</sup> Similarly, Piebald Senche-Servals which were used for travel out of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md), were rumored to have originally been bred by Summerset outcasts in a bandit cave.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Piebald-3)</sup>

The [Knights of the Silver Rose](Lore_Knights_of_the_Silver_Rose.md) were said to have once spent years finding the best felines to rid their fortress of pests and annoyances. Generations later, and they bred a type of Senche-Serval that could clear out [banekin](Lore_Banekin.md) infestations in a single day.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Silver Rose-12)</sup> Their kittens are said possess a keen sense of group tactics at an early age, and are known for their prowess at sweeping an area clean of [shalks](Lore_Shalk.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Silver Rose Kitt-13)</sup>

The sight of a spotted Senche-Serval in [Khefrem](Lore_Khefrem.md) is a sign of good luck. People tend to capture several of them to get a streak of fortune, and even train them to become mounts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKhefrem Serval-4)</sup> The artists of [Black Heights](Lore_Black_Heights.md) are known to paint their Senche-Servals with a harmless [oleander](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oleander) pigment.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Oleander-6)</sup>
