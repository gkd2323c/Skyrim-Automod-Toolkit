Per Your Requests **by [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md)** *A tart reply from Mirabelle to Wylandriah about a request for experimental items*
---
[Wylandriah](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md),

Your letter sent to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) was rife with grammatical errors and incomplete thoughts, making them difficult to discern. Could you please clarify the points below for us to ensure we're on the same page?

We have no record of a "cloud emulsifier" device or anything involving the magical manipulation of the clouds. Second, we can't send you a sample of the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md) for experimentation, as no such sample exists. And finally, in the fourteenth paragraph of your letter, you mention a substance called "[greenmote](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Greenmote)." We're assuming this was a simple mistake and you meant to write "[greenspore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Greenspore)." If that's the case, we have contaminated [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) carcasses with the disease available if needed.

We'd also like to thank you for sending us your notes regarding your experiments, we've all had quite a grand time reading them.

[Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md)
