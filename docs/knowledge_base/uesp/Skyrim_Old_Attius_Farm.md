[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Old_Attius_Farm.jpg) Old Attius Farm **Old Attius Farm** is an abandoned Imperial [farm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Farms) south-southeast of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

The first time you approach the farm, [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) will be battling three [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md). Once the ash spawn are dead, you will receive a miscellaneous quest objective to talk to the captain. Speaking with him initiates the quest [March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead). Outside the house is a dead [Redoran guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard_(dead).md) that carries an [Elven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword), [bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Bow), and arrows, and a full set of [Bonemold armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold#Bonemold_Armor) (the chest armor is the [pauldron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold#Bonemold_Pauldron_Armor) version).

The [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds a trapdoor to the house leading to **[Old Attius Farm Cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Attius_Farm_Cellar)**. You will need to enter the cellar during the quest [Buyer Beware](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Buyer_Beware).

## Walkthrough
The farm is along the road leading south from Raven Rock. There is little left of the farmhouse, but the plants in the two gardens still grow among the weeds. There is an empty chest on the porch, and a [trama root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root) grows through the back wall. In the left-hand garden are ten [ash yams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam) (including one growing outside the fence), three [scathecraws](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw), and a trama root. In the right-hand garden are eight ash yams, two scathecraws, and a trama root. An additional seven trama roots grow in the immediate vicinity, with a large number of scathecraw plants between the farm and Raven Rock.

## Notes
- Directly west of the farm is an [unmarked location on a small island](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unmarked_Places_Solstheim#Reavers.27_Folly). Three [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod) plants and a [heart stone deposit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone_Deposit) are found on the island. In the middle is a small camp with two bloodied bedrolls, a [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel), a box holding two bowls of [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal) and a pair of [bonemold gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Gauntlets), a food barrel, and two dead [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md). There is a boat on the southern side of the island, with a sack inside and a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on a crate nearby. Beware, as three [ash hoppers](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper.md) will emerge from the ground as you explore the camp.
- According to Captain Veleth, the farm was destroyed by the eruption of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) almost two hundred years ago, in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5).
- The dead Redoran Guard's inventory replenishes itself when the farm resets.
