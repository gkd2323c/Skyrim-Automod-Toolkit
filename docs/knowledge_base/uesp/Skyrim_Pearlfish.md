## Sources
Three guaranteed Pearlfish can be found in the ocean northwest of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), directly north of [Windward Ruins](Skyrim_Windward_Ruins.md), and can be caught without a fishing rod.

Several schools of Pearlfish spawn by the unmarked clam shack west of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md), and can be caught without a fishing rod.

A few feet south of the [Solitude Lighthouse](Skyrim_Solitude_Lighthouse.md) is a small group of Horkers. Right in between the lighthouse and the horkers are 3 Pearlfish in the shallow water.

A school of one to three in the shallows southwest of [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md). They're in a small inlet that has an oyster and a clam next to each other on the shore.

One school in the far northwest corner of the map along the coast in a cove next to the world border.

One school in the shallow ocean west of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), north of [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md).

One school southeast of [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md), off the northern tip of an island with two clams on it. They are somewhat deep underwater and might be difficult to spot from the surface.
