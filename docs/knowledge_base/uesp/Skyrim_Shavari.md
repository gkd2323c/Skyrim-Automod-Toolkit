She wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5) and carries a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shavari%27s_Note) detailing the task she has been set, along with an [elven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger).

## Related Quests
- **[A Cornered Rat](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md)**: Locate [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### A Cornered Rat
While in Riften she will follow you around. If you attempt to talk to her while in Riften she will simply brush you aside and say:

*"Pff. Go away, if you know what's good for you."* Once you have found Esbern she will throw off her disguise and announce:

*"And now you pay for meddling in the Thalmor's affairs."* *"The Thalmor's memory is long."* *"Esbern cannot elude us, fool."*
## Notes
- Another female Khajiit named Shavari is mentioned in the [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion) quest [Whispers of Death](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Whispers_of_Death) as requiring the [Dark Brotherhood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Dark_Brotherhood)'s services. - The base game version of Oblivion's *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV__Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions "Shavari" under the female Khajiit "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".
- Shavari is the only Khajiit that can be found inside a city. She can only be found in Riften, though.
- If Shavari attacks you in front of Riften residents, there is a chance that they will become hostile towards her and attack.
- If you give Shavari the slip by leaving Riften through one gate and reentering through another before entering the Ratway, she may not appear again even after you escort Esbern out of the city.
- She knows no spells despite her class and skills.
- She can be killed prior to rescuing Esbern, without incurring a bounty or reaction from the guards.
