[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-place-Blackreach_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blackreach_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackreach_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackreach_02.jpg) Blackreach Vulthuryol was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Dark-Overlord-Fire" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nahkriin-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dragon-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Shout-4)</sup>

It is unknown when or how Vulthuryol accessed [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), but temporal disturbances were a method known to teleport massive creatures from all over Tamriel into the caverns.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TC-5)</sup> As of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Mzark Cavern system acted as his lair.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Blackreach-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VUL-7)</sup> Vulthuryol was not recorded in the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md)'s *[Atlas of Dragons](Lore_Atlas_of_Dragons.md)*.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AOD-8)</sup>

In 4E 201, the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md#Last_Dragonborn) ventured to Blackreach.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Through use of the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), they awoke Vulthuryol. The two did battle in the Mzark Cavern, and the Dragonborn absorbed the dragon's soul.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VUL-7)</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Vulthuryol was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LAD-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Arngeir-11)</sup>
