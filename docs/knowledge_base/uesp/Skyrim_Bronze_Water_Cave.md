## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-quest-Beneath_Bronze_Waters.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Beneath_Bronze_Waters.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Beneath_Bronze_Waters.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Beneath_Bronze_Waters.jpg) Fishing supplies [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-place-Bronze_Water_Cave_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bronze_Water_Cave_Fishing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bronze_Water_Cave_Fishing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bronze_Water_Cave_Fishing.jpg) Fishing supplies The entrance to the cave is on the north shore of the semi-frozen [Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md). Several Dwemer bronze pipes on either side and in front of the entrance seem to be distributing hot water or steam into the lake.

### Bronze Water Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bronze_Water_Cave.jpg) Bronze Water Cave interior Upon entering, there is a short tunnel that opens into a small chamber containing more pipes along the walls. Two leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) have made this cave their home. On the ground are various bones, both human and animal, as well as blood and gore. Among the debris is a random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md). Against the east wall is an unlocked Dwemer [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and farther to the south (toward the exit), beside the same pipe, is an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

The cave's main purpose is to serve as an escape route from the flooded *[Irkngthand Sanctuary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand#Irkngthand_Sanctuary)* near the end of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest [Blindsighted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blindsighted), with the exit from *Irkngthand Sanctuary* in the back of the cave. During the quest, this cave will not contain any animals. After completion of the quest, Bronze Water Cave can be used to access *Irkngthand Sanctuary*.

## Bugs
- The [Dwarven spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) ambush outside Bronze Water Cave can be triggered prematurely due to the spiders not being disabled before the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) quest [Beneath Bronze Waters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beneath_Bronze_Waters). If the ambush is triggered prematurely, the quest will then be impossible to start, as the spiders are required for the quest. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.9a, fixes this bug.
- The trigger box for Kinthal's Saints bandit camp at Bronze Water Cave may attempt to disable itself even though it's controlled by an enable parent. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The Disable When Done flag has been removed as it's also flagged Do Once and won't trigger again anyway.
