The only shellfish found in Skyrim are [clams](Skyrim_Clam.md), and unlike in previous games they do not produce pearls. However, if [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) is installed, pearls and small pearls can be harvested in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) from [pearl oysters](Skyrim_Pearl_Oyster.md).

## Ingredients
Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 3 in the [Greathall](Skyrim_Greathall.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) ([Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)), in a locked display case
- 2 in [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 1 in [Valerica's Study](Skyrim_Valerica%27s_Study.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))
- 1 in [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> alongside 4 Small Pearls

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is a "rare" ingredient.

The remaining way to obtain pearls is in random loot, but the chances of pearls appearing in any given location are generally small. The best bets are:

- Uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) have a 2% chance of containing one
- [Warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md) and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) have a 0.5% chance of carrying one

## Notes
- Pearls are only a "rare" ingredient for apothecary merchants. For the other [leveled lists](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md) that might spawn them, they are an "uncommon" ingredient.

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot normally be bought from apothecary merchants, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
