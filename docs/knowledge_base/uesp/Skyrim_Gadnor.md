[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-npc-Gadnor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gadnor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gadnor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gadnor.jpg) Gadnor's corpse **Gadnor** is a dead [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(class).md) lying on top of a rock inside [Crystaldrift Cave](Skyrim_Crystaldrift_Cave.md). Lying beside him is [Gadnor's Staff of Charming](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gadnor%27s_Staff_of_Charming). A copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[Mystery of Talara, v 2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v_2)* can be looted from his corpse.

He wears randomly determined [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor). There is a 50% chance that it will be unlevelled [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md). If not, at levels 1-5, it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at levels 6-18 it can be [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at levels 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Horn_Armor). Additionally, he has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md), but at levels 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at levels 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Bracers), and at levels 19 and onward, it can be [scaled bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers).

He carries a leveled bow as well. From levels 1-3 it will be either a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) or a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md), from levels 4-9, it will be an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md), from levels 10-14 it will be an [orcish bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Bow), from levels 15-19 it will be a [dwarven bow](Skyrim_Dwarven_Bow.md), and from levels 20 and onward, it will be an [elven bow](Skyrim_Elven_Bow.md). He also carries twenty leveled arrows which follow the leveling of the acompanying bow. Lastly, he has a 25% chance of carrying a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or a bit of gold.

## Notes
- According to the game data he was supposed to play a part in the quest [The Mind of Madness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Mind_of_Madness) and even had a schedule; he was supposed to sleep from midnight to 8am and then wander around [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) asking the player for help. At some point during development, he was replaced with [Dervenin](Skyrim_Dervenin.md) and instead put in Crystaldrift Cave.
- With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) Creation, Gadnor is given a bit of backstory, and is now associated with the quest to get the artifact [Nerveshatter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nerveshatter).
