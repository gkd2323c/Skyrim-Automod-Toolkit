—Radac Stungnthumz Radac Stungnthumz was a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [weaponsmith](Lore_Smithing.md) and soldier who inhabited [Bamz-Amschend](Lore_Bamz-Amschend.md). Although he was merely a warrior and craftsman, he was remembered as a powerful mystic and author of ancient texts. After his death, he remained as a ghost and played a crucial role in the process of reforging and reigniting the [Trueflame](Lore_Trueflame.md), a legendary weapon of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg/200px-TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Trueflame.jpg) Trueflame In the ancient times, Radac was a soldier and a weaponsmith. He was responsible for crafting tools and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) for his troops. He lived and worked in the Dwemer city Bamz-Amschend.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RS-2)</sup>

As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Radac's name was known among the citizens of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). According to rumors, he was a powerful Dwemer mystic who once lived in Bamz-Amschend. It was believed that although he was long gone some of his writings could be preserved.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-YGG-3)</sup>

In the same year, the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was tasked by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) to reforge the [Trueflame](Lore_Trueflame.md) as a step to retaking their station and reuniting [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The sword had been broken into three pieces, one which the goddess provided. Another two were held by [King Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Hlaalu_Helseth.md)'s champion, [Karrod](Lore_Karrod.md) and [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md). Nerevarine ultimately obtained all the fragments. Once the pieces were recovered, the smith [Yagak gro-Gluk](Lore_Yagak_gro-Gluk.md) was tasked with reconstructing the blade, which was a success. It was the smith who shared the rumor of the mystic with the hero.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-YGG-3)</sup>

Seeking to enchant the blade, the Nerevarine then journeyed into [Bamz-Amschend](Lore_Bamz-Amschend.md), hoping to retrieve Radac's writings. There, they discovered Radac's ghost. Radac revealed that he was not a mystic, but a mere soldier and artisan, but agreed to help nevertheless, as his skills were more than sufficient to help the hero. He applied Pyroil Tar to the blade, which the Nerevarine had retrieved from the depths of Norenen-dur. With his help the blade was finally reforged and reignited.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBON-1)</sup>
