Tharhan **the Mutilant** was a notorious historical figure who took part in the Gradual Massacre in [1E 227](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_227). Some historians speculated that he and [Tharanus Ye Redde-Hand](Lore_Tharanus_Ye_Redde-Hand.md), a presumed ancestor of the noble [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) were one and the same person. Lady Euphemia Glaber's theory identified Tharhan with Tharanus. According to Chancellor [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) this equation was based on questionable interpretations of obscure and questionable footnotes with the intention of distorting them for slanderous political purposes. This connection was disproved by the Chancellor, who discovered the text of the "Scroll of Precursor [Saints](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md)" in the vaults below the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in [2E 541](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_541), which contained more details regarding the deeds of Tharanus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSRMT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HTON-2)</sup>
