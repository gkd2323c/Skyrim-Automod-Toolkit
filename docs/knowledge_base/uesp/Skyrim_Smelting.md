### Dwarven Metal Ingots
Dwarven Metal Ingots are not crafted from any known ores or ingots. Instead, they are made from melted down Dwemer metal scraps found in most [Dwarven Ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md). The scraps yield different numbers of ingots, as listed in the following table. For more information, see [Dwarven Metal Ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md). Confusingly enough, [*Bent* Dwemer Scrap Metal](Skyrim_Bent_Dwemer_Scrap_Metal.md) is smeltable, but regular [Dwemer Scrap Metal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Scrap_Metal) cannot be smelted at all (see [Bugs](#Bugs) for a workaround on regular Dwemer Scrap Metal). On general principle, you cannot smelt anything that doesn't start with an adjective, with one exception: the [Small Dwemer Lever](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Dwemer_Lever) starts with an adjective, but they still aren't smeltable. [Dwemer cookware](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Dwemer_Dishes) also cannot be smelted into Dwarven Metal Ingots.

## Notes
- NPCs can be seen shoveling coals into the smelter and pouring the liquid into a mold, though if you use the smelter, only the shoveling animation is seen.
- The [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md), [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md) and [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md) [Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) add a smelting pot, which is stirred like a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) but is in fact a smelter.
- Unlike other ores, [Stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> doesn't need to be smelted and is used in the state from which it is mined.
- With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, smelting ore (especially iron) is an excellent way to prepare for [building your own house](Skyrim_Construction.md).
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 1.2.6 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) gives smelters a visual heat blur effect.

## Bugs
- Occasionally, when you activate the Smelter, the animation will not start and you will not enter third-person view if you were in first-person view. When this happens, you will instantly crash upon smelting anything. The workaround is to exit the smelting interface, wait a few seconds, and then try again. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Inconsistently, regular Dwemer scrap metal cannot be smelted, while bent Dwemer scrap metal can. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13050))
