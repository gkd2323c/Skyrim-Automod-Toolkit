[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-npc-Lami.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lami.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lami.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lami.jpg) Lami at the shop counter **Lami** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Alchemy Journeyman) and the proprietor of the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md), an [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) shop in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). She is also the common [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in alchemy.

During the day she can be found at her store until it closes at 8pm, when she will head [home](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md) for a quiet night with her husband [Jorgen](Skyrim_Jorgen.md).

She wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0001be1b), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House) to her house, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Lami%27s_Hut) to her store, a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When talking to you, Lami's greetings almost always focus on either her store, or work in general:

*"If you need any alchemy supplies, I'll do my best to provide them. Assuming I haven't used them all up."* *"Jorgen and I both work hard, all the time. Morthal is important to both of us."* *"I don't get many customers, but that leaves me time to try things out. Experiment, I guess."* *"Oh, do come in! I hope I have what you need."* **Do you have a hard time getting supplies way out here?** *"No, no. Well, sometimes, but that's not the problem. I just often don't have much to do, so I tend to... I try things out. They don't usually work, but I try. Next thing I know, I've used up most of my stock."*

## Rumors
[Angeline Morrard](Skyrim_Angeline_Morrard.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) will refer you to Lami when asked if she can train you in alchemy by saying:

*"No, no. Mostly, I'm just a simple herbalist. Lami over in Morthal should be able to help you."* [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md), a resident of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), will also mention Lami when asked where she learned her alchemy skills, stating:

*"After we left Morrowind, we found ourselves in Skyrim. We wandered around a bit and ended up in Morthal where we took temporary residence with Lami, the town's alchemist. I was fascinated by her work, and over a period of a few years she taught me quite a bit."*
## Unused Dialogue
This dialogue can be found inside of the game's Dialogue Views files, but nowhere else. It seems like it was intended for Lami during [Pieces of the Past](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pieces_of_the_Past):

**What happened to Jorgen's wife?** **(Favor) I need the key to Jorgen's house.** *"I'm sorry, but... what if you're here to hurt Jorgen? I can't take that chance."* **(Failure)** *"You... you promise you'll help him? All right, here's the key to the backdoor of the house. Jorgen doesn't know I have it."* The first topic is player-only and doesn't have a response leftover, but it is quite interesting. It, combined with the dialogue beneath it, implies that Lami originally wasn't Jorgen's wife at all, but seemingly a close friend instead. This can also be seen in the final game, as Jorgen never mentions Lami, and she herself only ever makes one reference to him in a greeting. No one else ever addresses them as a couple, and they aren't even set up as a couple with a relationship marker.

## Notes
- Since Lami doesn't live in her shop, she may not be there when the store is scheduled to open at 8am. She doesn't leave her house until around 8am and it may take her up to an hour to walk there. She won't offer services until she's inside.
