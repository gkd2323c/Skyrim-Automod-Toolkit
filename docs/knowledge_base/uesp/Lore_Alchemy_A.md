### Adamantium Ore
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/TR-item-Adamantium_Rock_01.jpg/200px-TR-item-Adamantium_Rock_01.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-item-Adamantium_Rock_01.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-item-Adamantium_Rock_01.jpg) Adamantium Ore | [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-icon-ingredient-Adamantium_Ore.png) | [Tribunal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:Adamantium_Ore) | \| Burden \| <br> \| --- \| <br> \| Restore Magicka \| <br> \| Poison \| <br> \| Reflect \| | Burden | Restore Magicka | Poison | Reflect |

[Adamantium](Lore_Adamantium.md) (also called **Adamantine**) is a rare and ageless metal used for making valuable armor and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md). It is typically seen in a lustrous silvery color dubbed "Adamantine Silver", though adamantium equipment can also have a stone blue hue or a dark grey coloration. The ore is very heavy and can be used in alchemy. Adamantium armor can appear as either a high-quality heavy weight armor or medium weight armor. It is above the quality of [Mithril](Lore_Mithril.md) or [Orichalcum](Lore_Orichalcum.md), but is inferior to [Stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md) and [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md). The armor has a high potential for enchantment. Adamantium weapons can harm magical creatures that resist normal weapons.
