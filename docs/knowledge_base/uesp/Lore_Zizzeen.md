Grandee Zizzeen was a nobleman from [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md) during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RLS-1)</sup> He was related to [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md) and his mother Grandeya Fanesh, who both were [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md), whereas Zizzeen was a [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RLS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ABKF-2)</sup> Zizzeen was called a meritorious man of "most august memory". The poet Behrouz said that his rectitude was so high that when he accidentally entered a women's bath house, he "forthwith put out both his eyes, lest he commit an indecency".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ABKF-2)</sup> When King [Ramzi the Distrait](Lore_Ramzi_the_Distrait.md) of Sentinel died while Hammerfell was being ravaged by the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTH-3)</sup> Zizzeen attempted to usurp the throne and was supported by the Crowns, but later lost to Fahara'jad.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GZT-4)</sup>
