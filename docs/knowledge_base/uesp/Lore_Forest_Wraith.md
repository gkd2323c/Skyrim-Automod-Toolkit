# Forest Wraith

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/ON-tribute-druid-Forest_Wraith_%28cropped%29.png/200px-ON-tribute-druid-Forest_Wraith_%28cropped%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-druid-Forest_Wraith_(cropped).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-druid-Forest_Wraith_(cropped).png) Forest Wraith *"Have you ever used an axe to fell a tree? Used that wood to craft armor, maybe a weapon itself? Imagine if, upon your first stroke of the axe, the tree you were aiming for struck back."*
— *Druid Gastoc* Forest Wraiths (also known as **dray'hoon**) are evil [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md) that are filled with rage and longing, and are said to be embodiments of death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-ESOLive Firesong-3)</sup> They cannot be reasoned with, and if one has identified you as a threat, it will not relent.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DG-4)</sup> They are speculated to be related to the Allwither,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JG-5)</sup> the representation of death in [druidic](Lore_Druids.md) culture.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESODA-6)</sup>

Forest Wraiths are thought to have been created after the death of [Druid King Kasorayn](Lore_Kasorayn.md) at the hands of the Allwither Cult,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JG-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESODruid King-7)</sup> and were originally guardians but went rogue over the centuries.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-ESOLive Firesong-3)</sup> Some druids willingly "submit to the Green" to become one, allowing wild spirits to possess them and transform themselves into these wooden specters.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DG-4)</sup> Some however are forced to undergo the process unwillingly.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Like [Phoenix Moths](Lore_Moth.md#Phoenix_Moth) and other creatures that inhabit Galen, they represent echoes of the past, when magic was much wilder.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LATD-9)</sup>
