[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-creature-Boneman.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Boneman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Boneman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Boneman.jpg) A Boneman (Missile) **Bonemen** are black [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) found within the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). You can summon them to fight for you after learning [Conjure Boneman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Boneman) from a spell tome in the Soul Cairn. Like other creatures found in the Soul Cairn, bonemen leave behind a pile of black "soul ashes" when defeated. These yield a random-sized [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) and a Soul Cairn alchemical [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and have a 10 percent chance of containing an inexpensive piece of [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md). [Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md), whether fighting for or against you, will summon bonemen in combat.

## Gallows Hall Creation
The [Gallows Hall](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) lets you summon a permanent Boneman follower using the [Bone Forge](Skyrim_Bone_Forge.md).

- It is summoned using 1 [Bone Meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal), 1 [Greater Soul Gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Soul_Gem) and 1 [Frost Salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md)
- The Boneman summoned using the forge does not count towards your maximum number of summons, making it a free permanent follower.
- It does not regenerate health and is protected, being downed when its health is too low instead of being outright killed. It can be healed using the [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing) and [Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead) spells.

## Notes
- Bonemen can also be encountered in [Brittleshin Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brittleshin_Pass) during the quest [The Staff of Hasedoki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Staff_of_Hasedoki) as part of the [Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_Staff_of_Hasedoki.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). They will immediately disappear once you kill [Ivara of Olenveld](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ivara_of_Olenveld) and [Lushak gra-Ragdam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lushak_gra-Ragdam).
