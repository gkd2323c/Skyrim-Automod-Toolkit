[Sumiril's Book](Lore_Sumiril%27s_Book.md), Passage 3 *The story of a boy named Ostion*
---
The powerful sent [Ostion](Lore_Wilderking.md) to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). They told him to shape Valenwood and build a great city there. They sent builders to help. But Valenwood was not like the land where Ostion grew up. Valenwood was wild and angry and when the boy asked it to move, it said "No."
