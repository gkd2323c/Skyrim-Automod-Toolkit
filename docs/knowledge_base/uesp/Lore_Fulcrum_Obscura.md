# Fulcrum Obscura

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/ON-item-Fulcrum_Obscura.jpg/200px-ON-item-Fulcrum_Obscura.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Fulcrum_Obscura.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Fulcrum_Obscura.jpg) The Fulcrum Obscura The Fulcrum Obscura is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by [Prior Durdryn](Lore_Durdryn.md) of the [Keepers of the Dead](Lore_Keepers_of_the_Dead.md) with the assistance of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md). It was said to be able to recover lost and forgotten memories, of names and knowledge, including from the dead.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TPF-1)</sup>

## History
Durdryn was well known for his knowledge of Daedra and his association with the [Tribunal Gods](Lore_Tribunal.md). At some point, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) gifted the Prior a tusk from some unknown beast and asked him to create a relic from it. After several days of meditation, Durdryn summoned Hermaeus Mora to assist in creating this item.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TPF-1)</sup> The precise details are unknown in regard to any bargains made between the pair, but one source claims that Hermaeus Mora provided the runes needed to imbue the Fulcrum with the power to uncover secrets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OTFO-2)</sup>

Soon after, the completed Fulcrum Obscura was presented to Vivec, though there are no stories suggesting Vivec ever used the Fulcrum. However, there were several rumors over the years of Durdryn's use of the relic. In the last years of his life, Durdryn became increasingly obsessed with his creation and withdrew from society, and allegedly starved to death.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TPF-1)</sup> After Prior Durdryn's death, his fellow Keepers declined to use the relic, fearing its power and influence. It was then interred in Durdryn's tomb within the Necrom Necropolis, where is remained until some time around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TPF-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KOTF-3)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/ON-quest-A_Hidden_Fate_04.jpg/200px-ON-quest-A_Hidden_Fate_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Hidden_Fate_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Hidden_Fate_04.jpg) Curate Gadayn using a fragment of Fulcrum Obscura The Hidden Kindred cultists in service of their Princes and with the assistance of the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) [Torvesard](Lore_Torvesard.md), infiltrated the Necropolis to steal the Fulcrum Obscura. While their plan was disrupted, Torvesard managed to take half of the Fulcrum with him, and was able to use it to recover long-lost information that had dangerous consequences for the world.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KOTF-3)</sup> At some point after this, the Fulcrum was handed over to the Hidden Kindred leader Blightcrown. When Blightcrown was confronted and slain within the Mythos of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md), the missing half of the Fulcrum Obscura was retrieved by one of the Keepers and returned to its rightful place within the Necropolis.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AUF-4)</sup>

## Notes
- There are differing stories on the material used to create the Fulcrum Obscura. Abbot Invel's research says it was a tusk taken from a massive beast,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TPF-1)</sup> while an unknown Cipher of the Eye says it was an ancient bone that was linked to Necrom's history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OTFO-2)</sup>
