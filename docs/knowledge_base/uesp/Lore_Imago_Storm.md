*Mortals are short-lived, ignorant, and feeble by contrast with the daedra. But you mortals are also potent engines of change and innovation, of desperate and reckless improvisation and industry. Thus do we so prize the fruits of your mundane and arcane engineering. Thus do we bargain and plunder and steal to gain these treasures. We have lived too long, and grow dull and complacent. You live too short, and so are wonderfully sharp and inventive. Does that make sense?*
—Imago Storm (listen Link ⏵) Grand Vizier Imago Storm is the leader of [Dremora Clan](Lore_Dremora_Clan.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> His Oathfather is [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and his clan is in the service of [Clan Dagon](Lore_Clan_Dagon.md). Imago Storm, his Vassal Lords, and the Dremora clan, represent the principle of Destruction as Evolution, aspiring to arts and powers of ever-increasing potency and aesthetic refinement.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IS-2)</sup>

His Oathkin include [Faydra Shardai](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md) and [Xivilai Moath](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md).

## History
When he encountered an [apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) from the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) at the [Havoc Wellhead](Lore_Havoc_Wellhead.md), Imago Storm allied himself with them. The escalating sibling rivalry between Faydra Shardai and Xivilai Moath displeased him as he saw it as a cause for disaster, such as Moath instigating the conquest of the Battlespire<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BA-3)</sup><sup>:158</sup> which may attract the attention of the other Daedric Princes ([Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) in particular),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BA-3)</sup><sup>:159</sup> and Faydra's failure at the [Chimera of Desolation](Lore_Chimera_of_Desolation.md) which gave the apprentice the opportunity to add both the [Armor of the Savior's Hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Armor_of_the_Savior%27s_Hide) and the [Spear of Bitter Mercy](Lore_Spear_of_Bitter_Mercy.md) artifacts to their arsenal to use against Dagon's forces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BSL5-4)</sup> As a result, Imago made ​a deal with the Battlespire apprentice in an attempt to restore order to Clan Dagon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IS-2)</sup> Imago gave the apprentice half of Dagon's [incantory name](Lore_Nymic.md) after the apprentice banished Xivilai and Faydra for him in order to make it look like Dagon had been banished by an army of invincible battlemages rather than just one.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BA-3)</sup><sup>:159</sup>

With the Prince banished to the void of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) by the apprentice, Storm assumed temporary control of Clan Dagon and restored it to its normal stability.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IS-2)</sup>
