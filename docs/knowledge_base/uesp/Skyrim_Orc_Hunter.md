They chase and kill wild animals, and if spoken to, they will sell meat from their hunts to you.

If one hunter accidentally strikes another during their hunt, they may ignore their prey and fight each other instead.

## Bugs
- Orc hunters may turn hostile towards you or each other due to their agression value of "very agressive". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.11, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To manually fix this, select them using the console and enter `setav aggression 0` followed by `resetai`.
