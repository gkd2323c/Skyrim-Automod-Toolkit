[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Farmhand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Farmhand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Farmhand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Farmhand.jpg) Farmhand **Farmhands** are generic workers that you can ask your [Personal Steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) to hire for [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md). Once hired, two of them will work the farm and dwell in the [Goldenhills Farm Bunkhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenhills_Farm_Bunkhouse). If the bunkhouse hasn't been [constructed](Skyrim_Farming_Construction.md) yet, they will instead set up bedrolls and camp outside.

You can also personally hire [Angrenor Once-Honored](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Angrenor_Once-Honored), [Brenuin](Skyrim_Brenuin.md), [Embry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Embry), or [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi) instead of the two generic farmhands.
