**Conjure Ash Guardian** is an expert level conjuration spell that summons an [ash guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md) to aid you in combat. A [heart stone](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) is required to ensure that the resultant ash guardian does not attack you.

## Effects
- [Conjure Ash Guardian](Skyrim_Summon.md), summons indefinitely.

## Notes
- The spell is implemented as a [Rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) instead of a summon, meaning it differs from the other spells in several points: - An Ash Guardian does not count towards the number of units you have summoned, and does not replace or is replaced by any other summon.
- It is not affected by the [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls) perk, a second Guardian will always unsummon the first.
- A conjured Ash Guardian's soul can be captured, making it possible to farm greater-sized souls.
- The spell doesn't count as being cast on self and thus works even with active [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md).
- The Guardian does not follow you, but instead stays at the location marked by the rune.
- The stats of the Ash Guardian summoned are:

| Creature ([ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md)) | Lvl | Carries | Abilities | Attacks | [Soul](Skyrim_Souls.md) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ash Guardian <br> ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 01dbdc) | 30 | None | - [Resist Damage](Skyrim_Resist_Damage.md) 100% <br> - [Waterwalking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waterwalking) <br> - Immune to [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) <br> - Cannot be [Reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) | - Ash Spray spell (Embers and heated ash cause 35 points per second.) | Greater |

- The Ash Guardian spell can be quite useful for training skills. When conjured, it becomes a free standing creature; without a heart stone, it is in essence an on-demand enemy.
