[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-npc-Salonia_Caelia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Salonia_Caelia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Salonia_Caelia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Salonia_Caelia.jpg) Salonia Caelia **Salonia Caelia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). She's an underling of [Vingalmo](Skyrim_Vingalmo.md).

Salonia wears a set of black [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). She is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. She carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), as well as a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

## Quest-Related Events

### The Bloodstone Chalice
During [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md), you will be sent to fill the [eponymous chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) from the Bloodspring at [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md). After doing so, Salonia and another vampire, [Stalf](Skyrim_Stalf.md), will appear. They will claim that Harkon sent them, though their dialogue will indicate that they were sent by Vingalmo and [Orthjolf](Skyrim_Orthjolf.md):

- **Salonia**: *"It's really too bad, you know. The little accident you had here, completely unexpected..."*
- **Stalf**: *"Yeah, too bad. Lord Harkon's new favorite, dead so soon after joining the family."*
- **Salonia**: *"We're just lucky I was here to return the Chalice to Vingalmo, so he could make sure Harkon gets it back."*
- **Stalf**: *"Wait, what? That's not what we agreed. We take it back together."*
- **Salonia**: *"Idiot. You didn't really think I'd let you walk out of here either, did you? Vingalmo wants you both dead."*
- **Stalf**: *"Well that's just fine. Orthjolf told me to finish off anyone who got in the way."*

They will become hostile at this point and attack you and each other.

## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for her reads:

- *"Salonia won't talk about her past — her life before becoming a vampire. There are rumors that maybe she was part of the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md), maybe an assassin... Whatever it was, it wasn't pretty. No one in the court trusts her, especially due to her allegiance to Vingalmo. It's suspected that her presence is a response to Stalf, Vingalmo's answer to Orthjolf's apparent attempt to gain support by stacking the court."*

- The guide mistakenly refers to her under the name "Salonia Viria". An NPC with this name did [previously appear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Salonia_Viria) in the series, in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)'* s *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* DLC, and may be the cause for the error.
