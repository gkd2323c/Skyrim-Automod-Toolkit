[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-place-Honor%27s_Rest.jpg/200px-ON-place-Honor%27s_Rest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Honor%27s_Rest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Honor%27s_Rest.jpg) Honor's Rest, circa 2E 582 Honor's Rest is a tomb, built by [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md), [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md), and [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) masons after the destruction of the First [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md). It serves as the final resting place of [Baloth Bloodtusk](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md) and [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md), two great heroes and rivals, buried together side by side.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Trystan-1)</sup>

## History
*"Orcs, Redguards, and Bretons built this place. They buried their dead together.
* They say a bond is formed on the battlefield, even among enemies. Perhaps both sides tired of death in the end."* —Lozruth* During, the Siege of Orsinium, [King Joile](Lore_Joile.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) and his Reachman general, [Mercedene](Lore_Mercedene.md) of the [Winterborn](Lore_Winterborn.md), tricked the Orc hero Baloth Bloodtusk and his Savage Sons, to join King [Golkarr](Lore_Golkarr.md) of Orsinium. Afterwards, he manipulated Shinji into having a duel with Bloodtusk over Orsinium's fate. The duel was a ruse set up, to take out Orsinium and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)'s greatest Champions. The plot was largely successful, leading to the successful assassination of both Heroes. After their deaths, Bloodtusk's brother Rakoug and Shinji's friend, Lord Trystan decided to build them both a tomb in western [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). The tomb became known as Honor's Rest. It is built primarily of Orcish architecture; however, Redguard and Breton Elements can be found throughout the tomb.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAo OF-2)</sup>

The tomb was discovered in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by a pair of scholars who disagreed on whose tomb it was, and with the help of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), they managed to unseal the tomb, and experienced visions of Gaiden Shinji's and Baloth Bloodtusk's final moments during the Siege of Orsinium, and discovered the tomb belonged to both heroes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAo OF-2)</sup>
