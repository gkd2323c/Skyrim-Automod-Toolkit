## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md) in [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md).
2. Travel to [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md).
3. Talk to her father, [Verner](Skyrim_Verner_Rock-Chucker.md).
4. Return to Sylgja to receive your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-quest-Special_Delivery_%28Sylgja%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Special_Delivery_%28Sylgja%29_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Special_Delivery_(Sylgja)_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Special_Delivery_(Sylgja)_02.jpg) Sylgja's parents work in the mine.
### Shor's Stone
In [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) you will come across [Sylgja](Skyrim_Sylgja.md), who tells you that her parents live in [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) and asks if you will deliver a satchel of letters to her father there. She would take them herself, but she recently suffered a leg injury in [the local mine](Skyrim_Redbelly_Mine.md). Agree to deliver the letters for her, and she gives you [her satchel](Skyrim_Sylgja%27s_Satchel.md).

### Darkwater Crossing
Travel to Darkwater Crossing and find Sylgja's father, [Verner Rock-Chucker](Skyrim_Verner_Rock-Chucker.md), who will usually be hard at work in [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md) or resting in his house nearby. Verner is pleased with the letters and asks you to deliver a [satchel](Skyrim_Verner%27s_Satchel.md) in return, and suggests that Sylgia will be happy to compensate you for your efforts.

### Back to Shor's Stone
Return to Shor's Stone and give Sylgja the satchel from her father. Thanking you for your efforts, she rewards you with a random piece of jewelry.

## Notes
- After completing this quest you will be able to marry Sylgja.
- This quest are marked a suggested level 1+, like a [Truth Ore Consequences](Skyrim_Truth_Ore_Consequences.md), [Sleeping Tree Cave (Quest)](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md), and [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.
