[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Tacitus_Sallustius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tacitus_Sallustius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tacitus_Sallustius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tacitus_Sallustius.jpg) Tacitus Sallustius **Tacitus Sallustius**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md), is the hapless apprentice of [Ghorza gra-Bagol](Skyrim_Ghorza_gra-Bagol.md) at the public smithy in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

Tacitus wakes up at 8am and goes straight to the smithy with Ghorza. At 8pm they stop working and return to [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) for a four-hour dinner until 12am, when they sleep there.

He wears a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron#0005b69f) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0005b69e). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) along with a [selection of common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Conversations
During your first visit to Markarth, you may hear Ghorza scolding him:

**Ghorza**: *"Tacitus? What is this?"*
**Tacitus**: *"Uh, a nail?"*
**Ghorza**: *"Is that what you call it? In the Legion we would have called it 'useless.' Look how brittle the metal is!"*
**Tacitus**: *"I... I'm sorry. I just, well, the forge gets so hot and I thought...."*
**Ghorza**: *"You thought what? You would skip a step? Not heat the metal all the way through? Useless! Now do it again."*

Sometimes Tacitus and Ghorza will converse with each other:

**Tacitus**: *"When will I learn how to forge weapons and armor? Isn't that my job?"*
**Ghorza**: *"Your job is to do what I say. And I say you have many nails and fittings to make before you even touch a blade. Now get to work."*

**Ghorza**: *"You strike your metal like a new mother giving her babe a light slap. Hit it harder."*
**Tacitus**: *"I'm sorry, Ghorza. I just... flinch, and uh...."*
**Ghorza**: *"If you can't pound the dusk out of your iron, it'll break when it's finished. Hit. It. Harder."*
