# Nettlebane

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-item-Nettlebane.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nettlebane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nettlebane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nettlebane.jpg) Nettlebane (Skyrim) The Nettlebane is a crudely-engraved [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) dagger that was crafted by [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), built with the intention to harm the natural world and thus perform sacrifices on [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md). It has the ability to damage ancient flora older than metal, such as the [Eldergleam](Lore_Eldergleam.md), one of the oldest living things in Skyrim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRDanica-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-N-2)</sup>

It is unknown when the Nettlebane was created, but the Hagravens of Orphan Rock used it to sacrifice Spriggans as of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). When the [Gildergreen](Lore_Gildergreen.md) in [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) was dying, the priestess of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), Danica Pure-Spring enlisted the help of a [wandering traveler](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) to retrieve the Nettlebane from Orphan Rock and use it to acquire a piece of the Eldergleam. The warrior raided the witches' coven and obtained the Nettlebane. It is unknown whether they used it to sample the tree or not but the Gildergreen was restored.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRQuest-3)</sup>
