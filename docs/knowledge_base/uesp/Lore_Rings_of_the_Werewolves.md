# Rings of the Werewolves

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-item-Rings_of_the_Werewolves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Rings_of_the_Werewolves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Rings_of_the_Werewolves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Rings_of_the_Werewolves.jpg) Rings of the Werewolves The Rings of the Werewolves<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:664</sup> are the collection of four silver rings personally blessed by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Hunt [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), during the [Great Hunt](Lore_Great_Hunt.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BMHunt-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Majni-3)</sup> The collection is composed of **Ring of Bloodlust**,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ROB-4)</sup> **Ring of Instinct**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ROI-5)</sup> **Ring of the Hunt**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ROTH-6)</sup> and **Ring of the Moon**.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ROTM-7)</sup>

## History
Circa 3E 427 a collection of four rings was blessed by the Hircine himself, during the Great Hunt on Solstheim. Little is known of the exact history of the rings after the Great Hunt, except that they were kept by the members of the Frostmoon Pack, a group of the [werewolves](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) inhabiting the isle of Solstheim for at least approximately 213 years. As of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) they were owned by Majni, the alpha of the pack. It is unknown whether he kept the rings or traded them to another werewolf he deemed worthy of keeping them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BMHunt-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Majni-3)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-VTHAFC-8)</sup>

## Artifacts
Each of the rings was imbued with a unique boon.

### Ring of Bloodlust
Ring of Bloodlust is a simple [silver](Lore_Silver.md) ring imbued with the power that greatly increases the physical prowess of the wearer at the expense of the wearer's defense. It works only when in beast form.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ROB-4)</sup>

### Ring of Instinct
Ring of Instinct is a silver ring piked with a purple gem resembling [amethyst](Lore_Amethyst.md). When the wearer is transformed their perception of time is altered for a short period of time after the transformation.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ROI-5)</sup>

### Ring of the Hunt
Ring of the Hunt is a silver ring piked with a red gem resembling [ruby](Lore_Ruby.md) When the wearer is transformed their natural regenerative abilities are greatly increased.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ROTH-6)</sup>

### Ring of the Moon
Ring of the Moon is a silver ring piked with an orange gem resembling [garnet](Lore_Garnet.md). It was known to enhance the boons lycanthropes can obtain through the attunement to the [Totems of Hircine](Lore_Totems_of_Hircine.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ROTM-7)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-HOWL-9)</sup>
