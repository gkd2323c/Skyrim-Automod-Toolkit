# Rhogar the Destroyer

| Rhogar the Destroyer | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/ON-misc-Vaia%27s_Golden_Ash.png/200px-ON-misc-Vaia%27s_Golden_Ash.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Vaia%27s_Golden_Ash.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Vaia%27s_Golden_Ash.png) Vaia's Golden Ash Rhogar the Destroyer was a figure present in legends and myths of the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) branch of the cult of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md). Rhogar features in the Orsimer religious scripture *[The Book of the Warrior God](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Book_of_the_Warrior_God)* in the passage devoted to the heroine [Vaia](Lore_Vaia.md), the *[Vaia's Courage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Scroll_of_Vaia)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOV-1)</sup> Rhogar is also present in another passage from the same book,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ordatha-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup> and in the text by [Venerable Uugus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Venerable_Uugus).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CS-3)</sup>

Rhogar was said to wield a powerful enchanted hammer. Using its power they shattered entire armies. At a certain point, Rhogar stood against Vaia, who wielded a simple shield of the [ash](Lore_Ash.md) wood. Despite Rhogars' power and superior [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md), Vaia stood her ground as the hammer fell. Holding her shield above her head and believing that Trinimac watched over her, her resolve was not dampened. After a loud crash, Rhogars' mighty hammer was shattered and the small wooden shield survived and managed to repel the blow. Over the ages, the shield became a symbol. Vaia's Golden Ash - the image of the ash tree, became the symbol of the strength, heritage, and unity of the Orsimer people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOV-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CS-3)</sup>
