## Quill of Gemination
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-item-Quill_of_Gemination.jpg/200px-SR-item-Quill_of_Gemination.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Quill_of_Gemination.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Quill_of_Gemination.jpg) Quill of Gemination [Quill of Gemination](Lore_Quill_of_Gemination.md) is a unique custom-made [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) quill. It is no ordinary writing instrument; its remarkable ability to replicate text with meticulous precision made it a priceless tool, particularly when it came to the delicate task of securing signatures. The true marvel of this quill lay in its ability to flawlessly duplicate any writing it transcribed, ensuring that no detail was lost in the process. To function correctly, the quill relies on a unique and confidential ink formula known exclusively to its commissioner.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Quill_of_Gemination.md)
