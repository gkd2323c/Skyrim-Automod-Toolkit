[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-npc-Elgrim.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Elgrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elgrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elgrim.jpg) Elgrim **Elgrim**, an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary), is the proprietor of his namesake store [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md). He looks after both his wife [Hafjorg](Skyrim_Hafjorg.md) and his promising apprentice [Ingun Black-Briar](Skyrim_Ingun_Black-Briar.md). Should anything happen to Elgrim, his wife will take over the shop.

Elgrim's daily routine is heavily [bugged](#Bugs) so he will act unlike any other merchant. His shop is open 24 hours a day, regardless of what he's doing, so you will always be able to barter with him. However, Elgrim will sleep between 11am and 6pm and will then eat a one-hour dinner. He will then wander around the shop, at times eating, sleeping, or using the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). At 2am he takes his lunch, then returns to wandering before it is time for breakfast at 9am. He finishes his routine with an hour of wandering before his bedtime at 11am.

He wears typical merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c) with [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d) and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223b). He wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 20) and carries the [shop key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_Key), a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). Additionally, there is a 75% chance he may carry a random potion, another 75% chance he will carry a random, rare [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and, lastly, a 50% chance he will carry either a [paralysis poison recipe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Poison_Recipe) or an [invisibility potion recipe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Invisibility_Potion_Recipe). He also knows a few spells, specifically [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md).

## Dialogue
When you enter his shop, he lets you know that he's available to serve you:

*"I'm kind of busy, but if you need any potions or ingredients, let me know."* *"I'll be over here if you need any potions or ingredients."* His greetings:

*"You here for lessons like Ingun? Or did you wish to buy something?"* *"Do you have any idea how busy I am? My mixture could be burning as we speak!"* *"Take a look at my supplies if you must, but do hurry... I'm not getting any younger you know!"* Speaking to him:

**Do you work alone?** *"Hardly! In fact, I don't know how I get anything done with all of the distractions I have to put up with. My wife Hafjorg, well, she's a necessary evil I suppose. Not a lick of talent when it comes to alchemy, but she keeps me fed. Then there's Ingun Black-Briar. Bright young thing and a worthy apprentice, when she isn't wasting my rare ingredients."* **Why is your shop down here in the canal?** *"The moisture in the air is better for the ingredients. It isn't every day that you get your hands on fresh [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) or [painted troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Painted_Troll_Fat). It also makes it easier to gather water when the canal is right out the front door. Besides, I also prefer the quiet. I didn't open this business for browsing. Come in, buy what you need and get out."* When you exit a conversation with him:

*"Yes, yes... goodbye."*

## Notes
- In a scene with Ingun, Elgrim makes a direct reference to the rarity of [painted troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Painted_Troll_Fat). Painted troll fat appeared in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion) as a very rare alchemy ingredient, limited to a mere six samples in the entire game. - He also mentions that he gets this ingredient from "[Rythe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Rythe_Lythandas)" in [Cheydinhal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Cheydinhal). Rythe Lythandas also appeared in Oblivion and was related to the [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:A_Brush_with_Death) in which you could get the troll fat. Elgrim's dialogue implies that Rythe is still alive and living in the same city over 200 years later.

## Bugs
- Elgrim was clearly meant to go to bed at 11pm, but his bedtime was set to 11am, making him sleep during most of the shop's intended opening hours. Additionally, his lunch package was set to 2am instead of 2pm. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
