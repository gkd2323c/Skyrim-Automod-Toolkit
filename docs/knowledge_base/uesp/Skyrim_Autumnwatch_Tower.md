[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-place-Autumnwatch_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Autumnwatch_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Autumnwatch_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Autumnwatch_Tower.jpg) Autumnwatch Tower **Autumnwatch Tower** is a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) south of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). Its [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) teaches part of the [Marked for Death](Skyrim_Marked_for_Death.md) [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Bounty: Dragon](Skyrim_Bounty__Dragon.md)**: Kill a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) in its [lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Dragon Seekers](Skyrim_Dragon_Seekers.md)**: Go to a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) with [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) or [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) and kill the dragon. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The location comprises a pair of abandoned towers. A dirt path leads to the southern tower, and a stone path branching to the north leads to the northern tower. There is a [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) at the base of the southern tower. Stairs lead up toward the northern tower, past an old extinguished campfire, with a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sack and various items of clutter nearby. There is a non-respawning patch of [imp stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool) on your right, and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) just to the right outside the entrance to the northern tower.

Inside the northern tower, the bottom floor contains another tanning rack by the entrance, with a set of shelves to the right and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in the middle of the floor. The shelves hold two [deer hides](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide), two [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), and a leveled [potion of Fortify Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Potion). Climbing the spiral stone stairs leads directly to the top floor, where three unowned bedrolls can be found. There is also an [enchanted](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md) pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots) and a [hide helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Helmet) on a crate beside a straw bale. Finally, a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Jornibret's Last Dance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jornibret%27s_Last_Dance)* and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) are on the small crate by a chair.

Inside the southern tower, the bottom floor contains two imp stool fungi, several food sacks and barrels, a loose piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), and a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) on a wooden block. The second floor holds a bedroll, a random rare [book](Skyrim_Books.md), two loose pieces of firewood, a medium coin purse, and a wooden bowl holding a [chicken egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) beside a bale of straw; on the other side of the bale is a food barrel with an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) on top. The top floor contains a [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) that can be looted for a copy of *[Physicalities of Werewolves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves)* beside a crate with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top and the unlocked boss [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). In an open crate beside the skeleton are two bowls of [vampire dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust), and in another open crate on the other side of the chest are two samples of [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales). There is a stone bridge crossing the gap to the word wall.

Before the [dragons' return](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), the towers will be occupied by a group of [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). There are two bandits at the bottom of the northern tower, a bandit outside the southern tower, and a bandit chief at the top.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md). The dragon will likely detect you before you ascend the towers, and can sometimes detect the [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) of [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md) and will fly off to deal with them.

## Notes
- The tower can be reached from Arcwind Point via a wall of dark, sloped rock on the road to Arcwind directly south of the tower. Descending from this point lets you literally get the drop on the Autumnwatch dragon.
- If you fall off the stone bridge to the word wall, there is a small point in the rocks where you may get trapped and be unable to escape. Luckily you can still fast travel from here.
- The dragon at the tower may be killed by undead south of the cliff overlooking the location.
- A [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein) is 210 feet southwest of the tower.

## Bugs

There does not appear to be a definite fix for this, though loading from a previous save may cause the word to appear. You will hear the background music/chanting, and the sound that plays when you learn a word will play, but you will not learn the word.
