## History

### The Vvardvark Experiment
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg) A vvardvark [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/LG-cardart-Vvardvark_Experiment.png/200px-LG-cardart-Vvardvark_Experiment.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Vvardvark_Experiment.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Vvardvark_Experiment.png) Vvardvark Experiment (Legends) Little is known of the early life and background of Varkenel. He managed to ascend the ranks of House Telvanni, eventually attaining the esteemed title of Magister at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup>

Varkenel gained recognition for his creation of the Vvardvarks, an unintended outcome of his ambitious experiment to create a domesticated predator. These peculiar creatures, with their fusion of scales, fur, and unique head protrusions, resulted from Varkenel's attempt to transform Ashlands stock into household predators similar in role to housecats.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup> Varkenel conducted his experiments during a period shortly before 2E 582, during which the vvardvarks emerged as newcomers to the ecosystem of Vvardenfell.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDozen Banded Vvardvark-3)</sup> While he initially regarding his creation as a failure, his daughter developed an affection for a Vvardvark, leading him to permit it as a pet named "Master Scaly-Tale." Instead of eradicating them as intended, he created it a mate to ensure the species' survival.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVE-1)</sup> Within a few years, the vvardvarks rapidly became a common sight on the island of Vvardenfell.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVvardvark-4)</sup> At first, where they came from was considered a mystery, though rumors circulated that correctly linked them back to the Telvanni,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVvardvark-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDozen Banded Vvardvark-3)</sup> with some assuming they were created by a rogue member.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTuxedo Vvardvark-5)</sup>

The public had became endeared to the creatures, and many would be taken as pets.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VFAF-6)</sup> Although some claimed he was a rogue wizard,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTuxedo Vvardvark-5)</sup> he was granted the opportunity by the Archmagister [Nelos Otheri](Lore_Nelos_Otheri.md) himself to expand his experimentation, resulting in breeding programs for Vvardvarks with diverse colors and configurations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TVE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup> The most common of Vvardvark was the tan-colored breed known as the Demotic Vvardvark,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVvardvark-4)</sup> Other breeds of the animal included Dozen-banded Vvardvark, with vibrant tan and black stripes on its scales,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDozen Banded Vvardvark-3)</sup> the Tuxedo Vvardvark with scales resembling the plumage of the [chub loon](Lore_Chub_Loon.md), possibly bred to blend with the flightless birds,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTuxedo Vvardvark-5)</sup> and Mossy Vvardvarks, notable for the green moss growing on their backs.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Mossy_Vvardvark-7)</sup> Ashsorrow Vvardvarks, found near volcanic vents, were popular with [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) children.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

Varkenel also intended to create larger Vvardvarks that could serve as pack animals or mounts,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup> but it was Spellwright Domavas who ultimately brought this vision to life, expanding on Magister's work. These larger Hivethief Vvardvarks were eventually created, and over time, they became favored mounts for certain riders.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The large variety of vvardvarks caught attention of the members of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md)'s [Order of the Lamp](Lore_Order_of_the_Lamp.md). Their loyalty, surefootedness, exceptional tracking abilities, and the belief that they could detect magicka made them ideal companions.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

"Varkenel's Hat Trick" is a performance in which the actor reenacts the creation of the first Vvardvark via a magic trick by pulling it out of a hat, though it is historically inaccurate.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

### Other Creations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-crown_store-Pyrodraconic_Camel-Lizard.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Pyrodraconic_Camel-Lizard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Pyrodraconic_Camel-Lizard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Pyrodraconic_Camel-Lizard.jpg) Pyrodraconic Camel-Lizard Magister Nelos Otheri commissioned Varkenel for other chimeric animal creations, one of which was the Pyrodraconic Camel-Lizard. Varkenel achieved an intense hue recoloration, brimstone expectoration, and a blend of [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) and Telvanni magical techniques. While it was clearly not a true [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md), Varkenel insisted that the creature more than met the terms of the contract.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-PCL-12)</sup>

At some point, he resumed his work by incorporating draconic fleshweave into a new expensive product line known as the [Scaleborn](Lore_Scaleborn.md). Unlike his earlier experiments, this time he had access to authentic draconic materials, which was scavenged for him by the commissioners.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SS-13)</sup> He was comissioned by Queen [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md) to create the creatures for the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md),<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SR-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SW-15)</sup> and considered it a rare pleasure to collaborate with someone outside House Telvanni. He was especially pleased with his cooperation with the Queen of Rimmen and looked forward to accepting her offer of hospitality.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SW-15)</sup>

He operated on a timeline he considered reasonable and manageable.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SR-14)</sup> The Scaleborn creatures he created resembled [senche-raht](Lore_Senche-raht.md), [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md), [rams](Lore_Ram.md),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SS-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SR-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SW-15)</sup> and [bears](Lore_Bear.md), the latter being the fourth form in the series. While bears were not native to Elsweyr, he suggested them as they had proven to be a popular form in his previous works.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SB-16)</sup>

Varkenel also worked on a breed of [Quasigriffs](Lore_Quasigriff.md),<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-POHQ-17)</sup> creatures first bred by [Irastimil Direnni](Lore_Irastimil_Direnni.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-QG-18)</sup> He expressed genuine pleasure in collaborating with Fazudah on new beast concepts, praising the bold choices and striking colors they explored together. He was confident in the creations they produced together.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-POHQ-17)</sup> Thanks to Varkenel's generosity, Fazudah also had the opportunity to design a new Honorfeather breed of [gryphon](Lore_Gryphon.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-HFG-19)</sup> Varkenel's creations also inspired Fazudah’s work in other fields, including the art of body paint.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-HBP-20)</sup>
