[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-item-Ring_of_The_Beast.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_The_Beast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_The_Beast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_The_Beast.jpg) Ring of The Beast The Ring of The Beast is a [ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring) retrieved as part of the [Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest. The ring is made of three bands of bone, with two orange jewels set into it. Its enchantment gives you 100 more [health](Skyrim_Health.md) and causes your unarmed attacks to deal 20 more damage. Unlike most other rings, its bonuses will still apply when you are in [Vampire Lord form](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).
