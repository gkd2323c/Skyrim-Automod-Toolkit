Alfhedil Elf-Hewer was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) [barbarian](Lore_Barbarians.md) who was a master Axemen active in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTD-2)</sup> He was proficient in many axe combat styles such as the barbed axes of the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), the hollow-bladed axes of the western elves, and the six-foot-long axes favored in [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTD-2)</sup> Alfhedil eventually put down roots in the Dunmer stronghold Falensarano near [Tel Aruhn](Lore_Tel_Aruhn.md) where he took to training others.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTD-2)</sup> From this location he encountered and trained the likes of the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup> and Ellabeth, the Queen of the Axe.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTD-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Alfhedil shares his first name with one of the original [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Companions.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wall Tes5-3)</sup>
