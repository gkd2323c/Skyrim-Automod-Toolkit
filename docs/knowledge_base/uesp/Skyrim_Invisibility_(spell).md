*Caster is invisible for 30 seconds. Activating an object or attacking will break the spell.* Invisibility is an expert level [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell that causes the caster to become [invisible](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md) for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md), for 30 secs

## Perks
- [Illusion Dual Casting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Illusion_Dual_Casting), multiplies duration by 2.2 (rounded down)

## Bugs
