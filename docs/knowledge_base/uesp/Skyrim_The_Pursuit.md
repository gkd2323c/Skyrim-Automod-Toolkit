## Quick Walkthrough
1. Enter [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) through [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md).
2. Meet [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md) in the Ragged Flagon.
3. Speak with [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) in the Ragged Flagon.
4. Go to the backyard of [Riftweald Manor](Skyrim_Riftweald_Manor.md).
5. *(Optional)* Deal with [Vald](Skyrim_Vald.md).
6. Enter the house through the top door.
7. Go to [Mercer](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md)'s office.
8. Take the plans.
9. Return to Brynjolf.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-quest-The_Pursuit_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Pursuit_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Pursuit_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Pursuit_02.jpg) You're expected in the flagon
### A Thief Among Thieves
Head to the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) through [The Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md). Meet [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md) after entering through the door to the Ragged Flagon and follow her as she leads you to the Cistern, where [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md), Delvin and Vex are waiting with knives drawn. Karliah pleads clemency and hands over the journal you translated in the previous quest as proof of her innocence. Brynjolf is shocked at the revelations contained within and determines that they must open the vault to see the truth. Follow them as they head to the vault and watch as Delvin and Brynjolf use their respective keys to open it. As soon as the doors open, Brynjolf will let out a cry of horror at seeing the place completely empty; not a single gold coin is left. While Delvin is distraught at seeing their fortune gone, Vex's reaction is more violent; she pulls her dagger out and vows to kill their treacherous Guildmaster immediately. Brynjolf manages to calm the situation and asks to talk to you after assigning the other two to keep an eye out for Mercer. Tell him everything and he'll instruct you to break into [Mercer](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md)'s home to look for clues. However, the place is guarded by a Nord lout named [Vald](Skyrim_Vald.md) who is quite insistent on keeping people out. Brynjolf suggests you speak to Vex about him, since she was at one point intimately acquainted with Vald. Vex suggests the best course of action would be to erase [Vald's debt](Skyrim_Vald%27s_Debt.md) with [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) or run him through and take what you need off his body. You must pass a hard-level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade him to give you the key.

If you don't wish to kill Vald yourself, and don't want to pay the debt, you can sneak to the back of the house and shoot the ramp contraption. Vald will become hostile and chase after you. Lead him to the marketplace or any other area with guards and they will kill Vald. Loot his body and take the key. It is also possible to climb up the exterior of the Temple of Mara, by jumping onto the braziers and from there scaling the roof. From there, you can use Whirlwind Sprint to reach the balcony without ever interacting with Vald.

### A Plot Revealed
Get a good vantage of the mechanism under the ramp and attack it using a ranged weapon (magic, bows, and shouts all work). The ramp will crash down, providing access to the second floor and the rest of the house.

The first room is unguarded. The next has a single guard who should be facing the west wall, allowing you to sneak past easily. The stairs down are to the northwest. At the bottom, another guard will be patrolling.

When you reach the bottom of the stairs, immediately face east and there will be closed doors. Head through them. On the northeastern corner to the barred front door you'll see a Suspicious Cabinet. Open it and then open the false back panel. Inside is a stairway down to the cellar, which leads to an underground system of tunnels.

After avoiding the [pressure plate](Skyrim_Pressure_Plate.md) by the metal gate that you see when you first enter the tunnels, make a left and head south towards a room filled with circle-shaped traps. Looking at the tiles, head south two squares, west two squares, north two squares, west two squares, and south two squares in order to avoid the traps.

The hallway after the trap room has a pressure plate trap right around the corner that will launch a series of darts in front of the plate. Past this trap there is a chest on the right, which was on the other side of the metal gate at the beginning.

Following the hallway, you come to a hallway filled with timed pendulum blades and [battering rams](Skyrim_Battering_Ram.md). Sprinting is a useful way to get past them. Once at the other end, head down the ramp and stop at the closed door. There is a dart trap trigger on the left side of the door. On the other side of the door are, among other things, [Mercer's plans](Skyrim_Mercer%27s_Plans.md), a note titled [Many Thanks](Skyrim_Many_Thanks.md), a copy of the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Red Kitchen Reader](Skyrim_The_Red_Kitchen_Reader.md)*, and [Chillrend](Skyrim_Chillrend.md) in a case. On the desk is the [Bust of the Gray Fox](Skyrim_Bust_of_the_Gray_Fox.md), which can be sold to Delvin as part of [The Litany of Larceny](Skyrim_The_Litany_of_Larceny.md) quest. There is also a stash of jewels hidden in the bowl on Mercer's desk.

Continue along the path until you reach the entrance to the Ratway Vaults. Fall down the hole (you may be attacked by some [Vagrants](Skyrim_Vagrant.md) and [Lowlifes](Skyrim_Lowlife.md) even if you had previously cleared the Ratway) and turn right to reach the Ragged Flagon. Return to Brynjolf and hand over the plans.

## Notes
- If Vald's debt has been cleared by fetching Maven her quill and you decide to go with Brynjolf's suggestion of shooting down the ramp to the second floor, Vald becomes hostile upon seeing you for "missing" the shot.
- Three leveled generic bandits guard the inside of the house. However, three unique NPCs might've once been meant for this task. Three Nord bandits named [Hrolmir](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Hrolmir), [Skeggr](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Skeggr), and [Reves](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Reves) exist in the files, but they are very unfinished. All of their editor IDs are prefixed with **TG07**, though, the internal identification of the quest.
- It is possible to scale the roof of [Riftweald Manor](Skyrim_Riftweald_Manor.md) and approach the balcony from above, negating the need to activate the ramp.
- The quest marker in Riftweald Manor is not positioned at the quest target and is thus not useful here.
- Vald and the Marauders will attack each other if you lure the Marauders out of the house.
- Members of the Thieves Guild will have varying reactions if you attempt to talk to them before proving Karliah's innocence.

## Bugs
- If you loot Mercer's Plans from Riftweald Manor prior to completing [Hard Answers](Skyrim_Hard_Answers.md) you will be unable to progress through **The Pursuit**. Instead Karliah will stand outside Snow Veil Sanctum and not move or speak with you. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. The barrel people were using to gain early access to Mercer's house has been moved to avoid this from happening.

- Dropping the plans and entering console command `setstage tg07 10` will start the quest normally.
- When Brynjolf goes to open the vault door, it may already be open. This was likely because the door wasn't owned by him and so could be pushed open by any NPC who wandered too close. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- It's possible that Vald won't come talk to you at the gate when you bring him the note to clear his debt, becoming hostile towards you instead. This can occur if you've walked too close to the yard after the quest has started, but before erasing Vald's debt. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- If you have picked the lock to Mercer's back yard, Vald will be hostile and offer no talk option for the peaceful solution. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. The gate has been set to only open with the house key.

- To fix this, close the gate and wait outside the yard until he stands in front of you, then talk to him through the gate.
- If you enter Riftweald Manor prior to starting the quest, Mercer's Plans may already be present in the treasure room. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, addresses this issue. The note will now be disabled until the quest has begun. The fix from USKP 1.0 that blocked roof access prior to the quest has also been undone.
- If you accept a quest from Vex after the vault but before you talk to Brynjolf, you may not be able to speak to her about Vald. Though there will be a quest marker above her head, you will not get the option when speaking to her.
- There's a chance that neither the front or back gates to Riftweald Manor will become accessible, nor can the ramp be shot down by an arrow. Vald will also refuse to talk to you regardless of whether or not you complete Vald's Debt quest. If this happens, the only way to proceed forward is to scale the rooftops to the upper balcony, where the ramp would have fallen if shot by arrow. This is the only door that will open. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) It is possible to release the ramp using the console command `activate 4ec0b`
- The quest will fail to properly update to stage 50 if you enter Riftweald Manor any way other than the door at the top of the ramp. To prevent this, make sure you enter or exit the manor from that door before going into the treasure room and picking up Mercer's plans. <sup>**?**</sup>
