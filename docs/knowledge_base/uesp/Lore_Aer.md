[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Bear.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Bear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Bear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Bear.jpg) Iron Atronach Bear Aer, **the Conjurer Magnificent** was a pioneer [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) mage. She is accredited with the summoning of the first [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) [bear](Lore_Bear.md) in history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAB-1)</sup>

Little is known of Aer's history. She lived in the late years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and was a conjurer of some renown, earning her title of the Conjurer Magnificent. In [1E 2150](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2150), she performed the first summoning of Iron Atronach Bear.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAB-1)</sup>

By the [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the art of conjuring Iron Atronachs shaped like bears was popularized among the conjuration mages. Aer's notes survived to that time, and, according to them, the lumbering, ursine frame matched the atronach's temperament perfectly, making the calling-ritual far easier than traditional summoning.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAB-1)</sup>
