[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-place-Bal_Foyen.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bal_Foyen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bal_Foyen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bal_Foyen.jpg) Bal Foyen circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Bal Foyen is a small region on the southern coast of the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), east of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). It was a wild expanse of marshland and volcanic landscape until the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and the formation of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). Much of it was turned over to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md)'s former [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) slaves, who planted [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md) and herded [guar](Lore_Guar.md) in the former wastes. The Dunmer-built Fort Zeren is found in southern Bal Foyen, previously used as an entry point for the slaves brought from Black Marsh, and the Argonians established the town of [Dhalmora](Lore_Dhalmora.md) in the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Cases of [Corprus](Lore_Corprus.md) were reported in the region in [2E 581](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_581).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) invaded Bal Foyen as part of a large-scale invasion of Pact territory. The local defenders succeeded in driving off the invaders and warning the city of [Davon's Watch](Lore_Davon%27s_Watch.md) to the west due to the arrival of refugees from [Bleakrock Isle](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md), who had been forced to flee their homes after the island was sacked by the Covenant. However, the Pact suffered heavy losses and civilian casualties during a simultaneous attack on both the dockyards and Fort Zeren, crippling the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>
