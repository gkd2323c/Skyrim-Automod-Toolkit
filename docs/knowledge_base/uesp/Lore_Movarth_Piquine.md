Movarth Piquine was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) vampire who likely lived from sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IB-1)</sup> to [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup>

In life, Movarth was ironically a skilled vampire hunter. He began his career as a trainer in the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), which taught him skill in battle and the value of asking questions. He claimed mastery of unarmed combat. He was stoic and almost never surprised due to his dedication to hunt vampires. Tissina Gray directed him to a priest in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to learn about vampires. Using the priest's knowledge, Movarth hunted many different types of vampires across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), including the [Volkihar](Lore_Volkihar.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and the many tribes of northern [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Movarth failed to find any members of the [Cyrodiil Vampyrum Order](Lore_Cyrodiil_Vampyrum_Order.md) due to their ability to blend into society. When he returned to his mentor, the priest revealed that he was actually a Cyrodilic vampire himself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IB-1)</sup> Taken by surprise, Movarth fell to the priest, who fed on him and turned him into a vampire. The priest later anonymously published the story in the book [*Immortal Blood*](Lore_Immortal_Blood.md).

Suffering and anguish had marked him early in his life, destroying the features which would have otherwise been handsome.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-3)</sup> Movarth went on to become a master vampire and create his own coven. Although he was believed to have been destroyed a century earlier,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Movarth resurfaced in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) and planned to take over the town of [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md) in Skyrim. He turned a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) woman named Alva into a vampire, and commanded her to seduce the guardsmen of Morthal and infect them as well in preparation for a takeover. Movarth could then use the town for protection, and the townsfolk as cattle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> His plans were undone when suspicions were raised and the Jarl ordered a [stranger](Lore_Hero.md) to look into the strange events in town. Movarth and his coven were slain when a mob of villagers stormed his underground lair northeast of town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup>

## Notes
- In the base version of Skyrim, Movarth is a randomly selected [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) race. With [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, he is assigned to the Nord race, and will always appear as a Nord. However, in the lore Movarth was intended to be a Breton.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-3)</sup>
