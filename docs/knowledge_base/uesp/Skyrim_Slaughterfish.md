[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg) A Slaughterfish **Slaughterfish** are small, hostile fish with razor sharp teeth encountered in [bodies of water](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bodies_of_Water), both outside and in [caves](Skyrim_Caves.md). Slaughterfish are difficult to kill because entering deep water forces you to sheathe your weapons, necessitating attacks from land or shallow water. Their corpses may be looted for [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales), while their [eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg) can only be harvested from clusters found underwater. Dead slaughterfish are found in some dungeons.

With the *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md), you can use slaughterfish eggs to breed non-hostile slaughterfish in the fish hatchery at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md).

The [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup> makes it possible to fish for slaughterfish and then eat them raw or cooked.

## Hearthfire
The *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) lets you build a slaughterfish statue on a small base in the [trophy room](Skyrim_Trophy_Room.md) addition of any homestead. The materials needed are:

## Notes
- Slaughterfish might swim away from you and stay at a distance, even if you are still in the water (such as knee depth) and remain that way until you enter the deeper parts of water, where you are unable to attack. Best attacks in these instances are using a follower, or area-of-effect ranged magic (fireball, etc.) coupled with the [aura whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) or [detect life](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Detect_Life) shouts/spells.
