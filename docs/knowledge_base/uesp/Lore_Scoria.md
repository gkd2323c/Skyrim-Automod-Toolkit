# Scoria

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/ON-item-Scoria.jpg/200px-ON-item-Scoria.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Scoria.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Scoria.jpg) Vulk'esh Scoria [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-furnishing-Volcanic_Rock_Cluster.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-furnishing-Volcanic_Rock_Cluster.png) Vulk'esh Scoria Scoria is a type of volcanic rock formed when lava cools upon exposure to air. Though not typically favored for crafting, it holds great interest among skilled [alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md) and scholars.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vinilsare-1)</sup>

**Vulk'esh Scoria**, a special kind of scoria, is acquired from the shed outer crust of the [Vulk'esh](Lore_Vulk%27esh.md) creatures, believed to possess [magical](Lore_Magic.md) properties. While not considered hazardous when separated from the Vulk'esh body, it was thought to potentially instigate dangerous alchemical reactions. It was considered a rare material coveted by wealthy artisans. Some alchemists in [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) believed it could be utilized in crafting potions and powders for fire protection.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vinilsare-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tarrinore-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SFM-3)</sup> It has a gentle warmth when touched, without being excessively hot.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-QI-4)</sup> The Firepot in the central [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) was renowned for its high population of Vul'kesh, making it an excellent location to harvest this material.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SS-5)</sup>

## Notes
- The names of [Valkyn Skoria](Lore_Valkyn_Skoria.md), a notable [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) in service to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), as well [Scorions](Lore_Scorion.md), a type of Daedra, both bear a close resemblance to this material.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Scorion-7)</sup>
