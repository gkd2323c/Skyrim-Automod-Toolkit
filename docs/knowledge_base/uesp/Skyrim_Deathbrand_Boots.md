[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-item-Deathbrand_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Deathbrand_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Deathbrand_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Deathbrand_Boots.jpg) Deathbrand Boots The Deathbrand Boots are the boots of a set of armor that also includes [armor](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Armor.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Gauntlets.md) and a [helm](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Helm.md). They can be found in a treasure chest that has been unearthed by [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) to the north-northwest of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md), but only after you find the [treasure map](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Treasure_Map.md) during the related quest. In appearance, they are identical to [stalhrim light boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Light_Boots), and also have the same weight and [armor rating](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md). Their enchantment [fortifies your carry capacity](Skyrim_Fortify_Carry_Weight.md) by 10 points for every Deathbrand item you wear, maxing out at 40 points with the entire set equipped. They can be tempered with a piece of [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md), with the [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md) perk doubling improvement.

In life, the armor was worn by [its eponymous owner](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md), an infamous pirate captain. However, on the eve of his death, he drew a map and ordered his quartermaster, [Garuk Windrime](Skyrim_Garuk_Windrime.md), to divide his armor and bury a piece at each location throughout [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), as he didn't believe that any of his crew were worthy of succeeding him.
