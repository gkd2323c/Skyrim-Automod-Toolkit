## Walkthrough
Five reavers have made their home in this wreck on a small headland on the southern coast of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). Three burned unlootable bodies are on stakes outside the palisade around the beached wreck, with two [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod) plants to the left of the palisade entrance. Inside the palisade is a [heart stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone) deposit, a campfire with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) over it, and several crates being used as seats. A wooden ramp leads up to the deck of the wrecked ship, with various crates scattered over the deck.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-place-Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wreck_of_the_Strident_Squall.jpg) Below deck Through the doorway to the cabin area, on your right is an adept-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). At the front of the cabin area is a room with two bedrolls and a table with a lit goat horn. On the table is a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), a medium coin purse, and two [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple). Against the east wall is a set of shelves holding a random [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), three common books, a red apple, and a bowl of [apple cabbage stew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Cabbage_Stew), while hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

Descending the stairs, a set of shelves in front of you holds a random [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helmet), two [iron daggers](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), five random books, a medium coin purse, and an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox). To the left are a couple of unusable barrels, and to the right is a doorway to a room with two hanging cages, one of which contains a lootable skeleton. Two more lootable skeletons are on the floor.

Returning to the previous room, six barrels are to the left of the stairs in a slightly flooded area, two of which contain [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), with another flight of stairs descending to the next deck which is completely flooded. In the northern corner of the current deck are two more bedrolls. To the left of the bedrolls is a crack in the wall through which you can see into another area with a dead body, which can only be reached from the deck below. Along the eastern wall is an unlocked chest among some rocks and unusable barrels. The chest contains the object of a [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:First_Edition) for [Cindiri Arano](Skyrim_Cindiri_Arano.md). On top of one of the barrels is a bowl of apple cabbage stew.

Descending to the flooded deck below, an enchanted item of [waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md) and a [light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Candlelight) spell are recommended. At the bottom of the stairs is a lootable skeleton, to your right is a large hole in the hull of the ship, and farther to the right is a dead [alchemist](Skyrim_Alchemist.md) floating in the water carrying a couple of ingredients and an iron dagger and wearing a set of [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes). Ahead to the northwest, you will find a second lootable skeleton and a dead female [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian_(NPC).md), though she carries nothing of interest. A flight of stairs leads back up out of the flooded section to a new area previously seen through the crack. This small space contains a dead [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard_(dead).md), a [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar), a wooden bowl holding a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md), an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel), and a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack).
