[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-npc-Gilfre.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gilfre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gilfre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gilfre.jpg) Gilfre at the mill **Gilfre** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) and the owner of [Mixwater Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill). The [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) recently drew her five assistants off to fight, which means she will [buy firewood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) herself, and will also sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md) if the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on is installed. If her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) is raised high enough, she will become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) and will also allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

The whole mill is in a state of disarray, but Gilfre tries her best to keep it going. She wakes up every morning at 8am and immediately heads out to the mill, lifting and loading the giant logs of timber by herself. She keeps going for a whopping fourteen hours, interrupted only by a two-hour lunch inside her [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gilfre%27s_House) at 2pm. She finally stops working at 10pm and enters the house for a bit of relaxation before her bedtime at midnight.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd) and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), the [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Gilfre%27s_House), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
In typical Skyrim fashion, Gilfre will complain about the war and how it affects her when greeting you:

*"I used to have five good men who worked the mill with me. Then this war started and they all ran off. Idiots with mead in their blood."* *"There's plenty of good wood around here, but I just can't harvest it all by myself. Everyone able-bodied is fighting."* *"Of course, without functioning mills, who will make the arrows for our soldiers? Men have no sense of irony."*

## Notes
- Gilfre does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female Imperial face. She shares this quirk with [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md)'s innkeeper, [Valga Vinicia](Skyrim_Valga_Vinicia.md).
- [Gemma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gemma_Uriel) and [Ganna Uriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ganna_Uriel) may mention her in conversation. They also indirectly refer to her as a Nord, despite her appearing as an Imperial.
