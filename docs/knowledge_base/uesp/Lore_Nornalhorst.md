[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/OB-place-Nornalhorst.jpg/200px-OB-place-Nornalhorst.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nornalhorst.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nornalhorst.jpg) Nornalhorst circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Nornalhorst is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), located east of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup> Its lower area of *Nornalhorst Sel Gandrevla* can be roughly translated as *"Hall of Gifted Time"*.

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/ON-place-Nornalhorst.jpg/200px-ON-place-Nornalhorst.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Nornalhorst.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Nornalhorst.jpg) Nornalhorst circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Gray Vipers bandit gang had taken residence in the ruins.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> They preyed upon refugees who came to nearby [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md) fleeing from the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md), stealing their possessions and killing some.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jurana-3)</sup> They'd apparently killed Mercuro's brother Scarius, whom he buried with his ancestors, facing Nornalhorst, and swore to avenge him.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TS-4)</sup> Some soldiers were hired to retrieve various mementos from the ruins and return them to their rightful owners in Vlastarus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jurana-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mementos-5)</sup>

By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the subterranean halls of the ruins were occupied by a coven of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OB-6)</sup>
