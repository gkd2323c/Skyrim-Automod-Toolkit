Kantus Jeril was a [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md). For his heroism at [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md), he was appointed the first Count of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), a settlement located in the rugged [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is unknown if he is a direct ancestor of the Valga family, who ruled Chorrol at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).
