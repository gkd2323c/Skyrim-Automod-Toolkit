Notes on Khajiiti worship *A Khajiiti prayer-chant*
---
[![ON-concept-Khajiit Banner.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/f6/ON-concept-Khajiit_Banner.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Khajiit_Banner.png)
- Visitors to any [Temple of the Two-Moons Dance](Lore_Temple_of_Two-Moons_Dance.md) might hear these short prayers uttered from dim cloisters in the native [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) tongue, [Ta'agra](Lore_Ta%27agra.md). Worshiping Khajiit might be heard repeating these chants as they enter a trancelike state, sometimes alongside the temple's attendant Moon-Bishop.

- *[Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md), guide my steps.*
- *Reveal the moon-path I will walk.*
- *Bring [sweet sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md) to your ward.*

- *Roar of [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md), fill these ears.*
- *Make sharp my claws and mind.*
- *Grant First Cat's wisdom to my home.*

- *[Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s breath, fill my spirit.*
- *Whisper swift secrets in our ears.*
- *Hasten us towards the sky.*
