[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/MW-place-Valley_of_the_Wind.jpg/200px-MW-place-Valley_of_the_Wind.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Valley_of_the_Wind.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Valley_of_the_Wind.jpg) Valley of the Wind circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) The Valley of the Wind is a dried-out [foyada](Lore_Foyada.md) found on the northeast coast of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). It is a long, winding valley that curves upward into a J-shape toward the end. It is completely uninhabited and out of the way from the nearest settlements, such as [Vos](Lore_Vos.md) to the east and [Dagon Fel](Lore_Dagon_Fel.md) to the north across the water. It is avoided by the local populace because they claim that *"the winds howl, and the spirits are always awake."*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGeneric A-1)</sup>

## Geography
The Valley of the Wind lies on the northeastern slopes of Red Mountain, adjacent to another valley to the west called the Dry Camp Valley, which contains no water as the name suggests, but it works well as a safe haven from the harsh winds. The valley's entrance is marked by Airan's Teeth, two large stone spikes named after the [seer](Lore_Sight.md) blessed by [Azura](Lore_Azura.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGeneric V-2)</sup> There is another landmark in the area called the Needle, a tall rock column with a pale white rock at the top of it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWGeneric E-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWGeneric S-4)</sup>

In the middle of the valley is the Dareleth Ancestral Tomb and at the very end of it is the Cavern of the Incarnate, the final resting place of the many [Failed Incarnates](Lore_Failed_Incarnates.md) throughout the years.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONDovrosi-5)</sup> Right outside the valley is an old, ornate [wayshrine](Lore_Wayshrines.md) that looks over the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Vestige journeyed to the Valley to speak with the Failed Incarnates.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DD-6)</sup>

When the [Blight Outbreak](Lore_The_Blight.md) occurred in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) traveled to the Valley of the Wind and entered the Cavern of the Incarnate, where they met with past incarnates and acquired the [Moon-and-Star](Lore_Moon-and-Star.md). They emerged from the valley after being officially recognized as the true Nerevar Incarnate.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWPath-7)</sup>
