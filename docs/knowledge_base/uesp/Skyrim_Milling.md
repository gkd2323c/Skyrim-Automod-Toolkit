# Milling


In order to activate this animation sequence, you must activate an available grain mill. You will then proceed to push the grindstone in a circle around the mill for one revolution. A [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) can also be ordered to turn it. However, it is only an animation in the original version of the game, as the milling process yields no results.

With [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, you can activate small grain mills to create a [sack of flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> using 3 [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md).

## Notes
- There are three mills that have activators which do not function properly, even with Hearthfire installed: [Battle-Born Farm](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md), [Chillfurrow Farm](Skyrim_Chillfurrow_Farm.md), and [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md).
- There are five mills which do not have activators at all: [Katlas's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md), [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md), [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md), [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md), and [Snow-Shod Farm](Skyrim_Snow-Shod_Farm.md).
- The mills at [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> and [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Farming.md)</sup> need to be [constructed](Skyrim_Construction.md#Exterior).

## Bugs
- Activating a grain mill and backing out will cause it to continue moving until it reaches its starting position, seemingly under its own power.
- Activating a grain mill can cause you to face the same direction until roughly halfway through the rotation, then you'll move with the mill until it returns to its starting position. <sup>**?**</sup>
