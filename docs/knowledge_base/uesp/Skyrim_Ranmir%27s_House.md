[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-place-Birna%27s_Oddments.jpg/200px-SR-place-Birna%27s_Oddments.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Birna%27s_Oddments.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Birna%27s_Oddments.jpg) Birna's Oddments **Birna's Oddments** is a general goods store in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md).

It is a two-story building, located between the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) and [Kraldar's House](Skyrim_Kraldar%27s_House.md) and is home to [Birna](Skyrim_Birna.md) and [Ranmir](Skyrim_Ranmir.md). It consists of only one zone, which is the [interior](#Birna.27s_Oddments) of the building.

## Birna's Oddments
There is a row of pillars supporting a balcony at the front of the building and stairs up to it at the rear, to the right of a chicken coop containing a single [nest](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md). The main entrance leads directly into the shop, with the counter on your right, in front of the north wall. [Birna](Skyrim_Birna.md) spends her day standing behind the counter. The first time you interact with her she will offer to sell you the [coral dragon claw](Skyrim_Coral_Dragon_Claw.md) for 50 septims. There are several barrels and sacks of [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) around the building, but no displayed merchandise in the shop. Once you have completed the relevant [quest](Skyrim_Yngol%27s_Barrow.md), or improved Birna's brother [Ranmir](Skyrim_Ranmir.md)'s disposition, you will be able to take all low-value items freely from the shop and residence upstairs.

Opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a [mammoth tusk](Skyrim_Mammoth_Tusk.md) on the mantelpiece. To the right of the fireplace is an unlocked display cabinet containing two each of [petty](Skyrim_Petty_Soul_Gem.md), [lesser](Skyrim_Lesser_Soul_Gem.md), and [common](Skyrim_Common_Soul_Gem.md) [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). Above this are three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) hanging from a rack attached to the ceiling, two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md). On the counter is a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), four loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) and a bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), while under the counter is a copy of *[Dunmer of Skyrim](Skyrim_Dunmer_of_Skyrim.md)*, another small coin purse, a [lute](Skyrim_Lute.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

Against the north wall is a cupboard holding two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), three bottles of wine, and eight bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md). To the right is a set of shelves holding two [potions of minor healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md), a [potion of minor magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), a [potion of regeneration](Skyrim_Potion_of_Regeneration.md), a [potion of resist fire](Skyrim_Potion_of_Resist_Fire.md), two [flutes](Skyrim_Flute.md), a [bowl](Skyrim_Bowl.md), a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), six [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md), and several cheese wheels. Against the east wall is a narrow wooden table with an [Orcish dagger](Skyrim_Orcish_Dagger.md) on top. To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding four [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), with a set of shelves to the left of that, holding an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) and a [drum](Skyrim_Drum.md). Against the south wall is a flight of stairs up to the top floor, with a couple of crates and a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) under the stairs.

Upstairs, against the west wall, is a long wooden table and bench, with three bottles of alto wine, two fillets of [salmon meat](Skyrim_Salmon_Meat.md), a [chicken's egg](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), two bulbs of garlic, a couple of loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and several wedges of cheese on the table, and a loaf of bread on the bench. To the right is a small round wooden table and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing leveled items, with two more bottles of alto wine and five loose septims on top of the table. Coming out from the middle of the north wall is a large double bed with a dresser on either side, both containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Mystery of Talara, v3](Skyrim_Mystery_of_Talara,_v3.md)* on top. At the foot of the bed is another chest also containing leveled items. Above the stairs, against the east wall, is a set of shelves, but they only hold clutter, with a door out onto the balcony to the left.

## Notes
- Unlike other stores in the game, Birna's Oddments has no exterior sign.
- With the exception of some barrels and sacks, all containers in the area are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
- The display case lid collides with the rabbits above it when opened (resulting in humorous motion on the part of the rabbits).
