Battle of Champions *An announcement of a battle between Stormcloaks and the Legion*
---
There is talk in the taverns about a battle in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) between two champions, one [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), one [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). Yet while the stage has been set, the actors have not.

[Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced) has made it clear that the Stormcloak champion must prove their mettle by bringing him the pelts of five snow bears. While the task seems daunting, rumor has it that a warrior named [Klija Wolf-Claw](Skyrim_Klija_Wolf-Claw.md) of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md) has already slain the beasts, and has made her way to [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md).

For their side, [Legate Adventus Caesennius](Skyrim_Legate_Adventus_Caesennius.md) has made no mention of any ritual in selecting their champion. It makes you wonder if any willing soldier would do.
