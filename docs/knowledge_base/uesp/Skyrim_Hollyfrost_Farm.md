[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-place-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg) Hollyfrost Farm **Hollyfrost Farm** is a large farm with a single-roomed farmhouse east of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

The farm is owned by [Torsten Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Torsten_Cruel-Sea.md), but [Tulvur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tulvur), who lives on the farm, does most of the work. Tulvur and his two dogs, [Tiber](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tiber) and [Ysgramor](Skyrim_Ysgramor_(dog).md), live in the spacious L-shaped house on the property. Tulvur has little need of the novice lock that secures the house while he is away since his dogs watch the premises and attack any intruders.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
In the field behind the house are used to cultivate eleven bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) and ten [snowberry bushes](Skyrim_Snowberries.md). At the rear of the property is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). A chicken coop shelters the farm's two [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) while the three [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md) wander at will around the farm. There are several [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md) as you approach the farm.

### Hollyfrost Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/SR-interior-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hollyfrost_Farm.jpg) The interior of Hollyfrost Farm At the northern end of the farmhouse is a long wooden table with benches either side and a couple of barrels containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) against the north wall. On the table are two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a couple of loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese). Opposite the entrance on the east wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and on the mantelpiece are three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md). To the left of the fireplace is a barrel with a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) on top and to the right is a kettle containing four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and a long wooden table and bench. This table holds a couple more potatoes, a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), a loaf of bread and a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese). Under the table are two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) and a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), while hanging from racks over the table are two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) along with two garlic braids, two bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the right of the table is a set of shelves holding two bottles of alto wine, three bottles of wine and copies of *[Killing - Before You're Killed](Skyrim_Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed.md)* and *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)*.

Coming out from the south wall is a single unowned bed used by [Tulvur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tulvur). Further along the south wall is a chair, a food barrel and a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). At the western end of the room is a large double bed coming out from the west wall with an end table on either side, the one to the left has a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) hidden on the floor behind it and a copy of *[Scourge of the Gray Quarter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge_of_the_Gray_Quarter)* and a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling) on top and the one to the right holds four loose septims. At the foot of the bed is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). Along the north wall are a couple more barrels of foodstuffs and a small round table and two chairs with two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top of the table. Finally on the right as you enter, against the west wall is a cupboard and yet another barrel of food. The cupboard only holds clutter, but on top there is a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) and bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat).
