[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-place-Atheron_Residence.jpg/200px-SR-place-Atheron_Residence.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Atheron_Residence.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Atheron_Residence.jpg) Atheron Residence **Atheron Residence** is the home of the Atheron family in the lower Gray Quarter of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). It is located across the street from [New Gnisis Cornerclub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Gnisis_Cornerclub).

The house is inhabited by street vendor [Aval](Skyrim_Aval_Atheron.md), farm worker [Faryl](Skyrim_Faryl_Atheron.md) and their sister, [Suvaris](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron). It consists of one area, *[Atheron Residence](#Atheron_Residence)*.

## Atheron Residence
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Atheron_Residence_05.jpg) The Ground Floor Like all other houses in the Gray Quarter, the Atheron Residence is in poor shape. The front door opens to the sight of a sparsely decorated dining area with a ladder leading upstairs. Three food sacks sit next to the door, with an additional six sacks on the shelves near the two fireplaces. The eastern corner has a bit of food scattered around, some [dog meat](Skyrim_Dog_Meat.md), a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), and a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). The dinner table in the center holds some cheap food, a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), a slice of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), a [pile of salt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a sample of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). The most valuable items can be found in a [knapsack](Skyrim_Containers.md#Knapsack) underneath the ladder; this contains leveled items. Note that this knapsack respawns, and is not safe to store items in. An entrance in the western corner leads to a room with a few tables and a single bed, owned by [Suvaris Atheron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron). Along the northern wall are two sets of shelves with a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina) and two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), along with some common books. Next to the bed is a dresser with [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md) with a sample of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), another potion of minor stamina, and some [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md) on top. The small table nearby only has a bottle of alto wine and some bread on display. Lastly, in the southeastern corner stands a wardrobe with clothes.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-interior-Atheron_Residence.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Atheron_Residence.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Atheron_Residence.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Atheron_Residence.jpg) Second Floor When you head up the ladder you will find a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) near a table with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and a [green apple](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md) in a bowl. Nearby is a food sack and a dresser with clothes. The entrance in the northwestern corner leads to [Aval Atheron](Skyrim_Aval_Atheron.md)'s room, with a single bed against the western wall. The northern wall has a wardrobe and a dresser, both with clothes. The small table has a bottle of alto wine and a piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md). Back in the room with the ladder is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) and three food sacks. The table near the eastern wall holds a [boiled creme treat](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md) and a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple). The shelves near the entrance to the second room hold little of interest, with a tipped-over food sack on the ground nearby. The room is where [Faryl Atheron](Skyrim_Faryl_Atheron.md) sleeps, with a handy bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) on the ground next to the bed and the wardrobe with clothes. At the foot of the bed is an end table with clutter, with a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina) on top. Following the wall is another dresser, with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a copy of *[The Ransom of Zarek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ransom_of_Zarek)* on top, followed by a chest with random items, a food barrel and a food sack. The small table has an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md) and a slice of bread.

## Notes
- All containers in the area are non-respawning, with the exception of some barrels, sacks, and the knapsack.
