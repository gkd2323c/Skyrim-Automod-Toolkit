[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-npc-Gharol.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gharol.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gharol.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gharol.jpg) Gharol in the smithy **Gharol** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She is the forge-wife of [Chief Burguk](Skyrim_Chief_Burguk.md) and mother of [Umurn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Umurn) and [Lash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh). When Lash decided to leave the stronghold for mining duties in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md), the grief-stricken mother forged a special sword, which she will [ask you](Skyrim_Gharol%27s_Message.md) to deliver as a message to her daughter. She will also [pay you](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) for any [orichalcum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore) you bring her. As a smith, she also sells a [variety](#Standard_Merchandise) of weapons and armor (after you accept her quest). If you are [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs, she will [train](Skyrim_Trainers.md) you in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md).

As Burguk's second wife, Gharol rarely gets to spend time with her husband, who prefers the company of [Shel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shel). Instead, she works the [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) every day between 8am and 8pm, offering her various services. When the sun sets, she enters [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) for a four-hour dinner until her bedtime at midnight.

She wears typical blacksmith attire, a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff37) with matching [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b69e). In combat, Gharol wields two leveled weapons; at level 1 she uses a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md); at level 6 she uses an [Orcish war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe) and an [Orcish bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Bow). She carries leveled arrows for the bow and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

- *"You wish to trade in Orc steel?"*
- *"Strong gauntlets for strong hands."*
- *"Blades to kill your prey and your rivals."*
- *"I'm Burguk's forge-wife. I shape all the steel for the stronghold."*
- *"As forge-wife, I'm also in charge of our mine."*

Speaking to her:

- **Can you train me to better use Heavy Armor?**
- *"Use it right, and you won't feel a thing."*

- **Your weapons and armor are impressive.**
- *"It is said that an Orc first learns to wield a hammer in her mother's womb. By tradition, a mother always teaches her children how to smith. If a chief has a second wife, she is called the "Forge-wife" in honor of this."*

### Conversations
When working the forge, she will have random conversations with her son:

- **Gharol**: *"Imagine the heads of your enemies as you forge. Let your hatred work the steel."*
- **Umurn**: *"Yes, mother."*

- **Umurn**: *"The metal has been stubborn today. It refuses to bend."*
- **Gharol**: *"Save your anger for the forge. The steel cannot hear your whining."*
- **Umurn**: *"Sorry, mother. I'll focus on my work."*

- **Gharol**: *"Ah, is there any weapon finer than an Orc blade?"*
- **Umurn**: *"No, mother. It's no wonder our enemies fear the sight of an Orc warrior."*

During the family dinner in the longhouse, she will also try and convince Chief Burguk that she is a better wife than Shel:

- **Burguk**: *"I saw you working the forge today, wife. Your muscles have grown strong from the metal shaping!"*
- **Gharol**: *"Do they, my chief? Am I now favored enough to replace Shel by your side at the feast? Back your words with action!"*
- **Burguk**: *"No more talk of Shel. As chief, I will do what I please, so point your fiery gaze to our enemies."*

It'll be all she needs to know."*

- *"Thank you."*

- - **Not right now.**
- *"As you wish."*

## Bugs
- While Gharol offers expert training in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md), the dialogue to do so becomes unavailable once the player's Heavy Armor skill reaches 50 or above. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.9.26.0.8, fixes this bug.
