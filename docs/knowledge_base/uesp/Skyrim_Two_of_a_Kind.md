## Quick Walkthrough
1. Receive letter from the [Courier](Skyrim_Courier.md).
2. Read the Letter From [Mila Valentia](Skyrim_Mila_Valentia.md).
3. Find another [goldfish](Skyrim_Goldfish.md) for Mila Valentia.
4. Speak to Mila Valentia.
5. Follow Mila.
6. Read Mila's note.
7. Find Mila's secret hiding spot.

## Detailed Walkthrough

- **I got your letter. And I have a new companion for your golden friend right here.**
- *"This is great! Wow, thanks!
You're going to love it here. We have a lot of fun, and we look out for each other. Just like any family. Thanks! Here - this is for you."*

At this point you are given the [Key to Mila's strong box](Skyrim_Key_to_Mila%27s_Strong_Box.md) and *[Mila's Note on Very Secret Treasure](Skyrim_Mila%27s_Note_on_Very_Secret_Treasure.md)*. To get to the strong box, you have to step out the door of Dragonsreach and go around to the right and follow the wall back.
