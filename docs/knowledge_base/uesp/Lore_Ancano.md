Ancano was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) agent of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) assigned as "advisor" to the Arch-Mage of the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md). The other members of the College deeply and somewhat openly distrusted him due to his Thalmor allegiance. He vehemently denied that he was a spy, claiming only that the Thalmor wished to promote relations with the College. He was deeply distrustful of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), which he viewed as a rogue organization and enemy of the Thalmor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGI-1)</sup> Unbeknownest to others, he also met with the [Augur of Dunlain](Lore_Augur_of_Dunlain.md) in secret. When the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md) was brought to the College from [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), he turned on them, killing several mages, including Arch-Mage [Savos Aren](Lore_Savos_Aren.md). He sought to gain control over the Eye, and nearly created a devastating catastrophe which would have had enormous ramifications on [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) and possibly all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). However, he was thwarted and slain, by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), before he could do anything truly disastrous, and the Eye was subsequently secured by the Psijic Order.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SREye-2)</sup>
