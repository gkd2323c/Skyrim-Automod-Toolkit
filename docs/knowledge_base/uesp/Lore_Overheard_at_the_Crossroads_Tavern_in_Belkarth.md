Overheard at the Crossroads Tavern in Belkarth *An Undaunted adventurer retells his encounter with a Mantikora*
---
[![ON-concept-Mantikora.png](https://images.uesp.net/c/c9/ON-concept-Mantikora.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Mantikora.png)
- "First the [constellations](Lore_Astronomy.md#Constellations) fall down, and now creatures out of nightmares run wild! It was worse than any [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) I ever saw. [Shor](Lore_Shor.md)'s bones, I swear it had the nastiest parts of every monster I've ever fought! And here I was thinking those [welwa](Lore_Welwa.md) beasties were bad enough. When I stumbled on [this thing](Lore_Mantikora.md), it was surrounded by their corpses. I watched the four-legged terror grab one and literally tear it in half. No joke!"

- "It didn't seem to be hunting for food either; it just tossed the bloody bits aside. I must've uttered an oath, because it turned toward me and let out a wail that'd shake any of you. I know we [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) aren't supposed to show any fear, but by [the Eight](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), I'll admit it: I was daunted!"
