Haskill is the Chamberlain of [Lord Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), "the one constant in the ever-changing whims of the Madgod".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IWTDSI-1)</sup> He often takes on the appearance of an elderly [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) man in a melancholy yet flamboyant suit. Just like his colleague [Dyus](Lore_Dyus.md), former Chamberlain of [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md), his exact origins are unknown; but he has claimed to have once been a mortal who [mantled](Lore_Mantling.md) Sheogorath, becoming a [Vestige](Lore_Vestige_(creature).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CHAYQ-2)</sup> On another occasion, however, he has claimed to have been under the service of Sheogorath since the "beginning".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IWTDSI-1)</sup> He took care of all of the more administrative and mundane duties involved in running the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DSI-3)</sup> Despite having once been mortal, he has no desire to visit [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IWTDSI-1)</sup> He has a strong dislike of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and finds mortals to be irritating.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IWTDSI-1)</sup>

In the Second Era, around the time of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), the [Arch-Mage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) sought to regain the island of [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md). Though together with a [Mages Guild Adept](Lore_The_Vestige.md) they succeeded in passing Sheogorath's trials, the Madgod intentionally left its Daedric inhabitants forcing them to be cleared out. Haskill was among those on Eyevea and did battle with the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) before being defeated.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESO-4)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Haskill helped Sheogorath's [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) in his many endeavors, and eventually helped him stop the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) and free Jyggalag/Sheogorath of his curse.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESI-5)</sup> Haskill serves the Champion as the new Madgod, and has been known to substitute the new Sheogorath when summoned to [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> He also takes care of the Shivering Isles when Sheogorath goes on "vacations", and according to Sheogorath gets in "mind boggling trouble" while his master is away.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
