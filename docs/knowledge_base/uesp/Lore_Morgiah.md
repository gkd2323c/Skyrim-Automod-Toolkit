[![](https://images.uesp.net/e/ef/DF-npc-Morgiah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Morgiah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Morgiah.jpg) Morgiah as a Princess of Wayrest Queen Morgiah (born in [3E 384](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_384)) was the daughter of [Queen Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md) and [General Symmachus](Lore_Symmachus.md) of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), and the younger sister of Prince (and future King) [Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Hlaalu_Helseth.md).

Morgiah was named after Symmachus' mother, and was known to be well loved by her father. Sadly, the princess was born in troubled times; the kingdom was plagued with civil unrest as a result of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md). In [3E 391](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_391), at the age of eight, Morgiah accompanied her mother and brother on a journey to the Imperial City. Barenziah was there to plead with [the emperor](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) for aid, and hoped the presence of the children would soften his heart. However the (false) emperor remained unmoved, and that evening word came that Symmachus was killed in a revolt. The surviving royals were forced into exile. That same evening, Barenziah met King [Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md), and within a few years the pair were wed. Morgiah again became a princess, now of the Kingdom of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOB3-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB-2)</sup>

In the years prior to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Princess Morgiah had garnered a reputation as a "manipulative witch" among the people of the kingdom. She saw little future in Wayrest, as Eadwyre's children, Helseth and [Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md), fought for succession. As a result, Morgiah sought to arrange a marriage with King [Reman Karoodil](Lore_Karoodil.md), ruler of the Kingdom of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md) on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). To secure the king's hand, she endeavored to give him that which he desired most—to speak again with a son who had died. To this end, Morgiah made contact with the notorious necromancer [Mannimarco, the King of Worms](Lore_Mannimarco.md), in the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). With the help of an [Agent of the Blades](Lore_The_Agent.md), the deal was struck. Mannimarco held up his end of the bargain, and in return Morgiah promised to give him her "first", though exactly what this meant remains a mystery. Morgiah and Reman were wed after a three year engagement.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

As a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), Queen Morgiah was not particularly well-liked by the culturally xenophobic [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of Firsthold either, and is known to have quelled a revolt of [Trebbite Monks](Lore_Trebbite_Monks.md) during the early years of her rule.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TFR-4)</sup> The couple had two children by the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Goranthir and Rinnala, who were both Dunmer in appearance despite being half-Altmer. Regardless of appearances, however, the children were considered rightful heirs to the throne. In [4E 22](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_22), the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) successfully overthrew the monarchy in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) seizing control of the region, leaving Morgiah and her family to an unknown fate.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TGW-6)</sup>
