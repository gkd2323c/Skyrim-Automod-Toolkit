*By the Powers! You amaze me! Very well. Take the Typos Sophia. Much good may it do you in the Colleges. But you shall see. Yes, you'll see soon enough. These creatures you see here are but pups compared with their masters. As you dangle for eternity in some daedric pit of horrors, you'll wish you'd taken my advice and died gloriously with me in the ruins of Battlespire.*
—Clarentavious celebrating the defeat of [Lord Methats](Lore_Dremora_Methats.md) at the hands of the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSD-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Clarentavious Valisious, Venerable Artificer, was Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md)'s chief artificer<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mactana-2)</sup> in [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398) and the only surviving [battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) from [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s attack on the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSD-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-3)</sup><sup>:92</sup> He is described as a greybeard master of the Hammer and the harness and is known to have created the Great Star Galley, a [magical ship](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) which conveyed battlemages to the Battlespire's separate colleges.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LTTGMOTIL-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mactana-2)</sup>

He survived the attack by tricking the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) forces into thinking he was bound to one of the Daedra, and intended to unlock all the anchors holding the Battlespire into place so that it would drift from the mana streams endlessly.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AW-5)</sup> However, after the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) slew the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) [Lord Methats](Lore_Dremora_Methats.md), Valisious agreed to help the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) by giving them his Staff of the Typos Sophia, which prevents Daedra from binding mortals to them through possession.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LTTGMOTIL-4)</sup> After this he was told to wait and report to the [legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) that would eventually come after the crisis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BSD-1)</sup>
