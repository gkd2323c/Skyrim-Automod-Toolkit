[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/MW-place-Yasammidan.jpg/200px-MW-place-Yasammidan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Yasammidan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Yasammidan.jpg) The ruins of Yasammidan circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/ON-place-Yasammidan.jpg/200px-ON-place-Yasammidan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Yasammidan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Yasammidan.jpg) Yasammidan circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Yasammidan (or **Yasammidin**) is a [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) located on an island at the far northeastern tip of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), east of [Ald Velothi](Lore_Ald_Velothi.md).

The ruins were once an ancient site of [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) worship dedicated to the Prince of Destruction, [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). Like many of the Daedric ruins of Vvardenfell, they were sealed off by the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) to prevent worship of the Daedra Lords.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sinnammu-1)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/ON-interior-Reformer%27s_Laboratory.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Reformer%27s_Laboratory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Reformer%27s_Laboratory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Reformer%27s_Laboratory.jpg) The Reformer's laboratory During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the abandoned ruins were crawling with [Air Atronachs](Lore_Air_Atronach.md) and [Clannfear](Lore_Clannfear.md) who would attack all interlopers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Nevertheless, the [Urshilaku Tribe](Lore_Urshilaku_Tribe.md) made efforts to recover relics of historical importance from the rubble at Yasammidan circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), passing them into the care of their Wise Woman at the tribe's camp in [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Around this time, a [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) mage named Sondivel Ulres set up a secret laboratory within the ruin's interior. Better known as "the Reformer", Sondivel's experimentation focused on the use of [soul magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md) to manipulate a subject's memories and will, allowing him to bend a [slave](Lore_Slavery.md)'s will to his control without breaking their individuality​. He achieved this through the use of a metal alloy called [tyranite calx](Lore_Tyranite_Calx.md), and conducted experiments here on unwilling victims.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RJ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SJ-5)</sup> However, the laboratory was ransacked circa 2E 582 when one of his former slaves, Sharp-as-Night, uncovered its location through the use of [Warden](Lore_Warden.md) magic.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Sharp subsequently tracked Sondivel to [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) and killed him.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

### Third Era
As the threat from [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) began to grow in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Ordinators were no longer able to guard isolated Daedric ruins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sinnammu-1)</sup> As a result, Yasammidan began to be occupied by hostile Daedric cultists.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) received a vision from Mehrunes Dagon at his shrine within Yasammidan. Dagon tasked the Nerevarine with recovering [Mehrunes' Razor](Lore_Mehrunes%27_Razor.md) from Alas Ancestral Tomb, where it lay disused and rusted by the corpse of Varner Hleras. When the Razor was returned to the shrine, Dagon imbued it with his power and returned it to its former glory.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

Several [artifacts](Lore_Threads_of_the_Webspinner.md) of [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) were also liberated from the ruins by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) around this time.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
