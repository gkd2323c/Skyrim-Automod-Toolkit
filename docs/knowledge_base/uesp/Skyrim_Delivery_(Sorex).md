## Quick Walkthrough
1. After [Roggvir's](Skyrim_Roggvir.md) execution, ask [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) who's in charge of Solitude.
2. Deliver a bottle of [Stros M'Kai Rum](Skyrim_Stros_M%27Kai_Rum.md) to [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Entering Solitude by the main gate for the first time will trigger an execution, as hinted by the guard outside. The man fated to die is [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md), a former friend of [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) and a 'traitor to the Empire'. Talking to Sorex after the execution will open up discussion about the leaders of Solitude; continuing the conversation will end with him asking you to bring a delivery of [Stros M'Kai Rum](Skyrim_Stros_M%27Kai_Rum.md) to one of those leaders, [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md). Falk resides in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) and is nearly always found by [Elisif the Fair's](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md) side in the main room. Upon delivering the rum, he will thank you and reward you with a leveled amount of gold to complete the quest.

## Notes
- Upon finishing the quest, you'll have the option to marry Sorex. You'll also be able to take almost everything from the Winking Skeever for free.

## Bugs
- If you have already accepted the quest [No News is Good News](Skyrim_No_News_is_Good_News.md), Sorex will not offer this quest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
