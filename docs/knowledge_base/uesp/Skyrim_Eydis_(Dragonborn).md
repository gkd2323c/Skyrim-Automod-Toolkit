[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-npc-Eydis_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eydis_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eydis_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eydis_03.jpg) Eydis **Eydis** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_EncClassBanditMelee.md) who can be found in [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md). She wears a [Skaal coat](Skyrim_Skaal_Coat.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Skaal_Boots.md) and [hat](Skyrim_Skaal_Hat.md). She carries an enchanted [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) and her [journal](Skyrim_Eydis%27s_Journal.md), and may also carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold. Reading her journal will reveal that she came to Fahlbtharz with [Ulyn](Skyrim_Ulyn.md) to help him search for the [Visage of Mzund](Skyrim_Visage_of_Mzund.md), an enchanted Dwarven helmet. She and Ulyn were forced to defend themselves against [Dwarven spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md), and [Ulyn's journal](Skyrim_Ulyn%27s_Journal.md) indicates that he went further into the ruin while she stayed back to hold them off—to her obvious misfortune.
