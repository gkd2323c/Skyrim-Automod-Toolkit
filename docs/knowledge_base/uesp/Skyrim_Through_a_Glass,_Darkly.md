## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to the specified geyser pool in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md).
2. Attempt to take [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend_(item).md) and defeat your summoned [Shadow](Skyrim_Shadow.md).
3. Take either the greatsword or the battleaxe version of Shadowrend.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Inner Darkness
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-quest-Through_A_Glass%2C_Darkly_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Through_A_Glass%2C_Darkly_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Through_A_Glass,_Darkly_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Through_A_Glass,_Darkly_02.jpg) The Shadow Once you've installed the [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), you will receive this quest. The journal entry will inform you that an inner voice is drawing you to a geyser pool in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) in order to confront the darkness within yourself. The geyser can be found near the [Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md), to the northeast of it. Stand with the Atronach Stone at your back and walk toward a trio of pine trees in the water. Continue past the leftmost tree. The pool contains skeletal remains which have stained the water black, and will only begin to froth when you approach.

Above this anomaly, a weapon will appear. It is [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend_(item).md), a shadow weapon from the ruins of [Milchar](Lore_Milchar.md) in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Shadowrend is both a [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) and a [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md), and it will proceed to cycle between each version, with the weapon warping in and out of existence until you attempt to take it. Touching the weapon will summon a [Shadow](Skyrim_Shadow.md) enemy named "[player]'s Shadow". It is a shadowy clone of you, meaning it will wear your equipment and know all of your [spells](Skyrim_Spells.md), powers, and [shouts](Skyrim_Shouts.md).

Defeat the Shadow and claim Shadowrend from its remains to complete the quest. Whichever version of the weapon you didn't select will reappear above the pool, where it will hang in the air indefinitely. You can return to the pool any time in order to switch between the two versions of Shadowrend. You must have it in your inventory for the swap to function, otherwise it will say, "You lack the required item." Each version is a powerful Daedric weapon in its own right, with a 15% [weakness to magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md) enchantment.

## Notes
- Shadowrend originally [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shadowrend) in the *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* expansion for *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
- [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) also features an unrelated [clannfear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Shadowrend) and [item set](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Shadowrend_(set)).
- The [Shadow](Skyrim_Shadow.md) seems to be a valid target for the [Ebony Blade](Skyrim_Ebony_Blade.md).
- The Shadow may choose to instead attack nearby [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md).
- The name of this quest is a Biblical phrase from 1 Corinthians 13:12 (KJV). - It also appears quite similar to the name of another quest in *Oblivion*, [Through A Nightmare, Darkly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Through_A_Nightmare,_Darkly).
