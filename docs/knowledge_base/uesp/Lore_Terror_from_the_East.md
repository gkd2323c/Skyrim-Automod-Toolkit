Terror from the East *A poem about the Khajiit and the Dragons*
---
When [Demons](Lore_Dragon.md) came to [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s land
She knew that this was fate
Her [children](Lore_Khajiit.md) made a final stand
And opened the Crimson Gate

First came [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md) proud and strong
Golden crowned his golden mane
He saw these things did not belong
And that they must be slain

Down came mighty [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md) then
His blade burned shining blue
He fought the darkness back again
and ran the Demons through

Last came [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) mourning [Nirni](Lore_Nirni.md)
Whose cairn he made of bone
From wooded green to blackened sea
He watched over her alone
