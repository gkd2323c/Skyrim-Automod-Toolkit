[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-place-Iron_Tusk_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Iron_Tusk_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Iron_Tusk_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Iron_Tusk_Cave.jpg) Iron Tusk Cave **Iron Tusk Cave** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) on the island of [Giant's Tooth](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md). Its humid environment is home to the nearly-extinct Ironwood Tree, which can be harvested for [Ironwood Fruit](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup>: Make a bitter choice.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The entrance to the cave is next to the giant's camp on Giant's Tooth.

### Iron Tusk Cave
The interior is forested and home to a lone [snow fox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox). Several [snowberry bushes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry) grow here, as well as a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot). [Jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md) and [butterflies](Skyrim_Butterfly.md) can also be collected within the cave. You will be sent to harvest Ironwood Fruit from one of the trees here during the related quest. Once you do, two [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) and a [spriggan matron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spriggan_Matron) will appear and attack you, likely turning the snow fox hostile. There are six Ironwood trees in total, and they can be repeatedly harvested for fruit after the quest is over.
