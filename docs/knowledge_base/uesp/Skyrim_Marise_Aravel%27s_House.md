## Walkthrough

### Marise Aravel's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-interior-Marise_Aravel%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Marise_Aravel%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Marise_Aravel%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Marise_Aravel%27s_House.jpg) Marise Aravel's home The front door is apprentice-locked both at night and during the day while Marise is selling [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) at her cart by the marketplace. Her home is one room divided by a partial wall. The main living area consists of a central dining table and benches. On the table is a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two [boiled creme treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), some [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie). Against the east wall, opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) to the right.

On the south wall is a shelf holding a bottle of wine with two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) all hanging above the shelf. Against the partial north wall is a dresser and a set of shelves. On the shelves are two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of wine, a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a couple of loaves of bread and several pieces of cheese. On top of these shelves contained in a basket is a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Reality & Other Falsehoods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reality_%26_Other_Falsehoods)*. While above the wall are a couple of hanging racks that hold two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). There are sacks and barrels all containing foodstuffs all around the room.

To the north is her bedroom area, which consists of an owned single bed coming out from the east wall with several crates, sacks and an end table beside it. Under the bed is a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). On the end table is a bottle of wine and a copy of *[Watcher of Stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Watcher_of_Stones)*. On one of the crates is a copy of *[Kolb & the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kolb_%26_the_Dragon)*. There is a wardrobe against the north wall containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) along with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) and food barrel against the west wall.
