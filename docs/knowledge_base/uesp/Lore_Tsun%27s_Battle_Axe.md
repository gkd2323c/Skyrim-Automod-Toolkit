# Tsun's Battle Axe

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg) Tsun's Battle Axe [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Tsun%27s_Judgement.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Tsun%27s_Judgement.png) Tsun's Judgement Tsun's Battle Axe, (also written as **Tsun's Battleaxe**) is a weapon artifact associated with [Tsun](Lore_Tsun.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tsun1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAG2-2)</sup>

It was wielded by Tsun as he fought those seeking to cross the Whalebone Bridge in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Tsun-3)</sup> though it could also be found beyond [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) and used by mortals. When wielded, it made them more likely to successfully make use of their skills.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAG2-2)</sup>

At some point between [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176) and [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a [Sheathed Blade](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md) came upon the axe in the hands of a powerful [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) Shaman.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAG-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Another two-handed battleaxe, Tsun's Judgement, was named in honor of the god and bore an engraving of a bear, the animal associated with Tsun. It was otherwise unrelated to the divine weapon and was entirely mundane.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TJ-5)</sup>
