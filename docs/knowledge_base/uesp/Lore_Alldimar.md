# Alldimar

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blue_Palace.jpg) Blue Palace Alldimar ** the Ghostmaker** was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) High King of Solitude. Prior to becoming high king, he was a seasoned warrior and had engaged in numerous skirmishes against the [Reachfolk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reachfolk). He became the High King of Solitude through violent means. Forty years after the death of [Temylda](Lore_Temylda.md), Alldimar seized the throne by toppling a child heir whose name was lost to history. He succeeded in seizing power primarily through a good sense of battle tactics and knowledge of arcane matters that most Nords would sneer at.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>

Despite his bloody seizure of the throne, he proved to be an effective ruler, bringing peace to the land. However, all was not completely normal with Alldimar. Servants in the [Blue Palace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blue_Palace) reported seeing Alldimar talking to someone who was not there. They also whispered about one of his advisors, a woman named Freiretta, who kept to herself when not in Alldimar's presence. As these stories spread among Solitude's people, they became uneasy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>

A year and a day after Alldimar ascended to the throne, strange accidents began claiming the lives of many people in Solitude. The servants whispered that they started hearing disembodied voices around the high king's quarters. A year and a day after the first accident, Alldimar died. After his death, the skulls of every one of the accident victims were discovered nestled together on a secret shelf within the high king's quarters for some unknown purpose. Alldimar gained the moniker "the Ghostmaker" because of this discovery. Priests performed the proper rites for the displaced remains, although servants continued to report strange happenings in the Blue Palace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>

Tales of Alldimar's reign were compiled by [Erisa Sylbenitte](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Erisa_Sylbenitte) of the [Coterie of Organized Scholars](Lore_Coterie_of_Organized_Scholars.md) at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOS-1)</sup>
