# Dwarven Scarab

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/RG-quest-Investigate_the_Ruins_15.jpg/200px-RG-quest-Investigate_the_Ruins_15.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-quest-Investigate_the_Ruins_15.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-quest-Investigate_the_Ruins_15.jpg) Giant Dwemer Scarab (Redguard) **Dwarven Scarabs** or **Dwemer Scarabs** are [animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) that resemble organic [scarabs](Lore_Scarab.md) and vary in size, with the most notable example being the non-animate one in the Dwemer ruins of [Bthzark](Lore_Bthzark.md) on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), which could be transformed into the shape of Man.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Various Dwemer Scarab schematics were discovered in Bthzark, and in Mzuleft, located on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SPIM-2)</sup> Many of the small Dwemer Scarab constructs are present at the Dwarven ruins around [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) and in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and in the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md).

There is a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) knightly order named the [Order of the Scarab](Lore_Order_of_the_Scarab.md), where typically initiates must travel to the Dwemer ruins of Bthzark and "wrestle its mechanical men back into shape" before they can join the Knights of the Scarab.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1H-4)</sup>
