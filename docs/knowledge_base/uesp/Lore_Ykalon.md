[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Ykalon was found Ykalon is an area in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, before the Warp in the West it was a fiefdom, but then became a part of the Kingdom of Daggerfall after the Warp.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WITW-1)</sup> It is surrounded by [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md) to the east, [Urvaius](Lore_Urvaius.md), [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md), and the [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md) to the south, and [Northmoor](Lore_Northmoor.md) to the west. Its capital was the city of Ykalon. [Castle Llugwych](Lore_Castle_Llugwych.md), one of the headquarters of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) is located in this region. The [Plessington Clan](Lore_Plessington_Clan.md) is a noble family of [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) from Ykalon that led a knightly order called the [Order of the Cup](Lore_Order_of_the_Cup.md). In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Lord Plessington led the order and sent them off to right a wrong and come back with a story or two.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFCup-2)</sup>

[Thrafey](Lore_Thrafey.md) vampires are known to stalk prey here, and the regional deity is [Mara](Lore_Mara.md). Forces from Ykalon participated in the [Battle of Firewaves](Lore_Battle_of_Firewaves.md) which brought about the defeat of the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFOTU-3)</sup> Perseverance Day is a festival held every year by the people of Ykalon during [Sun's Dawn](Lore_Sun%27s_Dawn.md), though it was originally a solemn day of memorial for those killed in the battle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-4)</sup>

The events of the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) led to most of the forests in Ykalon being burned down.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WITW-1)</sup>
