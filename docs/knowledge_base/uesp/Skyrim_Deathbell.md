[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-flora-Deathbell.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Deathbell.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Deathbell.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Deathbell.jpg) A deathbell plant [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-book-Deathbell.png/100px-SR-book-Deathbell.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Deathbell.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Deathbell.png) Deathbell as depicted in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* **Deathbell** comes from a plant of the same name that is common in swampy areas, such as [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md). Despite what is said in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*, it does not have any [Fortify Alchemy](Skyrim_Fortify_Alchemy.md) effect—there are no ingredients that provide that effect.

Its [Damage Health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md) effect has a larger magnitude than standard ingredients (although other ingredients have even larger Damage Health effects; see the effect page for details). Its [Ravage Stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ravage_Stamina) poisons have the same magnitude as standard ingredients, but have a higher gold value.

- *"Folklore abounds about this flower found in the swamps of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md). Some stories claim it grows where unfortunate deaths have taken place, others insist it grows first and then lures unsuspecting people and animals to their doom. I have found no direct evidence to support these stories; indeed I found the flowers difficult to locate at all. While it is most well-known for its use in poisons, it would be remiss to overlook that the blooms of the Deathbell are very effective in mixtures for boosting one [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) skill."* - Agneta Falia from the *Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim*.

In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 3 guaranteed samples sold by [Enthir](Skyrim_Enthir.md)

## Notes
- Deathbell is one of 4 items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a *[Spell Tome: Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Storm_Atronach)*.
- Using deathbell in a ravage stamina poison gives a duration of 20 seconds instead of the standard 10 seconds. Since the ravage stamina effect is not affected by duration this only leads to an increase in the resulting potion's value. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
