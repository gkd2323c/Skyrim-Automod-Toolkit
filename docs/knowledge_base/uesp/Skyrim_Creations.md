# Creations


## Mods
[Mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods), short for **modifications** or **modules**, are user-made Creations for [*The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim*](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). They may add new content, change existing features/mechanics, fix bugs, add cheats, and more.

## Verified Creator Program
The [Verified Creator Program](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Verified_Creator_Program) features Creations for *[Skyrim Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md)* that have been vetted and approved for sale by [Bethesda Game Studios](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda_Game_Studios). The Bethesda Game Studios Verified Creator Program was introduced on December 5, 2023, coinciding with the release of [Special Edition Patch 1.6.1130](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#1.6.1130) which combined [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods) and [Creation Club Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) under one "Creations" menu and name. Mod authors may apply to be admitted into the Verified Creator Program. If they are accepted, they may submit their Creations to Bethesda's quality assurance process and await their verification. Users may purchase Creations released through the Verified Creator Program using [Creation Credits](Skyrim_Creations.md#Creation_Credits) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SRMOD-icon-Credits.svg), and the authors will receive royalties for each sale.

## Creation Club
**[Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)** was an in-game [microtransactional](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microtransaction) store for [Skyrim Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md). A [virtual currency](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_goods) called **Creation Club Credits** was sold within it, which was used to purchase unique [add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) called Creations. Each Creation released through Creation Club is official [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda) content, developed with third-party developers and contracted [mod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods) authors. As official content, Creation Club content does not disable [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) acquisition.

The service entered beta on September 28, 2017 and officially launched on October 4, 2017. It was never available for [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md).

All 74 Creations are included in the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md). Special Edition and [Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) users also receive free access to four Creations: [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md), [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md), [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), and [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md). Several other discounted Creations were offered for free either on a permanent or temporary basis. On Switch, the Anniversary Upgrade with all the Creations is available, but not the Creation Club itself.

As of December 5, 2023, Creation Club has been retired and the Verified Creator Program is intended to be its replacement. While Creation Club Creations are considered official content, [Verified Creator Program](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Verified_Creator_Program) Creations are considered third party [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods).

## Creation Credits
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SRMOD-icon-Credits.svg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SRMOD-icon-Credits.svg) Creation Credit icon Creation Credits (previously Creation Club Credits) are the currency for paid Creations. They may be purchased at the following rates:

- 750 Creation Credits - $7.99 / €6.99 / £5.99
- 1500 Creation Credits - $14.99 / €12.99 / £10.99
- 3000 Creation Credits - $24.99 / €20.99 / £18.99
- 5500 Creation Credits - $39.99 / €34.99 / £29.99

Additionally, 100 free credits are given to every user the first time they open the Creations menu.

Note that on PC and Xbox One, your credit balance was shared between Skyrim Special Edition and [*Fallout 4*](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fallout_4), the only other game that used the Creation Club. The Play Station 4 versions of these games did not have this feature.
