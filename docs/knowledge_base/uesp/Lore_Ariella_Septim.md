Crown Princess Ariella Septim was a child of [Emperor](Lore_Emperor.md) [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md), and heir apparent to the [Dragon Throne](Lore_Ruby_Throne.md) and the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md) as of [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389).<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-ARMIS-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

## History

### Heir to the Empire
During [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389), Ariella attended [Mid Year's festival](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mid_Year_Celebration)<sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup> in the Emperor's banquet hall some months before the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) began. The [ghost](Lore_Ghost.md) of [Ria Silmane](Lore_Ria_Silmane.md) would retroactively declare that the event was where evil took form in her master, the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md), [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-English Manual Arena-2)</sup> This holds at least some truth; it was here where General [Talin Warhaft](Lore_Talin_Warhaft.md) would regale to the Princess, Ariella Septim, about a time in his youth when a friend of his, Marten the Blade, used a [spell](Lore_Magic.md) of [illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) to change his own appearance into that of another to trick a group of bandits into thinking Marten was their leader. It was this anecdote that inspired Tharn to do something similar: to remove his Emperor, whom he was terribly jealous of, and take his place. While ruffling through possibilities in his mind, Tharn knew that he could not simply murder Uriel Septim VII, for in that case, Princess Ariella Septim would ascend to the throne. Such a scenario was unacceptable to Tharn, who would only change masters, and would be even more ashamed to serve a woman.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-ARMIS-1)</sup>

On the first day of [Hearthfire](Lore_Hearthfire.md) [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena Game-3)</sup> when Jagar Tharn enacted his conspiracy to take the place of his Emperor, he had cast a seal spell on the floor, trapping Uriel VII and his [Imperial Guard](Lore_Imperial_Guard.md) within a magical barrier of fire. Then did Tharn lay out his scheme to his royal adversary; to steal the visage of the living Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and transport the true Uriel VII to [a dimension where time passed more slowly](Lore_Oblivion.md), and when Uriel VII would eventually die, several generations would have grown up on Tamriel, with the glorious reign of "Uriel Septim VII" lying centuries in the past. Tharn assured the Emperor that he would take care of everything, including his precious child Ariella, who Tharn believed would "certainly understand" the value of fostering "family" alliances.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-ARMIS-1)</sup> Indicating that Tharn planned on bedding the girl, possibly to create an heir, it's unknown if a child was ever borne of this threatened union.

### Fate
Following these events, Ariella Septim never made a reappearance in written record. It's possible Ariella was dispatched with the Emperor's other children to various prisons disguised as schools during the Imperial Simulacrum,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Morrowind Real Barry-4)</sup> and/or got replaced with a [doppelganger](Lore_Doppelganger.md), like her brothers were rumored to have been.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Morrowind Game-5)</sup>

It is unknown if she abdicated her claim to the throne. By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), [Geldall Septim](Lore_Geldall_Septim.md) was understood to be the Crown Prince and heir apparent to the Dragon Throne.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Morrowind Game-5)</sup> By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) and the beginning of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), all of Uriel VII's known heirs were dead.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Baurus-6)</sup>

This indicates the Princess was currently around age fifteen during 3E 389. Meaning she was born around 3E 373-374.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-ARMIS-1)</sup> |
