—Anssulla<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAnssulla-1)</sup> Like the other races, the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) have their own culinary traditions. Grains and dried meat are staple foodstuffs for the Reachfolk. Feasts are rare in the Reach, as the land is unforgiving. When they do occur, the Reachfolk make a spectacle of it. Various clans of the Reach have different traditions related to eating. The [Starsinger Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Starsinger_Clan), for instance, considers spilling blood on a feast day unacceptable.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMedresi-2)</sup>

Alcohol is a commodity that the more open-minded clans of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) are willing to trade for. Some examples are [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) ale, [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) brandy, and [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) wine. Much of the Reach is unsuitable for the barley fields, vineyards, apiaries with which other cultures brew their drinks. Clans who don't have much contact with the outside world make do with what they have. Reach-made drinks are found primarily in the form of hard ciders. Various clans of the Reach have different traditions related to alcohol beverages.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DOTR-3)</sup>

## Utensils and Tableware
Reachman dishes and utensils are typically crafted from simple material or reused from other creators. Reachfolk typically eat with their hunting knives, a crust to sop with, and a tool that resembles a short quilting needle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRFL-4)</sup> Reachfolk of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) utilize kitchenware made of [Dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OMH-5)</sup> including plates,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> mugs,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> bowls,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> and cooking tools.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) has been used to make very highly sought after meat skewers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Reachfolk often turn goat skulls into drinking vessels such as goblets.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> It is considered impolite to decline a drink served in the vessel made of skull.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> The [Stranglehollow Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stranglehollow_Clan) used to toast each other with a drink mixed using the fingerbone of an ancestor.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Bodgrannagh-13)</sup>

| **Reachbread** | **Reachbread** is a unique type of bread unlike any other. Its prepared by first digging up various roots, which are then boiled and peeled. The roots are stuffed into a jar placed near a fire to bake. The finished product is scooped out of the jar and eaten.
