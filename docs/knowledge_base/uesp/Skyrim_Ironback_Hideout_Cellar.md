## Ironback Hideout Cellar
Once you have obtained the [Ironback Hideout Cellar Key](Skyrim_Ironback_Hideout_Cellar_Key.md) as part of the related quest, you will be directed to enter the cellar. Within, you will find an uncluttered entrance area containing an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md). The deceased Orc Kragrash used the basement to store his vampire hunting equipment, which is separated from you by a set of bars. The cellar key can be used to unlock the barred door and access these items. Behind the bars you will find a good deal of loot, including a treasure chest and a wall-mounted [silver greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Greatsword). Laid out on a table you will find an [ebony crossbow](Skyrim_Ebony_Crossbow.md), an [Elven crossbow](Skyrim_Elven_Crossbow.md), and several [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Fire](Skyrim_Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Fire.md) and [Shock](Skyrim_Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Shock.md). These crossbow items can usually only be crafted.
