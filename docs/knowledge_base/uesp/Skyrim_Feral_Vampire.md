[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-npc-Feral_Vampire.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Feral_Vampire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Feral_Vampire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Feral_Vampire.jpg) Feral Vampire The **Feral Vampire** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) encountered when exploring [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) with [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) during the quest [Chasing Echoes](Skyrim_Chasing_Echoes.md). After entering the secret entrance to the castle, you'll find her with some [death hounds](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md) before you make it to the courtyard. She is instantly hostile to you whether you have sided with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) or the vampires. She wears [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers), [footwraps](Skyrim_Footwraps.md), and a [Ring of Minor Destruction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Minor_Destruction), and wields an [Elven Dagger of Fear](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger_of_Fear.md). She also carries a [journal fragment](Skyrim_Journal_Fragment.md) which gives a little insight as to why she's down there.
