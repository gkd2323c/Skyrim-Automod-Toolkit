—In-game description in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* Cloudrest is one of the eight major cities in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> built on the highest peaks of [Eton Nir](Lore_Eton_Nir.md), the tallest mountain in the province. Its people hold great pride in their city and the large scope of the surrounding land they can view from high above.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> It is the home of the [Welkynars](Lore_Welkynars.md), an elite [knightly order](Lore_Knights.md) of [gryphon](Lore_Gryphon.md) riders that have protected the province for most of its history.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWOEN-3)</sup>

## Layout and Geography
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/ON-prerelease-Cloudrest.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Cloudrest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Cloudrest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Cloudrest.jpg) Cloudrest's entrance with the Aerie in the back [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/ON-banner-Cloudrest.png/90px-ON-banner-Cloudrest.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Cloudrest.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Cloudrest.png) The Banner of Cloudrest Cloudrest lies on the southern portion of Eton Nir, where it has a grand view of the central Summerset Isle plains to the south,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONCloudrest-4)</sup> and a view of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) from the highest available vantage point.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBIAG-5)</sup> The air is thin but is as strong and chilled as nirnroot wine.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoad Cloudrest-6)</sup> The city is built into a river valley that begins from a waterfall and flows in a river through the city's lower district. It leaves Cloudrest from another waterfall and through a river stream between the road and the [Ebon Stadmont](Lore_Ebon_Stadmont.md) forest to finally drain into the bay of western Summerset Isle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONCloudrest-4)</sup> Cloudrest is neighbored by several settlements, such as [Karndar Watch](Lore_Karndar_Watch.md) to the east, [Kings Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kings_Haven) to the north, and Old Falls to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

Cloudrest is divided into two sections: the lower section, which surrounds a river and connects to the city's main entrance, and the higher section, which is built on the valley face and mountain top. From the city gate is a hill that leads to another gate, which leads into the lower city. Not much can be said about the lower district, except that it is a single, long street over a river canal, with several bridges connecting to each side. It ends at the base of the waterfall, and from there continues east along the mountain face into a narrow, dead-end street.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONMap-7)</sup> Somewhere in the lower district is the city's marketplace with a direct path to the city gates.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WPA-8)</sup>

The upper district is a single main street above the waterfall, with small, narrow streets up the mountain, and a large, well-kept bridge over the lower section that connects to the Cloudrest Aerie. The Aerie is a fortress with four towers looming over the city and a central courtyard.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONCloudrest-4)</sup> There is an old passageway that runs inside the mountain and connects the upper district directly with the outer city gates.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CSE-9)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-creature-Z%27Maja.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Z%27Maja.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Z%27Maja.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Z%27Maja.jpg) Z'Maja of the Abyssal Cabal When Cloudrest was founded is unknown, but some of the city's ancient ruins are remnants of the prehistoric civilizations that originally lived on the Summerset Isles. The oldest ruins under Cloudrest and across the islands are made of [coral](Lore_Coral.md). The [Imperial Geographic Society](Lore_Imperial_Geographic_Society.md) theorized that the coral must have been transported from many miles away in the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md), and it would strongly suggest that the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) previously lived here.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-PGE3-13)</sup> The Welkynars have been active throughout Summerset's history and made Cloudrest their home because of its central location on the main island.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWOEN-3)</sup>

At some point, Essanyon Duure received visions of the end times, supposedly from the Divines, and gathered followers from across the Summerset Isles. One of his initial followers went to spread his gospel in Cloudrest, and in time, he amassed a lot of support. His ritual was set to take place in Ezduiin, a town in [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), but after a successful sacrifice and resulting assault on the town, Essanyon used an artifact called the Mallari-Mora and damned the souls of his followers to the ruins of Ezduiin.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ER-14)</sup>

Sometime before Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md)'s reign began in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580),<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ATUQ-15)</sup> the Canonreeve of Cloudrest was well-respected and came from a noble family of an unparalleled lineage. However, the Canonreeve was eventually arrested by the [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md). They took certain antiquities from a historical research project in the ruins under Eton Nir, altered manifests and records so that they could disappear without a trace, and sold them to foreign collectors. Rumors were spreading, and the Divine Prosecution began an investigation that led to the Canonreeve's persecution.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-COTDP-16)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Welkynars, led by Relequen at the time,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWOEN-3)</sup> battled with the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) sorceress, Z'Maja, a member of the Abyssal Cabal of wizards that tried to conquer Summerset Isle. Though there were casualties, their victory over Z'Maja was quick and smooth, and she was transported to the Aerie for interrogation. But while three of the Welkynars relished in their win, the other, Galenwe, was suspicious and believed Z'Maja was plotting something that could threaten Cloudrest and even the Welkynars' lives.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ON154-17)</sup> Her fears came true when Z'Maja used her powerful [shadow magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md) to take over the city. It began with a dark mist pouring down from the Aerie and consuming the lower city. Once people realized its danger, they desperately ran to the city gates, but were quickly consumed by the mist, losing any vitality.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WPA-8)</sup>

Z'Maja captured the Welkynars, Galenwe, Relequen, and Sirora, and commanded [shadow replicas](Lore_Shadow.md) of them from the Aerie. Olorime was the only one to escape, and she managed to save a couple of citizens and place them in the upper city.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WPA-8)</sup> But Olorime was at an impasse, and Cloudrest was virtually gone.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONOlorime-18)</sup> The city was finally recaptured when the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) arrived and assisted her in defeating the Sea Sload. It is unknown if each of the Welkynars was saved or if they perished, but Z'Maja and her shadow creatures were defeated, and Olorime was left to rebuild the order.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ONWoe-19)</sup> In an [alternate reality](Lore_Many_Paths.md) where the Sea Sload successfully conquered Summerset Isle, Cloudrest became Z'Maja's home and a place for aspirants to learn the ways of shadow magic.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-KTT-20)</sup>

In [3E 110](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_110), the kings of the Summerset Isles formed a unified alliance to combat the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md)<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-21)</sup> in their last-known invasion of the province, the [War of the Isle](Lore_War_of_the_Isle.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-PGE3OL-22)</sup> Despite having assistance from Emperor [Antiochus](Lore_Antiochus.md) and the [Imperial Navy](Lore_Imperial_Navy.md), the Maormer were defeated because of a supernatural storm accredited to the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-21)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-TWQ5-23)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city-state of Cloudrest was an active settlement. It was ruled by King Kelkemmenar and had a rivalry with [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup>

### Known Rulers
For the longest time, the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) served as counselors to the rulers of the Summerset Isles. If anyone wanted their counsel, their best method was either through a select few places or the kings themselves. They continued this service up until their island of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) first vanished in the Second Era, around when the Mages Guild was formed. When they returned several years later for the Third Era, they continued where they left off<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-FOA-24)</sup> until Artaeum disappeared a second time sometime in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) for whatever reason.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-SRTolfdir-25)</sup>

## Culture and Society
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/LG-cardart-Flesh_Sculpture.png/200px-LG-cardart-Flesh_Sculpture.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Flesh_Sculpture.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Flesh_Sculpture.png) Flesh Sculpting [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-creature-Darremalatu_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Darremalatu_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Darremalatu_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Darremalatu_02.jpg) A Welkynar knight and their Gryphon mount
- *For more information, see the: **[Culture](Lore_Altmer.md#Culture)** and **[Society](Lore_Altmer.md#Society_and_Religion)** sections on the Altmer page.*

The Altmer of Cloudrest are proud of their city and their land.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Because of its high placement on Eton Nir, Cloudrest acquired the nickname, the **Aerie of the Altmer**.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoad Cloudrest-6)</sup> The city has a unique architectural style that mixes different yet odd styles. The Imperial Geographic Society describes its buildings like strangler vines built over older structures.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-PGE3-13)</sup> Some of Cloudrest's regional foods and drinks include [golden ale](Lore_Ale.md)<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-ONAle-29)</sup> and [clarified coffee](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Coffee).<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-ONCoffee-30)</sup>

The Welkynars have served as Summerset Isle's protectors for most of its history. They chose Cloudrest as their headquarters because of its proximity to other cities and built their aerie at the city's pinnacle. They usually consist of four members, one for each cardinal direction. The process to become one takes many years, and an initiate must raise their gryphon companion from birth.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWOEN-3)</sup>

The Welkynars are not the only group based in Cloudrest. The Faculty of Chirurgeons is an elite group of [flesh-sculptors](Lore_Flesh_Sculpture.md) that has been active as late as the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). The [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) master of flesh sculpting, [Galathil](Lore_Galathil.md) studied with them and worked with nobility across the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md).<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-SRGalathil-31)</sup> The School of [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) is prevalent in Cloudrest and attracts Altmer talented in magic. Some of the most well-educated, courageous, and strong-willed become capable illusionists and practice the school of magic in various fields, whether on the battlefield or in academia.<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-LCL-32)</sup>
