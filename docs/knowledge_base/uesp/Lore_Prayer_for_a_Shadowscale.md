Prayer for a Shadowscale *A prayer to Sithis*
---
Hail to [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md), the ever-changing.

What is consumed is restored.

The nothing of the [Void](Lore_Void.md), the whisper of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).

Blood whetted blade, you have watered the roots.

Pass through Shadow and return.
