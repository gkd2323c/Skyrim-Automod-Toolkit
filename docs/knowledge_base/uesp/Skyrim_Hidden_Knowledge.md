# Dragonborn Quests

## Main Quest
[![Dragonborn Knotwork](https://images.uesp.net/2/22/SR-qico-Main_2.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-qico-Main_2.png) - **[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn_(quest).md)**: Travel to the island of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) to find out what's happening there. ([![SR-achievement-Outlander.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/SR-achievement-Outlander.png/18px-SR-achievement-Outlander.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Outlander.png) 20 points/Bronze)
- **[The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md)**: Uncover the source of [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s power in the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md). ([![SR-achievement-The Temple of Miraak.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-achievement-The_Temple_of_Miraak.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Temple_of_Miraak.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Temple_of_Miraak.png) 20 points/Bronze)
- **[The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md)**: Find a way to free the citizens of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).
- **[Cleansing the Stones](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md)**: Use the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) to cleanse each of the remaining [All-Maker Stones](Skyrim_All-Maker_Stones.md).
- **[The Path of Knowledge](Skyrim_The_Path_of_Knowledge.md)**: Discover the locations of the Black Books in order to stop [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md). ([![SR-achievement-The Path of Knowledge.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-achievement-The_Path_of_Knowledge.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Path_of_Knowledge.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Path_of_Knowledge.png) 20 points/Bronze)
- **[The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md)**: Travel to the realm of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md) to learn the rest of [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md).
- **[At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md)**: Confront [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) at the summit of [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md) and defeat him once and for all. ([![SR-achievement-At the Summit of Apocrypha.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-achievement-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.png/18px-SR-achievement-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.png) 40 points/Silver)

## [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) Side Quests
- **[Black Book: The Winds of Change](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Winds_of_Change_(quest).md)**: Learn the [Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Winds_of_Change_(book).md)'s hidden knowledge.
- **[The Final Descent](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md)**: Find out what happened to the [great-grandfather](Skyrim_Gratian_Caerellius.md) of [Crescius Caerellius](Skyrim_Crescius_Caerellius.md).
- **[March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead)**: Stop the [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) attacks on [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
- **[A New Debt](Skyrim_A_New_Debt.md)**: Help [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md) with his debt to [Mogrul](Skyrim_Mogrul_(Dragonborn).md).
- **[Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md)**: Save [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) from an assassination plot.

## [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) Miscellaneous Quests
- **[An Axe to Find](Skyrim_An_Axe_to_Find.md)**: Retrieve an [ancient Nordic pickaxe](Skyrim_Ancient_Nordic_Pickaxe.md) for [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md).
- **[Clean Sweep](Skyrim_Clean_Sweep.md)**: Clear the [Temple Ancestral Tomb](Skyrim_Temple_Ancestral_Tomb.md) of [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md).
- **[Feeding the Addiction](Skyrim_Feeding_the_Addiction.md)**: Convince an [innkeeper](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) to let a [drunken miner](Skyrim_Bralsa_Drel.md) get back to [the bar](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md).
- **[Fetch the Netch](Skyrim_Fetch_the_Netch.md)**: Obtain five samples of [netch jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Netch_Jelly) for [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md).
- **[First Edition](Skyrim_First_Edition.md)**: Locate an important [folio](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lusty_Argonian_Maid_Folio) for [Cindiri Arano](Skyrim_Cindiri_Arano.md).
- **[It's All In The Taste](Skyrim_It%27s_All_In_The_Taste.md)**: Distribute [Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md)'s [Sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Sujamma) to the citizens of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
- **[Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full)**: Recover the [bonemold formula](Skyrim_Bonemold_Formula.md) for [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md).
- **[Pain in the Necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pain_in_the_Necklace)**: Find [East Empire pendants](Skyrim_East_Empire_Pendant.md) for [Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md).
- **[Recipe for Distraction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Distraction)**: Locate a hidden stash of [Emberbrand Wine](Skyrim_Emberbrand_Wine.md) for [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

## [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) Side Quests
- **[Filial Bonds](Skyrim_Filial_Bonds.md)**: Find [Wulf Wild-Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood)'s brother, [Torkild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torkild), who may have become a [werebear](Skyrim_Werebear.md).
- **[Lost Legacy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legacy)**: Travel to [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md) and defeat [Vahlok the Jailor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vahlok_the_Jailor).
- **[A New Source of Stalhrim](Skyrim_A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.md)**: Find a [blacksmith](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md) missing from [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md).

## [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md) Side Quests
- **[Azra's Staffs](Skyrim_Azra%27s_Staffs.md)**: Retrieve a staff made by [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md) for Master [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).

- **[Healing a House](Skyrim_Healing_a_House.md)**: Help [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) repair the tower of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).
- **[Heart Stones](Skyrim_Heart_Stones.md)**: Find a [heart stone](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
- **[Lost Knowledge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Knowledge)**: Retrieve the [Black Books](Lore_Black_Book.md) for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
- **[Old Friends](Skyrim_Old_Friends.md)**: Find the source of attacks on [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).
- **[Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md)**: Find a new steward for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
- **[Telvanni Research](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telvanni_Research)**: Extract a sample from an [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md)'s research.
- **[Wind and Sand](Skyrim_Wind_and_Sand_(quest).md)**: Retrieve a copy of *[Wind and Sand](Skyrim_Wind_and_Sand_(book).md)* for [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
