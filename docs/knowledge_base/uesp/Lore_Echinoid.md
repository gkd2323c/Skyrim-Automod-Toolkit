# Echinoid

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/ON-furnishing-Solstice_Shell%2C_Fossilized_Echinoid.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Solstice_Shell%2C_Fossilized_Echinoid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Solstice_Shell,_Fossilized_Echinoid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Solstice_Shell,_Fossilized_Echinoid.jpg) The shell of a fossilized echinoid There are several different species of echinoids on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Echinoids are often sedentary aquatic invertebrates like [sea urchins](Lore_Sea_Urchin.md) and [sandcakes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sandcake).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Only Source-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTM-2)</sup> Other related species of bottom-dwelling invertebrates include [sea stars](Lore_Sea_Star.md) and [sea cucumbers](Lore_Sea_Cucumber.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Chyrisnia-3)</sup>
