Seeksxilt's Notes on Bar-Sakka **by [Seekxilts](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Seekxilts)** *Speculation on the mysterious guardian of Naj-Caldeesh* *
---
Almost no one spoke of the monster who guards Naj-Caldeesh. Nightmarish memories kept by the Shell-Tide villagers say that his footsteps shook the mountain, his claws felled trees, and that his tail cleaved stones when he slammed it down. Accounting for generations of fear carving the truth from the bedrock, Bar-Sakka still was a fearsome creature.

Little is known about the ancients' ability to train and command creatures. While we still share a close bond with some creatures, husbandry is not one of the foremost skills of any [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) village on [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md). This leads me to believe that Bar-Sakka was tied to the mysterious power that separates Naj-Caldeesh from all the other xanmeers of the period.

Before discovering the entrance, I had several theories about what Bar-Sakka could be. These theories ranged from an oversized feral [Naga](Lore_Naga.md) to a malformed [Hadolid](Lore_Hadolid.md). But seeing the carvings throughout Naj-Caldeesh, it is clear that Bar-Sakka was a [Vahath Behemoth](Lore_Vahath_Behemoth.md).

The Argonians of this xanmeer cared for his every need. That much is clear from the detailed work of the carvings themselves. The food they offered appears specially prepared, which implies labor and resources being allocated to a beast when they could have been kept for themselves. Bar-Sakka may have been more than a protector. He could have been their savior.

I will continue my search for more answers.
