[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/MER-art-Ascadian_Isles.jpg/200px-MER-art-Ascadian_Isles.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Ascadian_Isles.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Ascadian_Isles.jpg) Ascadian Isles [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/MW-place-Lake_Amaya.jpg/200px-MW-place-Lake_Amaya.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Amaya.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Amaya.jpg) Lake Amaya, a large lake in the Ascadian Isles The Ascadian Isles were fertile, lush agricultural lowlands mainly under [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) control in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)'s southwestern area. The climate was temperate and comfortable, with moderate rainfall. The region encompassed land as far south as [Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md), west to the [Foyada Mamaea](Lore_Foyada_Mamaea.md), north to the central [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) just past the Fields of Kummu shrine, and east to the large [Daedric shrine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Shrines) of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md). The land was rich in nutrients and therefore littered with small farms and plantations. The islands themselves, while scattered, were close enough for easy transportation, the only main obstacle being [Lake Amaya](Lore_Lake_Amaya_(Vvardenfell).md). Notable vegetation included large mushroom-like plants not unlike trees, [corkbulb](Lore_Corkbulb.md), [marshmerrow](Lore_Marshmerrow.md) and [muckspunge](Lore_Muckspunge.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

Major cities in the Ascadian Isles included the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) stronghold of [Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md), the Temple city of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), the market town of [Suran](Lore_Suran.md), and [Pelagiad](Lore_Pelagiad.md). The architecture was a mix of Hlaalu, Velothi, and Imperial. Additionally, many of the councilors of [Great House](Lore_Great_Houses.md) Hlaalu maintained plantations in the Isles to exploit the region's good growing soil, and to breed [netch](Lore_Netch.md) for their leather.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

The largest of these plantations was the Dren Plantation, a large farm in the center of the region, north of Vivec. In the mid-Second Era, it belonged to Mistress Rilasi Dren, who was later arrested for being involved in a corruption scandal with Marshal Hleran.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONQ-3)</sup> During the late third era, it was known for its connections with both House Hlaalu and the [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md).
