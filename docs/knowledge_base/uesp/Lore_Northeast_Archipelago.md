[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](File_LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_(Anthology).jpg.md) [](File_LO-map-Morrowind_Islands_(Anthology).jpg.md) Map of the islands (Anthology) The unnamed archipelago of the northeast reaches of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) is a collection of at least three islands in the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md), belonging to the greater easts of the province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> In terms of terrain, the rugged archipelago has a mix rocky hills and fertile plains with a number of settlements,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWElone-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Concept Map-4)</sup> and is in a colder part of the continent.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-5)</sup> On the largest island, [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) controls the palace of [Port Telvannis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Port_Telvannis),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LA-6)</sup> found on the south bay.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-SM-7)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-8)</sup> It is believed to be their capital city, as the High Telvanni wizards called the "Parliament of Bugs" is headquartered there.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Po M-9)</sup> The Telvanni prefer to tend their herds of [giant insects](Lore_Morrowind.md#Fauna) amid the rocky hills and islands of the extreme northeast.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1M-10)</sup> The [Indoril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:House_Indoril) rule towns on the two outer islands.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-8)</sup> During the reign of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), there was a series of [Imperial garrisons](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md#Known_Legion_Forts) established along the coastal Morrowind islands. These forts were attacked by [Uriel III](Lore_Uriel_III.md)'s forces in [3E 121](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_121), which Empress [Kintyra II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md)'s consort [Kontin Arynx](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kontin_Arynx) died defending, marking the start of the [War of the Red Diamond](Lore_War_of_the_Red_Diamond.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-11)</sup> There is a dungeon on the eastern part of the largest island, which the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) may have explored during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ARDungeon Map-12)</sup> There is a fourth small island found to the far north of the largest island.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONMap-13)</sup>

On the mainland, [Llothanis Heights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Llothanis_Heights) is the closest settlement to the islands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Concept Map-4)</sup> There is large island found north of the badlands of the mainland, located west of the island group,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> but it is unknown if it is considered part of the archipelago. There are also some small islands found in the strait between the archipelago and mainland.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-LOMap-14)</sup> Trading ships of the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company) sometimes sail through this strait for passage to the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md), and other times sail around the islands through the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) to go to ports on the [Northern Coast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Northern_Coast).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-EECMap-15)</sup>
