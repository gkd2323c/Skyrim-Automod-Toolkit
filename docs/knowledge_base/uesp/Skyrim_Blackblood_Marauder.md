[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Wizard A.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Wizard A.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Wizard A.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Wizard A.jpg) Blackblood Marauder (Bandit Wizard) These two Marauders are both [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit wizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard), differing only in their appearance. They wear a leveled set of [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes), which will be enchanted to either fortify [Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration), [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), or [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) or to [regenerate magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md). They are armed with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and may also carry a small amount of gold. Each can cast a leveled set of spells from the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md), [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md), and [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) schools, including one [armor spell](Skyrim_Armor_(spells).md), one [ward spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ward), one [healing spell](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) and one elemental damage spell.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Archer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Archer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Archer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blackblood_Marauder_Archer.jpg) Blackblood Marauder (Bandit Archer) This Marauder is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit archer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Archer). He wears [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots) and defends himself with a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with twenty leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows), both of which may be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality. The bow is also supplemented by a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), which also may be up to elven in quality. Additionally, he may carry a few [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) or a small amount of gold.

## Related Quests

- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md)**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Notes
- The archer does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face, albeit with a shaved head and the identifying face tattoo. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), [Lynoit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynoit), [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md), and many other generic NPCs.
