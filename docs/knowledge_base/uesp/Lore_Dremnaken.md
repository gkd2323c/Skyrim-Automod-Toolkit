Dremnaken are quadruped bestial [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that somewhat resemble reptilian [welwa](Lore_Welwa.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> with red scales, pointed ears, two sets of front-facing eyes, sharp fangs, and a long tail.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> These creatures are native to the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), invasive to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HFK-3)</sup> and rarely seen on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

Dremnaken are intelligent hunters that are capable of extreme cruelty, and have a desire to quite literally consume knowledge.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vo C-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HFK-3)</sup> Their power to consume knowledge was apparently given to them by [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) himself.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> They gore their prey and have been known to use magic to steal the victim's life spirit. Despite their bestial appearance, Dremnaken are capable of speech.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> They are known to hunt in packs.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Runt-7)</sup>

Dremnaken are often armored for battle.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Some, known as wrathsteeds, are ridden as fearsome war mounts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Dremnaken runts are diminutive in size but just as fierce as their larger pack mates.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Runt-7)</sup>
