## Clearspring Tarn
Beyond its aesthetic beauty, the tarn is mostly a landmark for the location of the [Clearspring Cave](Skyrim_Clearspring_Cave.md) that is hidden beneath it. An apprentice-locked wooden [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) can be found in the water along with a surprising variety of small fish that can be caught for use in [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md). [Goldfish](Skyrim_Goldfish.md) can also be found in the water.

You may encounter a pair of [hunters](Skyrim_Hunter.md) that are busy pursuing their quarry as there are a large number of elk in the region.

There is a small cave to the west of the tarn with a path heading northeast past the tarn, after a short way the path splits with the right fork leading back under the tarn to the entrance to Clearspring Cave and the left fork heading in the direction of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md).

To achieve "Cleared" status, kill the Troll in Clearspring Cave below.
