Mastery of Compassion **by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frandar_Hunding)**
---
In the season of life in which I searched beneath the [Evening Star](Lore_Evening_Star.md), I wandered friendless in strange lands.

Among outcasts and beggars, I learned pity for those who suffer and love for those who are lost. Their pain is my pain for all my future days.

With [Tu'whacca](Lore_Tu%27whacca.md)'s guidance, the [sword-singer](Lore_Sword-singers.md) must do the same to achieve Mastery of Compassion.
