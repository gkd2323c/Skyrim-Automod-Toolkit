[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-npc-Sylgja.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sylgja.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sylgja.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sylgja.jpg) Sylgja at the campfire **Sylgja** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md). Her parents are [Verner Rock-Chucker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Verner_Rock-Chucker) and [Annekke Crag-Jumper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Annekke_Crag-Jumper) in [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md). She has been unable to visit them due to a leg injury and only recovered at all thanks to a passing [priest](Skyrim_Erandur.md) of [Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mara).

Sylgja starts off her day by waking up at 5am and resting for an hour in [her house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sylgja%27s_House). At 6am, she proceeds to [Redbelly Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redbelly_Mine), where she works for most of the day. She will be mining for twelve hours until 6pm, when she finally stops work and heads home. She will relax in her house for an hour until 7pm, and then spends four hours around the campfire at Shor's Stone. She will be either cooking or simply sitting by the campfire until 11pm, when she heads back home to sleep. She sleeps for six hours and repeats her schedule the following day.

Like most miners, she wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). She is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Sylgja%27s_House) to her house, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

She can be [married](Skyrim_Marriage.md) after you have done her a favor by delivering a satchel to her father. If you choose to move in with Sylgja, you will have full access to her house and items and she will carry on with her regular schedule.

## Notes
- Sylgja mentions that a "priest of Mara on his way to Dawnstar" was the one who healed her. This is almost certainly [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md).
- Despite them being close family, there is no special dialogue when you visit her with her mother as a follower.
- It's possible that Sylgja was originally supposed to be suffering from a sickness, rather than a leg injury, as your dialogue for asking her about it is internally called `FFSS02Sylgja Sick Branch Topic`.
- Originally, the only woman in Shor's Stone was [Grelka](Skyrim_Grelka.md), who still appears in the final game as a marketplace vendor in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). This much can be gathered from earlier items for Sylgja's quest. Instead of **Sylgja's Satchel** and **Verner's Satchel**, you were meant to transport **[Package for Grelka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Package_for_Grelka)** and **[Package for Verner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Package_for_Verner)** instead. - Additionally, Sylgja is not involved in any scenes that play out between the miners when they sit around the campfire in the evening. Grelka is, and they were recorded in her voicetype, but they can never be heard due to them requiring Grelka to be in town, which she never is, due to never leaving Riften. The scenes would've gone as follows:

**[Grogmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grogmar_gro-Burzag)**: *"I've been watching the way you mine and I have to say, I'm very impressed."*
**Grelka**: *"I owe it all to my father who started me swinging the pickaxe when I was a barefoot little girl."*
**Grogmar**: *"Well, it shows. Your skill rivals some of the greatest miners I've known."*
**Grelka**: *"Thank you. It's nice to know someone admires me for more than just my appearance."* **[Odfel](Skyrim_Odfel.md)**: *"Grelka, do you fancy a walk tomorrow?"*
**Grelka**: *"Odfel, I've told you time and time again. I'm not interested in a relationship with you."*
**Odfel**: *"And why not? I'm handsome, strong and smart. What else could you possibly need?"*
**Grelka**: *"I need someone that cares about me more than they care about themselves. That person isn't you."* **[Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md)**: *"How are your parents faring, Grelka? Doing better than we are I would guess."*
**Grelka**: *"It's no feast in their neck of the woods either. Last time I was up there, father said they were barely scraping by."*
**Filnjar**: *"This [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) is taking its toll on all of us. Miners are some of the hardest hit."*
**Grelka**: *"That's what scares me. I'm afraid I'll travel to Darkwater Crossing and find it burned to the ground."*
