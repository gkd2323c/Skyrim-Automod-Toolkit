[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-npc-Silvia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silvia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silvia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silvia.jpg) Silvia **Silvia** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) resident of [Darklight Tower](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower.md). She seeks to become a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md), a desire that her daughter [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) doesn't share. She is a key figure in the quest [Repentance](Skyrim_Repentance.md).

Silvia wears [necromancer robes](Skyrim_Necromancer_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c36e8). She carries [staff of hag's wrath](Skyrim_Staff_of_Hag%27s_Wrath.md) and a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md). In a fight, she will defend herself with her staff and destruction magic; she may also summon a minion from Oblivion.

## Related Quests
- **[Repentance](Skyrim_Repentance.md)**: Help [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) prevent her mother Silvia from transforming into a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Silvia will speak to her daughter when you are outside:

- **Illia**: *"Greetings, mother."*
- **Silvia**: *"Daughter. Who is this you have brought me?"*
- **Illia**: *"I found a willing subject for you. Payment has already been discussed, of course."*
- **Silvia**: *"Splendid! Please take a seat on the chair behind me, would you?"*

- **Silvia**: *"I'll just prepare the subject now..."*
- **Illia**: *"I can't let you do this, mother. You have to die now!"*
