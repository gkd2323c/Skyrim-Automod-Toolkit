[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-npc-Edith.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Edith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Edith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Edith.jpg) Edith **Edith**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md), is an employee of [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md) and works in the depths of [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

She is incredibly dedicated to her mining duties and can be found hacking away 24 hours a day, without ever eating or sleeping.

She wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). She carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).
