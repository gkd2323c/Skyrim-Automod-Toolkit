[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-npc-Minette_Vinius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Minette_Vinius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Minette_Vinius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Minette_Vinius.jpg) Minette Vinius at The Winking Skeever **Minette Vinius** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) in [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md). She is the daughter of the inn's owner, [Corpulus Vinius](Skyrim_Corpulus_Vinius.md), and the sister of [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md).

Minette wears blue colored [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8), and is equipped with a simple [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Winking_Skeever) to her father's inn, a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child), and a small amount of gold. She spends her days playing outside with her friends [Svari](Skyrim_Svari.md) and [Kayd](Skyrim_Kayd.md).

## Notes
- Even though her father and brother are [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial.md), she is a Nord, and she has a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) name. However, her father's [cousin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Faida) is also a Nord.
