[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) *Dead Man's Dread* **Dead Man's Dread** is an ancient ship located within the [Grotto](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.md) on [Blackbone Isle](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md). It is initially filled with [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md), but can be claimed as a player home after completing the related quest.

Once you have claimed the ship as your own, a map inside can be used for instant [transport](Skyrim_Transport.md#Dead_Man.27s_Dread) to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), and [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). You can also unlock the two other markers on the map to allow travel to [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> (technically [Icewater Jetty](Skyrim_Icewater_Jetty.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>) and [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> by first discovering these locations. This mode of travel is one-way.

## Interior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dead_Man%27s_Dread.jpg) The captain's quarters What you'll see first after entering the cave is a path to get onboard the ship. After getting onto the deck, at your left will be the entrance. First sight you have's a steering wheel. Both sides will take you downstairs. There's a [Shrine of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay) and in front of it, to your left will be a room with 2 beds and to your right is the child's room, where you can find a [Scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar). The front door will take you to a hall with several rooms and some downstairs. If you go to the hall, first room will have 4 beds and several items and books. The room spotted at the left wall is a smithing room, and there'll be an [Anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), a [Tanning Rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), a [Workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) and a [Grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), also there are 4 [Scimitars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar). Across the hall entrance, you can find an [Arcane Enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md), an [Alchemy Lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) and many soul gems and ingredients. Also there's another room inside this place with 2 more beds and another [Scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar).

Whether you go downstairs at the main hall or jump in the hole at the center of it, you'll see a tavern place, and deeper will be a storage for the tavern. Across the tavern is the captain's quarters with a single mannequin. There's a way left between the tavern and the captains' quarters, this path takes you to another room with 2 more beds. Finally, if you go right, you'll find the last room with 2 calabooses

## Notes
- The ship's cupboards contain unique clothing items added by this [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).
- Although Blackbone Isle has a map marker and can be fast traveled to from the mainland, you are required to take the boat back to [Orphan's Tear](Skyrim_Orphan%27s_Tear.md) to leave the island. This makes *Dead Man's Dread* particularly inconvenient as a player home, requiring three loading screens to reach via fast travel. The bandits at Orphan's Tear also respawn, meaning you will likely be attacked upon landing.
- Version 1.6 of the [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md) adds map markers to all 5 locations you can fast travel to from inside the ship. This mode of travel remains one-way.

## Bugs
- The Ghostly Remains of the [Ghost Sailors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_Sailor) and [Ghost Captain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ghost_Captain) may not despawn and will instead remain on the deck. This happens when you read the [Pirate's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate%27s_Note) and return to Dead Man's Dread too soon to claim it as a home. - Wait for ten full days before reading the Pirate's Note and coming back to the island.
- The cooking pot in the kitchen by the bar on the bottom deck is set the wrong way round; activating it results in the player being positioned inside the wall, and leaving it results in the player being pushed inside the prison room. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The weapon stand in the blacksmith's room, aside from the three Scimitars already attached to it, may not be interactable. <sup>**?**</sup>
