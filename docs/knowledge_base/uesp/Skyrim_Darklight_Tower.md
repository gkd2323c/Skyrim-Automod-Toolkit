[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-place-Darklight_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Darklight_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darklight_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darklight_Tower.jpg) Darklight Tower **Darklight Tower** is a medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) southwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md), [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md), and [witches](Skyrim_Witch.md). It contains two zones: *[Darklight Tower](#Darklight_Tower)* and *[Darklight Chambers](#Darklight_Chambers)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md)**: Uncover an ancient Dwemer secret. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Repentance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance)**: Help [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) prevent her mother [Silvia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia) from transforming into a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The path leading south to the tower from the road between Riften and [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) is unmarked and difficult to spot and is occupied by leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md). Various species of harvestable mushrooms and plants grow throughout the area, including [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md), [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md), [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md), [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap), and [mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md). The tower itself is a dilapidated fort, with a large buttressed outer wall and a walkway along the top. An arch leads into a courtyard with several flights of stairs leading up to the entrance. As you approach, impaled goat heads and polished mammoth tusks betray the nature of the occupants inside.

### Darklight Tower
The first floor is the ruined entrance to the tower. Upon entering, you will see a woman named [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) battling a witch in the second room. Once the witch is defeated, Illia will speak to you and ask you to assist her in stopping her mother from becoming a hagraven. There is an adept-locked door to the northeast, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) and a couple food barrels behind it. Illia carries the [key](Skyrim_Darklight_Tower_Closet_Key.md) to open the door. Otherwise, there is nothing of note on this floor. Against the south wall a candlelit stairway climbing further up the tower.

At the top of the stairs is a balcony that overlooks the room below and circles to the west. There are two [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) in this area, one of which is leveled. At the far end of the balcony is a wooden door leading into a room with a hag, who will attack as soon as you or Illia enter. To the right of the southern stairwell is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy.md), with a round wooden table beside it holding a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a random [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a random empty [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). There is also an unlocked chest hidden under an overturned table in the northwestern corner.

The stairs lead up to the third floor, a two-tiered space containing several rooms that make up the living quarters of the witches in the tower. There is a leveled creature and a witch on this floor. There are two raised sections of flooring linked by a bridge, with stairs leading up to the larger platform opposite the door you entered through. At the top is an area with doorways to both north and south, both of which are blocked by raised spears. A lever is located behind the southern door. The furniture in this room includes four unowned single beds, a table holding a [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md) and a portion of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), and a variety of overturned furniture. The smaller of the two platforms contains a visible [spike wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spike_Wall) trap that is triggered by a [pressure plate](Skyrim_Pressure_Plate.md), and a cupboard holding a random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) and a random potion of healing, with a filled random soul gem on the ground in front of it. To the right of the cupboard is a table with a metal basin in the middle containing a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) and two bulbs of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md).

Beneath this area is a corridor heading west, which Illia will follow. The corridor turns south after a few paces, then passes a thick patch of cobwebs that conceal an unlocked chest on the right. The corridor continues, then turns east. There is a locked wooden door on the right that requires the key carried by Illia. Behind the door is a cupboard holding a random [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka), two random potions of healing, one potion of [stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), and one other random potion. There are also three food barrels in the corner, and two sets of shelves holding only clutter. The corridor continues east and up some stairs into another room containing a large fireplace. A cupboard near the fireplace holds two poisons and a copy of *[The Aetherium Wars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars)*. The room also contains a lever beside a set of spears blocking a doorway. This is the southern of the two sets of spears seen previously. The lever lowers both sets of spears. Illia will warn you that there is a hagraven ahead.

There is a flight of stairs behind the northern spears, with a closed wooden door at the top. The door leads to a room with a hagraven and two creatures. The hagraven will be opposite the door in her tent behind a table. She prefers to attack with [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md). On the table is a [hagraven claw](Skyrim_Hagraven_Claw.md), a random filled soul gem, a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Sun's Dawn, v2](Skyrim_2920,_Sun%27s_Dawn,_v2.md)*, and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger). The usual hagraven decor (impaled goat heads, etc.) surrounds the tent. To the left of the tent is another unlocked chest, while the ground in front of the table contains two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) that appear to have fallen from a rack above. To the right of the door you entered through is a flight of stairs that have caved in. There is a lootable [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) among the rubble, while to the north is a wooden door leading to the next zone, *Darklight Chambers*.

### Darklight Chambers
Beyond the door is a curved flight of stairs that leads up to a circular room with large wooden stakes in the middle and a hag and a witch standing next to them. Among the stakes is a dead [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md#Dunmer_Male). Once the enemies have been dealt with, Illia will approach a master-locked door to the south and exclaim that the door is locked. She will suggest taking the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower_Key) from one of the hags nearby, or just picking the lock. To the east is another corridor leading up to a door.

The hagraven with the key is located in the next room, a multi-tiered hagraven lair that also contains a hag. There is a short hallway lined with damaging spikes, with stairs leading up to a floor with a hag standing in a tent by an unlocked chest. The hagraven is across a bridge to the south. More stairs lead up against the north wall to another level with a master-locked chest and a cupboard holding three random potions of magicka and three random poisons. Crossing the bridge and climbing another flight of stairs leads to a table with a random empty soul gem, an iron dagger, and a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) on top.

Beyond the master-locked door is a corridor which leads to a flight of stairs. The stairs lead to another corridor containing two food barrels and ending at a small room with a leveled hag. This room contains a small table holding an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) next to a set of shelves against the west wall. On the shelves are two bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a random [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration), and two potions of magicka. Against the north wall is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md), while against the east wall is another unlocked chest. The only other item of interest in the room is an unowned bedroll in the middle of the floor. A damp corridor climbs north from here, then turns west and opens onto a balcony above the room with the wooden stakes in the middle. There are two leveled creatures and a hag on the balcony. In the middle of the balcony is a cupboard holding a random [potion of resist magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Magic) and a leveled potion of magicka, while the edge of the balcony features a table holding a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[Mystery of Talara, v3](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystery_of_Talara,_v3)*, another random filled soul gem, and a bottle of wine. Past the table are stairs curving up around the tower. At the top of the stairs, there is a square table holding an iron dagger and a wooden door leading outside, where Illia's mother [Silvia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia) waits to perform her dark ritual.

### Exterior: Top of the Tower
Exiting through the door leads onto a walkway that passes under an arch into a courtyard behind the tower. The area outside the tower is full of hagraven totems, including various animal heads on stakes, mammoth tusks, taproots in netting, and two sets of large ribs with animal skulls and large antlers attached atop Silvia's tent and a nearby rock. Silvia is a fairly adept caster, and carries the unique [Staff of Hag's Wrath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Hag%27s_Wrath). Once she has been dealt with, make sure to loot her body. There is a campfire next to a table holding a copy of the Destruction skill book *[Horror of Castle Xyr](Skyrim_Horror_of_Castle_Xyr.md)*, two random poisons, a leveled filled soul gem, two samples of [deathbell](Skyrim_Deathbell.md), and a sprig of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). Inside the tent is an unlocked, leveled boss chest. At the rear of the courtyard is an arch blocked by spears that are lowered by activating a lever to the left, while to the north are stairs leading up to the top of the surrounding walls and two towers, one of which is ruined.

Passing through the arch previously blocked by spears and following the path to the east will bring you to the [Ruins of Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund). If you take the path west from the gate, you will pass a fallen tree next to a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) skill book *[Proper Lock Design and Construction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Proper_Lock_Design)* on the way to the collapsed entrance of an unmarked Nordic barrow. A [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md) has taken up residence here, and guards an unlocked chest and a couple [burial urns](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md).

## Notes
- The largest concentration of scaly pholiota in the game can be found here.
- If you wish to explore the tower more thoroughly while progressing with Illia, she tends to wait for you if you are a certain distance away, reducing the worry of her charging into battle alone.
- There is an inaccessible room in the center of the top of the tower, right before exiting to face Silvia. The room contains a bookshelf holding magicka potions.
- If you climb the mountain to reach the courtyard behind the tower, Silvia will not appear unless you first go in to talk to Illia to start the quest [Repentance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance). Then you can climb around back to kill Silvia.

## Bugs
- Climbing the mountain and killing the enemies at the top of the tower instead of entering the tower's lower entrance may result in being unable to earn Illia as a follower. Instead, she will speak to thin air and permanently stand still, awaiting a response from the recently slain hag. If you sit in the chair at the top of the tower, she will tell her mother that she is going to kill her, then walk back inside.
- Everything at the top of the tower may respawn despite the dungeon itself being classed as a "no reset zone".
