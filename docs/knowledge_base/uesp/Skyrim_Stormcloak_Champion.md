The Stormcloak Champion is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior acting as the Champion of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).

If you steal [Klija's Snow Bear Pelts](Skyrim_Klija%27s_Snow_Bear_Pelts.md) from [Klija Wolf-Claw](Skyrim_Klija_Wolf-Claw.md) but decide to side with the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) during the related quest, the Champion will appear at the battle location on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) alongside several [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md). You will need to kill him and loot everything from his corpse in order to complete the quest. You can speak to him briefly before the battle commences.

The Champion wears [Storm-Bear armor](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Gauntlets.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Helmet.md), and in combat he wields [Tsun's Judgement](Skyrim_Tsun%27s_Judgement.md). He also carries the [Storm-Bear sword](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Sword.md) and [shield](Skyrim_Storm-Bear_Shield.md).
