# Afterdark Society

The Afterdark Society is a mysterious eerie faction of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) widely rumored about in Tamriel during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389) to [3E 399](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_399), comprised of "nonhuman heathen" who are said to gather at night in front of the temples of various cities.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Aren Rumor Dia-1)</sup> They are referred to as a religious organization, insomuch as the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) is a "religious organization."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE5-2)</sup>

One businessman hired some swordsmen to escort a relative of theirs elsewhere, but before they could begin the mission, the swordsmen were found "chopped into [Goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) Snacks" by the Afterdark Society.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Aren Quest Dialogue-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The Afterdark Society possibly refers to the enemies and monsters that can attack the player in settlements after dark in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)*.
