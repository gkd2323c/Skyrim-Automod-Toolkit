[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-npc-Aeri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aeri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aeri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aeri.jpg) Aeri **Aeri** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) who lives in [Aeri's House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aeri%27s_House) at [Anga's Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anga%27s_Mill). She [pays](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) for chopped firewood, and will also sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed. If her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) is raised high enough, she becomes a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) and will also allow you to cut your own lumber for free.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd), and a [cowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cowl). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Aeri%27s_House) to her house as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

She wakes up at 7am and goes outside, sits on the bench by her house and eats breakfast until 8am. She then goes to work at the mill until 7pm. At 7pm she eats dinner sitting on the same bench for one hour, then moves to her house and goes to sleep at midnight.

## Dialogue
Her potential greetings:

*"I run the mill, so I'm a busy woman. What is it?"* *"Always something going wrong. Mill needs more lumber or the saw needs sharpening."* *"A good lumberjack is quiet, hard-working, and honest."* *"Here for work? Get an axe and bring me all the wood you can chop."* She can be asked about the mill:

**So you're in charge of Anga's Mill?** *"That I am. My father left it to me after he died in that [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) against the Elves. I don't care what's going on in the world. [Rebellion](Skyrim_Civil_War.md). [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) awakening. The mill is enough trouble for me to worry about."*
