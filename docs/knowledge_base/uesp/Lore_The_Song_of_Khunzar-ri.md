The Song of Khunzar-ri *(A Mythic Hero's Song)*
---

- When the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) were only sixteen tribes,
- A hero ambled forth, all tall and tough and wise,
- How sleek he is! How fragrant! A five-claw? No, a ten!
- He wrestles [giants](Lore_Giant.md) just for fun and charges into [senche's](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) den!

- CHORUS
- Who is he? [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md)! Khunzar-ri!
- Sing it loud, sing it clear. Fighter, lover, poet, thief.
- He's one [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) to cheer!
- Who is he? Khunzar-ri! Khunzar-ri!
- Sing it proud, sing it strong! Brave and sly, rarely shy.
- He never steers you wrong!

- No dungeon cage can hold him, no lock can bar his way,
- Some stories claim that he's a [god](Lore_Gods.md), or so they say,
- He dragged the light from [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) down to the [growing fields](Lore_Moon_Sugar_Cane.md),
- And now we have [moon-sugar](Lore_Moon-sugar.md) and all the good it yields.
