[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Poor_Fishwife.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Poor_Fishwife.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Poor_Fishwife.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Poor_Fishwife.jpg) Poor Fishwife The **Poor Fishwife** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who may be found at [Riften's](Skyrim_Riften.md) docks. She is a possible target in the recurring [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md).

The Fishwife wears [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md) and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat.
