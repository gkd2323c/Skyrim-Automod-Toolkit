The All Flags Navy Started Here *A plaque immortalizing the first fourteen ships of the All Flags Navy*
---
The [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) started here, as the first ships arrived to help protect the [island](Lore_High_Isle.md) while the infrastructure was under construction. Before the shipyards could turn out new vessels, the peoples of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) sent their best ships to patrol the waters around High Isle. They were ships of the line from established navies, merchant vessels, freebooters, and mercenaries, all ready to defend the effort and eventually take the war directly to the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).

These were the initial fourteen ships that arrived to form the basis of the All Flags Navy. Long may they be remembered!

The Relentless, [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md), Baron-Admiral [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md).
Anvil's Song. Colovian. Baron-Captain Nolus Falvius.
The Ruby Trident. Colovian. Captain Haderus Viria.

The Pristine Halberd. Breton. Captain <scratched out>.
[Onsi](Lore_Onsi.md)'s Oath. [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md). Captain Afsarah.

The Wind-Like-Anger. [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md). Captain Hunileen.
Moonchaser. [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Captain Kalgnar.
Wandering Albatross. Knightly Order. Captain-Lord Nepas Lan.

The Black Gull. Freebooter. Captain Shoshara.
