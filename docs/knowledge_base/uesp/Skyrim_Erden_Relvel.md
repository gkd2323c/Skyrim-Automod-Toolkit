[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-npc-Erden_Relvel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Erden_Relvel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erden_Relvel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erden_Relvel.jpg) Erden Relvel **Erden Relvel** is an antagonist in the questline of [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md). He aims to continue spreading the destructive influence of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) as a puppet of the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur), and must be stopped.

He will appear at Tel Mithryn graveyard after the completion of [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest).md) is complete, and once you have solved the [puzzle of the armory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Arthamis%27_Note) in [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) and read Erden's [challenge to you](Skyrim_Erden_Relvel%27s_Note.md). He summons several [Ash Zombies](Skyrim_Ash_Zombie.md) to aid him in combat.

He wears the Mask of Dagoth Ur and the [Robe of the Lich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Robe_of_the_Lich). In his inventory is the final Fahlbtharz Forge Gem.
