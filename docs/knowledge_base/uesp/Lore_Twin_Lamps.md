# Twin Lamps

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/MW-npc-Ilmeni_Dren.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Ilmeni_Dren.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Ilmeni_Dren.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Ilmeni_Dren.jpg) Ilmeni Dren [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/LG-cardart-Twin_Lamps_Consul.png/200px-LG-cardart-Twin_Lamps_Consul.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Twin_Lamps_Consul.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Twin_Lamps_Consul.png) Twin Lamps consul [Im-Kilaya](Lore_Im-Kilaya.md) The Twin Lamps was an underground secret society of abolitionists in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) dedicated to rescuing and aiding escaped slaves. The group helped free slaves, primarily [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), and transported them out of Morrowind to safety.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MGD-1)</sup> They commonly used the phrase: *"Have you seen the Twin Lamps?"* to recognize fellow members, the answer required being: *"They light the way to freedom."*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/MW-quest-Alms_from_the_Argonian_Mission.jpg/200px-MW-quest-Alms_from_the_Argonian_Mission.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Alms_from_the_Argonian_Mission.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-quest-Alms_from_the_Argonian_Mission.jpg) Argonian Mission headquarters in Ebonheart [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/ON-quest-The_Double_Edge_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Double_Edge_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Double_Edge_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Double_Edge_02.jpg) Two Lanterns used by Twin Lamps predecessors The group derives its name from the practice of hanging two lanterns in the window of a farm to mark it as a safe house for those fleeing slavery. This tradition can be traced back to the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the group was headed by Ilmeni Dren, the daughter of Duke [Vedam Dren](Lore_Vedam_Dren.md). They used the Argonian Mission in [Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md) as a base of operations on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Unsurprisingly given the group's activities, the Twin Lamps had a rivalry at that time with [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Gentleman [Jim Stacey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jim_Stacey) requested an [adventurer](Lore_Nerevarine.md) to send a slaver's ring to Ilmeni Dren of the Twin Lamps in the name of [Bal Molagmer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bal_Molagmer) as a symbol of good faith and in hopes she would make a good use of it. It is unclear whether the ring was ultimately delivered or not.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ilmeni-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GJS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BR-7)</sup>
