# Ice Wraith


An account from the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) hunter, [Grundvik Cold-Fist](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Kyne%27s_Challenge__A_Hunter%27s_Companion_Characters_and_Summary), describes them as "a snaking skeleton of blue flame", "a collection of ice bones bound together by [nature magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nature_Magic), a sharp-ridged spine finishing at a malcontented jaw of bone and sharp teeth". These creatures can pass through their prey's bodies and afflict them with an infection.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KCAHC-3)</sup> They can also burrow into the ground and burst back up.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONWraith-4)</sup> When an Ice Wraith is killed, its body will combust into large spikes of ice that can cut the skin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KCAHC-3)</sup>

## Culture and Uses
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-concept-6877528019.jpg/200px-SR-concept-6877528019.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877528019.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-concept-6877528019.jpg) Concept art [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Ice_Wraith_Teeth.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Ice_Wraith_Teeth.png) Ice Wraith teeth As per Nordic custom, young men are sent into the wilderness to hunt ice wraiths as a rite of passage for citizenship.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1E-6)</sup> A known place of interest is [Serpentstone Isle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Serpentstone_Isle) northeast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md), where ice wraiths gathered around [the Serpent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Serpent), one of Skyrim's legendary [standing stones](Lore_Standing_Stones.md). When the [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md) were formed in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), they used this old Nordic tradition as a test for new recruits.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRGalmar-7)</sup>

An Ice Wraith can be harvested for quality [alchemy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alchemy) ingredients. Their [teeth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ice_Wraith_Teeth) are a prized ingredient<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HBIW-1)</sup> for a weakness to frost or fire poison, but can also grant invisibility when mixed with something like [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nirnroot) or [wings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Luna_Moth_Wing) of a [Luna Moth](Lore_Luna_Moth.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRAlchemy-8)</sup> The teeth can also be used to create fishing hooks.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

White Ice is another ingredient that can be harvested from Ice Wraiths or any magical species related to the cold. Its texture is flaked and should fluff without melting. Among its effects, white ice can keep diseases at bay and boost the consumer's agility.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AARTBOM-10)</sup>

## Notes
- The [shouts](Lore_Thu%27um.md) of [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) can summon [elemental wyrms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wyrm) that are similar to Ice Wraiths in appearance and movement.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONWyrm-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONStorm-12)</sup>
- When developing ice wraiths, the *Skyrim* developers didn't really know what an ice wraith looked like and didn't want to do a typical ghost-like specter. They worked with one of the concept artists who envisioned a serpentine crystal-and-ice creature.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Bt W-13)</sup>
