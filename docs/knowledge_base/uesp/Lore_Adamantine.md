# Adamantium

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/TR-ing-Adamantium_Ore.jpg/200px-TR-ing-Adamantium_Ore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-ing-Adamantium_Ore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-ing-Adamantium_Ore.jpg) A shard of adamantium ore Adamantium (also called **Adamantine**) is a rare and ageless<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> metal used for making valuable armor and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md). It is typically seen in a lustrous silvery color dubbed "Adamantine Silver",<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ASDye-2)</sup> though adamantium equipment can also have a stone blue hue<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> or a dark grey coloration.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup> The ore is very heavy and can be used in alchemy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MW-5)</sup> Adamantium armor can appear as either a high-quality heavy weight armor or medium weight armor. It is above the quality of [Mithril](Lore_Mithril.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup> or [Orichalcum](Lore_Orichalcum.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MW-5)</sup> but is inferior to [Stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MW-5)</sup> and [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup> The armor has a high potential for enchantment.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MW-5)</sup> Adamantium weapons can harm magical creatures that resist normal weapons.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup>

The oldest structure on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), the [Adamantine Tower](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md), was created by the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) using the material around [ME 2500](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_2500),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1-6)</sup> and it was intended to be a place for discussing the creation of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE3-7)</sup> Known for using the techniques of both [men](Lore_Man.md) and [mer](Lore_Mer.md) in crafting her exceptional wares, the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [Asterie Bedel](Lore_Asterie_Bedel.md) created the legendary claymore [Chrysamere](Lore_Chrysamere.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), which is one of the oldest mortal-made objects forged using Adamantium.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LAIA-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-AR.2FDF-9)</sup> The [Staff of Towers](Lore_Staff_of_Towers.md) which was constructed during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), features an adamantine shard for the Adamantine Tower for one of its eight segments.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOAO-10)</sup> The [Adamantium Helm of Tohan](Lore_Adamantium_Helm_of_Tohan.md) is an ancient artifact of obscure origins with immensely high potential for enchantment.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWHT-11)</sup>

Residing in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) is the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) Indalen family, who have been adamantium crafters for many generations. There are small deposits of Adamantium that can be found in the ruins of [Old Mournhold](Lore_Old_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWBI-12)</sup> The statue of [Ephen](Lore_Ephen.md) in his tomb underneath Mournhold depicts him wielding a hammer and poised on an adamantium anvil.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TTBVIII-13)</sup>

## Notes
- Differing in only one letter, Adamantine is not to be confused with [Adamantite](Lore_Adamantite.md), a widely available green material used to forge arms and armaments in [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) style during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOAdamantite-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOCM1-15)</sup>
- Adamantine is also not to be confused with [Dwarven Adamant](Lore_Dwarven_Adamant.md), which is a white-hued alloy of ores of unknown origin, believed to be the most durable material ever made. It is among the rarest materials on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ADW-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ADH-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ADS-18)</sup>
