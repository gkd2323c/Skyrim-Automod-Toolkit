[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-item-Hunting_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Hunting_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hunting_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hunting_Bow.jpg) A hunting bow [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-item-Bow_of_the_Hunt.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bow_of_the_Hunt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bow_of_the_Hunt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bow_of_the_Hunt.jpg) The Bow of the Hunt at its initial resting place The Bow of the Hunt is a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) found on an altar, next to a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*, inside [Clearspring Cave](Skyrim_Clearspring_Cave.md). This bow has a unique enchantment that does an additional twenty points of [animal damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Damage). [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Bow of the Hunt requires one [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) as well as the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the benefits of tempering being doubled if the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk is also unlocked.
