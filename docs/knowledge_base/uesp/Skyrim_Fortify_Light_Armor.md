*Alchemy description:* **Increases Light Armor skill by <mag> points for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases Light Armor skill by <mag> points.**
**Fortify Light Armor** increases your [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill](Skyrim_Skills.md).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Light Armor if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor and Necklaces *[of the Squire](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Light_Armor)* - Includes the enchantments *of the Minor Squire*, *of the Major Squire*, *of the Noble Squire*, *of the Extreme Squire*, *of the Eminent Squire*, and *of the Peerless Squire.*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Viriya's Charm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya%27s_Charm)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

## Notes
- Fortify Light Armor potions crafted via alchemy always last 60 seconds; this cannot be lengthened with Alchemy perks, skill level, and/or Fortify Alchemy enchanted apparel worn. What is increased is how much your Light Armor skill level is raised for that duration.
- Heavy Armor pieces (such as Iron Gauntlets) can be enchanted with the Fortify Light Armor effect, even though they derive no benefit from this.

- Dragonscale Armor *of the Peerless Knight*
- Elven Gilded Armor *of the Eminent Knight*
- Elven Gilded Armor *of the Noble Knight*
