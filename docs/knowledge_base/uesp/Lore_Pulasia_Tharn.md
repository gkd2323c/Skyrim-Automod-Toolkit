[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/LG-cardart-Abnur_Tharn.png/200px-LG-cardart-Abnur_Tharn.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Abnur_Tharn.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Abnur_Tharn.png) Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel Abnur Tharn, her husband [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/LG-cardart-Clivia_Tharn.png/200px-LG-cardart-Clivia_Tharn.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Clivia_Tharn.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Clivia_Tharn.png) Empress Regent Clivia Tharn, her daughter Pulasia Tharn was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the royal [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), through her marriage to the Chancellor of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md). She was his seventh wife,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> and was the mother of his sixteenth daughter, [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTFC4-2)</sup> Her daughter became Empress consort married to the two [Emperors](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) of the [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md): [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md) of the [Longhouse Dynasty](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) and [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md), the leader of the [Colovian Revolt](Lore_Colovian_Revolt.md), who overthrew the dynasty of Leovic. Following the disappearance of Varen, her daughter Clivia became Empress Regent.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> The fate of Pulasia following the events of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) is unknown.
