Mannimarco's Notes **by [Mannimarco](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mannimarco)** *A record of Mannimarco's actions in Eastern Solstice*
---
[First Entry]
The Reverse Planemeld initiated by my faithful [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md) proceeds with maddening slowness. The instructions I left for bringing my remains here and resuming my work were perfectly clear, but my appointed executor failed to deal the guilds a mortal blow. Their subsequent counterattack slowed his work. Without those delays, I might be writing these entries with my own hand instead of with my unfortunate nephew's.

As much as I rue the delay, I suppose whatever remains of Wormblood regrets it more. This arrangement proved rather more costly for him than he likely expected. My [soul](Lore_Soul.md) was just too much, and his body could not contain both he and I. Guess who didn't survive the process.

[Second Entry]
At last, some progress! That foolish Golden Knight of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) blundered into my power at the shrine of Mortha Khevur. My foes may have succeeded in bringing down the wall Wormblood raised, but they unwittingly handed me the tool I need to escape both this body prison and the ancient barriers ringing [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md). With [Darien Gautier](Lore_Darien_Gautier.md)'s Light, I can create a new Gift of Death to replace the one Wormblood lost. It is only a matter of time and patience.

[Third Entry]
Another delay. My minions report that the accursed Stirk Fellowship managed to destroy the shackle at Gristmung Hold. At this pace, they may very well be at Mor Naril's gates before the Reverse Planemeld is finished or a new Gift of Death is ready!

Fortunately, Wormblood displayed rather more foresight in this case than I have come to expect from him. His experiments with devices to capture souls showed promise, so he ordered the construction of improved soul reapers to serve as both weapons and defenses for our holdings in eastern Solstice. With my suggested improvements, these soul flayers will be even more powerful than Wormblood imagined. All we need do is delay our foes until I finish the fabrication of a new Gift of Death, and then it will not matter where they are or what they do. With a mere thought, I will be able to harvest their souls and put their mortal shells to whatever use I might contemplate.

In the meantime, I have decided to restore my own shell to life. I may need my full powers to deal with [Vanus](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) and whatever fools he has rounded up to hinder me, and that will require my original body.
