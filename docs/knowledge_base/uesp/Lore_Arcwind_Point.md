[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg/200px-SR-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg) Arcwind Point circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/ON-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg/200px-ON-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Arcwind_Point.jpg) Arcwind Point circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Arcwind Point is a ruined [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) structure located at the southern edge of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), in an isolated valley in the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup>

The location actually consists of several separate ruins. The most notable element is the double arch in the center of the valley. To the north is a ruined structure and a tower. To the southeast, a barrow, another ruin, and a few more arches can be found.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup>

A [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md) dedicated to body of Svolo, who possessed strength to kill a Dragon but not stamina to kill many, stands just near the main arch.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruin was overrun by the [Worm Cultists](Lore_Worm_Cult.md). Thallik Wormfather, the leader of the cult in [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), performed a necromantic ritual on the ghost of the giant [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md) in the barrow. The ritual was interrupted by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who killed Thallik, but that didn't stop him from merging his soul with Sinmur's, making the giant stronger.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Arcwind Point was infested with [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) and [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md). After the return of the [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), the ruins served as one of their lairs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>
