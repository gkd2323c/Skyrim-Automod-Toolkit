Elder Scroll *The contents of one the mysterious Elder Scrolls*
---
*This is an article about a specific Elder Scroll. For information on Elder Scrolls in general, see [Lore:Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md).*

This is the Elder Scroll stolen in the quest [The Ultimate Heist](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:The_Ultimate_Heist). Its symbols are identical to the [Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Thief) birthsign.

The background art of the [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) is a star map of Earth's southern polar region, containing within it most of the southern constellations. The more famous ones are Scorpius (tail part found at the left-hand side), Crux (found at the lower center of the Scroll), and Eridanus (found at the top left-hand side, with Achernar as the large patch).
