[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Giants' Gap is a region in the central area of [The Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It makes up the open area south of the [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md) and the [Northern Coast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Northern_Coast), and is surrounded on many sides by the [Pale Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pale_Mountains).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> The **Yorgrim Forest** is found in the southeastern part of the Gap.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> The Gap blends into the [White River Valley](Lore_Tundra_Plain.md) of Whiterun.

[Volunruud](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Volunruud) and [Heljarchen](Lore_Heljarchen.md) are found close to it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho JG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HHC-3)</sup>

## History
A battle of the Civil War in the 4th Era was fought here; it started as an Imperial ambush on an unsuspecting group of Stormcloaks, and the Stormcloaks were able to form a shield-wall and hold off the Imperial attack for hours.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SSS-4)</sup>
