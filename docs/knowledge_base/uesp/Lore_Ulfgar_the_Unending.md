Ulfgar the Unending (or, alternatively, **the Unyielding**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:985</sup> was an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) warrior from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) or possibly even the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). By his own personal account he and four close companions fought alongside the Atmoran legend [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) as members of the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md). Many centuries later he and his four companions embarked on a quest to find [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). However, his companions [Nikulas](Lore_Nikulas.md), [Erlendr](Lore_Erlendr.md), and [Hunroor](Lore_Hunroor.md) were betrayed by the wizard [Grimkell](Lore_Grimkell.md), who turned them into stone pillars at Brodir Grove on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were avenged by Ulfgar, who killed the wizard. Five hundred years later, Ulfgar was somehow still alive, and still searching for Sovngarde. In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), he met the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), who agreed to help him find the Nordic paradise. Upon investigating, the Nerevarine informed the ancient Nord that the only way to reach Sovngarde is to die an honorable death in combat. Ulfgar challenged the Nerevarine, hoping to meet such a fate. The Nerevarine accepted his challenge, and the two engaged in mortal combat. In the end, a fourth pillar appeared at Brodir Grove,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon2-3)</sup> and Ulfgar met his old friends in Sovngarde.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-UTU-5)</sup>

## Notes
- While Ulfgar states that the events that extended his lifespan occurred 500 years prior to his meeting the Nerevarine, in further dialogue he states that he and his Companions fought under [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) as members of the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Companions.md). This would place Ulfgar's age far higher than previously stated, and could imply that he has lost track of time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon2-3)</sup>
