Vvardenfell Flora and Fauna **by [Tilenra Sildreth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Tilenra_Sildreth), Telvanni Naturalist** *An examination of Vvardenfell's Second Era flora and fauna*
---
[![ON-concept-MW Ashlands.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/ON-concept-MW_Ashlands.jpg/600px-ON-concept-MW_Ashlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Ashlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Ashlands.jpg)
- The [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) landscape undergoes a constant cycle of death and rebirth. [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md)'s lava flows, eruptions and ash-fall cause die-back in the deciduous woodlands and mushroom jungles, violent upheaval alters the landscape and buries (or exposes!) the fallen ruins of ancient civilizations. But the island expands as lava hardens, and new plants and fungi emerge from the cooling ashes amongst the formations of igneous rock.

[![ON-concept-MW Landscape.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/ON-concept-MW_Landscape.jpg/600px-ON-concept-MW_Landscape.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Landscape.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Landscape.jpg) [![ON-concept-MW Shroom Beetle.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/ON-concept-MW_Shroom_Beetle.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Shroom_Beetle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Shroom_Beetle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Shroom_Beetle.jpg)
- Plant life flourishes in the rich soil fertilized by volcanic activity, and so Vvardenfell is home to a diverse set of biomes.

- Fungi quickly absorb rich nutrients from the volcanic soil and grow to enormous proportions to dominate the landscape. The large **[Ink-Cap Mushrooms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ink-Cap_Mushroom)** along the **[Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md)** thrive on the detritus of decaying swamp life, while smaller fungus varieties cover the rocks, trees and even some animals, such as the fungus-encrusted **[Shroom Beetle](Lore_Shroom_Beetle.md)**. Adorable!

- [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md), in the southwest and inland from the **Bitter Coast**, is still forested, though ash from Red Mountain eruptions threatens to denude the slopes.

[![ON-concept-MW Cliff Strider.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/ON-concept-MW_Cliff_Strider.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Cliff_Strider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Cliff_Strider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Cliff_Strider.jpg)
- Though barren, The **[Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md)** host a remarkable number of species. **[Ash Hoppers](Lore_Ash_Hopper.md)** skitter through the dry ash dunes and giant **[Xylaria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giant_Xylaria)** fungi extend their tendrils into the sky. Travelers beware! Hungry, reptilian **[Cliff Striders](Lore_Cliff_Strider.md)** lurk atop the crags.

- **[Fetcherflies](Lore_Fetcherfly.md)** build their hives where they can tap volcanic heat to gestate their eggs. When they hatch, the queen taps a source of magma to animate the nest, and it transforms into a walking **[Hive Golem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hive_Golem)** that relocates the swarm to new surroundings.

[![ON-concept-MW Geysers.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg/600px-ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Geysers.jpg) [![ON-concept-MW Vvardvark.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Vvardvark.jpg)
- **[Nix-Oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md)** roam the **[Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md)** in the northeastern part of the island, and though generally considered to be mild-mannered, they will not hesitate to defend their territory.

- Two-legged **[Vvardvarks](Lore_Vvardvark.md)** romp through the grasses and feed on small bugs. Some people keep these delightful creatures as pets, despite the smell.

[![ON-concept-MW Hunger.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/ON-concept-MW_Hunger.jpg/200px-ON-concept-MW_Hunger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Hunger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-MW_Hunger.jpg)
- Ancestral Tombs dot the landscape, and powerful [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) known as **[Hunger](Lore_Hunger.md)**, summoned as guardians, often lurk within.

- The largest mushrooms in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) appear in **[Zafirbel Bay](Lore_Zafirbel_Bay.md)**. This majestic species is cultivated by [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) wizards and take a wizard's lifetime (a thousand years) to grow. These fungus forests have a special ecology of their own—but that's a subject for another book.
