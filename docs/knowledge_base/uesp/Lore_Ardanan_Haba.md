Ardanan Haba was an Emperor of the [Yokudan Empire](Lore_Yokudan_Empire.md). Little is known of his reign and life, except that he held dominion over Yokuda several centuries before [1E 660](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_660). Certain scholars suggest that under his rule, different dissident factions left the empire and established themselves on the eastern islands. However, alternative accounts trace this migration to later Yokudan leaders, specifically during the times of [Mansel Sesnit](Lore_Mansel_Sesnit.md)'s diplomatic purges and [Randic Torn](Lore_Randic_Torn.md)'s controversial policies. A subset of scholars goes so far as to speculate that these exiled groups might have been [Sinistral Mer](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SH-1)</sup>
