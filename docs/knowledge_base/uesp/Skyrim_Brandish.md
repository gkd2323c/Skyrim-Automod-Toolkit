[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/SR-npc-Brandish.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Brandish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Brandish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Brandish.jpg) Brandish **Brandish** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md) who resides in the library of [Fort Neugrad](Skyrim_Fort_Neugrad.md) while it is occupied by [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). Because of his involvement outlined in the [Bandit Chief's journal](Skyrim_Bandit%27s_Journal_(Fort_Neugrad).md), he is in the Fort Neugrad library most of the time. Otherwise, he roams outside near the wagon.

He wears a set of [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000c5d12), and carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a leveled [health potion](Skyrim_Potions.md), and a leveled amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).

## Notes
- A lot of internal filenames refer to a "Brandish" in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), but these files have nothing to do with this NPC. The actual NPC that these files refer to is "Brandish Begin-Again", which was an earlier iteration/name for Riften marketplace vendor [Brand-Shei](Skyrim_Brand-Shei.md). For example, his [merchant chest](Skyrim_Containers.md#Merchant_Chests) and [service faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Services Riften Plaza Brandish), as well as several object references in Riften, are named after "Brandish" but assigned to, and used by, Brand-Shei.
- If you lockpick the door to the Library, but do not kill him after it, he may wander out.
