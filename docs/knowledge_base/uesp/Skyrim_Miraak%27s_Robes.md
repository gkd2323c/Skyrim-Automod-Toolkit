[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-item-Miraak%27s_Robes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak%27s_Robes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Robes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Robes.jpg) Miraak's Robes Miraak's Robes are a set of robes worn by [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md). They can be obtained after defeating Miraak during [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md). Its enchantment gives you fifteen percent [spell absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md) and has a chance to deal [poison damage](Skyrim_Poison_Damage.md) to an attacker when the wearer is hit. Equipping [Miraak's Boots](Skyrim_Miraak%27s_Boots.md) and/or [Miraak's Gloves](Skyrim_Miraak%27s_Gloves.md) will increase the spell absorption enchantment by 5% each, for a total of 25% spell absorption.

## The Tentacle Explosion
Whenever the wearer of Miraak's Robes is hit by any physical hits, magical attacks (including shouts) or traps, there is a small chance that tentacles will spawn out of the ground and surround the wearer. There is a very high chance those tentacles will explode. When they do, they stagger and deal 100 [poison damage](Skyrim_Poison_Damage.md) to everything in their Ao E radius which is about the same as a basic cloak spell.

Even though the Tentacle Explosion's damage is classified as poison damage, it is not affected by [Weakness to Poison](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Poison.md), but by [Weakness to Magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md).

## Notes
- With this item and either Miraak's boots or gloves equipped, the [Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) power and the [Atronach](Skyrim_Atronach_(perk).md) [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [perk](Skyrim_Perks.md), it is possible to achieve 100% spell absorption. The Tentacle Explosion will still trigger from spells and spell effects even if you have 100% spell absorption.
- A separate version of the robes ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 039151) with a similar enchantment to the [Archmage's Robes](Skyrim_Archmage%27s_Robes.md) is worn by Miraak during the first [Black Book cutscene](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md#Waking_Dreams). It is unobtainable without the use of glitches.

## Bugs
- When wearing these robes, there is a chance that the tentacle explosion will occur if you transform into a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>
- When wearing these robes, there is a chance that the tentacle explosion will occur if you use the lesser power [Bardic Knowledge](Skyrim_Bardic_Knowledge.md).<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
