- *A blast of flame that sets the target on fire, doing 4 damage per second for 15 seconds.*

**Ignite** is an apprentice level destruction spell that deals [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) and sets targets on fire, dealing 4 damage per second over 15 seconds, for a total of 60 damage, followed by burning for 30% of spell damage initially, decreasing linearly for 1 second.

## Effects
- [Ignite](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 4 pts for 15 secs
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Intense Flames Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 25 secs; if the [Intense Flames](Skyrim_Intense_Flames.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md), raises damage to 5 at first rank or 6 at second rank
- [Aspect of Terror](Skyrim_Aspect_of_Terror.md), raises damage by 10 pts (15 pts if both ranks of [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) are unlocked beforehand).

## Notes
- Ignite is essentially just a much more powerful version of [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md). It actually does as much damage as the expert-level [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md) spell (at a much lower cost), though spread out over 15 seconds. It's even cheaper to cast than Firebolt. The only downside is you need to be wearing [Ahzidal's Ring of Arcana](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Ring_of_Arcana.md) to use it.
- Like most Fire spells, it can be used to set off [Fire Runes](Skyrim_Fire_Rune.md) or [oil slicks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks) from a safe distance.
- Ignite stacks with itself should you cast it multiple times.
- The 10/15-point increase from [Aspect of Terror](Skyrim_Aspect_of_Terror.md) and both ranks of [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) applies to the per second damage, not the overall damage, resulting in a total of 150/225 points of bonus damage.
