The Scaleborn are a product line of artificial [draconic](Lore_Dragon.md) creatures commissioned in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by Queen [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md) of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) for use as mounts in the newly reformed [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SW-2)</sup> They were [magically](Lore_Magic.md) created with "draconic fleshweave" by the [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) Magister [Varkenel](Lore_Varkenel.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SW-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SS-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SB-4)</sup> most famous for his creation of [vvardvarks](Lore_Vvardvark.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LA-5)</sup>

Varkenel was previously commissioned to create a draconic creature, though the result was considered less than satisfactory.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PCL-6)</sup> However, he was able to incorporate draconic fleshweave in the creation of the Scaleborn due to his commissioners in Elsweyr having the means to scavenge authentic draconic materials.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SS-3)</sup> Varknel considered it a rare pleasure to collaborate with someone outside House Telvanni. He was especially pleased with his cooperation with the Queen of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) and looked forward to accepting her offer of hospitality.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SW-2)</sup>

He operated on a timeline he considered reasonable and manageable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> The Scaleborn creatures he created resembled [senche-raht](Lore_Senche-raht.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SS-3)</sup> [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SW-2)</sup> [rams](Lore_Ram.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> and [bears](Lore_Bear.md), the latter being the fourth form in the series. While bears were not native to Elsweyr, he suggested them as they had proven to be a popular form in his previous works.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SB-4)</sup>
