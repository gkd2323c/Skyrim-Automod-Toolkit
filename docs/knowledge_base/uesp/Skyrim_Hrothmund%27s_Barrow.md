[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg) Hrothmund's Barrow **Hrothmund's Barrow** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southwest of [Benkongerike](Skyrim_Benkongerike.md).

It contains the axe once wielded by [Hrothmund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hrothmund_the_Red). During the quest [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md), after you have helped a group of [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) clear [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) of [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md), you are sent here to witness a ritual confirming the hall's rightful leader.

## Related Quests
- **[Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md)**: Help [Bujold the Unworthy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bujold_the_Unworthy) retake [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow_02.jpg) Hrothmund's Bane In the open area in front of the entrance is a [pattern of large stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Hrothmund%27s_Bane). To the right of the entrance is an unlit brazier. [Frost trolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll) may be encountered nearby.

### Hrothmund's Barrow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Hrothmund%27s_Barrow.jpg) The large flooded chamber [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hrothmund%27s_Axe.jpg) Hrothmund's Axe The entrance leads into a natural cave with a [gold ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gold_Ore_Vein) and three patches of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) fungus. A short flight of stairs ascends to the north along the west wall. At the top of the stairs, a set of iron doors is on the northeast wall, while a concealed door on the southeast wall is opened by the pull chain to the left. Beyond the concealed door is a damp twisting tunnel ending at a second concealed door opened by the pull chain to the right. Beyond this door is a small shallowly flooded chamber containing a [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) and a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), with a [burial urn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Burial_Urn), a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), three loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), a [flawless garnet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Garnet), an [amethyst](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst), and a [flawless emerald](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Emerald) next to the chest.

Returning to the area outside the first concealed door, beyond the set of iron doors is a flight of stairs descending into a large flooded chamber. As you descend the stairs, the water will drain to reveal two paths on either side of a large stone sculpture. The paths rejoin and continue to the far end of the room, where a raised platform features a stone holding Hrothmund's Axe. Two chests flank the alcove with the stone.

## Notes
- Southwest of the entrance at an [unmarked location](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Reaver.27s_End) is an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox).
- Northwest of the entrance are two [sapphire geodes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_Geode). Due north of the second sapphire geode above a waterfall is a chest and a random [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md).
- Southeast of the entrance are two sapphire geodes, a silver ore vein, and a [corundum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein).
- Hrothmund's Axe is not an actual weapon, and cannot be obtained by any means.
- [Hrothmund's Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Hrothmund%27s_Barrow) previously appeared in [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon).
