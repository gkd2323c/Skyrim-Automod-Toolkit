*Alchemy description:* **Restore <mag> points of Cold.**
**Restore Cold** reduces your [Cold](Skyrim_Cold.md) level. The effect is moot when [Survival mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is toggled off.

The effect is only available in [food](Skyrim_Food.md) cooked with [Fire Salts](Skyrim_Fire_Salts.md). It is not found in spells, magic items, or ingredients.
