Mercer Frey was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) thief and skilled swordsman who lived during the second and early third centuries of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup> He began his career of thievery as a member of the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), where he learned from Guild Master [Gallus Desidenius](Lore_Gallus_Desidenius.md). His mentor was also secretly a member of the [Nightingale Trinity](Lore_Nightingale_Trinity.md), a group which Mercer was eventually inducted into.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TN-2)</sup> Mercer would go on to betray the Nightingales and [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) by desecrating the [Twilight Sepulcher](Lore_Twilight_Sepulcher.md) and taking the [Skeleton Key](Lore_Skeleton_Key.md) for himself.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GJ-3)</sup> He then used the key to commit crimes against the guild until his activities were discovered by Gallus around [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GJ-3)</sup> In retaliation, Mercer murdered Gallus, framed another Nightingale, [Karliah](Lore_Karliah.md), and took his late mentor's place as Guild Master.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup> He hunted Karliah for over two decades; she, in turn, did her best to undermine him and his allies.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GEC-4)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), with the help of a [new guild member](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), Karliah exposed Mercer, reformed the Nightingale Trinity, and saw Gallus finally avenged when Mercer was killed by the new guildmate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>

Mercer was once the owner of [Chillrend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chillrend), a famed [glass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Malachite) sword.
