[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/ON-place-Coral_Forest.jpg/200px-ON-place-Coral_Forest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Coral_Forest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Coral_Forest.jpg) Coral Forest circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Coral Forest is an iconic landscape on [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), located south of [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md). This place consists of a dense collection of petrified [coral](Lore_Coral.md) which creates a strange, labyrinthine landscape of collar pillars along the island's eastern shore, where [coral crabs](Lore_Coral_Crab.md), [mud hoppers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mud_Hopper) and [reef vipers](Lore_Reef_Viper.md) hatch and thrive.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IV3-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> It is commonly known to be a dangerous place for travelers and adventurers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Tilcalar, the Aldarch of the Monastery of Serene Harmony, secretly became the Earl of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md) and one of the three leaders of the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md). He was instructed by Kinlady Avinisse to activate an [Abyssal Pearl](Lore_Abyssal_Pearl.md) within the Coral Forest, most likely with intentions of creating an Abyssal Geyser near Shimmerene in order to sink Summerset Isle beneath the sea. His plans were foiled when [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) confronted and killed him in the Coral Forest. A [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) named [Valsirenn](Lore_Valsirenn.md) then magically transported the Abyssal Pearl to [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) so that this [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) artifact could be studied further.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
