[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/ON-place-Sadal%27s_Prison.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sadal%27s_Prison.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sadal%27s_Prison.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sadal%27s_Prison.jpg) Sadal's Prison Sadal's Prison is a small pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), created by the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) to imprison [Sadal](Lore_Sadal.md), one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md). It can be entered from the caldera of the [Tormented Spire](Lore_Tormented_Spire.md) in [Varanis](Lore_Varanis.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is a realm of lava and rock, scattered with Daedric ruins.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SFD-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md) attempted to free Sadal from his prison following the death of his son at the hands of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) invaders.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The forces of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) followed him to the Tormented Spire and talked him out of it. A small force including Tanval then entered Sadal's Prison and defeated him, allowing Sadal's [flame atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) guardians to bind him anew. Tanval was slain in the battle after sacrificing himself.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SFD-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Although [Balreth](Lore_Balreth.md) was imprisoned similarly to Sadal on [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md), his prison is on [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), in the volcano's caldera.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TDo B-4)</sup>
