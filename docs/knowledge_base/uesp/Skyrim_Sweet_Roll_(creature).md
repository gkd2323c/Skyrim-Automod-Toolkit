[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-creature-Sweet_Roll.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Sweet_Roll.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sweet_Roll.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sweet_Roll.jpg) Sweet Roll **Sweet Roll** is a trained [fox](Skyrim_Fox.md) found in the forest south-southwest of [Half-Moon Mill](Skyrim_Half-Moon_Mill.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-45707&y=-63812&zoom=17&legacy=true)

You can feed her a [sweet roll](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll.md) to make her your pet. She comes pre-equipped with an [Animal Collar](Skyrim_Animal_Collar.md) but this can be taken off if you desire. She cannot wear an [Animal Pack](Skyrim_Animal_Pack.md). She is essential and will not engage in combat.

Sweet Roll can be summoned using a [teleportation spell](Skyrim_Teleport_Pet__Sweet_Roll.md). She can be instructed to follow you even if you already have another pet following you. You can instruct her to wait at a city, wait at her current location (either temporarily or permanently), wait at your house, or to carry items. She can also be instructed to "go find something valuable", which will cause Sweet Roll to run to the nearest item of loot, such as valuable weapons, armor, jewelry, or certain miscellaneous treasure such as gems.<sup>[*verification needed — need full list*]</sup> If there is no such item nearby, you will receive a message stating that Sweet Roll couldn't find anything. Additionally, Sweet Roll may sometimes be holding a gem, soul gem, jewelry, or miscellaneous treasure when you talk to her, which the player can receive through a separate dialogue option.

The [*Thief's Journal*](Skyrim_Thief%27s_Journal.md) contains background information on Sweet Roll.
