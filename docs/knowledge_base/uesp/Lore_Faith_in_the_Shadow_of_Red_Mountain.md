Faith in the Shadow of Red Mountain **by [Canon Llevule](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Canon_Llevule)** *A Tribunal priest's sermon regarding the Dunmer faith*
---

- As we gather for worship here at the [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md), I look out upon you, my fellow faithful, and I see you are troubled, I see you are anxious, I see you shift in your seats and glance over your shoulders even as I speak with you. And I know why you are troubled and anxious, for who could not be troubled when [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) grumbles and seethes? Who could not be anxious when [Baar Dau](Lore_Baar_Dau.md) trembles in the sky, when unknown [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) appear in unexpected locales, when it is even said that the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) has risen among the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md)?

- But I say to you, fellow [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), have not all these things, and worse, happened before? Did our people not survive them, and become stronger every time? [What says](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md) our [Lord Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md) about such struggles? "Six are the guardians of [Veloth](Lore_Morrowind.md)," [says Vivec](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_6.md), "[three before](Lore_The_Anticipations.md) and they are born again, and they will test you until you have the proper tendencies of a hero."

- And so, once more, we are tested. And so, once more, we shall endure and prove worthy. For the [Three](Lore_Tribunal.md) are with us, and we are strong through our faith in the Three. Through we of the Temple, our Living Gods call upon you, call upon us all to honor them with our efforts.

- We call upon [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) to buttress our structures, physical and moral, building the new and rebuilding the old, and to keep us on the path of righteousness.

- We call upon [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) to plant, harvest, and preserve, to keep the river of trade flowing, to ensure prosperity even through fall of ash and breath of fume.

- We call upon [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) to be wise and forbearing, sharing of insights and [enchantments](Lore_Enchanting.md), for though they are not of the [Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), they are still of our people.

- And we call on you, followers of the Tribunal, to follow also in this time the example of [Saint Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md), whose courage is a watchword and whose endurance is a legend. For though we are anxious and troubled, we are proud and we are fierce, for we are the Dunmer, and for us, trouble is a gift from the gods. For [by trouble are we tested](Lore_House_of_Troubles.md), and by passing the test we know our true worth.

- Praise Vivec! All glory to the Three Most High! We walk with them, in wisdom and in strength, ever and anon.
