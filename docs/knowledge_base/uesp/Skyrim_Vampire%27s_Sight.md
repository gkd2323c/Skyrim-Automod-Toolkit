- *Improved night vision for 60 seconds.*

**Night Eye** is a lesser power possessed by all [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) that allows you to see better in the dark. The **Vampire's Sight** power (00 0c4de1) possessed by stage 3+ [vampires](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) is identical in all but name. There is also a third version possessed by [werewolves](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) (00 0cb10e), also called Night Eye, which is also identical, but unavailable to the player since you can't access your powers while in Beast Form.

## Effects
- [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(effect).md), for 60 sec

## Notes
- The [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spell [Vision of the Tenth Eye](Skyrim_Vision_of_the_Tenth_Eye.md) has the same visual effect, though it requires both hands and 3 seconds to cast, and only lasts for 30 seconds.
- The Vampire Greater Power [Embrace of Shadows](Skyrim_Embrace_of_Shadows.md) also has a Night Eye effect built in. This lasts for 180 seconds, but can only be used once per day.
