[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Elberon.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Elberon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elberon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elberon.jpg) Elberon Elberon Blackthorn is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) wizard who has entered the ruins of [Vardnknd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns) to shut down the malfunctioning Weather Witch within before it can threaten all of Skyrim. He has hired a band of hostile mercenaries to clear the ruins, and has set up camp in Vardnknd Gallery. However, this section of the ruins is currently being subjected to constant lightning strikes, and upon entering Vardnknd Gallery you will witness Elberon being struck by a lightning bolt while sitting at his desk, killing him instantly. His death will trigger the quest [The Dwarven Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dwarven_Crown).

He is wearing [Adept Robes of Illusion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adept_Robes_of_Illusion), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and a strange [Dwarven Crown](Skyrim_Dwarven_Crown.md), the latter of which you will be required to loot as part of the quest The Dwarven Crown. He also carries his [journal](Skyrim_Elberon%27s_Journal.md), which you will need to read as part of the quest [Forgotten Seasons](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons_(quest).md).

After you have recovered all four [Vardnknd Conduit Gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vardnknd_Conduit_Gem), [Elberon's Apprentice](Skyrim_Elberon%27s_Apprentice.md) will appear in Vardnknd Gallery along with several more mercenaries and will attack you on sight.
