Rasman **the Red** was the first Jarl of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md). Although he is considered the first Jarl,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WHCC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WHJL-2)</sup> in reality the first leader and the founder of the city of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) was [Jeek of the River](Lore_Jeek_of_the_River.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GH-3)</sup>

Little is known of Rasman's history. During his time, two special commemorative medallions featuring the image of a horse were crafted. One of these was passed down from Jarl to Jarl, but by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), no Jarl had ever deemed the other worthy of being given to a deserving individual—until the times of [Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), who offered it, along with other memorabilia, to a [worthy individual](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WHCC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WHJL-2)</sup>
