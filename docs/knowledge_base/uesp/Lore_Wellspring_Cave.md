[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/OB-place-Wellspring_Cave.jpg/200px-OB-place-Wellspring_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Wellspring_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Wellspring_Cave.jpg) Wellspring Cave circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Wellspring Cave is a natural cave found on the shores of [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md), in the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The cave is connected to an island containing the **Wellspring Grove**, in the eastern waters of Lake Rumare.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Like various other natural caves in Tamriel, Wellspring Cave is theorized to have served as a capillary for the living body of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), and once pulsed with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) who created it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Wellspring Cave is used by the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) at the [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md). When an associate is promoted to Apprentice, they are tasked with acquiring a staff from the Wellspring Grove. The wood found at the grove is used to craft mage's [staves](Lore_Staves.md), and so several guild mages tend to the cave and grove.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Raminus-3)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) had begun to make their appearance in Cyrodiil, targeting the Mages Guild in various ways. A group of cult members attacked Wellspring Cave, killing the mages that resided there. An [Apprentice](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) of the Mages Guild arrived at Wellspring Cave in time to witness the aftermath and deal with the last of the [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) in order to retrieve wood for a staff.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Afterward, the [Council of Mages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Council_of_Mages) began to investigate the situation.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Raminus-3)</sup>
