[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/AR-place-White_Haven.jpg/200px-AR-place-White_Haven.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-White_Haven.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-White_Haven.jpg) White Haven during the Imperial Simulacrum White Haven is a settlement located on the west-north region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), White Haven an active settlement in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It was ruled by Lady Gwynanna and had a rivalry with Meir Thorvale. It housed the temple of the Brotherhood of Temperance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> At the time, White Haven was neighbored by several settlements, including [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) to the east and [Meir Darguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Meir_Darguard) to the southwest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the settlement remained active. One individual originating from there was Nalcarya of White Haven, an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md) and merchant who, at that time, operated a shop in [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NOWH-3)</sup>
