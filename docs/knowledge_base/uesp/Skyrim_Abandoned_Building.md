[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-place-Abandoned_Building.jpg/200px-SR-place-Abandoned_Building.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Abandoned_Building.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Abandoned_Building.jpg) Abandoned Building The **Abandoned Building** is the home of [Bralsa Drel](Skyrim_Bralsa_Drel.md) and [Rirns Llervu](Skyrim_Rirns_Llervu.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

It is on the waterfront west of [Glover Mallory's House](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md), being the last building on the waterfront along the path to the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) to the west. Until the stone is cleansed during the quest [Cleansing the Stones](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the building is uninhabited. After the stone is cleansed, Bralsa Drel and Rirns Llervu will take residence.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance are two unusable barrels. To the right of the entrance is a lit brazier and three [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants. Along the back of the building on the waterfront are three crates and more unusable barrels. The entrance is unlocked.

### Abandoned Building
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-interior-Abandoned_Building.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Abandoned_Building.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building.jpg) First room [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_02.jpg) Second room [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Building_03.jpg) Upper floor The entrance leads into a squalid building littered with ash piles, cobwebs, and [tankards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tankard) and other minor items messily strewn about. In the middle of the room is a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on the floor. In the southeast corner is another bottle of Nord mead on an ash pile. Along the west wall is a sideboard holding a bottle of Nord mead, a bottle of [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma), a copy of *[Confessions of a Dunmer Skooma Eater](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Confessions_of_a_Dunmer_Skooma_Eater)*, and a bottle of [matze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matze), with a copy of *[The Poison Song, Book IV](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_IV)* on the wall ledge behind the dresser. Along the north wall is another bottle of matze on the wall ledge, a doorway to the next room, and two more bottles of Nord mead on the wall ledge and a crate, respectively.

The next room is circular, and features the stairs leading up to the upper floor. Along the curved west wall are two owned bed rolls, with a bottle of [flin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flin), a bottle of sujamma, a bottle of [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shein), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and a wedge of [goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) between them, and a crate holding another loaf of bread and a copy of *[The Five Far Stars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Five_Far_Stars)*. Along the north wall beneath the stairs are two ingredient urns and an unusable barrel. The small circular room on the upper floor contains two sacks, a meat barrel, three unusable barrels, and four crates.
