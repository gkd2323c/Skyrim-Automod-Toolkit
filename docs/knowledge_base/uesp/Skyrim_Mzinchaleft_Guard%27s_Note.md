Mzinchaleft Guard's Note **by [Maluril](Skyrim_Maluril.md)**
---
I'm sick of repeating myself. Hand this to the man replacing you between shifts, and ensure he reads it before you leave the post.

I am not to be disturbed. Permit no one in my chamber, ever. Certain people may come looking for me. I want those people dead.

You'll be back to raiding pig farms tomorrow if you foul this up.
