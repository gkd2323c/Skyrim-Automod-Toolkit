# Alma Rula

[![](https://images.uesp.net/e/ef/MW-shrine-Saint_Llothis.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-shrine-Saint_Llothis.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-shrine-Saint_Llothis.png) Saint Llothis, the "best-loved Alma Rula"The Alma Rula is a position within the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).

Little is known of exact details of the role of the Alma Rula, but they have some kind of influence on temple doctrine,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Galsa-1)</sup> and have authority alongside the Archcanons.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gen Dialogue-2)</sup>

All four [Ordinator Orders](Lore_Ordinators.md) obey the commands of the Alma Rula who resides in the city of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia_(city).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gen Dialogue Ordinator-3)</sup>

## History
Saint Llothis the Pious was among the most renowned of the Alma Rula. He was a contemporary and companion of the Tribunal. He formulated the central rituals and principles of the New Temple Faith and was the symbolic mortal bridge between the gods and the faithful.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Lives Saints-4)</sup>
