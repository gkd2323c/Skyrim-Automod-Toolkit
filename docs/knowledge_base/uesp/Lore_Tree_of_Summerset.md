# Tree of Summerset

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/ON-interior-Selfora_Temple.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Selfora_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Selfora_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Selfora_Temple.jpg) Tree of Summerset The Tree of Summerset is a tree sacred to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) people, found in the town of Selfora in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md). When [Veloth the Pilgrim](Lore_Veloth.md) led the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) to [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md), he planted the seeds of his homeland of [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Of the trees to sprout from this, only the Tree of Summerset survived, and thus Selfora Temple was built around the tree to memorialize it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONForlorn-1)</sup>
