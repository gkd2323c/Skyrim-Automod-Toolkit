# Baandari Pedlars

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/ON-place-Baandari_Trading_Post.jpg/200px-ON-place-Baandari_Trading_Post.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Baandari_Trading_Post.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Baandari_Trading_Post.jpg) The Baandari Trading Post (ESO) *Do not slander another child of the Baandari.
Remain mindful in all dealings.
A gift must be returned in kind.
A fair trade need not be fair only in gold.
Truth and cleverness need not be enemies.
Find what is lost, trade what is found, and leave what has no purpose.*
— *[The Way of the Baandari](Lore_The_Way_of_the_Baandari.md)* The Baandari Pedlars (or simply the **Baandari**) are a group of nomadic [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) merchants that travel all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) to sell their wares. Despite the animosity they experience from the other races, they are inclusive when it comes to recruiting non Khajiit into their ranks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESORari-1)</sup> They live by a code of conduct known as the *"Baandari Code,"* which states that loose items are rightfully salvageable, the peddlers must indulge clients in prophecies they want to believe in, and guarantee them of a product's utmost value and versatility. The Baandari Clan live by this lifestyle and with no other alternative as it is set in stone.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Cot BP-2)</sup> They considered themselves the *"wandering litter"* of [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md), the Bandit God of southern Tamriel. They adopt the god's will, as seen in their name.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESONisuzi-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vo FTK-4)</sup>

The Baandari tribe travel all over Tamriel, as far as the northern regions of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) and the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md), or the elven territories of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). They also build a seasonal camp in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)'s market gate.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CM7-5)</sup> The [Samar Pekoe](Lore_Samar_Pekoe.md) shrub of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) is commonly harvested by the Baandari to make smooth and tannic-flavored tea.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESONisuzi-3)</sup> They are greatly disliked by the other races. Some cases have described them as evil spirits incarnate and have the notion that their mercantilism is just a cover for their thievery and fraud.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Tot BP-6)</sup> They tend to camp outside of large cities and move on to their next destination,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESORa.27darri-7)</sup> but some cases, they settle in certain areas. Most notable is the **Baandari Trading Post**, found on a crossroad along the [Xylo River](Lore_Xylo_River.md) in [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md), towards the regional border to [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Baandari Trading Post was attacked by the Houndsmen, [Wood Elf](Lore_Wood_Elf.md) devouts of the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). For protection, the head of the Baandari Tradepost, Eraral-dro hired [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) mercenaries but suspected that they would turn on them. Fortunately, he was able to drive them out with the help of a [wandering traveler](Lore_Soulless_One.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOEnemy-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOTangled-9)</sup>

The Baandari use a variety of mounts, easily recognizable by their red and gold tack; some animals, like the Senche-Lion cubs, are trained from birth to prepare them for a life as mounts<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONPedlar Pack Senche Lion Cub-10)</sup> while pack ponies are popular enough with adoring children to be sold to them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONPedlar Pack Pony-11)</sup> Riders use Lunar Compasses to help them navigate the various provinces, which have also been known to be retrofitted into repurposed [Dwarven Spider](Lore_Dwarven_Spider.md) [Vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONBaandari_Dwarven_Spider-12)</sup> The Baandari also repurpose Dwarven Spiders to help retrieve inaccessible items.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONPedlar Pack Spider-13)</sup> They are known to breed Black Senche-Panthers as mounts to easily leave town at night<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>
