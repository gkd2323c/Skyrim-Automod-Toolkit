[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-item-Shadowrend.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shadowrend.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shadowrend.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shadowrend.jpg) Shadowrend in greatsword form **Shadowrend** is a Daedric artifact that can take the form of a [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) or [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md). You can find it in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) switching back and forth in form. To take it and complete the quest [Through a Glass, Darkly](Skyrim_Through_a_Glass,_Darkly.md), you must defeat your [shadow](Skyrim_Shadow.md): a clone of your player character. After completing the quest, the weapon can be switched out for the other form anytime at the location you found it. Shadowrend's enchantment makes the target temporarily [weaker to magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md). Tempering Shadowrend requires an [Ebony Ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Through a Glass, Darkly](Skyrim_Through_a_Glass,_Darkly.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowrend.md)</sup>: Confront the darkness within yourself to obtain a powerful weapon.

## Notes
- The enchantment does not stack with itself, meaning that if you hit a creature twice then its weakness to magic remains at 15%.
- The enchantment stacks in an additive way with poisons of weakness to magic.
- Shadowrend previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shadowrend) in *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)*.
- The surface of the weapons have a smoky effect, and they make a unique sound when drawn.
- The sword and axe versions resemble the [Daedric Claymore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Daedric_Claymore) and [Daedric Battle Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Daedric_Battle_Axe) models from [*TES IV: Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion), respectively. The axe version used the same model when it appeared in *Shivering Isles*, although the sword used a [different one](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Dragonsword_of_Lainlyn).
