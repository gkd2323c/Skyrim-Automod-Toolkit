# Esroniet

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/ON-crown_store-Hot_Pepper_Bantam_Guar.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Hot_Pepper_Bantam_Guar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Hot_Pepper_Bantam_Guar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Hot_Pepper_Bantam_Guar.jpg) A Hot Pepper Bantam Guar, native to Esroniet The spice island of Esroniet is located between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONHot Pepper Bantam Guar-2)</sup> The main port of the island is called [Black Harbor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Harbor).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RDa I-3)</sup> The port was captured in [3E 282](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_282) by [Uriel Septim V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_V), and in [3E 284](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_284) the island's ruler, [Prince Bashomon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bashomon), surrendered the whole kingdom to him. Uriel transformed it into a major base for the [Imperial Navy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Navy) and used it as a launching point for the invasion of Akavir in [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RDa I-3)</sup>

Esroniet is the source of much of Tamriel's nutmeg, cloves, mace, and hot peppers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONHot Pepper Bantam Guar-2)</sup> A type of vibrant red dye, extremely popular in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) around [2E 53](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_53), was made from crushed hot peppers imported from Esroniet.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PSR-4)</sup> The flame-eyed Hot Pepper [Bantam Guar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bantam_Guar) is native to the island.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONHot Pepper Bantam Guar-2)</sup>
