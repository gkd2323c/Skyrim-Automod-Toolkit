# Psijic Gauntlets

Psijic Gauntlets are magical implements used by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), designed for specific [magical](Lore_Magic.md) disciplines such as [nature](Lore_Nature_Magic.md), [healing arts](Lore_Restoration.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Warden-1)</sup> frost,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Frost-2)</sup> shock,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shock-3)</sup> and [fire](Lore_Elements.md#Fire) magic.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Flame-4)</sup> Aware that spellcasting often requires extended hands, the gauntlets provided strong protection for the back of the hands and forearms, while leaving the fingertips exposed to avoid interfering with the intricate gestures required by certain spells.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CMP-5)</sup>

Although [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) magic is uncommon among the Psijics,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shock-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Frost-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Flame-4)</sup> who favor [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-M-6)</sup> those who do master it signify their discipline by wearing specialized gauntlets designed for the destructive elements of shock, ice, and fire.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shock-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Frost-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Flame-4)</sup> In addition to gauntlets designed for destructive magic, the Psijics also employed [Warden](Lore_Warden.md) Gauntlets attuned to [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md), which aided the wearer in focusing nature-based magic and Restoration spells.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Warden-1)</sup>
