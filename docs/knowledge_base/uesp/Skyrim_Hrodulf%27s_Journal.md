Hrodulf's Journal **by [Hrodulf](Skyrim_Hrodulf.md)** *The journal of a man slowly being driven mad by something in his new house*
---
1.

I've arrived in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), and moved into an [old house on the hill by the shore](Skyrim_Hrodulf%27s_House.md). Whoever lived here before is long gone.

2.

I can see well out into the waters from my vantage here, and can hear all things behind and around. [Dunmer bandits](Skyrim_Reaver.md) wander the woods at night, but I don't fear them.

5.

There is a [calling](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md) from the depths; a rumble drone that sings to me at night. I've started sleeping in the basement + keeping a knife near. The call is loudest down here. I will to be ready for whatever is coming...

6.

it is as if a great machine reverberates beneath me

10.

i tire of waiting for the caller underneath to emerge

i walked to [town](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) + bought some digging tools: shovel, shovel, pick i started to break down the wall behind the bookshelf + dig down slow going i put the bookshelf back when i finish digging why? no houseguests here but i feel i have something to hide

13.

how can i make [bjorn](Skyrim_Bjornolfr.md) hear what i have heard? i must not lose him yet i must remain in this place for i know i will know the truth soon

23.

the murmurer in the earth and i we talk i lay my head against the dirt

51.

fire from the deep
