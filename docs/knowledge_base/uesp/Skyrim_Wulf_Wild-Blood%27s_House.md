## Walkthrough

### Exterior
Along the east wall is a pen adjacent to Edla's house holding a [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md), with two crates with a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) on top against the side of the house. Behind the pen are two [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower). The entrance has a novice lock.

### Wulf Wild-Blood's House
The entrance leads into the main room of the two-room home. On your left is a long table and bench against the south wall, there are chairs at either end as well. On the table is a mead barrel, a bowl containing three loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), there are two [snow fox pelts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Fox_Pelt), a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) and a wooden bowl containing [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md). Above the table is a wall shelf with animal skulls on it. There is also an open crate containing a [deer pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Pelt) and a quiver of [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow) against the south wall. In the southwest corner is a cupboard with a [goat hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk) and a wooden bowl containing a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) on the shelves. To the right against the west wall is a barrel with a mead barrel on top and a chair. To the right of these is a pair of wall shelves containing three fillets of [horker meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Meat), a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a horker tusk and two sacks.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Wulf_Wild-Blood%27s_House.jpg) Wulf Wild-Blood's House interior In the northwest corner is a doorway to a storeroom and to the right of this along the north wall is a fire pit complete with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking.md). In the fire is a cast iron pot containing a [horker loaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Loaf) and a portion of [clam meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat). Around the fire is a kettle containing a joint of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), a cabbage and a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato). To the right of the entrance is a stack of four barrels and a sack. On one of the barrels is a basket containing two cabbages. A set of shelves are to the left containing a [leg of goat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat), a fillet of horker meat, two bottles of ale and one bottle of alto wine. On the left of the shelves is a sack and between this and the fire pit is the doorway to the bedroom.

Walking into the bedroom, there is a single bed on the left against the wall with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) underneath. Beside the bed is a dresser with a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and a bowl containing a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) on top. There is also a chair in the northeast corner. Entering the storeroom, there is a narrow table against the west wall on your left with a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) underneath and a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) on the floor. There is a chair beside it. Against the north wall is an end table with a wooden plate containing a loaf of bread on top. Against the east wall that separates this room from the bedroom is a shelf containing a horker tusk and underneath a wood pile, two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), and a bucket containing six large pieces of [charcoal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal), with one more piece on the floor.
