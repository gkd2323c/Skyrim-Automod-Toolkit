# Corvus Direnni

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg) Flame Atronach Horse typical for Direnni cavalry **Arch-Conjurer**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAW-1)</sup> Corvus Direnni was a powerful mage of the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) clan. His spell secrets were recorded in his Grimoire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLODRB-2)</sup> Along with his contemporary [Calani Direnni](Lore_Calani_Direnni.md), he was a pioneer of the modern [School of Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md). Their binding rituals are still used when summoning lesser [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOOC-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TIG-4)</sup> However, Corvus in particular is lauded for codifying its rules.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-O-5)</sup> Corvus devoted his studies to perfecting variations on [Koron's](Lore_Koron.md) [Peremptory Summons](Lore_Koron%27s_Peremptory_Summons.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-WOT-6)</sup> He was apparently the first conjurer to interweave the summoning and binding of a daedra into a single spell, making the practice exponentially safer.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-POC-7)</sup> He took part in the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md) circa [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482), where his conjured creatures played a pivotal role.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-8)</sup> Direnni mages mounted on flaming horses first appeared during this battle.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FAH-9)</sup>

Corvus was the first to theorize that the [wayshrines](Lore_Wayshrines.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) correspond to the [pattern of stars](Lore_Astronomy.md) in the night sky and that they could be used as some sort of transportation network. He also used [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) research to devise and then promulgate the standard "portal" spells which were later put into use by the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-WOT-6)</sup>

He was known for conjuring numerous atronachs and shaping them into animal forms. He could summon Flame Atronach wolves of varied sizes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAW-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-FAPW-10)</sup> He was the first wizard to compel a Storm Atronach to assume the form of a great cat.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-SAS-11)</sup> He also could shape them into the forms of horses and camels. At some point in the history, he met the High King of Hammerfell [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md) and astounded him by summoning the storm atronach camel.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SAC-12)</sup> He researched [Frost Atronachs](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md) as well and theorized that the Frost Senche-Panther may actually be a form of atronach summoned from [Fourth Sinus of Takubar](Lore_Fourth_Sinus_of_Takubar.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-DFS-13)</sup>

He was responsible for mentoring numerous Direnni students for the Master Conjurer test. Famous [Peregrine Direnni](Lore_Peregrine_Direnni.md) was among his pupils.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-FAC-14)</sup>
