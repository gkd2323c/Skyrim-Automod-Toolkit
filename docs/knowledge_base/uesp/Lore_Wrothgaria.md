[![](https://images.uesp.net/3/38/DF-place-Wrothgaria.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Wrothgaria.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Wrothgaria.jpg) Wrothgaria Wrothgaria was the main city within the unconquered territories of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) immediately prior to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Wroth-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWITW-2)</sup> The mountains bordered [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md), [Dwynnen](Lore_Dwynnen.md), [Kambria](Lore_Kambria.md), [Koegria](Lore_Koegria.md), [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire_(fiefdom).md), [Menevia](Lore_Menevia_(county).md), [Orsinium Area](Lore_Orsinium_Area.md), [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), and [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon_(fiefdom).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map-3)</sup> The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline of the region was [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md), and the regional deity was [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DF-4)</sup> After the Warp in the West, Wrothgaria and much of the surrounding area became a part of the kingdom of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWITW-2)</sup>
