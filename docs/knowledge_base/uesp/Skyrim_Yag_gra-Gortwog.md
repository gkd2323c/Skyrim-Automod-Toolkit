[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-npc-Yag_gra-Gortwog.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Yag_gra-Gortwog.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yag_gra-Gortwog.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yag_gra-Gortwog.jpg) Yag gra-Gortwog **Yag gra-Gortwog** was an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [sorceress](Skyrim_CombatSorcerer.md) hired by [Sulla Trebatius](Skyrim_Sulla_Trebatius.md) to be part of his ill-fated expedition into the [Dwarven ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) of [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md).

Her corpse can be found in the Animonculory zone on a broken ramp at the bottom of a deep shaft. It appears she died fighting the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) found within. She vainly attempted to save [Endrast](Skyrim_Endrast.md) and was killed while covering his escape.

Yag wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance it will be [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ for both shoes and armor; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, she has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md), and at level 19 it can be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of her carrying a leveled shield, up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 12.
