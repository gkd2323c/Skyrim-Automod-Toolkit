[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus.jpg) Vigilant Tyranus **Vigilant Tyranus** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) encountered outside the [Abandoned House](Skyrim_Abandoned_House.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He is a [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md) and as such, has dedicated his life to rid Tamriel of Daedra, wherever they appear. During the quest [The House of Horrors](Skyrim_The_House_of_Horrors.md) his righteousness will be put to the test when he has to face the Daedric Prince [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).

He wields a leveled, unenchanted [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace) of the best possible quality and wears [apprentice robes of restoration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Robes_of_Restoration), a matching [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Hood) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c). As a Vigilant of Stendarr he is also equipped with the proper [amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Stendarr). He carries an assortment of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). He also knows several spells, specifically [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) and [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The House of Horrors
When in Markarth, a few feet up the street from the inn, you will find Tyranus standing outside the abandoned house, clearly on the lookout for a helping hand. He may even have caught an uninterested [Yngvar the Singer](Skyrim_Yngvar_the_Singer.md) and will keep asking:

**Tyranus**: *"So you don't know anything about this house?"*
**Yngvar**: *"No."*
**Tyranus**: *"Anyone seen entering or leaving? Any strange lights or unusual noises?"*
**Yngvar**: *"It's abandoned and it's always been abandoned."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_03.jpg) *"Someone's been here."* When you get near him, he will actively seek you out and ask the same question, saying:

*"Excuse me, but do you know anything about this house? Seen anyone enter or leave?"* **Why are you asking?** **No.** *"Seems no one in this city has."* **I'm not sure.** *"Damn. It's like everyone in this city has amnesia."* All three options will end with:

*"I'm with the Vigil of Stendarr. We believe this house might have been used for Daedra worship. Evil rites and so forth."* **Do you need any help?** *"I was actually just about to head on inside. Be good to have someone watch my back. Follow me, and keep your eyes open. Daedra are powerful creatures and tricksters. Never know what you'll find."* **Sorry. I don't know anything.** *"Thank you for your time."* Inside the house, Tyranus will look around and comment on the interior with:

*"Fresh food. No wood rot on the furniture. Someone's been here. Recently. But the people I asked say no one enters or leaves...."* A sudden sound will make him run away with the words:

*"Wait. Did you hear that? I think it came this way."* Approaching him will net you one of two random lines:

*"Eyes open."* *"Something's going on here."* In the next room he will comment:

*"That's it. Something's inside the house. Come on, we're getting to the bottom of this."* He will then run down the first set of stairs and approach the locked door, shouting:

*"Come out! We know you're here!"* *"There's another door. See if you can get it open."* If you talk to him, he will repeat:

*"See if you can get the door open."* When you touch the door, the furniture and items in the house will start floating in mid-air, causing Tyranus to panic and run upstairs, yelling:

*"Stendarr's Mercy! This isn't an ordinary Daedra. We have to get help."* The voice of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) will then echo through the rooms:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vigilant_Tyranus_02.jpg) *"It's you or me!"* ***"Weak. He's weak. You're strong. Crush him."***

Near the front door, a scared Tyranus will encourage you to leave the house, saying

*"You first. Come on. Let's go."* Talk to him and he will say:

*"We're getting out of here. Now."* When you try the front door you will find it locked and Molag Bal will speak once again: ***"No. Kill him. Crush his bones. Tear at his flesh. You will kill. You will kill, or you will die!"***

Tyranus will then reveal that he hears the voice as well, yelling:

*"Get out of my head, Daedra!"* *"I don't want to die. I can't die here!"* He will finally realize what he must do to survive and will draw his sword with:

*"The Daedra has us. It's you or me!"* He will then lose his essential status and proceed to attack, leaving you no choice but to kill him to advance the quest and get out of the house.

## Unused Dialogue
Tyranus has a bit of earlier dialogue for his initial interaction with you. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

His question would have differed slightly:

*"Excuse me, but do you know anything about this house? Who owns it? What it's used for?"* You would have just a single reply:

**No, I don't. Why are you asking?** To which Tyranus would say:

*"The Priesthood of Stendarr received a message that this house was a site of daedric worship some decades ago. With the war in Skyrim, we're worried that people may be turning to the daedra in desperation."* **I could help you investigate this house.** *"I was actually just about to head on inside. Be good to have someone watch my back. Keep your eyes open for any signs of daedra."* There are some other minor changes as well, an earlier iteration closer to his final explanation exists as well:

*"I'm with the Priesthood of Stendarr. We believe this house might have been used for daedra worship. Evil rites and so forth."* The only difference here is the use of the term "Priesthood of Stendarr" rather than "Vigil of Stendarr". The former seems to be an earlier name for the group which only gets used by Kleppr and Frabbi in the final game, in the initial starting rumor for the quest.

Another very minor difference is that one of your initial responses would have said **No, I haven't.** rather than just simply **No.**

## Notes
- When first entering Markarth, Tyranus will not be present due to the Forsworn-related scene in the market. After the scene, exit and enter Markarth — or enter and leave any building, like the Silver Blood Inn, for example — and he will spawn.
- The poltergeist effect that triggers as part of the quest can be avoided in order to keep the house tidy. If Vigilant Tyranus is killed before the effect starts, it will never occur, and all furniture will remain positioned normally, while the quest itself will proceed without problems.
- If you wish to loot Vigilant Tyranus's body, do it right after killing him. His body will disappear when you return with Logrolf.
