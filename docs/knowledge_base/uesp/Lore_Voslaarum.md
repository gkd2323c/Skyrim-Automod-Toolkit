[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/MER-art-Twin_Dragons.jpg/200px-MER-art-Twin_Dragons.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Twin_Dragons.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Twin_Dragons.jpg) Twin Dragons, Voslaarum and Naaslaarum Voslaarum was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Claw-Water-Twin" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIL-1)</sup>

Voslaarum and [Naaslaarum](Lore_Naaslaarum.md) were revered twin dragons, who lived beneath the Frozen Lake within the [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md). They were most likely killed at some point and resurrected by [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) upon his return,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> although it is possible that they survived until [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lake-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FV-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PRIMA-5)</sup><sup>:545, 999</sup>

At some point, in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), when the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) ventured to the Forgotten Vale, Voslaarum and Naaslaarum were resting beneath the ice of the lake and guarding a [Word Wall](Lore_Word_Walls.md). They fought against the hero but were ultimately defeated and slain.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lake-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FV-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PRIMA-5)</sup><sup>:545, 999</sup>

Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Voslaarum was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LAD-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Arngeir-7)</sup>
