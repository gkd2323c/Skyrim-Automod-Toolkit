*He was deceptively powerful. Wiry. Quick as a skeever-trap.*
— [Luciana Pullo](Lore_Luciana_Pullo.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PLP-1)</sup> Celedith was a powerful [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) spell-eater and a mage who took part in the defense of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) from [Reman Empire](Lore_Reman_Empire.md)'s invasion in [1E 2712](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2712).

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/ON-render-Proctor_Luciana.jpg/200px-ON-render-Proctor_Luciana.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Proctor_Luciana.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Proctor_Luciana.jpg) Luciana Pullo Little is known of Celedith's past. He was described as deceptively powerful, wiry and quick. In 1E 2712 he fought against with Luciana Pullo, an [Imperial Battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) and a lieutenant of [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman_Cyrodiil.md), during the Valenwood campaign. In the midst of the encounter, Luciana cleaved his staff with a [summoned](Lore_Conjuration.md) blade, when he was in the midst of casting a translocation spell leading to an explosion and a bright light. While Luciana survived and was transported into the Clockwork City, Celedith's fate remains unknown, and it is likely that he perished in the explosion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PLP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PLJ-3)</sup>

In [1E 2714](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2714), two years after Celedith's valiant defense, Valenwood succumbed to defeat. Emperor Reman Cyrodiil, emerging victorious, allowed the [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) to retain governance over Valenwood as an Imperial province. Simultaneously, he granted independence to numerous lesser nobles, strategically diluting their influence within the region.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3Val-4)</sup>
