[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/LO-map-Cyrodiil_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Cyrodiil_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Cyrodiil_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Cyrodiil_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Cyrodiil Mir Corrup is a resort settlement located in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). During the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Mir Corrup experienced a period of time where it was a prominent resort for nobles.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Mir Corrup is located in the mainland of [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md), nowhere near a large body of water,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dance-2)</sup> overlooking [Caer Suvio](Lore_Caer_Suvio.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LY:2920-3)</sup>

During the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), Mir Corrup was known for its hot springs. It was here that [Emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md)'s mistress, Rijja, was captured by the [Imperial Guard](Lore_Imperial_Guard.md) for suspected conspiracy against the Emperor. The only evidence for this was Rijja's supposed involvement with [Catchica](Lore_Catchica.md), an [Orma](Lore_Orma.md) tribeswoman who attempted to poison Crown Prince [Juilek Cyrodiil](Lore_Juilek.md) during the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md). Rijja had inadvertently met Catchica during her trip to Mir Corrup, which was followed by her arrest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Second-4)</sup> Mir Corrup has been active since the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), and the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) amidst the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dance-2)</sup>
