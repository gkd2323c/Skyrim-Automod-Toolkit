[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-item-Circlet_of_Waterbreathing.jpg/200px-SR-item-Circlet_of_Waterbreathing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Circlet_of_Waterbreathing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Circlet_of_Waterbreathing.jpg) Circlet of Waterbreathing The Circlet of Waterbreathing is a [circlet](Skyrim_Circlet.md) that can be found in the hands of a [Frozen Falmer Shaman](Skyrim_Frozen_Falmer_Shaman.md) at the northern end of the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>. In appearance, this circlet is identical to a [copper and onyx circlet](Skyrim_Copper_and_Onyx_Circlet.md). Its enchantment allows you to [breathe underwater](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

## Notes
- While this item is only available with the Dawnguard add-on, it exists in the game data of the original game, meaning it has a set [Form ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) where other items from Dawnguard have variable digits based on load order.
- This is one of only two guaranteed, disenchantable items with the Waterbreathing enchantment in the entire game, and the only one available without the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion.

## Bugs
- While this item was meant to appear in random loot and merchant inventories like other enchanted items, it was left out of the `LItem Ench Circlet All` leveled list, meaning this is the only Circlet of Waterbreathing found in the entire game, unless you enchant one yourself.
