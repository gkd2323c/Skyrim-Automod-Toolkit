# Hematite

[![](https://images.uesp.net/8/8f/ON-concept-Prince-Mephala-emblem.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Prince-Mephala-emblem.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Prince-Mephala-emblem.png) An emblem representing Mephala Hematite is a kind of black crystal.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HWS-1)</sup>

In addition to [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> it is a commonly employed material for creating devotional artifacts dedicated to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md). Within the collection of Mephala devotional items, there existed silvery-black worry stones adorned with engraved symbols of the Webspinner on their surfaces. These stones were notorious for inducing severe headaches when rubbed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HWS-1)</sup>
