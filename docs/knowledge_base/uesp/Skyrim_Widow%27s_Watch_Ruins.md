[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-place-Widow%27s_Watch_Ruins.jpg/200px-SR-place-Widow%27s_Watch_Ruins.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Widow%27s_Watch_Ruins.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Widow%27s_Watch_Ruins.jpg) Widow's Watch Ruins **Widow's Watch Ruins** is a small ruined [tower](Skyrim_Imperial_Towers.md) west-northwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) containing a [hag](Skyrim_Hag.md).

## Walkthrough
This is a typical tower that has tilted due to a landslide, making traversal slightly difficult. Once you have defeated the lone hag, you are free to explore. Keep climbing the structure to the top, then loop around counterclockwise until you reach a higher doorway. Through the doorway, ascend the wooden stairs and cross the wooden plank to reach an expert-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) partially hidden behind a table, next to which is a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](Skyrim_2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4.md)* behind a random [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md). There is nothing else of interest here.

## Notes
- Sometimes the book and shield can be dislodged and found elsewhere around the tower and surrounding area.
- Widow's Watch Ruins also appears in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) as [Sword's Point Watchtower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sword%27s_Point_Watchtower).
- Version 2.0.7 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces the Imperial tower map icon for Widow's Watch Ruins with the one for Nordic towers.
