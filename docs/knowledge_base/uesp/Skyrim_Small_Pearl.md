[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-ingredient-Small_Pearl.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Small_Pearl.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Small_Pearl.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Small_Pearl.jpg) A small pearl The **Small Pearl** is an [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and one of the rarest in the base game. Small Pearls, as well as [Pearls](Skyrim_Pearl.md), are only available as already-harvested samples in the original version of the game. The only shellfish found in Skyrim are [Clams](Skyrim_Clam.md), and unlike in previous games they do not produce any size of pearl. However, if [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) is installed, Pearls and Small Pearls can be harvested in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) from [Pearl Oysters](Skyrim_Pearl_Oyster.md).

## Ingredients
Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 1 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 1 in [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 1 in [Valerica's Study](Skyrim_Valerica%27s_Study.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))
- 4 in [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> ([Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md))
- 3 in the [Greathall](Skyrim_Greathall.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> of [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) ([Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)), in a locked display case
- [Alchemy laboratories](Skyrim_Alchemy_Laboratory.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> in player-built houses provide respawning samples once they are furnished with wash basins

This ingredient is *not* in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is an "uncommon" ingredient.

The remaining way to obtain Small Pearls is in random loot, but the chances of one appearing in any given location are generally small. The best bets are:

- [Peddlers](Skyrim_Peddler.md) may be randomly encountered in the wilderness (typically being attacked). These peddlers have an 8% chance of carrying 1-3 samples.
- Uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) have a 2% chance of containing one.
- [Warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md) and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) have a 0.5% chance of carrying one.

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot normally be bought from apothecary merchants, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general-goods merchants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
