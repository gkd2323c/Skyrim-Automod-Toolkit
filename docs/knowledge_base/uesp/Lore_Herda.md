Herda was an [empress](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). She ruled the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) at some point in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), prior to the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md). She is credited with improving relations with the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovia.md) following the dissolution of the [Alessian Reform](Lore_Alessian_Order.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3V-1)</sup>
