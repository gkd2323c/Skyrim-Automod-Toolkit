[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-place-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg/200px-SR-place-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg) Elgrim's Elixirs **Elgrim's Elixirs** is a damp alchemist's shop located on the lower level, by the canal that flows through the center of the city of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). Owned and run by [Elgrim](Skyrim_Elgrim.md) and his wife [Hafjorg](Skyrim_Hafjorg.md). It is opposite the entrance to [Beggar's Row](Skyrim_Beggar%27s_Row.md), near the northern watergate to [Lake Honrich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich).

## Walkthrough

### Elgrim's Elixirs
The proprietor [Elgrim](Skyrim_Elgrim.md) praises its location as ideal for keeping his [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) fresh, but his wife, [Hafjorg](Skyrim_Hafjorg.md), complains that it is merely the only location he could find that they could afford. Elgrim and Hafjorg both sell merchandise, although they share the same [merchant chest](#Standard_Merchandise) and basically the same purse. [Ingun Black-Briar](Skyrim_Ingun_Black-Briar.md) is also found in the store during the standard opening hours, as she is apprenticed to Elgrim, who is training her to be an alchemist.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.jpg) Elgrim's counter Upon entering, the shop counter is in front of you in an L-shape coming out from the end of the partition wall. Hanging above the partition wall are three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, four bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). Behind the counter are two sets of shelves holding a selection of ingredients, [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), and [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md) that are [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise). On the counter are more items that are displayed merchandise, including those hidden inside a stone bowl on the counter or hidden under the counter, along with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md), and a copy of *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)*. In the southeast corner is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), with a barrel containing ingredients behind it.

There are two chairs with a small table between them in the northwest corner, with a pile of firewood behind. There is a large fireplace in the west wall, with a wall shelf to the left holding a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a pile of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks underneath. In the southwest corner is a crate with a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) on top, and a small round wooden table and three chairs in front. On the table is a [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), and a bottle of wine. Near the middle of the south wall is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand with food sacks next to it.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Elgrim%27s_Elixirs_02.jpg) The bedroom Against the back of the partition wall are three barrels, two of which contain food, while the third contains ingredients. On top of one of them is a copy of *[Palla, volume 2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palla,_volume_2)*. To the right, in the middle of the wall is a double bed coming out from the wall with an end table holding an apprentice-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) to the right, a wall shelf above holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a dresser at the foot of the bed holding a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton), and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) on the floor. Opposite, against the south wall is a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with Ingun's Alchemy Chest (see [notes](#Notes)) to the left, along with a food barrel with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top in the southeast corner. Against the east wall is a crate, with the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger) on top and a wardrobe to the left.

## Notes
- Completing [Ingun's](Skyrim_Ingun_Black-Briar.md) [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Few_and_Far_Between) provides you with the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ingun%27s_Supply_Chest_Key) to her alchemy chest. It also allows you to freely take the contents of several barrels in the store.
- Even if you complete [Truth Ore Consequences](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Truth_Ore_Consequences), taking any of the other items in the store is still considered stealing. In particular, you are unable to freely take any of the alchemy ingredients sitting on the store's shelves and counters. These are available after also investing in the store, however.
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the left of the entrance door. There are several alchemical ingredients, potions, and poisons found around the store.
- Elgrim's Elixir's is one of very few stores where you can freely enter the private living space without being followed or asked to leave.
