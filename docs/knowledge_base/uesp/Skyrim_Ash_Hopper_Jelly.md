## Ingredients
- The only sample in the game can be found in [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), on a barrel next to the alchemy table downstairs.
- Ash hopper jelly can sometimes be found for sale at general goods merchants and alchemists on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
- Ash hoppers tend to appear in groups of two-three in the ash wastes of Solstheim, but not in the more frozen areas.
