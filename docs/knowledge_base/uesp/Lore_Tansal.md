[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bangkorai_Garrison.jpg) Bangkorai Pass, where Tansal's sword shattered Tansal was a master [swordsmith](Lore_Smithing.md) and [sword-singer](Lore_Sword-singers.md) from the early-mid [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He forged a sword first wielded by [Makela Leki](Lore_Makela_Leki.md)'s father, which bore a [serpent](Lore_Snake.md)'s crest. That sword would then pass on to his daughter, Makela, who became one of history's most well-known [Ansei](Lore_Ansei.md). The sword broke at the battle of [Bangkorai Pass](Lore_Bangkorai_Pass.md) while she fought King [Joile](Lore_Joile.md)'s armies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FTMSOML-1)</sup>
