[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-creature-Mistman.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Mistman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mistman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mistman.jpg) A Mistman **Mistmen** are floating, ghostly [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) found within the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). Mistmen are the mages of the Soul Cairn, as opposed to the warrior-like [wrathmen](Skyrim_Wrathman.md) and the lightly-armed [bonemen](Skyrim_Boneman.md). Mistmen have the ability to cast [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) and [Ice Spike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike), though they will resort to punching when they run out of [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md).

A [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell tome [Conjure Mistman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Mistman) can be found in the Soul Cairn, which summons a mistman for 60 seconds.

The powerful [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md) is also able to [summon](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn_Summon.md) mistmen, alongside wrathmen and bonemen.

## Gallows Hall Creation
The [Gallows Hall](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) lets you summon a permanent Mistman follower using the [Bone Forge](Skyrim_Bone_Forge.md).

- It is summoned using 1 [Bone Meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal), 1 [Greater Soul Gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Soul_Gem) and 1 [Soul Husk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Husk)
- The Mistman summoned using the forge does not count towards your maximum number of summons, making it a free permanent follower.
- It does not regenerate health and is protected, being downed when its health is too low instead of being outright killed. It can be healed using the [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing) and [Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead) spells.
