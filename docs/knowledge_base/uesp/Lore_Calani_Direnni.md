[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Flame_Atronach_Horse.jpg) Flame Atronach Horse typical for Direnni cavalry Calani Direnni was one of the prominent members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup> and the pioneer conjuration mage. Along with their contemporary, Arch-Conjurer [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md), Calani was a pioneer of the modern [School of Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md). Their binding rituals are still used when summoning lesser [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOOC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIG-3)</sup>

Calani took part in the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md) circa [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482), where creatures conjured by them and Corvus played a pivotal role.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-4)</sup> Direnni mages mounted on flaming horses first appeared during this battle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FAH-5)</sup>
