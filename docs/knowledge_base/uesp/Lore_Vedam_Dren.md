Vedam Dren was Duke of the Imperial District of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Of noble [descent](Lore_Dren.md), Dren was Hlaalu Grandmaster of the Vvardenfell district before he inherited his title. He ruled from the Grand Council Chambers in [Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md), surrounded by Dunmer and Imperial Counselors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTV-1)</sup>

Vedam Dren was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [knight](Lore_Knights.md) but also thought of himself as a noble, a leader and a diplomat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWVedam-2)</sup> Vedam is the older brother of [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md) head [Orvas Dren](Lore_Orvas_Dren.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWVedam-2)</sup> who ran the [Dren Plantation](Lore_Dren_Plantation.md), and the father of Ilmeni Dren,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWVedam-2)</sup> the leader of the abolitionist group [Twin Lamps](Lore_Twin_Lamps.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ilmeni-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GJS-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TL-5)</sup>

After losing over half the Tong's shipments in rapid succession, Orvas toyed with the idea of having Vedam assassinated, so that he might consolidate his power and succeed Vedam as Duke of Vvardenfell and Grandmaster of House Hlaalu.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Dren Note-6)</sup>
