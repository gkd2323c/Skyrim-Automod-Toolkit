Letter to Clexius **by [Avanessa Calladius](Skyrim_Avanessa_Calladius.md)** *A letter about Cyrodilic goblins invading Skyrim*
---
Dear Clexius,

It's as you feared. The news here in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) is troubling on many levels.

Now, I've studied [goblins](Skyrim_Goblin.md) all my life, and while customs differ from tribe to tribe, one thing has always held true. They are beholden to their shamans, and removing them will turn the entire tribe docile.

Moreover, we have seen this method work in practice, most famously with the Three Feather tribe near [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md). Yet still the rumors persist of goblin activity near the border.

Most disturbing is the news that the "[Blue God](Skyrim_Blue_God.md)" himself is leading them. Could it be [Malacath](Skyrim_Malacath.md) himself in some form?

Regardless, if some [Daedric being](Skyrim_Daedra.md) has found its way to this [realm](Lore_Mundus.md), this is a cause for concern. I'll be heading to [Gromm's Pass](Skyrim_Gromm%27s_Pass.md) in the morning to investigate.

Faithfully yours,
[Avanessa Calladius](Skyrim_Avanessa_Calladius.md)
