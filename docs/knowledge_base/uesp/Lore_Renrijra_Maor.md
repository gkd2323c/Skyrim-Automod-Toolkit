# Renrijra Krin

The Renrijra Krin (which translates as *The Mercenary's Grin*, *The Laugh of the Landless* or *The Smiling Scum*,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AT-1)</sup> and is often shortened to **Renrijra**) are a long-standing [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) criminal organization. The **Renrijra Maor** (also called the **Renrijra Pirates**) are a naval subgroup of the organization.

The Renrijra's name reflects the group's philosophy of bravery and enjoyment of life, while also indicating that they owe allegiance to no ruler. It was originally a derogatory term, but was adopted by the amused group. The organization has no fixed rules, preferring to call its tenets "thjizzrini", or "foolish concepts". Their ranks are made up of thieves, thugs, bandits, smugglers, pirates and saboteurs. They are ruthless in pursuing their aims, but depend on guerrilla tactics to operate, giving freely to Khajiit civilians in return for their support.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AT-1)</sup>

During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), the Renrijra Maor were hired by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) as privateers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> They raided the coast of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md), seeking to conquer the shores, which elicited an attack by the Breton Navy.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), they began operating along the western coast of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). They attempted to enslave the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) population of [Silent Mire](Lore_Silent_Mire.md), a village in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), but were driven off by a series of counter strikes. They were also responsible for helping to supply the failed Dominion invasion of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md), but changed allegiance to the local pirate boss Captain One-Eye after their leader was killed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Renrijra had proclaimed dedication to reclaiming areas of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) that they saw as rightfully belonging to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). They blamed the [Count](Lore_Marius_Caro.md) of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) for driving the Khajiit from the fertile land around [Lake Makapi](Lore_Niben_Bay.md) and the [River Malapi](Lore_River_Niben.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3-5)</sup> In this they disagree with their ruler, the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md), who was responsible for making the agreement with Cyrodiil. However, it was the belief of the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) that the organization was simply posing as freedom fighters in order to further their criminal agenda and their Cyrodilic [Skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) smuggling. In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Special Inquisitor Drels Theran of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) was in charge of suppressing the Renrijra insurgents.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
