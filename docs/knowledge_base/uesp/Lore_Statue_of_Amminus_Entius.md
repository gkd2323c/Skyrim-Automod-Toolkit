Statue of Amminus Entius *A plaque honoring an Order of the Hour knight*
---
If [the Order](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) has a heart, it was [Amminus Entius](Lore_Amminus_Entius.md), [Cavor](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md)'s oldest friend and the Order's first quartermaster. The fourth pillar of the Hour established the infrastructure for ensuring that Akatosh's knights were the best-fed, best-equipped, and best-trained force in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md).
