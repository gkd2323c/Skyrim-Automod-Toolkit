# Ferrofungus

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/ON-concept-Telvanni.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Telvanni.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Telvanni.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Telvanni.jpg) Telvanni equipment, some of which was made of ferrofungus Ferrofungus is a kind of [mushroom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mushroom) cultivated by members of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup> They [refined](Lore_Mycoturgy.md) pliant ferrofungus into wrought ferrofungus for use in crafting some of their equipment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Pliant_Ferrofungus-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wrought_Ferrofungus-3)</sup> [Forge-hardened](Lore_Smithing.md) ferrofungus was occasionally employed by Telvanni craftsmen when [metallic ore](Lore_Metals.md) was unavailable, particularly in the creation of [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) such as axes. Some pieces of Telvanni armor, including cuirasses, were constructed from overlapping plates of chitin, ferrofungus, or metal, shaped after shelf fungi or toadstool caps and scored with spore-gill lines. Their shields were similarly fashioned from layered chitin or ferrofungus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TS-1)</sup>
