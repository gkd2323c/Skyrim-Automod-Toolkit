[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-menu-Nordic_Jewelry.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Nordic_Jewelry.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Nordic_Jewelry.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Nordic_Jewelry.jpg) Nordic Jewelry **Nordic Jewelry** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds a new type of fine jewelry: Nordic circlets, amulets, and rings, all of which can be crafted after completing the related quest. There are also enchanted versions of these pieces which can be obtained. It was released on August 29, 2018 for 200 Creation Credits.

## Official Summary
Creation Club *Beset with a jewel made of the highest quality stalhrim, these elegant, handcrafted jewelry pieces combine traditional Nordic design with an elegance fit for a Jarl. Each gem is expertly chosen for its purity, color, and magical affinity. The ornate setting is made of the finest quicksilver that is guaranteed to retain its finish. Choose from three craftable options including a Nordic amulet, ring, or circlet, and travel the holds of Skyrim with style and grace. (Quest "Nordic Jewelry" starts by reading the Certificate of Authenticity obtained if you buy Nordic jewelry from Madesi, Bersi Honey-Hand, Endarie or Belethor.)* Sneak Peek - Aug 2018 *This set of fine jewelry comes with a circlet, amulet, and ring. In addition to merchants carrying normal copies, there is a short quest to get an enchanted version from the man who crafted these wonderful pieces.*
## Overview
This Creation adds various pieces of Nordic jewelry, made from [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) and resembling the [Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic) style of armor and weapons added by [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md). They can be purchased at general goods and jewelry [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) throughout Skyrim or crafted after completing a **[quest](Skyrim_Nordic_Jewelry_(quest).md)**.
