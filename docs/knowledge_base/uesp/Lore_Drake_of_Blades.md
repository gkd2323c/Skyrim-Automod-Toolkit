*Lord Akatosh, I am yours! I pledge my blades and my soul to your service! I am your shield and your vengeance, now and for all time!*
—Drake of Blades The Drake of Blades (real name unknown) was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) agent and member of the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md). During the long [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), in the absence of any true emperor to protect, the Dragonguard protected the Ruby Throne in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) from the petty warlords who ran rampant in Cyrodiil.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-R-1)</sup> They were driven underground by Empress Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) after captain [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) disappeared but continued to operate in the province under pseudonyms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup> In circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) she fought against forces of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) during the invasion of the Imperial City<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOIC-3)</sup> and became the immortal guardian of the [Sublime Brazier](Lore_Sublime_Brazier.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSub Braz-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/LG-cardart-Dragonguard_Outcast.png/200px-LG-cardart-Dragonguard_Outcast.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Dragonguard_Outcast.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Dragonguard_Outcast.png) Drake of Blades (Legends) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/ON-quest-The_Sublime_Brazier.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Sublime_Brazier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Sublime_Brazier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Sublime_Brazier.jpg) Father Egnatius performing the ritual on the Sublime Brazier to empower Drake of Blades [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/ON-interior-Dragonfire_Cathedral.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Dragonfire_Cathedral.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Dragonfire_Cathedral.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Dragonfire_Cathedral.jpg) The main chamber of the Dragonfire Cathedral Cathedral Little is known of the Drake of Blades before she joined the Dragonguard, beyond the fact that she was an Imperial.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MTC-5)</sup> Like other members of the Dragonguard, she was driven underground by Empress Regent Clivia Tharn.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup> She was known to be a secretive person and generally preferred to work alone, making only temporary allies.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TDOBAYQ-6)</sup> At some point she came into contact with [The Rat](Lore_The_Rat.md), a former advisor to Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TR-7)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup> She made contact with forces of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), but those meetings were infrequent and cursory. She sided with none of the sides during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), instead focusing on providing aid to the people who ended up in the Imperial City during the daedric invasion in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MTC-5)</sup>

The Drake of Blades played a substantial role in protecting the [Sublime Brazier](Lore_Sublime_Brazier.md) and thwarting [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s invasion of the Imperial City circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). She did this by scouting the Imperial City and gathering information from the three alliances and then worked with [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to stop Molag Bal's plot to snuff out the Dragonfires forever. The Drake and the Vestige blew up a mining operation in the Memorial District, saved Father Egnatius, stole a book about the Sublime Brazier from the Daedra and defeated the Xivkyn in the Arena District to obtain vital information from The Rat among other events. The Drake of Blades, Father Egnatius, Captain [Anatolius Caudex](Lore_Anatolius_Caudex.md), and the Vestige managed to defeat the waves of Daedra and prevented General Malivus from destroying the Sublime Brazier.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOIC-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup>

The Drake of Blades then performed a ritual with Father Egnatius to transform her into the guardian of the Sublime Brazier until another Dragonborn took the throne. Once this ritual was complete the Drake of Blades became the immortal guardian of the Brazier and the Dragonfire Cathedral, blessed with the power of Akatosh himself and forever bound to the temple. During the ritual, she experienced a vision and according to her own words could see everything.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup> She was granted immense power and immortality by Akatosh himself. Aside from immortality, she was bestowed control over flames as well as the ability to open portals despite the lack of the extensive magical training received before the blessing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSub Braz-4)</sup> Another ability granted to her by the Akatosh was the power of second [sight](Lore_Sight.md). Her precognitive abilities were likened to the ones possessed by the [Imperial Saints](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOFE-8)</sup> The Drake of Shields was told by the Drake of Blades to ensure that the Sublime Brazier's existence was known only by the Dragonguard and forgotten by history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODo B-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSub Braz-4)</sup>

Many prominent figures active in the decade following the onset of the Planemeld were immortalized in a painting commemorating their adventures. Among them was the Drake of Blades, portrayed watching over the celebration.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

## Notes
- On the [March 15, 2019](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:ESO_Live_Loremaster_Leamon_Tuttle_Introduction) episode of [ESO Live](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:ESO_Live), writer [Leamon Tuttle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Leamon_Tuttle) confirmed that the Drake of Blades is intended to be on the [autism spectrum](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/autism_spectrum). This had previously been mentioned by game director [Matt Firor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Matt_Firor) in an [interview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Matt_Firor_%26_Rich_Lambert_Summerset_Interview) on April 7, 2018.
