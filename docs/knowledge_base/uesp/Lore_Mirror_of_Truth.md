# Mirror of Truth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/ON-furnishing-Mirror_of_Truth%2C_Replica.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Mirror_of_Truth%2C_Replica.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Mirror_of_Truth,_Replica.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Mirror_of_Truth,_Replica.jpg) Mirror of Truth replica *The cracks in this ornate mirror remind many of something they can't quite recollect. And you dare not tell them.*
—Replica Mirror of Truth description<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Replica-1)</sup> The Mirror of Truth was an artifact associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md). It was said that those who looked upon it would reflect upon their innermost desires and self.

It was created by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), the [Watchling](Lore_Watcher.md) [fate-auditor](Lore_Fate.md) Scruut, and Leramil the Wise, using a shard from a Primordial Shattered Titan—a powerful [Shattered Shard](Lore_Shattered_Shard.md) that was one of Ithelia's first creations—as the focus. The forging of the Mirror was achieved through a ritual that utilized [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md)'s blade [Abolisher](Lore_Abolisher.md), [Azura](Lore_Azura.md)'s [Lamp of Clarity](Lore_Lamp_of_Clarity.md), and [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md)'s Skein of Secrets.<sup>[[*citation needed*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Template:Fact)]</sup> It was crafted with a shard of Ithelia's essence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOT-2)</sup>

The Mirror was used by the Vestige on Ithelia herself during her invasion of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md), allowing her to recall her forgotten realization that her course would doom reality as [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) foresaw. This granted her the moment of clarity she needed to decide to abandon her plan, which would've resulted in the unraveling of reality through the catastrophic damage it was causing to Fate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Scruut-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Leramil-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ithelia-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ithelias Fury-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Fate Forgotten-7)</sup>
