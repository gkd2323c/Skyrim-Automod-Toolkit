Necromancer's Letter *A letter about Necromancers taking over Fort Snowhawk*
---
M'lady,

We have driven the last of the bandits from [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md). Regretfully, the North Tower suffered some major damage in the final assault, but repairs are already underway.

As you predicted, the Fort should be an ideal site for our experiments. Much of it is ruined, of course, but its close proximity to the northern road should ensure us a steady supply of test subjects. We hope to have some preliminary results in the next few weeks.
