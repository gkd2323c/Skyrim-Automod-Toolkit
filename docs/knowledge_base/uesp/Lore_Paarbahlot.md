[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Order_of_the_New_Moon_02.jpg) Paarbahlot together with Joorahmaar and Laatvulon Paarbahlot is a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Ambition-Wrath-Great" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shout-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-P-4)</sup> He is a green dragon, similar to Bahlokdaan.

Little is known of Paarbahlot's past. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), together with [Joorahmaar](Lore_Joorahmaar.md), he ventured to the New Moon Fortress in [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md) to meet with [Laatvulon](Lore_Laatvulon.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Paarbahlot-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Joorahmaar-6)</sup> During the meeting, Laatvulon promised power to both Joorahmaar and Paarbahlot,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OONM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Laatvulon-8)</sup> and ordered the recruits of the [Order of the New Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_New_Moon.md) to offer them their power with the use of the [aeonstone](Lore_Aeonstone.md). The ritual was interrupted by the members of [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md)'s reformed [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md). Following the disruption of the ritual, Paarbahlot, together with Joorahmaar, left the fortress of the New Moon Cult.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OONM-7)</sup> Although Joorahmaar was later seen assisting Laatvulon in the [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md), Paarbahlot's allegiances and status as of then were unknown.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TDA-9)</sup>
