**Chief-Crier** Adara'hai was a leading figure of the [Twilight Cantors](Lore_Twilight_Cantors.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Patch Notes2.3.5-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CK-2)</sup> She was responsible for several high-profile instances of [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) vanquishing around the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-It M-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CC-4)</sup>

## History
Little is known of Adara'hai's early life. She had a twin sister, Ka'ishka, with whom she maintained a close relationship despite their paths diverging: Adara'hai entering the life of a Twilight Cantor, while Ka'ishka chose to live in the [Black Heights](Lore_Black_Heights.md), living a comparatively simple life.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Adara.27hai-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ka.27ishka-6)</sup> At some point, she composed a song detailing the armaments of the dro-m'Athra, intending to make use of that knowledge in her fight against them.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CMDM-7)</sup>

### The Temple of Seven Riddles
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/ON-npc-Adara%27hai_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Adara%27hai_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Adara%27hai_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Adara%27hai_02.jpg) Adara'hai reversing the damage caused by Rakkhat *Duty brought Adara'hai here.
When dro-m'Athra pounce, we Cantors are called to sing them back to the void. The price of beauty is horror, you see? Var var var.*
—Adara'hai<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Adara.27hai-5)</sup> The Twilight Cantors were called to the Temple of Seven Riddles in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by Elsweyr's Moon Bishops in the wake of a disaster. The temple's abbot desecrated the ancient seals keeping the dro-m'Athra from invading the temple, allowing the dark spirits to flood the premises.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Patch Notes2.3.5-1)</sup> Adara'hai managed to restore the seals and vanquish the dro-m'Athra with the help of the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-It M-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Adara.27hai-5)</sup>

### Black Heights
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/ON-quest-Chiaroscuro_Crossroads_03.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Chiaroscuro_Crossroads_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Chiaroscuro_Crossroads_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Chiaroscuro_Crossroads_03.jpg) Adara'hai performing an exorcism *Her sister is a Twilight Cantor, she travels the world wielding the secrets of Azurah to drive darkness out of the world. This one's simple life cannot compare, but she cherishes it all the same*
—Ka'ishka<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ka.27ishka-6)</sup> After the incident at the Temple of Seven Riddles, Adara'hai received a letter from her sister regarding her chronically ill brother-in-law worsening condition.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Believing her husband, Vijari, was nearing the end of his life, Ka'ishka noticed that he was acting strangely, and insisted that more than just his illness was affecting his behavior. Adara'hai traveled south to [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md), where she met up with her sister to discuss the possibility that an exorcism may be required. It is here that the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) met with her and agreed to assist Adara'hai in her endeavor.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Adara.27hai-5)</sup>

During their investigation, they learned that the Wall of Life had been corrupted years prior by a dro-m'Athra who had been promised a life free of suffering by [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md). The creature sought to drag Vijari into the darkness, seeking to end the suffering brought about by his condition. To this end, it defiled the Wall of Life, painting over it with the essence of its pain and suffering, creating a connection between the wall and the [Dark Behind the World](Lore_Dark_Behind_the_World.md). As a result of this vandalism, countless innocent souls who had come to the wall for their end of life pilgrimage fell into Namiira's grasp and became dro-m'Athra themselves. With Ka'ishka's assistance and [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md)'s blessings, Vijari was able to restore the ancient mural and purge it of Namiira's influence while Adara'hai and the Vestige vanquished the dark spirits from [Black Heights](Lore_Black_Heights.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CC-4)</sup>

### Holy Sabbatical
At some point in 2E 582, Adara'hai set out on a holy sabbatical, under the blessing of Azurah. During her absence, the Twilight Cantors were temporarily led by High Cantor Viti.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CK-2)</sup>
