## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Avrusa Sarethi](Skyrim_Avrusa_Sarethi.md).
2. Collect 20 [Jazbay Grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md).
3. Return to Avrusa Sarethi.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Avrusa Sarethi](Skyrim_Avrusa_Sarethi.md) needs a large amount of [jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md) in order to cultivate [nirnroots](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md). She's running low and unable to search out the ingredient herself, as she is preoccupied with her energetic sister and farm chores. She asks you to hunt down twenty grapes for her current nirnroot crop. As Avrusa notes, jazbay grapes are somewhat hard to come by. The plants are indigenous to the volcanic tundra region of [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md), and while desolate, the tundra is fairly easy to traverse with a few lesser [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) and non-hostile [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md).

There are a few locations where it can be found in greater quantities than usual. Along with the quantities available, they are:

- 49 around [Steamcrag Camp](Skyrim_Steamcrag_Camp.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 47 around [Witchmist Grove](Skyrim_Witchmist_Grove.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 35 around [Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 33 around [the Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 31 around [Bonestrewn Crest](Skyrim_Bonestrewn_Crest.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 26 around [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md)), especially along the road heading southeast
- 25 around [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 24 around [Broken Limb Camp](Skyrim_Broken_Limb_Camp.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 23 around [Cronvangr Cave](Skyrim_Cronvangr_Cave.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 22 around [Riverside Shack](Skyrim_Riverside_Shack.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))

Once you have collected twenty samples, head back to Avrusa who will thank you for your aid and reward you with five random potions. You can return to [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md) periodically to harvest [Nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) and [Crimson Nirnroot](Skyrim_Crimson_Nirnroot.md).
