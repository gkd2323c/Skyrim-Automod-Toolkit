- *For 60 seconds, opponents in melee range take 8 points frost damage and Stamina damage per second*

**Frost Cloak** is an adept level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that surrounds the caster in frost, causing [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) to the health and stamina of any enemies within melee range.

## Effects
- [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), 8 pts in 10 ft for 60 sec

## Perks
- [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Augmented_Frost.md), raises damage to 10 at first rank or 12 at second rank.

## Notes
- You can only have one Cloak spell of any kind active at a time. Casting a second will cancel the first.
- It is a 'smart' spell and does not damage NPCs or followers.
- Dual casting significantly increases its range (4.84 times the covered area), but does not increase damage.
- The spell will continue to the end of its duration even if you sheathe your weapons. It even operates underwater, making it an effective means of dealing with [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), since you can't use other attacks or spells while swimming.
- One strategy for using this spell is to cast it and then simply run in tight circles around your enemies. They may be unable to keep up and get a hit in, and the frost will slowly take them out.
