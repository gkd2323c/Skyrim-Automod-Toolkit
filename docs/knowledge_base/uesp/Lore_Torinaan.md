This article is about the historical figure. For the ruins, see Torinaan (place).

High Lord Torinaan was a Legendary [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) folk hero of the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md). Called "**the Foresailor**", he is said to have been the first [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) to set foot on the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) at a site called Nine-Prow Landing on the northern coast of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AECXII-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EOTR-2)</sup> When the Aldmer settlers were threatened by beasts known as [Gheatus](Lore_Gheatus.md), [Ilyadi](Lore_Ilyadi.md), and [Welwa](Lore_Welwa.md), Torinaan used the spells of his ancestors to slay them and render the land safe.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLOF-3)</sup> He is credited as the founder of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), the first and oldest Elven city in the Isles.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLOF-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FHA-4)</sup> A complex of ruins bearing Torinaan's name, standing just southeast of Firsthold, is dedicated to worship of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) and is a traditional ritual site for Altmeri rulers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RJ-5)</sup>
