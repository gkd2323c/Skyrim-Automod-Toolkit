From the journal of Ultavius Celatus **by [Ultavius Celatus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ultavius_Celatus)** *An Imperial soldier's reasons for fighting in the Alliance War*
---
[![ON-concept-Imperial heavy armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/d/d3/ON-concept-Imperial_heavy_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Imperial_heavy_armor.png)
- Grandfather understood. I think he was even a little proud of me as he helped me don the armor he once wore into battle. Mother and father begged me to stay, but if I remain here and try to ignore the world like they have for so long, what legacy am I leaving behind? I want to be someone. I want to make a difference. One day, I want my children to run carefree through the streets of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) in the glory of an [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) restored.

- I'll do whatever it takes to make that happen, and I can't stand idly here and wait for the outcome of [this war](Lore_Alliance_War.md). I will stand and fight if only to drive back the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). But whose service should I swear my blade to? Who can restore the Empire that [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) deserves?
