# All-Mother

The All-Mother is a deity who has been evoked by [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) such as the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md) Archmagister [I'ric Harad Egun](Lore_I%27ric_Harad_Egun.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEIV-1)</sup> Anything beyond their mere existence is unknown, although the [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) worship a deity known as the [All-Father](Lore_All-Maker.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOASS-2)</sup>
