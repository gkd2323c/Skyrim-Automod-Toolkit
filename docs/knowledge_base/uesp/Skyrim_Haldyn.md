[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Haldyn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Haldyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haldyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Haldyn.jpg) Haldyn **Haldyn** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) who can be found on the island of [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md), far northeast of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). He is the leader of a group of pirates called the Blood Horkers, who have been harassing ships in the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).

Haldyn is clad in a set of leveled [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor), including a cuirass, a pair of boots, and a 50% chance of a pair of gauntlets. All his equipment can be up to [steel plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate) quality or [Nordic carved armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic) if Dragonborn is installed.

[Adelaisa Vendicci](Skyrim_Adelaisa_Vendicci.md) will instruct you to kill him in the quest [Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md) in order to stop the pirates from plundering any more ships. Haldyn is skilled in [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic and relies heavily on shock spells.
