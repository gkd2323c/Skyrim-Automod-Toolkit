# Madness Ore

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-item-Madness_Ore.jpg/200px-SR-item-Madness_Ore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Madness_Ore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Madness_Ore.jpg) Madness Ore [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-item-Madness_Ingot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Madness_Ingot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Madness_Ingot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Madness_Ingot.jpg) Madness Ingot **Madness Ore** is a flexible, brown metal that's marbled with glowing gold veins, and occurs naturally in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It is added to the game by the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It is used to create high quality [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md). Madness Ore itself is very rare, but can be found occasionally in [dungeon boss chests](Skyrim_Containers.md#Chest), and in the inventory of [Khajiit caravan traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) after receiving [Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md)'s [note on his new wares](Skyrim_Note_on_New_Wares.md).

- Madness Ore cannot be mined. It can only purchased from Khajiit traders or found as random loot.
- 1 Madness Ingot is obtained by [smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) 2 Madness Ores. They cannot be purchased.

## Madness Smithing
The secret to [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) Madness equipment is learned by reading [Evethra's Journal](Skyrim_Evethra%27s_Journal.md), obtained from a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Amber_and_Madness_Smithing.md) which is granted upon reading the [Note on Amber and Madness Ore](Skyrim_Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore.md). Crafting the weapons and armor also requires learning [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md), the third perk in the "heavy armor" branch of the smithing skill's perk tree. Being able to craft Madness equipment essentially requires a minimum Smithing skill of 80 to unlock Ebony Smithing, and Ebony Smithing's prerequisite perks must also be unlocked.

All Madness items can be tempered using 1 Madness ingot.

## Madness Armor
Madness armor is a type of heavy armor. The armor and shield have the highest base armor rating for their respective slots in the game.

## Bugs
- Wearing the Madness armor piece may cause the game to crash. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- Madness Gauntlets do not benefit from the [Fists of Steel](Skyrim_Fists_of_Steel.md) perk. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.318, fixes this bug.
- The Madness Sword's base value is incorrectly assigned at 250 gold, which is much lower than the Madness Dagger's base value (800 gold). - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
- The USSEP may reset the Madness Sword's base value back to 250 gold when it shouldn't have done that. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.1, addresses this issue. The USSEP forgot to carry over the correct base value added with the Official Patch. Both fixes have since been merged together.
