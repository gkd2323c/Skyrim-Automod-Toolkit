*Every leader that falls, every warrior that bleeds—the vateshran knows them all. Each who survive the Rites earns a vateshran song that will last forever.*
— [Rites Matron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rites_Matron) of the Spiritblood Clan **Vateshran** Aydolan **of the [Spiritblood Clan](Lore_Spiritblood_Clan.md)**, the **Spiritblood Champion**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Preview-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive-2)</sup> was a [Reachman](Lore_Reachmen.md) storyteller and historian who followed the [Carinar the Sharp](Lore_Carinar.md) after the [Quicktalon Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Quicktalon_Clan) was divided by the patricide. He was the creator, and one of the first champions of the [Vateshran's Rites](Lore_Vateshran%27s_Rites.md) performed in the ancient caves of the [Vateshran Hollows](Lore_Vateshran_Hollows.md), which later served as the clan's coming of age trials,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup> as well as the trials for the chieftains.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Preview-1)</sup> Following his death he remained on the [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) as ancestral spirit to oversee the trials and guide the generations of Spritblood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup>

## History

### Ancient Past
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/ON-load-Vateshran_Hollows.png/200px-ON-load-Vateshran_Hollows.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Vateshran_Hollows.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Vateshran_Hollows.png) Aydolan's greatest achievement - Vateshran Hollows Aydolan was born in the Reach as a member of the [Quicktalon Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Quicktalon_Clan) at some point before [1E 2502](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2502),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup> before the times "anyone can remember".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Preview-1)</sup> Already as a child he was an exceptionally skilled and determined hunter, fighting foes much bigger than himself, overcoming the hardships through swiftness and determination. One story from his childhood involved a hunt in which, as a boy, he tracked a [stag](Lore_Stag.md) large enough to feed his entire family for days, following it without sleep until exhaustion nearly overcame him. When he finally drew his bow, fully focused on the prey, a [bear](Lore_Bear.md) suddenly seized his leg in its jaws. What followed was a brutal struggle that lasted for hours, knife against tooth and claw, continuing until the sun began to set. At last, both hunter and beast collapsed from exhaustion. Sensing defeat, the bear rose and began to leave, and in that moment he cut its throat. His family ate well for days afterward, and the bear's pelt was fashioned into a fine cloak.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup>

Eventually, [Carinar the Sharp](Lore_Carinar.md), the son of the chieftain of the clan plotted against his father and killed him. His actions led to the sundering of the Quicktalon Clan, which split into two factions - one which supported Carinar and the other loyal to the deceased chieftain. Aydolan was among the group of supporters of the Carinar and followed his new chieftain south. He was singing tales of the triumph and strength of Carinar during this travel and in turn was given the role and the title of the [Vateshran](Lore_Vateshrans.md) - one of the keepers of ancient tales and stories of their clan and kin. Given that the clan was newly formed he effectively became the first of the clan's Vateshrans and remained the most famous among them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup>

One of his most known achievements was the creation of the Vateshran's Rites, a set of trials held in the ancient caves of the Vateshran Hollows, where the veil between the planes of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) and [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md) was thin. They were prepared for the Spiritblood Clan members as a rite of passage,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup> as well as the trial for many chieftains of the clans of the Reach.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Preview-1)</sup> Everyone who survives the Rites earns a vateshran song that will last forever and the title of Spritblood Champion. Those who passed the trial were considered an adult and fully-fledged members of the Spiritblood Clan.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESORites_Matron-4)</sup> He passed the trial himself and obtained the title of the Spiritblood Champion.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOLive-2)</sup>

At some point in history, Aydolan died, but he decided to remain on the Nirn as an ancestral spirit to guide the next generations of the Reachfolk,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup> a practice that was not unheard of among certain clans of the Reach.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GSOTR2-5)</sup> He was not the only Spiritblood clan member to continue to oversee the Rites after the death. A group of Reachfolk spirits maintained parts of the trial located in the Hunting Grounds.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESORC-6)</sup>

### 2nd Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/ON-interior-Vateshran_Hollows_12.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Vateshran_Hollows_12.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Vateshran_Hollows_12.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Vateshran_Hollows_12.jpg) The portal to the Champion's Circle [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/ON-statue-Totem_of_the_Brave.jpg/200px-ON-statue-Totem_of_the_Brave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Totem_of_the_Brave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Totem_of_the_Brave.jpg) One of the Totems of the Brave in the Vateshran Hollows Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) agreed to take part in the Vateshran's Rites, undertaking a journey through the realms of Oblivion. Aydolan guided them through the many trials, as he had guided previous aspirants. During the journey, he entertained his companion with tales from his past and reflections on Reachfolk philosophy, while also offering valuable advice about the dangers ahead. After the Vestige overcame all but the final challenge, Aydolan met them in the Champion's Circle and revealed that the last test was to defeat his spirit in combat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup>

However, the battle between Aydolan and the participant never took place, as the trial was interrupted by [Maebroogha the Void Lich](Lore_Maebroogha.md), who consumed Aydolan's soul and unmade his very being in order to be able to manifest in this realm.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MTVL-7)</sup> The Vestige slew the lich, but the spirit of the first Vateshran of the Spiritblood Clan was absorbed into the Void and lost forever.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VR-8)</sup>

### Legacy
Despite the death of the First Vateshran the practice of the Vateshran's Rites remained alive for generations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAydolan-3)</sup> Even after his soul was destroyed the trials remained active and were maintained by three sisters of the Spiritblood Clan: Binder in the Dark, Lamenter of the Slain, and Rites Matron.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESORites_Matron-4)</sup>

As a Spiritblood Champion who passed the rites he was, just like other warriors of the Spiritblood who felt defending the ways of their ancestors, was honored by having Totem of the Brave erected in his honor,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> and earned a vateshran song that was passed down and sung forever.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESORites_Matron-4)</sup>
