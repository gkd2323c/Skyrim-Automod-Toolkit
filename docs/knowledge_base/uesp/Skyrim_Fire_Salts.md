## Ingredients
50 guaranteed samples found in 30 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 3 in [the Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 in [the Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 3 in [Nightcaller Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightcaller_Temple) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in [Septimus Signus's Outpost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 in [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 2 in [Forelhost Stronghold](Skyrim_Forelhost_Stronghold.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 inside [Labyrinthian Chasm](Skyrim_Labyrinthian_Chasm.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1–4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Can be created at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) from: 1 [Soul Gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) + 1 [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) + 1 [Ruby](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby)

## Creatures
Fire salts are dropped by both [flame atronachs](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md) and [flame thralls](Skyrim_Flame_Thrall_(creature).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Stoking the Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stoking_the_Flames)**: Find 10 fire salts to restore [Balimund](Skyrim_Balimund.md)'s forge. - 10 samples are needed for this quest.

## Notes
- Fire salts are needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create various flame atronach-related items, namely: a hostile [flame atronach](Skyrim_Flame_Atronach.md), a [Scroll of Conjure Flame Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Flame_Atronach), a [Staff of the Flame Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Flame_Atronach), or a *[Spell Tome: Conjure Flame Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Flame_Atronach)*.
- Once certain schematics are discovered during the [Ancient Technology](Skyrim_Ancient_Technology.md) quest, one pinch of fire salts is required for crafting ten [Exploding Steel Bolts of Fire](Skyrim_Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Fire.md) and ten [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Fire](Skyrim_Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Fire.md) at [Gunmar's Forge](Skyrim_Gunmar%27s_Forge.md).<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup>
- Fire salts can be used in [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) to cook [hot soups](Skyrim_Hot_Soups.md). The soups you can create with them are [hot apple cabbage stew](Skyrim_Hot_Apple_Cabbage_Stew.md), [hot beef stew](Skyrim_Hot_Beef_Stew.md), [hot cabbage potato soup](Skyrim_Hot_Cabbage_Potato_Soup.md), [hot cabbage soup](Skyrim_Hot_Cabbage_Soup.md), [hot clam chowder](Skyrim_Hot_Clam_Chowder.md), [hot crab stew](Skyrim_Hot_Crab_Stew.md), [hot creamy crab bisque](Skyrim_Hot_Creamy_Crab_Bisque.md), [hot Elsweyr fondue](Skyrim_Hot_Elsweyr_Fondue.md), [hot horker stew](Skyrim_Hot_Horker_Stew.md), [hot horker and ash yam stew](Skyrim_Hot_Horker_and_Ash_Yam_Stew.md), [hot ironwood soup](Skyrim_Ironwood_Soup.md), [hot potato crab chowder](Skyrim_Hot_Potato_Crab_Chowder.md), [hot potato soup](Skyrim_Hot_Potato_Soup.md), [hot roasted tomato crab bisque](Skyrim_Hot_Roasted_Tomato_Crab_Bisque.md), [hot tomato soup](Skyrim_Hot_Tomato_Soup.md), [hot vegetable soup](Skyrim_Hot_Vegetable_Soup.md), and [hot venison stew](Skyrim_Hot_Venison_Stew.md).<sup>([CC](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md))</sup>
