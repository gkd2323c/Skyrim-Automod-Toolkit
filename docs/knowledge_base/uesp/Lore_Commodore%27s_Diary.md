Commodore's Diary **by [Commodore Malfairre](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Commodore_Malfairre)** *A commodore's plan to evade his pursuers using deep druid magic*
---
We terrorized the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) for more than a decade and now these witless ingrates lose their spines because we're a tad outnumbered. "They need a miracle," they say. Well, I'll give them a miracle, all right. There's power in these baubles of the [druids](Lore_Druids.md). I don't speak their old, forgotten tongue, but I know the language of magic well enough to understand this tome holds the answer.

[Druids of the deep](Lore_Deep_Druids.md) called on powers with no equal, they say. That holds true, by my reckoning. There's a ritual in this tome that will make my fleet invincible. So long as the spell holds and the idols remain intact, our crews will never truly die. We'll make those [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) ring-kissers rue the day they thought they could punish us for taking our due.
