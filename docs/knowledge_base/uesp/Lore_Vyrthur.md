Vyrthur was a [Snow Elf](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) and the Arch-Curate of [Auri-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auri-El) who for millennia sought vengeance against his one-time patron for allowing him to become a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md). He was the brother of Knight-Paladin [Gelebor](Lore_Gelebor.md), the only other uncorrupted Snow Elf known to have survived beyond the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dawnguard-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vyrthur-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOS-3)</sup> The entire [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md) was once ruled by the Arch-Curate Vyrthur, assisted by his Prelates and Paladins.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Gelebor met the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) and requested that the hero kill Vyrthur in exchange for [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md), which was in Vyrthur's possession. Gelebor unknowingly playing right into his brother's hands, for the Last Dragonborn unwittingly brought the Arch-Curate exactly what he wanted: [Serana](Lore_Serana.md), a [Daughter of Coldharbour](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daughters_of_Coldharbour), a being of the purest evil in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Vyrthur sought revenge on Auri-El through the sundering of the sun, which serves as the connection between [Aetherius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherius), the plane of the gods, and [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), which would thus eliminate Auri-El's influence on Nirn. This was to be achieved through the corruption of Auri-El's bow, accomplished by soaking it in the blood of a Daughter of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). He had long ago publicized a prophecy called the [Tyranny of the Sun](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md), that was later revealed within the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), with the intent of drawing such a person to him. Fortunately, Serana and her companion were able to defeat and kill Vyrthur before he could bring his vengeance to fruition.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dawnguard-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vyrthur-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TOS-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Prior to the release of *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)*, strings pulled from *Skyrim*'s [Patch 1.5.26](Skyrim_Patch.md#Version_1.5.26.0) indicated that a Snow Elf Prince would appear as a character. An [Ancient Falmer Crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Falmer_Crown) asset was also made for the Prince, but ultimately went unused. As development continued, the Prince character evolved into Vyrthur as a former archpriest of Auri-El rather than a prince, though remnants of the original idea remained in the form of Vyrthur's throne.
