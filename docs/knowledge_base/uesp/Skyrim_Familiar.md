**Conjure Familiar** is a novice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a Familiar to aid you in combat. It is likely your first summoning spell, particularly if you are a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md), as this is one of their starting spells. The Familiar appears as a ghostly wolf, and is limited to melee attacks only.

## Effects
- [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Conjuration Dual Casting](Skyrim_Conjuration_Dual_Casting.md), multiply spell cost by 2.8 (base 299.6, before reductions) in exchange for increasing the duration to 132 seconds.

## Notes
- The Familiar is technically considered a Daedra, which only means that it is subject to [Banish Daedra](Skyrim_Banish_Daedra.md) spells.
- Though it appears as a ghost, it is *not* undead, and thus not subject to spells that affect the undead.
