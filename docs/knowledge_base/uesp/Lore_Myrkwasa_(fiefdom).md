[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/DF-map-Myrkwasa.png/200px-DF-map-Myrkwasa.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Myrkwasa.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Myrkwasa.png) Myrkwasa ca. 3E 405 Myrkwasa is a region in northwestern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the greater [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region of northwest [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It is divided by the arid, desert region further into the mainland and the tropical wet-dry climate of the coast,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMap-1)</sup> where a sparse grassland laden most of the province's shore.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup>

## History
When the Fiefdom of Myrkwasa was founded is unknown, but the region's name is associated with the coastal desert region in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md)'s vicinity.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMyrkwasa-3)</sup> The polity is mainly populated by the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of Hammerfell but hosts other races and subcultures, such as the [barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md) and [giants](Lore_Giant.md) who live in sizeable strongholds. [Harpy](Lore_Harpy.md) nests can also be found in Myrkwasa.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-4)</sup> The region is also the home of the [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md) clan of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), the major bloodline in the region and the Tide Witches, a [witches coven](Lore_Witches.md) based in central Myrkwasa, near Rhanotejur.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFMyrkwasa-5)</sup>

By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Fiefdom of Myrkwasa was ruled by an unnamed Lord and its patron deity was [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), who is represented through the [Temple of Kynareth](Lore_Temple_of_Kynareth.md). The region had only two cities, the capital of the same name and Zagiladax; much closer to the coast. Myrkwasa was neighbored by several regions, namely the unconquered [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) to the south, [Kairou](Lore_Kairou.md) to the west, [Pothago](Lore_Pothago.md) to the northwest, and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) to the northeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMap-1)</sup>

In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), Myrkwasa was conquered by the Kingdom of Sentinel after the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) changed the Iliac Bay region over a couple of days. At the time, it was ruled by the Regent Queen, [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md). Since then, all of northern Hammerfell had fallen into the kingdom's control.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWITW-6)</sup>
