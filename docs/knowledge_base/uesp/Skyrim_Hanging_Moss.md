## Ingredients
10-11<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 7-8<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations:

- 3 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [the White Phial](Skyrim_The_White_Phial_(place).md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 1 in [Haemar's Cavern](Skyrim_Haemar%27s_Cavern.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 1 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 1 in the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 1 in [Potema's Catacombs](Skyrim_Potema%27s_Catacombs.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 1 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 1 in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
