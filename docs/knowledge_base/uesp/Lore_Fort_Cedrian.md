## History
Fort Cedrian has existed since at least the sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Its crumbling remains could be viewed from the western bank of the Corbolo's [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) -infested waters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONcedrian-1)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Fort Cedrian's guard towers and turrets had crumbled away entirely, leaving very few above-ground stoneworks remaining. Only the underground part of the main fort and the mausoleum could still be entered and was then in use by a group of marauders, making the ruins a dangerous place for treasure hunters and explorers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBCedrian-2)</sup>
