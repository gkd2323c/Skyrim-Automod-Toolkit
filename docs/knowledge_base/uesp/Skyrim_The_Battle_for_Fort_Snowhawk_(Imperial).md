## Quick Walkthrough
1. Report for duty to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at the [Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Imperial_Camp).
2. Join the Legion soldiers gathering for the attack.
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Report for Duty
When speaking to [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) he will make a bitter comment about your decision to give up [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). He will then direct you to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) at the [Hjaalmarch Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hjaalmarch_Imperial_Camp). She will give you orders to rendezvous with a group of Imperial soldiers preparing to attack [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) and to assist in the assault.

### Join the Soldiers Preparing the Assault
Make your way to Fort Snowhawk. The fort is located a small ways west of hold capital [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), and the soldiers are waiting just southwest of the fort. As you approach the group, they will draw their weapons and attack the fort.

### Take the Fort
Assist your fellow soldiers in clearing the fort of Stormcloak rebels while the game updates you with the percentage of enemies remaining. The battle takes place in the exterior parts of the fort only, in the courtyard and on the walls.

Once you have killed all Stormcloak rebels, the quest will be completed. The [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md) quest will update and prompt you to return to General Tullius to report your success and receive your reward.

## Notes
- If [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) is given to the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) then this quest will not be given to you.
- Completing this quest will send [Sorli the Builder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) and her courtiers back to [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md), and re-install [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of Hjaalmarch.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md#Bugs) bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.
