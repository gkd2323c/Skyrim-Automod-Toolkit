[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-npc-Student.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Student.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Student.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Student.jpg) Student The **Student**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md), can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Magic_Student) in or near any major settlement (except [Orc Strongholds](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) or the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md)) after you have learned a [Ward](Skyrim_Ward_(spells).md) spell. As soon as the student sees you, he will ask for a simple task. He seems to see you as his hero, and simply wishes to learn how a ward spell works to improve his chances of joining the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). If you accept the student's request, he will instruct you to cast a ward spell and will then cast his own spell against your ward.

He wears leveled [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md), possibly hooded and/or enchanted, along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000c5d12). He may wield a leveled [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md) coupled with a possibly enchanted dagger that can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric_Weapons.md) in quality. He may also carry random warlock items and up to two random copies of [atronach forge recipes](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge_Recipe.md), which may be written in the [Daedric alphabet](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md). He knows the spells [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), and [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
He will run up to you excitedly, greeting you with:

- *"I've finally found you! I want to join the College of Winterhold. But I need help with my wards. Can you teach me?"*

- - **Sure, I can teach you.**
- *"Really? Great! Okay. So, first, you raise your ward and I'll throw a little spell at it. That way I can see the 'right' way to do things."*

- - **Maybe some other time.**
- *"Oh. All right... I guess I'll just keep practicing on my own. Sorry to bother you."*

If you offer to help him, he will prepare his spell, telling you:

- *"Get your ward ready."*

If you have not readied your ward:

- *"Come on. I don't want to hit you until you have your ward ready."*

Once you've cast a ward:

- *"Great! Now I'm going to cast some spells at you. Keep your ward up so I can study it."*

As he casts a spell against your ward, usually Flames:

- *"I think I see what I was doing wrong... All right. That should do it. I think I'm ready to try the College again. Thanks!"*

## Notes
- His spell has a random combination of effects.
- You do not need to be a member of the College of Winterhold to encounter the student.

## Bugs
- Sometimes, when accepting the student's request, he will get stuck in a loop, attacking you with his spell continuously. - A possible fix to this is to move around a little bit with your ward(s) up.
- The student may get attacked by the guards and NPCs that you have helped upon attacking you if encountered in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).
- Despite being able to view the items in the student's inventory, you cannot successfully [pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) the items. The pickpocket attempt may seem successful; however, the items stolen will not appear in your inventory afterwards. <sup>**?**</sup>
