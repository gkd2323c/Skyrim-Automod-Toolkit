[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/BM-place-Mortrag_Glacier.jpg/200px-BM-place-Mortrag_Glacier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Mortrag_Glacier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-place-Mortrag_Glacier.jpg) Mortag Glacier The Mortrag Glacier was a large glacier once located along the northwestern coast of the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) in the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md). [Castle Karstaag](Lore_Castle_Karstaag.md) was located just east of it. The glacier was hollowed out and a [Daedric Ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) was built within its interior, and it was used to host the [Hunter's Game](Lore_Hunter%27s_Game.md) during the [Bloodmoon Prophecy](Lore_Bloodmoon_Prophecy.md) of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). The icy tunnels were filled with [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md), the Hounds of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). The Prince kidnapped four champions from the island and brought them to the glacier, to act as the Prey. The glacier was destroyed when an Aspect of Hircine was defeated by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), ending the Hunt and marking the first time that the Prey succeeded in killing the Hunter. The glacier subsequently collapsed, becoming a jagged ice field.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup>

By the Fourth Era, the remnants of Mortrag Glacier would become known as [Mortrag Peak](Skyrim_Mortrag_Peak.md), one of the highest points on Solstheim. [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) was built on the now-exposed shoreline just south of the remaining glacial ice, and [Horker Stalhrim Island](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md#Horker_Iceberg) formed out of one of the ice floes where two [Stalhrim](Lore_Stalhrim.md) -containing coffins tumbled out of the glacier. <sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODB-2)</sup>
