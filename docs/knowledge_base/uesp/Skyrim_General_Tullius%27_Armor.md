[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-item-General_Tullius%27_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-General_Tullius%27_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-General_Tullius%27_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-General_Tullius%27_Armor.jpg) General Tullius' Armor General Tullius' Armor is the armor used by the eponymous [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md). The most distinguishing trait of this item is its appearance, with its gold trim and the ornate [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) symbols on it distinguish its owner as one of the highest ranking members of the Legion. The defensive capabilities of this armor can be improved at a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot).
