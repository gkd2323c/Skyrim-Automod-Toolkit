*Alchemy description:* **Restoration spells are <mag>% stronger for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Restoration spells cost <mag>% less to cast.**

There are two distinct **Fortify [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md)** effects: one from [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) -created potions and one granted by [enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) armor. Their effects are as follows.

## Alchemy
In potions, Fortify Restoration increases the magnitude of Restoration magic.

The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a potion of Fortify Restoration:

- [Abecean Longfin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Abecean_Longfin)
- [Cyrodilic Spadetail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail)
- [Hackle-Lo Leaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hackle-Lo_Leaf)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**1.25** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.27** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))
- [Pygmy Sunfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pygmy_Sunfish)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile)
- [Small Antlers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers)
- [Small Pearl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Pearl)
- [Yellow Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> (**1.25** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.27** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))

## Enchanting
In enchantments, Fortify Restoration decreases the [cost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Cost) of Restoration spells, and the use cost of Restoration based enchantments.

The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Restoration if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Circlets, Helmets, and Rings *[of Restoration](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Restoration)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Restoration*, *of Major Restoration*, *of Extreme Restoration*, *of Eminent Restoration*, and *of Peerless Restoration.*
- [The Bond of Matrimony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bond_of_Matrimony) - The Bond of Matrimony can be disenchanted, but the underlying effect is just a standard Fortify Restoration effect instead of the different effect that might be inferred from the name *Blessing of Mara.* Also, this effect cannot be applied to any item after it is learned.

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md)
- [Archmage's Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Archmage%27s_Robes)
- [Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots_of_the_Crusader), [Cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cuirass_of_the_Crusader), [Helm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helm_of_the_Crusader), and [Gauntlets of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>
- [Reforged Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Boots_of_the_Crusader), [Reforged Cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cuirass_of_the_Crusader), [Reforged Helm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helm_of_the_Crusader) and [Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>
- [Diadem of the Savant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Diadem_of_the_Savant)
- [Mentor's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mentor%27s_Ring)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Nahkriin](Skyrim_Nahkriin_(item).md)

The various Robes of Restoration use a different variant of the Fortify Restoration effect, and the [Saarthal Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saarthal_Amulet) uses a third variant

## Blessings
- The [Blessing of Mara](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Mara.md) provides 10% Fortify Restoration (restoration spells cost 10% less magicka to cast).

## Notes
- Notably, three ingredients for a potion of this effect ([Cyrodilic Spadetail](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cyrodilic_Spadetail), [Abecean Longfin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Abecean_Longfin), and [Small Antlers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers)) can be found in and along rivers; by following a large river downstream, players can collect dozens of potions' worth of these three ingredients quickly. An even easier method of collecting the Spadetails and Longfins is to take them from the fish barrels in the docks just outside of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), or by using the fish hatchery available at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>. [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) also offers this effect, and can be purchased at nearly any tavern and are found in fish barrels as well. [Yellow Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> has a stronger effect and 27 plants (55 with Special Edition) are available in the [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md).

## Bugs
- Fortify Restoration potions can be used to increase other [Fortify Skill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Skill) enchantments. This creates a game-breaking exploit that is discussed [here](Skyrim_Glitches.md#Infinitely_Powerful_Items). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.
- Drinking a Fortify Restoration potion before taking the perks [Extra Pockets](Skyrim_Extra_Pockets.md) and [Recovery](Skyrim_Recovery.md) will increase the effects of the perks permanently.
- Drinking a Fortify Restoration potion before activating [The Lady Stone](Skyrim_The_Lady_Stone.md), [The Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) or [The Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md) will increase their effects until another stone is activated. The description in the 'Active Effects' list doesn't show the increased effect magnitude.
- Drinking a Fortify Restoration potion before activating any of the [Shrine Blessings](Skyrim_Blessings.md) will increase their effects until the blessing is removed or refreshed, see [bugs](Skyrim_Blessings.md#Bugs). The description in the 'Active Effects' list reflects the increased effect magnitude.
- Drinking a Fortify Restoration potion before equipping any [Amulet of a Divine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_the_Divines) will increase its effect until unequipped. The description in the inventory and 'Active Effects' list shows the boosted effect (the only exception is the Amulet of Talos, which, due to its unique enchantment, does not show boosted effects in both the inventory and the 'Active Effects', though the Shouts sub-menu shows the shortened cooldowns properly), but the inventory description only lasts as long as the potion does, however the 'Active Effects' boosted description lasts as long as the amulet is equipped.
- Drinking a Fortify Restoration potion before equipping any enchanted apparel will increase its effect until unequipped. The description in the 'Active Effects' list properly reflects the increased effect magnitude, though in the inventory, the description reflects the increased effect magnitude only as long as the potion lasts.
