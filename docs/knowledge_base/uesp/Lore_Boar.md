Boars are furry wild [swine](Lore_Mammal.md#Swine) with dangerous tusks that are related to [domestic pigs](Lore_Pig.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hybrid-3)</sup> Boars are commonly hunted by various cultures of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) for their [hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bristleback_Leather), [meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boar_Meat), and [tusks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boar_Tusk), as well as for sport.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Their heads are popular hunting trophies in [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Heads-5)</sup> Roast boar is a popular [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) [dish](Lore_Nord_Cuisine.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Some species of boar are even kept as pets or mounts.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FU-8)</sup> Wild [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) boars have been bred with domestic pigs to create hybrid bristlegut pigs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Hybrid-3)</sup>

## Species

### Woodland Boar
Common woodland boars (or wild boars)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> are found throughout the wilds of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). They have tough hides, making them surprisingly dangerous despite their compact size.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-OBPrima-10)</sup><sup>:45</sup> These wild boars are sometimes captured and used as combatants in the famous [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) [Arena](Lore_Imperial_City.md#Arena_District).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

- **Found in:** [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Boar), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Wild Boar), Betrayal of the Second Era

### [Bristleback](Lore_Bristleback.md)
[Bristlebacks](Lore_Bristleback.md) or **Tusked Bristlebacks** (also sometimes called **Razorbacks**) are large tusked boars that can be found on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), and are known for their amazing ability to reflect magical effects used against them. The fur along their spines is barbed and stiff, lending to their name. They are often used by [Rieklings](Lore_Riekling.md), who have bred the creatures to be even more warlike and dangerous, as mounts. Their preferred climate is snowy mountaintops and temperatures well below freezing.

- **Found in:** [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Tusked_Bristleback), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Bristleback.md)<sup>([DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md))</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bristleback), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Tusked Bristleback), Betrayal of the Second Era

### [Daggerback](Lore_Daggerback.md)
[Daggerback](Lore_Daggerback.md) are a type of boar with quills found along their backs. They can be found in many places throughout Tamriel, including [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md), [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) and [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md). Their meat is said to have a strong taste, and is said to be the best in stew.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-AAIT-13)</sup>

### [Warthog](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Warthog)
[Warthogs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Warthog) are a type of swine known to be kept as exotic pets.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FU-8)</sup> The witches of the [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) supposedly could turn people into warthogs.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>
