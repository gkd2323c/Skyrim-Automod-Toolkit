Attrebus was a warlord and one of the many pretender [emperors](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). At some point after the death of Potentate [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md), he took control of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and made himself emperor. During his brief reign, he expelled the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) from Cyrodiil to rid the province of the foreigners who had ruled it during the first half of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup> Thousands of Akaviri refugees fled into [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and eventually settled in the [Rim Territories](Lore_Rim_Territories.md), where they founded the town of [Hakoshae](Lore_Hakoshae.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OABR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOMFDd-4)</sup> Ultimately, Attrebus lasted no longer than most of the pretender emperors of the Interregnum, and his fate is unknown.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup>
