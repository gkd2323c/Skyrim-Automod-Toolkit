# Koron's Perdurable Prison

Koron's Perdurable Prison is a spell named after [Koron](Lore_Koron.md). It was mentioned in the tale about [Prince Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md). It was also referred to simply as **Koron's Prison**. This spell was used by [Vizier Hafzifeh al-Yath](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Petty-Vizier_Hafzifeh_al-Yath) to bind a [Mournful Aegis](Lore_Mournful_Aegis.md) to his turban. The Vizier was known for his deep interest in [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) [magic](Lore_Magic.md) and for studying *[Principles of Conjuration](Lore_Principles_of_Conjuration.md)* by [Corvus Direnni](Lore_Corvus_Direnni.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PH-1)</sup> Corvus himself was renowned for his work on spells associated with Koron, such as [Koron's Peremptory Summons](Lore_Koron%27s_Peremptory_Summons.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOT-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POC-3)</sup>
