## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Elmus](Skyrim_Elmus.md) in [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md) before you decide to attack the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) or the [Rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md).
2. Go to the [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) and retrieve one bottle of [Ashfire Mead](Skyrim_Ashfire_Mead.md).
3. Head back to Elmus for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Find [Elmus](Skyrim_Elmus.md) at [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md) and talk to him. He requests that you bring him a bottle of [Ashfire Mead](Skyrim_Ashfire_Mead.md) from the [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md), which has been taken over by [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md). After that, travel over to the mead hall. If you have never visited it before, you will meet a riekling outside, trying to lead you to his tribe's [chief](Skyrim_Riekling_Chief.md). Just head inside, take at least one bottle of Ashfire Mead, and return to Elmus. Give him the mead and receive your reward.

## Notes
- If Thirsk is reclaimed for the Nords during [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md) or if [Elmus](Skyrim_Elmus.md) dies, this quest fails.
- Any Ashfire Mead will do; the bottle you give Elmus does not need to be from Thirsk.
