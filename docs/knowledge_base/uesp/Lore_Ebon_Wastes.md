[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/AR-place-Ebon_Wastes.jpg/200px-AR-place-Ebon_Wastes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Ebon_Wastes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Ebon_Wastes.jpg) Ebon Wastes (Arena) The Ebon Wastes is a settlement found in the region of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md), in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The village was built from the **Ebon Crypt**, the former mausoleum of a noble family called the [Gautiers](Lore_Gautier.md), who hailed from [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md). When it was taken over by a [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md), it was subsequently abandoned.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup>

## History
The site of the modern-day settlement once contained the Ebon Crypt, the burial place for the Gautier family of Camlorn. But in time, the crypt was inhabited by the necromancer, Thrandyke the Repugnant, who used the many bodies for his various experiments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup> It was later abandoned at some point, becoming a local legend across the surrounding region for containing a dark creature of immeasurable power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BOEC-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the local legend garnered the interest of the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md) of [Reachman](Lore_Reachman.md), who were invading Glenumbra during this time. Once they took hold of nearby [Cath Bedraud](Lore_Cath_Bedraud.md), the cult soon explored the Ebon Crypt to take hold of the ancient power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BOEC-2)</sup> The spellcaster, Shorrin Thorncastle was able to summon this dark power, a hulking [Bone Colossus](Lore_Bone_Colossus.md), the Ebon Lord. Their operation ended when an [adventurer](Lore_Soulless_One.md) delved into the Ebon Crypt and defeated both Thorncastle and the Ebon Lord.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup> The earliest reference to the settlement called Ebon Wastes is in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), it was south of Camlorn and the Skeffington Wood.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGMap-4)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Ebon Wastes was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Dunyrick and it had a rivalry with [Moonguard](Lore_Moonguard.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AR-5)</sup> Ebon Wastes was neighbored by several settlements, including [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) to the north and the [Ilessen Hills](Lore_Ilessen_Hills.md) to the south.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARMap-6)</sup>
