## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Hilund](Skyrim_Hilund.md) in [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md).
2. Collect fifty [Riekling spears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Spear) from various [Riekling](Skyrim_Riekling.md) dwellings.
3. Return to Hilund for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Hilund's Idea
After the quest [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md), the Nord inhabitants will start over inside [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md), although [Hilund](Skyrim_Hilund.md) knows that old habits die hard and that the Nords can fall back into coziness at any time. When asked how the hunt is going, she will tell you about an idea she had to prevent future [Riekling](Skyrim_Riekling.md) attacks: *"To tell you the truth, I've been a little distracted. I've got this... idea. I want to ring the whole hall with their little spears. Mocking us, from the past. Making us work harder. Fight better. The only problem is, I'm going to need a lot of spears. A lot. So, if you could find a bunch of them for me, that would make it easier on me."* After this speech, the quest will start right away. You will need a total of 50 [riekling spears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Spear) so she can decorate the hall.

### Find the Spears
Riekling spears are the preferred ranged weapon used by rieklings all over [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). The spears can be picked up from the ground once they have been thrown or, alternatively, can be found on the bodies of defeated rieklings (chance of up to 8 spears on all "missile" variants). Note that you will have to keep track of the amount of riekling spears in your inventory yourself, as the game will not even alert you when you reach the required amount of 50 spears. ### Redecorating Thirsk
Once you have acquired the 50 spears, return to Hilund in Thirsk Mead Hall. When you hand her the spears, she will assure you that you brought enough: *"Oh, I'm sure it will be. Give them here, and I'll get to work soon!"* She will then hand you a leveled amount of gold as reward. When you return later, you will notice that Hilund was serious. Outside the hall will be a full decoration of carefully placed riekling spears, a constant reminder to the Nords of past events, and a warning to all rieklings to stay away from Thirsk Mead Hall.
