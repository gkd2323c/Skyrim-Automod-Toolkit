[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Guard.jpg) Silver-Blood Guard **Silver-Blood Guards** are mercenaries hired by the [Silver-Bloods](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md) as guards in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md).

They wear a set of [steel armor](Skyrim_Steel_Armor.md) without a helmet, and carry a [steel shield](Skyrim_Steel_Shield.md), a [steel mace](Skyrim_Steel_Mace.md), a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) and a dozen [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md). They also have a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md) and the [key to Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Key_to_Cidhna_Mine.md).

## Dialogue
If you approach them they may say the following:

- *"You mess with the Silver-Blood family, you end up here."*
- *"Now this is real guard duty. Just throw them in the mine and be done with it."*
- *"I catch you stealing anything, I'll break your neck."*
