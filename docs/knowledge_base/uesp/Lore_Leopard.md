Leopards are a type of elusive,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSpotted Snow Senche-Leopard-1)</sup> large, predatory [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in the jungled regions of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Senche-Leopard-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONSenche Leopard-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONClouded Senche-Leopard-4)</sup> with **Snow Leopards** being found in colder regions such as [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Snow Leopard-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSpotted Snow Senche-Leopard-1)</sup> Leopards are known for their distinctive spotted patterns,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLeopard Spots-6)</sup> which aid them with blending into their surroundings in their forest homes.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tenmar Marks-7)</sup>

Many of the cats known as leopards in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Leopard**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Senche-Leopard-2)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-8)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-8)</sup> Leopards and senche-leopards are among the most feared predators of the [Tenmar Forest](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md) of Elsweyr.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONClouded Senche-Leopard-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tenmar Marks-7)</sup>

Many [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have leopard-like fur patterns.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLeopard Spots-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tenmar Marks-7)</sup> Some breeds of [snowy sabre cat](Lore_Snowy_Sabre_Cat.md) are named for their resemblance to snow leopards.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

## Variants
Clouded Senche-Leopards differ from the typical rosette fur pattern by having large cloud-like circle patterns on their fur. They are known for being intelligent yet stubborn.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONClouded Senche-Leopard-4)</sup> The Spotted Snow Senche-Leopard is a rare variety of senche-leopard. It is notable for its white fur.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSpotted Snow Senche-Leopard-1)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
Senche-Leopards are often used as mounts in Elsweyr. Common Senche-Leopards are bred as mounts for bearing an armed warrior while slinking through the steamy jungles of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md). Khajiiti riders also found it had broader applicability, and use it both as a fast courser for couriers, and as an elegant town mount for young Elsweyr nobles.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONSenche Leopard-3)</sup> The Clouded Senche-Leopard is a strong yet nimble mount, bred for hunting in the dappled shadows of the Tenmar forest in central Elsweyr. The Clouded is intelligent but stubborn, and will usually cooperate only with a rider it knows well, making them unsuitable as mounts in the Senche-Cavalry.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONClouded Senche-Leopard-4)</sup>

Leopards have some significance in Skyrim. In a dubious myth surrounding [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) and his [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md), "Khaalthhe the [Lynx](Lore_Lynx.md) -or-Leopard" and "Bjorga-mawr the Definitely-a-Leopard" are listed among his pets of renown.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-FHMC-11)</sup> In modern Skyrim, worked strips of snow leopard hide treated with [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) oil are used to polish blades.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Snow Leopard-5)</sup>
