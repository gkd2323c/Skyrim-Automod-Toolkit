[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/ON-place-Argent_Mine.jpg/200px-ON-place-Argent_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Argent_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Argent_Mine.jpg) The Argent Mine The Argent Mine was a [silver](Lore_Silver.md) mine located in southern [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), though its resources were said to be long exhausted by the late sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md#Sixth_Century).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOArgent Mine-1)</sup> Along with silver ore, the rare metal known as [Liquid Silver](Lore_Liquid_Silver.md) was also found there.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LS-2)</sup>

Some time after [2E 428](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_428), the forces of the [Order of the Silver Dawn](Lore_Silver_Dawn.md) were lead to the mine by their founder Adeliza Silver-Axe, whose family had owned the mine. The Order was a group dedicated to hunting [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and the Argent Mine became their secret base of operations. The mine's resources were turned to creating silver weaponry and armor to aid in the Order's fight against werewolves.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PJ-3)</sup> It was also where the Banner of the Order of the Silver Dawn was kept, a sacred relic of the organization.

For decades the Silver Dawn operated from their secret stronghold until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Magnar Child-Eater and his werewolf pack rampaged throughout High Rock and Wrothgar leading to a several-year conflict between them and the Order, with many casualties on both sides.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PJ-3)</sup> This conflict came to a bloody end when Magnar and his pack invaded the mine and slaughtered most of the Silver Dawn's forces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESODirdre-4)</sup> While the banner of the Order was eventually recovered<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSilver Linings-5)</sup>, the survivors of the Silver Dawn are thought to have abandoned the mine.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESODirdre-4)</sup>
