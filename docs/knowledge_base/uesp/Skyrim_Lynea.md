That leaves you with the task of teaching her.

## Related Quests
- **[Private Lessons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Lessons)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Teach Lynea how to fish.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
At the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in Markarth:

**You're the one in search of a fishing instructor?** *"Indeed."* **I know just the spot. We can fish off the docks near the Warrens.** *"If that's what you want. I'll see you there, soon."* Go to the docks near the Warrens and speak to her:

*"Is there anything else you need?"* **No, let's begin.** *"Of course."* Once you have caught three fish in front of Lynea, she will say:

*"Well now. I guess I owe you my thanks."* **Did you enjoy the lesson?** *"I couldn't be more pleased. Take this small token of my appreciation."* **As much as I enjoy fishing, I have other things to do. Pay up.** *"You could have just asked."* She will then give you her [amulet of Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynea%27s_Amulet_of_Dibella) as a reward.
