Kronin was a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md)<sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Chimer)</sup> noble from the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup> In life, he was the King of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md)<sup>[[Note 2]](#intnote_King)</sup> and a member of the [Ra'athim Clan](Lore_Ra%27athim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup> Kronin had two sons, [Cruethys](Lore_Cruethys.md) and [Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md), and a stepson, [Ephen](Lore_Ephen.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup> Kronin's house allegiance is never explicitly stated but the Ra'athim clan's ruling house was [House Mora](Lore_House_Mora.md) before it was adopted into [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) at an unknown time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB5-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-IWTB-3)</sup>

When the [Nordic Empire](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) drove Kronin and his sons from Ebonheart, the Chimer family took to raiding with intense campaigns of guerrilla warfare to attempt to take their land back.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEX-1)</sup> They were successful, and upon Kronin’s passing, the mantle of leadership was passed to his eldest son Cruethys, who was later succeeded by Moraelyn. Ephen would go on to be worshiped as a god of sorts. His [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) dagger, known as [Tooth](Lore_Dragon%27s_Tooth.md), which had a [dragon's](Lore_Dragon.md) tooth for a hilt, passed from the hands of Kronin, to Cruethys, to Moraelyn, and finally to Moraelyn's protégée, [Edward](Lore_Edward.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KEIV-4)</sup>
