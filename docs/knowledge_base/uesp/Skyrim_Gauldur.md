[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-npc-Gauldur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gauldur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gauldur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gauldur.jpg) Gauldur Archmage **Gauldur** was a powerful and renowned [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) who lived in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the opening days of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). A major source of his power was his [magical amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gauldur_Amulet). He is the father of three sons: [Jyrik](Skyrim_Jyrik_Gauldurson.md), [Mikrul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mikrul_Gauldurson), and [Sigdis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigdis_Gauldurson). For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Gauldur.md).

Gauldur is interred in [Reachwater Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwater_Rock). You will encounter Gauldur's spirit there as part of the related quest, where he will aid you in defeating the [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of his three sons and reforge the Gauldur Amulet.
