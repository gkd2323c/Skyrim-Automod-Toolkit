[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-npc-Morwen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Morwen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Morwen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Morwen.jpg) Morwen **Morwen** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md). She works as an apprentice to the smith [Baldor Iron-Shaper](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md) during the day. She becomes a candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) after delivering her amulet to [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). Her mother was Bera, a Skaal woman, and her father was a sailor on a trade ship that sank near the village. She is the only Skaal known to have not grown up on Solstheim; she first lived with her parents in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) before moving back to her mother's tribe.

Until the quest [The Fate of the Skaal](Skyrim_The_Fate_of_the_Skaal.md) has been completed, she is one of the villagers building around the Wind Stone. After being freed, she will walk back to the Skaal Village.

Morwen wears a [Skaal coat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Coat) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Boots) and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Gloves). She carries her [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morwen%27s_House_Key), a [steel greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"My mother was Skaal, but she left the village and I was born in Skyrim."* *"For returning my mother's necklace, you have my gratitude. It means more to me than you can know."* *"I'm relieved that you were able to save Baldor. I admit, I wasn't ready to become smith of the Skaal."* *"May the All-Maker protect us from whatever dark power took control of the Skaal."* *"You freed us from the dark dream, and for that the Skaal are grateful."* *"I've told young Nikulas that the world beyond our lands is no place for the Skaal, but he won't listen."* You may ask her to tell you what she did in Skyrim, before she returned to Solstheim:

**What did you do in Skyrim, before you came back here?** *"Well, my mother used to say that I was born an angry child. It's true that my heart has always been restless, and fighting was the only way I could find peace. I was a soldier for a time, and then a mercenary, but I was never truly happy until I came here."* **Have the dragons made life more difficult for the Skaal?** *"Well, the dragons are a constant danger, but so far they have taken little interest in our village. Thank the All-Maker for that! But have they forced us to change the way we live our lives? No, not at all."* As you exit dialogue, she may say:

*"Strength and courage be always with you."* During combat she can shout:

*"You'll regret starting this fight."* *"Did you think I would run?"* *"I don't want to kill you, but I will."* *"You leave me no choice!"*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Fate of the Skaal
After being freed from the Wind Stone:

*"Why was I building a temple?"* *"I must be going mad. But then, so must everyone else."* If spoken to while she's walking back to the village:

*"Sorry friend, but I'm on the road home. Find me there and perhaps we can talk."*
### The Gardener of Men
She will witness [Storn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider)'s death during [The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md). If you approach her while the tragedy occurs, she'll turn to you:

*"What have you brought into our village?"* After his death, she'll say:

*"Whatever that was... I trust you will make it pay for what it did to Storn."* *"Go. Let us mourn him in our own way."* She'll approach Storn's body to pay her respects.

**Morwen:** *"I don't really understand what you did, but I trust you did it for the good of the village. So... thank you."*
### [Skaal Village Dialogue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Village_Dialogue)
**What do you need?** *"I came across a few of my mother's old things not long ago. Among them was a necklace that my father gave to her when they were married. I believe she would want it placed upon her tombstone in Falkreath, as a symbol of her undying love and the great sacrifice she made for it. If you find yourself in Falkreath, please give this amulet to the priest Runil and tell him that I sent you. He will understand. I don't have much to give in reward, but return to me when the task is done and I'll do what I can. Thank you, Skaal-friend."* **Why did your mother leave the village?** *"A trade ship from Skyrim struck ice off the coast near the village, and the Skaal took in the surviving sailors. My mother fell in love with one of the sailors - my father - and eventually returned with him to Skyrim."* **Did she ever return here?** *"Sadly, no. She died in Falkreath, where I was born."* Returning to her:

**Runil agreed to your request.** *"You've brought warmth to my heart, outsider, and I'm in your debt. Please accept this as a token of gratitude. Once again, you've proven yourself an ally to the Skaal."*
### The Bonds of Matrimony
*"An Amulet of Mara? I haven't seen such a thing in a very long time."* **Interested in me, are you?** *"I cannot deny it. You remind me of all the things I miss about Skyrim, and my heart beats a little quicker when you are near. Do you feel the same?"*
