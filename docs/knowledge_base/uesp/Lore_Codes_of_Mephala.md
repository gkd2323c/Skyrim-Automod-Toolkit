# Codes of Mephala

— *[36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 11](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_11)* The Codes of Mephala,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LV11-1)</sup> also known as the **Mephala Entente** (translated as the **Code of Peace**),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PM-2)</sup> are a set of laws laid out in ancient times by [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) on how to evade the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md)'s enemies or to kill them with secret murder.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VF-3)</sup> Under the rule of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), the [Morag Tong](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morag_Tong) emerged as the followers and executioners of the Code, performing these sanctioned assassinations on [Great House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Great_House) members lawfully<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1M-4)</sup> in what was dubbed the Great Game.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PM-2)</sup>

According to [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), he was whispered and taught the Codes of Mephala while incubating as an egg.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LV1-5)</sup>

## Known Code Parts
*[The 36 Lessons of Vivec](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec)* contain several excerpts/interpretations of the Codes of Mephala, which include:

- *According to the Codes of Mephala there can be no official art, only fixation points of complexity that will erase from the awe of the people given enough time. This is a secret that hides another. An impersonal survival is not the way of the ruling king. Embrace the art of the people and marry it and by that I mean secretly have it murdered.*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LV11-1)</sup>

- *According to the Codes of Mephala, there is no difference between the theorist and the terrorist. Even the most cherished desire disappears in their hands. This is why Mephala has black hands. Bring both of yours to every argument. The one-handed king finds no remedy. When you approach God, however, cut both of them off. God has no need of theory and he is armored head to toe in terror.*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LV11-1)</sup>

According to the [35th lesson of Vivec, "The Scripture of Love"](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_35), this particular sermon's existence was demanded by the Codes of Mephala:

- *...all the other symbols of absolute reality are ancient ideas ready for their graves, or at least the essence of such. This scripture is directly ordered by the codes of Mephala, the origin of sex and murder, defeated only by those who take up those ideas without my intervention. The religious elite is not a tendency or a correlation. They are dogma complemented by the influence of the untrustworthy sea and the governance of the stars, dominated at the center by the sword, which is nothing without a victim to cleave unto.*<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LV35-6)</sup>
