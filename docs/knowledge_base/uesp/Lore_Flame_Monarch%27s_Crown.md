# Flame Monarch's Crown

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/BL-item-Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown.jpg/200px-BL-item-Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Flame_Monarch%27s_Crown.jpg) The Flame Monarch's Crown The Flame Monarch's Crown is a legendary [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) helmet. It offers protection equivalent to [Dragonscale](Lore_Dragon.md) armor, and possesses a myriad of powerful enchantments to protect the wearer from fire, spells, and physical damage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BL-1)</sup>

The origins of the Crown are shrouded in mystery. Its name refers to a [Flame Monarch](Lore_Flame_Monarch.md), a stronger form of the more common [Flame Atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Some claim that the helmet was worn by the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [demiprince](Lore_Demiprince.md) [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md) when he visited the collective realm of [Infernace](Lore_Infernace.md), home of the Flame Atronachs. Others insist that this tale is only conjecture.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BL-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Known as a realm of intense heat, some believe that the flame-resistant enchantments placed on the helm were the only thing that made Fa-Nuit-Hen's visit to Infernace possible.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BLFlame Knight-5)</sup>

After Fa-Nuit-Hen returned from Infernace, skilled [armorsmiths](Lore_Smithing.md) attempted to replicate the Crown. This led to the creation of the Flame Knight's Helmet, an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) helmet bearing similar, albeit lesser, enchantments.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BLFlame Knight-5)</sup>

The Crown is believed to have come into the possession of [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md) circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> in exchange for [sigils](Lore_Sigils.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BL-1)</sup>
