[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/ON-place-Hoperoot.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hoperoot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hoperoot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hoperoot.jpg) Hoperoot *Hoperoot? A pretty name for an ugly secret.*
—Tribune Alea Idolus of the West Weald Legion<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Hoperoot was a planned [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) city in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It was a living settlement, consisting of a massive tree-fort grown from the foothills of the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The fort also incorporated ruined fragments of [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) architecture, which were lifted into the air by its branches.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Hoperoot was grown by the [Dawnway Bosmer](Lore_Dawnway.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) to serve as the new capital of Dawnwood, a forest kingdom created via the destructive power of the [Wildburn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONantharion-4)</sup>

Secretly, a cult known as the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md) planned to use Hoperoot as a fortified headquarters during their attempted takeover of the entire West Weald.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSo S-5)</sup> After initially using [wildburn seeds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn_Seeds) to jungle the sparsely-inhabited [Strid Vale](Lore_Strid_Vale.md) region, the Recollection leader [Nantharion Rayn](Lore_Nantharion.md) declared himself King of Dawnwood and led peaceful Bosmer settlers across the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md) to found the settlement of Vashabar.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> He then began to oversee the establishment of Hoperoot as a second settlement.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONantharion-4)</sup>

With the help of [Greenspeaker](Lore_Greenspeakers.md) Sorilen and the [Shardborn](Lore_Shardborn.md), King Nantharion initiated a Wildburn ritual at Hoperoot to destroy the nearby city of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md). However, the ritual was interrupted by agents of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) led by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who managed to kill Sorilen and destroy the wildburn seed. This caused the seed to release its energy too early, burning down the entirety of Hoperoot.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSo S-5)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONantharion-4)</sup>

Following Hoperoot's destruction, King Nantharion's role as leader of the Recollection was revealed.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSo S-5)</sup> He was subsequently killed by the Vestige within the ruins of [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md), ending the Recollection's attempt at further expansion.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGambit-7)</sup>
