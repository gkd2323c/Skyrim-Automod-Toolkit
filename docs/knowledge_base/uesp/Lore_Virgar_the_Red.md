Virgar the Red (also known as the **Crimson Matron** and the **Concubine of Coldharbour**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vir-1)</sup> was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) matriarch who dwelled in the Orchard, a graveyard within [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).

An artifact known as a [Harvest Heart](Lore_Molag_Bal.md#Harvest_Hearts) was used by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) to control the vampires of the Orchard.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vir-1)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) proposed his idea of lobotomizing the vampires within the Orchard, an act she saw as cruel and distasteful,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vir-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HH-2)</sup> Virgar the Red proposed an alternate solution that would free the vampires from Molag Bal by severing their connection to him. In exchange for freeing her and her wards from Molag Bal, she promised to aid the forces of the [Fighters](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and [Mages Guilds](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in their efforts to stop the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). It is unknown how the Vestige resolved the situation.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HH-2)</sup>
