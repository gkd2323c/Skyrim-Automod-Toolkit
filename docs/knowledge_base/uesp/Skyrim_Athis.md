[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-npc-Athis.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Athis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Athis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Athis.jpg) Athis **Athis**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer One Handed Expert), is a member of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) living in [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) within the city of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). Once the Companions questline is completed, Athis can become a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), a new member of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), and a candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

When you enter Jorrvaskr for the first time, you will find Athis engaged in a brawl with [Njada Stonearm](Skyrim_Njada_Stonearm.md), a fight that he always loses. He's adept in [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) combat and will offer expert-level [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in it up to level 75, if you are a member of the Companions. He keeps quiet most of the time, but will not tolerate repeating himself. He is civil and loyal, but never at the forefront of any battles. Athis wakes at 6am and heads to the main hall to eat breakfast and wander around until 7am. He then heads outside to the training area where he'll socialize, drink, and watch people until noon, then goes downstairs to the Living Quarters to eat lunch. Around 1pm, he'll take to wandering around the main hall again. At 4pm, he'll go back down into the Living Quarters and walk around until 8pm before going to bed.

Athis wears a set of [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor) and a pair of normal [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He's equipped with a [Skyforge steel sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Sword) and an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md), and also carries a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Notes
- For some reason, Athis wears a duplicate of the outfit that's specifically designed for [Hewnon Black-Skeever](Skyrim_Hewnon_Black-Skeever.md), rather than just a matching set of boots with armor.
- After the quest [Blood's Honor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood%27s_Honor), Athis will be found lying wounded on the floor of Jorrvaskr and cannot be interacted with.
- Although his default gear is governed by the Light Armor skill, Athis is actually more proficient in heavy armor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, addresses this issue. His class has been given proficiency in Light Armor, since it's unique to him.

## Bugs
- Like many named Whiterun NPCs, Athis may attack you on sight for no apparent reason. - Commit a minor crime in front of a Whiterun guard, such as assault, then clear the resulting bounty. This should clear Athis' hostility, even in mid-combat.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the [console](Skyrim_Console.md), select Athis by clicking on him or entering the code `prid 1a6d6`, then enter the code `setav aggression 0`. Note that this only works if Athis isn't currently in combat with you.
- He may attack you on sight after you have captured Hjaalmarch for the Stormcloaks, even if you are a member of the Companions yourself. After Solitude has been conquered and thus the civil war has ended, he will revert back to his normal behavior. <sup>**?**</sup>
