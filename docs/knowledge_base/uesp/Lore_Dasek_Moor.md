[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/OB-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg/200px-OB-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg) Dasek Moor circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/ON-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg/200px-ON-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Dasek_Moor.jpg) Dasek Moor circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Dasek Moor is a ruined underground fort located along the [Gold Road](Lore_Gold_Road.md) just south of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md), on the border between the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) and [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) regions of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). On the surface, the fort is little more than a crumbling entrance. However, it contains an extensive underground complex, including ancient tombs and a living quarters known as the Maleboge.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The fort was already a crumbling ruin by [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), at which time the underground passages were sealed off. The ruins were regularly used as a secret meeting place by Count [Carolus Aquilarios](Lore_Carolus_Aquilarios.md), who had a campsite at the entrance to the ruins,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> and was also using the ruins of to spy on the Order of the Hour.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AC-3)</sup> Count Carolus used this campsite when he met with the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) to reveal that the mysterious [Black Dragon](Lore_Black_Dragon.md) who was targeting the Brotherhood was in fact the First Sword of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md), and that the old Enclave of the Hourglass was being made into a headquarters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AC-3)</sup> The Brotherhood subsequently decimated the Order and eliminated the Black Dragon in an act of revenge, neutralizing one of the Count's major political opponents in the region.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESODB-4)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Dasek Moor was home to a marauder gang. However, the fort's tombs were infested with undead, and several marauders were killed while attempting to clear the lower levels.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OB-5)</sup>
