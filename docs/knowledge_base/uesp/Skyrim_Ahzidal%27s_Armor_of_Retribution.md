[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Armor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Armor.png) Ahzidal's Armor of Retribution Ahzidal's Armor of Retribution is the chest piece of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). It can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after defeating its creator during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). This armor is identical in appearance to [ancient Nord armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Armor.md), but provides fifteen more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and weighs ten points more. Its enchantment gives you a 5% chance to [paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) enemies who strike you with a melee attack, making dealing with multiple melee enemies much easier. In addition, if you wear this armor along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points. Ahzidal's armor can be tempered with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), with the [Advanced Armors perk](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors_(perk).md) doubling improvement.

## Related Quests
- **[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)**: Assist [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) in his search for the relics of [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md) in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

## Notes
- The paralyze enchantment does not work if the armor is equipped onto a follower.
