[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-npc-Finna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Finna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Finna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Finna.jpg) Finna **Finna** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living at [Oslaf's house](Skyrim_Oslaf%27s_House.md) in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) with her husband [Oslaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf) and their daughter [Aeta](Skyrim_Aeta.md). She wears a [Skaal coat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Coat) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Boots) and [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Hat). She carries a [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), her [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House_Key), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[The Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md)**: Travel to the realm of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md) to learn the rest of [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"I welcome you, friend of the Skaal."*
- *"The dragons of old have returned! By the All-Maker, I hope they will leave our village alone."*
- *"There is strength and courage in your eyes. You have the spirit of a great hunter."*
- *"The [historian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tharstan) has not stopped speaking your praises."*

### Conversations
Finna may be seen having some random conversations with her husband Oslaf:

- **Oslaf**: *"There's a terrible ache in my back today."*
- **Finna**: *"Complaining to me won't make your pain go away, Oslaf. Go and see Edla, perhaps she has a remedy."*
- **Oslaf**: *"There's no time for that now, I have work to do."*
- **Finna**: *"Then get back to it, you lazy horker! The day is wasting."*

- **Oslaf**: *"Are the potatoes ready for eating? I want to roast them with some onions for the night's meal."*
- **Finna**: *"Be patient, my husband. The crop doesn't grow faster because it hears your growling stomach."*
- **Oslaf**: *"Hm, perhaps I'll make an offering to the All-Maker. He can make our potatoes ripen as surely as he makes the snow fall from the sky."*
- **Finna**: *"I'm sure the All-Maker has more important concerns than your potatoes, Oslaf."*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Gardener of Men
If spoken to while Storn is reading the black book:

- *"Storn! What is he doing?"*

After Storn dies, she approaches the corpse to pay her respects:

- **Finna:** *"May the All-Maker guide your soul to the next life."*

If spoken to:

- *"Storn was like a father to me. To all of us. I do not know what we will do without him."*
- *"Leave us, please."*
