[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/LO-map-Aldmeri_Dominion_%28Morrowind_Codex%29.png/200px-LO-map-Aldmeri_Dominion_%28Morrowind_Codex%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Aldmeri_Dominion_(Morrowind_Codex).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Aldmeri_Dominion_(Morrowind_Codex).png) Map showing the unmarked Cape of the Blue Divide The Cape of the Blue Divide, or simply the **Blue Divide**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SIAVG-2)</sup> is the body of water found between the provinces of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), and the general area of the [Second Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#Second_Dominion). It is bordered by the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) to the north and the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) to the southeast; and, like the Abecean Sea, the Blue Divide most likely belongs to the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map-3)</sup>

The Blue Divide's general scope is largely unknown, though the town of [Southpoint](Lore_Southpoint.md) is reportedly north of the body of water.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The island of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) is a strategic location, found north of the Cape of the Blue Divide.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1EH-5)</sup> It was because of this the island was targeted by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) during their conquest of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md). It was their closest foothold to the Cape, described as the waters of the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1EAD-6)</sup> The [Colovians](Lore_Colovia.md) made their earliest voyages around these waters, and the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), sacking various [Nedic](Lore_Nedic.md) settlements.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1EC-7)</sup> The Cape of the Blue Divide was also the general location of the [War of the Blue Divide](Lore_War_of_the_Blue_Divide.md), occurring in the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) between the provinces of Valenwood and the Summerset Isles.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ADi Fv7-8)</sup>

## Notes
- The Lane is an active sea route within the Blue Divide, with both [Haven](Lore_Haven.md) and [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md) serving as links in the line.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ASP-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONHenriel-10)</sup>
- It is named for the [Blue Divide](https://shannara.fandom.com/wiki/Blue_Divide) off the coast of [Morrowindl](https://shannara.fandom.com/wiki/Morrowindl) in *[The Elf Queen of Shannara](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Elf_Queen_of_Shannara)*.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-JJAm A-11)</sup>
