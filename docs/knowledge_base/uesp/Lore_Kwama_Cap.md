## Kanthleaf
Kanthleaf is a plant found all over the riverbank of the [Diceto River](Lore_Diceto_River.md), on the isle of [Aurdion](Lore_Auridon.md). Apart from the fact that they have petals, little is known about them.

**Found in:** - [Lore](Lore_Words_and_Philosophy.md)

## Kreshweed
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/MW-flora-Kreshweed.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Kreshweed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Kreshweed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Kreshweed.jpg) A Kreshweed Flower Kreshweed (or kresh-weed) is a coarse grass that grows throughout [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and particularly in the [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md) and [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) regions of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Kreshweed may also be found less commonly in other regions of Tamriel. It grows with especially great abundance near salt water. It can be refined into [Kresh Fiber](Lore_Kresh_Fiber.md), which is used in making cloth. Some species native to Morrowind require a large amount of it to survive.

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Kresh Fiber](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Kresh_Fiber) (Morrowind), [Kreshweed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Kreshweed) (ESO)
## Kwama Cap
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/ON-node-Kwama_Cap.jpg/200px-ON-node-Kwama_Cap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Kwama_Cap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Kwama_Cap.jpg) Kwama Cap Kwama Cap is a rare fungus that can be found in the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region. Aptly named, this mushroom is known to grow in places such as the Strifeswarm [Kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) Mine outside [Senie](Lore_Senie.md). It can be used to promote plant growth in certain rituals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Kwama Cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Stonefalls_Quest_Items#Kwama_Cap)
## Kyne's Breath
A species of flower found in [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) that is known for its curative properties. In particular, it can help to reduce fevers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Kyne's Breath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Eastmarch_Quest_Items#Kyne.27s_Breath)
## Kyne's Tears
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/ON-node-Kyne%27s_Tears.jpg/200px-ON-node-Kyne%27s_Tears.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Kyne%27s_Tears.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Kyne%27s_Tears.jpg) Kyne's Tears A rare plant whose delicate flowers are said to have magical properties, particularly involving purification.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HT-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The plant is said to grow where the tears of the goddess [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md) fall to the earth.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KT-5)</sup> It is normally found in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but some specimens have been sighted growing in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) near a shrine dedicated to Kyne which was stolen from [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The flower is normally invisible but a runestone which is considered blessed by the goddess allows them to be seen.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HT-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KT-5)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Kyne's Tears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Coldharbour_Quest_Items#Kyne.27s_Tears)
