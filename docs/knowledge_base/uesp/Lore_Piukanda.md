[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/ON-place-Piukanda.jpg/200px-ON-place-Piukanda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Piukanda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Piukanda.jpg) Piukanda circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/OB-place-Piukanda.jpg/200px-OB-place-Piukanda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Piukanda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Piukanda.jpg) Piukanda circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Piukanda is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) located between the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md), on the northeastern edge of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup> in a sparsely-wooded region known as Autumn Range.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a squad of [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) soldiers based in [Fort Glademist](Lore_Fort_Glademist.md) were ordered by General Khamagash to clear the nearby Ayleid ruins, including Piukanda, in search of enemy troops.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> However, they perished before returning, and their [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md) were raised as [undead](Lore_Undead.md), including that of Captain Kline.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The skeletal captain was ultimately freed from undeath by passing soldiers, but the interior of the ruins remained sealed at that time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins had been unsealed, and Piukanda's subterranean passages were swarming with undead.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
