# Trident

Tridents are a type of polearm that take the form of three-pronged [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md).

The [Trident-Kings](Lore_Trident-Kings.md) are an obscure faction believed to be the leaders of the [Dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CNC-1)</sup> The [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) use claw shapes in ritual tridents as an extension of their own slashing, clawed hands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KAa A-2)</sup> [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) Queensguard are said to wield shiny tridents while patrolling the streets of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). The [lizard bulls](Lore_Lizard_Bull.md) said to be found in the islands of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md) wield glittering tridents, similar to the Queensguard.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSCW-3)</sup>

The True-Sworn mercenaries used staves with heads shaped nearly like tridents, a design choice influenced by pragmatic considerations aligned with their tasks.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-100TS-4)</sup> The [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) used maces that were shaped like tridents.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-85GM-5)</sup> [Systrean](Lore_Systres.md) marines described their trident staves as the "masts of magic" and claimed that channeling magic down the tines of a trident felt different to traditional staves.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-115DM-6)</sup>
