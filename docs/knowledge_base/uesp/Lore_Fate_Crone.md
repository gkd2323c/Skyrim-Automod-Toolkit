# Fate Crone

| Fate Crone | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Female |

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-skill-Curative_Runeforms-Chakram_of_Destiny.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-skill-Curative_Runeforms-Chakram_of_Destiny.png) Fate Crone's runes The Fate Crone was an obscure [Apocryphal](Lore_Apocrypha.md) entity associated with the runes used by [Arcanists](Lore_Arcanists.md), who was described as resembling an ancient [Hag](Lore_Hag.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azandar-1)</sup> One way for an Arcanist to harness their power was by inscribing the runes of the Fate Crone to create objects, such as spinning mystical discs, also known as chakrams of destiny, which could grant magical protective shields. Similarly to the [Blind Man](Lore_Blind_Man.md) and [Baron of Breakers](Lore_Baron_of_Breakers.md) she was associated with such discs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azandar-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Chakram_Shields-2)</sup>

Arcanists sometimes attempted to seek power and knowledge from the Fate Crone, and [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) was known to summon her. In Azandar's description of the Fate Crone, he thought her a pleasant conversationalist, but she tended to be rather reserved and not very forthcoming with information.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azandar-1)</sup>
