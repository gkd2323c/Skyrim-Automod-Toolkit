[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-npc-Elberon%27s_Apprentice.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Elberon%27s_Apprentice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elberon%27s_Apprentice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Elberon%27s_Apprentice.jpg) Elberon's Apprentice **Elberon's Apprentice** is a hostile mage who will appear in the Vardnknd Gallery section of [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns) during the related quest, alongside several hostile mercenaries. He is the apprentice of [Elberon](Skyrim_Elberon.md), a [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) wizard who is fatally struck by lightning when you first enter Vardnknd Gallery.

He is wearing [Apprentice Robes of Illusion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apprentice_Robes_of_Illusion), an [apprentice hood](Skyrim_Apprentice_Hood.md), and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and wields a [Dwarven dagger](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md). He can use the [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md) and [Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(spell).md) spells.

He uses generic [bandit dialogue](Skyrim_Bandit.md#Dialogue).
