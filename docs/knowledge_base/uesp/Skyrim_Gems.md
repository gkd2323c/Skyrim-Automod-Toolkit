# Gems


All of the items on this page are traded by jewelry merchants, and can also be sold to general goods merchants.

## Valuable Gems
Most gems that you find are randomly generated, meaning that most of the possible locations are the same for all twelve types of gem. The gems that appear in randomly-generated loot depends on the player's level. The "Level (Loot)" column in the following table applies to most locations where gems are found; the "Level (Merchant)" column only applies to merchant inventories. Possible locations for random gems (all using the loot leveled list) are:

- [Grelka](Skyrim_Grelka.md)'s strongbox is guaranteed to contain three gems (more with Prowler's Profit; see below)
- All [Dwarven centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) are guaranteed to drop 1-3 gems.
- Boss chests and jewelry boxes in [Dwarven ruins](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) are guaranteed to drop one gem.
- [Asgeir Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md), [Bashnag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bashnag), [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md), [Gulum-Ei](Skyrim_Gulum-Ei.md), [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md), and [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) always carry multiple gems
- Each time you [mine](Skyrim_Mining.md) an ore vein, there is a 10% chance of receiving one gem in addition to the ore(s) that you normally receive.
- Most boss chests have a 10-25% chance of containing a gem.
- Most other sources of random loot (chests, enemy NPCs, etc.) have a small chance (typically 1% or less) of containing a gem.

### Prowler's Profit
Having the [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Prowler%27s_Profit.md) ability (from completing the quest [No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md)) causes many more gems to appear. Specifically, 1-4 additional gems are guaranteed to appear in each of the following locations:

- Dropped by [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md)
- Dropped by [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md)
- Carried by rich citizens
- All dead [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) (i.e., the ones that never come to life)
- Most treasure-type chests, including urns, burial urns, [Thief Caches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_Cache), and almost all boss chests
- An assortment of rare or unique containers such as the Confiscated Goods chests outside Riften, Aringoth's Safe, etc.

Possible outcomes for the extra gem roll:

| 1 gem | 2 gems | 3 gems | 4 gems |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 9.375% | 40.625% | 40.625% | 9.375% |

This means that most of the time you will get 2-3 additional (leveled) gems.

Statistically, urns and burial urns are by far the most common source of Prowler's Profit gems; it is entirely possible, assuming you loot every urn and burial urn you come across, to collect gems at a much faster rate than you will be capable of selling them.

## Notes
- [Circlets](Skyrim_Jewelry.md) contain onyx, jade, and blue moonstone. However, the gem inserts (and, in the cases of the Sapphire and Emerald Jade Circlets, the entire band) are only found in this headwear and not as separate gems.
- Real-life gems amber, corundum, malachite and moonstone appear as smithing materials (ingots and ores) but not as separate gems.
- Pearls appear as alchemy ingredients, but not as jewelry components.
