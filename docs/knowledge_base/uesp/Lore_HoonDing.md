# HoonDing

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/RG-place-Mage%27s_Guild_Future.jpg/200px-RG-place-Mage%27s_Guild_Future.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Mage%27s_Guild_Future.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Mage%27s_Guild_Future.jpg) Cyrus' face imposed on A'tor's body, grasping the Soul Sword *Be resolute, fear no sacrifice, and surmount every difficulty to win victory.*
—Hoonding's prayer stone The Hoon Ding, also known as **Hoonding**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cont Hoon-1)</sup> or **The Make Way God**, is the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) spirit of 'perseverance over infidels'. The Hoon Ding has historically materialized whenever the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) need to 'make way' for their people.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo FCR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Vo FTF-3)</sup>

## History
In the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), Hoon Ding is first known to have manifested as an avatar by [Diagna](Lore_Diagna.md). Diagna was instrumental to the defeat of the [Sinistral Mer](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md), as he brought [orichalc](Lore_Orichalcum.md) weapons to the Yokudan people to win the fight. A story surrounding The Hoon Ding Gong details Diagna crashing his fist against it, and swords fell from the walls of the [Orichalc Tower](Lore_Orichalc_Tower.md) like "needles from a dry pine".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LAB-4)</sup> In [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), he led a very tight knit group of followers against the [Orsimer](Lore_Orc.md) of the first [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), presumably the [Order of Diagna](Lore_Order_of_Diagna.md) during the [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md), during the height of their ancient power.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo FCR-2)</sup> He had then faded into obscurity, and is now little more than a local power spirit of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo Fit E-5)</sup>

[Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) was also an aspect of Hoon Ding.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cont Hoon-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Contra Hoon-6)</sup> During the arrival of the Yokudan people to Tamriel, he led them against [Malooc](Lore_Malooc.md) the Horde King, the enemy god of [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) who led an army of [giant goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) against the Redguards in the First Era. Then Hoon Ding appeared to drive out the goblin force all the way to the east.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo Fit E-5)</sup>

During the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), Hoon Ding was said to have been "a sword or a crown, or both". This refers to either or both Crown [Prince A'tor](Lore_A%27tor.md), the heir to the throne who was transformed into the [Soul Sword](Lore_Soul_Sword.md), and/or [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md), a [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) who wielded the Soul Sword. Together, they could have also been an aspect of Hoon Ding.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo Fit E-5)</sup> Cyrus was believed to be more than just an ordinary Redguard, which is one of the reasons why he was able to slay the legendary dragon [Nafaalilargus](Lore_Nahfahlaar.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-GT-7)</sup>

## Notes
- [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md) stated he learned a new motion or two when he observed the Hoon Ding "making way".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Fanny-8)</sup>
- [Ebonarm](Lore_Ebonarm.md) is a Yokudan deity or group of deities that share the same designation, with legends saying that Ebonarm is just another manifestation of the Hoon Ding. Many post-apocalypse manifestations of the Hoon Ding have individualized, such as Diagna, and Ebonarm may be among them.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MK-9)</sup>
- Hoon Ding's Watch was a Ra'Gada settlement in the southeastern [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r.md).
