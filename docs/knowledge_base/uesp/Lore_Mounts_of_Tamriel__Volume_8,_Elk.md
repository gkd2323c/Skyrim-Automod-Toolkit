Mounts of Tamriel: Volume 8, Elk **by [Fadus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fadus_Falto) of Skingrad** *A look at the husbandry of cervine mounts in Tamriel* *
---
I was fascinated, before beginning this survey, with the idea of sitting astride an [elk](Lore_Elk.md). These beautiful creatures are cultivated far from my home in [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), and until I found my way to the azure waters and verdant shores of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) I had never ridden a cervine beast for any length of time. My [druid](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md) host, a large and enthusiastic fellow from Stonelore Grove, helped me to understand the art of elk-raising across the continent. The majority of these beasts are reared through one of two traditions: [druidic](Lore_Druids.md) or [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).

Druids, I'm told, find the creatures the perfect compliment to their magical tradition. Other mounts are skittish during druidcrafted rituals and the fundamental forces they gather in their spellcasting. Elk and their relatives, deeply tied to the wilds as they are, find that kind of [magic](Lore_Magic.md) no more upsetting than a late-summer rainstorm. The [Wood Elves](Lore_Wood_Elf.md) of Valenwood, meanwhile, have a long tradition of rearing elk and [deer](Lore_Deer.md) as mounts simply because of need and availability.
