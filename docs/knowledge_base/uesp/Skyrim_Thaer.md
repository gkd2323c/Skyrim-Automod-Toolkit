[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-npc-Thaer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thaer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thaer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thaer.jpg) Thaer **Thaer** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), and the [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) [carriage](Skyrim_Carriage.md) driver. He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1da) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). He is equipped with an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe).

If you ask him what he knows about Solitude he'll tell you, *"Solitude's been the capital of Skyrim as long as I can remember. Most of the High Kings of Skyrim were Jarls of Solitude first. Ah, let's see, what else... The [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) is here, and the main office of the [East Empire Company](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md). If you need it, you can get a room for a fair price at the [Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), just inside the main gate."*
