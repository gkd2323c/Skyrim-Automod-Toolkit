Alessia Caro (née **Valga**) was the Countess of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). She was the daughter of Count [Charus Valga](Lore_Charus_Valga.md) and Countess [Arriana Valga](Lore_Arriana_Valga.md) of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt C-1)</sup> Around [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), she wed the Count of Leyawiin, [Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3C-3)</sup> She and her husband were from prominent [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) families, and it was widely speculated they would eventually be on the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Obrs-4)</sup> It was rumored that she was the real power behind the throne, not her husband.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBWit-5)</sup>

The union of Alessia and Marius was considered a perfect blend of love and wise political maneuvering, as they seemed to be well-matched on a personal level and were both staunch supporters of [Imperialization](Lore_Imperial.md) in Leyawiin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3C-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTL-2)</sup> The Caros' encouragement of Imperialization in Leyawiin divided its citizens; some of the city's residents supported this policy, while many of the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) favored the cosmopolitanism that had traditionally defined Leyawiin.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OSS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tsa-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BP-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-JP-9)</sup> Caro was uncomfortable with Argonian, Khajiit, and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) ways, but she especially disliked Argonians.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tsa-7)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CAC-10)</sup> There were rumors that Alessia and Marius had a secret torture room in [Castle Leyawiin](Lore_Castle_Leyawiin.md), where they tortured Argonian immigrants from [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-HM-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Dt S-12)</sup> The servants whispered that the Argonians were dragged into the basement and never seen again.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Dt S-12)</sup>

After becoming countess, Alessia dutifully visited her mother, Arriana, every month.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt C-1)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-HM-11)</sup> Caro was renowned for her beauty, wit, and grace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt C-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BP-8)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-PSSG-13)</sup> She occasionally held formal dinner parties in Castle Leyawiin, but during one of these, an unknown assailant cast a spell on the Countess and her guests that stripped them of all their possessions—clothes included.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-PSSG-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Sang-14)</sup>

Alessia bore several daughters with Marius in the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). By [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), her daughters had become infamous for poisoning guests to Leyawiin's court.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TIC-15)</sup><sup>:26</sup>
