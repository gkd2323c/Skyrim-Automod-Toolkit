[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-item-Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gloves.jpg) Gloves Prisoner's Cuffs are restraints used to bind a person's hand. They have the appearance of a common pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c1) in the inventory, although they appear to look like a pair of cuffs while worn.

A pair is initially worn by [you](Skyrim_Prisoner.md) during the first parts of the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md), with the bonds being cut after you enter into [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md), destroying the cuffs in the process. Another pair is worn by [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md), the guard who allowed [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) to escape from [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), during Roggvir's execution. After Roggvir dies, the cuffs cannot be retrieved from his body.

## Related Quests
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.

## Notes
- Only the first set of cuffs (`0010e039`) can be equipped.
