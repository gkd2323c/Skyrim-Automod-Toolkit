Imbel Genealogy *A look at the descent of the Imbel name through the male offspring*
---
The Imbel family traces lineage strictly through the male line of heirs, as any right-thinking nobility would. Therefore this family genealogy does not record the inconsequential female offspring.

- - Married Gustie Karna [1031](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1031)
- Buried in [Vardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* near [Suran](Lore_Suran.md)
