## Largashbur Cellar
Upon entering via the trapdoor, the chamber appears to be a natural cave rather than a normal cellar. There is a raised wooden platform to the northeast, with a mead barrel, a food barrel, a meat barrel, two chairs, and a small square wooden table holding a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md). To the southwest are three double-banked sets of shelves across the room, holding a large [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a small coin purse, three random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), eight common books, a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Battle of Red Mountain](Skyrim_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md)*, an [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) barrel, and twelve food sacks. Beyond the shelves is a counter running across the room, with a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) on top, and a small coin purse, a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), two bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and six bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) underneath. Against the northwest wall is a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing minor loot, with a round wooden table holding a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md) and two chairs to the right.
