## *[Part 1](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Folly_of_Isolation,_Part_1)*
Act One, Scene One

A [fool](Lore_The_Vestige.md), believing he knows better than the dashing main character, enters stage left. Before him stands the imposing sight of Heartholdhelm. Within, the three bloodthirsty races of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) await. The fool strides forward, facing certain dismemberment by angry swamp [lizards](Lore_Argonian.md) and [berserk barbarians](Lore_Nord.md) ….

## *[Part 3](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Folly_of_Isolation,_Part_3)*
Act One, Scene Four

A [harried-looking](Lore_Valaste.md) [High Elven](Lore_Altmer.md) woman sits at a table, surrounded by books. Her eyes are locked on the horizon, staring at nothing. Only the most perceptive would notice that a thin line of drool is sliding from the side of her mouth. Before her sits a massive tome, inscribed with the most intricate of runes ….

## *[Part 6](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Folly_of_Isolation,_Part_6)*
Act Two, Scene Two

A noble [High Elf](Lore_Altmer.md) looks imperiously down her nose at the assembled [Mages](Lore_Mages_Guild.md).

**[RAZUM-DAR](Lore_Razum-dar.md)**: These Mages would depart our borders with critical information, My Queen. They say they seek … isolation.

**QUEEN [AYRENN](Lore_Ayrenn.md)**: Truly? Perhaps you do not understand the meaning of loyalty, little magelings!
