[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/SR-npc-Omluag.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Omluag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Omluag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Omluag.jpg) Omluag at the smelter **Omluag** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who lives in [The Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md) and works at the [Smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He shares his room in the Warrens with [Hathrasil](Skyrim_Hathrasil.md), a co-worker at the Smelter. If you [offer to talk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You) to his boss about his working conditions, he will reward you with gold. He will also become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

He wakes at 7am and heads to work, carrying a bucket for two hours at 7am, 11am, and 3pm. His workday ends at 7pm, then he has a four-hour dinner at [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md). At 11pm, he goes back to the Warrens and wanders around there until midnight, when he sleeps.

Omluag wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes) along with a pair of [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). His inventory includes the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Omluag%27s_Room) to his room in The Warrens and some [poor items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Pocket_Contents). In combat, he relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
When approached at the Smelter he can say:

*"Can't talk. [Mulush](Skyrim_Mulush_gro-Shugurz.md) will beat me if I stop working."* *"I spill just a bit of metal, and Mulush takes it out of my hide."* *"Can't you see I'm working? Beat it."* If you meet him at Silver-Blood Inn, he'll tell you:

*"Drinks here are watered down, but they're cheap."* If [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md) has been completed, he'll comment on your escape, saying either:

*"I saw you. With the Forsworn. By the old gods, that was something."* *"I'll be damned. Someone who actually escaped Cidhna Mine. You're the talk of the Warrens."* When spoken to directly he can be asked some questions:

**Tell me about the Forsworn.** *"We've lost a lot of good people to that damn crusade. Drive out the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md), they say. Well, the Nord ain't driven out."* While he is working, you can also inquire about who else works in the smelter, but only if Mulush, Hathrasil, [Degaine](Skyrim_Degaine.md), [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md), and [Cairine](Skyrim_Cairine.md) are not dead.

**Who else works here?** *"Me and Hathrasil mostly. Mulush barks at us all day, but I wouldn't call that honest work. A few people in the Warrens used to work here. Degaine broke some bones a while back. Eltrys quit. Cairine is too sick."*
## Quest-Related Events

### A Few Words with You
**You're having trouble with Mulush?** *"He's a damn tyrant. Always being goaded on by the Silver-Bloods to get more work out of us. They pay us next to nothing. We get beaten if we make a mistake, and where are our kind [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) rulers? What are they doing? Typical."* **What if I talk to Mulush?** *"You're going to try to talk sense into that [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md)? I'll believe it when I see it."* **Sorry to hear that.** *"Sorries don't help anyone."* After you have taken care of his little problem:

**I convinced Mulush to ease up a bit.** *"I don't believe it. Someone sticking up for us. Here. It's a week's wages for me, but you've earned every coin."*
### The Forsworn Conspiracy
During [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md), you will be asked to investigate the murder that happened in the marketplace. You can ask Omluag about [Weylin](Skyrim_Weylin.md), the killer:

**Did you know Weylin?** *"The Forsworn? That's right. I'll say it. We all knew. Even that fat-headed, slave-driving Mulush knew. Just ask him about it. Bah. Another honest man lost in all this madness. And for what?"*
## Conversations
While at the smelter, Mulush can be heard bossing Omluag around:

**Mulush**: *"Careful. You spill one drop of silver, I'm taking it out of your hide."*
**Omluag**: *"I'm being as careful as I can."*
**Mulush**: *"Did you just talk back to me? I'm not paid to listen. I'm paid to keep you working. Now work."* **Omluag**: *"Mulush, we need a break. The heat from the smelter is too much."*
**Mulush**: *"Are you complaining? Work. If you have strength to whine, you have strength to smelt."* He and Hathrasil may also talk during work:

**Omluag**: *"Divines, I'm tired. This is too much work."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Just keep at it, or Mulush will beat you."* **Hathrasil**: *"Careful of the molten metal. That gets on you, kiss your fingers goodbye."*
**Omluag**: *"I know what I'm doing. Stop telling me."* **Hathrasil**: *"Surprised to see you working. Mulush almost broke your arm yesterday."*
**Omluag**: *"Does it look like I can afford losing a day's wage? That damn Orc isn't getting the best of me."* If [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has been completed, he may talk to Hathrasil about the dragons' return:

**Omluag**: *"First war, and now dragons. Everywhere they go, the Nords bring nothing but ruin."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Ain't nothing for it. They're still in charge."* Omluag may be seen having the following conversation with [Hathrasil](Skyrim_Hathrasil.md) in [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md):

**Omluag**: *"Another foul day at the smelter."*
**Hathrasil**: *"Mmmhmm."*
**Omluag**: *""Mmmhmm"? I've been working with you for over 10 years, and all you have to say is "mmmhmm"?"*
**Hathrasil**: *"Mmmhmm."*
**Omluag**: *"Sometimes I think we deserve these miserable lives of ours."*
## Unused Dialogue

This quest would be reworked into, and most of its dialogue would be retained for, an instance of the favor quest **A Few Words With You** rather than a city freeform quest in the final game.

Typical."* **How about I talk to Mulush? Convince him to ease off you.** *"You're going to try to talk sense into that Orc? I'll believe it when I see it."* **Sorry to hear that.** *"Yeah, yeah. I need to get back to work."* When you returned after taking care of Mulush:

**I convinced Mulush to treat you more fairly.** *"I don't believe it. Someone sticking up for us. Here. It's a week's wages for me, but you've earned every coin."*

I'll pay you for any information you can find."* All three of these people could be investigated. If you went to investigate Omluag, you would come across a note in his room in the Warrens, which contained a plan to blow up the smelter at which he works.
