*"I am Sahloknir! Hear my Voice and despair!"* —Sahloknir

Sahloknir was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Phantom Sky Hunter" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> Sahloknir was among the few dragons to survive the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md) era, instead meeting his end at the hands of the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) hero [Jorg Helmbolg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jorg_Helmbolg) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), with his remains being gathered by the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) and buried in a dragon mound east of [Kyne's Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kynesgrove).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup>

By [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373), local legends still claimed him to be the dragon buried near the town, and the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) recorded him in their atlas of dragons as being deceased by report.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup> In his long dormant state his mound received some attention from other groups later on such as the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), who built Dark Water Temple in the mountains right next to it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTDL-3)</sup> By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Keepers of the Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Keepers_of_the_Grove) erected a shrine to [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md) atop the mound, where they performed various prayers and rituals to the goddess.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOSo C-4)</sup>

Upon his return in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) began the process of resurrecting the many dead dragons buried throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) by the Dragon Cult in ancient times. Sahloknir was one of the earlier dragons to be brought back to life,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mo DB-5)</sup> where his resurrection was witnessed by [Delphine](Lore_Delphine.md) and the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md). Alduin saw the two and commanded Sahloknir to kill them, but the Last Dragonborn slew Sahloknir and absorbed his soul, proving to Delphine that they truly were Dragonborn.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ABit D-6)</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Sahloknir was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skyrim-7)</sup>
