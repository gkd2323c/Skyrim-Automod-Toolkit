[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-place-Windward_Ruins_Exterior.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windward_Ruins_Exterior.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windward_Ruins_Exterior.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windward_Ruins_Exterior.jpg) Windward Ruins **Windward Ruins** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Dwellings.md) southwest of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) containing [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md).

It is one of the three ruins visited for the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Destruction_Ritual_Spell.md). With the [Vigil Enforcer Armor Set](Skyrim_Vigil_Enforcer_Armor_Set.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, [Azarain](Skyrim_Azarain.md)'s corpse can be found here.

## Related Quests
- **[Destruction Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Destruction_Ritual_Spell.md)**: Learn the most powerful [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells Skyrim has to offer.
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).
- **[Unholy Vigil](Skyrim_Unholy_Vigil.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Vigil_Enforcer_Armor_Set.md)</sup>: Investigate the mysterious disappearances of [Vigilants of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilants_of_Stendarr.md) in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md).<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup>

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Windward_Ruins_02.jpg) Windward Ruins interior There is a central circular area surrounded by an enclosed outer passage. It is guarded by two [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md) and contains an adept-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) behind a tilted pillar by the outer wall of the passage. During the [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md) quest, you will find a larger-than-life, ghostly skeever within the ruin. During the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Destruction_Ritual_Spell.md), you will need to activate a plinth in the central area to place the book *[Power of the Elements](Skyrim_Power_of_the_Elements.md)* received from [Faralda](Skyrim_Faralda.md), then cast a [flame spell](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md#Spells) at the plinth; you will then advance the quest and receive the clue to the second location. There is a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberry.md) bush in the central area.

## Notes
- Three leveled [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) have set up a camp 580 feet to the northwest below a rock ledge. The only items of note in the camp are an unlocked treasure chest and a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Hypothetical Treachery](Skyrim_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md)*.
- [Agrane Peryval](Skyrim_Agrane_Peryval.md)'s camp can be found north of Windward Ruins if you have the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](Skyrim_Saturalia_Holiday_Pack.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed.
