[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-place-Treva_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Treva_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Treva_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Treva_River.jpg) The Treva River circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/ON-place-Treva_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Treva_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Treva_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Treva_River.jpg) The Treva River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Treva River is a small river at the center of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in southeastern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It flows northwest from [Lake Honrich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lake_Honrich) to [Lake Geir](Lore_Lake_Geir.md). It was perhaps named after the eponymous founder of Treva's Farm on its eastern bank, which was built sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONTreva-1)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the site instead hosted the fort Treva's Watch.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-S2-3)</sup>
