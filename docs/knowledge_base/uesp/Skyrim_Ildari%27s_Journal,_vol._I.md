Ildari's Journal, vol. I **by [Ildari Sarothril](Skyrim_Ildari_Sarothril.md)**
---
The fools have taken me in. Weak, pathetic men intent on looting this [ancient fortress](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md) with their crude mining. [Niyya](Skyrim_Niyya.md) is pleasant enough. I may choose to spare her when the time comes.

I'm still weak from [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md)'s betrayal. He promised me power and glory. He failed to mention the constant pain. And the voices. By the three, I would do anything to not hear the voices.

When my strength returns, I will have my vengeance upon my former master. I can feel the power of the [heart stone](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) beating inside me. I need to find a way to tap into its power. Then he shall pay in blood and fire and ash.
