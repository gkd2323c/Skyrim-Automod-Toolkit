## [Udyrfrykte](Lore_Udyrfrykte.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/BM-creature-Udyrfrykte.jpg/200px-BM-creature-Udyrfrykte.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-creature-Udyrfrykte.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-creature-Udyrfrykte.jpg) The Udyrfrykte The [Udyrfrykte](Lore_Udyrfrykte.md) (also spelled **Uderfrykte** or **Udefrykte**) is the name given to members of a bloodline of legendary [trolls](Lore_Troll.md). Initially thought to be a unique individual, there are now at least three known trolls that have been recorded in history under the name.

Two of the known trolls with the name are believed to be the offspring of the **Uderfrykte Matron**, also known as the **Horror of Dive Rock**. While her origins are unknown, it is said trolls were more impressive in ancient times, and her ancient lineage has plagued the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) for centuries. Common traits between her offspring include glowing green eyes, brownish fur, and a preference for lairing in ice caves.

**Found in:** - [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:The_Udyrfrykte), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Uderfrykte_Matron), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Udefrykte.md)

## [Umbra' Keth](Lore_Umbra%27_Keth.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SK-animation-Umbra%27Keth_Idle.gif/200px-SK-animation-Umbra%27Keth_Idle.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-animation-Umbra%27Keth_Idle.gif) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-animation-Umbra%27Keth_Idle.gif) The Umbra' Keth The Umbra' Keth, or *Shadow of Conflict*, was an entity created by the [War of Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md). It was intelligent, being capable of speech through corpses, and habitually referred to itself as the Shadow of Conflict. The monster was sought by [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) and [Pergan Asuul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Pergan_Asuul) for their own ends. The creature was destroyed by the "Soul of Conflict", a [hero](Lore_Hero.md) wielding the seven [Star Teeth](Lore_Star_Teeth.md).

**Found in:** - [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Umbra%27_Keth)

## [Unicorn](Lore_Unicorn.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/OB-creature-Unicorn.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Unicorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Unicorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Unicorn.jpg) A unicorn The [Unicorn](Lore_Unicorn.md) is a mythical creature that is native prey to the [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). They resemble a white [horse](Lore_Horse.md) with a sharp [horn](Lore_Unicorn_Horn.md), which curves slightly and has a delicate spiral pattern. It is commonly described as made of pure alabaster [ivory](Lore_Ivory.md), however in actuality, it is closer to [silver](Lore_Silver.md). Although they can be found in the Hunting Grounds, an aspect of Hircine known as Alrabeg the Hunter brings these creatures into the mortal plane of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), to hunt them in new forests.

The Unicorn is a very rare creature whose very existence is constantly doubted. Hircine has described the creatures as the first named, but last tamed. In a sense, creatures like this are considered the antithesis of the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). It is believed that the death of a unicorn strengthens the elements of darkness, while the death of [Fire Daedra](Lore_Fire_Daedra.md) strengthens the aspects of light. Unicorns are greatly valued as prey by Hircine, as a result have been driven nearly extinct on Nirn by his followers.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Unicorn), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Unicorn.md)<sup>([CC](Skyrim_Wild_Horses.md))</sup>, [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Unicorn)

## [Ursauk](Lore_Ursauk.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/ON-creature-Primal_Ursauk.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Primal_Ursauk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Primal_Ursauk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Primal_Ursauk.jpg) An ursauk [Ursauks](Lore_Ursauk.md) are [bear](Lore_Bear.md) -like [mammals](Lore_Mammal.md) with strong front legs native to the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md), and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Like bears, they are often used as mounts. Though fearsome in appearance, ursauks are capable of making staunch companions when properly socialized. Some [druids](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md) are capable of transforming into ursauks, while others use them as guardians.

Like bears, they likely orginate from the frozen continent of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md). Atmoran Snow Ursuaks are a thick furred species that are able to thrive in Tamriel's northern reaches.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ursauk)
