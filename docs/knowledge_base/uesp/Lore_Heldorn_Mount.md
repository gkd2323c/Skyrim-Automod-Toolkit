[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/AR-place-Heldorn_Mount.jpg/200px-AR-place-Heldorn_Mount.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Heldorn_Mount.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Heldorn_Mount.jpg) Heldorn Mount **Heldorn Mount** is a town that was founded by the time of the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), nestled within the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) south of [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), it was ruled by Baron Shetan and had a rivalry with [Belkarth Guard](Lore_Belkarth.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup>

Heldorn Mount is neighbored by several settlements, including [Drear](Lore_Drear.md) to the west,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> Dragonstar to the north, and [Skaven](Lore_Skaven.md) to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
