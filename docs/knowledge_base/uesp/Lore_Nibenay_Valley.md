[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/ON-place-Northern_Niben_Valley.jpg/200px-ON-place-Northern_Niben_Valley.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Northern_Niben_Valley.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Northern_Niben_Valley.jpg) The Northern Nibenay Valley [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/OB-map-Nibenay_Valley.jpg/200px-OB-map-Nibenay_Valley.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Nibenay_Valley.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Nibenay_Valley.jpg) Map of the Nibenay Valley The Nibenay Valley (also called the **Niben Valley**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBDecentius Opsius-1)</sup> or the **Nibenean Valley**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBGen-2)</sup> is a subregion of [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) in eastern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), centered around the [River Niben](Lore_River_Niben.md). It is described as a tropical region enclosed by an equatorial rain forest and broken up by rivers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1-3)</sup> At the southern end of the region lies [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md), while to the north, the region extends into the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) around [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md). South of the region lies the dark and brooding [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md); in the west, the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md); to the east, the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md); and to the northwest, the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md). Running from north to south are the Green Road in the west and the Yellow Road in the east. The city of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) lies in the western portion of the region.

[Bergamot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bergamot) grows particularly densely around Bravil, as does [Mandrake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mandrake) and [Nightshade](Lore_Nightshade.md). The eastern bank of the [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md) is thick with [Foxglove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Foxglove), [Monkshood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Monkshood), and [Summer Bolete](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Summer_Bolete). Many other plants thrive in the region, including [Nirnroot](Lore_Nirnroot.md).

[Olive](Lore_Olive.md) wood of the Niben Valley is commonly used by the [House Hexos](Lore_House_Hexos.md) in the process of crafting weaponry.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CMHH-4)</sup>

## History
Over the course of the [Skyrim Conquests](Lore_Skyrim_Conquests.md) launched by [High King Vrage the Gifted](Lore_Vrage.md) in [1E 240](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_240), the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) expanded across northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). By the end of the half-century campaign, the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) had annexed, among other regions, a "deep stretch" of the Nibenay Valley. This territory remained under [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s control until the [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) resulted in the collapse of the Nords' Empire by [1E 420](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_420).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1S-5)</sup>

Following their deadly ambush at Black Rocks set by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), the surviving members of the [Third Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) went on to make up the core of the new Ruby Legion, and proceeded to spend an uneventful year on garrison duty in the Nibenay Valley in [2E 863](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_863). Just when they were "getting used to" the jungle heat, the company was then posted to [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Attrebus-6)</sup>
