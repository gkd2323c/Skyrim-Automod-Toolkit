Arch Necromancer's Final Instructions *The necromancer details the final steps you must take*
---
In my dream, I am surrounded by my four apprentices, but they are no longer mortal, but spirits, their souls taking the form of greater gems.

To bring them to darkness, one must place them on the Altar of the Revenant, and turn greater to black. Only then will you be free.
