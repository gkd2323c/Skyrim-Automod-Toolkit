[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-item-Nightweaver%27s_Band.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightweaver%27s_Band.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightweaver%27s_Band.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightweaver%27s_Band.jpg) Nightweaver's Band The Nightweaver's Band is a [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) that can be obtained from [Festus Krex](Skyrim_Festus_Krex.md) as a reward for successfully hiding [The Gourmet](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md)'s body during the quest [Recipe for Disaster](Skyrim_Recipe_for_Disaster.md). In appearance, the ring is identical to a [silver amethyst ring](Skyrim_Silver_Amethyst_Ring.md). Its enchantment [fortifies Sneak](Skyrim_Fortify_Sneak.md) and [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) by 10 points, making you harder to detect and decreasing the magicka cost of your [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells, respectively.

Festus Krex will talk briefly about the ring when he rewards you with it, saying "*It's called the Nightweaver's Band. I wore this for years. I want you to have it now. It'll give your magic and sneakiness some much needed "oomph."*

## Related Quests
- **[Recipe for Disaster](Skyrim_Recipe_for_Disaster.md)**: Assume the identity of the famous [Gourmet](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md) in an effort to assassinate the Emperor.
