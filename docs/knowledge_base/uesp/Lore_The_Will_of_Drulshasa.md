The Will of Drulshasa **by [Drulshasa](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Drulshasa)** *A call for Dremora to sacrifice mortals to Molag Bal*
---
Subordinate [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md)! Hear and obey the will of Drulshasa!

We are strong. We despise the weak and hate the frail. And we abhor the mortals most of all because they are both weak and frail. The weak must be punished by the strong! It is our calling, and it is the order of our lord and master, [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).

Find mortals. Capture, but do not kill them. They must be alive when we sacrifice their wretched souls to Molag Bal.

Do not fail me, or you shall suffer in place of the mortals. Do this, because I command it!
