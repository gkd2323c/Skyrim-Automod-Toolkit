Veloth the Pilgrim **by [Cascius the Proud](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Cascius_the_Proud)** *A biography of Saint Veloth the Pilgrim*
---
Saint [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md), also known as Veloth the Pilgrim, is arguably the most famous, and certainly the most venerated, among the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) [pantheon of saints](Lore_Temple_Saints.md). Rising to prominence in the Late-Middle [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) on [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), Veloth supposedly sought a more ascetic and pure way of life for his followers and gathered them into a grand pilgrimage from the southwest regions of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) to the northeast. According to the contemporary texts, he "spared not a boat, ration, or strong-armed soul among his people in this exodus and toiled to reach the land of [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md)."

His mass pilgrimage to a new land, where stoic values were established, was successful. The race enjoyed a period of high culture, known to many as the Golden Age, where Veloth's guidance shaped generations of stonemasons and architects, as well as priests and common folk. Although he wielded a mighty warhammer— [Veloth's Judgment](Lore_Veloth%27s_Judgment.md) —Veloth is thought of mainly as a peaceful and scholarly soul, to which the Dunmer's healing enchantments that bear his name attest.

Veloth's power as a prophet was in no doubt, but his mossback teachings on the worship of Dunmer forebears are worthy of consideration, as he almost single-handedly began the god-cult worship of the "Good [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md)" prior to the coming of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md). Veloth's people honored him so much, his influence can be felt generations later. Those trekking over the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) southeast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) or hearing a Dunmer elder refer to their race as the "Velothi" still feel the presence of this world-shaping mentor of [Mer](Lore_Mer.md).
