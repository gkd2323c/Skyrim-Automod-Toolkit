[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-item-Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber.jpg) Sheogorath-Shaped Amber The Sheogorath-Shaped Amber is an unusually shaped piece of [amber](Skyrim_Amber.md) which bears some resemblance to the Daedric Prince [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md). It can be found at [Thoron's](Skyrim_Thoron.md) camp in the [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md).

It can be given to [Staada](Skyrim_Staada.md) during [Staada's quest](Skyrim_Staada_Quest.md) in order to keep the [Sword of Jyggalag](Skyrim_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md), though you may choose to keep both and fight her instead.

Its other use is as an ingredient at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md). Only one of the following options can be chosen, as the amber will be destroyed in the process (does not require the [Sigil Stone](Skyrim_Sigil_Stone.md)):

- 1. Summon Staada (hostile): Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + [Emerald](Skyrim_Emerald.md)
- 2. Create [Conjure Staada](Skyrim_Conjure_Staada.md) Spell Tome: Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + [Ruined Book](Skyrim_Ruined_Book.md) + [Bliss Bug Thorax](Skyrim_Bliss_Bug_Thorax.md) + [Sabre Cat Pelt](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat_Pelt.md)

## Notes
- Sheogorath-Shaped amber also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber) in the [Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles) expansion for [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion).
- Cannot be used for amber-smithing.
- The atronach forge recipes are the same as the [Golden Saint](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md) recipes, but with the regular amber swapped for this unique sample. It is mentioned in the [report on Atronach Forge experiments](Skyrim_Report_on_Atronach_Forge_Experiments.md) by [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md), but he deemed it too risky to try.
