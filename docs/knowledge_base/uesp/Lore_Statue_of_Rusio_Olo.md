Statue of Rusio Olo *A plaque honoring an Order of the Hour knight*
---
The third pillar of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md)'s original complement of warrior-priests, [Rusio Olo](Lore_Rusio_Olo.md), was instrumental in ending the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md)'s infestation of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) in [2E 437](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_437). He dispatched eight cultists and their leader singlehandedly.
