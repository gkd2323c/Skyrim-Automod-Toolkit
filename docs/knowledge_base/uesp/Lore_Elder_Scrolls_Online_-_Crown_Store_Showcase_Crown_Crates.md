# Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Crown Crates

This page lists **Crown Crate** information from the [Crown Store Showcase](Lore_Elder_Scrolls_Online_-_Crown_Store_Showcase.md).

## Akatosh Vs. Alduin Crown Crate Season
[Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md). The great [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) god of time, worshipped across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) since before recorded history. [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md). The World Eater, "First-Born" of the Dragons and the corruption of [a dark prophecy](Lore_Prophecy_of_the_Dragonborn.md). Their followers, cultists, and warriors have clashed for centuries. Dark blades spark against those of glittering gold. Fights within the family are always the most terrible. Akatosh v. Alduin. Which side will you choose?

## Akaviri Potentate Crown Crate Season
Conceived in treachery and secured by bloodshed, the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) ushered in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), along with a new chapter of [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) rule. For four-hundred years, the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md), [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md), and his heirs ruled a fractious continent. Over time, the snakemen's classical refinement merged with stubborn [Reman](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) values to create something completely new—a civilization built on the fault line of Tamriel and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md), blending the virtues of both into an unlikely whole.

Despite the roiling chaos of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), Potentate relics still find their way into hands of collectors. Will you herald the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md)'s return? The grace of Akavir and pride of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) are yours for the taking!

## Ayleid Crown Crate Season
Before the coming of the [First Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), [Elves](Lore_Elves.md) reigned supreme. The [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) sorcerer-kings held dominion over Tamriel's [heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md), harnessing the power of [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) alike. Now, within the Wild Elves' [bleached stone ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) and [crystal](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md) -lit crypts, ancient treasures still lay buried, waiting for brave adventurers to claim them… or die trying.

So, lift your torch and descend the [alabaster](Lore_Alabaster_(mineral).md) stairs. The wealth of the Ayleids awaits!

## Baandari Pedlar Crown Crate Season
The rattle of trinkets, the hiss of a tambourine, and the creak of a wagon's wheels can mean only one thing: [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md)! These famed [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) nomads travel the width and breadth of Tamriel, reveling in the freedom of the open road. While some regard these colorful wanderers with suspicion, others know that there's no better place to find unusual treasures than the back of a Baandari cart. So, step right up, walker, and have a look at these exotic wares!

## Buoyant Armiger Crown Crate Season
"Poetry is the sharpest blade a warrior can wield. Do you have the wherewithal to stand your ground, as does a proud [kwama queen](Lore_Kwama_Queen.md)? Or do you offer your throat as the gentle [guar](Lore_Guar.md)? Take up the [glassine](Lore_Malachite.md) armor of the [Buoyant Armiger](Lore_Buoyant_Armiger.md) in service to Lord [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)!

And in return for your service and artistry? Ride forth with treasures from the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). Arm yourself with flair and panache, or seek the best the [mycoturges](Lore_Mycoturges.md) have to offer. This is your moment, Armiger. In Vehk's name!"

## Carnaval Crown Crate Season
Blood on the air, [wine](Lore_Wine.md) in your cup, and [music](Lore_Music.md) all around you. It must be? It is! Carnaval is here! The [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) is throwing a party and everyone is just dying to get in. This raucous party celebrates living at its most debauched. Will you dance till you drop? Perhaps drink deeply of the dangerous brew in the kettle? And most importantly, will you walk away from this celebration with your [soul](Lore_Soul.md) intact? Let's find out together at Car-na-val!

## Celestial Crown Crate Season
Those who wander Tamriel in search of answers need only look to the [night sky](Lore_Astronomy.md). Guardians, omens, and sacred signs drift overhead ceaselessly, offering wisdom to any who seek it. For some, however, wisdom is not enough. Avatars of the [constellations](Lore_Constellations.md) — [Celestials](Lore_Celestial.md) —wield power that even the greatest sorcerers can scarcely comprehend. Now, their gifts are just within reach.

The time has come to gaze skyward and seize the blessings of the stars!

## Dark Brotherhood Crown Crate Season
Crimson limned shadows move just beyond sight. Blades glint in the darkness as the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md)'s children move to strike. A [Black Sacrament](Lore_Black_Sacrament.md) has been enacted. Will you take up the implements of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md)? [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) whispers secrets to those willing to do what must be done. Taste the flavor of fear, and enter the Sanctuary. After all. We Know.

## Dark Chivalry Crown Crate Season
When the silver trumpets fall silent and the [sun](Lore_Sun.md) sets upon the tourney field, a new breed of knight emerges. Clad in dark armor and driven by malice alone, these warriors parade through the shadows—ghoulish reflections of chivalric splendor. In this world of shattered lances and blood-spattered shields, glory is the province of killers and honor is a cruel joke. Do you dare renounce the code of chivalry and seize these profane gifts? The champions of darkness await.

## Dragonscale Crown Crate Season
Dragons! These ancient creatures, once locked away in the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md), have returned with a vengeance. Driven by an unquenchable thirst for conquest, they will stop at nothing to reclaim their place as the rightful rulers of Tamriel. While few mortals can match the strength of a raging Dragon, the contents of these mysterious crates might give you a taste of their awesome power!

## Dwarven Crown Crate Season
The ancient and long-lost race of the [Dwarves](Lore_Dwemer.md) delved deep beneath the surface of Tamriel, where they created subterranean cities of metal and stone maintained by [mechanical constructs](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md), automata that still work today millennia after their masters mysteriously disappeared. In the vaults below [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md), the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) collected thousands of rare Dwarven artifacts. When the city was sacked by the forces of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), these items were smuggled out, and are now appearing on the open market.

## Flame Atronach Crown Crate Season
It was the master summoners of the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) who first discovered that a [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md) could impose any desired shape on a [Flame Atronach](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md). For centuries the conjurers' standard spell for summoning Flame Atronachs has been Koron's Peremptory Summons, which is easily modified to change both the duration of the summoned atronach's binding and its form—for example, into that of a [Senche-Tiger](Lore_Senche-Tiger.md).

## Gloomspore Crown Crate Season
Few adventurers ever tread the damp and oppressive darkness of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md). Those that do find timeworn Dwarven ruins glowering over seas of shimmering geodes and grand fungal forests that shed ghostly clouds of luminous spores. These vistas inspire awe, but terror soon follows.

Chitinous monstrosities scuttle behind each glowing mushroom, and eyeless horrors lurk in every dark corner. Do you dare to peer into this eerie gloom? Lift your torch and behold these treasures … if you dare!

## Grim Harlequin Crown Crate Season
Any [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) cultist can tell you there are two sides to every jest. The frivolous antics of Baandari tricksters can bring smiles and laughter, but there is a dark side as well—the dimly lit stage of ridicule, cruelty, and shame. In the shadow of a wicked harlequin, laughter can raze kingdoms and jokes can cut to the bone.

Will you claim the power of joyous anguish? The unnerved masses await your performance!

## Hidden Kindred Crown Crate Season
Pestilence draws nightmarish visions from a fevered mind. Dark dreams fester in the soul, a plague of phantasms. The cult of the Hidden Kindred have united in their worship of [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) (the Queen of Nightmares) and [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) (the Blighted Lord). Seize the treasures of this unlikely alliance. Ride forth on [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) -tainted cult beasts, and wear the styles of these dark disciples. The Hidden Kindred offer power. Do you have the strength to claim it for your own?

## Hollowjack Crown Crate Season
Lord [Hollowjack](Lore_Hollowjack.md), the Lord of Mortal Fears! Once a year the portals open between [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and Hollowjack's [demi-plane](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) of [Detritus](Lore_Detritus.md), and items ghastly and gruesome come on to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s markets. Though merchants and mountebanks jest about the possible contents of their Hollowjack Crates, their jokes can't hide the terrible truth. Dare you open these haunted crates to reveal and release their dreaded contents?

## Iron Atronach Crown Crate Season
A [volcanic realm](Lore_Realm_of_the_Iron_Atronachs.md) drifts silently through the void of Oblivion. Impossibly dense and boiling with malice, this molten hellscape produces only one thing in abundance: [Iron Atronachs](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) —towering behemoths driven by a relentless appetite for destruction. While few [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) dare to call upon these titans, those that do gain powerful servants… or a grisly death.

Do you have the strength to tame these colossi? Summon up your iron will and claim these spoils of Oblivion for your own!

## Mirrormoor Crown Crate Season
[Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) sits at the center, a nexus of pathways through Oblivion. Yet long ago it was known by another name: [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md). Shattered glass catches the light of a thousand realms. [Moth](Lore_Moth.md) wings beat against a distant sky. This strange realm holds secrets only the brave might claim.

Will you take up its crystalline arms and armor? Seek answers for the dream-shadowed blank within your mind? The many paths of Oblivion await.

## New Moon Crown Crate Season
Not all threats to Tamriel sail overhead on leathery wings. Some lurk in the crime-ridden alleys of Senchal and the charred remains of villages long forgotten by those in power. Beware, people of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). The [New Moon Cult](Lore_New_Moon_Cult.md) rises!

This army of embittered outcasts pledged their lives to the ancient scourge, [Laatvulon](Lore_Laatvulon.md). In exchange, they received dark gifts in abundance. Now, you too can reap the spoils of this vile bargain!

## Nightfall Crown Crates
In the eternal night of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), the thirst for blood is rivaled only by the thirst for power. Within their gothic fortresses, [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) plot and conspire—seeking the ruin of meddlesome mortals and undead rivals alike. Through intrigue, murder, and worse, these dark sovereigns gather spoils beyond counting. Only the bravest (or most foolhardy) adventurers try to claim these treasures for themselves.

Do you dare gaze upon their blood-soaked plunder? Gather your courage and cunning. Nightfall draws near!

## Order of the Lamp Crown Crate Season
Raise up your blade, oh [Knight of the Lamp](Lore_Order_of_the_Lamp.md). The Mages Guild calls for aid! Stand as a bulwark, protect magic wherever it dwells, and take your oath as a Votary in the Order. Don the armor of your sacred charge and stand tall beside the adepts of the guild. Protect them as they seek ancient secrets in caves below, in realms beyond, and even in the mind within. As a Knight in service to spellcraft and sorcery your duty is clear. Will you serve?

## Psijic Vault Crown Crate Season
The [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) Vaults: for millennia the mages of the monastic order have been storing items rare and mysterious in their subterranean strong rooms, some strange, some dangerous, all valuable. Now, it seems a time breach was open in these vaults for an unknown period, spilling Psijic Vault Crates out into the wider world, and their weird contraband is beginning to appear on Tamriel's open market. They're going fast, because a Psijic Vault Crate might contain almost… anything.

## Ragebound Crown Crate Season
Stalk the wilds in frost-sharpened air. Tame mounts fueled by the fury of ancient battles. Track prey along the river [Karth](Lore_Karth_River.md) and sing songs of glory to [Red Eagle](Lore_Red_Eagle.md), Diamond's Doom. Do you dare take up arms alongside the [witchfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md)?

These venerable [clans](Lore_Reachfolk_Clans.md) hold fast to ancient traditions, passed down by the tale-telling [vateshrans](Lore_Vateshrans.md). What part will you play in their tales to come? When will you rage?

## Reaper's Harvest Crown Crate Season
Tamriel's covens of witches and warlocks spent all year crafting [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) objects of dark delight for the season of the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md). The sale of these occult oddities and twisted treats are usually restricted as contraband goods, but in honor of the Witches Festival they may be freely sold or given as gifts. (Please note that the merchants of Tamriel will not accept responsibility for curses bestowed or injuries sustained through the use of these arcane artifacts.)

## Scalecaller Crown Crate Season
The dark secrets of Scalecaller Peak and [Fang Lair](Lore_Fang_Lair.md) have remained undisturbed for untold centuries, but now their gates have been opened and their grim denizens have burst forth upon the wider world of Tamriel. Do you have the strength of will to master these sinister creations and use them for your own purposes?

## Sovngarde Crown Crate Season
A noble death is not the end. Beyond the crash of steel and final cries of glory lies a realm unlike any other. [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md)! The immortal realm of heroes where [mead](Lore_Mead.md) flows like a mighty river and great adventures wait behind every windswept hill.

Luminous beasts, king-forged weapons, and other aethereal wonders await you beyond the veil. Now, summon up your warrior spirit and seize them for your own!

## Stonelore Crown Crate Season
Beneath massive trees and deep underground in moss-clad caverns, the [druids](Lore_Druids.md) of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) practice their art. [Wisps](Lore_Wisp.md) flow like water as followers of the [True Way](Lore_True_Way.md) raise their hands under star-strewn skies. Will you don the garb of their ancient order?

Summon forth the glories of nature, arm yourself to fight the [Allwither](Lore_Allwither.md), and seek your rewards amidst the stone circles that dot the wilds. The glories of the green await!

## Sunken Trove Crown Crate Season
Look sharp there, sailor! Beneath the waters of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) lie treasures untold. Riches lost in sunken wrecks, the glittering remnants of ancient cities, and the bizarre dregs of mystic cults. Do you have what it takes to brave [Sea Elf](Lore_Sea_Elf.md) pirates and their [serpents](Lore_Sea_Serpent.md)? Those beasts are twice the size of any ship!

Aye! I knew you had it in you! Join our expedition, sail the dark waters, and plumb the depths! The spoils of the sea await!

## Unfeathered Crown Crate Season
From across the plains of Cyrodiil comes a war trumpet's cry. The soul quakes as gilded armor, gleaming in the sun, cuts across the battlefield for the first time in uncounted centuries. [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md)'s champion may have fallen an age ago, but those who venerate [the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril.md) still carry out his cruel will.

The dark will of the ancient [Heartland High Elves](Lore_Heartland_High_Elf.md) reborn, blood spilled in the Bright Lady's name. Can a remorseless champion such as Umaril ever truly be slain? Or will the Unfeathered one day make good his curse to cast down the [mortal gods](Lore_Divines.md)?

## Warrior Wave Crown Crate Season
A storm may snap a timber mast, a wave may sink a ship. But the will of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) is unbreakable. We wake ashore, face covered in sand and spitting up sea water. Like the tides we rise again! We ride into that thunderous horizon, and carve our names onto the sea itself. The typhoons, the beasts, the myths given form? Well, sailor, they're all part of the fun. Your voyage awaits! All hands on deck!

## Wild Hunt Crown Crate Season
In the [Beginning Times](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md)'s Naming gave all the creatures of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) their shapes. When the [Wood Elves](Lore_Wood_Elves.md) invoke the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md), creatures forget their Y'ffre-taught shapes and shift into unnatural forms. It's a dangerous power, because once unleashed, it's almost impossible to control, and the effects may spread beyond what was intended. Wild Hunt-affected creatures revert to the shapeshifting chaos of the Dawn Era, becoming part plant and part animal, as wonderful as they are abominable.

## Wraithtide Crown Crate Season
Beneath the turquoise waves, beyond the sun-drenched beaches of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), inky blackness stretches long fathoms into the dark below. There the dead lie dreaming, unquiet and hungry. But not for long! Avast, sailor, for the wraith tide rises!

With them comes lost wealth from the [All-Flags Navy](Lore_All-Flags_Navy.md), residuum from the great [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) necromancy pits, and pickings from the [Hadolid](Lore_Hadolid.md) brine hoards. You never know what the wraith tide will bring! Join our expedition, sail the Abecean sea, and brave the eerie glow of this high seas haunt.

## Xanmeer Crown Crate Season
Xanmeers! Those mysterious [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) step pyramids of the deep swamp. Made from stone as if intended to last thousands of years, but then largely abandoned due to—what? No one in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) seems to know, nor do they seem to care that many Xanmeers are packed with old relics others find valuable. Somehow those abandoned relics, enchanted and mundane, get smuggled out of Black Marsh, crated up, and then made available—to you!
