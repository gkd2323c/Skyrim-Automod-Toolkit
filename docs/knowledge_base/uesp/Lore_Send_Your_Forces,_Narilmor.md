[Narilmor](Lore_King_Narilmor.md),

Will you wait until the [Men](Lore_Man.md) are at your gates before you raise your sword against them? Enough excuses. The [coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) is clear and the storm is inland, leave your vigil and march to war. Our siege at [Mzulft](Lore_Mzulft.md) will fail if our supply lines are not maintained and they cannot reach us by sea!

The [Wrathstone](Lore_Wrathstone.md) is nearly in hand, don't throw it away out of stubborn [Meridian](Lore_Meridia.md) obligation!

Thadoril
