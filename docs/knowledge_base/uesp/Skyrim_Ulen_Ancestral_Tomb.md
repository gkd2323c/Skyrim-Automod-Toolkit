[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-place-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Ulen Ancestral Tomb The **Ulen Ancestral Tomb** is a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) ancestral tomb in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

It is southeast of the flight of stairs leading up to the [Temple](Skyrim_Temple.md), opposite the entrance to [Caerellius House](Skyrim_Caerellius_House.md).

It is where members of the Ulen family of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) are interred if they die on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). The last member of the family interred here was Vilur Ulen, who in 4E 95 was the lead conspirator in a House Hlaalu plot to assassinate the current [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) Councilor of Raven Rock, [Lleril Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md). The councilor ordered Vilur's execution for his part in the planned coup, which was intended to wrest control of Raven Rock from House Redoran.

## Related Quests

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance are three [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plants, a food urn, and a lit brazier. To the right of the entrance are four more scathecraw plants and a [trama root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trama_Root). The entrance is unlocked.

### Ulen Ancestral Tomb
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-interior-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Ulen Ancestral Tomb interior The entrance from outside leads into a corridor lined with four lit braziers and containing two benches and three ash yam urns which proceeds north to the tomb room. In the room, to the west is an alcove with a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Tomb_Key) -locked door to the [Temple Ancestral Tomb](Skyrim_Temple_Ancestral_Tomb.md). The key can be obtained as part of the quest [Clean Sweep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clean_Sweep). The room and an alcove to the north feature a total of four ash piles, with the central pile littered with bones and surrounded by steps and lit candles. Also in the room and north alcove are two ingredient urns, two ash yam urns, twenty-two unusable barrels, and two more lit braziers around the central pile.
