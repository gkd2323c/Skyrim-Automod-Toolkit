## Tel Mithryn Apothecary
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.jpg) Tel Mithryn Apothecary interior Elynea Mothren will sell apothecary supplies between 8am to 8pm. At other times, the entrance will be novice-locked.

The entrance leads into a small circular room comprising the entire interior. To the east is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) holding a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), six samples of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota), a [potion of plentiful stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Plentiful_Stamina), two [potions of ultimate magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Ultimate_Magicka), a [paralysis poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paralysis_Poison), a [draught of resist shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Resist_Shock), a sample of [bleeding crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown), and a sample of [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot), with two [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) and a [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) growing on the floor around the table. To the south is an open box containing two samples of bleeding crown, a sample of fly amanita, a sample of [imp stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imp_Stool), a sample of Namira's rot, and a sample of white cap, a crate with a [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) on top, a set of shelves with a potion of ultimate magicka on the floor in front of the shelves, a chair, and a white cap growing on the floor. The set of shelves holds a crate, a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), three [enchanter's philters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Philter), a [weak frenzy poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Frenzy_Poison), three potions of plentiful stamina, a [potion of vigorous magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigorous_Magicka), three paralysis poisons, a bowl of [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md), a sample of [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), a bowl of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust), a [swamp fungal pod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod), a sample of scaly pholiota, and two draughts of resist shock. To the west is a fly amanita growing on the floor next to a stack of crates, an owned single bed, and two [empty wine bottles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Empty_Wine_Bottle) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on the floor around the bed.

## Notes
- After completing the quest [Healing a House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_a_House), all low-value items in the building become unowned and free to take.
