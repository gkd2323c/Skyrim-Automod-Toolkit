[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lilmoth_02.jpg) Lilmoth circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Xhon-Mehl **the Fisher** was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) figure of importance. He served as the **Ancestral Ascendant Organ Lord of Lilmoth** at some point in history, long before [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48). A statue of his likeness was erected in [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md), but by the time of 4E 48, it was sunken into the marsh up to the snout.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:34</sup> It was not uncommon for more permanent stone structures to meet that fate there. Many of the stone villas of the Old [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Lilmoth suffered a similar fate, as swamp had reclaimed it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AHOL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TA-3)</sup>
