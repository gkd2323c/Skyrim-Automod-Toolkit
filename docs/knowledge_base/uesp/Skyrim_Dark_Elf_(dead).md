[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-npc-Dark_Elf_%28Peddler%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dark_Elf_%28Peddler%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Dark_Elf_(Peddler).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Dark_Elf_(Peddler).jpg.md) The dead Dark Elf peddler A dead **Dark Elf** peddler<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md)</sup> is found at a camp southeast of [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md). [His journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Peddler%27s_Journal) is found on his body, detailing how he made a deal with rieklings for netch leather and presumably died fighting off the nearby [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md). He wears a [Dunmer Outfit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Outfit) and [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Shoes). Additionally he carries a [Purple Mountain Flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) and a piece of [charcoal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charcoal), as referenced in his journal to be part of dyeing the leather. He may also carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a small amount of gold.
