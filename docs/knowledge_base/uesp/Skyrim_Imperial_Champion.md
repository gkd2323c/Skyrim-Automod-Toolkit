[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-npc-Imperial_Champion.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_Champion.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Champion.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Champion.jpg) Imperial Champion The Imperial Champion is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) warrior acting as the Champion of the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md).

If you decide to side with the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) during the related quest, the Champion will appear at the battle location on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) alongside several [Imperial soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md). You will need to kill him and loot everything from his corpse in order to complete the quest. You can speak to him briefly before the battle commences.

The Champion wears [Imperial Dragon armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Dragon_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Dragon_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Imperial_Dragon_Gauntlets.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Dragon_Helmet.md), and in combat he wields [Akatosh's Talon](Skyrim_Akatosh%27s_Talon.md). He also carries [Dragon's Oath](Skyrim_Dragon%27s_Oath.md) and the [Imperial Dragon shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Dragon_Shield.md).
