It is built along the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md), to protect the large body of water from the mass pirate activities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-2)</sup>

## History
The Istirus Outpost was built in the late years of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md). There was a growing piracy problem across the Strid River, and the fort was created to administrate the water route. However, when the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) commenced across Cyrodiil, around [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580), the outpost was abandoned and plundered by the alliances. It has also been contested over by the alliances, but eventually, none of them were able to acquire it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLoading-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> It is also located just east and outside of [Varen's Wall](Lore_Varen%27s_Wall_(place).md), a stone wall that separates the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) from the rest of the province.

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Istirus Outpost was used by the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md), who used the ruins as one of their battlegrounds. Three teams battled each other at Istirus.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOIstirus-1)</sup>

Many years later in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Fort Istirus was abandoned once again and inhabited by a coven of necromancers. The ruins were one of the four places across the provinces to contain an altar needed in the creation of [Black Soul Gems](Lore_Black_Soul_Gem.md). The phenomenon where these gems are created is known as the *"Shade of the Revenant."*<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HSN-4)</sup>
