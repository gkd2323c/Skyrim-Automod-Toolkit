Conversation with Olugush, Covenant soldier **by [Olugush](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Olugush)** *An Orcish soldier's thoughts on membership in the Daggerfall Covenant*
---
[![ON-concept-Stormhaven load screen.png](https://images.uesp.net/4/43/ON-concept-Stormhaven_load_screen.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Stormhaven_load_screen.png)
- "The other soldiers treat me like an equal most of the time, at least. I'm a little sick of the glares I get from some of the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), but the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) haven't been bad so far. Well, except the ones in [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) — [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) in particular. Guess they think they shouldn't have to associate with 'my kind.' They're going to have to get used to it, even with our less-than-friendly history. [The Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) is a done deal, the past is the past, and there isn't any way they could fend off the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) without us [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md). They need our strength and our smiths, and if they want those, then they're going to have to accept us and show some respect."
