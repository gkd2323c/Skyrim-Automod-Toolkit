# Magna-Geode

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/ON-furnishing-Magna-Geode.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Magna-Geode.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Magna-Geode.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Magna-Geode.jpg) A Magna-Geode Magna-Geodes are glowing, roughly oval-shaped crystals of varied sizes with a polyhedral structure, typically golden in color. They bear a resemblance in appearance to fragments of [meteoric glass](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Furniture-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Furniture2-2)</sup>

They are commonly found in places of worship dedicated to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of [Light](Lore_Elements.md#Light), the fallen [Magna Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md) [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md). One such location where multiple Magna-Geodes have been found was the [ruined](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) settlement of [Garlas Malatar](Lore_Garlas_Malatar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DOM-3)</sup> Another notable location was the [Kilkreath Temple](Lore_Kilkreath_Temple.md), located on [Mount Kilkreath](Lore_Mount_Kilkreath.md) and built in the ancient [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) architectural style. It housed a particularly special magna-geode.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MB-4)</sup>

Two of Meridia's artifacts, her [Beacon](Lore_Meridia%27s_Beacon.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SMB-5)</sup> and [Brilliance](Lore_Meridia%27s_Brilliance.md), are shaped like magna-geodes.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MB-4)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Quest-6)</sup> The Starry Globe was a Magna-Geode stored in the realm of [Deadlight](Lore_Deadlight.md), and it revealed a memory where [Celdina](Lore_Celdina.md) shared her philosophy on [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md) with her daughter [Mairead](Lore_Mairead.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Mairead-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Deadlight-8)</sup>

## Notes
- The crystal's name bears resemblance to the Magna Ge, a family of divine beings descended from Magnus, who crafted his daughters, including Meridia, from aether in his forges.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TAS-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-9C-10)</sup>
