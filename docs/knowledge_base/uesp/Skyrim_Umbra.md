[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-menu-Umbra.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Umbra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Umbra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Umbra.jpg) Umbra **Umbra** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds the [Umbra Sword](Skyrim_Umbra_(item).md), located in a new dungeon known as [Champion's Rest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Champion%27s_Rest). It was released on March 14, 2019 for 500 Creation Credits.

Some of the art was created by [Elianora](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3231858).

## Official Summary
Creation Club *The history of this Daedric artifact is recounted in the rise and fall of those that have wielded it. Forged from wicked hands and vile mind, legend says that Umbra itself lives. Umbra introduces a brand new Skyrim dungeon featuring stunning visuals, puzzle solving, and an exhilarating boss fight! Additional Art Support by Elianora. (Quest "Vile Whispers" starts by reading the Vigilant's Report in Champion's Rest, found in the mountains north-northeast of Riften.)* Sneak Peek - Mar 2019 *The history of this Daedric artifact is recounted in the rise and fall of those that have wielded it. Forged from wicked hands and vile mind, legend says that Umbra itself lives, its echoes whispering from the winding halls of Champion's Rest. Some even say the sword wields its bearer, but to find the answer, you must first defeat Umbra yourself. Umbra introduces a brand new dungeon to Skyrim featuring stunning visuals, puzzle solving, and an exhilarating boss fight, culminating in the acquisition of the iconic sword.*

## Notes
- Umbra was destroyed at the climax of [*Lord of Souls*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Lord_of_Souls), the second of two novels written by Greg Keyes. This took place in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), over 150 years before the events of *Skyrim*.
- This Creation includes the track "Daedra in Flight" from the [*Oblivion* soundtrack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Music) (named "MUSCombat Boss Umbra" in the files); it will play during the final boss fight.
