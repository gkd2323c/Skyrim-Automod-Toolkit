[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Mournoth is found Mournoth was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) fiefdom of northern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) at the east end of [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). Its northern border was formed by the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md), across which lay the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) provinces of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) and [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon_(fiefdom).md). To the west lay the region [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam_(county).md), to the southwest was the region [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md), and it was surrounded on all other sides by the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> The region's capital was the city of Mournoth. After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), it was purportedly under the control of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWITW-2)</sup>

The regional deity was [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) and the provincial capital was the city of Mournoth. There were no special holidays in this province. As this region was in Hammerfell, [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) were the primary race that made their homes in Mournoth. The dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region was [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
