Nohotogrha is an oasis within [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). The [Devotees](Lore_Satakals.md) of [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md) have been seen rolling in the desert sand sidewinder-fashion in continuous, hundred-mile stretches, from [Balhar](Lore_Balhar.md) all the way to the Nohotogrha oasis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-1)</sup>

The [face sculptor](Lore_Flesh_Sculpture.md) [Galathil](Lore_Galathil.md) of the [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) Faculty of Chirurgeons claimed to have walked with the Hollow-Faced Men of Nohotogrha for three years.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Galahad-2)</sup>
