When worn with it, the full suit offers 97 protection for 53 weight, outclassing [Ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) armor on both counts. With the standard helmet, the suit *matches* Ebony for protection, but weighs 7 (11.29%) weight units less.
## Notes
- Ironically, Falmer Hardened Armor is heavier than Falmer Heavy Armor, especially the cuirass (40 weight vs. 35). Also, so-called "hardened" armor offers less protection than heavy.
- Falmer can wear any combination of hardened and heavy armor, though both types of boots seem to be rarer than other pieces.

## Bugs
- The Falmer axes (both Honed and regular) use the "sword" sound effect when being sheathed and unsheathed, not the waraxe sound effect.
