## Notes
- Damaged magicka regeneration affects enemies who mostly rely on magic to deal damage, such as [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md), [fire mages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Mage), etc. Depriving them of magicka means they can no longer cast spells (once they run out of magicka) for the duration of the potion, and will be forced to use their melee weapon or a staff, if they have one.
- Damage magicka regeneration can be useful against [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), who rely on magicka to use their version of Shouts.
- The magnitude of damaged Magicka regeneration is always 100%, regardless of Alchemy perks and Fortify Alchemy enchanted apparel worn at the time of the creation of the poison; what increases is the duration, which is how long the affected will suffer stunted magicka regeneration.
- If you are a vampire, your magicka won't regenerate during the day when outdoors.
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md) levels will cause your magicka to regenerate more slowly.
