The Felling of Windweaper's Grove *A knightly edict calls for the death of any creature that threatens the construction of a temple of Arkay* *
---
By the order of Knightmaster Alyxe Justal and the edict of [1E 2907](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2907), we are hereby mandated to clear the Windweaper's Grove of all forms of wildlife both violent and benevolent.

From this day forth, no sort of tree-life, [lurchers](Lore_Lurcher.md), Graft-root beings, insects, or [squirrels](Lore_Squirrel.md) will stifle our site of construction. For that is the cause of this edict. Where the Windweaper's grove now stands, is being consigned to the flow of progress and civilization. Knightmaster Alyxe Justal commissioned the construction of a temple of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), in honor of the sacred grove protected by the [spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md), currently in residence there, as well as new barracks for the ever-growing militia protecting our great town.

In the name of [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md), may our work be productive and our toil not without merit.
May Arkay accept our temple, constructed in his name.
And, most importantly, may [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) allow our work to pass quickly.
