Matthild Built This Place *A poem dedicated to the life of Matthild* *
---
At sixty winters she left [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) for good

She found this place and made it her home

At sixty-one summers she built this place

She took only from the ground to make it

As is the custom in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)

At sixty-two summers she met [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md)

She offered to teach the ways of [her people](Lore_Nord.md)

For she knew much of making things

At sixty-two winters she taught [his people](Lore_Bosmer.md)

She learned as much as she taught

And was loved by all her students

At eighty winters she went to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md)

She left this place to all who would teach

And to all who would learn
