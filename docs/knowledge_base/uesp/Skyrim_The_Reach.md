[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-place-The_Reach.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Reach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Reach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Reach.jpg) Part of the Reach **The Reach** is a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) as its capital. It is initially affiliated with the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), but can be captured by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) after completing [Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Stormcloaks).md) and [The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md).

The terrain is very mountainous, containing the [Druadach Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Mountains). The far eastern edge of the hold from [Broken Tower Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Broken_Tower_Redoubt) in the north to [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) in the south is more hilly. The mighty [Karth River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River) and several smaller tributaries originate here.

The Reach is home to the native [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), who will attack any outsiders on sight.

## Flora and Fauna
The most characteristic flora are the [juniper berry bushes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries) which are native to the Reach. [Hanging moss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss), more common to caves, also seems to thrive there. [Mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) and sparse populations of [mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower) are to be found throughout the hold but in the northeast the flowers can be quite common.

## The Reach Quests
Quests that are started in or related to the Reach (except for radiant quests begun in other holds) are listed here:

### [Main Quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md)
- **[Alduin's Wall](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Wall.md)**: Locate the prophecy within [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md).
- **[Elder Knowledge](Skyrim_Elder_Knowledge.md)**: Recover an [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md) to learn an [Ancient Secret](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md).

### [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) Civil War Quests
- **[Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md)**: Reunite Skyrim under the leadership of the Empire. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Imperial).md)**: Retake [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) for the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) Civil War Quests
- **[Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md)**: Drive the Imperials from Skyrim. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Stormcloaks).md)**: Blackmail a [Talos worshipper](Skyrim_Raerek.md) into helping the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md)**: Take [this fort](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) in the name of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### [Daedric Quests](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md)
- **[The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md)**: Prepare a [feast](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md) for the [Lady of Decay](Lore_Namira.md).
- **[The Only Cure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Only_Cure)**: Slay an apostate [worshipper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orchendor) for the Daedra Lord [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).
- **[The House of Horrors](Skyrim_The_House_of_Horrors.md)**: Help [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) take revenge upon a servant of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)
- **[Mourning Never Comes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mourning_Never_Comes)**: Help [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md) get her revenge.
- **[Recipe for Disaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Recipe_for_Disaster)**: Assume the identity of the famous [Gourmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Balagog_gro-Nolob) in an effort to assassinate the Emperor.
- **[Kill Ma'randru-jo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Ma%27randru-jo)**: Assassinate [Ma'randru-jo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ma%27randru-jo) to complete a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) contract.
- **[Locate the Assassin of Old](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locate_the_Assassin_of_Old)**: Find the Dark Brotherhood [Assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin).

### [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)
- **[Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers)**: Help translate [Gallus's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gallus%27s_Encoded_Journal).
- **[No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md)**: Find the [24 gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) to restore the Crown of Barenziah.
- **[Silver Lining](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Lining)**: Recover a stolen [silver mold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Curious_Silver_Mold) for a [silversmith](Skyrim_Endon.md).
- **[The Litany of Larceny](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Litany_of_Larceny)**: Find items for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) throughout the [questline](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Primary_Quests).

### [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md)
- **[Evil in Waiting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Evil_in_Waiting)**: Seal away an [ancient evil](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md).
- **[Forbidden Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forbidden_Legend)**: Investigate the forgotten [Gauldur Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legends).
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).
- **[The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md)**: Find a new [Sybil](Skyrim_Fjotra.md) for the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md).
- **[The Lost Expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition)**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel).
- **[No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md)**: Escape from [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md)!
- **[Rebel's Cairn](Skyrim_Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest).md)**: Find [Red Eagle's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle) [sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle%27s_Bane).

### [Dawnguard Quests](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md)
- **[Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>: Search for the Dwemer's [Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge).
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

### [Creation Club Quests](Skyrim_Creation_Club_Quests.md)

- **[If I had a Hammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:If_I_had_a_Hammer)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Stendarr%27s_Hammer.md)</sup>: Steal a legendary hammer of the gods.

- **[Relics of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>: Reclaim the holy relics of Pelinal Whitestrake.

## Bugs
- Manually mining ore in Cidhna Mine can make it impossible to serve your sentence here properly. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.5, fixes this bug.
- The guard at Soljund's Sinkhole will always be an Empire-affiliated Reach Hold Guard, due to `Soljund Sinkhole Location` missing keywords for changing the garrison if the Stormcloaks take the Reach. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- The Imperial guard who patrols near Salvius Farm is called "Markarth City Guard", even though he isn't in the city. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The guard is now called "Reach Hold Guard".
- Due to the fact that [Ramati](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati), [Mani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani), and [Sudi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi) at [Frostflow Lighthouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse) are part of the Reach crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill them, then you could theoretically receive a bounty here. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
