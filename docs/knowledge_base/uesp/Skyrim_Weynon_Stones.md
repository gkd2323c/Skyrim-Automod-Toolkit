[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-place-Weynon_Stones.jpg/200px-SR-place-Weynon_Stones.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Weynon_Stones.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Weynon_Stones.jpg) The Weynon Stones The **Weynon Stones** are a [landmark](Skyrim_Landmarks.md) consisting of a circle of stones. It is located between [Korvanjund](Skyrim_Korvanjund.md) and [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md), just south of the path.

It is home to a pair of [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md) who will attack as you approach. This can be a challenging battle at low levels or if you don't have any resistance to frost magic.

## Walkthrough
As you enter the circle of stones, the two ice wraiths will appear and attack. There is an empty altar up a flight of stone stairs with a large stone head above it. Beside the altar is a [burial urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md#Urn). Behind the large stone head is a [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) with a novice-level lock containing random [loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md). Opposite this is a [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos). On the shrine are two [steel ingots](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md), a pair of [iron gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md), and two pieces of a [broken steel](Skyrim_Broken_Steel_Battle_Axe_Head.md) [battleaxe](Skyrim_Broken_Steel_Battle_Axe_Handle.md). The two pieces cannot be picked up. In the surrounding area there are several [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes.

## Notes
- The location's name may be a reference to [Weynon Priory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Weynon_Priory_(settlement)), as also suggested by the connection to [Talos worship](Lore_Talos.md).
