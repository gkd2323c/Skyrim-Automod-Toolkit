Dragon Skull Parchment *A fragmented verse from an ancient Nordic song, written in the language of the dragons* *
---
*Note: This parchment roughly translates to "And the Scrolls have foretold, of black wings in the cold, that when brothers wage war come unfurled. Alduin, Bane of Kings, ancient shadow unbound, with a hunger to swallow the world." as detailed in [Song of the Dragonborn](Lore_Songs_of_Skyrim__Revised.md).*
