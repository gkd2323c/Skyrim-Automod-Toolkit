[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-place-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg) Klimmek's House **Klimmek's House** is a residence in the southeastern part of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), located across from [Vilemyr Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn).

It is the home of [Klimmek](Skyrim_Klimmek.md) and [Bassianus Axius](Skyrim_Bassianus_Axius.md). It consists of one zone, *[Klimmek's House](#Klimmek.27s_House)*.

## Klimmek's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House.jpg) Kitchen and dining area Klimmek's house is laid out in an L-shape, with the front door entering to an open kitchen and dining area along one side of the "L", and the bedroom area situated on the other. To the left of the door as you come in is a dining table holding two loaves and one piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), a [chicken's egg](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). There are also two more chicken's eggs on the floor beneath the table, and a respawning food barrel in the corner to the right of the table. To the right of the door is a non-respawning cupboard holding pots and tankards on the shelves, along with a bucket and a sample of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) at the top. A respawning food barrel sits to the left of the cupboard.

To the left of the fireplace is another respawning food barrel, atop which sits a full [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md). Three gold coins rest on the mantel of the fireplace, and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) sits on the right side of the fireplace base. To the right of the fireplace is another dining table holding two [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), one [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, three [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), three loaves of bread, a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), and a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). Four more potatoes sit in a kettle to the left side of the table, and four woven baskets sit on the floor to the right. Above the table are some hanging ingredients, including dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two garlic braids, and two portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). Next to these hanging ingredients are two hanging racks, one of which holds two freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) while the other holds two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

On the bottom of a set of shelves to the right are two bottles of alto wine and three bottles of wine, along with the books *[The Code of Malacath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath)*, *[Kolb & the Dragon](Skyrim_Kolb_%26_the_Dragon.md)*, *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)*, and *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](Skyrim_A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun.md)*. Next to these shelves is a non-respawning wardrobe.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Klimmek%27s_House_02.jpg) Bedroom area The bedroom area of the house starts with a bed owned by [Bassianus Axius](Skyrim_Bassianus_Axius.md) along the southeast wall, next to a non-respawning end table with a copy of *[Nords Arise!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nords_Arise!)* sitting on top for reading material. The other owned bed (belonging to the [Ivarstead Klimmek House Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ivarstead Klimmek House Faction)) is found on the southwest wall, flanked by a non-respawning end table on either side and with a non-respawning chest at the foot of the bed. The left end table holds a farm [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000330b3) and a [potion of haggling](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Haggling), while the right one holds a basket and four gold coins. A pair of farm [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b) sits on the floor in the left corner, in front of a medium-sized [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). Along the left wall is a non-respawning wardrobe, atop which lie a [sabre cat pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt) and a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), and next to which rests a respawning food barrel with a bucket on top. Along the right wall is a small sitting area, with a table holding two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and a couple of tankards.

## Notes
- The front door has an apprentice lock, the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Klimmek%27s_House.md) to which is carried by both Klimmek and Bassianus.
- At the front door to the house is a respawning food barrel.
- Behind the house is a dead tree with a growth of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota).

## Bugs
- After you clear [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md), Klimmek's house along with it will be marked as cleared on mini-map.
