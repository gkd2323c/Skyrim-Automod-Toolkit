Hoag Merkiller was a short-lived High King/High Chieftain of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the mid-late fifth century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He was a seasoned warrior and [tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tongues)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1ES-1)</sup> from a prestigious background as a fighter and a monarch. His predecessor as high king was his father, [Kjoric the White](Lore_Kjoric_the_White.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTR-2)</sup> In his time serving under the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md), he used the pseudonym, **Fenja**, but he is remembered in [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) history as **HOAGA**, the Mouth of Mud.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-36Lo VS9-3)</sup>

## History
The precise date of Merkiller's birth is currently unknown, although he was alive during the lifespan of the [Nordic Empire](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md), in the early First Era and known to have been present in the [Conquest of Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Conquest_of_Morrowind), which ended in [1E 416](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_416).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1ES-1)</sup> In that time, he went under a pseudonym, Fenja, and destroyed seventeen [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) villages and two [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) strongholds. According to [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) mythology, HOAGA used an unheard technique to swallow the earth around him and use it on his fallen soldiers, to re-invigorate them, albeit at the expense of their speed. He was eventually driven out of the fight.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-36Lo VS9-3)</sup>

Years later in [1E 461](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_461), Hoag Merkiller and his father, Kjoric the White were present at the coronation of Emperor [Gorieus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorieus) of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), among many other legendary warriors at the time, including [Indoril Nerevar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Indoril_Nerevar) and [Dumac Dwarfking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dumac_Dwarfking). The circumstances regarding Hoag Merkiller's death and even his father is shrouded in inconsistency. While one source claims that Kjoric the White died in [Sungard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sungard) defending his post from the First Empire and Gorieus,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTR-2)</sup> others state that he died defending [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-4)</sup><sup>:574</sup> Afterward, Hoag Merkiller took the helm of High King, and joined the fight against the First Empire with the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTR-2)</sup> During his reign, he was well-acquainted with the Icehammer Clan of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), often fighting alongside them and appointing the head of the clan as his thane, but they eventually turned towards evil.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Vault-5)</sup>

At some point, he was involved in a battle with the Direnni that took place between 1E 461 and [1E 477](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_477) and lost in battle.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-6)</sup> It is widely recorded that the High King died at the [Battle of Glenumbria Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbria_Moors.md) in [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-FSo KW-7)</sup> but some other sources say that he died defending Falkreath and even fell at the exact same spot his father died on.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-4)</sup><sup>:574</sup> In any case, his heir,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTR-2)</sup> [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), ascended to the throne of High King<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-FSo KW-7)</sup> roughly around [1E 480](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_480).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRPlaque-8)</sup>
