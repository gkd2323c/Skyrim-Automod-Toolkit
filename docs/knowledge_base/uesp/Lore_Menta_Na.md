Menta Na, also known as the **Daedroth of Kora-Dur** or the **Terror of Kora-Dur**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is a [daedroth](Lore_Daedroth.md) monarch in the service of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). He is tasked by the Prince to cause havoc and terror in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The caverns of Kora-Dur in northern [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) acted as his lair.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MW-2)</sup> He is associated with a powerful Daedric mace known as the Mace of Menta Na.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMMN-3)</sup>

## History
During the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), Menta Na was Molag Bal's most favored daedroth, and served as one of his generals.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Anchor G-4)</sup> At some point he was recalled to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), and in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), he was transported back to Tamriel to protect a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md). However, he was quickly banished by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and the Anchor was lifted.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> After his defeat, his mace was recovered from the Dark Anchor he defended.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMMN-3)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Menta Na had returned to Tamriel, but had grown lazy and retreated to Kora-Dur, refusing to do Molag Bal's bidding. The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was sent by the Prince to banish the daedroth and send his soul to the [Outer Realms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Outer_Realms) to be punished. In return for this service, the Nerevarine was given the [Mace of Molag Bal](Lore_Mace_of_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MW-2)</sup>
