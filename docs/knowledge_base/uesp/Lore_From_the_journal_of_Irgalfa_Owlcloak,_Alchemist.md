From the journal of Irgalfa Owlcloak, Alchemist **by [Irgalfa Owlcloak](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Irgalfa_Owlcloak)** *The journal entry of an alchemist's travels through Bleakrock Isle*
---
[![ON-concept-wolf.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/2c/ON-concept-wolf.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-wolf.png)
- Even here on [Bleakrock](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md), there are [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) lessons just begging to be learned. Too bad I'm being hampered by ignorance. No one in that tiny village would let me set up my equipment in any kind of shelter, even when I offered to share the fruits of my labor for no fee!

- Working out in the field has had some unexpected challenges. Two days past, I was attacked by some scruffy-looking [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md) right as I was on the verge of a breakthrough! They must've been attracted by the liquefied [goat](Lore_Goat.md) liver I was heating with the berries of a native bush I hadn't seen before. Then, last night, for fear I'd lose my new potions to the frost, I placed my pack closer to the campfire than usual—too close, I guess, as I was jolted out of sleep by a minor explosion. Maybe I should have hired a guide; I feel like I'm going to learn more about surviving these damned wilds than about alchemy at this rate!
