*I'm Errandil, Living Saint of Arkay, and the Chapel's chief crusader against the wicked practice of necromancy.*
—Errandil<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Errandil-1)</sup> Errandil was a [Living Saint](Lore_Living_Saints.md) of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and served at the Great Chapel of Arkay in the city of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). He was a crusader against the practice of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) prevalent among the [Imperial Battle College](Lore_Imperial_Battle_College.md) and [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). Due to this, he approved of Arch-Mage [Hannibal Traven](Lore_Hannibal_Traven.md)'s stance on the Black Arts and its subsequent banning from the Mages Guild.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Errandil-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTC-2)</sup>

Errandil was slain in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) by a contingent of [Aurorans](Lore_Auroran.md) sent to desecrate the Chapel by [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) in his war against the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md). The Chapel was saved from desecration by the [Divine Crusader](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md), but not before the saint's untimely death.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Knights-3)</sup>
