# Order of the New Moon

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/ON-item-Dark_Aeon.png/200px-ON-item-Dark_Aeon.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Dark_Aeon.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Dark_Aeon.png) Prophecy of the Dark Aeon The Order of the New Moon, also known as the **New Moon Cult**, was a [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) rumored to have been created in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NCAR-1)</sup>

## History

### Merethic Era
When Elsweyr was composed of sixteen kingdoms, a dragon named [Kaalgrontiid](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) split off from the bulk of his kin in order to escape [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md)'s rule and sate his own pride and ambition.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LADragons-2)</sup> Kaalgrontiid and his dragons came to dominate the skies of Elsweyr, where he planned to become the equal of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) by consuming the power of the moon [Jode](Lore_Jode.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOKaalgrontiid-3)</sup> Kaalgrontiid established Dragonhold as his seat of power, and his Khajiiti followers built a great temple devoted to the Dark Aeon, the name of the new moon Kaalgrontiid hoped to become.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONMR-4)</sup>

However, in an effort to stop this plot and end the dragons' reign of terror, the great [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) recruited four companions: [Nurarion the Perfect](Lore_Nurarion.md), [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md), [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md), and [Cadwell the Betrayer](Lore_Cadwell.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kat D-5)</sup> Khunzar-ri tricked the dragons into entering the Plane of Jode and store their energy within Jode's Core, believing that they would combine the lunar power with their own.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOCadwell-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOKhamira-7)</sup> The gamble worked, and the dragons forced to retreat to the wilderness due to their weakened state.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kat D-5)</sup> Khunzar-ri then managed to lure the dragons into the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md), where he sealed them away.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kat D-5)</sup>

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/ON-crown_store-New_Moon_Priest_Style.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-New_Moon_Priest_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-New_Moon_Priest_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-New_Moon_Priest_Style.jpg) New Moon priests When Kaalgrontiid returned circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) due to [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md)'s actions, his lieutenant Laatvulon was tasked with recreating the cult in his master's name. He exploited the Khajiiti people whom are distraught over events such as the loss experienced during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), further made worse by the Dragons ravaging Elsweyr.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NCAR-1)</sup> Feeling that their gods have been absent, and seeing the god-like beings that dragons are, they looked to instead appease them for protection. As a result the New Moon Cult was created, with the Khajiit Ra'khajin instated as Laatvulon's [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md). With the promise that the cult would ascend alongside their masters, and promising Ra'khajin the glory of becoming their spiritual leader as the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md), Kaalgrontiid gained a valuable asset in manpower for his operations.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOZayri-8)</sup>

Kaalgrontiid found that his underling Laatvulon had failed him. Kaalgrontiid took command of the remnants of the New Moon Cult and ordered them to do his bidding.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-9)</sup> Kaalgrontiid planned to siphon the energy he obtained from Jode's Core through a giant cluster of [aeonstone](Lore_Aeonstone.md), hoping that he would become empowered.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-9)</sup> These stones would then be used to empower Dragons and collected on the island of [Dragonhold](Lore_Dragonhold.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESODA-10)</sup> Eventually, the island's massive collection of aeonstone was utilized by Kaalgrontiid in hopes of ascending to godhood and fulfilling the [Prophecy of the New Moon](Lore_Prophecy_of_the_New_Moon.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESODA-10)</sup> If Kaalgrontiid were to succeed, he would fulfill an ancient prophecy and become the Dark Aeon, becoming the third moon. The creation of this New Moon "presages a time of darkness and the birth of a new supreme being",<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbnur-9)</sup> and it would ultimately threaten to unravel the tapestry of time itself.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOJ-11)</sup>

However, through the combined efforts of [Nahfahlaar](Lore_Nahfahlaar.md), the reformed [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), and Queen [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md)'s forces, Kaalgrontiid was slain, his rage of dragons were defeated, and the New Moon Cult were ultimately defeated. <sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONMR-4)</sup>
