## Quick Walkthrough
1. Finish talking to [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md).
2. Meet your new family members.
3. Speak to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) and pick up your contracts.

## Detailed Walkthrough
After speaking to [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md), turn back and search the shelves for a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Sithis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis)*, and a set of [shrouded robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Robes). Enter the room behind the map table and pick up the [unusual gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md), sitting on the dresser to the left, for the quest [No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md).

### Accepting the Contract
When the gathering breaks up, move to the [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) to the north of the room and absorb the word for the [Marked for Death](Skyrim_Marked_for_Death.md) shout. If you pass near Babette she will remark *"Heard she pulled the ole' "Choose your victim" gag with you. Ah, I love that one..."*, shedding some light as to the real victim of the previous quest.

*When you accept a contract you will be unable to interact normally with your target, preventing you from completing any quests involving them. Check the notes section before taking any contracts.* Nazir moves to the brazier near the pool and awaits your "valued" input. No matter what way the discussion proceeds, he will mock your talents, going as far as saying *"I have no intention of getting invested in someone who may be dead tomorrow."* Finish speaking to Nazir to pick up your first contracts. There are three assassination jobs. You can do each one at a time and return for payment, or you can do all three and return to Nazir once completed. The three contracts are for [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Narfi), [Ennodius Papius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Ennodius_Papius) and [Beitild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Beitild).

## Notes
- Narfi is involved in the quest [The Straw that Broke](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Straw_that_Broke). Therefore, once the contract is accepted, it will not be possible to complete this quest.
- You do not have to listen to the gathering. If you talk to Nazir straight away, the group breaks up, however this is the only time this conversation is heard.
- The non-armor version of the Shrouded Armor consists of [Hand Wraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hand_Wraps), a [Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hood), [Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Robes), and [Shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Shoes). These are the clothing version of the armor, which is designed for mages. The [Maskless Cowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Cowl_Maskless) sitting with them is considered to be [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md).
