General Mercedene, also known as the **Beast of the Reach**, was a notorious [Reachman](Lore_Reachman.md) warrior and leader of the [Winterborn](Lore_Winterborn.md) clan during the mid- [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Said to be a vicious woman who reveled in blood and death, she is best known for her part in the infamous [Siege of Orsinium](Lore_Siege_of_Orsinium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTrystan-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKyrtos-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo OF-3)</sup>

Prior to leading the Winterborn, she served under her mother [Evandene Tusk Collector](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Evandene_Tusk_Collector) who was the previous leader of the clan. According to legend, Evandene led a raid on an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) village and decimated the populace. Not satisfied with her mother's punishment of the Orcs, Mercedene grew livid when she arrived with reinforcements. According to her *"Harvesting a tenth of the weeds yields a hardier strain of weed. Best to burn the field so its soil can take another crop."* In response, Mercedene immediately challenged her mother, killed her, cut out her heart, and ate it. Said to be devout in her worship of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md), more so than the Orcs themselves, Mercedene then decorated an altar to Malacath with Evandene's corpse.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTrystan-1)</sup>

Mercedene proved to be an able commander after she took over leadership of the Winterborn. The reputation of the Winterborn gained the attention of King [Joile](Lore_Joile.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), who hired Mercedene and her clan for his own ends.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTrystan-1)</sup> During the Siege of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), King Joile appointed Mercedene as his general against the Orcish forces, but also as an instrument to enact his grand deception. Although King Joile was allied with the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), he sought to total conquest of his neighboring kingdoms. To achieve this he sought to eliminate the two greatest heroes of both Orsinium and Hammerfall: [Baloth Bloodtusk](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md) and [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md). With their deaths, both kingdoms would be weakened allowing him an easier conquest of the entire region.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESORokaug-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo OF-3)</sup>

First, Mercedene kidnapped the brother of Baloth Bloodtusk and faked his death in order to draw Baloth into the war.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESORokaug-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Lt BB-5)</sup> Her deception was successful as Baloth forces arrived shortly after into Orsinium and reinforced the city. Next, King Joile implored Gaiden Shinji to face Baloth in a duel to decide the fate of the city and save the lives of his soldiers. Gaiden agreed to the duel and also marched his forces to Orsinium.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo OF-3)</sup> Mercedene's trap was set. She made her way into Orsinium and met with a contingent of disguised Winterborn soldiers who arrived behind the siege lines.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-KJOt GM-6)</sup> Once the duel Baloth Bloodtusk and Gaiden Shinji began, Mercedene ordered her archers to cut them both down. Her plan was successful and both heroes were slain.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo OF-3)</sup>

Before Mercedene could escape the city, Commander Reliana of the [Knights of the Dragon](Lore_Knights_of_the_Dragon.md) confronted her after uncovering evidence of Mercedene's betrayal. Both commanders fought, but Mercedene was ultimately killed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAo OF-3)</sup> It is unknown what happened to her corpse, but Mercedene's spirit was cursed to wander the ruins of Orsinium for centuries after.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Mt C-7)</sup>
