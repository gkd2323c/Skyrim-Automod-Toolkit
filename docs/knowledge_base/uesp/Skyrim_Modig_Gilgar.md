[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-npc-Modig_Gilgar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Modig_Gilgar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Modig_Gilgar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Modig_Gilgar.jpg) Modig Gilgar Modig Gilgar is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) necromancer who is encountered along the road near at the ruined house west of the [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-4648&y=-8009) during the quest [The Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Hasedoki.md). He has just defended his cart from three bandits, as evidenced by their bodies and the arrows stuck in his cargo. He will turn hostile if you get too close.

He carries the [Smuggler's Ledger](Skyrim_Smuggler%27s_Ledger.md), which can be pickpocketed from him or taken off his body if you choose to kill him. He wears [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md), [bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md), and a rare [mage hood](Skyrim_Mage_Hood.md). He carries a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) and knows various spells, using both in combat. He has 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Fireballs](Skyrim_Staff_of_Fireballs.md), [Staff of Ice Storms](Skyrim_Staff_of_Ice_Storms.md) or [Staff of Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Staff_of_Chain_Lightning.md), and carries mage-related random loot, while his cart holds two [black soul gems](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) and a book called [To Raise the Living](Skyrim_To_Raise_the_Living.md). Reading the book explains his presence in Skyrim. It reveals that his family consists of Orc necromancers hailing from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and that the Staff of Hasedoki was passed on through generations.

## Notes
- Modig is a reference to, and an apparent descendant of, [Koffutto Gilgar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Koffutto_Gilgar). Koffuto was another Orc necromancer with a similarly strange name who was also in possession of the Staff of Hasedoki in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
- Modig is found right next to the same house where the [Space Core](Skyrim_Space_Core.md) crash lands. However, [Fall of the Space Core, Vol 1](Skyrim_Fall_of_the_Space_Core,_Vol_1.md) is only officially available for the original version of *Skyrim*, and Creation Club content is only available for the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) or [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md).
