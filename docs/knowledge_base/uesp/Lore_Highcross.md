[3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Highcross is a village in the [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It lies roughly at the northernmost section of The Jester's Spine.

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the occupants were an eccentric bunch. For example, Bruscus Dannus concerned himself with nothing other than cleaning the road that ran through the settlement. Another eccentric resident was Runs-in-Circles, an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) whose very name gives enough explanation as to her erratic and often comical behavior.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) was tasked by one of the village residents, Mirili Ulven, with luring various creatures back to Highcross so that she could capture and study them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
