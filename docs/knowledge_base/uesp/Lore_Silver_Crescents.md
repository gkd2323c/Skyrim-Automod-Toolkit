# Silver Crescents

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-npc-Niruin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Niruin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niruin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niruin.jpg) Niruin, a former member The Silver Crescents are a prominent guild in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) that were active a number of years prior to [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). Their line of work is dangerous, but provides a great deal of money. They have contacts with the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). A [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) thief named Niruin was a member of the Crescents for a number of years, before being forced to leave by his father under the threat of being put in jail for his involvement with them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRNiruin-1)</sup>

## Notes
- [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worshippers claim they speak to [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) through [silvered](Lore_Silver.md) crescent mirrors,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SCM-2)</sup> but it is unknown if this has any connection to the origin of the gang's name.
