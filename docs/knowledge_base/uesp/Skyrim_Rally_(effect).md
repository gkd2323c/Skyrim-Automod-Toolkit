*Enchanting description:* **Targets won't flee for <dur> seconds and get extra health and stamina.**

**Rally** (a.k.a. **Courage**) increases the target's [Confidence](Skyrim_NPCs.md#Confidence) (willingness to attack), making them less likely to flee from combat. Fleeing NPCs will stop running and return to combat. In addition, Rally and Courage spells [fortify](Skyrim_Fortify_Health.md) the target's [Health](Skyrim_Health.md) and [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md). These are implemented through additional effects included in the spell, rather than being an integral part of the actual Rally effect.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect, but cannot be disenchanted:

- [Staff of Courage](Skyrim_Staff_of_Courage.md)
- [Staff of Inspiration](Skyrim_Staff_of_Inspiration.md)

## Spells
The following spells use the Rally group of effects. Following each spell is the specific variant of the effect that it uses:

- [Courage](Skyrim_Courage.md): *Courage* (`0001ea79`; `Influence Conf Up FFAimed`) - With the Master of the Mind perk, instead uses: *Courage - Master of the Mind* (`0009e0c4`; `Perk Master Mind Conf Up FFAimed`)
- [Rally](Skyrim_Rally_(spell).md): *Rally* (`0001ea76`; `Influence Conf Up FFAimed Area`) - With the [Master of the Mind](Skyrim_Master_of_the_Mind.md) perk, instead uses: *Rally - Master of the Mind* (`0009e0c5`; `Perk Master Mind Conf Up FFAimed Area`)

## Notes
- The various [Frenzy](Skyrim_Frenzy_(effect).md) spells include a secondary Rally effect.
- The [Elytra's Uplifting Aura](Skyrim_Elytra%27s_Uplifting_Aura.md) provides a Rally effect.<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>
- Non-hostile wildlife such as deer will attack you and your followers while under the effects of Rally. A minority of non-hostile NPCs will also attack you if under the effects of Rally. Typically, these are NPCs encountered during a number of random encounters such as the [Alik'r Warriors](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Warrior.md), [Revelers](Skyrim_Reveler.md), and [Vigilants of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md).
