[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-npc-Akar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Akar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Akar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Akar.jpg) Akar sitting on his bed **Akar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enc Class Werewolf) living under [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md). He is a member of the Frostmoon Pack and brother to [Majni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Majni), the pack leader.

Akar wears [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) with matching [bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) and [shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md). He wields a leveled [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md) in combat which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.

## Dialogue
When you approach him, he can greet you with:

- *"Grr..."*
- *"What?"*
- *"Hmph."*

If spoken to directly:

- **Who are you?**
- *"Akar. Majni's brother. I hunt. He talks. Go talk to him."*
- **You hunt? What do you hunt?**
- *"[Deer](Skyrim_Deer.md). [Sabers](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md). [Rieks](Skyrim_Riekling.md). [Boar](Skyrim_Bristleback.md). [Bear](Skyrim_Bear.md). I hunt soon. Go bother Majni."*
