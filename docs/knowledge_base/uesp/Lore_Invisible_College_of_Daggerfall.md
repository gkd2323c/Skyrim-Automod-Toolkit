# Invisible College of Daggerfall

The Invisible College of Daggerfall was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) academic institution devoted to the scholarly pursuits active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Little is known of the academy, except that it was based in [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and one of its members, [Landal Gevont](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Landal_Gevont) was the foremost authority on [Goblin](Lore_Goblin.md) culture.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Asda-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LG-3)</sup> He cooperated with [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) institution, the [Coterie of Organized Scholars](Lore_Coterie_of_Organized_Scholars.md) during his research.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Asda-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ATGC-4)</sup>

## Known Members

## Known Publications
- ***[Crafting Motif 71: Coldsnap Style](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crafting_Motif_71__Coldsnap_Style)*** by [Landal Gevont](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Landal_Gevont), Scholar of Goblins, Invisible College of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) — A guide to crafting armor and weapons in the Coldsnap style

## Notes
- In real life, an [Invisible College](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Invisible_College) is a term used to describe a non public network of researchers operating in an informal way. It is unclear whether the Invisible College of Daggerfall is a similar type of group.
