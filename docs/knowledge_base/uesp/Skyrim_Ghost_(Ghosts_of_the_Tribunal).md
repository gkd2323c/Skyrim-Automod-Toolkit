[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Ghost_%28Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghost_%28Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).jpg) The Ghost This **Ghost** is a [Dwemer](Skyrim_Dwemer.md) spectre encountered in [Fahlbtharz Forge](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md#Fahlbtharz_Forge). It is briefly seen when you enter the forge, but is quickly dispatched by [Hand Kenro Hlan](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro_Hlan.md). It wears [Dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Dwarven_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dwarven_Gauntlets.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Dwarven_Helmet.md).

He is a hostile spellcaster, but will be quickly dispatched by Kenro Hlan, who is equally displeased to see you.

He is wearing a full set of [Dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armor.md), with [helmet](Skyrim_Dwarven_Helmet.md), [boots](Skyrim_Dwarven_Boots.md), and [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dwarven_Gauntlets.md). He knows the spells [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and Magicka Recovery 2 (a 0-magicka spell to [Regenerate Magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) 2 pts). He will also know [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md), or [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md).

When banished, he will let out a gasp: *"Hunh..."*
