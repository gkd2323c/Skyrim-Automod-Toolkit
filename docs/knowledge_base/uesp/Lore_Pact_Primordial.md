# Pact Primordial

— [Ur-Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ur-Daedra)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTPP-1)</sup> The Pact Primordial is an ancient accord that binds [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md), preventing them from intruding into each other's realms without invitation. This law ensures the sovereignty of each Prince within their domain and stands as a formidable barrier against unwarranted interdimensional trespassing.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTPP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Scruut-2)</sup> The existence of the Pact Primordial was not a secret; it was well known among the denizens of Oblivion, though it was probably less familiar to mortals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Scruut-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Torvesard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Torvesard), the agent of [Ithelia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ithelia) delved into the Tranquil Catalog, seeking ways to circumvent the Pact Primordial. [Vaermina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vaermina) and [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) also sought to breach this restriction, with Vaermina successfully doing so by possessing the body of the mortal [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) wizard [Shelreni Baro](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shelreni_Baro) with the usage of [necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Necromancy) to enter [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) uninvited.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Scruut-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FLD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ACOF-4)</sup>
