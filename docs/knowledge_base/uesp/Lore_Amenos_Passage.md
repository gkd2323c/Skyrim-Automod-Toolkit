[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/ON-place-Mount_Firesong.jpg/200px-ON-place-Mount_Firesong.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mount_Firesong.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mount_Firesong.jpg) [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md) seen from across the passage The Amenos Passage is a body of water nestled between the four major islands of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md): [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md), [Galen](Lore_Galen.md), [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), and [Y'ffelon](Lore_Y%27ffelon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VGt HI-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VGt G-2)</sup> It is part of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) and bordered by the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CTL-3)</sup>

Several islands, such as [Fogbreak Lighthouse Island](Lore_Fogbreak_Lighthouse_Island.md) and [Whalefall](Lore_Whalefall.md) are located within the Passage.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Fogbreak-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Whalefall-5)</sup> Both on and below these islands, exotic [coral](Lore_Coral.md) may be found within the Passage due to the archipelago's close proximity to [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Lost Depths Stream-6)</sup>
