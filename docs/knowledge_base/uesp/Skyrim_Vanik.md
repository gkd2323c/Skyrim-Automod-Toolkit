[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-npc-Vanik.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vanik.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vanik.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vanik.jpg) Vanik **Vanik** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) found in [Forebears' Holdout](Skyrim_Forebears%27_Holdout.md) protecting the [Moth Priest](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) [Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md).

He is clad in a complete set of Dawnguard equipment. This includes the [cuirass](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md), matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Boots.md), as well as a [shield](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Shield.md) for blocking. He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md), and carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), as well as a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Events
He can be overheard talking to a fellow Dawnguard member:

- **Dawnguard**: *"Those vampires put up a hell of a fight. You think they turned him?"*
- **Vanik**: *"I don't know, maybe. Won't know until we get him out of there."*
- **Dawnguard**: *"How do we do that?"*
- **Vanik**: *"I'm working on it, now give me some silence so I can think."*
