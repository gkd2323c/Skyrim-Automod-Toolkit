Za-Rifah al-Danobia, the **Flower of Taneth** was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) noblewoman and the Queen of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) active in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLS-1)</sup>

## History
Little is known of Za-Rifah's past, though she hailed from the al-Danobia family,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zeira-2)</sup> the descendants of Danobia which has always had great influence in [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PL-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MF-4)</sup> She was the eldest daughter of her parents and sister of Falorah,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zeira-2)</sup> who later became Magnifica of Taneth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zeira-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MF-4)</sup>

She married Fahara'jad while he was still a young Prince of [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLS-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TABKF-5)</sup> In time, her [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) husband declared himself the King of Sentinel after the death of [Ramzi the Distrait](Lore_Ramzi_the_Distrait.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGTHammerfell-6)</sup> with Za-Rifah as his Queen consort. Together they had three children: [Maraya](Lore_Maraya.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLS-1)</sup> who later became Queen of High Rock;<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TFOR-7)</sup> Lakana,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLS-1)</sup> who became Duchess of [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire_(city).md);<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DN-8)</sup> and Crown Prince [Azah](Lore_Azah.md). Tragically, Za-Rifah died giving birth to their third child and, as recorded in contemporary accounts, was said to have been mourned by all the Redguards of Hammerfell.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRLS-1)</sup> Following her death, her sister Falorah grew really close to her widowed husband, though their relationship was not romantic in nature.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zeira-2)</sup>
