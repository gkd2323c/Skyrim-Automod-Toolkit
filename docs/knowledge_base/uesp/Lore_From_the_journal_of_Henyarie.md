From the journal of Henyarie **by [Henyarie](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Henyarie)** *A fish breeder laments the loss of his prized silverscales*
---
[![ON-concept-Auridon.png](https://images.uesp.net/8/8a/ON-concept-Auridon.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Auridon.png)
- Thirty years of work eaten by a visiting [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) "dignitary." Eaten. All of my careful work selecting only the most perfectly-shaped silverscales for breeding lost in an afternoon of ignorant indulgence. And worse: my own son is to blame. I ordered him quite clearly to accompany the cat-man at all times on our property. His disobedience will be properly addressed later.

- For now, I can only lament the loss of my best stock. The most beautiful [fish](Lore_Fish.md) in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), every shining scale a little triangle of perfection, every fine translucent fin ideally shaped—all lost. I found the scoundrel, languid and stuffed, dozing amongst their skeletons.
