[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/SR-npc-Addvild.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Addvild.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Addvild.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Addvild.jpg) Addvild working at Snow-Shod Farm **Addvild** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who works for [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Vulwulf_Snow-Shod.md) at [Snow-Shod Farm](Skyrim_Snow-Shod_Farm.md) with his recently married wife, [Leonara Arius](Skyrim_Leonara_Arius.md). He [appreciates](Skyrim_Gather_Wheat.md) honest work and will pay a septim each for [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and five septims each for [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md).

He starts his day at 6am with a one-hour breakfast at the farmhouse. At 7am, he goes outside to tend the crops and look after the farm animals until his bedtime at midnight, taking a break at 7pm to dine at the house for one hour.

He wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#.28000209A6.29) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). He wields a [leveled dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) in combat and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Regarding his position, he is extremely appreciative of Vulwulf Snow-Shod for trusting him to take care of the farm without supervision. This is evident in his oft-repeated phrases:

*"Vulwulf has been quite generous to let me take care of their farm considering I am not part of their family."* *"The Snow-Shod's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * are very trusting to let me run this place without their supervision."* Also when spoken to:

*"I'm glad there are guards outside the farm. I don't think I could defend the place alone."* *"If you're looking to purchase goods, I have none to sell. You should head into [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) instead."* *"Pretty quiet out here on the farm. My wife and I kind of like the solitude."* If you have cleared out [Aringoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aringoth)'s safe during the quest [Loud and Clear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Loud_and_Clear) and have not returned to [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md), Addvild will express concern about the nearby [estate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Estate):

*"Did you see the fires from Goldenglow Estate? I was afraid the whole place was going to burn to the ground!"*
## Conversations
He may converse with his Leonara:

**Leonara**: *"My darling, you've made me the happiest woman in all Skyrim."*
**Addvild**: *"It brings me joy to hear that, but what possessed you to say that so suddenly?"*
**Leonara**: *"I've already lost a family, and we're about to start a new one. I don't want that to happen again."*
**Addvild**: *"My sweet Leanora * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, I will never let anything happen to us. I promise."* **Leonara**: *"I'm not so sure the few guards they've assigned to the farm are enough."*
**Addvild**: *"It will have to do. Besides, if it came to blows, I'm sure the Snow-Shod's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * would come out here and assist us."*
**Leonara**: *"I suppose so. I just fear they won't make it out here in time."*
**Addvild**: *"Just worry about keeping the fields tended and let the Jarl worry about the war."*
