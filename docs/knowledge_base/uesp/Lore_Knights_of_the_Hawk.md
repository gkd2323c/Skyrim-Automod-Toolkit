# Knights of the Hawk

The Knights of the Hawk or the **Order of the Hawk** act as the noble warriors and protectors of the town and royal family of [Santaki](Lore_Santaki_(county).md). Many have trained since childhood to join the knightly order, for the honor and prestige.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
