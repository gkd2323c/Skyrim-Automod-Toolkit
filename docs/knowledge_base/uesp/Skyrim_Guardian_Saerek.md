[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-creature-Guardian_Saerek.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Guardian_Saerek.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Guardian_Saerek.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Guardian_Saerek.jpg) Guardian Saerek in Ragnvald **Guardian Saerek** is a boss-level [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) that waits in [Ragnvald Crypts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragnvald_Crypts).

He uses a leveled two-handed weapon and at higher levels, [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md) and [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) [dragon shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md). He carries a small amount of gold and possibly gems, jewelry, or a potion. He may drop [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md) when killed.

## Related Quests
- **[Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald_(quest).md)**: Discover the secrets of [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md).

## Notes
- Because Ragnvald's minimum level is 24, only the level 21 and 30 versions of Guardian Saerek will ever be encountered.
- Saerek's name is spelled "Sarek" in *[The Secrets of Ragnvald](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Secrets_of_Ragnvald)*.
