— *Jauffre, the Grandmaster of the Blades* Jauffre was the Grandmaster of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) during the closing days of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ob MQ-1)</sup> He was known to wear his trusty plate armor called "Cloudrest",<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> and wielded an [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) blade simply named "Akaviri".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History

### Honor Guard
Many years before the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), Jauffre served publicly within Emperor [Uriel VII's](Lore_Uriel_VII.md) [Blades Guard](Lore_Imperial_Guard.md#Blades_Guard) at the [Imperial Palace](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md). One night he was called to the Emperor's private chambers, there was a baby boy sleeping in a basket. Uriel told Jauffre to deliver the child somewhere safe, though he revealed nothing about the baby. Jauffre's intuition told him that it was his Emperor's son. From time to time, Uriel would ask about the child's progress.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Oblivion Jaufre-4)</sup> The boy, named [Martin](Lore_Martin_Septim.md), was raised by a farmer, whom he believed to be his true father.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBMartin-5)</sup>

### Oblivion Crisis
*Guardian of the Septim line and stalwart leader of the Blades, Jauffre holds the line against the unrelenting onslaught of Oblivion's chaos.*
—A description of Jauffre<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> By the time of Oblivion Crisis of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Jauffre was retired from public work, living as a simple monk within the [Order of Talos](Lore_Order_of_Talos.md) at [Weynon Priory](Lore_Weynon_Priory.md) near [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), but serving in secret as the ultimate Grandmaster of the Blades in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Oblivion Jaufre-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ob MQ-1)</sup> He served as the [Empire's](Lore_Third_Empire.md) chief strategist in the fight against [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s invasion from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ob MQ-1)</sup> At the onset of the Crisis, he took custody of the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md) and instigated the search for [Martin Septim](Lore_Martin_Septim.md), the only surviving heir to the throne. Later, the priory was sacked by the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md). Jauffre survived and got Martin to the safety of [Cloud Ruler Temple](Lore_Cloud_Ruler_Temple.md), but lost the Amulet.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TOC-7)</sup> He helped Martin decipher the means by which to defeat [Mankar Camoran](Lore_Mankar_Camoran.md), although it is unknown if he survived the remainder of the Crisis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ob MQ-1)</sup>
