Arthosiis was an ancient [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) who lived around the time of the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md). He was exiled from his home, so he, his devoted followers and the Dragon they served left Skyrim in an exodus to [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), possibly as a result of losing the war after [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md)'s banishment. In Wrothgar the exiles built a grand temple for the dragon before Arthosiis killed himself and his followers sealed themselves in the barrow, which later became known as Exile's Barrow. <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONNarsis-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONDilemma-2)</sup>

Due to the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md)' strict rule about not disturbing the resting places of the dead, the barrow remained untouched until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [Narsis Dren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Narsis_Dren) delved into the halls in search of a [Dragon priest mask](Lore_Dragon_Priest_Masks.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMazgroth-3)</sup> During his search for the mask through the numerous crypts and stone coffins, he accidentally awoke Arthosiis and his draugr guardians, after which he fled to a nearby Orcish village.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONNarsis Notes-4)</sup> He returned with the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who sought to discover why the Draugr had awoken. Within the barrow four [bas-reliefs](Lore_Statues.md) were found that told the tale of the ancient priest. Eventually, the Vestige encountered and defeated Arthosiis, while Narsis Dren stole the priest's mask for himself.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONDilemma-2)</sup>

As a Dragon Priest, he was a powerful mage, also able to wield the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md) in battle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TGATT-5)</sup>
