[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/OBR-place-Larsius_River.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Larsius_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Larsius_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Larsius_River.jpg) The Larsius River circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/ON-place-Larsius_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Larsius_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Larsius_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Larsius_River.jpg) The Larsius River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Larsius River is a fairly short river that rises just on the [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) side of the border with [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and flows north, then east, past the city of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) to enter [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md). At this confluence are fetid lowland swamps, teeming with insects and rank with sewage runoff from the city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBMap-2)</sup>

In the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the Larsius River marked the easternmost edge of [Darloc Brae](Lore_Darloc_Brae.md)'s kingdom in northern [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Khaj-3)</sup>
