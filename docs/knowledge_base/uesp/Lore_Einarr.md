[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Einarr was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) in the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). He succeeded [Isgeror White-Wave](Lore_Isgeror_White-Wave.md) as Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying the [Frost Giant](Lore_Frost_Giant.md) Guolog and presenting his foot as a battle trophy. He reigned as chieftain for six years before being killed by [Gisl Round-Gut](Lore_Gisl_Round-Gut.md) who would take his place as Chieftain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>

## Notes
- [Einarr the Younger](Lore_Einarr_the_Younger.md) was presumably his son and later became Chieftain as well.
