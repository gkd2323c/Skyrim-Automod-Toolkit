[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-npc-Irgnir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irgnir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irgnir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irgnir.jpg) Irgnir **Irgnir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works for [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md) at the [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). She is one of many Dawnstar citizens being [plagued by nightmares](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md), and if you speak to her, she will insist that the dreams are real.

She sleeps inside [her house](Skyrim_Irgnir%27s_House.md) every night between midnight and 8am and immediately heads out to Iron-Breaker Mine where she works the [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) all day long, her efforts overseen by the bossy Beitild. Her evenings are spent inside [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) where she usually dines and chats with a worker from the [competing mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md), namely [Fruki](Skyrim_Fruki.md). The only exception to this routine happens when you first arrive at Dawnstar, when she will be one of the angry citizens complaining to [Erandur](Skyrim_Erandur.md) inside the inn. [Karl](Skyrim_Karl.md) is her confidant.

She wears common [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes), and carries nothing in her pockets but a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Irgnir%27s_House) to her house, a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
Before [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) is completed, she will tell you:

*"These dreams are real I tell you. Real!"* Otherwise, she is not that interested in chatting.

*"I'm just one of the miners. I don't want any trouble."* *"You should speak to the Jarl if you need something, stranger."*
