[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-npc-Viarmo.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Viarmo.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viarmo.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viarmo.jpg) Viarmo [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-npc-Viarmo_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Viarmo_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viarmo_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Viarmo_03.jpg) Lunch at the [Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) **Viarmo**, a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md), is Headmaster of the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and as such, oversees the efforts of the three teachers, [Inge Six Fingers](Skyrim_Inge_Six_Fingers.md), [Pantea Ateia](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md), and [Giraud Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md). His pride and joy is the Burning of King Olaf, an annual festival held by the College in order to celebrate Solitude. Due to the recent murder of [High King Torygg](Lore_High_King_Torygg.md), the mourning widow and current Jarl, [Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md), has put a stop to the festival. She reasons that burning a king in effigy is in poor taste, and you will have to help out a discouraged Viarmo prove her wrong during the quest [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md).

His room is located in the northeastern wing on the ground floor of the college and he sleeps there every night between 7pm and 6am. He will then find his usual spot in the lobby where he enjoys a one-hour breakfast at 7am. He stays in the lobby until 10am when he heads up to the [Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) for a three-hour lunch with Giraud and Pantea (see [notes](#Notes)). At 1pm he is back in the lobby where he spends the entire afternoon. After a one-hour dinner at 6pm he goes straight to bed. This daily routine only triggers upon completion of Tending the Flames; until then, Viarmo will wander around the ground floor of the Bards College 24 hours a day, with a few major exceptions listed [below](#Tending_the_Flames).

Viarmo wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8715) along with a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bards_College) to the college and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

*"Welcome to the Bard's College. I am the headmaster here. How may I help you?"* *"Welcome to Solitude. Home of the arts here in Skyrim."* *"It is my personal mission to ensure that the Bards College preserves the great deeds of the past, in both poetry and song."* *"Never forget, the most important job of any bard is to entertain. The day you lose your audience is the day you cut your lute strings."* *"I generally don't involve myself in politics. Good tales deal with the issues of the day, but a wise bard remains a neutral observer."* *"We bards record history - we do not make it. You'd do well to remember that."* When spoken to:

**What do you know about dragons?** *"Not much, to be honest. Their return was a shock to us all. Giraud Gemane has some tomes about them in the library, if you're interested."* **Has the war affected your college much?** *"Not much. But as a bard, I find the whole affair depressing. There are no heroes in this war. No winners to be had and no real conclusion. If you want something a bard can dig into look to the dragons. A thousand years from now Skyrim will have changed rulers dozens of times but the return of the dragons, that story is once in an era."* If you have conquered Solitude for the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), he will change this statement: *"There are no heroes in this war. Even with Ulfric here in Solitude, there are no winners to be had. No real conclusion."*

## Rumors
All the [bards](Skyrim_Bard.md) performing all over Skyrim will point you towards the Bards College, and some of them will even mention the Headmaster: *"I think the Bards College might be looking for recruits. You should speak to the head of the college, Viarmo, if you're interested."*, *"If you go there, talk to Viarmo. He's the headmaster."* and *"If you're thinking of applying, talk to Viarmo in Solitude."* A statement from local food merchant [Jala](Skyrim_Jala.md) proves that Viarmo is in high regards: *"Everyone who's anyone. Viarmo bought an apple the other day, said he was looking for a new student."*, while one of the students at the college, [Jorn](Skyrim_Jorn.md), is less impressed: *"It's an honor to have Viarmo for instruction on drum, but he's far too reserved for my tastes."* and *"Viarmo is too concerned with precision. What of instinct? Hmm? What of passion?"*

## Bugs
- After the related quest, Viarmo may hold the burning torch from the festival permanently, even when he is sleeping in bed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, open the console and select Viarmo. Then, type `removeitem 0001d4ec 1`, followed by `equipitem 0001d4ec`, and finally `removeitem 0001d4ec 1`.
- Attack Viarmo until he kneels down on the floor; he won't die as he is essential. Once he does that, he shouldn't be holding the torch any longer. A bounty will be earned if you use this method. Another method is by reverse-pickpocketing another torch into his inventory. This fixes the issue with minimal bloodshed. From then on, he will only equip the torch when appropriate.
- This also seems to have been fixed with the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md).
- Due to two different bugs in [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), the recitation may not match your choices, and some of the recitation can never be heard at all. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. One of the bugs is fixed, however the other is outstanding—see the quest page for more details.
