# Guild Halls

### [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md)

- **[Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md)** — A musical school in Solitude. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bards+College)

### [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md)

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md)** — A large mountaintop fort that served as the major Blades temple and outpost in Skyrim, now long abandoned and in Forsworn territory. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sky+Haven+Temple)

### [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md)

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-College_of_Winterhold.png) **[College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md)** — A school of the arcane arts. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=College+of+Winterhold)

### [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md)

- **[Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md)** — The mead hall headquarters of the Companions. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Jorrvaskr)
- **[The Underforge](Skyrim_The_Underforge.md)** — An ancient chamber hidden underneath the Skyforge used as a ritual chamber by the Circle of the Companions. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+Underforge)

### [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Settlement.png) **[Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md)** — An active Dark Brotherhood sanctuary west of Falkreath. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dark+Brotherhood+Sanctuary)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Settlement.png) **[Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md)** — A disused Dark Brotherhood sanctuary on the shore of the Sea of Ghosts near Dawnstar. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dawnstar+Sanctuary)

### [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A large fort southeast of Riften which serves as the Dawnguard's base of operations. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Fort+Dawnguard+%28DG%29)

### [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(place).md)** — The headquarters of the Thieves Guild located with the sewers beneath Riften, known as The Ratway. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Thieves+Guild)
- **[The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md)** — A tavern that is part of the Thieves Guild's headquarters. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=The+Ratway)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Nightingale Hall](Skyrim_Nightingale_Hall.md)** — The home of the Nightingales between Riften and the Shadow Stone. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nightingale+Hall)
