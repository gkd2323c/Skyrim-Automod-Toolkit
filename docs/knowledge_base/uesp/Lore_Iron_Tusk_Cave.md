[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Giant's Tooth is an island off the coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), located in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) northeast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md). It is reachable by ferry from [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup>

The island was known for the temperate environments found within Iron Tusk Cave. The humid environments within this cave allowed for the survival of a grove of [Ironwood](Lore_Ironwood.md) trees.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> By the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) this once common tree was rendered nearly extinct in Skyrim, no longer being found at such northern latitudes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nft To K-2)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the island was populated by [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md). They had set up a camp outside Iron Tusk Cave, killing the fishermen who were already encamped there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SR-1)</sup> According to some accounts, the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) journeyed to the island that year to obtain some Ironwood Fruit for use in a cure for a man in an advanced state of [ataxia](Lore_Ataxia.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> However, it is uncertain if the Dragonborn ever set foot on the island.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
