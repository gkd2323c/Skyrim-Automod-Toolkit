## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-interior-Honeystrand_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Honeystrand_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeystrand_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Honeystrand_Cave.jpg) Honeystrand Cave interior The cave is northwest of the road intersection south of Ivarstead's southern bridge. A single leveled bear guards the entrance. There are three [beehives](Skyrim_Beehive.md) nearby (one near the entrance and two on trees in front of the entrance with a vacant beehive between them), making it an ideal place for harvesting [bees](Skyrim_Bee.md), [beehive husks](Skyrim_Beehive_Husk.md), and [honeycombs](Skyrim_Honeycomb.md). Also near the entrance is a beehive husk, two [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md) fungi, a [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md), and many trees the bears have damaged or killed.

### Honeystrand Cave
The entrance leads directly into a single-chambered cave occupied by up to three bears. Ahead is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) with a growth of [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) fungus to the left and a dead [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md#Bosmer_Male) beside it; the bandit carries a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Locked Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room)*. Hanging from the roof of the cave and from ledges are three [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) plants, while several [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug_Thorax) fly around the rear of the cave. Next to the west wall is a dead [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) with another patch of imp stool, and against the north wall is a patch of [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) fungus. The floor of the cave is littered with old bones, both human and animal, including a lootable skeleton.

## Notes
- A dead woodcutter's campsite is northeast of the cave, up a hill toward [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md). Southeast of the campsite on the highest peak is a strange [chicken](Skyrim_Chicken.md) nest built on barrels and skeletal remains guarded by a novice [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md). Three of the chickens suddenly die as you approach. The fourth chicken, already dead, may be revived by the necromancer to attack. Near the nest in an open box are three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) and one [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md). A [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md) plant is also nearby.
- Honeystrand Cave appears in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) as [Honeystrand Hill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Honeystrand_Hill).
