Calcelmo, Scholar of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md), is an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md) of great renown who dedicates himself to researching the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md). He and his nephew Aicantar study the [Dwemer ruins beneath Markarth](Lore_Nchuand-Zel.md). He is the author of the series of books known simply as *[Dwarves](Lore_Dwarves_(book).md)*. His next expected work is a book on the translation of the [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) language which is tentatively titled *Calcelmo's Guide to the Falmer Tongue*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dialogue-1)</sup>

Calcelmo was known in the academic world for his translations of the falmer language.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Several translations of Falmer texts are accredited to Calcelmo.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-JOMA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TB-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DOFA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TTS-6)</sup> While Calcelmo was considered eccentric for spending all of his time studying the Dwemer ruins, he did find the time to fall in love with a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) woman named Faleen.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FLTC-8)</sup> Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Calcelmo became an ally of the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) after they defeated a giant [frostbite spider](Lore_Frostbite_Spider.md) in the excavation site beneath Markarth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dialogue-1)</sup> Calcelmo would often request the Dragonborn to give them Dwarven trinkets they discovered while also occasionally assisting them with their research.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

Calcelmo had come into possession of several unique oddities found during his excavations. Among them was [Stendarr's Hammer](Lore_Stendarr%27s_Hammer.md), which was found deep beneath Markarth and put on display in his Dwemer museum, where it was promptly stolen.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Another discovery was a strange [mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md#Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab) fused with Dwarven metal. He offered to sell this mudcrab to the Last Dragonborn.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>
