Amminus Entius was an old friend of [Cavor Merula](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md) who became the first quartermaster of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) after it was re-established in [2E 432](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_432).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOAE-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OOTH-2)</sup> The fourth pillar and "heart" of the Hour, he established the infrastructure for ensuring that [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md)'s Knights of the Order were the best-fed, best-equipped, and best-trained force in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOAE-1)</sup>

As quartermaster, he designed the uniform for the Hour Knights based off [Reman Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) regalia, writing the guide to crafting the armor that would still be used over a century later.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CM40:Oot H-3)</sup> A statue to Entius was later erected in Knightsgrave to his memory as one of the Order's founding members.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KLL-4)</sup>
