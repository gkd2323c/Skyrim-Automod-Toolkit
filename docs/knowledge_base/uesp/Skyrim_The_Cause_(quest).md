## Quick Walkthrough
1. Await news from a courier.
2. Read *[Stranger's Plea](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stranger%27s_Plea)*.
3. Go to the Shrine of Stendarr.
4. Defeat the two [Mythic Dawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn) assassins.
5. Read *[Skorvild's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skorvild%27s_Journal)* and *[Mythic Dawn Orders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Orders)*.
6. Search for clues in the [Mythic Dawn Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Camp).
7. Search [Rielle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle).
8. Read *[Janus' Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Janus%27_Journal)*.
9. Retrieve the four [Ayleid Elemental Shards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Miscellaneous_Items).
10. Locate the Ayleid Elemental Shard Holders and place the Ayleid Elemental Shards.
11. Take the [Great Welkynd Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Great_Welkynd_Stone).
12. Kill [Norion the Undying](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Norion_the_Undying).
13. Read the *[Vigilant Enforcer's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilant_Enforcer%27s_Journal)*.
14. Locate the Oblivion Gate.
15. (Optional) Wear a set of Mythic Dawn robes.
16. Defeat [Vonos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vonos).

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-quest-The_Cause_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cause_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_02.jpg) Ambush at the shrine [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-quest-The_Cause_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cause_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_03.jpg) The Mythic Dawn camp This quest will start once you reach level 46. A courier delivers the *[Stranger's Plea](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stranger%27s_Plea)* to you to read. It is a request to meet from Skorvild, an ex-Mythic Dawn cultist who turned away from the cult and needs help stopping their next nefarious plan.

Travel to [the Two Pillars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Stendarr#The_Two_Pillars), which is near the center of Whiterun Hold's tundra, south of Swindler's Den and northeast of Broken Fang Cave. It is close to and within sight of the Shrine of Zenithar to the west.

Sadly for Skorvild, his former cult-mates will catch up with him before he can speak with you. Once you arrive at the shrine you will spot the body of [Skorvild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skorvild), and two Mythic Dawn assassins will attack you. Once you have killed the assassins, loot their bodies for the *[Mythic Dawn Orders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Orders)*, and then search Skorvild for his [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skorvild%27s_Journal).

You will then need to head to the Mythic Dawn Camp to investigate. The camp itself is found north of the [Alchemist's Shack](Skyrim_Alchemist%27s_Shack.md). The camp has three hostile [Mythic Dawn explorers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Explorer). Once they are dispatched you can pick up the *[Excavation Leader's Journal](Skyrim_Excavation_Leader%27s_Journal.md)*. The journal explains that the Mythic Dawn are attempting to reopen an Oblivion gate, and have been excavating across the valley to the south in the Jerall Mountains for a powerful artifact that will help them do it.

### Varlais Cavern and Rielle
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-interior-Rielle_10.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Rielle_10.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rielle_10.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Rielle_10.jpg) Vanus and the other dead Mythic Dawn Travel to [Varlais Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Varlais_Cavern), which can be found south of [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md). As you enter the cavern, you will find an excavation site inside that has cracked open an entrance to the Ayleid ruins of [Rielle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle). There are Ayleid casks and urns that can be looted throughout the chambers. As you get deeper into the ruin, you will enter a large room with the corpses of cultists and the corpse of [Janus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Janus), there are [wights](Skyrim_Wight.md) patrolling so be prepared for a fight.

After reading the journal, you will need to locate the elemental stones:

- [Elemental Shard - Wel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Miscellaneous_Items), found on the corpse of a Mythic Dawn Explorer
- [Elemental Shard - Sercen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Miscellaneous_Items), carried by a Wight Lord
- [Elemental Shard - Nen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Miscellaneous_Items), carried by a Wight Lord just before the door to [Rielle Crypt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle#Rielle_Crypt)
- [Elemental Shard - Alata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Miscellaneous_Items), carried by a Wight Lord in Rielle Crypt

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-quest-The_Cause_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cause_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_04.jpg) The shard holders Once you have found all the shards, you will need to head deeper into the ruins to find the shard holders, take care to avoid the traps. When it comes to placing the elemental shards in the holders you need to place them in specific holders:

- Wel goes in the **North** holder
- Sercen goes in the **South** holder
- Nen goes in the **West** holder
- Alata goes in the **East** holder

Once you have placed the shards, a secret door will open and reveal a large room with a [Great Welkynd Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Great_Welkynd_Stone). Take the stone and [Norion the Undying](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Norion_the_Undying), an [Ayleid Lich](Skyrim_Ayleid_Lich.md) with as much power as a [Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), will burst out of his coffin. Beware his minions, including two [Wight](Skyrim_Wight.md) archers who sleep in the coffins upstairs on either side of the room. Once Norion is killed you can search his body for the [Staff of Ehlno Ede](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede) and the [Rielle key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rielle_Key). You will be directed to defeat Norion once you take the Great Welkynd Stone even if you have already noticed Norion and woke him with a rune spell to defeat him early.

Go back to the room where you placed the four stones. At the south part of that room there is a small dead-end room where you can find a switch that opens the way out.

Head back into the main area of Rielle, where you will see Vigilants of Stendarr (including Vigil Enforcers if the [Vigil Enforcer Armor Set](Skyrim_Vigil_Enforcer_Armor_Set.md) Creation has been installed) fighting with Mythic Dawn agents. You can aid the Vigilants against the Mythic Dawn. Once you get back to the entrance of Rielle, there is a corpse of a Vigil Enforcer that has a [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilant_Enforcer%27s_Journal), read the journal and you will find out that the location of the Oblivion gate is in [Red Scar Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Scar_Cavern). Note: If you choose to wear Mythic Dawn Robes while exiting Rielle, the Vigilants outside will mistake you for a Mythic Dawn member and attack. If you do not wear the robes, they will let you pass.

### Red Scar Cavern
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SR-quest-The_Cause_06.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cause_06.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_06.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cause_06.jpg) Vonos There is a Mythic Dawn Guard outside of the cavern that can be tricked if you wear a set of Mythic Dawn robes, if you wish to avoid fighting. This will also work on the cultists inside. Continue through the cavern and you will eventually reach a door to the Mythic Dawn Temple. Once inside the temple, you will see an inactive Oblivion gate, you will then have to kill [Vonos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vonos). Once you kill him however, the Oblivion gate will open, the quest will finish and [The Consequences](Skyrim_The_Consequences.md) will begin.

## Bugs
- The quest may start the first time you get a courier.
- Norion the Undying may be triggered before you take the Great Welkynd Stone, which allows you to kill him and prevents moving forward past objective 150. Bug confirmed on [Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) Anniversary Edition. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](https://images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](Skyrim_Switch.md) On Switch, grab the stone in sneak mode before attacking Norion. When you need to read the journal, read it while looting the corpse.
- Either revert to a previous save or reset to a previous stage.
- Save before enter Rielle Crypt; if you see the Wight Lord near the entrance and not *waiting* to wake up when you approach, you have triggered the bug.
- If you leave the second area of Rielle or Rielle altogether without finishing the quest, the pressure plates in the floor won't activate for the Ayleid doors to slide open. - Solution to above: Use the console command `tcl` on PC to walk through the gate, then use it again to turn collisions back on. A button to raise the gates is found inside the main room. Your followers, if any, will now be able to accompany you to fight against Norion and the other enemies.
- On console, use two platters (one for each gate) and Whirlwind Sprint/Sprint to clip through the gates. Hold the platter to slightly above waist height (press and hold the interact button to move objects) to be flush with the gate, and then use Whirlwind Sprint. The platter will not phase through the gate with you and you can not pick it up from the other side, so if you do this with only one platter you will become stuck in the middle of the two locked gates indefinitely.
- Attempting to read the Excavation Leader's Journal causes a CTD and in addition the entrance to Rielle appears to be missing if you go to the location to skip reading the journal.
- Varlais Cavern marked as completed on the map before entering and clearing the instance. Cavern entrance also not visible at the location. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If you don't read the Janus journal before reaching the Great Welkynd Stone, the stone might not be an object you can take when you find it, blocking the final stages. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If before you take the Great Welkynd Stone, you trigger Niron's attacking by casting a rune spell on his crypt or attacking one of the wights in the open coffins, then after you defeat Norion and take the Great Welkynd Stone, the objective to defeat Norion will reappear and it will be impossible to remove from your journal until the quest has been completed by killing Vonos.
- The door to enter the Rielle Crypt as well as the option to open the gate behind will not show an option to enter or open. <sup>**?**</sup> - Solution to above: Deleting the Skyrim-Patch.bsa file from the Skyrim Special Edition\Data folder corrects the issue.
- The cave entrance may glitch with a large boulder in its place, typical of vanilla. The Mythic Dawn guard will hover inside the rock face and may emerge to fight. <sup>**?**</sup> - Solution to above: use the console command `tcl` on PC which allows you to disable collisions, retype it to to reactivate collisions
- It is possible to enter Red Scar Cavern and kill Vonos before initiating the quest. By heading to the back of the cavern with the un-interactable door and inspecting the door's handle, a prompt to open it may appear. This will take the player to the room with Vonos and the gate, where killing him will immediately begin [The Consequences](Skyrim_The_Consequences.md). The Oblivion Gate will remain open when the quest is begun, but the player may not be able to proceed with the quest as the oblivion gate cannot be interacted with.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage cc BGSSSE067_Quest stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest cc BGSSSE067_Quest`.
