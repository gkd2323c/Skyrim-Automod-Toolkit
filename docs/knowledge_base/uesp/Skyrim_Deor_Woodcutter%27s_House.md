## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). Along the east wall is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), a wood pile, and a [blue mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower). Along the west wall are two more wood piles. The entrance has a novice lock.

### Deor Woodcutter's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Deor_Woodcutter%27s_House.jpg) Deor Woodcutter's House interior The entrance leads into a large main room with an end table on your left with a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) and [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top. here are many sacksof food throughout. Against the south wall is a wood pile with six pieces of firewood and a woodcutter's axe leaning against the pile. Above is a wall shelf holding a sprig of snowberries. In the southwest corner is a doorway to a double bedroom. In the middle of the room is a small square table and two chairs. On the table is a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). Against the west wall is a crate, a barrel with a mead barrel on top and above it all is a wall shelf holding an [ice wolf pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Wolf_Pelt). To the right of the entrance are three barrels, with a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) on top of one of them. To the left is a set of shelves containing a copy of *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)*. Around the corner is a fire pit with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) and a cast iron pot containing a cabbage beside it. Above the cooking pot is a hanging rack containing three whole [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid. To the west along the north wall the room continues alongside the bedroom with a narrow table against the north wall with a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and a copy of *[The Woodcutter's Wife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Woodcutter%27s_Wife)* on top. At the rear against the west wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of ale, a cast iron pot containing three pieces of [clam meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat), an open crate containing two loaves of bread, and two [eidar cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel).

Entering the bedroom there is a cupboard on your left with a [deer hide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deer_Hide), a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and copy of *[Final Lesson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Final_Lesson)*. Against the west wall is a dresser with a bottle and [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a wooden bowl with a piece of clam meat inside. Coming out from the north wall is a double bed with an end table on either side. On the one on the left is a loaf of bread and bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), while on the one on the right is a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge).
