[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-creature-Ahzidal.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ahzidal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ahzidal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ahzidal.jpg) Ahzidal **Ahzidal** is a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) entombed within [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md) in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). He carries an [eponymous mask](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(item).md).

He specializes in fire-based magic, able to cast: [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), [Revenant](Skyrim_Revenant.md), [Raise Zombie](Skyrim_Raise_Zombie.md), [Flame Cloak](Skyrim_Flame_Cloak.md), and Fire Stream, a unique spell similar to the [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md) shout. His heavy armor set includes [Ahzidal's Armor of Retribution](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Armor_of_Retribution.md), [Ahzidal's Boots of Waterwalking](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Boots_of_Waterwalking.md), [Ahzidal's Gauntlets of Warding](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Gauntlets_of_Warding.md), [Ahzidal's Helm of Vision](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Helm_of_Vision.md), and his mask.

For more information, see the [lore page](Lore_Ahzidal.md).

## Related Quests

## Notes
- Ahzidal means "the Embittered Destroyer" (or literally "Bitter Destroyer") in the [Dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
