[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-npc-Heimvar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Heimvar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Heimvar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Heimvar.jpg) Heimvar **Heimvar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Blacksmith) who lives and works at [Solitude Blacksmith](Skyrim_Solitude_Blacksmith.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He is [Beirand's](Skyrim_Beirand.md) apprentice and can most often be found using the grindstone inside the shop. He is supposed to replace Beirand as blacksmith if something happens to him, but will not due to a [bug](#Bugs).

He wakes at 8am to start his workday, which ends at 8pm. He spends the rest of the day relaxing and wandering around the place until 12am, then he heads to bed.

Heimvar wears a grey [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron#0006ff37) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Solitude_Blacksmith) to the blacksmith, a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), as well as a selection of [middle-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When you approach him, Heimvar may greet you with:

*"Just arrive in the city? Is it true what they say, then? There are dragons about?"* *"We've got strong swords and sturdy armor, if you're looking to buy."* *"Apprentice blacksmith, at your service."* **(Beirand is alive)** *"Think I'll forge me a new sword, just in case things go awry here in Solitude. I'm the blacksmith's apprentice, you see."* **(Beirand is alive)** *"Blacksmith, at your service."* **(Beirand is dead)** Speaking to him:

**Do you make anything here that will kill dragons?** *"Ha! I like you. No man is a match for a dragon. Buying one of our blades wouldn't hurt your chances, though."* **What does this job pay?** *"Food, experience and a roof over my head in the finest city in Skyrim. If money's all you hold dear, then you you * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * end up like [Jaree-Ra](Skyrim_Jaree-Ra.md). A plague on the city."* **What's your problem with Jaree-Ra?** *"Captain Aldis can't prove it, but that [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) is up to no good."* **Jaree-Ra is dead.** (If you have completed the quest [Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!)) *"Really? I knew he was mixed up in something dangerous, but I hadn't heard he was dead."* Ending conversation with him will have him say:

*"If you're off to fight, talk to Beirand before you go. His armor is well forged and dependable."* *"Good hunting. Go kill some bandits for me."* *"May you * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * sword feast on [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) flesh."* **(Solitude is controlled by the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md))**
## Conversations
When working, he and Beirand may tease each other:

**Beirand**: *"Come on, Heimvar. My son could stoke the forge better than you do!"* **OR** *"You're making progress, Heimvar. But my boy could still work the bellows better than you do!"*
**Heimvar**: *"Yeah, but can he hammer the anvil for 8 hours straight?"* **OR** *"Sure, but I bet he can't hold his mead as well as me!"*
**Beirand**: *"Give him time. In a couple years, he'll be the age you were when you started!"* **OR** *"Right you are! Guess I'll keep training you... for now."*

## Bugs
- He is supposed to be the backup blacksmith in case something happens to Beirand. However, he is not set up properly, so if Beirand dies there is no longer anyone who will provide blacksmith merchant services in Solitude. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
