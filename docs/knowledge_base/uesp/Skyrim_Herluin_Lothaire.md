[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-npc-Herluin_Lothaire.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Herluin_Lothaire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Herluin_Lothaire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Herluin_Lothaire.jpg) Herluin Lothaire **Herluin Lothaire** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary) who operates a shop at [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). He will move in after you have completed two [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) special reputation jobs for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md). There is a usable [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md) inside his shop along with various [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) and [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md).

Herluin will spend all his time serving his shop. He sits on a chair or works at the alchemy lab present in the store. He will not barter with you if you have upset the Thieves Guild by breaking one of their rules. He will not offer you his services again until you [pay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reparations) for your offense.

Herluin wears [blue mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0006b46c), and is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He carries three leveled [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), of which two of them are filled, as well as a small amount of gold. He knows the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) and [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

*"So, name your poison! Ha ha ha. Oh, I never get tired of that one!"* *"Need some sort of toxin for a special occasion? That's where I come in."* *"I sell all sorts of poisons for almost every occasion."* When you have offended the Thieves Guild, he will not want your business but will give you some advice:

*"You want my advice? Pay your debt to [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) quickly and she'll forget the whole thing."*

## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads *"Herluin is young but is a very sharp apothecary with unique skills who can aid the Thieves Guild through his unusual concoctions. He is always preoccupied, a bitoff into space when being spoken to, but that shouldn't be mistaken for absentmindedness."* - Despite this, his voicetype and dialogue in-game make him out to be an older man.
