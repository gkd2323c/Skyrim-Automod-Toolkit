[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-npc-Legate_Emmanuel_Admand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Legate_Emmanuel_Admand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Emmanuel_Admand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Emmanuel_Admand.jpg) Legate Emmanuel Admand **Legate Emmanuel Admand** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) who leads the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) forces in [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). He can be found in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) if the Imperials control the Reach, or at [Reach Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reach_Imperial_Camp) if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) control the Reach.

He wears [Imperial armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bracers). He wields an [Imperial sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword) and a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger). For ranged combat, he carries an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow). He carries a number of [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) and his sleep outfit, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).
