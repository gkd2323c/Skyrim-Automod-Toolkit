[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-concept-wolf.png/200px-ON-concept-wolf.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-wolf.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-wolf.png) A Wolf Wolves are aggressive canines found throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are pack hunters and can often be found in packs of two or three, using their highly developed sense of smell to track their prey. When threatened, they may howl and thus draw in other members of their pack. When attacking, they will often attempt to circle around behind their prey to bite.

Wolves are related to domesticated [dogs](Lore_Dog.md), and the two can be bred to produce [wolfdogs](Lore_Wolfdog.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

In [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) tradition, the Goddess of Love, [Mara](Lore_Mara.md) is associated with the wolf. Often times she is also known as the "Mother Wolf" or the "She-Wolf".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DATN-3)</sup> Similar connection can be found among the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mara Wolf Markarth-4)</sup> A wolf is one of the many animals carved into puzzles on the dungeons of Skyrim.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRPuzzle Door-5)</sup>

The [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) see wolves as one of the most blessed of the creations of the [All-Maker](Lore_All-Maker.md), for their speed, agility and skills as hunter.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BMSkaal-6)</sup> One of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials is to slay the Wolf Guardian Spirit, one of the seven Guardian Beasts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KST-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRFroki-8)</sup> [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s aspect of speed takes the form of a wolf.

Wolves are known to carry infectious diseases and in some places, like [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), they are called [plague wolves](Lore_Plague_Wolf.md). The wolves from Cyrodiil may carry helljoint, the ones in Skyrim sometimes carry rockjoint and the wolves from Solstheim can carry yellow tick.

Wolves are hunted for their pelts, which can be tanned into leather. They are also occasionally domesticated and kept as companions and mounts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOPet-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup> [Dire Wolves](Lore_Dire_Wolf.md) are a related but separate species of canine.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-GDW-11)</sup>

Many people and factions have adopted the wolf as their coat of arms or symbol. The cities of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md)<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONKvatch Banner-12)</sup> and [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) use a wolf's head as their heraldry. For the latter, it represents the spirit of its people.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-OTTF-13)</sup> For the former, it was coming from Count [Carolus Aquilarios](Lore_Carolus_Aquilarios.md), who was known as the Wolf of Kvatch for his bravery and inherited the wolf's head as his symbol in the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TWATD-14)</sup> Emperor [Titus Mede I](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md)'s personal symbol was the wolf's head and anyone branded with it was part of his innermost circle.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-LOS-15)</sup><sup>:22</sup> Arguably most famously, the Queen of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude_(kingdom).md), [Potema](Lore_Potema.md) was known as the "Wolf-Queen" because in her youth, she invoked a resemblance to the wolf, and not because of her looks.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-TWQ2-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-BOTWQ-17)</sup> Her banners depict a wolf design.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-SRPotema Banner-18)</sup>

## Breeds

### Doom Wolf
Doom Wolves are a breed of giant wolf. They have black fur and glowing red eyes, and their howl is unnaturally distorted. Doom Wolves are used as mounts and were bred for size, savagery, and stealth from the fierce [timber wolves](Lore_Wolf.md#Timber_Wolf) of the southern [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md). They were adopted by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) for use as stealthy mounts, with the breed's glowing red eyes weighing heavily in the decision.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup>

Not to be confused with [Death Hounds](Lore_Death_Hound.md) and [Hell Hounds](Lore_Hell_Hound.md).

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Doom_Wolf)

### Gorne Striped Wolf
**Gorne Striped Wolves** (or **Gorne Wolves**) are wolves found on the Isle of [Gorne](Lore_Gorne.md), off the east coast of Morrowind, where they terrorized the island's settlers until they were suppressed in the Troubled House Hunt.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup> When Gorne was invaded by legions of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) from the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), many of the wolves were magically tamed by the Daedra for use as war beasts. In particular, a male [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md) named Solemn tamed a significant number.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Gorne_Striped_Wolf)

Helkarn Wolves are large wolves found in Elsweyr. They are trusting and gentle-natured, and after the villagers of [Helkarn](Lore_Helkarn.md) found them unsuitable as watchdogs, they bred them to mountable size.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup>

### Karthwolf

Some, known as Karthwolf Chargers, were bred for size and endurance to use as mounts,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup> while others, called Karthwolf Shepherds, protect their livestock and cull the weak from the herds.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOPet-9)</sup>

### Long-Legged Swamp Wolf
A species of wolves formerly found in Black Marsh. It lost great numbers in the Great Burn of [1E 2828](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2828) and eventually went extinct in the [2E 10 s](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_10).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Antiquity WTS-21)</sup>

- **Found in:** [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Blackwood_Antiquities#Wolf-Tail_Sash)

### Painted Wolf
Painted Wolves are a tan-colored variety of wolf native to the rugged badlands of [central](Lore_Sunforge.md) [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). The [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of these canyon lands have bred the local Painted Wolves into large, sure-footed mounts to help them navigate the broken terrain, allowing them to go places inaccessible to the more usual [camel](Lore_Camel.md) mounts.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOMount-10)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Painted_Wolf)

### Snow Wolf
[Snow wolves](Lore_Snow_Wolf.md) are white-colored and can be found in the far north of Tamriel. They are more resistant to frost than other wolf varieties and can cause frostbite to those they bite. The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) can use their pelts to create frost resistant armor.

- **Found in:** [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Snow_Wolf), [Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Snow_Wolf), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Snow Wolf), [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Dungeons#Encounters)

- Conjurers are able to compel [Flame](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) and [Storm Atronachs](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md) to adopt the form of wolves.
- A mushroom known as the [Canis Cup](Lore_Flora_C.md#Canis_Cup_Mushroom) is known to prevent wolves from attacking those who eat it for a short time. Upon consumption, it disguises its user scent by replacing it with one described by [Thane Oda Wolf-Sister](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Thane_Oda_Wolf-Sister) as "[...]that wonderful musky scent of fur and dirt and grass and old meat."
