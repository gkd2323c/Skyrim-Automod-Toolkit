# Ohmes

*When Jode is New and Jone is Full
Then shall be born the Ohmes
Hairless infiltrators that most cannot even see are Khajiit
Beautiful, yes, but more akin to Elves in visage
Still, their unique beauty offers advantage
The Ohmes are among our greatest spies.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/5/54/Mnew_Sfull.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Sfull.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Sfull.jpg) Ohmes are born when Masser is new and Secunda is full The Ohmes are a bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). They are said to resemble [elves](Lore_Elves.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> specifically [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), though they are sometimes shorter. To avoid being mistaken as such, many Ohmes tattoo or [paint](Lore_Cosmetics.md) their faces to resemble a feline aspect.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RDKA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-IWTB-4)</sup> They have also occasionally been described as [man](Lore_Man.md) -like and man-faced,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MUT-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1E-6)</sup> though it is the taller [Ohmes-raht](Lore_Ohmes-raht.md) who resemble the races of men more.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-IWTB-4)</sup> They are said to be the only furstock not to receive the blessing of fur.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Survival G-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> They are said to make excellent infiltrators.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup>

The [Imperial Geographical Society](Lore_Imperial_Geographical_Society.md) has claimed them to be the most common kind of Khajiit to be encountered outside of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), usually as adventurers or diplomats.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1E-6)</sup>
