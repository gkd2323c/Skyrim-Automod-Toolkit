**Battle Fury** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) added by the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion that improves the fighting ability of allies by speeding up their attacks, similar to the [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md) shout's effect on you.

## Related Quests
- **[Lost Legacy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legacy)**: Travel to [Vahlok's Tomb](Skyrim_Vahlok%27s_Tomb.md) and defeat [Vahlok the Jailor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vahlok_the_Jailor).

## Notes
- Like Elemental Fury, if this shout is used on an ally whose weapon is already enchanted, it has no effect.
