## Walleye
The **Walleye** is a type of fish that can be found in the rivers of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).

- The **Silver Walleye** can be found in the lakes of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Warmouth
The **Warmouth** is a type of fish that can be found in the rivers of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Wedgefish
The **Wedgefish** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) and [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md), off the coasts of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Weever
**Weevers** are a type of fish.

- The **Tum Weever** can be found in the cold river waters of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Whalefish
The **Whalefish** is a type of fish.

- The **Flabby Whalefish** could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).
- The **Oil-Eater Whalefish** can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). They were previously assumed to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Whitefish
The **Whitefish** is a type of freshwater fish.

- **Z'en's Whitefish** can be found in the lakes of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Wolf-Eel
The **Wolf-Eel** is a type of [eel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eel) -like fish that can be found in the foul waters of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Wrasse
The **Wrasse** is a type of fish.

- The **Akaviri Wrasse** can be found in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md), off the coast of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).
- The **Ancestor Wrasse** can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It was previously thought to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
