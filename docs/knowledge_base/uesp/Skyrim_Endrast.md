[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-Endrast.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Endrast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Endrast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Endrast.jpg) Endrast **Endrast**, a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [sorcerer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Sorcerer), was a local explorer of some renown who accompanied [Sulla Trebatius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sulla_Trebatius) on an expedition to the [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) Dwemer ruins.

After they were captured by [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), he managed to pick the lock on their cell so they could escape. Despite initially being saved by [Yag gra-Gortwog](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yag_gra-Gortwog), he succumbed to his wounds beneath Dwarven steam pipes in the animonculory not long after writing his final thoughts in his [journal](Skyrim_Endrast%27s_Journal.md), found nearby his corpse along with a piece of [charcoal](Skyrim_Charcoal.md), and a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).

Endrast wears [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) with no shoes.

## Notes
- Endrast was likely created with [Enthir](Skyrim_Enthir.md) as a base, as he still retains some of the latter's AI packages and factions. This also results in a number of oddities, as seen below. - At some point, the Unofficial Patch also removed the AI packages from his package list, although this isn't noted in the changelog.

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you have an old version of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) installed Endrast will trigger [Rejoining the College](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rejoining_the_College) if raised and you kill him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=21011))
- Due to the fact that he's part of the [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) crime faction, should you resurrect and re-kill him, then you could theoretically receive a bounty in that hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
