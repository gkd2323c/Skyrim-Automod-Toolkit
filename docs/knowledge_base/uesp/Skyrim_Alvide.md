[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-npc-Alvide.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alvide.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvide.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvide.jpg) Alvide **Alvide** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) camped out in [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md). She came to [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) for protection from the [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) but the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) won't let her in. She came with her husband [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md) who also lives in the camp.

Like the other refugees, Alvide will show up in Dayspring Canyon during the quest [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md). With no access to the fort, she will do very little but wander around, at times chopping some firewood or use the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). She never sleeps. She wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md#0001be1a) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b).

## Dialogue
She is worried about her situation, potentially saying:

- *"What if the vampires come here, after the Dawnguard? We'll be stuck right in the middle."*
- *"Barknar insisted we come here, said we'd be safe. He also said we'd be inside the walls."*
- *"At least Barknar and I are here together."*
- *"I hope we can go home soon. I don't like it here."*

## Notes
