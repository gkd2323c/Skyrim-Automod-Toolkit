**M'Sharra** is a former [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) thief who was a member of the [Crimson Dirks](Lore_Crimson_Dirks.md) before their disbandment some time before the [Skyrim Civil War](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CD9-1)</sup> In [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) she met the [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) Tyra Blood-Fire, when M'Sharra had attempted to steal silver out of the Nord's pocket. After hearing the Khajiit's story, Tyra took pity on her and M'Sharra was offered membership into the Crimson Dirks. The gang eventually made M'Sharra a suit of [silver](Lore_Silver.md) armor, at its heart was the silver piece M'Sharra had once tried to steal from Tyra.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Crimson Dirks eventually disbanded, and most of its members headed north to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in small groups before splitting up at the border, then refused contact in order to protect themselves and each other.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CD9-1)</sup> Unable to wear her armor in Skyrim in fear of exposing her identity, and suspecting that she was being followed, M'Sharra stored her armor in a shack northwest of [Rorikstead](Lore_Rorikstead.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MC-2)</sup> When M'Sharra took to begging in [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) and saw another member of the Crimson Dirks, Zaharia, M'Sharra called out to her but the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) did not reply. Despising her new life, craving her old one, and wanting to be a warrior once more, M'Sharra fought a Whiterun guard and was thrown into the Dragonsreach dungeon.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Her ultimate fate is unknown as the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) was not able to find her in the dungeon.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRCC-5)</sup>
