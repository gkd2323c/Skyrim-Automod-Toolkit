# Mermaid

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/ON-place-Anvil_05.jpg/200px-ON-place-Anvil_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Anvil_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Anvil_05.jpg) Mermaid statue, "The Mermaid of Anvil" (ESO) Mermaids (also called **Selkies**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESIVOOGG-1)</sup><sup>:353</sup> are mythical creatures with a presence in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md). They resemble young women with a long [fish](Lore_Fish.md) tail instead of legs, but the nature of these creatures is unknown.

The most well-known mermaid is the [Mermaid of Anvil](Lore_Mermaid_of_Anvil.md), an old stone [statue](Lore_Statues.md) that is beloved by the people of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md). The grandeur of the statue rivals other effigies dedicated to the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) or even [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) leading many to believe that the mermaid statue is based on a long-forgotten deity. Some rumors claim that it is a depiction of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) as she appeared to some ancient sea-dwelling civilization. It was apparently built before any structure was made in Anvil, implying that the statue was the foundation of the county city's creation. It has been called many names, such as the Brine Maiden and the Salty Serenader.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TMMo A-2)</sup> There is another legend, regarding the statue, in which it is called the Selkie of West Skerry and is described as a [seal](Lore_Seal.md) -maid. It is said that she would guide sailors through the dense fog around the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESIVOOGG-1)</sup><sup>:353</sup>

The sailors of [Saintsport](Lore_Saintsport.md) are known to carve [whale](Lore_Whale.md) bones into whistles that resemble reclining mermaids.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONContraband-3)</sup> Within the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), in the [Bliss](Lore_Bliss_(place).md) portion of [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md), the [Mad God](Lore_Sheogorath.md) is depicted on a fountain being held up by mermaids.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIBliss-4)</sup> The Thief God [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md) claims to have kissed a mermaid on the shores of [Khenarthia](Lore_Khenarthia.md), but he admits it may have just been a shapely fish considering it was during a moonlit night.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rajhin Shadow-5)</sup> Small stone mermaid figurines, often made of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) were commonly used as a fertility idols among the commoners of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EMS-6)</sup>

The word "mermaid" is occasionally used as an insult towards the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HTBC-7)</sup> Mermaid Whiskey is a type of [alcoholic beverage](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MW-8)</sup>
