# Gray-Mane

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-npc-Gray-Manes.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gray-Manes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gray-Manes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gray-Manes.jpg) The Gray-Manes The **Gray-Manes** are an old and influential clan in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). Before the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), the Gray-Manes were friends with their equally venerable neighbors, clan [Battle-Born](Skyrim_Battle-Born.md). However, the Gray-Manes have become strong supporters of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), as well as [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). The Battle-Borns sided with the Empire, and so now the clans are feuding.

[Eorlund Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Eorlund_Gray-Mane.md) is probably the clan's most renowned member, being the master smith at Whiterun's [Skyforge](Skyrim_Skyforge.md). Smiths all over Skyrim can be heard referring to his skill.

[House Gray-Mane](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md) can be found near the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md) in Whiterun.
