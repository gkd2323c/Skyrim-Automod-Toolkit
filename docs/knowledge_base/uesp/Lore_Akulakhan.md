# Akulakhan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/MW-place-Akulakhan.jpg/200px-MW-place-Akulakhan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Akulakhan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Akulakhan.jpg) [Akulakhan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Dagoth_Ur_(facility)#Akulakhan.27s_Chamber) from below, as seen in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind) Akulakhan, also called the **Second [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md)**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWVivec-1)</sup>, is the name of the [machine](Lore_Golem.md) [god](Lore_God.md) built by [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) under [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). Using the divine influence of the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md) to power Akulakhan, he planned to use it to drive all outlanders out of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), cast down the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), spread [Corprus](Lore_Corprus.md), and eventually take over all [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DUDialogue-2)</sup>

Dagoth Ur also planned to establish a theocracy based on the worship of Akulakhan with his [Ash Vampire](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md) brothers serving as the god priests of Akulakhan.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DUP-3)</sup> But, in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) arrived in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), recovered [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md)'s Tools—the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) artifacts [Wraithguard](Lore_Wraithguard.md), [Sunder](Lore_Sunder.md), and [Keening](Lore_Keening.md) —and led an assault against Dagoth Ur's stronghold. Using the power of the tools, the Nerevarine was able to destroy the tonal enchantments on the Heart, killing Dagoth Ur and destroying Akulakhan.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Morrowind-4)</sup>
