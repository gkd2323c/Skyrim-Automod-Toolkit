[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Hjordis.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hjordis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjordis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjordis.jpg) Hjordis **Hjordis** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [werewolf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enc Class Werewolf) living under [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md). She came to stay with the Frostmoon pack after her former pack was wiped out by [werebears](Skyrim_Werebear.md) in the northeast.

Hjordis wears [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) along with a pair of [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) and a pair of [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md). She wields a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) with leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md) or a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) in combat.

## Dialogue
If you are a werewolf, she will greet you with:

- *"What is it, pup?"*
- *"I don't trust you, whelp. Keep that in mind."*

If you aren't a werewolf she will only say:

- *"Get out of here."*

When spoken to directly:

- **Are you part of this pack, too?**
- *"For now. My pack lived in the mountains northeast of here, 'till they were wiped out. Damn werebears. [Majni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Majni) offered to help me reclaim my territory. I hunt with him until then. After that... we'll see."*

When exiting conversation:

- *"Goodbye, whelp."*
- *"Hmph."*
