[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg/200px-SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg) Four Skull Lookout **Four Skull Lookout** is a small [outdoor Nordic ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings) south of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md).

It is one of the three ruins visited for the quest [Destruction Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Destruction_Ritual_Spell.md). With the [Divine Crusader](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, the named bandits [Eigorn](Skyrim_Eigorn.md), [Oren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oren), [Remy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remy), and [Viparth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viparth) can be found here. They are in possession of the Crusader's Relics.

## Related Quests
- **[Destruction Ritual Spell](Skyrim_Destruction_Ritual_Spell.md)**: Learn the most powerful [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells Skyrim has to offer.
- **[Relics of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Relics_of_the_Crusader)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Divine_Crusader.md)</sup>: Reclaim the holy relics of Pelinal Whitestrake.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The easiest approach to the ruin is from the [Red Eagle Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle_Redoubt) lower camp (before the ascent). The ruin consists of a single room with open doorways to the northwest and southeast. It contains a single unlocked chest containing [leveled loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md) next to a campfire, with a searchable pot hanging over it and two unowned bedrolls around it. Next to one of these are an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and [shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md). Outside, in front of the northwestern doorway, is a pedestal, with a chair off to the left. It is the home of three bandits: a warrior, archer, and wizard.

There are three [juniper trees](Skyrim_Juniper_Berries.md) around the ruin, and a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) lodged against the southwestern side of the ruin. One [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) plant can be found to the left of the southeastern doorway. Nearby is a leveled animal that may get drawn into any battle with the bandits.

## Notes
- Directly west of the ruin, on a promontory toward the cliff, a [shrine of Zenithar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar) can be found along with minor loot including a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a couple of rolls of paper, several loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), and a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md).
- A [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) can be found among the rocks just in front of the pedestal.
- The bandits do not seem to respawn.<sup>[*verification needed — See [discussion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Four_Skull_Lookout)*]</sup>
