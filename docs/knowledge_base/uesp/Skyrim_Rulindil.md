[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-npc-Rulindil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rulindil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rulindil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rulindil.jpg) Rulindil **Rulindil** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) interrogator, and the Third Emissary of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md), located in the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). He reports to [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md), the First Emissary. You can overhear an argument with [Gissur](Skyrim_Gissur.md) about payment for his information. Afterwards, Rulindil heads down to the dungeon to interrogate [Etienne Rarnis](Skyrim_Etienne_Rarnis.md).

He wears [hooded Thalmor robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes), [Thalmor boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Boots), and [Thalmor gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Gloves). He also carries the [Interrogation Chamber Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Interrogation_Chamber_Key) and an [elven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger).

## Quest-Related Events
Rulindil can be overheard talking to Gissur:

**Gissur:** *"But, I need that money! I earned it. I have my own expenses you know..."* **Rulindil:** *"Silence! Do not presume, Gissur. You are most useful, but do not presume. We have other informants who are less... offensive."* **Gissur:** *"But no one else has brought you such valuable information, have they? Etienne, he's talked, hasn't he? He knows where that old man is you're looking for, he told me himself."* **Rulindil:** *"You'll get the rest of your money when we confirm his story. As agreed."* **Gissur:** *"So he has talked! I knew it!"* **Rulindil:** *"Everyone talks, in the end. Now, I have work to do. Leave me to it, if you ever want to see the rest of your payment."* **Gissur:** *"Can I... I could help you. He'd talk to me. He trusts me."* **Rulindil:** *"You'd like to come downstairs with me, is that it, Gissur? Shall we loose his bonds and put you in a cell together? You can ask him anything you like, and see how he answers."* **Gissur:** *"No, no. I'll... I'll wait outside."* **Rulindil:** *"That would probably be best. Now get out!"* While interrogating:

**Etienne:** *"Stop. Please. I don't know anything else. Don't you think I'd have told you already?"* **Thalmor Soldier:** *"Silence. You know the rules. Do not speak unless spoken to. That's all I know."* **Rulindil:** *"And his name is...?"* **Etienne:** *"I don't know his name. Like I've already told you a hundred- Ahhh!"* **Rulindil:** *"You know the rules. Just answer the questions. And where can we find this nameless old man?"* **Etienne:** *"Like I said, I don't know! I've seen him down in the Ratway.
