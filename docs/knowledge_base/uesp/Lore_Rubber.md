# Rubber

Rubber is an elastic material. Throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and beyond, rubber has found a multitude of applications across various cultures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM70-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RSM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FSB-3)</sup>

Rubber mallets, were used to alleviate discomfort associated with arthritic joints, though their effectiveness was a subject of a debate.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Joint_Hammer-4)</sup>

In ancient times, [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) utilized it in the fabrication of their garments. Rubber-soled sandals were a standard choice for footwear among all social classes, often paired with fringed leg wraps, anklets, and shin guards by those of higher status.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM70-1)</sup> Modern Argonians also utilize rubber. Teeba-Bassu players of the swampy region of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) were known to employ a hard rubber balls wrapped in death hopper hide, providing them a perfect grip even in the muddiest of conditions.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TBB-5)</sup> In [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), some of the rubber products were made of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) [sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md). Among the items made with the use of the Hist sap rubber were telescoping [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md) prods. They were properly insulated to protect against the electrical discharges of the wamasu.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Wamasu-6)</sup>

[Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) and Argonians alike were known to use rubber to craft toys. In [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), realistic rubber squeeze mouse toys made from cooked gray rubber were designed to amuse and startle with their lifelike squeaking.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RSM-2)</sup> Argonians were known to craft [frog](Lore_Frog.md) -shaped squeaky toys, known as the squeaky tsonashap. They were made of soft rubber, and were popular among their hatchlings.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Frog-7)</sup> Rubber was also used for other forms of amusement. Rubber-lined mammoth snout snorkels, perfect for diving, were available and sold by Argonian merchants.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Mammoth-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DTM-9)</sup>

Rubber was also utilized by the denizens of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). Residents of the [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s realm have found an unconventional use for rubber, utilizing it in the creation of thermal conductive mesh, which is then encased in cloth and repurposed as a flexible blanket.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TCM-10)</sup> Clockwork's inventiveness doesn't stop there. Nasal kerchiefs made from remarkably soft rubber squares were favored by some of its residents, despite its haphazard appearance and impractical nature.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-INK-11)</sup> They also utilized blood billows, made of rubber bladders. They were manually compressed to aid in the circulation of a patient's blood, ideally by a skilled [factotum](Lore_Factotum.md) or someone with a keen sense of rhythm.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-BB-12)</sup> In the city, thin rubber pouches were employed to store small meals and delicate research samples. They were airtight and suitable for vacuum sealing, aiding in preservation.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-PBA-13)</sup>

Marsh Waders were durable rubber overalls specially designed to effectively shield against both bog water and any potential inhabitants found within them. They were not uncommon in [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MW-14)</sup> Blood rugs were stain-proof and backed with rubber. They were a common sight in Tamriel.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-BR-15)</sup> Some festive blankets in Western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) were known for vibrant patterned linen on one side, complemented by a waterproof rubber backing on the other, serving as a protective barrier to keep clothing dry during snowy picnics.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FSB-3)</sup>

Rubber even found its way into the realm of thievery, with heavy clay water jugs featuring well-designed rubber seals. Clever thieves often repurposed these seals for clandestine climbing endeavors.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-SSWJ-16)</sup>
