[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-npc-Sirgar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sirgar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirgar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sirgar.jpg) Sirgar **Sirgar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) living in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md). He is the son of [Sorli the Builder](Skyrim_Sorli_the_Builder.md) and [Pactur](Skyrim_Pactur.md), the maintainers of [Rockwallow Mine](Skyrim_Rockwallow_Mine.md).

He sleeps in [Sorli's house](Skyrim_Sorli%27s_House.md) every night between 1am and 9am. At 10am he starts carrying lumber back and forth between the [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) and the porch and keeps going until 2pm. The rest of the afternoon and the entire evening is spent inside the house, relaxing, cooking, eating, or talking to his parents.

He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5) and has an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) equipped. He carries a copy of the [key to Sorli's House](Skyrim_Key_to_Sorli%27s_House.md) and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When you approach him:

- *"Guess the Empire's going to need plenty of iron what with all the fighting. That'll be good for us, I suppose."*
- *"Talk to my parents if you need something."*
- *"My parents keep a close eye on the miners. They can't be trusted too much."*
- *"I hear there's been fighting in Skyrim. Rebellion, maybe. Wonder if our iron is being used for weapons."*

If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), Sorli will be appointed jarl and will move to [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), along with Pactur. Sirgar will then find himself in charge of the mine, saying:

- *"I'm in charge now that my parents are in Morthal."*
- *"I figured that I would run the mine someday. I just didn't figure that would mean now."*
