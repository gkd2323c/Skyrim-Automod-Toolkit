[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-item-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg/200px-SR-item-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg) Staff of Hasedoki The Staff of Hasedoki is an artifact created by the great mage [Hasedoki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hasedoki), who had no equal during his time. His soul powers the staff, and provides powerful defensive capabilities. It can be found in the possession of [Ivara of Olenveld](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ivara_of_Olenveld), whom you will encounter during the related quest.

The staff does no direct damage, but instead relies on the fall damage caused by its powerful knockback effect.

Wielding the staff also provides a constant [Ward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ward) effect, which manifests as a slight shimmering of the air in front of you. This effect continues to apply even if the staff's enchantment charges are drained.

## Notes
- The Staff of Hasedoki [previously appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Staff_of_Hasedoki) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*. For more information, see the [lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Hasedoki).
- The constant Ward effect makes this staff a good defensive weapon against magic users.

## Bugs
- The staff is perfect for avoiding large bounties. While the first strike on civilians counts as assault, the large fine for murder will not occur if you kill a person with the staff's knockback effect. Guards will completely ignore you if they were not already hostile when you target them with this staff.
