Valmir's Orders *Orders to retrieve Rahgot's Dragon Priest Mask*
---
You will proceed to the ruins of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) to retrieve the [Mask](Skyrim_Rahgot_(item).md) from the [Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Dragon_Cult.md) there.

If you are discovered, impersonate an officer. It is unlikely that anyone from [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) will be clever enough to see through the disguise.

Once you have obtained the Mask, bring it to [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md).
