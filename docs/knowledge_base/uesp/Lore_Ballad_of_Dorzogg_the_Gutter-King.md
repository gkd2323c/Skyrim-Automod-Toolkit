Ballad of Dorzogg the Gutter-King **by [Shei-Beekus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Shei-Beekus) the Would-Be Bard** *A song about a champion of the Dragonstar Arena*
---
He never owned a castle,

Yet he always wore a crown,

And he loved the [skeevers](Lore_Skeever.md) and the cutpurses all the same,

He never lorded over but he always lorded true,

With a dozen trusty knights by his side,

They entered [the Arena](Lore_Dragonstar_Arena.md) and won against all odds,

And the legend of Dorzogg the Gutter-King spread far and wide.
