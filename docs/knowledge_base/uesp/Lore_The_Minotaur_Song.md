The Minotaur Song *An ode to the Minotaur*
---
Oh [minotaur](Lore_Minotaur.md), oh minotaur,
A beast of rage and ignoble glaring.
Oh minotaur, oh minotaur,
None can deny your noble bearing.

Around [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) ruins you gather,
Eternal guardians with hooves and horn.
What memories lead you to path there,
Is it [Belharza](Lore_Belharza.md) or the [Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) that you mourn?

Oh minotaur, oh minotaur,
Are you monster, [Mer](Lore_Mer.md), or [Man](Lore_Man.md)?
Oh minotaur, oh minotaur,
Tell us how you fit into the [Divine](Lore_Divines.md)'s plan!
