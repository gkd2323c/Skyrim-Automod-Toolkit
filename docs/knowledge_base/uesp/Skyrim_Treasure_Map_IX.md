## Explicit Instructions
- Travel to [Broken Helm Hollow](Skyrim_Broken_Helm_Hollow.md).
- At the entrance, move down to where the waterfall is crashing on the rocks.
- The chest should be there under the waterfall along the cliff. - Be careful not to get swept off, as this can be fatal to players with low hit points.
- If you follow the stream down, you can find a [Corundum Ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md) vein and a [Nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) plant.
