[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-npc-Leifur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Leifur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leifur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leifur.jpg) Leifur **Leifur** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [lumberjack](Skyrim_Lumberjack.md) working for [Aeri](Skyrim_Aeri.md) at [Anga's Mill](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill.md).

He shares the [Anga's Mill Common House](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.md) with fellow lumberjack [Kodrir](Skyrim_Kodrir.md) and sleeps there every night between midnight and 6am. After a one-hour breakfast, he heads outside and starts working; At 7am, he carries wood back and forth between the [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) and the common house. At 9am, he will work the mill and at 11am, he will once again carry firewood. At 1pm, he will return to working the mill, until he carries firewood for the last time at 3pm. At 5pm he will work the mill for two more hours until 7pm, when he enjoys a one-hour dinner in the Common House, where he also spends the rest of the evening.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1da) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.md) to the common house as well as a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
Leifur seems to be content with his simple life at the mill, and he will speak about his work if spoken to.

- *"Aeri's the boss. Should talk to her if you want work."*

- *"I work at the mill. Chop trees, carry logs, saw lumber, you know."*

## Conversations
He will occasionally engage in conversation with the grumpy Kodrir, at times regarding former co-worker [Ennodius Papius](Skyrim_Ennodius_Papius.md):

- **Leifur**: *"Whatever happened to Ennodius? He just up and left."*
- **Kodrir**: *"Don't know why you're asking me. But I'm glad that damn Imperial is gone. Something wrong about him."*
- **Leifur**: *"You're all heart, Kodrir. I bet Lady Mara is weeping a special tear for you right now."*

- **Kodrir**: *"I swear that woman Aeri doesn't appreciate all the work I do for her damn mill."*
- **Leifur**: *"Oh, I've heard this one before. Watch out Divines, poor Kodrir curses you all for giving him life!"*
- **Kodrir**: *"Watch your mouth with me you pup! Who do you think you are?"*
- **Leifur**: *"The man doing your job?"*
