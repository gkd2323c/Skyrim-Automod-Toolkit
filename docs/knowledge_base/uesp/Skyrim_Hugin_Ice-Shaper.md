[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hugin_Ice-Shaper.jpg) Hugin Ice-Shaper Hugin Ice-Shaper is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) found west of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md), in the ash wastes just north of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md). He was the Master Artisan responsible for creating [Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic) -style jewelry using [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md), but he was recently killed and robbed by [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md). You will come across his murderers standing over his body during the related quest.

He is wearing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md), and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md). His body is surrounded by several other items, including his [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a satchel, and [*Hugin Ice-Shaper's Notebook*](Skyrim_Hugin_Ice-Shaper%27s_Notebook.md). You will be required to read the notebook during the related quest, which will teach you how to craft Nordic jewelry for yourself. The nearby satchel contains valuable ore and stones, including the components necessary to craft a piece of Nordic jewelry and complete the quest.
