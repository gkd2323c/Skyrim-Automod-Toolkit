# Bezoar

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Minotaur_Bezoar.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Minotaur_Bezoar.png) Minotaur Bezoars A Bezoar stone is a hardened mass formed in animal stomachs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DJ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SAB-2)</sup> Bezoars find use in a variety of settings, including alchemy, medicine, craftwork and rituals.

Bezoars extracted from the stomachs of Albino [Guars](Lore_Guar.md) are known to have ritualistic uses, with one notable instance being a ritual performed by the [Drothmeri Army](Lore_Drothmeri_Army.md) of [Frathen Drothan](Lore_Frathen_Drothan.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DJ-1)</sup> A pair of enchanted bezoars was used to dispel the enchantment protecting the passage to [Varsa Baalim](Lore_Varsa_Baalim.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EB-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-UMR-4)</sup> Bezoars also find use in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md). Trichobezoar Extracts are potions renowned for their ability to remedy poison-related ailments and provide immunity to poisons.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TE-5)</sup>

[Minotaur](Lore_Minotaur.md) bezoars found use in the craftsmanship of the [Dreadhorn Clan](Lore_Dreadhorn_Clan.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MB-7)</sup>

[Alits](Lore_Alit.md) were also known for their bezoars, although little is known about their properties other than the fact that they were described as shimmering.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SAB-2)</sup>
