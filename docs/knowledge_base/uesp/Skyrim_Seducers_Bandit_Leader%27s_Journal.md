Seducers Bandit Leader's Journal **by [Seducers Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Seducers_Bandit_Leader.md)** *The journal of a Seducers bandit group's leader*
---
Dark work, this, but does it ever pay well. Once every few days, a merchant comes galloping through, defenseless as a babe. We [Seducers](Skyrim_Seducers_Bandit.md) always do our good deed, and lighten their load before we let them pass. It's the least we can do.

Moving north was the best thing we ever did. Leave [Ri'saad](Skyrim_Ri%27saad.md) to the milk drinkers of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) or as they call themselves, the [Saints](Skyrim_Saints_Bandit.md). They're probably still hounding him on the road to [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

[Svarig](Skyrim_Svarig.md) is behind on his visit. I wonder if his path from the west was more treacherous than usual.

We need a change in our fortunes. If we don't get paid soon, I fear the others might not stay much longer. Hopefully this new lead pans out.
