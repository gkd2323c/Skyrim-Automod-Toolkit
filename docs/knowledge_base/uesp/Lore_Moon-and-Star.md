# Moon-and-Star

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/MW-item-Moon-and-Star.jpg/200px-MW-item-Moon-and-Star.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Moon-and-Star.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Moon-and-Star.jpg) Moon-and-Star Moon-and-Star, or by its full name the great **Ring of the Ancestors**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue N-1)</sup> **One-Clan-Under-Moon-and-Star**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NMS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> is a Dwarven ring of the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) hero [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md). The artifact was forged by one of the smiths of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) Sorcerer-priest [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md) and blessed by the [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) Goddess [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). The ring lent Nerevar supernatural powers of persuasion and indisputable proof of identity, since any other who tried to wear it would be killed instantly.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ma SGD-4)</sup> The Moon-and-Star helped Nerevar unite the warring Chimer clans, and form the [First Council](Lore_First_Council.md) which included the Dwemer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue N-1)</sup>

Moon-and-Star played a role in the [Nerevarine Prophecies](Lore_Nerevarine_Prophecy.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NMS-2)</sup> and allowed the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), the reincarnation of Nerevar, to again unite the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) and [Ashlander clans](Lore_Ashlanders.md), this time in the battle against the forces of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur_(god).md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MW-5)</sup>
