[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-npc-Bouncer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bouncer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bouncer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bouncer.jpg) The Bouncer The **Bouncer** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) guarding [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md). He must be killed to complete [Supply and Demand](Skyrim_Supply_and_Demand.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Supply and Demand](Skyrim_Supply_and_Demand.md)**: Clear [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md) for the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

## Dialogue
Upon seeing you, he will draw his weapon and warn you away, saying:

- *"These fights are invite only. And you're not invited. Slag off."*

He will attack if you approach too closely.

## Notes
- Before version 1.9 of the [Official Skyrim patch](Skyrim_Patch.md#Version_1.9) the bouncer was simply named 'Bandit'.
- Another bouncer can be found during a random encounter finding a dead body near two dead pit wolves. A [small note](Skyrim_Small_Note.md) can be found on this dead bouncer which leads to [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md).
