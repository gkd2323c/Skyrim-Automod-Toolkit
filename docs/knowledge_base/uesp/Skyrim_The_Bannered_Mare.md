[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-place-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg) The Bannered Mare **The Bannered Mare** is an [inn](Skyrim_Inns.md) located in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)'s market area in the Plains District.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare.jpg) The Bannered Mare interior The inn has two stories split into multiple rooms. On the first floor there is the common room, a small bedroom, and the kitchen. The second floor contains three bedrooms split across two areas. Sitting in any seat in the inn will cause [Hulda](Skyrim_Hulda.md) to call [Saadia](Skyrim_Saadia.md) to serve you, and she will then seek you out and offer to sell [food and drinks](Skyrim_Food.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is by the stairs leading up to the Gildergreen tree. There are stone steps leading up to the main entrance, where there is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) carved into the wall to the right of the entrance door. There is a [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) next to the rear entrance that takes you directly into the kitchen. Once you have completed the special [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest: [Imitation Amnesty](Skyrim_Imitation_Amnesty.md), there is a Thieves' Cache barrel behind the inn, which is marked with the [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Cache shadowmark carved into the side of it.

### The Bannered Mare
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_03.jpg) The common room The common room centers around a fire pit in the middle of the room, next to which are two benches and a chair. [Mikael](Skyrim_Mikael.md) stands next to the chair when performing, and any of the town's residents may dance while he performs. [Sinmir](Skyrim_Sinmir.md) will stay in this area, alternating between sitting, standing, and occasionally dancing. On either side of the front door are small tables with three chairs. There is another table and two chairs against the south wall and another against the north wall. Just past the entrance to the kitchen area are two more chairs. In the southeast corner are two chairs, where [Uthgerd](Skyrim_Uthgerd_the_Unbroken.md) sits. On this table is a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Maven.md) from Maven to Christophe. Everything on the tables around the common room is displayed merchandise for sale that can be bought or stolen, except the [plates](Skyrim_Plate.md), [goblets](Skyrim_Goblet.md), [bowls](Skyrim_Bowl.md) and a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md).

To the south of the fire is the bar with two stools with [Hulda](Skyrim_Hulda.md) leaning on the counter. Behind the bar there are two cupboards holding a [potion of minor stamina](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Stamina.md) *, a [mammoth tusk](Skyrim_Mammoth_Tusk.md), a sample of [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) *, and a [leather strip](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md) as well as more items for sale that can be stolen or bought. There are a couple of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels against the west wall with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing leveled items between them, while against the east wall is a narrow table holding an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and more [Displayed Merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise). Hanging from a beam behind the counter are two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Everything on the counter is displayed merchandise, except for a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). Under the counter is a copy of *[Fall from Glory](Skyrim_Fall_from_Glory.md)*.

The eastern main floor bedroom consists of a single owned bed in the southeast corner, with an end table beside it holding a plate, bowl and goblet. Opposite the doorway, against the east wall is a small cupboard containing clutter, with a low table and chair in the northeast corner and a wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) against the west wall.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_02.jpg) Bannered Mare's kitchen The kitchen consists of a fire pit with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) over it against the west wall. A second entrance is to the right of it. A table and two chairs are in the northwest corner, with wall shelves holding plates and other clutter above it. Against the south wall is a set of shelves holding more displayed merchandise, with several food sacks on top. In the southeast corner is a food barrel with a cupboard to the left against the east wall holding more items for sale and the unique [Argonian Ale](Skyrim_Argonian_Ale.md), which is required for a [quest](Skyrim_Argonian_Ale_Extraction.md) received from [Brenuin](Skyrim_Brenuin.md). To the left of the cupboard is a table. On the table is the business ledger, an apprentice-locked [strongbox](Skyrim_Strongbox.md) containing random items and various items displayed for sale. Rising from the northeast corner along the east wall is a flight of stairs that lead to the top floor, with several barrels and sacks of foodstuffs underneath them.

At the top of the stairs there is an landing with a small wardrobe against the west wall. To the north is a wooden door into [Saadia's](Skyrim_Saadia.md) room. It consists of an owned single bed in the northeast corner, with an end table and washbasin beside it. At the foot of the bed against the north wall is a wardrobe, with an unlocked chest containing leveled items on top of it. To the left of the door as you enter is a small square table and two chairs, with a small coin purse on the table. The southern bedroom is Hulda's. It consists of a single owned bed in the southeast corner, with a copy of *[Thief of Virtue](Skyrim_Thief_of_Virtue.md)* under the bed. To the right is a chair and an end table. At the foot of the bed against the south wall is a narrow table, with a [lute](Skyrim_Lute.md) and a couple of [cups](Skyrim_Cup.md) on top. Against the east wall is a dresser with a [drum](Skyrim_Drum.md) and some pottery on top. If you [chop wood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) for Hulda, her bed becomes usable along with the rentable one.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Bannered_Mare_04.jpg) Room for rent In the northeastern corner of the common room there is a flight of stairs, with crates and sacks under it, leading up to the rentable room in the establishment. At the top of the stairs is a small landing with a cupboard in front of you and the door to a bedroom on your right. There is a large double bed against the west wall, with an end table to the right and a doorway to a balcony overlooking the common room to the left. A second end table is to the left of the doorway to the balcony and opposite against the east wall is a dresser, with a washbasin to the left. On the balcony is a cupboard against the south wall and a low table and two low chairs to the north.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom right of the entrance door. There is a mammoth tusk, two unlocked chests, a strongbox, and many food items in the store. The store also contains the unique [Argonian Ale](Skyrim_Argonian_Ale.md), found on the kitchen cupboard.
- Selling [firewood](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) to Hulda makes low value items freely available, including use of the two beds (Hulda's and rentable) which will then grant the [well rested](Skyrim_Well_Rested.md) bonus.
- The items marked with "*" become displayed merchandise, once you have the Merchant speech perk.
- All containers in the area, with the exception of some barrels and food sacks, are non-respawning.
- The game has quite a difficult time determining who the inn's backup innkeeper is supposed to be, as this was seemingly changed numerous times throughout development: - Saadia is a potential member of the Jobb Innkeeper faction, which means she can become a full member if the actual innkeeper dies. She also holds that same potential status in the Job Rent Room faction, and in the unused quest [Supply Line](Skyrim_Supply_Line.md), the backup innkeeper dialogue was assigned to her. Saadia was likely skipped for this backup role due to possible outcomes of her quest [In My Time Of Need](Skyrim_In_My_Time_Of_Need.md).
- [Olfina Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Olfina_Gray-Mane.md) is not a member of any of the relevant factions like Saadia, but her class is set to "Food vendor", like the average innkeeper, and she actually has the required scripts attached to rent out beds at the Bannered Mare. Also, both her boyfriend [Jon Battle-Born](Skyrim_Jon_Battle-Born.md), and owner of the [Drunken Huntsman](Skyrim_The_Drunken_Huntsman.md), [Elrindir](Skyrim_Elrindir.md), will refer to Olfina as the new innkeeper of the Bannered Mare if Hulda is dead. This was likely changed after Olfina was made possible Stormcloak housecarl.
- Mikael has the dialogue for the rumors Hulda usually gives out, something neither Saadia nor Olfina have. Besides this, he is also a potential member of the three required factions, just like Saadia was. However, unlike Olfina, there is no dialogue for other NPCs that mention Mikael running the inn, neither does he possess the required script to rent out beds. Despite this, Mikael seems to be the most likely candidate to take over in the final game.
- Ysolda has dialogue implying she wants to buy the inn from Hulda, while the latter also has dialogue implying she wants to sell to Ysolda. In the `Dialogue Whiterun` quest that contains aliases for important people in town, Ysolda is marked as the "Innkeeper Backup", but also as the "Pawnshop Owner Backup". Ysolda also starts out a spart of the Belethor's Goods service faction. While it seems she was more likely intended to take over Belethor's store, she lacks the finished scripting to take over either store.
- During [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), the building will have a barricade across the main entrance and both doors will be expert-locked. However, if you pick the lock, both Hulda and Saadia will have their normal speech options, welcoming you and are willing to trade. - Installing the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on and adopting [Lucia](Skyrim_Lucia.md) before the battle has begun will cause her to be transferred here until the battle is over. This happens because her editor location is here and CWCitizens Flee will pick her up in one of the aliases.
