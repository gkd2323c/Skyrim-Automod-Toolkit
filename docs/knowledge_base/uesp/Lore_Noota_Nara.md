[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/RG-place-Draggin_Tale_03.jpg/200px-RG-place-Draggin_Tale_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Draggin_Tale_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-place-Draggin_Tale_03.jpg) Head of the dragon that attacked Noota Nara *See the head, over there? It troubled my village of Noota Nara back home. Burned its walls to the ground. My sires roasted their spear-tips in the dragonfire -- that enchantment will pierce the dragon like no other, you know. Strike true in that way, and you'll send the Emperor's pet to quick eternity.*
—Dreekius<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dreekius-1)</sup> Noota Nara is a village in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), and the original home of the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) Dreekius. At some point prior to [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), it was attacked by a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) who burned the walls to the ground. By heating their spear-tips in the dragonfire, the Argonian villagers were able to harness the fire's enchantment and pierce the dragon. The dragon's head was later brought to the island of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) by Dreekius, and mounted above a mantel in the Draggin Tale Inn in [Port Hunding](Lore_Port_Hunding.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dreekius-1)</sup>

Information of the dragon's defeat at Noota Nara was given to [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) by Dreekius at the Draggin Tail Inn. Cyrus used this knowledge to aid in the killing of [Nafaalilargus](Lore_Nafaalilargus.md) in 2E 864.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>
