Krahjotdaan is a high [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Cold Maw Doom" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

Krahjotdaan was slain by the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) in [1E 2871](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2871), in the southern [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The dragon's name was supposedly confirmed to the Dragonguard by its own account.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOD-1)</sup> A fang was taken from its corpse and inscribed to depict their defeat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Their death was later documented by the Dragonguard in [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOD-1)</sup> Due to the nature of dragons, Krahjotdaan may not be truly dead and therefore could rise again.
