[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-creature-Nystrom.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Nystrom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nystrom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Nystrom.jpg) Nystrom **Nystrom** is the skeleton of an adventurer found in the [Twilight Sepulcher](Skyrim_Twilight_Sepulcher.md) who tried to brave the [Pilgrim's Path](Skyrim_Darkness_Returns.md) and failed. His attempt can be read about in [his journal](Skyrim_Nystrom%27s_Journal.md). Nystrom eventually met his end by the hands of his friend [Anders](Skyrim_Anders.md), as can be read in his [note](Skyrim_Anders%27s_Message.md).
