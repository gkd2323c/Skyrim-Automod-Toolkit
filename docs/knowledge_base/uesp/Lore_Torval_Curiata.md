# Torval Curiata

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/ON-npc-Grand_Inquisitor_Arsalan.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Grand_Inquisitor_Arsalan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Grand_Inquisitor_Arsalan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Grand_Inquisitor_Arsalan.jpg) Grand Inquisitor Arsalan [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/ON-npc-Inquisitor_Sabannar.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Inquisitor_Sabannar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Inquisitor_Sabannar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Inquisitor_Sabannar.jpg) A Torval Curiata Inquisitor The Torval Curiata, or simply **The Curiata**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LTAK-1)</sup> is the militant arm of those that uphold [Riddle'Thar](Lore_Riddle%27Thar.md)'s teachings and faiths across the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSabannar-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKrin.27ze-3)</sup> such as the [Temples of Two-Moons Dance](Lore_Temple_of_Two-Moons_Dance.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MZT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONInisashu-jo-5)</sup> The Curiata uphold the epiphany against those they deem heretics, like worshippers of [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and organizations like the [Twilight Cantors](Lore_Twilight_Cantors.md), who revere [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) improperly. They considered themselves weapons of faith.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSabannar-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKrin.27ze-3)</sup> Enforcers in their ranks are known as Inquisitors, while their leader is known as the Grand Inquisitor.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONCuriata-6)</sup>

These inquisitors are not known for their subtlety.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKrin.27ze-3)</sup> The Inquisition of the Torval Curiata is a summons in front of the Grand Inquisitor and a council of lunar clergy about a particular subject.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SOTI-7)</sup> Their methods of extracting information is said to "make your tail hairs bristle."<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONUzernji-8)</sup> The Curiata are presumingly based in [Torval](Lore_Torval.md), where the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) resides and where other groups like the [Bi-Lunar Guard](Lore_Bi-Lunar_Guard.md) are also found.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MBAC-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1-10)</sup> They hold the authority to relocate members of the lunar clergy, provided a formal petition is made,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LTAK-1)</sup> organize trials,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SOTI-7)</sup> as well to oversee temples.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Adara.27hai ESO-11)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when the *[Spirits of Amun-dro](Lore_Spirits_of_Amun-dro.md)*, writings on pre- [ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) myth by the eponymous [priest](Lore_Amun-dro.md) became widespread and caught the attention of Khajiit across [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md), Thava-ko of the Curiata and her fellow priests wrote a three-volume epistle refuting and denouncing it. Several of their problems with the text include its account on [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-EOTSOA1-12)</sup> both its inclusion of daedra like [Hermorah](Lore_Hermorah.md) and [Mafala](Lore_Mafala.md) and omittance of [Mara](Lore_Mara.md) and [S'rendarr](Lore_S%27rendarr.md),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-EOTSOA2-13)</sup> and its obsession of paths and laws that the Curiata deem as slavish obsession to Azurah, deference and respect to the daedra, and gateways into Lorkhaj's corruption.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-EOTSOA3-14)</sup> In the same year, the case of Zerith-var of the [Order of the Hidden Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_Hidden_Moon.md) drew the attention of the Inquisition, which marked him for death,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-MFGIA-15)</sup> but failed in their attempt to assassinate him.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ZV-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-IT-17)</sup> This eventually resulted in the death of Grand Inquisitor Arsalan,<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-IO-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-GIA-19)</sup> as Zerith-var was in the midst of saving Elsweyr from the threat of the Rajaska.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ZV-16)</sup>
