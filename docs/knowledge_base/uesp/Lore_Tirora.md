Sage Tirora was the sage of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She lived in [Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md) in the town of [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md). She was both a mage<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ST-1)</sup> and an author. She was responsible for compiling *An Accounting of Enemies*, a reference to threats commonly faced by Companions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Tirora was killed in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) stormed Fallowstone Hall. The surviving Companions led by [Vigrod Wraithbane](Lore_Vigrod_Wraithbane.md) burned her ashes to summon her vengeful spirit, allowing her to aid them in retaking the hall.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VW-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-STH-4)</sup> She was succeeded by Sage [Svari](Lore_Svari.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SS-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TROSS-6)</sup>

It was believed that her soul reached [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VW-3)</sup> However, in death Tirora's [soul](Lore_Souls.md) was claimed by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), and she was transformed into a [Soul Shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md) to spend eternity as a slave in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). She was then imprisoned in a cell at [the Great Shackle](Lore_The_Great_Shackle.md). Tirora was later freed by [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) to help destroy the Great Shackle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ST-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> With Molag Bal's defeat at the hands of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), her soul was freed from servitude and released back into the [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), allowing her to move on.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
