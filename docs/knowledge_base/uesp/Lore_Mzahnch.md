[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/MW-place-Mzahnch.jpg/200px-MW-place-Mzahnch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Mzahnch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Mzahnch.jpg) Mzahnch in 3E 427 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/ON-place-Mzahnch.jpg/200px-ON-place-Mzahnch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mzahnch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Mzahnch.jpg) Mzahnch in 2E 582 Mzahnch is a [Dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) located in the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) region of the isle of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), east of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> It was abandoned in [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700) when its original [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) inhabitants mysteriously disappeared.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DII-3)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the ruins of Mzahnch were allegedly used as a hideout by skooma dealers. Constable Mirel of [Suran](Lore_Suran.md) went missing and was ultimately killed during the investigation of the ruins. His corpse was found looted. The fatal wound was caused by the dagger of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) design.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dredyni_Imayn-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Constable_Mirel-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSJ-6)</sup>

As of [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) its interior was guarded by the [Dwarven Centurions](Lore_Dwarven_Centurion.md). [Kresh Fiber](Lore_Kresh_Fiber.md), [Gold Kanet](Lore_Gold_Kanet.md) and [Black Lichen](Lore_Black_Lichen.md) could be found in the ruin and on the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Dwarven author and tonal architect [Bthuand Mzahnch](Lore_Bthuand_Mzahnch.md) shares his surname with the name of this ruin.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BD-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TEOT-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-YB-9)</sup> His first name was also a name of another Dwemeri complex, [Bthuand](Lore_Bthuand.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Bthuand Morrowind-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Bthuand ESO-11)</sup>
