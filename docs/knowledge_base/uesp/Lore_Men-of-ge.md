# Men-of-ge

Men-of-ge were an ancient a tribe of [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people. They lived in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) and early [First](Lore_First_Era.md) eras and were among the men herded as slaves from across the [Niben](Lore_Niben.md) by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md). Many of them were eventually "destroyed" when the Flower King [Nilichi](Lore_Nilichi.md) made a great sacrifice to a long-forgotten [insect god](Lore_Insect_God.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TA-1)</sup>

At least some of the Men-of-ge were still active during [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). Garid, who belonged to the tribe of Men-of-ge was mentioned in [the Song of Pelinal](Lore_The_Song_of_Pelinal.md). He once witnessed [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) succumbing to madness and later asked him how it felt like. Pelinal responded that it felt "like when the [dream](Lore_Dream_Magic.md) no longer needs its dreamer."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOP-2)</sup>
