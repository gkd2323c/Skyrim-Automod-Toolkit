Rikke was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGCard-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Creature)</sup> legate of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). The Legate followed in the footsteps of her parents, who were also Legionnaires. During the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), she fought side-by-side with [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) and many others who would later become [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md). Though she came to believe Ulfric was a self-serving ego-maniac, she once counted him and many of his followers as friends. She held a great deal of anger toward Ulfric, as she believed the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) was the best chance at victory against the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md), but also deeply saddened by his decisions. She continued to worship [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) privately despite the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup>

During the Stormcloak Rebellion, she was the chief lieutenant of General [Tullius](Lore_Tullius.md), who ran the Empire's campaign. She was his closest adviser and strategist, and often gave him insights on Nordic culture that he, a [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) -born [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md), did not know or fully appreciate. They had disagreements, but she believed Tullius was the best hope for [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and the Empire. Rikke had a great many duties, including local recruiting in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), though she frequently found herself fighting, coordinating scouts, and manipulating enemy intelligence on the front lines.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup>
