[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg/200px-SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost.jpg) [Septimus Signus'](Lore_Septimus_Signus.md) outpost in the Ice Fields The Ice Fields (also called the **Winterhold Glaciers**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:142</sup> are a region of glacial fields found along the coast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The glaciers are found on the mainland around [Alftand](Lore_Alftand.md), [Saarthal](Lore_Saarthal.md), and Frostflow Lighthouse surrounded mostly by the [Winterhold Mountains](Lore_Winterhold_Mountains.md), with other glaciers found around [the capital](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) going east. The ice fields continue in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) through a series of arctic permafrost-covered glacial islands and icebergs along the [Northern Coast](Lore_Northern_Coast.md), some meeting the [Broken Cape](Lore_Broken_Cape.md). Glaciers on the mainland allow access to the sea through steep glacial canyons. Many adventurers perish in the glacial fields that surround the city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup>

## Geography
The edges of Winterhold Hold are less rocky and more glacial and feature a variety of lonely and lost barrows, including one said to be the tomb of the legendary warrior and founder of the [Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md), [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md). Half buried in the snow that blasts up the glacial valleys is a Shrine to [Talos](Lore_Talos.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> The "active, moving" glaciers are able to preserve many things, including ancient [mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> and entire ruins like Alftand.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tattoos-2)</sup>
