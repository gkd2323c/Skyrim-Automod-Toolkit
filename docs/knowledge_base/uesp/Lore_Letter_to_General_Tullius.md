Letter to General Tullius *A letter from the Emperor requesting the Legion to reclaim the Lord's Mail*
---
15th of Last Seed, [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201)

[General Tullius](Lore_General_Tullius.md),

During these times of war, I strongly believe it is important to inspire our population through a show of military strength and pageantry. To the younger among us, nothing is as inspiring as the sight of an [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) wearing the finest armors our [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) has ever set its hands to.

It is for this reason I am formally requesting that several [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) relics be placed back into the service of the Empire, and distributed among high ranking officials. We must do all we can to inspire awe, and help galvanize the image of our cause among the populace.

It has come to my attention that a paticular *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* artifact, the Armor of [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md), Gift of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), also known as The [Lord's Mail](Lore_Lord%27s_Mail.md), has recently gone missing. It is believed a group of [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) mercenaries have stolen it with the aim to sell to the highest bidder. They were last seen traveling south of [Fort Greymoor](Lore_Fort_Greymoor.md).

Find these mercenaries, and reclaim The Lord's Mail. Wear it proudly. Show the people that Kynareth favors the realm of the Empire.

[Emperor Titus Mede II](Lore_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II.md)
