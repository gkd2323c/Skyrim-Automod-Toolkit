[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Nimriel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nimriel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nimriel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nimriel.jpg) Nimriel at Pelagia Farm **Nimriel** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) who works and lives at [Pelagia Farm](Skyrim_Pelagia_Farm.md), which is owned by [Severio Pelagia](Skyrim_Severio_Pelagia.md). Her co-worker [Gloth](Skyrim_Gloth.md) also lives there. She takes over Pelagia Farm when Severio dies during the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md).

She sleeps from midnight to 8am, then wakes up to tend the farm's crops. She works until 8pm, when she heads inside the farmhouse and spends the rest of her day eating and relaxing.

Nimriel wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Pelagius_Farmhouse) to the farmhouse, as well as [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

When talking to him, Severio may mention Nimriel: *"I own the Pelagia Farm, just outside the city walls. Nimriel takes good care of things for me."*

## Notes
- Her relationship rank with her boss Severio is 4, indicating they are lovers, but neither party ever makes reference to this.
