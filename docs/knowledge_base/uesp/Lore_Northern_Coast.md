[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Northern Coast is a large ragged and rugged stretch of land and ice sheets situated primarily within the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), making up the frigid snow-covered coastline with the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:55</sup> It is considered to be a landmark of [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), as the Hold takes up a bigger portion of the blizzard-filled wastelands than other regions around its Sea Shore Foothills area. The Coast is contiguous across three provinces, including five holds within Skyrim and also extends into the Icy Shore of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and the Great Valley of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The frigid waters of the Northern Coast are tough to survive near,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:253</sup> and its treacherous nature causes many shipwrecks, necessitating the use of lighthouses.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:357</sup> Hardy flora such [snowberries](Lore_Snowberries.md) thrives in this area.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSo G-2)</sup>

## Geography
The westernmost part of the Northern Coast is the **Icy Shore** within the boundaries of High Rock, encompassing the [Wrothgarian](Lore_Wrothgar.md) cities of [Farrun](Lore_Fharun.md) and [Jehanna](Lore_Jehanna.md). Any coastline beyond that point borders either the [Great Bay](Lore_Great_Bay.md) or the [Azurian Sea](Lore_Azurian_Sea.md). To the east of the Icy Shore are the glaciers of Rkindaleft and mountainous areas north of the [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md), where the mysterious [Blackbone Isle](Lore_Blackbone_Isle.md) can be found off the coast.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CN-3)</sup>

West of the Forgotten Vale is the **Giant's Coast** of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), a rather empty tundra with a number of grottos, giant camps, and a few towers, such as Northwatch Keep.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Harrowstorm Names-4)</sup> Islands off the Giant's Coast include [Castle Volkihar](Lore_Castle_Volkihar.md), a mysterious keep dreaded by sailors of the region and home to the [Volkihar Clan](Lore_Volkihar.md), and [Kyne's Aegis](Lore_Kyne%27s_Aegis.md), a bastion that includes the town of Kyne's Perch. Land found further towards the Solitude Inlet gradually becomes more lush, featuring landmarks like the [Wolf's Eye Lighthouse](Lore_Wolf%27s_Eye_Lighthouse.md) on the cusp of the coast,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:357</sup> before meeting the [Great Arch](Lore_Great_Arch.md) and going further inland along the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) and ending at the [Karth River Forest](Lore_Karth_River_Forest.md).

The ice-encrusted shore east of the Great Arch never thaws, earning it the name of the **Frozen Coast**. The coastline extends into the territory of the Pale, [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md)'s neighbors to the east.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LS-5)</sup> Only a small part of it is found in northeastern Hjaalmarch Hold, being made up of the frozen Mouths of the Karth in the [Drajkmyr Marsh](Lore_Drajkmyr_Marsh.md) and the inland portions of the [Hailstone Valley](Lore_Hailstone_Valley.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Harrowstorm Names-4)</sup> The rest of it is made up of the entire northern portion of the Pale around its Sea Shore Foothills area.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:473</sup> The Coast is considered to end at the floating chunks of ice in the [Winterhold Glaciers](Lore_Winterhold_Glaciers.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:142</sup>

Past the [Ice Fields](Lore_Ice_Fields.md) and [Anthor Range](Lore_Mount_Anthor.md) is [Hsaarik Head](Lore_Hsaarik_Head.md), a prominent landmark of Skyrim seen as where the Northern Coast resumes, being the westernmost area of the **[Broken Cape](Lore_Broken_Cape.md)**. This land was once much bigger before the [Great Collapse](Lore_Great_Collapse.md) caused large portions of it to be swallowed by the sea. Numerous islands, such as [Japhet's Folly](Lore_Japhet%27s_Folly.md) and [Serpentstone Isle](Lore_Serpentstone_Isle.md) can be found off the coast.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Galmar-6)</sup>

The last area of the Northern Coast within Skyrim is **[Giant's Run](Lore_Giant%27s_Run.md)**, which includes the mouth of the [White River](Lore_White_River.md). Fulstrom Homestead is found on the eastern side, and the islands of [Bleakrock Isle](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) are found nearby.

The Northern Coast continues past the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) and into the Great Valley of Morrowind, near [Blacklight](Lore_Blacklight.md). It finally ends at the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md), across from the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Akaviri-7)</sup>

## History
The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) were the first Men to "return" to Tamriel, colonizing the northern coast of Tamriel before recorded history, led by the legendary [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAA-8)</sup>

The [Sea Giants](Lore_Sea_Giant.md) raid the northern coast when "the Pale grows frigid as a draugr's heart, horkers freeze to death on the rocks, and trolls weep from the bitter cold." A winter such as this was encountered in [2E 532](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_532), and in a frosty gale on the coast, one of their longships gliding through the snow and sleet.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-LTO-9)</sup>

[Torradan ap Dugal](Lore_Torradan_ap_Dugal.md) was born in the town of [Dunbarrow](Lore_Dunbarrow.md) on the northern coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the third century of the Third Era.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
