[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-creature-Japhet.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Japhet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Japhet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Japhet.jpg) Japhet's remains **Japhet** is a [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) whose remains can be found in the basement of [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md). Long ago, Japhet was the son of a farmer who set out with a small company of men from [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) to create a fortress on a small island near [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), an island that would come to be called Japhet's Folly. According to *[Japhet's Journal](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Journal.md)*, his followers left him one by one, but he refused to give up, and instead resorted to eating ice moss when farming proved impossible. He eventually died, convinced that the local rumors about ghosts had been right all along.
