[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Llewellyn_the_Nightingale.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Llewellyn_the_Nightingale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Llewellyn_the_Nightingale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Llewellyn_the_Nightingale.jpg) Llewellyn the Nightingale **Llewellyn the Nightingale** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) who can be hired to stay in [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md).

Llewellyn wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209A6) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209A5). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
After being hired, he will introduce himself:

- *"Allow me to introduce myself. I am the bard Llewellyn, known as "The Nightingale". Your steward has arranged for me to take service with you."*

When rehired if you have dismissed him he will say:

- *"Ah, excellent! We'll just call this a misunderstanding between friends. I've already forgotten it, myself."*
