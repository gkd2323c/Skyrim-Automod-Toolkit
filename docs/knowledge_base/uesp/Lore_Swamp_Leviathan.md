*Suddenly, it was as if the swamp itself dragged her down and swallowed her up. She disappeared without making a sound and never resurfaced. Matius could just barely make out the shape of something huge moving through the water. He noticed that even the insects had gone quiet.*
— *[Lost Tales of the Famed Explorer: Fragment III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lost_Tales_of_the_Famed_Explorer__Fragment_III)* Swamp Leviathans (or simply **leviathans**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Exp-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KLH-2)</sup> are massive [reptilian](Lore_Reptile.md) creatures of terrible power found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AAB4-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KLH-2)</sup> They are described as a mountain of spines, with nine-foot-long claws and a jaw that juts out, exposing their tusks,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AAB4-3)</sup> they additionally possess a large sail on their back.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Their hunting strategy has been known to involve trailing their prey for long periods of time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Exp-1)</sup> The [rats](Lore_Rat.md) of Black Marsh are known to prey upon them, overwhelming them by the hundreds and eating the massive creatures inside out.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AAB4-3)</sup>

Swamp leviathans were sometimes slain by the [Nagas](Lore_Naga.md) of the [Dead-Water Tribe](Lore_Dead-Water_Tribe.md) as a rite of passage.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-JO-5)</sup> The Argonian [Xit-Xaht Tribe](Lore_Xit-Xaht.md) of [Mazzatun](Lore_Mazzatun.md) used scrimshawed leviathan tusks as protective material in their armor.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) historically created [bugles](Lore_Instruments.md#Horn) from the horns of leviathans.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KLH-2)</sup> Skeletal remains believed to have belonged to swamp leviathans could be found in [Deepmire](Lore_Deepmire.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-JO-5)</sup>
