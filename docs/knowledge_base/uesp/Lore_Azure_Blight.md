# Azure Blight

*The blue got in them and made them sick. made them mad. They attacked us! We all ran. All ran. It's just me now. I need to get home. The Green will protect me. Protect me from the blue.*
— *[Maarselok Reconnaissance Log Four](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Maarselok_Reconnaissance_Log_Four)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/ON-creature-Azureblight_Cancroid.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Azureblight_Cancroid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Azureblight_Cancroid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Azureblight_Cancroid.jpg) The Azureblight Cancroid, a source of Azure Blight corruption The Azure Blight (or simply **Azureblight**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azureblight-1)</sup> was an [arcane](Lore_Magic.md) disease which appeared in the [Tenmar jungle](Lore_Tenmar.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Unleashed by the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Maarselok](Lore_Maarselok.md), who had awoken from his resting place deep beneath the mountains of eastern [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md) with the release of the great dragon [Kaalgrontiid](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his dragon followers from the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md). The Azure Blight was a corruptive plague created by the dragon's [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), it rapidly spread through the jungle, corrupting the life within and bringing it under the dragon's thrall, and threatened all of Grahtwood, and even the [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) tree, [Green-Sap](Lore_Towers.md), and the [Green](Lore_The_Green.md) itself.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo MESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Allanwen-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup> Maarselok's ultimate goal in unleashing the blight was to take control of the [Changeling](Lore_Changeling.md) high priestess of [Shagrath](Lore_Shagrath.md), [Selene](Lore_Selene.md), and combine her power with his own, which he believed would give him the ability to shape the world to his liking.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup>

Spread by being borne through the air by swarms of infected insects,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Oo TV-6)</sup> the disease was characterized by the bright azure glow which covered the forms of the infected and the stench of decay which accompanied it. The Azure Blight infected all forms of life, flora and fauna, and caused them to rot while simultaneously placing them under its maker's thrall. In addition it would corrupt [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md) and reanimate the dead to serve Maarselok, and even take on living forms, walking masses of disease that would fight intruders or masses that oozed corruption to help spread the blight further.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skeletal-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Cancroid-8)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-creature-Maarselok_04.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Maarselok_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Maarselok_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Maarselok_04.jpg) Maarselok, creator of the Azure Blight In 2E 582, ancient Dragons had appeared in Northern [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), reaping the land, following the release of the great dragon Kaalgrontiid and his dragon followers from the Halls of Colossus.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOTHOC-9)</sup>. The azure dragon Maarselok was awoken by the release of his brethren and took roost in a secluded mountain within the Tenmar Jungle. When Maarselok arrived in his new home, his [words](Lore_Thu%27um.md) boomed throughout the valley and the surrounding wilds started to take on an unnatural aspect. This was the beginning of the [arcane](Lore_Magic.md) disease known as the Azure Blight, which rapidly spread through the jungle.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo MESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Allanwen-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup> During his stay on the mountain, Maarselok would occasionally venture out to look for something, the Changeling Selene whose power he sought to combine with his own, believing it'd give him the ability to shape the world to his liking.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MRL2-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MRL3-11)</sup>

Evident by the bright azure glow which it caused to appear wherever it had taken root and the strong stench of decay which accompanied it, the Azure Blight caused infected life, whether flora or fauna, to rot, and turned its victims into the dragon's thralls.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Allanwen-3)</sup> Even nature spirits and the dead weren't spared, as the disease took control of the local [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Spriggan-12)</sup> and reanimated the corpses of the dead in the region into [undead](Lore_Undeath.md) servants of its creator.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Skeletal-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Oo TV-6)</sup> The disease would also take on forms to help propagate itself further, such as wooden [Lurcher](Lore_Lurcher.md) constructs and living masses of the blight that would ooze more corruption or fight off invaders.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Cancroid-8)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Lurcher-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

Borne through the air by swarms of infected insects,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Oo TV-6)</sup> the Azure Blight soon came to threaten Elden Root and the Green themselves.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Allanwen-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo MESO-2)</sup> Alarmed by the disease spreading through the land, King [Aeradan Camoran](Lore_Aeradan_Camoran.md) sent a [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) army to slay the dragon, however, unprepared for the nature of what they were about to face, the force was quickly overcome. Creatures infected with the Azure Blight rushed out of the corrupted jungle and drove the Bosmer force into the infected area before setting up an enirclement to prevent them from leaving. Surrounded on all sides by the Azure Blight, the Bosmer soon fell to the infection and became the dragon's thralls themselves.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo MESO-2)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Gadnalon-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-MRL4-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-NNN-17)</sup>

Fearful of losing more of his soldiers, King Camoran sent two emissaries, Allanwen and Gadnalon, to the region to treat with the Changeling Selene, and enlist her aid in stopping Maarselok and protecting [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) from his poison. The pair also enlisted the aid of a group of adventurers to deal with the situation. The group soon encountered Selene, who had been imprisoned and soul bound by the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) warlock Carindon. Carindon was convinced to loosen his hold so the group could treat with Selene, forcing them to subdue her again after she took on a different form and attacked. The party braved the infested lair of Maarselok, did combat with his blighted thralls and living masses of disease, the Azureblight Infestors, and destroyed the Azureblight Cancroid, a corrupted mass formed by the blight which oozed and spread the disease further, the heart of the mountain's corruption.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo MESO-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Cancroid-8)</sup>

The group repelled the dragon multiple times during the ascent and, aided by Selene, whose purifying glyphs cleansed the dragon's corruption and helped repel the dragon, eventually confronted Maarselok on the mountain's summit. Maarselok was a formidable foe, and used his blight to take control of Selene and infest the adventurers themselves using seeds of Azureblight. However Selene managed to struggle against his hold and cleanse the corruption with her own powers, and eventually Maarselok fell.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Maarselok-18)</sup>

Carindon, whose actions had violated an accord between the Mages Guild and Selene, was captured to await disciplinary action by guild Arch-Mage [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) himself. Selene escaped and would go on to work to cleanse the Azure Blight, which had persisted past the dragon's death, from the land.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Allanwen-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AZQ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup>

## Notes
- Maarselok's Azureblight has some similarities to the [Ash Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md), both being arcane diseases linked to [Aedric](Lore_Aedra.md) forces that rot and take control of those they infect and place them under the mental control of their creator. Those blighted by Dagoth Ur were in mental contact with him, whereas the Azure Blight infected, living or dead, were described as becoming "one with Maarselok" by Selene.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Dreamer-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Yakum H-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Dagoth-21)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Selene-5)</sup>
