*My task is to know and love the people of Bravil as if they were my own children. If you have ever been a parent, you know what a challenge that is.*
—Olava the Fair<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTF-1)</sup> Olava the Fair was a [Living Saint](Lore_Living_Saints.md) of [Mara](Lore_Mara.md) towards the close of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Olava worked tirelessly at the Great Chapel of Mara in the impoverished city of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), a task she often found daunting.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTF-1)</sup> Olava was brutally slaughtered alongside her fellow chapel residents in an attack by a group of [Aurorans](Lore_Auroran.md) sent by [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) to desecrate the chapel as part of his war against the [Nine Divines](Lore_Nine_Divines.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Knights-2)</sup>
