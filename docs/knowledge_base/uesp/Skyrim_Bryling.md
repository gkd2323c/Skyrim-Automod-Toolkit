[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-npc-Bryling.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bryling.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bryling.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bryling.jpg) Thane Bryling **Bryling** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), a thane of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), and the owner of [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine). She has a [house](Skyrim_Bryling%27s_House.md) in the city but spends most of her day in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md). Very rarely, she can be found browsing the marketplace in Solitude. She is evidently having a secret tryst with [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md), the steward of Solitude.

Bryling wears an upper-class outfit consisting of a [fur-trimmed cloak](Skyrim_Clothes.md#Fur-Trimmed_Cloak), [fine armguards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Armguards), [pleated shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pleated_Shoes), and a [copper and moonstone circlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Copper_and_Moonstone_Circlet). She wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and carries her house [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bryling%27s_House) and a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

She can be found in the Blue Palace for most of the day, from 8am to 8pm; eating lunch at 1pm and going to sleep at midnight.

## Related Quests
- **[Payment Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery)**: Tell Thane Bryling that the late shipment from [Rockwallow Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockwallow_Mine) will be arriving soon.

## Dialogue
Bryling may greet you by saying one of the following:

*"Thane Bryling, at your service."* *"I can always find time to share a word with a warrior."* Despite supporting the Empire, she respects the determination of the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md).

*"Many condemn the Stormcloaks, but I refuse. There is honor in fighting for what you believe."* She also comments on the war.

*"This godsforsaken war is dividing our people and destroying our land."* When closing conversation, she may use one of the following lines:

*"It's been an honor."* *"I'll get back to court business, then."*
## Quest-Related Events
During the quest [Payment Delivery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Payment_Delivery), you can tell Bryling that you have a message from [Pactur](Skyrim_Pactur.md). She expresses concern, and upon hearing that they're fine, she thanks you for delivering the message.

**Pactur asked me to deliver a message.** *"Is everything all right? I've not heard from [Sorli](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorli_the_Builder) or Pactur in some time."* **Everything is fine. The shipment will be here soon.** *"That's good news. I was starting to fear something had happened.
You have my thanks."*

## Notes
- Bryling is a potential member of the `Job Jarl Faction`, yet will never at any point become a jarl. She's also a member of the `Favor252Quest Giver Faction`, which is tied to the quest [Thane of Haafingar](Skyrim_Thane_of_Haafingar.md). Since the only other person in this faction is [Jarl Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md), it can be reasoned that Bryling was once meant to replace Elisif. The thane quest for other holds is also set up to work with both an Imperial-aligned and a Stormcloak-aligned jarl. It's unknown under which circumstances this was meant to happen. - The scenes Bryling has with Irnskar, or at the very least the scene concerning the status of the war, seems to have been written with Bryling as jarl in mind, as she explicitly refers to the citizens of Haafingar as "her subjects".
- The outfit Bryling wears is known as `Jarl Clothes Bryling`, which fits the naming scheme of the outfits of all other initially incumbent jarls. Notably, the only jarl that starts out *without* this distinction is jarl [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). Dialogue attached to Sorli the Builder in the final game was written before Idgrod's, and might have once belonged to Bryling serving as incumbent jarl for Morthal, as both women share the same voice type.
- A scene Bryling has with Irnskar implies that she owns the building in which Radiant Raiment is located, and that the sisters pay her rent. However, a scene that Erikur has with his sister [Gisli](Skyrim_Gisli.md) implies that *he* owns the building and the sisters pay *him* rent instead.
