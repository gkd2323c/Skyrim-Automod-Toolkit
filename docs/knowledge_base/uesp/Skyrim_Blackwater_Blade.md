[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-item-Blackwater_Blade.jpg/200px-SR-item-Blackwater_Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackwater_Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackwater_Blade.jpg) Blackwater Blade Blackwater Blade is a Cyrodiilic steel pirate artifact associated with the Blackwater Brigands. It is carried by [Celeste](Skyrim_Celeste.md), the leader of the Blackwater Brigands. Tempering the Blackwater Blade requires a [Steel Ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
