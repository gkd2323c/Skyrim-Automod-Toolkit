[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-npc-Mogrul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mogrul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogrul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogrul.jpg) Mogrul **Mogrul** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) found patrolling the exterior of [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md).

He is clad in a set of Dawnguard equipment. This includes the [cuirass](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md) as well as matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Boots.md). He is equipped with a [Dawnguard warhammer](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Warhammer.md) and a [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) supplemented with 20 [steel bolts](Skyrim_Steel_Bolt.md). He carries a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md) and a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

## Dialogue
Mogrul shares his dialogue with [Ollrod](Skyrim_Ollrod.md), [Tilde](Skyrim_Tilde.md), and [Vori](Skyrim_Vori.md). They talk about fighting the vampires.

- *"Got to keep alert. We don't want to get caught napping like the [Vigilants](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) did."*
- *"I don't think the vampires would be stupid enough to attack us here, but you never know."*
- *"I'm glad we're finally taking the fight to those monsters."*
- *"I hope the vampires try to attack us here. A lot easier than hunting them down in their holes."*
- *"[Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md) knows how to fight those monsters. He's been preparing for this day for a long time."*
- *"Now that the Dawnguard are back, the vampires' reign of terror is about to end."*
