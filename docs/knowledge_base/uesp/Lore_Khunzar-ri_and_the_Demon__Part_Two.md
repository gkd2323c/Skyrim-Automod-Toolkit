Khunzar-ri and the Demon: Part Two **An ancient Khajiiti myth as recorded by Moon-Singer Mizbina** *A Khajiiti legend detailing Khunzar-ri's conflict with dragons* *
---
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) were young and like kittens at play, the great hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) gathered a company of champions to combat the [demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his [brothers](Lore_Dragon.md). They had come to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) to steal the moons from the sky.

With [Nurarion](Lore_Nurarion.md) already at his side, Khunzar-ri turned his attention to the next champion he planned to recruit. "The power of the moons will be ours, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md)," taunted the demon. "How can two possibly hope to stop many?

Khunzar-ri said, "Because two shall become three, annoying demon. As soon as this one rescues [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md) from the Ayleid lair of creeping ebony plants."

These are the words, and the words are true.
