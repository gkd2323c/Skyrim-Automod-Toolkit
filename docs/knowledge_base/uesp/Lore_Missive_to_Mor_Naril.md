Missive to Mor Naril *A scrawled letter from during the Fellowship's assault on Gristmung Hold* *
---
[Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md),

Gristmung Hold is under attack. The Stirk Fellowship breached the gates and are advancing as I write this. We outnumber their forces, but they appear to be winning all the same. I know the wraithforge's energy is focused on completing the Planemeld, but any aid you can send our way would help ensure that the hold does not fall.

The echo fragment you sent with your research remains secure. The [knight](Lore_Darien_Gautier.md)'s stolen light carries a purpose beyond raw power. Something older, something I think you were correct to want to study. If the unthinkable occurs and the hold is lost, seek the grin that never fades. It rests among our experiments and research.

But know that we will endeavor not to make that necessary.
