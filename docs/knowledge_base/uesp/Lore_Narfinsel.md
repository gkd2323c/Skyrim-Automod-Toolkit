## History
In the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), an event known as the [Narfinsel Schism](Lore_Narfinsel_Schism.md) marked the beginning of a violent internal conflict among the Ayleid city-states of Cyrodiil. This conflict between the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) -worshipping [Barsaebics](Lore_Barsaebics.md) and the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping Daedraphiles reached a climax in [1E 198](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_198) at the Scouring of [Wendelbek](Lore_Wendelbek.md), which ended all organized opposition to Daedra worship.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> However, the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) began less than fifty years later, forcing most Ayleids to flee Cyrodiil or perish at the hands of the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md). Narfinsel subsequently fell into ruin.

Following the [Soulburst](Lore_Soulburst.md) of [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578), the ruins of Narfinsel became haunted by dangerous [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md). One such ghost was a guard captain named Captain Minwe who perished at the ruins. Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), her friend Lliae the Quick continually sent [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) soldiers to the ruins to put her spirit to rest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOGWND-2)</sup> Her efforts proved fruitless, as Minwe's ghost would always return after a time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The [Rock Bone Tribe](Lore_Goblin.md#Rock_Bone_Tribe) also attempted to take over the ruins and fought against the spirits of the Ayleid ghosts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESONARS-4)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins had been taken over by monsters from the wilderness.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
