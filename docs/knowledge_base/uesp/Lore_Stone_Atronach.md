Stone Atronachs are a type of [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) consisting of four legs and a central body with one eye, all made out of stone. Stone Atronachs can seemingly be summoned by manifesting from a variety of stone sources, including crumbling ruins, or it may be that some are actually [golems](Lore_Golem.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They can have unique personalities, with some enjoying playing near water.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

[Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) have enlisted stone atronachs into their workforce and use them for mining, excavating, and transport.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ANA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MMQS-4)</sup> They can be subject to cruelty to increase their efficiency, with overseers such as the Soulbinders Clan often scolding them, being constantly overworked to the point of exhaustion.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BGFScenes-5)</sup> Decommissioned atronachs may be used for torture practice.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-5TL-6)</sup> In the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), [Pelladil Direnni](Lore_Pelladil_Direnni.md) of [Clan Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) summoned an army of Stone Atronachs to build the [Blackrose](Lore_Blackrose.md) Prison.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Once-7)</sup>
