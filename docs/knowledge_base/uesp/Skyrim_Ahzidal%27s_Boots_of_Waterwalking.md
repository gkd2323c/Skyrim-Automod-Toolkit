[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-item-Ahzidal%27s_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ahzidal%27s_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ahzidal%27s_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ahzidal%27s_Boots.jpg) Ahzidal's Boots of Waterwalking Ahzidal's Boots of Waterwalking are a pair of boots that are part of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). They can be found on a plinth in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after the first phase of digging is completed during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). These boots are identical in appearance to [ancient Nord boots](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Boots.md), but provide four more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and weigh four points more. Their enchantment gives you the ability to [walk on water](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waterwalking).

In addition, if you wear these boots along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points. Ahzidal's boots can be tempered with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), with the [Advanced Armors perk](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors_(perk).md) doubling improvement.

## Related Quests
- **[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)**: Assist [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) in his search for the relics of [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md) in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

## Notes
- Though six relics of Ahzidal exist, you will only receive Ahzidal's Genius if exactly four relics are equipped. Equipping more will deactivate the effect. - Ahzidal's Genius can be increased to +12 if the Dragonborn has Necromage and is a Vampire. To do so requires transforming into Vampire Lord form and reverting after gaining Necromage. It appears to be reset by the transformation, but not by simply unequipping and re-equipping the relevant items. Ahzidal's Genius is unaffected by the Bloodworm Helm.
- It is unsure if this is tied to the waterwalking effect, or the boots, but when equipped jumping on water is 1.5-2 times higher than normal, and continuous use spells can be continued while jumping.
- When playing with the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) in [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), they allow you to cross arctic water without suffering the increase in [cold](Skyrim_Cold.md) penalties from immersion in it.
- Wearing these boots will not negate the damage from the water in [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md), nor the lava inside the Aetherium Forge.
- While wearing these boots, you can hand catch fish swimming near the surface and attack [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md) from above.
