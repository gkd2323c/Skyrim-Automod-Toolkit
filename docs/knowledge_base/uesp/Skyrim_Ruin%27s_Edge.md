[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-menu-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Ruin%27s_Edge.jpg) Ruin's Edge **Ruin's Edge** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds the eponymous [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge_(item).md) weapon, a [Dark Seducer](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md) bow. It was released during 2017 for 400 Creation Credits. The bow first [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Ruin%27s_Edge) in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)'s *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* [download](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads).

## Official Summary
*Wield this deadly dark bow, favored weapon of Syl, the Duchess of Dementia. Each arrow shot has the chance to cast a spell, like Paralyze, Silence, Demoralize, or Frenzy. (Weapon is located in Stony Creek Cave far southeast of Windhelm.)* (Note: The above mention of "Silence" refers to the [Drain Magicka](Skyrim_Drain_Magicka.md) effect, similar to the "[Silence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Silence)" effect the *Shivering Isles* version of the bow has.)

## Overview
Travel to [Stony Creek Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave) and recover [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge_(item).md) from the final boss of the dungeon.

## Notes
- Tempering the Ruin's Edge bow originally required both the [Daedric Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Smithing) and [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perks. This changed when the Creation received an update in February 2019, which made it so that only Arcane Blacksmith is needed.
- Like its appearance in *Shivering Isles*, the official summary refers to the fact that it is a dark bow, the [type of equipment](Skyrim_Dark.md) used by Dark Seducers. - Although it is a dark bow, in-game it doesn't have the same tempering requirements as the dark equipment introduced with the [Saints & Seducers Creation](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md).
