# Newt

| Newts | |
| --- | --- |
| Type | [Beast](Lore_Bestiary.md) (Amphibian) |
| Range | [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md) |

Newts are a species of scaley amphibians comparable to [salamanders](Lore_Salamander.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOPSal-1)</sup> They are the namesake of Newt Cave in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Newt Cave OB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Cave On-3)</sup>

**River-Newt** scale armor is used by [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) guards in [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-5)</sup> and this Imperial Newtscale armor has seen use throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Eye of newt is used in [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) brews created by [witches](Lore_Witches.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-10)</sup> [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md), the Nibenese [Imperial Battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md), once claimed he could turn people into newts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONAT-11)</sup>
