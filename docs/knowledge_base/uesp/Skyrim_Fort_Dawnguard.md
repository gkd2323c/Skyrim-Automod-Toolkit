[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Dawnguard.jpg) Fort Dawnguard **Fort Dawnguard** is a large [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) southeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) which serves as the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md)'s base of operations.

[†](#note_Crafting) The alchemy lab, arcane enchanter, and forge are not initially present in the fort, but added during restorations to the fort just before the Prophet quest ends. The fort is home to the ancient order of [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunters who guard the realm from the vampiric Volkihar clan. It is hidden in the east of [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), and can be used as a player home. It can only be reached via [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md).

The fort was built sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by the Jarl of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) to contain his son, who had contracted vampirism. He hired a large mercenary band called the Dawnguard to guard his son. The threat of vampires was eventually disregarded by the people of Skyrim, and the fort was abandoned. [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran) is using the fort as a base of operations for his attempt to rebuild the Dawnguard and combat the rising threat of the vampires.

## Notes
- Five members of the Dawnguard are available as [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) after the Dawnguard version of the quest [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md) is completed. They are noted with the [](Skyrim_Followers.md) icon in the NPC table above.
- The [All Forge](Skyrim_All_Forge.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> added by the [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) can be used to craft items usually restricted to Gunmar's forge.
- There are two war dogs in the dog pen in the northeast section of the fort, available as canine followers.
- You can buy armored [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md) for 500 gold from [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar).
- All 3 merchants here benefit from the [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md) perk. You can invest with [Florentius Baenius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius) as well.
- There is a grindstone and tanning rack on the roof, reachable by climbing the west stairway inside and exiting at the top. These are technically in Dayspring Canyon, not part of Fort Dawnguard, though they can only be accessed via the fort.
- If you use the snow on the east side of the mountain behind the highest balcony, you can potentially use it to walk along the ledge and drop down to the two lower balconies, and scale the mountain to get to the rest of Dayspring Canyon without backtracking through the fort. Don't drop down too far, though, or you'll instantly die once you land.
- You can access [Dead Drop Falls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Drop_Falls) through the southwest section of Fort Dawnguard. The entrance is between three combat mannequins and the armored husky paddock.
- If you become a vampire after siding with the Dawnguard in the Dawnguard questline, NPCs in the fort will refuse to speak to you until you [get yourself cured](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md).
- If you clip through the stone pillars in the enchanting area, you'll come across 3 merchant chests. Two of them are considered owned, but the third one isn't.
- If you have allied with the Dawnguard, [vampire scouts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Scout) will occasionally appear near the fast travel arrival point to the fort.
- If you have allied with the Volkihar vampire clan, all the bodies of named NPCs killed during the quest Destroying the Dawnguard will never disappear.
- A similar-looking magna-geode stone with the same shape and appearance as [Meridia's Beacon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Meridia%27s_Beacon) is found within Fort Dawnguard.
