He wears the [Fastened Shield of the Crusader](Skyrim_Fastened_Shield_of_the_Crusader.md), [Reforged Boots of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Boots_of_the_Crusader), [Reforged Cuirass of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Cuirass_of_the_Crusader), [Reforged Gauntlets of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Gauntlets_of_the_Crusader), and [Reforged Helm of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reforged_Helm_of_the_Crusader). He also carries the [Sword of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_the_Crusader).
