Bleakrock Watch Log, 12th First Seed *A Blackrock Isle guard logbook*
---
[![ON-concept-nord tower.png](https://images.uesp.net/7/72/ON-concept-nord_tower.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-nord_tower.png)
- *Sunrise*: Found Hrolrygg passed out at post (again). Nothing out of the ordinary, of course. No sign of trouble on the seas. Supply ship should show up today. Good thing; this [horker](Lore_Horker.md) jerky's getting old.

- *Mid-day*: Minor altercation in the village square. Everyone's getting restless. Seas still clear, as usual. Can't wait to get off this frozen rock.

- *Sunset*: Supply ship arrived late. Sent Hrolrygg to help unload. Looks like our mead shipment was cut down—that's going to be trouble. No reports of suspicious activity between here and mainland.

- *Midnight*: Damn [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md) raising a fuss tonight; doubled perimeter patrol. Cold.
