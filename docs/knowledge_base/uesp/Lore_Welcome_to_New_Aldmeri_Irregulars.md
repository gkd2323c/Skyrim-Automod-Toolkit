Welcome to New Aldmeri Irregulars **by [Aicantar of Shimmerene](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Aicantar_of_Shimmerene), Sapiarch of Indoctrination** *An introductory pamphlet for new Aldmeri Dominion combatants*

Welcome, [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md), [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md), and [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) warriors. Welcome to the elite force of the Aldmeri Irregulars!

[Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md), and [Men](Lore_Man.md) —most of all, Men—are best at war. They have drenched the mainland of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in rivers of blood. As long as their follies were confined to murdering one another, what they did in and around their so-called "[Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md)" was beneath the notice of the Altmer of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).

Then came the [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md). That catastrophe was entirely the fault of Men, but the Altmer had to repair it. Now the Men of the Empire have catastrophically blundered again, and all [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) is threatened. Our [good queen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Queen_Ayrenn) had no choice but to form the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) to conquer [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and ensure for the good of all who dwell in Nirn that Men never again tamper with forces beyond their comprehension and competence.

Only the Aldmeri—the High Elves and their noble allies, the Wood Elves and Cat-Men—have the wisdom and restraint to peaceably rule the disparate peoples of Tamriel. Though we are reluctant to take up this burden, events have shown that we must. Recent events prove that the Dragon Break was not a unique event. Men always follow the destructive path of their defender and apologist, the [Missing God](Lore_Lorkhan.md) whom we shall not name. This ends here. Once again, Elves shall rule Tamriel from [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) … this time, forever.

The world has gone wrong, and we must put it right. March proudly beneath the eagle banner of the Aldmeri Dominion!
