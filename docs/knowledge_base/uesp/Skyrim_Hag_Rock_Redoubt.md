## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The redoubt is a multi-tiered structure with multiple entrances and exits on different levels of the mountain. You must go through at least the exterior section in order to reach [Dead Crone Rock](Skyrim_Dead_Crone_Rock.md).

As you approach the site, there is a typical Nordic tower in front of you and a path leading past it to the south with tall arches high above. There is a Forsworn standing just outside an open doorway to the tower. Inside, stairs lead up and out the back to a wooden ramp winding up to the next level. Here there are another leveled Forsworn, an empty set of shelves, an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), an unowned bedroll, and some [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels. In front of the shelves is an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) (The Dagger may also be on the table next to the helmet and sword on the level above the shelves). There are wooden stairs leading up to a level with parts of the floor missing and a wooden table on the far side of a gap. On the table are a random [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md) and [sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). There are stairs up to the highest level, where there is a stone walkway to the start of a Forsworn encampment. There are many [juniper trees](Skyrim_Juniper_Berries.md) around the encampment.

Opposite towards the right is a Forsworn tent with a leveled magic-using Forsworn lying on a hay pile and hanging above a [garlic braid](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md). In front of the tent are a campfire and a wooden table with three random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) on top. Beside the tent to the left is a rack with a bunch of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), with a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) to the right. To the left of the walkway is a stone hut containing a leveled Forsworn, a set of shelves, an adept-locked chest, and an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). On the shelves are a random potion each of [healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), [magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), and [stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), and two [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Behind the stone hut are stairs leading up to more terraces with [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md) strung across the stairs, with each terrace guarded by several Forsworn. To the left of the hut is the main path seen previously with the high arches overhead with more Forsworn patrolling, and also the lowest entrance to the interior.

The next terrace has a Forsworn tent at the top of the stairs containing a hay pile, a food barrel, and a random potion of magicka, with a campfire in front and an empty pot hanging over it. To the right is a wooden block with a woodcutter's axe on top, and to the left is a rack with two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) hanging from it. There are also two tables, a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and two [tanning racks](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) in this area. One table has a [Forsworn bow](Skyrim_Forsworn_Bow.md), a quiver of [Forsworn arrows](Skyrim_Forsworn_Arrow.md), and a random [potion of true shot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_True_Shot) on top, while the other has a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md), a [deer hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md), and a random potion of stamina on top. To the right of the next set of stairs is a lean-to with an unowned bedroll under it and a campfire in front. Farther to the right is the lower of the alternative exits from the interior.

There is another flight of stairs up to the next terrace with a [tripwire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire) that triggers a [falling boulders](Skyrim_Falling_Boulders.md) trap at the bottom and a walkway over them. To the left of the stairs are two Forsworn tents, each containing a hay pile and the second also containing a table and chair, while to the right is another stone hut over a wooden spiral staircase leading up from the highest exit from the interior. One final flight of stairs leads up to the top terrace where a [Forsworn Briarheart](Skyrim_Forsworn_Briarheart.md) and a [monster](Skyrim_Monsters.md) are inside another Forsworn tent. The tent contains an unlocked chest. If the monster is a giant [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), it can get stuck in the tent, allowing for easy dispatch. From this terrace there is a path heading south to stairs leading up to the entrance to Dead Crone Rock.

### Hag Rock Redoubt
The easiest entry point will be the door on the lowest level of Hag Rock Redoubt labeled "Hag Rock Redoubt Ruin" (one of three such doors in the exterior area). This will normally be the first door you come to when approaching Hag Rock Redoubt. Once inside, your way up to the first level of the redoubt is lit by a brazier. Three Forsworn are found at the top of the incline, one patrolling and two dining at the table in the room to the right. Once you have the place to yourself, head to the dining table to retrieve the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Last Seed, v8](Skyrim_2920,_Last_Seed,_v8.md)* and two potions. Also find a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg). To the left of the table, behind the pillar, a sample of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) is on a crate, with a bucket beside it containing two samples of [hagraven feathers](Skyrim_Hagraven_Feathers.md) and an iron dagger. Due east from the table in a darkened alcove is an adept-locked chest.

To the north is a flight of wooden spiral stairs leading up to a jail area containing one leveled Forsworn guarding two adept-locked cells. The guard is sitting at the top of the stairs beside a cupboard holding an [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md), an iron dagger, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two bowls of [bone meal](Skyrim_Bone_Meal.md), a [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) flower, a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), a [burial urn](Skyrim_Burial_Urn.md), and a [Hag Rock Ruin Jail key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hag_Rock_Ruin_Jail_Key). The key will only open the cell directly across from the entrance on the west wall, which unfortunately yields nothing of interest. Beside the cupboard is a bucket containing two samples of [scaly pholiota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaly_Pholiota). The second cell is to the left of the entrance, to the south. Inside are the body of a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and a random potion of magicka. There is an [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) fungus growing among a cave-in to the left of this cell. Past the jail area is another passage heading north which takes a couple turns and ends at a wooden door.

Through the door is a Forsworn Briarheart's quarters. Most likely he will have his back to you working at an alchemy lab as you approach, allowing for an advanced sneak attack. On the stone table along with the lab are a random potion of healing, as well as a cast iron pot and a wooden bowl containing two samples of [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales) and three [slaughterfish eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Egg) between them. To the right of the stone table is a boss level chest, and beside that is a small round wooden table with a sample of [Jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md), a sample of [creep cluster](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md), and another slaughterfish egg. To the left of the alchemy lab are a food barrel with three sheaves of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) on top and a long wooden table with an iron dagger on top and an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md) and [battleaxe](Skyrim_Iron_Battleaxe.md) underneath. Also in the room are an unowned bedroll by the south wall, a wooden block against the north wall with a rabbit on top, and a cupboard against the east wall. On the shelves of the cupboard are a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a [Nordic barnacle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle), a bulb of garlic, a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and two random potions.

To the left of the cupboard is a wooden door leading to an iron door that leads back outside via the lower of the two exits. Almost directly across from the cupboard, facing west, is a hallway that splits into two passages. Straight ahead is a sideboard, on top of which is an apprentice- [trapped](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tension_Cable) chest that triggers a [spear trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spear_Trap) from the floor unless disarmed before opening. If you notice the arrangement of the spear receptacles on the floor, you will be able to avoid the trap by standing to the far right or left of the chest and flush against the sideboard. The cupboard shelf below the chest holds a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md), a bowl of bone meal, another sample of creep cluster, a [blue dartwing](Skyrim_Blue_Dartwing.md), and an open crate containing two pieces of [quicksilver ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore). The left-hand passage leads up stairs to an iron door that leads back outside via the higher of the two exits. Waiting outside the second exit are the Forsworn Briarheart and a monster, unless you previously dealt with all of the external Forsworn and the monster.
