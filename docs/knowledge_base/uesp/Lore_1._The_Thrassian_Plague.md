1. The Thrassian Plague *A plaque commemorating a series of events involving the All Flags Navy*
---
A terrible illness ravaged the continent of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) starting in the year [1E 2200](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2200). This deadly sickness, which came to be known as the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md), claimed more than half the population before it ran its course.

For a good portion of the early part of the century, the origin of the plague remained a mystery and the subject of much speculation. It first appeared along the southwestern shores of the continent, spreading outward from [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md), [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md), and [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md). Not even the islands of the [High Elves](Lore_High_Elves.md) were safe, and the city of [Corgrad](Lore_Corgrad.md) on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) was devastated by the disease.

The plague took a terrible toll that was measured in more than just lives lost. The political ramifications of the disease changed the direction of Tamriel. [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) was weakened, the population of [Illiac Bay](Lore_Illiac_Bay.md) was drastically reduced, and the tribes of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) decreased from sixteen to a mere two.

When it became evident that the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) were responsible for this unprovoked attack, steps were taken to deal with the threat once and for all.
