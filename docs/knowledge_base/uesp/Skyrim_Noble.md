[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Noble_Male.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Noble_Male.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Noble_Male.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Noble_Male.jpg) A traveling noble The male **Noble** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who is often seen on horseback accompanied by a lone [Imperial soldier](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md) and [wandering](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Nobles_Traveling_with_Bodyguard) across Skyrim's [holds](Skyrim_Holds.md).

His attire matches his status and consists of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8713) and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). He wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) of the best possible quality.

## Dialogue
He will refuse to speak to you and threatens to order his bodyguard to attack, stating:

*"I have nothing to say to you. Now move along, or I'll order my guards to attack."* Despite his threats, neither he nor his guard will become hostile unless attacked.

If his guard dies, he will still be snooty, although more humble:

*"Keep away. If I am harmed, people will come looking."*
## Notes
- This encounter originally involved more NPCs. The name of the encounter is "Nobles Traveling with Bodyguard", but there's only one noble in the final game. - The second [Noble](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Noble) exists in the files, with a base ID of `00102D63`, but goes unused.
- Looking at the scripting inside the quest, there are commented out leftovers for the female Noble, the horse she would've ridden, and a second bodyguard. This explains the noble in the final game referring to multiple "guards".
- While the unused female Noble has her voicetype set to "Female Condescending", there is no dialogue for her to be found in that voice. However, the two lines that the male Noble uses are also recorded in the **Female Commander** voicetype, but go completely unused and aren't even set up for the quest inside of the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit), they're only present in the voice files.
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* calls this encounter "The Nobles" and describes it as: *"Two noblemen are walking along the road escorted by two soldiers."*
