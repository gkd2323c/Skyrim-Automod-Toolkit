[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/AR-place-Vulnim_Gate.jpg/200px-AR-place-Vulnim_Gate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Vulnim_Gate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Vulnim_Gate.jpg) Vulnim Gate during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Vulnim Gate (historically called **Oulnim Gate**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-HRP-2)</sup> is a town found in west-central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), north of the city-state of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md).

The town of Oulnim Gate has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Vulnim Gate was an active settlement. It was ruled by Count Lhohte and had a rivalry with Lainebon Place.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Vulnim Gate is neighbored by several settlements, including [Riverview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverview) to the south, [Thorstad Place](Lore_Thorstad_Place.md) to the northeast, and [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn.md) to the northwest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup>
