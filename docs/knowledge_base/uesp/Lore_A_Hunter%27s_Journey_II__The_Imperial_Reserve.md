A Hunter's Journey II: The Imperial Reserve **by [Viola Fulcinius](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Viola_Fulcinius), Professional Hunter** *A hunter's recollections of the Imperial Reserve* *
---
I first discovered my love for hunting in my own backyard, so to speak. I grew up in the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md), and the grasslands of the [Imperial Reserve](Lore_Imperial_Reserve.md) were a mere half-day's walk from my father's house. From the time I was old enough to string a bow, I used to slip away to roam the Reserve and try my luck. [Deer](Lore_Deer.md), [antelope](Lore_Antelope.md), [pheasant](Lore_Pheasant.md), and the occasional [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) [boar](Lore_Boar.md) abounded in the finest hunting ground between [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).

Given the abundant game, I came to appreciate why the old emperors kept the place for themselves. My father told me that Emperor [Brazollus Dor](Lore_Brazollus_Dor.md) used to love hunting in these lands. In fact, he loved it a lot more than sitting on the Ruby Throne and trying to run the empire, so he told his potentate to deal with business in Cyrodiil while he built himself a villa near [Weatherleah](Lore_Weatherleah.md). His only official act was to declare a vast swath of the plains his own personal game reserve.

For a century or so, no one but the occasional poacher dared to take the Emperor's deer. But the [Potentates](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) who followed the [Reman Emperors](Lore_Reman_Dynasty.md) weren't interested in paying to guard herds they never personally hunted. The Imperial Reserve gradually became just a name on a map in the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md). Soon, the ordinary people of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md), including my father, and myself, quietly resumed hunting in the area. And why not?

I've hunted all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). I've tracked [mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), speared [nix-oxen](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and skinned [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md) in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). But I come back to the Imperial Reserve in Colovia at least once a year for the simple pleasure of a good, clean hunt in the land where I was born. Especially in the fall, when the air is crisp and the bucks are in the rut.

I'll see you on the hunt!
