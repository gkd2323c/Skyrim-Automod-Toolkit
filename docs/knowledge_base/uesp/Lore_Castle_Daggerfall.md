Castle Daggerfall (also called **Daggerfall Castle**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FE5-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONCastle-2)</sup> is the seat of power for the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and is located in the [capital city](Lore_Daggerfall.md). The castle sits at the city's highest point and overlooks the settlement and the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) from its unassailable position.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Castle-3)</sup>

## History
Before Castle Daggerfall was built, it was preceded by Daggerfall Overlook, an old Breton keep southeast of the city that sits above the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONOverlook-4)</sup> The castle appears in the historical fiction series, [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md) as the first scene, where a young prince [Edward](Lore_Edward.md) sits in the palace orchard and looks across the bay of Daggerfall. It was that moment when Edward first met King [Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md) and [I'ric Harad Egun](Lore_I%27ric_Harad_Egun.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KEI-5)</sup> It was occupied by King Corcyr I,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KEI-5)</sup> who is later referred to as Gerald in the story.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-KEIV-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KEVI-7)</sup> Back when the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md) was at its most powerful in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), [Raven Direnni](Lore_Raven_Direnni.md) was one of the first occupants of the castle.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AHOD-8)</sup>

In the early to mid [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Castle Daggerfall was occupied by [Deleyn Dynasty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Deleyn_Dynasty),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Castle-3)</sup> since its first monarch, King Donel Deleyn.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EGT-9)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a plot involving the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md) was uncovered, which culminated in an assassination attempt on King Casimir in the castle. The assassin, Verrik was defeated by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) while they guarded the king.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONAssassins-10)</sup> In that same year, the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) of [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md) stole one of the castle's ancient heirlooms, Raven Direnni's inflammable rug.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-KHL-11)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), King [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md) was given the [Totem of Tiber Septim](Lore_Totem_of_Tiber_Septim.md) from his friend, Lord [Woodborne](Lore_Woodborne.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) and he kept it in the castle's treasury. When the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) tried to take the totem from Woodborne's [home](Lore_Woodborne_Hall.md) but failed, they suspected it was located in Castle Daggerfall so Lady [Brisienna Magnessen](Lore_Brisienna_Magnessen.md) made contact with an [Agent](Lore_Agent.md) to infiltrate the castle and retrieve the totem. The Agent delved deep into Castle Daggerfall and successfully took the totem.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DFTotem-12)</sup> The fate of both the artifact and the Agent is currently unknown.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TWITW-13)</sup>
