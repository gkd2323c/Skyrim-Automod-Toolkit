[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-npc-Ghost_Adventurer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghost_Adventurer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_Adventurer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghost_Adventurer.jpg) A Ghost Adventurer drawing his bow The **Ghost Adventurer**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [archer](Skyrim_Bandit_Archer.md), is one of the [ghostly](Skyrim_Ghost.md) appearances outside of [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md). He is accompanied by several [Subjugated Ghosts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Subjugated_Ghost) and is forced to attack you against his will.

He wears randomly determined [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor) which includes a pair of boots and a 50% chance of a pair of gauntlets. His outfit may not be a matching set, as it can be a mixture of [fur](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), [leather](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), [scaled](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md), [hide](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), or [studded](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor) versions, depending on your level. He wields a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) and has a chance of carrying leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrow.md), as well as a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md). He also carries random [leveled loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md) and a small amount of gold.

Like the other ghosts forced to protect the Rannveig's Fast entrance, he will apologize for his attacks:

*"Stay back or I'll be forced to attack you!"*
*"Is someone there? Stay back! I don't want to attack you."*
*"Stay away!"*
*"I'm sorry! This isn't what I want!"*
*"I don't want to do this!"*
*"I don't have any choice! I'm sorry!"*
