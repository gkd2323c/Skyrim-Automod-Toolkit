Bones of the Forest *A short text on the god Y'ffre*
---
Penitent, be one with the moment. Feel the solidity of the ground under your feet, and taste the wind. By his will is our world manifest, and the rocks, and the trees, and the birds in the sky. [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) is with you at every turn, in every moment.

To complete your venerations here, intone: "In the Storyteller's name, I stand upon the bones of the world. I drink in the promise and power of nature's law, and breathe out my thanks."
