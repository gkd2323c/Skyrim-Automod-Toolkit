# Knights of the Moon

The Knights of the Moon were replaced by the [Order of the Candle](Lore_Order_of_the_Candle.md) as the protectors of the royal family of the [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md). They were officially led by Vhosek, but he was typically focused on court affairs more than on the order. Unofficially they were following charismatic sergeant Ulfahn. They were a relatively informal organization.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFGuide-1)</sup>
