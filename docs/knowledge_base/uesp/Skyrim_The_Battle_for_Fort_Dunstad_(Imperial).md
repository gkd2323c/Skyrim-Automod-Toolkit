## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from Legate Rikke at the Pale Imperial Camp.
2. Rendezvous with the legionnaires southeast of [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md).
3. Clear the fort of Stormcloak rebels.
4. Report the news to General Tullius.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Receiving the Quest
After completing A False Front, report for duty again to Legate Rikke at the Pale Imperial Camp.

Having learned that the Stormcloak-held Fort Dunstad is in need of reinforcements, she will command you to help to take the fort while it is vulnerable. She will instruct you to rendezvous with a task force of Imperial soldiers who are staging near the fort, southeast of your location. She will mark the meeting point on your map, and you will update your journal to note this objective.

### Join the Men Attacking the Fort
| A Route to the Rendezvous Point |

If you are following the paved road, you will pass two rocky areas to your left. There is an easily found iron ore vein in the second of these. <br> <br> You will soon reach another paved road. Turn left on this. After a moderate distance, the road will take you on a significant descent down the mountain. Keep your eye on the paving stones to avoid losing your way. [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-quest-The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_%28Imperial%2902.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_%28Imperial%2902.jpg)](File_SR-quest-The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial)02.jpg.md) [](File_SR-quest-The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial)02.jpg.md) A group of legionnaires preparing to converge on the fort As you approach the waiting soldiers, they will charge toward the fort. Your objective becomes to take it over from the rebels. As your team engages the Stormcloaks holding the fort, you will receive regular updates on the percentage of enemies remaining.

### Take Fort Dunstad
The entire battle will take place in the exterior areas of the fort.

If you have not yet developed an eye for distinguishing between Imperial friendlies and rebel enemies, the Stormcloaks are easily identified by their pointed helmets.

The battle will comprise multiple pairs and small clusters of engagements, with both sides using melee weapons and archery attacks. These engagements occur at multiple points in the two courtyard-like areas, and along the elevated wooden platforms around the semi-perimeter.

If you let them, your group will essentially take care of the battle for you. If you hang back and take the most passive approach to the fight, you may need only to restore your health once or twice when you are targeted. If you want to prioritize acquiring weapons and armor off of fallen soldiers, you should search them quickly because their bodies will disappear shortly after falling.

Often, there are actually some Stormcloaks remaining (or respawning) after the battle has been formally won, which you can engage and loot. Also, if you so wish, you may kill the remaining Imperials as well in order to loot them without getting a bounty.

When it's reported that all the rebels have been killed, you will have completed the objective of taking the fort and the quest itself.

### Report to General Tullius
Your objective then becomes to report to General Tullius at Castle Dour to receive your reward, but doing so technically occurs under the overarching [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md) quest. After you have taken the fort, The Battle for Fort Dunstad will clear from your journal. If you didn't flee the battle, Tullius will promote you to [Praefect](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md#Ranks) along with a leveled sword (see [Bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes
- If [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) is given to the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) then this quest will not be given to you.
- Completing this quest will exile [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md) and his courtiers to the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md), and install [Brina Merilis](Skyrim_Brina_Merilis.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of The Pale.
- After completion of this quest, like any Civil War quest under [Reunification of Skyrim](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md), unmarked by in-game references, you can approach Legate Rikke for a [medium leveled gold reward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Gold_Rewards); this is not always recommended, as entering her cell sets up the next quest in the series and, if triggered improperly, causes the missing *"Reporting for duty."* dialogue option (see [this](Skyrim_Reunification_of_Skyrim.md#Bugs) bug section). Return to Legate Rikke to receive the leveled gold reward only when you want to start the next quest in the series and skip the reward when the game doesn't prompt you to return to her. Don't approach Rikke between quests of different holds.

## Bugs
- After the quest, [Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial).md) becomes available and activates (even if it doesn't appear in your journal) upon your next load of the cell of [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) (usually relocated to [Rift Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Imperial_Camp)). If you do so prior to reporting to [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) (for example to get the leveled gold reward for this quest) the game activates [Compelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial).md) incorrectly causing a [bug](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial).md#Bugs) by generating and extra non-quest item copy of the [incriminating letter](Skyrim_Incriminating_Letter_(Anuriel).md). <sup>**?**</sup> - Avoid Rift Imperial Camp and Legate Rikke, go straight to report to General Tullius instead. Upon your next approach to Legate Rikke, [Complelling Tribute](Skyrim_Compelling_Tribute_(Imperial).md) will have set up correctly. This skips the leveled gold reward for this quest.
- Sometimes you can travel to the Pale Imperial Camp and there will be a quest marker in the camp itself, which does nothing on approaching it. <sup>**?**</sup> - This can sometimes be fixed by waiting for a few hours at that location. Eventually, Legate Rikke will arrive and the quest marker moves to her. You can then approach her and continue the quest.
- If you kill all the guards outside the fort before you are given this quest, and take the quest during the time it takes the guards to respawn, the quest will be bugged. Imperial soldiers won't appear near the fort, and the whole fort is going to be empty. On the quests page, you won't be able to press the button to show the quest location on the map and it will say you have failed to meet the Imperial soldiers and you have to take the fort yourself, with 100% remaining, except there are no soldiers in the fort. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) and type `setstage CWFort Siege Fort 10`. This should force the Stormcloaks and Imperials to spawn.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this you will have to complete the quest using the console command `completequest CWFort Siege Fort` and then getting the quest from Rikke.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can also advance the quest to completion with console command `setstage CWFort Siege Fort 9000` which advances the attack on the Fort to success and updates the objective to report to Tullius.
- This can also occur even if you have never been to the fort. Imperial soldiers won't appear near the fort, the whole fort is empty, and on the quests page you can't press the button to show the quest location on the map.
- Sometimes Stormcloaks will get stuck in place. They will not always be able to attack you, and you can not attack them either. They are essentially unkillable. <sup>**?**</sup> - There is a work around where you must open the console and use the kill command on them.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
