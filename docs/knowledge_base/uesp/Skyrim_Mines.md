# Mines

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Embershard_Mine.jpg) Embershard Mine [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Mine.png) This page lists all the **mines** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). Mines generally contain several veins of [ore](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md#Ores). Ore veins in uncleared mines, including unclearable mines such as Gloombound Mine, respawn in 10 in-game days, while ore veins in cleared mines respawn in 30 in-game days. For details on how to mine ore and lists of where various ores can be found, see [Mining](Skyrim_Mining.md).
