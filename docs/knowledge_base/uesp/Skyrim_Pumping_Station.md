[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-place-Pumping_Station.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pumping_Station.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pumping_Station.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pumping_Station.jpg) Pumping Station **Pumping Station** is a small [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) occupied by [Falmer servants](Skyrim_Falmer_Servant.md). It is part of the Dwemer city in the western part of Blackreach. There are no usable entrances to this building directly from the grounds of the city, as the main entrance has been blocked by a cave-in both inside and out; entry must therefore be made through either the [Silent City Catacombs](Skyrim_Silent_City_Catacombs.md) or the [Debate Hall](Skyrim_Debate_Hall.md).

## Pumping Station
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-interior-Pumping_Station.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Pumping_Station.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pumping_Station.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Pumping_Station.jpg) The interior, viewed from the door to the Debate Hall Upon entering from the [Silent City Catacombs](Skyrim_Silent_City_Catacombs.md), you emerge in the western part of the zone, at the bottom of a flight of stairs heading up to the east. At the top of the stairs is a closed gate to the south. A [Falmer servant](Skyrim_Falmer_Servant.md) may come to investigate any noise. Through the gate, you enter a large, partially demolished room containing a second Falmer servant. To the south, a cave-in blocks the exit to the grounds of the city. A flight of stairs in the northeast corner of the room leads up to a raised walkway that runs along the east side of the room, with doors to the north and south. There is a matching raised walkway along the west side of the room, above where you enter, but no stairs lead up to the western walkway; the only easy access to that walkway is via the exterior balcony to the north.

Through the door to the north of the eastern walkway is a small exterior semicircular balcony that leads to the door opening to the western walkway. At the southern end of the western walkway is a closed metal gate opening to an alcove containing an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), with a [Dwemer cog](Skyrim_Dwemer_Cog.md) leaning up against it. The southern door on the eastern walkway exits to an exterior bridge leading to the [Debate Hall](Skyrim_Debate_Hall.md).
