[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-East_Empire_Warden.jpg/200px-SR-npc-East_Empire_Warden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Warden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-East_Empire_Warden.jpg) East Empire Warden The **East Empire Wardens** take over the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) during [Scoundrel's Folly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scoundrel%27s_Folly). The wardens turn hostile if they spot you.

Each warden wears [hide bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers), [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots), and an open-faced [steel helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Helmet). They carry a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), two leveled [health potions](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a [small amount](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists) of gold.
