[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Lieutenant_Salvarus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lieutenant_Salvarus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lieutenant_Salvarus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lieutenant_Salvarus.jpg) Lieutenant Salvarus **Lieutenant Salvarus** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and a ranking [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md) member onboard the Emperor's personal ship [The Katariah](Skyrim_The_Katariah.md). He carries a [Katariah Master Key](Skyrim_Katariah_Master_Key.md) that will be useful during the related quest.

As well as the aforementioned key, he carries a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md), a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), some [food and drink](Skyrim_Food.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). Salvarus wields an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and is armored in a full set of [Penitus Oculatus gear](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Armor.md).
