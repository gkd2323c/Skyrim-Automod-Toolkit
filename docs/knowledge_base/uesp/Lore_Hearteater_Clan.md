# Hearteater Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/ON-npc-Nelmir_the_Hearteater.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Nelmir_the_Hearteater.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nelmir_the_Hearteater.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Nelmir_the_Hearteater.jpg) Nelmir the Hearteater ca. [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-texture-Hearteater Banner_01.png/200px-ON-texture-Hearteater Banner_01.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Hearteater Banner_01.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Hearteater Banner_01.png) Banner of Hearteater Clan Hearteater Clan, also known as **Hearteaters** was a [clan](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They were a force that raided northern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) and razed and pillaged a settlement known as the Highlander Camp.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HC-1)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Hearteater Clan ventured to Cyrodiil and razed a village north to [Fort Aleswell](Lore_Aleswell.md) in Applewatch Wood. In the same year, the place was known as Highlander Camp.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HC-1)</sup> The group was led by the necromancer known as Nelmir the Hearteater, who has occupied a large building on the western side of the razed village. They performed a number of sacrifices (including the slaughter of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md)) for reasons unknown.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NTH-2)</sup> The settlement they razed was previously inhabited by the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) population, some of whom they took as captives or killed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Nord_Captive-3)</sup> [Necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) and [shamans](Lore_Reachman_Shamans.md) were part of the invasion forces of the Hearteaters.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HW-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HN-5)</sup>
