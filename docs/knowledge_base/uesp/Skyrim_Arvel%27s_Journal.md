Arvel's Journal **by [Arvel the Swift](Skyrim_Arvel_the_Swift.md)** *A journal describing how Arvel came into possession of the Golden Claw and a hint of its true purpose*
---
My fingers are trembling. [The Golden Claw](Skyrim_The_Golden_Claw.md) is finally in my hands, and with it, the power of the ancient [Nordic](Skyrim_Nord.md) heroes. That fool [Lucan Valerius](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md) had no idea that his favorite store decoration was actually the key to [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(place).md).

Now I just need to get to the Hall of Stories and unlock the door. The legend says there is a test that the Nords put in place to keep the unworthy away, but that "When you have the golden claw, the solution is in the palm of your hands."
