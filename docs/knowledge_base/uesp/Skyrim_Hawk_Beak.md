## Ingredients
1 guaranteed sample is found in the following location:

- [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
[Hawks](Skyrim_Hawk.md) can be seen flying in the sky or found near their nests. When shot down, each hawk can be looted for one hawk beak and three [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md). There are 67 nests in 17 different locations where hawks can spawn. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 17 around the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 10 centered 300 ft ENE of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-58956&y=106667)
- 4 in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 4 around the [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 centered 280 ft SE of [Autumnwatch Tower](Skyrim_Autumnwatch_Tower.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=87391&y=-104395)
- 3 centered 300 ft NNW of [Froki's Shack](Skyrim_Froki%27s_Shack.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=93532&y=-95657)
- 3 around [Hela's Folly](Skyrim_Hela%27s_Folly.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 3 around [Orphan's Tear](Skyrim_Orphan%27s_Tear.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 3 around [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
