[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-item-Miraak%27s_Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak%27s_Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Gloves.jpg) Miraak's Gloves Miraak's Gloves are a pair of gauntlets worn by [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md). They are classified as [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md). They can be obtained after defeating Miraak during [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md). Their enchantment increases the spell absorption of [Miraak's Robes](Skyrim_Miraak%27s_Robes.md) by five percent.

## Notes
- With this item and Miraak's Robes equipped, the [Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) power and the [Atronach alteration perk](Skyrim_Atronach_(perk).md), it is possible to achieve 100% spell absorption.
