# Tojay-raht

*When Jode is waxing and Jone is waning
Then shall be born the Tojay-Raht
Masters of Fang and Claw
And the Dance of the Duel
They perfect Mind, Body, and Spirit
To become paragons of Soul
ja-Kha'jay given form
They train for the War at the End.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/e/e1/Mwaxing_Swaning.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Swaning.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaxing_Swaning.jpg) Tojay-raht are born when Masser is waxing and Secunda is waning<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Tojay-raht are a bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). While much about the Tojay-raht is unknown, it is evident that they are bipedal and have digitigrade legs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOTojay-raht Statue-3)</sup> Additionally, The Tojay-raht are renowned for their exceptional combat skills, making them masterful fighters in battle and they are larger than their non-raht counterparts, the [Tojay](Lore_Tojay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-AUAWTE-4)</sup>
