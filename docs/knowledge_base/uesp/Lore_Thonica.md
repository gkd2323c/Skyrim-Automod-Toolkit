Thonica was an [empress regent](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Septim Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md). She was the imperial consort of Emperor [Uriel Septim V](Lore_Uriel_V.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup> In [3E 285](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_285), Thonica gave birth to their son, [Uriel](Lore_Uriel_VI.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup> In [3E 290](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_290), Uriel V was killed in [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md) at the Battle of [Ionith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ionith).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RDa I-4)</sup>

Uriel VI was too young to rule in his own right, so following her son's coronation in 3E 290, Thonica was given a restricted regency until he reached his majority. During Thonica's regency, the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) retained the real power and enjoyed the unlimited and unrestricted power to enact laws and generate profits. She reportedly enjoyed even her limited regency. Uriel VI slowly assumed positions of responsibility over the years, but she and the council were reluctant to allow him to assume full power.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup> In [3E 307](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_307), Uriel VI was finally allowed to rule on his own, ending Thonica's regency.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup>
