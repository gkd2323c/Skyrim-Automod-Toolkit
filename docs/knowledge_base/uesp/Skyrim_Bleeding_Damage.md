*Enchanting description:* **Does <mag> bleeding damage for <dur> seconds.**

**Bleeding damage** is an effect applied to [war axes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) with the *[Hack and Slash](Skyrim_Hack_and_Slash.md)* perk, [battle axes](Skyrim_Battle_Axe.md) with the *[Limbsplitter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Limbsplitter)* perk, or when bashing enemies with the [Targe of the Blooded](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Targe_of_the_Blooded). The effect cannot be absorbed or resisted in any way, and affected enemies will visibly bleed for as long as the effect is active. Bleeding damage can only be inflicted on living NPCs and creatures; [Undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) and [Dwarven Automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md) are immune due to lacking blood.

## Magnitude and Duration
Each rank of the perks determine the magnitude and duration of bleeding damage dealt by weapons. The Targe of the Blooded is fixed at 3 points of bleeding damage for 5 seconds.

## Uses
The Targe of the Blooded cannot be disenchanted as the effect is actually implemented as a spell. Thus the effect is not available for use for [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md).

- [Targe of the Blooded](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Targe_of_the_Blooded): *Damage Health* (`0010582c`; `dun Targe Of The Blooded ME`)
- [*Hack and Slash* perk](Skyrim_Hack_and_Slash.md) and [*Limbsplitter* perk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Limbsplitter): *Bleeding Damage* (`000c367a`; `Perk Bleeding Damage`)

## Notes
- Weapons from [official add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) cannot inflict bleeding damage as the requisite perks for these weapons do not exist.
