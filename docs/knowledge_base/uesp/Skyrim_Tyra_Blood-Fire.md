[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-npc-Tyra_Blood-Fire.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tyra_Blood-Fire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tyra_Blood-Fire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tyra_Blood-Fire.jpg) Tyra Blood-Fire Tyra Blood-Fire is a hostile [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [nightblade](Skyrim_Nightblade.md) and former leader of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) bandit gang. During the related quest, she prays at the shrine of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) southwest of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

She wears a full set of enchanted [Ebony plate armor](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Ebony_Plate_Items.md#Ebony_Plate_Armor) and wields two matching [ebony swords](Skyrim_Ebony_Sword.md). She also carries a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), an [elven bow](Skyrim_Elven_Bow.md), 50 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md), and a [ruby](Skyrim_Ruby.md), as well as a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v1](Skyrim_The_Crimson_Dirks,_v1.md)* and a letter titled *[To (Player)](Skyrim_To_(Player).md)*.
