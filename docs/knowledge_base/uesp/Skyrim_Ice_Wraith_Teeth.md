## Ingredients
40 guaranteed samples can be found in 20 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 5 in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 5 in [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 4 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 3 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in [Nightcaller Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightcaller_Temple) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in the [Hall of the Vigilant](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in the [Sleeping Giant Inn](Skyrim_Sleeping_Giant_Inn.md) ([Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md))
- 2 in [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
One sample of ice wraith teeth is always dropped by an [ice wraith](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Ice Cold](Skyrim_Ice_Cold.md)**: Find five ice wraith teeth for [Marise Aravel](Skyrim_Marise_Aravel.md). - 5 samples are needed for this quest.

## Notes
- Ice Wraith Teeth are far smaller than the ones found on the creatures themselves.
- When dropped, Ice Wraith Teeth may lack Havok physics and instead float some distance off of the floor.
- Although the creatures the ingredient comes from are resistant to [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), the ingredient itself carries the Weakness to Frost effect.
