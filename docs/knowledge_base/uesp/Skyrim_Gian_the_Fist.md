[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-npc-Gian_the_Fist.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gian_the_Fist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gian_the_Fist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gian_the_Fist.jpg) Gian the Fist **Gian the Fist** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) located in [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) beneath the city of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). As his name suggests, he uses [unarmed](Skyrim_Combat.md#Unarmed_Combat) attacks that do significant damage.

Gian lives in the southwestern chamber of the Ratway and has booby-trapped it with [bear traps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap). He has an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md), and a hay pile at his disposal, although he never uses them. He is hostile and will attack you on sight.

Gian wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and the unique [gloves of the pugilist](Skyrim_Gloves_of_the_Pugilist.md). He carries a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), inn [food](Skyrim_Food.md), and a small amount of gold. If you are level 16 or above, he also wears a leveled [ring of regenerate stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Recuperation).

## Notes
- Even though Gian's room is booby trapped, he has a tendency to run straight through them to your position, if he detects your approach. Since he often fights the skeever(s) outside his room, his stepping on the traps can actually kill him, leading to you finding his corpse, or him killing himself as he rushes you.
