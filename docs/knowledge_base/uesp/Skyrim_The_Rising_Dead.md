## Quick Walkthrough
1. Reach level 5 and read the [anonymous letter](Skyrim_Anonymous_Letter_(Plague_of_the_Dead).md).
2. Travel to the ritual site north of [Southfringe Sanctum](Skyrim_Southfringe_Sanctum.md).
3. Find and read the [*Necromancer's Journal*](Skyrim_Necromancer%27s_Journal_(Plague_of_the_Dead).md) to complete the quest.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Rising_Dead_02.jpg) The Ritual Site After reaching level 5, a [courier](Skyrim_Courier.md) will approach you and deliver an [anonymous letter](Skyrim_Anonymous_Letter_(Plague_of_the_Dead).md). Read the letter to learn that a group of [Necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) plan to perform an ancient, dangerous summoning ritual to conjure an army of [zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md). The ritual site is located high in the mountains southeast of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), just north of [Southfringe Sanctum](Skyrim_Southfringe_Sanctum.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=31270&y=-99871&zoom=17&legacy=true).

Once you arrive at the ritual site, you will find a group of decomposing zombies standing over the bodies of the slain Necromancers. Clearly the ritual was successful, but the Necromancers lost control of their summoned undead. Defeat the zombies and approach the body of the Necromancer marked by the quest marker. He will be holding a leveled spell tome ([*Spell Tome: Conjure Zombie*](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Conjure_Zombie.md) for lower levels, or [*Spell Tome: Conjure Foul Zombie*](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Conjure_Foul_Zombie.md) for levels 11+) as well as a [journal](Skyrim_Necromancer%27s_Journal_(Plague_of_the_Dead).md). Read the journal to complete the quest and learn of the consequences of the ritual. You can also loot the area for two additional copies of *Spell Tome: Conjure Zombie*.

Following the conclusion of the quest, you will have a chance to encounter [zombie horde](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Zombie_Attack) world interactions randomly in the wilderness.

## Notes
- At release, this quest had no level requirement and the anonymous letter would be automatically added to your inventory upon installation. This was changed in [Patch 1.6](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#Version_1.6) with the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md).
- The unmarked location "[The Headless Skeleton](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places_Falkreath.md#The_Headless_Skeleton)" can also be found here, with it being next to the pine tree.
