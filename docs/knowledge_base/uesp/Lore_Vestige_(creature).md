# Vestige (creature)

**Vestiges** are created when a mortal creature has its [soul](Lore_Soul.md) replaced with a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [vestige](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md). The resulting Daedra forms an imitation of the mortal's original body from [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CCTAP-1)</sup> New forms of Daedra are known to be created in this manner. For example, the first [Daedric Titan](Lore_Daedric_Titan.md) was the Vestige of [Boziikkodstrun](Lore_Boziikkodstrun.md), a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The most well-understood Vestiges are the [Soul Shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md), imperfect Vestiges used as slaves by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CCTAP-1)</sup> Although usually members of the mortal [races](Lore_Races.md), Soul Shriven of lesser creatures also seemingly exist.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> [Haskill](Lore_Haskill.md), the Chamberlain of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), is the Vestige of a mortal who previously mantled the Prince following a [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The prophecies of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) speak of a specific "Vestige", the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) known as the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md). This Vestige, a Soul Shriven who had been re-attuned to [Anuic](Lore_Anu.md) magic,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CCTAP-1)</sup> was foretold to end Molag Bal's attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
