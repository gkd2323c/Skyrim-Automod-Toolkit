[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-interior-Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.jpg) Interior of Clan Shatter-Shield Office **Clan Shatter-Shield Office** is a small office in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) that is home to Clan Shatter-Shield's shipping operations.

The office is located on Windhelm's docks, between the [Argonian Assemblage](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage.md) and the [East Empire Company regional office](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_(place).md). It contains one interior zone: *[Clan Shatter-Shield Office](#Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md)**: Help the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) take care of some [pirates](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md).

## Clan Shatter-Shield Office
The building is a busy shipping hub run by the Shatter-Shields' employee, [Suvaris Atheron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron). There is a long wooden table and three chairs holding a copy of *[Thief of Virtue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_of_Virtue)* right in front of the entrance, with several empty carts to the east and west. The middle section is full of crates, and the rear section has a small wooden table and chair to the west, with the [business ledger](Skyrim_Business_Ledger.md), a level-locked non-respawning [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) containing random items, a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), and an important clue required for the relevant [quest](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md) - [Suvaris's logbook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Suvaris_Atheron%27s_Logbook) - on the table. An unlocked respawning empty [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) is to the east, and to the north is a set of shelves directly opposite the entrance holding copies of *[Of Fjori and Holgeir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir)* (see [notes](#Notes)), *[Beggar Prince](Skyrim_Beggar_Prince.md)*, *[Antecedants of Dwemer Law](Skyrim_Antecedants_of_Dwemer_Law.md)*, *[Report: Disaster at Ionith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Report__Disaster_at_Ionith)*, and *[The Wolf Queen, v5](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen,_v5)*, as well as a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), with a large pile of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks on and in front of the shelves.

## Notes
- The book *Of Fjori and Holgeir* relates to the quest [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md).
- None of the sacks in the office are [respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md), so they are safe to store items in.
