The Shadow King (real name unknown) was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) ruler during the early-mid [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He lead an unknown nedic clan that was allied with the [Duraki](Lore_Duraki.md) kingdom. He was the last of his title, as the Nedic civilization of the [Deathlands](Lore_Deathlands.md) fell with the arrival of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Not D-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSot B-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Shadow.png/100px-ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Shadow.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Shadow.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Shadow.png) The Shadow Banner In [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808), the High King of [Skyreach](Lore_Skyreach.md), [Durac](Lore_Durac.md) arranged a marriage between King [Kestic](Lore_Kestic.md) and his daughter, Saradin, to cement relations between both factions,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SD-3)</sup> much to the dismay of his court wizard and close friend, [Virmaril](Lore_Virmaril.md). However, this never came to be as the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) had landed on the shores of the Deathlands and spread across the region's outermost corners. The [Anka-Ra](Lore_Anka-Ra.md) moved to the far northeast, into the region of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md), and storming the Kingdom of Skyreach.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup>

Virmaril proposed a plan to High King Durac to fight against the Anka-Ra. The High King arranged the [council of kings](Lore_Council_of_Nedic_Kings.md), bringing the rulers of various Nedic clans into the Skyreach Catacombs. However, Virmaril murdered the council, taking control of the deceased kings and the interred dead in the catacombs with [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md). It was in vain however as he was sealed deep in the inner sanctum.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WSJ-5)</sup> Skyreach was subsequently besieged by the Anka-Ra and the nedic kingdoms were dissolved. Many years later in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a band of [adventurers](Lore_Soulless_One.md) delved into the Skyreach Catacombs when dark magic came from the ruins. The group faced the dead Nedic kings in waves until they managed to destroy them all at once.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSot B-2)</sup>

Although his real name was lost to the history, scholars have speculated that the title of Shadow King reflected the regions he ruled, or perhaps his individual sphere of power.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SE3-6)</sup>
