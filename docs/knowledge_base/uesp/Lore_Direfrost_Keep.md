[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Direfrost Keep, also called **Castle Direfrost**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Clans-1)</sup> is a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) castle located in the southern [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Direfrost Keep is the ancestral home of the Direfrost Clan of Nordic Witch Hunters, whose lineage goes as far back as recorded history. But as of now, Direfrost Keep has fallen into disrepair as the Direfrost Clan has fallen into obscurity. Deep in the Direfrost Meadhall lies the Direfrost Flame, a sacred fire that gives the Clan the access to commune with their ancestors in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDF-2)</sup> The Keep leads to **Direfrost Summit**.

## History
The Direfrost Clan have existed since before recorded history, known for being gentle rulers in northern Eastmarch, but for having a massive disregard for witchcraft. The Clan would become well-known for their persecution of witches, as Eastmarch was riddled with them. Direfrost Keep was at one point built, serving as their ancestral home where they would live for the rest of their lives. The northern winds of the Velothi Mountains are kept at bay from the Keep by the Direfrost Flame, their spiritual connection to their ancestors at Sovngarde.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Clans-1)</sup> The Keep has always been meant to safeguard this flame from those who sought to use it. The next patriarch of the Direfrost Clan would light the flame with the Torch of Heirs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDF-2)</sup> The Direfrost Clan would go on to have their reputation known across the region, slaying variously known witches and warlocks. Lorgar the Plague was beheaded at the steps of Direfrost Keep in an attempt to challenge Odrama Direfrost.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

However, the Direfrost Clan would fall to their own hubris as the witch Drodda of Icereach was able to defeat the patriarch of the Direfrost Clan, Agomar Direfrost, and take over Direfrost Keep. All hope was seemingly lost for the Direfrost Clan as Drodda ruled over the castle for several years. However, in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the last-living heir to Direfrost Keep, Eboric Direfrost of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md), sought the help of the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) and was able to defeat Drodda and reclaim the Direfrost Keep from the elements. What became of Eboric Direfrost, or the Keep is currently unknown.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
