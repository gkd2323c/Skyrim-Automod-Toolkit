- *Tax and Excise Status*: See Form 5, part A

Observation confirms adherence to strict watch schedules and proper care and adequate number of guard beasts ([senche-tigers](Lore_Senche-Tiger.md)).

- *Authorized Laborers*: Laborers in reasonable health, do not exhibit excessive tooth-rot or shakes associated with illicit activities. See Form 3 for registration status and laborer profile information.
