Nikulas was an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) warrior from the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He and four companions embarked on a quest to find [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md). However, Nikulas, [Erlendr](Lore_Erlendr.md), and [Hunroor](Lore_Hunroor.md) were betrayed by the wizard [Grimkell](Lore_Grimkell.md), who turned them into stone pillars at Brodir Grove on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). They were avenged by [Ulfgar the Unending](Lore_Ulfgar_the_Unending.md), who killed the wizard. All of the friends eventually found their way to Sovngarde.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup>
