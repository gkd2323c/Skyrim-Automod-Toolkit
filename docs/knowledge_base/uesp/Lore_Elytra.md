**Elytra** are large [mantis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mantis) -like arthropods indigenous to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Like many of the native creatures of the realm, there are color differences between the Elytra of [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) and [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), but they are otherwise identical.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSB-1)</sup> They are found in high concentrations in [root tunnel systems](Lore_Root.md), but inhabit areas throughout the entire Isles. They were also found within [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md)'s sewers, where a root tunnel system manifested as a result of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s influence, and a [madman's](Lore_Thoron.md) experiments with the [Sword of Jyggalag](Lore_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md). Some Elytra were smuggled out into the rest of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in hopes that they could be sold for a significant profit.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Some Elytra secrete a glowing green substance known as [Felldew](Lore_Felldew.md), prized as a powerful, addictive drug.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-We-3)</sup>

An Elytra's enlarged thorax can grow as large as a human male, and functions as a womb. It is also where Elytra's ichor forms. The ichor's purpose is to paralyze a foe, and if the Elytra deemed the paralyzed to be a worthy host, they will nest its eggs within the host.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AEL-4)</sup> They use deadly poisons to kill their prey,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Loading-5)</sup> which if gone unchecked, venom can slay the average man over a period of hours.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSB-1)</sup> After growing and feeding on the hosts body for a few days, the hatchlings will emerge.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AEL-4)</sup> Especially deadly is the Elytra Matron's venom, which can last much longer than the poisons found on the lesser varieties of the creature.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSB-1)</sup>

Despite their unsettling appearance, Elytra have the potential to be gentle, and may welcome people into their brood and allow them to make the root systems they inhabit their home.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AEL-4)</sup> Elytra nymphs can be kept as pets, and they may even allow people to freely harvest their venom.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ENSR-6)</sup>
