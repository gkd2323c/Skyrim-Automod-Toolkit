[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/ON-place-Buraniim_Isle.jpg/200px-ON-place-Buraniim_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Buraniim_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Buraniim_Isle.jpg) Buraniim Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Buraniim Isle is a small island in central [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), located off the coast from the town of [Mathiisen](Lore_Mathiisen.md). It is home to **Buraniim**, the ruin that was once part of the greater **Buraniim Sea-Complex** built by the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md) from which the isle gets its name.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BI-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Aelif-3)</sup>

The shallows of Buraniim Isle are rife with a unique subspecies of [ornaug](Lore_Ornaug.md). Known for being vibrant an playful, these blue and yellow ornaugs have been known to splash in the surf to attract the curiosity of would-be prey.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## History
At some point during or after the early [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) the site of Buraniim was built on the isle by the Ancients, the ancestors of the Auridon [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md). It was once a hub and presumably a part of a larger structure and a sea-complex that extended out into the sea. Over the years, during the times of the outburst of the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) the complex was abandoned and as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) it was primarily used as a retreat from the city life in nearby [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md). [Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md) was once part of this complex.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AE-2)</sup>

The ruins were briefly reoccupied in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) and the Daedric minions of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), acting as a lair within which the Daedra could sacrifice mortal souls to fill the [Mortuum Vivicus](Lore_Mortuum_Vivicus.md). They were also making preparations to construct a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md) in the isle. This was discovered by the scholar and author, [Fenlil the Wayfarer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fenlil_the_Wayfarer) who intended to inform the local authorities about this. It is unknown however if he succeeded in this endeavor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AE-2)</sup> The Daedra were swiftly purged from island, however by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), which was under the effective control of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) at the time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
