# Tourmaline

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-crown_store-Tourmaline_Swamp_Jelly.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Tourmaline_Swamp_Jelly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Tourmaline_Swamp_Jelly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Tourmaline_Swamp_Jelly.jpg) Tourmaline Swamp Jelly Tourmaline is a multihued crystalline mineral.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pet-1)</sup>

Tourmaline Swamp Jellies are vibrant subspecies of [Swamp Jellies](Lore_Swamp_Jelly.md). The [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) who named the subspecies cited the mineral as her inspiration, though she initially referred to it as 'watermelon jelly.' Its taste bears resemblance to the actual fruit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pet-1)</sup>

Tourmaline Steeple, located within the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) complex on the [Isle of Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md) was a building. Very little is known about it, except that it contained housing chambers of the author of *[Once](Lore_Once.md)*, [Beredalmo the Signifier](Lore_Beredalmo.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Once-2)</sup>
