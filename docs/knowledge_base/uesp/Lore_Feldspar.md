# Feldspar

Feldspar is a common mineral used in [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) craftsmanship and art. One notable use is crafting sculptures, such as ones depicting the legendary [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife.md) Balreth and Sadal, from individual large feldspar crystals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BOSS-1)</sup> [Porphyry](Lore_Porphyry.md), known for its uses in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) is sometimes found embedded with feldspar crystals, and encased in hard igneous rock.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAR-2)</sup>
