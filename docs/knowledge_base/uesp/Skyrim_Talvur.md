[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Talvur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Talvur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Talvur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Talvur.jpg) Talvur's corpse **Talvur** was a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) miner in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). His body is in a small unmarked camp just south of [Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole.md). Leaning against a nearby rock is the corpse of a his lover, [Karan](Skyrim_Karan.md), and next to her is [Karan's journal](Skyrim_Karan%27s_Journal.md). It reveals that Talvur was eloping with the daughter of a nobleman when they were killed, presumably by the animal which attacks you.

Behind the tent is a hollowed-out stump where Talvur has stored his worldly possessions, which you can loot.

If you return to the camp later on, you will find the camp has been removed, and a [shrine of Mara](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mara.md) has been placed nearby.

Talvur wears a randomly determined set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variants) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variants), and has a 50% chance of wearing a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md) (3 variants). He carried a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) that can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) in quality along with a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md). There is also a small chance that a tiny amount of gold can be found in his possession.
