[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-creature-Gleda_the_Goat.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Gleda_the_Goat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gleda_the_Goat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gleda_the_Goat.jpg) Gleda near the giant called Grok **Gleda the Goat** is a unique [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) that belongs to [Ennis](Skyrim_Ennis.md) of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). She is very friendly, and will follow you when you talk to her. She can be found trailing after a giant called [Grok](Skyrim_Grok.md), a ways south of Rorikstead, and, while the giant believes that he owns the creature, you may find yourself having to steal Gleda away from her large master in the quest [A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md)**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sam_Guevenne), as you recover from a bad hangover.

## Notes
- Though the game data refers to Gleda as male, it is an unreliable source as all animals are considered male.
- Gleda will not replace any creature followers as she is tied to a quest.
- She will not follow you into caves or some houses.
- Gleda will alert the guard if you engage in criminal activities and, as you cannot interact with her to make her wait, this makes sneaking past guards tricky.
- Gleda will sit and wait while you engage in battle but is virtually indestructible herself as she heals very rapidly.
- If you skip this portion of A Night to Remember (by either persuading, bribing, or intimidating Ennis), Gleda will eventually find her way back to her owner.
