*Heals the caster 10 points per second.* **Healing** is a novice level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will slowly [restore the health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) of the caster.

## Effects
- [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), 10 pts for 1 sec
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 10 pts for 1 sec; if the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Regeneration), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 15/sec.

## Notes
- Combined with the [Equilibrium](Skyrim_Equilibrium.md) spell from [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md), this can be an easy (if slow) way to train the Restoration skill.
- Comparing this to [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and [Close Wounds](Skyrim_Close_Wounds.md), Healing is generally the second most efficient of the three. It is the most efficient until you have 70 magicka, when it is passed by Close Wounds, and it is the second most efficient until you have 330 magicka, when it is passed by Fast Healing. Fast Healing and Close Wounds are obviously preferable in an emergency though, since they work instantly.
- Because all players start with this spell, the tome is only useful as decoration or as an item to sell to merchants.
