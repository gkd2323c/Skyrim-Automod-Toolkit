[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-npc-Velehk_Sain.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Velehk_Sain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Velehk_Sain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Velehk_Sain.jpg) Velehk Sain **Velehk Sain** is a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) pirate that is trapped in the [Planes of Oblivion](Lore_Planes_of_Oblivion.md) by a binding spell. He will only be encountered when nearing the completion of [Forgotten Names](Skyrim_Forgotten_Names.md), during which you will be able to summon him in [The Midden Dark](Skyrim_The_Midden.md).

As opposed to other Dremora who are usually clad in [Daedric armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Dremora_Equipment), Velehk instead wears [Hammerfell garb](Skyrim_Hammerfell_Garb.md) and a pair of [Redguard boots](Skyrim_Redguard_Boots.md), and like most pirates, he is equipped with a [scimitar](Skyrim_Scimitar.md), and has another spare scimitar in his inventory. Like other Dremora, he will drop a [daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Forgotten Names](Skyrim_Forgotten_Names.md)**: Summon the [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) pirate, Velehk Sain.

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Forgotten Names
When first summoned, he will ask:

- *"Where am I? You! Out with it, then."*
- **We're beneath Winterhold College. The Midden.**
- *"Ah, I was beginning to think the day would never come. So you broke the binding spell, did you? Matters little. Their souls are trapped in Oblivion all the same, and here I stay."*
- **You're going back where you belong, dremora.**
- *"Where I belong is back aboard my ship, on the Abecean. You've no hope of defeating me, pup. Don't bother. Why not broker a bargain, instead?"*

- - **Not a chance.**
- *"Have it your own way, meat."* (Turns hostile)

- **I'm listening.**
- *"Those kids are dead anyway. Killing me won't bring them back. I, on the other hand, have done quite a bit with my time on [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). Release me and I'll share a bit of my wealth with you."*
- **What wealth is this?**
- *"Years back, I had to ferret away some booty nearby. We'd sunk an Imperial Galleon off the coast. Good plunder, but we needed to travel light. We stashed what we couldn't carry, and I hid it with a trick I know. I can point you in the right direction, and we'll each go our separate ways."*
- **How could I even release you?**
- *"Names have power, lad/lass. You should know by now. Just say 'Velehk Sain, I release you.', and 'Poof!', I'm on my way."*

You will be presented with two options. The first one will have him reward you and then disappear, while the second will turn him hostile and render the treasure inaccessible:

- **Vehlek *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* Sain, I release you.**
- *"There's a smart lad/lass. Check the [map](Skyrim_Velehk_Sain%27s_Treasure_Map.md). The loot's hidden there. It'll show itself when you get near. See you on the other side, mortal."*

- **Velehk Sain, I banish you to Oblivion!**
- *"Clever try, scab, but it won't be that easy!"*

## Notes
- Velehk Sain is the subject of the book *[Pirate King of the Abecean](Skyrim_Pirate_King_of_the_Abecean.md)*.
- It can be deduced that the "Imperial Galleon" sunk by Velehk and his crew is actually at [Pilgrim's Trench](Skyrim_Pilgrim%27s_Trench.md). The magically hidden treasure is quite nearby, located to the southeast of the shipwreck on an island with a [Shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Velehk+Sain%27s+Locker)
- The [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spells [Banish Daedra](Skyrim_Banish_Daedra.md) and [Expel Daedra](Skyrim_Expel_Daedra.md) don't send Velehk back to Oblivion; they drain his health instead.
- Velehk Sain also appears in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Velehk).
