[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Safia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Safia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Safia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Safia.jpg) Safia [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-place-Red_Wave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Red_Wave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Red_Wave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Red_Wave.jpg) The Red Wave docked near the East Empire Company Warehouse **Safia**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade), is a pirate captain that commands the vessel [Red Wave](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md). She is the assassination target in the quest [Kill Safia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Safia). She is reputed to be a ruthless, proud fighter who leads a loyal and bloodthirsty crew, ready to smuggle anything for the right price.

She wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers). She wields a leveled enchanted [one-handed sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) (up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality), and carries a leveled potion or poison, leveled gold (range 50-250), a leveled [gem](Skyrim_Gems.md), a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Kill Safia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Safia)**: Kill a pirate captain on her own [ship](Skyrim_Red_Wave.md).

## Dialogue
If Captain Safia sees you, she will make various comments on your trespass:

*"You'd better have a damned good reason for being here..."* *"It takes a brave soul to wander onto my deck. It'll take more than that to leave in one piece."* *"I'd say you're either brave or stupid, but I can already tell which one it is."* *"You must have a death wish."*
## Quest-Related Events

### Kill Safia
If you are on the quest Kill Safia, talking to her will have her say:

*"Let me guess - you have some 'business' to discuss..."* You can answer her in three ways, though all options will end in her attacking you:

**I represent the Dark...** *"The [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)? Yeah, yeah, yeah. You think this is the first contract I've had on my life? I'm a pirate, sweetie. Assassination attempts are an occupational hazard. Now, shall we?"* **You face the Dread Lord...** *"[Sithis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sithis)? Let me guess: 'In the Void!' By the gods, you Dark Brotherhood are a predictable lot, aren't you? Okay, here's how this works. You try to assassinate me, and I kill you. Horribly. I have done this dance before, you know."* **(Remain silent)** *"Ah, I see. The old Dark Brotherhood 'silent intimidation' treatment. Oh, I know who you are. Don't be so surprised. You ain't the first assassin sent to kill me. Won't be the last. Now let's get this over with."*
## Rumors
If she is alive, crew member [Dorian](Skyrim_Dorian.md) will admit that he is intimidated by her:

*"I've only been a part of the Red Wave's crew for a few weeks, but I've already learned to stay out of Captain Safia's way."* Should you ask [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) about her, he will tell you:

*"Ah, yes. The pirate captain. A ruthless she-devil, by all accounts. Deadly with a blade. And let's not forget her crew - loyal and bloodthirsty, the lot of them. This is my final contract, and certainly the most challenging. Be careful with this one, my friend."* Talking to him again before killing Safia will also have him say:

*"You haven't faced that pirate captain yet, have you? Safia? I only ask because you're still alive..."* Guests at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) [party](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) may gossip about the Red Wave:

*"Apparently, the Red Wave has been docked in Solitude for well over a month, and with no sign of shipping out any time soon. Some say the ship suffered damage to her hull when she struck an iceberg, and Captain Safia can't afford the repairs. Of course, she would never admit such a thing. Captain Safia is far too proud a woman for that."* As for the Red Wave pirates, ask local citizen [Addvar](Skyrim_Addvar.md) about good fishing spots and he will describe the Red Wave as "thugs". Meanwhile, Thane [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), rumored to be dealing with them, will tell you:

*"There's pirates and then there's the crew of the Red Wave. They're in a class by themselves. They usually make runs along the coast shipping all sorts of contraband to and from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Rumor has it they can get you anything for the right price."*
## Notes
- Her skin color might make her appear to be [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md), but she is actually an Imperial with a skin tone that is unique to her and unavailable to you in character creation.
- Safia actually has two different warpaints applied on top of each other, the yellow face-covering war paint alongside black circles around her eyes. - Her crew members, except for Sabine Nytte, all have the same type of warpaint applied, similar to the [Blackblood Marauders](Skyrim_Blackblood_Marauder.md). However, neither of Safia's warpaints matches up with her crew's.
