**Aloe Vera** (or simply **aloe**) is a tall and leafy succulent plant found in warmer climates.

Aloe plants are found mainly in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> with pockets also located throughout the coastline and hills of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup> Different aloe species can also be found in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOATH-4)</sup> [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PFDA-5)</sup> [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOHendil-6)</sup> and even the fiery wastes of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Although not native to the province, aloe can be cultivated in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

## Varieties
Most aloe plants possess a green coloration and produce pale yellow flowers,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup> although certain flowering desert aloes produce striking orange flowers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PFDA-5)</sup> Some species of maroon aloe and red aloe possess darker, reddish leaves.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Marsh aloe is noted for its yellow and blue leaves, which sometimes curl together to grow as a pod.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

**Salloweed Aloe** grows near [Phaer](Lore_Phaer.md) on the island of Auridon. It can be turned into a powerful sedative for [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-HJ-13)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOHendil-6)</sup>

The **Xal-Vakka Aloe** of Vakka-Bok is a species of red aloe that is traditionally harvested by the [Root-Whisper Tribe](Lore_Root-Whisper_Tribe.md) of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md). It is known for its unnaturally fast healing properties, such as keeping wounds from festering and soothing burns. While many people do not know why it heals so well, it is theorized that the arcane magic of the [sun](Lore_Sun.md) gave the plant its abilities.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ONTuwul-14)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOATH-4)</sup>

## Usage
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/ON-quest-Aloe_That_Heals.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Aloe_That_Heals.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Aloe_That_Heals.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Aloe_That_Heals.jpg) Xal-Vakka Aloe being cultivated by the Root-Whisper Tribe [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/OBR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg/200px-OBR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-ingredient-Aloe_Vera_Leaves.jpg) Aloe Vera leaves, a useful [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) ingredient Aloe is best known for its powerful restorative effects, with its [leaves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aloe_Vera_Leaves) being a common ingredient in a variety of potions.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> When combined simply with pure water, the resulting mix can serve as an effective health potion.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>

The [Yokudans](Lore_Yokudan.md) were well aware of the herb's potential in medicine, often using it to staunch the flow of blood and to heal wounds. Its lacquer can be used to lessen the effects of [pox](Lore_Disease.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Fo H-17)</sup> According to legend, making a concoction of aloe and [ginkgo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ginkgo) can boost one's stamina,<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-SFo T-18)</sup> although its effects are inconclusive in practice.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

The famed [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) vintner Talan made use of aloe vera leaves to produce Frostdew Blanc, a restorative [wine](Lore_Wine.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup>
