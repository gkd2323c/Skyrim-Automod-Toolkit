—The Apprentice banishing Mehrunes Dagon<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MD-1)</sup> The Apprentice (b. [3E 372](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_372))<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Timeline-2)</sup> was the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) sent to the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) in [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398) after placing second in the Imperial Martial Arts competition that determined the next [Imperial Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperial_Guard) candidates to be tested there.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-intro-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CV-4)</sup> The Apprentice found the citadel captured by the legions of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and fought through powerful clans of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) and dealt with their leaders to rescue their fellow trainee, who may have been either Josian Kaid or Vatasha Trenelle,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Lft B-5)</sup> and banish Dagon with the [Sword of the Moon Reiver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sword_of_the_Moon_Reiver), which was acquired from the Prince's paramour, Si'ess the [Dark Seducer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mazken).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Battlespire-6)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1](#note_nb1) | |
| --- | --- |

The name, race, and gender of the Apprentice are ultimately player determined. The identity of the Apprentice's companion is determined by the gender the player chooses during [character creation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Character_Creation) in *[Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Battlespire)*; [Vatasha Trenelle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Vatasha_Trenelle) if playing as a man and [Josian Kaid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Josian_Kaid) if playing as a woman. The [introduction scene](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Quests) of *Battlespire* refers to the Apprentice with male pronouns.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-intro-3)</sup> A summary previously featured on the [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda) website used female pronouns for the Apprentice's companion.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-10th-7)</sup> A promotional rendering and pre-release screenshot of the Apprentice depicts them as a female.<sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup> - The decision to make the Apprentice's friend the opposite gender of the player character was due to Julian Le Fay's desire to "just throw a little bit of romance in the game" as he was "a sucker for that kind of stuff"<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-NGMag34-8)</sup><sup>:124-125</sup>
- The Apprentice is frequently referred to as a mortal by various Daedra throughout the siege on the Battlespire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MD-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ISD-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SED-10)</sup>
- [Divayth Fyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Divayth_Fyr) possessed a great variety of [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md) acquired by the Apprentice in the Battlespire. While Fyr is not the Apprentice, he may have some connection to the hero, and refuses to discuss how the artifacts came into his possession.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Divayth Fyr-11)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-GT-12)</sup>
