## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The Bulwark stands at the east end of Raven Rock, with its exterior nearly buried in ash. A short timbered tunnel burrows through the ash to the city's main gate, with the unlocked entrances to the garrison ("the Bulwark") and the jail facing each other just inside the gate. Two [Redoran guards](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) are posted outside these two entrances.

### The Bulwark Jail
The entrance leads into a narrow room running from west to east, with the expert-locked cell door dividing the space in two. Outside the cell, along the north wall are two unusable barrels, the prisoner belongings chest sitting on a grate showing an area below, two crates, a chair and a small table holding a bottle of [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shein), a bottle of [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma), a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a [cooked beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Beef) steak, a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and three food barrels next to the cell door. A single inattentive Redoran guard acting as jailor usually sits at the table.

Inside the cell, along the north wall is a set of shelves holding a bottle of [matze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matze), a bottle of sujamma, an [ash yam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam), a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), and copies of *[Bone, Part I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_I)* and *[Bone, Part II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_II)*, followed by a large unlit fireplace with five pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) covering a novice-locked grate to an escape tunnel. Along the south wall is an open box, a chair, an owned single bed, and another chair and a small round table holding a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two ash yams, a loaf of bread, and a [charred skeever meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Meat). The tunnel below the fireplace leads to the grate under the prisoner belongings chest, as well as another grate in the floor connected to a natural tunnel with a door to [Coldcinder Cave](Skyrim_Coldcinder_Cave.md).
