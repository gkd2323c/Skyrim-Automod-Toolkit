## Related Quests
- **[Blue in the Face](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_in_the_Face)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Goblins.md)</sup>: Investigate rumors of the Blue God's return.

## Notes
- The Spear of Bitter Mercy previously appeared in *[Battlespire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Items)*, *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Spear_of_Bitter_Mercy)*, and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Spear_of_Bitter_Mercy_Style).
- The weapon functions effectively the same as the [Giant Club](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Club) or [Dwarven Sphere Centurion Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Sphere_Centurion_Arrow) items, which cannot be directly picked up by the player, but can be picked up by followers. With console commands, you cannot add it to your inventory, but it can be spawned in as a physical object.
