*Undead up to level <mag> flee for 30 seconds.* The **Turn Undead** spells are [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells, which will cause an affected [Undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) enemy to flee from the caster for 30 seconds.

## Effects
- [Turn Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(effect).md), <mag> pts for 30 secs
- [Turn Undead Push](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 50 / 0 (see [bugs](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(spells).md#Bugs)) / 100 pts

## Perks
- [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md) increases magnitude by 25% and duration by 50%.
- [Restoration Dual Casting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restoration_Dual_Casting) increases duration by 120%, but does **not** increase effect level.

The effective levels of undead that can be turned with these spells are:

| Spell | Default | Necromage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Turn Lesser Undead | 6 | 7 |
| Turn Undead | 13 | 16 |
| Turn Greater Undead | 21 | 26 |

The durations are the same for all spell levels, varying only with perks applying (remember, for dual casting, you need both the perk and to be dual casting it):

| Perks | Duration (seconds) |
| --- | --- |
| None | 30 |
| Necromage | 45 |
| Dual Casting | 66 |
| Dual Casting, Necromage | 99 |

## Bugs
- The "Turn Undead Push" effect varies between these three spells. Notably in the "Turn Undead" spell, it has a magnitude of 0. This means it may stagger an opponent, but won't push them back. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, addresses this issue. The stagger effect for Turn Undead has been set to 75 pts.
