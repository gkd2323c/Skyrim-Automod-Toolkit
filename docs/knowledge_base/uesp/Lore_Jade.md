# Jade

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-trait_material-Jade.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-trait_material-Jade.png) Jade Jade is a kind of green [crystal](Lore_Crystals.md) often used for making jewelry and enhancing enchantments on armor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Jades and jade dust can be used in alchemy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The legendary [Necromancer's Amulet](Lore_Necromancer%27s_Amulet.md)'s skull is made of jade.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Jade is used for a variety of purposes by the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) such as corkscrew handles and statuettes shaped as the [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Jade inkwells are used to contain the green iridescent ink used for writing official decrees from the [Silvenar](Lore_Silvenar.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Jade is used by the Sul-Xan to fashion their armors.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SX-8)</sup>
