Saint Stental was a [Breton saint](Lore_Breton.md#Saints) who is best remembered for his act of heroism when he slew the [demon](Lore_Leaper.md) of Overlook Hill, rescuing the three Sisters of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) from a terrible fate. A memorial, named "The Saint Slays the Leaper", was erected in [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) to honor his courage in [2E 388](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_388), which depicts Stental armed with a sword facing a crouched [gargoyle](Lore_Gargoyle.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Stental-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
