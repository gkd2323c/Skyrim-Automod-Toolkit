## Ash Guardian
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg) An Ash Guardian **[Ash Guardians](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md)** are [atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md) -like ash creatures summoned by certain mages on Solstheim. A summoned Ash Guardian will be hostile and uncontrolled unless given a [Heart Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone).

This is a summary of information on Ash Guardians. See the main [Ash Guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md) article for more detailed information.

## Ash Spawn
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Spawn.jpg) An Ash Spawn **[Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md)** are hostile creatures found on Solstheim. They began to appear on the island after the [massive volcanic eruptions](Lore_Red_Year.md) of nearby Red Mountain early in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).

- Variations: Ash Spawn Skirmisher, Ash Spawn Immolator
- Loot: [Spawn Ash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spawn_Ash).

This is a summary of information on Ash Spawn. See the main [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) article for more detailed information.

## Ash Zombie
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ash_Zombie.jpg) An Ash Zombie **[Ash Zombies](Skyrim_Ash_Zombie.md)** are [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) victims who have been transformed into disfigured ash creatures by [Erden Relvel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Erden_Relvel) using the power of the [Mask of Dagoth Ur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask_of_Dagoth_Ur). As part of this transformation, the victim's face caves in and their lips are ritualistically stitched closed.

This is a summary of information on Ash Zombies. See the main [Ash Zombie](Skyrim_Ash_Zombie.md) article for more detailed information.
