## Official Summary
*This creation features seven classic staves from The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind. Purchase staves with unique enchantments from Master Neloth or buy an unenchanted staff and apply one yourself! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Items appear at vendors and in chests.)*

## Notes
- Staves with the unique ghost enchantments can only be acquired through purchase at a merchant or found as randomly generated container loot. The enchantments themselves cannot be acquired so cannot be Imbued upon a staff at an enchanter.
- The new staves retain some of the errors and inconsistencies from the base-game staves, such as the Staff of Thunderbolts casting Lightning Bolt instead of Thunderbolt, or the Grand Staff of Charming casting Calm instead of Pacify (and not being craftable).
