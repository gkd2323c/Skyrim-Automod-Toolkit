- *Heals the undead target 75 points, but not the living, atronachs or machines.*

**Heal Undead** is an adept level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will instantly restore the health of an undead target by 75 pts.

## Effects
- [Restore Health to Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health_to_Undead), 75 pts
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 75 pts; if the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Regeneration), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 112.5.

## Notes
- This spell is normally unavailable if you join the Dawnguard, as [Ronthil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ronthil) will be hostile to you. However, if you use a [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(effect).md) spell or item, you can calm him down so that he'll sell it to you. You must do this before you complete quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md) on Dawnguard side.
