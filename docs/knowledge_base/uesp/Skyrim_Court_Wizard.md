# Court Wizard


In court politics they are the equals of [housecarls](Skyrim_Housecarl.md), except on magical matters, but less important than [stewards](Skyrim_Steward.md). They are usually left alone by their jarl due to the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md)' suspicion and distrust of all things magical. They are also the only members of court that do not have to relocate if their [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) changes sides in the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md).

## Notes
- The jarls of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) and [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) do not employ court wizards. The absence of a wizard in Winterhold is likely due to the close proximity of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) (and the locals' disdain for magic), but the reasoning for the other two is unclear.
- [Aicantar](Skyrim_Aicantar.md), [Melaran](Skyrim_Melaran.md), and [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](Skyrim_Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver.md) all belong to the court wizard faction. They are all intended as replacements for the incumbent court wizards, but Calcelmo and Wuunferth are essential, preventing Aicantar and Dravynea from being needed. Melaran is supposed to replace Sybille in the event of her death, and he will confirm this in conversation, but he will never adopt her routine or sell anything. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, addresses this issue. Melaran will replace Stentor as intended.
