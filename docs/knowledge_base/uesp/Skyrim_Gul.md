[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-npc-Gul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gul.jpg) Gul **Gul** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) that lives in the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). She works in [Mor Khazgur Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine), an [orichalcum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore) mine located in the stronghold, with [Ghamorz](Skyrim_Ghamorz.md) and [Olur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olur).

She never sleeps, but instead uses her pickaxe on the many orichalcum ore veins in the mine, at times sitting down on one of the available chairs for a bite to eat.

She wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) with [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). She carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) (up to Orcish quality at level six), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). She also carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Larak's Longhouse](Skyrim_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md), although she never gets to use it.

- *"The mines are hard, but Malacath rewards those who rise to the challenge."*
