[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-item-Otar.jpg/200px-SR-item-Otar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Otar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Otar.jpg) Otar Otar is a malachite [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) obtained by killing the eponymous [Otar the Mad](Skyrim_Otar_the_Mad.md) within the [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md), [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md). The artifact's enchantment grants 30 points of [shock](Skyrim_Resist_Shock.md), [fire](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md), and [frost](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md) resistance. It can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [refined malachite](Skyrim_Refined_Malachite.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald_(quest).md)**: Discover the secrets of [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md).

## Notes
- Placing Otar on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses it gained from tempering.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a [special holder](Skyrim_Special_Holder.md) for this item.
