[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Anuriel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Anuriel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anuriel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anuriel.jpg) Anuriel **Anuriel** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) and the [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) to [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She is usually found at the Jarl's side in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) and will sleep from midnight to 8am in her bedroom just beyond the doorway to the inner part of the Keep. She is the girlfriend of [Unmid Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Unmid_Snow-Shod.md), and you can overhear a conversation between the two about sneaking out for a "training" session. Should the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) take control of Riften and have Jarl Laila [replaced](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md), Anuriel will be exiled to the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md).

Once the Jarl grants you permission, Anuriel can sell you a house called [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) in the city for 8,000 gold. In order to get permission you must complete the quest [Supply and Demand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Supply_and_Demand).

If you side with the Imperial Legion in the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), you will be sent by [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) to find material you could use to attempt to blackmail her to obtain information that could assist in the Imperial takeover of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). In the process, it is discovered that Anuriel is actually a [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) infiltrator put in place by Maven Black-Briar to manipulate Jarl Laila into defending Maven's interests and keep her blind to the activity of the Thieves Guild.

Anuriel wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000cee80) and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82). She is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"Welcome to Mistveil Keep. I'm Jarl Laila's steward, Anuriel."* *"As Riften's Steward I'm afraid I can provide only limited assistance, but speak your mind."* *"If you're here to report any instances of corruption, be confident that we're handling these rumors accordingly."* Greetings when exiled:

*"I'm afraid my days as the steward of Riften has come to an end."* *"The Imperials now control Riften and have placed Maven Black-Briar on the Jarl's throne."* *"Perhaps the Stormcloaks might rally to take back Riften one day. Only time will tell."* When spoken to:

**What do you do around here?** *"As steward, I serve as an intermediary between the Jarl and her subjects. I also handle the less important and day-to-day policies that effect daily life in Riften. Lately I've had my hands full suppressing these ridiculous accusations of corruption in the keep."* **What are the rumors of corruption you mentioned?** *"There are those that believe the Jarl does nothing for this city... that it's corrupt and run by the Black-Briar family. I can assure you that this is entirely false. We are fully in control of everything."* **Tell me about the Thieves Guild.** *"I'd hardly call them a Guild. More like unorganized rabble. In fact, I wouldn't say they were a threat at all. No, our resources should be spent on preparations for invasion."* Her goodbye:

*"You're welcome in Riften as long as you continue to obey our laws."*
