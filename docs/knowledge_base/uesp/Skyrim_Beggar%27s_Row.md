## Beggar's Row
Outside the door are several barrels that are free to take from. Beggar's Row is a heavily vaulted part of the sewer, that has two owned hay piles and one owned bedroll inside it. [Madesi](Skyrim_Madesi.md), [Edda](Skyrim_Edda.md) and [Snilf](Skyrim_Snilf.md) all sleep in here at night. There is a campfire to the north and a couple of chairs as well as several [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks and barrels around the place. Scattered about the floor are a couple of bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and various vegetables, a [raw rabbit leg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg) and several pieces of cheese. At the southwest connection there are two bottles of [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma). There is a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Dance in Fire, v7](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v7)* due north upon entering, under a fern. The flat sack near the tipped-over basket is non-respawning, while the other upright sack does respawn.
