## Ingredients
Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 3 samples in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md)'s [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md)

## Plants
Yellow Mountain Flower plants can be found growing in several locations:

- 27 plants (55 with [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md)) in the [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md), east of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md).
- 2 plants in [Volkihar Courtyard](Skyrim_Volkihar_Courtyard.md) after it is replanted.
- 2 plants in Vardnknd Gallery ([Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md)) in front of the Spring's Symphony entrance. Vardnknd Gallery is the central cavern in the [Runoff Caverns](Skyrim_Runoff_Caverns.md).
- 1 respawning plant at the [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md) home ([Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md)) if you complete the quest to own the property and then build the apiary.

## Notes
- Unlike the other three types of mountain flowers, the yellow mountain flower cannot be [planted](Skyrim_Greenhouse.md#Food_and_Ingredients) at [player-built](Skyrim_Construction.md) homes.<sup>([HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md))</sup>
