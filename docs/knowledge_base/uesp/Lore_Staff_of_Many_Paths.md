# Staff of Many Paths

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/ON-trailer-Gold_Road.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Gold_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Gold_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Gold_Road.jpg) The Staff of Many Paths with [fate](Lore_Fate.md) threads swirling around the top The Staff of Many Paths was a powerful [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) named for the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md), associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md) and filled with her power.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Beragon-1)</sup> It had the ability to drain, hold, and imbue beings and objects with energy.

The Staff was long guarded by the Oathsworn, followers of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) within their training ground of the Oathsworn Pit, until it was stolen by the [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md), a [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) cult that revered Ithelia. It is said that around the Staff strange things would often happen, weapons going missing, or initiates somehow thrashing a veteran trainer during their first lessons, causing the relic to be locked away.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dulzush-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Strife-3)</sup> The Recollection would use the Staff to imbue the [Greenspring Seeds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenspring_Seeds) which [Greenspeakers](Lore_Greenspeakers.md) normally use to grow new sections of forest with [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) energies that strengthened and corrupted the [magic](Lore_Magic.md), creating Wildburn Seeds which they used to cause a thick jungle to cover much of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) overnight, and to create the [Wildburn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn), a magical corruption of the land that mutated living beings. The leader of the Recollection, King [Nantharion](Lore_Nantharion.md), attempted to use the Staff to create a new Wildburn Seed, but the interruption of the ritual by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) caused the Seed's energies to be released prematurely, destroying the Recollection's living fortress of [Hoperoot](Lore_Hoperoot.md). <sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Darolith-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Greenspeaker Sorilen-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Seeds Suspicion-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Nantharion-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Beragon-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tribune-8)</sup>

At [Miscarcand](Lore_Miscarcand.md) Nantharion attempted to use the Staff to drain the energies of the great [Ayleid Well](Lore_Meteoric_Iron.md) in order to restore Ithelia to her ancient power and create a Wildburn that'd cover all of the West Weald. Though Nantharion was slain, the servants of Ithelia retrieved the Staff and channeled the Well's power into her, restoring her ancient might.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Nantharion-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Beragon-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Vargas-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Gambit-10)</sup> The Staff was last seen during Ithelia's attempted takeover of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md), wielded by [Torvesard](Lore_Torvesard.md), who used the artifact to drain the power of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) and imbue Ithelia with it. After Ithelia was granted clarity by the Mirror of Truth and decided to put an end to her plans, a desperate Torvesard turned the Staff on his Prince, absorbing the combined power of both Ithelia and Hermaeus Mora into himself and imprisoning both. Torvesard used the Staff to channel his power in battle. After Torvesard's defeat the Staff vanished in a flash of light and the stolen powers were returned to their respective Princes, the artifact's fate is not known.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Torvesard-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Ithelia-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Fate Forgotten Prince-13)</sup>
