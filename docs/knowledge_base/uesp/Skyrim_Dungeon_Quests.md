# Dungeon Quests


## Dungeon [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Darklight Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower)** — A medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) southwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md), [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md), and [witches](Skyrim_Witch.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Darklight+Tower) - **[Repentance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance)**: Help [Illia](Skyrim_Illia.md) prevent her mother [Silvia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia) from transforming into a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) southeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [Dragon Cultists](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Cultist), [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md), and the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Forelhost) - **[Siege on the Dragon Cult](Skyrim_Siege_on_the_Dragon_Cult.md)**: Enter the ruins of [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) and defeat [Rahgot](Skyrim_Rahgot_(creature).md) to recover his mask for [Captain Valmir](Skyrim_Captain_Valmir.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) south-southwest of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and the unique wispmother the [Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady_(creature).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Frostmere+Crypt) - **[The Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady.md)**: Find out what happened in [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) west of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Vokun](Skyrim_Vokun_(creature).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=High+Gate+Ruins) - **[A Scroll For Anska](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Scroll_For_Anska)**: Help [Anska](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anska) trace her roots in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Hillgrund's Tomb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb)** — A small [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) southwest of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Hillgrund%27s+Tomb) - **[Ancestral Worship](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancestral_Worship)**: Help [Golldir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golldir) save his [aunt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Agna) and stop a [necromancer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran) from defiling his [ancestral barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hillgrund%27s_Tomb).
- **[Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel)** — A large [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) accessed from inside [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nchuand-Zel) - **[The Lost Expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Expedition)**: Recover the journals from the expedition in [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Treva's Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch)** — A medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Treva%27s+Watch) - **[Infiltration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Infiltration)**: Help [Stalleo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalleo) take back [Treva's Watch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva%27s_Watch).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md), and the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Hevnoraak](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Valthume) - **[Evil in Waiting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Evil_in_Waiting)**: Seal away an [ancient evil](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(creature).md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) southeast of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and the ghost of [Kvenel the Tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kvenel_the_Tongue). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Volunruud) - **[Silenced Tongues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silenced_Tongues)**: Unlock the burial chamber of the Tongue Chieftain [Kvenel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kvenel_the_Tongue).

## Dungeon [Miscellaneous Quests](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) west of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Angarvunde) - **[Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde_(quest).md)**: Discover the treasure of [Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Ansilvund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ansilvund)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) north of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) led by [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ansilvund) - **[Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md)**: Kill [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven) in [Ansilvund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ansilvund).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Blind Cliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blind_Cliff_Cave)** — A medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southwest of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) along the banks of the [Karth River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karth_River) containing [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blind+Cliff+Cave) - **[The Affairs of Hagravens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Affairs_of_Hagravens)**: After discovering an [imprisoned hagraven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Melka), you must help her reclaim her lost tower.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Lighthouse.png) **[Frostflow Lighthouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse)** — A lighthouse west-southwest of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) containing [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), and [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Frostflow+Lighthouse) - **[Frostflow Abyss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Abyss)**: Uncover the tragic events inside [Frostflow Lighthouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostflow_Lighthouse).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md)** — A medium-sized [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) east of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) containing the [Mistwatch Bandits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mistwatch_Bandit) and their [leader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Leader). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mistwatch) - **[Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch_(quest).md)**: Help [Christer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Christer) locate his kidnapped wife [Fjola](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fjola).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Moss Mother Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern)** — A small, open-roofed [cavernous](Skyrim_Caves.md) grove lush with many kinds of flora and fungi, located due north of [Hunter's Rest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunter%27s_Rest) and northwest of [Half-Moon Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Half-Moon_Mill) which is inhabited by bears and spriggans. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Moss+Mother+Cavern) - **[Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md)**: Help an [injured hunter](Skyrim_Valdr.md) avenge his fallen companions.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md)** — A small ruined [Nordic tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Towers) east-northeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) disguised as guards. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nilheim) - **[Telrav's Request](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav%27s_Request)**: Escort [Telrav](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telrav) back to his camp in [Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md)** — A medium-sized [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) north of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) containing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Otar the Mad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Otar_the_Mad). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ragnvald) - **[Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald_(quest).md)**: Discover the secrets of [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Rebel's Cairn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rebel%27s_Cairn)** — A small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) northwest of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Rebel%27s+Cairn) - **[Rebel's Cairn](Skyrim_Rebel%27s_Cairn_(quest).md)**: Find [Red Eagle's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle) [sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Eagle%27s_Bane).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Giant_Camp.png) **[Sleeping Tree Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping_Tree_Cave)** — A [giant camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Camps) west of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sleeping+Tree+Camp) - **[Sleeping Tree Cave](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md)**: Question [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) about an interesting [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ysolda%27s_Message) you found on a [dead Orc](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulag).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum)** — A small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southeast of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) containing the conjurer [Bashnag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bashnag) and his [coven](Skyrim_Spellsword_(NPC).md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Southfringe+Sanctum) - **[The Savior of Selveni Nethri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Savior_of_Selveni_Nethri)**: Help a necromancer escape from [Southfringe Sanctum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Southfringe_Sanctum).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Ruin.png) **[Yngol Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_Barrow)** — A small [Nordic ruin](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) northeast of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) containing the [shade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol%27s_Shade) of an ancient Nord warrior. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Yngol+Barrow) - **[Yngol's Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol%27s_Barrow)**: Find the treasure unlocked by the [Coral Dragon Claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coral_Dragon_Claw).
