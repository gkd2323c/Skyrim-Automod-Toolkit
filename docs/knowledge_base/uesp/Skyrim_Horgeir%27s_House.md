## Horgeir's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House.jpg) Fireplace and single bed area The front door to this small home opens to a view of the fireplace at the center, with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) at its base and an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) on the mantel. On the floor to the right of the fireplace is a woven basket containing four [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) and five [green apples](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md), while four pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) sit in a pile to the left.

On the east side of the home is a small bedroom area, with a single bed in the northeast corner. A [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) can be found propped against the wall next to the head and two [drums](Skyrim_Drum.md) sit at the foot of the bed. Beneath the bed is a copy of the book *[Spirit of Nirn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spirit_of_Nirn)*. In the southeast corner, a small table holds a [flute](Skyrim_Flute.md), two [boiled creme treats](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md), three bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), and a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll). On the opposite side of the home, another bedroom area can be found in the northwest corner, with a double bed along the north wall. Next to the bed, a small table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) sits in the corner. A non-respawning wardrobe is situated next to the table, with a small non-respawning chest to its left.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Horgeir%27s_House_02.jpg) Dining area and double bed area The south wall on the west side of the home is host to a cozy dining area. A respawning food barrel rests next to the front door with some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese) on top, next to a large dining table with a pleasant array of food: an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), one [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), a serving of [baked potatoes](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md), a sweet roll, an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md), a serving of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), and two bottles of Nord mead. To the right of the table sits a woven basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), with a hanging rack above it holding three freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). A set of shelves next to the basket holds an additional two leeks, a garlic bulb, and three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), along with an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), and a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) on top. Above the shelves are several hanging ingredients, including two garlic braids, three portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and one dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). A respawning meat barrel and respawning food barrel sit in the corner next to the shelves.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horgeir%27s_House_Key) to which is carried by Horgeir, Lodvar, and Olda.
