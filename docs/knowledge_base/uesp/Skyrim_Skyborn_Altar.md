[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-place-Skyborn_Altar.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skyborn_Altar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar.jpg) Skyborn Altar **Skyborn Altar** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) east-southeast of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). Its [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) teaches part of the [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Bounty: Dragon](Skyrim_Bounty__Dragon.md)**: Kill a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) in its [lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Dragon Seekers](Skyrim_Dragon_Seekers.md)**: Go to a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) with [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) or [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) and kill the dragon. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The path to Skyborn Altar is just south of [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md); it can also be reached from the south by traveling between [Silent Moons Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Moons_Camp) and [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md). The dirt path is marked by two stone bird heads flanking the base of the stone steps heading up from the south.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skyborn_Altar_02.jpg) Before Dragon Rising Before the [dragons' return](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), the word wall will be guarded by a [wispmother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wispmother) and her [wisps](Skyrim_Wisp.md). In front of the wall is a sacrificial table, and on it is a [shrine of Akatosh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Akatosh), a dead [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), two leveled [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), a leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md), and a leveled weapon.

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md), which is resting on the wall. There is a [dead adventurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adventurer#Adventurer12) slumped against the right-hand side of the wall.

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has been completed, the boss chest is behind the pillar to the left of the word wall, and there is a burial urn a few feet north of the chest, hidden in the rocks.

## Notes
- A [silver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ore_Vein) is a short distance southwest from the bottom of the steps.
- Three [orichalcum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein) are 140 feet southeast of the altar [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&x=-3047&y=46305&zoom=16&legacy=true).
- A [moonstone ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein) is in a gap in the rocks to the northeast, between the altar and the [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&x=-3047&y=46305&zoom=16&legacy=true).
- An adept-locked chest is due south of the word wall. Follow the path down two flights of stairs, past the first tree on the left with a lump of rock next to it, and turn due east at the second tree. The chest is behind the tree, against the rock face.
- The dragon's death animation may plow into the rock from the north and leave a trail but no dragon. Climb down to the north to find the dragon's body.

## Bugs
- This location might not be marked as cleared even after killing all inhabitants. - Reloading a save before clearing it and trying again will fix this.
- The map marker location data for Skyborn Altar points to a random XMarker rather than to the map marker itself. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.3, fixes this bug.
