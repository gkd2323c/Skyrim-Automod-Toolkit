## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Corkarth Run was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Shalimar and had a rivalry with [Alavelis](Lore_Alavelis.md). Corkarth was neighbored by several settlements, including connections to Tear and [Silnim Dale](Lore_Silnim_Dale.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
