[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/SR-npc-Faida.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Faida.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Faida.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Faida.jpg) Faida at work in the tavern **Faida** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [food vendor](Skyrim_Food_Vendor.md) and the innkeeper of the [Four Shields Tavern](Skyrim_Four_Shields_Tavern.md) in the small village of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). She stays behind the inn's counter all day. During the [related quest](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).md), she will direct you towards the [Imperial Courier](Skyrim_Imperial_Courier.md).

Faida wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with some [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries some [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).md)**: Find and deliver some forged documents to the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md).
- **[Breaching Security](Skyrim_Breaching_Security.md)**: Kill and frame the [son](Skyrim_Gaius_Maro.md) of a high-ranking [Imperial guard](Skyrim_Commander_Maro.md).

## Quest-Related Events
At the start of Breaching Security, you will find Faida outside the [Penitus Oculatus Outpost](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Outpost.md) alongside her lover [Gaius Maro](Skyrim_Gaius_Maro.md) and his [father](Skyrim_Commander_Maro.md). Before starting his security inspections, Gaius shares a heartfelt goodbye with his father, and will then have a short moment with Faida:

- **Faida**: *"Well, this is it, then. Look after yourself, Gaius. You're doing your duty, and I'm proud of you. But you'd better come back to me. You hear?"*
- **Gaius**: *"Oh, Faida. I may travel alone, but you know I carry you always in my heart. I'll see you soon."*

After this, Gaius departs and Faida will head back to work at the inn.

## Dialogue
Faida can greet you by saying:

- *"Faida's the name. I keep the inn."*
- *"Something different about you, I can tell. Hope you ain't here to cause trouble."*
- *"The meals I serve ain't what I'd call a Jarl's feast, but they'll fill your belly."*
- *"Seen a lot of travelers lately. Mostly soldier * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, nowadays."*
- *"You met the [Lylvieves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lylvieve_Family%27s_House)? Quiet family, but they're the heart of Dragon Bridge."*
- *"Poor [Horgeir](Skyrim_Horgeir.md). Everyone in town knows about his [wife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olda)'s [affair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lodvar) but him. Probably better if he doesn't know."* (As long as no member of [Horgeir's family](Skyrim_Horgeir%27s_House.md) is around)
- *"I think the dragons are here to test us, and make sure we're still true Nords."*
- *"I was a barmaid at the [Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) when I met my husband, rest his soul. Wasn't for him, I'd still be working there!"*

- **Where can I learn more about magic?**
- *"Nords don't care much for magic. The [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) is about the only place it's not looked down on. Otherwise you could talk to the [Jarl](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md)'s [pet wizard](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md) in Solitude."*

## Conversations
[Julienne Lylvieve](Skyrim_Julienne_Lylvieve.md) is currently learning about the innkeeper trade from Faida. They can be found having the following conversations:

- **Julienne**: *"Miss Faida, may I ask you a question?"*
- **Faida**: *"Of course, Julienne. What's on your mind?"*
- **Julienne**: *"What do you do when a customer can't afford to pay for a meal or a drink?"*
- **Faida**: *"Since my customers are mostly local, I just knock on their front doors the next day."*

- **Faida**: *"Mind you don't forget to sweep up, Julienne. The floor's looking a bit dusty."*
- **Julienne**: *"I won't forget. I'll do it after I get the dishes cleaned."*
- **Faida**: *"You've been working hard lately. You'll make a fine innkeeper someday."*
- **Julienne**: *"If the [Nine](Lore_Nine_Divines.md) are willing!"*

## Rumors
Back in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), [Minette Vinius](Skyrim_Minette_Vinius.md) may mention her in response to her father saying that running a bar isn't a lady's job:

- *"What about 'Auntie Faida' in Dragon Bridge? She runs a bar, and you said she's one of your favorite girls!"*

## Notes
- According to relationship data as seen in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), she's the cousin of [Corpulus Vinius](Skyrim_Corpulus_Vinius.md). Minette Vinius also refers to this fact in a scene.
- It seems that Faida was once meant to be a part of a love triangle with Julienne and removed Dragon Bridge resident, [Tasius Tragus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tasius_Tragus). Tasius and Faida are marked as lovers in the Creation Kit, with Julienne being marked as a secret lover. - Tasius' role was seemingly given to a man named Voltus Agallon, who in turn got replaced by both [Commander Maro](Skyrim_Commander_Maro.md) and [Gaius Maro](Skyrim_Gaius_Maro.md). Like Tasius, Gaius is also shown to be in a relationship with Faida, although they aren't marked as such in the files.
