[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-npc-Prisoner.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Prisoner.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Prisoner.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Prisoner.jpg) The default player character **Prisoner** is the default name for the player character. Their name, race, gender, and appearance are subject to change during [character creation](Skyrim_Character_Creation.md). The player character is later identified as the **[Dovahkiin](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md)**.

You will play as the default character in the [playroom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Playroom) area in [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md).

## Notes
- The game data also lists eight non-player-related NPCs named "Prisoner", all identified as Falmer Prisoners who were possibly associated with a cut quest in [Chillwind Depths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chillwind_Depths).
- The player's Base ID and Ref ID are the same as those of [the player-character](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Bendu_Olo) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.

- Additionally, Skyrim's player-character is named [Bendu Olo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bendu_Olo) in promotional footage and images, which has also been used in promotional footage and images as the player character's name in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Nerevarine)*, *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Bendu_Olo)*, and *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:The_Warrior)*.
- The form IDs `00000007` and `00000014` are the default form IDs used for the player character in all Gamebryo and Creation Engine titles since *Oblivion*.
