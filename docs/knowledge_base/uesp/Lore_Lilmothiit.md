# Lilmothiit


They founded the settlements of Lilmoth and [Blackrose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blackrose), and the southern coastal regions of the province were considered to be their realm. Lilmoth later came to be occupied by merchants and neighboring Argonian tribes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHo L-1)</sup> Some of the Lilmothiit ruins of Blackrose were later turned into [Blackrose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blackrose_Prison) prison in a single day by [Pelladil Direnni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pelladil_Direnni), who summoned an army of [stone atronachs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stone_Atronach)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-O-7)</sup> at the request of Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Versidue-Shaie) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3A-5)</sup>

For many generations, the Lilmothiit were in decline, and they eventually withdrew their presence on the coasts to the inland regions around Blackrose.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LAMQA-3)</sup> In the Second Era, the Lilmothiit were scarcely seen. Several scholars from High Rock believed the ones in Lilmoth had succumbed to the Knahaten Flu,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AHo L-1)</sup> leaving behind many ruins for archeologists to scour.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LCLAYQ-2)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), in the region of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), a kind of sleek paper kite, painted bright orange and featuring a real fox's tail was a common toy. It was named Lilmothiit Fox-Kite, after the vulpine folk.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Contraband L-8)</sup>
