[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Mount Assarnibibi is a mountain found in the region of [Molag Amur](Lore_Molag_Amur.md), on the island-district of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). It is found in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The mountain summit contains a special shrine of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md), and many pilgrims pay their respects here, as well as the nearby [Mount Kand](Lore_Mount_Kand.md). The scope of the entire mountain is unclear, although the cave, Dubdilla is found under the mountain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWMolag Bal.5D.5D-1)</sup> The entrance to this cave can be found as far north as the southern outskirts of [Vos](Lore_Vos.md).

In Tribunal mythology, it was here that [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) oversaw the ninety-nine lovers of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), who eventually gave birth to [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) the Warden, one of the Tribunal.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWTholer-2)</sup> During [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) outbreak in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), a [Master](Lore_Nerevarine.md) of the Tribunal Temple was sent to Mount Assarnibibi, after completing the Pilgrimage of the Four Corners. Archcanon Tholer Saryoni ordered the Master to acquire the [Ebony Mail](Lore_Ebony_Mail.md). After returning with the artifact, Tholer Saryoni promoted them to the role of Patriarch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWEbony Mail-3)</sup>
