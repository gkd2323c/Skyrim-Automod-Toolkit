Shalks are large, occasionally aggressive [beetles](Lore_Beetle.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> which attack by spitting fire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOShalk Ability-2)</sup> They also bite attackers with their sharp mandibles, and can pack a bit more power into the maneuver by producing flames as they bite.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOShalk Ability-2)</sup> They are predatory creatures and will hunt larger animals such as [guar](Lore_Guar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Despite the danger, shalks are herded by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md), as well as the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlander.md), for their prized meat, [resin](Lore_Shalk_Resin.md), and carapaces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TIEGTT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BGN-5)</sup>

Shalks are native to mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) near the base of [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md), as well as [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). They tend to congregate near lava,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOOJKO-6)</sup> and are known to emerge from volcanic vents in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md). Shalks can also be found in the firey hellscape of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup> Shalks are known to live deep underground in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) but are uncommon to see.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## Variants
The most common variety of Shalk possess orange and red patterns, matching their often volcanic habitats.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup> A species of Shalk found on Vvardenfell, known as **Fiery Shalks**,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-RJ-9)</sup> have black shells and are smaller.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MW-10)</sup> The ash beetle is a more deadly variation of shalk that stalks the ashlands. They burrow beneath the ash and wait for prey to wander by. Sightings are extremely rare.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> In appearance, they more closely resemble a grey [assassin beetle](Lore_Assassin_Beetle.md) than a typical shalk.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

There is a subspecies of shalk known as the Horned Behemoth-Shalk. They were the organic inspiration for [Dwarven Dynastors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwarven_Dynastor).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> [Shroom Beetles](Lore_Shroom_Beetle.md), another type of beetle native to Morrowind, are sometimes known as Shroom Shalks.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

[Shalk Fabricants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shalk_Fabricant) are biomechanical shalks engineered by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md). [Skein Beetles](Lore_Skein_Beetle.md), also known as Skein Shalks, are [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) imitations that are native to the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md).

## Uses
Shalks possess flammable glands that can be used for arson.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOEV-15)</sup> Their meat is edible, and can be used in stews.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TIEGTT-4)</sup> Shalk chitin can be ground up and used to make poultices that ease burn wounds.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ESOPoultice Shalk-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ESOPT-17)</sup> Bloated shalks can be milked to obtain a blue substance that is used in tattoo ink.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ESOShalk Milk-18)</sup>

Shalk resins are the tough, soluble substances extracted from shalk hides. Shalk resins are used as glues and stiffeners in manufacturing bonemold and chitin armors, they can also be hardened to create a number of household objects.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BGN-5)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Resin-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ESOSundial-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-ESONail Trimmer-21)</sup> Shalk resin can also be used in alchemical formulas.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-SPR-23)</sup> A salve of shalk resin and crushed [bergamot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bergamot) seeds is proven to repel parasites, such as [toe-borers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Toe-Borer), [grassfleas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grassflea), and [fleshbloats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fleshbloat).<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ESODOW-24)</sup>
