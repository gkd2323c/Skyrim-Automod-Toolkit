Geirmund's Epitaph
---
**Lord Geirmund**
Archmage of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)
Lord Battlemage to [King Harald](Lore_Harald.md)

Magus, keep thy vigil eternal. Serve now in death as you did in life. By these seals our realm preserve. From traitors three and their [charm of strife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gauldur_Amulet).
