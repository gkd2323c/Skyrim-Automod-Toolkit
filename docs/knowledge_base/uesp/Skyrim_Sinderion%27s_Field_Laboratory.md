[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-place-Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.jpg) Sinderion's Field Laboratory **Sinderion's Field Laboratory** is a small [Dwarven building](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) serving as [Sinderion's](Skyrim_Sinderion.md) alchemical foothold in the cavern. The laboratory can be found closest to the entrance to Blackreach from [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md). After descending the steps, you should be able to see the laboratory on the right.

## Related Quests
- **[A Return To Your Roots](Skyrim_A_Return_To_Your_Roots.md)**: Go where a [famous alchemist](Skyrim_Sinderion.md) could not in order to aid ground-breaking research.

## Interior
Sinderion's remains can be found in the room, still clutching his [field journal](Skyrim_Sinderion%27s_Field_Journal.md), his body riddled with dwarven arrows. This is where Sinderion eventually found himself after having been shown a sample of [crimson nirnroot](Skyrim_Crimson_Nirnroot.md) by a passing adventurer during his time in [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), as his journal reveals. Reading the journal (or collecting crimson nirnroot from the plant in the room) will trigger a [quest](Skyrim_A_Return_To_Your_Roots.md) to finish Sinderion's research.

Sinderion had set up a full research station in the laboratory, including an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md), a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), a rare-type [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md), a copy of the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[De Rerum Dirennis](Skyrim_De_Rerum_Dirennis.md)*, and thirty different [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), which are listed in the table below.

Other items of particular interest are a pair of [Leather Boots of Hauling](Skyrim_Leather_Boots_of_Hauling.md) and an [Attunement Sphere](Skyrim_Attunement_Sphere.md). There is also a knapsack, two chests, a [dwarven dagger](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md), three [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) (one filled lesser soul gem, one empty greater soul gem, and one random soul gem), and three potions, in addition to a typical range of Dwemer household items and scrap metal.

## Notes
- This can be an especially valuable location in [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) while exploring [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) since there is a usable bed, cooking station, and safe storage.

## Bugs
- Taking the [Attunement Sphere](Skyrim_Attunement_Sphere.md) from this lab may render it permanently stuck in your inventory if the quest [Discerning the Transmundane](Skyrim_Discerning_the_Transmundane.md) has been started. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. Once the quest is complete, all related items can be discarded normally.
- If you have [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) as a follower, after leaving the laboratory, she will not follow you anymore and will instead just stand in front of the laboratory. Waiting or speaking to her will not help, but if you use one of the lifts, she will be teleported back to you and follow you as normal. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Use `moveto player` when at least a few steps away from the laboratory's entrance.
