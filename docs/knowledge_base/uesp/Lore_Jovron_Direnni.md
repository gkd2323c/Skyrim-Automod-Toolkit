Jovron Direnni was one of the prominent members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md), and the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md) in service of the Empress [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup> and the first Imperial Battlemage since the times of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-2)</sup>

Jovron was known to prove a valuable ally to the Empress, assisting her in creating peace in a time of turmoil.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup>

He was a descendant and an ancestor of several other famous figures. Famous noblewoman, [Raven Direnni](Lore_Raven_Direnni.md) was his distant ancestor. He was a great great grandson of [Pelladil Direnni](Lore_Pelladil_Direnni.md) and a grandfather of [Medora Direnni](Lore_Medora_Direnni.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup>

Little is known of Jovron's fate after the times of Empress Katariah, but it is known that Empress [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md) appointed the Archmagister [Welloc](Lore_Welloc.md) as the second Imperial Battlemage since the times of the Tiber Septim during her reign, between [3E 320](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_320) and [3E 339](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_339).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RTSB-3)</sup>
