[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/DF-map-Ephesus.png/200px-DF-map-Ephesus.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ephesus.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ephesus.png) Map of Ephesus Ephesus is an landlocked polity in the wider [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It is a remote territory high in the western [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFEphesus-1)</sup> located in a general area with known volcanic activity.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFDev Map-3)</sup>

## History
When the March of Ephesus is founded is unknown, however, the region's name is associated with the [central region](Lore_Ephesus.md) of [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONEphesus-4)</sup> where the historic [Bangkorai Pass](Lore_Bangkorai_Pass.md) is located, the natural choke point between [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and Hammerfell.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLoad Pass-5)</sup> Ephesus' main demographic are the local [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), but many races and subcultures exist in the region, such as the [barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md), the [Giants](Lore_Giant.md), and the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) in their mountain strongholds.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-2)</sup> The region's main [vampiric](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline is from the [Khulari](Lore_Khulari.md) clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFEphesus-1)</sup>

By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the March of Ephesus was ruled by an unnamed Marquis and its patron deity was [Mara](Lore_Mara.md), who is represented through the [Benevolence of Mara](Lore_Benevolence_of_Mara.md). The region had only one city, that being the capital of the same name.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFEphesus-1)</sup> In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), at the time ruled by Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md) conquered all of the land in northern Hammerfell following the magical phenomenon that occurred over a couple days, the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md). Ephesus along with the rest of the southern Iliac Bay fell under Sentinel's banner.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWITW-6)</sup>

Another byproduct of the Warp was the [King of Worms](Lore_King_of_Worms.md)' ascension to godhood<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LOTE-7)</sup> and disappearance from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). With his former haunt, [Scourg Barrow](Lore_Scourg_Barrow.md) now abandoned, it fell onto the Ephesus [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) to monitor any activity from there.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-LRPPH-8)</sup> By the late years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), after a failed expedition of mages went to Scourg Barrow, an aura of death permiated from it, killing any kind of life on the Dragontail Mountains. Eventually, the outside world lost all contact with Ephesus.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-TPWOTW-9)</sup>

## The City of Ephesus
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/DF-map-Ephesus_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-map-Ephesus_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ephesus_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ephesus_(city).png) Ephesus city in the region [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/DF-place-Ephesus_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-place-Ephesus_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Ephesus_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Ephesus_(city).png) Ephesus (Daggerfall) Ephesus is the walled, capital city of the eponymous march, located close to its southern border. It is the only city in the region and is the only settlement to be fully serviced with several businesses and guilds.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFEphesus-1)</sup>
