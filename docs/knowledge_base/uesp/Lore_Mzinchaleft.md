[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Mzinchaleft is a [Dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) located high in the Hailstone Valley of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is located near [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md). The ruin contains one of the few Great Lifts to [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).

Several expeditions were conducted into the ruins in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). [Calcelmo of Markarth](Lore_Calcelmo_of_Markarth.md) led one such project, returning to [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) with several artifacts and a rare, intact Dwarven Spider.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ALJ-1)</sup> Afterward, a group of bandits took over the exposed stronghold, using it as their headquarters as they raided the nearby roads.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MJ-2)</sup> Dwemer scholar Maluril Ferano later stayed with the outlaws, paying for whatever artifacts they found within the ruin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWO-3)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Ferano believed Mzinchaleft held a dormant power he sought to control.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Maluril-4)</sup>

## Notes
- A portion of its name, Mzinchaleft, holds a translatable meaning. "Mzin" signifies "against," while "chal" is derived from the longer phrase "chal fahl," which, according to [Calcelmo's Stone](Lore_Calcelmo%27s_Stone.md), can be translated to "do not desire," although its accuracy may be imperfect.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TOCS-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dwemeris-6)</sup>
