Ami-El (or **Amiel**) was the third known [emperor](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). His reign saw the Empire's transformation into a theocracy under the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) in the aftermath of their coup d'etat, as well as the expulsion of the remaining [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) from [Cyrod](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NOFR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKTA-3)</sup>

## Biography
In [1E 358](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_358), the joint army of Ami-El and the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) attacked the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md) of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MGp-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-XLAH-5)</sup> In [1E 361](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_361), the Alessian Order staged a coup d'etat in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), which led to the transformation of the Empire into a theocracy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKTA-3)</sup> After the coup, the Alessians initiated the Ayleid Pogrom and the enforcement of the [Alessian Doctrines](Lore_Alessian_Order.md#The_Alessian_Doctrines).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKTA-3)</sup> Circa 1E 361, [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md), King of [Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md), swore fealty to Ami-El.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup> By [1E 371](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_371), the number of Ayleid vassal states had significantly dwindled amid the Ayleid Pogroms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKTA-3)</sup> In [1E 372](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_372), Ami-El issued an ultimatum that abolished the last remaining Ayleid city-states and expelled the Ayleid population from Cyrod, including those that sided with [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).﻿<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NOFR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKTA-3)</sup>
