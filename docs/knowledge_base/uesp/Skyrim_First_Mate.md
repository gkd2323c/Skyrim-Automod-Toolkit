[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-npc-First_Mate.jpg/200px-SR-npc-First_Mate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-First_Mate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-First_Mate.jpg) First Mate The **First Mate**, an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is the second-in-command of a gang of [corsairs](Skyrim_Corsair.md) aboard the [Dainty Sload](Skyrim_Dainty_Sload.md), a ship tied up southwest of [Solitude Lighthouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Solitude_Lighthouse). He spends all of his days aboard ship.

The first mate wears a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md#00086991) and a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md#00086993), and wields a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) of the best possible quality. He also carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dainty_Sload_Footlocker_Key) to his footlocker and between 20 and 56 gold coins
