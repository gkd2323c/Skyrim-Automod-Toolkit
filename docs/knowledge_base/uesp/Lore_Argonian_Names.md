This is a list of all the known **[Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) names**, compiled from the games.

## Obtaining Names
Generally Argonians utilize two types of names from different languages: [Jel](Lore_Jel.md) and Tamrielic.

### Jel Names
Chukka-sei is an Argonian traditional Naming day<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONKloxu-1)</sup>, which comes in the Argonian season of Hist-Dooka<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOA-2)</sup> (Second Seed). On that day, a young Saxhleel licks the [Hist Sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md) and receives their name.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KFETA-3)</sup> It is not clear whether the young Saxhleel's name is granted by their elder tribesman or by the Hist itself, but the name is definitely obtained through a Hist<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KFETA-3)</sup>, hence native-born (born and raised to maturity in Black Marsh) Argonians that had successfully passed their Chukka-Sei would receive their aborigine name in the native Argonian language of Jel. Most likely this name is what they reference to outsiders as their "Black Marsh name".

### Tamrielic Names
Obtaining a Tamrielic name for an Argonian may vary between individuals. Sometimes, the Tamrielic name is a direct translation of their Jel name. For instance, an Argonian named "Haj-Ei" may take on the Tamrielic name "Hides His Eyes". "Haj" in Jel roughly means "hidden", and "Ei" means "eye" or "eyes".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Hides His Eyes-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
