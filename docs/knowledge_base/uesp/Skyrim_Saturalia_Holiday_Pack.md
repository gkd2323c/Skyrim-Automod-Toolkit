## Official Summary
*Once known as a day of debauchery, Saturalia has since transformed into a time of gift giving and cheer. Celebrate the holidays with these festive Saturalia garments, and spread merriment across Skyrim on your very own reindeer! Created by Virginia "Steelfeathers" Smith (Outfit and mount obtained from Agrane Peryval, west of Dawnstar.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds an unmarked camp north of [Windward Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins), just outside [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). You will be directed to the camp upon installation by a short [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saturalia_Preparation), although there is no obligation to buy any of the new items. The camp is inhabited by [Agrane Peryval](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Agrane_Peryval), a merchant who sells various festive items. His stock includes multiple sets of Saturalia [clothing](Skyrim_Clothing.md) as well as a [reindeer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reindeer) mount. The reindeer will return to camp if you switch to a different mount.
