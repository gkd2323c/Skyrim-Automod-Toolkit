[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/ON-crown_store-Noweyr_Steed_and_Noweyr_Pony.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Noweyr_Steed_and_Noweyr_Pony.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Noweyr_Steed_and_Noweyr_Pony.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Noweyr_Steed_and_Noweyr_Pony.jpg) Noweyr Steed and Noweyr Pony Noweyr (also known as the **Realm of Noweyr**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-NP-1)</sup> is a plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). A tale intertwining Noweyr and the thief- [god](Lore_Gods.md) [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md) exists. It is said that Rajhin pilfered enchanted purple horses and ponies from somewhere within the realms of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). Whenever inquired about the exact origin of these magical creatures, his worshippers were known to playfully respond with a cryptic and whimsical answer, saying, "Noweyr!".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NP2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NS-3)</sup>

## Notes
- In early game file descriptions this plane was known as the "**Realm of T'Which**" and the steed was originally described as *"This Daedric companion, conjured by Psijic magic in shape of a glowing Horse, was summoned from the little-known and possibly imaginary Oblivion demi-plane of T'Which."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NS-3)</sup>
