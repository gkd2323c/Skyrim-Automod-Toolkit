[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Odar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Odar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Odar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Odar.jpg) Odar **Odar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who works as the cook at the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). While he is content to remain a cook, his humble profession allows him to overhear a great deal of rumors, including gossip about the public's lack of faith in [Jarl Elisif](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md).

He sleeps with the rest of the staff in the chambers behind the kitchen between 8pm and 6am. He always starts his morning with a two-hour breakfast before spending the rest of the day strolling around the kitchen. The only exception to this routine occurs upon completion of [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), which leads him to visit the courtyard outside the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) every day between 1pm and 4pm.

He wears a [chef's tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Tunic) with a matching [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chef%27s_Hat) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#00018801). He carries a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached, he will either talk about his profession or share some court gossip:

*"Jarls come and jarls go, but a good cook stays for a lifetime. And I'm a very, very good cook."* *"Most of the time, people just forget I'm even here. That's why I hear so much good gossip."* *"Everyone knows [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) wields the real power in Solitude. [Elisif](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md)? Ah, she's a figurehead. A puppet."* *"Folks at court won't admit it, but [Sybille Stentor](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md) scares us all to death."* *"Somethin' just ain't right about that Sybille. You know, I've never seen her eat a thing? That can't be natural."* When you finish your conversation, he will return to his work:

*"All right. Back to slicing, dicing and baking."* *"Back to my errands."*
