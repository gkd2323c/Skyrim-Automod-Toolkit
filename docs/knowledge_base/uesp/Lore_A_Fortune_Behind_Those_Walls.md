A Fortune Behind Those Walls *A treasure hunter's journal recording his discovery of Frandar Hunding's riches*
---
This pillar isn't just a cenotaph for [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md). It's where his ancestors *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* hid his treasures! Hunding was the greatest hero of Bhosek's people. Why he never tried to crack open the monument is beyond me.

I don't know much about stonework. I can't exactly ask around, can I? If Bhosek catches wind of what I'm doing, he'll take it all for himself.

But how hard can it be? Hit the stone with a pick until it crumbles, then plunder everything inside. I'm sure to find a fortune behind those walls!
