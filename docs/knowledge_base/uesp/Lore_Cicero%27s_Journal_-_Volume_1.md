Cicero's Journal - Volume 1 **by [Cicero](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Cicero)** *The personal chronicle of a Dark Brotherhood assassin*
---
**18th of Evening Star, [4E 186](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_186)**
As I begin this new phase of my life, I have decided to finally keep a journal. So much has happened to me thus far, both within the Brotherhood and without - when I think there is no record of what has transpired, it almost seems an affront to [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) himself. So I am determined to make amends.

Yes, the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) has its own scribes and chroniclers, but it is their solemn task to record those events deemed important to the organization as a whole. Let this volume serve as the personal record of one man, a lowly assassin who has pledged his blade and his life for the Dark Brotherhood.

**23rd of Evening Star, 4E 186**
I have arrived safely in the [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) Sanctuary, and have been greeted warmly by Rasha and the others. Indeed, the level of support and acceptance shown by my new family is rather overwhelming. For this Sanctuary knows suffering, knows sorrow, for the ghosts of Purification still haunt its halls. So, who better to understand the plight of a brother who has lost home and heart? Who better to comfort one whose Sanctuary is no more?

The [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md) Sanctuary may be gone, but my dearest brothers and sisters will live forever in my dreams, just as their souls live forever by the Dread Father's side.

**1st of Rain's Hand, 4E 187**
Completed the baroness contract. She died well. Her handmaiden, less so.

**12th of Rain's Hand, 4E 187**
Cheydinhal suits me. With the destruction or abandonment of the other Sanctuaries, our contracts are plentiful, as are our bonuses.

Still, we seem to be losing our footholds throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) at an alarming rate. There are rumors that the Black Hand is split on our continued direction. Some favor expansion, the others consolidation.

My personal feeling is that the Dark Brotherhood needs to, at the very least, maintain the illusion of being everywhere at once. It has become exceedingly difficult to fulfill (or even establish) contracts in provinces where we no longer have a physical presence, like [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). The more we ignore Tamriel, the more people lose faith in the Dark Brotherhood - our power, our services, our dedication to the [Void](Lore_Void.md).

**27th of Rain's Hand, 4E 187**
The Listener, [Alisanne Dupre](Lore_Alisanne_Dupre.md), has been visiting with us for several days, down from her private residence in [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md). She and Rasha had been discussing the possibility of re-opening the [Shadowscale](Lore_Shadowscales.md) training facility of [Archon](Lore_Archon.md), in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), but ultimately decided we lacked the resources to follow through with the plan.

**27th of Rain's Hand, 4E 187**
Completed the Arena contract. I ultimately decided to pose as a starstruck fan, and immediately got into the Grand Champion's good graces. While escorting the arrogant fool through the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md), I slashed his throat and left the corpse for the [bears](Lore_Bear.md).
