# Dawnguard Magic


## Blessings
- **[Blessing of Auriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blessing_of_Auriel)** — You are 10% more effective with [missile weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md#Archery).
- **[Blood of the Ancients](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blood_of_the_Ancients)** — Your [Vampiric Drain](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md) spell absorbs [Magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) and [Stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) from your victims for ***x*** days.
- **[Tainted Blood of the Ancients](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tainted_Blood_of_the_Ancients)** — Your Vampiric Drain spell absorbs Magicka and Stamina from your victims for 1 day, but at the cost of 30 less [health](Skyrim_Health.md).

## Dragon Shouts
- **[Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md)** — Coax both magical and mortal energies from your hapless opponent.
- **[Soul Tear](Skyrim_Soul_Tear.md)** — Your Thu'um cuts through flesh and shatters soul, commanding the will of the fallen.
- **[Summon Durnehviir](Skyrim_Summon_Durnehviir.md)** — [Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md)! Hear my Voice and come forth from the Soul Cairn. I summon you in my time of need.

## Spells

### Conjuration Spells
- **[Conjure Boneman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Boneman)** — Summons a [Boneman](Skyrim_Boneman.md) Archer from the Soul Cairn for 60 seconds wherever the caster is pointing.
- **[Conjure Mistman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Mistman)** — Summons a [Mistman](Skyrim_Mistman.md) from the Soul Cairn for 60 seconds wherever the caster is pointing.
- **[Conjure Wrathman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Wrathman)** — Summons a [Wrathman](Skyrim_Wrathman.md) from the Soul Cairn for 60 seconds wherever the caster is pointing.
- **[Summon Arvak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arvak)** — Summons [Arvak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvak) in the Soul Cairn or Tamriel wilderness to act as your steed.

### Restoration Spells
- **[Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead)** — Heals the undead target 75 points, but not the living, atronachs, or machines.
- **[Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing)** — Heals the undead target 10 points per second, but not the living, atronachs or machines.
- **[Stendarr's Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Aura)** — For 60 seconds, undead in melee range take 10 points sun damage per second.
- **[Sun Fire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Fire)** — Ball of sunlight that does 25 points of damage to undead.
- **[Vampire's Bane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Bane)** — Sunlight explosion that does 40 points of damage in a 15 foot radius to undead.

## Lycanthropy & Vampirism
- **[Lycanthropy](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md#Skill_Perks)** — A description of the added features of Lycanthropy.
- **[Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)** — A description of the new features added with the Vampire Lord form. - With the addition of Vampire Lords, Skyrim's NPCs will no longer react with hostility toward any regular vampires, only toward Vampire Lords.

If the player is a werewolf and becomes a Vampire Lord, the player's lycanthropy will be cured. Later, however, the player can give up being a Vampire Lord and return to being a werewolf if desired.
