[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-npc-Ghorza_gra-Bagol.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghorza_gra-Bagol.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghorza_gra-Bagol.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghorza_gra-Bagol.jpg) Ghorza gra-Bagol **Ghorza gra-Bagol** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Her brother is [Moth gro-Bagol](Skyrim_Moth_gro-Bagol.md). She can be found during the day at the blacksmith's workshop near [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md). If anything happens to her, her apprentice [Tacitus Sallustius](Skyrim_Tacitus_Sallustius.md) will take over her workshop.

If you [retrieve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skilled_Apprenticeship) a [book about smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Last_Scabbard_of_Akrash) for her, she will give you a free lesson in [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) and become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md). She will also make you [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs, granting you access to all [Orc strongholds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds), if you don't already have such access.

She wakes up at 8am and goes straight to the workshop with Tacitus. They work there until 8pm, when they return to [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) for a four-hour dinner. Ghorza then sleeps there at midnight in a room she shares with her brother.

If Ghorza is killed, her body will appear in Markarth's [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md). Her body appears in the second alcove on the right, in the top right coffin when entering from within [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md).

Ghorza wears a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron#0005b69f) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). She also carries a copy of "Last Scabbard of Akrash" once you complete her quest for it.

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"Need a new blade?"* *"Got an axe here. Good for cleaving up [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md)."* *"How about some armor?"* *"You'll want some thick armor if you're traveling the Reach alone."'* *"Don't bother with Tacitus. Useless."* *"You'd think an apprentice could forge a few hinges. Not Tacitus."* When spoken to:

**Where'd you learn to smith?** *"Grew up in a stronghold. Every Orc girl tended the forge at some point. But a girl has to grow up, and life in the stronghold asked for a lot in return, so I joined the Legion. Mastered my trade there."* **You grew up in a stronghold?** *"That's right. An Orc stronghold. Kept to ourselves. Lived as our ancestors would have wanted. Wasn't enough for me, though. I left. Didn't want to end up as the third wife of some lowly chieftain."* **I need training in Smithing.** *"It's an honorable craft. I'll show you what I can."* Upon ending conversation with her:

*"Let me know if you need something forged."* *"Back to making weapons."*
### Conversations
During your first visit to Markarth, you may hear her chiding Tacitus:

**Ghorza**: *"Tacitus? What is this?"*
**Tacitus**: *"Uh, a nail?"*
**Ghorza**: *"Is that what you call it? In the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) we would have called it 'useless.' Look how brittle the metal is!"*
**Tacitus**: *"I... I'm sorry. I just, well, the forge gets so hot and I thought...."*
**Ghorza**: *"You thought what? You would skip a step? Not heat the metal all the way through? Useless! Now do it again."*

Sometimes she can be found instructing Tacitus:

**Tacitus**: *"When will I learn how to forge weapons and armor? Isn't that my job?"*
**Ghorza**: *"Your job is to do what I say. And I say you have many nails and fittings to make before you even touch a blade. Now get to work."*

**Ghorza**: *"You strike your metal like a new mother giving her babe a light slap. Hit it harder."*
**Tacitus**: *"I'm sorry, Ghorza. I just... flinch, and uh...."*
**Ghorza**: *"If you can't pound the dusk out of your iron, it'll break when it's finished. Hit. It. Harder."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Skilled Apprenticeship
**What's wrong with your apprentice?** *"Tacitus? Boy's an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md). I keep showing him how to treat the iron, but he never gets it. In the Legion, the Imperial smiths used to read books when they couldn't set their hammers straight. Wisdom of the old, I guess. If you find a book called 'The Last Scabbard of Akrash,' could you bring it to me? Tacitus sure isn't learning by example."* **I don't have time. Sorry.** *"Fair enough."* **I'll bring one if I find it.** *"Thanks."* Once you've found the book she requested, you can give it to her:

**Here's the book you wanted.** *"Books. Don't know why the Imperials like these so much. This should help the boy, though, Malacath willing. Here, let me show you how an Orc learns to smith."* She will then give you a free training lesson in Smithing.

An earlier iteration of Ghorza's quest reward dialogue can be found in the game's Dialogue Views files. It notably uses **Mauloch** instead of Malacath. Mauloch is an alternate name for the Daedric Prince that's not commonly used:

## Bugs
- When you talk to her, there may be an option to invest. When you select it, the only available option is to cancel the investment. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.

- You can kill Ghorza to get the investment option through Tacitus instead.
- Alternatively, you can use the console command `set Perk Investor Markarth Blacksmith to 0`.
- Investing with Ghorza also grants you an extra 500 septims with [Moth gro-Bagol](Skyrim_Moth_gro-Bagol.md), who cannot be invested in the normal way. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. Investing in Ghorza's shop will no longer add to Moth's investment pool in addition to her own.
- Sometimes, Ghorza will not serve as a Smithing trainer.
- If you marry Ghorza and [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) a [child](Skyrim_Child.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, she cannot be asked how the children are doing as she has no recorded dialogue for it.
