[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-item-The_Pale_Blade.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Pale_Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Pale_Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Pale_Blade.jpg) The Pale Blade The Pale Blade is a sword rewarded to you for completing [The Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady.md). The sword's enchantment causes instantaneous [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), and also inflicts its target with [fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md) for thirty seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Pale Blade requires one [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the benefits of tempering being doubled by the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk.

The sword is not to be confused with the *Blade of the Pale*, which is the reward for becoming [Thane of the Pale](Skyrim_Thane_of_the_Pale.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady.md)**: Find out what happened in [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md).

## Notes
- You can take the blade back to Frostmere Crypt and place it on the pedestal to recharge it.
- With both ranks of [Augmented Frost](Skyrim_Augmented_Frost.md) and [Dukaan](Skyrim_Dukaan_(item).md)'s unique enchantment in effect, the blade can inflict fear up to level 28 humans and creatures, although the increase in numbers will not be shown on the weapon. The effected level can also be further increased by using fortify destruction potions.
- Illusion perks seem to have minimal to no effect on the blade's fear enchantment (needs further testing).
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can enter `player.additem 000F5D2E 1` into the console and let the game's level system randomly select which version of this weapon to add to your inventory.

## Bugs
- The stamina damage effect on this weapon is actually the standard effect, but the descriptions list it as double. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. The enchantments now match the descriptions.
