The Real Nerevar *A Telvanni retainer's informal history of Nerevar*
---
[This [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) retainer's informal history of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) lists no sources.]

When the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) followed [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), they were many warring clans, with no law or leader in common. One Dunmer warlord, Nerevar, had the ambition to rule all the Dunmer.

In that time, House [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) were great enchanters, so Nerevar went in secret to a Dwemer smith and asked for an enchanted ring that would help him. The ring gave its wearer great powers of persuasion; for safety, it was enchanted to instantly kill anyone who wore it except Nerevar. The ring was called [Moon-and-Star](Lore_Moon-and-Star.md), and it helped Nerevar unite the various clans into the [First Council](Lore_First_Council.md).

Later, however, disputes over religion divided the Council, with House Dwemer and [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md) on one side and all the other [Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) on the other. Dwemer and Dagoth invited [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) and [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) clans as allies, and held northwest Morrowind, while Nerevar mustered the other Houses and [nomad tribes](Lore_Ashlanders.md) and marched to meet the Dwemer-Dagoth-Westerner forces.

The armies met at [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), a Dwemer stronghold. The Dwemer were defeated, with great slaughter, and terrible sorceries were used, resulting in the utter extermination of House Dwemer, House Dagoth, and their allies. Nerevar was killed in the battle, and his ring lost, but Nerevar's alliance survives in Morrowind's ruling political institution, the [Grand Council](Lore_Grand_Council.md).
