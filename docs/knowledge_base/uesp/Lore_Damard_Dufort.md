[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/ON-npc-The_Ascendant_Magus.jpg/200px-ON-npc-The_Ascendant_Magus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Ascendant_Magus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-The_Ascendant_Magus.jpg) Count Damard Dufort as the Ascendant Magus Damard Dufort was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) count of [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md), who resided in Castle Navire in [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md). He was the brother of [Duchess Elea Dufort](Lore_Elea_Dufort.md) and also her court mage. [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) traveled to Castle Navire, investigating the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md), and questioned Dufort, if he had any information to share. Dufort expressed suspicion of the [druids](Lore_Druids.md) of High Isle, believing that the storm that caused ships to go missing was created by environmental magic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-POI-1)</sup> After Elea was abducted by her steward, The Vestige rescued Elea and it was revealed that Damard was secretly the **Ascendant Magus**, the second-in-command of the Order. Elea was shocked to learn that her own brother was involved with the Order.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCAM-2)</sup> Damard would later kidnap [Lady Arabelle Davaux](Lore_Arabelle_Davaux.md), with the Vestige rescuing Lady Arabelle and killing Dufort, halting the Ascendant Order's plans.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TAS-3)</sup> Elea later expressed regret over the loss of her brother, but she realized there was no other way.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ACFP-4)</sup>
