[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-place-Ritual_Stone.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ritual_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ritual_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ritual_Stone.jpg) The Ritual Stone **The Ritual Stone** is one of the thirteen [Standing Stones](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) scattered across Skyrim, located east of Whiterun. It is located to the east of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) by the roadside, above the entrance to [Graywinter Watch](Skyrim_Graywinter_Watch.md).

*"Once a day, those under the sign of The Ritual can reanimate nearby corpses to fight for them."*

Activating the Ritual Stone grants you [the power](Skyrim_Powers.md#The_Ritual_Stone) to [raise corpses](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) to fight for you for about one hour of in-game time. The power also allows you to raise some corpses that can usually not be reanimated, such as [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) or [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md). Horses cannot be raised with the Ritual Stone power. Bodies raised by the Ritual Stone do not turn to ash after the effect expires and there is no limit to the number of corpses you can reanimate.

## The Ritual Stone
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-place-The_Ritual_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Ritual_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-The_Ritual_Stone_(active).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-The_Ritual_Stone_(active).jpg.md) The Ritual Stone, when activated The stone is on a rock outcrop between the road leading east from [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md). A path leads from the road, around the outcrop as it climbs and passes under several squared off arches. At the top is a hostile, leveled [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) who will attack as soon as they are aware of you. They are standing beside the standing stone and will raise two [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) to help them with the fight, one of these is an archer and the other uses a one-handed [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) and [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md). A circle of candles surrounds the stone and a few paces to the southeast is a [blue mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower).

## Notes
- If you reanimate a corpse and make it follow you to a location with more corpses (or NPCs that you can kill), you can end up with a large number of reanimated followers.
- The Ritual Stone power spreads out in a sphere several feet wide around the player when cast, reanimating any npcs or creatures within its radius.
- The Ritual Stone power works on corpses up to level 75. This limit can be increased using the [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md) perk.
- Placing the Ritual Stone power inside the [Aetherial Crown](Skyrim_Aetherial_Crown.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> allows you multiple castings by removing and equipping the crown. This can be used to build potentially unlimited armies of undead.
- While the Ritual Stone power is active, the number of summons or raised creatures the player may have at a moment is temporarily increased by 100.
- The Ritual Stone power can be useful for accessing corpses that have fallen out of bounds.
- The Ritual Stone power can be useful for bringing additional followers into the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.
- With the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, you can find a [Conjure Undying Ghost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Undying_Ghost) spell tome lying on a rock next to the stone.
