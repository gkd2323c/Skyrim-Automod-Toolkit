*Alchemy description:* **Increase Heavy Armor skill by <mag> points for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases Heavy Armor skill by <mag> points.**
**Fortify Heavy Armor** increases your [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill](Skyrim_Skills.md).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Heavy Armor if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- **Most** varieties of Armor and Necklaces *[of the Knight](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Heavy_Armor)* - Includes the enchantments *of the Minor Knight*, *of the Noble Knight*, *of the Major Knight*, *of the Eminent Knight*, and *of the Peerless Knight*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Viriya's Charm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viriya%27s_Charm)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Her Hand Armor](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal.md)</sup>

## Notes
- When Fortify Heavy Armor is crafted via a potion, only the magnitude (how much your Heavy Armor skill is raised) is increased by Alchemy perks, skill level, and Fortify Alchemy apparel worn; the duration (60 seconds) never is increased.
- The Fortify Heavy Armor enchanting effect can be placed on Light Armor pieces even though they don't benefit from it.

## Bugs
- Several items with "Knight" enchantments actually [fortify](Skyrim_Fortify_Light_Armor.md) your [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill instead of your Heavy Armor skill. In particular: - Dragonscale Armor *of the Eminent Knight*
- Dragonscale Armor *of the Noble Knight*
- Dragonscale Armor *of the Peerless Knight*
- Elven Gilded Armor *of the Eminent Knight*
- Elven Gilded Armor *of the Noble Knight*
- Glass Armor *of the Eminent Knight*
- Glass Armor *of the Noble Knight* **Mod Notes**: Item names should read "Squire", not "Knight"
