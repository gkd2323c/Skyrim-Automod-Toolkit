[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/AR-place-Karnver_Falls.jpg/200px-AR-place-Karnver_Falls.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Karnver_Falls.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Karnver_Falls.jpg) Karnver Falls during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Karnver Falls (also spelled **Karrnver Falls**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> is a location found in central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), near the mouths of the [Irk](Lore_Irk_River.md) and [Tark](Lore_Tark_River.md) north of the city-state of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md).

Karrnver Falls has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Karnver Falls was an active settlement was ruled by Lord Blebem and had a rivalry with [Corten Mont](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cartmont).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Karnver Falls is neighbored by several settlements, including [Verkarth City](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md) to the north, [Riverpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverpoint) to the east, and [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
