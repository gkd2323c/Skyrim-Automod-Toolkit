[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/SR-npc-Moric_Sidrey.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Moric_Sidrey.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Moric_Sidrey.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Moric_Sidrey.jpg) Moric Sidrey **Moric Sidrey** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilant_of_Stendarr.md). He led a group of Vigilants in [Ruunvald](Skyrim_Ruunvald_Excavation.md) and kept a [journal series](Skyrim_Discovering_Ruunvald.md) on the excavation, and later became charmed by [Minorne](Skyrim_Minorne.md). After being charmed, he wrote a [scripture](Skyrim_Minorne_(book).md) about her, found in Ruunvald.

He wears either novice robes [of Restoration](Skyrim_Novice_Robes_of_Restoration.md) or [of Alteration](Skyrim_Novice_Robes_of_Alteration.md), a pair of leveled [heavy boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots_2), and a 50% chance of a pair of [heavy gauntlets](Skyrim_Armor.md#Gauntlets_2), both up to [steel plate](Skyrim_Steel_Plate.md) quality. He also wears an [amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md) and is equipped with a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md). He carries a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md) and two random [books](Skyrim_Books.md).
