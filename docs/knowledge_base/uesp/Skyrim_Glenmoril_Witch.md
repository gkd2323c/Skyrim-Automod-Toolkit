[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-creature-Glenmoril_Witch.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Glenmoril_Witch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Glenmoril_Witch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Glenmoril_Witch.jpg) A Glenmoril Witch The **Glenmoril Witches** are five unique [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) living in the [Glenmoril Coven](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Coven.md). During the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) quest [Blood's Honor](Skyrim_Blood%27s_Honor.md) you will be tasked to retrieve at least one of their [heads](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch_Head.md) for [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md). They all possess the same spells, which include Fast Healing, Fireball, Close Wounds and Firebolt. They drop typical hagraven loot including [hagraven feathers](Skyrim_Hagraven_Feathers.md) and [hagraven claws](Skyrim_Hagraven_Claw.md). They also drop their [heads](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch_Head.md), which when thrown into the Flame of the Harbinger in [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) allows a [Werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) to be cured of lycanthropy through the casting out of the [Wolf Spirit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wolf_Spirit) from their soul.
