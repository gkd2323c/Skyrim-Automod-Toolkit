*Alchemy description:* **Target is <mag>% weaker to poison for <dur> seconds.**
Weakness to Poison has to be applied prior to a dose of poison for the weakness to take effect. For example, if the enemy is not weakened and you use a poison with **Weakness to Poison** and damage, the damage will not be affected by the weakness that time but the enemy will be affected in later uses of the poison.

## Notes
- Weakness to Poison is sometimes amplified by other weakness effects, and sometimes it is the amplifier of other weakness effects: - When applying Weakness to Poison with a weapon attack, it amplifies Weakness to Poison, Weakness to Magic, Weakness to Frost, Weakness to Fire, and Weakness to Shock. It is in turn only amplified by itself.
- When the player character drinks a potion with Weakness to Poison as a side effect, it does not boost any other weakness effect. It is conversely amplified by Weakness to Magic poison and by itself.
- The best way to maximise destruction output with Weakness Poisons is therefore like this: 1) Apply Weakness to Poison poison(s) 2) Apply Weakness to Magic poison 3) Apply Weakness to Fire/Frost/Shock poison.
- Weakness to poison is one of the potion effects that isn't available in standard potions found in the game world (such as Weakness to Cold, Weakness to Magic, etc.).
