## Walkthrough

### Exterior
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt_02.jpg) The exterior campsite Two Forsworn, one a melee fighter and the other an archer, have made camp outside the cave entrance. The campsite features a [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), five bedrolls, and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md). Also in the area are two [Namira's rot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Rot) fungi, a [chickens' nest](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), and a [chicken](Skyrim_Chicken.md).

### Bruca's Leap Redoubt
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.jpg) The main chamber The cave interior is quite small, consisting of a curving passage leading down to a chamber with wooden scaffolding. The [Forsworn Briarheart](Skyrim_Forsworn_Briarheart.md) shaman and its melee fighter bodyguard will likely be immediately alerted to your presence. However, by sneaking, you may snipe at them from the window-like opening straight down from the entrance. An [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) is on the ledge nearby. At the bottom of the passage is a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) and a [Dwarven arrow](Skyrim_Dwarven_Arrow.md).

Lying on a table in the chamber is the corpse of a Forsworn fighter who was in the process of being transformed into a Briarheart, with his [heart](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md) and an iron dagger placed nearby. A [bear pelt](Skyrim_Bear_Pelt.md), a [cave bear pelt](Skyrim_Cave_Bear_Pelt.md), and two [cow hides](Skyrim_Cow_Hide.md) are scattered around, and an [ingredient barrel](Skyrim_Barrel.md) of uncommon quality is near the table. A bookshelf holds a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Tragedy in Black](Skyrim_A_Tragedy_in_Black.md)*, a few ingredients (including one sample each of [juniper berries](Skyrim_Juniper_Berries.md), [sabre cat tooth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Tooth), [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat), and [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap)), and an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) of uncommon quality. A random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) is in a bucket nearby. The boss chest is behind the bookshelf, but beware the [bear trap](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) in front of it.

A few fungi grow in the cave, including one [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), three Namira's rot, and one white cap.

## Bugs
- Bruca's Leap Redoubt isn't marked as a cave, so cave-based radiant quests won't target it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- The Forsworn members here may attack their own chickens. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. Both factions have been set to be friendly towards one another to prevent this from happening.
- The cave contains a goat hide you cannot interact with.
