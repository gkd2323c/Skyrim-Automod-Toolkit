Bantam Guar are small, docile creatures native to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), that are part of the [scuttler](Lore_Scuttler.md) family. They are also commonly called "ugly [chickens](Lore_Chicken.md)" due to their appearance and behavior, and like chickens, are raised for eggs and meat. Despite the name, they are not related to [guar](Lore_Guar.md). They are, however, related to [cliff racers](Lore_Cliff_Racer.md) and [cliff darters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cliff_Darter). Unlike their cousins, bantam guar cannot fly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MFPO-1)</sup>

Declared the cutest creature in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) by the Cult of the Ancestor Moth,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> it is only natural for plushies created in their image to be used as a toy for children.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOContraband-3)</sup> Aside from the common brown-colored bantam guar, variants exist. In the spice island of [Esroniet](Lore_Esroniet.md), the Hot Pepper Bantam Guar can be found, sporting a look fitting of the island's staple foods.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHot Pepper Bantam Guar-4)</sup> The discovery of a pale variety of bantam guar with icy blue eyes in the cold north has led some to speculate that this kind came from [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), however, the famed scholar Phrastus believes that these theories are unlikely.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONABG-5)</sup>

Bantam Guars not only inhabit Tamriel, but can be found in the realms of Oblivion, where they display traits reminiscent of the realms they dwell in. Take into account the contrasting appearances of the [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) and [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) bantam guars, with the former being cold and azure, and the latter having rustic and volcanic traits.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
