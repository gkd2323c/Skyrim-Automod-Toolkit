## Mackerel
The **Mackerel** is a type of fish.

- The **Pellitine Horse Mackerel** can be found in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) and [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md), off the coast of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).
- The **Preposterous Mackerel** can be found in the lakes of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Merringar
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Merringar.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Merringar.png) A merringar The **Merringar** is a rare type of fish that can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), roughly a week's voyage north from mainland [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are considered to be a delicacy. Local fishermen hunt them in schools, but harvesting them is a dangerous endeavor as their blood can attract [dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md). Simocles Quo, the Master of Cuisine in Emperor [Uriel VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md)'s [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md), nearly died in such an event.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRKR-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Lore](Lore_The_Red_Kitchen_Reader.md)

## Mud Hopper
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-creature-Mud_Hopper.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Mud_Hopper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Mud_Hopper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Mud_Hopper.jpg) A mud hopper **Mud Hoppers** are amphibious fish which can survive both out of water and on the ocean floor. They can be found along the shoreline of bodies of water or in swampy areas. The brown and green variety can be found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md), while the vibrantly colored subspecies can be found in southern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), such as in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) and [Southern Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), as well as in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mud_Hopper)

## Mudskipper
**Mudskippers** are a type of amphibious fish that live in pools of mud that are found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) fish for mudskippers using strange poles known as "Glooba Jikei" to stir deep mud up to the surface.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

- The **Fringed Mudskipper** can be found in the foul waters of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Muskie
The **Muskie** is a type of fish that can be found in rivers on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).

- The **Blue Muskie** is native to the rivers of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s [western holds](Lore_Western_Skyrim.md), namely [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), and the area once known as [Karthwatch](Lore_Karthwatch.md).
- The **Onkobra Muskie**, named for the [Onkobra River](Lore_Onkobra_River.md) can be found in the rivers of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
