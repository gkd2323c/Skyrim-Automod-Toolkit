[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/ON-place-Greymoor_Keep.jpg/200px-ON-place-Greymoor_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Greymoor_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Greymoor_Keep.jpg) Greymoor Keep **Greymoor Caverns** is an area within the underground realm of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), found beneath the holds of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md), and [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The ancient fortress of [Greymoor Keep](Lore_Greymoor_Keep.md) lies beneath the city of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Do R-1)</sup>

## History
Throughout history, Greymoor Keep has often been traded between the hands of many rulers. During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), it came into possession of [Rada al-Saran](Lore_Rada_al-Saran.md), a [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md) and leader of the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Do R-1)</sup>
