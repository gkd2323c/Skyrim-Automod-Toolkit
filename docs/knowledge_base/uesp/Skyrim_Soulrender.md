[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-item-Soulrender.jpg/200px-SR-item-Soulrender.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Soulrender.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Soulrender.jpg) Soulrender Soulrender is a [scimitar](Skyrim_Scimitar.md) which is one of the twin swords of the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) pirate captain [Haknir Death-Brand](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md). The sword can be found on the corpse of Haknir's ghost in [Gyldenhul Barrow](Skyrim_Gyldenhul_Barrow.md), after defeating him. It is identical to a normal scimitar, except that its base damage is two points higher. Its enchantment only triggers when dual-wielded with [Bloodscythe](Skyrim_Bloodscythe.md), and will [absorb](Skyrim_Absorb_Magicka.md) 15 points of [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) with a 24% chance to dispel the enemy's magical defenses. It can dispel [armor spells](Skyrim_Armor_(effect).md), [invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(effect).md), [wards](Skyrim_Ward.md), [cloak spells](Skyrim_Cloak.md) and [healing spells](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md). Soulrender can be tempered with an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), with the [Daedric Smithing perk](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) doubling its improvement.

## Bugs
- Soulrender cannot be placed on a weapon rack or weapon plaque. It can be placed on a weapon rack in your house, with the Hearthfire add-on. It may take a few tries, but it does work.
