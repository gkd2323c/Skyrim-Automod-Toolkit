[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-npc-Jawanan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jawanan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jawanan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Jawanan.jpg) Jawanan **Jawanan** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [fletcher](Skyrim_Fletcher_(class).md). He will only appear as the vendor in the [Fletcher](Skyrim_Fletcher_(place).md) shop in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) if [Fihada](Skyrim_Fihada.md), the original owner, dies.

Jawanan wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Solitude_Fletcher) to the store, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
He will greet you by saying:

*"Ah, yet another new face. I'm from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), you see. I can hardly keep track of all the people I've been meeting."* *"Always good to see a fellow traveler. I hope you've found the roads relatively safe. Free from dragons, at the very least..."* *"Fletching suits me. It's difficult, and sometimes tedious. But a well made arrow strikes forth like the fury of a god."* *"If you're looking for any archery or fletching supplies, you should see [Fihada](Skyrim_Fihada.md). I'm just the apprentice."* **You're a long way from home, then.** *"Aye, but I'll be here in Solitude for longer still. Fihada's skill as a fletcher drew me to this place. I wanted to learn. And there is still much of Skyrim I wish to see. I hear there is a city, [Wind-Helm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), that is nearly white with... What do you call it? Ah yes! Snow. And far to the west lies a ruin. [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md). Inside, there is said to be a wall, black as night. And it speaks! In the ancient Dragon tongue! There are wonders in this place I never dreamed imaginable."* **I've been traveling all over. Even been to Helgen...** *"Where [the Dragon](Skyrim_Alduin.md) [attacked](Skyrim_Unbound.md)? We've heard rumors of death and... and fire from the skies. But no real news."* **The rumors are all true.** *"Then we should soon be selling arrows by the bushel. A sword is no good against a dragon."* Should you bid farewell to him:

*"Stop back anytime."* *"Good to talk to a fellow traveler."*
### Conversations
He will sometimes talk to Fihada:

**Fihada**: *"How's it coming there, Jawanan?"*
**Jawanan**: *"Slow, but looking good, sir."*
**Fihada**: *"Slow's the only way to do it! Work too hard and you'll warp the wood."*

**Jawanan**: *"Fihada, where did you learn how to make bows so well?"*
**Fihada**: *"The same place I learned my charm and quick wits: I used to be an Imperial ranger, my boy!"*
**Jawanan**: *"Wish I could learn some of that charm. I'm just good with bows, not people."*
**Fihada**: *"You'd make a better Imperial ranger than me. I may have charmed the locals, but my fellow soldiers hated my waggling tongue."*
