[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg) Ring of Kynareth The Ring of Kynareth is a unique ring. It can be found while [fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md). If you wear the ring while fishing at temperate lakes and streams, it will trigger a rainstorm, which can be useful for catching certain types of [fish](Skyrim_Fishing_Items.md). It can also be used to trigger the [Fortify Barter](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md) buff from [Turn of the Seasons](Skyrim_Turn_of_the_Seasons.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md)</sup>.

The ring is a rare quality junk item, which has a 3% chance (8% chance with the [Dwarven Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Dwarven_Fishing_Rod.md)) of being caught when no fish is caught. See our [Fishing activity page](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Catch_Chances) for more details.
