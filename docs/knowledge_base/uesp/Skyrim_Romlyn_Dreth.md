[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-npc-Romlyn_Dreth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Romlyn_Dreth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Romlyn_Dreth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Romlyn_Dreth.jpg) Romlyn Dreth **Romlyn Dreth** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) working for the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md). He is secretly stealing [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) from the meadery, and sells bottles of it for 10 gold each. He will ask the player to deliver a [Black-Briar mead keg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead_Keg) to [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) in [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). Completing this task will add him as a potential [spouse](Skyrim_Marriage.md), and will also make low-level items in Black-Briar Meadery freely available (including many bottles of Black-Briar mead).

Romlyn wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He carries two different keys (see [bugs](#Bugs)), three [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). He only knows a single [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell, specifically [flames](Skyrim_Flames.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Bonds of Matrimony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bonds_of_Matrimony)**: Find someone in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md).
- **[Under the Table](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Under_the_Table)**: Deliver a smuggled Black-Briar Mead keg to the inn at [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

*"You look like you could use a drink. What do you say to some cheap mead?"* *"I'm selling the best mead in town for cheap. Interested?"* When spoken to:

**Do you work for the meadery?** *"Sure. How do you think I get my hands on the good stuff and offer it so cheap? You think old Maven is just giving it away to anyone? Good thing she makes so much money. Makes it easier for merchandise like this to fall through the cracks."* **What are you selling?** *"Nothing but the best. Black-Briar Mead... fresh from the vat. Best of all, you don't have to pay the ridiculous prices Maven charges. So what do you say to that?"* **Sure, I'll take one. (Pay 10 gold)** *"Here you go. Oh, and keep it quiet. This stuff isn't exactly easy to get a hold of at these prices, if you catch my drift."* **No thanks.** *"Suit yourself. If you change your mind, I'll be around."* His goodbyes:

*"A Dreth never forgets a friend."* *"Remember, keep this to yourself."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Under the Table
**You're selling cheap mead?** *"No, no friend. You've got it all wrong. I'm not selling cheap mead. I'm selling good mead for cheap... Black-Briar Mead. I sell cases of it for half of what the inns and taverns pay through the Meadery. All I need is someone to deliver it for me. If anyone saw me leave Riften, they'd get suspicious."* **What kind of delivery?** *"I need someone to take this small keg of Black-Briar Mead to Wilhelm at the Vilemyr Inn in Ivarstead. Drop it off and he'll trade you something for the delivery. Keep it as payment for the legwork. Oh, and if you bump into Indaryn from the meadery, keep your mouth shut. He'd rat us out in a second."* **All right, I'll do it.** *"Good. Now get going."* **No way. That sounds illegal.** *"What, did you suddenly grow a conscience? Suit yourself."* If approached before making the delivery:

*"I believe you have a delivery to make."* If you informed Indaryn:

*"A Dreth never forgets a traitor. You'll pay for this!"* If spoken to after making the delivery:

*"Wilhelm was pleased with the delivery, well done."*
## Conversations
While at the meadery, his fellow worker [Niluva Hlaalu](Skyrim_Niluva_Hlaalu.md) may talk to him about Indaryn's threats:

**Niluva**: *"Do you know what Indaryn told me? He said if I'm late one more time he'll kick me right out the door!"*
**Romlyn**: *"That s'wit. One day, he'll regret pushing all of us around."*
**Niluva**: *"What do you mean?"*
**Romlyn**: *"All I'm saying is Indaryn's going to push someone too far one day and they're going to push back... hard."*

Sometimes, his boss [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md) may try to confront him in [The Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md):

**Indaryn**: *"Romlyn, I've been meaning to speak to you. I did a count at the meadery and we're four cases short... again."*
**Romlyn**: *"What are you saying? You think I had something to do with that?"*
**Indaryn**: *"I'm not accusing you of anything. Yet."*
**Romlyn**: *"The Dreth family doesn't steal, Indaryn."*
**Indaryn**: *"For your sake, I hope you're right. Because if it is you, and [Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) finds out, the Dreth family is going to be one member smaller."*

At other times in the inn, Romlyn can be seen suggesting a plan to his boss:

**Romlyn**: *"So, what do you think of my plan. You in or out?"*
**Indaryn**: *"If you think I'd would make a move like that without asking Maven, you're out of your mind. Doing that would be a fatal mistake."*
**Romlyn**: *"Come on, think about it. She'd reward us!"*
**Indaryn**: *"If you want to go out to Whiterun and burn that meadery down, go right ahead. And when the guard traces it back here, and Maven is implicated, I'll be sure to tell her it was your idea."*

Romlyn may also be seen having some interesting conversations with [Keerava](Skyrim_Keerava.md):

**Romlyn**: *"Have I told you about [Valen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Valen_Dreth), my dear?"*
**Keerava**: *"I love when you spin that yarn. Gets better every time you add something new."*
**Romlyn**: *"He killed six Imperial Guards before they dragged him into the prisons."*
**Keerava**: *"Was this before or after the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) attacked the [Emperor](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) and Valen single-handedly fought them off? Just shut up and drink, Romlyn."*

## Notes
- He is a descendant of [Valen Dreth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Valen_Dreth), and is apparently prone to telling exaggerated stories about him.
- Romlyn will continue to sell discounted [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) should you choose to marry him.

## Bugs
- Romlyn carries two different keys; [Romlyn's house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Romlyn%27s_House_Key) and [key to Romlyn Dreth's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Romlyn_Dreth%27s_House). The first one unlocks the door to his house in Riften, while the second one doesn't actually open anything in the game. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. The useless key gets removed from his inventory.
- If you turn Romlyn in to Indaryn during Under the Table, he will keep working at the meadery and continue to sell his under-the-table Black-Briar Mead, despite saying *"A Dreth never forgets a traitor."* each time you talk to him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. Romlyn will be jailed if you turn him in and he will no longer offer to sell you any mead.
- If you turn in Romlyn to Indaryn during Under the Table, he won't be given prison clothing like all other prisoners once he's sent to jail. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
