It contains only one zone, *[Bleakcoast Cave](#Bleakcoast_Cave)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The exterior of the cave more than explains the name with its dreary landscape and wood debris. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) can be found to the east of the entrance on the shoreline, and a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) grows nearby to the south. Skeletal remains are strewn about the outside of the cave, evidence of a large carnivore inside.

### Bleakcoast Cave
The cave's circular main chamber holds two frost trolls and the grisly remains of the trolls' meals, but nothing of interest. A third frost troll is in the smaller chamber to the west. Here also are the corpses of a [novice conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Conjurer) and a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) with a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) on the ground beside the bandit. On the novice's corpse you'll find a copy of the [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Battle of Sancre Tor](Skyrim_Battle_of_Sancre_Tor.md)*. Next to him is an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) covered in blood. Another ancillary chamber is to the north. A [skeletal torso is suspended, upside down](Skyrim_Easter_Eggs.md#Star_Wars) from the ceiling with a random, enchanted [greatsword](Skyrim_Greatsword.md) lying underneath . A fourth frost troll is in the chamber ahead along with a dead [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) next to a medium coin purse.

## Notes
- South of the cave entrance, a lonely hunter's camp can be seen across the water. The camp contains two small tents, each containing an unowned bedroll; the left-hand tent also contains a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md). There is a large coin purse on the back of a covered cart and a wooden table with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), some [horker meat](Skyrim_Horker_Meat.md) and [tusks](Skyrim_Horker_Tusk.md), and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill book *[Rislav The Righteous](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rislav_The_Righteous)* on and around it. There is an unowned [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) and dead horker by the campfire.
- From the small camp, head down to the water, follow the coast to the east, and just as it passes a huge broken ice floe on your left, you should find the remains of the camp's two hunters surrounded by three or four horkers. One of the hunters is carrying [treasure map VIII](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treasure_Map_VIII), which directs you to [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). By now you will be southeast of Bleakcoast Cave.
- Another iron ore vein can be found on the northwest end of the island.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
