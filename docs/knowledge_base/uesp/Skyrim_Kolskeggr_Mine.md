[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-place-Kolskegger_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Kolskegger_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kolskegger_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kolskegger_Mine.jpg) Kolskeggr Mine **Kolskeggr Mine** is a small [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) [mine](Skyrim_Mines.md) and [settlement](Skyrim_Settlements.md) east-northeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) initially containing [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). The mine contains only one zone, *[Kolskeggr Mine](#Kolskeggr_Mine)*; the settlement's residence is detailed on a [separate page](Skyrim_Pavo%27s_House.md).

A Forsworn invasion caused the miners to flee to [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine), where you can obtain a [quest](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest).md) from [Skaggi Scar-Face](Skyrim_Skaggi_Scar-Face.md) to free the mine. After the quest is completed, [Pavo Attius](Skyrim_Pavo_Attius.md) will relocate to Kolskeggr Mine and [buy gold ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) from you.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The path to the entrance is up a dirt track starting just before you reach [Pavo's house](Skyrim_Pavo%27s_House.md) coming from Markarth and passing under an arch just before the entrance. There are three Forsworn stationed near the entrance, one by the entrance and the other two next to Pavo's house, once the quest has been completed you can also find a [Markarth guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Guard) by the arch, who may fight the Forsworn on your next visit. There is a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) with a [bucket](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bucket) beneath it, two [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) growing to the right of the entrance with a [shovel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shovel) next to them, and a [hanging moss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hanging_Moss) growing from the tree. With *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)*, a [stone quarry](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md) can be found between Pavo's house and the mine itself.

### Kolskeggr Mine
Upon entering there is a ramp leading down into the mine. You will pass various carts, barrels and crates. As the tunnel bears to the west you will see a crossroads in front of you. There is a Forsworn patrolling north-south across your path. Dealing with them is likely to alert most of the occupants of the mine including the boss. Up to two Forsworn including the boss will come from the northern tunnel to the right and another two will approach from the western tunnel ahead. This can be a difficult fight, as all Forsworn are leveled and will attack with a combination of melee, magic, and bows.

The southern tunnel is a dead end with a gold ore vein. The northern tunnel leads past a barrel with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and [garnet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garnet) to another three gold ore veins; there is a hole in the ceiling that can be used as a shortcut from deeper in the mine back to the exit. Back at the crossroads and taking the tunnel to the west, the tunnel turns to the north as you pass more barrels, crates, sacks, a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack), a rack with a pheasant hanging from it and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). Continuing along the tunnel, it opens out into a well lit cavern with a wooden structure in front of you. If you have remained undetected, you will find two Forsworn patrolling or mining, with one by the wooden structure and one below the structure. Next to the ramp up are two large shelving units; the first contains a [potion of strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Strength) and a leveled [stamina potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), and the second contains a knapsack. To the right of these are some more barrels, sacks and crates one of which has a bottle of alto wine on top. Farther along beside the structure is a cart with a canvas roof containing an open crate containing two [gold ingots](Skyrim_Gold_Ingot.md). Around this part of the cavern are a total of five gold ore veins.

Returning to the start of the wooden structure and following it up and to the east, you will pass a turning on your left (north) leading to a dead end containing two gold ore veins. Just past this is a gold ore vein on the north wall. Follow the path to the east to another Forsworn-patrolled junction; straight ahead leads to two more gold ore veins at another dead end and a turning south leads upwards to two gold ore veins on your left. Turning west you will pass a table containing two gold ingots, a loose gold coin and three gold ore samples. On your right immediately after the table is the seventeenth and final gold ore vein. This is where the boss, a [Forsworn Briarheart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Briarheart), will patrol. Just past this is the hole previously mentioned that can be used as a shortcut back to the exit.

## Notes
- This is the only active gold mine in Skyrim. It also contains more than four times the number of gold ore veins found at any other single location, at seventeen.
- It is recommended to leave the Forsworn Briarheart alive, as otherwise the mine's respawn time is 30 days rather than 10.
- An unused message known internally as `Freeform Kolskeggr To Do Message` is titled "Civil War To Do". The message reads *"The [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) activates Kolskeggr's Mine's destructible object at this time."* The destructible object mentioned here is a leftover of the larger vision for the Civil War, which would have had you sent on missions to sabotage resources at farms, mills, and mines.
