*A blast of fire that does 25 points of damage. Targets on fire take extra damage.* **Firebolt** is an apprentice level destruction spell that deals [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) and sets targets on fire, dealing 30% damage and scaling down quadratically over 1 second.

## Effects
- [Firebolt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), 25 pts for 0+1 sec
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Intense Flames Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 15 secs; if the [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames), raises damage to 31.25 at first rank or 37.5 at second rank
- [Aspect of Terror](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aspect_of_Terror), raises damage by 10 pts (15 if both ranks of Augmented Flames are unlocked first).

## Notes
- Like most Fire spells, it can be used to set off [Fire Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Rune) or [Oil Slicks](Skyrim_Traps.md#Oil_Slicks.2FOil_Lanterns) from a safe distance. Also, casting it or merely having it ready to cast in an area with [Flammable Gas](Skyrim_Traps.md#Flammable_Gas) will result in a massive explosion.
