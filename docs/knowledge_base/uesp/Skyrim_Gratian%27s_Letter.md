Gratian's Letter **by Rendellus Thandarian**
---
[Gratian](Skyrim_Gratian_Caerellius.md),

I would appreciate if you could help us with a situation we've encountered in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). It seems that the miners there have stumbled across some sort of ruins under the surface near shaft three, and we need your expertise to take a look. I could send someone else if you're busy, but I figured you'd want to go since I seem to recall that you're originally from [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). Let us know if you make any progress.

Rendellus Thandarian
