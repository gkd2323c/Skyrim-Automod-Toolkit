[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) A map of Hammerfell **High King** Amdashir was the ruler of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) during an unknown period of history, at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Little is known of his reign, except for his contacts with the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) nobility. At some point he received expensive napkin holders made of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) from one of the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) nobles from Colovia as a gift. It is unclear what kind of relationship High King Amdashir had with the Colovians.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Contraband-1)</sup>

Long after his reign,<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> his napkin holders were among the curios that could be found around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Seond Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Contraband-1)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1.](#note_nb1) | As of 2E 582, names of several High Kings and Queens preceeding King [Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md), who ruled the Hammerell during the discovery of Amdashir's napkin holders, were known. Amdashir was not among them, implying that his reign preceded the times of several of the important figures of the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWAA-2)</sup> It is also unknown whether or not he belonged to this dynasty. |
