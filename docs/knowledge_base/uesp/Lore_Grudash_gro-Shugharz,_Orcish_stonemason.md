Grudash gro-Shugharz, Orcish stonemason **by [Grudash gro-Shugharz](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Grudash_gro-Shugharz)** *An Orcish stonemason's thoughts on Breton and Redguard architecture*
---
[![ON-concept-Redguard tower.png](https://images.uesp.net/c/ce/ON-concept-Redguard_tower.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Redguard_tower.png)
- "They're even worse than the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md). Look at all this fancy stuff: engravings, gold highlights, fragile domes. Well, I guess when your city isn't constantly assaulted and destroyed by everyone on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), you can afford the luxury of making pretty buildings.

- I just don't see the point. We need to build quickly, and build strong. I was told I could learn a lot from the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md)' work. I guess their constructions are impressive in scale, and they do manage the heat just fine, but I don't really see the applications for us back home. If you meet any of their stonemasons, send 'em my way. Maybe they can prove they know something worthwhile."
