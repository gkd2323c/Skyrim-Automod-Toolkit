# Shatter-Shield Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.jpg/200px-SR-place-House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-House_of_Clan_Shatter-Shield.jpg) House of Clan Shatter-Shield [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg) Torbjorn Shatter-Shield; the clan's patriarch ca. 4E 201 The Shatter-Shield Clan is an ancient noble family of [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) from the city of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), with an expansive history from the city's shipping industry and relieved of work after living from generations of "old money." While they spearhead the operation, all the hard work and paperwork is done by their workers down at the harbor office and the Argonian Assemblage,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:552</sup> the latter of which has been held in great regard by their higher-ups.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSot GQ-2)</sup> The sigil of Clan Shatter-Shield is an enchanted iron warhammer called Aegisbane.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRGuard-3)</sup>

When the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) established their presence in Windhelm, they became direct competitors with the Shatter-Shield Clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:550</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [Torbjorn Shatter-Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield) was the family patriarch. The Shatter-Shield Office began to work with the [Blood Horkers](Lore_Blood_Horkers.md), a group of pirates from the inhospitable island, [Japhet's Folly](Lore_Japhet%27s_Folly.md) to attack EEC trading ships<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SAL-4)</sup> and halt shipment between [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). This arrangement ended when their island base was sacked by EEC mercenaries and their leader, Haldyn was killed.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRRise-5)</sup>

In the same year, the Shatter-Shield Clan had become an object of pity when one of their own, Friga Shatter-Shield was one of several victims murdered by the notorious killer, [the Butcher](Lore_Calixto_Corrium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:550</sup> Not long after that, their prized treasures including their family heirloom, Aegisbane was stolen from their home by the bandit lord Alain Dufont, who used a close friend of the clan to infiltrate their home.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> He later took shelter in the nearby ruined city-state, [Raldbthar](Lore_Raldbthar.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SRMuiri-7)</sup> At around the same time, the family was one of the few in Skyrim to possess one of the rare [Stones of Barenziah](Lore_Stone_of_Barenziah.md), which were in circulation following the crown's theft from [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The stones were eventually recovered by the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) and the crown was restored.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRStone-8)</sup>
