# Nature Magic

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/ON-render-Warden.jpg/200px-ON-render-Warden.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Warden.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Warden.jpg) A Warden using nature magic [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/LO-people-Jephre.jpg/200px-LO-people-Jephre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-people-Jephre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-people-Jephre.jpg) Jephre, God of the Forest, as depicted by the Altmer Nature Magic is the magical ability to control nature by harnessing the power of [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md). Nature magic came about through the sacrifice of [Jephre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) and the [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md) to create the laws and principles of nature.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESODringoth-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VOF-2)</sup> The natural world is also deemed under the domain of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), one of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) and goddess of the wind and air.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Nature magic has been used by [druids](Lore_Druids.md) to overcome their physical limitations,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSWC-5)</sup> shapeshift into various animals,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-LADCG-6)</sup> terraform the environment around them,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONSHV1-7)</sup> or even invoke natural disasters to consume their foes.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> One druid named [Belaigh](Lore_Belaigh.md) slew a giant sea monster called [the Molmor](Lore_Molmor.md) by transforming its own body into an island.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BATM-9)</sup> Druids are also animists<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONHIDS-10)</sup> who can call upon [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md) to aid them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONLOTB-11)</sup> One rather extreme case of nature spirits aiding druids occurred in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). By stealing the authority of the [Druid King](Lore_Druid_King.md), the [Ascendant Lord](Lore_Bacaro_Volorus.md) was able to gain the service of the [Spirit of Mount Firesong](Lore_Spirit_of_Mount_Firesong.md) and even merge with it to threaten the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) with volcanic destruction.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TIThrone-12)</sup> Some [Wyrd Covens](Lore_Wyrd_Covens.md) also draw their power from the [Earthbones](Lore_Ehlnofey.md), and revere nature and the elements.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> In addition, [Wardens](Lore_Wardens.md) are able to use nature magic to cause plants and fungi to grow rapidly. They can also call animals to come to their aid, and wield frost spells to combat their foes.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOWarden-14)</sup> [Greenspeakers](Lore_Greenspeakers.md) shape the environment into settlements such as villages and even cities with the use of their Pact Magic. They enhance the growth of [Graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md) trees and branches to form furniture, [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md), and even housing called Pod-Homes.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ONDorinlas-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-PME-16)</sup> Others such as [Reachman Shamans](Lore_Reachman_Shamans.md) or Daedra-worshipping Wyrd Covens may receive dark nature powers from [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md),<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-EGTBangkorai-17)</sup> or [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) like [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONLoad VW-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TGW-19)</sup> Wise Women of the [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) tribes also use magic grounded in the natural world.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-AWW-20)</sup> Others still may gain power over the wilderness through pacts with nature spirits such as the [Viridian Sentinel](Lore_Viridian_Sentinel.md),<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TVS-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-Demara-22)</sup> or were born with it such as the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-SB-23)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ONSumiril-24)</sup>
