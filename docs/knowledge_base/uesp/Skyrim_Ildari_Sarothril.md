[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-npc-Ildari_Sarothril.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ildari_Sarothril.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ildari_Sarothril.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ildari_Sarothril.jpg) Ildari Sarothril **Ildari Sarothril** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Conjurer) residing at [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md). She is a former apprentice of [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md), but now bears a grudge against him. Ildari is the one responsible for the ash spawn attacks on Tel Mithryn, as well as the withering of the tower. She wears [Telvanni robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Telvanni_Robes) with a [heartstone necklace](Skyrim_Heartstone_Necklace.md), and she carries a [Dwarven dagger](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md) and a [Staff of Lightning Bolts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Lightning_Bolts).

She can be a powerful opponent in battle, using the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md) and [Thunderbolt](Skyrim_Thunderbolt.md) against you, and she can also conjure a [flame](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), [frost](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md), or [storm](Skyrim_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md) atronach depending on her level. She improves her armor rating with the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spells [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), [Ironflesh](Skyrim_Ironflesh.md), and [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), all of which are strengthened by her [Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md) perks. She also uses the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md), and [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), as well as [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Old Friends](Skyrim_Old_Friends.md)**: Find the source of attacks on [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
She will shout out to taunt you:

- *"You've gone far enough. Neloth is a fool to think he could send some low life to finish me off."*
- *"Ugh! The heart stone protects me!"*
- *"So Neloth is afraid to face me himself!"*
- *"Pawn of Neloth!"*

## Notes
- She is immune to all forms of [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md).
- There is no other way to kill Ildari other than to rip her Heartstone out, as asked to by Neloth. As soon as she is put into the bleedout state, she becomes immune to all other forms of damage (melee weapons, archery, magic and unarmed attacks, as well as Shouts) other than choosing the option to "Rip Heart Out".

## Bugs
- Her Telvanni robes can be looted from her corpse. [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) wears a set as well, but this is the only player-obtainable version of them. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
- Upon putting Ildari in Bleedout, the player is given a perk that enables the prompt to "Rip Heart Out", and that adds a multiplier of 0.01 to the magnitude of Illusion effects on the player. This persists until the perk is removed upon killing Ildari. This notably makes the [Ring of Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Khajiit) and [Ring of the Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_the_Wind) very weak. <sup>**?**</sup> - The perk can be removed via `player.removeperk 0403d597`, it will be reacquired when Ildari is next put into Bleedout.
- When given the prompt to "Rip Heart Out", Ildari might die at the beginning of the long animation rather than at the end, so you end up holding no one's shoulder and not ripping out any visible heart, all while Ildari is dead on the floor.
