He wears green colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8). He is accompanied by his friend [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(child).md), who will sell you a waypoint to [Deep Folk Crossing](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md) for one septim.
