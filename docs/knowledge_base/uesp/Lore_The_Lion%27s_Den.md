[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Lion's Den (also known as **Steelhead Cleft**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Lion-1)</sup> is a mountain pass in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), between the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md) to the north and the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) to the south. Specifically, it directly branches eastern [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md), where modern-day [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md) is located to western [Rift Hold](Lore_Rift_Hold.md), on the Great Riften Road intersection near Honeystrand Hill.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRMap-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMap-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLion-4)</sup>

There are conflating stories of how the valley acquired its name. One story claims it was a historical breeding ground for local [mountain lions](Lore_Mountain_Lion.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITLD-5)</sup> but another comes from the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), when the [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) took it over.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Lion-1)</sup> Many caves exist across the mountain pass, such as Giant's Crush,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLion-4)</sup> Haemar's Shame,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRHaemar-6)</sup> and excavated tunnels created by the Lion Guard.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLion-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg/200px-MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Skyrim_Anthology_Map.jpg) The Lion's Den is the road east of Helgen and south of Ivarstead Locals of the Rift know the valley as a historical breeding ground for mountain lions that [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) of the late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) often hunted for pelts. Over the years, locals avoided the area in favor of harvesting furs from domestic livestock.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITLD-5)</sup>

In the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), a tribe of [Giants](Lore_Giant.md) took residence in the mountains nearby and were aggressively territorial. People characterized them as angry hermits and even built a gate to the valley's entrance to prevent anyone from entering the region.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ITLD-5)</sup> But with the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in full motion, the Lion Guard, the elite soldiers of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) built a forward camp in the Lion's Den. An [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) scouting party led by Thragof was captured and interrogated by the Covenant, while another party discovered the camp from afar. An [Agent](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the Pact assisted the second party by eliminating the three highest-ranking Lion Guard officers and helped Thragof rescue his soldiers and leave behind the traitor.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONValuable-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONPrisoner-8)</sup>

By the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the pass was a major road depicted on maps of the province<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRMap-2)</sup> and branched the eastern Falkreath intersection between [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) and the [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md), where Helgen is now located, and the Great Riften Road intersection south of [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md). At some point, an [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md) built a shack on the valley's east end, where the autumn forest meets the snowy trail.
