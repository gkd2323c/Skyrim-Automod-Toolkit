[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Wind_Scour_Temple.jpg) Wind Scour Temple Wind Scour Temple is a secret fortress located in the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Hidden beneath Hoon Ding's Watch, it was established as a sanctuary by the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) in ancient times and also was known to house a vast archive of accumulated knowledge. It also served as the tomb for the four greatest Grandmasters of the Dragonguard, [Ghelin-Brol](Lore_Ghelin-Brol.md), [Vashu-Pir](Lore_Vashu-Pir.md), [Jaiv-Yora](Lore_Jaiv-Yora.md), and [Ettiene Volusus](Lore_Ettiene_Volusus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRFB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSai-3)</sup> On his search for the fabled [Horn of Ja'darri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Horn_of_Ja%27darri), [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md) briefly visited the sanctuary in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESODL-2)</sup>
