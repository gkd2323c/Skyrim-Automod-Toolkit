[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-npc-Minorne.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Minorne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Minorne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Minorne.jpg) Minorne **Minorne** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) found in [Ruunvald Temple](Skyrim_Ruunvald_Temple.md). She charmed the temple's leader [Moric Sidrey](Skyrim_Moric_Sidrey.md) and the [Vigilants](Skyrim_Charmed_Vigilant.md) within into worshipping her as a god. She wields the [Staff of Ruunvald](Skyrim_Staff_of_Ruunvald.md). She must be killed in order to free Florentius.

## Related Quests
- **[Bolstering the Ranks](Skyrim_Bolstering_the_Ranks.md)**: Find [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md) and recruit him into the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

## Notes
- Minorne does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female High Elf face.
- If she is killed before Moric Sidrey and the Charmed Vigilant in the chamber with her, both will immediately also die.
