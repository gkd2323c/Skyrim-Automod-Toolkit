[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci.jpg) Proventus Avenicci **Proventus Avenicci** is the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) to [Jarl Balgruuf](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). He is also the father of [Adrianne Avenicci](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adrianne_Avenicci), from whom he allegedly takes advice. He can be found in [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) and, when he is not with the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), will usually be found sitting down at a table at the back part of the Great Porch, or eating in the large tables in the main part of Dragonsreach. He will sell you a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) called [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md) in the city for 5,000 gold. He will also sell upgrades to the house, but will only do so if he is inside Dragonsreach. The dialogue option to purchase housing upgrades will not be available if he is on the Great Porch or in the Dragonsreach Jarl's Quarters. Proventus will also sell you [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup> for 7,500 gold at any time.

If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over Whiterun, he will move to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). There he can be found in the basement of the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md), but he will not sell any property or upgrades. These options are moved to [Brill](Skyrim_Brill.md), the steward who replaces him. He can be found in the Great Porch area of Dragonsreach.

Proventus wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8715) along with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Dialogue
His greetings can be:

*"I serve Jarl Baalgruuf * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * as steward."* *"The Jarl is, as you can imagine, very busy. Perhaps I can assist you?"* *"The task of Steward is more important than you realize. The Jarl often asks for my invaluable council."* *"I'm kept so busy, I don't get to see my daughter as often as I'd like. Perhaps you've met her? Adrianne? She owns the shop, [Warmaiden's](Skyrim_Warmaiden%27s.md)."* When he is in Whiterun, he will gladly answer your questions regarding the city:

**What's the city's history?** *"Ah, splendid! Our fine city has a proud history, and I'm happy to share what I know. The history of Whiterun begins with [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md), the hall of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md). For a long time, it was the only building on the mountain. As time passed, a thriving community sprang up around Jorrvaskr, as was common with mead halls. Today, Whiterun is the jewel of Skyrim. I can say without boast that is the greatest city in all the holds."* **Tell me about the city's districts.** *"Well, there are three of them. Arranged in three tiers, as you may have noticed. The Plains District is so named because it's the closest of the three to the plains that surround the city. The inn and marketplace are found there. Most of the city's residents live in the Wind District, which is the middle of the three. Dragonsreach occupies the highest, which we call the Cloud District. From here, the Jarl can see all that goes on within his city."* **Why is the keep called Dragonsreach?** *"That is an old tale, but well known to those of us who serve in the keep. Long ago, there lived a mighty hero of the Nords. [Olaf One-Eye](Lore_Olaf_One-Eye.md). Olaf did battle with a fearsome dragon named [Numinex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Numinex). The struggle between Olaf and the dragon culminated in a mighty duel atop [Mount Anthor](Skyrim_Mount_Anthor.md), with Olaf the victor. Olaf returned in triumph to Whiterun. By his decree, the city's keep was rebuilt as a prison for Numinex. And so, from that time until this, our great keep has been called 'Dragonsreach.'"* Proventus may comment on a [dragon](Skyrim_Odahviing.md)'s [capture](Skyrim_The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie.md) atop Dragonsreach:

*"I'll be glad when that beast is gone from Dragonsreach. I never understood what the point of all this was."* If he and Balgruuf are exiled to the Blue Palace, he may tell you,

*"Be warned, the Jarl is in a foul mood since the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) overran Whiterun."* If you end conversation with him while he is in Whiterun:

*"Enjoy your visit to Dragonsreach."* Otherwise, he will say:

*"If there's any more I can help you with, just let me know."*

### [Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Proventus_Avenicci_02.jpg) *"If you'll excuse me, I'll return to my duties."* Upon first arriving at Dragonsreach, you will likely witness Proventus Avenicci arguing with Balgruuf:

**Proventus**: *"My lord. Please. This is no time for rash action. I just think we need more information before we act. I just..."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Who's this, then?"*

**Balgruuf**: *"What do you say now, Proventus? Shall we continue to trust in the strength of our walls? Against a dragon?"*
**Irileth**: *"My lord, we should send troops to [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md) at once. It's in the most immediate danger, if that dragon is lurking in the mountains..."*
**Proventus**: *"The [Jarl of Falkreath](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) will view that as a provocation! He'll assume we're preparing to join Ulfric's side and attack him."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Enough! Irileth, send a detachment to Riverwood at once."*
**Irileth**: *"Yes, my Jarl."*
**Proventus**: *"We should not..."*
**Balgruuf**: *"I'll not stand idly by while a dragon burns my hold and slaughters my people!"*
**Proventus**: *"If you'll excuse me, I'll return to my duties."*
**Balgruuf**: *"That would be best."*

### [The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md)
After asking Jarl Balgruuf about the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md), [Hrongar](Skyrim_Hrongar.md) will speak up and tell you more. However, an unenthusiastic Proventus will object to his claims:

**Hrongar**: *"Didn't you hear the thundering sound as you returned to Whiterun? That was the voice of the Greybeards, summoning you to High Hrothgar! This hasn't happened in ... centuries, at least. Not since [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) himself was summoned when he was still Talos of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md)!"*
**Proventus**: *"Hrongar, calm yourself. What does any of this Nord nonsense have to do with our friend here? Capable as he/she may be, I don't see any signs of him/her being this, what, 'Dragonborn.'"*
**Hrongar**: *"Nord nonsense?! Why you puffed-up ignorant... these are our sacred traditions that go back to the founding of the First Empire!"*
**Balgruuf**: *"Hrongar. Don't be so hard on Avenicci."*
**Proventus**: *"I meant no disrespect, of course. It's just that... what do these Greybeards want with him/her?"*
**Balgruuf**: *"That's the Greybeards' business, not ours."* (to you) *"Whatever happened when you killed that dragon, it revealed something in you, and the Greybeards heard it. If they think you're Dragonborn, who are we to argue? You'd better get up to High Hrothgar immediately. There's no refusing the summons of the Greybeards. It's a tremendous honor."*

He can then be spoken to and asked questions:

**Who are the Greybeards?** *"Near as I can tell, a bunch of ancient Nord hermits who live on top of a mountain. Never heard of them talking to anybody before. If they really do want to talk to you, you'd better go find out what they want. You never know."* **What's it mean to be Dragonborn?** *"The only thing I remember is the old story of Tiber Septim being called Dragonborn by the Nords, before they switched sides and joined the Empire. No doubt those Greybeards up at [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md) will talk your ear off about it."* **What do you know about [Shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md)?** *"Old Nord folk tales always talk about Shouting their enemies to pieces. I always thought it was just a myth. If you think you might be able to do that kind of thing, the Legion could certainly use your help. Go talk to the Greybeards, see what they can tell you."*
### [Message to Whiterun](Skyrim_Message_to_Whiterun_(Imperial).md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-quest-Message_to_Whiterun_%28Imperial%29.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Message_to_Whiterun_%28Imperial%29.jpg)](File_SR-quest-Message_to_Whiterun_(Imperial).jpg.md) [](File_SR-quest-Message_to_Whiterun_(Imperial).jpg.md) The court discussing the upcoming attack If you are a member of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md) will order you to give an important message to Balgruuf. Inform the jarl that Tullius wishes to help him defend against [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s attack on Whiterun.

**Balgruuf**: *"Proventus, what do you make of all this? If Ulfric were to attack Whiterun..."*
**Proventus**: *"As in all things, Lord, caution... I urge us to wait and see."*
**Irileth**: *"Prey waits."*
**Balgruuf**: *"I'm of a mind with Irileth. It's time to act."*
**Proventus**: *"You plan to march on [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md)?"*
**Balgruuf**: *"I'm not a fool, Proventus. I mean it's time to challenge Ulfric to face me as a man, or march his Stormcloaks up to the gates."*
**Proventus**: *"He'll do no such thing! A dagger in the back is all you could expect!"*
**Irileth**: *"He was rather straight forward with [Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md)."*
**Proventus**: *"Torygg? He simply walked up to the boy and murdered him!"*
**Irileth**: *"That 'boy' was High King of Skyrim."*
**Balgruuf**: *"I'm not the High King, but neither am I a boy. If Ulfric wants to challenge my rule in the old way, let him. Though I suspect he'll prefer to send his 'Stormcloaks' to do it for him."*
**Irileth**: *"True. He's already proven his personal strength. Now he seeks to prove his army's."*
**Proventus**: *"Then might I urge you to consider General Tullius's request? I mean, if you are bent on offending Jarl Ulfric..."*
**Irileth**: *"Ulfric is the one who has offended. But, Proventus has a point. Ulfric has made it clear. In his mind, to refuse his claim is to side with the Empire."*
**Proventus**: *"And what harm is there in letting a few legionnaires die in place of your own men?"*
**Balgruuf**: *"It seems cowardly."*
**Irileth**: *"Was it cowardly then to accept the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md)?"*
**Balgruuf**: *"This again?! That was different. Was I given a chance to object to the terms of the treaty? No. The Jarls weren't asked. We were told. And we had to like it."*
**Proventus**: *"The chests of gold didn't hurt."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Damnit! This isn't about gold!"*
**Irileth**: *"It's time to decide."*
**Proventus**: *"Lord, wait. Let us see if Ulfric is serious."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Oh, he's serious. But so am I."*
**Irileth**: *"Finally."*

Alternatively, if you have joined the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), Ulfric will tell you to deliver his axe to Balgruuf, who will discuss this with Proventus and Irileth as outlined above.

After the debate, Balgruuf will address you and ask that you deliver an axe to Ulfric. After this he will speak to Proventus:

**Balgruuf**: *"Proventus. Bring me my pen. And the good parchment."*
**Proventus**: *"Are we writing a letter, Lord?"*
**Balgruuf**: *"Yes, to General Tullius. I need to make a few things clear before I accept these Legionnaires of his."*

Proventus may attend [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md)'s party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). Though he seems to enjoy the gathering, he is nervous from being around so many important people.

**Enjoying the party, Proventus?** *"Oh, hello there. I didn't expect to see you here. Yes, I am indeed. Elenwen always serves the best wine at her parties. The food is excellent as well. So many important people from all across Skyrim."* **What do you do for a living?** *"I'm the Steward of Whiterun. I serve Jarl Balgruuf in Dragonsreach. *"Don't let me keep you."*
### [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>
With the [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, you will be directed to Proventus when you read the [*Homestead For Sale*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Homestead_For_Sale) notice in order to purchase [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md), but only if he is currently the steward of Whiterun. You will then be able to purchase "a homestead outside of the city" from him for 7,500 gold. Once you purchase the property, he will hand you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Tundra_Homestead) and say, *"Wonderful! Here is the key to your new home."*

## Conversations
While he is steward of Whiterun, Proventus may speak the other members of the court:

**Proventus**: *"My lord, our provisions are running dangerously low. Our stores of meat, wine and grain are all but depleted."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Purchase more supplies, then. That's your job as Steward, isn't it? Why do you trouble me with these details?"*
**Proventus**: *"My lord, since Ulfric Stormcloak's uprising, the cost of goods has doubled. I'll need more coin if I'm to provision us properly."*
**Balgruuf**: *"Our coffers are nearly empty. I can scarcely afford to pay the guards, and we need every one of them in these troubled times. You'll have to make do with what we have, Proventus."*
**Proventus**: *"Yes, my lord."*

**Hrongar**: *"Proventus, you must help me. We need to convince my brother to do more for the war effort."*
**Proventus**: *"What would you have me do, Hrongar? Your brother is the Jarl. You know I can't question his judgement."*
**Hrongar**: *"I'm not asking you to defy the Jarl, just to open his eyes. Use subtlety and suggestion to turn his thoughts to the war. You're his steward, he'll listen to you."*
**Proventus**: *"Leave me out of your intrigues, Hrongar. If you have something to say to the Jarl, say it with your own tongue."*

**Irileth**: *"I am concerned about the Jarl's safety. Too many of our guards have been redeployed to fight the Stormcloaks."*
**Proventus**: *"What would you have me do? If we do not send men to fight in the war, you'll find yourself guarding one of Ulfric's men."*
**Irileth**: *"And if an assassin slips past the night watch, we won't have a Jarl at all. Without Balgruuf's leadership, Whiterun will descend into chaos."*
**Proventus**: *"Nonsense. If Balgruuf falls, someone will take his place, for better or worse. In the meantime, we have a war to win. And win we must."*
