[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ysgramor.jpg) Ysgramor **Ysgramor**, "the harbinger of us all", was an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) who came to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) before recorded history as a refugee fleeing civil war in Atmora. He is generally regarded as the first human ruler, and king, of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Ysgramor.md).

During the events of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md), Ysgramor has long since died and his soul resides in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md). His legacy is purportedly carried on by the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md), a group of nonpartisan mercenaries based in the city of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) who are renowned as impartial arbiters on matters of honor. During the [main quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md), you travel to Sovngarde to end [Alduin's](Skyrim_Alduin.md) reign of terror. When you first enter the [Hall of Valor](Skyrim_Hall_of_Valor.md) during the quest Ysgramor will greet you.

Ysgramor wears a full set of [ancient Nord armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Armor.md), except the [helmet](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Helmet.md), and carries his legendary axe on his back.

## Related Quests
- **[Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(quest).md)**: Seek out [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) within [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
He greets you with:

- *"Welcome, Dragonborn! Our door has stood empty since Alduin first set his soul-snare here. By Shor's command we sheathed our blades and ventured not the vale's dark mist. But three await your word to loose their fury upon the perilous foe. [Gormlaith the fearless](Skyrim_Gormlaith_Golden-Hilt.md), glad-hearted in battle; [Hakon the valiant](Skyrim_Hakon_One-Eye.md), heavy-handed warrior; [Felldir the Old](Skyrim_Felldir_the_Old.md), far-seeing and grim."*

Approaching him again before completing the quest will result in either his first greeting or:

- *"The three await your word to loose their fury upon the perilous foe."*

Additionally, if you have completed the Companions questline he may also say when approached:

- *"Do not fail [Kodlak](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md). He's earned his place here, and does not deserve to fall prey to Alduin's insatiable hunger."*

## Notes
- Even if you have Wuuthrad, Ysgramor always has it with him as well.
- Ysgramor is portrayed as a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md), but according to lore, he is actually an [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md).
- He is slightly taller than other Nords.
