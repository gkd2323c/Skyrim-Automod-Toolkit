# Savilla's Stone

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/OB-item-Savilla%27s_Stone.png/200px-OB-item-Savilla%27s_Stone.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Savilla%27s_Stone.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Savilla%27s_Stone.png) Savilla's Stone Savilla's Stone is a large crystal ball with unique [scrying](Lore_Sight.md) properties. During the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) it was hidden in the catacombs of the [Temple of the Ancestor Moths](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md), guarded by the moth priest guardians. In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the stone was stolen from the temple on the orders of the [Gray Fox](Lore_Gray_Fox.md), who later used its scrying abilities to plan his grand heist in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), where an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) was stolen from the Imperial Library.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBGF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBBreaking-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBHeist-3)</sup> After the heist was successful, the stone ended up the in the guildhall of the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md).
