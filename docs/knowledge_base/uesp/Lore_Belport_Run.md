[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/ON-place-Belport_Run.jpg/200px-ON-place-Belport_Run.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Belport_Run.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Belport_Run.jpg) Belport Run circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/AR-place-Belport_Run.jpg/200px-AR-place-Belport_Run.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Belport_Run.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Belport_Run.jpg) Belport Run during the Imperial Simulacrum Belport Run is a settlement located on a small island northeast of the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), within the [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md), the body of water between two major islands of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). It neighbored [Karndar Watch](Lore_Karndar_Watch.md), which was located across the sea from Belport Run, on the Summerset Isle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

The island on which the settlement of Belport Run was built appeared uninhabited and unsettled as of the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). It was home to [coral](Lore_Coral.md) structures and a small copse.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

The settlement was already marked on maps during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> placing its foundation date between 2E 582 and 2E 864.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup>

By the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Belport Run was an active settlement, being ruled by Count Kalallon. It had a rivalry with [West Guard](Lore_Dawnbreak.md), a town located in the northern parts of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
