## Sources
Three guaranteed Pygmy Sunfish can be found in a pool within [Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md) and can be caught without a fishing rod.

Three more can sometimes be found immediately north of [Steamcrag Camp](Skyrim_Steamcrag_Camp.md) in the lake or north of [The Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) in the nearby hot spring.

It can be caught at Heartwood Mill, west of Riften, by donning the Lucky Fishing Hat and using the Alik'ri Fishing Rod.
