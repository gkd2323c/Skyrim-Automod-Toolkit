# Ring of Kynareth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Kynareth.jpg) Ring of Kynareth The Ring of Kynareth is an [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) [silver](Lore_Silver.md) ring with a [sapphire](Lore_Sapphire.md) gemstone associated with the goddess [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md). It possesses the ability to [influence the weather](Lore_Weather_Magic.md), causing rainstorms to arise when worn while fishing in temperate lakes and streams.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ROK-1)</sup>

Very little is known of the Ring of Kynareth's origins and history. Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) it was found in a body of water in the [province](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Provinces) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) while fishing circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
