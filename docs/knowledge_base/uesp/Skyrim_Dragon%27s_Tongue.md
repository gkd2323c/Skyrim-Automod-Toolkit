[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-flora-Dragon%27s_Tongue.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Dragon%27s_Tongue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Dragon%27s_Tongue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Dragon%27s_Tongue.jpg) A dragon's tongue plant **Dragon's Tongue** is a conspicuous yellow-flowered plant found growing in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md)'s volcanic tundra. It is also sometimes grown as an ornamental plant.

It is one of only three (four with the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on and five with the [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)) ingredients with a [Fortify Two-handed](Skyrim_Fortify_Two-Handed.md) effect.

## Ingredients
44 guaranteed samples can be found in 26 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 5 in the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Dibella).md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 3 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 around [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 3 in [the Treasury House](Skyrim_The_Treasury_House.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 3 in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [Ansilvund Burial Chambers](Skyrim_Ansilvund_Burial_Chambers.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 2 in [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [the White Phial](Skyrim_The_White_Phial_(place).md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 centered 240 ft ENE of the [Twilight Sepulcher](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twilight_Sepulcher) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-66644&y=-77874)

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
