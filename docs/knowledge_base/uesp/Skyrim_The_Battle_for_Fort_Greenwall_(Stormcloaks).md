## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) at the [Rift Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.md).
2. Meet the Stormcloaks south of the fort.
3. Take the fort.

## Detailed Walkthrough
You will only receive this quest if you chose to give [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) to the Imperial Legion during the quest [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). Go to the [Rift Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Rift_Stormcloak_Camp.md) and talk to [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md). Galmar will tell you to go to [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md) and meet the Stormcloaks preparing for the attack. Meet with the men south of the fort, and then attack the fort. Your journal will keep track of the percentage of enemies left. Once all the Imperials are dead, you will have to head back to [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) for the next part in the [Liberation of Skyrim](Skyrim_Liberation_of_Skyrim.md) quest.

## Notes
- This quest will take the place of [The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md).
- Completing this quest will re-install [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) as [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of The Rift.
