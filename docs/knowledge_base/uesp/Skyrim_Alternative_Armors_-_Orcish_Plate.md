[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.jpg) Orcish Plate armor **Alternative Armors - Orcish Plate** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds a variant of [Orcish armor](Skyrim_Orcish.md) that is based on the [Orcish Plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Orcish_Plate) armor set from *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.

## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of heavy Orcish armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Smith 'n Slash" starts by reading "Guard's Dossier: Yakhtu gra-Orkulg" at the Guard Barracks inside the gate in Whiterun.)*

## Notes
- Both the name of the quest as well as the name of its added NPC are references to *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*. In the [Imperial City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Imperial_City), an Orc named [Urbul gro-Orkulg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Urbul_gro-Orkulg) ran a weaponry shop named [Slash 'N Smash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Slash_%27N_Smash).
