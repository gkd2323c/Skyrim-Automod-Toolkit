Chime of the Endless *Notes on a Khajiiti Claw-Dance chime*
---
This small chime protected Ujirra, adept of the [Ziz Kurah](Lore_Whispering_Claw.md), from the merciless attack of a [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) that killed all others at her adeptorium. By her rhythmic tapping of the chime, Ujirra composed a hymn to [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) that kept her safe through that horrible night. The hymn was unfortunately lost, but the chime survived.

The [college](Lore_Bards_College.md) restricts the use of this chime out of respect to our [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) compatriots.
