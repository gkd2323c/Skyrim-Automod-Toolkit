# Military Forts

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Greenwall.jpg) Fort Greenwall [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) This page lists all the **military forts** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). [Military camps](Skyrim_Military_Camps.md) are listed on a separate page.

The locations using the fort map marker can be roughly divided into three types. Forts affected by the **Civil War** appear in the first section. The occupants of these forts are initially bandits and mages. As the [Skyrim Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) progresses, either the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) or [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) will take over and occupy the fort.

Forts can serve as **settlements** for factions: the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md), the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, and the [Volkihar Vampire Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.

Other forts are set up as standard **dungeons**, with respawning enemies and a boss you must kill to clear the dungeon.
