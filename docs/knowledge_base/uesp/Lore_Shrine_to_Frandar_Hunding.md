Shrine to Frandar Hunding *A plaque dedicated to Frandar Hunding, famed Yokudan sword-singer*
---
In memory of [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md), who conceived the Way of the Sword and taught the [Ansei](Lore_Ansei.md) to summon the [shehai](Lore_Shehai.md) sword spirits.

With the Ansei, he defeated Emperor [Hira](Lore_Hira.md) and led his people to freedom in the distant land of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).
