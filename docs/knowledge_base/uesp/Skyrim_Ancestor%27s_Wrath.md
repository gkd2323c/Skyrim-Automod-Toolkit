*For 60 seconds, opponents that get too close take 8 points fire damage per second.* **Ancestor's Wrath** is a greater power possessed by all [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) that surrounds the caster in flames, causing [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) to any enemies within melee range and setting them on fire, dealing 20% of the immediate spell damage over 2s.

## Effects
- [Ancestor's Wrath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), 8 pts in 10 ft for 60 sec
- [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md): [Resist Freezing Water](Skyrim_Cold.md)

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames), raises damage to 10 at first rank or 12 at second rank.

## Notes
- This power is essentially identical to the spell [Flame Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Cloak), though with 0 casting cost and a once-a-day limitation.
- You can only have one Cloak spell of any kind active at a time. Casting a second will cancel the first.
- The spell will continue to the end of its duration even if you sheathe your weapons. It even operates underwater, making it an effective means of dealing with [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), since you can't use other attacks or spells while swimming.
- One strategy for using this spell is to cast it and then simply run in tight circles around your enemies. They may be unable to keep up and get a hit in, and the fire will slowly take them out.
- After the initial 60 seconds, there is a 2 second tapering period with reduced effect.
- When [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, this ability allows swimming in freezing water without taking damage or suffering additional [cold](Skyrim_Cold.md).
