**Conjure Mistman** is an adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a [Mistman](Skyrim_Mistman.md) to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure Mistman](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancy) Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md)

## Notes
- The Mistman is undead, and thus subject to spells that affect the undead.
