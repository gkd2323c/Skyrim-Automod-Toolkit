Mantra of Redemption *A hymn sung during Khajiiti exorcisms*
---
[Lost Cat](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md), you have wandered where the Moonlight cannot reach, but I am waiting in the Twilight, so turn your ears to me.

[Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md) lies in darkness, leading you astray. Close your mind to his truthless tune and turn your tail this way.

Let [Azurah's](Lore_Azurah.md) words fill your heart and drive the darkness out. My song will guide you home.

Step back through the [Lunar Lattice](Lore_Lunar_Lattice.md) and bathe in loving light.

Rejoice, Lost Cat, you are found.
