[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg) Shattered Shoals The Shattered Shoals are a series of shoals and islets off the western coast of [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md).

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#First_Aldmeri_Dominion) [Gold Fleet](Lore_Gold_Fleet.md) was struck by a hurricane near Khenarthi's Roost and ran aground on the shoals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RZD-2)</sup>
