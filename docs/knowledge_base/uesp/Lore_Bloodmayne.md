[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/ON-interior-Bloodmayne_Cave.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Bloodmayne_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Bloodmayne_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Bloodmayne_Cave.jpg) The Bloodmayne Cave ecosystem Bloodmayne is a forested area found in the [Nibenay Valley](Lore_Nibenay_Valley.md). It is specifically located on the northside of the [Larsius River](Lore_Larsius_River.md), but specifically between [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) and [Anutwyll](Lore_Anutwyll.md) to the southeast and [Wenyandawik](Lore_Wenyandawik.md) to the northwest. It lends its name to a cavern on the riverbed called **Bloodmayne Cave**, but in the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), it was also used for the historical **Castle Bloodmayne**, which sat in the dead center of the area, in the middle of a shallow marsh, surrounded by [willow](Lore_Willow.md) trees.

## Further Information
Bloodmayne Cave is known to host a wide variety of creatures from the nearby province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), such as [stranglers](Lore_Strangler.md) and [spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md), [assassin beetles](Lore_Assassin_Beetle.md), and even the fire-breathing [shalk](Lore_Shalk.md). These creatures tend to spill over into the wealds of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) and congregate in natural caverns and groves such as Bloodmayne.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Bloodmayne-1)</sup> These caves were largely untouched, even during the war-torn period in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), when the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) laid waste across central Cyrodiil. Among the abundance of creatures, one of the strongest was a powerful spriggan called Acanthia, the Chosen of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Whatever significance this title has is unknown. During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), there was a prison escape in Bravil and the fugitives took residence in Bloodmayne Cave. Burz gro-Khash, Guardian of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) in [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to deal with the threat, who came back successfully.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBFugitives-2)</sup>

When the Three Banners War took place in Cyrodiil, a large stronghold called Castle Bloodmayne sat at the top of the hill, above the river valley and northwest of Bravil. It was located in the general territory occupied by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) and it lies on the [Green Road](Lore_Green_Road.md). It came equipped with the usual amenities that these strongholds have, such as a lumbermill, a quarry, a farmstead, and even a waypoint on the [Transitus Network](Lore_Transitus_Network.md), which connected all over central Cyrodiil. Control over Castle Bloodmayne has wavered between the Aldmeri Dominion and the other alliances, the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) and the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), but the exact timeline in which they held control is imprecise.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONBloodmayne Castle-3)</sup> At some point later, Castle Bloodmayne was torn down and there was nothing left by the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).
