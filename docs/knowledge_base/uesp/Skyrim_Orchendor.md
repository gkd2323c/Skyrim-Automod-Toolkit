[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Orchendor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Orchendor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Orchendor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Orchendor.jpg) Orchendor **Orchendor** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [spellsword](Skyrim_Spellsword_(class).md) found at [Bthardamz Arcanex](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md). He is a former servant of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md) and was asked by the Daedric prince to gather the survivors of a plague attack on [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) and lead them to [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md). Inside the Dwemer ruin, Orchendor quickly lost his way and made a personal army out of the [Afflicted](Skyrim_Afflicted.md). Because of his betrayal, Peryite will ask you to kill Orchendor during the [related quest](Skyrim_The_Only_Cure.md).

He wields a leveled, enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) of the best possible quality and a randomly chosen [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md). He carries a wide array of loot, including leveled gems, and a boss-leveled amount of gold. He wears [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md) until level 8, when he may shift into enchanted robes. He also wears a leveled [ring of Destruction](Skyrim_Ring_of_Destruction.md) and a leveled necklace with a [healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) enchantment. His footwear is leveled as well and can be either [light](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor) or [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor). The light footwear ranges from a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) at level 1, to a pair of [dragonscale boots](Skyrim_Dragonscale_Boots.md) at level 46. The heavy footwear ranges from [iron boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md) at level 1 to [daedric boots](Skyrim_Daedric_Boots.md) at level 48. Lastly, he wears a pair of gauntlets with the exact same stats as his footwear.

He has a broad arsenal of [spells](#Spells) from various magic schools as well as a toxic vomit attack. Upon being hit between three to five times in a row, he will teleport a short distance away from you. When he comes out of the teleportation, there is a brief window where he is unable to attack or be attacked.

Orchendor has an innate 100% [magic resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md), a side-effect of his Teleportation ability, making him an extremely difficult opponent if you rely heavily on magic. This also makes it impossible for him to be affected by certain enchantments or effects, such as [soul trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md), without the use of a [Weakness to Magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md) poison.

## Notes
- He is one of the best candidates for [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) due to his level cap and abilities.
- The confusingly named "Frost Cloak Freeze" spell, used only by Orchendor, is actually a variant of [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) that does not carry the slow effect. It may have been considered too difficult for him to inflict slow and be able to teleport.
