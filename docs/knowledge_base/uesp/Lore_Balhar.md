Balhar is a location found in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). The [Devotees](Lore_Satakals.md) of [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md) have been seen rolling in the desert sand sidewinder-fashion in continuous, hundred-mile stretches, from Balhar all the way to the [Nohotogrha oasis](Lore_Nohotogrha.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG1.2FH-1)</sup>
