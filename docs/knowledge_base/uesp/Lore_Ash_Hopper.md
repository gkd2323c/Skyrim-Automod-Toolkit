[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Hopper.jpg) An Ash Hopper on Solstheim (Skyrim) Ash Hoppers are moderately large insects comparable to [grasshoppers](Lore_Grasshopper.md) found on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), southern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), and the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md). Ash Hoppers thrive in volcanic environments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Somehow, they even found their way into the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), and are able to survive there comfortably. They have since become quite common in the realm.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FOTD-2)</sup>

The Amber Ash Hopper is native to the upper Ashlands and the slopes of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) and can be taken in as low maintenance pets.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONAmber Ash Hopper-3)</sup> The Deep-Moss Ash Hopper is native to the fungal-forested uplands and the coastal marshes of southern Vvardenfell. They are the mascot bugs of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) due to their admiration of their green carapaces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONDeep Moss Ash Hopper-4)</sup>

The Ash Hoppers of Solstheim differ in size, their head shape is somewhat similar to that of a [Scrib](Lore_Scrib.md). Unlike their docile cousins on Vvardenfell, Solstheim's hoppers are hostile to travelers and they can carry [droops](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Droops).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Their meat and jelly is used in cooking and alchemy,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> and their chitin can be used in creating traditional [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) armor types. Their shells can also be made into lava-resistant overshoes.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
