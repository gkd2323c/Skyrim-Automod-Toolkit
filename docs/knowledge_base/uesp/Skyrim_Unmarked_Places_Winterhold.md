### Altar of Xrib
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-place-Altar_of_Xrib.jpg/200px-SR-place-Altar_of_Xrib.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_of_Xrib.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Altar_of_Xrib.jpg) Altar of Xrib | - POISnowy22 |
| --- |

On the peak just south of [Sightless Pit](Skyrim_Sightless_Pit.md), and close to its exit through the [Abandoned Cave](Skyrim_Abandoned_Cave.md), there is a large pyramid-like shrine with an altar atop it. A [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) is standing right at the altar and will turn hostile as you approach. Two more hostile skeletons will rise at the base of the shrine, where there are also more dead skeletons scattered around. On the altar, you can find the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Doors of Oblivion](Skyrim_The_Doors_of_Oblivion.md)*, as well as a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md), a [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), and some [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md). Leaned against the shrine are [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and a [banded iron shield](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Shield.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=99070&y=75749&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Avalanche Pass
[![SR-place-Avalanche Pass.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-place-Avalanche_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-Avalanche_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Avalanche_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Avalanche_Pass.jpg) - POISnowy23

The path southwest up the mountain to [Mount Anthor](Skyrim_Mount_Anthor.md) continues to the south, then east, looping back to the [Shrine of Azura](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Azura.md). It appears that an avalanche befall a party of [refugees](Skyrim_Refugee.md) in the open on this path. Their three bodies, along with a wrecked cart piled up with their belongings, can be found partially buried in the snow. Among the detritus are a few useful items: a coin purse, an apothecary's satchel, a leveled battleaxe, and a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) skill book *[Enchanter's Primer](Skyrim_Enchanter%27s_Primer.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=110606&y=62406&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dead Man's Treasure
File:SR-place-Dead Man's Treasure.jpg - Wilderness {45, 20}

Far northeast of the [Wreck of the Winter War](Skyrim_Wreck_of_the_Winter_War.md) lies a [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), six [gold ingots](Skyrim_Gold_Ingot.md), a random enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) and a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill book *[Beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(book).md)* all found beneath a flag and beside a crate on an isolated shore. Follow the shore north from the barrels until you have to enter the water to move around some icy rocks, follow them around to the right and the flag will on the shore behind. The dagger may have rolled down towards the water's edge. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=188237&y=83874&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dwarven Monument: Mount Anthor Summit
[![SR-place-POISnowy24.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-POISnowy24.jpg/200px-SR-place-POISnowy24.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POISnowy24.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-POISnowy24.jpg) - POISnowy24

After traveling through [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md), you can exit out to Ironbind Overlook. If you then follow the rough path east up the mountain, you will come across the ruins of a small Dwemer shrine, guarded by two [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md). Resting at the foot of the shrine is a leveled, enchanted [warhammer](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md), with two small, unlocked Dwarven jewelry boxes flanking it. Some potions can be found atop the taller pillar; you have to use [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) to access at this point. A [quicksilver](Skyrim_Quicksilver.md) ore deposit can be found just uphill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=84798&y=64637&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Fisherman's Camp: Slaughterfish Bay
[![SR-place-Fisherman's Camp Slaughterfish Bay.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-place-Fisherman%27s_Camp_Slaughterfish_Bay.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fisherman%27s_Camp_Slaughterfish_Bay.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fisherman%27s_Camp_Slaughterfish_Bay.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fisherman%27s_Camp_Slaughterfish_Bay.jpg) - POISnowy20

Northwest of the [Wreck of the Winter War](Skyrim_Wreck_of_the_Winter_War.md) are two abandoned tents, a campfire and a rowboat. On one crate is a [Fisherman's Journal](Skyrim_Fisherman%27s_Journal_(camp).md). The skeletons of the [former](Skyrim_Advard.md) [occupiers](Skyrim_Skeggr.md) can be found underwater, near a capsized boat, a few yards east of their camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=166600&y=71480&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Frozen Mammoth
[![SR-place-Frozen Mammoth.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-place-Frozen_Mammoth.jpg/200px-SR-place-Frozen_Mammoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frozen_Mammoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frozen_Mammoth.jpg) - POINorthern Coast13

To the west of [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md), there is a mammoth partly encapsulated in a block of ice, which can be looted for a mammoth snout. Its body is pierced with five [Dwarven arrows](Skyrim_Dwarven_Arrow.md), a random battleaxe, and a large bolt from a Dwemer ballista. There is an iron ore vein and an orichalcum ore vein nearby. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=81492&y=90637&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Hunter's Last Stand: Sea Shore of Ghosts
[![SR-place-Hunter's Last Stand Sea Shore of Ghosts.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SR-place-Hunter%27s_Last_Stand_Sea_Shore_of_Ghosts.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hunter%27s_Last_Stand_Sea_Shore_of_Ghosts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Last_Stand_Sea_Shore_of_Ghosts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Last_Stand_Sea_Shore_of_Ghosts.jpg) - POINorthern Coast06b

Southeast of [Bleakcoast Cave](Skyrim_Bleakcoast_Cave.md) there are two dead hunters, two dead horkers, and a live horker. One of the hunters carries [Treasure Map VIII](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_VIII.md). There are four ore veins in close proximity: three iron ore veins—one east, one west, and one south—and a quicksilver vein to the southeast. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Treasure+Map+VIII)

### Rundi's Mistake
[![SR-place-Rundi's Mistake.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-place-Rundi%27s_Mistake.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rundi%27s_Mistake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rundi%27s_Mistake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rundi%27s_Mistake.jpg) The body of [Rundi](Skyrim_Rundi.md), a deceased College of Winterhold student, is located east-southeast of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) lying on a circular stone altar surrounded by [frost runes](Skyrim_Elemental_Rune.md). There are some items on the altar itself, such as [Rundi's dagger](Skyrim_Rundi%27s_Dagger.md), a few alchemy ingredients, a [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), and a copy of the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) skill book *[Mannimarco, King of Worms](Skyrim_Mannimarco,_King_of_Worms.md)*. You can find a set of blue mage robes and a pair of boots on Rundi's corpse. A [Staff of Ice Spikes](Skyrim_Staff_of_Ice_Spikes.md) may also be found lying on the ground. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Rundi&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Trapped for Eternity
[![SR-place-Trapped for Eternity.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-place-Trapped_for_Eternity.jpg/200px-SR-place-Trapped_for_Eternity.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Trapped_for_Eternity.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Trapped_for_Eternity.jpg) - POINorthern Coast21

Approaching the seashore almost due east of Winterhold, are two brittle skeletons, both long dead. A triggered Bear Trap is the clue to how they came to rest here forever. An [Apothecary's Satchel](Skyrim_Containers.md#Apothecary.27s_Satchel) with ingredients and a copy of the skill book *[Mannimarco, King of Worms](Skyrim_Mannimarco,_King_of_Worms.md)* can be found next to one of the skeletons. ### Yisra's Beachside Combustion
