[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Alexia_Vici.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alexia_Vici.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alexia_Vici.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alexia_Vici.jpg) Alexia Vici **Alexia Vici** is an elderly [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md). She is only present during the [wedding](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md) of her daughter, [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md). She is also the aunt of [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md).

Alexia wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd).

## Related Quests
- **[Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md)**: Assassinate [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) at her own wedding.

## Quest-Related Events

### Bound Until Death
At the wedding, Alexia can be found in a political debate with [the groom's](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md) [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) father, [Vulwulf Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Vulwulf_Snow-Shod.md):

**Vulwulf**: *"Typical Imperial rot. You speak of Skyrim like you know the land, know its people. You're from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)! You know nothing about us! Nothing!"*
**Alexia**: *"Oh, I know enough. I know those trouble-making Stormcloaks refuse to submit to [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) authority. Such seditious behavior. Why it's treason!"*
**Vulwulf**: *"And when the elves marched into your beloved Cyrodiil, and everyone took up arms against them, wasn't that treason? Against the [Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)? Huh?"*
**Alexia**: *"Why that's not the same thing at all. The Dominion were invaders. Conquerors. We had to fight them, to preserve our own way of life."*
**Vulwulf**: *"My point exactly."* She is clearly unhappy with her daughter's marriage, as she can be found saying:

*"What my daughter sees in that... barbarian, I'll never know."* *"The thought of paying a dowry to that, that... Stormcloak turns my stomach."* *"My nephew, the Emperor. Too busy to attend the wedding of his beloved cousin. We won't stand for this slight, I assure you."* If spoken directly to:

**Having a good time?** *"I most certainly am not. I've just lost my daughter to a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) beast, and my nephew Titus is too busy playing Emperor to even show up."* When Vittoria Vici dies, Alexia can be found screaming:

*"My daughter! Dead! Noooooo!"*
