Zumog Phoom, **Lord of the Dark Arts**, was a necromancer who served [Queen Euraxia](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md) of [House Tharn](Lore_House_Tharn.md) as her court wizard during the [Euraxians'](Lore_Euraxians.md) attempted conquest of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), being the chief necromancer of his queen's gravecallers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CZP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ZPD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MQE-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ZPL-4)</sup> A key part of his scheme was to fully resurrect [Cadwell the Betrayer](Lore_Cadwell.md) (an evil Nedic knight from the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md)) and use him as an wartime ally—a special military operation which Phoom was in charge of conducting.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MQE-3)</sup>

## History
Zumog Phoom hails from northernmost climes of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Later, Phoom became have been a ranking member of the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) and a student of [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CZP-1)</sup> Sometime thereafter, Phoom proclaimed himself a "Lord of the Dark Arts."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CZP-1)</sup> Later Phoom served Euraxia Tharn and helped her during her take the city-state of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md);<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CZP-1)</sup> in exchange for providing an unyielding army of undead to the Queen, Phoom was able to have access unlimited supply of bodies with which to practice his craft.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ZPL-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Orcs typically adopt a gendered prefix before their last name ("gro-" or "gra-") outside strongholds, Zumog Phoom's name does not follow this standard.
- Zumog Phoom was originally meant to take on the role that [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md) does in *[Seasons of the Worm Cult](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Seasons_of_the_Worm_Cult)*, having returned from the dead. A cut book called [Zumog Phoom the Mighty!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Zumog_Phoom_the_Mighty!) is an example of this.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CZP-1)</sup>
