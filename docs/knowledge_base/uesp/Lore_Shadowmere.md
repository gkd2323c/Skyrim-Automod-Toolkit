# Shadowmere

Shadowmere is a mysterious [black horse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Horse) with glowing red eyes associated with the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md). The horse's gender is unclear; as it is inconsistently referred to as both male and female.

The horse is seemingly immortal, having lived for over two hundred years. It is very hard to kill the horse in combat, due to its incredible regeneration. If slain, Shadowmere will reappear within days. The horse is of a magical nature, and is known to rise from the ground when summoned. It is always decorated with a stylized Dark Brotherhood saddle.

Shadowmere was once owned by [Lucien Lachance](Lore_Lucien_Lachance.md), a Speaker for the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md). The horse served him well, and Lucien was fond of it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), following the third [Purification](Lore_Purification.md) in the history of the Dark Brotherhood, Lachance gave the horse to [his new Silencer](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) as a token of his trust and love.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Shadowmere had come into the possession of [Astrid](Lore_Astrid.md), the leader of the last remaining Dark Brotherhood sanctuary. She eventually gave the horse to a [new member](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) of the Brotherhood who had been appointed as Listener by the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md). Lachance, who served the Listener as a spectral assassin, continued to maintain a bond with the horse even in death.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
