[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-npc-Sonir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sonir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sonir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sonir.jpg) Sonir **Sonir** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) who can be hired to stay in [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md).

Sonir wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209A6) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209A5). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
After being hired, she will introduce herself to you:

- *"Good day to you. I am Sonir, the bard that your steward has hired. Please let me know if there's anything specific you want to hear."*

When rehired if you have dismissed her she will say:

- *"As you wish. We can consider this incident nothing more than an unfortunate misunderstanding."*

## Bugs
- Sometimes Sonir can get stuck in the fireplace if it is built. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) On PC, she can be unstuck using console. Type `prid xx019632`, Enter, then type `moveto player`.
- She can't be dismissed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.1, fixes this bug.
- The dialogue option with your [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) to hire Sonir may disappear if you purchase all other house improvements beforehand. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- Sonir is mistakenly set as part of Crime Faction Falkreath rather than Crime Faction Hjaalmarch.
