Baladas Demnevanni (full name **Serjo Telvanni Baladas Demnevanni**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MTTIL-1)</sup> was an ancient [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) Councilor who lived in [Gnisis](Lore_Gnisis.md) on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). Like his ancestor Revus Demnevanni,<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-ESOLive1-2)</sup> Baladas dwelled within the Velothi tower of Arvs-Drelen, held an interest in the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md). Baladas was said to have been at least 2000 years old.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MTTIL-1)</sup> and used his age as a justification for his refusal to pay Imperial taxes, claiming that he has been alive longer than the "empire of men", and the existence of Gnisis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWBaladas-3)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Gnisis)</sup>

Baladas Demnevanni was obsessed with uncovering the secrets of the Dwemer, including their technology and the circumstances of their disappearance, hence his nickname of **Klickety Klack**.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MTTIL-1)</sup> Despite being a member of House Telvanni, he cared little for their politics. Meanwhile, Councilor [Aryon](Lore_Aryon.md) was plotting to introduce many reforms to Telvanni ethics and practices. Despite Baladas' disinterest, Aryon sought to recruit Baladas to put his vote against the more conservative Councilors, including Archmagister [Gothren](Lore_Gothren.md). Aryon sent a Lawman to deliver his offer, and Baladas, despite being reclusive, agreed on the condition of the Lawman recovering three rare Dwemer books: *[Nchunak's Fire and Faith](Lore_Nchunak%27s_Fire_and_Faith.md)*, *[Antecedents of Dwemer Law](Lore_Antecedents_of_Dwemer_Law.md)*, and *[Chronicles of Nchuleft](Lore_Chronicles_of_Nchuleft.md)*.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWBD-4)</sup>

After becoming a councilor of House Telvanni, Baladas directed a member of Mouth rank to explore the depths of [Bthungthumz](Lore_Bthungthumz.md), a Dwemer ruin in the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md). The object of this mission was to retrieve the ring of [Dahrk Mezalf](Lore_Dahrk_Mezalf.md), a Dwarven spectre who was said to have worked as a smith under the great Dwemer inventor [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWDM-5)</sup>

However, Baladas's time as Councilor would be short. An unknown individual hated him enough to issue a writ of execution for the sum of 5,000 gold, requesting the Grandmaster of the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) to assassinate him. The request was fulfilled, bringing a swift end to Baladas' tenure.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWWBD-6)</sup>
