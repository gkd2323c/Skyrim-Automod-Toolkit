[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradlecrush_Rock.jpg) Cradlecrush Rock circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/ON-place-Cradlecrush.jpg/200px-ON-place-Cradlecrush.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cradlecrush.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cradlecrush.jpg) The Orc stronghold at Cradlecrush circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Cradlecrush is a location in the [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) region of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), located far southwest of the city of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), on the banks of the [White River](Lore_White_River.md).

Cradlecrush was the site of an ancient [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) stronghold, which existed until at least [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oo S-1)</sup> In that year, [Fildgor Orcthane](Lore_Fildgor.md), brother of [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md), High King of Eastern Skyrim, reappeared in Eastmarch and attempted to overthrow his brother. The Orcs of Cradlecrush allied themselves with Fildgor, and captured [mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) belonging to local [giants](Lore_Giant.md), to use them against [Fort Amol](Lore_Amol.md). Their plans were foiled by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who attacked the fort with the help of angry giants, and then killed the Orc chieftain, Lob the Cleaver. The remaining Orcs fled from the stronghold afterwards, and the giants settled there in their place.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), nothing remained of the stronghold, and the area became the site of a small giant camp, **Cradlecrush Rock**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SR-3)</sup>
