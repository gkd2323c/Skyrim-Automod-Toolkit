[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/LO-map-Morrowind_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Morrowind_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Map of Morrowind Ald Marak (also called **Ald Malak**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920v6-1)</sup> is a strategic fortress found on the shores of Lake Coronati, alongside its sister city, Ald Iuval. It is found in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Ald Marak is known to contain high sturdy walls, and deep waters from the Coronati, making it impossible to assault on foot en masse.

The battle over Ald Marak was a pivotal point in the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md). In [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920), Crown Prince [Juilek Cyrodiil](Lore_Juilek.md) commanded the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) after pushing the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dark_Elves.md) back into Morrowind. The Warrior-Poet, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) prepared for an impending attack at Lake Coronati, but because of faulty intelligence, Vivec's armies were positioned on another side of Lake Coronati, leaving Ald Marak open.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSo AM-2)</sup> The Legion used [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) magic to successfully infiltrate the area and takeover Ald Marak. Over in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), the people celebrated their victory over the Dark Elves, all the while in Ald Marak, Juilek Cyrodiil and his friend, [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md) held a meeting with Vivec. Juilek made an agreement with the Tribune, the Empire attained strategic border forts such as Ald Marak, in exchange for protection by the Imperial Navy and no hostilities from the Empire, as well as land in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920v6-1)</sup>
