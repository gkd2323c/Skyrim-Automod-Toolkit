[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Battle_Axe.jpg) Tsun's Battle Axe Tsun's Battle Axe is the primary weapon for the shield-thane of [Shor](Lore_Shor.md), [Tsun](Skyrim_Tsun.md). The weapon lacks any enchantment and is unobtainable through any means except the [console](Skyrim_Console.md). Even if you do add it to your inventory, the weapon is marked as unplayable and will not be usable.
