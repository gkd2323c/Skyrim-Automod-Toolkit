# Dark Seducer


They are added to *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)* by the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), and they only appear as summoned Daedra.

## Dark Seducer Types
There are two types of Dark Seducers, based on their preferred fighting style (melee fighters and archers).

### Melee Fighters
These Dark Seducers belong to the [Combat Warrior1H](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) NPC [class](Skyrim_Classes.md) and use one-handed weapons in battle. They wear a unique version of [Dark Seducer Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Seducer_Armor) available only to them and wield a [Dark Mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Mace), [Dark Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Sword), or [Dark War Axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_War_Axe).

### Archers
These Dark Seducers have the [Ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) NPC class and wield bows to fight from a distance. They wear the same unique Dark Seducer armor as their melee counterparts and use a [Dark Bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Bow).

They also carry a [Dark Dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Dagger), and a quiver of 40 [Dark Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Arrow).
