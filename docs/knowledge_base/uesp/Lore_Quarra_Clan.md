# Quarra Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/LG-cardart-Quarra_Clan_Bloodkin_02.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Quarra_Clan_Bloodkin_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Quarra_Clan_Bloodkin_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Quarra_Clan_Bloodkin_02.jpg) Volrina Quarra, matriarch of the Quarra Clan
- *"Above all clans, we stand alone! We have few limitations to our power, so great is our gift. Our dark powers make us the perfect predators. Those who live in the sunlight fear our name."* —Quarra Clan motto<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Quarra Dialogue-1)</sup>

The Quarra Clan is one of the three major [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines found in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). It was founded by the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [Volrina Quarra](Lore_Volrina_Quarra.md) sometime in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), after she went on a mission to destroy the [Berne Clan](Lore_Berne_Clan.md) for killing her brother.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOVQ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VN-3)</sup> The Warriors of the Quarra fear nothing, and with good reason. Of all the Vvardenfell clans, they are the strongest physically.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Quarra Dialogue-1)</sup> They are not subtle, and would rather slaughter an entire village than choose a few on which to feed. The Quarra were known to prey on outsiders who came by the cemetery near [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md) at night, until they were eradicated by mercenaries hired by the [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-In N-4)</sup> By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Clan Quarra was still led by Volrina Quarra and from the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin of Druscashti. They also had a presence in the many ancestral tombs around the island.

Some sources claim that Volrina Quarra was slain in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), as a vial of her blood was wanted by both Mistress Dratha of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> and the [Berne Clan](Lore_Berne_Clan.md);<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and that the [Aundae Clan](Lore_Aundae_Clan.md) then killed many of the remaining members of the clan.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Among [King Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md)'s changes to the traditional power structure of Morrowind circa [3E 429](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_429), it was suspected that there could be a revolutions in the shadows among vampires that sought to overturn the old ways of the Quarra.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-9)</sup>

## Notes
- During the development of *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*, there were plans for the Quarra to have a presence in [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). They would have been headquartered in Pinemoon Cave and led by a vampire named Jelkar.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Vamp Quests-10)</sup>
