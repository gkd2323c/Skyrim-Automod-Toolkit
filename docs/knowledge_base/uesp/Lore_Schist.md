# Schist

Schist is a type of rock used in [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) craftsmanship. It found use in Reachfolk art, particularly in the crafting of idols linked to their religious beliefs, including those associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). As of the year [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), finely crafted stone idols resembling the antlers of Hircine, the Lord of the Hunt, crafted from schist by the Reachfolk and adorned with bone inlays, were discovered throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RHOI-1)</sup>
