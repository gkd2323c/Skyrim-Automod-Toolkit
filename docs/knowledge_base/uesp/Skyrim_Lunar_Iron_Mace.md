[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-item-Lunar_Iron_Mace.jpg/200px-SR-item-Lunar_Iron_Mace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Iron_Mace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Iron_Mace.jpg) A Lunar Iron Mace The Lunar Iron Mace is a special [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) that can be occasionally found at [Silent Moons Camp](Skyrim_Silent_Moons_Camp.md). Its enchantment causes [light damage](Skyrim_Light_Damage.md) over one second on whoever is struck by the mace. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Lunar Iron Mace requires one [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
