An Unusual Alliance **by [Aicantar of Shimmerene](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Aicantar_of_Shimmerene), Sapiarch of Indoctrination** *An Aldmeri Dominion notice calling for unity*
---
Three allies whose people rarely agree on anything. Three races as diverse as any within the world. Three states at war for their very lives, but unable to reconcile their differences in moments of peace. More than allies, but less than friends.

And that must change.

Our [Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) is beset on many sides. The [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) and the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) attack us from the outside, but we have other enemies more deadly and more dangerous to our alliance than those external foes.

If our wartime bonds are to remain strong, we must forge relationships and alliances in peace. Our [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) hunters must provide food for our civilians as well as our soldiers. Our [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) traders must provide goods as well as weapons. And our [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) wizards must spend their magicka wisely to win the peace as well as [the war](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).

Our first step is to join together in celebration as one of our alliance members, the Bosmer, renews [their divine pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) with a joining between their two spiritual leaders, [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md) and the [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md). Let all members of the Aldmeri Dominion recognize this wedding and participate in the revels that follow.

Our best wishes to the Silvenar, the Green Lady, and the Bosmeri people. May this new generation know a strength in peace as well as war.
