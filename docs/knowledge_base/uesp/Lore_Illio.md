Illio was an [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) pilot and early historical explorer. He manned the ship *Pasquiniel*, which followed a [waystone](Lore_Waystone.md) south of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), in search of [Old Ehlnofey](Lore_Old_Ehlnofey.md). Given the direction of his ship, it is believed his journey would have taken him to the site of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md). While his ship never returned, his contemporary [Topal](Lore_Topal.md) went northeast instead and became legendary for mapping [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Fot N-1)</sup>
