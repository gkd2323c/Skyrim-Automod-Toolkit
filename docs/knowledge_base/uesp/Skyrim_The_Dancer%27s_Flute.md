The Dancer's Flute, also known as the Dancer's Pipe, is a flute on display in [Calixto's House of Curiosities](Skyrim_Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities.md). Its appearance is identical to a generic [flute](Skyrim_Flute.md). It weighs the same, but it is worth less.
