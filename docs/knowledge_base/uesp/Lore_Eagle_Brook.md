[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Eagle's Brook (also called **Eagle Brook**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a village located on the coastal highlands of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md), in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Eagle's Brook is the home of nearby [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md)'s artisans and craftsmen who serve the high nobility of the kingdom. The settlement also serves as a key port to the city-state, one that bridges it to the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) from the north. Because it is a port town, Eagle's Brook sees a regular import of [seafood](Lore_Breton_Cuisine.md#Meats_and_Fish) and prepares it daily.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTAC-2)</sup>

## History
Eagle's Brook was taken over by the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md) led by Lord Velian in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). The necromancers would sacrifice people on the docks, turning them into [zombie](Lore_Zombie.md) thralls. They would then transport these zombies to other areas across the region, such as [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) and [Cath Bedraud](Lore_Cath_Bedraud.md), to reinforce their numbers against the [Lion Guard](Lore_Lion_Guard.md) and [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMerien-3)</sup> Dame Clarique led the Lion Guard militia against the cult in Eagle's Brook and apprehended a former Red Rook bandit and prior resident of Eagle's Brook, Tamien Sellan. The bandit wanted to help his hometown by employing a spell called Merien's White Mask. Created by his father, the spell could be used to hide the surviving villagers. With the help of [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), the spell was unleashed. After the ritual, the Vestige managed to speak with Tamien's father, Merien Sellan, who was stuck in a pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) known as [Desolation's End](Lore_Desolation%27s_End.md), created by Lord Velian.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMask-4)</sup>

Merien infiltrated the pocket realm, hoping to collapse the plane and disrupt the ritual from within by sacrificing himself. Tamien wanted to save the village without losing his father, while Merien wanted the Vestige to defeat the Bloodthorn Cult before they coudl depart Eagle's Brook aboard boats laden with zombies. It is unknown which course of action the Vestige took, but Eagle's Brook was saved and Tamien Sellan left Glenumbra for good.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSource-5)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Eagle Brook was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Theodyn and had a rivalry with [Moonguard](Lore_Moonguard.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Eagle Brook was neighbored by several settlements, including [Kings Guard](Lore_Kings_Guard.md) to the south and [Meir Darguard](Lore_Meir_Darguard.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARMap-6)</sup>
