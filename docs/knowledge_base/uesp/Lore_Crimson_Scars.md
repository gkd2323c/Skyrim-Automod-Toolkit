# Crimson Scars

The Crimson Scars were a group of [vampiric](Lore_Vampirism.md) assassins who split off from the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) during the year [3E 421](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_421).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GJ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TET-2)</sup> Their name derived from the unusually vicious neck lacerations left by these assassins and their jagged blades.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBdownload-3)</sup>

They were founded by Dark Brotherhood member [Greywyn Blenwyth](Lore_Greywyn_Blenwyth.md), who, after receiving a "vision" from [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md), conceived an elaborate coup whereby he would infect the ranks of Dark Brotherhood with [Porphyric Hemophilia](Lore_Porphyric_Hemophilia.md), eventually creating enough [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) to overthrow the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md). Unfortunately for Blenwyth, one of the Brotherhood members he turned, a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) named Silarian, betrayed the Scars to the Black Hand. The Hand then ordered the second "[Purification](Lore_Purification.md)" in the history of the Dark Brotherhood, which destroyed the Crimson Scars, leaving Greywyn and [Rowley Eardwulf](Lore_Rowley_Eardwulf.md) as the only survivors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GJ-1)</sup>

Greywyn subsequently retreated to [Deepscorn Hollow](Lore_Deepscorn_Hollow.md) and attempted to restore it to its former glory as a new headquarters for the Crimson Scars. However, he ultimately abandoned this goal after receiving a second vision from Sithis indicating disapproval of Greywyn's vampirism.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GJ-1)</sup>
