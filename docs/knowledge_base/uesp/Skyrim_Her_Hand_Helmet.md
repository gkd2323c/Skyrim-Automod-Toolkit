[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-item-Her_Hand_Helmet.jpg/200px-SR-item-Her_Hand_Helmet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Helmet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Helmet.jpg) Her Hand Helmet Her Hand Helmet is the helmet of the [set of armor](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor_Set.md) used by the members of Almalexia's personal guard. The face mask incorporated into the design bears the visage of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md). Alongside this helmet, this set of equipment also includes [boots](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Boots.md), [cuirass](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor.md), and [gauntlets](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Gauntlets.md). This helmet has been enchanted to [fortify blocking](Skyrim_Fortify_Block.md), allowing for Her Hands to better safeguard their charge. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the helmet requires a piece of [Chitin Plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

There is also a version of this armor that does not have an enchantment already placed upon it, leaving its magical effects up to you.
