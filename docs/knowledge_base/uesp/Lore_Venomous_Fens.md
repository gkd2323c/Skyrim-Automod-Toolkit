[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/ON-map-Venomous_Fens.jpg/200px-ON-map-Venomous_Fens.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Venomous_Fens.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Venomous_Fens.jpg) Map of Venomous Fens circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Venomous Fens** is a region located in the southwestern portion of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) and is on the western border of the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) province. To the north of Fens is the [Reticulated Spine](Lore_Reticulated_Spine.md), to the east is the [Leafwater](Lore_Leafwater.md) region, and to the south is the entrance to the Blackwood Bog.

The town of [Hissmir](Lore_Hissmir.md) is located in the center of the region and serves as the central hub of the Fens. North of the town is [Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(place).md), a [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) village founded by [Lord Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(person).md), who was close to the late [Empress Tavia](Lore_Tavia.md), wife of [Emperor Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md). Northwest of Hissmir are the ancient ruins of Arx Corinium and the White Rose Prison, both of which were created by the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md). The [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of Loriasel houses the [Mnemic Egg](Lore_Mnemic_Egg.md).
