[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/SR-place-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-place-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg) Watchtower Barracks entrance **The Pit** is a ruined military fort in Falkreath Hold used as a fighting pit where you are challenged to fight beasts and fighters. It contains three zones: *[The Pit Dungeon](#The_Pit_Dungeon)*, *[The Pit](#The_Pit)* and *[Watchtower Barracks](#Watchtower_Barracks)*.

It can only be accessed if you choose the **Power** option during [A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md).

### The Pit Dungeon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit_Dungeon.jpg) The dungeon hallway After you choose the power option during [A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md) you will then pass out and awake in a dungeon only wearing ragged robes and ragged boots. Exit the cell and you can explore the hallway and other cells, one cell has a dead hunter in it and another has a dead Thalmor soldier with a full set of Elven light armor. You can also pickup a set of iron armor and a health potion on the table in the main hallway.

### The Pit
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-interior-The_Pit.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Pit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Pit.jpg) The Pit As you enter the arena, you will have to fight a Pit Fighter and a [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md). Once they have been killed, the two [sabre cats](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) will be released from their cages and you will have to kill them. The pit champion, [Neski Skull-Sabre](Skyrim_Neski_Skull-Sabre.md) will then enter the arena, kill her and you can then loot her body for the [Bittercup](Skyrim_Bittercup.md), [Grand Champion's Sword](Skyrim_Grand_Champion%27s_Sword.md), and the [Grand Champion's Helm](Skyrim_Grand_Champion%27s_Helm.md). She also carries the note [On the Bittercup](Skyrim_On_the_Bittercup.md).

There is a large number of dead bodies laying about the pit, these are; a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton_(dead).md), [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md), [reaver](Skyrim_Reaver.md), [master vampire](Skyrim_Master_Vampire.md), [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit_(dead).md), [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf_(dead).md), and the [corpse](Skyrim_Corpse.md) of an Imperial soldier. There are also two skeletons that can be looted as well.

There are 11 pit bandits on the upper level that cannot be reached that are watching the fight.

### Watchtower Barracks
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks.jpg) Your belongings [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Watchtower_Barracks_02.jpg) Pit Boss room You will then have to pull the lever next to the door in order to enter the Watchtower Barracks. Climb the stairs and you will have to enter the room on the left in order to kill the Pit Boss and the two Pit Bandits. Loot the pit boss for the [pit key](Skyrim_Pit_Key.md), which can be used to retrieve your belongings from the chest in the previous room with an iron door. There is also a [note](Skyrim_Pit_Fighter%27s_Note.md) next to the chest, with a flawless diamond, amethyst and an emerald on top of it.

You can then unlock the door to Skyrim, where you will be near the [Falkreath Watchtower](Skyrim_Falkreath_Watchtower.md).
