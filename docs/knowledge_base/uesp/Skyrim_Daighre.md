[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-npc-Daighre.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Daighre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daighre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daighre.jpg) Daighre **Daighre** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) living in [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine), just outside [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She is the mother of [Erith](Skyrim_Erith.md).

Daighre wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). Like most miners, she is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Daighre%27s_House) to [her house](Skyrim_Daighre%27s_House.md), as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine_(quest).md)**: Clear the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) from [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Kolskeggr Mine
When the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) attack her friends she will feel very sorry for them and during the quest will remark:

*"Poor [Pavo](Skyrim_Pavo_Attius.md) and [Gat](Skyrim_Gat_gro-Shargakh.md). To have lived through a Forsworn attack."* *"Another day, another settlement gets attacked. No place in the [Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) is safe from the Forsworn."*
## Dialogue
When approached she can say:

*"I'm the hardest worker in Left Hand. Better than any man."* *"Most women would rather die than dig, but I have a daughter to raise."* *"My Erith has her father's eyes. She's all I have left of him."*
## Conversations
