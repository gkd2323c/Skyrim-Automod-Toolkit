## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) in the [Tel Mithryn Apothecary](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.md).
2. Find the rare alchemy [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) she requests.
3. Return to Elynea.

## Detailed Walkthrough
After completing the quest [Healing a House](Skyrim_Healing_a_House.md), speak to [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) in the [Tel Mithryn Apothecary](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.md) and she'll request that you bring her a particular rare alchemy [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md).

The ingredient she requests can be one of the following: [briar heart](Skyrim_Briar_Heart.md), [daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md), [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md), [hagraven claw](Skyrim_Hagraven_Claw.md), [vampire dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust), or [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md). Once you have collected the ingredient, return to Elynea for your reward.

| Giant's Toe | *Hmm. I'm running out of giant toes. I'll pay extra for one.* | *A bit ripe, isn't it? Here. Take this.* | 100 gold |
| Hagraven Claw | *As a matter of fact, I need a hagraven claw. I'll pay extra if you find one.* | *Nasty bitches, aren't they? Here's your fee. * | 100 gold |
| Vampire Dust | *How about some vampire dust? Bring me some of that and I'll pay handsomely.* | *Not bad for an amateur? Take this.* | 100 gold |
| Briar Heart | *You got a briarheart? That's what I really need. Neloth keeps taking all of mine.* | *You could have cleaned it. It's still bloody.* | 100 gold |
| Void Salts | *You find some void salts and you bring them to me. I'll beat anyone else's price.* | *Hmm. Better than average quality. I'll take it.* | 200 gold |

## Notes
- If you already have the ingredient in your inventory when Elynea gives you the quest, you can immediately give it to her and complete the quest without exiting dialogue.
