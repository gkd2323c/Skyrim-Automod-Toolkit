[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-place-Foyada_Drura.jpg/200px-ON-place-Foyada_Drura.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Foyada_Drura.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Foyada_Drura.jpg) Foyada Drura ca. 2E 582 Foyada Drura is one of the several [foyadas](Lore_Foyada.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the island district of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Wise Woman Asani-1)</sup> The volcanic ravines (or "fire-rivers") are valleys formed by lava flows from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), functioning as drainage basins for lava when the volcano is active, or alternatively forming pathways to travel on when dormant and devoid of lava.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWGen F-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWAjira-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TPP-4)</sup> The foyada starts from the caldera of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) and travels south through the Ashlands in the west, surrounding [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md) on the northwest side. Hleran Ancestral Tomb is found at the end.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Wise Woman Asani-1)</sup> When [Skar](Lore_Skar.md) fell in ancient times, Dranoth Hleran's people cried out for the quiet life with crops and strong houses, silencing their Velothi hearts and settling beside the fire river of Drura.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dranoth Hleran-5)</sup> Foyada Drura was filled with lava at the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AMap-6)</sup> The foyada had dried up by the time of [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWMap-7)</sup>
