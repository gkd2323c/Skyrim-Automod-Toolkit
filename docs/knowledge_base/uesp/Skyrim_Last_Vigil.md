[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-place-Last_Vigil.jpg/200px-SR-place-Last_Vigil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Last_Vigil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Last_Vigil.jpg) Last Vigil **Last Vigil** is a small [camp](Skyrim_Camps.md) northeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

Upon reaching level 80, you will be approached by the [Ebony Warrior](Skyrim_Ebony_Warrior.md), who will challenge you to meet him at this location to battle him in a duel.

## Related Quests
- **[The Ebony Warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebony_Warrior)**: Locate and defeat the [Ebony Warrior](Skyrim_Ebony_Warrior.md).

## Walkthrough
The camp is high in the snowy [Velothi Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Velothi_Mountains). Several [ore veins](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md) are found along the path, which starts east of [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md).

The only distinguishable features of the camp are a collapsed tent and some skeletal remains. The camp contains a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a [moonstone ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moonstone_Ore_Vein), a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein), and a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Art of War Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Art_of_War_Magic)*. Before starting the quest [The Ebony Warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ebony_Warrior), the camp will be occupied by a single leveled [creature](Skyrim_Creatures.md), which could be a [snow bear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snow_Bear) or a [frost troll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Troll) at higher levels. After starting the quest, the camp will be occupied by the Ebony Warrior, with a dead frost troll nearby.

## Notes
- Along with the [Northern Maiden](Skyrim_Northern_Maiden.md), this is one of only two locations added by *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* within [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md), and the only discoverable one.
- On the local map, the location is called *Ebony Warrior's Camp*.
