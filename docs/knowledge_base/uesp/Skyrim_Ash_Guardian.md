[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ash_Guardian.jpg) An ash guardian **Ash Guardians** are atronach-like [ash creatures](Skyrim_Ash_Creatures.md) summoned by certain mages on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). They resemble the [Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md) except they are encased in stone rather than lightning. They will attack unless they feed on [heart stones](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone).

Ash Guardians can be [conjured](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Ash_Guardian) once you have completed the quest [From the Ashes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:From_the_Ashes). The Ash Guardian never occupies a conjuration slot; you can still have one more summon (or two more, if you have the [Twin Souls](Skyrim_Conjuration.md#Twin_Souls) perk) in addition to the Ash Guardian. Moreover, only one Ash Guardian can exist at any given time; summoning a second one will make the existing one vanish. This is true whether you summon it with or without a heart stone, or one guardian with a stone and another without a stone.

Aside from the ones summoned by you, only two Ash Guardians are encountered in the game: One during the quest *[From the Ashes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:From_the_Ashes),* the other at [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md) during *[Old Friends](Skyrim_Old_Friends.md)*. Both are hostile to you.

## Related Quests
- **[From the Ashes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:From_the_Ashes)**: Help [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md) by killing an ash guardian.
