**Vateshran** Allaghach was a known [historian and storyteller](Lore_Vateshrans.md) among the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-STOTDFB1-1)</sup> Little is known about their life, but they were devoted to spreading the tales of the Dawn of Karth, emphasizing the importance of trials, suffering, bitterness, and pain. They lived before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). A quote underscoring the significance of hardships and struggle, and mentioning a matronly crone descending from the mountains to impart these revelations was credited to them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BRR-2)</sup>

## Quotes
- *"A matronly crone came down from the mountains, feather-winged and ever-shrouded. She delivered a message straight from the Dawn of Karth. The blood of Men will spill upon the soil, for their trials must be lived in bitterness and pain."*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BRR-2)</sup>
