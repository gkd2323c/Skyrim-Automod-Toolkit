Mavos Siloreth was a renowned [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) mage that lived during the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) amid a conflict between the Chimer and the [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md). He is known for having sealed one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md), [Balreth](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md#Balreth). Mavos was among the mages that traveled to [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) from [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) with the intent to seal the Brothers of Strife.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Mavos utilized twin [Flame Atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md), Blaze and Ragebinder, to draw Balreth's power. In the end, Mavos was able to subdue Balreth but had later passed away. His remains were interred into the necropolis of [Othrenis](Lore_Othrenis.md).

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Mavos' spirit was contacted by an [Agent](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) intending to find a way to seal Balreth into [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md) once more. Siloreth showed the Agent his memories of when he initially sealed Balreth. With this knowledge, the Pact was able to seal Balreth. Later, Tanval Indoril summoned [Sadal](Lore_Sadal.md) at the [Tormented Spire](Lore_Tormented_Spire.md). Mavos and the other mages that sealed the Brothers were present at the Spire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
