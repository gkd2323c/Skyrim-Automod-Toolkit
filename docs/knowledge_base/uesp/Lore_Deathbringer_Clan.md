# Deathbringer Clan

The Deathbringer Clan (or **Clan Deathbringer**) is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) clan led by Valkynaz Seris, in service to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). The clan is based in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). Although primarily composed of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTCMF-1)</sup> the clan also counts [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md) among their ranks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

The Deathbringers rose to prominence in Coldharbour after betraying [Foolkillers Clan](Lore_Foolkillers_Clan.md), resulting in their imprisonment and banishment from the realm.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ODC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Based out of the Pyramid, the Deathbringers rose high in Molag Bal's service, and were tasked with protecting the way to the Endless Stair and Molag Bal's inner sanctum. The clan's reign ended in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when an exiled Foolkiller named [Lyranth](Lore_Lyranth.md) allied herself with an invasion force from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). In return for slaying Seris, Lyranth gave the army access to the Endless Stair on their quest to end the Planemeld. With Seris vanquished, the Foolkillers returned to Coldharbour and took control of the Pyramid for themselves.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTCMF-1)</sup>
