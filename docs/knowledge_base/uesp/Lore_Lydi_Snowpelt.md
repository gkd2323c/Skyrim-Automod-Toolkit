Lydi Snowpelt was one of the chieftains and leaders of the [Rageclaw Clan](Lore_Rageclaw_Clan.md). She was active in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), participating in the Rageclaw Clan's invasion of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_02.jpg) Rageclaw Clan Camp at the Three Tribes Camp Little is known of Lydi Snowpelt's past. Despite the matriarchal nature of the Rageclaws,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTR-2)</sup> she was not the only one of the Rageclaws who bore the title of chieftain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LS-3)</sup> She shared this title with Frostfang, a male member of the clan.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVigrod-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CF-5)</sup> They were ruling together, but the nature of their relationship was unknown.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LS-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CF-5)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVigrod-4)</sup> Another member of the clan, [Stral Blackthroat](Lore_Stral_Blackthroat.md), was also considered a clan leader at that time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONVigrod-4)</sup>

Lydi, together with [Frostfang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frostfang) and [Stral Blackthroat](Lore_Stral_Blackthroat.md) made an alliance with two other Reachfolk clans: the [Boneshapers](Lore_Boneshaper_Clan.md) and the [Stonetalons](Lore_Stonetalon_Clan.md), and signed the treaty on 3rd of the Frost Fall in 2E 582.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOTTC-6)</sup> The clans aligned themselves with the [Cult of the Black Worm](Lore_Cult_of_the_Black_Worm.md), which promised the power and blessings of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Online:Bermund-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DU-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-WN-9)</sup> and invaded [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOTTC-6)</sup>

Under their leadership, the Rageclaw Clan participated in warfare-related activities. When their allies of the Boneshaper Clan surrounded the [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md), choked off passage between [Stony Basin](Lore_Stony_Basin.md) and the [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) valley,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup> kidnapped local miners, and used tunnels as a site for their rituals,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-BTS-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MTM-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Unearthed-12)</sup> their forces, led by Chieftain Frostfang, assaulted the [Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md) to steal [Wuuthrad](Lore_Wuuthrad.md) in the hopes of reviving the [half-giant](Lore_Half-Giant.md) chieftain, [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md) and his army. They suffered great losses and Frostfang died during the fights, but they managed to take the great axe from the Nords.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-STH-13)</sup>

Ultimately, the Reachmen alliance was defeated, however. Their allied forces, led by Stral Blackthroat, were vanquished in the [Vernim Woods](Lore_Vernim_Woods.md),<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ONShroud-14)</sup> and their attack on the Boulderfall Pass was stopped.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SOTH-15)</sup> Lydi, together with other clan leaders: [Vivian Witchclaw](Lore_Vivian_Witchclaw.md) of the Stonetalons and [Kevinne Blightheart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kevinne_Blightheart) of the Boneshapers was defeated by the [King's Arrow](Lore_The_Vestige.md) at the encampment west of Boulderfall Pass, as per request of dying [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) soldier Melril.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-RTC-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Melril-17)</sup> The future of the clan following his death is unknown, but their invasion of the Rift was thwarted.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ONShroud-14)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-TN-18)</sup>
