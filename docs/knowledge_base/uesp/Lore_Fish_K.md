## Khaj'Roh
The **Khaj'Roh** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) and [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md), off the coast of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Killifish
**Killifish** are a type of freshwater fish.

- **Banded Killifish** are a species that can be found in the lakes of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Knifefish
**Knifefish** are a type of fish.

- The **Ghost Knifefish** can be found in the lakes of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- The **Whisper Knifefish** can only be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It was previously thought to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Koi
**Koi** are a variety of [carp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Carp) that can be found in the lakes of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).

- The **Ice Koi** can be found in the lakes of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Kuuyicet
The **Kuuyicet** is a type of fish found in the rivers of [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
