[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/ON-place-Viridian_Lake.jpg/200px-ON-place-Viridian_Lake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Viridian_Lake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Viridian_Lake.jpg) Viridian Lake circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Viridian Lake is a small lake in the [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) region of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), situated close to the borders with [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). The lake is probably named after the nearby Viridian Woods, which have been known to be inhabited by witch covens, particularly the [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup> Arlimahera's Sanctum, Forsaken Stronghold, and Martyr's Crossing are all found along the banks of the lake.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/Shadowkey_Map.jpg/200px-Shadowkey_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Shadowkey_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Shadowkey_Map.jpg) A map showing a lake in the Western Reach - Viridian Lake may be synonymous with the lake seen in the map of the Western Reach in *[Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Shadowkey)* given its location west of [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md). Martyr's Crossing's crossing is found on the west end of Viridian Lake and shares a similar name to [Azra's Crossing](Lore_Azra%27s_Crossing.md) found west of the *Shadowkey* lake, but there has been no confirmation if they are the same location.
