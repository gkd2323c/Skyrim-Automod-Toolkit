Hobs are a [race](Lore_Race.md) of hairless [monkey](Lore_Monkey.md) -like creatures of unclear origin, who inhabited the floating island [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup> They have long arms and legs, and very long, thin, delicate fingers. Their faces are described as [goblinesque](Lore_Goblin-ken.md), with huge toothless mouths and large green eyes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 1, Chapter 8</sup>

Hobs can speak very simple [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md), though they seem to have their own language as well.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 3, Chapter 10</sup> They were used as assistants by chefs in the kitchens of Umbriel, and were usually tasked with fetching specific ingredients. Troops of them could sometimes be found harvesting [bloodball](Lore_Bloodball.md) fruit in the Marrow Sump.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 3, Chapter 1</sup> Supposedly, some chefs would train their Hobs to perform "[unconventional](Lore_Sex.md)" favors for them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 2, Chapter 4</sup>

Their exact origins are unclear as many of the species that inhabited Umbriel came from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) alike. It was unknown whether the Hobs of Umbriel were creatures that reproduced or grew in a normal way, or if they were grown from proforms like the other people of Umbriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:Part 3, Chapter 10</sup> Like the rest of the inhabitants of Umbriel, the Hobs ended up in the [Realm of the Hist](Lore_Realm_of_the_Hist.md) when Umbriel found itself there, and presumably made it their home.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup>

Hobs are potentially the namesake of Hob's Fall Cave, an icy cave found in the [Ice Fields](Lore_Ice_Fields.md) of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
