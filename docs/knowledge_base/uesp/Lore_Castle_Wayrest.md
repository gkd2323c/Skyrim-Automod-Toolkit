[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/DF-place-Castle_Wayrest.jpg/200px-DF-place-Castle_Wayrest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Castle_Wayrest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Castle_Wayrest.jpg) Castle Wayrest (Daggerfall) Castle Wayrest (also called **Wayrest Castle**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONCastle-1)</sup> or **Wayrest Palace**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE5-2)</sup> is the seat of power for the [Kingdom of Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) and is located in the [capital city](Lore_Wayrest.md). The castle is arguably the most famous castle in all of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and is noted for its sweeping spires that provide a grand view of the entire countryside. Its classical stone-keep architecture had set the standard for noble housing for centuries.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ATGTTC-3)</sup> The castle lies in the eponymous **Palace District** of Wayrest city,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONWayrest-4)</sup> and is found on the north end of Wayrest Boulevard.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WJOTB-5)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/ON-place-Wayrest_Castle.jpg/200px-ON-place-Wayrest_Castle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Wayrest_Castle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Wayrest_Castle.jpg) Castle Wayrest ca. 2E 582 The [Gardner](Lore_Gardner.md) family built walled palaces on the High Rock side of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md), and over time, the settlement grew around them. But Castle Wayrest was originally built by King [Aphren Gardner](Lore_Aphren_Gardner.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WJOTB-5)</sup> who reigned over Wayrest in the mid [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), after King [Farangel](Lore_Farangel_Gardner.md)'s reign started in [1E 1100](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1100).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WJOTB-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONBlaise-6)</sup> A pathway was constructed to connect Castle Wayrest with the Wayrest Sewers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LGSewers-7)</sup> When the Gardner dynasty died in the wake of the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md), the Earl of Cumberland, [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) took up the mantle of King and started the Cumberland Dynasty in [2E 563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TOAM10-8)</sup> Before he had founded the second [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), [Kurog gro-Bagrakh](Lore_Kurog_gro-Bagrakh.md) and his [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) companions traveled to Wayrest and met with King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) in the castle.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TCOKK2-9)</sup>

In time, the Cumberland dynasty vacated the throne and the Horley dynasty continued in their place. Eventually, the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) took over the kingdom and its castle.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WJOTB-5)</sup> The first known Septim to rule as King was [Cassynder Septim](Lore_Cassynder_Septim.md), son of former Emperor [Pelagius III](Lore_Pelagius_Septim_III.md) and former Empress Regent [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-10)</sup><sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Septim)</sup> He eventually left the throne due to his declining health and left it to his half-brother, [Uriel](Lore_Uriel_Septim_IV.md), who in turn succeeded him as Emperor Uriel Septim IV despite not being a blood-related Septim.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-10)</sup>

In [3E 390](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_390), after fleeing from the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), King [Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md) married the widow Queen of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md). She also brought her children, [Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md) and [Morgiah](Lore_Morgiah.md) to live with them in Castle Wayrest.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BOQB3-11)</sup> In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), an [Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) visited Castle Wayrest and made contact with several members of the royal family, such as [Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md) and Morgiah.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DFMorgiah-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-DFElysana-13)</sup> In that same time, the regent Queen of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md) tasked the adventurer with stealing a painting from Castle Wayrest's treasury, an heirloom that originally belonged to the late King of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md). Once they reached the treasury, they were ambushed but they successfully escaped Wayrest with the painting.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-DFPainting-14)</sup>

On the 7th of [Sun's Height](Lore_Sun%27s_Height.md), [4E 188](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_188),<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-CJ2-15)</sup> Wayrest was sacked and taken over by corsairs when King Barynia left the city gates open for them and barred the castle's gates.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-LGIntro-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LGThrone-17)</sup> In the midst of the chaos, King Barynia was confronted by a [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) assassin. Whether he was spared or killed is unknown, but the King disappeared from Wayrest and left the kingdom to his daughter.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LGThrone-17)</sup>
