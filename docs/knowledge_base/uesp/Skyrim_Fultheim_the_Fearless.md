[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless.jpg) Fultheim the Fearless [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless_Unmasked.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless_Unmasked.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless_Unmasked.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fultheim_the_Fearless_Unmasked.jpg) Fultheim unmasked **Fultheim the Fearless** is one of the three hostages in the [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) quest. He is a Nord mercenary who works out of Skyrim. He bears no particular ills against any person, but when asked about his potential assassination, he reasons that as a sellsword, any number of people could have put a contract out on him as he has been proven to be too difficult to kill through battle. When intimidated, he admits to sometimes getting carried away during battle.

Fultheim wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots), and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers), along with an [execution hood](Skyrim_Execution_Hood.md).

Despite his name, he is quite afraid while he is being held captive. He fights back tears and begs for his life, insisting that he is just a soldier who follows orders. He also thinks that somebody might pay to have him killed because he slaughtered sleeping people, which he thought was immoral.

## Dialogue
When approached for the first time, he will say:

- *"What did I do? Please, whatever it is, I'm sorry."*

At other times, he will tell you one of the following:

- *"I... I can hear you talking out there. Please, let me go. I've done nothing to you."*
- *"Is this about that raid last week? I told Holgrim there was no honor in killing sleeping men, but he wouldn't listen! It wasn't my fault, I swear!"*
- *"I'm just a soldier. Killing is my job! Surely you can't fault a man for doing his job?"*
- *"Please, oh please, I don't want to die..."*

If you kill one of the other captives and then approach Fultheim, he will cry out:

- *"Oh, gods, please! Let me live! I'll do anything you want!"*

You can ask him a few questions before making your choice.

- **Who are you?**
- *"My name is Fultheim. I'm a soldier. Well, mercenary, really. You know, a... a sellsword. I've lived in Skyrim all my life.
That's all! I'm a nobody, really. So can't you just let me go?"*
- **Would someone pay to have you killed?**
- *"What? Oh gods, I don't want to die..."*
- **Answer me, or die! (Intimidate)**
- *"Please! I don't know! I mean, I'm a soldier. I've killed people. When I was ordered to. Maybe there were some times... some times I got carried away?
But war is war. Right? Nobody could blame me for that. Could they?"*
- **Shhh... Don't be afraid. You can tell me. (Persuade)**
- *"Okay, well... I guess it's possible. I've been selling my sword arm for years now. Killed a lot of people. Could be someone wanted revenge. But... But you're not going to kill me. Right?"*

Upon death, Fultheim will scream,

- *"Aiiiieee!"*

[Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) will comment on his fate, depending on who else has been killed:

- *"Oh ho. The whimpering Nord, eh? Yes, yes, I can see how you'd come to that conclusion. Interesting choice."*
- *"The timid warrior and the [sly Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vasha). Must have been one or the other, right?"*
- *"The blubbering barbarian and the [tenacious mother](Skyrim_Alea_Quintus.md). Must have been one of them, right?"*
- *"Well, well, aren't we the over-achiever? Three possibilities, three victims. Must have been one of them, right? So why take chances..."*

Should you choose to kill Astrid, you can approach Fultheim, who will beg you to untie him, saying:

- *"Please... Please! Release me! Cut my bonds!"*

If you do, he will be immensely grateful.

- *"Thank you! Thank you! I'll never speak of this to anyone, I promise!"*

Any time after his release, he can be found, repeating his promise:

- *"Thank you! Thank you! I won't tell anyone what happened here, I swear it!"*
