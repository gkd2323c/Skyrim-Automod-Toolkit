[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d6/OB-map-Green_Road.jpg/200px-OB-map-Green_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Green_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Green_Road.jpg) Map showing the route of the Green Road The Green Road is one of the major colored roads of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It runs from the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md) in the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) and travels south through the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) and along the western [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md) until it reaches [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) on the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). Along the way, it passes around [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) and the [Larsius River](Lore_Larsius_River.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBGreen-1)</sup> Some maps depict this road traveling directly south into [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) where it reaches [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap EW-3)</sup><sup>[[Note 1]](#intnote_Road)</sup>

For an unspecified amount of time, the western Lower Niben, which included adjacent portions of the Green Road, was part of Elsweyr. The border has long been disputed between Elsweyr and [County Leyawiin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:County_Leyawiin) in southern Cyrodiil. It was not until the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) was an agreement settled between leaders at the time, the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) and Count [Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md). This decision was not taken lightly by some [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) groups, namely the [Renrijra Krin](Lore_Renrijra_Krin.md) warriors, who started a rebellious cause to take the land back.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AT-5)</sup> For travelers and caravans, the Green Road south of the [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md) had become hostile territory.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GTL-6)</sup>
