Uriel Septim II was the fifth emperor of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for eighteen years, from [3E 64](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_64) to [3E 82](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_82). His reign was beset by blights, plagues, and insurrections, and his poor management of the Empire's financial policies left it bankrupt and in debt.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> He was preceded by his father, [Uriel I](Lore_Uriel_I.md), and was succeeded by his son, [Pelagius II](Lore_Pelagius_II.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

## Biography
Uriel Septim was the son of [Uriel Septim](Lore_Uriel_I.md) (later Emperor Uriel I).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> He had three children: [Pelagius](Lore_Pelagius_II.md), [Amiel](Lore_Amiel_Septim.md), and [Galana](Lore_Galana_Septim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWQ1-3)</sup> In [3E 64](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_64), Uriel I died, so Uriel was crowned Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TET-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup>

Uriel II's reign witnessed the beginning of the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md)'s disunification, and it would not return to the level of unity seen during Uriel I's reign until the days of [Pelagius IV](Lore_Pelagius_IV.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-5)</sup> The reign of Uriel II was plagued by pestilences, insurrections, and blights.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> During his reign, a [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) rebellion broke out in [Torval](Lore_Torval.md), and [Mantiarco](Lore_Mantiarco.md), King of [Solitude](Lore_Haafingar.md), gave Uriel II millions of gold coins to aid the Empire in suppressing the rebellion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWQ1-3)</sup> In exchange for the assistance, the Emperor agreed to arrange the marriage of Galana to Mantiarco or pay back the gold to the King.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWQ1-3)</sup> Uriel II's poor management of the Empire's financial policies left it in debt, and by the end of his reign, the [Imperial Treasury](Lore_Imperial_Treasury.md) was bankrupt.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQ3-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BWQ-7)</sup> His tenderness and kindheartedness did not serve Tamriel well, and little justice was done.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

In [3E 67](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_67), his granddaughter [Potema](Lore_Potema.md) was born, and she was immediately presented to Uriel II, who viewed the solemn, intense babe and reportedly commented, "She looks like a she-wolf about ready to pounce."<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BWQ-7)</sup> In [3E 80](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_80), an ambassador representing Mantiarco arrived at the [Imperial Palace](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), asking Uriel II to honor the marriage agreement, but unbeknownst to the ambassador, Galana was preparing to marry the Duke of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWQ1-3)</sup> In order to not harm relations with the Kingdom of Solitude, Uriel II and Prince Pelagius instead arranged the marriage of Potema to Mantiarco.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWQ1-3)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BWQ-7)</sup> In [3E 82](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_82), Uriel II died after eighteen years of rule and was succeeded by Pelagius, to whom he left many problems due to his poor management of the Empire's financial and judicial policies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BWQ-7)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TET-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup>

## Notes
