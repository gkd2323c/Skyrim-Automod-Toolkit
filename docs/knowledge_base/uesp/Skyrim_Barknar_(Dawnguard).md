[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Barknar_%28Dawnguard%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Barknar_%28Dawnguard%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Barknar_(Dawnguard).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Barknar_(Dawnguard).jpg) Barknar **Barknar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) camped out in [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md). He came to [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) with his wife [Alvide](Skyrim_Alvide.md) for protection from the [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) but the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) won't let him in.

He will show up in Dayspring Canyon during the quest [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md) and will do very little but wander around, at times chopping some firewood or using the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). He never sleeps. He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with a [cowl](Skyrim_Cowl.md#00017696) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd).

## Dialogue
He will explain his situation, saying things like:

- *"I've no love for living out of a tent, but I'll take that over being ripped apart."*
- *"Why can't they just let us in? We won't get in the way!"*
- *"It looks like this is as close as we're going to get being safe from the vampires."*
- *"I hope Alvide doesn't think less of me, but I can't protect her on my own. We're safer here."*

## Notes
- Barknar does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md), [Lynoit](Skyrim_Lynoit.md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), and many other generic NPCs.
- Strangely, Barknar lacks any kind of schedule. He will simply idle where he was put. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.3, addresses this issue. He has been given a default sandbox package.
