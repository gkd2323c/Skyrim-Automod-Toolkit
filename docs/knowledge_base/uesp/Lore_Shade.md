Shades are dark, hateful, and hungry [spirits](Lore_Ghost.md) that have become trapped in the [Void](Lore_Void.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTEo E-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONGRites Matron-3)</sup> They have been twisted by being in contact with [Namira](Lore_Namira.md)'s energy, especially that which is emitted by the [Dark Heart](Lore_Dark_Heart.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGVerandis-4)</sup> Namira's energy manifests as dark corruptions known as "voidstuff", which has been used by some [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachmen.md) witches, empowering them and turning them to Shades known as **Voidmothers** (also known as **Void [Liches](Lore_Lich.md)**), which can consume the souls of their victims.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONGRites Matron-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONGwenyfe-5)</sup> Some Reach clans may look to dark avenues to twist and abuse these dark corruptions to gain power, which when wielded, can cause great calamities.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONGwenyfe-5)</sup>

Shades display a form of hierarchy, with Voidmothers on top, commanding regular shades and shade colossi to attack their enemies.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONVoidmother-6)</sup> Shade Colossi serve as brute force, and are a source for spawning common shades.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONSC-7)</sup> Regular shades are relatively weak, and instead rely on numbers to overwhelm their enemies.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONLyris-8)</sup> Void Rot are slimy creatures with shadowy tendrils comparable in appearance to [Voriplasms](Lore_Voriplasm.md), and are created by voidstuff.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONVR-9)</sup> They can create a tether to make a barrier for a Voidmother, requiring their deaths to open the way.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONVS-10)</sup> Some mages have managed to keep them tractable by applying a sleeping potion on it, thus "taming" them.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Notable Voidmothers
- **[Nathari](Lore_Nathari.md)** — Leader of the Ghostsong Clan in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Nathari-12)</sup>
- **[Elgroalif](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elgroalif)** — A matron of the Ghostsong Clan who unleashed dark ritual in [Four Skull Lookout](Lore_Four_Skull_Lookout.md) in 2E 582.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Elgroalif-13)</sup>
- **[Maebroogha](Lore_Maebroogha.md)** — The Void Lich who destroyed the spirit of [Vateshran](Lore_Vateshrans.md) [Aydolan](Lore_Aydolan.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MTVL-14)</sup>
- **Della** — A Voidmother who rewarded Namira's chosen with a void-touched welwa.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-VW-15)</sup>

## Notes
- Shades are comparable to the Khajiiti [dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md), dark spirits who first came about after [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md)'s [sundering](Lore_Dawn_Era.md#Convention); [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md) corrupted him, transforming him into the first dro-m'Athra.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ESOFDF-16)</sup>
- Shades leave behind an inky black substance known as Void Essence.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> Although valuable, this should not be confused with the [Void Essence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void_Essence) harvested from [Flesh Atronachs](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md), which is a type of red gemstone used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>
