# Orma


Orma are born without eyes, becoming masters of their other senses, including that of touch.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLYFE:2920-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TP-3)</sup> Their skulls are peculiar for lacking eye sockets. According to bone scroll canisters, one such skull was used as a door marker for a room containing powerful artifacts in [Scourg Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scourg_Barrow).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TPWarofthe Wormgod-4)</sup>

One of the few known members of the race was [Catchica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Catchica), who was with [Reman](Lore_Reman.md)'s army at [Caer Suvio](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Caer_Suvio) when [Juilek Cyrodiil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Juilek) was poisoned before the battle at [Bodrum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bodrum). She later worked at [Mir Corrup](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mir_Corrup), a resort in [Nibenay](Lore_Nibenay.md), to falsely implicate the Emperor's mistress in a plot to poison the Emperor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLYFE:2920-2)</sup>
