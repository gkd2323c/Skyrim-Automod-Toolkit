[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/LG-cardart-Mannimarco.png/200px-LG-cardart-Mannimarco.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mannimarco.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mannimarco.png) Mannimarco with the Staff of Worms The Staff of Worms is the legendary treasured [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) of [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md). It is a powerful and dreadful [staff](Lore_Staff.md) with the capability to reanimate corpses, and a powerful symbol of authority within the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup> This animated corpse remains mute and shows only minimal intelligence, but it unswervingly follows and defends the caster.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOW-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOW2-4)</sup> Mannimarco used the staff for many of his spells and rituals, notably tearing open Mundus during the [Soulburst](Lore_Soulburst.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup> and battling [Abnur Tharn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Abnur_Tharn).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Cot W-6)</sup>

## History
Mannimarco wielded the staff during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> and around the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> During the resurgence of the Worm Cult following the end of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), the Staff was wielded by Mannimarco's chosen successor and new leader of the Cult, [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTC-1)</sup>

Sometime in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), a necromancer collected items for his lair, ranging from trinkets to artifacts that fit the morbid and macabre. He had become fixated with obtaining the artifacts of Mannimarco; the [Bloodworm Helm](Lore_Bloodworm_Helm.md) and the Staff of Worms. He recruited an apprentice named Naara, who then attempted to slay him and take the artifacts he had found for herself. This would be her downfall, for the apprentice got trapped within the room which had been turned into a puzzle. If Naara wanted to take the artifacts, she would have to earn them by using them to figure out the puzzle to be freed from the trap. Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), The Last Dragonborn located the Necromancer's lair at Mara's Eye Pond and completed the puzzles there. They navigated the necromancer's puzzle, earning the right to keep Mannimarco's artifacts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SRDD-9)</sup>
