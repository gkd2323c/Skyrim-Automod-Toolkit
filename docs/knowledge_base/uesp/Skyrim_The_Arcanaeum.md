[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-interior-The_Arcanaeum.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Arcanaeum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Arcanaeum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Arcanaeum.jpg) Inside The Arcanaeum **The Arcanaeum** is the library located within the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md), zealously maintained by [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md).

Collated over hundreds of years, the College's library contains a wealth of information, placed at the fingertips of those who require it - albeit under the ever-watchful eye of Urag gro-Shub. It is located on the first floor of the main college building, between the [Hall of the Elements](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Elements.md) and the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md). If you choose to join the College, you may find yourself adding significantly to the collection of books available, and may even be gifted with some as rewards.

## The Arcanaeum
There are four entrances to the Arcanaeum: one from the antechamber in the **Hall of Elements**, one from the **Arch-mage's Quarters**, and two from the roof of the College. It is a large circular room with large locked and unlocked non-respawning bookcases lining most of the walls. By the entrances are five unlocked bookcases; however, everything in the Arcanaeum is owned, and taking items is considered stealing. There is a [mammoth tusk](Skyrim_Mammoth_Tusk.md) on top of one of the unlocked bookcases, with a round wooden table and chairs nearby. There are many tables and chairs arranged in small groups around the room, with stacks of common and less common books on every available surface. There are many bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) as well as [goblets](Skyrim_Goblet.md) on the tables, but strictly no [food](Skyrim_Food.md).

The counter is at the far end, where [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) can always be found, overseeing anyone within the Arcanaeum. He is very protective of the books found in the Arcanaeum, and will repeat warnings to take care of any books found here every time you talk to him. With the [Arcane Archer Pack](Skyrim_Arcane_Archer_Pack.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, [*Spell Tome: Telekinesis Arrows*](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Telekinesis_Arrows.md) and [*Telekinesis Arrows Research Notes*](Skyrim_Telekinesis_Arrows_Research_Notes.md) can be found atop the counter next to Urag. Under the counter are two bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), and behind it against the rear wall is a locked display case containing four rare books; however, the lock cannot be picked, and no key exists in-game to unlock either the various locked bookcases or the display case.

Farther around to the left is the non-respawning investigator's chest, which is master-locked. The [key](Skyrim_Investigator%27s_Key.md) to open it is found on a table in [the Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) along with a copy of the [*Midden Incident Report*](Skyrim_Midden_Incident_Report.md) regarding the disappearance and subsequent deaths of four previous students. Inside the chest are four rings that are needed for the gauntlet in **the Midden**, as part of the unmarked quest [Forgotten Names](Skyrim_Forgotten_Names.md).

There are partition walls segregating the outer ring of the room from the central reading area. You will often find one or more of the college trainers in the Arcanaeum reading. Urag gro-Shub, like other [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md), is able to make you [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs, once you have completed a couple quests for him.

## Notes
- Urag will sell you a variety of books that weren't fit to be added to the Arcanaeum's collection.
- When completing the quest [Alduin's Bane](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Bane.md), you can sell the [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md) to Urag for 2000 gold. However, if you have the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on installed, you will need the Elder Scroll for this questline. You can buy it back from him if needed, but you will not be able to sell it again.
- Like all other bookcases in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), books can be read from the unlocked bookcases with no repercussions, but as they are marked as owned, taking them is considered stealing.
- The books inside the display case are (from left to right): [*Arcana Restored*](Skyrim_Arcana_Restored.md), [*Imperial Report on Saarthal*](Skyrim_Imperial_Report_on_Saarthal.md), [*Herbane's Bestiary: Automatons*](Skyrim_Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Automatons.md), and [*The Falmer: A Study*](Skyrim_The_Falmer__A_Study.md).
- The name "Arcanaeum" is possibly an allusion to the Belgian [Mundaneum](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mundaneum).

## Bugs
- Urag may always greet you as though you're in the Arcaneum, even if he isn't there himself. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- After A Matter of Pride is finished, some [Boethiah Cultists](Skyrim_Boethiah_Cultist.md) may respawn in the Arcanaeum when the area resets. From there, they can follow you wherever you go, and they can appear anywhere, including inside player homes. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.629, fixes this bug.
