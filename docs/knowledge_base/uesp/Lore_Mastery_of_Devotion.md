Mastery of Devotion **by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frandar_Hunding)** *Frandar Hunding's insights on achieving mastery*
---
In the season of life in which I marched beneath the [Sun's Height](Lore_Sun%27s_Height.md), before the power of the gods did I kneel. I dedicated myself wholly unto to the spirit of the sword to forge my [Shehai](Lore_Shehai.md), which I would wield for all my future days.

With [Tall Papa](Lore_Tall_Papa.md)'s blessing, the [sword singer](Lore_Sword-singer.md) must do the same to achieve Mastery of Devotion.
