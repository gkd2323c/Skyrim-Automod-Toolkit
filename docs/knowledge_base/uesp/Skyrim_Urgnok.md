[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-npc-Urgnok.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Urgnok.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Urgnok.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Urgnok.jpg) Urgnok in his Dwarven mail **Urgnok** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) found in the [Ruins of Bthalft](Skyrim_Ruins_of_Bthalft.md) south of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). He wears a full set of [Dwarven mail](Skyrim_Dwarven.md#Dwarven_Mail). He fought in the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) and was once a member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) bandit gang before he became a gladiator in the [Imperial City Arena](Lore_Imperial_City.md#Arena_District). Urgnok rose to the rank of Bloodletter.

He is the focus of *[The Crimson Dirks, v8](Skyrim_The_Crimson_Dirks,_v8.md)*.
