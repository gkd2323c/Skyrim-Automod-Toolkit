Tancano the Elder's Journal **by [Tancano the Elder](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Tancano_the_Elder)** *Notes on the construction of Root Sunder*
---
The south wall rotated two degrees last night. I've measured it thirty-six times. The [Welkynd Stones](Lore_Welkynd_Stone.md) must be causing it. The growths run the length of the city, built into every wall and foundation. This must be how the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) thought to control the jungle: [a city](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Root_Sunder) that could grow against the plants. Brilliant strategy, though, obviously, it ultimately failed.

Only the door in the east chamber doesn't move. Strange. Responds slightly to rhythms. Perhaps a secret knock?
