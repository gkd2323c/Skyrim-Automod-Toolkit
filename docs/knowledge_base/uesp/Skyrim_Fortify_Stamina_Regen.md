*Alchemy description:* **Stamina regenerates <mag>% faster for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Stamina regenerates <mag>% faster.**
**Regenerate Stamina** (a.k.a. **Fortify Stamina Regen**) increases the rate at which the target naturally regenerates [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md).

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Stamina Regen if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Necklaces and Rings *[of Invigoration](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Stamina_Regen)*
- All varieties of Armor, Necklaces and Rings *of Recuperation*
- All varieties of Armor, Necklaces and Rings *of Rejuvenation*
- All varieties of Armor, Necklaces and Rings *of Renewal*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Helm of Oreyn Bearclaw](Skyrim_Helm_of_Oreyn_Bearclaw.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md)</sup>
- [Predator's Grace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Predator%27s_Grace)

## Food
The following food items provide a temporary version of this effect:

- [Homecooked Meal](Skyrim_Homecooked_Meal.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> 25 pts for 600 secs

## Powers
The following powers grant a bonus to stamina regeneration rate:

- [Bardic Knowledge](Skyrim_Bardic_Knowledge.md): Fortify Stamina Rate, 50 pts for 300 secs in 250 ft

## Notes
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md) levels will cause your stamina to regenerate more slowly.
- If Survival Mode is active, a [Hunger](Skyrim_Hunger.md) level of Well Fed (less than 80) will cause your stamina to regenerate 10% faster.

## Bugs
- Although rings of Fortify Stamina Regen can be found in loot, you cannot apply the enchantment onto rings yourself. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.4, fixes this bug.
