## Alchemy
The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a poison of Weakness to Frost:

- [Abecean Longfin](Skyrim_Abecean_Longfin.md) (1st effect)
- [Ash Hopper Jelly](Skyrim_Ash_Hopper_Jelly.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Chokeweed](Skyrim_Chokeweed.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**1.33** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.36** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png)) (1st effect)
- [Elves Ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md)
- [Fire Salts](Skyrim_Fire_Salts.md) (1st effect)
- [Gold Kanet](Skyrim_Gold_Kanet.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**1.33** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **1.36** × [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))
- [Ice Wraith Teeth](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith_Teeth.md) (1st effect)
- [White Cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) (1st effect)

## Notes
- Weakness to frost is strengthened by weakness to magic.
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) players will suffer from environmental [Cold](Skyrim_Cold.md) damage 25% faster.
