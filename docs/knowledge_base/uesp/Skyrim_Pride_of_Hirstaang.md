- *Summons a [Bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) for 60 seconds wherever the caster is pointing. Resist 50% of frost damage for 60 seconds. Health regenerates 50% faster for 60 seconds.*

**Pride of Hirstaang** is an Expert level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Arcane Accessories](Skyrim_Arcane_Accessories.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Effects
- [Pride of Hirstaang](Skyrim_Bear.md), for 60 secs
- [Regenerate Health](Skyrim_Regenerate_Health.md), 50 pts for 60 secs
- [Frost Resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md), 50 pts for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](Skyrim_Summoner.md), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Twin Souls](Skyrim_Twin_Souls.md), lets you have two Bears summoned at the same time.

## Notes
- This spell originally [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes#Pride_of_Hirstaang) in the [Spell Tomes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes) DLC for *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
