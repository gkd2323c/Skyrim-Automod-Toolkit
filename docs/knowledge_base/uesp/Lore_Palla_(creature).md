# Palla (creature)

*I turned the lonely bend that intersected with the outskirts of Mir Corrup, and a large shadow rose from the ground where it had been sitting, waiting for me.* *"Palla," I groaned. "Pal La."*

*"Kiss me," it howled.*

*And that brings my story up to the present moment. Love is red, like blood.*
— *[Palla](Lore_Palla.md)* Palla is the name of a misshapen humanoid beast, who was said to have escaped from a mad wizard's laboratory and went on to menace [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). In appearance it is described as having taloned hands, and as being large, misshapen, and monstrous. It is apparently intelligent and capable of speech. It was slain by the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) warrior Xarlys, who herself died in the struggle against it. Xarlys would become well-known in [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) for slaying the beast.

Palla was accidentally resurrected by a member of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), who had become infatuated with Xarlys after viewing a statue commemorating her battle with Palla. The mage had been confused and thought that the warrior's name was Palla, and had used a strange [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) artifact to try and revive her, constantly whispering the name in affection. When the mage realized his mistake, Palla found him, also appearing to have affection for him. The fate of the two is unknown.

All that is known of Palla is written in a [published tale](Lore_Palla.md) of the same name, authored by [Vojne Mierstyyd](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vojne_Mierstyyd), who may be the mage from the story.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Palla-1)</sup>
