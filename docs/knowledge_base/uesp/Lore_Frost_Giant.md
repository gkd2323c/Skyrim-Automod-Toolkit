Frost Giants are a sentient race of huge and sinewy [humanoid beasts](Lore_Beastfolk.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> They possess four to five eyes, and have two curved horns decorating their forehead. White, shaggy hair covers their bodies. They are extremely strong and have regenerative abilities, provided to them by their "icy" blue blood,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FGBloodmoon-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WSA-3)</sup> which can be stunted by fire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FGBloodmoon-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FGDawnguard-4)</sup> Frost Giants are elusive and rarely seen on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are called "cousins" of [common giants](Lore_Giant.md), implying some relation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LG-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-500COTOYTR-6)</sup> They are very similar in physiology to [Frost Trolls](Lore_Troll.md).

They are known to wield clubs similar to those of other giants and are usually only clothed with loincloths.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FGDawnguard-4)</sup> Some are known to collect things such as bones.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup><sup>:546</sup>

According to the skalds of the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md), [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) slew so many Frost Giants that he was covered in their blue blood. The rest of his foes fled the field and Frost Giants avoided the more populated areas of the province, thereafter becoming much rarer to find.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WSA-3)</sup> He and his [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md) were the first to wear the [blue war paint](Lore_Woad.md) based on these battles,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WSA-3)</sup> and modern [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) still wore such body markings.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-YCBM-7)</sup>

Only three Frost Giants are known to have lived on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md): Guolog, slain by Einarr;<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup> [Karstaag](Lore_Karstaag.md), killed in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) during the Hunt of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md);<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> and a Frost Giant who was blamed for the unnaturally cold fog which appeared in a valley circa [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48) (although [Lord Hleryn Sathil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Infernal_City/Characters#Lord_Hleryn_Sathil) actually caused it).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Lo S-10)</sup> At least five Frost Giants lived in the secluded [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They stole the [Snow Elf](Lore_Falmer.md) [Paragons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Paragons) from the shrine of [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md) located there. In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Frost Giants were slain and the paragons retrieved.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FGDawnguard-4)</sup> The spirit of [Karstaag](Skyrim_Karstaag.md) was later summoned by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
