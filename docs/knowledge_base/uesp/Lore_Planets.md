[Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) high priests use jeweled rotating and counter-rotating orreries to determine which Aedric planet to worship.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOCH-1)</sup> Number Eight is important in their culture as it is considered the Number of the Planets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTSN-2)</sup> They named planets after Elven Aedra, but refer to the same celestial objects as Mannish cultures.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOCC-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-LSP:OAC-4)</sup>

The other astral bodies in the sky above Nirn, the moons and dominion planets, are believed to actually be the planes of the gods as well as the gods themselves, as seen from the mortal plane. They are said to actually be different planes of existence in their own right, infinite in size and mass, with their appearance as spheres being only a visual phenomena caused by mortal mental stress.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-Cosmology-5)</sup>

The largest shooting stars are actually planets with independent orbits. One of the most well-known is the Rogue Plane; [Baan Dar](Lore_Five_Finger_Dance.md).<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-Cosmology-5)</sup>

According to some accounts, the mortal realms were formed by the Aedra out of [Creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Co S-6)</sup> Though scholars largely agree no mortal beings live on planets such as Akatosh or [Arkay](Lore_Arkay_(planet).md), [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) theorized that the power [Ayleid Wells](Lore_Ayleid_Wells.md) reroute back towards the heavens was being collected by someone.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Azandar Dialogue-7)</sup>

## Nirn
[Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) (which means '[Gray Maybe](Lore_Aurbis.md)' in [Ehlnofex](Lore_Ehlnofex_Languages.md)),<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> or the **mortal Aurbis**, is the Mortal Plane, the planet upon which Tamriel and the mortal races reside. It floats in the void of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) and comprises the core of the region known as [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). Some sources use the terms *Nirn* and *Mundus* synonymously, though others describe Mundus as a more expansive structure which extends past Nirn itself and encompasses additional regions and [planes](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md).. The origin of Nirn is a matter shrouded in much ambiguity, and different creation myths propose different events leading to its creation. It is commonly thought to have been created during the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md) by the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) and [Magna Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md), based on [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md)'s inspiration and, per some sources, [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md)' designs. Though other sources propose different origins, which include Nirn being a direct creation of [Anu](Lore_Anu.md), or being a [deity](Lore_Gods.md), the goddess Nirni. Some sources claim that the existence of Nirn and Mundus, normally impossible, was allowed to come into being through the power of the [Magna-Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md) [Londa-Vera](Lore_Londa-Vera.md), the Green Star, the embodiment of the feminine power of [magic](Lore_Magic.md) that exists everywhere and nowhere at once.

## Akatosh
The planet [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh_(planet).md), sometimes called **AKHAT**, is one of the Dominion Planets found in the skies of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). According to the Warrior-Poet, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), it is one of the eight worlds known to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md). There are no known satellites orbiting around it, but it marks the eye of the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md) [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md). As with all astral bodies seen in the skies above [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and their corresponding deities, the planet Akatosh is believed to be the plane of the god Akatosh as well as the god himself, as seen from the mortal plane. It is said to actually be a different [plane of existence](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) in its own right, infinite in size and mass, with its appearance as a sphere being only a visual phenomenon caused by mortal mental stress. [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) is said to have ruled from here before [Convention](Lore_Convention.md) took place in [ME 2500](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_2500).

## Arkay

## Baan Dar
The [Five Finger Dance](Lore_Five_Finger_Dance.md) (also called **Baan Dar**) is the realm of [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md), the Bandit God and trickster spirit of thieves and beggars in primarily [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) culture. Among the deity's followers, the Five Finger Dance is considered the greatest party in existence and many compete in an event called the Boast to earn Baan Dar's favor and enter his realm. These events are held all once a year in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and are overseen in secret by the Daughters of Baan Dar.
