Stranger's Plea **by [Skorvild](Skyrim_Skorvild.md)** *A letter requesting aid against a resurgent Mythic Dawn* *
---
<Alias=Player>,

I come to you with an urgent plea. I have done unspeakable things which I have come to regret, and the consequences are imminent. If you know of the Oblivion Crisis, then you know I make no jest when I say the fate of Tamriel is at stake.

It would be safer for us to speak further in person. Please meet me at the Shrine to Stendarr, located bewteen *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* Fort Greymoor and Rorikstead. I will lay bare my sins in Stendarr's presence. Perhaps he will grant me forgiveness.

- [Skorvild](Skyrim_Skorvild.md)
