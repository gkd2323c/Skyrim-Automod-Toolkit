## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) on the road leading to [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md).
2. Speak to [Vantus Loreius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius) at the Loreius Farm.
3. Make your decision: - Persuade Vantus Loreius to fix the wheel.
- Turn in Cicero to the wandering guard.

## Quest Conflicts
Beginning [Innocence Lost](Skyrim_Innocence_Lost.md) or [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) will create some minor restrictions on your options in this quest. Entering the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md) or starting [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) will cause this quest to fail. However, none of these contingencies are of significant consequence to progression through the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) questline or through the game in general, as this quest is a relatively minor one.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Stranded Jester
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_02.jpg) The Stranded Jester On the road north of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and near [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md), you will encounter a very odd jester named [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md). He claims that he is from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and is moving to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). His wagon, which he says contains the remains of his mother, has broken down by the roadside. He asked [Vantus Loreius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vantus_Loreius), the owner of the nearby farm, to help him fix his wheel but Loreius refused. Cicero will ask you to convince Loreius to help him so he can move on.

Speaking to Vantus Loreius will reveal that he was unwilling to help because of Cicero's odd behavior, and he speculates that Cicero's box likely contains [Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) or other illegal cargo, and doesn't want to chance aiding him in any illicit activity. Vantus asks that you convince a patrolling guard—either a [Roadside Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Guard) or [Pale Hold Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pale_Hold_Guard) —to arrest Cicero.

### Helping Cicero
To help Cicero, you must pass a very easy level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade Vantus to do the right thing and fix the wagon wheel. Vantus will reconsider and promise to help Cicero on his way. After you notify Cicero, he will thank you and give you a certain amount of gold depending on your current level.

### Turning in Cicero
To turn in Cicero, you need to find the guard that patrols the roadway near the farm. He patrols very close to the wagon and is frequently within sight of the wagon. The guard will place Cicero under arrest. Cicero promises to make Loreius pay and chastises you for not being of assistance. After notifying Vantus, he thanks you for bringing him peace and gives you gold.

### Consequences
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Delayed_Burial_03.jpg) *"So many knife wounds."* Cicero is an [assassin](Skyrim_Assassin.md) and the Keeper of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). The coffin he is transporting contains the remains of the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md). If you finish this quest then join the Brotherhood later on, he will tell you that he was transporting the Night Mother. Choosing to help Cicero will lead to him showing gratitude if you join the Brotherhood. If you turn him in and later return to the farm, you'll find that Vantus Loreius and his wife, [Curwe](Skyrim_Curwe.md), have been slaughtered (although if you wait too long the bodies will be gone). A passing guard will remark *"You don't want to go up there, citizen. It's a terrible sight. The farmer and his wife murdered in the middle of the night. So many knife wounds."* If you later join the Dark Brotherhood, Cicero will be slightly upset with you for not helping him. Whichever attitude he shows towards you will carry no practical implications if you join the Brotherhood. Your decision to help Cicero or turn him in also has no effect on whether you can make him a follower or not if you join the Brotherhood.

## Notes
- The quest was supposed to be initiated by overhearing a conversation between [Uthgerd the Unbroken](Skyrim_Uthgerd_the_Unbroken.md) and [Skulvar Sable-Hilt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skulvar_Sable-Hilt) in front of the [Whiterun Stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables), but the scene was cut from the game.
- If you do not interact with Cicero prior to joining the Dark Brotherhood, there will be alternate dialogue options when you meet him in the Falkreath [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md). Unfortunately, you will have progressed too far into the Dark Brotherhood questline for this quest to be available by then.
- If you do not interact with Cicero prior to destroying the Dark Brotherhood, this quest will be permanently unavailable.
- Even after you've convinced Loreius to help, Cicero and his broken wagon may remain on the road indefinitely unless you [join](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) or [destroy](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) the Dark Brotherhood, in which case he will move on regardless of whether you convinced Loreius or not.

## Bugs
- If you steal the horse next to Cicero's carriage and ride it away from the farm, the horse will disappear, and the game will crash shortly thereafter. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. A block activation script has been placed on the horse to prevent this.
