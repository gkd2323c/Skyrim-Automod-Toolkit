[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri.jpg) Idesa Sadri [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idesa_Sadri_02.jpg) Idesa speaking with Grimvar **Idesa Sadri** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) living in the [House of Clan Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Cruel-Sea.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and is the sister of [Revyn Sadri](Skyrim_Revyn_Sadri.md). She assists in the upbringing of [Hillevi](Skyrim_Hillevi_Cruel-Sea.md) and [Torsten Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Torsten_Cruel-Sea.md)'s son, [Grimvar Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Grimvar_Cruel-Sea.md).

When you first enter Windhelm, you can find her near the [Aretino Residence](Skyrim_Aretino_Residence.md), trying to convince Grimvar not to talk to [Aventus Aretino](Skyrim_Aventus_Aretino.md). After having this conversation, she will begin her normal schedule. She will spend most of her time at her employer's home, beginning her day there at 7am by getting out of bed. She will spend most of her day with Grimvar. At 8am, she will have breakfast with him, then she will spend the next three hours by his side in the house. After that, she will stand around the house until 6pm, when it is time for dinner. On Mordas, Middas, and Fredas, the two will eat at home. However, on Tirdas and Turdas they will instead go out for a meal at [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md). On the weekend days of Loredas and Sundas, the two will forgo dinner, instead just hanging around their home. The weekend is also the only time Idesa will leave Grimvar's company. She will head down to the [Argonian Assemblage](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage.md) at 11:05pm until midnight. At midnight, on every day of the week, she will retire to her bed in the House of Clan Cruel-Sea. She will wake up at 7am the next day to go about her business.

Idesa wears a set of merchant's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff45) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries her house [key](Skyrim_Clan_Cruel-Sea_House_Key.md) and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When you first enter Windhelm, you will find her outside the Aretino Residence, talking with Grimvar about Aventus Aretino:

- **Grimvar**: *"Then it's true, what everyone is saying? That Aventus Aretino is doing the [Black Sacrament](Lore_Black_Sacrament.md)? Trying to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)?"*
- **Idesa**: *"Oh, Grimvar... always with the nonsense. No, no, of course not. Those are just tales..."*
- **Grimvar**: *"Fine. Then I'll invite him out to play. He lives right there. I'm going to knock on his door..."*
- **Idesa**: *"No, child! Wait! That boy, that house - they're cursed."*
- **Grimvar**: *"Ha! Then I'm right. I knew it. He's trying to have somebody killed!"*
- **Idesa**: *"All right. I won't deny it, child. What you heard is true. But Aventus Aretino walks a dark path. His actions can lead only to ruin. Now. Enough. We will speak no more of this. I am the only friend you need."*

- **Did I hear you say something about a cursed child?**
- *"Hmm. Yes, it is quite sad. A young boy, Aventus Aretino, lost his mother recently, and was sent to an orphanage. But he has returned home, and people have heard strange chanting. They say it's the Black Sacrament, the ritual to contact the Dark Brotherhood. Why a little boy would want to contact a group of murderers is beyond me, but he is inviting evil into this city."*

Idesa will make numerous comments about her job taking care of Grimvar when speaking to her.

- *"I try to keep Grimvar safe, but these are dangerous times."*
- *"Ever tried taking care of children? It can be a nightmare."*
- *"When Grimvar asks me why [women are being killed](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md) around the city. I don't even know what to say to him."*

While at the Argonian Assemblage, she will also state how she occasionally brings Grimvar there to humble him:

- *"I like to bring Grimvar down here sometimes. It reminds him how lucky he is."*

## Notes
- Numerous aliases within the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) suggest that Idesa was originally envisioned as an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) named **Sings-of-Dreams**. The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* also refers to her under this name. - Her AI package that has her go to the Argonian Assemblage is called `Shahvee And Sings Of Dreams Hang Out SS21x2`, which implies she was originally meant to seek out [Shahvee](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shahvee), and that the two women were at least on a friendly basis.
- Internally, the name "Idesa" only gets used three times outside of her own IDs. Once in the relationship with Grimvar, once in the relationship with her brother Revyn, and once in the marker to enable her coffin in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Windhelm).md). All other markers bear Dreams' name. In fact, Dreams has a separate coffin marker placed in the hall which goes unused at all times.
- While relationship data in the Creation Kit confirms that she is the sister of Revyn Sadri, she also shares her surname with [Feran Sadri](Skyrim_Feran_Sadri.md), [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md), and numerous other Dunmer from *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* and its expansions.
- Idesa is one of the very few Dunmer in Windhelm who doesn't need to live in the Gray Quarter.
- Idesa states she brings Grimvar down to the Argonian Assemblage, but she can never be observed doing this.
- With version 2.0.4 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), Idesa will become the new pawnbroker of [Sadri's Used Wares](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Used_Wares) if [Revyn Sadri](Skyrim_Revyn_Sadri.md) is dead.
