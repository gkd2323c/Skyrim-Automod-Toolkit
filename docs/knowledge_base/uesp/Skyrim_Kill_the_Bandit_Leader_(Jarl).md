## Radiant Options
This is a radiant quest which is received from [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) if he is the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). The bandit camp is chosen from the following list: [Bilegulch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bilegulch_Mine), [Cracked Tusk Keep](Skyrim_Cracked_Tusk_Keep.md), [Embershard Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Embershard_Mine), [Knifepoint Ridge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knifepoint_Ridge), [Bannermist Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bannermist_Tower).

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md), the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md).
2. Travel to the assigned dungeon.
3. Kill the [bandit leader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Leader).
4. Return to Siddgeir.

## Detailed Walkthrough
The normal prerequisite for this quest is [Rare Gifts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rare_Gifts), a [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quest which can be given by six different people. If you have received that quest from any of the other five, then you cannot receive it from Siddgeir, and you must complete the other version before you can get it from Siddgeir. It is advisable that, if possible, Siddgeir's version of Rare Gifts should be completed soon after leaving Helgen, as the mead he wants can be found near the exit to the keep.

If you have [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, then at level 9 a courier will deliver a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name)_of_(Jarl%27s_City).md) from the Jarl of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). Reading it will trigger the Falkreath version of [Build Your Own Home](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Build_Your_Own_Home) and, when this quest is running, talking to [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md) will allow you to start the Bandit Leader quest without any prerequisites.

After completing the prerequisite quest or receiving the letter from Siddgeir, talk to him in order to initiate this quest.

Once you have reached the assigned dungeon, you will only have to kill the bandit leader of that dungeon; killing the lesser enemies is not mandatory, but would make your job easier.

## Notes
- Siddgeir is the only Jarl of Falkreath who gives this quest, which is the last prerequisite he requires before starting the [Thane of Falkreath](Skyrim_Thane_of_Falkreath.md) quest. If [Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md) is jarl, then he will already consider you a friend, and start that quest without any further prerequisites.
- The quest will not be available if you have another ["kill the leader" bounty quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Bandit_Boss) active.
- The target location and boss are radiantly chosen the first time entering the Falkreath location, usually long before Siddgeir gives the quest.
- if you have the concurrent quest [Kill Helvard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Helvard), remember never to turn in the killing bandit quest right after killing Helvard, or you will lose the chance to elevate Jarl's relationshiprank to 2, thus never be able to receive the next quest to become a thane nor be authorized to buy the property [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md), the right method is either to turn in the killing bandit quest before killing Helvard, or go somewhere else after killing Helvard, then come back to turn in the killing bandit quest, the reason behind is about relationshiprank, the relationshiprank mechanism is also mentioned in [Kill Helvard page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kill_Helvard#Notes).

## Bugs
- If you start this quest after Rare Gifts, before you reach level 9 and then get a letter from a courier, a sidequest to see jarl will remain active forever.

- If you did not talk to [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md) during the [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md) quest yet, you will not get the option to ask the jarl for any jobs, nor will you be able to tell him you got the [letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Jarl_(Jarl%27s_Name).md). This will block you from becoming a thane and thus accessing Lakeview Manor until you progress the main quest. If you want to evade starting [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) to avoid enabling dragons in Skyrim but still want access to Lakeview Manor, you can progress the Bleak Falls Barrow quest to the point where you need to give Farengar the tablet and stop there. This will make Siddgeir give you this quest and you will be able to progress normally to became the thane. <sup>**?**</sup>

- If you start or complete this quest while having a "kill the leader" bounty quest active for the same location, the kill will just act as if you completed that quest, and not credit you for this quest. <sup>**?**</sup>
