[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/DF-npc-Cyndassa.png/200px-DF-npc-Cyndassa.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Cyndassa.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Cyndassa.png) The maid of Queen Aubk-i Cyndassa was a lady-in-waiting employed by Queen [Aubk-i](Lore_Aubk-i.md) of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md); she worked in the kitchen of [Castle Daggerfall](Lore_Castle_Daggerfall.md). She played a pivotal role in the events leading up to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md). As the maid of her Queen, she was the one that received [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_VII.md)'s letter intended for the Dowager Queen [Mynisera](Lore_Mynisera.md) from the [Knights of the Dragon](Lore_Knights_of_the_Dragon.md) courier that the [Emperor](Lore_Emperor.md) sent and transmitted it to Aubk-i herself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cyndassa-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ES10A-2)</sup>

Cyndassa claimed that she saw the Queen's jaw drop in amazement after reading the fateful letter sent by Uriel VII and that Aubk-i informed her that she would transmit the letter to the intended recipient, Myniseria, herself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cyndassa-1)</sup> Some sources claim Cyndassa was the reason the letter went missing, stating that she read the letter, and while she did not fully understand its content, she understood that it was important and sold it to the highest bidder, [Gortwog gro-Nagorm](Lore_Gortwog.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ES10A-2)</sup>

At some point, Cyndassa's brother became a [werewolf](Lore_Werewolf.md), and despite considering him as good as dead in that state, she grew to pity him. When [the Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) approached her for information, she sent them to kill her brother in exchange for information on the letter.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cyndassa-1)</sup> Her brother was said to have been menacing the region he was found in for weeks by the time the agent slew him. The peasants who lived in the area were known to have delighted in his killing.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TB-3)</sup>
