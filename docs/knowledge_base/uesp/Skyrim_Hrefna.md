[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-npc-Hrefna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hrefna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrefna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrefna.jpg) Hrefna in Goldenrock Mine [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Hrefna_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hrefna_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrefna_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrefna_02.jpg) *"Do you ever want to go to Morrowind, Sondas?"* **Hrefna** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) who lives with her mother [Tormir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tormir) at the small mining settlement, [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md).

While her mother is mostly preoccupied working in [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md), Hrefna leads a lonely life in the settlement, although [Sondas Drenim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sondas_Drenim) has taken up the role of caretaker. She starts the day at 7am with a one-hour breakfast, then follows her mother into the mine where she watches her work for four hours. When Tormir surfaces for a break at noon, Hrefna follows her and waits by the campfire until 1pm when she locates Sondas inside the mine. At 5pm she returns with Sondas to the campfire where she spends the rest of the evening. She was supposed to crawl into one of the tents at 10pm for eight hours of sleep, but doesn't due to a [bug](#Bugs).

She wears red colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). She is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and carries a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child), as well as a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
Hrefna will be very happy to talk with you and will mention most of the other inhabitants of the settlement when spoken to:

- *"Hi! I've never seen you around here before. Are you a miner too?"*
- *"Sondas takes care of me when mom's not around."*
- *"[Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md) used to take me diving way down deep in the river. I miss him."*
- *"Mom says the Empire is mean, but I don't think they're that bad."*
- *"The [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) are named after [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). He's a bad man, but don't tell the soldiers that."*

## Conversations
Hrefna often talks to her mother or Sondas:

- **Hrefna**: *"And Meieran thinks that we should go there."*
- **Tormir**: *"I don't want to hear another word about what you're learning from the elf! It's high time you learned to be a Nord, too!"*
- **Hrefna**: *"But mama..."*
- **Tormir**: *"He's a half-decent farmer. Ask him to teach you to farm!"*

- **Hrefna**: *"Sondas, I've heard about [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)."*
- **Sondas**: *"Oh? What about it?"*
- **Hrefna**: *"That it was black and gross and nobody wanted to live there anymore."*
- **Sondas**: *"I'll have to take your word on that one, my dear."*

- **Hrefna**: *"Do you ever want to go to Morrowind, Sondas?"*
- **Sondas**: *"I've been in Skyrim my whole life. Why would I want to leave?"*
- **Hrefna**: *"To see the fires!"*
- **Sondas**: *"I think I like the lake here just a little more."*

## Notes
- Even though she has a pickaxe and walks around [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md) during the day, she will never be seen mining.
- Hrefna and Tormir seem to have been intended to have resided in a house known as "Crossinghouse".
- Her apparent role model, a male elf called "Meieran", cannot be found in-game and doesn't even exist in the files. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.0.4 of the [Unofficial Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) alters all dialogue that refers to Meieran to instead refer to Derkeethus. As such, the scene with her mother gets changed to:

- - - **Hrefna**: *"And Derkeethus thinks that we should go there."*
- **Tormir**: *"I don't want to hear another word about what you're learning from the Argonian! It's high time you learned to be a Nord, too!"*
- **Hrefna**: *"But mama..."*
- **Tormir**: *"He's a half-decent farmer. Ask him to teach you to farm!"*

## Bugs
- Hrefna was supposed to sleep from 10pm to 6am, but her sleep package mistakenly includes other activities, causing her to stay up all night. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
