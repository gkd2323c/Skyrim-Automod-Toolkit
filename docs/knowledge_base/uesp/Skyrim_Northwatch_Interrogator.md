[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-npc-Northwatch_Interrogator.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Northwatch_Interrogator.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Northwatch_Interrogator.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Northwatch_Interrogator.jpg) Northwatch Interrogator The **Northwatch Interrogator** can be found in the torture room of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). He wears standard [Thalmor Justiciar](Skyrim_Thalmor_Justiciar.md) equipment and carries the [Northwatch Keep Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Northwatch_Keep_Key), which opens the back door to the keep.

His methods are brutal, considering the desiccated remains of a person on one of the tables in the room, and the number of ancient embalming tools found.
