[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear.jpg/200px-SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear.jpg) Orphan's Tear **Orphan's Tear** is a [shipwreck](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shipwrecks) west of [Ravenscar Hollow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ravenscar_Hollow) on the northern coast.

The ship has broken into two sections; one section is partially submerged and holds the cabin, while the other is almost fully submerged. It is occupied by three [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Restless](Skyrim_The_Restless.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup>: Search for an ancient pirate ship.

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Orphan%27s_Tear_02.jpg) Orphan's Tear bandit camp There are three bandits based here, who will become hostile and attack as soon as you approach. They are a melee fighter, an archer, and a magic user. Once they are taken care of, you can proceed with exploring the shipwreck. The bandits' campsite on the shore consists of a campfire and a large tent with two bedrolls and a table holding some cheese wedges and a wheel, and an adept-locked chest containing random [leveled loot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists) just outside the tent. Near the tent, there are four [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) hanging from a rack and a bucket containing three more salmon behind a small boat, all of which can be harvested. Beside the bucket of salmon are some crates with two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation installed, there is also a fishing pole here, with some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) found a bit farther up the shore. Fish that can be found in freezing waters can be found here. There is also a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) lying on a wooden block in front of the large tent.

Inside the shipwreck's cabin, you will see a table propped up against the wall with an unlocked chest hidden behind it. Also, if you look hard enough, you can find two bottles of [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) and a [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka) beside the bedroll. There are a couple of barrels on this level containing foodstuffs. Proceed down the stairs into the water to find several barrels containing common [food](Skyrim_Food.md) and also a pair of [iron boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) near a bed. If you proceed farther down one level you will see a skeleton trapped underwater to the left of the stairs, along with another unlocked chest surrounded by four barrels. To the right of the stairs is a [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka).

There are two harvestable clams between the two parts of the boat and two [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod) plants next to the large tent. The other submerged section of the shipwreck does not have any notable loot, however, other than a few barrels.

## Notes
- There is a total of 45 [Nordic barnacle clusters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle_Cluster) in the area, where you can harvest [Nordic barnacles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle).
- There is an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) southeast of the campsite.
