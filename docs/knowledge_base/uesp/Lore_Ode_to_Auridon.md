Ode to Auridon *A tribute to Auridon*
---
Of all the lands in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md),
[Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), she outshines them all.
With her marble halls and towering spires,
Her beauty surpass'd them all.

From [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md) to the [First among Holds](Lore_Firsthold.md),
Her strength outstrips them all.
In shadowed glade and quiet ruin,
Her calm surprises all.

In steel and ship and flopping fish,
Her coin out-trades them all.
With beauty and wit and subtle grin,
Her [Queen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Queen_Ayrenn) is best of all.
