# Calcelmo's Stone

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-quest-Hard_Answers.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Hard_Answers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hard_Answers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Hard_Answers.jpg) Calcelmo's Stone [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-misc-Dwemeris_inscription.jpg/200px-SR-misc-Dwemeris_inscription.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Dwemeris_inscription.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Dwemeris_inscription.jpg) A close up of the inscription Calcelmo's Stone is an artifact bearing inscriptions in both the [Dwemer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwemer_Alphabet) and [Falmer](Lore_Falmer_Alphabet.md) alphabets. It was owned by [Calcelmo](Lore_Calcelmo.md), the court wizard of [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) and one of the most recognizable [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) scholars at the time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRCalcelmo-1)</sup> The stone was kept in his tower in [Understone Keep](Lore_Understone_Keep.md) and under heavy protection.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SREnthir-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRAnswers-3)</sup>

When Calcelmo acquired the stone is unknown. Sometime before his death in [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRKarliah-4)</sup> the Guildmaster of the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md), [Gallus Desidenius](Lore_Gallus_Desidenius.md) infiltrated Calcelmo's Tower and used the stone to learn the Falmer language after receiving a tip from a friend in [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SREnthir-2)</sup> Years later while trying to follow his trail, another [member](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) of the Thieves Guild entered the tower and took a rubbing of the stone to translate Gallus' journal.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRAnswers-3)</sup>

The Falmer inscription may be translated almost fully using our knowledge of the [Ayleid language](Lore_Ayleid_Language.md), and the result is relatively close to the official translation given by [Kurt Kuhlmann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Kurt_Kuhlmann). The inscriptions are transliterated below:

#### Dwemer:

```
Chun thuamer arkngd chend duathand, th ahvardn btham. Amz thuamer ahrkanch kemelmzulchond aka
Mora, th thuangz ahrk, th duum melz thuabtharng, th kanthaln duabcharn mzin thuastur, btharumz thua
mer zel. Abakch duumarkng tuathumz amakai, th abakch avatheled kagr tuamkingth mzan. Du chal fahl
ngark, che du fahl bthun ur. Du chal fahl ngalft, che du bthun ur. Du abak chal thu abazun nchur
duabthar, nchul duanchard. Th ur thuanchuth irknd, ur irkngth eftardn, thunch fahlz. Bthun abak dua
mzual th nchuan duarkng, chun fahlbthar thuanchardch anum ralz, th eftar thuachendraldch kagren thua
vanchningth.

```

#### Falmer:

```
ye sa sou meldi calne tarn va nou molagnenseliye trumbi nou bala
ilpen av sou meldi nagaiale as guntumnia spantelepelaelia arani morae
ye sou liebali racuvane ye nu rautane sye ye nu hautalle nou buroi
gume sou gravuloi sa metane sye garlis frey as gandra dwemera tarcel
lane sou agea ye frey as emeratis avatheledia carelle sou anyamissi
bisia silya nu hecta sou arcten rias nu nemalauta ge nu hecta sou epe
gandra rias ne nemalauta ge nu frey sepa sye arcta varlor denai
cullei noue staneiaye ry sou alasil auta ry loria shanta abagaiavoy
malautavoy fey nou darre ye alata nou malae asma moraga sou anyamis
av sercen pado ye gethena sou wend narilia vey emeratu sou oia bisia

```

#### Literal Translation of the Falmer Script:

```
And so your exiles CALNE portal in our fire-water halls and TRUMBI our power
ILPEN of your driven died by GUNTUMNIA SPAN-tower-speak-throat kings of (at)mora
and your LIEBALI were cast down and we RAUTANE and we HAUTALLE our vassals
GUME your GRAVULOI so METANE SYE caverns FREY by Dwemer gifts TARCEL
LANE your wisdom and FREY by music of "Avatheled" CARELLE your new lives
SILYA we exile your acknowledgement RIAS we NEMALAUTA GE we exile your
spoken gift RIAS not NEMALAUTA GE we FREY SEPA SYE accept VARLOR treaties
foods we STANEIA and as your vision AUTA as darkness comes do not fear
MALAUTAVOY FEY our DARRE and light NOU love by what MORAGA your lives
of SERCEN before and GETHENA your final journey VEY music your new eternity

```

#### Official Translation of the Falmer Script:

```
And so it was that your people were given passage to our steam gardens, and the protections of our power.
Many of your people had perished under the roaring, snow-throated kings of Mora,
and your wills were broken, and we heard you, and sent our machines against your enemies, to thereby take you under.
Only by the grace of the Dwemer did your culture survive,
and only by the fifteen-and-one tones did your new lives begin.
We do not desire thanks, for we do not believe in it. We do not ask for gratitude, for we do not believe in it.
We only request you partake of the symbol of our bond, the fruit of the stones around us.
And as your vision clouds, as the darkness sets in, fear not.
Know only our mercy and the radiance of our affection, which unbinds your bones
to the earth before, and sets your final path to the music of your new eternity.[UOL 1]

```
