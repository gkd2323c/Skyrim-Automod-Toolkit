[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-npc-Uraccen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Uraccen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Uraccen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Uraccen.jpg) Uraccen **Uraccen** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [witchblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchblade) held prisoner in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md). He has been sentenced to life in prison for being a member of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). He is the father of [Uaile](Skyrim_Uaile.md), an undercover Forsworn agent.

## Dialogue
He is the first prisoner that you can talk to when you arrive. He will greet you with:

*"What are you in for, new blood?"* You can choose between three possible replies, the second of which is based on your current bounty:

| | Your Dialogue | Uraccen's Reply |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Nothing. I'm innocent.** | *"Innocent? So was I, for the first one. The other murders were all me, though."* |
| 2 | **Theft.** (no bounty) | *"Petty thieving, huh? Just like poor [Grisvar](Skyrim_Grisvar_the_Unlucky.md). Hopefully you'll learn your lesson faster than he has."* |
| **Assault.** (bounty = 1-999) | *"Violent one, huh? Best keep that to yourself, new blood. Others find out, they'll consider that a challenge."* | |
| **Murder.** (bounty = 1000+) | | |
| 3 | **Killing a guy who asked too many questions.** | |

Regardless of your response, he will then go on with:

*"My advice? Serve your time at the pickaxe and get out. You don't want to end up getting a shiv in the guts over a bottle of Skooma."* **Skooma? What's that?** *"Bottles of [Moon Sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar). [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) use it as a... pick me up. Good way to pass the time. Prisoners smuggle the stuff in. Only currency we have down here."* **A shiv?** *"Small blade. Easy to hide. I mean, sure, you could just swing a pickaxe into someone's face, but people tend to see that coming. Got a problem with a prisoner? Get a shiv. Hear Grisvar has a spare, if you could get him to part with it."* **Where are the guards?** *"They come in here once a week to clean out the bodies, grab any ore we've mined, and beat down the troublemakers. That's the only time when we get food, too. And if there's not enough ore mined up, we don't get any."* **What are you in for?** *"A [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) nobleman I served was stabbed in the night. Wasn't me, but I knew I'd be blamed. So I ran. Joined the Forsworn. Started killing. Got caught. Now I'm here."* **Why'd you join the Forsworn?** *"Because life was better under the old ways. No Nords and their laws. One day, the Forsworn will paint the walls of Markarth in their blood."* **(If you're not a Nord)** *"Because life was better under the old ways. No Nords and their laws. One day, the Forsworn will paint the walls of Markarth in your kinsmen's blood. Best you not be there on that day, Nord."* **(If you are a Nord)** After this his greetings can be:

*"I left behind my daughter Uaile when I was taken."* *"How old is my daughter now, I wonder? In prison, you lose track of time."* *"I'm just another Forsworn sent to rot here in this mine."*
## Quest-Related Events

### No One Escapes Cidhna Mine
**Where's Madanach?** *"If you're asking, that means you're the new lifer. Tough luck, friend. Those guards sold you out but good. No one talks to Madanach, I'm afraid. Not without getting past [Borkul the Beast](Skyrim_Borkul_the_Beast.md)... And you don't want to talk to Borkul the Beast."* **Borkul the Beast?** *"Madanach's guard. Big, even for an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md). Heard he ripped a man's arm off and beat him to death with it. He's old-fashioned like that."* If you decided to escape with Madanach, Uraccen cannot be talked to and will only say:

*"No turning back now."*
## Unused Dialogue
In the game's Dialogue Views files, there's an earlier version of Uraccen's guard-related question. He would've mentioned [Urzoga gra-Shugurz](Skyrim_Urzoga_gra-Shugurz.md) by name. No one ever mentions her by name in the final game:

**Where are the guards?** *"Aren't any. Besides Urzoga and her goons at the gate, we're all alone in this hole. They always keep one gate closed, and you can only open them from the inside. No way out but to serve your time."*

## Notes
- While his backstory is kept pretty ambiguous in the final game, [unused dialogue](Skyrim_Uaile.md#Unused_Dialogue) for his daughter, related to multiple unused quests, paint a different picture of Uraccen. In the final game, he's depicted as merely a Reachman who joined the Forsworn after a Nord nobleman he served under was murdered. However, the early dialogue implies that he was a completely innocent man, until he was framed for being a member of the Forsworn by [Degaine](Skyrim_Degaine.md) on orders of [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md), so that the latter could take Uraccen's daughter Uaile into service, because he fancied her. It's also implied that Uraccen is the brother of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md)'s [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md).
