# Briricca Company

The Briricca Company of Cyrodiil<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-POM-1)</sup> was a company operating in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Not much is known about their trade, but an agent of the company had deep knowledge of [dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [slavery and slave trade](Lore_Slavery.md#Dunmer).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-POM-1)</sup>
