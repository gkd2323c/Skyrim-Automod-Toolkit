# Firstmages

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg/200px-ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive.jpg) Aetherian Archive The Firstmages were a group of powerful mages that resided in [Aetherian Archive](Lore_Aetherian_Archive.md) in [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md). Said to be unparalleled in the arcane arts, they began to occupy the tower in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and it was here that they found the Great Source, which granted the Firstmages long lives. This allowed them to study indefinitely, and many of their magical experiments took place in the archive's upper floors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOArn-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOEar-2)</sup>

When the [Celestial Mage](Lore_The_Mage.md) arrived during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), her very presence destroyed the Great Source. Many of the Firstmages entered her service as guardians and began to worship her, although a small minority avoided the upper floors in fear of their lives.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAnir-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-COTLO1-4)</sup> The [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) were made aware of the opening of the Archive, and flocked to the tower in an attempt to be the first to scale it. The Undaunted ascended the tower and fought through the Mage's guardians before defeating the Celestial herself in battle. <sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMT-5)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_%28Hero%27s_Return%29.jpg/200px-ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_%28Hero%27s_Return%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_(Hero%27s_Return).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Aetherian_Archive_(Hero%27s_Return).jpg) Within the Aetherian Archive At some point, the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) had placed arcane wards around the [Aetherian Archive](Lore_Aetherian_Archive.md), the remnant of the "once-Great Source of knowledge and power", guarded by the fractured fraternity of the Firstmages, in order to prevent its misuse. In 2E 582, Brulo Rlane, a wizard trained by [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) himself, went rogue and broke through the wards and entered the tower, seeking to claim the Great Source for his own. As Brulo's actions had the potential to threaten all of Tamriel, Arnure of the Firstmages reached out to Galerion for aid. In an attempt to delay his former master, Brulo opened planar breaches that unleashed [shadowy](Lore_Shadow.md) [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) beings to slow Galerion’s progress. While Galerion began sealing the rifts, he summoned the Vestige to the Archive to battle the Daedra. As the Vestige fought through the summoned forces, including a powerful [Gloam Knight](Lore_Gloam_Knight.md), Galerion confronted and ultimately defeated Brulo in a magical duel. With the danger passed, Galerion remarked that Nirn would sleep peacefully, unaware of how close it had come to disaster. The two quickly departed from the tower, as the power of the Great Source was too much for either the Vestige or Galerion to linger around long.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Galerion Rogue-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Rogue-7)</sup>
