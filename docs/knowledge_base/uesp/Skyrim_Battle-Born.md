# Battle-Born

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SR-npc-Battle-Borns.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Battle-Borns.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Battle-Borns.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Battle-Borns.jpg) The Battle-Borns The **Battle-Borns** are a rich, influential clan in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) who are staunch supporters of the Empire and the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). Before the civil war, the Battle-Borns were old friends with their neighbors, the equally venerable [Gray-Manes](Skyrim_Gray-Mane.md). However, since the Gray-Manes have sided with the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), the clans are now feuding.

[House of Clan Battle-Born](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Battle-Born.md) stands across the street from the Gray-Mane [residence](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md). [Olfrid](Skyrim_Olfrid_Battle-Born.md) is the clan's vocal patriarch. Other Battle-Borns include [Alfhild](Skyrim_Alfhild_Battle-Born.md), [Bergritte](Skyrim_Bergritte_Battle-Born.md), [Idolaf](Skyrim_Idolaf_Battle-Born.md) and [Jon](Skyrim_Jon_Battle-Born.md). [Lars](Skyrim_Lars_Battle-Born.md) is the youngest member, who is usually seen being bullied by fellow child [Braith](Skyrim_Braith.md).

The Battle-Borns' success is due in part to their owning [Battle-Born Farm](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md) just outside the city, which is a very lucrative business.

## Notes
- The exact relationship of the different Battle-Borns is somewhat convoluted. Olfrid refers to Idolaf as his son and the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) marks him as such, even though Idolaf is married to Olfrid's daughter. Idolaf is also marked as Jon's father, even though all dialogue indicates that Jon is either his brother-in-law or his brother.
