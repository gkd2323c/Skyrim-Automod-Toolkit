# Mammal

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-creature-Cow_02.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Cow_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Cow_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Cow_02.jpg) A cow, a type of mammal There are many species of mammals on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCYB-1)</sup> Mammals can produce [milk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Milk) which they use to feed their young,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Suckling Pig-3)</sup> and most possess hair or fur.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ga E-4)</sup> Mammals are known for being warm-blooded and resilient to various climates.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OArg-5)</sup> Those that live in the sea often have insulating blubber.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Eot O-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

Many of the intelligent [races](Lore_Race.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) are mammalian.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OArg-5)</sup> Such examples include: [Men](Lore_Men.md), [Mer](Lore_Mer.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Elf Breast Feeding-8)</sup> [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> and [Imga](Lore_Imga.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Io D-11)</sup> However, the aforementioned examples and other intelligent races are not included on this list for simplicity.
