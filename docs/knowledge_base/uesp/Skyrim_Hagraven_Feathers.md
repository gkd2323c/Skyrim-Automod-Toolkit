## Ingredients
64 guaranteed samples can be found in 30 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 9 in [Hag's End](Skyrim_Hag%27s_End.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 7 around [Witchmist Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Witchmist_Grove) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 5 in [Serpent's Bluff Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Serpent%27s_Bluff_Ruins) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 4 around [Cradle Stone Tower](Skyrim_Cradle_Stone_Tower.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Deepwood Redoubt](Skyrim_Deepwood_Redoubt.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 inside [Deepwood Vale](Skyrim_Deepwood_Vale.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in [Nightcaller Temple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightcaller_Temple) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 around [Orphan Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orphan_Rock) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Hagraven feathers found strewn around hagraven dwellings such as the one at Witchmist Grove will respawn after a time, on a similar timeline as plant regrowth.

## Creatures
All [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) are guaranteed to drop one feather, and may drop as many as three. This includes the [Glenmoril Witches](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch.md) and named hagravens such as [Drascua](Skyrim_Drascua.md), [Melka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Melka), and [Moira](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moira).

## Related Quests
- **[A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md)**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sam_Guevenne), as you recover from a bad hangover.

## Notes
- During the quest [A Night to Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md), you are asked to pick up hagraven feathers (among other items). As a result, all hagraven feathers in your inventory will be marked as quest items until the end of the quest. This makes all your feathers weightless, but they cannot be used or removed from your inventory.
