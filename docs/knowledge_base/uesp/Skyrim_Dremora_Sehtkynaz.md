[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Dremora_Sehtkynaz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dremora_Sehtkynaz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Sehtkynaz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Sehtkynaz.jpg) Dremora Sehtkynaz **Dremora Sehtkynaz** is a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) mage whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos) as part of the [Arms of Chaos](Skyrim_Arms_of_Chaos.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins) in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: Dremora Sehtkynaz, [Dremora Lyrkynaz](Skyrim_Dremora_Lyrkynaz.md), and [Dremora Sovereign](Skyrim_Dremora_Sovereign.md). Dremora Sehtkynaz is the first Dremora to appear.

Dremora Sehtkynaz utilizes powerful [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) magic in combat and can summon a [frost atronach](Skyrim_Frost_Atronach.md). He carries a dagger and mace along with a [Blue Sigil Stone](Skyrim_Blue_Sigil_Stone.md). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) when killed.
