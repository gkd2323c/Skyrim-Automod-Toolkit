Arctus was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) and the High Priest of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). He preached the Demented beliefs of [Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md) in the Sacellum of [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md). His rival was [Dervenin](Lore_Dervenin.md), the Priest of [Mania](Lore_Mania.md). Arctus came to power within the Sacellum at the whim of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md); he could only preach his Demented teachings if the [Cold Flame of Agnon](Lore_Cold_Flame_of_Agnon.md) was lit for Dementia. He was responsible for aiding anyone trying to replace the Duke of Dementia, which requires a sacrifice of the current duke's heart to Arden-Sul.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shivering-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SI-npc-Arctus.jpg/200px-SI-npc-Arctus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Arctus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Arctus.jpg) Arctus circa 3E 433 (Oblivion) As High Priest, Arctus helped Sheogorath's [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) on several occasions, working to halt the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shivering-1)</sup>

Eventually, his rival Dervenin no longer held his position as High Priest of Mania.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It is unclear what this meant for his own tenure as High Priest.
