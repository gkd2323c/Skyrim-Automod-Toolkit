# Dwarven Ruins

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-place-Mzulft.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mzulft.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mzulft.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mzulft.jpg) Mzulft, a large Dwarven ruin [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) This page lists all the **Dwarven ruins** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md)** — A large Dwarven ruin southwest of Winterhold containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Alftand)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Arkngthamz](Skyrim_Arkngthamz.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small Dwarven ruin southeast of Dushnikh Yal containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Arkngthamz+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Avanchnzel](Skyrim_Avanchnzel.md)** — A medium-sized Dwarven ruin west of Riften containing Dwarven automatons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Avanchnzel)
- **[Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md)** — An immense, naturally lit cavern inhabited by hordes of Falmer and containing the ruins of a Dwemer city. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blackreach)
- **[Debate Hall](Skyrim_Debate_Hall.md)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by Falmer and their servants.
- **[Derelict Pumphouse](Skyrim_Derelict_Pumphouse.md)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by a Dwarven spider.
- **[Farm Overseer's House](Skyrim_Farm_Overseer%27s_House.md)** — A small Dwarven building in Blackreach.
- **[Hall of Rumination](Skyrim_Hall_of_Rumination.md)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by Falmer and their servants.
- **[Pumping Station](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pumping_Station)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by Falmer servants.
- **[Reeking Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reeking_Tower)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by frostbite spiders.
- **[Silent City Catacombs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_City_Catacombs)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach occupied by Falmer.
- **[Silent Ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silent_Ruin)** — A small Dwarven ruin in Blackreach.
- **[Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory)** — A small Dwarven building in Blackreach serving as Sinderion's alchemical foothold in the cavern.
- **[War Quarters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Quarters)** — A small Dwarven building in Blackreach.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md)** — A large Dwarven ruin west-northwest of Karthwasten containing Afflicted and Dwarven automatons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bthardamz)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Deep Folk Crossing](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md)** — An ancient Dwarven bridge over a weir with structures at either end in the northern part of the Reach. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Deep+Folk+Crossing)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A large Dwarven ruin east of the Water Stone containing rieklings, albino spiders, oil spiders, and Dwarven automatons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Fahlbtharz)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Great Lift at Alftand](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Alftand.md)** — A Dwarven lift to Blackreach located northeast of Nightgate Inn and southeast of Wayward Pass. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Great+Lift+at+Alftand)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Great Lift at Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Mzinchaleft.md)** — A Dwarven lift to Blackreach located south of Mzinchaleft. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Great+Lift+at+Mzinchaleft)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Great Lift at Raldbthar](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Raldbthar.md)** — A Dwarven lift to Blackreach located west-southwest of Anga's Mill and east of Irkngthand. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Great+Lift+at+Raldbthar)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Irkngthand](Skyrim_Irkngthand.md)** — A large Dwarven ruin west of Windhelm containing bandits, Dwarven automatons, Falmer, frostbite spiders, and skeevers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Irkngthand)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Kagrenzel](Skyrim_Kagrenzel.md)** — A small Dwarven ruin southeast of Windhelm containing Falmer. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Kagrenzel)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Kagrumez](Skyrim_Kagrumez.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A small Dwarven ruin south of the Temple of Miraak. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Kagrumez)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md)** — A medium-sized Dwarven ruin southwest of Dawnstar containing bandits, Dwarven automatons, Falmer, and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mzinchaleft)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft)** — A large Dwarven ruin south-southeast of Windhelm containing Dwarven automatons, Falmer, and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mzulft)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Nchardak](Skyrim_Nchardak.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A large Dwarven ruin north-northeast of Tel Mithryn. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Nchardak)
- **[Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel)** — A large Dwarven ruin accessed from inside Understone Keep. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nchuand-Zel)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Raldbthar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar)** — A medium-sized Dwarven ruin west-southwest of Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Raldbthar)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Reachwind Eyrie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwind_Eyrie)** — A small Dwarven tower east-southeast of Markarth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Reachwind+Eyrie)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Ruins of Bthalft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft)** — A small Dwarven ruin south of Ivarstead containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ruins+of+Bthalft)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Ruins of Rkund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Rkund)** — A small Dwarven ruin in the mountains southwest of Riften containing a wispmother and wisps. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ruins+of+Rkund)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md)** — A small Dwarven tower in Blackreach. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Tower+of+Mzark)
