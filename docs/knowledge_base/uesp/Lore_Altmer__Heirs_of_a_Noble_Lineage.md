Altmer: Heirs of a Noble Lineage *A plaque extolling the Altmer and their accomplishments* *
---
As the (self-proclaimed) purest line descended from the ancient [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) people have built an empire of grace, refinement, and strength. In this room are several artifacts showcasing their remarkable artistic accomplishments and their heavy burden of rulership.
