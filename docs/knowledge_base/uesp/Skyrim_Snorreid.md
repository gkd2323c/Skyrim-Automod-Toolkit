[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Snorreid.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Snorreid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snorreid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Snorreid.jpg) Snorreid **Snorreid** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who lives in the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md). He spends his days idly sleeping on one of bedrolls in the top section of the warehouse's southern part. He will only rise from his slumber should he catch you trespassing.

His modest life means his only possessions are the [farmer clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd) he wears.
