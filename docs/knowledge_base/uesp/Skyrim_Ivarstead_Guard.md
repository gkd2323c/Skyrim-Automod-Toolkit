[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard.jpg) Ivarstead Guard (male) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard_Female.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivarstead_Guard_Female.jpg) Ivarstead Guard (female) **Ivarstead Guards** are special [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) that can be found at [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). If [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) is conquered by the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), they will all be replaced with [Imperial Guards](Skyrim_Imperial_Guard.md).

They are all clad in [Riften guard armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Armor), a matching [helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Helmet) and [shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Shield), and a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md). They are armed with a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) with 15 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and either a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) or a [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe) (all three variations will be iron quality at level 1-3 and steel quality from level 4). Additionally, they have a 50% chance of carrying a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch) and a slim chance of carrying some food, drink or an amulet.

Like other guards, Ivarstead guards have standard [guard dialogue](Skyrim_Guard_Dialogue.md).

There are four guards stationed in Ivarstead:

- Two guards patrol the southeastern part of town, from the bridge to the path leading to [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md).
- Two guards patrol the northeastern part of town, from [Fellstar Farm](Skyrim_Fellstar_Farm.md) to the bridge leading to the 7,000 steps.
