It contains only one zone, *[Sleeping Tree Cave](#Sleeping_Tree_Cave)*.

## Related Quests
- **[Bounty: Giant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Giant)**: Kill a problematic [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) for a bounty reward. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Sleeping Tree Cave](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md)**: Question [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) about an interesting [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ysolda%27s_Message) you found on a [dead Orc](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulag).

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The camp is almost due west of the [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md). The [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) inhabiting the camp will attack those who ignore their warnings to leave. The [Sleeping Tree](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sleeping_Tree) grows from a pool in the center of the camp along with two [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) plants. The tree emits a purplish glow, and its [sap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sleeping_Tree_Sap) increases health for a limited time with temporary side effects of slowed movement and blurred vision. You can use a sap spigot on the tree to harvest a sample of Sleeping Tree Sap every three in-game days.

### Sleeping Tree Cave
The entrance to the cave is northwest of the tree. The cave is occupied by a single giant that seems to have killed an Orc named [Ulag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulag). A dose of Sleeping Tree sap and [Ysolda's message](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ysolda%27s_Message) can be found on his body. Next to Ulag's body is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing [leveled loot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists). Three [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) are atop a ledge to the right of the chest, and an [orichalcum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein) is along the south wall next to a [mammoth cheese bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl).

Reading Ysolda's message activates a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Cave_(quest).md) in which [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md) will offer money for Sleeping Tree sap. During the quest, Ysolda will tell you two possible origin stories of the tree if asked: that the tree is a piece of the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) which broke off when the volcano [erupted](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5), or that it was a spore that fell off a flying island. The latter story is likely a [reference](Skyrim_Elder_Scrolls_Historical_References.md#The_Infernal_City_and_Lord_of_Souls) to the book *[The Infernal City](Lore_The_Infernal_City.md)*.

## Notes
- Just southeast of the camp is an unmarked circle of stones containing [a puzzle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spinning_Symbol) and treasure. The puzzle is a standard stone-matching puzzle, with a handle in the middle of the circle next to a stone archway. The stone to the east should be turned to show a snake toward the handle, the stone to the west should show a whale, and the stone to the north should show an eagle. Pull the handle to open a pit in the ground and get the treasure, which includes a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Hypothetical Treachery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Hypothetical_Treachery)* in a chest.
- Opposite the above circle of stones on a small hill east of the camp are some Nordic ruins with an altar in the center. Lying on this altar is a deceased conjurer, some alchemy ingredients, and a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Liminal Bridges](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Liminal_Bridges)*. On the northeast side of the altar, hidden under vegetation, is another dead conjurer. Two hostile [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) inhabit the ruins. To the northeast is a unique third skeleton wearing a [black mage hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Hood) standing by three mysterious graves.
- Near the camp to the north is a mammoth skeleton with a human skeleton inside.

## Bugs
- The Sleeping Tree sap spigot can become detached from the tree and fall off. This isn't a problem, as it still gives you the sap even when it's no longer attached to the tree. More problematically, the spigot can also float off and become hard to locate. Area effect spells such as Fireball will affect the spigot, so if it comes off the tree, refrain from using such spells near the pond. You can drag the spigot like any other world item, so if you prefer to keep it safe and don't mind walking, you can take the spigot to a safe place near a fast travel point. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can use the [console command](Skyrim_Console.md) `prid 000AED8B` followed by `moveto player` to teleport the spigot to you if it gets lost. For the best results, stand on even ground. The spigot might otherwise appear inside the terrain where you cannot access it.
