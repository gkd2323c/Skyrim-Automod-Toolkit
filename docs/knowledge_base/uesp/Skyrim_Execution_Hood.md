[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-item-Execution_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Execution_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Execution_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Execution_Hood.jpg) Execution Hood [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-item-Execution_Hood_02.jpg/200px-SR-item-Execution_Hood_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Execution_Hood_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Execution_Hood_02.jpg) The white, unused Execution Hood An Execution Hood is a face-obscuring hood that can be found on [Alea Quintus](Skyrim_Alea_Quintus.md), [Fultheim the Fearless](Skyrim_Fultheim_the_Fearless.md), and [Vasha](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vasha). The Execution Hood conceals the entirety of the face behind its black threads, to hide the identity of the person beneath it, or those judging the wearer. While it looks like it would obstruct eyesight if worn, putting it on will not block your vision.

## Related Quests
- **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md)**: Repay your debt to [the Family](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), and show you are willing to kill on command.

## Notes
- Despite its appearance, this item has the "Armor Heavy", "Armor Helmet", and "Armor Material Dwarven" keywords attached to it.
- A white version of this hood (0005a9df) also exists, although it cannot be found under normal circumstances and is also marked as unplayable.
- When this item is removed from a dead body, the body's eyes will eerily remain open for a short time after it is removed before they quickly close.
- This item can be equipped simultaneously with a [Penitus Oculatus Helmet](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Helmet.md), [Falmer Helmet](Skyrim_Falmer_Helmet.md) or [Jagged Crown](Skyrim_Jagged_Crown.md) by first equipping a [Dragon Priest Mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) together with the helmet and then swamping the mask for the hood.
