[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Gavaudon was found Gavaudon was a [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) fiefdom in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), northeast of the mouth of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It was bordered by [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) to the west, the Bjoulsae River to the south, and the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) hedged the remainder of the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Its capital was the city of Gavaudon. The [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md) clan was the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in Gavaudon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DF-2)</sup>

During the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), Gavaudon was caught in a battle between [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) and [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).
