[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Stromm.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Stromm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stromm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stromm.jpg) Stromm **Stromm** was a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_CombatMageConjurer.md) who, along with other mages from [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), ignored the advice of [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) and ventured into the [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) [Nchuand-Zel](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel.md). He stayed behind to study the [living quarters](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel_Quarters.md) of the ruin while his colleagues forged ahead. He kept a [diary](Skyrim_Stromm%27s_Diary.md) during their journey into the ruin, which recorded, among other things, their fight against the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), a fight they eventually lost.
