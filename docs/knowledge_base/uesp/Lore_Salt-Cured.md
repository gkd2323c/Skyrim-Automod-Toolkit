# Salt-Cured

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg/200px-ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Western_Solstice.jpg) Solstice [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/ON-furnishing-Argonian_Houseboat.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Argonian_Houseboat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Argonian_Houseboat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Argonian_Houseboat.jpg) Tide-Born twin-hulled canoe The Salt-Cured were a sub-tribe of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) that lived on the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), considered part of the greater [Tide-Born tribe](Lore_Tide-Born.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AWS-1)</sup>

They were known as a group composed mostly of fishermen who spent much of their lives on the water in large twin-hulled canoes, with living platforms spanning the space between the hulls. They generally remained near the reefs in a loose flotilla, but traveled into deeper waters of the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md) when following the [fish](Lore_Fish.md). The Salt-Cured were also known to use smaller boats to journey back to the island to trade goods with other sub-tribes and at the village of Shell-Tide.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AWS-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Some Tide-Born Argonians believed that dreams borne on the tide carried omens, which is why they slept in their houseboats even during the fiercest storms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AH-2)</sup>
