Items - [Steel Horse Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Horse_Armor)

## Notes
- This Creation, combined with [Horse Armor - Elven](Skyrim_Horse_Armor_-_Elven.md), recreate the infamous [Horse Armor Pack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Horse_Armor_Pack) from *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
- Equipping a horse with Horse Armor will give it a temporary health boost of 1000.
- Horse Armor and Saddles cannot be worn at the same time. So If the saddle is changed while the horse is in horse armor the armor will be removed and placed into your inventory. It can be placed back on the horse again for 50 gold (as long as it's in your inventory. Otherwise it'll be another 500 gold.) The saddles will cost 100 gold to be placed every time.
- The Steel Horse Armor weighs 35 in carry weight while the Elven Horse Armor weighs 25 in carry weight
