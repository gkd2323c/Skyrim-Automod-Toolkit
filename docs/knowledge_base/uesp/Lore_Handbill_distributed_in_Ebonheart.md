Handbill distributed in Ebonheart *An advertisement for stonemasons to help repair Ebonheart Pact keeps*
---
[![ON-concept-Keep walls.png](https://images.uesp.net/6/6f/ON-concept-Keep_walls.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Keep_walls.png)
- Experienced stonemasons needed!

- Receive excellent pay, see beautiful [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), find steady work on incredible ancient fortifications, and receive the adoration of the people as you repair and maintain keeps under [Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) control. Your efforts will help us secure victory in the most important battle of our time.

- Must have proof of apprenticeship with a registered master mason, provide own tools, and be ready for immediate departure. Combat training a plus! Inquire at your local barracks.
