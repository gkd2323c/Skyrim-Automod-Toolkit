* Only appears after [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md).
** Only appears after the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) quest [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md).
*** Only appears after the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) quest [Message to Whiterun](Skyrim_Message_to_Whiterun_(Imperial).md).
**** Owns [Chillfurrow Farm](Skyrim_Chillfurrow_Farm.md) but sleeps here.
† This person only sells goods at the market.

†† Backup innkeeper for [Hulda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hulda); also backup merchant for [Belethor](Skyrim_Belethor.md).
