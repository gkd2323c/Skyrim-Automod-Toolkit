Thassad II was [Crown](Lore_Crown.md) High King of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) until [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862). Though it was traditionally a Forebear city, the High King of the Crowns sat in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md)'s [palace](Lore_Samuruik.md). Thassad strook a truce with [Baron Volag](Lore_Volag.md) during his reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md) set up a tower on his [island](Lore_Isle_of_N%27Gasta.md) during Thassad's reign.

Upon his death by natural causes, Sentinel was taken by the Forebears, led by Volag. His son, [Prince A'tor](Lore_A%27tor.md), attempted to recapture the city but was eventually defeated by combined forces of the Forebears and [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s fleet at the [Battle of Hunding Bay](Lore_Battle_of_Hunding_Bay.md) near [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGIntroduction-4)</sup>

## Notes
