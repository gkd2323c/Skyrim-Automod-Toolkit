[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-npc-Silda_the_Unseen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silda_the_Unseen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silda_the_Unseen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silda_the_Unseen.jpg) Silda the Unseen warming her hands **Silda the Unseen** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](Skyrim_TrainerPickpocketExpert.md) living as a [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). She is an expert [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) for the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skill. Due to her life on the street, she is also one of three persons present when the Butcher strikes again during [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md).

She lives her entire life walking the streets of snowy Windhelm, at all times trying to get a septim from the townsfolk or keep warm by the fireplaces scattered around town. At 8am she can be found in front of the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md), at 4pm she stands between the main gate and [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) and at midnight she will head over to the Market and warm her hands at the forge. Once you have met the [requirements](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md#How_to_Start_the_Quest) to trigger Blood on the Ice, she can be found in the graveyard, along with the other witnesses, all of them looking at the dead body of [Susanna the Wicked](Skyrim_Susanna_the_Wicked.md).

She wears typical beggar clothing consisting of [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md) and [ragged boots](Skyrim_Ragged_Boots.md). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [lower class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
She has several lines prepared for begging towards either you or other citizens:

- *"Spare a coin for a poor old woman?"*
- *"A few septims for my supper is all I ask."*
- *"I ask only for a little charity."*
- *"Will you spare a few coins to help someone who's got nothing?"*
- *"I ain't askin' for much, just a few septims."*
- *"The Divines smile on those who show mercy an' charity."*

When you talk to her:

- *"A few septims ain't nothing. You can spare that, can't you?"*
- *"Spare a coin for a poor old woman, milord/milady?"*
- *"Spare a coin? Talos rewards the generous."*
- *"I hope the wind dies down a bit before tonight. Hard when you've got to sleep outside."*

- **Train me to be a better Pickpocket.**
- *"Up to no good, are we? I'll teach you, but it'll cost you."*

When finishing a conversation she can say:

- *"The Divines smile on a charitable soul."*
- *"Don't forget them who's less fortunate than yourself."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Blood on the Ice
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-quest-Blood_on_the_Ice2.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Blood_on_the_Ice2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Blood_on_the_Ice2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Blood_on_the_Ice2.jpg) Silda at the graveyard (top left) Once [Blood on the Ice](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md) is initiated, Silda will be in the graveyard, and will be shocked:

- *"Another one. Terrible."*
- **Did you see what happened here?**
- *"I heard a scream and came running, but she was already... like this... when I got here."*

If you have yet to complete Blood on the Ice during her normal dialogue she can say:

- *"Nervous being alone at night. Especially these days, with the Butcher prowling the streets."*

## Notes
- Despite her claiming to sleep on the street, she's never actually seen doing so. In fact, Silda never sleeps at all due to her lacking a sleep package. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.5, addresses this issue. She now sleeps from 3am until 8am on a bedroll inside of the [Temple of Talos](Skyrim_Temple_of_Talos.md), left of the entrance. This fix retroactively makes her statement make even less sense, though.
