[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-npc-Teeba-Ei.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Teeba-Ei.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teeba-Ei.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Teeba-Ei.jpg) Teeba-Ei **Teeba-Ei** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [scout](Skyrim_CombatScout.md) working as a miner at [Rockwallow Mine](Skyrim_Rockwallow_Mine.md) in [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md). If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), he will become the [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) for his former employer, [Sorli the Builder](Skyrim_Sorli_the_Builder.md).

Like the rest of the miners employed at Stonehills, Teeba-Ei sleeps in the small camp outside Rockwallow Mine every night between 1am and 6am. After waking up he heads directly into the mine and spends twelve hours mining [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md), at times pausing to eat or relax. At 6pm he heads down to the camp and enjoys a two-hour dinner with his friend [Jesper](Skyrim_Jesper.md), before he heads back into the mine, working until his bedtime at 1am.

One major exception to his daily routine occurs if the Stormcloaks take over Hjaalmarch as part of the civil war. His employer Sorli the Builder will then be appointed [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) and will move to [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). Teeba-Ei will become her housecarl and will stand by her side inside [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md) every day between 8am and 8pm. Once the court closes, he returns to Stonehills and works and sleeps as before. At 8am on the next morning he returns to Morthal and his new duties as housecarl. (see [bugs](#Bugs))

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1da) with [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). He carries his [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Longhouse.md) to the nonexistent longhouse, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Teeba-Ei is obviously not pleased with Skyrim's cold weather.

- *"Stonehills' warmth is a welcome change in this cold, bleak land."*

- *"Teeba-Ei the hatchling never imagined working a mine in this cold place. Greater things were dreamt of."*

- *"This is not where Teeba-Ei was meant to live out the rest of his days."*

### Conversations
Every once in a while, Teeba-Ei and mine boss [Gestur Rockbreaker](Skyrim_Gestur_Rockbreaker.md) will have a brief conversation.

- **Gestur:** *"Is everything well with you, my scaly friend?"*
- **Teeba:** *"Teeba-Ei is cold, but Teeba-Ei works hard as always."*
- **Gestur:** *"Yes, well... That's all we ask of you. Carry on."*
- **Teeba:** *"Yes sir."*

## Notes
- Even after he is appointed housecarl, unlike most new members of a Jarl's court, Teeba-Ei's dialogue doesn't change.
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

- "*The sole survivor of a wrecked trader vessel, Teeba-Ei wandered into Stonehills nearly frozen to death. He was taken in and cared for, and was grateful that few questions were asked about how he came to be there. He now gladly serves Sorli, working the mine, and is willing to follow her everywhere, even if that means going to Morthal as her Housecarl.*"

## Bugs
- Because the housecarl alias in the `Dialogue Morthal` quest only provides him a package keeping him close to the jarl from 8am to 8pm, outside of those times, Teeba will resume his old schedule. Because this schedule expects him to be in Stonehills, he will start wandering from Morthal to Stonehills every night. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
- Teeba-Ei carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Longhouse.md) to a nonexistent longhouse. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- Pactur may accuse Teeba-Ei of trespassing in Highmoon Hall because Teeba-Ei was never assigned to the relevant [faction](Skyrim_Morthal_Longhouse_Faction_(Both_Sides).md) like all the other possible occupants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
