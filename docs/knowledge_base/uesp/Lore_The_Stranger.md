[These are the words of the prophecy called "The Stranger." [I](Lore_Nerevarine.md) wrote them down as she spoke them to me.]

When earth is [sundered](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), and skies [choked black](Lore_The_Blight.md),
And sleepers serve the [seven curses](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md),
To the hearth there comes a stranger,
Journeyed far 'neath [moon and star](Lore_Azura.md).

Though stark-born to sire uncertain,
His [aspect](Lore_Birthsigns.md) marks his certain fate.
[Wicked](Lore_Sixth_House.md) stalk him, [righteous](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) curse him.
[Prophets](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md) speak, but all deny.

Many trials make manifest
The stranger's fate, the curses' bane.
Many [touchstones](Lore_The_Seven_Visions.md) try the stranger.
Many fall, but [one remains](Lore_Nerevarine.md).
