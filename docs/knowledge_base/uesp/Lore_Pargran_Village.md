[3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389) **Pargran Village** was a village located in eastern Skyrim, roughly equidistant to [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), and [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). Pargran Village had road connections to [Vernim Wood](Lore_Vernim_Wood.md) and [Dragon Wood](Lore_Dragon_Wood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Pargran Village was an active settlement ruled by Lord Kjarke. It had a rivalry with Reich Corigate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>
