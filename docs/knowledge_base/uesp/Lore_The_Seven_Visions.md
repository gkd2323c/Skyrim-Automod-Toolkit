The Seven Visions *The prophecy that foretells of the Nerevarine*
---
[These are the words of the prophecy called "Seven Visions of Seven Trials of the Incarnate." [I](Lore_Nerevarine.md) wrote them down as she spoke them to me.]

seven trials
What he puts his hand to, that shall be done.
What is left undone, that shall be done.

first trial
On a [certain day](Lore_Birthsigns.md) to uncertain parents
[Incarnate](Lore_Nerevarine.md) [moon and star reborn](Lore_Nerevar.md).

second trial
Neither [blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) nor age can harm him.
The [Curse-of-Flesh](Lore_Corprus.md) before him flies.

third trial
In caverns dark [Azura](Lore_Azura.md)'s eye sees
And makes to shine the moon and star.

fourth trial
A stranger's voice unites the [Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md).
Three Halls call him Hortator.

fifth trial
A stranger's hand unites the [Velothi](Lore_Ashlanders.md).
Four Tribes call him Nerevarine.

sixth trial
He honors blood of the [tribe unmourned](Lore_House_Dagoth.md).
He eats their sin, and is reborn.

[seventh trial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Tribunal:The_Mad_God)
His mercy [frees](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md) the [cursed false gods](Lore_Tribunal.md),
Binds [the broken](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), redeems [the mad](Lore_Almalexia.md).

one destiny
He speaks the law for [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md)'s [people](Lore_Dunmer.md).
He speaks for [their land](Lore_Morrowind.md), and names them great.
