[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg) Khenarthi guiding a spirit to the Sands Behind the Stars The Sands Behind the Stars, also known as **Llesw'er**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HWCTF-1)</sup> is the realm to which the souls of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) travel at the end of their days. Described as a paradise promised to the Khajiit by the [Riddle'Thar](Lore_Riddle%27Thar.md), souls are carried there by [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s embrace, as long as they have followed the true path of the moons. Choice plays a central role in the process of death and rebirth for Khajiit, who have to earn their way to paradise, as Khenarthi rewards their faithfulness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SWKS-2)</sup> It is said that the realm is filled with dunes formed of [sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md), and a "warmth without end".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ta T-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLMD-4)</sup> Legends state that the name "Llesw'er" may have been the inspiration for name of the Khajiiti homeland, [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1E-5)</sup> It is said that Khajiiti spirits await here until the [Next Pounce](Lore_Kalpa.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLMD-4)</sup> where Khenarthi will call upon their combined might to fight for creation at the end of time.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSS-6)</sup> It is also believed that it is in the [Moonshadow](Lore_Moonshadow.md) where [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) tends the Gates of the Crossing—the bridge between Nirni and the afterlife beyond.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONMM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONSR-8)</sup> It is at this place where Khajiit souls initially go, and if deemed worthy, they are then guided to the Sands Behind the Stars.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Book-9)</sup><sup>:152</sup>
