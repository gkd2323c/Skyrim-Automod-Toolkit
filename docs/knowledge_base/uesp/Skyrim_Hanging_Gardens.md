Hanging Gardens *A travel guide to the Hanging Gardens of Wasten Coridale, written in both Dwemeris and Aldmeris*
---
*This book was apparently written in [Dwemer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwemer_Alphabet) and translated to [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris.md). Only fragments of the Aldmeris is readable, but it may be enough for a scholar of Aldmeris to translate fragments of other [Dwemer](Skyrim_Dwemer.md) books.*

...guide [Altmer](Skyrim_Altmer.md) -Estrial led with foot-flames for the [town](Lore_Wasten_Coridale.md) -center where lay dead the quadrangular gardens...

...asked the foundations and chains and vessels their naming places...

...why they did not use solid sound to teach escape from the [Earth Bones](Lore_Earth_Bones.md) nor nourished them with frozen flames...

....the word I shall have once written of, this "art" our lesser cousins speak of when their admirable ignorance...

...but neither words nor experience cleanses the essence of the strange and terrible ways of defying our ancestors' transient rules.

*The translation ends with a comment in Dwemer in a different hand, which you may be translated as follows:*

"Put down your ardent cutting-globes, Nbthld. Your Aldmeris has the correct words, but they cannot be properly misinterpreted."
