# Old Cyrodilic

This article is about the language of antiquity. For the modern language sometimes colloquially called "Cyrodilic", see [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md).

Old Cyrodilic, also known as **[Slave](Lore_Slavery.md#Ayleid)'s Cant**<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-KKP-1)</sup> or simply **Cyrodilic**, is the language from which [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md) descended.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-2)</sup><sup>:6</sup>

A variation of Old Cyrodilic ([Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md)) was once spoken by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWE-3)</sup> In [1E 2813](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2813), Cyrodilic became the language of all legal documents, replacing [High Elvish](Lore_High_Elvish.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-2)</sup><sup>:6</sup>
