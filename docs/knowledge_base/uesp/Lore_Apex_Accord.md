# Apex Accord


## History
The Blackcaster Mages Guild, led by Felix "Blackcaster" Augustus, were said to have left the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) to practice their arts in [Craglorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Craglorn). Intrigued by the sight of the then-empty Apex Towers of Elinhir, the Blackcasters pleaded with the town's mayor for access to the towers, but were denied multiple times. Sometime later, a band of [Iron Orcs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iron_Orcs) came down from the mountains to lay siege to the city. The Blackcasters drove off the Orcs using their magic and were said to have won the love of the people of Elinhir, who then uplifted Felix Augustus as their new mayor. Shortly after, the Apex Accord was struck.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFE-2)</sup>
