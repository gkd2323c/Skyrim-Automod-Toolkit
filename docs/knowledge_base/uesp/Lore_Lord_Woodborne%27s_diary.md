Page 7
...from childhood. I know that [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md) would not refuse
his aid, but I must bide my time to ask. After all,
[Elysana](Lore_Elysana.md) is still heiress of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) and I am her
[bethrothed](Lore_Marriage.md). So it should be that when [Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md) dies
(which he is bound to do, sooner rather than later)
I will be king.

If it weren't for that bitch queen and her brats,
I would and could be patient. Never trust a [dark elf](Lore_Dark_Elf.md)
the old saying goes, for you cannot read truth in
their eyes. If [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md) has her way, [Helseth](Lore_Helseth.md) will
be heir and Elysana will be married off to some far-
off kingdom. [Morgiah](Lore_Morgiah.md) is already promised to the
heir of [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), but they keep her around to spread
the poison against Elysana, and especially, me.

Barenziah doesn't trust me, this I can tell.
I will deal with her just as I did with [Lysandus](Lore_Lysandus.md).
The fool should have agreed to support me. [Gortwog](Lore_Gortwog.md)
and his [orcs](Lore_Minat.md) almost saved him. I'll deal with those
sub-humans once I sit upon the throne of Wayrest.

First it is important to solidify my power base.
I can (almost certainly) rely on [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md) to support
my bid for the throne, if Helseth is made heir. I
have a spy network equalling Eadwyre, Gothryd,
[Akorith-i](Lore_Akorithi.md), or the [Emperor](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md). Perhaps the [Necromancers](Lore_Necromancers.md)
and the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md)'s networks are more extensive, but
I doubt it. The problem is raw physical power. I do
not have a standing army. I need the gold to raise...
