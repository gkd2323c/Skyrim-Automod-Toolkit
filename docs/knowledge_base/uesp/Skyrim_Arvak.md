[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-creature-Arvak.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Arvak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Arvak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Arvak.jpg) Arvak **Arvak** is a [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) that can be summoned from the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md) after completing [Soul Cairn Horse Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Cairn_Horse_Quest). He can be summoned with the apprentice level conjuration spell [Summon Arvak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arvak).

Arvak has several noteworthy advantages over other mounts: he can't be permanently killed, he costs no money, and you don't need to worry about losing him. Despite being a summoned horse, he can be kept indefinitely (until you dismount) as long as you are riding him when his summon time of 60 seconds expires. Additionally, Arvak and the [Daedric Horse](Skyrim_Daedric_Horse.md) are the only horses that can be used in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md), [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md), or on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).

Like any other creature conjured, Arvak is vulnerable to being banished. This can be particularly dangerous if you are riding him near hostile conjurers or Thalmor wizards, because you will fall to the ground when he is banished and be vulnerable to attacks until you can regain your footing.

## Related Quests
- **[Soul Cairn Horse Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Cairn_Horse_Quest)**: Find Arvak's skull.

## Notes
- Arvak can be seen running around the Soul Cairn once in a while before completing the quest. Approaching him will cause him to vanish. If you follow him at a distance he will lead you to a mysterious [soul merchant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morven_Stroud).
- Although Arvak appears to be undead, he is considered by the game to be a normal animal and is therefore not affected by undead-related spells. Blood will also spurt when he is attacked by enemies, as if he were a flesh-and-blood creature.
- Arvak is based on the flaming horse developed during the [Game Jam](Skyrim_Game_Jam.md).
- The name Arvak is derived from Old Norse Árvakr (early awake), one of the horses pulling the chariot (or sun itself) of the sun god Sól.
- Arvak has a unique sound when running and sprinting, sounding like creaking and clattering bones.
- You cannot summon Arvak in walled cities, such as [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
- If you don't immediately mount him after he's been summoned, Arvak will follow you around, like most other summons do. However, he is a poor choice for combat, as he shares the same lack of courage "standard" horses have during hostile situations if you are not riding them.
- Arvak does not immediately vanish should you dismount after the 60 second timer runs out. There is also a second stage of the timer if you are still mounted on him after the minute runs out: the effect "Summon Arvak" will show 0 seconds, but then slowly climb up to 5 remaining seconds, and this tends to fluctuate between anywhere from 5-1 seconds on the timer. Whatever time is left when you dismount is also the time you have to remount before he dissipates.
- Like other horses, Arvak can catch diseases.

## Bugs
- Arvak's skull isn't removed from your inventory when returned to his master. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Despite Arvak being a unique creature, you can actually summon two Arvaks provided you have the [Twin Souls](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) perk. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13371))
- Arvak cannot be summoned in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). The magicka will still be lost and your hand will still do the animation, but Arvak will not appear. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
- Arvak also cannot be summoned in the [Deadlands](Skyrim_Deadlands.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>. The magicka will still be lost and your hand will still do the animation, but Arvak will not appear. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
- Arvak also cannot be summoned in [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md). The magicka will still be lost and your hand will still do the animation, but Arvak will not appear.
- Summoning Arvak once may cause [Shadowmere](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere) to vanish if possessed, and may even prevent Shadowmere from appearing after [The Cure for Madness](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md) is completed. - You can make Shadowmere your main horse again if you are able to find the last location you left him. Generally, he'll be waiting for you at [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) main entrance. To take possession of him, mount his back, wait a few hours, then fast travel while still on his back. Next time you fast travel (even by walking), Shadowmere will follow you again.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you are unable to find Shadowmere's location, you can return Shadowmere to you by using the console commands `prid 9CCD8` followed by `moveto player`.
- Despite Arvak being a unique creature, the original Arvak still can be found in the Soul Cairn while you ride on the summoned Arvak. <sup>**?**</sup>
