[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-npc-Lost_Paladin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lost_Paladin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lost_Paladin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lost_Paladin.jpg) The Lost Paladin The Lost Paladin is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) who is encountered at [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) during the quest [The Lost Paladin](Skyrim_The_Lost_Paladin.md). The quest requires you to kill him and take [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere_(item).md) from his remains.

## Related Quests
- **[The Lost Paladin](Skyrim_The_Lost_Paladin.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Chrysamere.md)</sup>: Find and claim the [Sword of Heroes](Skyrim_Chrysamere_(item).md).

## Notes
- Though he wears [ancient Nord](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord.md) armor and is labeled as belonging to the same faction as the [Dragon Cultists](Skyrim_Dragon_Cultist.md) of Forelhost, the quest stages in the The Lost Paladin quest indicates that he is the most recent wielder of Chrysamere.

## Bugs
- The Paladin does not have [protected](Skyrim_Protected_NPCs.md) status. He may be killed early, and not by you, if a [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) (especially a higher-level dragon such as [Ancient](Skyrim_Dragon.md#Ancient_Dragon)) attacks you outside Forelhost and then switches targets. Presumably you have to hurry to the battlements and loot his ghostly remains before the area resets. <sup>**?**</sup>
