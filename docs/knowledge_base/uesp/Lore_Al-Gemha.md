# Al-Gemha

The al-Gemha were an ancient a tribe of [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people. They lived in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) and early [First](Lore_First_Era.md) eras and were among the men herded as slaves from across the [Niben](Lore_Niben.md) by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LCL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup>
