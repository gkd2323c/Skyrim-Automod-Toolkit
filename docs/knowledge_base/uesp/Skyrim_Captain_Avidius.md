He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d) and is armed with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md). He carries the [Katariah Master Key](Skyrim_Katariah_Master_Key.md), along with two leveled potions of [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) and at least 25 gold.
