## History
Little is known of Tamlien's life, except that he was the King of [Alinor](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTQ-2)</sup> His death was foreseen by [Oracle](Lore_Sight.md) Marieve.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VG-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ayrenn as the new Queen of Alinor, per tradition, traveled to the ruins of [Tanzelwil](Lore_Tanzelwil.md) on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) to commune with the dead and seek their approval to the throne. She hoped that if the ancestors approved her, the Altmeri people would accept her as the rightful heir of King Tamlien.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTQ-2)</sup>

- | [1.](#note_nb1) | Queen Ayrenn inherited the throne from her father Hidellith, who died in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580).
