## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is next to [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md), at the end of the path through town, past [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md). There is a good view of the bay from the partially covered porch in front of the house, between [Brina's house](Skyrim_Brina%27s_House.md) and [The Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md). To the left of the main steps up to the porch is a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush. To the left of the building an area has been fenced in, however apart from a pile of firewood there is nothing in this area.

### Irgnir's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Irgnir%27s_House.jpg) Five skeever plaques Another single room dwelling. On your left, against the southwest wall is a long wooden table holding a bottle of each, [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and two [boiled creme treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat). On the floor to the left is a basket containing three cabbages and another basket to the right contains two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and four [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot). To the right of this is a set of shelves holding two leeks, several loaves of bread, two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulbs, three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), two [gourds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) and several cheese wheels. Above there are two garlic braids, three bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md).

In the western corner and along the northwest wall are three barrels, two of which containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md). Further to the right against the same wall is a round wooden table and two chairs. On the table are six loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md). Opposite the door on the northeast wall is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front, two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on the mantelpiece and three pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) on the hearth. To the left is a pile of firewood with five [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) heads mounted on plaques above. To the left of these is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

To the right of the fireplace is a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe). Further to the right against the northeast wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and a chair beside it. Coming out from the southeast wall is a large double bed with two bottle of alto wine on the floor to the left of it, a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) at the foot of the bed and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and chair to the right. There is an unlocked wooden [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) against the southwest wall, with two more pickaxe to the right, next to the door.
