***The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim Legendary Edition*** is an edition of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) that bundles together the game and its three [paid add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md). It was released in June 2013 for PC, Xbox 360, and Play Station 3.

It was [formerly available](http://store.steampowered.com/app/2028782/) on [Steam](Skyrim_Trading_Cards.md), and was removed when the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) was released. The standard version of the game without DLC remains [available](https://store.steampowered.com/app/72850/The_Elder_Scrolls_V_Skyrim/) for purchase, albeit unlisted at Bethesda's request.
