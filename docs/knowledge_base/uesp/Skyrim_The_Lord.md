[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_(active).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Lord_Stone_(active).jpg) The Lord Stone, when activated Activating The Lord Stone will add 50 points to your [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) rating and 25% [magic resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md). This makes it easier to reach the [armor rating](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and [magic resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) caps. Its effect is generic and useful for characters of all levels and play styles, providing decent all-around protection. [The Atronach Stone](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) is more effective at mitigating magic, but comes with a drawback and offers no physical protection.

## The Lord Stone
The Lord Stone is located on top of a stone base with the remains of a large arch on either side of it. It can be reached by following the path that leads past the [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). The path starts to the west of the [Hall of the Vigilant](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md). There are several piles of rocks marking the route; some have the remnants of banners hanging from them and flapping in the wind. You'll pass [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> along the path; once you reach a long flight of stone steps cut into the mountain leading up to the Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon, turn to face northeast. You'll see the Lord Stone a short distance up a hill.

The Lord Stone is home to a group of three [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md), at least one of whom is an archer. There are three unowned bed rolls arranged around a small campfire in front of the standing stone. There is also a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) at the southwest corner of the base of the structure upon which the stone is set. The path near the shrine is home to a leveled animal, which can be an [ice wolf](Skyrim_Ice_Wolf.md), [ice wraith](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), [snow bear](Skyrim_Snow_Bear.md) or [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md), depending on your level when on the mountain pass.

## Notes
- A [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) with The Lord Stone and [Agent of Mara](Skyrim_Agent_of_Mara.md) power will have a permanent 65% resistance to all magic. This can be achieved straight out of [Helgen](Skyrim_Unbound.md) at level 1 with almost no combat involved.
- Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) assigns The Lord Stone to [The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) instead.
