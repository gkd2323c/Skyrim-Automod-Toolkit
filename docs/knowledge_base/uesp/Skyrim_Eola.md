[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-npc-Eola.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eola.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola.jpg) Eola **Eola** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade) initially found in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) where she reluctantly resides to satisfy a very special [hunger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:DA11Cannibal Faction). Due to the desecration of the corpses, the hall had to be closed down and you are asked to investigate further, leading to the quest [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md) and an encounter with the Daedric Prince [Namira](Lore_Namira.md). She can be recruited as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) after clearing out [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave) and becoming the Champion of Namira.

She is a skilled mage with [a wide array](#Spells) of spells at her disposal and the perks to match them. Additionally, she wields a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and carries a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) with twelve [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). In addition to her Restoration spells, she carries up to three [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) along with some food and [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). She wears [studded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor) with a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Taste of Death
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f8/SR-npc-Eola_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eola_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola_02.jpg) *"Meet me there."* Eola will only show up in the Hall of the Dead once you have spoken to the faithful priest of Arkay, [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md), who will ask you to investigate the hall and hand you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Markarth_Hall_of_the_Dead_Key). As soon as you enter the crypt, Eola will seductively speak to you from what appears to be inside your head:

*"Not many would walk blindly into a crypt, smelling of steel and blood, but not fear. I feel the hunger inside of you. Gnawing at you. You see the dead and your mouth grows wet. Your stomach growls. It's all right. I will not shun you for what you are. Stay. I will tell you everything you have forgotten."* She will then appear from nowhere and speak normally:

*"You were young when you first tasted human flesh, weren't you? A brother or sister had died? An accident, of course. Then the hunger set in. Curiosity. What's the harm in just one bite? It's okay, now. You've found a friend who understands you. You can let go of your guilt."* **Guilt? What are you talking about?** or **I'm not a cannibal. What are you trying to do to me?** *"A lot of our kind block out the memory of their first meal. The shame is too much. But you don't need to hide anymore. Namira, the Lady of Decay, accepts you for what you are. She has a place for us, where we can sate our appetites without judgment."* **Your tricks won't help you, monster!** *"To Oblivion with you then!"* After the first speech, two new options are available:

**Where is this place?** *"It's inside Reachcliff Cave. But the dead have stirred from their slumber recently, and I was forced here. Meet me there. We will fight our way to Namira's embrace together."* **I want no part of this.** *"Very well. But should the hunger call out to you, I'll be waiting outside Reachcliff Cave."* In both cases she will send you back to Brother Verulus with a comforting:

*"Until then, tell the people of Markarth that their dead won't be disturbed, anymore. We have bigger plans ahead."* She will then cast an invisibility spell on herself and run all the way to [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave) southeast of Markarth. If spoken to while she is running she will say:

*"Not here. We will meet at Reachcliff Cave."* Once there, she will patiently await your arrival. When you arrive near the entrance, she will actively seek you out and say:

*"You've come. The draugr infesting Namira's sanctuary are inside."* **I could use a hand.** *"I was hoping you would say that."* **Wait here. I'll take care of them.** *"Then I'll wait."* Should you approach her again, she will remind you of the task:

*"Clear Namira's sanctuary of the undead."* If you asked her to follow you, she will help wipe out the residing [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) enemies, while shouting out random battle cries:

*"Namira grind your bones for my bread!"* *"I will drink your blood and feast on your flesh!"* *"I will feast on you!"* *"Glory to Namira! "* *"Glory to the repulsive!"* When the final draugr is slain, Eola will once again approach you, saying:

*"You've done it. The shrine is ours again. Now we need to prepare a grand feast to welcome you to Namira's coven. You will have the honor of bringing a fresh kill for the main course. And I know the perfect person."* **Who is it?** or **Just tell me who needs to die.** *"A priest, filled with the taste of an easy life. Brother Verulus, from Markarth. Give him this gold. Tell him you need Arkay's help exploring an old cavern for treasure. And when he stands in Namira's presence, she will take care of the rest."* If you talk to her again and she can say:

*"Bring Verulus here. The Lady of Decay will do the rest."* *"Many used to come to these caves. The forgotten. The wretched."* Once you bring Verulus to Eola:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Eola_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eola_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eola_03.jpg) *"I've invited you to dinner."* **Verulus**: *"Who... who are you? What's going on?"*
**Eola**: *"Priest of Arkay. I'm your friend."*
**Verulus**: *"You're my... friend...."*
**Eola**: *"Yes. I'm your friend, and I've invited you to dinner."*
**Verulus**: *"I've been invited to dinner.... I'm so hungry...."*
**Eola**: *"Why don't you lay down and rest, while we get the meal ready?"*
**Verulus**: *"I need to lay down. I'll just be a minute...."*
**Eola**: *"Come with me. Our feast is about to begin."*
**Eola**: *"The meal is on Namira's table. Go ahead. Carve."*

After the conversation, a hypnotized Brother Verulus will lie down on the altar and sleep. When approached, Eola will say:

*"The meal is on Namira's table. Go ahead. Carve."* *"Don't be shy. You should be the one to carve."* When Verulus is dead, she will invite you with:

*"He looks so... sweet. Go ahead. Have the first bite."* *"This is your initiation into the coven. Go ahead. Have the first bite."* After your conversation with Namira, she will be quite impressed and say:

*"I knew when you walked into the Hall of the Dead that you were special. And here you are. Champion of Namira. Keeper of her ring. You're everything I hoped you would be."* **I feel sick.** *"There, there. It will pass. Going too long without a proper meal can make the stomach weak. Feel free to stay and mingle with your new coven, if you have the strength."* **Thank you, Eola. For everything.** *"It's an honor, champion. Feel free to stay and mingle with your new coven."* She will then head down to the dinner table, staring at the participants. At this point, you can ask her if she started the coven:

**You started the coven of Namira?** *"No. This coven has existed for thousands of years. Namira is a goddess. She has been worshipped since time began. I'm honored to be leading the latest generation of Namira's faithful. Those who have embraced what they are."* After dinner is over Eola will say the following, dependent on who is being spoken to:

## Unused Dialogue
Eola, or Glooredhel (see below), has quite a substantial bit of early and unused dialogue. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

Her initial confrontation with you in the Hall of the Dead was originally meant to play out like this:

*"So why are you here? Are you one of the "righteous" servants of Arkay? Or are you willing to listen to me?"* You would then have two options:

**You've desecrated this temple! Show yourself and fight!** *"Very well. It's been a while since I had something fresh to eat."* **Who are you? Why are you disturbing the dead?** *"I'm simply a forgotten woman who isn't afraid to eat what others would find foul. I'm coming out now. I have something I wish to ask you."* You could've also walked away, to which she would say:

*"I'm coming out. I have something I wish to ask you."* You were also apparently meant to gather ingredients for the feast at one point. The only reference to this removed portion of this quest is this dialogue, no follow-up or previous lines exist:

**Where could I find Garlic?** *"Garlic shouldn't be hard to find. Any general store or kitchen should have some."* **"Blood of grapes"? What does that mean?** *"Wine. A red wine would be the most appropriate for our meal."* **Why do we need nightshade?** *"Nightshade is a common poison ingredient. In small doses, it will add the right spice to our meal."*
## Notes
- Eola is referred to as **Glooredhel** by the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit), indicating her name (and likely her race as well) was changed during development.

## Bugs
- Eola lacks all generic follower responses, such as to being asked to do something or trade, due to her voice type not actualy being supported by the `Dialogue Follower` quest.
- As a follower, Eola may not attack anyone, but rather just stop, as if alerted to enemies but without entering combat.
- When you meet Eola at Reachcliff Cave and tell her to wait, she'll continuously force conversations on you, making it difficult to enter the cave.
- After inducted into the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), Eola will use the lines she says as greetings after you receive the [Ring of Namira](Skyrim_Ring_of_Namira.md), instead of the generic Blades greetings.
