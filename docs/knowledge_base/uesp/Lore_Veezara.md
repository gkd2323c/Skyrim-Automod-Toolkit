Veezara was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) assassin and member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md). Veezara was one of the last known [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscale.md), as such he was trained by the Dark Brotherhood since the day of his birth. For a time he served as an assassin in service to the King of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRVeezara-1)</sup> Eventually he was honorably discharged from service, and went on to wander [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) before he eventually made his way to the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup><sup>:315</sup> The Sanctuary was the last remaining one on Tamriel, in the outskirts of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md), and was active in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). There he become a loyal servant [Astrid](Lore_Astrid.md), the leader of the Sanctuary.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRVeezara-1)</sup>

Veezara was introduced to a [new member](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) of his Dark Brotherhood family, saw the arrival of [Cicero](Lore_Cicero.md) and the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md), and assisted his new family member with their escape after assassinating [Vittoria Vici](Lore_Vittoria_Vici.md) at her wedding, only to later be murdered by the [Penitus Oculatus](Lore_Penitus_Oculatus.md) during the massacre of the Falkreath Sanctuary. However, conflicting reports suggest that the latter events never happened, except for his murder during the massacre. In any case, with Veezara's death it is possible that the Shadowscales have become extinct.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-4)</sup>
