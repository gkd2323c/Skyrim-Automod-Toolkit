# Naga-Kur

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/ON-npc-Raj-Kaal_Seelan.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Raj-Kaal_Seelan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Raj-Kaal_Seelan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Raj-Kaal_Seelan.jpg) Raj-Kaal Seelan, chieftain of the Naga-Kur in 2E 582 The Naga-Kur (known as the **Dead-Water Tribe** in Tamrielic) is a tribe of [Nagas](Lore_Naga.md), a rare breed of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md). Their village is known as [Dead-Water Village](Lore_Dead-Water_Village.md) and they control vast swaths of northern [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) and are greatly feared by the Argonians of the surrounding villages.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOM-1)</sup>

## Culture
The Path of the Lily is sacred to the Naga-Kur. Hatchlings ascend to the rank of warrior by walking the Path, ready to defend the tribe and survive the terrors of the swamp. Failure means death and a return to the Hist.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The Naga-Kur often adorn their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor with pieces of fallen comrades. To craft a weapon from the skin and bones of their warriors is considered an honor.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CMDS-3)</sup>

They were known to organize Death Hunts for some of the nastiest things in Murkmire.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AA3-4)</sup>
