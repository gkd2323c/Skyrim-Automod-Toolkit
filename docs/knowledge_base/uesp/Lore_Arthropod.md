# Arthropod

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-lure-Insect_Parts.png) An insect [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-misc-Spider_02.png) An arachnid [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Crab.png) A crustacean There are many different species of arthropods on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Arthropods are typically chitinous invertebrates with hard carapaces, who vary in size greatly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Red-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Silt Pl-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Horror-3)</sup> Commons forms of arthropods are **insects**<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Olith-6)</sup> and **insectoids**,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Elytra-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BLScrib-8)</sup> as well as eight-limbed **arachnids**.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-HFSV-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Horror-3)</sup> These types of arthropods are often referred to informally as **bugs**,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Olith-6)</sup> **crawlers**,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> or **creepy crawlies**.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Another major variety of arthropod are **crustaceans**, which are typically aquatic or semi-aquatic, and often possess claws.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Red-1)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ESOCaptain-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-YB-17)</sup>

[Dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) share traits with arthropods such as crustaceans.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-AAFCS-18)</sup> Though it is unclear if they are classified as such given their shared traits with other creatures such as [cephalopods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cephalopod).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-19)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2H-20)</sup> [Nixads](Lore_Nixad.md) are insectoid in appearance, though their nature as [fay](Lore_Faerie.md) makes their relation uncertain.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-21)</sup> There are many Daedra that imitate or resemble arthropods, primarily [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) and her servants in the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Wpet-22)</sup> The [Inferniums](Lore_Infernium.md) and [Scorions](Lore_Scorion.md) of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) are more examples of this.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-WFP-23)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-SP-24)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-Mazandi-25)</sup>
