— *[Tombs of the Kinrulers](Lore_Tombs_of_the_Kinrulers.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTK-1)</sup> **Kinlady** Psynande Corelanya **the Voyager** was the ruler of [Clan Corelanya](Lore_Clan_Corelanya.md) and the city of [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md) during the first half of the 12th century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). She earned renown for her ventures into the eastern seas and her passion for exploration.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTK-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt
### Reign
Very little is known about Psynande's reign, aside from the fact that she succeeded her cousin [Orlemar](Lore_Orlemar_Corelanya.md) as ruler of the realm in 1E 1101.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTK-1)</sup> She inherited a domain fraught with rising tensions between the Corelanya and the local [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tribes. While the Tide-Born Argonians posed little military threat, the Stone-Nest proved far more capable of thwarting Corelanya expansion through both open conflict and targeted assassinations. Psynande's specific policies regarding the Argonian conflict were not known, though the growing hostilities between Solstice's two peoples would ultimately erupt under her successor's rule.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HOCC4-2)</sup>

Psynande herself became better known for her seafaring pursuits. She led expeditions into the eastern seas, a legacy that earned her the title the Voyager. She died in [1E 1165](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1165).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTK-1)</sup>

### Legacy
Following her death, she was succeeded as the leader of the Clan Corelanya by her nephew [Kevarian](Lore_Kevarian_Corelanya.md). Psynande was laid to rest in the Corelanya Crypt and remembered as the explorer. Her memory was honored, and she was held in reverence.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOTK-1)</sup>
