King Kurog's Promise **by [Kurog gro-Orsinium](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#King_Kurog_gro-Bagrakh)** *A royal agreement granting Malak's Maw to Gruznak gro-Volkar*
---
Herewith the Agreement between His Majesty [King Kurog](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md), Monarch of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), and the Oathbound Warchief, Gruznak gro-Volkar, and herein the terms:

Insofar as the deaths of [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md) are pleasing unto [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md) and deemed useful as a matter of policy:

For each Dark Elf skull obtained by the Oathbound, Gruznak gro-Volkar shall be granted title to Malak's Maw for one year.

Furthermore, Warchief Gruznak and his Oathbound are hereby absolved of crimes against the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md), including (but not limited to) murder, banditry, and the felonious impersonation of traveling entertainers.

— Kurog gro-Orsinium
