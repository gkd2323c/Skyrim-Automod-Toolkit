[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/LO-map-The_Chain.jpg/200px-LO-map-The_Chain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-The_Chain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-The_Chain.jpg) A map showing the location of The Chain [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/RG-load-Catacombs.jpg/200px-RG-load-Catacombs.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-load-Catacombs.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-load-Catacombs.jpg) Closer view of the islands (top right) The Chain is a group of at least three islands in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1-1)</sup> south of [Hegathe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hegathe) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and west of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), near the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md). The two smaller islands trail off to the west.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The island was considered by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) to be Hammerfell's territory in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MCMap-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OCMap-4)</sup>

The [coral](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Coral) kingdoms of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) are described as a set of islands southwest of the Chain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1-1)</sup>
