[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-creature-Keeper_%28Battleaxe%29.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Keeper_%28Battleaxe%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Keeper_(Battleaxe).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Keeper_(Battleaxe).jpg) The Battleaxe Keeper [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/SR-creature-Keeper_%28Bow%29.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Keeper_%28Bow%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Keeper_(Bow).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Keeper_(Bow).jpg) The Bow Keeper **Boneyard Keepers** are towering servants of the [Ideal Masters](Lore_Ideal_Masters.md) who reside in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). Charged with the incarceration of [Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica), they herd imprisoned souls and drain their energy to maintain the barrier in front of the Boneyard. Three appear during [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md) and do not respawn.

They all wear [dragonplate armor](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Boots.md), and [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dragonplate_Gauntlets.md), and will each drop a filled [black soul gem](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md). At level 45 and higher, the first Keeper ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 0064ab) will also drop a [dragonbone mace](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Mace.md), the second ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 0071ec) will drop a [dragonbone battleaxe](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Battleaxe.md), and the third ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 0071eb) will drop a [dragonbone bow](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Bow.md) along with ten [dragonbone arrows](Skyrim_Dragonbone_Arrow.md) (all unenchanted versions).

## Related Quests
- **[Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md)**: Search for [Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica) in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

## Notes
- All Keepers use the "skeleton" sound effects.
- All Keepers have an attack which damages stamina, yet the weapons they drop are not enchanted. Additionally, the archery Keeper will break any wards you have active (including the one cast by [Spellbreaker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spellbreaker)) when you are struck by the arrows. All other archery-type enemies do not have this property.
- The full powered version of [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) can ragdoll a Keeper easily.
