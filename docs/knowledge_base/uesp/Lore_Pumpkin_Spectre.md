Pumpkin Spectres are humanoid [pumpkin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pumpkin_Vine) [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) native to the [demi-plane](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) of [Detritus](Lore_Detritus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pumpkin Spectre-1)</sup> [Hollowjack](Lore_Hollowjack.md) is a Pumpkin Spectre and a [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) who rules over Detritus.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMask-2)</sup> Those seen in Detritus display very disturbing behavior, such as playing kick-ups with skulls, dancing maniacally, and cannibalism. They are not inherently hostile to visitors, showing a propensity to spook them with sudden scares and shushing them when witnessing their devious acts.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PSSo D-3)</sup>

Hollowjack sometimes summons these creatures to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the Witches Festival to torment mortals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Pumpkin Spectre-1)</sup> Visions of these nightmarish beings are also summoned by hedge wizards during the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md), a holiday that takes place on the 13th of [Frostfall](Lore_Frostfall.md) each year.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
