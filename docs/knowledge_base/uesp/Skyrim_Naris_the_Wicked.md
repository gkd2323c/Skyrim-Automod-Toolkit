## Dialogue
When first encountered, Naris is standing watching a pile of burning corpses, and can be overheard talking to himself about his victims, saying:

- *"Ah, the sweet smell of burnt flesh. Nothing better really."*
- *"They die too quickly. Maybe I'll try and hold off on the paralysis next time. Give me more of a moving target. Good practice."*
