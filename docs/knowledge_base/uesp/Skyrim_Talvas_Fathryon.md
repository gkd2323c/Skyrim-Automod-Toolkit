He is currently the apprentice of [Master Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).

He wears [adept robes of Conjuration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adept_Robes_of_Conjuration) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0006b46c), and he carries a small amount of gold. He knows a number of spells, namely: [Ironflesh](Skyrim_Ironflesh.md), [Conjure Flame Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Flame_Atronach.md), [Conjure Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md), [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md), [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md), and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md).

When you first pay a visit to Tel Mithryn, you will find Varona in the center of the settlement engaged in conversation with Talvas Fathryon. Varona is obviously concerned about Talvas' conjuration experiments and countless attempts to summon an [ash guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md), and she will try to get Talvas to act responsibly, with little luck. The dialogue will differ slightly if Neloth is in Raven Rock as part of the quest [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn_(quest).md):

## Notes
- Talvas' store and training are only accessible while within the Tel Mithryn area. His store becomes unavailable outside the area as his merchant chest is static and required for trade. His store also contains an extensive collection of standard Destruction and Conjuration spell tomes, as well as tomes for additional spells added by the Dragonborn add-on.
- Being set at a static level 25, Talvas is unique among followers. This makes him very valuable for low level characters, as his services can be acquired directly from level 1, though getting through his quest might be a challenge at that point.
- Talvas is the only follower on Solstheim who you can hire as a [steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md), due to him being the only one using a voice type from the base game, rather than a Dragonborn-specific one.

## Bugs
- As a trainer in Conjuration and a follower, it is possible to receive 'free' training by taking the gold paid for the training from his inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
- If the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) is installed, [Agmaer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Agmaer) may have the same dialogue as Talvas when asking him to follow you. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
- Talvas has the Augmented Shock perk assigned but doesn't know any lightning spells. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. Talvas will now be assigned the Augmented Frost perk instead.
- Talvas' merchant inventory may be replaced with items given to him by you while he is a follower. Waiting for this stock to reset may not resolve this problem. <sup>**?**</sup> - One possible solution is to dismiss him, hire another follower, and return to Talvas after his stock has had time to reset.
- When Talvas is your follower, the option to ask “What is this place?” may be available in places like Raven Rock as well as the mainland, though he still responds that it's Master Neloth's Tower, which clearly doesn't make sense and would be incorrect under those circumstances. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.0.8, addresses this issue. A condition check has been added to the dialogue topic to prevent this from happening.
- When first meeting him and you ask "Are you practicing a spell?", he is supposed to reply, "Yes. I'm trying to summon an Ash Guardian. I'm just having trouble deciphering Master Neloth's handwriting. I don't mean to be rude, but I need to concentrate." However, the middle sentence has a tendency to not be played, so what you hear instead (and what is also displayed in subtitles) is: "Yes. I'm trying to summon an Ash Guardian. I don't mean to be rude, but I need to concentrate." <sup>**?**</sup>
