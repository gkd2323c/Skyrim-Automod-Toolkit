# Exiled Sun-Eaters

The Exiled Sun-Eaters of Numaneh were an exiled tribe of [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). Little is known about them, and as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), telling their tale was forbidden according to [the Unveiled Azadiyeh](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Unveiled_Azadiyeh),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TUA-1)</sup> the Songbird of [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md), a storyteller,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GOTOT-2)</sup> expert in old Redguard lore,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-STJOA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BBS-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ZMH-5)</sup> and a [follower](Lore_Crown.md) the original [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) pantheon.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PFOE-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-THUS-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-HHH-8)</sup> They were listed alongside the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) and [Silverhoof Horsemen](Lore_Silverhoof_Horsemen.md) in response to the question about various isolated Redguard communities and tribes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TUA-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Despite their similar name, they share no connection with the Sun-Eaters, a clan of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) worshippers, led by the matriarch [Shuxaltsei](Lore_Shuxaltsei.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOSWETL-9)</sup>
- They also are not connected to the [Dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md) Sun-Eaters.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-DM-10)</sup>
