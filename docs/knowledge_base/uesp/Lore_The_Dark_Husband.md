The Dark Husband *On Sithis, Dark Husband of the Night Mother*
---
Like [Secunda](Lore_Secunda.md) follows [Masser](Lore_Masser.md), [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) follows the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md). Her husband pursues, but she seldom pauses, leaving him eternally in shadow.

Sithis has many names, and many parents … or none at all. But his Fangs … the [Fangs of Sithis](Lore_Fangs_of_Sithis.md) still bite. They take particular pleasure in envenoming the weak-minded, the foolish, the greedy, or the headstrong, but the Fangs can destroy the powerful and the wise as well.

Beware those born under [the Shadow](Lore_The_Shadow.md), and all those who seek to use its power. The downfall of our ancestors is tied to the Fangs of Sithis, and our scales remain darkened by his touch.
