Sirayar's Note **by [Sirayar](Skyrim_Sirayar.md)**
---
Met a hunter from far out west. Bought him a drink and found out a wagon was headed up toward Skyrim along the southwestern border from Hammerfell. He should pass by Sunderstone Gorge on his way through.

Not a lot of folks on that road these days. Still, he didn't say how many there were or what they looked like. We need to find out more before we know for sure it's Ismal.
