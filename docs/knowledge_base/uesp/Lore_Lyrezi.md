# Lyrezi

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/ON-npc-Lyrezi.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Lyrezi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Lyrezi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Lyrezi.jpg) Lyrezi, progenitor of the bloodline Lyrezi is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with the ability to turn invisible and magically silence their enemies. The bloodline dominates an area of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) - specifically the regions of [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire_(fiefdom).md), [Koegria](Lore_Koegria.md), [Menevia](Lore_Menevia_(county).md), [Orsinium Area](Lore_Orsinium_Area.md), the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) and [Balfiera](Lore_Isle_of_Balfiera.md). They are the enemies of the [Garlythi](Lore_Garlythi.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFFactions-1)</sup>

The bloodline is named after [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi_(person).md), who was from a coven of nomadic Nedes from ancient [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). He was involved in the attempted cremation of [Lamae Bal](Lore_Lamae_Bal.md), the first vampire. After awakening on the pyre, Lamae brutally raped the men of the tribe, seemingly passing on the gift of vampirism in the process.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sot BM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OLBTM-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The Lyrezi believe the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) to be a vampire from a [realm](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) outside of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MAS-4)</sup>
