J'datharr's Note *Mark information for a Thalmor Assassin*
---
Description of target:

[Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) male, goes by [Malborn](Skyrim_Malborn.md). Believed to be working for the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), so approach with caution.

He has inside knowledge of our procedures, so he will be warier than usual. Malborn is not his real name - he's now been identified as a survivor of a family of traitors who were all believed to have died in a fire in [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md).

Do not risk him evading us. He's likely trying to leave [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). Make sure of your kill, and do not implicate [us](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).
