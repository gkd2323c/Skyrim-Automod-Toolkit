[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-place-Mistveil_Keep.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mistveil_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistveil_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistveil_Keep.jpg) Mistveil Keep circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/ON-place-Old_Mistveil_Manor.jpg/200px-ON-place-Old_Mistveil_Manor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Old_Mistveil_Manor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Old_Mistveil_Manor.jpg) Mistveil Manor circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Mistveil Keep (also called **Mistveil Estate** and **Old Mistveil Manor**) is a large stone mansion traditionally located on the southern side [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). It has served as the castle of the city's Jarl.

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Mistveil Estate was put up for sale following the death of old Thane Snelgar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OMM-1)</sup> It was not the residence of the Jarl but was instead a separate mansion found outside the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

When Hosgunn Crossed-Daggers became Jarl in [4E 98](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_98), he took 7 years to build a massive castle which became a visual reminder of the people's oppression which earned it the nickname "Hosgunn's Folly." In [4E 129](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_129) the people of Riften overwhelmed the guard and burned down the castle with Hosgunn still in it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OCD-3)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Mistveil Keep had become the Jarl's residence and seat of power, and was now within the walls of Riften.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup>
