[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-item-Silver-Blood_Family_Ring.jpg/200px-SR-item-Silver-Blood_Family_Ring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver-Blood_Family_Ring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Silver-Blood_Family_Ring.jpg) Silver-Blood Family Ring The Silver-Blood Family Ring is a [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) that will be given to you by [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md) if you killed [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) during [your escape from Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md). Befitting the background of its former owners, its appearance is identical to a [silver ring](Skyrim_Silver_Ring.md). Its enchantment [improves weapon and armor smithing](Skyrim_Fortify_Smithing.md) by 15%.

## Related Quests
- **[No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md)**: Escape from [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md)!

## Notes
- It is possible to obtain Madanach's reward as well as this ring. By running through the mine with Madanach until the point at which you get the [Armor of the Old Gods](Skyrim_Armor_of_the_Old_Gods.md) and the matching [boots](Skyrim_Boots_of_the_Old_Gods.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Gauntlets_of_the_Old_Gods.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet_of_the_Old_Gods.md), then stalling him until the other Forsworn have escaped the area will give you the opportunity to kill him, thus earning you the animosity of the surviving Forsworn prisoners, which they will express by attacking you once you leave. After defeating them, you can talk to Thonar, who will give you this ring as a sign of his thanks.
