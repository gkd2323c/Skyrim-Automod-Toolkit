[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Ogol.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ogol.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ogol.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ogol.jpg) Ogol **Ogol** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_GuardOrc1H.md) found in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md). He is easy to spot among the other Orcs as his apparel is more akin to a city dweller than someone in an [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md). After [Malacath](Skyrim_Malacath.md)'s curse is lifted by completing [The Cursed Tribe](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md), he will respect you enough to become a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). He spends his days wandering around Largashbur, not straying too far from the campfire. At night he sleeps with the other Orcs in the [longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md).

As compared to other followers, Ogol has one of the smallest default inventories. Wearing only [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d), pretty much anything with an armor rating above 1 is a step up for him. He wields a removable [steel war axe](Skyrim_Steel_War_Axe.md), and a hidden [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) with [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md).

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
During The Cursed Tribe, he will be hostile towards you, stating:

- *"Our tribe has survived this long without you interfering. We'll be fine."*

However, after Yamarz is killed, he will say:

- *"I am... surprised that [Gularzob](Skyrim_Gularzob.md) was named chief."*
- *"I will serve Gularzob as best I can, even though I am his elder."*

He may also declare:

- *"We are all blood here. One family, one tribe."*
