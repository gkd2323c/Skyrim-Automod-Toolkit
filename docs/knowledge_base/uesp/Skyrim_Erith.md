[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-npc-Erith.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Erith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Erith.jpg) Erith **Erith**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [child](Skyrim_Children.md), is the daughter of [Daighre](Skyrim_Daighre.md). She can be found in [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine), just outside [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), and is usually in the company of her faithful companion [Torom](Skyrim_Torom.md).

She wears [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). She carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Daighre%27s_House) to [Daighre's house](Skyrim_Daighre%27s_House.md), a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child), and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue
When approached she can say:

*"Why is everyone so upset? If there's [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), the Jarl will take care of them, right?*"*"Hope [Pavo](Skyrim_Pavo_Attius.md) will be able to go back to his mine soon. Everyone needs a home."* *"What'cha doing in Left Hand? Do you dig up rocks too, like my mom?"* *"Why do they call it Left Hand Mine? Can the miners use their right hands too?"* *"Have you met [Torom](Skyrim_Torom.md)? He's the best dog in the Reach."*
## Conversations
**Daighre**: *"[Skaggi](Skyrim_Skaggi_Scar-Face.md) tells me that you were bothering him today. Can't you and Torom just play by yourselves?"* **Erith**: *"But mom, it's so boring with just me and Torom. I was just asking Skaggi some questions."* **Daighre**: *"You leave him alone. I don't want to hear Skaggi telling me you've been bothering him again!"* **Erith**: *"Yes, ma'am."* **Erith**: *"Mama, can we talk. You know, about papa?"* **Daighre**: *"Of course, sweetie. What'd you like to know?"* **Erith**: *"I don't know. What'd he look like?"* **Daighre**: *"He had your soft face, and that silly grin you get when you've done something wrong. That's his, too."*
## Notes
- Should you choose to adopt Erith, you will see Torom in your house every so often since his AI will have him seek out Erith, regardless of her location.
