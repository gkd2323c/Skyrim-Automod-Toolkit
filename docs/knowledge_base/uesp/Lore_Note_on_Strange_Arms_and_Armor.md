Note on Strange Arms and Armor *A bandit's thoughts on some newfound otherworldly arms and armor*
---
My back is killing me carrying all this loot back to the camps. [Thoron](Lore_Thoron.md) says the gear is better than anything a blacksmith could forge. "Remnants of an ancient battle between the [Golden Saints](Lore_Aureal.md) and [Dark Seducers](Lore_Mazken.md) themselves," he tells us. Still, I have my doubts. What's a wizard know about stopping an [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) arrow coming at your forehead with a poison tip and a full gust of wind?

Plus it don't look right, and it doesn't feel right. Still, the boss swears by it, and if I call our great leader a liar, I'm a dead man come morning.

Still, if he asks me to bring one of those creepy looking [bugs](Lore_Elytra.md) back for him, that's where I draw the line.
