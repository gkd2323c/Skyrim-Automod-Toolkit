Lord Kain (sometimes spelled **Khane**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKain-1)</sup> was a noble knight from the [Barony of Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md), in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Lord Kain was the younger brother of Baron [Shrike](Lore_Shrike.md), who ruled over his domain with an iron fist. Kain was one of his most outspoken critics, and together with Lord [Harth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Harth), they formed the [Knights of the True Horn](Lore_Knights_of_the_True_Horn.md).

## History
Lord Kain was the younger brother of Baron Shrike, who ruled over Lainlyn around [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405). Together with Lord Harth, a noble of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md), he created the Knights of the True Horn, whose name is a subtle attack on Shrike's knightly order, the [Host of the Horn](Lore_Host_of_the_Horn.md). Lord Kain resided in the remote Lainlyn town of Gysotuara and in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), where the Knights were gathering themselves for the inevitable rebellion.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDC-2)</sup> He specifically lived in the Barony of [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFKain-1)</sup>

Sometime later, Lord Kain gathered his forces and staged a coup d'etat in Lainlyn, in the attempt to dethrone Baron Shrike. However, he was defeated and gave his order the message that they would reunite later some other place. Lord Kain died shortly after the battle, but was encountered by a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) necromancer named Arielle Jurard. Through the dark arts of [Necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md), Lord Kain came back to life and left with Jurard, to meet with the remaining Knights at [Battlehorn Castle](Lore_Battlehorn_Castle.md), a stronghold in the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LJJ-3)</sup>

When they arrived at Battlehorn Castle, they were greeted by Lord Jaren, who noticed Kain's gruesome condition. Lord Kain moved like an old man, lacking the power to properly speak, heavily-covered in a cloak, and leaving a foul odor in his wake. When Lord Jaren learned about his true condition and Jurard's deed, he tricked the necromancer to tend Kain in the underground grotto. Lord Jaren and Lord [Garridan](Lore_Garridan_Stalrous.md) buried the two in the grotto, where they remained for several decades and became the True Horn's darkest secret.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LJJ-3)</sup> Many years later in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Lord Kelvyn passed Battlehorn Castle to the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LKW-4)</sup> who uncovered the grotto and defeated the [Lich](Lore_Lich.md), Arielle Jurard and the undying warrior, Lord Kain.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OB-5)</sup>
