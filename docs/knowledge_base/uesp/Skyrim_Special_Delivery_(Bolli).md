## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md).
2. Deliver a purchase agreement to [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md) can generally be found in the market section of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He will gladly tell you of his ownership of [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md), and is even willing to share something about his business with you:

- **Who buys all your fish?**
- *"I sell my catch across a good portion of Skyrim... it's worth its weight in gold to me. The only untapped market left is Markarth. However, I just got word that [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md) at the [Silver Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md)* [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * might be interested. If you're headed that way, I would appreciate you delivering a [purchase agreement](Skyrim_Purchase_Agreement_(Bolli).md) for me. You'd be paid, of course."*
- **Okay, I'll do it.**
- *"Good. Kleppr will pay you as soon as you arrive with the letter. Here you are."*

Travel to Markarth and talk to Kleppr in his inn:

- **I have a purchase agreement for you.**
- *"Ah, Bolli finally got someone out here. Well it's about time. I've been waiting for weeks. Well, thank you for bringing it I suppose. Have a good day."*
- **Aren't you forgetting something?**
- *"What? Oh! How could I have possibly forgotten. Your reward, of course... of course. Here you are. So sorry about that."'*

He will give you a [silver ingot](Skyrim_Silver_Ingot.md), plus one or more leveled ingots in return.
