# Towers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a9/SR-place-Pinefrost_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Pinefrost_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pinefrost_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Pinefrost_Tower.jpg) Pinefrost Tower This page lists all the **towers** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

### [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) Imperial Towers

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Abandoned Prison](Skyrim_Abandoned_Prison.md)** — A small Imperial prison southwest of Windhelm containing the ghosts of its drowned prisoners. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Abandoned+Prison)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Falkreath Watchtower](Skyrim_Falkreath_Watchtower.md)** — A small ruined tower north of Falkreath containing a necromancer. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Falkreath+Watchtower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Pinefrost Tower](Skyrim_Pinefrost_Tower.md)** — A small ruined tower northwest of Dragon Bridge containing leveled animals. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Pinefrost+Tower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Refugees' Rest](Skyrim_Refugees%27_Rest.md)** — A small ruined tower east of Windhelm containing leveled animals. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Refugees%27+Rest)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Rift Watchtower](Skyrim_Rift_Watchtower.md)** — A small tower east of Ivarstead containing Orc bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Rift+Watchtower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Shor's Watchtower](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Watchtower.md)** — A small tower north-northeast of Shor's Stone. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Shor%27s+Watchtower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Snowpoint Beacon](Skyrim_Snowpoint_Beacon.md)** — A small tower southwest of Winterhold containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Snowpoint+Beacon)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Stendarr's Beacon](Skyrim_Stendarr%27s_Beacon.md)** — A small tower southeast of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Stendarr%27s+Beacon)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Whitewatch Tower](Skyrim_Whitewatch_Tower.md)** — A small tower north-northeast of Whiterun. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Whitewatch+Tower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Imperial_Tower.png) **[Widow's Watch Ruins](Skyrim_Widow%27s_Watch_Ruins.md)** — A small ruined tower west-northwest of Solitude containing a hag. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Widow%27s+Watch+Ruins)

### [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) Nordic Towers

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Bannermist Tower](Skyrim_Bannermist_Tower.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower northwest of Falkreath containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bannermist+Tower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Bleakwind Bluff](Skyrim_Bleakwind_Bluff.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower inhabited by hagravens and Forsworn, located northwest of Rorikstead on an outcrop of rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Bleakwind+Bluff)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Cradle Stone Tower](Skyrim_Cradle_Stone_Tower.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower west of Bard's Leap Summit containing a hagraven. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Cradle+Stone+Tower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Dead Crone Rock](Skyrim_Dead_Crone_Rock.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower inhabited by Forsworn, located south-southwest of Markarth, just above Hag Rock Redoubt. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dead+Crone+Rock)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> — A mage tower player home east of Morthal. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Myrwatch+%28CC%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower east-northeast of Ivarstead containing bandits disguised as guards. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Nilheim)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Peak's Shade Tower](Skyrim_Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.md)** — A small ruined Nordic tower east of Falkreath containing a spriggan. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Peak%27s+Shade+Tower)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[South Skybound Watch](Skyrim_South_Skybound_Watch.md)** — A small Nordic tower and ruin northeast of Helgen containing bandits and a giant frostbite spider. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Skybound+Watch+Pass)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Sundered Towers](Skyrim_Sundered_Towers.md)** — A small pair of linked ruined Nordic towers southeast of Karthwasten containing Forsworn and hagravens. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sundered+Towers)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Tower.png) **[Valtheim Towers](Skyrim_Valtheim_Towers.md)** — A small pair of linked ruined Nordic towers east of Whiterun containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Valtheim+Towers)
