[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Legate_Skulnar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Legate_Skulnar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Skulnar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Skulnar.jpg) Legate Skulnar **Legate Skulnar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) who can be found in the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) if the Imperials control [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md), or at the [Falkreath Imperial Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falkreath_Imperial_Camp) if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) control the hold.

Skulnar is clad in a set of Imperial heavy armor. This includes an [Imperial armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Armor), a pair of [Imperial bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bracers), and a pair of [Imperial boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Boots). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Sword) for close-range combat and uses an [Imperial bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Bow) supplemented with 13 [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) when the need arises. He also carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger).
