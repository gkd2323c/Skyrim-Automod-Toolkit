White Rose is a location in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It is home to a breed of black cockerel with flesh rendered toxic by its diet of poisonous beetles, and a bland, plum-like fruit that the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) call the [lizardfruit](Lore_Lizardfruit.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOSC-1)</sup>

Black cockerel and lizardfruit obtained from an expedition to White Rose and prepared with [Argonian culinary techniques](Lore_Argonian_Cuisine.md) were among the many dishes served during the infamously decadent Feast of Saint Coellicia that was hosted by Emperor [Brazollus Dor](Lore_Brazollus_Dor.md) at his estate in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOSC-1)</sup>

## Notes
- A defunct [Imperial](Lore_Empire.md) prison named White Rose Prison was located in the Black Marsh region of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), although it is not known if the prison was built on the same site as White Rose.
