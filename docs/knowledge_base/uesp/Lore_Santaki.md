[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Santaki (or the **Santaki Ruins**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONExplorer-2)</sup> is a [dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) located in the northwestern [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md), in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). In its heyday, it was a [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) settlement but unlike other ruins that have been plundered and explored, sites like Santaki have remained largely untouched.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Santaki-3)</sup> The metalworks of Dwarven [smiths](Lore_Smithing.md) in Santaki are identifiable by its repeating squared-arrow pattern.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONConstruct Anti-1)</sup>

After settling in [Volenfell](Lore_Volenfell.md) in [1E 420](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_420), the Rourken Clan of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) found the Alik'r Desert rife with untouched, rich minerals and spread themselves out to build deep mines and workshops. Santaki was one of these sites,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Santaki-3)</sup> constructed just outside of present-day [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) to the south.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSantaki-4)</sup>

The smiths of Santaki contributed with other Rourken workers to create the Rourken Steamguards, the gauntlets of King [Rourken](Lore_Rourken_(person).md)'s high honor guard, by making its plating with its signature style.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSteamguards-5)</sup> The [Guardian's Eye](Lore_Guardian%27s_Eye.md), an artifact of immense power and importance in Volenfell once stopped in Santaki for a brief maintenance.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TJ1-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TJ2-7)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), bandits led by the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) named Tarrent Hareno took up residence in Santaki. At around the same time, the explorer Tharayya delved into Santaki and found her first [Dwemeris](Lore_Dwemeris.md) inscriptions of the Guardian's Eye. With this clue, she tracked the artifact in Volenfell.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TJ1-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TJ2-7)</sup> The right leg of the Precursor, the first [factotum](Lore_Factotum.md) created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) was used by the Dwemer in Santaki as a sphere fabricator. It was collected by an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who helped repair the Precursor.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONPrecursor-8)</sup>
