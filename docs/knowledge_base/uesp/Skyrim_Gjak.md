[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-npc-Gjak.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gjak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gjak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gjak.jpg) Gjak **Gjak** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and works at [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md). He mines the [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) for [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md) all day long.

Like his colleague, [Bodil](Skyrim_Bodil.md), Gjak never surfaces from the mine and spends his entire life working, relaxing, or eating.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He is equipped with a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), and carries a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
Gjak will greet you by telling you about how long he has been mining. He seems to be fairly content with his life.
