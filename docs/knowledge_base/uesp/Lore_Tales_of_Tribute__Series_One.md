Greetings all Roisters!

It is with great pride that the [Roister's Club](Lore_Roister%27s_Club.md) Founders introduce the following decks for tournament play:

[RED EAGLE](Lore_Red_Eagle.md)
Seize the savage power of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md)! This deck tells the tale of Reach King Faolan's profane pact and his desperate war against the soldiers of [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md)'s legions. What are you willing to sacrifice to achieve victory?

THE DUKE OF CROWS
Rumors of [talking crows](Lore_Crow_Daedra.md) swirl in taverns and guild halls across the continent. The Duke of Crows deck explores the origins of this strange [Blackfeather](Lore_Blackfeather_Court.md) fellowship and its ceaseless quest for shiny treasures!

[RITEMASTER CELARUS](Lore_Celarus.md)
The mysterious [Psijics](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) return to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)! Led by the enigmatic Loremaster [Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md), the Order of PSJJJJ attends to some mysterious business here in Tamriel—and inspires this deck of replenishment and rejuvenation.

[SAINT PELIN](Lore_Pelin.md)
[High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md)'s heroic stand against the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) in [1E 1029](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1029) is the stuff of legends. How better to commemorate that grand victory than with a [Tale of Tribute](Lore_Tales_of_Tribute.md)? Focusing on the heroic beadle, Saint Pelin, this deck will hold your opponent at bay just like the iron gates of [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md)!

GRANDMASTER [DELMENE HLAALU](Lore_Delmene_Hlaalu.md)
Of all the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), none can rival the insatiable greed of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md). Hearkening back to the end of the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md) and the Hlaalu's age of gilded glory, Grandmaster Delmene Hlaalu will guide you to ever greater prestige and profit!

[RAJHIN](Lore_Rajhin.md)
Trickster, thief, and … deity? This deck tracks the cunning cat, Rajhin, on one of his most legendary heists—stealing the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md) from Empress [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md)! Is this story true? Who can say? The Purring Liar leaves everyone bewildered!

SORCERER KING [ORGNUM](Lore_Orgnum.md)
Emerging from the mists of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md), the undying king of the [Sea Elves](Lore_Maormer.md) sails forth to do battle! This deck spins tales of brine and bloodshed, tracking one of [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md)'s most devastating raids on the [High Elf](Lore_Altmer.md) fleet. Now, you can step into this villain's soggy boots and unleash attacks of your own!

[FRANDAR HUNDING](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md)
Wield the [Way of the Sword](Lore_Sword-singers.md) with this legendary tale of the [ansei](Lore_Ansei.md)! Telling the story of Hunding's life, this deck provides limitless flexibility—allowing you to attack the opponent from every angle!

Good draws and good fortune!
The Founders
