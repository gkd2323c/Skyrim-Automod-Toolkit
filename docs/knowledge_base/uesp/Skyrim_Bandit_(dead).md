# Bandit (dead)


These **bandits** are deceased NPCs found in a variety of locations in Skyrim, particularly in caves and in the wilderness. Their races and inventories will vary, but all are dead and are found in fixed locations.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-npc-Bandit_%28NPC%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bandit_%28NPC%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Bandit_(NPC).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Bandit_(NPC).jpg.md) Ilinalta's Deluge These **bandits** are all female [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [wizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Wizard).

They wear [fur armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Armor) while their footwear is randomly determined. There is always a 50% chance of a pair of [fur shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Shoes) and a 50% chance of leveled footwear; from level 1-5 they can be [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots), from level 6-18 they can be [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots), and from level 19 it can be [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots). They carry a random [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), some random loot, some gold, and either a potion of health or magicka.

Despite their condition, they also know a variety of spells, specifically [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), magicka recovery, and three [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells, specifically [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md), and [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md).

They can be found in the following locations:

- In the prison corridor of [Fort Greenwall Prison](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md#Fort_Greenwall_Prison), along with several [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) nearby.<sup>[[¹]](#intnote_civilwar)</sup>
- In [Ilinalta's Deluge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ilinalta%27s_Deep#Ilinalta.27s_Deluge), in the final room in front of [Malyn Varen's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malyn_Varen) skeletal corpse.
- [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md) in the dining area behind a tipped over table
- In the first room of [Irkngthand Arcanex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand#Irkngthand_Arcanex), just past the second flight of stairs.<sup>[[²]](#intnote_tg)</sup>
- In the second chamber of [Shimmermist Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shimmermist_Cave), alongside two other dead bandits in a small alcove.
- On top of a rock southeast of [Harmugstahl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Harmugstahl), with a nearby [sabre cat](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) and the Destruction [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md), *[Horror of Castle Xyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horror_of_Castle_Xyr)*. Lastly, there is a 20% chance of them carrying a leveled shield, up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 12.

The carry an amount of gold and either a [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md), or a [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), all of them leveled to elven quality at level 22. Additionally, they will carry three potions and some random loot such as [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md).

They can be found in the following locations:

- In the first room of [Irkngthand Arcanex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand#Irkngthand_Arcanex), next to a bedroll on the right.<sup>[[*]](#intnote_tg)</sup>
- Slightly past the campsite in [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave), near several [bear traps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bear_Trap).
- Outside of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md), near the campsite. This bandit may also be [reanimated](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie) by a nearby leveled [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-22887&y=89100)

Additionally, he will carry six arrows of the highest possible quality and a [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) leveled to elven quality at level 20. He also carries a bit of random loot and some gold.
