# Temple Zero Society

—Xal of the [Marukhati](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Maruhkati_Selectives)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup> The Temple Zero Society or more simply Temple Zero (sometimes abbreviated as T0) is a secretive organization of scholars,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-the_seed-2)</sup> conspiracy theorists, and revolutionaries which follows their own interpretation of [Marukhati](Lore_Marukh.md) [philosophy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Marukhati_Selective) and studies esoteric historical, mythological and metaphysical subjects. Their philosophy emphasizes the marvel and wonder of the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) at the expense of logic and internal consistency; the theories developed from this philosophy are termed 'Monkey Truth'.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

They are often regarded as heretics, ranting fools,<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and pseudo-intellectuals by their detractors,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-the_seed-2)</sup> and they were subject to persecution by the [Septim Empire.](Lore_Third_Empire.md)<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-pge2-7)</sup><sup>[[UOL 5]](#cite_note-null-8)</sup>
