## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir).
2. Listen to Tolfdir.
3. Follow [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md).
4. Speak with [Quaranir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir).
5. Find the [Augur of Dunlain](Skyrim_Augur_of_Dunlain.md).
6. Report to [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### An Orb Worth Noting
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-quest-Good_Intentions_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Good_Intentions_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions_02.jpg) Quaranir freezing time to speak with you After your conversation with Urag gro-Shub about the newly recovered books, the librarian suggests you speak with Tolfdir about the findings. Tolfdir can be found in the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements), in deep rumination about the orb from [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md), or [the Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md). Let him know about your findings and he will begin to discuss the Eye with you. He will explain how the markings seem to be unique, as they do not appear to be [Elven](Lore_Aldmeris.md), [Daedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Alphabet), or [Ayleid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleid_Language). Ancano will interrupt Tolfdir's musings and demand that you follow him to meet a mysterious visitor who has asked to speak with you.

### A Moment in Time
Follow Ancano to the Arch-Mage's quarters and you will meet Quaranir, another representative from the [Psijic Order.](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) Quaranir will freeze time in order to speak privately with you. He will explain that you must prevent anyone from using the Eye of Magnus, as he fears the consequences its misuse will bring. He will suggest that you seek out the Augur of Dunlain within the College, who may be able to give you better guidance. Quaranir will then unfreeze time and leave, confusing Savos and angering Ancano. Speaking to the various mages about the Augur will lead you to either Tolfdir or [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md) for more information. Tolfdir will tell you about the Augur immediately, but you must pass an easy-level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to convince Mirabelle. Either way, you will learn that the Augur is a former student at the College, but something befell him as a result of his own ambitions. He can now be found in [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden) in his altered state.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-quest-Good_Intentions_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Good_Intentions_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions_03.jpg) The Augur of Dunlain
### The Unearthing
You can access the Midden from a trap door in the northern corner of the courtyard or by the stairwell in the [Hall of Countenance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_Countenance). Making your way to the Augur of Dunlain is relatively easy; you will likely encounter a few [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md) and [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), along with a small number of [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) and [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). Proceed into [The Midden Dark](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden#The_Midden_Dark) and continue until you reach a locked door. When you approach it, the Augur will warn you from the other side that your efforts will only end in disappointment. The door will then open.

### The Augur
The Augur will inform you that others (including Ancano) have previously sought information about the Eye of Magnus without realizing the dangers it poses. While the Augur speaks only of disaster for the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md), he seems encouraged by your path and offers to help you further. He will then inform you that you will need the [Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_Staff_of_Magnus.md) to avert the disaster the Psijics warned you about, and urges you to share this information with [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md).

### More than a Mere Apprentice
Speak to Savos and inform him of the Augur's message. It takes a bit of convincing to get Savos motivated and interested in this turn of events. Eventually, he will direct you to Mirabelle Ervine for more information on the Staff and then give you the [Mage's Circlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mage%27s_Circlet), impressed with your performance thus far.

## Notes
- After Quaranir freezes time but before you speak to him you can steal any item in the Arch-Mage's Quarters without acquiring a bounty.
- If you follow Quaranir out of the College after he freezes time in the Arch-Mage's Quarters, he will walk through Winterhold and enter [The Frozen Hearth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth).
- After you receive the objective to "Report to Savos Aren", [Atronach Forge Recipes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge_Recipe) will begin to appear in random loot.
- When the Eye of Magnus appears in the Hall of the Elements, there is an option to "Activate Eye of Magnus" when you point your crosshairs over it in close proximity, but doing so has no effect.

## Bugs
- When you enter the Arch-Mage's Quarters and go upstairs, time may stop yet Quaranir is not there, and you're stuck forever. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console and manually advance the quest by entering `setstage MG04 40`.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using the console command `prid 0002BA3D` and then `moveto player`, will move him to the College, and the quest should proceed normally.
- When the quest begins, you may be able to use the door linking the Arcaneum to the Arch-Mage's Quarters, even though the Arch-Mage's Quarters is supposed to be off-limits at that point. Entering prior to being told to go there by Ancano will freeze out the quest, requiring you to exit the game via the console. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- You may not get the next quest "Revealing the Unseen" after completing the quest. This appears to be permanent and cannot be fixed by reloading. As the quest does not load, Mirabelle will have normal dialogue and the quest is impossible to begin. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9, fixes this bug.
- When talking to Ancano after talking to Tolfdir, you may get locked in dialogue with him permanently, preventing you from doing anything. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console and manually advance the quest by entering `setstage MG04 30`.
- Ancano may not open the door to the Arch-Mage's Quarters and lead you there. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console command and manually advance the quest stage by entering `setstage MG04 30`. The quest marker above Ancano's head should disappear. The door to the Arch-Mage's Quarters should be unlocked. You are now free to talk to Quaranir and continue the storyline.
- The Augur of Dunlain may be unable to speak his expected lines if you go to the Midden before asking where he is. This is due to a set of bad conditions on the quest script. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.8, fixes this bug.
