It was released on October 4, 2017 for 300 Creation Credits. The sword last appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.

## Official Summary
*Known as the Paladin's Blade and Sword of Heroes, this legendary claymore is imbued with powerful magic, like Fire Resistance, Spell Absorption, and devastating damage. (Weapon can be found in Forelhost southeast of Riften.)*
## Overview
This Creation adds the quest **[The Lost Paladin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Lost_Paladin)**. It does not appear in your journal, but you must go to [Forelhost](Skyrim_Forelhost.md) to fight and kill the [Lost Paladin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Paladin), from whom you will loot [Chrysamere](Skyrim_Chrysamere_(item).md), a two-handed [greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greatsword) which offers [fire resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md), [spell absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md), and slow health regeneration when equipped.
