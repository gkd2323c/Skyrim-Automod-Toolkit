# Black Hands Dagger

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-weapon-Daedric_Dagger.png) *This ritual dagger has been passed from one Grandmaster to another for years. I believe you are the one who should wield it now and darken its blade with blood.*
—Eno Hlaalu<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EH-1)</sup> Black Hands Dagger is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) dagger with a powerful [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) to cause blindness and absorb health from those it strikes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHD-2)</sup> It was a ritualistic weapon wielded by the leaders of the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EH-1)</sup>

## History
Little is known about the Black Hands Dagger's history. It served as a ritual blade and was named after [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), the patron of the Morag Tong, who is also known as the Black Hand. By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the dagger had long been traditionally passed from one Grandmaster of the Morag Tong to the next. At that time, it was in the possession of the then-current Grandmaster, Eno Hlaalu. The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) received the dagger from Eno Hlaalu after executing [Severa Magia](Lore_Severa_Magia.md), the Night Mother of the [Dark Brotherhood's](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) branch, shortly before the end of Eno's tenure as Grandmaster.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EH-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESM-3)</sup>
