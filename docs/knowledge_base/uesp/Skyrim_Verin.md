[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-npc-Verin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Verin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Verin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Verin.jpg) Verin **Verin** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). If he survives, he, along with all other survivors, will make his way out to Bloodchill Cavern. The next time you return to the area, he will no longer be present.

He wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md) and [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md). He wields a leveled dagger.
