## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Drevis Neloren](Skyrim_Drevis_Neloren.md).
2. Find the book in [the Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum).
3. Find the book in [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md).
4. Find the book in [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md).
5. Find the book in [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden).
6. Return to Drevis Neloren.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### First Steps
Once you have level 100 (see [bugs](#Bugs)) in the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skill, travel to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). Once there, find [Drevis Neloren](Skyrim_Drevis_Neloren.md). Ask him if you can learn anything more about Illusion magic. He will give you a spell, [Vision of the Tenth Eye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vision_of_the_Tenth_Eye), and ask you to find four [Master Illusion texts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Master_Illusion_Text). You can only see these books for a thirty second period each time you cast the vision spell.

### Hidden Books
1. The [first book](Skyrim_Master_Illusion_Text_(2).md) is on a table in [the Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum). It is in the west on the outer ring. It appears on the same table as the book *[The Legend of Red Eagle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle)*.
2. The [second book](Skyrim_Master_Illusion_Text_(3).md) can be found in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md). On the landing of the upper level, at the top of the stairs, it lies on the second step on the landing.
3. The [third book](Skyrim_Master_Illusion_Text_(1).md) is on a barrel in the upstairs storeroom of the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md). This is the room opposite of the room with the [Arcane Enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md).
4. The [last book](Skyrim_Master_Illusion_Text_(4).md) is down in [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden), on a table next to the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md).

### Return to Drevis Neloren
Give all the books to [Drevis Neloren](Skyrim_Drevis_Neloren.md) and he will give you the master-level Illusion spell tome [Hysteria](Skyrim_Hysteria.md). You can then also buy the other three master-level tomes from him: [Call to Arms](Skyrim_Call_to_Arms.md), [Harmony](Skyrim_Harmony.md), and [Mayhem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mayhem).

## Bugs
- The quest only requires a skill level of 90, but Drevis's opening dialogue has a requirement of level 100, which is inconsistent with the quest and the other master level quests. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
- Sometimes the Master of Illusion Text books are not where they are indicated above (though they should be nearby). This is usually caused by using a spell near the location before the quest starts.
- Sometimes one of the Master of Illusion Text books will be marked as stolen in your inventory, making it impossible to give the books to Drevis and complete the quest. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The illusion book in any of the locations might not appear when casting Vision of the Tenth Eye, most commonly the books at the Atronach Forge and in the Arcanaeum.
