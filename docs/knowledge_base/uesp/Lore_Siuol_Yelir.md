[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Siuol Yelir [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg/200px-RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-Nalonga_and_Ravan.jpg) Map showing Siuol Yelir by Maiko K'Elmar Siuol Yelir is an island in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md), south of the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls), that is possibly considered a part of the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago. It lies just south of the much larger [Nalonga](Lore_Nalonga.md). [Ravan](Lore_Ravan.md) neighbors it to the east, and to the northeast is [Asil Yelir](Lore_Asil_Yelir.md), with which it shares the word "Yelir".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
