[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.jpg) Elven Hunter Armor **Alternative Armors - Elven Hunter** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds an alternative variant of [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) armor that is based on the [Elven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Elven) armor set from *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.

The quest added by this Creation was made by [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Official Summary
*Includes three pieces of light Elven Hunter armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Once a Hunter" starts by reading Guard Dossier: Aesrael in the Falkreath Barracks.)*
