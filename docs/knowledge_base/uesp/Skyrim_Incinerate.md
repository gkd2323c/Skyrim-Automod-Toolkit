*A blast of fire that does 60 points of damage. Targets on fire take extra damage.* **Incinerate** is an expert level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) and sets targets on fire, dealing 30% of the immediate spell damage and scaling down quadratically over 1 second.

## Effects
- [Incinerate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), 60 pts
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Intense Flames Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 15 secs; if the [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames), raises damage to 75 at first rank or 90 at second rank
- [Aspect of Terror](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aspect_of_Terror), raises damage by 10 pts (15 if both ranks of Augmented Flames are unlocked first).

## Notes
- Incinerate is essentially just a much more powerful (and expensive) version of [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md).
- Like most Fire spells, it can be used to set off [Fire Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Rune) or [Oil Slicks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks) from a safe distance.
- Incinerate has blank explosion. Casting the spell near guards and NPC may incur bounty.
