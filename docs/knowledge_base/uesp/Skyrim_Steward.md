# Steward


The game begins with a steward in each of eight holds in Skyrim—the jarls of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) do not employ stewards. Some are affiliated with either the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) or the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), while three others will serve either allegiance. When a jarl is replaced after you help a side in the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) to capture a city they do not control, or through the negotiations made during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md), some of the newly-instated jarls will seek to employ a new steward of their own, while two will be retained by the new jarl. If the [Battle for Solitude](Skyrim_Battle_for_Solitude.md) is won, [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s entire court will merely change allegiance. [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md) will simply continue his role, as the government of that hold is not replaced.

Deposed Imperial governments will seek refuge in the basement of the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) in Solitude, while deposed Stormcloak governments will go into exile in the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). Only the jarl (and possibly their family), their housecarl, and their steward will be evicted from the building, and they will be unfriendly towards you.

As part of their job, they will hand out rewards for most [bounties](Skyrim_Bounty_Quests.md) given for their hold, even if they were not the one to give the bounty to you. Though the jarl is responsible for deciding if someone is allowed to [buy a house](Skyrim_Houses.md) in their city, it is left to the steward to sell the house and the furnishings.

## Personal Stewards<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, certain [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) can be hired to become a [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) upon purchase and [construction](Skyrim_Construction.md) of a new house.

## Notes
- As the Jarls of the Pale do not have stewards at their court, when you receive a bounty letter in that hold, they will be left signed "-- [...]" due to the absence of a steward.
