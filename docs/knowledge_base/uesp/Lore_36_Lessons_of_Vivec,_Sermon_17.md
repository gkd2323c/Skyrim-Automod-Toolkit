[The Thirty-Six Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md), Sermon Seventeen **by [Vivec](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vivec)** *Book 17 of a series of words of wisdom relating to Vivec*
---

- 'I am an atlas of smoke.'

With this, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) become greater than he had been. These were the days of [Resdaynia](Lore_Resdayn.md), when [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) lived under the wise and benevolent rule of the [ALMSIVI](Lore_Almsivi.md) and their champion the [Hortator](Lore_Nerevar.md).

- 'Seek me without effort for I take many shapes.'

The Hortator was still trying to subdue the heavens with an axe. He was thrown out of the library of the sun by the power of [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md). Vivec found him in a grub field outside of the swamps of the [Deshaan Plain](Lore_Deshaan_Plain.md). They walked for a span in silence, for Nerevar had been humbled and Vivec still had mercy in his hand.

Soon they were walking across [the eastern sea](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) to the [land of snakes and snow demons](Lore_Akavir.md). Vivec wanted to show the Hortator the fighting styles of foreign tongues. They learned the idiom stroke from the pillow book of the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) king. It is shaped like the insight of this page. The Tsaesci serpents vowed to have their vengeance on [the west](Lore_Tamriel.md) at least three times.

They walked farther and saw the spiked waters at the edge of the map. Here the spirit of limitation gifted them with a spoke and bade them find the rest of the wheel.

The Hortator said, 'The edge of the world is made of swords.'

Vivec corrected him. 'They are the bottom row of the world's teeth.'

They walked to the north to the [Elder Wood](Lore_Atmora.md) and found nothing but frozen bearded kings.

They came to [the west](Lore_Yokuda.md) where the [black men](Lore_Redguard.md) dwelt. For a year they studied under their [sword saints](Lore_Ansei.md) and then for another Vivec taught them the virtue of the little reward. Vivec chose a king for a wife and made another race of monsters which ended up destroying the west completely. To a warrior chief Vivec said:

- 'We must not act and speak as if asleep.'

Nerevar wondered if there was anything to learn in [the south](Lore_Aldmeris.md) but Vivec remained silent and only led them back to [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md).

- 'Here,' Vivec said, 'is the last of the last. Within it the [Sharmat](Lore_Dagoth_Ur_(god).md) waits.'

But they both knew that the time was not ready to contest the Sharmat and so they engaged in combat with each other. Vivec marked the Hortator in this way for all of the [Velothi](Lore_Chimer.md) to see. He sealed the wound with the blessing of [Ayem](Lore_Almalexia.md) - [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). At the end of the battle, the Hortator found that he had gathered seven more spokes. He attempted to attach them and form a staff but Vivec would not let him, saying, 'It is not the time for that.'

Nerevar said, 'Where did I find these?'

Vivec said that they had collected them from around the world, though some had come invisibly. 'I am the wheel,' he said, and took that shape. Before the emptiness at the center could live too long, Nerevar put in the spokes.

The ending of the words is ALMSIVI.
