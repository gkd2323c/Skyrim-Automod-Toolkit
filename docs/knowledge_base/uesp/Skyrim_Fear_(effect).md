*Alchemy description:* **Creatures and people up to level <mag> flee from combat for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Creatures and people up to level <mag> flee from combat for <dur> seconds.**
**Fear** (a.k.a. **Rout**, **Hysteria**, or **Demoralize**) decreases the confidence of the targets, causing them to flee from battle. The Fear effect can have several uses: evening the odds if you are outnumbered and/or cornered in a tough fight, making an enemy flee so you can either run away or heal, or making a tough enemy flee so you can deal with the weaker ones first. You can also use it on singular enemies in enclosed spaces (such as ruins or caves) so you can attack them without reprisal, though you will have to chase them as they continue to flee from you. If you fight and injure an enemy under this effect, they will still flee rather than canceling its effects and turning to fight.

Although Fear is considered a negative effect and will result in a poison, it does not benefit from the [Poisoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Poisoner) perk while you are practicing [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md). The fear effect does nothing to the player.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fear if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All weapons *[of Cowardice](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#Fear)*
- All weapons *of Despair*
- All weapons *of Dismay*
- All weapons *of Dread*
- All weapons *of Fear*
- All weapons *of Terror*

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Arm of the Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arm_of_the_Moon)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Arms_of_Chaos.md)</sup>
- [Bolar's Oathblade](Skyrim_Bolar%27s_Oathblade.md)
- [Ruin's Edge](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge_(item).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Ruin%27s_Edge.md)</sup>
- [The Pale Blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale_Blade)

## Spells
The following spells use the Fear group of effects. Following each spell is the specific variant of the effect that it uses:

- [Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(spell).md): *Fear* (`0001ea77`; `Influence Conf Down FFAimed`) - With the [Master of the Mind](Skyrim_Master_of_the_Mind.md) perk, instead uses: *Fear - Master of the Mind* (`0009e0c1`; `Perk Master Mind Conf Down FFAimed`)
- [Rout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rout): *Rout* (`0001ea7a`; `Influence Conf Down FFAimed Area`) - With the Master of the Mind perk, instead uses: *Rout - Master of the Mind* (`0009e0c2`; `Perk Master Mind Conf Down FFAimed Area`)
- [Hysteria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hysteria): *Hysteria* (`0007e8d8`; `Influence Conf Down FFSelf Area250`) - With the Master of the Mind perk, instead uses: *Hysteria - Master of the Mind* (`0009e0c3`; `Perk Master Mind Conf Down FFSelf Area250`)

## Notes
- If the [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) perk is unlocked, the fear effect *Intense Flames Fear* (`00012fcd`; `Perk Intense Flames Conf Down FFAimed`) is added to all [Fire Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) spells.
