**The Ghost of the Green** was a [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) hero and vigilante active throughout the 5th century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLorne-1)</sup> He was an assassin that smote the enemies of [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md). When the Bosmer sacrificed deer hearts and boar tusks to him, an execution would soon follow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TGOTG-2)</sup> He always left behind a fern frond on the bodies of his targets, and always struck with a razor-sharp Wood Elf arrow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TGOTG-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLorne-1)</sup>

## History
In the fifth century of the Second Era, the Ghost of the Green was a Bosmeri assassin that would slay the enemies of the Green. He was dressed in green leathers and a frightful skull. Killing with his bow, it was said that no one could escape his wrath.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TGOTG-2)</sup> He was eventually apprehended by the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) pair of jurisreeve's, Jurisreeve Lorne and Soravil.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLorne-1)</sup> Decades later, the Ghost of the Green would apparently return and start killing once more, though in actuality this was an imposter.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
