## Related Quests
- **[Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages)**: Search for the Dwemer's [Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge).

## Notes
- The enchantment works on all [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) in the game with the exception of [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md).
- The enchantment is useful for when you don't want to engage in combat, or need to escape from a higher level enemy that you cannot defeat. Bashing them with this shield is very unlikely to kill them and causes them to flee for the 15 second ethereal effect.
- Even though the shield is of Dwarven make, the armor bonus from [Ancient Knowledge](Skyrim_Ancient_Knowledge.md#Ancient_Knowledge), rewarded by the [Unfathomable Depths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unfathomable_Depths) quest, does not apply to this item.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.
