[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/SR-creature-Ursine_Guardian.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ursine_Guardian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ursine_Guardian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ursine_Guardian.jpg) Ursine Guardian The **Ursine Guardian** is a spectral [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) located in the woods near [Twilight Sepulcher](Skyrim_Twilight_Sepulcher.md).

To reach the plateau that the bear is found on, follow the gully to the north and when you reach the cliff wall turn south. The guardian will appear in the center of a fairy circle comprising eight [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) mushrooms. Details can be found [here](Skyrim_Unmarked_Places.md), under smaller places of interest.

## Related Quests
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

## Notes
- Despite being spectral in appearance, the guardian spirits seem to retain all aspects of their represented species and are killable with normal weapons. They leave behind ghostly remains, but the loot table matches their represented species as well.
- It will not appear until you are actually on the part of the quest that asks you to hunt it.
- Like living bears, it is capable of transmitting [Bone Break Fever](Skyrim_Bone_Break_Fever.md).
- Southeast of the Ursine Guardian, on a nearby lookout overseeing the forest below, is a Shrine of Akatosh, two Dragon's Tongue, a potion, and a copy of the enchanting skill book [A Tragedy in Black](Skyrim_A_Tragedy_in_Black.md).
