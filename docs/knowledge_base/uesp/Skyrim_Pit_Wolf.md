[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-creature-Wolf.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf.jpg) A Wolf [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-creature-Ice_Wolf.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ice_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ice_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ice_Wolf.jpg) An Ice Wolf **Wolves** are low-level, hostile canines found throughout the wilds of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md) and [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>. They can be encountered alone or in packs of two or three, often hunting other animals. You will often hear their howls before they are spotted. When attacking, wolves typically circle around behind their prey, and their bite has a chance of infecting you with [Rockjoint](Skyrim_Rockjoint.md). Wolves will not attack beast form [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) unless provoked.

## Locations
- [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md) — Two pit wolves in cages at the entrance of the cave
- [Faldar's Tooth](Skyrim_Faldar%27s_Tooth.md) — A large number of wolves are found in the fort, although some of them will attack the bandits as well
- [White River Watch](Skyrim_White_River_Watch.md) — An imprisoned wolf can be saved but it will still attack you after killing all the bandits
- Along the path from [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) to [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md)

## Hearthfire
The *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) lets you build wolf statues in the [trophy room](Skyrim_Trophy_Room.md) addition of any homestead. Available options include a regular wolf on a large base and an ice wolf on a small base. The materials needed for each option are:

## Bugs
- Wolves will stop howling after *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* is installed. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.5.39, fixes this bug.

- - [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](https://images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](Skyrim_Switch.md) The Official Skyrim Switch Edition Patch, version 1.1.177, fixes this bug.

- The "Pack Member" wolves (Howl Summon Wolf, DLC1Howl Summon Ice Wolf, DLC1Howl Summon Werewolf) don't attack vampires or other hostile wilderness creatures due to them being members of the same wilderness creature factions. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. This began when the "Pack Member" wolves were duplicated from the generic wolves. Bethesda forgot to remove them from these wilderness factions afterwards.
