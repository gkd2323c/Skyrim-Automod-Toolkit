## Notes
- A slaughterfish egg can be deposited in the [Fish Hatchery](Skyrim_Fish_Hatchery.md) at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> to produce non-hostile slaughterfish and respawning egg nests.
- Slaughterfish eggs are also categorized as [raw food](Skyrim_Raw_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- Slaughterfish eggs are never found in dead slaughterfishes' inventories; instead what is found is [slaughterfish scales](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Scales.md).
- Although it is not a plant, you can harvest an extra egg from a slaughterfish egg nest if you have the Green Thumb perk from the Alchemy skill tree.
