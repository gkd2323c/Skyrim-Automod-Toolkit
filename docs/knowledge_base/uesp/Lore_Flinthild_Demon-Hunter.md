**Flinthild Demon-Hunter** was a warrior of renown in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). She was a member of [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md)'s kra'jun, a group of heroes in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) that strove to drive out the so-called [demons](Lore_Dragon.md) that plagued the land. She was the second individual recruited by Khunzar-ri, following [Nurarion the Perfect](Lore_Nurarion.md).

## History
In order to combat the [most terrible of the demons](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md), Khunzar-ri ventured north of Elsweyr to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), having heard of Flinthild's strength. He eventually found her at a great feast in [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), where they engaged in a drunken brawl for three days straight. Seeing the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md)'s grace and form, Flinthild became enamored with, and subsequently kidnapped, him. Though she planned to wed Khunzar-ri, she thought differently after sobering up and released him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Fez-1)</sup>

At some point after meeting, Khunzar-ri rescued Flinthild from an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Partsof3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Weapon-4)</sup> After saving her, Flinthild joined Khunzar-ri in the fight against the demons, followed by [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md) and [Sir Cadwell](Lore_Cadwell.md). The demons were overwhelmingly powerful and when fighting them proved to be futile, Cadwell suggested that it may be best to convince the demons that they were now a willing part of their plans. In preparation, Anequina [attuned](Lore_Shadow_Dance.md) to Shadow Dance Temple and incited the [moons](Lore_Moons.md) to align to form an eclipse, allowing the Moon Gate to open a portal to the Plane of [Jode](Lore_Jode.md), where the object the demons desired, [Jode's Core](Lore_Jode%27s_Core.md), rested. The demons' ambitions would be their undoing, as the demons accessed the [Plane of Jode](Lore_Plane_of_Jode.md), but Khunzar-ri convinced them to not simply absorb the power of the core like they originally intended, but instead store their energy within it in the guise that they would combine the lunar power with their own.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOCadwell-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOKhamira-6)</sup>

The demons fell for the trick and were weakened, fleeing to the wilderness. As suggested by Nurarion the Perfect, Khunzar-ri lured them to the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md), an ancient set of ruins. Flinthild placed a shard of the demons' essence interred within an urn of moonlight in the halls, while Khunzar-ri convinced the demons to enter. Once they did, the kra'jun locked them within the halls, where they would remain for millennia.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
