[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Camel.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Camel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Camel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Camel.jpg) Iron Atronach Camel Rozaba af-Nizhadal was a famous [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) mage. She was known to conjure and ride [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) [camels](Lore_Camel.md) across the deserts of Hammerfell. She referred to these creatures as "glass-treaders", as the atronach's scorching feet left behind glass tracks wherever she rode.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAC-1)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the art of conjuring Iron Atronachs shaped like camels was popularized among conjuration mages. At that time adventurers in Hammerfell were still finding glass tracks from where the atronach's scorching feet seared the sand.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IAC-1)</sup>
