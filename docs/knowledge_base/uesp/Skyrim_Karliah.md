[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-npc-Karliah.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karliah.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah.jpg) Karliah **Karliah** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) and a member of the [Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). She is also a former member of Skyrim's [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) but has spent the last 25 years in exile after allegedly murdering her former Guildmaster, Nightingale, and lover: [Gallus](Skyrim_Gallus.md). She will eventually become a quest giver and temporary follower after advancing in the Thieves Guild questline. After the main Guild quest she resides in Nightingale Hall.

Karliah's unique outfit consists of [her armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah%27s_Armor), [her gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah%27s_Gloves), [her boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah%27s_Boots), and [her hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah%27s_Hood). She also carries around a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), leveled [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows) and [her bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karliah%27s_Bow). After the quest [Trinity Restored](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trinity_Restored), her outfit changes to a set of [Nightingale armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Armor), which includes a pair of [Nightingale gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Gloves), a pair of [Nightingale boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Boots) and a [Nightingale hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Hood). She also switches to the [Nightingale bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Bow) which she subsequently gives to you after the quest [Blindsighted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blindsighted).

According to the book [*The Nightingales Vol.2*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Nightingales_Vol._2), Karliah's maternal grandparents are [Queen Barenziah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barenziah) and a Nightingale thief by the name of Drayven Indoril. Karliah's mother Dralsi taught her the art of thievery and how to survive in Skyrim living as a rogue but did not induct Karliah into the Nightingales before her death. Karliah was inducted by Gallus himself.

### Speaking With Silence
When you and Mercer Frey make your way through Snow Veil Sanctum, you will soon approach a puzzle-ring lock. Mercer will be able to open it, but as soon as you enter the chamber, Karliah will shoot you with a poisoned arrow, which causes you to fall to the ground. Mercer will then approach Karliah, and a conversation between the two can be heard:

**Karliah:** *"Give me a reason to try."*
**Mercer:** *"You're a clever girl, Karliah. Buying [Goldenglow Estate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goldenglow_Estate) and funding [Honningbrew Meadery](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Meadery.md) was inspired."*
**Karliah:** *"'To ensure an enemy's defeat, you must first undermine his allies.' It was the first lesson Gallus taught us."*
**Mercer:** *"You always were a quick study."*
**Karliah:** *"Not quick enough, otherwise Gallus would still be alive."*
**Mercer:** *"Gallus had his wealth and he had you. All he had to do was look the other way."*
**Karliah:** *"Did you forget the Oath we took as Nightingales? Did you expect him to simply ignore your methods?"*
**Mercer:** *"Enough of this mindless banter! Come, Karliah. It's time for you and Gallus to become reunited!"*
**Karliah:** *"I'm no fool, Mercer. Crossing blades with you would be a death sentence. But I can promise you the next time we meet, it will be your undoing."*

I made a split second decision to get you out of the way and it prevented your death."* You can choose to respond in three different ways:

Of course... it's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * the only outsider Gallus trusted with the knowledge of his Nightingale identity."* At your remark that the word "Nightingale" has been mentioned again, she will go on to explain: *"There were three of us. I promise to join you there as soon as I can."* Once you've traveled to Winterhold and have spoken to Enthir, the quest [Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers) will begin.

### Hard Answers
Once you've gained a copy of the translation from [Calcelmo's Tower](Skyrim_Calcelmo%27s_Tower.md), you are to return to Enthir and Karliah, who can now be found in the [cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth_Cellar) of the Frozen Hearth. Once you hand over the translation to Enthir, he will begin to translate the contents of Gallus's journal and a conversation between himself and Karliah will occur:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-npc-Karliah_05.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karliah_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah_05.jpg) *"Anything else, Enthir? Anything about... the Nightingales?"* **Enthir:** *"Hmm. This is intriguing, but highly disturbing. It appears that Gallus had suspicions about Mercer Frey's allegiance to the Guild for months. Gallus had begun to uncover what he calls an "...unduly lavish lifestyle replete with spending vast amounts of gold on personal pleasures.""*
**Karliah:** *"Does the journal say where this wealth came from?"*
**Enthir:** *"Yes. Gallus seems certain that Mercer had been removing funds from the Guild's treasury without anyone's knowledge."*
**Karliah:** *"Anything else, Enthir? Anything about... the Nightingales?"*
**Enthir:** *"Hmm. Yes, here it is. The last few pages seem to describe "the failure of the Nightingales" although it doesn't go into great detail. Gallus also repeatedly mentions his strong belief that Mercer desecrated something known as the Twilight Sepulcher."*
**Karliah:** *"Shadows preserve us. So it's true..."*
**Enthir:** *"I'm not familiar with the Twilight Sepulcher. What is it? What's Mercer Frey done?"*
**Karliah:** *"I'm sorry Enthir, I can't say. All that matters is that we deliver your translation to the Guild immediately. Farewell, Enthir... words can't express..."*
**Enthir:** *"It's alright Karliah. You don't have to say a word."*

After you have spoken to Enthir, you are directed to speak again with Karliah. Approaching her, she will remark: *"We must hasten to Riften before Mercer can do any more damage to the Guild."*
***"Gallus's journal mentioned the "Twilight Sepulcher.""***
*"You've come this far, so I see no harm in concealing it any longer. The Twilight Sepulcher is the temple to Nocturnal. It's what the Nightingales are sworn to protect with their lives."*
***"Why does it require that type of protection?"***
*"Everything that represents Nocturnal's influence is contained within the walls of the Sepulcher. Now it seems Mercer's broken his oath with Nocturnal and defiled the very thing he swore to protect."*
***"Thieves and temples. It just doesn't add up."***
*"I felt the same way when Gallus first revealed these things to me. I think given time, you'll understand what I mean."*
***"I'd understand better if less mystery was involved."***
*"As a Nightingale, I've been sworn to secrecy regarding the Sepulcher. I know the Guild doesn't do much to foster faith, but I'm going to have to ask that you continue to trust me."*
***"Very well, we do it your way for now."***
*"I'll make for Riften and scout the situation; see if I can discover what Mercer's up to. When you're ready, meet me at the Ragged Flagon. In the meantime, I wanted you to have this. It belonged to Gallus, but given the circumstances I think he'd approve."*
***"I'll put it to good use."***
*"If the Guild isn't willing to listen to reason, you might have to."*

Karliah will then hand you a [Nightingale Blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Blade). If you approach her before heading off to Riften, you are able to talk to her about several topics:

I think some of these people are beginning to suspect who I am. Are you ready to face the Guild?"* You can answer with any of the three following dialogue options:

| Your Dialogue | Karliah's Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| ***"I'm ready. Let's go."*** | *"Keep your eyes open. I'm not sure what to expect when we enter the cistern."* |
| ***"What if Mercer's there?"*** | *"Then we show them Gallus's journal and hope for the best. Remember, we have proof and all he's got is his word."* |
| ***"No, I'm not."*** | *"Time is of the essence here, please hurry back."* |

Once you finally decide that you're ready to enter the Cistern, both you and Karliah will make your way there through the Ragged Flagon. When you enter the Cistern, you will be immediately confronted by a hostile Brynjolf, Delvin, and Vex. Brynjolf will initiate the conversation first by making accusations towards Karliah:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_03.jpg) *"Please, lower your weapons so we can speak. I have proof that you've all been misled!"* **Brynjolf:** *"You better have a damn good reason to be here with that murderer."*
**Karliah:** *"Please, lower your weapons so we can speak. I have proof that you've all been misled!"*
**Brynjolf:** *"No tricks, Karliah or I'll cut you down where you stand. Now what's this so-called proof you speak of?"*
**Karliah:** *"I have Gallus's journal. I think you'll find its contents disturbing."*
**Brynjolf:** *"Let me see. No, it... can't be. This can't be true. I've known Mercer too long..."*
**Karliah:** *"It's true, Brynjolf. Every word. Mercer's been stealing from the Guild for years, right under your noses."*
**Brynjolf:** *"There's only one way to find out if what the lass says is true. Delvin, I'll need you to open the Vault * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *."*

At this point, all four thieves will start to make their way towards the guild's vault to confirm what was written in Gallus' journal:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Delvin_Mallory_04.jpg) *"He didn't need to pick the lock."* **Delvin:** *"Wait just a blessed moment, Bryn. What's in that book? What did it say?"*
**Brynjolf:** *"It says Mercer's been stealing from our vault for years. Gallus was looking into it before he was murdered."*
**Delvin:** *"How can Mercer open up a vault that needs two keys? It's impossible. Could he pick his way in?"*
**Vex:** *"That door has the best puzzle locks money can buy. There's no way it can be picked open."*
**Karliah:** *"He didn't need to pick the lock."*
**Delvin:** *"What is she on about?"*
**Brynjolf:** *"Use your key on the vault, Delvin. We'll open it up and find out the truth."*
**Delvin:** *"I've used my key, but the vault's still locked up tighter than a drum. Now use yours."*
**Brynjolf:** *"By the Eight! It's gone, everything's gone! Get in here, all of you!"*
**Delvin:** *"The gold, the jewels... it's all gone."*
**Vex:** *"That son-of-a-bitch! I'll kill him!"*
**Brynjolf:** *"Vex! Put it away... right now. We can't afford to lose our heads... we need to calm down and focus."*
**Delvin:** *"Do what he says, Vex. This isn't helpin' right now."*
**Vex:** *"Fine. We do it your way. For now."*
**Brynjolf:** *"Delvin, Vex... watch the Flagon. If you see Mercer, come tell me right away."*

Afterwards, all four thieves will depart from the vault. If you approach Karliah, you can ask her how Mercer could have gotten into the vault. She will answer very vaguely to your question: *"All I can tell you for now is that he's abused his authority as a Nightingale."* When you argue about her vague answer about the Nightingales, she will continue to tell you: *"As a Nightingale, I took an oath and was sworn to secrecy. Be patient and your time will come."*

After you have shown [Mercer's plans](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mercer%27s_Plans) to Brynjolf, you will be told that Karliah has requested to speak to both of you. At this point, the quest [Trinity Restored](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trinity_Restored) will begin.

## Notes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-npc-Karliah_13.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karliah_13.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah_13.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karliah_13.jpg) Karliah without her hood - She is voiced by [Moira Quirk](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moira_Quirk).
- Sometimes when leaving Snow Veil Sanctum, a frost troll will have wandered near Karliah's camp. She can become stuck in her conversation because of this. Reloading an earlier save and killing the troll before she progresses through her dialogue solves the problem.
- Karliah carries a wide variety of arrows which can be [pickpocketed](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md). Her inventory respawns with each new encounter, making her a great source of arrows. If you save frequently between each item, you can reload if you get caught.
- Karliah is mentioned in the in-game book, [*The Nightingales Vol.2*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Nightingales_Vol._2).
- She is the only [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) in the game with purple eyes instead of the standard Dark Elf red eyes.
- If you attack her and enter combat mode in Irkngthand during the quest [Blindsighted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blindsighted), she will follow you and come to the first floor, but if you leave Blindsighted, she won't follow you out but stop combat with you. Go back to Irkngthand and invite her to be your quest follower, you can bring Brynjolf and Karliah out.
- During the quest Blindsighted, you can ask Brynjolf and Karliah to "stay here" and give the weapon and jewelry you want them to equip by stealing, but they won't change their armor.

## Bugs
- You may not be able to find Karliah in [Nightingale Hall](Skyrim_Nightingale_Hall.md) at the end of the Thieves Guild quest line. This is most likely caused by not completing enough sidequests for Vex or Delvin. If this is the case, you will instead find her at the very end of the [Snow Veil Sanctum](Skyrim_Snow_Veil_Sanctum.md), the place where she shot you. The actual bug is apparently caused by the quest [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md) never starting. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) It might be possible to fix this issue using the `setstage TGLeadership 50` console command.
- [![On Xbox](https://images.uesp.net/3/33/Xbox.svg)](Skyrim_Xbox.md) On occasions, she may be found wandering [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) upon trying to return to the Nightingale Hall after the completion of Under New Management.
- Karliah may also be found stuck in a wait package in the cellar of [The Frozen Hearth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). - [![On Xbox](https://images.uesp.net/3/33/Xbox.svg)](Skyrim_Xbox.md) One way to fix this issue is to redo the previous quest (get the rubbing and return to the cellar of The Frozen Hearth) and she will be unstuck. Go through the dialogue, then wait 24 hours before meeting her at The Ragged Flagon.
- [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md) 1.9 was supposed to remove Karliah's invisibility spell when Speaking With Silence is finished, but the script did not have the necessary statement to do so. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
