[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-npc-Urog.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Urog.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Urog.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Urog.jpg) Urog **Urog** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) living in [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). She is the daughter of the tribe's chieftain, [Mauhulakh](Skyrim_Chief_Mauhulakh.md), and sister to [Dushnamub](Skyrim_Dushnamub.md). [Yatul](Skyrim_Yatul.md) and [Bolar](Skyrim_Bolar.md) are her great-aunts.

She can be found using a training dummy to practice her axe skills from 7am to 7pm, right in front of the stronghold's [longhouse](Skyrim_Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.md). After that, she heads inside to eat until midnight, after which she heads to bed.

## Dialogue

- *"Yatul's been teaching me to hunt. I'm still learning, but she's the best teacher I could have."*
- *"My brother keeps trying to spin these fantasies on me about our aunts and our father. He's crazy."*
- *"Sometimes I wonder if my aunts are right about my father -- he does seem weak."*

## Conversations
Urog can be seen engaging in conversations with both her aunt, Yatul, and her father:

**Yatul**: *"Come, child, I will tell you how to track a snow cat."*
**Urog**: *"But you said that was impossible."*
**Yatul**: *"No, just very difficult. You have to look for shift in various shades of white."*
**Urog**: *"My eyes are sharp."*
**Yatul**: *"They are, but your mind has to be sharp, too."*

**Urog**: *"What's the most dangerous beast you've ever killed?"*
**Yatul**: *"Oh, probably a troll."*
**Urog**: *"They're fearsome!"*
**Yatul**: *"They are indeed. Nothing like your mother, though!"*
**Urog**: *"That's what everyone keeps saying. But you didn't kill her. That's what I was asking."*
**Yatul**: *"Oh of course. My mistake."*

**Urog**: *"What about my father? Was he a good hunter?"*
**Yatul**: *"Oh, you've seen your father just trying to chase a skeever from his house. He's a bit soft."*
**Urog**: *"Sometimes I'm surprised he's still the chief."*
**Yatul**: *"We all are, my dear, but sometimes the Divines reward those who don't seem to deserve it. Call it dumb luck."*

**Mauhulakh**: *"How is your hunting with Yatul?"*
**Urog**: *"We are strong. We get the job done."*
**Mauhulakh**: *"I'm proud of you for helping provide for us."*
**Urog**: *"Well someone has to provide around here."*
**Mauhulakh**: *"What is that supposed to mean?"*
**Urog**: *"Nothing."*

**Mauhulakh**: *"I don't know what your aunts told you, but..."*
**Urog**: *"They're not my aunts. They're your aunts. And they told me enough."*
**Mauhulakh**: *"What are you talking about?"*
**Urog**: *"About how the Divines cursed you. What horrible thing did you do to deserve it?"*
**Mauhulakh**: *"Nothing! I... it's ill fortune. That's all."*
**Urog**: *"Well, your ill fortune means I have no mother."*
**Mauhulakh**: *"I... I'm sorry."*

**Mauhulakh**: *"Haven't I always been a good father? A good chief?"*
**Urog**: *"Yatul and I do all the hunting. [Mul](Skyrim_Mul_gro-Largash.md) and [Gadba](Skyrim_Gadba_gro-Largash.md) do all the mining. It seems like all you do is find more wives to get killed."*
**Mauhulakh**: *"That's not fair, now."*
**Urog**: *"Just... try and keep out of our way."*

## Notes
- Urog has two additional lines of dialogue which she was supposed to say after having completed [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) `Narzulbur Free Form Wife Quest`, which involved getting an [unused NPC](Skyrim_Uglarz.md) to return to Narzulbur in order to serve as Mauhulakh's new wife. She would inform you that Uglarz instructs her now, rather than Yatul: *"Uglarz is a great huntress. Perhaps she can teach me something new."* and *"I never would have thought, but Uglarz is an even better hunter than Yatul was. The things I'm learning now..."*
