[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-place-Stonefang_Isle.jpg/200px-ON-place-Stonefang_Isle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stonefang_Isle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stonefang_Isle.jpg) Stonefang Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/ON-map-Stonefalls.jpg/200px-ON-map-Stonefalls.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Stonefalls.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Stonefalls.jpg) A map of Stonefalls Stonefang Isle is an island located north of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md), in northern [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). It is home to the [Daedric ruins](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) of Stonefang Temple, built atop the subterranean Stonefang Cavern beneath. The island was first occupied during the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) by the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) people, ancestors to the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), who built an extensive Daedric temple from [basalt](Lore_Basalt.md) rock. It was dedicated to the [Good Daedra](Lore_Good_Daedra.md): [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), and [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOC-3)</sup> Eventually the island and the temple became abandoned.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The ruins were briefly reoccupied in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) by the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) and the Daedric minions of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), acting as a lair within which the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) could sacrifice mortal [souls](Lore_Souls.md) to fill the [Mortuum Vivicus](Lore_Mortuum_Vivicus.md). The Daedra were swiftly purged from island by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), which was under the effective control of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) at the time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
