[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-place-The_Pale.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Pale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Pale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Pale.jpg) The snow-covered pine forests of the Pale on a rare sunny day **The Pale** is a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in northern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with its capital in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). It is initially affiliated with the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), but can be captured by the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) after completing [A False Front](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md) and [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad_(Imperial).md). It can also change sides as part of negotiations during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md).

The Pale is sparsely populated, with safe places for travelers few and far between. The snowy terrain that makes up most of the hold is unsuitable for farming; however, the far southern edge, where not mountainous, is more hospitable.

One of three entrances to [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) is in the depths of [Raldbthar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar), a [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) south of [Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md), the largest lake in the Pale. [River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim) flows from this lake.

## Flora and Fauna
[Snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) thrive in the harsh, snow-covered environment, but the sturdy [blue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower) and [purple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) variants of the mountain flower can also be found. Along the coast, [Nordic barnacles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle), [clams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam), and [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spiky_Grass) dominate.

[Giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) are common here, as are the usual denizens of the snow: [sabre cats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowy_Sabre_Cat), [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md), [ice wraiths](Skyrim_Ice_Wraith.md), [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md), and [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md).
