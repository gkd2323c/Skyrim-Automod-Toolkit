Cook's Journal **by an Unknown Bandit Cook** *The journal of a cook that took the phrase dog-eat-dog world a little too literally*
---
Meat! Meat meat meat! That's all they ever want!

I made them some nice [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md) and they threw them in my face!

I told them that if they'd bring me some [fish](Skyrim_Fish.md) or [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison) I'd cook it up, but all they ever do is waste their wages at the ring.

But maybe there is a way I can get them [some meat](Skyrim_Dog_Meat.md).
