# Narfinsel Schism

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/ON-place-Narsinfel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Narsinfel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Narsinfel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Narsinfel.jpg) [Narfinsel](Lore_Narfinsel.md), the namesake of the war The Narfinsel Schism was a civil conflict that spanned the [Late Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#Late_Merethic_Era) to [1E 198](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_198).

The war was held between the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The war's conclusion, brought in by the Scouring of [Wendelbek](Lore_Wendelbek.md), marked the end of any organized resistance to Daedra worship in Cyrodiil. The lasting effects of the Schism were theorized to have greatly contributed to the fall of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) and the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), nearly half a century later.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ASIV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CAATAYQ-2)</sup>

## Major Battles
- **Scouring of Wendelbek**, 1E 198. Marking the end of the war, King Glinferen of [Atatar](Lore_Atatar.md) led a combined force of [Daedraphile](Lore_Daedraphile.md) warriors against the traditionalist, Aedra-worshipping [Barsaebics](Lore_Barsaebics.md) of Wendelbek, driving them south into [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).
