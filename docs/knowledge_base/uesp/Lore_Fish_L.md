## Lamprey
The **Lamprey** is a type of fish that can be found in the foul waters of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).

- The **Mud Lamprey** can be found in the lakes of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).
- The **Sanguine Lamprey** can be found in the foul waters of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Lingweloce

## Lizardfish

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Loach
The **Loach** is a type of fish.

- The **Black Anvil Loach** can be found in the foul waters of the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).
- The **Gray Loach** can be found in the foul waters of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).
- The **Larich Loach** can be found in the rivers of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).
- The **Rustbelly Loach** can found in the foul waters of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).
- The **Solitude Loach** can be found in the rivers of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).
- The **Wen Loach** could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

The **Lodsilungur** is a type of fish that can be found in the lakes of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).<sup>[†](#intnote_qc)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Longfin
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-ingredient-Abacean_Longfin.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Abacean_Longfin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Abacean_Longfin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Abacean_Longfin.jpg) An Abecean Longfin The **Longfin** is a common type of fish that can be found in saltwater across Tamriel.

- The **Abecean Longfin** can be caught in ponds, lakes, and other bodies of water and can be used as an ingredient in [alchemy](Lore_Abecean_Longfin.md).
- The **Morthal Longfin** can be found in the rivers of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Abacean_Longfin.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Common_Fish)
