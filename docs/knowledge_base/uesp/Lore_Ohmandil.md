[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/ON-place-Torinaan.jpg/200px-ON-place-Torinaan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Torinaan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Torinaan.jpg) Torinaan **Saint ** Ohmandil was a revered figure among the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COSO-1)</sup>

Ohmandil attained sainthood at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Relic_Rescue-2)</sup> Their circlet was kept as a holy relic in Torinaan,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COSO-1)</sup> among other prized items of religious and cultural value.

In 2E 582, the sacred site of Torinaan was corrupted by [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). The ancient guardians that guarded the relics were corrupted by the Daedra. Monastic Nenaron, who served as a relic keeper, recruited [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to rescue the relics from the site so they could be relocated to a safer location. The adventurer managed to retrieve the relics, and they were later relocated.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MN-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Relics-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Relic_Rescue-2)</sup>
