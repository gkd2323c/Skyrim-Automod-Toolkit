[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-item-Amulet_of_Bats.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amulet_of_Bats.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Bats.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Bats.jpg) The Amulet of Bats The Amulet of Bats is an [amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet) retrieved as part of the [Amulets of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest. The silver-colored chain of the amulet is made of small bones and vertebra, connected to a pendant of a bat skull, with red gems in the eye sockets; as opposed to the bronze-colored chain and green gems of the other [Amulet of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power), the [Amulet of The Gargoyle](Skyrim_Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.md). Its enchantment has no effect when you are in human form, but causes your [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) ability Bats to [drain health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) from nearby enemies when it is used, in addition to its usual ability to allow you to dodge damage for two seconds.

## Notes
- Another unobtainable [Amulet of Bats](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Amulet_of_Bats) exists in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit). This amulet has the same model as the Amulet of The Gargoyle, and was meant to give you the Bats power while a vampire lord.
