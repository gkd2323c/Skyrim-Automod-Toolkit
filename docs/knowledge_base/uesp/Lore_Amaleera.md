*These tomes require my strict attention. Some of them have a tendency to … misbehave.*
—Amaleera<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Amaleera-1)</sup> Amaleera was a powerful [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) mage,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Amaleera-1)</sup> and a member of the [Conclave of the Eleven Forces](Lore_Conclave_of_the_Eleven_Forces.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RI-2)</sup> the ruling council of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijiic_Order.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oriandra-3)</sup> She was also the librarian,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Amaleera-1)</sup> and keeper of the Temporal Tomes in Amaleera's Psijic library,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOTT-4)</sup> although the primary responsibility for overseeing the protection and maintenance of the Psijic libraries as a whole rested with Loremaster [Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md) at that time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Celarus-5)</sup>

## History
Little is known about Amaleera's past before she joined the Order.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Amaleera-1)</sup> During the [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) crisis related to the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Amaleera, as a member of the Conclave of the Eleven Forces, participated in a complex scrying ritual led by the Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oriandra-3)</sup> After uncovering the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md)'s involvement, the Ritemaster requested that the council determine a new investigative focus, expecting that Amaleera and [Iradal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iradal) would be able to explore new avenues for the investigation.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RI-2)</sup> Following the death of Ritemaster Iachesis, the remaining members of the Conclave, including Amaleera, honored Iachesis's dying wish by agreeing to appoint Valsirenn as the interim Ritemaster.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Valsirenn-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RI-2)</sup> This decision was made until the Conclave could come to a consensus on a more permanent and suitable candidate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oriandra-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Valsirenn-6)</sup>

As a member of the Conclave she held an important role within the Order and was responsible for maintaining the Temporal Tomes,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOTT-4)</sup> also known as the Elusive Manuscripts.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EM-7)</sup>

The [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) possessed the ability to peer into [fate](Lore_Fate.md) lines beyond the borders of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Among the secrets they uncovered were tomes that spoke of [alternative timelines and realities](Lore_Many_Paths.md), where pivotal events unfolded in other ways. These tomes were stored in Amaleera's Psijic library on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), five of those tomes escaped the library and moved to various places associated with their contents.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOTT-4)</sup>

The *[Coral Aerie Temporal Tome](Lore_Coral_Aerie_Temporal_Tome.md)* authored by Varallion, spoke of a reality where Lord [Bacaro Volorus](Lore_Bacaro_Volorus.md) of the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md) had succeeded, and as such it ventured to the island of the [Coral Aerie](Lore_Coral_Aerie.md), where some of his supporters had established a base.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CATT-8)</sup>

The *[Garden of Sacred Numbers Temporal Tome](Lore_Garden_of_Sacred_Numbers_Temporal_Tome.md)* spoke of threats endangering [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) from the [Void](Lore_Void.md). While sacred numbers were invoked to stop these threats, they eventually succeeded in that reality. The tome could be located in the Garden of Sacred Numbers on [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), a place dedicated to the contemplation of [numerology](Lore_Numerology.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-GOSNTT-9)</sup>

The *[Karnwasten Temporal Tome](Lore_Karnwasten_Temporal_Tome.md)*, by Raavarion, spoke of a reality where the [Sea Sloads](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) took over Summerset. It was found in the northern parts of the isle, in the grotto of [Karnwasten](Lore_Karnwasten.md), one of the bases of the Sea Sload invaders.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-KTT-10)</sup>

The *[Traitor's Vault Temporal Tome](Lore_Traitor%27s_Vault_Temporal_Tome.md)*, by Rullinalion, spoke of a reality where the infamous [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) took over the world. The tome relocated to the Traitor's Vault, a place on Artaeum where many of his experiments were contained.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TVTT-11)</sup>

The *[Vault of Moawita Temporal Tome](Lore_Vault_of_Moawita_Temporal_Tome.md)* described a reality where the Scouring of the Moors occurred in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) and contained a description of the [Scarf of the Obscene](Lore_Scarf_of_the_Obscene.md), an artifact of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md). The tome moved to the Vault of Moawita, where the Psijics stored many dangerous artifacts.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-VOMTT-12)</sup> All five books were eventually rediscovered and returned to the Psijic's custody.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOTT-4)</sup>
