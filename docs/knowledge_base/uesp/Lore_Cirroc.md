**Cirroc the Lofty** was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) Harbinger of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). He was among the notable Harbingers recorded by [Swyk the Long-Sighted](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Swyk_the_Long-Sighted). His legacy of martial prowess and bladework are retained by the Companions of the modern day.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) Jorrvaskr, the mead hall of the Companions. Cirroc first came to [Jorrvaskr](Lore_Jorrvaskr.md) as a servant, but when he was disrespected by an honorless warrior, he quickly proved his skill.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> He saved the life of Harbinger [Tulvar the Unmentioned](Lore_Tulvar_the_Unmentioned.md), earning him the title of honorary Companion. With his speed and cunning, he grew to be one of the fiercest Shield-Brothers the Companions had to offer. Eventually, he became Harbinger himself, the first one in history to not be of [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) heritage.

Cirroc's rise expanded existing rifts within the faction over the purity of Atmoran blood and [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s legacy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup> These rifts would finally fracture the guild when [Henantier the Outsider](Lore_Henantier_the_Outsider.md), an [elf](Lore_Mer.md) would be made Harbinger, causing many Companions to lay down their weapons and leave.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> Unsurprisingly,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup> Cirroc's reign was short, but his martial ability and swordsmanship were notable.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Cirroc the Lofty's name is an [easter egg](Skyrim_Easter_Eggs.md#Star_Trek) which alludes to [Cirroc Lofton](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cirroc_Lofton), the actor that played Jake Sisko in the television series [Star Trek: Deep Space Nine](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Star_Trek:_Deep_Space_Nine).
- According to a dream of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) Harbinger [Kodlak Whitemane](Lore_Kodlak_Whitemane.md), Cirroc, despite being a Redguard, ascended to [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Kodlak-3)</sup>
