# Temples


**[Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md)** — A small Aedric temple dedicated to Dibella, the Aedric goddess of love, beauty, and artistry. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Dibella) **[Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md)** — The main temple of Solitude and Skyrim, housing shrines to the Eight Divines. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+the+Divines) **[Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md)** — A small temple in Whiterun devoted to the goddess Kynareth. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Kynareth) **[Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md)** — A temple in Riften devoted to the Aedric goddess Mara, one of the Eight Divines and goddess of mortal understanding, love and compassion. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Mara) **[Temple of Talos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Talos)** — Located just northwest of Candlehearth Hall in Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Temple+of+Talos) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Temple of Xrib](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Xrib)** — A medium-sized cave south-southwest of Winterhold containing Falmer and chaurus. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sightless+Pit) A Snow Elf temple, added by the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) official add-on, is found in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).

**[Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A relatively well-preserved portion of the Chantry of Auri-El in the Forgotten Vale. Other temples, added by the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) official add-on, are found on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
