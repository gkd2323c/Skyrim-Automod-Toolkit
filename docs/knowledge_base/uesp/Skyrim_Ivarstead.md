[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/SR-place-Ivarstead.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ivarstead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ivarstead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ivarstead.jpg) Ivarstead **Ivarstead** is a milling [town](Skyrim_Towns.md) in the western [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

It is at the base of the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md) on the shores of [Lake Geir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Geir), with one of the forks of the [Darkwater River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River) powering the sawmill on its way to join the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md). The town consists of five buildings, with [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md) just to the east overshadowing the settlement. Some of the town's residents believe the barrow to be haunted; the innkeeper [Wilhelm](Skyrim_Wilhelm.md) willingly tells you more. He runs [Vilemyr Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn) at the southern end of the town opposite [Klimmek's house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Klimmek%27s_House). To the north is [Fellstar Farm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellstar_Farm) opposite [Temba Wide-Arm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temba_Wide-Arm)'s sawmill, and across the river is [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi)'s destroyed house.

It is a town in decline, with fewer travelers visiting since the haunting of the barrow began. Some residents fear for the town's survival, as many of the townspeople have either left for [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) or want to move away. Pilgrims and travelers often pass through the town on their way up the "[7,000 steps](Skyrim_Climb_the_Steps.md)" to [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md).

## Notes
- If *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* is installed, one [clay deposit](Skyrim_Clay.md) is southeast of [Narfi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Narfi)'s dilapidated house and one [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry) is west of it. 2 more clay deposits are on opposite sides of the bridge on the southern edge of town.
- Ivarstead is marked on your map without anyone mentioning the town once you start the quest [The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md) (from [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md)).
- You can ask all residents except Narfi what they know about High Hrothgar.
- After the main quest is completed, if you look up at the Throat of the World, you may see the dragon [Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) flying around.
- Ivarstead also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ivarstead) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
