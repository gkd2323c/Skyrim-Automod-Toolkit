# Imperial College

The Imperial College is an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution. Little is known of the academy, except that it publishes books on topics of the research of [magic](Lore_Magic.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup>, philosophy,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-W-2)</sup> and religious beliefs.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup> One of its elective courses was Cyrodilic philosophy. Its upper-level course is described as rather esoteric in nature.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-W-2)</sup> Books published by the scholars of the academy were widely available during the [Second](Lore_Second_Era.md), [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md), and [Fourth Eras](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VOF-3)</sup> One of the titles of the College was Praeceptor,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> a title shared with the [Imperial Battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) of [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KA-4)</sup>

## Notes
- An institution known as the first Imperial College of [Battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) was founded at some point between [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243) and [1E 498](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_498).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-XL-5)</sup> It is unknown if those colleges were connected.
- [Imperial College of the Voice](Lore_Imperial_College_of_the_Voice.md) was founded by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) in [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGTTE-6)</sup> It is unknown if those colleges were connected.
