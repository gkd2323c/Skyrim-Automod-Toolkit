Ask the many-eyed forest giant, the Ilyadi, how bucolic life became after sharing the land with the early Aldmer and you'll get no answer, for they are as extinct as the [Gheatus](Lore_Gheatus.md) or [Welwa](Lore_Welwa.md).*
—An excerpt from *[The Improved Emperor's Guide to Tamriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Improved_Emperor%27s_Guide_to_Tamriel_Summerset_Isles)* Ilyadi (or **Illyadi**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TF-1)</sup> are a type of [fay](Lore_Faerie.md) that once inhabited the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup> Legends describe them as many-eyed forest giants taller than the trees, with eyes that cover their heads.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup> It is unclear if they have any relation to other tree-like spirits.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

They were once far more common, and attacked the early [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), but were fought off by [Torinaan](Lore_Torinaan.md)'s use of ancestral lore and [meteoric glass](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md) to channel aetherial energy in the [form](Lore_Destruction.md) of [lightning](Lore_Shock.md), ice, and fire to kill them,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLo F-4)</sup> and they were exterminated in a few short years.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset-3)</sup> They are now supposedly extinct,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset-3)</sup> though some have sought out ways to bring them back from extinction.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup>
