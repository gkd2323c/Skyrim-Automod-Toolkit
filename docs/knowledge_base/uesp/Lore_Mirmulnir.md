Mirmulnir is a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Allegiance Strong Hunt" (or more accurately "Loyal Mortal Hunter")<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

Mirmulnir was one of many dragons to survive the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), and one of the few who successfully avoided persecution by the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md). By [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373), he was still purportedly alive, having last been sighted by the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) in the [Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) in [2E 212](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_212).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup>

It is unknown if Mirmulnir was slain before the return of his master [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). Upon his return, Alduin began the process of resurrecting the many dead dragons buried throughout Skyrim in ancient times by the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md). Mirmulnir was one of the first dragons to make a reappearance, when he attacked a watchtower west of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), killing several guardsmen. The Jarl of Whiterun, [Balgruuf the Greater](Lore_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), sent a group of soldiers to deal with the dragon. Among them was the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), whose Dragonblood was revealed when Mirmulnir was slain and his soul absorbed. This event was noticed by the [Greybeards](Lore_Greybeards.md), and the Dragonborn was summoned to [High Hrothgar](Lore_High_Hrothgar.md). Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Mirmulnir was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRDragon Rising-3)</sup>
