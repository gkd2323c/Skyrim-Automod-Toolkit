[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/SR-npc-Aicantar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aicantar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aicantar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aicantar.jpg) Aicantar **Aicantar** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Conjurer), and is the nephew of [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md).

Like his uncle, Aicantar is heavily involved in [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) research, where he often spends time by his uncle's side or, in some cases, in Calcelmo's [Dwemer Museum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum#Dwemer_Museum). However, he does not share all of his uncle's zeal for research.

He wears [blue mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). He carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum_Key) to the [Dwemer Museum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Nchuand-Zel) to [Nchuand-Zel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nchuand-Zel), and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

He normally remains in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) with his uncle.

During the quest [Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers), Aicantar will be present at Calcelmo's Laboratory, where unlike the guards, he will not be hostile, but evidently distressed by your breaking in. However, once you reach the Wizard's Tower, he will arrive with [Captain Aquilius](Skyrim_Captain_Aquilius.md) and two Wizard's Guards after you have duplicated the writing on Calcelmo's Stone, having alerted them to the presence of an intruder. All four will be hostile to you on sight, but you may choose not to engage them and run outside, as they will not follow. He will remain in the tower until [the next quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pursuit) in the Thieves Guild questline begins. Provided he is still alive following Hard Answers, he will return to his normal place at Calcelmo's side and will not remember your presence in the laboratory.

## Quest-Related Events

### Hard Answers
When you enter Calcelmo's Tower you can overhear him talking to the guards:

**Wizard's Guard**: *"Sir, are you serious?"* **Aicantar**: *"You heard me, captain- our work must not be disturbed. Kill any intruders on sight. Call for backup if you need it. But let no one through."* **Wizard's Guard**: *"...As you say, sir."* If Aicantar is alive when you make a rubbing of Calcelmo's Stone, he will burst into the room with his guards and [Captain Aquilius](Skyrim_Captain_Aquilius.md):

**Aicantar**: *"Accidents like this just don't happen. Someone is trying to sabotage my uncle's research."* **Aquilius**: *"I...all right. If there is a thief, he won't leave this tower alive. But shouldn't we inform Master Calcelmo?"* **Aicantar**: *"I'll deal with my uncle. Just...go! Scour this place from top to bottom!"* **Aquilius**: *"All right men, spread out and search the tower. No telling who - or what - we're dealing with here."*
## Dialogue

*"Goodbye. Be careful if you head into the ruins."* *"Back to my studies, then."*

## Notes
- After the Hard Answers quest is finished, he may sometimes not rejoin Calcelmo in Understone Keep, instead remaining in the Wizard's Tower for the rest of the game.<sup>[*verification needed — what causes this to happen? (see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:talk_Aicantar#Conflicting_information))*]</sup>
- Aicantar is identified as the backup Court Wizard, in case Calcelmo dies. However, since Calcelmo is essential and doesn't have a scripted death, Aicantar never provides merchant services during normal gameplay. - He has set of dialogue that comes with his new position. He would instead greet you with: *"Oh, excuse me. With my uncle gone, things are a bit of a mess."* He would offer a look at his wares with: *"Did you need something? A staff? Some enchanting materials?"* or *"I'm not half the wizard my uncle was, but I still have a few spells worth buying."*
- If you [reanimate](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) Aicantar then lose him, he may reappear from thin air and drop dead after [Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers) is complete.
- Adopted male children who are living in Markarth may comment *"Aicantar let me see his museum. It's really nice."*
- The author of *[Before the Ages of Man](Skyrim_Before_the_Ages_of_Man.md)* is named Aicantar of Shimerene. However, they are likely not the same person, as Aicantar of Shimmerene was the "Sapiarch of Indoctrination" and authored propaganda for the First Aldmeri Dominion in the mid-Second Era.
