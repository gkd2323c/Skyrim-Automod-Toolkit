The **Traveling Merchant** can be encountered during radiant [side quests](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Vampire_Side_Quests) for the vampires if you ally with them when playing [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md). Their appearance as a traveling merchant is a disguise to cover their true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunter. They wear a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md). They carry a [Dawnguard War Axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md), an enchanted [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md), three [steel bolts](Skyrim_Steel_Bolt.md), and an enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md).

If you ask to see their stock, they'll reply *"My wares? I, uh... Oh so sorry, I'm all sold out."* Accusing them of being disguised will result in an offended *"I beg your pardon. I don't know what you're getting at, but whatever it is, it's not appreciated."*

His appearance as a traveling merchant is a disguise to cover his true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md). Would he have been available for Hide and Seek, his dialogue would have remained unchanged from the traveling merchant used in [The Hunt](Skyrim_The_Hunt.md).

## Bugs
- The [Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md) quest was intended to have three potential targets: a [bard](Skyrim_Traveling_Bard.md), merchant, or [pilgrim](Skyrim_Traveling_Pilgrim.md). However, a [Visiting Advisor](Skyrim_Visiting_Advisor.md) will be chosen instead of the merchant, meaning you will never encounter him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
