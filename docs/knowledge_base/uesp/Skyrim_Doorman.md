[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Doorman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Doorman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Doorman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Doorman.jpg) Doorman The **Doorman** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found inside [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md), a drug den for [Redwater skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma). He wears [studded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor), a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), a [scaled helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Helmet) and a pair of leveled [heavy gauntlets](Skyrim_Armor.md#Gauntlets_2). He also carries [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and a leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace).

## Dialogue
When first approached:

- *"Hold it. If you're going downstairs, you keep your weapons to yourself or we'll be getting better acquainted. Head on in and behave yourself."*

If you talk to him after that, he says:

- *"Go on downstairs. You're bothering me."*

## Notes
- If still alive, he will be renamed to "[Vampire's Thrall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall)" when you leave.
