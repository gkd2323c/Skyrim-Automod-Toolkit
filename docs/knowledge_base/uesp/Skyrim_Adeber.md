[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-npc-Adeber.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Adeber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adeber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adeber.jpg) Adeber **Adeber** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works at [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine). He isn't really a talker because he is too busy digging out ore.

Adeber will spend all of his time working inside the mine, never taking a break to eat, sleep, or go outside. He wears a set of [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#00080699). He carries around a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks) to [Left Hand Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Miner%27s_Barracks.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
