You may meet any of the Five. All are true and right and death-in-the-woods. All are worthy of reverence.*
—Uraccanach the Witchman<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOLH-1)</sup> Uraccanach **the Witchman** was a revered [shaman](Lore_Reachfolk_Shamans.md) among the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). He fervently advocated for the recognition and adherence to the five distinct aspects of Hircine: Alrabeg the Hunter, Storihbeg the Manbeast, Uricanbeg the Great Stag, Gulibeg the Quick Fox, and Hrokkibeg the Mighty Bear, and was known to cooperate with [Academy of Chorrol](Lore_Academy_of_Chorrol.md) to some degree, leading to the transcription and publication of some of his statements and texts by a scholar affiliated with the institution.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOLH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-C31-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C65-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/ON-crown_store-Skinchanger_Style.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Skinchanger_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Skinchanger_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Skinchanger_Style.jpg) Skinchanger Armaments [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/ON-crown_store-Huntsman_Style.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Huntsman_Style.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Huntsman_Style.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Huntsman_Style.jpg) Huntsman Armaments Uraccanach's lineage was deeply rooted in the shamanic traditions, boasting a heritage that he asserted spanned an impressive five generations of Witchmen.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C65-3)</sup> Little is known of his past, but Uraccanach resided in the Druadach Mountains within the Reach. Over time, he emerged as a renowned shaman, revered for his extensive understanding of Hircine's Five Aspects and the appropriate accoutrements for those seeking to pay them homage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOLH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-C31-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C65-3)</sup> His teachings resonated with his people's interpretation of Hircine, and did not differ greatly from those shared by shamans of the [Shrine of the Hunt-Father](Lore_Shrine_of_the_Hunt-Father.md) led by High Shaman [Glynroch](Lore_Glynroch.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-HSG-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Events-5)</sup>

Uraccanach authored texts that meticulously detailed the proper attire and artifacts that paid homage to these Five Aspects. His writings encompassed both those that emulated Hircine's Daedric servants, the [Huntsmen](Lore_Huntsman.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C65-3)</sup> and those tied to the Skinchanger and the festive celebrations of the [New Life Festival](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-C31-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-NLFI-7)</sup>

At some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Uraccanach encountered [Juno Procillus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Juno_Procillus), a scholar affiliated with the Academy of Chorrol. During this encounter, Uraccanach's insights about the Five Aspects were documented and transcribed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOLH-1)</sup> Additionally, it is known that Juno Procillus published another text attributed to Uraccanach,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C65-3)</sup> although one of the other texts was solely authored by the shaman himself, and not attributed to the Academy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-C31-2)</sup>

Uraccanach was known to comment on the [Huntsman's Warmask](Lore_Huntsman%27s_Warmask.md), a powerful relic associated with Hircine tied to both his own Reachfolk culture and that of the [Ket Keptu](Lore_Ket_Keptu.md). He spoke of the various aspects Hircine could reveal to those who wield the mask, mentioning forms such as the flank of the [unicorn](Lore_Unicorn.md), the skull of the [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md), bloody claws, bloody hooves, or even the paw print of the [fox](Lore_Fox.md). According to Uraccanach, whichever visage appeared, the wearer would be blessed in witnessing it.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-HW-8)</sup>
