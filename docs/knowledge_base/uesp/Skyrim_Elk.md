# Deer

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-creature-Deer_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Deer_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Deer_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Deer_02.jpg) A deer **Deer** are non-aggressive animals found throughout [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md). Quite shy by nature, they will flee when approached or threatened, but they can become hostile and attack while under the influence of a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md). They are hunted for their [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), [hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md), and [large](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Large_Antlers) or [small](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_Antlers) antlers. There are two different species of deer in Skyrim, though the differences between the two are largely aesthetic. "Deer" resemble caribou or reindeer. "Elk" resemble the extinct *Megaloceros*. Occasionally, either species can be heard bugling for mates.

When hunting a deer, it is advisable to try to kill them in one hit, as they move swiftly and have a tendency to change direction when fleeing. Alternatively, you can poison an arrow with a [slow](Skyrim_Slow.md) effect, to allow for an easier second or third shot. You may also cast a [Fury](Skyrim_Fury.md) spell on them to prevent them from fleeing. [Sneaking](Skyrim_Sneak.md) can be problematic, since the detection eye does not indicate whether deer detect you.

Smiths should note that unlike deer *hides*, the deer *pelts* occasionally found in hunter's camps cannot be tanned into [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md).

In the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md), you are tasked with hunting the unique [white stag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Stag). It is considerably larger than a standard deer, and its fur is much lighter in color.

Vale deer<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> are exclusive to [Darkfall Passage](Skyrim_Darkfall_Passage.md) and the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), and the light patches on their pelts glow in darkened environments.

- Variations: Elk, Vale Deer<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>
- Unique deer: [White Stag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Stag)

## Notes
- While present in all areas of Skyrim, some locations and places have a higher volume of deer and elk. These places include: the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) (specifically around [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md) is a herd of 6), the valley near [Silverdrift Lair](Skyrim_Silverdrift_Lair.md), and the forests of [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), particularly around [Lake Honrich](Skyrim_Lake_Honrich.md) and [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md). Conversely, [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) has very few deer spawning locations; this hold is more populated by wild [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md).
- In addition to the trophy deer in the Trophy Room, most of the rest of the rooms of the house offer options for either adding antler mounts or deer heads (of either types) to the walls. The entryway has the option to add two elk heads, for example.
