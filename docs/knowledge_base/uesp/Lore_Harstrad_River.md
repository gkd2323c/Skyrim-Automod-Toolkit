[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg/200px-SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) The Headwaters of Harstrad circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The Harstrad River is a river in northern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) which empties into the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) along the northeastern coast of the island.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>

Before the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> the Harstrad river formed the eastern boundary of the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md) on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). It sprung up near the center of the island and flowed north, merging with the [Isild River](Lore_Isild_River.md) in the [Isinfier Plains](Lore_Isinfier_Plains.md) and then out into the Sea of Ghosts. The Wind Stone was located on its western shore, the Tree Stone was near its headwaters, and its banks were dotted with barrows and [snow wolves](Lore_Snow_Wolf.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup>

The cataclysms of the Red Year substantially changed the landscape of Solstheim.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> The delta of the river has moved a little northeast, and the Wind Stone is now located on its eastern shore. The body of the river was substantially truncated, as the headwaters, now inhabited by [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md), emerge further north on the island. When the fungal tower of [Tel Mithryn](Lore_Tel_Mithryn.md) began suffering a serious malady in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), [taproot](Lore_Taproot.md) soaked in the headwaters of the river was used to heal the living building.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>
