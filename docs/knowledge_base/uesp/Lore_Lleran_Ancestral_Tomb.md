# Lleran

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/MW-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-MW-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Lleran Ancestral Tomb [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/ON-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lleran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Lleran Ancestral Tomb The Lleran clan is an ancient family of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) that lives in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They are one of the thirty revered families of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATOTTRF-1)</sup> and are a sub-faction of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLleran-2)</sup> The **Lleran Ancestral Tomb** is located in the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md) region, east of Zainsipilu.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLleran-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWLleran-3)</sup>

## History
With the other twenty-nine ancient families of Vvardenfell, the Lleran clan hid the [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md), the secret repository of the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) and placed a clue in their ancestral tomb.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBradyn-4)</sup> Its location remained a secret for many years until Bradyn of the Library of Vivec enlisted an [outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to find the tombs and uncover it in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They were able to find all thirty tombs and find the lost Library of Andule.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAncestral-5)</sup> In the same year Benae Lleran was a laborer in [Velyn Harbor](Lore_Velyn_Harbor.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BL-6)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Daynillo Lleran was a monk of the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md) active in [Holamayan](Lore_Holamayan.md) monastery.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DL-7)</sup> Goron Lleran was a novice of [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) and a pilgrim at [Molag Mar](Lore_Molag_Mar.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GL-8)</sup> Salver Lleran was a Spellwright of House Telvanni who provided [spellmaking](Lore_Spellmaking.md) services in [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SL-9)</sup> He was known client of the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) Audenian Valius.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-CL-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-AV-11)</sup> Gragus Lleran was a [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) assassin who was sent to kill [Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) Councilor [Athyn Sarethi](Lore_Athyn_Sarethi.md), but was foiled by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-GRL-12)</sup> As of then the Lleran Ancestral Tomb was guarded by the [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWLleran-3)</sup>
