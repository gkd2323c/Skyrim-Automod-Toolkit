[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/OB-place-Trumbe.jpg/200px-OB-place-Trumbe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Trumbe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Trumbe.jpg) Trumbe circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/ON-place-Trumbe.jpg/200px-ON-place-Trumbe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Trumbe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Trumbe.jpg) Trumbe circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Trumbe (translated as *"[of] protection"*) is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruin located in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), in the rugged highlands northwest of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md). It was abandoned by its Ayleid occupants after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) and fell into ruin.

During [Varen's Rebellion](Lore_Varen%27s_Rebellion.md) of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), Trumbe was cut off from the rest of the Gold Coast by [Varen's Wall](Lore_Varen%27s_Wall_(place).md), a situation which persisted for many years thereafter.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>. During the return of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md), a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) incursion from the plane of [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md) occurred near Trumbe.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOGold Road-2)</sup>

On Fredas, the 12th day of [Rain's Hand](Lore_Rain%27s_Hand.md) [3E 431](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_431), a group of adventurers led by Grommok gro-Barak entered the ruins of Trumbe in search of treasure. The ruins were crawling with undead at this time and the adventurers fled, although they managed to drag a locked metal container back to their camp nearby. After eventually managing to open the container, the adventurers discovered a trove of [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) treasure, including the Talisman of Abetment and the enchanted swords [Dawnfang and Duskfang](Lore_Dawnfang_and_Duskfang.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SI-4)</sup> The ruins were still plagued by undead circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
