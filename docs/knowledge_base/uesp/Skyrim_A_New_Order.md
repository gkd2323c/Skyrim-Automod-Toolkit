## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran).
2. Find [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar) and help him defeat the [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md).
3. Find [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) and get her to come to [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md).
4. Return to Fort Dawnguard and listen to Isran's speech.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Reinforcements
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-quest-Bloodline_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bloodline_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bloodline_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bloodline_03.jpg) The attack on [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) When you have declined [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)'s offer at [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md), you are told to return to [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran) at [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) and provide information about [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md). Upon arriving in the courtyard, you will find Isran, [Celann](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Celann), [Durak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Durak), and newcomer [Vori](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vori) doing their best to fight a group of attacking [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). Help them defeat the vampires, then report back to Isran who will be waiting at the main gate, showing great anger that his hidden fortress was exposed so soon. When you tell him that the vampires were looking for a woman trapped inside [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md), he will say: *"A woman? Trapped in there? That doesn't make any sense. Who is she? More importantly, where is she?"* However, he will forget about the woman once you tell him the vampires claimed an Elder Scroll: *"By the Divines, this couldn't get much worse. This is more than you and I can handle."* He will then realize it is time to bring in some powerful reinforcements to Fort Dawnguard: *"If they're bold enough to attack us here, then this may be bigger than I thought. I have good men here, but... There are people I've met and worked with over the years. We need their skills, their talents, if we're going to survive this. If you can find them, we might have a chance."* He will then tell you about the two persons, namely [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) and [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar): *"I think we'll want Sorine Jurard. Breton girl, whip-smart and good with tinkering. Fascination with the Dwemer. Weapons in particular. Last I knew, she was out in the Reach, convinced she was about to find the biggest dwarven ruins yet. You'll also want to find Gunmar. Big brute of a Nord, hates vampires almost as much as I do. Got it into his head years back that his experience with animals would help. Trolls in particular, from what I hear."* He will then send you on your way, and you will have to convince Sorine and Gunmar to join the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md), and use their special skills to help out against the vampire menace.

### Gunmar
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-npc-Gunmar_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gunmar_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunmar_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunmar_02.jpg) [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar) fighting the bear Gunmar will show up randomly in the wilderness in Skyrim or, if you have installed [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md), on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), or somewhere else with [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) content (see [notes](#Notes)). Follow your map marker to his location, and you will find the huge [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) in [sneaking](Skyrim_Sneak.md) position, seemingly on the hunt for game, warning you: *"Mind yourself. There's a vicious bear on the loose."* He then explains that he has chased a bear for two weeks, and that he finally managed to corner it. Keep in mind, if there is not a map marker in mainland Skyrim, check Solstheim. When you tell him that Isran needs his help, he will forget the bear for a while: *"Isran? Needing someone else's help? Never thought I'd hear that. I'm afraid he's a few years too late. I've moved on. I have more important business to attend to. Besides, he can handle anything alone! He assured me so himself. What could he possibly need my help with?"* When you tell him about the vampire threat and the Elder Scroll, he will reconsider, although he wants you to help him track down the bear and kill it. He will then lead you to a cave near his initial starting point:

**Possible locations**: [Bonechill Passage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonechill_Passage) • [Bronze Water Cave](Skyrim_Bronze_Water_Cave.md) • [Brood Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brood_Cavern) • [Clearspring Tarn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clearspring_Tarn) • [Cronvangr Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Cave) • [Crystaldrift Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crystaldrift_Cave) • [Fallowstone Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fallowstone_Cave) • [Honeystrand Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honeystrand_Cave) • [Horker Island](Skyrim_Horker_Island.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> • [Pinepeak Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pinepeak_Cavern) • [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

There is always one specific bear you will have to kill, although you will often get in battle with several creatures at once. When the bear is dead, a journal update will tell you to speak with Gunmar, who will thank you for the assistance, and agree to help against the vampires: *"You've helped me, so I suppose the least I can do is find out what Isran wants. He's still at that fort near Stendarr's Beacon, I assume?"* He will then be on his way to Fort Dawnguard.

### Sorine Jurard
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-npc-Sorine_Jurard_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sorine_Jurard_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sorine_Jurard_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sorine_Jurard_02.jpg) [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) Sorine Jurard will turn up in the wilderness near an unmarked [Dwemer convector](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Convector), just south of [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). She will be drifting around, obviously looking for something, mumbling: *"What would mudcrabs want with my satchel, anyway?"* and *"Just one gyro. One, and I can get back to work. Where are they?"* When you talk to her, she will ask if you have seen a sack full of [Dwemer gyros](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Gyro) and will proceed to tell you that a [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) may have taken it. When you tell her that Isran needs her help, she will respond: *"Isran? Wants me? No, you must be mistaken. He made it exceedingly clear the last time we spoke that he had no interest in my help. I find it hard to believe he's changed his mind. He said some very hurtful things to me before I left."* When told about the vampire threat and the Elder Scroll, she will change her mind, although she will not do anything before she gets a Dwemer gyro. You can now choose between two options, although the second option will differ if you already have a Dwemer gyro in your inventory. If you do, you can hand it to her and she will be on her way to Fort Dawnguard. You can also choose to persuade her, although you need a [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill of 50+ to be successful. If everything else fails, you can look around for her satchel. To find it, starting at Sorine, first face southeast. In this direction, you can see a tree near the water. Travel to the tree. After reaching it, face west. From here, you can see the satchel on the ground a few feet away. When you return to her with one of the gyros she will finally be convinced and walk all the way to Fort Dawnguard for an encounter with Isran.

### Isran's Speech
When you return to Fort Dawnguard, you will see that Isran has tightened security and made several changes to the exterior, including a gate and more wooden barricades. Enter the fort and you will find Gunmar and Sorine in the main hall, with Isran looking down at them from the balcony above. Before long, Isran will seal off the chamber, trapping the two new recruits, and shower them both with concentrated sunlight as a test. The following conversation will take place:

**Gunmar**: *"All right Isran, you've got us all here. Now what do you want?"*
**Isran**: *"Hold it right there."*
**Sorine**: *"What are you doing?"*
**Isran**: *"Making sure you're not vampires. Can't be too careful. So, welcome to Fort Dawnguard. I'm sure you've heard a bit of what we're up against. Powerful vampires, unlike anything we've seen before. And they have an Elder Scroll. If anyone is going to stand in their way, it's going to be us."*
**Sorine**: *"This is all well and good, but do we actually know anything about what they're doing? What do we do now?"*
**Isran**: *"We'll get to that. For now, get acquainted with the space. Sorine, you'll find room to start your tinkering on that crossbow design you've been working on. Gunmar, there's an area large enough for you to pen up some trolls, get them armored up and ready for use. In the meantime, we're going to get to the bottom of why a vampire showed up here looking for you. Let's go have a little chat with it, shall we?"*

Once Isran has removed the barrier, the quest will end and the next quest, [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md), will initiate. Gunmar and Sorine will then find their respective spots in the western area of Fort Dawnguard. Their presence will net you several benefits. Sorine is a master trainer in [Marksman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Marksman), and will also sell and buy items. She will also hand you several [quests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard#Related_Quests), while Gunmar is a master trainer in [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md), will sell and buy weapons and armor, and will sell you an [armored troll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armored_Troll) to aid you in battle. The next time you visit Fort Dawnguard, Sorine and Gunmar will change into their Dawnguard armor, a blacksmith's forge and arcane enchanter will be found near the smithing area, and armored trolls can be found in the pen across from the smelter. Exiting to [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md) (including all 3 balconies) and re-entering from there will suffice.

## Notes
- As they are not [protected](Skyrim_Protected_NPCs.md), both Durak and Celann may be slain in the vampire attack at the beginning of the quest.
- If there is no quest marker for Gunmar and *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* is installed, the radiant location might be in [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). If so, traveling to Solstheim will result in the marker appearing normally at that location if the quest is active. The same can happen if you install [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) content, depending upon what it is.
- Sorine makes a comment that she saw a mudcrab the other day while discussing where her gyros have gone. This is likely a subtle reference to the previous installment, *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, where "I saw a mudcrab the other day" was a sentence commonly uttered by many NPCs.
- If you are a vampire, Isran will stop his speech and ask you to get yourself cured before anything else happens. This means that Sorine and Gunmar will wait in the main hall until you have completed [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md).
- During the quest, you can freely roam inside [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) without danger to yourself. You can ask [Feran Sadri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Feran_Sadri), [Hestla](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hestla), and [Ronthil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ronthil) what they have for sale, and you can ask Ronthil and [Fura Bloodmouth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth) for training. The portcullis will be down though, and you'll have to [clip](Skyrim_Glitches.md#Clipping) through it or the diagonal walls around it to gain access. In addition, everything inside will be owned, and will need to be stolen. To add insult to injury, everyone inside will be hostile once you finish the quest.
- You may [randomly encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) a [vampire assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Assassin) at any point after completing this quest.

## Bugs
- The quest may fail to start after refusing Harkon's gift and being returned to the landing.<sup>[*verification needed — needs more details/further testing to determine the reason*]</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can fix this by entering either `setstage DLC1Hunter Base Intro 20` or `setstage DLC1Hunter Base Intro 30`, followed by `setobjectivedisplayed DLC1Hunter Base Intro 20 1` or `30 1` into the console.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If the above does not work, it may be because you encountered a living Vigilant Tolan in Dimhollow Crypt, and he died. He needs to be alive before the above commands will work. To fix this, you can resurrect him. Open the console and enter `prid 02003532` to select him, then enter `resurrect`. Close the console and wait 5 seconds. Then reopen the console and attempt the above fix.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If neither the above work, then *playerfollowercount'* s value may be the issue. To fix this load a save from BEFORE you open Serana's sarcophagus. Dismiss any current followers, using `set playerfollowercount to 0` if necessary (may as well to be safe) then proceed through the quest as normal.
- After rejecting Harkon's "gift" in Bloodline, there may be no indication that you completed that quest, which can lead to confusion upon finishing this one. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If you clear the bear den before talking to Gunmar, he will just stand there telling you to slay the (dead) bear. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.1.1, fixes this bug.
- The bear that Gunmar wants you to help him eliminate may be missing due to being deleted by the game's cleanup script. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.1.3, fixes this bug.
- Upon entering the fort, Gunmar and/or Sorine may end up behind the instantly-closing gate, making it impossible to continue the quest. - Exiting and reentering the fort or using the wait function will sometimes fix this and trigger the quest completion.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console, click on each NPC and type `moveto player` one at a time to bring them to your side of the gate.
- Upon completing the quest, the gate may not reopen. <sup>**?**</sup> - Leave Fort Dawnguard, and immediately re-enter. The gate should now be open.
