[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Armor.jpg) Guild Master's Armor The Guild Master's Armor is the chest piece of the unique set of equipment given to the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), and is the quest reward for completing [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md). Alongside this item, this set of equipment includes [boots](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Gloves.md), and a [hood](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Hood.md). Its appearance is similar to that of the [Thieves Guild Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Armor) for the regular members, except for its darker color and additional badges to distinguish the rank associated with the armor. As it is essentially an upgrade to the regular set of guild armor, this armor has a superior armor rating and gold value to the basic set, although it weighs more. Like its similar appearance, this armor also has a correlative, yet more powerful, enchantment to the more common version of this armor. The armor [fortifies carry weight](Skyrim_Fortify_Carry_Weight.md) by 50 points, versus 20 points for normal Thieves Guild Armor and 35 points for the upgraded version. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this item requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, and also requires that the related quest be completed.

## Related Quests
- **[Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md)**: Become the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Notes
- There is another version of this armor used by [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md) which has a better armor rating, but lacks the enchantment that this copy has on it. However, his version cannot be acquired under normal circumstances, as it is not marked as a playable item.
- Due to the quest requirement, this item cannot be tempered if added through the [console](Skyrim_Console.md).
