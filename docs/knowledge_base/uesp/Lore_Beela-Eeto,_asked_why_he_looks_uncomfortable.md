Beela-Eeto, asked why he looks uncomfortable **by [Beela-Eeto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Beela-Eeto)** *An Argonian's thoughts on wearing Dunmer style armor*
---
[![ON-concept-Dunmer medium armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/8/88/ON-concept-Dunmer_medium_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Dunmer_medium_armor.png)
- "It itches. It is too tight, as if a serpent clutches me in its coils! I thought it would be a sign of good will, having this armor crafted for me in the [Dark Elven](Lore_Dunmer.md) style, but I can't move.

- The space for my tail is too small, and these leather plates! How will I dodge and weave in battle? I do not understand how they bear this constriction!

- Perhaps there is a better way to show my good intent and allegiance to [the Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md)."
