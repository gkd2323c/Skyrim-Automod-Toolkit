# Shield of Ysgramor

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shield_of_Ysgramor.jpg) The Shield of Ysgramor (Skyrim) The Shield of Ysgramor was buried within the tomb of that famous hero of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) in the Late [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). It's unknown if [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) ever wielded this shield in battle. The wielder of the shield is both harder to kill and resistant to [magic](Lore_Magic.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOY-1)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), it was found by members of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) within the tomb.

## Notes
- This artifact is identical in appearance to the artifact [Pandemonium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Pandemonium) from *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)*.
