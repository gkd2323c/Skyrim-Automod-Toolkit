Regarding Your Loss **by Reginn Limilus**
---
Lady [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md),

I've made inquiries into the loss of your mead shipment to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), and I've come to the conclusion that they were waylaid by a group of mercenaries under the employ of the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md). According to my sources, these mercenaries were not acting under orders and simply attacked the caravan for supplies. I've forwarded a request for compensation on your behalf to the [Imperial Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) and the mercenaries in question have already been dealt with accordingly. Again, I wish to extend my deepest apologies for this unfortunate mishap and I assure you that any future shipments into our nation will be met at the border by an [Imperial patrol](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and personally escorted to its final destination.

Reginn Limilus
Grand Steward, [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) Trade Relations
