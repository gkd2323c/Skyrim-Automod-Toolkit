[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bonestrewn_Crest.jpg) Bonestrewn Crest **Bonestrewn Crest** is a mountaintop [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) south of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). Its [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) teaches part of the [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md) [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Bounty: Dragon](Skyrim_Bounty__Dragon.md)**: Kill a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) in its [lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Dragon Seekers](Skyrim_Dragon_Seekers.md)**: Go to a [dragon lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md) with [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) or [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) and kill the dragon. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
The lair is riddled with mammoth bones, hence the name "Bonestrewn". It has a blueish mist, and a number of [dragon's tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue) plants grow here. It can be accessed via a slope on the south side. The word wall is found at the top of the hill.

Before the [dragons' return](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), the word wall will be guarded by three [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md).

After the dragons' return, the word wall will be guarded by a leveled [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md).

Regardless of whether [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) has been completed, the boss chest is north of the word wall.

## Notes
- There are two [iron ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) here; one is on the right just before reaching the top of the slope, and the other is to the right just after reaching the top.
- One [orichalcum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein) can be found 140 ft west-southwest, at the bottom of the mountain.
- The dragon that spawns here does not rest on the word wall or the ground before it; instead, it constantly circles the mountain until it detects you or a follower, whereupon it will initiate combat.
- Due to the location's proximity to [Steamcrag Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamcrag_Camp) and another [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) herding two [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) in the tundra below, the dragon might choose to fight them during its battle with you.
- Bonestrewn Crest also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bonestrewn_Crest) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
