[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-npc-Hjalfar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hjalfar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjalfar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjalfar.jpg) Hjalfar **Hjalfar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) sailor found at the [Water Stone](Skyrim_Water_Stone.md) in Solstheim.

Initially from [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), Hjalfar, along with fellow sailors [Liesl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Liesl), [Benkum](Skyrim_Benkum.md), and [Palevius Lex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palevius_Lex), decided to cast anchor along the western coast of Solstheim and immediately fell prey to [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s spell. He works around the clock at the stone, oblivious to the world around him. Once the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), the entire crew will return to their boat on the coast. When you leave the area, they will all disappear from the game.

He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d) and a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#0004223b), and he carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md).

## Dialogue
He shares his dialogue lines with his fellow sailors:

- *"Think we've got to get back to our ship now."*
- *"We're already late. Not sure why we decided to take such an extended shore time."*
- *"When the tide goes, we'll go with it."*
- *"It's a long way back to Windhelm from here."*
- *"Hope our shipments are still good. How long has it been?"*
- *"Best be shoving off now."*
