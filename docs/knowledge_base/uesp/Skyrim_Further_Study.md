## Quick Walkthrough
1. Ask Swims-In-Deep-Water for work.
2. Read the bounty letter.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speak to Swims-In-Deep Water for work:

- **I'm looking for work.**
- *"I understand. It's all in this note."*

He will then hand you a bounty for a randomly selected fish, this fish can be one of the four below:

- [Pygmy Sunfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pygmy_Sunfish)
- [Angler Larvae](Skyrim_Angler_Larvae.md)
- [Pearlfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearlfish)
- [Spadefish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish)

The alchemists who can request these fish are:

- [Angeline Morrard](Skyrim_Angeline_Morrard.md)
- [Arcadia](Skyrim_Arcadia.md)
- [Bothela](Skyrim_Bothela.md)
- [Edla](Skyrim_Edla.md)
- [Elgrim](Skyrim_Elgrim.md)
- [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Frida](Skyrim_Frida.md)
- [Hafjorg](Skyrim_Hafjorg.md)
- [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md)
- [Nurelion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nurelion)
- [Quintus Navale](Skyrim_Quintus_Navale.md)
- [Vivienne Onis](Skyrim_Vivienne_Onis.md)
- [Zaria](Skyrim_Zaria.md)

## Notes
- With version 4.3.7 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), this quest will no longer pick an alchemist who isn't actually assigned to the Job Apothecary Faction since the quest is intended to use someone who is currently performing that job function rather than being a backup for one.
- Although [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md) and [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> are also assigned to the Job Apothecary Faction as well, they don't request fish for this quest.

## Bugs
- Zaria isn't part of the Job Apothecary Faction. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
