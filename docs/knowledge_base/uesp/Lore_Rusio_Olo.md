Rusio Olo was the third pillar of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md)'s original complement of warrior-priests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SORO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OOTH-2)</sup> During his tenure, he was instrumental in ending the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md)'s infestation of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) in [2E 437](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_437). He was said to have dispatched eight cultists and their leader singlehandedly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SORO-1)</sup>

A statue to Rusio was later made in Knightsgrave to his memory as one of the Order's founding members.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KLL-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Rusia shares his last name with [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md), the king of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) from the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-4)</sup>
