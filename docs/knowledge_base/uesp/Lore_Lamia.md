*The time has come to demonstrate the true power of my kind. We are not simply monsters who eat children! We are so much more.*
—Tzik'nith<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tzik.27nith-1)</sup> Lamias (or **Lamiae**) are a species of amphibious [reptilian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reptile) [beastfolk](Lore_Beastfolk.md). They have a serpentine appearance, with the torso of a woman and the tail of a [snake](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Snake). Despite appearing to be exclusively female, it is assumed they are hermaphroditic or sexless. They repoduce by laying eggs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGT-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KC:AHC-3)</sup> Lamias are larger than most other mortal [races](Lore_Races.md), and according to one unconfirmed fable, are capable of interbreeding with them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> They appear to be cold-blooded and are known to lounge in the sun.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAMurk2-5)</sup>

Found in various parts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), they can live in water or on land, although they rarely venture far from the water, and are known to take up residence in abandoned ruins.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOLamia-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAMurk2-5)</sup> The smaller, more aquatic Lamia of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) are considered a distinct variety from the larger scaley "desert Lamias" found in other parts of Tamriel such as [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KC:AHC-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGT-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOLamia-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFLamia-7)</sup> They are damaged by the magical protections that safeguard temples and other holy places,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFLamia-7)</sup> although this does not appear to be the case for [Meridia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Meridia)'s Chapel of Light in the [Hollow City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hollow_City).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## History
Whispers claim that Lamias are descended from the spawn of [Lamae Bal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lamae_Bal), a [Nede](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede) who was violated by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KC:AHC-3)</sup>

Lamias have had little impact on history due to their primitive nature and hostile attitude towards the people of Tamriel. Notably, the Lamias of [Tempest Island](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tempest_Island) took part in the Lamia Invasion of [2E 435](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_435),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MCV2-9)</sup> and a group of Lamias are thought to have fought against [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) during the Tamrielic invasion of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tzik.27nith-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Circa 2E 582, [Malazar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Malazar), the [Celestial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Celestial) [Serpent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Serpent), recruited Lamias to serve as footsoldiers in his army. They fought alongside the [Scaled Court](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scaled_Court) when the [Undaunted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Undaunted) assaulted the Serpent's stronghold of Sanctum Ophidia.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Behavior and Diet
Despite being reviled as monsters, Lamias are an intelligent species; they can speak [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md), can cast powerful magic, and adorn themselves with jewelry.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOLamia-6)</sup> They are also fluent in the [Nymph](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nymph) language, and are known to make peace with those that can speak it.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-DFNymph-12)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFLamia-7)</sup> They are incredibly protective of their eggs,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> though it has been claimed they later abandon them and leave the immature Lamias to fend for themselves.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGT-2)</sup>

They seemingly have their own religion, venerating entities known as the [Egg Mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Egg_Mother) and the [Great Egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Great_Egg).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tzik.27nith-1)</sup> Lamias choose not to erect permanent structures or cities of their own, instead preferring to occupy the ruins left behind by man and mer.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAMurk2-5)</sup> Groups of Lamias are often led by a queen.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Lamias have been known to group together in the wilderness outside cities during the [Witches Festival](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Witches_Festival), alongside many other sinister magic users. The creatures they create or summon are said to be capable of plaguing Tamriel for eons.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-WFDF-16)</sup>

Lamias eat their prey whole, resulting in a distended stomach while they slowly digest. They have been known to prey on people,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAMurk2-5)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> often being accused of eating children.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ACTB-18)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tzik.27nith-1)</sup> They are often known to prey on the men of other races, luring them with their mesmerizing singing voices.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KC:AHC-3)</sup> They are also said to possess a hypnotic gaze called the *Lamia Mesmera*.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-AE-19)</sup> In combat, they rely on debilitating shrieks and powerful claws that drain an opponent's stamina. Curares can also summon spectral Lamias.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-SSL-20)</sup>

## Uses
The [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) have been known to harvest Lamia-scale to craft ritualistic shawls.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup>
