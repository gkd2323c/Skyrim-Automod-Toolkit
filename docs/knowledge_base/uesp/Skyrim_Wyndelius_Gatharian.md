[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-npc-Wyndelius_Gatharian.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wyndelius_Gatharian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wyndelius_Gatharian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wyndelius_Gatharian.jpg) Wyndelius pretending to be a ghost **Wyndelius Gatharian** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md) inhabiting [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md) in search of the [Sapphire Dragon Claw](Skyrim_Sapphire_Dragon_Claw.md) to gain access to [Shroud Hearth Depths](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Depths.md).

He appears spectral when encountered, which is later revealed to be due to a [potion](Skyrim_Philter_of_the_Phantom.md) he consumes. He is hostile to you and, upon killing him, one can read his [journal](Skyrim_Wyndelius%27s_Journal.md). The journal explains why he is pretending to be a ghost; to make sure the locals at [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) would not interrupt him in his search for the claw. Over time, his mind started to break down and by the time you find him, Wyndelius is of the belief that it is his destiny to guard the barrow.

## Related Quests

## Notes
- In the *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* [download](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads) in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion), there is a Dark Elf named [Syndelius Gatharian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Syndelius_Gatharian). Syndelius and Wyndelius share their race and have very similar first names, although it is unknown if they are meant to be related or if Wyndelius' name is just a reference.
- Wyndelius shares a surname with [Vanryth Gatharian](Skyrim_Vanryth_Gatharian.md), another Dark Elf that is a blacksmith in the Ragged Flagon after the player has improved the [Thieves Guild.](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild.md) It is unknown whether Vanryth and Wyndelius are related. Same goes for Vanryth being related to Syndelius.
- Wyndelius is immune to "bleeding damage" even though he isn't really a ghost.
- Wyndelius is never moved to his starting patrol marker in Shroud Hearth Barrow due to custom link keywords being assigned to his reference.
- Wyndelius reacts differently if raised as a [dead thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md). When revived, he will go to ghost form and will speak normally (using lines from the "Male Ghost" voicetype) without moaning or groaning. Additionally, when he dies, he will release a wave of energy, similar to that of [Rally](Skyrim_Rally_(spell).md) or [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(spell).md) (including the spells' sound effects), though it does nothing.
- If you pickpocket the potion, and do not take his journal or potion on the table, the potion on him will respawn every month. If he is kept as a dead thrall, the potion on him will respawn every month as well. <sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Wyndelius_Gatharian#Philter_of_the_Phantom)*]</sup>
