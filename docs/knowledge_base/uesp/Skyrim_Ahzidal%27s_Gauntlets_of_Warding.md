[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Gauntlets.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Gauntlets.png) Ahzidal's Gauntlets of Warding Ahzidal's Gauntlets of Warding are a pair of gauntlets that are part of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). They can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after the third phase of digging is completed during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). These gauntlets are identical in appearance to [ancient Nord gauntlets](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Gauntlets.md), but provide four more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and weigh two pounds more. Their enchantment decreases amount of damage blocked by your [wards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ward) by 25%, and causes your wards to [absorb](Skyrim_Absorb_Magicka.md) half the magicka of spells cast against you. In addition, if you wear the gauntlets along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points. Ahzidal's gauntlets can be tempered with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot), with the [Advanced Armors perk](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors_(perk).md) doubling improvement.
