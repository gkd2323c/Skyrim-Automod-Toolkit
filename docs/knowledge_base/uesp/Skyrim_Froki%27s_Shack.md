[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-place-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-place-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg) Froki's Shack **Froki's Shack** is a small [shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md).

The [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) hunter [Froki Whetted-Blade](Skyrim_Froki_Whetted-Blade.md) lives here with his grandson [Haming](Skyrim_Haming.md), a survivor of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md). He is a follower of the old ways and a devout worshipper of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md). Froki will guide you through the [Sacred Trials of Kyne](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md) if you allow him. It consists of one area, *[Froki's Shack](#Froki.27s_Shack)*.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
To the left of the entrance is a basket containing a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), two loose [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), and a quiver of the same, with a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) nearby. To the right of the door is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). In the cliff below the shack, there is an [orichalcum ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein) with a [diamond](Skyrim_Diamond.md) visible and collectible on the surface of the vein. There is a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on the ground beside the vein. A [purple mountain flower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) and a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md) grow near the [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block).

A [shrine of Talos](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Shrine_of_Talos) can be found outside the shack farther south in the mountains, surrounded by [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots), an [Imperial light shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Shield.md), a copy of the [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[King](Skyrim_King.md)*, a copy of *[The Amulet of Kings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Amulet_of_Kings)*, a satchel, five loose [gold coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), a large [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a [heavy Imperial helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Helmet.md), and an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md). There are two [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes on the way from the shack to the shrine.

### Froki's Shack
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Froki%27s_Shack.jpg) The shack's interior Upon entering, you will find a [steel battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe) to the left of the door. There is a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) on your left, with a large shelving unit next to it. The shelves hold a woodcutter's axe, an [eye of sabre cat](Skyrim_Eye_of_Sabre_Cat.md), a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill book *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*, a [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), and an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing). A small table and two chairs are opposite the door, with a [luna moth wing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth_Wing) and a [butterfly wing](Skyrim_Butterfly_Wing.md) on the table. Against the southeast wall is a single bed and an unlocked chest containing minor loot.

## Notes
- Even after you complete the trials and Froki says he counts you "as a friend," the items in the shack can't be taken without stealing.
- Though Haming lives here with his grandfather, there is only one small bed, and Froki sleeps in it.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the basket, leather strip, iron arrows, and woodcutter's axe just outside the shack as owned.

## Bugs
- On the wood chopping block outside to the north-northwest of the shack, you can find a respawning [Diadem of the Savant](Skyrim_Diadem_of_the_Savant.md). It is likely this was a mistake, as the Creation Kit specifies the diadem was meant to be a unique reward for completing [Shalidor's Maze](Skyrim_Shalidor%27s_Maze.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. It changes this instance of the diadem into a leveled circlet with a [Fortify Archery](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Archery) effect.
- The woodcutter's axe may be partially embedded into the wood chopping block, making the Diadem of the Savant or the archery circlet hard to pick up. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The floor of the shack is higher than it should be. This is evident from the door's position on the inside, the low ceiling, and the proximity of the mounted animal heads to the chairs and table. <sup>**?**</sup>
