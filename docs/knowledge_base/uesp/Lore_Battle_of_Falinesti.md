Battle of Falinesti *A brief account on the aftermath of the battle of Sphinxmoth and the fate of commander Eagle Eye*
---
Beaten at the battle of [Sphinxmoth](Lore_Fort_Sphinxmoth.md), the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) commander Eagle Eye ordered his troops into an orderly retreat. Unknown to Eagle Eye, the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) were seeking bloody revenge. As the Khajiiti troops rested somewhere just outside the Vale of Falinesti, the Bosmer set upon them from every side. The battle was short and vicious. Before long, Khajiiti blood filled the vale. The survivors were all butchered and it is said that some were even roasted alive.

Relics Sought:
