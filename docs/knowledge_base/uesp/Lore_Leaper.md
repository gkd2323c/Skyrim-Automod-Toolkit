Leapers or **Leaper Demons** are a type of being spoken of in the myths of [Men](Lore_Men.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Stental-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7F-2)</sup> They are considered a type of "demon" though what exactly this appellation signifies is unclear.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Stental-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7F-2)</sup> One Leaper was portrayed as a [gargoyle](Lore_Gargoyle.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Statue-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Stental-1)</sup> though it is unknown if this is what the creature truly looked like.

Leaper Demons are spoken of in a dubious [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) myth, which portrays the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) as having been the "Leaper Devil King" or "King of Leapers". He was described as benevolent creature who leaped around [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and would preserve pieces of [kalpas](Lore_Kalpa.md) before they were consumed by [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md). Though Alduin was said to have eventually cursed the Leaper Demon King to become Dagon, making him unable to leap anymore and condemning him to eventually destroy all the pieces of kalpas he had once saved.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7F-2)</sup> Other myths portray Mehrunes Dagon as created by the [Magna Ge](Lore_Magna_Ge.md) to destroy [Lyg](Lore_Lyg.md) in the prior kalpa.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MDC4-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TNC-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-PGE2Con-6)</sup>

Saint [Stental](Lore_Stental.md) was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) [saint](Lore_Breton.md#Saints) who is best remembered for slaying the demon of Overlook Hill, rescuing the three Sisters of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) from a terrible fate. A memorial, named "The Saint Slays the Leaper", was erected in [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) to honor his courage in [2E 388](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_388), which depicts Stental armed with a sword facing the Leaper demon, depicted as a crouched gargoyle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Stental-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Statue-3)</sup>
