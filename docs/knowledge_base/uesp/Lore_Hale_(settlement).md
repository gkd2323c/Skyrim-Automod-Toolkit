[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SI-place-Hale.jpg/200px-SI-place-Hale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Hale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Hale.jpg) Hale circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Hale is a village in the [Mania](Lore_Mania.md) region of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It lies along the northern coast, southeast of the Isle of Flame and just north of the ruins of [Milchar](Lore_Milchar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the occupants were mostly a collection of artists. A [Knight of the Thorn](Lore_Knights_of_the_Thorn.md) named Pyke settled here after entering the Shivering Isles through the newly-opened Strange Door in [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>
