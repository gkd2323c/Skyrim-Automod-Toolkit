[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-npc-Hreinn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hreinn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hreinn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hreinn.jpg) Hreinn **Hreinn**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md), is the son of [Kleppr](Skyrim_Kleppr.md) and [Frabbi](Skyrim_Frabbi.md), and the brother of [Hroki](Skyrim_Hroki.md). He helps his parents run the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

Not surprisingly, Hreinn spends his entire life at the inn. The southwestern bedroom belongs to him, and he sleeps there every night between midnight and 8am. After waking up he heads down to the taproom and immediately starts doing his duties for the inn. After twelve long hours listening to the constant fights between his parents, at 8pm he retires to his room for some peace and quiet before his bedtime at midnight.

He wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006ff38) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a0), and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). He carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
A man of few words, he will only greet you briefly, saying:

*"My father Kleppr is the one you want to talk to. I just clean up."* (If neither of his parents are dead.) *"My mother and father yell a lot. Always have."* (If neither of his parents are dead.)
## Conversations
Regardless of his silence, he will be more than willing to participate in the family discussions, as he is obviously discouraged with the relationship between his parents:

**Hreinn**: *"Father, why do you and mother fight so much?"*
**Kleppr**: *"We don't fight, son. We, um, talk. Loudly."*
**Hreinn**: *"Will I need to talk so loudly with my wife when I get married?"*
**Kleppr**: *"Dibella's Heart, son, I hope not."* **Kleppr**: *"How are your chores coming, my son?"*
**Hreinn**: *"Today I've cleaned the linens, washed the floors, and checked in on all our guests."*
**Kleppr**: *"Ah, my son, you'll be a fine innkeeper someday, like your father."*
**Hreinn**: *"But mother says you're a terrible innkeeper!"*
**Frabbi**: *"That's right! Worst innkeeper in all of Skyrim!"*
**Kleppr**: *"Never mind your mother, Hreinn. You listen to me, and you'll be a great man one day."* **Frabbi**: *"How are your chores coming, son?"*
**Hreinn**: *"I've been cleaning up spills and there is fresh linen for the rooms."*
**Frabbi**: *"Aren't you wonderful! Why can't your father be more like you and your sister?"*
**Hreinn**: *"Are you going to walk over to father and start hitting him again?"*
