# Dark Brotherhood


For historical information about the Dark Brotherhood, see the [lore page](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).

## Joining
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-quest-Sanctuary_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Sanctuary_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Sanctuary_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Sanctuary_02.jpg) Welcome to the Family Joining the Dark Brotherhood requires you to first complete the quest [Innocence Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost). You can start that quest by overhearing a rumor that [Aventus Aretino](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino) is performing a ritual called the Black Sacrament, speaking to Aventus yourself, or by talking to the orphans of [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) about Aventus Aretino.

After completing Innocence Lost, you will be able to join the Dark Brotherhood in the quest [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md).

## Destroying the Dark Brotherhood
To destroy the Dark Brotherhood, kill Astrid instead of a hostage when you arrive in the shack during the quest [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) You will then fail that quest, and the quest [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) will appear in its place. This will be your only opportunity to attack the Dark Brotherhood.

## Advantages to Joining
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/SR-place-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.jpg) The Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary - Free access to the [Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), although it can also be accessed while destroying the Dark Brotherhood. - Inside the Sanctuary is a word wall for the [Dragon Shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) [Marked for Death](Skyrim_Marked_for_Death.md) and an [Unusual Gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md).
- Access to the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md), which has some useful items when upgraded, also becomes available near the end of the questline. - This includes [Dark Brotherhood Initiates](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Initiate.md), who cannot be killed, and a space with attributes similar to a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md).
- Cicero will offer to follow you during adventures if spared during [The Cure for Madness](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md).
- You receive your own set of [shrouded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Armor), which boosts several of the skills most useful to an assassin. The only other source of this armor is named Dark Brotherhood members. - You may obtain [shrouded robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Robes), [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Shoes), [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hood), and [hand wraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Hand_Wraps), which yield no armor rating but have other enchantments useful to assassins.
- [Ancient Shrouded Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Shrouded_Armor) is also attainable by completing optional questlines.
- The [Blade of Woe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Woe), a dagger with higher base damage than all other daggers except the [dragonbone dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonbone_Dagger)<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup>, which has the same base damage. - Can also be looted from Astrid's body if you choose to destroy the Dark Brotherhood.
- [Windshear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windshear), a unique scimitar with a powerful stagger effect.
- The unique [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) [Shadowmere](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shadowmere), which has the highest health and stamina of any horse available to you.
- The [Summon Spectral Assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Spectral_Assassin) power.
- All Dark Brotherhood members are friendly to blood-starved [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) (stage 4), and can be fed upon. - This is no longer a unique benefit if the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on is installed.
- Access to [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md), the Master Trainer for [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md), and [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md), the Master Trainer for [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md).

## Radiant Conversations
There is a radiant dialogue system for the Dark Brotherhood members. The conversation starts with a random line from one participant. This is followed with a random line in response, and a further random question by the first speaker. The final line is still randomly selected, but each member of the Brotherhood has a choice of three unique lines and two generic lines to finish with. Some members have more than three unique lines, but they can only use three depending on the second participant. Other members in the vicinity may chip in with random generic phrases depending on the stage of the conversation.

## Notes
- Joining the Brotherhood adds a few lines of NPC dialogue, like with other [factions](Skyrim_Factions.md). Some [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) will say *"Psst, I know who you are.... Hail Sithis."*
- Sometimes you may [encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin_Sent_to_Kill_the_Player) a [Dark Brotherhood assassin](Skyrim_Assassin.md) who carries a [contract for your assassination](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin%27s_Note) as a [random occurrence](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md). This will only happen if you have not completed [Innocence Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost), and can happen multiple times.

## Bugs
- [Deceiving the Herd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deceiving_the_Herd) can trigger its kill selection setup before you have been told to actually do anything. This can cause the quest to react to Dark Brotherhood kills inappropriately. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.2.1, addresses this issue. Keep in mind though that using a Dark Brotherhood target for Deceiving the Herd is perfectly valid. All the fix does is prevent Deceiving the Herd from registering the kill until you're actually told to make one.
- Two assassination targets, Agnis and Maluril, incorrectly belong to the Dark Brotherhood faction, which can cause you to get expelled from the Dark Brotherhood if they get attacked at the wrong time. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
