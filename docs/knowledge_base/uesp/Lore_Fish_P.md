## Pearlfish
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-ingredient-Pearlfish.png/200px-SR-ingredient-Pearlfish.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Pearlfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Pearlfish.png) A pearlfish The **Pearlfish** is a small fish, often found in temperate lakes and streams, whose name comes from the way they gather near [clams](Lore_Clam.md) along the northern shore of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FM-1)</sup> They also can be found off the coast of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) and in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), off the coasts of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).

They are used for their alchemical properties and as food.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearlfish)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Perch
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-ingredient-Silverside_Perch.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Silverside_Perch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Silverside_Perch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Silverside_Perch.jpg) A Silverside Perch [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Perch.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Perch.png) A Perch The **Perch** is a type of fish.

- The **Cambray Perch** can be found in the lakes of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).
- The **Elsweyr River Perch** can be found in [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md)'s rivers.
- The **Pirate Perch** can be found in the rivers of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).
- The **Silverside Perch** can commonly be found in ponds, lakes, and other bodies of water across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).
- The **Stone Circle Perch** can be found in the lakes of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).
- The **Tiger Perch** can be found in the rivers of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- The **Yellow Perch** can be found in the lakes of Cyrodiil.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silverside_Perch), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Common_Fish)

## Pike
The **Pike** is a type of fish.

- The **Alcaire Pike** can be found off the coast of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).
- The **Blackspotted Pike** and the **Shimmerpike** can be found in the rivers of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).
- The **Chillwind Pike** can be found in the rivers of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s western Holds.
- The **Eastmarch Pike** can be found in the lakes of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).
- The **Longmouth Pike** can be found in the sunny river waters of the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).
- The **Stingerpike** can be found in the foul waters of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Piranha

- The **Xylo Piranha**, named for the [Xylo River](Lore_Xylo_River.md), can be found in the rivers of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Pogfish
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Pogfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Pogfish.png) A Pogfish The **Pogfish** is a long, bright type of fish with silver scales and a claw-shaped mouth. They use their powerful fins to swim up and down the rivers of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), most commonly in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FM-1)</sup>

After being gutted, one way to prepare and store them for consumption is to cut them into long strips and salt them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup><sup>:28</sup>

**Found in:** - [The Infernal City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels/Creatures), [Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pogfish)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
