[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Torkild_the_Fearsome.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torkild_the_Fearsome.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torkild_the_Fearsome.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torkild_the_Fearsome.jpg) Torkild the Fearsome **Torkild the Fearsome**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) to hunt [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

Torkild wears a set of [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md). He carries a leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) restoration along with a small amount of gold. In combat, he relies on a leveled [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md) that can be up to [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.
