The **Xylo River Basin** is the central region of the [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md) region. The area gets its name from the [Xylo River](Lore_Xylo_River.md), which runs all the way from the [Silvenar Vale](Lore_Silvenar_Vale.md) area. The Xylo River Basin is where the Xylo River ends from the [Strid River](Lore_Strid_River.md). The capital of the region is the township of [Vulkwasten](Lore_Vulkwasten.md), which is well known for being a brewery making fermented beverages. Towards the end of the Basin, the grand Wood Orcish settlement of Jathsogur serves as the headquarters of the [Drublog Clan](Lore_Drublog_Clan.md) of [Wood Orcs](Lore_Wood_Orc.md).
