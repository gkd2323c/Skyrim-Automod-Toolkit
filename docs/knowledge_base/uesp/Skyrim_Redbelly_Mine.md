[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Redbelly_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Redbelly_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Redbelly_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Redbelly_Mine.jpg) Redbelly Mine **Redbelly Mine** is a small [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) [mine](Skyrim_Mines.md) in western [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) initially containing [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md). It contains only one zone, *[Redbelly Mine](#Redbelly_Mine)*.

The villagers describe the mine as the lifeblood of their settlement. It gets its name from the red mist said to flow throughout the mine; the mist's smell is described as similar to the sulfur vents in the volcanic tundra. The local [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md), [Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md), will request your aid in clearing the mine of a group of frostbite spiders that recently invaded after the discovery of some [unusual ore](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Ore.md). The miners will return to work after the mine has been cleared. [Grogmar gro-Burzag](Skyrim_Grogmar_gro-Burzag.md) will [buy iron ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) from you, despite the fact that the mine contains only ebony ore.

## Related Quests
- **[Mine or Yours](Skyrim_Mine_or_Yours.md)**: Help [Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md) by ridding the Redbelly Mine of [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md).
- **[Mine Ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md)**: Get paid to [mine](Skyrim_Mining.md) ore. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) and three pieces of [ebony ore](Skyrim_Ebony_Ore.md) are outside the mine entrance, which is west of [Filnjar's house](Skyrim_Filnjar%27s_House.md).

### Redbelly Mine
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-interior-Redbelly_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Redbelly_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Redbelly_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Redbelly_Mine.jpg) Mine interior The mine features one ebony ore vein on the ground on the descent just before the bottom level and two more ebony ore veins on the ground on the bottom level.

## Notes
- Redbelly Mine [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Shor%27s_Stone) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) as an ebony mine named "Shor's Stone Mine".
- Printed literature and dialogue in-game refer to this as an iron mine. Additionally, Grogmar gro-Burzag will buy iron ore from you, however the veins produce ebony ore. It is debated whether or not this is a bug or intentional.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.6, fixes this bug.
