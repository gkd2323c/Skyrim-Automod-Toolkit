**Fjori** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) huntress. When she was 29 years old, she fell in love with the warlord [Holgeir](Skyrim_Holgeir.md) on the field of battle, where they fought until her sword broke his axe and his shield dulled her blade. With Fjori and Holgeir united, there came a time of peace for the clans of the forest. When Holgeir was bitten by a poisonous snake, Fjori journeyed to obtain a cure from the Akavir which instantly cured him. However, then Fjori too was bitten by a snake, she died immediately due to exhaustion from her journey. Holgeir had a [great tomb](Skyrim_Ansilvund.md) built, and after its completion, he took his own life, hoping to rejoin her in [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).

During the quest [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md), a conjurer named [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven), distraught over the loss of her husband Saeel Al-Skaven, is raising an army of [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) which includes both Fjori (as a Draugr) and Holgeir (as a Draugr or Dragon Priest) in its ranks.

## Related Quests
- **[Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund_(quest).md)**: Kill [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven) in [Ansilvund](Skyrim_Ansilvund.md).

- **Holgeir**: *"Now we can rest in peace once again. Come Fjori, my love..."*
- **Fjori**: *"Take this with our gratitude."*
