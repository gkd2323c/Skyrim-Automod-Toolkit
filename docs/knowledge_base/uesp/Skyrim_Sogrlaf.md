**Sogrlaf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) crewman aboard the [Northern Maiden](Skyrim_Northern_Maiden.md). He can be found wandering at the ship all day.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd), and a [cowl](Skyrim_Cowl.md#00017696). He is armed with a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) and carries some gold change.

## Dialogue
When spoken to his greetings are:

- *"The Northern Maiden is ship-shape and ready to go."*
- *"Talk to [Captain Gjalund](Skyrim_Gjalund_Salt-Sage.md) if you're looking to book passage."*
- *"Captain Gjalund does the business around here... I'm just a ship's mate."*
- *"If you're looking for passage, talk to Captain Gjalund."*
