Poem for Zaharia **by [Fathrys](Skyrim_Fathrys.md)** *A love poem*
---
[Zaharia](Skyrim_Zaharia.md), my desert flower

You are the seed from which love sprouts

I would break the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) itself

to pluck you from the ground

Oh Zaharia, your very name is a spell

that burns hotter than [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) fire

Thus I bring you this [armor of scales](Skyrim_Studded_Dragonscale_Armor.md)

so that I may stand inside your flame
