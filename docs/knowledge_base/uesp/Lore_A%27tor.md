Prince A'tor was the son of High King [Thassad II](Lore_Thassad_II.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). After his father's death in [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862), the [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) faction, led by [Baron Volag](Lore_Volag.md), took control of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md), the capital of Hammerfell. Prince A'tor rallied his [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) supporters at his principality of [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) and launched a bloody attempt to recapture the city by sea. However, he was taken by surprise by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s assistance of the Forebears, and was eventually defeated by their combined forces at the [Battle of Hunding Bay](Lore_Battle_of_Hunding_Bay.md), near Stros M'Kai.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGIntroduction-2)</sup>

Mortally wounded, the [Archmage Voa](Lore_Voa.md) placed his soul within a soul gem, disguised as an amulet in the shape of a dragon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGIntroduction-2)</sup> Following an attempt to resurrect him, his soul was later fused with his longsword to create the [Soul Sword](Lore_Soul_Sword.md), which was instrumental in liberating the island of Stros M'Kai from the clutches of [Lord-Admiral Richton](Lore_Amiel_Richton.md). From the Soul Sword, he advised his lover, now Queen-Regent [Iszara](Lore_Iszara.md), in rulership.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Raze-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-GIKurt Kuhlmann-4)</sup> He was believed to be an aspect of the [Hoon Ding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hoon Ding), along with [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vo Fit E-5)</sup>
