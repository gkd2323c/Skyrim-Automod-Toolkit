Letter from Erwan **by [Erwan](Skyrim_Erwan.md)**
---
Dear brother, are you well? I heard this terrible rumor that you died. You always said I'd be the one to kill you.

That wasn't very nice of you, to break a promise to your only sister. Unless there's another sister you didn't tell me about? So now you're not only dead, but you're a liar too. Tsk, tsk.

Anyways, I know you love to read so I'll keep this short. Let me know if you're not dead so I can come over and hug you with my knife.

[Erwan](Skyrim_Erwan.md)
