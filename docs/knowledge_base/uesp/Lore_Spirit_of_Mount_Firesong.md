The Spirit of Mount Firesong was an ancient [nature spirit](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md) bound to [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md) long ago by [Druid King Kasorayn](Lore_Kasorayn.md). When summoned by [Lord Bacaro Volorus](Lore_Bacaro_Volorus.md), it took an appearance akin to a giant ethereal humanoid [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIThrone-1)</sup>
