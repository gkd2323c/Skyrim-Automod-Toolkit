[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-npc-Idolaf_Battle-Born.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Idolaf_Battle-Born.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idolaf_Battle-Born.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Idolaf_Battle-Born.jpg) Idolaf Battle-Born **Idolaf Battle-Born** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) and a member of clan [Battle-Born](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born) living in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). He is fixated on the feud between his family and the [Gray-Manes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane), as evidenced by his insistence that you choose a side as soon as you enter Whiterun. He clearly believes that anybody not supporting his family opposes them.

Idolaf is clad in a set of Imperial light equipment. This includes an [Imperial light armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor), a pair of [Imperial light bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Bracers), and a pair of [Imperial light boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Battle-Born) to [the clan's house](Skyrim_House_of_Clan_Battle-Born.md), as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

So don't talk to me about suffering!"* At the start of the quest you can witness Idolaf and his father [Olfrid](Skyrim_Olfrid_Battle-Born.md) in a feud with [Fralia Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Fralia_Gray-Mane.md) in the marketplace:

**Olfrid**: *"Foolish old woman! You know nothing! Nothing of our struggles, our suffering!"*

**Olfrid**: *"Do you believe this old hag? 'Holding him'? Why I've got him in my cellar. He's my prisoner. Face it, cow! Your stupid son is dead! He died a Stormcloak traitor. And you... you best keep your mouth shut before you suffer the same."*

That's all I have to say."* **(Failure)** *"Between the two of us, his family is better off thinking he's dead. Thorald and I have a history, all right? We used to be friends. Like I said, his family is better off believing he's gone, because he's never coming back"* **(Success)** If you succeeded:

**How do you know this?** *"I used some contacts in the Legion, asked some questions. Was told I should mind my own business."* **If Thorald is alive, where is he?** *"Last I heard, he'd been carted off to [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). Ever heard of it? If you go in there, you don't come out. Ever. Here, see for yourself. This is the last word I received. I'm telling you, he's gone for good."*
## Notes
- Idolaf's disposition towards you will change depending on the reply to his initial question. Should you reply that you support the Battle-Borns, Idolaf will remain friendly even if the Stormcloaks [gain control of the city](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md). Likewise, should you reply that you support the Gray-Manes, Idolaf will remain hostile even if the Empire [gains control of the city](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md).
