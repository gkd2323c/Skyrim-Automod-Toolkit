[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-npc-Zaria.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Zaria.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaria.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaria.jpg) Zaria **Zaria** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) who immigrated to [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) in order to explore her passion for [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md) more freely. She fell in love with [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) and named her store [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md), where she also lives.

She wakes up at 8am and opens her store. During this time, she can sometimes be found mixing some potions behind the counter. At 8pm she closes her store and spends the evening relaxing until midnight, when she heads to bed.

She wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#0004223b). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Grave_Concoctions) to her store, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached, Zaria can greet you with:

*"Someone new, perhaps someone looking for me to mix something up for them?"* *"Don't let the shop's name frighten you away. I have plenty of reliable tonics and healing potions to sell."* *"Falkreath's warriors always return, one way or another."* *"If either these [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) or [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial.md) had some red in their blood this war would be over."* When you are browsing her store:

*"Call upon me if I can be of any service."* *"Browse as much as you like, please."* Speaking to her:

**Why name your store Grave Concoctions?** *"I know it's a bit strange. Not exactly a name to bring comfort to the sick and ailing who come to buy a poultice or salve. But what you must understand about Falkreath is that our town is defined, for better or wrose * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, by the large and ancient cemetery here. That's why the inn is called [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md), the farm is called [Corpselight Farm](Skyrim_Corpselight_Farm.md), and so on. I suppose it's sort of a running joke."* **There aren't many Redguards in Skyrim. What brought you here?** *"Well, my family back in Hammerfell didn't approve of my interest in lethal poisons and death in general. So I left, and wandered north. When I found this town, with its huge cemetery, I felt right at home. I opened the shop and I've never looked back. This is where I belong."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Clear-Headed
Once you obtain a glassfish, head to Grave Concoctions in Falkreath to speak to Zaria:

**Here's the glassfish you requested in your bounty.** *"I'm grateful for your efforts. Here, take this."* She will then give you 200 gold and a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaria%27s_Note_from_Jarl) from the Jarl.

**Thanks for the coin. And... a note from the Jarl?** *"Maybe you can help me."* The note from the Jarl states that a guard has been stealing skooma from the evidence chest, and that the Jarl would like for the guard to have an invisibility potion poured into an empty skooma bottle to scare the guard straight. Speak to Zaria to obtain the invisibility potion:

**I'll deliver the disguised potion in exchange for the Jarl's ring.** *"Thank you. Here, this is for you."* **I'll return when the deed is done. Hopefully he learns his lesson.** *"Good luck out there."* She will then hand you a [Disguised invisibility potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Disguised_Invisibility_Potion).

Once you have dealt with the guard, head back to Zaria and speak to her:

**I don't think that guard will be using skooma any longer.** *"Excellent. You've done us a great service. Here is your reward."* She will then hand you an enchanted ring.

## Notes
- Zaria was once intended to work at [Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md)'s mill, indicated by her belonging to the lumberjack faction and her unused dialogue above.
- She was also at one point intended to serve as housecarl, presumably after the Stormcloaks took Falkreath, as she is in the housecarl faction, still has an unused package to exile her to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), and is marked as the backup housecarl in Falkreath's dialogue quest.
- [Zaria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Characters#Zaria) and her [store](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Grave_Concoctions) also appear in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*.
- The shop and shopkeeper have no associated quest to raise Zaira's disposition, which is usually necessary to allow the PC to take small value items from the store. Investing 500 septims through the Investor perk will raise her disposition and allow you to take items, which, when made into potions, will recoup the cost. For example, the ingredients Frost Mirriam, Giant's Toe, and Rock Warbler Egg can now be taken for free and make an expensive potion.
