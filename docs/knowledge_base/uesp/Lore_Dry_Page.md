Dry Page *A bleak Nordic poem* *
---
When the snow returns
and the rivers freeze,
[Sun](Lore_Sun.md) hides like a child
between its mother's knees.
The wise [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) knows
that [the end](Lore_Death.md) comes soon,
He grows impatient
awaiting his doom.
