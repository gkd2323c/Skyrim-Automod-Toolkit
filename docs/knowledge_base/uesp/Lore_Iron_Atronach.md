# Iron Atronach

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-creature-Iron_Atronach.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Iron_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Iron_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Iron_Atronach.jpg) Iron Atronach (ESO) Iron Atronachs (sometimes called **Earth Atronachs**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> are a type of [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATRQUEST-2)</sup> They are towering behemoths composed of solid and molten [iron](Lore_Iron.md) and usually take a roughly humanoid form.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Iron Atronachs come from an unnamed [volcanic realm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Realm_of_the_Iron_Atronachs) of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), an impossibly dense molten hellscape that produces them in abundance.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Showcase-4)</sup> Iron Atronach [names](Lore_Names.md#Iron_Atronach) usually appear with the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) clan name "Mynar".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

Iron Atronachs have a relentless appetite for destruction<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Showcase-4)</sup> and are often found in the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), the volcanic realm of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), the Prince of Destruction.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> They attack using their brute strength and the molten heat of their bodies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Some Iron Atronachs possess a magical aura that damages anyone who dares to get too close . Electricity-based spells do not damage Iron Atronachs, but heal them instead. They can be harmed by any type of weapon.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DF-6)</sup>

Few mortal [conjurers](Lore_Conjuration.md) dare to summon Iron Atronachs to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), despite them making powerful servants.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Showcase-4)</sup> Those that do sometimes end up dead.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGuar-7)</sup> Some mages, not satisfied with simply summoning Iron Atronachs, weave powerful [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) spells to assume the shape of an Iron Atronach.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Iron Poly-8)</sup> The [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) were known to use Iron Atronach-styled weapons and armor.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

## Mounts
Sufficiently powerful mages can bend summoned Iron Atronachs into the form of mounts. The magic-wielding knights of Zenithar's Crucible are known to charge into battle atop Iron Atronach Horses; it is said no force can stand against such a charge.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> The first Iron Atronach Senche was summoned by [Maalanrie Direnni](Lore_Maalanrie_Direnni.md) of [Clan Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) when she was restrained by bandits.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> The mage [Tarvyn Othren](Lore_Tarvyn_Othren.md) was the first to summon an Iron Atronach Guar, which resulted in the deaths of his companions.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOGuar-7)</sup>

[Aer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aer), the Conjurer Magnificent, summoned the first Iron Atronach Bear in [1E 2150](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2150); the calling-ritual for summoning Iron Bears is known to be far easier than most, due to the lumbering form of a bear perfectly matching the temperament of an Iron Atronach.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> The famous [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) conjurer [Rozaba af-Nizhadal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rozaba_af-Nizhadal), was known to ride Iron Atronach Camels across the deserts of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). She referred to these creatures as "glass-treaders", as the atronach's scorching feet left behind glass tracks wherever she rode.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

Only the strongest mages can bend an Iron Atronach into a lupine form; Iron Atronach Wolves are the most difficult form to summon, as the solitary atronachs despise the pack reliance of [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup>

### Variants
- **Black Forge Iron Senches** are a unique form of Iron Atronach Senche. They are formed from [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) -alloy iron and soul-searing blue light, and were created at the same forges that created the [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md) used by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) during his attempted [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). They are named for [the Black Forge](Lore_The_Black_Forge.md), a facility in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) where the Anchors were created. Although they appear to be freezing cold, they can be comfortably mounted.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
- **Sunflare Iron Wolves** are a unique form of Iron Atronach Wolf. They are summoned in the form of a [meteor](Lore_Meteors.md), which crash lands prior to the atronach taking shape. They possess an intense magical glow, said to resemble the ruby radiance of a sunset. These atronachs possess the ability to summon a storm of meteorites when they howl.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
- **Viridian Iron Steeds** are a unique form of Iron Atronach Horse. Their flames have taken on the green hue of [aeonstone](Lore_Aeonstone.md) during the summoning ritual, either by mistake or intentionally.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

## Golems
Some Iron Atronachs are actually not Daedra at all, but a type of [Iron](Lore_Iron_Golem.md) [Golem](Lore_Golem.md) which have been artificially constructed and animated by magic.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ATRMAN-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-HFAQUEST-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup> The Earthgore Amalgam was an Iron Atronach created from a mix of fused metal and stone by the [Bloodroot Forge](Lore_Bloodroot_Forge.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup>
