[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-creature-Gatekeeper.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Gatekeeper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gatekeeper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Gatekeeper.jpg) Gatekeeper The **Gatekeeper** is a named [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) found in the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md). She is encountered after learning a word of the shout [Dragon Aspect](Skyrim_Dragon_Aspect.md), where she and other leveled draugr break out of their coffins.

The Gatekeeper carries the [Temple of Miraak Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_of_Miraak_Key), a one-handed ebony weapon, and a leveled piece of jewelry.
