Ash Vampires, also known as **Heartwights**, are powerful immortal [ash creatures](Lore_Ash_Creature.md) created by the connection forged by [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) to the power of [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md). They are similar in appearance to Dagoth Ur himself, possessing sharp claws and a [third eye](Lore_Third_Eye.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AVamp-1)</sup> Considered to be distinct from actual "[vampires](Lore_Vampire.md)" themselves. The terms "Ash Vampire", and "Blood Vampire" are used to distinguish between the two kinds of vampires. In addition, these creatures are not actually [undead](Lore_Undead.md), and it isn't possible to catch [vampirism](Lore_Vampirism.md) from them.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VOV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOTD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ger V-4)</sup> Despite this, many on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) considered them as a powerful form of undead.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ger V-4)</sup>

There were only seven known Ash Vampires, they were considered brothers to Dagoth Ur and were chief among his servants, serving as his lieutenants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vivec-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Gares-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-7)</sup> The [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) claims them to have once served as the original leaders of [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Hapshat-8)</sup> with [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) describing them as powerful and cunning sorcerers of old.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vivec-5)</sup> They were known to have slept alongside Dagoth Ur under Red Mountain for thousands of years.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-PTDDU-9)</sup> Dagoth Ur was able to give portions of his power and immortality to these few individuals of his original House.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vivec-5)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-PTDDU-9)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-7)</sup> As such when they appeared to die, they would eventually be revived at the Heart.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vivec-5)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Dagoth Ur's plan for his brethren was for them to be established as the god-priests of [Akulakhan](Lore_Akulakhan.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

One of the Ash Vampires was known to have slain [Peakstar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Peakstar), the last of the known [failed Incarnates](Lore_Failed_Incarnates.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWPeakstar-12)</sup> All seven of the Ash Vampires were slain by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vivec-5)</sup>
