## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Silnim Dale was an active settlement. It was ruled by Count Helseth and had a rivalry with [Othrenis](Lore_Othrenis.md).

## Notes
- Silnion Vale shares a similar name and location to the Vale of the Ghost Snake, but it is unlikely they are the same.
