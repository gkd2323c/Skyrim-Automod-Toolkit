---
Survey of the Holdings of Jarl Gjalund

As Witnessed by Slafknir the Scribe, so Sworn by the Old Gods and the New

[Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) - AHROLSEDOVAH - The Jarl's Holding, with Plentiful Water and Pasturage. Home of [Jorrvaskr](Lore_Jorrvaskr.md), the Far-Famed Hall of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md).

[Rorik's Steading](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rorikstead) - RORIKHOFKAH - A Small Farmstead in the Western Plains. Grain, Leather, Horses.

[Granite Hill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Granite_Hill) - QUETHSEGOL AHROL - Three Farms and an Inn, just North of the [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md). A Market is Held here Weekly.

[H'roldan](Lore_Old_Hroldan.md) - AHROLDAN - A Spacious Wooden Hall and Pasturage, recently Seized from the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md). [Silver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silver) and [Iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iron) as Tribute from the Natives.

[Bromjunaar](Lore_Labyrinthian.md) - BROMJUN1R - An Old Settlement, much Reduced from Former Days. Lumber and Stone.

[Korvanjund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Korvanjund) - KORVANJUND - A Small Fortified Settlement. Hides and Meat.

[Volunruud](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Volunruud) - VOLUNR5D - A Fortified Wooden Hall near [Giants' Gap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giants%27_Gap). Meat and Worked [Ivory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ivory).

[Hillgrund's Steading](Lore_Ivarstead.md) - HILLGRUNDHOFKAH - A Large Farmstead Near the Base of the [Monahven](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md). Grain, [Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mead), Honey.
