Torc of Baloth Bloodtusk *Notes on a legendary warrior's necklace*
---
Known as the Wild Boar of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), [Baloth Bloodtusk](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md) battled the enemies of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), especially the invading hordes of the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md).

His legendary torc, a heavy chain bearing a hammer, saved him from a Redguard's attack when it deflected a blade thrust. The chain snapped, however, and the torc was lost to history near what is today called [Honor's Rest](Lore_Honor%27s_Rest.md).
