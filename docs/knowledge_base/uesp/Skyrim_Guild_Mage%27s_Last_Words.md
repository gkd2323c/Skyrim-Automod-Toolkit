Guild Mage's Last Words *A Guild Mage's thoughts before his end*
---
I am done. My wounds are too deep, and so I have chosen to remain and lock this area. All I can do is pray that Sellonus made it out and sealed this dreaded place shut forever. At least then my death will not be in vain.
