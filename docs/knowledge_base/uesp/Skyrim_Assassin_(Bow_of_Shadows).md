## Related Quests
- **[In the Shadows](Skyrim_In_the_Shadows.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bow_of_Shadows.md)</sup>: Save the Jarl of Whiterun from an assassination plot.

## Quest-Related Events
The assassin will not appear if the Jarl is occupied, and will not appear if you haven't started the quest [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md). The Jarl is considered occupied when the following quest stages are active:

- [Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md) - Stage 10: When Jarl Balgruuf is showing you to [Farengar](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md).
- [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) - Stages 10, 12, 13, 15, 20, 110, 120, 130, 150: During the scenes inside Dragonsreach at the beginning of the quest and after the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) summon you to [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md).
- [The Fallen](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md) - Stages 25, 27 - When persuading the Jarl about to trap [Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) in Dragonsreach.
- At any point during [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), [Battle for Solitude](Skyrim_Battle_for_Solitude.md), or [Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md).
