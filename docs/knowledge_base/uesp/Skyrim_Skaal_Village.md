[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg/200px-SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Skaal_Village.jpg) Skaal Village **Skaal Village** is a [village](Skyrim_Towns.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).

The village is home to the [Skaal tribe](Lore_Skaal.md), who strive to live in harmony with nature in accordance with their worship of the [All-Maker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:All-Maker). They live a simple existence in the frozen north of Solstheim, subsisting on hunting and minor agriculture, but are also familiar with the secrets of forging [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md).

The village's eight buildings circle the central well, with a communal [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and a meat preparation shed sheltering a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on either side of the well. All other crafting stations are found outside the houses of [Baldor Iron-Shaper](Skyrim_Baldor_Iron-Shaper.md), [Deor Woodcutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter), and [Edla](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Edla). Chief [Fanari Strong-Voice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice)'s imposing greathall and shaman [Storn Crag-Strider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider)'s more modest hut are at the east end of the village, while the remaining residents' homes are to the west and north.

## Notes
- Next to the village cooking pot hangs a unique [dead horker](Skyrim_Horker_(dead).md).
- Next to the village cooking pot is an unusable bedroll. The closest places to sleep are [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md) to the north-northwest, [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md) to the south-southeast, or [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) to the south-southwest, depending on your progress in the quests [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md) or [The Chief of Thirsk Hall](Skyrim_The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall.md).
- Skaal Village also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Skaal_Village) in *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)*. In *Bloodmoon*, the village has a unique architectural style similar to Thirsk. However, in *Dragonborn*, Thirsk still looks visually distinct, while the buildings in Skaal Village are repurposed from [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), albeit with an added layer of snow on the roofs.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays Skaal Village in regular its state; the previous Solstheim satellite map displayed Skaal Village the way it did at the beginning of the *Dragonborn* storyline.
- Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch) marks the charcoal pieces, dead salmon, and ice wolf pelt as owned by the village.

## Bugs
- The village residents will never sleep, as all beds in the village are incorrectly owned by [Crescius Caerellius](Skyrim_Crescius_Caerellius.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- The village residents have crime detection and will call for guards if they witness a crime. However, they do not actually have guards or a jail and have no way of arresting you.
