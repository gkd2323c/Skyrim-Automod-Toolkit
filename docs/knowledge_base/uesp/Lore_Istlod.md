Istlod was the High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and Jarl of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) during the late second century of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRSybille-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-3)</sup> He was credited with holding Skyrim together for nearly twenty-five years.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRSybille-2)</sup> After the signing of the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md) by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) in [4E 175](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_175), Istlod was forced to make outlaws of all [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) agents residing in his province.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-3)</sup> In some accounts, he is said to have formally placed bounties on their heads.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-3)</sup> Upon Istlod's death, his son [Torygg](Lore_Torygg.md) succeeded him as Jarl of Solitude, and the [Moot](Lore_Moot.md) elected him High King of Skyrim.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRSybille-2)</sup>
