[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/ON-place-Glenumbra_Moors.jpg/200px-ON-place-Glenumbra_Moors.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Glenumbra_Moors.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Glenumbra_Moors.jpg) Glenumbra Moors circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Glenumbra Moors is a small area in central [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), best known for being the site of the famous [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>

The battle took place in [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482) between the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) and the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) forces.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ALo Sa S-2)</sup> Powerful individuals fought on both sides: [Aiden](Lore_Aiden_Direnni.md) and [Raven Direnni](Lore_Raven_Direnni.md)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DRD-3)</sup>, King [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Rt R-4)</sup> and King [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md) of [Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md) all fought on the Direnni side, whereas the mighty werewolf [Faolchu](Lore_Faolchu.md) fought on the Alessian side.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup> The Alessian Order lost the battle, although the losses suffered by the Direnni are said to have led to their eventual downfall.

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), after [Angof](Lore_Angof.md) the Gravesinger raised Faolchu, [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) visited the battle site in search of a way to kill him, and traveled back in time to experience a part of the battle with the help of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>
