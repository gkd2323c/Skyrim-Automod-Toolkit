[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-npc-Isabelle_Rolaine.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Isabelle_Rolaine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Isabelle_Rolaine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Isabelle_Rolaine.jpg) The corpse of Isabelle Rolaine **Isabelle Rolaine** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [sorceress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Sorcerer) who is found dead in [Hob's Fall Cave](Skyrim_Hob%27s_Fall_Cave.md), near the entrance. In life she was the lover of [Ranmir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ranmir), a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) living in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). She can be found wearing a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0001be1b).

Some time ago she disappeared sending Ranmir into a drunken stupor with sorrow. In the undelivered [letter](Skyrim_Isabelle%27s_Letter.md) to Ranmir found on her corpse, it's explained that Isabelle left to try to solve the financial troubles the couple were suffering from. Isabelle went to [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), an old friend. Vex tipped her off to a potentially lucrative target for theft in Hob's Fall Cave, which would alleviate her and Ranmir's money troubles permanently. Based on the numerous empty wine bottles near her corpse it seems Isabelle may have imbibed some liquid courage before attempting the theft, only to either pass out or be taken by surprise in her intoxicated state and killed.

## Related Quests
- **[Drowned Sorrows](Skyrim_Drowned_Sorrows.md)**: Find Isabelle, [Ranmir's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ranmir) lost love.

## Notes
- She is described as a young woman by NPCs in the game, but when her body is found she looks like an old woman. In addition to this, her voice type causes her to sound older if resurrected. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. She will look like a young woman as described, though her voice still sounds old when reanimated.
