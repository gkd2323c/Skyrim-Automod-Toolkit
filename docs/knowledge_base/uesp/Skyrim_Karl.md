[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-npc-Karl.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karl.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karl.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karl.jpg) Karl working outside Iron-Breaker Mine **Karl** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) working in and around [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

The owner of the mine, [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md), suspects he drinks on the job and she is indeed correct. He never sleeps and spends his entire night inside the mine either mining, eating or drinking and keeps going until sunrise. At 8am he heads outside and finds the nearby [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) where he works twelve solid hours, under the watchful eye of his employer. As soon as the clock strikes 8pm, Karl takes off at a running pace towards [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md), where he spends the entire evening downing beverages and dancing on the porch.

As a miner, he owns a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) which will be his weapon if attacked. He only carries some [lower-class items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor). He wears a set of [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080697) and cheap [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699).

## Dialogue
Before [Waking Nightmare](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md) is complete, the entire population of Dawnstar shares the same nightmare and Karl is no exception.

*"Can't sleep well no matter how much drink I put in me."* He will also readily admit his heavy drinking and how little he cares about Beitild.

*"I'm no one special, just a miner who swings a pickaxe for his drinking money."* *"Beitild thinks I drink on the job. Well, what does it matter if I do? Huh?"* After killing Beitild in the Dark Brotherhood questline, he will give you new responses when you talk to him. Even if nobody caught you killing her.

*"God's strike you down."*
### Conversations
While working the smelter, Karl will be exposed to Beitild's unfriendly competition against her former husband [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md):

**Beitild:** *"Keep at it. We need to work twice as hard now that fat-headed Leigelf has declared war!"* **Karl:** *"We're at war with Leigelf? Isn't he your husband?"* **Beitild:** *"No he isn't! He's the enemy, and we are going to crush him."* **Karl:** *"Drafted into a war, how could my life get worse?"* Beitild also urges Karl to work harder:

**Beitild:** *"Work hard, now! The pride of Iron-Breaker Mine is on the line."* **Karl:** *"Pride of the what? This isn't the Legion, boss. We're miners, not soldiers."* **Beitild:** *"You'll shut your mouth and get back to work, or you won't be a miner or a soldier by the day's end. Hear me?"* **Karl:** *"Uh, yes ma'am."*
## Notes
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states that Karl lives with fellow miner [Irgnir](Skyrim_Irgnir.md) at [her house](Skyrim_Irgnir%27s_House.md). While Karl actually is a part of the house's locklist, ensuring he could theoretically enter the building, none of his packages ever make him go there. - The house contains a double bed, showing that Karl could at least potentially have the room to sleep there.
