# Miscellaneous Items


- [Animal Hides](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Hides): hides, pelts, and leather
- [Animal Parts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Parts): bones, scales, tusks, skulls, etc.
- [Gems](Skyrim_Gems.md): gems and gem-containing miscellaneous items
- [Ores and Ingots](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md): ores, ingots, and scrap metal.
- [Soul Gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md): soul gems used for [soul trapping](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md)
- [Tools](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tools): lockpicks and other items categorized as tools by vendors

For items involved in quests, see [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md).

All items added by the [official add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) have reference IDs that change, thus they are documented as "xx.....". More details on why this happens, and how to find them are found [here](Skyrim_Form_ID.md).

## Bugs in a Jar
These are unique live bugs captured in jars. Each jar also carries a unique inscription etched into the inside of the lid. These jars serve no apparent purpose (nor do their inscriptions have a known meaning), and there is no associated quest or reward for collecting them. However, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup>, and [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)'s trophy room<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup> contain a special holder for each one. If you tip the jars in any direction, the insects inside will always appear to remain in the same position. Additionally, the bee jar may emit a buzzing sound when you are nearby.

The butterfly, moth, and torchbug jars [respawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respawn) along with the areas where they are found, allowing multiple copies of these three jars to be obtained. The bee and dragonfly jars are in areas that do not respawn. With unofficial patch installed, the butterfly doesn't respawn anymore.

The [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds three new bugs in a jar: a bliss bug, a green butterfly, and a purple butterfly.

## Torches
The duration of a torch is 240 seconds, and the radius of its light is 512. Other items, such as lanterns and candlesticks, cannot be used as sources of light. Torches, despite having a value, cannot be bought from or sold to any merchants, even with the Merchant perk. Torches given to followers will never burn out. Holding a torch improves [Warmth](Skyrim_Fortify_Warmth.md) by 50 points when [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active.
