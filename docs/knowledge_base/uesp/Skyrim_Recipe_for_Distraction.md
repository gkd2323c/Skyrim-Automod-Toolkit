## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md).
2. Find the hidden stash of [Emberbrand Wine](Skyrim_Emberbrand_Wine.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
3. Return to Captain Veleth.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speak to [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). He tells you that he's concerned about some [Emberbrand Wine](Skyrim_Emberbrand_Wine.md) being stashed by his men. He'd like you to locate the stash and report back to him. He suggests you search near any abandoned buildings in the area:

- **Is it difficult commanding the Redoran Guard?**
- *"It can be. Even the most experienced warriors can fall prey to petty distractions. They usually didn't amount to much, but they make my job that much harder."*
- **Petty distractions?**
- *"Some of my own men have become quite accustomed to a spirit they called '[Emberbrand Wine](Skyrim_Emberbrand_Wine.md).' Despise the stuff myself. It's powerful and tremendously addicting... I've seen men fail to finish even a single flask. If I knew where they were stashing the bottles, I'd put an end to it myself."*
- **I could track the stash down for you.**
- *"You would? That would be helpful. I just don't have time to do it myself right now."*
- **Any idea where I can begin?**
- *"They're too smart to have left it in the Bulwark. I'd search one of the abandoned houses on the outskirts of town."*

Locate the [abandoned building](Skyrim_Abandoned_Building.md) in town, which is just past the [blacksmith](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md). Stay outside, and head along the road southwest of the abandoned building to find eight jars of the wine in a barrel between the foundations of two destroyed houses nearby. Return to Captain Veleth for your reward:

- **I found the Emberbrand Wine stash.**
- *"Good, this will help me keep things running without having to worry about a bunch of soldiers falling asleep on duty. Here, take this for your trouble."*

## Notes
- The barrel containing the wine does not exist until you start the quest.
- It is not necessary to take the wine to complete the quest - merely finding the barrel and seeing its contents is enough. If you do take it, you can keep it for your own use.
- You won't be able to speak to Captain Veleth to turn in this quest if you've only just started the quest [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md), as he will only say, *"I'm sorry. I've already said too much. Talk to Second Councilor Arano if you're willing to help us."* Speaking to Arano to advance Served Cold will allow you to converse with Veleth and turn in this quest.
