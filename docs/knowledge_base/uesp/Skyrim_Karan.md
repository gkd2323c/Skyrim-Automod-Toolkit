[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-npc-Karan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karan.jpg) Karan's corpse **Karan** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md), and daughter of a prominent citizen in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Her body leans against a rock at a small unmarked camp just south of [Soljund's Sinkhole](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soljund%27s_Sinkhole). Lying on the ground nearby is the corpse of her lover, [Talvur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Talvur), and next to her is [her journal](Skyrim_Karan%27s_Journal.md). It reveals that she was eloping with the poor Dunmer miner when they were killed, presumably by the animal which attacks you.

Behind the tent is a hollowed-out stump where Talvur has stored his worldly possessions, which you can loot.

If you return to the camp later on, you will find the camp has been removed, and a [shrine of Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mara) has been placed nearby.

Karan wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d), and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223b). She also carries some gold on her person.
