[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg/200px-SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-map-Hjaalmarch.jpg) Hjaalmarch highlighted within Skyrim, [4E 182](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_182) Hailstone Valley is a geographical area created by the [Hjaal River](Lore_River_Hjaal.md) in [Hjaalmarch Hold](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) of Western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The Valley seperates Hjaalmarch Hold from [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) of Western Skyrim and [The Pale Hold](Lore_The_Pale.md) of Eastern Skyrim ([Old Skyrim](Lore_Old_Holds.md)).

[Hjaal River](Lore_River_Hjaal.md) is a river flows through eastern borders of Hjaalmarch Hold and feed by [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). It also creates a small lake down to south Hjaalmarch which is became a settling site of northern Whiterun Hold [Giants](Lore_Giant.md).
