[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-npc-Dremora_Lyrkynaz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dremora_Lyrkynaz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Lyrkynaz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Lyrkynaz.jpg) Dremora Lyrkynaz **Dremora Lyrkynaz** is a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) mage whom you must summon during the quest [The Arms of Chaos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arms_of_Chaos) as part of the [Arms of Chaos](Skyrim_Arms_of_Chaos.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). Once you have obtained the [Sovereign Band](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sovereign_Band), you will be directed to use it at a Daedric altar east of [Skytemple Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins) in order to summon three Guardians of the Sigil Stones. Activating the altar will summon three Daedra in short succession: [Dremora Sehtkynaz](Skyrim_Dremora_Sehtkynaz.md), Dremora Lyrkynaz, and [Dremora Sovereign](Skyrim_Dremora_Sovereign.md). Dremora Lyrkynaz is the second Dremora to appear, and will be summoned as soon as you kill Dremora Sehtkynaz.

Dremora Lyrkynaz utilizes powerful [shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) magic in combat and can summon a [storm atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md). He carries a dagger and warhammer along with a [Green Sigil Stone](Skyrim_Green_Sigil_Stone.md). He will drop a [Daedra Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) when killed.
