[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/62/SR-npc-Christer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Christer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Christer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Christer.jpg) Christer **Christer** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who is searching for his wife, [Fjola](Skyrim_Fjola.md). He believes her to be a hostage in the [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md) bandit stronghold, and offers a reward if she can be rescued.

Christer wears a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5), a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6), and a [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md#000209aa). He carries no items.

Christer will spend all of his time in a supply closet near the entrance to the Mistwatch North Tower. If you convince him to leave, he runs to the exit, never to be seen again after he leaves.

## Related Quests
- **[Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch_(quest).md)**: Help Christer locate his kidnapped wife [Fjola](Skyrim_Fjola.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Mistwatch
After entering Mistwatch, Christer will call out to you by saying:

- *"Hey, who's there?"*

After a moment, he will ask that you come speak to him:

- *"You - over here, quick. Before any of them show up!"*

Continuing to stall will have him tell you that he needs assistance:

- *"Please, stranger! I need your help!"*

Approaching Christer will have him ask for your assistance:

- *"Stendarr's mercy - you aren't one of them. Please, you've got to help me!"*
- **What's going on?**
- *"It's my wife, Fjola! I think she's being held in this tower."*
- **Calm down. Just tell me what happened.**
- *"I'm sorry, I'm just... you're right. Let me explain. Fjola, my wife, left the farm on errands and never came back. It's been months, and I've been searching for her since. I heard a rumor that these bandits were ransoming captives, so I thought she may be here. I managed to sneak past the guards and get this far, but... I don't think I can go on. I'm no warrior! Please, can you look for her?"*

- - **You're sure she's being held here?**
- *"Honestly? I have no idea. But I've looked all over and the kidnappings here started around when she disappeared. So I have to hope..."*

- - **Very well. I'll look for her.**
- *"Thank you! I don't have much, but I can give you some coin for the trouble. One of the guards dropped this key while I hid. I wager it will come in handy for you. Please hurry!"*

Speaking to Christer before discovering Fjola will have him claim that he does not wish to speak to you until Fjola is found:

- *"Until you can find out whether Fjola is here there's nothing I care to to * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * discuss."*

Returning to Christer after killing the Bandit Leader or agreeing to lie to Christer will have him question why Fjola isn't with you:

- *"What news? Why isn't Fjola with you?"*

If you listened to Fjola, you will have two dialogue options, though Christer will be pleased either way:

- - **She's dead, but I found this.**
- *"That... I gave it to her on our wedding day. Help me, Divines... it's true isn't it? I'm sorry. Keep the ring. I cannot bear the weight of them in my heart, and I must make the journey alone now. Thank you for... thank you."*

- - **I found this ring, but no sign of her.**
- *"Her marriage band? Then there's hope - she was here! I have to move on if I want to find her. Please, take the coin I promised. Blessings of Stendarr on you!"*

If you killed Fjola, you will only have this dialogue option, and Christer will immediately become hostile:

- - **I had to kill her. She attacked me.**
- *"What? My Fjola would never... You monster! I'll kill you for this!"*
