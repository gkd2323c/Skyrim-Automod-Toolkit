# Eyes of the Queen

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/BT-misc-Eyes_of_the_Queen.png/200px-BT-misc-Eyes_of_the_Queen.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-misc-Eyes_of_the_Queen.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BT-misc-Eyes_of_the_Queen.png) Eyes of the Queen Banner The Eyes of the Queen, or simply the **Eyes**, were a personal guard and intelligence organization that served [Queen Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) of the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). Though the Eyes were formally established in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580) upon Ayrenn's accession to the throne, they existed as an underground agency working to secure Ayrenn's rise to power for many years beforehand.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cariel-1)</sup> The Eyes served as an elite corps responsible directly to the Queen,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ayrenn-2)</sup> and played a central role in suppressing the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md), an Altmeri supremacist group that attempted to depose Ayrenn in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup> Operatives from the Eyes of the Queen often acted independently, doing things that they believed would be best for [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
