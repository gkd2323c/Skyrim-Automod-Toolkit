[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/OB-place-Barren_Cave.jpg/200px-OB-place-Barren_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Barren_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Barren_Cave.jpg) Barren Cave circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Barren Cave is a natural cave network found in the [Cheydinhal Foothills](Lore_Cheydinhal_Foothills.md), in the western [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is located along the [Blue Road](Lore_Blue_Road.md).

## History
Like all natural caves, these tunnels once served as the capillaries of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), pulsing with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the cave was located at the mouth of a waterfall west of [Blue Road Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blue_Road_Keep), a strategically important fortification built on top of the region's volcanic springs. At that time it was occupied by the Bloodborn, a group of Cyrodilic [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Barren Cave was located next to a body of water and continued to be used as a vampire den.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> **
