[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-npc-Anwen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Anwen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anwen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anwen.jpg) Anwen **Anwen** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [priestess](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest) of [Dibella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dibella). She can be found in the inner sanctum of the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md).

She becomes available for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) after [The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md).

She wears [hooded monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Monk_Robes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000bacd7), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Temple_of_Dibella) to the temple of Dibella, a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Dibella_Inner_Sanctum) to the inner sanctum, and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). She also knows a few spells, specifically [conjure familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md), [flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [heal other](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Other) and [fast healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).
