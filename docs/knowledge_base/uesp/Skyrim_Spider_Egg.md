## Ingredients
151 guaranteed samples can be found in 44 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 12 in [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 12 in [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 8 in [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 7 in [Reeking Tower](Skyrim_Reeking_Tower.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 7 in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 7 in [Volskygge Passages](Skyrim_Volskygge_Passages.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 7 in [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 6 in [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 6 inside [Southfringe Sanctum](Skyrim_Southfringe_Sanctum.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
