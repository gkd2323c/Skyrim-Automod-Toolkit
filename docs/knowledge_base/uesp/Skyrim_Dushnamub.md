[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-npc-Dushnamub.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dushnamub.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dushnamub.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dushnamub.jpg) Dushnamub **Dushnamub** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard Orc2H) and resident [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) for [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) in [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md). He is the son of [Chief Mauhulakh](Skyrim_Chief_Mauhulakh.md), and brother of [Urog](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urog).

Dushnamub comes out of the [longhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse) around 8am and heads down to the forge to work. He doesn't offer any services until he is in place, and the walk can take him about an hour. At 8pm he heads back into the longhouse for dinner until going to sleep at midnight.

Dushnamub wears a set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He wields a leveled warhammer, which can be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality starting at level 6. He also carries a selection of [lower class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

His mother died giving birth to him, and he was raised by his great-aunt, [Bolar](Skyrim_Bolar.md). He is aware that his tribe is not common with Orcish tradition and will state so when approached.

In addition to his blacksmithing services, if you have any [ebony ore](Skyrim_Ebony_Ore.md), Dushnamub will purchase it from you.

## Dialogue

- *"Haven't you seen how my aunts act with my father? It's beyond strange."*
- *"I never knew my mother, but I doubt she ever acted as affectionate with my father as [Yatul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yatul) and Bolar do."*
- *"My sister is blind. I told her not to spend so much time with Yatul, but she won't listen. Thinks she's nice."*

### Conversations
Dushnamub can often be found confiding in his father:

- **Dushnamub**: *"Don't you ever wonder, father?"*
- **Mauhulakh**: *"All the time."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"But your aunts don't even seem to care."*
- **Mauhulakh**: *"They are good women. They love me. You will speak no more ill of them."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"I've just never heard of a midwife losing all her mothers like that."*

- **Dushnamub**: *"Father, I'm worried about Gadba and Mul."*
- **Mauhulakh**: *"What about them?"*
- **Dushnamub**: *"You don't worry about two Orc men coming into your stronghold? That they might challenge you?"*
- **Mauhulakh**: *"They're miners. Harmless."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"I've just learned not to trust luck around here."*

- **Mauhulakh**: *"Someday, you can have your own stronghold."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"I think it may have to be far away from here."*
- **Mauhulakh**: *"Oh, if you're worried about me trying to meddle with you..."*
- **Dushnamub**: *"It's not you I'm worried about."*

## Notes
- Dushnamub has two additional lines of dialogue which he was supposed to say after having completed [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) `Narzulbur Free Form Wife Quest`, which involved getting an [unused NPC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Uglarz) to return to Narzulbur in order to serve as Mauhulakh's new wife. He would speak positively of his father's new wife: *"Glad my father seems to have found someone. I just hope she can hold her own against my aunts."* and *"I think I like this new mate of my father's. I mean... nothing like that. I just think she's nice."*
