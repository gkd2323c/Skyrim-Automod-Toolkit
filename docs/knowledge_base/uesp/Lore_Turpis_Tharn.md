[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/OB-place-Cheydinhal_02.jpg/200px-OB-place-Cheydinhal_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Cheydinhal_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Cheydinhal_02.jpg) Cheydinhal ca. 3E 433 **General** Turpis Tharn, also known as **Turpis "Volte-Face" Tharn** was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the royal [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), ancestor and descendant of numerous famous figures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> Turpis took part in the [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md), which took place in years [1E 2321](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2321)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COTF-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSOP7-3)</sup> - [1E 2331](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2331).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1C-4)</sup>

Members of the Tharn family participated in the war on both sides, acting as captains and leading mercenary companies. Following the end of the war, he was in possession of the lands around the Cheydinhal that became the home of the House.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-QC-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> He became the **Earl of Outer Cheydinhal** and married a niece of Admiral [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) and became the ancestor of numerous descendants.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup>

## Notes
- "[Volte-face](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Volte-face)" is an expression that means to make a complete switch of policy or opinions.
