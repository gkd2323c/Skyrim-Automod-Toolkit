Beloren-Kaie was a [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) proconsul and an heir of Potentate [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md). Beloren-Kaie was the last surviving heir of Savirien-Chorak, but he met the same fate as all the other heirs, being killed by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> After Beloren-Kaie's death, his bodyguard, [Lein-Barduik](Lore_Lein-Barduik.md), assumed his identity, a deception that was not discovered until [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTP-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-npc-Shade_of_the_Founder.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Shade_of_the_Founder.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shade_of_the_Founder.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shade_of_the_Founder.jpg) Lein-Barduik Little is known about the background of Beloren-Kaie except that he was an heir of Potentate [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ashur-3)</sup> After the murders of Savirien-Chorak and nearly all of his heirs in [2E 430](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_430)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBOD-4)</sup> or [2E 431](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_431),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1ETWR-5)</sup> Beloren-Kaie, his last surviving heir, escaped from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) with his bodyguard and only protector, Lieutenant [Lein-Barduik](Lore_Lein-Barduik.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> Beloren-Kaie was eventually tracked down by a [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) assassin named Methas Daram, but instead of protecting the Proconsul, Lein-Barduik fled.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ashur-3)</sup> Beloren-Kaie then fought the assassin, and they mortally wounded each other, leading to their deaths that day.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup>

After Beloren-Kaie's death, Lein-Barduik looted his corpse and assumed his identity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> Lein-Barduik fled with other [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) who sought refuge in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) after their expulsion from Cyrodiil following the assassination of Savirien-Chorak.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ashur-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OABR-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTS-7)</sup> After settling in Elsweyr, Lein-Barduik, under the guise of Beloren-Kaie, founded the town of [Hakoshae](Lore_Hakoshae.md), becoming its first magnate.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup>

Shortly after Hakoshae's founding, Lein-Barduik held their first [Proving Festival](Lore_Proving_Festival.md), a festival where the people prove themselves to their ancestors.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TPF-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MFD-9)</sup> During the first Proving Festival, Lein-Barduik summoned Beloren-Kaie, who attempted to kill him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> In response, Lein-Barduik cast him back into the realm of the dead.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> Summoning Beloren-Kaie caused Lein-Barduik to cancel future Proving Festivals, and it was eventually forgotten by the people of Hakoshae.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TPF-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MFD-9)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/ON-npc-Akaviri_Spirit.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Akaviri_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Akaviri_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Akaviri_Spirit.jpg) Beloren-Kaie's spirit Lein-Barduik later died, and Hakoshae continued to be governed by his descendants.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MFD-9)</sup> For over a century, Beloren-Kaie waited for another Proving Festival so that he could reveal the truth about Lein-Barduik's treachery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup> Lein-Barduik's descendants believed that they were descended from Beloren-Kaie; however, this was mostly kept secret due to the threat of the Morag Tong.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LTFD-10)</sup> Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Lein-Barduik's descendant Magnate Feina-Darak found a text referencing the Proving Festival and held the town's first festival since Lein-Barduik's time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTP-2)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MFD-9)</sup>

During the festival, a grandson of Methas Daram, Ashur, arrived in Hakoshae to investigate a rumor that one of Savirien-Chorak's heirs had survived. Enlisting the help of a [traveler](Lore_The_Vestige.md), the two investigated Hakoshae, while the latter partook in the Proving Festival.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTP-2)</sup> As part of the Proving Festival, Feina-Darak wrote Beloren-Kaie's name on a paper lotus and placed it in a nearby river, allowing his spirit to once again enter the realm of the living to judge her.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOProconsul-1)</sup>

The investigation led Ashur and the traveler to Feina-Darak's manor. In the manor's cellar, they witnessed Beloren-Kaie's spirit drag Feina-Darak through a portal to the Hakoshae Tombs, where he intended to exact his revenge. In the tombs, Ashur and the traveler encountered the spirit of Lein-Barduik, who unsuccessfully attempted to stop them from learning the truth. After defeating the spirit of Lein-Barduik, they spoke with Beloren-Kaie's spirit, who revealed the truth behind the town's founding. Ultimately, Beloren-Kaie spared Feina-Darak and forced her to disclose the truth about her heritage to Hakoshae's inhabitants. After Feina-Darak revealed the truth about her heritage to the people of Hakoshae, Beloren-Kaie, feeling avenged, returned to the afterlife.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DOTP-2)</sup>
