Letter from Sabjorn **by [Sabjorn](Skyrim_Sabjorn.md)**
---
[Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md),

This is the final letter I'll be sending to you. I'm sick to death of your underhanded methods. A little competition never hurt anyone and [Honningbrew](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Meadery.md) has barely scratched the surface of your business. I've lost two more shipments this month to these so-called "bandits." If I find out you're involved, I'm taking it to the Jarl.

Sabjorn
