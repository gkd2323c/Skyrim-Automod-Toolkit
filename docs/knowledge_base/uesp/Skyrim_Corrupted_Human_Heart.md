[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg) The Corrupted Human Heart A **Corrupted Human Heart** can be found at [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) during the quest [Crypt of the Heart](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md). It is the heart of [Simon Rodayne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne). As part of the quest, you will have the option to either place it in his grave to damn his soul, or purify it using the [Shrine of Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mara) next to his grave to turn it into [Simon Rodayne's Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne%27s_Heart).

## Combinations
The Corrupted Human Heart has the same effects as normal [human hearts](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md), [Simon Rodayne's Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne%27s_Heart), and [Mort Flesh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mort_Flesh)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup>.
