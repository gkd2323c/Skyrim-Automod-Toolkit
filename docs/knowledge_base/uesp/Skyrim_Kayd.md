[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Kayd.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kayd.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kayd.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kayd.jpg) Kayd **Kayd** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) and the son of [Sayma](Skyrim_Sayma.md) and [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md). He can be found running around in the city of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) with his friend, [Minette Vinius](Skyrim_Minette_Vinius.md). Like children in other cities, you can play tag with the two of them. He wears red colored [child's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Child%27s_Clothes) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bits_and_Pieces) to [Bits and Pieces](Skyrim_Bits_and_Pieces.md), a selection of [food and toys](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Child), and a small amount of gold. Like several other residents of Solitude, he will attend Vittoria Vici's wedding, where he will mention attending the wedding of his aunt and how the cake at that wedding was bigger.

## Related Quests
