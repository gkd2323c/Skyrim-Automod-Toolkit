# Cult of Emperor Zero


Cuhlecain was worshipped for his wisdom, notably when he appointed Talos (who later became known as Tiber Septim) as a general. He was also venerated for his bravery, as shown by his actions during the battle for the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). The aspect of the Emperor Zero was shaped in the palace gardens of the [Imperial Palace](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) so to provide advice to Tiber Septim, similarly to the aspects of other former emperors.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1-1)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), there was a ruined tower located south of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) known as the [Temple of the Emperor Zero](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Temple_of_the_Emperor_Zero). Its only occupant was the Court Wizard of Castle Bravil, Fathis Aren.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OOGG-2)</sup>
