*Enchanting description:* **Time between shouts is reduced 20%.**

**Fortify Shouts** is an effect that reduces the cooldown time between [shouts](Skyrim_Shouts.md). The magnitude of this effect is alternatively expressed either in percentage or in points. An effect with a magnitude of 0.2 point actually causes a 20% reduction in time between shouts. A magnitude of 0.2 reduces the `Shout Recovery Mult` actor value from its base value of 1.0 to 0.8.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect, but none of them can be disenchanted:

- [Amulet of Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md)
- [Ogmund's Amulet of Talos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ogmund%27s_Amulet_of_Talos)
- [Raerek's Inscribed Amulet of Talos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raerek%27s_Inscribed_Amulet_of_Talos)
- [Storm-Bear Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Armor)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md)</sup>

## Temporary Effects
- The [Blessing of Talos](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Talos.md) reduces the time between shouts by 20%.
- The third word of [Dragon Aspect](Skyrim_Dragon_Aspect.md) reduces time between shouts by 20%.

## Notes
- The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) reassigns this effect to the dragon priest mask [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(item).md) (the mask is supposed to have a unique enchantment that has this effect with a standard 20% fortification, but a wrong enchantment is applied to the mask instead), so if you have the patch and the [Civil War Champions](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) in your load order, you can fully remove the shout cooldown time by stacking all the **Fortify Shouts** effects from [Blessing of Talos](Skyrim_Blessing_of_Talos.md), [Amulet of Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md), [Storm-Bear Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storm-Bear_Armor)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md)</sup>, [Dragon Aspect](Skyrim_Dragon_Aspect.md) - 3rd rank and the dragon priest mask [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(item).md).

## Bugs
- The dragon priest mask [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(item).md) is supposed to also have this enchantment, but due to a glitch it is never applied. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, addresses this issue. It grants the standard 20% fortification to Morokei.
- A display bug causes the effect text in-game to appear an incorrect value in some cases. Whenever an in-game display shows the 0.2 magnitude, it rounds the magnitude value, making the text say that the effect is 0% instead of 20%. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.318, fixes this bug.
