[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-place-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg) Chillfurrow Farm with Whiterun in the background **Chillfurrow Farm** is a small farm just outside of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), owned by [Nazeem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem). It is located south of [Battle-Born Farm](Skyrim_Battle-Born_Farm.md).

Despite technically being Nazeem's property, he is always absent. The daily maintenance of the farm is in the hands of his employee, [Wilmuth](Skyrim_Wilmuth.md), who also lives in the farmhouse. A [Whiterun Guard](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md) will look after the farm around the clock, and another guard will cross the front yard several times a day. It consists of one zone, *[Chillfurrow Farm](#Chillfurrow_Farm)*.

### Chillfurrow Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Chillfurrow_Farm.jpg) Inside Chillfurrow Farm Farm owner [Nazeem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem) is reputed to be an arrogant snob among the citizens of Whiterun, but has definitely provided his employee, [Wilmuth](Skyrim_Wilmuth.md), with some luxurious surroundings. The front door opens to the sight of the traditional huge fireplace with two [cooking spits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) and a small table with some common books. Above the fireplace hang three samples of [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md), three [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), and two fresh [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). In the southeastern corner is a small table with two bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and a large dinner table with some food, including [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie), three [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), and another bottle of wine. The northeastern corner has a food sack and a food barrel, and on top of the latter, an [eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel) and a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel). While the nearby cupboard contains clutter, the shelves above have a head of [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), a chunk of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison), and some [horse meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Meat) on display. A small working desk decorates the southwestern corner, with two [honey nut treats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat) in a bowl and several books. Underneath the tabletop is an end table with clutter, on the floor, a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). A food sack is leaning against a set of shelves along the western wall with even more books.

An entrance to a small storage unit can be found next to the fireplace and it contains six food sacks, a goat cheese wheel on top of a barrel, and a wardrobe with clothes. On top of the wardrobe sits a piece of [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat) and a [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). The bedroom contains Wilmuth's owned bed along with a handy keg and a [goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet) next to his pillow. The end table underneath contains clutter, the sack underneath the bed contains food, and the [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) is non-respawning, unlocked, and contains leveled items. Adjacent to the bed, along the northern wall, is a dresser with clothes, with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a coin purse, an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md), and a copy of the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill book *[The Importance of Where](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Importance_of_Where)* on top.

## Notes
- All dressers, end tables, and cupboards in the location are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the sacks, bucket, and pitchfork outside of the building as owned by the farm.

## Bugs
- The [grain mill](Skyrim_Grain_Mill.md) on the property was supposed to be able to produce [sacks of flour](Skyrim_Sack_of_Flour.md) with [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed. However, the one at Chillfurrow Farm doesn't work.
- Nazeem was supposed to visit this farm every Loredas and Sundas at 12pm, but his patrol package is set to 'any' meaning it will always take precedence. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
