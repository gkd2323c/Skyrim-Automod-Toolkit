# Horse

**Horses** are quadrupedal animals used for travel. They may occasionally be eaten, but this is considered in bad taste by certain cultures, and their hides can be tanned into leather. It is said that since the dawn of time, wherever there are humans, there have been horses. As such they originate in various continents, such as [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), and [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Scholars believe that when human settlers arrived from Atmora, they brought with them shaggy breeds of horse adapted to the cold climate, but they also discovered the many horses native to Tamriel itself, undomesticated at the time by the Elves, who relied on different beasts.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMT1-2)</sup>

There are many different varieties of horses found across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), but cannot adapt to [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)'s harsh climate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWGaro-3)</sup> They are bred for many purposes, including combat, sport, and labor. Horses can be outfitted in armor.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBHA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DWH-5)</sup> As with many creatures, horses can be reanimated by necromancers.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FLC-6)</sup>

Two [Septim](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md) emperors died from falling off a horse. The first was [Cephorus I](Lore_Cephorus_I.md) in [3E 140](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_140) at sixty-three years old.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TWQ8-7)</sup> The second one was [Uriel VI](Lore_Uriel_VI.md) in [3E 320](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_320), who despite the efforts of the finest Imperial healers, could not be saved.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TTET-9)</sup>

## Breeds

### Bay Horse
A breed of horse in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), with a dark brown color. The [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) train many of these as mounts, and they can also be found wild in Cyrodiil.

- **Found in:** [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Bay_Horse)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/OB-creature-Bay_Horse.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Bay_Horse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Bay_Horse.jpg) Bay Horse (Oblivion)

- **Found in:** [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Black_Horse), [Skyrim Creation Club](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse)

### White Horse
White Horses are a type of horse breed. In [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), they are associated with [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and considered the strongest breed in the province.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-OBClesa-15)</sup> The southern [Nibenese](Lore_Nibenese.md) county of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) uses the Ivory Horse as their coat of arms. But its origins are unknown and is associated with a couple myths. One tradition ties it to the ancient hero, [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) but one from the [days of myth](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) claims that [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) was the home to a white horse of divine strength and shining beauty that protected the [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md)'s banks.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-TGTL-16)</sup>

- **Found in:** [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:White_Horse)

### Yokudan Charger
The **Yokudan Charger** is a breed found in the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LOTYC-17)</sup> They are descendants of the gray Yokudan Warhorses brought from [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) by the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONYokudan-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ACTB-19)</sup> These horses are exceptional for their ability to traverse the Alik'r Desert's terrain, unlike other horse breeds. They are also the primary steed of choice for the [Gallants of Hammerfell](Lore_Gallants_of_Hammerfell.md), the knights of the province. The [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) family in [Aswala Stables](Lore_Aswala_Stables.md) breed and exclusively sell Yokudan Chargers.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LOTYC-17)</sup> The Galvanic Storm Steed is a breed of Yokudan Charger that is infused with the magic of [Storm Atronachs](Lore_Storm_Atronach.md).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ONGalvanic-20)</sup>

### Yokudan Warhorse
The **Yokudan Warhorse** was a breed from [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md), the largest island on Yokuda. These horses were brought across the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) by [Yaghoub](Lore_Yaghoub.md),<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LOTYC-17)</sup> who later founded [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-STJOA-21)</sup> These horses live on through the Yokudan Charger of modern-day Alik'r and because of this, some people call the original warhorses, the Forebear.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-LOTYC-17)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ACTB-19)</sup>

## Horse-like Beings

### Frost Mare
Although horselike in appearance, Frost Mares are composed of solid ice, appearing as if carved from a [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) glacier. The [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) theorized that the creatures originate from the [Fourth Sinus of Takubar](Lore_Fourth_Sinus_of_Takubar.md). Frost Mares are rarely used by mortals as mounts. They are not to be confused with Frost Atronach Horses, which are just normal [Frost Atronachs](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md) compelled to take the shape of a horse by powerful [conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) magic.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

- **Found in:** [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Frost_Mare)

- [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Frost_Mare.jpg) Frost Mare (ESO)

### War Master
War Master is the steed of the [God](Lore_Gods.md) of War [Reymon Ebonarm](Lore_Ebonarm.md). War Master is said to be a massive golden stallion who Ebonarm rides into battle. War Master's golden radiance has been compared to a summer's setting [sun](Lore_Sun.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup>
