# Storm Mage

**Storm Mages** are enemy spellcasters who specialize in the [Shock Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) branch of [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic. They are typically found in a few caves, keeps, and ruins across [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) known as [warlock lairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs). Like other elemental mages ([fire mages](Skyrim_Fire_Mage.md) and [ice mages](Skyrim_Ice_Mage.md)), they belong to the [Fire/Frost/Shock Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Frost_Shock_Mage) [NPC](Skyrim_NPCs.md) class.

The type of storm mage you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level storm mages even at high levels, but boss-level storm mages will always be at the highest possible level.

### Storm Mages
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Storm_Mage_02.jpg) A powerful Arch Electromancer fires a bolt of lightning Storm mages rely on powerful shock-based spells in combat, and carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), [orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger), [dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md), [elven](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md), [glass](Skyrim_Glass_Dagger.md), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c5d12) along with [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md), [hooded black mage robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes.md), robes of [Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka_Rate.md).

In addition to the gear listed above, storm mages have a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). They also have [atronach forge recipes](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge_Recipe.md) in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in the mages' inventories in-game is set at 0%.

On death, storm mages have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis)
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie)
- **Destruction:** [Scroll of Blizzard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard), [Scroll of Fire Storm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm)
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms), [Scroll of Harmony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony), [Scroll of Hysteria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria), [Scroll of Mayhem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem)
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle)

The lowest level storm mages (Novice Storm Mages) do not have any miscellaneous items; they are equipped only with the boots, robes, and daggers listed above.

Electromancers and Arch Electromancers have the same gear as lower-level storm mages, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Chain_Lightning) and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

## Dialogue
All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Notes
