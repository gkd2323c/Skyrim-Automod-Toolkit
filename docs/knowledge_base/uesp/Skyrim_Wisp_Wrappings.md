## Ingredients
This ingredient is *not* in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants. It cannot normally be bought from them, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants.

- ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is an "uncommon" ingredient.

With [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed, one sample can be found in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) in a locked display case.

The remaining ways to obtain already-harvested wisp wrappings all have relatively low probabilities. The best bets are:

- [Peddlers](Skyrim_Peddler.md) may be randomly encountered in the wilderness (typically being attacked). These peddlers have an 8% chance of carrying 1-3 samples.
- Uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) have a 2% chance of containing one.
- [Falmer magic casters](Skyrim_Falmer.md#Shaman), [warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md), and [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) have a 0.5% chance of carrying one.

## Creatures
[Wispmothers](Skyrim_Wispmother.md), including [the Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady_(creature).md), always drop wisp wrappings. However, wisps and wisp cores do not drop wrappings.
