Like [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md), it contains an area for displaying unique items. It also includes a [staff enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md) from [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md). It was released on August 29, 2018 for 400 Creation Credits.

This Creation was made by [Elianora](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3231858), the same author as [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md).

## Overview
This creation adds **[Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)**, a mage tower east of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). The tower is originally locked, but can be unsealed by completing a short [quest](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(quest).md). It can then be used as a free player home.

The tower comes fully-furnished and consists of two interior spaces separated by a magic portal. It contains many [crafting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crafting) stations, including an [oven](Skyrim_Oven.md), a [staff enchanter](Skyrim_Staff_Enchanter.md) (one of two outside [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md)), and an "[All Forge](Skyrim_All_Forge.md)" which allows you access to crafting options usually exclusively available at the [Skyforge](Skyrim_Skyforge.md) and [Gunmar's forge](Skyrim_Forge.md#Gunmar.27s_Forge). The upper floor houses an indoor alchemy garden, with eleven plots of [Fertile Soil](Skyrim_Fertile_Soil.md) for gardening. The property also possesses a large numbers of display cases and racks, with places to store unique [artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) and items such as [dragon priest masks](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) and [bugs in a jar](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Bugs_in_a_Jar).

## Notes
- A [previous version of *Anora's Journal*](Lore_Anora%27s_Journal_(old).md) made reference to the [Warlock's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock%27s_Ring), which was supposedly stored in Myrwatch by its original inhabitants. However, the ring was not included with Myrwatch, and the Creation was updated shortly after release to remove all mention of it. The free [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) Creation released on November 11, 2021 made the ring available via the quest [No Contest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:No_Contest) by fishing at a spot close to Myrwatch.
- The unique Smelting Pot can also be found in Bloodchill Manor. It functions exactly the same as a smelter.
- The Arcane Enchanter within the Gallery has a unique look to it featuring a skull.
- There's an abundance of the useful ingredient Hanging Moss all around the rooms.
