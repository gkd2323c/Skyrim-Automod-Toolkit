[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-npc-Reach_Hold_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reach_Hold_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reach_Hold_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reach_Hold_Guard.jpg) A Reach Hold Guard The **Reach Hold Guard** can be found in [Left Hand Mine](Skyrim_Left_Hand_Mine.md). He guards the entrance to the mining site located on the far southern side of the small mining settlement. He stands just opposite of the [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) near the entrance.

He wears the standard set of [Markarth Guard's armor](Skyrim_Markarth_Guard%27s_Armor.md), including a [Markarth Guard's helmet](Skyrim_Markarth_Guard%27s_Helmet.md), a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md), and a pair of [fur gauntlets](Skyrim_Fur_Gauntlets.md). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and a [Markarth Guard's shield](Skyrim_Markarth_Guard%27s_Shield.md). He also carries an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md), a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), and has a 75% chance of carrying 20 [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md). He may also carry, albeit a small chance, an [amulet](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Amulets_of_the_Divines) of one of the Eight Divines, some inn food, and a small amount of gold. He will not be replaced by a Stormcloak Soldier if Markarth is taken by the Stormcloaks.

He has no unique dialogue and, in all respects, is just a normal guard.
