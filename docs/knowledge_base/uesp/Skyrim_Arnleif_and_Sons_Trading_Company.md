## Arnleif and Sons Trading Company
The store consists of three rooms: a large main area where the store is located and two smaller rooms with two doors on the north wall. The store has fallen on hard times due to the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) constantly capturing shipments of goods. The store does not display any of its merchandise. Upon entering there is a short corridor into the main room, with a raised section around the walls of the room and two pillars that support the ceiling, with a counter between them with wings from the pillars towards the back of the store forming a square U-shape. [Lisbet](Skyrim_Lisbet.md) will spend most of the day stood behind the counter while [Imedhnain](Skyrim_Imedhnain.md) wanders the store and sweeps up.

There is little to nothing of interest on the raised section even with a large number of shelving units. There is a [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) on a shelf against the west wall and an archery target in the northwestern corner. There is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel, an expert-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) containing random items, and a [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger) on a stone ledge all between the two doors at the rear of the store, against the north wall. There are [a piece of sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese) and two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on two different shelves against the east wall. Everything else on the raised section is clutter. There are stairs down to a sunken middle of the room halfway along each of the walls. Descending to the sunken area there are even more shelves against the raised section around the room, with a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) in a wooden bowl on a dresser to the west, a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Dance in Fire, v7](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v7)* on a crate to the north, a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) to the east, and a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd) on a shelf to the south. To the west of the counter is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). On the west side of the counter is a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) with a garlic braid hanging above, while on the east side of the counter is a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) with a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) hanging above. Under the counter is a copy of *[The Code of Malacath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Code_of_Malacath)*.

The bedroom to the northwest consists of two owned single beds; one to the east and one to the west both having stone ledges as part of the walls. The ledge to the east has an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple) on top, while the other ledge has a loaf of bread and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge). There is also a dresser against the north wall. The room to the northeast consists of a single owned bed against the east wall and a huge fireplace to the north with a cast iron pot on the floor beside it holding a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks) and a potato. Against the west wall is a long wooden table with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a portion of grilled leeks, a potato, a loaf of bread and a cabbage in a cast iron pot. To the right of the table is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) on a stone ledge with a copy of *[Dwarves, v3](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarves,_v3)* leaning against it. The chest contains leveled items. To the left of the table is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand. There are also a few food sacks throughout the building.

## Notes
- Once Lisbet has been befriended by completing [Lisbet's Missing Shipment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lisbet%27s_Missing_Shipment), the shop can be freely looted, right down to openly picking the Strongbox towards the back and taking any gold within.
- The store will stay open for business until you enter for the first time, after which it will keep normal hours.
- The [key to the store](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company) is carried by both Lisbet and Imedhnain. If you choose to save Brother Verulus in [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md), the key can be looted off of Lisbet's body without consequence.
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the top left of the entrance door.
- If anything happens to Lisbet, her assistant, Imedhnain, will take over running the store.
- All containers in the area, with the exception of some barrels and sacks, are non-respawning.

## Bugs
- The front door of this store may be locked at all hours of the day. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.8, fixes this bug.

- Picking the lock and entering the shop will resolve this, and the shop will lock/unlock at the normal times thereafter.
