# High Wizard of Valenwood

The High Wizard of Valenwood was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) [mage](Lore_Magic.md) who held high station in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGJffer-1)</sup> The High Wizard of Valenwood became one of the victims to the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of [Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md), who used their [legendary Flask](Lore_Flask_of_Lillandril.md) that had the capability of completely neutralizing mages due to its magic reflective capabilities. These Altmer performed these assaults on Bosmer mages as favors for the Barons of [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EA3-2)</sup>

One legend about the defeat of the High Wizard of Valenwood states that he was defeated by a mere stable boy who was armed with the Flask of Lillandril.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGJffer-1)</sup>

## Notes
- One other document mentions the title of High Wizard, and attributes it to an individual known as [Carni Asron](Lore_Carni_Asron.md) who was a renowned teacher, mage and [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) credited with creation of the legendary [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) [Mentor's Ring](Lore_Mentor%27s_Ring.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TL-3)</sup> What the race of Carni Asron was, or if they have any connection to the High Wizard of Valenwood station, is unknown.
