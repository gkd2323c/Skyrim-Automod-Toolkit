[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-npc-Saints_Bandit_Leader.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Saints_Bandit_Leader.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saints_Bandit_Leader.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saints_Bandit_Leader.jpg) Saints Bandit Leader The **Saints Bandit Leader** is a unique Saints Bandit. The leader's race and gender are radiant, similar to other radiant bandit bosses like [Krev the Skinner](Skyrim_Krev_the_Skinner.md). Despite his/her name, he/she merely leads one of the groups on behalf of the true gang leader of the saints bandits, [Kinthal](Skyrim_Kinthal.md). He/she has a [note](Skyrim_Notes_on_the_Blacksmith.md) that starts a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Golden_and_Dark_Smithing.md) to unlock [Golden](Skyrim_Golden.md) and [Dark](Skyrim_Dark.md) smithing.
