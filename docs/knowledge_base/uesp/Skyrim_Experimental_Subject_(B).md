## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
2. Allow him to cast a [new silence spell](Skyrim_Spells.md#Silence) on you.
3. Demand repayment for the spell's failure.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Eighth Silence
[Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) is excited about his new research. Offer to help and he will explain that he is working on a new silence spell. If you agree to help Neloth will reply, *"Excellent. If this works, I'll prepare a version that you can cast yourself, if you ever wish to inflict... er, study silence."* He will then prepare and cast the spell.

### Tentacle Things
You will be rendered blind for a short time, and your stamina will drain. In the dark, Neloth will speak to you, while a panicked [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md) interjects:

| **Talvas:** | *"Master Neloth! What have you done to him/her? What are those things growing out of his/her eyes?"* |
| --- | --- |
| **Neloth:** | *"Well that's unexpected. I presume you are blind, given the state of your eyes. It's nothing that can't be fixed. The spell that is. I think I can fix the spell. It should only take a couple more years. But first, I need some data. Try wiggling them. No, don't shake your fist. Wiggle the tentacle things where your eyes used to be."* |
| **Talvas:** | *"I think I'm going to be sick."* |
| **Neloth:** | *"Hmm. From the way they are waving about, I'm assuming they aren't under your control. And I see now that your tongue is also...well, it's probably best if I don't say. Don't bother trying to scream though."* |
| **Talvas:** | *(Vomits)* |
| **Talvas:** | *"Is it over yet? Does he/she have eyes again?"* |
| **Neloth:** | *"It looks like it's beginning to wear off. Hmm. Your eyes appear to be completely back to normal. Interesting."* |

### Compensation
Your sight will automatically be restored at this point. You can ask Neloth for compensation:

- **I think you owe me for that spell.**
- *"I suppose I do. It's too bad it wore off so quickly though. Unfortunately you don't appear to be suffering from any side effects. Anyway, take this for your time."*

He will give you 250 gold.
