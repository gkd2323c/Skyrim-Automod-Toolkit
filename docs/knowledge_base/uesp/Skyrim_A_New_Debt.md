## Quick Walkthrough
1. Be informed of your inherited debt by [Mogrul](Skyrim_Mogrul_(Dragonborn).md).
2. Pay the debt or kill Mogrul.

## Detailed Walkthrough
After the completion of the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md) you will be made aware of a debt that [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md) owed to a money lender by the name of [Mogrul](Skyrim_Mogrul_(Dragonborn).md) operating out of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). Now that Drovas is under the protection of [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) and out of Mogrul's reach, Mogrul expects you to make good on Drovas' debt. To help entice you to pay the debt, he will send his [thugs](Skyrim_Mogrul%27s_Thug.md) after you until the debt is paid.

Mogrul will approach you while you are in Raven Rock:

- *"It seems you've inherited a situation."*
- **Situation? What kind of situation?**
- *"Drovas Revli * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * owes me 1000 septims. At least he did until you set him up with that [Telvanni](Lore_Telvanni.md) wizard. Now he's untouchable. The way I figure it, it's your fault he can't pay me. So I guess I'm going to have to collect it from you."*

You will then be presented with several dialogue options:

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **I'm so sorry! Here, take the money.** | *"See, that wasn't so hard, now was it. Now get outta here."* | Pay 1000 gold |
| **And if I don't pay you?** | *"Nothing for now. But watch your back. I'll be sending collectors to find you. They won't ask so nicely. One way or the other, I'll get my money."* | Nothing |
| **Or I could just kill you now.** | *"Take your best shot, fetcher! But if you draw on me, the guards will put the bounty on your head, not mine."* | Nothing |
| **Please be patient. I'll get you your money.** | *"Patience is for fools. When you are tired of looking over your shoulder, you'll pay me."* | Nothing |
| **How about you take half? (Intimidate)** | **Passed**: *"Uh... yeah. That sounds good. Half. I think that will work."* <br> **Failed**: *"How about I don't. You don't scare me."* | **Passed**: Pay 500 gold <br> **Failed**: Nothing |

If you decide to pay him later:

- **About that money Drovas owes you...**
- *"Tired of running? I knew you were weak. Now pay me."*

At this point, you will have the same dialogue options as above.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-A_New_Debt_02.jpg) Mogrul's Thugs want all your money! If you don't want to pay Mogrul, your only alternative is to kill him, with the risk of the [Redoran Guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) intervening to stop you. However, if you are observant of your surroundings while you are in Raven Rock, you can detect Mogrul and his bodyguard [Slitter](Skyrim_Slitter.md) approaching you to demand that you pay up if you haven't been informed of your debt yet. He will follow you out of town if you are patient and don't run too far ahead, allowing you to discreetly dispatch him without drawing the guards' ire.

You can also visit Drovas and inform him of Mogrul's harassment. While he will not be willing to pay the debt on his own initiative, you can persuade him to provide you with whatever money he can to help you pay off the debt.

## Bugs
- If Mogrul is killed inside the Retching Netch while you are hidden, [Slitter](Skyrim_Slitter.md) will search for you. If you unhide so that he finds you and then attacks you and you flee outside, he will not follow you. Subsequently, even if you immediately re-enter the Retching Netch, Slitter will not be hostile to you, and will still tell you to talk to Mogrul. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Mogrul can still send thugs after you, even though this quest has been completed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
