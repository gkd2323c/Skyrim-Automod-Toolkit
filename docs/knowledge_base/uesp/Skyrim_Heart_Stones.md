## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md).
2. Find a [heart stone](Skyrim_Heart_Stone.md) deposit on the southern coastline of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
3. Return to Neloth for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
If you ask Neloth if he needs help with his research, he may ask to buy a Heart Stone from you. If you have one on you, you can simply sell it to him. Otherwise, they can be mined from [deposits](Skyrim_Heart_Stone_Deposit.md) nearby. He will reward you with 250 gold.

## Notes
- This quest is radiant, and may be completed multiple times, particularly at low levels.
