[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-place-Aretino_Residence.jpg/200px-SR-place-Aretino_Residence.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Aretino_Residence.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Aretino_Residence.jpg) The Aretino Residence in Windhelm The **Aretino Residence** is a house in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) owned by the Aretino family. After the death of Naalia Aretino, her son [Aventus](Skyrim_Aventus_Aretino.md) was sent to [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) until his sixteenth birthday. However, Aventus ran away from the orphanage and returned home to perform the [Black Sacrament](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md).

The house is found on the eastern side of Windhelm, above the Gray Quarter. The house is easy to spot, since its building has a curious shape, spanning over the road. It consists of one area, *[Aretino Residence](#Aretino_Residence)*.

## Aretino Residence
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_02.jpg) Inside Aretino Residence The front door opens to a set of stairs leading up, and you will hear [Aventus Aretino](Skyrim_Aventus_Aretino.md) chanting somewhere in the house. The first room is dimly lit and contains a non-respawning end table with some random books and a knocked-over bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on the floor. Along the southern wall sits a non-respawning dresser, with a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and a non-respawning food sack on top.

The neighboring room is lit up by numerous candles used by Aventus to perform the ritual. Next to the door is a respawning knapsack and a set of shelves with some [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), a [potion of minor magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), and a small bowl with [frost salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md). Behind the shelves is a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Hypothetical Treachery](Skyrim_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md)*. Along the eastern wall is Aventus' owned bed and another non-respawning dresser with a bottle of alto wine on top. In the adjacent corner is a small dinner table with some food, a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), a [leg of goat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat), a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), and a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). The unlit fireplace holds two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) and a non-respawning pot. On the floor is a devastating [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_the_Steward) written by [Jorleif](Skyrim_Jorleif.md), the personal steward of [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Aretino_Residence_03.jpg) The alcove In the southern alcove sits Aventus, chanting the Black Sacrement over and over, frustrated that the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) doesn't hear his plea. For the ritual he has set up a number of candles, along with a chunk of [human flesh](Skyrim_Human_Flesh.md) and a [human heart](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md). The obligatory tome *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)* lies next to him, with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a sample of [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) on top. Talking to Aretino will trigger the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Innocence Lost](Skyrim_Innocence_Lost.md).

## Notes
- When you first approach the house, you will overhear a conversation between [Idesa Sadri](Skyrim_Idesa_Sadri.md) and [Grimvar Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Grimvar_Cruel-Sea.md) about Aventus Aretino.
- There is a "Danger" shadowmark on the bottom of the arch in front of the house.
- As the house is scripted to reset, it provides a supply of both human hearts and flesh. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. It removes the ritual items but the house still respawns.

## Bugs
- After completing the quest [Innocence Lost](Skyrim_Innocence_Lost.md), [Angrenor Once-Honored](Skyrim_Angrenor_Once-Honored.md) carries around a key to the Aretino Residence. He should have moved into the house after the quest, but the scripting is incomplete. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.3, fixes this bug.
