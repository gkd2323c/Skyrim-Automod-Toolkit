*Alchemy description:* **Drains the target's Magicka by <mag> points per second for <dur> seconds.**
This effect is a variant of [Damage Magicka](Skyrim_Damage_Magicka.md). It causes a total of <mag>×<dur> damage.

As with the other Damage Magicka variant, [Ravage Magicka](Skyrim_Ravage_Magicka.md), it is only available from poisons.

## Alchemy
The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a poison of Lingering Damage Magicka:

- [Hagraven Claw](Skyrim_Hagraven_Claw.md)
- [Ogre's Teeth](Skyrim_Ogre%27s_Teeth.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md)</sup> (**2** × [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim TAG-icon-Heavy_Attack.png), **2.14** [![Value](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-item-Gold-heads.png/22px-SR-item-Gold-heads.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold-heads.png))
- [Purple Mountain Flower](Skyrim_Purple_Mountain_Flower.md)
- [Pygmy Sunfish](Skyrim_Pygmy_Sunfish.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

## Notes
The duration of a Lingering Damage Magicka poison is always 10 seconds, regardless of Alchemy perks, skill level, and Fortify Alchemy enchanted worn apparel at the time of poison creation; what is increased is the magnitude, or how much magicka is drained per second.
