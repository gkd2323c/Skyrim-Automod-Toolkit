# Sleeping Tree Sap

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sleeping_Tree_Sap.jpg) A bottle of Sleeping Tree Sap (Skyrim) Sleeping Tree Sap is highly valued for its ability to make people feel much healthier for a time, although side effects include temporary slowness and blurry purple vision. A spigot was installed on the [Sleeping Tree](Lore_Sleeping_Tree.md) in [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) to harvest the sap, and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) trading caravans have been known to pay highly for samples.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSleeping-1)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) merchant from [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) named Ysolda hired an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) named Ulag to visit the camp regularly and harvest the sap.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-YM-2)</sup> Ulag was killed by the giants, but an [adventurer](Lore_Hero.md) came across his corpse in the cave and became Ysolda's supplier of the drug, who then sold it on to the Khajiit traders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSleeping-1)</sup>
