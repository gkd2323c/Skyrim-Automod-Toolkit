Welloc was a wizard active during the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTSB-1)</sup> He was most likely the Archmagister of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) before being appointed by Empress [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md) to the rarely-filled but powerful position of the [Imperial Battlemage of Tamriel](Lore_Imperial_Battlemage_of_Tamriel.md). He was the second Imperial Battlemage since the days of Tiber Septim.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE4-2)</sup>

Between late 3E 399 and 3E 426, an [illusionist](Lore_Illusion.md) named Berevar Bero was known to have publicly criticized Welloc, saying that he was "an accomplished diplomat" but not "a powerful student of the School of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md)".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTSB-1)</sup> Others vociferously defended Welloc and cited how on one occasion he cast Vampiric Cloud on the rebel army of [Blackrose](Lore_Blackrose.md), thereby sapping their strength and skill and giving it to their opponents.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTSB-1)</sup>
