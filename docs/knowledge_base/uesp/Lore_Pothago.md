[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Pothago is found Pothago is a region on the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. When it was a duchy it was surrounded by [Myrkwasa](Lore_Myrkwasa_(fiefdom).md) to the east and [Kairou](Lore_Kairou.md) to the south. Pothago's main city is the city of Pothago.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md) clan is the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.

On the 18th of Sun's Dusk, the people of Pothago celebrate [Hel Anseilak](Lore_Hel_Anseilak.md), with it being the holiest day for them. The ancient way of the [sword-singers](Lore_Sword-singers.md) and their rich heritage is remembered and honored on this day.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Pothago came under the dominion of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWINTW-3)</sup>
