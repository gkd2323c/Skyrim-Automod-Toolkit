The longhouse contains two zones: *[Mauhulakh's Longhouse](#Mauhulakh.27s_Longhouse)* and *[Mauhulakh's Cellar](#Mauhulakh.27s_Cellar)*.

The longhouse is home to [Chief Mauhulakh](Skyrim_Chief_Mauhulakh.md) and his aunts and children.

## Walkthrough

### Mauhulakh's Longhouse
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse.jpg) The longhouse It's a typical Orc longhouse with a central room for cooking and eating with wings at either end for sleeping. There is a large fireplace opposite the entrance with a three pots hanging over it, none of them usable as a cooking pot. The middle pot contains two [baked potatoes](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md). To the left is a long wooden table that has a [leg of goat](Skyrim_Leg_of_Goat.md), two bulbs of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), two [rock warbler eggs](Skyrim_Rock_Warbler_Egg.md), a joint of [raw beef](Skyrim_Raw_Beef.md), two [leeks](Skyrim_Leek.md) and three [tomatoes](Skyrim_Tomato.md) in a bowl on top, while in a basket underneath are two [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). Hanging above is another leg of goat, a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), a garlic braid, a bunch of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the left of the table is a set of shelves holding mostly clutter with a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), six bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) and a [salt pile](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md). There are two dining tables in the room, one on your left as you enter is set for four and holds two portions of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), a bottle of Nord mead and a [purple mountain flower](Skyrim_Purple_Mountain_Flower.md) in a tankard. The second table is to the right of the fireplace and is set for two and holds a rock warbler egg, a bottle of Nord mead and also has a purple mountain flower in a tankard. In the southeast corner is a set of shelves holding two [leather strips](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md), three common books, a [red](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md) and [green apple](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md) and a set of clothes.

The wing to the northwest contains four single beds, two against each wall and is where [Chief Mauhulakh](Skyrim_Chief_Mauhulakh.md) sleeps along with his children [Dushnamub](Skyrim_Dushnamub.md) and [Urog](Skyrim_Urog.md). The room contains an end table, a dresser with a [boiled creme treat](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md) on top, a master-locked chest and at the end a pile of sacks and barrels mostly containing food, except one barrel that contains [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and has a common book on top. The southern wing contains a double bed where [Bolar](Skyrim_Bolar.md) sleeps, with an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) leaning against the side of the bed. In this room, there is an end table, a dresser, an adept-locked chest, and a small set of shelves holding copies of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Knights of the Nine](Skyrim_The_Knights_of_the_Nine.md)*, *[The True Nature of Orcs](Skyrim_The_True_Nature_of_Orcs.md)* and *[The Code of Malacath](Skyrim_The_Code_of_Malacath.md)* along with a bottle each of alto wine and wine. There is also a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) leaning against these shelves. On top of the dresser, there is a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a [long taffy treat](Skyrim_Long_Taffy_Treat.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

### Mauhulakh's Cellar
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Mauhulakh%27s_Longhouse_02.jpg) The cellar Despite lying below the longhouse, this area must be entered from outside. It is a small room with several sets of shelves against the walls. These hold a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md), a [deer hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md), three baskets containing [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md), tomatoes and red and green apples, four sheaves of wheat, twenty-three bottles of Nord mead, a kettle containing a bottle of [weak aversion to fire](Skyrim_Weak_Aversion_to_Fire.md), a [cow hide](Skyrim_Cow_Hide.md), a copy of the [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) skill book *[Song Of Hrormir](Skyrim_Song_Of_Hrormir.md)* and a chest with a leveled lock.
