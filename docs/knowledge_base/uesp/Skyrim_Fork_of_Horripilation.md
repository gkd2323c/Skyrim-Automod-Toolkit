[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-item-Fork_of_Horripilation.jpg/200px-SR-item-Fork_of_Horripilation.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Fork_of_Horripilation.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Fork_of_Horripilation.jpg) Fork of Horripilation The Fork of Horripilation is an artifact of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), which can be obtained through the quest [Put A Fork In It](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Put_A_Fork_In_It). The unique enchantment on the daedric utensil allows you to slay the Bull Netches in the quest while holding the fork, but is useless afterwards.

## Related Quests
- **[Put A Fork In It](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Put_A_Fork_In_It)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Staff_of_Sheogorath.md)</sup>: Pass [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md)'s trial to earn his staff.

## Notes
- The fork was [encountered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Fork_of_Horripilation) previously in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, where it was a quest item in [Sheogorath's Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Sheogorath%27s_Quest). The fork was also seen again in the [*Shivering Isles*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering), used in its corresponding [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:The_Fork_of_Horripilation).
- It may be possible to deal damage to the Bull Netches with an offhand weapon or shield while holding the fork.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Fork_of_Horripilation)*]</sup>

## Bugs
- The Fork of Horripilation may be seen at a companion's feet when equipped. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
