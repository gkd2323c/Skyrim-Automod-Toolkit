[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-Deep_Folk_Crossing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Deep_Folk_Crossing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Deep_Folk_Crossing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Deep_Folk_Crossing.jpg) Deep Folk Crossing **Deep Folk Crossing** is an ancient [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) bridge over a weir with structures at either end in the northern part of [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md).

A Dwemer convector, used for a [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md#Optional_Quests) quest, can be found just across the bridge. With the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) add-on installed, an [Aetherium Shard](Skyrim_Aetherium_Shard.md) can also be found at the location.

You can be given the location of this bridge by the children [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(child).md) and [Bottar](Skyrim_Bottar.md) in a [random encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dwemer_Junk_Peddlers).

## Deep Folk Crossing
Across the bridge on the northward path, you will see a stone plinth. On this stone plinth is an [Aetherium Shard](Skyrim_Aetherium_Shard.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> and a Dwemer Convector along with a leveled helmet and sword, which are always a [Dwarven Helmet](Skyrim_Dwarven_Helmet.md) and [Dwarven Sword](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sword.md), respectively, from level 12.

Approaching the bridge from the south, halfway across on the right is a platform with stone steps up facing due east. Looking from here at the southern bank, you will see a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) directly in front of you. Following the river downstream, you will find an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md), approximately level where the path turns towards the east and [Druadach Redoubt](Skyrim_Druadach_Redoubt.md); a few meters farther downstream is a second nirnroot. There is nothing of interest upstream except the spring which is the source of the river.

## Notes
- [Katria's journal](Skyrim_Katria%27s_Journal.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> reveals that a Dwemer city originally called Bthar-zel (meaning "allied city") is located at this site. Katria is correct, with the actual ruins of Bthar-zel being directly beneath it and an entrance to it at the end of the bridge. However, by the time of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md), the city is inaccessible.
- A short distance downstream, between the first and second waterfall below the bridge on the southern bank, lies a dead Adventurer wearing the rare [Hooded Blue Robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Blue_Robes.md) and carrying a copy of [The Aetherium Wars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars).
