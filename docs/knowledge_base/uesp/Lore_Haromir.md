Haromir of Copper and Tea was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) sorcerer-king who ruled before and during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup> Shortly before the fall of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-2)</sup> he was called to a duel by [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) at the site of Tor (later called [Sancre Tor](Lore_Sancre_Tor.md)),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup> in the [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) region of [Cyrod](Lore_Cyrod.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Oblivion-3)</sup> Haromir was killed by Pelinal, who ate his neck-veins while screaming praise to [Reman](Lore_Reman.md), a name that no one knew yet.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup>
