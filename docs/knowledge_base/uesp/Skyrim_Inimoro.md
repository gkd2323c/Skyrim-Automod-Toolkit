[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-npc-Inimoro.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Inimoro.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Inimoro.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Inimoro.jpg) Inimoro **Inimoro** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who acts as a bodyguard for [Cuinanthil](Skyrim_Cuinanthil.md) in Markarth. He wears [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots) and [steel nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets). He wields a [Steel Mace of Ice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace_of_Ice).
