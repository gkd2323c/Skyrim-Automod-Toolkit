Used in conjunction with a soul gem, the Sword allows the wielder the opportunity to imprison an enemy's soul in the gem. Naenra was executed for her evil creation, but not before she was able to hide the Sword. The Umbra Sword is very choosy when it comes to owners and therefore remains hidden until a worthy one is found.* — [Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Yagrum_Bagarn), [Tamrielic Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Tamrielic_Lore)

The greatsword artifact Umbra is an enchanted [two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) sword in the possession of the male Imperial warrior [Umbra](Skyrim_Umbra_(person).md) that can be found in the Nordic ruins of Champion's Rest, through the [Vile Whispers](Skyrim_Vile_Whispers.md) quest.

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Umbra.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Vile Whispers](Skyrim_Vile_Whispers.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Umbra.md)</sup>: A [malevolent Daedric entity](Lore_Umbra.md) long thought destroyed has resurfaced in the [Velothi Mountains](Skyrim_Velothi_Mountains.md).

## Notes
- The Umbra Sword previously appeared in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Umbra_Sword)*, *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Umbra_(sword))*, and *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Umbra)*.
- Umbra is unique in that it has three enchantments on it.
