Survey of the Holdings of Jarl Gjalund

As Witnessed by Slafknir the Scribe, so Sworn by the Old Gods and the New

[Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) - AHROLSEDOVAH - The [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md)'s Holding, with Plentiful Water and Pasturage. Home of [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md), the Far-Famed Hall of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).

[Rorik's Steading](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) - RORIKHOFKAH - A Small Farmstead in the Western Plains. Grain, Leather, Horses.

[Granite Hill](Lore_Granite_Hill.md) - QUETHSEGOL AHROL - Three Farms and an Inn, just North of the [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). A Market is Held here Weekly.

[H'roldan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Old_Hroldan) - AHROLDAN - A Spacious Wooden Hall and Pasturage, recently Seized from the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md). [Silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) and [Iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) as Tribute from the Natives.

[Bromjunaar](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) - BROMJUN1R - An Old Settlement, much Reduced from Former Days. Lumber and Stone.

[Korvanjund](Skyrim_Korvanjund.md) - KORVANJUND - A Small Fortified Settlement. Hides and Meat.

[Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) - VOLUNR5D - A Fortified Wooden Hall near [Giants' Gap](Lore_Giants%27_Gap.md). Meat and Worked [Ivory](Lore_Ivory.md).

[Hillgrund's Steading](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) - HILLGRUNDHOFKAH - A Large Farmstead Near the Base of the [Monahven](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md). Grain, [Mead](Lore_Mead.md), Honey.
