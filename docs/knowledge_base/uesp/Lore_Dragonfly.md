# Dragonfly

Dragonflies, sometimes called **Dartwings** or **Dovah-Flies**, are small flying insects commonly found in southern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HGTS-1)</sup> and around bodies of water in the [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md), [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md), and [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) regions of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). A number of varieties exist, such as the Blue and Orange Dartwings, white Regal Dovah-Flies, and a number of biomechanical [Fabricant](Lore_Fabricant.md) variants from the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It is unknown whether the fly variants are different breeds or merely color variations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HGTS-1)</sup> Dartwings are prized for their use in alchemy. If hand-raised from larval form, they can become devoted companion, and a common children's sport in Vvardenfell called "dodge-dovah" involves dragonflies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPet-2)</sup>

Dragonflies have also inspired jewellers to make brooches into the shape of them<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>, and the creation of Dovah-Fly swatters in the Clockwork City<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>.
