[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-item-Shrouded_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Hood.jpg) Shrouded Hood A Shrouded Hood is a cowl designed for use by members of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), as reflected by its dour look and enchantment. Its grim appearance is reinforced by the other clothing items that make up its set, which include [hand wraps](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.md), [robes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Robes.md), and [shoes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Shoes.md). The hood has been enchanted to [fortify sneaking](Skyrim_Fortify_Sneak.md), making it easier to remain hidden from prying eyes and attentive ears.

## Bugs
- This hood is classified as a helmet, i.e., armor, but is not governed by either the light armor or heavy armor skill. Instead of having the keyword "Armor Helmet", it should have the keywords "Armor Clothing" and "Clothing Head". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
