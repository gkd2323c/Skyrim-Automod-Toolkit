[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Garakh.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Garakh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garakh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garakh.jpg) Garakh **Garakh** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Blacksmith) living in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

During the day she can found smithing in and around the shack with the forge, though she will not buy or sell goods (see [bugs](#Bugs)). At night, she goes into the [longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md) to eat and sleep.

She wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) with a pair of [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and is armed with a leveled [war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Axe), which may be up to [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish.md) in quality.

## Dialogue

- *"Only a true Orc knows how to temper Orichalcum properly."*
- *"The old traditions are passed down from one generation to the next. The secrets of smithing are passed down as well."*
- *"Every blade I make honors Malacath. Every rivet, every plate of armor is for him."*
- *"[Lob](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lob) will one day take my place, and bring honor to our tribe as I have."*

During [The Cursed Tribe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cursed_Tribe), Garakh will comment on the curse that has befallen her stronghold:

- *"We have angered Malacath, and so we must be punished."*

## Bugs
- Garakh was apparently supposed to be the stronghold's blacksmith [merchant](Skyrim_Merchants.md), selling and buying blacksmith goods. However, a faction was not created to connect her to her merchant chest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. Once fixed, she is affected by [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md), but not [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md).
