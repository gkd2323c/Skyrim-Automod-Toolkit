# Soul Trap

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg/200px-ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg) Soul Trap Soul Trap (or **Soultrap**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup> is a [soul magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md) [spell](Lore_Magic.md) used to capture an entity's [soul](Lore_Soul.md) within a [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md) upon death. Some variations of the spell could also directly harm the target as well.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> Although traditionally categorized under the School of [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> it was historically considered to be a [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md) spell during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OB-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> When cast, the spell is generally purple or blue in color.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OB-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

The origins of soul-trapping can be traced back to [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) and [Vastarie](Lore_Vastarie.md), former [Psijics](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) who departed [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) in the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) to pursue their studies of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) and soul magic. Together, the two conspired to enter [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) and steal the secret of soul-trapping from [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) himself. It was Vastarie who entered Coldharbour and first discovered the existence of [black soul gems](Lore_Black_Soul_Gem.md). She returned to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) with a cache of these crystals, which Mannimarco then began to study in earnest.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TLo V-8)</sup>

The main use case for soul-trapping is to fill soul gems for the purposes of [enchanting](Lore_Enchanting.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-GMo ST-9)</sup> Many practitioners of magic oppose the ethics of this practice. Although responsible for the discovery of black soul gems, Vastarie deemed them to be deeply flawed creations and set about creating the first ever [Sigil Geodes](Lore_Sigil_Geode.md) to enable mages to trap souls in a way that prevented them from being used for enchanting.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TLo V-8)</sup> Some mages prefer to use [ammonites](Lore_Ammonite.md) imported from the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) as a more moral way to obtain extra magicka.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Mot EA-10)</sup> [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md), founder of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and contemporary of Mannimarco and Vastarie, introduced guild strictures against the use of soul-trapping on sentient mortals. This resulted in the categorization of souls into the legal White Souls of animals and beasts, and the Black Souls of fellow mortals.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-GMo ST-9)</sup> In time, it became impossible for standard Soul Trap spells to capture a Black Soul within a regular soul gem, instead requiring the use of the more dangerous and elusive black soul gems.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

[Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [vestiges](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) can be capturing using Soul Trap in the same manner as other souls.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> Souls are sometimes captured without a soul gem, such as through the use of an animus geode.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

With his apotheosis in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TWit W-13)</sup> Mannimarco gained a divine body known as the [Necromancer's Moon](Lore_Necromancer%27s_Moon.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Lot E-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-NM-15)</sup> This moon began to block the light of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay_(planet).md) from reaching [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) every eight days, allowing for the creation of black soul gems through the application of Soul Trap magic.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-NM-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
