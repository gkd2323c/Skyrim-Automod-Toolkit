[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Scar (also known as the **Scar of Anequina**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Meirvale-1)</sup> is a large desert that runs through [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), in the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). The Scar acquired its name due to the nature of its geography. It is intrinsically carved into the region, leaving the surrounding area wounded with harsh wastelands and treacherous cliffs, and the incision as a winding chasm. It is the home of Anequina's outlaw and the criminal underground, which is exemplified by their largest settlements, [Orcrest](Lore_Orcrest.md) and The Stitches. The Scar has been divided into many smaller regions named after it, such as "Scar's End" and "Scab Ridge". It also contains several landmarks, including the Ashen Scar, Scar's Edge, and the Weeping Scar.

## History
Because of the Scar's intrinsic and natural design, it has been the subject of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) myths and legends, specifically on its origin. It has been speculated that the Scar was created from the legendary [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) hero, [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md). Although some myths point to [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md), Dragon King of the Cats. It is said that Alkosh marked the region with his bare claws. But regardless, the Scar has been referenced since ancient times.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOZamarak-2)</sup> But while its origin is riddled in speculation, the Scar's culture is not. Before it became the Scar as we know it, it was known as the Kingdom of [Meirvale](Lore_Meirvale.md), one of Elsweyr's sixteen kingdoms, known for their distillers and brewmasters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSK-3)</sup> The people would drink to their heart's content, that was until the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) in [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260). The sixteen kingdoms each fell to the dangers of the Thrassian Plague, including Meirvale. What little of the people's kingdom culturally became the Scar, where the Khajiit drank themselves into sorrow.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Meirvale-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Aa PAI-4)</sup> What remained of Meirvale was absorbed into the growing powers of the Kingdom of [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md), as was the Scar.

But as far as the ancient past, the Scar has always contained the north's outlaws. Another nation in the region, the Kingdom of Orcrest was known for its thieves and rogues, as far as the Merehic Era.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSK-3)</sup> And for an unspecified amount of time, the town known as the Stitches has always housed Elsweyr's outlaws and crime lords.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOIsadati-5)</sup>
