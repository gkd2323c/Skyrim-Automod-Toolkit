## Oanna
The **Oanna** is a type of fish that can be found in the foul waters of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Ono
The **Ono** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), off the coast of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Opah
The **Opah** is a type of fish that can be found in the rivers of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Oreodory
The **Oreodory** is a type of fish.

- The **Copper Oreodory** is a type of oreodory that can be found in foul waters of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Orod
The **Orod** is a type of fish that can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It was previously thought to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Oto
The **Oto** is a type of freshwater fish that lives in lakes.

- The **Hoaga Oto** can be found in lakes on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).
- The **Zebra Oto**, named after [zebras](Lore_Zebra.md), can be found in lakes in [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
