# Four-Score War


The war ended when [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Versidue-Shaie) signed the Cervant Truce with [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> As a result, Morrowind remained the only Tamrielic nation independent of the Second Empire until its collapse in [2E 430](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_430).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1-P-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBNarina-5)</sup>

### Major Battles
- **Battle of Bodrum**, 20th of First Seed, [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920). Forces led by Vivec ambushed the Cyrodilic army and almost completely destroyed it.
- **Battle of Ald Marak**, 16th of Mid Year, [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920). A heavily armored army led by Crown Prince [Juilek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Juilek) took the Dunmer by surprise due to faulty intelligence on their part. Commanding the mystics in his army to cast water-breathing spells, his army passed through Lake Coronati, with the majority of the army commanded by Vivec being unable to respond to the attack in time.
- **Battle of Black Gate**, Sun's Height, [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920). While a truce between the warring parties had been called, the Empire sacked the fortress of [Black Gate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Gate).

## Notes
- [Dram](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dram)'s ancestors were distinguished [Nightblades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nightblades) in the Four-Score War.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RGArchive-6)</sup>
- [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md), led by [Delmene Hlaalu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Delmene_Hlaalu) amassed great wealth and prestige, exploiting the state of the Northeastern Tamriel after the Four-Score War.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HIP-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TOT1-8)</sup>
