[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-map-Daggerfall_Covenant.jpg/200px-ON-map-Daggerfall_Covenant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Daggerfall_Covenant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Daggerfall_Covenant.jpg) Partial map of Daggerfall Covenant territory Madding Wind Prison is a penitentiary located somewhere within [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) territory. Criminals who have commmited crimes against the Covenant are held there as prisoners. The Daggerfall Five were detained and imprisoned there prior to the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Painted Eye seized [Borderwatch](Lore_Borderwatch.md) and demanded that the Five be liberated.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dot PE-1)</sup>
