It is worn by [Erden Relvel](Skyrim_Erden_Relvel.md), who must be killed during the quest [Ashen Heart](Skyrim_Ashen_Heart.md). Its enchantment is equal to the magicka regeneration rate of [Master Robes](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Master_Robes), but is a guaranteed drop and does not have to be purchased.
