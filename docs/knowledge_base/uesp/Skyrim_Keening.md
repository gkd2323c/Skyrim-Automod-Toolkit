[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-item-Keening.jpg/200px-SR-item-Keening.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Keening.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Keening.jpg) Keening Keening is a unique dagger crafted by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [Lord Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md). Keening, [Sunder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sunder) and [Wraithguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wraithguard) are the three legendary tools created to control the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md). [Arniel Gane](Skyrim_Arniel_Gane.md), who is researching what happened to the Dwemer, had Keening brought to Skyrim during the quest [Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md). His goal is to use it in an experiment to finally solve the mystery of the Dwemer's disappearance.

Before the completion of the quest Arniel's Endeavor, Keening has a "Mortal Wound" effect, lowering maximum health by 5 points as long as it is equipped.

## Related Quests
- **[Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md)**: Uncover an ancient Dwemer secret. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Notes
- Keening also previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Keening) in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind). Unlike many of the recurring artifacts that appear in Skyrim, it retains an almost exact physical resemblance to the item found in the older game, albeit with different enchantments.
- When usable (see bug below), the enchantment delivers "Keening's Sting" on every hit. Visually, it appears to combine the three special draining effects together. It can be resisted with magic resistance.
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- Keening may fall through the floor at the end of [Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- Keening can be placed upon a weapon rack, but it won't stay there. This usually results in the loss of the dagger when it falls off into unreachable areas. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- Keening cannot be equipped by followers due to a flag meant to keep NPCs from using the item. However, this version of the weapon is meant to be played and only appears to have the flag due to a duplication error when the weapon was created. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, fixes this bug.
- Keening's enchantment has a charge capacity of zero, making it unusable under normal circumstances. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, addresses this issue. The enchantment now has infinite charges.

- The enchantment can be used if a set of gear with [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) effects is worn that reduces casting cost by a total of 100%.
- Keening cannot be improved at the grindstone. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. It can be improved with a [Dwarven metal ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven.md) and benefits from the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven.md#Dwarven_Smithing) perk.
- Despite being a dagger, Keening's detection sound level is marked as normal, making it as loud as a sword when swinging it.
