Ancestor Lizards (or **Useful Lizards**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAMQA2-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HL-2)</sup> are semi-aquatic,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lo S-3)</sup> tree-dwelling swamp [lizards](Lore_Lizard.md) native to the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHelstrom Ancestor Lizard-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NORP-5)</sup> They have webbed hands with help them swim in muddy water and hunt for [feathery-gilled wrigglers](Lore_Worm.md). They are known to hunt amidst twisting [cypress](Lore_Cypress.md) roots, where predators from the deeper parts of the swamp can't reach them. After eating they bask in the [sun](Lore_Sun.md) on logs, amidst other members of their species.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lo S-3)</sup>

## Relation to Argonians
Scholars believe that [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) were uplifted from these lizards.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHelstrom Ancestor Lizard-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-NORP-5)</sup> The Argonian allegory known as the *Parable of Becoming* states that the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) found the humanoid forms of [Men](Lore_Men.md) and [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) useful, and molded the form of the swamp's lizards after them to create Argonians.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAMQA2-1)</sup> This theory appears to be an accurate account; in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), the Argonian [Mere-Glim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels/Mere-Glim) received a vision while on [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) of life as a lizard, before the root and the taste of [Hist Sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md) came and transformed both his body and mind.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Lo S-3)</sup>

It is possible that the Ancestor Lizard is also the ancestral form of various other creatures, including [Bear-Lizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bear-Lizard), [Camel-Lizards](Lore_Camel-Lizard.md), [Guar-Lizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Guar-Lizard), [Horse-Lizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Horse-Lizard), [Senche-Lizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Senche-Lizard), and [Wolf-Lizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wolf-Lizard), all of which are said to be descendants of "Useful Lizards" which were molded by the Hist. This family of creatures are called [Lizard-Steeds](Lore_Lizard-Steed.md), and are believed to have been molded into "proven shapes" by the Hist to be used as mounts by the Argonians.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Scaly-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HL-2)</sup>

## Variants
There are a variety of different Ancestor Lizard species. The Helstrom Ancestor Lizard is a green-skinned lizard; they are named for the city of [Helstrom](Lore_Helstrom.md) and are known for making great pets.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHelstrom Ancestor Lizard-4)</sup> The Lilmoth Ancestor Lizard is a black-skinned variant which possesses striking scarlet hues. These lizards are also kept as pets and are known to comfort the pain of others by nuzzling them. They are named for the city of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md), and some claim that they are the ancestors of the local [Bright-Throat Tribe](Lore_Bright-Throat_Tribe.md), a coastal tribe of [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) Argonians famed for their bright skin coloration.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Duskfall Ancestor Lizards are black-scaled ancestor lizards valued as gifts from the past by Argonian [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md) worshippers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
