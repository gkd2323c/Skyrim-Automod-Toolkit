[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Maul.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Maul.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul.jpg) Maul **Maul** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) and the right hand man of [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md), the leader of the Black-Briar family. Should Maven be named [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) when the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) claim the hold, Maul becomes Maven's [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). He is a former member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) and is the brother of [Dirge](Skyrim_Dirge.md). Maul will readily point you towards [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) in the marketplace, or [Aventus Aretino](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He will also provide some useful guidance if you show him an [unusual gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md).

When you first enter Riften, Maul will be stationed outside [Aerin's house](Skyrim_Aerin%27s_House.md), keeping an eye on all new arrivals. When you try to pass, he will actively seek you out and ensure you do not intend to cause trouble for the Black-Briar family. When the conversation is over, Maul will exit Riften through the western gate and find his spot near [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md) where he will keep an eye on the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) around the clock (see [bugs](#Bugs)). If Maven Black-Briar is appointed Jarl, Maul will move into [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) where he will sleep in the southeastern chamber between midnight and 8am. He then enters the throne room for a two-hour breakfast with Maven and [Hemming Black-Briar](Skyrim_Hemming_Black-Briar.md). The rest of the day is spent leaning against a pillar in the throne room, only interrupted by two-hour meals at 1pm and 7pm.

Maul wears a full set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), including a pair of steel gauntlets ([steel Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets) or [steel Imperial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Imperial_Gauntlets) versions) and a pair of steel boots ([steel cuffed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots) or [steel shin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shin_Boots) versions). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 20 and above, and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Manor_Key) to [Black-Briar Manor](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) and a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
The first time you visit Riften, Maul will actively seek you out and say:

*"I don't know you. You in Riften lookin' for trouble?"* If you walk past him and avoid the conversation entirely, he will stay put and say:

*"Hey, you. Get over here, I want to talk to you."* If you just exit the conversation he will get angry:

*"You can pretend not to hear me all you want... but you better stay out of the Black-Briars' business."* If you continue, three options will be available to you, each followed by a warning from Maul:

**Just passing through.** *"Yeah? Well, I got news for you; there's nothing to see here. Last thing the Black-Briars need is some stranger stickin' their nose where it doesn't belong."* **What's it to you?** *"Don't say something you'll regret. Last thing the Black-Briars need is some loudmouth tryin' to meddle in their affairs."* **I'm not scared of you.** *"That's the wrong answer. Last thing the Black-Briars need around here is some troublemaker tryin' to steal a piece of the action."* He will then elaborate on the Black-Briars and himself:

*"The Black-Briars have Riften in their pocket and the Thieves Guild watchin' their back, so keep your nose out of their business. Me? I'm Maul. I watch the streets for 'em. If you need dirt on anythin', I'm your guy... but it'll cost you."* If you try and exit conversation at this point, or if you fail the speech check, Maul will get angry, saying:

*"Yeah, walk away like a good doggie. Just stay out of the Black-Briars' business and maybe we'll give you a bone."* He will not talk to you anymore, simply dismissing you with:

*"I'm through with you. Get out of my way."* *"You aren't worth my time. Piss off."* *"Stay out of the Black-Briars' business and you'll live longer."* You must pass an easy [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade him to stay in conversation with you:

| Option | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| Got any dirt on this? <br> **(Show unusual gem)** | *"Only way you could have found one of those is by stealin' it. Guess you'll fit in around here better than I thought. So what do you want to know?"* |
| Dirt, huh? I'm not exactly clean myself... **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: *"Then we're speakin' the same language. Good. So what do you want to know?"* <br> **Failed**: *"Then you're stupider than you look. You better watch your ass around here."* |
| Here, what can you tell me? <br> **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: *"Pleasure doin' business with you. So what do you want to know?"* <br> **Failed**: *"You don't have enough to earn my loyalty."* |
| How about you tell me for free? <br> **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: *"All right, all right, let's not get too hasty now. So what do you want to know?"* <br> **Failed**: *"Ha ha! Don't make me laugh. Just stay out of the Black-Briars' affairs. You'll live longer."* |
| I'll just stay out of your way. | *"Suit yourself. I'll be at the docks if you change your mind."* |

You can then choose from a few topics and he will have some useful information for you. If you ask him what an unusual gem is worth, he'll respond:

**Tell me what this gem's worth.** *"Do I look like a merchant to you? You're going to have to take that up with [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) in the Thieves Guild. Get in good with them and you'll find her."* **Tell me more about the Black-Briars.** *"Well, you got Maven, she pretty much runs the whole operation. She's got friends in high places if you know what I mean. She's also got ties to the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) and the Thieves Guild, so basically no one can touch her. Just remember, if she tells you to do somethin'... you damn well better do exactly what she says."* **Know anything about the Thieves Guild?** *"You kiddin'? My brother Dirge works in their hideout. I used to run with them myself, but took a job with Maven after they started hittin' a rough patch. If you want to get in on that action, find [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) in the marketplace. I'm sure he could use someone like you."* **Any news involving the Dark Brotherhood?** *"The only thing I heard is a rumor that a boy named [Aventus Aretino](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino), in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), has been attempting to contact the Dark Brotherhood. He used to live here in Riften at the [Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) on the other side of town but I think he ran away. Can't say I blame him."* Maul will then take his spot at the Riften dockside and will prove a lot less talkative than promised, although his greetings will differ depending on your progress within the Thieves Guild quest line:

*"What did you want?"* (If successfully passed the persuasion)
*"Get in good with Brynjolf, then we'll talk."* (during [A Chance Arrangement](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Chance_Arrangement))
*"You want to talk? Finish those jobs for Brynjolf first."* (During [Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md))
*"Dirge told me you made it into the Guild. You need dirt on anything, you talk to me."* (After starting [Loud and Clear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Loud_and_Clear))
*"What did you want to know?"* / *"Back again? What is it this time?"* (If you are a member of the Thieves Guild)

When he moves into Mistveil Keep, Maul will be a bit more talkative:

**Tell me your thoughts on the war.** *"Hey, if the Empire and the Stormcloaks want to kill each other over some foolish cause, I say let 'em. All I care about is two things: watchin' Maven's back and countin' the gold she pays me to do it."* **I take it you protect the Jarl?** *"As long as she continues to keep me at her side, she can count on my blade to protect her. To be honest, I doubt someone would be stupid enough to make an attempt on Maven... sorry, Jarl Maven's life. But just in case, I'll be ready for them."* When you exit conversation:

*"Be seein' you around."*
## Conversations
In Mistveil Keep, Maul will still take care of Maven's business, both official and unofficial:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-npc-Maul_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Maul_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul_03.jpg) *"I've spoken to Brynjolf and he assures me it'll be taken care of."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-npc-Maul_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Maul_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Maul_04.jpg) *"Your mother seems to have taken to her seat quite well."* **Maven**: *"Any word about our little problem in the Ratway?"*
**Maul**: *"I've spoken to Brynjolf and he assures me it'll be taken care of."*
**Maven**: *"Next time you speak to him, tell him to clean up his own messes. I'm not about to jeopardize this position for a bunch of low-life thugs."*
**Maul**: *"I'll let him know."*

**Maul**: *"My agents are telling me that we've appropriated the caravan from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)... with minimal losses."*
**Maven**: *"For what it's costing me, I should be handed the merchandise on a silver platter."*
**Maul**: *"The coin isn't just for the men, there's a lot of people that need to be compensated in order to make this happen."*
**Maven**: *"And a hefty cut for yourself too I'm sure. Just see to it the items are secured and the rest disposed of properly this time."*

Maul will occasionally talk to Hemming about the questionable promotion of Maven:

**Maul**: *"Your mother seems to have taken to her seat quite well."*
**Hemming**: *"Yes, she's been grooming herself for this for quite a long time."*
**Maul**: *"It's surprising the Council is allowing a Nord rule Riften under the Imperial flag. That can't be their first choice."*
**Hemming**: *"Let's just say my mother knows which pockets to line and keep it at that."*

Court wizard [Wylandriah](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md) will often face problems with Hemming Black-Briar's abusive behavior and will try and enlist Maul to help her out:

**Wylandriah**: *"Maul, can I ask a personal favor of you?"*
**Maul**: *"Well that depends on what the favor is, now doesn't it."*
**Wylandriah**: *"It's Hemming. He's making advances and I can't bear the man's presence. Is there anything you can do?"*
**Maul**: *"I'll talk to him, but he's stubborn. He's like his mother; he sees what he wants and he won't quit until he has it."*
**Wylandriah**: *"Anything you can do to keep his filthy hands off of me would be a welcome change."*

## Notes
- An alternative greeting was recorded for Maul, but never made it into the game:

*"The name's Maul; I watch the streets for the Black-Briars. If you need dirt on anything, I'm your guy."* - Despite not technically being a Thieves Guild member, he still belongs to the Thieves Guild faction. This is because he was a former member of the Thieves Guild.
- If you fail any of the speech checks, he will refuse to speak to you unless you join the Thieves Guild.
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads:

*"Maul is a close friend of Hemming Black-Briar; when he was ambushed by bandits. Maul dove into the fray to save him. Ever since then, they have been partners in crime, with Maul assisting Hemming in his more nefarious activities assigned by Maven Black-Briar. He does not live within Riften, as he wants his identity to remain anonymous. Maul's brother is Dirge, part of the security at the Ragged Flagon."* - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) With version 3.0.4 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), Maul will no longer display a topic to ask about the Unusual Gems if you already entered the Thieves Guild and talked to Vex in No Stone Unturned.
- Despite the claim that he "doesn't live within Riften", Maul's residence of choice seems to be the Black-Briar Manor within the city.

I'd just mess everything up."* - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, fixes this bug.
