# Tang Mo (place)

Tang Mo (meaning the **Thousand Monkey Isles**) is a nation of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). It is inhabited by many breeds of monkey-folk, collectively known as the [Tang Mo](Lore_Tang_Mo_(race).md). The monkey-folk are kind, brave and simple, but can raise armies when they must, as all other Akaviri nations have tried to enslave them at one point. Every year, the [demons](Lore_Kamal_(race).md) of [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(place).md) thaw out after winter and attempt to invade Tang Mo, but the monkey-folk always successfully repel them. They are enemies of the [snake-folk](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) of [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(place).md), but have become allies of their once-bitter enemies, the [tiger-folk](Lore_Ka_Po%27_Tun_(race).md) of [Ka Po' Tun](Lore_Ka_Po%27_Tun_(place).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
