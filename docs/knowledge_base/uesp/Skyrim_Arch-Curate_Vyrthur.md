[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.jpg) Arch-Curate Vyrthur [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arch-Curate_Vyrthur_02.jpg) *"Your pathetic powers are no match for mine!"* **Arch-Curate Vyrthur** is a [Snow Elf](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) located in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). He is the brother of [Knight-Paladin Gelebor](Skyrim_Knight-Paladin_Gelebor.md) and the only other uncorrupted [Snow Elf](Lore_Falmer.md). He created the [prophecy](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md) which [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) is chasing in the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) questline, after having been forsaken to [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) by [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md). He sought revenge through the corruption of Auri-El's influence on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) —his [bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) —which would have been achieved by dousing the artifact in the blood of a Daughter of Coldharbour, a being of the purest evil in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

He wears a set of ancient Falmer armor, including [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Falmer_Boots), [cuirass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Falmer_Cuirass), and [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Falmer_Gauntlets). He carries an [Elven Dagger of Blizzards](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger_of_Blizzards.md), as well as some random [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md) and [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md). He knows a number of spells, namely [Drain Life](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md), [Conjure Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md), [Vampiric Invisibility](Skyrim_Embrace_of_Shadows.md), [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md), [Icy Spear](Skyrim_Icy_Spear.md), [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md), [Banish Daedra](Skyrim_Banish_Daedra.md), and [Command Daedra](Skyrim_Command_Daedra.md).

## Dialogue
During the quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md), Gelebor asks you to kill Vyrthur in exchange for [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md). He is found sitting on a throne in [Auriel's Chapel](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Chapel.md). As you approach he will begin to speak to you about [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md).

- *"Did you really come here expecting to claim Auriel's Bow? You've done exactly as I predicted and brought your fetching companion to me. Which, I'm sorry to say, means your usefulness is at an end!"*

He will then release the [Frozen Chaurus](Skyrim_Frozen_Chaurus.md) to attack you. After defeating them, he will say:

- *"An impressive display, but a wasted effort. You delay nothing but your own deaths!"*

He will then use his power to pull down the ceiling and release half the [Frozen Falmer](Skyrim_Frozen_Falmer.md) to attack you:

- *"Finish them!"*

After defeating the Frozen Falmer, the now angry Vyrthur will say *"This has gone on long enough."* as he summons an [Ancient Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Ancient_Frost_Atronach.md) and releases the rest of the Frozen Falmer. During the fight, Serana remarks that his life will end here; however, he merely replies:

- *"Child, my life ended long before you were born!"*

After defeating the Falmer and atronach, a now fearful Vyrthur will say:

- *"No... I won't let you ruin centuries of preparations..."*

When Serana tells him to surrender and hand over the bow he will say:

- *"Death first!"*

He will then use all his power to destroy Auriel's Chapel with you inside and run outside to the Temple Balcony. Exhausted from using all of his power he will begin to talk to Serana, allowing him time to recover.

- **Serana**: *"Enough, Vyrthur. Give us the bow!"*
- **Vyrthur**: *"How dare you. I was the Arch-Curate of Auri-El, girl. I had the ears of a god!"*
- **Serana**: *"Until the "Betrayed" corrupted you. Yes, yes. We've heard this sad story."*
- **Vyrthur**: *"Gelebor and his kind are easily manipulated fools. Look into my eyes, Serana. You tell me what I am."*
- **Serana**: *"You're... you're a vampire? But Auriel should have protected you..."*
- **Vyrthur**: *"The moment I was infected by one of my own Initiates, Auri-El turned his back on me. I swore I'd have my revenge, no matter what the cost."*
- **Serana**: *"You want to take revenge... on a god?"*
- **Vyrthur**: *"Auri-El himself may have been beyond my reach, but his influence on our world wasn't. All I needed was the blood of a vampire and his own weapon, Auriel's Bow."*
- **Serana**: *"The blood of a vampire... Auriel's Bow... It... it was you? You created that prophecy?"*
- **Vyrthur**: *"A prophecy that lacked a single, final ingredient... the blood of a pure vampire. The blood of a Daughter of Coldharbour."*
- **Serana**: *"You were waiting... all this time for someone with my blood to come along. Well, too bad for you... I intend on keeping it. Let's see if your blood has any power to it!"*

Recovering enough to resume the fight, he will once again attack. As you kill him, his final words will be *"Farewell."* or *"Auri-El, forgive me..."*

## Notes
- His editor ID reveals his full name to be "Althadan Vyrthur".
- In the game files, he is part of a newly-created race known as `Snow Elf Race`. It is largely based off of the [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) race, and even bears the name "High Elf", but for all purposes Vyrthur can be considered to be a [Snow Elf](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md). - The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* classifies him as the only member of the "Unique Falmer" section under the larger Falmer bestiary entry.
- Despite being a vampire, Vyrthur is still susceptible to poisons. This is because he is not actually a vampire as recognized by the game code: vampires of each race are treated as separate variants of the normal NPC races, and there is no proper vampire variant of the Snow Elves.
- Despite being a Snow Elf, Vyrthur's blood cannot be harvested for use in the [Discerning the Transmundane](Skyrim_Discerning_the_Transmundane.md) quest.
- Since [Knight-Paladin Gelebor](Skyrim_Knight-Paladin_Gelebor.md) cannot be killed or pickpocketed, Vyrthur has the only set of [Ancient Falmer Armor](Skyrim_Falmer_Equipment.md#Falmer_Armor) that you can retrieve in the game. - With version 2.0.2 of the [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch) and up, he additionally wears the Ancient Falmer Crown, which is also otherwise unobtainable.
