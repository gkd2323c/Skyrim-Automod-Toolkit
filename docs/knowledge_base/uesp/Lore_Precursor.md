The Precursor was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) prophet who became active during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3V-1)</sup> The Precursor preached that the old Forest God [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) was returning with new gifts for his people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3V-1)</sup> His rise was concurrent with other impactful [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) events—the first ever complete rooting of the walking tree city [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md), and the first [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) in several centuries—although it is unknown if these were unrelated or all a sign of a large shift to come for the Bosmer people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE3V-1)</sup>
