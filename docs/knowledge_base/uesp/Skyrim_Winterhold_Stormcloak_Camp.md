[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-place-Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold_Stormcloak_Camp.jpg) Winterhold Stormcloak Camp **Winterhold Stormcloak Camp** is a [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [camp](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Camps.md) in [Winterhold Hold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md), located northeast of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and south of [Snow Veil Sanctum](Skyrim_Snow_Veil_Sanctum.md).

This camp will only be present if the [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) hold [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) and the rest of Winterhold Hold, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) are camped here under the command of [Kai Wet-Pommel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kai_Wet-Pommel), except during the related quest, when [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) takes command. Like similar military camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has no [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). Unlike most Stormcloak camps, it has no [Stormcloak quartermaster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Quartermaster).

## Winterhold Stormcloak Camp
It is a typical Stormcloak camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. Beside the campfire is an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md). Around the campfire are four small tents containing unowned hay piles. Next to one of the small tents is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block), and next to another is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). West of the campfire, next to a stone pillar, is the hospital tent, which contains three hay piles occupied by wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). To the right of the hospital tent is a table with a built-in [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md). Up the hill from the hospital tent is the commander's tent, containing an owned bedroll, an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), and an end table. To the right of the commander's tent are some barrels. Up the hill from the commander's tent is the command tent, containing a lit brazier, a dresser, and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the commander spends most of their time.

Any time [Winterhold Hold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) is controlled by the Stormcloaks, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Legion](Skyrim_Joining_the_Legion.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents.

## Notes
- A [Shrine of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Arkay) can be found at this location if the camp isn't present.
- An [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) and a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein) can be found southeast and northeast of this camp, respectively.
- This wiki's current Skyrim satellite map displays the shrine; the previous one displayed the military camp.
