[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-item-Nightingale_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor.jpg) Nightingale Armor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Male.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Male.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Male.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Male.jpg) A male [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) in Nightingale Armor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Female.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Female.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Female.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Armor_Female.jpg) A female [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) in Nightingale Armor Nightingale Armor is part of a set of light armor given to you for joining the [Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). Its enchantment grants a [Fortify Stamina](Skyrim_Fortify_Stamina.md) and [Resist Frost](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md) effect. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Nightingale Armor requires [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. When wearing the armor, guards may comment *"That armor... it's like nothing I've ever seen. Like forged midnight..."*

The full set of Nightingale equipment also includes [boots](Skyrim_Nightingale_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Nightingale_Gloves.md), and a [hood](Skyrim_Nightingale_Hood.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Trinity Restored](Skyrim_Trinity_Restored.md)**: Become one of the Nightingales, [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md)'s guardians.

## Notes
- This Light Armor set is found during the Thieves Guild questline, as reward for the quest [Trinity Restored](Skyrim_Trinity_Restored.md). Tempering the pieces requires one sample of [Void Salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) each and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. Tempering is not augmented by any smithing perk.
- Wearing a full set of Nightingale Armor gives you a 25 point armor bonus from the [Nightingale Armor Full Set](Skyrim_Nightingale_Armor_Full_Set.md) ability.
- The level that the armor registers is the level you are at the start of the mission [Trinity Restored](Skyrim_Trinity_Restored.md), not the moment the armor is received.

## Bugs
- Even with the proper perks and items required, sometimes the armor won't let you temper it at all.
- If you have Fine-Cut Void Salts in your inventory, it may cause all of your regular Void Salts to be unusable to temper the Nightingale Armor. After you dispose of the Fine-Cut Void Salts, it is again possible to temper the armor.
