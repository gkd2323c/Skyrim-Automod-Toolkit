[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Pass.jpg) Shrine at Wayward Pass **Wayward Pass** is a [pass](Skyrim_Passes.md) through the mountains in the southwest of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md). It is the resting place of an ancient traveler, adorned by a [shrine of Arkay](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Arkay.md).

The pass is halfway between [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) and [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md). The entrance and exit of [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md) are on either side of the pass, but there are no paths to them.

At the crest of the hill, you will find an altar surrounded by five other graves, some with floral placements in front of them. Atop the altar you will find an [amulet of Arkay](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Arkay.md), an [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md), the [Ancient Traveler's Skull](Skyrim_Ancient_Traveler%27s_Skull.md) (appears as a regular skull), a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), and two [linen wraps](Skyrim_Linen_Wrap.md). In front of it, there are a [steel shield](Skyrim_Steel_Shield.md) and a pair of [iron boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md).
