# Agacephs

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) Black Marsh, the homeland of the Agacephs Agacephs are a breed of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) that live in [Middle Argonia](Lore_Middle_Argonia.md), in the inner parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), close to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup> Most Argonians share a basic morphological pattern but differences between different kinds of Argonians can range from minimal to significant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> Agacephs have some distinct differences in their appearance compared to the Argonians most commonly seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Most of the Agacephs Argonians have needle-like faces and vary wildly in colors, having a whole rainbow of colors, including bright greens and oranges, some Agacephs are lemon-colored or ruby-red. They possess whip-like tails which they use to help steer the rafts they use to travel. They care little for their imperialized brethren and find little of the world outside Black Marsh worth seeing, but they are kinder to outsiders than other inner tribes, such as the [Nagas](Lore_Naga.md), [Paatru](Lore_Paatru.md), and [Sarpa](Lore_Sarpa.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAA4-1)</sup>
