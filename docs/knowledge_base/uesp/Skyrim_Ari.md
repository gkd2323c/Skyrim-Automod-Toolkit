[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SR-npc-Ari.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ari.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ari.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ari.jpg) Ari's corpse **Ari** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) that was killed by [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) when her party chased a [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) sow into [Moss Mother Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern). After [Niels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Niels) was ambushed and almost instantly killed, she took off running before being overcome. The only survivor of their party was [Valdr](Skyrim_Valdr.md) who requests that you help avenge his fallen comrades. If you help Valdr, he will bury Ari's corpse.

Ari wears [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md) (either [fur](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), [hide](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), or [studded](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor)), with either [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) or [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), and a 50% chance of a pair of bracers (either [fur](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) or [hide](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md)). She carries a [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) (either [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) or [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md)) with a 20% chance of a shield (either [hide](Skyrim_Hide_Shield.md) or [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md)), along with a small amount of gold. She may also carry other minor items and loot.
