[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator.jpg/200px-SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator.jpg) The Sky Orchestrator [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Sky_Orchestrator_02.jpg) The Orchestrator in its frost stage The Sky Orchestrator is a large [Dwarven Spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) found in [Vardnknd Skykiller Gallery](Skyrim_Runoff_Caverns.md#Vardnknd_Skykiller_Gallery), the final section of [Runoff Caverns](Skyrim_Runoff_Caverns.md). It is controlling the malfunctioning Weather Witch and needs to be destroyed to prevent the storm within the caverns from turning into a province-wide natural disaster.

You will first encounter the Orchestrator in the [Vardnknd Gallery](Skyrim_Runoff_Caverns.md#Vardnknd_Gallery) section of the dungeon. It can be seen at the top of the stairs leading to the Skykiller Gallery when you first enter the chamber. However, as soon as you approach, the Orchestrator will turn and retreat inside the Skykiller Gallery, causing the stairs to sink into the floor and the door to be sealed behind an impassable gate. You will need to progress the quest to follow it.

The Sky Orchestrator can be a challenging fight and possesses several stages. Initially the Orchestrator will enter frost mode and deal Ao E frost damage. This will then switch to fire mode, at which point the Orchestrator will be doing fire damage and summoning [Enraged Spriggans](Skyrim_Enraged_Spriggan.md). Finally, it will enter lightning mode and deal significant shock damage until it is destroyed.

The Orchestrator's remains can be looted for the [Ward of the Seasons](Skyrim_Ward_of_the_Seasons.md), a unique Dwarven cuirass. It is the final boss of the dungeon and its defeat will allow you to complete the related quest.
