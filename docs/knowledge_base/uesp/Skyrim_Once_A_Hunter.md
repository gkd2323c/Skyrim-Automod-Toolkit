## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read [Guard Dossier: Aesrael](Skyrim_Guard_Dossier__Aesrael.md) or [Guard's Bounty Letter Draft](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Bounty_Letter_Draft.md) at the [Falkreath Barracks](Skyrim_Falkreath_Barracks.md).
2. Go to [Aesrael](Skyrim_Aesrael.md)'s camp near [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) and obtain his key.
3. Open Aesrael's chest and pick up the Elven Hunter Armor.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter.jpg) Rifle through papers in the guard barracks. [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Once_a_Hunter_03.jpg) Aesrael's camp To begin this quest travel to the [Falkreath Barracks](Skyrim_Falkreath_Barracks.md). Once there, read *[Guard Dossier: Aesrael](Skyrim_Guard_Dossier__Aesrael.md)* or *[Guard's Bounty Letter Draft](Skyrim_Guard%27s_Bounty_Letter_Draft.md)* which will grant you the quest and direct you to the forests west of [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md).

Once there, locate [Aesrael](Skyrim_Aesrael.md)'s camp. You must obtain [Aesrael's Key](Skyrim_Aesrael%27s_Key.md) from him, either by pickpocketing or killing him. Note that both Aesrael and his pet wolf [Dusk](Skyrim_Dusk.md) will be hostile.

After the key is obtained, use it to open the chest near his tent, which will contain the [Elven Hunter Armor](Skyrim_Elven_Hunter_Armor.md), [Boots](Skyrim_Elven_Hunter_Boots.md), and [Gauntlets](Skyrim_Elven_Hunter_Gauntlets.md), as well a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v7](Lore_The_Crimson_Dirks,_v7.md)* and some leveled loot.

## Notes
- The Fishing side quest [Clear-Headed](Skyrim_Clear-Headed.md) overlaps here in the barracks.
- The location of Aesrael's camp overlaps with the final location of the [Redguard Elite Armaments](Skyrim_Redguard_Elite_Armaments.md) creation that is at the entrance to Sunderstone Gorge. So his wolf "Dusk" may attack while doing that quest.
- This is the lightest armor in the game.
- After the quest is completed, pieces of Elven Hunter Armor can appear in leveled lists of loot (such as the leveled cuirass in the last chamber of [Shroud Hearth Barrow](Skyrim_Shroud_Hearth_Barrow.md#Shroud_Hearth_Depths)). It will also be added to the inventory of [Blacksmiths](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md), who will begin selling pieces of Elven Hunter Armor. In contrast to most other alternative armors, it will not appear in the crafting menu, merchant chests, or the inventory of NPCs before this.
