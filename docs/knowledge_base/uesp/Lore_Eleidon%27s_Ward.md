# Eleidon's Ward

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg/200px-MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Eleidon%27s_Ward.jpg) Eleidon's Ward (Morrowind) Eleidon's Ward is an enchanted shield especially built for [Eleidon](Lore_Eleidon.md), a renowned holy [Breton](Lore_Breton.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-1)</sup><sup>:241</sup> knight of legend. One of the many stories about him is that he saved a baron's daughter from sure death at the hands of a warlord, and the baron spent all his riches on the creation of the shield to show his gratitude. Eleidon's eponymous shield grants the wielder the opportunity to heal wounds.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup>

In the [Fifth Century](Lore_First_Era.md#Fifth_Century) of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), in the midst of High King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md)'s reign in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the revered [Mordrin Hanin](Lore_Mordrin_Hanin.md) was entombed with Eleidon's Ward in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), in close proximity to [Ashurnabitashpi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ashurnabitashpi), southwest in the cave of Ibar-Dad.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HW-3)</sup>

[Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), at one point befriended the sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWYB-4)</sup> At Fyr’s behest, Yagrum Bagarn wrote and compiled centuries of personal notes into a book that would become known as *[Tamrielic Lore](Lore_Tamrielic_Lore.md)*,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-1)</sup><sup>:241</sup> listing Eleidon's Ward among the artifacts of "unimaginable significance". By the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), copies had spread across Tamriel, offering readers rare insights into these legendary items,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TA-5)</sup> and Fyr himself hoped to utilize this knowledge in his search for Eleidon's Ward and the other artifacts detailed in the book.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-MWFR-6)</sup>

Circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), [Elante of Alinor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elante_of_Alinor) and her [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) colleague Badama Andarys uncovered Mordrin Hanin's tomb within Ibar-Dad. The expedition ended in disaster when their summoning failed to conjure anything more than [scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) to aid their excavation, and the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) killed Badama.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWIbar-7)</sup> Elante managed to survive the encounter,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OBElante-8)</sup> but the notes she left behind were used to locate the tomb deep within the depths of Ibar-Dad. There, the Nerevarine defeated the tomb's guardians, and plundered Mordrin's tomb of Eleidon's Ward and the rest of his valuables. <sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWIbar-7)</sup> Later that year, the shield was sold to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md), who put it on display in her Museum of Artifacts in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tribunal-9)</sup>
