## Pale Imperial Camp
It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. There is a quartermaster's tent, with a long wooden table out front with various [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Weapons.md) and [steel weapons](Skyrim_Steel_Weapons.md) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) on top of the table and inside the tent. Next to this is the workstation for the [Imperial quartermaster](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md), consisting of a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md), and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). North of the campfire is a row of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. East of the campfire is the command tent, containing an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. Next to the command tent is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the right of the tent is a [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md). South of the campfire is the hospital tent, which contains three bed rolls occupied by two wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](Skyrim_Potion_of_Health.md) and a random [potion of healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md). Just outside the tent, on the right is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), and to the left are some [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels with a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) leaning against them. To the right of this tent are two picketed [horses](Skyrim_Horses.md). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing.

Any time [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes
- There is a [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md) vein northeast of the camp.
