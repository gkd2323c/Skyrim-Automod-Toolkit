Faded Diary *The diary of a refugee from Morrowind who never found safety*
---
...another dream of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) erupting. People running as flaming rocks the size of cantons fell from the sky...

...can still see my brother's outstretched arm, as he tried to reach the [silt strider](Lore_Silt_Strider.md) and walked right out into the boiling waters...

...not just [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), all of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) was hit by the rocks. There's no work and no food will grow under the ashes. We are going to try for [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)...
