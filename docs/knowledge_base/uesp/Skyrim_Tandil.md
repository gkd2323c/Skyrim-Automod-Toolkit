[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Tandil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tandil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tandil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tandil.jpg) Tandil **Tandil**, a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [sorcerer](Skyrim_CombatSorcerer.md), is a member of the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) who arrives at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) along with [Quaranir](Skyrim_Quaranir.md) and [Gelebros](Skyrim_Gelebros.md) to secure [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md).

He wears a full set of [Psiijic robes](Skyrim_Psiijic_Robes.md), which includes the [hood](Skyrim_Psiijic_Hood.md), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Psiijic_Boots.md), and a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Psiijic_Gloves.md).

Should you approach him, Tandil will only say, *"It is time for us to go."*
