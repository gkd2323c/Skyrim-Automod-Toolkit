[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-npc-Leyathil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Leyathil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leyathil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leyathil.jpg) Leyathil **Leyathil** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). She's behind the locked door and can't be interacted with before talking with the [Waiter](Skyrim_Waiter.md). If she survives the dinner, she can later be found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) at [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), but in fact she will be disabled after walking out of [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
She has two dialogue options, but you can't normally access them for they are only available before talking with the [Waiter](Skyrim_Waiter.md) and she's behind a locked door that is only unlocked once you speak with the Waiter.

- **Are you here for the dinner party?**
- *"Of course."*

- **Do you think the food will be any good?**
- *"Of course."*

If you save her from the vampires:

- *"I suppose thanks are in order."*
