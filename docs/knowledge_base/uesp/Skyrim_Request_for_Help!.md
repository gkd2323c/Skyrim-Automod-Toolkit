Request for Help! **by [Madena](Skyrim_Madena.md)**
---
Lady [Mjoll](Skyrim_Mjoll_the_Lioness.md),

I hope this letter finds safe passage to your hands. We are in desperate need of help and we have nowhere else to turn. Everyone in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) is having nightmares. These horrible visions plague our slumber and leave us feeling frightened and tired even after a full night's sleep. I myself have awoken from one of the dreams bathed in cold sweat after having one of these nightmares. The things that I saw were simply too appalling to describe. There's a priest of Mara that claims he might be able to help us, but so far, he simply sits within the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) scribbling notes. If you could find it in your heart to make the journey to our city and help us, I would forever be in your debt.

Madena
