# Order of the Albatross

[![Order of the Albatross Banner](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/ON-banner-Order_of_the_Albatross.png/45px-ON-banner-Order_of_the_Albatross.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Order_of_the_Albatross.png) The Order of the Albatross was a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) active in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup> These swashbuckling warriors were often singers or bards but they also tended to engage in piracy.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Isobel-2)</sup> Fontenot Leila founded the order to give those children of nobility who could not look forward to their parent's inheritance a means of securing their own legacies. Their numbers, both in active knights and percentage of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) explored, greatly exceeded that of other knightly orders based in the archipelago. This was made possible by the order's focus on adventure, discovery, and daring feats, which drew initiates from the mainland.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup>.

The Sapphire Tournament, hosted by [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md) at Castle Navire,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> serves as a proving ground for squires seeking entry into the Systrean knightly orders. The Order of the Albatross is one of the three orders that take part, with the other two being the [Order of the Iron Knot](Lore_Order_of_the_Iron_Knot.md) and the [Oaken Order](Lore_Oaken_Order.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGST-4)</sup> Each sets its own distinctive trial. Participation earns favor, though each order selects only one or two aspirants for knighthood. Most contenders attempt a single challenge, but those who succeed in all three are granted a powerful boon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Isobel-2)</sup>
