The **Thieves Guild** is the headquarters of [the Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) located with the sewers beneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), known as [The Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md).

The headquarters are based within [The Ragged Flagon - Cistern](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern.md) and a bar named [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). For full details on these places, see their individual pages.

## Walkthrough

### Thieves Guild Headquarters
There are two entrances to the headquarters: one via [the Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) into [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md), and a second directly into [The Ragged Flagon - Cistern](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon_-_Cistern.md) hidden beneath a tomb that dominates the small graveyard to the east of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)'s [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Riften).md).

You can only access the second entrance once you have joined the guild and completed [Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md). To enter the headquarters using this entrance, press a small button in the center of the tomb; this opens a secret stone staircase. Descend it and open the sewer covering. To exit directly from the Cistern, climb the ladder in the alcove to the southwest, then pull a chain to retract the tomb.

As you progress the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) [questline](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Quests), complete reputation quests for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md), and complete miscellaneous guild quests, the headquarters will be upgraded, and additional thieves will move in.

## Notes
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) An extra room is added to the Flagon by the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch). This room is located to the left of the door leading to [the Ratway Vaults](Skyrim_The_Ratway_Vaults.md). It is mainly filled with beds for those who live in the Flagon.
- The special features within the Flagon only appear as the extra merchants move in after completing the required reputation quests.
- There is a "Guild" [Shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the left of the entrance door from the Ratway Vaults.
