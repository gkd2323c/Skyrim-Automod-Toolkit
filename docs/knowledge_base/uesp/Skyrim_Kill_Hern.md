## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to [Half-Moon Mill](Skyrim_Half-Moon_Mill.md).
2. Kill [Hern](Skyrim_Hern.md) the vampire.
3. Return to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Hern is a fairly easy kill, even if he is a vampire. He is usually with his wife in Half-Moon Mill's only home. They can both be killed with any weapons—as long as Hern dies, the mission completes. During the day, you'll find him outside, hanging out on a bridge. You can sneak into a shack next to the bridge and attack him safely with a ranged attack from an open window. His wife usually won't notice if it's a one-hit kill. You could also wait until morning for his wife to leave for the lumber. She always leaves before him, presenting a window where he can be found alone inside the house. If you have [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) installed, and her disposition is high enough for her to give you free lumber, you can ask her for free lumber when they are together inside the house; she will go outside to supervise your cutting of logs, and you will be alone with Hern. Also when they are entering or leaving the house if you engage Hern in conversation and hold him behind you can then sneak attack and kill him after Hert leaves the area.

Alternatively, like most other assassination targets, you have the option to talk to Hern which gives you three dialogue choices, two of which will turn Hern hostile.

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *"I know what you are, nightspawn."* | *"Well then, you won't be leaving here alive!"* | Becomes hostile and attacks |
| *"Assassin versus vampire. A fight for the ages."* | *"Ah, so the Brotherhood has come for old Hern, has it? Well then, let us meet as equals."* | Becomes hostile and attacks |
| *(Remain silent)* | *"Hmph. Don't say much, but there's a strange aura about you. You walk in the shadow of death... Leave here. Leave here and never return."* | Hern walks away |

### Reward
| Levels | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| 1–9 | 250 |
| 10–19 | 400 |
| 20–29 | 500 |
| 30–39 | 600 |
| 40+ | 750 |

Note that there is no reward for killing Hert, although if she witnesses you killing Hern, you can kill her to avoid the bounty.

## Notes
- Attacking Hern can incur a bounty, just like any other assault. Speaking to him will provide two dialogue options that will cause him to attack you, preventing a bounty. Either that, or kill any last witness (usually his wife).
