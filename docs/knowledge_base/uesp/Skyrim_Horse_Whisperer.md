## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to a [stables](Skyrim_Stables.md).
2. Purchase a map and book for 250 gold.
3. *(Optional)* Track down and [tame](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC) all seven wild horses.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Horse Tamer
Upon reading *[Soran's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soran%27s_Journal)* located on [Urag gro-Shub's](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) desk in [the Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum), this quest will automatically begin and will direct you to the nearest [stables](Skyrim_Stables.md).<sup>[*verification needed — how is this selected?*]</sup> You will need to speak with the hostler, i.e. the NPC who sells [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). (NOTE: I received the opportunity at the Whiterun Stables in a new game to buy the map without performing any of the actions previously outlined.) You will be presented with the option to purchase a wild horse map for 250 gold. You will receive both a [Map of Wild Horses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Map_of_Wild_Horses) and a book called *[Wild Horse Notes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse_Notes)*. In conjunction with reading the book, the map can be examined in your inventory in order to locate seven [wild horses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse).

Upon making this purchase, this quest will conclude. However, you will then receive seven miscellaneous objectives tasking you with locating and [taming](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC) every wild horse. The objectives themselves are rather useless as they do not provide a map marker for the individual horses. Instead, you will need to use the map and book to travel to the approximate location and search for the horse. The book itself advises that [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) will be useful in carrying out this task. Note that wild horses will flee if you get too close, so it may help to sneak up on them.

To tame a wild horse, you must remain mounted on the horse while it bucks. It has a chance to ragdoll you when bucking, which will force you to chase down the fleeing horse once you get back on your feet. [Sneaking](Skyrim_Sneak.md) can help prevent the horse from fleeing too far. Once mounted for a sufficient length of time, the horse becomes an owned mount. If you switch to a different mount, it will slowly make its way back to the location you originally found it.

### Find and tame the Black Horse
This horse can be found northwest of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), just south of the map marker for [Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md). The book refers to nearby ruins, the ruins in question being Cracked Tusk Keep. This location is close to a [necromancer](Skyrim_Necromancer.md)'s ritual site, and the resident necromancer might interrupt the taming process if you get too close.

### Find and tame the Chestnut Horse
This horse can be found east of [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), along the path leading towards [Falkreath Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Falkreath_Stormcloak_Camp.md). It's off of the north side of the road, just under halfway from Helgen to the Falkreath Stormcloak Camp.

### Find and tame the Dapple Brown Horse
This horse can be found in the forest south of [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md), across the river but before you reach the [dragon mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mound.md). This can obviously be a dangerous place to tame a horse if the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) is still nearby.

### Find and tame the Grey Spotted Horse
This horse can be found up the hill to the north of [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md). Beware the nearby [sabre cats](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md) who will attack you and the horse if you get too close.

### Find and tame the Pale Mare
This horse can be found among a group of snowy pines just south of [Yngol Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_Barrow).

### Find and tame the Red Horse
This horse can be found on the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md), northwest of [Whiterun Stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables). It is a very easy mount to locate and tame, particularly at the start of the game.

### Find and tame the White Spotted Horse
This horse can be found directly west of [Stony Creek Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave), west of the road, on a green cliff overlooking the volcanic tundra below.

## Notes
- Each wild horse can be [named](Skyrim_Horses.md#Horse_Naming CC) and [saddled](Skyrim_Horses.md#Saddles CC), which are two other mechanics added by this Creation. To do this, you must visit a stable with the horse as your active mount.
- Horses on even slight inclines will sometimes throw you further than expected, possibly causing significant damage or killing you.
