[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Silus_Vesuius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silus_Vesuius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silus_Vesuius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silus_Vesuius.jpg) Silus Vesuius in his museum **Silus Vesuius**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [destruction mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Destruction), is a citizen of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and the curator of the [Museum of the Mythic Dawn](Skyrim_Silus_Vesuius%27s_House.md), which is just a few artifacts and objects relating to the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) in display cases inside his home. The reason he created the museum is because he is a part of the family bloodline which was a part of the Mythic Dawn, a cult which attempted to bring the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) to Tamriel, during the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) 200 years earlier. Talking to him completes a quest to [visit the museum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Visit_the_museum_in_Dawnstar), received via a courier's note. He speaks reverently of the Mythic Dawn, despite their insidious background, and asks you collect the pieces of the dismantled [Mehrunes' Razor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mehrunes%27_Razor), a powerful weapon.

Despite being a proud descendant of a Mythic Dawn agent and an expert in lore surrounding Mehrunes Dagon, his plan to restore Mehrunes' Razor begins to flounder. Upon gathering the pieces you will travel with Silus to a nearby shrine to the Daedric Prince where Silus attempts to summon him, in vain. But when you try to summon Mehrunes Dagon, he reveals that he has no interest in helping Silus repair the Razor at all and that Silus' life must be taken if the Razor is to be restored, prompting a brief fight between you and Silus. After Silus' death, the Prince of Destruction gladly repairs the blade and "tests your strength".

However, if you decide to spare Silus, Dagon summons two Dremora to dispatch him. If you defeat them before they kill him, he will return to his museum with the shards of the razor, and will remain friendly to you although access to the razor will be denied to you forever onward.

Silus wears [Mythic Dawn robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Robes), a pair of [Mythic Dawn gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Gloves), and a pair of [Mythic Dawn boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn_Boots). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) quality, and carries a selection of [upper-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Notes

- Despite what Silus says about the cult, the Mythic Dawn still appears as active in *[The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)*<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Cause.md)</sup>. This fact can't be discussed with Silus.
