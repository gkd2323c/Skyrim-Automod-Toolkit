Fort Swampmoth is a fortress in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It was maintained by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) during the time of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md). During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), rumors swirled that the Legions had been recalled from Fort Swampmoth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBRumors-1)</sup>
