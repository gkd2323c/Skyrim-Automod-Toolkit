[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-place-Fort_Neugrad.jpg/200px-SR-place-Fort_Neugrad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Neugrad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Fort_Neugrad.jpg) Fort Neugrad circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Neugrad Watch was a small settlement in southwestern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), contemporary with the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> During the Simulacrum, Neugrad Watch was ruled by Lord Fenrens and had a rivalry with [North Keep](Lore_North_Keep.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) to the southwest, and [Oakwood](Lore_Oakwood.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup> It has been claimed that a group of "Wild [Automatons](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md)" destroyed the settlement circa [4E 100-105](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_100-105), with the refugees moving to [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-SINE-3)</sup> By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the settlement had been abandoned and all that remained were the ruins of **Fort Neugrad**, occupied by a small number of guards.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Because of avalanches in the [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) and the destruction of Helgen by [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md), a group of bandits successfully overran the fort in 4E 201. The old library was looted by Brandish, a member of the bandits, in search of magical items.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The bandits had intended to clear out before the Jarl of Falkreath could react, but the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) retook the fort for use as a strong point during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). However, morale was low and reinforcements were unable to make it through the closed Pale Pass.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The fort served as a point of contention between the Legion and the [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md), with the Stormcloaks vying for control of the fort in order to conquer Falkreath Hold.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
