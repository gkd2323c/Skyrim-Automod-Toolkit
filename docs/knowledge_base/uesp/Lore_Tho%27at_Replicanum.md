Tho'at Replicanum, also known as **the Living Glass**, the **Glass Knight**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IN-2)</sup> and **She Who Became Vivified Ink**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup> is a [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) known for her invasion of the [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). She was overall an enigma, for how and even why she came to the Archive was unknown, except that she was in search for something.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup> One theory proposed by the keeper of the Infinite Archive, Master [Malkhest](Lore_Malkhest.md), is that perhaps Tho'at has always been in the archive, though he quickly discarded that thought.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup> Though her origins were originally a mystery, what was initially known is that she transformed herself into a malleable being made entirely of ink.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It was later revealed that she is associated with the forgotten [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-5)</sup> and Tho'at herself resembles the glass-like Daedra from the realm of [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESO2024GR-7)</sup>

The danger Tho'at poses is exemplified by her power of bringing myths from books into reality.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup> The books of the Infinite Archive offered a host of ink to work with, which Tho'at stripped off the books and coalesced it into black voids which dripped into floor<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup> creating [Maligraphies](Lore_Maligraphy.md), [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) creatures composed of vivified ink which are pieces of Tho'at without individuality. They were created from fragments of her [nymic](Lore_Nymic.md) combined with the ink of the archive's tomes via Daedric [magic](Lore_Magic.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup> These forms can include mortals of different races and affiliations, other daedric entities, [animals](Lore_Bestiary.md), [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirits.md), [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), [shades](Lore_Shade.md), [celestials](Lore_Celestial.md), [undead](Lore_Undead.md), and even [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) or [clockwork](Lore_Factotum.md) [constructs](Lore_Construct.md). They can replicate not only the physical appearance of the forms they copy but also the accompanying equipment, including powerful [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Enemies-9)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Bosses-10)</sup> Each maligraphy's strength depended on the page they were created from, translating the power in the contents of the page to physical prowess.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup> Due to the nature of the archive, a potentially infinite amount of maligraphies may have formed, though they are normally unable to sustain themselves outside of the archive,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup> some have found a way to escape its confines.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Tho'at made her presence known and attacked the Infinite Archive. Master Malkhest looked for outsiders to put a stop to her, recruiting through the connections of [Gabrielle Benele](Lore_Gabrielle_Benele.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup> and utilizing [seekers](Lore_Seeker.md) that scouted potential adventurers.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Tho'at's Malagraphies proved to be a challenge, so the adventurer or two that chose to participate in saving the Archive were aided by augmentations provided directly from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md). The defeated maligraphies faded into puddles of ink.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAIA-6)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Enemies-9)</sup> In order to confront Tho'at Replicanum, the adventurers were tasked with defeating maligraphies to gather enough nymic fragments (or more accurately [paleonymic](Lore_Paleonymic.md) fragments) for Master Malkhest to summon an instance of the Daedra Lord and banish her.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-13)</sup> However, before she was slain, Tho'at created [Maligraphic](Lore_Maligraphy.md) copies of herself, replicating herself hundreds of times across the Infinite Archive. Though copies, the Tho'at replicas were considered entirely new entities and required the gathering a new nymic entirely. Thus, getting rid of Tho'at permanently required finding and banishing each and every copy of her by gathering the distinct nymic of each.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-15)</sup> At least one of her slayers of her copies was a member of the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md), who took her sword as a trophy.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-16)</sup> The daedra lord would continue evolving after every defeat and thus each encounter was harder than the last. The person that defeated the final evolution of Tho'at Replicanum was dubbed the "Inkslayer".<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-17)</sup>

Despite the final evolution's defeat, the Tho'at replicas and Maligraphies would continue to propagate through the Archive, and the Vestige and Malkhest would continue working to banish their spreading presence from the realm.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MM-3)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-18)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-19)</sup>
