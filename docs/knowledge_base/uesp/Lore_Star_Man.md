*And it was agreed. The Star Man led the Yokudans by ship, following the path of the Warrior across mountains and vast deserts. And victory followed them, and famine and desolation fled before them.*
— *[How the Yokudans Chased the Stars](Lore_How_the_Yokudans_Chased_the_Stars.md)*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTYCTS-1)</sup> Star Man was a legendary [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) who led his people on a journey following the [Constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) of [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md). He charted the movements of the stars and saw that when the Warrior constellation was high in the sky, victory followed, but when it disappeared, famine and desolation came. He convinced his kinsmen to follow the Warrior's path, and as they sailed across mountains and deserts, victory followed them while famine and desolation fled. They revered the Warrior's three charges: [the Lord](Lore_The_Lord.md), [the Lady](Lore_The_Lady.md), and [the Steed](Lore_The_Steed.md), offering them gifts and incense as tokens of respect and devotion. Led by the Star Man they reached their destination at the Warrior's apex, built a temple and tomb, and became eternal guardians among the stars. It is said that the location of the temple remains hidden, but those who seek it need only follow the Warrior's path.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTYCTS-1)</sup>
