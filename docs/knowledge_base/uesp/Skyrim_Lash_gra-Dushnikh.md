[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Lash_gra-Dushnikh.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lash_gra-Dushnikh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lash_gra-Dushnikh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lash_gra-Dushnikh.jpg) Lash gra-Dushnikh **Lash gra-Dushnikh** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) living in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md). She is originally from [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md). Apparently she left there on bad terms with her mother [Gharol](Skyrim_Gharol.md), as per her response to the completion of [Gharol's Message](Skyrim_Gharol%27s_Message.md). If you side with [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md) when completing the [Sanuarach Mine quest](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine_(quest).md), you will increase her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) and thus become [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) at [Orc strongholds](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) if you aren't already.

During your first time in Karthwasten, Lash will be with the other miners as a group of [Silver-Blood mercenaries](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Mercenary.md) take over the mine, after which she will resume her normal schedule: she sleeps in [Karthwasten Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) between midnight and 8am, then heads outside to work for twelve hours, ending her workday by relaxing in the barracks until her bedtime.

Lash wears a set of [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Miner%27s_Clothes.md#00080697) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and is equipped with a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md). She carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Karthwasten_Miners%27_Barracks.md) to the [miner's barracks](Skyrim_Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Sanuarach Mine
If you have yet to help [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md) reclaim his mine, she will remark on the problem:

- *"You should talk to Ainethach. He runs the mines. Used to anyway."*
- *"Ainethach needs to get rid of those Silver-Blood. Then maybe we can go back to work."*

If he is dead:

- *"Ainethach's dead, but that doesn't mean we can stop working."*

### The Heart of Dibella
During [The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md), asking Lash about a young girl in Karthwasten:

- **I'm looking for a young girl who lives around here.**
- *"I thought [Enmon](Skyrim_Enmon.md) and [Mean](Skyrim_Mena.md) had a little girl, but I haven't seen her in a while."*

### Gharol's Message
Upon bringing the sword to Lash:

- **Your mother Gharol wants you to have this sword.**
- *"So that's it, then. 'Don't come back.' Thank you. Here. You honored my mother's task and should be rewarded. Now, I need to be alone."*

## Conversations
Occasionally, she can be heard threatening [Belchimac](Skyrim_Belchimac.md) over his careless remarks:

- **Belchimac**: *"I don't like bunking with Orcs. It's not... clean."*
- **Lash**: *"If you want me to leave, you could challenge me to a fight. I promise to stop once you've lost an arm."*
- **Belchimac**: *"What I meant to say was... I love having an Orc here in the barracks. They're so... pleasant."*

- **Lash**: *"Why do your people claim the Reach is theirs? The Orcs have been in these lands just as long."*
- **Belchimac**: *"You wouldn't understand, Lash. Your people aren't smart enough for politics."*
- **Lash**: *"Is that so? I'll remember that next time we're in the mine tunnels... alone."*
- **Belchimac**: *"Did... did I say you weren't smart? I meant to say that I'm not smart, Lash. I'm just not smart enough to answer your questions."*

She will also have friendlier conversations with [Ragnar](Skyrim_Ragnar.md), but they will only be heard if you have not resolved the mine dispute in favor of Ainethach:

- **Lash**: *"I've been thinking, would you like to join me in hunting?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"You want me to hunt with you? I thought you preferred being alone."*
- **Lash**: *"Your arms and legs are fit for catching strong, agile prey. I could use your help."*
- **Ragnar**: *"I'd be honored, but I have work to do in the mines."*

- **Lash**: *"When will Sanuarach Mine be open?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"I don't know. Whenever those Silver-Blood men decide to leave."*

If you sided with Ainethach, Lash and Ragnar will have these conversations instead:

- **Ragnar**: *"I haven't seen you hunting in a while."*
- **Lash**: *"I can afford to buy meat now. I prefer working in the mines, anyway."*

- **Lash**: *"Tell me, Ragnar, what do you think of the Orcs?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"Cunning warriors and skilled workers. It's good to have you here, Lash."*
- **Lash**: *"I asked what you thought of the Orcs, not about me."*
- **Ragnar**: *"Oh. I'm sorry. Well, it's good to have you here, anyway."*

- **Lash**: *"You have strong arms. You'd make a good Orc."*
- **Ragnar**: *"Um... thank you, Lash. You'd... make a fine Nord?"*
- **Lash**: *"Don't say words you don't mean."*

## Notes
- A script attached to the quest Gharol's Message reveals that the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of Dushnikh Yal was originally known as **Shugurzbur** instead. This same script also reveals that Gharol's name was originally **Sharn**. - Interestingly, there are two internal references that refer to her by the name Lash gra- **Shugurz**. This implies that, just like her final name, her early name was also meant to reference her home stronghold.
- It is unknown what this means for the other two Orcs in the final game with the same possible demonym, siblings [Mulush gro-Shugurz](Skyrim_Mulush_gro-Shugurz.md) and [Urzoga gra-Shugurz](Skyrim_Urzoga_gra-Shugurz.md). It's unclear if they were also supposedly from Shugurzbur, or if they just got named after the stronghold was renamed, and no ties were meant to be implied.
