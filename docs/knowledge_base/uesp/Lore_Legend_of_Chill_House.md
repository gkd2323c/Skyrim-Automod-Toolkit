Legend of Chill House *A chilling note* *
---
Frigid air fills the darkness of Chill House as death's inevitable wind blows through the forlorn structure. Noises, familiar yet their source indeterminate, seem to rise from beneath the earth, bedeviling the listener with memories of childhood stories: Grome of the [Goblin-Ken](Lore_Goblin-ken.md), and The [Imp](Lore_Imp.md) with Two Hammers. I mustn't listen to the noises in Chill House—I mustn't! For if I listen too long and too closely, I might just…

Go mad.
