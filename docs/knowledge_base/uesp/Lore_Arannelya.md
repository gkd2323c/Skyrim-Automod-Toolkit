# Arannelya

| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) | Gender | Female |

Lady Arannelya was a [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) general during the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md). She led the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)'s invasion of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) while her counterpart, Lord [Naarifin](Lore_Naarifin.md), kept the [Imperial legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) occupied in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Her main force crossed western Cyrodiil, bypassing [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md), and rendezvoused with smaller Dominion forces that landed on Hammerfell's southern coast, quickly overrunning the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) defenses. Over the next two years, Arannelya unsuccessfully besieged [Hegathe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hegathe) and briefly captured [Skaven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skaven) before being driven back across the [Alik'r Desert](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alik%27r_Desert) by an army of [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) veterans and [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md). Although the campaign in Cyrodiil ended in [4E 175](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_175), the conflict continued in Hammerfell for another five years, during which time Lady Arannelya presumably continued to command the Dominion army.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TGW-1)</sup>
