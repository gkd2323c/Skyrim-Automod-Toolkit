### Adventurer's Campsite
[![SR-place-Adventurer's Campsite.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-place-Adventurer%27s_Campsite.jpg/200px-SR-place-Adventurer%27s_Campsite.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Adventurer%27s_Campsite.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Adventurer%27s_Campsite.jpg) - Chillwind Depths Exterior01

This campsite is down the path from [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md). The [Adventurer's Journal](Skyrim_Adventurer%27s_Journal.md) states that they were here to investigate the cause of recent caravan attacks. Three bedrolls are present.

### Ambushed Caravan
[![SR-place-Ambushed Caravan.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Ambushed_Caravan.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ambushed_Caravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ambushed_Caravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ambushed_Caravan.jpg) - POITundra22

Southeast of Dragon Bridge, on the side of the road, is a caravan loaded with crates, rugs, and a knapsack. Next to it are the corpses of a horse, a Nord man, and a Redguard woman, the latter carrying the [Merchant's Journal](Skyrim_Merchant%27s_Journal.md). [Falmer Arrows](Skyrim_Falmer_Arrow.md) stuck in the horse and more Falmer arrows and a [Falmer Ear](Skyrim_Falmer_Ear.md) lying around suggest a [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) attack, possibly related to the nearby [Chillwind Depths](Skyrim_Chillwind_Depths.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-92152&y=67427&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Black Arts Burial Ground
[![SR-place-Black Arts Burial Ground.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground.jpg/200px-SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground.jpg) [![SR-place-Black Arts Burial Ground 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Black_Arts_Burial_Ground_02.jpg) - POITundra18

Just outside of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), to the southeast, a [Nordic barrow](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) can be found, guarded by a leveled [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). Inside, a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Black Arts On Trial](Skyrim_The_Black_Arts_On_Trial.md)* rests atop a shelf. To the left, the body of a novice [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) with a dagger in his chest can be found in front of an open tomb, next to a table with a [Scroll of Turn Lesser Undead](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Turn_Lesser_Undead.md) on top of it. A [Staff of Revenants](Skyrim_Staff_of_Revenants.md) is located to the left of this table, on the floor. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-29831&y=60736&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Collapsed Burial Ground
[![SR-place-Collapsed Burial Ground.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-place-Collapsed_Burial_Ground.jpg/200px-SR-place-Collapsed_Burial_Ground.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Collapsed_Burial_Ground.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Collapsed_Burial_Ground.jpg) - POINorthern Coast09

West of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) and south of the [Abandoned Shack](Skyrim_Abandoned_Shack.md) is a Nordic ruin with two Draugr corpses, a novice-locked [dungeon chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), a burial urn and nightshade. The northern corpse contains the sneak skill book [*Sacred Witness*](Skyrim_Sacred_Witness.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-31349&y=88630&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dead Mammoth
[![SR-place-Dead Mammoth.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-place-Dead_Mammoth.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dead_Mammoth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dead_Mammoth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dead_Mammoth.jpg) - POITundra23

### Dragon Burial Mound
[![SR-place-Dragur Burial Mound.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound.jpg) [![SR-place-Dragur Burial Mound 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dragur_Burial_Mound_02.jpg) Southeast of [The Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md), there is a Nordic barrow that is partially submerged in the swamp, and is being guarded by five [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md). A chest with a novice-level lock and a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) skill book *[The Locked Room](Skyrim_The_Locked_Room.md)* can be found inside. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-48095&y=76564&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Ghost Barrow
[![SR-place-Ghost Barrow.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-place-Ghost_Barrow.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ghost_Barrow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ghost_Barrow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ghost_Barrow.jpg) File:SR-place-Ghost Barrow 02.jpg - POISnowy08

If you head over the hills southeast of [Hjaalmarch Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch_Stormcloak_Camp.md), you will come across a trap door grate set in a platform with a set of steps leading up to it. Opening the trap door will give access to a spiral staircase leading down to a sealed sarcophagus. If you come here between 8pm and 5am, either on your own or following the ghost that leads you here from [Kjenstag Ruins](Skyrim_Kjenstag_Ruins.md), you will find two leveled bandits in the process of looting the now-opened tomb. The tomb can then be looted for its leveled treasure: a necklace, an enchanted weapon, and a shield. One of the bandits carries a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill book *[A Dance in Fire, v2](Skyrim_A_Dance_in_Fire,_v2.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-5831.04&y=78534&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Hamvir’s Summit Hunter's Camp
[![SR-place-Harmvir's Summit Hunter's Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-place-Harmvir%27s_Summit_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Harmvir%27s_Summit_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Harmvir%27s_Summit_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Harmvir%27s_Summit_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg) - POISnowy06

North of Hamvir’s Rest, south of Labyrinthian, this camp can be easily accessed by heading east from Drelas’ Cottage. There are two hunters with tents and bedrolls.

### Karth River Henge
[![SR-place-Karth River Henge.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-place-Karth_River_Henge.jpg/200px-SR-place-Karth_River_Henge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karth_River_Henge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Karth_River_Henge.jpg) - POITundra21

East-southeast of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md), on a promontory in a bend of the river, you will find a [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) working at an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), which has a copy of the alchemy skill book [*Mannimarco, King of Worms*](Skyrim_Mannimarco,_King_of_Worms.md) placed on it. Next to the alchemy lab is a bowl containing [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md).

There is the body of a dead [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_(dead).md) in a ritual circle of candles. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-90753.7&y=79615.8&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Riverside Bandit Camp
[![SR-place-Riverside Bandit Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-place-Riverside_Bandit_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverside_Bandit_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Bandit_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverside_Bandit_Camp.jpg) - POITundra39

East of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md), there is a camp under a rocky outcropping inhabited by two bandits. There are two bedrolls around the campfire, as well as a novice-locked chest containing leveled loot. Right next to the chest lies a copy of the Block skill book *[Warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(book).md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-88178&y=82803&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Sabre Cat Rock
[![SR-place-Sabre Cat Rock.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/SR-place-Sabre_Cat_Rock.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sabre_Cat_Rock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sabre_Cat_Rock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sabre_Cat_Rock.jpg) - POITundra20

Northeast of [Broken Tower Redoubt](Skyrim_Broken_Tower_Redoubt.md) and west of [Robber's Gorge](Skyrim_Robber%27s_Gorge.md) is a pair of sabre cats guarding Nordic ruins and their dead victims including a Nord woman and two hunters. In the bloodied wreckage is a necklace, two [coin purses](Skyrim_Gold.md#Currency), a helm and a [satchel](Skyrim_Containers.md#Satchel). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-92997&y=46398&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Swamp Pond Massacre
[![SR-place-Swamp Pond Massacre.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-place-Swamp_Pond_Massacre.jpg/200px-SR-place-Swamp_Pond_Massacre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Swamp_Pond_Massacre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Swamp_Pond_Massacre.jpg) - POITundra25

North-northwest of [The Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md), there is a small uninhabited camp with two hay piles and a smoking firepit. A copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[The Exodus](Skyrim_The_Exodus.md)* can be found here. There is no other notable loot. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-58836&y=86714&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### The Conjurer's Caravan
[![SR-place-The Conjurer's Caravan.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan.jpg) [![SR-place-The Conjurer's Caravan 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Conjurer%27s_Caravan_02.jpg) - POISnowy03

Southwest of the [Great Lift at Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Mzinchaleft.md), northeast of [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md), you will come across a battle between a group of [conjurers](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) driving a horse-drawn wagon, and a group of [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md). The wagon contains a pair of crates holding a selection of [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) and [alchemical ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), two satchels containing more ingredients, and a copy of the Speech skill book *[A Dance in Fire, v6](Skyrim_A_Dance_in_Fire,_v6.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=1234&y=72609&zoom=17&legacy=true)
