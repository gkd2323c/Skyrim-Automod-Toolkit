Letter with Singed Edges **by [High Kinlady Estre](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Estre)** *A letter proposing an alliance to overthrow Queen Ayrenn*
---
To my lord [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md), King of Worms,

I am interested in coming to an arrangement in regards to the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). We share a common enemy in [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) and her [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). I won't stand in the way of your goals. I merely wish a small demesne of my own. [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), with [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) as my capital.

We're both intelligent, powerful creatures. I'm certain we can come to an arrangement that suits us both.

I eagerly await your response.

— [High Kinlady Estre](Lore_Estre.md) of the [Errinorne](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md)
