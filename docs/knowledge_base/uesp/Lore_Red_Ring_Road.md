[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/OB-map-Red_Ring_Road.jpg/200px-OB-map-Red_Ring_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Red_Ring_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Red_Ring_Road.jpg) Map showing the route of the Red Ring Road The Red Ring Road circles [Lake Rumare](Lore_Lake_Rumare.md) and the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).

The settlements of [Weye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Weye), [Aleswell](Lore_Aleswell.md), and [Pell's Gate](Lore_Pell%27s_Gate.md) are located along the road. It crosses several bridges, including an impressively large bridge which spans the [Upper Niben](Lore_Upper_Niben.md), as well as other smaller bridges which cross the [White Rose River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:White_Rose_River). Many [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) ruins and abandoned [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) forts also border the road. Due to its central location, the Red Ring Road connects to most other major roads in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TGRAMJ-2)</sup>
