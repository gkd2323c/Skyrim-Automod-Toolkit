# Seran

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/MW-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-MW-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Seran Ancestral Tomb [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-ON-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seran_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Seran Ancestral Tomb The Seran clan is an ancient family of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) that lives in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They are one of the thirty revered families of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATOTTRF-1)</sup> and are a sub-faction of [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSeran-2)</sup> The **Seran Ancestral Tomb** is located in the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) region, in close proximity to [Khuul](Lore_Khuul.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSeran-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWSeran-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png/200px-LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Temple_Conjurer.png) Methal Seran conjuring a Flame Atronach [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/OB-place-Seran_Camp.jpg/200px-OB-place-Seran_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Seran_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Seran_Camp.jpg) Seran Camp in Cyrodiil With the other twenty-nine ancient families of Vvardenfell, the Seran clan hid the [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md), the secret repository of the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) and placed a clue in their ancestral tomb.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBradyn-4)</sup> Its location remained a secret for many years until Bradyn of the Library of Vivec enlisted an [outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to find the tombs and uncover it in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They were able to find all thirty tombs and find the lost Library of Andule.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAncestral-5)</sup> In the same year a member of the family Nuula Seran lived in [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md) of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) and served as an armsman in the [Fighter's Guild](Lore_Fighter%27s_Guild.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NS-6)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Velyna Seran was a commoner living in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) who became involved in an affair with Taren Andoren, a noble from the city.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VS-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TA-8)</sup> Members of the family included [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) affiliates such as Miraso Seran of [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md), an Initiate and [enchantress](Lore_Enchanting.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MS-9)</sup> and [Methal Seran](Lore_Methal_Seran.md) of [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md), a Diviner, eminent priest, scholar and master [conjurer](Lore_Conjuration.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Methal S-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-GTA-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Ald.27ruhn-12)</sup> Madura Seran was a pilgrim and Layman of the Temple taken captive by [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) outcasts, and was later freed through the efforts of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Madura S-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Darius-14)</sup> The family also produced less reputable figures, such as Banor Seran, a [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md) member who operated a public house in [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md),<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-BS-15)</sup> and Faver Seran, a common bandit based at Milk.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-FS-16)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Olyn Seran had fled Morrowind and taken refuge in the forests of Cyrodiil.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-AF-17)</sup> He was also a powerful conjurer, and a cultist of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-AL-18)</sup> Although willing to train others, he demanded proof of their ability to serve Molag Bal in their own way, requiring them to summon a [Faded Wraith](Lore_Faded_Wraith.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-OS-19)</sup> Although he spent most of his time at the shrine of his patron deity,<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-OS-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-MBS-20)</sup> Seran Camp was located to the northeast of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) and to the southeast of [Bloodrun Cave](Lore_Bloodrun_Cave.md).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-SC-21)</sup>
