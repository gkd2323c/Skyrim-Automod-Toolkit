[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/OB-place-Wendir.jpg/200px-OB-place-Wendir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Wendir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Wendir.jpg) Wendir circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Wendir (meaning *Journey* in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) located west of Hackdirt, in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Its subsection of *Wendir Nagasel* translates to "Hall of Death".

During the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), Wendir became home to one of the [Ten Ancestors](Lore_Ten_Ancestors.md), Ayleid statues originally held in the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) which were dispersed to cities across Cyrodiil for safekeeping.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBUmbacano-2)</sup> Wendir subsequently fell into ruin after the Ayleids' downfall.

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero.md) entered the ruins and retrieved the ancient Ayleid statue, which was found in the deepest chamber of Wendir Nagasel, at a time when the entire compound was overrun by undead. The statue was later sold to [Umbacano](Lore_Umbacano.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OBCollector-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The settlement's original Ayleid name has been lost to time. The name "Wendir" only came into use some time after the fall of White-Gold.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBUmbacano-2)</sup>
