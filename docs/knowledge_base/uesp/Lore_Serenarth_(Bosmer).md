Treethane Serenarth was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) Treethane active circa [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578). She was encountered by [Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md) on his ill fated journey across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). When Flaccus came upon Serenarth, she was embroiled in a battle with a [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) captain who she managed to slay, but she collapsed from exhaustion into the harbor. Flaccus noticing this saved Serenarth and the Treethane gave the painter safe passage to [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGTValenwood-1)</sup>
