[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-creature-Stump.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Stump.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Stump.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Stump.jpg) Stump in front of the steps of The Sleeping Giant Inn **Stump** is a [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) that belongs to [Frodnar](Skyrim_Frodnar.md) and lives in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md). He follows Frodnar around during the day and spends the night outside.

## Notes
- [Attacking or killing Stump](Skyrim_Crime.md) will result in a bounty in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md) and will cause the residents of Riverwood to turn hostile.
- Frodnar and [Dorthe](Skyrim_Dorthe.md) can be heard having a conversation about a prank involving Stump: - **Dorthe**: *"Wait, so... a costume? You're not serious..."*
- **Frodnar**: *"Sure I am. We get some furs and tree branches, paint them white, and then tie them to Stump. Instant [Frostbite Spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md)!"*
- **Dorthe**: *"Frodnar... nobody is going to believe that your dog is a... Frostbite Spider. And if they do... they'll kill him. That's not much of a prank."*
- **Frodnar**: *"Oh yeah, and what would you rather do?"*
- **Dorthe**: *"How about... we play tag! You're it!"*
- **Frodnar**: *"Hey, no fair! Get her, Stump!"*

## Bugs
- If Frodnar is orphaned and you [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) him, Stump will continue to follow Frodnar, causing him to travel from Riverwood to your house and back every day. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- If Stump gets stuck anywhere (he sometimes spawns inside [Hod and Gerdur's House](Skyrim_Hod_and_Gerdur%27s_House.md) and can't get out, and he can get stuck below the terrain at times), this will bug both Frodnar and Dorthe, who follow him around. They will stand idly for the rest of the game, Frodnar near the inn and Dorthe just down the road to the south a few yards behind a bush. To fix this, type `PRID 1E62B`, then type `moveto player`. Stump will pop to you, then resume his usual wandering route, and the two children will behave normally. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Stump has no factions assigned, which is inconsistent with the way the other named dogs in the game are set up. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.1, addresses this issue. Stump is now part of Dog Faction, Creature Faction, and Town Riverwood Faction.
- Stump may appear dead from unknown causes. <sup>**?**</sup>
