[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-npc-Nimphaneth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nimphaneth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nimphaneth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nimphaneth.jpg) Nimphaneth **Nimphaneth** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) cannibal that appears at the table in [Reachcliff Cave](Skyrim_Reachcliff_Cave.md) during the later stages of [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md) quest. She cannot otherwise be found elsewhere in the world. If she survives the events of The Taste of Death, she will remain in Reachcliff Cave with [Eola](Skyrim_Eola.md) and [Sanyon](Skyrim_Sanyon.md).

During and after the banquet she will switch between eating or just pacing around the dining hall.

Nimphaneth wears a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c36e8) along with [necromancer robes](Skyrim_Necromancer_Robes.md), [hooded necromancer robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Necromancer_Robes.md), robes of [Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka_Rate.md). She also carries a leveled dagger up to [glass](Skyrim_Glass_Dagger.md), or an enchanted dagger of any quality up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md).

In addition to the gear listed above, she also has a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) (including a higher chance for [black soul gems](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) above level 20), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).

On death, she has a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls: [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis.md), [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie.md), [Scroll of Blizzard](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Blizzard.md), [Scroll of Fire Storm](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Fire_Storm.md), [Scroll of Call to Arms](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms.md), [Scroll of Harmony](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Harmony.md), [Scroll of Hysteria](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Hysteria.md), [Scroll of Mayhem](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Mayhem.md), [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead.md) or a [Scroll of Guardian Circle](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Taste of Death
During The Taste of Death, she may comment on the [food](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md):

- *"You've brought us quite the meal."*
- *"I love this part of the meal."*

After the quest, Nimphaneth will greet you with:

- *"It's an honor, Keeper of the Ring."*
