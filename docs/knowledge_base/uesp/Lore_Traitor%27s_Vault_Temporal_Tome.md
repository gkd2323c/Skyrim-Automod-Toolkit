Traitor's Vault Temporal Tome **by [Rullinalion](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Rullinalion)** *A letter from a world where Mannimarco's plot succeeded during the Planemeld*
---
Someday, surely, someone will find this. I don't know how, but the Old Ways guide me in this as in all things.

The few of us that still live, if you can call this living, are huddled in the crypt. The [dead](Lore_Undead.md) that walk the world have some difficulty finding us down here. We're all a fair hand at [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md), now. We have to be, to have survived this long.

[Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) is a charnel house. The world is a sepulcher. That bastard, that butcher, [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md). He rules [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) from atop a throne carved of bone and [souls](Lore_Soul.md), an abomination stitched into the sky over [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). I've seen it myself.

I still don't know what happened. I was providing relief to the folk of [Weye](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Weye), constantly caught in the turmoil of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md). When the [sky opened up](Lore_Soulburst.md), I saw [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) beyond. I heard some stories later, in the long mad dash through [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) to the sea, that some adventurers tried to stop the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md). Went up against [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) himself. Yet somehow, the King of Worms emerged the victor.

Now the mage I used to sharpen quill nibs for is a [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) -damned [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md). If [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md) is still out there somewhere, he must be laughing. We gathered all the supplies we can find on the island and we're going to try to do a smaller-scale version of the ritual to remove the isle from Nirn. We don't know if it will work, as we are so few. The [Loremaster](Lore_Celarus.md) is dead. And part of me feels as though we're running.

Oh, Cas. I wish you were still here. I had so much I didn't say to you. But maybe someday you'll read this. And you'll know.

Old Ways Guide You In Dark Places,
Rullinalion
