# Coral

Coral is a type of aquatic rocklike structure, formed from the bones of tiny sac-animals,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAugur-1)</sup> that comes in many different varieties.

In [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) society, it is prominently used in their architecture. Dense corals supposedly provide the entire foundation of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) and the [Coral Tower](Lore_Coral_Tower_(Thras).md) itself was made from blood-red coral before its collapse.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JOTE-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOCoral Plating-3)</sup> The Sload are also said to use tangled nests of coral formations to move around, and it's even used in their armorsmithing, with different types of corals providing different advantages in battle, such as lightness or durability.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOCoral Plating-3)</sup>

Some of the most ancient ruins of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) are thought to have been built by the Sload and are made of coral, including the original architecture of [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3Summerset-4)</sup> In Morrowind there is a naturally-occurring bioluminescent, and possibly sentient, coral species that grows on the lands around the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md).

According to the [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md), coral [ghosts](Lore_Ghost.md) are horrifying to behold and are said to resemble old cheese.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAugur-1)</sup> However, the Augur was regarded by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) as a notorious liar when it was not being asked a direct question.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

One of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s Dragon claws was made out of coral and it was the key to [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md)'s tomb in his eponymous [barrow-hill](Lore_Yngol_Barrow.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRCoral-6)</sup>

## Antler Coral
**Antler Coral** (also known as **Ocean Antler Coral**) is a type of underwater coral, not to be confused with the rarer terrestrial [Land Coral](#Land_Coral). The [Coral Tower](Lore_Coral_Tower_(Coldharbour).md) of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) appears to have been partly reconstructed from Antler Coral which was transported to the realm from [Thras](Lore_Thras.md).

## Land Coral
**Land Coral** (sometimes **Land-Coral**) is a bizarre, bioluminescent, and possibly sentient coral variety that grows on land. They can only very rarely be found along the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) of both mainland Morrowind and Vvardenfell.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TIEGTTM-11)</sup> The coral at [Vivec's Antlers](Lore_Vivec%27s_Antlers.md) is strongly assciated with the [Coral Heart](Lore_Coral_Heart.md). They only come shaped as Antler Coral and the tips of the coral give off a natural fire-like bioluminescent glow.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TJ-12)</sup> Land Coral can come in a variety of sizes and has the potential to grow large enough to tower above the landscape. It can be used to make snorkels, an apparatus for breathing underwater.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOContraband-13)</sup>

## Spiral Skein Coral
In the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md) of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), coral comes in at least two known types. The Brittle-Vein coral thrives on the remains of people who fell victim to the spiders from the realm, and the Spiral Skein Elkhorn coral gives off a welcoming bioluminescence that the spiders use to lure victims into their webs.
