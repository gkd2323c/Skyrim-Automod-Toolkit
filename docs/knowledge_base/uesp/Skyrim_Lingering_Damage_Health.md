*Alchemy description:* **Causes <mag> points of poison damage for <dur> seconds.**
This effect is a variant of [Damage Health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md). It causes a total of <mag>×<dur> damage.

As with the other two Damage Health variants, it is only available from potions.

## Notes
- When boosting the effect of a Lingering Damage Health poison (via Alchemy perks or Fortify Alchemy enchanted apparel), only the magnitude of the poison is affected, *not* the duration, which is fixed at 10 seconds.
- Using the [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) [exploit](Skyrim_Glitches.md#Infinitely_Powerful_Items), it is possible to craft Lingering Damage Health poisons which can deal hundreds of damage per second, enough to even kill [Legendary Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) rather quickly.
