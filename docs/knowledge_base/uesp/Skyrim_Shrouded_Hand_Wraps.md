[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-item-Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Hand_Wraps.jpg) Shrouded Hand Wraps Shrouded Hand Wraps are a style of gloves used by the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). The hand wraps are a part of a set of Dark Brotherhood-themed clothing that also includes a [hood](Skyrim_Shrouded_Hood.md), [robes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Robes.md), and [shoes](Skyrim_Shrouded_Shoes.md). The wraps bear an enchantment that allows the wearer to do double the damage they would normally do when [backstabbing](Skyrim_Backstab.md) a target, making them valuable attire for assassinations.

## Bugs
- These hand wraps are classified as gauntlets, i.e., armor, but are not governed by either the light armor or heavy armor skill. Instead of having the keyword "Armor Gauntlets", it should have the keywords "Armor Clothing" and "Clothing Hands". - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
