[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/MER-art-Sahrotaar_And_Miraak.webp/200px-MER-art-Sahrotaar_And_Miraak.webp)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Sahrotaar_And_Miraak.webp) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Sahrotaar_And_Miraak.webp) Sahrotaar and Miraak Sahrotaar was a serpentine [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name could mean "Mighty Servant", "Mighty Slave",<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Shout-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Odahviing-3)</sup> "Phantom Word Slave" or "Phantom Word Servant"<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Prima-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PAR-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Odahviing-3)</sup> in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

At a certain point in history, Sahrotaar swore allegiance to [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md) and considered him the only true [Dovahkiin](Lore_Dragonborn.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sahrotaar-5)</sup> Miraak used Sahrotaar as his personal mount.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TTOM-6)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) traveled to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) to challenge Miraak. Through use of the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), they swayed Sahrotaar to betray Miraak. He helped them to reach the First Dragonborn on the summit of Apocrypha where the Last Dragonborn confronted Sahrotaar's former master. During the fight Miraak absorbed Sahrotaar's soul to regain vitality, killing him in the process.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ASA-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Miraak-8)</sup> Having had his soul devoured by another dovah, Sahrotaar was killed permanently and cannot be raised again.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-LAD-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Arngeir-10)</sup>
