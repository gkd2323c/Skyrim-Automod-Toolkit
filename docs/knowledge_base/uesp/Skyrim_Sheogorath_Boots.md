[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-item-Sheogorath_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sheogorath_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Boots.jpg) Sheogorath Boots [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg) Sheogorath's attire, while worn The Sheogorath Boots are a pair of unique boots worn by the eponymous [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Madness, [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), when he is encountered during the quest [The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md). As it is not possible to injure Sheogorath under normal circumstances, the boots cannot be acquired under regular game conditions.

## Notes
- The boots have a strikingly different appearance while worn to the item's model in the inventory. As the boots cannot normally be acquired, this is likely due to the fact that the incorrect item model would normally not ever be seen.
