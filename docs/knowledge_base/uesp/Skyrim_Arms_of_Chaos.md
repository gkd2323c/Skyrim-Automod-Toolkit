[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-menu-Arms_of_Chaos.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Arms_of_Chaos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Arms_of_Chaos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Arms_of_Chaos.jpg) Arms of Chaos **Arms of Chaos** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that involves restoring artifacts of chaos related to the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md). It was released on March 14, 2019 for 500 Creation Credits.

This Creation was primarily made by [Private Eye](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/887024), while the quest was created by [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Official Summary
Creation Club *The original Staff of Chaos, or Balac-thurm, was shattered into pieces and lost to time. In the years since, mages have sought to recreate its magic. Follow the path of these mages as you obtain an ancient ring and restore the artifacts of chaos, including two new staves and an enchanted amulet! Creation by Private Eye. Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "The Arms of Chaos" starts by reading Hyenril's Journal at Skytemple Ruins, north of the College of Winterhold.)* Sneak Peek - Mar 2019 *The original Staff of Chaos, or Balac-thurm, was a relic of untold power, shattered into pieces and lost to time. In the years since, mages have sought to recreate its magic, traveling to worlds beyond the realms of mortal men. Follow the path of the mages as you obtain an ancient ring and restore the artifacts of chaos, including two new staves and an enchanted amulet!*
