- *Sacrifice a friendly conjured or reanimated minion to summon 2 Sundered Shades for 60 seconds.*

**Soul Split** is an Apprentice level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Effects
- Kills the target
- [Conjure Sundered Shade](Skyrim_Conjure.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Necromancy](Skyrim_Necromancy.md), Greater duration for spell.
- [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md), increases Health with 100.

## Notes
- Sundered Shades are immune to **Soul Split**.
- Sundered Shades spawn on the same spot making it look like there is only one until you move away.
- The Soul Split spell was inspired by a *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* card [of the same name](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Soul_Split).
