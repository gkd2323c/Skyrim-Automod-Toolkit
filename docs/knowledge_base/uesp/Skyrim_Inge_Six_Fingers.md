[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-npc-Inge_Six_Fingers.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Inge_Six_Fingers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Inge_Six_Fingers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Inge_Six_Fingers.jpg) Inge Six Fingers **Inge Six Fingers** is an [elderly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Old_People_Race) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md) and the Dean of Lute at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). When you are officially declared a member of the college at the end of [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), she will ask you to retrieve the first hand-crafted eight-string lute ever created, starting the quest [Finn's Lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute).

At the Bards College, classes start very early in the morning, so Inge gets out of bed at 4am and positions herself in the downstairs library, ready to teach [Ataf](Skyrim_Ataf.md), [Illdi](Skyrim_Illdi.md), [Jorn](Skyrim_Jorn.md), and [Aia Arria](Skyrim_Aia_Arria.md) a lesson (see [bugs](#Bugs)). At 8am she observes her colleague [Pantea Ateia](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md) teach the same four students in the eastern classroom. The class ends at noon and Inge takes a two-hour break, either eating, relaxing, or strolling around. She then joins the Dean of History, [Giraud Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md), for the 2pm class in the library on the ground floor. When class is dismissed at 6pm and most of the teachers and bards head for [the local tavern](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md), Inge instead heads straight down to her quarters and goes to bed.

She wears a set of farmers [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452f). She carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bards_College) to the college and a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

## Related Quests
- **[Finn's Lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Finn%27s_Lute)**: Return an invaluable [lute](Skyrim_Finn%27s_Lute_(item).md) to Inge Six Fingers.

## Dialogue
As a teacher she is strict and to the point, a behavior reflected in her greetings:

*"I'm Dean of Lute at the Bards College, and no, I don't give private lessons."* *"It takes two things to be a good teacher. The first is patience. The second is a firm wooden stick for rapping knuckles."* *"Only reason I'm so good with a lute is because I'm old. It takes a lifetime to master."* If you haven't joined the college yet, she will also tell you not to bother:

*"You here to apply at the Bards College? Well don't bother - you're too damned old. Start past four, and it's all wasted effort."* Her goodbyes:

*"We're done then? Good."* *"Glad that distraction's over..."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Finn's Lute
After [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), Inge will be somewhat sad and will start to say:

*"It makes me sad whenever I think about it."* **Why are you sad?** *"Hmm? Oh, sorry. My mind wanders sometimes. I was just thinking about Finn's Lute. Over a year ago, thieves broke into the college and made off with a lot of valuable things. Mostly gold and silver. But they also stole Finn's Lute. We just learned where the bandits fled to, and I very much want that lute back."* **What's so important about Finn's Lute?** *"Finn was the bard who invented the eight course lute that is commonplace today. Until he found a way to string the extra courses, bards only had six course lutes. The lute they stole is Finn's original eight course lute. I doubt they even realize its true value."* When you return [Finn's Lute](Skyrim_Finn%27s_Lute_(item).md) to her, she will be grateful and reward you with some free training:

**I've found Finn's Lute.** *"What? You did? Oh sweet Divines! You did! There is no way I can pay you what this Lute is worth. But I can teach you a few tricks I've learned over the years."*
## Conversations
If she runs into college headmaster [Viarmo](Skyrim_Viarmo.md), they may have a brief conversation:

**Viarmo**: *"How are your students coming along?"*
**Inge**: *"Some of them have a long way to go, but they're mostly passable. Don't let them hear I said that, though!"*
**Viarmo**: *"Of course not! Complacency is the bane of art. They're in good hands with you, Inge."*

She may also approach either Aia, Illdi, or Jorn and ask them the same question:

**Inge**: *"Your solo performance is about a week away. I expect to hear your early rendition tomorrow, understand?"*
**Jorn**: *"I've converted the classic tale of Eyldi the Bear to be performed with drums. I think you'll be impressed!"*
**Aia**: *"At this point, I hardly see how your advice would improve it. It's flawless."*
**Illdi**: *"Oh, thank you! I've been working on an original piece. I hope it can stand up to the classics!"*
**Inge**: *"I'll be the judge of that. Now, back to your practice."*
