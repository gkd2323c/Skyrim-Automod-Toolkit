[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-npc-Nazir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nazir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nazir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nazir.jpg) Nazir considering his next contract **Nazir** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Light Armor Master) and a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). He will offer you contracts upon joining the Dark Brotherhood. He is also the master [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md).

Nazir wears a unique set of [Redguard clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes) with a matching [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000e0dd4). In combat, he relies on a [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar).

When inquired about himself, he will reveal that he grew up in the deserts of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), where *"blood is a shade of crimson I can scarce describe"*. He is apparently not proud of his past (though he will not explain what he did), and explains that after the Dark Brotherhood saved him, he tried to forget it.

He often makes jokes or sarcastic comments about the contracts he sends you on—for example, after reporting back and telling Nazir a [mine owner](Skyrim_Beitild.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) is dead, he says *"Of course she is. I hear the mining business is extremely cutthroat. And those hours... they're murder."* He seems to enjoy making these jokes, commenting *"I could do this all day."*

He is not very happy with the arrival of [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) and the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md), saying that he dislikes a vast majority of entertainers, and jesters in particular. He also says that he's not too keen on long-dead corpses of old women, and that although he'll make an exception for the Night Mother, he makes it clear that he'll only ever answer to [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md).

He is one of the few survivors of the attack on the sanctuary during the quest [Death Incarnate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Incarnate).

After the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) is established, he will permanently relocate to the main room and can still offer you contracts that have not yet been completed. If Cicero is there, he will not be surprised, but he'll make a futile request of you to stop him from dancing and singing.

## Dialogue (General)
**You:** *"What can you tell me about yourself?"*
**Nazir:** *"Ah, well, I'm Redguard, as I'm sure you've noticed. Though not like most of my kind you'll find in Skyrim, that's for damned sure. I am a child of Hammerfell, born of the sands of the mighty Alik'r. In that great desert, blood is a shade of crimson I can scarce describe. My past is long and storied, and not altogether proud. But the Dark Brotherhood saved me from myself. And I have never looked back."*

Alternatively, if you are a Redguard, his opening lines will change to, *"Ah, well, I'm Redguard, as I'm sure you've noticed. Though not like you. Hmph. No, definitely not like you."*

### Once Cicero arrives with the Night Mother
Once the contract is finished:

**You:** *"What are your thoughts on Cicero and the Night Mother?"*
**Nazir:** *"I don't like mimes, minstrels, thespians, acrobats, jugglers, troubadours or tumblers. Flutists give me a headache. I particularly hate jesters. As a rule, I'm also not crazy about the corpses of old women. For the Night Mother, I'll make an exception. But Astrid is the only mistress I serve."*

### During [Whispers in the Dark](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whispers_in_the_Dark)
**You:** *"Astrid thinks a Family member may be conspiring with Cicero."*
**Nazir:** *"Does she? Listen, brother/sister... Astrid is my mistress, and I worship the ground she walks on. But this Cicero business has her a bit... paranoid."*

### During [The Cure for Madness](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md)
**You:** *"Any thoughts on the current situation?"*
**Nazir:** *"Going after Cicero now would be a fool's errand. That little jester is currently being digested in Arnbjorn's stomach, mark my words."*

- Nazir is voiced by [Erik Todd Dellums](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erik_Todd_Dellums).
- Although Nazir often complains about bards, he doesn't say anything if you [join the Bards College](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md).
- Nazir has the exact same face as another NPC, namely the [Ebony Warrior](Skyrim_Ebony_Warrior.md).
- The [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) DLC was once meant to have an easter egg referencing Nazir. The ice cave [Frossel](Skyrim_Frossel.md) was meant to contain an unused safe internally known as `DLC2Frossel Safewith Lock`. It would've held a [fork](Skyrim_Fork_(item).md), a [knife](Skyrim_Knife_(item).md), [Redguard clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Clothes), a matching [Redguard hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Hood), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000e0dd4), and a [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar). These items are Nazir's clothing and weapon of choice, and the utensils might refer to his passion for cooking.
