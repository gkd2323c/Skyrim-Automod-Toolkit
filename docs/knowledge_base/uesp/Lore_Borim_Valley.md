## History
At some point before [3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389), General [Talin Warhaft](Lore_Talin_Warhaft.md) and his men routed their enemies in the valley. They would have defeated and captured them all if not for the brave sacrifice of a young sergeant on the opposing side, who held a pass long enough for his men to retreat to safety, knowing that this action would cost him his life. General Warhaft would later recount this story to his child, the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>
