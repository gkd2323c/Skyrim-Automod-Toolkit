Magnifico Z'za, also known as the **Silent Magnifico**, was a noble of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) who lived during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). Following the death of his daughter Haruzeh at the *Running of the Dunerippers*, the distraught Z'za retired to his house in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) and never spoke again. He spent the last of his days playing a long series of hammergammon with his trained [monkey](Lore_Monkey.md) that was named "Lizard".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot SM-1)</sup> He had a folding hammergammon board of precious wood inlays, complete with [ivory](Lore_Ivory.md) pieces and [jade](Lore_Jade.md) multiplier die.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MIHS-2)</sup> At some point after his death, his home was later put up for sale and tagged under his name, as houses formerly owned or occupied by celebrities are believed to generate more interest and therefore sell for higher prices.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Hot SM-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Homestead Int-3)</sup>
