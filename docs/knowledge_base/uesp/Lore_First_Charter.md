# First Charter


During the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the House of Orsimer Glories in the newly rebuilt Orsinium sought to recover various relics from Orcish history to display in their exhibits.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Umutha-3)</sup> To that end they employed relic hunters to search for missing relics across the land. The First Charter was eventually found in the wilds of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) by [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and returned to the House of Orsimer Glories, where it was put on display.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGlories-4)</sup>
