## Walkthrough
The camp, which is on a cliff overlooking Dragon Bridge, can be accessed by following the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md) upstream (southwest) from Dragon Bridge and then descending upon the camp, or by starting from [Bruca's Leap Redoubt](Skyrim_Bruca%27s_Leap_Redoubt.md) and heading east. It consists of two Forsworn tents, one on either side of a campfire. Sharpened stakes and stretched hides surround the camp, which features [blue](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle), and two patches of [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) fungus on a huge tree stump. The camp is home to two Forsworn, one of whom is typically found inside one of the tents, while the other is on the cliff edge overlooking the town, with a quiver of [Forsworn arrows](Skyrim_Forsworn_Arrow.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top of a piece of wooden boarding at their feet. The first tent contains an unowned hay pile, a pair of [Forsworn gauntlets](Skyrim_Forsworn_Gauntlets.md), a bottle of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md), and two [deer pelts](Skyrim_Deer_Pelt.md). The second tent contains a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) as well as a long wooden table holding a tactical map of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), a [Forsworn axe](Skyrim_Forsworn_Axe.md), an iron dagger, a random [potion of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Magicka), two bottles of ale, and a [leg of goat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat).
