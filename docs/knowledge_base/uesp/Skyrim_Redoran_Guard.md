**Redoran Guards** are [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [soldiers](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) in the service of House Redoran, serving in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) under [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md).

They all wear a full set of [bonemold armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Armor) with a [bonemold shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Shield) and defend themselves with an [elven sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Sword) supplemented by an [elven bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Bow) with twelve [elven arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Arrow). All the guards also carry a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000f1229) and may carry a small amount of gold. There are two exceptions: one of the guards found working at the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) is missing both the shield and the helmet from his armor, and the guard stationed in [The Bulwark Jail](Skyrim_The_Bulwark_Jail.md) also carries the [Raven Rock Jail Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Jail_Key).

During the quest [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md), two Redoran guards will be sent to [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md) to assist you in destroying the force amassed there by [Vendil Ulen](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md). When you arrive to meet them, you will find them dead, killed by the [Morag Tong assassins](Skyrim_Morag_Tong_Assassin.md) there.
