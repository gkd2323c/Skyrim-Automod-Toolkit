## Evette San's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Evette_San%27s_House.jpg) The interior of Evette San's house Upon entering there is a cupboard on your right holding an [iron helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Helmet) and a couple of sacks. On your left against the northeast wall is a small set of shelves holding a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk), a [cave bear pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cave_Bear_Pelt), a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and a basket holding two bunches of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md). To the right is a barrel and basket containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md). On top of the barrel are six [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot). Against an interior half-height wall is a small wooden table and two chairs. On the table are two portions of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), two [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), and a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb. Over the table are racks holding four [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and three [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md).

Past the interior wall, against the northwest wall, is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) in a cast iron pot on the hearth. Opposite against the southeast wall is a long table with various cheese, several loaves of bread, a couple of [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a [raw rabbit leg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg), a portion of grilled leeks, a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a seared slaughterfish steak and a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). Under the table is an open crate containing a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) and an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing). To the right is a second open crate containing vegetables.

To the southwest is the rest of the house, including stairs up to the top floor. On the left is a bedroom with a single bed in the southern corner with an end table to the right and a couple of sacks at the end of the bed. Against the southeast wall is a wooden bench and against the northwest wall a small set of shelves holding a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a mead barrel.

Back out in the corridor, to the left of the staircase to the rear of the house is a storage area with two cupboards. One holds two [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), a bottle of alto wine, an eidar cheese wedge, a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), a [goat cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), four [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), several loaves of bread, and a haunch of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison). The other holds a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack) and an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace). Between them are several barrels of foodstuffs. There are also two sets of shelves under the stairs which hold various cheese wheels, cabbages, potatoes, a [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration), loaves of bread, two sheaves of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) and a copy of *[Songs of the Return, Vol 19](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Songs_of_the_Return,_Vol_19)*. Hanging from a rack beside a cupboard are three garlic braids.

At the top of the stairs is a wooden bench. In the southern corner is a table holding a loaf of bread, with a cupboard to the left of this holding another knapsack and iron mace. Continuing along the southeast wall is another wooden bench and a stack of barrels all containing foodstuffs. Against the northwest wall is a single bed with an end table holding a bunch of tundra cotton to the left of it. Against a railing to the northeast are two unlocked [chests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest).

## Notes
- There is a "Loot" [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the right of the entrance door.
- The [key to the house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Evette_San%27s_House) is carried by [Bendt](Skyrim_Bendt.md), Evette, and Octieve. Bendt, however, does not leave the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) at any time, has no dialogue about the Sans, or have any other possible reason for carrying the key.
