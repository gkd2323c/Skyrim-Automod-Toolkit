—Morlia, a member of the College of Tomes<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup> Vahath Behemoths (or simply **Vahaths**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VHJ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mor Behe-3)</sup> are giant feathered [reptilian](Lore_Reptile.md) beasts found on the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md) that were created by ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) to act as guardians.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Stone-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vahath-5)</sup> They are in actuality thought to be an offshoot of Argonians themselves, modified in their eggs through ancient [arcane](Lore_Magic.md) meddling in the days of myth and legend.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Stone-4)</sup> Most accounts claim they were created when Stone-Nest tribe turned to [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) powers when the [Corelanyans](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) invaded Solstice in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), and used dark rituals to change hatchlings in their eggs into beasts to drive the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) away. Other tales suggest Vahath behemoths are even older, though it is uncertain if these are true.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup> While they have certain similarities, they are not to be confused with [Xal-Krona](Lore_Xal-Krona.md), better known simply as Argonian Behemoths, who are created through the ingestion of [Hist sap](Lore_Hist_Sap.md). The Vahath is suspected to be the more ancient variety of transformed Argonian.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mor Behe-3)</sup>

Vahath behemoths are described as warriors cursed to relish excessive violence and death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Stone-4)</sup> They often attack by rolling around or throwing rocks.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vahath-5)</sup> The process of their creation was said to have turned them into brutes, unlike the similar Xal-Krona they have lost the power of speech, but they still are said to retain a clever malice. Some are intelligent enough to tame and keep [voriplasms](Lore_Voriplasm.md) as pets,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup> and are even seemingly capable of using [magic](Lore_Magic.md). Some have been known to defend themselves by creating clouds of [disease](Lore_Disease.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Gaulm-6)</sup> They have been to known to try and destroy ancient Corelyanan ruins, with some believing this is them following their nature of trying to drive out invaders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mor-1)</sup>

Ancient soapstone totems depicting Vahath behemoths have been discovered that are estimated to have been created by the Stone-Nest tribe in the Mid-First Era, around the eleventh or twelfth century.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Stone-4)</sup>

## Notes
- Former series writer [Michael Kirkbride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Kirkbride) has made numerous references in the past to the Argonians utilizing giant or heavy "feathered flu-tyrants" during the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-7)</sup> though he never elaborated on what this meant. Vahath Behemoths resemble this concept due to being giant feathered Argonian creations capable of weaponizing diseased attacks.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Gaulm-6)</sup>
