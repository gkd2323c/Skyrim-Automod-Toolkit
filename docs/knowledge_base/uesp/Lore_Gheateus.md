Gheatus (or **Gheateus**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TF-1)</sup> are a [fay](Lore_Faerie.md) variant described as ancient godlike beings that once inhabited the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wansalen-3)</sup> According to legend, they are rock-spirits that took the form of a man or a group of men who were formed by the earth itself,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wansalen-3)</sup> and they are said to have been surrounded by a host of minor [Sylphim](Lore_Sylphim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TF-1)</sup>

They were once far more common and attacked the early [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md), but were fought off by [Torinaan](Lore_Torinaan.md)'s use of ancestral lore and [meteoric glass](Lore_Meteoric_Glass.md) to channel aetherial energy in the [form](Lore_Destruction.md) of lightning, ice, and fire to kill them,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TLo F-4)</sup> and they were exterminated in a few short years.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset-6)</sup> Legend has it that the excavation of Wansalen was halted, and the halls abandoned, when the [Elven](Lore_Aldmer.md) miners roused and angered an ancient Gheatus, but the [Sapiarchs](Lore_Sapiarchs.md) dismiss this story as a fable with no truth to it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Wansalen-3)</sup> They are now supposedly extinct,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset-6)</sup> though some have sought out ways to bring them back from extinction.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Rt W-5)</sup>
