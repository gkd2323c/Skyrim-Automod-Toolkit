# Cathnoquey

| --- | --- |
| Type | Archipelago<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PG2-1)</sup> |

Cathnoquey is an archipelago that lies somewhere between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). It was conquered by [Uriel Septim V](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uriel_Septim_V) in [3E 276](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_276),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> and was still an inhabited [Imperial](Lore_Empire.md) territory and trade hub circa [3E 331](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_331).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PG2-1)</sup> According to dubious Imperial records, its main inhabitants are the [Chimeri-quey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chimeri-quey), a tribal remnant of [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) composed of fisherfolk who live on the coastlines in grand flotilla-townships of advanced design, and the [Keptu-quey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Keptu-quey), [men](Lore_Men.md) with tan brown skin said to have a strange obsession with drawing [bulls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bull) and living in leaderless societies in military fortresses.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PG2-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MKP-3)</sup> One of its major exports is wasabi,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PG2-1)</sup> a substance that has been popular in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) since ancient times.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-500COTOYTR-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-SFA-5)</sup>

## Notes
- In [Michael Kirkbride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Kirkbride)'s descriptions of the archipelago, the inhabitants of Cathnoquey were inspired by the culture of the [Native Hawaiians](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Native_Hawaiians).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PG2-1)</sup> Kirkbride had spoken about how during his time on series, he wished he had introduced more diverse peoples on Nirn that physically resembled real-world ethnic groups that otherwise had no analogues in the setting, such as [Pacific Islanders](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pacific_Islander).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MKP-3)</sup>
