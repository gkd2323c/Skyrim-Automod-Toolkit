---
In the 726th year, [Kinheir Iniel](Lore_Iniel_Corelanya.md) returned to [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), leading a strong expeditionary force. In previous explorations, she discovered the Daedric temple-city of Mor Naril in eastern Solstice, but the hostile [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) of the region prevented her from exploring the ruins. This time she was determined to plumb the secrets of the—[indecipherable]

—dark fane, Iniel discovered a great altar, a well in stone. Daedric inscriptions named it the wraithforge, a place for the binding and unmaking of [souls](Lore_Soul.md) to release and utilize boundless energy. There too she found the Gift of Death, yet—[indecipherable]

—the Argonian tribes surrounded the [Corelanyan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) forces in Mor Naril. For days Kinheir Iniel held the beast-folk at bay with potent [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md), but the island natives answered by calling forth [great stone statues](Lore_Voskrona_Guardian.md) and gave them eyes with which to see, bringing them to life.

Necromancy was of no avail against colossals of walking stone—[indecipherable]—following their mighty war-statues, the Argonians drove Kinheir Iniel and her followers from Mor Naril. The kinheir was obliged to retreat. But the stone warriors watch over Mor Naril to the present day.
