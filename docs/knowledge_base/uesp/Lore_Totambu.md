[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/DF-map-Totambu.png/200px-DF-map-Totambu.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Totambu.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Totambu.png) Totambu ca. 3E 405 Totambu is an inland fiefdom of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), south of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> It was named after [Na-Totambu](Lore_Na-Totambu.md), the ruling class of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) and progenitors of the Hammerfell [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) faction.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-2)</sup>

Totambu borders [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md) to the northwest, [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam_(county).md) to the north, [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth_(fiefdom).md) to the northeast, the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) to the south, and [Kozanset](Lore_Kozanset_(barony).md) to the west.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> The main deity of this [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) -dominated region is [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). The military force of Totambu is the [Order of the Scarab](Lore_Order_of_the_Scarab.md). They also served as the official guard for the royal family of Totambu, based in the city of Totambu. The [Khulari](Lore_Khulari.md) clan is the predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-3)</sup>

After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Totambu came under the dominion of the Kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) ruled by the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) faction.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TWINTW-4)</sup> Later, however, it became a site of a [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md) rebellion against [King Lhotun](Lore_Lhotun.md) of Sentinel. Totambu declared independence from Sentinel and pledged allegiance to the Crown leader Ayaan-si, High Prophet of [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md). Even though Lhotun's kingdom surrounded Totambu on all four sides, its people kept holding their resistance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-2)</sup> The Knights of the Scarab remained a military force in the region even after the Warp.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
