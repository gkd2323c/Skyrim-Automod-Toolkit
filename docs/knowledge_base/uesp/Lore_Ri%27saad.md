Ri'saad is the leader and patriarch of the [Khajiit Caravans](Lore_Khajiit_Caravans.md), a small syndicate of itinerant merchants based in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:199</sup> Ri'saad is an opportunist; originally from Elsweyr, he sought to make a profit in Skyrim during the [Civil War](Lore_Skyrim_Civil_War.md) and the [Dragon Crisis](Lore_Return_of_the_Dragons.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRRi.27saad-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ma-3)</sup> All of the Khajiit caravaneers in Skyrim answer to Ri'saad, who is seen as a kind patriarch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ma-3)</sup> He is the richest of the three caravan merchants and has exclusive contracts among Skyrim's wealthiest cities.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:199</sup> He was a known owner of the [Daedric](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md), the [Ring of Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ring_of_Khajiit).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SA-4)</sup> He is married to Atahbah, who travels with his caravan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:281</sup> His honorific -ri is rare as it is only used for great leaders and highly respected people among the Khajiit.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Ri'saad has previously assisted the author [Adonato Leotelli](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Adonato_Leotelli).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Ri'saad sought out help from the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) on several occasions. Most notably, he once explained that his caravan had been beset by two strange new groups of bandits wearing golden and dark armor. This led to the Dragonborn uncovering the plot of the mad mage [Thoron](Lore_Thoron.md). Ri'saad rewarded the Dragonborn after they took care of the bandits.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BOP-7)</sup> Additionally, Ri'saad and the Dragonborn secured an alliance between the Khajiit Caravans and the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TGCFQ-8)</sup> Ri'saad himself may have assisted the Dragonborn by rooting out a [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) assassin who was after an associate of the Dragonborn, [Malborn](Lore_Malborn.md), inadvertently saving Malborn's life.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-FTA-9)</sup>
