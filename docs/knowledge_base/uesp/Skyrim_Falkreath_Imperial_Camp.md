[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp.jpg) Falkreath Imperial Camp **Falkreath Imperial Camp** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) [camp](Skyrim_Imperial_Camps.md) in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md), located east-northeast of [Glenmoril Coven](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Coven.md) and north of [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Falkreath_Imperial_Camp_02.jpg) Falkreath Imperial Camp This camp will only be present if the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) hold [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) and the rest of Falkreath Hold, whether through martial might or via the negotiation during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). [Imperial Soldiers](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier.md) are camped here under the command of [Legate Skulnar](Skyrim_Legate_Skulnar.md), except during the related quest, when [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md) takes command. Like similar Imperial camps that only exist when the hold has changed hands, it has neither a [quartermaster](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md) or any [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). Unlike most military camps, it also has no [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block).

## Falkreath Imperial Camp
It is a typical Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. There is an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) beside the fire. To the west of the campfire is the command tent, containing a round table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. South of the campfire is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the right of this tent is a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md). To the southeast is the hospital tent, containing three bed rolls, two of which are occupied by wounded soldiers, and a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). To the left is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md). Northeast of the campfire is a row of three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. To the right of the first of these tents is an owned [steel helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Helmet). There are many [mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md) and [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch) in and around the camp.

Any time [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents.

## Notes
- Two ore veins are near the camp: a [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md) vein north of the camp, and an [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) vein northeast of the camp.
