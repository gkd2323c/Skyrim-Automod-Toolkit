[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-item-Amulet_of_Mara.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amulet_of_Mara.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Mara.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Mara.jpg) Amulet of Mara The **Amulet of Mara** is a necklace which allows you to cast spells from the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) school of magic for 10 percent less magicka. Wearing this amulet will also indicate to NPCs that you are available for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md), as long as you have not already gotten married. It can be purchased for 200 gold from [Maramal](Skyrim_Maramal.md) or acquired during the last stage of [The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md) quest from [Dinya Balu](Skyrim_Dinya_Balu.md), both at the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). It may occasionally be available for purchase from various general goods merchants, and will also appear randomly in chests and other loot drops if you can find it.
