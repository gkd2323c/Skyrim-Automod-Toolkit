[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/ON-place-Kulati_Mines.jpg/200px-ON-place-Kulati_Mines.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kulati_Mines.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kulati_Mines.jpg) Kulati Mines Kulati, also known as **Kulati Mines** was a mine and one of the places in the province of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) that was known for its [amethysts](Lore_Amethyst.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KM-1)</sup> It was named alongside [Dak'fron](Lore_Dak%27fron.md) and [Zareth M'Kai](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zareth_M%27Kai) as one of the most notable areas of amethyst mining in the province.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AMITA-2)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) the mines were overrun by [spiders](Lore_Spider.md) and [Spider Daedra](Lore_Spider_Daedra.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Issal-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Kasal-4)</sup> A group of the miners who arrived from [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) was killed by the arachnids, with only one survivor remaining. He later abandoned the mines and sought a better future in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Laurisac_Vien-5)</sup>. [An adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was hired by the mine foreman Kasal to find the people who remained in the mine after he closed the operation down. The last miner inside was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) Seqbar, who was followed by a concerned betrothed Shagora.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Kasal-4)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Shagora.27s_Journal-6)</sup> The adventurer ventured to the mines and discovered the [daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) influence inside the amethyst mine. They found the lost miner and his beloved, but it unknown whether they managed to rescue the miner, his partner or failed at the mission and left both inside the mines,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CC-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Seqbar-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Shagora-9)</sup> in the clutches of Spider Daedra Naluroen, who woven an illusion to entrap them inside.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Naluroen-10)</sup>

Due to the [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md)'s return to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) in the same year, recruits of the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) forces were trained in various areas of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The adventurer was tasked to train some of the soldiers in Kulati Mines.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Zahari-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SD-12)</sup>
