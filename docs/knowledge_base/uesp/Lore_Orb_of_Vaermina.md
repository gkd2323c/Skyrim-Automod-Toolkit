# Orb of Vaermina

The Orb of Vaermina, named for [its patron](Lore_Vaermina.md), was an orb apparently used for scrying.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGInterlude-1)</sup> At some point before [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), it was in the possession of a wizard named Arkved, by whom it was apparently *"snatched from the dreams of [Vaermina's] followers"*.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBVaermina-2)</sup>. It was recovered by Vaermina's champion, who received the [Skull of Corruption](Lore_Skull_of_Corruption.md) in return.

According to the [Moth Priest](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) [Kellen](Lore_Kellen.md), the Orb was used by Lord [Naarifin](Lore_Naarifin.md) —then in the service of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) —to spy on [Titus Mede II](Lore_Titus_Mede_II.md) and his armies. It was recovered by the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) from the Emperor's chambers, who either destroyed or kept it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGInterlude-1)</sup>
