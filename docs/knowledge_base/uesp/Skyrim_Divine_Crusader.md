[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-menu-Divine_Crusader.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Divine_Crusader.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Divine_Crusader.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Divine_Crusader.jpg) Divine Crusader **Divine Crusader** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds two sets of enchanted equipment, namely the Crusader's Relics and the Reforged Crusader's Relics. It was released on October 4, 2017 for 500 Creation Credits. The original Crusader's Relics first appeared in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)'s [Knights of the Nine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Knights_of_the_Nine) [download](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads).

This Creation was made by [Maty743](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2215129).

## Official Summary
*Now you, too, can wield the powerful equipment of the Divine Crusader, Pelinal Whitestrake - once reserved only for the revered Knights of the Nine. Created by Maty743. (Armor can be found by exploring Four Skull Lookout far east-northeast of Markarth.)*
## Overview
A group of four bandits (two [bandit thieves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bandit_Thief) in the original version) are camping at [Four Skull Lookout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Skull_Lookout), [Remy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Remy) and [Viparth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Viparth) are hostile, the other two are dead and equipped with the new items. Specifically, [Oren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oren) has the [Mace of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace_of_the_Crusader) and the heavy Relics of the Crusader armor set, while [Eigorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eigorn) has the [Sword of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_the_Crusader) and the light Reforged Relics of the Crusader armor set.

The light armor [Fastened Shield of the Crusader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fastened_Shield_of_the_Crusader) is not actually a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md); rather, it is the only item in the game to utilize the "shoulder" equipment slot. As such, it can simply be equipped for an additional armor boost without affecting any of your other gear. Notably, it does not negate the *[Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md)* perk.
