[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-place-Snapleg_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Snapleg_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Snapleg_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Snapleg_Cave.jpg) Snapleg Cave **Snapleg Cave** is a medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) northwest of [Rift Watchtower](Skyrim_Rift_Watchtower.md) occupied by [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md) and [monsters](Skyrim_Monsters.md). It has one interior zone: *[Snapleg Cave](#Snapleg_Cave)*.

## Related Quests
- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There is an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) a few paces downhill to the north of the entrance, as well as several patches of [mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), a [scaly pholiota](Skyrim_Scaly_Pholiota.md) fungus growing from a rotten tree trunk, and a [canis root](Skyrim_Canis_Root.md) all near the entrance. The entrance is marked with sharpened stakes and other symbols typical of [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md).

### Snapleg Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave.jpg) Snapleg Cave hag The entrance leads into a short tunnel descending to the south and opening into a sinkhole marked with the heads of two [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md) on stakes. An earthen ramp descends to the right. At the bottom there are three live skeevers feasting on a dead [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md). The passage onward is to the south, and is eerily lined with taproot talismans hanging on the walls. Ahead the tunnel turns to the east before opening into a chamber with a grisly elk head mounted on mammoth tusks. This display marks a small encampment with two tents, both occupied by [hags](Skyrim_Hag.md). A skeever, [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md), or [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md) will also be wandering around the camp.

A [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) is simmering on a stand beside a fire in front of the first tent. Inside, a table has a random [potion of magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), a bowl of [frost salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md), and a [goat hide](Skyrim_Goat_Hide.md). Under the table is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). To the left of the first tent is a rock formation, on top of which is an open crate hidden behind some foliage; the crate contains a [Staff of Paralysis](Skyrim_Staff_of_Paralysis.md), four [soul gem fragments](Skyrim_Soul_Gem_Fragment.md), a [deathbell flower](Skyrim_Deathbell.md), and a [potion of strength](Skyrim_Potion_of_Strength.md). The second tent is to the east of the first, but only displays more trophy heads.

The exit is to the south. A random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) is on the ground just to the left of the mouth of the tunnel. The tunnel twists and turns a fair amount, until a mass of light cobwebs in front holds a desiccated corpse. At this point the tunnel turns to the right, by a patch of [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) fungus, more of which is found near the end of the tunnel and in the next chamber. There is another skeever, frostbite spider, or troll in this chamber. The remains of a [mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) are on the ground, with a mammoth skull lodged in a hole in the ceiling with [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) growing from it. A [spellcaster trap](Skyrim_Spellcaster_Trap.md) that fires [ice spikes](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) is to the east, next to the exit tunnel. Remove the [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) from the pedestal to disarm the trap. To the north is a gap between rocks in the wall, through which you can see a chamber containing a chest, [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), and some barrels; this is the final chamber from which is a shortcut back to the entrance.

The next tunnel has a profusion of light cobwebs and opens into a relatively large cavern infested with frostbite spiders and numerous [egg sacs](Skyrim_Egg_Sac.md), two of which can be harvested. A tunnel heads north from this cavern, while to the east, at the rear of the cavern is a dead [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) in front of an unlocked chest. As you approach the bandit and chest, a giant frostbite spider will descend from a hole in the ceiling and attack along with any spiders that haven't already been alerted to your presence. The tunnel heading north leads to a small area with some thick cobwebs to the west concealing an unlocked chest, and to the north is a chain that opens a hidden door.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_02.jpg) Snapleg Cave hag Behind the door is another chamber with a single hag in front of a tent. In the tent are three barrels with a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Breathing Water](Skyrim_Breathing_Water.md)*, a random [potion of healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md), a random potion of magicka, two samples of [slaughterfish scales](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Scales.md), and three [slaughterfish eggs](Skyrim_Slaughterfish_Egg.md) on top. A second tent holds only a dead [elk](Skyrim_Elk.md) and two dead [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Just outside this tent are two barrels containing [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), with a random potion on top of one of them. There are two fly amanita fungus patches and several hanging mosses growing in this chamber. The exit to the next room is to the southwest.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Snapleg_Cave_04.jpg) Snapleg's prisoner A wooden bridge spanning a small stream leads to the last occupied room of the cave. The body of a [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) is in the water to the right of the bridge, but most notably, up ahead a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) is trapped in a cage behind an apprentice lock. A [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) and a hag are in a tent farther back to the south. The spriggan will not turn hostile if you free her, and makes a valuable ally against the enemies. Once the enemies are dispatched, if the spriggan survived, she flees the cave and vanishes at the exit. There is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) to the left of the tent. By the mouth of the tent is a barrel of ingredients with two random [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md) on top. Inside the tent is a table with two empty random soul gems and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top. To the right of the tent, against the cave wall is yet another barrel containing ingredients. To the west a wooden ramp leads up into the final room that contains the alchemy lab, chest, and barrels seen previously. The chest is unlocked and will likely contain your quest target if a radiant quest directed you here, and the barrels contain foodstuffs. The spriggan may be waiting by some spears blocking the way. Use the chain to the right to lower them. Exit onto a ledge overlooking the first encampment, but don't drop down off the ledge; instead, follow it to the right and through to the sinkhole, where you can drop down right by the exit. In front of you is a column that it is possible to jump onto, but apart from being able to jump to reach a hanging moss, there is nothing else of interest on the column.

## Notes
- Snapleg Cave also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Snapleg_Cave) in [Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
- Even if not freed from the cage, the peaceful [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) will run to [Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md) and stand in the thicket near the swimming hole.
- There is a "Small Bird" (Ref ID: 00 09CAE9) hidden inside the wall of the first major chamber of the cave. It can only be seen by clipping through the walls or in the Creation Kit, and is unique to this cave.
- Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) assigns Snapleg Cave to [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) instead.

## Bugs
- One of the goats may be partially glitched into the ground.
- There's a deathbell in a crate in the first room that is incorrectly owned by [The Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md). Taking it therefore counts as stealing. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- The spellcaster trap that shoots ice spikes may fire in rapid three-spike volleys (rather than singular spikes, as it should), making it especially deadly. If it happens to be shooting three-spike rounds, it will also be heard shooting at the wall leading up to it, well before you reach it. <sup>**?**</sup>
