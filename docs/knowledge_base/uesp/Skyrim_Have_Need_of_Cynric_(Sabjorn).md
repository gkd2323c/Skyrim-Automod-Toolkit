[Sabjorn](Skyrim_Sabjorn.md),

This is my third and final offer. I feel that I've been more than generous with the amounts I've stated to buy your meadery. There's no reason for competition to stifle both of our businesses. Unified, we could make [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) a household name and bury anyone else who tries to start a similar business in Skyrim. If you wish to accept my offer, please come to [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) and stay at my [manor](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Manor.md) as a guest. I think you'll find that being a part of the Black-Briar family would be both lucrative and life-saving.

[Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md)
