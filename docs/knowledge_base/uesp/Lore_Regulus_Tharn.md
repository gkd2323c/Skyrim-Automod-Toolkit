[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/ON-crown_store-Imperial_Battlemage_Costume_Pack.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Imperial_Battlemage_Costume_Pack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Imperial_Battlemage_Costume_Pack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Imperial_Battlemage_Costume_Pack.jpg) Various Imperial battlemages Regulus Tharn was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the royal [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), ancestor and descendant of numerous famous figures. He was one of many members of the Tharn family who served nobly and well under the [Emperors](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) of the [Reman Dynasty](Lore_Reman_Empire.md). He was paramount in reviving the tradition of the [Imperial Battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) during his times.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup>

In the times prior to the Reman Dynasty, during the days of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) the original Imperial Battlemages were an elite unit separate from the [Legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md). In time, the idea of a "battlemage" became less novel, and Imperial Battlemages began to serve alongside the regular Legions as spellcasters. During the times of the Reman Dynasty, partially due to the efforts of Regulus Tharn, the Imperial Battlemages were further exalted with the creation of the "Imperial Battlemage" title, a prestigious position traditionally granted to a unit's leader.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CATLA2-2)</sup>
