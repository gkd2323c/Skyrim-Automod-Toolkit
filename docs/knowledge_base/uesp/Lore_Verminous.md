[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Verminous_Fabricant.jpg) A Verminous Fabricant, a biomechanical imitation of the Verminous The Verminous are bipedal [reptilian](Lore_Reptile.md) beasts that were once common across [Velothi](Lore_Velothi.md) [lands](Lore_Morrowind.md), but went extinct long before the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) created [Verminous Fabricants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verminous_Fabricant), artificial [imitations](Lore_Fabricant.md) that bore the name of the Verminous, to preserve part of the past and serve as a reminder of things that once were.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LACW-1)</sup> The original Verminous were perceived as a "small", as in relatively unnoteworthy species.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LACW-1)</sup> Knowledge of the Verminous had long faded from public consciousness following their extinction, as the residents of modern-day [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FH-2)</sup> and even some residents of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) were unfamiliar with the species the fabricants were based on.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ASo F-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Verminous Fabricants are modeled after dinosaurs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-4)</sup><sup>:317</sup> They share many visual similarities to [dromaeosaurs](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dromaeosauridae) or [troodontids](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Troodontidae) specifically.
- [Verminous Catfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verminous_Catfish) are a species of [fish](Lore_Fish.md) that were once presumed extinct but could be found in the Clockwork City, though it is unknown if they share any qualities with the Verminous or Verminous Fabricants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOVC-5)</sup>
