[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-npc-Valga_Vinicia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valga_Vinicia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valga_Vinicia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valga_Vinicia.jpg) Valga Vinicia **Valga Vinicia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) and innkeeper of the [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). She has 100 gold available to barter with and buys food.

Valga tends to the inn's counter 24 hours a day, never taking a break to drink, eat, or sleep.

She wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a0). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_The_Dead_Man%27s_Drink) to the inn as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[Moss Mother Cavern](Skyrim_Moss_Mother_Cavern_(quest).md)**: Help an [injured hunter](Skyrim_Valdr.md) avenge his fallen companions.

## Dialogue
Valga will greet you with:

*"Welcome to Dead Man's Drink."* *"I escaped fighting in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) only to have it track me down again in Skyrim."* *"In Falkreath if I don't know it, it's not worth knowing."* *"I wouldn't complain if we could string together a few sunny days now and then."* **Where can I learn more about magic?** *"The people of Skyrim don't like magic much, so about the only place you're likely to find a teacher is the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md)."* **Heard any rumors lately?** **(If you're level 16 or higher)** *"Folks have gone missing the past few weeks. Word now's that our hunters are gone, too. Say, if you're traveling, could you keep an eye out for them? [Valdr](Skyrim_Valdr.md) and [his](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ari) [men](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Niels). I think they were after a [bear](Skyrim_Bear.md) up [north](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moss_Mother_Cavern)."* When exiting conversation, she'll say:

*"You hear any juicy gossip in town, be sure to share it with me!"* *"I'll be right here if you need anything else."*
## Conversations
She can often be seen conversing with other Falkreath citizens:

**[Narri](Skyrim_Narri.md)**: *"At some point we should hire [Tekla](Skyrim_Tekla.md) on here. If she could just spend less time serving [Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md)..."*
**Valga**: *"I wish I could afford to pay you both, Narri. I really do."* **Valga**: *"Tekla! It's good to see you, girl. How's Dengeir treating you?"*
**Tekla**: *"As he should I suppose."*
**Valga**: *"Hmmm, well don't take what you don't have to."* **[Solaf](Skyrim_Solaf.md)**: *"Keep them coming and keep them strong, Valga."*
**Valga**: *"Will do."*
## Unused Conversations
She was also supposed to have a conversation with local apothecary, [Zaria](Skyrim_Zaria.md). However, Zaria never leaves [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md) so the conversation will never be heard:

**Zaria**: *"The way this war is going you'll soon have plenty of veterans like me to buy drinks."*
**Valga**: *"The way this war is going there might not be anyone left to drink soon."*
## Notes
- Valga does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female Imperial face. She shares this quirk with the owner of [Mixwater Mill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mixwater_Mill), [Gilfre](Skyrim_Gilfre.md).
- The dialogue tied to the rumor about Moss Mother Cavern refers to an NPC known as **Brandr** internally. This person might've been an earlier iteration of Valga.
