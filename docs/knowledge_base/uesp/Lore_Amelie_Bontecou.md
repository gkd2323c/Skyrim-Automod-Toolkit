[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Amelie Bontecou was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) in the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>

She became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying the previous Chieftain [Grjotgaror](Lore_Grjotgaror.md) and presenting his head as a battle trophy. She was chieftain for three years.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
