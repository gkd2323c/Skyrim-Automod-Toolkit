Anders's Message **by [Anders](Skyrim_Anders.md)** *A dead bandit's note about betraying his partner only to end up trapped in a well*
---
I can't believe it. I came all this way, solved all of those ridiculous riddles just to end up here! After I memorized [Nystrom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nystrom)'s [clues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nystrom%27s_Journal) he'd collected from Lythelus, I slit his throat. Now I guess the joke's on me, because I'm stuck here and I don't see a way out. Hope someone comes by soon, I'm getting hungry.
