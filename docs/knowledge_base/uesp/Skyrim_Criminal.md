[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-npc-Criminal.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Criminal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Criminal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Criminal.jpg) A version of the criminal The **Criminal** is a rogue [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) who has [escaped jail](Skyrim_Escaped_Criminal.md), and can be found in any of the nine [holds](Skyrim_Holds.md). Your job is to kill them, but they can be difficult to track down as they constantly travel back and forth. Their appearance is random and their equipment and stats are leveled ([any from this list](Skyrim_Bandit.md#Light_Armor)).
