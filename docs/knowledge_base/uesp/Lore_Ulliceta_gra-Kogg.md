Ulliceta gra-Kogg was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) mage that lived during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

Ulliceta was from the Kogg Clan of Orcs, but was later inducted into the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) around [2E 232](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_232). She was forced to abandon her previous life in favor of the Old Ways, which is the central focus of the Psijic Order. Ulliceta gra-Kogg would study at the Psijic Order for many years, gaining a reputation among her teachers, being called *Adma'na*, which is "poor listener" in [High Elvish](Lore_High_Elvish.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONUlliceta-1)</sup> Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ulliceta had uncovered the Eye of Rememberance and decided to traverse the Traitor's Vault, which was formerly [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md)'s laboratory during his time on [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). Ulliceta and an [associate](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the Psijic Order explored the ruins, uncovering Mannimarco and [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md)'s early history and rivalry. However, Ulliceta was caught by the Relicmaster, Glenadir. This resulted in Ulliceta's banishment from the Psijic Order. She would later relocate to the newly-built second [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md), where she remained at the Temple of Ire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HFU-2)</sup>

Ulliceta gra-Kogg quickly joined the local [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) and eventually became the Magister of the Mages Guild at [Nova Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md#Nova_Orsinium) as of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Ulliceta was at the forefront of defending the use of [Necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) against Voth Karlyss, the Magister of [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md). In the end, Arch-Mage [Hannibal Traven](Lore_Hannibal_Traven.md) banned guild mages from practicing the Black Arts at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). It was revealed that not only was Ulliceta an apologist of Necromancy, but a practitioner; when the [Order of the Lamp](Lore_Order_of_the_Lamp.md) attempted to apprehend her, she was able to escape their clutches. Ulliceta's argument in favor of Necromancy was documented in *[The Black Arts On Trial](Lore_The_Black_Arts_On_Trial.md)*, as Traven felt it was worthy of inclusion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBOT-3)</sup> Her ultimate fate is unknown.
