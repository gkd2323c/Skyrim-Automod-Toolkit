*"Calling all adventurers, sellswords and spellslingers, heroes and ne'er-do-wells!
Do you long for a greater challenge? Try your might at the Battlegrounds, where games of glory and gore await you! We seek the greatest champions of the land for our contests of strategy and skill."* Battlemaster Rivyn was the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) head of the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md), a guild created to gather the greatest fighters in Tamriel for bloodthirsty sport.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Rivyn Dialogue-1)</sup>

He advertised this guild in major cities throughout Tamriel, through flyers<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-For Glory-2)</sup> or word of mouth at local Gladiator's Quarters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOGladiator-3)</sup> He encouraged newcomer gladiators to participate regardless of winning or losing,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tutorial-4)</sup> whilst offering more specialized challenges, alongside rewards, to less novice champions. These challenges focused on one of three aspects: Personal contribution, striving for victory, and repeated participation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Rivyn Dialogue-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dailies-5)</sup> To gladiators who achieved victory, completed matches and especially those who distinguished themselves on a weekly basis, he'd send personal letters and rewards.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Letter Congrats-6)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_2E_582.md), he sent a letter to the [Hero of the Outer Watch](Lore_Hero.md#Hero_of_the_Outer_Watch) requesting assistance in dealing with a mysterious third party which was illegally using, and damaging, the Circle of Champions brand. This was later discovered to be a part of one of [Deslandra](Lore_Deslandra.md)'s many plots throughout Tamriel.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Cyrod Gazetteer-7)</sup>
