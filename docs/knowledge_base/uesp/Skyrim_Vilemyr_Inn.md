## Vilemyr Inn
It is a typical Nord inn, with a fire pit down the middle of the common room. The L-shaped counter is at the southeast end, with five long wooden tables along the southwest and northeast walls. There are three doorways leading to bedrooms, two on the northeast side and a larger one on the southwest side. Against the southeast wall is a large vat, several food barrels and a pile of firewood, with racks hanging from the ceiling in front of them. These hold eight [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), also hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md). These are in front of a set of shelves holding five bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) and three bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). On the counter are several pieces of cheese, a [salmon steak](Skyrim_Salmon_Steak.md), a bottle of alto wine and a bottle of wine. Under the counter is a copy of *[Herbane's Bestiary: Hagravens](Skyrim_Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Hagravens.md)*, five loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md), a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) and an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md).

Either side of the doorway into the larger bedroom are a set of shelves holding several loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), six bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine, seven bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) and three bunches of elves ear. The long wooden table against the walls typically hold bread and a selection of wine and alto wine. However the table in the middle of the northeast wall also holds two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) and a [pine thrush egg](Skyrim_Pine_Thrush_Egg.md), while the table in the northern corner of the room also holds a selection of cheeses and two salmon steaks.

The larger bedroom is the room you will be shown to if renting a room for the night. It consists of two single beds; one in the southern corner, one in the western corner. The second of these is used by [Lynly Star-Sung](Skyrim_Lynly_Star-Sung.md). There is a long [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) against the northwest wall that contains only minor loot. There is also a small square wooden table and chair against the northeast wall holding a small coin purse, four loose septims, and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

The two smaller bedrooms are opposite the larger bedroom. The left-hand bedroom is used by [Gwilin](Skyrim_Gwilin.md) and contains a single owned bed in the northern corner with an unlocked chest and wardrobe at the foot of the bed and a small round wooden table and chair beside the bed. On the table is a bottle of [Black-Briar mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md). The right-hand bedroom is used by [Temba Wide-Arm](Skyrim_Temba_Wide-Arm.md) and has a single owned bed in the eastern corner, with an unlocked chest and dresser at the foot of the bed and a small round wooden table and chair beside it. Both of these chests contain only minor loot as well.

Many of the town's residents will often visit the inn during the evening.
