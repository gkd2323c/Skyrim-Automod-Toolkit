The terrain around it is a mix of plains and mudflats.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-2)</sup> The battlefield of Baldaras is found nearby.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-3)</sup>

## History
During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Greenheights was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Helkan and had a rivalries with [Karththor Heights](Lore_Karththor_Heights.md), [Sailen Vulgate](Lore_Sailen_Vulgate.md), and [Helnim Wall](Lore_Helnim_Wall.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-4)</sup> Greenheights was neighbored by several settlements (many of them rivals), including [Draloris](Lore_Draloris.md) to the southwest, Helnim Wall to the west, Karththor Heights to the north, and Sailen Vulgate to the south.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup>
