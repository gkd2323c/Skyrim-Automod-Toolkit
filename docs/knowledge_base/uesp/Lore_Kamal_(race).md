# Kamal (race)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/ON-item-armor-Akaviri_Shield_1.jpg/200px-ON-item-armor-Akaviri_Shield_1.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Akaviri_Shield_1.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-armor-Akaviri_Shield_1.jpg) Kamali Shield The Kamal (or **Kamali**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> are a race of "snow demons" who live in [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGTTE3OL-2)</sup> The word means "Snow Hell".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MA-3)</sup> According to *[Mysterious Akavir](Lore_Mysterious_Akavir.md)*, the snow demons spend the winter frozen solid, but, when summer comes, they thaw out and attack [Tang Mo](Lore_Tang_Mo_(place).md), always unsuccessfully.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MA-3)</sup>

## History
In [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTTEP-4)</sup> the Kamal invaded [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) under their king, [Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal](Lore_Ada%27Soom_Dir-Kamal.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGTTE1S-5)</sup> reportedly searching for a person or object they called the "[Ordained Receptacle](Lore_Ordained_Receptacle.md)".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSAI-6)</sup> The Kamal fleet bypassed [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) entirely and landed near [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) in Eastern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), catching the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) off-guard and sacking the city.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Jt SK-7)</sup> Oddly, the Kamal army then headed south but ignored [Riften](Lore_Riften.md), marching into the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region of Morrowind. There, on a beach just to the west of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) that would later be named [Vivec's Antlers](Lore_Vivec%27s_Antlers.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESO-8)</sup> they were surrounded by a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) army led by [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) and a Nord army led by Prince [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSAI-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Jt SK-7)</sup> The Kamal fought ferociously, hoping to last long enough for their fleet to arrive and evacuate them, but, at the last moment, a phalanx of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) joined the fray. With the aid of a tidal wave summoned by [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESO-8)</sup> the Kamal were obliterated by the three unlikely allies.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSAI-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Jt SK-7)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GTTEP-4)</sup>

This was not the last Tamriel would see of the Kamal, however. Sometime before [2E 812](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_812), remnants of the Kamal invasion force who had somehow survived the slaughter at Vivec's Antlers resurfaced in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), seizing the Ruby Throne from the successors of the warlord [Attrebus](Lore_Attrebus_(warlord).md). The Kamal were soon joined by the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md), who had previously fled to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) to avoid Attrebus' persecution. The Kamal and Rimmen Akaviri attempted to rebuild the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md), but the effort was doomed to failure.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-PGTTE1E-9)</sup>
