[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-item-Her_Hand_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Her_Hand_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Her_Hand_Boots.jpg) Her Hand Boots Her Hand Boots serve as the leg armor for the [set of armor](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor_Set.md) used by the members of Almalexia's personal guard. Alongside these boots, this set of equipment also includes [cuirass](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Armor.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Gauntlets.md), and a [helmet](Skyrim_Her_Hand_Helmet.md). The armor has been enchanted to [fortify Stamina](Skyrim_Fortify_Stamina.md) by seventy points, improving combat capabilities of Almalexia's ordinators. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) these boots requires a piece of [Chitin Plate](Skyrim_Chitin_Plate.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

There is also a version of this armor that does not have an enchantment already placed upon it, leaving its magical effects up to you.
