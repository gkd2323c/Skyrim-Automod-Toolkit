**Notice**

I hereby challenge any who would read this notice to a fishing contest! Let us test our mettle and place bets on who the greatest fisher this side of the Karth river really is.

I shall match any wager, so bet as much as you dare. I warn you, however, that none have bested me. In fact, every challenger that has faced me has never set foot in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) again, no doubt out of shame.

Meet me on the docks if you think you're up to it.

-- Brutius
