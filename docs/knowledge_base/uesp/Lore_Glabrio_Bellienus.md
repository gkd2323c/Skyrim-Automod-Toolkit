Glabrio Bellienus was the Personal Secretary to the [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) during the reign of [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md), circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Package-1)</sup> He was seemingly charged with dispatching orders to localized agents of the [Imperial Order of Blades](Lore_Blades.md) on behalf of the Emperor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Package-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Designer [Ken Rolston](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Ken_Rolston) was known to be inspired by the [Elizabethan era](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabethan_era) of history for the making of [*The Elder Scrolls III*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind);<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Oral History-2)</sup> Of similar note to the Bellienus character, [Queen Elizabeth I of England](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Elizabeth_I)'s own secretary, [Francis Walsingham](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_Walsingham), served as her spymaster who authored a highly effective intelligence network on behalf of his queen.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Francis Walsingham-3)</sup>
