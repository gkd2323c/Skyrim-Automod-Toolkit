# Firstblade

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Sword.jpg) Firstblade (Skyrim) Firstblade,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> also known as **Miraak's Sword**, is a powerful sword linked to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). It appears as a primal green blade with writhing sclerotic [watcher](Lore_Watcher.md) tentacles and [Seeker](Lore_Seeker.md) -sinew Threads wrapped around it,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWCodex-2)</sup> which extend to attack targets and drain their stamina during combat.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Miraak-3)</sup> When it is swung it takes the shape of a tentacle and grows in length, and even when not being swung, the sword writhes with tentacles around it, with them parting around the base of the sword to expose a green eye. It can be tempered with the use of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) and [daedra hearts](Lore_Daedra_Heart.md), much like other [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) weapons.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-4)</sup>

It is unknown if Firstblade was seen after the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), but by the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) the [Skaal](Lore_Skaal.md) of Solstheim knew it as a strange sword that belonged to a renegade Dragon Priest named Miraak far in the distant past.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> The sword was in possession of the renegade [dragon priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), perhaps dating back to the time before the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), and was in his possession during his time in Apocrypha. In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), he used the sword while battling the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) at the summit of Apocrypha, who killed him and took possession of the blade.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-At So A-5)</sup>
