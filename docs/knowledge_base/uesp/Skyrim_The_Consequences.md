## Quick Walkthrough
1. Inspect [Vonos](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vonos)' corpse and retrieve his journal.
2. Read [Vonos' Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vonos%27_Journal).
3. Enter the Oblivion Gate.
4. Defeat [Valkyn Methats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valkyn_Methats) and [Valkyn Gatanas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valkyn_Gatanas) and gather [Torment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torment) and [Scourge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge).
5. Return to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).
6. Escape [Red Scar Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Scar_Cavern).

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-interior-Red_Scar_Cavern.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Red_Scar_Cavern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Red_Scar_Cavern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Red_Scar_Cavern.jpg) The portal is open While delivering the killing blow to the leader of the [Mythic Dawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mythic_Dawn), Vonos, you feel something has gone wrong. His death has triggered the opening of the Oblivion Gate. Search his body to find his journal and read it to uncover the whole story and find you've been used to become the unwitting instrument of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), resulting in the final opening of the gate. Enter the [Deadlands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deadlands) by the portal to put an end to all of this.

In the Deadlands, aside from a strange flora and Fire Atronachs, you'll come across two powerful [Daedra](Skyrim_Daedra.md) wielding equally powerful artifacts, [Valkyn Methats](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valkyn_Methats) carries [Torment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torment), [Valkyn Gatanas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valkyn_Gatanas) carries [Scourge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scourge) and [Daedric Gauntlets of Negation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cause_Items#Daedric_Gauntlets_of_Negation). They can be found by taking either of two forks in the road where it spans the lava pools. Beware the Spiddal Sticks flanking the right-hand path where it crosses the lava, as the poison Spiddal gas is difficult to avoid here.

Kill Valkyn Gatanas and his [Daedric Horse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Horse) (at the end of the left path) to retrieve Scourge. In one of the containers behind them is the Summon Daedric Horse spell tome.

Kill Valkyn Methats and his Flame Atronachs (found at the end of the right path, past the Spiddal Stick "gate") to retrieve Torment. This will put a stop to the invasion. Return to Tamriel and find a way out of Red Scar Cavern. This will complete the quest.

On your return to Tamriel, the Vigilants will be raiding the cavern and battling the Mythic Dawn a second time. If you attack either faction, they will become hostile.

## Notes
- After the quest is complete, the Oblivion Gate still remains open, allowing you to return and harvest the new flora. The surviving Vigilants will stay guarding the place to make sure nothing comes out or any Mythic Dawn comes back.

- After exiting the cavern, weight changes for no reason.
