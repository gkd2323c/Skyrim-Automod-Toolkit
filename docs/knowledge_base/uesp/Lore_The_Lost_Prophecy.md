The Lost Prophecy **by [Gilvas Barelo](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Gilvas_Barelo)** *One of the Nerevarine Prophecies*
---
[from the Apographa of the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md), annotated by [Gilvas Barelo](Lore_Gilvas_Barelo.md), Abbot of [Holamayan](Lore_Holamayan.md)]

From seventh sign of eleventh generation,

Neither Hound nor [Guar](Lore_Guar.md), nor Seed nor Harrow,

But Dragon-born and far-star-marked,

Outlander Incarnate beneath [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md),

Blessed Guest counters seven curses,

Star-blessed hand wields thrice-cursed blade,

To reap the harvest of the unmourned house.

Notes

Lines 1-3: 'Of ancient family, but not of the four great [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) clans. Born under foreign stars and the sign of the [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) -- the [Imperial](Lore_Empire.md) sign.'

Line 4-5: 'Outlander Incarnate' appears as a formal epithet, stressing the linkage between the words. The Outlander Incarnate is a 'blessed guest', one not born of the tribes but accepted as a guest with rights of hearth and hospitality. Under Red Mountain he will confront and balance against seven curses. See the [Seven Curses prophecy](Lore_The_Seven_Curses.md); also, [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) is served by his seven kin, once great wizard-lords, called '[ash vampires](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md)' by the Ashlanders.

Line 6-7: 'Star-blessed' suggests [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), the [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) and patron of magic, fate, and prophecy. 'Thrice-cursed blade' may refer to a [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md) called [Keening](Lore_Keening.md), associated in certain legends with the [Battle of Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) craftlord [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md). 'Reap the harvest' is a reference to the proverb, "You harvest from the seeds you plant," which means you get what your labors deserve, in both a positive sense of reward and negative sense of punishment. The 'unmourned house' could be either or both of the lost [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of the Dunmer -- House Dwemer and [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md).
