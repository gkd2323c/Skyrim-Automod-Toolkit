# Stormcloaks


[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-banner-Stormcloaks.png/200px-SR-banner-Stormcloaks.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-banner-Stormcloaks.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-banner-Stormcloaks.png) Stormcloak banner The Stormcloaks (known more formally as the **Sons and Daughters of Skyrim**) were the main organization of rebels in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md). Strongest in eastern Skyrim and based in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), they were originally founded by [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) as a private army to advance his interests. A few years later in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), after Ulfric killed [High King Torygg](Lore_High_King_Torygg.md), their ranks swelled with those who supported his call for an end to the ban on [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) worship, as well as action against the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) and the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md). The Stormcloaks claimed to be the only ones who could call themselves true Sons (or Daughters) of Skyrim, although their ranks were not strictly limited to [Nords](Lore_Nord.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>
