# Skeever

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-creature-Skeever.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Skeever.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeever.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeever.jpg) A Skeever **Skeevers** are small, aggressive, rat-like animals that attack on sight. They may be encountered in dark areas, such as [caves](Skyrim_Caves.md) or [dungeons](Skyrim_Dungeons.md), as well as in the wilderness of Skyrim. They are generally considered to be the weakest enemy in the game, although they often carry the disease [Ataxia](Skyrim_Ataxia.md), which lowers your [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) and [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) skills. Skeever corpses may be looted for [skeever tails](Skyrim_Skeever_Tail.md) to be used as ingredients in alchemy. Dead skeevers found roasting over fires yield [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), but it is not possible to roast a skeever yourself.

## Notes
- The Venomfang Skeever is encountered during the [Dampened Spirits](Skyrim_Dampened_Spirits.md) quest in [Honningbrew Basement](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Basement.md).
- Skeevers may be present in the [cellar](Skyrim_Cellar.md) addition of Hearthfire homes. Any NPCs that reside in your house may mention that they heard odd noises coming from the basement. [One of these skeevers](Skyrim_Cellar.md#Notes) is sometimes non-aggressive and may be spared to become a pet.<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- [Adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) male [children](Skyrim_Child.md) may also bring home and adopt a unique skeever, [Biter](Skyrim_Biter.md).<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- [Corpulus Vinius](Skyrim_Corpulus_Vinius.md), the owner of [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), claims that skeevers weren't always as large as they are now.
- A [greater power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Power) named [Polymorph Skeever](Skyrim_Polymorph_Skeever.md) exists in game data which would have allowed you to transform into a skeever. However, the power is bugged and not found in the game.
