## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Skjor](Skyrim_Skjor.md) or [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) in [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md).
2. Travel to the randomly-determined location.
3. Rescue the citizen from the location's enemies.
4. Escort the citizen back to their hometown.
5. Return to Skjor or Vilkas for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Either [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) or [Skjor](Skyrim_Skjor.md) has received notice that someone has been kidnapped, and the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) have been asked to attempt a rescue. He will send you to the location of the kidnapped individual and tell you to free them from the location and then escort them to their nearby home. See Radiant Options below for possible variations of this quest.

Note that this is a repeating quest: each time you go to Vilkas or Skjor (when told to ask a Companion for work), he will give a different version of the quest.

## Radiant Options
This quest has several radiant options. The criteria used to select victims for this quest are:

- The victim must live in one of the marked habitation areas listed below.
- Must not have unique voice acting.
- Must not be a part of the following factions: [Player Housecarl Faction](Skyrim_PlayerHousecarlFaction.md), [The Companions](Skyrim_The_Companions.md), [Current Hireling](Skyrim_CurrentHireling.md), [Job Jarl Faction](Skyrim_JobJarlFaction.md), or [Current Follower Faction](Skyrim_CurrentFollowerFaction.md).
- Must not be marked as an [Essential NPC](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md).
- The victim's faction can not be related to you.
- Must be a unique character (excludes [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md), etc.).

## Notes
- If [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) is installed, some radiant locations may be on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). If you are in Skyrim, you will not receive a quest marker pointing you to the quest location, but must travel to Solstheim first (at which point any quest markers you have to quests in Skyrim will disappear).
- If you complete this quest successfully, then the citizen you rescue becomes a friend of yours. This may affect thane quests and other aspects of the game.
- Unlike most other radiant Companions quests, this quest starts immediately when it is offered, giving you no chance to refuse it.
