[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/SR-npc-Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced.jpg) Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced **Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) commander found at the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He is removed from the game if the Empire seizes Windhelm, with [Legate Hrollod](Skyrim_Legate_Hrollod.md) taking his place in the palace.

Yrsarald wears a set of [Stormcloak officer armor](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Armor.md) along with a matching pair of [bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Battle of the Champions
With the [Civil War Champions](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, Yrsarald will be involved in the quest [Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md). If you have not yet joined the Stormcloaks, you will have the option to speak to him about becoming the Stormcloaks' Champion, after giving him 5 [Snow Bear Pelts](Skyrim_Snow_Bear_Pelt.md):

- **I hear you're looking for a champion. Here's what you asked for.**

Once he agrees to let you act as Champion, he will say, *"You've done well."* You can answer either with **What's the mission?** or **I think we can help each other.** The conversation will then continue the same as below.

If instead you have already joined the Stormcloaks before installing this Creation, you will have received [his letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_the_Stormcloaks.md) and can then speak to him to accept his invitation to become Champion:

- **I heard you're in need of a champion.**
- *"This won't be easy. But that's why I'm sending you. You got the grit to get this done?"*

You can reply in a multitude of ways:

- **I have the grit, and then some.**
- *"Good. You've got a real fire in you. I like that... Talos guide you."*

- **They're as good as dead.**
- *"Excellent. Put the fear of the Nine into those motherless dogs."*

- **I'll do my best.**
- *"Good. And remember - keep the gods, and they'll keep you."*

- **The Nine will guide us to victory.**
- *"That's what I wanted to hear. Good luck out there."*

- **I'm a bit busy at the moment.**
- *"I see. Well, make it quick, or do you have better things to do than fight for the people of Skyrim?"*

If his request is accepted he will hand you the Storm-Bear Chest Key.

## Dialogue
When you have not joined either the Stormcloaks or the Imperial Legion he can say:

- **How does one join the Stormcloaks?**
- *"[Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s not running a social club. You don't join anything. Either you're a true son / daughter of Skyrim, or you're not. If you are, then you talk to the man himself. If you're not, well friend, you'd best walk away before this gets ugly."*

- **How do you feel about this war?**
- *"Skyrim is the birthplace of humanity. The birthplace of honor. And those snowbacked Imperials renounced both when they laid down before the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md). Worse still, to pay for the privilege to keep his throne, the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) sells our very gods - [Talos](Lore_Talos.md), no less! - to the damn [elves](Skyrim_High_Elf.md). And what did [Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md) do? He and the [Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) who supported him? They took the Emperor's gold. Sold out their people for a slave master's "[peace treaty](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md)." Ulfric did right killing him. Soon we'll rid Skyrim of elves, their bloody Justiciars, and the Jarls in their pockets. The whole damn lot of them. And we'll cleave through the Legion to get to them."*

- **Why are you called Stormcloaks?**
- *"At first they called us that to belittle our cause. But we gladly accept being named for Ulfric Stormcloak, the only true High King of Skyrim. We are the true sons and daughters of Skyrim, and Ulfric... he's the truest of us all. We fight to make our land great once more, to raise up strong, noble [thanes](Skyrim_Thane.md), and lay low false Jarls and the cowards who hide behind them. The sound of our fury is a storm about to break on the heads of our enemies. Our friendship, a cloak of true brotherhood for all who would wear it."*
