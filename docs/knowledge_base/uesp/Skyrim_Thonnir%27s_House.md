## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The house is to the north of the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md). There are two [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) bushes around the building. Two flights of stairs lead up to the decking at the front, which has a wooden bench against the building and a pile of firewood beside it. To the right are a couple of small wooden boat that have been dragged out of the water and overturned for storing. At the end of the jetty that runs in front of the house a small wooden boat has been moored.

### Thonnir's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thonnir%27s_House.jpg) Thonnir's home On your left when entering is a long wooden table holding two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), with a fish barrel to the left and a basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek). To the right of this is a set of shelves holding two more leeks, four [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), several pieces of cheese on top and hidden under basket on the bottom shelf is a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Aevar Stone-Singer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aevar_Stone-Singer)*. Above the shelves are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) all hanging from the ceiling. To the left of the shelves, hanging from the ceiling is a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). In the southwestern corner is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel and next to this against the west wall is a tall wardrobe and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot.

In the northwestern corner is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead). Coming out from the north wall is an owned double bed, where Thonnir sleeps. There is a large fireplace opposite the door in the middle of the north wall. On the mantlepiece there is a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and in front of the hearth a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand. To the right of the fireplace, hidden behind a basket is a unique item identified as [Michaela's Flagon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Michaela%27s_Flagon).

In the northeastern corner, against the east wall is a single owned bed where Virkmund sleeps. Next to the bed is a [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) and a pair of [drums](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum) are at the foot of the bed. In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs holding a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) and three bottles of Nord mead.
