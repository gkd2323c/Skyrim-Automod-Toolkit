Elden Root Safety Precautions *Warnings to visitors of Elden Root*
---
[![ON-concept-Elden Root.png](https://images.uesp.net/7/7c/ON-concept-Elden_Root.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Elden_Root.png)
- Be welcome among the branches of the great [graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md), but be aware:

- Look out for falling refuse and the scavengers it attracts on the ground beneath [the city](Lore_Elden_Root.md).

- Do not hack, cut, burn, or otherwise damage the tree. Violators face expulsion.

- Inter-branch travel via swinging vines is not recommended for visitors.

- Be cautious when walking after enjoying fine [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) beverages—some branches are more narrow than they might appear.

- You are responsible for your own safety. Any injuries resulting from your choice to ignore these warnings will be met with an appropriate amount of laughter.
