[3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) Privateer's Hold is a dungeon located near the northern border of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), a kingdom in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Specifically, it is north of the town, Gothway Garden, and just south of the river that splits the kingdom from its neighbor, [Tulune](Lore_Tulune.md). Privateer's Hold is a large dungeon under the earth and its signature feature is the large room and the throne high above it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFPrivateer-1)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), an [Agent](Lore_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) was en route to the kingdom's [capital](Lore_Daggerfall.md) before an unnatural storm conjured by [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md) wrecked the ship and left them stranded.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFStorm-2)</sup> The warrior managed to safely swim into the dungeon's caves and camp out until a mudslide sealed them in. They pressed forward and traversed through the dungeon and eventually escaped into the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) at large.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFPrivateer-1)</sup>
