Several **miners** can be found dead inside the [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md) during the earlier stages of the quest [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). Living miners can be found walking around outside the barrow during later stages of the quest.

## Related Quests
- **[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)**: Assist [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) in his search for the relics of [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md) in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).
