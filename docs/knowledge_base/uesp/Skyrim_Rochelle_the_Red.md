[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-npc-Rochelle_the_Red.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rochelle_the_Red.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rochelle_the_Red.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rochelle_the_Red.jpg) Rochelle the Red **Rochelle the Red** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bandit](Skyrim_EncClassBanditMelee.md) who may kidnap your [spouse](Skyrim_Marriage.md) if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed. She demands a [ransom](Skyrim_Ransom_Note.md) of 5,000 gold for your spouse's safe return. You can either pay the ransom, or kill her and the bandits yourself.

Her location depends on the [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) of the house your spouse was kidnapped from:

**[Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md)**: [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md)

**[Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md)**: [Bilegulch Mine](Skyrim_Bilegulch_Mine.md), [Cracked Tusk Keep](Skyrim_Cracked_Tusk_Keep.md), [Embershard Mine](Skyrim_Embershard_Mine.md), [Knifepoint Ridge](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md)

**[Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md)**: [Orotheim](Skyrim_Orotheim.md), [Robber's Gorge](Skyrim_Robber%27s_Gorge.md), [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md)
