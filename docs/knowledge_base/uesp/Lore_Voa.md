Archmage Voa was the Archmage of the [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) during the reign of [Prince A'tor](Lore_A%27tor.md) of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). After Voa's death, he was succeeded by [Archmage Jaganvir](Lore_Jaganvir.md), who was considered an unworthy successor by local guild members.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGAvik-1)</sup>

As chief advisor to the prince and a loyal [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGAvik-1)</sup> he served aboard the flagship during the [Battle of Hunding Bay](Lore_Battle_of_Hunding_Bay.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864). When the prince was mortally struck by an arrow, Voa saved his life in two pieces by placing his body in a stasis field and transferring his [soul](Lore_Soul.md) into a [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md). Voa used his magical ring to accomplish these feats. He died shortly afterward when the dragon [Nafaalilargus](Lore_Nafaalilargus.md) burned the flagship.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGFalicia-2)</sup>

The prince's body was saved by the [Restless League](Lore_Restless_League.md) and kept in secret storage in Stros M'Kai's temple of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) under the protection of the Brothers of Arkay.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGBasil-3)</sup> Due to some trick of undercurrent in the wake of the battle, Voa's body and ring ended up in the goblin caverns below Stros M'Kai.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-KJ-4)</sup> [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) found the cavern while trying to track down Kithral, an acolyte at the temple of Arkay, who theorized the location of Voa's final resting place. Cyrus traversed the caverns to locate the Archmage's ring and killed the massive [ogre](Lore_Ogre.md) guarding it.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RGArchmages Ring-5)</sup>

The soul gem containing A'tor's soul would end up in the hands of the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) necromancer [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md), via [Iszara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Iszara), but eventually was reclaimed by [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md). With Voa's ring and A'tor's soul gem, Cyrus brought the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) witchmother Saban to the temple of Arkay to restore the prince to life. As fate would have it, the soul transferred to the prince's sword, becoming the [Soul Sword](Lore_Soul_Sword.md). Despite this failure, Cyrus rallied the Restless League to topple the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md)'s military occupation of the island.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RGRaze The Palace-6)</sup>

## Notes
- During his tenure as Archmage, Voa used his close relationship with A'tor to ensure the Mages Guild had a monopoly on sales of all magical items, to the detriment of local business owners.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-RGGerrick-7)</sup>
- Voa's ring bore the symbol of the Crowns upon it.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RGVoas Ring-8)</sup>
