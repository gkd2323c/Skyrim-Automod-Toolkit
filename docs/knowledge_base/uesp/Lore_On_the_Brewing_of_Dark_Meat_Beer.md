On the Brewing of Dark Meat Beer **by [Anonymous Bosmeri Brewer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Anonymous_Bosmeri_Brewer)** *Instructions on brewing Bosmeri alcohol with insects and meat*
---
The majority of this book is lifted from a previous unofficial text, called *[The Dreadful Theft of the Sun's Dusk Ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Dreadful_Theft_of_the_Sun%27s_Dusk_Ale)*.

---
First of all, it takes years for all the meat and bugs to fall in. And they have to fall naturally. [Crosswings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crosswing) have a fierce bitterness when they die afraid.

Then, you must stretch seventeen strings of the thinnest [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md) -gut over each barrel. Press aged meat onto the strings, being careful not to let any fall before it has properly rotted. Then dust the meat with the finely chopped skins of the red-striped [frog](Lore_Frog.md) to make sure the crosswings are drunk and happy when they hit the drink.

You do this every month for three or more years, until you have enough meat and bugs to fill the barrel. And that's not even counting the enchantments. You must have spells to attract the crosswings and [mimics](Lore_Mimic.md). And spells to keep out the [flipbacks](Lore_Flipback.md), [tree-hoppers](Lore_Tree-Hopper.md), and [hoarvor](Lore_Hoarvor.md) larvae.

There are spells to encourage the green mold and prevent the brown and white molds. Then you have to seal the barrel for nine or more years until the last of the green mold turns. Good thing we have long lives, eh?

— Anonymous [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) Brewer
