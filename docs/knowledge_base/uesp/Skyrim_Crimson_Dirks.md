# Crimson Dirks


These former members are featured in the "Alternate Armors" series of [Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) and provide an overarching plot to the quests featured in those individual releases.

For more information, see the [main lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crimson_Dirks).

## Related Quests
- **[Ancient Ice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Ice)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Stalhrim_Fur.md)</sup>: Bring a murderer to justice in northern [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).
- **[Beyond the Grave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beyond_the_Grave)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Plate.md)</sup>: Follow the trail of a set of valuable [Daedric Plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Plate_Armor) armor.
- **[Bones for a Crow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bones_for_a_Crow)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dragon_Plate.md)</sup>: Hunt down the Forsworn Crowstooth.
- **[Brothers in Irons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Brothers_in_Irons)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Iron.md)</sup>: Investigate a wanted criminal.
- **[Double-Edged](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Double-Edged)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Leather.md)</sup>: Determine the fate of a Thalmor agent.
- **[Fan Favorite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fan_Favorite)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dwarven_Mail.md)</sup>: Track down a fugitive Arena combatant.
- **[Gambler's Edge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gambler%27s_Edge)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Scaled.md)</sup>: Search for an ill-fated gambler at the pit fights in [Cragslane Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern).
- **[Heart of Crimson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_of_Crimson)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Ebony_Plate.md)</sup>: Confront the murderous leader of a bandit gang.
- **[Mightier than the Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mightier_than_the_Sword)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.md)</sup>: Track down some Dwarven loot and beat the competition.
- **[Missing Merchant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Missing_Merchant)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Daedric_Mail.md)</sup>: A powerful set of armor has come on the market.
- **[Once A Hunter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Once_A_Hunter)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Elven_Hunter.md)</sup>: Track down a hunter with a dangerous past.
- **[Over the Edge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Over_the_Edge)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Steel_Soldier.md)</sup>: A criminal has sensed his past catching up with him.
- **[Smith 'N Slash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Smith_%27N_Slash)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Orcish_Plate.md)</sup>: Defeat an [Orc blacksmith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yakhtu_gra-Orkulg) and her bandits to obtain an enchanted set of armor.
- **[Tilted Scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tilted_Scales)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dragonscale.md)</sup>: Retrieve a set of ancient armor from an old warrior.
- **[When the Cat's Away](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:When_the_Cat%27s_Away)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Silver.md)</sup>: Find a stash of [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) armor.
