## Ingredients
11 guaranteed samples can be found in 3 different locations:

- 8 in [Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 2 in [Irkngthand Arcanex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Irkngthand_Arcanex) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 1 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

With the [Dawnguard add-on](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) 1 more guaranteed sample can be found in 1 different location:

- 1 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

With the [Dragonborn add-on](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) 12 more guaranteed samples can be found in 1 different location:

- 12 in [Kagrumez](Skyrim_Kagrumez.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> ([Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
[Dwarven centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) (including [Dwarven centurion guardians](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Centurion_Guardian) and [Dwarven centurion masters](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion_Master.md)) are guaranteed to drop at least one sample of Dwarven oil. All other automatons, in particular all varieties of [Dwarven spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md), [Dwarven spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md), and [Dwarven ballistae](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ballista.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>, have a 50% chance of dropping one sample.

## Notes
- Dwarven oil can be combined with an [albino spider pod](Skyrim_Albino_Spider_Pod.md) at the [imbuing chamber](Skyrim_Imbuing_Chamber.md) in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) to create an [oil spider](Skyrim_Oil_Spider_(item).md).<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
