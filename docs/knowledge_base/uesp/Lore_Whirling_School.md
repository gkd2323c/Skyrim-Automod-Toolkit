# Whirling School

The Whirling School was a group of Velothi philosophers, who according to the accounts present in *[The 36 Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*, were once taught by [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) following the defeat of the [Moon Axle](Lore_Moon_Axle.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S20-1)</sup>

### Sermon 20
The origins of the first Whirling School is described in *[Sermon 20](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_20.md)*. It was created shortly after the fall of [Moon Axle](Lore_Moon_Axle.md), one of the [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)'s [Monster Children](Lore_Monster_Children.md). The lines Moon Axle was made of were collected by Velothi philosophers and placed in caverns. Vivec spent a year teaching the scholars how to turn the lines of his son into the spokes of mystery wheels.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S20-1)</sup>

Vivec's early interactions with his first students led him to formulate several observations:

** *Alike the egg-layered universe is this morbid possession of three-distant coverage, soul-wrecked and alive, like my name is alive. In this cloister you have discovered one walking path, hilled like a sword but more coarsened. So edged it is that it has to be whispered to keep the tongue from bleeding, where its signs evacuate their former meanings, like empires that tarry too long.*

*The sword is estrangement from statesmanship.*

*Look on the estimable lines of my son, now crafted star-wise, his every limb equidistant from the center. Is he solved because I will it so? There cannot be a second stage. Think on the theory that my existence promulgates the five elements and alike the egg-layered universe I am cause for great density. Here is a thought that can break the wagon's axle; here is another that can soar.*
—Vivec<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-S20-1)</sup>
### Sermon 21
*[Sermon 21](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_21.md)* focuses on Vivec's teachings and contains the Eight Scriptures of the Wheel, formed during his time at the Whirling School. They are as follows:<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>

1. *The Spokes are the eight components of chaos, as yet solidified by the law of time: static change, if you will, something the lizard gods refer to as the Striking. That is the reptile wheel, coiled potential, ever-preamble to the never-action.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
2. *They are the lent bones of the Aedra, the Eight gift-limbs to SITHISIT, the wet earth of the new star our home. Outside them is the Aurbis, and not within. Like most things inexplicable, it is a circle. Circles are confused serpents, striking and striking and never given leave to bite. The Aedra would have you believe different, but they were givers before liars. Lies have turned them into biters. Their teeth are the proselytizers; to convert is to place oneself in the mouth of falsehood; even to propitiate is to be swallowed.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
3. *The enlightened are those uneaten by the world.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
4. *The spaces between the gift-limbs number sixteen, the signal shapes of the Demon Princedoms. It is the key and the lock, series and manticore.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
5. *Look at the majesty sideways and all you see is the Tower, which our ancestors made idols from. Look at its center and all you see is the begotten hole, second serpent, womb-ready for the Right Reaching, exact and without enchantment.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
6. *The heart of the second serpent holds the secret triangular gate.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
7. *Look at the secret triangular gate sideways and you see the secret Tower.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>
8. *The secret Tower within the Tower is the shape of the only name of God, I.*<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-S21-2)</sup>

### Sermon 22
*[Sermon 22](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_22.md)* opens with Vivec departing the First Whirling School and returning to the space that was not a space.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-S22-3)</sup>
