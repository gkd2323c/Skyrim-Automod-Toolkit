[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/MW-place-Foyada_Bani-Dad.jpg/200px-MW-place-Foyada_Bani-Dad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Bani-Dad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Bani-Dad.jpg) Foyada Bani-Dad Foyada Bani-Dad is one of the several [foyadas](Lore_Foyada.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the island district of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The volcanic ravines (or "fire-rivers") are valleys formed by lava flows from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), functioning as drainage basins for lava when the volcano is active, or alternatively forming pathways to travel on when dormant and devoid of lava.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen F-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWAjira-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup>

The foyada starts at the caldera of Red Mountain and travels northwest through the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) to the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), where the ravine's seamouth is marked by a derelict shipwreck. This headland marks the coastal divide between the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md) and the north Ashlands.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dialogue Urshilaku-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWMeet Sul-5)</sup> Bani-Dad appears to be one of the largest foyadas on the island, second only to [Mamaea](Lore_Foyada_Mamaea.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWMap-6)</sup>

## Geography
There are many locations found inside Foyada Bani-Dad, including [Ularradallaku](Lore_Ularradallaku.md), Vemynal, and the Charma's Breath entrance to [Kogoruhn](Lore_Kogoruhn.md) found close to its source near Red Mountain, Shishi and [Bthungthumz](Lore_Bthungthumz.md) found in the middle,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWAryon-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MWShishi Report-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWBaladas-9)</sup> and finally Druscashti and Sha-Adnius found at the end near the seamouth. Many caves are found around the foyada, with notable nearby locations including the [Urshilaku Camp](Lore_Urshilaku_Tribe.md) traditionally set up in a low hollow near the seamouth,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dialogue Urshilaku-4)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONUrshilaku-10)</sup> [Ashurnabitashpi Shrine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ashurnabitashpi)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dialogue Urshilaku-4)</sup> and Falasmaryon to the east,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWTanis Mahdi-11)</sup> and [Khuul](Lore_Khuul.md) and [Maar Gan](Lore_Maar_Gan.md) to the west.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWTheldyn Virith-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Dialogue Maar Gan-13)</sup> [Ald'ruhn](Lore_Ald%27ruhn.md) and Endusal are found between the Foyada Mamaea and Bani-Dad.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MWEndusal-14)</sup>

A foyada surrounding Falasmaryon serves as a tributary of Bani-Dad.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-VSH-15)</sup> Lava from Bani-Dad appears to flow downwards into the Forgotten Wastes as well.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Forgotten Wastes-16)</sup> Foyada Bani-Dad and its tributaries were filled with lava at the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) and [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-AMap-17)</sup> The foyada had dried up by the time of [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), and the [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md) went through it.
