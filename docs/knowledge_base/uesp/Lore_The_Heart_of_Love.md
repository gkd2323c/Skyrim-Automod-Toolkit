The Heart of Love *A short text on the god Mara*
---
Know, penitent, that [Mara](Lore_Mara.md) is always with you. Within your breast beats her love and affection. The symbol of her floral star adorns the walls at the wedding chapel. Her priests tend to the needs of husband and wife. And always is her gaze upon the young.

To complete your venerations here, intone: "Fivefold blessings upon the lost and lovelorn. The Heart pumps the blood that connects us across the [aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md). May her grace always be upon me."
