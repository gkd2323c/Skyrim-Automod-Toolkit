[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/OBR-place-Xavara.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Xavara.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Xavara.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Xavara.jpg) Xavara at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) (Oblivion Remastered) Xavara is a ruin in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), located in the swamps of [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), northeast of [Fellmoor](Lore_Fellmoor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup> Like the other ancient ruins that dot the realm, Xavara was originally built by the [Predecessors](Lore_Predecessors.md), mortal inhabitants of the Isles whose civilization was wiped out during a previous [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP-2)</sup> Very few surface ruins remain, and [root tunnels](Lore_Root.md) have broken through into Xavara's underground passages.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Xavara was home to a group of [Zealots](Lore_Zealots.md), outcast mortals who worship [Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The subterranean halls of the ruins were populated at this time by the Zealots and their [undead](Lore_Undead.md) minions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup> In particular, Xavara was noted for the [Flesh Atronachs](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md) that the Zealots could [conjure](Lore_Conjuration.md) for their protection.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The ruins were cleared out that year by [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>
