[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e1/SR-npc-Headless_Horseman.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Headless_Horseman.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Headless_Horseman.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Headless_Horseman.jpg) Headless Horseman [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-easter_egg-Legend_of_Sleepy_Hollow.jpg/200px-SR-easter_egg-Legend_of_Sleepy_Hollow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-easter_egg-Legend_of_Sleepy_Hollow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-easter_egg-Legend_of_Sleepy_Hollow.jpg) The horseman riding towards Hamvir's Rest The **Headless Horseman** is a headless [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) riding a spectral horse who [roams around Skyrim](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Headless_Horseman) at night. If you follow him, he will lead you to [Hamvir's Rest](Skyrim_Hamvir%27s_Rest.md), making various stops along the way. He can easily be followed on horseback, and will sometimes interact with you and his surroundings. He is difficult to keep up with if you are not mounted, but it is possible: sprint whenever you can, and if you have access to the [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) shout, you can use it to easily catch up to him. Alternately, using the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk to heal your Stamina quickly will also allow you to keep up.

The Headless Horseman wears a full set of unique armor that is identical to [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor) (with the added effect of making his head invisible), and carries a leveled [battleaxe](Skyrim_Battleaxe.md) on his back.

### Horse
His [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) has a [Base ID](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) of `0010BF90`, and is simply named 'Horse'. It is ethereal (weapons/attacks pass through it) and leaves a smoky blue trail behind it. It can be ridden like a normal horse but cannot be interacted with in any other way. If you are attacked while not mounted on the horse, it will attack the aggressor as other horses do when not being ridden. It does damage like a normal horse, but cannot be hurt, and has infinite stamina. The horse will also behave like an owned horse, meaning you can make it fast travel with you, but it will start heading back to where it spawned once you dismount. The horse reacts with the normal horse sound effect when it would normally take fall damage, but does not actually take any damage.

## Notes
- The Horseman will always start his ride between the hours of 10pm and 5:30am; however, he can be encountered during the daytime on his way to Hamvir's Rest.
- The Horseman is flagged as a ghost in the game data, making him immune to any attacks or spell effects.
- While normally going unseen, his head is actually exactly the same as the [Aspiring Mage](Skyrim_Aspiring_Mage.md)'s.

## Bugs
- Sometimes the Horseman can be found chasing his horse instead of riding it.
- The Horseman may glitch into an erratic motion sequence, in which he literally flies around in a seemingly random manner. This may occur if he is way behind his normal travel schedule, such as being encountered during the day. He can sometimes be found stuck in trees.
