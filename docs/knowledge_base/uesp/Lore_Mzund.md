[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Dwarven Helmet.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Dwarven Helmet.png) The Visage of Mzund Mzund is a [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md). Its exact location is unknown, though it was no further than eighteen days' travel from [Arkngthamz](Lore_Arkngthamz.md). Mzund is known to contain an ancient [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) library.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KJ-1)</sup> It was abandoned in [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700) when its original Dwemer inhabitants [mysteriously disappeared](Lore_Dwemer.md#Disappearance_of_the_Dwemer).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DII-2)</sup>

In the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the publication of *[The Aetherium Wars](Lore_The_Aetherium_Wars.md)* by [Taron Dreth](Lore_Taron_Dreth.md) prompted the explorer Katria to enter Mzund in search of the Aetherium Forge. Katria was the true author of the research published by Dreth, and within Mzund she discovered an ancient tome containing a map of [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) [Dwemer kingdoms](Lore_Empires.md#Dwemer_Kingdoms). Soon after visiting Mzund, she headed to Arkngthamz, where she fell to her death when an earthquake ripped open the ground beneath her, creating a chasm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KJ-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Katria-3)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) wizard Ulyn and his companion Eydis ventured to the ancient Dwemer site of Fahlbtharz in search of a Dwarven helmet known as the Visage of Mzund.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-UJ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EJ-5)</sup> They perished before being able to reach the artifact.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ulyn-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Eydis-7)</sup> The helmet was later discovered by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) within the treasure room inside Fahlbtharz. It bestowed the wielder the ability to release a powerful steam attack known as the Breath of Nchuak.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-VOM-8)</sup>
