[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-npc-Dealer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dealer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dealer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dealer.jpg) The Dealer The **Dealer** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) found behind a gated counter in [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md), where she sells [Redwater Skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Skooma).

She wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a1) and [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a0) and carries a small amount of gold, as well as a [Redwater Den Backrooms Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redwater_Den_Backrooms_Key).

## Related Quests
- **[The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md)**: Fill the [Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) from the Bloodspring at [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md).

## Dialogue
When you first arrive at Redwater Den and approach her, she can say:

- *"Ah, welcome, welcome! I don't believe we've seen you here before, have we? You can buy from me, then join any booth you'd like."*
- *"You seem tense. Join a booth and relax a bit. Or did you need another fix?"*

She can be asked questions when spoken to directly:

- - **What is this place?**
- *"Heh... wait, you're serious? This is the Redwater Den, purveyors of the best Skooma outside of [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md)."*

- - **How about a sample? (Persuade)**
- *"Since it's your first time... sure. Find a booth and enjoy."*

- - **What is so special about your skooma?**
- *"The secret is our mystic Redwater Spring. The magic in the waters opens the mind to experience Skooma in a way like never before."*

If asked again:

- **How about a sample? (Persuade)**
- *"Well... so long as you don't tell anyone. Here take it and slip into a booth before someone notices."* (Success)

- *"How about you pay for your habit like everybody else. We're not running a charity here."* (Failure)

When you leave Redwater Den, she'll call out to you as you go:

- *"Be sure to enjoy some of our Redwater Skooma before you leave."*
- *"Don't leave, you'd have better dreams here..."*
- *"Fine, suit yourself."*

## Conversations
She can be overheard speaking to a vampire:

- **Dealer**: *"That's the last of them. Poor sods can't resist after they get a taste of your dosed Skooma."*
- **Vampire**: *"The water from the spring makes the skooma so much more potent, we'll have all the thralls we'll ever need. Good thing the boss heard that old farmer running his mouth off in the tavern about finding this place."*
