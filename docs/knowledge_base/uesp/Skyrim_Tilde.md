[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-npc-Tilde.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tilde.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilde.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilde.jpg) Tilde **Tilde** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) who patrols [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md) outside of [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md).

She is clad in a complete set of Dawnguard heavy equipment. This includes the [cuirass](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Armor.md), matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Boots.md), as well as a [shield](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Shield.md) for blocking. She is equipped with a [Dawnguard war axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md) and a [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) supplemented with 12 [steel bolts](Skyrim_Steel_Bolt.md). She carries a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md) and a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

## Dialogue
Tilde shares her dialogue with [Mogrul](Skyrim_Mogrul_(Dawnguard).md), [Ollrod](Skyrim_Ollrod.md), and [Vori](Skyrim_Vori.md). They talk about fighting vampires.

- *"Got to keep alert. We don't want to get caught napping like the [Vigilants](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) did."*
- *"I don't think the vampires would be stupid enough to attack us here, but you never know."*
- *"I'm glad we're finally taking the fight to those monsters."*
- *"I hope the vampires try to attack us here. A lot easier than hunting them down in their holes."*
- *"[Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md) knows how to fight those monsters. He's been preparing for this day for a long time."*
- *"Now that the Dawnguard are back, the vampires' reign of terror is about to end."*
