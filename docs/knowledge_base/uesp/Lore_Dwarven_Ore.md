# Dwarven Ore

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/ON-node-Dwarven_Ore.jpg/200px-ON-node-Dwarven_Ore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Dwarven_Ore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Dwarven_Ore.jpg) Dwarven Ore Dwarven Ore is a naturally occurring ore deposit that could be found across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Dwarven Ore acquired its name due to the visual resemblance it bears to [Dwarven Metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md) when forged; however, it lacks the metal's durability, and metallurgists have concluded that it has no connection to the metal harvested from actual Dwemer devices.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KV-1)</sup>

It also features in poetry. The Dwarven ore was used as a substitute for the Dwarven metal and was used together with [brass](Lore_Brass.md) and [tin](Lore_Tin.md) in a poem about Lady Murcien, a lady who thought she was a [Dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lady_Murcien.27s_Folly-2)</sup>
