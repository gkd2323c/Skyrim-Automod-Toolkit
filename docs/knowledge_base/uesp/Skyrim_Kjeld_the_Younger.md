[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-npc-Kjeld_the_Younger.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kjeld_the_Younger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjeld_the_Younger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kjeld_the_Younger.jpg) Kjeld the Younger **Kjeld the Younger** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) living at [Braidwood Inn](Skyrim_Braidwood_Inn.md) in [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md). His parents, [Iddra](Skyrim_Iddra.md) and [Kjeld](Skyrim_Kjeld.md), manage the inn, as well as nearby [Steamscorch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine).

While the other miners sleep in the camp outside, Kjeld shares a room with his father at the Braidwood Inn where he sleeps every night between midnight and 7am. After a one-hour breakfast he enters the mine and spends eleven hours hacking away at the [malachite ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malachite_Ore), interrupted only by one-hour meals at noon and 6pm. At 7pm he spends a bit of time with the other miners in the camp before entering the inn for his bed at midnight.

He wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). He uses a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) in combat, and carries a spare [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) as well as [lower class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
The younger Kjeld is rather cocky, and will often brag that he can beat you in a fight, although there is no option to [brawl](Skyrim_Brawl.md) with him. He may rudely brush you off, or warn you that his father runs the town.

- *"You don't look so tough. I'll bet I could take you."*

- *"I've never seen you before. Leave me alone!"*

- *"Why are you still here?"*

- *"My pa runs this town, and you're going to be in big trouble if you mess with me."*

### Conversations
While he appears to be cocky, Kjeld the Younger is, in reality, nothing but a huge child, as evident by the conversations he has with his parents. Note that Kjeld the Younger was supposed to have a sister named Froa, but she is nowhere to be found in the game, although the conversations can still be heard:

## Notes
- According to game data, [Susanna the Wicked](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Susanna_the_Wicked) is tagged as his girlfriend. No-one in the game ever makes mention of this, however.
- Kjeld the Younger will not take over as publican of the inn if his parents are dead.
