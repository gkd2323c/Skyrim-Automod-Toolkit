Indulgence in Dune (Excerpt) *An excerpt of a travel guide promoting the Grinning Moons inn*
---
[![ON-concept-Khajiit building.png](https://images.uesp.net/d/de/ON-concept-Khajiit_building.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Khajiit_building.png)
- You can find anything in [Dune](Lore_Dune.md) if you have the coin and know where to look. Travelers seeking the full experience of [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) culture would do well to spend time at the Grinning Moons inn. Be prepared to pay a hefty sum, but know that what you will receive in exchange is worth every bit. I have never enjoyed more luxury in all my travels, and no one does indulgence like the Khajiit.

- You will be treated as royalty; from the sumptuous over-stuffed pillows and soft sheets to the finest that Khajiiti cuisine has to offer, your every desire will be attended. Expertly prepared local dishes and dangerously sweet beverages can be enjoyed in the dining hall, accompanied by indigenous musicians and dancers. I can guarantee that you won't want to leave.
