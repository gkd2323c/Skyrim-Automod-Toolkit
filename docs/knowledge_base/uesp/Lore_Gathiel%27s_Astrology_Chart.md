Gathiel's Astrology Chart **by [Treethane Rolon](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Treethane_Rolon)** *Notes on The Lady constellation*
---
[The Lady](Lore_The_Lady.md) is the sign of indomitable will and unflagging health. When she shines, she smiles on the sick and makes them well and rewards those who persevere in hardship.

(This is Gathiel's sign. It has been ascendent *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* since the night the forest started to turn on us. An omen of her good health, or something else?— Treethane Rolon.)
