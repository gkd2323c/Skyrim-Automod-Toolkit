[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/ON-misc-Lakemire_Xanmeer_Manor_Fish.jpg/200px-ON-misc-Lakemire_Xanmeer_Manor_Fish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Lakemire_Xanmeer_Manor_Fish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Lakemire_Xanmeer_Manor_Fish.jpg) Fish Fish are aquatic animals that live underwater. They possess gills that allow them to breathe, and most possess scales that cover their bodies. Some fish are eaten or used as [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) ingredients by the inhabitants of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

Fish are often caught by baiting them in the water with their preferred food and using nets or fishing poles to catch them. Fishermen, fisherwomen, and fisherfolk are those who catch fish for a living.
