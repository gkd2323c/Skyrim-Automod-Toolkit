# Death Hound

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Death_Hound.jpg) A Death Hound (Dawnguard) Death Hounds also known as **Daedric Hounds** and **Vampiric Hounds**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-goths-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-coldharhound-2)</sup> are monstrous, undead [canines](Lore_Dog.md) who can often be found in the company of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) or mortals.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Carmina-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-daedrahound-5)</sup> Some death hounds have been known to imprint with mortals who rescue them, in one known case with a vampire hunter.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Carmina-4)</sup> They have a bite as cold as the grave, which freezes its prey's flesh.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup> Their meat is edible, and they often wear valuable collars.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRDH-6)</sup> Death Hounds can track down people if they get a whiff of their blood; they are even capable of detecting the scent of a vampire lair from long distances.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Carmina-4)</sup>

Death hounds are voracious, indiscriminate predators that raze ecosystems where they are present.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup> They eviscerate and consume prey, not for survival, but due to their temperament.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup> Death hounds will leave nothing left alive if their populations are left unchecked.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup> Despite how harmful they can be to the environment, their presence can be vital to the ecology of the area they inhabit.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EOTDH-3)</sup> Not all Death Hounds are vicious; some can be very protective of their masters and even a loyal friend to mortals who own a death hound. In some cases refusing to drink their blood. Like vampires, death hounds are blood drinkers, with each one having their own preferences. At least one death hound was known to prefer the taste of [sheep's](Lore_Sheep.md) blood.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Carmina-4)</sup> Death Hound's are also are used by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and his daedric forces to patrol [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-coldharhound-2)</sup>
