[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/SR-npc-Varona_Nelas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Varona_Nelas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Varona_Nelas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Varona_Nelas.jpg) Varona Nelas **Varona Nelas** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in the small settlement of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md) who has the questionable honor of being personal steward to town founder [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md). Like the other residents, she is overworked and estranged by her employer's eccentric personality and wishes herself far away. However, she is soon going to [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) to pick up supplies, leading to her early demise and the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md).

Varona wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000f8713) and matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000cee82), and carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tel_Mithryn_Steward%27s_Key) to her house and a small amount of gold.

Varona will die (see [notes](#Notes)) after you visit Tel Mithryn for the first time, so a few steps must be followed to see her daily routine and witness the many conversations between her and the other inhabitants. First, you must not leave Tel Mithryn; second, you will have to complete [From the Ashes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:From_the_Ashes) before she dares venturing outside. After that, her daily routine is a busy one: She starts the day at 6am and paces around her quarters for two hours. At 8am she visits [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) in the local [Apothecary](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Apothecary.md), at 9am she visits the cook [Ulves Romoran](Skyrim_Ulves_Romoran.md) in the [Tel Mithryn Kitchen](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.md), at 10am she paces around outside, at 11am she checks up on Master [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) in the [tower](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md), then she heads back to her quarters for a one-hour lunch. At 1pm she paces around outside, at 2pm she visits Elynea, at 3pm she visits Ulves, and at 4pm she once again spends an hour outside. At 5pm she checks up on Neloth before going home for a one-hour dinner. At 7pm she makes one last visit to Elynea and then takes the rest of the evening off. Before the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md) is cleansed she was supposed to work on the shrine between 10pm and 6am but doesn't due to a [bug](#Bugs).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### From the Ashes
When you first pay a visit to Tel Mithryn, you will find Varona in the center of the settlement engaged in conversation with [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md). Varona is obviously concerned about Talvas' conjuration experiments and countless attempts to summon an [ash guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md), and she will try to get Talvas to act responsibly, with little luck. The dialogue will differ slightly if Neloth is in Raven Rock as part of the quest [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn_(quest).md):

**Varona**: *"Master Neloth has tasked Elynea with repairing the tower. Why aren't you doing this in the lab, where it would be safer for all of us?"*

*"I need to tell Master Neloth about that Ash Guardian, but I don't dare leave my home."* When everything is safe and Talvas has taken his spot inside the tower, she will start her daily routine.

## Notes
- When you arrive in Tel Mithryn for the first time you will find Varona arguing with [Talvas Fathyron](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md). She will then seek you out and engage in conversation. This actually marks the beginning of her death as speaking to her will cause her to die the next time you leave Tel Mithryn (going to the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md) is enough to kill her), marking the start of the quest [Reluctant Steward](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md).
- Another [Varona Nelas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Varona_Nelas) was encountered in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind). While they share the name and a loose affiliation with House Telvanni, Varona's conversations with Elynea suggest that Varona has been on Solstheim her entire life, while the Varona from Morrowind is found on Vvardenfell, confirming they are not the same person. - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)'s *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV__Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* also mentions "Varona Nelas" under the female Dark Elf "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".

## Bugs
- Before the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md) has been cleansed, Varona was supposed to work there every night between 10pm and 6am. Due to a developer oversight, her default (sandbox) package takes precedence and the work package never activates. This also means that her "wake-up" line will never be heard: *"Who brought me here? I demand to know who did this! I've got to get back. Tel Mithryn is probably falling apart."*
- The zones activating Varona's scripted death are placed in very close vicinity of Tel Mithryn, making it possible to actually see her die.
- Sometimes Varona's body will show up without clothes.
