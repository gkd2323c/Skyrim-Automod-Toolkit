[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-npc-Muiri.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Muiri.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Muiri.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Muiri.jpg) Muiri at [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) **Muiri** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary). She grew up in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) with her best friend [Nilsine Shatter-Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nilsine_Shatter-Shield). After Nilsine's sister Friga Shatter-Shield was killed by the [Butcher of Windhelm](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md), Muiri met and began a relationship with [Alain Dufont](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alain_Dufont). However, he was just using her to get close to the Shatter-Shield Clan and steal their valuables, including the family heirloom [Aegisbane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aegisbane). The Shatter-Shield Clan blamed Muiri for this and forced her out of Windhelm and onto the streets. Muiri eventually ended up in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), where she started working as an assistant to [Bothela](Skyrim_Bothela.md) at [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md). Bitter at the betrayal by Alain Dufont and being abandoned by Nilsine Shatter-Shield, she performs the [Black Sacrament](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother) and requests that the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) kill Alain.

Starting from midnight, Muiri will sleep at the Hag's Cure until 6am, she will then have breakfast for an hour before returning to bed for an hour. Work then begins for Muiri at the Hag's Cure from 8am through until 1pm, where she takes a one hour lunch break, then returns to work until 7pm. From 7pm till midnight she leaves the Hag's Cure to enjoy a 5 hour meal, after which she will return to the Hag's Cure to sleep.

Muiri wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6), including a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and has a copy of the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Hag%27s_Cure) to the Hag's Cure. She also carries [common loot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists) and a small amount of gold. As Muiri was trained by Bothela, she knows all of the same spells she does. From the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) school, she has access to [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md); from the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) school she has [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md); she can also use the [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) spell from the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) school.

## Dialogue
When spoken to while she works at the store with [Bothela](Skyrim_Bothela.md) still alive, Muiri will greet you by stating her job:

*"I'm Bothela's assistant. She's the one you should talk to if you need an herb or a potion."* Should Bothela be dead, then she will take over the store, although she makes it clear that she's not ready for it:

*"I've taken over the Hag's Cure, since Bothela is gone."* *"I'm not sure if half these potions work. But they should. I think."* *"I wish Bothela was still running the store. I'm not ready for this."* If spoken to after [Mourning Never Comes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mourning_Never_Comes):

*"Thank you again for resolving my... problem."* However, even if Bothela is around, Muiri will make it clear that she has difficulties with her tasks:

*"There's so much to learn about the shop. I'll never get it all straight."* When exiting conversation:

*"I should get back to memorizing recipes."* *"Now, is lavender an herb or a poison? Hmm...."* If Muiri is spoken to outside the store:

*"Why can't the men in this city leave me alone?"*

## Notes
- If you choose to [destroy the Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md), or if you decide not to murder Nilsine Shatter-Shield, then you can still marry her if Bothela is killed and you invest with Muiri after she takes over the shop. However, killing Bothela yourself will lower Muiri's [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md).
- Originally, Alain Dufont was to appear as a prisoner in Windhelm. The quest's main objective would remain the same, but rather than revenge on Nilsine, Muiri would want Alain disgraced in front of all of Windhelm. In order to complete this objective, you would've had a fight with him in the Pit, Windhelm's removed arena.
- Windhelm guards may mention her if they see you in possession of Aegisbane, saying *"That hammer... Why that's Aegisbane, heirloom of Clan Shatter-Shield! Last I heard, it was stolen by a couple of swindlers. Alain and... Muiri. Yeah."*
