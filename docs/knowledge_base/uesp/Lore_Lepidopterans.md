# Lepidopterans

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-hat-Moonshadow_Wings_Mask.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Moonshadow_Wings_Mask.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Moonshadow_Wings_Mask.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Moonshadow_Wings_Mask.jpg) Moonshadow Wings Mask The Lepidopterans were a cult devoted to the worship of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Dusk and Dawn, [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). Those followers of Azura were known for hiding their faces behind intricate [butterfly](Lore_Butterfly.md) -themed masks called Moonshadow Wings Masks, named after Azura's plane, [Moonshadow](Lore_Moonshadow.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWM-1)</sup>

The exact details about the cult are unclear, but the Moonshadow Wings Masks gained popularity in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) and the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). During this time, those masks grew into a widespread trend among the disillusioned youth of mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWM-1)</sup>
