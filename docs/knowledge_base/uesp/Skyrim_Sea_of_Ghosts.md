[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg) The Sea of Ghosts along the northern coast of Skyrim The **Sea of Ghosts** is a vast ocean to the north of Skyrim.

The Sea of Ghosts is part of the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md); it meets the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) off the coast of northwestern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) along the shores of northern [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Some exploration of the sea has yet to be completed, and icebergs are a common sight in its cold, misty waters. The island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) is located in the Sea of Ghosts, and the island's [Harstrad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Harstrad_River), [Iggnir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iggnir_River), and [Isild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isild_River) rivers flow into it. The [Atmorans](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atmoran) sailed from their homeland, the northern continent of [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) across this sea.
