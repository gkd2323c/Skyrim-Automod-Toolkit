## Faction Quests

### [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) Quests
- **[Ancient Technology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Technology)**: Retrieve Dwemer schematics to improve technology for the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Bolstering the Ranks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolstering_the_Ranks)**: Find [Florentius Baenius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius) and recruit him into the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).
- **[Cleansing Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cleansing_Light)**: Kill the boss of a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) lair. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md)**: Kill a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) masquerading as a citizen. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Hunting the Monster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_the_Monster)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) target. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[A Jarl's Justice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Jarl%27s_Justice)**: Kill a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) masquerading as a [Visiting Advisor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Visiting_Advisor). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Lost Relic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Relic)**: Retrieve a relic of the original [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Preemptive Strike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Preemptive_Strike)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) before it turns its allies. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Rescue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rescue)**: Rescue a victim from [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### [Vampire](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) Quests
- **[Amulets of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power)**: Retrieve the Amulets of Night Power. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Ancient Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Power)**: Retrieve [vampire parts](Skyrim_Ancient_Vampire_Arm.md) from varied locations around Skyrim. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Culling the Beast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Culling_the_Beast)**: Kill the thin-blooded [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Deceiving the Herd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deceiving_the_Herd)**: Frame the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) for murder. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Destroying the Dawnguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Destroying_the_Dawnguard)**: Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) leaders.
- **[The Gift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gift)**: Turn your [spouse](Skyrim_Marriage.md) into a [creature of the night](Skyrim_Vampire.md).
- **[The Hunt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hunt)**: Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) spy masquerading as a citizen. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[New Allegiances](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances)**: Turn the target into a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Protecting the Bloodline](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Protecting_the_Bloodline)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) before it turns its allies. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic)**: Retrieve the [Ring of the Erudite](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Erudite.md) and the [Ring of The Beast](Skyrim_Ring_of_The_Beast.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
