Estre of [Errinorne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:House_Errinorne) was the High Kinlady and Canonreeve of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) and the leader of the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md) as the Veiled Queen. She was the wife of Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md) and the sister-in-law of Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md). As the leader of the Veiled Heritance, Estre sought to supplant Ayrenn, destroy the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), and expel all non-Altmer from the Summerset Isles.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Estre-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WDt V-2)</sup> To achieve this goal, Estre and her faction employed a variety of terroristic methods, received assistance from [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bo TE-3)</sup> and formed a pact with Sealord [Malleroth](Lore_Malleroth.md) of the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) High Command.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTVQ-4)</sup> During Ayrenn's sojourn in [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Veiled Heritance attempted to assassinate her, but they were ultimately defeated, and Estre was killed.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Auridon-5)</sup>

## History
Estre was a member of [House Errinorne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:House_Errinorne), a noble family known to have been present in [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) since the late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Estre-1)</sup> Estre was a worshiper of Mehrunes Dagon.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TBo A-6)</sup>

Around [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580), Estre married Prince Naemon, who became the heir to the Throne of [Alinor](Lore_Kingdom_of_Alinor.md) following the disappearance of his older sister, Ayrenn, in [2E 573](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_573).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Estre-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ATUQ-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TIEG-8)</sup> However, in 2E 580, Ayrenn returned and was crowned Queen of Alinor over Naemon.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ATUQ-7)</sup> Estre was quite unhappy about Ayrenn's return, as she wanted to be queen.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ayrenn-9)</sup> After Ayrenn's return in 2E 580, Estre formed the Altmeri supremacist group, the Veiled Heritance, in opposition to Ayrenn and the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WDt V-2)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Lit ES-10)</sup> Although Estre was the leader of the Veiled Heritance, her identity as the Veiled Queen remained a closely guarded secret.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Alandare-11)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when Estre and the Veiled Heritance learned that Ayrenn was to sojourn in [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), they began preparing to assassinate her.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AI-12)</sup> Under Estre's orders, agents of the Heritance took control of the College of Aldmeri Propriety.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ZL-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-AD-14)</sup> She ordered the Heritance to take over the mine of Del's Claim to use as a base to attack Ayrenn's forces.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-DC-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DCR-16)</sup> Estre even formed a pact with Sealord [Malleroth](Lore_Malleroth.md) of the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) High Command. This agreement stipulated that the two factions would collaborate in the conquest of Auridon, and Estre would become the island's viceroy under Maormer suzerainty.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TTVQ-4)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ODt SE-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-MM-18)</sup> However, she had no intention of allowing this arrangement to come to pass, and she intended to use the Sea Vipers of the Pyandonean Armada simply as a distraction.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ODt SE-17)</sup> Estre plotted to assassinate the [Wilderking](Lore_Wilderking.md) in order to destabilize the Dominion, tasking [Aranias](Lore_Aranias.md), who possessed a natural talent for nature magic, along with Andur, with finding and killing him.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Awt W-19)</sup>

In 2E 582, Estre accompanied Ayrenn to [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md) and [Tanzelwil](Lore_Tanzelwil.md) during her sojourn in Auridon.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-MDAA-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-AHS-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-It Not Q-22)</sup> Estre was present for the Heritance's assassination attempts on Ayrenn at Vulkhel Guard and Tanzelwil, but both plots were thwarted.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-AHS-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-It Not Q-22)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-Rot Q-23)</sup> During her royal progress in Auridon, Ayrenn wished to smooth over relations with Estre by granting her a higher position within the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) and planned to hold a formal ceremony in Skywatch for this purpose.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ayrenn-9)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-Razum-24)</sup> While Ayrenn was in Skywatch, an [Eye of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md) infiltrated the Heritance's base on [Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md), where they discovered that Estre was the Veiled Queen.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Wt V-25)</sup><sup>[[26]](#cite_note-TVF-26)</sup> The Eyes of the Queen then interrupted the practice ceremony in Skywatch Manor, revealing to Ayrenn that Estre was the Veiled Queen. Estre promptly escaped by teleportation and began an open conflict with the Dominion.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-TVF-26)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/ON-quest-Blessings_of_the_Eight_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Blessings_of_the_Eight_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Blessings_of_the_Eight_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Blessings_of_the_Eight_02.jpg) Estre making her bargain with Mehrunes Dagon After her escape, Estre traveled to [Torinaan](Lore_Torinaan.md), a holy site of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Divines.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bo TE-3)</sup><sup>[[27]](#cite_note-Colaste-27)</sup> There, she made a bargain with Mehrunes Dagon, offering her soul in return for the backing of his [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) to replace the now mostly subdued Heritance.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bo TE-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Estre-1)</sup> As part of her agreement with Mehrunes Dagon, she corrupted Torinaan's shrines and unleashed a horde of Daedra upon the holy site.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-Colaste-27)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Bo TE-3)</sup> Estre then traveled to [Dawnbreak](Lore_Dawnbreak.md), where the town's canonreeve, Sinyon, continued to support her.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-Sinyon-28)</sup><sup>[[29]](#cite_note-UMft K-29)</sup> Once there, she unleashed a horde of Daedra upon Dawnbreak, leaving the town largely in ruins before continuing north.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-Sinyon-28)</sup><sup>[[30]](#cite_note-Tt A-30)</sup><sup>[[31]](#cite_note-TGo A-31)</sup>

After leaving Dawnbreak, Estre went to [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), and she was let into the city by its ruler, High Kinlord Rilis XIII, who was unaware that she was the Veiled Queen.<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-Curime-32)</sup> Once inside Firsthold, she opened four Oblivion Gates across the city to attempt to seize control with her Daedric army, took Rilis hostage, and sealed Firsthold Castle with magical wards. Eventually, Ayrenn's forces arrived, and they and the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) destroyed the wards and closed three of the Oblivion Gates. They then entered the Refuge of Dread in [the Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) through the Oblivion Gate in Firsthold Castle, where they fought and killed Estre.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-Bt B-33)</sup><sup>[[34]](#cite_note-SAT-34)</sup>

Prior to her death, [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) coerced Estre into submitting herself to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TBo A-6)</sup><sup>[[35]](#cite_note-TDo D-35)</sup><sup>[[36]](#cite_note-OBA-36)</sup><sup>[[37]](#cite_note-Lw SE-37)</sup> As a result of submitting herself to Molag Bal, Estre's soul was claimed by the Prince of Domination after her death and came to reside in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). There, Estre was confined to the Cliffs of Failure, forced into a cycle of never-ending battle against others who failed to uphold bargains made with Molag Bal.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Estre-1)</sup><sup>[[38]](#cite_note-Observer-38)</sup> Eventually, an [adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) arrived at the site and joined in the game in an attempt to rescue a group of Mages Guild members who found themselves caught up in it. The game's organizer ordered the adventurer to pick a prisoner to ally with, and Estre was included among the choices.<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-TEW-39)</sup> The adventurer won the game, and as a reward, they were allowed to choose between rescuing the mages or their chosen ally. What decision they made, and whether the adventurer even chose to align themselves with Estre, is unknown.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-ABWS-40)</sup><sup>[[41]](#cite_note-ATi YS-41)</sup><sup>[[42]](#cite_note-TWot W-42)</sup>
