[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/SR-npc-Bolund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bolund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bolund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bolund.jpg) Bolund working at the lumber mill **Bolund** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) that owns and operates [Falkreath's](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) lumber mill. He lives in the general store [Gray Pine Goods](Skyrim_Gray_Pine_Goods.md) with his brother [Solaf](Skyrim_Solaf.md). He tends to have a rather rude personality. He will sell [lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> to you if [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) is installed.

From 7am to 8pm, he wanders around the sawmill, cutting wood. At other times he will wander the town, sometimes being in the store and sometimes visiting the [inn](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md). He never sleeps.

He wears farmer attire, including [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006c1d9) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5) and carries his copy of the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Gray_Pine_Goods) to Gray Pine Goods, an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
If you are a Nord he will greet you with:

*"A hearty welcome to Falkreath! All of Skyrim's true sons are welcome here!"* If you are not a Nord he will simply say:

*"I can't believe we let provincials like you wander Skyrim."* If you play as an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) he will be downright hateful and say:

*"Stay out of my way, boot."* He may further demonstrate his dislike of outsiders and [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) sympathizers when he remarks:

*"You'll find my brother Solaf and I are the only true Nords in Falkreath."* He also dislikes local innkeeper [Valga Vinicia](Skyrim_Valga_Vinicia.md) and will say:

*"That Valga Vinicia is a gossip and a troublemaker."*
### Conversations
When Bolund meets his brother meet inside their store, they may engage in conversation:

**Bolund**: *"Another day in this graveyard."*
**Solaf**: *"Falkreath is a fine place to be, Bolund."*
**Bolund**: *"Falkreath is where people go to die. We could be fighting, taking back Nord land!"*
**Solaf**: *"Falkreath is our home, a place where we have good jobs under a good Jarl. Until the war comes to us we stay put."* At the inn, he can be observed being his usual blunt self while talking to serving girl [Narri](Skyrim_Narri.md):

**Bolund**: *"Hey gossip, bring me a drink."*
**Narri**: *"Bolund, you're going to die of thirst if you keep calling me that."* He was also originally intended to be rude towards [Lod](Skyrim_Lod.md), although the latter never sets foot inside the inn in the final game, making the scene go unused:

**Bolund**: *"Hey, Lod, how is the worst farmer in the hold doing?"*
**Lod**: *"The Gentle Repose is barren, it was barren the day my father claimed the land and it's barren now."*
**Bolund**: *"Sure it is, Lod. Sure it is."*
## Notes
- His brother Solaf is a true [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), although much more forgiving than Bolund. When asked about his brother he will say: *"His name's Bolund. He's the one who works the lumber mill down by the river. If you meet him, don't expect much in the way o' conversation. He ain't one for talking, especially with folk he don't know."* and * "Don't mind Bolund. He's young and gets riled easily."* Even when you enter Gray Pine Goods for the first time, he will make excuses for his brother: *"Well met. Unlike my brother, I've no dislike of strangers."*
