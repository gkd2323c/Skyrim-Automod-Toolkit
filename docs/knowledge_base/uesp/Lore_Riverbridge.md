[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Riverbridge is a settlement found in west-central [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> in the Central Plains region and on the western banks of the [Thir River](Lore_Thir_River.md). It was a territory occupied by [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-2)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Riverbridge was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Casseth and had a rivalry with [Heimlyn Keep](Lore_Heimlyn_Keep.md) and Stonefalls.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-3)</sup> Riverbridge was neighbored by several settlements, such as [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) to the northeast, [Oaktown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oaktown) to the south, and [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls_(settlement).md) to the north-northeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Riverbridge is not to be confused with a settlement in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) [of the same name](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Riverbridge_(Black_Marsh)).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR2-4)</sup>
