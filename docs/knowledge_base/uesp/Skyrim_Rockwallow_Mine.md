## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) is outside the mine entrance, which is east of [Sorli's house](Skyrim_Sorli%27s_House.md).

### Rockwallow Mine
The entrance is a short, ice-filled tunnel leading to a central icy cave containing two wooden ramps up to platforms each within reach of an iron ore vein: one directly in front of you (to the east) and the other on your right (in the southwest). The latter has a bedroll beside a low table holding a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Orsinium and the Orcs](Skyrim_Orsinium_and_the_Orcs.md)*. In the northeast and southeast, two short tunnels lead to dead ends, each with an iron ore vein at the end. Several [pickaxes](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) and shovels are found throughout the mine.

## Notes
- During the day, there are as many miners as ore veins, making it difficult to mine.
