[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-creature-Lis.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Lis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lis.jpg) Lis the Dark Brotherhood Spider **Lis** is a friendly [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Creatures.md#Frostbite_Spider) and a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md).

It lives in a pool near the alchemy lab in the [Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Sanctuary.md), where [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md) usually is. Attacking it counts as assaulting a guild member, and will cause the entire sanctuary to attack you.

Lis dies when the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary is torched by the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md) during [Death Incarnate](Skyrim_Death_Incarnate.md).

## Notes
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states she is [Gabriella](Skyrim_Gabriella.md)'s familiar, who is treated more like a pet. She is also stated to be the sanctuaries' unofficial mascot.
- You do not have to kill Lis to complete the quest [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md). - During the quest Lis is often out of its pool and may attack you with other members of the sanctuary.
- Lis is the only non-hostile frostbite spider ever found in the game.
- Lis reappears in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* as the card [Sanctuary Pet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Sanctuary Pet).
