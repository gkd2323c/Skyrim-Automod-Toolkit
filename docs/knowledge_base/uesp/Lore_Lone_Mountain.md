# Lone Mountain

Lone Mountain is an inselberg found in the dead center of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s [Tundra Plain](Lore_Tundra_Plain.md) that stands as the only mountain in the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> The mountain is considered part of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) and is found close to [Fort Greymoor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Greymoor) and a dragon mound. It is an oddity among the province's peaks, most of which are part of larger ranges. The closest mountain ranges are the [Skyborn Range](Lore_Skyborn_Range.md) to the north and [Brittleshin Hills](Lore_Brittleshin_Hills.md) to the south. The [River Hjaal](Lore_River_Hjaal.md) runs to the north side.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRMound-2)</sup>
