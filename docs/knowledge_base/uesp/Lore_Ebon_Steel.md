# Ebony-steel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/ON-crown_store-Ebon_Steel_Dwarven_Spider.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Ebon_Steel_Dwarven_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ebon_Steel_Dwarven_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ebon_Steel_Dwarven_Spider.jpg) A vamidium made of Ebon Steel [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/ON-crown_store-Ebonsteel_Knight_Arms_Pack.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Ebonsteel_Knight_Arms_Pack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ebonsteel_Knight_Arms_Pack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ebonsteel_Knight_Arms_Pack.jpg) Ebonsteel equipment Ebony-steel, also known as **Ebon Steel**, and **Ebonsteel** is an alloy. It is unknown if this alloy actually contained [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) and [Steel](Lore_Steel.md) or has been simply named as such, but it was a dark, durable substance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESDS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EKS-3)</sup>

It was used by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESDS-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EDS2-4)</sup> along with other materials, such as [adamant](Lore_Adamant.md), [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), [vitrine](Lore_Vitrine.md), and [dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md), for constructing certain [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VDH-5)</sup>

Ebony-steel was also used by the knights all across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EKS-3)</sup> and by the soldiers of the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md). Maces made of the ebony-steel had a tripartite head of three heavy [eagle's](Lore_Eagle.md) wings: one for each of the races of the alliance: [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md), [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CM-6)</sup>
