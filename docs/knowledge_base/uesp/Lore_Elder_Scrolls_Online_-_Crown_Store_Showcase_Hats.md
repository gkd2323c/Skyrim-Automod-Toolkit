# Elder Scrolls Online - Crown Store Showcase/Hats

This page lists **hats** from the [Crown Store Showcase](Lore_Elder_Scrolls_Online_-_Crown_Store_Showcase.md).

### Abah's Watch Turban
Not even a fez is as cool as a hat shaped like an [onion](Lore_Onion.md). Now you can feel dapper yet comfortable under the desert sun by donning the Abah's Watch Turban, the head-wrapping of the stylish [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) guards!

### Abah's Watch Turban with Mask
It's incognito time: at your next desert soiree, be stylish yet mysterious, intriguing and tantalizing, by combining the Abah's Watch Turban with a feature-concealing mask. No one will ever suspect you're actually crashing the party.

### Alliance Rider Hood
Show your allegiance! Wear the Alliance Rider Hood and proudly display your army's bold heraldic symbol, whether it's the Eagle, the Lion, or the Dragon! The appearance automatically conforms to your character's alliance in the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).

### Camlorn Top Hat Pack
The Top of Sundas Morning to you in your [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) Top Hat, with a buckle, a [shamrock](Lore_Shamrock.md), or a "lucky bouquet." This bundle includes all three!

### Daedric Death Mask
[Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) Worshiper, do you dare wear a mask that strikes fear into the [souls](Lore_Soul.md) of ordinary mortals? Then the Daedric Death Mask is for you! For as is graven upon its metal horn bands in the [dread script of Oblivion](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md): FEAR ABIDES.

### Deep Elf Executioner's Hood
Ancient engravings tell us that a [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) executioner, or "Chun Abakch," always concealed his or her features under a hood of black [spidersilk](Lore_Spidersilk.md), a material that was also used by the Dwemer for funeral shrouds. Feeling sinister? This could be your look!

### Dibella's Doll Mask Pack
When worshipers of [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md) wish to honor the Lady of Love by engaging in anonymous bestowal of affection, they don an exquisite porcelain Dibella's Doll Mask. This bundle includes the Dibella's Doll Mask for [Humans](Lore_Human.md) and [Elves](Lore_Mer.md) as well as two variations, for Khajiiti *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* and [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md).

### Hat Pack: Winter Broadbrims
Winter is here, and it's time to look dashing, through the snow! This festive bargain bundle includes the Winter Corsage, Winter Garland, and Winter Wreath Broadbrim hats.

### House of Reveries Mask Pack
Includes the Breath of Y'ffre, Tranquil Reverie, Enigmatic Mood, and Garden Serenade Masks. The Breath of Y'ffre gave creation its names and shapes, Tranquil Reverie connotes the serene mind, Enigmatic Mood could turn in any direction, while the Garden Serenade promises gentle repose and loving acceptance.

### Jester's Festival Hat Pack
Why wait for others to do it when you can make a fool of yourself? Three jester's hats, no waiting: the Bi-Color Coxcomb Cap, the Ass-Ears Cap, and the Fool's Cap and Masque. Jingly! (2017)

Jest for you—three jester's hats, no waiting: the Bi-Color Coxcomb Cap, the Ass-Ears Cap, and the Fool's Cap and Masque. Jingly! (2018)

### Jhunal's Owl Mask
[Wise-Wing](Lore_Wise-Wing.md) the [Owl](Lore_Owl.md) is associated in northern Tamriel with the god [Jhunal](Lore_Jhunal.md) or [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md). During the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md) she is said to warn the unwary away from graveyards and witches' covens where they may come to harm. Her owl mask is sure to inspire others to trust in your wisdom! It's a real hoot. (2016)

Wise-Wing the Owl is associated in northern Tamriel with the god Jhunal or Julianos. During the Witches Festival she is said to warn the unwary away from graveyards and witches' covens. Her owl mask is sure to inspire others to trust in your wisdom! It's a real hoot. (2017)

### Midyear Mayhem Crowns
This bundle includes all three [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) crowns celebrating the Midyear Mayhem event: the Circlet, the Coronet, and the Diadem.

### Moonshadow Wings Mask
The [Lepidopterans](Lore_Lepidopterans.md) are a sect of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) cultists who conceal their features with elaborate [butterfly](Lore_Butterfly.md) masks. These have recently become a fad among the jaded youth of mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) —and now you can wear one, too!

### New Life Festival Cap
Celebrate the turning of the year and the return of [Magnus](Lore_Magnus_(god).md) the Sun in this festive [New Life](Lore_New_Life.md) cap. With a bobbing ball of fur on the end of its tapering blue velvet wimple, everyone will know you're the life of the winter party! (2016)

Celebrate the turning of the year and the return of Magnus the Sun in this festive New Life cap. With a bobbing ball of fur on the end of its tapering blue velvet wimple, everyone will know you're the life of the festival! (2017)

### New Life Monk's Cap
For those who wish to celebrate New Life without tawdry and garish display, we offer this more modest festival hat, well suited to austere monks, simple merchants, Resolutes of Stendarr, and holy knights vowed to poverty. Because New Life is for everyone!(2016)

### Nightmare Demon Mask Pack
Incarnate the anger of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) itself with this visage of nightmare terror evoking the dreaded [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) at their most frightening. This bundle includes the Nightmare Daemon Mask for Humans and Elves along with the variations for Khajiiti *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* and Argonians. Daemonic!

### Rajhin's Cat Mask
Feeling feline? Get cat-faced! During the Witches Festival, no one perpetrates more cunning and devious tricks than [Rajhin](Lore_Rajhin.md) the Footpad, the Purring Liar. This mask enables you to adopt one of the many faces of that consummate trickster.

### Reveries Operatic Mask Pack
Go theatrical! Four more melodramatic House of Reveries Masks, including the Flowervine, Red Visage, Quarto, and Veiled Masks. Flowervine is chosen when an actor must represent growth, nature, or an aspect of Y'ffre; when the Red Visage appears onstage, expect tragedy; the Quarto is the face of the Unreliable Narrator; and the Veiled Mask represents a character whose motives are obscure.

### Silvenari Felt Bycoket
This bycoket is the dashing felt cap worn by [the Silvenar](Lore_The_Silvenar.md)'s Own [Jaqspurs](Lore_Jaqspurs.md), the famous archers who form the personal guard of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) "Voice of the People." This elite unit has been revived for the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) by the latest Silvenar. Their hat looks as good on men as it does on women.

### Sun's Dusk Winter Hood
The New Life Festival is upon us, and we have an array of hats to celebrate! You'll be toasty warm even in [Sun's Dusk](Lore_Sun%27s_Dusk.md) with this festive furry hood, crowned with a spray of lucky mistletoe that will make you welcome everywhere. (2016)

The New Life Festival is upon us, and we have an array of hats to celebrate! You'll be toasty warm even in Sun's Dusk with this festive furry hood, crowned with a spray of lucky mistletoe that will make you welcome everywhere. Pucker up! (2017)

### Z'en Vengeance Kiss-Me-Not Cap
When a Wood Elf vows a Vengeance Oath to the god [Z'en](Lore_Z%27en.md), they may wear this tusk-encrusted cap as a symbol of having sworn off physical affection until the vendetta is complete. Admittedly, the Kiss-Me-Not is also sometimes worn just as a joke. Because the Bosmer are like that.
