# Ordained Receptacle

The Ordained Receptacle was a mysterious object (or a person) sought by [Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal](Lore_Ada%27Soom_Dir-Kamal.md), the King of [Kamal](Lore_Kamal_(place).md), during his [campaign](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md) in [Eastern Skyrim](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSAI-1)</sup>

## History
In [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GTTEP-2)</sup> the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md) began. A fleet originating from the [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) nation of Kamal, under the command of King Ada'Soom Dir-Kamal, invaded northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Strangely, the [Kamali](Lore_Kamal_(race).md) fleet bypassed the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md), [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), sailing around the northeastern coast of Tamriel into the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). They made landfall at the mouth of the [White River](Lore_White_River.md), just northeast of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSAI-1)</sup>

[Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) intelligence from the Kingdom of Eastern Skyrim reported that few captured Akaviri spoke Tamrielic, and most died under interrogation. One account mentioned that Dir-Kamal was searching for an item or a person or called the Ordained Receptacle. According to [Yngmaer Raven-Quill](Lore_Yngmaer_Raven-Quill.md), Royal Historian of the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md), this may have been a misinterpretation or a flawed translation. Its name suggested its importance to the Kamal. Whether they ever found it was unknown, but the invasion was ultimately repelled.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSAI-1)</sup>

## Notes
