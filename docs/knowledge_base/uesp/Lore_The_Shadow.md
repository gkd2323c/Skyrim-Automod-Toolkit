# The Shadow

*This one sees … a shadow. The flash of steel and fell whispers ….*
*This one sees … nothing. A chill void.*
*It must be the Shadow that guides you. Cold, cautious. Patient. Shadow is among the deepest signs, for it represents what is not known. Tread not too far down that path, walker, if you want to return.*
—Baandari fortune-telling for those born under the Shadow<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Shadow is a constellation of five stars which is in the night sky during [Second Seed](Lore_Second_Seed.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It is one of the [Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md)'s charges.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Those born under the sign of the Shadow are thought to have the ability to hide in shadows.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

[Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) who followed the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) modeled their [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and armor after the constellations. Each part of their armaments were crafted in honor of different constellation. Pauldrons, epaulets and arm cops were linked to the Shadow, "who hideth beneath the Second Seed, uncovering only to strike from below". They believed that Shadow was unseen although seen, shadowing the sky's every move, and their shields were meant to emulate the Shadow's nature - "to follow thy form, faithful and silent, and protect betimes ye striketh".<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CM41-5)</sup>

The Shadow is traditionally associated with the [Void](Lore_Void.md). While some myths for the Shadow portray the themes of unbeing typically associated with the Void, others portray the Shadow as the dual to the light of [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md), representing the void through which the stars shine.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The constellations are present in ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) poetry.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWC-7)</sup> In one of their songs The Shadow is "lying through the night."<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Coyle-8)</sup>

The Dwemer associate the Shadow with the letter g.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

[Argonians](Lore_Argonians.md) born under the Shadow may be offered to the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) to become [Shadowscales](Lore_Shadowscales.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup> Argonian accounts warn those born under the Shadow from seeking to use the power of the artifact of [Fangs of Sithis](Lore_Fangs_of_Sithis.md) and link the downfall of their ancestors to this artifact.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

According to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) the charges, or the Lesser Stars (the Shadow among them) are protected by the Guardians in the Sky who stand together against [the Snake in the Stars](Lore_The_Snake_in_the_Stars.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ARANA-12)</sup>

According to the [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) fortune tellers, the Shadow is also linked to the void and associated with coldness, patience, and cautiousness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HK-1)</sup> The Shadow was also believed to "hide ones from destiny's cunning hounds".<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Uriel-13)</sup>
