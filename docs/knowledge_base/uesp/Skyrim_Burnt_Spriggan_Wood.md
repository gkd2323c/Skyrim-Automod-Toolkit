## Ingredients
2 guaranteed samples can be found in the following 2 locations:

- 1 in [Bristleback Cave](Skyrim_Bristleback_Cave.md), in a small crate with quills, near the second Riekling "barrel ambush"
- 1 in [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)), in a shelf next to the alchemy table downstairs

## Notes
- Although Burnt Spriggan Wood has the effect of Weakness to Fire, the Burnt Spriggans themselves are completely immune to the element.
- Burnt Spriggan Wood can sometimes be found for sale by alchemists on Solstheim (such as [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md)) and the one general goods merchant ([Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md)).
