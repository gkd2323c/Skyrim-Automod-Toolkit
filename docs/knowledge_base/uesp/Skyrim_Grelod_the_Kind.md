[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind.jpg) Grelod the Kind **Grelod the Kind** is the [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) sadistic headmistress of the [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She beats the children and refuses to allow their adoption. During the quest [Innocence Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost), [Aventus Aretino](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino), an escapee from the orphanage, is attempting to contact the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) to arrange for her assassination.

She is quite poor, as evidenced by her apparel: a simple set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). She carries exactly 15 gold and a copy of the rare book *[The Pig Children](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pig_Children)*.

With nothing better to do in life, Grelod spends her entire day surrounded by the children she hates, from the time she wakes up at 5am until she goes to bed at 1am. When not yelling at her assistant [Constance Michel](Skyrim_Constance_Michel.md), she isolates herself in her room or wanders restlessly around the orphanage, occasionally cooking a meal at the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in the sleeping area. At 10am she escorts the four children outside to the small yard behind the house and watches them play for a single hour. At 3pm, the orphans are allowed a one-hour meal and Grelod will join them. The only exception to this routine is when you enter the orphanage for the very first time, when she will give a cold-hearted speech to the children, basically telling them how worthless they are.

## Dialogue
When you enter the orphanage for the very first time, she will give a cold-hearted speech to the children:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod_the_Kind_02.jpg) *"Nobody needs you, nobody wants you."* **Grelod**: *"Those who shirk their duties will get an extra beating. Do I make myself clear?"*
**Orphans**: *"Yes, Grelod."*
**Grelod**: *"And one more thing! I will hear no more talk of adoptions! None of you riff-raff is getting adopted. Ever! Nobody needs you, nobody wants you. That, my darlings, is why you're here. Why you will always be here, until the day you come of age and get thrown into that wide, horrible world. Now, what do you all say?"*
**Orphans**: *"We love you, Grelod. Thank you for your kindness."*
**Grelod**: *"That's better. Now scurry off, my little guttersnipes."*

After this speech, Grelod will return to avoiding the children. While Constance sincerely cares about the welfare of the residents, talking to Grelod herself reveals how hostile she is to everybody:

*"What do you want? You have no business being in here!"* *"Riff-raff! That's all you Riften people are!"* *"This is an orphanage, not an inn. Begone from here."*
## Conversations
The children in the orphanage are not the only ones suffering from Grelod's tyranny and bad moods. Every once in a while, Grelod will yell:

**Grelod:** *"Constance!"* **Constance:** *"Yes, Grelod?"* This will start a number of mean-spirited complaints about the children's behavior, followed by a few long-suffering responses from Constance:

**Grelod**:
*"Samuel is getting to be a problem. Fancies himself something of a 'leader' to the other children. Put an end to the nonsense, or I will."*
*"Hroar's crying is keeping me up at night. I'll give you one chance to talk the tears out of him, or he's getting the belt."*
*"I can't abide Samuel's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * constant talk of his parents. 'They're coming for me.' 'This is only temporary.' The boy must accept his fate."*
*"Seven septims are missing from my purse. I know it was Runa. You get those coins back, or by Stendarr, I'll see her rot in the dungeon."*
*"This place is filthy. I've a mind to cancel all town privileges unless those brats start pulling their weight."*
*"The stores are running low. We'll need to water down the milk again. Besides, don't want the little darlings getting fat."*
*"We haven't had an adoption in months. At this rate, we'll be stuck with these brats forever. We need to get the word out. A sale, if need be."*
**Constance**:
*"All right. I'll take care of it."*
*"I'll handle it. No need to blow things out of proportion."*
*"Please, just let me handle it. It's not a problem."*
*"Okay, okay. I'll deal with it."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Spread the Love
If you aim to hand the cold-hearted woman one of [Dinya Balu](Skyrim_Dinya_Balu.md)'s heart-warming pamphlets during [Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love), you will be rejected right away:

**Here, sister. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"Unless Mara wishes to come down and help me with these urchins, I don't really give a damn about her blessings."*
### Innocence Lost
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-npc-Grelod-the_Kind_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grelod-the_Kind_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod-the_Kind_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grelod-the_Kind_03.jpg) *"Die, Grelod! Die!"* In [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), a former resident of Honorhall Orphanage, [Aventus Aretino](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aventus_Aretino), lives alone in his former [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aretino_Residence). He is known all over Skyrim as the kid who tries to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) for a kill. When you break into his house, you will find the orphan kneeling next to a skeleton, body parts, and lit candles. After a brief introduction, Aventus will explain he wants Grelod dead.

Back in the Orphanage, Grelod will now be a bit more talkative, although stubborn and impossible to scare. Three options will be available to you, but will have absolutely no impact on Grelod, who will just send you away:

**The Dark Brotherhood has come for you.** *"The what? Why how dare you! I will not be intimidated in my own orphanage! Get out! Get out this instant!"* **Aventus Aretino says hello.** *"Aretino? Why that little bastard! You tell him I'm coming for him! And when I find him, it will be the beating of his miserable life!"* **(Remain silent)** *"What are you staring at, you worthless piece of gutter trash? I simply must start locking the doors again..."* After any one of her rejections, she will not speak to you again and will only say:

*"You're trying to frighten me, hmm? Do your worst!"* *"You don't scare me."* Grelod's final word in life will be a long:

*"Aiiiiiiieeeeeeee!!"* With Grelod dead, all the orphans will cheer as a brighter future lies ahead, although Constance Michel will be understandably shocked at the incident and will run around screaming. She will quickly get over it and Aventus Aretino will be grateful back in Windhelm.

## Notes
- With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, you will be able to [adopt children](Skyrim_Adoption.md) from the orphanage if Grelod is dead by speaking with [Constance Michel](Skyrim_Constance_Michel.md).
- Grelod ties with [Kyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kyr) as the two NPCs with the lowest health in the game, which is one point.
