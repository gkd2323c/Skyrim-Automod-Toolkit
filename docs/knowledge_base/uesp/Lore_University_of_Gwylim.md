# University of Gwylim


The University of Gwylim itself is found in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVerita-1)</sup> however, the term Gwylim is a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) word.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Teddy PP-3)</sup> [Phrastus of Elinhir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Phrastus_of_Elinhir) originally taught at the university before being forced to flee to [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md) due to the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LEo ERZI-4)</sup> There is a forbidden section of Gwylim University containing books,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Azandar Dialogue-5)</sup> and it is unknown what else it carries. They have a satellite campus called the Gwylim Annex in Solitude.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVerita-1)</sup>

The Antiquarian Circle is the University's most prestigious historical society, which scours the continent for lost antiquities to collect and catalogue.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONVerita-1)</sup>

## History and Overview
The university's faculty are known to have an interest in practical pedagogy,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PSo M-6)</sup> and is known to offer training in the magical school of [Illusion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Illusion).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Silence-7)</sup> Tjurhane Fyrre was also one of the finest sages of the university. His book on Ayleidic culture, *Nature of Ayleidic Poesy*, was published by the university in [2E 12](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_12).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Fyrre was also responsible for writing a number of unpublished biographies on various Ayleid kings, including King [Narilmor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Narilmor) of [Garlas Malatar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Garlas_Malatar).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Other Ayleidic texts were also published by the university, including *[Ayleid Cities of Valenwood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleid_Cities_of_Valenwood)*, written by the [Esteemed Historian Homfrey](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Homfrey) in [2E 455](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_455).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup> "The Gwylim Praxis" by the Aureate Serpent is a book that describes methods of draining magicka from intelligent creatures at the moment of death, but it is unknown what its connection to the university is.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TLo DRB-11)</sup>

The wizard and prominent author [Gabrielle Benele](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gabrielle_Benele) was once a member of the university before she joined the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) around the time of the Three Banners War.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PSo M-6)</sup> She would later rejoin the university through the Antiquarian Circle and serve as their chief arcanist and leader.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONAITCC-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONYvara-13)</sup> She also invited a number of important figures to the University of Gwylim for interviews and discussion seminars, such as Madam Whim of [Fargrave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fargrave), [Azandar al-Cybiades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azandar_al-Cybiades), and Master [Malkhest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Malkhest).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-LAMM-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-LATA-15)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-LAIA-16)</sup> The famed sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Divayth_Fyr) also attended one of these discussion seminars, and revealed himself to be a generous patron to the university.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-LAHT-17)</sup>

At some point, the university funded a scholarly expedition to the ruins of [Skyreach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Skyreach) in [Craglorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Craglorn), led by Historian Reginus Buca and Ancientist Verita Numida.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-18)</sup> The scholar Mederic Vyger was a lecturer at the university and an expert on [Nedes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede) who helped the [Scaled Court](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Scaled_Court) in their study of the Skyreach ruins in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), although he left the cult after awakening the ancient [Celestial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Celestial) creatures in Skyreach Pinnacle and subsequently aided the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in restoring their wards.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

*[Ancient Tales of the Dwemer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ancient_Tales_of_the_Dwemer)*, [Marobar Sul](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Marobar_Sul)'s infamous eleven volumes of fictional stories about the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) published around [2E 670](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_670), was originally presented as a serious, scholarly work based on research in the archives of the University of Gwylim. The stories proved popular, and were accepted as fact. However, scholars later surmised that Marobar Sul was simply a pseudonym for [Gor Felim](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Gor_Felim), a prolific writer of the era, and the writings were debunked.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-20)</sup> The university continued to publish the surviving fragments of Sul's writings, although they were noted as being works of fiction.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-21)</sup>

*[Opusculus Lamae Bal ta Mezzamortie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Opusculus_Lamae_Bal_ta_Mezzamortie)*, written by the scribe [Mabei Aywenil](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mabei_Aywenil), was first translated and published by the university in [3E 105](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_105), or [2E 105](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_105).<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-22)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> The university first published *[Frontier, Conquest, and Accommodation: A Social History of Cyrodiil](Lore_Frontier,_Conquest.md)* in [3E 344](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_344).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-23)</sup> Doctor Intricatus of Gwylim University was noted for his ethnographic research into the "Primal" style of weapons and armor, worn by [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) and other primitive beastfolk.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-24)</sup> *The Gwylim Praxis* is a rare book written by the [Aureate Serpent](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#The_Aureate_Serpent), which details methods of draining magicka from intelligent creatures at the moment of death.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TLo DRB-11)</sup>
