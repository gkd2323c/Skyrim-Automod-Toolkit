# Dakoi

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-npc-Dakoi.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Dakoi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Dakoi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Dakoi.jpg) An Argonian man donning "Dakoi's fins"*In our village, we assume the role of Dakoi to help challenge ourselves with change, rather than resist and fight against the current.*
—Dakoi<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dakoi-1)</sup> Dakoi is a legendary [fish](Lore_Fish.md) said to surface only during the [New Life](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md) season. It was sought in the waters of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md), within the [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) region of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), as many believed that catching one could ensure a year of good fortune.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AN-2)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/ON-place-Stormhold_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Stormhold_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stormhold_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Stormhold_02.jpg) Stormhold According to [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) beliefs, the Dakoi was regarded as a rare and cunning fish. Each New Life season, it was said to return to their waters. Those who succeeded in catching and eating the Dakoi were believed to be granted good fortune and growth in the year to follow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Yesei-3)</sup> Some also believed that Bloodwine was an especially effective bait for attracting it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Yesei-3)</sup>

Among some Argonians, there is a custom of donning attire symbolizing the Dakoi during New Life, meant to challenge oneself to embrace change rather than resist or fight against the current. Despite this custom, its participants still seemed to express the belief in the existence of the real fish.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Dakoi-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-THFD-4)</sup>

Little is known about non-Argonian beliefs regarding the Dakoi, aside from the fact that some [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) anglers and [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) have corroborated some of these beliefs regarding the existence of the fish and its appearance during the New Life season.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AN-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Nenilon-5)</sup>
