[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-npc-Ulfr_the_Blind.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulfr_the_Blind.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulfr_the_Blind.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulfr_the_Blind.jpg) Ulfr the Blind **Ulfr the Blind** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) sitting at a table in [White River Watch](Skyrim_White_River_Watch.md). He guards the lower exit of the cave, quite a feat considering his blindness. He is the uncle of [Hajvarr Iron-Hand](Skyrim_Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.md), the leader of the group.

He can be found reading his blank book, *[Ulfr's Book](Skyrim_Ulfr%27s_Book.md)*, at the table.

Ulfr wears a leveled set of [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md), including a cuirass, a pair of boots, a shield, and a 50% chance of a pair of gauntlets. All of this armor can be up to [steel plate](Skyrim_Steel_Plate.md) in quality except the shield which can only be up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) in quality. He carries a small amount of gold and may also have a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) in his possession. In combat, he relies on a leveled [one-handed](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) weapon which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality.

## Dialogue
When he hears you approach him, you will be forced into dialogue with him and he will ask:

- *"Eh? Who's there? Rodulf, that you?"*

- **Yeah.**
- *"Boss was looking for you - said he'd be up at the summit. Better not keep him waiting."*

- **He's dead. You're next.**
- *"Hey! Somebody get down here! Help!"* **(This will cause two additional bandits to spawn and rush to Ulfr's aid and all three of them will then begin to attack you.)**

If you talk to him after choosing the first option, he will simply say:

- *"Good luck out there."*

## Notes
- According to a [note](Skyrim_Note_to_Rodulf.md) from Hajvarr, it appears that some of the other gang members are making fun of Ulfr, presumably because he is blind. As you venture further through the cave, you will hear two bandits conspiring against Hajvarr. One of these bandits seems to be quite fed up with having Ulfr as a watchman and will agree to the rebellion, saying, *"...Yeah. We can't have that rheumy-eyed fool as our watchman, even if he is Hajvarr's uncle. Time we dealt with both of them."*
- If you kill the two bandits that come to Ulfr's aid and then sheathe your weapon when fighting him, he'll stop fighting and return to the table where he was sitting.
- Despite being blind, line of sight still seems to affect whether or not he can detect you.
