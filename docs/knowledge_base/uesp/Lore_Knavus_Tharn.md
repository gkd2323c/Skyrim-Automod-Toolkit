—Knavus Tharn<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AT-1)</sup> Knavus Tharn was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the royal [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), ancestor and descendant of numerous famous figures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HTON-2)</sup>

Little is known of Knavus' past, except that he originated from the prestigious Tharn family. He was born in the early years of [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), long before the birth of his great-grandson in [2E 418](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_418).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-C-3)</sup>

He was a descendant and an ancestor of several other famous figures. [Tharanus Ye Redde-Hand](Lore_Tharanus_Ye_Redde-Hand.md) of [Fanacas](Lore_Fanacas.md), [Vilius Tharn](Lore_Vilius_Tharn.md), the Blade-Serrator and Master of the Abbatoir and Arch-Prelate [Fervidius Tharn](Lore_Fervidius_Tharn.md) of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md), General [Turpis "Volte-Face" Tharn](Lore_Turpis_Tharn.md), [Regulus Tharn](Lore_Regulus_Tharn.md) and Minister [Excoraeus Tharn](Lore_Excoraeus_Tharn.md) were his distant ancestors. He was also a great-grandfather of the famed Chancellor [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) and Queen of [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) [Euraxia Tharn](Lore_Euraxia_Tharn.md), a great-great-grandfather of [Empress Regent](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), and ancestor of many other famous individuals of the House Tharn such as [Septima Tharn](Lore_Septima_Tharn.md) and [Javad Tharn](Lore_Javad_Tharn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HTON-2)</sup>
