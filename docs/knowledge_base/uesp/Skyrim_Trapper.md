[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-npc-Trapper_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Trapper_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Trapper_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Trapper_02.jpg) A Trapper [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-npc-Trapper.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Trapper.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Trapper.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Trapper.jpg) A Trapper Two **Trappers** are found outside of [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md) near a caged [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md). Freeing the wolf will cause them to attack you, though the wolf will join you against them.

The first (base ID: `000D1686`) wears leveled [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor_(item).md) with leveled [light boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Boots), plus a 50% chance of a leveled [light helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Light_Helmets) (all up to [scaled](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled) quality), and carries a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and at least 26 gold.

The other (base ID: `000D1685`) wears [hide armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Armor), [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1), a pair of farm [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) (four variants), plus a 50% chance of a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat) (three variants). This trapper carries a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality), either a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) or [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow), at least seven [iron arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Arrow), five pieces of [raw meat](Skyrim_Food.md#Raw_Meat), a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md#Poor), and 3-27 additional gold. There is also a chance of up to five [animal parts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Animal_Hides#Unprocessed).

## Bugs
- Occasionally the wolf stands outside of but next to the cage and is always friendly. The attack of the trappers is still triggered by opening the cage and the wolf will still fight alongside you.
