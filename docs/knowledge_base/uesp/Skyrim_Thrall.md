## Effects
- [Conjure <type> Thrall](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 86313600 secs (999 days)

or - [Conjure Potent <type> Thrall](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 86313600 secs (with [Elemental Potency](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Potency) perk)

## Perks
- [Elemental Potency](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Potency), summons the more powerful Potent <type> Thrall.

## Notes
- Casting cloak spells, or transforming into a Vampire Lord<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, will dispel any summoned thralls.
- The Thrall spells are not actually permanent, though they may as well be. They last for 999 real-world days (about 2 years, 9 months), though it will be less if you sleep/wait/fast travel a lot. However, the odds of any Thrall actually surviving long enough to expire naturally are almost nil unless you're specifically trying to do that for some reason.
- The stats of the Atronachs summoned are:
