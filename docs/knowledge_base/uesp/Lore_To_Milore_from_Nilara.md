To Milore from Nilara **by Nilara** *A letter describing the changes to Mournhold following the Red Year*
---
Milore,

I hope the day finds you well. Well, Athras and I have finally mustered the courage to return to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) and pick up the pieces. We haven't been back to the manor in almost fifty years, but things haven't changed much. The city feels a bit empty... [Barenziah](Lore_Barenziah.md)'s palace is still completely unoccupied and most of the [Redoran Guard](Lore_Redoran_Guard.md) have been reassigned to other parts of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They've continued to make improvements to [Mournhold Temple](Lore_Mournhold_Temple.md), removing the last vestiges of [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md)'s mark and adding a new sanctuary. [Plaza Brindisi Dorom](Lore_Plaza_Brindisi_Dorom.md) has changed, as well. [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) has erected a large building there to house all of the new pilgrims and priests that seem to have flocked here to pay their respects at the temple.

Athras has returned to work as a jewelcrafter and he's gaining quite a following. He's bringing in a good bit of coin, a little of which I've sent to your as usual. Things are going well, and at this rate, we'll have the manor restored to its former glory by winter. I know you're tired of hearing about it, but I implore you to reconsider my offer. We have plenty of room here, and both of you are always welcome to join us. I know it's tough out there, but suffering in that horrible place just to prove a point isn't necessary anymore. Just a little sisterly advice.
