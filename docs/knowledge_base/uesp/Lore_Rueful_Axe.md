# Rueful Axe

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg) The Rueful Axe The Rueful Axe is a Daedric [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md), [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), as part of a terrible bargain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Rueful Axe-1)</sup> It takes the form of a large [iron](Lore_Iron.md) battleaxe engraved with images of [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md), although it is actually made of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRAxe-2)</sup> It is enchanted to steal the strength of anything it strikes, as well as a bane for beasts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades Rueful Axe-1)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a number of visual replicas designed in the Rueful Axe's image were seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONRAS-3)</sup>

Sometime after the end of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) came across the Rueful Axe in their journey. The [weapon](Lore_Weapons.md) is thought to have been looted from an extravagant chest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Clavicus Vile gave the axe to Sebastian Lort, a powerful [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) conjurer whose daughter was a worshipper of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) and cursed with [lycanthropy](Lore_Lycanthropy.md). To cure his daughter, Sebastian prayed to Vile for his aid. In response, the Prince gave him the axe, allowing him a means of "curing" his daughter-- by slaying her.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRBarbas-5)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Clavicus Vile tasked the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) with retrieving the axe from Sebastian. He was residing in a cave named Rimerock Burrow in [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md), [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). In return, the Dragonborn requested that Vile would forgive [Barbas](Lore_Barbas.md), whom he had banished to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Vile gave the Dragonborn the option to, either keep the Rueful Axe and use it to slay Barbas, temporarily vanquishing him to the void of Oblivion, or going through with the original deal in which Vile would take both Barbas and the Axe in return for the [Masque of Clavicus Vile](Lore_Masque_of_Clavicus_Vile.md). It is unknown which option the Last Dragonborn chose.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SRDBF-6)</sup>
