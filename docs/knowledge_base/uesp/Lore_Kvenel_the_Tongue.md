Kvenel the Tongue was an ancient [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) hero, and master of the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md). It is said his favored [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) to carry into battle were a hand-axe and sword, named [Okin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Okin) and [Eduj](Skyrim_Eduj.md) respectively.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HVN-1)</sup> According to legend, Kvenel commissioned master [blacksmith](Lore_Smithing.md) Haman Forgefire to forge Okin for him, to match his beloved sword, Eduj.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Lo HF-2)</sup>

Upon his death, Kvenel was entombed in [Volunruud](Lore_Volunruud.md), [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). His burial chamber was sealed with a special lock that could only be opened with two ceremonial replicas of Okin and Eduj, which were then put on display and guarded in other parts of the tomb.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup> Memory of his life faded as the eras passed and he was largely forgotten, save for old hymns and mythic accounts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HVN-1)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) explored Volunruud and encountered the ghost of Kvenel. After defeating the spirit, Kvenel's legendary weapons were retrieved from his remains.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim-3)</sup>
