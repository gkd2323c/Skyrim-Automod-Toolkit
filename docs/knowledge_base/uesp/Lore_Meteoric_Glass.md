# Meteoric Glass

Meteoric Glass (also called **Aetherial Crystals**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Carac Dena-1)</sup> is a variety of [Aetherial Fragment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherial_Fragment). Compared to the more common [meteoric iron](Lore_Meteoric_Iron.md), it rarely falls to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mft S-2)</sup> The glass is crystalline and comes in various colors. It is prized for being magical in nature and highly enchantable.

The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md), known for harnessing Aetherial Fragments, used the glass to create artifacts like [Welkynd Stones](Lore_Welkynd_Stone.md) and [Varla Stones](Lore_Varla_Stone.md). The crystals were also used as a source of light in Ayleid settlements, likely due to their abhorrence of fire, which they saw as an impure and primal form of light. [Skyshards](Lore_Skyshard.md) were believed to be shards of Aetherial magicka that carry the essence of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). They are sometimes linked to [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) or [Anu](Lore_Anu.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSIC-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> [Vakka Stones](Lore_Vakka_Stone.md) are a type of meteoric glass found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MQI-5)</sup>, while [Culanda](Lore_Culanda_Stone.md) and [Malondo Stones](Lore_Malondo_Stone.md) are a variety of meteoric glass used by the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) use beads of meteoric glass in their jewelry crafting, harnessing the arcane potential of the glass using an unknown crafting technique.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

When properly cultivated, meteoric glass crystals are able to grow and multiply. The Ayleids were only beginning to cultivate the material when they were wiped out by the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). Naturally occurring meteoric glass is a common sight in Ayleid ruins, and can often be seen growing on the crumbling limestone walls of these underground structures.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Enchanted pieces of meteoric glass, such as Welkynd Stones, can grow in the same way.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

The seven [Star Teeth](Lore_Star_Teeth.md) are similar in nature to meteoric glass, but it is unknown if they are made of the material. The origins of the [Jewel of Fire](Lore_Jewel_of_Fire.md) also suggest a link to the glass.
