Necromancer's Journal *A necromancer binds an undead wolf to his service* *
---
This is truly remarkable. Through my experiments with these [undead wolves](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf_(creature).md), I have discovered a method to increase my power.

By consuming their [totems](Skyrim_Totem_Bone.md); objects that bind them between life and death itself,
I can bind their spirits to my own life force.

I have already consumed two totems and their hosts are now completely under my control. But this is not enough, I need a third totem to be sure.
