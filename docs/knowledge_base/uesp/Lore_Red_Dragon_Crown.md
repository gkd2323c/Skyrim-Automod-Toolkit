# Red Dragon Crown

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/AR-npc-Uriel_Septim-Deluxe.png/200px-AR-npc-Uriel_Septim-Deluxe.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-npc-Uriel_Septim-Deluxe.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-npc-Uriel_Septim-Deluxe.png) Emperor [Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md) wearing a crown (Arena) The Red Dragon Crown (also called the **Imperial Diadem**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-1)</sup> **Imperial Crown**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sat D-2)</sup> and **Cyrodilic Crown**)<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGArchive-3)</sup> is the main symbol of office worn by the [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). By the time of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), it was thought to be the main symbol of power in the Empire. However, it is simply a mundane piece of jewelry; in contrast, the [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md) was also a sacred symbol of the Empire which possessed real power.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
