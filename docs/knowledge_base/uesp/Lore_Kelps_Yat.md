[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/AR-place-Dragon_Grove.jpg/200px-AR-place-Dragon_Grove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Dragon_Grove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Dragon_Grove.jpg) Dragon Grove during the Imperial Simulacrum Kelps Yat (also called **Dragon Grove**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> is a village found in southwestern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), specifically in its tropical rainforest-filled peninsula on the shores of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-2)</sup> and near the city-state of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap-3)</sup>

The town of Kelps Yat has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGMap-3)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Dragon Grove was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lady Klaltaki and had a rivalry with [Chasetown](Lore_Chasetown.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AR-1)</sup> Dragon Grove is neighbored by several settlements, including [Sunkeep](Lore_Sunkeep.md) to the northeast, and Hegathe to the southeast.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup>
