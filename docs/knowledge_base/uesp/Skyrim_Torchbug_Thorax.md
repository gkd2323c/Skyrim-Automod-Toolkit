## Ingredients
33 guaranteed samples can be found in 13 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 9 in [the Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 6 in [Forsaken Crypt](Skyrim_Forsaken_Crypt.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 3 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in [Cragwallow Slope](Skyrim_Cragwallow_Slope.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is an "uncommon" ingredient.

## Creatures
Torchbugs appear at insect spawning sites, but only between 8pm and 11pm. Some of the sites (50 in all) generate only torchbugs, each one spawning at most 10 insects. The majority of the sites generate up to three nocturnal insects, with a random mix of both torchbugs and [luna moths](Skyrim_Luna_Moth.md). These sites also typically generate [blue](Skyrim_Blue_Butterfly.md) and [monarch](Skyrim_Monarch_Butterfly.md) butterflies between 6am and 6pm. In total, there are 576 spawning sites in 153 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 22 around [Talking Stone Camp](Skyrim_Talking_Stone_Camp.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 17 inside [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 14 around [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 13 around [Lund's Hut](Skyrim_Lund%27s_Hut.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 13 around [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 12 around [Swindler's Den](Skyrim_Swindler%27s_Den.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 11 around [the Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 11 inside [Shadowgreen Cavern](Skyrim_Shadowgreen_Cavern.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))

- 11 around [Whitewatch Tower](Skyrim_Whitewatch_Tower.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))

## Notes
- Torchbug thorax is one of 4 items needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create a *[Spell Tome: Soul Trap](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Soul_Trap.md)*.
- If they are flying too high to catch, you can use the first part of the shout [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) to knock them dead out of the air, down onto the ground.
- Torchbug thorax is actually the abdomen of the insect, rather than the thorax. The thorax is the middle section, where the legs and wings attach to the body, while the abdomen is the end part of the insect.
- Torchbug thorax is only an "uncommon" ingredient for merchant chest [lists](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md); for all other lists and purposes, it is a "common" ingredient.

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot normally be bought from apothecary merchants, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
