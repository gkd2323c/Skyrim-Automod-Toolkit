[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-place-Redoran%27s_Retreat.jpg/200px-SR-place-Redoran%27s_Retreat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Redoran%27s_Retreat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Redoran%27s_Retreat.jpg) Redoran's Retreat **Redoran's Retreat** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) west-northwest of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and a [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md). ## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The cave is on the plains west of Whiterun, at the end of a path that winds behind the rock into which the entrance is built. The entrance is thus concealed from the road between [Fort Greymoor](Skyrim_Fort_Greymoor.md) to the south and [Hamvir's Rest](Skyrim_Hamvir%27s_Rest.md) to the north. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is above the cave entrance to the southeast. Three unusable barrels and a crate are on either side of the entrance.

### Redoran's Retreat
Many fungi grow in the cave, comprising a total of twelve [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md), two [blisterwort](Skyrim_Blisterwort.md), two [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md), one [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md), two [Namira's rot](Skyrim_Namira%27s_Rot.md), and one [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md). Food barrels and sacks, crates, and unusable barrels are found throughout the cave. The entrance leads into a passage that descends to the west, turns north, and passes a narrow braced section to enter the first chamber, which is lit by strings of lanterns. A set of shelves on the left holds four bottles of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md), a bandit patrols near a rock pillar, and a [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)) wanders the area. Behind the pillar to the east is a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) and a small wooden table to the left holding six loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md) and two [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-interior-Redoran%27s_Retreat_Second_Chamber.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Redoran%27s_Retreat_Second_Chamber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Redoran%27s_Retreat_Second_Chamber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Redoran%27s_Retreat_Second_Chamber.jpg) Loot chamber To the west is a narrow twisting passage that opens into a second chamber, where another bandit patrols and a bandit boss is seated at the rear. The chamber features a central rock pillar and two [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md) hanging from the ceiling. Some wooden planking on the ground to the left supports three unowned bedrolls, and a small wooden table holds a bottle of ale. To the right is a small shelving unit holding a [leg of goat](Skyrim_Leg_of_Goat.md) and two [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). A second shelving unit to the right of the first holds a random [healing](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Healing.md), [magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), or [stamina potion](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Stamina.md). At the rear of the chamber is a campfire with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md). A copy of the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Last Seed, v8](Skyrim_2920,_Last_Seed,_v8.md)* sits on a crate to the east. To the right, an unlocked boss chest is protected by a [bear trap](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) in front. The chest holds [Treasure Map IV](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_IV.md). Along the north wall are many food barrels on more wooden planking.

## Notes
- The cave's name may refer to [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).
