[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/SR-place-Solitude_Sawmill_%28wood_mill%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Sawmill_%28wood_mill%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-Solitude_Sawmill_(wood_mill).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-Solitude_Sawmill_(wood_mill).jpg.md) Solitude Sawmill **Solitude Sawmill** is a wood mill southwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). It consists of a house and a sawmill. It is owned by [Hjorunn](Skyrim_Hjorunn.md), a Nord who employs an Orc named [Kharag gro-Shurkul](Skyrim_Kharag_gro-Shurkul.md). You can sell firewood to Hjorunn, and also buy [sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> here after installing [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) by speaking to Kharag gro-Shurkul.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The path leading to the mill has several [thistles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch) along it. There are two garden plots containing a total of fifteen [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and 20 [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants. Alongside the house are several barrels and sacks all containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md), towards the rear of the house are two chicken nests containing [eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg). Beside the mill is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). Along the shore just downstream of the mill is a second grindstone, two food barrels, a table on which are three [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) and a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) and a firepit with an empty pot over it. With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation installed, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) can be found on the shore near the firepit. The path continues past this and an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) a few feet further along, then passes a [clay deposit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clay_Deposit)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> to a small damaged jetty where a small boat has been moored. Inside the boat is a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing) and bucket containing three [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). A [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) is on top of a post at the end of the jetty. Another clay deposit is on a small island southwest of the sawmill.

### Solitude Sawmill
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Sawmill.jpg) Solitude Sawmill interior Upon entering there is a half-height internal wall on your left and two open crates along the northeast wall containing a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [rock warbler egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg), an [orange dartwing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing), four [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and three bulbs of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md). On the other side of the internal wall is a table holding two potatoes, two [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a [raw rabbit leg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Rabbit_Leg), a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and several pieces of cheese. Under the table are two open crates containing a sample of fly amanita, a gourd, a rock warbler egg, an orange dartwing, three leeks and two potatoes. Beside the table is a basket containing potatoes and two cabbages. Against the southwest wall is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, two loaves of bread, a cabbage, and a potato. To the right of this is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front. On the hearth is a piece of [raw beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raw_Beef) and a cabbage in a cast iron pot. There is a second interior wall to the right of the fireplace with a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a [plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate) holding a portion of grilled leeks on top. There is a rack holding three garlic braids hanging from the ceiling.

Against the northeast wall are the stairs up to the top floor. Under the stairs are a set of shelves holding two potatoes and a cupboard holding a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack), an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace) and a woodcutter's axe. To the left are two barrels and sacks of foodstuffs. In the western corner of the building is a bedroom with a single unowned bed with an end table beside it. Behind the internal half-height wall beside the fireplace is a table and chairs set for two with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), two portions of grilled leeks, two seared slaughterfish, a loaf of bread, an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) and a bulb of garlic.

At the top of the stairs is a wooden bench, with a table and two chairs to the left, in the western corner. On the table is a loaf of bread and bottle of wine. Above the table is a wall shelf holding three copies of *[The Firmament](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Firmament)*. Against the southwest wall is a small unit holding two [goblets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet), a plate, a common book and an unlocked display cabinet containing an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). Beside the unit is a sack. The top floor only covers half of the building and has a railing around it. In the southern corner is a second single unowned bed with an end table beside it and an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of the bed.

## Notes
- The [key to the millhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Solitude_Sawmill) is carried by Hjorunn and Kharag.
- Unlike most other houses, it is locked with a novice-level lock during the day, but unlocked at night.
