[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Vampire_Assassin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vampire_Assassin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Assassin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Assassin.jpg) Vampire Assassin The **Vampire Assassin** is a female [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) that can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) if you have completed the quest [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) for the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). She carries the [assassin's writ](Skyrim_Assassin%27s_Writ.md) and drops [vampire dust](Skyrim_Vampire_Dust.md) and some random [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) when she dies. She also spawns with some (dead) skeletons, which she will often raise in battle. However, she don't appear at all, if you are a member of the [Harkon's](Skyrim_Harkon.md) [court](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md).

## Notes
- You can still encounter the assassin even after finishing the Dawnguard questline.
