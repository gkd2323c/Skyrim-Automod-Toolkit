Ayleid Reference Text **by [Raelys Anine](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Raelys_Anine)** *A series of Ayleid phrases and their translations*
---
*Note: In * [Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) *, the book is called "Ayleid Inscriptions Translated" and the name of the author is "Beredalmo the Signifier".*

---
The following inscriptions were painstakingly transcribed and interpreted over many long years, and are preserved here for all time.

<center>--
*Av molag anyammis, av latta magicka.*</center>"From fire, life; from light, magic."

<center>*Barra agea ry sou karan.*</center>"Wear lore as your armor."

<center>--
*Agea haelia ne jorane emero laloria.*</center>"Wisdom learned by pain is a reliable guide in dark times." [literally, "Terrible wisdom never betrayed the loremasters."]

<center>--
*Nou aldmeris mathmeldi admia aurane gandra sepredia av relleis ye brelyeis ye varlais.*</center>"Our exiled Elven ancestors heard the welcoming gifts of peace in the streams and beech trees and stars." ["Mathmeldi" means literally "from-home-driven."]

<center>--
*Suna ye sunnabe.*</center>"Bless and blessed be."

<center>--
*Va garlas agea, gravia ye goria, lattia mallari av malatu.*</center>"In the caverns of lore, ugly and obscure, shines the gold of truth."

<center>--
*Vabria frensca, sa belle, sa baune, amaraldane aldmeris adonai.*</center>"The foaming wave, so thunderous, so mighty, heralds the lordly Elves."
