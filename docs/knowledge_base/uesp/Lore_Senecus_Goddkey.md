Senecus Goddkey was a [Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md) officer of the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1HR-1)</sup> active in the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He is most well-known as the short-time Provisional Governor of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) after Baron [Volag](Lore_Volag.md) of the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md) disappeared from the public eye.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup>

After the Purge of the North drove out the Crowns from Sentinel in [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862), Baron Volag and his Forebears took over the [capital city](Lore_Sentinel.md), however, as the Crowns were cornered in [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md), Volag and his forces retreated to the wilderness and left Sentinel without a leader.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGTobias-3)</sup> As per tradition in the newly formed [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), Colovian officers; some of the most exemplary members of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s army were named Provisional Governors of human regions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1HR-1)</sup> Senecus Goddkey was given the status of Provisional Governor in Sentinel and took up [Samuruik](Lore_Samuruik.md); Sentinel's seat of power as his headquarters.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RGTobias-3)</sup>

Under his reign, the city underwent an Imperial reorganization and became an exotic retreat for nobles across the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). People from [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) and [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) traveled to Sentinel to dine on local cuisine, enjoy native craftsmanship and visit the Royal Theatre.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup> However, after the events that transpired in Stros M'Kai in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), in which fellow Governor [Amiel Richton](Lore_Amiel_Richton.md) was assassinated, Baron Volag returned from hiding and retook Sentinel for himself. What happened to Senecus Goddkey from that is unknown.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGIszara-4)</sup>
