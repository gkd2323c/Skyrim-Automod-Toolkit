Arkan, also known as **Arkan the Gifted**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFSo BDe-1)</sup> was a scribe from [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), active in the very-early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). His most well-known work was transcribing the text depicting legends of [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md), the Bandit God of the South. His work was published in [2E 24](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_24) and he came to believe in the god, as some sort of force that plays a part in the world.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFSo BD-2)</sup> The first scroll was part of a collection found by a nomad in the caves of [Lake Vread](Lore_Lake_Vread.md), in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), and Arkan transcribed them as well. What became of the second scroll is unknown, however, the third scroll was among the rarest books across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), portraying the tale of Baan Dar's theft of the [Thirty-Seventh Sermon of Vivec](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_37.md) before he could write it. A copy was kept in the Library of Dusk, found in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLo DRB-3)</sup>
