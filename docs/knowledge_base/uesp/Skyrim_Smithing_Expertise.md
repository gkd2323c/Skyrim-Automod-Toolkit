*Enchanting description:* **Raises the wielder's Smithing abilities, and does <mag> shock damage to enemies on hit.**

## Enchanting
Only one item uses this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Smithing Expertise if you find it and disenchant it:

- [Notched Pickaxe](Skyrim_Notched_Pickaxe.md)

## Notes
- The enhancement is always +5 smithing and with a variable on damage based on enchanting skill.
- The effect's editor ID is `dun Volunruud Pickaxe Ab Equip`, suggesting the Notched Pickaxe was originally obtainable in [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) instead of the top of the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md).

## Bugs
- The Smithing Expertise enchantment restores damage to your [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill rather than enhancing it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, addresses this issue. However, version 1.3.3 prevents the pickaxe from being disenchanted, meaning the enchantment only exists on the now-unique pickaxe.
