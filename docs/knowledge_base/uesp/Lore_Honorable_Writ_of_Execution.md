Honorable Writ of Execution *A generic Morag Tong execution writ* *
---
*All writs bear a name at the top.*

---
The afore-mentioned personage has been marked for honorable execution in accordance to the lawful tradition and practice of the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md) Guild. The Bearer of this non-disputable document has official sanctioned license to kill the afore-mentioned personage.
