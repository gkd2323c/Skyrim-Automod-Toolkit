[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-npc-Eriana.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eriana.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eriana.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eriana.jpg) Eriana **Eriana**, a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md). If you choose to side with Sinding, you will have to kill her.

Eriana wears a set of [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) along with [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md) and a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). For close ranged combat, she relies on a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) that can be up to [Elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality and a leveled [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) that can be up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) in quality. For long ranged combat, she relies on a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) and twenty [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md), both of which can be up to Elven in quality.

## Related Quests
- **[Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md)**: Participate in the Daedric Prince [Hircine](Skyrim_Hircine.md)'s Great Hunt.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
She will only say:

- *"Greetings, fellow hunter."*
- *"Lead on."*

## Notes
- Eriana is one of two NPCs in the game, with the other being [Sabine Nytte](Skyrim_Sabine_Nytte.md), to have a race-specific voice type (in her case this is "Female Nord") to not actually be a member of said race.
