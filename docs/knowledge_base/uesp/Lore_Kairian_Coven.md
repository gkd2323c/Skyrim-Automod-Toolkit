# Kairian Coven

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-major_adornment-Crone%27s_Wicker_Coronal_02.jpg/200px-ON-major_adornment-Crone%27s_Wicker_Coronal_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-major_adornment-Crone%27s_Wicker_Coronal_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-major_adornment-Crone%27s_Wicker_Coronal_02.jpg) Crone's Wicker Coronal The Kairian Coven was a coven of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [witches](Lore_Witches.md) known to have been active before or during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONCWC-1)</sup>

## History
At an unspecified time before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the Kairian Coven fractured, with many of its members joining a rival group of Reachfolk witches known as the [Icereach Coven](Lore_Icereach_Coven.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONCWC-1)</sup>

## Culture
Within the coven, there existed a ceremonial role known as the seedswoman. Her duty was to craft a wickerwood coronal each year, formally known as Crone's Wicker Coronal, which was used to crown the coven's leader. After wearing the coronal for exactly a year and a day, the leader would then pass it on to a dark champion, offering it as a mark of recognition to a like-minded, twisted soul. Following the schism, this practice began to fade. It is unclear whether the Kairian Coven was formally disbanded or merely abandoned certain customs as their numbers dwindled.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONCWC-1)</sup>
