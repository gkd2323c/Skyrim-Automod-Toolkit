Improvised Memorial *An epitaph found in the Bloodroot Forge*
---
Throm Ulfgard died here. His foe was the stone. After two fortnights it bested him. His battle won't be heard in song, but may [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md) greet him all the same.
