## Related Quests
- **[Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md)**: Participate in [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)'s Sacred Trials by hunting the guardian beasts of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

## Notes
- Despite being spectral in appearance, the guardian spirits seem to retain all aspects of their represented species and are killable with normal weapons. They leave behind ghostly remains, but the loot table matches their represented species as well.
- It will not appear until you are actually on the part of the quest that asks you to hunt it.
- From the Sabre Cat Guardian Spirit follow the ice wall southwest. A novice-locked chest and a copy of the restoration skill book *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](Skyrim_2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4.md)* can be found in a small alcove to the southeast.
