[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-npc-Valkyn_Methats.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valkyn_Methats.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valkyn_Methats.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valkyn_Methats.jpg) Valkyn Methats Valkyn Methats is one of the main antagonists of [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). He and [Valkyn Gatanas](Skyrim_Valkyn_Gatanas.md) are to serve as heralds for the coming of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md).

He wields the unique sword [Torment](Skyrim_Torment.md) in combat.

He has a 75% [resistance to fire](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md).
