[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/ON-map-Dawnmead.jpg/200px-ON-map-Dawnmead.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Dawnmead.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Dawnmead.jpg) Map of Dawnmead circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Dawnmead** is the easternmost region of the greater [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) region, located on the eastern end of the [Crescent River](Lore_Crescent_River.md). Dawnmead itself is a subregion of the northern region of Elsweyr, [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md). The city of [Dune](Lore_Dune.md) is the largest Khajiiti settlement in the region. The ruins of [Fort Sphinxmoth](Lore_Fort_Sphinxmoth.md) lie on the northern edge of the region and have been occupied by several ruffians and groups. Thizzrini Arena is a well-known arena in Reaper's March, with a wide variety of competitors. There are a few settlements located in the area, including Pa'alat and S'ren-ja.
