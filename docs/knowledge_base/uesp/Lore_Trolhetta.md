Its located approximately at the intersection of the [Jerall](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md), [Velothi](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) and [Valus](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) mountain ranges.

## History
The mountain has changed hands many times over the centuries. There are [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) tales of a family of [giants](Lore_Giant.md) who lived atop the mountain. In the story of the Silver Werewolf, the titular character found out that the family had been kidnapped by a tribe of goblins, and journeyed to free them so that they could be reunited at their home on Trolhetta.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSW-1)</sup> In the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), it belonged to a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) clan, who built an extensive [Daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) shrine on its peak. Beneath the shrine are a vast series of lava tunnels that twist and wind through the mountain's core.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONLoading-2)</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Trolhetta was considered a part of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), one of the [Old Holds](Lore_Old_Holds.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the summit of Trolhetta was the site of the battle between the forces of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) and the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md), led by the fused spirits of the legendary [giant](Lore_Giant.md) [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md) and the necromancer Thallik Wormfather. The battle was won by the Pact forces using the power of [Wuuthrad](Lore_Wuuthrad.md), which ended Sinmur's threat to the Rift and resulted in King Jorunn being honored by the spirits of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) and his [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md) atop the mountain.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSS-4)</sup>

Access to the mountain was limited to a cave in the southeastern corner of the Rift.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON-3)</sup> The cavern system of Gromm's Pass located around the same area was open in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), allowing [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) to travel from Cyrodiil to Skyrim through the Jeralls, but it is unknown if it was related to the caves that provided access to Trolhetta's summit.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Goblins-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SR-6)</sup>
