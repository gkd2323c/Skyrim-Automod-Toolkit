[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/SR-npc-Olfina_Gray-Mane.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Olfina_Gray-Mane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olfina_Gray-Mane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olfina_Gray-Mane.jpg) Olfina Gray-Mane **Olfina Gray-Mane** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [food vendor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor) who lives in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) alongside the rest of her [family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gray-Mane). Despite the general uneasiness between her family and the [Battle-Born family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battle-Born), Olfina is in a relationship with [Jon Battle-Born](Skyrim_Jon_Battle-Born.md), although both try and keep the relationship a secret from their families.

She usually wakes up and leaves [House Gray-Mane](Skyrim_House_Gray-Mane.md) at around nine in the morning to patrol the city, sometimes going to [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md). She patrols until 1pm, when she browses the local market. At 4pm, she enters [the Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) to work there. She heads home at about 2am for sleep. Interestingly, despite her bold words, she is scripted to flee from all combat.

If Whiterun is taken over by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md), the [current jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) will be deposed and replaced with [Vignar Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md), for whom Olfina will be the new [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md). If she is housecarl, she will also be present for [the capture](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md) of the dragon [Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) on the Dragonsreach Porch, although due to her cowardice she won't be much help in bringing him down.

Olfina wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0). She is equipped with a [Skyforge steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Dagger), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_Gray-Mane) to the clan's house, as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Fallen
During the quest [The Fallen](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md), after you have convinced Vignar to use his palace as a dragon trap, Olfina will only comment:

*"I don't know how you talked the Jarl into this plan of yours."* She will head up to the great porch of Dragonsreach and scout the landscape for dragons saying nothing but:

*"Let's get this over with."* When [Odahviing](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) arrives, she will flee from the battle and hide. When he is down, she will shout:

*"Get it into the trap before it kills us all!"* Once the dragon is stuck in the trap, she will position herself at a safe distance while you talk to Odahviing.

### The World-Eater's Eyrie
After a while, when the quest [The World-Eater's Eyrie](Skyrim_The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie.md) is initiated, she will start wandering around the captured dragon and will, when [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md) turns up and makes bad suggestions to the captured dragon, say:

*"Farengar, very bad idea. Even for you."* Once Odahviing is freed, she will go down to the dragon and ensure it keeps its promise. When you climb onto the dragon she will comment:

*"You're either the bravest person I've ever met, or the biggest fool."*
### Missing In Action
During the quest [Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md), you may ask Olfina if there's anything she can tell you about [Thorald](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md), her missing brother:

**Is there anything you can tell me about Thorald?** *"It's been a while now. I miss him, but it's clear he's not coming back. He knew what he was getting into. I don't... I don't blame anyone."*
## Conversations
While browsing the market, Olfina may talk with various people:

**[Anoriath](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Anoriath)**: *"Ah, Olfina. How may I help you?"*
**Olfina**: *"Got any sausages? I'm cooking a special breakfast for a friend, and they're his favorite."*
**Anoriath**: *"Would your good friend be named Jon, by any chance? I've spied the two of you talking quite intimately."* **OR** *"Glad to see you're making new friends. I know Jon's death was hard on you."* (if Jon is dead)
**Olfina**: *"Best you forget whatever you think you saw, elf. Such idle talk can prove hazardous to one's health."*

**[Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md)**: *"Are you still working at the Bannered Mare in the evenings, Olfina?"*
**Olfina**: *"And why shouldn't I? Just because I'm a Gray-Mane doesn't mean I can't earn my own keep."*
**Ysolda**: *"I didn't mean... I'm sorry I asked."*

**Olfina**: *"Did you raise your prices, Carlotta?"*
**[Carlotta](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia.md)**: *"I had to. The farmers are charging me double for the produce I sell."*
**Olfina**: *"Let me guess, they're saying it's because of this trouble with the Stormcloaks?"*
**Carlotta**: *"Exactly. Road patrols were cut in half because the Jarl needed soldiers. Now the bandits are bolder than ever. They've been raiding the farms for food, and supplies are scarcer than ever. Maybe I should sell swords and armor, instead."*
**Olfina**: *"Maybe, but they wouldn't taste as good as a loaf of fresh-baked pumpernickel."*

## Notes
- Similar to how he carries a letter from her, there's an unused [letter from Jon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Jon) present in the files that likely would've been carried by Olfina. If taken, it could be used in the same manner to get information from Jon during Missing in Action.
- Olfina's ref, as well as her relationship markers, refer to her as **Olfina the Golden**. One relationship marker also calls her **Olfine the Golden**, which is likely just a typo. - She is also called this in the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*.
- In the Creation Kit, Olfina Gray-Mane has scripts set up to rent rooms in The Bannered Mare, implying she was intended to be a backup innkeeper. However, due to her status as the Stormcloak housecarl, this seems to have been scrapped. - Both Jon and [Elrindir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elrindir) will gain dialogue that mentions Olfina having taking over the Bannered Mare, should Hulda die.
- Talking to Olfina while she's at work will only lead to generic responses from her, such as *"Yes?"* and *"Need something?"*
- Despite the fact that Olfina is part of the "food vendor" class, she isn't actually a food merchant herself, making it impossible to buy food from her.
