**Fire Rune** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, causing [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) and setting targets on fire, dealing 30% of the immediate spell damage, scaling down quadratically over 2 seconds. You can't cast it just anywhere. When you press the cast button, the cross-hair will let you know if you are pointing at a valid surface within range. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- [Fire Rune](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 50 pts for 2 sec

## Perks
- [Rune Master](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rune_Master) increases range to 100 feet.

## Notes
- You can set off fire runes (your own or others) using any other Destruction spell from a distance.
- Fire Rune does not benefit from the Augmented Flames perk.
- Fire Rune’s damage can be increased with fortify destruction potions, weakness to magic poisons, and weakness to fire poison.
- A Fire Rune can also be cast by bashing with the [Dawnguard Rune Hammer](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Rune_Hammer.md), by using stamina instead of magicka.
