# Southern Sea

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/ON-interior-Cave_of_Broken_Sails.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Cave_of_Broken_Sails.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Cave_of_Broken_Sails.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Cave_of_Broken_Sails.jpg) A cove on the Southern Sea The Southern Sea (known as the **Sea of [Serpents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Serpent)** or the **Serpentwild** in the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) tongue)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LATSFS-1)</sup> is the body of water that borders most of the southern coast of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It is a part of the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) and borders both the [Cape of the Blue Divide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cape_of_the_Blue_Divide) and the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls) in the west. Its waters include both [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) and [Oliis Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oliis_Bay).

The Southern Sea encompasses large cities that are vital in trade, such as the harbor-city of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md), or the trading-towns of [Lilmoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lilmoth) and [Southpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Southpoint). Compared to the corsairs and pirates of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), the ones in these seas keep a much lower profile, to avoid the passing [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) and [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) warships. A large portion of these seafarers are [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Co BS-2)</sup>

## Tamriel

### Black Marsh
The province's southern coast is shared between the Southern Sea and the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). The only cities on the former are the towns of Lilmoth and [Soulrest](Lore_Soulrest.md), though the latter is found between both of the bodies of water. This area of Black Marsh was once occupied by the nomadic [Beastfolk](Lore_Beastfolk.md) known as the [Lilmothiit](Lore_Lilmothiit.md), though little of their civilization remains throughout the coast.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3EA-3)</sup> In Murkmire, the region where Lilmoth is located, the land gives way and fades into the water. Legend has it that Murkmire extended further south but was swallowed by the Southern Sea.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Murkmire-4)</sup> There are scattered ruins across the coast and floating villages like [Alten Meerhleel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Alten_Meerhleel), or even Lilmoth. The island of [Norg-Tzel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Norg-Tzel) is found off the coast, southeast of Lilmoth.

### Elsweyr
The region of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md) holds the province's coastline through the jungle treetops of the [Tenmar Forest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tenmar_Forest). The land extends southeast into a region known as the [Quin'rawl Peninsula](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Quin%27rawl_Peninsula) where the aforementioned city of Senchal is located. Since ancient times, Senchal has been known for its harbors and nautical people.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSK-5)</sup> Which gave way to their success as one of the largest trading cities on Tamriel. Senchal is the Southern Sea's gathering spot for pirates, buccaneers, and all sorts of seafarers looking for illicit goods.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1ETEC-6)</sup> Another city on the Southern Sea is the home of the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md), [Torval](Lore_Torval.md). The Khajiit of Torval often exploited the bounties of the sea, trading it with the ancient kingdom of [Ne Quin-al](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ne_Quin-al).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE3EE-7)</sup> The [Halls of Colossus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Halls_of_Colossus) are found on the south coast.

The island of [Khenarthi's Roost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Khenarthi%27s_Roost) is found off the province's coast, southwest of Senchal. Like Senchal and Torval, Khenarthi's Roost was an ancient kingdom, known as Khenarthia. The ancient Khajiit were experts in harvesting [Moon Sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moon_Sugar), which is often grown on Elsweyr's coast, as well as expert fishermen.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSK-5)</sup> The town of [Mistral](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mistral) is the island's largest settlement, and it is surrounded by a large ravine where the Southern Sea fills in.

### Valenwood
The region of [Grahtwood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Grahtwood) is found along the southern coast. The cities of [Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Haven), [Greenheart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenheart), and [Southpoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Southpoint) are found on the Southern Sea. Where the sea meets the Cape of the Blue Divide is uncertain. The coast is largely unpopulated, save for these cities as most of the coast is covered in large mountains. The town of Greenheart is the only one of these cities to not have a port, as it is nestled deep in the mountains. The Cave of Broken Sails, is a large cove found between Haven and Southpoint. It is one of the many hidden pirate bases found along the Southern Sea.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOLoad Co BS-2)</sup> The region that encompasses Haven, Southpoint, and the Cave of Broken Sails is known as the [Long Coast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Long_Coast).

## Other Lands

### Pyandonea
Some sources suggest that [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md), home of the Maormer, lies within the Southern Sea.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Co BS-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Grotto LS-9)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LATSFS-1)</sup> The archipelago is surrounded by dangerous waters and impenetrable mist, providing camoflague for Maomeri ships and their tame [sea serpents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_Serpent).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE3Other-10)</sup> Tamrielic trade guilds that venture too far south inevitably fall victim to Maormer piracy, often requiring payment of dues to a clan captain for safe passage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LATSFS-1)</sup>

### Solstice
The tropical island of [Solstice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Solstice) is located south of Black Marsh.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Historically, Tamrielic mainlanders have considered the island to be cursed, leading to its relative isolation.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> This is due in part to the dangers of sailing to Solstice, which is surrounded by perilous waters and home to many shipwrecks.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup> The [Corelanyan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) capital of [Sunport](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sunport) is the main port of call on the island.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
