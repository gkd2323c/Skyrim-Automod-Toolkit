[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SR-item-Firiniel%27s_End.jpg/200px-SR-item-Firiniel%27s_End.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Firiniel%27s_End.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Firiniel%27s_End.jpg) Firiniel's End [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bound_Until_Death_04.jpg) Gabriella's Cache Firiniel's End is an [elven bow](Skyrim_Elven_Bow.md) that is left in a convenient location to perform the assassination of [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) during her wedding by [Gabriella](Skyrim_Gabriella.md), as part of [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md). The bow is enchanted to do twenty points of [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) upon strike, while also [slowing](Skyrim_Slow.md) the target by fifty points for three seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Firiniel's End requires an ingot of [refined moonstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone), as well as the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perk. Any improvements to the weapon from the use of a grindstone are doubled if the [Elven Smithing](Skyrim_Elven_Smithing.md) perk is also unlocked.

While preparing for the assassination of Vittoria, speaking to Gabriella will have her reveal to you the location of this bow: *"I hope you don't mind, but I took the liberty of surveying the site of the reception. There's a small parapet, just opposite the balcony, that would prove an excellent spot for a long-range kill. I left something there for you. If you're inclined to take that approach."* Gabriella was also kind enough to include six arrows and an [Elixir of True Shot](Skyrim_Elixir_of_True_Shot.md), to allow for an easier long-ranged kill of the target.
