# Add-ons


*[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)*, *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)*, and *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* are included as part of the [Legendary Edition](Skyrim_Legendary_Edition.md) and the Special Edition. Neither of the free add-ons, [Fall of the Space Core, Vol 1](Skyrim_Fall_of_the_Space_Core,_Vol_1.md) and the [High Resolution Texture Pack](Skyrim_High_Resolution_Texture_Pack.md), are available for the Special Edition. The latter add-on is made redundant for this release, however, as the Special Edition uses high-resolution textures in place of the original assets. The [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) includes *Dawnguard*, *Hearthfire*, and *Dragonborn* and all [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) content.

## Creation Club
[![SR-misc-Creation Club Logo.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png/200px-SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-Creation_Club_Logo.png) **[Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)** was an in-game [microtransactional](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Microtransaction) store for [Skyrim Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md). A [virtual currency](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Virtual_goods) called **Creation Club Credits** was sold within it, which was used to purchase unique add-ons called [Creations](Skyrim_Creations.md). Each Creation released through Creation Club is official [Bethesda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Bethesda) content, developed with third-party developers and contracted [mod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods) authors. As official content, Creation Club content does not disable [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md) acquisition.

The service entered beta on September 28, 2017 and officially launched on October 4, 2017. It was never available for [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md).

All 74 Creations are included in the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md). Special Edition and [Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) users also receive free access to four Creations: [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md), [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md), [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), and [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md). Several other discounted Creations were offered for free either on a permanent or temporary basis. On Switch, the Anniversary Upgrade with all the Creations is available, but not the Creation Club itself.

As of December 5, 2023, Creation Club has been retired and the Verified Creator Program is intended to be its replacement. While Creation Club Creations are considered official content, [Verified Creator Program](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Verified_Creator_Program) Creations are considered third party [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods).
