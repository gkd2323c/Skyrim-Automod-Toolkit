# Reptile

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-misc-Lizard.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-misc-Lizard.png) A lizard, a type of reptile There are many different species of reptiles in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LGReptile-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Kill R-2)</sup> Reptiles all have scaly skin, while some also have feathers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Durzog-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Most species reproduce by laying eggs,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EGT-7)</sup> but some are capable of giving live birth.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGP-9)</sup> Reptiles are typically cold-blooded, and thus struggle to survive in cold-envrioments.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONArg-11)</sup> Though this is not true of all reptiles.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONArg-11)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Durzog-5)</sup> Certain immortal beings are reptilian in form, the most famous of which being [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md). Lesser [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) such as [Clannfear](Lore_Clannfear.md) and [Daedroths](Lore_Daedroth.md) also resemble reptiles.
