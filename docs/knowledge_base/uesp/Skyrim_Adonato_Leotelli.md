[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-npc-Adonato_Leotelli.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Adonato_Leotelli.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adonato_Leotelli.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Adonato_Leotelli.jpg) Adonato Leotelli working on a new book **Adonato Leotelli** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md) and author, known for his books *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)* and *[Ghosts in the Storm](Skyrim_Ghosts_in_the_Storm.md)*. He is staying at [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

Adonato spends all of his time at Candlehearth Hall, socializing, eating, or sleeping at random intervals throughout the day, although he can always be found on the second floor between noon and 8pm, busy writing his next book.

Adonato wears a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md#000cee80) and a pair of [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md#000cee82). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to defend himself if threatened. He also carries a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). His [journal](Skyrim_Adonato_Leotelli%27s_Journal.md) can be found laying on a table in the Hall.

## Quest-Related Events

### Delivery
Asking him what kind of writing he does will make him ask for a [favor](Skyrim_Delivery.md):

- **What kind of writing do you do?**
- *"I write drama, friend. The legends and history of Skyrim made to excite and inspire. Poor [Giruad Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md)* [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) has been waiting for my latest work, 'Olaf and the Dragon,' for weeks now. Roads are a mess."*

- - **I'll deliver the book for you.**
- *"You're sure? When you see Giruad * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *, ask for a tip. I'm sure he'll be in a good mood after reading this."*

- - **That's a shame.**
- *"Indeed it is."*

## Dialogue
When approached, Adonato will comment on [recent events](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md) in Windhelm:

- *"I still can't believe Isabella's gone. She was such a helpful young lass."*

He can also say:

- *"Know any tales of nobility and courage? I'm keen to record them."*
- *"I'll find some way to finish my book."*
- *"Now more than ever, the world needs tales of heroism."*
- *"I'm a writer by trade. Not that the people of Skyrim do much reading."*

He can exit conversation with:

- *"May you lose yourself in the pages of a good book!"*
- *"As you travel Skyrim, make sure you stop and take in the view from time to time!"*
