## [Cities](Skyrim_Cities.md)
Each city (with the exception of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md)) is located in a different [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) and is home to that hold's ruler, or [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md). The five largest cities (the major cities) are walled, allowing their maps (or *cells* as they are called in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit)) to be treated separately from the main map. [Carriages](Skyrim_Carriage.md) are available for hire outside each major city to transport the player to any other major city.

Fast traveling to a major city places you just inside its main gate, but each major city also has a keep or palace where the jarl resides, which is a separate fast-travel destination and is listed below. Additionally, Riften has a third fast-travel destination once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), namely the entrance to the guild's [headquarters](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(place).md).
