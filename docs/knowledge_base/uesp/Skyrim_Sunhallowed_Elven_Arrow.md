[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-item-Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrows.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrows.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrows.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sunhallowed_Elven_Arrows.jpg) A quiver of Sunhallowed Elven Arrows A Sunhallowed Elven Arrow is an [elven arrow](Skyrim_Elven_Arrow.md) that has been blessed by [Knight-Paladin Gelebor](Skyrim_Knight-Paladin_Gelebor.md). The arrows are visually similar to the elven arrows that are needed to create them, with a gray shaft instead of a golden one, and the fletchings alternating between gray and white. The quiver is similar, being a more pallid color compared to the strong golden one of normal arrows. The arrowhead, however, is a bright, glowing gold; not the standard gold of regular elven arrows. When fired at the sun during the day with [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md), it will cause a [sunburst](Skyrim_Sun_Damage.md), doing damage to any target caught in the blast, with [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) taking triple damage. It is the counterpart of the [Bloodcursed Elven Arrow](Skyrim_Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrow.md).

When used against enemies with Auriel's Bow, they cause small explosions similar to a sunburst attack. The effect isn't dependent on the bow's enchantment, so it can be used even if the enchantment is empty, though the arrows are destroyed by the attack, so it isn't possible to retrieve the arrows from bodies or missed shots in the environment. If you use the arrows with other bows, there are no special effects and they behave like regular arrows. This is the same if you give sunhallowed arrows to followers.
