[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/ON-item-weapon-Trinimac_Bow.jpg/200px-ON-item-weapon-Trinimac_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-weapon-Trinimac_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-weapon-Trinimac_Bow.jpg) Bow crafted to emulate Elsarel's weapon Elsarel was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) master of bow and a hero held in high regard by the Orc branch of the Cult of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md). According to the scriptures of the Trinimac faithful Elsarel was known for their great feats of archery. One of the well-known feats, recorded by [Venerable Uugus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Venerable_Uugus) was slaying the colossal [Daedroth](Lore_Daedroth.md) Zetthax. It is said that the Orsimer hero managed to slay the monster with a single arrow through the eye. It is also believed that Elsarel honored their god Trinimac, the Golden Champion by practicing archery with the bow for 20 hours out of every 24.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS-1)</sup>
