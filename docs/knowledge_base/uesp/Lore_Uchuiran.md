[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/ON-load-Coldperch_Cavern.jpg/200px-ON-load-Coldperch_Cavern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Coldperch_Cavern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Coldperch_Cavern.jpg) Coldperch Cavern Uchuiran was one of the thirteen [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachman.md) warlords of the [Winterborn Clan](Lore_Winterborn.md). He lived during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) period, around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), and fought against the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) for his ancestral homeland, [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKK-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LTM-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/ON-creature-Kraala_Birdsong.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Kraala_Birdsong.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Kraala_Birdsong.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Kraala_Birdsong.jpg) Kraala Birdsong, his lover During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the Winterborn Clan ruled over Wrothgar amidst the reign of the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) of the Reach.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONAlga-4)</sup> When the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) had formed and the Orcs were diplomatically granted full control of the Wrothgar territory, the Winterborn fought against them as the ongoing [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) was raging on in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Uchuiran was one of the thirteen leading warlords of the splintered clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKK-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LTM-3)</sup>

Despite that, [Urfon Ice-Heart](Lore_Urfon_Ice-Heart.md) was considered the leader of the Winterborn in general as of 2E 582.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKK-1)</sup> Little is known of the other eleven leaders. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Uchuiran's forces were stationed in Coldperch Cavern, where Kraala Birdsong, a [hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) lover of Uchuiran, was transforming both their forces and prisoners into birds.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LTM-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BC-5)</sup> In reality, Kraala despised both the Orcs and the Winterborn, though she found the Reachfolk useful in some ways.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-KJ-6)</sup> She was eventually slain by mercenaries hired by the local Orcs.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BC-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BOTB-7)</sup>

Little is known of Uchuiran's fate, as he was not present during the mercenaries attack.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BC-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BOTB-7)</sup> Although, as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), King Kurog claimed that Urfon was the last of the thirteen Winterbon leaders, before defeating him and his forces at [Frostbreak Fortress](Lore_Frostbreak_Fortress.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOKK-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FKAG-8)</sup> letters mentioning Uchuiran as alive were found in Reachfolk camps in Coldperch Cavern at roughly the same time.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WN-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LTM-3)</sup> Regardless, the remaining Winterborn forces were ultimately defeated during the siege of [Frostbreak Fortress](Lore_Frostbreak_Fortress.md) in 2E 582 by [Kurog gro-Orsinium](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md)'s forces and [allies](Lore_The_Vestige.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-FKAG-8)</sup>
