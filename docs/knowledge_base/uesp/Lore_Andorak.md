Andorak Septim was the King of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) and the son and heir of [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Uriel IV](Lore_Uriel_IV.md). He was the grandson of Empress [Katariah](Lore_Katariah.md) and the nephew of Emperor [Cassynder](Lore_Cassynder.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-2)</sup>

After Uriel IV died in [3E 247](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_247), the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) voted to disinherit Andorak, and instead they proclaimed Uriel IV's cousin [Cephorus](Lore_Cephorus_II.md) as emperor because they considered him more closely related to the original [Septim](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md) line. For the first nine years of Cephorus II's reign, Andorak's loyalists battled the [Imperial](Lore_Third_Empire.md) forces in a rebellion. In an act that the Sage [Eraintine](Lore_Eraintine.md) described as "[Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s heart beating no more", the Elder Council granted Andorak the Kingdom of Shornhelm in [3E 256](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_256), ending his rebellion. Andorak's descendants still ruled Shornhelm as of the turn of the fifth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BHOTE3-2)</sup>
