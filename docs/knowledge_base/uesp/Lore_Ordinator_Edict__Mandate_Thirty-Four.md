[Ordinator Edict](Lore_Ordinator_Edict.md), Mandate Thirty-Four **by [Inquisitor Nivos Uveran](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Inquisitor_Nivos_Uveran)** *Ordinator decrees for the citizens of Vivec* *
---
Let it be known:

Destroying, moving, or otherwise vandalizing Temple property is a grievous sin. Temple icons and structures serve as extensions of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md)'s divine authority. Any assault on said icons and structures is considered an assault on the Tribunal's divine personages. Such an assault carries a penalty of death, followed by Rites of Forgetting and disposal of remains in the Pyres of Purification. Behold the price of blasphemy.

Victory for the Three,

Inquisitor Nivos Uveran
