[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Mogdurz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mogdurz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogdurz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogdurz.jpg) Mogdurz **Mogdurz** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works in [Gloombound Mine](Skyrim_Gloombound_Mine.md), right next to [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md). She spends her whole day mining the [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) and [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) ore veins in the caves.

She wears a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Blacksmith%27s_Apron.md#0006ff37) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md#0005b69e). She carries a small amount of gold, a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), and a war axe which can be either [steel](Skyrim_Steel_War_Axe.md) or [Orcish](Skyrim_Orcish_War_Axe.md) quality, depending on your level.
