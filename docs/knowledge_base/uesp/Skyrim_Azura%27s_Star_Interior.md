[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-interior-Azura%27s_Star_Realm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Azura%27s_Star_Realm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Azura%27s_Star_Realm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Azura%27s_Star_Realm.jpg) Azura's Star Interior **Azura's Star Interior** is a small alternate dimension within [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md) containing [Malyn Varen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malyn_Varen) and any [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) that he summons.

The interior is brightly lit and composed of bluish crystalline structures, with angled ice-like shards thrusting up from the floor and down from above. The floor is made of this same material, forming many hexagonal segments at different heights.

## Related Quests
- **[The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star)**: Help find and repair [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md).

## Walkthrough
You are transported inside the star by either [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) or [Nelacar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nelacar), depending on your choices during [The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star). Regardless of your choice of ally, you will be transported to the same place, and the zone will not change depending on your choice. When you speak to Azura or Nelacar and say that you are ready, they will trap your soul and transport you inside the star in order to defeat [Malyn Varen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Malyn_Varen) to cleanse Azura's Star, and in the case of Nelacar, to sever the star's link to Azura.

When you enter, Malyn will engage you in conversation. He will initially assume his disciples have sent him a fresh soul, but will then realize you are different. Regardless of your response, he will claim to have conquered mortality and run off, saying he's sacrificed too much to surrender now. Follow him down a narrow path, which spirals gradually around and down to a roughly circular area with a central collection of larger shards thrusting upwards. There are a total of three leveled [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) in addition to Malyn for you to face. Once Malyn is defeated, either Azura or Nelacar will speak to you and transport you out of the star to your previous location. If you wish to loot any [Daedra hearts](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) or other items from the Dremora, you should leave Malyn for last. As Malyn and the Dremora all attack using magic, some form of magical defense is recommended; you may be simultaneously attacked by all three elements. Apart from Malyn and the Dremora, there is nothing else of interest within the star.

## Notes
- Followers are unable to accompany you inside the star.
- You are unable to return to this location after Malyn is defeated.
- If you manage to raise a Dremora inside the star and bring it outside, it will never disintegrate after the spell effect ends, and may thus be raised indefinitely.<sup>[*verification needed — needs confirming.*]</sup>
