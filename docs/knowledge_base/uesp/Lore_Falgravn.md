Lord Falgravn was a [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md) who terrorized the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) for untold ages. His origins are unclear, but legends say that he was a pirate. He and his crew attacked a whaling vessel, but when they went below decks to survey their latest catch, all they found was corpses and [bloodfiends](Lore_Bloodfiend.md). He'd been traveling the Sea of Ghosts "since [Ysmir](Lore_Wulfharth.md)'s reign". He would vanish for a few hundred years at a time before returning.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOOgvar-1)</sup> Falgravn was rumored to be able to twist people's minds and bend them to his will. He was also said to sink ships in frothing gyres of blood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LTTO-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Lord Falgravn mounted an invasion on [Kyne's Aegis](Lore_Kyne%27s_Aegis.md), an island bastion off the coast of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFFKA-3)</sup> The sea was rough for days prior to his arrival, and the island's defenders hadn't realized what they were up against until Falgravn's longships broke through the fog.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOOgvar-1)</sup> The vampire lord brought an army of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) and [sea giants](Lore_Sea_Giant.md) along with him, and they besieged the island for days.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-BSJ-4)</sup> Lord Falgravn's ultimate goal was to reach the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) ruins atop the island and perform a ritual within to empower himself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOOgvar-1)</sup> His forces razed the island, then Falgravn kidnapped a group of Nord soldiers and prepared the ritual. He was killed by the [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) before he could do further damage.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFFKA-3)</sup> An Amulet of Bats and the Bloodstone Chalice were in his possession at the time of his demise.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOBat Amulet-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOBlood Chalice-6)</sup>
