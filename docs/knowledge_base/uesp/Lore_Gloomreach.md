[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Gloomreach is a widemouthed cavern found between the slopes of the [Druadach Mountains](Lore_Druadach_Mountains.md) and the Karth River Canyon, in the southeastern part of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup> The deep cavern system<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> is known for its luminescent fungal ecosystem.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Rigurt-3)</sup>
