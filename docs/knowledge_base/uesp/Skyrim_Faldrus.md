[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-npc-Faldrus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Faldrus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Faldrus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Faldrus.jpg) Faldrus **Faldrus** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) who can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dark_Elf_on_Pilgrimage_to_Azura.27s_Shrine) wandering in the wilderness. He is on a pilgrimage to the [Shrine of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura) and may point you to it.

When he arrives at Azura's shrine, he will join [Aranea Ienith](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md) in front of the statue and raise his arms in praise. However, he will no longer provide further dialogue options.

## Related Quests
- **[Visit the Shrine of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Visit_the_Shrine_of_Azura)**: Help find and repair [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md).

## Dialogue
When near him, he will greet you with:

- *"I'm on a pilgrimage to the Shrine of Azura, the Goddess of Twilight. If you'll excuse me."*
- **Where is this shrine?**
- *"In the mountains to the northeast. I'll mark it on your map."*

## Notes
- Even when he is at the [shrine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura), he will give the same dialogue responses as he does when originally met and has no further dialogue.
- Faldrus does not have unique facial data. Instead, he uses the default male Dark Elf face, albeit with different hair.

## Bugs
- The location for the [Shrine of Azura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura) may not get marked on your map, despite Faldrus saying that he will do so. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
