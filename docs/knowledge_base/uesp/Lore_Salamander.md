# Salamander

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/ON-creature-Salamander.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Salamander.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Salamander.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Salamander.jpg) A large salamander (ESO) Salamanders are amphibians often found in tropical or moist regions such as the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), and [Southern Elsweyr](Lore_Southern_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOPSal-1)</sup> They can vary in size from very small to extremely large.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Castles Rulings-3)</sup> The largest salamander ever recorded, Graveld, was put on public display at the [Royal Menagerie](Lore_Sil-Var-Woad.md), but later escaped to the wilderness and was ordered to be killed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Just Farewell-4)</sup> All the large Salamanders of southern Tamriel are known for having elemental powers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLava Line-5)</sup>

Large salamanders are capable of being very dangerous, with them attacking prey using their long tongues and tails.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSal Ability-6)</sup> They also defend themselves with their intense elemental magic.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOSal Ability-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLava Line-5)</sup> Some salamanders have a knack for one particular brand of elemental magic. It's not too unusual to find a salamander that is talented primarily in producing natural shock, flame or frost magic instead of utilizing all three in equal measure.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOFSS-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOSSS-8)</sup> The toxin skin salamander is a breed that relies on venom instead of magic.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOTSS-9)</sup> Scholars studying arcane husbandry have learned of [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) rituals that grant luminescent qualities to common salamanders.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOIGS-10)</sup>

[Newts](Lore_Newt.md) are a comparable species of amphibian.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOPSal-1)</sup> The [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) Salamanders was the name of a gladiatorial team that fought in the Dragon Games.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-CGG-11)</sup>
