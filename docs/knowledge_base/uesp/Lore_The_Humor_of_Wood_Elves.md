The Humor of Wood Elves **Collected by [Telenger the Artificer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Telenger_the_Artificer)** *A collection of Bosmer jokes*
---
[Vulkwasten](Lore_Vulkwasten.md), widely known for its fermented beverages, is home to some of the friendliest [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) [I](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md)'ve ever met. Like most of their kind, they are industrious and get along well with most people. After completing research into their brewing methods, I stayed overnight with a local family.

As a historian, I realize one can learn much about a culture by studying its humor. Therefore, I copied down several of their witticisms for future examination. Perhaps by considering their amusements, we will gain further insight into the mind of the Bosmer.

A [skeleton](Lore_Skeleton.md) walks into the tavern and says, "I'd like some [rotmeth](Lore_Rotmeth.md). And a mop."

Q: Why did the [monkey](Lore_Monkey.md) fall out of the tree?
A: It was dead.

Q. What's brown and sticky?
A. A stick.

Person One: Ask me if I'm a tree.
Person Two: Are you a tree?
Person One: No.

Q. Which side of a [raven](Lore_Raven.md) has the most feathers?
A. The outside.

Q. What has three heads, is ugly, and smells?
A. My mistake! You don't have three heads!

Q. What's light as a feather, but cannot be held for very long?
A. Breath.

Q. Imagine your boat's sinking in [slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) -infested waters. How do you survive?
A. Stop imagining!

Q. Why do [thunderbugs](Lore_Thunderbug.md) eat raw meat?
A. They never learned to cook.

Q. Why do [bees](Lore_Bee.md) buzz?
A. Because they can't whistle.
