# Amber

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-item-Amber.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amber.jpg) A Piece of Amber [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-item-Refined_Amber.jpg/200px-SR-item-Refined_Amber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Refined_Amber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Refined_Amber.jpg) A refined amber ingot [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-container-Hollowed_Stump.jpg/200px-SR-container-Hollowed_Stump.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-container-Hollowed_Stump.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-container-Hollowed_Stump.jpg) A Hollowed Stump **Amber** is a glimmering orange material from the Shivering Isles. It was added to the game by the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). They can be found within [hollowed stumps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hollow_Stump) in the Root Tunnels under [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md), and in the inventory of [Khajiit caravan traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) after receiving [Ri'saad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ri%27saad)'s [note on his new wares](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_New_Wares). It is used to create high-quality [weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md) and light [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md).

- Varying amounts of Amber are obtained by searching Hollowed Stumps.
- 1 Refined Amber is obtained by [smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) 2 Amber.
- Amber Smithing requires the Glass Smithing perk and therefore requires a 70 Smithing skill.
- Amber Armor is made from Refined Amber, [Refined Moonstone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Refined_Moonstone), and [Leather Strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips).
- Amber weapons are level 27 weapons, made from Refined Amber, Refined Moonstone, Leather Strips, and [Firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md).

## Amber Smithing
The secrets to [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) Amber equipment is learned by reading [Evethra's Journal](Skyrim_Evethra%27s_Journal.md), obtained from a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Amber_and_Madness_Smithing.md) which is granted upon reading the [Note on Amber and Madness Ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore). Smithing the armor also requires learning Glass Smithing, the third perk in the "light armor" branch of the smithing skill's perk tree. Due to this, crafting Amber armor and weapons essentially requires a Smithing skill of 70, and the [Advanced Armors](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors.md) perk must have already been unlocked.
