- *A 100 point fiery explosion centered on the caster. Does more damage to closer targets.*

**Fire Storm** is a master level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals massive [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) to all targets within 100 feet of the caster.

## Effects
- [Fire Storm 100](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 100 pts in 100 ft - Sets targets on fire, starting at 30% damage and scaling down quadratically over 4 seconds.
- [Fire Storm 65](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 75 pts in 65 ft
- [Fire Storm 25](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 50 pts in 25 ft
- [It Burns Hysteria](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 7 secs in 100 ft; if the [Intense Flames](Skyrim_Intense_Flames.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md), raises damage by 25% at first rank or 50% at second rank. The damage values are compounded the closer your targets are, so the damage break down is as follows:

| Distance | default | Rank 1 | Rank 2 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ≤ 25 ft | 225 | 281 | 337 |
| ≤ 65 ft | 175 | 218 | 262 |
| ≤ 100 ft | 100 | 125 | 150 |

## Notes
- This is by far the most expensive spell to cast in terms of magicka, limiting its practical uses. However, if you can obtain 100% [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), this downside will be eliminated.
- Firestorm also hits airborne targets, such as Dragons, though they have to be hovering within 100 feet (the furthest the spell can reach) in order to have any effect.
- This spell will hit everything around you, including allies, so you should only use it if you're traveling alone or with followers who are marked [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md).

- - If you have [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) installed, you can embark on the quest [The Winds of Change](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Winds_of_Change_(quest).md); one of the three rewards is the ability to not damage your follower while in combat (though your follower can still die by other means, such as stray arrows or explosions caused by other enemies while in bleedout), making the Fire Storm 100% follower-friendly. <sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Fire_Storm#Companion.27s_Insight)*]</sup>
