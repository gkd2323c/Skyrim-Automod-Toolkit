[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/AR-place-Darkarn_Place.jpg/200px-AR-place-Darkarn_Place.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Darkarn_Place.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Darkarn_Place.jpg) Darkarn Place (Arena) The Darkarn River is a body of water found in the north-central parts of the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). The stream is found most far north in [The Rim](Lore_The_Rim.md) near the Shadow Dance Temple,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Co JC3-1)</sup>. The river has its name loaned to the village of **Darkarn Place**, which is found near the city-state of [Alabaster](Lore_Alabaster.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Darkarn Place was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Akh'soud, and it had a rivalry with [Valley Guard](Lore_Valley_Guard.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including Alabaster to the southeast, River Keep to the northwest, and Valley Guard to the north.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The Darkarn River shares a very similar name to the Dakarn Clan of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) brigands that waged war with the [Vinedusk Rangers](Lore_Vinedusk_Rangers.md) of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), across the provincial border in [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). They were active in the late years of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md). Remnants of this clan became the Duneguard Outwalkers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ALP-4)</sup>
