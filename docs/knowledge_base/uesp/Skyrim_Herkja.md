[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Herkja.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Herkja.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Herkja.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Herkja.jpg) Herkja [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-quest-Dragonborn_12.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Dragonborn_12.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_12.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_12.jpg) Herkja working on the Temple of Miraak **Herkja** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who can be found at [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md) after completing the quest [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md). Prior to this, she can be found working on the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md), alongside her companion, [Sirkjorg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sirkjorg), or at [Bujold's Retreat](Skyrim_Bujold%27s_Retreat.md) if the temple is cleansed.

Herkja wears [banded iron armor](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Armor.md) with iron [boots](Skyrim_Iron_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md). She wields a [Nordic mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic) in combat.

## Dialogue
When spoken to at the Mead Hall, she will impatiently say:

- *"We just keep waiting for [Bujold](Skyrim_Bujold_the_Unworthy.md) to say the word..."*
