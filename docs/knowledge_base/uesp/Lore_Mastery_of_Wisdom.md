Mastery of Wisdom **by [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Frandar_Hunding)** *Frandar Hunding's insights on achieving mastery*
---
In the season of life in which I was exposed to the [Hearth's Fire](Lore_Hearthfire.md), into deep contemplation did I withdraw to ponder the nature of truth. I sharpened my mind as my blade and defined the principles I would honor for all my future days.

With [Tava](Lore_Tava.md)'s blessing, the [sword-singer](Lore_Sword-singer.md) must do the same to achieve Mastery of Wisdom.
