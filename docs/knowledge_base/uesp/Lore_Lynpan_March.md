## History
The [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) in [1E 369](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_369) occurred when King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) attempted to conquer Valenwood in the name of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md). Many of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) that partook in the hunt continued to live on by the late- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). One such example was King [Dead Wolf-Deer](Lore_Dead_Wolf-Deer.md) stalked the Lympan March for an unknown amount of time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup> According to [Amalien](Lore_Amalien.md), scholar of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md), Dead Wolf-Deer was an amalgamation of different creatures, such as a [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md) and a [crocodile](Lore_Crocodile.md), and that his likeness may be inscribed on the [Ring of the Wild Hunt](Lore_Ring_of_the_Wild_Hunt.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONHunt Anti-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Lynpar March was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Elisnor and had a rivalry with [Moonmont](Lore_Moonmont.md). The only temple in the village was the Conclave of Charity.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Eldenroot](Lore_Eldenroot.md) to the west, [Karthdar Square](Lore_Karthdar_Square.md) to the northwest, and [Wasten Brukbrook](Lore_Wasten_Brukbrook.md) to the far south.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The Lynpan March is mentioned in the [Pocket Guide to the Empire, Second Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_Second_Edition#Better_is_a_Better_Word:_The_Lynpan_March). It was flooded away per Empress [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md)'s orders after she and her entourage were confronted by King Dead Wolf-Deer. The entire march was apparently wiped away to whereabouts unknown and the Empress' personal favorite [falcon](Lore_Falcon.md), Crow was sacrificed.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2E-5)</sup>
