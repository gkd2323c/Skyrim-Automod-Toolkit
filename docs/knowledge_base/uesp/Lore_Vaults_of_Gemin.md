[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/AR-map-Black_Marsh_%28annotated%29.jpg/200px-AR-map-Black_Marsh_%28annotated%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Black_Marsh_(annotated).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Black_Marsh_(annotated).jpg) Black Marsh The Vaults of Gemin are a ruined structure located in the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) city-state of [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMurkwood-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CS-2)</sup><sup>:34</sup>

The vaults once belonged to the [Conclave of Baal](Lore_Conclave_of_Baal.md), an organization based out of Stormhold. They were used to store an ancient tablet which could be used to decipher the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) in order to divine the current location of [Murkwood](Lore_Murkwood.md). However, the vaults sank into the ground when an associate of the organization cast a powerful spell with disastrous results. The crumbling ruins of the vault became partially flooded and infested with monsters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Idolan Lancaster-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Hero.md) was sent into the ruins by the Conclave to recover the ancient tablet.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Notes
- For game-specific information, see the [*Arena* article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Vaults_of_Gemin).
- In the game files of *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*, there is a concept map titled "Vaults of Gemin" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/eso/?world=vog), with Daedric writing in the bottom right corner reading "JEREMY RAYMOND" (a content designer from Zenimax Online Studios). But this dungeon was cut from the final game.
- The Conclave of Baal has not been mentioned since *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)*, but the Daedra [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) is [based off](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Real-World_References) the real-world demon of the same name.
