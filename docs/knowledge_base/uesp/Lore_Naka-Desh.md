# Naka-Desh

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Blackwood_Borderlands.jpg) Blackwood, the home of the Naka-Desh The Naka-Desh (or **Riverbacks**) are a breed and tribe of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) native to [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). Naka-Desh are morphologically unique from other Argonians, described as having "wide faces, large eyes, and broad webs adorning their forearms and throats". Outsiders have perceived them as secretive and mysterious, but this was a misconception caused by the Naka-Desh seeing little benefit in traveling beyond the boundaries of their [Hist](Lore_Hist.md)'s roots. These Argonians are not known as curious, and don't care for cultural exchange, with the exception of riddles. They are known to use [osheeja gars](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Osheeja_Gar) to catch other [fish](Lore_Fish.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOBR-1)</sup> They were close friends to [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) and were known to appreciate their [music](Lore_Music.md) skills.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COS-2)</sup>
