Habd's Journal **by [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md)** *A journal detailing Habd's purchase of the Frostflow Lighthouse and the events leading up the massacre of his family by Falmer*
---
After years of sailing from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) to [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) and back, [Ramati](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ramati) and I have finally saved enough money to buy that [old light house](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md) we spotted on the coast and we are moving in next month.

----

These [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) really know how to live. It may be cold, but you can't beat the tranquility of the watching icebergs to live out your silver years. Ramati loves the place too, despite the occasional leaky ceiling. [Mani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mani) and [Sudi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sudi), unfortunately, seem miserable here; I know the kids are old enough to strike out on their own, but I hope they'll grow to love the peace and quiet here as I have.

----

Mani and Sudi continue to bicker and harass each other and they seem to have started in on their mother as well. Ramati keeps finding things missing from the cellar. Sudi said she heard a noise down there but Mani just thinks their mother is going mad from isolation. The very thought of it is ridiculous, she's always wanted to retire here!

----

Sudi keeps saying that she hears scratching coming from the cellar at night. I think she's just trying to get her mother's goat, but I'd better head into town to get a few traps just in case it's [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md). Should only take a couple days. I just hope those two go easy on Ramati while I'm gone.

----

Oh gods. Oh gods why?

I returned home to find my beautiful Ramati killed and this thing, this horrible [bug-like creature](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) ransacking our home. I managed to kill the damned thing, I think it might be a [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md)! I've never seen anything like it! Oh gods poor Ramati.

It must have come out of the cellar. I'm going down there and locking the door behind me to make sure they don't escape. I don't know if I'll make it out, but I have to save my family!

-- [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md)
