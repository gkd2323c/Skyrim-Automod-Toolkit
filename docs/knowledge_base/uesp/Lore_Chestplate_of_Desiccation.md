---
Interview of Wuja-Nakal, Former Slave
Transcribed by Relicmaster Glenadir

Yes, our [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) jailers would often use this as a punishment. For one of my kind, there are few greater displeasures than the feeling of dryness. We are used to heat of course, but the humid heat of the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) swamps. Not this.

They had more mundane means to dry out a [Saxhleel](Lore_Argonian.md). They would lock us in cages, allow the summer's sun to do their work. But during the rainy days, or as the fog rolled across the islands, they would force us to wear this. Bind our hands so we couldn't remove it. Wait for the chestplate to take effect.

The feeling was slow, but after the first time. The dread was choking, knowing what was to come. It would begin as a slight discomfort, almost just an itch. The tips of your scales would feel it first, but slowly it would sink down to your center. Your tongue would feel shriveled in your mouth. You would close your eyes, but nothing could soothe them. Your hide would slowly tighten, inch by inch.

They always made sure you didn't die, no matter how much you craved it.
