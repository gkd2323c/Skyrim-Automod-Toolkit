[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-menu-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Staff_of_Hasedoki.jpg) Staff of Hasedoki **Staff of Hasedoki** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that adds the eponymous [Staff of Hasedoki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Hasedoki), an artifact from *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*. It was released on October 4, 2018 for 250 Creation Credits.

The quest added by this Creation was made by [Kris Takahashi](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/1102501).

## Official Summary
*It's said the wizard Hasedoki traveled all of Tamriel in search of his equal, before binding his soul to a staff. Through the years this mysterious weapon has changed many hands, as if warding off anyone who isn't worthy enough to claim it. Now, it's your turn. Quest created by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "The Staff of Hasedoki" starts by reading the Smuggler's Trade Notes at the bandit camp west of Dragonsreach.)*

## Notes
- Modig is a reference to, and an apparent descendant of, [Koffutto Gilgar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Koffutto_Gilgar). Koffuto was another Orc necromancer with a similarly strange name who was originally encountered in *Morrowind*, he was also the one in possession of the Staff of Hasedoki.
- Necromancer Ivara apparently hails from [Olenveld](Lore_Olenveld.md), a location off the coast of Skyrim, only ever mentioned once before in *[The Exodus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Exodus)*.
- Two of the books added by this Creation, *Arkay the Enemy* and *The Light and the Dark*, previously appeared in *Morrowind* and *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* respectively.
