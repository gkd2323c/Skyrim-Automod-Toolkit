## Ingredients
47-49<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 33-34<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 4 frost atronachs can be found in and around the Chill (Winterhold's prison). It is far northeast of Winterhold. Can also be found by committing a crime in Winterhold.
- 3 in [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 3 in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Calixto's House of Curiosities](Skyrim_Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Knifepoint Mine](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Mine.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 2 in the [Palace of the Kings Upstairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 2 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Can be created at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) from: 1 [Sapphire](Skyrim_Sapphire_(item).md) + 1 [Soul Gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) + 1 [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile)

## Creatures
Frost salts are dropped by both [frost atronachs](Skyrim_Frost_Atronach.md) and [frost thralls](Skyrim_Frost_Thrall_(creature).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Dravynea's Frost Salts](Skyrim_Dravynea%27s_Frost_Salts.md)**: Find some frost salts for [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](Skyrim_Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver.md). - 1 sample is needed for this quest.
- **[Salt for Arcadia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_for_Arcadia)**: [Farengar](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md) asks you to deliver some frost salts to [Arcadia](Skyrim_Arcadia.md). - During this quest, you are asked to deliver Farengar's frost salts. They are the same item with a different name.

## Notes
- Frost salts are needed at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) to create various frost atronach-related items, namely: a hostile [frost atronach](Skyrim_Frost_Atronach.md), a [Scroll of Conjure Frost Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Frost_Atronach), a [Staff of the Frost Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_the_Frost_Atronach), or a *[Spell Tome: Conjure Frost Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Frost_Atronach)*.
- Once certain schematics are discovered during the [Ancient Technology](Skyrim_Ancient_Technology.md) quest, one pinch of frost salts is required for crafting ten [Exploding Steel Bolts of Ice](Skyrim_Exploding_Steel_Bolt_of_Ice.md) or ten [Exploding Dwarven Bolts of Ice](Skyrim_Exploding_Dwarven_Bolt_of_Ice.md) at [Gunmar's Forge](Skyrim_Gunmar%27s_Forge.md).<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup>
