[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-npc-Ulundil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulundil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulundil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulundil.jpg) Ulundil **Ulundil** is an exceptionally cheerful [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) who runs the [Windhelm Stables](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables.md) outside of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He will sell you a bay [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) with white markings for 1000 gold. His wife, [Arivanya](Skyrim_Arivanya.md), takes over selling the stables' horses if Ulundil dies.

He wakes up at 6am and has a two-hour breakfast with Arivanya, then at 8am heads outside to work at the stables. His workday ends at 8pm, and where he goes to depends on the [day](Lore_Calendar.md#Days_of_the_Week): on Tirdas and Turdas, he dines at [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md), staying at the place for around three hours before he goes home to sleep. On other days, he goes straight to bed.

He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5) and carries on him a [Windhelm Stables key](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables_Key.md) and some [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common). In combat, he relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"Oh, pardon me. Was I in your way?"*
- *"I hope someday we'll get to know each other better."*
- *"I see why the Nords like Skyrim. This crisp air is wonderful for the lungs!"*
- *"You seem like such a nice person. I'd love to hear more about you."*

Speaking to him:

- **Aren't you worried about the dragons?**
- *"My pa used to say there ain't no use worryin' about the things you can't change. If it's my fate to be ended by a dragon, so be it. Meantime, I'll live my life the way I want to."*

- **Can I buy a horse?**
- *"It's for sale, if you've got the coin."*

- - **Here's my offer... (1000 gold)**
- *"That's your best offer? I suppose so. She's the one with the saddle."*

- - **On second thought, I'll walk.**
- *"Didn't think you had the coin for it anyway."*

If you have already bought a horse from him:

- **Can I buy a horse?**
- *"Not right now. You bought my last one."*

His goodbyes:

- *"Sometimes the difference between life an' death is a swift steed."*
- *"Sorry about the smell. It's a stable, after all."* **(If he is inside the Windhelm Stables)**
