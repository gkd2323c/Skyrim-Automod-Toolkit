# Witchmothers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-quest-The_Witchmother%27s_Bargain.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Witchmother%27s_Bargain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Witchmother%27s_Bargain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Witchmother%27s_Bargain.jpg) A Witchmother stirring a cauldron in her brewery The Witchmother coven is the name for a loose coven of powerful [witches](Lore_Witches.md) known as **Witchmothers**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vanima-1)</sup> who venerate [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) and carry out her bidding. The number of coven members is unknowable and ever-changing,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Taerma-2)</sup> and they have been known to take on new members from all over [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vanima-1)</sup> The people of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) believed the coven physically gathered in the southern forests of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) during the [Witches Festival](Lore_Witches_Festival.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-3)</sup> They congregated inside Olyve's Brewery, which could also be accessed elsewhere through strange doors found in multiple locations, such as in northern Glenumbra, [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), and [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The Witchmothers provide a protective role to the people of Tamriel. They act as [seers](Lore_Sight.md), scrying for disturbances in the [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md) that could threaten [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). They play a particularly important role during the Witches Festival by serving as caretakers in the time of Harvest—their name for the ritualized killing of the most vile creatures on Tamriel that takes place during the Witches Festival. They also monitor dangers that emerge following [Nocturnal's Summoning Day](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nocturnal%27s_Summoning_Day) on the 3rd of Hearth Fire each year.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Taerma-2)</sup>

The coven specializes in brewing foul concoctions in large cauldrons. They use these to scry<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Taerma-2)</sup> or to enhance the power of their reapers, supplicants who come to them during the Harvest and are transformed into living [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md) to carry out the coven's will.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Olyve-5)</sup> Witchmothers are also associated with [crows](Lore_Crow.md) and can materialize from a murder of crows when summoned with a crow caller during the Witches Festival.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Servants of the Witchmothers are known to utilize arms and armor bearing stylized [pumpkin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pumpkin) imagery.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

They welcomed supplicants, offering them the opportunity to study and potentially join the coven's ranks. They also permitted individuals from outside the coven to serve them, performing various mundane tasks. These outsiders aspired to earn the coven's favor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vanima-1)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Alamorto-8)</sup>

## History
During the Witches Festival celebrations, an enigmatic and elusive merchant known as [the Impresario](Lore_The_Impresario.md) partnered with the Witchmothers. She held Witchmother Olvye in high regard, praising her as a generous and remarkable individual.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TI-9)</sup> Witchmother Olyve mixed pumpkins, guts, and the essence of death itself within her cauldron, and lured adventurers to take a drink from them, promising them that they will gain power.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WFEWO-10)</sup> If she didn't gather the required ingredients needed to make the brew, she would return to the forests south of Camlorn and consume the drink known Witchmother’s Party Punch until she is able to try again the next year. While she was active during the [interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WFEWO-10)</sup> she had not been seen for a while as of [4E 197](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_197).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-3)</sup>

Witchmother Taerma made brief appearances at the Impressario's camp, seeking assistance from adventurers. Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Witchmother Taerma scried the approach of the [Crowborne Horror](Lore_Crowborne_Horror.md), a powerful [Wraith-of-Crows](Lore_Wraith-of-Crows.md) that planned to attack Nirn. With the help of [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Taerma repeatedly lured the Horror to a pocket realm deep in the Evergloam known as the [Garden of Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Garden_of_Shadows), drawing it further away from Nirn and draining some of its power for the coven's own purposes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Taerma-2)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
