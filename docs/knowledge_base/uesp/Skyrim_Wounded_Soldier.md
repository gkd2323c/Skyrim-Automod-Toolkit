[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-npc-Wounded_Soldier.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wounded_Soldier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wounded_Soldier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wounded_Soldier.jpg) Wounded Soldier The **Wounded Soldier** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) and [Whiterun guard](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md) committed to the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

Heavily wounded, he will do absolutely nothing but lie on one of the healing altars being nursed by either [Danica Pure-Spring](Skyrim_Danica_Pure-Spring.md) or [Acolyte Jenssen](Skyrim_Acolyte_Jenssen.md).

He wears [Whiterun guard's armor](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard%27s_Armor.md) with a pair of [fur boots](Skyrim_Fur_Boots.md) and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
He cannot be properly spoken to, only at times groaning:

- *"The pain is so great..."*
- *"Unghhh... oww..."*
- *"I can't bear it... make it stop..."*

## Notes
- Unlike other Whiterun guards, the Wounded Soldier isn't replaced by a [Stormcloak Soldier](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Soldier.md) should Whiterun be captured by the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) during the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md), nor is his yellow Whiterun guard's armor replaced with a Stormcloak uniform.
