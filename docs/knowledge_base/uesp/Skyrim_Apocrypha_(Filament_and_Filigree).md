[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-quest-Black_Book_Filament_and_Filigree.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Black_Book_Filament_and_Filigree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Black_Book_Filament_and_Filigree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Black_Book_Filament_and_Filigree.jpg) The Filament and Filigree Black Book in Kolbjorn Barrow **Filament and Filigree** is an area of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s [plane of Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Oblivion), [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md). There is only one zone in the area, *[Filament and Filigree](#Filament_and_Filigree)*.

The [Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book__Filament_and_Filigree_(book).md) needed to access Filament and Filigree is found inside [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md). You must complete the quest [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md) in order to retrieve the book.

The realm is inhabited by Daedra called [Seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md) and [Lurkers](Skyrim_Lurker.md). Tentacles constantly breach the acidic dark waters and sometimes whip at you, causing a significant amount of damage if they manage to hit you. The acidic waters with the tentacles are also corrosive and cause damage if stepped in.

## Filament and Filigree
Filament and Filigree opens on a dim platform facing a dark corridor. The [darkness](Skyrim_Traps.md#Darkness) here is dangerous, literally; entering it will inflict continuous heavy damage which can not be resisted by any means, including the [Become Ethereal](Skyrim_Become_Ethereal.md) shout. Floating [Wisp-Lanterns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wisp-Lantern) drift above the platforms, providing safe havens of light to walk in. Other forms of light will also work to prevent damage (e.g. torches, [Candlelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Candlelight), [Magelight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Magelight)), so long as they keep away the darkness.

Ahead and to the right is a table and a font of magicka. On the table are two random soul gems and a copy of the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Three Thieves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Three_Thieves)*. Hidden behind the left statue at the bottom of the stairs to the left is a pod. At the top of the stairs is a closed gate. The scrye to open the gate is to the right, and to the left is a pod and a [scroll of invisibility](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Invisibility). An eye floats along to the right of the long platform ahead, but does not provide full protection from the darkness. Along the platform are two seekers and a lurker, and a lurker rises from the pool at the top of the stairs at the other end. At the top of stairs at the end of the platform is the black book *Filament and Filigree*, a vessel to the left, and a table to the right with copies of the [Spell Tome: Healing Hands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Healing_Hands) and the [Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Seeker) (only if your [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill is at least level 40), a [scroll of mass paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis), and three soul gems (one [black](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md) and two random). To exit this part of Apocrypha, use the non-circular part of the black book.

The three [Powers](Skyrim_Powers.md) offered by this book are:

- [Secret of Arcana](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Arcana) — Spells cost no magicka for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Protection](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Protection) — You take half damage for 30 seconds.
- [Secret of Strength](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Secret_of_Strength) — Power attacks cost no stamina for 30 seconds.
