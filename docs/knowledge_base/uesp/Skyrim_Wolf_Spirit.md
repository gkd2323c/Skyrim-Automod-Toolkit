[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/SR-creature-Wolf_Spirit.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Wolf_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf_Spirit.jpg) Wolf Spirit A **Wolf Spirit** is a creature connected to [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md). The Wolf Spirit is presumed to be the source of the power and the curse that werewolves bear. Curing lycanthropy requires throwing the head of a [Glenmoril Witch](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch.md) into the Flame of the Harbinger in [Ysgramor's Tomb](Skyrim_Ysgramor%27s_Tomb.md) in the presence of the werewolf to be cured. Doing this releases the Wolf Spirit from the werewolf's soul, which must be defeated in order to cleanse the person.
