# Norvayn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/MW-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-MW-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Norvayn Ancestral Tomb [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/ON-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg/200px-ON-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Norvayn_Ancestral_Tomb.jpg) Norvayn Ancestral Tomb The Norvayn clan is an ancient family of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) that lives in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). They are one of the thirty revered families of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ATOTTRF-1)</sup> and are a sub-faction of [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONNorvayn-2)</sup> The **Norvayn Ancestral Tomb** is located in the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md) region, northwest of [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) and northeast of Hlormaren Stronghold.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONNorvayn-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWNorvayn-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/MW-npc-Elynea_Norvayn.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Elynea_Norvayn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Elynea_Norvayn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Elynea_Norvayn.jpg) Elynea Norvayn With the other twenty-nine ancient families of Vvardenfell, the Norvayn clan hid the [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md), the secret repository of the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) and placed a clue in their ancestral tomb.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONBradyn-4)</sup> Its location remained a secret for many years until Bradyn of the Library of Vivec enlisted an [outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to find the tombs and uncover it in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They were able to find all thirty tombs and find the lost Library of Andule.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONAncestral-5)</sup> In the same year Benae Lleran was a laborer in [Velyn Harbor](Lore_Velyn_Harbor.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BL-6)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Elynea Norvayn was a chief of security of the [Caldera Mine](Lore_Caldera_Mine.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EN-7)</sup> Adil Norvayn was a bandit who occupied cavern of Kumarahaz.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AN-8)</sup> As of then the Norvayn Ancestral Tomb was guarded by the [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWNorvayn-3)</sup>

## Notes
- The clan shares its name with [Norvayn Bay](Lore_Norvayn_Bay.md), where [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) was constructed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Elone-9)</sup>
