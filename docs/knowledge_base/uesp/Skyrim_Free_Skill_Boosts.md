## Combat
All - +1 to all Combat skills for completing the quest [Rjorn's Drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rjorn%27s_Drum) for [Giraud Gemane](Skyrim_Giraud_Gemane.md).
- +5 to all Combat skills by selecting "The Path of Might" from the [Oghma Infinium](Skyrim_Oghma_Infinium.md) after completing the quest [Discerning the Transmundane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Discerning_the_Transmundane).<sup>[‡](#intnote_Oghma)</sup>

- +1 to Archery for completing the quest [Filial Bonds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Filial_Bonds) for [Wulf Wild-Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood).<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

Block - +1 to Block for completing the quest [Dungeon Delving (Caves)](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Caves).md) for [Roggi Knot-Beard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roggi_Knot-Beard).<sup>[†](#intnote_Radiant)</sup>

- +1 to Block (and +1 One-handed) for completing the quest [Dungeon Delving (Bandits)](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits).md) for [Amren](Skyrim_Amren.md).<sup>[†](#intnote_Radiant)</sup>
- +1 to Block (and +1 One-handed) for completing the quest [The Ghost of Old Hroldan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan).
- +1 to Block for completing the quest [Filial Bonds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Filial_Bonds) for [Wulf Wild-Blood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood).<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

Heavy Armor - +1 to Heavy Armor for completing the quest [Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md) for [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md).<sup>[†](#intnote_Radiant)</sup>

## Notes
[†](#note_Radiant) Only one radiant quest of a similar type can be assigned at any given time. You must clear the radiant quest from your miscellaneous quests in order to be eligible to receive another of the same type. [‡](#note_Oghma) When reading the Oghma Infinium, you may only choose one set of skill boosts: combat, magic, or stealth. For the other two skill groups, the bonus becomes unavailable.

## Free Training
[Trainers](Skyrim_Trainers.md) who are also [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) can train you for "free".<sup>[¥](#intnote_USKP)</sup> They will still charge you, but leave profit from training in their inventory. Simply request to see their inventory while they are an active follower, and take your gold back from any training sessions. This can be done with the following NPCs:

- [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) members - [Aela the Huntress](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md) (Archery - up to 75)
- [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md) (One-handed - up to 75)
- [Farkas](Skyrim_Farkas.md) (Heavy Armor - up to 90)
- [Njada Stonearm](Skyrim_Njada_Stonearm.md) (Block - up to 75)
- [Vilkas](Skyrim_Vilkas.md) (Two-handed - up to 90)

- Other NPCs - [Faendal](Skyrim_Faendal.md) (Archery - up to 50)
- [Talvas Fathryon](Skyrim_Talvas_Fathryon.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> (Conjuration - up to 90)

[¥](#note_USKP) This is no longer possible as of version 1.2.6 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch).
