Prayer to Hircine *A prayer to the Daedric Lord of the Hunt*
---
I lift my weapons to [you](Lore_Hircine.md)!
Bless them, and guide them true—

A bow!
A sword!
A dagger!
A claw!

Let the sport begin!
Let the blood spill!
Hunter and hunted,
In a chase to the death!
