[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/OB-place-Fort_Farragut.jpg/200px-OB-place-Fort_Farragut.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Farragut.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Fort_Farragut.jpg) Fort Farragut circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/ON-place-Farragut_Keep.jpg/200px-ON-place-Farragut_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Farragut_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Farragut_Keep.jpg) Farragut Keep circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Fort Farragut (also known as **Farragut Keep**) is a ruined military fort located on the foothills of the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) northeast of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup>

During the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Farragut was an expansive and strategically important keep. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was initially located within the territory held by the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), and its main function was the defense of the Elder Scroll of Ghartok, which was held in a temple to the northeast. The keep changed hands many times over the course of the war.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

The fort was abandoned by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) during the relatively peaceful [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), Fort Farragut was no more than a ruin. It had been taken as a lair by [Lucien Lachance](Lore_Lucien_Lachance.md), a Speaker for the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) who administered the nearby Cheydinhal Sanctuary. As such, it was infested with undead skeletal guardians in service to the Brotherhood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> Lachance was forced to flee the ruins in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) after he was framed as a traitor.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The fort was placed under watch by the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) at this time, in case Lachance attempted to return.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Notes
- Fort Farragut lends its name to Farragut's Mail, a legendary piece of chainmail armor. The mail was once worn by Ocheeva, a [Shadowscale](Lore_Shadowscale.md) who served as Matron of the Cheydinhal Sanctuary at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
