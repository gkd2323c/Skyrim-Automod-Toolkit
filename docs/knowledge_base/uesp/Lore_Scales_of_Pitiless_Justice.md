# Scales of Pitiless Justice

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/OBR-item-Scales_of_Pitiless_Justice.jpg/200px-OBR-item-Scales_of_Pitiless_Justice.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Scales_of_Pitiless_Justice.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Scales_of_Pitiless_Justice.jpg) Scales of Pitiless Justice (Oblivion Remastered) The Scales of Pitiless Justice are a legendary set of enchanted scales possessed by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOPJON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOPJOB-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Brotherhood seemingly used the scales to bless high ranking members. For those affected, bounties placed on their heads for murder and assault would fade far more rapidly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SOPJON-1)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the scales were possessed by Vicente Valtieri, a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) member of the Brotherhood's [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) sanctuary. They were offered as a bonus reward for a contract within the [Imperial Prison](Lore_Imperial_Prison.md), in return for sparing the lives of any guards encountered.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VV-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SFE-4)</sup> At the time, the scales were used to magically enhance the bearer's strength, intelligence and agility, but at the cost of their personality.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VV-3)</sup> It is unknown if the bonus was actually given.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SFE-4)</sup>
