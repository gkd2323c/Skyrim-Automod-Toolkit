A Request to Meet **by [Staada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staada)** *A request to make an exchange*
---
Thank you for your efforts to protect my [realm](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md).

I believe you possess something that I am in search of, and I require it before I return home. It is the artifact from the Shivering Isles that served as the focus of [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md)'s depraved rituals.

Meet me beneath the arch of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), upon the shore, so that we might make an exchange.

-- [S](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staada)
