Anomee-Ja, Moss-Weaver **by [Anomee-Ja](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Anomee-Ja)** *The crafting notes of an Argonian robe-weaver*
---
[![ON-concept-Argonian light armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/fd/ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Argonian_light_armor.png)
- "The best reeds and moss come from deep in the swamp, where most dryskins prefer not to tread. A soft lining of hanging moss gives comfort to my robes; the dried and treated reeds give a thick sturdiness to the soft leather in key areas which need support.

- The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) guide my hand as I weave the patterns. I envision soft moonlight over the deep swamps, the safest places for my kind. It is no small effort, but I know that each robe I weave will bring the comfort and protection of our home to my [egg-brothers](Lore_Argonian.md) and -sisters, no matter where they must go."
