# Useful Potions

| --- |

This article provides examples of useful potions you can create using the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) skill.

Only a few single-effect potions are listed here. Any single effect can be obtained by combining two ingredients with the same effect listed on the [Alchemy Effects](Skyrim_Alchemy_Effects.md) page. The information provided here assumes that you don't have the [Purity](Skyrim_Alchemy.md#Purity) perk unless specified.

### Expensive Potions
These are the ten most profitable potions that can be brewed from ingredients that are sold by apothecary merchants. The profit is calculated by subtracting the base price of the ingredients from the value of the resulting potion with 100 Alchemy and no perks. The profit should be considered a relative measure, not an absolute amount. Potions made with [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe), [Garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), and [Nordic Barnacle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle) are also very valuable, but the ingredients are not sold by merchants. Since the release of Rare Curios and its inclusion in Anniversary Edition, the most expensive potion that can be crafted is [Salmon Roe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Roe), [Garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), and [Fungus Stalk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fungus_Stalk).
