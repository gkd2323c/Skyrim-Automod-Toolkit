[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-npc-Hefid_the_Deaf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hefid_the_Deaf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hefid_the_Deaf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hefid_the_Deaf.jpg) Hefid the Deaf **Hefid the Deaf**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggar_(class).md), is part of the insane community found in the [Ratway Warrens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ratway_Warrens) underneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).

Hefid is locked in a dark room and will not emerge unless her attention is broken. This can be done either by behaving violently around her, or if [Knjakr](Skyrim_Knjakr.md) the Chef, using the grinding wheel up the stairs, notices you and attacks you. She will flee, running to other parts of the Ratway Warren.

She carries a plethora of random items, which she'll constantly obsess over: a [burned book](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Burned_Book), a [bucket](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bucket), a [flawless garnet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flawless_Garnet), an [inkwell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Inkwell), and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). In addition she wears [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) and carries a spare [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic).

## Dialogue
When she is not fleeing, you may hear her ramble:

*"Inkpot. Stone. Bucket. Book. Knife."* *"Bucket. Knife. Book. Inkpot. Stone. No, no, no."* *"Knife. Yes. Book. Yes. Bucket. Yes. Inkpot? No."* She may also maniacally shout:

*"Ha ha ha ha! No! Never find me!"* Attempting to speak with her will result in her yelling:

*"Go away. Go away. Go away!"* She will then turn hostile.

## Notes
- Hefid is the only person in-game of the beggar class, to wear the common beggar robes, to not actually *be* a beggar. She also uses the voicetype that all other female beggars do.
