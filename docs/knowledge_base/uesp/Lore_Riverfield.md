It is home to the [House of Reveries](Lore_House_of_Reveries.md), a famed acting troupe.

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price.jpg/200px-ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price.jpg) Rellenthil Sinkhole Many re-purposed Aldmeri ruins dot the town, including its most famous building: the amphitheater where the [House of Reveries](Lore_House_of_Reveries.md) holds its auditions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> The House of Reveries was active in the settlement from its earliest days.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBIAG-4)</sup> By [2E 232](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_232), it was already renowned for its exquisite wine and the grand performances of the troupe.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RI-5)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a large sinkhole mysteriously opened up near the town. It was soon discovered that the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) K'Tora, in collaboration with the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md), had created the uncommon phenomena in the firm and unchanging foundations of Summerset Isle. A monk of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) by the name of [Valsirenn](Lore_Valsirenn.md), with help from the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), managed to stabilize the walls to prevent them from crumbling down and the sinkhole from growing deeper and wider.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-APOGP-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-RTCLS-7)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/AR-place-Riverfield.jpg/200px-AR-place-Riverfield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverfield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Riverfield.jpg) The town during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) At the same time, Abyssal Pearls were used by members of the Court of Bedlam to power an Abyssal Geyser on the coast near Rellenthil, which threatened to make the very land be swallowed by the ocean and summoned hordes of beasts from the deep sea.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup>

After the threat of the sinkhole was averted, Rellenthil's canonreeve's son—Nedoril—publicly acknowledged his implication in Daedra-worship, as well as conspiring with the newly founded Court of Bedlam and with the Sea Sloads. For this, he was executed by the [Eye of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md) [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md) without a trial, fearing that the canonreeve might oppose the Crown to protect his son. Canonreeve Farmeldo affirmed his allegiance to [Queen Ayrenn](Lore_Queen_Ayrenn.md) after the event.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

The town was still in existence during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), where it became known as Riverfeld.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-West Tamriel Map-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), it was known as Riverfield and was ruled by Duke Solimon. It had a rivalry with [Archen Grangrove](Lore_Archen_Grangrove.md), [Corgrad Wastes](Lore_Corgrad_Wastes.md), and Marnor Keep.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Ebon Stadmont](Lore_Ebon_Stadmont.md) in the northwest, Old Falls in the northeast, Riverwatch in the west, and [Rosefield](Lore_Rosefield.md) in the east.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Map Arena-10)</sup>

## Government
Rellenthil has been an integral part of the realm of the Summerset Isles since its foundation by the Alinor Monarchs. A canonreeve is tasked to rule the settlement.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/ON-prerelease-Rellenthil.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Rellenthil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Rellenthil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Rellenthil.jpg) The Amphitheater of Rellenthil The town is known for its acting troupe, the House of Reveries, founded by Grand Maestro [Ingress](Lore_Ingress.md). It consists of anonymous, masked artists who regularly make performances in the town's amphitheater. Those who join the House must hide their true identities, to prevent them from using their role for personal gain. The Manor of Masques is their base of operations, but its members travel all across Summerset to perform. Every traveling troupe is led by a Maestro. In contrast, a Grand Maestro acts as the leader of the entire House of Reveries.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBIAG-4)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Ho RT-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Ho RH-13)</sup> Most of the Rellenthil is affiliated with the House of Reveries in some way or another, even if they are not members of the troupe.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Eilayne-14)</sup>

The Maestros of this famed troupe have organized performances in Rellenthil's amphitheater since the earliest days of settlement. As heirs to the long [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) legacy of cultural arts, membership is exclusive: Only true talents may join, and they are selected by the Grand Maestro in Rellenthil.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBIAG-4)</sup> Nobles especially appreciate the performances of certain members of the House, who often entertain highly esteemed social circles in Summerset.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup>
