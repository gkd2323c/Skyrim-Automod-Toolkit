# Order of the Iron Knot

[![Order of the Iron Knot Banner](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-banner-Order_of_the_Iron_Knot.png/55px-ON-banner-Order_of_the_Iron_Knot.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Order_of_the_Iron_Knot.png) The Order of the Iron Knot was a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) active in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup> Their founder, Baroness [Belene Chatillon](Lore_Belene_Chatillon.md), sailed with the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) itself. According to war records, she fought the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) in-person and even lost an arm to Sload rot. In honor of her sacrifice and bravery, some members of the knightly order wore a black gauntlet on one hand. They were known for their martial skills, which they honed on the road and at tournaments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO-1)</sup>

The Sapphire Tournament, hosted by [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md) at Castle Navire,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> serves as a proving ground for squires seeking entry into the Systrean knightly orders. The Order of the Iron Knot is one of the three orders that take part, with the other two being the [Order of the Albatross](Lore_Order_of_the_Albatross.md) and the [Oaken Order](Lore_Oaken_Order.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONGST-3)</sup> Each sets its own distinctive trial. Participation earns favor, though each order selects only one or two aspirants for knighthood. Most contenders attempt a single challenge, but those who succeed in all three are granted a powerful boon.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Isobel-4)</sup>

The Order of the Iron Knot provided maps made out of raised paper to accommodate the splinter group of [Moth Priests](Lore_Moth_Priests.md) that fled [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) following the death of [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
