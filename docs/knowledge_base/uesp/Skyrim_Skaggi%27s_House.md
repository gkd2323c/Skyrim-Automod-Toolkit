## Walkthrough

### Exterior
It is a single story Dwemer style dwelling, with a novice-locked door. It is opposite the smelter outside of the mine. A [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) can be found wandering around outside.

### Skaggi's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Skaggi%27s_House.jpg) Skaggi's house interior The house consists of a single room with a large fireplace on the east wall with two [cooking pots](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and a third pot containing a [rabbit haunch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rabbit_Haunch) and [carrot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot). To the left of the fireplace, on the floor is a mead barrel and basket containing two sheaves of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), while to the right is a pile of firewood. Opposite the fireplace, coming out from the west wall is a large double bed with an end table to the right holding a bunch of [juniper berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries) and two bunches of [jazbay grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md), one in a wooden bowl. To the left of the bed, on a ledge is a copy of *[Killing - Before You're Killed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed)* and on the floor, in a bucket are copies of *[The Holds of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim)* and *[The City of Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_City_of_Stone)*. Against the south wall is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest), while against the north wall is square wooden table and two chairs, with a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) on the table.
