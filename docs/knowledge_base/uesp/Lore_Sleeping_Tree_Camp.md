The Sleeping Tree grows from a pool in the center of the camp. Sleeping Tree Cave is located inside the camp, northwest of the tree.

## History
The [Sleeping Tree](Lore_Sleeping_Tree.md) is a strange leafless tree found in [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) in *[Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)*. It grows in a misty pool beside a small cave now named after the tree. Several legends exist on the origins of the Tree. Some say that a piece of rock was blasted from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) during the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) and landed in Skyrim, and the Tree grew from the resulting crater. Others say that it grew from a spore which fell from a "floating island", referring to [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md), in which case the Tree is one of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) of Umbriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRYsolda-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup>

For an unknown amount of time, giants made the cave and the surrounding area of the tree their home.

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) merchant from [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) named Ysolda hired an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) named Ulag to visit the camp regularly and harvest the tree's [sap](Lore_Sleeping_Tree_Sap.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-YM-3)</sup> Ulag was killed by the giants, but an [adventurer](Lore_Hero.md) came across his corpse in the cave and became Ysolda's supplier of the drug, who then sold it on to the Khajiit traders.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skyrim-4)</sup>
