[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Dawnguard_Scout.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dawnguard_Scout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Scout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Scout.jpg) A Dawnguard Scout **Dawnguard Scouts** are [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) of any [race](Skyrim_Races.md) that sometimes appear at the fast travel arrival point just outside the [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) [keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). They arrive in groups of three (one melee and two archers) and will attack immediately if you are allied to the [Volkihar vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) and are a [vampire lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md). However, they don't appear at all if you are a member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

Dawnguard scouts each carry a [war axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md) and [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md). They may also have a small amount of gold on them. They wear only [Dawnguard armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Dawnguard_Armor): light, heavy, or a mix of the two. Sometimes they do not have the helmet or the gauntlets.

## Notes
- If you attack them before they become hostile, you'll get a bounty in Whiterun, despite Castle Volkihar being in Haafingar. To avoid a bounty, wait until they become hostile, then attack.
- After completing quest [Destroying the Dawnguard](Skyrim_Destroying_the_Dawnguard.md) on Vampire side, they disappear permanently from game, after [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) lost the war against to [Volkihar Vampire Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md).
