# Greywacke

Greywacke is a kind of stone. A number of greywacke stones depicting one of the three guardian [constellations](Lore_Constellations.md): [the Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md), [the Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md), and [the Mage](Lore_The_Mage.md) were found around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). Those idols made of greywacke were known as the Guardian Stones.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GS-1)</sup>
