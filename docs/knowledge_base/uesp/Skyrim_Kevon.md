[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-creature-Kevon.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Kevon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Kevon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Kevon.jpg) Kevon The [skeletal](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) remains of **Kevon** can be found in a jail cell in [Castle Dour Dungeon](Skyrim_Castle_Dour_Dungeon.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He was the last known person to visit [Blackbone Isle](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md), and you will need to read a [note](Skyrim_Kevon%27s_Note.md) he left behind as part of the related quest. He hid a [map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Map_to_Blackbone_Isle) to the island behind a loose brick in his cell before he died.
