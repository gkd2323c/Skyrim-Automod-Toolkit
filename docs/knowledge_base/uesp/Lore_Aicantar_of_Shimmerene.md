# Aicantar of Shimmerene

Aicantar of Shimmerene was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [author](Lore_Books_by_Author.md) from [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md), and the [Sapiarch](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) of Indoctrination active around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), whose works mainly consisted of propaganda for the first [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md).

He was responsible for several works of propaganda relating to the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), namely *[Crimes of the Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Crimes_of_the_Daggerfall_Covenant.md)*,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTDC-1)</sup> *[Regarding the Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Regarding_the_Ebonheart_Pact.md)*,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTEP-2)</sup> *[An Unusual Alliance](Lore_An_Unusual_Alliance.md)*,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AUA-3)</sup> and *[Welcome to New Aldmeri Irregulars](Lore_Welcome_to_New_Aldmeri_Irregulars.md)*.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WTNAI-4)</sup> He is also the author of several official [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) documents, such as [a handbill](Lore_Thalmor_Handbill.md) regarding Dominion etiquette and [responses](Lore_Response_to_Citizen_Inquiries.md) to inquiries submitted to the Presidium of Aldmeri Cultural Illumination and Dissemination.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TH-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RTCI-6)</sup> Arguably Aicantar's most notable work is *[Before the Ages of Man](Lore_Before_the_Ages_of_Man.md)*, a chronicle of the [Dawn](Lore_Dawn_Era.md) and [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) Eras which survived in popular literature well into the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BTAOM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BTAOMESO-8)</sup> He also wrote guidelines for the apparel, armor, and equipment of the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CM25-9)</sup> Additionally, he engaged in correspondence explaining various noteworthy topics related to the affairs of the Aldmeri Dominion.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AYQ-10)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Aicantar was the subject of an assassination plot in Shimmerene. The [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) recruited Apraxic Mer—outcasts forced to live in the city's sewers—whom they attempted to persuade into murdering Aicantar, hoping to cause discord and turmoil in Shimmerene. The plot was uncovered by a [newcomer](Lore_Soulless_One.md), however, who killed the Court of Bedlam's agent in Shimmerene, putting an end to their scheme.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-11)</sup>

## Notes
- Aicantar of Shimmerene is not to be confused with Aicantar, the nephew of the famed scholar [Calcelmo of Markarth](Lore_Calcelmo_of_Markarth.md).
