Mounts of Tamriel: Introduction **by [Fadus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fadus_Falto) of Skingrad** *An introduction to a work cataloging mounts found around Tamriel* *
---
Submitted for consideration, care of the esteemed [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) and their august review board.

The works included herein represent the latest in my ongoing series attempting to catalog the cultural contributions of the many and varied peoples of the [continent](Lore_Tamriel.md). My treatise, "We Are All [Earth Bones](Lore_Earth_Bones.md) Under the Skin" is in consideration for the Gwylim prize, and I hope you find these works stand as worthy corollaries to that body of evidence.

Each volume will consider a beast. And not just any beast, but the beasts (large or small, thick or thin) that are utilized as riding mounts. I leave no subject untouched: from the origins of the common [horse](Lore_Horse.md), to the [bizarre bugs](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) of [Vardenfell](Lore_Vardenfell.md), to the rare [constructs](Lore_Construct.md) left behind by the [Dwarves](Lore_Dwemer.md). I appreciate your time and attention, and hope that these volumes are as entertaining to read as they were to compile.

May the [Father of Equations](Lore_Julianos.md) keep you safe in these trying times,

Fadus Falto of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md)
