## Walkthrough
The camp is a short distance from the road that runs between Dawnstar and [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md); if *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* is installed, it is just east of [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md). Two [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and two [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) occupy the camp. The camp is on a ledge with a rock overhang above, with the mammoths normally found below the ledge. The typical painted stones and mammoth bones surround the camp. The fire has a ring of rocks circling it. Two [charred skeevers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charred_Skeever_Hide) are on stakes cooking over the fire. A few paces to the southwest of the fire is an expert-locked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) under the overhang, and to the south are three large [mammoth cheese bowls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mammoth_Cheese_Bowl). The first giant is near the fire, while the second giant is either a short distance east next to a dead mammoth or roaming the tundra south of the camp.
