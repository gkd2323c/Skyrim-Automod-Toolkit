Hoarvors are giant [ticks](Lore_Tick.md) that drink large amounts of blood to survive. They are famously native to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) but can also be found in other parts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They can generally be found among dense foliage and in damp locations. Riverbeds and filth-ridden areas are a good place to find them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IEGT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Hoarvor ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dance In Fire-3)</sup> Hoarvors are not quick to strike a lone enemy. However, in groups or in the presence of abundant food, they are known to strike swiftly and with ferocity. They don't hunt so much as they come upon their prey.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho SB-4)</sup> Hoarvors will commonly feed on sleeping, injured, dead and dying creatures.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dance In Fire-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kyne C-5)</sup> They are skilled at locating areas of strong blood flow from which to feed, where they drink from using a tongue-like proboscis.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HF-6)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Kyne C-5)</sup>

Hoarvor infestations are notoriously difficult to stamp out. Simple things like a drunkard left passed out in the streets at night or a pet left uncared for is enough to attract an infestation. It is said that placing frost salts in a circle around one's home is useful when trying to deter hoarvors from approaching.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-HF-6)</sup> [Jagga](Lore_Jagga.md) and similar substances are known to drive them into a frenzy.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho SB-4)</sup> Hoarvors are associated with [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md), and can cross from the [Dark Behind the World](Lore_Dark_Behind_the_World.md) to Nirn in areas ravaged by the [Dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Uses and Culture
Pickled hoarvor is a common snack for road-bound travelers.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Hoarvor skin and bloat-sacs can be used to create a variety of goods.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Hoarvor Contraband-9)</sup>

Some Bosmer admire hoarvors, husbanding them or keeping them as pets.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho SB-4)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

During the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), the [Lord Warden](Lore_Lord_Warden_Dusk.md) of the [Imperial City Prison](Lore_Imperial_Prison.md) conducted experiments resulting in the conception of a strain of necrotic hoarvor. They possessed the ability to spit a corrosive substance.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BN-11)</sup> Had [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s schemes not been stopped, these hoarvors would have been sent to fight mortals on the front lines to help end the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) for the Daedra.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Similarly the [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) of the Abyssal Cabal were known to raise [undead](Lore_Undead.md) necrotic **Sea Ticks**, evidentially a species related to Hoarvors native to somewhere in the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>
