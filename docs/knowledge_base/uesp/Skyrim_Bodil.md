[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Bodil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bodil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodil.jpg) Bodil **Bodil** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works under [Beitild](Skyrim_Beitild.md) at [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

Like her colleague, [Gjak](Skyrim_Gjak.md), she never surfaces from the mine and spends her entire life working, relaxing, or eating.

She wears a pair of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00080699). She wields a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and carries [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
When approached she can say:

*"Here to dig. That's all."* *"Dawnstar's got its problems, but as long as the ore is good, I'll stay."*
## Notes
- Bodil actually owns a bedroll located in the mine. However, due to her not having any kind of sleep package set up, she will never been seen using it.
