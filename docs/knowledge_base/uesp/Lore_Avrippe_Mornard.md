Avrippe Mornard was the Duke of the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) and head of [House Mornard](Lore_House_Mornard.md) during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). During [Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md), Avrippe supported King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md) of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) against his liege lord, King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup> After Ranser's defeat in [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567), Emeric stripped Avrippe of his dukedom, granted it to House Mornard's rival, [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md), and reduced the Mornards to the title of count.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho HD-4)</sup> Emeric then exiled him to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where he died in [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup>

## Biography
Avrippe Mornard was a member of [House Mornard](Lore_House_Mornard.md), the ruling family of the Duchy of [Systres](Lore_Systres.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup> He eventually became the Duke of the Systres.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup> He had a brother.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mornard-5)</sup> He had a son named Leonard.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup>

In [2E 562](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_562)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup> or [563](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_563),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup> the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) reached the Systres and spread throughout the archipelago with alarming speed, killing commoners and nobles alike. As a shipping hub, the Systres suffered terribly from the Knahaten Flu—stricken by wave after wave of steadily escalating strains of the disease. In [2E 565](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_565), Duke Avrippe sealed the Systres off entirely, barring anyone from entering or leaving the archipelago. As the archipelago was dependent on trade, the Systres fell into destitution. When the plague receded, King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) viewed his vassal Avrippe unfavorably, as he had ensured his own safety and fortune at the expense of his people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup>

Troubled by Emeric's reproach, Avrippe looked to other noble houses to secure his position.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup> When King [Ranser](Lore_Ranser.md) of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) declared war on [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) in [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), Avrippe provided him with knights and arms, breaking his ties of vassalage to Emeric.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho HD-4)</sup> After Ranser was defeated in [2E 567](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_567) and once matters were settled on the mainland, Emeric turned his attention to the Systres.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup>

As punishment for supporting Ranser, Emeric stripped Avrippe of his dukedom and stewardship of the Systres and awarded it to the Mornards' rival, [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md), led by Donoven Dufort, reducing House Mornard's title to count.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ho HM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho HD-4)</sup> Additionally, Emeric seized two-thirds of House Mornard's holdings and granted them to House Dufort.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ho HD-4)</sup> Emeric then exiled Avrippe to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), where he died in [2E 568](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_568).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup> Leonard succeeded him as the head of House Mornard.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SHV7-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Mornard-5)</sup>
