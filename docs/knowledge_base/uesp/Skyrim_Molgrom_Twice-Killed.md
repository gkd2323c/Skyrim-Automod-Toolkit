[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-npc-Molgrom_Twice-Killed.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Molgrom_Twice-Killed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Molgrom_Twice-Killed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Molgrom_Twice-Killed.jpg) Molgrom Twice-Killed in Riften Jail **Molgrom Twice-Killed** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) and a former member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md). He is currently doing time in the [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md) as a punishment from [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) who disapproved of his methods. He wears only a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) and a pair of [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps).

Twice-Killed is a self-given name despite the fact that he hasn't even been killed once. He started using the title after a night out a few years ago when he was trying to get [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) drunk at the [Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md). He was stabbed after telling someone who was making too much racket to keep it down, but Vex saved him.

## Dialogue
When approached, Molgrom will be dismissive of you, saying things like:

*"What? What are you looking at?"* *"Get away from me."* *"Piss off. I'm busy."* He will only be available for dialogue if you are a member of the Thieves Guild, in which case he will instead greet you with complaints:

*"Brynjolf sent you to rub it in?"* *"Waste of talent throwing me in here, right?"* *"Can't wait to get out of here."* When spoken to directly he can be asked questions about himself:

**Twice-Killed?** *"Got into a scrap a few years ago at the Ragged Flagon. Some idiot who stole himself a guard's sword and kept boasting about it.
