[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Linwe.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Linwe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Linwe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Linwe.jpg) Linwe **Linwe** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [thief](Skyrim_CombatThief.md) and the leader of the Summerset Shadows in Skyrim. He resides in [Uttering Hills Cave](Skyrim_Uttering_Hills_Cave.md), from where he conducts the business of the Shadows around [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He uses [Niranye](Skyrim_Niranye.md) in Windhelm as the [fence](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Fences) for the group, but rarely sees her personally. He wears a special set of armor that has similar properties to the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Armor.md) set of armor. It is made up of [armor](Skyrim_Linwe%27s_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Linwe%27s_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Linwe%27s_Gloves.md), and [hood](Skyrim_Linwe%27s_Hood.md).

His style of thievery differs from the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) in that he prefers stealing from the recently deceased in the Halls of the Dead, but like the Guild he does not kill them himself. He is not averse to robbing the graves of the more long-term dead.

He is also the author of two letters, the *[First Letter from Linwe](Skyrim_First_Letter_from_Linwe.md)* and the *[Second Letter from Linwe](Skyrim_Second_Letter_from_Linwe.md)*.

## Bugs
- It is possible to obtain unlimited copies of Linwe's unique armor set. This can be exploited by using The Ritual Stone Power or Dead Thrall, and Fast Traveling to a nearby location (prior copy must be obtained from his corpse beforehand).
- Installing [Official Patch 1.9](Skyrim_Patch.md) can cause Linwe's armor set to no longer benefit from the Matching Set bonus. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.2, addresses this issue. Bethesda overlooked his armor when working on the Official Patch. The error has now been reversed and the Matching Set bonus has been reinstated.
