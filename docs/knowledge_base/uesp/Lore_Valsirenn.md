Valsirenn was high ranking [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) monk and member of the [Conclave of the Eleven Forces](Lore_Conclave_of_the_Eleven_Forces.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt C-1)</sup> She was instrumental in stopping the [Daedric invasion](Lore_Daedric_Triad.md) of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) and dispatching the Abyssal Cabal.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-2)</sup> Following the death of [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) she took the position of interim Ritemaster and became the temporary leader of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md), a long-standing monastic group of mages dedicated to studying the [Old Ways](Lore_Mysticism.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn Ritemaster-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/ON-quest-The_Queen%27s_Decree.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Queen%27s_Decree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Queen%27s_Decree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Queen%27s_Decree.jpg) Valsirenn investigating Monastery of Serene Harmony
### Early Life
At some point before [2E 282](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_282) in the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), a very young Valsirenn joined the Psijic Order after being personally recognized by Ritemaster Iachesis. As of then [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) was still one of isles in the [Summerset Archipelago](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). She married fellow Psijic initiate Leythen and they started a family together.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt C-1)</sup> At some point she gave birth to their daughter Ealdaawe. Ealdaawe contracted an unknown illness, and though the Order worked tirelessly to save her (including Iachesis), she died when she was young after succumbing to the illness. Valsirenn was not there for Ealdaawe's final moments, and threw herself into her work after her death, driving her and Leythen apart. Soon afterwards Leythen abandoned Psijic Order during his field mission.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn TTS-4)</sup>

She eventually became full-fledged monk. She was known as curious teacher, scholar devoted to arcane research and one of the Psijic Order's experts on mysticism and esoteric knowledge.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt C-1)</sup>

### The Daedric War
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-npc-Earl_Leythen_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Earl_Leythen_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Earl_Leythen_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Earl_Leythen_02.jpg) Valsirenn stops Leythen at the College of Sapiarchs Soon after Queen Ayrenn opened borders of the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Valsirenn was sent by the [Ritemaster Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md) to investigate a warning from [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) about Daedric plot involving Summerset.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sotha-5)</sup> She also tried to locate her husband's current whereabouts. Her investigation led her to the Monastery of Serene Harmony in [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md). She joined forces with [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md). They uncovered the signs of Daedric worship and cruel treatment of the newcomers in the monastery grounds and found out the [Abyssal Pearl](Lore_Abyssal_Geysers.md) in the monk's quarters. It was sent to [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) for further investigation. After Aldarch of the Monastery who turned out to be member of the Court of Bedlam was dispatched by the Vestige she bestowed them an invitation to the Artaeum.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOTQD-6)</sup>

To continue her investigation, Valsirenn traveled to the large sinkhole that had recently opened up near [Rellenthil](Lore_Rellenthil.md). It was caused by [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) K'Tora, who in collaboration with the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md), had created the uncommon phenomena in the firm and unchanging foundations of Summerset Isle. Valsirenn, with help from the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), managed to stabilize the walls to prevent them from crumbling down and the sinkhole from growing deeper and wider.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-APOGP-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RTCLS-8)</sup>

Soon afterwards, K'Tora invaded Artaeum and captured Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md). T'Kora attempted to extract the Ritemaster's deepest memories using the [Orbs of B'Raken Drel](Lore_Orbs_of_B%27Raken_Drel.md), but his plan was foiled when Valsirenn with the aid of [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) retrieved the staff from the Psijic Relic Vaults and used it to breach K'Tora's mind barrier and kill him.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Val Buried-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONBM-10)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/ON-quest-The_Dreaming_Cave_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Dreaming_Cave_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Dreaming_Cave_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Dreaming_Cave_02.jpg) Valsirenn exploring the Spiral Skein Valsirenn was tasked with warning the sages of [College of Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) about the threat to the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md). When she and the Vestige arrived to the College's grounds in Lilandril they found out that the Sapiarchs were under attack. They assisted in saving the College from Daedric threat. After a short and aggressive confrontation, Valsirenn found out that her long lost husband was worshipper of Mephala and responsible for the attack.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn TTS-4)</sup> She took Leythen back to Artaeum where he was briefly imprisoned.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOTDC-11)</sup>

She ventured to the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md) together with the Vestige on behalf of Daedric Prince [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) to rescue her champion [Darien Gautier](Lore_Darien_Gautier.md) from clutches of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md). They succeeded and returned to Artaeum.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESOTDC-11)</sup>

Once Ritemaster Iachesis regained his lost memories Valsirenn, together with the Vestige and [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md)'s champion [Darien Gautier](Lore_Darien_Gautier.md) ventured to [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md), where the group attempted to learn where Iachesis had hidden the [Heart of Transparent Law](Lore_Heart_of_Transparent_Law.md). When they reached the ritual site and learned that the Heart was inside Iachesis' body, Mephala and Clavicus Vile appeared and tried to retrieve the crystal. They were interrupted by Nocturnal, who betrayed them, banished them from her realm, and tore the Heart from Iachesis' body, killing him.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOLi T-12)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-quest-Buried_Memories_03.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Buried_Memories_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Buried_Memories_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Buried_Memories_03.jpg) Valsirenn wielding the Ul'Vor Staff Soon after the death of the Ritemaster Iachesis she traveled to the gates of Crystal Tower to oversee Sapiarch's attunement ritual. During the process the Nocturnal forces launched an attack at the scholars. Despite her efforts two Sapiarchs responsible for the ritual were killed. The Psijic Order reluctantly decided to strike a bargain with Mephala and Clavicus Vile in order to stop Nocturnal from gaining control of the Crystal Tower.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOANA-13)</sup>

Valsirenn together with her fellow Psijic monk Oriandra and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) stayed at Artaeum in order to maintain the connection between the Psijics and the volunteers who ventured to Crystal Tower to fight against Nocturnal. Vestige accompanied by Darien Gautier and her husband Leythen entered the Crystal Tower and managed to stop Nocturnal from using the [Heart of Transparent Law](Lore_Heart_of_Transparent_Law.md), gaining control of the Crystal Tower and using its power to spread her influence to every possible reality. However only the Vestige returned from this mission alive. Valsirenn entered the Tower to ensure their safe return to the Artaeum.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOTCT-14)</sup>

Soon after the threat was ended Valsirenn, together with the Vestige, [Proxy Queen Alwinarwe](Lore_Alwinarwe.md) and a group of Summerset citizens convinced the leader of Sapiarchs to gather allies in order to defend the Crystal-Like-Law <sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ANA-15)</sup> and became the temporary Ritemaster of the Psijic Order <sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn Ritemaster-3)</sup> honoring the dying wish of previous Ritemaster Iachesis.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Iachesis-16)</sup>

### Deadlands
At some point during [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Valsirenn was studying Eleventh Force, Change inside the Burn in [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) but wound up in synchroseisiac state and lost attunement with reality. Thanks to the assistance of the Vestige and the guidance of Daedric sage [Rrvenk](Lore_Rrvenk.md), she managed to overcome this issue and regain her lost attunement. <sup>[[17]](#cite_note-TFOC-17)</sup> She studied under sage Rrvenk for short amount of time and attended the celebrations in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) after the realm was saved by the Vestige.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn AAH-18)</sup> She was also present during Eveli Sharp-Arrow's speech in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) Castle after the threat from the Deadlands was eliminated.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-ESOValsirenn HSE-19)</sup>

### Legacy
Artaeum returned to Tamriel circa [2E 730](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_730), with it now public knowledge that Iachesis no longer led the order, but the Psijics would not offer any explanation as to where the Isle had gone or of Iachesis' demise.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-FOA-20)</sup> The position of the leader of Psijic Order was taken by [Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md) at some point before [3E 110](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_110).<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-FOA-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TDOO-21)</sup>
