# Sword Not Held

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/MW-concept-Vivec.jpg/150px-MW-concept-Vivec.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-concept-Vivec.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-concept-Vivec.jpg) Temple depiction of Vivec wielding the sword not held The sword not held is an artifact of the [Tribunal](Lore_Almsivi.md) god [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV20-1)</sup> The sword not held is a curved,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV20-1)</sup> bright,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BAT-2)</sup> [silver](Lore_Silver.md) longsword<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup> that, as the name implies, was not grasped when used in combat. When Vivec went to fight one of his monster children, [Moon Axle](Lore_Moon_Axle.md), he learned that the monster was immune to spears. Instead of using his spear, [Muatra](Lore_Muatra.md), he used the sword not held.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV20-1)</sup> Vivec also utilized the blade to some degree against his other monster children, [City-Face](Lore_City-Face.md) and the [Ruddy Man](Lore_Ruddy_Man.md).

A [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) legend speaks of a battle between Vivec and the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) in which Vivec gave the Daedra Lord his sword rather than dishonor himself by fighting an unarmed foe.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup> One particular Dunmeri fable claims Vivec severed a withered hand from a cripple using his sword in order to teach the lesson that it was better to let go of an evil thing than to cling to it for pity's sake.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BAT-2)</sup> Another legend speaks of a sword meeting between Vivec and [Cyrus the Redguard](Lore_Cyrus.md) in which Vivec wielded the sword not held.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Vivecmeeting-4)</sup>

Vivec is depicted with the artifact in various shrines and tapestries. Vivec's sigil was that of the sword, he was known as the Sword of the Triune, and [his city](Lore_Vivec_City.md) was referred to as the City of Swords.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LOV23-5)</sup>
