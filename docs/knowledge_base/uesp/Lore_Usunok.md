Usunok was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) hero held in high regard by the Orc branch of the Cult of [Trinimac](Lore_Trinimac.md). He features in the Orsimer religious scripture *The Book of the Warrior God* and has a passage titled *The Rage of Usunok* devoted to him and his deeds. In this passage, he is described as a great hunter and warrior, known for his fury, charging only with his fists and the blessing of Trinimac, delivering a single, fatal blow to his foe.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOU-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ordatha-2)</sup> His deeds influenced the metalworking of the Trinimac Cult faithful. Those who follow the way of Trinimac call their rounded maces "fists of Usunok" in honor of his deeds. His strength was considered legendary, he could fall down a full-sized [mammoth](Lore_Mammoth.md) with a single punch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CS-3)</sup>

Usunok was associated with rage and strength and Orc priests of Trinimac could bestow his blessings to the faithful, which greatly imbued their physical strength for a short period of time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Elder_Akarah-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAOAK-5)</sup>

He was also revered by the extremist branch of the Orc cult of Trinimac, the [Vosh Rakh](Lore_Vosh_Rakh.md) and his blessings were bestowed during the ritual of the Gauntlet. They believed that to accept the blessing of Usunok is to obtain strength to overcome the challenge.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Elder_Akarah-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAOAK-5)</sup>
