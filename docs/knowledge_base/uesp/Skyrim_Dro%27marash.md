[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-npc-Dro%27marash.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dro%27marash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dro%27marash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dro%27marash.jpg) Dro'marash **Dro'marash** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Speechcraft Journeyman) who follows [Ahkari](Skyrim_Ahkari.md)'s [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). He can be found walking the roads between [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) and [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), or camped outside either city. He is a common-level [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md).

Dro'marash wears a full set of [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), including a pair of steel gauntlets (two variants), and a pair of steel boots (two variants). He is equipped with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).
