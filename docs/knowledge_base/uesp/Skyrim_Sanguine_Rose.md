It will summon a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) to aid in your fights for 60 seconds.

## Related Quests
- **[A Night To Remember](Skyrim_A_Night_To_Remember.md)**: Find your lost friend, [Sam](Skyrim_Sam_Guevenne.md), as you recover from a bad hangover.

- If you kill the Dremora it will not drop a [Daedra Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md).
- Unlike in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Sanguine_Rose)*, the staff's spell in *Skyrim* doesn't need to hit anyone in order to work.
- If you've given the staff to a follower, they may use it. However, if you accidentally attack the summoned Dremora, it will retaliate unless you sheathe your weapon.
- The Dremora summoned is leveled according to the chart at the right.
- As of version 1.5, the staff now benefits fully from Conjuration perks (but only the ones that also affect conjured [Dremora Lords](Skyrim_Dremora_Lord.md)).
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- If you have the Conjuration perk [Elemental Potency](Skyrim_Elemental_Potency.md) the dremora will no longer summon. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.5, fixes this bug.
