# Jagged Crown

Of dragons from the blue brought down,
Births the walking winter's woe,
The High King in his Jagged Crown.*
—Ancient Nordic verse<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGalmar-1)</sup> The Jagged Crown is a symbol of office for the High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), a symbol of the might and power of the ruler of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) going back to the time of High King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) ([1E 143](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_143) - [221](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_221)), or even before. Legend holds that it was forged from the bones and teeth of ancient [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) by Harald himself. A portion of the power of every king who has worn it is said to be contained within.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGalmar-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COF-2)</sup>

The Jagged Crown heralds back to a time before there were [Moots](Lore_Moot.md), when the High King reigned by right of arms and collective adulation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGalmar-1)</sup> But in [1E 369](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_369), High King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md) was killed by the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) when he traveled to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to urge for a joint war against the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AHOD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3V-4)</sup> His body was secretly returned to Skyrim, and the Jagged Crown was interred with him in [Korvanjund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Korvanjund). While legend held that the crown was buried with Borgas, the resting place of the last in the line of the [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) dynasty was lost to history in the ensuing [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) which engulfed Skyrim.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRGalmar-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRJagged-5)</sup> Thousands of years would pass before the Jagged Crown would again see the light of day. Due to the instability that occurred following the War of Succession, the [Crown of Verity](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crown_of_Freydis) was created in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) to succeed the Jagged Crown. Its goal was to help unify Skyrim behind a new monarch.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-COF-2)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md), possessing the Jagged Crown became a key point of contention between the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and the [Stormcloaks](Lore_Stormcloaks.md), as it was believed that the crown would serve as a potent symbol of legitimacy, and so both factions sought to reclaim the crown for their claimants, [Elisif the Fair](Lore_Elisif_the_Fair.md) and [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) respectively. Eventually, Ulfric's housecarl, Galmar Stone-Fist, learned of the location of Borgas' tomb from sources that he refused to divulge, and when spies for the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) learned of this discovery, a race to reclaim the crown began.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRJagged-5)</sup>
