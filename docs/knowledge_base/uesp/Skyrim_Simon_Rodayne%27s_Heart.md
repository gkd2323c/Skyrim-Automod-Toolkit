**Simon Rodayne's Heart** is the purified heart of [Simon Rodayne](Skyrim_Simon_Rodayne.md). You will initially find it at [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) as a [Corrupted Human Heart](Skyrim_Corrupted_Human_Heart.md) during the quest [Crypt of the Heart](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart.md), but you will have the option to transform it into this item by activating the [Shrine of Mara](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mara.md) at his grave. Placing the purified heart in his grave will allow Simon's ghost to peacefully move on.

## Combinations
Simon Rodayne's Heart has the same effects as normal [human hearts](Skyrim_Human_Heart.md), the [Corrupted Human Heart](Skyrim_Corrupted_Human_Heart.md), and [Mort Flesh](Skyrim_Mort_Flesh.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup>.
