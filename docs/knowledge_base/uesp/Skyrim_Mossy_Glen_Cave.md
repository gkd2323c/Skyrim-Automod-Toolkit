[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-place-Mossy_Glen_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mossy_Glen_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mossy_Glen_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mossy_Glen_Cave.jpg) Mossy Glen Cave **Mossy Glen Cave** is a small, unmarked bear den only accessible via [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md).

It is a natural cave with several stone pillars and plant matter throughout the cave.

## Exterior
There are two entrances, the first of which is found by heading uphill on a path to the north, past the point along the main path where you first see [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) upon entry to the canyon. There is a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush outside this entrance, among the rocks to the left. To find the second entrance, head north from the entrance of Fort Dawnguard. When the path reaches its first switchback, continue due north, and you will pass between wooden palisade wall sections; the cave entrance is in the northern corner behind some trees. There are two snowberry bushes by this entrance.

## Mossy Glen Cave
The cave is dense with vegetation. Entering via the first entrance leads into an area with a [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md). On the second ledge along the wall to your left, there are two unlocked chests with minor loot. In the northernmost room, there are four leveled [bears](Skyrim_Bear.md). After defeating the bears, check the west side of the room for another unlocked chest containing minor loot. On the east side of the cave, a narrow tunnel leads from the second entrance. There is another narrow tunnel directly opposite, which leads to the open area by the skeever. A [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) and four [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) fungi can be found inside the cave.

## Bugs
- Navmesh in Mossy Glen Cave has several issues with triangles going up walls, broken links, and a complete lack of cover data. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3, fixes this bug.
