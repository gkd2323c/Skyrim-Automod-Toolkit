[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-place-Shimmermist_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shimmermist_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shimmermist_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shimmermist_Cave.jpg) Shimmermist Cave **Shimmermist Cave** is a medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) northeast of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) containing [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) and [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md). It contains two zones: *[Shimmermist Cave](#Shimmermist_Cave)* and *[Shimmermist Grotto](#Shimmermist_Grotto)*.

## Related Quests
- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
Outside the cave are several menacing Falmer totems that should be a sign to stay away. There is also a patch of [bleeding crown](Skyrim_Bleeding_Crown.md) fungus just to the right of the entrance. This cave system can be very difficult for low-level players to clear.

### Shimmermist Cave
Entering the cave, it is strikingly unique with a plethora of bioluminescent flora and natural lighting. There are many [glowing mushrooms](Skyrim_Glowing_Mushroom.md) to be harvested throughout the cave system and [chaurus eggs](Skyrim_Chaurus_Eggs.md) found throughout the second zone. The ambiance is beautiful with shimmering opalescent ceilings, green glows, and flowing mist. Having a [light](Skyrim_Light.md) source or [Night Eye](Skyrim_Night_Eye_(power).md) is recommended after this room as [traps](Skyrim_Traps.md) are hard to see and areas where the Falmer dwell are the darkest portions of the cave.

The entrance leads into a passage that descends to the north before turning east and continuing to descend. On your right you will pass an [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) fungus, and ahead you will be able to see an earthen bridge crossing a pool of water fed by two waterfalls pouring down the walls. There are ramps leading down into the water, where you can catch a [direfish](Skyrim_Direfish.md) and five [tripod spiderfish](Skyrim_Tripod_Spiderfish.md). Above you on the north wall is a ledge that is used as a short-cut back to the exit. On the far side of the bridge is a leveled creature (from a [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) up to at least a chaurus). The path enters another narrow passage that continues to the east before turning south and opening out into a chamber with a ledge above you. There is a patch of [Namira's rot](Skyrim_Namira%27s_Rot.md) and [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) growing in the darkened passage, and right by the exit into the next chamber is a [tripwire](Skyrim_Tripwire.md) that triggers a [claw](Skyrim_Claw.md) trap from above. There is a Falmer standing on the ledge keeping watch, and on the right is a [Falmer hive](Skyrim_Falmer_Hive.md) containing a concealed Falmer. To the left of the hive are the bodies of three [dead bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md) with several [steel weapons](Skyrim_Steel_Weapons.md) on the ground among them. To the east is a Falmer tent with a closed gate blocking access to the interior. There is an unlocked Falmer [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) to the left, and inside the tent is a small fire and a patch of [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) fungus. The path continues past the tent and curves around to the west as it climbs a ramp up. There is another fly amanita and a bleeding crown fungi along this path. At the top there is an opening heading west that leads into another chamber, where the Falmer previously seen was standing on the edge of the ledge. Following the ledge west along the edge you come to a rock with two [Falmer helmets](Skyrim_Falmer_Helmet.md), a [Falmer bow](Skyrim_Falmer_Bow.md) and quiver of [arrows](Skyrim_Falmer_Arrow.md), a [Falmer war axe](Skyrim_Falmer_War_Axe.md), and two loose chaurus eggs on top. Farther to the rear against the west wall are four more patches of fly amanita fungus. To the south is another narrow passage that after a few turns and passing a patch of bleeding crown fungus opens out into yet another chamber with several rock pillars around the chamber and a leveled Falmer standing beside an unlocked Falmer chest, with three white cap fungus beside them at the rear. To the right of the chest is a passage descending to the south. The passage leads to the entrance to *Shimmermist Grotto*.

### Shimmermist Grotto
The grotto is the deepest portion of the cave system and by far the deadlier one. Falmer are found in larger numbers and, descending deeper, many chaurus egg sacs begin to appear. The passage leads out on to a ledge with two leveled Falmer opposite, next to a Falmer tent with gates on the entrance. In front of the tent is a dead [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md), and inside are several chaurus egg sacs and an unlocked Falmer chest. There is an earthen ramp leading down to the southwest. At the bottom a narrow passage heads west and leads to a chamber guarded by a chaurus. There is a skeleton in the middle of the floor. The exit is to the south; after a short passage it turns and opens into another chamber with two leveled chaurus in a feeding pit below. There is a ramp down on your right and a bridge that crosses the pit ahead. In the bottom of the pit there are three more skeletons. Another ramp leads back up on the far side, east of the bridge. To the west is an unlocked Falmer chest, to the right of a narrow passage. This continues to the west before turning south and entering a wider passage guarded by a leveled Falmer. There are a couple more fly amanita and white cap fungi growing in the passage (see [bugs](#Bugs)). The architecture changes to that found in Dwemer ruins, with a pair of metal doors at the end.

Beyond the doors is a room containing a leveled Falmer and a [Dwarven centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) standing in a docking station. Both enemies are formidable foes, and separating them is key (see [notes](#Notes) for tactics that may help to defeat them). Low-level players will likely die in one or two hits from either one, even from being near the Dwarven centurion as its steam vents can be fatal. Dwarven centurions have difficulty maneuvering corners and can't fit into small spaces, which can be exploited to destroy it with ranged attacks. In front of this is a stone table with two loose chaurus eggs, three [Dwemer cogs](Skyrim_Dwemer_Cog.md), a [Falmer sword](Skyrim_Falmer_Sword.md), a Falmer war axe, and various pieces of Dwemer metal. To the right of the docking station is an unlocked Falmer chest and a Falmer tent. To the east under a ledge is a partially buried dresser containing various Dwemer metal items. In the eastern corner is a ramp leading up to the ledge and the passage back to the first zone. The passage returns you to the chamber containing the pool, on the north side on a ledge above the water. From here retrace your steps to the exit.

## Notes
- It is possible to bypass the initial Falmer cave area and go straight to the grotto. In the first room with the bridge and waterfalls, cross the bridge then immediately bear left and jump up the rocks to the tunnel that leads to the grotto. You will emerge on a stone shelf directly across from the Falmer; the Falmer will be able to access your position, but the centurion will not if you back up slightly. It is also possible to reach this ledge by climbing the rock in the center of the bridge and using the [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).
- If the centurion is lured to the preceding hallway, it will become stuck, as the corridor is too short for its tall frame. You may then attack the centurion from range, but beware its steam venting. Another effortless method to defeat the centurion is to hide inside the small hut in the centurion's room. It cannot enter the hut through the small entrance, and will be vulnerable to attack.
- Two [quicksilver ore veins](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Ore_Vein.md) are outside. The first is in front of a large rock west-southwest of the cave entrance, and the second is south of the rock.
