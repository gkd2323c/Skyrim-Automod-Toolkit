**Chief** Darragh was a [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) [clan](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) leader of the [Eagleseers](Lore_Eagleseer_Clan.md) who led the clan in years leading to [2E 560](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_560).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AYATEC-1)</sup>

Little is known of Darragh's past, but he became the Chief of the Eagleseer Clan at some point before the spring of 2E 560. As of then, he hosted a visit of a scholar from the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md), Glabrian Tuliel, who traveled to the Eagleseers' dhun to study the Reach's way of life. Although puzzled and reluctant at first, Darragh agreed to let Glabrian stay at his clan's dhun. It was believed that he agreed only to have Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md) owe him a small favor. During the year the scholar visited the clan's holdings three more times. Three days before the return of Glabrian, in the middle of the winter, Darragh was killed by [Six-Fords](Lore_Six-Fords.md), who were ancient enemies of the clan. The Six-Fords intercepted an Eagleseer hunting party and killed Chief Darragh, who took part in the hunt. His former advisor, Aislin used to blame Tuliel for his death, which led to scholar's departure from the Eagleseer's territory. The last year of Darragh's life was documented in Glabrian's work that described his time with the clan. It was published four years later, in [2E 564](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_564) during Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md)'s reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AYATEC-1)</sup>
