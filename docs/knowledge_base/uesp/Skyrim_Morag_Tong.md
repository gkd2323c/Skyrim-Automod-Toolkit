# Morag Tong


For more information on the Morag Tong, see the [lore article](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morag_Tong).

## Related Quests
- **[Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md)**: Save [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) from an assassination plot.

### World Interactions
- [Morag Tong Competition](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Morag_Tong_Competition): If you join the Dark Brotherhood, the Morag Tong may attack you.

## Notes
- The [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) member [Ravyn Imyan](Skyrim_Ravyn_Imyan.md) was a former member of the Tong. - Ravyn also states that *"When the Red Mountain erupted in Morrowind, the Tong fled... spread to the nine winds. We made a pact that one day, we might reunite... but I believe that day is long in coming."* Despite this claim, the organization would appear active on Solstheim during the events of *Dragonborn*. Although it's possible that the Tong had reformed since, due to there being a gap of 200 years between the Red Year and the aforementioned events on Solstheim.
- According to the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, [Irileth](Skyrim_Irileth.md), the housecarl to [Jarl Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), was a skilled assassin, having been trained by the Morag Tong in Morrowind.
