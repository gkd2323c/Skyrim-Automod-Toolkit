[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-npc-Agna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Agna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Agna.jpg) Agna lying dead in [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md) **Agna** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and [Golldir](Skyrim_Golldir.md)'s aunt, whom you help look for if you accept the side quest he offers, [Ancestral Worship](Skyrim_Ancestral_Worship.md), outside [Hillgrund's Tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md). She went into the tomb in attempt to stop the necromancer [Vals Veran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vals_Veran) from defiling her ancestors, but met her untimely end before reaching him.

She wears a leveled [light cuirass](Skyrim_Armor.md#light) with light boots, and may also wear light gauntlets and/or a light shield. She would have wielded a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and carries a random selection of [beverages](Skyrim_Food.md#Beverages), [food](Skyrim_Food.md), [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), [ingots](Skyrim_Ingots.md), tools, and gold.
