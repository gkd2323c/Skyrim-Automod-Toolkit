—Hircine Brinarch was a leader of a commando of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) Dark Witnesses who took over the Fallen Grotto from the [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) to become a [Briarheart](Lore_Briarheart.md). He was active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOTB-1)</sup>

## History

### Attack on the Fallen Grotto
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/ON-quest-The_Heart_of_the_Beast_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Heart_of_the_Beast_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Heart_of_the_Beast_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Heart_of_the_Beast_02.jpg) Brinarch's projection in the Hunting Grounds In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Dark Witnesses sought to reclaim the [areas](Lore_Western_Reach.md) of Bangkorai that once belonged to the Reachmen.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Brinarch-2)</sup> A Brinarch led a group of Dark Witnesses in an attack on the [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md), taking over the Fallen Grotto, a sacred place devoted to Hircine. After taking the grotto, he used the shrine of Hircine to directly commune with the Huntsman in the [Hunting Grounds](Lore_Hunting_Grounds.md) mirror realm of the grotto. He sought Hircine's approval for a briar heart, which the Prince approved. Brinarch received the boon and became a Briarheart.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOTB-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WL-3)</sup>

Since the [Wyrd](Lore_Wyrd.md) fell prey, they lost Hircine's favor, which was instead granted to the invaders. Hircine refused to grant an audience to the disgraced coven, forcing them to resort to having [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) contact him in their stead. He responded once the heart of one of the Wyrd sisters was presented to him. Intrigued by the act of self-sacrifice by the coven, Hircine offered the hunter's test as a chance to earn back the Grotto; the adventurer was to track down and defeat his Aspects of Strength, Speed, and Guile.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOTB-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Hircine-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WN-5)</sup>

The Prince brought a mere shade of the Hunting Grounds to the Fallen Grotto, where therein the challenge began. After the adventurer slew the aspects, the final challenge was between two hunters. The victor was requested to present the heart of the defeated to the prince of the Hunt.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SOTH-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Brinarch-2)</sup>

The fledgling Briarheart lost the duel. The adventurer prevailed, and Brinarch's briarheart was torn out and offered to Hircine as tribute, winning the Huntsman's favor back for the coven.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BQI-7)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOTB-1)</sup>

### Afterlife
Brinarch's spirit was sentenced to wander the Hunting Grounds with a gaping hole where his heart once was, as a reminder of his failure.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Hircine-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THOTB-1)</sup>
