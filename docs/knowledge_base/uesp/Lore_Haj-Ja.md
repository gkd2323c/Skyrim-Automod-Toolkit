# Haj-Ja

| Haj-Ja | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) | Gender | Unknown |

Haj-Ja was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [smith](Lore_Smithing.md) of some renown. Little is known about them, but as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), they were already considered famous. Although little is known of their achievements, they were known to work with [copper](Lore_Copper.md) and craft flatware. A collection of engraved and burnished copper plates and flatware crafted by Haj-Ja were found all around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the tumultuous period of the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup> Haj-Ja, similarly to many other smiths of that time,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CF-2)</sup> was known to stamp all their products with their mark.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CE-1)</sup>
