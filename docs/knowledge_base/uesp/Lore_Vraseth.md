# Vraseth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-npc-Vraseth.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Vraseth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Vraseth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Vraseth.jpg) Vraseth, progenitor of the bloodline Vraseth (or **House Vraseth**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with nimbleness. The bloodline dominates an area of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) - specifically the regions of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [Betony](Lore_Betony.md), [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md), [Kambria](Lore_Kambria.md), [Tulune](Lore_Tulune.md) and [Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Glenumbra_Moors_(barony).md). They at one point made a rare alliance with the [Thrafey](Lore_Thrafey.md) bloodline. They were the enemies of the [Travelers League](Lore_Travelers_League.md), a branch of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) responsible for teleportation before the organization collapsed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFFactions-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFTravelers-3)</sup>

The bloodline is named after [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth_(person).md), who was from a coven of nomadic Nedes from ancient [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). He was involved in the attempted cremation of [Lamae Bal](Lore_Lamae_Bal.md), the first vampire. After awakening on the pyre, Lamae brutally raped the men of the tribe, seemingly passing on the gift of vampirism in the process.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Sot BM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OLBTM-5)</sup>
