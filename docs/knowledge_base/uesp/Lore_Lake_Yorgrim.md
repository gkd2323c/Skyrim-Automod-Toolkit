[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Yorgrim.jpg) Lake Yorgrim Lake Yorgrim is a lake in the eastern [Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), to the west of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md). It is the source of [River Yorgrim](Lore_River_Yorgrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup> In or around the time of the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Viinturuth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Viinturuth) was killed and then interred in a burial mound near the shores of the lake.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup>
