[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_Hold.jpg) Whiterun Hold **Whiterun Hold** is a [hold](Skyrim_Holds.md) in central [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), with its capital in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). It is initially neutral in Skyrim's [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), but both sides are actively competing to gain control of Whiterun. As a result, it is the site of the first battle of the war, Battle for Whiterun ([Imperial](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Imperial).md) or [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md)). The Imperial Legion will secure the allegiance of the incumbent Jarl [Balgruuf](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md), who seeks reinforcements in the face of an imminent Stormcloak siege, and Balgruuf's government, now aligned with the Empire, will accordingly remain in control of the hold should the Imperial Legion successfully defend the city; otherwise, the victorious Stormcloaks will put [Vignar Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Vignar_Gray-Mane.md) in power.

## Topographical Overview
The hold borders six of the other eight [holds](Skyrim_Holds.md) in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), all but [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) and [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md).

Whiterun Hold is mostly covered in tundra and bordered by two major rivers (the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) to the south and the [River Hjaal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal) to the north), which makes the land fertile and well suited to farming.

### Flora and Fauna
The tundra is home to all manner of [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) and [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md), from the large ([giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) and [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md)) to the small ([foxes](Skyrim_Fox.md) and [goats](Skyrim_Goat.md)). The White River, flowing down from [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md), is home to a large population of [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md).

The tundra is rich in [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md), [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton) and [butterflies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Butterfly). At night the butterflies are replaced by [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) and [luna moths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Luna_Moth). Another location of interest is [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), where the pine forest stretching up from Falkreath Hold takes by. Here are forest plants like [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) and [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle) more common than on the tundra. All types of [mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mountain_Flower), except the yellow variant, can be found throughout the hold. The frail [red](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) variant however seems to thrive best around the rivers by Whiterun. The snowy parts, like [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md), are only home to the humble [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md).

### Routes and Pathways
As the central hold in Skyrim, its main roads are in good condition, but a few of the more remote roads are not as well maintained. There are main roads heading southward to [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) through Riverwood and [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), eastward into [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) and [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), northward to [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), and westward to [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md) through Rorikstead and to [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). Travelers should be encouraged to see that the main roads are regularly patrolled by [Whiterun Guards](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md).
