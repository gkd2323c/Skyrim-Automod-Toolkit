## Creatures
Goldfish appear at Goldfish spawning sites. Each spawning site generates three Goldfish. There are 13 spawning sites in 9 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 around the unmarked [Talos](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) shrine south of [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md)
- 6 around [Clearpine Pond](Skyrim_Clearpine_Pond.md)
- 6 around [Stony Creek Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stony_Creek_Cave)
- 6 around [Mara's Eye Pond](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Eye_Pond.md)
- 3 around [Clearspring Tarn](Skyrim_Clearspring_Tarn.md)

There is also one goldfish spawning site outside [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md), which may be useful for completing the [Heart of Gold](Skyrim_Heart_of_Gold.md) quest due to its proximity to [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).
