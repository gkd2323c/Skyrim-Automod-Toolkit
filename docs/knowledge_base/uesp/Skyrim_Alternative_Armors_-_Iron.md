## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of heavy Iron armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Brothers in Irons" starts by reading Nightgate Inn Patron's Note in Nightgate Inn.)*
