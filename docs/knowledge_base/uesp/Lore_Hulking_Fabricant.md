[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-audio-Hulking Fabricant_Moan.wav) The metallic call of a Hulking Fabricant Hulking Fabricants are [Fabricants](Lore_Fabricant.md) created by [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) that resemble a cross between an undersized [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md), a [scorpion](Lore_Scorpion.md), and a [chaurus](Lore_Chaurus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HFC-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Mark-3)</sup> They have wide jaws, a crest of horns or growths on their heads, two organic forelimbs, four to six metal legs, and a metal scorpion-like tail.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HFC-2)</sup> Unlike most other Fabricants, they do not appear to directly resemble any extant creatures of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Hulking Fabricants dwell in [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HF-1)</sup> but have in occasion made it to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRAttack Mournhold Quest-4)</sup>

Hulking Fabricant Elixirs, extracted from the remains of the creatures, give an increase to one's strength.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## History
When the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) came to [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) after [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s fall in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) sought to trick, entrap, and destroy them as part of an insidious plot. She first turned on Sotha Sil and slew him in his [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), then unleashed its mechanical inhabitants into her own city in order to frame her old friend, among them the Hulking Fabricants.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRAttack Mournhold Quest-4)</sup> Her ruse lured the Nerevarine to Sotha Sil's city, where she hoped that the Nerevarine would be killed by the inhabitants. When the Nerevarine persevered, Almalexia tried to finish the job herself. But her powers failed her, and she died at the Nerevarine's hand in the Clockwork City.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRAlmalexia-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

The Clockwork City and its people, creatures, and constructs were still around in the Fourth Era. Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a battle occurred between the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) and [Mecinar](Lore_Mecinar.md), and the fate of the city and its inhabitants remains unknown. Some stories claim that the [Forgotten Hero](Lore_Forgotten_Hero.md) destroyed the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md) that powered the Clockwork City, and its inhabitants were forced to abandon it as it ceased to function.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LRTCCTCF-8)</sup> Another account holds that the hero used the Heart to harness its power and fought Mecinar on equal ground, allowing the Heart to live on along with the city.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LRTCCKJE-9)</sup>
