They are sometimes hunted by [foxes](Skyrim_Fox.md) and other larger predators. Rabbits are rather small yet very fast, making them a tough hunt. ## Notes
- Rabbits can be brought home as pets by adopted children in [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md).
- The [Forgotten Seasons](Skyrim_Forgotten_Seasons.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) adds red-eyed, green-eyed, and white-eyed rabbits. These creatures have strange patterning on their backs and can only be encountered in the [Autumn's Bells](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns#Autumn.27s_Bells) section of [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns).
- Skyrim [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) adds many new [spawn points](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md#Notes) where rabbits may appear. These rabbits will be replaced by hostile [Bonewolves](Skyrim_Bonewolf.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md)</sup> or [Zombies](Skyrim_Zombie.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup> if you have either the [Bone Wolf](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md) or [Plague of the Dead](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md) Creations installed, respectively.
