[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Norion_the_Undying.jpg) Norion the Undying **Norion the Undying** is an ancient [Ayleid Lich](Skyrim_Ayleid_Lich.md) who guards the Great Welkynd Stone of [Rielle](Skyrim_Rielle.md) from those who seek to steal it. In [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) Creation, you will have to battle him to obtain the stone and leave Rielle alive.

Similar to a [Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), he floats off the floor during combat and wields a staff, this one being the [Staff of Ehlno Ede](Skyrim_Staff_of_Ehlno_Ede.md), which has a combined [Storm Wall](Skyrim_Wall_of_Storms.md) and [Drain Stamina](Skyrim_Absorb_Stamina.md) effect. He carries two [Welkynd Stones](Skyrim_Welkynd_Stone.md) and the [Rielle Key](Skyrim_Rielle_Key.md).
