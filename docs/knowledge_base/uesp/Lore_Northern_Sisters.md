# Northern Sisters

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/OB-item-Calcinator.png/200px-OB-item-Calcinator.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Calcinator.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-item-Calcinator.png) Calcinators The Northern Sisters are a group of [stars](Lore_Stars.md). Their alignment holds the key to the use of the calcinator. Traditional [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) [alchemists](Lore_Alchemy.md) from the province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) have a belief that the phases of the [moons](Lore_Moons.md) and their alignment with stars determine the precise proper orientation of the calcinator. Following the full moon, for each subsequent night, the Calcinator should be turned clockwise by one twenty-eighth of a full circle. However, if the alchemist finds themselves closer to the [Southron pole star](Lore_Southron_Pole_Star.md) than the Northern Sisters, the rotation should be counter-clockwise instead.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CT-1)</sup>
