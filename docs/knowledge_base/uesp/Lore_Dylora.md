Dylora is a [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md) who, like others of her kind, has a flimsy loyalty and has been seen in the service of more than one [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md). She has two sisters, Vika and Jansa, who have joined her in filling high-ranking positions during their service to the Princes that they have served.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Anchor-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THASI-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SIV-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIAJ-4)</sup> Together, they are known as the Seductive Sisters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Anchor-1)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), the Seductive Sisters were considered the most deadly Mazken in [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s service<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Anchor-1)</sup> and served as generals to his invading forces.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Anchor G-5)</sup> They were tasked with protecting Dark Anchors but were slain by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), and the Anchor was destroyed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Anchor-1)</sup> During this time, Dylora was known to have wielded a powerful [Lich](Lore_Lich.md)'s staff, but it was recovered from the Dark Anchor's ruins after her defeat.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Dylora and her sisters had changed their allegiance to serving [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). Jansa became the Captain of the Palace Guard of [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md), and Vika became interested in the conflict of [Cylarne](Lore_Cylarne.md). That year, Dylora was serving as the commander of the Mazken's home of [Pinnacle Rock](Lore_Pinnacle_Rock.md) in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) when the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md) began. The forces of Order invaded Pinnacle Rock to cripple the realm's army, and Dylora was taken prisoner.<sup>[nb1](#intnote_A)</sup> She was freed by Sheogorath's [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) and aided in the liberation of the stronghold. Although she perished with the other Mazken when the Wellspring of the Mazken was frozen by Order, they were revived when the Champion destroyed the crystals, stopping the flow of [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md). Dylora would then lead the Mazken to remove any remaining remnants of Order from their home, allowing them to resume their normal duties.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THASI-2)</sup>

## Notes
- [<sup>nb1</sup>](#note_A) This event only occurs if the player chooses to become the Duke/Duchess of Mania during the [Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles) main quest. This will lead Duchess [Syl](Lore_Syl.md) to betray Sheogorath due to the death of [Thadon](Lore_Thadon.md). Her betrayal led the Mazken to be caught off-guard, allowing for the invasion of Pinnacle Rock. If the player instead chooses to become the Duke/Duchess of Dementia , Thadon will survive and attack the [Aureal](Lore_Aureal.md)'s home of [Brellach](Lore_Brellach.md) in retaliation for Syl's death.
