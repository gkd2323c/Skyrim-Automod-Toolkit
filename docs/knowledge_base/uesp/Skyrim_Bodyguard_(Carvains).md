[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Bodyguard_%28Carvains%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bodyguard_%28Carvains%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodyguard_(Carvains).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodyguard_(Carvains).jpg) The Carvains' bodyguard This **Bodyguard** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) who can be found guarding [Plautis](Skyrim_Plautis_Carvain.md) and [Salonia](Skyrim_Salonia_Carvain.md) Carvain during a [random encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#On_the_way_to_the_Wedding) in the wilderness. They have traveled from [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to Skyrim in order to participate in the wedding between [Vittoria Vici](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici.md) and [Asgeir Snow-Shod](Skyrim_Asgeir_Snow-Shod.md), but never make it there. According to Plautis the second time you meet him, he is ultimately eaten by a dragon. The bodyguard wears either [Imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md) or [studded Imperial armor](Skyrim_Studded_Imperial_Armor.md), along with [Imperial light boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md) and matching [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Bracers.md), and [helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Helmet.md). He carries a matching [shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Shield.md), [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md), and a [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md).

## Notes
- Despite seemingly being an Imperial soldier, Stormcloaks will not attack him on sight. This is due to him not belonging to the normal Civil War-related factions which would normally provoke members of the other side to attack him.
