[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Solitude.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude.jpg) Solitude, capital of Skyrim [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-place-Solitude_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_02.jpg) The view from Solitude's port [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-place-Solitude_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_03.jpg) Solitude at street level **Solitude** is a major city on the northwest coast of Skyrim, and the capital of both [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md) and the entire province. It is the largest city in Skyrim and seat of the province's High King, and is built on a natural arch over the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). The ruler of Solitude is [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Elisif the Fair](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md), widow to the late [High King Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md), with her throne in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md).

The city houses many important buildings, including the [headquarters](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) in Skyrim, the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md), and the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md). The [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) is also near Solitude.

When you enter Solitude for the first time, [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md) is executed for treason.

For more information on Solitude, see the [lore article](Lore_Solitude.md).

## Notes
- [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) is a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) available for purchase after completion of certain quests for the jarl. It may be purchased from the steward for 25,000 gold.
- 6 health potions (3 standard, 2 plentiful, and 1 minor) can be found at certain points around the city. You can take all 6 aforementioned potions for free, and they all respawn every 10 days.
- [Hawks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk) can be seen circling the sky above the city. You can kill them for their [beaks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hawk_Beak) and [feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md), both of which are alchemy ingredients.
- Solitude also appears in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Solitude)* and [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Solitude).

## Bugs
- With the Skyrim Special Edition a door to "Quick Test Cell" can be seen on the local map near the entrance to the city. This door is otherwise invisible and can't be interacted with. It is a placeholder door for the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) which changes it to an entrance to the [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md). Without the Creation it is just an exit from the [CTest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Test_Cells#CTest) cell. <sup>**?**</sup>
