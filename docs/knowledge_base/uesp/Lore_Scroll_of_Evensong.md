Evensong **by [Moon-Singer Bokkari](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Moon-Singer_Bokkari)** *A treatise outlining the theory of a song that works in tandem with a sword philosophy to deal with powerful dro-m'Athra*
---
Are our songs the blade or the arm that swings it?

Is the distinction important? One can do little without the other.

We are the same, yet as different as dawn and dusk. As two notes in harmony. One cannot be without the other, and together they are something else entirely.

Find beauty in your many differences, but see yourself bonded by what makes you the same. Partnership.

Find yourselves to be tamiiti.
