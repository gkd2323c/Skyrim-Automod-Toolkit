# Order of the Hidden Moon

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/ON-quest-Path_of_the_Hidden_Moon_04.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Path_of_the_Hidden_Moon_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Path_of_the_Hidden_Moon_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Path_of_the_Hidden_Moon_04.jpg) Members of the Hidden Moon in their crypts The Order of the Hidden Moon was an ancient [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) order dedicated to [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md), based in what became the Ashen Scar. They were responsible for dealing with the [Dro-m'Athra](Lore_Dro-m%27Athra.md), by using a form of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) which would allow the dark spirits to redeem their corrupted souls and to pass through The Crossing. The [Moonlight Blade](Lore_Moonlight_Blade.md) was the special weapon of their order, blessed by Azurah herself.

## History
Khajiit mythology holds that Azurah founded the Order of the Hidden Moon and selected a litter of Khajiit to follow her commandments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tt TM-1)</sup>

At some point in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), a schism occurred when one of its adepts, [Arum-Khal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Arum-Khal) grew dissatisfied, renouncing Azurah and sought to take power for himself. Many of his fellow adepts followed him and the resulting civil war destroyed much of the temple complex and surrounding area. The survivors were unable to slay the now-lich Arum-Khal and instead sealed his spirit within the crypts. The Order became a memory and legend, until the time around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), where Arum-Khal's awakening caused the [undead](Lore_Undead.md) of the Ashen Scar to rise as well. However, some spirits of the Order still remain in the ruins, ready to stop the powerful Dro-m'Athra Lich once and for all.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MJ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMM-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSR-4)</sup>
