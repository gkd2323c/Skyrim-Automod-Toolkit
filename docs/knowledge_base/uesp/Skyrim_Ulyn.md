[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Ulyn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ulyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ulyn.jpg) Ulyn **Ulyn** is a dead [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md) who can be found in [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md).

Ulyn wears a [Dunmer outfit](Skyrim_Dunmer_Outfit.md) with Chitin [boots](Skyrim_Chitin_Boots.md), [bracers](Skyrim_Chitin_Bracers.md) and a [helmet](Skyrim_Chitin_Helmet.md). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), his [journal](Skyrim_Ulyn%27s_Journal.md), a leveled [weapon](Skyrim_Weapons.md), and some gold. Nearby lies a bow and a lit [torch](Skyrim_Torch.md).

He and [Eydis](Skyrim_Eydis_(Dragonborn).md) were searching for the [Visage of Mzund](Skyrim_Visage_of_Mzund.md), a Dwarven helmet. He adventured further into the ruins while Eydis held off the [Dwarven spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) that attacked them. He was killed when attempting to activate the boiler.
