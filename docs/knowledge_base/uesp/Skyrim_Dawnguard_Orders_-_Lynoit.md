Dawnguard Orders - Lynoit
---
[Lynoit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynoit),

We've received word of an increase in [Vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) activity in western [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). In the coming weeks, we plan to dispatch several more agents to the region, and need to establish a network of wilderness supply drops to support them.

You are to plant caches in the following locations:

- - - On the small island south of [Solitude Sawmill](Skyrim_Solitude_Sawmill.md).
- Due east of the entrance to the ruin [Volskygge](Skyrim_Volskygge.md).
- On the east side of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) Bridge.

If these reports are to be believed, the situation may be far more dangerous than you realize. Be cautious.
