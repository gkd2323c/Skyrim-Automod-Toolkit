We mortals cannot understand the mind of one who encourages his own children to tear each other apart.*
— *The Sick and the Doomed* by Terinvel Drathan<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAH-1)</sup> **Ashbound Hall** is a prison and a combat arena built by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) to contain the [souls](Lore_Soul.md) of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) who have displeased him. Molag Bal forces the vampires within to fight each other for his entertainment. In the second era, the hall would be pulled from [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) and into [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), during [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md)'s and [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md)'s reverse [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), severely damaging the hall as it fell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-scoutmihn-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Siril-3)</sup> In order for the vampires trapped inside to transverse through the prison the vampires had to acquire the aspects of Molag Bal. Special tokens that allowed them to travel freely through the different areas.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DAJ-4)</sup> Vampires then had to solve a riddle in order to transverse using the tokens.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-atttd-5)</sup> The vampires would fight tooth and nail to acquire these tokens, sometimes even tearing each other to pieces over them. Some of the lighted areas of the prison were designed to burn vampires in the same way the sun would. If they remained in those areas for too long, this only working on vampires that were bound to the prison as it was harmless to outsiders. This was believed to force vampires out of hiding in order to encourage more fighting.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Azatha-6)</sup> Ancient vampires were unable to leave the prison do to being completely bound to it, even after Ashbound Hall was flung from Coldharbour.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Aof P-7)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DAJ-4)</sup>

Sometimes a vampire would be cast into the Halls of Torment and transformed into a form that could never taste blood again.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-mj-8)</sup> There were no mortals in Ashbound Hall; for substance vampires were forced to feed upon each other in order to survive and to gain power though this never fully sated them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DAJ-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Siril-3)</sup> There appeared to be some wildlife they could feed on in certain areas of the prison.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Aofwlbfup-9)</sup>

The prison somewhat known from stories and interviews done by those who have studied vampiric lore, though little is understood about it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Siril-3)</sup> The interior of Ashbound Hall was of vampiric architectural design reminiscent of the architecture found within [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Ado AH-10)</sup>
