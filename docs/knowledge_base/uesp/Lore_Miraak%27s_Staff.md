# Miraak's Staff

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-item-Miraak%27s_Staff.jpg/200px-SR-item-Miraak%27s_Staff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Staff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Miraak%27s_Staff.jpg) Miraak's Staff Miraak's Staff is a powerful [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) [staff](Lore_Staves.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MS-1)</sup> It has the power to unleash Mora's Agony,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MA-2)</sup> summoning writhing apocryphal tentacles that stagger and poison any they come into contact with.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MS-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:604</sup>

## History
Sharing its name with its original wielder, the First [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md) [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), the staff was used by him alongside his sword, the [Firstblade](Lore_Firstblade.md). In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), he battled the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) at the summit of Apocrypha, wielding the staff during their clash.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Miraak-4)</sup> Following his defeat, the weapon was claimed by the victor.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Quest-5)</sup>

## Notes
- Mora's Agony inflicted by the staff,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MS-1)</sup> bearing a resemblance to the power the Last Dragonborn possibly obtained from one of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s [Black Books](Lore_Black_Book.md) *[The Hidden Twilight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Book__The_Hidden_Twilight)*.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MA-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-THT-6)</sup>
