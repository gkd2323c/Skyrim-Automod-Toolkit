# Other Unique Items

**Other Unique Items** covers any unique items that do not fit into any other category of item. These items are available in Skyrim in fixed locations, unlike most equipment in the game, which are randomly generated or generic equipment.

Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md) article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md) article. For a list of the other available weapons, see the [miscellaneous item category](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Items-Miscellaneous).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sapphire_Paragon.jpg) The Sapphire Paragon The [Sapphire Paragon](Skyrim_Sapphire_Paragon.md) is located in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). To find it, go to the area where the [Amethyst Paragon](Skyrim_Amethyst_Paragon.md) was found, northwest of the Shrine of Learning, and there is a path up the mountain. At the top, turn right towards the waterfall and you should see both the [frost giant](Skyrim_Frost_Giant.md) with the Sapphire Paragon and the gem platform directly across the river from him. If placed into the gem holder of the [Paragon Platform](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform), it will create an opening to the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md), with an abundance of leveled gems, several pieces of leveled elven gear, and three chests of leveled loot.

For more information, see the [Sapphire Paragon](Skyrim_Sapphire_Paragon.md) article.
