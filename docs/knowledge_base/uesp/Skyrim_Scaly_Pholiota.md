## Ingredients
51 guaranteed samples can be found in 32 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 4 in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 4 in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 4 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Drelas' Cottage](Skyrim_Drelas%27_Cottage.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 3 in [the White Phial](Skyrim_The_White_Phial_(place).md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
