# Senche-Cat

*I have been told that the senche-mounts of the Khajiit folk are not nearly as intelligent as their riders, though—confusingly for us non-Khajiit—they do share a name, in part, with two of the cat-folks' furstocks. I must also admit that the Baandari Pedlar who told me this accompanied his explanation with a broad feline wink, so perhaps the information is not entirely reliable.*
—Lady Honnorah af-Lahreq<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LH-1)</sup> [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/ON-concept-Senche_tiger.png/200px-ON-concept-Senche_tiger.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Senche_tiger.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Senche_tiger.png) A Senche-Tiger Senche-Cats, also known as **Senches**, are large, quadrupedal [felines](Lore_Cat.md) most often found in southern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> primarily [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), but can also be found in other provinces thanks to breeding.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSenche Cats-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOTesusah-4)</sup> They are differentiated from the other big cat species of Tamriel by their often larger sizes,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup> but they may be confused with other species due to culture and location, as well as their similar appearances.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-5)</sup> During the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), senche-cats and other creatures of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) were a chaotic hybrid of plant and animal, before the god [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md) established their forms as we know them today. Their original monstrous forms can be seen when the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) invoke the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONWild Hunt Senche-7)</sup>

The relationship between Senche-cats and the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) are somewhat mysterious, and difficult to understand for non-Khajiit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LH-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-AMA-8)</sup> The Khajiiti people consider themselves cousins or distant relatives to the Senche-cats,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-9)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-11)</sup> and the Senche-cat's secondary term, Senche, is also used to describe the [Senche](Lore_Senche_(furstock).md) and [Senche-raht](Lore_Senche-raht.md) Khajiit furstocks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LH-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1E-12)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-RDKMW-13)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOTesusah-4)</sup> Some have even described Senche-cats as a subspecies or primitive variety of Khajiit.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AAR-14)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Kyne C-15)</sup><sup>:155-156</sup> However, Senche-cats and Khajiit Senches are said to be distinct species,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LH-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-LSFP-16)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-AMA-8)</sup> and the Senche-cats are incapable of speech.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-11)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-AMA-8)</sup> The Khajiit do feel a strong kinship towards the lesser cats,<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-AMA-8)</sup> and have been able to tame them with ease.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Kyne C-15)</sup><sup>:156-157</sup> The Khajiit are also said to sense an intelligence in the Senche-cats that other races cannot,<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-AMA-8)</sup> and some have been known to treat them as if they were intelligent.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-17)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-18)</sup>

[Sabre Cats](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md), also considered cousins to Senche-cats, are similar to them but are not considered senches.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ONSabre Cat-19)</sup> According to a Khajiiti legend, sabre cats are actually a cursed offshoot of the Khajiit themselves.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-To D-20)</sup> Potentially contradicting this story however, are accounts of sabre cats existing during the lifetime of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-21)</sup>

Senche-cats are often hunted by the various peoples of Tamriel, primarily the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), for their pelts and meat. Their meat is said to have a very sweet taste due to the large cats having a taste for [moon sugar](Lore_Moon_Sugar.md), a trait they share in common with their intelligent Khajiit cousins.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Kyne C-15)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Sugarbelly-22)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-23)</sup> The hunting of senche-cats is thought to be considered distasteful to the Khajiit themselves.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Kyne C-15)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AAR-14)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

## Variants
Like the Khajiit themselves, Senche-Cats come in many different varieties. Depending on their appearance,<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-LSFP-16)</sup> Senche-Cats may be referred to as; Senche-Cheetahs, Senche-Cougars, Senche-Jaguars, Senche-Leopards, Senche-Lions/Lionesses, Senche-Panthers, Senche-Servals, and Senche-Tigers. Details on these types are listed below.

A variety of spotted Senche-cat tamed by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-Ra Gada-25)</sup>

A variety of Senche-cat that resemble [mountain lions](Lore_Mountain_Lion.md). Senche-Cougars dwell in east central Elsweyr, where they prowl in the cliffs and escarpments for [goats](Lore_Goat.md) and [marmots](Lore_Marmot.md). They have been domesticated by the Khajiit.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONSenche Cougar-9)</sup>

### Leopards
The majority of Senche-leopards have rosette fur patterns similar to those found on jaguars, and their patterns lend their name to those that can also be found on Khajiit.<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-ONLeopard Spots-30)</sup> The common Senche Leopards are bred as mounts for bearing an armed warrior while slinking through the steamy jungles of Pellitine.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-ONSenche Leopard-31)</sup> The Spotted Snow Senche-Leopard ss a rare variety of sence-leopard. It is notable for its white fur.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-ONSpotted Snow Senche-Leopard-32)</sup> Clouded Senche-Leopards differ from the typical rosette fur pattern by having large cloud-like circle patterns on their fur.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-ONClouded Senche-Leopard-33)</sup>

### Lion/Lioness
A variety of Senche-cat that resemble [lions](Lore_Lion.md). Males are referred to as lions, and have a mane. Females are referred to as lionesses. Senche-lion cubs may possess rosette fur patterns. The common lion is tan in color. Lionesses are used as fierce and powerful mounts, and hail from the broad savannahs of Anequina.<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-ONSenche Lioness-34)</sup> The Black Senche-Lion is a completely black breed preferred by thieves as mounts. The most notable Black Senche-Lion was the thief god Rajhin's mount, Umbrage, who was rumored to talk and even briefly fly.<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Lion-35)</sup> Senche-Lion prides are known to roam near the city of Riverhold, and are used as mounts and for herding livestock by the pastoral tribes of Northern Anequina. The lions of these prides have been dubbed Noble Riverhold Senche-Lions.<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-ONNoble Riverhold Senche Lion-36)</sup> Little is known about the white senche-lion. However, they are very rare, as smugglers claim they can make a year's fortune selling them. <sup>[[33]](#cite_note-37)</sup>

### Panthers
Panthers have black fur. Black Senche-Panthers are a purely black furred variety of panther. They are said to have bred by Baandari Pedlars, for use as a mount and for the ease of leaving town after dark.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Panther-38)</sup> Striped senche-panthers are similar to the non striped variant, but they differ in that they have stripes.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-11)</sup> Shadowghost Senches are an oversized breed of black senche-panthers, differing visually in their white spots. They are associated with the Daedric Prince Namiira, who is thought to send them as mounts to bear away those who are doomed to die.<sup>[[35]](#cite_note-ONShadowghost Senche-39)</sup> Indigo Senche-Panthers are a variety of senche that were bred for a reclusive family in Rivenspire. They possess black fur with a purple sheen. Rumors claim that when left to its own devices, it stalks werewolves for amusement.<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-ONInidigo Senche-40)</sup>

### Tigers
The Senche-Tigers' most defining feature is their stripes. Striped Senche-Tigers are a semi-domesticated version of the wild Senche-Tiger, bred for size and manageability. They notably have golden colored fur.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche-Tiger-44)</sup> Tenmar Senche-Tigers prowl in the Tenmar forest, where they are camouflaged by the shadows of the canopy. They have a brown striped pattern and are also used as mounts.<sup>[[41]](#cite_note-ONTenmar Senche-Tiger-45)</sup> Moonlight Senche-Tigers are sleek and silvery, and have been used as guardians for [Khajiiti temples](Lore_Temple_of_Two-Moons_Dance.md) dedicated to the [moons](Lore_Moons.md) ever since the revelation of the [Riddle'Thar](Lore_Riddle%27Thar.md).<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-ONMoonlight Senche-Tiger-46)</sup>
