** *"The text on every Elder Scroll is fluid, reflecting the changing nature of reality itself. They're like cats.The final section of this particular Scroll beckoned me to action, refusing to change no matter how often I read it."*
—Sister Terran Arminus<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Terran-1)</sup> **Moth Sister**<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-QAA-2)</sup> Terran Arminus was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [Moth Priest](Lore_Moth_Priests.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). After receiving a vision from the [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) in her care, she set things in motion to prevent the [Planemeld Obverse](Lore_Planemeld_Obverse.md) from being completed at the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POESO-3)</sup>

## History
Little is known about her early history, but she was one of the few remaining Moth Priests found in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) and subsequent invasion by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s daedric forces.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Terran-1)</sup> Prior to the daedric occupation of the city, she was the keeper of one of the Elder Scrolls and after receiving a vision<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TN-4)</sup> from it of a future crisis, Sister Terran hid the scroll in the library of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) so it was in place to prevent the worst from happening.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Terran-1)</sup>

The crisis the Elder Scroll foresaw was the Planemeld Obverse, an attempt to restart the Planemeld by using the scroll in a ritual to drag the White-Gold Tower into [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), these events came to pass when Empress Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md) came to the tower in an attempt to retake it from the invading Daedra. Sister Terran accompanied the Empress Regent and her bodyguards, and accurately predicted the arrival of a group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) which included [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) that would help retake the White-Gold Tower.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TN-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Terran-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Clivia-5)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POESO-3)</sup>

Sister Terran had foreseen the Empress Regent's betrayal and theft of the scroll once its hiding place had been revealed. She then guided the Undaunted group to the top of the White-Gold tower as the realms of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) shifted and merged. At the pinnacle of White-Gold, Empress Regent Clivia Tharn revealed herself to be [Molag Kena](Lore_Molag_Kena.md) in truth. She had come to the tower to begin the Planemeld Obverse with the power of the Elder Scroll. When the Undaunted group killed Molag Kena, Sister Terran arrived and reclaimed the Elder Scroll.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POESO-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Terran-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Clivia-5)</sup>

Outside the events at the White-Gold Tower, Sister Terran was known to have answered questions regarding Elder Scrolls in correspondence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-QAA-2)</sup> She was also responsible for assigning Sister Chana Nirine to the mission in Lep Seclusa and corresponded with Brother Leono Vibidus regarding it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MTCSC-6)</sup>
