[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-place-Errinorne_Isle_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Errinorne_Isle_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Errinorne_Isle_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Errinorne_Isle_02.jpg) Errinorne Isle ca. 2E 582 Errinorne Isle, also called the **Veiled Isle**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOWV-1)</sup> is an island found off the coast of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Errinorne Isle is named after [House Errinorne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:House_Errinorne), a noble house that ruled [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md). Errinorne Island was used as a shipping and warehouse complex for the city of Skywatch, but it was later abandoned after the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) took control of the island. The only landmark is the Veiled Keep, which is found in the center of the island.

## History
Errinorne Isle is named after House Errinorne, who ruled Skywatch from the Late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) onward.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> In the center of the island lies the Errinorne Academy, whose purpose is entirely unknown.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOLoad TVK-3)</sup> Errinorne Isle was part of the [Buraniim Sea-Complex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Buraniim_Sea-Complex) since the time of the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md), but it would eventually become a shipping and warehouse complex for Skywatch.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AEIII-4)</sup> When the [Sack of Skywatch](Lore_Sack_of_Skywatch.md) occurred in [1E 1301](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1301), the Sload of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) took over Errinorne Isle. The Errinorne Academy was destroyed by the Sload.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOLoad TVK-3)</sup> The Sload controlled Errinorne Isle for many years following the Sack of Skywatch. Their control over the island ended when a remnant of the [All-Flags Navy](Lore_All-Flags_Navy.md) obliterated the Sload around [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260). Errinorne Isle has been abandoned ever since.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AEIII-4)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Errinorne Isle was occupied by the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md), a group of [High Elf](Lore_High_Elves.md) supremacists who were strongly opposed to the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#First_Dominion). The ruined academy became the Veiled Keep, the Heritance headquarters. The island would become their training grounds for new recruits. At the same time, an [Agent](Lore_Soulless_One.md) of the Dominion infiltrated Errinorne Isle, going through the trials for recruits in the effort of entering the Veiled Keep and gaining intel on the group. It was revealed that High Kinlady [Estre](Lore_Estre.md) of Skywatch was the leader of the Heritance, and the Agent was able to swiftly defeat the leaders of the Heritance. Estre then escaped Skywatch and began her battle against the Dominion on Auridon.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOWV-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLt V-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOTVF-6)</sup> With the fall of the Veiled Heritance, Errinorne Isle was once again abandoned.
