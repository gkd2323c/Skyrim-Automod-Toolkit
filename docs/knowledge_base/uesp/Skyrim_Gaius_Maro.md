[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Gaius_Maro.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gaius_Maro.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gaius_Maro.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gaius_Maro.jpg) Gaius Maro at the start of his journey in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) **Gaius Maro** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), an officer of the [Penitus Oculatus](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus.md) and the son of [Commander Maro](Skyrim_Commander_Maro.md). He is [appointed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Father) by his father to "personally verify the security of every city in the province" in advance of the [Emperor's](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) visit to Skyrim. Gaius wears a complete set of Penitus Oculatus equipment. This includes the [Penitus Oculatus armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Armor), a pair of [Penitus Oculatus bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Bracers), a pair of [Penitus Oculatus boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Penitus_Oculatus_Boots), and a [Penitus Oculatus helmet](Skyrim_Penitus_Oculatus_Helmet.md). He is equipped with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and carries a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch).

## Related Quests
- **[Breaching Security](Skyrim_Breaching_Security.md)**: Kill and frame the son of a high-ranking [Imperial guard](Skyrim_Commander_Maro.md).

## Quest-Related Events
At the start of [his journey](Skyrim_Breaching_Security.md), you will witness his father talking to him, regarding his safety before he leaves.

- **Gaius Maro** *"Father, you worry too much. I'll be fine."*
- **Commander Maro**: *"I know you will. But all the same, remember everything I said. Stay alert, and when you get to the cities, make your observations and move on."*
- **Gaius Maro**: *"I understand, But you're being paranoid. I'm inspecting security, not charging off into battle. There's not a lot that can go wrong."*
- **Commander Maro**: *"Son, when the Emperor's safety is concerned, anything could go wrong. Off with you, now. And good travels."*
- **Gaius Maro**: *"Farewell, father. I'll return as soon as I'm able."*

After this, Gaius will share a heartfelt farewell with his lover, [Faida](Skyrim_Faida.md), owner of the [Four Shields Tavern](Skyrim_Four_Shields_Tavern.md):

- **Faida**: *"Well, this is it, then. Look after yourself, Gaius. You're doing your duty, and I'm proud of you. But you'd better come back to me. You hear?"*
- **Gaius**: *"Oh, Faida. I may travel alone, but you know I carry you always in my heart. I'll see you soon."*

When you encounter him on the road, he will tell you:

- *"Keep back, citizen. I have important business I must attend to."*

- - **After I kill you, I'm going to kill the Emperor.**
- *"What? Filthy assassin! We'll see who lies dead!"*

- - **Traveling alone can be so dangerous...**
- *"Is that a threat? Back off, or by the Eight I'll cleave that arrogant head from its shoulders."*

- - **(Remain silent)**
- *"I'm sorry, but I really don't have time for this. Move along."*

If you try and talk to him afterwards, you will get an angry response from Gaius:

- *"You are trying my patience, citizen. Back away. Now."*

## Notes
- An NPC by the name of **Voltus Agallon** originally had the task of checking up on security for the Emperor's arrival. The dialogue Gaius uses when speaking to you, as well as one of the quest-related scripts in use by him still refer to Voltus internally.
- He cannot be reanimated.

## Bugs
- Gaius Maro's travel and sleep schedule during Breaching Security doesn't conform to the [note that has his schedule on it](Skyrim_Gaius_Maro%27s_Schedule.md). The sleep packages would begin at midnight which would cause him to end up traveling at night on the wrong day in order to reach the next city. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. All of the eat packages have been shortened to 4 hours and the sleep packages now begin at 10:00 pm and run for 8 hours so his schedule should match up with the note now.
