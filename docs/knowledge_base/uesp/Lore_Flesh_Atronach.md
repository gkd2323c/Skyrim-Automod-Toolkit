[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/ON-quest-Castle_of_the_Worm_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Castle_of_the_Worm_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Castle_of_the_Worm_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Castle_of_the_Worm_02.jpg) A laboratory for creating Flesh Atronahchs Flesh Atronachs are enchanted flesh [golems](Lore_Golem.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HFAQUEST-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AHing-3)</sup> animated by [flesh magic](Lore_Flesh_Magic.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIRV-4)</sup> or [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOUL-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> They are considered undead, categorized as reanimated abominations alongside [skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md) and [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOUL-5)</sup> A [soul](Lore_Soul.md) or Daedric [vestige](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) is bound to the flesh, and the husk is reanimated.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOUL-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESORD-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KCAHC-8)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIRV-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AHing-3)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Due to this, some sources even describe flesh atronachs as [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that simply require some assembly to summon.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOSFlesh Atro Parts-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> They are believed to represent the element of true Flesh in a similar manner to [Elemental Daedra](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SIRV-4)</sup> They appear as a sewn-together conglomeration of skin and muscle, sometimes adorned with mystical symbols<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KCAHC-8)</sup> or wearing an iron collar.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TSB-12)</sup>

Some flesh atronachs have several glowing runes and energy spots on their bodies, the color of which depends upon how powerful the atronach is.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TSB-12)</sup> The foreheads of such atronachs are known to hold an embedded [Void Essence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void_Essence), a typically red gem with various [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) uses.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> Flesh atronachs from [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) have [azure plasm](Lore_Azure_Plasm.md) oozing from between their sutures.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOBlue Atro-15)</sup>

They are healed by poison<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DFFlesh At Ability-16)</sup> and their magicka increases if hit by spells.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-SIFlesh At Ability-17)</sup> Some emit an aura that damages opponents if they get too close.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DFFlesh At Ability-16)</sup> Many flesh atronachs have the ability to hurl fire at their target.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONFlesh Ability-18)</sup> Additionally, they can crush mortals flat with their arms, which can be massive stubs of flesh with rusted metal blades infused in its skin.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONFlesh Ability-18)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KCAHC-8)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TIEGTTS-19)</sup>

[Relmyna Verenim](Lore_Relmyna_Verenim.md) was obsessed with flesh atronachs animated by Daedric vestiges, which were a part of her research into the element of Flesh. [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) brought her to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) to allow her to continue her research, and flesh atronachs spread throughout the realm.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-20)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup> The [Gatekeeper of the Fringe](Lore_Gatekeeper_of_the_Fringe.md) was the result of her experiments.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-SIHaskill-20)</sup> The followers of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md) are also known to utilize flesh atronachs, distinguished by their black and red mottled skin, reminiscent of [Spiderkith](Lore_Spiderkith.md).<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-ESORed Atro-22)</sup>

## Flesh Colossus
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/ON-creature-The_First_Flesh_Colossus.jpg/200px-ON-creature-The_First_Flesh_Colossus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_First_Flesh_Colossus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-The_First_Flesh_Colossus.jpg) An armored flesh colossus (ESO) The **[Flesh Colossus](Lore_Flesh_Colossus.md)** is a larger, more powerful version of the flesh atronach. The first flesh colossus was created in secret by [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) when he was still training as a [Psijic](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) monk on the island of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup> They were among [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s forces during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-ESOFlesh Col-24)</sup> Flesh colossi can taint the ground around them with wells of putrid disease and poison.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-ESOColossus Ability-25)</sup>
