Highmourn Dizi *Notes on an Akaviri flute played at Savirien-Chorak's funeral*
---
A flute of [Akavari](Lore_Akaviri.md) design, this instrument was played at the funerary procession for the last [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md), [Savirien-Chorak](Lore_Savirien-Chorak.md).

It is said that as the sad and haunting notes of the dizi were played, the Akaviri faithful in attendance broke into tears, hearing the distant voice of their [homeland](Lore_Akavir.md). Even non-Akaviri were moved by the mournful, yearning tones, some even more than the Akaviri themselves.
