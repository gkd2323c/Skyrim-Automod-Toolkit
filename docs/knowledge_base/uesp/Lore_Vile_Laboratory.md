[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/ON-place-The_Vile_Laboratory.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Vile_Laboratory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Vile_Laboratory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Vile_Laboratory.jpg) The Vile Laboratory circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Vile Laboratory is a location in the Black Garrison region of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), used by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) for his many horrific experiments. The building itself is an emulation of a [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin, although the complex is crawling with undead and [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) guardians. It appears to be run by a [Xivilai](Lore_Xivilai.md) named Overseer Xvarik.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

The laboratory has seemingly been in existence since the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), as it is where the skeleton of the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Boziikkodstrun](Lore_Boziikkodstrun.md) was brought after his flesh was consumed by Bal. Here the skeleton was infused with "blood-of-darkness", which re-awakened it as a [Vestige](Lore_Vestige_(creature).md). The skeleton was also modified according to Bal's own design, before being plunged into the Azure Chasm to create the first [Daedric Titan](Lore_Daedric_Titan.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Indeed, experiments concerning [vestiges](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) and [souls](Lore_Souls.md) appear to be the norm in the Vile Laboratory. The [Xivkyn](Lore_Xivkyn.md) are the result of experiments with vestigial hybridization in the laboratory between [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) and Xivilai.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

At some point, a [Light of Meridia](Lore_Light_of_Meridia.md) was taken to the laboratory for safekeeping, in an attempt to keep the artifact out of [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md)'s clutches. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), two [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) members were imprisoned within the Vile Laboratory after being separated from the rest of the [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamriel.md) invasion force. The mages, a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) named Gadris and a [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) named Zur, underwent an experimental soul-meld process, and were subsequently cast out of the laboratory to die. Confined to a single body and desperate to separate their souls, the weakened pair began work on a transmutation potion to sneak back into the ruins. With help from the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), the "Soul-Meld Mage" managed to overpower Xvarik and reach the catalyst needed for the separation procedure, although only one of the mages survived.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The Vestige also recovered the Light of Meridia from the ruins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>
