Stendarr's Divine Spear **by [Ptolus the Bright](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ptolus_the_Bright), [Resolute](Lore_Resolutes_of_Stendarr.md) of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md)** *A poem extolling the power of Stendarr's magic*

Smiting all that is unholy,
Transfixing it with point and glow,
Elevates us with its aura,
Nullifies the wicked foe.
[Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), [undead](Lore_Undead.md), beasts and [manbeasts](Lore_Lycanthropy.md),
Abominations it strikes down.
Re-anoint us, stalwart [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md),
Resolute with spear and crown!
