Merric at-Aswala **Resolute War Horn**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOSAC-1)</sup> was a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) member of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and follower of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMerric-2)</sup> during the time of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) crisis and rose to the position of Guildmaster in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOWot C-3)</sup> Prior to joining the Guild, he served as a guard of the [royal family](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) and became a teacher and eventual mentor to [Prince Azah](Lore_Azah.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mt CPA-4)</sup>

A few years after becoming Guildmaster, several Fighters guildhalls across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) were attacked by the resurgent [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) and Merric investigated the matter. His search led him to the [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) Mages Guild sanctum, where he was killed destroying a previously unknown device known as a Soul Reaper created by the cult.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOAGi C-5)</sup>
