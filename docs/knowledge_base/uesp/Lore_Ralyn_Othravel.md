Ralyn Othravel was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) member of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) and the Grand Commander of the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). He was also the wielder of the legendary [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg/200px-MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Ghostgate.jpg) Ghostgate It is unknown when Ralyn Othravel was born. By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), he already held the rank of Grand Commander of the Ordinators,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup> and as such he answered to the Grandmaster [Berel Sala](Lore_Berel_Sala.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GDO-3)</sup> He also held the rank of Disciple within the Tribunal Temple. As of then he was stationed at [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md), where he helped safeguard the rest of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) from the threat contained within the [Ghostfence](Lore_Ghostfence.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RO-1)</sup> He was known as a person of importance at Ghostgate. Alongside him, Ordinator Knight Commander [Enar Dralor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Enar_Dralor) and Grand Marshal [Galdal Omayn](Lore_Galdal_Omayn.md) of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) were stationed there.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-2)</sup>

Aside from Auriel's Bow, which required great personal effort to obtain, he also wielded the rare Gavel of the Ordinator. While he donned [Indoril armor](Lore_Ordinators.md#Equipment),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RO-1)</sup> it did not necessarily indicate his [Great House](Lore_Great_House.md) affiliation.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Aside from being an accomplished warrior, Othravel was also a competent [spellcaster](Lore_Magic.md) in his own right, wielding an array of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) school spells.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RO-1)</sup>

In 3E 427, Mistress [Therana](Lore_Therana.md), an eccentric Councilor of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), discovered the bow's location through unknown means, although she claimed she could smell its [ash yam](Lore_Ash_Yam.md) scent. She requested [a member](Lore_Nerevarine.md) of House Telvanni to deliver it to her. It is possible that they forcibly took the bow from Ralyn Othravel and gave it to Therana, who grudgingly accepted it.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Therana-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWAB-5)</sup>
