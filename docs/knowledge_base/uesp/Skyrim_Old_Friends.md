## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md).
2. Investigate the graveyard near Tel Mithryn using the [Ring of Tracking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth%27s_Ring_of_Tracking).
3. Return to Neloth.
4. Go to [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md) and kill [Ildari Sarothril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari_Sarothril).
5. Return to Neloth for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Ring of Tracking
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-quest-Old_Friends.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Old_Friends.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends.jpg) Something is wrong at the graveyard. With his steward [killed](Skyrim_Reluctant_Steward.md), his tower [withered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_a_House), and several other annoyances related to [Ash Spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md), master Telvanni wizard [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md) finally loses his patience when three Ash Spawn attack his [tower](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md). Once you have killed the Ash Spawn, enter the tower and talk to the wizard who will reveal it is finally time to investigate who is behind the attacks: *"Yes. I have enemies. Legions of them back in Morrowind. However, in this case I have reason to believe it is someone in Solstheim."* When asked if he wants the responsible persons dead, he will respond: *"Not yet. That may come later. First I need to identify the culprit. I've enchanted this ring to find the source of the attacks. Here, take it."* He will then hand you the [Ring of Tracking](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Neloth%27s_Ring_of_Tracking) and explain that you need to wear it in order for it to work. The ring was enchanted with a bit of ash from the last Ash Spawn attack and will allow you to follow the magical link back to the source of the summoned Ash Spawn. However, it has its disadvantages: *"The link is weak, so you'll have to be within about a hundred feet of the source."* Neloth will then order you to walk around the grounds while wearing the ring. Equip the ring and leave the tower behind.

### From Beyond the Grave
Once outside, head north to the field of mushrooms and then east towards a lone evergreen tree at the shore, until you reach [a small graveyard](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=81000&y=28000) inhabited by a peaceful [netch](Skyrim_Netch.md) family. With the ring equipped, one of the coffins will have a clear bluish glow. As you approach the graveyard, the expected Ash Spawn will appear and attack, so fight it and investigate Ildari's sarcophagus. The coffin lacks a dead body, and contains a [staff of calm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Calm) and a [heart stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone). Grab the stone and return to Neloth in the tower.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-npc-Neloth_05.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Neloth_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Neloth_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Neloth_05.jpg) *"She lives. Seek her in Highpoint Tower."* If you have already played through [March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead), the name Ildari should be familiar, due to the [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari%27s_Journal) you [found](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead#Falx_Carius) when you helped [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) with his Ash Spawn problems in [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md). When you hand the stone to Neloth, he will be quite surprised and reveal [Ildari Sarothril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari_Sarothril) to be a former apprentice of his: *"Ildari? Then it's my own fault. She was my apprentice before Talvas. She volunteered for one of my experiments involving heart stones. It was quite annoying when she died."* He will then elaborate on how she died: *"My necropsies of Spriggans led me to believe that a heart stone could be implanted in a person's chest, granting them great power. I was considering replacing my heart with a heart stone. Fortunately Ildari offered to let me operate on her first. Of course this was before your [briarheart expedition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Briarheart_Necropsy). Now I know that I did the surgery all wrong."* When you tell him that the sarcophagus was empty, he will be quick to put two and two together: *"That's impossible. Wait... unless she didn't actually die! The heart stone could have kept her right at the edge of life and death. Could it be? Could Ildari still be alive? Could she be my nemesis? Wait a moment while I cast a more specific divination."* Moments afterward, Neloth will cast a spell that will light up most of the tower, and will start to talk in a completely different voice, as if a spirit is talking through him: *"She lives. Seek her in Highpoint Tower."* Neloth will then return to normal and immediately order you to head to said tower, as he can't be bothered himself. The last thing he will say is: *"Go rip that thrice cursed heart stone from her chest."*

### Highpoint Tower
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-place-Highpoint_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Highpoint_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Highpoint_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Highpoint_Tower.jpg) [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md) [Highpoint Tower](Skyrim_Highpoint_Tower.md) is located north of Ildari's former hideout, [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md), southeast from [Brodir Grove](Skyrim_Brodir_Grove.md). Enter the tower via a tunnel on the north side; fight the guarding Ash Spawn and head up the stairs. Once inside, head down the spiral staircase. Two stories down, you will come across a table with *[Ildari's Journal, vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari%27s_Journal,_vol._I)*, where a weakened Ildari Sarothril describes how she was saved by some miners, and how she is planning her revenge on her former master back in Tel Mithryn. Continue down the stairs and head west until you reach a chamber, filled with egg sacks from various spiders. As you enter the chamber, beware of the [spellcaster trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spellcaster_Trap) to the north, as it can complicate your battle against the spiders considerably. Multiple paths are available here; the tunnel to the south leads to a huge spiders nest and some useful veins, while the tunnel with the spellcaster trap is the way towards your goal. At the first intersection, enter the room with the three Ash Spawn and pick the fight, then proceed to the north and remove the [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) from yet another spellcaster trap. In the next chamber, a [tripwire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripwire) triggers some [falling boulders](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Falling_Boulders). Use the [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md) if needed, and beware the [elemental rune](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elemental_Rune) in the center of the room. Head down the eastern path until you reach the rare sight of an elemental rune on the wall, with tunnels leading north and south. Head south until you reach a jail-like area with several cells.

The table in the center of the jail has *[Ildari's Journal, vol. II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari%27s_Journal,_vol._II)*, detailing how Ildari was able to survive Neloth's operation, and how her paranoia against the very people who saved her slowly increases due to the [heart stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_Stone) implemented in her chest. She also describes how she will keep a few of them as prisoners for future experiments. Before long, [Niyya](Skyrim_Niyya.md) will call to you from her cell. When you pick the lock, she will come out and thank you: *"Thank you for rescuing me. That witch was going to kill me soon, I just know it."* She will then tell you the background: *"We were digging in these mines when she found us. She was hurt so bad. We took pity on her and nursed her back to health. She repaid our kindness by attacking us in the middle of the night. Those of us that didn't die were made prisoners."* She will go on to explain how Ildari used her saviors for experiments and will angrily encourage you to make her suffer a painful death. She will then rush towards the exit, with plans to leave Solstheim for good.

### Kill the Witch
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-quest-Old_Friends_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Old_Friends_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends_05.jpg) An [Ash Guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md) is born Return to the elemental rune, head north and locate the passage to the east. A wooden ramp leads down while a wooden catwalk goes forward with a crate holding meat on top. Follow the catwalk to find two orichalcum veins to mine. Return to the ramp to proceed to the next chamber. In the next chamber is a glowing [sapphire geode](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_Geode) on the ground, while another can be reached by traversing up the wooden structure. There is another orichalcum ore veins on the other side of the room from the first geode and a gold ore vein near the wooden structure's ramp. Proceed forward in a northwestern direction until you reach a large chamber with a strange device in the middle. Before long, Ildari will speak from her spot above: *"You've gone far enough. Neloth is a fool to think he could send some low life to finish me off."* With these words, she will activate the device, creating an [Ash Guardian](Skyrim_Ash_Guardian.md). At this point, numerous Ash Spawn will emerge as well, so pick the tough battle and dispose of the Ash Guardian. With the area cleared, start traversing the woodworks to the southwest and explore the path thoroughly for some treasure and ingredients. Once you reach a ledge with a table and a random rare book, loot the shelves for various potions and other loot and read through *[Ildari's Journal, vol. III](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari%27s_Journal,_vol._III)*. In this final volume, Ildari describes how she patiently started getting her vengeance on Neloth, and how she is responsible for the death of [Varona Nelas](Skyrim_Varona_Nelas.md), and the withering of Tel Mithryn tower. Proceed to climb the woodworks and follow the tunnel leading southwest, killing all the Ash Spawn that emerge on the way.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-quest-Old_Friends_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Old_Friends_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Old_Friends_04.jpg) [Ildari Sarothril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari_Sarothril) Once you reach a broken-down wall, two paths are available. The path to the northeast leads to a dead end and an open chest with some minor loot while the adjacent path leads to a wooden bridge. Cross the bridge and follow the tunnel as it twists and turns until you reach the final chamber, the lair of [Ildari Sarothril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ildari_Sarothril). Ildari will be on the woodworks above and will have two [elemental traps](Skyrim_Traps.md#Elemental_Traps) shooting at you at all times. Dodge the magical attacks and locate the entrance to the southwest and follow the path as it leads you to the second floor. Ildari will now start attacking you. She is a powerful sorceress with several spells at her disposal, although she will mainly use her [staff of lightning bolts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Lightning_Bolts) to attack you. After the battle, Ildari will kneel down. With Ildari helpless, follow Neloth's orders and rip out the heart stone from her chest, forever ending her vengeful scheme. Once everything is quiet, claim a reward from the boss-leveled chest and search the shelves for jewelry, ingredients, and other loot. Activate the lever and watch the spikes disappear from the entrance to the southeast. Head up the stairs, to the very top of the tower, and pull the chain for a very quick escape route back to Solstheim.

### Reward
Back in Tel Mithryn, Neloth will be happy to see the end of his nemesis and will, for once, reward you accordingly: *"Well, well. You have proven to be a more valuable ally than I anticipated. I have a staff for you, but I would also like to make you a member of my household. A member of House Telvanni. No need to thank me. I know you are overwhelmed with gratitude."* He will then hand you a random, leveled staff and tell you that your new membership of the household means very little for the time being, although he will promise you a bed and a chest in his tower (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

## Notes
- Highpoint Tower is blocked by ash until this quest has been initiated.
- A complete set of [Skaal equipment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skaal_Boots) can be obtained in Highpoint Tower during this quest, atop the earthen archway in the tunnel past the alchemy lab area.
- If your Archery skill is good enough, you can kill Ildari while she is talking before the Ash Guardian is raised as she stands in the open on a platform beyond the summoning circle.
- Ildari can be pickpocketed if you're in stealth mode when you rip her heart out. The heart will be removed once you close the menu.

## Bugs
- Even though Neloth tells you he will keep a bed and chest for you, there is not a chest specifically for you and all of the beds are still owned. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.

- The bed in the Steward's House is unowned from the start, although [Varona Nelas](Skyrim_Varona_Nelas.md) (or later [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md)) usually sleeps in it.
- Neloth may fail to cast the more specific spell to find Ildari, so the quest marker remains as "Talk to Neloth". - Leaving and reentering Tel Mithryn may allow Neloth to cast the spell, so the quest can progress. Attacking Neloth may also work, although this will result in a bounty.
- If Neloth is in the enchanting room, push/bump/shove him into the main room.
- The animation for ripping out Ildari's heart may not activate. - Wait at least 10 seconds after you're given the prompt to rip Ildari's heart out
- The game may crash when attempting to rip out Ildari's heart. - Save your game near Ildari as a precautionary measure.
- If you have opened Ildari's sarcophagus prior to this quest, the heart stone may not be inside it when the quest begins, preventing you from completing it. - If you open the sarcophagus prior to the quest you will find a staff. The heart stone will be present once you have started the quest.
- The Ring of Tracking may lead you to an invisible object near Tel Mithryn instead of Ildari's sarcophagus. - Activating the sarcophagus will continue the quest normally.
- Upon putting Ildari in Bleedout, the player is given a perk that enables the prompt to "Rip Heart Out", and that adds a multiplier of 0.01 to the magnitude of Illusion effects on the player. This persists until the perk is removed upon killing Ildari. This notably makes the [Ring of Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Khajiit) and [Ring of the Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_the_Wind) very weak. <sup>**?**</sup> - The perk can be removed via `player.removeperk 0403d597`, it will be reacquired when Ildari is next put into Bleedout.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DLC2TT2 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DLC2TT2`.
