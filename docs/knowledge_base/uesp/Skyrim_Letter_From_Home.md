Letter From Home **by Emma** *A letter from a wife to a husband in the Blades*
---
My dearest Lorant,

I know that there is no power that will stop them from taking you away from me and into battle upon foreign fields, for I understand that this is your duty as captain of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md). As your wife, I should respect such things. Nevertheless, the fear that you may never return troubles me.

Know that I think of you always. We are bound together, in this life and in the next.

Your beloved,

Emma
