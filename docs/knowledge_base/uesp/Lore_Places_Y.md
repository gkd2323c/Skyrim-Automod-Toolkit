## Yasammidan
[Yasammidan](Lore_Yasammidan.md) (or **Yasammidin**) is a [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) located on an island at the far northeastern tip of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), east of [Ald Velothi](Lore_Ald_Velothi.md).

The ruins were once an ancient site of [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) worship dedicated to the Prince of Destruction, [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). Like many of the Daedric ruins of Vvardenfell, they were sealed off by the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) to prevent worship of the Daedra Lords.

## Yath
[Yath](Lore_Yath.md) (also spelled **Vathi**) is a large island due south of [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md) in the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago, between the [Azurian Sea](Lore_Azurian_Sea.md) and the [Sea of Pearls](Lore_Sea_of_Pearls.md). Yath is arid and rugged, with a spine of jagged mountains. The small island of [Bahia](Lore_Bahia.md) is its nearest neighbor. One known settlement is Makan, found on the west side of the island.

Yath is home to a breed of [snake](Lore_Snake.md) called the Yath Asp, which according to [the Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) are huge and have a big temper. They used to serve [Lefthanded Elves](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md) as pets, and Asp-Leather was fashioned by them into garments resistant to cuts and gouges. It was also known for its turquoise tilework and body decorations adorned with turquoise accents, the glint of steel, and the wings associated with the Warrior Wave.

## Yellow Road
The [Yellow Road](Lore_Yellow_Road.md) road runs north to south from the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) to [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) along the east side of [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md).

## Yeorth Burrowland
The [Yeorth Burrowland](Lore_Yeorth_Burrowland.md) was an area near [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) in southern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and was close by two notable sites from the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md), the [Ravennian Forest](Lore_Ravennian_Forest.md) and [Cryngaine Field](Lore_Cryngaine_Field.md). The burrowland is where the armies of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) encamped after the battle of [Reich Gradkeep](Lore_Reich_Gradkeep.md) and before the [Battle of Cryngaine Field](Lore_Battle_of_Cryngaine_Field.md).

The 18th of [Morning Star](Lore_Morning_Star.md) was known as the Waking Day, when the people of the Yeorth Burrowland woke the spirits of nature after the winter, somewhat like their more reverential ancestors, in one of the oldest holidays in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).

## Y'ffelon
[Y'ffelon](Lore_Y%27ffelon.md), nicknamed the **Forbidden Island**, is a volcanic island of [the Systres](Lore_The_Systres.md) archipelago, north of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) and east of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md). [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) is found to the east, separated by the [Amenos Passage](Lore_Amenos_Passage.md). Y'ffelon is found in the center of the archipelago, and is dominated by the sacred volcano of [Mount Firesong](Lore_Mount_Firesong.md), whose roots run all through the Systres. The entire archipelago is volcanic, with many steam vents, hot springs, and other such features found in the island's back country, and tapping into the earth-energies of the island can lend immense magical power. The [Druids of Galen](Lore_Druids_of_Galen.md) were able to instill magic on Y'ffelon to balance nature and bring life to the land.

## Ykalon
[Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) is an area in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, before the Warp in the West it was a fiefdom, but then became a part of the Kingdom of Daggerfall after the Warp. It is surrounded by [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md) to the east, [Urvaius](Lore_Urvaius.md), [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md), and the [Ilessan Hills](Lore_Ilessan_Hills.md) to the south, and [Northmoor](Lore_Northmoor.md) to the west. Its capital was the city of Ykalon. [Castle Llugwych](Lore_Castle_Llugwych.md), one of the headquarters of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) is located in this region. The [Plessington Clan](Lore_Plessington_Clan.md) is a noble family of [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) from Ykalon that led a knightly order called the [Order of the Cup](Lore_Order_of_the_Cup.md). In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Lord Plessington led the order and sent them off to right a wrong and come back with a story or two.

[Thrafey](Lore_Thrafey.md) vampires are known to stalk prey here, and the regional deity is [Mara](Lore_Mara.md). Forces from Ykalon participated in the [Battle of Firewaves](Lore_Battle_of_Firewaves.md) which brought about the defeat of the [Camoran Usurper](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md). Perseverance Day is a festival held every year by the people of Ykalon during [Sun's Dawn](Lore_Sun%27s_Dawn.md), though it was originally a solemn day of memorial for those killed in the battle.

## Yneslea
[Yneslea](Lore_Yneslea.md) is a region of land that lies somewhere between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). It was conquered by [Uriel Septim V](Lore_Uriel_Septim_V.md) in [3E 279](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_279) during the opening stages of his campaign against the Dragon Land. By [3E 288](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_288), unrest had broken out, causing the emperor to divert two legions that had been intended as reinforcements to pacify the inhabitants instead, ultimately contributing to the failure of the invasion.

## Yngol Barrow
[Yngol Barrow](Lore_Yngol_Barrow.md) (also called the **Barrow of Yngol**) is an ancient [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) ruin found in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), in the region of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md). It is known as the burial ground of [Yngol](Lore_Yngol.md), the eldest son of the Harbinger, [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md). In some sources, Yngol Barrow is found below the cliffs of [Hsaarik Head](Lore_Hsaarik_Head.md), though an entrance can be found along the [White River](Lore_White_River.md). It is constructed in the [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md) tradition.

## Yokuda
[Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md) is a continent west of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) which partially sank into the sea during the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). It was the ancestral home of the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), as well as an ancient race of [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) known as the [Lefthanded Elves](Lore_Lefthanded_Elves.md). It was the birthplace of several famed Redguard heroes, including [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) and his son [Divad](Lore_Divad_Hunding.md). It was also the nation that birthed the fabled martial tradition of [sword-singing](Lore_Sword-singer.md). The native language of Yokuda was called [Yoku](Lore_Yoku.md), but after the Yokudan migration to [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808), it was largely replaced to help stabilize foreign trade.

## Lake Yorgrim
[Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md) is a lake in the eastern [Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md), to the west of [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md). It is the source of [River Yorgrim](Lore_River_Yorgrim.md). In or around the time of the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Viinturuth](Lore_Viinturuth.md) was killed and then interred in a burial mound near the shores of the lake.

## River Yorgrim
The [River Yorgrim](Lore_River_Yorgrim.md) (also called the **Yorgrim River**) is a short tributary in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) which connects [Lake Yorgrim](Lore_Lake_Yorgrim.md) to the [White River](Lore_White_River.md). It flows east out of the eponymous lake in [the Pale](Lore_The_Pale.md) and enters [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md), dropping through a series of waterfalls and rapids until it merges with the White River near the entrance to [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md). A [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) burial mound is located near the river, and superstition about nearby caves leaves many locals reticent to swim in its waters.

## Ysgramor's Teeth
[Ysgramor's Teeth](Lore_Ysgramor%27s_Teeth.md) is a mountain range in southeast [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) between the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md) and the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md). This range marks the border between [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) and [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md), and it is because of this range that the Rift can maintain its temperate climate and avoid the cold winds of the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). The main road runs along the base of the mountain and branches separately into either a pass that leads into [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md), or a sharp incline past Snapleg Cave and toward [Lake Geir](Lore_Lake_Geir.md). There is a series of uncharted dirt roads on the western alps that come together at the north end of [Ivarstead](Lore_Ivarstead.md).

## Yrinthi River
The [Yrinthi River](Lore_Yrinthi_River.md) is seemingly a major waterway, accessible by ship from the ports of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) in the heartland of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It flows westward across the continent before eventually emptying into the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md). Riding across the entire river from start to end takes a number of weeks.
