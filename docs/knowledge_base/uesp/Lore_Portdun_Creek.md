[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/AR-place-Portdun_Creek.jpg/200px-AR-place-Portdun_Creek.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Portdun_Creek.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Portdun_Creek.jpg) Portdun Creek during the Imperial Simulacrum Portdun Creek (formerly known as **Portdun Watch**) is a town found in [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md), a region of the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It was originally an outwork built for Firebrand Keep, the enclave of the [Knights of the Flame](Lore_Knights_of_the_Flame.md). It protected the road between the city of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) and the castle-town of [Alcaire Castle](Lore_Alcaire_Castle.md), but it was later abandoned.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Watch-1)</sup> However, it eventually became a prominent settlement.

## History
Portdun Watch was built as an outwork of Firebrand Keep and served to protect the road between Wayrest and the castle-town of Alcaire. At one point, the tower was abandoned,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Watch-1)</sup> and it became a bandit hideout that attacked passing caravans.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPortdun-2)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Portdun Creek was an active settlement. It was ruled by Baroness Belladabyth and had a rivalry with [Old Gate](Lore_Old_Gate.md) and Vermeir Wastes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Markwasten Moor](Lore_Markwasten_Moor_(settlement).md) to the northwest and [Norvulk Hills](Lore_Norvulk_Hills.md) to the south.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Map Arena-4)</sup>
