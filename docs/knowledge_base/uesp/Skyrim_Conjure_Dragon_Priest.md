**Conjure Dragon Priest** is an expert level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a spectral [Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) to aid you in combat. It cannot be learned by the player through normal means, but it is one of the spells that may be cast by the [Konahrik](Skyrim_Konahrik.md) mask when the wearer is badly damaged.

## Effects
- [Conjure](Skyrim_Summon.md) [Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increases range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Atromancy](Skyrim_Atromancy.md), extends duration to 120 secs.
