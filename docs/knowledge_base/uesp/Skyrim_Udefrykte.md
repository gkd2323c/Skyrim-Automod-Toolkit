[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-creature-Udefrykte.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Udefrykte.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Udefrykte.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Udefrykte.jpg) The Udefrykte The **Udefrykte** is a unique [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md) found in the ice cave portion of [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) during the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [The Cure for Madness](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md).

The Udefrykte has a set level of 22; therefore, it may be advisable for lower-level characters to sneak past it. If you plan on taking on the Udefrykte, strong [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) spells, scrolls, or a weapon with a strong fire enchantment are recommended. Like all other trolls, it has the ability to regenerate health over time, so it is better to kill it as quickly as possible without giving it a chance to heal significantly.
