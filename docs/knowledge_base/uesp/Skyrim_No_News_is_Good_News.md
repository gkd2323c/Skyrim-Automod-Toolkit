## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Angeline Morrard](Skyrim_Angeline_Morrard.md) in [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md).
2. Get some news from [Captain Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md).
3. Return to Angeline Morrard.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Mother's Request
[Angeline Morrard](Skyrim_Angeline_Morrard.md) is the shopkeeper of [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). In your conversation with her, she will ask you if you have been to [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). If you tell her that you haven't been there before, she will be understanding and you can talk to her about it later. If you tell her that you have indeed been to Whiterun, she will become very animated. It seems her daughter, Fura, was assigned to Whiterun after she joined the [Imperial Army](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and Angeline was hoping to get some recent news about her. Although Angeline has tried contacting [Captain Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md), she didn't get any information from him. If you are willing to help her get more information about her daughter, anything you can find out would be welcome.

### A Need To Know Basis
Captain Aldis can be found training the guards in the courtyard of [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) during the day. When you tell him that Angeline sent you for information about her daughter, Aldis will respond, *"Generally posting information is need to know only"*. Several options are available for you to continue the conversation, including persuasion and intimidation. You must pass an easy [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade him to reveal Fura's fate.

### The Casualty List
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-quest-No_News_is_Good_News_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_News_is_Good_News_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_News_is_Good_News_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_News_is_Good_News_02.jpg) *"She needs to be told."* The captain will then give you the news: *"Her daughter was sent to Whiterun, the skirmishes there have been violent. The whiterun * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * legate, he needed to know the Stormcloak positions. He sent out a party to scout the area. They happened upon a large force... I'm told none of them made it back. Private Morrard... Angela's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * daughter was in that scouting party. She needs to be told"*.

### The Bearer of Bad News
Return to Angeline Morrard with the news. Angeline is eager to hear about her daughter. She is understandably grief-stricken when you tell her the bad news: *"I told her. I told her the imperial * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * army would kill her. That this was a war of Jarls and Imperial Generals and we had no part in it. She just wouldn't hear it"*. Angeline can't thank you for the news, *"not for this"*, but she acknowledges that you have done her a service. You get no reward from her, but she will have a more friendly [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you and allow you to freely take many low-value items in her shop.

## Bugs
- If you have already accepted this quest before speaking with [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) he will not offer his favor quest, [Delivery (Sorex)](Skyrim_Delivery_(Sorex).md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- If you have completed the Stormcloak missions, especially [Battle for Solitude](Skyrim_Battle_for_Solitude.md), before speaking with Aldis, this quest will be impossible to finish as it would lead you to a bare area where Aldis once stood. Because of this, it is highly recommended this quest is done before finishing the Stormcloak missions. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can also be fixed by entering the following in the console: `setstage Solitude Freeform01 20`. This will set the stage of the quest to where you have to return to Angeline, and will allow you to complete the quest.
- Captain Aldis will incorrectly refer to female characters as "sir" when he is asked about Angeline's daughter. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- You can order Captain Aldis to give you the information even if you're not of the proper rank in the Imperial Legion, or if you're not a member of the Legion at all. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- You can exit the dialogue with Captain Aldis right after Persuade/Intimidate check before advancing quest stage. Therefore it can be repeated for leveling Speech up.
