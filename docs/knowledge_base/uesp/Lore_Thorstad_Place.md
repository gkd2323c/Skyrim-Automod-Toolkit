## History
The earliest mentions of Thorstad Place date it to the mid- [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), when it marked the westernmost point of [Verkarth](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md)'s kingdom. The [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) laid claim to Thorstad Place and everything in between it and the eastern city, [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md). Their power brought the attention of Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md) of the [First Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), who launched a large-scale invasion on the Gray Host. Their conflict culminated in a siege on the [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md), where the Gray Host was defeated. After their capital was razed to the ground, all of central Hammerfell and Thorstad Place was liberated.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGHAH1-3)</sup> Pots from Thorstad contained the remains of the Gray Host and were instrumental in their brief revival in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BP-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Thorstad Place was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lady Lhovim-e and it had a rivalry with [Riverview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riverview) and Verkarth City.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Verkarth City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Verkarth_City) to the east, [Vulnim Gate](Lore_Vulnim_Gate.md) to the south,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup> and [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) to the north, toward [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap HR-5)</sup>
