# Reaver

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/BM-npc-Reaver_Group.jpg/200px-BM-npc-Reaver_Group.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-npc-Reaver_Group.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BM-npc-Reaver_Group.jpg) A reaver raiding party (Bloodmoon) Reaver is a term used by the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) to refer to out-islanders, people who inhabit the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) and raid the northern coasts of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). These raiders spend most of their time at sea and generally target one-hut settlements and hunting camps, dropping off small raiding parties rather than beaching their ships for fear that their vessels would be stolen or burned.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The strongest warriors in sea reaver groups are considered [barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWDesc-2)</sup>

Reavers were a common sight on Solstheim both before and after the events of the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Many [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) reavers are [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) in origin.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PRIMA-5)</sup><sup>:186</sup>

## Notes
- The term "reaver" is used in other contexts as well. It usually refers to berserkers or individuals who have gone feral.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
- Not all Nordic reavers confined their activities to the northern islands. Certain tales recount the exploits of reavers like Ulgar Stonebeard, who opted to plunder the Reach instead.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ST-10)</sup>
