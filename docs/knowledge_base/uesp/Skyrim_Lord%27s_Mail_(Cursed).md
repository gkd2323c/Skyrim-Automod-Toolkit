[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Lord%27s_Mail(m)_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Lord%27s_Mail(m)_02.png) Lord's Mail (male) The **Lord's Mail (Cursed)** is an artifact created by [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md). It is [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md) and reduces the maximum health, stamina and magicka of the wearer. It counts as [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md) for the purpose of the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk. It is part of the quest [Gift of Kynareth](Skyrim_Gift_of_Kynareth.md).
