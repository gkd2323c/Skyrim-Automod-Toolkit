[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Mace.jpg/200px-SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Mace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Mace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Lunar_Steel_Mace.jpg) A Lunar Steel Mace The Lunar Steel Mace is a special [steel mace](Skyrim_Steel_Mace.md) that can be occasionally found at [Silent Moons Camp](Skyrim_Silent_Moons_Camp.md). Its enchantment causes [light damage](Skyrim_Light_Damage.md) over one second on whoever is struck by the mace. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Lunar Steel Mace requires one [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the tempering being twice as effective if you have gained the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk.
