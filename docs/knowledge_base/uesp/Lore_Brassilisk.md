Brassilisks are [fabricants](Lore_Fabricants.md) resembling [snakes](Lore_Snake.md). They are created from interconnected metallic squares, with exposed copper wiring that allow for their snakelike movements.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONBrassilisk-1)</sup> Some are created with wings to mimic the [Sep Adder](Lore_Sep_Adder.md). They are a popular choice as pets among the denizens of the Clockwork City. They can be powered by either soul gems, animo cores, or steam power.

Several variants of the Brassilisk are known to exist. The winged **Ebony Brassilisk** is an experimental black variation with certain key components fabricated from an [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md), alloy for enhanced durability out in the storms of the Radius.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONEbony Brassilisk-2)</sup> The **Foxbat Brassilisk** is a mysterious winged green variant of unknown origins found in the Radius; it is suspected that they are assembled by a rogue Incarnatorium somewhere out in the Radius.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONFoxbat Brassilisk-3)</sup> The rare winged **Psijic Brassilisks** glow blue as a result of being powered by blue [soul gems](Lore_Soul_Gem.md), brought back from [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) at the end of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) by Sotha Sil himself.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONPsijic Brassilisk-4)</sup> The red **Steam-Driven Brassilisk** is an experimental winged variant powered by steam stored under high pressure, which was invented by the [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostles.md) due to soul gems being in short supply.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONSteam-Driven Brassilisk-5)</sup>
