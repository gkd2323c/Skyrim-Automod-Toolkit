# Keys

| --- |

This article lists all **keys** in the game, including information on where the key is found and what door or chest it opens. Unless noted, all keys have a weight and value of 0.

The [Wax Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wax_Key) perk automatically gives you a copy of any of the pickable locks listed below, the first time you pick it after taking the perk. Keys listed as unobtainable may be obtained in this fashion. Conversely, some keys may be unobtainable with the Wax Key perk because their doors are never locked.

### Notes
Abbreviations in superscript give additional information about people, keys or locks:

- <sup>QI</sup> (Key Name): The key is a quest item; it can't be dropped. Most keys lose this property after their associated quest is solved (though sometimes they are removed from your inventory instead).
- <sup>OD</sup> (Found): Obtainable after death; can only be acquired after the actor has been killed.
- <sup>NP</sup> (Found): You cannot pickpocket this key, you must receive it through dialogue with the person.
- <sup>NF</sup> (Found/Opens): This person or location is not currently found in-game.
- <sup>EF</sup> (Opens): Being detected while opening this lock may get you kicked out of a faction.
- <sup>KR</sup> (Opens): The lock is not pickable with a lockpick. You will need the actual key to open this lock.
- <sup>USKP</sup> (Bugs): [![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Computer.svg) This bug is fixed by the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch).

## Notes
- The [Skeleton Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skeleton_Key) functions as a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) rather than as a key, and as such is not included here.
- You cannot remove keys from your inventory by dropping them, but you can store them in a container or corpse if you wish.
- Some doors and containers that have keys can be picked. Others that say "Requires Key" cannot. - If you attempt to pick a door or container which requires a key that isn't in your inventory, a "This lock cannot be picked. It requires a key to open." message will appear on the screen.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
