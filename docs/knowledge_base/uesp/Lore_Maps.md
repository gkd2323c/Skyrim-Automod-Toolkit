## Tamriel

### Anthology Map
[![MER-art-Arena Anthology Game Map.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg/400px-MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) The most recent and authoritative map of Tamriel, from *[The Elder Scrolls Anthology](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls_Anthology)*.

### Oblivion Codex Map
[![LO-map-Tamriel (Oblivion Codex Compilation).jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/LO-map-Tamriel_%28Oblivion_Codex_Compilation%29.jpg/400px-LO-map-Tamriel_%28Oblivion_Codex_Compilation%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Tamriel_(Oblivion_Codex_Compilation).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Tamriel_(Oblivion_Codex_Compilation).jpg) A compilation of the province maps in *The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion*'s update to the Official Codex.

### Second Era Tamriel
[![ON-map-Tamriel.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/ON-map-Tamriel.jpg/400px-ON-map-Tamriel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Tamriel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Tamriel.jpg) Map of Tamriel in the Second Era, showing the three alliances and Imperial territory, as seen in *The Elder Scrolls Online*.

### Arena Map of Tamriel
[![AR-map-Arena (small).png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/AR-map-Arena_%28small%29.png/400px-AR-map-Arena_%28small%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Arena_(small).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Arena_(small).png) The complete map of Tamriel as seen in the first Elder Scrolls game, [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena). Arena is the only game that has been set in all of Tamriel. For more detailed maps of the individual provinces, see [this page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Maps).

### West Tamriel
[![RG-map-West Tamriel-1024x768.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/400px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) A map of West Tamriel, attributed to one of Bethesda's developers (Hugh Riley). Part of this map is shown on the cover of the game [Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard), although it was not used for gameplay.

### High Rock, Hammerfell and Skyrim Border
[![Shadowkey Map.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/Shadowkey_Map.jpg/400px-Shadowkey_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Shadowkey_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Shadowkey_Map.jpg) A map of northeast Hammerfell showing the borders of Skyrim and High Rock, as seen in the game [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Shadowkey).
