# Trading Cards


After completing a set of cards, the option to craft a badge commemorating the feat will become available. Alongside the badge, you will receive experience points to boost your Steam Account Level, an emoticon of one of the [cities](Skyrim_Cities.md) map emblems that can be used in chat or in discussions, a random profile background image themed around Skyrim that you can use on your Steam community profile, and a chance at receiving a coupon for a different game or DLC. There are five badge levels with increasing amounts of rewarded experience, requiring up to five complete sets. There is also a sixth badge for collecting a full set of foil cards.

The Summer Getaway Sale card is included in a separate set and cannot be received in-game. It was part of the Steam Summer Sale 2013 event, along with the related rewards. The "Cozy Cottage" [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md) items are similarly not available in-game, and were released as part of the Steam Winter Sale 2018 event.

The [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) and [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) do not have trading card support.
