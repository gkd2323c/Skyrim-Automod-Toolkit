**Queen** Afsar was a powerful [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) monarch who ruled [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) in the Late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). She was remembered as one of the people who changed the course of Redguard history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.jpg) Tu'whacca's Throne Little is known about Afsar's life. During the late First Era, Hammerfell faced a succession crisis following the death of its ruling king without a clear heir, leading to nobles vying for the throne. Afsar asserted her authority through traditional means, by eliminating her rivals. Despite the violent beginnings, her reign was notably peaceful.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-QA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RA-3)</sup>

Following her demise, she was laid to rest in [Tu'whacca's Throne](Lore_Tu%27whacca%27s_Throne.md) in Alik'r. According to [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) scholars, alongside Armiger [Rhina](Lore_Rhina.md) and Yokeda [Razul](Lore_Razul.md), she was among the most powerful and dangerous [mummies](Lore_Mummy.md) buried there. During the Withered Hand crisis in Alik'r in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), she was one of the many risen dead, but she was eventually returned to rest thanks to the efforts of the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) and an adventurer who aided them at their task.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ramati-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-QA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RA-3)</sup>
