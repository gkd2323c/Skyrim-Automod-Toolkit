# Atronite

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/BL-item-Atronite.jpg/200px-BL-item-Atronite.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Atronite.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Atronite.jpg) Atronite (Blades) **Atronite** is a mysterious stone from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). It is often left behind on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) when a powerful [Atronach](Lore_Elemental_Daedra.md) is defeated or dispelled.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades-1)</sup>

Atronite is used by [smiths](Lore_Smithing.md) for jewelry crafting and for tempering high-end weapons and armor. It is a rare and valuable gemstone, popular and valued by the nobility as a sign of strength and power. In appearance, it is a multihued [crystal](Lore_Crystals.md) that emits an eerie sheen. Its coloration can include shades of blue, purple, and red.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades-1)</sup>

Shards of Atronite have been used as an exotic form of [currency](Lore_Currency.md#Atronite) on occasion. [Odar's kingdom](Lore_Hero.md#Dynasty_of_Odar_the_Brave) was known to use Atronite to trade for rare goods.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
