[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-npc-Luaffyn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Luaffyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Luaffyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Luaffyn.jpg) Luaffyn **Luaffyn**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [rogue](Skyrim_Rogue.md), is a bard who can be found in [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

She does very little in life, except [performing](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) for the regulars at Candlehearth Hall, always standing in the same corner in the upside area of the tavern. She never eats or sleeps.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Notes
- Despite being a Dark Elf at the inn where they generally aren't welcome, none of the patrons will ever mention her in any capacity. In fact, no one in the entire city will acknowledge her existence, in dialogue or otherwise.
