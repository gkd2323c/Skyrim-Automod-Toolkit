## Quick Walkthrough
1. Supply specific fish to random innkeepers

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is a repetitive quest. [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md) will ask you to supply certain types of fish to randomly chosen innkeepers ([Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) being a possible destination). The quest can be completed four times.
