## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get the contract from [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md).
2. Kill [Helvard](Skyrim_Helvard.md).
3. Return to [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Once you have spoken to Nazir you will have found out that [Helvard](Skyrim_Helvard.md) is the Housecarl to the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). You may find Helvard in the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Falkreath).md) in Falkreath.

If you approach Helvard before attacking him, you will be given 3 dialogue options, you can only pick one as you won't be able to speak to him again.

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *"You protect the Jarl. But who protects you?"* | *"And why would I... Oh. Oh! Guards! Guards, I need assistance!"* | Turns hostile and attacks |
| *"The Dark Brotherhood has come! "* | *"Fiend! You will not leave here alive! Guards! Guards!"* | Nobody will come, but Helvard turns hostile and attacks |
| *(remain silent)* | *"Right. Well. If you have some kind of official business with the jarl, I'm afraid now's not the time. Go on then. Off with you."* | Walks away |

An alternative is to use [stealth](Skyrim_Stealth.md) and kill Helvard while he sleeps in the Jarl's Longhouse.

## Bugs
- Completing this quest may make it impossible to purchase [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> from [Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.

- If [Dengeir of Stuhn](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md) becomes or already is jarl, you may still purchase the property.
- You may also still purchase property if you complete Siddgeir's quests after killing Helvard, or exile him in [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and later retake [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) for him.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can inspect the [relationship rank](Skyrim_Disposition.md) with the jarl by using the `player.getrelationshiprank` console command (normally you should get -2 foe, or worse) likewise you may set a good relation (positive, max 4) with the jarl by using this `player.setrelationshiprank 19822 4` command. A relationship rank of 3 or better will allow you to purchase property.
