[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/71/SR-npc-Karita_%28warrior%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Karita_%28warrior%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karita_(warrior).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Karita_(warrior).jpg) Karita **Karita** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md), and a pilgrim encountered in the 7000 steps that lead to [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md). She is sitting and meditating in front of the fourth etched tablet.

She wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md), [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), [steel cuffed boots](Skyrim_Steel_Cuffed_Boots.md), a [gold and emerald circlet](Skyrim_Gold_and_Emerald_Circlet.md), an [amulet of Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md), and a [leveled](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) of fortify one-handed. She also carries two books about the ancient High King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md): *[Five Songs of King Wulfharth](Skyrim_Five_Songs_of_King_Wulfharth.md)* and *[The Arcturian Heresy](Skyrim_The_Arcturian_Heresy.md)*.

## Related Quests
- **[The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md)**: Meet the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md).

## Dialogue
She may greet you with:

- *"Keep an eye out for wolves if you're headed up the path to High Hrothgar."*

You may ask her who she is, to which she will reply:

- *"Just a pilgrim. I'd prefer to leave it at that, if you don't mind."*

You may also ask her if she heard the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) calling the [Dovahkiin](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md):

- *"I was just outside Ivarstead when it happened. It's an exciting moment. Nothing like this has happened in centuries."*

If you ask her what she's doing on the path to High Hrothgar, she will tell you.

- *"Walking the Steps. Meditating on the emblems. I make this trip every few years."*

## Notes
- Karita is only enabled once The Way of the Voice begins. When you reach High Hrothgar, she's disabled again.
