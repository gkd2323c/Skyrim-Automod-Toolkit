## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The exterior of the mine is the Orc stronghold of Mor Khazgur. The path leading up to the entrance passes the stronghold's [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) on the left and a wooden lookout platform on the right. There are two [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and three [orichalcum ingots](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ingot.md) immediately outside the entrance in an open-sided shelter.

### Mor Khazgur Mine
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/ba/SR-interior-Gloombound_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Gloombound_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gloombound_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Gloombound_Mine.jpg) Mine interior The entrance leads into a passage descending to the north, that leads into a large chamber with wooden scaffolding along the north and east walls and wooden planking shoring up the other two walls. Directly in front of the scaffolding is a long wooden table holding two orichalcum ingots, a piece of [orichalcum ore](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ore.md) (which is free to take if you are [blood-kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs), and a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md). There are many pickaxes and several empty carts found in the mine. To the left of the table is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing minor loot. Climbing the ramp to the left to the first level leads to two orichlcum ore veins on the left. There is a basket containing a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) beside the first of these. Between the two veins is a passage heading down to the north, with two barrels of [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) at the top, on the left. At the bottom are two more ore veins. Returning to the first ledge, along the front edge of the ledge are a couple of stacks of crates, barrels, two of sacks of foodstuffs, a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) leaning against one crate and a loose piece of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) beside another.

In the main chamber there is a second ramp leading up from the first ledge heading east to a higher level. There is an ore vein on your left on the wall with a bedroll underneath it, a second ore vein on the wall to the east with a small wooden table in front, holding a bottle of alto wine, a bottle of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), two portions of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), a [seared slaughterfish](Skyrim_Seared_Slaughterfish.md) steak, and some bread and cheese. For the final ore vein; from the ground floor of the mine, there is a short tunnel to the west with an ore vein at the end.
