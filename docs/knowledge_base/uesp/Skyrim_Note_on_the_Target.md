Note on the Target *A mask carrier is revealed*
---
My sources tell me the courier in charge of carrying the mask is a Redoran Guard.

It seems, however, our guard has plans of his own for the mask. He's arranged a meeting with a buyer after midnight, in the cellar of the Old Attius Farm.

His greed angers the Goddess. Dispose of them both and return the artifact to her rightful hands.
