[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whiterun_02.jpg) Whiterun Jsashe was a [witch](Lore_Witches.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and a self-proclaimed priestess of [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md). When a series of natural disasters devastated [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), many locals blamed Jsashe. But soon after, they exalted her and she was named the "Witch-Queen of Whiterun" and wielded effective control of the [county](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md). Despite that, her [magic](Lore_Magic.md) had yet to provide prosperity to the land in [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
