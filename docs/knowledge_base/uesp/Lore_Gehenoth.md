*I must also warn you that outside these walls is a fearsome creature that has slaughtered any who fight it. It appears when you rest. Do not fight it, you are not ready. Run.*
—Eustacia<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Eus-1)</sup> The **Gehenoth** is a monstrous creature that was known to attack those who rested beyond the walls of [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md) circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Eus-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Its attacks are capable of afflicting people with [Frost Limbs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Frost_Limbs), a debilitating affliction that is especially harmful to spellcasters.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gehenoth-3)</sup> As the **Gehenoth Thriceborn**, it fought alongside the forces of the [Ice Tribes](Lore_Ice_Tribes.md) and allied creatures during their final assault on the town. Its defeat at the hands of the [Hero of Dawnstar](Lore_Hero_of_Dawnstar.md) marked the end of the conflict.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

In appearance the creature is bipedal and covered in spikes and plating. It has piercing blue eyes that became red when Thriceborn.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gehenoth-3)</sup>

## Notes
- It is possible for the Hero of Dawnstar to kill the Gehenoth before it appears as the Gehenoth Thriceborn, with it potentially dropping a Keenblood Blade, a powerful longsword.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Gehenoth-3)</sup> It is unknown if this means there are in-fact multiple Gehenoths or if it somehow can be revived.
- The strongest warriors of Ice Tribes are also sometimes known as Thriceborn.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The relation (if any) this title has to the concept of [reincarnation](Lore_Reincarnation.md) is unknown.
