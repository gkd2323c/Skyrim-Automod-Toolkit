## History
During the height of [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) rule, Sercen was a settlement infamous for brutalizing [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) slaves. Legends speak of "the gut-gardens of Sercen", one of many examples of "art-torture" that slaves were subjected to for the Ayleids' pleasure.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup> Following the fall of the Ayleids, Sercen was sealed shut and fell into ruins.

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a band of mercenaries led by a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) named Rasha laid claim to the loot within the ruins, and set up camp outside while attempting to unseal the entrance.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Rasha-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SOS-5)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins had been opened and were occupied by marauders and bandits.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Oblivion-1)</sup>
