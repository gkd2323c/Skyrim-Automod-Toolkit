## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to the [Shrine of Azura](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Azura.md).
2. Speak with [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md).
3. Go to [Ilinalta's Deep](Skyrim_Ilinalta%27s_Deep.md).
4. Claim the [Broken Star](Skyrim_Broken_Azura%27s_Star.md).
5. Give the star to [Aranea](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md) or [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md).
6. Cleanse the star from within.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Twilight of the Future
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-quest-The_Black_Star_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Black_Star_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_02.jpg) [Aranea Ienith](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md) All over Skyrim, innkeepers will readily tell you about the Shrine of Azura, a monument built by the [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) after they fled Morrowind. Additionally, an [encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) with the wandering Dunmer [Faldrus](Skyrim_Faldrus.md) will point you in the same direction, so make your way to the shrine; it is located in the snowy mountains south of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). The gigantic statue faces south atop a mountain and a long staircase. The only remaining worshipper at the Shrine, the mage [Aranea Ienith](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md), will claim that your arrival was expected, as Azura herself foresaw it and transferred the knowledge to her.

The prediction involves a "fortress endangered by water, yet untouched by it" and finding an "elven mage able to turn the brightest star as black as night". Aranea will admit her description to be more cryptic than useful and will direct you to the snowy town of Winterhold for clues. In Winterhold, numerous people can help, directing you to talk to a mage named [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md).

### The Black Star
Nelacar will provide the information that you seek, while reluctant at first, thinking you another applicant for the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). If you reveal yourself as an agent of Azura, you will have to pass an easy-level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) or he will dismiss you outright. He will not say no to a bit of coin either, and will break down if you intimidate him (see [bugs section](#Bugs)).

Either way, he will quickly ask you what you know about [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) and provide a quick introduction, stating that they all break after use, except for one. He will then tell you the story of one [Malyn Varen](Skyrim_Malyn_Varen.md), a former teacher at the College, who examined the Daedric artifact [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md) along with a few colleagues and students. Nobody knew that Malyn was actually ill and dying and was using the Star to save himself. But by trying to make himself immortal, he lost his sanity in the process. One day, the experiment escalated to the point where Malyn killed a student and attempted to use her soul for research and, as a result, was banished from the College. He, along with a few loyal disciples, moved to the secluded depths of [Ilinalta's Deep](Skyrim_Ilinalta%27s_Deep.md). Nelacar will finish with a plea that you don't return the Star to Azura. His request will be left hanging, as you can do nothing but travel to Ilinalta's Deep and search for Malyn and the Star.

### Ilinalta's Deep
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-interior-Ilinalta%27s_Deep.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ilinalta%27s_Deep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ilinalta%27s_Deep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ilinalta%27s_Deep.jpg) A fisherman without a soul What turned Ilinalta into Ilinalta's Deep remains a mystery, as the entire fort suddenly sank into [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md) with only the rooftops sticking out of the water. According to local legend, the place is cursed and travelers are known to go missing. Upon entering, the earthly remains of a Fisherman, his bones still in shackles, will welcome you. Examine his knapsack for a [journal](Skyrim_Fisherman%27s_Journal_(Ilinalta%27s_Deep).md) and some minor loot, then proceed forward and take a left at the waterfall. Not surprisingly, given the background story provided by Nelacar, the inhabitants include [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) and their [Undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) allies, so be on the lookout for rare ingredients and [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md). The fourth room, almost completely flooded, contains two necromancers and an underwater [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). Two water-soaked sleeping areas follow, with the second one containing the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Breathing Water](Skyrim_Breathing_Water.md)* (floating or on the ceiling, in a side room whose entrance is underwater), and the last containing the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) skill book *[A Tragedy in Black](Skyrim_A_Tragedy_in_Black.md)*, an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md), and an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md), along with a locked door hiding minor loot. The next area includes a necromage, who will use frost spells, and a resurrected [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) on the other side of a pond. Dispose of the opposition, locate a nearby chest for some treasure, then pull the chain on the wall. This not only lowers the bridge behind you, it also provides access to the second zone further ahead. Stairs down the east wall into the water lead to a locked door, behind which potions and a chest are to be found. Take the western exit, and consider unlocking the door for a few potions. Proceed forward, remove the bar from the door and reenter the very first chamber of the zone, this time on a different level. You can now easily gain access to the second zone through the northern door, and a new bridge leading downstairs will make future exploration much easier.

### Malyn Varen
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-quest-The_Black_Star.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Black_Star.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star.jpg) The remains of [Malyn Varen](Skyrim_Malyn_Varen.md) Proceed carefully forward through the tunnel and you will overhear two necromancers having a conversation on how to find another soul for the insatiable Star:
*"More souls are needed for the Star. The last one died before he could be harvested."*
*"We can't take another villager from the surface so soon. I told you to prepare everything properly!"*
*"We can just sacrifice another disciple. Apprentice Haerlon will be no great waste."*
*"Yes. He'll do."*
Dispose of them and look around the area for a few [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md) and the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill book *[The Doors of Oblivion](Skyrim_The_Doors_of_Oblivion.md)*. A few potions can be found behind the eastern door, while the western tunnel leads to an imprisonment area guarded by a skeleton and yet another necromancer. In the final chamber, a boss-level necromancer will quickly reanimate three skeletons; after the fight, head up the stairs where you will find a dead bandit lying in her own blood, the earthly remains of [Malyn Varen](Skyrim_Malyn_Varen.md), along with his revealing *[Grimoire](Skyrim_Malyn_Varen%27s_Grimoire.md)*, a boss-leveled chest, and [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Broken_Azura%27s_Star.md), currently broken due to Malyn's experiments. Taking the Star on the floor next to the dead bandit has no consequences, other than a choice: either bring the Star to [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md) in Winterhold or to [Aranea](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md) at the Shrine.

### A Soul to Keep
If you return to the Shrine, you will speak to Azura. She will tell you that it is unusable until you have purged Varen's soul from the star. Likewise, Nelacar will tell you the same thing. The difference is the Star's use afterwards. If you bring it to Azura, you will be able to use it to trap any sized soul except for black souls (i.e., souls from [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md)). If you bring it to Nelacar, you will be able to trap all souls, including black, though Nelacar tells you that it can only trap black souls (see [bugs section](#Bugs)).

Malyn Varen now resides inside the Star, but, to stay alive, needs more and more souls, which were provided by his disciples until now. After a bit of examination, Nelacar will see the opportunity to finish what he and Malyn started all those years ago, and will suggest that you cleanse the Star. If you accept, he will trap your soul and send you into the Star to confront Malyn. | [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-quest-The_Black_Star_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Black_Star_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_05.jpg) Azura's Star **Aranea** Head back to the Shrine of Azura, and an eager Aranea will immediately take the broken Star from you, then invite you to communicate with [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). Place your hands upon the altar, and Azura will speak from the planes of Oblivion. She will say how grateful she is to see the Star returned, but will also provide the bad news: due to Malyn's occupation of the Star, the artifact will be useless to you until you venture inside and banish Malyn's soul by defeating him. Once you are ready, Azura will promise to watch over you as you are transported inside the Star's realm. |

### Inside the Star
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-quest-The_Black_Star_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Black_Star_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Black_Star_03.jpg) Malyn Varen inside the Star Once you have made your choice, you will be transported [inside the star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star_Interior.md) to battle Malyn Varen. The interior of Azura's Star is a light-blue, surreal world with spikes and chiming sounds. Malyn will be waiting for you just inside, eager to feast on yet another soul; he will, however, quickly realize that something is wrong and rush further into the Star, throwing a few leveled [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) at you. The interior offers very little to hide behind, making stealth a difficult option for combat. It is not possible to fall over the ledges, so proceed forward and remember to collect the valuable [Daedra Hearts](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) from fallen enemies. Once you have defeated Malyn, depending on your earlier choice, the Star will turn either black or white and you will be returned to your mortal body in Skyrim. If you went with Aranea, you will be given the restored [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md), capable of capturing White Souls. Additionally, Azura will set Aranea free and she may become a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). If you went with Nelacar, you will receive [The Black Star](Skyrim_The_Black_Star_(item).md).

## Notes
- Upon completing the quest, two necromancers may hunt you down. One of them carries *[a scrawled note](Skyrim_A_Scrawled_Note.md)* that reads: *"Malyn Varen's death will not go unpunished. Find the holder of Azura's Star and enact vengeance, or it will be your souls I rend for my enchantments."*
- Some people will comment if you have Azura's star in your possession: - Guards react with awe, saying *"By Shor, is that... is that Azura's Star? How did you come to possess such a rare treasure?"*
- Nelacar reacts negatively, saying, *"I don't like doing business with Azura's faithful... make it quick."*
- The Azura-worshipping denizens of Solstheim, however, have no unique reactions.
- It is possible to defeat Malyn before he can flee further into the star by immediately exiting conversation with him and using an attack that paralyzes or staggers him, such as a [power attack](Skyrim_Power_Attack.md), a spell or poison with a [Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) effect, or [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md). This is especially useful if you are having trouble dealing with the magical damage this location's foes throw at you.
- It is possible to reach [Ilinalta's Deluge](Skyrim_Ilinalta%27s_Deluge.md) by using Whirlwind Sprint at the highest rock nearby. If you retrieve Broken Azura's Star before starting the quest, you can give it to Nelacar but not Aranea Ienith.
- Once you kill Malyn Varen you will be pulled out of the star, so be sure to loot all desired items from him and the other foes as soon as possible.

- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DA01Intro, DA01 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DA01Intro, DA01`.
