[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-npc-Kust.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kust.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kust.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kust.jpg) Kust **Kust** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [monk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Monk) from [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), where he tends to the biggest graveyard in Skyrim. He is the assistant to town priest [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md) who tends the shrine to [Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arkay), and they both live in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md).

Kust wakes up early at 5am and immediately heads up to [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md) for four hours of mead and food. At 9am he walks down to the graveyard and watches it for ten hours before returning to the tavern at 7pm for a one-hour dinner. He then walks directly back to the Hall of the Dead and goes to bed.

He wears a set of [iron armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor), including matching pairs of [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_of_Arkay) to the Hall of the Dead as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
*"Strangers like you bring war. I've seen enough war."* *"You have the look of a killer."* *"Show respect while you're here and you and I will be fine."* *"These graves hold the sons and daughters of Falkreath."* *"Arkay helps us find peace with death."*
### Conversations
Kust might have a brief conversation when he runs into Runil in the garveyard:

**Kust**: *"The graveyard has been lonely. Few pay homage to the dead."*
**Runil**: *"Few want to be reminded of mortality in a time of war, Kust."* He was also intended to have a similar interaction with [Thadgeir](Skyrim_Thadgeir.md), but seeing as he never visits the graveyard, it cannot play:

**Thadgeir**: *"There are a lot of good men buried here, Kust."*
**Kust**: *"Men aren't good or bad. They're just men."*
## Unused Dialogue
Kust was supposed to play a part in two quests that never made it into the final game: In [Lod's Lot](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#Lod.27s_Lot), local blacksmith [Lod](Skyrim_Lod.md) would have stolen a book from a grave. Upon telling this to Kust, he would have responded:

**Lod has a book he stole from a grave.** *"I knew something was up. I'll make sure he doesn't disrespect this place anymore. Honesty is a rare thing to find these days. Thank you."* In [Runil's Dark Past](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#Runil.27s_Dark_Past), his own boss would turn out to be a former member of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md), and Kust would be understandably upset:

**Runil was one of the Thalmor during the war...** *"I knew he was troubled but one of the Thalmor? I wish I could say I did not believe it. Thank you for telling me."*
## Notes
- His internal relations have him set up as an ally to Runil, and marked as a rival to both [Lod](Skyrim_Lod.md) (presumably a leftover of his earlier characterization) and [Bolund](Skyrim_Bolund.md).
