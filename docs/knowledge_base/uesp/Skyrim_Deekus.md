[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f6/SR-npc-Deekus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Deekus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Deekus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Deekus.jpg) Deekus [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-quest-Kill_Deekus.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Kill_Deekus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Deekus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Deekus.jpg) Deekus guarding his loot **Deekus** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Assassin) who resides at a campsite on the beach near [Hela's Folly](Skyrim_Hela%27s_Folly.md). He is a target for [assassination](Skyrim_Kill_Deekus.md) in a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) contract.

Deekus spends most of his time wandering around the campsite, guarding the items he has collected from the nearby shipwreck. He wakes up at 8am and immediately begins to wander around his campsite. He can usually be found having a quick meal or drink while seated on the nearby chair. After a day of patrolling around the campsite, he heads to his bedroll at midnight where he will sleep for the next eight hours.

Deekus wears a set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). The campsite has a locked chest and several containers. On the tables and near the bed are various [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md) and gold, as well as a [statue of Dibella](Skyrim_Statue_of_Dibella.md), all of which will be counted as stolen regardless of if Deekus is killed. Mind the [bear traps](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) near the chests. All items found in the camp respawn after a period of time, whether he is alive or not. [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) will provide you with some information on Deekus if you ask him to tell you about your contract. Deekus likes to scavenge shipwrecks, and will run away if threatened.

## Related Quests
- **[Kill Deekus](Skyrim_Kill_Deekus.md)**: Slay an Argonian looting a shipwreck.

## Quest-Related Events
Like most assassination targets, you may choose to have a conversation with Deekus. Upon first speaking with him, he'll say:

- *"Hrm. You go find your own wreck. This one's mine."*

You'll then get three options:

| Topic | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| **I'm going to kill you. Slowly. Painfully.** | *"You'll have to catch me first!"* |
| **(Remain silent)** | *"Go away! I don't know who you are, but this is my spot! Whatever's on that ship is mine!"* |
| **I'm sorry, egg brother, but I've been sent to kill you.** (If the player is an [Argonian)](Skyrim_Argonian.md) <br> **I'm sorry, Argonian, but I've been sent to kill you.** (if the player is not Argonian) | *"What? Why? I don't understand... I have done nothing to wrong you. Please, mercy!"* |

If you backed out of the conversation and he hasn't turned hostile yet, attempting to speak to him again will net you a hostile:

- *"Go away! This is my wreck! Mine!"*

## Notes
- Due to not being part of any crime faction Deekus will not become hostile if he sees you in [werewolf](Skyrim_Beast_Form.md) or [vampire lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) forms, or if he watches you transform.
- [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) will provide you with some information on Deekus if you ask him to tell you about your contract, saying, *"He's an Argonian layabout, likes to scavenge shipwrecks. My guess is he'll run if threatened. Or swim, if given the chance."*
