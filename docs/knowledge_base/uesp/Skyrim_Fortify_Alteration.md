There are two distinct **Fortify [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md)** effects: one from [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) -created potions and one granted by [enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) armor. Their effects are as follows.

## Alchemy
In potions, Fortify Alteration increases the duration of Alteration magic.

The following alchemy ingredients can be used to create a potion of Fortify Alteration:

## Enchanting
In enchantments, Fortify Alteration decreases the [cost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Cost) of Alteration spells, and the use cost of Alteration based enchantments.

The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Fortify Alteration if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor, Helmets, and Rings *[of Alteration](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Alteration)* - Includes the enchantments *of Minor Alteration*, *of Major Alteration*, *of Extreme Alteration*, *of Eminent Alteration*, and *of Peerless Alteration.*
- All varieties of Robes of Alteration

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Archmage's Robes](Skyrim_Archmage%27s_Robes.md)
- [Diadem of the Savant](Skyrim_Diadem_of_the_Savant.md)
- [Mentor's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mentor%27s_Ring)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Vokun](Skyrim_Vokun_(item).md)

## Items to Enchant
The Fortify Alteration effect can be applied to the following items:

- Circlets, Hoods, and Helmets
- Chest Pieces
- Necklaces
- Rings

## Notes
The alchemy version of Fortify Alteration does not affect the Alteration spells [Transmute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Transmute_Mineral_Ore) and [Telekinesis](Skyrim_Telekinesis.md).
