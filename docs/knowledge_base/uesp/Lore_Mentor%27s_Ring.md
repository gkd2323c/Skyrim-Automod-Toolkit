# Mentor's Ring

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg) The Mentor's Ring (Legends) The Mentor's Ring is a prized possession for any apprentice to [magic](Lore_Magic.md). It lends the wearer the ability to increase their intelligence and wisdom, thus making their use of magic more efficient. The High Wizard [Carni Asron](Lore_Carni_Asron.md) is said to be its creator, and he loaned it to apprentices while they were studying under him. After Asron's death, the ring and several other of his possessions vanished and have been circulated throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TL-1)</sup>

[Yagrum Bagarn](Lore_Yagrum_Bagarn.md), the last living [dwarf](Lore_Dwemer.md), at one point befriended the sorcerer [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWYB-2)</sup> At Fyr’s behest, Yagrum Bagarn wrote and compiled centuries of personal notes into a book that would become known as *[Tamrielic Lore](Lore_Tamrielic_Lore.md)*,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-3)</sup><sup>:241</sup> listing the Mentor's Ring among the artifacts of "unimaginable significance". By the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), copies had spread across Tamriel, offering readers rare insights into these legendary items,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TA-4)</sup> and Fyr himself hoped to utilize this knowledge in his search for the Mentor's Ring and the other artifacts detailed in the book.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWFR-5)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), rumors circulated that the Mentor's Ring was lost by a wielder in a [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) Ancestral Tomb in the [Bitter Coast](Lore_Bitter_Coast.md) on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The rumors contained some truth to it, as it was found in the area within the Samarys Ancestral Tomb, where it was stored within a ceremonial ash urn belonging to a Lord Brinne. The ring would later be recovered by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> The Mentor's Ring was later found in a body of water by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) while fishing circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
