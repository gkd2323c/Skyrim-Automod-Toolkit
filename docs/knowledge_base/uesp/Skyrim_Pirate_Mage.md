[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Pirate_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Pirate_Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pirate_Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Pirate_Mage.jpg) Pirate Mage **Pirate Mages** are [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md) enemies encountered on Solstheim.

They wear [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) with a pair of leveled [light boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots) (up to [scaled](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) quality), and wield a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md). They carry other miscellaneous bandit items, have a chance of carrying gold, and have a chance of carrying one [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) at higher levels. Their spells vary depending on level, but always include one [armor effect](Skyrim_Armor_(effect).md) spell, one [ward](Skyrim_Ward.md) spell, and at least one spell with both [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) and [restore stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md) effects. In addition, they use one or two elemental [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells depending on level, which can deal either [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), or [shock](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) damage.

You are guaranteed to encounter one pirate mage at [Haknir's Shoal](Skyrim_Haknir%27s_Shoal.md), seeking the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md), along with a [pirate](Skyrim_Pirate.md) and [pirate captain](Skyrim_Pirate_Captain.md) for companions. They will become hostile as soon as they notice you.

## Related Quests
- **[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md)**: Find the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Deathbrand
You may also encounter a pirate mage [randomly](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Arguing_about_Haknir) in the wilds of Solstheim with a pirate. They will argue briefly, then attack each other after the mage refuses to go with the pirate. If you stand aside during their fight, the pirate may lose the battle, and the surviving pirate mage will simply stand there, and not turn hostile against you. You can loot a copy of the book *[Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(book).md)* from the pirate's corpse, which will activate the related quest upon reading.

**Pirate**: *"I told him 'no' once already."*
**Pirate Mage**: *"We've got work to do."*
**Pirate**: *"I'm not chasing that map. It's a fool's errand. I'm done. Leave me alone."*
**Pirate Mage**: *"Boss said to bring you back or bring back your corpse."*
**Pirate**: *"Is that so? Well, you'll never get that treasure now!"*
**Pirate Mage**: *"Time to die!"*

## Notes
- Unlike the pirate mage encountered at Haknir's Shoal, the pirate mage encountered randomly (Base ID `xx039723`) is always male (rather than the gender being determined randomly), is not a member of any factions, has an [aggression](Skyrim_Aggression.md) value of 0 (Unaggressive), and has a [morality](Skyrim_Morality.md) value of 3 (No Crime).
