[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/ON-load-Loth%27Na_Caverns_%28old%29.jpg/200px-ON-load-Loth%27Na_Caverns_%28old%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Loth%27Na_Caverns_(old).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Loth%27Na_Caverns_(old).jpg) An exterior part of the caverns circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Loth'Na Caverns (or **Loth' Na Caverns**) are a series of dozens of small caves in the [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md) region of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Entrances exist both west and northeast of [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md).

Like all natural caves, these tunnels once served as the capillaries of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), pulsing with the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LS1-1)</sup> Some of the lower sections of the caves are filled with lava. The caverns are connected by a secluded valley, crossed by streams and with a small hill at the center. The ancient [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) kingdom of [Skyreach](Lore_Skyreach.md) extended north to the caverns, which was used as a place of worship by the Nedes. These star worshippers built Skyreach Temple deep within the caves, and Loth'Na became a site of pilgrimage as part of the Gray Passage.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TGP-2)</sup> The temple was left to fall into ruins after the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokudan.md) invasion of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> The caves also contained a [Shadowgate](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SK-4)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/ON-interior-Skyreach_Temple.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Skyreach_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Skyreach_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Skyreach_Temple.jpg) Scaled Court in Skyreach Temple [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/ON-load-Skyreach_Temple.jpg/200px-ON-load-Skyreach_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Skyreach_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Skyreach_Temple.jpg) Skyreach Temple The [Scaled Court](Lore_Scaled_Court.md) commandeered the [lamia](Lore_Lamia.md) -infested caverns in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). They fortified the base heavily, bringing in vast numbers of cultists and [giant snakes](Lore_Giant_Snake.md). An [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) assassin of the Scaled Court named Visskar was also stationed at Loth'Na due to the location's extreme importance to the cult.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> [Cassipia](Lore_Cassipia.md), the Regent of Serpentine Stratagems and an ex-member of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), chose Skyreach Temple as the site of her ritual to infuse herself with [nirncrux](Lore_Nirncrux.md) and become a [Celestial](Lore_Celestial.md). She betrayed [the Serpent](Lore_The_Serpent.md) and the Court, declaring herself "[the Exalted Viper](Lore_The_Exalted_Viper.md)" and occupying the temple ruins with her followers. Kelman Locke journeyed to Loth'Na to dissuade her from her plans, but instead he was killed. [The Thief](Lore_The_Thief.md) and the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) subsequently entered the caverns, killing Visskar and destroying Cassipia before she could complete the ritual.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESO2-6)</sup>

### Third Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SK-quest-Pilgrim_Ghost_Ring.jpg/100px-SK-quest-Pilgrim_Ghost_Ring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-quest-Pilgrim_Ghost_Ring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-quest-Pilgrim_Ghost_Ring.jpg) The fortifying crystal [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SK-place-Loth%27Na_Caverns.png/200px-SK-place-Loth%27Na_Caverns.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-place-Loth%27Na_Caverns.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SK-place-Loth%27Na_Caverns.png) Shadowgate in Loth'Na Caverns circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397) During the [War of the Bend'r-mahk](Lore_War_of_the_Bend%27r-mahk.md), the invading forces of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), under the command of General Duvais, occupied the caverns in an attempt to take the city of Dragonstar. The soldiers harnessed the power of a magical "fortifying crystal" within the caves, but when a group of pilgrims arrived to visit the crystal they were killed by Duvais. In [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397), the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Hero.md) entered Loth'Na to assassinate Duvais on the orders of Porliss Caith, an Argonian agent of Hammerfell. The ghosts of the murdered pilgrims were also put to rest by this hero, and the crystal smashed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SK-4)</sup>
