[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-npc-Visiting_Advisor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Visiting_Advisor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Visiting_Advisor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Visiting_Advisor.jpg) Visiting Advisor at the White Hall in Dawnstar The **Visiting Advisor** is a vampire masquerading as an advisor to [jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md) across Skyrim.

As part of [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md)'s radiant [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) quests, you will need to eliminate him. During [A Jarl's Justice](Skyrim_A_Jarl%27s_Justice.md), you will have the option of either [stealthily](Skyrim_Stealth.md) killing him to avoid a bounty or [pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) his [orders](Skyrim_Orders_(DG).md) and bringing these to the jarl, who will then give you a free hand to deal with him.

He wears a [vampire hood, robes, gloves, and boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Equipment.md), and carries an enchanted ring and an enchanted dagger. As a vampire, when dead he will drop a portion of [Vampire Dust](Skyrim_Vampire_Dust.md) and possibly a leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md).

## Bugs
- The Visiting Advisor is one of three potential targets for the [Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md) quest, in place of the [Traveling Merchant](Skyrim_Traveling_Merchant.md). As a result, there is no dialogue to convince him to follow you. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.0.2, addresses this issue. The Traveling Merchant replaces the Visiting Advisor as one of the potential targets

## Notes
- While the advisor is described as a vampire, visually looks like one and uses [Vampiric Drain](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md), they are actually part of the normal "Nord" race, and not the "Nord Vampire" race. This means that spells such as [Sun Fire](Skyrim_Sun_Fire.md) and [Stendarr's Aura](Skyrim_Stendarr%27s_Aura.md) have no effect on them, and the advisor is detected by [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md), rather than [Detect Dead](Skyrim_Detect_Undead.md).
