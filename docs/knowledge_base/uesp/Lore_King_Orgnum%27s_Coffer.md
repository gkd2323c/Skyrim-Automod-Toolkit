# King Orgnum's Coffer

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png/200px-AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png) King Orgnum's Coffer King Orgnum's Coffer is a legendary [magical](Lore_Magic.md) chest, small and ordinary-looking but weightless, once owned by King [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md) of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS128-1)</sup><sup>:128</sup> Once per day a random number of gold coins appear within the Coffer,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup> though in Orgnum's possession it was an unlimited supply.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS128-1)</sup><sup>:128</sup>

King Orgnum lost his Coffer in a battle against Emperor [Antiochus Septim](Lore_Antiochus.md) during the [War of the Isle](Lore_War_of_the_Isle.md). With the loss of this source of endless wealth, Orgnum could not afford a fleet as great as he had had, leading to Pyandonea losing much of its military power.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AQD-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS128-1)</sup><sup>:128</sup> It is said that when found the Coffer always disappears again, but not before producing enormous wealth for its owner.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CS128-1)</sup><sup>:128</sup> Where and why it vanishes is a mystery. The coffer washed up in the Summerset Isles and has become an elusive artifact ever since.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARArtifact-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CS-5)</sup><sup>:128</sup> It was last seen in the hands of the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup>
