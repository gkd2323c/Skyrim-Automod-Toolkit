*The best victory is to completely and totally humiliate your opponent.*
—Armsmaster Festil Festil was an armsmaster<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> who dedicated his life to strategy. He was a seasoned warrior, having been in numerous life and death conflicts over a span of at least two generations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena CQ-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>

## History

### Background
Festil is described as having fought in various historical conflicts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena CQ-1)</sup> In one story from his travels, he recalls having known a king in faraway land, whose inventors created a wondrous balloon of such size, it could transport dozens of people through the air to any place they chose.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena CQ-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 3]](#intnote_nb3)</sup>

### Eternal Champion's Weapons Class
In the golden years of his life he taught a class of students (including the [General Warhaft's child](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md), a minor part of the [Imperial Court](Lore_Imperial_Court.md))<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> how to fight an opponent and how to strategize during war. He could also convey hints to his students about recognizing magical weaponry. While Festil was "very old" then he still took his role as a teacher very seriously, in fact he once commented at this time that it is all he has left in life. One of his sayings was: "the intent of your opponent can be seen in his blade."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena CQ-1)</sup>
