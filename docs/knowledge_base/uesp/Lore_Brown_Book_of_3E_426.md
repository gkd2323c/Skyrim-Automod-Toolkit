Brown Book of Great House Telvanni *A yearbook of the affairs of the Telvanni Council*
---

- *The Brown Book is a yearbook of the affairs of the [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) Council of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) District for [3E 426](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_426). It lists the current members of the council, their residences, and their representatives in [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md). It also chronicles significant events and council actions for the year.*

### Council Actions
In response to repeated protests from [Duke Dren](Lore_Vedam_Dren.md) and representative of the other [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md), Telvanni Council reminded them that, according to ancient law and custom, Telvanni Council places no constraint on the ambitions and enterprise of its individual members. If the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) or other House Councils wish to dispute Telvanni exploration and colonization of the wastes and wildernesses of Vvardenfell, they are welcome to do so, with the Councilors' best wishes, but Telvanni Council will not contribute its resources or authority to such endeavors.

The council renews its objection to proposals placed before Duke Dren and the Grand Council concerning slavery and slave trading in Vvardenfell District. The right to own and trade slaves is guaranteed by the terms of the [Treaty of the Armistice](Lore_Armistice.md), and Telvanni Council will not entertain any discussion of abridgements of those rights.
