[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/DF-map-Ilessan_Hills.png/200px-DF-map-Ilessan_Hills.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ilessan_Hills.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ilessan_Hills.png) Ilessan Hills ca. 3E 405 The Ilessan Hills is a political entity found in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the region of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). It is a landlocked region known for its ancient pine forests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TGEB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TTBL-2)</sup> It mostly consists of a vast mountain range that spills into neighboring countries and the wetlands in the northeast section of the barony.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFMap-3)</sup> The region is the home of the local [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md), an ancient coven of [witches](Lore_Wyrd.md) that worship the [natural world](Lore_The_Green.md) and the less-feral aspects of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGW-4)</sup>

## History
Rumors of the Glenmoril Wyrd living in the forest have existed since before recorded history<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EGTB-5)</sup> (which started roughly around High King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md)'s reign over [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1SR-6)</sup> They live in a place called the Gloomy Cave, far from the most rudimentary [goat](Lore_Goat.md) trails in the wilderness<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-EGTB-5)</sup> near Old Chrystausa's Place in east-central Ilessan Hills.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFGlenmoril-7)</sup> The wyrd have had a long, conflicting history with the [Reach Clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) that goes back thousands of years, relative to [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGW-4)</sup>

For the longest time, the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) of High Rock have lived in the region.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFIlessan-8)</sup> Other races and factions also live in the Ilessan Hills, such as [barbarians](Lore_Barbarians.md), [giants](Lore_Giant.md) and the [orcs](Lore_Orc.md), who live in strongholds.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-9)</sup> The [Haarvenu](Lore_Haarvenu.md) are the most dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFIlessan-8)</sup> In her adventures across Tamriel, Princess [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) supposedly danced with the [Nereids](Lore_Nereid.md) of the Ilessan Hills.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TROQA-10)</sup>

By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Barony of Ilessan Hills was ruled by an unnamed Baron and its patron deity was [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), who is represented through the [Order of Arkay](Lore_Order_of_Arkay.md). Many cities dotted the region's landscape, including the capital of the same name, Aldhead Rock, Baelwall, Baelway, Blackley, Blackworth, Burgham, Charbeth, Croming, Crossfort, Crossham, Deerway, Kirkvale, Gallotower, Galloworth, Gothwood, Graygate Hollow, Holmont, Holway, Ipstry, Kirkbrone, Longbeth, Newbrone, Newmarket, and Westfield Moor. The region was neighbored by several nations, including [Daenia](Lore_Daenia_(duchy).md) to the east, [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) to the south, [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md) to the west, [Northmoor](Lore_Northmoor.md) to the northwest, [Shalgora](Lore_Shalgora.md) to the southeast, and [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFIlessan-8)</sup>

On the 9th of [Frostfall](Lore_Frostfall.md), in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the kingdoms of the Iliac Bay were enraptured in warfare and destruction occurred across the region. A great fire broke out between the Ilessan Hills and Daenia, spreading further north into [Phrygias](Lore_Phrygias.md) and Ykalon, to as far as the [Wrothgarian Mountain](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) foothills. By the 11th, the Iliac Bay changed from several, disparate nations merged into the four major kingdoms. Ilessan Hills were absorbed by the Kingdom of Daggerfall, at the time ruled by King [Gothryd](Lore_Gothryd.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TWITW-11)</sup> Like other conquered territories, Ilessan Hills was able to retain its original name but is sometimes referred to as **Daggerfallian Ilessan Hills**.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LRPTESP-12)</sup>

## City of Ilessan Hills
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/DF-place-Ilessan_Hills_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-place-Ilessan_Hills_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Ilessan_Hills_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Ilessan_Hills_(city).png) Ilessan Hills (Daggerfall) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/DF-Location-14956-Automap.png/200px-DF-Location-14956-Automap.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-14956-Automap.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-14956-Automap.png) Map of Ilessan Hills ca. 3E 405 The **Ilessan Hills** (also called **Ilessen Hills**)<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AR-13)</sup> is the walled capital of the eponymous barony, in the midst of the mountain range right next to the border toward Glenpoint.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFIlessan-8)</sup>

During the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Ilessen Hills was an active settlement. It was ruled by Baron Andywyr and had a rivalry with [Kings Guard](Lore_Kings_Guard.md). Ilessen Hills is neighbored by several settlements, such as [Ebon Wastes](Lore_Ebon_Wastes.md) to the north and [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint_(city).md) to the south.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ARMap-14)</sup>
