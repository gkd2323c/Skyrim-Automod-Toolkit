Loreth the Fangleman was an enchanter from the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) best remembered as the creator of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md), also known as the Staff of Unity and Chaos, or the Balac-Thurm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Final Lesson-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-The True Barenziah Part8-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOAUA-3)</sup>

## History

### Origins
Loreth's prominence is traced back to the First Era, circa [1E 500](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_500).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Final Lesson-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ESOAUA-3)</sup> He began as an apprentice to the hermetic enchanter Dalak, alongside another student named Uthrac. Under the command of his master, he ventured to western [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to check on another enchanter by the name of Peothil, doing his best to avoid the [Alessian Reform](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). He entered the Caves of Peothil, only to find the man had died during one of his experiments. Loreth remained in those caves for some time, learning on his own, and studying Peothil's discoveries. After the defeat of the Alessians at the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md), he left the [Heartland](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and returned to his old master, only to find that Dalak had passed on. Loreth and Uthrac would eventually part ways, with Uthrac becoming a Direnni retainer while Loreth forged his own path.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Final Lesson-1)</sup>

### Enchanter
Loreth took the skills he had learned in exile to make a most powerful Staff, from the very essence of the Land itself: the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Final Lesson-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARSilmane-4)</sup>

### Legacy
Loreth would go on to father a familial line, with one of his distant descendants creating the [Arms of Chaos](Lore_Arms_of_Chaos.md) based on Loreth's work.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
