# Memory

| --- |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/ON-interior-Eldbur_Sanctuary_%28The_Mind_Trap%29_06.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Eldbur_Sanctuary_%28The_Mind_Trap%29_06.jpg)](File_ON-interior-Eldbur_Sanctuary_(The_Mind_Trap)_06.jpg.md) [](File_ON-interior-Eldbur_Sanctuary_(The_Mind_Trap)_06.jpg.md) A mind trap composed of memory **Memory** is the faculty by which the mind stores and remembers information. It is a trait possessed by mortals and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) alike.

Memory exists physically and can manifest outside the mind. Memories can be [crystallized](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crystals), creating transparent crystals with zero imperfections and an ability to catch and hold light.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> On [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), [water](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Water) is composed of memory. When a mortal dies, their memories become water, and in this way all the memories of Tamriel's histories are stored within its waters. This fact is considered to be one of the most dangerous secrets about the natural order of the world, and is therefore closely guarded by the Demon of Knowledge [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Through the use of [magic](Lore_Magic.md), it is possible to visit past memories. For example, the [Order of the Hidden Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Order_of_the_Hidden_Moon) would achieve this by invoking the rites of Jhaz Ku-ji'zho via the ritual burning of [moon sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Moon_Sugar).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> [Memory stones](Lore_Memory_Stone.md) are magical gems that can be used to record and project a person's memory.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Memories can be weaponized by [mind magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mind_Magic), such as via the use of mind traps—illusory prisons constructed by the victim's own mind, where their memories can be extracted or manipulated by the caster.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Oriandra-5)</sup>

The [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) are known to take great interest in memories. Hermaeus Mora has dominion over memories<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> and possesses the ability to alter them at will.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> [Vaermina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vaermina) collects the memories of mortals within the [Dreamstride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dreamstride).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> The [Daedra Lord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedra_Lord) [Hollowjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hollowjack) feeds off mortal fear and collects the shattered memories of terrified mortals in his realm of [Detritus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Detritus).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
