[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/SR-npc-Olaf_One-Eye.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Olaf_One-Eye.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olaf_One-Eye.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Olaf_One-Eye.jpg) Olaf in the hall of Sovngarde King **Olaf One-Eye** was a [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) Jarl of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) (and later High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)) famous for imprisoning the [Dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Numinex](Lore_Numinex.md) within his palace, which was dubbed [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) in honor of this feat. After death, the dragon's skull was kept as a trophy, and can still be seen on the wall above the throne in Dragonsreach.

Due to his fame, Olaf was eventually named High King of Skyrim. However, the bard [Svaknir](Skyrim_Svaknir.md) wrote a scathing verse decrying him as a fraud and a tyrant. Olaf responded by imprisoning the bard and destroying every copy of the verse which could be found. Ancient bard texts claimed that Olaf and his men fabricated the orthodox tale to ensure his place as Jarl and possibly to aid in his bid to become High King. An effigy of Olaf is ritually burned in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) as part of an annual festival known as "The Burning of King Olaf".

He wears a full set of [steel plate armor](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Armor.md), except the [helmet](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Helmet.md). He carries a [steel warhammer](Skyrim_Steel_Warhammer.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
If you have completed the quest [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), Olaf may greet you by saying:

- *"You freed Svaknir, a fool of a poet, but an honest enemy who has earned my respect. Sadly, he's now lost in the soul-snaring mist - defeat Alduin, and his doom avert - I hope to greet him in this Hall as a friend."*
