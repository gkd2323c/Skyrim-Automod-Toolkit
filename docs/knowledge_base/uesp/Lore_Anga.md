[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/OB-place-Anga.jpg/200px-OB-place-Anga.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Anga.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Anga.jpg) Anga circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Anga (meaning *Iron* or *Cold* in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOEld Angavar-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Angalayond-2)</sup> is an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) [ruin](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) located in the Anga Valley, a region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)'s [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) found north of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), just off the [Silver Road](Lore_Silver_Road.md) to [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md).

Little is known of Anga's history. Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) an Ayleid researcher named Brelynd Verano attempted to access Anga's interior, but the ruins had been sealed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

At some point, a cult of [Namira](Lore_Namira.md) worshippers known as the Forgotten sealed themselves within the ruins to live out their lives in squalor and darkness. Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), priests of [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md) invaded the ruins bearing torches to "cleanse" the darkness within and save the souls of the Forgotten. Having spent so many years living in total darkness, the light burned the Forgotten, leaving them helpless. In response, Namira tasked the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) with entering Anga and casting a spell on the priests, extinguishing their torches and allowing the Forgotten to kill them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OBNamira-4)</sup> In addition to the living occupants, a secret chamber within the ruins was also haunted by the undead at this time.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
