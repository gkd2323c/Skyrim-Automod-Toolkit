# Imperial City Archives

The Imperial City Archives (also referred to as the **Imperial Archives**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AT-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup> is an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) institution based in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). It served as a repository of valuable Imperial records, such as deeds to holdings and estates within the Empire's bounds,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DTBM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DTFS-5)</sup> court proceedings for a military tribunal,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades-1)</sup> diplomatic marriage agreements between kingdoms,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQ1-6)</sup> and records of the Imperial noble houses.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AT-2)</sup>

One of its branches, the Documents Division, handled matters regarding documents of ownership. Bearers of appropriate ownership documents could amend them to rename the official names of their residence by submitting the proper forms and payments to the appropriate Construction Charter (such as the Anvil Construction Charter or Cyrodiil Construction Charter) and by filing duplicate forms with the Documents Division.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DTBM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DTFS-5)</sup> It is unknown when the institution was founded, but it was active during the [Second](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AT-2)</sup> [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DTBM-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DTFS-5)</sup> and [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Blades-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EJ-3)</sup>

The Imperial City Archives were pillaged by the [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) during [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md) and invasion of the Imperial City. Piles of books and scrolls were gathered and burned in mass fires.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONSergianus-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TICGA-8)</sup> There was once an archive in the [Arboretum District](Lore_Arboretum_District.md), but was laid to ruin by then.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONRuined-9)</sup>

## Notes
- In [2E 541](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_541), the "Scroll of Precursor [Saints](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md)" was supposedly discovered in the vaults below the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) by Chancellor [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-HTON-10)</sup> It is unknown, however, if those vaults were related to this institution.
- The [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md) also has a Hall of Records, which along with diplomatic documents separated by year, province and kingdom, housed the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQ1-6)</sup>
