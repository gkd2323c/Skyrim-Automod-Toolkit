— *[Shad Astula Academy Handbook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shad_Astula_Academy_Handbook)* Shad Astula Academy was a school of [magic](Lore_Magic.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), located near the city of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). Initially founded by and for the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) and blessed by the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), the academy opened enrolment up to train the wider [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOFalan-1)</sup> Although focused primarily on the arcane arts, its students were also educated in writing, leadership, and military strategy in preparation for assuming leadership roles in the Pact and Great Houses.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RTD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SAC-3)</sup> The entrance requirements and curriculum were infamously rigorous, but resulted in high-quality graduates produced in half the time of a traditional [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) education.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PSOM-4)</sup>

The academy was built on the foundations of much older buildings, and some of the underhalls, such as the library vaults, were still in use during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-5)</sup> Students were ferried between Mournhold and the school by a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [ghost](Lore_Ghost.md) who owed the academy a substantial debt and was consequently bound to serve as a boatswain.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOBoatmaster-6)</sup>

Students at Shad Astula learned from master mages in a highly permissive and experimental environment, designed to weed out the weak and rapidly enhance the skills of the strong.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SAAH-7)</sup> The curriculum divided the magical arts into schools, such as [Restoration](Lore_Restoration.md) and [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md), which was a novel approach at the time that would quickly be adopted by the Mages Guild.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PSOM-4)</sup> Students who graduated from the academy were given a pin, with those who completed their master exams being gifted a timepiece by the Arch-Mage.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOPin-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOHourglass-9)</sup> Student use of [skooma](Lore_Skooma.md) and strong alcoholic beverages were banned, with instructors suggesting students limit themselves to [mazte and flin](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md) if they wished to decompress. The alcohol ban did not extend to teaching staff.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOFalan-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Shad Astula was under the leadership of Arch-Mage Valeyn when it was attacked by the Maulborn during an attempted takeover led by General Darvasen, Valeyn's disgruntled former assistant who was expelled for experimenting on students.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOValeyn-10)</sup> [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was able to stop the Maulborn and free students enslaved by the ancient [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) relic known as the [Jagged Lens of Compulsion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Jagged_Lens_of_Compulsion).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Notes
- Though [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) is credited as creating the schools of magic in *[The Black Arts On Trial](Lore_The_Black_Arts_On_Trial.md)*, the book *[Proposal: Schools of Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Proposal__Schools_of_Magic)* has the scholar [Gabrielle Benele](Lore_Gabrielle_Benele.md) propose to adopt the schools of magic from Shad Astula within the Mages Guild, a suggestion which Vanus [rejects repeatedly](Lore_What%27s_an_Arcanist%3F_Part_1.md). *[A Minor Maze](Lore_A_Minor_Maze.md)* attributes them to [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md).
