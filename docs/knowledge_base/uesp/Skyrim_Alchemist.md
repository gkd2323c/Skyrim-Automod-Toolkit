[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-npc-Alchemist.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alchemist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alchemist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alchemist.jpg) A dead alchemist at Evergreen Grove **Alchemists** are dead [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurers](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) found throughout Skyrim. They all wear [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots) and [Black Mage Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes). They all carry a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), a rare [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger). There is one in a cell in [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md), one in a hanging cage outside of [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md), one at the giant camp [Cradlecrush Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cradlecrush_Rock), one on a rock in a river near [Talking Stone Camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Talking_Stone_Camp), and one on a sunken altar at [Evergreen Grove](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Evergreen_Grove). They can be a good source of ingredients in the beginning of the game.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SR-npc-Alchemist_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alchemist_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alchemist_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alchemist_02.jpg) The dead female alchemist in Clearpine Pond This **Alchemist** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) found at [Clearpine Pond](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clearpine_Pond) up against a fallen tree. She wears [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12). She carries a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), a [rare ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), and a copy of the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[De Rerum Dirennis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:De_Rerum_Dirennis)*. Beside her is an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel) containing one common, uncommon, and rare ingredients each, with a 25% chance of containing one random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), [of magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Magicka), or [of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina). There are also two [taproots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taproot) and a [blisterwort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort) found beside her, along with an empty [flower basket](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flower_Basket).

## Notes
- One more alchemist (see Base ID 00099805 above) is found in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) after [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) is installed, on a table in [Ronthil's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ronthil) quarters.
- Strangely, both types of Alchemists know the spells [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md), [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), even though they are dead and cannot use them.

All three alchemists wear leveled, enchanted robes; from level 1-7, it will be [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes). From level 8 and up, the enchantments will match your level, and will fortify either [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md), [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md), or [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md). From level 10, there is also a 25% chance the robes will have a [regenerate magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) enchantment. Lastly, all of them wear the same type of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12).

They all carry a sample of nirnroot, some random, rare [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to glass quality at level 18).
