## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) to speak with [Aela the Huntress](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md) about work.
2. Clear out the beast invading a citizen of Skyrim's home or shop.
3. Return to Aela for payment.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Job
Once you have completed the [Take Up Arms](Skyrim_Take_Up_Arms.md) quest, you can begin work as a Companion. As one of the group's leaders, Aela the Huntress will allocate jobs to those looking for work. When you approach Aela she will inform you that: *"A citizen of <a location in Skyrim> has asked for our help. It seems a predator has invaded their <home, shop, farm>, and someone needs to clear the beast out."* Accept the job and your journal will be updated with the location and the type of predator you need to exterminate.

The location can be any of at least forty-two houses in Skyrim. The list includes most houses (other than inns) that are occupied by standard town citizens. The location will be limited to holds that you have previously visited, so early in the game you may only receive destinations within Whiterun Hold, but later in the game there are many more possible destinations. Note that if you have the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on installed, the location may be on Solstheim, in which case your quest marker will not appear while you are in Skyrim.

## Notes
- If [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) is installed, some radiant locations may be on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). If you are in Skyrim, you will not receive a quest marker pointing you to the quest location, but must travel to Solstheim first (at which point any quest markers you have to quests in Skyrim will disappear).
- Aela can also give you the quest if you encounter her while out hunting in Skyrim.
- The animals that spawn will not be restricted to that animal's natural habitat. For example, you could find a snow bear or snowy sabre cat in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md) or [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), even though these places have more temperate climates.
- Sometimes you are technically considered trespassing when you enter one of these homes, even though you have permission to be there. Though you will not get a bounty, your followers will still refuse to speak to you, insisting that *"You're not supposed to be in here."* while in the house. However if the door is locked and you are seen picking the lock, then you will receive a bounty of 5 septims.

## Bugs
- After killing the beast and returning to Aela, your quest will be acknowledged as complete, but it may remain in your quest list. If this happens, Aela will not give you any further quests to do (though it will still trigger The Silver Hand quest if you are far enough in the Companion's Quest Line). This can be circumvented by loading a save prior to reporting back to Aela and using unrelenting force on her before talking to her.
- After killing the beast, the owner of the home will not give any thanks and instead will accuse you of trespassing. If you do not leave immediately he or she will call the guards and you will be [arrested](Skyrim_Crime.md) upon exiting the house.
