Tombs of the Kinrulers *Records of the interred Corelanyan Kinrulers*
---
Attend, ye who visit these hallowed halls. Herein lie in eternal repose the mortal remains of [Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) kinladies and kinlords who ruled over this realm of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), our home in exile.

Kinlady [Earlenque the Great](Lore_Earlenque_Corelanya.md) (reigned 1E 909 - 1E 1017)
Matriarch, exile, founder of the realm. Honored be her name.

Kinlord [Orlemar the Builder](Lore_Orlemar_Corelanya.md) (reigned 1E 1017 - 1E 1101)
Son of Earlenque, liege of Western Solstice. Honored be his memory.

Kinlady [Psynande the Voyager](Lore_Psynande_Corelanya.md) (reigned 1E 1101 - 1E 1165)
Cousin of Orlemar, explorer of the eastern seas. Honored be her memory.

Kinlord [Kevarian the Defender](Lore_Kevarian_Corelanya.md) (reigned 1E 1165 - 1E 1194)
Nephew of Psynande, civilizer of the wilds. Slain in honorable battle.
