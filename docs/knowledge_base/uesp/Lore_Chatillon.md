# Chatillon

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/ON-npc-Phylgaux_Chatillon.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Phylgaux_Chatillon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Phylgaux_Chatillon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Phylgaux_Chatillon.jpg) A noble member of Chatillon family The Chatillon family is a distinguished Breton noble house with a historical connection to the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO1-1)</sup>

## History

### First Era
While the precise founding date of House Chatillon remains shrouded in mystery, the earliest known records acknowledging its existence date back to a period of the establishment of the All Flags Navy and the legendary assault on [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) by [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) in [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260). During this era, the house had already attained the status of a barony. One of its members, Baroness [Belene Chatillon](Lore_Belene_Chatillon.md), who participated in the assault on Thras, bore the title of a [knight](Lore_Knights.md). Additionally, she was the revered founder of the renowned [chivalric order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) known as the [Order of the Iron Knot](Lore_Order_of_the_Iron_Knot.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKO1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TB-2)</sup>

### Second Era
During the Second Era, House Chatillon's status and activities remain shrouded unclear. However, they were extant. A noble of the family, Phylgaux Chatillon was present as an observer at a jousting tournament held in Navire on [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PC-3)</sup>

In the mid-Second Era, during the Interregnum, Chatillon was a common surname among Bretons, particularly in the regions such as [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OC-4)</sup> [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-FC-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EC-6)</sup> High Isle,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GC-7)</sup> and outside [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). People with the Chatillon surname could be found in various roles, from soldiers,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OC-4)</sup> to merchants,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GC-7)</sup> and bards.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BC-8)</sup> Chatillon commoners could be also found abroad, in places such as [Haven](Lore_Haven.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Or C-9)</sup> and [Morthal](Lore_Morthal.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BC-8)</sup>

Enzo Chatillon, the esteemed [alchemist](Lore_Alchemy.md), effectively combated the [snag-weevil](Lore_Weevil.md) infestation in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) with his award-winning alchemical solution.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EC-6)</sup>
