## Walkthrough

### Exterior
In front of the entrance is a porch attached to the house, complete with a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), with a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) and a [scathecraw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scathecraw) plant to the right of the porch, and three more scathecraw plants to the left. A [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Guild shadowmark is on the wall of the house to the left of the entrance. To the right of the entrance around and beyond the smelter are six unusable barrels and another scathecraw plant. Along the back of the building on the waterfront is another scathecraw plant among more unusable barrels. The entrance has an adept-level lock.

### Glover Mallory's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House.jpg) Glover's living area The entrance leads into a cooking and living area on the lower floor of the house. In the southeast corner are two food barrels, with two copies of *[The Poison Song, Book II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Poison_Song,_Book_II)* on the wall ledge to the right. In the southwest corner are two chairs and a table holding two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), a [green apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Apple), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a sample of [Felsaad tern feathers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Felsaad_Tern_Feathers), and copies of *[History of Raven Rock, Vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._I)* and *[Vol. II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:History_of_Raven_Rock,_Vol._II)*. In the northwest corner is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand and a sideboard holding a bottle of [matze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matze), a copy of *[The Red Year, Vol. I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Year,_Vol._I)*, and an [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox). Along the north wall is a doorway to the next room. In the northeast corner are two food urns.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_04.jpg) Stair room The next room is circular, and features stairs leading up to the upper floor and down to the basement. Along the curved west wall are two food urns, six sacks, a food barrel with a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top, and a narrow table holding two more bottles of Nord mead, an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie), and a copy of *[The Axe Man](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Axe_Man)*.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_02.jpg) Glover's sleeping area The small circular room on the upper floor is the sleeping area. To the west near the top of the stairs is a chest, two chairs, and a small round table holding a red apple, a [boiled creme treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat), and a copy of *[The House of Troubles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_House_of_Troubles)*. To the south is an owned single bed. To the southwest is a sideboard holding two large [coin purses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and a copy of *[Ahzidal's Descent](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ahzidal%27s_Descent)*.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_05.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_05.jpg) Basement stairs The small circular basement room features a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glover%27s_Guild_Key) -locked door on the south wall. To the southwest is a set of shelves holding copies of *[Bone, Part I](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_I)* and *[Part II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone,_Part_II)*. To the west are two straw bales, a wood pile, and a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe). To the north beneath the stairs are three food barrels and another wood pile.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Glover_Mallory%27s_House_03.jpg) Glover's locked room Beyond the key-locked door is a room dedicated to the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), with guild banners hanging on the wall opposite the door. In the northeast corner are two ingredient barrels and a chair. In the southeast corner is a [thief cache](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thief_Cache) with the [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Cache shadowmark painted on the side; unlike all other caches, this cache doesn't include a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger).

On the south wall between the banners is a shield rack with a leveled enchanted [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) and a leveled enchanted [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md). On the wall ledge below the rack is a leveled [light feet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Light_Feet) potion, a leveled [pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) potion, a leveled [healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) potion, a [strong box](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), and a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glover%27s_Letter) to Glover's [daughter](Skyrim_Sapphire_(person).md).

Along the west wall are two sideboards flanking a display case. The sideboard to the left of the display case holds three potent [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md) and two [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) poisons, six bottles of [Black-Briar Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead), one bottle of [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sujamma), one bottle of [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shein), two empty [lesser soul gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lesser_Soul_Gem), and one filled lesser soul gem. The display case contains a complete set of [Blackguard's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unique_Armor#Blackguard.27s_Armor). The sideboard to the right of the display case holds four [sweet rolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), three bottles of [Black-Briar Reserve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Reserve), five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md), seven lockpicks, one large coin purse, two medium coin purses, and one small coin purse.

## Notes
- The [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House_Key) carried by Glover Mallory only unlocks the entrance to the house. After completing the quest [Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full), he will hand you the key that unlocks the basement door. - You don't need to be a part of the Thieves Guild in order to be able to access his basement storage room: you can get to it by using a plate (platter, wooden plate, etc.) pressed up against the door and using the [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) shout or running up against it to pass through the plate and into the room. All the items there are free to take, but you'll need another plate and the same method to get back out. Also, unless you're a friend to him, this process should only be used when he is out of the house (either during normal business hours, or when he's working on the shrine outside of town), as being there otherwise might be considered trespassing.

## Bugs
- All the Blackguard's Armor items will respawn when Glover's basement resets, allowing multiple copies of the set to be obtained. Glover's Letter also respawns along with the rest of the house, allowing you to give it to Sapphire repeatedly and receive unlimited Exquisite Sapphires, since she is not coded to respond only once to it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=13442))
- The Blackguard's Armor pieces found here don't have an Armor Material keyword assigned so the full set doesn't count toward the *Matching Set* perk. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
