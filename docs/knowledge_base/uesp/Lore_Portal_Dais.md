# Portal Dais

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-place-Portal_Dais_%28Deadlands%29.jpg/200px-ON-place-Portal_Dais_%28Deadlands%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Portal_Dais_(Deadlands).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Portal_Dais_(Deadlands).jpg) A portal dais A portal dais, (also spelled **portal-dais**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TLOD-1)</sup> is a raised platform designed to host a portal. There were many of them in the Deadlands, crisscrossing the landscape. By the mid-Second Era, they had fallen much into disuse.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MW-2)</sup>

In [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), specifically in the city of [Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md), there was also a portal dais intended to transport individuals to the island of the [College of Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md). The complex had intentionally been set apart to function as a sanctuary and a dedicated space for scholarly pursuits. Given that research and academic endeavors demand seclusion and a tranquil environment, the Sapiarchs employed magic for their travel between the island and the mainland. Access to the dais for those outside the Sapiarch ranks required special permission. A regent, like Proxy Queen [Alwinarwe](Lore_Alwinarwe.md), had the authority to grant such permission. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the portal dais was manned by Portal Master Astirhil.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PMA-3)</sup>
