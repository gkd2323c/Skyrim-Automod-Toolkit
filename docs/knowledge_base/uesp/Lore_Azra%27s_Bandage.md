# Azra's Bandage

Azra's Bandage is a ring with magical healing properties.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SKAzra Ring-1)</sup> The ring was kept incased in a shadow-infused crystal by [Pergan Asuul](Lore_Pergan_Asuul.md) within [Earthtear Caverns](Lore_Earthtear_Caverns.md) before being retrieved by the [Soul of Conflict](Lore_Soul_of_Conflict.md) circa [3E 397](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_397). After it was retrieved by the hero, Pergan Asuul lamented that it was the handiwork of [Azra](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md) and was not meant for hands such as theirs.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shadowkey P-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EC-3)</sup>
