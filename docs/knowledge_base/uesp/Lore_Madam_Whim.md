Madam Whim was a [Dark Seducer](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) information broker who was operating from her establishment in [Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) from at least the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), and was considered a fixture of the extra-planar city.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mt CWhim-1)</sup> She once served a [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), but broke ties them a long time ago, and claimed have used many different names and appearances over the centuries.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LMAMa M-2)</sup>

During [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) and the [Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md)'s assault on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and Fargrave in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Whim provided her services to [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and their allies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOGo O-3)</sup>
