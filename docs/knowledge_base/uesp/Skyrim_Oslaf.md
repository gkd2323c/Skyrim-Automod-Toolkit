[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-npc-Oslaf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Oslaf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oslaf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oslaf.jpg) Oslaf [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-quest-Dragonborn_11.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Dragonborn_11.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_11.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_11.jpg) Oslaf working on the Temple of Miraak **Oslaf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) found laboring at the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md) with [Yrsa](Skyrim_Yrsa.md) during the quest [The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md).

After completing the related quest [At The Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md) he can be found living in his [house](Skyrim_Oslaf%27s_House.md) in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md) with his [wife](Skyrim_Finna.md) and [daughter](Skyrim_Aeta.md). Even after you liberate him from Miraak's influence, he will not be very friendly to you, stating that you are still an outsider in his eyes.

## Related Quests
- **[The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md)**: Uncover the source of [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)'s power in the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md).

## Dialogue
When approached he can say:

- *"The others call you a friend, but I still see an outsider."*
- *"At his age, Tharstan should have known better than to fumble about in dark tombs."*
- *"We'll talk another time, perhaps."*
- *"The Skaal value silence over needless talking."*

## Quest-Related Events

### The Gardener of Men
Oslaf will witness Storn's death and pay his respects to the corpse.

- **Oslaf:** *"You always protected us. I hope you find peace in the next life. Goodbye, old one."*

If you speak to him after Storn's death, he'll say:

- *"You brought this thing into our village. I do not know why Storn trusted you, but he did. Make sure he did not die for nothing."*

## Conversations
Oslaf may be seen having some random conversations with his family and other villagers:

- **Oslaf**: *"There's a terrible ache in my back today."*
- **Finna**: *"Complaining to me won't make your pain go away, Oslaf. Go and see Edla, perhaps she has a remedy."*
- **Oslaf**: *"There's no time for that now, I have work to do."*
- **Finna**: *"Then get back to it, you lazy horker! The day is wasting."*

- **Oslaf**: *"Are the potatoes ready for eating? I want to roast them with some onions for the night's meal."*
- **Finna**: *"Be patient, my husband. The crop doesn't grow faster because it hears your growling stomach."*
- **Oslaf**: *"Hm, perhaps I'll make an offering to the All-Maker. He can make our potatoes ripen as surely as he makes the snow fall from the sky."*
- **Finna**: *"I'm sure the All-Maker has more important concerns than your potatoes, Oslaf."*

- **Aeta**: *"Papa, when can I go on my first hunt?"*
- **Oslaf**: *"I've told you before, little cub, you're not old enough yet."*
- **Aeta**: *"I just want to see. I promise not to get in the way of the hunters, and I'm not afraid of the beasts in the forest."*
- **Oslaf**: *"I have given my answer, little cub. I know you are brave and have the heart of a great she-wolf, but all things have a time and place."*
- **Aeta**: *"Yes, papa."*

- **Oslaf**: *"When will you make another snowberry pie, Yrsa? It's been a long time."*
- **Yrsa**: *"The snowberries are not yet sweet enough. I tasted one just this morning."*
- **Oslaf**: *"Just make sure that when the berries ripen, you make more pies than you did last time."*
- **Yrsa**: *"Yes, I remember that you ate one of them all by yourself. Maybe this time, you will leave some for the rest of us!"*
- **Oslaf**: *"Not if I can help it!"*

## Notes
- Oslaf has additional dialogue recorded for when you would have [freed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md) him from working at the [Wind Stone](Skyrim_Wind_Stone.md): *"Finna? Aeta? Where are my wife and daughter?!"*, *"By the All-Maker, please let my wife and daughter be safe."* and *"I must get home, outsider."* as he returns to the village. However, he won't actually be found at the Stone, as he is forced to work at the [Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_Temple_of_Miraak.md).

- While entranced at the Temple of Miraak, Oslaf can be backstabbed using silent weapons like daggers without drawing aggression so long as he does not see the player as the attack connects. This combined with his essential status and constant health regen makes him an excellent source of Sneak XP, particularly if dual-wielding low-damage weapons like iron daggers, knives, or forks.
