[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-item-Windshear.jpg/200px-SR-item-Windshear.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Windshear.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Windshear.jpg) Windshear [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Katariah_02.jpg) Windshear, stuck in *The Katariah's* bowsprit Windshear is a unique [Scimitar](Skyrim_Scimitar.md) which can be found lodged inside of the bowsprit of *[The Katariah](Skyrim_The_Katariah.md)*. As *The Katariah* cannot be accessed until the quest [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md) has begun, this weapon is similarly impossible to acquire until that quest has been started. This means that the weapon is unavailable if you decide to complete the quest [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) instead.

Windshear's unique enchantment causes its attacks to [stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md) an opponent when hit and causes its bash attacks to [paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) them for a tenth of a second. As the enchantment only works for its bash attack, the enchantment will not work if a spell or another item, such as a shield or secondary weapon, is being held in the other hand. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) Windshear requires a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. The benefits of tempering Windshear are doubled if the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk is unlocked.

## Bugs
- The scimitar may spawn below the bowsprit instead of on it or may fall to the seabed below. - If it's in mid-air, you can walk off the spar near the end and grab it as you fall. Alternatively, you can shoot it with an arrow so it falls, then retrieve it from wherever it lands.
- The scimitar erroneously has a 100% stagger enchantment attached when the actual effect is delivered through a perk that functions exactly as the text on the weapon describes. It is only supposed to have a chance to ragdoll victims when block-bashing with it, but it also unintentionally staggers victims of regular attacks or power attacks regardless of the victim's size 100% of the time. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, addresses this issue. The stagger enchantment has been removed from the weapon. The knock-down effect works by using a perk on the player that casts a spell which uses a script to knock down the target and therefore it doesn't need the Ench Stagger enchantment.
