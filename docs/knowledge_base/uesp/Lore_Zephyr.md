# Zephyr

Zephyr is a legendary [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) bow.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CZ-1)</sup> Forged from [Dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md), the bow is noted for its exceptional draw speed, allowing the user to fire arrows much faster than with a regular Dwarven bow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SR-2)</sup> Zephyr is also said to have been imbued with acid, allowing the user to melt through armor with each shot.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CZ-1)</sup>

## History
Zephyr's most famous wielder was Katria, a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) adventurer who died while searching for the legendary Aetherium Forge beneath Bthalft in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Katria died while searching for a shard of [Aetherium](Lore_Aetherium.md) within the ruins of [Arkngthamz](Lore_Arkngthamz.md). An earthquake hit the complex while she was within, causing her to fall to her death within a cavern near the ruined city's subterranean entrance. In death, Katria remained bound to the mortal plane as a [ghost](Lore_Ghost.md), unable to rest without proof of the Aetherium Forge's existence.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) encountered Katria's spirit while exploring Arkngthamz circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201). The ghost led the Dragonborn to her body, locating Zephyr in the process. The Dragonborn recovered the bow with Katria's blessing. Together, the two of them located the Aetherium shard in the depths of the ruins, ultimately using it to access the Aetherium Forge. With her discoveries proven to the world, Katria's spirit could finally rest.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
