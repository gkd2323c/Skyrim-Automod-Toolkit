A Kiss, Sweet Mother *A description of the Black Sacrament in order to enlist the Dark Brotherhood*
---

![S](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/S_letter.png/54px-S_letter.png)
o you wish to summon the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)? You wish to see someone dead? Pray, child. Pray, and let the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) hear your plea.

You must perform that most profane of rituals - the Black Sacrament.

Create an effigy of the intended victim, assembled from actual body parts, including a heart, skull, bones and flesh. Encircle that effigy with candles.

The ritual itself must then commence. Proceed to stab the effigy repeatedly with a dagger rubbed with the petals of a [Nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plant, while whispering this plea:

"Sweet Mother, sweet Mother, send your child unto me, for the sins of the unworthy must be baptized in blood and fear."

Then wait, child, for the Dread Father [Sithis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sithis) rewards the patient. You will be visited by a representative of the Dark Brotherhood. So begins a contract bound in blood.
