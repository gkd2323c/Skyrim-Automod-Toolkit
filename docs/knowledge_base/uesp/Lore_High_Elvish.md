# High Elvish

**High Elvish**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> (alternatively **Altmeri**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Beast Galen-2)</sup> **High Elven**<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDC-3)</sup><sup>:6</sup> or **Elvish**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Live1-4)</sup> is the language used by the [High Elves](Lore_High_Elves.md) of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). It is directly descended from [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris_(language).md), and as such is very similar to its relatives like [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md), [Ald Chimeris](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ald_Chimeris), and the [Falmer language](Lore_Falmer_Language.md). For the longest time, High Elven was the common language used in all legal documentation until it was later replaced by [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md) in [1E 2813](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2813).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDC-3)</sup><sup>:6</sup> The common tongue of the empire, Cyrodilic, is based on the Altmer's speech and writing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-5)</sup><sup>:7</sup> To date, there are no known texts written in this language, but some words are known.

## Pronunciation
In Elvish, there is no soft "c", so when a word begins with "Ce" or "Ci" it is pronounced with a [/k/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English) sound. For example "Cey-Tarn Keep" is pronounced like "Kay-Tarn Keep". Similarly, the letter G is always pronounced [/ɡ/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English), never [/dʒ/](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Help:IPA/English).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Live2-6)</sup>

| - [Avuuma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Avuuma_the_Whirlwind) <br> - [Belanaril](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Belanaril) - probably *Final (Lightning) Strike* - Gryphon of the Welkynars associated with the western cardinal direction (the final dusk)<sup>[[77]](#cite_note-Wo EN-83)</sup> <br> - [Cel-hinwe](Lore_The_Flight_of_Gryphons.md) - perhaps *Red Wind* <br> - [Falarielle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Falarielle) - *Beautiful Cold* or *Beautiful North* - Gryphon of the Welkynars associated with the northern cardinal direction (the cold snow)<sup>[[77]](#cite_note-Wo EN-83)</sup> <br> - [Darremalatu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Darremalatu) - probably *Merciful Truth* (lit. *Mercy-Truth*) - Gryphon of the Welkynars associated with the eastern cardinal direction (the healing dawn)<sup>[[77]](#cite_note-Wo EN-83)</sup> <br> - [Graveld](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Graveld) - *Old Ugly* or *Ugly Elder* (lit. *Ugly-Old*) <br> - [Haeliata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Haeliata) - *Terrible Father* (identified by Justiciar Farowel as male, and as the mate of Nagravia) <br> - [Iliata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Iliata) <br> - [Kargaeda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Kargaeda) - (Shares the same "-eda" suffix as *Silaeda*. Since most Altmer seem to name their gryphons based on their appearance or magical abilities, Kargaeda's name might be related to his appearance as an [Aurulun Gryphon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Aurulun_Fledgling_Gryphon): *"A rare, tropical breed with dark plumage highlighted by streaks of vivid orange. Like the last embers of the sun lingering in the night sky."*<sup>[[78]](#cite_note-Gryphon Log-84)</sup>) | - [Mafremare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mafremare) - *Frost (?)* (Probably related to Mafremare's plumage, being a [Snowcap Gryphon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Snowcap_Fledgling_Gryphon): *"A white, alpine breed far from her natural habitat."*<sup>[[78]](#cite_note-Gryphon Log-84)</sup>) <br> - [Nagravia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Nagravia) - *Is Ugly* (She's identified by Justiciar Farowel as the mate of Haeliata, and her name should probably be translated in context as *She-Who-Is-Ugly*) <br> - [Ofallo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ofallo) <br> - [Silaeda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Silaeda) - *Shining (?)* (Given the context of its magical abilities and those of its Welkynar master, the name is probably related to heat, fire or the sun) - Gryphon of the Welkynars associated with the southern cardinal direction (the fiery sun)<sup>[[77]](#cite_note-Wo EN-83)</sup> <br> - [Sunnawel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sunnawel) - *Blessed Sky* <br> - [Welkadra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Welkadra) - *Sky-(?)-Ancestor* (Given its seemingly out of place and unique ability name "Elder Shockwave", perhaps this is what this hechatere's name is meant to translates to, something like *Ancestral Sky-Break*) |
