[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-npc-Captain_Hargar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Captain_Hargar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Hargar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Hargar.jpg) Captain Hargar **Captain Hargar** is the [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) leader of the [Blackblood Marauders](Skyrim_Blackblood_Marauder.md) residing in the caved-in [Broken Oar Grotto](Skyrim_Broken_Oar_Grotto.md). He wears [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md) with matching [leather boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Boots). He wields a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md) that can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality, and carries a small amount of gold.

He is working together with [Jaree-Ra](Skyrim_Jaree-Ra.md) and his sister [Deeja](Skyrim_Deeja.md) on some big raid [scheme](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!). When you track down the former to the grotto during the related quest, Hargar will try and protect his ally. Apparently, Hargar was locked up inside of the [Castle Dour Dungeon](Skyrim_Castle_Dour_Dungeon.md) up until recently, when he escaped. Solitude's executioner, [Ahtar](Skyrim_Ahtar.md) will send you to kill him during his [quest](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Kill the Bandit Leader](Skyrim_Kill_the_Bandit_Leader.md)**: Do a favor by slaying a bandit leader. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!)**: Help run a [cargo ship](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Icerunner) aground.

## Notes
- Hargar's [journal](Skyrim_Hargar%27s_Journal.md) can be found on the table at the uppermost level of the wooden tower in Broken Oar Grotto, and will inform you of some hidden treasure nearby. The key to the master-locked chest can be pickpocketed from him or looted off his corpse after he's killed.
- It is advisable to complete Ahtar's quest before any other quests in Broken Oar Grotto, as killing Captain Hargar will render that quest inaccessible, thus making it difficult to ever use Ahtar as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md).
