[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/ON-place-Hightide_Keep.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hightide_Keep.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hightide_Keep.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hightide_Keep.jpg) Hightide Keep ca. 2E 582 Hightide Keep is an [ancient ruin](Lore_Elven_Ruins.md) on the [Blue Divide](Lore_Blue_Divide.md)'s shores, in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). It was once a massive and heavily manned fortress that defended [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) but was destroyed during the [Sack of Skywatch](Lore_Sack_of_Skywatch.md) in [1E 1301](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1301). Now it is a shambling ruin hidden under the city's promontory. Hightide Keep was originally built by the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md), the [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) ancestors of the modern-day [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AEVII-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3-2)</sup>

In its heyday, it was a sprawling, painstakingly maintained and manned complex. During the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md)'s attack on Skywatch, many magical constructs were created and expended in the keep, destroying dozens of Sload warships. The city eventually fell when a Sload warcaster, after ingesting several volatile [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) reagents, threw himself at the base of the city's cliffs and created a great explosion that caused the greater rock face to fall to the sea. Hightide Keep was destroyed when it plunged into the sea.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AEVII-1)</sup>

The ruins contain a magical [crafting site](Lore_Smithing.md) infused with the special [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md) called Night's Silence, created by the mad wizard Mesanthano, who previously lived in a tower in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MT-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHightide-4)</sup> How his enchantment appeared in Hightide Keep is unclear. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruins were inhabited by [skeleton](Lore_Skeleton.md) warriors and a [bone colossus](Lore_Bone_Colossus.md) named Garggeel.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONHightide-4)</sup>
