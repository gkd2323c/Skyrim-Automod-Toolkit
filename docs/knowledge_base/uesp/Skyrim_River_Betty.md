[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-item-River_Betty.jpg/200px-SR-item-River_Betty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-River_Betty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-River_Betty.jpg) A river betty **River Betties** are [fish](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Aquatic_Creatures) that can be caught in ponds, lakes, and other [bodies of water](Skyrim_Bodies_of_Water.md). Despite their name, they are no more likely to be found in rivers than other fish in the game.

[Damage Health](Skyrim_Damage_Health.md) poisons made with river betty are 150% stronger than (2.5x as strong as) those made with other alchemy ingredients with this effect; they are the strongest poison ingredient that can be obtained in unlimited quantities. See the effect page for details.

They are also one of only three ingredients with a [Fortify Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md) effect in the base game. The [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) also adds [Burnt Spriggan Wood](Skyrim_Burnt_Spriggan_Wood.md).

## Ingredients

- 3 in the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 1 on [Orphan Rock](Skyrim_Orphan_Rock.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 1 in [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 1 in [Valerica's Study](Skyrim_Valerica%27s_Study.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- Fish barrels have a 25% chance of containing river betties.

## Creatures
Fish appear at fish spawning sites. Dartwings hovering above water can often be a visual cue; indicating the location of an underwater spawning site. Each spawning site generates one to three fish, which are randomly selected from various types of fish. In ponds and shallow waters, betties have a 20% chance of appearing; in deeper water, betties have a 14% chance of appearing. There are 691 spawning sites in 128 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:
