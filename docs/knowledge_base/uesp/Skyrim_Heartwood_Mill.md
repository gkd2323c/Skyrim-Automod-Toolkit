[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-place-Heartwood_Mill.jpg/200px-SR-place-Heartwood_Mill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heartwood_Mill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heartwood_Mill.jpg) Heartwood Mill **Heartwood Mill** is a struggling lumber mill on the shores of [Lake Honrich](Skyrim_Lake_Honrich.md), west of [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md).

The mill is run by [Grosta](Skyrim_Grosta.md) and her husband [Leifnarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifnarr), although Leifnarr has disappeared leaving her to run it by herself. She lives here with her young son [Gralnach](Skyrim_Gralnach.md) who helps as much as he is able.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A [Rift Guard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rift_Guard) patrols into the mill and towards the bridge keeping them safe. There is a [chicken coop](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) with two nests outside of the house, a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) and many mountain flowers that can be found in the immediate area. Between the house and saw mill is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) and [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md). With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) creation, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) and a bucket containing a [Fishing Rod](Skyrim_Fishing_Rod.md), a [Blue Dartwing](Skyrim_Blue_Dartwing.md), and three [Orange Dartwings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orange_Dartwing) can be found here. Fish from [Temperate Lakes](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Biomes) can be caught here. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is just south of here.

### Heartwood Mill
It is a small single story house consisting of a main living room and a single bedroom. Entrance leads straight into the living area with a set of shelves to the left, against the east wall holding a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of minor stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), two [boiled creme treats](Skyrim_Boiled_Creme_Treat.md), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), two [green](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md) and two [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), two [cabbages](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) and several pieces of cheese. In the southeastern corner is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel, a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) is to the right, along the south wall, with a bunch of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md) on top. A small square wooden table with two chairs in the southwestern corner holds a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a loaf of bread and an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md). Under the table is a [chicken's egg](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md). Against the west wall is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand, with a wall shelf to the right holding clutter. Hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). The door to the bedroom is on the north wall.

The bedroom contains two single owned beds, one in the southeastern corner and the other in the northwestern corner. There is an unlocked wooden [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) to the left of the first bed and a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) on the floor between them. To the right of the second bed is a food barrel with a copy of *[Herbane's Bestiary: Hagravens](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Hagravens)*. Against the north wall is a fireplace.

## Notes
- You can buy [sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> here after installing [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) by speaking to [Grosta](Skyrim_Grosta.md). You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so.
- Version 1.2.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) adds a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) here.

## Bugs
- Some nodes in the mill's exterior may not be grounded properly which can likely contribute to Grosta and Gralnach being unable to enter and/or exit their home consistently. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
