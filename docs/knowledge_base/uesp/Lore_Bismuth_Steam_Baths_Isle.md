[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/ON-place-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.jpg/200px-ON-place-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.jpg) Bismuth Steam Baths Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Bismuth Steam Baths Isle is a small tropical island in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md), situated directly south of the larger island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

The island's flora includes flame lilies, summertide flowers, orange and purple gorse, palm trees, grasses, and banana trees. The island also contains significant deposits of [bismuth](Lore_Bismuth.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/ON-load-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.png/200px-ON-load-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Bismuth_Steam_Baths.png) The inside of Bismuth Steam Baths At an unknown point after the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md)'s first arrival on Solstice, the area was developed into a refined retreat for Corelanyan nobles. The elegant getaway featured an outdoor pool by the shore, indoor steam baths, and spacious facilities designed for leisure and entertainment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSI-2)</sup> The island takes its name from the retreat complex.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the property on the isle was available for purchase as a private residence.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AAA-3)</sup>
