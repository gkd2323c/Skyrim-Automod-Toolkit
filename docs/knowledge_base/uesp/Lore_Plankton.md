# Plankton

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sea_of_Ghosts.jpg) The Sea of Ghosts, a body of water Plankton are tiny organisms that float in water,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ROTES-1)</sup> classified as either [animals](Lore_Bestiary.md) or [plants](Lore_Flora.md).

[Septimus Signus](Lore_Septimus_Signus.md) referenced plankton metaphorically in his *[Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls](Lore_Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)*. He sought to illustrate one of the origins of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) by imagining a life beneath the waves, endowed with sharp vision and surrounded by beautiful fabric. By placing the fabric over one's gills, one would begin to breathe in and absorb its threads. While the plant fibers nourish the [soul](Lore_Souls.md), "the wretched plankton would pollute the cloth until it stank to heavens of [prophecy](Lore_Sight.md)". He posed the question of whether one is the sea, the breather, or the fabric, or if one is the breath itself.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ROTES-1)</sup>
