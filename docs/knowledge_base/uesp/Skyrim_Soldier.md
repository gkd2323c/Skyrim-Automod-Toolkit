[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-npc-Soldier.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Soldier.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Soldier.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Soldier.jpg) The Orc leader The **Soldier** is essentially a highwayman; however, he is usually [encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Bandits_dressed_as_Imperial_Soldiers) with two others all dressed as Imperial guards. They aren't actually Imperials (as evidenced by the three naked corpses near them, and the fact that they aren't dressed properly). He wears [Imperial Armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Boots.md) and [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Bracers.md). He's always armed with a [Steel Battleaxe](Skyrim_Steel_Battleaxe.md).

When you come close to them, the Orc will greet you by saying: *"You there! Ah... citizen. This area is off limits. You are interfering in Imperial business. Yeah, so you'll have to pay us a fine... of say, 100 gold. Pay up, citizen."*

You can simply pay the 100 gold fine by saying:

- "'Here, take it. (Pay <Global=WE17Gold Amount> gold)"
- *"The, um, Emperor thanks you... Now shove off! Er, rather, have a nice day, citizen."*

Or you can deny by saying:

- "'<Global=WE17Gold Amount> gold? You can have it, if you can take it from me."
- *"If you insist. Come on boys. This'll be fun."*

You could also simply say:

- "'I'm afraid I don't have any gold to spare."
- *"Fine. If you can't pay with coin, you'll pay with your life!"*

If you have joined up with either civil war faction in the game, then another option will become available. If you have joined the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), then you'll be able to say:

- "'I'm in the Imperial Legion, and I'm damn sure you're not."
- *"Well in that case. You'll have to hand over your life as well as your coin."*

However, if you have joined the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebellion, the option will instead be:

- "'There's only three of you Legionaries versus one Stormcloak? Hardly seems fair."
- *"We'll see about that. Won't we boys?"*

Any option except paying the fine will make him and the two other "soldiers" hostile. If you pay the fine and attempt to talk to him again, he will just say: *"Go on, get out of here!"*
