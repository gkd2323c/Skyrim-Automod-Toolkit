[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-place-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg/200px-SR-place-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) The entrance to Blackbone Isle Grotto **Blackbone Isle Grotto** is a small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) connected to the sea, situated on [Blackbone Isle](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md). The cave is initially filled with [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md). It houses a haunted ship named *[Dead Man's Dread](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place).md)*, which can be repurposed as a player home after completing the related quest.

## Related Quests
- **[The Restless](Skyrim_The_Restless.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup>: Search for an ancient pirate ship.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The entrance to this cave can be found directly next to the boat connecting Blackbone Isle to [Orphan's Tear](Skyrim_Orphan%27s_Tear.md). For more information, see the [Blackbone Isle article](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md).

### Blackbone Isle Grotto
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto.jpg) Blackbone Isle Grotto [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Blackbone_Isle_Grotto_02.jpg) The skull Blackbone Isle Grotto is a flooded cavern containing *Dead Man's Dread*, a famed ship which became trapped underground roughly six hundred years ago. Two undead enemies block your path when you first approach the ship, although they do not respawn. A giant skull has been hewn into the stone wall of the cavern. In the mouth of giant skull, behind and to the right of the fire is a treasure chest. To reach it you can use [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) from the ship's bowsprit and aim for the mouth.
