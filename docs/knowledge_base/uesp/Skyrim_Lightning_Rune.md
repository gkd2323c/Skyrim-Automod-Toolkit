**Lightning Rune** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, causing [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) to health and magicka. You can't cast it just anywhere. When you press the cast button, the cross-hair will let you know if you are pointing at a valid surface within range. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- [Lightning Rune](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md), 50 pts for 1 sec

## Perks
- [Rune Master](Skyrim_Rune_Master.md) increases range to 100 feet.

## Notes
- You can set off lightning runes (your own or others) using any other Destruction spell from a distance.
