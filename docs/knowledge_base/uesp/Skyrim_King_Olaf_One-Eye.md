## Bugs
- If you manage to knock Olaf to a spot directly behind one of the draugr thrones, he may go idle, stop attacking you, and simply stand behind the chair with his weapon drawn. This is most easily done with three words of the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout from the top platform of his chamber. <sup>**?**</sup> - Knocking Olaf from his stuck position behind the chair will cause him to start attacking you again.
- Even though Olaf is supposed to be leveled, he will almost always appear as the strongest version possible, regardless of current player character level.
