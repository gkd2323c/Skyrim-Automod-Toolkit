[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Blackguard%27s_Hood.jpg) Blackguard's Hood The Blackguard's Hood is the hood of a set of armor that also includes a [chestpiece](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blackguard%27s_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blackguard%27s_Boots) and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blackguard%27s_Gloves). It can be found along with the rest of the set in the basement of [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md)'s [house](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory%27s_House.md), however Mallory will only give you the key to his basement after you complete [Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full). The hood is also only available to [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) members, as you must complete [Meet the Family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Meet_the_Family) to receive Paid in Full. Its appearance is identical to the unobtainable [Thieves Guild Variant Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thieves_Guild_Variant_Hood), though this hood provides five more points of [protection](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_Rating). Its enchantment functionally serves as an upgrade to the base [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Hood.md), [improving prices](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md) by 25 points, a bonus five points greater than that of the [Guild Master's Hood](Skyrim_Guild_Master%27s_Hood.md) (and with 2 more points of protection) without having to complete the entire Thieves Guild questline to obtain it. It can be tempered with a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Paid in Full](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paid_in_Full)**: Recover the [bonemold formula](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold_Formula) for [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md).

## Bugs
- All the Blackguard's Armor items will respawn when Glover Mallory's basement resets, allowing multiple copies of the set to be obtained. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The Blackguard's Armor pieces don't have an Armor Material keyword so the full set doesn't count toward the *Matching Set* perk. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.7, fixes this bug.
- The Blackguard's Hood, like other [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Hood.md) variants, has no model for vampires. Vampires attempting to equip the hood will appear bald. This also enables player vampires to equip it alongside other headgear. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.5.62.0.8, fixes this bug.
- You can get the hood without joining the Thieves Guild by taking a wooden plate, holding it in front of you, and running at door to glitch through. You'll need another plate to get out of the room if you wish to attempt this. It may take a few tries to work.
