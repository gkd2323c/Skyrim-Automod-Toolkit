[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-npc-Sebastian_Lort.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sebastian_Lort.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sebastian_Lort.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sebastian_Lort.jpg) Sebastian Lort **Sebastian Lort** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Classes.md#Conjurer) who may be found in [Rimerock Burrow](Skyrim_Rimerock_Burrow.md), far west and slightly north of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). During [Clavicus Vile's daedric quest](Skyrim_A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend.md), [Barbas](Skyrim_Barbas.md) tells you that Sebastian was a powerful wizard whose daughter worshipped [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). When she became a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md), Sebastian asked [Clavicus](Skyrim_Clavicus_Vile.md) for a cure, who gave him [The Rueful Axe](Skyrim_The_Rueful_Axe.md) to kill her with.

The burrow is a small, linear cave. There is an initial entry area, guarded by a leveled atronach, then a work area, and in the very back down a short path is a bedroom area. When you enter, Sebastian may be hunched over an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanters.md) in the work area.

You may find a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](Skyrim_2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9.md)*, along with a small library of other books. There is also a chest containing [leveled loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md).

## Unused Dialogue
Sebastian has two lines of dialogue that go unused due to not being conditioned properly:

- *"Clavicus sent you didn't he?"*
- *"You can't have the Axe! It's all I have left now!"*

## Notes
- Sebastian uses the Male Condescending voice type, differing from normal warlocks. None of the generic enemy warlock dialogue is recorded in this voice type, preventing him from using it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. His voice type gets set to Male Warlock so he can now use all the generic dialogue as expected.
