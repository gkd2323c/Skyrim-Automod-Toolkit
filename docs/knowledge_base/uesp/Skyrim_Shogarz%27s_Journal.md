Shogarz's Journal **by [Shogarz gra-Batul](Skyrim_Shogarz_gra-Batul.md)** *A historical betrayal is made again* *
---
I've long suspected the "Darkwater Snake" is my older brother, Urzog. The fact that he would take on that moniker made it all the more obvious.

It may be that same poison that ran in grandfather's blood is what made him betray us. Are we doomed to repeat the mistakes of our ancestors? Sometimes I wonder.
