# Fast

| [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-spell-Detect_Life.png) | Fast |
| --- | --- |
| School | [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) |
| Type | Other |

**You move <mag>% faster.**

This effect increases the character's movement speed by the specified percentage.

There are only three rings that use this effect, all obtainable from the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

## Enchanting
Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

- [Bloodthirst](Skyrim_Bloodthirst.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dawnfang_%26_Duskfang.md)</sup>
- [Boots of Blinding Speed](Skyrim_Boots_of_Blinding_Speed.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Netch_Leather_Armor.md)</sup>
- [Ring of Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Khajiit)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Ring of the Wind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_the_Wind)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>
- [Warlock's Ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warlock%27s_Ring)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>

## Notes
- [Fortify Illusion](Skyrim_Fortify_Illusion.md) will increase the effect on the Ring of Khajiit and the Ring of the Wind, allowing the character's movement speed to be increased even more.
- [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) will increase the effect on the Warlock's Ring.

## Bugs
- Upon putting [Ildari Sarothril](Skyrim_Ildari_Sarothril.md) into Bleedout, the player is given a perk that adds a multiplier of 0.01 to the magnitude of [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) effects on the player. This persists until the perk is removed upon killing Ildari. This notably makes the Ring of Khajiit and Ring of the Wind very weak. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The perk can be removed using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `player.removeperk 0403d597`; it will be reacquired when Ildari is next put into Bleedout.
