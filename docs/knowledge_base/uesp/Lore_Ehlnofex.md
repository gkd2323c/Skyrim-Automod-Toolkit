# Ehlnofex


## Usage, Language Family, and Genetic Relationship
According to legend, before the creation of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), the Ehlnofey lived on one of the twelve worlds of Creation.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TAA-2)</sup> Ehlnofex was related to the language of the [Umbrielans](Lore_Umbriel.md#Inhabitants), with speakers of the two tongues being able to converse with one another.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIC-3)</sup><sup>:67</sup> The tongue was introduced to Nirn in the prehistoric, non-linear days of the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-4)</sup> The original Ehlnofex has been described as a dangerous language with characters that constantly distort as one looks at them.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Thief-5)</sup>

It was passed on to the mortal races, eventually evolving into [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris_(language).md) and every subsequent [Elvish](Lore_Mer.md) tongue.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-6)</sup> It influenced [Atmoran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nordic) —with the two tongues sharing the word "[Talos](Lore_Talos.md)"—and the subsequent tongues of [Men](Lore_Men.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1C-7)</sup> such as [Nordic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nordic).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAH-8)</sup>

Ehlnofex and the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md) magics of the [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md) were preserved by the early [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) in the [Middle Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#Middle_Merethic_Era), when they separated from the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md), allowing them to retain ancient runes and magic which the other elves had began to forget.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Bt Ao M-9)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Descent-10)</sup> However, even their preservation eventually evolved into a new tongue known as [Ayleidoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleidoon).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-11)</sup> By the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the language, while still studied, was considered dead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TIC-3)</sup><sup>:47</sup>

[Archmage Ulfsild](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ulfsild_the_Evergreen)'s library beneath [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md), rediscovered by the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), was theorized to hold texts in some previously unknown dialects of Ehlnofex.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TLtt MG-12)</sup>
