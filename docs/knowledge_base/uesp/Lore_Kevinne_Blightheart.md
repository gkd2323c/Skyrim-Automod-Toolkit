[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_03.jpg/200px-ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Three_Tribes_Camp_03.jpg) Boneshaper Clan Camp at the Three Tribes Camp **Chieftain** Kevinne Blightheart was a [Reachman](Lore_Reachmen.md) leader of the [Boneshaper Clan](Lore_Boneshaper_Clan.md) active in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup> He made an alliance with two other Reachfolk clans: the [Rageclaws](Lore_Rageclaw_Clan.md) and the [Stonetalons](Lore_Stonetalon_Clan.md) and signed the treaty on 3rd of the Frost Fall in 2E 582.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTTC-2)</sup> The clans aligned themselves with the [Cult of the Black Worm](Lore_Cult_of_the_Black_Worm.md), which promised power and blessings of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Online:Bermund-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DU-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WN-5)</sup> and invaded [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOTTC-2)</sup>

Under his leadership, the Boneshaper Clan participated in warfare-related activities. They surrounded the [Shor's Stone](Lore_Shor%27s_Stone.md), choked off passage between [Stony Basin](Lore_Stony_Basin.md) and the [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) valley,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WTH-1)</sup> kidnapped local miners, and used tunnels as a site for their rituals to resurrect [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md)'s spirit.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BTS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MTM-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Unearthed-8)</sup> His people also practiced espionage and worked with agents of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TT-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AET-10)</sup>

Ultimately the Reachmen alliance was defeated, however. Their allied forces, led by [Stral Blackthroat](Lore_Stral_Blackthroat.md), were vanquished in the [Vernim Woods](Lore_Vernim_Woods.md),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONShroud-11)</sup> and their attack on the Boulderfall Pass was stopped.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SOTH-12)</sup> Kevinne, together with other clan leaders: [Vivian Witchclaw](Lore_Vivian_Witchclaw.md) of the Stonetalons and [Lydi Snowpelt](Lore_Lydi_Snowpelt.md) of the Rageclaws was defeated by the [King's Arrow](Lore_The_Vestige.md) at the encampment west of Boulderfall Pass, as per request of dying [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) soldier Melril.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-RTC-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Melril-14)</sup> The future of the clan following his death is unknown, but their invasion of the Rift was thwarted.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONShroud-11)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TN-15)</sup>
