## Cannibal Lancet
The **Cannibal Lancet** is a type of fish that could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Carp
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Carp.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Carp.png) A Carp The **Carp** is a large type of fish that can be found in temperate lakes and streams, commonly found throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are popular as food and as pets.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FM-1)</sup> [Koi](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Koi) are a distinct variety of carp.

- The **Grass Carp** can be found in the rivers of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md)
- The **Salasso Carp** can found in the lakes of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md)
- The **Scum Carp** can be found in the foul waters of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md)

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Carp.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Catfish
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Catfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Catfish.png) A Catfish [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Glass_Catfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Glass_Catfish.png) A Glass Catfish The **Catfish** is a very large, nosy type of fish with whiskers. They can be found in warm large waters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FM-1)</sup> Catfish skin is known to be used for parchment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> A common spotted species of catfish can be found in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). Catfish is used as an ingredient in the kitchens of Umbriel.

- The **Bilious Catfish** can be found in the foul waters of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- The **Fif Cat** can be found in polluted water within the region of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md) in [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).
- The **Forlorn Catfish** can be found in the lakes of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- The **Glass Catfish** can be found in the warm rivers of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) and in the underground cave waters of Skyrim.
- The **Ogrim Goonch** can be found in the foul waters of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).
- The **Stream Catfish** can be found in the rivers of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).
- The **Thorny Catfish** can be found in the rivers of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).
- The **Verminous Catfish** can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). It was previously presumed to be extinct.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Catfish.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Char
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Arctic_Char.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Arctic_Char.png) An Arctic Char The **Char** is a type of fish that is related to [salmon](Lore_Salmon.md) and can be found in the lakes of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).

- The **Arctic Char** can be found on the coastal shores of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), especially near shipwrecks. They are also found further out in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) as they can be fished off the coasts of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md)
- The **Frigid Char** can be found in the lakes of [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md) and [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Arctic_Char.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Chub
The **Chub** is a small fish that can be found in foul water across Tamriel. They are used as bait to catch larger fish.

- The **Deshaan Chub** can be found in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).
- The **Ichory Chub** can be found in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).
- The **Lake Chub** can be found in [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Cichlid
The **Cichlid** is a type of fish that can be found in the warm lakes of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md). [Flowerhorns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Flowerhorn) are a distinct variety of cichlid.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Cod
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Cod.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Cod.png) A Cod The **Cod** is a type of large fish with three dorsal fins that can be found in differing areas of Tamriel. They often can be found in freezing waters in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FM3-4)</sup> [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) sometimes export salted cod to other cities across Tamriel.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1-5)</sup>

- The **Eucla Cod** can be found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md), off the coasts of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md).
- The **Morid Cod** can be found in the saltwater of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md).
- The **Murray Cod** can be found in the lakes of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).
- The **Northpoint Cod** can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), off the coast of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).
- The **Suncleft Cod** can be found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), off the coasts of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md).
- The **Yokudan Cod** can be found in the rivers of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Cod.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
