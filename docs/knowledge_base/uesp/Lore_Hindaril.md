Hindaril was a notoriously powerful [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). He was so powerful that those who sought to destroy him were unsuccessful, no matter what they tried. Instead, all they could do was imprison him underground, within a cave system known as Redwater Slough on the banks of the North [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md), in the [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Hindaril continued to survive below ground for an unknown period of time. The partially-flooded passages of Redwater Slough became home to vampires and other forms of [undead](Lore_Undead.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a former [Glenmoril](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) [witch](Lore_Witch.md) named Melisande sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) in search of Hinadril's location in order to defeat him and claim his [ashes](Lore_Vampire_Dust.md). Melisande required the dust of a powerful vampire to create a cure for vampirism, and the Hero duly located and killed Hindaril within Redwater Slough, much to Melisande's surprise. His ashes were subsequently used to create a cure for Countess [Rona Hassildor](Lore_Rona_Hassildor.md) of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), thus allowing her to die.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VC-3)</sup>
