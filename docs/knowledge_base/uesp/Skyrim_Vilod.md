[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/SR-npc-Vilod.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vilod.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vilod.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vilod.jpg) Vilod **Vilod** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) living in [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) at the beginning of the game. He is the proprietor of [The Lost Man's Reprieve](Skyrim_The_Lost_Man%27s_Reprieve.md). He is not encountered again after the tutorial, presumably having perished in [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md)'s attack.

He wears [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) and [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.

## Quest-Related Events

### Unbound
During the carriage ride at the beginning of the game, [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) will speak of Helgen and mention Vilod: *"I wonder if Vilod is still making that [mead with juniper berries](Skyrim_Mead_with_Juniper_Berry.md) mixed in."* He is present at the execution, although hidden from view. He can be heard shouting as a Stormcloak rebel is beheaded:

- *"Justice!"*

You will encounter him again while [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) is escorting you to the keep. He is seen wounded and sitting down near [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md). If you approach, he can be heard making a dying request:

- **Vilod:** *"Tell my family I fought bravely."*
- **Imperial Soldier:** *"The wounds are deep, Vilod! I can't stop the blood."*

This is the last you see of him.

## Unused Dialogue
Due to being an innkeeper, he has the same dialogue in relation to learning magic as other innkeepers. If you had been able to ask him where to learn more about magic, he would have responded with:

- *"That'd be the [College](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). I suppose you'll be headed there, then. Well, don't let me get in your way."*

## Notes
- Five bottles of [mead with juniper berry](Skyrim_Mead_with_Juniper_Berry.md) may be found on the second story of the house that you jump into during the dragon attack.

- The enable for the bottles wasn't set correctly, so they don't appear as you run through during the introduction. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.

This can happen if Helgen is immediately returned to after escaping; he will be walking around near the headman's corpse and the chopping block. You can ask Vilod about learning magic at this time, and receive the quest to visit the College. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
