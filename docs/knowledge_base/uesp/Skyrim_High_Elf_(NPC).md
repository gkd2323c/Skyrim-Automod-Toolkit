[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-npc-High_Elf_%28NPC%29_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-High_Elf_%28NPC%29_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-High_Elf_(NPC)_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-High_Elf_(NPC)_02.jpg) A dead female High Elf in Fort Greymoor Prison These dead High Elves are female [citizens](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who wear randomly determined farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) (7 variants) or a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and random [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) (4 variants). They also have a 50% chance of wearing a random hat (3 variants). They have a 25% chance of carrying a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or a small amount of gold.

- Within [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md), to the left after passing through the secret corridor leading into the bandit hideout.
- Within [Fort Greymoor Prison](Skyrim_Fort_Greymoor_Prison.md), situated in a novice-locked cell.
- Located at the bottom of a [spike pit trap](Skyrim_Spike_Pit.md) within the second zone of [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.
