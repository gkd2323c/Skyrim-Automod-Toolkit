Bendu Olo, known variously as the ** Colovian Emperor**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONMer-1)</sup> **Sailor-King of Anvil**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SHV3-2)</sup>, **West King of Anvil** and **Dealer of Swift Justice to the Foul Spot of Thras**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-3)</sup> was the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) King of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and Baron-Admiral of the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSR-4)</sup> who led the fleet against the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) shortly after the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md) of [1E 2200](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2200).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE3Ham-5)</sup> [Syrabane](Lore_Syrabane.md) is said to have aided him in this victory,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VOF-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> which is marked as the time when Colovia began to overshadow eastern [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), leading to the [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup> His notes became a rare source of information on the Sload, and were disseminated by scholars for many years.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-3)</sup>

## Biography
Bendu Olo became King of Anvil at some point before [1E 2241](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2241), when he called for a fleet to be built to sail to Thras and avenge the Thrassian Plague.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SHV3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-3)</sup> Olo was joined in his efforts by [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md), [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md), [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Tsona-9)</sup> [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SHV3-2)</sup> [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md), [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TAFNSH-10)</sup> and [Nords](Lore_Nord.md),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DRS-11)</sup> and the fleet was dubbed the All Flags Navy.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1Wild-3)</sup>

Bendu Olo was said to have spared no expense preparing his fleet for the war against the Sload. According to some scholars he fully employed as much as three-fourths of Tamriel's shipbuilders while outfitting the All Flags Navy to brave the treacherous waters surrounding Thras.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSR-4)</sup>

The majority of the fleet was built in the shipyards of [Gonfalon Bay](Lore_Gonfalon_Bay.md) and All Flag's Islet on the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md). Other large shipyards used for the fleet included those in [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md) in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SHV3-2)</sup> and Shipwright's Regret on the eastern shore of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It was there where the mixed, provincial fleets were outfitted into the swiftest and strongest armada to ever sail the seas.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Za.27ji-12)</sup>

After the final assault on Thras in [1E 2260](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2260), which sank the continent and saw half of the All Flag's Navy lost, Olo and his remaining captains returned to the Systres. On the Systres the captains signed the [Concordat of Fraternity](Lore_Concordat_of_Fraternity.md), binding them and their races to the cause of "peace and cooperation", before sailing back to their homelands.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SHV3-2)</sup> Various Breton and Colovian laborers and engineers remained on the Systres, now a part of the Kingdom of Anvil, to build a monument to the All Flags Navy. Many of them decided to remain in the Systres after the completion of the monument, becoming the ancestors of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) inhabitants of the archipelago.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SHV4-13)</sup>

When he returned to Anvil he began converting what was then known as Anvil Fortress into [Anvil Castle](Lore_Anvil_Castle.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SZLMIw LS-14)</sup>

He was elderly and in failing health in [1E 2290](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2290), when he visited the dedication ceremony for the [Colossus of Gonfalon Bay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Statues_Notable_Figures#Bendu_Olo), built in his honor.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-colossus-15)</sup>

### Legacy
In the period after Olo's victory over the Sload the Colovian Estates began to overshadow the richer and more populous [Nibenean East](Lore_Nibenay.md), which would lead Colovia to secede from the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in [2321](Lore_First_Era.md#1E). The ensuing [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md) saw the Empire collapse, and rule over the Nibenay Valley reverted to a mercantile-magocracy.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PGE1C-8)</sup>

The Systres would remain the territory of Anvil until [1E 2327](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2327), when the costs of the War of Righteousness forced Anvil's rulers to sell the archipelago to a group of Breton coin-barons.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SHV4-13)</sup>

Taking the title Earl of Outer [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md) following the War of Righteousness in the [2300 s](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2321), General [Turpis "Volte-Face" Tharn](Lore_Turpis_Tharn.md) married a niece of Admiral Bendu Olo and set about fathering numerous descendants,<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-HTON-16)</sup> thereby connecting the [Tharn family](Lore_Tharn.md) to Olo's lineage. [Rusio Olo](Lore_Rusio_Olo.md), possibly Bendu's descendant, was instrumental in ending the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md)'s infestation of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) in [2E 437](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_437).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-So RO-17)</sup>

## Notes
- Bendu Olo's name is used frequently by Bethesda as a placeholder for the player's name in pre-release material: - In *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, various pre-release screenshots have the player's name listed as "[Bendu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-Screenshot27.jpg)" or "[Bendu Olo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-Screenshot57.jpg)".
- In *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, Bendu Olo's name appears as the [default name for the player character](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Bendu_Olo) in the [*Oblivion* Construction Set](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion_Mod:Construction_Set).
- For *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*, the example character in the game manual and the official tutorial for creating a quest in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) is named Bendu Olo.
- In the preview video for *[Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)* shown at Bethesda's E3 conference in 2018 and character creation [preview](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-prerelease-Character.png), the name of the player character is Bendu Olo.
