# Scripted Deaths

This article provides a list of all Skyrim [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md) who die because of a **scripted death** at some point in the game. A scripted death is one where a game script forcibly kills the character, no matter what choice the player makes. Scripted deaths are usually caused by a quest. [Essential NPCs](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md) are not immune to scripted deaths, though their essential status must be removed before the death script takes place.
