## Lanceata Pholiota
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/ON-flora-Lanceata_Pholiota.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Lanceata_Pholiota.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Lanceata_Pholiota.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Lanceata_Pholiota.jpg) Lanceata Pholiota Lanceata Pholiota is a large mushroom grown within the region of Shadowfen in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It is cultivated in caves, where [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md) are a necessary but dangerous part of its cultivation. It can be used in Argonian rites to track spirits.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOClarity-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOFal-Xoc-2)</sup>

## Lantern Mantis
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/ON-flora-Lantern_Mantis.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Lantern_Mantis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Lantern_Mantis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Lantern_Mantis.jpg) Lantern Mantis Lantern Mantis are a species of carnivorous plant which live in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They attract prey with the bioluminescent bulb which dangles from the "mouth" of the plant. When the prey is distracted it will stab out with their claw-like growths which resemble the front legs of a [mantis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mantis) bug. Generally they feed on smaller animals but will lash out at anything that gets too close.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Lantern_Mantis)

## Lavender
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/OB-flora-Lavender.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Lavender.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Lavender.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Lavender.jpg) Lavender Lavender is an evergreen plant found abundantly in the [Nibenay Basin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Nibenay_Basin) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). ## Lettuce
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/OB-flora-Lettuce_Plant.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Lettuce_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Lettuce_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Lettuce_Plant.jpg) A Lettuce Plant Lettuce is a commonly grown crop across [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), being one of the main foodstuffs. The leaves protect the edible head of the plant. A head of lettuce is a typical offering to [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), and the vegetable is a common sight in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) as a result.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Lettuce](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Lettuce)
## Leviathan Pizzle
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/79/ON-flora-Leviathan_Pizzle.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Leviathan_Pizzle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Leviathan_Pizzle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Leviathan_Pizzle.jpg) Leviathan Pizzle This fungi grows in Murkmire and is known to the Black-Tongues as one of the fourteen xal-fek or "sacred plants".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Wakumat-6)</sup> It is described as having a spotted cap, phosphorescent glow and hanging fringes<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>, however it easy to get this species mixed up with many other toxic varieties of mud-blossom<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Wakumat-6)</sup>. It is known to have invigorating effects and is one of the ingredients in the unique "Dread Fathering Elixir" used by the Black-Tongues during Bonding Rituals as to maximize the number of hatchlings born under the sign of the Shadow.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Leviathan Pizzle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Murkmire_Quest_Items#Leviathan_Pizzle)

## Lily
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/ON-furnishing-Flower_Patch%2C_Flame_Lily.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Flower_Patch%2C_Flame_Lily.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Flower_Patch,_Flame_Lily.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Flower_Patch,_Flame_Lily.jpg) Flame Lily from Solstice [Lilies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lily) are a variety of flowering plant that grows from bulbs, and can be found in a myriad of colors and forms. The lily flower is considered a symbol of [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Dibella Icons-8)</sup> Specific lily species include the Flame Lily, [Canna Lily](Lore_Flora_C.md#Canna_Lily), [Lover's Lily](#Lover.27s_Lily), [Tiger Lily](Lore_Tiger_Lily.md), [Sacred Lily](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sacred_Lily) and [Horn Lily](Lore_Horn_Lily.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), [Lore](Lore_Dibella.md)

## Luminous Russula
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/ON-node-Luminous_Russula.jpg/200px-ON-node-Luminous_Russula.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Luminous_Russula.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-node-Luminous_Russula.jpg) Luminous Russula Luminous Russula is a wide-capped, squat toadstool mushroom with a mottled brown, white, and green color, witch flecks of purple in the stalks. It grows prominently at the base of trees in the Bitter Coast region of Vvardenfell, as well as on the floors and walls of caves.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> If [kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) dig in a location where luminous russula grows naturally, they will eat it regularly, with no obvious effect on their behavior and well-being.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Luminous Russula](Lore_Luminous_Russula.md)
