[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-item-Nightingale_Blade.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Blade.jpg) Nightingale Blade The Nightingale Blade is a sword, originally owned by Gallus, given to you by [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md) during the quest [Hard Answers](Skyrim_Hard_Answers.md). Its enchantment absorbs the [health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) and [stamina](Skyrim_Absorb_Stamina.md) of whoever is slashed by it for one second. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Nightingale Blade requires one [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. The sword itself is similar in color to ebony, with the [Nightingale](Skyrim_Nightingales.md) insignia engraved on the guard.

## Bugs
- The highest level version of the blade (`000f6527`) cannot be tempered. **Mod Notes**: Temper Weapon Nightingale Blade05 incorrectly requires Nightingale Blade04 instead of Nightingale Blade05. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The blade is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk.
