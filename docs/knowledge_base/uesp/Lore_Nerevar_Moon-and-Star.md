Nerevar Moon-and-Star *An Imperial scholarly work on the legend of Indoril Nerevar*
---
*[This is a selection from a series of monographs by various [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) scholars on [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlanders.md) legends.]*

In ancient days, the [Deep Elves](Lore_Dwemer.md) and a great host of outlanders from [the West](Lore_Skyrim.md) came to steal the land of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md). In that time, [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) was the great khan and warleader of the [House People](Lore_Great_Houses.md), but he honored the Ancient Spirits and the Tribal law, and became as one of us.

So, when Nerevar pledged upon his great Ring of the Ancestors, [One-Clan-Under-Moon-and-Star](Lore_Moon-and-Star.md), to honor the ways of the Spirits and rights of the Land, all the Tribes joined the House People to fight a great [battle at Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md).

Though many Dunmer, Tribesman and Houseman, died at [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), the Dwemer were defeated and their evil [magicks](Lore_Magic.md) destroyed, and the outlanders driven from the land. But after this great victory, the [power-hungry khans](Lore_Tribunal.md) of the Great Houses slew Nerevar in secret, and, setting themselves up as [gods](Lore_Gods.md), neglected Nerevar's promises to the Tribes.

But it is said that Nerevar will [come again](Lore_Nerevarine.md) with his ring, and cast down the false gods, and by the power of his ring will make good his promises to the Tribes, to honor the Spirits and drive the outsiders from the land.
