# Yoku

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/RG-comic-Page_23-Speaking_Yoku.jpg/200px-RG-comic-Page_23-Speaking_Yoku.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_23-Speaking_Yoku.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-comic-Page_23-Speaking_Yoku.jpg) [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md)' father speaking Yoku, referring to him as "Sura"[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/RG-misc-Starstone Runes.png/200px-RG-misc-Starstone Runes.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Starstone Runes.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Starstone Runes.png) Yokudan runes written upon a Yoku Starstone [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/RG-misc-Ra Gada Runes.png/200px-RG-misc-Ra Gada Runes.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Ra Gada Runes.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-misc-Ra Gada Runes.png) Ra Gada runes found in the catacombs of [Stros M'Kai Palace](Lore_Port_Hunding.md) Yoku, sometimes called **Old [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md)**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ghr-1)</sup> **Old Yokudan**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-THFOS-2)</sup> or **High-Yokudan**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sis His-3)</sup> was the ancient native language of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md). After the Yokudan migration to [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) around [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808), the settlers began to assimilate into the larger Tamrielic theater and the language was almost entirely replaced to help stabilize foreign trade.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1Ham-4)</sup> However, certain groups of Yokudans maintained their language and traditions outside of mainstream Redguard society.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RG-5)</sup> The famous Redguard poet, [Weltan](Lore_Weltan.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) wrote all of his work in Yoku despite also being fluent in Tamrielic.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TA.27r-6)</sup> By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the language was still spoken, albeit rarely.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LOS-7)</sup> There is a regional variant of Yoku known as the Banthan dialect. It derives from the eponymous region of [Bantha](Lore_Bantha.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ghr-1)</sup>

Yoku has 48 different versions of the word "honor", which all trace their roots to ugak-ta.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LVSM-8)</sup> In the Yoku language, [chrysocollas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chrysocolla) held a strong association with defense. Interestingly, in Yoku, the term for defense is simply a different tense of the word for offense, rather than being a distinct word.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LAB-9)</sup>

Despite the geographical proximity of the [Lefthanded Elves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lefthanded_Elf), Yoku and the Sinestral language diverge considerably.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
