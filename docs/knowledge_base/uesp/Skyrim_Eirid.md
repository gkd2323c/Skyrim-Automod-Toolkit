[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-npc-Eirid.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eirid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eirid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eirid.jpg) Eirid **Eirid** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) living in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). Her parents are [Haran](Skyrim_Haran.md) and [Dagur](Skyrim_Dagur.md), and they run [The Frozen Hearth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Frozen_Hearth) inn. She wanders the inn from 8am to noon, goes outside to play till 6pm then returns to the inn and sleeps in the basement at night.

Eirid wears red colored [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000e80a8). She carries a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child) and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue

- *"There's not many people left in Winterhold, so I only have Assur to play with, and sometimes he's mean."*
- *"He always wants to play 'Hunt the Elf', and he makes me be the elf."*
- *"Assur says his pa says the College made lots of bad things happen, but the mages I've seen look nice."*
- *"The mages don't let us go in the College. They said it's not safe in there."*
- *"Assur and I are playing a game. Don't tell him where I am!"*

Should the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) take over Winterhold, Assur and [his](Skyrim_Korir.md) [family](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thaena) will be exiled. Eirid can be found commenting on this:

- *"Assur had to move away because of the Empire. But we get to stay."*

Should you talk to Eirid during [Containment](Skyrim_Containment.md), where Winterhold is disrupted by [Magic Anomalies](Skyrim_Magic_Anomaly.md), she will be scared:

- *"Did you make something bad happen?"*

## Conversations
Her parents can be heard discussing putting an end to Assur and Eirid playing 'Hunt the Elf':

- **Haran**: *"I understand Eirid's been playing 'Hunt the Elf' again."*
- **Dagur**: *"It's just children playing, Haran. I wouldn't fret over it."*
- **Haran**: *"I'm not 'fretting.' I don't want Eirid playing those sorts of games!"*
- **Dagur**: *"All right, all right. I'll speak to her."*

## Notes
- The "Hunt the Elf" scene between Eirid and Assur does not work due to invalid condition checks.
