[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-place-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg) Filnjar's House **Filnjar's House** is a house in the small mining settlement of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md).

It is the residence of town [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) [Filnjar](Skyrim_Filnjar.md). It consists of one area only, *[Filnjar's House](#Filnjar.27s_House)*.

As is custom with the houses of blacksmiths, all tools are located on the porch outside the house.

## Filnjar's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Filnjar%27s_House.jpg) The interior of Filnjar's House The house consists of a ground floor and a basement, the latter used mostly for storage. The front door opens to the sight of a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md), an [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) leaning against the fireplace, a small table with two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) and a copy of *[The Cabin in the Woods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Cabin_in_the_Woods)*. Next to the table is Filnjar's owned bed with a minor loot chest and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Shoes) on the floor. In the southwestern corner sits a clothes wardrobe with a [blacksmith's apron](Skyrim_Blacksmith%27s_Apron.md) on top, along with a small table that holds five gold coins, a coin purse and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). Lastly, a food barrel stands in the adjacent corner.

To the left of the entrance stands a table with a variety of food and drinks; one bunch of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), one bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and one of regular [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and two bottles of [nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). The shelves next to the table hold even more food, two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a cluster of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), three [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) and a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md). Hanging above the shelves are three [dried elves ears](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), and one sample of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). Finishing off the area is a food barrel in the northeastern corner, right above the stairs leading down to the basement.

The basement is spacious but pretty barebones. A food barrel stands at the foot of the stairs, next to a table with a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) and three [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md). Adjacent to this table is another table with a single loaf of bread. In the western part of the room, past the hay piles, is a food barrel, a wardrobe with clothes, and an unlocked chest with some random gold and minor loot. More loot can be gathered from an end table that has two [blacksmith's potions](Skyrim_Blacksmith%27s_Potion.md) on top.
