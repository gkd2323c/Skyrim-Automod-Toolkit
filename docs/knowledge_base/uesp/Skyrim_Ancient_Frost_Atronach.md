[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Ancient_Frost_Atronach.jpg) Ancient Frost Atronach The **Ancient Frost Atronach** is a creature summoned by [Arch-Curate Vyrthur](Skyrim_Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.md) during the end confrontation of the quest [Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md). It is similar to a regular [frost atronach](Skyrim_Frost_Atronach.md), but significantly stronger.

## Related Quests
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).
