[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-npc-Thief_%28NPC%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thief_%28NPC%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thief_(NPC).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thief_(NPC).jpg) The dead eldery thief This **Thief** is a deceased [elderly](Skyrim_Old_People_Race.md) [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) found in the [slave pens](Skyrim_Irkngthand_Slave_Pens.md) of [Irkngthand](Skyrim_Irkngthand.md) during the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest [Blindsighted](Skyrim_Blindsighted.md).

He was tied up by the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) in the torture chamber, the very first room of the slave pens, right near a [spinning blades](Skyrim_Spinning_Blades.md) trap. The instruments and the blood spatters on the floor suggests he was tortured to death.

He only wears [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md), his revealing [notes](Skyrim_Thief%27s_Last_Words.md), and a leveled [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md).
