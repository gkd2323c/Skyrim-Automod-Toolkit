# Horde Mountain

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0d/MW-concept-33.jpg/200px-MW-concept-33.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-concept-33.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-concept-33.jpg) Vivec City, the remains of Horde Mountain Horde Mountain was one of the eight [monster children](Lore_Monster_Children.md) from the unholy union of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), who initially escaped being slain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV12-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOV18-2)</sup> Three former minor [Chimer Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) helped subdue Horde Mountain for Vivec. The Warrior-Poet was overjoyed by this and consolidated these three houses into a new order, his personal guard, the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV24-3)</sup> Vivec then pierced Horde Mountain with [Muatra](Lore_Muatra.md), killing it. The Living God then dispersed the bones to form a new city for him and his Armigers, [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md), City of Swords.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV24-3)</sup> [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) blessed the city with her [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) to serve as its honor guard, and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) blessed the holy city with a protective spell.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV24-3)</sup>

## Notes
- [Michael Kirkbride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Kirkbride) likened Horde Mountain to "[Bruegel's Tower of Babel](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Tower_of_Babel_(Bruegel)) running around like a cosmic wire skirt frame across the desert".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-4)</sup>
