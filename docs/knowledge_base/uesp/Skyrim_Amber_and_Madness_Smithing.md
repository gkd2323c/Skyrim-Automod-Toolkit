## Quick Walkthrough
1. Investigate the area north of [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md).
2. Kill [Evethra](Skyrim_Evethra.md).
3. Read [Evethra's Journal](Skyrim_Evethra%27s_Journal.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
After defeating [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md) for [Restoring Order](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md) (or by sneaking to avoid his detection while he is still alive), you can loot the items on his work table. The note *[Note on Amber and Madness Ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore)* will give you a [miscellaneous quest](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Quests.md) to investigate the area north of Mistwatch.

Travel to Mistwatch, a tower just east of the Atronach Stone. In the geyser fields just north of the tower, you'll find a stone altar and the camp of the mad blacksmith [Evethra](Skyrim_Evethra.md). Approach her and the objective will update to "Kill Evethra". Do so, then loot [her journal](Skyrim_Evethra%27s_Journal.md) along with the unique [Arcane Blacksmith's Apron](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith%27s_Apron.md).

Read the journal to complete the quest and gain the ability to craft Amber/Madness items using the [Glass Smithing](Skyrim_Glass_Smithing.md) and [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md) perks respectively.

## Notes
- The [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend.md) [creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) takes place to the west in close proximity to this quest's location.
- Due to the large number of hostiles in the area, Evethra may leave her camp to fight [bears](Skyrim_Bear.md), [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md), or a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md). She is marked [essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md) until you receive the objective to kill her, but can be difficult to locate because of this.
- Although when you read the journal there is a message "You can now create new equipment with Glass Smithing" and a similar one for Ebony Smithing, to forge the new items you need to use the "Misc" section of the menu, not the Glass or Ebony section.
