This dead **Mythic Dawn Explorer** is found at [Rielle](Skyrim_Rielle.md) during [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause_(quest).md).

They wear Mythic Dawn Robes, Mythic Dawn Gloves, and Mythic Dawn Boots, and they carry a one-handed weapon as well as leveled gold.
If the [Adventurer's Backpack](Skyrim_Adventurer%27s_Backpack.md) Creation is installed, the explorers will wear various unenchanted backpacks.
