[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-npc-Harald.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Harald.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Harald.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Harald.jpg) Harald **Harald** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who works inside [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). He is named in *[Azarain's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azarain%27s_Journal)* as a possible member of a Daedric cult, although it is eventually revealed that he is a [vampire's thrall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire%27s_Thrall), under the control of [Fenrik](Skyrim_Fenrik.md).

Initially, he carries a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and a selection of [poor loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

You will be tasked with [pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) or following Harald during the related quest to see if he will lead you to the Daedric cult. At this point he carries an [elven dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger), a [quicksilver ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ingot), an [elven dagger of fear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger_of_Fear), a selection of [poor loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor), and some [bloody rags](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloody_Rags). Attempting to pickpocket him will update your journal. If you follow him at night, he will use a button at the back of Quicksilver Mine to enter a hidden chamber dedicated to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). Upon discovering this, you will be tasked with killing Fenrik and his other thralls, and Harald will attack you.

A *[Miner's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Note)* can be found on his body should he perish before finding the secret door.
