[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Lucia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lucia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lucia.jpg) Lucia **Lucia** is a homeless [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) who wanders around the streets of the Wind District in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). She can usually be found outside the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md), sitting on the benches around the Gildergreen tree.

Lucia wakes up at 8am and heads to the area outside the Temple of Kynareth, where she wanders around, [begging](Skyrim_Quest_all_Beggars_Have.md) all day. At 8pm, she goes to the [Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare), where she stays inside for four hours. Then at midnight, she goes out behind the inn and sleeps on the ground. She gets up again at 8am the next morning and repeats the same schedule.

## Related Quests
- **[Quest all Beggars Have](Skyrim_Quest_all_Beggars_Have.md)**: Take pity on a beggar. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Dialogue
When you first approach her:

*"Mister/Lady, could you spare a coin?"* *"I'm so hungry..."* *"Just one gold? Please?"* Speaking to her:

**Why are you begging?** *"It's... it's what [Brenuin](Skyrim_Brenuin.md) said I should do. He's the only one that's been nice to me since... since mama... Since she died. My aunt and uncle took over our farm and threw me out. Said I wasn't good for anything. I wound up here, but... I I don't know what to do. I miss her so much..."* You will then have a dialogue option to [adopt](Skyrim_Adoption.md) her. You can only go through with the adoption if you have a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) with a child's bed and container.

## Rumors
Your adopted girls sometimes may tell you that:

*"I played with Lucia in the park today. I feel sorry for her."*
## Notes
- The aunt, uncle, and farm that she mentions cannot be found in-game.

## Bugs
- Lucia may return to Whiterun if you have moved her elsewhere. Selecting the dialogue to tell her to go home will cause her to return to her proper home. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- When agreeing to let Lucia stay outside a little longer, she may call you "Pa!" even if you are a female character. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Lucia may completely disappear from the game. - This bug can be fixed by telling your other adopted child you are moving. Lucia will then reappear at the new home.
- Lucia may get stuck inside [Carlotta Valentia's](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia.md) house. - This can be fixed by picking the lock of the door, which is otherwise always locked.
