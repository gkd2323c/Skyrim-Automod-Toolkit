[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-item-The_Gauldur_Amulet.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Gauldur_Amulet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Gauldur_Amulet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Gauldur_Amulet.jpg) The complete Gauldur Amulet The Gauldur Amulet is an [amulet](Skyrim_Amulet.md) that can be obtained from the [Forbidden Legend](Skyrim_Forbidden_Legend.md) quest by combining its fragments. In appearance, this amulet is identical to an [ancient Nord amulet](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Amulet.md), while the fragment amulets are visibly parts of the whole amulet. It increases your [health](Skyrim_Health.md), [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md), and [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) by 30 points.

A Dunmer mage known as [Daynas Valen](Skyrim_Daynas_Valen.md) dedicated his life to searching for the amulet, discovering the past and locating the fragments of the Gauldur Amulet. Once you get all three fragments, you can reforge it back into The Gauldur Amulet, which provides the bonuses of all three pieces.

## Notes
- The Gauldur Amulet and fragments cannot be disenchanted.
- The amulet is [fully permanent](Skyrim_Permanent_Items.md).
- It is the only item in the game which fortifies all three attributes; with Extra Effect from the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) skill line, just two can be applied to a qualifying item.
- The Gauldur Amulet is one of the few things in the game (aside from quest items) that are entirely weightless.
