[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/AR-map-Temple_of_Agamanus.png/200px-AR-map-Temple_of_Agamanus.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Temple_of_Agamanus.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Temple_of_Agamanus.png) The Temple of Agamanus in Elsweyr The Temple of Agamanus is a [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) ruin located south of [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md), in the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). It is dedicated to the eponymous [Sphinx](Lore_Sphinx.md) of [Gazia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gazia), [Agamanus](Lore_Agamanus.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARRiddles-1)</sup> and prior to its abandonment, the temple greeted pilgrims on their journey.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARAgamanus-2)</sup>

## History
At some point in the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a faction of warrior-priests led by Sir Galandir took over the Temple of Agamanus and partook in rituals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARAgamanus-2)</sup> They later attacked the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in Corinthe and took a tablet that they believed was sacred to their order. In actuality, the tablet held the key to decipher an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), one that could reveal the location of the [Halls of Colossus](Lore_Halls_of_Colossus.md). The Guild's loremaster, Turamane ap' Kolthis enlisted the help of the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md), who needed to locate the halls for their fourth piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARHalls-3)</sup> Sir Galandir left the temple and ordered his knights to guard it with their lives.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARAgamanus-2)</sup> The Eternal Champion delved into the ruins and located the tablet in its depths. With it returned to the Mages Guild, the champion was able to locate the Halls of Colossus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARHalls-3)</sup>

Ever since the Eternal Champion defeated [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) and ended the Imperial Simulacrum, Imperial-loyalists took pilgrimages across Tamriel to re-trace the champion's steps throughout their quest,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AMM-4)</sup> and sometimes stop at the Temple of Agamanus.

## Notes
- The name is spelled as "Agamamnus" at one point in the quest, which is presumably a misspelling.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARRiddles-1)</sup>
