# Cyrodiil Vampyrum Order

*To you whom We have seen
Stalking at night by eyes keen
Transcendant of savages
Sating thirst sans avarice
Your coffers stay stuffed
By social graces robust
None know your nature;
save Us
None share your fate;
save Us
None welcome you as kin;
save Us.*
— [Manifesto Cyrodiil Vampyrum](Lore_Manifesto_Cyrodiil_Vampyrum.md) The Cyrodiil Vampyrum Order, simply called "Our Order" by its members,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MCV-1)</sup> and popularly nicknamed the **Cyrodilics**,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWarofthe Wormgod-2)</sup> is a powerful [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) tribe of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md). Much like the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md), the order has ousted all competing clans from the Imperial Province and become the single dominant bloodline within its borders.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IB-3)</sup> Members of the order are experts of concealment and political manipulation, so much so that the clan's true name is unknown, having been lost to history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IB-3)</sup> What they lack in numbers, the vampires make up for in influence and the manipulation of society.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MCV-1)</sup> The clan considers itself to be a civil fraternity and sees all other vampiric tribes as inferior, although it remains wary of the more enlightened clans such as the [Glenmoril Wyrd](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) or the Whet-Fang sodality.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MCV-1)</sup> The order has a strict conduct to ensure discretion among its members. The order honors its Dual Patrons: [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) the Kin-father, who all vampires owe their existence to; and [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), who they owe for their successes and social stature, and who helps them control their blood craving.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MCV-1)</sup>

The clan is made up of members who are influential in society and have procurement in politics. It is implied they are an ancient clan whose creation preceded their control of Cyrodiil from the rival bloodlines. As in the case of [Janus Hassildor](Lore_Janus_Hassildor.md), they do not tolerate scattered nomadic vampires who are either alone or have nests and factions of their own as they could challenge their authority if crossing into the province.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OB-4)</sup> Mylvor and Maerdra Tyricles were an Imperial noble couple who were part of the Order.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWarofthe Wormgod-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Although the order doesn't appear in any games per se, it is mentioned in the book [*Immortal Blood*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Immortal_Blood) in [*Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion) and the [Vile Lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Vile_Lair) plugin book [Manifesto Cyrodiil Vampyrum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Manifesto_Cyrodiil_Vampyrum), and it can be assumed that most vampires encountered in the game are of the bloodline.
