[4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Wolfskull Cave is a natural cavern found in the alps of [Mount Kilkreath](Lore_Mount_Kilkreath.md) in the [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) Hold, amidst the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It acquired its name from the Wolf-Queen of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md), [Potema Septim](Lore_Potema.md), who used the underground fortress in the cavern for [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) rituals.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRFalk-1)</sup> Her reign of terror ended in [3E 137](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_137) when her kingdom was sacked by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) in the aftermath of the [War of the Red Diamond](Lore_War_of_the_Red_Diamond.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bot WQ-2)</sup> Ever since then, the locals have been convinced that it is haunted by a malevolent spirit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRFalk-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf.jpg) Wolfskull Fortress brimming with dark magic As theorized by the *Speluncus Tamrielicum*, the various natural caverns across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), such as Wolfskull Cave, originally worked as capillaries and nodes for the living body that is [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and that it once pulsed the divine essence of the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) that created it, but they later died down.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONLoad Weeping-3)</sup>

It gained its name from Potema Septim, the Wolf-Queen of Solitude, who reigned over the kingdom between [3E 81](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_81) and 3E 137. In the latter half of her life, she waged civil war against the Third Empire and started the War of the Red Diamond. By the time the battlefront had reached her domain, she utilized necromancy, her kingdom was riddled with death,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bot WQ-2)</sup> and this cave system was used for her dark rituals. Ever since then, the cave earned the name, *"Wolfskull"* and it became the source of superstition, leaving many people to believe that it was haunted.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRFalk-1)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a group of necromancers took refuge in Wolfskull Cave and resurrected several [Draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) and [Skeletons](Lore_Skeleton.md). Odd lights from the cave and missing travelers had raised the suspicion of Varnius Junius, a citizen from [Dragon Bridge](Lore_Dragon_Bridge.md). This issue was reported to the Court of Solitude, but they were unable to assist them or send someone to check. However, the jarl's steward, Falk Firebeard sent an [adventurer](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) to investigate, where they learned about a plot to resurrect Potema Septim. The adventurer was able to defeat the necromancers and their ritual master, but the evil spirit escaped the caves and returned to Solitude's catacombs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRWolf-4)</sup>
