**Dwarven Centurions** (also known as **Dwemer Centurions** and **Steam Centurions**) are heavily armored [constructs](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) with either an extendable mace or hammer on one arm and a spring-loaded blade in the other. Some can emit a scalding blast of steam.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim Cent-1)</sup> They are not as common of a sight as lesser constructs in Dwarven ruins. *[Ruins of Kemel-Ze](Lore_Ruins_of_Kemel-Ze.md)* implies that the centurions have a weakness to frost and magic attacks, due to the steam that drives them, although they are highly resistant against fire, frost, and shock. They are sometimes found attached to a charging gantry when not in use.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

While they are typically taller than most men, they can vary in size. Many of the Steam Centurions found in Morrowind are actually shorter than men.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## Unique Dwarven Centurions

### Dwarven Mechanical Giant
A massive version of the centurion, the **Dwarven Mechanical Giant** (or **Steam Golem**), has an extendable mace on one arm. It is very deadly and can only be killed if attacked on its heel. A Mechanical Giant was located in [Bthzark](Lore_Bthzark.md), but was defeated by [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) in [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864) when he attacked its only weak point.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
