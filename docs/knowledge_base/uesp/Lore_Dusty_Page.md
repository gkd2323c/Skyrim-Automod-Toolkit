Dusty Page **by [Fjokki the Bard](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fjokki_the_Bard)** *A fragment from the Art of Love and Swordplay, the autobiography of Fjokki the Bard*
---
[Fjokki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fjokki_the_Bard) lifted his singing blade, Lovemaker, above the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md)'s head. Her silver hair shone in the moonlight as if made from the [same steel](Lore_Steel.md) as the sword. A single tear rolled down her ebon cheek.

"Don't cry. You knew I couldn't stay," whispered Fjokki. And then he was gone.
