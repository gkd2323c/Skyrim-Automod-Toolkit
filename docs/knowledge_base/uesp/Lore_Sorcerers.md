# Sorcerers

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/ON-render-Sorcerer.jpg/200px-ON-render-Sorcerer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Sorcerer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Sorcerer.jpg) A Sorcerer using Storm Calling Sorcerers are [magic](Lore_Magic.md) users who draw their power primarily from the [planes of Oblivion](Lore_Planes_of_Oblivion.md). Though spellcasters by vocation, sorcerers rely most on [summonings](Lore_Conjuration.md) and [enchantments](Lore_Enchanting.md). They make use of [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md) to summon [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) to aid them, and they curse their enemies using [Dark Magic](Lore_Dark_Magic.md). Their commanding of [undead](Lore_Undead.md) and Daedric servants is thought to gratify their egos. Many sorcerers have perfected a kind of unique elemental magic called Storm Calling, utilizing the power of lightning to become consummate combat mages.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind Sorc-2)</sup> They have been described as greedy for magic scrolls, rings, armor and weapons.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Morrowind Sorc-2)</sup> The magical disciplines of sorcerers are known collectively as **sorcery**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Although Dark Magic is often associated with "evil wizards" in the common parlance, it serves as a necessary safeguard against the power of rogue sorcerers by negating, draining, and preying upon the magicka of other mages. It also serves as a useful tool to counter the spells of Daedra.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Notable Sorcerers

- [Aryon](Lore_Aryon.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWSorcerer-5)</sup>: A Telvanni Councilor and former student of Divayth Fyr
- [Bahtra at-Hunding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bahtra_at-Hunding)<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>: An adventurer of grand renown
- [Divayth Fyr](Lore_Divayth_Fyr.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWSorcerer-5)</sup>: A well-renowned mage from Morrowind and among the oldest living people on Tamriel
- [Elante of Alinor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Elante_of_Alinor)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OBSorcerer-7)</sup>: An Altmer archaeologist with a fascination for Daedric shrines
- [Elenwen](Lore_Elenwen.md)<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRSorcerer-8)</sup>: First Emissary of the Thalmor in Skyrim during the Stormcloak Rebellion
- [Gothren](Lore_Gothren.md)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWSorcerer-5)</sup>: A powerful Sorcerer-Lord and former Archmagister of House Telvanni
- [Kintyra II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TMOP-9)</sup>: The eighth monarch of the Septim Dynasty and Empress of the Third Empire
- [Quintilla](Lore_Quintilla.md)<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TWQ1-10)</sup>: The former Princess of Camlorn and later wife to Emperor Pelagius II
