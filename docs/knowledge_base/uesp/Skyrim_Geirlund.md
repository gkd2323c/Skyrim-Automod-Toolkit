[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Geirlund.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Geirlund.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Geirlund.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Geirlund.jpg) Geirlund **Geirlund** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who will be called upon by [Avulstein Gray-Mane](Skyrim_Avulstein_Gray-Mane.md) to help [rescue](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md) his [missing brother](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md) from the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).

He will appear at the rendezvous outside of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md) together with his friend [Vidrald](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vidrald) to aid you, but only if you choose to let Avulstein accompany you and bring reinforcements. They will help you clear the yard and inside of the keep, but might die along the way. Once Thorald is freed, and Geirlund is still alive, then he and Vidrald will stick around the keep forever. (see [bugs](#Bugs))

He wears a leveled [light cuirass](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor) and leveled [light boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Boots), both of which may be up to [scaled](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled) in quality. In combat, he wields a leveled [warhammer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warhammer) which may be up to [daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) in quality.

## Related Quests
- **[Missing In Action](Skyrim_Missing_In_Action.md)**: Rescue a [missing Stormcloak supporter](Skyrim_Thorald_Gray-Mane.md) from the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
If spoken to he can say:

- *"I owe Thorald my life. Helping to save him is the least I can do."*
- *"No one should ever be left to the Thalmor."*

## Notes
- Geirlund's class is geared towards one-handed weapons, even though he uses a warhammer (two-handed weapon) in combat. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.1, addresses this issue. Geirlund's class was changed to Combat Warrior2H.
