Error creating thumbnail: File missing [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Iron_Sword.jpg) Iron Sword The Iron Sword is a [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) made from [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md). It specifically requires two [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot) and one [leather strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strip) to make, although acquiring one through other means is not difficult. [Tempering](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tempering) an iron sword simply requires an iron ingot, although it will also require the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk if it is [enchanted](Skyrim_Enchanting.md).

The sword is constructed out of iron, with small dents and imperfections across it. The grip, pommel, and guard are made of the same metal. The grip has a spiral across it to improve the grasp.

## Related Quests
- **[Dungeon Delving](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits).md)**: Do a favor by retrieving an item from a bandit hideout. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Gharol's Message](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gharol%27s_Message)**: Deliver [Gharol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gharol)'s sword to [Lash](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lash_gra-Dushnikh).

## Dialogue
Iron swords can cause a few reactions if you have one equipped from [guards](Skyrim_Guard_Dialogue.md), primarily negative in nature:

*"Pigsticker like that's not going to get you far. Best visit the blacksmith."* *"That iron sword's pretty undependable. You should try steel."* *"Iron sword, huh? What are you killing, butterflies?"* A guard equipped with an iron sword (or any other of the main types of swords) that sees you equipped with a [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) may also comment:

*"Favor the bow, eh? I'm a sword man, myself."*
