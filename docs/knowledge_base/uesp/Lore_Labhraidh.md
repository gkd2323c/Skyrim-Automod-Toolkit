[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/ON-place-Murcien%27s_Hamlet.jpg/200px-ON-place-Murcien%27s_Hamlet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murcien%27s_Hamlet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Murcien%27s_Hamlet.jpg) Murcien's Hamlet Labhraidh was a [Reachman](Lore_Reachfolk.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RE-2)</sup> leader of a commando associated with the Reachfolk Dark Witness Clan, who attempted to reclaim northern [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) from the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md). He led the group who attacked the town of [Murcien's Hamlet](Lore_Murcien%27s_Hamlet.md). He was active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TCOE-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/ON-npc-Medya_Zurric.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Medya_Zurric.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Medya_Zurric.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Medya_Zurric.jpg) Labhraidh's mother Medya Zurric Labhraidh was born during the [Jester's Festival](Lore_Jester%27s_Festival.md), on 28 Rain's Hand, [2E 542](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_542), to village elder Medya Zurric, one of the survivors of [Durcorach](Lore_Durcorach.md) the Black Drake's burning of the settlement of Murcien's Hamlet in northern Bagkorai.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VR-4)</sup> At some point, he joined the Dark Witnesses, and records of his birth were hidden from the public by his mother.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TCOE-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Squire_Theo_Rocque-5)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), all survivors of Durcorach's invasion except for Medya had died, and no inhabitants of the village were aware of the existence of Labhraidh. Following Durcorach's attack, the Reachfolk did not raid the settlement, avoiding it completely.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Rolbert_Foucher-6)</sup>

That changed, however, in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), when Labhraidh led the Dark Witnesses in an attack on Murcien's Hamlet.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DW-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Squire_Theo_Rocque-5)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Kahaba-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SEB-9)</sup> He intended to kill the Breton inhabitants of Murcien's Hamlet, while sparing civilians of Reachfolk ancestry.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WWBS-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TRAC-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MZ-12)</sup> During the attack, he fought against knights and soldiers from [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md). He murdered Sir Etienne Hu, a Breton knight who was lured to Labhraidh's family house as he was receiving treatment from Medya. He later attempted to kill Sir Etienne's squire, Theo Rocque, but was stopped and killed by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in front of his mother.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Muzgraga_gra-Khambol-13)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TCOE-3)</sup>
