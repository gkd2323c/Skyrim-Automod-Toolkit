[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-npc-Watchman_Sindras.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Watchman_Sindras.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Watchman_Sindras.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Watchman_Sindras.jpg) Watchman Sindras **Watchman Sindras** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) warrior who can be found inside [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md). He can be recruited as a follower if you choose to join the Tribunal Temple.

There are two different ways to earn his loyalty as a follower, depending on which dialogue option you use with the Matriarch. If you defeat Sindras and three other warriors in combat, he can be asked to follow you immediately after. If you Persuade the matriarch instead, Sindras will only become a follower after completing [Ashen Heart](Skyrim_Ashen_Heart.md).
