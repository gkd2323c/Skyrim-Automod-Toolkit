Ephrem Benirus was the Count of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) during the mid-sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GCGP2-1)</sup>

During the reign of Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md), he served as the Imperial Prefect of the city. After the Count of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md), [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md), rebelled against the emperor, Benirus sent the Imperial forces stationed in Anvil to seize Varen's property and holdings. Varen's nephew, [Carolus Aquilarios](Lore_Carolus_Aquilarios.md), repelled the attack after he rallied the single cohort of the Second Legion that Varen left in his command, as well as the Kvatch Warders.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TWATPQ-2)</sup> After numerous failed invasions against Kvatch, Anvil's defenses were practically nonexistent. The pirate queen [Fortunata ap Dugal](Lore_Fortunata_ap_Dugal.md) exploited this weakness and quickly took control of the city with her crew. Count Benirus was deposed and paraded through the streets wearing a slave collar.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CC-3)</sup> Fortunata proclaimed herself Provincial Governor and took up residence in Anvil Castle.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TWATD-4)</sup> Items bearing Ephrem's image were ordered destroyed, and the docking fees he had set were more than doubled.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CEBC-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AT-6)</sup>

In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a large manor in Anvil bore his name. Located where the Anvil Repository once stood in the Second Era, Benirus Manor was owned and inhabited by Lorgren Benirus.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OB-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-LB-8)</sup>
