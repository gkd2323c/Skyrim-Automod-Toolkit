[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Scouts-Many-Marshes.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Scouts-Many-Marshes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scouts-Many-Marshes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Scouts-Many-Marshes.jpg) Scouts-Many-Marshes [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.jpg) The target of much of Scouts-Many-Marshes' anger **Scouts-Many-Marshes** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) employed as a dock worker in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He and his clansmen reside within the [Argonian Assemblage](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage.md) and can be seen working around the Windhelm docks. He offers [common-level](Skyrim_TrainerLightArmorJourneyman.md) [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md). After completing a task for him, it is possible to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Scouts-Many-Marshes.

Marshes begins his day at 6:05am with a quick breakfast. He will eat for an hour before returning to bed for an extra hour. At 8am, he truly begins his day by going out onto the docks to work. He will spend the next twelve hours of his day begrudgingly working out on the docks. He will return to his bed at 8pm after a hard day of labor to prepare for the next day.

He wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to defend himself. Besides that, he carries a [key](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage_Key.md) to the Argonian Assemblage and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
When approached, he may make a remark on the Argonians' willingness to work for any of the local ships.

- *"[Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.md), [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md)... we'll work for any ship that pulls in, as long as they can pay."*

He might make a comment about the local environmental conditions:

- *"The water is cold, but it's a lot better than having to work up in the dry air."*

Scouts-Many-Marshes may talk about how he wishes there was not the ever present racial tension in Windhelm:

- *"I wish the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md), Argonians and [dark elves](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) in Windhelm got along better."*

Or about how the Argonians are doing the hard work that the Nords are not willing to do themselves:

- *"We Argonians do the work the Nords don't want to do."*

When ending a conversation with him, he may say goodbye in two different ways. If you are an Argonian, he will say:

- *"I hope we will speak again soon, marsh-friend."*

If of a different race you will instead hear:

- *"May the earth beneath your feet be always soft."*

You can ask him about himself.

- **How long have you lived in Windhelm?**
- *"I came here as a hatchling. The only thing colder than Skyrim is the Nords themselves, but we stick together."*

You can also ask about why all of the dock workers are Argonians.

- **Why are all the dock workers Argonians?**
- *"We work where we can get work. The Nords don't like unloading their ships, so we do it instead.
Sometimes cargo goes missing."*

You can ask him for Light Armor training while your Light Armor skill is below level fifty.

- **I need training in Light Armor.**
- *"You'll be able to run circles around the big, slow brutes in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md)."*

If you complete the [Battle for Windhelm](Skyrim_Battle_for_Windhelm.md) and [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) is dead, he may greet you differently.

- *"It's a new day in Windhelm. The [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) are gone, and so is their prejudice."*

- **Are you glad to see Ulfric Stormcloak gone?**
- *"You have no idea.
Did you know it was his decree that forbade the Argonians from living inside the city walls?
I hope in his next life, he's reborn as an Argonian forced to live in a slum because of some bigoted Nord dictator.
I'm joking, of course, but I'm a lot happier seeing the Empire running things in Windhelm."*

## Quest-Related Events

### A Few Words with You
Marshes will make a comment on how the Shatter-Shields are treating Argonians poorly if you are not currently doing the quest.

- *"We're used to getting by on scraps, but the Shatter-Shields want everything for nothing."*
- **Someone giving you trouble?**
- *"[Torbjorn Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.md). I wish someone would beat the coin out of his fat fists. He clings to every Septim.
He says an Argonian's labor is only worth a tenth of a "proper Nord worker." My people are not slaves!"*

You can offer your sympathies and refuse the quest.

- **Sorry to hear that.**
- *"We will make do somehow. We always have."*

Alternatively, you can accept the quest.

- **What if I talk to Torbjorn?**
- *"You can try, but we've gotten nowhere talking to him ourselves."*

If you succeed in talking Torbjorn down, Scouts-Many-Marshes will thank you by giving you a reward of two fortify-skill potions or three restoration potions. You will also be able to marry him.

- **Torbjorn will be paying you all more.**
- *"You talked Torbjorn down? I'm impressed.
Here, I was saving this for a rainy day. Maybe now I can afford a few more for my people. Take it."*

### Unused Conversations
Scouts-Many-Marshes was originally supposed to visit the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](Skyrim_New_Gnisis_Cornerclub.md), but his schedule never has him go there. While there, he was meant to take part in a number of conversations with [Ambarys Rendar](Skyrim_Ambarys_Rendar.md). He could ask Ambarys about the Nords:

- **Marshes:** *"Why do the Nords bother you so much, Ambarys?"*
- **Ambarys:** *"Why do you even need to ask? They treat your people as bad as they do mine."*
- **Marshes:** *"I think that's just their way. The Nords don't like anybody who's not a Nord, but they're not bad people, deep down."*
- **Ambarys:** *"Look around you. Don't you know what their little war is about? They want all non-Nords out of Skyrim. That means you and me."*
- **Marshes:** *"Oh, I doubt that very much. Who would load their ships, then?"*
- **Ambarys:** *"Just you wait and see."*

And another where Ambarys asks how much he is paid:

- **Ambarys:** *"How much do they pay you per day?"*
- **Marshes:** *"That's really none of your business..."*
- **Ambarys:** *"How much?"*
- **Marshes:** *"Eight septims per day, plus lodging."*
- **Ambarys:** *"Eight septims for breaking your back, and then you live in squalor. How much is your dignity worth?"*
- **Marshes:** *"If it's all the same to you, Scouts-Many-Marshes prefers to eat and drink in peace."*
- **Ambarys:** *"Sorry, my scaly friend, you've come to the wrong place. Around here we don't ignore what's happening in the world."*

## Notes
