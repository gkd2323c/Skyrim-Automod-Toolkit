# Apostles of Light

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SI-npc-Ra%27kheran_Plot.jpg/200px-SI-npc-Ra%27kheran_Plot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Ra%27kheran_Plot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-npc-Ra%27kheran_Plot.jpg) The Apostles of Light The Apostles of Light were an order of [Heretics](Lore_Heretics.md) in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). They were led by Ciirta, a woman who had witnessed [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s disappearance during the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Because of her belief that Sheogorath had abandoned his people to die, she founded a separatist faction of those who had lost their loyalty to Sheogorath. They called themselves the "seekers of light", and, as a symbol of this, they used [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) magic to emit light from their robes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Luminary Kaz was second-in-command of the order.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> The Apostles were based in the [Howling Halls](Lore_Howling_Halls.md), where they planned to one day storm Sheogorath's palace and overthrow the [Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SI-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Sheogorath's [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) infiltrated the group in an attempt to get Ciirta's eye, needed for the reconstruction of the [Staff of Sheogorath](Lore_Staff_of_Sheogorath.md). Ra'kheran‎ confronted the Champion, seeing past the disguise, and requested the Champion to retrieve several ritualistic daggers for him and a group of rebels in the order, as Ciirta had banned them from possessing weaponry. Once armed, Ra'kheran led an attack on Ciirta and the Champion took her eye.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SI-4)</sup> Following the death of their leader, Ra'kheran attempted to take control, to the dismay of the surviving loyal Apostles.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The group later seemed to have disbanded and became indistinguishable from the common Heretics found throughout [Mania](Lore_Mania.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Notes
- A working name for the group was "Apostles of the Song", this info remains in the game in stage 105 of the respective quest.
