[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/DF-map-Ayasofya.png/200px-DF-map-Ayasofya.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ayasofya.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Ayasofya.png) Map of Ayasofya Ayasofya is a region on the western coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). It was a county bordered by [Tigonus](Lore_Tigonus_(fiefdom).md) to the north, the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) to the southeast, [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md) to the south, and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) to the west in the [3rd Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Its main city is the city of Ayasofya. On the 2nd of Last Seed, the residents of the area celebrate a holiday known as [Maiden Katrica](Lore_Maiden_Katrica.md) in honor of a warrior who saved them during a previous war.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

After the event known as the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Ayasofya became a part of the Kingdom of Sentinel<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWITW-3)</sup>
