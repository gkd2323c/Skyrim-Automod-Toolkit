[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-npc-Illia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Illia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Illia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Illia.jpg) Illia **Illia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental) who lives in the [Darklight Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower) along with [her mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silvia), [witches](Skyrim_Witch.md), and [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md). Her mother plans to become a hagraven, but she needs a human sacrifice for this. Illia wants to stop her mother by killing her.

Upon arriving in the tower, you witness Illia killing a witch. She explains the situation about her mother and asks you if you can help her. If you agree, the quest [Repentance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repentance) is started. After completing the quest, you can ask her to become your [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). She will return to the tower when you dismiss her. She can also be [recruited](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md) into the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) and become a [personal steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>.

She is a very powerful mage and favors [frost spells](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage#Spells). She wears [blue mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd). She also carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower_Closet_Key), which opens two doors inside the tower - one containing two barrels and a chest, and the other containing a few barrels with fruit and a cupboard with some potions standing on it. She is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger), which can be up to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) quality.

The things we do here - evil, evil things. No more!"* **Why did she need to die?** *"We were supposed to find a sacrifice. Human sacrifice. I told her I couldn't do it and she attacked. My mother is going to become one of those things. I can't be a part of that. Gods, I can't run away from this. I have to stop her. I'm going up there to stop her. This cycle must end. I don't think anyone else here is about to have a change of heart."* **I don't have time for this.** *"Then I must do this alone."* If you refused to help at first and you approach her again:

*"You've decided to help?"* If you speak to her as you're going up the tower:

## Dialogue
If spoken to after the quest she can be recruited as a follower:

**You could come with me.** *"I suppose it would be better than sitting around think about all this. I could also do some good for a change. Sure, I'll go with you."*
## Notes
- If Illia is dismissed or replaced as your follower (for example, by the Companion quests), she can usually be found again in the entrance to [Darklight Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darklight_Tower). If she isn't at Darklight Tower, go north towards [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) using the pathway there. Turn left when the dirt road turns into stone trails. If she is not there travel to where you dismissed her and deactivate all quests, then set a waypoint to Darklight Tower and use Clairvoyance to follow the path she would be taking.
- Illia favors Frost spells. This can be particularly devastating in combat to both enemies and you, as she frequently uses [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md) after about level 20. If you specialize in melee, you should be particularly careful with her as a follower.
- Illia has no crime faction assigned and will therefore talk to [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) and [Vampire Lords](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>.
