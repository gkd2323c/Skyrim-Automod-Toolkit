[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Garvey.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Garvey.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garvey.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Garvey.jpg) Garvey **Garvey**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [scout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Sneak Expert), is one of the poor and sick of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He resides in [The Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md), and is an expert level [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md). He spends most of the day standing with his back up against the wall near the entrance to the Warrens.

He wears a set of [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) and [ragged boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Garvey%27s_Room) to his room and a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Weylin%27s_Room) to Weylin's room.

## Related Quests
- **[The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)**: Investigate a murder in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to discover a sinister plot surrounding the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

## Dialogue
His potential greetings are:

*"The Warrens isn't a place for your type. What do you want?"* *"Warrens ain't for you. Trust me."* When spoken to directly:

**What are the Warrens?** *"It's where you go if you can't afford a room anywhere else. About the time they opened the [mines](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), someone got the idea to throw beds in here. Laborers. The sick. The lame. We're all here."* **So, only the poor or the sick live here?** *"That's right. Welcome to Markarth, traveler. Thank the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) you can choose to live somewhere else."*

## Rumors
During **The Forsworn Conspiracy**, asking [Mulush gro-Shugurz](Skyrim_Mulush_gro-Shugurz.md) for the key to Weylin's room will have him refer you to Garvey:

*"You want Garvey. He handles the keys for the Warrens. About the only thing that disease-riddled native is good for."*
## Unused Dialogue
Garvey's second topic, where he reaffirms the type of people living at the Warrens, bears the internal name of `Dialogue Markarth Garvey Miserable Topic`. This is due to the fact that your prompt originally read:

**This place looks miserable.** This earlier iteration can only be seen in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit).

## Notes
- Despite what Garvey says, no one in the Warrens has any disease you can catch. These diseases are only present on wild animals in *Skyrim*, unlike *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, where beggars also carried such diseases. - Similarly, despite Mulush describing him as "disease-riddled", you cannot catch anything from him, nor does he seem to suffer any visible discomfort like Cairine.

## Bugs
- Garvey has three eat packages and a sleep package that get blocked due to the fact that his main package, `Markarth Warrens Garvey Front Package`, has no duration set.
