King Styriche (also known as the **Vampire of Verkarth**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOSK-1)</sup> was the leader of the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md), a massive army comprised of the undead and countless lycanthropic beasts which was defeated at the [Bangkorai Garrison](Lore_Bangkorai_Garrison.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSOHR-2)</sup> He ruled over [Verkarth](Lore_Verkarth_(Hammerfell).md), a city in central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It is unknown when he became a vampire. Styriche made a deal with Molag Bal that condemned him and the entire Gray Host to [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) upon their deaths.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MBSTE-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Rada-4)</sup> For this, he became reviled by his last surviving brother-in-arms, [Rada al-Saran](Lore_Rada_al-Saran.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MBSTE-3)</sup>

Under the leadership of [Empress Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md), the Empire invaded Verkarth in [1E 1029](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1029), routing the forces of the Gray Host towards the Bangkorai Garrison. There, they met their fateful end at the hands of Saint [Pelin](Lore_Pelin.md) and the Garrison's soldiers, and the Gray Host was damned to suffer at the hands of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) for eternity.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSOHR-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMOSP-5)</sup> Molag Bal briefly forced Styriche to fight on his behalf during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOAnchor-6)</sup> Styriche's remains were entombed by the Pyre Watch in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) along with the rest of the Gray Host.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONJO-7)</sup>
