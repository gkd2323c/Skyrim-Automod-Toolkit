# Meet the Character

**Meet the Character** is a running series of articles created by [Zeni Max Online Studios](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Zeni Max_Online_Studios) alongside the release of *[Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*. The series is focused on revealing new lore regarding specific characters from upcoming [DLC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:DLC) and [Chapters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Chapters). The series can be found on the official [ESO website](https://www.elderscrollsonline.com/en-us/news/category/meet-the-character).
