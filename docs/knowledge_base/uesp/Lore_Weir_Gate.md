[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/BS-Level1_Weir_Gate.png/200px-BS-Level1_Weir_Gate.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-Level1_Weir_Gate.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-Level1_Weir_Gate.png) The Weir Gate, blocked by a Hefhed [sigil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Sigils) The Weir Gate was a semi-permanent portal between Tamriel and the [Slipstream Realm](Lore_Slipstream_Realm.md) in which the [Battlespire Academy](Lore_Battlespire.md) was later established. It was known to have existed since at least [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOScute-1)</sup> It was connected to the [pillar of light](Lore_Pillar_of_Light.md) on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md); once the pillar was entered, the user would arrive at the Weir Gate in the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSIntro-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

In [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398), during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), the Weir Gate was sealed by [Mehrunes Dagon's](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) forces with a powerful [sigil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sigils) in the shape of the [Daedric letter](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) "Hefhed". This prevented the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) reinforcements from entering the Weir Gate, and so the [battlemages](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md) were sealed within.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Years after Dagon's invasion was defeated, the mage [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md) and his apprentice used the Weir Gate to access the shattered remains of the academy. Despite an extensive search through the ruins, they discovered that all links to Oblivion had been broken.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
