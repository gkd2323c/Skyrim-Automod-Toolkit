[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.jpg) Hajvarr Iron-Hand **Hajvarr Iron-Hand** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and the leader of a group of bandits in [White River Watch](Skyrim_White_River_Watch.md). He can be found at *White River Outlook*, a zone outside the cave, higher up the mountain. He is the nephew of [Ulfr the Blind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfr_the_Blind), a fellow bandit. He wears the unique [Ironhand Gauntlets](Skyrim_Ironhand_Gauntlets.md), a pair of [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) Nordic gauntlets with a [fortify two-handed](Skyrim_Fortify_Two-Handed.md) enchantment.

His [journal](Skyrim_Hajvarr%27s_Journal.md) can be read to gain insight in his life as a bandit, as well as his thoughts on several recent occurrences.

## Notes
- He isn't happy with the way his fellow partners in crime are treating his uncle and has written a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_to_Rodulf) to fellow bandit Rodulf explaining he won't tolerate any more "jokes" concerning Ulfr.
- [Carriage](Skyrim_Carriage.md) drivers may comment on Hajvarr's presence: *"Got keen eyes? Might see nasty old Hajvarr up on White River Watch. Piece of work. Jarl should just run him out and have done with it."* Likewise, [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) guards may warn you: *"Careful if you cross the river to the east. Old Hajvarr Iron-Hand and his brigands are holed up in that cave, White River Watch."*

## Bugs
- Hajvarr is set to respawn, despite being a unique NPC. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, addresses this issue. Hajvarr no longer respawns after he is killed, and is instead replaced with a normal Bandit Chief when the dungeon resets.
