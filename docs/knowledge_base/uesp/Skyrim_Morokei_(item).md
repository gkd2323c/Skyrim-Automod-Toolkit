[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-item-Morokei.jpg/200px-SR-item-Morokei.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Morokei.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Morokei.jpg) Morokei Morokei is a moonstone [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) gained by killing [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(creature).md) within [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md). The mask's enchantment doubles [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md). It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [refined moonstone](Skyrim_Refined_Moonstone.md).

## Notes
- Placing Morokei on its pedestal at [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md) will remove any bonuses that it gained from tempering.
- Morokei has the second lowest armor rating of the masks, only slightly higher than that of the [Wooden Mask](Skyrim_Wooden_Mask.md).
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning that they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- Morokei means "Glorious" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a [special holder](Skyrim_Special_Holder.md) for this item.

## Bugs
- Morokei has an incorrect enchantment assigned: instead of its unique enchantment that doubles [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) and [fortifies shouts by 20%](Skyrim_Fortify_Shouts.md), its in-game enchantment is the same as all [<Apparel> of Recovery](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#of_Recovery). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
