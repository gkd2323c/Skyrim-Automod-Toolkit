### Council Affairs of Note
King Hlaalu [Athyn Llethan](Lore_Athyn_Llethan.md), High Councilor and Lord of Morrowind, grants relief to merchants complaining of high tariffs on imported alcoholic beverages.

The council is pleased to report a reduction in the incidence of theft and violent crime in the Hlaalu House Districts, thanks to the vigilance of the [Legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and stern sentences by the magistrates. The council laments the unfortunate disturbances of the public peace resulting from the increasingly aggressive competition between the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) and the [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md) for control of the black markets.

A minor tax revolt in [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) was suppressed without undue harm to life and property. The council sent deputations to the [Duke](Lore_Vedam_Dren.md) to express their concerns over the high tax rates and the injurious effect of high tariffs on trade.
