# Bloodthorn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-item-Bloodthorn.jpg/200px-SR-item-Bloodthorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bloodthorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Bloodthorn.jpg) Bloodthorn (Skyrim) The Bloodthorn, also known as the **Bloodthorn Dagger**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BDOTT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Patch-2)</sup> is an impressive<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:259</sup> steel dagger of unknown origins, associated with various [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) factions.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ITH-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Forsworn-5)</sup> The weapon is imbued with an enchantment that drains vitality from those struck by it as well as traps the souls of those killed by it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Bloodthorn-6)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the Bloodthorn was in possession of [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) wise woman Laganakh of [Seamount Clan](Lore_Seamount_Clan.md). It may have been connected to the [Bloodthorn Cult](Lore_Bloodthorn_Cult.md), as it shares its name with the faction. Laganakh gifted it to the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) after they helped her to investigate the hideout of the Bloodthorn Cult lair on [Betnikh](Lore_Betony.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ITH-4)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the Bloodthorn was found by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) on top of the Hag's End in the northern parts of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HE-7)</sup> It was previously in the possession of the [witch](Lore_Witches.md) who was sacrificed on the altar in the Hag's End.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:259</sup>

## Known Owners
- Laganakh<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ITH-4)</sup>
- Unknown Witch<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HE-7)</sup>

## Notes
- In ESO, this item is named "Bloodthorn Dagger of the Trainee" due to being part of the [Armor of the Trainee](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Armor_of_the_Trainee) set. Wearing several items from this set will fortify health, magicka, and stamina.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AOT-8)</sup> It is enchanted to restore magicka to the wielder and deal additional damage to the target on hit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BDOTT-1)</sup> Formerly its name was just "Bloodthorn Dagger".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Patch-2)</sup>
- It was listed alongside numerous other artifacts in ESO's Cyrodiil Campaigns.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Campaigns-9)</sup>
