*Improves the caster's armor rating by <mag> points for 60 seconds.* These spells improve your [Armor Rating](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Armor_Rating), providing additional protection from physical attacks.

## Effects
- [Armor](Skyrim_Armor_(effect).md), <mag> pts for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Stability](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stability), raises duration to 90 seconds.
- [Alteration Dual Casting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alteration_Dual_Casting), increases duration to 132 seconds, if cast with both hands. - Both can be applied, raising the duration to 198 seconds.
- [Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md), increases the spells' magnitudes according to the chart below:

| Spell | Default | Rank 1 | Rank 2 | Rank 3 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Oakflesh | 40 | 80 | 100 | 120 |
| Stoneflesh | 60 | 120 | 150 | 180 |
| Ironflesh | 80 | 160 | 200 | 240 |
| Ebonyflesh | 100 | 200 | 250 | 300 |

## Notes
- *Mage Armor* bonuses only apply if you are not wearing any armor (other than a shield). Otherwise, the default value still applies.
- *Stability* and *Alteration Dual Casting* perk effects stack rendering their duration to 198 secs, if cast with both hands.
- Vampire players with Necromage perk gain 25 percent more armor rating, armor bonus effect also last longer.
- Armor spells do not stack. They are concurrent with the effect of the strongest spell taking precedence over other armor spells.
