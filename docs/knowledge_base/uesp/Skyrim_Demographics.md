## Notes
- Only characters with unique names are included here.
- The [Elder](Skyrim_Elder.md) race is not strictly a race in terms of lore, but is considered a different race by the game. Likely, the majority of these can be considered to be Nords, but this is speculative and cannot always be confirmed.
- People that appear only during certain quests were not counted.
- People were counted for the place they first appear in game (unless it is during [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)).
- Either [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) or [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) will appear in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md), depending on your choice during Unbound. As they both are Nords they were counted as one person for this data.
- People in [dungeons](Skyrim_Dungeons.md) were not counted.
- [Random encounters](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) were not counted.
- As Khajiit caravans travel between two cities in different holds, they were counted only for "Total" in the "By Hold" section.
- [Aringoth](Skyrim_Aringoth.md) was counted as Altmer though he is referred to as a Wood Elf in [Loud and Clear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Loud_and_Clear), and version 1.2.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) changes his race to Bosmer.
- People in areas added by [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) were not counted.
- Bards and carriage drivers that can be hired for your [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) house were not counted.
- All [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) members including [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) were counted for Falkreath.
- Crew of [Northern Maiden](Skyrim_Northern_Maiden.md) was not counted as they appear in both Skyrim and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).
