# Prostitutes Guild

[![](https://images.uesp.net/b/bf/DF-NPC-Flat_184_8.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-NPC-Flat_184_8.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-NPC-Flat_184_8.png) A prostitute The Prostitutes Guild was sanctioned in [2E 321](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_321) by Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) with the approval of the [Guilds Act](Lore_Guild_Act.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Each local chapter of the Prostitutes Guild has a Guildmistress who handles appointments with clients and oversees the safety of the guild's [prostitutes](Lore_Prostitution.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOT4-2)</sup>

The Prostitutes Guildhouse of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) is a large, almost palatial building located in an affluent part of the city.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOT4-2)</sup>
