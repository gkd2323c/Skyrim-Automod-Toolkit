### Council Affairs of Note
King Hlaalu [Athyn Llethan](Lore_Athyn_Llethan.md), High Councilor and Lord of Morrowind, imposes favorable tariffs on [flin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Flin) [an imported fortified Imperial alcoholic beverage]. The council protests the continuing burdensome tariffs on the native beverages [sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sujamma), [greef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greef), and [shein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shein).

Smuggling and organized crime have become increasingly aggressive and violent in the Redoran House Districts. The councilors blame local corruption, weakened enforcement, and aggressive competition between the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) and the [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md).

An unfortunate tax revolt in [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) was put down after significant property damage and loss of life. The council warned that such disturbances might spread to Ald'ruhn if the heavy burden of Imperial taxes were not alleviated.
