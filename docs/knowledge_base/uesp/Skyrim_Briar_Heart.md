## Ingredients
10-11<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 7-8<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations:

- 2 in [Gallows Rock](Skyrim_Gallows_Rock.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 2 in [Japhet's Folly Towers](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly_Towers.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 on the table by the Word Wall south of [Bard's Leap Summit](Skyrim_Bard%27s_Leap_Summit.md)
- 1 in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 1 in [Glenmoril Coven](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Coven.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 1 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))
- 1 in [Soljund's Sinkhole](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soljund%27s_Sinkhole) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 1 in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> in a locked display case

It is categorized as a rare ingredient, meaning that most apothecary merchants have a 21% chance of carrying 1-4 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found only in rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
While their exact nature is unknown, due to its natural loot location from [Forsworn Briarhearts](Skyrim_Forsworn_Briarheart.md), it seems they really do serve as actual hearts; from the look of events at [Lost Valley Redoubt](Skyrim_Lost_Valley_Redoubt.md), dead Forsworn are resurrected in a ceremony by [hagravens](Skyrim_Hagraven.md), replacing their human hearts with these items. Interestingly enough, pickpocketing the briar heart from a Forsworn Briarheart will instantly kill the target.

## Notes
- The base magnitude of the [Fortify Block](Skyrim_Fortify_Block.md) effect for this ingredient is 2 instead of 4. This is only relevant if [honeycomb](Skyrim_Honeycomb.md) and briar heart are combined, in which case the resulting potion will have a 50% smaller magnitude than a typical custom Fortify Block potion.
- If you pickpocket a Briar Heart from a Forsworn Briarheat, he will die instantly.
- Briar Hearts resemble real world Dragonfruit in shape, though the in-game ingredient is implied to be a seed, not a fruit.

## Bugs
- Forsworn Briarhearts may send [Hired Thugs](Skyrim_Hired_Thug.md) after you for pickpocketing their Briar Hearts. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
- Events during the [Repairing the Phial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Repairing_the_Phial) quest can permanently turn all Briar Hearts into quest items; you can't drop them or use them in any way. See the quest page for tips on how to avoid this, or fix it if it has already occurred. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
