[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-npc-Hans.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hans.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hans.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hans.jpg) Hans Hans is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who can be found outside [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md). His body lies next to an [empty wine bottle](Skyrim_Empty_Wine_Bottle.md), a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), a still-lit [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), and *[Sindnar's Tower Notes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sindnar%27s_Tower_Notes)*. He was killed by wildlife while attempting to unseal the tower's entrance. He failed to figure out how to activate the seal, although his efforts will aid you during the related quest.

He is wearing [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md), and [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md). He carries two torches along with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and possibly some gold. His body can be looted for *[Hans's Journal](Skyrim_Hans%27s_Journal.md)* and [Spell Tome: Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Flames).

His body (and the nearby clutter) disappears after first entering the Myrwatch Gallery.
