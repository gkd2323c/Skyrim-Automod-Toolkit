[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/BL-quest-The_Pit_of_Pestilence.jpg/200px-BL-quest-The_Pit_of_Pestilence.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-The_Pit_of_Pestilence.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-quest-The_Pit_of_Pestilence.jpg) The Pit of Pestilence The Pits,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRPeryite-1)</sup> also known as **Peryite's Pits**<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Benedict Cartiabatch-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ICDL-3)</sup>, or the **Pits of Pestilence** (sometimes singular **Pit of Pestilence**),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Benedict Cartiabatch-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-4)</sup> is a realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Planes_of_Oblivion.md), created and ruled over by [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md), the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Pestilence. Here, Peryite guards the lowest orders of Oblivion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TBD-5)</sup>

It is unknown what the realm looks like overall. One part of the realm appears as a rocky cave system with occasional skylights filled with a hazy green poisonous fog, floating green embers, and some underground plants and is described as permeated with a noxious presence of miasma. This area of the realm has been home to necromancers and undead.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-4)</sup> Another part of the realm resembles the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), with lava seas, volcanic islands, and ruined structures. It is also home to many of the same Daedra as the Deadlands.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBPeryite-6)</sup> The [Imperial Geographical Society](Lore_Imperial_Geographical_Society.md) believes that the realm is usually inaccessible to mortals,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ICDL-3)</sup> but there have been several exceptions.

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/OB-place-Peryite%27s_Realm.jpg/200px-OB-place-Peryite%27s_Realm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Peryite%27s_Realm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Peryite%27s_Realm.jpg) A portal to Tamriel in Peryite's Realm Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Hidden Kindred created "Nightmare Alchemy" mounts by using tinctures derived from the Pits and [Quagmire](Lore_Quagmire.md). They used reagants such as bones salvaged from the Pits,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NAE-7)</sup> [Tenmar](Lore_Tenmar_Forest.md) plants watered from the Pits,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-NAS-8)</sup> ooze from Pits-born [plague hoppers](Lore_Death_Hopper.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-NAT-9)</sup> and slime formed from fungal spores mixed with Pits [minerals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Minerals) to create the key components for the mounts.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-NAV-10)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), five worshippers of Peryite performed a ritual at the Prince's hidden shrine, located along the banks of the [Silverfish River](Lore_Silverfish_River.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The spell was intended to summon Peryite, but instead, the souls of the worshippers were sent to the Pits, where they became trapped. The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) stumbled upon the motionless bodies of the worshippers by the shrine, and was tasked by Peryite to travel to a plane in the Pits and recover their souls. The plane consisted of a volcanic island inhabited by many [daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), with a Blood Well tower and a series of volcanic caves called the Sightless Grotto. The Hero rescued the souls of the worshippers and returned them to Tamriel to earn the Prince's favor.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBPeryite-6)</sup>

Circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), a mysterious portal opened in [Rivercrest](Lore_Rivercrest.md) that lead to the Pit of Pestilence. [The Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(person).md) entered it and fought through a "pestilent horde" of [Wights](Lore_Wight.md) and [Necromancers](Lore_Necromancers.md) in order to prevent its noxious miasma from crossing over.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Blades-4)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Peryite tasked the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) with slaying Orchendor, a former priest of Peryite who had abandoned the Prince. In death, Orchendor's soul was sent to the Pits, where Peryite punished him for his disobedience.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SROnly-11)</sup>
