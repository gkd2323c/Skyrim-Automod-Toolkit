[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-npc-Desperate_Gambler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Desperate_Gambler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Desperate_Gambler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Desperate_Gambler.jpg) Desperate Gambler The **Desperate Gambler** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who may appear in the [Windhelm Barracks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windhelm_Barracks) during the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). He will only be enabled if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) picks him as the contract giver.

He will be chosen randomly from a list of ten people who performed the Black Sacrament and want someone killed. When approached, he will choose between a total of ten possible targets, hand you a leveled amount of gold and blindly expect you to carry out the task.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0003452f). He carries a copy of *[A Kiss, Sweet Mother](Skyrim_A_Kiss,_Sweet_Mother.md)* and wields an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md).

He shares his dialogue with the nine other potential quest givers; see [this section](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md#Detailed_Walkthrough) for details.
