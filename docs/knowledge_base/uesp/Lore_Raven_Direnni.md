Raven Direnni was an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) noblewoman. She is often mentioned alongside her cousins, [Aiden](Lore_Aiden_Direnni.md) and [Ryain Direnni](Lore_Ryain_Direnni.md), all of whom played a major role in the historical [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md), but beyond that, she is sometimes accredited as the creator of [enchanting](Lore_Enchanting.md), a [magical](Lore_Magic.md) discipline in which you use a [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md) to embed magical powers into an inanimate object.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/ON-prerelease-Ancestral_High_Elf_Style.png/200px-ON-prerelease-Ancestral_High_Elf_Style.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Ancestral_High_Elf_Style.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Ancestral_High_Elf_Style.png) Ancestral High Elf Style [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/ON-item-Raven_Direnni%27s_Inflammable_Rug.jpg/200px-ON-item-Raven_Direnni%27s_Inflammable_Rug.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Raven_Direnni%27s_Inflammable_Rug.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Raven_Direnni%27s_Inflammable_Rug.jpg) Raven Direnni's Inflammable Rug Raven Direnni was born into the noble clan of Altmer, the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) of [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md). According to some sources, Raven Direnni was credited with creating enchanting before the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup> but others such as *[Once](Lore_Once.md)*, and the existence of prominent enchanters from [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) prove otherwise.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Once-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AD-3)</sup>

According to *Once*, before Raven Direnni's discoveries, the art of enchantment was unique and that a successful attempt was a one-in-twenty chance. Raven Direnni eventually created the [Rules of Eldritch Binding](Lore_Mentioned_Texts.md#Rules_of_Eldritch_Binding), which made the process much more successful.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Once-2)</sup> One of the first magic items she supposedly created was an inflammable rug.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONContraband-4)</sup>

When the Direnni Hegemony went to war with the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) in the early First Era, Raven Direnni lived in [Castle Daggerfall](Lore_Castle_Daggerfall.md) as one of its earliest occupants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AHOD-5)</sup> To prepare for the war, Raven Direnni outlined the design of armor later classified as Ancestral High Elf style.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CM89-6)</sup> The war came to a climax at the [Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Glenumbra_Moors.md), where the Direnni defeated the Alessians led by [Faolchu](Lore_Faolchu.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONGrahla-7)</sup> Raven, with her cousins, [Aiden](Lore_Aiden_Direnni.md), and [Ryain](Lore_Ryain_Direnni.md) were able to defeat the Alessians and force them out of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DRD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Once-2)</sup>

Years after her time, Raven Direnni's inflammable rug remained in Castle Daggerfall for many years until it was stolen by the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) of [Abah's Landing](Lore_Abah%27s_Landing.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). It was then placed in a collection of similarly rare and valuable items.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-KHL-8)</sup>
