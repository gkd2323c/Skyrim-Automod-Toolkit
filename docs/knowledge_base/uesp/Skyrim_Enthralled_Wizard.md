These **Enthralled Wizards** are [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) found in [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) during [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md). Formerly students of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md), their spirits have been enthralled by the [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(creature).md).

They wear [hooded black mage robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes.md) and warlock [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). They each wield a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) (up to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) in quality) and may drop a [scroll](Skyrim_Scrolls.md) and [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) when killed. Oddly, they can cast [Vampiric Drain](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md) against you despite not being vampires.

Both enthralled wizards in Labyrinthian join together to cast an energy barrier to shield Morokei during the related quest. In order to defeat Morokei and complete the quest, you must attack them to interrupt their casting. It is later revealed that they were the two companions ([Atmah](Skyrim_Atmah.md) and [Hafnar Ice-Fist](Skyrim_Hafnar_Ice-Fist.md)) [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md) entered the chamber with, and that he sacrificed them to keep Morokei contained.

## Related Quests
- **[The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md)**: Retrieve the [Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_Staff_of_Magnus.md).

## Notes
- Their extraordinarily high magicka allows them to cast Vampiric Drain for over 55 minutes.
- A separate NPC named "Enthralled Wizard" can be found in game data, but is not used anywhere in the game. Information on this NPC can be found on the [Unused NPCs](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Enthralled_Wizard) page.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. It no longer bleeds and has the same Bandit Wizard class as the other ghost.
