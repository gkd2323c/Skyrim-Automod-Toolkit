*One of the Riekrs found a book among the crates. He asked me what it was. It appeared to be a book of recipes. I told him as much and he eagerly took a bite out of it. "Recipes no taste good," he said and spit the book out.*
—An excerpt from *[Riekrs of Wrothgar: Observations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Riekrs_of_Wrothgar__Observations)* Riekrs (or **Riekr Goblins**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM71:CS-1)</sup> are a race of [magically](Lore_Magic.md) gifted ice [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) from the province of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NJW-2)</sup> related to other [Goblin-ken](Lore_Goblin-ken.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM71:CS-1)</sup> They are sometimes described as [crossbreeds](Lore_Multiraciality.md) between standard Goblins and [Rieklings](Lore_Riekling.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Live-3)</sup> or as just a larger variety of Riekling.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CM71:CS-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Live-3)</sup> The Riekr are social creatures; each goblin is expected to be able to pull their own weight within their tribe, and those who can't are not treated kindly.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RWO-4)</sup>

Their skin color varies between shades of white and blue. Many of them have long white hair, as well as prominent beards or mustaches.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/ON-npc-King-Chief_Edu_02.jpg/200px-ON-npc-King-Chief_Edu_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King-Chief_Edu_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-King-Chief_Edu_02.jpg) A Riekr King Chief on his throne (ESO) The Riekr have a unique initiation system for those who show magical capabilities where they are exiled and have to buy their way back. The mage must then wait a certain period before they are allowed to buy their way back into the tribe with a food offering such as a freshly slain [deer](Lore_Deer.md), [bear](Lore_Bear.md), or [echatere](Lore_Echatere.md). Once the mage buys their way back into the tribe, they quickly rise in status to become a revered member of the tribe, gaining influence with the chief.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RWO-4)</sup>

Riekr chiefs own objects that indicate their position of power in the tribe. The Riekr known as King-Chief Edu stole and used an ancient scepter created by the Orgak Orc clan, as a symbol of his power.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Another Riekr chief who was notably a spellcaster had a totem as his symbol of power.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup>

Riekr are known to work closely with the [Snow Ogres](Lore_Ogre.md) that also inhabit Wrothgar. They are said to provide their Ogre allies with weapons and armor they wouldn't normally have access to.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Live-3)</sup> Riekr have a mostly antagonistic relationship with the more "civilized" peoples that reside in Wrothgar, sometimes organizing raiding parties to kidnap hunters.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup> However they have been known to welcome outsiders in their ranks as long as they smell like a Goblin.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RWO-4)</sup> Riekr have also been known to display attraction towards Orcish women.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

Like other Goblins, Riekr tame wild beasts such as feathery [durzogs](Lore_Durzog.md). Also like other Goblins, Riekr are capable of speaking [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup> though often require training to do so.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-YLFE-10)</sup> Some Riekr are tamed and raised to be servants, doing tasks such as setting tables.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-YLFE-10)</sup>

## Named Riekr Tribes

### Rock-Breakers
The Rock-Breakers inhabited Bonerock Cavern and were led by Chief Iziku. The Rock-Breakers were a tribe who, as their name implies, were known for breaking rocks.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RWO-4)</sup> In the Second Era, a group of hunters were captured by them. Their chieftain was killed by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and their totem stolen.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESO-7)</sup>

### King-Chief Edu's Tribe
A tribe led by King-Chief Edu was found near [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md#Second_Orsinium), and included snow Ogres and many tames durzogs among their ranks. When Edu escaped from his servitude under human masters,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-YLFE-10)</sup> he led the tribe in many raids against caravans, including kidnapping civilians. Adventurers were contracted to kill Edu and his underlings and free the kidnapped traders.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Rof P-11)</sup>

## Notes
- A book relating to a cut quest described tribes having been observed to eat [boar](Lore_Boar.md) meat, vomit it back into bowls, then trade the bowls with fellow tribesmen and eat each other's vomit.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MFJ-12)</sup> Cut dialogue also mentioned that they are observed to bathe in filth and sometimes eat their young, with some suggesting that these behaviors maintain their social structure.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-Nellic Sterone-13)</sup>
- As the race of the [Ice Tribes](Lore_Ice_Tribes.md) is not explicitly identified, the Riekr's similar appearance and habitation of the close-by areas suggests that they may related in some way.
