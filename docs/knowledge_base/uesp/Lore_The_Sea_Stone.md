The Sea Stone *A plaque honoring a druidic contribution to the All Flags Navy*
---
According to legends, this ancient vessel was provided and crewed by [Stonelore Circle](Lore_Stonelore_Circle.md) druids and sailed into battle with the [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) when the fleet assaulted [Thras](Lore_Thras.md). After it returned to [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), Archdruid Orlinan donated it to put on display as part of the memorial.
