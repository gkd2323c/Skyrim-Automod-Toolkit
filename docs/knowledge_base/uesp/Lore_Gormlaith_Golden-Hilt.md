[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png/200px-LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Call_of_Valor.png) Felldir, Hakon and Gormlaith's spirits.(Legends) Gormlaith Golden-Hilt was an [Atmoran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atmoran) hero and [Tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tongue) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). She was an accomplished warrior; at least four [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) fell to her blade during the legendary [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GGH-1)</sup> Gormlaith, [Felldir the Old](Lore_Felldir_the_Old.md), and [Hakon One-Eye](Lore_Hakon_One-Eye.md) were the leaders of the rebellion against [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-3)</sup> and the first mortals to learn how to wield the [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), apart from the [Dragon Priests](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGATT-4)</sup> They were taught by [Paarthurnax](Lore_Paarthurnax.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Party-5)</sup> During the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md), Hakon and his allies sought the help of [Miraak](Lore_Miraak.md), the First Dragonborn, in defeating Alduin, but Miraak refused to help.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Miraak-6)</sup> Undeterred, together, they fought against Alduin atop the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md). Finding that they had no means to achieve a final victory, the companions successfully sent the wyrm forward through time using an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), though Gormlaith was killed before they could banish him. Thousands of years later, the spirits of Gormlaith and her compatriots assisted the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Hero.md) in the final battle against their nemesis in the realm of [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Skyrim2-3)</sup> She also aided the Last Dragonborn in their other battles, when called to their aid with the Call of Valor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GGH-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-COV-7)</sup>
