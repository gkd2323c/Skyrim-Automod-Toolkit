Florian Lanctot, priest of [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md) **by [Florian Lanctot](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Florian_Lanctot)** *A Breton priest's thoughts on finding common religious grounds within the Daggerfall Covenant*
---
[![ON-concept-Temple interior.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/2c/ON-concept-Temple_interior.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Temple_interior.png)
- "We must work together to find common ground with our allies. This has been simple with the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md), who tend to worship the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), though they do have some strange ideas about them that seem to come from the intermixing with their ancestors' [gods](Lore_Gods.md). Only the staunchest traditionalists still worship the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) gods, and even then there are parallels with the Divines. The [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md), though, they insist upon worshiping [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md) alone. This causes the Redguards great consternation and more than a few raised eyebrows among the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) faithful. I worry that this fundamental difference may be difficult to reconcile, especially if the Orcs continue to be so vocal and aggressive about the matter."
