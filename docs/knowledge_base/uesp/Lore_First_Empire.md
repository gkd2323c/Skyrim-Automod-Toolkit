# First Empire

**First Empire** can refer to three things:

- [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), the first Imperial Empire formed by Queen Alessia
- [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md), the collective term for the Nordic kingdom during a phase of expansion in the First Era
- [First Empire (Mournhold)](Lore_First_Empire_(Mournhold).md), an Empire which the city-state of Mournhold was historically a part of
