# Market District

A **Market District** (also called a **Trade District**) is a section of a city or smaller settlement where merchants sell goods and services. The following are places with a Market District:

- [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md)
- [Davon's Watch](Lore_Davon%27s_Watch.md)
- [Imperial City](Lore_Market_District_(Imperial_City).md)
- [Orcrest](Lore_Orcrest.md)
- [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md) (Wells District)
- [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) (Market Square)
