[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-npc-Corpulus_Vinius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Corpulus_Vinius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Corpulus_Vinius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Corpulus_Vinius.jpg) Corpulus Vinius **Corpulus Vinius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [food vendor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor) and the owner of [The Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He will rent out a room to you for 10 gold a night and offers merchant services. He is the father of [Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) and [Minette Vinius](Skyrim_Minette_Vinius.md), who both want to take over the inn when Corpulus retires. Until then, they help out with the daily maintenance. Corpulus also has a cousin in [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md), the proprietor of the [Four Shields Tavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern), [Faida](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Faida). Should anything happen to Corpulus, Sorex will take over running the inn.

Corpulus can be found welcoming guests and offering his services in the taproom of the Winking Skeever 24 hours a day, without ever pausing to eat or sleep. He will only leave his spot behind the counter if you rent a room for the night, and politely show you to your room.

Corpulus wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0005b6a0). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), his [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Winking_Skeever) to the tavern, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [middle-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When greeting you:

*"Welcome to the Winking Skeever, friend."* *"If you need a good rumor or two, I think I can oblige..."* *"Traitors, civil wars, dragons. Bah. Those troubles don't cross my doorway. So sit, relax."* *"Welcome to the Winking Skeever. Take a load off and enjoy the atmosphere."* **Why is this place called the Winking Skeever?** *"Well, as it turns out, I had a pet [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) when I was a boy and he used to wink."* **You kept a skeever as a pet?** *"They were smaller back then."* **I guess that's as good a reason as any.** *"I thought so."* **Where can I learn more about magic?** *"Magic? You're on the wrong side of Skyrim. You want Winterhold... that College there. They teach it.
Otherwise you can try the [Jarl's wizard](Skyrim_Sybille_Stentor.md). I wouldn't, though."* When asked about rumors, he will point you towards a few local quests, starting with [Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!):

*"I've heard [Jaree-Ra](Skyrim_Jaree-Ra.md) is hanging around the Wells District, looking for new blood to hire."* Next, he will point you towards the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md):

*"I think the Bards College might be looking for recruits. You should speak to the head of the college, [Viarmo](Skyrim_Viarmo.md), if you're interested."* He also mentions a traveler from Dragon Bridge:

*"Varnius Junius stopped by here on his way to court. He was worried about something. I'm sure [Falk Firebeard](Skyrim_Falk_Firebeard.md) could tell you what."* Lastly, he heard rumors about a [crazy person](Skyrim_Dervenin.md) wandering the streets near the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md):

*"People say there's a man wandering the streets near the Blue Palace. He seems distressed but he's not making any sense."* When you exit conversation he will say one of the following:

*"Remember the Winking Skeever next time you're footsore."* *"Need anything, you just say the word."*
