[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-npc-Aeta.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aeta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aeta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aeta.jpg) Aeta **Aeta**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md), is the daughter of [Oslaf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf) and [Finna](Skyrim_Finna.md). She lives in her family's [house](Skyrim_Oslaf%27s_House.md) in [Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md). She is also one of the few villagers, along with Finna, [Nikulas](Skyrim_Nikulas_(Dragonborn).md), [Frea](Skyrim_Frea.md), and [Storn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Storn_Crag-Strider), not under Miraak's control after you exit the Temple of Miraak.

She wears a unique set of dark brown [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes_(Dragonborn).md) with an apparent dusting of snow on the shoulders, and a pair of unique [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes_(xx03c81a).md). She carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf%27s_House_Key) to her house and a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child). She is much more like her mother, as she is friendly towards strangers, unlike her father.

## Dialogue
When spoken to she can say:

- *"I want to be a great hunter like [Wulf](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wulf_Wild-Blood). I practice stalking prey on the rabbits and the foxes in the woods."*
- *"Mother worries when I walk too far from the village. She says a dragon might snatch me up, but I'm not afraid."*
- *"The elders talk of great cities much bigger than our village, but why would anyone need so many buildings?"*

### Conversations
Aeta may be seen talking to her father about her first hunt:

- **Aeta**: *"Papa, when can I go on my first hunt?"*
- **Oslaf**: *"I've told you before, little cub, you're not old enough yet."*
- **Aeta**: *"I just want to see. I promise not to get in the way of the hunters, and I'm not afraid of the beasts in the forest."*
- **Oslaf**: *"I have given my answer, little cub. I know you are brave and have the heart of a great she-wolf, but all things have a time and place."*
- **Aeta**: *"Yes, papa."*

## Quest-Related Events
If spoken to during the [Gardener of Men](Skyrim_The_Gardener_of_Men.md) while Storn is being sacrificed:

- *"What's happening to Storn?"*

Once Storn is dead:

- *"What does it mean? What was that? What happened to him?"*
- *"Leave me alone."*

She will approach the body to pay her respects.

- *"It can't be... please, All-Maker, send him back to us..."*

## Notes
- Aeta will be sent to [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) and can be [adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md) if both her parents are killed.<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> - This is not possible until the completion of [At The Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md), as her [father](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oslaf) is marked essential until then. - If you adopt Aeta, you won't be able to move her back to Solstheim. This includes [Severin Manor](Skyrim_Severin_Manor.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).
- Her dialogue for seeing a dead body (*"Oh no! By the gods! This can't be happening!"*) is the same as other children, even though the Skaal believe in a single god.
- Aeta is the only child that can be found on Solstheim.
