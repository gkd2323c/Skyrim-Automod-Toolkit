Kothringi Leviathan Horn *Notes on a Kothringi musical instrument*
---
The extinct [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) people of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) have left very few relics behind, but the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) cherishes this mournful instrument as a reminder of their culture and the ravages of the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md).

This cheerful bugle was fashioned from the horn of a [leviathan](Lore_Swamp_Leviathan.md), a creature of terrible power that roams Black Marsh, and dyed with silver-blue pigments to match the skin color of the lost Kothringi people.

The loud, ringing tones are as joyful and uplifting as the Kothringi people in their prime.
