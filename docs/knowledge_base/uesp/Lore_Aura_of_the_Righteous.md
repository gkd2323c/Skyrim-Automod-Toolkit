Aura of the Righteous **by [Ptolus the Bright](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Ptolus_the_Bright), [Resolute](Lore_Resolutes_of_Stendarr.md) of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md)** *Notes on the different forms of Stendarr's gift*
---
Insomuch as all fell things abhor the light, so has [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md) gifted all those who invoke His Name with the ability to clothe themselves in a righteous aura of blessed light. Over time, even as evil's many Abominations found new ways to afflict [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s mortals with destruction and death, the Priests and [Resolutes of Stendarr](Lore_Resolutes_of_Stendarr.md) have adapted Stendarr's glowing gift to many purposes, whether to smite, to defend, or to heal.

For offensive means, Stendarr's gift most often manifests as a piercing beam of light, resembling a spear of purifying sunlight and oft referred to in those terms.

As a form of armor, Stendarr's gift may take the form of a diffuse aura that surrounds the righteous, mitigating the attacks of the abominable, or it may focus into an almost-palpable shield to ward off a specific threat.

Worshipers who eschew all forms of violence, such as the [Harmonious Masters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Harmonious_Masters) of [Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md), have turned Stendarr's glowing gift into a tool for [healing](Lore_Restoration.md), and then freely spread their knowledge of cleansing and healing rituals, even to Stendarr's more militant adherents. For even in the hands of a warrior, how can the curing of ills be an ill thing?
