# Gold Fleet

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shattered_Shoals.jpg) The beached *Prowler*, later restored to seaworthiness The **Gold Fleet** was a major expeditionary naval force of the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md#First_Aldmeri_Dominion), active circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), that was intended to complete a circuit of the Dominion's coastal territories as both a diplomatic gesture and a show of strength.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ODB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFL-2)</sup> Expensive and numerous [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) ships such as the *Falconer* and the *Peregrine* made up the core of the fleet, while a squadron of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) privateer vessels (including the *Little Alkosh*,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLAL-3)</sup> the *Mara's Teat*, the *Prowler*<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TPL-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LK-5)</sup> and the *Rusty Claw*) joined after stopping over in [Haven](Lore_Haven.md) to embark a contingent of [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) marines.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLAL-3)</sup> On approach to [Khenarthi's Roost](Lore_Khenarthi%27s_Roost.md), however, the fleet was swept ashore by a hurricane, running aground in the [Shattered Shoals](Lore_Shattered_Shoals.md) with the loss of all ships save for the *Prowler*.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CA-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SF-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RZD-8)</sup> Although many—including the fleet commander—perished in the storm,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-CK-9)</sup> some Dominion sailors survived, and sought refuge in the nearby port of [Mistral](Lore_Mistral.md), which strained the capacity of the local guard to accommodate them all.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-HAa S-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TYf YP-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-UD-12)</sup> To make matters worse, [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) raiders soon arrived on the beaches to scavenge the wrecks.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RZD-8)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CA-6)</sup>
