[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-npc-Rondach.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rondach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rondach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rondach.jpg) Rondach **Rondach** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) and works in [Understone Keep's](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) kitchens. He seems to be terrified of his superior, [Anton Virane](Skyrim_Anton_Virane.md). Rondach's sister [Voada](Skyrim_Voada.md), is also Anton's assistant. Their father is [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md), the landlord of [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md).

He sleeps at one of the bedrolls in the kitchen from midnight to 6am, when he wakes up for a one-hour breakfast before starting his workday. He has one-hour meals at noon and 5pm. At 7pm his workday is over and he spends the rest of the day relaxing in the kitchen.

Rondach wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d9) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
He will express his fear of Anton.
