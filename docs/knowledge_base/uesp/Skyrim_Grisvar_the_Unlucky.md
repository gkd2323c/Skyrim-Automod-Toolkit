[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/16/SR-npc-Grisvar_the_Unlucky.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grisvar_the_Unlucky.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grisvar_the_Unlucky.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grisvar_the_Unlucky.jpg) Grisvar in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) **Grisvar the Unlucky** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) who has been locked up in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) for life after committing many crimes. He carries two [shivs](Skyrim_Shiv_(item).md). In exchange for a bottle of [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma), he will give you a shiv.

If you decide to accept [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md)'s help he will order you to kill Grisvar as a *"show of loyalty"*.

## Related Quests
- **[No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md)**: Escape from [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md)!
- **[Shiv](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shiv)**: Obtain a [simple weapon](Skyrim_Shiv_(item).md) from a fellow prisoner.

## Dialogue
His potential greetings are:

*"I got locked up one too many times. Now I'm in for life."* *"Jarl decided I was too much of a problem. Threw me in here with the Forsworn."* *"I swear, if I ever get out of here, I'm giving up thieving. Really."* When spoken to directly:

**How long have you been here?** *"First it was 6 months, then a year, then 2 years. Now I'm in for life."* **What are you in for?** *"The first time? Thieving. The second time? Thieving. The third time? Thieving. It kind of keeps going like that."*

### No One Escapes Cidhna Mine
After speaking to Borkul the Beast:

**I need a shiv.** *"Ah, you want protection? I can get you what you need. Maybe you can do something for me, first? Duach has a bottle of Skooma. Finest distilled Moon Sugar. I'm shaking just thinking about it."* **I'll get it for you.** *"Thank you, friend. Don't worry. I'll have that shiv ready for you."* **I don't think so.** *"All right, but if you change your mind. Not like we're going anywhere."* If you already have skooma:

**I already have some skooma.** *"Ah, you're a clever one aren't you? Now, here's your shiv. Promise you won't ever use it on me, okay?"* Once you return with skooma:

**I have your skooma.** *"Here's your shiv. Promise you won't ever use it on me, okay?"* When spoken to after Madanach asks you to kill Grisvar:

**Madanach says hello.** *"What? No. No don't kill me. Please!"*

The one who sliced open that pickpocket they threw in here?"* **Braig**: *"The Blackblood * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * girl? ## Notes
- In the scene between Braig and him, they are actually talking about Eisa Blackthorn, who, despite them misremembering her last name, can actually be found in-game. Her [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eisa%27s_Journal) mentions having served in Cidhna Mine for a year. - The same scene is internally also number "**02**", but there is no previous scene between the two men to be found.
