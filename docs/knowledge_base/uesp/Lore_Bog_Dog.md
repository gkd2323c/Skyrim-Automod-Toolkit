Bog Dogs are a species of stout pack hunters native primarily to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), mainly in the [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) and [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) regions. They disguise themselves in the marshy regions they live with vegetation that appears to grow in the fur on their backs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Enemy-1)</sup> Despite their name, they have no relation to actual [dogs](Lore_Dog.md), though they're capable of being tamed and make loyal pets.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Pet-2)</sup>

Bog Dogs can also be found in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md), primarily around [Shipwright's Regret](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Shipwright%27s_Regret), where they are known as **Moss Hounds**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Saltwater Bog Dogs native to the islands off of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s southern coasts, such as [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> are a distant relative to the mainland variety. They are said to prefer rolling over warm sands and seaweed instead of in murky swamps.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Bog Dog dung is one ingredient in a traditional [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) concoction, said to bring good luck.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The Black Marsh Bog Dogs was the name of a gladiatorial team that fought in the Dragon Games.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CGG-7)</sup>
