**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Flounder
The **Flounder** is a type of fish.

- The **Azurain Flounder** can be found in [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) and the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), off the coasts of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).
- The **Nirn Flounder** can be found in the lakes of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- The **Stormhaven Flounder** can be found in the Iliac Bay, off the coast of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).
- The **Systres Flounder** can be found in the foul waters of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) and [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md).

**Found in:** - [The Infernal City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels/Creatures#Notes), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Flowerhorn
**Flowerhorns** are type of [cichlid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Cichlid).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Fretfish
The **Fretfish** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md), as well as in the underground rivers of Arkthzand Caverns in [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).
