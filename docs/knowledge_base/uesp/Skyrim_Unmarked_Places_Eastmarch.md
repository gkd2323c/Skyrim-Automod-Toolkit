### Cronvangr Summoning Altar
[![SR-place-Cronvangr Summoning Altar.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-place-Cronvangr_Summoning_Altar.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cronvangr_Summoning_Altar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cronvangr_Summoning_Altar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cronvangr_Summoning_Altar.jpg) - POIVolcanic Tundra05

East of [Cronvangr Cave](Skyrim_Cronvangr_Cave.md) and southwest of [Witchmist Grove](Skyrim_Witchmist_Grove.md) are watery ruins guarded by two necromancers. The altar has a soul gem, potions and burial urns. A skeletal dead novice conjurer and seven [Draugr corpses](Skyrim_Containers.md#Draugr), one impaled, are all lootable. At the eastern edge of the pool is a dungeon chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=127035&y=-5466&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Frost Troll Den: Uttering Hills
[![SR-place-Frost Troll Den Uttering Hills.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-place-Frost_Troll_Den_Uttering_Hills.jpg/200px-SR-place-Frost_Troll_Den_Uttering_Hills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frost_Troll_Den_Uttering_Hills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Frost_Troll_Den_Uttering_Hills.jpg) - POIVolcanic Tundra04

### Hunter's Camp: Sulphur Soaking Pools
[![SR-place-Hot Spring Hunter's Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-place-Hot_Spring_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hot_Spring_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hot_Spring_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hot_Spring_Hunter%27s_Camp.jpg) - POIVolcanic Tundra06

West of [Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md) you can find a hunter's camp, where three naked hunters live and appear to be relaxing in the hot waters of one of many springs characteristic of the region. Among their possessions is a [banded iron shield](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Shield.md) and a copy of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) skill book *[Cherim's Heart](Skyrim_Cherim%27s_Heart.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=112691&y=-24574&zoom=17&legacy=true)

#### Bugs
- The banded iron shield isn't marked as owned by the hunters, but they will still be hostile if you take it. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.2, fixes this bug.

### Hunter's Camp: Windhelm Plateau
[![SR-place-Hunter's Camp Windhelm Plateau.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-place-Hunter%27s_Camp_Windhelm_Plateau.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hunter%27s_Camp_Windhelm_Plateau.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Camp_Windhelm_Plateau.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Hunter%27s_Camp_Windhelm_Plateau.jpg) - POISnowy18

Directly south of [Hlaalu Farm](Skyrim_Hlaalu_Farm.md), east of [Brandy-Mug Farm](Skyrim_Brandy-Mug_Farm.md), up in the cliffs is a hunter camp containing a campfire with cooking pot, a wood chopping block with a locked chest nestled in the wood behind it, three tents and three hunters. There is also a table with venison, goat meat, a snow bear pelt and a deer pelt. Next to the table is a deer carcass with an iron war axe. In the tent closest to the hanging rack is a coin purse. The first time the camp is visited, a Frost Troll will be attacking the Hunters, who almost always lose the fight. The frost troll can respawn along with the hunters on subsequent visits. This camp is replaced by [Windhelm Military Camp](Skyrim_Windhelm_Military_Camp.md) during the Civil War. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Windhelm%2BMilitary%2BCamp)

### Lucky Lorenz's Shack
[![SR-place-Lucky Lorenz's Shack.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-place-Lucky_Lorenz%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lucky_Lorenz%27s_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lucky_Lorenz%27s_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lucky_Lorenz%27s_Shack.jpg) - POIVolcanic Tundra08

West of the [Abandoned Prison](Skyrim_Abandoned_Prison.md) is [Lucky Lorenz](Skyrim_Lucky_Lorenz.md)'s house. The house appears to have been destroyed by a fallen tree and its contents have been scattered outside the house—some food, ingredients ([troll fat](Skyrim_Troll_Fat.md) and [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md)), and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill book *[Rislav The Righteous](Skyrim_Rislav_The_Righteous.md)*. Lorenz's corpse holds *[Treasure Map IX](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_IX.md)* and a copy of *[The Lusty Argonian Maid, v1](Skyrim_The_Lusty_Argonian_Maid,_v1.md)* can be found near him. A [shrine of Dibella](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Dibella.md) sits inside the house. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lucky+Lorenz)

### Mistwatch Folly
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-place-Mistwatch_Folly.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mistwatch_Folly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistwatch_Folly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mistwatch_Folly.jpg) Mistwatch Folly - POIVolcanic Tundra09

Located north of [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md), the ruins are guarded by three skeletons whom will attack if you approach the ruins. The embalming table in the center of the ruin holds two ingots of [refined moonstone](Skyrim_Refined_Moonstone.md).

### The Mournful Giant
[![SR-place-Mournful Giant.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/SR-place-Mournful_Giant.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mournful_Giant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mournful_Giant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mournful_Giant.jpg) - POIVolcanic Tundra13

A giant is found mourning over the loss of his mammoth, that lays dead in a small hot-spring pool. Unlike other giants in the game, when approached, the giant will not act hostile unless attacked.
