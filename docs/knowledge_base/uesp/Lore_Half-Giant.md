# Half-Giant

Half-Giants are the descendants of [Men](Lore_Man.md) and [Giants](Lore_Giant.md) or [Sea Giants](Lore_Sea_Giant.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ao HGs-1)</sup> They take on the qualities of their [mixed](Lore_Multiraciality.md) heritage, which can be passed down to their descendants.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle Lyris Sai-2)</sup> Half-Giants tend to be much taller then the other races, but not nearly as tall as their Giant forebears, with some exceptions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ao HGs-1)</sup>

[Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md), also known as Sinmur the Terrible and the Giant of Legend, was a [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) Half-Giant chieftain from [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONSvari-3)</sup> Leading hundreds of his kin, he is perhaps best remembered for his epic conflict against [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) and the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Sot R27-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONUN-5)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Half-Giant and Sea Giant raiders led by [Vampire Lord](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md) [Falgravn](Lore_Falgravn.md) stormed the shores of [Kyne's Aegis](Lore_Kyne%27s_Aegis.md), but were defeated by a group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TFFKA-6)</sup>

## Notes
- Individuals with Giant ancestry such as [Lyris Titanborn](Lore_Lyris_Titanborn.md) are also colloquially known as Half-Giants.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mt CSH-7)</sup>
- Braegha-Fin in ESO's [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md) was [originally planned](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Cut_Content/Rivenspire#The_Hillmen_of_Rivenspire) to have the spirits of a race of giants called "hill giants". Rivenspire would have had Bretons with hill giant heritage called hillmen that were larger in size, similar to Half-Giants. These hillmen would have also had some Nordic heritage.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
