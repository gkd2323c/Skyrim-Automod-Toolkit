[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) Lake Honrich circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b1/ON-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) Lake Honrich circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lake Honrich is a lake in the eastern [Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md). [Riften](Lore_Riften.md) is located on the shores of Lake Honrich,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OCD-1)</sup> and a set of islands called the [Isle of Gold](Lore_Isle_of_Gold.md) can be found at its center.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-JTSK-2)</sup> The lake is drained by the [Treva River](Lore_Treva_River.md).

In the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), the lake, then called Lake Honnith, was sparsely populated by [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) compared to neighboring regions, and were subject to battles with [Harald](Lore_Harald.md)'s forces circa [1E 139](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_139). Later that same year, it was discovered that the nearby [Forelhost](Lore_Forelhost.md) was possibly the last hold out of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md) before it was exterminated.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSSJ-3)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the estate of Skald's Retreat served as the home for the most renowned bards in the [Old Holds](Lore_Old_Holds.md), and was later subject to being merged with the [Quagmire](Lore_Quagmire.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The honey farm of [Goldenglow Estate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Goldenglow_Estate) was later located on the Isle of Gold in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
