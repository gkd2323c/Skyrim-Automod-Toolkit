# Main Quest


1. **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack. ([![SR-achievement-Unbound.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-achievement-Unbound.png/18px-SR-achievement-Unbound.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Unbound.png) 10 pts/Bronze)
2. **[Before the Storm](Skyrim_Before_the_Storm.md)**: Inform the [Jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) about the dragon attack on [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md).
3. **[Bleak Falls Barrow](Skyrim_Bleak_Falls_Barrow_(quest).md)**: Retrieve the [Dragonstone](Skyrim_Dragonstone.md). ([![SR-achievement-Bleak Falls Barrow.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png/18px-SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Bleak_Falls_Barrow.png) 10 pts/Bronze)
4. **[Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md)**: Investigate a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) sighting near [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).
5. **[The Way of the Voice](Skyrim_The_Way_of_the_Voice.md)**: Meet the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md). ([![SR-achievement-The Way of the Voice.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Way_of_the_Voice.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
6. **[The Horn of Jurgen Windcaller](Skyrim_The_Horn_of_Jurgen_Windcaller.md)**: Retrieve a [Greybeard](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) artifact from [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md).
7. **[A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md)**: Prove you are Dragonborn to [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md) by slaying the dragon [Sahloknir](Skyrim_Sahloknir.md).
8. **[Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md)**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). ([![SR-achievement-Diplomatic Immunity.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png/18px-SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Diplomatic_Immunity.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
9. **[A Cornered Rat](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md)**: Locate [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md).
10. **[Alduin's Wall](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Wall.md)**: Locate the prophecy within [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md). ([![SR-achievement-Alduin's Wall.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png/18px-SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Alduin%27s_Wall.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
11. **[The Throat of the World](Skyrim_The_Throat_of_the_World.md)**: Receive a lesson from [Paarthurnax](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(dragon).md).
12. **[Elder Knowledge](Skyrim_Elder_Knowledge.md)**: Recover an [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md) to learn an [Ancient Secret](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md). ([![SR-achievement-Elder Knowledge.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png/18px-SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Elder_Knowledge.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
13. **[Alduin's Bane](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Bane.md)**: Exploit the Time-Wound to learn [Dragonrend](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md) and defeat [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) at the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md).
14. **[The Fallen](Skyrim_The_Fallen.md)**: Capture and interrogate [an ally](Skyrim_Odahviing.md) of [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md). ([![SR-achievement-The Fallen.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png/18px-SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-The_Fallen.png) 20 pts/Bronze) - (optional) **[Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md)**: Enforce a ceasefire to secure [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md).
- (optional) **[Paarthurnax](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(quest).md)**: Deal with a [potential threat](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(dragon).md).
15. **[The World-Eater's Eyrie](Skyrim_The_World-Eater%27s_Eyrie.md)**: Battle through [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md) to reach [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md)'s portal to [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).
16. **[Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(quest).md)**: Seek out [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) within [Sovngarde](Skyrim_Sovngarde_(place).md).
17. **[Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md)**: Put an end to the [World-Eater](Skyrim_Alduin.md). ([![SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png/18px-SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Dragonslayer.png) 50 pts/Gold)
