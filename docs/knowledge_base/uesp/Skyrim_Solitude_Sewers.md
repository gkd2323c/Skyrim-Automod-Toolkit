[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-place-Solitude_Sewers.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Sewers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Sewers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Sewers.jpg) Solitude Sewers entrance **Solitude Sewers** is a sewer system located in western Solitude, overrun with [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md)'s influence. The sewers are being encroached upon by a [root tunnel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Root) from the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). The sewers themselves are inaccessible until starting [Restoring Order](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md). It is inhabited by [Elytra](Skyrim_Elytra_Nymph.md) and [Corrupted Spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) resembling [Gnarls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gnarl).

Be sure to loot the many [hollowed stumps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hollowed_Stump) throughout the dungeon, as they usually contain [amber](Skyrim_Amber.md), a rare material needed to craft amber arms and armor. Right before reaching [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md)'s lair, you will discover a bunch of [Golden](Skyrim_Golden.md) and [Dark](Skyrim_Dark.md) equipment scattered around. A [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Strange_Arms_and_Armor) on the barrel next to the battlefield will reveal that this was the site of a battle between rival [Golden Saints](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md) and [Dark Seducers](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md), and that the [Saint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saints_Bandit) and [Seducer bandits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seducers_Bandit) took their equipment from this site. Further along is Thoron's lair, where you will find that he is conducting a ritual with the [Sword of Jyggalag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sword_of_Jyggalag). He is a tough opponent, but after slaying him, his [journals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thoron%27s_Journal) reveal that madness had already taken root underneath Solitude, as these root tunnels were already here when Thoron took residence. He only deepened the breach between [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and the Shivering Isles by using the Sword in his attempt to return to the Isles.

After killing Thoron, be sure to loot the nearby table of the rare [madness ore](Skyrim_Madness_Ore.md) which is needed for another [quest](Skyrim_Nerveshatter_(quest).md) and the unique [Sheogorath-Shaped Amber](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber). Additionally, be sure to read the *[Note on Amber and Madness Ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_on_Amber_and_Madness_Ore)* to obtain a [miscellaneous quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amber_and_Madness_Smithing) to unlock the secrets of forging madness and amber gear.

## Notes
- The dungeon ambient track "Wind from the Depths" from the *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* [soundtrack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Music) plays while in this dungeon.
- The root tunnel dungeon tile set this dungeon uses was added to the base game and is fully available as a modder's resource.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Saintsand Seducers-1)</sup>

## Bugs
- [![Nintendo Switch Logo.svg](https://images.uesp.net/5/5d/Nintendo_Switch_Logo.svg)](Skyrim_Switch.md) The "Wind from the Depths" track does not play in the Solitude Sewers. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The plants here are unaffected by the [Green Thumb](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Green_Thumb) perk. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
- The Note on Strange Arms and Armor and the Note on Amber and Madness Ore both respawn when the dungeon itself does. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.3, fixes this bug.
