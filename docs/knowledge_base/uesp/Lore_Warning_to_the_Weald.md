Warning to the Weald **by The [Recollection](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Recollection)** *An ultimatum from the Recollection*
---
[![ON-book-Nantharion Signature.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/f3/ON-book-Nantharion_Signature.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-book-Nantharion_Signature.png) To all who occupy [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md),

Long ago your ancestors stole these lands from the Saliache, the people you refer to as [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) or Heartland Elves. You squat amid the [ruins of our cities](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md). You decorate your palaces with our lost treasures. You cut and plow fields that were once lush forests.

We are now taking it all back.

Gather your things and leave while you can. We do not blame you for the actions of your ancestors, and we wish you no harm. But if you defy us, if you try to keep from us what your forebears stole, you perpetuate their ancient crimes. In that case, our justice will be swift and merciless.

The [Recollection](Lore_Recollection.md)
