**Saint** Jahn was an [Imperial saint](Lore_Imperial_Saints.md) known in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). He is the namesake of [St. Jahn's Wort](Lore_St._Jahn%27s_Wort.md), a [plant](Lore_Flora.md) whose [nectar](Lore_St._Jahn%27s_Wort_Nectar.md) was known for its [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) properties, commonly found in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md), [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), and [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) regions of Cyrodiil. Little is known about Jahn, except that the plant's name was already established by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Jahn-1)</sup>

It is believed that saints are granted [second sight](Lore_Sight.md) by the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), allowing them to perceive the world in a way that differs from other mortals.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE-2)</sup> Many of the Imperial saints were typically interred along the [Green Emperor Way](Lore_Green_Emperor_Way.md) among other prominent figures of [the Empire](Lore_The_Empire.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GTTIC-3)</sup> However, it is unknown whether Jahn possessed the sight or was ever buried there.
