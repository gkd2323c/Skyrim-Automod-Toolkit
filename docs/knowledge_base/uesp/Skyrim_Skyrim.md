[Dragon Shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) play an integral role in the story as you discover your fate as the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md). *Skyrim* is available on PC, [Xbox 360, Xbox One](Skyrim_Xbox.md), [Play Station 3, Play Station 4](Skyrim_PlayStation.md), and [Nintendo Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md).

***[Skyrim Legendary Edition](Skyrim_Legendary_Edition.md)*** is a bundled release of the game with the three major [add-ons](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) for PC, Xbox 360, and Play Station 3. ***[Skyrim Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md)*** is a remastered version of the game for PC, Xbox One, and Play Station 4. It includes the three major add-ons, uses an upgraded engine with many visual improvements, and has several important technical differences from the classic version of the game. A virtual reality version, ***[Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md)***, is available on Play Station VR and as a standalone game on PC. The Switch and VR releases are based on the Special Edition. The ***[Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md)*** is a Special Edition bundle that includes all DLC and Creation Club content.

The ancient home of the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md), Skyrim, teeters at the brink of destruction. With the death of High King [Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md), a [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) breaks out, with brothers slaying brothers across the province's frozen tundras and rocky crags. Many, especially the rebel [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) led by [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), wish to secede from [the Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), which has been in decline since the Oblivion Crisis. Many loyalists, on the other hand, prefer the unity and security provided by the Empire.

The [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) foretell the return of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s primeval darkness, the [Dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md), and the coming of a mortal born with the soul of a dragon, the [Dovahkiin](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md) or "Dragonborn"—one who will have the power to permanently defeat his greatest foe and end the dark reign of the consumer of worlds: [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md).

Your journey will be beset with peril. Just as remnants of the kingdom's shadow guardians, the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), will offer their aid, crafty agents of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md), seeking to advance their own cause, will impede your quest. Acquire knowledge and prove yourself Dragonborn, and the revered [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) will grant you an audience to learn of their ancient Draconic arts.

Now great adventurer, free yourself from bondage; take up blade or spell, bow or axe; fulfill your destiny or postpone it indefinitely.
