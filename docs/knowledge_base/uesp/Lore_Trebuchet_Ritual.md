Behold the ritual of making for the Grand and [Thaumaturgical](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md) and Most Puissant [Trebuchet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Trebuchet) of Overarching Peril.

He that touches this parchment to the workings of the Trebuchet of [Granvellusa](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Granvellusa) and releases its spirits therein shall apprehend before him an full and ready Engine of Mighty Destruction for the subjugation of the Foes of all [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md).

[Forromeo](Lore_Forromeo.md) has spoken. The world shall hear. Let all peoples tremble before the everlasting might and grandeur of Clan Direnni.
