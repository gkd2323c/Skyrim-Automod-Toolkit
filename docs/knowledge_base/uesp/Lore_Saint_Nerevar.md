Saint Nerevar *A Tribunal Temple pamphlet about the life of Indoril Nerevar*
---
[From a short [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) pamphlet for Western readers.]

Ages ago, [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) was the greatest [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) general, First Councilor, and companion of [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md), [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md), and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), who, with the power of the great Ring of the Ancestors, [One-Clan-Under-Moon-and-Star](Lore_Moon-and-Star.md), united the [Dunmer Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) to confront the evil [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), the treacherous [House Dagoth](Lore_House_Dagoth.md), and their Western allies at [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md).

By Providence, the faithless Dwemer were utterly destroyed, and their allies defeated, but Nerevar, mortally wounded in combat with the traitor [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur_(god).md), was driven from Red Mountain. Nerevar died not long after of his wounds, but he lived to see the birth of the Temple, and to bless the unity of the Dunmer into the safekeeping of [Almsivi](Lore_Almsivi.md), the Temple, and all its [communion of saints](Lore_Temple_Saints.md).
