[†](#note_Enhanced) The Enhanced versions have an additional property: *Attacks with this crossbow ignore 50% of armor.* This effect is not considered an enchantment, so enhanced crossbows can be enchanted like any normal unenchanted weapon, though they will only display the text for this effect and not for any enchantment that you may place on it. [††](#note_Speed) Despite saying they have the same speed as the long bow, all crossbows fire significantly slower than most of the normal bows. Unlike with normal bows, however, it is possible to circumvent this by means of an exploit: unequipping the bow then re-equipping it by means of hotkeys will cause the crossbow to be reloaded.

## Notes
- Unlike with the original *Dawnguard* crossbows, the ones added with Creation Club can be crafted at any [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md).
- The Stalhrim crossbows require you to finish "[A New Source of Stalhrim](Skyrim_A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.md)" before you can craft them.
- If you purchase a Dwarven Crossbow or its Enhanced variety, [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) may ask for it.

## Bugs
- Dwarven crossbows equipped to (some? all?) followers can get into a glitch where the follower continually raises and lowers the crossbow but never fires. Equipping a steel crossbow or a Creation Club crossbow fixes the problem. <sup>**?**</sup>
