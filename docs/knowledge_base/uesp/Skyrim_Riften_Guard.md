[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-npc-Riften_Guard_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Riften_Guard_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Riften_Guard_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Riften_Guard_2.jpg) Male Riften Guard **Riften Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md). Should the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) forces gain control of Riften, they will be replaced by [Imperial guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Guard) under the service of [Jarl Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md).

They will protect you from enemy creatures. It can be assumed that some of the guards aren't as law-abiding as they may seem. On your first visit to Riften, one will ask you to pay a "toll" (as he is later revealed to be the "man" of a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md)) though this payment can be avoided by persuasion or intimidation. Riften guards (aside from the extortion encounter) however, act and carry the same loot as standard guards, including having standard [guard dialogue](Skyrim_Guard_Dialogue.md).

Riften guards are clad in a set of Riften guard's equipment. This includes a [Riften guard's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Armor), a [Riften guard's helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Helmet), and a [Riften guard's shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Guard%27s_Shield). They also wear a pair of [fur boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots).
