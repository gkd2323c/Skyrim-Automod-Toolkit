[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Esbern.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Esbern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Esbern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Esbern.jpg) Esbern **Esbern**, an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Conjurer), is a chronicler and agent of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) who has survived the investigations of the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) by hiding in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)'s [Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md). Long obsessed with the foretold return of the dragon [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md), the World-Eater, Esbern serves as your mentor, guide, and advisor. Despite this, once he learns of [Paarthurnax's](Skyrim_Paarthurnax_(dragon).md) existence, he will demand that you slay him and will refuse you any aid until you have done so.

Esbern wears [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#0006ff43) initially, but changes into [mourner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mourner%27s_Clothes) once you reach Sky Haven Temple with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12), and carries an [ebony dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Dagger) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor). In combat, he relies on mainly Destruction spells, and occasionally summons leveled atronachs.

## Notes
- If you have not started any quests involving him, he will simply open the porthole and tell you to go away, promptly closing it afterwards.
- After arriving at Sky Haven Temple, Esbern can grant the [Dragonslayer's Blessing](Skyrim_Dragonslayer%27s_Blessing.md) which gives a 10% increased Critical Hit Chance vs. dragons for five days.
- Esbern is [voiced](Skyrim_Voice_Actors.md) by the late [Max von Sydow](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001884/).
- He can be a very useful follower. After you free him from his cell during [A Cornered Rat](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md), he will be an invincible follower, and will remain so as long as you avoid [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md) (and therefore [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md)).
- Alternatively, you can take him to Delphine and have them both follow you for two invincible followers. You may also have a regular follower with you.
- He shares his name with another person in [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Esbern).

## Bugs
- When you first meet Esbern during [A Cornered Rat](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md) as part of the main quest, sometimes his voice will be missing and/or he doesn't answer the door. Visit [this page](Skyrim_A_Cornered_Rat.md#Bugs) for details and possible solutions.
- After arriving in Sky Haven Temple, Esbern may not engage in the correct dialogue to activate or complete any of his quests.
- If you tell Esbern to wait somewhere, he may disappear when you return for him. - He can sometimes be found in Riverwood (waiting to engage in conversation with [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md)), in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), or traveling the road.
- After being escorted to Sky Haven Temple, Esbern might appear wherever you fast travel. - To fix this, you must unpatch your Skyrim game. It only appears on patch 1.5 and after the Alduin's Wall quest.
- After completion of the quest [Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md), Esbern may randomly be found wandering dungeons.
