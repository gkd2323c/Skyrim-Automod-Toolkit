# Keerilth

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/ON-npc-Shademist_Striker.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Shademist_Striker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shademist_Striker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shademist_Striker.jpg) A Keerilth vampire The Keerilth Clan is one of the four [vampiric](Lore_Vampire.md) clans that inhabit the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). The Keerilth are known for using and transforming into mist, even when underwater. However, they cannot travel far in their mist form. At one point, they were confronted and hunted by [Movarth Piquine](Lore_Movarth_Piquine.md), a vampire hunter that tracked various clans across the provinces. Through his confrontation, he learned many things about the Keerilth, among this was their weaknesses.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IB-1)</sup> The Keerilth are not known for hunting the living as prey, rather indirectly through the [Hoarvor](Lore_Hoarvor.md), who feast on them constantly. In fact, the Keerilth keep to themselves, away from the populace.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOGadinas-2)</sup> They are known to live in the region of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md), specifically in the Shademist Moors.

Keerilth vampires appear to be cannibals as well, as indicated by human remains found in stews within their camp.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESODeadly-3)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Keerilth Clan was approached by a member of the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md), who promised them immeasurable power and the end of their constant hiding. He taught the clan an accursed ritual that utilized the Skull of Vargarion, an ancient vampire to control an unlimited supply of thralls. Many members followed through with this agreement and formed an alliance with the Veiled Heritance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOGadinas-2)</sup> Thus the land surrounding [Longhaven](Lore_Longhaven.md) became tainted by this dark magic, and several people were enthralled to enter the moors, promptly feasted on. The strange activity caught the attention of Mel Adrys, who delved into Shademist Moors and tracked the vampire coven in the old sea coves. Together with another [wandering traveler](Lore_Soulless_One.md), the two were able to disrupt the ritual, kill the heritance supporters, and save Longhaven.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOMist-4)</sup> The Keerilth Clan was known to do mercenary work in a place known as the Glittering Grotto, where they often clashed with the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) for their valued gemstones.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOHeist-5)</sup>
