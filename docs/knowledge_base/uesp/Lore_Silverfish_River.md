[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/OB-place-Silverfish_River.jpg/200px-OB-place-Silverfish_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Silverfish_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Silverfish_River.jpg) The Estuary of the Silverfish River The Silverfish River is one of two rivers that drains from the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md), on its western front in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), to the [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md). The [Yellow Road](Lore_Yellow_Road.md) runs over the river, near the estuary.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup> The former city-state of [Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md) is found on the river mouth, not far from [Fort Grief Island](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Grief_Island) and across the old chapel, Cadlew. Further into the river are secluded settlements and waystations, ideally the Imperial Bridge Inn and the shrine to [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBSilverfish-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FG-3)</sup>

Much like the rest of the Imperial Province, there are several Imperial and Ayleid ruins found deeper into the wilderness, such as Fort Flecia and Ondo. An off-path road from Fort Flecia runs over the northernmost tip of the river and ends at the temple-ruins of [Malada](Lore_Malada.md). The Lover Stone, one of the [standing stones](Lore_Standing_Stones.md) in Cyrodiil is found on the river, between the Imperial Bridge Inn and the Shrine of [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBSilverfish-2)</sup> The Silverfish extends far east from the Niben Bay, toward the province's fringe<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup> and the murky region, [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md). By that point, the river tributaries were nameless and the local flora becomes alien compared to the gardens in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). The closest settlement at that point is the shanty-town, Slough Point, which is swarmed by [fleshflies](Lore_Fleshflies.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAA1-5)</sup>
