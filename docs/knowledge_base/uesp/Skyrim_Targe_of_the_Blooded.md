[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-item-Targe_of_the_Blooded.jpg/200px-SR-item-Targe_of_the_Blooded.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Targe_of_the_Blooded.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Targe_of_the_Blooded.jpg) Targe of the Blooded Targe of the Blooded is a unique shield wielded by [Umana](Skyrim_Umana.md), a female [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) who is part of a group of adventurers in the Dwemer ruin of [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md). Although the shield has no magical enchantments, it does have large steel spikes that will cause bleeding damage when using the shield to bash opponents. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [steel ingots](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md).

## Notes
- It cannot be disenchanted.
- It cannot be enchanted, even with the [Extra Effect](Skyrim_Extra_Effect.md) perk.
- It can be improved without the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.
- The bleeding damage effect stacks upon itself. If you perform several quick shield bashes in sequence, your opponent will suffer from multiple bleeding damage effects simultaneously.
- Despite being immune to bleeding, [Draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) are affected by the bleeding caused by being bashed with this shield.
- [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> and [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md) contain a special holder for this item.
