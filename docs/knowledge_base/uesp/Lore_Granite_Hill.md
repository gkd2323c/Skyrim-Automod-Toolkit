[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/AR-place-Granitehall.jpg/200px-AR-place-Granitehall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Granitehall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Granitehall.jpg) Granitehall Granite Hill (**Quethsegol Ahrol** in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ho JG-1)</sup> literally *[Bone-of-earth](Lore_Earthbones.md) Hill*)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup> is a region located east of [Sungard](Lore_Sungard.md) and far north of the town of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md). The town of **Granitehall** takes its name from the region.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup>

It is neighbored by several settlements, including Karthwasten Hall in the northwest, and [North Keep](Lore_North_Keep.md) in the southeast.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Map Arena-4)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/SR-creature-Vuljotnaak.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Vuljotnaak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vuljotnaak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vuljotnaak.jpg) Vuljotnaak at the Great Henge Granite Hill was historically a notable settlement, having been in existence since the time of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ao D-5)</sup> During the reign of Jarl Gjalund of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md), it was part of [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md) and consisted of three farms and an inn, and held a weekly market.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ho JG-1)</sup> At some point the region came under the administration of [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md).

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Granitehall was an active settlement, being ruled by Duke Fenrald. It had a rivalry with [Oakwood](Lore_Oakwood.md) and [Karthwasten Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Karthwasten_Hall).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena-3)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Granite Hill continued to pay taxes to Falkreath and had its own emissary.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The dragon [Vuljotnaak](Lore_Vuljotnaak.md), who was buried near Granite Hill,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ao D-5)</sup> was resurrected by [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) upon his return.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Granite Hill was originally slated to be implemented into *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*, but was cut before release. However, the area in-game still appears under the name "Granite Hill" when looking at the map near [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md), where the Great Henge Resurrection dragon mound is found.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-2)</sup>
