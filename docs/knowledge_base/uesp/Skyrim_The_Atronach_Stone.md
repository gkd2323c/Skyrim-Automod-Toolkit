[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Atronach_Stone_%28active%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Atronach_Stone_%28active%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-Atronach_Stone_(active).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-Atronach_Stone_(active).jpg.md) The Atronach Stone when it's activated *"Those under the sign of The Atronach absorb a portion of incoming spell damage and have a larger pool of Magicka, but recover it more slowly."*

The stone adds a passive power that increases [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md) by 50 points and adds 50% [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md), but decreases [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) by 50%.

The Atronach a useful Stone for mages thanks to its sizable [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) effect, while the Spell Absorption can be utilized by any character wishing to reduce damage and effects from incoming magic. The reduced magicka regeneration can be overcome with [perks](Skyrim_Restoration.md#Skill_Perks), [potions and enchantments](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md), or by simply fortifying magicka even further (see Notes).

## The Atronach Stone
The stone is located almost due south of [Bonestrewn Crest](Skyrim_Bonestrewn_Crest.md), east-northeast of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) in the southern-most part of the hot springs. It is found at the top of a mound with several other stones jutting out at an angle around it, surrounded by roiling volcanic pools. At its base is a steaming, shallow pool where a couple of [jazbay grape](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md) plants are growing. Other nearby vegetation that can be harvested include some [dragon's tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue) plants and [creep clusters](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md).

## Notes
- With no other modifiers, this Stone reduces your base magicka regen from 3% per second to 1.5% per second out of combat.
- Because magicka regenerates at a percent rate of your total magicka, increasing total magicka actually increases the amount of magicka recovered per second. A character with 200 magicka and 50% reduced regeneration recovers the same amount of magicka per second as a character with 100 magicka and base magicka regen.
- [High Elves](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) have a natural affinity for The Atronach Stone, combining their already high magicka pool with the [Highborn](Skyrim_Highborn.md) power to nullify the reduced magicka regen.
- The Atronach Stone is one of only a few methods of obtaining the rare Spell Absorption effect. - Combining it with the [Breton's](Skyrim_Breton.md) [Dragonskin](Skyrim_Dragonskin.md) power grants 100% Spell Absorption, making the player completely immune to magic for 60 seconds. This can be used to trivialize some of the most powerful enemies in the game, such as [Dragon Priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), who deal damage exclusively through use of magic.
- If you choose to forge the [Aetherial Crown](Skyrim_Aetherial_Crown.md) during [Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages), The Atronach Stone can be combined with [The Apprentice Stone](Skyrim_The_Apprentice_Stone.md) to achieve 50% Spell Absorption, +50 magicka, and 50% magicka regeneration, with the downside of 100% [weakness to magic](Skyrim_Weakness_to_Magic.md).
- The Spell Absorb effect also has a chance of absorbing incoming diseases. This is because diseases are a magical effect.
- You can take advantage of otherwise mundane magic effects to recharge your magicka. Jumping up and down on a forge, for instance, will provide a small but noticeable boost to magicka regeneration.
