# Vounoura

| --- | --- |
| Type | Island |
| Continent | [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) |

Vounoura is an island roughly a month's voyage away from [Tel Aruhn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tel_Aruhn) in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920ES-1)</sup> It serves as a haven where [Morag Tong](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morag_Tong) assassins retire when they become too famous to continue their work.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920ES-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FAD-2)</sup> Mjahlar Virian, a veteran member of the Morag Tong, journeyed to the island before passing on the mantle of assassin to his daughter [Naryu Virian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Naryu_Virian).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SM-3)</sup>
