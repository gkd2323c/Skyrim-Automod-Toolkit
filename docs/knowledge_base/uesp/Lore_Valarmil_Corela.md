## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png/200px-ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png) Summerset Isles
### Reign
Valarmil Corela was a contemporary of Veloth and became one of his followers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC1-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> She followed the prophet throughout the turbulent years when the [Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md) and the High King sought to suppress his growing movement. When Veloth ultimately chose to lead his followers eastward in search of a land where they could freely practice their beliefs, not all of his disciples chose to follow. Some could not bring themselves to abandon their homes and wealth. Others remained because of kinship or duty. Valarmil was among those who decided to remain in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Although she and her household did not join the exodus, they continued to privately practice the faith that Veloth had taught them, even as the worship of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) fell into disfavor across the isles in the aftermath of the Velothi departure.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC1-1)</sup>

### Legacy
Centuries passed, and though Velothi beliefs were increasingly suppressed, the descendants of Valarmil endured. The Corelanya Clan grew into a noble house of considerable wealth, influence, and martial strength. Yet their quiet devotion to the Daedric powers remained a persistent political liability. No High King trusted the Corelanyas enough to grant them a fiefdom, and their religious convictions left them vulnerable to scrutiny.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC1-1)</sup>

Valarmil features prominently in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC1-1)</sup>
