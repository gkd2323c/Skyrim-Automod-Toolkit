# Cities


Fast traveling to a major city places you just inside its main gate, but each major city also has a keep or palace where the jarl resides, which is a separate fast-travel destination and is listed below. Additionally, Riften has a third fast-travel destination once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), namely the entrance to the guild's [headquarters](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(place).md).

### Major Cities
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Markarth.png) **[Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md)** — A major city in the southwest of Skyrim near the border of High Rock, and the capital of the Reach. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Understone+Keep) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Riften.png) **[Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)** — A major city in the southeast of Skyrim near the borders of Morrowind and Cyrodiil, and the capital of the Rift. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Riften) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Castle_2.png) **[Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md)** — A castle located at the southern end of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mistveil+Keep) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Landmark.png) **[Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(place).md)** — The headquarters of the Thieves Guild located with the sewers beneath Riften, known as The Ratway. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Thieves+Guild) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Solitude.png) **[Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)** — A major city on the northwest coast of Skyrim, and the capital of both Haafingar and the entire province. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Solitude) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Solitude Palace.png) **[Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md)** — The current seat of power for Solitude, Haafingar, and Skyrim; and home to the High King of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Blue+Palace) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Whiterun.png) **[Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md)** — A major city in the center of Skyrim, and the capital of Whiterun Hold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Windhelm) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Palace.png) **[Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md)** — A large castle at the far end of the Valunstrad district in Windhelm.
