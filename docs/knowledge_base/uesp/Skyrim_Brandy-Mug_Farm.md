[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-place-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg) Brandy-Mug Farm **Brandy-Mug Farm** is a [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) farm, a short distance east of [Windhelm Stables](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables.md).

It is a modest single-roomed farmhouse, that is home to [Bolfrida Brandy-Mug](Skyrim_Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug.md). It consists of two zones: the [exterior](#Exterior) and the [interior](#Brandy-Mug_Farm).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
As you approach the farmhouse from the road there is a blue mountain flower to the right of the path to the front door. In the fenced off field beside the farmhouse [Bolfrida](Skyrim_Bolfrida_Brandy-Mug.md) cultivates eight bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) and a large number of [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes. In the field, there is a chicken coop, along with two [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md), a [goat](Skyrim_Goat.md) as well as a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) to the rear of the field and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) by the farmhouse.

### Brandy-Mug Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Brandy-Mug_Farm.jpg) Brandy-Mug farm interior Upon entering there is a set of shelves on your left, against the north wall holding two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md), three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) and several pieces of cheese on top. On the floor to the left is a basket containing two more leeks and hanging from the ceiling above are two garlic braids, three bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). In the northeastern corner there is a barrel containing [foodstuffs](Skyrim_Food.md) and coming out from the center of the east wall is an owned double bed with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing leveled items at the foot of the bed.

In the southeastern corner is a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md) anched against the south wall a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) with a rack hanging from the ceiling to the right holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). In the middle of the south wall, opposite the entrance is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front and on the mantelpiece is a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Daughter of the Niben](Skyrim_Daughter_of_the_Niben.md)*. To the right of the fireplace, also against the south wall is a long wooden table and bench with a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), several loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a potato, an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md) and a sprig of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md). Under the table is a [potion of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor). In the northwestern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs with four more sprigs of snowberries on top.

## Notes
- Other than most barrels and sacks, all containers in the farm are [non-respawning](Skyrim_Respawning.md) and safe to store items in.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the barrels outside the building as owned by the farm.
