[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/ON-place-Riverhold_04.jpg/200px-ON-place-Riverhold_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Riverhold_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Riverhold_04.jpg) The savannah outside Riverhold in 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/ON-item-Corinthe_Fragment.jpg/200px-ON-item-Corinthe_Fragment.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Corinthe_Fragment.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Corinthe_Fragment.jpg) Trees of the savannah The **High Savannah** is an area of arid savannah and dry grassland that stretches along the northwest corner of [northern](Lore_Anequina.md) [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GTNE-1)</sup> Its largest settlement is the city of [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> There are twisted trees found around the region,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RRR-3)</sup> and stiff savannah breezes blow through [The Rim](Lore_The_Rim.md) from the savannah.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLain-4)</sup>

It is home to a variety of fauna, particularly felines<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AAIT-5)</sup> domesticated by the native Khajiit such as the Midnight Fennec Fox<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DFF-6)</sup> and Pride-King Lion, the latter of which are the top predators of the area.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PKL-7)</sup> "Savannah Grassware" consisting of light but sturdy woven bowls are fashioned out of the native plants.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SG-8)</sup>

Pelinal Whitestrake spilled the blood of many Khajiit when traveling south from Cyrodiil.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAzin-9)</sup> The Baandari traditionally set up camp in the region and dance in the grass.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-VVVRB-10)</sup>
