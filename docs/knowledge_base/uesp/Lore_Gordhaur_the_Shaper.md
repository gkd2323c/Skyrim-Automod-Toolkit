[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/OB-place-Ninendava.jpg/200px-OB-place-Ninendava.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Ninendava.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Ninendava.jpg) Ninendava Gordhaur the Shaper was one of the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) Kings.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Saromir-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Pelinal-2)</sup> He was leader of the city of [Ninendava](Lore_Ninendava.md). In [1E 242](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_242), he was challenged to a duel by [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md), who smashed his head on a goat-faced altar at Ninendava and killed him. Pelinal used a plague spell to prevent him from reforming by [welkynd-magic](Lore_Welkynd_Stone.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Pelinal-2)</sup>

He is considered an infamous Ayleid King, compared to figures such as [Fire King Hadhuul](Lore_Hadhuul.md), [Haromir](Lore_Haromir.md) of Copper and Tea, the Flower King [Nilichi](Lore_Nilichi.md), and [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Saromir-1)</sup>
