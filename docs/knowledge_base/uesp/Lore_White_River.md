[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-White_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-White_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-White_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-White_River.jpg) The White River circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/ON-place-White_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-White_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-White_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-White_River.jpg) The White River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The White River is the longest river in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), stretching from the south of the province to the northeast corner. Its source is [Lake Ilinalta](Lore_Lake_Ilinalta.md), north of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md). It flows northeast to join the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) past [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), the mouth of the river is called Slaughterfish Bay and is part of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md)'s borders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-1)</sup> Along the way, it passes through [Riverwood](Lore_Riverwood.md) and near [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md). [Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md) and [River Yorgrim](Lore_River_Yorgrim.md) are the two main tributaries of the White River.

The waterfalls where the White River meets the Yorgrim River are known as **Dead Man's Fall**. The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of the Lower Yorgrim will jump off the waterfalls and into the river as part of a [New Life Fesitval](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:New_Life_Festival) tradition called the Snow Bear Plunge.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPlunge-2)</sup>
