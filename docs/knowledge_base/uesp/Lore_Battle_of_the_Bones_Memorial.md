Battle of the Bones Memorial *A statue commemorating a bloody Nordic conflict*
---
On this hill, the great [Unnvald Ironhand](Lore_Unnvald_Ironhand.md) met the [necromancers](Lore_Necromancy.md) of [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md) in battle. With the blade Bonebane in hand, the [undead](Lore_Undead.md) were left shattered and ruined.

May this stand forever as a tribute to our fallen. And as a warning to our enemies.
