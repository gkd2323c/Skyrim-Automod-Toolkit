Sudi's Journal **by [Sudi](Skyrim_Sudi.md)** *A journal detailing the moments from Sudi moving into Frostflow Lighthouse until her family's massacre by Falmer* *
---
I hate it here. Why did we have to move to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) anyway? I miss the big port cities, there was always something to do and new people around. Or even being cabin girl on one ships *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* we used to sail on would be better than this! [Mani](Skyrim_Mani.md) says we should run away, but I can't leave Mother and Father alone out here... Oh what to do?

----

We've been here for two months now and there is nothing to do! From the top of the [lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md) we can see all the way to the [College at Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md), but [Father](Skyrim_Habd.md) says that magic is for [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worshippers. The only interesting thing to have happened lately is that [Mother](Skyrim_Ramati.md) snapped at us at dinner and accused us of stealing things out of the cellar. I did hear scratching down there the other night, but I thought it was just Father cleaning fish.

----

I caught Mani in the cellar a couple days ago going through some the barrels and crates. At first I thought it was him all along making those scratches to play a trick on me, but I heard them again last night and Mani was in bed. Father finally said he would go get some traps and poison from town in case it's [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md). I'm really scared, it doesn't sound like skeevers to me.

----

Mani won't listen, he thinks I'm just making up the noises and keeps sneaking into the cellar! Oooh why is he so stupid some times? I don't know why he keeps going down there but I've found the [cellar key](Skyrim_Mani%27s_Cellar_Key.md) he copied and hid it in Mother's favorite keepsake so he won't find it. The scratching is getting louder.
