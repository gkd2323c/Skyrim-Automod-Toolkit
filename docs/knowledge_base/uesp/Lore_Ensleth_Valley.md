The Ensleth Valley is an inland region of northwestern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) wrapped above by the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup> A lone stream flows down through the valley, with a big salt lick at the stream's headwaters; the salt attracts wildlife such as the [elk](Lore_Elf.md), [deer](Lore_Deer.md), and [muskrey](Lore_Muskrey.md), making the valley a good place for game hunting.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup> Hunters from [Oleer Mar](Lore_Oleer_Mar.md) are known to visit the valley.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup>

Another valley and the coastal village of [Sathil](Lore_Sathil.md) precede the Ensleth Valley from the southwest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup>
