[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cd/SR-creature-Bone_Wolf.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bone_Wolf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bone_Wolf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bone_Wolf.jpg) The pet Bone Wolf A pet Bone Wolf can be obtained during the related quest by returning its [Totem Bone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Totem_Bone). It is initially found locked inside a cage at a necromancer's camp.

The Bone Wolf is essential, cannot be hit by attacks, and will not engage in combat. Its main function is to carry items, with a carry weight of 200 and the ability follow you even if you already have another pet follower. You can instruct it to wait at a city, wait at its current location, wait at your house, or to carry items. It can also be summoned using a [teleportation spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Teleport_Pet__Bone_Wolf).

### Bone Wolf's Revenge
| *"Increases damage on the undead by 25% while Bone Wolf is in your company."* |
| --- |

When following you, the pet Bone Wolf grants an ability named "Bone Wolf's Revenge" which makes your spells and weapons more effective against undead. This can stack multiplicatively with the [Bloodworm Helm's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodworm_Helm) enchantment, or the [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md) perk for spells.

### Related Quests
- **[Let Sleeping Wolves Lie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Let_Sleeping_Wolves_Lie)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bone_Wolf.md)</sup>: Stop a necromancer from raising undead wolf minions.

### Bugs
- If the Bone Wolf is brought to [Druadach Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt) after siding with [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) during the quest [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md), it will be hostile towards the friendly Forsworn there. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Adding the Bone Wolf to the [Druadach Redoubt Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt Faction) faction (Ref ID `E3609`) will fix this problem.

- If the Bone Wolf is present when time is frozen during the [Under Saarthal](Skyrim_Under_Saarthal.md) quest, attacks will no longer pass through it.

## Bugs
- Bone Wolves may be unaffected by spells that target the undead. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- The "Revenge" perk will cause you to take extra damage as an undead if you are a vampire. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- Creation Club zombies may fail to spawn in certain locations while Bone Wolves are present. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
