[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/SR-item-Tyrant%27s_Bane.jpg/200px-SR-item-Tyrant%27s_Bane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tyrant%27s_Bane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tyrant%27s_Bane.jpg) Tyrant's Bane The Tyrant's Bane is a [unique](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md) [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) added by [Alternative Armors - Dragon Plate](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dragon_Plate.md). It's carried by [Crowstooth](Skyrim_Crowstooth.md), and can be retrieved after killing her during [Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md). The axe has the same appearance as the [forsworn axe](Skyrim_Forsworn_Axe.md), but has a higher base [damage](Skyrim_Damage.md). Tempering it is not possible.

## Related Quests
- **[Bones for a Crow](Skyrim_Bones_for_a_Crow.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Alternative_Armors_-_Dragon_Plate.md)</sup>: Hunt down the Forsworn Crowstooth.

## Notes
- The **Tyrant's Bane** can be disenchanted.
