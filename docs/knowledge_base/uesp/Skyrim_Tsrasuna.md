[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Tsrasuna.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tsrasuna.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsrasuna.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsrasuna.jpg) Tsrasuna **Tsrasuna**, a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is one of the hunters who shows up in [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) hunting [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

She is clad in a set of [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), matching pairs of [bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), and [a matching helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md). She wields a [long bow](Skyrim_Long_Bow.md) coupled with twenty [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md) and [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md). She also carries a leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) of [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md).
