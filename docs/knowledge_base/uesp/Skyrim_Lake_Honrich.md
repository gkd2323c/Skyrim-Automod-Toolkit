[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) Lake Honrich **Lake Honrich** is a lake in the eastern [Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), immediately southwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). It is drained by the [Treva River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River) and was named Lake Honnith in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). [Goldenglow Estate](Skyrim_Goldenglow_Estate.md) occupies a cluster of islands in the middle of the lake.

Lake Honrich is a popular spot for fishermen across Skyrim, and it even has a [fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) found on its eastern shores in Riften.
