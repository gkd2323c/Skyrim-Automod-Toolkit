[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Pinepeak Cavern is a cave next to the path up the [Throat of the World](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md), in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). It is a natural cavern with a deep and expansive sub-structure that refugees have often used as a sanctuary in centuries of tumultuous times. While some of the local [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) claim that it is safe, others refuse to enter the cave because of the fear that it may cave in on them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONLoad Pinepeak-1)</sup> Unbeknownst to them, Pinepeak Cavern has a tomb deep in the cave system built for one of the [Five Hundred Companions](Lore_Five_Hundred_Companions.md), Hakra,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONNetapatuu-2)</sup> who died in battle against the [half-giant](Lore_Half-Giant.md) chieftain, [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOTR27-3)</sup>

## History
According to the Speluncus Tamrielicum, the reason why natural caverns such as Pinepeak are so abundant is that they may have served as a system of capillaries and nodes that pulsed the divine essence of its [Aedric](Lore_Aedra.md) creators.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLoad Weeping-4)</sup> [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) and his Five Hundred Companions fought a large battle against the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) Chieftain, Sinmur and the [giant](Lore_Giant.md) clans of ancient Skyrim until Sinmur stood his last stand. Many companions died in this final battle, including Hakra and her fellow companion, [Valdur](Lore_Valdur.md). After Sinmur had been felled by Ysgramor and his legendary axe, [Wuuthrad](Lore_Wuuthrad.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOTR27-3)</sup> both Hakra and Valdur were buried in the western corners of the Rift, with Hakra placed in Pinepeak's tomb, to protect the land in death from threats to Skyrim's longevity.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONValdur-5)</sup> Hakra's spirit was bound to the tomb via burial urns of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md), [Stuhn](Lore_Stuhn.md), and [Shor](Lore_Shor.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONHakra-6)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) had ambushed the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) at Pinepeak Cavern, during their plan to enslave Hakra's spirit to resurrect Sinmur. With a shard of Hakra's essence, the [King's Arrow](Lore_The_Vestige.md) was able to work with Hakra and release her from the tomb's ward. Once they destroyed the final urn, the Worm Cult had taken full control of her spirit and sent it to attack the King's Arrow. The warrior was not only able to defeat the companion, but also the Worm Cult that infested the cave and with Hakra's spirit liberated, the King's Arrow traveled south to Taarengrav, where the Worm Cult conducted the same plan for Valdur's spirit.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONTomb-7)</sup>

Several years later in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the entrance to Hakra's tomb had been lost and the cave was used as [bear](Lore_Bear.md)'s den. Some hunters tried to take out the bear but have failed.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SRPinepeak-8)</sup> The [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) sometimes get contracted work to eliminate the bear that lives in Pinepeak Cavern.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SRAnimal-9)</sup>
