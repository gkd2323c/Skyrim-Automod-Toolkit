**Call of Valor** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) that summons a hero to fight alongside you. [Tsun](Skyrim_Tsun.md) teaches this shout to you at the end of [Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md), the conclusion of the [main quest](Skyrim_Main_Quest.md).

Using one word summons [Gormlaith Golden-Hilt](Skyrim_Gormlaith_Golden-Hilt.md), two words summons [Felldir the Old](Skyrim_Felldir_the_Old.md), and three words summons [Hakon One-Eye](Skyrim_Hakon_One-Eye.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Dragonslayer](Skyrim_Dragonslayer.md)**: Put an end to the [World-Eater](Skyrim_Alduin.md).

## Notes
- If Call of Valor is used while a conjured creature is still active, the shout will dispel the creature and replace it with the summoned hero. The reverse can happen as well; if a summoned hero is active, a [Conjure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure) spell will dispel the hero and replace it with the conjured creature. This even applies to summoning [Durnehviir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Durnehviir), as well, as he is considered a summoned creature: calling for Durnehviir's help will dispel the heroes of Sovngarde, and summoning a hero of Sovngarde will dispel Durnehviir.
- The heroes summoned by Call of Valor can be healed by restoration spells such as [Healing Hands](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_Hands) and the shout allows one to keep the summoned hero indefinitely, unless another creature is summoned.
- It is not possible to have the [Spectral Assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spectral_Assassin) and the Hero of Sovngarde summon out together, even with the [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls) perk. Summoning one after the other will relinquish the former. The same applies with the [Arniel's Shade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arniel%27s_Shade) [summon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Arniel%27s_Shade).
- If you happen to be in the path of the hero's Unrelenting Force shout, you will be ragdolled just as much as any enemy you were standing next to.

## Bugs
- Like [Conjure](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure) spells, the shout may be absorbed by any active [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md) effects. If the shout is absorbed, a hero will not be summoned, but the shout's cooldown will still be triggered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
