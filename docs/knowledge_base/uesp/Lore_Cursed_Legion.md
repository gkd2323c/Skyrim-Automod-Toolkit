# Cursed Legion

The Cursed Legion of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) was a legendary traveling militia of [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) warriors that existed before the founding of [Nova Orsinium](Lore_Nova_Orsinium.md). They served as champions of the Orcs, enforcing whatever borders they could for the proliferation of their people. The memory and accomplishments of the Cursed Legion has endured into the modern day.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-16AOM12-1)</sup>

The only known person who purportedly rode with the Cursed Legion is an Orc named Tunner. According to *[Orsinium and the Orcs](Lore_How_Orsinium_Passed_to_the_Orcs.md)*, "Old Tunner" was an elderly servant to a [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) nobleman in [3E 399](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_399), but he had once been a trainer to other Orc warriors (presumably during his service in the Cursed Legion).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HOPTO-2)</sup>
