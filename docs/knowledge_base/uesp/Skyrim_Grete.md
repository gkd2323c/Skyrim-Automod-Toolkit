[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Grete.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grete.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grete.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grete.jpg) Grete **Grete** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md) who only appears as the musician during the party at the start of the quest [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) if the first choice bard, [Illdi](Skyrim_Illdi.md), is dead.

She wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd) and does not carry anything else.

During the party, she will be found inside of the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md), playing instrumental music for the guests. Once the quest is over, she will be removed from the game.

## Related Quests
- **[Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md)**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Diplomatic Immunity
She seems to enjoy herself at the party and will happily tell you:

- *"I hope you're enjoying the music, sir/ma'am."*

She will also tell you about the benefits of playing at Thalmor parties:

- *"These parties are a great way to earn a little extra money."*
- *"It's good practice. Most people barely notice what I'm playing."*

While all the aforementioned lines are shared with Illdi, she also has one unique line when she tells you about her big dream:

- *"I hope to get into the Bards College one day."*
