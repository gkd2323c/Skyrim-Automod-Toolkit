# Tide-Born

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/ON-place-Shell-Tide_Village.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shell-Tide_Village.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shell-Tide_Village.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shell-Tide_Village.jpg) Shell-Tide Village The Tide-Born, also known as the **Izta-Ahuak** are one of the two [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) cultural groups native to [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md) (The other being the Stone-Nest) and have various settlements on the western half of the island. Their ancestors arrived to the island prior to the [Duskfall](Lore_Duskfall.md)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> and thus, unlike their mainlander cousins, they are not linked to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) and instead revere [the Tides](Lore_The_Tide.md). This relates to their belief that the Tide-Born are the reborn memories of Argonians with an unfinished destiny.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VOFS-2)</sup>

The Tide-Born also have a few known sub-tribes found around Western Solstice; [Salt-Cured](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salt-Cured), Palm-Backed and [Mihuitleel](Lore_Mihuitleel.md). In recent times, they also absorbed extant members of a destroyed [Naga](Lore_Naga.md) tribe once found on the island.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOTWC-3)</sup>
