The Founding of Bloodtoil *The history of the village of Bloodtoil*
---
We spent the winter in [Falinesti](Lore_Falinesti.md), only to return and find our holy site overrun by [Drublog](Lore_Drublog_Clan.md) squatters claiming it as their own. The faithful of [Z'en](Lore_Z%27en.md) returned with an overwhelming force of rangers and removed the vile beasts.

Our forefathers' village, renamed Bloodtoil, has been reclaimed. But the shrine is unclean, filled with relics to [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md). A new shrine to Z'en will be dedicated shortly.

The [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) threatened to return, but the faithful of Z'en do not fear their petty threats. Z'en ensures debts are paid in kind.
