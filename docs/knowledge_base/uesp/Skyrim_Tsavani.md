[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/db/SR-npc-Tsavani.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tsavani.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsavani.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsavani.jpg) Tsavani **Tsavani** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and the cook at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). She wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). During your [infiltration](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md) of the Embassy, she will be present in the kitchen.

## Related Quests
- **[Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md)**: Infiltrate a party at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).

## Quest-Related Events
She acknowledges your presence, and [Malborn](Skyrim_Malborn.md) blackmails her with her [moon sugar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Moon_Sugar) addiction to prevent her revealing you to the guards:

**Tsavani:** *"Who comes, Malborn? You know I don't like strange smells in my kitchen."* **Malborn:** *"A guest, feeling ill. Leave the poor wretch be."* **Tsavani:** *"A guest? In the kitchens? You know this is against the rules..."* **Malborn:** *"Rules, is it, Tsavani? I didn't realize that eating Moon Sugar was permitted. Perhaps I should ask [the Ambassador](Skyrim_Elenwen.md)..."* **Tsavani:** *"Tss! Get out of here, I saw nothing."* If you talk to her after this conversation, she'll only say, *"Go. Out. Before I change my mind."*

## Bugs
- Tsavani may say "Go. Out. Before I change my mind." even if spoken to before Malborn talks to her.
