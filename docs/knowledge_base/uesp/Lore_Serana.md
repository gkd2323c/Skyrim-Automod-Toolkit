Serana is the estranged daughter of Lord [Harkon](Lore_Harkon.md) and [Valerica](Lore_Valerica.md). She and her family became [Vampire Lords](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Vampire_Lord) after her father made a deal with [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and founded the [Volkihar](Lore_Volkihar.md) clan. Her mother hid her within Dimhollow Crypt to keep her out of the reach of her father and his goal to complete the [Tyranny of the Sun](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md) prophecy,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valerica-1)</sup> which sought to plunge [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) into eternal darkness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Harkon-2)</sup>

## History
Serana was born to [Valerica](Lore_Valerica.md) and [Harkon](Lore_Harkon.md), who was initially a cruel tyrant that ruled over an unknown but purportedly massive portion of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). As Harkon's mortal life neared its end, he sacrificed one thousand innocent souls to the [Daedric Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Prince) Molag Bal to transform himself into a Vampire Lord.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Harkon-2)</sup> Valerica and Serana were offered to Molag Bal on his summoning day, as was expected of them. After the ceremony, which few were known to survive, the two were likewise made Lords, with the further distinction of being [Daughters of Coldharbour](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daughters_of_Coldharbour).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Valerica-1)</sup> Serana's transformation was particularly unpleasant and emotionally scarring due to Molag Bal's cruel nature.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Serana-3)</sup>

Despite his newfound [immortality](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Immortality), Harkon was unsatisfied, loathing his kind's weakness to the [sun](Lore_Sun.md). After centuries of searching, he found and became obsessed with the [Tyranny of the Sun](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md) prophecy, which required the blood of Serana or her mother to power [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) and blot out the sun.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Harkon-2)</sup> In defiance of Harkon, Valerica fled with Serana and took two of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) that were needed to fully understand the prophecy. While Valerica hid in the [Soul Cairn](Lore_Soul_Cairn.md) with hers, Serana was sealed inside Dimhollow Crypt with the other scroll on the 4th of [Hearthfire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hearthfire),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup> at some point prior to the rise of the [Septim Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) at the end of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Sleep)</sup> Valerica constructed a complicated suspended animation tube to store her in, and placed [gargoyle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gargoyle) statues to frighten away the curious.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-No DC:V3-5)</sup>

Centuries later, in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Serana was awakened by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who had been sent to investigate Dimhollow Crypt by the [Dawnguard](Lore_Dawnguard.md), a group of vampire hunters.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Isran-6)</sup> The hero accompanied Serana as they escorted her back to Castle Volkihar.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Bloodline-7)</sup> Some time later, Serana approached the Dragonborn to find the two other Elder Scrolls required to understand the complete prophecy.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Serana-3)</sup> The pair ventured into the Soul Cairn to find Valerica and her Elder Scroll. Upon locating her, Serana convinced Valerica to help them by revealing her frustrations with her and Harkon using her as a pawn to enable to prevent the prophecy. After acquiring the Elder Scroll, she began to come to terms with the fact that the path she was on would lead her to killing her father, whose obsession with the prophecy she deemed too great to be reasoned with.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Serana-3)</sup>

After reading the Elder Scroll using a ritual at an [Ancestor Glade](Lore_Ancestor_Glade.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-UV-8)</sup> the duo traveled to the [Forgotten Vale](Lore_Forgotten_Vale.md) and encountered two of the last known [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md), one of whom was [Vyrthur](Lore_Vyrthur.md), who revealed he had written the Tyranny of the Sun prophecy long ago, and had arranged events in such a way to acquire Serana's blood and complete the ritual for his own uses.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Vyrthur-9)</sup> The two then killed Vyrthur, claiming Auri-El's Bow and constructing a final plan to end Harkon.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Serana-3)</sup> Together, Serana and the Last Dragonborn confronted and slew Lord Harkon at Castle Volkihar. Serana was then free to live out her life however she saw fit, expressing a desire to continue adventuring with the Last Dragonborn.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Serana-3)</sup> It is unknown if she eventually sought out a cure for her [vampirism](Lore_Vampirism.md) or if she chose to remain a vampire.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Cure-10)</sup>
