**Synod Researchers** are [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [citizens](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who can be found dead in [Mzulft](Skyrim_Mzulft.md).

The researchers each wear [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and a pair of [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md). They carry a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) (up to [glass](Skyrim_Malachite.md) quality) and a 10% chance of a random leveled [scroll](Skyrim_Scrolls.md).
