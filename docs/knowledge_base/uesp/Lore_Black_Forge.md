[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/ON-place-The_Black_Forge.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Black_Forge.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Black_Forge.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Black_Forge.jpg) The Black Forge The Black Forge is a massive facility in southeastern [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). The arms and armor that [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s troops bear are [forged](Lore_Smithing.md) here,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOTP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPOVG-2)</sup> as are innumerable tools used in the Daedric Prince's schemes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPOVG-2)</sup> Notably, the [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md) and the [Great Shackle](Lore_The_Great_Shackle.md) were constructed here.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOPOVG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBF-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLS-4)</sup> Ebony-alloy cold-iron is a powerful material used in construction at the Black Forge, and the secret to [smithing](Lore_Smithing.md) with it is a secret closely guarded by the higher-ranking [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) (formally referred to as "Overkyn") assigned there.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLS-4)</sup>

The Black Forge is also where [Black Forge Iron Senches](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Forge_Iron_Senche) were created. This unique form of [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) is composed of ebony-alloy iron and bound into the form of a [senche](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) for use as a mount.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

It is incredibly dangerous to work within the Black Forge. Many of the [Soul-Shriven](Lore_Soul-Shriven.md) thralls assigned here only last a few shifts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOLS-4)</sup>
