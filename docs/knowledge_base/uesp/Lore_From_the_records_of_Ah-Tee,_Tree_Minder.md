[![ON-concept-Hist Tree.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/22/ON-concept-Hist_Tree.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Hist_Tree.png)
- Something stirs within the Tree. I feel uneasy, as though eyes peer at me from under dark waters. I checked once again for the sapthief beetles that so disturbed the Tree last season, even though this feeling is different, but found no evidence of their return. I stayed among the roots late into the night, unable to sleep with the unsteady vibration in my bones. I have cared for this [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) nearly all my years and have always been able to find the cause of the few troubles it feels. I shall call out to the fellow Tree Minders —perhaps they know more than I.
