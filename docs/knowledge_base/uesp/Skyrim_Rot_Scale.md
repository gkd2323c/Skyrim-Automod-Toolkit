[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-flora-Rot_Scale.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Rot_Scale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Rot_Scale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Rot_Scale.jpg) Rot Scale Rot Scale is an [unobtainable ingredient](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Ingredients) added to `Update.esm` by [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) [Patch 1.5.97](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md#Version_1.5.97). This means that the ingredient can be added to your inventory via [console commands](Skyrim_Console.md), and the plant is available to be used in [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Main_Page).

With [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md), it can be obtained from Rot Scale plants in [Solitude Sewers](Skyrim_Solitude_Sewers.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>.
