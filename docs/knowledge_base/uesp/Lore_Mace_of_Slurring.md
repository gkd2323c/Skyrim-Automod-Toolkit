# Mace of Slurring

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/TR-weapon-Mace_of_Slurring.png/200px-TR-weapon-Mace_of_Slurring.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Mace_of_Slurring.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-weapon-Mace_of_Slurring.png) The Mace of Slurring (Tribunal) The Mace of Slurring was crafted near the end of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) by [Kyanka](Lore_Kyanka.md), a slightly deranged [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [weaponsmith](Lore_Smithing.md) and [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md). He had a rather warped sense of humor, and apparently delighted at the thought of reducing opponents to a gibbering mess before crushing them to death. However, the mace saw little use.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRTorasa-1)</sup> It was known to drain eloquence of its victims.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MOS-2)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the mace was recovered by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) from Golena Sadri and sold to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md) of the [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) Museum of Artifacts.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Tribunal1-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tribunal2-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Tribunal3-5)</sup>
