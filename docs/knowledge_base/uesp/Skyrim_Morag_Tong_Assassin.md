**Morag Tong Assassins** are members of the [Morag Tong](Skyrim_Morag_Tong.md) guild encountered at [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md) in Solstheim during the quest [Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md). They were hired by [Vendil Ulen](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md) for use in his plot to assassinate [Councilor Lleril Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md). After completing this quest, if you are a member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), you may be [randomly attacked](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Morag_Tong_Competition) on Solstheim by one to three assassins, one of which will be carrying a [writ](Skyrim_Writ_of_Execution__(player).md) explaining why the Morag Tong want you dead.

They wear [Morag Tong armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong_Armor), which is a variant of [Chitin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin) armor, and come in two varieties, a one-handed melee version and a missile version. Both variants can make use of a 20 second vampiric invisibility spell similar to [Embrace of Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Embrace_of_Shadows), which they may use to escape if their health drops too low, as well as a 30 point [Fortify Sneak](Skyrim_Fortify_Sneak.md) spell. The melee variant can also cast [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md).
