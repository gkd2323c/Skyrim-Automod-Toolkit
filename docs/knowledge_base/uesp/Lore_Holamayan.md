[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-place-Holamayan_Monastery.jpg/200px-ON-place-Holamayan_Monastery.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Holamayan_Monastery.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Holamayan_Monastery.jpg) Holamayan circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Holamayan is a monastery and sacred temple of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), located on an isolated island off the eastern coast of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> It lies along [Azura's Coast](Lore_Azura%27s_Coast.md), south of [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md) and east of [Mount Assarnibibi](Lore_Mount_Assarnibibi.md). The monastery has been a pilgrimage site for thousands of years,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NARM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBORM-3)</sup> with ferries coming from as far away as [Ebonheart](Lore_Castle_Ebonheart.md). Secretly, Holamayan became the home of the [Dissident Priests](Lore_Dissident_Priests.md), who disputed the doctrines of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md). Thus, the monastery became a repository for the Apographa, the hidden writings the Temple had suppressed. The monastery itself is built underneath the surface of the island, with its entrance hidden by hills and brush. It is further protected by a magical stone hood that raises only during the times of Azura, dawn and dusk.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWVevrana-4)</sup> Within, the monks pay homage to Azura and the [saints](Lore_Temple_Saints.md) of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) (not including the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md)).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup>

In the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) undertook a pilgrimage to this temple of Azura to speak with her. She confirmed that the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) had devious plans which had to be stopped at all costs, leading to the beginning of the [War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NARM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TBORM-3)</sup> The [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) came to the island in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) seeking guidance from the monks on the Lost Prophecies of the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> Following the defeat of [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) and the vindication of the Ashlander prophecies, the monastery made plans to rededicate itself to the study of prophecy.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWMehra-5)</sup>
