# Places


Tamriel is located on a planet called [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) in the realm of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), together with the continents of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md), [Atmora](Lore_Atmora.md), [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md), and [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md). [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris.md) and [Lyg](Lore_Lyg.md) are other landmasses that do not have solid evidence for their physical existence. The [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md) lies west of Tamriel and the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) surrounds the remainder. Islands of note that are not usually considered part of any continent include [Cathnoquey](Lore_Cathnoquey.md), [Esroniet](Lore_Esroniet.md), [Roscrea](Lore_Roscrea.md), [Thras](Lore_Thras.md), and [Yneslea](Lore_Yneslea.md).

Beyond Mundus, the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) contains numerous [Planes of Existence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Existence) including the 'outer realms' of [Aetherius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aetherius) and [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), and the infinite [Void](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Void).

## Provinces of Tamriel
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg/200px-MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-Arena_Anthology_Game_Map.jpg) Tamriel - **[Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)** — A huge and mostly uncharted region of densely vegetated wetlands at the southeastern tip of Tamriel. The reptilian [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) thrive in these foreboding swamps.
- **[Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)** — Also known as the Imperial Province, it lies in the central-south part of Tamriel and it's the home of a humanoid race known as the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md). The center of their Empire and seat of governance, Cyrodiil is also known as "the Heartland".
- **[Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md)** — Divided across arid badlands to the north and a more populous sub-tropical zone in the south, this region marks the southern coast of Tamriel. Elsweyr is home to the cat-like [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).
- **[Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)** — Lying in western Tamriel, this province is dominated by the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md) desert. Hammerfell is inhabited by the human race [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md).
- **[High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md)** — Home to the [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md), this province is divided into multiple Breton city states and minor kingdoms. It is a temperate coastal region in northwestern Tamriel.
- **[Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md)** — The province in the northeast corner of Tamriel is home to the [Dunmer (Dark Elves)](Lore_Dunmer.md). It consists of a continental mainland and a large island in the center called [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), separated by an inlet from the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).
- **[Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)** — This is the most northern province of Tamriel and it is inhabited by a human race called [Nords](Lore_Nord.md). It is a cold and mountainous region.
- **[Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md)** — A province in southwestern Tamriel consisting of fourteen islands of varying size. The Isles are the homeland of the [Altmer (High Elves)](Lore_Altmer.md).
- **[Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)** — The densely forested, sub-tropical region that forms the southwest coastal plain of Tamriel. Valenwood is home to the [Bosmer (Wood Elves)](Lore_Bosmer.md) and [Imga](Lore_Imga.md).
