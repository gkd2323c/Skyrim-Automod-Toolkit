[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-npc-Drifa.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Drifa.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drifa.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Drifa.jpg) Drifa **Drifa** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [pawnbroker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawnbroker) living in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). She owns the [Pawned Prawn](Skyrim_Pawned_Prawn.md) with her husband [Bersi Honey-Hand](Skyrim_Bersi_Honey-Hand.md), and will take over the business if anything happens to him. She cares deeply for him but thinks that he spends too much time worrying about crime and corruption in Riften and not enough about himself. He believes that someone is trying to make a move to take the Pawned Prawn from them, but she thinks he is just being paranoid.

Drifa wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8715) and a pair of [fine boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#000cee82). She is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pawned_Prawn_Key) to her store, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
Drifa greets visitors to the Pawned Prawn with:

*"Hello. Welcome to the Pawned Prawn."* *"If there's anything you need, I'd be glad to help you."* Her normal greetings:

*"My husband Bersi will be the death of me. He spends too much time worrying about Riften and not enough about himself."* *"All the crime and corruption in Riften is really getting to him... and now that we hear rumors about the dragons, he's been very depressed."* *"Bersi's afraid that someone's making a move to take the Pawned Prawn from us. I think he's being paranoid."* *"We both own the Pawned Prawn together, but he's singlehandedly kept the place running despite all our problems."* *"I just care about my husband so much and I hate to see him in this state."* She will say goodbye with

*"Be careful out there... Riften can be dangerous."*
## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Spread the Love
During the quest [Spread the Love](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spread_the_Love), you have the option to give her a [missive](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara), which she will accept gratefully with:

*"Oh, how kind! Thank you."*
### Taking Care of Business
During the quest [Taking Care of Business](Skyrim_Taking_Care_of_Business.md), you have the option of persuading Bersi Honey-Hand to repay his debt with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) by breaking his prized Dwarven urn. While you are breaking it and Bersi is lamenting his ill fortune, Drifa will loudly tell her husband to

*"Just pay them, Bersi!"*
## Conversations
Drifa may also be seen having random conversations with her husband Bersi or other Riften residents:

**Bersi**: *"Drifa? I was looking through our books and there's an entry for 'spices.' Says we spent 300 septims. What is that?"*
**Drifa**: *"Spices? I'm certain I have no idea what you're talking about."*
**Bersi**: *"You sure? It was in your handwriting. If you can't keep the books straight then let me do them."*
**Drifa**: *"Oh, yes... spices. That was a special order for someone in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). Should be along any day now. Don't let it worry you, my dear."* **Drifa**: *"We got a few pieces of jewelry at the [Prawn](Skyrim_Pawned_Prawn.md), Madesi. Want to come take a look at them later?"*
**[Madesi](Skyrim_Madesi.md)**: *"I wish I could, milady. Sadly, I haven't sold enough of my own jewelry let alone buy some from you. I'm sorry."*
**Drifa**: *"Oh, I'm so sorry. I'm certain Bolli * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * would be happy to lend you a few septims."*
**Madesi**: *"Much appreciated, Lady Drifa, but I'll manage."* **Drifa**: *"[Mjoll](Skyrim_Mjoll_the_Lioness.md)! I'm glad I found you."*
**Mjoll**: *"What is it, Drifa? What's the matter?"*
**Drifa**: *"It's Bersi. He's getting so angry about the corruption in Riften, he said he's going to walk right up to [Maven](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) and give her a piece of his mind."*
**Mjoll**: *"If I see him, I'll make sure he doesn't do anything brash. Don't worry."* She may be accosted by either [Snilf](Skyrim_Snilf.md) or [Edda](Skyrim_Edda.md), Riften's two beggars. Either way, she will take pity on them, telling Snilf, *"Very well, please use it for food."* and Edda, *"Oh, very well. Just try and clean yourself up."*

## Notes
- Drifa uses Pawned Prawn greetings even when she's not manning the store if Bersi is dead.
- Several lines of dialogue incorrectly refer to Drifa as being married to [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, addresses this issue. The audio and dialogue text correctly refer to [Nivenor](Skyrim_Nivenor.md) instead.
- A [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Ceremony) addressed to Drifa from [Talen-Jei](Skyrim_Talen-Jei.md) asking about an Argonian wedding ceremony in [the temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) appears next to [Dinya Balu](Skyrim_Dinya_Balu.md)'s bed in the temple. However, Drifa has no connection with the temple. Given where the letter is found, it's likely Dinya Balu was meant to be the recipient instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, addresses this issue. The letter is addressed to Dinya instead.
