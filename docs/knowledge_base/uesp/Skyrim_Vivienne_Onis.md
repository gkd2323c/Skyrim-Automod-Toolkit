[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-npc-Vivienne_Onis.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vivienne_Onis.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vivienne_Onis.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vivienne_Onis.jpg) Vivienne Onis **Vivienne Onis** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary) who works at [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) with her aunt, [Angeline Morrard](Skyrim_Angeline_Morrard.md). She becomes the owner of the store if anything happens to Angeline.

Vivienne wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Angeline%27s_Aromatics) to her aunt's store, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

[Sorex Vinius](Skyrim_Sorex_Vinius.md) reveals that he grew up with [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md) and Vivienne and that the three were once friends. However, Roggvir ruined their friendship by teasing and bullying him once Sorex told him about his secret affection towards Vivienne.
