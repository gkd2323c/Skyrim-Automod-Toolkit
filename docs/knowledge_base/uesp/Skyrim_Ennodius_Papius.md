[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius.jpg) Ennodius Papius **Ennodius Papius**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md), is a former [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) now living an isolated existence in a small camp a short distance up the hill from his old workplace, [Anga's Mill](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill.md). He is one of the first assigned targets after joining the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md). He does not exist prior to the [related quest](Skyrim_Kill_Ennodius_Papius.md).

He wanders around all day long, at times sitting on a nearby rock, and, if the weather gets tough, warms his hands over the fire. At midnight he crawls into the tent for eight hours of sleep.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Quest-Related Events

### Kill Ennodius Papius
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ennodius_Papius_02.jpg) *"What? What do you mean? Oh! Oh... no... No!"* When handing you the contract on Ennodius' head, [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md) will provide a few facts about the target: *"He lives just outside Anga's Mill, in the woods. He was once the miller. Now Papius is just a paranoid recluse who thinks someone is out to get him. And he's right."* When you arrive at Anga's Mill, you may overhear a conversation between Ennodius' old colleagues, [Kodrir](Skyrim_Kodrir.md) and [Leifur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifur):

**Leifur**: *"Whatever happened to Ennodius? He just up and left."*
**Kodrir**: *"Don't know why you're asking me. But I'm glad that damn Imperial is gone. Something wrong about him."*
**Leifur**: *"You're all heart, Kodrir. I bet Lady Mara is weeping a special tear for you right now."*

When you approach the paranoid Imperial at his camp, he will be quite anxious, asking:

- *"Why are you here? Get away from me!"*

Three options will be available:

| Topic | Dialogue |
| --- | --- |
| *"You were right to be paranoid, Ennodius."* | *"What? What do you mean? Oh! Oh... no... No!"* |
| *"Die, worm!"* | *"Aiiiiiiiieeeeee!"* |
| *(Remain silent)* | *"Who are you? You... you're with the Dark Brotherhood, ain'tcha? I knew it. I knew you'd come for me!"* |

All three options will make him flee with the words:

- *"By the gods! Somebody help me!"*

## Notes
- Ennodius was supposed to be able to tell you a bit about himself if you met him outside the related quest. However, since he only spawns during the quest, this dialogue will never be heard: *"I used to work at Anga's Mill, but I... I had to leave."*, *"Someone's after me I tell you. They're watching me. Somewhere."* and *"Don't talk to me. You could be one of them."*
- Under the waterfall that is just upstream from Ennodius' camp, you can find three quicksilver ingots, a battleaxe, a barrel, a chest, and a strongbox (but see [bugs](#Bugs)).
