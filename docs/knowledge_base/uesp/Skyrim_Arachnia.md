[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-creature-Arachnia.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Arachnia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Arachnia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Arachnia.jpg) Arachnia **Arachnia** is a trained [frostbite spider](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) trapped in a cage inside [Cronvangr Cave](Skyrim_Cronvangr_Cave.md). You can free it to make it your pet. Unlike normal frostbite spiders, it has a blue coloration. It cannot wear an [Animal Collar](Skyrim_Animal_Collar.md) or an [Animal Pack](Skyrim_Animal_Pack.md). It is essential and will not engage in combat.

Arachnia can be summoned using a [teleportation spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Teleport_Pet__Arachnia). It can be instructed to follow you even if you already have another pet following you. You can instruct it to wait at a city, wait at its current location (either temporarily or permanently), wait at your house, or to carry items. It can also be instructed to "spin a web trap", which will cause Arachnia to place a large web on the ground. If an enemy runs over it, they will be briefly [paralyzed](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) and covered in web. Enemies immune to paralysis will not walk over the web trap. Additionally, there will sometimes be a dialogue option to harvest [Potent Frostbite Venom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Frostbite_Venom) from it.

The [*Vampire Naturalist's Journal*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Naturalist%27s_Journal) contains background information on Arachnia.
