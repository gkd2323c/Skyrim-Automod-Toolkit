Correct Ways of Slaying Ra-Netu: 12 *An Ash'abah text describing a ritualistic beheading* *
---
Though a [Ra-Netu](Lore_Ra-Netu.md) is an abomination in the sight of [Tu'whacca](Lore_Tu%27whacca.md), and an offense to the godly of all peoples, it is not therefore to be treated with disrespect. For a human body is a sacred chalice, whether it be filled with the divine liquor of a mortal soul, or the profane offal of an unholy essence.

To that end the [Ash'abah](Lore_Ash%27abah.md) are charged with banishing the unholy essence while doing all that is needful to preserve the sacred chalice. And so we smite the Ra-Netu with the Seventeen Strikes, while uttering the Plea for Forgiveness.

Correct Ways of Slaying Ra-Netu

Strike Twelve: The Comely Beheading

— To feint with a high cut toward the approaching Ra-Netu
— To step past the Ra-Netu on the opposite side while turning the blade
— To utter the Plea for Forgiveness
— To bring the forte of the blade down upon the Ra-Netu between the third and fourth bones of the neck, shearing through from behind
— To utter the Humble Apology
— To collect the severed head, lest it be misplaced in the affray, and set it near the body for later interment
