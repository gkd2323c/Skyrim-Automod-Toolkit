[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-item-Muiri%27s_Ring.jpg/200px-SR-item-Muiri%27s_Ring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Muiri%27s_Ring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Muiri%27s_Ring.jpg) Muiri's Ring Muiri's Ring is a [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) that can be obtained from [its eponymous owner](Skyrim_Muiri.md) as a reward for assassinating [Nilsine Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_Nilsine_Shatter-Shield.md) during the quest [Mourning Never Comes](Skyrim_Mourning_Never_Comes.md). In appearance, the ring is identical to a [silver ring](Skyrim_Silver_Ring.md). Its enchantment [fortifies alchemy](Skyrim_Fortify_Alchemy.md) by 15 points, making your created potions and poisons more powerful.

## Related Quests
- **[Mourning Never Comes](Skyrim_Mourning_Never_Comes.md)**: Help [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md) get her revenge.

## Notes
- If you have killed [Alain Dufont](Skyrim_Alain_Dufont.md) before starting the related quest, Muiri will never ask you to kill Nilsine and you are blocked from ever receiving this ring.
- Another, unobtainable version of Muiri's Ring (base ID `0006b219`) exists in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit). It is identical to the obtainable copy, but doesn't have an enchantment.
