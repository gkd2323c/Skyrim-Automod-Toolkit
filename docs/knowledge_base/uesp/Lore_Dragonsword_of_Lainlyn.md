# Dragonsword of Lainlyn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/OBR-item-Dragonsword_of_Lainlyn.jpg/200px-OBR-item-Dragonsword_of_Lainlyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Dragonsword_of_Lainlyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-item-Dragonsword_of_Lainlyn.jpg) The Dragonsword of Lainlyn (Oblivion Remaster) The Dragonsword of Lainlyn is a unique sword named for the city of [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> It is enchanted to deal fire damage to targets and effect them with fatigue. It features of a design of two snakes making up the hilt.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OB-1)</sup> It was wielded by [Lord Kain](Lore_Kain.md) of the [Knights of the True Horn](Lore_Knights_of_the_True_Horn.md).
