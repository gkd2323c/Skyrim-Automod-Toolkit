**Uncle Leo** is an undead plaything of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). His true origins are unknown, as he is associated with several different tales which involve his gruesome end at the hands of the Mad God.

## History

### Possible Origins
According to one story recounted by Sheogorath himself, in life Leo was a handsome and promiscuous mortal man who had a girl in every city from [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) to [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md). However, his looks faded with age, and he grew insane from lack of female attention. Sheogorath offered to restore Leo's youth, but in return he was forbidden from bedding the same woman twice. Leo agreed, but a few years later fell in love with a woman named Mjoll. He assumed that his contract with the Mad God would be forfeit if he married; instead, his skin melted off like wax on his wedding night and he became Sheogorath's.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

Another tale involves a pair of silver-framed [amber](Lore_Amber.md) spectacles, which Sheogorath gave to a sage who wanted to understand the absolute truth of the world around him. However, upon seeing the world through the spectacles, the sage flung himself from the highest tower in [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md). Although cracked from the fall, this dangerous relic became known as Uncle Leo's Spectacles and continued to plague the mortals of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMadness-2)</sup>

### Undeath
After his death, Sheogorath brought Leo to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), where he reformed as a gigantic [Flesh Atronach](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md). He became a guest in the Chateau of the Ravenous Rodent in [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md). Leo began to fall in love with the [rats](Lore_Rat.md) there, which Sheogorath regarded as an embarrassment. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Mad God sent a member of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) to kill the atronach for his own amusement.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> That same year, the Guild's [Order of the Eye](Lore_Order_of_the_Eye.md) tracked down and recovered Uncle Leo's Spectacles from the Bonesnap Ruins in [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md) after the relic mysteriously appeared on Tamriel due to the ongoing [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOMadness-2)</sup> Uncle Leo is also associated with another grisly artifact known as Uncle Leo's Choker, a foul-smelling discoloured leather choker which appears to be made from human skin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Sheogorath had resurrected Uncle Leo as a decaying [zombie](Lore_Zombie.md). He lived in [Crucible](Lore_Crucible.md) with an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) madwoman named Muurine. He was seemingly able to communicate with Muurine, and was very concerned by the death of the first [Gatekeeper of the Fringe](Lore_Gatekeeper_of_the_Fringe.md). That year, Leo's arm fell off and Muurine was executed for treason against Lady [Syl](Lore_Syl.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
