## Detailed Walkthrough
**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](Skyrim_Saturalia_Holiday_Pack.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), this quest no longer appears in your journal. Nevertheless, the rewards can still be bought from [Agrane Peryval](Skyrim_Agrane_Peryval.md).**

This short quest will be added to your journal upon installing the [Saturalia Holiday Pack](Skyrim_Saturalia_Holiday_Pack.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). You will be directed to locate a merchant who has set up shop near [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) in advance of [Saturalia](Lore_Saturalia.md), a festival which takes place on the 25<sup>th</sup> of [Evening Star](Lore_Evening_Star.md). Note that the in-game time of year is irrelevant for the purposes of this quest.

The merchant's camp can be found just outside Dawnstar, north of the [Windward Ruins](Skyrim_Windward_Ruins.md). It is located close to a [World Interaction](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) spawn point which may prove problematic. The merchant is [Agrane Peryval](Skyrim_Agrane_Peryval.md), who can be found at the camp along with a tame [reindeer](Skyrim_Reindeer.md). Simply speak to Agrane to finish the quest; you are under no obligation to buy anything from him.

If you do choose to make a purchase, his stock includes several unique items: three sets of festive clothing ([Saturalia Boots](Skyrim_Saturalia_Boots.md), [Saturalia Clothes](Skyrim_Saturalia_Clothes.md), [Saturalia Gloves](Skyrim_Saturalia_Gloves.md)) and a [bill of sale](Skyrim_Reindeer_Bill_of_Sale.md) for the nearby reindeer. Once purchased, the reindeer can be used as a mount, and will return to this camp if you switch to another mount.
