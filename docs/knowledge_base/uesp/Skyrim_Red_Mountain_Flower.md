[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-flora-Mountain_Flower.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Mountain_Flower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Mountain_Flower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Mountain_Flower.jpg) The red variety of the mountain flower plant **Red Mountain Flower** is harvested from the red-flowered variety of mountain flower. Other varieties of mountain flower yield [blue mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mountain_Flower), [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower), and [yellow mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yellow_Mountain_Flower)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, but the flowers have different alchemical properties. The blue, purple, and red mountain flowers are commonplace in the foothills and lower elevations of mountains (below the snowline).

It is one of three (five with the [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)) ingredients that contain both [Restore Magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Magicka) and [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) effects, but the other two ([briar heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Briar_Heart) and [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm)) are much less common than mountain flowers.

## Ingredients
98 guaranteed samples can be found in 45 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 16 centered 350 ft E of the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=38750&y=112980)
- 16 in [Yngvild Throne Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngvild_Throne_Room) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 6 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).
