[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Sayma.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sayma.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sayma.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sayma.jpg) Sayma **Sayma** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) general goods [merchant](Skyrim_Merchants.md) who runs [Bits and Pieces](Skyrim_Bits_and_Pieces.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). She is married to [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md) and they have one son, [Kayd](Skyrim_Kayd.md).

Sayma lives the life of a busy merchant. She wakes up at 8am, and will open up the shop for business. She spends most of her time behind the counter ready to deal with potential customers, but will sometimes move to the nearby chair for a quick bite or drink. After twelve hours of dutifully serving customers, she will leave the store and head toward the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md). She will stand outside the entrance to the Bards College for two hours, usually in the company of [Illdi](Skyrim_Illdi.md) and [Greta](Skyrim_Greta.md). She will head home at 10pm, where she will spend two more hours wandering around the house and occasionally talking with her husband about the dwindling amount of business at the store. She will then head to bed at midnight, where she will sleep for eight hours.

Sayma wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0003452e) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f). She carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bits_and_Pieces) to the store and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). In combat, she relies on an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"Welcome. Look around. We've got everything you could want in life. At least... that's what Beirand always tells me."* *"Welcome to Bits and Pieces. I'm sure you'll find what you need here."* *"Hmph. You know, you've got a certain... fire in your eyes. Just like my Beirand. Please, feel free to look around. We've got a bit of everything."* *"Beirand said you got here just before the execution. Must have been a lovely first sight."* Speaking to her:

**Were you at Roggvir's execution?** *"No. That was an ugly affair. [High King Torygg](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md)'s death has turned the whole town on its head. And between you and me? He wasn't even that good a king. All of those rambling speeches about the Empire this, and the Empire that.."* **Who is Beirand?** *"Beirand's my husband. He works the forge up at [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). I barely see him nowadays, what with him filling orders for the [army](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)."* After finishing a transaction:

*"Come back to Bits and Pieces anytime."* *"Make sure to recommend us to your associates."*
### Conversations
She can also be found having a discussion with either Beirand or Kayd when they are both inside the Bits and Pieces store:

**Sayma:** *"Sure has been quiet around the shop lately."* or *"We hardly ever get any new customers anymore."*, then *"I suppose in [wartime](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), people aren't looking to part with their old junk."* or *"Not that I mind the break of course, but still."*
**Beirand:** *"It's okay, dear. More work than ever down at the blacksmith."* or *"We'll make it through, dear. We always do."*
or **Kayd:** *"Who needs customers and their old junk?"* or *"Father oughtta punch them in the mouth! That'd get people in here!"*

## Notes
- Her dialogue will be slightly different if Beirand is not alive.

## Bugs
- Sayma repeats her greeting dialogue about you being there for Roggvir's execution due to the greeting not having the Say Once flag checked. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- Due to there only being two beds in Bits and Pieces, Sayma may not be able to sleep if Beirand and Kayd beat her to bed. She will wander around the house all night long if no beds are available. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. One of the single beds is replaced by a double bed.
