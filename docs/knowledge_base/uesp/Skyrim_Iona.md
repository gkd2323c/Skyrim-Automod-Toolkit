[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-npc-Iona.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Iona.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Iona.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Iona.jpg) Iona **Iona** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who is assigned to you as a [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) when you become a [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). She does not exist until this happens and she is available to [follow](Skyrim_Followers.md) you as soon as she does.

Her default equipment is [steel cuffed boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Cuffed_Boots), [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor), and [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets). She carries a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield) and a [steel war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_War_Axe).

She is a potential [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) partner and [Blades recruit](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md).

## Bugs
- Although supposedly protected like most followers, it seems that Iona can die in regular combat, without being hit by the player.

- If you attack one of the chickens outside of [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md), Iona will be hostile when you enter.

- Iona can sometimes vanish from [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) and be disabled. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, use the command `prid a2c93` to select Iona, followed by `enable` to enable her again. If that doesn't fix the problem she might have died, which can be remedied with the command `resurrect 1`.
