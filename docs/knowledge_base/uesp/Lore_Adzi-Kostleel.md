# Adzi-Kostleel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) Black Marsh, the homeland of Adzi-Kostleel The Adzi-Kostleel are one of the many tribes of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). They are known for their oral history, surrounding the creation of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). Their version of the [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) creation story was written down by [Solis Aduro](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Solis_Aduro), titled *[Children of the Root](Lore_Children_of_the_Root.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTR-1)</sup>

Records of their existence date back to before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). By that time, the text written by Solis Aduro was already available to the public. The creation story of Adzi-Kostleel documented by Solis Aduro, gathered from their oral tradition, is not corroborated by any other known sources.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTR-1)</sup>

According to this creation story, their religion included deities like [Atak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atak), [Kota](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kota), and [Atakota](Lore_Atakota.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-COTR-1)</sup> The Shadow of Atakota is also acknowledged by at least some of the [Nisswo](Lore_Nisswo.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Walks-Under-Shadow-2)</sup>
