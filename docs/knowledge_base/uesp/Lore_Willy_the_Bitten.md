Willy the Bitten was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) who was transformed into a monster-mer in the [fourth century of the First Era](Lore_First_Era.md#Fourth_Century) by the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup>

The Wild Hunt that Willy was a part of came about during the reign of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) High King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md), the last member of the [Ysgramor Dynasty](Lore_Ysgramor_Dynasty.md). Borgas was a staunch supporter of the Prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md) but a conflict was growing between the Empire and the [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md). Borgas set off for [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to urge a joint war with Skyrim against the Bosmer. Before he could get there however, he was slaughtered at the hands of [King Dead Wolf-Deer](Lore_Dead_Wolf-Deer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3V-2)</sup> What specific role Willy the Bitten played in the campaign against Borgas is unknown.

Sometime after the defeat of Borgas, Willy the Bitten returned to [Silvenar Grove](Lore_Silvenar_Vale.md) to haunt over the land and was still believed to be there as of [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-1)</sup>
