Vasi Hadrach was the Admiral of the [New West Navy](Lore_Imperial_Navy.md) during the ninth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and one of the subordinates of [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md). He was assassinated by a rebel [Reachman](Lore_Reachman.md) in a brothel of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md) at some point before [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), when the title of the Admiral of the New West Navy was already held by his successor, [Amiel Richton](Lore_Amiel_Richton.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Richton-1)</sup>
