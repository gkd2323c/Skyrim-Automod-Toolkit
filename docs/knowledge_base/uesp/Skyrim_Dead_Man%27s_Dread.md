## Overview
This Creation adds the quest **[The Restless](Skyrim_The_Restless.md)**, which will lead you to a subterranean [ship](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place).md) on an [island](Skyrim_Blackbone_Isle.md) in the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). Within this haunted wreck you will find relics of [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md), the protagonist of *[Redguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Redguard:Redguard)*. After you have cleared the island of its undead inhabitants, you can claim the ship as your [player home](Skyrim_Houses.md). It comes fully furnished with [crafting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crafting) stations, a mannequin, and plenty of storage.

This Creation also adds the [Blackwater Blade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blackwater_Blade), an artifact that previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Blackwater_Blade) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
