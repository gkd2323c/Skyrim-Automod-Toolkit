# Bloodwraith Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/ON-npc-Shadow_Knight.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Shadow_Knight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shadow_Knight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Shadow_Knight.jpg) The Shadow Knight and Dark Mage wearing Boethiah's banner The Bloodwraith Clan (or **Bloodwraiths**) are a clan of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) in service to [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md). Bloodwraiths devote themselves to the purpose of challenging and testing champions in great tournaments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ODC-1)</sup> They fight in the [Dragonstar Arena](Lore_Dragonstar_Arena.md), where they sacrifice themselves and their mortal victims to honor the Prince.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DA-2)</sup> They are led by two powerful Dremora known only as the Shadow Knight and the Dark Mage, who lead the clan in never-ending arena contests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ODC-1)</sup>
