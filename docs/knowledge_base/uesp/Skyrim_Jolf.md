[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-npc-Jolf_%28ferryman%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Jolf_%28ferryman%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Jolf_(ferryman).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Jolf_(ferryman).jpg.md) Jolf **Jolf** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and the [ferryman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ferryman) for [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1da), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd), and carries an [iron war axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_War_Axe). You can hire him to sail you to [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) or [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) for 50 gold, or [Icewater Jetty](Skyrim_Icewater_Jetty.md) for 500 gold.
