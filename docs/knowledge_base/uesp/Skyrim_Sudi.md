[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Sudi.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sudi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sudi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sudi.jpg) Sudi **Sudi** was a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) whose family got more than they bargained for when they bought [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md). When she started hearing strange scratching noises from the basement her father [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md) left for town to buy traps believing they had a [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) infestation. It turned out to be far worse though as [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) and [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) tunneled in through the cellar from [Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss_(place).md). They killed her mother [Ramati](Skyrim_Ramati.md), dragging her brother [Mani](Skyrim_Mani.md) and herself into their lair. The Falmer soon killed Mani, while keeping her captive to feed their brooding [chaurus reaper](Skyrim_Chaurus_Reaper.md).

After returning home, Habd chased after his children and was captured as well after succumbing to a poisonous chaurus bite. His final fatherly act was to give his daughter a [dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) before being dragged away to be eaten. Sudi took the dagger as a means to escape and killed herself before she too could be eaten alive. Her corpse can be found still behind bars and lying atop a [scrawled page](Skyrim_Scrawled_page.md) and a [bloodstained note](Skyrim_Bloodstained_note.md). She can be found wearing some farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). Her [journal](Skyrim_Sudi%27s_Journal.md) can be found in her and Mani's room, detailing her life prior to the massacre.

## Related Quests
- **[Frostflow Abyss](Skyrim_Frostflow_Abyss.md)**: Uncover the tragic events inside [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md).

## Notes
- Sudi does not have a unique facial model, she instead uses the default female Redguard face. She shares this quirk with her mother, [Ramati](Skyrim_Ramati.md), oddly making them look identical.
