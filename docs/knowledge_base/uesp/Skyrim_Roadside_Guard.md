[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-npc-Roadside_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Roadside_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Roadside_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Roadside_Guard.jpg) The Roadside Guard The **Roadside Guard** will appear in the game only if [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md) is under [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) control. This may be because you are aligned with the Imperial Legion, and captured it during the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) questline, or it may be transferred to the Legion during the negotiations in [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md). The Roadside Guard will then replace the [Pale Hold Guard](Skyrim_Pale_Hold_Guard.md), and will perpetually patrol the road near [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md). If you have a bounty in the Pale, then he will arrest you like any other guard.

He wears a set of [Imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md) and wields an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and [bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) in combat. He also carries a set of [common clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md) and some [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Delayed Burial](Skyrim_Delayed_Burial.md)**: Convince an unwilling [farmer](Skyrim_Vantus_Loreius.md) to help fix a stranded [jester's](Skyrim_Cicero.md) broken wagon wheel.

## Dialogue
If you approach him before starting the related quest, or after it has been completed, he can be found saying:

- *"You need something?"*
- **No, nothing. I'll just be moving on.**
- *"Well all right, then."*
