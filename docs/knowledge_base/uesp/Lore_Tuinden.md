**Kinlady** Tuinden was the queen consort of King [Hidellith](Lore_Hidellith.md) of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md). She was the mother of Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) and Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIEG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LCL-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/ON-place-Glister_Vale.jpg/200px-ON-place-Glister_Vale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Glister_Vale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Glister_Vale.jpg) Glister Vale Little is known about Tuinden's life before her marriage to King Hidellith. She was a Kinlady, an Altmeri noblewoman. In due time, as ordained by the Scrolls, Tuinden and Hidellith conceived a child. She gave birth to her daughter, whom they named Ayrenn as the Praxis dictated. She gave birth on the 5th of the Second Seed in the year [2E 555](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_555), a date considered very auspicious. She later gave birth to her eldest son, Naemon, and at least one more son.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup>

Following the death of Hidellith in [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580), Ayrenn, who had disappeared years earlier, returned to the Summerset Isles and arrived in Firsthold. Ayrenn greeted Tuinden before the ceremony celebrating her return in [Firsthold](Lore_Firsthold.md), and she took part in the festivities. The return of Ayrenn preceded her coronation as Queen of Alinor on the 7th of Frost Fall, 2E 580.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIEG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATUQ-2)</sup>

Tuinden and her immediate family owned lands in Glister Vale in central [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). The Tower of the Vale served as their summer home, although following the death of Hidellith, it fell into disuse and disrepair.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GV-4)</sup>
