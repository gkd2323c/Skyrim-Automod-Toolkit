[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/ON-map-Calambar.jpg/200px-ON-map-Calambar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Calambar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Calambar.jpg) Map of Calambar circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). **Calambar** is the central region of the island of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), located in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Calambar is the home of the city-state of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md), which overlooks the [Blue Divide](Lore_Blue_Divide.md) to the east. Several smaller islands inhabit the region including [Errinorne Isle](Lore_Errinorne_Isle.md) and [Buraniim Isle](Lore_Buraniim_Isle.md). The town of [Mathiisen](Lore_Mathiisen.md) is located south of Skywatch and is rather close to the mountains near the region of [Iluvamir](Lore_Iluvamir.md). The ruins of Quendeluun and Ezduiin are also located here.
