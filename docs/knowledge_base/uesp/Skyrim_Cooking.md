# Cooking


All of the recipes listed below are available at the beginning of the game, even if their associated ingredients are not. For recipe information related to baking, see [this page](Skyrim_Baking.md).

Recipes added by [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md), [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md), and [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) are annotated with "DB", "HF", and "CC" superscripts, respectively.

## Notes
- The animation of using a cooking pot features whoever's in charge stirring the pot with a wooden spoon and occasionally tapping the spoon on the sides of the pot. The animation of using a cooking spit also features this, as well as whoever's in charge either basting the meat with the liquid in the pot via the wooden spoon or rotating the meat around.
- The [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md), [Bloodchill Manor](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md) and [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md) [Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) add a smelting pot, which is stirred like a cooking pot but is in fact a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md).
- If a cooking station is higher off the ground than normal, you can still use it, though you'll hover an inch or two off the ground while cooking.
- If you create any of the items from this list, the Potions Created statistic is increased by 1 each time.
