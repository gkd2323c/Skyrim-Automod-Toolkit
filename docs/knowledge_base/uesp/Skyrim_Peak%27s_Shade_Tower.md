[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-place-Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Peak%27s_Shade_Tower.jpg) Peak's Shade Tower **Peak's Shade Tower** is a small ruined [Nordic tower](Skyrim_Nordic_Towers.md) east of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) containing a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md).

## Related Quests
- This location is one of many potential targets for [one or more of the Radiant quests](Skyrim_Radiant.md#Quest_Locations).

## Walkthrough
Following the main road east out of Falkreath toward [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md), the path leading up to the tower is marked by a cairn on the right side of the road. Three [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), one [purple mountain flower](Skyrim_Purple_Mountain_Flower.md), and a patch of [mora tapinella](Skyrim_Mora_Tapinella.md) fungus on a large tree stump are found just outside the entrance.

The tower has long eroded, and the only inhabitant is a spriggan. The interior is overgrown with vegetation, including two [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), one purple mountain flower, and two [thistles](Skyrim_Thistle.md). It is also a good place to find [blue butterflies](Skyrim_Blue_Butterfly.md) and [monarch butterflies](Skyrim_Monarch_Butterfly.md) during the day, and a few [luna moths](Skyrim_Luna_Moth.md) or [torchbugs](Skyrim_Torchbug.md) at night. Hidden among the vegetation along the west wall of the tower is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md).

## Notes
- With *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* installed, the spriggan may be an Earth Mother, depending on what level you approach the ruin.
