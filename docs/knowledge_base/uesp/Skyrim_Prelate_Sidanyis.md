He wears [monk robes](Skyrim_Monk_Robes.md#xx01570c) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#xx01570b). He carries an [elven dagger](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Touching the Sky
He will greet you with:

- *"Welcome, Initiate. This is the Wayshrine of Illumination. Are you prepared to honor the mantras of Auri-El and fill your vessel with His enlightenment?"*
- **Yes.**
- *"Then behold Auri-El's gift, my child. May it light your path as you seek tranquility within the Inner Sanctum."*

When approached, he will bless you with:

- *"May Auri-El's brilliance illuminate your path."*

## Notes
- Sidanyis is a member of the [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora_(class).md) class, which determines the level of his skills.
