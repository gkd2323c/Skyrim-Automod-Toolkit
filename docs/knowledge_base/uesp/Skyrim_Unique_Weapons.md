# Unique Weapons

**Unique Weapons** are weapons available in Skyrim generally found in fixed locations, unlike most equipment in the game, which are randomly generated or generic equipment (see [Generic Magic Weapons](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md)). Some of these weapons can be improved through [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md). Improving enchanted weapons requires the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md) article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md) article. For a list of the other available weapons, see the [weapon category](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Weapons).

The arrows are visually similar to the elven arrows that are needed to create them, with a gray shaft instead of a golden one, and the fletchings alternating between gray and white. The quiver is similar, being a more pallid color compared to the strong golden one of normal arrows. The arrowhead, however, is a bright, glowing gold; not the standard gold of regular elven arrows. The effect isn't dependent on the bow's enchantment, so it can be used even if the enchantment is empty, though the arrows are destroyed by the attack, so it isn't possible to retrieve the arrows from bodies or missed shots in the environment. If you use the arrows with other bows, there are no special effects and they behave like regular arrows. This is the same if you give sunhallowed arrows to followers.

## Notes
- This is the only weapon in the game that grants you the [Fiery Soul Trap](Skyrim_Fiery_Soul_Trap.md) effect when disenchanted.</noinclude>
- Since Fiery Soul Trap's [effect charge and strength](Skyrim_Enchanting_Effects.md) are keyed to the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill instead of [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill, Fiery Soul Trap can be used to enchant a weapon with a long-lasting 10pt Fire Damage effect irrespective of Destruction skill level.
- If Warlord Gathrik uses [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md) on [Salma](Skyrim_Salma.md), that may cause her to pick up the Battleaxe. If she does, you'll either have to pickpocket the weapon from her, or kill her and loot it from her corpse.

## Bugs
- Sometimes, the Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls will be hovering in the air. This occurs when you are of a high enough level for Warlord Gathrik to be a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md). When he gets off the throne, it will be thrown into space. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- Shooting the axe with an arrow or spell should cause it to fall to the ground.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using the console command `player.additem 40002 1` will place a copy of the battleaxe in your inventory.

You can also get him to equip a planted melee weapon by leading a hostile encounter back towards him so that he enters the fight against your enemies without turning hostile toward you. Alternatively, if you plant a melee weapon on him, he may equip it on his own if you leave the area and return on a different night.
