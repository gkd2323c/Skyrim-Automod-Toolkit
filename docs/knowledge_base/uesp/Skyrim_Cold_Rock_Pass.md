[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-interior-Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) Cold Rock Pass **Cold Rock Pass** is a small [passage](Skyrim_Caves.md) through a mountain south-southwest of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) containing a [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md).

Although the interior of the ruin is named "Cold Rock Pass", the map markers for the two entrances are named "North Cold Rock Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=North+Cold+Rock+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true) and "South Cold Rock Pass" [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=South+Cold+Rock+Pass&zoom=14&legacy=true), counting as two separate locations for the "Locations Discovered" statistic. The northern entrance is south of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), and the southern entrance is northeast of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md).

## Walkthrough

### Exterior: North Cold Rock Pass
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-North_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) North Cold Rock Pass The northern entrance is found by following the path southwest of Morthal toward [Eldersblood Peak](Skyrim_Eldersblood_Peak.md); when you pass a banner attached to a post on your left, the entrance is on the next switchback to the left. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is along the path near the entrance, and several [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes grow in the vicinity.

### Exterior: South Cold Rock Pass
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg/200px-SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-South_Cold_Rock_Pass.jpg) South Cold Rock Pass The southern entrance is found by ascending the stone stairs leading north to the left of the entrance of [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md). A dead [skeever](Skyrim_Skeever.md) is next to the entrance. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is down the hill to the entrance.

### Cold Rock Pass
Upon entering via the nothern entrance, you enter a natural cave heading down and to the west. Ahead is a wooden crate near a lootable skeleton and a sack of [food](Skyrim_Food.md). To the south, to the left of an earthen ramp leading up is a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md). At the top of the ramp the cave opens up in front of you, with a couple rock pillars supporting the ceiling and a frozen pond of water being fed by a small waterfall. Southwest of the waterfall on a higher ledge is a frost troll. The ramp leading up to it is along the northwest wall of the cave.

The ledge has a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein) on the southwest wall of the cave. Among the bones and blood splatters on the ground are some [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Weapons.md) or [steel weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons) and a dead skeever or two. There may be the body of a [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) by the edge, although this may have fallen off and be lying on the ice below. Near the frost troll is a dead [elk](Skyrim_Elk.md) and an open chest holding a set of random armor, a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), and two [potions of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). To the south, below the ledge is a large shallow pond that has mostly frozen over, with the path through the cave beside it. Against the east wall, opposite the waterfall is a food barrel next to an unlocked chest. At the southern end of the pond is a second corundum ore vein, with a ramp up to its left leading past a cart containing a food barrel, a second barrel beside it, and a second lootable skeleton, ending at the southern entrance.

## Notes
- Although the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* places this location in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), the game data states it is in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.1, addresses this issue. The pass has been set to be part of Hjaalmarch instead.

- Technically, the pass itself is a transition between the two holds, with its southern entrance in Whiterun Hold and its northern entrance in Hjaalmarch, so either could be said to be applicable.
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces all [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bushes outside North Cold Rock Pass with the snow-covered version of the same plant.
- In [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md), this dungeon will not warm you up, unlike most interior locations.
