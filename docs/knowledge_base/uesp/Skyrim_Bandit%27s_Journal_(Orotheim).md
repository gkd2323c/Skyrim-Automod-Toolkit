Bandit's Journal *A bandit's complaints about their inability to kill a mammoth* *

Damn those [Giants](Skyrim_Giant.md). Just one [Mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) would feed us for a month, but they herd them around like prized cattle, never taking an eye off them. We've hunted this area dry. Unless we get a kill soon, we'll have to find somewhere else to camp.
