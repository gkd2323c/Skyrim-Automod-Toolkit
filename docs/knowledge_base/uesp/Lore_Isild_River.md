[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Isild_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Isild_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isild_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Isild_River.jpg) Isild River circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) The Isild River is a river in northern [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md) on the [Felsaad Coast](Lore_Felsaad_Coast.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>

Before the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md) of [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> the Isild River ran northwest from the frozen [Lake Fjalding](Lore_Lake_Fjalding.md) until it merged with the [Harstrad River](Lore_Harstrad_River.md) at the foot of the [Moesring Mountains](Lore_Moesring_Mountains.md). The river then continued to its mouth on the icy Felsaad Coast, west of [Skaal Village](Lore_Skaal_Village.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Bloodmoon-1)</sup> It was reportedly a beautiful part of the island, at the right time of year.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ENTB-4)</sup>

The cataclysms of the Red Year substantially changed the landscape of Solstheim.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HORR-3)</sup> Many of the landmarks remained the same; the river is still west of Skaal Village and flows from Lake Fjalding to the coast. However, the Isild and Harstrad rivers no longer meet. Instead, each empties separately into a small bay on the northern coast.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Dragonborn-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The name *isild* comes from Danish and Norwegian words *is* meaning *ice* and *ild* meaning *fire*, translating it to *Icefire River*.
