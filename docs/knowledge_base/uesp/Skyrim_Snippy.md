[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-creature-Snippy.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Snippy.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snippy.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Snippy.jpg) Snippy **Snippy** is a unique [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) being raised by [Viriya](Skyrim_Viriya.md) in her attempts to farm mudcrabs. It grows from the [juvenile mudcrab](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juvenile_Mudcrab) you bring her during the quest [In A Pinch](Skyrim_In_A_Pinch.md) and will be found following her around [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) docks the next time you visit her. Snippy will grow when you complete [Clear-Headed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clear-Headed) and reach full size when you complete [Private Lessons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Private_Lessons).

## Notes
- Snippy is technically three separate creatures, depending on its current size. - The youngest version has base ID: `xx033A45` and ref ID: `xx033A4B`.
- The second version has base ID: `xx033A46` and ref ID: `xx033A4C`.
- The oldest version has base ID: `xx033A47` and ref ID: `xx033A4D`.

## Bugs
- None of the three versions of Snippy are revealed by [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md).
