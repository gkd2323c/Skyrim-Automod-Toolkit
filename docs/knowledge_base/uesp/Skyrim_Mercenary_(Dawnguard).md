He wears leveled [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor), including boots and gauntlets. He carries a leveled shield, an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a leveled one-handed weapon, a leveled [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), 20 leveled [arrows](Skyrim_Arrow.md), and some gold.

If you come near Taron Dreth, the mercenaries will be irritated, and give you one of three warnings:

- *"Out of our way."*
- *"You need anything, talk to Master Dreth."*
- *"Stand aside."*

## Notes
- The Nord mercenary does not have unique facial data. Instead, he uses the default male Nord face, albeit with different hair. The Breton also has a default face for his race, although again with a beard and altered hair.

## Bugs
- Taron and the mercenaries may not become hostile after mentioning Katria to Taron Dreth. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Taron's mercenaries may attack prior to dialogue finishing, which can make engaging the fight difficult. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
