## Official Summary
*Includes two pieces of heavy Daedric Plate armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Beyond the Grave" starts by reading Death of a Crimson Dirk in Dragonsreach Dungeon in Whiterun.)*
