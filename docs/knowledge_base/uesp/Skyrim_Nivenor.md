[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-npc-Nivenor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nivenor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nivenor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nivenor.jpg) Nivenor **Nivenor** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who lives in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). Wife to the charitable [Bolli](Skyrim_Bolli.md), she finds his passion for giving silly and pointless. She instead prefers to use their wealth for luxuries.

According to a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Requested_Report) found in [their home](Skyrim_Bolli%27s_House.md), Nivenor appears to be cheating on her husband with multiple individuals. If you play as a male character, this is further confirmed in her comments directed toward you during encounters where she offers you her company. Bolli himself has engaged in extramarital affairs with [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md).

Nivenor lives a fairly relaxed life, spending much of her time relaxing at home or shopping. She wakes up at 6am and has a one-hour breakfast inside the house. She will then wander around the house for the next four hours, occasionally sitting down, having a quick bite to eat or drink, or using the [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) inside the house. She leaves the house at 11am to head to the market plaza in the center of Riften. She will spend the next four hours here, browsing the various stalls in search of new luxuries to purchase. At 3pm, she leaves the plaza and will make her way to the [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md). She can be found there for the next four hours, sitting in a chair in her office doing paperwork. After a fairly short and perhaps not too difficult work day, she will head back home at 7pm. She will relax inside the house for the remainder of the evening, until she heads to bed at midnight.

Nivenor wears a set of [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#00086991) along with a matching pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Boots#00086993). She carries her [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bolli%27s_House_Key) to the house, a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Fishery_Key) to the Riften Fishery, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), and a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich). In combat, she relies on a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) that can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality. During the related quest, she is also in possession of [Nivenor's Strongbox Key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nivenor%27s_Strongbox_Key)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Gray_Cowl_Returns!.md)</sup>.

*"If you're looking for a handout, you'll want to speak with my husband."* **(If you are female.)** *"If you're looking for a handout, you'll want to speak with my husband. If you're looking for company, I suppose I could do worse."* **(If you are male.)** *"Bolli's around somewhere, planning to feed the poor with his nonsense about Mara."* *"With my husband's new found religion, what I do with our coin and what I do all day seem less important to him. Suits me."* *"At this rate, he'll give the entire Riften Fishery away to charity. Then how will I live?"* *"Such a waste of time preaching to the poor. If they're that hungry, they should just grow some food or something."* *"I spend every Friday * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * at the fishery, so I know the meaning of hard work. All those forms to fill out and file; it's exhausting!"* *"Mark my words. *"If you're looking for a handout, you're not going to get one from me."* *"Can you believe my former husband used to give most of our gold to the poor?"* When exiting dialogue she'll simply say:

*"Excuse me."*

## Notes
- In one of her greetings, Nivenor uses the term "Friday". This is the real-world day, the Elder Scrolls equivalent of which being "Fredas". It also doesn't line up with her actual schedule, as she spends every day inside of the fishery, not just Friday/Fredas.
