[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Ravyn_Imyan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ravyn_Imyan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ravyn_Imyan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ravyn_Imyan.jpg) Ravyn Imyan **Ravyn Imyan** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) who used to be a [Morag Tong](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Morag_Tong) assassin, and will join the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) once you have completed at least three of the [special reputation quests](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Special_Job_Quests).

Although he is no longer an assassin, Ravyn likes to keep his skills honed. He practices his archery 6pm to 10pm in the Cistern and from 10pm to 2am in the training room. He wanders around the Cistern for another two hours before taking a nap between 4am and 8am in the bed next to the [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) statue. He will spend the rest of the day strolling around and chatting to his fellow guildmates, taking a two-hour break for lunch at 2pm.

He wears the standard [Thieves Guild armor](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Armor_Alt), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Gauntlets_Alt), and [boots](Skyrim_Specialty_Gear.md#Thieves_Guild_Boots_Alt). He is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality), a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) and twelve [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). He also carries two [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and up to 27 gold coins.

## Related Quests
- **[The Pursuit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pursuit)**: Break into [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md)'s [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riftweald_Manor) and search for clues.
- **[Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md)**: Become the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Pursuit
When you arrive accompanied by [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md), he'll seethe:

*"Vex is ready to tear you apart. I hope you have a good excuse for bringing Karliah here."* Once you've cleared her name, however, he'll be enraged:

*"Punishment's too good for [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md). The only penalty for his betrayal is death."*
### Under New Management
Before speaking to [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md):

*"Brynjolf's been looking for you about the Guild Master ceremony."* If spoken to during the ceremony:

*"Shouldn't the ceremony be completed first?"*
## Dialogue
When first met, he'll greet you with:

*"Hmph, so you're the one everyone's falling over."* *"I don't know, you don't look so impressive to me. I mean, you're no [Gray Fox](Lore_Gray_Fox.md), but you must be doing something right."* After that he'll be quite abrasive:

*"I'm busy, is this important?"* *"Are you bothering me for a reason?"* *"Oh, this should be good."* When you stop talking to him, he'll remark:

*"Finally. Now I can get back to work."* *"Don't hurry back, I won't be waiting."* You can ask him about his past:

**So, where do you hail from?** *"[Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) of course. Any Dunmer worth their weight in ash would tell you the same.
I used to be a member of the Morang * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Tong."* **Who are the Morang *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* Tong?** *"We were an assassins guild that was sanctioned by the Empire to provide public as well as private executions. We did this in the name of [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), whom I used to quite fervently worship."* **Was it anything like the [Dark Brotherhood?](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md)** *"No. In fact, the Tong were quite the bitter rivals with the Dark Brotherhood. Where they operate within the shadows, we operated on the side of justice."* **Why didn't you stay with the Tong?** *"I didn't have a choice. When the Red Mountain erupted in Morrowind, the Tong fled... spread to the nine winds. We made a pact that one day, we might reunite... but I believe that day is long in coming."* **Perhaps you should have joined the Dark Brotherhood.** *"Have you taken leave of your senses? If the Dark Brotherhood discovered I had been a Morang * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Tong, they would come after me with a vengeance. Better to stay out of sight and ply my trade with the Thieves Guild than risk a contract on my head."* **Well, your secret is safe with me.** *"I should hope so. The Dark Brotherhood is also known to place contracts on those who associate with the Morang * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Tong as well. So, unless you wish a visit from one of their own, I'd suggest you follow your own advice and keep your mouth shut."* Once you've become Guild Master, he'll temper down:

*"I promise to live up to your expectations."* If you get kicked out, he'll refuse to speak with you:

*"I may be new around here, but at least I know how to follow the rules."*
## Notes
- In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, the summary for him reads *"Ravyn was a member of the Morag Tong (Morrowind's assassin's guild and bitter rivals of the Dark Brotherhood) and left it when the Red Mountain erupted and the great evacuation of Morrowind occurred. He is in the Guild biding his time until the Tong one day re-forms."*
- If you are a member of the Dark Brotherhood, your dialogue with Ravyn does not get altered to reflect this.
