# Passion Dancer Blossom

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/ON-prerelease-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) Passion Dancer Blossom [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) Passion Dancer Blossom Passion Dancer Blossom, also known as **Ardor's Flowering** is a floating teal-colored flower associated with [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md), known to thrive in the presence of her passionate followers. Some believe that if one surrenders to Dibella's passion and dances in her chosen way, she may reward them with the gift of one of her vibrant blooms.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PDB-1)</sup> These flowers were among the many otherworldly items in the stock of [the Impresario](Lore_The_Impresario.md), an eccentric traveling merchant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TI-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LATH-3)</sup> To conjure one, it was necessary to use the softly glowing petals of a mystical Chartreuse lily,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CL-4)</sup> an enchanted silver flute to summon benevolent [spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESF-5)</sup> and the music of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), transcribed in a form comprehensible to mortal minds.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MSM-6)</sup>
