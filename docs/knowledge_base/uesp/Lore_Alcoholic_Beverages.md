[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/ON-render-Drinking_Nord.jpg/200px-ON-render-Drinking_Nord.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Drinking_Nord.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Drinking_Nord.jpg) Nords drinking in a tavern [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/ON-render-Breton_Tavern.jpg/200px-ON-render-Breton_Tavern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Breton_Tavern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Breton_Tavern.jpg) A drunk Breton **Alcoholic beverages** are popular across Tamriel.

## Culture
The [Brewers Guild](Lore_Brewers_Guild.md) and the [Vintners Guild](Lore_Vintners_Guild.md) are both officially sanctioned guilds that specialize in both beer and wine respectively. They were given approval by the Akaviri-Potentate, [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md), when he signed the [Guild Act](Lore_Guild_Act.md) in [2E 321](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_321).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOTFG-1)</sup> The town of [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) is famous for its wines and vineyards.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

[Saint Seryn the Merciful](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Saint_Seryn_the_Merciful) is the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) patron saint of brewers and distillers.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOTS-3)</sup>

The ancient [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) kingdom of [Meirvale](Lore_Meirvale.md) in central [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) was noted for its breweries and distilleries.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSK-4)</sup> Those that remained after the kingdom's downfall now live in the Stitches and are known for drowning their sorrow in drink.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONLoad Meirvale-5)</sup>

The [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md)' natural resistance to poison also makes them resistant to the effects of alcohol, needing more cups than other races for the effects to kick in.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) have a high tolerance to alcohol but take longer to recover.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ARAlcoholic-7)</sup>

It is believed that certain cultures on Nirn make alcoholic products from [cacti](Lore_Cactus.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MZP-8)</sup>

### Sun's Dusk Ale
**Sun's Dusk Ale**, also known as **Dark Meat Beer**, is a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) Ale made from [crosswings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Crosswing), [mimics](Lore_Mimic.md), and green mold. It smells like "juicy steak marinated with plum wine and coriander". [Imga](Lore_Imga.md) dislike the taste of the Ale.

To make it one must stretch seventeen strings of [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md) -gut over each barrel, then fresh [boar](Lore_Boar.md) meat is pressed onto the strings, with the brewer being careful not to let any fall before it has properly rotted. Then the meat is dusted with the finely chopped skins of the red-striped frog to make sure the crosswings are drunk and happy when they fall. This is done every month for three or more years, until there is enough meat and bugs to fill the barrel.

Enchantments and spells are used in the process as well to attract the crosswings and mimics, to keep out the flipbacks, tree-hoppers, and [hoarvor](Lore_Hoarvor.md) young, and to encourage green mold and prevent brown and white mold.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-34)</sup>

## Cider
Ciders are a common drink in High Rock and among the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md).

Reach ciders range from dark, musty, and sweet. Depending on the variety of apples used and the time of year they're pressed. Apple orchards are few and far between in the Reach, but plenty of wild apples grow in the forests and river valleys. The clans that live in those regions each have their own preferred method for brewing up their favorite cider.<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-DOTR-40)</sup> Reachfolk cider is relatively well known outside of the Reach and praised for its quality.<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-ONJZ-41)</sup><sup>[[40]](#cite_note-ESORichien_Thelin-42)</sup>

## Jagga
**[Jagga](Lore_Jagga.md)** is an alcoholic drink made from fermented pig's milk which is popular in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[42]](#cite_note-ADIF-44)</sup>

To make it one must:

Set several shallow pans of boiled pig's milk cream where the cat can't step on them and leave alone for a day, then stir several times per day thereafter. Strain the whey into a separate vessel, keeping the curds in their pans. Break the curds apart with a spoon, then allow the curds to dry into shards. Stir daily to keep them from spoiling. Once dry, use these shards to thicken skimmed pig's milk, or to add more cream to plain pig's milk. Bottle and let ferment for 3 weeks.

One can drink the whey immediately, or use it in other recipes.<sup>[[43]](#cite_note-45)</sup>

## Mead
[Mead](Lore_Mead.md) (also called **Nord Mead**, or **Honey Wine** by the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md)) is an alcoholic beverage made from fermented honey and water, alternatively with molasses, and sometimes strained with mashed grains. It is a very popular drink that can be found primarily in the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), but it can also be seen across the continent of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), as brewing is the basis of any culture. Mead is very popular among the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), who usually import it from mainland Skyrim. Meaderies across the provinces have their own ingredients and recipes, mixing in their own spices and fruits like [Juniper Berries](Lore_Juniper_Berries.md).

### Black-Briar Mead
**Black-Briar Mead** is a beverage produced in the [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) city of [Riften](Lore_Riften.md). Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Black-Briar Mead was an especially potent drink popular with the local Nords, which was shipped as far abroad as [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) during the days of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). In the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), production of Black-Briar Mead resumed in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) when the [Black-Briar](Lore_Black-Briar.md) family of Riften attempted to gain a foothold in the mead industry. Its major competitor was [Honningbrew Mead](#Honningbrew_Mead), although by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) Honningbrew had been eliminated and Black-Briar obtained a monopoly on production.<sup>[[45]](#cite_note-TG03-47)</sup> **Black-Briar Reserve** is a higher quality, more expensive, and more potent mead, although the brewery has been known to simply fill the bottles with regular mead.

Another known variant is the **Blackbriar [195](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_195) Berry-Blend**.
