Thalmor Orders **by [Elenwen](Skyrim_Elenwen.md)** *A communique from a Thalmor agent to a subordinate scout*
---
[Agent Sanyon](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier_(corpse).md),

In response to your report dated 22nd Morning Star 401 *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*, your request for an expeditionary force is hereby denied.

Sanyon, this is the seventh report you have filed this month, and not one of your leads - not one!- has turned up so much as a shred of evidence that a [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) [shrine](Skyrim_Shrines.md) exists in the [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md) region. No prisoners. No documents. Nothing!

Our forces are stretched thin enough as it is, and I have better missions - better agents - to assign them to. If you feel so sure of your informant, investigate this yourself. Come back with proof. Or not at all.

By my hand and seal,
Elenwen
