[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/ON-furnishing-Orcish_Banner%2C_Golkarr.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Orcish_Banner%2C_Golkarr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Orcish_Banner,_Golkarr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Orcish_Banner,_Golkarr.jpg) A banner representing Golkarr, featuring the seal of Clan Igrun Golkarr was the [Igrun](Lore_Igrun_Clan.md) ruler of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) during the great siege in [1E 950](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_950). After bolstering his army with fleeing refugees from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), he attempted to broaden [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) holdings. He sought to capture the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) and force the kingdom of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) to provide compensation for its services. Naturally, this did not settle well with their neighboring kingdoms, especially the Kingdom of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md). King [Joile](Lore_Joile.md) of Daggerfall allied with [Gaiden Shinji](Lore_Gaiden_Shinji.md) of the [Order of the Diagna](Lore_Order_of_the_Diagna.md) and mounted a joint attack on Golkarr's kingdom.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WJOTB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3O-2)</sup> Eventually, after a 30 year siege, the allied forces breached Orsinium's Smelter Gate and overran the city in [1E 980](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_980).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WTB:N-3)</sup>

Legend says Gaiden Shinji himself killed Golkarr,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGSOO-4)</sup> however, Shinji himself fell in combat with [Baloth Bloodtusk](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md) prior to Joile's own death in [1E 973](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_973)<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GS-6)</sup> and the final sacking of Orsinium in [1E 980](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_980).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WTB:N-3)</sup> Bloodtusk had initially refused to come and defend Orsinium because he believed Golkarr to have done much to provoke and earn the wrath of the Bretons.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Golkarr lived long enough to welcome Bloodtusk's belated support,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-GS-6)</sup> and was still alive when the duel between (and assassinations of) Shinji and Baloth took place.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Lt BB-8)</sup> Golkarr was also a name of a settlement only found on certain maps of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SA-9)</sup>
