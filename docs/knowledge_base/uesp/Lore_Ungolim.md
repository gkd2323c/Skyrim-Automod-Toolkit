*The Night Mother will guide us.*
—Ungolim<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ungolim L-1)</sup> Ungolim was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) who had ascended to the position of Listener of the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md) by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Ungolim maintained a private residence in [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), and visited the statue of the Lucky Old Lady on a daily basis. He received the "Whispers of Death" from the [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md), and conveyed her will to the Speakers of the Black Hand.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> He was assassinated by [a Silencer](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) manipulated by [Mathieu Bellamont](Lore_Mathieu_Bellamont.md) in 3E 433.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AKBD-4)</sup>
