# Torture Victim

These four **torture victims** are found in the [Dawnstar Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) after the torture chamber is purchased from [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) in the quest [Where You Hang Your Enemy's Head...](Skyrim_Where_You_Hang_Your_Enemy%27s_Head....md). When spoken to enough, each of them will reveal the location of hidden treasure.

## Notes
- They can be used for target practice when grinding skills. By using the [Ice Form](Skyrim_Ice_Form.md) shout, you can remove the torture victims from their shackles and level up your [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) skill, though after being unshackled they will not move from their position.
