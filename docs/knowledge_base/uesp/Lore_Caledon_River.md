## Notes
- The Caledon River has never appeared physically in the games or on any official maps of Tamriel. But it was also coined before the invention of the [Nibenay River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nibenay_River) during *Redguard*'s development.
- The extract location of the Caledon River is not confirmed, but the context supplied explains that [the Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) often swam in its waters when they were living at the Imperial City, thus indicating that the river is located somewhere around there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall Background Game-1)</sup>
- Caledonia is a feminine forename used by [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
