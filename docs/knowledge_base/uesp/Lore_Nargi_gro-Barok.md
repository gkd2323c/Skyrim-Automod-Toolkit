[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/ON-quest-Trials_and_Tribulations_07.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Trials_and_Tribulations_07.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Trials_and_Tribulations_07.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Trials_and_Tribulations_07.jpg) Sword of the High King (bottom left) High King Nargi gro-Barok was a conquering [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) king who was active early in Tamriel's history. According to legends in the [Hall of Heroes](Lore_Hall_of_Heroes.md), Nargi was given a thrice-forged Orcish steel blade as a gift from [Mauloch](Lore_Mauloch.md) himself, titled the Sword of the High King.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ta T-1)</sup>
