# Frog-Metal

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Scalloped_Frog-Metal.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Scalloped_Frog-Metal.png) Scalloped Frog-Metal [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-item-Thrassian_Stranglers.jpg/200px-ON-item-Thrassian_Stranglers.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Thrassian_Stranglers.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Thrassian_Stranglers.jpg) Thrassian Stranglers Frog-Metal (also called **Buoyant Steel**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup> or **Syrabane's Steel**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM112-2)</sup> is a rare and highly valued [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) alloy first discovered by [Syrabane](Lore_Syrabane.md), the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) ancestor-god of magic in the mid- [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup> As one of its names suggests, the metal is incredibly light, so much so that it can float in water. Seafarers and warriors of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) often wore frog-metal armor to ride the waves fearlessly, but over time it was nigh extinct. It was particularly valued by those from [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md), an island in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM112-2)</sup>

## History
Frog-Metal was discovered by Syrabane, the Aldmeri warlock that joined [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) in his mission to seek justice against the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTOS-3)</sup> Syrabane fashioned the metal into armor for early [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) mariners<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup> and since then, it has been a staple among travelers and sea [knights](Lore_Knights.md) around Amenos and the Abecean Sea.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM112-2)</sup>

In the twenty-ninth century of the First Era, vast troves of Frog-Metal were discovered underneath Amenos and it bolstered a shift in industry for many on the island from fishing to mining the alloy.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SH6-4)</sup> Though Frog-Metal from Amenos is still traded,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> over time, Frog-Metal became increasingly rare. The writer, Matthias Vervins transcribed an old text on how to create frog-metal armor from a knight hailing from antiquity.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM112-2)</sup>

At some point in time, samples of frog-metal were recovered from [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) by the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). These samples were then forged into Thrassian Stranglers, gauntlets that wield Sload magic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONSteel Anti-1)</sup>
