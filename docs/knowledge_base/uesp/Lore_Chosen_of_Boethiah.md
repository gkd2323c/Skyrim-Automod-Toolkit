The Chosen of Boethiah<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> (sometimes **Boethiah's Chosen**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> or the **Chosen Ones**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> was a [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) Cult dedicated to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), with notable influence in [Skyrim Province](Lore_Skyrim.md) under the leadership of Oviron Salith, a Dunmer wizard of "terrifying power."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

## History

### Skyrim Civil War
*The [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) will rise to their true power! I will be one of the chosen ones, once the oppressors are routed out of our promised land! Our Prince is guiding us!*
—A Chosen of Boeithah<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a small party of [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) that survived the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) purges after the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), the [Sheathed Blades](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md), are called out of hiding to address the threat posed by an uprising conspiracy in the land.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> A preacher named Tiresi Dreelo of Boethiah's Chosen had been visiting the lords and serfs of Skyrim, attempting to begin a war between the Empire and the Nords.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> After investigating further, the Sheathed Blades decide they need to warn the High King and visit his palace in Solitude, however they are delayed by Dunmer cultists in robes that block the way (one of the Dunmer is named Taven Rathis), and by the time they reach [Torygg](Lore_Torygg.md), Ulfric Stormcloak had been there standing over the High King's corpse.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

The Chosen of Boethiah were a vast conspiracy operating across all of Skyrim Province. Their secret plan was to promote dissent and violence across the Nord homeland by purposefully stoking the flames of [Civil War](Lore_Skyrim_Civil_War.md) between the Imperials and Stormcloaks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Should their plans should succeed, they attain more and more positions of responsibility all over the province.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> The leader of the Chosen, High Priest Oviron Salith, was an influential member of Jarl [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s court within [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> The Chosen had their own hidden temple within that city. In one series of events, their temple is destroyed by the [Sheathed Blades](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md), and the Chosen go out into the land, now more active than ever before.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

The Sheathed Blades are forced to pick a side in the war.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> They eventually track down Taven Rathis, and interrogated her on the cult's plans.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> In one instance, they were forced to chase her down during the Siege of Whiterun.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> In another, they fetch their information by facing down the son of an Orcish chief named Bazga, after winning, the Orcish Chief reveals what he knows about the cult.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

### Attribution's Share
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/ON-place-The_Champion%27s_Arena.jpg/200px-ON-place-The_Champion%27s_Arena.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Champion%27s_Arena.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-The_Champion%27s_Arena.jpg) Attribution's Share After further and further investigation, the Sheathed Blades tracked down the leader of the Chosen to the cave of [Monahven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Monahven), the great mountain also known as the Throat of the World. There, High Priest Oviron Salith had another secret temple, which was host to his most loyal followers and a portal leading to [Attribution's Share](Lore_Attribution%27s_Share.md), the Realm of Boethiah. When the Sheathed Blades arrive, Oviron jumps through the portal, and the Blades may choose to follow after.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

After following Salith into Oblivion, the Sheathed Blades are subject to the whims of Boethiah. For hundreds of years, utterly lost in the plane, they hunt Ovirion Salith. They die dozens of times, but somehow come back to life, again and again. Nightmarish creatures swallow them whole. Before, Boethiah finally unleashes them from her realm, and sends them back to Tamriel to execute her errands.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup>

### The Chosen Ones
Back to present day, Oviron Salith and the Sheathed Blades return to Tamriel. At this time, Prince Boethiah brings forth a countless number of people to Tamriel from Attribution's Share, whom she calls her Chosen Ones.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Each of her Chosen are being haunted by and mentally manipulated by the Prince, forced to listen to her and do her bidding, with the only way to escape this life is by ending their own.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Her ultimate plan is to control all of Tamriel, beginning with Skyrim; spreading dissent and chaos and rebellion in all quarters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Following his release from Boethiah's Plane, Oviron thought to turn against Boethiah, and invited his nemeses, the Sheathed Blades to join him in this.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> The fate of Oviron, the Sheathed Blades, and the Chosen of Boethiah cult, is ultimately unknown.

- **Taven Rathis**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-1)</sup> Female Dunmer, Member of the Chosen. Prevented the Sheathed Blades from saving High King Torygg.
