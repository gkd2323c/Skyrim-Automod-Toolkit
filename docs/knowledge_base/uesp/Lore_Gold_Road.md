[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/OB-map-Gold_Road.jpg/200px-OB-map-Gold_Road.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Gold_Road.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-map-Gold_Road.jpg) Map showing the route of the Gold Road The Gold Road runs east to west between the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md), passing through [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md), [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), Ostumir and [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TGRAMJ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup> It originally diverged at the Brina Cross north of Anvil to connect to the settlement of [Sutch](Lore_Sutch.md),<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup> but it was taken off the main route sometime in the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) after the settlement's abandonment.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup>

The Gold Road supposedly continues on to [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), passing west of the Shrine to [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md) near Sutch.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

## Notes
- *[Gold Road](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Gold_Road)* is also the name of the eighth [Chapter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Chapters) for *[The Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)*, which takes place mainly in the region of [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).
