## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the glass mine was purchased by the [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) master, [Shelreni Baro](Lore_Shelreni_Baro.md)<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OTPOAM-8)</sup> and news spread across the region for a new job opportunity.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONOmalen-9)</sup> Everyone in the village was put to work but when the Hidden Kindred, a [daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) cult led by Kindred Rector Nyleth arrived into town under the guise of masons and laborers, people in Alavelis began to experience nightmares of building an arch, and then a statue. While the cultists of [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) commandeered the Green Glass Inn,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONSadalas-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OTPOAM-8)</sup> the Dreamcarver Clan of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) led by Kynreeve Ryl came to secure the town. The plan was to construct an inconspicuous portal to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md), the plane of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) and infiltrate it.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OTPOAM-8)</sup>

An inconsistency in the threads of fate was found in Alavelis and an agent of Hermaeus Mora, a [watchling](Lore_Watchling.md) known as Scruut was sent to town. With the help of a [warrior](Lore_The_Vestige.md) chosen by Hermaeus Mora themself, the two delved into the ruined town to uncover the conspiracy. Their search brought them to the portal deep in the Alavelis Mine and into Apocrypha, where they found Alavelisians dead and turned into [Hushed](Lore_Hushed.md). They eventually tracked the cultists and Dreamcarvers to the Tranquil Catalog, a stronghold that houses an index for everything in the realm. The warrior, Fate's Chosen was able to defeat the Kynreeve.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONDream-10)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Amber Forest was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Casien and had a rivalry with [Corkarth Run](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Corkarth_Run).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AR-6)</sup> Amber Forest was neighbored by several settlements, including [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md) to the west, Old Keep to the far west-southwest, [Sailen Vulgate](Lore_Sailen_Vulgate.md) to the north-northeast, and [Silnim Dale](Lore_Silnim_Dale.md) to the far southwest.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ARMap-11)</sup>
