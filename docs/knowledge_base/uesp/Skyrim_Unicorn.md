[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-creature-Unicorn.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Unicorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Unicorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Unicorn.jpg) Unicorn The **Unicorn** is a unique [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) which can be found near the pond just west of [Lost Prospect Mine](Skyrim_Lost_Prospect_Mine.md) during the related quest. It is a [wild horse](Skyrim_Wild_Horse.md) and must be [tamed](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC) by continuing to mount it until it breaks.

Once tamed, the Unicorn becomes an owned mount. If you switch to a different mount, it will slowly make its way to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) courtyard.

## Notes
- According to [*Soran's Journal*](Skyrim_Soran%27s_Journal.md), this Unicorn was brought forward in time by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) after the species went extinct during [Hircine's quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Hircine) in [*Oblivion*](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion). As such, it is supposedly the last Unicorn in existence.
- The Unicorn can be equipped with [Elven](Skyrim_Elven_Horse_Armor.md) and [Steel Horse Armor](Skyrim_Steel_Horse_Armor.md) when the [Horse Armor - Elven](Skyrim_Horse_Armor_-_Elven.md) or [Horse Armor - Steel](Skyrim_Horse_Armor_-_Steel.md) creations are installed.
- The Unicorn can change saddles.
- The Unicorn can change names as well.
