[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Solitude_Guard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Solitude_Guard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Solitude_Guard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Solitude_Guard.jpg) Solitude Guard **Solitude Guards** are [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) who act as the military and police force of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). These guards serve under [Jarl Elisif](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md). Should the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) forces gain control of Solitude, they will be replaced by [Stormcloak guards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stormcloak_Guard). They are often local recruits for the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), undergoing training led by [Captain Aldis](Skyrim_Captain_Aldis.md) in the courtyard of [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). They can also be found patrolling Solitude.

A Solitude guard wears Solitude Guard's Armor, Solitude Guard's Helmet, Imperial Boots, and Imperial Bracers and carries a Solitude Guard's Shield, an Imperial Bow, and an Imperial Sword.
