[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-item-The_Bond_of_Matrimony.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Bond_of_Matrimony.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Bond_of_Matrimony.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Bond_of_Matrimony.jpg) The Bond of Matrimony The Bond of Matrimony is a [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) that both you and your spouse will receive from [Maramal](Skyrim_Maramal.md) after [your marriage ceremony](Skyrim_The_Bonds_of_Matrimony.md) is complete. In appearance, this ring is identical to a [gold ring](Skyrim_Gold_Ring.md). Its enchantment [fortifies your Restoration skill](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) by 10 points, decreasing the magicka cost of all Restoration spells.

## Related Quests
- **[The Bonds of Matrimony](Skyrim_The_Bonds_of_Matrimony.md)**: Find someone in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

## Notes
- The Bond of Matrimony cannot be sold to vendors.
- The effect applied by the Bond of Matrimony is exactly the same as that of an [Amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md), though both effects stack. The amulet is slightly more noteworthy, as [Rings of Restoration](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Fortify_Restoration) occur as random loot, while Necklaces of Restoration do not.

## Bugs
- The Bond of Matrimony can be disenchanted, and the Blessing of Mara enchantment learned, but it cannot be applied to any items during enchanting, making the resulting XP gain from disenchanting the only benefit for doing so. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. The item can no longer be disenchanted.
