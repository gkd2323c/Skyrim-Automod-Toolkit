# Unique Armor

**Unique Armor** is a type of armor available in Skyrim that is generally found in fixed locations, unlike most equipment in the game, which are randomly generated or generic equipment (see [Generic Magic Apparel](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md)). Some of these armors can be improved through [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md). Improving enchanted armor requires the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

Many of these items are quest rewards, although some of the most valuable quest rewards are listed at [Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md) (unique items, but with level-dependent statistics) and [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) (unusually powerful unique items with unique appearances and enchantments). For unique items that are needed to complete quests, please see the [Quest Items](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md) article. For other unique items in the game, see the [Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md) article. For a list of the other available armor, see the [armor category](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Armor).

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Armor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Ancient Nord Armor.png) Ahzidal's Armor of Retribution [Ahzidal's Armor of Retribution](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Armor_of_Retribution.md) is the chest piece of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). It can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after defeating its creator during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). This armor is identical in appearance to [ancient Nord armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Armor.md), but provides fifteen more points of [protection](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md) and weighs ten points more. Its enchantment gives you a 5% chance to [paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) enemies who strike you with a melee attack, making dealing with multiple melee enemies much easier. In addition, if you wear this armor along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points. Ahzidal's armor can be tempered with a [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md), with the [Advanced Armors perk](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors_(perk).md) doubling improvement.
