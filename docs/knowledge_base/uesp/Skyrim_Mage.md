[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Mage_%28NPC%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mage_%28NPC%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mage_(NPC).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mage_(NPC).jpg) The dead mage The **Mage** is a dead [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) located in one of the three cages in the [torturer](Skyrim_Torturer.md)'s room in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md).

Your companion during [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md) will suggest that you loot the mage's body. To do so, you will need to use the lockpicks given to you by [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) or [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md), or retrieve the [lockpicks](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) from a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) on the nearby table and [pick](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) the lock to his cage. This will allow you to retrieve the first set of mage's clothing available in the game— [novice robes](Skyrim_Novice_Robes.md) and a [novice hood](Skyrim_Novice_Hood.md) —as well as two [potions of minor magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md), and 25 gold. Beside him in the cell is a further four gold pieces, another potion of minor magicka, and a copy of the [Spell Tome: Sparks](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Sparks.md). Even if your character is not planning to be a spell-caster, it is worth retrieving these items for their resale value (or for [disenchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md)).
