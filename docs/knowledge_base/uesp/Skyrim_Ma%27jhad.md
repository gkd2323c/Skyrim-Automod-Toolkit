[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-npc-Ma%27jhad.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ma%27jhad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27jhad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ma%27jhad.jpg) Ma'jhad **Ma'jhad** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [warrior](Skyrim_TrainerLockpickExpert.md) who travels with [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md)'s [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). He can sometimes be found outside [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) or [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and is an expert-level [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md). He will be a [fence](Skyrim_Fence_(merchant).md) for [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) members after completing the quest [Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Caravan_Fence_Quest.md).

Ma'jhad wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0004223d). He is equipped with a [steel greatsword](Skyrim_Steel_Greatsword.md), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
When asked for training, he will warn:

- *"All right, but if you get arrested don't blame it on me."*
