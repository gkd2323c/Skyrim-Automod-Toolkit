**Note:** The First Era of Tamriel is also sometimes referred to as the *First Age.* The early-to-middle part is known as the *Classical Period*.

## First Century

1E 0 — The [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) is founded by [King Eplear](Lore_Eplear.md).

- The Camoran Dynasty of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) is founded by King Eplear.
- The [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) use this newly formed kingdom as a buffer from the influence of the [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) ([Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md)) kingdom. The [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) becomes an independent city-state, and the heart of the Ayleid Empire.

## Second Century

1E 143 — [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) is crowned High King of the Nords.

- Harald, the 13th descendant of the legendary [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s line, relinquishes his ancestral holdings in Atmora, and consolidates Skyrim as an independent kingdom with [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) as its capital. He is later recognized as the first historical ruler of the Nords.

1E 184 — King [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) establishes the first Moot of the Nords.
1E 198 — The Scouring of Wendelbek.

- The [Narfinsel Schism](Lore_Narfinsel_Schism.md), a late- [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) internal conflict pitting [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) -worshiping [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) clans against [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshiping clans, comes to a climax at [Wendelbek](Lore_Wendelbek.md). King [Glinferen](Lore_Glinferen.md) of [Atatar](Lore_Atatar.md) led a combined force of Daedraphile warriors against the traditionalist [Barsaebics](Lore_Barsaebics.md) of Ayleidoon. The Barsaebics were driven out of the [Heartland](Lore_Heartland.md) into northwest [Argonia](Lore_Argonia.md), ending organized opposition to Daedra-worship in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

## Third Century

1E 240 — The [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) Conquests occur.

- From 1E 222 - 272, the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) expands across [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), swallowing them up by 1E 240. Some sources interpret the Skyrim Conquests to have run from 1E 240 to [1E 415](#1E_416).

1E 242 — The [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) erupts in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

- [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md), also known as the Slave Queen, leads the predominantly human slaves against the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) Empire, with the help of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and some rebel Ayleid rulers. They eventually conquer all of Cyrodiil.

1E 243 — Taking of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md).

- The [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) finally falls into the hands of the rebels, marking the end of Ayleid supremacy in Cyrodiil, and the Slave Queen [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) founds the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md). The remaining Ayleids that opposed Alessia seek refuge with [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)'s [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md) and the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), while the rebel Ayleid lords who sided with Alessia continue to rule parts of Cyrodiil as vassals of the new Empress Alessia. Some famous human heroes are [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md), who leads the attack of White-Gold Tower, and [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md), who single-handedly defeats many of the Ayleid kings, including the Ayleids' demigod champion [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md).
- From 1E 243 to [498](#1E_498), the new kingdom of humans, under the rule of Empress Alessia and her descendants, prospers and rules the nearest kingdoms of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md). They build a strong national identity as they gradually become more united. The Empress Alessia creates the new religion of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md), fusing the pantheons of the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and Cyrodiilic tribes, who followed [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) deities.

1E 246 — [Daggerfall City](Lore_Daggerfall.md) is founded.

- The north half of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), including the current province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), is conquered by invading [Nords](Lore_Nord.md), who bring a rough sort of civilization with them. One of the Nords' first civilized acts is a census - the *[Book of Life](Lore_Book_of_Life.md)*. Listed on page 933 of the book is this entry: "North of the Highest bluffs, south of the moors, west of the hills, and east of the sea is called DAGGERFALL. 110 men, 93 women, 13 children under 8 years of age, 58 cows, 7 bulls, 63 chickens, 11 cocks, 38 hogs live here."

1E 266 — Empress Alessia dies.

- [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) (or [Shezarr](Lore_Shezarr.md) in the form of Pelinal) arrives at the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and transforms the dying Alessia into the first of the Cyrodilic saints. Thus, Empress Alessia becomes the first gem in the Cyrodilic [Amulet of Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md). This is the beginning of the Covenant between the [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md) and Akatosh, which helps to protect [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).

## Fourth Century

1E 355 — Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) rises to prominence.

- Clan Direnni, the only known [High Elven](Lore_Altmer.md) ruling family remaining in human lands, claims power in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) through political maneuvering and various machinations.However, some consider their rule in the years leading up to its establishment as part of the Direnni Hegemony as well.

1E 358 — Skyrim attacks the Western Reach

- Emperor Ami-El of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) lends his forces to Skyrim in a military campaign against the Direnni Hegemony in the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md). Anti-Aldmer sentiments rise as Skyrim loses more and more of Greater Bretony to the Direnni.

1E 361 — The [Alessian Doctrines](Lore_Alessian_Doctrines.md) are enforced.

- The [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) becomes the dominant political factor in the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md), and their doctrines are enforced throughout [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). The doctrines impose the very strict religious teachings of the prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md), who, after reportedly receiving visions from the ghost of the Slave Queen [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md), begins to question Elven rule, calling for a suppression of Elven elements within the pantheon of the [Eight Divines](Lore_Eight_Divines.md). The Order enforces strict codes of behavior which preclude most forms of entertainment. The remaining [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) lordships, once tolerated by the Alessian Empire, are abolished.

1E 369 — High King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md) dies and the [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) begins.

- When High King Borgas of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md) is killed during the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md) and the [Moot](Lore_Moot.md) fails to elect [Jarl Hanse](Lore_Jarl_Hanse.md) as the next ruler of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), the War of Succession erupts across the [Nordic Empire](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md). The conflict leads to the Empire's dissolution as its territories in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) break away. The war ends in [1E 420](#1E_420) with the [Pact of Chieftains](Lore_Pact_of_Chieftains.md).

1E 374 — The Ayleid vassal-state of [Nenalata](Lore_Nenalata.md) is massacred by the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md).

- Nenalata, the last remaining Ayleid city in Cyrodiil is given an ultimatum to leave the province or face extermination. King [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md) gathers those who wished to leave and begins an exodus to the lands of the [Direnni Hegemony](Lore_Direnni_Hegemony.md). Shortly afterwards the Alessian Army arrives at Nenalata and massacres all who choose to stay behind.

1E 376 — The traditional [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) emperors are overthrown.

- The traditional tenets of the Yokudan Empire are overthrown, and although each successive emperor remains a figurehead for the empire, his powers are very much reduced. The [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) people see 300 years of almost continuous civil war between provincial lords, warrior monks, and brigands, all fighting each other for land and power before the appearance of the hero [Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md). <sup>[[nb 3]](#intnote_nb3)</sup>

## Fifth Century

1E 416 — The [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) are driven out of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).

- The united [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) drive the Nords, weakened from the [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md), out of modern-day Morrowind, which was then called [Resdayn](Lore_Resdayn.md). [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) and [Dumac](Lore_Dumac.md) become leaders of the [First Council](Lore_First_Council.md) of Resdayn, and it flourishes under their leadership. Peace among the Chimer and the Dwemer brings unprecedented prosperity to their land.<sup>[[nb 2]](#intnote_nb2)</sup>
- The Dwemer clan [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) rebel against this alliance with the Chimer and self-exile themselves. According to legend, "the Rourken chieftain threw his mighty hammer, [Volendrung](Lore_Volendrung.md), across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), promising to lead his clansmer to 'wherever it should fall.'" The land where they would arrive was thus called Volenfell (i.e., [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)).

1E 420 — The [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) [War of Succession](Lore_War_of_Succession.md) ends.

- The Skyrim War of Succession ends with the Pact of Chieftains. The war resulted in Skyrim losing control of the other provinces in northern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and though their borders continue to shift occasionally, they never regain their former stature.
- [Olaf One-Eye](Lore_Olaf_One-Eye.md) is crowned High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

1E 482 — The [Battle of Glenumbria Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbria_Moors.md)

- The battle between the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md) and the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) Elves of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), under the command of [Aiden Direnni](Lore_Aiden_Direnni.md). The Order is soundly defeated by the Elves at the cost of much of the Elven power in High Rock. The [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) High King [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md) is killed during the battle.
- The province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), under the new High King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md), submits to a fiery reinstatement of the traditional [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) pantheon. Alessian temples are destroyed, and remnants of its priesthood seek refuge in the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md).

1E 484 — [The Hollow City](Lore_The_Hollow_City.md) is created.

- After a trap by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), a city near Nenalata is pushed through [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and into [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). Wards from [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) prevent Bal from interfering with the city, creating a permanent pocket free of his influence within Bal's own realm.

1E 498 — Elven power in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) is ended.

- The Elves of High Rock, after losing much power in the [Battle of Glenumbria Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbria_Moors.md) against the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md), are replaced by their Breton subjects, but allowed to retain Balfiera and control of the Adamantine Tower. One account claims that the Direnni had already completed their withdrawal to Balfiera by the time the Redguards arrived in Tamriel, A third, contradictory source places the final stages of the Direnni’s flight from High Rock sometime around or after the ninth century of the First Era.<sup>[[nb 5]](#intnote_nb5)</sup> Yet another source claims that the Direnni remained dominant in High Rock until the middle of the First Era.

## Sixth Century

1E 522 — The [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) leave the Summerset Isles and establish a kingdom in Hammerfell.

- [Sundavar Corelanya](Lore_Sundavar_Corelanya.md) landed on the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) and founded a kingdom from the ruined Ayleid city of [Salas En](Lore_Salas_En.md).

1E 533 — [High King Ysmir Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md) dies.

1E 580 — A great fire occurs on the fortieth birthday of Baron Montclair.

- Stoneglow Enchantments crafted Sparkle Sticks for the occasion, which had inadvertently caused the fire.

## Seventh Century

1E 609 — King [Thagore](Lore_Thagore.md) defeats [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md).

- King Thagore of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md) defeats the army of Glenpoint and becomes the preeminent economic, cultural, and military force in southern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), a position the kingdom has precariously kept ever since.

1E 617 — [Mansel Sesnit](Lore_Mansel_Sesnit.md) is assassinated.

- Sesnit, the military dictator of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md), is assassinated and a commoner, [Randic Torn](Lore_Randic_Torn.md), takes over the government of the Yokudans. He rules for 120 years.

1E 668 — [The War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md); [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) erupts.

- The first eruption of Morrowind's gigantic volcano, since known as [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). Ash spews into the sky and hides the sun for a year. This year is also called Sun's Death or the Year of Winter in Summer.

## Eighth Century

1E 700 — [Battle of Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md).

- The approximate date of the famous battle between the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), led by [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) and [Dumac Dwarfking](Lore_Dumac.md), which concludes [the War of the First Council](Lore_The_War_of_the_First_Council.md). Both Nerevar and Dumac perish. These events pave the way for the formation of [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), which would last until just short of the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

1E 780 — The [War of the Singers](Lore_War_of_the_Singers.md).

- Emperor [Hira](Lore_Hira.md) attempts to wrestle control of the empire from the people by exterminating the sword-singers. Frandar Hunding leads the resistance and crushes his army at the foot of Mount Hattu. Shortly after, Frandar, his son, and his army migrate to Hammerfell as the first warrior wave - the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md).

1E 792 — The destruction of [Yokuda](Lore_Yokuda.md).

- Yokuda [sinks](Lore_Yokuda.md#Destruction), supposedly at the hands of the Hiradirge. The bulk of the refugees flee east to the island [Herne](Lore_Herne_(island).md), while the rest continue on to Hammerfell. There isn't much left of the continent except for several islands.

## Ninth Century

1E 808 — [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) arrive again in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).

- A second warrior wave of [Yokudans](Lore_Yokuda.md), the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md), drives the [beastfolk](Lore_Beastfolk.md) and [Nedic](Lore_Nedic.md) peoples from [Volenfell](Lore_Volenfell.md). Over time, the name "Ra Gada" becomes corrupted into "Redguard", and the race claims Hammerfell as their homeland. The beastfolk refugees escape from Hammerfell and join the Orcs at [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).

1E 853 — Most of the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) are exterminated by Yokudan colonists

- Led by King [Xakhwan](Lore_Xakhwan.md) of [Yath](Lore_Yath.md), the Yokudans face the Corelanya clan at the Ash'abah Pass and finally drive them from Hammerfell. King Xakhwan was killed during the conflict and was succeeded by one of his sons.

1E 874 — Warlord [Thulgeg](Lore_Thulgeg.md)'s army of [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) and [Goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) are driven from Hammerfell by the Yokudans

- [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) forces deny the survivors passage through the [Bangkorai Pass](Lore_Bangkorai_Pass.md), forcing them to flee northeast through the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) before they finally reach [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md).

## Tenth Century

1E 950 — Assault on [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) begins.

- The annihilation of the [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) capital Orsinium begins through the joint efforts of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), the new kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), and the [Order of Diagna](Lore_Order_of_the_Diagna.md). It takes constant effort over 30 years to drive the Orcs from their capital.

1E 983 — [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) is founded.

- The [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) city is established by the [River Horse Bretons](Lore_River_Horse_Bretons.md), after the [Bjoulsae](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) trade was opened up thanks to the fall of Orsinium.

## Eleventh Century

1E 1029 — [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) is admitted into the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md).

- After the legions of Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md) deposed King Styriche of Verkarth, Styriche fled west with his infamous Gray Host of vampires and werewolves, pillaging along the way. The Gray Host was halted by the Bangkorai Garrison of High Rock, and Hestra's forces killed the survivors. Impressed by the Bangkorai Garrison's actions, Hestra allowed High Rock to be admitted into the Empire. [House Dorell](Lore_House_Dorell.md) of [Northpoint](Lore_Northpoint.md) and [House Montclair](Lore_House_Montclair.md) are granted a barony.

1E 1033 — [Alessians](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) take on piracy in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).

- Empress [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md), fed up with the pirates hiding in the depths of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), calls for the head of the most famous brigand, ["Red" Bramman](Lore_Red_Bramman.md), as part of a larger campaign. Their incursion provided some of the first outsider accounts on the true culture of the land of the [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md).

## Twelfth Century

1E 1100 — The kingdom of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md) is founded.

- Wayrest's rapid expansion following the defeat of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) in [1E 980](#1E_980) culminates the merchant power earning the right to be called a kingdom and city-state.

## Fourteenth Century

1E 1301 — Sack of [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md)

- The [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) of [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) attack the city of Skywatch.

1E 1306 — Hyperagonal Collapse Incident

- Twenty-seven [Psijic Monks](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) are converted into thin films of glistening liminal particles after an incident at the Eldbur Monastery for Mystic Inquiry on [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md).

## Fifteenth Century

1E 1427 — [Battle of Duncreigh Bridge](Lore_Battle_of_Duncreigh_Bridge.md)

- The battle between [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) and the neighboring hamlet of Sensford. It is celebrated each year by Anticlere with a march down Sensford's main street, usually resulting in many injuries and sometimes brief wars between the towns' knightly orders. The battle ultimately achieves nothing, as both sides continue to boast of their own antique lineage.

## The [Middle Dawn](Lore_Middle_Dawn.md)
The longest [Dragon Break](Lore_Dragon_Break.md) ever known, sometimes called the Middle Dawn, is said to have taken place across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) for one thousand and eight years. Some scholars think the whole matter was the result of an error in a timeline.

## Twenty-Third Century

1E 2200 — The [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) release the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md).

- For the next several centuries, this brutal plague devastates more than half of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s population, concentrated along the western coast nearest to [Thras](Lore_Thras.md).

1E 2260 — The [All Flags Navy](Lore_All_Flags_Navy.md) launch their final assault on Thras

- After claiming several major victories at sea against the Sload and their great sea beasts, Olo and his great fleet (comprising ships from every nation of Tamriel) sails against the Sload in retribution for the Thrassian Plague, sinking their kingdom with mighty magics.

## Twenty-Fourth Century

1E 2321 — The [War of Righteousness](Lore_War_of_Righteousness.md) begins.

- The War of Righteousness breaks out when Western [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) isolates itself from the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) and establishes the [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md). This follows years of internal strife within the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md)'s bloated priesthood and territorial movements to curtail or outlaw the Order's religion.

1E 2331 — The War of Righteousness ends.

- After a decade of conflict, the War of Righteousness concludes and the Alessian Order and Empire are dissolved. The Empire's former territories solidify their independence, and its heartland, Cyrodiil, splits into Eastern and Western halves that continue to diverge culturally over the following centuries.

## Twenty-Fifth Century

## Twenty-Eighth Century

1E 2703 — The First [Akaviri](Lore_Akavir.md) Invasion occurs

- [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) of Akavir land in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), cutting a swathe of destruction through [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), spurring [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman.md) to rally the [Colovian West](Lore_Colovia.md) and [Nibenean East](Lore_Nibenay_Valley.md). Cyrodiil is united for the first time in centuries. The unified army defeats the Akaviri at the [Pale Pass](Lore_Pale_Pass.md) along the Cyrodiil-Skyrim border. Some sources claim the invasion began in 1E 2700.

1E 2703 — The [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) is established

- Following the defeat of the Akaviri by [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I, the Cyrodiils find themselves not only united as a nation, but responsible for the further protection of the northern human kingdoms at large.

1E 2704 — Emperor [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I divides [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) between [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)

- In an attempt to limit the possibility of the [Reachmen](Lore_Reachmen.md) uniting against him, Emperor Reman Cyrodiil divided their territory between the provinces of Skyrim and High Rock. The natives resisted fiercely, and there was never a decade when the troops of Solitude or Evermore wouldn't be sent in an attempt to pacify them.

1E 2714 — [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) is conquered by the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md).

- After centuries of unrelenting warfare along the border with [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and a plague from [Thras](Lore_Thras.md), the land of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) finally falls to the Second Empire. Although the province became occupied by the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) and the Kingdom of Grahtwood was established, they continued to experience resistance from some natives for several years.

1E 2729 — The city-state of [Southpoint](Lore_Southpoint.md) was founded on the 1st of Rain's Hand.

- After Governor [Zantonius](Lore_Zantonius.md) had fought a raid of [Wood Orcs](Lore_Wood_Orcs.md) at [Reman's Bluff](Lore_Reman%27s_Bluff.md), he was awarded a plot of land for a fort. But he converted it into a fortified settlement, which later became Southpoint.

1E 2762 — Emperor [Reman](Lore_Reman.md) I dies.
1E 2762 — Emperor [Kastav](Lore_Kastav.md) is crowned.

## Twenty-Ninth Century

1E 2804 — Emperor [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md) assumes the throne of Cyrodiil.

- Reman II deposes the incompetent [Kastav](Lore_Kastav.md) and is credited with negotiating an end to the Winterhold Rebellion and leading the Second Empire into a golden age. He would later begin a war of conquest against Argonia and Morrowind. His coronation took place later in 1E 2812.

1E 2811 — The [Blackwater War](Lore_Blackwater_War.md) begins.

- A military expansion is launched by [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md) against Argonia, eventually resulting in the formation of the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).

1E 2837 — The province of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) is created.

- The [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) succeeds in seizing a large part of the swamplands of Argonia to create the province of Black Marsh. Its provincial status was revoked after the dissolution of the Second Empire.

1E 2840 — The [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md) begins.

- [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md) declares war on [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The war drags on for 80 years.

1E 2843 — Emperor [Reman II](Lore_Reman_II.md) dies.

- According to the inscription on his tomb in Sancre Tor, he dies "in battle against the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dunmer.md)".

1E 2843 — Emperor [Brazollus Dor](Lore_Brazollus_Dor.md) is crowned.

- The little-known Brazollus Dor disdains the responsibilities of governing, preferring to spend his time at his country estate near [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md) while his Potentate, [Sidri-Ashak](Lore_Sidri-Ashak.md), handled all matters of state. This is said to be the emperor's wisest decision, as Sidri-Ashak proved to be a gifted administrator and man of integrity.

1E 2877 — Emperor [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md) is crowned.

## Thirtieth Century

1E 2920 — The last year of the First Era.

- Emperor [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md) and his son and heir Prince [Juilek](Lore_Juilek.md) are both assassinated by the [Morag Tong](Lore_Morag_Tong.md). This follows the sacking of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) fortress of Black Gate, breaking a truce between the Empire and Morrowind. [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) assumes the Imperial Throne, and declares the start of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).
- [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) destroys [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) and [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) are too late to prevent its destruction, but manage to banish Mehrunes Dagon back to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).
