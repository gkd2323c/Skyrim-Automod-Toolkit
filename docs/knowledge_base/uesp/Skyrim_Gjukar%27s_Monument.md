[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/38/SR-place-Gjukar%27s_Monument.jpg/200px-SR-place-Gjukar%27s_Monument.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gjukar%27s_Monument.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Gjukar%27s_Monument.jpg) Gjukar's Monument **Gjukar's Monument** is a stone monument commemorating an ancient battle southeast of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md).

A sword, a shield, and a few flowers are at the base of the monument. During the quest [The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md), a ghost named [Ruki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruki) can be found here searching for her husband [Fenrig](Skyrim_Fenrig.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md)**: Help the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) spread the cause of love throughout Skyrim.

## Notes
- An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is near the road south of the monument.
- A [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) is among the rocks north of the monument.
- Due east of the monument is an [unmarked ruin](Skyrim_Shrines.md#Crumbling_Bastion) containing a [shrine of Zenithar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Zenithar), an [amulet of Zenithar](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Zenithar.md), a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Second Seed, v5](Skyrim_2920,_Second_Seed,_v5.md)*, two [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), and a [satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Satchel), with a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) directly above these items on top of the ruin.
- Southeast of the monument near the road is a pool with the remains of a long-dead colossal [mudcrab](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) surrounded by its smaller living cousins. These remains will be joined by the [Mudcrab Guardian Spirit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit) during the quest [Kyne's Sacred Trials](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md).
