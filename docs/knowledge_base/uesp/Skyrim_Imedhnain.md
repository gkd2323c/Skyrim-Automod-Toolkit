[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-npc-Imedhnain.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imedhnain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imedhnain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imedhnain.jpg) Imedhnain **Imedhnain** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [food vendor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Food_Vendor) living in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He works at [Arnleif and Sons Trading Company](Skyrim_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company.md) and will take over the shop if [Lisbet](Skyrim_Lisbet.md) dies. He is related to [Cosnach](Skyrim_Cosnach.md).

He sleeps in the northwestern bedroom every night between midnight and 8am. The rest of his day is spent doing his job in the main trading room, either wandering, sweeping, eating, or relaxing in a chair. If he takes over the store, he will take the spot behind the counter every day between 8am and 8pm, then spend the evenings strolling around.

He wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0001be1b) and a [hat](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209aa). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), his [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company), and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
When you approach Imedhnain he can say:

*"Lisbet handles the customers. I just check the shipments."* *"Feel free to take a look around the store."* *"I've been at Arnleif and Sons all my life. It's a good store."* *"I remember old Arnleif building each shelf in this store himself."* *"Old Arnleif would be proud of Lisbet. She's kept this place running, thick and thin."* *"I brought in my cousin [Cosnach](Skyrim_Cosnach.md) to help me handle shipments."* If Lisbet is dead, upon entering the shop Imedhnain will say:

*"With Lisbet gone I will be taking care of you."* When spoken to again, he may also say:

*"Let me know if there's something you need to buy."* *"I'm keeping Arnleif and Sons going as best I can."* *"Never thought I'd be running the store. I'm not used to it."* When you exit conversation he can say:

*"I better get back to sorting the dry goods."* *"Now where did I put that shipment of forks..."*
