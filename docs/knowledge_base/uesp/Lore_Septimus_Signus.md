*What is this... it's... it's just a book?! I can see. The world beyond burns in my mind. It's marvelous....*
—Septimus Signus' final worlds before dissolving<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSeptimus-1)</sup> Septimus Signus was a brilliant scholar from the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md) who dedicated his life to studying the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTES-2)</sup> a pursuit which drove him mad.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSeptimus-1)</sup> In [4E 195](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_195),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> his book *Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls* was published.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ROTES-2)</sup> Eventually, he had trouble even using coherent sentences, and often spoke in verse.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> He discovered a large [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) lockbox in an ice cave north of the College of Winterhold. He and [Urag gro-Shub](Lore_Urag_gro-Shub.md) were colleagues, and Urag last saw Septimus years before his death.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRUrag-5)</sup> Somewhere along the way, Septimus became a disciple of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), the [Daedric prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of the unknown, who led him to believe this lockbox contained the [Heart of Lorkhan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heart_of_Lorkhan), compared to which the knowledge of the Elder Scrolls was a mere "passing awareness".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSeptimus-1)</sup>

Septimus murdered, sowed dissent, and spread plagues on behalf of the Demon of Knowledge to gain his assistance in acquiring the Heart. But in order to unlock the lockbox, he needed the blood of the Dwemer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SRSeptimus-1)</sup> With the aid of an [assistant](Lore_Hero.md), he collected the blood of the various [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) races and used the mixture as a substitute. However, once inside, Septimus did not find the Heart, but rather, the [Oghma Infinium](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oghma_Infinium), which had been sealed within by persons unknown to deprive Hermaeus Mora of its power. Before Septimus could comprehend that he had been betrayed and used by the daedra, he disintegrated, and Hermaeus Mora's new champion, the assistant, claimed the Oghma Infinium for themself.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Dt T-6)</sup>

## Notes
- | [^1](#note_nb1) | Given his name, it is highly likely that Septimus was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md). |
| --- | --- |
- The internal scripting for Septimus Signus's death is "[DA04Septimus Ascension](https://cslist.uesp.net/index.php?game=sr&script=SF_DA04Septimus Ascension_0001A333)". The script includes debug traces that state "Septimus ascending..." and "Septimus vanished?", suggesting something else may have happened to Septimus. In esoteric lore, there is a concept associated with attaining forbidden divine knowledge known as "zero sum", with the people who reach this state being described as "evaporating" or "vaporizing".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Suitors-8)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-EEATD-9)</sup> Though it cannot be said for certain if this was Septimus' fate.
