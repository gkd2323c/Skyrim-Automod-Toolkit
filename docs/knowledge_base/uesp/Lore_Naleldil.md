# Naleldil

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/ON-mount-Gloomspore.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Gloomspore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Gloomspore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Gloomspore.jpg) A collection of Gloomspore creations [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/ON-crown_store-Ice-Glow_Salamander.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Ice-Glow_Salamander.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ice-Glow_Salamander.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Ice-Glow_Salamander.jpg) Ice-Glow Salamander, imbued by Naleldi with the use of Ayleid ritual **Mage** Naleldil was a [mycoturge](Lore_Mycoturgy.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup> and a renowned master of arcane husbandry who created unique and exotic mounts and pets infused with fungal spores known as the [Gloomspore creatures](Lore_Gloomspore_Creature.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IGS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-G12-3)</sup> Her creations were sought after by scholars, adventurers, and anyone in need of a reliable and sturdy companion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-G12-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-G2-4)</sup> Using her extensive knowledge of [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) and mycoturgy, Mage Naleldil crafted a range of mounts with distinct features and properties. She integrated research left behind by [Ahzidal](Lore_Ahzidal.md), a grand [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) in her work.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-G4-5)</sup>

Among her notable creations were the Gloomspore [Bears](Lore_Bear.md), which were infused with [mora tapinella](Lore_Mora_Tapinella.md) to provide fortuitous protection to their riders,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-G12-3)</sup> and glowing bear cubs, crafted from spores acquired from traveling merchants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-G4-5)</sup>

The Gloomspore [Horses](Lore_Horse.md) were crafted with [giant lichen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Giant_Lichen) and were known for their beauty and cautionary nature, providing a sense of safety from ambushes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-G1-6)</sup> The pony-fungus creatures were made using manic morels from [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-G8-7)</sup>

Mage Naleldil also experimented with hypha facia to create Gloomspore [Wolves](Lore_Wolf.md), which were eerie mounts with howls that sent chills down the spine.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-G11-8)</sup> The wolf pups were created with oxblood fungus spores instead, to instill fierce guardianship.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-G7-9)</sup>

It was believed that Gloomspore [Guars](Lore_Guar.md), created with the use of [bleeding crown](Lore_Bleeding_Crown.md) were among the toughest creations of Naleldil. They were known for their thick fungal hide.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-G2-4)</sup> The Gloomspore Guar Calf was another popular creation that had calming properties, although it was known to be gassy.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-G10-10)</sup>

She utilized [scaly pholiota](Lore_Scaly_Pholiota.md) for the Gloomspore [Camels](Lore_Camel.md), which were designed to bear heavy loads without complaint.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-G6-11)</sup>

The Gloomspore [Senches](Lore_Senche.md) were created with thunder fungi,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-G5-12)</sup> and the Senche Cubs were known for their cute features.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-G9-13)</sup> She also created another breed of fungal Senche, known as Nighthaunts. They were imbued by the power of cult of [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) curiosities that intensified their glow and gave them a grim appearance. However, Mage Naleldil insisted that they were not evil.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-G3-14)</sup>

Naleldil's arcane studies were not limited to mycoturgy. She discovered an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ritual that granted luminescent qualities to common [salamanders](Lore_Salamander.md), which, due to an unexpected population explosion, she was known to gift to those who impressed her.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IGS-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Gloomspore creatures also occur naturally in the caverns of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-GC-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Blackreach-16)</sup>
- It is known that Gloomspore Weapons can be created with the usage of mycoturgy. It is unknown, however, whether or not Naleldil was their creator.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Gloomspore_Style-17)</sup>
