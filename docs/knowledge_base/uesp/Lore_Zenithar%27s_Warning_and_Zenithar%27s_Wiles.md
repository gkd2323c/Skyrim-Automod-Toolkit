# Zenithar's Warning and Zenithar's Wiles

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/MW-npc-Jon_Hawker.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Jon_Hawker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Jon_Hawker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Jon_Hawker.jpg) Jon Hawker Zenithar's Warning and Zenithar's Wiles are a set of gloves associated with the God of Commerce [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). They were imbued with magical properties and effects such as reducing an adversary's ability and willingness to fight, and increasing people's disposition towards the wearer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zenithar.27s_Warning-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zenithar.27s_Wiles-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RID-3)</sup>

## History
In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Zenithar appeared to the Nerevarine during their service to the [Imperial Cult](Lore_Imperial_Cult.md) as a Redguard named Jon Hawker. He was held prisoner at Nammu, a smuggler's cave in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md)'s [Grazelands](Lore_Grazelands.md). After he was freed, the Nerevarine received his gauntlets, Zenithar's Warning and Zenithar's Wiles as a reward. Jon Hawker said that his captors took everything except for the gloves, whose creator he expressed fondness for. He advised the Nerevarine to wear them in good health, and in remembrance of him.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RID-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-JH-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMP-5)</sup><sup>:173</sup>

## Artifacts

### Zenithar's Warning
Zenithar's Warning is the left glove of the set. It appears to be a common glove but it is imbued to reduce an adversary's ability and willingness to fight, blinding and silencing them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zenithar.27s_Warning-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMP-5)</sup>

### Zenithar's Wiles
Zenithar's Wiles is the right glove of the set. It appears to be a common glove but it is imbued to charm people and increase their disposition towards the wearer.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Zenithar.27s_Wiles-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TMP-5)</sup>
