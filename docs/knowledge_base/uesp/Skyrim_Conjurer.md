# Conjurer


The type of conjurer you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level conjurers even at high levels, but boss-level conjurers will always be at the highest possible level.

### Conjurers
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Conjurer_02.jpg) A Novice Conjurer blasting a gout of fire with a ward ready in his other hand Conjurers use Destruction magic and summon allies to fight for them in combat, and they carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), [orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger), [dwarven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Dagger), [elven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Dagger), [glass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glass_Dagger), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000c5d12) along with [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes), [hooded black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes), robes of [Alteration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Alteration), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Magicka_Rate).

In addition to the gear listed above, conjurers have a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). They also have [atronach forge recipes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atronach_Forge_Recipe) in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in-game is set at 0%.

On death, conjurers have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis)
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie)
- **Destruction:** [Scroll of Blizzard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard), [Scroll of Fire Storm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm)
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms), [Scroll of Harmony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony), [Scroll of Hysteria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria), [Scroll of Mayhem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem)
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle)

Master Conjurers and Arch Conjurers have the same gear as lower-level conjurers, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Chain Lightning](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Chain_Lightning), [Staff of Fireballs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs), or a [Staff of Ice Storms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Ice_Storms) and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

## Dialogue
All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Bugs
- Most conjurers will summon their atronachs before attacking you with Destruction magic, but conjurers with the Conjure Storm Atronach spell will not summon Storm Atronachs. This is likely due to an issue with their combat style script.
