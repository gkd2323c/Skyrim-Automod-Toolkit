# Stendarr's Hammer

Stendarr's Hammer is a hammer rumored to have once been wielded by [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md), the God of Justice.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWTorasa-1)</sup> One of his other titles, the God of Righteous Might,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> is fitting as the hammer is very heavy. It is made of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) and [sapphire](Lore_Sapphire.md), which can also be used to further temper it<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The weapon is enchanted to drain or damage the health of those it strikes, at the cost of the wielder's stamina.

Stendarr's Hammer was one of the first [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md) to be added to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md)'s museum in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md). The hammer is too heavy for most people to use in combat; it took four men to simply lift the artifact onto its display podium in the museum. Its origins are unknown, but it was still in Aram's possession circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWTorasa-1)</sup>

Sometime after the end of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md), circa [4E 180](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_180), the [Warrior](Lore_Hero.md) came across Stendarr's Hammer in their journey. The hammer is thought to have been looted from an extravagant chest.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

In the year [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the hammer was found deep under [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md). It was acquired by [Calcelmo](Lore_Calcelmo_of_Markarth.md) and put on display in his private [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) museum in Markarth, though it was stolen soon after he acquired it.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
