[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Solitude.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude.jpg) Solitude **King** Hylfrald was the ruler of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). He ruled from his seat of power in [Solitude](Lore_Solitude.md). Little is known of his reign, except that as of then [Reachfolk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Reachfolk) marauders were active at the borders of his kingdom, northwest of [Karthwasten](Lore_Karthwasten.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FCR-1)</sup>

One of his vassals, Falkfyr Snowmason was sent on a mission to track down the band of the Reachmen marauders. He ventured into the mountains but was trapped in a massive cavern due to a blizzard and collapsed ground. He never returned and died in the caverns of [Blackreach](Lore_Blackreach.md), underneath [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FCR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Quest-2)</sup>
