[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-npc-Alvasorr_the_Rat.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alvasorr_the_Rat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvasorr_the_Rat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alvasorr_the_Rat.jpg) Alvasorr the Rat **Alvasorr the Rat** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) priest of Namira, who is found in [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md).

Alvasorr wears a set of [Falmer Armor](Skyrim_Falmer_Armor.md) and the [Dismal Visage](Skyrim_Dismal_Visage.md). He has a [Falmer Bow](Skyrim_Falmer_Bow.md) and the unique Falmer axe [Namira's Itch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Namira%27s_Itch), and carries up to 10 [Falmer Arrows](Skyrim_Falmer_Arrow.md). He knows a level-dependent elemental attack spell, a healing spell, a ward spell and an armor spell.
