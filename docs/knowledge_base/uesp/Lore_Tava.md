# Tava

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/ON-place-Tava%27s_Beak.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tava%27s_Beak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tava%27s_Beak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tava%27s_Beak.jpg) Rock carving of Tava Tava (the **Bird God**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VOF-2)</sup> also known as the **Lady of the Air**) is the spirit of the air and goddess of weather in the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) pantheon, and has dominion over all elements of sun and storm, save only for the stars, which belong to [Ruptga](Lore_Ruptga.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Tava is most famous for leading the Yokudans to the isle of [Herne](Lore_Herne_(island).md) after the destruction of their homeland. She has since become assimilated into the mythology of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md). She is still very popular in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) among sailors, and her shrines can be found in most port cities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VOF-2)</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the city of [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn.md) was called "Tava's Blessing" before being renamed after the Lainlyn family.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AUA-4)</sup>

The redtail [hawk](Lore_Hawk.md) is considered sacred to Tava, as is the goshawk.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONWriting Quill-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWAHD-6)</sup> Conversely, [harpies](Lore_Harpy.md) are seen as abominations by her faithful.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONHarpy Egg-7)</sup>

## Worship
The second [Warrior Wave](Lore_Ra_Gada.md#The_Warrior_Waves), or "Tavan" wave, so-called because it was spearheaded by the [Hattu](Lore_Hattu_Mountain.md) Metropoles brigade, which revered Tava greatly, had their arms and armor repleted with bird imagery.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Contraband_R-9)</sup>

One of [Hegathe](Lore_Hegathe.md)'s most iconic landmarks is the Shrine of Tava, a place of veneration for both the [Crowns](Lore_Crowns.md) and the [Forebears](Lore_Forebears.md). It is highlighted by the *Fresco of the [Goshawk](Lore_Goshawk.md)*, a mural depicting Tava nestled together with his mates, which was meant to symbolize the union of the two factions.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWAHD-6)</sup>

Priests of Tava are known to bless astrolabes for Redguard high rank royal naval officers.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Contraband B-10)</sup>
