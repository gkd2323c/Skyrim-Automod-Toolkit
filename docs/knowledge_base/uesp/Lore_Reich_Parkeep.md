[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/AR-place-Reich_Parkeep.png/200px-AR-place-Reich_Parkeep.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Reich_Parkeep.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Reich_Parkeep.png) Reich Parkeep Reich Parkeep (also called **Reich Parthkeep**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEIII-1)</sup> and **Selethis**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-2)</sup> is a settlement in the northwestern section of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup> in the highlands near the [Velothi Mountains](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md) and under the jurisdiction of [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-2)</sup>

The village is mentioned in the historical fiction series [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md). On the road, [Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md) and [Mith](Lore_Mith.md) find a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) slave abused by his master near Reich Parthkeep and freed him from his service. The slave, however, decided to follow them ever since. He was hence known as Moraelyn's Slave despite becoming equals. He also became known as [Mats](Lore_Mats.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-KEIII-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Reich Parkeep was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Helkan and had a rivalry with Cormar View and [Verarchen Hall](Lore_Verarchen_Hall.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AR-4)</sup> Reich Parkeep was neighbored by several settlements, such as [Cormar View](Lore_Cormar_View.md) to the north-northeast and [Silgrad Tower](Lore_Silgrad_Tower.md) to the south.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup>
