## Windhelm Military Camp
It features several small tents, which contain no bed rolls or hay piles. Several tree stumps are scattered in the area, with one near the central campfire being a [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md). In the middle of the camp is a table holding Imperial weapons, as well as an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). To the southeast past most of the tents is another campfire. Up the hill to the southwest is another small tent, a large empty tent, and the command tent, containing a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. In the western section of the camp, three catapults are aimed at Windhelm.

When this camp is not present, a hunter's camp will be here instead.
