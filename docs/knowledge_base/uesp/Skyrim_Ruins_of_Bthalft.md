[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ruins_of_Bthalft.jpg) Ruins of Bthalft The **Ruins of Bthalft** are a small [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) south of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md).

The hidden entrance to the [Aetherium Forge](Skyrim_The_Aetherium_Forge.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> can be found here.

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-quest-Lost_to_the_Ages_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Lost_to_the_Ages_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Lost_to_the_Ages_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Lost_to_the_Ages_05.jpg) The entrance to the Aetherium Forge This location is used by three (two without [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) installed) bandits as their camp. Due to the ruin's proximity to [Rift Imperial Camp](Skyrim_Rift_Imperial_Camp.md), the bandits and soldiers often engage each other. There are arches at the northern and southern ends of the camp with hanging [bone chimes](Skyrim_Bone_Chimes.md). Between the arches is a stone-floored area with several crates, barrels, and sacks. A third arch to the southeast leads up to the statue containing the machinery to gain access to the Aetherium Forge. Near the northern end are wooden stairs leading up to an adept-locked wooden [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), some crates, a barrel containing [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md), and a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sack. A [Dwemer Convector](Skyrim_Dwemer_Convector.md), used for [Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md), is behind the wall to the north between the two arches.

With Dawnguard installed, a tower can be raised after finding all four [Aetherium Shards](Skyrim_Aetherium_Shard.md) during [Lost to the Ages](Skyrim_Lost_to_the_Ages.md). It gives access to the Aetherium Forge.

## Notes
- One [corundum ore](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore.md) vein is east of the circular area.
- Without Dawnguard installed, some hanging [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids and [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) can be harvested here.

## Bugs
- Although the bandits aren't supposed to respawn at this location after Dawnguard is installed, the bandits can still respawn even with the add-on.
- Due to a rare inventory reference corruption bug, it may be possible to attempt to place an Aetherium Shard on the pedestal here with the Dawnguard add-on and have the pedestal instead remove a different item from your inventory. As a result, Lost to the Ages will then become stuck and you will be unable to open the entry into the Aetherium Forge. The removed item is permanently lost, and the shards are then permanently stuck in your inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. The DLC1LD_Aetherium Pedestal script has been altered to deal directly with the Misc Object base forms instead of using alias reference checks. This allows the quest to continue on with proper activation of the pedestal and removal of the actual Aetherium Shards. This ISN'T a fix the underlying inventory reference bug that caused this. It is only a workaround to prevent this quest from getting blocked by it.
