Wormmouths are armless bipedal reptiles native to [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). They are related to [guar](Lore_Guar.md), [alits](Lore_Alit.md), and [kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-1)</sup> These vermin have a maggot-shaped body propelled on two legs, and have a beaked mouth that is almost as large as the rest of their bodies, making them resemble a mouth on legs. Sharp teeth line the inside of their mouths, and their backs are lined in plates of armored scales.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Wormmouths appear to range in height from the size of a large [rat](Lore_Rat.md) to as tall as an alit.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESO-3)</sup> Due to Hammerfell's arid environment, wormmouths get their fluids from the plants and animals they eat,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-1)</sup> but larger ones are also known to prey on people.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-2)</sup>

Though wormmouths typically dwell in the arid regions of Hammerfell,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-1)</sup> the Nubbed Wormmouth is known to dwell in the mountains located along the cold border between [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and Hammerfell.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Shadowkey-2)</sup> Wormmouths were also known to stray into the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), near the border to [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md). They even inhabited [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOMW-4)</sup>

Wormmouths are sometimes kept as pets.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Wormmouth tongues are sometimes harvested for [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) purposes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
