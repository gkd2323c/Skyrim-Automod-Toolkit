[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/ON-interior-Shalidor%27s_Shrouded_Realm.jpg/200px-ON-interior-Shalidor%27s_Shrouded_Realm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Shalidor%27s_Shrouded_Realm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-interior-Shalidor%27s_Shrouded_Realm.jpg) Shalidor's Shrouded Realm Shalidor's Shrouded Realm is a small pocket realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) created by the Arch-Mage [Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md) in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). It is a realm of arboreal forest, floating in a wondrous sky which bathes the terrain in an eternal purple light.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup> Several waterfalls cascade into the void below, and the realm is seemingly home to birds. Ancient [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) infrastructure was built upon this floating island, including two tall towers with a barrow below.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

Shalidor created his Shrouded Realm as an early experiment, prior to his work on [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md) and the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOLoad-1)</sup> The realm can be entered by passing through the entrance to what appears to be an ancient Nordic barrow, located northeast of the ruins of [Labyrinthian](Lore_Labyrinthian.md) where Shalidor constructed his famous Labyrinth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRUrag-3)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the realm was available for purchase as a private residence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
