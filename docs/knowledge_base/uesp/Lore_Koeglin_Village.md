[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/ON-place-Koeglin_Village.jpg/200px-ON-place-Koeglin_Village.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Koeglin_Village.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Koeglin_Village.jpg) Koeglin circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Koeglin Village (or simply Koeglin) is a port town in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), located in the [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire.md) region on the western edge of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md), southeast of the pass that leads to [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

The village is surrounded by farmlands to the north. A lighthouse is located on a small island off the coast of Koeglin.

An ebony mine used to be exploited by the people of Koeglin. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md) [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) sent an expedition to the old mine to check if the crystals found there were suitable to create [soul gems](Lore_Soul_Gem.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The same year, an expedition of [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) slavers known as the Ember-Eye Slavers, raided Koeglin and the nearby port of Steelheart Moorings, using the mine as hideout for their operations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> It was also plagued by [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md)'s nightmares.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-FTJ-6)</sup>

## Notes
- Unused texts, cut from the final version of [Elder Scrolls Online](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), suggest that Koeglin Village in the Second Era corresponds to the city of [Koegria](Lore_Koegria.md) of the Third Era.
