**Heart of Order** is an ingredient added by the [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). It can be purchased from [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md). With the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) Creation installed, [Thoron](Skyrim_Thoron.md) will be in possession of two guaranteed samples.
