Staada's Helmet is a unique [Golden Saint Helmet](Skyrim_Golden_Saint_Helmet.md) worn by [Staada](Skyrim_Staada.md). Staada will reward you with her helmet during the [Staada Quest](Skyrim_Staada_Quest.md) if you give her either the [Sheogorath-Shaped Amber](Skyrim_Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber.md) or the [Sword of Jyggalag](Skyrim_Sword_of_Jyggalag.md). The helmet [increases](Skyrim_Fortify_Stamina.md) the wearer's [stamina](Skyrim_Stamina.md) by 70 points.

## Related Quests
- **[Staada Quest](Skyrim_Staada_Quest.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>: Meet with a mysterious figure regarding a [mad wizard](Skyrim_Thoron.md)'s failed ritual.

## Bugs
- Unlike regular Golden Saint Helmets, Staada's Helmet cannot be tempered.
- It is possible to obtain multiple copies of Staada's Helmet during the related quest. After choosing to give Staada the Sword of Jyggalag, you can exit the conversation and drop the sword before it is removed from your inventory. You will still receive the helmet and can pick up the sword again to repeat the glitch. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.318, fixes this bug.
