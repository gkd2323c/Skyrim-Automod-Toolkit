[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/ON-place-Isle_of_Contemplation.jpg/200px-ON-place-Isle_of_Contemplation.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Isle_of_Contemplation.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Isle_of_Contemplation.jpg) The Isle of Contemplation circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Isle of Contemplation is a small island in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), located off the northern coast of [Vafe](Lore_Vafe.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IOC-1)</sup> It is a recursive island, being found within a lake completely within [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), whose waters flow from the [Diceto River](Lore_Diceto_River.md) to the [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IOC-1)</sup> It is described as magical and serene, and one of the most beautiful places on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> This island was the namesake of a kind of [Indriks](Lore_Indrik.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IOCI-3)</sup>

One of the last members of the [Veiled Heritance](Lore_Veiled_Heritance.md) on Summerset sought refuge on the isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FB-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-K-5)</sup>
