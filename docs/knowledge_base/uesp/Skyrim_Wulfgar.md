[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/32/SR-npc-Wulfgar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Wulfgar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wulfgar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Wulfgar.jpg) Wulfgar **Wulfgar**, an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest), is one of the reclusive [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) living at the monastery at [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md), and one of your initial tutors in the [Way of the Voice](Lore_Way_of_the_Voice.md). Wulfgar will react when interacted with, greeting you with a muttered *"Dovahkiin"* and causing the surroundings to rumble in doing so.

He wears a set of [Greybeard's robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes) with the matching [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Hood) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots). He carries a [wooden bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wooden_Bowl), a [knife](Skyrim_Knife_(item).md) and a [fork](Skyrim_Fork_(item).md), a [quill](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quill), a [roll of paper](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roll_of_Paper), a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [venison chop](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Chop), a jar of [honey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and a piece of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread).

Wulfgar knows the following shouts: [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md), [Dismay](Skyrim_Dismay.md), [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md), [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md), [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md), [Ice Form](Skyrim_Ice_Form.md), [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md), and [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md).
