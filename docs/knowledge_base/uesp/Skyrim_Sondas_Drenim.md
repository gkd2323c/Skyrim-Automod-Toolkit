[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/53/SR-npc-Sondas_Drenim.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sondas_Drenim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sondas_Drenim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sondas_Drenim.jpg) Sondas Drenim **Sondas Drenim** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [food vendor](Skyrim_Food_Vendor.md) and a resident of [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) who works as a miner at [Goldenrock Mine](Skyrim_Goldenrock_Mine.md). He's on friendly terms with his fellow miners and seems to be happy with the settlement's lakeside location. According to [Hrefna](Skyrim_Hrefna.md), Sondas takes care of her if her [mother](Skyrim_Tormir.md) is away.

He goes to bed early, at 9pm, only to wake up early on the following day at 4am. He will then wander around the camp for three hours, until it's time for his breakfast at 7am. After his breakfast, Sondas will start his long workday at 8am and head into the mine. The rest of his day will be spent mining, only interrupted by his final meal of the day at 5pm.

Sondas wears a set of barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a1) along with a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0005b6a0). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) along with a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
When spoken to, Sondas will talk about general mining things, as well as his [homeland](Lore_Morrowind.md):

- *"I try to think of it as coaxing the ore from the rock."*
- *"I keep hearing stories about Morrowind. Sounds like a wretched place."*
- *"I hear there's baked slaughterfish tonight."*

Sondas is also willing to speak about other topics, such as the ongoing [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md), as well some general information about the settlement:

- **Has the war affected you?**
- *"I'm lucky enough that none of my friends have been killed. Of course we have soldiers coming through all the time."*

- **What can you tell me about Darkwater Crossing?**
- *"[Those falls](Skyrim_Darkwater_Pass.md)... dangerous place. [Derkeethus](Skyrim_Derkeethus.md) thought he could find some good fish in the pools. Haven't heard from him in a long while."*

## Quest-Related Events

### Delivery
If asked if he's worked in the mine long, Sondas will bring up a problem:

- **Have you been working in the mines long?**
- *"This mine is only a few years old, but I've been digging up ore for over a century. We're getting to the point where the tunnels are getting deep into the rock. People complain of bad coughs and foul air. I've seen this before. We need some medicines to combat the ore dust. I was about to send a [message](Skyrim_Sondas%27s_Note.md) to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) for aid."*

- - **I could deliver that for you.**
- *"Thank you. Take it to [Quintus](Skyrim_Quintus_Navale.md), in the White Phial."*

- - **That's a shame.**
- *"We'll get by."*

Once delivered, Quintus will reward you, but Sondas will never mention it again. However, he will become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

## Conversations
Sondas can be heard talking to Hrefna or her mother Tormir:

- **Hrefna**: *"Sondas, I've heard about Morrowind."*
- **Sondas**: *"Oh? What about it?"*
- **Hrefna**: *"That it was black and gross and nobody wanted to live there anymore."*
- **Sondas**: *"I'll have to take your word on that one, my dear."*

- **Hrefna**: *"Do you ever want to go to Morrowind, Sondas?"*
- **Sondas**: *"I've been in Skyrim my whole life. Why would I want to leave?"*
- **Hrefna**: *"To see the fires!"*
- **Sondas**: *"I think I like the lake here just a little more."*

- **Tormir**: *"Thanks for looking after Hrefna."*
- **Sondas**: *"Oh, it's no trouble. She's a smart little girl. Might talk your ear off, but she's fun."*
- **Tormir**: *"Well, I know she thinks the world of you."*
- **Sondas**: *"Well, I'll try to be a good influence, then."*

- **Tormir**: *"Sondas, does Hrefna ever mention Meieran to you?"*
- **Sondas**: *"She hardly ever mentions anything else."*
- **Tormir**: *"I'm just worried about what he's teaching her."*
- **Sondas**: *"I wouldn't worry about that. He's a good elf. I trust him."*
- **Tormir**: *"You're probably right."*

## Notes
- Sondas was at one point intended to be an innkeeper, as shown by the internal names of his scenes with Tormir, which are all prefixed with "**Dialogue Darkwater Crossing Inn**". His class and outfit seem to be remnants of this.
- Should you choose to marry Sondas and move to one of your houses, you will see Hrefna in your house every so often since her AI will have her seek out Sondas, regardless of his location.
- Both Sondas and Tormir will speak of a male elf called "Meieran". He cannot be found around Darkwater Crossing, however, and doesn't even exist in the game at all. - Version 2.0.4 of the [Unofficial Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) alters all dialogue that refers to Meieran to instead refer to Derkeethus, as such, the scene with between them gets altered to:

- **Tormir**: *"Sondas, does Hrefna ever mention your friend to you?"*
- **Sondas**: *"She hardly ever mentions anything else."*
- **Tormir**: *"I'm just worried about what he's teaching her."*
- **Sondas**: *"I wouldn't worry about that. Derkeethus is a good man. I trust him."*
- **Tormir**: *"You're probably right."*
