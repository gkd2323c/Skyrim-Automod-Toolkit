[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-place-Western_Watchtower.jpg/200px-SR-place-Western_Watchtower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Western_Watchtower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Western_Watchtower.jpg) The Western Watchtower The **Western Watchtower** is a small [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) west of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md)**: Investigate a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) sighting near [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

## Walkthrough
The tower is along the main road heading west from Whiterun, allowing the [Whiterun guards](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md) in the area to watch for any potential threats approaching from that direction. It can be entered via the crumbling wall that forms a ramp upwards. Outside the tower the area between the fallen walls contains a training dummy with some Iron Arrows shot into it. Inside the tower are two unowned bedrolls and a small table with a lantern. The second floor contains an archery target and a bale of straw, while the top floor is empty. There are two [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) banners flying from the tower.

Four Whiterun guards will be stationed at the tower to keep watch for enemies (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Once the quest [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) is complete, the area between the fallen walls contains two burnt corpses with loot and the charred remains of a wooden hut.

## Notes
- There are two [iron ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) in the rocks to the west.
- The Western Watchtower will not be marked as a location on your map until the quest Dragon Rising begins.
- The Imperial Legion banners will be replaced by the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) banners if the [Stormcloaks capture Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun_(Stormcloaks).md) during the [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) questline. - Version 1.3.3 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) replaces the default Imperial Legion banners with yellow Whiterun banners to better reflect that Whiterun Hold is originally neutral in the Civil War questline.
- Version 4.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) replaces Western Watchtower's fort map icon with the one used for [Imperial towers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Towers).
