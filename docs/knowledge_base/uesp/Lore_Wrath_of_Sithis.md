The Wrath of Sithis refers to the mysterious punishment threatened by [*The Five Tenets*](Lore_The_Five_Tenets.md) of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md). It is a [spirit](Lore_Spirit.md) of vengeance that acts as a conduit for [Sithis](Lore_Sithis.md)' wrath, targeting Dark Brotherhood members that break one of the Five Tenets.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBExile-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBWrath-2)</sup> It often takes the appearance of a [gloom wraith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gloom_Wraith),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBWrath-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> or a [shadowy](Lore_Shadow.md) [ghost](Lore_Ghost.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The Wrath of Sithis is said to be a worse punishment than expulsion for traitors of the Brotherhood. The spirit has been known to take the very [souls](Lore_Souls.md) of its targets.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TFT-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOWrath-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Those who have been exiled from the Dark Brotherhood will be allowed to rejoin if they survive a visit from the Wrath of Sithis.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBExile-1)</sup> This can only occur twice; individuals become forever exiled if they break one of the Tenets a third time, even if they manage to slay the spirit.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBEternal-9)</sup>

In addition to the curses and magical attacks typical of other gloom wraiths, the Wrath of Sithis typically wields an enchanted [silver](Lore_Silver.md) dagger known as the Dagger of Discipline. These daggers can be claimed by those who defeat the spirit and survive its visitations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBWrath-2)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

## History
The list of the spirit's victims include the [Black Dragon](Lore_Black_Dragon.md), a former Silencer who betrayed the Brotherhood and assassinated many of its members on behalf of the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md). Following her defeat in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), her soul was claimed by Sithis and dragged into the [Void](Lore_Void.md) by his Wrath.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOWrath-7)</sup>

The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) may or may not have slain the Wrath of Sithis one or more times in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBExile-1)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBAnother-11)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OBEternal-9)</sup>
