Tullar-dra was the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) people.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azahrr-1)</sup> The exact period of his rule is unknown but it was before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Little is known about Tullar-dra's life.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tullar-dra-2)</sup>

He died at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). His remains are preserved in the Tomb of the Manes in [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) Necropolis, a huge tomb complex located southwest of the Rimmen itself. He was buried together with other prominent Manes: [Zebiden-jo](Lore_Zebiden-jo.md) and [Jinninji-ri](Lore_Jinninji-ri.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tullar-dra-2)</sup> His spirit's rest was briefly interrupted in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) escorted the remains of the ancient Khajiiti ruler Jora-ri to the burial chamber in the Tomb of the Manes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jora-ri-4)</sup>
