[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/OB-place-Camp_Ales.jpg/200px-OB-place-Camp_Ales.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Camp_Ales.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Camp_Ales.jpg) A camp in the Imperial Reserve (Oblivion) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/OBR-place-Camp_Ales.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Camp_Ales.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Camp_Ales.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Camp_Ales.jpg) A camp in the Imperial Reserve (Oblivion Remastered) The Imperial Reserve is a grassland area extending across the southern section of the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md). It lies west of the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) and extends west towards the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). The Imperial Reserve is sparsely populated, containing only a few small hamlets such as [Hackdirt](Lore_Hackdirt.md) and [Weatherleah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Weatherleah). Aside from that, the Reserve is dotted with many small bandit camps, and is known as a refuge for outlaws and [Daedra](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) worshippers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The Reserve is prime hunting ground due to its flat land and sparse trees. It is home to abundant game, including [deer](Lore_Deer.md), [antelopes](Lore_Antelope.md), [pheasants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pheasant), and [boars](Lore_Boar.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AHJ2-5)</sup>

The Reserve was established during the reign of Emperor [Brazollus Dor](Lore_Brazollus_Dor.md) in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AHJ2-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The Emperor spent more time hunting in the Colovian Highlands than ruling the [Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md); indeed, his only official act was to declare a vast swath of the plains his own personal game reserve. This lasted for about a century, but the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) subsequently neglected to enforce this claim, and local [Colovians](Lore_Colovian.md) began to once again hunt the Emperor's deer.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AHJ2-5)</sup>
