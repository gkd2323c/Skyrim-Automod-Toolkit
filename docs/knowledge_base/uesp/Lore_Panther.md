*Many an unwary traveler has met an untimely end at the claws of a Senche-Panther under the thick canopies of Valenwood. This solitary distant relative of the Khajiit has an uncanny intelligence and rarely befriends Men or Elves, but it remains a lifelong companion to any it deems worthy.*
—A description of a Senche-Panther<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-1)</sup> Panthers, more specifically **Black Panthers**,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bl Body Mrk-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dark Spirit-3)</sup> are a type of large, predatory [feline](Lore_Cat.md) found in the forested regions of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Senche-Panther-4)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-1)</sup> Panthers are solitary intelligent hunters,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-1)</sup> who are known for their distinctive black fur.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bl Body Mrk-2)</sup>

Many of the cats known as panthers in southern Tamriel are actually a type of [Senche-Cat](Lore_Senche-Cat.md), known as a **Senche-Panther** or **Black Senche-Panther**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Panther-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONShadowghost Senche-6)</sup> differentiated by their larger size.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-7)</sup> The two species may often be confused however due to their similar appearance.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Tuttle-7)</sup> Some [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) are noted as panther-like,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup> having fur colorations that resemble those of panthers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Bl Body Mrk-2)</sup>

## Variants
Striped Senche-Panthers possess stripes as their name implies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStriped Senche Panther-1)</sup> Shadowghost Senches are an oversized breed of black senche-panthers, differing visually in their white spots.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONShadowghost Senche-6)</sup> Indigo Senche-Panthers possess black fur with a purple sheen. Rumors claim that when left to their own devices, they stalk [werewolves](Lore_Werewolf.md) for amusement.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONInidigo Senche-9)</sup>

## Uses and Culture

### Domestication
Senche-Panthers are often used as pets or mounts. Black Senche-Panthers are said to have bred as mounts by [Baandari Pedlars](Lore_Baandari_Pedlars.md) for the ease of leaving towns after dark atop one.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONBlack Senche Panther-5)</sup> Indigo Senche-Panthers were bred as mounts for a reclusive family in [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONInidigo Senche-9)</sup> [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) and those whom they associate with employ the services of Kynbound Senche-Panthers.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Kynbound-10)</sup>

### Uses
Some [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) ladies use sun parasols made out of panther skin to keep their scales from drying out.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Parasol-11)</sup>

### Art, Religion, and Influence
Black panthers and senche-panthers are sometimes the focus of Khajiit myths. [Clan-Mother Ahnissi](Lore_Ahnissi.md) taught how [Rajhin the Purring Liar](Lore_Rajhin.md) had so many shadows that some of them took on lives of their own, becoming black senche-panthers who stalk freely through Elsweyr.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Kitten-12)</sup> The Khajiit once acknowledged an unnamed dark spirit, born from [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md)'s grief after the death of [Fadomai](Lore_Fadomai.md) and [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md), that sometimes appears in songs as a black panther, a [warrior in ebony armor](Lore_Ebonarm.md), or a hidden sword.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Dark Spirit-3)</sup> Shadowghost Senches are associated with the Dark Spirit [Namiira](Lore_Namiira.md), who is thought to send them as mounts to bear away those who are doomed to die.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONShadowghost Senche-6)</sup>

The [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) [Hollowjack](Lore_Hollowjack.md) has some association with cats,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-13)</sup> and senche-panthers are known to be found in his realm of [Detritus](Lore_Detritus.md).<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-15)</sup>

Panthers are the namesake of the [Panther River](Lore_Panther_River.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). There is a Khajiit saying that goes "You can only yank a senche-panther's tail so many times before it turns and gobbles you up."<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-16)</sup> In the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) archipelago, there are [obsidian](Lore_Obsidian.md) rods with tips shaped like a panther's claw, known as Claw Scepters. The purpose of these implements is unknown.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Scepter-17)</sup>
