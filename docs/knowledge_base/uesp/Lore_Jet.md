# Jet

[![](https://images.uesp.net/b/b0/ON-concept-Prince-Nocturnal-emblem.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Prince-Nocturnal-emblem.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Prince-Nocturnal-emblem.png) An emblem representing Nocturnal Jet is a rough, dark gemstone.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TN-1)</sup>

Enchanted jet gemstones were occasionally imbued with the ability to emit a soft weeping sound when exposed to light, making them especially favored among [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md)'s worshippers, as she is the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) associated with darkness, night, and thievery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TN-1)</sup>

The term "jet black" originates from the stone jet and is used to describe the hue of many dark-colored objects and materials such as [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jet1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jet2-3)</sup> and [onyx](Lore_Onyx.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TG-4)</sup> and even hair colors.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-RB-5)</sup>
