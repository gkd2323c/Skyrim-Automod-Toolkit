# Knights of the Owl

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/DF-map-Glenpoint.png/200px-DF-map-Glenpoint.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Glenpoint.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Glenpoint.png) Map of Glenpoint The Knights of the Owl are a [Breton Knightly Order](Lore_Breton_Knightly_Orders.md) responsible for protecting and fighting for the royal family of [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md). Only those of the most refined fighting skills and dedicated to the welfare of Glenpoint are considered for inclusion in the knightly order. The knights are the royal escorts of the Baron and treated with unparalleled respect in all of Glenpoint.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-D2-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>:100</sup>
