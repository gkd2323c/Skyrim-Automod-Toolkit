*A dreary place, don't you agree? And guarded by a pompous upstart -- he's no more a god than I am.*
— [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) The Blind God is an obscure god who ruled over and guarded the [Mantellan Crux](Lore_Mantellan_Crux.md), a realm of [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> He was known to get quite jealous, and [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), who was a known visitor to this realm and had dealings with the Blind God, considered him to be a pompous upstart of a god.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> He manifested as a large skull with empty eye sockets, and had many smaller skulls in his service.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> Circa [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417) an unknown [agent](Lore_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) infiltrated the Crux with the express purpose of attaining the [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md). Among numerous other obstacles, this agent passed the trial the Blind God set forth and ultimately retrieved the Mantella.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup>
