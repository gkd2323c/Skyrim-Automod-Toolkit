Letter from Harkon **by [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)** *Harkon seeks a Moth Priest*
---
The prophesied time has come. I have reclaimed one my *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* lost Elder Scrolls, and now I must have a [Moth Priest](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) to read it. For the one who brings me a Moth Priest, I promise a high place in my court and the gift of my potent blood.

Go forth and find me a Moth Priest. This is my command.

[Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md)
