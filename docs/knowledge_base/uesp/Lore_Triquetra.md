# Triquetra


The [Clever Women](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Clever_Men) of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) are keepers of an ancient ceremony whereby blood is bound to the tides through the use of runes, thereby transforming one's skin to mirror the sea's restless waters. They are the only group known to possess that knowledge and the secrets of the Runic Seabrume. One of the symbols engraved on the skin is the triquetra.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-RS-7)</sup>

The triquetra was also used as a symbol by the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleids.md). For them, it symbolized land, sea, and sky. It served as the emblem of House [Dynar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Laloriaran_Dynar), the royal house of [Nenalata](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nenalata); the symbol of a triquetra carved into [meteoric iron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Meteoric_Iron) has been found on torcs made by the Ayleid jewelcrafters of Nenalata.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MIT-8)</sup> The symbol is also present in [Eld Angavar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eld_Angavar), an abandoned realm created by a clan of [Barsaebic Ayleids](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barsaebic_Ayleids).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EA-9)</sup>

By the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the triquetra was also used on [Cyrodilic](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) shortswords.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
