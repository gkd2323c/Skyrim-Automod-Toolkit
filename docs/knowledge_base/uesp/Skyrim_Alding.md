[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-npc-Alding.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alding.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alding.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alding.jpg) Alding **Alding** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md). Along with [Gjuk](Skyrim_Gjuk.md) and [Irlof](Skyrim_Irlof.md), he forms the crew of [Blood Horker](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md) Captain [Stig Salt-Plank](Skyrim_Stig_Salt-Plank.md). He and the rest of the crew only exist during [Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md), after you give the logbook to [Orthus Endario](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orthus_Endario), where they are found in the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md), partaking in their regular "festivities".

After the initial scene plays out, he will be found sitting in place at a bench inside of the inn, enjoying a tankard of mead or eating bread.

He only wears a [roughspun tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roughspun_Tunic) with some [footwraps](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Footwraps).

## Related Quests
- **[Rise in the East](Skyrim_Rise_in_the_East.md)**: Help the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:East_Empire_Company) take care of some [pirates](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md).

## Conversations
When you enter the inn, you will find him and Stig harassing the local bard and barmaid, [Karita](Skyrim_Karita_(bard).md):

**Karita**: *"You beasts are the worst."*
**Stig**: *"Come now, dearie, surely we aren't all that bad."*
**Karita**: *"Just... leave me alone! I've brought your mead, now drink it and shut your filthy mouths."*
**Alding**: *"Oh, we've got other things in mind for our mouths, sweetie."*
