# Lurker

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-creature-Lurker.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Lurker.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lurker.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lurker.jpg) A Lurker **Lurkers** are Daedric creatures that come from the murky waters of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s realm of [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md). They appear as giant amphibious humanoids, and serve their Prince by guarding forbidden knowledge. Lurkers attack with swipes of their huge hands, and use their long legs for stomping attacks, which have a 100 point [stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md) effect within 16 feet. They can also attack from afar by spewing tentacles from their mouths. They are very dangerous opponents, and should be fought one at a time. They also have the ability to regenerate health.

## Notes
- The [Soul Tear](Skyrim_Soul_Tear.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> shout will successfully revive Lurkers as allies, unlike [Seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md).
- The [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout will not knock down a Lurker, even with all three words.
- Lurkers are susceptible to [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralysis.md) so poisons of Paralysis can be very useful in taking them down. Even though they have a resistance to poisons, poisons of [lingering damage health](Skyrim_Lingering_Damage_Health.md) can help combat their constant health regeneration, especially notable when playing on a harder difficulty (Expert and up). If you utilize the [fortify restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) glitch, you can make poisons which will easily counter their fast health regeneration, or keep them paralyzed for a long time. Similarly, you can also stack the [Marked for Death](Skyrim_Marked_for_Death.md) and [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md) Shouts to gain a further edge.
- Lurkers can get knocked off/can fall off whatever platform they may be standing on and sink to the bottom of the brackish "water" in certain chapters of Apocrypha. Once this happens, the only way to find where exactly they are is through the [Aura Whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) Shout and/or the [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life.md) spell. Once it is dead, it is advisable to *not* enter the water to find the body as the water is opaque black and finding the body will be nearly impossible.
