## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Septimus Signus](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus.md).
2. Find the [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md) in [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md).
3. Fill the [Blank Lexicon](Skyrim_Blank_Lexicon.md).
4. Give the Lexicon to Septimus Signus.
5. Harvest blood from a High Elf, Wood Elf, Dark Elf, Falmer, and Orc.
6. Give the collected blood samples to Septimus Signus.
7. Collect the [Oghma Infinium](Skyrim_Oghma_Infinium.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Signus's Gesture
Far north of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), there is a small cave called [Septimus Signus's Outpost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost). Inside, a strange [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) cube is being studied by [Septimus Signus](Skyrim_Septimus_Signus.md). He claims the "heart of a God," "heart of you and me," is located inside.

He asks you to help him see through the cube. To do so, he plans to use the power of an [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll.md). However, he does not possess the knowledge to use the Elder Scroll. To help him, you must travel to the Dwemer ruin [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) and charge the Lexicon.

He gives you two items:

- [Attunement Sphere](Skyrim_Attunement_Sphere.md)
- [Blank Lexicon](Skyrim_Blank_Lexicon.md)

### Alftand
Be prepared: Alftand is an extensive Dwemer ruin occupied by [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) and Dwemer constructs, including [Centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md). Once there, travel through the ruin, following your destination marker, until you reach Blackreach, a massive underground cavern below Alftand. Alternatively, if you do not feel your level or equipment are sufficient to penetrate Alftand, it is actually unnecessary to do so; [Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md) and [Raldbthar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar) both access Blackreach as long as the player has an Attunement Sphere, and both are faster and easier than Alftand. A more complete walkthrough can be found on the Alftand and [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) pages.

At this point you will have access to a [Dwemer lift](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Alftand.md) for quick travel between Blackreach and the surface, in case you want to make a trip back to town before delving further underground. When you are ready, follow the quest marker through Blackreach until you reach your destination, The [Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md). Inside, you will find the Oculory, a large Dwemer machine that occupies the last room of the Tower. Go up the curving stairs along either wall to find a control panel with a stand that has a slot for the Lexicon. Place the Lexicon atop the stand to activate the machine's control panel.

To decipher the code, press the second button from the right four times, pausing between each pressing to allow the Oculory to complete its realignment. This will cause the Lexicon to open and the third button from the right be revealed. Now press the third button twice, which will align the lenses such that they direct shafts of light onto the round, green windows of the Oculory, and also uncover the far left button. Finally, press the far left button once and watch as the Oculory reaches its correct alignment, revealing the Elder Scroll. Take the now-completed [Runed Lexicon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runed_Lexicon) from the stand, then go to the center of the Oculory and retrieve the Elder Scroll from its cradle for the concurrent quest [Elder Knowledge](Skyrim_Elder_Knowledge.md). Once you have both the Elder Scroll and the Lexicon, return to Septimus Signus at his outpost.

### Daedric Prince of the Unknown
To continue past this point, you must be level 15 or higher. If you aren't level 15 yet, giving Septimus the Lexicon will result in him dismissing you; then, you must wait until a courier delivers a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Septimus_Signus) from him to continue. Once you have returned to the outpost, talk to Septimus again. He'll read the Lexicon and tell you he searched for it with the help of the Daedric Prince of Knowledge, [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md). He tells you to harvest the blood of the mer races in Tamriel. Head for the outside of the cave. You will see a [Wretched Abyss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wretched_Abyss) that has the talk option. Talk to the abyss, and you will learn that the voice coming from the abyss is that of Hermaeus Mora. He claims that Septimus is a good follower, but after he discovers how to unlock the Dwemer box he will have outlived his usefulness. He will then tell you that you should take Septimus's place, which you can agree to or disagree. Neither choice will affect the outcome of the quest. Hermaeus Mora wishes for you to speak with him again after you have completed Septimus's task.

### Blood Harvest
In order to harvest blood, you need to search the corpse of someone of the appropriate race while the [Essence Extractor](Skyrim_Essence_Extractor.md) is in your inventory. The five races whose blood you need to harvest are [Altmer](Skyrim_High_Elf.md), [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md), [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), and [Orsimer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orsimer). You don't necessarily have to go slaughtering elves to satisfy the quest as there are many corpses of each race to be found around Skyrim; the pages detailing locations for each race are [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf_(NPC).md), [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf_(dead).md), [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf_(dead).md), and [Orc](Skyrim_Orc_(dead).md), while a list of Falmer infested locations can be found [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Falmer_Hives). There is also a list of [dead bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md) of all races to be considered. A select few locations are listed below as they contain the corpses of more than two races:

- Altmer, Bosmer, Falmer, and Orc bodies can be found in [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md), although if you've followed the suggested route through Alftand to get to Blackreach, you'll need to return quickly or many of the bodies will have been cleared out.
- Bosmer, Dunmer, Falmer, and Orc bodies can be found in [Liar's Retreat](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md).
- Bosmer, Dunmer, and Orc bodies can be found in [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md).
- Bosmer, Dunmer, and Orc bodies can be found in [Silverdrift Lair](Skyrim_Silverdrift_Lair.md).
- Altmer are also part of the Thalmor and can be found at the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).

### Returning to Septimus
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Oghma_Infinium.jpg/200px-SR-item-Oghma_Infinium.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Oghma_Infinium.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Oghma_Infinium.jpg) The Oghma Infinium Upon acquiring different blood samples, you will be prompted to return to Septimus Signus's Outpost. Give the blood samples to Septimus and wait for him to open the cube. Following him inside will reveal, much to the surprise of the delusional quest giver, that it was not the "Heart of a God" that was stored inside the cube, but rather a book. His surprise is cut short as his usefulness to Hermaeus Mora has been outlived and he is promptly disposed of in a flash of light.

It is revealed that the book on a pedestal is actually the *[Oghma Infinium](Skyrim_Oghma_Infinium.md)*. Hermaeus Mora will greet you again, calling you his champion. You can reply with "What now?" or "I am not your champion, monster." Again, there are no consequences to the completion of this quest with either choice. Through conversation with the Daedric prince, you learn that it was sealed here for a reason, so that no more of its dangerous knowledge could be shared with the world of mortals—something that Hermaeus Mora wishes undone.

At this point, the quest is complete. Reading the book will reveal some of the knowledge hidden within. Specifically, you can choose from three different paths: magic, shadow, or might, and receive +5 permanent points in the following skills, depending on the choice:

- **The Path of Might:** Archery, Block, Heavy Armor, One-handed, Smithing, Two-handed
- **The Path of Shadow:** Alchemy, Light Armor, Lockpicking, Pickpocket, Sneak, Speech
- **The Path of Magic:** Alteration, Conjuration, Destruction, Enchanting, Illusion, Restoration

## Notes
- You will automatically start this quest during the course of [Elder Knowledge](Skyrim_Elder_Knowledge.md) or [Scroll Scouting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_Scouting)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> if you have not already started it, as the Elder Scroll is required for both the main questline and for the Dawnguard core questline. This quest does not have to be completed in order to complete either questline, however.
- After opening the cube, leaving [Septimus Signus's Outpost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Septimus_Signus%27s_Outpost) and letting the cell respawn will cause the dwemer puzzle door to close permanently, potentially locking in the Oghma Infinium.
- You don't have to access [Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md) through [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md), as it's also possible to reach it through the two other linked Dwemer ruins, [Raldbthar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar) and [Mzinchaleft](Skyrim_Mzinchaleft.md), by using the sphere given by Septimus on the apparatus at the end of these dungeons.
- The "Heart of a God" to which Septimus refers is actually the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md). However, there is no Heart inside the chamber.
- The retrieved Elder Scroll can be sold to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md) in the College of Winterhold's Arcanaeum for 2000 gold. However, do not do so until you have completed [Alduin's Bane](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Bane.md), as it is essential to the main quest. If *[Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)* is installed, the Elder Scroll is also needed to complete its [main questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests), though you can buy it back from Urag if you have already sold it to him.
- If you give the lexicon to Septimus before reaching level 15, it may be necessary to wait for a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Septimus_Signus) from him to arrive via courier. Simply returning to him when you reach that level will not work.
- Corpses fed on while in Beast Form or with the [Ring of Namira](Skyrim_Ring_of_Namira.md) cannot be harvested for blood. Avoid feeding on the named elven corpses inside Alftand as you will have to find samples in other, potentially far apart, locations. However, if *Dawnguard* is installed, you may be able to harvest corpses while in Beast Form, as long as you have advanced far enough in the quest to have been given the extractor.
- You can keep the Oghma Infinium after use by dropping it before exiting the inventory upon its initial use, however, it will only be a book, and will not grant the bonuses again. Otherwise it will disappear after you use it.
- If *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* is installed, Hermaeus Mora will not be represented as a large purple shape. Instead, he is shown as the tentacled form seen during the quests of the DLC. If you have completed the *Dragonborn* quest line before speaking to Mora in this quest, he will use unique dialogue referring to the events of *Dragonborn*.
- You can't harvest your own blood if your character is elven. - You also can't harvest the blood of any of the [Snow Elves](Skyrim_Snow_Elf.md) encountered in the *Dawnguard* quest line.
- As of version 2.0.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), this quest appears to have lost several properties due to unknown causes, possibly from bad official patches that have since been undone. Not many people seem to have been affected by this so it may have been caused by a Steam beta patch that went bad and was quickly corrected. These properties will now be restored allowing the quest to be completed.

## Bugs
- The [Attunement Sphere](Skyrim_Attunement_Sphere.md) and [Runed Lexicon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runed_Lexicon) are still considered quest items after the quest completion and therefore cannot be removed from inventory. Since the Sphere is not removed during the quest it will stay forever in inventory. The Runed Lexicon is correctly removed during quest and will be stuck only if you pickpocket/loot it back from Septimus Signus. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. Both objects will not be quest items anymore upon quest completition

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The console commands `player.removeitem 3a3dd 1` and `player.removeitem 3532c 1` can be used to remove both items from inventory.
- Septimus is supposed to be lifted into the air and turned into ashes after approaching the Oghma Infinium book and saying the final line. However, there is a bug where the event is sometimes not triggered due to a bad animation registration event which then makes the quest impossible to complete. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- The most reliable solution to this problem is to kill Septimus. Once the quest has progressed to where he should be dead, killing him does not count as a crime.
- Alternatively, reload an earlier save (before giving the blood to Septimus). If inside, exit the cave and wait for an hour while outside before re-entering. This can fix the event to trigger normally.
- If you kill Septimus right after he gives you the stage to harvest blood, you will fail the quest, but the game will still prompt you with the "Harvest blood" option every time you loot the corpses of Orcs or elves. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- You may not have the "Harvest blood" option despite having the Essence Extractor in your inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Use the console command `player.addperk 79af5`.
- If you are finding the Elder Scroll for a [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) quest, the journal entry will still say "Find the Elder Scroll for Paarthurnax" even if you have never met him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
