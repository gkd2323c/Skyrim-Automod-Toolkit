## Exterior
Outside of the assemblage is Windhelm's docks where, during the day, the occupants of the assemblage work; no matter the weather conditions. To the northeast of the door is a wooden rack holding seven [salmon](Skyrim_Passive_Creatures.md#Salmon_.28dead.29) that can be freely harvested. On the docks there is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) available for you to use.

## Argonian Assemblage
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a6/SR-interior-Argonian_Assemblage.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Argonian_Assemblage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Argonian_Assemblage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Argonian_Assemblage.jpg) Inside the Argonian Assemblage Both inside and out, the Argonian Assemblage is dark and in disrepair; a perfect place for the prejudiced and proud [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) to keep their unwanted Argonian workers. The dockworkers retire at the end of the work day to their shared living quarters. Inside, there is not much to look at. Cobwebs hang from the ceiling, and a dull fire burns under empty cooking pots. There are four beds, one for each resident of the assemblage.

There are several round wooden pillars supporting the high ceiling around the room. On your left as soon as you enter is a round wooden table. Against the west wall is a cupboard holding a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md) and bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). A dresser to the right holds a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Thief](Skyrim_Thief_(book).md)*, and a [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) on top. There is a second dresser further to the right with two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) on top and a [drum](Skyrim_Drum.md) and chair beside it. Against the north wall is a tall wardrobe. Both this and the dressers all contain various items of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md). There are several crates further along the wall. Next to these is an alcove containing a square table and two chairs with a bottle of wine, a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), and a couple of wedges of cheese on top and an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md) on the floor underneath. More crates are to the right of the table with a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) on top of one of them, containing leveled items.

Along the east wall is a long fire pit with several unusable pots hanging over it, a bottle of wine on the floor to the left, a cast iron pot containing three [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) on the right-hand edge of the surround, and two pieces of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) to the right of the pit. In front of it is a long wooden table and bench with a bottle of wine, a [baked potato](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md), a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile), a loaf of bread, a [pheasant breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pheasant_Breast), and a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) all on top. There are also several unusable barrels in the southeastern corner.

There are four beds in a row to the left of the middle of the room; each bed has an end table either at the head or foot of it. The third has a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) on top and the fourth has a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) and bowl of [troll fat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Troll_Fat) on top.

## Notes
- Because the master-level lock on the entrance door resets often, it seems [Scouts-Many-Marshes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scouts-Many-Marshes) and [Stands-In-Shallows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stands-In-Shallows) become trapped inside after turning in for the night, and cannot come out the next day unless the door is unlocked by you.
- Even if you are an Argonian, you will still receive a bounty for trespassing unless you are married to one of the residents.
- There is an [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Empty [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) to the bottom left of the entrance door.
- Three of the four reptilian residents offer quests.
- The cupboard and the sack on its upper shelf, the four end tables, the two dressers, and the wardrobe are non-respawning. Consequently, they are safe storage. The small sack, and the knapsack are not safe.
