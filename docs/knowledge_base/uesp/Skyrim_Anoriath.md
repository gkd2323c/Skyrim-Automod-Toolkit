[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-npc-Anoriath.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Anoriath.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anoriath.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Anoriath.jpg) Anoriath [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-quest-Kill_Anoriath.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Kill_Anoriath.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Anoriath.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Kill_Anoriath.jpg) Anoriath defending himself **Anoriath**, a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) [scout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Scout) and hunter, owns a vendor stall in the [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) market, and is co-owner of [the Drunken Huntsman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Drunken_Huntsman) with his brother [Elrindir](Skyrim_Elrindir.md). He played a part in the amusing tale of how the combination of shop and pub got its name, although only his brother will tell it. He is one of the targets of a [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) [assassination contract](Skyrim_Kill_Anoriath.md).

From 8am to 7pm, Anoriath operates his butcher's stall, selling many types of meat. He claims to hunt every day, but once he closes the stall he heads to The Drunken Huntsman, where he eventually sleeps.

Anoriath wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with a pair of [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md) and a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a [hunting bow](Skyrim_Hunting_Bow.md) supplemented with twenty [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md). He carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Quest-Related Events

### Kill Anoriath
As with most Dark Brotherhood targets, you may speak to him before you kill him. He will tell you:

- *"You're making me... nervous. Is there something you need?"*

You can answer him in three ways:

| Your Options | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Only cowards hunt animals. True game walks on two legs.** | *"Are you... threatening me? Walk away fool. It's not worth it."* | Walks away |
| **I am the predator. You are my prey.** | *"Is that so? We shall see, villain. We shall see!"* | Becomes hostile and attacks |
| **(Remain silent)** | *"Well now, you are a strange one. You remind me a bit of myself, when I'm stalking a deer. I'm not quite sure how I feel about that..."* | Walks away |

If he walks away, attempting conversation with him will only have him say:

- *"I find your presence... unnerving. Please depart."*

## Dialogue
Upon greeting him, he will say:

- *"Do you hunt? The plains outside Whiterun are ripe * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * with game."*

When he is running his stall, he will also advertise his goods to passersby, saying:

- *"The finest cuts, fresh from the wilds!"*
- *"Chops and steaks fit for a jarl! Come and see my selection of finest-quality meats!"*
- *"Tired of bread and stew? Had enough of fish and fowl? Treat yourself and your kin to a choice cut of steak or a tender rack of ribs!"*

Talking to him there will have him claim:

- *"You should try the venison. It's delicious."*

When you end conversation with him, he can say:

- *"Good hunting."*
- *"Remember, I've got the freshest meat in all of Whiterun."*
- *"Right. Till next time."*

If you meet him at the Drunken Huntsman, he can say:

- *"Need something? Talk to my brother, Elrindir."*
- *"Remember, I've got the freshest meat in all of Whiterun."*

While Anoriath is at his stall, you may ask him if he kills all this game himself:

- **Did you kill all this game yourself?**
- *"Oh, yes. Hunting is my great passion. That's why my brother and I opened a shop to sell hunting supplies. It's the Drunken Huntsman, down by the main gate. He tends the store, and I've got this stall."*

## Rumors
Should you ask, Elrindir will reveal how he and Anoriath came up with the name "Drunken Huntsman":

- *"Yes, I hear that question often. My brother Anoriath and I came up with it one night after consuming... rather too much mead. After our drinks, we went out for a moonlight hunt. We got separated, and in his drunken state my brother mistook me for a deer. He shot an arrow that pierced my... well, my rump. After such a memorable adventure, we knew we had the name for our shop."*

## Conversations
Anoriath can be seen having random conversations with his brother Elrindir, as outlined below:

- **Anoriath**: *"I've been thinking of hunting out beyond the boundaries of Whiterun hold."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Again? You just had an "extended hunt" last month. To the Pine Forest, wasn't it."*
- **Anoriath**: *"That was three days. I'm thinking more like three weeks."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Three weeks? You're mad! You can't keep the stand closed for that long!"*
- **Anoriath**: *"Why not? Business has been terrible. Most folk would rather hunt their own meat than buy ours."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Nonsense. That's just an excuse for you to avoid working, and "commune with the greenwood like our fathers of old" and blah blah."*
- **Anoriath**: *"Congratulations, brother. You've been living in Skyrim so long, you've become a Nord."*
- **Elrindir**: *"At least the Nords don't shirk their responsibilities."*

- **Elrindir**: *"Our stock of arrows is running a bit low."*
- **Anoriath**: *"All right. I'll fletch some more when I get the time."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Good. I'll do the same. We've still got plenty of shafts from that Riverwood shipment."*

- **Anoriath**: *"It's been two months, and no word from father."*
- **Elrindir**: *"Don't worry, brother. I'm sure he's fine. We've waited longer before receiving a letter."*
- **Anoriath**: *"Might be worth riding out to Solitude, see if a messenger ship's come in."*
- **Elrindir**: *"If it'll put your mind at ease, then I agree. But not now, all right? We need the business from the stand."*

He may also talk with various customers about his goods at the market:

- **[Nazeem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nazeem)**: *"Do you have anything even remotely fresh?"*

- **[Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md)**: *"I've got a recipe for venison stew that I want to try out. Will you have venison anytime soon?"*

## Notes
- Anoriath was originally supposed to physically hunt outside of Whiterun. Dialogue was even recorded for him in case you spoke to him: *"Oh! You startled me. I wasn't expecting company out here."* and *"Shhh. You'll scare the deer."* However, the dialogue has been deliberately blocked from being accessed by being attached to a nonexistent quest stage, and Anoriath has no AI packages that take him outside of the city, despite Nazir's Dark Brotherhood contract specifying you could kill him while hunting and not incur a bounty. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, originally altered this. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=15588)) A new hunting AI package was created for Anoriath, and the nonsense condition was removed from his two lines, making them available again. However, the only suitable "hunt and patrol" package to base Anoriath's on was inextricably linked to making the actor who uses it hostile to everything in sight. As such, this created a larger issue and the change was reverted in version 2.0.5a. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=16554))

However, investing in him has no actual effect—his gold is not permanently increased because his merchant chest does not contain the `Perk Investor Store Upgrade` bonus gold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. He can no longer be invested in.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.

The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.9, fixes this bug.
