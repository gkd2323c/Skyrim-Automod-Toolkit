[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/OB-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg/200px-OB-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg) Pell's Gate circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Pell's Gate is a settlement located east of the [White Rose River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:White_Rose_River) in the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It can be found where the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md) joins with the [Green Road](Lore_Green_Road.md), in the region known as the North Weald.

## History

### Second Era
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/ON-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg/200px-ON-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Pell%27s_Gate.jpg) Pell's Gate circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Before the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md) of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Pell's Gate was seemingly a well-established town. However, [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) patrols became less frequent when war broke out, and bandits began to threaten the town and raid its livestock.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It was eventually overrun by the Homestead bandit clan from the nearby ruins of [Fort Homestead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Homestead), who then destroyed and occupied the settlement. Several of the townsfolk managed to escape, and fled to the town of [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

### Third Era
By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Pell's Gate was only a collection of shacks. At some point, a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) girl from the village named Lenwin came into possession of the [Umbra Sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Umbra_Sword). The sword caused her to grow bloodthirsty, and she began calling herself Umbra after the sword. Eventually she departed Pell's Gate to join a group of mercenaries, before ultimately returning to the area and taking up residence in the nearby [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of [Vindasel](Lore_Vindasel.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md) tasked the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) with tracking down the sword, which resulted in Lenwin's death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> During the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) later that year, farmers from Pell's Gate were recruited by the Imperial Legion to close a nearby [Oblivion Gate](Lore_Oblivion_Gate.md). After the Gate was destroyed, the fortifications that surrounded it grew into the town of [Ione](Lore_Ione.md).

### Fourth Era
By [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48), the towns of Sweetwater and Easterbridge had also sprung up in close proximity to Pell's Gate.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
