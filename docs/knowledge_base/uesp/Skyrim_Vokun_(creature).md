[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-creature-Vokun.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Vokun.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vokun.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Vokun.jpg) Vokun **Vokun** is a high-ranked [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) found in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md), east of Solitude and northeast of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md). As soon as you enter the room with his sarcophagus, he erupts from within and proceeds to attack. As with all dragon priests, he is a formidable undead foe.

Vokun drops his [eponymous mask](Skyrim_Vokun_(item).md) upon death. It grants a 20% reduction in casting cost for all spells from the Conjuration, Illusion and Alteration schools.

Vokun is the guardian of the scroll you must retrieve for [Anska](Skyrim_Anska.md) in the [related quest](Skyrim_A_Scroll_For_Anska.md), as the scroll lies in the chamber behind his crypt. Although he is a shock-based Dragon Priest, he carries a [Staff of Fireballs](Skyrim_Staff_of_Fireballs.md), which he will use almost exclusively to anything else. Vokun has the ability to summon a [storm atronach](Skyrim_Storm_Atronach.md), as well as a [lightning cloak](Skyrim_Lightning_Cloak.md) and [greater ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md).

## Related Quests
- **[A Scroll For Anska](Skyrim_A_Scroll_For_Anska.md)**: Help [Anska](Skyrim_Anska.md) trace her roots in [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md).

## Notes
- "Vokun" means "shadow" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- In the Creation Kit, Vokun is called **"Ianusu"**.
