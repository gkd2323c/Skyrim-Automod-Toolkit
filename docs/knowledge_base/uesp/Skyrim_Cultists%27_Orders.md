---
Board the vessel [Northern Maiden](Skyrim_Northern_Maiden.md) docked at [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). Take it to [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), then begin your search. Kill the False Dragonborn known as <Alias=Player> before <Alias.Pronoun=Player> reaches [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md).

Return with word of your success, and [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) shall be most pleased.
