Breaking the Cycle of Tyranny **by [Alla Llaleth](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Alla_Llaleth)** *Propaganda from the Ebonheart Pact criticizing the Daggerfall Covenant*
---
The brash young kingdoms of the [Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) want to reestablish the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) of [Men](Lore_Man.md). They are too callow and immature to realize that the Empire was a failed experiment whose time has passed. Now is a time of crisis for all of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md), and [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) cannot afford to have these unlearned barbarians destabilizing [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and jeopardizing our efforts to stave off world-destroying doom.

The [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) will not allow the Daggerfall Covenant to establish another bloody-handed dynasty of [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) tyrants. Time and again throughout history, armies of men have marched in from the rim of Tamriel to conquer its center, enjoying a brief moment of power before sliding into inevitable discord and decay. It's time to break this destructive cycle once and for all. We need to defeat the armies of the Covenant, depose their kings, and bring their chastened successors into a new Tamriel Pact, where wiser heads shall prevail.
