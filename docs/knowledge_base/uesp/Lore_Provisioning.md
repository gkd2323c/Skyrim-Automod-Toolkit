## Notable Chefs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/ON-prerelease-Cooking.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Cooking.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Cooking.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Cooking.jpg) Various Chefs - **Arinmir Larethiath** — An Altmer and Assistant Sapiarch of Culinary Arts of the Fourth Era, with an expertise of mixology
- **Asaldak** — Famous Khajiit chef of the Second Era and a former personal chef of the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md)
- **[Balagog gro-Nolob](Lore_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md)** — One of the greatest chefs of the Fourth Era, known publicly only as "the Gourmet"
- **[Donolon](Lore_Donolon.md)** — Famous Breton confectionery chef of the Second Era, whose clientele included the likes of nobles and even [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md)
- **[Gratia Arquitius](Lore_Gratia_Arquitius.md)** — Famous Imperial chef of the Second Era whose signature dish is known to facilitate camaraderie
- **[Makes-Many-Soups](Lore_Makes-Many-Soups.md)** — Famous Argonian chef potager of the Second Era whose numerous soups are celebrated all over Nirn
- **[Orzorga](Lore_Orzorga.md)** — Famous Orcish chef of the Second Era, who has several of her recipes remain well-known into the Fourth Era
- **Pameryawen** — Famous Corelanyan Altmer chef of the Second Era and author of *[Corelanyan Cuisine](Lore_Corelanyan_Cuisine.md)*
- **Rhazeez** — A famous Redguard chef of the Second Era that was said to be the most innovative chef in all of Tamriel
- **S'jirra** — Famous long-lived Khajiit chef active during the late Third Era and into the Third Century of the Fourth Era

In addition to mortal chefs, some otherworldly entities are also associated with the art of provisioning. The [Demi-Prince of Pastries](Lore_Demi-Prince_of_Pastries.md) is a daedric entity whose name suggests a connection to the art of confectionery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sheo-1)</sup>
