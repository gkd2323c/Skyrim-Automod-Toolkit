Legend of the Yokudan Chargers **by [Honnorah af-Lahreq](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Honnorah_af-Lahreq)** *A pamphlet promoting the Yokudan Charger breed of horses*
---
The [Gallants of Hammerfell](Lore_Gallants_of_Hammerfell.md) are known far and wide as [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s finest sword -wielders, but they are known equally well for their skill as riders of [horses](Lore_Horse.md). A [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) Gallant is nearly always depicted with a sword in one hand, and the reins of a loyal steed in the other.

And no breed of horse is more closely associated with the Knights of the Desert than the [Yokudan Charger](Lore_Yokudan_Charger.md). Indeed, the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) Charger is the mightiest of breeds, the pride of all who trade in horseflesh in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Faced with the heat and harsh terrain of the [Alik'r](Lore_Alik%27r.md), lesser horses falter and die, while the Yokudan Charger survives, even thrives. The Charger reacts to a challenge like his rider the Gallant: head up, nostrils flaring, ever prepared to show his or her mettle.

Here at [Aswala Stables](Lore_Aswala_Stables.md) we breed and sell only Yokudan Chargers of certified descent, steeds of proven lineage that count among their forebears the horses [Yaghoub the Seafarer](Lore_Yaghoub.md) brought in his fleet from [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md). We are a little bit off the beaten track, but we count ourselves lucky to be situated in the awe-inspiring heart of our magnificent Alik'r Desert, and when you see the steeds we have on offer we think you'll agree it's worth the trek.

The Yokudan Charger: a grand tradition, and for us, a sacred trust. Come, accept the hospitality of Aswala Stables, and judge for yourself.
