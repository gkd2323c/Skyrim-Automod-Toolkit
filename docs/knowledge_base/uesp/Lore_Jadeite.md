# Jadeite

Jadeite is a kind of green crystal. It had various applications, including crafting decorative figurines for promoting alcohol brands,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-T2-1)</sup> and containers like reliquiaries and presentation boxes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-T3-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Contraband J-3)</sup>

Certain [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) [tarot](Lore_Tarot.md) cards were known for their engraved jadeite placards.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-T1-4)</sup>

Some of the sharpening stones of the [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) [Morkul](Lore_Morkul_Clan.md) Forge were shown in jadeite presentation box sets which contained three sharpening stones of different density and coarseness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-T3-2)</sup>

Reliquaries, said to house a purported lock of hair belonging to [Leki](Lore_Leki.md), were discovered meticulously preserved within small, finely carved jadeite boxes, serving as reliquiaries.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Contraband J-3)</sup>
