[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-place-Velothi_Mountains.jpg/200px-SR-place-Velothi_Mountains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Velothi_Mountains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Velothi_Mountains.jpg) The Velothi Mountains as viewed from [Stendarr's Beacon](Skyrim_Stendarr%27s_Beacon.md) The **Velothi Mountains** are a mountain range that stretches along the eastern border of [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

They are the mountain range separating [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) from [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).

The mountains form the eastern border of the Skyrim regions of [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) and [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). In the south the mountain range meets the [Jerall Mountains](Skyrim_Jerall_Mountains.md).

Some points of interest include the orcish stronghold of [Narzulbur](Skyrim_Narzulbur.md), [Sacellum of Boethiah](Skyrim_Sacellum_of_Boethiah.md), [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md), or the Dwemer ruins of [Kagrenzel](Skyrim_Kagrenzel.md) and [Mzulft](Skyrim_Mzulft.md). The [Dunmeth Pass](Skyrim_Dunmeth_Pass.md) leads through the mountains and into northern Morrowind. High in the mountains lies the ancient temple of [Skuldafn](Skyrim_Skuldafn.md), along the [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) border.

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Velothi_Mountains.md).
