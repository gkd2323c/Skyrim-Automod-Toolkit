[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) Map of Tamriel showing the Irk River The Irk River (or *River Irk*) is a waterway flowing from central [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) to the waters of northern [Hew's Bay](Lore_Hew%27s_Bay.md). It begins at the bay, east of [Gilane](Lore_Gilane.md) and west of the [Tark River](Lore_Tark_River.md), and ends at the [Karnver Falls](Lore_Karnver_Falls.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

Rivers in Hammerfell, such as the Irk are characterized by [Ginkgo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ginkgo) trees. Their leaves are inconspicuous to the naked eye but easily identified by their peculiar half-moon shape.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SFOT-2)</sup>
