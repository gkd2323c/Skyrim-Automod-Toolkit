## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to an innkeeper or wait for a [courier](Skyrim_Courier.md).
2. Kill [Erwan](Skyrim_Erwan.md) at [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md) and obtain *[Erwan's Journal](Skyrim_Erwan%27s_Journal.md)* and the [Ring of Masser](Skyrim_Ring_of_Masser.md).
3. Obtain the set of [Daedric mail](Skyrim_Daedric_Mail.md) armor from [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speak to an innkeeper and ask them for work. You will be handed a note titled *[Missing Merchant](Skyrim_Missing_Merchant_(note).md)* and the quest begins.

You learn that the jarl has put out a call for aid regarding the disappearance of a merchant, [Gunther Frost-Foot](Skyrim_Gunther.md), who was last seen in the area of [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md) east of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). You will be tasked to go there and investigate.

Traitor's Post, a destroyed cabin, is currently home to a gang of bandits led by a Breton female named [Erwan](Skyrim_Erwan.md). Upon killing Erwan, you will be prompted to loot her body and read her [journal](Skyrim_Erwan%27s_Journal.md). Erwan, it seems, is quite disturbed, and currently considers the corpse of Gunther to be her "best friend". She mentions in passing that she sold a suit of [Daedric mail](Skyrim_Daedric_Mail.md) to a Khajiit caravaner named [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md), but that he should be willing to trade the armor for a ring she currently possesses. This ring is the [Ring of Masser](Skyrim_Ring_of_Masser.md), which you will find on Gunther's naked corpse within the cabin.

You will be prompted to track down Ma'dran, whose [trade caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) travels between Windhelm and [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). You then have the choice of either trading the ring to him for a set of [Daedric mail](Skyrim_Daedric_Mail.md) armor, or paying 5,000 gold for the armor. The ring has a base value of 5,211, so this is a fair trade if you don't wish to keep it. The quest will complete once you have obtained the set of armor, and you will subsequently begin to find Daedric mail items as random loot throughout the world.

## Notes
- Regular Daedric armor starts appearing in leveled lists at level 48, but this Creation is not level-locked and can be completed by characters of any level. - Paired with [the Bittercup creation](Skyrim_Bittercup.md) and specifically choosing [to fill the Altar of Power](Skyrim_The_Pit_(quest).md), you can get a full set of Daedric armor at level one, with both normal and mail gauntlets.
