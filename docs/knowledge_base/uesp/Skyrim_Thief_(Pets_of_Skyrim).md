This **Thief** is a dead [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) found in the forest south-southwest of [Half-Moon Mill](Skyrim_Half-Moon_Mill.md). He was evidently killed by [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md), based on the scattered [arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md) and wolf carcasses surrounding him. His pet [fox](Skyrim_Fox.md) [Sweet Roll](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll_(creature).md) stands over his corpse, and is the target of the related quest.

He carries some ill-gotten treasure, five [sweet rolls](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll.md) and the [*Thief's Journal*](Skyrim_Thief%27s_Journal.md). You need to read his journal as part of the related quest; however, be aware that it's [bugged](Skyrim_Pets_of_Skyrim_(quest).md#Bugs).
