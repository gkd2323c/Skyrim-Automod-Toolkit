There are two variations of this item — the unenchanted version can be crafted at a forge and differs from regular [Dragonscale Gauntlets](Skyrim_Dragonscale_Armor.md) only in appearance, whereas the enchanted version has an added [Fortify Unarmed Damage](Skyrim_Fortify_Unarmed_Damage.md) effect, and cannot be crafted but can be bought from vendors and found as random loot.

---

### Unenchanted Version
Crafting this item requires 2 [Dragon Scales](Skyrim_Dragon_Scales.md), 1 [Iron Ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md), 1 [Leather](Skyrim_Leather.md), and 2 [Leather Strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips).
