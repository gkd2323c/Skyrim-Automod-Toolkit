## History
Following the fall of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243) at the end of the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), the clan of Anutwyll escaped largely intact as they fled southwest beneath the canopy of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). They are presumed to settled there and adopted the [Green Pact](Lore_Green_Pact.md) under the terms of the native [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), eventually being absorbed into their local population.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ASi V-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the entrance to Anutwyll was sealed, though its well was still active. The nearby military stronghold of [Castle Bloodmayne](Lore_Castle_Bloodmayne.md) and Temple for the Elder Scroll of Mnem were located just to the west and southwest of the ruins respectively, having been the sites of much contention during the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the ruins had been opened, but its subterranean halls crawled with monsters.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OB-4)</sup> Carandial, a [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) scholar, camped out near the ruins and explored them to study Ayleid society.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Carandial-5)</sup>
