[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-npc-Grenwulf_the_Brawler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Grenwulf_the_Brawler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grenwulf_the_Brawler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Grenwulf_the_Brawler.jpg) Grenwulf the Brawler's ghost **Grenwulf the Brawler** is a [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) found in [Cronvangr Broodlair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cronvangr_Broodlair). He wears [Hide Armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), [Hide Boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and the [Fists of Randagulf](Skyrim_Fists_of_Randagulf.md). He is involved in the [Caught in a Web](Skyrim_Caught_in_a_Web.md) quest.

## Related Quests
- **[Caught in a Web](Skyrim_Caught_in_a_Web.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Contest.md)</sup>: Search for legendary artifacts in a spider den.

## Notes
- There are in fact two versions of Grenwulf the Brawler: - The ghost found in Cronvangr Broodlair has a base ID of `FExxx80E` and a ref ID of `FExxx810`.
- His body is actually considered to be a container in game data, and has a base ID of `FExxx80A` and a ref ID of `FExxx80B`. The [Fists of Randagulf](Skyrim_Fists_of_Randagulf.md) can be found on the body.
