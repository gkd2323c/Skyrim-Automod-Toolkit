*Terrible Daedric monsters and foul necromancy were unleashed on those who opposed the kinlord. Many valiant Corelanyas—and not a few Argonians who chose to take up arms against the tyrant—fell in those dark days. Istamil's victory seemed inevitable. But his cousin Vinutilmo Corelanya, a devout knight and humble scholar, arose to lead what remained of the opposition.
Guided by dream-visions, Vinutilmo sought the aid of the Daedric queens Azura, Meridia, and Nocturnal. They joined together to check the power of Molag Bal, giving Vinutilmo the opportunity to penetrate the dark defenses of Mor Naril and confront Kinlord Istamil. In the Temple of the Final Dark, Vinutilmo slew the tyrant and put an end to his alliance with the Lord of Brutality.*
— *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)* by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup> **Regent** Vinutilmo Corelanya was the leader of the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) who introduced the faith of the Three Queens and overthrew the tyrannical necromancer [Istamil](Lore_Istamil_Corelanya.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>

## History

### Sealing of Mor Naril
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/ON-place-Vinutilmo_Palace.jpg/200px-ON-place-Vinutilmo_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vinutilmo_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Vinutilmo_Palace.jpg) Vinutilmo Palace *Giving reverence to the Three Queens who aided him, Vinutilmo sealed the gates of Mor Naril and led his forces away from the dark temple. Many among his followers urged him to take the title of kinlord, but Vinutilmo refused. Instead, he asked to be recognized as regent, one who ruled not through his own authority but as a steward of the power with which he had been entrusted.
Following Vinutilmo's example, all Corelanya leaders since have likewise taken the title of regent instead of kinlord or kinlady. We have also followed his pious example, eschewing the dark powers among the Daedra and placing our trust in the Three Queens who guide and protect us. The Queens willing, it will always be so for our people.*
— *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)* by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup> Vinutilmo Corelanya was a devout knight and scholar who rose in opposition to his cousin, the tyrannical Kinlord Istamil, during a period of civil war in [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md). As Istamil unleashed [necromantic horrors](Lore_Undead.md) and [Daedric monsters](Lore_Daedra.md) with the aid of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), Vinutilmo united the remaining opposition and sought the help of the Daedric queens [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md), and [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md). Guided by [visions](Lore_Sight.md), and with their aid, he penetrated the fortress of Mor Naril, defeated Istamil in the Temple of the Final Dark, and ended the tyrant's reign. Honoring the Three Queens, Vinutilmo sealed the fortress and led his people away from darkness. Choosing humility over power, he declined the title of Kinlord and instead became Regent of the Corelanya Clan, a steward of the realm rather than a ruler by right.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup>

### Reign
Very little is known about Vinutilmo's reign. After his victory, the Corelanyas distanced themselves from darker powers and embraced the faith of the Three Queens,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup> which eventually became the primary faith of the Sunporters.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VOFS-2)</sup> Some sketches of Vinutilmo from his time as regent have survived.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TP-3)</sup>

### Legacy
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/ON-place-Cathedral_of_the_Three_Queens.jpg/200px-ON-place-Cathedral_of_the_Three_Queens.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cathedral_of_the_Three_Queens.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cathedral_of_the_Three_Queens.jpg) Cathedral of the Three Queens [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt Following his death, Vinutilmo was buried in the Corelanya Crypt, an honor afforded to most rulers of the clan on Solstice.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RK-4)</sup> Vinutilmo features prominently in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md). The title of regent continued to be used for generations.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC5-1)</sup> Vinutilmo Palace, the seat of the Regency of Sunport, was named after him.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-VP-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VGTWS-6)</sup> Among the preserved depictions of him were flawed planning sketches for a formal portrait. Regent Vinutilmo was shown seated on a throne with a sword resting across his lap. Some of these sketches were still in circulation around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TP-3)</sup>
