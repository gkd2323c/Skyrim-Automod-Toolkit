# Fire Mage

**Fire Mages** are enemy spellcasters who specialize in the [Fire Damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) branch of [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) magic. They are typically found in a few caves, keeps, and ruins across [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md) known as [warlock lairs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Warlock_Lairs). Like other elemental mages ([ice mages](Skyrim_Ice_Mage.md) and [storm mages](Skyrim_Storm_Mage.md)), they belong to the [Fire/Frost/Shock Mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Frost_Shock_Mage) [NPC](Skyrim_NPCs.md) class.

The type of fire mage you encounter depends on your level. You will continue to meet low-level fire mages even at high levels, but boss-level fire mages will always be at the highest possible level.

### Fire Mages
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fire_Mage.jpg) A Novice Fire Mage attacks with a blast of flame Fire mages rely on their devastating fire-based spells in combat, and carry a leveled dagger in case of close-quarters fighting ([iron](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), [steel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), [orcish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Dagger), [dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven_Dagger.md), [elven](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md), [glass](Skyrim_Glass_Dagger.md), or an enchanted dagger of any quality from iron to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md)). They also have a 10% chance to carry a leveled [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) or Destruction staff.

They wear a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c5d12) along with [black mage robes](Skyrim_Black_Mage_Robes.md), [hooded black mage robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Black_Mage_Robes.md), robes of [Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md), robes of [Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md), robes of [Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md), or robes of [Fortify Magicka Rate](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka_Rate.md).

In addition to the gear listed above, fire mages have a small chance to carry [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), [poisons](Skyrim_Poisons.md), [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), Novice or Apprentice level [spell tomes](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md), [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) (common, uncommon, or rare), [recipes](Skyrim_Recipes.md), and a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md). They also have [atronach forge recipes](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge_Recipe.md) in their inventories, but their chance of appearing in-game is set at 0%.

On death, fire mages have a 10% chance to drop potions and poisons, and a 5% chance to drop one of the following scrolls:

- **Alteration:** [Scroll of Mass Paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mass_Paralysis)
- **Conjuration:** [Scroll of Conjure Storm Atronach](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Conjure_Storm_Atronach), [Scroll of Dread Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Dread_Zombie)
- **Destruction:** [Scroll of Blizzard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Blizzard), [Scroll of Fire Storm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fire_Storm)
- **Illusion:** [Scroll of Call to Arms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Call_to_Arms), [Scroll of Harmony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Harmony), [Scroll of Hysteria](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Hysteria), [Scroll of Mayhem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Mayhem)
- **Restoration:** [Scroll of Bane of the Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Bane_of_the_Undead), [Scroll of Guardian Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Guardian_Circle)

Pyromancers and Arch Pyromancers have the same gear as lower-level fire mages, but have a 50% chance to carry a [Staff of Fireballs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Fireballs) and do not have the leveled lists for atronach forge recipes.

All members of the Warlock faction share a set of generic dialogue which they will use when in combat:

*"You cannot even conceive of the power I wield..."*

*"Fire, frost, or lightning - you will suffer at my hands!"*

*"Don't you see? I am master of the arcane!"*

*"Behold my power!"*

*"The elements are mine to command!"*

## Notes
- All the generic fire mages that are Dark Elves are listed twice in the leveled lists, which means fire mages are slightly more likely to be Dark Elves than one of the other races.
