## History
In the latter years of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) and the [Imperial Army](Lore_Imperial_Army.md) were gathering to the west from Silgrad Tower and [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md) for their invasion of Morrowind. House Redoran was well prepared to flank the armies across the border but the other Great Houses were either reluctant or uncooperative. After the [Armistice](Lore_Armistice.md) was signed between the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and Morrowind,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OM-4)</sup> the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) took over Silgrad Tower and used it as an outpost on the border.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TRB1-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Silgrad Tower was an active settlement. It was ruled by Count Shalpar and had a rivalry with [Karththor Dale](Lore_Karththor_Dale.md). Silgrad Tower was neighbored by several settlements, including [Reich Parkeep](Lore_Reich_Parkeep.md) to the north, [Verarchen Hall](Lore_Verarchen_Hall.md) to the east-southeast, and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARMap-5)</sup> to the west toward [Riften](Lore_Riften.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARMap SR-6)</sup>
