# Mollusk

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Clam_Brief.png) A bivalve [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Octopus.png) A cephalopod [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Snail.png) A gastropod There are many different species of mollusks (or **molluscs**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Codex-1)</sup> on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md). Mollusks are all soft-skinned invertebrates and many possess protective shells, most of them are aquatic or semi-aquatic.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Net-2)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Codex-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Octo-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TNaut-4)</sup> They are split into three main groups: **bivalves** which are mostly sedentary hard-shelled creatures like [clams](Lore_Clam.md) and [kollops](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kollop),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Codex-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Prima-5)</sup><sup>:213</sup> **cephalopods** which are tentacled aquatic creatures like [octopuses](Lore_Octopus.md) and [nautili](Lore_Nautilus.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Octo-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TNaut-4)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOcto-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tel-7)</sup> and **gastropods** which are slimy slow-moving creatures like [snails](Lore_Snail.md) and [slugs](Lore_Slug.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAAYQ-8)</sup>

Mollusks are eaten by many of the cultures of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> many can be caught by fishermen.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Net-2)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOFish-12)</sup> The dead shells of mollusks, known as seashells, are often found washed up on beaches and collected so that they may be used decoratively.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Jar-14)</sup> The inhabitants of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) are known to make use of mollusks somewhat frequently. Kollops and other Morrowind mollusks are known to produce [pearls](Lore_Pearl.md) which are harvested due to their rarity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Codex-1)</sup> Members of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) are known to make cephalopod helms.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Tel-7)</sup>

[Sload](Lore_Sload.md) and [Dreugh](Lore_Dreugh.md) share traits with mollusks such as a gastropods and cephalopods.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAAYQ-8)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-15)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2H-16)</sup> Though it is unclear if they are classified as such, given their shared traits with other creatures such as amphibians and crustaceans.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-AAFCS-17)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TPWormgod-18)</sup> Several [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) also share traits with mollusks, such as [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) and his servants.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-19)</sup>
