[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg/200px-SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg)](File_SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) [](File_SR-place-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) City entrance The **Hall of the Dead** is a large building in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) where the corpses of the deceased are laid to rest.

There are two entrances to Markarth's Hall of the Dead. The first is found near the entrance to the [Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) and the [Markarth Ruins](Skyrim_Markarth_Ruins.md). The second is found inside [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md), near [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md). The Hall is home to [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md), and is the starting point for the quest [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md). It consists of one zone, *[Hall of the Dead](#Hall_of_the_Dead)*.

## Hall of the Dead
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_%28Markarth%29.jpg)](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) [](File_SR-interior-Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).jpg.md) Hall of the Dead interior There is very little within the hall. To the west of the shrine is a food barrel, two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two [eidar cheese wedges](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), and a sample of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md). To the east of the shrine is the skill book *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4)* above an owned bed.

## Notes
- You are required to [pick](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) either adept-locked door to enter without the key. The key is given to you by Brother Verulus during the related quest.
- If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) capture Markarth (whether Markarth is taken by force or given to the Stormcloaks during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md)), [Ondolemar](Skyrim_Ondolemar.md) will perish, and his remains will be found here.
- There is a secret room in the Hall. It can be seen on the map to the left of the altar of [Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arkay), if you stick to the wall.
