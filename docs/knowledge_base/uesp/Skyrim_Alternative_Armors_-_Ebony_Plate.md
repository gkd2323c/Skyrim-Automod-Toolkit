## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of light heavy * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Ebony armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Heart of Crimson" starts by reading the Letter from Tyra Blood-Fire delivered by courier upon reaching level 32.)*
## Overview
In the quest [Heart of Crimson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_of_Crimson), you are tasked with hunting a woman named Tyra Blood-Fire.
