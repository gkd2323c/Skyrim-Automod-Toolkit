[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f0/ON-place-Shornhelm.jpg/200px-ON-place-Shornhelm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shornhelm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Shornhelm.jpg) Shornhelm Iphilia Montclair was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noblewoman who was the Queen of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) and the Countess of [House Montclair](Lore_House_Montclair.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). At some point before [2E 532](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_532), she married the King of Shornhelm, [Hurlburt](Lore_Hurlburt.md), becoming his queen consort.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SCCo TN-1)</sup>

In 2E 532, she gave birth to her son and the legitimate heir of King Hurlburt, [Phylgeon](Lore_Phylgeon_Montclair.md). When she died in [2E 544](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_544), the leadership of the house passed to twelve-year-old Phylgeon, who became the thirty-eighth Baron of House Montclair.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SCCo TN-1)</sup>
