## Albacore
The **Albacore** is a type of [tuna](Lore_Tuna.md).

- The **Obdurate Albacore** can be found in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) and [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), off the coasts of the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md).
- The **Opaline Albacore** can be found in the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) and [Lower Niben](Lore_Lower_Niben.md), off the coast of [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Alewife
The **Alewife** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), off the coast of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Anchovy
The **Anchovy** is a type of fish. Cannibal [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) anchovies were listed as one of the (clearly fictional) creatures the Gateway Inn in [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md) was free from in a fraudulent paper written by Uleni Heleran, declaring that the establishment was "[ghost](Lore_Ghost.md) -free".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Ghost-Free_Papers)

## Angelfish
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Angelfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Angelfish.png) An Angelfish The **Angelfish** is a type of fish that can be found in untouched freshwater lakes in mild climates, but they are far from delicate. While they are small they can be quite aggressive towards other fish. They can be found in temperate lakes in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FM-2)</sup>

- The **Emperor Angelfish** can be found in the [Niben Bay](Lore_Niben_Bay.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Angelfish.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Angler
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Angler_Larvae.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-ingredient-Angler_Larvae.png) Angler Larvae [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Angler.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Angler.png) An Angler The **Angler** or **Anglerfish** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), off the coasts of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). While the adults are fished for food, Angler Larvae are sought for their alchemical properties.

- The **Dusk Angler** can found in the foul waters of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).
- The **Humpback Angler** could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Angler.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
