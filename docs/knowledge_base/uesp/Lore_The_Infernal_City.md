***The Infernal City*** is the first of two [novels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels) written by [Greg Keyes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Greg_Keyes) that follow the story of *[The Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls)* between the events of *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* and *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Beth Press Release-1)</sup> The book was published by [Del Rey Books](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Del_Rey_Books), part of the [Random House Publishing Group](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Random_House), on November 24, 2009.

The floating city of [Umbriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Umbriel) is making its way across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and destroying the lands it passes over, killing the people and raising the dead to power the city.

[Annaïg Hoïnart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Anna%C3%AFg_Ho%C3%AFnart), a 17-year-old [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) girl, and her [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) friend, [Mere-Glim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Mere-Glim), accidentally fly onto Umbriel and are captured by its inhabitants; [Sul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Sul), an aged [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), joins Prince [Attrebus Mede](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Prince_Attrebus_Mede), the 22-year-old son of Emperor [Titus Mede](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Emperor_Titus_Mede), in search of a way to stop the city; and [Colin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels_Colin_Vineben), an Inspector for the [Penitus Oculatus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Penitus_Oculatus), searches for the prince when he goes missing during his quest. Their adventures take them along the first steps in attempting to stop the most recent threat to Tamriel.
