## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md).
2. Speak to the [waiter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waiter).
3. Sit at the table and read the [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Please_Read_Aloud).
4. Defeat either the [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) or the guests and [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).
5. Speak to the survivor to receive leveled gold and the key to your new home.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Player as a Non-Vampire
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-prerelease-Bloodchill_Manor.png/200px-SR-prerelease-Bloodchill_Manor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Bloodchill_Manor.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Bloodchill_Manor.png) The guests await When you arrive at the manor, a number of guests are milling around the front door listening to a bard. Just inside, another reveler is drinking and dancing. Proceed down the hallway to the [waiter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waiter). No matter what you say, the outcome will be the same; with him handing you a [dinner menu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dinner_Menu) (Version 1) and directing you and the other guests to your seats.

Sit down at the table, in a place of honor across from the door. Once you sit down, read the note on your plate named [Please Read Aloud](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Please_Read_Aloud) to gain some insight into this mysterious dinner party and proceed with the quest. After you read the last page of the letter, the waiter will approach [Galtius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Galtius) across from you and bite him from behind, killing him. As the other guests rise from their seats in a panic, the bard in the room, the waiter, and two gargoyles will attack.

Deal with them, and be careful of the waiter: his lightning attacks are strong enough to one-shot lower-level players. [Resist magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) gear and [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) might help. Once all the vampires and their stone minions are dead, speak with the "survivor." Some of the other guests may have survived your battle but the one to speak with is the Orc [Durian](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Durian), who is revealed to be a member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md):

**The vampires are all dead. And the will says the house belongs to me.** *"Very good. But you've created a debt between us. Here. Take this. We are even now."* He will then hand you a leveled amount of gold but no [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor_Key), and you will become the official owner of the manor. This completes the quest. The doors along the entry hallway will open and you can begin using your new base.

If there are any surviving guests besides Durian, they will return to sit at the dinner table amidst the carnage. Once you leave and reenter the cave, all of the bodies and debris will be cleaned up (including the vampiric bard near the entrance, which was presumably dispatched by Durian on your way into dinner), any surviving guests will have left, and the dinner table will be cleared out of the trophy room to allow your unimpeded access.

### Player as a Vampire
If the player is a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md), things play out a little differently. When you arrive at the manor, pass through the guests and speak to the [waiter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waiter) at the end of the hall. After choosing one of four options, the waiter will give a generic response and hand you the [dinner menu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dinner_Menu) (Version 2). He will then turn and lead you to the banquet table.

Once there, sit and read through the feast note [Please Read Aloud](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Please_Read_Aloud). After doing so you are instructed to begin the feast, by drinking the blood of the already enthralled [Parven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Parven). At that point, the waiter will approach [Galtius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Galtius) and bite him from behind, killing him. After Galtius's death Parven will also terminate and the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) will enter the building, initiating battle.

After disposing of the Dawnguard and the remaining guests, speak to the waiter once more.

**The Dawnguard and the nobles are dead.** *It would seem so.* **The note seemed to imply the last survivor would get the house.** *I'm so glad you succeeded. Here. Please take this for your trouble.* At this point the waiter will give you the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bloodchill_Manor_Key) to the manor and some leveled gold, completing the quest. Three more dialogue options are given; all will end the conversation. The doors along the entry hallway are now open for use, and when you leave and reenter the cavern all of the destruction from your fight will be cleared away.

## Notes
- After the attack in the non-vampire version, Durian is renamed Dawnguard even if the player has not started the Dawnguard quest line.
- It is significantly easier to complete the quest as a Vampire, especially at lower levels, as the vampires are very threatening spellcasters and the gargoyles in the dining hall will also come to life and attack the player if they are not a vampire.

## Bugs
- You may receive a radiant quest from the Companions to intimidate the waiter, if you brawl the waiter and don't kill him, the quest will softlock, as your player will be unable to move when re-entering Bloodchill Cavern. <sup>**?**</sup>
- If you attack and kill the bard, waiter, and gargoyles before reading the note, the quest will still complete properly, but you will be left unable to move if you then read the note at any point after.
- If you start the fight and leave Bloodchill Cavern before finishing the fight, the doors will be closed and the gargoyles will not respond to any action, making the quest impossible to complete. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this bug, use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `setstage cc EEJSSE005_Quest 100`.
- This bug can also be fixed by attacking the person outside, which skips to the vampire attack and causes the door to open. However, the gargoyles will remain unresponsive.
- If playing on consoles, a work around exists. Abilities with a large area of effect such as Kyne's Peace can be used to aggro the Waiter behind the locked doors. When leaving the cave, any aggressive vampires should appear outside with you after a small delay, making it possible to kill them. The disguised Dawnguard member will now reward you, completing the quest, and unlocking all the previously locked doors of the manor.
- After the quest is complete, the *Dinner Invitation* when read in your inventory may now be a blank sheet of paper. <sup>**?**</sup>

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage cc EEJSSE005_Quest stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest cc EEJSSE005_Quest`.
