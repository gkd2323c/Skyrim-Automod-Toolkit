## History
During the Middle [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), early [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) explorers constructed a wizard tower at Bal Fell while mapping the coasts of Vvardenfell after fleeing [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris.md). This tower survived into the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), when it was inhabited by [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> It was said to have been built on an ancient center of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worship,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWDialogue-2)</sup> and in the First Era a great shrine to [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) was built there by the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt V-1)</sup> Eventually Bal Fell grew to become a city of the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), known for its dry, crowded streets. It existed at least into the early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), after the founding of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). The great sorceress Seryne Relas once lived in Bal Fell.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

In time, Bal Fell was abandoned, and the only thing left standing were the Daedric ruins. Daedra often plagued the ruins, and the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) were charged with keeping away Daedra worshippers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) priest of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) named Mad Griskild set up his workshop in the ruins and began experimenting with alchemy and Daedric magic to [create magically enhanced](Lore_Spider_Crafting.md) [spiders](Lore_Spider.md). An expedition to the ruins led by a famous [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) archaeologist, Lady Clarisse Laurent, fell victim to Griskild's mind-controlling spiders, but the expedition members were saved by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who killed the Nord. Lady Laurent later sent Griskild's research to a scribe living near [White Ridge Barrow](Lore_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOAWOT-6)</sup> which ultimately led to the creation of unique magical spiders that would be rediscovered in the barrow in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DB-7)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the ruins had obtained a nasty reputation, and wizards from [Great House Telvanni](Lore_Great_House_Telvanni.md) set up competing camps of hirelings and adventurers to explore and loot the ruins.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Gt V-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWDialogue-2)</sup> In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), House Telvanni and the Mages Guild sent expeditions to clear out the Daedra. They were aided by the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md), who looted the [Ten Pace Boots](Lore_Ten_Pace_Boots.md) from the ruins.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MW-8)</sup>

The ruins were likely destroyed in [4E 5](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_5), when [Baar Dau](Lore_Baar_Dau.md) crashed into Vivec City. This impact devastated the surrounding area, creating [Scathing Bay](Lore_Scathing_Bay.md), a large crater filled with boiling water from the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
