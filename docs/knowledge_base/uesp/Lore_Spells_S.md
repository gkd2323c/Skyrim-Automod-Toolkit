## Shock
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Shock.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Shock.png) Shock (Oblivion) **Shock** or **Shock Damage** is an offensive spell type taught under the School of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md). It deals elemental lightning damage to the target, which can harm the target's magicka reserves as well as their health.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> "Finger of the Mountain" is the name of a particularly infamous shock spell granted by the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) pillar at [Cloud Top](Lore_Cloud_Top.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## Silence
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Silence.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-icon-Silence.png) Silence (Oblivion) **Silence** is a dual illusion and mysticism spell that temporarily prevents the target from casting spells. More specifically, it is a spell that extinguishes sound<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mysticism-3)</sup> and bends sound in a shape like a cone. In the book, *[Silence](Lore_Silence.md)* written by Ganpheril Kimeth, the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) mage, Erer Darothril teaches an aspirant wizard named Oristian Silverthorn the means to perform the spell. As he used Silence on his glass and tapped on it, he had Silverthorn close his eyes and picture the sound as a physical entity. Silverthorn was able to successfully learn this technique and apprehend the rogue battlemage, Egroamaro in [Indoranyon](Lore_Indoranyon.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Silence-4)</sup>

Silence is also an [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) trait. Some ingredients, when mixed to create a poison of silence include [bergamot seeds](Lore_Bergamot_Seeds.md), a [black rose](Lore_Black_Rose.md), [harrada](Lore_Harrada.md), [malachite](Lore_Malachite.md), [rat meat](Lore_Rat_Meat.md). As per [Shad Astula](Lore_Shad_Astula.md)'s curriculum, Silence is taught in Illusion I among other simple manipulations.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SAC-5)</sup> The clan of [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md) from the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), the [Lyrezi](Lore_Lyrezi.md) are proficient in the silence spell.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DFLyrezi-6)</sup> Some notable weapons and artifacts are enchanted with silence. The [Ebony Blade](Lore_Ebony_Blade.md); [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md)'s daedric artifact,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-WRTTEB-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-DFEbony-8)</sup> the [Mazken](Lore_Mazken.md) dark bow of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md); [Ruin's Edge](Lore_Ruin%27s_Edge.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SHRuin-9)</sup> the Dwemer tower shield; [Spellbreaker](Lore_Spellbreaker.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TL-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWSpell-11)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Silence), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Silence), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Silence)

**Spell schools:** [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md), [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md)
## [Soul Trap](Lore_Soul_Trap.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg/200px-ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-skill-Soul_Trap.jpg) Soul Trap [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-effect-Soultrap.jpg) [Soul Trap](Lore_Soul_Trap.md) (or **Soultrap**) is a [soul magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md) [spell](Lore_Magic.md) used to capture an entity's [soul](Lore_Soul.md) within a [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md) upon death. Some variations of the spell could also directly harm the target as well. Although traditionally categorized under the School of [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md), it was historically considered to be a [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md) spell during the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Soul_Trap), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Soultrap), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Soul_Trap), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Soul_Trap)

**Spell schools:** [Soul Magic](Lore_Soul_Magic.md), [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md), [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md)
## [Spellmirror](Lore_Spellmirror.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg/200px-LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Shalidor_Statue.jpg) Shalidor, forger of the Spellmirror [Spellmirror](Lore_Spellmirror.md) (or **Shalidor's Mirror**) is a [spell](Lore_Magic.md) used to reflect incoming hostile spells back at their caster. It is classified under the school of [Thaumaturgy](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md), and [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md). It is also part of the 7th Circinate. Spellmirror was forged by the [Arch-Mage Shalidor](Lore_Shalidor.md). Shalidor bore his Spellmirror into battle, possibly during the [Battle](Lore_Battle_of_Rourken-Shalidor.md) of [Rourken](Lore_Rourken.md) -Shalidor, which also saw the use of the [Spellbreaker](Lore_Spellbreaker.md) shield with similar properties.

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Shalidor%27s_Mirror), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Shalidor%27s_Mirror)

**Spell schools:** [Mysticism](Lore_Mysticism.md), [Thaumaturgy](Lore_Thaumaturgy.md)
