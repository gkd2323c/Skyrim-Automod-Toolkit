Song of the Spirits *A haunting song promises eternal rest* *
---
The night is dark,

The ground is deep,

Its warmth can keep you still.

Your pain forget,

Your anguish gone,

Your slumber will not end.

I am your guard,

I am your hope,

I will not fail my charge.

The night is dark,

The ground is deep,

You shall not rise today.
