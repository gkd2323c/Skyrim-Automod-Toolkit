[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/SR-place-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg) Thaumaturgist's Hut The **Thaumaturgist's Hut** is an [alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) shop in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md).

The shop is located across from [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md), where its proprietor [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md) runs the shop during normal business hours (8am - 8pm). There are two floors, each with their own entrance. However, the doors are never locked, possibly because it has the only [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) available in Morthal. If you are on good terms with the owner, all the lower value [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) found here are free to take and [respawn](Skyrim_Respawning.md) every few days. No one lives or sleeps here and there are no beds in the building.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There are two [swamp fungal pods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod) near the building, one near the front door and the other around the back next to the stairs leading up to a balcony. The balcony winds around the building and at the front is the second entrance.

### Thaumaturgist's Hut
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.jpg) First floor The main entrance is on the ground floor and enters into the shop. Opposite you is a fireplace, on the mantlepiece are several [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) sold by [Lami](Skyrim_Lami.md). To the right of this, coming out from the east wall is the counter. On the counter is a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and under the counter are three small [coin purses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). Behind the counter against the south wall are a cupboard and set of shelves holding more potions and [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) sold by Lami and four bottles of wine, two bottles of alto wine and a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead).

Between the cupboard and shelves is a barrel of ingredients and a pile of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks. To the right of the door is a dresser holding more ingredients sold by Lami, a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [potion of minor healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge). Next to the dresser is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest). To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding four [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Under this is a barrel of food and to the left of the barrel is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). On top of the lab are more ingredient and potions you can buy from Lami and a [weak frenzy poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weak_Frenzy_Poison). There are stairs against the north wall leading to the top floor. Under the stairs is a pile of firewood and several barrels, on top of these are even more ingredients sold by Lami.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut_02.jpg) Second floor At the top of the stairs, against the east wall is a long wooden table and bench holding two bottles of alto wine, several loaves of bread, several wedges of cheese, two [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) that you can buy from Lami and two pieces of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat) on a wooden plate. To the right, still against the same wall is a set of shelves holding an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace). Against the south wall is an unlocked chest with dressers on either side holding four common books, a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl) containing three bottles of [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) that are sold by Lami, two bottles of alto wine and five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md). There is a door to the outside in the middle of the west wall and a square wooden table to the right. To the right of the table, above the stairs are two food barrels.

## Notes
- Since Lami doesn't live here, the opening hours are approximate. She doesn't leave her [house](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md) until around 8am and it may take her an hour to walk to the shop.
- A copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Response to Bero's Speech](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Response_to_Bero%27s_Speech)* can be found on top of a shelf on the bottom floor, next to a troll skull.
