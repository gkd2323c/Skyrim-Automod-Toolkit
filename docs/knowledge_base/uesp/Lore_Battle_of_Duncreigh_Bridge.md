# Battle of Duncreigh Bridge

The **Battle of Duncreigh Bridge** took place in [1E 1427](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1427) between [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) and the neighboring hamlet of Sensford in High Rock.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It apparently changed nothing, as both villages continued to possess their individual ruling families. The citizens of Anticlere celebrate each anniversary of the battle by marching down Sensford's main street, an event that usually results in fighting and a large number of injuries on both sides.
