Kestic was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) king during the early-mid [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). In his time, he was **the Forest King**, leading the northern clans of the forest region. He was the last of his title, as the Nedic civilization of the [Deathlands](Lore_Deathlands.md) fell with the arrival of the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) in [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Not D-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSot B-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Wood.png/100px-ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Wood.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Wood.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-texture-Craglorn Nedic Banner Wood.png) The Forest Banner Kestic hailed from the northern forest region of the Nedic kingdoms, where he eventually became their leader and represented them in various matters between the other nations. In [1E 808](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_808), the High King of [Skyreach](Lore_Skyreach.md), [Durac](Lore_Durac.md) arranged a marriage between King Kestic and his daughter, Saradin, to cement relations between both factions,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SD-3)</sup> much to the dismay of his court wizard and close friend, [Virmaril](Lore_Virmaril.md). However, this never came to be as the [Ra Gada](Lore_Ra_Gada.md) had landed on the shores of the Deathlands and spread across the region's outermost corners. The [Anka-Ra](Lore_Anka-Ra.md) moved to the far northeast, into the region of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md), and storming the Kingdom of Skyreach.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup>

Virmaril proposed a plan to High King Durac to fight against the Anka-Ra. The High King arranged the council of kings, bringing the various Nedic kings into the Skyreach Catacombs, including Kestic. However, Virmaril murdered the council, taking control of the deceased kings and the interred dead in the catacombs with [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md). It was in vain however as he was sealed deep in the inner sanctum.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VJ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-WSJ-5)</sup> Skyreach was subsequently besieged by the Anka-Ra and the kingdoms such as the forest region were dissolved. The Forest King was regarded by later historians as a legendary figure, counted among other luminaries such as the [Spirit King](Lore_Spirit_King.md) and the [Frost King](Lore_Frost_King.md). Scholars have speculated that these titles reflected the regions they ruled, or perhaps their individual spheres of power.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SE3-6)</sup> Many years later in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a band of [adventurers](Lore_Soulless_One.md) delved into the Skyreach Catacombs when dark magic came from the ruins. The group faced the dead Nedic-kings in waves until they managed to destroy them all at once.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSot B-2)</sup>
