# Items


## Finding Items
Depending upon whether you are looking for a specific item or just browsing for general information, there are several different ways to navigate through the many item-related pages.

### Search
If you know the exact name of the specific item you want to find, you can search for it [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Special:Search) or at the search box at the top of the page. 0x000F71CD

### Categories
Within the category **[Skyrim-Items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Items)** are many subcategories listing nearly every item in the game by function. If you don't know the exact spelling of a specific item, browsing through one of the categories may help you to find it.
