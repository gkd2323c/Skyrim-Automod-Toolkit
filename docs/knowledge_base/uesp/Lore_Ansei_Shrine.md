Ansei Shrine *A monument commemorating the birthplace of Ansei Maja*
---
This grand monument, sculpted by the most talented artist, Jamshald Lainlyn, was erected here in the [one hundred and second year](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_102) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) to honor the esteemed [Ansei](Lore_Ansei.md), and to commemorate the birthplace of Ansei Maja, may she ever watch over the [Alik'r desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) and protect us all from the threat of the vile arts.
