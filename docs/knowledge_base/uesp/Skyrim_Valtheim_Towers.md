## Walkthrough
The towers straddle the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md) along the main road east of Whiterun. They support a stone bridge spanning the river high above. Along the road just outside the southern tower is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). The treasure chest marked on [Treasure Map II](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_II.md) is on the rocky riverbank just east of the southern tower.

Approaching from the main road, a Bandit by the cooking pot guards the passage, extorting a toll from the player when they come in the vicinity. She will demand 200 gold, which can be paid to keep her and her gang calm. Alternatively, by passing a low-level [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) check, she will accept only 50 gold, granting Speech XP (see Notes). Refusing to pay, entering the tower, or loitering in the area for too long will cause her and all the other Bandits to become hostile.

The southern tower contains a [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at its base rigged with a [swinging mace](Skyrim_Swinging_Mace.md) trap. The trigger may be deactivated by [picking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) a leveled lock. Stairs inside rise to the second level, from which an exterior wooden ramp leads up to the stone bridge and the third floor of the tower. At the top is a platform normally guarded by an archer. A second archer patrols the bridge between the towers.

Across the bridge, the northern tower has two interior levels, with a dresser on the first floor and an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) on the second. A copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Black Arrow, v2](Skyrim_The_Black_Arrow,_v2.md)* is on the bedside table on the third level. Above this is a platform where you can find the bandit chief, who carries a random [enchanted weapon](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md). A random enchanted bow can normally be found on the table here.

The first floor has a second door leading out to the mountains. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is immediately north of the door, and to the right is another archer whose chair is near a [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md). A [clay deposit](Skyrim_Clay_Deposit.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> is near the riverbank just west of the northern tower.

## Notes
- In the local map and quest journal, Valtheim Towers is designated "Valtheim Keep".
- If you approach the bandit who demands the toll in a different form, such as [beast form](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) or [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, they will still demand money, even if you are attacking.
- Persuading or intimidating the toll bandit gives [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill experience. Moving away from the towers sufficiently far resets the toll demand; going west a short distance to a fallen tree that half covers the road is sufficient. This allows for fast Speech leveling.
- If the location is set as the destination for a radiant quest, the Bandit outside will not ask for a toll and all Bandits will be hostile.

## Bugs
- There seems to be an issue with the "Intimidate" option when dealing with the bandit who demands the toll. Even if your intimidation is successful, 200 gold is removed from your inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- If you succeed in persuading the bandit to accept 50 gold instead of 200, the 50 gold is never added to the bandit's inventory. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
- The intimidation option for the bandits here doesn't have a goodbye flag set if you succeed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.6, fixes this bug.
- [Aela the Huntress](Skyrim_Aela_the_Huntress.md) might send you here for the quest [Striking the Heart](Skyrim_Striking_the_Heart.md), but there may be no one here to kill. This prevents you from completing the quest and advancing in the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) questline. - Reload a save from before you were assigned the quest and obtain one with a different target location.
- It may not be possible to pick up the enchanted bow in the northern tower. - Saving and loading the new save fixes the problem.
- Sometimes when the bandit leader is killed, this location will not be marked as cleared. Reloading a save and killing him again does not solve this problem. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `setlocationcleared 000192c1 1` to force the game to mark it as cleared.
