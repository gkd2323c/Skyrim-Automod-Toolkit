- *"The Golden Eye will reward our devotion with security and prosperity. This I promise you, as your high priest."* —Uluscant<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UM-1)</sup>

Uluscant was the High Priest of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) in the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) city [Bisnensel](Lore_Bisnensel.md). He overthrew the rule of King [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md) between [1E 453](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_453) and [1E 460](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_460) by resorting to visions and murder.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BOAR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ALOSAS-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UM-1)</sup>
