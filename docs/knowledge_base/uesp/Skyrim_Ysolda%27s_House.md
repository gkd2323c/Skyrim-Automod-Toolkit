[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/51/SR-place-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg) Ysolda's House **Ysolda's House** is a small house in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), located behind [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md) and [Belethor's General Goods](Skyrim_Belethor%27s_General_Goods.md).

It is the home of local merchant-to-be [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md), who lives there alone. It consists of one area only, *[Ysolda's House](#Ysolda.27s_House)*.

There are two [shadowmarks](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) found here: an "Empty" shadowmark is to the left of the entrance door and a "Thieves' Cache" shadowmark is above a chest on the side of the house.

## Ysolda's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c5/SR-interior-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ysolda%27s_House.jpg) Ysolda's House interior The house is rather small and cramped and it is divided into two sections by a wooden wall. The first room contains a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) beside a huge fireplace and a table that contains a [wolf pelt](Skyrim_Wolf_Pelt.md) and a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). Two sets of shelves hold nothing but two food sacks and some [leather strips](Skyrim_Leather_Strips.md). The second room is Ysolda's bedroom and contains her owned bed and an end table that contains clutter and has a bottle of alto wine on top and a pair of boots on the floor below. A wardrobe hold various [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) while a small table has a bowl with three [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md) and a copy of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](Skyrim_A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun.md)*. At the end of Ysolda's bed is a chest that contains some gold and/or some minor loot.

## Notes
- If you chose to live here after marriage, sleeping in the single bed will still give you your [Lover's Comfort](Skyrim_Lover%27s_Comfort.md) resting bonus, but the house does respawn after ten days.
- If you choose to marry Ysolda you can move in with her, but she will not open her store until you move into your own house.
