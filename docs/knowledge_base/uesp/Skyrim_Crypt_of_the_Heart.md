## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read *[Crypt of the Heart - Draft](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart_-_Draft.md)* in the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md).
2. Travel to the bridge next to [Reachcliff Secret Entrance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Secret_Entrance) and follow the [Ghost Knight](Skyrim_Ghost_Knight.md) to his grave.
3. Defeat the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) at the grave and read *[Forsworn Shaman's Note](Skyrim_Forsworn_Shaman%27s_Note.md)*.
4. Locate the [Beldama Witches](Skyrim_Beldama_Witch.md) at [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) and take the [Corrupted Human Heart](Skyrim_Corrupted_Human_Heart.md).
5. Return to the knight's grave and make a choice: - Place the corrupted heart in the grave and defeat the knight's corrupted spirit in combat, then loot his grave for a set of Ebony Spell Knight Armor.
- Use the [Shrine of Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mara) to cleanse the knight's heart, then return it to his grave to be given a set of Ebony Spell Knight Armor by the appeased spirit.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Order of the Crypt
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart.jpg) A lone flower marks the site of the ghostly apparitions This quest will automatically begin upon installing the [Spell Knight Armor](Skyrim_Spell_Knight_Armor.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). You will be directed to visit the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to seek out rumors that might have crossed the border from [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). Once at the inn, your quest marker will direct you to a guest room containing a book named *[Crypt of the Heart - Draft](Skyrim_Crypt_of_the_Heart_-_Draft.md)*. Reading it will reveal the story of the Order of the Crypt, a knightly order from [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md), and the tragic tale of a knight named [Simon Rodayne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne) who fell in love with (or was tricked by) a witch of the [Beldama Wyrd](Lore_Beldama_Wyrd.md) named [Daenella](Skyrim_Daenella.md). Their relationship brought the order into conflict with the witches, driving the coven further and further east. It is said that Simon's ghost haunts the banks of the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md), below his hilltop tomb.

You will then be directed to investigate these ghost sightings by travelling to a bridge that crosses the Karth River just next to [Reachcliff Secret Entrance](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Secret_Entrance). On this bridge, you will find a lone [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md); when approached, a [Ghost Knight](Skyrim_Ghost_Knight.md) will appear behind you and will wander off to the south, towards [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md).

Follow the ghost as it makes its way up the slope. It will guide you to a grave west of Dushnikh Yal, which is surrounded by three hostile [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). Kill the attackers and loot the [Forsworn Shaman](Skyrim_Forsworn_Shaman.md)'s body. She will be carrying a *[Forsworn Shaman's Note](Skyrim_Forsworn_Shaman%27s_Note.md)*, which you must read to progress the quest. It reveals that the Beldama Wyrd has come to [the Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md) in search of Daenella in order to punish her for her betrayal. They have taken Simon's heart from his grave and have brought it to [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) to defile it. Surrounding the grave, you will find the bodies of two [Knights of the Crypt](Skyrim_Knight_of_the_Crypt.md) who had been sent to the Reach to track down and kill the Beldama witches. If you wish, you can loot their bodies for a full set of [Iron Spell Knight Armor](Skyrim_Iron_Spell_Knight_Armor.md) and [Steel Spell Knight Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Spell_Knight_Armor).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bd/SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_03.jpg) Daenella and her lover's heart
### A Stolen Heart
Journey to [Karthspire Camp](Skyrim_Karthspire_Camp.md) and prepare for a fight; the campsite is swarming with Forsworn, and with this Creation installed you will have to deal with the added threat of [Beldama Witches](Skyrim_Beldama_Witch.md) and a [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) called the [Beldama Ward Mother](Skyrim_Beldama_Ward_Mother.md). They are located on the eastern side of the camp.

Once you've subdued the many enemies at the campsite, approach the sacrificial altar where the Beldama witches are found. On it, you will find the corpse of Daenella, who had been captured by the witches and killed with the knowledge that the Ward Mother planned to corrupt her beloved's heart and return it to his grave tainted, damning his soul for eternity. Sure enough, you will find a [Corrupted Human Heart](Skyrim_Corrupted_Human_Heart.md) on a pedestal in front of the altar. Take it and return to the knight's grave.

### A Choice
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_04.jpg) Lovers reunited [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Crypt_of_the_Heart_05.jpg) A set of ebony armor is your reward Once you have the heart, you will be instructed to return it to the knight's grave, with the optional step of first purifying the heart of corruption.

If you choose to place the corrupted heart within the grave, the corrupted spirit of Simon Rodayne will appear and attack you for damning his soul. He wields a [bound sword](Skyrim_Bound_Sword.md) in combat. Once you banish the ghost, you will be directed to loot his grave for a full set of enchanted [Ebony Spell Knight Armor](Skyrim_Ebony_Spell_Knight_Armor.md) in order to complete the quest. This set has enchantments focused on offense (see [Spell Knight Armor items](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Knight_Armor_Items#Ebony_Spell_Knight_Armor)).

If you instead wish to purify his heart, you must first activate the [Shrine of Mara](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Mara) found at his tomb. Once this is done, place [Simon Rodayne's Heart](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Simon_Rodayne%27s_Heart) within his grave. This will cause a brief apparition of Simon and Daenella, finally reunited in death. The ghostly lovers will then disappear, and in their place a full set of enchanted Ebony Spell Knight Armor will materialize above the grave. This set has enchantments focused on defense. Take the armor to complete the quest.

Regardless of which option you choose, you will now be able to craft Spell Knight armor from [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md), [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md), and [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md) at any [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md), provided you have the necessary skill perks.

## Notes
- The name of this quest (and the related book) is a reference to the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md), which the Order of the Crypt is sworn to protect.
- Once the Crypt of the Heart-Draft is read at Silver-Blood Inn you can head straight to the gravesite of Simon Rodayne circumventing the bridge ghost.
- The Beldama coven can be attacked directly without going through Karthspire Camp by heading southwest from [Soljund's Sinkhole](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soljund%27s_Sinkhole) and approaching the altar from above.
- At Karthspire Camp, the dead giant on the altar and the giant's toe will be absent during this quest, replaced by Daenella and the Corrupted Human Heart.

## Bugs
- The Knights of the Crypt may not spawn around Rodayne's grave.
- The quest may mark as completed after picking up three of the armor pieces, making it impossible to pick up the fourth.
