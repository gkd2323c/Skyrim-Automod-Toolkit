# Alfiq-raht

*When Jode is waning and Jone is waxing
Then shall be born the Alfiq-Raht
Secret-Keepers and War-Weavers
These masterminds mold the shape of our kingdoms
Strengthening us at times, tempering us in others
To ensure we remain hardy and strong For what is to come.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/e/e5/Mwaning_Swaxing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Swaxing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mwaning_Swaxing.jpg) Alfiq-raht are born when Masser is waning and Secunda is waxing<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> The Alfiq-raht are a quadrepedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). Little is known about their appearance except that they are slightly bigger than the [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Survival G-2)</sup> They are sensitive about being mistaken for Alfiq, as their larger size is considered a matter of pride.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Survival G-2)</sup> They are said to make great leaders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> "As crazy as an Alfiq-raht in heat" was used as an expression in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Turshan-3)</sup>
