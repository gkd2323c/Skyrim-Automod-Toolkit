*You think what you do has meaning? You think you slay me, and I am dead? It is just dream and waking over and over, one appearance after another, nothing real. What you do here means nothing. Why do we waste our breath on you?*
—Dagoth Reler, an Ascended Sleeper<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sleeper D-1)</sup> Ascended Sleepers are highly intelligent and aggressive distorted half- [mer](Lore_Mer.md), half-beast [ash creatures](Lore_Ash_Creature.md). Originally [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) cultists, they have become powerful [magical](Lore_Magic.md) beings. They are among the most feared beings in [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md)'s revived [Sixth House](Lore_Sixth_House.md), alongside [Ash Vampires](Lore_Ash_Vampire.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Monster Experts-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gares-3)</sup> They rank highly in the Sixth House and are the final and most powerful mutation of the common ash creatures transformed through Dagoth Ur's [Ash Blight](Lore_Ash_Blight.md) and [Divine Disease](Lore_Corprus.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Plan-4)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Gares-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Dagoth-5)</sup> Stronger cultists who do not become simple Corprus beasts advance through the stages of mutation and enlightenment before eventually reaching the powers of Ascended Sleepers.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Plan-4)</sup> These cultists start by becoming [Ash Slaves](Lore_Ash_Slave.md) and [Ash Zombies](Lore_Ash_Zombie.md), progressing into [Ash Ghouls](Lore_Ash_Ghoul.md), then finally becoming Ascended Sleepers.

In appearance their faces are covered in strange tentacles filled with holes,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Sleeper-6)</sup> these tentacles are seemingly used as [musical](Lore_Music.md) [instruments](Lore_Instruments.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7)</sup> They can afflict their opponents with a variety of deadly Blight diseases as well as damaging frost, fire, and shock attacks.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Sleeper-6)</sup> In personality these creatures act above petty concerns. They have little concern for their own deaths, believing that if they are slain they will return as if waking from a dream.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Sleeper D-1)</sup> When slain, these creatures' bodies disappear,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Sleeper-6)</sup> [ash salts](Lore_Alchemy_A.md#Ash_Salts) can be harvested from their remains.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWGen Ash Salts-8)</sup>

After the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) killed Dagoth Ur and his Ash Vampire kin, the remaining ash creatures and Corprus beasts survived and still had to be hunted down and destroyed.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Vivec-9)</sup> Dagoth Ur's followers largely fell into despair after his death.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Dreamer-10)</sup> It is unknown if any Ascended Sleepers still remain on [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).
