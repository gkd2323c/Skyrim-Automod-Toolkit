[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/SR-npc-Malkus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Malkus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Malkus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Malkus.jpg) Malkus **Malkus** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) found in [Forebears' Holdout](Skyrim_Forebears%27_Holdout.md). He ambushed the Moth Priest [Dexion Evicus](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) south of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) and took him to Forebears' Holdout in an attempt to enthrall him. On the body of a dead vampire near Dragon Bridge, you will find a *[note](Skyrim_Vampire%27s_Note.md)* from Malkus. If one sided with the vampires, then he will be found dead next to the magical barrier, slain by the Dawnguard agent [Vanik](Skyrim_Vanik.md).

Malkus wears a set of light gray colored [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) with a [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) enchantment, and a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), as well as a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold. He also carries the special [Weystone Focus](Skyrim_Weystone_Focus.md) used to create the magical barrier that keeps Dexion imprisoned.

## Quest-Related Events
You will find Malkus near the magical barrier, slowly succeeding in enthralling the Moth Priest, saying:

**Malkus**: *"The more you fight me, the more you will suffer, mortal."*
**Priest**: *"I will resist you, monster. I must!"*
**Malkus**: *"How much longer can you keep this up, Moth Priest? Your mind was strong, but you're exhausted from the struggle."*
**Priest**: *"Must... resist..."*
**Malkus**: *"Yes, I can feel your defenses crumbling. You want it to end. You want to give in to me. Now, acknowledge me as your master!"*
**Priest**: *"Yes. Master."*

## Notes
- He is the only vampiric Orc in the entire game.
- A [letter](Skyrim_Letter_from_Harkon.md) from [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) explains why Malkus kidnapped Dexion, although it is not found in the game.
