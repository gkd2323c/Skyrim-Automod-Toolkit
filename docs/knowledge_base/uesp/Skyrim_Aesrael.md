[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/SR-npc-Aesrael.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Aesrael.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aesrael.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Aesrael.jpg) Aesrael Aesrael is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) who is encountered at a camp west of [Sunderstone Gorge](Skyrim_Sunderstone_Gorge.md) during the quest [Once A Hunter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Once_A_Hunter). He grew up as an orphan and gave up his life as a notorious member of the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) bandit gang, now living at a camp with his wolf [Dusk](Skyrim_Dusk.md). He is hostile and will attack on sight.

Aesrael wears [Hide Armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md) and [Hide Boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md). He wields a leveled bow, carries 50 steel arrows, a random amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), [Aesrael's Journal](Skyrim_Aesrael%27s_Journal.md) and [a key to his chest](Skyrim_Aesrael%27s_Key.md), and has a 25% chance to carry a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md). The key is needed for the related quest to unlock the chest containing the [Elven Hunter Armor](Skyrim_Elven_Hunter_Armor.md) and a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v7](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v7)*.

He is accompanied by Dusk, who will likely detect you before he does and attack.
