[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Gonfalon Head Lighthouse is a tower that anchors the western end of [Gonfalon Bay](Lore_Gonfalon_Bay.md) (both the large bay and the eponymous city) in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres_Archipelago.md). As its name suggests, it is named after Gonfalon Head, which stands on the Sapphire Coast of southern [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md). The beacon is a monument blessed by [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md), the goddess of the winds and sea, and a beloved landmark by local mariners.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VGTHI-1)</sup> An order dedicated to Kynareth uses her idol to start the divine light at the top.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMenoit-2)</sup>

## History
The order of Kynareth who watch the lighthouse employ a keeper and believe that the lighthouse's idol blesses [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) with the divine's light. The idol otherwise has no purpose or worth outside of the lighthouse. There have been two known lighthouses on Gonfalon Head; the second beacon was built in the early first century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMenoit-2)</sup> during the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SH6-3)</sup> By the sixth century, it was in a dangerous state of disrepair, until Duchess [Elea Dufort](Lore_Elea_Dufort.md) ordered for its complete renovation.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMenoit-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md) pillaged the lighthouse and stole the idol to sabotage it. As a result, ships carrying leaders of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) crashed on the coast. The famed investigator [Arabelle Davaux](Lore_Arabelle_Davaux.md) enlisted [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to investigate the lighthouse and its keeper, Menoit. The Vestige helped Menoit by retrieving the idol from the Ascendant Order's camp to the west. With it returned to the tower, Light-Keeper Menoit performed a ritual to reignite the light and provided the Vestige with the intel they needed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKnights-4)</sup>
