## Paralysis
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/LG-cardart-Paralyze.png/200px-LG-cardart-Paralyze.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Paralyze.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Paralyze.png) A paralyzed nord [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-icon-effect-Paralyze.jpg) **Paralysis**, sometimes known as **Paralyze**, was a spell belonging to [alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) and [illusion](Lore_Illusion.md). It freezes the afflicted creature in place, not letting them attack or move. It is considered a type of poison.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARERPoison-1)</sup> High Elves possess natural resistance to paralysis.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFAltmer Stats-2)</sup>

Known variants of this spell include:

- [Medusa's Gaze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Spells_M#Medusa.27s_Gaze)

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:The_Known_Spellbook), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Paralysis), [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Spells), [Dawnstar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Dawnstar:Magic), [Stormhold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Stormhold:Magic), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Paralysis_(spell)), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Paralyze), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Paralyze_(spell).md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Rune_of_Eldritch_Horror), [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Paralyze), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Paralyze), [Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Sheogorath%27s_Gauntlet)

**Spell schools:** [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md), [Illusion](Lore_Illusion.md)
## Passwall
**Passwall** was a powerful [alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) spell of the 6th Circinate. It allowed the user to burrow through dungeon walls.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARPasswall-3)</sup> The spell was actively used during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), but by [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) its secrets had been lost for nearly a generation.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SR-4)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Passwall)

**Spell schools:** [Alteration](Lore_Alteration.md)
