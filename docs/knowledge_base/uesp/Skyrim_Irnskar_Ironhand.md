[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand.jpg) Irnskar Ironhand [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Irnskar_Ironhand_02.jpg) *"What news have you heard from the war, Irnskar?"* **Irnskar Ironhand** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who lives in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) where he acts as Thane [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md)'s personal protector. He is owed a substantial gambling debt by [Octieve San](Skyrim_Octieve_San.md), an old [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) who also lives in Solitude. During the [related quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Few_Words_with_You), you will have to settle things for Octieve San.

Irnskar lives a busy life in Solitude, although not exactly as intended (see [bugs](#Bugs)). He sleeps in [Bryling's House](Skyrim_Bryling%27s_House.md) between midnight and 8am. He then spends the entire day inside the house, and only ventures outside on two occasions; at 10am he visits [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md) for two hours, and at 2pm he visits [Solitude Blacksmith](Skyrim_Solitude_Blacksmith.md), also for two hours.

Irnskar wears a set of iron [armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots), and [gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gauntlets). He carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Bryling%27s_House) to Bryling's house, and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
When you approach him, he will say:

*"Unless we have specific business, I'm not interested."* *"When I was a young rascal, I broke a man's arm with a single punch. Course, I had a horseshoe hidden in my glove..."* Due to the bug in his schedule, his other greeting can only be heard when Bryling is at home between 8pm and midnight:

*"I think Bryling's the one you want to talk to. I'm mostly just here to scare away the riff raff"*. If Bryling is dead, he will replace her (at least temporarily) as thane, and will greet you with:

*"You don't want to talk to me. I'm just a stand in for a dead Thane."*
## Conversations
Irnskar may be seen talking with Bryling about everything from the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) to her new gown from [Endarie](Skyrim_Endarie.md):

**Bryling**: *"What news have you heard from the war, Irnskar?"*
**Irnskar**: *"You won't like it, my lady."*
**Bryling**: *"I'm well aware that I won't like it. But it's my duty to keep my people safe. Tell me."*
**Irnskar**: *"Possession of the holds change on an almost daily basis, but I can report that Haafingar is still secure. Your people are safe."*
**Bryling**: *"I'm not just talking about my subjects. I'm talking about my people: the proud Nords of Skyrim."*
**Irnskar**: *"I told you you wouldn't like the news."* **Irnskar**: *"My lady, I'm pleased to inform you that you have a new gown for court, specially tailored by madame Endarie."*
**Bryling**: *"Really? How did we afford that? Besides, I like the gown I already have."*
**Irnskar**: *"Madame Endarie insisted. She made it free of charge, in honor of your lenience about last month's rent discrepancies."*
**Bryling**: *"That was suspiciously nice of her. I thought she hated everyone."*
**Irnskar**: *"She does, my lady. But apparently, she hates you just a little less than the others."*

**I'd like you to forgive Octieve's debts.** *"Debts are debts. Either he pays them, or his kind do."* You can then choose between four options, with differing replies, displayed in the table below:

## Bugs
- Irnskar was supposed to keep watch in the lobby of the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) from 8am to 8pm, but an oversight in his `Solitude Palace Lobby Sandbox8x12` package prevents it from activating, thus keeping him in the house all day long. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
