[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-npc-Sosia_Tremellia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sosia_Tremellia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sosia_Tremellia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sosia_Tremellia.jpg) Sosia Tremellia **Sosia Tremellia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) living with her husband [Skaggi Scar-Face](Skyrim_Skaggi_Scar-Face.md), the owner of [Left Hand Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Left_Hand_Mine) south of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

When you arrive at the mine for the first time, you will find all inhabitants gathered outside, circled around the two survivors from [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md). Sosia will be there too, but will not participate in the heated conversation about the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) attack. After the meeting, she will return to her regular duties. She sleeps in her husband's [house](Skyrim_Skaggi%27s_House.md) every night between midnight and 6am and starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. At 7am, she ventures outside, and uses a pickaxe on the rock next to the front door, or wanders around carrying her basket with herbs. At noon and 6pm she enters the house for quick one-hour meals. After her dinner, she stays inside until it's time to go to bed.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1d8) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452f) and wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries her [house key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Skaggi%27s_House) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).
