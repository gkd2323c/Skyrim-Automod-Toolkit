[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/09/SR-npc-Rahd.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Rahd.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rahd.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Rahd.jpg) Rahd's body **Rahd**, also known to his fellow bandit compatriots as **Longhammer**, was a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) who was slain by the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) and his corpse can be found at the furthest section of the [Liar's Retreat Den](Skyrim_Liar%27s_Retreat.md). He was serving as the barkeep there until the Falmer tunneled in and attacked. It appears that he has been heavily tortured and a [Falmer sword](Skyrim_Falmer_Sword.md) has been thrust into his abdomen, just below the sternum.

Next to his body, you can find [The Longhammer](Skyrim_The_Longhammer.md), a unique warhammer. It has a 30% greater attacking speed than other warhammers, eclipsing even the mighty [Daedric warhammer](Skyrim_Daedric_Warhammer.md) in damage per second, making it one of the most powerful weapons in the game.

Rahd can be found wearing some barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) along with a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md#0005b6a0). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) along with a small chance of also carrying a small amount of gold or a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md).
