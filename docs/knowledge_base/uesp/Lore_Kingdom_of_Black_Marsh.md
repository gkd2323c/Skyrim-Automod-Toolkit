# Kingdom of Black Marsh


Throughout the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), several major cities in Black Marsh were governed by various monarchs. Before he was the Emperor of Tamriel, [Magnus Septim](Lore_Magnus_Septim.md) ruled the Kingdom of Lilmoth.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TWQ5-8)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late Third Era, the major cities were ruled by a monarch: Queen Demia in [Gideon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gideon),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Gideon-9)</sup> King Claudios in [Blackrose](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blackrose),<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Blackrose-10)</sup> King Herula in [Archon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Archon),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Archon-11)</sup> King Germanus in the capital, [Helstrom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helstrom),<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Helstrom-12)</sup> Queen Artomeda in [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth_(kingdom).md),<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Lilmoth-13)</sup> Queen Heciana in [Soulrest](Lore_Soulrest.md),<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Soulrest-14)</sup> Queen Aphiana in [Stormhold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Stormhold),<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Stormhold-15)</sup> and King Tibus in [Thorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Thorn).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Thorn-16)</sup>

Whatever the existence of an Argonian monarchy may be, by the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) a political group known as the [An-Xileel](Lore_An-Xileel.md) formed and were instrumental in defending Black Marsh from [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s invasion. The kingdom later seceded from the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) after the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) incited the Argonians to mount a massive uprising against southern Morrowind.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-RT4-17)</sup> The exact structure of the An-Xileel is unknown, but they were known to have had an Archwarden by the name of Qajalil in [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48),<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-TIC-18)</sup><sup>:30</sup> and were led by **The Organism**, the ruling council based in [Lilmoth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lilmoth).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-TIC-18)</sup><sup>:37</sup>
