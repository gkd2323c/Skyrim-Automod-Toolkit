Nedic Dueling Swords **by [Merethic Society](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Merethic_Society)** *A plaque describing two ancient dueling swords*
---
Although a barbarous and savage society, the ancient [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) people (possible ancestors of the modern [Nords](Lore_Nord.md)) were famed for their skill in metallurgy. Seen here are two pristine dueling swords from [that era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), once wielded by the [draugr](Lore_Draugr.md) lord Haltaf in his subterranean lair.
