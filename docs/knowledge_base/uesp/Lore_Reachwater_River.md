[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/ON-place-Reachwater_Rock.jpg/200px-ON-place-Reachwater_Rock.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reachwater_Rock.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reachwater_Rock.jpg) Reachwater River (ESO) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg) Forsworn Camp by the Reachwater River circa 4E 201 The Reachwater River is one of the waterways within [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FCRR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KR-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup><sup>:524</sup> It originates to the southeast of the [Briar Rock Ruins](Lore_Briar_Rock_Ruins.md) and to the west of [Vateshran Hollows](Lore_Vateshran_Hollows.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRE-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRS-5)</sup> Notably, the entrance to Reachwater Rock, the tomb of Archmage [Gauldur](Lore_Gauldur.md), is concealed behind one of its cascading waterfalls. Both it and the Reachwater Cavern, where the tomb is located, are named after the river.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RR-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-RR2-7)</sup> The river's course eventually concludes as it joins the [Karth River](Lore_Karth_River.md) further north, south of [Karthspire](Lore_Karthspire.md) and the [Sky Haven Temple](Lore_Sky_Haven_Temple.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BOW-8)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TRE-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TRS-5)</sup> Among the numerous [Forsworn](Lore_Forsworn.md) camps, one was prominently situated along the river's shore.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FCRR-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The Reachwater Pass, situated in the northern part of the Reach, despite its similar name, is not connected to the Reachwater River. Instead, it lies north of an unknown Karth's tributary that originates to the west of [Bthar-Zel](Lore_Bthar-Zel.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DM-9)</sup>
