## [Heart of Valenwood](Lore_Heart_of_Valenwood.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/ON-place-Heart_of_Valenwood.jpg/200px-ON-place-Heart_of_Valenwood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Heart_of_Valenwood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Heart_of_Valenwood.jpg) The Heart of Valenwood The [Heart of Valenwood](Lore_Heart_of_Valenwood.md) is an ancient tree located in the **Valenheart**, the metaphorical heart of the [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) forest. In truth, the tree serves as a physical symbol of the forest's [soul](Lore_Soul.md); the true Heart of Valenwood is suffused throughout the entirety of the forest. Nevertheless, life-giving power seems to emanate from the center, like lifeblood flowing from a heart.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Hectahame)

## Hibiscus
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/ON-furnishing-Flower%2C_Grandmother_Hibiscus.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Flower%2C_Grandmother_Hibiscus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Flower,_Grandmother_Hibiscus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Flower,_Grandmother_Hibiscus.jpg) A grandmother hibiscus A flowering plant can be found in several regions across Tamriel and are popular garden flowers in certain parts of the Rift.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAG-1)</sup> The flowers are commonly orange, but can appear in other colors. The flower of the hibiscus is also used in some tea blends.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EC-2)</sup>

The [Martyrs' Copperleaf](Lore_Flora_M.md#Martyrs.27_Copperleaf) from the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) is said to have a similar scent to a hibiscus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOMC-3)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

## [Hist](Lore_Hist.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/ON-place-Hatching_Pools.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hatching_Pools.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hatching_Pools.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hatching_Pools.jpg) Hist Tree The [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) are a species of giant spore trees growing in the innermost swamps of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) are known to have deep connections with the Hist, calling themselves "people of the root", and licking the leaking sap of their trunks in religious rites. The trees are, in fact, a sentient race, claimed to be more ancient than all the races of man and mer.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

## Hist Bulb
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/ON-flora-Hist_Bulb.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Hist_Bulb.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Hist_Bulb.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Hist_Bulb.jpg) Hist Bulb Hist Bulbs are a species of plants native to [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) and possibly other parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). They have bioluminescent growths and produce a natural insect salve when threatened.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSLVTS-4)</sup> It is unknown if they are related to the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).
