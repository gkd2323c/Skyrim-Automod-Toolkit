[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/ON-map-Topal_Island_%28Blackwood%29.jpg/200px-ON-map-Topal_Island_%28Blackwood%29.jpg)](File_ON-map-Topal_Island_(Blackwood).jpg.md) [](File_ON-map-Topal_Island_(Blackwood).jpg.md) Topal Island overhead (ESO) Topal Island is a far-off island region<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FTTRT-1)</sup> (sometimes depicted as a chain of three islands),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Classic Tamriel Map-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Arena Deluxe-3)</sup> located in the middle of [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md). The island region is located roughly southeast of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md) and northwest of [Soulrest](Lore_Soulrest.md), adjacent to the strip of land around [Xi-Tsei](Lore_Xi-Tsei.md) that belongs to [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Additionally, the volcanic islet of [Grand Topal Hideaway](Lore_Grand_Topal_Hideaway.md) lies off the eastern coast of Topal Island.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The island shares its namesake with Topal Bay, which was named after [Topal the Pilot](Lore_Topal_the_Pilot.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

Sometime after the [Empire of Cyrodiil](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md)'s collapse, Topal Bay pirates terrorized the waters around the island. Voldar, the Terror of Topal Bay, plundered several ships west of Topal Island, and the Blue Wamasu, a notorious band of pirates, would often hang around Topal Island's coast.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-POTB-6)</sup>

By [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), the island was considered a territory of the [Elsweyr Confederacy](Lore_Elsweyr_Confederacy.md) by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1Map-7)</sup> However, by the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Empire considered the isle to be a territory of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MCMap-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-OCMap-9)</sup>

## Notes
- The island first appeared as an unnamed chain of three islands on the [*Arena* map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Bethsoft_Website_Map_Of_Tamriel.jpg), not part of any particular province. Later maps depict it is as one large isle in [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md).
- An early name for it was Topal Isle,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-10)</sup> and there were once plans to feature it as a [battleground](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Battlegrounds) in ESO.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
