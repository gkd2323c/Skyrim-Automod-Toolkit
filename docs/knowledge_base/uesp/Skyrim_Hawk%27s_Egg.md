[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/SR-ingredient-Hawk%27s_Egg.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Hawk%27s_Egg.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Hawk%27s_Egg.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Hawk%27s_Egg.jpg) Hawks' Nest **Hawk's Eggs** can be harvested from hawks' nests.

Hawks' nests look like other birds' nests which can be found throughout Skyrim, but you can tell the difference before seeing their name by the [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md) which may be lying next to them. They are only found in two places in the game, making these the only two sources of the ingredient: On the roofs of [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) and [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md) after building the [Main Hall](Skyrim_Main_Hall.md) addition. You can reach these nests with a tower-style addition (such as the Alchemy Laboratory or the Library), or by using [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md) from a proper position for either property. You can also climb up the rocks next to Lakeview Manor and jump to the roof.

## Notes
- Although you can also find a nest and two [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md) around it on the roof of [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md), it is a [Rock Warbler](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg) nest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.11, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=23030)) The nest was changed to a third hawks' nest. It can be reached in the same ways as the other two.
- Hawk's eggs have the same alchemical properties as [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md). Combining these two ingredients will therefore result in all four effects being discovered at once.
- [Hawk](Skyrim_Hawk.md) nests are present in Skyrim without having Hearthfire installed, such as those found in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). However, they cannot be activated and contain no egg, and are usually inaccessible. In the event that you can access them, the moment you kill its owning hawk, the nest will vanish.

## Bugs
- Hawk nests may never respawn due to incorrect seasonal settings. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.5, fixes this bug.
