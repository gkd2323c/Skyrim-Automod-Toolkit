- Please note: there is a miniscule *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* risk of itchiness, irritability, and minor hair growth, but what a small price to pay to defend yourself and those you love from the horrors of feral [vampirism](Lore_Vampirism.md)!
