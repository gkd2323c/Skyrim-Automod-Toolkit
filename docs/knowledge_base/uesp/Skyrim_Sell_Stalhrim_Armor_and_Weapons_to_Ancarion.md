## Quick Walkthrough
1. Agree to sell [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) equipment to [Ancarion](Skyrim_Ancarion.md).
2. Sell stalhrim equipment.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/12/SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Northshore_Landing_02.jpg) The [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) ship at [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md) During the quest [A New Source of Stalhrim](Skyrim_A_New_Source_of_Stalhrim.md), you will encounter the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) at an abandoned dock with a ruined shack located at the northwestern tip of Solstheim, [Northshore Landing](Skyrim_Northshore_Landing.md). The landing is inhabited only by a few [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md) and will return to having only mudcrabs after the completion or failure of the quest. They seek to obtain [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) equipment for the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md).

As soon as you near the ship, a [Thalmor Guard](Skyrim_Thalmor.md#Generic_Thalmor_Members) will approach you and suggest you move along. You now have two options, one to begin negotiations with Ancarion, and the second to begin conflict. You can choose to fight the entire crew or you can ask to talk to the leader. The latter will make him point you towards [Ancarion](Skyrim_Ancarion.md), who will be wandering around on-deck.

Ancarion will be angry from the start but willing to talk. When you mention the Stalhrim map, he will admit to torturing Baldor, and then turn his attention to you: *"So you know my purpose here, then? I suppose you must have found that dullard of a blacksmith. Unfortunately for you, my mission here is also a secret. To protect it, I have no choice but to silence you. Now, give me one good reason that I should not kill you where you stand."* Three options are available. Note that successfully completing the third option and starting the hidden quest requires a hard [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) check skill. The check is either at least 75 or [53](Skyrim_Persuasion.md).

Return to Skaal Village and seek out the blacksmith who will have returned from his captivity by now. When you hand him the map he will provide a very special reward, namely the ability to create [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) weapons and armor: *"I know you faced great danger to bring this map to me. There are no words to tell how glad my heart is. Thank you, brave one. I name you friend of the Skaal, and I will trust you with the knowledge of forging stalhrim. If you bring stalhrim here to my forge, you can use my tools to make what you will from it."* This completes the quest, but will also attract the attention of residing historian [Tharstan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tharstan) who will seek you out with a new [assignment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Legacy).

Stalhrim equipment can be found as random loot on Solstheim. [Glover Mallory](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md) when at reasonably high appropriate levels (35+) may be stocked with Stalhrim equipment, so you may be able to buy weapons off of him and keep the [mined Stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim_Source.md) you have earned. When you complete the prerequisite quest, it can also be forged if you have the [Ebony Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Smithing) perk. Return to Ancarion when you have obtained a stalhrim weapon or armor piece, and he will be willing to purchase it. There is, however, a bug that makes it so he does not have any gold.

## Notes
- With the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk, Ancarion will buy other items as well as stalhrim equipment.

## Bugs
- If you choose the option of selling Stalhrim armor and weapons to Ancarion, he may not have any gold because he is not the owner of his supposed merchant chest, nor a member of the technical [Ancarion Services](Skyrim_Factions_A.md#DLC2Ancarion Services Faction) faction, to which his chest is linked.
- To remove the incomplete quest, you can remove it by typing `setobjectivecompleted DLC2SV02Ancarion Merchant 10 1`.
