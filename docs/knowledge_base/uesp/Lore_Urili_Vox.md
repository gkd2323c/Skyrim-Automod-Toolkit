Lady Urili Vox was a high-ranking [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) [mehra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mehra) in the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAlmalexia-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOAspera-3)</sup> She held the postion of the Magistrix of Morals and served as a hand maiden to [Lady Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) during the [Age of Heroes](Lore_Age_of_Heroes.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DZS-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ADESO-5)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup>

## History
Urili Vox's son, Meram Vox, had been convinced by the Daedric Prince [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) to stand against the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md), believing them to be false gods. Urili attempted to convince her son that such actions were folly, but he refused to follow her advice. After he attempted to steal the artifact Veloth's Judgement from the Tribunal Temple at [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), he was caught and put down by the Lady Almalexia right in front of Urili.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mf H-6)</sup>

After these events she too came under the influence of Boethiah, and in secret Vox led a group called the Maulborn -- heretics who sought to overthrow the [Tribunate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tribunate) and restore [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) to the old ways before their reign, as her son Meram wanted.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Mf H-6)</sup> As a part of the master plan, the Maulborn created the [Llodos Plague](Lore_Llodos_Plague.md) and spread it across [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) to weaken support for the Tribunal.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TSo T-7)</sup> She later invaded the Tribunal Temple at Mournhold and obtained [Veloth's Judgement](Lore_Veloth%27s_Judgement.md), which she used as a weapon against Almalexia's followers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TSo T-7)</sup> Vox was finally slain on Almalexia's behalf by a hero called [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in the depths of Eidolon's Hollow (an ancient [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) shrine dedicated to the [Good Daedra](Lore_Good_Daedra.md)) in easternmost Deshaan.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TJo V-8)</sup>
