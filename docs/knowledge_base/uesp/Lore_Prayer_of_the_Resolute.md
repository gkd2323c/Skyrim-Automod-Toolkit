Prayer of the Resolute *Religious doctrine from the Resolutes of Stendarr*
---
Blessed be the name of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md), the God of Mercy.

He strengthens and unifies his [Resolutes](Lore_Resolutes_of_Stendarr.md) through his wisdom and blessings.

He calls us by day to train with sword and shield to strengthen our might; and by night to pray in his name to strengthen our souls.

He takes pity upon us, his humble servants, and grants unto us mercy.

His holy light of truth will cast out the forces of darkness and rain justice upon [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) abominations.

Glory shall be his, forever.
