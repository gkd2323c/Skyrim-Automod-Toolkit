# Bound Weapon

| --- | --- |
| School | [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) |
| Type | Offensive |

**Creates a magic <weapon> for <dur> seconds. Sheathe it to dispel.**

**Bound Weapon** causes the spellcaster to be equipped with a magical version of the specified weapon for a duration of time, after which the weapon vanishes. To dispel the weapon before the time has elapsed, [sheathe](Skyrim_Controls.md#Combat) the weapon, or simply equip another spell or weapon in that hand.

## Types of Bound Weapon
In each case, the second (Mystic) version of the weapon is the one that appears if the [Mystic Binding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mystic_Binding) perk has been unlocked.

## Notes
- Bound weapons cannot be disarmed.
- Unlike physical weapons, Bound ones weigh nothing.
- Bound weapons can be used in places where weapons are not supposed to be available, such as [jail](Skyrim_Jail.md), [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), and the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md).
- Bound weapons cannot be used in conjunction with [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md) due to their perk enchantments.
- Bound Crossbows, Greatswords, Maces, War Axes, and Warhammers do not exist.
- If the [Soul Stealer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Stealer) perk has been unlocked, bound weapons automatically cast [Soul Trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md) on strike. This version of Soul Trap works somewhat differently to the enchantment applied to non-conjured weapons: the soul is trapped without the purple "soul absorbing" smoke that swirls around you, and the hissing sound effect. In the case of the Soul Stealer perk, the victim glows purple (as with the other Soul Trap), but that is all.
- If the [Oblivion Binding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Binding) perk has been unlocked, bound weapons automatically cast [Banish](Skyrim_Banish.md) and [Turn Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(effect).md) on strike. Both effects are level 99, so they will affect any susceptible monster.
- Equipping a weapon or spell while using a bound weapon will cause the bound weapon to vanish. - The exceptions to this are the Bound Sword and Bound Dagger<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>. It is possible to equip another one-handed weapon or spell in the other hand. Note which hand the weapon appears in so you can choose the correct one. When cast normally, the weapon will appear in whichever hand you cast the spell from. If dual-cast, the weapon will appear in your left hand, so you can afterwards equip something else in your right. You can even dual-wield two Bound Swords/Daggers by casting them separately.
- You can also use a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md) along with the one-handed bound weapons, but only in your left hand, so the weapon must be cast with the right hand, and thus cannot be dual-cast, since dual-cast always equips the weapon in the left hand.
- It is not possible to use any kind of Bound Weapon while on [horseback](Skyrim_Horses.md). Mounting a horse automatically sheathes your weapon first, canceling the spell. You cannot cast spells while mounted.
- Conjured weapons can be [poisoned](Skyrim_Poisons.md). However, they cannot be enchanted and they also cannot be improved at a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), due to them being conjured by a spell. In addition, the poison will disappear if you sheath the poisoned bound weapon or if the spell expires.
- Conjuring a weapon makes noise, which can attract unwanted attention while you are sneaking. This can be prevented with the [Quiet Casting](Skyrim_Quiet_Casting.md) perk.
- Using bound weapons in combat also increases your [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill, on top of the skill associated with the bound weapon used ([One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) for the Bound Sword and Bound Dagger<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>, [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) for the Bound Battleaxe and [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) for the Bound Bow). No Conjuration experience is gained by casting a Bound Weapon spell out of combat; however, if you cast a conjuration spell before combat, and later enter combat with the conjuration effect still in place, some Conjuration experience is awarded. If you kill someone with a bound weapon, the weapon's purple glow will be seen during the killcam cutscene.
- The Bound Bow will also give you a quiver of 100 [Bound Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Arrow) upon casting, which acts as any other bound weapon. Bound Arrows have a damage rating of 24, the same as [Daedric Arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Daedric_Arrow). - The [Arcane Archer Pack](Skyrim_Arcane_Archer_Pack.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> adds an adept conjuration spell tome to the game, [Bound Quiver](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Bound_Quiver), which gives you a lesser power to summon 100 Bound Arrows separately (so it can be cast while you wield a bow to prevent the need to recast Bound Bow). Unlike the Bound Arrows from Bound Bow, these arrows are affected by Mystic Binding, granting them a damage rating of 32 (considerably higher than the previously most powerful arrow, the [Dragonbone Arrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonbone_Arrow) with 25 damage). So to maximize damage with Bound Bow, you need to cast Bound Quiver *after* casting Bound Bow, a combined base damage rating of 56.
- You can use the arrows of your choice along with the Bound Bow (e.g. Dragonbone Arrows, which are the only ones that are better than Bound/Daedric Arrows without including [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)), by equipping the arrows after casting the spell, though you can't get your Bound Arrows back without recasting the spell. Regular arrows will still deplete as normal if you use them with a Bound Bow. You cannot, however, use the Bound Arrows from the Bound Bow Spell along with a regular bow (as unequipping the Bound Bow will also make the quiver vanish). - The Arcane Archer Pack's Bound Quiver provides the ability to use Bound Arrows with a regular bow.
- Any Bound Arrows that you have fired (whether they hit or missed) will continue to exist in the world even after the spell expires until you leave the area. They cannot be picked up or found in corpses' inventories like other arrows. As they are very easy to see, they can be useful as markers to remind yourself where you've been. The arrows can contribute to bloating a save game, however.
- The Bound Bow is an exceptionally desirable weapon in the early stages of the game - unmodified through perks, it is very fast and of Daedric power. The relevant Spell Tome is found in [Fort Amol Prison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fort_Amol_Prison) under a lantern in a bucket, guarded by five mages in total. Casting the spell will require 183 magicka at 15 (base) Conjuration skill, <180 as of 17. This amount of magicka may be achieved as early as level 1 via [Necromancer Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromancer_Amulet) (50 magicka bonus and + 25 fortify conjuration) or any combination of [Novice Hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Hood) (30 magicka), [Atronach](Skyrim_The_Atronach_Stone.md) [Standing Stone](Skyrim_Standing_Stones.md) (50 magicka, found just to the east of Fort Amol) and [Altmer *Highborn*](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) racial bonus (50 magicka) as well as outright through periodic consumption of [Elsweyr Fondue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elsweyr_Fondue) (100 magicka).
- With [Patch 1.9](Skyrim_Patch.md), the Bound Bow with Mystic Binding garners more XP per hit than any other bow in the game.
- Version 2.0.8 of the [Unoffocial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) removes the draw and sheath sounds for Bound weapons.

## Bugs
- Occasionally, when casting [Bound Battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bound_Battleaxe), you may have no weapon or spells show up as equipped but will constantly have the animations of carrying the bound axe. You cannot draw any weapons or spells even if you change equipment, and cannot sheathe this phantom axe. This effect does not seem to wear off when the spell would have ended. - The issue appears to only arise when you cast in the third person view, so as long as you remember to switch to first person before casting you shouldn't have any problems.
- Sitting down will fix this.
- Equipping a one handed weapon will fix this.
- The invisible bound axe can be "unequipped" by equipping hotkeyed spells.
- Although the Soul Stealer perk causes Bound Weapons to cast Soul Trap on enemies they hit, there is no animation of that due to the perk not having the effect script attached. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- The Bound Bow will turn raised undead even when you don't have the [Oblivion Binding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Binding) perk because the corresponding effect item has no conditions set. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- The Bound Bow enchantment for [Oblivion Binding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Binding) has an incorrect magnitude setting of 50 instead of 99. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- Both the Bound Sword and Bound Dagger magic effect have their skill use multiplier set at 10, as opposed to the other bound weapons that have it set at 1, causing much greater xp gain. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.9 and 2.1.2 (respectively), fixes this bug.
- While sneaking, casting bound weapons can occasionally result in a longer than usual delay.
- The Bound Sword and Bound Dagger have a 0 critical damage multiplier instead of 1. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The Bound Dagger has incorrect 1st person animations attached, specifically sword animations, causing the dagger not to be silent as it should be. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.2, fixes this bug.
- Graphical effects for the Bound Bow don't match up with the effects when Mystic Binding is in effect. All other bound weapons have effects that match each other. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.2.5, fixes this bug.
- If you have the perk, Soul Stealer may not activate when using a bound weapon; instead, you'll get the notification "Your weapon has insufficient charges" as if it were a regular, enchanted weapon. <sup>**?**</sup>
- In a bug similar to the one above, Oblivion Binding may not activate when using a bound weapon on a hostile, conjured creature, meaning it does nothing beyond the damage of the weapon on hit.
- When using the [Aetherial Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherial_Shield)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> in your offhand, the effects of the shield will no longer work after sheathing the bound bow. Re-equipping the shield fixes the issue. <sup>**?**</sup>
