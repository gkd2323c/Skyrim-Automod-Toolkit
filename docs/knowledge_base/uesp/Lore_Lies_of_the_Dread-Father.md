Lies of the Dread-Father **by [Nisswo Xeewulm](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Nisswo_Xeewulm)** *A poem regarding Sithis from the Clutch of Nisswo*
---
Round-tongues give it form and shape
The it that is turned into he
They whisper to his [decayed bride](Lore_Night_Mother.md)
To honor him, to worship him

They name it [father, dreaded so](Lore_Sithis.md)
They pray with blades of dreaded blood
They speak one facet of the truth
Something clinging to their tongues

Shapelessness given form
Change turned to stagnation
One truth that becomes untruth
[A brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) of something eyes
