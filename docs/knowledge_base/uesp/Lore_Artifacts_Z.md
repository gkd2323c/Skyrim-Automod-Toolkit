## Zenithar's Blessed Awl
[Zenithar's Blessed Awl](Lore_Zenithar%27s_Blessed_Awl.md) is a tool for weavers of thread, reed, and wood. Believers in the god of commerce [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md) claim that "through the awl, all designs are made possible, threads hold strong, and work progresses easily."

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Craglorn_Quest_Items#Blessed_Awl)

## Zenithar's Bleeding Whetstone
[Zenithar's Bleeding Whetstone](Lore_Zenithar%27s_Bleeding_Whetstone.md) is a lesser known [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) associated with god [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). Little is known of the sickle or of the practices it was tied to, though it was said to have been associated with blood rites long before such rituals became linked with sinister or macabre connotations. According to [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) it was quickly forgotten.

**Found in:** - [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Samara_Gautier)

## Zenithar's Warning and Zenithar's Wiles
[Zenithar's Warning and Zenithar's Wiles](Lore_Zenithar%27s_Warning_and_Zenithar%27s_Wiles.md) are a set of gloves associated with the God of Commerce [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). They were imbued with magical properties and effects such as reducing an adversary's ability and willingness to fight, and increasing people's disposition towards the wearer.

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Zenithar%27s_Warning)

## Zephyr
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Dwarven_Bow.jpg) Zephyr [Zephyr](Lore_Zephyr.md) is a legendary [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) bow. Forged from [Dwarven metal](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md), the bow is noted for its exceptional draw speed, allowing the user to fire arrows much faster than with a regular Dwarven bow. Zephyr is also said to have been imbued with acid, allowing the user to melt through armor with each shot.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Zephyr.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, [Legends Asia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Zephyr), [Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Zephyr)
