## Radiant Options
This is a radiant quest that you can receive from either [Korir](Skyrim_Korir.md) or [Kraldar](Skyrim_Kraldar.md), whichever is the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md). The jarl lives at the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md).

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Befriend the Jarl of Winterhold.
2. Assist three people of Winterhold.
3. Return to the jarl for your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This is an umbrella quest for several other quests that must be completed to become Thane of Winterhold. In order to receive this quest you must first aid the current jarl. If the jarl is Korir, then you must usually [Retrieve the Helm of Winterhold](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Jarl_-_Caves).md) for him first; however, if he was ousted from Winterhold during [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) and reinstated following [The Battle for Fort Kastav](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Kastav.md), that is sufficient to begin this quest. If the jarl is Kraldar, whether he was installed via [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md) or [Rescue from Fort Kastav](Skyrim_Rescue_from_Fort_Kastav.md), then the completion of that quest is sufficient to fulfill the prerequisite.

Once you have completed the prerequisite, speak with the jarl again and he will ask you to [assist the people of the hold](Skyrim_Making_Friends_and_Influencing_People.md). You must assist three people in the hold, usually by completing [favor quests](Skyrim_Favors.md) for them. Note that many of the quest givers are non-essential NPCs, so their deaths would result in a loss of potential quests to complete. If they die after you assist them, they still count towards your Thane status. You can also befriend merchants if you have the [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md) perk by investing in their stores.

### Other Quests
The following quests also increase the disposition of citizens of Winterhold, and therefore count towards this objective:

- **[The Black Star](Skyrim_The_Black_Star.md)**: Help find and repair [Azura's Star](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md).
- **[New Allegiances](Skyrim_New_Allegiances.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>: Turn the target into a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### College Quests
Completing any quest which increases the disposition of the mages at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) will also count towards this objective, including:

- **[Arniel's Endeavor](Skyrim_Arniel%27s_Endeavor.md)**: Uncover an ancient Dwemer secret. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Brelyna's Practice](Skyrim_Brelyna%27s_Practice.md)**: Help [Brelyna Maryon](Skyrim_Brelyna_Maryon.md) by allowing her to practice her spells on you.
- **[Fetch me that Book!](Skyrim_Fetch_me_that_Book!.md)**: Fetch a book for [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md)) (second time only)
- **[First Lessons](Skyrim_First_Lessons.md)**: Join the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md).
- **[J'zargo's Experiment](Skyrim_J%27zargo%27s_Experiment.md)**: Help [a student](Skyrim_J%27zargo.md) test out a fiery new spell.
- **[Onmund's Request](Skyrim_Onmund%27s_Request.md)**: Help [Onmund](Skyrim_Onmund.md) negotiate the return of a family amulet from [Enthir](Skyrim_Enthir.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Out of Balance](Skyrim_Out_of_Balance.md)**: Purify the focus points for magical energy around the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md).
- **[Restocking Soul Gems](Skyrim_Restocking_Soul_Gems.md)**: Help [Sergius Turrianus](Skyrim_Sergius_Turrianus.md) to fill his stock with [soul gems](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md).

[Enchanting Pick-Up](Skyrim_Enchanting_Pick-Up.md) and [Shalidor's Insights](Skyrim_Shalidor%27s_Insights.md) do not count as they do not affect anyone's disposition.

### Reward
After helping three inhabitants, the journal tells you to return to the jarl. The reward, the [Blade of Winterhold](Skyrim_Blade_of_Winterhold.md), is a random, leveled, enchanted sword. There is a 50% chance that it will be a sword (one-handed), and a 50% chance it will be a greatsword (two-handed).

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage Favor257 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest Favor257`.
