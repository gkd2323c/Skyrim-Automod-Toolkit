### A Bloody Trail
[![SR-place-A Bloody Trail.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-place-A_Bloody_Trail.jpg/200px-SR-place-A_Bloody_Trail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-A_Bloody_Trail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-A_Bloody_Trail.jpg) - POISnowy27a
- POISnowy27b
- POISnowy27c

Located northwest of [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) and South-southeast of [Shrine of Mehrunes Dagon](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) and west of [Stonehill Bluff](Skyrim_Stonehill_Bluff.md). This trail of blood leads to two dead Bosmer, with one of them carrying [Treasure Map VI](Skyrim_Treasure_Map_VI.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Treasure+Map+VI)

### Bandit's Hovel
[![SR-place-Bandit's Hovel.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel.jpg) [![SR-place-Bandit's Hovel 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Bandit%27s_Hovel_02.jpg) - POISnowy02

Southeast of [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) and northeast of [Ustengrav](Skyrim_Ustengrav.md) is a burnt house occupied by a bandit with a [common chest](Skyrim_Containers.md#Chest), lit fireplace and boards added for a makeshift roof over a bed. The state of the house changes during the [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) quest. Presumably, a dragon attack results in two burnt corpses, lit embers, scorches and a burnt carriage. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-13753&y=92254&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Barnacle Boat
[![SR-place-Barnacle Boat.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/28/SR-place-Barnacle_Boat.jpg/200px-SR-place-Barnacle_Boat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Barnacle_Boat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Barnacle_Boat.jpg) - POINorthern Coast14

West of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) along the northern coastline, within bowshot of the [Wreck Of The Brinehammer](Skyrim_Wreck_Of_The_Brinehammer.md), there is a small overturned boat guarded by [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md). Under the boat is a copy of the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) skill book *[Daughter of the Niben](Skyrim_Daughter_of_the_Niben.md)*, a [scimitar](Skyrim_Scimitar.md), a pair of [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md), and a [knapsack](Skyrim_Knapsack.md) containing random loot. In the immediate area are four [Nordic barnacle clusters](Skyrim_Nordic_Barnacle.md) and three [spiky grass](Skyrim_Grass_Pod.md) plants that can be harvested for their respective ingredients. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=6809&y=108140&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dawnstar Frost Troll Den
[![SR-place-Dawnstar Frost Troll Den.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d0/SR-place-Dawnstar_Frost_Troll_Den.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dawnstar_Frost_Troll_Den.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar_Frost_Troll_Den.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar_Frost_Troll_Den.jpg) - POINorthern Coast01

West of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) there is a troll cave which contains an unlocked treasure chest and a copy of the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill book *[Death Blow of Abernanit](Skyrim_Death_Blow_of_Abernanit.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=21583&y=105421&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Horker Standing Stones
[![SR-place-Horker Standing Stones.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-place-Horker_Standing_Stones.jpg/200px-SR-place-Horker_Standing_Stones.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Standing_Stones.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Horker_Standing_Stones.jpg) Northeast of [High Gate Ruins](Skyrim_High_Gate_Ruins.md) is a small island with Nordic standing stones that is also home to horkers.

### Ice Shard Wild Animal Den
[![SR-place-Ice Shard Wild Animal Den.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-place-Ice_Shard_Wild_Animal_Den.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ice_Shard_Wild_Animal_Den.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ice_Shard_Wild_Animal_Den.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ice_Shard_Wild_Animal_Den.jpg) - POISnowy09

Northwest of the [Weynon Stones](Skyrim_Weynon_Stones.md) is a cave inhabited by four [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md). A dead [mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) can be found outside the cave, and a dead [elk](Skyrim_Elk.md) can be found inside the cave. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=32256&y=50896&zoom=16&legacy=true)

### Mammoth Graveyard
[![SR-place-Mammoth Graveyard.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-place-Mammoth_Graveyard.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mammoth_Graveyard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mammoth_Graveyard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mammoth_Graveyard.jpg) - POITundra03

To the west of [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md) up the hill is a small clearing littered with the remains of 25 mammoths. Two [poachers](Skyrim_Poacher.md) can be found here, and will turn hostile if you get too close. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=25280&y=26225&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Shoreline Bandit Camp
[![SR-place-Shoreline Bandit Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-place-Shoreline_Bandit_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shoreline_Bandit_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Bandit_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Bandit_Camp.jpg) - POISnowy16

West of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), three [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) have set up a camp below a rock ledge. The only items of note in their camp are an unlocked treasure chest and a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[A Hypothetical Treachery](Skyrim_A_Hypothetical_Treachery.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=11053&y=101689&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Shoreline Lovers' Tent
[![SR-place-Shoreline Lovers' Tent.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a5/SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent.jpg) [![SR-place-Shoreline Lovers' Tent 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/ff/SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shoreline_Lovers%27_Tent_02.jpg) - POINorthern Coast15

On the shoreline east from Dawnstar Sanctuary. In the tent are two bedrolls, three empty wine bottles, an Amulet of Mara, sixteen Red Mountain Flowers, two Boiled Creme Treats and two pairs of shoes. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=38824&y=112992&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Sunken Treasures
[![SR-place-Sunken Treasures.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-place-Sunken_Treasures.jpg/200px-SR-place-Sunken_Treasures.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sunken_Treasures.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Sunken_Treasures.jpg) - POINorthern Coast20

Northwest of the [Wreck Of The Brinehammer](Skyrim_Wreck_Of_The_Brinehammer.md) are two islands. Face due north from the tip of the largest (eastern) one and dive until a sunken chest that contains *[Sithis](Skyrim_Sithis.md)* is found.

### Wayward Peak Summit
[![SR-place-Wayward Peak Summit.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-place-Wayward_Peak_Summit.jpg/200px-SR-place-Wayward_Peak_Summit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Peak_Summit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Wayward_Peak_Summit.jpg) On the mountain high above the [Great Lift at Alftand](Skyrim_Great_Lift_at_Alftand.md), there is a tattered flag flying. At the base of the flag is a leveled mace, a leveled helmet, and a satchel. Further back on the mountain is a silver ore vein. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=84928&y=59152&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Yorgrim Forest Spider Trap
[![SR-place-Yorgrim Forest Spider Trap.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/SR-place-Yorgrim_Forest_Spider_Trap.jpg/200px-SR-place-Yorgrim_Forest_Spider_Trap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yorgrim_Forest_Spider_Trap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yorgrim_Forest_Spider_Trap.jpg) - POISnowy25

Southwest of [Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md), along the path between [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md) and [Irkngthand](Skyrim_Irkngthand.md), you will find a pile of rubble with a lootable skeleton, a coin purse, and an unlocked Dwemer chest. As you approach, three [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) will drop from two flanking trees and attack. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=75192&y=35962&zoom=17&legacy=true)
