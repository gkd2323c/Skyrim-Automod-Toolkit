[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Samara [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/ON-creature-Assassin_Beetle.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Assassin_Beetle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Assassin_Beetle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Assassin_Beetle.jpg) A Tamrielic Assassin Beetle, the much larger descendant of Yokudan Samara Scarabs Samara is an island in the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) archipelago, between the [Azurian Sea](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Azurian_Sea) and the [Sea of Pearls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sea_of_Pearls).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup> Located southeast of [Akos Kasaz](Lore_Akos_Kasaz.md), it is low, lush, and warm, with many natural harbors.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLIOOY-2)</sup> Other nearby islands include [Irranhu](Lore_Irranhu.md) and [Moni](Lore_Moni.md). A sizable bay juts into the southern coast and almost divides the island into a western and eastern half.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Map-1)</sup>

[Assassin Beetles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Assassin_Beetle) were known to the Yokudans as Samara Scarabs, perhaps indicating it as their place of origin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-THOS-3)</sup>
