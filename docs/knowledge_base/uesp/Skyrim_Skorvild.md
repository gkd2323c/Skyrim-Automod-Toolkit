[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/SR-npc-Skorvild.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Skorvild.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skorvild.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Skorvild.jpg) Skorvild **Skorvild** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) destruction mage and former member of the Mythic Dawn, whose body can be found at [the Two Pillars](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Stendarr.md#The_Two_Pillars) during [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause_(quest).md).

He wears [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md), [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). He carries a full set of Mythic Dawn clothing, which includes the [robes](Skyrim_Mythic_Dawn_Robes.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Mythic_Dawn_Gloves.md), and [boots](Skyrim_Mythic_Dawn_Boots.md). He also carries a [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) and his [journal](Skyrim_Skorvild%27s_Journal.md).
