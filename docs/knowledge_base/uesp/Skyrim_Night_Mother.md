# Night Mother


The Night Mother is being transported by [Cicero](Skyrim_Cicero.md) to the Falkreath Sanctuary when his wagon wheel breaks and they are stranded together near [Loreius Farm](Skyrim_Loreius_Farm.md). Cicero and the Night Mother will make it to the Sanctuary with or without [your help](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Delayed_Burial), and will live there until [the destruction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Death_Incarnate) of the Falkreath Sanctuary. After the quest [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md), she will be transported to the [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) by [Babette](Skyrim_Babette.md) and [Nazir](Skyrim_Nazir.md), where she will remain indefinitely.

## Notes
- She is not technically an NPC. She is implemented as a talking activator, meaning she is just an inanimate object with some associated dialogue. Therefore, she does not have a race, health, magicka, Form ID, or any other statistics normally associated with NPCs. However, she will shed red blood if her corpse husk is attacked within her coffin with a bladed weapon.
- In *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*, the Night Mother's remains were merely a skeleton. However, 200 years later in Skyrim, she has a mummified appearance.
- When she speaks to you her corpse will appear brighter than normal.
