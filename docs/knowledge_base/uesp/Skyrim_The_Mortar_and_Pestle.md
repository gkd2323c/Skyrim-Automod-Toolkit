[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-place-The_Mortar_and_Pestle.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Mortar_and_Pestle.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Mortar_and_Pestle.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Mortar_and_Pestle.jpg) The Mortar and Pestle **The Mortar and Pestle** is the alchemy shop in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), between [Brina's House](Skyrim_Brina%27s_House.md) and [Beitild's House](Skyrim_Beitild%27s_House.md).

It is run by the elderly [Frida](Skyrim_Frida.md) who also lives above the main trading room.

It contains only one zone, *[The Mortar and Pestle](#Walkthrough)*.

**NOTE**: Like all other stores, The Mortar and Pestle has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietor Frida and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## Walkthrough
The main trading room is dimly lit and Frida can be found behind the counter every day between 8am and 8pm. Without the displayed merchandise, a lot of beverages reveal themselves. On the shelves of a non-respawning cupboard behind the counter are three bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine) and four bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) along with seven bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). Atop the counter are three bottles of alto wine and two bottles of wine. Underneath the counter are four additional bottles of wine, a single bottle of alto wine, and a [potion of resist shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Resist_Shock). There is also a small hidden [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), two non-respawning sacks with food, and a copy of the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Hearth Fire, v9](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Hearth_Fire,_v9)*. Above the shop counter are two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), some [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md), and two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), as well as three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). Along the wall next to the cupboard sits a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum), a non-respawning chest, and a respawning meat barrel. Underneath the stairs leading to the second floor is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) and a respawning food barrel.

Frida sleeps in her owned bed on the second floor, where even more beverages sit on some shelves next to a respawning food barrel in the northeastern corner: six bottles of wine and two bottles of alto wine. A dinner table is set with two wedges of [goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge) and one [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge), with two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on the side. There is also a [chicken's egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) and two bottles of alto wine. A non-respawning dresser in the corner holds clothes and on its top sit a few spare ingredients: one [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot), one [creep cluster](Skyrim_Creep_Cluster.md), one [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell), and one sample each of [frost salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md), [fire salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Salts), [ectoplasm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ectoplasm) and [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal). On the other side of the bed are five gold coins on a table and a non-respawning unlocked chest.
