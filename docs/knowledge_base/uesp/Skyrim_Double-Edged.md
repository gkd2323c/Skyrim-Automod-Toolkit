## Quick Walkthrough
1. Go to the [Cliffside Retreat](Skyrim_Cliffside_Retreat.md) and read [Zaharia's note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note).
2. (Optional) Disrupt Zaharia's plan and remove the [Note to Ehlhiel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_to_Ehlhiel) in [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md).
3. Go to [Lost Echo Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Echo_Cave) and defeat the person there.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/03/SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png/200px-SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Double-Edged03.png) The note sitting on the dresser [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg) Zaharia waiting for Ehlhiel This quest starts at [Cliffside Retreat](Skyrim_Cliffside_Retreat.md), with a barrel that has [Zaharia's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note) on top of it alongside [Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier__Ehlhiel). Read the note to start the quest, you can either help Zaharia, a [Crimson Dirk](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md), and kill the traitor Ehlhiel, or you can help save Ehlhiel's life.
If you decide to save Ehlhiel, head to the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in Solitude, read and take the [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Note_to_Ehlhiel) on the dresser upstairs on the right.

Either way go to [Lost Echo Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_Echo_Cave), where you will have to defeat [Zaharia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia) if you took the note from the Temple of the Divines or [Ehlhiel](Skyrim_Ehlhiel.md) if you didn't. Afterwards loot your reward from Zaharia.

## Notes
- Although the quest entries read that a Thalmor double agent betrayed a member of the Blades, *[Guard's Dossier: Ehlhiel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guard%27s_Dossier__Ehlhiel)* and *[Zaharia's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaharia%27s_Note)* reads that Ehlhiel betrayed the [Crimson Dirks](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) for the city guard captains and the [East Empire Company](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company). This suggests that the storyline of the quest was changed at some point, but the quest objectives were not updated to match.
