[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-npc-Gunther.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gunther.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunther.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gunther.jpg) Gunther **Gunther** (or **Gunther Frost-Foot**) is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) merchant who went missing at [Traitor's Post](Skyrim_Traitor%27s_Post.md). He was killed by [Erwan](Skyrim_Erwan.md) and her gang of [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) who currently occupy the destroyed cabin at Traitor's Post. You will be tasked with investigating his disappearance during the related quest. You will find his body propped against a chair near the ruined fireplace within the cabin, holding a [broom](Skyrim_Broom.md).

Erwan appears to be quite mentally disturbed and considers Gunther's corpse to be her "best friend". She has stripped the corpse naked and given him nothing but the [Ring of Masser](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Masser) to wear. This ring is quite valuable and you will need to loot it from his corpse as part of the related quest. He is also carrying a small amount of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) as well as a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v5](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v5)*. Based on a [letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Edward) held by Erwan, it appears that Gunther was lured to his doom by a man named Edward in order to deliver this book to Erwan.
