Misplaced Journal *A journal of an unknown vampire* *
---
Hardarel and I managed to remain friends, in spite of everything in this place working against even the simplest gestures of kindness. We worked together, we shared knowledge, and we found aspects. We kept our secrets from the others. But she is gone. For days now or weeks maybe. Time is meaningless in here.

I traded an aspect of Corruption for information from a wounded [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) warrior. He holds great favor with the elder and knows much. He said she is beyond any help, cast into the Halls of the Tormented. For what reason, I will probably never know. If there was even a reason. We have all learned of the lost within those halls. I cannot say souls, because [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) owns our [souls](Lore_Soul.md) and does what he pleases, and it pleases him to leave some of his vampire children wandering, craving the heat of blood, but in a form that can never taste it again. Mindless, neither alive nor undead. Gone.

My poor friend.
