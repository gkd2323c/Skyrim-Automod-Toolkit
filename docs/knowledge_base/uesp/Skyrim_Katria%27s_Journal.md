Katria's Journal **by [Katria](Skyrim_Katria.md)** *Research notes about the location of an ancient Dwemer forge*
---

- 17th of Sun's Height

Damn that scheming elf! Damn him! He steals my notes, my discovery, and [publishes it](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Wars) under his name! Twenty years of my life spent digging through those ruins, and what do I get? A dedication? 'Friend and Colleague' my arse. He's just mocking me!

Well, I'll have the last laugh. [Taron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Taron_Dreth) may have my theory, but he doesn't have any proof, not yet. If I can find [the Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge) first, I can show the world this is my discovery. Mine, not his!

- 4th of Last Seed

I've got a lead. One of the moldy old books in [Mzund](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mzund) mentioned the Forge. Ruined through and through, but I was able to copy down a few scraps, including a map of the first-era Dwemer kingdoms. Need to cross-reference it with modern borders when I get back.

[![SR-book-Dlc1ld mapsmall.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/2a/SR-book-Dlc1ld_mapsmall.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Dlc1ld_mapsmall.png) Here's the result:

**[1 - Arkngthamz](Skyrim_Arkngthamz.md)**

- This one, 'Arkngthamz', was on the inscription, too. Apparently the main Aetherium research center. Seems like the place to start.

**[2 -](Skyrim_Deep_Folk_Crossing.md)**

- Mentioned as a 'Bthar-zel' ('allied city'?). Not [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md), though. Looks like a smaller site, north or northwest of there, on the river.

**[3 -](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raldbthar)**

- The primary source of Aetherium. Extracted from some deeper mine?

**[4 -](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft#Dwarven_Storeroom)**

- A storage site for raw Aetherium, just outside a major city. Apparently, it had to be kept outside because the Aetherium was 'harmonically volatile'.

**[5 -](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft)**

- No name, no identifying information, but page after page of ruined diagrams. Couldn't make out much, but it looks like - an astrolabe, a gear, some sort of [crest](Skyrim_Aetherium_Crest.md) in [four pieces](Skyrim_Aetherium_Shard.md)? Could this be the location of the Forge? Something important, at least.

- 22nd of Last Seed

Arkngthamz. My map may be rough, but it was enough to get me here. There may be something to this after all.

- 23rd of Last Seed

Reached the Treasury. There's a Tonal Lock here, still active. Judging from the corpses, it's safe to say the traps are still active, too.

Couldn't find any clues, so I'll make a sketch and work through this carefully. Five Resonators. Five tones. Just have to hit them in the right order. Let's see...
