## Ingredients
33 guaranteed samples can be found in 16 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 4 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 4 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 3 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
