Crow's Spell of Binding *A Daedric incantation*
---
By the snare of [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)

And the trail of [Namira](Lore_Namira.md)'s slime

I bind this place

Three times seven times

By [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)'s curse

And the skull of [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md)

I bind (name of victim)

To this summoning arena

By the [razor](Lore_Mehrunes%27_Razor.md) of [Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)

And the chains of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)

When the time is at hand

You will answer my call
