[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-npc-Fire_Spirit.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fire_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fire_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fire_Spirit.jpg) Fire Spirit The **Fire Spirit** will be encountered in [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) during the quest [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md). It is found inside a chamber with a flaming sealed door. Approaching the door will spawn the spirit.

The Fire Spirit wears an unobtainable set of [ancient Nord armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Armor.md) and wields two leveled [ancient Nord swords](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md), which may be up to [honed](Skyrim_Honed_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md) in quality. It also knows the [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) and [Wall of Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wall_of_Flames) spells. The Fire Spirit also has a special passive ability that gives it its flaming appearance, as well as providing a 33 percent [resistance to fire](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md) at the cost of a 33 percent [weakness to frost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weakness_to_Frost).
