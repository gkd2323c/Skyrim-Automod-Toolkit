[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-npc-Captain_Aquilius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Captain_Aquilius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Aquilius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Captain_Aquilius.jpg) Captain Aquilius **Captain Aquilius** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who only appears during [Hard Answers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hard_Answers) as you leave [Calcelmo's Tower](Skyrim_Calcelmo%27s_Tower.md) after copying the tablet. If [Aicantar](Skyrim_Aicantar.md) is still alive, he will instruct Aquilius to search the building.

He wears [steel armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor) if you are below level 18, and [steel plate armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate_Armor) if you are above. He carries a [banded iron shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Banded_Iron_Shield) if you are below level 6 and a [steel shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Shield) if you are above. In addition, he wields a leveled one-handed weapon and carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Calcelmo%27s_Laboratory_Key) to [Calcelmo's Laboratory](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md#Calcelmo.27s_Laboratory), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_Museum_Key) to the [Dwemer Museum](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md#Dwemer_Museum), and leveled amount of gold.
