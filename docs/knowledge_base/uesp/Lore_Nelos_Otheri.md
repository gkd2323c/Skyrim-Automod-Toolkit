- *"Otheri's hand is a steady one upon the great ship that is Telvanni."*

Nelos Otheri was Archmagister of the [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) chapter of the [Telvanni Council](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), based in [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md). He ruled over the tower of Tel Naga, and his Mouth was Angharal Suth. He was also renowned for his mastery of [mycoturgy](Lore_Mycoturgy.md), and served as a patron to a group of esteemed mycoturges acknowledged as the finest in the entire [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SMSS-2)</sup> In addition, he extended his support to Magister [Varkenel](Lore_Varkenel.md)'s endeavors in experimenting with the creation of new animal species, particularly the [Vvardvarks](Lore_Vvardvark.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup> As the leader of House Telvanni, he was the one who officially rejected the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WRTP-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TP-4)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) he was conducting studies on [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) artifacts in Mzanchend,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MORJ-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IFL-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Lothnarth-7)</sup> west of Sadrith Mora.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Mzanchend-8)</sup> He was assisted by various guards,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Lothnarth-7)</sup> and apprentices including an [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) wizard Arowende.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Arowende-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ADO26-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ADP23-11)</sup> Nelos dedicated his studies to the intricate machinery within those ruins.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Morrowind_Quest_Items-12)</sup> Many mages, such as Saduro worked in Magister Otheri's laboratories.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MTCSIS-13)</sup> Having made a significant discovery, Nelos chose not to divulge his research findings, causing magisters like [Gothren](Lore_Gothren.md) to desire his research notes.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MG-14)</sup>

In the same year a magically talented [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) slave owned by Nelos,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-EO-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Angharal_Suth-16)</sup> [Sun-in-Shadow](Lore_Sun-in-Shadow.md) met with the council of Telvanni mouths and was freed from her bonds, then proceeded to the rank of a hireling, in service of the Archmagister, initially hoping to liberate Eoki, another slave of Nelos, talented in [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) related magic.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-SIS-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-Eoki-18)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-AHOHT-19)</sup> With help from an [outlander](Lore_The_Vestige.md), she attained the role of oathman<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-ONObjections-20)</sup> and along the way, clashed with Magisters such as Gothren of [Tel Aruhn](Lore_Tel_Aruhn.md). Those schemes involved theft of Otheri's research journals for Gothren.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-SIS-17)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Arith_Sendrul-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-MMAM-22)</sup> As time passed, Sun-in-Shadow continued her work to become a magister.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-ONHeart-23)</sup>
