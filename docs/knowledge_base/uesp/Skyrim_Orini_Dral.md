[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8b/SR-npc-Orini_Dral.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Orini_Dral.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Orini_Dral.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Orini_Dral.jpg) Orini Dral patrolling Riften Warehouse **Orini Dral** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and bodyguard to [Sarthis Idren](Skyrim_Sarthis_Idren.md). He protects Sarthis by patrolling inside the entrance to [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md), and watching for intruders. He wears leveled armor and is equipped with a one-handed weapon.

## Related Quests
- **[The Raid](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Raid)**: Help the [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) to uncover and stop the illegal [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) trade in town.
