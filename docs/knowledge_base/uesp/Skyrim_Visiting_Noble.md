[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-npc-Visiting_Noble.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Visiting_Noble.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Visiting_Noble.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Visiting_Noble.jpg) Visiting Noble The **Visiting Noble** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior2H.md) who is spawned in the main dining area of [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) if chosen as the target of a contract for the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). The Visiting Noble is one of ten potential targets who can be chosen by the [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quest system.

He will rudely brush you aside with *"Would it kill you to let a man go about his business in peace? Really, the nerve..."*

The Visiting Noble wears [merchant clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0004223c) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He carries a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) for protection.

## Related Quests
- **[The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md)**: Yet another child has prayed to their [mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md).

## Notes
- This NPC typically will move around the more heavily populated dining area where attempting to kill him will most likely incur a bounty. However, there are instances where he enters the cooking area opposite [Farengar Secret-Fire](Skyrim_Farengar_Secret-Fire.md)'s quarters. This offers a greater opportunity to sneak attack the Visiting Noble without incurring a bounty, as there will be a lower likelihood of the presence of other NPCs in the area.
