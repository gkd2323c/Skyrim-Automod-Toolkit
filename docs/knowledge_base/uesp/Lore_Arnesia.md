[![](https://images.uesp.net/6/68/MW-shrine-Saint_Roris.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-shrine-Saint_Roris.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-shrine-Saint_Roris.png) Saint Roris, martyr of the Arnesian War Arnesia is a location or region in the eastern parts of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), near [Archon](Lore_Archon.md) and [Thorn](Lore_Thorn.md). [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) civilizations of Black Marsh, such as the [Barsaebic Ayleids](Lore_Barsaebic_Ayleids.md) and the [Cantemiric Velothi](Lore_Cantemiric_Velothi.md), were known to settle and inhabit territories around the Arnesia, as well as Archon and Thorn, before their extinction.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PG-1)</sup> There is a round island off the east coast of this area, which has a dark green/gray terrain when viewed from overhead, different from anything seen in the rest of the province.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-2)</sup>

Little is known about the settlement itself, but it lends its name to the conflict between [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md), the [Arnesian War](Lore_Arnesian_War.md). It took place in [3E 396](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_396).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup> This conflict started as a slave revolt in the southern lands of the [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md), and escalated into a full-scale war between Black Marsh and [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3M-4)</sup> The cause of the war was the death of a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) trader named [Roris](Lore_Roris.md), who was later canonized into a [saint](Lore_Tribunal_Saints.md) by the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LOTS-5)</sup> Morrowind's armies ultimately defeated the Argonians and expanded Morrowind's territory to the south, but as of [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432) the Argonians began to reclaim land conquered during the Arnesian War.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE3A-6)</sup> It was likely among the ancient territories that were "stolen by Argonia" that [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md) sought to reclaim.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DUP-7)</sup>

The code phrase "Orphan of Arnesia" was used by the members of the [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427). [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) was privy to this information, however.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Disguise-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Nileno_Dorvayn-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Neminda-10)</sup> Even when [Hlaalu Helseth](Lore_Hlaalu_Helseth.md) outlawed slavery across Morrowind,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE3M-4)</sup> slavers were still active in Arnesia by the latemost Third Era.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBBR-11)</sup>
