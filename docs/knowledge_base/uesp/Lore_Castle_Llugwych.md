[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/DF-place-Castle_Llugwych_02.png/200px-DF-place-Castle_Llugwych_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Castle_Llugwych_02.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Castle_Llugwych_02.png) Castle Llugwych, headquarters of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), as seen in *[Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)* **Castle Llugwych** is a large fortress in the central part of [Ykalon](Lore_Ykalon.md) in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. It serves as the headquarters of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) in the region. Because of its sensitive function, the castle is well-guarded.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

At some point during the [Agent's](Lore_Agent.md) investigation circa [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) - [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), a follower of the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md) gave them a letter wishing to meet them. When the Agent met up with them, the follower explained that [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md) and his [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) had stolen an item from the Underking, cursed it, and then gave it to the Blades. The Underking wished for no harm to fall on the Blades and so asked the Agent to enter Castle Llugwych and remove the cursed item.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
