**Soul Tear** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) that is added with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md). At full strength, it casts [soul trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md) upon foes, drains their health for 300 points on impact, and if the target dies within 2 seconds, it [resurrects](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) the corpse to fight for you for 1 minute.

- The shout affects only the first target it impacts.
- The Reanimation works on most creatures (akin to the [Ritual standing stone](Skyrim_The_Ritual_Stone.md) effect) such as [mammoths](Skyrim_Mammoth.md) and [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md). They abide by standard [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) summon rules and perks, as well as the standard one summon (two with [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls)) limit. Upon death, the thrall will crumble into a pile of ash.

Shouting one or two words will push objects a slight distance away.

## Notes
- You can learn all three parts of the shout quickly by summoning [Durnehviir](Skyrim_Durnehviir_(dragon).md), listening to him, waiting two hours, and then repeating until all three words are learned.
