Praise Be (Ancestor Song) *A religious hymn honoring various Aedra* *
---
*Note: [A verse honoring Stendarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Bardic_Performances#Praise_Be_.28Ancestor_Song.29) is missing from this book but is sung by NPCs in-game.*

---
(Music: deliberate and stately)

Praise we now [Mara](Lore_Mara.md)
O Goddess of Love
Bless us with children
Our mother above

CHORUS

Clap hands in praise
Exalt the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md)!
Clap hands and praise
Our ancestral lines!

Praise we now [Xarxes](Lore_Xarxes.md)
Who transcribes our story
Honor the scrivener
Archivist of glory

CHORUS

Praise we now [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md)
O first [Ehlnofey](Lore_Ehlnofey.md)
God of the forest
Earth Bones showed the way

CHORUS

Last praise [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md)
Forefather of all
Formed us to be like him
Wise, noble, and tall

CHORUS
