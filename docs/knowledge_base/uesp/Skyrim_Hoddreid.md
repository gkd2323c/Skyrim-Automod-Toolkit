[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-npc-Hoddreid.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hoddreid.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hoddreid.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hoddreid.jpg) Hoddreid **Hoddreid** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md), and one of the hunters who went to [Bloated Man's Grotto](Skyrim_Bloated_Man%27s_Grotto.md) to hunt [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

When you arrive, she is already dead at the first campfire, presumably from wounds sustained fighting Sinding. She can be found wearing [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c). She carried an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and there is also a high chance that a small amount of gold can be found in her possession.
