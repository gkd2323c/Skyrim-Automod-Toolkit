## Ingredients
25-27<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> guaranteed samples can be found in 17-18<sup>([DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md))</sup> different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 3 in [Fellglow Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellglow_Keep) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 2 in [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in [Hag Rock Redoubt Ruin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hag_Rock_Redoubt_Ruin) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 2 in [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 2 in [the Midden Dark](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden_Dark) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 in [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))
- 2 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in [Valerica's Study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> ([Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel).

## Notes
- In a [recipe](Skyrim_Recipes.md) purchased from an apothecary, Creep Cluster is referred to as Creep Cluster Root. The different name does not indicate a different ingredient.

## Bugs
- In the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md), Creep Clusters are misaligned when grown in a plot of [Fertile Soil](Skyrim_Fertile_Soil.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, making it very difficult to harvest them. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.0, fixes this bug. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=21364))

- If grown in the small garden outside the HF house, the best way to harvest them is to walk out of the garden and approach from the outside until you have a fence post directly in the line of sight to the harvestable creep cluster. If you aim for this fence post, you can harvest them 9 times out of 10.
- See [this page](Skyrim_Greenhouse.md#Bugs) for more tips.
