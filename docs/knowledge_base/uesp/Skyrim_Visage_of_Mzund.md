[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Dwarven Helmet.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Dwarven Helmet.png) Visage of Mzund The Visage of Mzund is a unique [Dwarven Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Helmet) that is found within a treasure room in the [Fahlbtharz](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md) Grand Hall. This helmet [fortifies your stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fortify_Stamina) by 60 points, while also adding the lesser power [Breath of Nchuak](Skyrim_Breath_of_Nchuak.md). The Visage of Mzund can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with a [Dwarven Metal Ingot](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md), with the [Dwarven Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwarven_Smithing) perk doubling improvement.

## Notes
- Like regular Dwarven Helmets, the Visage of Mzund is not affected by the [Ancient Knowledge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Knowledge) ability.
- Usage of the helmet's power can cause enemies killed by the effect to launch high into the air.
- The effect is similar to that of the Dwarven Centurion's steam attack, though it is not the same: it deals less damage and has a different visual effect.
- The Visage of Mzund is one of two pieces of headwear in the game that has a Fortify Stamina enchantment, similar in strength to that of an item of Peerless Stamina (+70 stamina), though ten points less. The other is [Rahgot's mask](Skyrim_Rahgot_(item).md).
- Breath of Nchuak power requires you to have 25 stamina to cast and reduces your stamina by 25. If you have no stamina (such as being a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) out during the day), you cannot use the helmet's power.
- If you drink a [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) potion (one bought/found or an overpowered one made by you) before equipping the helmet, the enchantment description does not change to show the boost, but the description of the effect in the Active Effects sub-menu shows the correct value.
