# Montalion

Montalion is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with the ability to cure paralysis and the power of [teleportation](Lore_Teleportation.md). The bloodline dominates the area around the mouth of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) - specifically the regions of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md), [Bhoriane](Lore_Bhoriane.md), [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon_(fiefdom).md), [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam_(county).md), [Lainlyn](Lore_Lainlyn_(barony).md) and [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth_(fiefdom).md). They are the enemies of the [Selenu](Lore_Selenu.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The anonymous author of [*Vampires of the Iliac Bay*](Lore_Vampires_of_the_Iliac_Bay.md) was once a member of the Montalion clan.

## Notes
- Although similarly named, the Motalion Necropolis in [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md) has no known links to the bloodline.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MN-2)</sup>
