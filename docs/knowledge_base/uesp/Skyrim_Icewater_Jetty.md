[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b0/SR-place-Icewater_Jetty.jpg/200px-SR-place-Icewater_Jetty.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Icewater_Jetty.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Icewater_Jetty.jpg) Icewater Jetty **Icewater Jetty** is a small isolated [dock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Places#Docks) far west of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and northwest of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md).

The boat at the pier can be used to reach the nearby island to the northwest, where [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) is located. Two [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grass_Pod) plants, a [canis root](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Canis_Root), a [Nordic barnacle cluster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Barnacle), and a tree stump with [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) growing on it may be found in the area. Nothing else of interest is in the vicinity.

## Notes
- You can hire a [ferryman](Skyrim_Transport.md#Ferrymen) to take you to this location for 500 gold, from [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), Solitude, or [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). You can also travel here using the map in *[Dead Man's Dread](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread_(place).md)*<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Dread.md)</sup> once this option has been unlocked.
- It is not mandatory to use the boat to access Castle Volkihar; it is possible to swim to the castle instead.

## Bugs
- The boat may overlap with the dock. - This appears to have been fixed with the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md).
