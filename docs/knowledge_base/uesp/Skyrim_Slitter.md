[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-npc-Slitter.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Slitter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Slitter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Slitter.jpg) Slitter **Slitter**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md), is [Mogrul](Skyrim_Mogrul_(Dragonborn).md)'s right-hand man. They both go into the [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) inn, [the Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md), for a short time during the day. He is not talkative, and will only warn you to honor any agreement made with Mogrul. He wears [chitin armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Armor) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chitin_Boots), and he carries a leveled [battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Battleaxe).

## Dialogue
He is not talkative, but he has brusque greetings such as:

*"I work for Mogrul. You got a problem, you talk to him."* *"Mogrul doesn't lend coin to outlanders. Look somewhere else."* *"You looking for trouble? Stay out of my way."* *"Haven't you got something better to do?"* *"Get away from me, outlander."* *"You should listen to Mogrul."*
## Bugs
- If you kill Mogrul and then reanimate him, Slitter will continue to follow his corpse. This is due to a continuing AI Package. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- He will also continue to direct you to talk to Mogrul and state that he works for Mogrul even if Mogrul is dead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
