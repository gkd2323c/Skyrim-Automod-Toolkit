[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-npc-Yisra.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Yisra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yisra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Yisra.jpg) Yisra and the Flame Cloak tome **Yisra** was a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) student enrolled at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). Her charred corpse can be found in a circle of scorched earth east of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md), along the shore immediately south of [Yngvild](Skyrim_Yngvild.md). By speaking to [Phinis Gestor](Skyrim_Phinis_Gestor.md), you can learn she was one of several students that have gone missing recently. However, nothing seems to come from finding her remains.

[Yisra's necklace](Skyrim_Yisra%27s_Necklace.md) and a small amount of gold can be found on her body, and a copy of *[Spell Tome: Flame Cloak](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Flame_Cloak.md)* is set before her corpse, so one can assume she got the incantation wrong. Her necklace and the tome are not quest-related and respawn after a few days.

## Notes
- Yisra was to be included in an [unfinished quest](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) in which you are tasked with finding her and the three other missing apprentices. In the quest, you bring [Yisra's Necklace](Skyrim_Yisra%27s_Necklace.md) back to Phinis Gestor, who will say *"Gods, this is hers, isn't it? She will certainly be missed."*
- There are two versions of Yisra in game data. The one used in game is considered to be a container (a burnt corpse), the other one that not actually used in the game has base form ID `000D4FF9`.
- There are gouts of flame surrounding the immediate area around her body. Unlike most fire found in the game world, these will damage anyone who stands in them, be it you, your horse or a follower. They will also keep applying fire damage every three seconds, if you continue to stand in one.
- Yisra's corpse is set to respawn meaning you can get infinite copies of Yisra's necklace. - Version 4.2.8 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) modifies that and prevents them from respawning.
