[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/04/DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png/200px-DF-map-Iliac_Bay_%28labeled%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Iliac_Bay_(labeled).png) The area in which Yeorth Burrowland is found The Yeorth Burrowland was an area near [Anticlere](Lore_Anticlere.md) in southern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), and was close by two notable sites from the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md), the [Ravennian Forest](Lore_Ravennian_Forest.md) and [Cryngaine Field](Lore_Cryngaine_Field.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FWOB-1)</sup> The burrowland is where the armies of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) encamped after the battle of [Reich Gradkeep](Lore_Reich_Gradkeep.md) and before the [Battle of Cryngaine Field](Lore_Battle_of_Cryngaine_Field.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FWOB-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NWOB-2)</sup>

The 18th of [Morning Star](Lore_Morning_Star.md) was known as the Waking Day, when the people of the Yeorth Burrowland woke the spirits of nature after the winter, somewhat like their more reverential ancestors, in one of the oldest holidays in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HOTIB-3)</sup>
