# Crab

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/ON-furnishing-High_Isle_Crab%2C_Steamed.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-High_Isle_Crab%2C_Steamed.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-High_Isle_Crab,_Steamed.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-High_Isle_Crab,_Steamed.jpg) A Steamed Crab Crabs are [crustaceans](Lore_Crustacean.md) that possess two front-facing appendages with claws on the end. There are several known species of crab, with the most prevalent being the [mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md). Other crabs are typically considered lesser than mudcrabs in aspects such as ferocity.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Most crabs have hard shells, but softshell varieties also exist.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Crabs of all species can grow to absurdly large sizes.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Species

### Blue Sandcrab
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/ON-creature-Blue_Sandcrab.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Blue_Sandcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Blue_Sandcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Blue_Sandcrab.jpg) A blue sandcrab Blue Sandcrabs are a blue variant of crab found along the coast of the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) in the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Large specimens sometimes cannibalize smaller ones.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SCESO-7)</sup>

### Blue-Backed Siltcrab
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/ON-creature-Blue-Backed_Siltcrab.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Blue-Backed_Siltcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Blue-Backed_Siltcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Blue-Backed_Siltcrab.jpg) A Blue-Backed Siltcrab Blue-Backed Siltcrabs are a small species of blue crab native to [Galen](Lore_Galen.md). They can only be found in tide pools along the coast of the island and are considered a delicacy.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Quen-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Veshin-10)</sup>

### Burglar Crab
Crustaceans that eat snails and smaller crabs, and move into the empty shells afterwards.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

### [Coral Crab](Lore_Coral_Crab.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6b/ON-creature-Coral_Crab.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Coral_Crab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Coral_Crab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Coral_Crab.jpg) A coral crab Occasionally referred to as the "Coral Mudcrab",<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> the [Coral Crab](Lore_Coral_Crab.md) is a type of sandcrab native to the beaches of the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). Usually blue, purple, or red in coloration, the crabs are distinguished for the large [coral](Lore_Coral.md) growths on their shells. Both the crab and the coral can grow to a significant size, posing a danger to gourmands who attempt to harvest their eggs from the sandy beaches where they are buried.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

### [Emperor Crab](Lore_Emperor_Crab.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png/200px-SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab.png) An Emperor Crab [Emperor Crabs](Lore_Emperor_Crab.md) are gigantic semi-aquatic crabs who were once native to the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), contemporary with the early settled [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md). The species was known to be on the decline and incredibly rare by the [Age of Heroes](Lore_Age_of_Heroes.md), with the species dying out sometime long before [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), with [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) claiming to be the ones who hunted them to extinction. They are described as being as large as cathedrals and some naturalists believe they are related to the [mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md). One source claims that they devolved into the modern mudcrab after abandoning divine pursuits in favor of mundane satisfactions.

### [Hermit Crab](Lore_Hermit_Crab.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/ON-pet-Hermit_Crab.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Hermit_Crab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Hermit_Crab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Hermit_Crab.jpg) A Hermit Crab [Hermit Crabs](Lore_Hermit_Crab.md) are small orange crabs that hide in mollusk shells for protection. They are most commonly seen along the sandy coasts of [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md) archipelago.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> They are considered a delicacy by the [Hee-Tepsleel](Lore_Hee-Tepsleel.md) tribe of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md). Hermit Crabs are sometimes kept as pets.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Mulberry Hermit Crabs are a variety that possess a deep purple coloration as opposed to the usual orange.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-16)</sup> Giant Hermit Crabs can be found in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md).

### [Gravelclaw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gravelclaw)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/ON-creature-Gravelclaw.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Gravelclaw.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Gravelclaw.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Gravelclaw.jpg) A gravelclaw [Gravelclaws](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gravelclaw) are terrestrial crabs found living on the rocky slopes of the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Their shell are grey and lumpy, giving the crabs a rocky appearance.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-17)</sup> The meat of elder gravelclaws is succulent, and its pleasant aroma is irresistible to many wild predators. The meat is commonly used by hunters and trappers as bait.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup>

### Iliac Bay Crab
Iliac Bay Crabs are native to the coastline of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md). They have a distinctive blue shell that often makes them a target for fisherfolk. However, this also makes these crabs popular as pets and traveling companions.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-19)</sup>

### [Mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/82/ON-creature-Mudcrab.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Mudcrab.jpg) A mudcrab The [Mudcrab](Lore_Mudcrab.md) (or **Mud Crab**) is a common variety of crab capable of camouflaging itself as a small rock. They can be found along every shore and waterway of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), and are also a common sight underground. They are not typically aggressive unless cornered or provoked. However, if a mudcrab ever gets a taste of meat it becomes a hunter, and may be a threat to livestock. Mudcrabs are usually not dangerous unless they swarm, as they rely solely on their pincers and burrowing abilities in combat. Mudcrabs are capable of stripping a corpse down to its bones in days, which usually takes weeks for lesser crabs. Some mudcrabs can grow to enormous sizes, and rare albino variants are known to exist.

Although they are inquisitive by nature and can be kept as pets, most citizens of Tamriel regard mudcrabs as disgusting vermin. Mudcrabs have been characterized by certain [fish](Lore_Fish.md) enthusiasts as unhappy creatures, "dour and sunless like the soil in which they sleep". Mudcrabs are prized for their sweet crab meat and legs, and their chitin is sometimes used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).

### Mole Crab
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/MW-item-Telvanni_Mole_Crab_Helm.jpg/200px-MW-item-Telvanni_Mole_Crab_Helm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Telvanni_Mole_Crab_Helm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Telvanni_Mole_Crab_Helm.jpg) Telvanni Mole Crab Helm Mole Crabs are native to Morrowind. [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) fashion light armor helmets out of their shells.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-MWMole Helm-20)</sup>

### Sessile Crab
Sessile crabs are crustaceans that lived in the submerged sections of [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md)'s Marrow Sump. They have squat, thorny bodies, with venomous claws on the end of twenty-foot-long yellow and viridian tentacles. They do not locomote, and instead attach themselves to the bottom of the body of water they inhabit, moving their long tentacles slowly through the water to try and catch prey. They prey upon quick [nickfish](Lore_Fish_N.md#Nickfish), which the toxins in the sessile crab's claws can kill almost instantly.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-TIC-21)</sup><sup>:189</sup>

### Striped Pincer Crab
Little is known about Striped Pincer Crabs, except for the fact that their claws are utilized in the production of scissors.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-Treasures L-22)</sup>
