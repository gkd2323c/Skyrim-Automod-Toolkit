Imago's Notes about Neonymics **by [Imago Storm](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Imago_Storm)** *A note describing the neonymics of three Daedra Lords*
---
[Dagon's](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) incantory [neonymic](Lore_Neonymic.md) is Djehkeleho-dehbe-effehezepeh. The [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) characters are Djeh Koh Leh Oh -- Deh Beh -- Feh Ee Zeh Peh, or, in [Tamrielic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Languages), JKLO-DB-FEZP.

[Xivilai's](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md) neonymic is Wegerohseh-chehkohieu. The Daedric characters are Weh Geh Roh Seh -- Cheh Koh Eiu, or, in Tamrielic, WGRS-CKU.

[Faydra's](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md) Neonymic is Nepehkweh-kodo. The Daedric characters are Neh Peh Kweh -- Koh Doh, or, in Tamrielic, NPK-KD.
