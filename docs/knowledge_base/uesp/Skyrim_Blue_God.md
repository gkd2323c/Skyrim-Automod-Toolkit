[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2b/SR-npc-Blue_God.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Blue_God.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blue_God.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Blue_God.jpg) The "Blue God"The Blue God is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) masquerading as a deity of the same name, specifically the [Goblin](Skyrim_Goblin.md) interpretation of [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md). He is hostile and you will need to kill him during the related quest.

He carries the *[Blue God's Journal](Skyrim_Blue_God%27s_Journal.md)* and wields a [Goblin Totem Staff](Skyrim_Goblin_Totem_Staff.md) in battle.

His journal reveals that he was originally from the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md), where he was considered a black sheep. He unintentionally became the leader of a goblin tribe after visiting Gromm's Pass with full blue body paint, which made the goblins mistake him for the Blue God. His secret was almost discovered when the goblin [Gogh](Skyrim_Gogh.md) caught him applying the blue dye to keep up the act. The imposter sentenced Gogh to death in the goblin's fighting pit. With enough goblin troops gathered, he planned to assault Largashbur for his ill treatment from its inhabitants.
