It was released on April 5, 2018 for 300 Creation Credits. The Creation's features work well with [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md).

## Official Summary
*Make Skyrim that much more accommodating with this cozy movable campsite, complete with a lean-to for sleeping, campfire for warmth and some cooking, and a new fast travel marker (Creation craftable via Forge)*
## Overview
This Creation adds the ability to set up a campsite out in the wilderness. It consists of a shelter to sleep in, a campfire to [cook](Skyrim_Cooking.md) a limited number of food items, and a crate to sit on. Once placed, the campsite also gets a map marker which can be used for fast travel. To set up or move your campsite, you will first need to craft [Camping Supplies](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Camping_Supplies). This item requires three [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) and one piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and can be crafted at any [forge or anvil](Skyrim_Forge.md). Upon setting up camp for the first time, you will be given a brief tutorial message:

*"Campsites allow you to rest and stay warm while exploring the wilderness. You can cook a limited number of meals over the campfire. Your campsite is marked on your map so you can locate and fast travel to it later."* The reference to staying warm refers to the [Warmth](Skyrim_Warmth.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md)</sup> mechanic added by the [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) Creation. The Camping Creation is intended to be used in conjunction with Survival Mode, due to the new requirements relating to warmth and sleep which are imposed in that mode. With Survival Mode enabled, the campfire will allow you to warm up by staying near it, although Argonians may need to light a torch as well. It will also allow you to craft a limited number of [hot soups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hot_Soups) for later use.

This Creation also adds a new [loading screen](Skyrim_Loading_Screens.md) relating to campsites and a new "Camping" section to the in-game Help menu.

## Notes
- Hot soups can no longer be crafted at a campfire with the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md). Only meals with food that is caught or hunted can be crafted, such as fish and rabbits.
- The [Well Rested](Skyrim_Well_Rested.md) bonus is not granted when sleeping at a campsite.
- Each instance of camping supplies is one use only. They do not return to your inventory upon choosing to break camp.
- In Survival mode, sleeping in your camp will still leave you Drained, as it counts as sleeping outdoors.
- Only one campsite can be set up at a time. You do not need to break camp before creating a new campsite. The old camp will break upon creation of a new campsite.
