# Heretics

The Heretics of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) are a group of outcast mortals found in [Mania](Lore_Mania.md). They follow the Manic teachings of [Arden-Sul](Lore_Arden-Sul.md) to a fault, believing him to be the true lord of the Shivering Isles. They view [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) as a mortal posing as a [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), and seek to depose him for being unjust and insane. According to the Heretics, [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) cannot be princes, only vile servants. As such, they specialize in summoning and binding [Hungers](Lore_Hunger.md) to do their bidding. Their ultimate goal is to sack [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md) and force the other mortals to convert to their beliefs and live in the wilderness following the destruction of Sheogorath's mortal body.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The Heretics are hostile towards most other inhabitants of the Isles, and wear uniform robes to distinguish themselves. They have a particular vendetta against the [Zealots](Lore_Zealots.md), their [Demented](Lore_Dementia_(place).md) counterparts. The highest ranks of the Heretics were said to be "transformed".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The [Apostles of Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Apostles_of_Light) were a faction of Heretics whose opposition to Sheogorath stemmed from an intimate knowledge of the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> A group of Heretics in [Fain](Lore_Fain.md) worshipped a being known as [Gyub](Lore_Gyub.md), Lord of the Pit.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GLOTP-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SF-5)</sup>
