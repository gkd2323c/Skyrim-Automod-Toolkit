Drelyne Llenim was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) member of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) and a Curate of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup> She was one of the wielders of the legendary [Auriel's Shield](Lore_Auriel%27s_Shield.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AS-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ce/MW-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg/200px-MW-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) Auriel's Shield Little is known about Drelyne Llenim's past. Being an Armiger, it was likely that she hailed from the [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOV-3)</sup> By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), she already held the rank of Disciple within the Tribunal Temple.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup> As of then she was stationed at [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md) under the command of the Grand Mashal [Galdal Omayn](Lore_Galdal_Omayn.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-4)</sup> where she helped safeguard the rest of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) from the threat contained within the [Ghostfence](Lore_Ghostfence.md).

In addition to her famed shield, she donned chitin armaments and wielded a fireblade. Aside from being an accomplished warrior, Llenim was also a competent [spellcaster](Lore_Magic.md) in her own right, wielding an array of [Destruction](Lore_Destruction.md) school spells.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup>

It is unclear how or when she came into possession of Auriel's Shield, but by that time, she was already wielding it.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup> In 3E 427, she lost possession of the prized artifact as the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) sold Auriel's Shield to [Torasa Aram](Lore_Torasa_Aram.md)'s Museum of Artifacts in [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TA-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TM-6)</sup>
