[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-ingredient-Spriggan_Sap.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Spriggan_Sap.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Spriggan_Sap.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Spriggan_Sap.jpg) Spriggan sap for sale **Spriggan Sap** is only available as already-harvested samples. Contrary to what is stated by the official Game Guide, it is **not** dropped by [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) —they drop [taproots](Skyrim_Taproot.md) instead.

It is one of only three (four with the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on) ingredients with a [Fortify Alteration](Skyrim_Fortify_Alteration.md) effect.

## Ingredients
18 guaranteed samples can be found in 13 different locations:

- 3 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 3 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 1 in [Ansilvund Excavation](Skyrim_Ansilvund_Excavation.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 1 in [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 1 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 1 in [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md) ([Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md))
- 1 in [Jorgen and Lami's House](Skyrim_Jorgen_and_Lami%27s_House.md) ([Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md))
- 1 in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 1 in [the Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 1 in [the Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 1 in [Soljund's Sinkhole](Skyrim_Soljund%27s_Sinkhole.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 1 in the [Tower of Mzark](Skyrim_Tower_of_Mzark.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 1 in the [Sleeping Tree Camp](Skyrim_Sleeping_Tree_Camp.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- Version 2.0.0 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) allows [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) to drop this ingredient or [taproot](Skyrim_Taproot.md) upon death.
