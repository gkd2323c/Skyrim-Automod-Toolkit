# Keepers of the Razor


That year, the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) was contacted by [Silus Vesuius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silus_Vesuius), a Mythic Dawn enthusiast who had found the Razor's scabbard. He hired the Dragonborn to retrieve the three missing pieces from the Keepers' descendants, whom he had located after meticulous research into their family histories. The Dragonborn defeated the descendants, marking an end to the Keepers' legacy, and brought the pieces to a shrine to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) to have the Razor restored.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SV-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POTP-3)</sup>
