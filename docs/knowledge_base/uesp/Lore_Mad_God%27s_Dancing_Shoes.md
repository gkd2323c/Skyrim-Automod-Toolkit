# Mad God's Dancing Shoes

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/ON-item-Mad_God%27s_Dancing_Shoes.jpg/200px-ON-item-Mad_God%27s_Dancing_Shoes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Mad_God%27s_Dancing_Shoes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Mad_God%27s_Dancing_Shoes.jpg) Mad God's Dancing Shoes *Why do I need a reason for everything? Mortals always need a reason to do things. For example: people like to think that light chases away the shadows! I prefer to think that without a few lights, there'd be no shadows to enjoy.*
— [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MGDS-1)</sup> Mad God's Dancing Shoes are a pair of jester-like footwear associated with the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Madness [Sheogroath](Lore_Sheogorath.md). They were crafted entirely from materials native to the Shivering Isles on a Sheogorath's whim. They are imbued to create unpredictable magical effects whenever their wearer performs a vault. These effects could include [conjuring wheels](Lore_Cheesemancy.md) of [cheese](Lore_Cheese.md), inflicting various forms of damage upon the wearer's foes, enhancing the wearer's speed, restoring stamina, or even reducing the magicka cost of spells, among other possibilities.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MGDS-1)</sup>

Among the components used in their creation were eyelets fashioned from [Baliwog](Lore_Baliwog.md) pearls. Opalescent and energetically potent, the pearls were known to hold a magicka charges. It was speculated that the cultist who sought them out for use in such crafting was highly dedicated and talented, and perhaps not entirely sane. Its laces were animate and made from [Gnarl](Lore_Gnarl.md) fiber. On one occasion, they crawled out of their container and attempted to reach the nostrils of one of the researchers studying them. The essence within these laces was alive. It was speculated that with sufficient hydration and nutrients, an entirely new Gnarl could be grown from the fibers alone. Its soles were fashioned from [Grummite](Lore_Grummite.md) flesh, with numerous Daedric glyphs burned into the hide. Its toecaps were comically curled, in a style typical of jesters. They were made from [amberweave](Lore_Amber.md) cloth and stitched using a needle of [madness metal](Lore_Madness_Ore.md). Some speculated that the toecaps were directly tied to Sheogorath himself, or perhaps to a high-ranking cultist and suggesting they were once part of a ceremonial outfit of some kind. The heels were made from [Hunger](Lore_Hunger.md) leather, oversized and remarkably resilient, almost magically so. No fewer than three Hungers were said to have given their hides in the making of this artifact. Some speculated that the leather may have come from a so-called Hunger farm somewhere within the Shivering Isles.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MGDS-1)</sup>

Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a member of the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) at the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) followed several leads to locate multiple parts of the shoes. After unearthing and reassembling them, the artifact was sent to the Circle's scholars for study.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MGDS-1)</sup>
