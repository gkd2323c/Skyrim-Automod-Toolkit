*As in all things, repetition begets confidence, and confidence begets excellence.*
—Undeya<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Undeya-1)</sup> Undeya was a powerful [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) mage,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Undeya-1)</sup> and a member of the [Conclave of the Eleven Forces](Lore_Conclave_of_the_Eleven_Forces.md),<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> the ruling council of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijiic_Order.md) active duing the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oriandra-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Pearl_of_Great_Price_02.jpg) Undeya, Iachesis and Valsirenn working on the augury ritual Little is known about Undeya's past before she joined the Order. She believed that the true marks of a Psijic monk are precision and artistry. Knowing a spell is not enough; one must strive to refine and perfect it. She emphasized the significance of repetition, for in all pursuits, repetition breeds confidence, and confidence leads to excellence. She held that one should never become complacent in their studies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Undeya-1)</sup>

During the [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) crisis related to the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Undeya, as a member of the Conclave of the Eleven Forces, participated in a complex scrying ritual led by the Ritemaster [Iachesis](Lore_Iachesis.md). They tried to learn more about the threat posed by [Abyssal Pearls](Lore_Abyssal_Pearl.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oriandra-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-APOGP-3)</sup>

Following the death of Ritemaster Iachesis, the remaining members of the Conclave, including Undeya, honored Iachesis's dying wish by agreeing to appoint [Valsirenn](Lore_Valsirenn.md) as the interim Ritemaster.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Valsirenn-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Iachesis-5)</sup> This decision was made until the Conclave could come to a consensus on a more permanent and suitable candidate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oriandra-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Valsirenn-4)</sup>
