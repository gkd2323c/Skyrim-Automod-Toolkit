Coils of the Father *A short text on the god Auri-El*
---
Penitent, give thanks and praise to the soul of [Anu](Lore_Anu.md) the Everything, father to us all. The scales and fangs and flame of the creator envelop all of the people. Always.

To complete your venerations here, intone: "By the Fixed Center and his hand in our lives, we are all made safe. [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md), grant me the stability of the Divine. Be always at my side."
