[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Hylian_Shield.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-armor-Hylian_Shield.png) Hylian Shield The Hylian Shield is a shield obtained using [amiibo](Skyrim_Amiibo.md) or in a chest upon the [Throat of the World](Skyrim_Throat_of_the_World.md). It is only available in the [Nintendo Switch](Skyrim_Switch.md) version of the game.

## Notes
- [Hylian Shields](https://zeldawiki.wiki/wiki/Hylian_Shield) are recurring items in the *[The Legend of Zelda](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Legend_of_Zelda)* series, [Hylians](https://zeldawiki.wiki/wiki/Hylian) being an elf-like race of humans from the series.
