[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-place-Meeko%27s_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-place-Meeko%27s_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Meeko%27s_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Meeko%27s_Shack.jpg) Meeko's Shack **Meeko's Shack** is a run-down [shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) owned by the canine follower [Meeko](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Meeko), who has inherited the home from his unfortunate [owner](Skyrim_Nord_(dead).md#Nord1).

It is located on the edge of the [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) marshland between [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md), a short distance from the road leading from Morthal and [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md).

## Meeko's Shack
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Nord_%28Meeko%27s_Shack%29.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nord_%28Meeko%27s_Shack%29.jpg)](File_SR-npc-Nord_(Meeko%27s_Shack).jpg.md) [](File_SR-npc-Nord_(Meeko%27s_Shack).jpg.md) Meeko's former owner Around the shack are several plants and flowers including three [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plants, a [thistle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch) and three [purple mountain flowers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower).

The shack is still fairly structurally sound. Inside there is a small set of shelves to the left of the doorway with a [leek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), three [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple), two [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top. Beside this, in the northern corner is a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel with a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[A Dance in Fire, v6](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Dance_in_Fire,_v6)* on top. Against the northeast wall is a set of shelves holding a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and a bowl containing three [tomatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tomato). To the right of this is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid and a [rabbit](Skyrim_Rabbit.md). Against the southeast wall is an unowned single bed with the corpse of [Meeko's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Meeko) former [owner](Skyrim_Nord_(dead).md#Nord1) laying on top. It can still be used to rest. At the foot of the bed is an end table with a [journal](Skyrim_Journal_(Meeko%27s_Shack).md) written by Meeko's former owner. It explains that he had succumbed to [Rockjoint](Skyrim_Disease.md) and that Meeko "was always a loyal companion" and would "be able to take care of himself".

## Notes
- Meeko runs back to the shack if you find him on the road.
- There is an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) to the northwest.
- A [dragon mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md) can be found to the west of the shack.
- While technically owned by Meeko, none of the items inside the shack are owned and will not be marked as stolen should you take them.
- After you have discovered Meeko and his dead master, the body and bed will be disposed of automatically upon the next cell reset.
