[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-npc-Dawnguard.jpeg/200px-SR-npc-Dawnguard.jpeg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard.jpeg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard.jpeg) A generic member of the Dawnguard **Dawnguard** are radiant members of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). They are randomly encountered in the wilderness and, if you are a member of [Harkon's](Skyrim_Harkon.md) [court](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md), are hostile toward you.

They attack in groups of three and can appear upon fast traveling to a location. If you are a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md), they will attack on sight. These assassins will continue to appear even if the Dawnguard [questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Main_Quests) has been completed.

Two Dawnguard appear along with Durak to start the quest "Dawnguard".

Typically, they wear either a set of [Dawnguard armor](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md) or heavy Dawnguard armor and are armed with a [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md) and a [Dawnguard warhammer](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Warhammer.md) or a [Dawnguard war axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md). They may also carry small amounts of gold and minor random loot.
