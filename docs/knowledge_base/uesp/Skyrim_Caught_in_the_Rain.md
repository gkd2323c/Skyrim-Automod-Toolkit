## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) and speak with [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md).
2. Read [List of Rainy Weather Fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Rainy_Weather_Fish).
3. (Optional) Read [Fishing Mastery, v2](Skyrim_Fishing_Mastery,_v2.md).
4. Obtain a [Catfish](Skyrim_Catfish.md).
5. Obtain a [Pearlfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearlfish).
6. Obtain a [Pygmy Sunfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pygmy_Sunfish).
7. Obtain a [Spadefish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish).
8. Obtain Swims-In-Deep-Water's [Lucky Fishing Hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lucky_Fishing_Hat).
9. Hand your fish over to Swims-In-Deep-Water and decide on whether to keep the Lucky Fishing Hat or give it back.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_02.jpg) The fishing camp at Geirmund's Hall Speak to Swims for this next challenge:

- **I'm ready for the next fishing challenge.**
- *"It's all in this note."*
- **I see, these fish come out in the rain. And... you lost your lucky fishing hat?**
- *"It speaks the truth."*

- - **Alright. I'll be back soon with the fish. And the hat.**
- *"My thanks to you."*

- - **I'll get started. I just wish I didn't have to get soaked.**
- *"My thanks to you."*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9c/SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Caught_in_the_Rain_03.jpg) Fish tank after completion of the quest He then hands you a [list of rainy weather fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Rainy_Weather_Fish), which tells you that you need to catch a:

- [Catfish](Skyrim_Catfish.md)
- [Pearlfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearlfish)
- [Pygmy Sunfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pygmy_Sunfish)
- [Spadefish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spadefish)

You can also optionally pickup a copy of [Fishing Mastery, v2](Skyrim_Fishing_Mastery,_v2.md) from the shelf in the backroom. Travel to [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md), where you can fish up Swims' lucky fishing hat, which when worn will cause thunderstorms while fishing at temperate lakes and streams, making this quest much easier.

Once you have caught the required fish, return to Swims where you can either return his hat to him or lie that you didn't find it:

- - **Here are the fish you were after. I also found your lucky fishing hat.**
- *"You are like my brood to me. Here. A token."*

- - **Here's the fish for the challenge. I couldn't find your lucky fishing hat. (Lie)**
- *"It can't be helped. Thank you. Here this is for you."*

Either option leads to you getting an Argonian fishing rod and the ability to create more, if you give Swims his hat back, he will also reward you with 400 gold:

- **Thanks for the Argonian fishing rod, and the coin. I'll put them to good use.**
- **An Argonian fishing rod? Thank you. I will be sure to put it to good use.**
- *"My thanks to you."*

## Bugs
- The journal states that you can find pygmy sunfish in streams, when in reality they are found in lakes when it is raining. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
- The objective to "Fish up Swims-In-Deep-Water's lucky fishing hat" and a quest marker to the respective fishing spot appear right after the quest is given, but the [Lucky Fishing Hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lucky_Fishing_Hat) can only be fished after the *[List of Rainy Weather Fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Rainy_Weather_Fish)* is read. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.2, addresses this issue. The objective for fishing up the hat will no longer be displayed until after the note is read.
- The fish tank that's supposed to receive the fish you turn in during the quest was never properly set in the script properties, which can lead to the fish not being added to it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 4.3.3, fixes this bug.
