[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Volk.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Volk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Volk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Volk.jpg) Volk **Volk** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) found in [Ruunvald](Skyrim_Ruunvald_Excavation.md). He came there to help with the excavation after [Moric Sidrey](Skyrim_Moric_Sidrey.md) requested more assistance. From the information found in his [journal](Skyrim_Volk%27s_Journal.md), he may have been the last member of the Vigilants left unaffected by the [warlock](Skyrim_Warlock.md) [Minorne](Skyrim_Minorne.md)'s charms. Like the rest of the charmed vigilants, he will be hostile and cannot be spoken to.

## Related Quests
- **[Bolstering the Ranks](Skyrim_Bolstering_the_Ranks.md)**: Find [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md) and recruit him into the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

## Notes
- Volk does not have a unique facial model; he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md), [Lynoit](Skyrim_Lynoit.md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), and many other generic NPCs.
