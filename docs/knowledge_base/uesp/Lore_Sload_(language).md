# Sload (language)

This article is about the language. For the race, see [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).

Sload is the [language](Lore_Linguistics.md) spoken by the eponymous [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) and [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tangerine-1)</sup> It is a guttural "clacking" tongue,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IJA-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPWormgod-3)</sup> understood by [Yaghra](Lore_Yaghra.md) both spoken and [telepathically](Lore_Telepathy.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IJA-2)</sup> Observations of the tongue and the race as a whole by [Bendu Olo](Lore_Bendu_Olo.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) concluded that Sload has no written form.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1TWR-4)</sup> However, by the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), an obscure text written by the infamous Sload [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md) had surfaced.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-NKK-5)</sup>

There is no word in Sload for 'adventure'. The closest equivalent translates to 'tragic disaster'.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1TWR-4)</sup> The Sload word for the [Tangerine Dragon Frogs](Lore_Dragon_Frog.md) translates to 'snacks'.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tangerine-1)</sup>

- The book *[N'Gasta! Kvata! Kvakis!](Lore_N%27Gasta!_Kvata!_Kvakis!.md)* is actually a slightly altered version of the [Esperanto language](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esperanto), though the [translation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Easter_Eggs#N.27Gasta.21_Kvata.21_Kvakis.21) has nothing to do with the *[Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls)* series.
