Umaril is Undone **by [Thadoril](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Thadoril)** *A letter reporting the death of Umaril the Unfeathered*
---
[Narilmor](Lore_King_Narilmor.md),

I can confirm it myself: [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) died at the [Temple of the Ancestors](Lore_Temple_of_the_Ancestors.md). My own brother carried a piece of the [mongrel](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) who dishonored us back home. They left the bastard's head as a gift to the [cattle-queen](Lore_Alessia.md) before leaving.

We must forge a unified front against these upstart [Men](Lore_Man.md) or we might share the same fate as our liege-lord. I trust I'll have your support.

Thadoril
