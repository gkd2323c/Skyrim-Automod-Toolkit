[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-quest-Black_Book_The_Winds_of_Change.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Black_Book_The_Winds_of_Change.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Black_Book_The_Winds_of_Change.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Black_Book_The_Winds_of_Change.jpg) The Winds of Change Black Book in Raven Rock Mine **The Winds of Change** is an area of [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md)'s [plane of Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Oblivion), [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md). There is only one zone in the area, *[The Winds of Change](#The_Winds_of_Change)*.

The [Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book__The_Winds_of_Change_(book).md) needed to access The Winds of Change is found inside [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md). You must progress in the quest [The Final Descent](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md) in order to retrieve the book.

The realm is inhabited by Daedra called [Seekers](Skyrim_Seeker.md) and [Lurkers](Skyrim_Lurker.md). Tentacles constantly breach the acidic dark waters and sometimes whip at you, causing a significant amount of damage if they manage to hit you. The acidic waters with the tentacles are also corrosive and cause damage if stepped in.

## The Winds of Change
The Winds of Change opens on a dim platform, with a bridge over the murky water ahead. Beyond the bridge and up two sets of stairs is a room with five pedestals guarded by a seeker. Starting with the pedestal nearest the stairs and moving clockwise, the pedestals hold a random book and a random soul gem on the first, nothing of interest on the second, two random books on the third, a random book and a copy of the [Spell Tome: Conjure Seeker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spell_Tome__Conjure_Seeker) (only if your [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill is at least level 40) on the fourth, and a random soul gem on the fifth.

To the east and northeast, two fonts of stamina flank a scrye that opens the nearby gate. Beyond the gate is a table to the left with two soul gems (one random and one leveled), followed by a font of magicka and a pod to the left. Southwest of the pod is a table with a leveled soul gem, a font of stamina, and a scrye that opens another nearby gate. Beyond the gate is a corridor guarded by a seeker. Along the corridor are two fonts of magicka, with another scrye that opens the gate at the end of the corridor.

Beyond the gate is a large room with a central pool from which a lurker rises to attack. East of the pool is a font of magicka next to a ramp that ascends to a small platform with a scrye and two pedestals flanking a table. The pedestal on the left holds two [scrolls of frenzy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Frenzy) and a [black soul gem](Skyrim_Black_Soul_Gem.md), the pedestal on the right holds two [scrolls of fear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Fear), and the table holds two black soul gems and a copy of the [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Words and Philosophy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Words_and_Philosophy)*. The scrye opens the gate on the other side of the room. West of the pool is a font of magicka and another scrye, which extends the stairs up to the gate. Beyond the gate is a vessel and the black book *The Winds of Change*. To exit this part of Apocrypha, use the non-circular part of the black book.

The three [Abilities](Skyrim_Abilities.md) offered by this book are:

- [Companion's Insight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Companion%27s_Insight) — Your attacks, shouts, and destruction spells do no damage to your followers when in combat.
- [Lover's Insight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lover%27s_Insight) — Do 10% more damage and get 10% better prices from people of the opposite sex.
- [Scholar's Insight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scholar%27s_Insight) — Reading Skill Books gives you an extra Skill Point.
