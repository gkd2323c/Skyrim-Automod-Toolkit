[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/ON-place-Senchal_Palace.jpg/200px-ON-place-Senchal_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Senchal_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Senchal_Palace.jpg) Senchal Palace ca. 2E 582 Senchal Palace is the seat of power for not just the city of [Senchal](Lore_Senchal.md) but for the southern kingdom of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md). It sits at the center of town, where all parts of Senchal, from the southern streets to the northern harbor, meet in the middle. When it was the seat of a kingdom, the monarch and their royal family lived in Senchal Palace, but after the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) killed nobles of the Pellitine bloodline, it was the home of the Senchal City Council and briefly its military protectors, the [Shields of Senchal](Lore_Shields_of_Senchal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONRenmus-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/ON-furnishing-Khajiit_of_the_Moons_Replica.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Khajiit_of_the_Moons_Replica.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Khajiit_of_the_Moons_Replica.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Khajiit_of_the_Moons_Replica.jpg) A replica of the Khajiit of the Moons It is unknown when the Senchal Palace was built, but it hosted the Senchal royal family as early as the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). One of its priceless treasures is a tapestry called the Khajiit of the Moons, a symbol of Senchal's status.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONBebbia-3)</sup> King [Dro'Zel](Lore_Dro%27Zel.md) and Queen Hasaama hosted a party for every [Heart's Day](Lore_Heart%27s_Day.md) and at the end of most days, King Dro'Zel danced with guests in the ballroom. But in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920), after hearing the [Legend of the Lovers](Lore_Legend_of_the_Lovers.md) from a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) bard from [Gil-Var-Delle](Lore_Gil-Var-Delle.md), King Dro'Zel summoned [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) to destroy the town.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-2920V2-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HOSAO-5)</sup>

Dro'Zel was later appointed as one of Emperor [Reman III](Lore_Reman_III.md)'s advisors<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-2920V11-6)</sup> and one of several aspects of how much foreign influence has helped Senchal recover from the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md). In time, the [Akaviri Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md), [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) took up residence in Senchal Palace.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HOPAO-7)</sup> In [2E 324](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_324), Versidue-Shaie was assassinated in Senchal Palace by an unknown assassin,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HOSAO-5)</sup> with the words "[MORAG TONG](Lore_Morag_Tong.md)" sprawled on the wall with his blood. This moment was important in history for a few reasons. This attack was considered the Morag Tong's first known murder in history,<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TBOD-8)</sup> even when they have been active for years, with their most famous assassination being Reman III.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-2920V12-9)</sup>

It was also monumental in the province's history as it marked the [Elsweyr Confederacy](Lore_Elsweyr_Confederacy.md)'s decline, followed by the royal family's death at the hands of rebels and the [old capital](Lore_Ne_Quin-al_(city).md)'s sacking.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HOSAO-5)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1-10)</sup> By the sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Senchal was ruled by Governor Z'har Tasluz, a vassal to the [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) royal family.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOS-1)</sup> In [2E 561](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_561), he made a speech in front of Senchal Palace. A small part of it was about [Tideholm](Lore_Tideholm.md), an island off the coast, east of [South Guard](Lore_South_Guard.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONLoad Tideholm-11)</sup>

After the [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md) killed Z'har Tasluz and the rest of the royal Pellitine bloodline, Senchal fell into chaos and was essentially ruled by criminals and outlaws. When the [Thirteenth Legion](Lore_Thirteenth_Legion.md), led by General Meric Renmus came in at Emperor [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md)' request, they re-established order, even when the Emperor disappeared and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) itself became lawless. With the help of locals, the Senchal City Council was formed, and without the Empire, the Thirteenth Legion became the city's protectors, the Shields of Senchal, and used Senchal Palace as their headquarters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONRenmus-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Southern Elsweyr dealt with the return of [Dragons](Lore_Dragons.md) and their new order, the [New Moon Cult](Lore_New_Moon_Cult.md). With help from the reformed [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), the threat was over and Elsweyr was saved. The newly named Queen of Anequina, [Khamira](Lore_Khamira.md) held a ceremony to celebrate the [heroes](Lore_Hero.md) that saved them and formally restore the Elsweyr Confederacy by accepting Senchal and Meric Renmus' warriors. Whether he joined Khamira or returned to Cyrodiil with the Shields is unknown, but peace was restored.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONPride-12)</sup>

Under the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), the city of Senchal, and by extension, Senchal Palace was the seat of power for the eponymous kingdom in Elsweyr.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TBOD-8)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Senchal Palace was occupied by King Huskir,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-AR-13)</sup> who would often give out quests to any [adventurer](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) that approached the court.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ARPalace-14)</sup>
