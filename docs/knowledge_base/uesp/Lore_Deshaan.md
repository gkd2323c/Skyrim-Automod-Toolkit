[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/ON-place-Deshaan.jpg/200px-ON-place-Deshaan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Deshaan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Deshaan.jpg) Deshaan circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Deshaan (also called the **Deshaan Plain** or **the Deshaan**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1M-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-36L:17-2)</sup> is a broad, fertile plain in the southern mainland of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). Most of the Morrowind population is gathered in the high uplands and fertile [Thir River](Lore_Thir_River.md) valleys of central Morrowind, in and around Deshaan.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POT-3)</sup> It borders [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) and the [Fungal Lowlands](Lore_Fungal_Lowlands.md) to the north, [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to the west, as well as the Dejasyte desert,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-The Seed-4)</sup> [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md), and [Thornmarsh](Lore_Thornmarsh.md) regions of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) to the south. It is composed of three smaller regions: [Redolent Loam](Lore_Redolent_Loam.md) in the west, [Lagomere](Lore_Lagomere.md) in the center, and [Siltreen](Lore_Siltreen.md) in the east.

The region is mainly composed of fertile plains and lush fungal forests which slope down and merge with [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) farther south, with sizable swamps making up the southeastern part of the region.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1M-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup>. The local [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) take advantage of the exceptionally fertile black soil, cultivating [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md) and cash crops, and breeding [kwama](Lore_Kwama.md) in deep mines for their eggs.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EGTMorrowind-6)</sup> Like elsewhere in Morrowind, [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) ruins, [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruins, and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) tombs dot the landscape. The western area of Redolent Loam is home to the city of [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md), situated on the southern banks of [Lake Hlaalu](Lore_Lake_Hlaalu.md). Deshaan's largest city, [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), sits in the center of Lagomere, surrounded by [Lake Amaya](Lore_Lake_Amaya_(Deshaan).md). The Vale of the Ghost Snake, a sacred site of the [Ashlander](Lore_Ashlander.md) [Mabrigash](Lore_Mabrigash.md) tribe, lies in the southeastern swamps. The eastern region, Siltreen, contains a large shrine devoted to [Saint Veloth](Lore_Saint_Veloth.md), where his relics are kept. The eastern town of Selfora is home to the [Tree of Summerset](Lore_Tree_of_Summerset.md), a sacred tree said to have been planted by Veloth from seeds taken from his homeland.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONForlorn-7)</sup>

## Fauna
The fauna of Deshaan includes [guar](Lore_Guar.md), [netches](Lore_Netch.md), [kagouti](Lore_Kagouti.md), [durzogs](Lore_Durzog.md), and [alit](Lore_Alit.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup> Tiger Guar are a legendary breed of large wild guar found only on the Deshaan, and are a highly prized game animal for noble huntsmen; their stripes have been compared to those of a [tiger](Lore_Tiger.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-9)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-10)</sup> The [banded guar charger](#Banded_Guar_Charger) is a breed descended from the tiger guar.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EDAYQ-9)</sup>

## History
The Dunmer have generally lived across Morrowind and the Deshaan Plain since they first migrated from the distant southwest. Vivec once found Nerevar in a grub field outside of the swamps of the Deshaan Plain, and went for a walk with him.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-36L:17-2)</sup> In the wake of the [Year of Sun's Death](Lore_Sun%27s_Death.md) in [1E 668](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_668), less than half of the province was left in ruin and Deshaan experienced significant climate disruption resulting in the population to permanently shift towards the south into the Deshaan plain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1M-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-AMOD-11)</sup>

During the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), a group of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) traveled north out of Black Marsh and established the farming settlement of [Silent Mire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silent_Mire) along the eastern coast of Siltreen.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Vara-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Leel-13)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Maulborn, a militant group seeking to depose the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) and restore worship of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md), unleashed the [Llodos Plague](Lore_Llodos_Plague.md) across Deshaan in a direct assault on [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESO-5)</sup>
