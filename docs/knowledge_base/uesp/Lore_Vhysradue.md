*[Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s finest swordmasters lie dead at my feet. What hope do you have?*
—Vhysradue<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMV-1)</sup> **Sword Master** Vhysradue was a renowned [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) grandmaster warrior known for numerous conquests, fearless challenges, and a century-long commitment to surpassing the greatest swordmasters of Tamriel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RH-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-place-Hill_of_Shattered_Swords.jpg/200px-ON-place-Hill_of_Shattered_Swords.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hill_of_Shattered_Swords.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Hill_of_Shattered_Swords.jpg) Hill of Shattered Swords Little is known about Vhysradue's past. Around [2E 482](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_482), she journeyed to [Anequina](Lore_Anequina.md) and staked her claim on the Hill of Shattered Swords southeast of [Riverhold](Lore_Riverhold.md). While the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of Anequina expected her to eventually succumb to old age, she defiantly remained on the hill for a century, taunting and challenging anyone who passed by.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RH-2)</sup> Living for such an extended period, she showcased a [longevity](Lore_Lifespan.md) that surpassed the usual expectations for her kin.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RH-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Avita Pitio-3)</sup>

She amassed a collection of old banners, believed to be trophies from past conquests,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RH-2)</sup> and wielded the banners of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), and [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) strategically in battle, linking fury with the Pact, and vitality with the Dominion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMV-1)</sup>

The Sword Master emerged victorious against numerous champions from various regions, including some of Tamriel's most skilled swordmasters. The hill was adorned with the remains of her adversaries.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMV-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FC-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-HOSS-5)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ri'hirr, a Khajiit associated with the Northern Elsweyr Defense Force enlisted mercenaries to eliminate her and retrieve personal mementos from the bodies of fallen challengers, to provide solace to their families. During that year, she was finally defeated by these hired challengers, who were later rewarded by representatives of the Defense Force.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RH-2)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SOTS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-FCM-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Akaviri swordsmen have been described as possessing a power similar to the [thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md), called "[kiai](Lore_Kiai.md)".<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-COTS-8)</sup> Some of Vhysradue's abilities were identical to the Ardent Flame magic of [Dragonknights](Lore_Dragonknights.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SMV-1)</sup> techniques that stem from [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) methods.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Book1-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Book2-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Book3-11)</sup>
