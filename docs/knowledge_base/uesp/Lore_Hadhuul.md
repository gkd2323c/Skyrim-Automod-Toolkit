The Fire King Hadhuul (or **Hadhuul the Fire King**) was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) leader who ruled before and during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup> He is remembered as a particularly cruel slave master; tales from his realm say the slaves were subjected to tormenting [drugs](Lore_Drugs.md), and children were set aflame for nighttime tiger sport.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TA-2)</sup> Shortly before the fall of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-3)</sup> he was slain by [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) on the granite steps of Ceya-Tar (later called [Ceyatatar](Lore_Ceyatatar.md))<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup> in the [Great Forest](Lore_Great_Forest.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Oblivion-4)</sup> It was his first and last defeat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSOP3-1)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Hadhuul's spirit returned to the ruins of Ceyatatar, along with bound [flame atronachs](Lore_Flame_Atronach.md) and the ghosts of his army. The atronachs posed a threat to the nearby town of [Vlastarus](Lore_Vlastarus.md), although the Fire King was banished before he could cause any harm.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
