*A soul that deserved transcendence. May her song be heard in the hiss of every piston and the sigh of every spring.*
—A plaque commemorating Sotha Nall<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SN-1)</sup> [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/ON-place-Ald_Sotha.jpg/200px-ON-place-Ald_Sotha.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ald_Sotha.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Ald_Sotha.jpg) Ald Sotha Sotha Nall was a [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and the older sister of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) God [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md). Nall lived at the minor estate of [Ald Sotha](Lore_Ald_Sotha.md), where it is presumed she perished when [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) attacked and destroyed Ald Sotha, leaving Sotha Sil as the only known survivor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFSV-2)</sup>

Sotha Sil memorialized her in the form of a projection within the Elegiac Replication at the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) with a plaque underneath lamenting her death as that of a "soul that deserved transcendence".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SN-1)</sup> Multi-planar mnemonic receptacles, also known as [Stars](Lore_Stars.md), in the [Mnemonic Planisphere](Lore_Mnemonic_Planisphere.md) also contained recordings of her voice.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-star-3)</sup> Certain [Clockwork Apostles](Lore_Clockwork_Apostles.md) also theorized the [factotums](Lore_Factotum.md)' voices were that of Sotha Nall and that part of her "animus" was contained within the factotums due to the verbal artifacts such as "burning beds ... screaming", which were most likely Sotha Nall's final traumatic memories.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFSV-2)</sup>
