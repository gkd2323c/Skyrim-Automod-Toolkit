[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/SR-npc-Savos_Aren.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Savos_Aren.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Savos_Aren.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Savos_Aren.jpg) Savos Aren doing some research [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/SR-place-Winterhold.jpg/200px-SR-place-Winterhold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Winterhold.jpg) What's left of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) after [The Great Collapse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:On_the_Great_Collapse) **Savos Aren** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer_(class).md) and the current Arch-Mage of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). Always buried in work and his own thoughts, Savos has entrusted Master Wizard [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md) with most of his daily duties, a decision that has left a lot of the lesser-ranked College [members](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md#People) quite puzzled and frustrated. During Aren's leadership of the College of Winterhold, the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) sent [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md), one of their agents, to be his "advisor", though Aren seems strangely unaware of his hidden agenda. Unfortunately, when you bring in [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md), Ancano powers himself with the artifact and Savos Aren will realize his advisor's true intentions too late.

As Arch-Mage, Savos has his own [quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md), located above the [Arcanaeum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Arcanaeum), and he spends most of his time there. He starts the day at 7am with a one-hour breakfast, then heads down the stairs to the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements) where he spends three hours wandering around. At noon he takes a one-hour lunch in his quarters. At 2pm on Morndas to Fredas, he heads out into the courtyard where he stays until 6pm, occasionally [talking](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md#Miscellaneous_Events) to his second-in-command, Mirabelle Ervine. At 7pm he takes a one-hour dinner. At all other times, he can be found inside his quarters, either relaxing in a chair, eating, or using the [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md). He was supposed to sleep in his bed between 1am and 6am but doesn't due to a [bug](#Bugs). There are several exceptions to this routine; see [this section](#Quest-Related_Events) for details.

He wears the enchanted and unique [Archmage's robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Archmage%27s_Robes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots_(Arch-Mage).md) with a [Resist Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Shock) enchantment. He carries nothing but a [red apple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple). He knows a number of spells, specifically [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md), [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md), [Close Wounds](Skyrim_Close_Wounds.md), [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), and [Flame Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flame_Cloak). He also knows the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spells [Conjure Frost Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md) and [Conjure Dremora Lord](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Dremora_Lord).

## Dialogue
His normal greetings:

*"What you learn here will last you a lifetime. Several, if you're talented."* *"It's no secret that the College's reputation in Skyrim is... tainted."* *"Please don't do anything to anger the locals. They don't need additional reasons to hate us."* *"It is not the place of the College to become involved in local politics."* *"Someday the College may be fully accepted by the Nords. I hope that my work as Arch-Mage may help."* When you first speak to him:

*"You are new here, are you not? We have not spoken."* **No, we haven't.** *"Then allow me to introduce myself. I am Savos Aren, Arch-Mage of the College of Winterhold. I am quite content to see nearly any aspect of magic explored and investigated here. But I do not and will not approve of any research or experiments that cause purposeful harm to your fellow members of the College. Are we clear?"* **No, sir.** *""Sir"? How quaint. Then allow me to introduce myself. I am Savos Aren, Arch-Mage of the College of Winterhold. I am quite content to see nearly any aspect of magic explored and investigated here. But I do not and will not approve of any research or experiments that cause purposeful harm to your fellow members of the College. Are we clear?"* He will then provide a wealth of information on the College and its background:

**Dangerous research must be a problem here.** *"Not often, no. Some risks must be taken, to be sure. I am simply trying to avoid untimely deaths. We also must make an effort to avoid worsening what Skyrim thinks of us."* **What's the purpose of the College?** *"Purpose? I should think it's self-evident. Magic is a true power, not something to be shunned by commoners or treated as an amusing diversion by politicians. It shapes worlds, creates and destroys life... It deserves proper respect and study. The College is a place where we can focus on that, without the pressures of the world weighing down on us."* **I'd like to know more about the College's history.** *"I've seen documents dating back to the late [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Beyond that, it's hard to say. Events in the last several hundred years resulted in a significant loss of our history, I'm afraid."* **What happened in the last few hundred years?** *"[The Great Collapse](Lore_Great_Collapse.md), as it has come to be known. An unfortunate natural disaster that ravaged the area. The College fared far better than the city of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), but it was not left untouched."* **What caused the Great Collapse?** *"No one is sure of the cause. Some believed the eruption of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) had far-reaching consequences that were only felt years later. I know there are some who have blamed the College, said that we were responsible. I assure you this is not the case."* **Tell me more about the Great Collapse.** *"The [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) practically came alive. No one was expecting it. Monstrous waves battered the shore for weeks on end. Winterhold was ancient and weathered, but it couldn't withstand the sea's fury. Entire districts of the city were lost overnight. The waves receded in time, but the damage was irreversible. Most residents of Winterhold abandoned what was left of the city. The College survived, and so here we remain."*
## Quest-Related Events

### Under Saarthal
Savos will keep a low profile until the very final stage of [Under Saarthal](Skyrim_Under_Saarthal.md) when [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) asks you to deliver the news about the strange Orb directly to the Arch-Mage. At that point, Savos will skip his entire schedule and will sit in his quarters, reading a book all day long and only takes a break to wander around between 2am and 7am. When you say that you need to speak with him about [Saarthal](Skyrim_Saarthal.md), he will respond:

**I need to speak to you about Saarthal.** *"Please don't tell me that another one of the apprentices has been incinerated. I have enough to deal with right now."* **We've found something in Saarthal, and Tolfdir thinks it's important.** *"Very well, I trust that you wouldn't be here were it not significant. Thank you for bringing this to my attention."* **We found some sort of... orb. Tolfdir wanted you to see it.** *"I... see. I trust that Tolfdir will provide a more... specific explanation. Thank you for bringing this to my attention."* Either option will lead to:

*"Tolfdir normally looks after your little group, yes? Since he's apparently occupied, and I will need to see this discovery for myself, I think perhaps you should begin researching the subject. Speak with Urag in the Arcanaeum. See if he is aware of anything that matches your discovery. And... good work. The next time you find yourself exploring Nordic ruins, perhaps [this](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Magelight) will be helpful."* Both replies will finish Under Saarthal and start the quest [Hitting the Books](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hitting_the_Books). You can also ask him if he ever met with the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) and he will respond:

**Have you ever met with the Psijic Order?** *"Personally? No, not I. One of their number used to advise the Arch-Mage when I was but an Apprentice here. But that was a great many years ago, before all the members of the order were called back to the [Isle of Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md), and it disappeared entirely."*
### Good Intentions
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/SR-quest-Good_Intentions.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Good_Intentions.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Good_Intentions.jpg) A [visitor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir) from the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md) When you have returned the missing books to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md), thus initiating the quest [Good Intentions](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md), the Arch-Mage's Quarters will be locked once again because of a very special visitor from the [Psijic Order](Skyrim_Psijic_Order.md). When [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md) has insulted [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) and asked you to follow him, he will lead you up to the Quarters where a puzzled Savos Aren will be accompanied by Psijic Order member [Quaranir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quaranir). After slowing down time, Quaranir will privately warn you against the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md). When you return to normal, Quaranir will leave the College grounds and Savos Aren will be just as confused as Ancano:

*"I'm... I'm not sure what happened. A monk from the Psijic Order, here, after all these years, and then he just leaves. I hope we didn't offend him somehow."* **What did the Psijic want?** *"Beyond asking for you, he never said. Very strange indeed."* **Have you ever heard of the Augur of Dunlain?** *"Has Tolfdir been telling stories again? I thought I made it quite clear that this was a subject inappropriate for conversation. Please don't allow him to continue to discuss the subject."* Still not realizing the potential threat from the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md), he will then return to his normal schedule until you return from [The Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden).

After your informative conversation with the Augur of Dunlain, you are told to report back to Savos Aren with the alarming news about the Eye. When you surface from the Midden, Savos Aren will be found in the [Hall of the Elements](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hall_of_the_Elements) around the clock, finally interested in the Eye:

**I have important information for you.** *"Really? And what might that be?"* **We need to find the Staff of Magnus.** *"I'm sorry, what? Well... I'd certainly love to have such a powerful staff, but I'm not really sure that any of us need it."* **It's connected to the orb we found.** *"And how do you know this?"* **I spoke with the Augur of Dunlain.** *"Did you really? And he specifically mentioned the Staff of Magnus? I... I'm impressed with your initiative. Of course, someone will need to follow up on this."* **You mean me, don't you?** *"I certainly do. Since you went so far as seek out the Augur for advice, I thought you'd be more enthusiastic."* **What should I do next?** *"A most impressive attitude. Keep this up, and you'll do quite well for yourself."* Both options will lead him to point you towards [Mirabelle Ervine](Skyrim_Mirabelle_Ervine.md):

*"Something as specific and ancient as the Staff of Magnus... I'm not sure we'd ever find something like that... I seem to recall Mirabelle mentioning the staff somewhat recently. Why don't you see if she can tell you anything? I'm quite pleased with your progress, you know. You've certainly proven yourself to be more than a mere Apprentice. Well done. [This circlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mage%27s_Circlet) once proved invaluable to me. I hope it can be of use to you now."* This ends the quest and starts the next one, [Revealing the Unseen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen).

### Revealing the Unseen
When you return from [Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft) you will find that [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md) finally snapped and sealed off the Hall of the Elements in its entirety, making it impossible for anyone to get in. As you enter the College you will find Savos, at last aware of the danger present in his College, and engaged in conversation with Master Wizard Mirabelle Ervine about what to do and how to lift the spell:

**Mirabelle**: *"I don't know. It's like a ward, but who's casting it? Ancano? How?"*
**Savos**: *"I don't care what it is, I want it down now! I want to know what he's doing in there!"*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/SR-npc-Ancano_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ancano_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ancano_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ancano_04.jpg) *"Ancano! Stop this at once! I command you!"* When you talk to Savos you will have three different topics to choose from:

**I know where to find the Staff of Magnus.** *"Excellent. I'd suggest that we go retrieve it immediately, but right now we have more pressing matters. Ancano has somehow locked himself in the hall. He's up to something, and I intend to put a stop to it. Help us get through this, will you? We're throwing everything we have at it."* **What's going on?** *"Ancano. He's in there, doing... something. We don't know what. We're trying to get in now. I will have his head for this, I assure you. Help take this down, will you? We're throwing everything we can at it."* **What's so important about the Staff of Magnus?** *"I don't know. If the Augur suggested it, there must be a good reason for locating it. I'm afraid I don't see the connection, though."* He will then turn around and help Mirabelle break down the barrier, making way to Ancano and the Eye of Magnus. If he is spoken to again:

**I know where to find the Staff of Magnus.** *"Now's not the time. We need to put a stop to whatever it is that's going on."* Savos will then run into the room and confront Ancano, while Mirabelle is understandably worried:

**Mirabelle**: *"What's going on?"*
**Savos**: *"Ancano! Stop this at once! I command you!"*
**Mirabelle**: *"Don't go near him!"*

A flash of white light will then emerge from the Eye of Magnus and knock you off your feet. When you come to, you will find Mirabelle wounded and Savos Aren nowhere to be seen. Sadly, when you enter the courtyard, you will find the dead body of Savos Aren, seemingly killed by the blast. Shocked College members will be gathered near the fountain but Tolfdir will quickly approach you and point your attention elsewhere, as [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) is in desperate [need of help](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Containment). When you return from Winterhold, Savos Aren's body will be gone.

### The Staff of Magnus
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/99/SR-place-Labyrinthian.jpg/200px-SR-place-Labyrinthian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Labyrinthian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Labyrinthian.jpg) [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md), where secrets will be revealed During the quest [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) you will find that, as a student in the College, Aren led a party of five other students on an exploration to the ancient ruins of [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md), in the hope of finding rewards hidden in the ruins. However, they underestimated the dangers the ruins held, the exploration went awry, and the students began to die one by one, leaving Aren as the sole survivor. As you approach the entrance to Labyrinthian, you will encounter the ghosts of the expedition, Savos Aren included, talking about what they think will be an easy exploration. Besides Savos, the party contains the now deceased mages [Atmah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atmah), [Girduin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Girduin), [Takes-In-Light](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Takes-In-Light), [Hafnar Ice-Fist](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hafnar_Ice-Fist), and [Elvali Veren](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elvali_Veren). Savos Aren, seemingly younger and less experienced, wears [blue mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blue_Mage_Robes) and a pair of regular [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c) and will have no spells at his disposal. Near the entrance, the ghosts will have this conversation:

**Savos Aren**: *"Come on, we're finally here! Let's not waste any more time!"* **Takes-In-Light**: *"Are we truly sure this is a good idea?"* **Atmah**: *"We'll be back at the College before anyone even knows we're gone."* **Girduin**: *"You would care about that, since you're the Arch-Mage's favorite!"* **Savos Aren**: *"Don't forget, this whole idea was Atmah's to begin with."* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"Let's just get inside, see what's in there."* All the apprentices are obviously very excited, unaware of what they will encounter inside Labyrinthian. As you progress through the ruin, you will see the ghosts on several occasions, although it becomes clear that they are in way over their head as the group gets smaller and smaller. In the very first chamber, you will witness the group preparing themselves:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_03.jpg) *"I can't believe we're doing this."* **Elvali Veren**: *"I can't believe we're doing this."* **Savos Aren**: *"Can you imagine the looks on their faces when we come back?"* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"You keep talking like you're sure we'll find something useful in here."* **Girduin**: *"Given the history of this place, it's more than likely there's still some amount of power here."* **Savos Aren**: *"Enchanted weapons, tomes of ancient knowledge, Shalidor's secrets themselves -- who knows what we could find!"* **Takes-In-Light**: *"And what if... What if there are things guarding this place?"* **Atmah**: *"Against six College-trained mages? I think we'll be fine."* However, the group obviously encountered the [skeletal dragon](Skyrim_Skeletal_Dragon.md). In the next chamber, they will reflect on how to deal with the loss of Girduin and the fact that they can't escape Labyrinthian:

**Elvali Veren**: *"We... we have to go back. We can't leave Girduin..."* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"We barely made it out alive, and you want to go back in?"* **Atmah**: *"It's too late. There isn't enough of him left to go back in after."* **Takes-In-Light**: *"Gods, what have we done?"* **Savos Aren**: *"We can't go back. Might as well go forward. We can still do this."* **Atmah**: *"Savos is right. We can make it if we just stay alert."* As you proceed through Labyrinthian, a voice will start to address you, thinking of you as Aren: *"Have you returned, Aren? My old friend?"*, *"Do you seek to finish that which you could not?"*, and *"You only face failure once more..."* When the source of the voice finally realizes that Aren is nowhere in the ruin, it will say: *"You... You are not Aren, are you? Has he sent you in his place?"* and *"Did he warn you that your own power would be your undoing? That it would only serve to strengthen me?"* As for Savos and his failed expedition, you will encounter the ghosts again near the end of [Labyrinthian Thoroughfare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Labyrinthian_Thoroughfare), with another party member dead:

We have to keep moving!"* **Atmah**: *"Where's Elvali? She was right behind me."* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"Dead. Something grabbed her from behind. Gone before I could do anything."* **Takes-In-Light**: *"This is insanity. We never should've come here."* **Atmah**: *"You're right. This is all my fault. Should we turn around, head back?"* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"I don't think going back is a good idea."* **Savos Aren**: *"Going back would be the end of all of us. We keep pushing forward, and we'll make it. We will!"* **Atmah**: *"Come on, you can make it. Let's go."* Just before the entrance to the final chamber of the ruin, the ghosts will appear again, this time without Takes-In-Light and all seemingly in acceptance that this will be their demise:

**Atmah**: *"We shouldn't have left her there to die!"* **Savos Aren**: *"What else could we do? Stay there and die with her? She refused to go on, we didn't have a choice!"* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"This is it, you know. Through this door. Can you feel it?"* **Atmah**: *"We're not going to make it, are we?"* **Hafnar Ice-Fist**: *"We stay together, no matter what. Agreed?"* **Atmah**: *"I'll be right with you."* **Savos Aren**: *"Agreed. We all stay together."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Staff_of_Magnus_04.jpg) [Athmah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atmah) and [Hafnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hafnar_Ice-Fist) enthralled Inside the chamber of Dragon Priest [Morokei](Skyrim_Morokei_(creature).md) you will find two [enthralled wizards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enthralled_Wizard) who bear a close resemblance to [Atmah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Atmah) and [Hafnar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hafnar_Ice-Fist). They are both casting an energy barrier on Morokei and you will have no choice but to destroy them both in order to fight the priest. As you are heading for the exit, you will encounter the ghost of Savos Aren one last time, now all alone and shouting apologies to his former friends who he had to sacrifice in order to keep Morokei sealed away in Labyrinthian:

**Savos Aren**: *"...I'm sorry, friends. I'm so sorry! I had no choice! It was the only way to make sure that monster never escaped! I promise you, I'll never let this happen again! I'll seal this whole place away..."* He will then disappear, never to be seen again.

## Unused Dialogue
For [The Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) a full recollection from Savos Aren can be found in the game data. It is divided into seven different scenes and it appears the developers initially intended for you to be guided through Labyrinthian by Aren's ghost. The unused dialogue provides the last pieces of the puzzle and Aren's sad memories of the tragic events:

| Scene | Savos Aren: |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | *"I knew you'd come eventually. It would seem I'm bound to this place. The bitter irony of it all -- my greatest failure, and even in death I can't escape it. I never meant for any of what happened here. Tried to seal it up, lock it away forever. But now it all comes out again..."* |

## Notes
- The [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) you meet in [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md) is an alternative version of Savos Aren, with the Base ID (`000D07DB`).
- When talking to [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md), Arch-Mage Savos Aren's shady Thalmor advisor, he will explain his role at the College: *"I am an advisor to the Arch-Mage. The Thalmor wish to promote relations with your College. I am at the Arch-Mage's disposal if he requires advice."* If you ask how often this happens, Ancano will admit that Aren rarely seeks his help: *"Perhaps not as often as he should. In time, I believe he will be more trusting."*

He likes to believe the College is removed from the rest of Skyrim."* and *"If he could, Savos would float the the * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * entire College above Skyrim, for all the Nords to see."* Many of them see Mirabelle as the next Arch-Mage as she is in charge anyway: *"She's the Master-Wizard here.
