Echateres are large herbivorous mammals<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOOrz KS-1)</sup> with tusks, pointed hooves, and large antlers. Found mainly in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), they serve the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) as livestock and beasts of burden. They provide meat, fur,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AAE-2)</sup> vellum,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> and milk for the Orcs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOOrz KS-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOBul-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DDDDH-5)</sup> Echateres are stubborn and ornery.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOEchalette-6)</sup> Those found in the wild are usually hostile, using their tusks and antlers when in battle, with capabilities to send shockwaves through the ground using their immense weight.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Combat-7)</sup>

Young echateres are called echalettes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOEchalette-6)</sup> They are generally docile and fawn-like.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Bad Ech-8)</sup> Badger Ruff Echalettes are black and white striped echalettes that are exceptionally irritable and fierce for young echateres. They've been known to chase away [sabre cats](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Bad Ech-8)</sup> During the spring, echalettes are occasionally born with temporarily spotted fur that helps them camouflage in the sun-dappled forests. This pattern fades as the echatere matures.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

Echateres are herbivores. They are known to eat sedge grass, snow truffles, and echatere moss.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOOrz-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TFo TF-11)</sup> They are also prized by Orcs for their ability to find and dig for snow truffles.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AQuest Of Succ-12)</sup>
