[The Thirty-Six Lessons of Vivec](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md), Sermon Fifteen **by [Vivec](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Vivec)** *Book 15 of a series of words of wisdom relating to Vivec*
---
These were the days of [Resdaynia](Lore_Resdayn.md), when [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) lived under the wise and benevolent rule of the [AMLSIVI](Lore_Almsivi.md) and their champion the [Hortator](Lore_Nerevar.md). When the gods of [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) would retreat unto their own, to mold the cosmos and other matters, the Hortator would at times become confused. [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md) would always be there to advise him, and this is the third of the three lessons of ruling kings:

'The ruling king will remove me, his maker. This is the way of all children. His greatest enemy is the [Sharmat](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md), who is the false dreamer. You or he is the shingle, Hortator. Beware the [wrong walking path](Lore_Walking_Ways.md#First_Walking_Way_-_The_Numidium). Beware the crime of benevolence. Behold him by his words.'

'You alone, though you [come again and again](Lore_Nerevarine.md), can unmake him. Whether I allow it is within my wisdom. Go unarmed into [his den](Lore_Dagoth-Ur_(place).md) with these words of power: AE GHARTOK PADHOME [CHIM] AE ALTADOON. Or do not. The temporal myth is man. Reach heaven by violence. This magic I give to you: the world you will rule is only an intermittent hope and you must be the letter written in uncertainty.'

The ending of the words is ALMSIVI.
