[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-npc-Revus_Sarvani.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Revus_Sarvani.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Revus_Sarvani.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Revus_Sarvani.jpg) Revus Sarvani [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/26/SR-quest-My_Pet_Nix-Hound.jpg/200px-SR-quest-My_Pet_Nix-Hound.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-My_Pet_Nix-Hound.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-My_Pet_Nix-Hound.jpg) Revus with a tamed [nix-hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound_(creature).md) **Revus Sarvani**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) merchant, is the owner of Dusty the [silt strider](Skyrim_Silt_Strider.md), and can be found southwest of the [Sun Stone](Skyrim_Sun_Stone.md), northwest of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md). He personally trained Dusty since she was a larva, and is staying with the now-elderly silt strider until she passes in peace. He sells a variety of items, including a [Kagrumez Resonance Gem](Skyrim_Kagrumez_Resonance_Gem.md) which can be used for the [related quest](Skyrim_The_Kagrumez_Gauntlet.md).

Even if the small camp has a tent and a bedroll, Revus never sleeps. Instead, he spends all of his time wandering, eating, warming his hands by the fire, or just relaxing. He wears the [outfit](Skyrim_Dunmer_Outfit.md) and [shoes](Skyrim_Dunmer_Shoes.md) of a Dunmer and carries a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md), as well as an assortment of ingredients and other items, all of them for sale.

With the [Nix-Hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, Revus will have a tamed [Nix-Hound](Skyrim_Nix-Hound_(creature).md) at his camp and will send you a [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Nix-Hound_(Revus).md) offering to sell it to you as a pet. You can purchase it from him for 400 gold by saying *"I'd like to purchase the Nix-Hound"*, to which he will wordlessly hand you a [spell tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Teleport_Pet_Nix-Hound.md). If Revus is dead prior to installing the Creation, the Nix-Hound will instead be passed on to [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md) who will offer to sell it on his behalf.

## Dialogue
When he sees you, he will ask:

- *"From that look on your face I'm guessing you've never seen a [Silt Strider](Lore_Silt_Strider.md) before?"*

- **No, what is it?**
- *"This is Dusty. I personally trained her from a larva. Feels like a lifetime ago now. She hasn't been in traveling condition for quite a while now. I've tried to let her go off on her own but she insists on staying here."*
- **How do you survive out here?**
- *"We get by trading odds and ends with the locals. I've decided to stay here and make sure she passes in peace. It's the least I can do for her. Every once in a while a local makes it out here. Although, they usually just want to catch a glimpse of Dusty. Silt Striders are pretty rare now."*

- **About Silt Striders...**
- *"What would you like to know?"*

- - **Why are they so rare?**
- *"The blast from the [volcano](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) wiped most of them out. Found Dusty here in a small cave on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). Silt Strider cocoons are pretty tough."*

- - **What were they used for?**
- *"Mostly for transporting goods and people. They were pretty commonplace in Morrowind, but that was ages ago. A Silt Strider carapace is so strong it can withstand the harshest of weather. Even the legendary sandstorms of Vvardenfell's [Molag Amur](Lore_Molag_Amur.md) Region were said to barely make a dent in them. Of course, that's all ash now."*

- - **Enough about this.**
- *"All right."*

## Notes
- He is not affected by the *[Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md)* perk.
- All of his initial items for trade can be [pickpocketed](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md). However, items sold to him cannot be pickpocketed back.
- He bears the same name as the author of *[Light Armor Forging](Lore_Light_Armor_Forging.md)*.
- His silt strider is meant to remain static, and therefore doesn't actually move from its position.
- A quote by a Revus Sarvani appears on the [loading screen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Loading_Screens) for [Dreudurai Glass Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Dreudurai_Glass_Mine) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
