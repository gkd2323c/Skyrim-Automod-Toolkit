[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-npc-Ri%27saad.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ri%27saad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ri%27saad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ri%27saad.jpg) Ri'saad outside of Whiterun **Ri'saad** is a [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md) [pawnbroker](Skyrim_Pawnbroker.md), accompanied by [Atahbah](Skyrim_Atahbah.md), [Khayla](Skyrim_Khayla.md) and [Ma'randru-jo](Skyrim_Ma%27randru-jo.md). His caravan is found traveling the roads of Skyrim between [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) and [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md), stopping outside of each city and setting up tents for two full days before moving on. He buys and sells every type of item and is a good supplier of [ebony ingots](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md), [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md) and [moon sugar](Skyrim_Moon_Sugar.md). [Ma'dran](Skyrim_Ma%27dran.md) and [Ahkari](Skyrim_Ahkari.md) are other [Khajiit traders](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) who work for Ri'saad.

Ri'saad wears a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md#000f8715) and a pair of [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md#000cee82). He is equipped with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), and carries a selection of [upper-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Rich).

## Dialogue
When approached, he may greet you with:

- *"Welcome. If I cannot serve you, I am sure that one of my other traders can do so."*
- *"I have traveled far across Tamriel to serve you."*
- *"Perhaps you have encountered Ma'dran or Akhari * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] *? Both are sworn to me, and both have worthy goods to offer."*

If spoken to directly you can ask him a few questions:

- **Why sell your goods in Skyrim?**
- *"An astute question, for we are far from home and this is a cold, hard land. The wise trader finds the best opportunities, even if he must travel far to find them. Skyrim is a ripe opportunity indeed. The dragons and the war have scared many other traders away, but for those with courage, there is much profit to be made."*

- **I'm curious about your homeland.**
- *"The Khajiit hail from a distant land called [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), bordered on the north by [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and the south by the glistening blue waters of the sea. Elsweyr is an arid land of deserts and rocky canyons, where the sun shines warmly, always. There are cities so ancient, the sands have swallowed them whole. But now I will say no more, for I miss my home greatly."*

When exiting conversations he will politely say:

- *"Was there anything else, or is our transaction complete?"*

## Quest-Related Events

### Find the Thalmor Assassin
If you free Malborn during [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md), Ri'saad and his caravan members will be found near [Windhelm Stables](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables.md) (where [Ma'dran's Caravan](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md#Ma.27dran.27s_Caravan) usually camps) and he can be asked to point out the Thalmor assassin. He will oblige gladly, pointing out [J'datharr](Skyrim_J%27datharr.md):

- **I'm looking for a Khajiit... might be a Thalmor spy.**
- *"Ah. You must mean that one over there. He's not one of us. Thalmor filth, eh? Giving all Khajiit a bad name."*

### Thieves Guild Caravan Fence Quest
During his related quest, Ri'saad will be more cautious when he greets you:

- *"You approach as if you know us, stranger. Who are you and what do you want?"*
- **I bring an offer from the Thieves Guild.**
- *"Yes, I've heard the Guild is rising back to power. An alliance would be most beneficial to both parties. I'll consider the offer, but I'll have to discuss it with the other caravans."*
- **Perhaps this will sweeten the deal? (Give satchel)**
- *"Ah, Moon Sugar. I could smell it on you. I'm pleased with this offer, and accept. Take my word to the leaders of your guild and tell them we look forward to a prosperous and profitable future. If you happen upon any of our caravans in your travels, we'd also be more than willing to pay you a fair sum for any of your stolen goods. Give my regards to Tonilia. Tell her I'll contact her soon."*

### Balance of Power<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>

- **I've heard you've been running into trouble along the road.**
- *"It's all in this [note](Skyrim_Ri%27saad%27s_Note_on_Bandit_Attacks.md)."*

## Notes
- Ri'saad is mentioned in the book *[Ghosts in the Storm](Skyrim_Ghosts_in_the_Storm.md)*. - Despite the author of said book, [Adonato Leotelli](Skyrim_Adonato_Leotelli.md), having traveled with Ri'saad's caravan personally, he will never mention him or the caravans in dialogue. The other Khajiit will never mention the death of apparent member Bhisha, either.
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states the following about Ma'dran's caravan: *"Ma'dran's route is unique in that he shares a common stop with Ri'saad's caravan: Windhelm, However, Ma'dran's route runs from Windhelm to Solitude instead of to Markarth."* Ri'saad's caravan does not have a stop at Windhelm.
- If you are a Khajiit, there is no alternate dialogue option for asking about "his" homeland. The option simply does not appear.
- If you free Malborn during [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md), Ri'saad and his caravan will move near [Windhelm Stables](Skyrim_Windhelm_Stables.md) as part of the quest [Find the Thalmor Assassin](Skyrim_Find_the_Thalmor_Assassin.md). They will resume their usual schedule when the [hidden timer](Skyrim_Find_the_Thalmor_Assassin.md#Notes) runs out or when the assassin is dead.
