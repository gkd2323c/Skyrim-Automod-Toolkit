# Imperial Academy of Records and Histories

The Imperial Academy of Records and Histories was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution devoted to the research of the history active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Little is known of the academy, except that one of its associate historians [Augusta Purusius](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Augusta_Purusius) authored *[The Sublime Brazier](Lore_The_Sublime_Brazier.md)*, a text devoted to the legend of [Sublime Brazier](Lore_Sublime_Brazier.md) hidden beneath the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and published at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSB-1)</sup>

## Known Members
