# Ada

The Ada (meaning **spirit**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-2)</sup> are a type of [deity](Lore_Gods.md). Most known Ada were demi-deities, derived from pairings of mortals and the [Et'Ada](Lore_Et%27Ada.md), the original spirits. It is said that the Ada can change things through love,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP5-3)</sup> with an example of this ability coming in the form of the Ada [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md) creating a new race, the [Minotaur](Lore_Minotaur.md), from his union with [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OM-4)</sup> Ada are said to be ambitious beings that can never know death.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LAFNJTR2-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSOP3-6)</sup>

## Known Ada
- [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md), like his "nephew" Morihaus, has been described as a [Demigod](Lore_Demigod.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TFS-7)</sup> His parentage is unknown, but it was said that he was thought to be the [Shezarrine](Lore_Shezarrine.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP5-3)</sup> and the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) likened him to [Shor](Lore_Shor.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TSOP4-8)</sup> Despite his hatred of god-talk, Pelinal himself spoke of a twin nature he shared with [Aka](Lore_Akatosh.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TSOP6-9)</sup>
- [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril.md) was the son of an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) mother with his father being a god of the prior [kalpa](Lore_Kalpa.md) giving him the blood of the Ada.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSOP7-10)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TSOP3-6)</sup> He also furthered his immortality by binding himself to the realm of his mistress [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md), giving him a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) nature.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Oblivion-11)</sup>
- [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md) was the Demigod son of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP5-3)</sup> His father is unknown but he took on the shape of a man bull.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP5-3)</sup>
- [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LAFNJTR2-5)</sup> was the [Demiprince](Lore_Demiprince.md) scion of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-LAFNJTR-12)</sup> His mortal parent was unknown but he took on the appearance of a demonic [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-AMB-13)</sup>
