## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Hillevi Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Hillevi_Cruel-Sea.md).
2. Deliver a bottle of [Nightshade Extract](Skyrim_Nightshade_Extact.md) to [Wuunferth the Unliving](Skyrim_Wuunferth_the_Unliving.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Hillevi Cruel-Sea](Skyrim_Hillevi_Cruel-Sea.md) has a market stall in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), selling produce. When asked whether she grows the crops all by herself she will mention that her family owns [Hollyfrost Farm](Skyrim_Hollyfrost_Farm.md) outside the city, and, on top of that, also receive payment from [Wuunferth the Unliving](Skyrim_Wuunferth_the_Unliving.md) for every portion of [Nightshade](Skyrim_Nightshade.md) they plant. She will ask you to deliver a bottle of [Nightshade Extract](Skyrim_Nightshade_Extact.md) to the court wizard.

Wuunferth can be found in the upstairs area of the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) during the day and wanders around in the throne room at night. Hand over the extract and he will thank you with a leveled amount of gold. He mentions that the extract is very poisonous, but he does not reveal its purpose.
