[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Commander_Caius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Commander_Caius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Commander_Caius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Commander_Caius.jpg) Commander Caius **Commander Caius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Imperial_Soldier_(class).md) stationed in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). He is in charge of the [guards](Skyrim_Whiterun_Guard.md) patrolling the hold under Jarl [Balgruuf the Greater](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md).

Caius wears a set of [Whiterun guard's armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Guard%27s_Armor) and a pair of [fur boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fur_Boots). He is equipped with a leveled [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) which can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) capture Whiterun, his position is taken by [Sinmir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sinmir). Caius will then move to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) together with Balgruuf, [Proventus Avenicci](Skyrim_Proventus_Avenicci.md) and [Irileth](Skyrim_Irileth.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits)**: Put [Maven Black-Briar's](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) competition out of business.

## Dialogue
As long as Whiterun is under control of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), he may greet you in a number of ways. He may simply introduce himself:

*"I'm the commander of the guard here in Whiterun."* *"My boys'll keep you safe. Don't you worry."* *"You're not thinking of causing trouble here in this beautiful city, now are you? 'Cause I'd strongly advise against such foolishness."* *"Yes? What is it?"* When exiled:

*"I'll always be a commander, even if those Stormcloak dogs have stripped away my power."* *"The Legion will regain control of this city. Mark my words."*

### Dampened Spirits
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-npc-Sabjorn_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sabjorn_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn_04.jpg) *"Oh come now, this is mead... not some wine to be sipped and savored."* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-npc-Sabjorn_05.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sabjorn_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn_05.jpg) *"I said move!"* **Commander Caius**: *"Well, Sabjorn. Now that you've taken care of your little pest problem, how about I get a taste of some of your mead?"*
**Sabjorn**: *"Help yourself, milord. It's my finest brew yet... I call it Honningbrew Reserve. I think you'll find it quite pleasing to your palate."*
**Commander Caius**: *"Oh come now, this is mead... not some wine to be sipped and savored."*

After pouring himself a taste of the poisoned mead, Commander Caius will start to feel bad:

**Commander Caius**: *"By the Eight?! What... what's in this?"*
**Sabjorn**: *"I... I don't know. What's wrong?"*
**Commander Caius**: *"You assured me this place was clean! I'll see... see to it that you remain in irons for the rest of your days!"*
**Sabjorn**: *"No, please! I don't understand..."*
**Commander Caius**: *"Silence, idiot! I should have known better... to trust this place after it's been riddled with filth."*
**Sabjorn**: *"I beg you... please. This is not what it seems!"*

The sickly Caius will then walk over to Mallus and say:

*"You... you're in charge here until I can sort this all out."* While Mallus accepts the responsibility, he will walk over to Sabjorn again:

**Commander Caius**: *"And you... you're coming with me to Dragonsreach. We'll see how quickly your memory clears in the city's prisons. Now move."* **Sabjorn**: *"Look, I assure you, this is all just a huge misunderstanding!"* The captain will then draw his weapon and shout:

*"I said move!"*
## Notes
- Caius' dialogue after the Stormcloaks have taken Whiterun suggests that he wasn't intended to be exiled to the Blue Palace, but rather be allowed to stay in Whiterun without his rank. - This issue has been addressed by version 1.2.4 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:USKP). Caius' `CWExile` AI package is removed, and he instead stays in [The Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare) for the remainder of the game.
