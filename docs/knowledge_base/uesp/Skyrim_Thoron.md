[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-npc-Thoron.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Thoron.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thoron.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Thoron.jpg) Thoron, the Mad Conjurer Thoron is the main antagonist of the [Saints & Seducers](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). His damage abilities are [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md) and [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md). For defensive spells he will cast [Ironflesh](Skyrim_Armor_(spells).md#Ironflesh) and [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Ward_(spells).md#Greater_Ward). For healing, he casts [Close Wounds](Skyrim_Close_Wounds.md). He also knows the spells [Command Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Command_Daedra) and [Expel Daedra](Skyrim_Expel_Daedra.md), and might use them to dispose of any Atronachs or Familiars you summon.

During battle, he summons [Golden Saints](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md) and [Dark Seducers](Skyrim_Dark_Seducer.md) at certain health thresholds, and will disengage by teleporting back to his original spot. When he has 4/5 of his health left, he summons a single Golden Saint Warrior. At 3/5 of his health, he summons a Dark Seducer Warrior and Archer. Finally, at 2/5 of his health, he summons a Golden Saint Warrior and Archer.

He always carries four spell tomes to summon a Golden Saint and Dark Seducer warriors and archers. He also carries two volumes of his three series journal. He also carries two [Rare Curios](Skyrim_Rare_Curios.md) common ingredients, and two rare ingredients. He will always carry two [Hearts of Order](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heart_of_Order). He always wears a unique [Ring of Disrobing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Disrobing), and a unique [Madness Helmet of Recovery](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Madness_Helmet_of_Recovery).
