Red Eagle's Song **Transcribed from the oral tradition by [Varana Tappo](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Varana_Tappo), [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Scribe serving the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md)** *A song about Red Eagle*
---
Remember, remember, child of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md)
[Faolan](Lore_Faolan.md)'s bloody story
Remember, remember, child of the Reach
Red Eagle's final glory

Shrieking eagles cried his name
As he rose from mother's womb
A child born with the blood-elk's eye
Whose rage spelled [diamond](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Empire_of_Cyrodiil)'s doom

Despair, despair as [Hestra](Lore_Hestra.md) came
South-born hag of [White-Stone Tower](Lore_White_Gold_Tower.md)
With bands of [iron](Lore_Iron.md) spears and shields
She turned the king-hearts black and sour

He warred and howled as seasons passed
Slaying the Empire's [daughter and son](Lore_Imperial.md)
But [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) fell by arrow and spear
Till clan-friends he had none

At last he traded his Eagle's heart
To the [ravens](Lore_Hagraven.md) who covet our power
In his breast they planted the [briar seed](Lore_Briar_Heart.md)
That grows from the [corpse's flower](Lore_Briar_Heart_Tree.md)

Thousands fell to his flaming sword
As the blood-sun rose and set
Despite the bite of a hundred arrows
He fought till death he met

Let all remember Faolan's rage
For it dwells in the heart of us all
Any who covet the sacred Reach
Will suffer our spears and fall!
