[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-npc-Horm.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Horm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Horm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Horm.jpg) Horm **Horm** is the adult [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) son of [Geimund](Skyrim_Geimund.md) and [Fridrika](Skyrim_Fridrika.md), and works with his father in the [Solitude Stables](Skyrim_Solitude_Stables.md) at [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) outside [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He will sell the stables' horses if his father dies.

He wakes at 8am to eat breakfast for an hour, spending the rest of his morning inside the house. At noon, he heads outside to work at the stables, only interrupted by a one-hour dinner at 6pm inside the house. He goes to sleep at midnight.

He wears a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b), and a [hat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hat#000209aa). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
Horm's entire life focuses on horses, this will be clear when he greets you:

*"Looking for a horse? You'll want to talk to my father, Geimund."* (If Geimund is alive) *"Need a horse? I guess I'm the one to talk to, since my da passed..."* (If Geimund is dead) *"I grew up tending some of these horses. They're almost family."* *"When I was a boy, there was a year we couldn't afford enough feed. So I snuck my dinner out to the stables, and gave it to the horse."* *"I want to check on Grani, one of our horses. Goodbye, stranger."*
## Notes
- When spoken to, his father Geimund will mention him: *"With a wife like Fridrika and a son like Horm, I'm the luckiest man in Skyrim."*
