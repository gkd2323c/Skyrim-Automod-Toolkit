## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The house is located on the edge of the water behind the [Thaumaturgist's Hut](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md), along the jetty from [Alva's house](Skyrim_Alva%27s_House.md) in Morthal. There is a covered porch along the front of the building and continuing around to the left. There are two wooden benches and a food barrel to the left of the front door. In the area to the left are two more wooden benches, a large pile of firewood and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md).

### Falion's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Falion%27s_House.jpg) Interior This is a one-room home, from where Falion also sells his services and items. On your left upon entering is a long wooden table with a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel beside it. On the table is a sample of [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md), several loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak) and four [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg). Above the table, hanging from the ceiling are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). To the right of the table is a basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), with a hanging rack above holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

In the northwestern corner is a chair and against the north wall is a set of shelves holding copies of *[The Great War](Skyrim_The_Great_War.md)*, *[Azura and the Box](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azura_and_the_Box)* and the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Catalogue of Armor Enchantments](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Catalogue_of_Armor_Enchantments)*, as well as a [Draught of Extra Magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Draught_of_Extra_Magicka), a [Potion of Extra Magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Extra_Magicka), an [Enchanter's Potion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Potion), an [Enchanter's Draught](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Enchanter%27s_Draught), six bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), two filled [petty soul gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Petty_Soul_Gem), two filled [common soul gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Common_Soul_Gem) and one empty common soul gem. The [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) become available for purchase from Falion once you have the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) [skill perk](Skyrim_Skill_Perks.md). There is an [arcane enchanter](Skyrim_Arcane_Enchanter.md) in the northeastern corner.

Against the east wall is a single owned bed coming out from the wall, with a round wooden table holding two bottles of [Black-Briar mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Mead) and a copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Rain's Hand, v4](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:2920,_Rain%27s_Hand,_v4)* to the left of the bed. In the middle of the east wall is a fireplace with a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) on the mantelpiece and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand in front. To the right is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Against the south wall is a square wooden table and two chairs. On the table are five loose [coins](Skyrim_Gold.md) and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). In the southwestern corner, along the west wall is a second single owned bed with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) at the foot of it.
