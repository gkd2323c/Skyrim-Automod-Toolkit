## Quick Walkthrough
1. Obtain a [glassfish](Skyrim_Glassfish.md) for [Zaria](Skyrim_Zaria.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md).
2. Read Zaria's [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaria%27s_Note_from_Jarl) from the Jarl.
3. Give the [Disguised Invisibility Potion](Skyrim_Disguised_Invisibility_Potion.md) to the guard in the [Falkreath Barracks](Skyrim_Falkreath_Barracks.md).
4. Return to Zaria.

## Detailed Walkthrough
This quest can be started after [In A Pinch](Skyrim_In_A_Pinch.md) by speaking to Viriya:

- **Know of anyone in need of fishing work around Skyrim?**
- *"You're headed to Falkreath. It's all in this note."*

She will then hand you a [bounty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Glassfish) for a glassfish for [Zaria](Skyrim_Zaria.md) in Falkreath. Once you obtain a glassfish, head to [Grave Concoctions](Skyrim_Grave_Concoctions.md) in Falkreath to speak to Zaria:

- **Here's the glassfish you requested in your bounty.**
- *"I'm grateful for your efforts. Here, take this."*

She will then give you 200 gold and a [note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Zaria%27s_Note_from_Jarl) from the Jarl.

- **Thanks for the coin. And... a note from the Jarl?**
- *"Maybe you can help me."*

The note from the Jarl states that a guard has been stealing skooma from the evidence chest, and that the Jarl would like for the guard to have an invisibility potion poured into an empty skooma bottle to scare the guard straight. Speak to Zaria to obtain the invisibility potion:

- **I'll deliver the disguised potion in exchange for the Jarl's ring.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you."*
- **I'll return when the deed is done. Hopefully he learns his lesson.**
- *"Good luck out there."*

She will then hand you a [Disguised Invisibility Potion](Skyrim_Disguised_Invisibility_Potion.md). You will then need to head to the [Falkreath Barracks](Skyrim_Falkreath_Barracks.md) to talk to the guard where you will have two options:

- **Seems like you don't have any trouble keeping the edge off while on duty.**
- *"I beg your pardon, sir. I don't know what you're getting at, but whatever it is, it's not appreciated."*
- **Here, take this. My way of saying thanks.**
- *"I understand. Now let's pledge never to speak of this again."*

- **Here's a bottle of skooma I found laying about. I trust you'll lock it away?**
- *"Of course."*

Either option leads to you needing to follow the guard into the jail, you can then overhear him say:

- *"Soon. I can almost taste it."*

He will then drink the potion, turn invisible and then shout:

- *"Oh, no! By the Gods! This can't be happening! Gods help us!"*

As you exit the jail, the guard will become visible again and say:

- *"I don't feel... right... I'm going to lay down... for awhile..."*

Head back to Zaria and speak to her:

- **I don't think that guard will be using skooma any longer.**
- *"Excellent. You've done us a great service. Here is your reward."*

You will then be rewarded with the [Ring of Surroundings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_Surroundings).

## Notes
- When the invisible guard reappears after a short amount of time his inventory can then be picked and the Disguised Invisibility Potion can be stolen back and kept.
- Killing the guard after he drinks the potion will still allow you to collect the reward from Viriya since the guard will not "be using skooma any longer." You will however almost certainly incur a bounty since other guards are nearby.

## Bugs
- The dialogue to speak to the guard may not appear. <sup>**?**</sup> - Attack the guard, then go to jail and serve your time. After this, speaking to the guard gives you the related dialogue.
- The note from the Jarl may remain a quest item after completion.
