[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-creature-Naaslaarum.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Naaslaarum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Naaslaarum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Naaslaarum.jpg) Naaslaarum dives underwater **Naaslaarum** is a named [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) in [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). It guards a [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) for [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md), along with [Voslaarum](Skyrim_Voslaarum.md).

Like all living dragons, Naaslaarum and Voslaarum will not spawn until after [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) is completed, to prevent early access to [shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

If it is completed, they will be lurking in the water of the frozen lake near the Portal of Resolution, right under the ice, where you can only see them by diving. When you approach them, wittingly or not, they will both burst out through the ice and attack you.

## Notes
- "Naaslaarum" literally translates to "Tooth Water Twin".

## Bugs
- Naaslaarum and Voslaarum still may not appear even if you have completed Dragon Rising.
