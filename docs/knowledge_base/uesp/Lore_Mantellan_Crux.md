[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_06.png/200px-DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_06.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_06.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_06.png) The Mantella [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_39.png/200px-DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_39.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_39.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-quest-Journey_to_Aetherius_39.png) The Mantella is released from Aetherius The Mantellan Crux was a small, self-contained plane located in [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) that was designed as a puzzle to test those who would seek the heart of [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Nulfaga Jt A-2)</sup> Its name was derived from the [Mantella](Lore_Mantella.md), a large soul gem that was once used to power the Numidium. The plane served as the resting place and prison of the Mantella after it was blasted from [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) at the dawn of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) by [Zurin Arctus](Lore_Zurin_Arctus.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMR-1)</sup>

The Mantellan Crux was guarded by the [Blind God](Lore_Blind_God.md), who took the form of a Giant Skull.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jt A-3)</sup> The Blind God was known to get quite jealous, and [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), who was a known visitor to this realm of Aetherius and had dealings with the Blind God,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jt A-3)</sup> considered him to be a pompous upstart of a God.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Skulls Jt A-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Sheo-5)</sup>

The Mantellan Crux is a realm of floating islands within a star filled void. To travel through the realm safely required levitation, which could be facilitated by the enchanted busts and lanterns found within the realm. The Great Pyramid, which was inhabited by vampires and other undead, was a notable structure in the Mantellan Crux. Also within the Mantellan Crux were the Sword, a structure that had the appearance of a giant sword, and the Crossbow, a structure shaped like a crossbow.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jt A-3)</sup>

There are legends about the realm containing a doorway that leads to Oblivion, which could explain the Daedra found within the realm. However, the existence of this doorway is unconfirmed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Do O-6)</sup>

The Mantella was rediscovered in [3E 401](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_401) by [Nulfaga](Lore_Nulfaga.md), a renowned sorceress and the Queen Mother of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). In her astral travels, Nulfaga discovered it was being held within the Mantellan Crux.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFC-7)</sup> This discovery precipitated a series of events that led to the recovery of the Mantella in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417) by an unknown [agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), culminating in the phenomenon known as the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jt A-3)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TWITW-8)</sup>
