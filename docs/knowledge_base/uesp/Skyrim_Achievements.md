# Achievements


On the Xbox 360, a total of one thousand Gamer Points are available in the base game, or 1,550 points with all add-ons installed; the "Points" column in the following table provides the Gamer Points associated with each specific achievement. On the PC, Steam achievements stand on their own and don't include points. In addition, use of [console commands](Skyrim_Console.md) and [mods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Mods) on the PC version does not prevent the awarding of achievements. On the PS3, the Trophies are sorted into types: Bronze, Silver and Gold. Earning every trophy from the base game will unlock [a Platinum trophy](#Platinum_Trophy).

The [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) has a separate achievement list for Steam, Xbox One, and PS4, meaning they can be earned twice by playing both versions of the game. Note that the use of mods (including [Verified Creations](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Verified_Creations)) disables Special Edition achievement progress for both PC and console. Since the Special Edition comes with all three major add-ons, the achievement list contains all of the below add-on-related achievements by default (i.e. 75 achievements or 76 trophies). The use of [Creation Club Creations](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) does not disable achievements as they are official content, however the Creation Club content does not add any additional achievements.

Similar to the Special Edition, [Skyrim VR](Skyrim_Skyrim_VR.md) also has a separate achievement list for Steam and PS4. The use of mods disables achievement progress on PC.

More detailed information on how to unlock each achievement can be obtained by clicking on the achievement's name.

## Missable Achievements
Most of the achievements can be obtained regardless of your previous game choices. However, a handful of achievements can be missed:

- [![SR-achievement-Oblivion Walker.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png/30px-SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Oblivion_Walker.png) **[Oblivion Walker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Walker)** (30 pts/Silver) — Collect 15 Daedric [Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md) - There are a total of sixteen available Daedric Artifacts that qualify for this achievement, five of which can be missed depending upon your choices during each associated [Daedric Quest](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md). See [Oblivion Walker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oblivion_Walker) for more details.
- [![SR-achievement-One with the Shadows.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c6/SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png/30px-SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-One_with_the_Shadows.png) **[One with the Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:One_with_the_Shadows)** (30 pts/Silver) — Returned the Thieves Guild to its former glory - Can be missed if Erikur dies before completing The Dainty Sload. After starting Bound Until Death, he is no longer essential and can be killed.
- [![SR-achievement-With Friends Like These....png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png/30px-SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-With_Friends_Like_These....png) **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md#Achievements)** (10 pts/Bronze) — Join the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md)
- [![SR-achievement-Bound Until Death.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png/30px-SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Bound_Until_Death.png) **[Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md#Achievements)** (10 pts/Bronze) — Complete Bound Until Death
- [![SR-achievement-Hail Sithis!.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis%21.png/30px-SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis%21.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Hail_Sithis!.png) **[Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md#Achievements)** (30 pts/Silver) — Complete Hail Sithis! - All three Dark Brotherhood achievements become unavailable if you choose to [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md)
- [![SR-achievement-War Hero.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-achievement-War_Hero.png/30px-SR-achievement-War_Hero.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-War_Hero.png) **[War Hero](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:War_Hero)** (10 pts/Bronze) — Capture [Fort Sungard](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md) or [Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_Fort_Greenwall.md) - The associated quest ([The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md) or [The Battle for Fort Greenwall](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Greenwall_(Imperial).md)) can be skipped depending upon the outcome of [Season Unending](Skyrim_Season_Unending.md).
- [![SR-achievement-Auriel's Bow.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png/30px-SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Auriel%27s_Bow.png) **[Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow_(achievement).md)** (20 pts/Bronze) — Use the special power of [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) - [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md) is not marked as a quest item after completing [Kindred Judgement](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kindred_Judgement), so this achievement can be missed if the bow is lost or despawns.
- [![SR-achievement-Werewolf Mastered.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png/30px-SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Werewolf_Mastered.png) **[Werewolf Mastered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Werewolf_Mastered)** (20 pts/Bronze) — Acquire 11 [werewolf perks](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md#Skill_Perks) - After curing lycanthropy twice (either using a [Glenmoril Witch Head](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Witch_Head.md) or by becoming a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md)), the player will never be able to become a werewolf or acquire werewolf perks again.
- [![SR-achievement-Stalhrim Crafter.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png/30px-SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Stalhrim_Crafter.png) **[Stalhrim Crafter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Crafter)** (20 pts/Silver) — Craft an item out of [Stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) - The associated quest can be missed if [Deor Woodcutter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deor_Woodcutter) or [Fanari Strong-Voice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fanari_Strong-Voice) dies before it starts.

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) [![On Play Station](https://images.uesp.net/6/64/Playstation.svg)](Skyrim_PlayStation.md) Achievements can occasionally be missed by Steam and PS4, even though they have been completed in-game. - To fix this, reload an earlier save and re-complete the achievement.
- Making sure that the "Enable the Steam Overlay while in-game" is enabled (from the Steam client's properties) before starting the game prevents this issue.
