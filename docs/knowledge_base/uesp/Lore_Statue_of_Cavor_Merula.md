Statue of Cavor Merula *A plaque honoring an Order of the Hour knight*
---
[Cavor Merula](Lore_Cavor_Merula.md), warrior-priest of [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), revived the [Order of the Hour](Lore_Order_of_the_Hour.md) in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) in [2E 432](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_432) in response to the coming of the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md). His devotion and guardianship remains the cornerstone of the Order's mission.
