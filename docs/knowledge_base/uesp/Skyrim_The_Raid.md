## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md) after completing the [Helping Hand](Skyrim_Helping_Hand.md) quest.
2. Report to the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) about the presence of a [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md) dealer in town.
3. "Deal" with [Sarthis Idren](Skyrim_Sarthis_Idren.md), and find evidence to locate his sources.
4. Return to the jarl with the evidence.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Illegal Trade
After completing the [Helping Hand](Skyrim_Helping_Hand.md) quest, you can ask [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md) where she obtained her [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md). She is not forthcoming with the details, however you can pass an easy-level [Speech check](Skyrim_Speech.md#Persuasion_Options) to persuade her to tell you the truth.

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| I think you owe me one. **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: Okay, okay, I'll tell you. <br> **Failed**: It's not worth dying over. | **Passed**: Reveal the supplier <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| Here, does this help? **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: Okay, okay, I'll tell you. <br> **Failed**: It's got nothing to do with the coin... this is my life we're talking about. | **Passed**: Reveal the supplier <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| And who says I won't? **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: Okay, okay, I'll tell you. <br> **Failed**: You can't threaten me any worse than they have! | **Passed**: Reveal the supplier <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| Okay, never mind. | Thank you. | Nothing |

At last, she will reveal her supplier as [Sarthis Idren](Skyrim_Sarthis_Idren.md) and his base as [Riften Warehouse](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse.md). She explains that the warehouse is always locked and that only the jarl has a key.

The next step is to report the presence of a skooma dealer to the jarl. The current jarl in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) will acknowledge Sarthis and his illegal operations. However, the dealer has informants in the town guard because he disappears every time they raid the warehouse. Perhaps, an outsider may be able to surprise Sarthis in the warehouse, and *"'convince' him to close down his operation"*. If you accept the mission, you will be given the [Riften Warehouse Key](Skyrim_Riften_Warehouse_Key.md) to unlock the warehouse.

### The Raid
The warehouse is located at the southern end of Riften's docks. [Orini Dral](Skyrim_Orini_Dral.md) is patrolling the warehouse, and will immediately spot you. Sarthis is in the basement and will come rushing upstairs to aid his bodyguard. Orini is a modest opponent, but Sarthis can prove a difficult opponent to defeat.

When they are dead, loot Sarthis' body for [his key](Skyrim_Sarthis%27s_Key.md). Descend the stairs in the northeastern corner, turn around and use the key to unlock the door. On the shelves there are five bowls of [moon sugar](Skyrim_Moon_Sugar.md) and six bottles of skooma. On the table there is another bowl of moon sugar and two bottles of skooma. Inside Sarthis' satchel there is a [note](Skyrim_Shipment%27s_Ready.md) with information regarding Sarthis' sources. Make sure to read the note to advance the quest.

### It's Not Over Yet
Return to the jarl with the evidence and news of Sarthis' demise. The jarl will be grateful, saying, *"It's nice to receive good news for a change"*, but will ask you to continue the crusade, which starts the [next quest](Skyrim_Supply_and_Demand.md).

## Notes
- If Wujeeta dies before you report the skooma dealer to the jarl, you will fail the quest, making it impossible to become thane of the Rift regardless of who the jarl is.
- If Sarthis is killed by anyone other than you, you will fail the quest. The key and the note can still be obtained, but no relevant dialogue option will be available from the jarl to proceed.
- If you manage to sneak past or Calm Orini, you can spare his life. Only Sarthis has to die for the quest to progress.
- This is the second of three required quests that are required to unlock the option to become [Thane of the Rift](Skyrim_I_Done_Got_Thaned!.md). There are additional requirements noted on that quest page.
- The penultimate objective, "Recover skooma source information", will only register as completed if you read the note, unlike most other objectives of this type which only require you to take a note.
- Despite [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) telling you to deal with Sarthis 'discreetly', stealth is not required to succeed in the quest.

## Bugs
- Even though you were given a key by the jarl, and it is occupied by enemies, you will be considered to be trespassing in the warehouse. If you have a follower they will react as such. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- Due to an engine limitation (see [this discussion](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19602)), the player will still incur an increment in their "Trespasses" stat after reading the note.
- There is a way to enter without it counting as trespassing. When you open the door, before you enter, immediately open the menu and make a fast travel. You will see that you will still enter the warehouse. Then load the game that was saved by making the fast travel. You will be in front of the door but now it will be open. Turn into a werewolf and access the warehouse. It will no longer count as trespassing.
- If your follower insists on reporting you for stealing the note, you will need to exit the warehouse to ask them to wait.
- The quest marker may be stuck at "Recover skooma source information" and point to the apothecary's satchel, even after you have killed Sarthis yourself and taken the Shipment's Ready note.
