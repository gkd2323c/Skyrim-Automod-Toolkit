*"For my transgression, I was to be whipped by Glathis himself. In the courtyard, Glathis struck his first lash. I grabbed his whip and strangled him with it. I'll never forget the look he gave me as the light drained from his eyes.*"
—Heita-Meen<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup> Vicecanon Heita-Meen was an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) vicecanon in [Stormhold](Lore_Stormhold.md) during the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). She was largely responsible for the Argonians' entry into the Pact and for the abolition of the enslavement of her people within it. Heita-Meen was also an author. She penned *[From Argonian to Saxhleel](Lore_From_Argonian_to_Saxhleel.md)*,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup> and *[Wisdom of the Tides](Lore_Wisdom_of_the_Tides.md)*.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WOTT-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/ON-npc-Walks-in-Ash.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Walks-in-Ash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Walks-in-Ash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Walks-in-Ash.jpg) Walks-in-Ash [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/ON-npc-Keshu_the_Black_Fin.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Keshu_the_Black_Fin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Keshu_the_Black_Fin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Keshu_the_Black_Fin.jpg) Keshu the Black Fin, another Argonian leader associated with the formation of the Ebonheart Pact Heita-Meen was born during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and raised as a house-slave, receiving little education.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HMAYQ-3)</sup> Her master was Councilman Glathis Dres of [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md), known for his cruelty even by Dres standards. She worked on a [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md) plantation in [Thorn](Lore_Thorn.md). Circa [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572), after being beaten unconscious for seating the guests at a banquet out of order, she planned an escape with her fellow slaves. They fled into the [Thornmarsh](Lore_Thornmarsh.md), and stumbled into a troop of treacherous Argonians from the [Archein](Lore_Archein.md) tribe, who were employed by the Dres. It was while she was held captive in the Archein village that Heita-Meen received a vision from the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md), who wanted her to defend the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) and [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) in battle.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup>

The Archeins escorted them back to Thorn, which was nearly empty of Dunmer following [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md)'s call to arms to defend [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) against the [Second Akaviri Invasion](Lore_Second_Akaviri_Invasion.md). Seeing this, Heita-Meen strangled Glathis with his own whip, and swiftly challenged the centurion of the Archein guards for her position by right-of-combat. After killing the centurion and gaining command of the Archeins, Heita-Meen marched on Stormhold, intending to do the same there.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup> Instead, the troop encountered Walks-in-Ash, who was able to convince Stormhold's Shellbacks to join without bloodshed. The army then marched on [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) in Morrowind to fight the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MTCWIA-4)</sup> They arrived at [the final battle](Lore_Battle_of_Vivec%27s_Antlers.md) near [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) and helped to send the invaders fleeing.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup>

The Argonians' participation in the battle helped contribute to the signing of the Ebonheart Pact between Eastern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), Morrowind, and [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Heita-Meen was released from slavery and appointed as the Argonian vicecanon of Stormhold, where she shared power with the Nord Vicecanon Hrondar and the Dunmer Vicecanon Servyna. Guided by the Hist, Heita-Meen's actions had allowed the Argonians to preserve their culture and reclaim their lands under the Pact.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FAt S-1)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a3/ON-quest-The_Dream_of_the_Hist.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Dream_of_the_Hist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Dream_of_the_Hist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Dream_of_the_Hist.jpg) Heita-Meen and the Mnemic Egg At some point between [2E 578](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_578) and [2E 581](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_581) she met with [Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md) and given him a guide to travel throughout Shadowfen.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIGTTBM-5)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Vicecanon Hrondar was assassinated by the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), who replaced him with a skin-stealer in preparation for a full invasion of [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md). The Dominion then began murdering Stormhold citizens for use by the skin-stealers, targeting only Dunmer in order to shift the blame onto disgruntled former slaves or [Shadowscale](Lore_Shadowscale.md) assassins.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VHM-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTS-7)</sup> Eventually the Dominion's actions came to light, and Heita-Meen journeyed with the other vicecanons to the ruins of [Ten-Maur-Wolk](Lore_Ten-Maur-Wolk.md), where they became trapped by Dominion forces. The Pact successfully defeated the invaders at the Battle of Ten-Maur-Wolk, and Hrondar was revealed to be an imposter. Along with Servyna, Heita-Meen traveled throughout Shadowfen in an attempt to disrupt the invasion's sinister intentions: to destroy the Argonians' connection to the Hist, resulting in the theoretical extinction of the race.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VHM-6)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GTTT-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TTSS-9)</sup>

Along with the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Heita-Meen entered the Dominion-occupied ruins of Loriasel, which housed the [Mnemic Egg](Lore_Mnemic_Egg.md). After defeating the invasion's mastermind, an [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) alchemist named Ruuvitar, Heita-Meen approached the Egg and became trapped in a vision of the Hist, whose collective consciousness had been partially corrupted by the Dominion. The Vestige entered into the same vision and saved the Hist, rescuing Heita-Meen from her trance.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VHM-6)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TDA-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TDOTH-11)</sup>

Following the Dominion's defeat, she transported the Mnemic Egg to [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) for safe storage, and attended the [Konunleikar](Lore_Konunleikar.md) festival thrown in High King [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md)'s honor.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VHM-6)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-STS-12)</sup> She also partook in the battle against the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) in [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VHM-6)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SS-13)</sup> The Egg was later pledged to the Pact at a [Great Moot](Lore_Great_Moot.md) at [Skuldafn](Lore_Skuldafn.md), as a representation of the Argonian people's commitment to the alliance.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-SOS-14)</sup>

At some point in 2E 582, she responded to a series of questions via correspondence, addressing Argonian religious traditions related to the Hist, their cultural understanding of the passage of time, and some other related topics.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-HMAYQ-3)</sup> She was known for her proficiency in combat with [staves](Lore_Staff.md), and she appears in some paintings depicting the Ebonheart Pact.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIGTTBM-5)</sup>
