[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/ON-place-Quagmire.jpg/200px-ON-place-Quagmire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Quagmire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Quagmire.jpg) Quagmire Quagmire, or the **Dreamstride**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is a realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), created and ruled over by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Nightmare, [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md). It is a realm of horrors, where reality shifts upon itself in seemingly impossible ways. Every few minutes, lightning flashes and the realm morphs into a terrifying scene, each one more frightening than the last.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDOO-2)</sup> Mortal sleepers often slip into this realm without any help at all.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

From her citadel in the center of the realm, Vaermina reaches out into [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) and collects the memories of sleeping mortals, leaving nothing but visions of horror and despair.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WN-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Erandur-5)</sup> These experiences, known as nightmares, involve the mind of a mortal traveling to Quagmire. Through magical teleportation, it is possible for a mortal to enter Quagmire while awake, as was done by the mage [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TDOO-2)</sup> It is inhabited by [Omens](Lore_Omen.md), daedric servants of Vaermina, who sends them to haunt the inhabitants of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TBOD-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TOD-7)</sup> Wasps inhabiting the realm weave their nests from nightmares.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DW-8)</sup>

## History
A cult of wealthy Daedra worshippers were once rewarded by Vaermina with a small pocket of Quagmire. Using a mining operation at Hozzin's Folly on [Bleakrock Isle](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md) as cover, the family successfully created a portal to the realm and consecrated an "Unspeakable Sigil" within, seemingly to maintain the pocket. Work began on constructing a temple to Vaermina within the realm, but the pocket remained much smaller than the worshippers had expected and [scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) began to appear both inside the realm and within the mine on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> After a disastrous ritual, Hozzin's Folly was left abandoned, and the pocket realm was destroyed in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when the portal was reopened and the sigil smashed.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOHF-10)</sup>

Quagmire and Mundus have been known to partially merge where Vaermina's influence is strong, usually in the area surrounding one of [her artifacts](Lore_Vaermina.md#Artifacts). The island of [Skald's Retreat](Lore_Skald%27s_Retreat.md) briefly merged with the realm in 2E 582, sending its inhabitants into a deep sleep.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESORIFT-11)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the [Orb of Vaermina](Lore_Orb_of_Vaermina.md) was stolen by the wizard Arkved, who brought it to his tower in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md). The interior of the tower seemed to enter Quagmire, with reality becoming warped inside.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-OBVaermina-12)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the town of [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md) was plagued by continuous nightmares due to the close proximity of the [Skull of Corruption](Lore_Skull_of_Corruption.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WN-4)</sup>
