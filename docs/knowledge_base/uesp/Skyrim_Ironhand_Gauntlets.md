[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-item-Ironhand_Gauntlets.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ironhand_Gauntlets.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ironhand_Gauntlets.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ironhand_Gauntlets.jpg) Ironhand Gauntlets The Ironhand Gauntlets are the gauntlets used by the [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit.md) [Hajvarr Iron-Hand](Skyrim_Hajvarr_Iron-Hand.md), the leader of the bandits who operate out of [White River Watch](Skyrim_White_River_Watch.md). The gauntlets themselves are actually [steel Nordic gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Nordic_Gauntlets), despite their name. They have been enchanted to [fortify two-handed](Skyrim_Fortify_Two-Handed.md) attacks by fifteen points. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Ironhand Gauntlets requires an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the benefits of the alterations doubling if the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk has also been unlocked.
