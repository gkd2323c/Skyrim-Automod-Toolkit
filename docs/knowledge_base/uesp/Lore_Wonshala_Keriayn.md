*I have a piece of the voidguide. I will trade it for a weapon called SCOURGE. It's here on Battlespire, and its functions are protected by a magical warding keyword, but that's all I know. Deliver me SCOURGE, and provide me with the keyword, and you shall have your voidpiece.*
—Wonshala Keriayn to the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WKBSD-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Wonshala Keriayn (also known as **Vonshala**) is a feared [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md)<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> who has often filled high-ranking positions to the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) she has served, notably [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) and later [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), joined by her two comrades, [Methats](Lore_Methats.md) and [Sumeer Jabran](Lore_Sumeer_Jabran.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSDSW-3)</sup> She solidified her role in Oblivion as the commander of [Spider Daedra](Lore_Spider_Daedra.md). Perthan Vorn and Perthan Serni are both known to serve her.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-5)</sup><sup>:102</sup> She has been known to take the form of a beautiful woman, wearing a gown made of flowing water.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

## Biography
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), Keriayn was the brawler of the trio known as the Dremora Travelers, and were considered the most fearless of their kind in [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s service<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup> and served as generals to his invading forces.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Anchor G-7)</sup> They were tasked with protecting Dark Anchors but were slain by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md), and the Anchor was destroyed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup> A [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md) staff which they looted during their conquests was recovered from the Dark Anchor's ruins after their defeat, though it later found its way to a Frost Atronach named [Nomeg Rine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Nomeg_Rine) who terrorized [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

By the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), Wonshala and her comrades had changed their allegiance to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and she became the commander of his Spider Daedra. During the invasion of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) in [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398), she sought the artifact [Scourge](Lore_Scourge.md) and was willing to trade with the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) for it. The Apprentice gave her Scourge in exchange for a piece of the voidguide, but when she said the keyword 'Equality' and attempted to strike down the hero, the weapon banished her to the Outer Darkness of Oblivion's voidstreams.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WKBSD-1)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-5)</sup><sup>:113</sup>
