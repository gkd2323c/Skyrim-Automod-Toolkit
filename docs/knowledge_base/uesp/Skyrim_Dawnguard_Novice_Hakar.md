[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/SR-npc-Dawnguard_Novice_Hakar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dawnguard_Novice_Hakar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Novice_Hakar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dawnguard_Novice_Hakar.jpg) Dawnguard Novice Hakar **Dawnguard Novice Hakar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and a novice [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunter who appears as a [random encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Dawnguard_Novice_Hakar) after completing either [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) for the [Volkihar vampires](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) or [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) for the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

Hakar wears [Dawnguard heavy armor](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Armor.md), [Dawnguard heavy boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Heavy_Boots.md), and an [amulet of Stendarr](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Stendarr.md), and he carries a simple [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). The equipment he drops on the ground includes more Dawnguard heavy armor, as well as a [key](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Cache_Key.md) to Dawnguard caches and his [orders](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Orders_-_Hakar.md). The orders state that his repeated failure as a member of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) have demoted him to a courier and his final assignment is to refill the Dawnguard caches scattered around [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

Upon meeting him, two vampires will be attacking and he will be in distress, it will be up to you to save him, as he pleas to you during combat. If he survives the encounter, he will run up to you and say that he cannot handle the stresses of vampire hunting. He will drop all of his equipment on the ground before he runs away. He will run to [Frostfruit Inn](Skyrim_Frostfruit_Inn.md) in [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) if he isn't killed by some random encounter along the way. Dawnguard Novice Hakar can most commonly be found directly West of White River Watch, on the road to Valtheim Towers.

## Dialogue
When first encountered:

- *"Gods, Vampires! I... I can't take this anymore! I'm not cut out for this!"*

During combat he will yell out:

- *"Help me!"*

If he survives he will say:

- *"Look, take whatever you want. I'm getting out of here!"*

## Notes
- Hakar does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Lynoit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynoit), [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md), and many other generic NPCs.
