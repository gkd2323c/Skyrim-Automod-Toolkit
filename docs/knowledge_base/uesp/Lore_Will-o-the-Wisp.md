[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/OBR-creature-Will-o-the-Wisp_02.jpg/200px-OBR-creature-Will-o-the-Wisp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-creature-Will-o-the-Wisp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-creature-Will-o-the-Wisp_02.jpg) A will-o-the-wisp in a petrified forest (Oblivion Remastered) **Will-o-the-Wisps** (or **Will o' the Wisps**), also called **true [wisps](Lore_Wisp.md)**, are luminous beings seemingly composed of gas and spiritual energy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Twenty-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WM-2)</sup> They are lone predators found in swampy areas of southern [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WM-2)</sup> such as [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) and the [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Twenty-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OB-3)</sup> as well as the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DRD-4)</sup> They can only be harmed by [silver](Lore_Silver.md), [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md), or [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md). They are immune to normal damage and poison, and have a variety of extremely dangerous [magic](Lore_Magic.md). They are also very fast and have the ability to become nearly invisible.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OB-3)</sup>

Confusion surrounding their true nature leads many to assume that will-o-the-wisps are simply swamp gas.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Natural curiosity about the strange lights produced by will-o-the-wisps can sometimes lure townsfolk into the swamp; once weakened by the will-o-the-wisp's magic, these victims become easier prey to other predators.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> The [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) view them as malevolent, but even the youngest of elves know how to avoid them.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DRD-4)</sup>

Some theorize that the wisps that normally surround [wispmothers](Lore_Wispmother.md) are a subspecies of the will-o-the-wisp, though their behavior is very different.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WM-2)</sup>

**Shines** are a harmless cousin of the will-o-the-wisp that are known to loom around the Imperial ruins of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

## Uses
Like wisps and wispmothers, the remains of will-o-the-wisps are a plentiful source of [Glow Dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Glow_Dust), a valuable [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) ingredient.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OB-3)</sup> Supplicants seeking to summon [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) sometimes leave an offering of Glow Dust at her shrine at dawn or dusk.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
