[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit_%28item%29.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit_%28item%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit_(item).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit_(item).jpg) Sheogorath Outfit [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg/200px-SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Sheogorath_Outfit.jpg) Sheogorath's attire, while worn The Sheogorath Outfit is the clothing worn by the eponymous [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Madness, [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), when he is encountered during the quest [The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md). Alongside this colorful garb, Sheogorath's clothing also includes his unique [boots](Skyrim_Sheogorath_Boots.md). As it is not possible to injure Sheogorath under normal circumstances, the outfit cannot be acquired under regular game conditions.

## Notes
- The outfit has a strikingly different appearance while worn, compared to the item's model in the inventory. As the clothes cannot normally be acquired, this would not have been a concern, as the item model would normally not ever be seen.
