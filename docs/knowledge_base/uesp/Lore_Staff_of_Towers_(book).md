Staff of Towers **by [Holnevn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Holnevn)** *Alessian writings on a powerful staff and its relationship to the mythical Towers* *
---
From the writings of Holnevn—blessed apostle of St [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md)
Transcribed by Relicmaster Glenadir

Cursed be the hands of [Elf-Wizard](Lore_Mer.md) [Anumaril](Lore_Anumaril.md)! Cursed be the pride of [Anui-El](Lore_Auri-El.md)'s get! Lo, did their lithe and wicked fingers weave [the world's undoing](Lore_Dragon_Break.md). While [Umaril](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) and his [kin](Lore_Ayleid.md) suffer a [Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md)'s death, [this](Lore_Staff_of_Towers.md) [staff](Lore_Staff.md) —this sin in eight pieces—remains.

[Adamantine](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md): primordial and solemn.

[Red](Lore_Red_Mountain.md): brooding and blood-soaked.

[Crystal](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md): profane and inscrutable.

[Orichalc](Lore_The_Towers.md#Orichalc): silent and forgotten.

[Snow-Throat](Lore_Throat_of_the_World.md): cold and forbidding.

[Green-Sap](Lore_The_Towers.md#Green-Sap): vibrant and wise.

[Brass](Lore_Numidium.md): striding and powerful.

[White-Gold](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md): boundless and eternal.

All straight, yet twisted! Bound in metal, stone, and blasphemous oaths! May these eight effigies never find a hand to wield them. May this Staff of [Towers](Lore_The_Towers.md) remain silent, inert, and beyond the cruel mage's sight. Now and for all time.
