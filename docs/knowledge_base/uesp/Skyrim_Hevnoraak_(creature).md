[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/SR-creature-Hevnoraak.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Hevnoraak.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Hevnoraak.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Hevnoraak.jpg) Hevnoraak **Hevnoraak** is one of eight named [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) found in Skyrim. He is found in [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md), and is the subject of the quest [Evil in Waiting](Skyrim_Evil_in_Waiting.md). [His eponymous mask](Skyrim_Hevnoraak_(item).md) can only be obtained when he is dead. This mask grants immunity to all types of poisons and diseases, except for those which ignore poison and disease resistance. He carries a [unique staff](Skyrim_Hevnoraak%27s_Staff.md) that creates a [wall of lightning](Skyrim_Wall_of_Storms.md).

Hevnoraak knows the following spells: [Chain Lightning](Skyrim_Chain_Lightning.md), [Command Daedra](Skyrim_Command_Daedra.md), [Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md), [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), [Ebonyflesh](Skyrim_Ebonyflesh.md), [Lightning Cloak](Skyrim_Lightning_Cloak.md), and [Thunderbolt](Skyrim_Thunderbolt.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Evil in Waiting](Skyrim_Evil_in_Waiting.md)**: Seal away an ancient evil.

## Notes
- Hevno means "brutal" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).
- Although he can cast both Chain Lightning and Thunderbolt, he may only rely on his unique staff for ranged attacks.

## Bugs
- Hevnoraak has a faulty hitbox when coming out of his coffin: arrows and ranged magic that is targeted at his lower half often phases through as if his legs didn't exist, but ranged attacks at his torso and head still land hits as expected. However, the reverse might also be possible: ranged attacks might go through his torso but hit his legs. Area-of-effect magic (like Fireball and various Shouts) and melee attacks don't seem to be affected by this bug. <sup>**?**</sup>
