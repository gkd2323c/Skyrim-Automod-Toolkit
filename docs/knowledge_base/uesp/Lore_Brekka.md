*Animal husbandry experts from Hammerfell and Wrothgar came together in a unique Covenant collaboration to create a good-natured pack animal with a hearty constitution and a pleasing aesthetic. The soft-coated, lovable brekka is the result.*
—A description of Brekkas<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RB-1)</sup> Brekkas are strange soft-coated feathery pack animals that are vaguely [camelid](Lore_Camel.md) in appearance.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Camel-2)</sup> They were created by [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) animal husbandry experts from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) as part of a unique [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) collaboration to create a good-natured pack animal with a hearty constitution and a pleasing aesthetic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RB-1)</sup> Brekkas are known to be hardy creatures, with different breeds being better adapted to certain harsh conditions.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EFB-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CWB-4)</sup> They congregate in matriarchal herds and their calves are known for being affectionate.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EFB-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESBC-5)</sup>

The Russet Brekka is a type of Brekka with orange feathers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RB-1)</sup> The Evenfall Brekka or Evenshade Brekka is a darkly colored breed of brekka well-adapted to harsh climates.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-EFB-3)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESBC-5)</sup> Chalk-White Brekkas are bred to handle high heat and arid environments with ease. Their pale coloring keeps them cool, and wasteland travelers say they need far less water than their pigmented counterparts.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CWB-4)</sup> Their calves are noted for their hardiness and bravery, as they will venture away from the herd to confront everything from [ash hoppers](Lore_Ash_Hopper.md) to [desert lions](Lore_Lion.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CWBC-6)</sup>
