Manufactory Repair Parts *A list of supplies needed for repairs*
---
The Manufactory assembly lines are out of order. It seems if I press one of the side buttons on the main console, I can summon a dwarven sphere overseer. I'll need to give it the following in order for it to be able to repair the system:

3 large cogs
3 actuators
1 units of oil
