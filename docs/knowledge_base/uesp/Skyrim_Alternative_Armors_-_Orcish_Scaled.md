## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of light Orcish armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Gambler's Edge" starts by reading Guard's Dossier: Antonius at the Mistveil Keep Barracks in Riften.)*
