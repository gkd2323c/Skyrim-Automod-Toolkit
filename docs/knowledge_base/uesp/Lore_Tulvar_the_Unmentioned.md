Tulvar the Unmentioned was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) Harbinger of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Tulvar was mentioned briefly by [Swyk the Long-Sighted](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Swyk_the_Long-Sighted) in his record of the most notable Harbingers, but was not considered notable enough for a separate entry. Tulvar granted the title of the honorary Companion to [Cirroc the Lofty](Lore_Cirroc_the_Lofty.md), who later became the first Harbinger of other heritage than [Atmoran](Lore_Atmora.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) Jorrvaskr, the mead hall of the Companions. Little is known of Tulvar's history or leadership, except for the fact that they granted the stature of an honorary Companion to Cirroc the Lofty after he saved Tulvar's life. Soon after, the [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md), who first arrived at Jorrvaskr as a servant, became the most capable of Shield-Brothers in the hall and eventually succeeded Tulvar as the Harbinger.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup>
