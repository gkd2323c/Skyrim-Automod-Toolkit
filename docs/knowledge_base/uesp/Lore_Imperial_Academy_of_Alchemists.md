# Imperial Academy of Alchemists

The Imperial Academy of Alchemists was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution devoted to the research of [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) active during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Little is known of the academy, except that they were addressed by a known scholar [Herminius Sophus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Herminius_Sophus) in his text devoted to the nature of [Tel Var Stones](Lore_Tel_Var_Stones.md), *[On the Tel Var Stones](Lore_On_the_Tel_Var_Stones.md)* in which he shares his recipes and findings with the academy.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OTS-1)</sup>
