[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/LO-map-High_Rock_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-High_Rock_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](File_LO-map-High_Rock_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg.md) [](File_LO-map-High_Rock_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg.md) Map of High Rock Greater Bretony (also known as **Greater [Betony](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Betony)**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-1)</sup> is a region found within [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). It is considered one of the major lands (along with the [Dellese Isles](Lore_Dellese_Isles.md), the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) [tribes](Lore_River_Horse_Bretons.md), and the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md)) that the clans of the province inhabit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1H-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POT-3)</sup> Around [1E 358](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_358), the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md) was approaching its final years, and in one of its last campaigns attacked the [Western Reach](Lore_Western_Reach.md) with the aid of Emperor [Ami-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ami-El) of Cyrodiil. The campaign failed and Skyrim lost more and more of Greater Bretony to the Direnni.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
