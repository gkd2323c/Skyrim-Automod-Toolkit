[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) The only known map showing the location of Pankor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/RG-load-Catacombs.jpg/200px-RG-load-Catacombs.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-load-Catacombs.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-load-Catacombs.jpg) Closer view of the island (left) Pankor is a small island marking the westernmost extent of the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), far to the southwest of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)'s coast and northwest of [the Systres](Lore_Systres.md). A very small unnamed group of islands lies to the southwest. A bay on Pankor's east coast nearly splits the island into a northern and southern half.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
