# Spiderkith

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-npc-Spiderkith_Archer.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Spiderkith_Archer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Spiderkith_Archer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Spiderkith_Archer.jpg) A Spiderkith (ESO) *Kissed by spider, Spiderkith, Daedric legend, tale, and myth!*
—Folktale rhyme<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-skin-1)</sup> Spiderkith are intelligent humanoid [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that serve [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-dadiachesis-3)</sup> They have webbed skin that appears almost desiccated. Their veins glow dull red beneath their skin.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESO-4)</sup> Their skin can range in color from navy<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESONavy-5)</sup> to red.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-skin-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESORed-6)</sup> Red-skinned spiderkith have glowing red eyes,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESORed-6)</sup> while darker spiderkith have dull, empty grey eyes.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESONavy-5)</sup> Few known spiderkith have skin that is black like charcoal.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Some spiderkith have hair white and grey in color, but many do not.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

They are capable of speech,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> and wield [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) and magic in combat as many other humanoid daedra do.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOAbility-9)</sup> The spiderkith can be seen commanding Daedra of lesser intelligence in the [Spiral Skein](Lore_Spiral_Skein.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESOZikal-10)</sup> They can weave webs, and are known to raise [spiders](Lore_Spider.md) or [skein spiders](Lore_Skein_Spider.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> They treat their arachnids as a mortal might treat pets, livestock or children, and oft refer to their spider charges as their brood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> They are known to feed on mortal flesh.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> Apart from raising spiders, capable spiderkith mages can also summon them from the Spiral Skein.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ESONurse-11)</sup> All spiderkith can project poison at their prey with a technique known as the Spiderkith Kiss.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOKiss-12)</sup>

The spiderkith are cruel and observant. Unfortunate mortals may find themselves suffering a slow, painful death within a spiderkith's web.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup> Their sense of smell and touch is often more than enough to sense a mortal's presence. They can rely upon keen senses to detect the slightest movement, the smell of sweat and blood, or the feeling of one's body heat to know that prey is nearby.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOAmbient-2)</sup>
