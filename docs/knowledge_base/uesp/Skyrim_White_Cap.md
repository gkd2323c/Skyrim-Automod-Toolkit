## Ingredients
21 guaranteed samples can be found in 14 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 4 in [Dustman's Crypt](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Crypt.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Arcadia's Cauldron](Skyrim_Arcadia%27s_Cauldron.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 2 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 2 in [Tolvald's Gap](Skyrim_Tolvald%27s_Gap.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
