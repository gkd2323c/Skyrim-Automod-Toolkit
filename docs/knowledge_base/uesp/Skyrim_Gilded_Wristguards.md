[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-item-Gilded_Wristguards.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gilded_Wristguards.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gilded_Wristguards.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gilded_Wristguards.jpg) Gilded Wristguards Gilded Wristguards are unique [gauntlets](Skyrim_Gauntlets.md) located inside *[The Katariah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Katariah)*, inside the Emperor's Quarters. As they are located upon *The Katariah*, they cannot be acquired until the related quest [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md) has been started. Obviously, this means that they become unobtainable if you choose to do the quest [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) instead. Being regal attire, these golden wristguards are detailed with looping gold patterns.

## Related Quests
- **[Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md)**: One of the biggest [contracts](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md) in [Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) history is yours to fulfill.

## Notes
- Although the only copy of this item is in the possession of [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md), the editor ID for them is "Clothes **Jarl** Gloves". This is because the gilded wristguards were originally part of the Jarls' outfits and were meant to be combined with variants of **Clothes Jarl** (e.g. the outfit that [Jarl Balgruuf](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) and many others wear.) This can also be seen in concept art for the Jarls' outfits.

## Bugs
- The wristguards cannot be tempered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. They can now be tempered with leather.
- In a female player character's inventory, they appear as [Nightingale Gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Gloves). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, fixes this bug.
- The wristguards contort very badly when sitting down while wearing them. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.4, fixes this bug.
