Sinmur, Giant of Legend *A gravestone marking the remains of an ancient Giant chieftain*

The bones of [Sinmur](Lore_Sinmur.md) the Terrible are entombed here forever. The heroes Fireheart and Darkhammer sacrificed all to end Sinmur's reign of savagery. The [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) will forever remember their names.
