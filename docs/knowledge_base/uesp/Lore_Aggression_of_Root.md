# Aggression of Root

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/ON-render-Corruption_of_Root.jpg/200px-ON-render-Corruption_of_Root.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Corruption_of_Root.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Corruption_of_Root.jpg) Corruption of Root, appearing as an Aggression of Root [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/ON-creature-Aggression_of_Root.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Aggression_of_Root.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Aggression_of_Root.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Aggression_of_Root.jpg) Aggression of Root Aggressions of Root are uncommon [nature spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md). According to the [Druids](Lore_Druid.md), they speak with the voice of [plants](Lore_Flora.md) and protect nature where it feels threatened. They are known for attracting a wide range of dangerous wildlife to any area they inhabit, creating a hazardous environment for nearby residents and travelers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup> They can command other nature spirits in battle such as [Forest Wraiths](Lore_Forest_Wraith.md) and [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md), as well as vegetation such as [Stranglers](Lore_Strangler.md) and creatures such as [Fauns](Lore_Faun.md). They are capable of speech and have a discordant tone.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oakenclaw-2)</sup>

One such aggression of root, known as Oakenclaw, took residence in Lake Olo in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md) Incursions. An adventurer was hired by the local [Legion of the West Weald](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) to deal with the danger it posed and to warn the locals of the threat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LA-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oakenclaw-2)</sup> The situation was serious enough that the West Weald Legion issued a warning bulletin stating that Lake Olo and the nearby vicinity are off-limits to unauthorized personnel.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-WB-3)</sup> Another aggression of root took residence in Haldain Lumber Camp during that period.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AOR-4)</sup>

A powerful nature spirit from the Earthen Root Enclave, known as the [Spirit of Root](Lore_Spirit_of_Root.md), was known to take the form of an aggression of root when it was corrupted by the [Firesong Circle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Firesong_Circle). When it appeared in this form, it was referred to as the Corruption of Root.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-COR-5)</sup>
