[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f7/SR-npc-Lisette.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lisette.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lisette.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lisette.jpg) Lisette plays the Lute [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Lisette_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lisette_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lisette_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lisette_02.jpg) *"That's definitely going in my part of the Edda."* **Lisette** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard_(class).md) who lives and [performs](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) at [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), spending most of her day there.

When you first enter Solitude, Lisette will be one of the townsfolk present at [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md)'s execution. Afterwards, Lisette spends her entire life at the Winking Skeever, singing, playing, and taking requests around the clock. She occasionally sits down for a bite to eat and during the night, she will prefer to sit in the upstairs area and relax. After you have completed the quest [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), this routine changes; she starts leaving the inn at 9am and spends three hours in the courtyard outside the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md), talking to [Una](Skyrim_Una.md) and [Vivienne Onis](Skyrim_Vivienne_Onis.md). At noon she returns to her duties at the Winking Skeever.

She wears [scaled boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md), and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When you first enter Solitude, Lisette will be present when Roggvir is about to be executed. If you speak to her, she will reject you with:

*"Sorry, I need to pay attention so I can remember this."* After the beheading, Lisette will comment:

*"That's definitely going in my part of the Edda."* Her normal greetings:

*"Feel free to make requests."* *"There's nothing like a good song to lift the spirit in troubled times."* *"Have a drink, rest your feet and stay a little while."* *"Uh oh. A fellow bard. I best not miss my notes or forget any lyrics."* **So you're a member of the Bards College?** *"Only in that I'm a graduate. I work at here at the Winking Skeever. I would highly recommend it though. Talk to [Viarmo](Skyrim_Viarmo.md) if you're interested."* **Does being a bard pay well?** *"Oh, no. But being surrounded by good music, good drink, and good friends is all I've ever asked for."* **What's the word around town?** *"Generally, if you want rumors, you should talk to Corpulus. That said... I did see a [visitor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Varnius_Junius) pass through on his way to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md)."* When exiting conversation:

*"You should think about staying the night. Corpulus keeps a clean inn."* *"Stick around! Still plenty of songs to sing and mead to drink."*
## Conversations
If Lisette runs into Vivienne Onis, they will have some brief conversations, at times dependent on your progress with the quest [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md):

**Lisette**: *"It's too bad the Burning of King Olaf was called off this year. I think some of the younger bards would really have gotten a kick out of it."* / *"I think our flutist here has gotten better since the Burning of King Olaf."*
**Vivienne**: *"Well, you can understand Elisif not wanting it. It is a shame though."* / *"I'm so glad we got to have it. I thought for sure Elisif would never consent."*

**Vivienne**: *"Do you miss your days training at the College?"*
**Lisette**: *"Absolutely not. I prefer to be near enough to come visit but free to come and go as I please."*

**Vivienne**: *"I could listen to him/her play all day."*
**Lisette**: *"He's/She's okay. A little breathy and a little harsh on the notes."*
**Vivienne**: *"Speaking of harsh. You Bards don't cut each other any slack do you?"*
**Lisette**: *"Not in our nature."*

She may also run into [Una](Skyrim_Una.md), a maid from the Blue Palace:

**Una**: *"I keep coming out here thinking I'll see what others see in this... frivolity."*
**Lisette**: *"Your * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * a hard woman to please Una."*
**Una**: *"I'm pleased when the palace is clean, I'm pleased when the beds are made on time, I'm pleased when I * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * Erdi isn't around. Most of those don't happen very often."*

## Notes
- Lisette delivers a greeting telling the player to feel free to make requests even when she is not currently performing at the inn.
