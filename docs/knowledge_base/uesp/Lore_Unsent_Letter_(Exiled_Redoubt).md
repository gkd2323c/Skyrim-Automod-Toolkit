Unsent Letter *A stock form letter offering condolences* *
---
Dear _____ Family,

The loss of a child is a hardship that no parent should ever endure. If you should need a sympathetic friend, please keep us in mind. This dreadful war has taken your future and given you only sorrow in return. This is not what was promised to us by the leaders of our Alliances. The deaths caused by this accursed conflict outweigh the security of rebuilding the empire. Senseless destruction is not good for any of the peoples of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Not when it costs the lives of our children.

It is too early for anyone in your position to do anything but grieve for your child. But should you feel the same as we do, we urge you to join us in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) when you are ready.

Cato Albus
