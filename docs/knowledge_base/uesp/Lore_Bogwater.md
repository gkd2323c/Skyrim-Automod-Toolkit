[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/OB-place-Bogwater.jpg/200px-OB-place-Bogwater.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Bogwater.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Bogwater.jpg) A campsite at Bogwater [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/OBR-place-Bogwater.jpg/200px-OBR-place-Bogwater.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Bogwater.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-place-Bogwater.jpg) Bogwater (Oblivion Remastered) Bogwater is a swamp located in southern [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), on the eastern coast of [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md) in the province of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is surrounded by the Niben Forest to the north, Blackwood Bog to the northeast, and [Gloomire](Lore_Gloomire.md) in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) to the southeast.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup> A small island to the south hides the entrance to the ancient [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) complex known as [Deepscorn Hollow](Lore_Deepscorn_Hollow.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OB-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-No Lt S-3)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a renegade [Shadowscale](Lore_Shadowscale.md) royal assassin named Scar-Tail fled Black Marsh as a fugitive and set up camp at Bogwater. The [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) Royal Court sent an agent to track him down and execute him for this act of treason. Scar-Tail managed to kill the would-be assassin, but was badly injured in the fight. Teinaava, a fellow Shadowscale and member of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md), subsequently learned of his former egg-brother's treason and sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to Bogwater to assassinate him. The Hero arrived at the camp to find Scar-Tail close to death. Scar-Tail offered treasure in exchange for his life, but it is unknown whether he was spared.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
