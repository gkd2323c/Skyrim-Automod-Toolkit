[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hrodulf.jpg) Hrodulf **Hrodulf** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) found along the coast of [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) near a boat south of his old [house](Skyrim_Hrodulf%27s_House.md). He was presumably killed by the [burnt spriggan](Skyrim_Burnt_Spriggan.md) standing over his body. He only possesses a pair of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). Judging by his [journal](Skyrim_Hrodulf%27s_Journal.md), he had become obsessed with the noises of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) machinery still operating beneath his house, and refused to leave despite the [pleadings](Skyrim_Letter_(Dragonborn).md) of his lover [Bjornolfr](Skyrim_Bjornolfr.md), who lived in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Despite [Hrodulf's warnings](Skyrim_Bloodstained_Letter.md), Bjornolfr eventually came to find Hrodulf, but he was killed by a pair of [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) who had taken up residence in his lover's house. Next to Hrodulf's body is an apprentice-locked strong box and a chest, both of which contain a decent amount of loot. There is an [amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md) and a [gold diamond ring](Skyrim_Gold_Diamond_Ring.md) in the boat resting on one of the wharfs.
