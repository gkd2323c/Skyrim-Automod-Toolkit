[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/20/SR-place-Valindor%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Valindor%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Valindor%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Valindor%27s_House.jpg) Valindor's House **Valindor's House** is a house which is owned by [Valindor](Skyrim_Valindor.md).

It is located on the lower levels of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), alongside the canal which flows through the city. It is next door to [Marise Aravel's House](Skyrim_Marise_Aravel%27s_House.md), and below the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md).

## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/05/SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Valindor%27s_House.jpg) Valindor's house This is a single-room dwelling, with a partial wall dividing it into separate areas. The main living space consists of a fireplace against the east wall, two chairs pulled up in front, a small table with a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on it between them, and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on the hearth. Along the north wall, a set of shelves holds two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll), several [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie), several pieces of cheese, and a bottle of [Alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine). Against the west wall is a dresser, with a copy of the book *[Of Crossed Daggers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers)* on top. A dining table, a bench, some barrels and some sacks of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) stand beside the partial wall; hanging above are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and two bunches of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). The table is lain with a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), a portion of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), an [Eidar cheese wheel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), a bottle of Alto wine, and a bottle of Nord mead.

Beyond the partition is a sparse bedroom, consisting of an owned, single bed; on an end table beside it lies a copy of the book *[Horker Attacks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Attacks).* To the right is a wardrobe, storing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md); opposite this, along the south wall, is an unlocked wooden [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot.

## Notes
- There is a [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot [shadowmark](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) on the left doorstep.
- Once you have joined the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), a barrel that is carved with the [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Cache shadowmark appears opposite the entrance, on the walkway that travels alongside the canal.
- If you infect Valindor with vampirism during the [New Allegiances](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest, a coffin will appear in his house<sup>[*verification needed — location?*]</sup> and can be used to sleep and get the vampire rested bonus. Valindor does not seem to use it however, and will still sleep in his old bed.
- During [New Allegiances](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest, the door to Valindor's House will lock if night falls while inside it. Unlocking the door at this point is considered a crime. Just wait until 04:00 AM for the door to unlock itself.
