# Dumonte

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/ON-map-Gavaudon.jpg/200px-ON-map-Gavaudon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Gavaudon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Gavaudon.jpg) Gavaudon, the ancestral home of Dumontes The Dumonte family is an old and famous [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) line known for having a considerable familial connection with the Clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) of [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md). During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) they were known to live in [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-1)</sup> a region in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), within the larger region of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ON2-3)</sup>
