[![ON-book-Nantharion Signature.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/f3/ON-book-Nantharion_Signature.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-book-Nantharion_Signature.png) Mystic Tarim,

After [Hoperoot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Hoperoot), it is clear we face tenacious foes. [Hermaeus Mora](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Hermaeus_Mora)'s minions fight against their own freedom, but we must protect our [Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Ithelia) nonetheless.

To this end, you must ensure the Skein of Secrets cannot be used against us. [Torvesard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Torvesard) believes it was sealed away in the vaults of [Niryastare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Niryastare) long ago. Find a way to retrieve the Skein and destroy it.

I must take a different path. The power slumbering in the Darkened Home can restore our Prince—I am sure of it. All I need do is plant enough seeds and use the Staff to awaken it.
