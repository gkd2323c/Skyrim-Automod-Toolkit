# Demigod

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/93/ON-tribute-alessia-Morihaus%2C_Sacred_Bull_%28crop%29.png/250px-ON-tribute-alessia-Morihaus%2C_Sacred_Bull_%28crop%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-alessia-Morihaus,_Sacred_Bull_(crop).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-tribute-alessia-Morihaus,_Sacred_Bull_(crop).png) Morihaus, one of the most well-known demigods A Demigod is a divine being of unclear specifications. It is possible they are the [Aedric](Lore_Aedra.md) counterpart to the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [Demiprinces](Lore_Demiprince.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAFNJTR-1)</sup> making them the offspring of any divine being of non-Daedric origins and a lesser being. In some cases, the term might simply refer to a very powerful spirit or ascended mortal.

## Known Demigods
- [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md) is the most well-known example of a demigod,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LAFNJTR-1)</sup> being called the son of [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP-2)</sup> and blessed by her with the [Lord's Mail](Lore_Lord%27s_Mail.md).
- [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) is said to be a demigod in some sources.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TFS-3)</sup>
- The [Green Lady](Lore_Green_Lady.md) of the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) has been said to possess the qualities of a demigod.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Lo G-4)</sup>
- The [Mad Architect](Lore_Mad_Architect.md) has been referred to as a demigod.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOTubidan_the_Alarmist-5)</sup>
- The three living gods of the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) are occasionally referred to as demigods.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-COTFC7-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> In addition, [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) was possibly a demigoddess even before she became an actual goddess. Temple myth holds that on [Mount Assarnibibi](Lore_Mount_Assarnibibi.md), [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) oversaw the ninety-nine lovers of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) who gave birth to her.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tholer-8)</sup>
- While generally being considered one of the [original spirits](Lore_Et%27Ada.md),<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TM-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-VOF-10)</sup> some sources portray [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md) as a "great Elvish [demi-god](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Demi-God)" or a "quasi-mythical Elvish deity."<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ACS-11)</sup>
- [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) was a mythic [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) hero and warrior who is sometimes elevated to the status of a god,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TSK-12)</sup> or him being created by one with conflicting accounts. Some say [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) created him from moonlight, another origin states he was created from [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s breath, and a third from the tears of joy from [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md) after [Sheggorath](Lore_Sheggorath.md) made him laugh at [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s expense.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESOKRO-13)</sup>

## Notes
- [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) was supposedly the son of an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) and a god of the previous [kalpa](Lore_Kalpa.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSOP-2)</sup> possibly making him a demigod.
- [Dearola](Lore_Dearola.md), the Daughter of Hircine, is termed a demigoddess,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-TTWT-14)</sup> but would be more accurately described as a demiprince.
- A [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) named Thurindil claimed to be the son of [Julianos](Lore_Julianos.md) and [Mohi-Titona](Lore_Mohi-Titona.md), Queen of Akavir. If true, this would make him a demigod, however it is much more probable these are the ramblings of a madman.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-OB-15)</sup>
- [Baan Dar](Lore_Baan_Dar.md)'s daughter was known to manifest as a female [elf](Lore_Mer.md) named Aniel.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Aniel-16)</sup>
