# Shagrath

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/ON-creature-Shagrath%27s_Host.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Shagrath%27s_Host.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Shagrath%27s_Host.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Shagrath%27s_Host.jpg) Shagrath's Host Shagrath is the God of Spiders, an obscure goddess whose [High Priestess](Lore_Selene.md) once threatened all of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) with an army of spiders.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARUlandra-1)</sup> She is associated with [the Green](Lore_The_Green.md) and the birth of the world, and [spiders](Lore_Spider.md) are her blessed creatures.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Online-2)</sup> [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md) believed that she was responsible for showing the changeling [Selene](Lore_Selene.md) how to take form. Whether Shagrath was lost to time or still exists in some form remains a mystery,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt C:S-3)</sup> though her magic boons remain effective even in modern times.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SH-4)</sup> Few ever escape Shagrath's web once ensnared, marked by her whispersilk threads. Those who do bear a webbed brand on their faces. Shagrath marks those of interest with the silver strands adorning their bodies. They can choose to follow her intricate designs or weave their own paths; Shagrath prefers the latter.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SFFM-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SFBM-6)</sup>

## Notes
- In early drafts of the Selene's Web dungeon in ESO, there was an objective to "destroy Shagrath's Eggs".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7)</sup>
- Vanus Galerion speculated that Shagrath may actually be conflated with another entity from the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md) who possesses an affinity for spiders (presumably [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md)).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Mt C:S-3)</sup>
