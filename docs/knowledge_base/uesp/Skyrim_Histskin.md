*Invoke the power of the Hist to recover health ten times faster for 60 seconds.* **Histskin** is a [Greater Power](Skyrim_Powers.md#Greater_Powers) possessed by all [Argonians](Skyrim_Argonian.md), which allows them once a day to increase their rate of health regeneration ten-fold for 60 seconds.
