[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/SR-npc-Dremora_Merchant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Dremora_Merchant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Merchant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Dremora_Merchant.jpg) The Dremora Merchant The **Dremora Merchant** is a [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md) that can be summoned by the [Black Market](Skyrim_Black_Market.md) power, one of the reward options from the quest [Black Book: Untold Legends](Skyrim_Black_Book__Untold_Legends_(quest).md). When summoned, he approaches you and goes into the sell screen. He wears [black robes](Skyrim_Black_Robes_(xx0376da).md) and a pair of [Boots](Skyrim_Boots_(xx0376d9).md).

The Dremora merchant has a more pleasant personality than many of his kind—he speaks with a refined voice more akin to that of a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) than the low-pitched growl common to his race, and seems to delight in providing his services to you, unlike the [Dremora Butler](Skyrim_Dremora_Butler.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Black Book: Untold Legends](Skyrim_Black_Book__Untold_Legends_(quest).md)**: Learn the [Black Book](Skyrim_Black_Book__Untold_Legends_(book).md)'s hidden knowledge.

## Dialogue
He will greet you by saying one of the following:

- *"Ah, my favorite customer."*

- *"You have many things I wish to buy."*

- *"So soon? You must be hungry for a deal."*

- *"Ah, a fresh mortal who seeks to bargain with me."*

- *"So, we meet again."*

When you offer to sell your items to him, he will reply with one of the following:

- *"I'm sure we can strike a bargain."*

- *"Haggling with you can be so...entertaining."*

- *"Let's make a deal."*

- *"I can hardly wait."*

Once you have finished bartering with him, he will part ways by making one of the following remarks:

- **Dremora Merchant:** *"Until you summon me again."*
- **Dremora Merchant:** *"I do so love dealing with mortals."*
- **Dremora Merchant:** *"Ah, sweet [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). Soon I shall return."*

## Notes
- His inventory is leveled, and includes weapons, armor and arrows. At higher levels, he is a reliable source of [Daedric items](Skyrim_Daedric.md). - The Dremora Merchant is also a possible source for [Dragonplate armor](Skyrim_Dragon_Items.md#Dragonplate_Armor).
- The Dremora Merchant sometimes can sell Daedric and Dragon items even before they start appearing in the world, making him one of only a few locations to do that. Another such example is [Glover Mallory's Thief Cache](Skyrim_Glover_Mallory.md). <sup>[*verification needed — At which level do they become available?*]</sup>
- While he doesn't have a large selection, his gold count is always 2000, making him a good choice if you need to sell some gear immediately.
- When selling items to the Dremora Merchant, you'll receive 9% less gold than from other merchants. If you don't have rank 2 or higher of the [Haggling perk](Skyrim_Haggling.md), the Dremora Merchant will also charge 25% more for purchased items. - He won't accept stolen items unless you have the [Fence](Skyrim_Fence.md) perk.
- He is not affected by any other [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) perks.
- The quest [Buy Dwarven artifact](Skyrim_Buy_Dwarven_artifact.md) can be triggered when buying Dwarven equipment from him.
