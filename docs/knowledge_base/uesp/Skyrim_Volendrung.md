[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/SR-item-Volendrung.jpg/200px-SR-item-Volendrung.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Volendrung.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Volendrung.jpg) Volendrung Volendrung, also known as the Hammer of Might, is said to have been created by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) of the now-abandoned clan of Rourken, hundreds of years before they disappeared from [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Like the dwarves who created it, Volendrung is prone to disappearing suddenly. Unlike the Dwemer, Volendrung is also prone to resurfacing unexpectedly. It is the reward for completing [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)'s daedric quest, [The Cursed Tribe](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md). It can be [tempered](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) with an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md). Its swing speed is faster than other warhammers (the same speed as battleaxes and greatswords). It is the second fastest warhammer in the game, after [the Longhammer](Skyrim_The_Longhammer.md), which is the fastest two-handed, non-artifact, weapon in the game.

## Related Quests
- **[The Cursed Tribe](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md)**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) of [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) by [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md).

## Notes
- Volendrung is the only Daedric artifact you do not need to possess for the Oblivion Walker achievement/trophy, as the quest completes (and increments your total) while the hammer is transformed on the altar.
- In [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Volendrung), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Volendrung), and [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Volendrung), Volendrung had the ability to absorb health and paralyze opponents, but it was unenchanted in [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Volendrung).
- Guards will occasionally comment on the weapon if you have it equipped, saying *"That is one... big hammer"* or *"What do you aim to do with that hammer, friend? Knock down a house?"*
- Despite its higher speed giving it an abnormally high DPS value, there are two base weapons that outdo it: the dragonbone battleaxe and the dragonbone warhammer.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.
- The orange, pulsing light in the center of the head still glows when the enchantment is depleted.

## Bugs
- This weapon is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk.
- Volendrung is not flagged as being made of Dwemer material.
