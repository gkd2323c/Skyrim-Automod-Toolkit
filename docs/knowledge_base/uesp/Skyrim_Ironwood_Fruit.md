[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/SR-ingredient-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg) Ironwood Fruit [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/SR-flora-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Fruit.jpg) Ironwood Fruit on an Ironwood tree **Ironwood Fruit** is a rare ingredient that can only be harvested from [Iron Tusk Cave](Skyrim_Iron_Tusk_Cave.md) on the island of [Giant's Tooth](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Tooth.md). This island can only be reached if you choose the Altar of Nothing during the quest [A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md). You will be sent to the island to retrieve a single Ironwood Fruit, which will be marked as a [quest item](Skyrim_Quest_Items.md). However, the ingredient will no longer be marked as a quest item after the quest has completed, and can be harvested and used freely.

The fruit can be purchased from [Khajiit caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md) after completing the quest [A Dying Wish](Skyrim_A_Dying_Wish.md), even if the Altar of Nothing was not chosen.

## Plants
Six Ironwood Fruit plants exist, namely the rare [Ironwood trees](Lore_Ironwood.md) that grow within Iron Tusk Cave. The fruits on these trees respawn. The fruit is also affected by the *[Green Thumb](Skyrim_Green_Thumb.md)* perk, so 12 fruit can be harvested in one trip.

## Other Uses
A single [cooking](Skyrim_Cooking.md) recipe calls for the use of Ironwood Fruit. [Ironwood Soup](Skyrim_Ironwood_Soup.md) can be made with one sample of Ironwood Fruit, one [Charred Skeever Hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), and one [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile). You may need to cook a bowl of this soup during the quest A Dying Wish in order to treat [ataxia](Skyrim_Ataxia.md). In fact, drinking this soup will [cure all diseases](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md).
