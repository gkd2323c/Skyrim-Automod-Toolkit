J'zhar's Journal **by [J'zhar](Skyrim_J%27zhar.md)** *The journal of one of the Khajiiti brothers on an ill-fated expedition*
---
This one is at his wit's end. I signed [J'darr](Skyrim_J%27darr.md) and myself up for this expedition to try to get him clean of the [Skooma](Lore_Skooma.md). I brought a small supply to try bring *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* him down slowly, but the storm has had us trapped in the glacier for weeks.

The others have not yet caught on that one with fur should not shake so much from the cold, but I've run out of the little skooma I brought and J'darr is getting pretty bad. He's started hallucinating creatures coming out of the ice and the ruins, *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* The others are starting to think he may be behind [Valie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valie)'s disappearance, but I know he would never do something like that.
