[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/SR-npc-Azzadal.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Azzadal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azzadal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azzadal.jpg) Azzadal **Azzadal** is the [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md) of a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md) who can be met after jumping from [Bard's Leap Summit](Skyrim_Bard%27s_Leap_Summit.md). If the jump is successfully completed, Azzadal will appear and say:

- *"Been a long time since someone took the leap. Longer still since any survived it. I once performed the entirety of the Poetic Edda from atop Bard's Leap before trying my luck. Well, you can see how that turned out."*

After his dialogue, he will play a song and you will receive two points to your [speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill.

He wears full [Hammerfell garb](Skyrim_Hammerfell_Garb.md), including an [Alik'r hood](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Hood.md) and a pair of [Redguard boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Redguard_Boots). He carries a [scimitar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scimitar).

## Notes
- In [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Skyrim_-_The_Adventure_Game)'s card numbered 184 titled "The Reachmen", Azzadal is referenced as being the bard of [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) during his rule of [The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md). Madanach agrees to help the [Sheathed Blades](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md) in return for helping Azzadal complete his poetic leap from Bard's Leap Summit.
