## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of heavy Steel armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Over the Edge" starts by reading Suicide at Dragon Bridge in Four Shields Tavern at Dragon Bridge.)*
