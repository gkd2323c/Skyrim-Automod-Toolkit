Khargol gro-Boguk (also spelled **Khargol gro-Bogúk**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWProphecies-1)</sup> was an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) [barbarian](Lore_Barbarians.md) who was a master of the unarmored fighting style and lived on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWKhargol-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWWD-3)</sup> Khargol was friends with the Orc woman known as 'the Wraith' Kepkajna gra-Minfang.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWWD-3)</sup> He mentored her in the ways of the unarmored style which helped her maintain her wedding dress on a contentious travel to her wedding.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWWD-3)</sup> These events went on to be published in the book; *[The Wraith's Wedding Dowry](Lore_The_Wraith%27s_Wedding_Dowry.md)*.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWWD-3)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), Khargol was set up in the abandoned [Dwemer tower](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) near the town of [Dagon Fel](Lore_Dagon_Fel.md) where he continued to offer to train others in the art of the unarmored combat.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWKhargol-2)</sup>
