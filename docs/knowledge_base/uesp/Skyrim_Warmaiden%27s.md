[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-place-Warmaiden%27s.jpg/200px-SR-place-Warmaiden%27s.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Warmaiden%27s.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Warmaiden%27s.jpg) Warmaiden's **Warmaiden's** is a smithy and weapons store in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). It is located just inside the main gate, right next to [Breezehome](Skyrim_Breezehome.md).

The store is owned by [Ulfberth War-Bear](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ulfberth_War-Bear) and his wife [Adrianne Avenicci](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adrianne_Avenicci). It consists of one area, *[Warmaiden's](#Warmaiden.27s)*.

Like all other stores, Warmaiden's has a number of fixed wares on display scattered around the main trading room. These can be bought from the proprietors and will disappear from the room when purchased. For a list of these special items, see [this section](#Displayed_Merchandise).

## Warmaiden's
During business hours, Adrianne is often smithing in the outside workshop while Ulfberth sells her products inside the store. Both Adrianne and Ulfberth are merchants (see [notes](#Notes)), though it is for Adrianne the store was named. Apparently interested in swords even as a child, she received "Warmaiden" as a nickname from her father.

Without the [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise), the main trading room is completely empty, save for five food sacks scattered around the room. Ulfberth can be found here every day between 8am and 8pm, offering his merchant services. Behind the counter is a door leading to a small dining area with a burning fireplace and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md). There is also an alternative exit to Whiterun.

On the balcony of the second floor is a small table with some [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), right next to the door leading to the bedroom. The bedroom contains a minor loot chest in the northwestern corner and, in the adjacent corner, a dresser with some [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#Clothes). On top of the dresser sits the store's [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger), a locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), and a plate with a [boiled creme treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boiled_Creme_Treat). The double bed stands at the southern wall with an end table on either side, both with clutter. There is a rare copy of *[Killing - Before You're Killed](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Killing_-_Before_You%27re_Killed)* on top of one end table and a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale) and a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on the other.

The workshop outside includes a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md), a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) and a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelting.md).

## Notes
- If Adrianne is inside, she and Ulfberth share the same [merchant chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Merchant_Chest) (essentially the same inventory and gold); however, when outside during business hours, she has a separate merchant chest with independent merchandise and gold. Therefore, you can do much more business at the store if you approach each of them individually while Adrianne is outside. Once you enter the shop, however, Adrianne will share Ulberth's chest until they reset.<sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>
- You can invest in Adrianne and Ulfberth separately if you catch Adrianne outside the shop.
- [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> may mention Warmaiden's when asked what he has for sale: *"It's no Warmaiden's, but I've got a decent supply."*

## Bugs
- After leaving the shop, it may become locked, even during business hours. - It will remain locked until you break in, or may unlock itself if you leave the area and come back. If someone notices you picking the lock, it is a crime. However, Ulfberth will act normally and won't accuse you of trespassing, assuming you broke in during the day.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:talk_Warmaiden%27s#Warmaidens_Permanently_Locked)*]</sup>
- Ulfberth may present dialogue that appears to allow for multiple investments, but only the first will count.
- Items sold to Ulfberth are removed from your inventory but gold may not be received.
- The tub of water that's part of the forge outside doesn't exist as a physical object (you can walk though it as if it weren't there) nor something to interact with to enter the smithing interface (there is no option to when scrolling over it) like all other forges.
- If you buy something from Adrianne then head inside and buy something from Ulfberth, then exit the store and ask Adrianne what she has for sale, her shop inventory will now mirror Ulfberth's, down to the gold available and what he had for sale, even though she is still outside and should have her own separate inventory. <sup>**?**</sup>
