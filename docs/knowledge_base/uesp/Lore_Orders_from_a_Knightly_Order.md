Orders from a Knightly Order **by [Knight-Commander Varaine](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Knight-Commander_Varaine)** *An edict to recover the Memory Stone of Makela Leki*
---
Dame Doisne,

Through King [Eamond](Lore_Eamond.md), [High King Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) has requested the order's assistance in doing a favor for [King Fahara'jad](Lore_Fahara%27jad.md). Centuries ago, a group of [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) exiles sought refuge in [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md). Not trusting the exiles, the king held them in an underground prison. Unfortunately, their status was never resolved, and the prison became their crypt—the Crypt of the Exiles.

Among the exiles was a warrior named Ulbazar who had stolen a Redguard artifact, the [Memory Stone](Lore_Memory_Stone.md) of [Makela Leki](Lore_Makela_Leki.md). King Fahara'jad has asked to have it be returned. Retrieve the stone from Ulbazar's sepulcher and bring it to Madaima, a Redguard scholar at Martyr's Crossing. She will return it to King Fahara'jad's Impervious Vault in [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md).

Saint [Pelin](Lore_Pelin.md) watch over you,

Knight-Commander Varaine
