## Notes
- Several instances of [real world profanity](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Profanity) exist in *[The Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls)*. These include shit,<sup>[[64]](#cite_note-MWF3-64)</sup> bitch,<sup>[[65]](#cite_note-Ulfric-65)</sup> son-of-a-bitch,<sup>[[66]](#cite_note-RBG-66)</sup> retarded,<sup>[[67]](#cite_note-Fools-67)</sup> dammit,<sup>[[68]](#cite_note-Glenroy-68)</sup> arse,<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-ME-48)</sup> ass,<sup>[[69]](#cite_note-Erasmo-69)</sup> asshole,<sup>[[70]](#cite_note-Tobias-70)</sup> bastard,<sup>[[71]](#cite_note-Ragjar-71)</sup> whore,<sup>[[72]](#cite_note-Ilnori-72)</sup> and slut.<sup>[[73]](#cite_note-Erikur-73)</sup> There is an allusion to the word fuck,<sup>[[74]](#cite_note-74)</sup> but the full word has never been used in the series. Instead, it is often substituted with setting-related profanity such as "fetch" (e.g. "fetcher" or "fetching")<sup>[[48]](#cite_note-ME-48)</sup> or "tusk" (e.g. "tusker" or "tusking").<sup>[[57]](#cite_note-OTT-57)</sup><sup>[[75]](#cite_note-Arushna-75)</sup>
