[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-npc-Lurbuk.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lurbuk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lurbuk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lurbuk.jpg) Lurbuk [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Laid_to_Rest_02.jpg) The mob outside [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md) **Lurbuk** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bard.md) found at the [Moorside Inn](Skyrim_Moorside_Inn.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). The local innkeeper, [Jonna](Skyrim_Jonna.md), claims that the only reason she hasn't booted him out is because there aren't any other customers for him to bother. A contract is offered on him in the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) questline. He is known as one of, if not the worst bards in Skyrim, a fact he seems either oblivious of, or just ignores.

Lurbuk wakes up at 6am and heads to the main room of the inn to play an instrument for the entirety of the day, occasionally chatting with Jonna, before heading to sleep at 1am. During the quest [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md), Lurbuk is one of the town members that band together and form a mob outside of [Movarth's Lair](Skyrim_Movarth%27s_Lair.md). Like most of the mob, however, he is too afraid to actually enter the lair and heads back to town soon after.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5). He carries some [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common) and an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace).

## Notes
- Despite his bad reputation, Lurbuk is one of the only bards to never sing any songs unprompted and you cannot ask him to perform any songs, either. The closest he gets to actually singing are the improvised "songs" during the Dark Brotherhood contract quest.
- According to the game data, Lurbuk is the son of [Chief Burguk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Burguk) of [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md). Their relationship is also marked as "rivals" and as such, you may [receive a letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:An_enemy%27s_gratitude) from Chief Burguk after having killed Lurbuk. - In the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, he's referred to as **Lurbuk gro-Dushnikh**, which is another tie to the location.
