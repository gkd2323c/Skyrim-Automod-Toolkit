[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Self_Doubt.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Self_Doubt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Self_Doubt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Self_Doubt.jpg) Pelagius's Self Doubt **Self Doubt** is an aspect of [Pelagius the Mad](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Mad.md)'s [mind](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_a_Madman.md). There are two Self Doubts attacking each other during the fight between [Confidence](Skyrim_Confidence_(NPC).md) and [Anger](Skyrim_Anger.md). They exist purely to get in your way.

They each wield a [bound battleaxe](Skyrim_Bound_Battleaxe.md), but otherwise carry nothing.
