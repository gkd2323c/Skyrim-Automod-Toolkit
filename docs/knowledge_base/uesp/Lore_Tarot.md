# Tarot

A Tarot is a deck of cards used for fortune telling.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TJ-1)</sup> Some tarot decks possess actual [magical](Lore_Magic.md) properties.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TC-2)</sup> Among the various known tarot deck variations, some consist of 78 cards,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TR-3)</sup> while others have 34 cards.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TJ-1)</sup> Some cards are considered works of art of ornamental value,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TJ-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TC-2)</sup> games for entertainment,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TR-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TD-4)</sup> and in the case of some decks, ritual objects,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TJ-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TD-4)</sup> and with decks of magical properties - magical curiosities.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TC-2)</sup>

## Culture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/90/MER-art-The_World.jpg/200px-MER-art-The_World.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-The_World.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MER-art-The_World.jpg) A tarot card (Tarot card) There are many different types of tarot decks and variety of artwork and style of cards. Some decks are known for their depictions of various Daedric Princes.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TD-4)</sup> Calling Cards of Arcana are a set of tarot cards carrying real magical power. They are a set of painted cards, each with a unique magical signature.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TC-2)</sup>

### Khajiit
Among the known [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) tarot decks, there are 34-card [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) tarot decks. Some of these are known to feature engraved [jadeite](Lore_Jadeite.md) placards, imported from the city of [Alabaster](Lore_Alabaster.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TJ-1)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/ON-prerelease-Ravenwatch_Markarth.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Ravenwatch_Markarth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Ravenwatch_Markarth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Ravenwatch_Markarth.jpg) Members of the House Ravenwatch
### House Ravenwatch
The deck of 78 cards known as the Ravenwatch Tarot had its name derived from the noble [House of Ravenwatch](Lore_House_Ravenwatch.md) of the [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). These cardboard cards are often coloful and decorated with strange [symbols](Lore_Symbols.md) and images.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TR-3)</sup>
