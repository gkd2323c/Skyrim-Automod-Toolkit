The source of the river is Lake Geir at the western end of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). The river flows into [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) to join the White River south-southwest of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

## Walkthrough
The Darkwater River, or simply the Darkwater, is a river in eastern Skyrim connecting Lake Geir to the White River. The lake feeds into the river near [Nilheim](Skyrim_Nilheim.md) in the western Rift. The river flows northeast into Eastmarch, cascading down the cliff which divides the two holds. After a series of pools and waterfalls, the river bends northwest near [Darkwater Crossing](Skyrim_Darkwater_Crossing.md) and merges with the White River a few miles downstream, south-southwest of Windhelm and west of the fetid bogs of Eastmarch. Forts, ruins, caves, and major roads line its banks, and the fishing is said to be good.

A large tributary flows separately from Lake Geir. It passes north through [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), curves northeast to create its own series of waterfalls down the cliff, and connects with the Darkwater River in Eastmarch near [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md) shortly before the main river merges with the White River.
