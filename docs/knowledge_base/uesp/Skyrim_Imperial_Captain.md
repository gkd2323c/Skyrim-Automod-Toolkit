[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-npc-Imperial_Captain.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_Captain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Captain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_Captain.jpg) The Imperial Captain The **Imperial Captain** is a [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) and one of the first people you meet in the game. She is in charge of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) soldiers stationed at [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) and orders your execution during the tutorial. If you choose to follow the [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md) into [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md), she will become hostile (see [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)). If you choose to follow Hadvar into the keep, the captain will not be seen again.

She uses an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) in combat, and also carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She wears a full set of Imperial heavy armor: [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md), [Imperial boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Boots.md), [Imperial bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Bracers.md), and [Imperial officer's helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Officer%27s_Helmet.md). This is the first full set of heavy armor you can obtain in the game.

## Related Quests
- **[Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md)**: Escape [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) custody and a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) attack.

## Quest-Related Events

### Unbound
When the carts stop at the execution area, she may say, *"Get these prisoners out of the carts. Move it!"* After the prisoners have exited the cart, she will say, *"Step towards the block when we call your name. One at a time."* When [Lokir](Skyrim_Lokir.md) attempts to escape, she will call out *"Halt!"* and then, *"Archers!"* causing them to kill Lokir. After this scene, she will ask, *"Anyone else feel like running?"* When [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md) notes that [your name is not on the list](Skyrim_Unbound.md#Keep_Your_Head) of those to be executed, she dismisses his concern by saying *"Forget the list! He/She goes to the block."*

The first time Alduin roars, the following dialogue will take place:
**Hadvar**: *"What was that?"*
**General Tullius**: *"It's nothing. Carry on."*
**Imperial Captain**: *"Yes, General Tullius."*

She will then turn to the [Priestess of Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priestess_of_Arkay), and say, *"Give them their last rites."*

When calling for you to be executed, the Imperial captain refers to you depending on your race.

After she says this, Alduin will roar again, prompting the following conversation:
**Hadvar**: *"There it is again. Did you hear that?"*
**Imperial Captain**: *"I said, next prisoner!"*

When Alduin finally swoops down upon Helgen, she will ask, *"Sentries! What do you see?"* If you choose to enter the keep with Ralof, after you pick up [Gunjar](Skyrim_Gunjar.md)'s gear, she will approach from the other room with an Imperial soldier and you will overhear her say, *"Come on soldier! Keep moving!"* She will then command, *"Get this gate open."* When she sees you and Ralof she will shout, *"It's the escaped prisoners!"* You will then be forced to kill her. If you go with Hadvar instead, she is never seen again in the game.
