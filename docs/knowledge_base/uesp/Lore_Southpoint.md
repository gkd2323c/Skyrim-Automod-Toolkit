## History
The city was founded in [1E 2729](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2729) by the [Colovian](Lore_Colovia.md) governor [Zantonius](Lore_Zantonius.md), born in [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md). It became the main port and [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) colony in the Kingdom of Grahtwood, then an administrative division of the [Second Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) in Valenwood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFo S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONload Golden-3)</sup>

In [2E 580](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_580) was incorporated to the [First Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) along with the rest of Camoran-controlled Valenwood after the Blacksap Rebellion was put down by [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md) and [Aeradan Camoran](Lore_Aeradan_Camoran.md) joined forces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-WTNAI-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TROC-5)</sup> In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Southpoint's citizens began killing one another in the streets and Dominion soldiers cordoned off the town. [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) had taken control of the Mayor, but the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) ended the madness, dealing with both the corrupt Imperial mayor and the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md), and the city remained in the Dominion until its eventual dissolution.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESO-6)</sup>

In [2E 830](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_830), the city was again annexed by the Second Aldmeri Dominion after the intervention of [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) in another Camoran civil war.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PGE1AD-7)</sup> However, the catastrophic use of the [Numidium](Lore_Numidium.md) by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) resulted in the conquest of both the entirely of Valenwood and the Summerset Isles in [2E 896](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_896), ending the Second Aldmeri Dominion control of the settlement, which was incorporated to the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BHot E1-8)</sup>

During the Third Era, the city remained in Empire hands except for the [Camoran Usurper Rebellion](Lore_Camoran_Usurper.md) between [3E 249](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_249) and [3E 267](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_267).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TR-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TFOTU-10)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the city-state of Southpoint was an active settlement. It was ruled by Queen Mindra, and it had a rivalry with [Eldenroot](Lore_Elden_Root.md) and [Haven](Lore_Haven.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Arena-11)</sup> Southpoint was neighbored by several settlements, including [Green Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Green_Hall) to the northeast, and Stonesquare to the north.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Map Arena-12)</sup> The [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) that ended the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) and weakened the Empire provided the opportunity for the Thalmor to seized the Summerset Isles. In [4E 29](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_29), a Thalmor-backed coup overthrew Valenwood's government, routing Imperial forces and their Bosmer supporters. Alinor and Valenwood then proclaimed a union, and a new Aldmeri Dominion was formed. The city fell into Aldmeri hands for the third time in history.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TGW-13)</sup>

## Culture
An [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) -imported celebration, the Rites of [Xarxes](Lore_Xarxes_(god).md) Memory, was held annually by the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) in its cathedral.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOCxmwc-14)</sup>

## Notes
- *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* was originally conceived as a fighting game featuring a tournament that took the player to each of Tamriel's cities to challenge different gladiatorial teams. According to a file from that stage of development left behind in the final game, Southpoint's gladiatorial team would have been called "the [Chimeras](Lore_Chimera.md)".<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-15)</sup>
