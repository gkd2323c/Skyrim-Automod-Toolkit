[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-npc-Runil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Runil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil.jpg) Runil **Runil** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trainer Conjuration Journeyman) of [Arkay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Arkay), and the keeper of [Falkreath's](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md).

Although he may be in charge of taking care of Falkreath's shrine to Arkay, Runil actually lives a fairly relaxed lifestyle. He wakes up at 5am and makes his way out of the Hall of the Dead to head to the local inn, [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md). He will spend his time here sitting, drinking, and socializing with the other regulars that come in throughout the day. He leaves the inn at 8pm to return to the Hall of the Dead. He will sweep the floor and otherwise relax inside the building until he goes to bed at midnight. The only exception to this schedule is when he conducts Lavinia's [last rites ceremony](#Ill_Met_By_Moonlight) which happens when you first enter Falkreath.

He wears [monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monk_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001364d). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_House_of_Arkay) to the Hall of the Dead, as well as a selection of [lower class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
He may greet you with:

*"Kust is responsible for the cemetery here in Falkreath, I take care of the Shrine."* *"War is not new to Falkreath. You have only to stand in our cemetery to see that."* *"The number of graves in this cemetery never decreases."* *"Without birth there is no life, without death there is only stasis. Such is the truth of Arkay."* *"[Dengeir](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md) deserves more respect than his nephew, [Jarl Siddgeir](Skyrim_Siddgeir.md), affords him."* When exiting dialogue, he may say:

*"Life is far too short my friend, don't waste it!"* *"We'll all meet Arkay sooner or later. Best to enjoy the time we've got."* **Do you tend this graveyard alone?** *"Oh, merciful Arkay, no! I am too old and frail, and this graveyard is too large. [Kust](Skyrim_Kust.md) is my assistant. He helps me tend the headstones and keep our cemetery clean, as befits a place of rest."* When asked for [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) [training](Skyrim_Trainers.md), he will caution you with:

**Can you teach me about Conjuration?** *"I'll teach you some of what I know, but be careful."* In his journal he mentions that he is trying to avoid the [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) in Skyrim: *"I have heard that they [the Thalmor] and their agents are abroad in Skyrim, rooting out worshippers of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md). I must be sure to keep a low profile."*

You may be asked by [Thadgeir](Skyrim_Thadgeir.md) to [deliver](Skyrim_Delivery.md) the [ashes of Berit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Berit%27s_Ashes), his war companion, to Runil, "who'll know the right blessings for Berit's soul." When you give the ashes to Runil, he will respond:

**I have a delivery from Thadgeir.** *"Ah, Berit's ashes. He was a good man. Not many warriors grow to a fine, old age. Thank you for bringing this to me. I'll make sure he gets his rites. Here, for your trouble."*
## Quest-Related Events

### Ill Met By Moonlight
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-npc-Runil_03.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Runil_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil_03.jpg) *"...and may we one day rejoin them in eternity"* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Runil_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Runil_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Runil_02.jpg) *"I came into Arkay's light after the Great War."* When you first visit Falkreath, he will be seen preaching to [Mathies](Skyrim_Mathies.md) and [Indara Caerellia](Skyrim_Indara_Caerellia.md) in the graveyard, saying:

*"The god Arkay was once like us, bound to winding mortality. But he willingly gave up this existence that we might better understand the vagaries of life and death. It is through the ebb and flow of this cosmic tide that we find renewal and, in the end, peace. May the spirit of Lavinia and all those who have left this world and its suffering know the beloved serenity of Aetherius...and may we one day rejoin them in eternity."* This all in reference to their recently deceased daughter, Lavinia, who was murdered by [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md).

### Dungeon Delving
**You're a priest of Arkay?** *"I am. I came into Arkay's light after the Great War. So many died by my hands, my magics. I'm fortunate that Kust and the people of Falkreath have come to accept me as one of their own. Could you do an old elf a favor? I misplaced my journal in a cave I was exploring. I'll pay for its return."* **I'll do it.** *"Blessing of Arkay to you for your help."* **I can't do that right now.** *"Very well."* When exiting dialogue after receiving this quest, he will say:

*"That journal means a lot to me, if you find it."* Once you have retrieved his journal, go to him and return it. After you do this, he will say:

**I have your journal.** *"Thank you. It's a collection of regrets that I try not to forget. Here, I promised you this."* He will then give you a leveled amount of gold.

### Skaal Village Dialogue
After having accepted [Morwen's](Skyrim_Morwen.md) quest, and having received [her mother's necklace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bera%27s_Necklace), you must tell him that Morwen sent you, to which he will reply:

**Morwen of Skaal Village sent me to ask a favor of you.** *"Morwen, you say? Now there's a name I haven't heard in a long time. Left to join her mother's people on Solstheim, as I recall. I knew her parents quite well, you know. Bera and Ulfarr were good people, very much in love. Now, what can I do for young Morwen?"* **She's not exactly young anymore.** *"Ah, well, I suppose she'll always be that fiesty * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * little girl I used to know. Of course, when you reach my age, you'll find that the word 'young' is a very relative term."* **She wants this placed at her mother's grave.** *"Ah yes, Bera's silver necklace. It was a wedding gift and she rarely took it off. I'll be glad to oblige. What a touching gesture, to display the symbol of her devotion in such a way. Tell Morwen I'll be happy to grant her request. I am sure it will bring joy to her mother and father as they walk in Aetherius."* He will then take the necklace and allow you to return to Morwen.

## Conversations
Runil was supposed to speak with both Mathies and Indara as they visited the Hall of the Dead, but seeing as neither of them do so in the final game, these scenes never play.

**Runil**: *"Ah, Indara. Arkay's blessing upon you and your husband. Spend as much time here as you need."*
**Indara**: *"Thank you, Runil. Your support means much to us."* **Mathies**: *"The temple is the one place my wife finds peace these days, Runil."*
**Runil**: *"It is a hard time for all of us. I hope that you too can find some sanctuary here, Mathies."* Similarly, Thadgeir was supposed to have a short interaction with Runil as well, but Thadgeir never comes to the graveyard in order for this interaction to happen.

**Runil**: *"You visit us often, Thadgeir."*
**Thadgeir**: *"Most of my friends are buried here."* When running into his assistant at the graveyard, both men will also reflect on the war:

**Kust**: *"The graveyard has been lonely. Few pay homage to the dead."*
**Runil**: *"Few want to be reminded of mortality in a time of war, Kust."*

## Notes
- [Runil's journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runil%27s_Journal) reveals that he had a prophetic dream of the return of [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) and the [Dovahkiin](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md). - The journal also suggests that Runil fought in the Great War on the side of the Aldmeri Dominion as a leader of a unit of battlemages.
- Originally, he was named **Urunil**, instead. This name still gets used as his alias in all the scenes he is involved with, as well as an early line from Thadgeir's delivery quest present in the Dialogue Views files.
