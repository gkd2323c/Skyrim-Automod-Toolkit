# Lives of the Emperors

***Lives of the Emperors*** is a historical book written by [Niso](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Niso) some time after [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417). The book likely deals with the Emperors of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), namely the [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md). The contents of the text is largely unknown; only two short extracts exist.

The first extract describes the devastating political effects of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md):

- *"During the 10-year rule of the Impostor [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md), the provinces were shattered by four devastating wars, and the political policies of the Imperial administration fell into shambles. But for the Restoration, there would be no Empire today."*

The second extract describes the Second Numidian Effect, better known as the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md):

- *"Among the most significant results of the Second Numidian Effect are the foundation of the [orc](Lore_Orc.md) city-state [Nova Orsinium](Lore_Nova_Orsinium.md); the transfiguration and apotheosis of [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md), King of Worms; and the return of [Zurin Arctus](Lore_Zurin_Arctus.md), the [Underking](Lore_Underking.md), to the affairs of men."*

Both of these events took place during the reign of [Emperor Uriel Septim VII](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VII.md), in the final years of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).

## Notes
- The book does not appear in any of the games. The extracts are taken from the [History section of the Codex](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Elderscrolls.com_Archive/Codex#History), originally hosted at the [official Elder Scrolls website](http://elderscrolls.com/).
