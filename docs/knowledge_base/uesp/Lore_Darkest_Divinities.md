Darkest Divinities **by [Skald Skullsplitter](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Skald_Skullsplitter)** *A poem dedicated to the Daedric Princes*
---
Though [man](Lore_Men.md) and [mer](Lore_Mer.md) knew sin
since [time began](Lore_Dawn_Era.md),

The [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md)
watch each mer and man

[Azura](Lore_Azura.md) lights the day
from dawn to dusk

[Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)
calls up memories

[Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md)'s [Wild Hunt](Lore_Great_Hunt.md)
tracks blood and scent and musk

[Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md)
pursues conspiracies

Foul [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md)
remembers every curse

[Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)
knows destruction's wrath

Mad [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)
hums a nonsense verse

Stern [Jyggalag](Lore_Jyggalag.md)
sets order on its path

Dark [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)
takes blood and souls as wealth

[Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) lives
in worlds that dreamers see

Though none can match
unseen [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md)'s stealth

Or hedonistic
[Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md)'s revelry

For every troubled soul
that must aspire,

A Daedric Prince
can grant each dark desire
