### Dragon Bridge Lumber Camp
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp.jpg) Ground floor The building consists of a ground floor and a basement. On the ground floor, a double bed owned by [Lodvar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lodvar) can be found on the east wall, flanked by a non-respawning unlocked chest containing leveled items on the left and a small table on the right. The table holds two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and five loose gold coins. Another bottle of alto wine lies on its side near the foot of the bed. A set of shelves on the north wall holds a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and three bottles of alto wine. A respawning food barrel sits in the southwest corner, and at the center of the room is a large dining table holding two [goat cheese wedges](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), one [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a bottle of alto wine, a [pine thrush egg](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), and two pieces of [salmon meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Meat). Beneath the table are two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/86/SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Dragon_Bridge_Lumber_Camp_02.jpg) Basement In the basement, a respawning food barrel can be found beneath the stairs, along with another on the north wall nearby. A small table holds a goat cheese wedge, a loaf of bread, a pine thrush egg, and a bottle of wine. Between the barrel and table, a hanging rack above holds four freshly caught [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Across from the table, on the south wall, four non-respawning sacks lie on the floor. Next to the sacks is a non-respawning dresser with a [lute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lute) and an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) sitting on top, a [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) on the floor to the right, and a large respawning sack to the left. Another respawning food barrel can be found in the southeast corner, next to a set of shelves holding [miner's clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Miner%27s_Clothes#00080697) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on the top shelf, a [common soul gem](Skyrim_Common_Soul_Gem.md), five gold coins, and a [saw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Saw) on the middle shelf, and two bottles each of wine and alto wine on the bottom shelf. To the left of these shelves is a non-respawning cupboard which holds copies of the books *[The Holds of Skyrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Holds_of_Skyrim)*, *[Of Crossed Daggers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Of_Crossed_Daggers)*, *[Herbane's Bestiary: Ice Wraiths](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Ice_Wraiths)*, and *[Alduin is Real](Skyrim_Alduin_is_Real.md)*, along with two loaves of bread, a bottle of wine, and a bottle of alto wine. A single bed, also owned by Lodvar, is situated with the head along the north wall. At the foot of the bed is a pair of miner [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699), and a small non-respawning unlocked chest containing leveled items sits to the left side. Above the bed are some hanging ingredients, including two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, one dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), and two portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), as well as a hanging rack holding three freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md).

## Notes
- The front door has an apprentice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumber_Camp_Key) to which is carried by [Horgeir](Skyrim_Horgeir.md).
- [Sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> cannot be purchased at the lumber mill here.
- There is a non-respawning pot located at the fire near the wood chopping block.
- All cupboards, end tables, and dressers in the area are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
