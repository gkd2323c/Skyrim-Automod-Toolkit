[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/ON-render-Dagonic_Quasigriff.jpg/200px-ON-render-Dagonic_Quasigriff.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Dagonic_Quasigriff.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Dagonic_Quasigriff.jpg) Quasigriff Irastimil Direnni, also known as the **Creature Creator**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SPG-2)</sup> was one of the members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Once-3)</sup> as well as a pioneer geneticist and skilled mage.

Irastimil was responsible for the creation of [Quasigriffs](Lore_Quasigriff.md), large, feathered, wingless quadrupeds that are related to [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md). They were created through a combination of wizardry and selective breeding. According to their creator, their wingless frame kept their upkeep costs low.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-QG-4)</sup>

Despite Irastimil's aim to create mounts that could maintain superiority over the [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) steeds,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-QG-4)</sup> a breed of quasigriffs was adopted by the Breton nobility themselves. Colorful Baysong Quasigriffs, with vibrant sea-tinted feathers, found their home in the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md). Their creation was accidental, as they were never intended to be bred by the Direnni.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BQ-5)</sup> Another, likely unintended variant of the quasigriffs, known as Dagonic Quasigriffs were the product of the daedric corruption of the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md). They possessed dark plumage, sharpened claws, and were known to be prone to rage.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DQ-6)</sup>

Despite being artificial creations, some breeds of Quasigriffs are considered sacred by the faithful of [Auri-El](Lore_Auri-El.md). Aurielic Quasigriffs, who typically possess golden plumage are customarily taken from the nest almost as soon as they break through the shells of their eggs. Inhabitants of [Balfiera](Lore_Balfiera.md) often pamper, preen, and provide for these quasigriffs. Their fallen feathers are used as fletching by local archers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AQ-7)</sup>

Irastimil was also responsible for experiments on [salamanders](Lore_Salamander.md), [guars](Lore_Guar.md), and [pony guars](Lore_Pony_Guar.md). They created a hybrid of a salamander and a guar as well as a combination of pony guar and salamander during their stay in [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup> Some of the hybrids were stolen by the locals in the [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SPG-2)</sup>
