[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Gavros_Plinius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gavros_Plinius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gavros_Plinius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gavros_Plinius.jpg) Gavros Plinius **Gavros Plinius**, an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [sorcerer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Sorcerer), is a member of the order of the [Synod](Skyrim_Synod.md), and can be found upon entering [Mzulft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft) during [Revealing the Unseen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Revealing_the_Unseen).

You will find Gavros in the very first room of Mzulft, heavily wounded and seemingly dying. His final words are: *"Crystal.... gone... [coughing] Find... Paratus... in Oculory..."* After saying those final words, he dies. He wears [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c), and carries a [research log](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Research_Log) as well as a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mzulft_Key) necessary to go further into the ruins. He is mentioned by his fellow Synod member [Paratus Decimius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Paratus_Decimius), who states that Gavros claimed that the idea of using a crystal in the Oculory was his own.
