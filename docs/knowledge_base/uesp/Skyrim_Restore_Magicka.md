*Alchemy description:* **Restore <mag> points of Magicka.**
**Restore Magicka** is an effect that refills the target's [magicka](Skyrim_Magicka.md). It can be used to replenish magicka lost by casting spells, or to repair damage caused by a [Damage Magicka](Skyrim_Damage_Magicka.md) effect. Restore Magicka will not increase magicka past its base value.

All characters naturally [regenerate magicka](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md) over time at a rate that is based on your maximum magicka, thus magicka is also restored when you [sleep](Skyrim_Sleeping.md) or wait.

## Notes
- Magicka regeneration is also possible using [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md) effects.
- A potion made from any combination of White Caps, Grass Pods, and Red Mountain Flowers actually ravages your magicka instead of restoring it.
