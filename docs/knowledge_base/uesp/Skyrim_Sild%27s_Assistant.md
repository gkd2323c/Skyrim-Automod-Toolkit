[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-npc-Sild%27s_Assistant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sild%27s_Assistant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sild%27s_Assistant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sild%27s_Assistant.jpg) Sild's Assistant **Sild's Assistant**, a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md), lies dead near [Sild the Warlock](Skyrim_Sild_the_Warlock.md) in the depths of [Rannveig's Fast](Skyrim_Rannveig%27s_Fast.md).

She wears [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) while her footwear is randomly determined. There is always a 50% chance of a pair of [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md) and a 50% chance of leveled footwear; from level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md), from level 6-18 it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md), and from level 19 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md).

If you fall into Sild's trap, he will briefly mention her, although he will [mistakenly](Skyrim_Sild_the_Warlock.md#Bugs) refer to her as being male: *"Oh, and don't mind my assistant here. He may have seen better days, but he's still more help to me dead than he was alive."*

## Bugs
- Despite Sild referring to his assistant as male, the body you find is that of a female. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. The corpse now appears as male to fit the dialogue.
