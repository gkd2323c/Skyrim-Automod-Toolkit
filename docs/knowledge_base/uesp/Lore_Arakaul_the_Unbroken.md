Arakaul the Unbroken *An ode to Baloth Bloodtusk's loyal friend*
---
Killed his chief but left his clan,
Humbled not by [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) or [Man](Lore_Man.md),
Unfettered [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) forever.

With oaken shield did he defend,
Guarding [Baloth](Lore_Baloth_Bloodtusk.md) til the end,
Loyal friend forever.

Fought not for nation, coin, or king,
His brotherhood was everything,
Savage Son forever.
