Daedrats are [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) vermin from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) resembling large, ragged [rats](Lore_Rat.md) with glowing blue eyes,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Rat Appearance-2)</sup> that sometimes sneak into [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) through [Oblivion Gates](Lore_Oblivion_Gate.md) or portals.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Schick2015-3)</sup> Some [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) consider them cute despite the fact that Daedrats are considered vermin,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAmb-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jack Dae-4)</sup> so if they catch them they kill them swiftly instead of torturing them, like they do with loose [banekin](Lore_Banekin.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Schick2015-3)</sup> They are fed on by some [vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), such as the [Bloodmist Clan](Lore_Bloodmist_Clan.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOAmb-1)</sup> Their meat is largely gristle, and highly enjoyed by [ogrim](Lore_Ogrim.md) when rancid,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Rat Meat-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONBrihana-6)</sup> but is known to upset [dogs'](Lore_Dog.md) stomachs in even a fresh state.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Kharz-7)</sup>

Daedrats, as many Daedra, can vary in appearance depending on their plane of origin. Daedrats that originate from [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) are scruffy creatures with exposed spines and skulls, blue fur and cold blue eyes.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Rat Appearance-2)</sup> Jackdaw Daedrats are bony rodents that enjoy collecting shiny objects. This behavior is reminiscent of how [Nocturnal's](Lore_Nocturnal.md) [Crow Daedra](Lore_Crow_Daedra.md) act.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Jack Dae-4)</sup> Deadlands Daedrats originate from [Mehrunes Dagon's](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md) [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md), and are distinguished by their fiery red fur.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Dead Dae-8)</sup> Lustrated Daedrats are Daedrats that have been Lustrated in the [Colored Rooms](Lore_Colored_Rooms.md). A notable instance of a time where these Daedrats have come to exist would be the period following the end of Nocturnal's invasion of the Colored Rooms. A number of [Evergloam](Lore_Evergloam.md) Daedrats unknowingly followed Nocturnal's invasion force into the realm. After the invasion was quelled, the Daedrats were rounded up and purified, then banished from the plane.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Lus Dae-9)</sup> The Slate-Skinned Daedrat is a grey Daedrat with black striping and cold blue eyes. They differ from the average Coldharbour Daedrat in appearance. Their appearance stems from their diet, which consists of corpses touched by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md). Scholars seeking to research or oppose the Lord of Brutality often study these rodents for insight.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Sla Ski Dae-10)</sup> Despite being daedra, Daedrat [spirits](Lore_Ghost.md) persisting after death have been documented, such as when the explorer Theldyn Faden killed a Daedrat and it ended up following him home as a ghost.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Ghostly Daedrat-11)</sup>

**Ravenous Rodents** are tiny daedrats that stare with forlorn eyes, considered to be [The Mad God's](Lore_Sheogorath.md) pests. They chitter quietly, and those who listen carefully may be able to understand what they have to say, with great calamity befalling those who listen to their lies.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Ravenous Rodent-12)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-TWot R-13)</sup> When [Uncle Leo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Uncle_Leo) became a guest in the Chateau of the Ravenous Rodent in [Dementia](Lore_Dementia_(place).md), he began to fall in love with the rats there, which Sheogorath regarded as an embarrassment.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ESO-14)</sup> The [Order of the Eye](Lore_Order_of_the_Eye.md) takes serious measures to contain any of the rodents smuggled into Tamriel from the Shivering Isles.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Ravenous Rodent-12)</sup>
