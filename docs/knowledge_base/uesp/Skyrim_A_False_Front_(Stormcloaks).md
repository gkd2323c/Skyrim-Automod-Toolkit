## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get your orders from [Galmar](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md).
2. Locate the [courier](Skyrim_Imperial_Courier.md) at [Four Shields Tavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Four_Shields_Tavern) or [Frostfruit Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostfruit_Inn).
3. Retrieve [Imperial documents](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Documents) from him.
4. Return them to Galmar Stone-Fist.
5. Take the forged documents to [Legate Taurinus Duilis](Skyrim_Legate_Taurinus_Duilis.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md).
6. Return to Galmar Stone-Fist.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Acquiring the documents
[Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) will tell you that you need to deliver false orders to an Imperial Legate in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) but first you intercept and steal plans from an [Imperial courier](Skyrim_Imperial_Courier.md). He tells you that [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md) and [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) are frequented often by Imperial couriers, and you should look in the inns of those places.

Head to Rorikstead or Dragon Bridge and speak to the innkeeper in the local inn. To find out the courier's location, you must either persuade, bribe, or intimidate the innkeeper.

| Option | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| His life is in danger. **(Persuade)** | **Passed**: It is? Well that's different. <br> **Failed**: Yeah, sure it is. You don't fool me. | **Passed**: Find the courier <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| Maybe some gold will help? **(Bribe)** | **Passed**: Well, if you insist... <br> **Failed**: Seems your purse is lighter than you thought. | **Passed**: Find the courier <br> **Failed**: Nothing |
| I don't have time for this... I can get rough with you if I have to. **(Intimidate)** | **Passed**: Now, now! That won't be necessary. | **Passed**: Find the courier |
| Never mind. | Hmph. | Nothing |

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-quest-A_False_Front_%28Stormcloaks%29.jpg/200px-SR-quest-A_False_Front_%28Stormcloaks%29.jpg)](File_SR-quest-A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).jpg.md) [](File_SR-quest-A_False_Front_(Stormcloaks).jpg.md) *"His life is in danger."*
*"It is? Well that's different."* The innkeeper will then tell you that the courier just left, so you could go after them, or you can wait for the courier to come back. It's better to find the courier in the wilderness because you will get a bounty for killing them. The courier may be far from town, so you it might be better to wait for them to come back and then follow them out of the town. Once you find the courier, saying you want the documents will make the courier attack you immediately.You may also pickpocket the documents off of the courier. The courier may also get into a fight with Stormcloaks or wild animals. Stormcloaks and hostile bandits often frequent the courier's route.

### Delivering the documents
Once you have the documents, head back to the Stormcloak camp and give them to Galmar. He will then forge false information and will then tell you to take them to the [Legate](Skyrim_Legate_Taurinus_Duilis.md) in Morthal. Head to Morthal and enter the [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md), the Legate is through the doorway, under the stairs, to the left. Talk to him and give him the documents, if you are not wearing a Legion uniform he will ask about it, but upon explaining yourself he will take the documents and give you five gold. Now report back to Galmar to prepare for [the Battle for Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Snowhawk_(Stormcloaks).md).

## Notes
- This quest and [A False Front (Imperial)](Skyrim_A_False_Front_(Imperial).md) are different radiant versions of the same quest.
- Since Rorikstead is in Whiterun hold, which has already been conquered by the Stormcloaks, you can simply wait for him to show up there and watch the Stormcloak guards slaughter him.
- When you speak to the courier demanding the documents he will become hostile and attack you, but killing him will still count as a murder. The easiest way to avoid the murder charge is to get a little in front of him on the road and trigger the bandits hostilities at Robber's Gorge. They usually capably dispose of him. Without your character triggering the bandit ambush he usually makes his trips unharmed.
- If you are wanted in Hjaalmarch, you will be unable to complete this quest until your bounty is resolved, as the Imperial Legate will try to arrest you when you speak with him.

## Bugs
- Be careful about entering Frostfruit Inn to confront the courier while he is in there. He will usually be sleeping in the right hand room when you enter, and when he rises will stand up on the wrong side of the bed causing him to clip through the wall and even fall off the map.

- To fix this, wait 1 hour in the inn. The courier will then jump to the other side of the bed allowing you to continue with the game as normal.

- Killing the Imperial courier will count as murder, even if he attacks you first. If a nearby NPC witnesses your "murder", you will incur a bounty in Hjaalmarch, Haafingar, or Whiterun Hold. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
