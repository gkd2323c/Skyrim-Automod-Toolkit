Flower King Nilichi was one of the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) kings active prior to or during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). He was responsible for the eradication of a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) tribe, the [Men-of-ge](Lore_Men-of-ge.md), through a great sacrifice to an [Insect God](Lore_Insect_God.md) whose name was lost to history.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TA-1)</sup>

He is considered an infamous Ayleid King, compared to figures such as [Fire King Hadhuul](Lore_Hadhuul.md), [Haromir of Copper and Tea](Lore_Haromir.md), [Gordhaur the Shaper](Lore_Gordhaur_the_Shaper.md), and [Umaril the Unfeathered](Lore_Umaril.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Saromir-2)</sup>
