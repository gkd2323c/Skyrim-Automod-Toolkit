[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/ON-place-Silaseli_Ruins.jpg/200px-ON-place-Silaseli_Ruins.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silaseli_Ruins.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Silaseli_Ruins.jpg) Silaseli Uuril was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) king who ruled from [Silaseli](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silaseli) in northern [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md). Uuril had twenty-three children. His youngest child was Urenenya, who was renowned for her beauty.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup>

An astute and ambitious ruler, Uuril secured his family's power for generations by marrying twenty-two of his children to both rivals and allies. Many suitors traveled from far and near lands to marry Uuril's last unwed child, Urenenya, hoping to become connected to the king.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup> However, Urenenya refused to be wed, defying Uuril.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PUL-2)</sup> Urenenya refused to be married off because she was in love with a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) engineer named Klathzgar.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PUL-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PFN-3)</sup> With great reluctance, Uuril confined Urenenya to a tower at Silaseli, intending to release her only if she agreed to an arranged marriage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup>

After Urenenya fell gravely ill, Uuril had his healers attempt to restore her health, but they could not cure her maladies. Taking pity on his dying daughter, Uuril allowed Urenenya to leave her tower. Soon afterward, Urenenya disappeared, and Uuril never learned her fate. Uuril later died and was buried at Silaseli.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup>
