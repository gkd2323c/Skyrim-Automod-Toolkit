[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dc/SR-creature-Queen_Potema.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Queen_Potema.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Queen_Potema.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Queen_Potema.jpg) Queen Potema gathering power in Potema's Sanctum Queen Potema was the granddaughter of [Uriel Septim II](Lore_Uriel_Septim_II.md) and former Queen of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). In [3E 121](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_121), she launched a rebellion known as the [War of the Red Diamond](Lore_War_of_the_Red_Diamond.md) and overthrew her niece Empress Kintyra II so that her son could become Emperor Uriel Septim III. Although the war ended in [3E 127](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_127), it was another ten years, capped off by a month-long siege at her castle in Solitude, before Potema herself was defeated. She has been described as "unambiguously evil", and is remembered as one of the most dangerous necromancers in the history of Tamriel. For more information, see the [lore article](Lore_Potema.md).

While [investigating a cave](Skyrim_The_Man_Who_Cried_Wolf.md), you will interrupt a ritual to summon her. She is little more than a disembodied voice at this point.

However, some time later it appears the summoning did at least partially work and you are sent to deal with her after one of her undead servants tunnels into the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). The second encounter is when the actual physical entity of Queen Potema will be seen.

This is one of the more unique battles in the game; details can be found in [The Wolf Queen Awakened](Skyrim_The_Wolf_Queen_Awakened.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-creature-Potema%27s_Remains.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Potema%27s_Remains.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Potema%27s_Remains.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Potema%27s_Remains.jpg) Potema's Remains in Potema's Sanctum Her remains consist of a skeleton. Her spirit can respawn several times from her remains, but once she is finally dead you can take [her skull](Skyrim_Potema%27s_Skull.md), which is her link to the present and the only thing tying her to the mortal plane.

Potema's remains wear a [fine raiment](Skyrim_Fine_Raiment.md), along with a [copper and moonstone circlet](Skyrim_Copper_and_Moonstone_Circlet.md). She is armed with a leveled sword enchanted with [shock damage](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md#Shock_Damage).

Aside from her ghost ability, she knows the following spells: [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Lightning Cloak](Skyrim_Lightning_Cloak.md), [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), [Reanimate Corpse](Skyrim_Reanimate_Corpse.md), and [Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md). Additionally, she knows the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md).

## Notes
- She is the subject of the books *[Biography of the Wolf Queen](Skyrim_Biography_of_the_Wolf_Queen.md)* and *[The Wolf Queen, v1](Skyrim_The_Wolf_Queen,_v1.md)* to *[v8](Skyrim_The_Wolf_Queen,_v8.md)*.
- She appears in two different forms, as a wisp (the top infobox) and as a skeleton (the bottom infobox).
- Potema's Remains can be soul trapped during the fight with her. Doing so may prevent the bug in which she respawns from occurring.
- Potema's Remains use the Draugr attack animations and sound effects.
