The Furstock Song *A song about the different breeds of Khajiit* *
---

- CHORUS
- Clever [Dagi](Lore_Dagi.md), agile [Suthay](Lore_Suthay.md), burly [Pahmar](Lore_Pahmar.md), [Senche-raht](Lore_Senche-raht.md),
- When [Azurah](Lore_Azurah.md) gave out presents, she sure gave us a lot,
- Handsome [Cathay](Lore_Cathay.md), nimble [Tojay](Lore_Tojay.md), sneaky [Ohmes](Lore_Ohmes.md) and [Alfiq](Lore_Alfiq.md),
- So many [shapes and sizes](Lore_Furstock.md), that's what makes us unique!

- Now, we all adore the Dagi, who love climbing trees,
- Far better than the [Wood Elves](Lore_Bosmer.md) with their pink and knobby knees,
- They swing between the branches with quickness and real ease,
- As lovely as a feather in a light midsummer breeze.

- CHORUS

- Here's some good advice: just leave the [Senches](Lore_Senche.md) alone,
- Their claws are sharp as razors and their jaws can break your bone,
- They'll rip an [Elf](Lore_Mer.md) to pieces in the blink of an eye,
- So only pick a fight if you are eager to die!

- CHORUS

- Our dear old mum, Azurah, made the Alfiq so small,
- That when they hunker down you just can't see them at all,
- They say that it is magic! Who am I to argue?
- There really is no limit to the things [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) can do!

- CHORUS
