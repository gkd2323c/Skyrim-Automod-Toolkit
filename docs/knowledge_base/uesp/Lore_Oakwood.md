[3E 389](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_389) Oakwood was a town located in southern [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), northeast of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) and southwest of [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md). It was found east of [North Keep](Lore_North_Keep.md) and had a road connection to [Neugrad Watch](Lore_Neugrad_Watch.md) in the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Oakwood was an active settlement, being ruled by Duke Eren. It had a rivalry with [Granitehall](Lore_Granitehall.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup>

No trace of the settlement remained as of [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim-2)</sup> It has been claimed that circa [4E 100-105](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_100-105) the settlement was mysteriously burned down, with there being a fire that started in the sacred grove and rapidly engulfed the entire town. The cause of this alleged fire was never found. Refugees from the town were said to have moved to [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-SINE-3)</sup>
