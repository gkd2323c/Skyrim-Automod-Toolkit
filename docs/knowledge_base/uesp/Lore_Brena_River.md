[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/OB-place-Brena_River.jpg/200px-OB-place-Brena_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Brena_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Brena_River.jpg) The Brena River [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-place-Brena_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Brena_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brena_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Brena_River.jpg) Brena River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Brena River is a fairly short, steep-banked river that flows into the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) just south of the city of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md). The main branch rises in the [Colovian Highlands](Lore_Colovian_Highlands.md) and forms part of the border between [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), flowing westward along the northern edge of the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). It is joined by another branch from within Hammerfell at a point on the border roughly north of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md). The Brena is an important trade route to the interior of the continent, and has occasionally been preyed upon by pirates.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GCGP1-1)</sup>

The [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) complex of [Varlaisvea](Lore_Varlaisvea.md) lies at the mouth of the Brena, on the Hammerfell side of the border.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
