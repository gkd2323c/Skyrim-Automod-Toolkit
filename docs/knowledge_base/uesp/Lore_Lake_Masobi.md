[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4f/MW-place-Lake_Masobi.jpg/200px-MW-place-Lake_Masobi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Masobi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Lake_Masobi.jpg) Lake Masobi Lake Masobi was a lake in the northwest corner of the [Ascadian Isles](Lore_Ascadian_Isles.md). The busy trading village of [Suran](Lore_Suran.md) was on its north and east shores. The Nabia River flowed into the lake in its northeastern corner, connecting it to [Lake Nabia](Lore_Lake_Nabia.md). Lake Masobi, in turn, flowed into the craggy coastline of southeastern [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). It also joined to [Lake Hairan](Lore_Lake_Hairan.md) in the west via a small channel, and [Lake Amaya](Lore_Lake_Amaya_(Vvardenfell).md) lay to the northwest. [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(city).md) was due south. In the days before the [Red Year](Lore_Red_Year.md), it was surrounded by rich and productive agricultural fields, and some [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) mansions dotted its shoreline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Morrowind-1)</sup> Considering the cataclysms of the Red Year,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRY-3)</sup> though, it's unclear if the lake still exists.
