Belharza **the Man-Bull** was the son of the Empress [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md) and her consort, the [demigod](Lore_Demigod.md) [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md), and is believed to be one of the first [minotaurs](Lore_Minotaur.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OM-1)</sup> He succeeded his mother as ruler of the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) upon her death in [1E 266](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_266). Little is known about the emperor's life and reign as most records of the period were destroyed following the rise of the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OM-1)</sup> Private correspondence between Belharza and Alessia is held in the Library of Dusk in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md), though its contents are unspecified.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLo DRB-2)</sup> According to some scholars, the curvature of Belharza's horns (infamously depicted in Tertia Gallus's heretical renderings circa [1E 1300](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1300)) are one of the only aesthetic features that sets him apart from Morihaus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Horns-3)</sup> He was said to have wielded a weapon called the Axe of Belharza.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Campaigns-4)</sup> It was theorized that early Alessian priests might have honored him and his father with sacramental oils.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Oil-5)</sup> It was speculated by some scholars that legends about Belharza were topic of interest even among the [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Rail-6)</sup>

Certain sources erroneously dismiss Belharza's Minotaur origins and assert he was a [Man](Lore_Man.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Oil-5)</sup> who "met his fate at the biting-tips of [Elven](Lore_Mer.md) spears."<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTOM-7)</sup> A surviving document from an Alessian Order sect indirectly implies that Belharza was troubled by large [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) hordes.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-COTF-8)</sup> One controversial interpretation of an ancient tablet fragment called the Belharza Stone asserts that the precursors of the xenophobic Alessian Order may have openly clashed with Belharza, and violently purged the minotaurs from the Empire.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OM-1)</sup>

Belharza's Band was a magically imbued [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) band linked to him.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-BB-9)</sup>

## Notes
- The crown of his mother, Alessia was set with "Twin Red Diamonds, one for Herself and one for Belharza to Come, who was even then Quickening."<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SMDD-10)</sup>
- [Ancestor moths](Lore_Ancestor_Moth.md) that served under Belharza during their corporeal stage were still in use at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
