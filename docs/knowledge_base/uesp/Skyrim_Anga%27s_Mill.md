[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Anga%27s_Mill.jpg/200px-SR-place-Anga%27s_Mill.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Anga%27s_Mill.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Anga%27s_Mill.jpg) Anga's Mill **Anga's Mill** is a small wood mill built along the north bank of the [River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim), west of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). It consists of two houses and a sawmill. The mill is owned by [Aeri](Skyrim_Aeri.md), a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) woman who inherited the sawmill from her father who died in the Great War. A short distance along the western road is the camp where [Ennodius Papius](Skyrim_Ennodius_Papius.md) lives.

## Walkthrough
The mill has two houses, [Aeri's House](Skyrim_Aeri%27s_House.md) just across from the saw mill structure and [Anga's Mill Common House](Skyrim_Anga%27s_Mill_Common_House.md) up the hill to the west. Outside the mill, [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) range freely. There are two [wood chopping blocks](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) and a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md). There are five barrels containing food and [snowberries](Skyrim_Snowberries.md). You can buy [sawn logs](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> here after installing [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) by speaking to Aeri. You can also cut your own for free if she has given permission to do so. Up the hill, two nests with [chickens' eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) are outside the Common House. A [stone quarry](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> can be found here as well.

## Notes
- If you travel west a short distance down the road from the Mill, you will come across the tent that Ennodius Papius sleeps in during his related quest.
- There is a waterfall in the [River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim), due west of Anga's Mill, with a dry alcove behind it. The alcove holds an adept-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox), a barrel containing alchemical ingredients, a lootable skeleton, two quicksilver ingots, a weapon on top of the crate, and a buried lootable chest. A third ingot is underwater a few paces downstream. Nearby there are mudcrabs, Nordic barnacle clusters and a camp. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=102440&y=30783&zoom=17&legacy=true)
- Five [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) veins can be found around the area: - One can be found along the bank of River Yorgrim.
- One lies across the river, opposite the mill.
- Two are located near Ennodius' camp; one to the south and the other to the west.
- One can be found just northwest of Aeri's house.
