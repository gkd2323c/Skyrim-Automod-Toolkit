[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a1/SR-npc-Serana.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Serana.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Serana.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Serana.jpg) Serana **Serana** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire_(class).md#Vampire) found inside a sarcophagus in [Dimhollow Cavern](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Cavern.md), where she is called **Mysterious Woman** until she shares her real name with you. Serana is the long lost daughter of [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) and [Valerica](Skyrim_Valerica.md), and is in possession of an [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll_(Sun).md). Upon introduction, she will know if you are a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) or not, and she will ask you to take her to [her home](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).

Throughout the [Dawnguard questline](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md), Serana accompanies you as an obligatory follower regardless of whether you side with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) or [Volkihar Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md). Taught in the dark arts by her mother, she is a talented necromancer and will often reanimate corpses to assist the two of you in battle. As a magic user and vampire, she uses a variety of spells in battle and wields an [Elven Dagger](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md) for close combat.

In regards to her personality, she possesses a natural defensiveness that doesn't come off as cold. Skillfully, she navigates away from difficult subjects and redirects conversations toward topics you're more inclined to discuss. These defenses stem from her quick temper and intense passion, which she guards against so others won't bear the brunt of them. While she genuinely cares for those who befriend her, she only invests so much as they demonstrate their worthiness. Additionally, she carries a hint of self-deprecating humor and self-awareness. Although intelligent, she maintains a degree of naïvety. Having been sealed in Dimhollow Crypt for centuries, she harbors a curiosity about the world's current state.

## Background
Serana had a very close relationship with her mother growing up. In her youth, she was distant from her father, though never against him in any capacity. By vampire standards, her relationship with her mother was as loving and nurturing as it could be, given their circumstances. Even as a child, hers was a fairly lonely life. Her mother taught her necromancy and other forms of magic, allowing her to become a powerful mage in her own right.

A thousand years ago, her father became obsessed with a prophecy called the [Tyranny of the Sun](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md), which would require the blood of a pure-blooded vampire to block out the sun forever, allowing vampires to reign supreme. To prevent this from happening, Serana's mother sealed her away in Dimhollow Crypt to hide her from her father.

## As a Follower
In many ways, Serana is a unique follower.

### Finding Her
Her main location during gameplay depends on your allegiance. If you join the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md), you can find her at [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md). If you choose the [Volkihar Vampire Clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md), however, you can find her at [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). After completing the Beyond Death quest, she can also be found in Valerica's study. After completing the Dawnguard campaign, she often roams Skyrim in search of adventure. If you find her, you can recruit her, regardless of the location.

### Attire
Serana wears a [Vampire Royal Armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Royal_Armor.md) and a pair of [Vampire Boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). She dons a [unique hood](Skyrim_Serana_Hood.md) when outside, which visually obscures her headgear.

She might wear the attire you place in her inventory, although she completely ignores those included in the [Arcane Accessories](Skyrim_Arcane_Accessories.md) DLC. Her attire choice logic ignores the fact that she has no need for items that bestow cold resistance while she is a vampire.

### Behavior
Serana does not behave like other followers. She does not use one of the four personas of the base game (Imperial, Nord, Orc, or Arrogant). Her feedbacks are playful. While the other followers act as if they are on an invisible leash, Serana behaves more like an independent person. She sticks close to you, but might sit, read, eat, lean on a wall, use crafting stations (anvil, smelter, workbench, arcane enchanter, etc.), cook, or even mine ore veins. All of these actions are visual only; they have no gameplay impact.

You can tell Serana to wait, come along with you, or exchange inventory items, but she might decline. While the Bloodline quest is active, she refuses to exchange inventory items, as she doesn't trust you. In response to a "Wait here" command, she tells you, in no ambiguous terms, that she will continue to follow you, albeit with a distance.

Serana is not interested in joining the Blades, becoming your steward, or marriage. She does, however, react to an [Amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md).

### Combat
Serana is a magic user and wields an [Elven Dagger](Skyrim_Elven_Dagger.md) for close combat. She has the highest health among the game's followers.

She will frequently, if not always, reanimate corpses, but they later turn into ash piles when their duration ends or if they die. Thus, she is less useful if you plan to reanimate extremely powerful magicians as dead thralls, for example [Sild the Warlock](Skyrim_Sild_the_Warlock.md), [Arch-Curate Vyrthur](Skyrim_Arch-Curate_Vyrthur.md), [Orchendor](Skyrim_Orchendor.md), [Malkoran](Skyrim_Malkoran.md), [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](Skyrim_Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven.md), [Malyn Varen](Skyrim_Malyn_Varen.md), or [the Caller](Skyrim_The_Caller.md). Outside of this, her necromancy is powerful enough to reanimate non-humanoid entities.

The spells she uses depend on her level. She has a [Drain Life](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md) spell that gets stronger with higher levels (even if her vampirism is cured). Additionally, in every case she knows the following spells:

| Level | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12–19 | [Raise Zombie](Skyrim_Raise_Zombie.md) | [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) | |
| 20–27 | [Reanimate Corpse](Skyrim_Reanimate_Corpse.md) | [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) | |
| 28–37 | [Reanimate Corpse](Skyrim_Reanimate_Corpse.md) | [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) | [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md) |

Since she is a vampire she will be immune to all diseases, and poisons; and because she is also a Nord she has complete immunity to any frost-based attacks. However, she will also have Weakness to Fire 50% and No Health, Magicka, or Stamina regeneration in sunlight.

The [Healing Hands](Skyrim_Healing_Hands.md) spell does not heal Serana. She requires [Necromantic Healing](Skyrim_Necromantic_Healing.md) instead. Nevertheless, the game has flagged Serana as [Essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md); she never dies.

## As an NPC
During the quest [Chasing Echoes](Skyrim_Chasing_Echoes.md), she will offer to turn you into a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md). After completing the quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md), she can be asked to turn you into a Vampire Lord at any time. If you sided with the Dawnguard, while a Vampire Lord they will refuse any interactions with you, stating that you need to get cured immediately. This starts [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md).

She can also transform any [elven arrows](Skyrim_Elven_Arrow.md) in your possession into [Bloodcursed elven arrows](Skyrim_Bloodcursed_Elven_Arrow.md) after obtaining [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

- **Vyrthur was saying something about vampire blood and the bow...**
- *"It sounds like... that's the last bit of the prophecy. The blood of a Daughter of Coldharbour can corrupt the bow."*
- **Can I use your blood, then?**
- *"Sure... do you have any arrows, though? They would have to be of elven quality, I'd guess."*
- **You're a Daughter of Coldharbour.**
- *"So I am. I guess we could use my blood. Do you have any arrows. Elven arrows. I'm not just going to bleed on any old thing."*

Afterward, she will grant you arrows:

- **I'd like to treat some arrows with your blood.**
- *"Let's just get this over with."*
- **I'm not going to take your blood right now.**
- *"Oh. All right, then."*

If you have insufficient arrows:

- **I'd like to treat some arrows with your blood.**
- *"I think you'll need to find some arrows, first. Only elven ones will work for this, remember?"*

## Curing Serana's Vampirism
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/SR-npc-Serana_04.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Serana_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Serana_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Serana_04.jpg) Serana after she is cured of vampirism If you side with the Dawnguard, you can convince Serana to cure her vampirism after her father's death. However, there is a way to lock yourself out of this line of inquiry: after Serana brings her [Elder Scroll](Skyrim_Elder_Scroll_(Sun).md) to Fort Dawnguard, ask her, "Have you always been a vampire?" During this conversation, if you ask if she has ever thought about getting cured, she will refer to her condition as a gift, insisting that she will never seek a cure for it. You can tell her to seek a cure after her father has been defeated, but she declines and refuses to speak to you about it further.

After [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md), once you've have left Harkon's room, if you are not a vampire yourself, you can ask Serana if she's ever considered being cured. Unless you pick a self-centered response, she will immediately leave to see [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md) for a cure. For the next three days, she will stay with Falion, at which point, she will become human and return to Fort Dawnguard. The conversation goes as follows:

- **Have you thought about getting cured of vampirism?**
- *"No... why?"*

Three options become available:

- **Look what it did to your family.**
- *"Even before that, we weren't the most normal family. We did worship a Daedra, remember?"*
- (✔ Success)
- **You could be your own person again.**
- *"It would be nice not to always be thirsty."*
- (✔ Success)
- **I think I'd feel more comfortable.**
- *"So you take a major decision for me, and make it all about you?"*
- (❌ Fail; terminates conversation)

If the first part of the conversation is successful, two options will become available:

- **If you want to cure yourself, I'll support you.**
- *"I'll speak to Falion. I've heard he knows about these things."*
- (✔ Success)
- **I don't think I can help you with this.**
- *"(Sarcastically) Very supportive!"*
- (❌ Fail; terminates conversation)

If you convince her to cure herself, two options become available. Neither change the outcome:

- **I want to come with you.**
- *"I know you do, but this is something I need to do alone. I'll see you again. Soon."*
- **Good luck.**
- *"I'll see you soon."*

If Serana chooses to become human, she will no longer be able to provide you with bloodcursed arrows nor turn you into a vampire. Her eyes will revert to her mortal blue eyes, although due to a [bug](#Bugs), they will be yellow instead of white on the outside. She will no longer be undead; thus, she can be healed by your Restoration healing spells, she won't be damaged by Restoration combat spells that target undead, and she appears with Detect Life instead of Detect Dead. Her combat spell usage remains the same; she continues to use a vampiric Drain Life attack along with her other attacks.

[![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can reverse the effects of your choices in conversation by typing the following into the console: `Set Papyrus Quest Var DLC1NPCMental Model Told Player She Didnt Want Cure_var False`. You will now be able to convince her to get cured. If she still refuses to talk about being cured, type the following into the console: `Set Papyrus Quest Var DLC1NPCMental Model Cut Off Cure Discussion_var False`. You can force the quest to start by typing the following into the console: `setstage DLC1Serana Cure Self Quest 10`. If the Serana cure quest should become stuck you can use `setstage DLC1Serana Cure Self Quest 100` to finish the quest after stage 10 and have Serana back, without her vampirism.

After she is cured, the following conversation occurs:

- *"There you are. I'm ... I'm back."*
- **You're not a Vampire anymore?**
- *"All clean. I feel like I can breathe again for the first time since I was turned. The world is alive, and so am I, for once."*
- **I was wondering when I'd see you again. **
- *"Here I am. Human as ever. It's like the world's alive again. Just like me."*
- **Come with me.**
- *"I knew you'd miss me."*

## Notes
- Serana is voiced by noted voice actress [Laura Bailey](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Laura_Bailey_(voice_actress)). ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm1154161/)) She would return to voice [Dame Isobel Veloise](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Isobel_Veloise) in *[The Elder Scrolls Online: High Isle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:High_Isle)*.
- Serana's hairstyle is unique to her, and is not available to the player in [character creation](Skyrim_Character_Creation.md) or [appearance changes](Skyrim_Galathil.md).
- Depending on your dialogue choices, at some point during or after [Chasing Echoes](Skyrim_Chasing_Echoes.md), you can ask Serana to marry you. However, she will refuse indefinitely, stating that even walking near a temple scares her. Despite this, she will follow you into any of the temples during normal travel. She will not marry you even if she has been cured of vampirism.
- If Serana is a follower and you have lycanthropy, corpses she has reanimated can still be killed and fed on before they vanish completely. Bloodlust will still be extended and perk progress will still be made.
- While following you, Serana interacts with most activators, which sometimes results in odd behavior. She tends to use any nearby [crafting](Skyrim_Activities.md#Crafting) apparatus, sit in most chairs on her own or even on the floor, chop firewood, mine ore veins, read books, eat food, dance, drink, and clap for bards at taverns. If she has reanimated a corpse, it will join in and perform similar tasks.
- When indoors, Serana's reanimation spell is able to penetrate walls and revive dead bodies you cannot see.
- Even after you finish the Dawnguard main questline, Serana remains an essential character and can never be killed. This can make her a very valuable follower, as she is already quite powerful.
- If Serana is cured of her vampirism, she will still wear her hood outside, and still complain about the weather. She will also still be able to use her [vampiric drain](Skyrim_Vampiric_Drain.md) spell in combat. - Version 3.0.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch) removes the vampiric drain spell from Serana's inventory if she is cured of her vampirism.
- When Serana is initially rescued, she will join you in addition to whatever follower you currently have. However, during the quests Chasing Echoes and Beyond Death, she will replace any follower you currently have active, and will be unable to be dismissed until those quests are over, though the option to do so is present.
- If you're a normal vampire, Serana will still be willing to turn you into a Volkihar vampire. However, she will not offer to do so during the quest Chasing Echoes.
- If you bring Serana to [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md), she will kneel and meditate the same way as the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md).
- If you bring Serana to [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>, she may comment on the island's lack of vegetation.
- Serana cannot be asked to [join the Blades](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md).
- If you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md), it can be challenging to use Beast Form and have the Savage Feeding perk if Serana is your follower as she tends to raise corpses in battle. This can prevent you from feeding unless you manage to feed on raised corpses after they die again but before they turn into a pile of ash.
- If she is wearing her default armor, she will automatically wear or take off her hood depending on where she is. If she is given new armor to wear, then she will not automatically switch back and forth, but will constantly wear her hood if she had it on when she was given the new armor, unless she is given a helmet. She will wear new armor if it has a higher armor stat than Vampire Royal Armor; if she is given new boots or gauntlets to wear, but still has the Vampire Royal Armor, she will continue to wear and take off her hood.
- If she is given tempered Vampire Royal Armor, her displayed inventory for trading will not show it being worn. However, her Damage Resist value in the console will increase by the tempered amount.
- If you decline to become a vampire and finish the Dawnguard expansion, after killing Harkon, his sword can be used by you, but only without its enchantments active. The best option to take advantage of the absorption enchantments is to provide it to Serana, the only vampiric follower a Dawnguard can have.
- During the quest [Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md), she will refuse to take any orders from you or trade items with you. The dialogue options are still there, however. She will follow orders issued by long-pressing the action button from afar. This can be used to give her equipment.
- In the Soul Cairn, she does not have the speech option to 'do something for you'. However, if you hold down the speech action key while pointing the cursor just in front of her feet on the floor, she will then be able to perform actions upon your request like other followers. Just don't tell her to 'wait' or she'll get bugged.
- The dialogue to start Serana on the path to curing her vampirism is only available if she is following you. If she is no longer a follower and you speak with her, the necessary options will not appear.
