[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/SR-npc-Raen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Raen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Raen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Raen.jpg) Raen **Raen** is a dead [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) found in the first part of [Halldir's Cairn](Skyrim_Halldir%27s_Cairn.md). Her body can be found with her dead friends, [Vidgrod](Skyrim_Vidgrod.md) and [Agrius](Skyrim_Agrius.md), next to the magical cairn. According to [Agrius's Journal](Skyrim_Agrius%27s_Journal.md) she and her friends were kicked out of the [Knifepoint](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Ridge.md) Bandit Gang and took refuge in Halldir's Cairn where they were possessed by [Halldir](Skyrim_Halldir.md). She was the first to sacrifice herself to him.

She wears a randomly determined set of [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance she will wear [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md), and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ for both shoes and armor; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, she has a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md), while up to level 5 there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), from level 6 it can also be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md), and from level 19 it can also be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of her carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

Her one-handed weapon is random and leveled as well (up to elven quality at level 22) and can be either a [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), a [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) or a [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md). She also carries a bit of random loot, food and drink, with a slim chance of a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and/or some gold.

Raen's body, along with her two friends, is consumed by Halldir to make his three elemental copies during your fight with him.
