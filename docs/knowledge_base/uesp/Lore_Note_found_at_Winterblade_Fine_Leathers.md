[![ON-concept-Medium Nord armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/0e/ON-concept-Medium_Nord_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Medium_Nord_armor.png)
- Pa,

- I'm not cut out to make armor. I'm sorry, but I hate everything about it! I hate skinning the [stags](Lore_Deer.md) and [sabre cats](Lore_Sabre_Cat.md) and [bears](Lore_Bear.md), I hate the stink of making the leather, and I hate the stitching and studding. I know you wanted me to carry on the family tradition, but I just can't do it anymore. It's time for me to follow my own dream. I took my own gold and I've set out to learn magic like I always wanted. I'm going to join the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md), and you can't stop me. I'll send money for you and Ma as soon as I get on my feet. You'll see; I'll make you proud even if I do hate making armor!

- - Thaman
