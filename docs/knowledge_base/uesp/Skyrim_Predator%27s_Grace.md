[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg/200px-SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Predator%27s_Grace.jpg) Predator's Grace Predator's Grace is a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) that are found next to a chest, above a waterfall, to the right of the [Hag's End](Skyrim_Hag%27s_End.md) top level. These unique boots fully [muffle](Skyrim_Muffle_(effect).md) the sound of movement the wearer makes, while also increasing the rate of [stamina regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Stamina.md) by one percent. Predator's Grace can be [tempered](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Notes
- Strangely, the boots increase stamina regeneration by only 1%. This might have been a coding error, as both enchantment effects are internally coded to have a magnitude of 1 pt, which (inconsistently) means 100% for Muffle and 1% for Regenerate Stamina. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.5, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=24573)) The Regenerate Stamina effect is changed to 100%.
- Predator's Grace also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Legendary_Encounters) in [Skyrim - The Adventure Game](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim TAG:Skyrim_-_The_Adventure_Game).
