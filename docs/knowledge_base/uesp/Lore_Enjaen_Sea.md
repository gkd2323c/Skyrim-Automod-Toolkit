[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7d/SI-place-Enjaen_Sea.jpg/200px-SI-place-Enjaen_Sea.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Enjaen_Sea.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Enjaen_Sea.jpg) The Enjaen Sea as seen from [Fain](Lore_Fain.md) The **Enjaen Sea**, much like all things in [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s realm, is the second half of a greater whole: the vast ocean that surrounds the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). It encompasses the eastern half of the Isles and is estimated to converge into the [Emean Sea](Lore_Emean_Sea.md) somewhere between the peninsulas of [Heretic's Horn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Heretic%27s_Horn) and Madgod's Boot in the south, and Saints Watch and the Isle of Flame in the north. The Enjaen coast borders the entire run of the Jester's Spine Mountains and is dotted with a few campsites, as well as forming an inlet that neighbors [Deepwallow](Lore_Deepwallow.md) and the capital, [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The seawater of the Enjaen contains trace [metals](Lore_Metals.md) which can be detected in the [salt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salt) crystals via magical examination. An [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) admixture of Enjaen saltwater can be used in the [smithing](Lore_Smithing.md) of chain links for jewelry; these Enjaen Saltchain Links are particularly transfixing, and are used to craft [Daedric](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md) medallions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
