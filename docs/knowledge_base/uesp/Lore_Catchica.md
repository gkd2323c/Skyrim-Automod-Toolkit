Catchica was a notorious poisoner from [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), active during the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and early [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She hailed from the [Orma](Lore_Orma.md), a tribal society of eyeless [Men](Lore_Men.md) known for their mastery of other senses. By [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920), she had become an experienced and elderly woman.

In the popular historical fiction *[2920, The Last Year of the First Era](Lore_2920,_The_Last_Year_of_the_First_Era.md)*, Catchica is depicted as being present at the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) camp in [Caer Suvio](Lore_Caer_Suvio.md) during the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md) in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920). While at the camp, [Prince Juilek Cyrodiil](Lore_Juilek.md) was poisoned just before the Battle of Bodrum, and she was accused of the crime by Potentate [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md), who sought to cover up his involvement.

Later, Catchica worked at a hot springs resort in [Mir Corrup](Lore_Mir_Corrup.md), where she devised a scheme to falsely implicate the Emperor's mistress in a plot to poison him. The mistress inadvertently crossed paths with Catchica and was subsequently arrested by the Imperial Guard.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
