[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/SR-npc-Mena.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mena.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mena.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mena.jpg) Mena **Mena** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) living in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md) with her husband [Enmon](Skyrim_Enmon.md). She has a daughter, named [Fjotra](Skyrim_Fjotra.md), who has been taken captive by a group of [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md).

Mena leads a very simple life at Karthwasten, waking up at 7am and going to sweep her porch for 12 hours straight until she finally quits at 7pm and heads inside for some relaxation until her bedtime at 12pm.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0), a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd), and a [cowl](Skyrim_Cowl.md). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Enmon%27s_House.md) to [Enmon's house](Skyrim_Enmon%27s_House.md) as well as a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md)**: Find a new [Sybil](Skyrim_Fjotra.md) for the [Temple of Dibella](Skyrim_Temple_of_Dibella.md).

## Dialogue
When spoken to she can say:

- "*Strangers are nothing but trouble.*"
- "*[Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md)'s in charge of Karthwasten. You should bother him.*"
- "*What do you want? Can't people leave us alone?*"
- *"Karthwasten has enough problems. Leave us alone."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### The Heart of Dibella
You can ask her about Fjotra:

- **I'm looking for a young girl who lives around here.**
- *"Oh, I... I'm sorry. I just can't... you'll have to speak to my husband."*

Before stage 70 of [The Heart of Dibella](Skyrim_The_Heart_of_Dibella.md), the following 2 scenes might take place.

**Mena**: *"But why did they take her? What could they want with our little girl?"*
**Enmon**: *"I don't know. The guards are out looking for her, now. They'll be back soon."*
**Mena**: *"You keep saying that. What if she's gone forever? What if they've done something to her?"*

**Enmon**: *"Are you all right, dear?"*
**Mena**: *"The house is so... empty, Enmon... and quiet."*

When you're on stage 70 or afterwards, these 2 different scenes will play instead.

**Enmon**: *"Are you all right, dear? I thought I saw a tear in your eye a moment ago."*
**Mena**: *"Oh, I'm fine, Enmon. I just... I still miss her."*
**Enmon**: *"I miss her, too, but I'm glad she's safe."*
**Mena**: *"So am I. I hope she's happy at the temple."*

**Mena**: *"Enmon, dear, do you have any regrets? About letting her go?"*
**Enmon**: *"No... no, we did the right thing. The temple will take good care of her, and one day she'll be helping all of Skyrim."*
**Mena**: *"You're right. She'll be a great Sybil, won't she?"*
**Enmon**: *"Of course she will. She's our daughter, after all."*
