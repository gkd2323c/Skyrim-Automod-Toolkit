# Hapsleet

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Black_Marsh_%28Anthology%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Black_Marsh_(Anthology).jpg) Black Marsh, the homeland of the Hapsleet Hapsleet are a breed of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) that live in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Hapsleet have some distinct differences in their appearance compared to the Argonians most commonly seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). Although little is known of their behavior, culture and appearance, their existence was known to scholars during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOBR-1)</sup>
