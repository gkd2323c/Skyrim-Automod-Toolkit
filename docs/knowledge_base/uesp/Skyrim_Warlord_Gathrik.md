[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik.jpg) Warlord Gathrik [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Warlord_Gathrik_02.jpg) Gathrik as a dragon priest **Warlord Gathrik** is the end boss of the Nordic ruin [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md). Depending on level, he is either a [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) or a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md). He is also accompanied by other [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) or draugr. He is found sitting on a throne facing the [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) for the [Become Ethereal](Skyrim_Become_Ethereal.md) [shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md). On the back of the throne is the [Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls](Skyrim_Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.md), which can be disenchanted for its unique [Fiery Soul Trap](Skyrim_Fiery_Soul_Trap.md) enchantment.

During the quest [Coming of Age](Skyrim_Coming_of_Age.md), [Beem-Ja](Skyrim_Beem-Ja.md) and [Salma](Skyrim_Salma.md) will help you explore Ironbind Barrow. After defeating Gathrik, Beem-Ja reveals that he is trying to absorb Gathrik's power, and needs to use you as a blood sacrifice. Killing Beem-Ja prevents Gathrik's power from being absorbed.

## Related Quests
- **[Coming of Age](Skyrim_Coming_of_Age.md)**: Help a [daring adventurer](Skyrim_Salma.md) and [her companion](Skyrim_Beem-Ja.md) retrieve their riches.

## Notes
- Warlord Gathrik is 1.25x as large as other draugr.
