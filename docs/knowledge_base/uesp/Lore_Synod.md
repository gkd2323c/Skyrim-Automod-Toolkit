# Synod

The Synod is one of the organizations formed after the dissolution of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) at the start of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the other being the [College of Whispers](Lore_College_of_Whispers.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:40</sup> The two groups are rivals, and constantly strive to earn the favor of the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md). The Synod played a part in defending the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) from [Umbriel](Lore_Umbriel.md) circa [4E 48](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_48).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup>

Unlike the College of Whispers, the Synod continued the Guild's ban on [Necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md), and has also banned at least some other aspects of [Conjuration](Lore_Conjuration.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRFaralda-3)</sup> The Synod does not share knowledge quite as freely as the Mages Guild once did. One might have a chance of learning certain spells after a few years of paying the organization's dues.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:39</sup> The Synod uses a special system of encryption that makes use of some symbols from [Tamrielic](Lore_Tamrielic.md). It is a specific system that, once memorized, can make deciphering it effortless. For instance, keys to a particular part of a message may be found in the next passage. Larger parts may also represent whole ideas, like a spell, artifact, or certain energies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup><sup>:197</sup>

The Synod is headed by a Grand Council. Ranks within the Synod include Attendant and First Adjunct. One of the divisions within the Synod is the Binder's Conclave, which enchanted a focusing crystal for use at the Oculory of [Mzulft](Lore_Mzulft.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RL-4)</sup> Both the College of Whispers and the Synod have attempted to form alliances with the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md), but despite pressures the independent organization has remained neutral.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRFaralda-3)</sup> [Hierem](Lore_Hierem.md), the former minister of Emperor [Titus Mede I](Lore_Titus_Mede_I.md) was a member of the Synod,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup><sup>:198</sup> and reportedly held vast influence over it.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LOS-2)</sup><sup>:96</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the Synod attempted to use the Oculory of Mzulft to locate [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md) of great magical power, but the [Eye of Magnus](Lore_Eye_of_Magnus.md) prevented the Oculory from working correctly. The Eye was being held at the College of Winterhold at the time, which may have embittered relationships with the Synod.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SRUnseen-5)</sup>

A Synod researcher, Yvonne Bienne, authored *[Catalogue of Weapon Enchantments](Lore_Catalogue_of_Weapon_Enchantments.md)* and *[Catalogue of Armor Enchantments](Lore_Catalogue_of_Armor_Enchantments.md)*. She is known to have taught students (such as Severio Scerius) in the art of enchanting.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> A member of the Synod named Lazarum, who lived in [4E 8](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_8), invented a spell to allow people to fly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIC-1)</sup><sup>:39</sup>
