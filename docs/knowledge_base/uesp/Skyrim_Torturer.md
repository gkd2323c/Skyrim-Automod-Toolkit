[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b5/SR-npc-Torturer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torturer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer.jpg) Torturer The **Torturer** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [destruction mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Destruction) who practices his art on behalf of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) in its efforts to wipe out the [Stormcloak rebels](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md). You will encounter him in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) shortly after entering it during the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md).

He wears a set of [Imperial light armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Armor) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Imperial_Light_Bracers), along with a [torturer's hood](Skyrim_Torturer%27s_Hood.md). He wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and, being a mage, uses the [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md) and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md) spells.

If you entered the keep with [Ralof](Skyrim_Ralof.md), the torturer and his [assistant](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torturer%27s_Assistant) will turn hostile on sight.

If you entered the keep with [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md), you will encounter the torturer and his assistant battling two Stormcloak soldiers amidst the corpses that litter the room. Should he survive, he will immediately engage in conversation with Hadvar:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/SR-npc-Torturer_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torturer_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer_02.jpg) *"You have no authority over me, boy."* **Torturer**: *"You fellows happened along just in time. These boys seemed a bit upset at how I've been entertaining their comrades."*
**Hadvar**: *"Don't you even know what's going on? A dragon is attacking Helgen!"*
**Torturer**: *"A dragon? Please. Don't make up nonsense. Although, come to think of it, I did hear some odd noises coming from over there."*
**Hadvar**: *"Come with us. We need to get out of here."*
**Torturer**: *"You have no authority over me, boy."*
**Hadvar**: *"Didn't you hear me? I said the keep is under attack!"*
**Assistant**: *"Forget the old man. I'll come with you."*

At this point, Hadvar will notice a dead [mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mage) locked in a cell and say:

**Hadvar**: *"Wait a second. Looks like there's something in this cage."*
**Torturer**: *"Don't bother with that. Lost the key ages ago. Poor fellow screamed for weeks."*
**Hadvar**: *"See if you can get it open with some picks. We'll need everything we can get."*
**Torturer**: *"Sure, take all my things. Please."*

Should you approach him, he will simply say, *"Leave me to my work. Unless you plan to stay and help?"* When you leave with Hadvar (and the assistant if he is alive), he will just comment, *"There's no way out that way, you know..."*

Should you murder him and/or his assistant in cold blood, Hadvar will not turn hostile even if you do this right in front of him, nor will it incur a bounty or any other negative consequence. In fact, Hadvar might even subtly encourage your actions by commenting, *"These bastards call themselves Imperial Legionnaires..."*
