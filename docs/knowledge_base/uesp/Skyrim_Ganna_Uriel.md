[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/SR-npc-Ganna_Uriel.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ganna_Uriel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ganna_Uriel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ganna_Uriel.jpg) Ganna Uriel **Ganna Uriel** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [lumberjack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lumberjack) and miner found in [Kynesgrove](Skyrim_Kynesgrove.md) and the [Steamscorch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine) nearby. Ganna and her sister, [Gemma Uriel](Skyrim_Gemma_Uriel.md), came to Kynesgrove to start a lumber mill but after the mill was finished they found out the trees were sacred, and they were not allowed to cut them. Losing all their money in building the now useless mill, they earn a living by mining at the Steamscorch Mine in Kynesgrove. Ganna will [buy](Skyrim_Chop_Wood.md) [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md) for its full value.

In order to pay the debts from the failed lumber project, Ganna reluctantly works the mine with her sister, and hacks away with her [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe) every day between 8am and 5pm. She eats two one-hour meals a day, at 7am and 5pm, at the [Braidwood Inn](Skyrim_Braidwood_Inn.md). Like most other residents of Kynesgrove, she sleeps in a tent in the neighboring camp, every night between 10pm and 6am. At all other times she wanders around the camp or spends time at the inn.

Ganna wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd) and [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
When you approach her, she will tell the full story through her greetings:

- *"Seen the remnants of the grove? That... well, that was me. Me and my sister."*
- *"Came here to start a lumber mill. Who knew the Nords cared about a bunch of old trees?"*
- *"But... well, they did. Sacred, they said. So we work the mines until we've worked it off. Fair enough, I guess."*

She elaborates further if you ask her about her misfortune:

- **Are things not going well for you?**
- *"We got the mill up and running before we found out that the big stand of trees on the hill was sacred. Now we can't cut them, and we can't afford to buy another mill somewhere else. So we scrounge whatever we can from around the tundra."*

## Notes
- Ganna and Gemma appear to switch personalities when talking to each other. While Gemma is naturally cynical and Ganna is more cheerful, this appears to be the other way around in their scenes.
- While only ever hinted at in dialogue, the mill the sisters built was seemingly meant to appear in-game. The [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit) identifies the bank of the river just down the hill from Kynesgrove as `Kynesgrove Frostbark Lumber Mill Exterior`. There are no other references to this **Frostbark Lumber Mill** to be found anywhere, but it can be assumed this mill belonged to the Uriel sisters.
