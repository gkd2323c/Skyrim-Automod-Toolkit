## Walkthrough
The towers are at the top of a series of stone staircases and winding paths interspersed with landings occupied by the Forsworn camped at [Red Eagle Redoubt](Skyrim_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.md). The entrance to the towers is a level-locked iron door which can be unlocked with the [Red Eagle Tower key](Skyrim_Red_Eagle_Tower_Key.md) carried by the Forsworn Briarheart of the redoubt. Inside is a copy of *[The Legend of Red Eagle](Skyrim_The_Legend_of_Red_Eagle.md)* and a random pair of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Gauntlets.md) on a sideboard opposite. A flight of stairs to the right ascends to a doorway outside to a wooden ramp up to the next level, where a raised wooden bridge is on the right and a doorway is to the left. Just inside on the right is a chain that lowers the bridge. At the back of the room behind a flight of wooden stairs is an [urn](Skyrim_Urn.md), while the stairs lead up to another level with more stairs up to the highest level; both of these levels are empty.

Once you have lowered the wooden bridge, you may cross to a second tower to the east. On the far side is a doorway ahead and a wooden ramp down to the left. Through the door, the room contains a novice-locked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) and a flight of wooden stairs leading up, with another flight above these leading to the top; again, these levels are both empty. Descending via the ramp brings you to a doorway with a broken sideboard opposite holding a random [potion of magicka](Skyrim_Potion_of_Minor_Magicka.md) and a random empty [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md). There are also stairs leading down on the right as you enter. At the bottom is a pile of rubble with a random [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) among it, with an iron door opposite secured by a bar on this side preventing entrance from the other side.

## Notes
- This location cannot be independently [cleared](Skyrim_Dungeons.md#Clearing). It will only show up as such when the boss-level creatures in Red Eagle Redoubt have been killed, as the game considers these zones one unit for the purpose of calculating clears. - Sundered Towers itself will never add to your count of cleared locations, and therefore does not contribute to the [Delver](Skyrim_Delver.md) achievement.
- Although the door bar prevents easy access to Sundered Towers from the plateau, this can be circumvented by climbing a rock ramp opposite the third tree south of the barred door, then jumping up more rocks to reach the tower with the bridge release chain. This approach allows [Red Eagle Ascent](Skyrim_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.md#Red_Eagle_Ascent) to be traversed downward instead of upward.

## Bugs
- The key might not be on the corpse of the Briarheart.
- The book may clip through the pillar on the altar, making it irretrievable. <sup>**?**</sup> - Cast an explosive area-of-effect spell such as [Fireball](Skyrim_Fireball.md), and the book should reveal itself, allowing you to grab it.
- Killing and looting the Briarheart may fail to complete the corresponding quest objective for [Repairing the Phial](Skyrim_Repairing_the_Phial.md). <sup>**?**</sup> - Kill and loot the Briarheart before talking to [Quintus Navale](Skyrim_Quintus_Navale.md), which should mark the quest objective as complete. Then, return to Quintus and proceed with the quest.
