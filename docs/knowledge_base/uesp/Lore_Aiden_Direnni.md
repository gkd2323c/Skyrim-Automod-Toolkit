# Aiden Direnni

Prince Aiden Direnni was a member of the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) Clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PAR-1)</sup> An Altmer folk-hero, he appears in myths as the last king of a heroic line.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOOrthuna-2)</sup>

Often spoken of alongside his cousins [Ryain Direnni](Lore_Ryain_Direnni.md) and [Raven Direnni](Lore_Raven_Direnni.md), Aiden won many battles against the [Alessian Empire](Lore_Alessian_Empire.md) in [1E 480](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_480).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DRD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TFL-4)</sup> During this time he reluctantly invoked the [Rights Charter](Lore_Rights_Charter.md), allowing him to conscript his [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) subjects into the war.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ALo SS-5)</sup> His crowning achievement was leading Direnni forces to victory against the Alessians in the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md) in [1E 482](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_482), ending their ambitions of conquering [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Once-6)</sup> The [Day of Release](Lore_Day_of_Release.md) was celebrated thereafter by the people of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) to commemorate his victory.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONDOR-7)</sup>

Over three thousand years later, around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), his broken sword was recovered by the [Merethic Society](Lore_Merethic_Society.md) to be displayed in their museum in [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONMC-8)</sup>
