[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-npc-Stenvar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Stenvar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stenvar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Stenvar.jpg) Stenvar at his table in Candlehearth Hall **Stenvar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior2H.md) and a mercenary living in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He can usually be found on the second floor of [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md).

Stenvar wears a full set of [steel armor](Skyrim_Steel_Armor.md), including a pair of steel gauntlets (two variants), and a pair of steel boots (two variants). He is equipped with an [iron greatsword](Skyrim_Iron_Greatsword.md).

Upon hiring him as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), Stenvar becomes a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md). He can also be brought to [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) to be recruited as a member of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md).

## Dialogue
Stenvar may greet you with:

- *"Well, look who it is. Been keeping out of trouble much? I hope not!"*
- *"If you need some skulls crushed, I'm still available for hire."*

When told to follow, he will eagerly say:

- *"Good! Let's go find something to fight"*

But when told to wait, he will be more sedate:

- *"Patience isn't exactly my strong suit, but alright."*

And when told to leave, he will jibe:

- *"You're letting me go? What's wrong, is my skill with a blade making you look bad? Hah! I'm kidding, friend. Until we meet again!"*

If you ask him to trade items, he will half-joke:

- *"Sure, feel free to hand me all the gold you want."*

Stenvar seems to dislike caves as when you are in one he comments on how he can't stand the chill; when you are outside the entrance of one he says:

- *"A cave, dark and dangerous."*
