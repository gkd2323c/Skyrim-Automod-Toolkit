[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-place-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg/200px-SR-place-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg) Solitude Blacksmith **Solitude Blacksmith** is a smithy in the western side of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

It is worked by [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md) and [Heimvar](Skyrim_Heimvar.md). The interior is quite large, and there is a decent number of things that can be stolen. [Heimvar](Skyrim_Heimvar.md) can usually be found at a grindstone downstairs.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The blacksmiths are found within the grounds of [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md), just outside the walls. It is found at the top of a ramp leading up from the market area. From the top of the ramp turn to the right. On the end of the wall separating the ramp from the blacksmiths is a [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Loot. To the right of the entrance is a cart containing several [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), and a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd). Further along is the [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) where Beirand can usually be found working. At the rear, against the wall is a wooden table with several items of Beirand's stock that will disappear when purchased. In addition to the forge there is a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) and [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) as well as several barrels of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) all around the forge area.

### Solitude Blacksmith
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a0/SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith.jpg) Heimvar working on the grindstone Upon entering you are in a wide room with a central pillar and a door to the residence opposite. On the right is a firepit, with a stone wall around it. There are three [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot) on the top of the wall and a grindstone beside it. Above the firepit is a rack holding two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). On your right as you enter, against the south wall is a barrel containing foodstuffs and eight [iron ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ingot) on top. Beside this is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, five potatoes, a cabbage and a couple of loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread). In an alcove to the east are two large piles of firewood and a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md).

In the northeastern corner are two small units holding a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), two [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips) and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). On the north wall are two wall shelves holding fifteen iron ingots, two silver ingots and four [steel ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot). To the left of these is the door to the residence and to the left of that is a small unit containing clothes with a rack holding two [elves ears](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids. Further along the wall is a barrel of foodstuffs and a set of shelves holding four leather strips and two pieces of [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) and in the northwestern corner a stack of more barrels containing food.

Through the door into the residence on the left is a set of shelves holding several cheese wheels, a cabbage, five potatoes and a [potion of regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Regeneration). On the right is a table and chairs set for two, with two [cups](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cup), two portions of [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), two [seared slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Seared_Slaughterfish), some bread, an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) and a bulb of garlic. Above the table are three wall shelves holding two [goat cheese wheels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wheel), several cups and a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton). Directly opposite the door against the northeast wall is an alcove containing several barrels of food, a table holding two leather strips, three iron ingots and a cabbage, with an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) underneath and a bale of straw behind. Beside the table is basket containing two cabbages and five potatoes.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Solitude_Blacksmith_02.jpg) Heimvar's room To the east is a door into Heimvar's bedroom, which contains a tall wardrobe on the right as you enter, against the north wall a narrow table holding an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a [bowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bowl) containing a [deathbell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell) flower and bunch of tundra cotton. Above this is a wall shelf holding three common books. Against the east wall is an empty set of small shelves. There is an unowned single bed in the southeastern corner with a wall shelf above on the east wall holding two leather strips. There is an open crate at the foot of the bed containing a bunch of tundra cotton and to the right of the bed is a table holding a cup.

The stairs up to the top floor are to the west, with a barrel of food on the left of them. At the top of the stairs against the north wall is a small cupboard with a [jug](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug) on top. Opposite the top of the stairs is a door into Beirand's bedroom. To the right of this is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand, with a narrow table to the right of this, holding a bowl of bread, a plate holding a [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese) and an iron dagger. Above these is a wall shelf holding one iron and one steel ingot. Against the south wall is a bookcase holding a [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health), nine common books, a [flute](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flute) and a [drum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Drum). In front of this is a low table and chair holding a common book, a [goblet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goblet) and a [plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Plate) of bread and cheese.

Beirand's bedroom consists of a single unowned bed in the southeastern corner, a wardrobe on your left as you enter the room, a small unit against the east wall holding a level-locked [strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Strongbox) and a plate of bread and cheese. In front of this is a low table and chair holding the [business ledger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Business_Ledger), with a wooden bench against the north wall. Further along the wall is a narrow table holding an iron dagger and bowl containing a bunch of tundra cotton. Above this is a wall shelf holding a bowl of [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Refugees](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Refugees)*. Against the west wall is a tall set of shelves holding two [slaughterfish scales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Slaughterfish_Scales), a bowl of [glow dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glow_Dust), two bottles of [frostbite venom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostbite_Venom), a deathbell flower, a bowl of void salts and a basket containing two bunches of [lavender](Skyrim_Lavender.md).

## Notes
- In [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), [Radiant Raiment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Radiant_Raiment) was located in this building.
- With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, this shop is an excellent place to stock up on iron for [housebuilding](Skyrim_Construction.md) purposes.

## Bugs
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The Blacksmith sign when ran and jumped into may make you suddenly end up in a large body of water with nothing around you. When checked on the map it tilts the map upwards as if you were in the sky. Has also occurred on Playstation 3 and can be fast traveled away from.
- [Heimvar](Skyrim_Heimvar.md) is officially the backup blacksmith for Solitude Blacksmith. However, Heimvar is not set up properly, meaning that if [Beirand](Skyrim_Beirand.md) dies there is no longer anyone who will provide blacksmith merchant services in Solitude. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
- Solitude Blacksmith isn't correctly flagged as a store or dwelling, and is also missing the TGBusiness keyword for Thieves Guild radiant quests even though it has all the required parts in place. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
