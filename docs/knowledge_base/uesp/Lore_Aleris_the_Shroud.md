Aleris the Shroud *A record of the first champion of the Blessed Crucible*
---
Aleris, the first Champion of the Blessed Crucible, held that title for four decades. She was renowned for her skill with [illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) [magic](Lore_Magic.md) and her deft swordplay. Few were able to match her skill in the arena or her favor with the crowd.

Usurped: None

Defeated By: [Crow Bringer](Lore_Crow_Bringer.md)
