—Rigurt the Brash<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTB-1)</sup> Vigli Storm-Breast was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) thane and diplomat from [Eastern Skyrim](Lore_Eastern_Skyrim.md) who served as the leader of the Nord Cultural Exchange and was active during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTLG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/ON-place-Windhelm.jpg/200px-ON-place-Windhelm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Windhelm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Windhelm.jpg) Windhelm [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/ON-crown_store-Rigurt_the_Brash.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Rigurt_the_Brash.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Rigurt_the_Brash.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Rigurt_the_Brash.jpg) Rigurt the Brash, Vigli's subordinate and later his successor Little is known about Vigli's past, but at some point he achieved the positon of a Thane, and became leader of the Nord Cultural Exchange.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTLG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup> Since King [Jorunn](Lore_Jorunn.md) initiated the exchange to help unify the various factions of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ysmair-4)</sup> Vigli was appointed its leader no earlier than [2E 572](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_572).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-GTEP-5)</sup>

He operated from [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md) as of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), frequently sending Rigurt on diplomatic missions to various places, often as soon as Rigurt returned from the last one.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTLG-2)</sup> Vigli was convinced of Rigurt's inaptitudes,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTB-1)</sup> and yet, he sent him on diplomatic missions to [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTLG-2)</sup> [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTB-1)</sup> and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup> Despite this, Rigurt regularly reported back from these assignments.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTLG-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CF-3)</sup> Rigurt was also active in other regions, including [Western Skyrim](Lore_Western_Skyrim.md), and [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md) where he was sent by King Jorunn himself, and [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md), on a mission from the Ebonheart Pact. Surprisingly, all these ventures often ended in success, despite the odds.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTB-1)</sup>

Later during the same year, Rigurt the Brash, who had previously held the title of the Ambassador-at-Large and Leader of the Glorious Expedition for the Nord Cultural Exchange,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RTB-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MTCV-6)</sup> was elevated to the position of leader of the entire Nord Cultural Exchange by the time he visited the [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md). The exact circumstances behind this promotion were unclear.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BTB-7)</sup>
