Lilytongue *Notes on an ancient Aldmeri harp*
---
Master harpists played the [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmeri.md) harp Lilytongue in the High Court of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) since time immemorial. Lilytongue was the instrument used in some of the most enchanting arias produced by the great composer Rolmelval. The harp disappeared during the [War of the Uvichil](Lore_War_of_the_Uvichil.md) and was presumed destroyed. A new royal harp was commissioned to replace it.

Lilytongue resurfaced in the last century after being lost for more than five hundred years. It now sits in a place of honor in the [Bards College](Lore_Bards_College.md) as an example of the history, eminent beauty, and unparalleled artistry of the [High Elf](Lore_High_Elf.md) crafters.
