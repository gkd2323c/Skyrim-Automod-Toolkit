- [Drifa](Skyrim_Drifa.md),

- I was wondering if you could speak to [Maramal](Skyrim_Maramal.md) about perhaps presiding over a [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) ceremony for myself and [Keerava](Skyrim_Keerava.md). She's the love of my life and I wish to be with her forever, but only if we can be wed in traditional [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) fashion. Traditional Argonian bonding ceremonies are somewhat long and complex, involving unique rituals spoken in our native language. I'd be more than happy to provide a book describing what I'm looking for as long as Maramal doesn't mind.

- [Talen-Jei](Skyrim_Talen-Jei.md)
