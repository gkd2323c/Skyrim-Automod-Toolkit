[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SI-place-Fain.jpg/200px-SI-place-Fain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Fain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Fain.jpg) Fain at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Fain is a ruin in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), located on one of the peaks of the Jester's Spine Mountains.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup> Like the other ancient ruins that dot the realm, Fain was originally built by the [Predecessors](Lore_Predecessors.md), mortal inhabitants of the Isles whose civilization was wiped out during a previous [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP-2)</sup>

The ruins were once home to a group of [Heretics](Lore_Heretics.md) who worshipped an unholy being known as [Gyub](Lore_Gyub.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GLot P-3)</sup> The Heretics sacrificed "volunteers" to Gyub by dangling them over the Pit of Fain (a root tunnel which drops vertically down the center of the mountain), reciting a prayer to Gyub, and dropping the sacrifice into the Pit, never to be seen again.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GLot P-3)</sup> There is no evidence of Gyub's existence; the sacrifices were likely eaten by the many beasts which lived in the root caves at the bottom of the Pit. Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the Heretics here were preparing for an obscure ceremony that they called the "Rebirth", for which they needed a large number of "volunteers" to be sacrificed.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SF-4)</sup> The ruins were cleared out later that year by [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>
