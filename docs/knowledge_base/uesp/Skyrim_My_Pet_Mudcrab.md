## Quick Walkthrough
**Note**: This quest no longer appears in your journal following the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md) update.

1. Read a [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab.md).
2. Buy a mudcrab from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md).

**Note**: You can also steal [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab.md) from Calcemo to acquire ownership and the associated spell. This works on unpatched versions, such as the Nintendo Switch.

## Detailed Walkthrough
**Note: This quest was originally added to your journal upon installing the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). With the release of the [Anniversary Edition](Skyrim_Anniversary_Edition.md), this quest no longer appears in your journal. The mudcrab can still be purchased from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md).**

Once you've installed the Dwarven Armored Mudcrab Creation, you will receive this quest and a [notice of sale](Skyrim_Notice_of_Sale_-_Dwarven_Mudcrab.md), which directs you to talk to Calcelmo in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md).

Travel to Understone Keep in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) and meet Calcelmo in the ruins section of the keep. From him, you'll be able to purchase the [ownership deed](Skyrim_Ownership_Deed_-_Mudcrab.md). Once you've done so, you'll find the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md) beside the arcane enchanter. You'll also receive a [spell tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Teleport_Pet_Mudcrab.md) that teaches you a spell to [teleport the mudcrab](Skyrim_Teleport_Pet__Dwarven_Mudcrab.md) to you.

The mudcrab serves as an animal follower, with the added ability to carry items and allow you to send it to specific locations.
