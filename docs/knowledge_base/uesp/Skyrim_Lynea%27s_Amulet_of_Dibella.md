[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Amulet Of Dibella.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Amulet Of Dibella.png) Lynea's Amulet of Dibella Lynea's Amulet of Dibella is a unique amulet given to you by [Lynea](Skyrim_Lynea.md) for completing the quest [Private Lessons](Skyrim_Private_Lessons.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>. It increases your speechcraft by 15 points and your stamina by 40 points.

## Related Quests
- **[Private Lessons](Skyrim_Private_Lessons.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Teach [Lynea](Skyrim_Lynea.md) how to fish.

## Notes
- Lynea wears another version of this amulet (ID [xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 000E61) which cannot be worn or obtained by the player.
