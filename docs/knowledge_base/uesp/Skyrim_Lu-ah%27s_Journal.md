Lu-ah's Journal **by [Lu'ah Al-Skaven](Skyrim_Lu%27ah_Al-Skaven.md)**
---
Twenty-five years I have grieved for my husband, and sought revenge against the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md) for his death. I have researched the [magicks](Skyrim_Magic.md) to return his spirit to a body and something about the story [of Fjori and Holgeir](Skyrim_Of_Fjori_and_Holgeir.md) has drawn me here...

----------

[Holgeir](Skyrim_Holgeir.md) would make the perfect vessel to bring my Saeel back to life.

----------

I have managed to raise [the dead](Skyrim_Draugr.md) here and use them as laborers to clear the way to the main burial hall. Something about the methods used by the ancient [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) has kept them incredibly well preserved. Would that these [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) had half their sense. We could have used them to push the [elves](Lore_Mer.md) right off the continent and formed a new state to combat the empire.

----------

And now, when I am so close, a war breaks out. The sleeping bear of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), who would not come to aid us in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), awakens now that the Empire has abandoned them as well. They think they know suffering at the hands of the Empire? They know nothing. I would see both the Empire and these sons of Skyrim *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* into [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) myself.
