*You're a SMART manling. You bring me the voidguide, or you can't see the SECRET parts. Nyah-nyah.*
—Rishaal Tamir offering a deal to the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) (listen Link ⏵) Rishaal Tamir is the Daedra Lieutenant of Scamp Clan (also referred to as "Dagon's Loyal Troops"),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BScamp-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Spider-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-3)</sup><sup>:102</sup> and appears in the form of a small humanoid girl.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RIshaal Tamir-4)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> Her [incantatory protonymic](Lore_Nymic.md) was **Peytifar**, based upon [Roht](Lore_Roht.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

During the Daedric invasion of the [Imperial Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md), Tamir was among the Daedra commanders whose armies were raiding the facility.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-3)</sup><sup>:102</sup> There was much infighting between the four competing factions of the occupation, but Tamir's goal was to acquire a piece of the Voidguide for the leader of all of the invading clans, [High Lord Sumeer Jabran](Lore_Sumeer_Jabran.md). During this time Tamir herself was stationed as the Teleport Guard at the Battlespire Caitiff Teleportal, a position where she commanded her [Scamps](Lore_Scamp.md) to seek the missing voidpieces, likely unaware they had been the cause of the Voidguide's initial splintering in the first place.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BScamp-1)</sup> Here she struck a deal with the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md), who brought her a recovered voidpiece in exchange for a Zyr [Sigil of Entry](Lore_Sigil.md) which originally belonged to Zenaida Nacarra, allowing the mortal passage through the Teleportal. After receiving the voidpiece, Rishaal teleported from her post and promptly delivered the crucial item to Sumeer, who in turn tasked one of his servants with its safekeeping.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Athenaeum-3)</sup><sup>:102</sup><sup>:112</sup>

## Notes
- | [^](#note_nb1) | Tamir is visually described in the game's dialogue files as a "cute little girl demon."<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-6)</sup> It is unknown what kind of Daedra the character is, other than that she is one. However, many characters in *Battlespire* (the [Dremora](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Dremora#Unique_Dremora), [Herne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Herne#Unique_Herne), [Perthan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Spider_Daedra), and [Xivilai](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Battlespire:Xivilai_Moath) for example) have their Daedric clan/race name placed before their given name, which may or may not indicate that "Rishaal" is the Daedric clan/race name of Rishaal Tamir. |
| --- | --- |
- During early development Rishaal Tamir was intended to have a completely different look, but that visual concept was recycled for the game's [Herne](Lore_Herne_(creature).md) enemies instead.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-RTCA-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-8)</sup> The faun-esque leg style of Tamir in the concept art also matched the leg styles of the Scamps, who she commands over.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-RTCA-7)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Tamir's final character sprite is distinct for being the only 2D animated character sprite made for the game, whereas all the other characters and enemies were first made in 3D before being converted to 2D sprites.
