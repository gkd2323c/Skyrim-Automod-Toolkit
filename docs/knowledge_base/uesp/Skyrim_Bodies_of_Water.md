# Bodies of Water

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Honrich.jpg) Lake Honrich This page lists all the named **bodies of water** (lakes, ponds, rivers, and seas) in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md).

## Lakes

- **[Lake Fjalding](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Fjalding)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A frozen lake near Thirsk Mead Hall. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&x=66298&y=62779)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **[Lake Geir](Skyrim_Lake_Geir.md)** — A lake in the western Rift. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lake+Geir)
- **[Lake Honrich](Skyrim_Lake_Honrich.md)** — A lake in the eastern Rift, immediately southwest of Riften. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lake+Honrich)
- **[Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md)** — A lake in west-central Falkreath Hold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lake+Ilinalta)
- **[Lake Yorgrim](Skyrim_Lake_Yorgrim.md)** — A lake in the eastern part of the Pale. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lake+Yorgrim)

## Ponds

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Clearpine Pond](Skyrim_Clearpine_Pond.md)** — A pond with a small island, located west-southwest of Solitude, just to the northeast of Pinemoon Cave. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Clearpine+Pond)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Clearspring Tarn](Skyrim_Clearspring_Tarn.md)** — A small mountain pool on the cliff overlooking Eastmarch, due east of Snapleg Cave and west of Shor's Stone. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Clearspring+Tarn)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Evergreen Grove](Skyrim_Evergreen_Grove.md)** — A grove of trees concealing a small pond fed by a rocky stream, just southwest of Half-Moon Mill. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Evergreen+Grove)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Clearing.png) **[Mara's Eye Pond](Skyrim_Mara%27s_Eye_Pond.md)** — A small lake due east of Gallows Rock. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Mara%27s+Eye+Pond)

## Rivers

- **[Darkwater River](Skyrim_Darkwater_River.md)** — A river connecting Lake Geir to the White River. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Darkwater+River)
- **[Harstrad River](Skyrim_Harstrad_River.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A river in northern Solstheim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Harstrad+River)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **[Iggnir River](Skyrim_Iggnir_River.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A river in southern Solstheim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Iggnir+River)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **[Isild River](Skyrim_Isild_River.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A river in northern Solstheim on the Felsaad Coast. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Isild+River)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- **[Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md)** — A river flowing from Markarth to Solitude. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Karth+River)
- **[River Hjaal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Hjaal)** — The main river that flows through the center of Hjaalmarch. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=River+Hjaal)
- **[River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim)** — A river flowing from Lake Yorgrim to Windhelm. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=River+Yorgrim)
- **[Treva River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Treva_River)** — A river in the center of the Rift. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Treva+River)
- **[White River](Skyrim_White_River.md)** — The longest river in Skyrim, stretching from the south of the province to the northeast corner. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=White+River)

## Seas

- **[Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md)** — A vast ocean to the north of Skyrim. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Sea+of+Ghosts)

## Notes
- The [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md) adds water shaders, giving each one a more realistic look.

## Bugs
- Water may appear blurry when loading a saved game after creating a save underwater. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
- The water flow may fail to render properly. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
