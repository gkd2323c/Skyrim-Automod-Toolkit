## Effects
- [Summon](Skyrim_Conjure.md) [Wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md), for 60 seconds in 75 ft

## Perks
- [Totem of Ice Brothers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Totem_of_Ice_Brothers)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, summons [ice wolves](Skyrim_Ice_Wolf.md)
- [Totem of the Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Totem_of_the_Moon)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, summons [werewolves](Skyrim_Werewolf.md)

## Notes
- If the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) is installed, wearing the [Ring of the Moon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ring_of_the_Moon) will increase the duration to 75 seconds.
- All wolves and werewolves summoned are named Pack Member, and their stats are:
