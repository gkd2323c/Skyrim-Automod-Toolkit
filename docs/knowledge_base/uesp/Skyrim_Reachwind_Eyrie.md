[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4d/SR-place-Reachwind_Eyrie.jpg/200px-SR-place-Reachwind_Eyrie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reachwind_Eyrie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reachwind_Eyrie.jpg) Reachwind Eyrie **Reachwind Eyrie** is a small [Dwarven](Lore_Dwemer.md) tower east-southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). It contains only one zone, *[Reachwind Eyrie](#Reachwind_Eyrie)*.

The tower is perched atop a large cliff overlooking the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md), surrounded by some of Skyrim's greener lands. The building is remarkably well-preserved, considering the thousands of years it has stood unlocked.

## Walkthrough

### Reachwind Eyrie
Upon entering, there is a stone bench on either side of the door. Continuing up the winding stairway, you will come to a small room strewn with various [Dwemer scrap](Skyrim_Ores_and_Ingots.md#Dwemer_Metal_.28Smeltable.29) and [Dwemer dishes](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Dwemer_Dishes). There is also a Dwemer dresser that has a 25% chance of containing 1-9 gold, as well as up to three random Dwemer scrap (75% chance each). If you accumulate all the scrap metals found in the room, you can smelt a total of 23 [Dwarven metal ingots](Skyrim_Dwarven_Metal_Ingot.md) (more if the random items found in the dresser are smeltable).

Apart from Dwemer clutter, there are a few items of interest resting on the desk with the Dwemer banner hanging above it. These include a copy of the [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Chimarvamidium](Skyrim_Chimarvamidium.md)*, a random [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), a random [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), and a random [helmet](Skyrim_Helmet.md), which can be either light or heavy. Also, there is an apprentice-locked chest containing random [loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md). There are also two [wine bottles](Skyrim_Wine.md) lying around the room.

### Exterior: Reachwind Eyrie Overlook
Reachwind Eyrie Overlook is an outdoor balcony that is accessible through a door to the left of the desk. Looking out of the balcony, you will see a spectacular view of western Skyrim. You will be able to see [Pavo's House](Skyrim_Pavo%27s_House.md) toward the northwest, and [Kolskeggr Mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md) and [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md) toward the north. Looking out to the northeast, you can see the ruined [Sundered Towers](Skyrim_Sundered_Towers.md) and the tower at [Bleakwind Bluff](Skyrim_Bleakwind_Bluff.md) slightly east of it. Finally, if you look toward the east, you should be able to see [Old Hroldan Inn](Skyrim_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md).

To the left of the balcony door lies a birds' nest containing a [pine thrush egg](Skyrim_Pine_Thrush_Egg.md). There is a random [one-handed sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) resting on the parapet of the balcony. To the right of the balcony rests an expert-locked chest containing random loot.

## Notes
- This may be the tower of [Gauldur](Lore_Gauldur.md), as *[Daynas Valen's Notes](Skyrim_Daynas_Valen%27s_Notes.md)* reveals that he was buried near it, and [Reachwater Rock](Skyrim_Reachwater_Rock.md) isn't too far away.
- The guards at [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md) may add this location to your map if you talk to them.
- [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) may spawn here at higher levels.
