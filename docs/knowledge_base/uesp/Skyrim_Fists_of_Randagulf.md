[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-item-Fists_of_Randagulf.jpg/200px-SR-item-Fists_of_Randagulf.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Fists_of_Randagulf.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Fists_of_Randagulf.jpg) Fists of Randagulf The Fists of Randagulf are artifact gauntlets originally worn by Randagulf, one of the mightiest warriors from Skyrim in Tamrielic history. It is [heavy armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_(item).md) and enchanted with the following: Melee and unarmed attacks do 20% more damage. Block 20% more damage with your shield.

It is meant to be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot). It should count as [Steel Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Armor) for the purpose of the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk. It can be obtained through the quest [Caught in a Web](Skyrim_Caught_in_a_Web.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Caught in a Web](Skyrim_Caught_in_a_Web.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_The_Contest.md)</sup>: Search for legendary artifacts in a spider den.

## Notes
- The Fists of Randagulf previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Fists_of_Randagulf) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.

## Bugs
- The Fists of Randagulf cannot be tempered, as the temper recipe points to the Ice Blade of the Monarch instead. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130.0, fixes this bug.
