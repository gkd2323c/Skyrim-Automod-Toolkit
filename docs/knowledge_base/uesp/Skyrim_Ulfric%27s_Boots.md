[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Boots.jpg) Ulfric's Boots [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ulfric%27s_Clothes.jpg) Ulfric's clothing, while worn Ulfric's Boots are the eponymous boots of [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md). They are made of cloth, but have metal plates over the shins for some added protection. They share their basic design with [pleated shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pleated_Shoes), although the shoes lack the added metal plates.

## Notes
- They appear as pleated shoes when worn by a female.

## Bugs
- This item will not appear as the right color in the inventory, instead appearing to look like the pleated shoes it is based off of. Its world model will still have its unique appearance, however. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.2, addresses this issue. Ulfric's boots will now appear as they should when viewed in the inventory.
