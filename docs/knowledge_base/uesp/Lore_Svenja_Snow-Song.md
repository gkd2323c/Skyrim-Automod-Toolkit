Svenja Snow-Song was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) who served as the advisor to two of the chieftains of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the [Udyrfrykte](Lore_Udefrykte.md) attacked and slaughtered several inhabitants of Thirsk, including the chieftain [Skjoldr Wolf-Runner](Lore_Skjoldr_Wolf-Runner.md), before Svenja drove the beast off with arrows. When the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) arrived at Thirsk, Svenja enlisted their help in slaying the Udyrfrykte. After the Nerevarine slew the Udyrfrykte and returned to the mead hall with the beast's heart, Svenja directed them to the tomb of [Hrothmund the Red](Lore_Hrothmund_the_Red.md) to receive his blessing to become the new chieftain of Thirsk. After they received Hrothmund's blessing and assumed the role of Chieftain of Thirsk, Svenja became the Nerevarine's advisor, second-in-command, and overseer of the mead hall in their absence.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMHM-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BSvenja-2)</sup>

After the Nerevarine reportedly journeyed to [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md), [Agnar the Unwavering](Lore_Agnar_the_Unwavering.md) became the new chieftain of Thirsk, and Svenja became his advisor. They later married.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AJ-3)</sup>

Svenja was haunted by the Udyrfrykte's massacre of Thirsk, which caused her to wake up night after night screaming. To confront her fears and prevent any more destruction, Svenja and Agnar traveled across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in hopes of finding and slaying the [Uderfrykte Matron](Lore_Uderfrykte_Matron.md), the progenitor of the Udyrfrykte. Around [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a shepherd spotted it in the remote highlands of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), and Svenja and Agnar then tracked it across the border into [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). In Cyrodiil, a hunter told them about the Horror of Dive Rock, which Svenja and Agnar concluded was the matron. They established a camp on the overlook at [Dive Rock](Lore_Dive_Rock.md), and after three days of watching, they spotted the matron. Once they confronted the matron, Svenja was quickly struck down and devoured, forcing Agnar to retreat to the camp, where he was killed as well.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AJ-3)</sup> The [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) later slew the Uderfrykte Matron and found Svenja's remains.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
