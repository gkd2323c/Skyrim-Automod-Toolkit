[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/SR-item-Karliah%27s_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Karliah%27s_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Karliah%27s_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Karliah%27s_Bow.jpg) Karliah's Bow Karliah's Bow is the version of the [Nightingale Bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Bow) used by NPCs, such as [Karliah](Skyrim_Karliah.md) and [Nightingale Sentinels](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Sentinel). It shares its appearance with the player version, although it lacks its enchantment and is worth less gold. Curiously, this version of the weapon does more damage, weighs less, and is faster than the highest leveled version of the player's bow. The characters who have a copy of this weapon will never drop it, and it does not show up when [pickpocketing](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) them, meaning it cannot be obtained during normal gameplay.
