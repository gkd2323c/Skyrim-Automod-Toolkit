Taron Dreth was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) mage who claimed to be the "world's foremost expert" in [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) metallurgy and forging techniques. He was supposedly the author of *[The Aetherium Wars](Lore_The_Aetherium_Wars.md)*,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAW-1)</sup> but he actually plagiarized the work from the master under whom he studied, a secret he was willing to kill to protect.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-K-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KJ-3)</sup> At last report, he was traveling in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md), most likely looking for evidence to support his stolen theories on [Aetherium](Lore_Aetherium.md). He was killed in the wilderness in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRTaron-4)</sup>
