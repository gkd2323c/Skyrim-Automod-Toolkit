[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-npc-Torturer%27s_Assistant.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Torturer%27s_Assistant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer%27s_Assistant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Torturer%27s_Assistant.jpg) Torturer's Assistant The **Torturer's Assistant** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) [barbarian](Skyrim_Barbarian.md) encountered in [Helgen Keep](Skyrim_Helgen_Keep.md) during the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md).

He is working under the [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)'s resident [Torturer](Skyrim_Torturer.md) and you will, during your escape with [Hadvar](Skyrim_Hadvar.md), find him battling a few [Stormcloak soldiers](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) in the torture room midway through the keep. If you manage to rescue him, he will join you as a very early [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md), although for a limited time only.

He wields an [iron mace](Skyrim_Iron_Mace.md) and wears a set of [Imperial light armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md) and [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Bracers.md).

After the torture room is cleared, he will stay in the background while Hadvar argues loudly with his stubborn master and say nothing but *"Worry about yourself."* After listening to the conversation for a while, he will impatiently say, *"Forget the old man. I'll come with you."* and join you and Hadvar as you explore the cave. During the trek, he will only say, *"We need to find a way out of here."* After defeating the Stormcloak soldiers in the room with the bridges, he will suddenly have second thoughts about his master, saying, *"I better stay back and see to the old man. Good luck you two."* and stay behind.
