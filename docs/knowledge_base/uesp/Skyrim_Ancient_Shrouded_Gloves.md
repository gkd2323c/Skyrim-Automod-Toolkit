[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/SR-item-Shrouded_Gloves.jpg/200px-SR-item-Shrouded_Gloves.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Gloves.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Shrouded_Gloves.jpg) Shrouded Gloves Ancient Shrouded Gloves are the gloves for a set of armor found on a dead [assassin](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood_Assassin.md) in [Hag's End](Skyrim_Hag%27s_End.md). Alongside this piece, this set of armor also includes an [armor](Skyrim_Ancient_Shrouded_Armor.md) piece, a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Ancient_Shrouded_Boots.md), and a [cowl](Skyrim_Ancient_Shrouded_Cowl.md). It shares its appearance with regular [Shrouded Gloves](Skyrim_Shrouded_Gloves.md). However, Ancient Shrouded Gloves have a superior armor rating, lower weight, and higher gold value than the normal version of this armor. This armor's enchantment provides double damage when [backstabbing](Skyrim_Backstab.md), the same as the common version. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) Ancient Shrouded Gloves requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

Completing [Breaching Security](Skyrim_Breaching_Security.md) by killing [Gaius Maro](Skyrim_Gaius_Maro.md) in a hold capital will result in [Gabriella](Skyrim_Gabriella.md) giving you [Olava's Token](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olava%27s_Token). This token can be given to the eponymous [Olava the Feeble](Skyrim_Olava_the_Feeble.md), who will read your future. After making some various predictions, she will say: *"...Wait! There's something else... A potential for adventure, and wealth. It is a ruin, ripe for the plunder. [Deepwood Redoubt](Skyrim_Deepwood_Redoubt.md). Far to the northwest... Through there is... Hag's End. The last resting place of an assassin of old. A Dark Brother, who bequeaths his ancient earthly possessions... to you."* This prediction will add a handle behind the throne in Hag's End, which will open up a wall to the final resting place of the assassin in question, and this equipment set.
