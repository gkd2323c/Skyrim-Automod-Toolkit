# Stag-Heart Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/ON-hat-Stag-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg/200px-ON-hat-Stag-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Stag-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Stag-Heart_Skull_Sallet.jpg) Stag-Heart Skull Sallet [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/00/ON-place-Reachwind_Depths.jpg/200px-ON-place-Reachwind_Depths.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reachwind_Depths.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reachwind_Depths.jpg) Reachwind Depths Stag-Heart Clan was one of the numerous [clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStag Sallet-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RDLS-2)</sup>

Reachfolk of the Stag-Heart clan were known to worship the [Great Spirit](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) in his aspect of Uricanbeg, the Great Stag. They believed that Uricanbeg's hooves drum the Blood Summons. Members of the clans were considered a sect by some. Antlered helmets were typical headdresses for the Stag-Hearts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONStag Sallet-1)</sup>

Similarly to the many other Reachfolk clans, they were distrustful of the underground dwellings of the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RDLS-2)</sup>

One of the members of the clan, Raddolf, specifically warned against venturing to the Reachwind Depths.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RDLS-2)</sup>
