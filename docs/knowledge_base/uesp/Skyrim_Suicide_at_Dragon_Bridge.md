Suicide at Dragon Bridge *A note regarding a missing person* *
---
One of the locals who frequents the inn has been reported missing. I was about to tell the innkeeper to report it to the guard, but Aldepius said he saw the person in question leap off Dragon Bridge.

A sad state of affairs, but not our concern.
