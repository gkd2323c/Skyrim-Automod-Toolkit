# Jewel of the Sphinx

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sweet_Khenarthi%27s_Song_03.jpg) Khenarthi depicted as a Sphinx The Jewel of the Sphinx was a highly valued artifact, regarded as one of the most renowned relics of [Khenarthi](Lore_Khenarthi.md)'s priesthood. It was described as Khenarthi's prized jewel.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCTV8-1)</sup>

## History

### Second Era
At some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Chronicler Firandil of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) traveled to a [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) temple, where Priest Sazarali agreed to let him and his fellow mages study the Jewel of the [Sphinx](Lore_Sphinx.md) in exchange for their search for the temple's ancient secrets. An agreement was made, stating that as long as Firandil explored the temple's hidden knowledge, Warlock Ulelfanya and the other mages of the Guild would be permitted to study the jewel. This arrangement was considered a diplomatic success between the priesthood and the guild.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCTV8-1)</sup>

## Notes
- Sphinxes are associated with the province of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). [Igon](Lore_Igon.md), the Sphinx of Canus, and [Agamanus](Lore_Agamanus.md), the Sphinx of Gazia, are revered entities in Elsweyr,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARRiddles-2)</sup> with the latter having a temple dedicated to him, known as the [Temple of Agamanus](Lore_Temple_of_Agamanus.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARAgamanus-3)</sup> [Fort Sphinxmoth](Lore_Fort_Sphinxmoth.md) is also a prominent location within the province.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FS-4)</sup>
- Khenarthi is depicted in tapestries in the form resembling a real-world [Greek Spynx](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sphinx#Greece).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOSB-5)</sup>
