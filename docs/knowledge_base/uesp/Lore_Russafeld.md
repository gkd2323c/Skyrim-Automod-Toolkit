## History
Russafeld grew around the Red Temple, which is an ancient [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) shrine to consecrated to [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md), built, most probably during the [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) or early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Russafeld means "scarlet shrine" in some dialects of Aldmeris, which may explain where the Red Temple gets its curious name. But the most commonly held opinion states that it's a shortening or corruption of "Dread Temple."<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-RTCLS-3)</sup>

Caanerin, the [indrik](Lore_Indrik.md) matriarch, had attacked Russafeld more than once. Prior to the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), she fawned a whole herd of lesser indrik and destroyed half of Russafeld. Ancient indriks like Caanerin enjoy a deep connection to Y'ffre. It is unknown her connection to the Red Temple and the status of Russafeld as a holy place of worship to the Green. After that, the Sapiarchs erected warding stones to keep her from returning. However, she returned to her ancestral glade near the center of Summerset Isle around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Divine Prosecution's hirelings killed her and restored the magical protections, saving Russafeld.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

During the brief period during the mid-Second Era when [Queen Ayrenn](Lore_Queen_Ayrenn.md)'s decree legalized migration to Summerset Isle, many [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) moved from their homeland in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) to work on the fields of Russafeld.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> However, Wood Elf manpower presence was not unheard of before that time, when the Ghost of the Green first struck in Summerset due to Bosmer sentiment of exploitation by their Altmer overlords.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

The town was still in existence during the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), when it became known as Rosfeld.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-West Tamriel Map-2)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), it was known as Rosefield and was ruled by Lord Lillandlas. It had a rivalry with Holly Falls.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Riverfield](Lore_Riverfield.md) in the west, and [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md) in the east.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Map Arena-9)</sup>

## Government
Russafeld has been an integral part of the realm of the Summerset Isles since its foundation by the Alinor Monarchs. Neither a canonreeve or a kinlord is known to be tasked to rule the settlement and its rich hinterland, but a simple bailiff.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ESO-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>

## Culture
This settlement is known as the birthplace of wine in the Summerset Isles. Its vineyards span nearly the entire stretch of land between [Rellenthil](Lore_Rellenthil.md) and Shimmerene. Russafeld reds are the most appreciated alcoholic beverage by the Altmer.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-OBIAG-12)</sup> It is also a very popular touristic spot and retirement resort for the elite.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup> The craftsmen of Russafeld are renowned for their complex basket-weaving techniques.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-Contraband R-15)</sup> Russafeld is known for production of weapon oil with distinctive faint scent of grapeseed.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-Contraband R2-16)</sup>
