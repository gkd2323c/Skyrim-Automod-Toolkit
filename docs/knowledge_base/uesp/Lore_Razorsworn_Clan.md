# Razorsworn Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/ON-npc-Kothan_the_Razorsworn.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Kothan_the_Razorsworn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kothan_the_Razorsworn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kothan_the_Razorsworn.jpg) Kothan the Razorsworn (ESO) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/ON-creature-Karzikon_the_Razorsworn.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Karzikon_the_Razorsworn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Karzikon_the_Razorsworn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Karzikon_the_Razorsworn.jpg) Karzikon the Razorsworn (ESO) Razorsworn Clan is a [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) clan in service to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Destruction, [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). They often serve as marauders and assassins and are frequently dispatched to strike at Dagon's enemies in other realms. They are enemies of the [Firescourge Clan](Lore_Firescourge_Clan.md), whom they view with contempt. In the Second Era, the Razorsworn Clan begrudgingly followed the authority of the Ruinblood Clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ODC-1)</sup> Despite being a Dremora clan, they also had [Ruinachs](Lore_Ruinach.md) and [Havocrels](Lore_Havocrel.md) among their ranks.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KO-3)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), one of the Razorsworn-aligned Ruinach, Karzikon was sent to Doomvault Porcixid in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-KA-2)</sup> Another member, the Havocrel Kothan, was roaming Mehrunes Dagon's realm, the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KO-3)</sup>
