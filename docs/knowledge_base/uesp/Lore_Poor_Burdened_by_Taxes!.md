Poor Burdened by Taxes! *A Black Horse Courier article regarding the taxation of city folk*
---
Recently Captain Hieronymus Lex of the [Imperial Watch](Lore_Imperial_Watch.md) collected the taxes from all citizens in the [Waterfront District](Lore_Waterfront_District.md) of the city. Although the laws are clear that all citizens of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) must pay taxes, it has been 53 years since anyone applied that law to the poor and destitute of the Waterfront.

Although members of the Watch approached by the [Courier](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Black_Horse_Courier) declined to comment on the success of the venture, one of the Watchmen who asked not to be named suggested the operation was "a complete... wossitsname? You know. Starts with an 'f'. Right. Complete fee-ass-ko, is what it was."

In a related story, miscreants have recently broken into the South Watchtower. An anonymous source reports that a small sum of money was stolen from the office of Hieronymus Lex. By remarkable coincidence, the sum corresponds exactly to the taxes collected by Captain Lex from the Waterfront.

The ever-vigilant Captain Lex has renewed his call to capture the infamous thief, the [Gray Fox](Lore_Gray_Fox.md). He is petitioning for a bounty to be put on the legendary master thief's head.
