King Anumaril was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) arch-mage and king of [Abagarlas](Lore_Abagarlas.md), a city of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) worshippers in a deadly feud with the [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md) -worshipping people of [Delodiil](Lore_Delodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BTMM-1)</sup> During the course of the war, Anumaril began preparations for a [necromantic](Lore_Necromancy.md) ritual, the Mortuum Vivicus, which would drain all life from Delodiil.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CJ-2)</sup> This prompted a daring raid by the Meridians, who were able to infiltrate Abagarlas, kill Anumaril's entire family, and destroy the city entirely.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TWOD-3)</sup> Anumaril also supposedly ruled from the [Temple of the Ancestors](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) at some point.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONCelarus-4)</sup>

At some point, Anumaril created the [Staff of Towers](Lore_Staff_of_Towers.md), a powerful artifact whose eight segments corresponded to one of the metaphysical Towers that shape the fabric of Nirn. Around the time of the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md), Anumaril gifted Filestis the Remnant of Light and told him to take it to ["the cold sunset limit"](Lore_Rivenspire.md), where he was followed by Ayleid immigrants.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Following this, he fled to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AE4TET-6)</sup> Hailed as one of the greatest architects of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), he was responsible for several constructions in the province, such as the Great Seal of [Belarata](Lore_Belarata.md) and the archives in the Reliquary of Stars.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONLa-7)</sup>

One of his more notable creations was the Orrery at [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md). On his travels to the great tree, he found the Stone of the Green-Sap Tower, the Perchance Acorn, and tried to recreate the [Tower](Lore_The_Towers.md) of [White-Gold](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md). His attempt failed, due to a fundamental incompatibility between Ayleid and Bosmer magic and belief.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-AE4TET-6)</sup> Anumaril produced an heir, Prince Malyon, with his wife, Queen Palolel.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TUT-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SACPC-9)</sup>

## Notes
- There is an artifact called the Heart of Anumaril, which "brought life" to the Orrery in Elden Root many years ago.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONMeleras-10)</sup> It was later used to imprison the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) Ukaezai in the Reliquary of Stars.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-TBY-11)</sup>
