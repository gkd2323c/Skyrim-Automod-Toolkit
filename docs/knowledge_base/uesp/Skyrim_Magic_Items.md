# Magic Items


## Types of Magic Items
There are a few different ways to find magic items.

- Some are given as **quest rewards.** - Most of these items are [leveled items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md), which means that the quality of the item and the strength of its enchantment depends upon your character's level.
- Other quest rewards - in particular, those given for completing the [Daedric Quests](Skyrim_Daedric_Quests.md) - are considered to be [artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md).
- Most magic items are found as **random loot**. The items that can appear in random loot are determined by your character's level. - The random weapons are all listed on [Generic Magic Weapons](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md); the [Weapons](#Weapons) section on this article provides some general information.
- The random apparel (armor, clothing, and jewelry) is all listed on [Generic Magic Apparel](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md); the [Apparel](#Apparel) section on this article provides some general information.
- A few magic items can be **purchased from merchants**. - Some merchants sell [unique enchanted items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md).
- You can create your own **[custom magic items](Skyrim_Enchanting.md)**.
- If you have different items with the same effect, the effects will 'stack' i.e. if you have a ring and a circlet which both fortify Smithing by 25%, your smithing will be fortified by 50%.

## Weapons

### Weapon Leveled Lists
All [generic magic weapons](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md) are most commonly found as random loot, either in chests or on boss-level [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md). The weapons that can appear are determined by your character's level using various [leveled lists](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists). In general, the weapon's quality dictates the level at which it will first appear.

### Weapon Articles
- **[Weapons](Skyrim_Weapons.md):** Base (unenchanted) weapons **except** for arrows and staves
- **[Ammunition](Skyrim_Ammunition.md):** All arrows (enchanted and unenchanted)
- **[Staves](Skyrim_Staves.md):** All staves
- **[Generic Magic Weapons](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Weapons.md):** All magic weapons found in random loot
- **[Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md):** Leveled magic weapons, generally received as quest rewards
- **[Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md):** Unique weapons, available from merchants or received as quest rewards
- **[Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md):** Unusually powerful one-of-a-kind weapons

- [Skyrim-Weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Weapons): Category listing all of the weapons in the game

## Apparel

### Apparel Leveled Lists
The magic items listed on [Generic Magic Apparel](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md) are in general found as random loot, either in chests or on boss-level [NPCs](Skyrim_NPCs.md). The level at which it may start to appear can be determined from the armor quality.

### Apparel Articles
- **[Armor](Skyrim_Armor.md):** Base (unenchanted) armor
- **[Clothing](Skyrim_Clothing.md):** Base (unenchanted) clothing and jewelry
- **[Generic Magic Apparel](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md):** All magic apparel that can be found in random loot
- **[Leveled Items](Skyrim_Leveled_Items.md):** Leveled magic apparel, generally received as quest rewards
- **[Unique Items](Skyrim_Unique_Items.md):** Unique apparel, available from merchants or received as quest rewards
- **[Artifacts](Skyrim_Artifacts.md):** Unusually powerful one-of-a-kind apparel
