[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/SR-place-Lake_Fjalding.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Fjalding.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Fjalding.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Fjalding.jpg) Lake Fjalding **Lake Fjalding** is a frozen lake near [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md).

It is the largest lake on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), although it is covered by ice year-round. It is commonly used as a mating ground for the [horkers](Skyrim_Horker.md) of the island. The [Iggnir River](Skyrim_Iggnir_River.md) to the south and the [Isild River](Skyrim_Isild_River.md) to the north meet here. The [Isinfier Plains](Lore_Isinfier_Plains.md) lie to the south.
