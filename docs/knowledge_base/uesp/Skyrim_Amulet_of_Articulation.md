[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/SR-item-Amulet_of_Articulation.jpg/200px-SR-item-Amulet_of_Articulation.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Articulation.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Amulet_of_Articulation.jpg) The Amulet of Articulation The Amulet of Articulation is an amulet artifact given to you by [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) for becoming the new Guildmaster of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) in the quest [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md). Its enchantment grants a unique bonus to [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md), which increases the chance of successfully persuading someone. It is also unusual in that it is one of only two amulets in the game to provide an [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md) bonus, the other being the [Locket of Saint Jiub](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Locket_of_Saint_Jiub).<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>

## Related Quests
- **[Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md)**: Become the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Notes
- Wearing the Amulet of Articulation allows you to automatically succeed at all persuasion checks in the game regardless of skill.
- The amulet also increases the Speech skill by its listed "articulation" value, but because you pass all persuasion checks anyway, this only improves buying and selling prices.
- The armor rating does improve with a higher [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) skill. Getting hit while wearing it will increase the Light Armor skill, yet it does not interfere with the [Mage Armor](Skyrim_Mage_Armor.md), [Custom Fit](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md#Custom_Fit)/[Well Fitted](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Well_Fitted), or [either of the](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md#Matching_Set) [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perks; moreover, unlike a shield, it *will* benefit from the Light Armor perks.

## Bugs
- There are seven different versions of the Amulet of Articulation that you can receive (and an eighth version that is NPC-specific), each with a different strength enchantment. The intent was probably for the version received to be level-dependent, but instead it is random. All seven versions are equally likely at all levels. You can get the highest one by repeatedly loading a save and talking to Brynjolf, though it may take some time. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, addresses this issue. The progression has been corrected as follows: PC level 1/8/15/22/29/36/43.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Right after becoming Guild Master, when you talk to Brynjolf a second time to get the Amulet, if you quicksave before he finishes speaking, you can reload the quicksave to quickly re-roll the Amulet speech stat.
- The displayed enchantment magnitudes and the actual enchantment magnitudes do not match. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, addresses this issue. The enchantment magnitudes have been adjusted to match the descriptions.
