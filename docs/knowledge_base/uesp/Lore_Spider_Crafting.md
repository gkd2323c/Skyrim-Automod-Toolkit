# Spider Crafting

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-activity-Imbuing_Chamber.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Imbuing_Chamber.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Imbuing_Chamber.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Imbuing_Chamber.jpg) Imbuing Chamber Spider Crafting<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PRIMA-1)</sup><sup>:665</sup> is an art of magic involving the creation of artificial [spiders](Lore_Spider.md) imbued with magical properties.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SJ-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> It is considered adjacent to [Alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) and Daedric magic.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLCL-5)</sup>

Spiders created through Spider Crafting are often referred to as **Spider Scrolls**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup> although they bear little resemblance to magical [scrolls](Lore_Scrolls.md) created through the art of [enchanting](Lore_Enchanting.md).

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Servos_Rendas.jpg) Servos Rendas [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0b/ON-npc-Mad_Griskild.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Mad_Griskild.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Mad_Griskild.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Mad_Griskild.jpg) Griskild Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) priest of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) named Mad Griskild set up his workshop in the abandoned [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) ruins of [Bal Fell](Lore_Bal_Fell.md) in the southern parts of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and began experimenting with [Alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) and Daedric magic to create magically enhanced spiders. An expedition to the ruins led by a famous [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) archaeologist, Lady Clarisse Laurent, fell victim to Griskild's mind-controlling spiders, who used them to abduct scholars and force them to dance in honor of his patron, the Mad God Sheogorath.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MG-6)</sup> The expedition members were ultimately saved by the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), who killed Griskild. Lady Laurent later sent Griskild's research to a scribe living near [White Ridge Barrow](Lore_White_Ridge_Barrow.md) on [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), who was known for his obsessive fascination with spiders.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOAWOT-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLCL-5)</sup>

This ultimately led to the creation of unique magical spiders that were later rediscovered in the same barrow at some point in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) by the [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) siblings Merilar Rendas and Servos Rendas.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WRB-8)</sup> They were testing their creations on bandits. Eventually, Merilar, driven by ambition and paranoia killed her brother.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MJ-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SEN-10)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SJ-2)</sup> Merilar was killed in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) by a [wandering adventurer](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md), who found the Imbuing Chamber and research notes of the Dunmeri siblings. The further fate of Spider Crafting is unknown.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-WRB-8)</sup>

## Usage
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg) Merilar Rendas using Flame Spiders in combat [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/ON-quest-A_Web_of_Troubles_02.jpg/200px-ON-quest-A_Web_of_Troubles_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Web_of_Troubles_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-A_Web_of_Troubles_02.jpg) Griskild forcing researchers to dance through the Mind Spiders One of the known methods of spider crafting involves the usage of the Imbuing Chamber. This device allowed mixing ingredients put in the receptacle. Recipes for spider creation followed a simple pattern, with untarnished or damaged [albino spider](Lore_Spider.md#Albino_Spider) pods being the core components. Pods could be combined with various [gems](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gems) for the desired effect. [Rubies](Lore_Ruby.md) imbued the created spider with the element of fire, [sapphires](Lore_Sapphire.md) with frost, [amethysts](Lore_Amethyst.md) with shock, and [emeralds](Lore_Emerald.md) gave spider venomous qualities. Damaged spider pods could only be used to create a dead spider that explodes imbued with the desired element.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> It was theorized that the purity of the gem could enhance the imbuing process in an unforeseen way,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-SEN-10)</sup> increasing number of spiders created.

The untarnished pods, however, had more uses. Not only could they be imbued with gemstones to grant created spiders elemental qualities, but with the addition of a [salt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salt) [pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Salt_Pile), they could be granted the elemental cloak. Unlike the spiders created with damaged pods, they do not explode on contact with the desired foe. Without the addition of salt, however, they were only separate from those with undamaged origins in the fact that they were alive and could be commanded to jump in the desired direction. The untarnished pods could also be combined with [Dwarven oil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwarven_Oil) or a [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md). Those spiders created through the combination of pods and Dwarven oil were known as the Oil Spiders. They were known to secrete a flammable oil when threatened. Spiders created with the usage of Soul gem were known as Mind Control Spiders or Mind Spiders, and had the ability to influence and even control the minds of people.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup>

## Known Types

### Flame Spiders
Flame Spiders are created by combining rubies with albino spider pods.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup>

- **Exploding Flame Spiders** are created through the combination of damaged albino spider pods and rubies.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-REFS-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-REFS2-12)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> They are dead and cause a flame explosion on contact with enemies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Jumping Flame Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods and rubies.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-RJFS-13)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-RJFS2-14)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created with damaged pods they are initially alive and jump on their foes to explode in the elemental explosion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Flame Cloaked Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods, rubies, and salt.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-RFCS-15)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-RFCS2-16)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created without salt they do not have explosive properties and instead they are cloaked in fire.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

### Frost Spiders
Frost Spiders are created by combining sapphires with albino spider pods.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup>

- **Exploding Frost Spiders** are created through the combination of damaged albino spider pods and sapphires.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-REFRS-17)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-REFRS2-18)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> They are dead and cause frost explosion on contact with enemies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Jumping Frost Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods and sapphires.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-RJFRS-19)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-RJFRS2-20)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created with damaged pods they are initially alive and jump on their foes to explode in the elemental explosion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Frost Cloaked Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods, sapphires, and salt.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-RFRCS-21)</sup><sup>[[22]](#cite_note-RFRCS2-22)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created without salt they do not have explosive properties and instead they are cloaked in frost.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-Scroll-Jumping_Frost_Spider.png) Jumping Frost Spider and Frost Cloaked Spider (Skyrim)

### Shock Spiders
Shock Spiders are created by combining amethysts with albino spider pods.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup>

- **Exploding Shock Spiders** are created through the combination of damaged albino spider pods and amethysts.<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-RESS-23)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-RESS2-24)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> They are dead and cause a lightning explosion on contact with enemies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Jumping Shock Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods and amethysts.<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-RJSS-25)</sup><sup>[[26]](#cite_note-RJSS2-26)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created with damaged pods they are initially alive and jump on their foes to explode in the elemental explosion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Shock Cloaked Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods, amethysts, and salt.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-RSCS-27)</sup><sup>[[28]](#cite_note-RSCS2-28)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created without salt they do not have explosive properties and instead they are cloaked in lightning.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

### Poison Spiders
Shock Spiders are created by combining emeralds with albino spider pods.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup>

- **Exploding Poison Spiders** are created through the combination of damaged albino spider pods and emeralds.<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-REPS-29)</sup><sup>[[30]](#cite_note-REPS2-30)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> They are dead and cause poison explosion on contact with enemies.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup> [Web Mothers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Web_Mother) have been known to toss exploding poison spiders resembling these at foes to defend themselves.<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-31)</sup>

- **Jumping Poison Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods and emeralds.<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-RJPS-32)</sup><sup>[[33]](#cite_note-RJPS2-33)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created with damaged pods they are initially alive and jump on their foes to explode in the elemental explosion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

- **Poison Cloaked Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods, emeralds, and salt.<sup>[[34]](#cite_note-RPCS-34)</sup><sup>[[35]](#cite_note-RPCS2-35)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Imbuing_Chamber-4)</sup> Unlike spiders created without salt they do not have explosive properties and instead they are cloaked in poison.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup>

### Other types of Spiders
- **Pack Spiders**<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLCL-5)</sup> are created through combination of intact albino spider pods and [bone meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bone_Meal).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-RPS-36)</sup> They may be used to haul items.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-37)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Cut)</sup>
- **Light Spiders**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOLCL-5)</sup> also known as **Glowing Spiders** are created through combination of intact albino spider pods and [glowing mushroom](Lore_Glowing_Mushroom.md).<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-RGS-38)</sup> They may be used as a source of light.<sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-39)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Cut)</sup>
- **Oil Spiders** are created through the combination of intact albino spider pods and [Dwarven oil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Dwarven_Oil).<sup>[[36]](#cite_note-ROS-40)</sup> They were known to secrete a flammable oil when threatened.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup><sup>[[37]](#cite_note-SS-41)</sup>
- **Mind Control Spiders**,<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-RMCS-42)</sup> also known as **Mind Spiders**,<sup>[[39]](#cite_note-ESOMS-43)</sup> are created through combination of intact albino spider pods and [soul gems](Lore_Soul_Gems.md).<sup>[[38]](#cite_note-RMCS-42)</sup> They were known to possess the ability to control minds.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SSS-3)</sup><sup>[[37]](#cite_note-SS-41)</sup> Those affected by them could hear voices compelling them to perform certain actions.<sup>[[40]](#cite_note-ESODE-44)</sup><sup>[[41]](#cite_note-ESOM-45)</sup><sup>[[42]](#cite_note-ESOS-46)</sup>
