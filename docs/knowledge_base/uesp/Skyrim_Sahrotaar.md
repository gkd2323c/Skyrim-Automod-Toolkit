[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-creature-Sahrotaar.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Sahrotaar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sahrotaar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Sahrotaar.jpg) Sahrotaar **Sahrotaar** is a unique [serpentine dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Serpentine_Dragon) found inside the *[Black Book: Waking Dreams](Skyrim_Black_Book__Waking_Dreams_(book).md)* during the quest [At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md).

His name might mean either "Mighty Servant" or "Phantom Word Servant" in the [dragon language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).

Sahrotaar will appear after you learn the [Dragon Aspect](Skyrim_Dragon_Aspect.md) shout, and it is then necessary to use the [Bend Will](Skyrim_Bend_Will.md) shout on him to tame him and progress through the quest.

Sahrotaar knows the following shouts: [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md), [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm_(shout).md), and [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md).

## Related Quests
- **[At the Summit of Apocrypha](Skyrim_At_the_Summit_of_Apocrypha.md)**: Confront [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) at the summit of [Apocrypha](Skyrim_Apocrypha.md) and defeat him once and for all.

## Quest-Related Events
He shares several lines of dialogue with [Krosulhah](Skyrim_Krosulhah.md), as they are both in the faction "[Combat dialogue for dragons allied to Miraak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat_dialogue_for_dragons_allied_to_Miraak)":

*"My thuri Miraak is the only true Dovahkiin."* *"Krin mey. You are a fool to challenge Miraak. His thu'um is supreme."* *"Your death has been foretold."* *"Aam uv dir. Those that will not serve Miraak must die."* If you use [Dragonrend](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md) on Sahrotaar, he will comment on it:

*"Bein Rotmulaagge! What foul Words are these?* *"No dovah would stoop to such vile tahrovin."* Once he has landed on the ground, he will say:

*"Now we will see whose thu'um is the stronger!"* *"You are strong, Dovahkiin. Rinik mul. But not strong enough."* Upon taming him, he will say:

*"Hail, thuri. Your thu'um has the mastery. Climb aboard and I will carry you to [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md)."* At this point, the [dragon riding](Skyrim_Dragon_Riding.md) tutorial is provided while riding him. He will warn you of Miraak's strength while carrying you:

*"Beware. Miraak is strong. He knew you would come here."* When you reach Miraak at the summit, he will chide Sahrotaar, saying, *"Sahrotaar, are you so easily swayed?"* At that point, a battle against Miraak ensues, and you can choose whether to fight him on Sahrotaar's back or on foot. When Miraak regenerates for the third time, Sahrotaar will meet his demise, as Miraak absorbs his soul.

## Notes
- Sahrotaar also appears briefly during the quest [The Temple of Miraak](Skyrim_The_Temple_of_Miraak.md), waiting for Miraak to mount him while talking to you.
- Sahrotaar will be the third dragon that has its soul absorbed by Miraak during gameplay, providing Sahrotaar has not been killed beforehand.
