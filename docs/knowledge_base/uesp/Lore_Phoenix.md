Phoenixes are mythical [bird](Lore_Bird.md) creatures present in [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOG-1)</sup> and [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DC-3)</sup> folklore. Little is known about phoenixes except for their intense red plumage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOG-1)</sup> **Aurbic Phoenix** imagery is associated with the school of [Restoration](Lore_Restoration.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MTAP-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SR-5)</sup>

The [Gryphon](Lore_Gryphon.md) Cel-hinwe was believed to be the phoenix by some members of the ancient Altmer tribe of progenitors of the Kinlords of [Sunhold](Lore_Sunhold.md) because of the intense red coloration of its feathers and how they appeared in the firelight in the day of its hatching.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TFOG-1)</sup> [Phoenix Moths](Lore_Moth.md#Phoenix_Moth) are moths native to [the Systres](Lore_The_Systres.md) isle of [Galen](Lore_Galen.md), and are known for their regenerative powers and affinity with ash and fire.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DC-3)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BGTGB-6)</sup> They are believed to be echoes of ancient times of wild magic.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LA-7)</sup> The [Druids](Lore_Druids.md) claim to have created them, though this is doubtful.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BGTGB-6)</sup>

Armor and [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) named after Phoenixes were sold in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-GEN-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Phoenix-9)</sup>
