Jinninji-ri was the [Mane](Lore_Mane.md) of the [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) people during the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), before the unification of the Sixteen Kingdoms. Little is known about Jinninji-ri's life. He was responsible for the installation of reflectors in the Temple of the Dance in [Dune](Lore_Dune.md) during one of the many temple refurbishings circa [1E 2902](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2902).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESOShazah-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOKhali-2)</sup> When aligned properly and attuned to the moonlight, those reflectors were capable of projecting visions of past events.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESORTD-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-The_Moonlit_Path-4)</sup>

He died at some point before the ascension of Mane [Zebiden-jo](Lore_Zebiden-jo.md) in circa [2E 182](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_182). His remains are preserved in the Tomb of the Manes in [Rimmen](Lore_Rimmen.md) Necropolis, a huge tomb complex located southwest of the Rimmen itself. He was buried together with other prominent Manes: Zebiden-jo and [Tullar-dra](Lore_Tullar-dra.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Jinninji-ri-5)</sup> His spirit's rest was briefly interrupted in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [The Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) escorted the remains of the ancient Khajiiti ruler Jora-ri to the burial chamber in the Tomb of the Manes.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RS-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Jora-ri-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Although [Rid-Thar-ri'Datta](Lore_Rid-Thar-ri%27Datta.md) was dubbed the First Mane there were numerous Manes, Jinninji-ri among them, who lived chronologically before him.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Other Mane-8)</sup> [Lawrence Schick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Lawrence_Schick) stated that it could be "transcription error, but that would invalidate all these lovely theories" and as such refused to confirm that notion.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LSP-9)</sup> Founding date of the [Pridehome](Lore_Pridehome.md) in the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) further confirms existence of Manes prior to the ascension of Rid-Thar-ri'Datta.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ONDoomstone Load-10)</sup>
- Khajiiti ritual icons depicting stylized lions, carved from large pieces of quartz were occasionally crafted during Jinninji-ri's life.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Rollin-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-QI-12)</sup>
- The recipe of a [drink](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md) known as Timestorm originated in Elsweyr during the reign of Mane Jinninji-ri.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-GBOOD-13)</sup>
