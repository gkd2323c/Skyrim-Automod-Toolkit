[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-npc-Traveling_Dignitary.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Traveling_Dignitary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Dignitary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Dignitary.jpg) Traveling Dignitary The **Traveling Dignitary** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who spawns in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s eastern end if the [Night Mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md) chooses a contract who asks you to kill this NPC in the quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). The Traveling Dignitary is one of ten potential targets chosen by the [Radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quest system.

The Dignitary wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086991) along with a pair of [fine boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086993). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat.

## Related Quests
- **[The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md)**: Yet another child has prayed to their [mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md).

## Dialogue

- *"I'm dying for a rest. I swear, I could lie down right now, and just sleep forever."*

## Notes
- The Traveling Dignitary may be difficult to kill as she is spawned in an area that is often highly populated with other NPCs. Therefore, it is recommended that the Traveling Dignitary be killed at night when most NPCs are no longer roaming the streets. You may consider killing the Traveling Dignitary from range with a bow or spell, or by using the [Poisoned](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md#Skill_Perks) perk to place poison in her pockets.
