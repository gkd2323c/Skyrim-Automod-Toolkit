[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/SI-place-Cylarne.jpg/200px-SI-place-Cylarne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Cylarne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Cylarne.jpg) Cylarne at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Cylarne was a ruin in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md) at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), located on the Isle of Flame. It is the oldest known ruin in the Isles,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TP-1)</sup> and is rumored to be the original capital of the Shivering Isles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LSSI-2)</sup> The ruins of Cylarne are home to the [Cold Flame of Agnon](Lore_Cold_Flame_of_Agnon.md), which is fed by the altars of Rapture and Despair.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESI-3)</sup> The [Dark Seducers](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) and [Golden Saints](Lore_Golden_Saint.md) constantly fought over controlling the ruin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESI-3)</sup>

In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) was sent to Cylarne to relight the Great Torch in [New Sheoth](Lore_New_Sheoth.md), which was extinguished by the coming [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md). The Champion put an end to the conflict by manipulating a planned attack from the Saints, and brought the holy Flame back to New Sheoth.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESI-3)</sup>
