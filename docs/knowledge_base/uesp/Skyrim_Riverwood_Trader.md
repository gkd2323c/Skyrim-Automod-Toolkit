[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-place-Riverwood_Trader.jpg/200px-SR-place-Riverwood_Trader.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverwood_Trader.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Riverwood_Trader.jpg) The Riverwood Trader The **Riverwood Trader** is a small general trade store located in [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md).

The store sells a variety of weapons, clothes, armor, and other items. It is run by local shopkeeper, [Lucan Valerius](Skyrim_Lucan_Valerius.md), and his sister, [Camilla Valerius](Skyrim_Camilla_Valerius.md). Lucan usually displays a beloved [Golden Claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw) decoration on the counter. When you first encounter them, the claw has been stolen by the thief [Arvel the Swift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arvel_the_Swift).

If anything happens to Lucan, Camilla will take over running the store. If they both die, it will be abandoned completely.

## Walkthrough

### Riverwood Trader
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader.jpg) Riverwood Trader downstairs In front of the shop are some piles of firewood, several barrels of [food](Skyrim_Food.md), and a cart filled with [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). At the rear are a flight of wooden stairs leading to the balcony above.

In the southern corner of the downstairs room is an L-shaped counter which Lucan stands behind when he is in the store. In addition to the [displayed merchandise](#Displayed_Merchandise) there are four loose [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) coins on the countertop as well as the [Golden Claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Golden_Claw), once you have retrieved it. Under the counter is a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), and hanging from the ceiling are two bunches of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md), a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid and a rack holding three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). Against the southwest wall is a cupboard and set of shelves filled with more displayed merchandise, while against the northwest wall is a barrel, an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) and a dresser containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md). Opposite the front door is a fireplace with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand in front of it. In front of the fireplace is a round wooden table and chair where Camilla sometimes sits when she is in the store. There are two bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), and a wedge of cheese on top of the table.

To the left of the fireplace is a rack hanging from the ceiling holding two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md) with a food barrel underneath and a set of shelves to the left holding only clutter. The stairs leading up to the residence above are against the northeast wall with a pile of firewood and some more food barrels underneath.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Riverwood_Trader_02.jpg) Riverwood Trader upstairs Upstairs there are two owned single beds in the southern and western corners, with a dresser and round wooden table between them. On the dresser are three common books and on the table are two bottles of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a common book and three loose gold coins. At the foot of both beds are novice-locked chests containing minor loot. In the center of the room is a long wooden table with three bottles of alto wine, several loaves of bread, and pieces of cheese. Against the southeast wall is a set of shelves holding an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace) and more clutter. In the northern corner, over the stairs, are a couple of unusable barrels and a square wooden table, with a door leading outside onto a balcony overlooking the main road through town.

## Notes
- All low-value items in the store can be freely taken once you have completed [The Golden Claw](Skyrim_The_Golden_Claw.md) quest.
- Before The Golden Claw is completed, the Riverwood Trader will remain open all day, every day, and Camilla and Lucan will never leave the store or sleep.
- After the Golden Claw is returned to Lucan and Camilla, it is displayed on the counter but is never for sale - not even with the "Merchant" perk. If you want it back again (for instance to display in one of your properties), you will have to steal it.
- If you place items in the cupboard behind Lucan's counter, they will show up in his inventory. Purchasing stolen items you place in there will remove their stolen tags. Don't put torches in there, though, as they won't add to what Lucan has for sale.
- Multiple internal references to this location bear the name "Lucan's Dry Goods", which is also the name that still gets displayed on your local map in the final game. - The store's related investor list bears the internal name of `Perk Investor Riverwood Whiteriver Trader List`, implying that "Whiteriver Trader" or "White River Trader" might've also once been an early name.
