# Grabber

The Grabbers are a people from the [Adjacent Place](Lore_Adjacent_Place.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOV27-1)</sup> and are said to "have never built a city of their own". The [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) teaches that the Grabbers tried to conquer [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) at some point by transforming the concept of [Ha-Note](Lore_Ha-Note.md) into a monster named [City-Face](Lore_City-Face.md). The monster was stopped by the god [Vivec](Lore_Vivec_(god).md), who showed the Grabbers the error of their false hope.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

The [Pocket Cabal](Lore_Pocket_Cabal.md) was able to cause Grabbers to come into the world sideways by causing [magical](Lore_Magic.md) anomalies that disrupted the normal non-cardinal points.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LOV26-3)</sup>

The [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md) claims to have an alternate form in the Adjacent Place. It has mentioned antagonistic beings it calls "antipodal math-grabbers" and claims that where it's from, the warriors wield swords made of pure math.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
