[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Mercenary.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Mercenary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Mercenary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Silver-Blood_Mercenary.jpg) A Silver-Blood Mercenary Three **Silver-Blood Mercenaries** can be found at [Sanuarach Mine](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine.md) in [Kathwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md). When you first enter the town, you'll find that the townsfolk are in disagreement with the mercenaries, who were sent by the Silver-Blood family in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to seize the mine, and are not allowing them to work there. You can choose to side with the leader of the mercenary band, [Atar](Skyrim_Atar.md), or to help the townsfolk during the related [quest](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine_(quest).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Sanuarach Mine](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine_(quest).md)**: End the dispute over [Sanuarach Mine](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine.md).

## Dialogue
When you approach the mine, they'll try to stop you, saying:

- *"We're in control of this mine. No sudden moves."*

After the quest, they will leave the mine and only say:

- *"Time to find another job."*

## Notes
- There is no bounty for killing the mercenaries inside the mine, even if you attack first.
