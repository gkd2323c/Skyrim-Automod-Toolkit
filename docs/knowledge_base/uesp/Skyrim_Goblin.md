# Goblin


It is possible to recruit a Goblin named [Gogh](Skyrim_Gogh.md) as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md). Unlike other Goblins who lack any weapons, Gogh wields a Daedric artifact known as the [Spear of Bitter Mercy](Skyrim_Spear_of_Bitter_Mercy.md).
