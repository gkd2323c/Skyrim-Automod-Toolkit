## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Feran Sadri](Skyrim_Feran_Sadri.md).
2. Retrieve both of the Amulets of Night Power.
3. Return to Feran.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Talk to [Feran Sadri](Skyrim_Feran_Sadri.md) in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) and he will tell you of two amulets which will augment your powers as a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md). He will direct you to two random locations, one containing the [Amulet of Bats](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Bats.md) and the other containing the [Amulet of The Gargoyle](Skyrim_Amulet_of_The_Gargoyle.md). The amulet may be in possession of a boss or in the chest at the end of a dungeon. Head to the two dungeons shown on your map, retrieve the amulets, and return to Feran, who will give you several [potions of blood](Skyrim_Potion_of_Blood.md) as a reward.

## Notes
- It appears that two additional amulets, the [Amulet of Draining Grip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Draining_Grip) and the [Amulet of Drain Magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulet_of_Drain_Magicka), may have been planned for this quest, but were later abandoned.

## Bugs
- Since the quest may choose a random corpse to place an amulet, it's sometimes possible for the corpse to have already vanished, leaving you stuck with a quest marker pointing to an empty location on the floor.
