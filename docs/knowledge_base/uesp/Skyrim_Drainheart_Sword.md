[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg/200px-SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Drainheart_Sword.jpg) A Drainheart Sword A Drainheart Sword is a translucent [Honed Ancient Nord Sword](Skyrim_Honed_Ancient_Nord_Sword.md) wielded by some of the ghostly [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) found within [Labyrinthian](Skyrim_Labyrinthian.md). It is a part of a set of similar phantom weapons, alongside the [Drainblood Battleaxe](Skyrim_Drainblood_Battleaxe.md) and [Drainspell Bow](Skyrim_Drainspell_Bow.md). Its physical appearance would be identical to the mundane version of this weapon were it not for its spectral look and the particles that flow out of it while moving with it equipped. A Drainheart Sword is enchanted to [absorb stamina](Skyrim_Absorb_Stamina.md) for fifteen points upon hit over a period of a second. It is not possible to [improve](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon.

## Notes
- The Drainheart Sword shares its appearance with [Ghostblade](Skyrim_Ghostblade.md).

## Bugs
- The weapon cannot be tempered. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. It can now be tempered using [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md), and it now benefits from and requires the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk.
- The absorb stamina enchantment differs from other absorb stamina enchantments, allowing you to disenchant it and have two absorb stamina enchantments. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) fixes this bug.
