*A fiery explosion for 40 points of damage in a 15 foot radius. Targets on fire take extra damage.* **Fireball** is an adept level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) over an area upon projectile impact and sets targets on fire for 30% of immediate spell damage, scaling down quadratically over 2 seconds.

## Effects
- [Fireball](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage), 40 pts in 15 feet for 2 sec
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Intense Flames Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md), 99 pts for 15 secs; if the [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames), raises damage to 50 at first rank or 60 at second rank
- [Aspect of Terror](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aspect_of_Terror), raises damage by 10 pts if [Intense Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Intense_Flames) is also unlocked<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:talk_Fireball)*]</sup>

## Notes
- Fireball will damage friends as well as foes in its area of effect (though it will not damage the caster). With this in mind, use this spell carefully if you have [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) who fight melee. Even [Protected NPCs](Skyrim_Protected_NPCs.md) (though not [Essential](Skyrim_Essential_NPCs.md) ones) can be killed in this manner.
- Like most Fire spells, it can be used to set off [Fire Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Rune) or [Oil Slicks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oil_Slicks) from a safe distance. Also, casting it or merely having it ready to cast in an area with [Flammable Gas](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Flammable_Gas) will result in a massive explosion.
- It is suggested that you aim for the target's feet rather than their body. This decreases their chances of avoiding damage if you miss.

## Bugs
- Spam casting this spell can cause the game to crash. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.342, fixes this bug.
