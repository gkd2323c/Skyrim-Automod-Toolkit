# Dawnguard Places


- **[The Aetherium Forge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Aetherium_Forge)** — An ancient Dwemer forge, guarded by several [Dwarven automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Grove.png) **[Ancestor Glade](Skyrim_Ancestor_Glade.md)** — A small glade in a cavern east-southeast of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ancestor+Glade+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Dwemer.png) **[Arkngthamz](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arkngthamz)** — A small [Dwarven ruin](Skyrim_Dwarven_Ruins.md) southeast of [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md) containing [Dwarven automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md), [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), and [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Arkngthamz+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md)** — A large [castle](Skyrim_Castles.md) on an island far northwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) connected to [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Volkihar+%28DG%29) - **[Volkihar Courtyard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Courtyard)** — The courtyard attached to [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) and [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md)** — A large [fortress](Skyrim_Castles.md) on an island far northwest of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) connected to [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md) which serves as the [Volkihar vampire clan](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md)'s home. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Castle+Volkihar+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Darkfall Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkfall_Cave)** — A small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) south of [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md) containing [frostbite spiders](Skyrim_Frostbite_Spider.md) and [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Darkfall+Cave+%28DG%29) - **[Darkfall Passage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkfall_Passage)** — A small cave only accessible through [Darkfall Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkfall_Cave) serving as the only access to the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).
- **[Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md)** — The wilderness area surrounding [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md), accessed through a cave entrance southeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dayspring+Canyon+%28DG%29) - [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Fort.png) **[Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md)** — A large [fort](Skyrim_Military_Forts.md) southeast of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) which serves as the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md)'s base of operations. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Fort+Dawnguard+%28DG%29) - **[Dead Drop Falls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dead_Drop_Falls)** — A medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) in [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md).
- **[Mossy Glen Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mossy_Glen_Cave)** — A small, unmarked bear den only accessible via [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md)** — A medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) east of [Stonehills](Skyrim_Stonehills.md) and southwest of [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md) where [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) is trapped. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Dimhollow+Crypt+%28DG%29) Contains a [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) which teaches part of the [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md) shout.
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Forebears' Holdout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forebears%27_Holdout)** — A small [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) southeast of [Dragon Bridge](Skyrim_Dragon_Bridge.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Forebears%27+Holdout+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Pass.png) **[Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md)** — An isolated region in northwestern Skyrim once inhabited by the [Snow Elves](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) worshipping the god [Auri-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auri-El). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Forgotten+Vale+%28DG%29) Contains a [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) which teaches part of the [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md) shout. - **[Darkfall Grotto](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkfall_Grotto)** — A small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).
- **[Forgotten Vale Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale_Cave)** — A small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md) containing [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) and one of the rare [shellbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug).
- **[Forgotten Vale Forest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale_Forest)** — A small grove in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), which can only be accessed by using the [paragon platform](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform) with the [ruby paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby_Paragon).
- **[Forgotten Vale Overlook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forgotten_Vale_Overlook)** — A small river valley in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), which can be accessed by using the [paragon platform](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform) with the [emerald paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Paragon).
- **[Glacial Crevice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glacial_Crevice)** — A small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md), which must be traversed to reach the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md).
- **[Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md)** — A relatively well-preserved portion of the Chantry of [Auri-El](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auri-El) in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md).
- **[Sharpslope Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sharpslope_Cave)** — A small cave in the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md) containing [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md), [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), and one of the rare [shellbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shellbug).
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Docks.png) **[Icewater Jetty](Skyrim_Icewater_Jetty.md)** — A small isolated [dock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Other_Places#Docks) far west of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and northwest of [Northwatch Keep](Skyrim_Northwatch_Keep.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Icewater+Jetty+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Moldering Ruins](Skyrim_Moldering_Ruins.md)** — A small [camp and concealed cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dwellings) west of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) containing [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md) and dead [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Moldering+Ruins+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Shack.png) **[Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md)** — A [shack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) northwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md) containing [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) and [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Redwater+Den+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Cave.png) **[Ruunvald Excavation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruunvald_Excavation)** — A medium-sized [cave](Skyrim_Caves.md) east of [Shor's Stone](Skyrim_Shor%27s_Stone.md) containing [Vigilants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Charmed_Vigilant) charmed by the conjurer [Minorne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Minorne). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Ruunvald+Excavation+%28DG%29)
- **[Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md)** — A [plane of Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Planes_of_Oblivion) haunted by lost [souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul). - **[Boneyard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boneyard)** — An enclosed graveyard in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).
- **[Reaper's Lair](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reaper%27s_Lair)** — An enclosed ruin in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

## Notes
- Several leveled lists for [creatures](Skyrim_Creatures.md) have been modified with the add-on, so it is possible to encounter [new creatures](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Creatures.md) in many places which existed in the original version of the game.
- The following changes are also made to these specific places which exist in the original game: - [Arcwind Point](Skyrim_Arcwind_Point.md) has an added [word wall](Skyrim_Word_Wall.md) which teaches part of the [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md) shout.
- The [Ruins of Bthalft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruins_of_Bthalft) area allows access to the Aetherium Forge with use of the four [Aetherium Shards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Aetherium_Shard) found during [Lost to the Ages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lost_to_the_Ages).
- The [Hall of the Vigilant](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Vigilant.md) is destroyed if you are above level 10. (Otherwise it is destroyed upon completion of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md) quest.)
