[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/24/SR-npc-Legate_Hrollod.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Legate_Hrollod.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Hrollod.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Legate_Hrollod.jpg) Legate Hrollod **Legate Hrollod** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) commanding the [Eastmarch Imperial Camp](Skyrim_Eastmarch_Imperial_Camp.md). He spends all his time in the camp and is not hostile toward you, unless you spend too long in the camp and aren't a member of the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). Should the Legion win the [war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) by taking [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) and killing [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), Hrollod will take up the place of [Yrsarald Thrice-Pierced](Skyrim_Yrsarald_Thrice-Pierced.md) in the court of the new [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md), [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md) at the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md).

Hrollod wears [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Boots.md) and [bracers](Skyrim_Imperial_Bracers.md). He wields an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md). For ranged combat, he carries an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md). He carries a number of [steel arrows](Skyrim_Steel_Arrow.md) and his sleep outfit, a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md).

## Dialogue
When you have not joined either the Imperial Legion or the Stormcloaks he can say:

- **How do you feel about this war?**
- *"You mean Ulfric's so-called "[Stormcloak rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md)"? The Nord kings - oh, excuse me, "[Jarls](Skyrim_Jarl.md)" - they can't seem to control their own people. So the Legion has stepped in to keep order. Sadly, the Empire's stretched a little thin these days, and we've gotten very few reinforcements. So we've been forced to recruit locally. But rest assured, citizen. We'll put an end to this uprising... and things will get back to normal soon enough."*

- **What's the Imperial Legion doing in Skyrim?**
- *"Not sure I understand the question. The Legion's always been in Skyrim. We have garrisons throughout all the provinces of the Empire. Our mission is to serve the [Emperor](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md), uphold the law, and protect the citizenry. Skyrim's days are darkening, and the Legion will soon be called into service like never before. But we stand ready, as always, to meet that call."*

- **How do I join?**
- *"Think you've got the mettle, eh? Honestly, the Legion can use all the able bodies it can muster. We have our hands full. A [dead king](Skyrim_High_King_Torygg.md), a rebellion, and now [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md)? Skyrim's a mess. If you're serious, make your way to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). Our headquarters is [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md). You find [General Tulius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md)* [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * there, commanding our forces in Skyrim. But talk to [Legate Rikke](Skyrim_Legate_Rikke.md). She handles the local recruiting."*
