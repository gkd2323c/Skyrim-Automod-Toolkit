[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bc/SR-npc-Reclamation_Priest.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Reclamation_Priest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reclamation_Priest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Reclamation_Priest.jpg) Reclamation Priest The **Reclamation Priest** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) priest of the [New Temple](Skyrim_Temple.md) living in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). He can be found inside the Temple during the quest [Her Word Against Theirs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Her_Word_Against_Theirs).

He wears the [robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priest_Robes), [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priest_Hood), and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priest_Boots) of a Temple Priest. He carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger), a [Raven Rock Temple key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raven_Rock_Temple_Key), and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor). He knows the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spells [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) and [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

You can choose to betray the heretical [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md) by handing their [propaganda letters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Propaganda_Letter) to this priest as part of the related quest. If you chose to do this, he will later appear within [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) in the company of three members of the [Redoran Guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) and will attack the Temple faithful within.
