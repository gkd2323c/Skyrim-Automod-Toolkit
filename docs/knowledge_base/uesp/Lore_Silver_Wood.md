[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/ON-place-Sil-Var-Woad.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sil-Var-Woad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sil-Var-Woad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sil-Var-Woad.jpg) Sil-Var-Woad circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Sil-Var-Woad is a zoo located on the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), within the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Sil-Var-Woad is the host of the **Royal Menagerie**, which holds a wide range of exotic animals that come from the mainland of Tamriel, including [Mammoths](Lore_Mammoth.md) and [Lions](Lore_Lion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It even serves as the home of the pets owned by the [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) royalty. The village of Sil-Var-Woad would be renamed into **Silver Wood**, following the arrival of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md), as they wrote maps of the Summerset Isles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History
During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Royal Menagerie of Sil-Var-Woad was the home of Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md)'s childhood pet, Lotus, an [Indrik](Lore_Indrik.md). At the time, Indrik was taken care of by an Altmeri Keeper named Talomar, while the zoo was administrated by Conservator Vinelore. Vinelore was opposed to Queen Ayrenn's open borders policy for the Summerset Isle, which allowed non-High Elves into the sub-continent. Vinelore had conducted sacrilegious experiments on the local Indriks, attempting to learn about their abilities, and turning them into more powerful and violent beasts. All to attain the High Elves' natural primacy over the mainland races of Tamriel. To investigate these experiments, a member of the [Eyes of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md) had sabotaged the zoo, causing the animals to ransack across Sil-Var-Woad, closing the site to the public. Talomar and a [Newcomer](Lore_Soulless_One.md) soon underwent an investigation themselves, and ended up uncovering Vinelore's plot. The Newcomer managed to put an end to the plot and Vinelore herself before it involved Lotus. Accounts vary whether Talomar attempted to rebuild the zoo, or simply let the animals go free.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Silver Wood was still an active settlement, being under the reign of Lord Coreman. It had a rivalry with [Thorheim Guard](Lore_Thorheim_Guard.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arena-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Dusk](Lore_Dusk.md) in the southwest, and [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md) in the northwest.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Map Arena-5)</sup>
