# Vivec's Lyre

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/ON-item-Vivec%27s_Lyre.jpg/200px-ON-item-Vivec%27s_Lyre.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Vivec%27s_Lyre.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Vivec%27s_Lyre.jpg) Vivec's Lyre Vivec's Lyre is a musical instrument in the possession of the [Tribunal](Lore_Almsivi.md) god [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md). Vivec's use of his Lyre is emphasized as one of the many contributors to his dualistic nature, being a warrior of the Blade and bard of the Lyre.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vt WP1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MTCV-2)</sup> Vivec is sometimes given the appellation "the Lyre".<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vt WP1-1)</sup> The instrument is often depicted alongside Vivec's spear [Muatra](Lore_Muatra.md) in various shrines,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Shrine MWsoitworks-3)</sup> and tapestries as the harp and spear are considered symbols of Vivec.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cont Cufflink-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Banner ONsoitworks-5)</sup> As a musician and a Warrior-Poet, Vivec often writes his own ballads that he then sings in his temple.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VDO-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-OSDO-7)</sup>
