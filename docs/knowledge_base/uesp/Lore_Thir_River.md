[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/ON-place-Thir_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Thir_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Thir_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Thir_River.jpg) Thir River The Thir River (or *River Thir*)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CC-1)</sup> is a large waterway in central [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), flowing through the regions of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) and [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Thir ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MWMap-3)</sup> It travels from the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) in the north to the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) in the east.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ARMap-4)</sup> Its spring is found between [Lagomere](Lore_Lagomere.md) and [Siltreen](Lore_Siltreen.md) close to [Silnim Dale](Lore_Silnim_Dale.md) in the southeast,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DSH-5)</sup> it then continues as a stream to Tal'Deic Fortress, where the Thir widens considerably. Flanking the fertile [Deshaan Plain](Lore_Deshaan_Plain.md), it flows out east into the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) and west to the ancient House capitals of [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), where it empties into [Lake Amaya](Lore_Lake_Amaya_(Deshaan).md) (also called Lake Thir),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LD-6)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CC-1)</sup> and south to [Narsis](Lore_Narsis.md), where it splits off into a tributary called the Loqueach River.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DSH-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CC-1)</sup> In the north, it flows out from a water source atop the peaks near the Obsidian Gorge, and heads north through the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(place).md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONNilae-7)</sup> meeting the lava flows of [Ash Mountain](Lore_Ash_Mountain.md) and [Tormented Spire](Lore_Tormented_Spire.md), before it encircles the islands of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md). Its delta is found around Ebonheart and [Vivec's Antlers](Lore_Vivec%27s_Antlers.md), straight south of [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_(city).md), where the Thir River empties into the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Thir ESO-2)</sup>
