# Voice Actors

| --- |

*[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)* features a cast of more than 70 **voice actors**, many of whom are also notable film and television actors. Although some NPCs have unique voices, the large majority of them have one of more than 50 "voice types", where generic lines of dialogue, such as greetings, may be reused across many different characters. The list below is organized by voice actor, and does not list every voiced NPC—for more comprehensive lists of NPCs, organized by voice *type*, see [this category list](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Voice Types).

## Main Characters

### Max von Sydow
[Von Sydow](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Max_von_Sydow) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001884/)) plays [Esbern](Skyrim_Esbern.md), a chronicler and agent of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) who has survived in hiding.

### Christopher Plummer
[Plummer](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christopher_Plummer) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001626/)) plays [Arngeir](Skyrim_Arngeir.md), a powerful [Greybeard](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) elder.

### Joan Allen
[Allen](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joan_Allen) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000260/)) plays [Delphine](Skyrim_Delphine.md), one of the last remaining members of the Blades.

### Michael Hogan
[Hogan](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Michael_Hogan_(Canadian_actor)) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0389581/)) plays [General Tullius](Skyrim_General_Tullius.md), General of the Imperial Legion in Skyrim.

### Vladimir Kulich
[Kulich](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vladimir_Kulich) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0474520/)) plays Nordic Jarl [Ulfric Stormcloak](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md), leader of the Stormcloak Rebellion.

### Wes Johnson
[Johnson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Wes_Johnson) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0426432/)) returns to the *[Elder Scrolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:The_Elder_Scrolls)* series to play [Sheogorath](Skyrim_Sheogorath.md), [Emperor Titus Mede II](Skyrim_Emperor_Titus_Mede_II_(real).md), the [Spectral Assassin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spectral_Assassin), the [Wretched Abyss](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wretched_Abyss) (Hermaeus Mora), and once again voices the [Dremora](Skyrim_Dremora.md). He also plays [Hermaeus Mora](Skyrim_Hermaeus_Mora.md) in the *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* add-on.

### Peter Jessop
[Jessop](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peter_Jessop) ([IMDb](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0422247/)) plays [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md), the First Dragonborn, and the main antagonist of the *[Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)* add-on.
