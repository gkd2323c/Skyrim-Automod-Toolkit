[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4e/SR-creature-Goat.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Goat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Goat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Goat.jpg) A Goat **Goats** are non-aggressive animals generally found on mountain slopes, though they can become hostile and attack while under the influence of a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md). They are hunted for their [hides](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Hide), [meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leg_of_Goat), and [goat horns](Skyrim_Goat_Horns.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>, but occasionally drop nothing. In addition to those found in the wild, domestic goats are found around farms and towns. Attacking someone's livestock will earn you a bounty.

## Hearthfire
The *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) lets you build a goat statue on a small base in the [trophy room](Skyrim_Trophy_Room.md) addition of any homestead. The materials needed are:

## Notes
- With the addition of the *Hearthfire* add-on, goats can drop horns for house improvements. However, the horns that they drop are completely different in size, shape and color from what is on the goats themselves.
- The hides goats drop are the only instance in the game where two hides are needed to produce one leather.
