[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/21/MW-place-Caldera_Mine.jpg/200px-MW-place-Caldera_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Caldera_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Caldera_Mine.jpg) The Caldera Mines ca. 3E 427 Caldera Crater is a large crater surrounded by steep hills near the tail-end of the [West Gash](Lore_West_Gash.md), in the province of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). For some time, it was a location of little note that grew the unique Caldera Mushroom<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONFelder-1)</sup> and contained the ancestral tomb of the Uveran clan, one of Vvardenfell's thirty revered families.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ATOTTRF-2)</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), it was the den of a dangerous [nix-ox](Lore_Nix-Ox.md), and the site was known by locals as **Nilthog's Hollow**.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONNilthog-3)</sup> In the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a rich deposit of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) was discovered here and the **Caldera Mine** was built by the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) to extract it.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWCaldera-4)</sup> It is the namesake of the mine's charter town, [Caldera](Lore_Caldera.md).

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/MW-place-Caldera.jpg/200px-MW-place-Caldera.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Caldera.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Caldera.jpg) The Imperial charter town of Caldera Little can be said about Caldera Crater throughout history. The Uveran clan hid their clue to the ancient [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md) in their ancestral tomb,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTombs-5)</sup> meaning they could have built it here as early as the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), contemporary to the era of High Velothi Culture.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TLOA-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-BTAOM-7)</sup> In the mid-Second Era, a rogue nix-ox named Nilthog the Unbroken took residence in the crater, but Bravosi Felder discovered a new species of mushroom there and hired the [Ordinators](Lore_Ordinators.md) of [Vivec City](Lore_Vivec_City.md) to extract them. The Ordinators passed this task to [outlanders](Lore_The_Vestige.md) who not only defeated the nix-ox but extracted the mushrooms.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONOxen-8)</sup>

After Vvardenfell was re-opened to the rest of the Empire in [3E 414](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_414), the [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) quickly secured land and were often at odds for specific pieces of territories.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ASHOM-9)</sup> [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) coveted the land around Caldera Crater and the [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) -based Caldera Mining Company was granted exclusive rights by the Empire to harvest the [raw ebony](Lore_Raw_Ebony.md) in it.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWGen Caldera-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWGen Rumors-11)</sup> Some of the most important people behind the mines were Odral Helvi, the Hlaalu governor of Caldera,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MWOdral-12)</sup> Cunius Pelelius, a charter-holder for the mine,<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-MWPelelius-13)</sup> and Stlennius Vibato, the mine's boss,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MWVibato-14)</sup> who used [slave](Lore_Slavery.md) labor led by the head slave, Dahleena.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-MWDahleena-15)</sup> [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) was infuriated with the land grab, claiming that the mine's charter town Caldera was in their territory and that they should have gotten the charter for mining.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MWGen Caldera-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWGen Rumors-11)</sup>

The other competing Great Houses, the Redoran and Telvanni plotted to undermine the operation and House Hlaalu's involvement. House Telvanni sent four agents to the mines, where they committed several thefts and disappearances. The [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) was hired to get rid of them.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-MWAgents-16)</sup> House Redoran, especially Councilor Garisa Llethri was adamant in shutting it down by destroying it from the top. A Redoran [agent](Lore_Nerevarine.md) was sent to Caldera to find evidence of corruption. In the Governor's Hall, they found a secret ledger that proved Odral Helvi was siphoning funds from the mines to fund a personal project called the Ashlands Management Fund. After Odral Helvi was exposed, he was arrested and sent to the mainland to be put on trial.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-MWCorruption-17)</sup> Next was to have the mine shut down, at least temporarily. The Redoran agent learned that if the head slave Dahleena was removed then it would halt production. Whatever method they used is unknown, but their plan was successful.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-18)</sup>
