[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/AR-place-Raven_Spring.jpg/200px-AR-place-Raven_Spring.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Raven_Spring.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Raven_Spring.jpg) Raven Spring during the Imperial Simulacrum Raven Spring is a small village in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), located in the foothills of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) near the border of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). Raven Spring is mentioned in the historical-fiction series, [King Edward](Lore_King_Edward.md), in which the [titular character](Lore_Edward.md) and his companions travel to a village of [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md) in a high meadow near Raven Spring.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Raven Spring was an active settlement. It was ruled by Baron Andoryan and it had a rivalry with [Cloud Spring](Lore_Cloud_Spring.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including Dunkarn Haven to the south, Karthgran Vale to the north, and Hammerfell to the southeast, towards [Dragonstar](Lore_Dragonstar.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
