# Perena

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/ON-crown_store-Nedic_Perena_Armor.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Nedic_Perena_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Nedic_Perena_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Nedic_Perena_Armor.jpg) Traditional Perena armor The **Perena** were a tribe of [Nedes](Lore_Nede.md) that inhabited [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Lady Clarisse-1)</sup> and [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Watchers-2)</sup> They were prominent followers of the [Cult of the Stars](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Watchers-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Explorer-3)</sup> and members of the [Duraki](Lore_Duraki.md) kingdom.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> When [Virmaril the Betrayer](Lore_Virmaril.md) murdered and subjagted the Nedic kings at Skyreach, the seat of Duraki power, three Perena [soul mages](Lore_Soul_Magic.md) - Garalan, Armok, and Shavmar - descended into the city's catacombs and sealed the [lich](Lore_Lich.md) behind a ward that drained his power.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Watchers-2)</sup> Much of the race's history, including the sealing of Virmaril, is only known through a relic dubbed the Perenaal Fragment.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Explorer-3)</sup> Other sources about Perena existed and were preserved, however. One of these was their legend that the Cult of the Stars learned soul magic from a "white-bearded stranger" who shared similarities with [Shezarr](Lore_Shezarr.md), the God of Man.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TFOS-8)</sup>
