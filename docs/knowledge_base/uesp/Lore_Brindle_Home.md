## History
During the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), Castle Brindle was an expansive and strategically important fortress. It had its own farm, mine and lumbermill, and was heavily fortified. It was considered to be in the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md)'s territory.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

By the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), nothing remained of the keep. Brindle Home consisted of a small village and stables. For a time, an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) warrior named Melus Petilius served as the town's guardian, killing off hordes of [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) when they attacked the settlement and clearing out a nest of [minotaurs](Lore_Minotaur.md) single-handedly. However, Petilius swore never to raise a weapon again when his wife died of a fever while he was away fighting, and he built a cabin outside of town to be closer to her grave. His piousness disgusted [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), who sent the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to taunt him at his wife's grave in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433). Enraged, Petilius killed the Hero with a cursed mace, damning himself and allowing Bal to claim his [soul](Lore_Soul.md). The Hero was later returned to life by the Prince to torment Petilius further.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
