[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/SR-npc-Ugor.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ugor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ugor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ugor.jpg) Ugor **Ugor** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [ranger](Skyrim_Ranger_(class).md) found in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

She was supposed to sleep from midnight to 8am, but doesn't due to an oversight (see [bugs](#Bugs)). Outside quest related events, she just hangs out inside [Largashbur Longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md). She wears [steel armor](Skyrim_Steel_Armor.md) with matching boots and gauntlets, and carries a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md) and [steel shield](Skyrim_Steel_Shield.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Cursed Tribe](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md)**: Help lift the curse placed on the [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) of [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md) by [Malacath](Lore_Malacath.md).
- **[Rebuilding the Blades](Skyrim_Rebuilding_the_Blades.md)**: Recruit new members for the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Cursed Tribe
She is likely first encountered with a group of other Orc warriors in combat with a [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) outside the locked gate of Largashbur. Helping defeat the giant is recommended, as she can easily fall during the attack. Her survival will lead to a scripted argument between herself and [Atub](Skyrim_Atub.md) that would unlock the gate and start the [related quest](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md). She will first direct her attention to you:

**Ugor**: *"Halt! You have no business here, outsider. Leave at once."*
**Atub**: *"Ugor, no! This may be the one we need."*
**Ugor**: *"We need nothing from outsiders! [Yamarz](Skyrim_Chief_Yamarz.md) will provide for us."*
**Atub**: *"We cannot carry on this way! You know we are doomed if we do not do something!"*
**Ugor**: *"Yamarz charged me with keeping outsiders away from Largashbur. You would have me disobey him?"*
**Atub**: *"You were charged with keeping us inside the walls. Have faith, Ugor. I only wish the best for our tribe."*
**Ugor**: *"Fine, it's your neck."*

If Ugor still lives after completing [The Cursed Tribe](Skyrim_The_Cursed_Tribe.md), she will join as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) should you ask.

## Dialogue
After The Cursed Tribe is complete she can say:

- *"The days of staying within Largashbur's walls are over."*
- *"Malacath demands much of us now. We will not fail him again."*
- *"Gularzob has been chosen as chief. He will one day be great, but he has much to learn."*

## Notes
- Although her default gear is governed by the Heavy Armor skill, Ugor is actually more proficient in light armor.
- When first met, Ugor's behavior is caused by a package known as `DA06Archer Hold Position`. However, Ugor is not an archer and does not normally carry a bow or arrows.
- Ugor has a lower minimum level than the other Orcs at Largashbur. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, raised her minimum level to 10, but version 4.2.4 reverts this.

## Bugs
- Ugor's sleep package is overridden by her sandbox package, so she never sleeps. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- It is possible to find the corpses of Ugor and the other unnamed Orcs, unable to start the event that would unlock the gate, leaving you to find another way to enter Largashbur to talk to Atub. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.

- It is possible to jump over the wall instead.
