**Strangulation** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that [absorbs the health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) of a target, upon projectile impact, and heals the caster over 3 seconds.

## Effects
- [Absorb Health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md), 16 points for 3 seconds.

## Perks
- [Apprentice Destruction](Skyrim_Apprentice_Destruction.md), reduces the spell cost by 50% to 42 magicka.

## Notes
- [Dwarven Spheres](Skyrim_Dwarven_Sphere.md#Dwarven_Sphere), [Dwarven Spiders](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md#Dwarven_Spider), and [Dwarven Centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md#Dwarven_Centurion) are all resistant to *Strangulation*. As such, no damage will be dealt by the absorption effect, nor will any health be received in return.
- This spell also [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes#Strangulation) in the [Spell Tomes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Spell_Tomes) [official add-on](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Downloads) for *[The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)*.
