[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-npc-Shadr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Shadr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shadr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shadr.jpg) Shadr **Shadr** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) living in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He works as [Hofgrir Horse-Crusher's](Skyrim_Hofgrir_Horse-Crusher.md) assistant in the [stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md). A newcomer from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), the unfortunate Shadr recently fell for a scam orchestrated by [Sapphire](Skyrim_Sapphire_(person).md), and now owes her a considerable amount of gold. During the [related quest](Skyrim_Stabilized.md), you will have to convince Sapphire to forget about his debt and leave him alone.

Having learned the equestrian trade back in his native Hammerfell, Shadr spends all of his time with the horses, under the watchful eye of stable owner Hofgrir Horse-Crusher. He sleeps in the stable building every night between midnight and 6am and tends to the horses for the rest of the day, at random times taking breaks inside the house for a quick meal. If Hofgrir dies, however, Shadr will change his daily routine completely and will almost abandon his beloved horses. He will still get out of bed at 6am but will start the day with a one-hour meal, then spend all day in Riften, preferably seated on the bench where he is found speaking with Sapphire upon first entering town. At 9am he will browse the market for four hours and will return to the stables for meals at 1pm and 6pm.

He wears a set of typical farmer's [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a6) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000209a5) and wields a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) (up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality at level 20). He carries the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riften_Stables_Key) to the stables, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
When working at the stables, Shadr will share his passion for horses and provide a bit of background information on his employer and mentor:

*"Hofgrir is a great teacher. I've learned so much about horses from him."* *"Horses are all I know. I've been riding since I could walk."* *"If the forest wasn't so dangerous, I'd spend every spare moment riding."* He can also talk about his job at the stables, the way he does so is dependent on many factors:

*"If it's horses you want, you'll want to speak to Hofgrir. I'm afraid I'm in too much trouble to help you."* **(Stabilized not completed, Hofgrir dead*, not at the stables)** *"If it's horses you want, you'll want to speak to Hofgrir at the stables."* **(Stabilized completed, Hofgrir alive, not at the stables)** *"If it's horses you want, I'm not sure I can help you. I have too much on my mind as it is."* **(Stabilized not completed, Hofgrir dead, not at the stables)** *"If it's horses you want, you're talking to the right person."* **(Stabilized completed, [Balimund](Skyrim_Balimund.md) dead*, at the stables)** **You work at the stables?** *"Yes. Hofgrir took me in when I arrived in Riften about a year ago. He's been teaching me everything he knows. Horses are my whole life. Back in Hammerfell, I helped my family raise horses at our farm. I hope to open my own stables one day, but I think it's going to take a lot longer than I expected."* **(If Hofgrir is alive)**

## Bugs
- Shadr's line "If it's horses you want, you'll want to speak to Hofgrir. I'm afraid I'm in too much trouble to help you." incorrectly checks if Hofgrir is dead, rather than alive. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.7, fixes this bug.
- Helping Shadr will allow you to ride the horses at [Riften Stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md) for free, due to him being in the same ownership faction as the stablemaster. The problem is that horses cost 1,000 gold, making them way too valuable to let stablemasters let you ride them for free after completing a small favor. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. ([details](https://afktrack.afkmods.com/index.php?a=issues&i=19062)) The ownership of the horses was changed to just Hofgrir, rather than the faction, preventing this issue.
- Shadr's four lines about his work at the stables have an incorrect value on a condition which checks whether he has the Job Hostler keyword and could never play as a result. One of these lines was also incorrectly checking if Balimund was dead, when the line is about Hofgrir. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.2, fixes this bug.
