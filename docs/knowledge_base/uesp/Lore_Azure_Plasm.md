# Azure Plasm


When a [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) native to Coldharbour is slain or banished, its morphotype, or vestige, is sent back there. Within Coldharbour are secret grottoes where the process of plasm-accretion can be witnessed. It is here the Azure Plasm drains from pools adjacent to the grottoes and slowly coalesces around a vestige to eventually reform the Daedra. It is usually a terrifying and lengthy process for the Daedra involved, although it can be prevented through resurrection with the use of [Soul Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Soul_Magic).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> The process is identical for mortals whose soul is under the protection of a [Daedric Prince](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Prince), such as [Soul Shriven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Soul_Shriven); the Azure Plasm coalesces around a vestige and forms a corporeal body with the semblance of the shape it wore in life.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup> Should this individual die, the nature of the vestige is such that their body simply reforms upon death and the cycle repeats itself.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CCTAP-3)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Jaseen-10)</sup>

A phenomenon that involves Chaotic Creatia causing a physical transformation in regions of Nirn has been observed around sites of Oblivion incursion. During the Planemeld, the Chaotic Creatia of Coldharbour would seep into Nirn in areas around [Dark Anchors](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md), and, responding to the will of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), gradually transform the infested land to mirror Coldharbour itself.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GBAYQ-11)</sup>

The first [Daedric Titan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedric_Titan) is said to have been formed when the skeleton of the [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) [Boziikkodstrun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Boziikkodstrun) was submerged in a pool of Azure Plasm, an act which is said to have given the Titan its body, brain, and brawn.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DDTT-12)</sup>
