# Gold Coast Trading Company

[![ON-banner-Gold Coast Trading Company.png](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c9/ON-banner-Gold_Coast_Trading_Company.png/100px-ON-banner-Gold_Coast_Trading_Company.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-banner-Gold_Coast_Trading_Company.png) The Gold Coast Trading Company (**GCTC**),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CG-1)</sup> was a company that was a seller of goods, during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). These goods included dangerous creatures, imported from other areas of Tamriel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TCG-2)</sup> As their name suggests, they operated in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). Before her assassination, the famous pirate [Fortunata ap Dugal](Lore_Fortunata_ap_Dugal.md) rose in the ranks to become a powerful shipping magnate in the company.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PQOTGC-3)</sup> The company's sigil featured a [triskelion](Lore_Triskelion.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Logo-4)</sup>

In the years prior to the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Gold Coast Trading Company had a significant role in the construction of the [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) settlement of [Seyda Neen](Lore_Seyda_Neen.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> Despite Gold Coast's role in the village's growth it was administered and protected by House Hlaalu.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The Gold Coast Company was also responsible for distributing Crown Crates around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), which included [experience scrolls](Lore_Scrolls.md), potions, and poisons produced by the Company.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Logo-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Rewards-7)</sup> Some of the contents rewarded by the crates were smuggled items that appeared on the open market, such as [vamidiums](Lore_Vamidium.md) smuggled out of the vaults below [Arcane University](Lore_Arcane_University.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Dwarven Crate-8)</sup> The crates were delivered by Pacrooti and sold for [Crowns](Lore_Currency.md#Crowns), with the [Crown Gems](Lore_Currency.md#Crown_Gems) being rewarded in exchange for unwanted rewards.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Pacrooti-9)</sup>
