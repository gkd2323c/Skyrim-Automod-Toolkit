[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-npc-Eimar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Eimar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eimar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Eimar.jpg) Eimar working in the Honningbrew Meadery **Eimar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who works at the [Honningbrew Meadery](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Meadery.md).

He wakes at 6am for a one-hour breakfast, then starts his workday. During work, he can usually be found sitting at the bar, drinking, or cleaning the place. He takes a break at noon to eat for one hour. His workday ends at 8pm, when he has a four-hour dinner before going to bed at midnight.

Eimar wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006C1D8) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0003452F). He wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) in combat.

## Related Quests
- **[Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits)**: Put [Maven Black-Briar's](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) competition out of business.

## Dialogue
Eimar seems very happy with his job and has nothing but praise when speaking to him:

*"If you need to sample some of the finest mead in Skyrim, you're in the right place."* *"If you're looking to purchase any mead, speak to the owner, [Sabjorn](Skyrim_Sabjorn.md)."* *"We're a small meadery, but growing quickly. Sabjorn's only been running this place for a short time now and look at it."* *"[Mallus](Skyrim_Mallus_Maccius.md) is teaching me everything he knows about brewing. Hopefully, I can take over his position some day."* However, after the quest [Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits), he will no longer be as enthusiastic:

*"It's a shame the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) took this place over. I never liked it as much as our [Honningbrew](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead)."* *"If you're looking to purchase any mead, speak to the brewmaster, Mallus."*
