[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7a/DF-map-Santaki.png/200px-DF-map-Santaki.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Santaki.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-map-Santaki.png) Santaki ca. 3E 405 Santaki is a small, landlocked county in the northern portion of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and is located in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. Santaki's position between the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) and the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) provides it access to two different biomes. The desert wastes in the southwest half of the county and the forests in the smaller northeast half.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFSantaki-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFMap-3)</sup> The [Knights of the Hawk](Lore_Knights_of_the_Hawk.md) is Santaki's local [knightly order](Lore_Knights.md) but despite that, they are mainly based in [Antiphyllos](Lore_Antiphyllos.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFHawk-4)</sup>

## History
The region is primarily populated by the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) who live across Hammerfell but other minor races and subcultures also live in Santaki, namely the [Orcs](Lore_Orc.md) who live in their stronghold of Ratttor.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDungeons-5)</sup> The [Khulari](Lore_Khulari.md) are the most predominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFSantaki-2)</sup>

By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), Santaki was ruled by an unnamed Count and its patron deity was [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md), who is represented through the [Temple of Stendarr](Lore_Temple_of_Stendarr.md). The county was mostly surrounded by unconquered territories, specifically the Alik'r Desert to the west, and the Dragontail Mountains to the east, but to the south, it was bordered by [Dak'fron](Lore_Dak%27fron.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFMap-3)</sup> In [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), the [Miracle of Peace](Lore_Miracle_of_Peace.md) occurred across the Iliac Bay, and the smaller nations of the region were annexed by the main kingdoms. Santaki was taken over by the [Kingdom of Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), at the time ruled by Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWITW-6)</sup>

## The City of Santaki
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/DF-Location-27709-Automap.png/200px-DF-Location-27709-Automap.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-27709-Automap.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-Location-27709-Automap.png) Map of Santaki (city) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/DF-place-Santaki_%28city%29.png/200px-DF-place-Santaki_%28city%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Santaki_(city).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-place-Santaki_(city).png) Santaki (Daggerfall) **Santaki** is a walled city located in Hammerfell and it is the capital of the eponymous county in the northern section of the province. It is found in the desert, near the border toward Dak'fron.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-DFcity-7)</sup> By the fifth century of the Third Era, Santaki was the region's only city, and as such, had access to most services in the area. The only [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) chapters in Santaki were available here and the Bank of Santaki had only one other business in the region, that being in the town of Badai.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFSantaki-2)</sup>
