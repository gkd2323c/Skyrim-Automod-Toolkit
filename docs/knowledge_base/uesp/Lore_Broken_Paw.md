# Broken Paw

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/df/ON-npc-Kauzanabi-jo.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Kauzanabi-jo.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kauzanabi-jo.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Kauzanabi-jo.jpg) Kauzanabi-jo, a member of the Broken Paw The Broken Paw is one of many [Khajiiti](Lore_Khajiit.md) clerical orders. The Lunar Clergy comprises various monastic orders, including the Broken Paw, [Brotherhood of the Fang](Lore_Brotherhood_of_the_Fang.md), the [Order of the Crescent](Lore_Order_of_the_Crescent.md), and the Order of the Claw. Unlike some of the more combat-oriented orders, the Broken Paw places a greater emphasis on scholarly reflection rather than aggressive combat. Some Broken Paw members were active in [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md) during [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Kauzanabi-jo-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Azahrr-2)</sup>
