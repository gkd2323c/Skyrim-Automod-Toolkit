# Einar

| Race | Unknown | Gender | Male |
| Resided in | [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) | | |

**King** Einar was the ruler of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) at some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582). Little is known of his reign, but maps of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md) from his times were known for their depictions of fantastic creatures along the edges of the parchment. Such maps were still found around the [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Seond Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Contraband-1)</sup>

## Notes
- According to some sources, Mount Einar was a mountain neighboring [High Hrothgar](Lore_High_Hrothgar.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPFrom The Ashes-2)</sup> Its connection to King Einar is unknown, however.
