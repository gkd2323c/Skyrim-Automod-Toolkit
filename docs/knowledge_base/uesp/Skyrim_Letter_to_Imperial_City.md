Letter to Imperial City **by [General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md)**
---
This is the third letter I am attempting to send to the [Imperial Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) for guidance in the matter surrounding the threat to [Fort Frostmoth](Skyrim_Fort_Frostmoth.md).

A sizable [dark elf](Skyrim_Dunmer.md) force has established a stronghold on the southwestern portion of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). I've given them ample opportunity to surrender, but all attempts to communicate with them have met with conflict.

I will continue to do whatever's necessary to ensure the safety of Fort Frostmoth and rid [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) of these invaders until I receive orders stating otherwise.

[General Falx Carius](Skyrim_General_Falx_Carius.md)
Garrison Commander, Fort Frostmoth
