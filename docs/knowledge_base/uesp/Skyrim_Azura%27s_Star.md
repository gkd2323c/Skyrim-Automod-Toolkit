[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg/200px-SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg) Azura's Star Azura's Star is a Daedric Artifact awarded to you by [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) if you purify her broken star by returning it to [Azura's Shrine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrine_of_Azura) as part of [The Black Star](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Star) quest; you will also get the [priestess](Skyrim_Aranea_Ienith.md) as a follower. If you return it to [Nelacar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nelacar) instead, the reward is the [Black Star](Skyrim_The_Black_Star_(item).md). The star is a zero weight reusable grand soul gem. Recharging weapons or enchanting objects with Azura's Star merely uses up the soul that it was carrying, not the star itself, unlike what happens to a regular soul gem. The possibility of multiple uses makes the star beneficial for both [enchanters](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) and other adventurers.
