[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-creature-Skeggr.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Skeggr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeggr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Skeggr.jpg) Skeggr **Skeggr** is a skeleton that can be found underwater, near a capsized rowboat, just north of the [Wreck of the Winter War](Skyrim_Wreck_of_the_Winter_War.md). He is next to [Advard](Skyrim_Advard.md).

He can also be carrying a leveled one-handed weapon on him.

To the west of the capsized boat, you can find their small camp. On one of the crates there is a [fisherman's journal](Skyrim_Fisherman%27s_Journal_(camp).md).
