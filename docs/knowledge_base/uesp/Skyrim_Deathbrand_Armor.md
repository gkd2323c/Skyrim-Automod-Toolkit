[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/31/SR-item-Deathbrand_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Deathbrand_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Deathbrand_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Deathbrand_Armor.jpg) Deathbrand Armor The Deathbrand Armor is the chest piece of a set of armor that also includes [boots](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Gauntlets.md) and a [helm](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Helm.md). It can be found in a treasure chest that has been unearthed by [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) to the north-northwest of the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md), but only after you find the [treasure map](Skyrim_Deathbrand_Treasure_Map.md) during the related quest. The armor's appearance is identical to [stalhrim light armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stalhrim_Light_Armor) and also has the same weight and [armor rating](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md). Its enchantment [fortifies your stamina](Skyrim_Fortify_Stamina.md) by 15 points for every Deathbrand item you wear, maxing out at 60 points with the entire set equipped. It can be tempered with a piece of [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md), with the [Ebony Smithing](Skyrim_Ebony_Smithing.md) perk doubling improvement.

In life, the armor was worn by [its eponymous owner](Skyrim_Haknir_Death-Brand.md), an infamous pirate captain. However, on the eve of his death, he drew a map and ordered his quartermaster, [Garuk Windrime](Skyrim_Garuk_Windrime.md), to divide his armor and bury a piece at each location throughout [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md), as he didn't believe that any of his crew were worthy of succeeding him.
