# Cheesemancy

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/10/LG-cardart-Cheesemancer.png/200px-LG-cardart-Cheesemancer.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Cheesemancer.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Cheesemancer.png) A Cheesemancer Cheesemancy is an arcane art focused on the manipulation of [cheese](Lore_Cheese.md). Those who practice this art are known as Cheesemancers. While it has a loose connection to the mad inhabitants of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cheesemancer-1)</sup> its existence is recognized among the common people of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SG-2)</sup><sup>:139-140</sup> Among the known practitioners of this art were some [Imperials](Lore_Imperial.md). Cheesemancers had the ability to command cheese blocks to levitate. Some cheesemancers regarded the cheese associated with their craft as delicious, considering some others to have poor taste.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cheesemancer-1)</sup>

[Ja'dasha](Lore_Ja%27dasha.md), author of *The Survival Guide to Tamriel*, regarded Cheesemancy as one of the magical arts that defies the strict classifications of the Schools of Magic, placing it alongside other arcane practices like [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md), [auramancy](Lore_Auramancy.md), and [weather magic](Lore_Weather_Magic.md). However, she expressed uncertainty about the practical applications of a magical art focused on manipulating cheese.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SG-2)</sup><sup>:139-140</sup>
