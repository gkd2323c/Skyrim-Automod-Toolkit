[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-place-Abandoned_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-place-Abandoned_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Abandoned_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Abandoned_Shack.jpg) The Abandoned Shack The **Abandoned Shack** is an isolated [house](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shacks) in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md), east of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

This is where you will awaken the first time you sleep after completing [Innocence Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost) and receiving the [mysterious note](Skyrim_Mysterious_Note_(DB).md). The quest [With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md) will automatically start upon awakening. This is also where you can instead begin [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) by killing [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md), which will cause the previous quest to fail. The shack cannot be accessed before this point, as the door is locked and requires a key; however, it remains unlocked after you leave it.

## Related Quests
- **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md)**: Repay your debt to [the Family](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), and show you are willing to kill on command.
- **[Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md)**: Finish the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) and rid Skyrim of their kind.

## Abandoned Shack
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7f/SR-interior-Abandoned_Shack.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Abandoned_Shack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Shack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Abandoned_Shack.jpg) The shack's interior Upon awakening in the shack, you will see in the southwest corner an empty set of shelves with Astrid sitting on top. Against the west wall is an empty, unlocked chest, a small cage, an empty dresser, and another set of shelves. The north wall has a cart, an empty barrel, a fireplace with piles of firewood on either side, and a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) in front. Against the east wall are three kneeling prisoners— [Fultheim the Fearless](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fultheim_the_Fearless), [Alea Quintus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alea_Quintus), and [Vasha](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vasha) —with their hands bound and their faces covered by [execution hoods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Execution_Hood). There is another small cage in the southeast corner. There is a locked door (key required) in the center of the south wall, which leads outside. To the left is a bench, some more firewood, and a chair, while to the right is a mead barrel and a sack. Towards the middle of the room is an unowned single bed with a small table next to it. There is blood splattered over the south, east and west walls. You must kill at least one of the prisoners (or Astrid) in order to get the key either by being given it by Astrid or by taking it off her body. The map marker for the shack is only added after Astrid gives you the key, unless you have already found it prior to being brought here. The shack will remain uninhabited, aside from the corpses of anyone you killed.

## Notes
- South-southeast of the shack is a hilltop with seven stone pillars, marking the ruins of some forgotten structure. Scattered about the ruins are a burial urn, a novice-locked chest, and the body of a [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) containing a copy of the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Sacred Witness](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sacred_Witness)*. The chest is found behind some rubble, underneath a twisted tree. The burial urn and draugr are on the opposite side of the collapsed pillar, behind the chest. Two [corundum ore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore) veins can be found nearby.
- The [Blade of Woe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blade_of_Woe) as well and a full set of Shrouded equipment ([Shrouded Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Armor), [Shrouded Gloves](Skyrim_Shrouded_Gloves.md), [Shrouded Boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Boots), [Shrouded Cowl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shrouded_Cowl)) can be obtained by killing Astrid if you choose not to join the Brotherhood.

## Bugs
- Installing [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) may cause a bad navmesh inside the Abandoned Shack, which disconnects the door. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- If When Friends Like These... is set to transport you here while [The Ghost of Old Hroldan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ghost_of_Old_Hroldan) is about to begin, that quest would be interrupted and potentially broken by the kidnapping scene. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, addresses this issue. The fact that these happen to be the only two quests in the vanilla game that require you to sleep is likely why this went unnoticed for so long.
