# Chicken

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ab/SR-creature-Chicken.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Chicken.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Chicken.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Chicken.jpg) A chicken Chickens are docile, domesticated [birds](Lore_Bird.md) common throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). They are bred for their [eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chicken%27s_Egg) and meat. Although mainly encountered around farms and smaller settlements, they can often be found within cities and towns. Female chickens are known as **hens**, while males are known as **roosters** or **cockerels** (or simply **cocks**).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

[Badgers](Lore_Badger.md) are known to prey on chickens, making them a common nuisance for farmers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

## Breeds

### Alcaire Blue Cockerel
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/ON-crown_store-Alcaire_Blue_Cockerel.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Alcaire_Blue_Cockerel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Alcaire_Blue_Cockerel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Alcaire_Blue_Cockerel.jpg) Alcaire Blue Cockerel The cockerels of [Alcaire Castle](Lore_Alcaire_Castle.md) are known for their loud, incessant, and punctual crowing. They make noise from the early hours well into the morning.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

### Betva-Hen
A type of a hen that lives in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). Their feathers are often worn by [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) elders.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PWF-4)</sup>

### Black Cockerel
The Black Cockerel is native to White Rose. Its coloring is caused by a diet of toxic beetles, which stain its flesh black.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

### Cropsford Hen
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/ON-pet-Cropsford_Hen.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Cropsford_Hen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Cropsford_Hen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Cropsford_Hen.jpg) Cropsford Hen The Cropsford Hen is known for its protective nature. It is named for the town of [Cropsford](Lore_Cropsford.md) but is widely used across Cyrodiil.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

### Crusader's Coin Chicken
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/ON-crown_store-Crusader%27s_Coin_Chicken.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Crusader%27s_Coin_Chicken.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Crusader%27s_Coin_Chicken.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Crusader%27s_Coin_Chicken.jpg) Crusader's Coin Chicken The Crusader's Coin Chicken has flaming red feathers. This aggressive barnyard fowl has been used as an alternative to guard dogs by knights on crusade. They crow quite loudly.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

### Daemon Chicken
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/ON-pet-Daemon_Chicken.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Daemon_Chicken.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Daemon_Chicken.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Daemon_Chicken.jpg) Daemon Chicken Assumed to be simple black chickens by some, the Daemon Chicken is speculated to originate from the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) or [Ashpit](Lore_Ashpit.md), lays flaming eggs, and has eyes that glow red after dark.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Pet DC-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Pet DCK-9)</sup>

### Riverwood White Hen
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/ON-crown_store-Riverwood_White_Hen.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Riverwood_White_Hen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Riverwood_White_Hen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Riverwood_White_Hen.jpg) Riverwood White Hen The Riverwood White Hen is a hardy domestic chicken found all along Skyrim's [White River](Lore_White_River.md), said to have been originally bred in the old town of [Riverwood](Lore_Riverwood.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Pet RWC-10)</sup>

### Mara's Spring Chicken
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/37/ON-pet-Mara%27s_Spring_Chicken_02.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Mara%27s_Spring_Chicken_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Mara%27s_Spring_Chicken_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Mara%27s_Spring_Chicken_02.jpg) Mara's Spring Chicken As a sign of the blessing of [Mara](Lore_Mara.md), certain breeds of chickens (such as the Spring Chicken) lay eggs with shells in a variety of colors.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Pet MSC-11)</sup>
