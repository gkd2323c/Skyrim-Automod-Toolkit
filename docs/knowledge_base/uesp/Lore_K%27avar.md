[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/DF-npc-Lord_K%27avar.png/200px-DF-npc-Lord_K%27avar.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_K%27avar.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-npc-Lord_K%27avar.png) Lord K'avar in the citadel of Sentinel Lord K'avar was a noble from the Kingdom of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> He was a veteran of the [War of Betony](Lore_War_of_Betony.md). During a battle in the [Glenpoint](Lore_Glenpoint.md) Foothills, he received a disfiguring injury from a fireball cast by a [Dark Elf](Lore_Dark_Elf.md) mercenary nightblade.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFCN-2)</sup> After the war, K'avar commanded the [Order of the Candle](Lore_Order_of_the_Candle.md) along with Lord Vhosek. Their knightly order was charged with the protection of Sentinel, replacing the defunct [Knights of the Moon](Lore_Knights_of_the_Moon.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFGuide-3)</sup>

In the years prior to the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Lord K'avar allied himself with the [Order of the Black Worm](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md), plotting to overthrow Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md) and usurp the throne of Sentinel. K'avar went into hiding once the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) got involved, although he was later arrested and imprisoned in the dungeons of Castle Sentinel. He then escaped and fled to Castle [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md). Akorithi ordered his execution, but it is unknown if this was carried out.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFK.27avar1-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFK.27avar2-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DFK.27avar3-6)</sup>
