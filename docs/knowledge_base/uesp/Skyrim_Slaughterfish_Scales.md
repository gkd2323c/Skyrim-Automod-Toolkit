## Ingredients
71 guaranteed samples can be found in 32 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 in [Glenmoril Coven](Skyrim_Glenmoril_Coven.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 6 in [Tolvald's Cave](Skyrim_Tolvald%27s_Cave.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 5 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 4 in [Japhet's Folly Towers](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly_Towers.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 4 in the [Thalmor Embassy, Elenwen's Solar](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy,_Elenwen%27s_Solar.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 4 in [Vittoria Vici's House](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Slaughterfish scales are always dropped by [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md).
