## Eastmarch Imperial Camp
It is a very small and compact Imperial camp, consisting of a central campfire with a [cooking spit](Skyrim_Cooking_Spit.md) hanging over it, around which the rest of the camp is arranged. To the west is a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block). To the northeast is the command tent, containing an end table and a large table covered with a map of Skyrim showing the progress of the civil war and the various forts and cities held by each side. This is where the Legate spends most of their time. To the left of the command tent is the Legate's tent, containing an owned single bed, with an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) at the foot of the bed and an end table beside it. To the left, forming the northwest side of the camp, are three small tents containing unowned bed rolls. To the left of the tents is the hospital tent, which contains three bed rolls occupied by three wounded soldiers and a low table holding a random [potion of health](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Health) and a random [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). Outside is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Labs.md). To the southwest is the quartermaster's tent, in front of which is the workstation for the [Imperial quartermaster](Skyrim_Imperial_Quartermaster.md), consisting of a long wooden table between an [anvil](Skyrim_Anvil.md) on the left, a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) on the right, and a [workbench](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to the left of the tent. On the table and inside the tent are various [iron](Skyrim_Iron_Weapons.md) and [steel weapons](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Weapons) and [armor](Skyrim_Armor.md). Farther to the east are two picketed [horses](Skyrim_Horse.md). They are owned, and taking them is considered stealing.

Any time [Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md) is controlled by the Imperials, this camp will not be present. As long as you haven't completed [Joining the Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Joining_the_Stormcloaks.md), you are free to sleep in the small tents and trade with the quartermaster.

## Notes
- After receiving orders for the Battle for Windhelm, the camp will be removed.
- The camp [reappears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Eastmarch_Camp) in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*.
