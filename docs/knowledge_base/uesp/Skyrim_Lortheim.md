[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a7/SR-npc-Lortheim.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Lortheim.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lortheim.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Lortheim.jpg) Lortheim **Lortheim** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [priest](Skyrim_Priest.md) who lives and works in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) at the [Temple of Talos](Skyrim_Temple_of_Talos.md) with his wife, [Jora](Skyrim_Jora.md).

Lortheim spends his entire life inside the Temple, and sleeps next to his wife every night between midnight and 8am, interrupted only by a one-hour breakfast at 6am. He then drifts around the Temple hall all day long and only stops for a one-hour dinner at 6pm. Despite what his wife claims, he never sets foot in the [Palace of the Kings](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings.md) (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

He wears [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat, and carries some [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md) and [hooded monk robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Monk_Robes.md).

## Dialogue
Lortheim is a dedicated follower of Talos stating:

- *"I've spent my life serving Talos. I don't plan to stop now, no matter what the Empire says."*

He continues to offer his opinion on the ban of Talos worship:

- *"Talos created the Empire. You'd think they'd show him more respect."*

He hopes that one day the ban on Talos worship will end, stating:

- *"Someday all of Skyrim will worship Talos openly, as we do here."*

As long as [Fort Amol](Skyrim_Fort_Amol.md) is in Stormcloak hands, Lortheim will give his view on the war:

- *"The way I see it, [Ulfric](Skyrim_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md)'s fighting a holy war."*

When leaving conversation, Lortheim will offer you blessings;

- *"May Talos shield you from all harm."*
- *"May Talos bless your days."*

If you side with the Empire during the civil war quests and kill Ulfric Stormcloak, Lortheim will question the future of Talos worship:

- *"With Ulfric dead and the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) gone, who will have the courage to champion the cause of Talos?"*

## Notes
- Lortheim wears blue mage robes, which are commonly worn by court mages, rather than [hooded monk robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Monk_Robes.md), which are usually worn by priests and priestesses. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
