## Ingredients
132 guaranteed samples can be found in 74 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 7 in [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))
- 5 in [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 5 in [the White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 4 in [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 4 in [Fellglow Keep](Skyrim_Fellglow_Keep.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 4 inside [Labyrinthian Chasm](Skyrim_Labyrinthian_Chasm.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 4 in [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other guaranteed samples include:

- The three dead skeletons found during the quest [Unbound](Skyrim_Unbound.md) always carry bone meal.
- The burned corpses found at the [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md) in the quest [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) always carry bone meal.

## Creatures
Bone meal is always dropped by [dragon priests](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), including all of the named dragon priests who carry [masks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mask). Nearly all other types of undead have a 25% chance of dropping bone meal, with the exception of [ghosts](Skyrim_Ghost.md) (who drop [ectoplasm](Skyrim_Ectoplasm.md)) and [zombies](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Plague_of_the_Dead.md)</sup> (who drop [mort flesh](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mort_Flesh)) . This includes all varieties of [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), [corrupted shade](Skyrim_Corrupted_Shade.md) and [wrathmen](Skyrim_Wrathman.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, as well as numerous named undead creatures and already-dead versions of the creatures.

## Notes
- There is a unique version of bone meal named [Berit's Ashes](Skyrim_Berit%27s_Ashes.md) that shares the same properties as regular bone meal. Combining these two ingredients will therefore result in all four effects being discovered at once.
- Bone meal is also used in the crafting and tempering of [Bonemold armor](Skyrim_Bonemold_Armor.md).
