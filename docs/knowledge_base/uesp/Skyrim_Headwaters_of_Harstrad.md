[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg/200px-SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) Headwaters of Harstrad The **Headwaters of Harstrad** are the source of the [Harstrad River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Harstrad_River).

It can be found a short distance downhill to the southeast from the entrance to [Benkongerike](Skyrim_Benkongerike.md), or by following the river upstream from the [Wind Stone](Skyrim_Wind_Stone.md) to the west-southwest.

## Related Quests
- **[Healing a House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Healing_a_House)**: Help [Elynea Mothren](Skyrim_Elynea_Mothren.md) repair the tower of [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md).

## Exterior
The cave entrance is found behind the waterfall cascading down a rock face just below the entrance to Benkongerike. On the bank as you approach the waterfall is a leveled [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) that will attack as soon as it detects you. There is a [pearl oyster](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster) next to a [nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) downstream on the south shore, another on the north shore just before you reach the first large rock that is partially in the water and a third can be found underwater on the river bed between these two.

## Harstrad Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/42/SR-interior-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Headwaters_of_Harstrad.jpg) Headwaters of Harstrad The entrance places you in a naturally lit, shallowly flooded cave, with a rock pillar directly in front of you. There is a waterfall descending the wall to the southeast, and a leveled spriggan standing on a raised area to the right of you, which will attack as soon as you are detected. At the top of the dry bank on the right is a lootable [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md) in front of an unlocked boss level chest. If you are here as part of the relevant quest, you may activate the headwaters by looking at and "activating" the shallow pool. There is nothing else of interest in the cave.
