[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-item-Gauldur_Blackblade.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gauldur_Blackblade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gauldur_Blackblade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gauldur_Blackblade.jpg) The Gauldur Blackblade The Gauldur Blackblade is a [Honed Ancient Nord Sword](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord.md#Ancient_Nord_Weapons) found at [Folgunthur](Skyrim_Folgunthur.md) in the possession of [Mikrul Gauldurson](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mikrul_Gauldurson). The weapon's enchantment [absorbs health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) from whoever is slashed by it over one second. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) the Gauldur Blackblade requires one [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. As is customary for [Honed Ancient Nord weapons](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord.md#Ancient_Nord_Weapons) (a.k.a. "Draugr weapons"), the benefits of tempering are doubled if you have acquired the [Dwarven Smithing](Skyrim_Dwarven_Smithing.md) perk.

## Notes
- It is possible to obtain two Gauldur Blackblades, one from Mikrul in Folgunthur, and one from his spirit form in [Reachwater Rock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachwater_Rock). To obtain the latter, the fallen spirit Mikrul has to be looted before he teleports back to his brothers.
- It appears from testing (see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Gauldur_Blackblade#leveling)) that the level of the Gauldur Blackblade is set either when you read the book to gain the quest or when you get near enough to the dungeon that the entrance "spawns". This doesn't mean actually finding the entrance, just getting close enough that the game loads the dungeon entrance cell. If you want a high level item, you should avoid reading the book and avoid the geographic areas around the dungeons.
