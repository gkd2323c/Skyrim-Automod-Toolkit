# Bat

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-creature-Bat.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Bat.jpg) A bat [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-crown_store-Bat_Swarm%2C_Domesticated.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Bat_Swarm%2C_Domesticated.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Bat_Swarm,_Domesticated.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Bat_Swarm,_Domesticated.jpg) A bat swarm Bats are flying mammals often found in caves and dungeons, traveling in groups. They are associated with [Vampires](Lore_Vampire.md), as Vampire Lords can transform into a cloud of bats, or use bats to attack enemies in combat. Tamriel's bats are diverse in diet and appearance.

**Giant Bats** are also found throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) and [Solstice](Lore_Solstice.md). Certain breeds have been bred for their milk in [Ska'vyn](Lore_Ska%27vyn.md) and [Goldfolly](Lore_Goldfolly.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

## Species

### Brown Steeple Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b6/ON-crown_store-Brown_Steeple_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Brown_Steeple_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Brown_Steeple_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Brown_Steeple_Bat.jpg) Brown Steeple Bat The **Brown Steeple Bat** is a prolific insectivore.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

### Dappled Cactus Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/ON-crown_store-Dappled_Cactus_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Dappled_Cactus_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Dappled_Cactus_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Dappled_Cactus_Bat.jpg) Dappled Cactus Bat The **Dappled Cactus Bat** is a species found in arid climes that feeds primarily on cactus fruit. Nicknamed "Friends of the Caravan", trackers may follow their guano to find safe sources of cacti to rehydrate with when in need.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

### Duskfire Nectar Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e2/ON-crown_store-Duskfire_Nectar_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Duskfire_Nectar_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Duskfire_Nectar_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Duskfire_Nectar_Bat.jpg) Duskfire Nectar Bat The **Duskfire Nectar Bat** feeds on flower nectar, and has a vibrant orange pelt. They are a common sight around farms that cultivate flowering fruit.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

### Glacier Bat
**Glacier bats** are a type of bat native to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They can be found in the dungeons surrounding the city of [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

### Long-Winged Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/ON-prerelease-Long-Winged_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Long-Winged_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Long-Winged_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Long-Winged_Bat.jpg) Long-Winged Bat **Long-Winged Bats** are prolific insectivores.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

### Skav Bat
**Skav Bats** are giant dairy bats from the Caverns of Wry, located north of [Ska'vyn](Lore_Ska%27vyn.md). The locals harvest the bats' milk and ferment it, creating a [cheese](Lore_Cheese.md) that is moist, crumbly, and piquant.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LHALAYQ-7)</sup>

### Snow Throat Fruit Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/ON-crown_store-Snow_Throat_Fruit_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Snow_Throat_Fruit_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Snow_Throat_Fruit_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Snow_Throat_Fruit_Bat.jpg) Long-Winged Bat The **Snow Throat Fruit Bat** possesses natural camouflage that hides them from predators in snowy weather.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

### Venin-Bat
**Venin-bats** are a species found in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), known to be a nuisance to residents of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>

### Xylo Pollen Bat
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/ON-crown_store-Xylo_Pollen_Bat.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Xylo_Pollen_Bat.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Xylo_Pollen_Bat.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Xylo_Pollen_Bat.jpg) Xylo Pollen Bat The **Xylo Pollen Bat** is another nectarivore that pollinates flowers along [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md)'s [Xylo River](Lore_Xylo_River.md). They are an integral part of the forest's ecosystem.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup>
