# Suthay-raht

*When Jode is New and Jone is Waning
Then shall be born the Suthay-Raht
Born to be tested
Among Mother's Others
Fit to endure harsh punishment
Burden bearers, they stand alone
And in the end we will see their triumph.*
— *[All Our Perfect Forms](Lore_All_Our_Perfect_Forms.md)* [![](https://images.uesp.net/a/a6/Mnew_Swaning.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Swaning.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Mnew_Swaning.jpg) Suthay-raht are born when Masser is new and Secunda is waning<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> The Suthay-raht are a bipedal [furstock](Lore_Khajiit.md#Furstocks) of [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md). They are sometimes, incomprehensibly for Khajiit themselves, nicknamed "Ja-Khajiit" or "Ja'Khajiit".<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> They are similar in build to the races of man, but like [Suthay](Lore_Suthay.md), they have digitigrade legs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWKhajiit-4)</sup> They are typically similar in height to men, but some are said to have trouble fitting in small spaces.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Survival G-5)</sup> They are completely covered in fur of different colors and patterns and have a tail. Claws are present on hands and feet, and their heads appear very cat-like.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWKhajiit-4)</sup>

Suthay-raht are known to be good jumpers, agile, sneaky and having a bold spirit. This makes them good adventurers and traders. They are however not as good warriors as Cathay-raht.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Khajiit Dialogue MW-6)</sup> They are said to be fit to endure harsh punishment, described as burden bearers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOPF-1)</sup> Suthay-raht are known to purr and hiss.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Khajiit Voiced MW-7)</sup> They also have slight phallic barbs.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DGFP-8)</sup>

The Suthay-raht furstock was commonly sighted in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-RDKMW-9)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IWTB-2)</sup> specifically on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), towards the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Khajiit Dialogue MW-6)</sup>
