[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a4/SR-item-Konahrik.jpg/200px-SR-item-Konahrik.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Konahrik.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Konahrik.jpg) Konahrik [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Spectral_Dragon_Priest.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Spectral_Dragon_Priest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Spectral_Dragon_Priest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Spectral_Dragon_Priest.jpg) Spectral Dragon Priest summoned by the mask Konahrik is a [golden](Skyrim_Gold.md) [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) [mask](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest_Mask.md) gained by obtaining all other masks from the base game and bringing them to [Bromjunaar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Bromjunaar_Sanctuary.md).

The artifact's enchantment has a chance to trigger a variety of effects when the wearer's health is reduced below 15%. With a chance of 15%, it causes an explosion that knocks back enemies and items in a moderate radius (1024 units, approximately 15m), as well as granting a [Flame Cloak](Skyrim_Flame_Cloak.md) lasting for 12 seconds, and casting [Grand Healing](Skyrim_Grand_Healing.md). With a chance of 3%, the above effect happens as well as [Conjure Dragon Priest](Skyrim_Conjure_Dragon_Priest.md) being cast, summoning a Spectral Dragon Priest for 60 seconds.

Konahrik can be [improved](Skyrim_Workbench.md) with [gold ingots](Skyrim_Gold_Ingot.md).

## Notes
- The full description of the enchantment *Konahrik's Privilege* is: *"When low on health, has a chance to heal wearer and damage nearby enemies, or summon a powerful Dragon Priest defender"*.
- The dragon priest summoned by the mask's enchantment counts as a conjured creature. It can cast [Incinerate](Skyrim_Incinerate.md), [Thunderbolt](Skyrim_Thunderbolt.md), [Icy Spear](Skyrim_Icy_Spear.md), [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md), [Lightning Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Cloak), [Dread Zombie](Skyrim_Dread_Zombie.md), [Greater Ward](Skyrim_Greater_Ward.md), and [Conjure Storm Atronach](Skyrim_Conjure_Storm_Atronach.md) (though its storm atronach does not count as yours). After the spell ends, the dragon priest will vanish in a cloud of black smoke, sometimes leaving behind an unlootable ash pile.
- The player must be looking at a surface within casting range in order for the Dragon Priest to spawn. - The range is affected by the [Summoner](Skyrim_Conjuration.md#Summoner) perk.
- All dragon priest masks are considered to be circlets in terms of equipment slots, meaning they can be worn simultaneously with some bugged items, as detailed under [circlets](Skyrim_Circlet.md).
- Konahrik means "Warlord" in the tongue of the [dragons](Skyrim_Dragons.md).
- Konahrik has the highest base armor rating of any helmet in the base game, surpassing even the [Daedric Helmet](Skyrim_Daedric_Helmet.md). From level 45 onward, [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(item).md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> has the highest armor rating.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
- Due to the necessity of having [Nahkriin](Skyrim_Nahkriin_(item).md) (as well as all the other priest masks), it is impossible to legitimately get this mask until after Alduin has been defeated and the main quest completed, since there is no way back from Skuldafn until then.
- This is a good mask to give to followers, since they get to low health more easily than the player without the risk of dying.
- Konahrik is the only Dragon Priest mask that is not worn by a Dragon Priest, or having a priest that shares its name. It's also one of two Dragon Priest masks that are unique in appearance; the other is Miraak.
