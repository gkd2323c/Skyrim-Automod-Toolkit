[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-item-Space_Core.jpg/200px-SR-item-Space_Core.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Space_Core.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Space_Core.jpg) Space Core The Space Core is a mysterious alien construct that suddenly plunges from the sky and crashes on the ruined house [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?centeron=Space+Core) near [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md), setting the ruins on fire. It is constantly animated, saying a line of dialogue when activated, placed in your inventory, and idle. Many of these lines refer to the land and events of Skyrim.

The Space Core can be taken to the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) in [The Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) and placed on the pedestal to power the forge. By placing an ingot of [Refined Moonstone](Skyrim_Refined_Moonstone.md) (or a piece of [Moonstone Ore](Skyrim_Moonstone_Ore.md)) and an [Iron Helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md) into the offering box, a [Dovahcore Helmet](Skyrim_Dovahcore_Helmet.md) can be made.

If blasted hard enough, like by an Ao E spell or a Giant's club, the Space Core will be launched back into space and removed from the game.
