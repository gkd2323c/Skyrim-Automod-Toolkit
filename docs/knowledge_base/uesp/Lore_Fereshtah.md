*Truly the Divines have shown me the answer. For has not the greatest Forebear Grandee, Ebrahim of Sentinel, a fair and lissome daughter, Fereshtah? And has not the greatest Crown Grandee, Murahd of Rihad, a fierce and clever daughter, Arlimahera?*
— [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup> **Queen** Fereshtah was a prominent [Forebear](Lore_Forebears.md) [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) noblewoman active during the mid– [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was a contemporary of High King [Ja-Fr](Lore_Ja-Fr.md) and his successor [Ar-Azal](Lore_Ar-Azal.md), who became her husband and whom she married alongside the Blood Queen [Arlimahera](Lore_Arlimahera.md). She was the daughter of [Ebrahim](Lore_Ebrahim.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), the greatest Forebear Grandee of her time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/AR-place-Hegathe.jpg/200px-AR-place-Hegathe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Hegathe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Hegathe.jpg) Hegathe in the Third Era [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/ON-place-Sentinel.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sentinel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sentinel.jpg) Sentinel in the Second Era Little is known of Fereshtah's life. Her father Ebrahim hailed from the kingdom of Sentinel, and by the time of High King Ja-Fr's death and Ar-Azal's accession to the throne, he was regarded as the greatest Grandee among the Forebears. As of then Fereshtah was known for her grace and beauty.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

When Ar-Azal became king, he enjoyed little support from either faction: the Forebears distrusted him because he was a [Crown](Lore_Crowns.md), while the Crowns resented him because his father had failed to destroy the Forebears. Ar-Azal prayed in his High Tower overlooking the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and sought counsel from Admirable Zaqeeb, a Priestess of [Satakal](Lore_Satakal.md) and his mentor. She gave him a sacred draught to guide his dreams, and he beheld Satakal in the form of a "Snakehead Potentate", who told him he would prove a worthy king if he solved a riddle whose answer lay at the Shrine of [Tava](Lore_Tava.md). Awakened by the cry of Tava's sacred goshawk, Ar-Azal went to the shrine and studied a fresco of the bird in its nest with two mates. Taking this as a sign Ar-Azal sought to mend the divide by [marrying](Lore_Marriage.md) both Fereshtah and Arlimahera, the daughter of Grandee [Murahd](Lore_Murahd.md) of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md), who held a position among the Crowns comparable to that of Fereshtah's father among the Forebears, thereby bringing Hammerfell closer to unification under the banner of the [Phyllocid Dynasty](Lore_Phyllocid_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWAA-1)</sup>

Little is known of Fereshtah's political role as Ar-Azal's wife, but Queen Arlimahera became remembered as a ruthless leader and conqueror in her own right.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBOD-2)</sup> In any case, the descendants of this union of three held positions of power throughout Hammerfell for generations.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TRLo S-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TABKF-4)</sup>
