Thonar's Journal **by [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md)** *Revelations that Thonar Silver-Blood is having secret dealings with the Forsworn* *
---
[Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) is becoming unruly. You'd think that 20 years in prison would calm a beast like him down a bit. Maybe I should have let the [Jarl](Skyrim_Igmund.md) execute him after the uprising after all.

Still, he's been invaluable in getting rid of several "problems" over the years. Maybe I'm overreacting. No one knows about our little arrangement. Not even the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). I wonder how they would react knowing their "King in Rags" was one of my most important assets?

This little shadow rebellion of his better not start to include me, though. If I find out he's even thinking about double-crossing me, I'll make sure he dies inside [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md), like the animal he is.
