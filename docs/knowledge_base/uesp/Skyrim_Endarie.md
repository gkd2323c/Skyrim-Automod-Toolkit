[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Endarie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Endarie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Endarie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Endarie.jpg) Endarie **Endarie** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [tailor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tailor) living in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). She runs [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md) along with her twin sister [Taarie](Skyrim_Taarie.md), and she can be found behind the counter in the store while Radiant Raiment is open. Endarie's personality is not very welcoming, and she is frequently rude to you. If you invest in her shop, however, her [disposition](Skyrim_Disposition.md) towards you will increase.

She sleeps from midnight to 8am, then wakes up to work in Radiant Raiment. At 8pm she closes the store and spends the rest of the day relaxing around the store.

Endarie wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006ff45) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0004223d). She carries the [key to the store](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Radiant_Raiments) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When you approach her, she may greet you with:

*"Oh, what delight. Another charming customer..."* *"Let me just drop everything to help you. I don't mind. Really."* *"I can help with purchases, but if you came to talk, see my sister. She's the one who gets on with people. It's her one virtue, really."* *"So good to see you again. How can I be of service?"* Speaking to her, if you have completed [Lights Out!](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lights_Out!), [Tending the Flames](Skyrim_Tending_the_Flames.md), or [The Wolf Queen Awakened](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Wolf_Queen_Awakened), or have invested in her business, will change her dialogue:

**Do you get a lot of business like that?** *"Radiant Raiment has the finest attire in all of Skyrim. That is how we get business."* *"I suppose I would, but you're the only one who gets that greeting."* *"Oh, we do all right. Rumor is, you had a hand in the Blackblood Marauders' demise... I'm impressed."* *"Radiant Raiment has the finest attire in all of Skyrim. That is how we... Wait. You're that new bard, the one who saved the Festival. Aren't you? That was a good bit of work."* *"It's never been an issue. People say you helped with the problems at Wolfskull Cave. I applaud anyone who assists the court."* Her dialogue will also change depending on your disposition with her:

**Bad day?** *"You'd think so, wouldn't you? I'm fine. Thank you for asking."* *"It is when my customers waste time chatting, and not buying."* **You seem to be in a good mood.** *"Only when you're around. The rest of these fools I could do without."* Her goodbyes:

*"Finally..."* *"Thank the gods for the end of yet another pointless conversation..."*
## Conversations
**Taarie**: *"Sister dear, could you try sewing those sleeves a little tighter? We can't have our customers looking sloppy because of your work."*
**Endarie**: *"Oh certainly, dear sister. We couldn't afford to have our customers look like the simple pigs they are, now could we?"*
**Taarie**: *"Good. And while you're at it, perhaps you should sew your mouth shut when our fine customers are within earshot, hrm?"* [Irnskar Ironhand](Skyrim_Irnskar_Ironhand.md) may also talk with [Bryling](Skyrim_Bryling.md) about a new gown by Endarie:

**Irnskar**: *"Madame Endarie insisted. She made it free of charge, in honor of your lenience about last month's rent discrepancies."*
**Bryling**: *"That was suspiciously nice of her. I thought she hated everyone."*
**Irnskar**: *"She does, my lady. But apparently, she hates you just a little less than the others."*
## Notes
- Endarie has the exact same facial data as her sister, [Taarie](Skyrim_Taarie.md). - The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states that the two are twin sisters, so it's possible they're meant to be identical twins.
