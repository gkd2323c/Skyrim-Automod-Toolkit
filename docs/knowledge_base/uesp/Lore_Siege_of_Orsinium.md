The Siege of Orsinium (also called the **Great Siege of Orsinium**)<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TGSOO-5)</sup> was a long drawn out conflict between the combined forces of [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) and [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) rulers and the [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) clans and [goblin-ken](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Goblin-ken) inhabitants of the first [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) (now called Old Orsinium). In response to the Orcish threat to the Kingdom of Wayrest, a Breton–Redguard–Yokudan alliance was formed which, after a campaign of more than 30 years, destroyed the First [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) in the [Wrothgarian Mountains](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wrothgarian_Mountains).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1WR-6)</sup>

## Background
King [Golkarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Golkarr) of the Orcs amassed an army and was determined to seize control of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1WR-6)</sup> upon which villages and the nomadic Breton [Bjoulsae River Tribes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bjoulsae_River_Tribes) lived.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup> They intended to force Wayrest to pay to regularly use the river for travel and trade.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1WR-6)</sup> Naturally, this did not settle well with their neighboring kingdoms, especially the Kingdom of [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). In [1E 948](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_948), [King Joile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Joile) of Daggerfall sent a letter to [Gaiden Shinji](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gaiden_Shinji) of the [Order of Diagna](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Order_of_Diagna), which proposed a joint attack on Orsinium. Agreeing to provide aid, an alliance was formed between the Order of Diagna, the armies of Daggerfall (High Rock), and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) (Hammerfell). The Bjoulsae River Tribes also agreed to lend their aid.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONABS-9)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TRHOKE-10)</sup> The armies amassed and began a campaign for the destruction of Orsinium.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WJOTB-11)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-PGE3O-12)</sup> Two years later, in [1E 950](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_950) the siege began.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-WOBN-13)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TPC-14)</sup> [Guthrag Stone-Talker](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Guthrag_Stone-Talker)'s diplomatic prowess was acknowledged as one of the factors in postponing the siege for more than a decade.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-GM-15)</sup> The siege was seen as a response to the Bjoulsae Raids on Breton villages carried out by the [Bagrakh Clan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bagrakh_Clan).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-ESOTrystan-16)</sup>
