## Zebra
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/ON-mount-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg/200px-ON-mount-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-mount-Pellitine_Mustang.jpg) A Pellitine Mustang, a zebra-horse hybrid Zebras are a type of striped equine native to the subtropical forests of [Pellitine](Lore_Pellitine.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zorse-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CBM-2)</sup> Their subtle vertical stripes are used for camouflage under the forest canopies. They are smaller than [horses](Lore_Horse.md) but are capable of [interbreeding](Lore_Sex.md) with them. The Pellitine Mustang, a favorite of the First [Corinthe](Lore_Corinthe.md) Hussars, is a hybrid breed created by mating a Anequine Mustang horse with a zebra from Pellitine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zorse-1)</sup>

Some [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) have stripe patterns resembling that of a zebra.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CBM-2)</sup> Zebras are the namesake of certain species of [fish](Lore_Fish.md), such as the [zebra pompano](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Zebra_Pompano) and [zebra oto](Lore_Zebra_Oto.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Pellitine_Mustang)

## [Zombie](Lore_Zombie.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/OB-creature-Zombie.jpg/200px-OB-creature-Zombie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Zombie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-creature-Zombie.jpg) A zombie A [zombie](Lore_Zombie.md) is a reanimated corpse, risen from the dead, mostly through the use of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md). They can also [come about](Lore_Plague_Husk.md) through certain [diseases](Lore_Diseases.md) such as the [Llodos Plague](Lore_Llodos_Plague.md). When a necromancer wishes to create a zombie, they must take a corpse to a suitable site and enact rituals to resurrect the corpse. As part of an ancient tradition, those wanting an apprenticeship to a necromancer will sometimes create a zombie and show it to their prospective teacher. Zombies are also [conjurable](Lore_Conjuration.md) and mages can learn how to summon them from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md).

Zombies can come in many forms, and in various stages of decay, they don't even require a head, and sometimes zombies can be found missing various limbs. Some zombies are known to vomit at people, as part of their attacks. The bite of certain zombiess can pass on a rotting disease that if it isn't treated will turn the victim into a zombie. Zombies are also known carriers of [disease](Lore_Disease.md), they are known to pass on the following diseases, [Ataxia](Lore_Ataxia.md), [Astral Vapors](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Astral_Vapors), [Chanthrax Blight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Chanthrax_Blight), [Collywobbles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Collywobbles), [Greenspore](Lore_Greenspore.md), [Rattles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rattles), [Rust Chancre](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rust_Chancre), [Serpiginous Dementia](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Serpiginous_Dementia), [Swamp Fevers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Swamp_Fever), [Wither](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wither), and [Yellow Tick](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Yellow_Tick).

- Variations: Azra Zombie, Bloodthorn Zombie, Dawn Zombie, Dread Zombie, Dusk Zombie, Headless Zombie, Tunnel Zombie, Zombie Guardian
