# Daedric

**Daedric** may refer to the following:

- [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), the immortal beings that did not take part in the creation of the Mundus, and thus retain the full breadth of their power
- [Daedric Alphabet](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md), used by the Daedra
- [Daedric Equipment](Lore_Daedric_Equipment.md), the armor and weapons used by the forces of Oblivion
