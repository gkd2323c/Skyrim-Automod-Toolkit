*A freezing whirlwind that does 40 points of frost damage per second to Health and Stamina.* **Ice Storm** is an adept level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that fires a slow-moving area-effect projectile that deals [frost damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage) to the health and stamina of any enemies that it passes over, and slows targets' movement for 5 seconds.

## Effects
- [Ice Storm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage), 40 pts in 15 ft
- [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md), 50 pts for 5 secs
- [Deep Freeze Paralyze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 3 secs in 15 ft; if the [Deep Freeze](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deep_Freeze) perk has been unlocked
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Frost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Frost), raises damage to 50 at first rank or 60 at second rank

## Notes
- Ice storm will not dissipate after it hits an enemy. It can be used to hit multiple targets in a line.
- It is great for killing [slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), as the spell will travel underwater. It can also kill large amounts of [bees](Skyrim_Bee.md) and [torchbugs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torchbug) easily. The spell can also be used to kill fish in the water for easier harvesting, such as salmon and Histcarp.
- Dual casting will increase both damage and size of projectile while decreasing its range.
- The Ice Storm whirlwind can travel through objects and even walls, and can so be used to damage or kill enemies in different rooms.
- Ice Storm travels slower than other frost spells. Take this into account when attacking faster enemies, such as Dragons and wolves.
