[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/36/SR-npc-Vighar_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vighar_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vighar_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vighar_02.jpg) Vighar **Vighar** is a hostile [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) who inhabits [Bloodlet Throne](Skyrim_Bloodlet_Throne.md). You are sent to [kill him](Skyrim_Dark_Ancestor.md) by his descendant, [Dengeir of Stuhn](Skyrim_Dengeir_of_Stuhn.md), and he will only spawn while on that quest.

Like all other vampires, [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) changes his appearance, giving him glowing yellow eyes, a different nose and [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Dark Ancestor
As you enter the arena area:

- **Vampire**: *"And who is this?"*
- **Vighar**: *"It is no matter, this fool will be the next act of the show."*

## Notes
- Technically speaking, Vighar is just a generic leveled vampire boss with a unique design. He is internally referred to as `dun Bloodlet Throne Vampire Boss`, and his name is added via a quest-related message.
- Vighar is the only member of the `Elder Race Vampire` in the base game, as well as its expansions.
- He is the only known member of Dengeir's family whose name doesn't end with "geir".
