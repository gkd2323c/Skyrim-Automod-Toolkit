## Notes
- Bliss Bugs are also used in the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) for a variety of recipes related to [Golden Saints](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md) listed [here](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Report_on_Atronach_Forge_Experiments). - A secret recipe to create a tome to summon [Staada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staada) uses the [Sheogorath-Shaped Amber](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sheogorath-Shaped_Amber), a [Ruined Book](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book), a Bliss Bug Thorax, and a [Sabre Cat Pelt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sabre_Cat_Pelt).
- Despite its name, it is actually an abdomen of the [Bliss Bug](Skyrim_Bliss_Bug.md). Thorax refers to an insect's midsection.
