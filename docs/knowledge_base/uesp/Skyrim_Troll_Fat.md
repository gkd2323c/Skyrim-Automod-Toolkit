- 2 in [Calixto's House of Curiosities](Skyrim_Calixto%27s_House_of_Curiosities.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Hjerim](Skyrim_Hjerim.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Jorrvaskr Living Quarters](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr_Living_Quarters.md) ([Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md))
- 2 in [Knifepoint Mine](Skyrim_Knifepoint_Mine.md) ([Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md))
- 2 in [the Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in [Nightcaller Temple](Skyrim_Nightcaller_Temple.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 2 in the [Palace of the Kings Upstairs](Skyrim_Palace_of_the_Kings_Upstairs.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))
- 2 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Sadri's Used Wares](Skyrim_Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.md) ([Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md))

- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Creatures
Troll fat is dropped by all varieties of [troll](Skyrim_Troll.md), including [frost trolls](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md), the [Guardian Troll Spirit](Skyrim_Guardian_Troll_Spirit.md), and [Udefrykte](Skyrim_Udefrykte.md).
