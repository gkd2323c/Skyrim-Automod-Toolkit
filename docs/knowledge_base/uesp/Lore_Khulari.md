# Khulari

Khulari is one of the [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines of the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) area. Those of the bloodline are gifted with the ability to paralyze their prey. The bloodline dominates an area of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), specifically the regions of [Santaki](Lore_Santaki_(county).md), [Totambu](Lore_Totambu.md), [Kozanset](Lore_Kozanset_(barony).md), [Ephesus](Lore_Ephesus_(march).md), and the [Dragontail Mountains](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md). They are the enemies of the [Anthotis](Lore_Anthotis.md) bloodline.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> They infest a cave system with ruins of the [Rourken Clan](Lore_Rourken.md) [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) surrounding [Ganada Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ganada_Pass) in the [Dragontails](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-2)</sup>
