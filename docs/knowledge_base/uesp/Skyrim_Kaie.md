[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/SR-npc-Kaie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kaie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kaie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kaie.jpg) Kaie, fighting her way out of Markarth **Kaie**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [nightblade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightblade), is a member of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) who meets with [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) and the [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) escapees and gives them their equipment just before leaving the Markarth Ruins. She will follow Madanach, fight alongside the Forsworn, and run all the way to [Druadach Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt) where she will settle down.

Kaie is fully clad in Forsworn equipment. This includes [Forsworn armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Armor), a pair of [Forsworn boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Boots), a 50% chance of a pair of [Forsworn gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Gauntlets), and a 50% chance of a [Forsworn headdress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Headdress). She is equipped with either a [Forsworn sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Sword) or a [Forsworn axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Forsworn_Axe), and carries two [potions of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Healing). She knows the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell [Raise Zombie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Raise_Zombie), and the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md).

## Quest-Related Events
When she meets you at the gate to Markarth, she will have a brief conversation with Madanach:

**Kaie**: *"Madanach. I've brought what you asked for."* **Madanach**: *"Good work. Get ready while I have a word with our favorite outsider."* When Kaie hands the escapees their equipment, she may say:

*"It wasn't easy getting all your things. A few guards had to have their throats slit."* During the escape, she will shout:

*"For the Forsworn!"*
## Notes
- Kaie does not possess the usual Forsworn face tattoos seen on generic members. She shares this quirk with the imprisoned Forsworn also seen during the quest.
