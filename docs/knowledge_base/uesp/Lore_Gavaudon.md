[2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Gavaudon is a region in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), within the larger region of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md). It borders [Menevia](Lore_Menevia.md) to the west, [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) to the north, and [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth.md) to the east. The region contains the town of [Wind Keep](Lore_Wind_Keep.md), the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) ruins of [Norvulk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Norvulk_Ruins), as well as the enormous ruined statue called the Weeping Giant.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ON2-2)</sup> It is the home of the [Dumontes](Lore_Dumonte.md), an old clan known for having a considerable familial connection with the [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AVAYQ-3)</sup>

Gavaudon offers rich alluvial soil due to the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) flood plains, but the threat of vampire attacks and Orc raids from the mountains weeds out all but the most hardy frontiersmen. Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the region was considered a fiefdom.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-EGTHigh Rock-4)</sup>
