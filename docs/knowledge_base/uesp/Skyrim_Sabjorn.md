[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ae/SR-npc-Sabjorn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sabjorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sabjorn.jpg) Sabjorn at the bar in Honningbrew Meadery **Sabjorn** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who lives in and owns [Honningbrew Meadery](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Meadery.md), a newly established competitor to the [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). Sabjorn had the courage to turn down an offer from [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md), and as a result, you will have to cause Sabjorn's downfall during the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) quest [Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits).

Before the quest, Sabjorn runs his Honningbrew Meadery peacefully, with the aid of his assistants, [Mallus Maccius](Skyrim_Mallus_Maccius.md) and [Eimar](Skyrim_Eimar.md). He will stand behind the counter offering assorted foodstuffs and his special [Honningbrew Mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honningbrew_Mead) from when he wakes up at 6am until he retires to his private quarters at midnight.

Sabjorn wears a set of barkeeper [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0005b6a0). He carries a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and nothing else.

## Related Quests
- **[Dampened Spirits](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dampened_Spirits)**: Put [Maven Black-Briar's](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md) competition out of business.

*"What are you gawking at? Can't you see I have problems here?"* **Is something wrong?** *"Are you kidding me? Look at this place. I'm supposed to be holding a tasting of the new Honningbrew Reserve for the Captain of the Guard. If he sees the meadery in this state, I'll be ruined."* **I might be able to help.** *"Oh really? And I don't suppose you'd just do it out of the kindness of your heart, would you? I hope you're not expecting to get paid until the job's done."* Three options will be available:

**Captain**: *"By the Eight?!/Shor's Bones! What... what's in this?"*

**Captain**: *"You assured me this place was clean! I'll see... see to it that you remain in irons for the rest of your days!"*

A confused Sabjorn will then find himself in the dungeon of [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md), unaware about the huge scheme set up against him.

While the plot thickens in the Guild, Sabjorn will spend the rest of his days behind bars in Dragonsreach dungeon. If you approach him, he will hint that he figured out the person behind his downfall:

*"It was you, wasn't it. I should of * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * known better. Now look at me."* *"You've sent an innocent man to jail. I hope your * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * proud of yourself."* *"Maven be damned. One day, she'll get hers. I can promise you that."* *"Leave me alone. Haven't you done enough damage already?"*
## Rumors
While Sabjorn puts on the cheerful act, his assistant Mallus Maccius is overly bitter about everything, saying:

*"If you want to buy something, go find Sabjorn. Otherwise, piss off."* *"To tell you the truth, Sabjorn isn't much of an owner. Slave driver, sure."* *"I've never met someone like him that revels so much in other's * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * misery."* The other worker at the meadery, Eimar, sees little wrong with his employer and will comment:

*"If you're looking to purchase any mead, speak to the owner, Sabjorn."* *"We're a small meadery, but growing quickly. Sabjorn's only been running this place for a short time now and look at it."*
## Unused Rumors
Unused lines for an unknown speaker only found in the game's Dialogue Views files would've elaborated on Sabjorn's background:

**Anything else you can tell me about Sabjorn?** *"His father was some sort of shipping merchant. Made a bit of coin before he died at the hand of some giants. Sabjorn used it all to open the meadery. Why he decided to butt heads with Maven Black-Briar I have no idea. Funny thing is he didn't have nearly enough coin to do this on his own. I guess he borrowed the rest."*
## Notes
- When Sabjorn leaves the meadery with the Guard Captain, he will be disabled from the game. He is re-enabled once you finish Dampened Spirits, this time in Dragonsreach dungeon.
