[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Kvenel_the_Tongue.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kvenel_the_Tongue.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kvenel_the_Tongue.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kvenel_the_Tongue.jpg) Kvenel the Tongue **Kvenel the Tongue** is an ancient [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) hero who died long ago and was buried in [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md). He now lives in his burial chamber as a [ghost](Skyrim_Ghost.md). For historical information, see the [lore article](Lore_Kvenel_the_Tongue.md).

Kvenel dual-wields two unique items: [Eduj](Skyrim_Eduj.md), a sword, and [Okin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Okin), an axe; both are enchanted with [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md). He also has two backup weapons, which are leveled but not necessarily of maximum quality. The rest of his equipment is a [variant](Skyrim_Unobtainable_Items.md#Ancient_Nord_Helmet) of [ancient Nord steel](Skyrim_Ancient_Nord.md), which cannot be looted and is considerably stronger than the obtainable kind.

As a ghost, he has an additional 100% [frost resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md) on top of his Nord ability, and is also completely [immune to poison](Skyrim_Resist_Poison.md). He is, however, vulnerable to [paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md).

## Related Quests
- **[Silenced Tongues](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silenced_Tongues)**: Unlock the burial chamber of the Tongue Chieftain Kvenel.

## Notes
- His health is astronomical for his level, considering that a level 30 PC would have a maximum of 390. He also has an impressively high stamina, allowing for nearly unlimited use of power attacks.
- Kvenel is immune to all [anti-undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(effect).md) spells, despite being below their level limit.
- Messages for him resisting shouts and other similar reactions use a generic title such as "Draugr Deathlord", depending on the leveled base enemy type, rather than his name.
- He cannot be detected by any [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(spell).md) or [Detect Dead](Skyrim_Detect_Dead.md) spells, but can be detected by [Totem of The Hunt](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md) and the [Aura Whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) shout.

- The remains of Kvenel may have to be looted twice to obtain both Eduj and Okin.
- Kvenel was not configured with the proper keywords for a ghost NPC. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.8, fixes this bug.
- Kvenel respawns when the dungeon resets. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.5, addresses this issue. A leveled draugr boss will now replace him.
