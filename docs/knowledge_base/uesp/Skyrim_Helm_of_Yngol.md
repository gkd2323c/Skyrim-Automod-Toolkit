[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-item-Helm_of_Yngol.jpg/200px-SR-item-Helm_of_Yngol.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Helm_of_Yngol.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Helm_of_Yngol.jpg) The Helm of Yngol [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-interior-Yngol_Barrow_04.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Yngol_Barrow_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Yngol_Barrow_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Yngol_Barrow_04.jpg) The remains of Yngol, with his helmet The Helm of Yngol is a unique helmet that belonged to [Yngol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol%27s_Shade), one of the original members of the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md). After his death, he and his helmet were locked away in [Yngol Barrow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Yngol_Barrow). The helm itself provides its wearer with a thirty percent increase to [frost resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Frost.md). [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Helm of Yngol requires a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk, with the benefits of the alterations doubling if the [Advanced Armors](Skyrim_Advanced_Armors.md) perk is also unlocked.

## Notes
- The Helm of Yngol is considered to be a part of the [Steel Plate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Plate#Steel_Plate_Armor) set, and not the Ancient Nord set. As this helmet is considered to be part of that set, you can use the Helm of Yngol to replace a Steel Plate Helmet or [Ahzidal's Helm of Vision](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Helm_of_Vision.md), and still gain the [Matching Set](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md#Matching_Set) perk.
- Unlike other Draugr or Ancient Nord-styled helmets, the helm looks identical for both males and females instead of the female version having antlers.
- The Helm of Yngol is one of two helmets in the game that have an elemental resistance enchantment; the other is the [Ancient Helmet of the Unburned](Skyrim_Ancient_Helmet_of_the_Unburned.md).
