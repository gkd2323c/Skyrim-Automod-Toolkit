# Nordic Dwellings

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg/200px-SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Four_Skull_Lookout.jpg) Four Skull Lookout [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) This page lists the ancient **Nordic dwellings** in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md). This page only lists the smaller ruins, and the larger [Nordic ruins](Skyrim_Nordic_Ruins.md) are listed on separate pages and use different map marker icons.

- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin south of Karthwasten containing bandits. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Four+Skull+Lookout)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Hamvir's Rest](Skyrim_Hamvir%27s_Rest.md)** — A small outdoor graveyard and Nordic ruin northwest of Whiterun containing skeletons and a draugr. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Hamvir%27s+Rest)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Journeyman's Nook](Skyrim_Journeyman%27s_Nook.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin southeast of Winterhold containing a bandit. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Journeyman%27s+Nook)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Kjenstag Ruins](Skyrim_Kjenstag_Ruins.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin east-northeast of Morthal. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Kjenstag+Ruins)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Lost Valkygg](Skyrim_Lost_Valkygg.md)** — A small Nordic ruin northeast of Labyrinthian containing draugr. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Lost+Valkygg)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Moldering Ruins](Skyrim_Moldering_Ruins.md)**<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> — A small camp and concealed cave west of Rorikstead containing vampires and dead draugr. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Moldering+Ruins+%28DG%29)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[North Skybound Watch](Skyrim_North_Skybound_Watch.md)** — A small Nordic tower and ruin northeast of Helgen containing bandits and a giant frostbite spider. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Skybound+Watch+Pass)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Roadside Ruins](Skyrim_Roadside_Ruins.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin northwest of Falkreath containing a spriggan. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Roadside+Ruins)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Skytemple Ruins](Skyrim_Skytemple_Ruins.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin north of the College of Winterhold containing a draugr and skeletons. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Skytemple+Ruins)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Snowclad Ruins](Skyrim_Snowclad_Ruins.md)**<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> — A small outdoor Nordic ruin northwest of the Temple of Miraak containing werebears. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=solstheim&centeron=Snowclad+Ruins)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Windward Ruins](Skyrim_Windward_Ruins.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin southwest of Dawnstar containing skeevers. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Windward+Ruins)
- [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-mapicon-Nordic_Dwelling.png) **[Yorgrim Overlook](Skyrim_Yorgrim_Overlook.md)** — A small outdoor Nordic ruin on the southern slope of the mountain range directly south of Winterhold. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=Yorgrim+Overlook)
