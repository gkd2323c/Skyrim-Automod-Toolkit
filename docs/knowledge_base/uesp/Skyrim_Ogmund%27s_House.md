It is the home of local bard and [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) trainer [Ogmund](Skyrim_Ogmund.md), who usually hangs out at the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md). It is located next door to [Endon's house](Skyrim_Endon%27s_House.md) and directly above [Arnleif and Sons Trading Company](Skyrim_Arnleif_and_Sons_Trading_Company.md).

It consists of one area, *[Ogmund's House](#Ogmund.27s_House)*.

## Ogmund's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ogmund%27s_House.jpg) The interior of the house Upon entering you will see this is one open room with a dining/kitchen area to the west, a raised storage in front and another raised area for sleeping to the east.

Immediately on your left as you enter is a stone shelf containing a [thistle branch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thistle_Branch), a [snowberry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Snowberry) sample, a bottle of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a cup and a plate. To the right is a barrel with four [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md) samples on the floor around it. Further along the wall next to the dining table is a copy of the book *[Fall from Glory](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fall_from_Glory)*, a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) sample and an [iron mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Mace). On the dining table is a lute, a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and one of [Alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a goblet, two bowls, two cups and various foodstuffs. Behind the dining table to the west is the kitchen area with more foodstuffs on stone shelves with a portion of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) and a garlic braid hanging from these. There is also a rack with two rabbits and a pheasant. There is a cook pot behind the table.

As you climb the stairs to the storage area there is a stool with a lit candle and goblet, a chair and flute next to this and a barrel behind. Against the north wall is a stone sideboard containing a mead barrel, a plate, more food, an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a drum, [iron gauntlets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Gauntlets) and a chest (this will contain the object of the quest, if that has been started). An [iron shield](Skyrim_Iron_Shield.md) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Boots) are on the floor to the right of the chest, next to a practice dummy.

The sleeping area contains a bed with a chest, a sack and a copy of *[Azura and the Box](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Azura_and_the_Box)* to the left. To the right of the bed is an end table with a flute on top, while a small stone table further to the right holds a copy of *[The Song of Pelinal, v3](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Song_of_Pelinal,_v3)*, one medium and one large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), a goblet, a [honey nut treat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Honey_Nut_Treat) and two bottles of wine. To the left of the exit is a stone shelf containing a [salt pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile) and a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) on a plate, with a basket containing more foodstuffs on the floor beside it.
