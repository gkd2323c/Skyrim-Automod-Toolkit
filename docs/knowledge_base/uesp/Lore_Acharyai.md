# Acharyai

The Acharyai is the name of the supposed first spirit or spirits in existence, the distant ancestor or ancestors of the traditionally worshipped [gods](Lore_Gods.md): the [Aedra](Lore_Aedra.md) and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md). They are acknowledged by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) and considered fundamental to their complex system of [Ancestor Worship](Lore_Ancestor_Worship.md), known to them as the Old Ways. As the foundation of their faith, the nature and existence of the Acharyai is one of the first things any neophyte of [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md) will learn.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TOW-1)</sup> [Centaurs](Lore_Centaur.md) practice the same devout system as the Psijics, presumably including the Acharyai.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DUG-2)</sup>

The concept of anything existing before the Daedra has been considered a paradoxical impossibility, even to those in contact with the Psijic Order.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-GMFR-3)</sup>

## Notes
- What relation, if any, the term "Acharyai" has to "Alcharyai" (a term used to refer to [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) in some dialects) is unknown.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Po M-4)</sup>
- [Anu](Lore_Anu.md) and [Padomay](Lore_Padomay.md) are sometimes called the parents of the et'Ada,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Vo Fi T-5)</sup> but their relation to the Acharyai is unknown.
- [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) has been referred to as 'elder than Ada' in obscure texts.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
- [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) herself claims the title of [Ur-dra](Lore_Ur-dra.md) and has said she existed "before [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md)".<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONGeysers-7)</sup>
