[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ef/SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron.jpg) Suvaris Atheron [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Suvaris_Atheron_02.jpg) Suvaris being harassed by Angrenor and Rolff **Suvaris Atheron** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who resides in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), in the segregated Gray Quarter. She has two brothers, [Faryl](Skyrim_Faryl_Atheron.md) and [Aval](Skyrim_Aval_Atheron.md), with whom she shares the quarters of the eponymous [Atheron Residence](Skyrim_Atheron_Residence.md). Suvaris works for [Torbjorn Shatter-Shield](Skyrim_Torbjorn_Shatter-Shield.md) at the [Clan Shatter-Shield Office](Skyrim_Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office.md), the main competitor of the [East Empire Company](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_(place).md) in Windhelm.

When first visiting Windhelm, you will witness [Rolff Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Rolff_Stone-Fist.md) and [Angrenor Once-Honored](Skyrim_Angrenor_Once-Honored.md) harassing Suvaris, as well as accusing her of being a spy for the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md). This scene will occur regardless of the time of day, or any other appointments Suvaris has. When not being threatened by [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md), Suvaris begins her day at 8:05am by heading out of her home down to her office. She will spend the next twelve hours working, breaking only at 1pm to have lunch at the office. After ending the workday at 8pm, she will head down to the [New Gnisis Cornerclub](Skyrim_New_Gnisis_Cornerclub.md) to drink. She will finally return home at midnight, to go to sleep in preparation for the next day.

Suvaris wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0), a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c1), and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries a [key](Skyrim_Atheron_Residence_Key.md) to the Atherons' house, a [key](Skyrim_Clan_Shatter-Shield_Office_Key.md) to the Shatter-Shield office, as well as a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
Approaching Suvaris may have her greet you by mentioning the shipping capabilities of her employer.

- *"Looking for shipping? We'll always offer better service than the East Empire Company."*

She might also grumble about the [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) dock workers from the [Argonian Assemblage](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage.md).

- *"These lazy Argonians better get their tails moving."*

She might also mention the recent loss of one of her employer's daughters at the hands of a [serial killer](Skyrim_Blood_on_the_Ice.md).

- *"You hear about the Shatter-shield girl? It's a tragedy."*

She will also talk about Torbjorn's desire for money from the shipping operation, as long as he is still living:

- *"Torbjorn wants the shipping operations to bring in cash, and I make it happen."*

Suvaris can first be encountered just inside the primary gate to Windhelm, being harassed by [Rolff Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Rolff_Stone-Fist.md) and [Angrenor Once-Honored](Skyrim_Angrenor_Once-Honored.md):

- **Rolff:** *"You come here where you're not wanted, you eat our food, you pollute our city with your stink and you refuse to help the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md)."*
- **Suvaris:** *"But we haven't taken a side because it's not our fight."*
- **Angrenor:** *"Hey, maybe the reason these gray-skins don't help in the war is because they're Imperial spies!"*
- **Suvaris:** *"Imperial spies? You can't be serious!"*
- **Rolff:** *"Maybe we'll pay you a visit tonight, little spy. We got ways of finding out what you really are."*
- **Suvaris:** *"(Sigh)"*

Should you speak to Suvaris after witnessing this scene, she will ask about your standing on the issue if you are not a Dunmer:

- *"Do you hate the dark elves? Are you here to bully us and tell us to leave?"*

If you tell her that you do hate the Dunmer, the conversation ends.

- **Yes, I do hate the dark elves.**
- *"Then we have nothing more to talk about."*

It continues if you tell her you don't hate them. You can then tell her that you noticed the way Rolff treated her. Asking her why anyone would think she is a spy will have her respond by claiming that the Nords will cling to any reason to hate them, imaginary or not. Also, telling her that you don't hate her people will raise her disposition towards you, causing her to treat you as a friend.

- **No, I don't hate your people.**
- *"You've come to the wrong city, then. Windhelm's a haven of prejudice and narrow thinking, unworthy of one such as you."*
- **Looked like those Nords were giving you trouble.**
- *"Nothing new there. Most of the Nords living in Windhelm don't care much for us, but Rolff is the worst by far.
He likes to get drunk and walk around the Gray Quarter yelling insults at us in the small hours of the morning. A real charmer, that one."*
- **Why would anyone think you're a spy?**
- *"Some of these Nords will come up with any excuse to despise us.
And it isn't just the dark elves they hate -- they make a target of the Argonians as well.
In fact, just about anyone who isn't a Nord is fair game for their bullying."*

### Quest-Related Events
Visiting the East Empire Company's office in Windhelm will lead to an encounter with [Orthus Endario](Skyrim_Orthus_Endario.md). He will apologize for the poor state that the office is in and will inform you that the East Empire Company cannot afford to keep the place running in proper shape ever since a [group of pirates](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md) started attacking their shipping operations. He will state that their competition, the Shatter-Shield shipping operation, are the only ones safe from the attacks, and will suggest that they are involved with the pirates. Asking him if he has proof will have him admit that he does not, but will instead have him request you gather it by obtaining [Suvaris Atheron's Logbook](Skyrim_Suvaris_Atheron%27s_Logbook.md). Obtaining her logbook and giving it to Orthus will have his suspicions confirmed by entries mentioning Suvaris meeting with the pirates. While Suvaris was involved with the pirate operations, she will not make any comment about it, or face any kind of punishment for her role in the attacks.

## Rumors
Although you won't hear it from him, apparently her brother Aval strongly dislikes that Suvaris and her brother Faryl are employed by Nords, as Faryl can be heard stating:

- *"My sister and I both work for the Nords. Our brother is ashamed of us, but at least we can affort * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * to eat."*

## Unused Dialogue
As shown by the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), Torbjorn was originally meant to refer you to Suvaris, but the line didn't make it into the final game:

- *"You want to talk about shipping matters, speak with Suvaris Atheron."*

## Notes
- It's possible that Suvaris was originally designed as a male character. Her [logbook](Skyrim_Suvaris_Atheron%27s_Logbook.md) uses a male pronoun to refer to her, her brother Faryl refers to having "brothers" in a line of dialogue, and based on the [naming convention](Lore_Dunmer_Names.md), the name "Suvaris" is usually reserved for males.
- In her logbook she makes reference to an "Uncle Mithorpa". An [unused Dark Elf](Skyrim_Unused_NPCs.md#Mithorpa_Nasyal) with this same first name was also meant to appear as a farmer in [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md).
- Also in her logbook, it's implied that she harbors a dislike towards the Argonian dockworkers, as well as apparently supplying [Stands-In-Shallows](Skyrim_Stands-In-Shallows.md)' [skooma](Skyrim_Skooma.md), in order to keep the other workers motivated.
