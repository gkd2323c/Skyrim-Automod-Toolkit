## Abolisher
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-misc-Abolisher.png/200px-ON-misc-Abolisher.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Abolisher.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Abolisher.png) Abolisher [Abolisher](Lore_Abolisher.md) is an artifact created by the Daedric Prince Boethiah to aid in [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md)'s imprisonment, described as a pitted blade with an edge that can cut the veil between worlds. It held the power to open [portals](Lore_Portals.md), destroy them, or reopen sealed portals. In addition it could be used to sever the link the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md) provided to a different reality, rendering it inaccessible. Abolisher was ultimately destroyed when it was used as part of a spell originating from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), which was provided to [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in order to repair the [reality tears](Lore_Fate.md) created by Ithelia's power that were threatening to unravel all existence. After the spell was cast Abolisher became a normal sword, its Daedric power fully expended.

**Found in:** - ESO

## Akaviri Sunderblade
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg/200px-BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BL-item-Akaviri_Blade.jpg) Akaviri Sunderblade The [Akaviri Sunderblade](Lore_Akaviri_Sunderblade.md), also shortened to just **Sunderblade** is a legendary katana that belonged to the first leader of the [Akaviri](Lore_Akaviri.md) [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md), [Ghelin-Brol](Lore_Ghelin-Brol.md) who were sworn to the protection of the [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Reman Cyrodiil](Lore_Reman_Cyrodiil.md). It was known for its potent enchantment that could disintegrate weapons and armors of the foes of the wielder. Numerous superb katanas were fashioned to look like the legendary Sunderblade and were used for centuries by high-ranking [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) [Blades](Lore_Blades.md) before the order was disbanded.

As of [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the legendary blade was available for purchase in the Three Brothers Trade Goods shop in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) from Imperial merchant Sergius Verus. It is unknown whether or not [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) purchased the blade or not, however.

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Unique_Items#Akavari_Sunderblade), [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Sunderblade)

## Arcanist Tomes
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/ON-render-Arcanist.jpg/200px-ON-render-Arcanist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Arcanist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Arcanist.jpg) An arcanist with her tome [Arcanist tomes](Lore_Arcanists.md#The_Arcanist.27s_Tome) are sentient artifacts created by the forces of Apocrypha, the Oblivion realm of Hermaeus Mora. One becomes a spellcaster known as an arcanist when one is bonded to one of them. These tomes are the object of which arcanists learn of the spells they wield and the vector through which their spells are cast. In other words, their power is derived from the book itself: knowledge alone is one thing, but arcanists are unique in that the tome they wield is integral to their spellcasting. All mages use runes of some kind, but the runework wielded by arcanists is extremely elaborate on top of producing unique feats of spellwork. Some mortals hypothesize these tomes to be pieces of Apocrypha itself.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Arcanist)

## Aurbical Abacus
The [Aurbical Abacus](Lore_Aurbical_Abacus.md) was an ancient [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) relic created by distilling the knowledge and wisdom of twenty generations of the priest-wizards of the Yokudan god [Zeht](Lore_Zeht.md). It had the physical appearance of having glass gears and ivory pistons, and suffused with the blue glow of star-magic. It was used to track the movements of the moons, constellations, and planets and calculate the exact turn of the seasons. It detected the directional origin of star-magicka, or "[varliance](Lore_Varliance.md)," and stored it as data. It can also predict the weather and even control it on a continental scale. It was brought to [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) after Yokuda sank, by the Zeht priesthood, where it was buried in the old [Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md) family tomb known as Bahraha's Gloom.

## Auriel's Bow
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-item-Auriel%27s_Bow.jpg/200px-SR-item-Auriel%27s_Bow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Bow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Bow.jpg) Auriel's Bow [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md), (also spelled **Auri-El's Bow**) is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) used by the elven god [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md). Although it commonly takes the form of a modest elven [moonstone](Lore_Moonstone.md) bow, it has also appeared made of [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md). Either way, it is one of the most powerful weapons in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

The bow draws its power from [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) itself, channeling it through the [sun](Lore_Sun.md). It has the ability to turn any arrow into a "missile of death", although its enchantment effects vary: it has been known to cause magical fire or shock damage, drain an opponent's stamina and magicka reserves, or harness the power of the sun. It can also make the wielder immune to lesser attacks, and is especially devastating when used against the undead. Occasionally it can be seemingly unenchanted. Without Auriel's power behind it, however, the bow uses its own store of energy for its power. Once exhausted of this energy, the bow will vanish, abandoning its owner to reappear elsewhere.

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Auriel%27s_Bow), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Auriel%27s_Bow), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Auriel%27s_Bow_(artifact)), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Arms_of_the_Ancestors)

## Auriel's Shield
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg/200px-SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Auriel%27s_Shield.jpg) Auriel's Shield [Auriel's Shield](Lore_Auriel%27s_Shield.md) is an [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) used by the elven god [Auriel](Lore_Auriel.md), the [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), Dragon God of Time. It takes the form of a small shield, sometimes rounded, made from either [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) or [moonstone](Lore_Moonstone.md). It has several magical abilities that help to make its wielder nigh invulnerable. Its known enchantments include resistance to fire, shielding from either magical or physical attacks, healing properties, magical armor enhancement, and spell reflection. Sometimes it also appears to be seemingly unenchanted.

The shield has the ability to absorb the power of blocked attacks; this energy can then be released by bashing an opponent with the shield, delivering a devastating physical blow. Its shielding ability is supposedly unsurpassed. The shield is twinned with [Auriel's Bow](Lore_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).

Like many great artifacts, the shield has a life and personality of its own, and does not feel bound to its user. A popular fable tells of it abandoning its owner in her greatest hour of need, but this story is thought to be apocryphal. Legend says it was created by [Anuiel](Lore_Anuiel.md) at Auriel's request so that the latter could use it in his campaign against the forces of [Lorkhan](Lore_Lorkhan.md) during the [Dawn Era](Lore_Dawn_Era.md).

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Auriel%27s_Shield), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Auriel%27s_Shield), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Auriel%27s_Shield), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Shield.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Relics_of_the_Rebellion)

## Azra's Staffs
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f2/SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg/200px-SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azra%27s_Staff_of_Daedric_Command.jpg) One of Azra's Staffs [Azra's Staffs](Lore_Azra%27s_Staffs.md) are a collection of [staves](Lore_Staff.md) created and [enchanted](Lore_Enchanting.md) by the original Shadowmage [Azra Nightwielder](Lore_Azra_Nightwielder.md), whose advances in [magic](Lore_Magic.md) were legendary. In addition to his role in the development of [Shadow magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md), he was also known as a pioneer of staff enchantment. His techniques were considered rudimentary by the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), but his genius remained evident throughout the ages. While Azra himself wielded a personal staff, its properties are unknown.

**Found in:** - [Shadowkey](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shadowkey:Azra_Nightwielder), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Azra%27s_Staffs.md), [Castles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Castles:Azra%27s_Staff)

## Azura's Crystal
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Etched_Crystal.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-quest-Etched_Crystal.png) Azura's Crystal **Azura's Crystal** is an artifact of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md). It is capable of capturing [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [essences](Lore_Souls.md#Vestiges), acting as a prison for the vestige and preventing the Daedra from returning to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). After being 'charged' by a captured essence, the Crystal can be used as a power source for magical devices such as Void Keys.

## Azura's Star
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/60/SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg/200px-SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Azura%27s_Star.jpg) Azura's Star [Azura's Star](Lore_Azura%27s_Star.md) (also called the **Twilight Star**) is a Daedric [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), and is prominently used as her symbol. In appearance it looks like a large, intricately designed throwing star or gemstone, with eight star-like prongs. In the right hands, Azura's Star acts as a reusable [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gem.md) of almost unlimited capacity. It is considered to possess legendary magical powers. This makes it highly sought by mages and assassins. It can only capture white [souls](Lore_Souls.md), but was once corrupted by mortals to trap black souls instead, becoming the **Black Star**. Using Azura's Star empties it, freeing the trapped soul to make its journey to [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). Some believe Azura's Star can turn living creatures into statues.

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Azura%27s_Star), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Azura%27s_Star), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Azura%27s_Star), [Oblivion Mobile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/OBMobile:Blood_of_the_Daedra), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Azura%27s_Star.md)
