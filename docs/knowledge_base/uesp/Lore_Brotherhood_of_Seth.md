# Brotherhood of Seth


In the years before the Simulacrum, the Imperial Battlemage, [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md) was once a mage-priest of the brotherhood. There, he stole books of powerful spells and learned of the whereabouts and uses of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mo T4-4)</sup> In some matter, Brother Barnabas of [Tethis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tethis) had learned about Emperor [Uriel VII](Lore_Uriel_VII.md)'s capture at the hands of Tharn and suddenly went mad for justice. Senior Brother Halfas Varn and the other priests tried to calm him down, but in the next day, he killed Brother Martinus and fled to the [Mines of Khuras](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mines_of_Khuras), taking a scroll that could reveal the location of a piece of the Staff of Chaos, the [Crypt of Hearts](Lore_Crypt_of_Hearts.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ARHalfas-5)</sup>

Halfas Varn was later approached by the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md), who was seeking the missing piece. They were sent to the Mines of Khuras, to hopefully retrieve Brother Barnabas and the map, in exchange for the location of the Crypt of Hearts. The warrior discovered Barnabas' body deep in the mines and was able to recover the map for the brotherhood. Halfas Varn was able to use the map to decipher an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) that revealed the dungeon's location.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ARCo H-6)</sup>
