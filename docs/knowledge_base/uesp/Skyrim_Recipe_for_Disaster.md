## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Festus Krex](Skyrim_Festus_Krex.md).
2. Find [Anton Virane](Skyrim_Anton_Virane.md) and make him talk.
3. Kill Anton Virane.
4. Find [Balagog gro-Nolob](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md) and kill him.
5. Take the [invitation](Skyrim_Gourmet%27s_Writ_of_Passage.md) off of Balagog's corpse.
6. *(Optional)* Hide Balagog's corpse.
7. Return to Festus Krex.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Pinch of Assault
After [dealing with Cicero](Skyrim_The_Cure_for_Madness.md), [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) is anxious to get back on task. She mentions something about a famous, elusive chef and a crazy scheme to impersonate him while cooking for the Emperor. She then points you to [Festus Krex](Skyrim_Festus_Krex.md), who will be directing this phase of the Emperor's assassination. Festus explains that your new objective is to kill [the Gourmet](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md) and while assuming his identity, poison the meal being prepared for the Emperor. What complicates this matter is that the Gourmet's identity is known by only a select few. Luckily, Festus has obtained an autographed copy of the Gourmet's book [Uncommon Taste](Skyrim_Uncommon_Taste_-_Signed.md) owned by [Anton Virane](Skyrim_Anton_Virane.md). This suggests that Virane may know the true identity of the Gourmet.

### Carving the Cook
Your first task is to track down Anton Virane, obtain the Gourmet's name, and kill Virane to prevent his interference in the operation. He works at [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) in the [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) kitchen. He is at first not willing to give you any information, but after some special persuasion, he will quickly reveal the name of the Gourmet: an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) named [Balagog gro-Nolob](Skyrim_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md). After he reveals the name it is advisable to tell Virane that he will not be harmed or to remain silent, else he will start running around in a panic. Wait for an opportunity to present itself and kill him, preferably without any pesky witnesses present who could cause you to incur a bounty. Conversely, instead of having him remain in the crowded kitchen it may be better to scare him and have him run out to a more private place where you can kill him with no witnesses. When this is finished, it's on to the main course...

You'll have a few options with Anton once he asks you if he's free to go:

| Topic | Dialogue | Response |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Of course, Anton. Thank you. (Lie)** | *"Okay. All right. Wonderful. You're welcome! I'll just be on my way, then..."* | Anton walks away |
| **I'm afraid not. Loose ends, and all that.** | *"No. Oh, please no..."* | Anton runs away |
| **(Remain Silent)** | *"So... we're done?"* | Anton walks away |

### Just a Dash of Blood
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/SR-quest-Recipe_For_Disaster_3.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Recipe_For_Disaster_3.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Recipe_For_Disaster_3.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Recipe_For_Disaster_3.jpg) Balagog gro-Nolob, the second assassination target for this quest The Gourmet, Balagog gro-Nolob, can be found at the [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md), either inside or outside, depending on the time of day: during the afternoon, he stands at the dock right outside the inn and gazes out over the pond, making it very easy to sneak attack him for a quick kill. After he is dead take the [writ of passage](Skyrim_Gourmet%27s_Writ_of_Passage.md) from him, and simply drag his body into the pond (see Notes below) to fulfill the bonus objective. Another way to dispose of the body is to use the [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout to send it into the water. At night, Balagog gro-Nolob can be found in the [Nightgate Inn Cellar](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn_Cellar.md), which can be entered either from inside the inn or via a small hatch near the north exterior wall. Balagog will be asleep much of the time, but as there are no witnesses, the kill will be easy whether he is awake or not. When presented with an opportunity, kill him and take the Writ of Passage from his corpse. Make sure not to kill him in the chair that he sits in because his body may get trapped in the chair, rendering him unable to be dragged to a hiding spot. To successfully hide the body in the cellar, drag him behind the haystack or drop him into the large barrel at the end of the cellar next to the ladder. If you kill him while he cowers behind the wine barrels, this will automatically count as hiding the body. If you successfully hide the body, you will be awarded with the [Nightweaver's Band](Skyrim_Nightweaver%27s_Band.md). You may also have a small conversation with him:

| Topic | Dialogue | Reaction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| *"You've served your last meal... Gourmet."* | *"Gourmet? Why I... Um... Oh dear. You're going to kill me now, aren't you?"* | Cowers behind the barrels of wine. |
| *"Soon, the Emperor will die. Murdered, by the Gourmet."* | *"What? The Emperor? But I... Oh. Oh, by the gods, no. No, you can't do this. You can't!"* | Cowers behind the barrels of wine. |
| *(Remain Silent)* | *"Yes, well. Perhaps the proprietor of this establishment can assist you. Please, you're starting to... frighten me."* | Ends conversation. |

### No More Soup for You
With the Writ of Passage in hand, return to the sanctuary for your reward. Astrid greets you with special news: All of the pieces are in place, and the [time is at hand](Skyrim_To_Kill_an_Empire.md). She instructs you to head to [Castle Dour](Skyrim_Castle_Dour.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) to fulfill your duties as Listener.

## Notes
- If Balagog drops his dagger when he is killed, it will still count as part of his body, allowing you to drag just the dagger to one of the hiding locations specified above to satisfy the bonus requirements of this quest. This is useful if you are having difficulty dragging his body. Alternatively, open his inventory and take the dagger to make his body easier to drag.
- If you decapitate Balagog, you'll need to hide his head along with the rest of his corpse.
- If you kill Balagog outside and drag him into the pond, the water will turn red, as if tainted with blood.
- Be careful if you have the [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md) perk for lightning-based magic active, or if the [Dragonborn Force](Skyrim_Dragonborn_Force.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> enhancement for your Unrelenting Force shout is active, as they can turn Balagog's corpse into an ash pile, making it impossible to hide.
- If you have the writ of passage in your inventory when you encounter [Balbus](Skyrim_Balbus.md), you can convince him that you are the Gourmet, and the star-struck Balbus will give you a collection of ingredients he has acquired to impress the Gourmet.
- If you fail to hide the Gourmet's body, don't retrieve his papers, or if you spare Anton Virane, [Festus Krex](Skyrim_Festus_Krex.md) will criticize you, depending on the circumstances: - If you don't hide the Gourmet's body, Festus will be angry with you, saying *"Good, good. And you've got the Writ of Passage, too, I see. So there's that. I've also received word from Markarth. It would seem Anton Virane has suffered an untimely fate, as well. Good. But unfortunately, that inn is abuzz with the news of somebody killing one of their guests! What, hiding the body was too much for you to handle? Bah! Useless. Here's your gold. Now go see Astrid. The time to move against the Emperor has finally come. Sithis help us all."*
- If you tell Festus that the Gourmet is dead but you don't have his papers with you, Festus will be upset, and say *"Good. And his Writ of Passage? Oh wait, let me guess - you don't have them. Fool! Amateur! Without those papers you'll never be able to present yourself as the Gourmet! Off with you! Back to the body to retrieve them!"*
- If the Gourmet is dead and you have the papers, but you spared Anton Virane, Festus will go on a similar rant, and say *"Good, good. And you've got the Writ of Passage too, I see, so that's done. But why? Why, pray tell, have I received word that Anton Virane is still walking around Markarth, hale and healthy as the day he was born? Back to Markarth with you! Virane must be silenced! He's a potential witness, you witless dagger jabber!"*

## Bugs
- Balagog's corpse may be positioned in or around furniture after dying, making it impossible to drag it to a hiding place and complete the bonus objective.

- - Casting Firebolt or similar area-of-effect spells on his body may free his body from the furniture.

- When talking to Anton Virane, you can exit dialogue before telling him you are going to kill him, then restart dialogue with him and repeat the intimidation check to get additional Speech experience. This can be repeated as often as you like to level your [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) skill. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.
- After your conversation with Anton Virane is finished, the script may not update the quest to remove his essential status and allow you to kill him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
- Occasionally, when attempting to take the Gourmet's Writ of Passage, it will not show up in his inventory. <sup>**?**</sup> - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can give the Gourmet the Writ by going into the [console](Skyrim_Console.md), clicking on the Gourmet, and then entering `additem 0003beb6 1`.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Alternatively, you can enter `setstage db08 200` to complete the quest, `player.additem 3beb6 1` to give yourself the Writ, then `setstage db09 5` to start the next quest.
- [Hadring](Skyrim_Hadring.md)'s and [Fultheim](Skyrim_Fultheim.md)'s dialogue topics about Balagog no longer make sense after you kill him. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, addresses this issue. A condition check has been added so that the topic is unavailable once Balagog is dead.
- If you choose to "Eat" Balagog with the [Ring of Namira](Skyrim_Ring_of_Namira.md) equipped, it may trigger a "complete" for the bonus objective, but you may not get the bonus reward. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Balagog may appear dead and naked in his room in the Nightgate Inn Cellar without the Writ of Passage, making the quest impossible to finish. <sup>**?**</sup>
