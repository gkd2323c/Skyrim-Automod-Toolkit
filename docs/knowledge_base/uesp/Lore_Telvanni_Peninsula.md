[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/ON-load-Telvanni_Peninsula.png/200px-ON-load-Telvanni_Peninsula.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Telvanni_Peninsula.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Telvanni_Peninsula.png) The eastern reaches of the Peninsula [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/LO-map-Morrowind_%28Morrowind_Codex%29.png/200px-LO-map-Morrowind_%28Morrowind_Codex%29.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Morrowind_Codex).png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Morrowind_(Morrowind_Codex).png) Map of Morrowind, showing the Telvanni Peninsula The Telvanni Peninsula was a region in the eastern part of mainland [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), and is bracketed by the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) to the west and the [Padomaic Ocean](Lore_Padomaic_Ocean.md) to the east.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TSAI-1)</sup> It was composed of two regions: the fertile river valleys of the [Fungal Lowlands](Lore_Fungal_Lowlands.md), and the rocky volcanic cliff coastlines of the [Padomaic Crest](Lore_Padomaic_Crest.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VG:TP-2)</sup> The land south of the region is made up of the coastal [Bal Foyen](Lore_Bal_Foyen.md) and fertile [Deshaan Plain](Lore_Deshaan.md) areas,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1M-3)</sup> while the land north of it is made up of badlands and arid desert plateaus,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-5)</sup> whose inhabitants wear Dust Adept clothing to adapt to its dry environment.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TDA-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CA-7)</sup> As the [Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) presently control the heartlands and entire east coast,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GHo M-8)</sup> and the [Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) lost all holdings on the mainland, it is unknown what it is now named or if the region still exists.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Neloth DB-9)</sup>

## Geography
The Telvanni Peninsula borders [Bal Foyen](Lore_Bal_Foyen.md) and [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) to the south. It shares the Inner Sea waters of [Zafirbel Bay](Lore_Zafirbel_Bay.md) with Vvardenfell, separated by the Vvardenfell Rift.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ro KZ-10)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MWConcept Map-11)</sup> The land north of Necrom is separated by a ridge and controlled by Indoril, with [Firewatch](Lore_Firewatch.md) being the largest city in the region. The northern extremities are very dry, while the southern reaches are more temperate.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-4)</sup> The landscape consists of fertile plains in the east, and badlands in the west.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MWConcept Map-11)</sup> In particular, the north has arid desert plateaus, mesas, and high steppes.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-5)</sup> Inhabitants of the northern peninsula wear "Dust Adept" clothing to adapt to its dry environment.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TDA-6)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CA-7)</sup> In the Padomaic Ocean, the [isles](Lore_Northeast_Archipelago.md) surrounding the palace of [Port Telvannis](Lore_Port_Telvannis.md)<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LAHT-12)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MWMap-13)</sup><sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-TXGL-14)</sup> are found across the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) to the northeast.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Morrowind Map-15)</sup> The archipelago is a mix of rocky, rugged terrain and flat plains.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Morrowind Dialogue-16)</sup><sup>[[14]](#cite_note-MWElone-17)</sup><sup>[[UOL 4]](#cite_note-Concept Map-18)</sup> The island of [Vounoura](Lore_Vounoura.md) is found roughly a month's voyage away from Tel Aruhn,<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-2920ES-19)</sup> but it is unknown whether it is near the Peninsula. The peninsula is visible from [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) across the waters of the Inner Sea.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ESO-20)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg) Fungal Lowlands
### Fungal Lowlands
The [Fungal Lowlands](Lore_Fungal_Lowlands.md) are a spacious tract of wilderness and river valleys making up the western half of the peninsula. It is composed of flatlands along the river, with a number of secluded clearings between the road and the coast.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VG:TP-2)</sup>

Its main settlement is Ald Isra, where the Mouths of the Telvanni Magisters convene. The [Dwemer ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) of [Kemel-Ze](Lore_Kemel-Ze.md) is found along the coast, with easy access to it from boats and nearby mainland villages, such as Marog.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ro KZ-10)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/94/ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg/200px-ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Padomaic_Crest.jpg) Padomaic Crest
### Padomaic Crest
The [Padomaic Crest](Lore_Padomaic_Crest.md) is a volcanic range making up the eastern half of the peninsula. The eastern end of the formation creates a secondary peninsula where the city of [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md) lies.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VG:TP-2)</sup>

The eastern coast of Morrowind is a coastline of treacherous cliffs, especially in the region around Necrom.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Ro KZ-10)</sup>

The island of [Gorne](Lore_Gorne.md) is found north of Necrom, in the bay between the Padomaic Crest and the northern peninsula.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-FOTN-21)</sup>

## History
In [3E 121](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_121), King [Uriel Mantiarco](Lore_Uriel_III.md) of [Solitude](Lore_Solitude_(kingdom).md) coordinated a three-way attack. In his second attack against the Empire, Uriel's forces targeted multiple Imperial garrisons on coastal islands in Eastern Morrowind. While the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) was occupied with the battle in Morrowind and with the first attack in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), the King captured the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and proclaimed himself Emperor Uriel Septim III. One of the Empire's casualties here was Empress [Kintyra II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md)'s consort, Kontin Arynx.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-22)</sup> By the time the [War of the Diamond](Lore_War_of_the_Red_Diamond.md) took place, all of northern Morrowind supported Emperor Uriel III and [Potema](Lore_Potema.md), the Wolf Queen of Solitude.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-BHOTE2-23)</sup>

## Politics
Despite the name, [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md)'s control over the peninsula has waxed and waned over the centuries. During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), House Telvanni cared little for what lies outside of their sanctuary on the coast and controlled a number of strongholds (such as Ald Isra, Tel Baro, Tel Dreloth, Tel Hlurag Ven, and Tel Rendys) across the length and breadth of the peninsula.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-LAHT-12)</sup><sup>[[20]](#cite_note-TGHa TU-24)</sup><sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Ruby Shalk-25)</sup> The main exclusion to this was the City of [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md), which was under control of the [Keepers of the Dead](Lore_Keepers_of_the_Dead.md) (an arm of the [Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md))<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-CG-26)</sup><sup>[[23]](#cite_note-PF-27)</sup> and remained independent from any great houses,<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-28)</sup> and the island of Gorne which was administrated by [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md).<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-29)</sup> The Temple also administered [Sailenmora](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sailenmora).<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-Sailenmora-30)</sup> [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md) had already began carving territory in the southwest, and the [Camonna Tong](Lore_Camonna_Tong.md) head quartered themselves in a den called Camonnaruhn just south of the Dres.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-Camonnaruhn-31)</sup> Few [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md) make their home on the Peninsula.<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-ONNusum-32)</sup>

By the end of the Second Era, House Telvanni's territory on the peninsula was limited solely to the hills and islands of the extreme northeast, who preferred to tend their herds of giant insects amid the rocky arid hills and islands of that corner.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1M-3)</sup><sup>[[UOL 5]](#cite_note-ESOLive-33)</sup> By that time the [Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) controlled most of it, particularly the east coast up to Necrom and west coast all along the Inner Sea.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GHo M-8)</sup>

In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Indoril control much of the central highlands, eastern coast and western coast, while House Dres controlled the southeast coast surrounding [Alavelis](Lore_Alavelis.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-GHo M-8)</sup> House Telvanni's holdings on the peninsula once consisted of Tel Aedrin, [Velothis Haven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Velothis_Haven),<sup>[[29]](#cite_note-MWFalura Llervu-34)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-5)</sup> and [Llothanis Heights](Lore_Llothanis_Heights.md).<sup>[[30]](#cite_note-Arena-35)</sup> Baldaras,<sup>[[UOL 6]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-36)</sup> [Greenheights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Greenheights), [Helnim Wall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Helnim_Wall), [Sailen Vulgate](Lore_Sailen_Vulgate.md), and [Draloris](Lore_Draloris.md) lie within the borders of the [Indoril District](Lore_House_Indoril.md). [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md) also controlled the ancient fortress of Bal Tereg, found near Firewatch on the inlet of Ouada Gla'amnir.<sup>[[UOL 6]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-36)</sup>

The [Bathogorgen](Lore_Bathogorgen.md), who have the power of shapeshifting into virtually anything, are the dominant [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) clan of the region.<sup>[[UOL 6]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-36)</sup> The Dusksaber vampires were active in the peninsula during the Second Era.<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-37)</sup>

As of the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md), the Telvanni have no territory on the peninsula or anywhere else on the mainland; their properties are all on Vvardenfell and [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Neloth DB-9)</sup>
