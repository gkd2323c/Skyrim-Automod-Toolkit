[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-npc-Leontius_Salvius.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Leontius_Salvius.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leontius_Salvius.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Leontius_Salvius.jpg) Leontius Salvius **Leontius Salvius** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) under the employ of [Eydis](Skyrim_Eydis.md) at the [Old Hroldan Inn](Skyrim_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md). His parents are [Rogatus](Skyrim_Rogatus_Salvius.md) and [Vigdis](Skyrim_Vigdis_Salvius.md) at the [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md). Leontius will sleep between midnight and 7am in the inn. He will spend the rest of the day working outside. He normally does not provide any services, but should Eydis pass away he'll take over as publican at the inn. As publican he is actually quite personable, but lacks the historical spin that Eydis puts on a stay at Old Hroldan.

He wears barkeep [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md#0005b6a0), he wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Old_Hroldan_Inn.md) to the inn and some [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Related Quests
- **[Rogatus's Letter](Skyrim_Rogatus%27s_Letter.md)**: Deliver a letter to a [farmer's](Skyrim_Rogatus_Salvius.md) son.

## Dialogue
While Eydis is alive, he may tell you about the inn, saying:

- *"I work for Eydis. She runs the inn."*
- *"You should head on inside. Eydis or [Skuli](Skyrim_Skuli.md) can take care of you."*

If Eydis has died, he will tell you:

- *"Skuli owns the inn with his mother gone, but I handle the rooms."*
- *"The Old Hroldan Inn is still open, bless poor Eydis's memory."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue
During the quest [Rogatus's Letter](Skyrim_Rogatus%27s_Letter.md), you will be able to deliver [his father's letter](Skyrim_Rogatus%27s_Letter_(item).md):

- **I have a letter from your father.**
- *"What's this? I guess some things never change. Thanks for bringing this to me. Here, for your trouble."*
