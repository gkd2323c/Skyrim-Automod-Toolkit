[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/AR-place-Laintar_Dale.jpg/200px-AR-place-Laintar_Dale.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Laintar_Dale.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Laintar_Dale.jpg) Laintar Dale during the Imperial Simulacrum Laintar Dale is a historical settlement within the province of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup> In the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) it was the site of a [Snow Elf](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) city called **Laidal**, which remains buried beneath the modern settlement.<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Laintar Dale was an active settlement, being ruled by Lord Mojan. It had a rivalry with [Helarchen Creek](Lore_Helarchen_Creek.md). It was neighbored by several settlements, including Dunpar Wall to the west, Helarchen Creek to the northwest, [Amol](Lore_Amol.md) to the northeast, [Whiterun](Lore_Whiterun.md) to the southwest, [Dragon Wood](Lore_Dragon_Wood.md) to the east, and [Pargran Village](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pargran_Village) to the southeast. As of then it also had road connections to Whiterun, [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), and Amol.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Arena-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Map Arena-2)</sup>

## Notes
- | [1.](#note_nb1) | Certain scholars claim that Laidal (or Laidal'dal in later renditions), an ancient [Snow Elf](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) settlement where Wulfric, the youngest son of [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md), [married](Lore_Marriage.md) a Snow Elf named Eormi, corresponds to the ruins beneath Laintar Dale. A speculative notion suggests a potential connection to issues with the Laintar Dale sewers. However, this hypothesis lacks substantiating evidence and relies on dubious etymologies.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TW-3)</sup> |
