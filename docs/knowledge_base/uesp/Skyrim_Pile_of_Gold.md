# Gold

This article is about the material and currency. For information on Golden Saint equipment, see [Golden](Skyrim_Golden.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-item-Gold_Ingot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gold_Ingot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold_Ingot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold_Ingot.jpg) A Gold Ingot [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f9/SR-item-Gold_Ore.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gold_Ore.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold_Ore.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold_Ore.jpg) 3 pieces of Gold Ore [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/SR-activity-Mining_%28Gold%29.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Mining_%28Gold%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Gold).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Gold).jpg) A Gold Ore Vein [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-item-Gold.jpg/200px-SR-item-Gold.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Gold.jpg) Some coins and coinpurses **Gold** is the standard currency of Tamriel. Although officially the coins are called **Septims**, gold is the name used in your inventory and many other places. Gold is also a metal that can be [mined](Skyrim_Mining.md), [smelted](Skyrim_Smelter.md), and [forged](Skyrim_Forge.md) into jewelry. Gold ore and ingots cannot be directly minted to convert them into currency, however, many [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) (Blacksmiths, Jewelers, and General Goods merchants) will buy them from you.

- 3 Gold Ores are obtained by [mining](Skyrim_Mining.md) Gold Ore Veins. They cannot be purchased.
- 1 Gold Ingot is obtained by [smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) 2 Gold Ores. They cannot be purchased.
- Gold Smithing to create jewelry is possible from the start of the game, without unlocking any [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks.

## Mineral Sources
In addition to the following guaranteed sources, gold ore may be randomly found in Falmer loot starting at level 10. In addition, gold ore can be created by [transmuting](Skyrim_Transmute_Mineral_Ore.md) [Silver Ore](Skyrim_Silver_Ore.md). It can also be created by [master transmuting](Skyrim_Master_Transmute.md) [Iron Ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bittercup.md)</sup>.

There are a total of 78 veins, 23 ores, and 48 ingots at guaranteed locations. Many individual veins are scattered outside across Tamriel. Only notable locations are included in the following list, in particular places where multiple veins are located near each other and interior areas containing veins. The [Gold Veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Gold_Veins) category contains a more complete list of places containing single veins, and all exterior ore veins are shown on [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=the+Skyrim+map&search=Gold+Ore+Vein).

## Notes
- Septims can't be dropped. However, they can be stored in containers.
- 1 Gold Ore is needed for the quest [Ringmaker](Skyrim_Ringmaker.md).
- Gold Ore can be sold to [Pavo Attius](Skyrim_Pavo_Attius.md) for the quest [Mine Ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md). You do not need to finish his quest for clearing out the Forsworn from [Kolskeggr mine](Skyrim_Kolskeggr_Mine.md) in order to sell him gold ore.
- 1 Gold Ingot is needed at [Workbenches](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve [Konahrik](Skyrim_Konahrik.md).
- Ore veins (all types) sometimes do not respawn normally. See [Mining (Bugs)](Skyrim_Mining.md#Bugs) and elsewhere on that article and its talk page.
- Although the Gold Jeweled Necklace requires two Flawless Amethysts to create, three gems are clearly visible on the pendant itself.
