[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-npc-Sigar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sigar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sigar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sigar.jpg) Sigar roaming the wilderness **Sigar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and a cannibal. After the conclusion of the quest [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md), you may [encounter him in the wilderness standing over a dead person's body](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Namira_Cultist_Offers_the_First_Bite). His reaction depends on your actions in the quest.

Sigar wears [ragged robes](Skyrim_Ragged_Robes.md), and is equipped with a leveled [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md) which can be up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality. He can cast the spells [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Heal Other](Skyrim_Heal_Other.md), [Muffle](Skyrim_Muffle_(spell).md), and [Invisibility](Skyrim_Invisibility_(spell).md). He follows the corpse if you move or [reanimate](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) it, although he won't enter interior cells after the corpse.

## Related Quests
- **[The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md)**: Prepare a [feast](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md) for the [Lady of Decay](Lore_Namira.md).

## Quest-Related Events
If you completed the quest by partaking in the feast, Sigar hails you, saying:

- *"It is the Champion of [Namira](Lore_Namira.md)! I was just about to eat. Here, you should have the first bite."*

If you failed the quest without ever getting the feast, for example by killing [Eola](Skyrim_Eola.md) upon your first meeting, Sigar panics, saying:

- *"Don't look at me. I didn't kill her. I was just... making sure the body got buried."*

If you saved [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md) at the last moment and killed Eola and the other cannibals, Sigar will be hostile. He will also be hostile if you complete the quest and then kill all the cannibals and Eola straight afterwards.
