# Grievous Twilight

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Grievous_Twilight.jpg) A Grievous Twilight (ESO) Grievous Twilights are large, intelligent winged [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that are skilled in magic and melee combat. They have clawed limbs, black horns, bluish-purple skin, glowing blue eyes, spiked tails, and possess the ability to teleport, making them formidable foes. They are believed to be a corruption of the [winged](Lore_Winged_Twilight.md) [twilights](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Twilights) twisted into a more frightening creature by [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-QC2015-1)</sup> They have a brutish masculine form with a skeletal visage more so in the image of Bal,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-AS-3)</sup> and unlike winged twilights, their wings are separate limbs from their arms.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> They are often associated with and found serving Molag Bal,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NJ:IC-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> but they can also be found under [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ESOClock-5)</sup>

When they teleport, they disappear in a shroud of black mist, which can sometimes be seen traveling to where they will reappear.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> It is suspected that their teleportation is a form of [shadow magic](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md), along with the shadow bolts they fire at opponents.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BT-6)</sup> Their cold claws are clammy to the touch and smell of brimstone, and can be used with ritual wine to create ash that can apply protective wards to everyday objects. Wards made from Grievous Twilight claws will induce great pain in any Daedra who attempts to interact with the warded object.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-SJ-7)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PT-8)</sup> Vampire clans such as the Gray Host and Thorn Legion make use of spectral Blood Twilights, which have unique [blood magic](Lore_Blood_Magic.md) abilities.

## History
During the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), many Grievous Twilights served Molag Bal in his campaign in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) as soldiers and generals. An exceptionally large Grievous Twilight, [Lord Warden Dusk](Lore_Lord_Warden_Dusk.md), was the cruel overseer of the [Imperial City Prison](Lore_Imperial_City_Prison.md),<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ICPDR-9)</sup> and had previously trapped the living essence of the [Foolkillers Clan](Lore_Foolkillers_Clan.md) in the armor he wore.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

Amid the return of [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md), a number of Grievous Twilights made up the command of the [Shardborn](Lore_Shardborn.md), such as Baron Rize and Count Ryelaz.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Knot Awaits-11)</sup>
