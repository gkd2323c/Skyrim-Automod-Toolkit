## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) in the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) when your Alteration skill is 90 or higher.
2. Find [Kahvozein's Fang](Skyrim_Kahvozein%27s_Fang.md).
3. Harvest [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [heartscales](Skyrim_Dragon_Heartscales.md).
4. Return to Tolfdir.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Kahvozein's Fang
To initiate this quest, you must have an [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) skill of 90 or higher. Speak with [Tolfdir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolfdir) and ask if there is anything new that he can teach you. He tells you that he has been working on a new spell but, to complete his research, he needs [heartscales](Skyrim_Dragon_Heartscales.md). These must be cut from a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md)'s skeleton and can only be done using a dagger called [Kahvozein's Fang](Skyrim_Kahvozein%27s_Fang.md). He asks you to retrieve the blade, which can be found in a radiant [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md) dungeon.

### The Heartscales
Once you have the dagger, you must either slay a dragon or locate a dragon corpse in order to retrieve the heartscales. If you intend on hunting down a live dragon, it is not necessary to slay it using Kahvozein's Fang. You need only equip the dagger in your right hand (it won't work with your left hand) and loot the creature's corpse. The heartscales will automatically be added to your inventory.

Once you have the scales, bring them back to Tolfdir. He will reward you with the [Dragonhide](Skyrim_Dragonhide.md) spell tome, and will now sell the [Mass Paralysis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mass_Paralysis) spell tome.

## Notes
- It is possible to find Kahvozein's Fang in a dungeon and collect heartscales from a dragon's corpse before the quest is given.
- You can harvest heartscales even if the dragon disintegrates.
- Once the quest is complete, you can continue to use the Fang to collect a heartscale from each dragon corpse, in addition to the standard dragon loot. These scales can't be used for crafting, but are no longer quest items and can be sold to vendors.
- While it is not required to complete [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) to start this quest, completing it is necessary in order to find a dragon suitable for heartscale harvesting. Although the [Skeletal Dragon](Skyrim_Skeletal_Dragon.md) can be found and defeated beforehand, it does not yield scales of any kind. Heartscales cannot be harvested from [Krosulhah](Skyrim_Krosulhah.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> either, nor can they be collected from [Mirmulnir](Skyrim_Mirmulnir.md). An exception to the last point can happen if a Draugr Overlord is lured near one of the Blackreach lifts, using Dread Zombie to get it inside. Once inside, it can be attacked and its Unrelenting Force Shout will release [Vulthuryol](Skyrim_Vulthuryol.md), whose soul will be absorbed and its heartscales looted.
- Resetting your Alteration skill level after accepting the quest will prevent you from turning in the heartscales until you reach Alteration level 90 again.

## Bugs
- In your quest log, this quest is mistakenly decorated with the [Side Quests](Skyrim_Side_Quests.md) knotwork (as displayed in the quest header), instead of the correct [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) knotwork that is shown on all other college quests. This does not have any known effect on gameplay. In particular, this quest is *not* considered to be a Side Quest for the purpose of the [Sideways](Skyrim_Sideways.md) [achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md). **Mod Notes**: The quest Type is "None" instead of "Mages Guild"- ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.1, fixes this bug.
- After retrieving the heart scales, it is possible that you will not be able to talk to Tolfdir and you will be unable to finish the quest. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can complete the quest by using the console command `player.setstage 000D0755 200`.
- The dagger's name is misspelled "Kavohzein's Fang" in both the Quest List objective and the map objective text.
