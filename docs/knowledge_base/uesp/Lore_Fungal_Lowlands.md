[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fungal_Lowlands.jpg) Fungal Lowlands circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Fungal Lowlands** are a coastal region in east-central [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VG:TP-1)</sup> found within the [Indoril District](Lore_House_Indoril.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-GHOM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TGHU-3)</sup> It is a spacious tract of wilderness and fertile river valleys spanning along the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-POT-4)</sup> with a number of secluded clearings between the roads and the coast. The [Sun's Death](Lore_Sun%27s_Death.md) eruption of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) laid waste at least half of Morrowind and led to a permanent shift of population south towards the broad southern plains,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-POT-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-PGE1-5)</sup> where the flat land is now often for camping along the river.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VG:TP-1)</sup> It borders the regions of [Bal Foyen](Lore_Bal_Foyen.md) to the west, the [Padomaic Crest](Lore_Padomaic_Crest.md) to the east, and the region of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) to the south. Together with the Padomaic Crest, it was considered one of the two regions making up the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md). The settlements of Ald Isra and Camonnaruhn were once found here, while the town of [Sailenmora](Lore_Sailen_Vulgate.md) emerged between the lowlands and the Crest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-VG:TP-1)</sup>

## Notes
- In a concept map for *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, this area comprising this region was originally planned to primarily be made up of badlands.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MWMap-6)</sup>
