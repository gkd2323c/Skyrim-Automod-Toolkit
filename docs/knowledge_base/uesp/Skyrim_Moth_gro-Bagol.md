(However, see [Ghorza's Bugs section](Skyrim_Ghorza_gra-Bagol.md#Bugs).) He cannot be invested in directly. [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6c/SR-quest-Coated_in_Blood.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Coated_in_Blood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Coated_in_Blood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Coated_in_Blood.jpg) Moth working at the forge **Moth gro-Bagol** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) and the personal [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md) for [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md) of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He is the brother of the other Orc blacksmith in Markarth, [Ghorza gra-Bagol](Skyrim_Ghorza_gra-Bagol.md). You can normally find him in his personal quarters in the [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md): From the level just below the jarl's throne, walk north, then east. If you [get him](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coated_in_Blood) a [Daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md), he will reward you with a random leveled piece of armor and make you [blood-kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) if you aren't already. He will also become a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

Moth wears a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron#0005b69f) along with a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e). He wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and carries a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When approaching him:

*"I'm the Jarl's personal blacksmith, but that doesn't mean I can't sell you a few pieces for a fair price."* *"The [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) has always been good to the Orcs, but this war of theirs is none of my concern."* *"I served in the Legion with [Thongvor Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md). He's a good man and a tough soldier."* *"I learned to forge when I was a pup, then I joined the Legion and mastered it."* Speaking to him:

**What kind of weapons do you forge?** *"Anything. Served in the Legion with my sister, Ghorza, for over 10 years. We've seen every piece you can imagine. Have you met her? She runs the forge near the smelter. Just don't bother talking to [her apprentice](Skyrim_Tacitus_Sallustius.md). Boy has no talent."*
### Conversations
Sometimes he can be found conversing with Thongvor Silver-Blood:

**Thongvor**: *"How's the forge today?"*
**Moth**: *"It's fine. Much better than the rickety tools I had in the Legion."*
**Thongvor**: *"Remember that old rusty sword I had? Damn thing must have had over a hundred chips in it."*
**Moth**: *"Well, if you had learned to swing it correctly. A sword is an extension of the arm, not a meat cleaver."*
**Thongvor**: *"I was about to say how impressed I was that you always managed to put it back together."*
**Moth**: *"Flattery doesn't suit you, Thongvor."*

**Moth**: *"Hello, my friend. Come to visit the forge?"*
**Thongvor**: *"I grow tired of counting coin all day. Put steel in my hand, not gold."*
**Moth**: *"Your family's success burdens you, does it?"*
**Thongvor**: *"Sometimes I miss the Legion, back when the Empire stood up for Skyrim."*
**Moth**: *"No politics, Thongvor. I told you before."*

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Coated in Blood
You can also ask Moth about recommendations on buying some armor:

**I'm thinking of buying some armor. Any recommendations?** *"Depends on what you need. A [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor) is good for travel. Less to carry. A [heavy armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Heavy_Armor) weighs you down, but no true warrior goes without it. If you want some heavy armor, I might have a deal we could work out. I need a [Daedra's Heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md). Jarl wants me to cool his new sword in blood. You get me a heart, I get you some armor. Interested?"* **Deal.** *"Good hunting."* **No. Sorry.** *"Very well."* Once you've given him the requested item:

**I have your daedra's heart.** *"Good. I'd prefer using water to cool my metal, but it's what the Jarl wants. Here. Some of my best armor. Wear it well."* He will then reward you with a random leveled piece of armor.
