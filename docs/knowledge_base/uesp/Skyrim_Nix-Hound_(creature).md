[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/SR-creature-Nix-Hound_%28pet%29.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Nix-Hound_%28pet%29.jpg)](File_SR-creature-Nix-Hound_(pet).jpg.md) [](File_SR-creature-Nix-Hound_(pet).jpg.md) Pet Nix-Hound A pet Nix-Hound can be purchased from [Revus Sarvani](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Revus_Sarvani) (or [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) if Revus is already dead) as part of the related quest.

The nix-hound is essential, and will not engage in combat. Its main function is to carry items, and it has a carry weight of 75 and it will follow you even if you already have another pet following you. You can instruct it to wait at a city, wait at its current location, wait at your house, or to carry items. It can also be summoned using a [teleportation spell](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Teleport_Pet__Nix-Hound). The nix-hound cannot be detected by Detect Dead or Detect Life.

### Scrounging
The pet nix-hound has an ability that will occasionally give you a random [ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md). When this happens, you will receive a message saying, *"Nix-Hound found something..."* and the ingredient will be added to your inventory.

This ability will start working as soon as the creation has been activated - you can receive items from the hound even before you have purchased it<sup>[*verification needed — See bugs*]</sup>. Four to eight hours of game time after becoming active, you will get your first ingredient. Thereafter, every 16 to 25 hours of game time you have an 80% chance of again getting an ingredient.

The ingredients will be randomly chosen from two leveled lists appropriate to the area you are currently in - the hound distinguishes between being either in Solstheim, or not. For example, you can get [Ash Hopper Jelly](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Hopper_Jelly) while in Solstheim, but not while in mainland Skyrim. Other than that, any [base game ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) or Dragonborn ingredient can be gifted to you - but not ingredients added either through [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Items.md#Alchemy) or [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire_Items.md#Ingredients).

## Bugs
- Do not bring the pet nix-hound to [Druadach Redoubt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach_Redoubt) after siding with [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) during the quest [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md), as it will be hostile towards the friendly Forsworn there. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Adding the nix-hound to the [Druadach Redoubt Faction](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Druadach Redoubt Faction) faction (Ref ID `E3609`) will fix this problem.
- The nix-hound will start giving you items as soon as the Creation has been activated, even before you have finished the introduction quest and purchased it (although it seems to happen only when Nix Hound pet creature is loaded in, that is, when Player is near it.)
- There is an invulnerable hostile nix-hound near [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). <sup>**?**</sup>
- The pet nix-hound is not revealed by [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md).
- It may be impossible to purchase the pet Nix-Hound in some non-English languages. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.640, fixes this bug.
