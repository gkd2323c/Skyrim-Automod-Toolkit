[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/SR-npc-Taarie.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Taarie.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Taarie.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Taarie.jpg) Taarie **Taarie** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [tailor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tailor) living in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). She runs a tailor shop called [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md) with her twin sister [Endarie](Skyrim_Endarie.md) and can be found there during the day.

Of the two sisters, Taarie is the friendlier, though still critical of your attire. Both sisters share the same merchant inventory and gold, so there is no need to deal with Endarie's rough personality in order to buy and sell at Radiant Raiment. However, unlike Endarie, Taarie doesn't mind the shop all day. Instead she goes out to wander Solitude, gathering wood from the Hall of the Dead and dropping it off outside the store. You will not be able to purchase anything from her outside, and will have to go in and purchase from Endarie.

Taarie wears [fine clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fine_Clothes#000f8715) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000cee82). She also carries the [key to Radiant Raiment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Radiant_Raiments) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), along with a selection of [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

Upon seeing you, she will comment on your outfit. If you ask her about it, she'll ask you to do a [favor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fit_for_a_Jarl) for her. Taarie is a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) after agreeing to complete her favor and investing in her shop.

## Dialogue
Taarie may greet you with:

*"Excuse me, I'm trying to watch a traitor to the Empire lose his head."* **(During Roggvir's execution)** *"If you're heading to the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md), you might want to rethink that outfit."* *"If you ever want to replace those old rags, stop by Radiant Raiment."* *"Why, hello! Here to buy? If you have to ask the price, you may be in the wrong store."* **(At her store)** *"I do hope you'll remain in Solitude. This city could do with some new blood."* Speaking to her:

**Do you always insult people when you meet them?** *"It's not an insult, just an observation. If I were walking around with a wound, I would want you to tell me to see a doctor. Your outfit is something of an open wound... where attire is concerned. Not many people care, these days. The world is the worse for it."* **What's wrong with my outfit?** *"Nothing at all. It's very... nice. If you want something with a little more style, however, you should come by our shop. Radiant Raiment. We could fix you right up."*

Taarie can be seen having a short conversation with her sister Endarie about her sewing:

**Taarie:** *"Sister dear, could you try sewing those sleeves a little tighter? We can't have our customers looking sloppy because of your work."* **Endarie:** *"Oh certainly, dear sister. We couldn't afford to have our customers look like the simple pigs they are, now could we?"* **Taarie:** *"Good. And while you're at it, perhaps you should sew your mouth shut when our fine customers are within earshot, hrm?"*
## Notes
- She is the only [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) that you can marry in the game.
- If you invest in Endarie's store, you will be unable to invest in Taarie's store; they share the same goods, so investing in both of them would result in the store being invested in twice. You will thus be unable to raise Taarie's disposition high enough to make her available for marriage. - You can work around this, but both sisters will need to be present in the shop when you invest money in their shop. Speak with one sister and agree to give an investment, then immediately quit conversation and start dialogue with the other; you will be able to invest in the second sister as well.
- If you're wearing Radiant Raiment fine clothes before obtaining the [Fit for a Jarl](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fit_for_a_Jarl) quest, she will still comment on your outfit negatively. After agreeing to model for [Jarl Elisif](Skyrim_Elisif_the_Fair.md), you will get the exact copy of Radiant Raiment fine clothes that you wore.
- Taarie has the exact same facial data as her sister, [Endarie](Skyrim_Endarie.md). - The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* states that the two are twin sisters, so it is possible that they are meant to be identical twins.
