# Feyfolken (artifact)

Feyfolken is a supposed artifact of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), which is only known from a [series of historical fiction books](Lore_Feyfolken.md). It is a quill enchanted by a daedra by the name of Feyfolken serving Clavicus Vile at the request of Thaurbad, a sage for the Temple of Auri-El.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It transfigures the writing of the user into scripture of unparalleled quality.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The trade-off of wielding such an item is that the quill manipulates the thoughts of its beholder. In the case of Thaurbad, he was driven to suicide, even writing notes to whomever would discover his body.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
