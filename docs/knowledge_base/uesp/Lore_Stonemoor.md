[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/AR-place-Stonemoor.jpg/200px-AR-place-Stonemoor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Stonemoor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Stonemoor.jpg) Stonemoor during the Imperial Simulacrum (Arena) Stonemoor is a location found in southeast [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), found in between the city-states of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) and [Elinhir](Lore_Elinhir.md), near the [Brena River](Lore_Brena_River.md) on the border of [Colovia](Lore_Colovia.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup>

Stonemoor has been in existence since at least the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md), around [2E 862](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_862).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup> During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late- [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the town of Stonemoor was an active settlement was ruled by Lady Nhosemka and had a rivalry with Vulkneu Town.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> Stonemoor is neighbored by several settlements, including Rihad to the west, [Helland](Lore_Helland.md) and [Cartmont](Lore_Cartmont.md) to the north, Stonedale to the northeast,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARMap-3)</sup> and [Sutch](Lore_Sutch.md) to the south.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup>
