[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fd/LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Hammerfell_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Hammerfell_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) Hammerfell Cape Shira is an area comprising the western end of mainland [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It has a lush tropical climate,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARTerrain-1)</sup> though the northern coast is rocky. [Salas En](Lore_Salas_En.md) is found here.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TSEE-2)</sup> [Yaghoub](Lore_Yaghoub.md) sailed northwest to round the Cape, becoming the first of the Ra Gada to discover the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-STJOA-3)</sup>

Cape Shira roughly corresponds to the location of the regions of [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md), [Kairou](Lore_Kairou.md), and [Pothago](Lore_Pothago.md) in the Iliac Bay.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Map IB-4)</sup>
