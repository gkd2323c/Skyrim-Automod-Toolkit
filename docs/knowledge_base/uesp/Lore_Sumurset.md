— *[Our Blessed Isles: A Guide](Lore_Our_Blessed_Isles__A_Guide.md)* Summerset<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> (also referred to as: **Summerset Isle**, the **Eternal Isle**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGGerrick-1)</sup> and **Blessed Isle**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOProxy Queen-3)</sup> is a large island to the southwest of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s mainland. It is the largest of the three main islands in the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), which encompasses over a dozen more smaller islands. The province's capital city of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md) is found here.

## Geography
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/ON-place-Summerset_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Summerset_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Summerset_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Summerset_02.jpg) The landscape of Summerset Isle circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The landscape of Summerset Isle is similar to that of its sister island of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md), with a warm climate, colorful forests, idyllic meadows, and craggy mountains. Most of the southern part of the island and a central region in the north are covered with mountains, with a central pass linking the western and eastern parts of the island.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Summerset ESO-4)</sup> The highest mountain on the island is [Eton Nir](Lore_Eton_Nir.md), located in the northern region. [Coral](Lore_Coral.md) is abundant on the shores of Summerset Isle; the [Coral Forest](Lore_Coral_Forest.md) on the eastern shore is one of the best-known coral formations.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CF-5)</sup> Coral formations are sometimes found far from the sea, which suggests original occupation by the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup> A river with two tributaries named [The Clott](Lore_Clott_River.md) flows into the northeastern shore.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The isle is home to the city of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md), the capital of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md) and the seat of the High Elven rulers since time immemorial. The city of [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) sits atop Eton Nir, the highest mountain. Other major cities include [Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md), [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md), [Sunhold](Lore_Sunhold.md), and [Dusk](Lore_Dusk.md). The [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md), one of the eight mythical [focal points](Lore_The_Towers.md) of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md), was once located in the northern mountains. Like Auridon, Summerset is also dotted with [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) ruins.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-7)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/ON-place-Crystal_Tower.jpg/200px-ON-place-Crystal_Tower.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Crystal_Tower.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Crystal_Tower.jpg) The Crystal Tower Summerset Isle was colonized along with the other island in the archipelago by the Aldmer when they came from [Aldmeris](Lore_Aldmeris.md) in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). The ancestors of the modern [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) formed a caste-based society and established the city of Alinor as their capital. The [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) was a period of unrest, with internal conflicts between the city-states, as well as [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) and [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) invasions. In the third millennium of the First Era, Summerset Isle was struck by the [Thrassian Plague](Lore_Thrassian_Plague.md), which depopulated whole towns such as [Corgrad](Lore_Corgrad_Wastes.md). Since early times, Summerset Isle had been off-limits to almost all non-Altmer. In their isolation, the elves of the Isle developed a unique architectural style, more similar to the [Falmeri](Lore_Falmer.md) style than to the architecture of Auridon.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOWasting Away-8)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-EGTSummerset Isles-9)</sup> The population on the Summerset Isle was three to four times larger in the late First Era, than it was in the mid Second Era.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-10)</sup>

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/ON-place-Alinor.jpg/200px-ON-place-Alinor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alinor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Alinor.jpg) Alinor, the capital In the sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), Queen [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md), daughter of King [Hidellith](Lore_Hidellith.md), formed the first [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), an alliance with [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) whose goal was to conquer [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and seize the [Ruby Throne](Lore_Ruby_Throne.md). Ayrenn chose [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) in Valenwood rather than Alinor to be the capital of the Dominion, and her cousin [Alwinarwe](Lore_Alwinarwe.md) ruled Summerset on her behalf. Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Ayrenn issued a [decree](Lore_An_End_to_Isolation.md) that opened the borders of Summerset Isle for outsiders, which resulted in an influx of immigrants.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-7)</sup> It remained open until roughly [2E 814](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_814) when foreign traders were only allowed at its ports.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1AD-11)</sup>

The Second Era also saw the Abyssal Cabal's plot to sink Summerset Isle. The Cabal were a group of [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) who wanted to reclaim the island for their underwater kingdom of [Ul'vor Kus](Lore_Ul%27vor_Kus.md). They joined forces with the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md), an alliance of the cultists of [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md), [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), and [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md) who attempted to gain control over the Crystal Tower around 2E 582. Both groups were defeated by the combined efforts of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-7)</sup>. At the end of the Second Era, the island was conquered by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) in his unification of Tamriel under the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).

In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Summerset Isle, along with the rest of the province, was officially incorporated into Tiber Septim's Empire. In [3E 110](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_110), the island was almost lost to the [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) in the [War of the Isle](Lore_War_of_the_Isle.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup> In the late fourth century of the Third Era, Summerset Isle was visited by the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md), who retrieved one of the pieces of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) from the Crystal Tower.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Arena-12)</sup> In [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the island was devastated by the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md), with the hordes of the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) managing to topple the Crystal Tower. The forces of the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) contributed greatly to repelling the Daedra, which later allowed them to gain control of the whole province, and ultimately form the Third Aldmeri Dominion, the dominant force in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the early [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-RT-13)</sup>

The apprentice of [Balagog gro-Nolob](Lore_Balagog_gro-Nolob.md), [Urzhag gro-Larak](Lore_Urzhag_gro-Larak.md), visited the Summerset Isles several times around the end of the Second Century of the Fourth Era during his travels across Tamriel which saw him learn from a variety of races to improve his cooking skills. During one of these visits, he got permission with two other outsiders to research with some limitations in the College of Sapiarchs. Though the Isles were opened up, many of the native inhabitants were still hesitant to outsiders.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-Cookbook2-14)</sup>

## Architecture
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/ON-place-Cloudrest.jpg/200px-ON-place-Cloudrest.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cloudrest.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Cloudrest.jpg) The city of Cloudrest atop Eton Nir, the highest mountain on Summerset Isle The architectural style of many cities in Summerset Isle is unique from its counterparts on [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md). White marble construction, ornate glass and metalwork, and enormous elegant towers are a signature found across the island.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-7)</sup> According to some, Altmer build such vast towers because of the belief that they were once gods, and so they aspire to reach the heavens once more.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-twitch1-15)</sup>

The modern architectural style of Summerset has been refined for thousands of years, as is evident by the numerous ruins that dot the island sharing a similar style. Altmer architects are said to look into the past for inspiration and add new layers of refinement, elegance, and detail to their structures. This discipline of continual improvement is in line with the overarching cultural concept known as the "Path to Alaxon", where many Altmer dedicate their long lives striving for perfection in their life or work.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-ESOOlnewil-16)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-twitch1-15)</sup>

Although human traders to the island have famously described the architecture of Summerset as being made from glass or insect wings,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-PGE1AD-11)</sup> this is believed to not be entirely accurate and was simply the viewpoint of outsiders attempting to explain what they saw.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-twitch1-15)</sup>

## Flora
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/ON-place-Russafeld_03.jpg/200px-ON-place-Russafeld_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Russafeld_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Russafeld_03.jpg) Grape fields [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png/200px-ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-summerset-Summerset_Isle.png) Crystal Tower and Summerset's wilderness Summerset is known for its beautiful cherry blossom trees that come in pink and white varieties.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-TTWC-17)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-TTPC-18)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-TG-19)</sup> It also has red and purple maple trees as well as gingko,<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-AMR-20)</sup><sup>[[19]](#cite_note-AMP-21)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-TG-19)</sup> sea grape,<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Sea_Grape-22)</sup> spruce,<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Spruce-23)</sup> and mangrove.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-Mangrove-24)</sup> Small plants that grow in the area include blue and purple wisteria,<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-AW1-25)</sup><sup>[[24]](#cite_note-AW2-26)</sup> yellow oleander,<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-YOleander-27)</sup> pearlwort,<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-Pearlwort-28)</sup> and glasswort.<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-Glasswort-29)</sup>

## Fauna
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/13/ON-creature-Gryphon.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Gryphon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Gryphon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Gryphon.jpg) A gryphon [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/ON-creature-Indrik.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Indrik.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Indrik.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Indrik.jpg) An indrik Summerset Isle was originally inhabited by species such as [Gheatus](Lore_Gheatus.md), [welwa](Lore_Welwa.md), and [Illyadi](Lore_Illyadi.md), which were exterminated by the [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) when they colonized the island (although the welwa were reintroduced into the island's ecosystem in the Second Era<sup>[[28]](#cite_note-RW-30)</sup>). The most iconic Summerset animals are [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md) and deer-like [indriks](Lore_Indrik.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOSummerset-7)</sup> Additionally, the [canah](Lore_Canah.md) is the exotic bird of Summerset Isle, bred for its beautiful feathers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGGerrick-1)</sup>

## Notable Places
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/LO-map-Summerset_Isle_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Summerset_Isle_%28Oblivion_Codex%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Summerset_Isle_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Summerset_Isle_(Oblivion_Codex).jpg) A map of the whole province
[Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md)
- The capital of the Summerset Isles. It is located on the western shore, along the Oleander Coast.
[Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md)
- A city perched atop Eton Nir, the tallest mountain.
[Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md)
- Also known as Crystal-Like-Law, this structure was of symbolic importance to the Altmer just as White-Gold was for the Ayleids.
[Dusk](Lore_Dusk.md)
- A city on the southeastern tip of the island.
[Eton Nir](Lore_Eton_Nir.md)
- The tallest mountain in the Summerset Isles, located near Cloudrest. It is the site of some of the Isles' most amazing architecture.
[Lillandril](Lore_Lillandril.md)
- A city that is home to the [College of Sapiarchs](Lore_College_of_Sapiarchs.md), located on the western shore.
[Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md)
- A city on the eastern shore, also known as the City of Lights.
[Sollicich-on-Ker](Lore_Sollicich-on-Ker.md)
- Birthplace of [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md).
[Sunhold](Lore_Sunhold.md)
- A city with the largest port on the Isle, located on the southern shore. The main road to Sunhold winds its way through the Eastern Pass.
