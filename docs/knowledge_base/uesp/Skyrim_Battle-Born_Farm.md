## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The farm consists of several fields containing ten [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), five [gourds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), and six bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md). Alfhild will buy any leeks, gourds, or wheat you have. There is a [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) in a pen and three [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) wandering freely. The farm has lots of equipment around the property including a [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md), which is south of the house, a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md), and a [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) that are either side of the cow pen. There is also a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) which is under a covered store with a table and chair next to it and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) on the table. There are two [chicken nests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) next to a walled field. There is a [quicksilver ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Quicksilver_Ore_Vein), two [corundum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) to the north and one to the northeast, across the road from the property. There is an [iron ore vein](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Ore_Vein) among some rocks to the south as well. Around the house are several [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels and large vats, with a few flowering plants at the back of the house and along the roadside.

### Battle-Born Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Battle-Born_Farm.jpg) Inside Battle-Born Farm The house is a single-room property with a stack of food barrels and sacks in the southwestern corner. Against the west wall is a narrow wooden table with a bowl of [red apples](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Red_Apple) on top. To the right, also against the west wall, is a bookcase holding eleven common books and a bunch of [tundra cotton](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tundra_Cotton). In the southeastern corner is a square wooden table and two chairs, with an alcove to the left, along the east wall. The alcove serves as a bedroom and contains a double bed in the southeastern corner with an end table to the left holding a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and a [sweet roll](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sweet_Roll) in a bowl. In the center of the room, toward the back, is a fire pit with a [cooking spit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooking_Spit) over it. In the northwestern corner of the room is a cupboard holding several pieces of crockery, two bottles of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a [sliced goat cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Goat_Cheese), a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), a [leathers strip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips), and a rack of [dog meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dog_Meat). To the right of this is a food barrel with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top. Further along the north wall are a couple of wall shelves that hold a small [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse), crockery, and a couple of food sacks. Against the east wall is a long wooden table and chairs, with a [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a bowl of [carrots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carrot), a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread), a bowl containing three [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg), and a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge). There is an [apple pie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apple_Pie) by the hearth and a narrow wooden table against the partition wall between the bedroom and cooking areas. On this table is a bowl containing loaves of bread and crockery. There are also four [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) and two bunches of [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md) hanging from a beam that supports the roof.

## Notes
- Though you can find and activate a [grain mill](Skyrim_Milling.md) here, it will only lead to an animation, with no actual function in the game.
- The guard outside the farm will not be replaced should the Stormcloaks take Whiterun.
- All containers in the area, other than some barrels and sacks, are non-respawning and safe to store items in.
- Even if you help Alfhild Battle-Born, everything in the farmhouse may still be considered owned.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Version 2.1.1 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) marks the sacks, barrels, cow, pichfork, and bottles of wine outside the building as owned by the farm.
