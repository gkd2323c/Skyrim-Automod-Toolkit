## Ingredients
59 guaranteed samples can be found in 33 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 6 in [Alftand Animonculory](Skyrim_Alftand_Animonculory.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 6 in [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 5 in [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))
- 3 in [Forelhost Refectory](Skyrim_Forelhost_Refectory.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 3 in [Guldun Rock Cave](Skyrim_Guldun_Rock_Cave.md) ([Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md))
- 3 in [Raldbthar Deep Market](Skyrim_Raldbthar_Deep_Market.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md). Other possible locations for already-harvested samples include:

- 14% chance of 1-3 samples being carried by [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) members (encountered during the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) questline).

## Notes
- Charred skeever hides are also categorized as [food](Skyrim_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- The base magnitude of the [Cure Disease](Skyrim_Cure_Disease.md) effect for this ingredient is 2 instead of 5. The only real implication of this reduced magnitude is that if charred skeever hide and [hawk feathers](Skyrim_Hawk_Feathers.md) are combined, the resulting potion will cost 64% less than a typical custom Cure Disease potion.
- If you have the Green Thumb perk from the Alchemy skill tree, each charred skeever you encounter in the world will give two charred skeever hides, not just the one you get without the perk.
