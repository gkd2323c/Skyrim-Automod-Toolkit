[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/ON-concept-Abah%27s_Landing.jpg/200px-ON-concept-Abah%27s_Landing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Abah%27s_Landing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Abah%27s_Landing.jpg) Abah's Landing circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Abah's Landing, the **Jewel of Khefrem**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Zabani-1)</sup> is a harbor city on the eastern coast of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md) in southern [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). It is a major port on the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

During the period of [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) invasions of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) known as the [Tavan Wave](Lore_Ra_Gada.md#The_Warrior_Waves), Prince [Hubalajad](Lore_Hubalajad.md) became the first to attempt to settle Hew's Bane (then known as Khefrem's Boot), seeing strategic value in its naturally protected harbor.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IDo PH-3)</sup> There, he established a small settlement. Originally little more than a soldier's camp, it would eventually grow into the walled city of Abah's Landing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It was named after [Ra Abah](Lore_Ra_Abah.md), a warrior who arrived there with Prince Hubalajad, who fought local Nedes to protect attacked noble, and was the first person to land.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TCOH-5)</sup>

By the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), royal power in Abah's Landing had been entirely supplanted by a group of criminal cartels known collectively as the "Merchant Lords". The hinterlands of Hew's Bane were populated primarily by smuggler crews and [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) pirates, with whom the Lords actively did business.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MFDL-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-v1-7)</sup> The city-state of Abah's Landing thus developed a deep rivalry with the kingdom of [Taneth](Lore_Taneth.md) to the north, whose traders usually bore the brunt of this thievery they harbored.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ESOTG-8)</sup>

A local criminal gang known as the [Thieves Guild](Lore_Thieves_Guild.md) was founded in Abah's Landing in the Second Era, and ultimately grew to become a Tamriel-wide organization.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Zeira-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup>
