## [Yaghra Larva](Lore_Yaghra.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/ON-pet-Larval_Yaghra.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Larval_Yaghra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Larval_Yaghra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Larval_Yaghra.jpg) A yaghra larva Yaghra Larva are [spider](Lore_Spider.md) -like larval Yaghra found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). They are usually found accompanying stronger Yaghra.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Yaghra_Larva)

## [Yaghra Monstrosity](Lore_Yaghra.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/ON-creature-Yaghra_Monstrosity.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Yaghra_Monstrosity.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Monstrosity.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Monstrosity.jpg) A yaghra monstrosity Yaghra Monstrosities are large, hostile, [spider](Lore_Spider.md) or [crab](Lore_Crab.md) -like sea creatures found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md). Monstrosities are large Yaghra that are similar in body structure and shape to their larvae, but have hard exoskeletons, crab-like claws, and carry large shells on their backs like [hermit crabs](Lore_Hermit_Crab.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> Monstrosities are steadfast commanders. They produce Larval Yaghra, and overwhelm the battlefield through rapid reproduction. They have an almost unnatural yet commanding bond with their lesser brethren.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOSCESG-2)</sup><sup>:220</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Yaghra_Monstrosity)

## [Yaghra Spewer](Lore_Yaghra.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/ON-creature-Yaghra_Spewer.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Yaghra_Spewer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Spewer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Spewer.jpg) A Yaghra Spewer Yaghra Spewers are hostile sea creature found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). The Yaghra Spewer has bioluminescent shells and possess poison sacks in their necks, which they use to spew a corrosive substance. It is this ability for which they are named.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSpewer-3)</sup> Spewers share a similar social standing with Striders, but exhibit a more conservative ranged combat style. They appear to call out targets for Striders to focus on, and are intent on creating dangerous areas on the battlefield for their enemies to avoid.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOSCESG-2)</sup><sup>:220</sup> They do this by spewing poison, which pools on the ground and is harmful to stand in.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOSpewer-3)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Yaghra_Spewer)

## [Yaghra Strider](Lore_Yaghra.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/ON-creature-Yaghra_Strider.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Yaghra_Strider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Strider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Yaghra_Strider.jpg) A yaghra strider Yaghra Striders are hostile sea creature found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) and [Artaeum](Lore_Artaeum.md). The Yaghra Strider has a spined exoskeleton and attacks with [crab](Lore_Crab.md) -like claws.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> Striders are extremely aggressive. They show an impressive amount of loyalty to the greater Monstrosities, whom they view as their leaders. They also appear to revere the [Sea Sloads](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) as their gods. Striders act as soldiers, defending Yaghra nests.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TESOSCESG-2)</sup><sup>:219</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Yaghra_Strider)
