[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/ON-place-Seaveil_Spire.jpg/200px-ON-place-Seaveil_Spire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seaveil_Spire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seaveil_Spire.jpg) Seaveil Spire circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Seaveil Spire is an abandoned [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) sanctuary dedicated to [worship](Lore_Cult_of_the_Stars.md) of [the Tower](Lore_The_Tower.md). It is located within the lagoon of an atoll somewhere off the coast of [Betony](Lore_Betony.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SSB-1)</sup>

The islet's lagoon is noted for a strange underwater anomaly: a vast air pocket beneath the water's surface, maintained by some unknown force throughout the millennia. In the early years of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md), a Nedic tribe devoted to worship of the Tower discovered the undersea anomaly and settled the atoll. They built a temple inside the anomaly, under the water's surface. At some point, the site was abandoned by its builders. Millennia later, the edifice and anomaly remained, providing a secure and wondrous escape from the surface world.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ruins were available for purchase as a private residence.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
