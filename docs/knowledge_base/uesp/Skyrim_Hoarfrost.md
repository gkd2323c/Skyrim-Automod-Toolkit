[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4a/SR-item-Hoarfrost.jpg/200px-SR-item-Hoarfrost.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hoarfrost.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Hoarfrost.jpg) Hoarfrost Hoarfrost is an [ancient Nordic pickaxe](Skyrim_Ancient_Nordic_Pickaxe.md) carried by [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md), who can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md). It is in all ways identical to a normal ancient Nordic pickaxe, and can be used to mine [Stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md) in place of one. Its enchantment deals 15 points of [frost damage](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), [slows](Skyrim_Slow.md) the target by fifty percent for three seconds, and has a 2% chance to [freeze](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) the target for 5 seconds. Hoarfrost can be obtained by either killing Ralis at the end of the related quest, or by sparing his life, hiring him as a follower, and then taking the pickaxe from his inventory. It can also be obtained by pickpocketing.

## Related Quests
- **[Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md)**: Assist [Ralis Sedarys](Skyrim_Ralis_Sedarys.md) in his search for the relics of [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md) in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md).

## Notes
- It is possible to get two Hoarfrosts by making Ralis Sedarys your follower, taking Hoarfrost, and sacrificing him at the Shrine of Boethiah. Another copy of Hoarfrost can be found when looting his corpse.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- Hoarfrost has no tempering recipes and cannot be improved at grindstones. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.4, addresses this issue. It can be improved with an [iron ingot](Skyrim_Iron_Ingot.md).
- Hoarfrost is missing the Vendor Item Tool keyword. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
