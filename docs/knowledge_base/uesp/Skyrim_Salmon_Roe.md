[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-ingredient-Salmon_Roe.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Salmon_Roe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Salmon_Roe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Salmon_Roe.jpg) Salmon Roe **Salmon Roe** is an ingredient that can be harvested from *jumping* [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md) in streams (non-jumping and dead salmon do not carry any). Some swimming salmon can be killed (not caught) to obtain the ingredient. Salmon killed with an [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) shout or a bow will also yield Salmon Roe. Three salmon can be killed at once by the weakest version of the shout.

Salmon Roe's [Fortify Magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md) duration is only 5 seconds, while the [Waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md) effect is 60 seconds. This makes it unique, as Fortify Magicka is normally 60 seconds and Waterbreathing 5 seconds. Furthermore, the Waterbreathing effect is valued at 15.4 times higher than it is for other alchemical ingredients. Salmon Roe can therefore be used to create the most valuable potions in the game that do not use ingredients from the Rare Curios creation. The most expensive is:

- [Garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) + [Nordic Barnacle](Skyrim_Nordic_Barnacle.md) + Salmon Roe

Followed by:

- [Chicken's Egg](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) + [Nightshade](Skyrim_Nightshade.md) + Salmon Roe

And:

- [Histcarp](Skyrim_Histcarp.md) + [Jazbay Grapes](Skyrim_Jazbay_Grapes.md) + Salmon Roe

## Ingredients
1 guaranteed sample can be found in up to 3 locations.

- 1 in each [kitchen](Skyrim_Kitchen.md) wing (if constructed)

## Sources
Salmon can be found in almost every flowing body of water. Locations with guaranteed jumping salmon are:

- 3 at the [Windstad Manor fish hatchery](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md#Fish_Hatchery)

Two unique dead salmon yield roe when harvested. Their location is:

- 2 at [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> by the waterfalls

Salmon that swim in shallow water (areas that are marked as pond water), such as the area near the [East Empire Company Warehouse](Skyrim_East_Empire_Company_Warehouse.md) near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), under the bridge leading into Windhelm, or the [fish hatchery](Skyrim_Fish_Hatchery.md) at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md), will yield salmon roe when "killed". They will not yield roe if they are simply harvested. Other areas of water that qualify are the area around the sunken ship in Lake Ilinalta and the harbor of [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

## Notes
- Salmon roe has the longest waterbreathing duration of any ingredient in the game, at a full 60 seconds.
- Salmon roe will make a water-like sound when you pick it up, or store/take it out of a container.
- Even at the base value of the Alchemy skill (15) with no Fortify Alchemy effects, a crafted potion of waterbreathing with salmon roe will last 360 seconds. If you have a high level of Alchemy and wear overpowered Fortify Alchemy apparel (possible with the [Fortify Restoration](Skyrim_Fortify_Restoration.md) glitch), these values can skyrocket to 3,000 or more seconds of waterbreathing time.
- Possible ways of harvesting salmon roe include: - Shooting fish with a bow/crossbow. Type of arrows/bolts used doesn't matter, as they will die from an iron arrow shot from a non-upgraded long bow versus a dragonbone arrow shot from a Legendary quality dragonbone bow. Dead fish will float in the water and be able to be picked up (even though no prompt appears), yielding one salmon meat and one salmon roe. In order to be able to reach some groups of fish further from shore, you can crouch (enter Sneak Mode) and enter the water, but just before it reaches the depths where you begin to swim. Fish can be viewed and shot at underwater this way. Note that any arrows that strike and kill the fish cannot be recovered (they are not added to your inventory when the fish are taken).
- Using the [Drain Vitality](Skyrim_Drain_Vitality.md), [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md), [Animal Allegiance](Skyrim_Animal_Allegiance.md), [Cyclone](Skyrim_Cyclone.md) or [Clear Skies](Skyrim_Clear_Skies.md) shout at a group of fish. Some deeper water groups of fish might only take damage from certain angles, so field testing may be necessary. Hitting NPCs with either of the first two Shouts mentioned can cause them to become hostile, so be careful when around non-followers.
- The [Ice Storm](Skyrim_Ice_Storm.md) [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell kills fish in water.
- If the water is really shallow (knee deep), melee attacks (but not unarmed ones) and bow bashes will kill fish, allowing them to be picked up.
- Version 2.0.3 of the [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch) reverses the duration for salmon roe's fortify magicka and waterbreathing effects. It also reduces the high magnitude of the fortify magicka effect.
