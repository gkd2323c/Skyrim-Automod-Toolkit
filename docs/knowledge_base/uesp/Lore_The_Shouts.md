# The Shouts

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/TR-place-Mournhold_Temple.jpg/200px-TR-place-Mournhold_Temple.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Mournhold_Temple.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-place-Mournhold_Temple.jpg) Mournhold Temple (Morrowind) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bb/TR-creature-Almalexia_04.jpg/200px-TR-creature-Almalexia_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-creature-Almalexia_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:TR-creature-Almalexia_04.jpg) Lady Almalexia (Morrowind) This article is about the Chimeri guild. For the Dragon Shouts, see [Thu'um](Lore_Thu%27um.md).

The Shouts were a [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) guild of counselors and advisors to [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) and the realm during the times of [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

They operated within the capital of the Chimer, [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), and carried the wishes of the people, as the Velothi were predominantly virtuous at that time. Their interactions occasionally led to disputes, requiring [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md)'s intervention and guidance to make them effective.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

They are featured in the *[36 Lessons of Vivec, Sermon 8](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_8.md)*, where they observe a dialogue between [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), Sotha Sil, and Almalexia, "to which the Shouts were silent in sudden reading." As of the time when Sermon 8 was written, they were already regarded as ancient and forgotten.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

The [Hands of Almalexia](Lore_Hands_of_Almalexia.md) were another institution dedicated to Almalexia, notably for her protection, although they were active after her ascension to godhood.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TR-2)</sup>
