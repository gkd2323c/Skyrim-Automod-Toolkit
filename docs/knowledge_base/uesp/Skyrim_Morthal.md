[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-place-Morthal.jpg/200px-SR-place-Morthal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Morthal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Morthal.jpg) Morthal **Morthal** is a city in the north of Skyrim, and the capital of [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md). It is initially aligned with the [Empire](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md), but [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Idgrod Ravencrone](Skyrim_Idgrod_Ravencrone.md) seems uninterested in the conflict, claiming to sense bigger things afoot in the world. Her reclusive nature and mysticism has fed a general perception that she is an ineffective ruler. Many residents are concerned about the war, which brings unwelcome outsiders, and would prefer to continue to live in isolation. The city itself is a fairly small, humble settlement of little economic or strategic importance besides its small lumber mill. Only the mill, an [apothecary](Skyrim_Thaumaturgist%27s_Hut.md), and an [inn](Skyrim_Moorside_Inn.md) support its weak economy. Sitting on the southern edge of the Drajkmyr marsh, Morthal is often shrouded in a thick fog. The ominous twisted trees and torches glowing in the fog like wisps present a foreboding appearance to travelers. The swampy surrounding lands are rich in [deathbells](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbell), [swamp fungal pods](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Swamp_Fungal_Pod), and [giant lichen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Lichen). Leveled creatures ([trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md) and rarely [chaurus](Skyrim_Chaurus.md) in open space) spawn in the area at night.

For more information on Morthal, see the [lore article](Lore_Morthal.md).

## Notes
- With *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* installed, you can purchase [a plot of land](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) in Hjaalmarch to build a house on. Also, two [stone quarries](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md) will be present.
- It's possible that Hroggar's house once had an interior cell, or was at least intended to have one. Unused location data for a "Burned House" with the editor ID of `Morthal Burned House Location` exists, but no other corroborating leftovers.
- Just outside town to the southeast is a Nordic barrow guarded by a leveled [skeleton](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). Inside, a copy of the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Black Arts On Trial](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arts_On_Trial)* rests atop a shelf. To the left, the body of a novice [conjurer](Skyrim_Conjurer.md) with a dagger in his chest can be found in front of an open tomb, next to a table with a [Scroll of Turn Lesser Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_of_Turn_Lesser_Undead) on top of it, and a [Staff of Revenants](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Staff_of_Revenants#Staff_of_Revenants). [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-29832&y=60737&zoom=8&legacy=true)
- Also outside town to the northwest, another Nordic barrow contains a novice-locked chest with a copy of the [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Locked Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Locked_Room)* beside it. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-48095&y=76564&zoom=8&legacy=true)
- Despite going unnamed in the final game as it has no interiors or map markers, the *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V__Skyrim__Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* refers to the local mill as **Marshdeep Camp**.
- Music doesn't play outdoors when you're here, except during special events like dragon fights.
- Morthal also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Morthal) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).
