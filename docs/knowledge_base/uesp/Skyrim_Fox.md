## Notes
- With the *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md), your [adopted children](Skyrim_Adoption.md) can bring home either variant as [pets](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pets).
- As a pet, the fox may have unpleasantly obtrusive sound effects for its breathing.

## Bugs
- Both variations of foxes fail to respawn due to missing respawn flags in the game data. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
