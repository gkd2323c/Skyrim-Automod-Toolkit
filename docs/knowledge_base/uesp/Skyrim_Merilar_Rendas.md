[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Merilar_Rendas.jpg) Merilar Rendas **Merilar Rendas** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [sorceress](Skyrim_CombatSorcerer.md) living in [White Ridge Barrow](Skyrim_White_Ridge_Barrow.md).

She and her brother [Servos Rendas](Skyrim_Servos_Rendas.md) discovered how receptive Solstheim's [albino spider](Skyrim_Albino_Spider.md) is to magic when exposed to [rubies](Skyrim_Ruby.md). Merilar was driven insane by the power and by the strange fumes emitted by their experiments, and locked her brother in a cage to die. She is hostile and will throw [jumping flame spiders](Skyrim_Jumping_Flame_Spider_(item).md) at you.

Merilar wears [novice robes of alteration](Skyrim_Novice_Robes_of_Alteration.md) along with a [novice hood](Skyrim_Novice_Hood.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c). In addition to her jumping flame spiders, she carries [her journal](Skyrim_Merilar%27s_Journal.md), a [glass dagger](Skyrim_Glass_Dagger.md) and a [key](Skyrim_Merilar%27s_Cage_Door_Key.md).
