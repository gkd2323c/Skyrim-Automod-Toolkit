## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Harrald](Skyrim_Harrald.md) in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md).
2. Retrieve his blade from [Balimund](Skyrim_Balimund.md).
3. Return to Harrald.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Slain Wall
Harrald is the son of Jarl [Laila Law-Giver](Skyrim_Laila_Law-Giver.md) and lives in [Mistveil Keep](Skyrim_Mistveil_Keep.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). Take a look at him and you may find something strange. As the son of the jarl, he has only a dagger. If you ask him, he will tell you why: *"I was practicing in the yard when the guard I was sparring with feinted when I specifically told him to duck! My blade bit right into the stonework next to the keep and needed serious repairs."* He'd like you to fetch his blade from [Balimund](Skyrim_Balimund.md).

Talk to Balimund right outside [The Scorched Hammer](Skyrim_The_Scorched_Hammer.md), "a stone's throw" from the keep. He will give the sword to you and comment on Harrald's laziness and carelessness.

Hand over the sword to Harrald and he will be rather ungrateful. *"Took your sweet time getting it to me. Here you go, for your trouble."* He will then reward you with two [leveled](Skyrim_Gems.md#Valuable_Gems) gems.

## Notes
- Although this is not a [favor quest](Skyrim_Favors.md), it does count towards [becoming thane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:I_Done_Got_Thaned!).
