# Dragonskin (perk)


**Description**: *Negate 50% of incoming damage, except from the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md).*
**Editor ID**: `cr Dragon Resist NPCs`
**Form ID**: `001046bd`

**Dragonskin** is a perk that all members of the Dragon Race possess, which includes all [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) found in the game. It negates 50% of incoming damage from all creatures and NPCs,<sup>[*verification needed — Do dragons take half damage from damage sources? e.g. Traps.*]</sup> but not from you.
