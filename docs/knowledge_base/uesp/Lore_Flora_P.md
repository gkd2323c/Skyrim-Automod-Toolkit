## Palm Tree
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/ON-furnishing-Palm_Tree_Cluster.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Palm_Tree_Cluster.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Palm_Tree_Cluster.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Palm_Tree_Cluster.jpg) Palm Trees **Palm Trees** are a type of tree that grows in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s warmer climates. Their leaves are used in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md) to create a levitation potion.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFPalm-1)</sup> They grow all over the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md) region in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and variants like the [Cave Palm](Lore_Cave_Palm.md) are found exclusively in the region.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONAlik.27r-2)</sup> Their fruit can be used as an ingredient for a healing potion.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONMorwha-3)</sup> They can also be found in other adjacent places like [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-RGStros-4)</sup>

Parts of the Old Imperial Quarter in the [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) city of [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) are said to be overgrown with sleeping palm trees and [bamboo](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Bamboo).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TIC-5)</sup><sup>:17</sup> Palm oil is turned into a balm and used by [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) to moisturize their spines.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONTreasures Palm-6)</sup> Palm trees also grow on the island of [Stirk](Lore_Stirk.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONStirk-7)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Hammerfell), [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Witch_Covens#The_Tide_Witches), [The Infernal City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Greg_Keyes_Novels/Plants), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Conservatory_Furnishings/Trees)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Palm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Palm)

## [Passion Dancer Blossom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Passion_Dancer_Blossom)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg/200px-ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-pet-Passion_Dancer_Blossom.jpg) Passion Dancer Blossom [Passion Dancer Blossom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Passion_Dancer_Blossom), also known as **Ardor's Flowering** is a floating teal-colored flower associated with [Dibella](Lore_Dibella.md), known to thrive in the presence of her passionate followers. Some believe that if one surrenders to Dibella's passion and dances in her chosen way, she may reward them with the gift of one of her vibrant blooms. These flowers were among the many otherworldly items in the stock of [the Impresario](Lore_The_Impresario.md), an eccentric traveling merchant. To conjure one, it was necessary to use the softly glowing petals of a mystical Chartreuse lily, an enchanted silver flute to summon benevolent [spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md), and the music of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md), transcribed in a form comprehensible to mortal minds.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Passion_Dancer_Blossom)

## [Plankton](Lore_Plankton.md)
[Plankton](Lore_Plankton.md) are tiny organisms that float in water, classified as either [animals](Lore_Bestiary.md) or [plants](Lore_Flora.md).

[Septimus Signus](Lore_Septimus_Signus.md) referenced plankton metaphorically in his *[Ruminations on the Elder Scrolls](Lore_Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)*. He sought to illustrate one of the origins of the [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) by imagining a life beneath the waves, endowed with sharp vision and surrounded by beautiful fabric. By placing the fabric over one's gills, one would begin to breathe in and absorb its threads. While the plant fibers nourish the [soul](Lore_Souls.md), "the wretched plankton would pollute the cloth until it stank to heavens of [prophecy](Lore_Sight.md)". He posed the question of whether one is the sea, the breather, or the fabric, or if one is the breath itself.

**Found in:** - [Lore](Lore_Ruminations_on_the_Elder_Scrolls.md)

## Pomegranate
**Pomegranates** are a type of fruit that grows in shrubs. It has the most cultural influence in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and can be found across the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md). In [Satakalaam](Lore_Satakalaam.md), where [bees](Lore_Bee.md) pollinate them,<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-BBS-11)</sup> and [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md), where pomegranates, [figs](Lore_Fig.md), and [olives](Lore_Olive.md) grow across the green shoreline. In honor of the city's founder, Grandee [Yaghoub](Lore_Yaghoub.md), the [Redguards](Lore_Redguard.md) of the [kingdom](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md) celebrate [Koomu Alezer'i](Lore_Koomu_Alezer%27i.md) by eating pomegranates.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-STJOA-12)</sup> Pomegranate wine is the most popular type of wine in the Alik'r Desert<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONTreasures P-13)</sup> and it is an ingredient in [seared venomsteaks](Lore_Redguard_Cuisine.md#Seared_Venomsteaks), a dish native to the desert region.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-RFDD-14)</sup>

In [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md) mythology, [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) approached [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) in the ash wastes of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and forced a wedding ceremony known as the Pomegranate Banquet. The Daedra Lord summoned his minions and created a banquet with pomegranates strung across the badlands and tents raised.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-36LOVS12-15)</sup>
