**Elemental Flare** is an effect that deals magic (see [Bugs](#Bugs)) and [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) to the target. Each version of the effect consists of a magic blast that deals **<mag>** damage in 15 feet, in addition to [tapering damage](Skyrim_Magic_Overview.md#Tapering_Durations) for 2 seconds (approximately 20% of the base damage). The initial blast is then followed by a 15 foot shock explosion which deals varying amounts of damage depending on the spell. This shock explosion then creates a shock hazard which deals 15 points of shock damage over 1 second.

## Notes
- The shock damage's strength is not affected by [dual-casting](Skyrim_Dual-Casting.md).

## Bugs
- Despite the spell descriptions claiming that Elemental Flare deals fire damage, the blast is not affected by [Resist Fire](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md).
