[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-item-Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.jpg/200px-SR-item-Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Battleaxe_of_Fiery_Souls.jpg) The Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/SR-interior-Ironbind_Barrow_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Ironbind_Barrow_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ironbind_Barrow_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Ironbind_Barrow_03.jpg) The throne in Ironbind Barrow The Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls is a unique [steel battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Battleaxe) located on the back of [Warlord Gathrik](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Warlord_Gathrik)'s throne in [Ironbind Barrow](Skyrim_Ironbind_Barrow.md). Its [unique enchantment](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fiery_Soul_Trap) does ten points of [fire damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fire_Damage) over one second, and [soul traps](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md) the target for five seconds. [Tempering](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) this weapon requires a [steel ingot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Ingot) as well as the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk. Improvements to the weapon are doubled if the [Steel Smithing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Smithing) perk is also unlocked.

Like other weapons with fire enchantments, this battleaxe benefits from the [Augmented Flames](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Flames) perk and will inflict 15 points of fire damage at the 2nd level.

## Notes
- This is the only weapon in the game that grants you the [Fiery Soul Trap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fiery_Soul_Trap) effect when disenchanted.
- Since Fiery Soul Trap's [effect charge and strength](Skyrim_Enchanting_Effects.md) are keyed to the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) skill instead of [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill, Fiery Soul Trap can be used to enchant a weapon with a long-lasting 10pt Fire Damage effect irrespective of Destruction skill level.
- If Warlord Gathrik uses [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md) on [Salma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salma), that may cause her to pick up the Battleaxe. If she does, you'll either have to pickpocket the weapon from her, or kill her and loot it from her corpse.

## Bugs
- Sometimes, the Steel Battleaxe of Fiery Souls will be hovering in the air. This occurs when you are of a high enough level for Warlord Gathrik to be a [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md). When he gets off the throne, it will be thrown into space. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- Shooting the axe with an arrow or spell should cause it to fall to the ground.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Using the console command `player.additem 40002 1` will place a copy of the battleaxe in your inventory.
