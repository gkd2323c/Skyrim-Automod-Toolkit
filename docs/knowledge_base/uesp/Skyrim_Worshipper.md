[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2f/SR-npc-Worshipper_Nord_Male.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Worshipper_Nord_Male.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Worshipper_Nord_Male.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Worshipper_Nord_Male.jpg) A Nord man murdered for worshipping Talos Four **Worshippers** can be found dead at the Shrine of Talos to the southwest of [the Guardian Stones](Skyrim_The_Guardian_Stones.md).

There are two dead male [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmers](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md). Both wear [hooded monk robes](Skyrim_Hooded_Monk_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000bacd7), and they are both equipped with a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md) and a [small amount of gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Loot Gold Change25).
