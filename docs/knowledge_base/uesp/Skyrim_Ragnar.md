[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2c/SR-npc-Ragnar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ragnar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ragnar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ragnar.jpg) Ragnar **Ragnar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives and works in [Karthwasten](Skyrim_Karthwasten.md). He is [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md)'s right-hand man, claiming that his employer is "a good man".

He sleeps in [Karthwasten Miner's Barracks](Skyrim_Karthwasten_Miner%27s_Barracks.md) from midnight to 8am, and works in [Sanuarach Mine](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine.md) between 8am and 8pm. He spends the rest of the evening relaxing in the barracks. The only exception to the schedule is during your first visit to Karthwasten, when he and the other miners will be seen [arguing](Skyrim_Sanuarach_Mine_(quest).md) with [Atar](Skyrim_Atar.md) and his fellow [mercenaries](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Mercenary.md).

Ragnar wears a set of [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Miner%27s_Clothes.md), a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1), and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). He is equipped with a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), and carries a [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Karthwasten_Miners%27_Barracks.md) to the miner's barracks, as well as a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
If Ainethach is alive:

- "*I'm [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md)'s right-hand man. I help supervise the mines."*
- *"Not many Nords would work for a Reachman, but Ainethach is a good man."*

If you have not resolved the situation, he also will comment on the mine ownership dispute:

- *"[Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) attacks are bad enough, but those sellswords are worse."*
- *"You should talk to Ainethach if you have business with the mine. Not that there's much these days."*

If Ainethach is killed:

- *"Ainethach is dead. What will happen to Karthwasten now?"*

## Conversations
Ragnar will sometimes talk with his fellow miner, [Lash gra-Dushnikh](Skyrim_Lash_gra-Dushnikh.md), but the following conversations will only be heard if you have not resolved the mine dispute in favor of Ainethach:

- **Lash**: *"I've been thinking, would you like to join me in hunting?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"You want me to hunt with you? I thought you preferred being alone."*
- **Lash**: *"Your arms and legs are fit for catching strong, agile prey. I could use your help."*
- **Ragnar**: *"I'd be honored, but I have work to do in the mines."*

- **Lash**: *"When will Sanuarach Mine be open?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"I don't know. Whenever those Silver-Blood men decide to leave."*

If you have sided with Ainethach, Ragnar and Lash will have these conversations instead:

- **Ragnar**: *"I haven't seen you hunting in a while."*
- **Lash**: *"I can afford to buy meat now. I prefer working in the mines, anyway."*

- **Lash**: *"Tell me, Ragnar, what do you think of the Orcs?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"Cunning warriors and skilled workers. It's good to have you here, Lash."*
- **Lash**: *"I asked what you thought of the Orcs, not about me."*
- **Ragnar**: *"Oh. I'm sorry. Well, it's good to have you here, anyway."*

- **Lash**: *"You have strong arms. You'd make a good Orc."*
- **Ragnar**: *"Um... thank you, Lash. You'd... make a fine Nord?"*
- **Lash**: *"Don't say words you don't mean."*

At other times Ragnar will talk with [Belchimac](Skyrim_Belchimac.md), a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) miner:

- **Belchimac**: *"Damn Nords. Always getting in the way of us honest folk."*
- **Ragnar**: *"What did you say? You lazy coward, I'll cut your throat."*
- **Belchimac**: *"Nothing, Ragnar. Please... don't hurt me."*
- **Ragnar**: *"I... I'm sorry, Belchimac. I didn't mean that."*

- **Ragnar**: *"You're always talking about the Forsworn. You aren't thinking of joining them, are you?"*
- **Belchimac**: *"Maybe I will. What do you think about that?"*
- **Ragnar**: *"I'm warning you. Those people are killers. You'd never last a day with them."*
