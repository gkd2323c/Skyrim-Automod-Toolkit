[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-creature-Mikrul_Gauldurson.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Mikrul_Gauldurson.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mikrul_Gauldurson.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Mikrul_Gauldurson.jpg) Mikrul during [Forbidden Legend](Skyrim_Forbidden_Legend.md) **Mikrul Gauldurson** is the youngest of the three brothers encountered during the [Forbidden Legend](Skyrim_Forbidden_Legend.md) quest. Together with his brothers, [Jyrik](Skyrim_Jyrik_Gauldurson.md) and [Sigdis](Skyrim_Sigdis_Gauldurson.md), they conspired to murder their [father](Skyrim_Gauldur.md) in his sleep, then divide his [amulet](Skyrim_The_Gauldur_Amulet.md) between them. Consumed by power, he and his brothers laid waste to the surrounding villages until the [High King](Lore_Harald.md) sent [Archmage Geirmund](Lore_Geirmund.md) leading a company of battlemages to stop them. Mikrul was run to the ground in [Folgunthur](Skyrim_Folgunthur.md), the ancient barrows at the foot of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). After fighting for three days and nights, Mikrul was finally defeated. Mikrul's tomb was sealed with the [Ivory Dragon Claw](Skyrim_Ivory_Dragon_Claw.md). Mikrul is equipped with the [Gauldur Blackblade](Skyrim_Gauldur_Blackblade.md), a leveled one handed sword with an [Absorb Health](Skyrim_Absorb_Health.md) enchantment on it, as well as the spells [Frost Cloak](Skyrim_Frost_Cloak.md) and [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md).
