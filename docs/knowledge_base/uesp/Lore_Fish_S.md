## Salmon
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-creature-Salmon.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Salmon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Salmon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Salmon.jpg) A leaping Salmon The **Salmon** is a gray type of fish that can commonly be found in bodies of water around [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They are often seen jumping to ascend waterfalls and rapids. While the other species of fish tend to swim near the surface of bodies of water, salmon may often be seen either near the surface or in the depths. Its pinkish-orange flesh is a popular food source, and can be eaten raw or salted and cooked.

- The **Crag Salmon** can be found in the lakes of [Craglorn](Lore_Craglorn.md).
- The **Ghost Salmon** can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), near [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) and [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md).
- The **Jarl Salmon** can be found in the lakes of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).
- The **Midget Salmon** can be found in the foul waters of the [Alik'r Desert](Lore_Alik%27r_Desert.md).
- The **Sockeye Salmon** can be found in the rivers of [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Salmon_(creature).md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Common_Fish)

## Shark
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/83/ON-misc-Render_of_the_Loch.jpg/200px-ON-misc-Render_of_the_Loch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Render_of_the_Loch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-misc-Render_of_the_Loch.jpg) A Shark **Sharks** are a type of fish. They are known to pose a danger to those who swim in deep waters.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

- The **Bala Shark** can be found in the lakes of [Hew's Bane](Lore_Hew%27s_Bane.md), while the **Beggar Shark** can be found off its coast in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md).

- The **Howling Shark** can be found in the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md) and Sea of Ghosts, off the coasts of the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md).
- The **Magrove Shark** can be found in the [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md), off the coast of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md).
- **Pad-sa Sharks** are scrappy survivors that inhabit fresh and salty waters, usually found from the mouth of the [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md) to the [Topal Bay](Lore_Topal_Bay.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>
- The **Sleeper Shark** can be found in the sunny waters of the Abecean Sea, off the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).
- The **Skyrim Gurry Shark** can be found in the Sea of Ghosts off the coast of [Haafingar](Lore_Haafingar.md) and [Hjaalmarch](Lore_Hjaalmarch.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESO-6)</sup>
- The **Weeping Pygmy Shark** can be found in the Inner Sea, off the coasts of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).

## Sheartooth
**Shearteeth** are a type of fish found in the [Sump](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Sump) on [Umbriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Infernal_City/Umbriel). They are described as having a "maw full of teeth". They can reach at least fifteen feet long and have long whip-like tails, dual swimming fins on the underside, teeth that "would shame a shark", and a tough hide. They were usually non-aggressive to Skraws but would attack if the sump considered someone an intruder.

**Found in:** - [The Infernal City](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Infernal_City/Creatures#Sheartooth)

## [Slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md)
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Slaughterfish.jpg) A Slaughterfish [Slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) are large, aggressive fish with razor-sharp teeth found in the waters of nearly all of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and even in other realms such as [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). They are often found in large groups that will swarm unwary prey quickly and lethally, some specimens have been known to grow at least twice as large than average due to plentiful meals.

Slaughterfish tend to prefer foul waters but can thrive in a variety of aquatic environments across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), such as deep inland lakes and underground rivers. They have even been sighted in the heavily polluted, oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md), where most other fish species had been rendered near-extinct.

- Variations: Rumare Slaughterfish, Small Slaughterfish, Giant Slaughterfish, Crab-Slaughter-Crane, Blind Slaughterfish, Electric Slaughterfish

**Found in:** - [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Slaughterfish), [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Slaughterfish), [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Slaughterfish), [Skyrim](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Slaughterfish), [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Slaughterfish)
