## History
The exact history of Bleakwind Bluff is largely unknown. It was constructed on the borderlands between the territories of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) and the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyim-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup> As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the tower was already constructed, but its purpose, allegiances, and role were unclear.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the structure had fallen into a state of ruin and decay. It acted as an outpost for the [Forsworn](Lore_Forsworn.md), an extremist faction among the Reachfolk. It was inhabited by both the Forsworn members and their matrons, the [Hagravens](Lore_Hagraven.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyim-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PRIMA-3)</sup>
