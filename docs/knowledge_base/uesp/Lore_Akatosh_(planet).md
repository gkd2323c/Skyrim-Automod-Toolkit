# Akatosh (planet)

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/OB-interior-Orrery_%28labeled%29.jpg/200px-OB-interior-Orrery_%28labeled%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Orrery_(labeled).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-interior-Orrery_(labeled).jpg) The names of the heavenly bodies around [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/OBbsign_Warrior.jpg/200px-OBbsign_Warrior.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBbsign_Warrior.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBbsign_Warrior.jpg) The Warrior Constellation The planet Akatosh, sometimes called **AKHAT**,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-36LOVS3-1)</sup> is one of the Dominion Planets found in the skies of [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Cosmo-2)</sup> According to the Warrior-Poet, [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md), it is one of the eight worlds known to the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-36LOVS3-1)</sup> There are no known satellites orbiting around it,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBOrrery-3)</sup> but it marks the eye of the [Warrior](Lore_The_Warrior_(constellation).md) [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md). As with all astral bodies seen in the skies above [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) and their corresponding deities, the planet Akatosh is believed to be the plane of the god Akatosh as well as the god himself, as seen from the mortal plane. It is said to actually be a different [plane of existence](Lore_Planes_of_Existence.md) in its own right, infinite in size and mass, with its appearance as a sphere being only a visual phenomenon caused by mortal mental stress.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Cosmo-2)</sup> [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md) is said to have ruled from here before [Convention](Lore_Convention.md) took place in [ME 2500](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_2500).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BAM-4)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

According to some accounts, the mortal realms were formed by the Aedra out of [Creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Co S-6)</sup> Though scholars largely agree no mortal beings live on Akatosh or [Arkay](Lore_Arkay_(planet).md), [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) theorized that the power [Ayleid Wells](Lore_Ayleid_Wells.md) reroute back towards the heavens was being collected by someone.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Azandar Dialogue-7)</sup> In [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)'s [33rd sermon](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_33.md), he calls Akatosh the **north-pole-star** of warriors, referencing its position on the Warrior constellation.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-36LOVS33-8)</sup> It also describes Vivec's account of [Baar Dau](Lore_Baar_Dau.md)'s fall. In his telling, he sends [Nerevar](Lore_Nerevar.md) to the Heavens and to destroy the meteor. There, he made peace with the north-pole-star and other pole-stars, allowing him to wander among their charges and giving him red sight to find Baar Dau in the Hidden Heaven.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-36LOVS33-8)</sup>

The [Spilled Sand](Lore_Spilled_Sand.md) is an obscure realm of existence associated with [Alkosh](Lore_Alkosh.md).It consists of an endless expanse of sand interspersed with an oasis of golden trees, and a colossal slumbering golden dragon with scales of pure gold. The twin moons, Jode and Jone, can be seen in the sky alongside a series of stars. The realm is described as not a place but rather a "story that has and will be told time and time again" and "myth made manifest", it is said to exist at once within and outside the tapestry of time, and beyond even the Sands Behind the Stars.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Nahfahlaar-9)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Ja.27darri-10)</sup> When questioned on whether the Spilled Sand is the realm of Alkosh itself, Ja'darri would not outright confirm it, answering instead that Alkosh is both weaver and embodiment of time's tapestry and all always exist within time's realm regardless of where they are.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Ja.27darri-10)</sup> The Mask of Alkosh allows for one to journey to this realm when awoken via the blood of a dragon.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Dragonguard-11)</sup>

The "myriad kingdoms of Akha along the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md)", the realities beyond and surrounding Aurbis are thought to be ruled over and safeguarded by Alkosh, who inherited the crown of Akha.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-TWS-12)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-Leramil-13)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-Ithelia-14)</sup> At least once, the visions in [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md) depicted light transforming into the letters AKHAT.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TN-15)</sup>
