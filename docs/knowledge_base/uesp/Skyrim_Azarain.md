[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/SR-npc-Azarain.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Azarain.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azarain.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Azarain.jpg) Azarain **Azarain** is a [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) who has come to [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) to investigate disappearances of his fellow Vigilants in the region. He was staying at the [Windpeak Inn](Skyrim_Windpeak_Inn.md) but during the related quest you will find his corpse at the [Windward Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Windward_Ruins) outside of town, having been murdered by a Daedric cult.

You will need to read his [journal](Skyrim_Azarain%27s_Journal.md) to progress the quest, which can be found on his body in addition to a full set of [Vigil Veteran armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_Veteran_Armor), a random leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace) up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality, two random leveled [potions of restore health](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [potion of cure disease](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Cure_Disease), and one book or recipe (*[Physicalities of Werewolves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Physicalities_of_Werewolves)*, *[The Book of Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Book_of_Daedra)*, *[Varieties of Daedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Varieties_of_Daedra)*, *[Immortal Blood](Skyrim_Immortal_Blood.md)*, *[Cure Disease Potion Recipe](Skyrim_Recipes.md#000f5cb8)*, or *[Resist Poison Potion Recipe](Skyrim_Recipes.md#000f5cc5)*). A lit [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch) can also be found next to his body.

If [reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md), he can cast three spells: [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md).
