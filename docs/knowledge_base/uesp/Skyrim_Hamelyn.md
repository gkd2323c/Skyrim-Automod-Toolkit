[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-npc-Hamelyn.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hamelyn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hamelyn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hamelyn.jpg) Hamelyn, crafting a potion in Honningbrew Basement **Hamelyn** is a hostile [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [bandit wizard](Skyrim_Bandit_Wizard.md) living in [Honningbrew Basement](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Basement.md).

According to the [journal](Skyrim_Hamelyn%27s_Journal.md) he carries, he was imprisoned in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) for ten years, having been called a "madman", but escaped and enrolled at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) as an apprentice alchemist. There, he writes, his instructors beat him because they were jealous of his genius, and he was expelled.

He is breeding an army of [Venomfang skeevers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venomfang_Skeever), in preparation for his day of revenge, and has discovered that the ingredients available in the meadery are perfect for the task. However, his skeevers are also being exploited by an unhappy [meadery employee](Skyrim_Mallus_Maccius.md) as part of a [plot](Skyrim_Dampened_Spirits.md) to get rid of the meadery's [owner](Skyrim_Sabjorn.md).

He wears some [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md) with matching [bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) and a pair of [Imperial light boots](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Boots.md). He also carries two leveled potions, one of [restore health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) and one of [restore magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Magicka) and a random piece of enchanted [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), with a chance of additionally carrying a leveled [ingot](Skyrim_Ingots.md) and/or a small amount of gold. He can cast a leveled selection of spells from the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md), [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) and [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) schools.
