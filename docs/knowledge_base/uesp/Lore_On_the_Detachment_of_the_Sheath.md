On the Detachment of the Sheath from the Integument **by [Arch-Prelate Fervidius Tharn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Arch-Prelate_Fervidius_Tharn)** *An explanation of an aspect of Aurbis*
---
Though all given Concavities, or sheaths within the integument of the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md), are necessarily contained by the Aurbis, Right Reaching dictates that a defined sheath may be detached from the integument by invocation of [Mnemoli](Lore_Mnemoli.md). Upon intercourse with the [star-orphan](Lore_Magna_Ge.md), the Beseeching Alesstic performs eversion of the organ of thought, an employment of the Hurling Disk that recapitulates the truth that a circle turned sidewise is a [Tower](Lore_The_Towers.md). By same-truth, twisting the enveloping sheath into the [middle dawn](Lore_Dragon_Break.md) (to the number of seventeen) brings it to untime and unplace.

Eventualism, of course, predicts reabsorption upon depletion of the Wheeling Force, but the absence of duration may render even eventuality moot.
