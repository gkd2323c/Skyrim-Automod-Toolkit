[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/SR-place-Rorik%27s_Manor.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rorik%27s_Manor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rorik%27s_Manor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rorik%27s_Manor.jpg) Rorik's Manor **Rorik's Manor** is the small home of [Rorik](Skyrim_Rorik.md), the head of [Rorikstead](Skyrim_Rorikstead.md) and his best friend and companion [Jouane Manette](Skyrim_Jouane_Manette.md).

While the name suggests that it would be a grand house, Rorik's Manor is not much bigger than the other farmhouses in Rorikstead, which he owns. It is a single-story L-shaped building located at the northwestern end of the village.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The only items of interest are a campfire with an empty pot hanging over it and an unowned bedroll beside it. There are two [cows](Skyrim_Cow.md) in an animal pen to the east of the house.

### Rorik's Manor
The fireplace is opposite the entrance and has a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front of it and three loose [septims](Skyrim_Gold.md) on the mantelpiece. The western end of the house is filled with several bales of straw, barrels and a large pile of firewood. To the right of the fireplace, against the north wall is a long wooden table and a kettle containing four [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md). On the table are two more potatoes, a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, a [chicken's egg](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), several loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), a [sliced eidar cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Eidar_Cheese.md), a [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md) and three [seared slaughterfish](Skyrim_Seared_Slaughterfish.md). Above the table are two bunches of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and two garlic braids. To the right of these is a rack hanging from the ceiling which holds two [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

To the right of the table is a flower basket containing four more chicken's eggs. Further along the north wall is a set of shelves holding two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and three of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md). In the northeast corner is a dresser containing a selection of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), with a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a [jug](Skyrim_Jug.md) and two [goblets](Skyrim_Goblet.md) on top. Beside this, coming out from the east wall is a single owned bed, where [Jouane Manette](Skyrim_Jouane_Manette.md) sleeps. There is an end table beside the bed and an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) containing minor loot beside that.

A counter coming out from the east wall to the right of all this with several sacks and a barrel, all containing [food](Skyrim_Food.md) around it. On the counter are several bottles of wine, alto wine and [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md) along with several pieces of cheese. Under the counter is a copy of *[Spirit of the Daedra](Skyrim_Spirit_of_the_Daedra.md)* and an empty [petty soul gem](Skyrim_Petty_Soul_Gem.md). There is a chair and [lute](Skyrim_Lute.md) behind the counter and a wardrobe further to the right of that. Coming out from the south wall is a large double bed that [Rorik](Skyrim_Rorik.md) uses with an end table on either side. On the left-hand end table is a [potion of haggling](Skyrim_Potion_of_Haggling.md) and beside it on the floor is a medium coin purse. The right-hand end table has four loose septims on top. To the right of the bed against the west wall are a couple of barrels with more bottles of wine and alto wine on them, while further right is a small round wooden table and two chairs. On this table is a couple of bottles of Nord mead.
