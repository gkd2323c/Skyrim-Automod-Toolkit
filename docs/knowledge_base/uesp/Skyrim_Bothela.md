[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/SR-npc-Bothela.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bothela.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bothela.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bothela.jpg) Bothela in her store **Bothela** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [apothecary](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vendor Apothecary) merchant who lives in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). She owns and runs [the Hag's Cure](Skyrim_The_Hag%27s_Cure.md) with her assistant and friend [Muiri](Skyrim_Muiri.md). She expresses regret at the situation of the [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md). [Odvan](Skyrim_Odvan.md) in [Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_Cidhna_Mine.md) is her grandson.

Bothela begins her day at 8am when she gets out of her bed to open her shop. She will spend her entire day inside her shop handling customers. After twelve hours of work, she will lock up the shop for the night and head to bed. She will wake up at 8am to repeat her schedule.

Bothela wears [black mage robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black_Mage_Robes) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000c5d12), and is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md). She carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_the_Hag%27s_Cure) to the Hag's Cure and a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common). She knows a few spells from various schools. From the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) school she knows [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md); from the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) school she knows [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md); she can use the spell [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md) from the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) school.

## Dialogue
Approaching Bothela while her shop is open will have her greet you in a number of ways:

*"The Hag's Cure is here for all your discreet needs..."* *"I have potions for disease, love sickness, irritating children..."* *"A little bit of old Reach magic can cure whatever ills * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * you...."* *"Oh yes... you'll need one of my special brews..."* *"Not scared of an old woman now, are you?"* On ending a conversation with Bothela, she will offer two unique remarks:

*"Now you let me know if you need a cure... or anything else."* *"I have herbs to mix."* When spoken to directly:

**What's your opinion on Markarth?** *"We'd be better off without the Silver-Bloods. They send their brute, [Yngvar](Skyrim_Yngvar_the_Singer.md), to shake some coins from me every few days. And [Betrid Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Betrid_Silver-Blood.md) is always asking for more potions to fuel [Thonar](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md)'s 'ambitions.' Gives him a temper fiercer than any bear."* **What do you know about the Forsworn?** *"Only that they break an old woman's heart. So many friends, so many kin, all lost trying to rebuild a past that was over long ago."*
## Quest-Related Events

### Bothela's Discreet Delivery
Asking her about her shop's unique name will have her offer you a job to [deliver a potion](Skyrim_Bothela%27s_Discreet_Delivery.md):

**The "Hag's Cure." That's a unique name.** *"Comes with living to a ripe old age, people start thinking there's something magical about you. Then, the insults. Still, a little knowledge of plants and potions can get you by. Not that anyone likes to admit buying things from you. See? I even have a little [potion](Skyrim_Stallion%27s_Potion.md) for the [Steward](Skyrim_Raerek.md). Mind handing it over to him? Just say it should solve that problem he has."* **I'll make sure he gets it.** *"Thank you."* **I don't have time right now.** *"Pity."* Should you initially deny the request, asking her about her shop again will provide you with another chance to take it.

## Unused Dialogue
Bothela has unused dialogue tied to multiple [quests](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) she was once meant to partake in. This dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

### Freeform Markarth I
In this quest, you would've been sent by [Uaile](Skyrim_Uaile.md) to pick up a potion for [Nepos the Nose](Skyrim_Nepos_the_Nose.md).

**I'm here for Uaile's potion.** *"Ah, yes. Clever way of keeping that lecher's hands away from her. But you didn't hear that from me."* This reply from Bothela would be your first hint as to Uaile and Nepos' real relationship.

### Freeform Markarth M2
This quest, despite its name, is actually related to The Forsworn Conspiracy. Originally, [Eltrys](Skyrim_Eltrys.md) was meant to give you the names of three people he suspected of having Forsworn ties, and [Nana Ildene](Skyrim_Nana_Ildene.md) would be among them. If you went to investigate Ildene, you would come across her switching notes with Bothela. Afterward, you could confront Bothela about this:

**What were you and Ildene talking about?** *"That's between two old women to know. Stay out of business that isn't yours."* (Failed) *"My nephew Odvan is in Cihna Mine. I've promised to pass along her notes if she promises the Forsworn help him get out. Look at me, almost 80 and still playing these games. I don't even know who's involved anymore, really. So many dead drops and messengers just to get so much as a note out."* **I need that note, Bothela. The Forsworn have to be stopped.** *"You won't stop them, dear, but if it'll make you feel better. Here. It's yours."* The contents of this note are unknown.

## Rumors
Her grandson Odvan will voice a thought about his grandmother:

*"I wonder how grandma Bothela is doing. She's probably worried sick about me."* The [Markarth City Guard](Skyrim_Markarth_City_Guard.md) will also talk about her abilities as a healer:

*"Caught a case of [Rockjoint](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rockjoint), once. Bothela at the Hag's Cure fixed me right up."* If Bothela is killed, Muiri will take over the Hag's Cure and will occasionally make a comment about her late employer:

*"I've taken over the Hag's Cure, since Bothela is gone."* *"I wish Bothela was still running the store. I'm not ready for this."*
## Notes
- Bothela has a face tattoo that's normally only seen on members of the Forsworn (it's also unavailable to you in character creation). She and Eltrys are the only named NPCs with this trait, yet neither person's tattoo will ever get addressed.
- There is some confusion about her relationship to Odvan. In all of Bothela's dialogue, she refers to him as her nephew. But in Odvan's dialogue, he once refers to her as his grandma and once implies her to be his aunt. Meanwhile, their relationship data has them set up as grandchild and grandparent, adding confusion.
