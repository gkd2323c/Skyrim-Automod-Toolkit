[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Umana.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Umana.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Umana.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Umana.jpg) Umana in Alftand **Umana** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) found in the last chamber inside [Alftand](Skyrim_Alftand.md) cathedral. Upon entering the room, she will speak to an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) soldier named [Sulla Trebatius](Skyrim_Sulla_Trebatius.md) about leaving this cursed place. If you stay out of sight, they will start fighting each other, which usually leads to the death of Sulla. It doesn't matter which one you kill first; the other one will still be hostile. She carries a unique shield called [Targe of the Blooded](Skyrim_Targe_of_the_Blooded.md).

Umana wears a set of [steel plate armor](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Armor.md) along with a matching pair of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Steel_Plate_Boots.md). In combat, she relies on her unique shield and a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md), which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality.

## Conversations

- **Umana**: *"Sulla, let's just get out of here. Hasn't there been enough death?"*
- **Sulla**: *"Oh, of course you want me to leave. Just waiting for me to turn my back. So you can have all the glory for yourself!"*

## Notes
- She is the author of *[Umana's Journal](Skyrim_Umana%27s_Journal.md)*.
