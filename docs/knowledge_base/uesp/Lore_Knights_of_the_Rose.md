# Knights of the Rose

The Knights of the Rose also known as the **Knights of the White Rose**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFGuide-1)</sup> have the honor of being the guard and strong arm of his majesty, [King Eadwyre](Lore_Eadwyre.md) of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest_(kingdom).md). The greatest [knights](Lore_Knights.md) in his court are members of the order and they have demonstrated their worth in internal and inter-provincial struggles throughout the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) and [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) they were led by [Lord Darkworth](Lore_Darkworth.md), a nobleman in Wayrest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-D1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-D2-3)</sup>

In [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405) the knights were quite divided, with some supporting any action of King Eadwyre, others furious about the possible prospect of a Dark Elf heir and many upset at the founding of [Orsinium](Lore_Orsinium.md) due to the displacement of the peasantry around the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFGuide-1)</sup>
