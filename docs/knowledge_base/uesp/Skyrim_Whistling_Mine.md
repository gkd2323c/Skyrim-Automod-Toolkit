[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-place-Whistling_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-place-Whistling_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whistling_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Whistling_Mine.jpg) Whistling Mine **Whistling Mine** is a small [iron](Skyrim_Iron.md) [mine](Skyrim_Mines.md) and [settlement](Skyrim_Settlements.md) southeast of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). It contains only one zone, *[Whistling Mine](#Whistling_Mine)*.

The mine is the only consistently inhabited settlement in the [hold of Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md) besides the city of the same name. All of the miners originally began digging the mine thinking it would make them rich. [Angvid](Skyrim_Angvid.md) and [Badnir](Skyrim_Badnir.md) seem to have lost their morale, while [Gunding](Skyrim_Gunding.md) and [Thorgar](Skyrim_Thorgar.md) still claim the mine will eventually be successful, possibly through the discovery of [silver](Skyrim_Silver.md) or [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) ore. Thorgar, the mine's owner, will [buy iron ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) from you.

## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The mine lies along the southern road between Winterhold and [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), at the base of the mountains. A [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) is just outside and to the right of the entrance, with three pieces of owned [iron ore](Skyrim_Iron_Ore.md) on a crate beside it. Two sacks can be found next to the crate. A [Winterhold guard](Skyrim_Winterhold_Guard.md) patrols the road in front of the mine, and there is an [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) encounter just north on the road. A path rising to the west starts just south of the mine and leads to [Mount Anthor](Skyrim_Mount_Anthor.md) and the [Shrine of Azura](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Azura.md). A [stone quarry](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md)<sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup> can be found down the road north of the mine.

### Whistling Mine
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e9/SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Whistling_Mine.jpg) Mine interior It is one of the smallest mines in Skyrim, containing only two [iron ore veins](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md). Upon entering the mine, the first chamber serves as the camp for the four miners. In the center of the chamber is a campfire with a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) on a stand beside it. Four bedrolls are east of the campfire, and a table with a [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md) and chairs is to the north. To the west of the table are a couple of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrels, one with a copy of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Heavy Armor Forging](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor_Forging.md)*, while a coin purse rests on a hay bale nearby. There are several other food sacks and barrels around the chamber. To the west is an ice tunnel that leads to a second chamber with a wooden ramp up to the two iron ore veins on the north wall.

## Notes
- The Winterhold guard who patrols the road near Whistling Mine is the only one who can be seen with the [Winterhold Guard's Shield](Skyrim_Winterhold_Guard%27s_Shield.md), as the guards in the city of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) don't carry it due to a [bug](Skyrim_Winterhold.md#Notes).

## Bugs
- When using USSEP, the Winterhold guard who patrols the road near Whistling Mine may have two Winterhold Guard's Shields, holding one and having another one in his/her inventory. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, addresses this issue. The inventory copy has been removed. This was missed during the USKP 1.2.4 fix which took place back in 2012.
