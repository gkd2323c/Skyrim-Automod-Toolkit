## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with an innkeeper, jarl, or steward to receive the bounty.
2. Kill the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) at the indicated location.
3. Speak with the jarl or steward of the hold to receive your reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Here There Be Dragons
After your adventures at [Whiterun's](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [Western Watchtower](Skyrim_Western_Watchtower.md) during [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md), a proliferation of dragons are about. In your travels, ask an innkeep, jarl or steward for some work. You may learn that a [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) has been harassing travelers and citizens around its [lair](Skyrim_Dragon_Lairs.md), and the jarl of the hold has put out a bounty on the dragon. You are an enterprising sort, with newly acquired [dragon soul absorbing powers](Skyrim_Dovahkiin.md) and [shouts](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md), so take up the job.

### The Dragon Hunter
Seek out the offending dragon in its lair. There is a [wide variety of dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md#Abilities) that you may encounter in each lair you visit. Once defeated, and the dragon soul is absorbed, be sure to remove the [dragon bones](Skyrim_Dragon_Bone.md) and [dragon scales](Skyrim_Dragon_Scales.md). These are quite valuable in the local shops, and can be used to make and improve [dragonbone and dragonscale armor](Skyrim_Dragon_Armor.md).

### The Bounty
Once the dragon is dead, you will be directed to the [jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) or [steward](Skyrim_Steward.md) of the hold to receive your payment.

## Notes
- If you are tasked to kill a dragon at a lair that you have already cleared, the dragon there will respawn. However, killing a respawned dragon (whether it has respawned because of this quest, or naturally after 10 or 30 days) will probably result in not getting a dragon soul.
- You need to have completed [Dragon Rising](Skyrim_Dragon_Rising.md) first before receiving this quest.

## Bugs
- The dragon at Mount Anthor may not spawn, and the quest target will point to an incorrect location. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) command `setstage BQ04 100` to skip to the next stage, or `setstage BQ04 200` to jump directly to quest completion.
