*The growing tension between Solstice's two peoples finally came to a head in the time of Kinlord Kevarian, Orlemar's grandson. Three short wars in 1E 1172, 1E 1190, and 1E 1194 saw Corelanya expeditions invade Stone-Nest xanmeer-cities and Stone-Nest warbands ravage Corelanya estates up to Sunport's very walls. In the stalemate that followed, several Corelanya kinlords were assassinated, until Kinlady Torinwe finally sought to make peace.*
— *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)* by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> **Kinlady** Torinwe Corelanya was the leader of the [Corelanya Clan](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) at some point in the middle years of the 12th century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Peace between the Corelanya Altmer and the local [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) tribes was achieved during her tenure thanks to her efforts.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup>

## History

### Reign
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/29/ON-place-Sunport.jpg/200px-ON-place-Sunport.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sunport.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Sunport.jpg) Sunport *In 1E 1244, Torinwe and the chieftains of the Stone-Nest People met at the village of Xor-Hist. Kinlady Torinwe put an end to further Corelanya expansion into eastern Solstice, while the Argonians recognized the lands currently under Corelanya control. Most importantly, both peoples were free to travel all parts of the island, so long as they did not make any new permanent dwellings in the others' territory. This restored Argonian access to various shrines and sacred places across Solstice, alleviating one of the main grievances behind the strife.
To this day, we still honor the borders and principles established in the Peace of Xor-Hist.*
— *History of Clan Corelanya* by Regent [Karinwe Corelanya](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> Little is known about Torinwe's early life. She rose to lead Clan Corelanya following the deaths of several other kinlords, assassinated amid ongoing conflicts with the Argonians. Torinwe was the first Corelanya ruler to actively seek peace.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> In [1E 1244](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_1244), she met with the chieftains of the Stone-Nest tribe at the village of Xor-Hist.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VGTES-2)</sup> Accompanied by Tuintirrimo of the Corelanyan Guard and Pamaldawe of the Collegium Praxis, she signed the treaty with Mahei-Gei, the representative of the Tide-Born, and Tlekeus, who spoke for the Stone-Nest.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POXH-3)</sup> During this meeting, Torinwe agreed to halt further Corelanya expansion into eastern Solstice, while the Argonians recognized the lands already under Corelanya control. Crucially, both peoples were granted freedom to travel anywhere on the island, provided they did not establish permanent settlements within each other's territories. This restored Argonian access to important shrines and sacred sites across [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), addressing one of the key causes of the earlier conflict.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POXH-3)</sup> They also agreed to cooperate in the event of an attack on the Solstice. In such circumstance, they agreed to temporarily waive the previous articles for the sake of mutual defence.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POXH-3)</sup> By that time, the House Corelanya had lost interest in maintaining their old estates in the eastern part of the island during her rule.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VTKF-4)</sup> During the negotiations, a dish known as Nocturnal's Mystery was served by a devout follower of [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md) to Torinwe and the Argonian elders. The chef sought to recreate the Argonian delicacy Saxhleel Nagahssee, but adapted it to suit Altmeri palates.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CC-5)</sup>

### Legacy
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg/200px-ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Corelanya_Crypt.jpg) Corelanya Crypt *ARTICLE THE FIRST
* As long as all spoken-for inhabitants of Solstice adhere to the articles laid out in this document, they will lay down their arms against one another and vow to refrain from taking any hostile actions regarding ancestral or recent grievances.

ARTICLE THE SECOND
As set forth by this assembled congregation, the territories belonging to the Corelanya, Stone-Nest, and Tide-Born are hereby recognized as the sovereign land of the peoples thereof. Corelanya and Argonian alike agree not to encroach on the others' lands or impede them from crossing from one territory to another. Henceforth, no permanent dwellings shall be constructed in another's territory without express and written permission.

ARTICLE THE THIRD

In times when Solstice is threatened, we will temporarily waive the previous articles for the sake of mutual defense. *
—* Peace of Xor-Hist *<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-POXH-3)</sup>* Following her death, Torinwe was laid to rest in the Corelanya Crypt, alongside all other Kinrulers except for the last, [Istamil](Lore_Istamil_Corelanya.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RK-6)</sup> The borders and principles established in the Peace of Xor-Hist remained respected as late as [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> The Corelanyans refrained from expanding further into Argonian lands, while the Stone-Nest Argonians gradually grew accustomed to their presence. This led to a relatively long period of peace and stability, lasting until the time of Istamil and [Ryethamil](Lore_Ryethamil_Corelanya.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-HOCC5-7)</sup>

Torinwe features prominently in *[History of Clan Corelanya](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:History_of_Clan_Corelanya)*, authored by Regent [Karinwe](Lore_Karinwe_Corelanya.md), the leader of Clan Corelanya during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HOCC4-1)</sup> She is also mentioned in *[Corelanyan Cuisine](Lore_Corelanyan_Cuisine.md)* by [Pameryawen](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Pameryawen).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CC-5)</sup>
