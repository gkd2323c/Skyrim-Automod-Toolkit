# Wildblood Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8a/ON-npc-Bralthahawn.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Bralthahawn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Bralthahawn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Bralthahawn.jpg) An ambassador from Arenthia, who worked closely with the Reachfolk of the Wildblood Clan ca. [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Wildblood Clan was one of numerous [clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup>

Wildblood Clan's members were known for their custom of crafting personal talismans, each of them being unique to that person and their family. It represented the relationship between hunter and prey, and was deeply personal to [their beliefs about life and death](Lore_Reachfolk_Death_Beliefs.md). Those talismans were an important part of guiding their loved one's spirits home and were traditionally kept by living relatives of the deceased.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WWT-2)</sup>

At least some members of the Wildblood Clan were following the teachings of the [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WWT-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/ON-creature-Matron_Grygarsnit.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Matron_Grygarsnit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Matron_Grygarsnit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-creature-Matron_Grygarsnit.jpg) Leader of the Thornroot Clan, Matron Grygarsnit circa 2E 582 In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), during the times of [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) invasion of the Reach, the Wildblood Clan was attacked by the members of the [Thornroot Clan](Lore_Thornroot_Clan.md), led by a [Hagraven](Lore_Hagraven.md) known as Matrogn Grygarsnit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Grygarsnit-3)</sup> Under her leadership Thornroots became violent and began attacking clans capturing their people and transforming them into [briarhearts](Lore_Briarheart.md), to ensure Thornroot Clan survival during the Gray Host intrusion.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Medone-4)</sup>

Due to the Gray Host attack, a lot of the Reachfolk clans stayed in [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) at the behest of Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Medone-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CVR-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONCaddach-6)</sup> It is unknown whether or not Wildblood Clan decided to seek refuge behind the city walls, but they had a presence in the city. In the same year,Wildblood Clan chief contacted an ambassador of the scholarly organization from [Arenthia](Lore_Arenthia.md). They formed relations with scholars - in exchange for granting them the knowledge they requested the scholarly group for aid. The ambassador of the group acted as an intermediary between the clan and various contractors willing to aid them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup>

Numerous members of the Wildblood Clan became kidnapped and subjected to the Briarheart ritual against their will. They hired [an adventurer](Lore_The_Vestige.md) in order to destroy ritual tools of the Thornroots to cripple their ability to alter their captives. Wildbloods hoped to use that weakness to rescue some of their captured brethren.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TTB-7)</sup>

Thornroot Clan also stole a totem of power that held religious significance to the Wildblood Clan. It was a carved wooden totem, and likely a personification of one of the [Great Spirits of the Reach](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-HT-8)</sup> The effigy was taken to their area of operations in [Briar Rock Ruins](Lore_Briar_Rock_Ruins.md) and used by the Hagraven to draw power from. Some time later, the totem was taken by the hired adventurer and given back to the Wildbloods, likely causing the quelling of the violent tendencies of the Thornroot Clan.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-WHIO-9)</sup>

While the Wildbloods doubted that the Thornroots allowed many of their clanmates to survive, they still asked for help to retrieve their personal talismans left behind in Briar Rock ruins that were in the possession of the capturers, which caused yet another attack against the Thornroot Clan. The talismans were then successfully retaken thanks to the help of an adventurer and sent back to the Wildbloods.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-B-1)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WT-10)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-WWT-2)</sup>
