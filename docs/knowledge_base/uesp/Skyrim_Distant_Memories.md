## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Brand-Shei](Skyrim_Brand-Shei.md).
2. Find [Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lymdrenn_Tenvanni%27s_Journal) in [Wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md).
3. Take the journal to Brand-Shei.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[Brand-Shei](Skyrim_Brand-Shei.md) is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) trader in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). He can usually be found at his stall at the market, or in a cell in the [Riften Jail](Skyrim_Riften_Jail.md) if you've helped [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) frame him for theft. As a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md), he has an oddly [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) name (such as [Beem-Ja](Skyrim_Beem-Ja.md), [Gulum-Ei](Skyrim_Gulum-Ei.md), [Talen-Jei](Skyrim_Talen-Jei.md)). In fact, he was raised Argonian. *"I ended up orphaned then taken in by a kindly Argonian family in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md)."* He hopes one day to find out what happened to him and he has got one clue. When he was found by his Argonian father, he was wrapped in a blanket bearing the symbol of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md), one of the great houses in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) long ago. He'd like you to keep an eye on anything that might provide him with the answers he is looking for in your travels. If you ask him why was he searching in Skyrim, he will tell you something about a matron who had served for House Telvanni and remind you to investigate a ship named "[The Pride of Tel Vos](Skyrim_Wreck_of_The_Pride_of_Tel_Vos.md)".

Travel to the northeast coast of Skyrim and you can find the wreck of The Pride of Tel Vos. A group of three [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) have a camp here. However, you can hide yourself from them easily. You can also choose to wipe them out and take their treasures. In the northern half of the ship at the waterline, you can find a waterlogged chest containing the item you need: [Lymdrenn Tenvanni's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lymdrenn_Tenvanni%27s_Journal). This journal would be the key to the answers Brand-Shei is looking for.

Return to Brand-Shei and give the book to him. He will be very excited. *"Yes... yes. This is it! I don't know how you found this stranger, but you've obviously succeeded where I had failed."* Then he will offer you the key to his strongbox under his stand in the marketplace. You are free to take anything inside, including some [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md), and a random novice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) [spell tome](Skyrim_Spell_Tomes.md).

## Notes
- This quest can be completed by finding the journal in the wreck first, then talking to Brand-Shei afterward.
- Since the lockbox's contents are leveled, the [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Prowler%27s_Profit.md) perk will give you more value.
- After completion of this quest, some items in Haelga's Bunkhouse will be free to take, as this is Brand-Shei's residence at the start of the game.
