Casik was the King of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) of the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARCasik-1)</sup> In his time, he had a reputation for his extreme ruthlessness.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AREbonheart-2)</sup>

When Casik was born and became the King of Ebonheart is unknown, but he assumed his role by the Imperial Simulacrum. King Casik hoped to rediscover the lost mines of [Dagoth-Ur](Lore_Dagoth-Ur_(place).md) and use the raw metal of the mountain to create the finest weapons and armor in the realm. He also wanted to revive the forgotten art of [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) [smithcraft](Lore_Smithing.md). He had the [Anvil of Mithas](Lore_Anvil_of_Mithas.md) but needed the [Hammer of Gharen](Lore_Hammer_of_Gharen.md) for it to reveal its location.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARCasik-1)</sup>

At the time, he heard rumors of the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md)'s search for Dagoth-Ur, where the last piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) was left behind by [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARCasik-1)</sup> He offered to help the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) uncover the dungeon's location if they could retrieve the hammer from [Black Gate](Lore_Black_Gate.md). Once the Eternal Champion returned with the artifact, King Casik struck it on the Anvil of Mithas and provided them with Dagoth-Ur's location.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ARDagoth-3)</sup>
