[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-map-Menevia.jpg/200px-ON-map-Menevia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Menevia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Menevia.jpg) Map of Menevia [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/11/ON-place-Menevia.jpg/200px-ON-place-Menevia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Menevia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Menevia.jpg) Menevia Menevia is a region in central [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), within the larger region of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md). It borders [Alcaire](Lore_Alcaire.md) to the west, [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) to the north, and [Gavaudon](Lore_Gavaudon.md) to the east. The region contains the city of [Wayrest](Lore_Wayrest.md), as well as the small settlement of Farangel's Landing. Around [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Menevia was home to Pariah Abbey, where the Spirit Wardens worshipped [Azura](Lore_Azura.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ON-1)</sup>
