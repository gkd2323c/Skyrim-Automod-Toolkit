[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/22/OB-place-Nornal.jpg/200px-OB-place-Nornal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nornal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nornal.jpg) Nornal at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Nornal was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) settlement located near the banks of the [Corbolo](Lore_Corbolo_River.md) and [Reed](Lore_Reed_River.md) rivers in the northern [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), south of [Lake Poppad](Lore_Lake_Poppad.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMap-1)</sup> Its subterranean areas of *Nornal Ageasel*, *Goriluch*, and *Varlasel* can be translated as *"Hall of Secrets"*, *"Obscure [unknown]"*, and *"Hall of Stars"*, respectively. The settlement eventually fell into ruin at some point after the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md).

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the entrance to Nornal was sealed off by caved-in dirt, and the exterior ruins were infested by the [zombies](Lore_Zombie.md) of dead [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) citizens, the reanimated skeletons of [Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) soldiers, [giant Daggermaw Bats](Lore_Giant_Bat.md), and [wraiths](Lore_Wraith.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> A group of adventurers gathered at the nearby [Drakelowe Keep](Lore_Drakelowe_Keep.md) to journey to Nornal and battle the evil creatures defiling the grounds in order to seek out the vast treasures that lay within the ruins.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ct A-3)</sup> It is uncertain what happened to them, as their flyer was found next to the bodies of a dead [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) and [Soul Shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

By the time of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), the ruins had been taken over by a band of marauders, while the deeper halls swarmed with undead.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OB-4)</sup> [Mathieu Bellamont](Lore_Mathieu_Bellamont.md), while posing as fellow speaker [Lucien Lachance](Lore_Lucien_Lachance.md), left a dead drop for a [Silencer](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) inside the ruins, instructing them to assassinate [Ungolim](Lore_Ungolim.md), who—unbeknownst to the Silencer—was in fact the [Listener](Lore_Listener.md) of the [Black Hand](Lore_Black_Hand.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-AKBD-5)</sup> The ex- [Glenmoril](Lore_Glenmoril_Wyrd.md) witch Melisande tasked those infected with [vampirism](Lore_Vampire.md) with retrieving an empty grand [soul gem](Lore_Soul_Gems.md) from the ruins as part of a ritual to cure the disease.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VC-6)</sup>
