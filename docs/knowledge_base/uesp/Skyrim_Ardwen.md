[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-npc-Ardwen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ardwen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ardwen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ardwen.jpg) Ardwen on her pilgrimage **Ardwen** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) pilgrim to the [Gildergreen](Lore_Gildergreen.md) that can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Pilgrim_visiting_the_tree_in_Whiterun) with her [dog](Skyrim_Dog.md) after [The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature) is complete, if you restored the Gildergreen with sap. She does not appear if you completed the quest by bringing a sapling back. She will continue traveling from where you encounter her until she reaches [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

She is a respectable [elemental mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Elemental), able to cast a leveled selection of spells from the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md), [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md), and [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) schools. Her outfit consists of [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c).

## Related Quests
- **[The Blessings of Nature](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Blessings_of_Nature)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](Lore_Gildergreen.md) for [Danica Pure-Spring](Skyrim_Danica_Pure-Spring.md).

## Dialogue
If spoken to, she will tell you the reason for her journey:

- *"We are traveling to [the Shrine of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md) in Whiterun. I hear the Gildergreen is more beautiful than ever since it's been restored. They say that eating the fruit it bears will add years to your life."*

## Notes
- If you follow her, she will eventually end up standing in front of the Gildergreen in Whiterun and will begin praying there.
- After completing her related quest, she will often just end up standing near the [Whiterun stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables) 24 hours a day. She can be engaged in conversation there, but will only say *"Hello"*, with no further dialogue available. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. Her random encounter quest will now terminate if she has arrived and you change locations to anywhere other than Whiterun.
