[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/SR-npc-Saffir.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Saffir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saffir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Saffir.jpg) Saffir inside Amren's house **Saffir** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) who lives in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) with her husband [Amren](Skyrim_Amren.md) and their daughter [Braith](Skyrim_Braith.md). Lately she has been having arguments with her husband over his continued hunt for his [father's sword](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Bandits).md#Amren.27s_Family_Sword) which has cost the family a significant amount of money. While she understands that his family's past is important, she realizes that they are running out of money.

She is quite the bookworm and can be found reading inside her home from when she wakes up at 8am until she goes to bed again at 11pm. On occasion, she puts down the book for a quick meal or a bit of conversation with her husband and daughter when they are at home, but otherwise she prefers to lose herself in a book instead of socializing.

She wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd), and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1), and equips an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) while also carrying a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Amren%27s_House) to [Amren's house](Skyrim_Amren%27s_House.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
When spoken to she can say:

*"Nothing like a good adventure or romance to lose yourself in."* *"I import books whenever I can. Most [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) works are too bawdy and violent for my tastes."* *"My daughter Braith says I read too much. 'Play with me, mother!' Girls need to learn how to take care of themselves."* *"Really, I wish my girl would read more. It would certainly keep her out of all those fights."* If you interrupt her while she is reading she will comment:

*"Excuse me, but I'm trying to read."* *"Damn it, I lost my page..."* *"Back to my book..."*. If found outside, she will impatiently state:

*"I just want to go home and read."* *"If you'll excuse me, I really just want to get home."*

## Conversations
The only time she is seen outside the house is the first time you enter Whiterun, when she will be engaged in a hefty discussion with her husband:

**Saffir**: *"I know your family's honor is important to you, but we can't afford it!"*
**Amren**: *"It took me weeks to find that thieves' den. I can't stop now, and I can't get the sword on my own."*
**Saffir**: *"So you're willing to starve your wife and daughter to reclaim some rusty old sword?"*
**Amren**: *"I just need to hire one, maybe two good men. You won't starve."*
**Saffir**: *"I'll put it plainly. You can claim your sword, or you can keep your wife. If you set foot outside the gate, I won't be here when you return."*
**Amren**: *"Saffir! Wait... I...."* Young Braith has bigger problems getting in contact with her mother. She has a crush on local boy [Lars Battle-Born](Skyrim_Lars_Battle-Born.md) but will find absolutely no help from her book-obsessed mother:

**Braith**: *"Mother? Can I talk to you?"*
**Saffir**: *"Not now, Braith."*
**Braith**: *"But..."*
**Saffir**: *"Not now, Braith."* **Braith**: *"Mother?"*
**Saffir**: *"What?"*
**Braith**: *"I wanted to ask you something. It's about a boy..."*
**Saffir**: *"Not now, Braith. Go... go ask your father."*
