# Personal Steward


- [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) in the Pale
- [Goldenhills Plantation](Skyrim_Goldenhills_Plantation.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup> in Whiterun Hold
- [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md) in Falkreath Hold
- [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) in Hjaalmarch

To hire a follower, simply ask them to come with you, then travel to the build site. They should approach you and volunteer for the job, or if not, you can ask them yourself once there. You will need to first build at least the [Small House Layout](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout) before the option to hire them appears. Your steward will patrol the exterior of your property, as well as use various items in and around your house. Once a steward has been chosen for a particular homestead, the choice is permanent; you cannot replace that steward with someone else later. With the exception of the stewards for Goldenhills Plantation, which can be fired and rehired as many times as you want.

## Commands

### Improvements
You can ask them to make improvements to the house, which will allow you several options:

- Hire a [carriage driver](Skyrim_Hearthfire_NPCs.md#Carriage_Drivers) for 500 gold
- Hire a [bard](Skyrim_Hearthfire_NPCs.md#Bards) for 1500 gold
- Purchase a [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) for 1000 gold (if you have built the [exterior](Skyrim_Construction.md#Exterior) Stable option)
- Purchase a [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) for 200 gold (if you have built the exterior Animal Pen option) - Your cow will provide [jugs of milk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk) over time in the cupboard closest to the butter churn, in your [kitchen](Skyrim_Kitchen.md) if you have built it.
- Purchase up to three [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) for 25 gold (if you have built the exterior Animal Pen option) - Your chicken(s) will have a nest where you will be provided with [chicken's eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg).
- There is little advantage, other than completeness, to purchasing more than one chicken—you only get one nest even with three of them.

### Building Materials
You can ask your steward to purchase building materials for you:

- [Lumber](Skyrim_Sawn_Log.md): 20 for 200 gold
- [Stone](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md): 20 for 100 gold
- [Clay](Skyrim_Clay.md): 20 for 20 gold

### Furnish a Room
You can ask your steward to purchase furnishing options for the house. When you ask a steward to decorate part of your home for you, they will *not* do so immediately. They will gradually add furnishing options over time, which can be a very slow process. This is one way to avoid the chore of collecting all the required materials yourself, though you'll miss out on all the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) experience you'd gain from crafting hundreds of [nails](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nails) and [iron fittings](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Fittings), plus many [hinges](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hinge) and [locks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lock). If you have them decorate a section of your home, they will eventually add *all* possible furnishing options to that section of the home; if you prefer *not* to have certain furnishing options added, you should build furnishing options for that section of the home yourself. You can pay them to furnish all parts of your property, except for the Cellar, which requires you to build all furnishings by yourself. The costs of each section of the home are as follows:

| - [Small House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Small_House_Layout): 1000 gold <br> - [Entry Room](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall#Entryway): 1000 gold <br> - [Main Hall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Main_Hall): 3500 gold | - [Alchemy Laboratory](Skyrim_Alchemy_Laboratory.md): 2500 gold <br> - [Armory](Skyrim_Armory.md): 2000 gold <br> - [Bedrooms](Skyrim_Bedrooms.md): 2000 gold | - [Enchanter's Tower](Skyrim_Enchanter%27s_Tower.md): 2500 gold <br> - [Greenhouse](Skyrim_Greenhouse.md): 1500 gold <br> - [Kitchen](Skyrim_Kitchen.md): 1500 gold | - [Library](Skyrim_Library.md): 1500 gold <br> - [Storage Room](Skyrim_Storage_Room.md): 1000 gold <br> - [Trophy Room](Skyrim_Trophy_Room.md): 1500 gold |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

Note that the cost for each section will be the same, regardless of how much of the furnishings you have already built yourself.

If you don't have the patience to wait for your steward to furnish, see the [notes](#Notes_2) section at the bottom of the page how to make them work faster.

### Other
You can also ask your steward where to buy materials such as [glass](Skyrim_Glass_(item).md) or [straw](Skyrim_Straw.md). They will direct you to a nearby general goods store: [Belethor's General Goods](Skyrim_Belethor%27s_General_Goods.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) (from Heljarchen Hall), [Gray Pine Goods](Skyrim_Gray_Pine_Goods.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) (from Lakeview Manor), or [Bits and Pieces](Skyrim_Bits_and_Pieces.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) (from Windstad Manor).

### Farming<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Farming.md)</sup>
After hiring a personal steward for Goldenhills Plantation<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md)</sup>, you can order them to furnish areas of the house:

- Kitchen for 750 gold
- Dining area for 500 gold
- Alchemy and enchanting lab for 1000 gold
- Loft for 500 gold
- Master bedroom for 750 gold
- Child's bedroom for 500 gold
- Library and display room for 750 gold

You can also order them to:

- Hire two [farmhands](Skyrim_Farmhand.md) for 500 gold
- Buy a horse for 1000 gold
- Buy a cow for 300 gold
- Buy two goats for 200 gold
- Buy three chickens for 100 gold

## Notes
- The amount of time it takes for stewards to furnish your house depends on the number of times you leave the area. The fastest way to complete the process is to find the border of your property and cross it repeatedly until the furnishing is done (PC players can quickly find the border using the `tb` console command). There are several areas where this can be performed, including:

Lakeview Manor: Go south from your entrance door. After the carriage driver and just before the small pond next to Pinewatch are two large trees growing next to the rocks on your right. The border is between the trees. Windstad Manor: Go straight east from your entrance door, up the hill. The border is between the two trees growing next to the rocks on your left. (There is a third tree on your right.) Heljarchen Hall: Go straight south towards Loreius's farm and pass your stable and carriage driver. Where the rocks on your right end are two blue mountain flowers. One of them is growing next to a dead branch. The border is between the two flowers. - Stewards who are Blades will stay at the house that they are steward of, and not go back to [Sky Haven Temple](Skyrim_Sky_Haven_Temple.md).
- Once a steward has been chosen for a particular homestead, you can sill ask them to follow you. - The exception is Goldenhills Plantation; an active steward there cannot be asked to follow you (as opposed to Hearthfire stewards), so they must be fired to do so.
- Goldenhills Plantation needs a steward present for you to collect any profits the farm has made. Though you cannot collect gold from there while you have no steward, profits will still accumulate over time despite not having a steward assigned. Simply hire a steward to collect all the money.
- There is no option for Stewards to furnish your cellar.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you want to dismiss a steward, you can do so using the console. - Dismiss the steward of Lakeview Manor in Falkreath Hold:
`Set Console Scope Quest BYOHHouse Building
Empty Ref Alias House1Steward
Set Papyrus Quest Var BYOHHouse Falkreath b Have Steward False`
- Dismiss the steward of Windstad Manor in Hjaalmarch:
`Set Console Scope Quest BYOHHouse Building
Empty Ref Alias House2Steward
Set Papyrus Quest Var BYOHHouse Hjaalmarch b Have Steward False`
- Dismiss the steward of Heljarchen Hall in the Pale:
`Set Console Scope Quest BYOHHouse Building
Empty Ref Alias House3Steward
Set Papyrus Quest Var BYOHHouse Pale b Have Steward False`
- If you put these commands into a bat file, then the Empty Ref Alias will fail, but the other 2 will work, allowing you to appoint a new steward at that holding, but your old steward will still have steward dialogue.

- Personal stewards may vanish after being appointed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.

- Entering and exiting the house a few times should restore them.
- Personal stewards may tell you that you don't have enough gold when you attempt to furnish rooms, even when you do. This is usually caused by furnishing a room before asking the steward to do it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Personal stewards may not sleep at all and instead just stand next to the bed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.2, fixes this bug.
- Upon arrival at the house hirelings may offer to be a steward but may not have the normal steward dialogue and instead offer to go adventuring for free or a price depending on the mercenary. They will, however, wander off and do things on the property like any other steward. Waiting at least 24 hours after dismissing refreshes the dialogue that allows you to hire them again. At that point they will have normal steward options. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- It is not possible to ask a hireling to become your steward after bringing them to the property due to an erroneous condition that blocks the Potential Hireling faction. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- When hiring a steward, BYOHHouse Building Script may fail to check to make sure they were your current follower before simply dismissing one. This would result in any follower you DID have getting dismissed instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- You may be able to attempt to hire the same steward for more than one home. Doing so introduces some unresolvable bugs that will simply end up breaking the steward entirely. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.0, fixes this bug.
- You may be able to hire a bard before the Main Hall is built, which can cause them to not show up at all. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.0, addresses this issue. The option to do so until then has been removed.
- If the chickens at your house are killed, they cannot be replaced because the game is never told to reset them when they die. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- When a follower offers to become your steward, the offer is set up to be a one-time decision that cannot be revisited, but the DLC dialogue does not properly reflect the finality of this decision. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- On some occasions, particularly when hiring a bard is the only option left, the option of making upgrades for the home will be absent. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.

- Killing your cow and triggering a load screen (entering the house) may trigger the dialogue for home improvements with your steward. Not selecting hiring a bard first will cause the option to disappear again.
- Having your steward buy a [horse](Skyrim_Horses.md) for your property can prevent the horse's ownership from being set. This is inconsistent with the way the vanilla horse purchase works which adds them to the proper factions. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, fixes this bug.
- After furnishing a room by using a steward, the furnishings may not actually appear.
- Whenever hirelings are subsequently "hired" for adventuring, they will lose their steward dialogue upon being released from service. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 2.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Followers may not give the option to become stewards if Dragonborn is enabled. - Followers that have to be paid to follow may not present the choice for Stewardship, but Followers that call you friend and follow upon request will present the choice upon arrival to the home.
- If you decline a follower's offer to become a steward (with the option "Let me think about it" or something similar), you will not get the option later on to tell them that you want them to become steward and take them up on their earlier offer.
- Faendal may still talk about how he works at the lumber mill in Riverwood if he's hired as a steward for one of the homesteads. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.1, fixes this bug.
