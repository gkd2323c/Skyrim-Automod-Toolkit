## Fabricant Flora
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e0/ON-flora-Fabricant_Cherry_Blossom.jpg/200px-ON-flora-Fabricant_Cherry_Blossom.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Fabricant_Cherry_Blossom.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-flora-Fabricant_Cherry_Blossom.jpg) Fabricant Cherry Blossom Sotha Sil's realm is completely synthetic and as a result of the soil being completely metallic real vegetation cannot grow there. Sotha Sil has created various types of metallic vegetation that mimic real ones found on Nirn and they include Cherry Blossoms, Oak, Maple, Spruce, Cypress, and other types of Flora such as shrubs.

## [Ferrofungus](Lore_Ferrofungus.md)
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Pliant_Ferrofungus.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-style_material-Pliant_Ferrofungus.png) A Ferrofungus [Ferrofungus](Lore_Ferrofungus.md) is a kind of [mushroom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Mushroom) cultivated by members of [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md). They [refined](Lore_Mycoturgy.md) pliant ferrofungus into wrought ferrofungus for use in crafting some of their equipment. [Forge-hardened](Lore_Smithing.md) ferrofungus was occasionally employed by Telvanni craftsmen when [metallic ore](Lore_Metals.md) was unavailable, particularly in the creation of [weapons](Lore_Weapons.md) such as axes. Some pieces of Telvanni armor, including cuirasses, were constructed from overlapping plates of chitin, ferrofungus, or metal, shaped after shelf fungi or toadstool caps and scored with spore-gill lines. Their shields were similarly fashioned from layered chitin or ferrofungus.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Pliant_Ferrofungus)

## Fire Fern
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/MW-flora-Fire_Fern.jpg/200px-MW-flora-Fire_Fern.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Fire_Fern.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-flora-Fire_Fern.jpg) A Fire Fern The Fire Fern is a perennial herb native to Morrowind. The flowers are inconspicuous and often hidden. The glossy, evergreen foliage and blossoms are resistant to conditions of high heat and bright light found in Vvardenfell. A petal from this plant placed under one's tongue will provide protection from heat and fire.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

They grow primarily on [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), in Molag Amur, and on Azura's Coast.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Kwama love eating fire fern, but it also agitates them.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Fire Petals](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Fire_Petal)

## Flax
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/OB-flora-Flax_%28Blue%29.jpg/200px-OB-flora-Flax_%28Blue%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Flax_(Blue).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-flora-Flax_(Blue).jpg) Flax Flax have thin stems and many small leaves that flower with either a pale pink, yellow, or blue head. It can be found across Tamriel, although it grows widely in the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md) region of Cyrodiil. It is mainly used in the production of linen, although its seeds can be of use to alchemists.
