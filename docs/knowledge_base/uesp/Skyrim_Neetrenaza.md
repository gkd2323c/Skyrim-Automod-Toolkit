[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-npc-Neetrenaza.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Neetrenaza.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Neetrenaza.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Neetrenaza.jpg) Neetrenaza **Neetrenaza** is an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) [scout](Skyrim_CombatScout.md) and dock worker who lives in the [Argonian Assemblage](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage.md), outside of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). He maintains a very bitter attitude about the [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) that he works for.

Neetrenaza begins his day at 7:10am by having a quick breakfast for an hour. After that hour is done, he will return to his bed for fifty more minutes. At 9am he will head outside to work on the docks. He will end the workday after ten hours at 7pm. Neetrenaza will then head back inside to return to his bed for a short nap. He will wake up once more at 7:45pm for a two hour long dinner, before heading back to bed to rest for tomorrow.

Neetrenaza wears a set of [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0006c1da) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) to defend himself. Besides that, he carries a [key](Skyrim_Argonian_Assemblage_Key.md) to the Argonian Assemblage and a selection of [lower-class items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Poor).

## Dialogue
When you approach Neetrenaza, he may comment on his disdain for the Nords.

- *"The Nords don't appreciate us, but so what? I don't appreciate them right back."*

He might also mention the dangers of being down on the docks.

- *"The docks can be a dangerous place. Don't expect me to save you if you fall in."*

If you are not an Argonian, he will suggest that you should leave the Argonian-populated docks.

- *"Look, we don't have much love for your kind down here. Probably best if you just left."*

Argonians will instead hear a much more cheerful welcome to the docks.

- *"Haven't seen you here before, egg brother/sister. Welcome!"*
