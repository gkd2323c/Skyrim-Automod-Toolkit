[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/89/SR-creature-Bran.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bran.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bran.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bran.jpg) Bran **Bran** is one of two armored [huskies](Skyrim_Husky.md) living in [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md), along with [Sceolang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sceolang). Both of them can be found within a wooden pen in the cave section for Fort Dawnguard. They are located in the northeastern corner of the fort, to the right of the entrance to [Dead Drop Falls](Skyrim_Dead_Drop_Falls.md). Each becomes available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md#Creatures) if you join the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) and complete the quest [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md). They function exactly like the other animal followers, having only 'wait' and 'follow' as commands, following the typical behavior of running and attacking things on sight, and are allowed to be recruited in addition to an NPC follower. With the [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) add-on, Bran can be adopted by your [child](Skyrim_Child.md) and become a [pet](Skyrim_Adoption.md#Pets).

## Dialogue
Being spoken to when not currently an active follower:

- *(Whine)*
- **What's wrong? You want to go hunt some vampires?**
- *(Bark)*
- **Come on boy, let's go.**
- *(Bark)*
- **Sorry, maybe next time.**
- *(Growl)*
- **I've already got an animal with me, maybe next time.** (If another animal follower is currently following you)
- *(Growl)*

As a follower:

- **Wait here.**
- *(Whine)*
- **Follow me.**
- *(Bark)*
- **Time for you to head home.**
- *(Whine)*
