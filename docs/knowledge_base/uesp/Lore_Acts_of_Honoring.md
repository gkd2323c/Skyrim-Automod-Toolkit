Acts of Honoring **by [Nisswo Xeewulm](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Nisswo_Xeewulm)** *A poem dedicated to Sithis*
---
Through fields you till
Through broken earth
Through crops that you will grow
Honor [that which is change](Lore_Sithis.md)

Hear nothing words
Hear many tongues
Hear one truth within it all
Honor that which is void

With bloodied fang
With crushing blow
With your last stuttering breath
Honor that which is death
