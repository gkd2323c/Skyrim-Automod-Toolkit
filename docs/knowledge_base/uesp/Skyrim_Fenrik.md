[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Fenrik.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Fenrik.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fenrik.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Fenrik.jpg) Fenrik Fenrik is an ex- [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) who has contracted [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md). He is the leader of a Daedric cult and has made a pact with [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) to sacrifice nine of his fellow Vigilants in return for a vampirism cure. He has constructed an altar to Molag Bal within a hidden chamber inside [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).

You will be tasked with killing him and his [thralls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Thrall) during the related quest.

He wears a full set of [Vigil Corrupted armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigil_Corrupted_Armor) and wields a random leveled [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace), which can get up to [Daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality. He also carries two random leveled [potions of restore health](Skyrim_Potions.md#Potion_of_Minor_Healing), a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch), and his [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fenrik%27s_Journal).

He knows three spells: [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md).
