Midden Incident Report
---
The missing students were found in [the Midden](Skyrim_The_Midden.md) this morning. Dead, as expected. None of us bothered keeping a detect life charm for the search at this point.

The bodies were found together, each suffering the same deformities; peeled and bubbling skin on the arms and face. [Conjurer's](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) burn, as it's commonly referred to around the College. There's little doubt they were attempting a summoning ritual well beyond their capabilities.

The relic nearby put any doubt in this theory to rest. I admit that I've never seen one like it - a large, segmented sculpture of a gauntlet, the [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) sigil 'O' emblazoned upon the palm. Attempts to move it were in vain. I must show it to Archmage Sedoth during his upcoming visit. Perhaps he will know more.

While we couldn't move the relic, I was able to pry four "rings" from it. I'm sure there's a connection between them and the ritual the students were attempting.

I'll store these in [the Arcanaeum](Skyrim_The_Arcanaeum.md) until we can consult with a conjuration master to learn more.
