## Plants
200 Wild Spiky Grass plants are found in 1 location. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 200 inside [Runoff Caverns](Skyrim_Runoff_Caverns.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))

## Notes
- Wild Grass Pods have the same alchemical properties as normal [Grass Pods](Skyrim_Grass_Pod.md). Combining these two ingredients will therefore result in all four effects being discovered at once.
