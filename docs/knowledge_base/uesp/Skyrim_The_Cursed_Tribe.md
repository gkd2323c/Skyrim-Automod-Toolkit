## Quick Walkthrough
1. (Optional) Hear rumors that something is happening in [Largashbur](Skyrim_Largashbur.md).
2. Help the [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) of Largashbur repel a [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) attack.
3. Speak to [Atub](Skyrim_Atub.md).
4. Bring a bowl of [troll fat](Skyrim_Troll_Fat.md) and a [daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) to Atub.
5. Observe Atub's ritual.
6. Speak with [Chief Yamarz](Skyrim_Chief_Yamarz.md).
7. Meet Yamarz at [Fallowstone Cave](Skyrim_Fallowstone_Cave.md).
8. Protect Yamarz as he journeys to the [Giant's Grove](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Grove.md).
9. Defeat the giant.
10. Take [Shagrol's Hammer](Skyrim_Shagrol%27s_Warhammer.md) back to Largashbur.
11. Place Shagrol's Hammer on the shrine in Largashbur.
12. Collect your [reward](Skyrim_Volendrung.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### Giant Attack
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e4/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_02.jpg) A giant attacking the Orcs at Largashbur The [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) camp is located in [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md) southwest of Riften. As you approach the town for the first time, a [giant](Skyrim_Giant.md) will be actively attacking the Orcs at the gate. It's advisable to hurry in and help distract the giant, as it will kill unnamed Orcs left and right and may kill potential followers such as [Ugor](Skyrim_Ugor.md). After defeating the giant, Ugor and a caster named [Atub](Skyrim_Atub.md) will begin bickering. Atub will apologize to you and explain that their tribe and leader have been cursed. She has a ritual that may be able to remove it, but needs a bowl of [troll fat](Skyrim_Troll_Fat.md) and a [daedra heart](Skyrim_Daedra_Heart.md) to perform it. She'll ask you to retrieve them. Details on where to find them are provided on their individual pages, however the closest place to get both items is in the Jorrvaskr Living Quarters in Whiterun.

### Malacath's Ritual
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/18/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_03.jpg) *"Now, we begin the ritual..."* After you bring the ingredients to Atub, she will enter [Largashbur Longhouse](Skyrim_Largashbur_Longhouse.md) and speak with [Chief Yamarz](Skyrim_Chief_Yamarz.md) about performing the ritual. The Chief isn't thrilled about the ritual, but ultimately agrees to follow her out to the altar. Atub successfully reaches [Malacath](Skyrim_Malacath.md), whose disembodied voice berates Yamarz for his weakness, abhorring Yamarz for being so weak that even giants openly attack his stronghold. Malacath demands that he kill the giant's leader and return with his club as an offering. Yamarz is irked and demands your assistance in his task, promising lots of gold for helping him.

### Hammer Time
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_04.jpg) *"I'll lead the way, but you'd better back me up."* Yamarz will run off towards [Fallowstone Cave](Skyrim_Fallowstone_Cave.md). If you fast travel there you'll meet him at the entrance and avoid the long trek. He leads the way through the cave, which is remarkably spacious, but rather straightforward. [Bears](Skyrim_Bear.md) and [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md) dwell in the cave and pose some threat, but the giant in the huge main chamber should be far enough away to be avoided entirely. Yamarz will charge down several ledges following the flow of a stream to a pool on the lowest level. He will continue to follow the stream deeper into the cave out of the main chamber and into a smaller cavern occupied by a second giant that can't be so easily avoided. Continue down the tunnel to the southeast as it wends its way around to a final small chamber littered with bones, likely from the predation of the two bears that make it their home. After dealing with them, follow the quest marker up the path to the left and out to [Giant's Grove](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Grove.md).

### Yamarz's Fork
Once in the grove, Yamarz will offer to pay you even more gold if you kill the giant yourself and let him take credit. Either way, you're left fighting the giant in a very small area.

| [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/74/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_05.jpg)"Of course, I can't let you leave here alive. Wouldn't want anyone finding out."**Help Yamarz** If you agree, or if you just kill the giant on your own, he will betray you afterwards, saying that no one can ever know the truth of what happened here. He is extremely well-armored and has tremendous blocking ability, but doesn't hit hard. After killing him, Malacath will praise you for removing both the giant and the conniving, weakling chief. He asks you to take [Shagrol's Hammer](Skyrim_Shagrol%27s_Warhammer.md) back to Largashbur and whip the other Orcs back into shape. | [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_06.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_06.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_06.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe_06.jpg)"This should only take a second."**Honor Malacath's Wishes** If you persuade Yamarz to perform his duty, he'll be killed almost immediately by the giant and you'll be left to kill the giant anyway. Malacath will again deride the fallen weakling Chief and send you back to Largashbur. |
| --- | --- |

After defeating the giant, the only way out is back via Fallowstone - only this time you get to the entrance via a long ledge. Just after you enter back into the cave, take the tunnel almost immediately to your left, and follow the ledge round to the left, past the locked chest (unlock and loot, if you've not done so already). Keep going until you see the waterfall ahead of you, then jump down and cross the stream just in front of the waterfall, then follow the route back to the cave entrance.

### Claiming Volendrung
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/58/SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe.jpg/200px-SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-The_Cursed_Tribe.jpg) A gift from Malacath Once you return, speak with Atub and tell her of Yamarz's fate. Malacath will speak once more, naming [Gularzob](Skyrim_Gularzob.md) the new chief. He will command you to place Shagrol's Hammer on the shrine, where it transforms into the fearsome [Volendrung](Skyrim_Volendrung.md), the Hammer of Might. Make sure you take it from the shrine where it rests in the skull's antlers.

## Notes
- Quest stage 10 is never set. It suggests this quest could have originally been started by getting a rumor, but no such rumor exists in the final game or its files.
- Unused dialogue for Atub and Yamarz suggests a slightly different start to this quest, with Atub sending you to speak with Yamarz, and him sending you to an unknown location known as "Malacath's Shrine". Only after conversing with Malacath there, would Atub perform a ritual at the stronghold, which likely would have resulted in the final task for Yamarz, although what this would've entailed is unknown, although it seems likely that the quest from there on would remain the same. An unused talking activator internally known as `DA06Shrine` seems to be the only remnant of this shrine.
- After you finish the quest, you can recruit [Lob](Skyrim_Lob.md), [Ogol](Skyrim_Ogol.md), and [Ugor](Skyrim_Ugor.md) as followers. Be warned, however, that they can die even before you arrive and can die after you finish the quest (see bugs).
- After you complete this quest, you will become [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the Orcs if you were not already.
- If you leave the area without picking up Volendrung, it will be lying on the ground behind the altar upon your return.
- You will need to level up via skill increases to or beyond level 9 for the quest to be eligible to start. You cannot use console commands to get an arbitrarily high level and start the quest.
- Yamarz's quick death against the giant appears [scripted](Skyrim_Scripted_Deaths.md), and only happens if you *don't* say you'll fight the giant yourself. If you say you'll fight the giant, but then get Yamarz involved in the fight afterwards, he will have his normal Health and can be used as a decoy of sorts.
- Fallowstone Cave can be visited and cleared prior to this quest. However, accepting this quest turns it into a giant-themed zone.
- You cannot fast travel directly from Giant's Grove back to Largashbur. You must manually backtrack through Fallowstone Cave via the main entrance. You can fast travel once you exit.

## Bugs
- Triggering combat in Largashbur can block quest progression. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Even after completing this quest and gaining Malacath's forgiveness for the tribe and ridding them of the curse, giants will still attack them after enough time has passed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.
- Atub and Garakh were apparently intended to be merchants after the quest (apothecary and blacksmith respectively), but do not function as merchants themselves. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, addresses this issue. They were assigned the wrong settings. Once fixed, they will be affected by [Master Trader](Skyrim_Master_Trader.md), but not [Investor](Skyrim_Investor.md).
- Upon arriving at Largashbur, the giant may be attacking but the gate may be closed and locked. Ugor may also have been killed by the giants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.5, fixes this bug.

- To get around this, proceed west from the gate along the stronghold's wall and turn left at the end. You can jump over the wall from the slanted support poles between the wall and the adjacent rocky mountain base. The NPCs will then behave as if the gate had been unlocked. Speak to Atub to initiate the quest. She is usually located atop the guard tower next to the gate.
- If you arrive at Largashbur before level 9, the giant will not be attacking. If you then fast travel there later with a follower, ready to fight the giant, you may be able to save Ugor if you kill the giant fast enough.
- Sometimes if you tell Yamarz to kill the giant, the giant will not respond to Yamarz's attacks and will be killed. When you talk to Yamarz he will behave as though you killed the giant for him and attack you. When you strike back he will die instantly. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) If you notice this happening before the giant dies, use the [Console](Skyrim_Console.md) and select the giant, then type `disable`, hit Enter, then type `enable`. This should reset the giant and get him to attack.
- It is possible to complete the quest without Yamarz dying by killing the giant yourself and using a [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(effect).md) effect on Yamarz on your way back to Largashbur. The objective for protecting Yamarz remains marked as incomplete, but the quest will still complete. The pacified Yamarz will continue his normal routine at his longhouse. However, every time you revisit the stronghold, you will have to pacify him as he will turn hostile. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, addresses this issue. Yamarz has been made immune to Pacify effects.
- If [Gularzob](Skyrim_Gularzob.md) is killed before the quest ends, Malacath will still name him the new chief as if he was alive. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.8, addresses this issue. He is essential until after completing the quest.
- If you kill the giant without making a deal with Yamarz, Malacath will incorrectly say that the giant took care of Yamarz. **Mod Notes**: The check is only if Stage 100 is completed, which only occurs if you actually make a deal with Yamarz.
- If you kill the giant and Yamarz, Malacath may say that *"Giants took care of Yamarz"*, and if you allow the giant to kill Yamarz, Malacath may say that you took care of both "giants" and Yamarz.
- After placing Shagrol's Hammer on the shrine, Volendrung may not appear. Try loading a previous save and attempting again.
- It seems that transforming into a werewolf renders you unable to transform back while in Giant's Grove, which can make the fight against the giant (and potentially Yamarz as well) much more difficult. Once you leave Giant's Grove, you can use the wait function (1-2 hours is enough) to transform back; until that point, attempting to wait will give you a "you can't wait in this location" message.
- Unlike most companions in quests, if you hit Yamarz by mistake even once at any point during the quest (before he attacks you), it may count as an assault and earn you a 40 gold blood price owed to the Orcs. You can yield and pay Yamarz right away, though the gold won't be found on him when he dies. If this occurs in front of witnesses, however (such as during a random encounter passing through [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)), you will also get a bounty in [The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md). <sup>**?**</sup>
- If you attack Yamarz early, then kill the giant you have to kill in the quest, then return to the stronghold you can finish the quest but if you wait a few days or so Yamarz will return. Both he and Gularzob will act like they're the chief.
- Sometimes after bringing the required items to Atub for her to complete the ritual, she will walk up her tower and do absolutely nothing. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) A workaround on PC is to stand at the longhouse door and use the `moveto.player` console command on Atub.
- It is possible that Yamarz will be on the upper ledge when entering the cave. - You can climb the cliff to the ledge he is on and bypass most of the cave to arrive in Giant's Grove.
