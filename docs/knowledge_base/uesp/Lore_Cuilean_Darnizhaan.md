[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/61/ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg/200px-ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Seat_of_Detritus.jpg) Detritus [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/ON-npc-Loremaster_Celarus.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Loremaster_Celarus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Loremaster_Celarus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Loremaster_Celarus.jpg) Loremaster Celarus Cuilean Darnizhaan was a great sage and member of the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md). In life, they were the Psijic Proctor of Abyssal Fragments.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LHDR-1)</sup> They were quoted by Loremaster [Celarus](Lore_Celarus.md) in his publication *[The Old Ways](Lore_The_Old_Ways.md)*,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOW-2)</sup> as well as by scholarly institutions such as the [School of Julianos](Lore_School_of_Julianos.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFJulianos-3)</sup>

Cuilean believed that no subject is too trivial to warrant research and discovery, and that "the greater scheme of things" is too broad of a concept, hence the creation of their most recited quote. This belief led Darnizhaan down the path to study [Detritus](Lore_Detritus.md), a demi-plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) and the realm of the [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md), [Hollowjack](Lore_Hollowjack.md). Cuilean labeled people constantly targeted by Hollowjack's fear feeding as "Hollowjack's Haunted". Taking cases of his "hauntings" in the Psijic Order's library and their research, they wrote a treatise on Hollowjack called *[Lord Hollowjack's Dread Realm](Lore_Lord_Hollowjack%27s_Dread_Realm.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LHDR-1)</sup>

## Quotes
The quote attributed to Darnizhaan originally derives from their treatise, *Lord Hollowjack's Dread Realm* where they wrote *"The power of knowledge is infinite: the power of ignorance can shatter [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) like [glass](Lore_Glass_(common).md)."*<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LHDR-1)</sup> A shortened form of the quote was cited by both Loremaster Celarus of the Psijic Order and the School of Julianos. In Celarus's *The Old Ways*, the passage appeared as, *"The power of ignorance can shatter ebony like glass."* The School of Julianos preserved a variant reading: *"The power of ignorance can truly shatter [mithril](Lore_Mithril.md) like glass."*<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DFJulianos-3)</sup> The same wording also appeared in certain versions of *The Old Ways*."*<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TOWD-4)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup>*
