[The Song of Pelinal](Lore_The_Song_of_Pelinal.md)
Volume 8: On His Revelation at the Death of the Al-Esh *Pelinal's words at the deathbed of Alessia*
---
*[Editor's Note: This is the oldest and most fragmentary of all the extant Pelinal texts. It is, however, likely closest to the original spoken or sung form of the Song, and therefore has great value despite its brevity. Strangely, it appears that [Pelinal](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) is present at [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md)'s deathbed, although he was killed by [Umaril](Lore_Umaril_the_Unfeathered.md) earlier in the saga (years before Alessia's death). Some scholars believe that this fragment is not actually a part of the Song of Pelinal, but most accept its authenticity although there is still much debate as to its significance.]*

"... and left you to gather sinew with my [other half](Lore_Akatosh.md), who will bring light thereby to that mortal idea that brings [the [Gods](Lore_Aedra.md)] great joy, that is, freedom, which even the Heavens do not truly know, [which is] why our Father, the... [Text lost]... in those first [days/spirits/swirls] before [Convention](Lore_Dawn_Era.md#Convention)... that which we echoed in our earthly madness. [Let us] now take you Up. We will [show] our true faces... [[which eat] one another in amnesia each Age."](Lore_Kalpa.md)
