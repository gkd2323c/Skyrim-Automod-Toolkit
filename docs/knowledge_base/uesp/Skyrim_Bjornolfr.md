[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-npc-Bjornolfr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bjornolfr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjornolfr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjornolfr.jpg) Bjornolfr **Bjornolfr** is a dead [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) found in a hidden Dwemer room at the back of [Hrodulf](Skyrim_Hrodulf.md)'s [house](Skyrim_Hrodulf%27s_House.md). When entering the house, you may hear some [reavers](Skyrim_Reaver.md) laughing about a [letter](Skyrim_Letter_(Dragonborn).md) which Bjornolfr wrote to Hrodulf before his death. This letter reveals that Bjornolfr was concerned for his lover, that he wished Hrodulf would return to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), and that he intended to come to Solstheim if that didn't happen. When you find Bjornolfr's body, he carries a [bloodstained letter](Skyrim_Bloodstained_Letter.md) from Hrodulf warning him not to come there.

Bjornolfr wears [novice robes of Conjuration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Robes_of_Conjuration), a [novice hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Novice_Hood), and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c). His body is surrounded by a few coins and a [Nordic dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nordic_Dagger).
