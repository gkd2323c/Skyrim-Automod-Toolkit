(Transcribed from the oral tradition by Varana Tappo, Imperial Scribe serving the Longhouse Emperors.)

Give heed, [blood kin](Lore_Reachman.md)
Hear us sing out
Our time falls short
As shadows grow long
Mighty [spirits](Lore_Spirit.md)
With wide eyes wait
[Wolf](Lore_Wolf.md) fangs stay sharp
To save the pack

Give heed, [Great Beast](Lore_Hircine.md)
Hear us sing out
With sinew tough,
And muscle tight
Through us give strength
And our spirit shall pour
Upon the dirt
For your reward

Give heed, [Black Fly](Lore_Namira.md)
Hear us sing out
Filled with nothing
Know our hunger
Decay's vigor
And Void's might
They empty our chests
Of soul or need
