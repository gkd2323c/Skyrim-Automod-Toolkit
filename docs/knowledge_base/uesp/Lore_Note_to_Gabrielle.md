Note to Gabrielle **by [Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md)** *Vanus Galerion's report on what's occurring in eastern Solstice*
---
[Gabrielle](Lore_Gabrielle_Benele.md),

Imprisonment has not dampened my curiosity or my desire to constantly expand the scope of my knowledge. And it helps that many of my captors like to hear the sounds of their own voices. I was able to secure these sheets of parchment and a quill and ink from a lazy guard. Still, I best not tarry. Let me tell you what I have learned.

First, as I think I made clear when I was able to contact you through my staff, the [Worm Cult](Lore_Worm_Cult.md) has started a new [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), in reverse! They are dragging chunks of [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) to this isolated island! That's not all. They have plans to expand their efforts throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) —perhaps all of [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md)!

Second, I met the cult's current leader, [Wormblood](Lore_Wormblood.md), but the next time he came to see me, he was possessed by [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md)! Somehow, some way, that damn necromancer has found a way to return.

Third, the soul reapers that killed [Merric](Lore_Merric_at-Aswala.md) and that our [friend](Lore_The_Vestige.md) and I discovered in that worm nest before I was captured. Mannimarco has been working his mages ceaselessly on improving the design. And by improving, I mean making more deadly. These new reapers, called soul flayers, will expedite the harvesting of [souls](Lore_Soul.md). They are no longer passive collectors. These devices, once activated, kill all living things in the surrounding area by releasing necrotic bursts of energy. Then they harvest the newly expunged souls like the reapers before them.

Fourth, I actually got Mannimarco to tell me where the harvested souls go upon collection. (He never could resist gloating to a captive audience.) The souls are sent to an ancient Daedric crucible called the Wraithforge, where they are converted into pure, usable energy. From there it gets confusing. He claimed the Wraithforge was part of Mor Naril, a [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) temple somewhere on this island. But he also intimated that it is in Coldharbour, and he's using the Planemeld to retrieve it.

Fifth, the flayers use an improved power core created from perfect [bismuth](Lore_Bismuth.md) crystals. These are being mined at the Kaz-Neesak Mine.

Sixth, I am about to be moved to a new location. I do not know where.

I convinced my guard, Baar-Jasa, to take this note and get it to you. Use this information to find a way to overcome these soul flayers.

[Vanus Galerion](Lore_Vanus_Galerion.md)
