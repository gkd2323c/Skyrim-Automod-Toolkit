[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/ON-place-Green_Hall.jpg/200px-ON-place-Green_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Green_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Green_Hall.jpg) Green Hall circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Green Hall** is a forest region situated in the northeast of the region of [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md), within the province of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). Green Hall is the overall center of Grahtwood, as the [Graht-oak](Lore_Graht-oak.md) cities of [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md) and [Cormount](Lore_Cormount.md) are found anchored at each side of the region, both of which have extensive roles in the province's history. The main road runs between Elden Root, located in the dead-center of Grahtwood, to the trading settlement of Cormount, found on the border near the region of [Reaper's March](Lore_Reaper%27s_March.md). The road also diverges west towards the [Tarlain Heights](Lore_Tarlain_Heights.md), where it heads to the rest of Valenwood. Minor townships such as [Karthdar](Lore_Karthdar.md) are found between Cormount and Elden Root. It also goes south into the [Long Coast](Lore_Long_Coast.md). Beyond the road is open-land, primarily occupied by Bandits and other criminals. The ruins of the Reliquary of Stars and Ossuary of Telacar are found in the area.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the Tower of [Vastarie](Lore_Vastarie.md) in Green Hall served as a center of [magical](Lore_Magic.md) learning, where the [lich](Lore_Lich.md) Vastarie taught her particular brand of [necromancy](Lore_Necromancy.md) to a number of apprentices. Vastarie disappeared circa [2E 485](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_485), when she was lured into the lost [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) [ruins](Lore_Ayleid_Ruins.md) of Laeloria beneath her tower and became trapped within by the forces of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TLo V-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Green Hall was present in the area, being under the rule of Lady Sinia. It had a rivalry with Stonesquare.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including Eagle Vale in the southeast, [Southpoint](Lore_Southpoint.md) in the southwest, and [Wasten Brukbrook](Lore_Wasten_Brukbrook.md) in the north.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Map Arena-5)</sup>
