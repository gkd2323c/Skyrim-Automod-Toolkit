Incriminating Letter **by D.M.**
---
*The first paragraph is nearly identical to [another note](Skyrim_Incriminating_Letter_(Anuriel).md) of the same name, penned by [Maven Black-Briar](Skyrim_Maven_Black-Briar.md), which * is * found in the game. The signature "D.M." on this note likely stands for [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), with whom Maven is closely associated.*

---
<Alias=Jarl> has shown great wisdom in choosing you as <Alias.Pronoun Pos=Jarl> Steward. A position well befitting your character, as it demands great cunning, grace, and, shall we say, a decided talent for performing one's duties tactfully and with much discretion.

The Jarl wasn't acting in <Alias.Pronoun Pos=Jarl> best interest by turning down my gracious offer. But you were able to see past <Alias.Pronoun Pos=Jarl> short-sightedness. In the end, I'm quite sure the Jarl will thank you for your cleverness in this matter.

I am in your debt. Commensurate with your continued cooperation, I grant you a boon.

It is only a matter of time before our efforts bear fruit.

--D.M.
