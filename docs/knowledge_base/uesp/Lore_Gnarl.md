Gnarls, also known as **Walking Trees**, are one of the strangest creatures of the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md). Like the [Elytra](Lore_Elytra.md), this animated plant roams almost anywhere on the Isles. Said to be one of [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s truly unique creations, the Gnarl has the most unusual trait of being able to use magic cast upon it, and harness that power to bolster its own defense, becoming resistant to just that element for a short time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SB-1)</sup>

There is much not understood about them, such as whether they have genders and how they propagate.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LW-2)</sup> They are believed to be caretakers of the Shivering Isles' [Root System](Lore_Root.md), severe trauma to the root system walls results in the formation of [amber](Lore_Amber.md) deposits as part of its natural defense mechanism, Gnarls clear away the amber and sometimes bring it to the surface.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> They also have a truce of sorts with other creatures of the Isles such as the [Baliwog](Lore_Baliwog.md) and [Elytra](Lore_Elytra.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LW-2)</sup> They seem to have some relation to [Spriggans](Lore_Spriggan.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and are sometimes classified with them.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LG-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Utter-Leyton-6)</sup>

[Gnarl bark](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gnarl_Bark) is often harvested from the bodies of Gnarls for use in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-7)</sup> The bark is sometimes turned into gnarl-fiber to be used as shoelaces. Such laces can still be alive and have been known to attack people. With sufficient hydration and nutrients, it's possible a whole new Gnarl could be grown from just these fibers.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESODancing Shoes-8)</sup>
