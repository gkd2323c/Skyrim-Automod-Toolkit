[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/92/ON-furnishing-Sea_Elf_Galleon_Helm.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Sea_Elf_Galleon_Helm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sea_Elf_Galleon_Helm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Sea_Elf_Galleon_Helm.jpg) Sea Elf Galleon Helm **Admiral** Uhlchesis was a [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) cultist of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) and a famous sailor known as the scourge of the [Eltheric Ocean](Lore_Eltheric_Ocean.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SEGH-1)</sup>

At some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Uhlchesis was an active raider in the region of the Eltheric Ocean. His fleet was known to raid the shores of Alinor more than a dozen times before Hermaeus Mora called him to [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). According to scholars of the [Antiquarian Circle](Lore_Antiquarian_Circle.md) of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md) Uhlchesis and his fleet sacked enough gold for a hundred galleon helms. Soon after Uhlchesis was summoned to Apocrypha most of the ships of his fleet were thought to be lost.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SEGH-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) a member of the University of Gwylim's Antiquarian Circle tracked down and discovered a galleon helm, that was believed to once belong to Uhlchesis' fleet. It once graced the deck of one of the Maormer raiding ships and forged from gold looted from the [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SEGHD-2)</sup> Although typically [Pyandonean](Lore_Pyandonea.md) it was more extravagant and lacked traditional serpent head imagery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SEGH-1)</sup> It was instead adorned by the tentacle motif.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SEGHA-3)</sup>
