[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/ON-map-Cambray_Hills.jpg/200px-ON-map-Cambray_Hills.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Cambray_Hills.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Cambray_Hills.jpg) Map of Cambray Hills circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Cambray Hills** is the central subregion of the region of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md) in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md). The settlements of the Cambray Hills are a hodgepodge various different Breton cultures, with the woodlands of Cambray being well-known for being haunted and being the center of Glenumbra's various superstitions and myths. The city-state of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) is situated on the northern hills that overlook the area, it is also known as the West Center of Glenumbra, housing the great aristocrats and poets of the region, as well as the famed Cambray Hills Academy of Art.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cont Academy-1)</sup>

The port town of [Aldcroft](Lore_Aldcroft.md) is prominent in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md), serving as a gateway for resources between the Iliac and Camlorn. Another village serving the same purpose for the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) is the hamlet of [Eagle's Brook](Lore_Eagle%27s_Brook.md). On the southern end of the area is the [Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Glenumbra_Moors.md), known for the [Battle of Glenumbra Moors](Lore_Battle_of_Glenumbra_Moors.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
