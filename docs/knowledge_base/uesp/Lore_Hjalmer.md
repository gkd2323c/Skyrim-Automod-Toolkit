Hjalmer was the High King of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup> and the fourteenth monarch descended from [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md) and his [line](Lore_Ysgramor_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup> He reigned for only one year, from [1E 221](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_221) to [1E 222](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_222) until his passing. He was preceded by [Harald](Lore_Harald.md) and succeeded by [Vrage](Lore_Vrage.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TDC-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup> both of whom are more remembered for their contributions to history than Hjalmer.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup>

## Notes
- Hjalmer's relationship with Harald and Vrage is not explicit. *[The Daggerfall Chronicles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Daggerfall_Chronicles)* claims that Vrage was his youngest son, however, the [King Edward](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:King_Edward) historical fiction says Vrage was Harald's second son.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KEX-3)</sup> If Hjalmer was actually his brother, it would corroborate the fact that three of Harald's sons managed to outlive him, with the other two known sons being Vrage<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KEX-3)</sup> and [Olmgerd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Olmgerd).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWEnnbjof-4)</sup>
