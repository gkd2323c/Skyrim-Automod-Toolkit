## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Garan Marethi](Skyrim_Garan_Marethi.md) or [Fura Bloodmouth](Skyrim_Fura_Bloodmouth.md).
2. Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) leaders at [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md).
3. Return to Garan or Fura.

## Detailed Walkthrough
After completing [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md), [Garan Marethi](Skyrim_Garan_Marethi.md) or [Fura Bloodmouth](Skyrim_Fura_Bloodmouth.md) in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) may offer this quest. You will be asked to kill the leaders of the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) to "avoid an all-out war". Upon nearing [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md) in [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md), several Dawnguard members will turn hostile and attack you. Make your way into the fort. It is best to try and kill the leaders as stealthily as possible because in groups they can be difficult to defeat. [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md) also uses a powerful spell called [Stendarr's Aura](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Aura) that will drain your health if you come into melee range, but only if you are a vampire. Once you have defeated the leaders of the Dawnguard, return to Garan or Fura for your reward. He or she will congratulate you on killing the leaders of the Dawnguard, but comment that the Dawnguard will still remain active even with its leaders dead.

## Notes
- All NPCs within Fort Dawnguard must be eliminated in order to complete this mission. There are eight in total: [Agmaer](Skyrim_Agmaer.md), [Beleval](Skyrim_Beleval.md), [Celann](Skyrim_Celann.md), [Durak](Skyrim_Durak.md), [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md), [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md), [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md), and [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard).
- It is not required that you kill the Dawnguard members patrolling outside the Fort. [Sceolang](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sceolang) and [Bran](Skyrim_Bran.md) also don't need to be killed.
- During this quest, Isran, Sorine Jurard, Florentius Baenius and Gunmar is no longer considered essential and can be killed.
- The Dawnguard members will be unaware of your presence at first, but once they're alerted, they will immediately attack on sight due to you being a Vampire Lord. This is despite the fact that Isran met you during [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(quest).md) and sent you to [Dimhollow Crypt](Skyrim_Dimhollow_Crypt.md) during [Awakening](Skyrim_Awakening.md), indicating he must have assumed that you became a vampire yourself, forcing him to rebuild the order alone.
- You can use [Calm](Skyrim_Calm.md) or [Pacify](Skyrim_Pacify_(spell).md) on [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) and [Florentius Baenius](Skyrim_Florentius_Baenius.md) to buy the [new Restoration spells](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Magic.md#Restoration_Spells) and [bolts](Skyrim_Bolt.md) from them. This can be repeated until they are killed.

## Bugs
- Both Garan and Fura refer to Fort Dawnguard being in the Reach, when it's actually in the Rift. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 1.1.3, fixes this bug.
- The bodies of the Dawnguard members are never cleaned up once this quest is finished. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dawnguard Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dawnguard_Patch), version 2.1.1, fixes this bug.
- Celann may have 15 crossbows on his corpse, which is clearly an error as it was a copypasta matching the amount of steel bolts he's given. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
- Isran's "essential" flag might not get removed, meaning he can't be killed. - Use the `setstage DLC1RV10 100` or `setstage DLC1VQ01Misc Objective 250` console command to bypass this.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage DLC1RV10 stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest DLC1RV10`.
