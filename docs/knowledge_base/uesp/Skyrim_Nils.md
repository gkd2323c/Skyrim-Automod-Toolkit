[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/27/SR-npc-Nils.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nils.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nils.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nils.jpg) Nils, chopping firewood **Nils** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) working at [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md) in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) as a cook and caretaker. He takes over running Candlehearth Hall if anything happens to [Elda Early-Dawn](Skyrim_Elda_Early-Dawn.md).

Nils owns a room inside Candlehearth Hall, on the ground floor, and sleeps there every night between 10pm and 8am. Immediately after waking up, he spends two hours with his axe on the [wood chopping block](Skyrim_Wood_Chopping_Block.md) near the market. At 10am he carries the chopped wood all the way to the Gray Quarter and unloads it in front of [Sadri's Used Wares](Skyrim_Sadri%27s_Used_Wares.md). At noon he once again chops wood, and at 2pm he carries the wood to the [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) outside the [Blacksmith Quarters](Skyrim_Blacksmith_Quarters.md). At 4pm he finally enters Candlehearth Hall and will spend the rest of his day in the kitchen area, cooking, relaxing, or wandering around.

Nils wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) along with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings give a bit of insight into his character:

- *"Name's Nils, I'm the cook up at Candlehearth Hall."*
- *"Can't just throw the big logs on the fire. Got to have the little bits, too."*
- *"When I'm cutting wood, I pretend there's an [Imperial soldier](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)'s head on the stump."*

## Notes
- Elda may mention him in a conversation with [Brunwulf Free-Winter](Skyrim_Brunwulf_Free-Winter.md), saying *"Nils is a hard worker, but I wouldn't trust him to run things."*
- Nils wrote the book *[Chaurus Pie: A Recipe](Skyrim_Chaurus_Pie__A_Recipe.md)*.
- If he takes over Candlehearth Hall, he will not stand behind the counter. You can, however, acquire his innkeeper services around the clock, simply by finding him.
