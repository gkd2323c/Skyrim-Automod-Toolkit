[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-npc-Namasur.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Namasur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Namasur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Namasur.jpg) Namasur **Namasur**, a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [destruction mage](Skyrim_TrainerDestructionExpert.md), is a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) living in [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md).

Namasur wears a set of black colored [vampire armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Armor.md) with a matching pair of [boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md). He is equipped with a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality. He carries random [leveled bandit loot](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md), as well as a 25% chance of carrying 1-9 gold.

If you sided with the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md), he will be one of the vampires outside the front gate during the quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md)**: Confront [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md).

## Dialogue
His potential greetings are as follows:

- *"I'm a loyal servant of the lord of the castle, nothing more."*

- *"The feast never ends here in Castle Volkihar."*

- *"Sit down and enjoy yourself."*

- *"There's plenty for all."*

## Notes
- Although his class is as an Expert-level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) trainer, he does not offer any.
- If the vampires are joined, Namasur may never leave the dining table, instead sitting there for eternity.
