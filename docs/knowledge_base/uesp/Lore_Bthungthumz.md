[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/72/MW-place-Bthungthumz.jpg/200px-MW-place-Bthungthumz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Bthungthumz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Bthungthumz.jpg) Bthungthumz in the Third Era [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/81/ON-load-Ald_Carac.jpg/200px-ON-load-Ald_Carac.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Ald_Carac.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Ald_Carac.jpg) Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Bthungthumz**, also known as **Ald Carac**, is a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) ruin located in the northwestern section of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). They served as Dwarven fortifications that date to the War of the First Council, early in the First Era.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONAld Carac-1)</sup> It was also referred to as a village.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BD-2)</sup>

It was occupied by the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) to serve as a site for bloodsport.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOBGs-3)</sup> In the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), an adventurer was tasked by [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) with retrieving the ring of [Dahrk Mezalf](Lore_Dahrk_Mezalf.md), a dwarven spectre that was said to have worked as a smith under the great Dwemer inventor [Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BD-2)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWDM-4)</sup>
