[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7b/SR-item-Nightingale_Boots.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Boots.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Boots.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Boots.jpg) Nightingale Boots The Nightingale Boots are part of a set of armor given to you for joining the [Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). Its enchantment grants a [Muffle](Skyrim_Muffle_(effect).md) effect. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Nightingale Boots requires [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

The full set of Nightingale equipment also includes [armor](Skyrim_Nightingale_Armor.md), [gloves](Skyrim_Nightingale_Gloves.md), and a [hood](Skyrim_Nightingale_Hood.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Trinity Restored](Skyrim_Trinity_Restored.md)**: Become one of the Nightingales, [Nocturnal](Skyrim_Nocturnal.md)'s guardians.

## Notes
- The enchantment magnitude of the highest-leveled boots is superfluous, as no Muffle effect greater than 1 point will make them any more effective.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Nightingale_Boots)*]</sup>

## Bugs
- The magnitude of 35 for the muffle enchantment on the highest level version of the boots is not in line with the other two. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, addresses this issue. It changes the muffle magnitude to 0.75.
