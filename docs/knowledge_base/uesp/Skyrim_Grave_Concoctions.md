## Walkthrough

### Exterior
There is a wooden bench in front of the house and a patch of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) mushroom growing to the right. It is difficult entering with [stealth](Skyrim_Stealth.md) due to a [guard](Skyrim_Falkreath_Guard.md) being posted opposite outside the Jarl's Longhouse.

### Grave Concoctions
The store is run by [Zaria](Skyrim_Zaria.md), a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) immigrant that moved to Falkreath to explore her love of [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md). The store's name honors the large cemetery - a tradition shared by many of the businesses in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md), such as [Corpselight Farm](Skyrim_Corpselight_Farm.md) and [Dead Man's Drink](Skyrim_Dead_Man%27s_Drink.md) - although she always comments on the name, showing concern that it might put you off.

Upon entering, the counter is immediately on your left, on which there is a bottle of [alto wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Alto_Wine), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), a large [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) and several [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md) that can be bought or stolen, while under the counter is a copy of *[On the Great Collapse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:On_the_Great_Collapse)* and a [giant's toe](Skyrim_Giant%27s_Toe.md). Behind the counter in the southwestern corner of the building is an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) and [food](Skyrim_Food.md) barrel, which is where Zaria normally spends her time making [poisons](Skyrim_Poison.md) and potions. Beside the lab is a basket containing a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) and, against the east wall, a cupboard holding several potions and [ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) that she sells, along with a [gourd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd). Hanging from the ceiling above the cupboard are two [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, a bunch of [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md) and a bunch of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). Opposite the door is a fireplace, with several barrels containing ingredients to the left. On the hearth is a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe) and on the mantelpiece there are a few poisons and potions on display.

To the right of the fire is another cupboard, above this are three more bunches of elves ear and two more of frost mirriam. The cupboard only holds clutter. To the right of the cupboard obscured by baskets is a copy of the [Alchemy](Skyrim_Alchemy.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[De Rerum Dirennis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:De_Rerum_Dirennis)*. Coming out from the middle of the west wall is an owned single bed, with an end table to the left and a dresser to the right. On the end table is a bottle each of wine and alto wine, while on the dresser is a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace). To the right against the north wall is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) containing minor loot. To the right of this is a round wooden table and two chairs, holding a bottle of wine on one and a bottle of wine and a loaf of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) on the other.

## Notes
- The name for the inside of the store appears as "Watchman's Hut" on the local map. This is due to the location data present being `Falkreath Watchmans Hut Location`. This implies the building might've had a completely different purpose altogether originally.
- Grave Concoctions [also appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Grave_Concoctions) in [Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends).
