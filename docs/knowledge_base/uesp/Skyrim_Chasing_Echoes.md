## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak to [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md).
2. Explore [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md).
3. Investigate the Moondial.
4. Explore Castle Volkihar's Ruined Tower.
5. Locate Valerica's Journal.
6. (If not a vampire) Become a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) or get partially soul trapped.
7. Enter the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

[Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) will speak to you after the [Moth Priest](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) reads the Elder Scroll, saying she may know where to find one of the scrolls. She thinks her mother, [Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica), may have information on it, and may even possess it. Serana believes Valerica may have left a clue in the [courtyard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Courtyard) at [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md). Though you will not be able to enter through the Keep, Serana knows of a secret entrance you can use. If you have a follower at this point, you must dismiss them. Simply telling them to wait is not enough: Serana won't accompany you unless you send your follower home.

When you reach the castle, head northwest, along the coastline. When you reach the castle's old docks, you will be attacked by four high-powered [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md). Use the secret entrance to the [undercroft](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Undercroft) and fight your way through [death hounds](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md), [skeevers](Skyrim_Skeever.md), skeletons, and a [feral vampire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Feral_Vampire).

After dealing with the vampire, you will arrive at a stone archway with a raised drawbridge. Head up a set of stairs on the opposite corner of the room to find the lever needed to lower the bridge. Cross the bridge and take a left as instructed by Serana: this leads into an open area inhabited by death hounds. Climb the stairs in the southern section and pass through a tunnel leading to a room with a [giant frostbite spider](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Giant_Frostbite_Spider). You can snipe at the spider from the tunnel, as it's too large to enter the tunnel. Once you've dispatched it, pull a lever to lower another bridge, giving you access to the courtyard.

The [courtyard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Courtyard) itself is abandoned and derelict, and Serana will immediately tell you there is something wrong with the moon dial. You will need to find the three missing pieces and place them in the moon dial in order for a secret entrance to open.

- The [Half Moon Crest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Half_Moon_Crest) is in the center of a pond lying against a rock in the northwest corner of the courtyard.
- The [Full Moon Crest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Full_Moon_Crest) is behind a patch of nightshade lying against the castle's exterior wall in the fenced off area identified as Valerica's garden.
- The [Crescent Moon Crest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crescent_Moon_Crest) is up the stairs, to the right of the entrance to [Volkihar North Tower](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_North_Tower) lying on the floor beside a table and chairs.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-quest-Chasing_Echoes_05.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Chasing_Echoes_05.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Chasing_Echoes_05.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Chasing_Echoes_05.jpg) Serana opening the portal to the Soul Cairn Once all pieces are back in place, the dial will swivel to reveal steps leading down to the [ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_Ruins) below the castle. Fight your way past more [gargoyles](Skyrim_Gargoyle.md) and skeletons until you reach a room with gargoyles and a fireplace. Serana will tell you there must be a secret entrance somewhere, and sure enough, you can either turn the candlestick near the fireplace or pull the chain behind the gargoyle to reveal a secret door. Note that pulling the chain will unleash the stone gargoyle. There is also a [vampire royal armor](Skyrim_Vampire_Royal_Armor.md) near the shelf to the right.

Head through the secret door to [Valerica's study](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Study). Here you will need to search for her [journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica%27s_Journal), in the central bookcase west of where you entered. After Serana reads it, she will tell you to find certain ingredients in the laboratory:

- The [Finely Ground Bone Meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Finely_Ground_Bone_Meal) is on the table by the entrance beneath the mammoth skull.
- The [Soul Gem Shards](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Shards) are on the first wardrobe up the stairs that lead east.
- The [Purified Void Salts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purified_Void_Salts) are on a shelf with other ingredients, near the farthest point of the raised area of the room, on the balcony above the entrance.

All of these items are in silver bowls, and will be added to your miscellaneous inventory when you pick them up, as they are considered quest items. Note that "regular" [bonemeal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemeal), [soul gem fragments](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Gem_Fragment), and [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) will not work.

Once you have all three, place them in the vessel in front of the portal, which Serana is typically standing next to. She will then add her blood to the mixture, opening a portal to the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md).

If you are vampire (whether by siding with the Volkihar or through "regular" infection when siding with the Dawnguard), you will be able to enter straight away. If you are not a vampire, you will suffer some health damage when attempting to use the portal. Serana will explain that to enter the Soul Cairn you must be "dead" and then give you a choice:

- Allow her to turn you into a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).
- Allow her to trap part of your soul in a soul gem. - This is the only way to enter the Soul Cairn without becoming a vampire and will add the [Weakened Soul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Weakened_Soul) effect, which reduces your health, stamina, and magicka by 45 points (as well as their regeneration rates) while in the Soul Cairn ([click here](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md) or [here](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md) for workarounds).

Choosing either will allow you to enter the Soul Cairn.

## Notes
- Before entering the Cairn, you can exit the laboratory through a door in the southern corner. This leads to the *Castle Volkihar Balcony*, a small outside area from which you can fast-travel and then return to the Soul Cairn without having to traverse the castle ruins and courtyard again. A foot chest near the door also provides easy access to a container to stash loot while fast-travelling. - If you exit before deciding whether to be become a Vampire Lord or have your soul captured, Serana will remain in the study, even if you fast travel. This presents a small window of opportunity to acquire two followers: leave the castle grounds, recruit a follower and return. Though your new follower won't enter the Castle, they will not be dismissed, and once you complete [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md) they will continue following you, as will Serana. This allows you to have two followers for the rest of the game.
- If playing on [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) (which disables fast travel), the [Become Ethereal](Skyrim_Become_Ethereal.md) Shout can allow the balcony exit to remain a shortcut outside by jumping off the ledge. Otherwise you'll have to backtrack through the dungeons to go back outside.
- Choosing to become a Vampire Lord and then returning to Fort Dawnguard will prompt [Florentius](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Florentius_Baenius), [Sorine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard), or [Gunmar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gunmar) to direct you to [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md), thus beginning the quest [Rising at Dawn](Skyrim_Rising_at_Dawn.md). Other Dawnguard members, including [Isran](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Isran), will regard you with caution and disgust. You will not be able to use the Dawnguard's services until you cure yourself.
- While this quest or [Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md) are active, you will be unable to dismiss Serana from your party. If you are on the Dawnguard side and a [werewolf](Skyrim_Lycanthropy.md), it can be challenging to use Beast Form and have the Savage Feeding perk as Serana tends to raise corpses in battle. This prevents you from feeding unless you manage to feed on raised corpses after they die again but before they turn into a pile of ash.
- From the courtyard you have access to two more zones of the castle - the [east](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_East_Tower) and [north](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Volkihar_North_Tower) towers - which contain further loot.
- Serana may repeat dialogue about her mother being on the other side of the portal if you should decide to leave the ruins before entering the portal.

## Bugs
- When asked to put the ingredients inside the vessel, they will be removed from your inventory, but the vessel will not be filled, and Serana will keep telling you to put the ingredients in, causing the quest to be unfinishable. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) It is possible to manually advance to the next stage of the quest by opening the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) and typing `setstage DLC1VQ04 75` .
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can also be caused by multi-follower mods. Typing 'set playerfollowercount to 0' will let the quest continue normally. Once back out of the Soul Cairn set the value back to the number of followers you have.
- When asked to speak to Serana after putting the ingredients inside the vessel, speaking to Serana does not advance the quest. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) It is possible to manually advance to the next stage of the quest by opening the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) and typing `setstage DLC1VQ04 90`.
- Dead Thralls cannot enter the Volkihar Courtyard. Entering the Courtyard can cause the thrall(s) to vanish from the game after returning from the Soul Cairn. Kill and leave the bodies of the thrall(s) in a safe place before entering the Courtyard.
- When Serana asks for the journal, it might not be removed from your inventory. - It can be dropped or stored once you rescue her mother and return from the Soul Cairn.
- You may not be able to enter the Soul Cairn. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Open the console and type `set DLC1VQ04Soul Trapped to 1`.
- It is possible to get stuck in a loop where Serana tries to turn you into a vampire if you had previously elected to become a vampire and then for some reason did not enter the Soul Cairn to advance the quest. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.6, addresses this issue. The script call that actually performs the bite has been moved from Stage 110 into the dialogue topic itself, which will run whether you'd done this portion before or not.
