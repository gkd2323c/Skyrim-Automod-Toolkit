¥R. (fl. [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864)), whose full name is unknown, was a well-traveled [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) individual with a vague role within the (alleged) [Second Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) as the military attaché for the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md), while his father and uncle were councilors on the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1PR-2)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/LO-misc-Aldmeri-Dominion.jpeg/150px-LO-misc-Aldmeri-Dominion.jpeg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Aldmeri-Dominion.jpeg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-misc-Aldmeri-Dominion.jpeg) A sketch drawn by ¥R ¥R's father was opposed to [Andel Crodo](Lore_Andel_Crodo.md)'s policy of non-intervention, and eventually passed away, but ¥R chose not to follow in his father's footsteps. As punishment for not doing so, ¥R was sent off by his uncle to become the military attaché for the Imperial City during the time of the [Tiber War](Lore_Tiber_War.md). But ¥R's uncle had also advised him to take a sojourn across the Outerlands of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) before taking on his post, which ¥R successfully completed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1PR-2)</sup>

During this sojourn, ¥R acquired a pamphlet of "propaganda", which had been generated by [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s new regime and was entitled *[A Pocket Guide to the Empire and its Environs](Lore_Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition.md)*, describing Tamriel and its many cultures. Throughout his preceding journey in the Outerlands, ¥R wrote his own commentary inside the guide, refuting claims made within it he deemed to be false, as well as noting observations of things he had seen throughout his travels.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1PR-2)</sup> He also included many drawings and sketches of the lands and their people. Notable encounters in his travel include one with Basil, the leader of the [Restless League](Lore_Restless_League.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1HF-3)</sup> and one with an ancient [Greybeard](Lore_Greybeards.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1S-4)</sup>

After finally settling in the Imperial City circa [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864), ¥R forwarded the pamphlet with his additions to his uncle. His sojourn across the world spurred the belief in ¥R that Tiber Septim was still on the ascendant may have truly been the "[Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md)" as foretold, despite what the oracles back home have predicted. Thus, in his aforementioned forward, ¥R included a letter urging his uncle to submit his findings to the full Council, pleading with them to discontinue Andel Crodo's policy of non-intervention and begin pulling for all the [Elder Races](Lore_Mer.md) to unite at last, or face doom at the hands of [men](Lore_Man.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1PR-2)</sup>

## Notes
- A decade and a half after having written the *Pocket Guide to the Empire*, writer [Michael Kirkbride](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Michael_Kirkbride) retroactively coined the idea that ¥R's comment in [The Wild Regions](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition_The_Wild_Region) passage about not being aware of the War with the Trees is somehow indicative of ¥R having been metaphysically deemed an "[Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md)" by the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-PGE1WR-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MK-1)</sup>
