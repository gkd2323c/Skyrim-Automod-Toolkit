[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg/200px-ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Halcyon_Lake.jpg) Halcyon Lake Halcyon Lake (also called **Lake Bjoulsae**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GOTPQ-1)</sup> is a lake on the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) situated in eastern [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), in the [Mournoth](Lore_Mournoth.md) region of northern [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md). The city of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) is not far to the southwest.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONHalcyon-2)</sup> There is a prominent island in the middle of the lake.

Half-submerged near the island are the ruins of [Bisnensel](Lore_Bisnensel.md), an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) city founded by [Laloriaran Dynar](Lore_Laloriaran_Dynar.md), the Last King of the Ayleids, in the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). However, he was forced out by a growing cult of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md), which occupied the ruins for some time afterwards.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BOAR-3)</sup> As the desertification of Bangkorai spread north, the lake was destined to dry up. In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), a local [nereid](Lore_Nereid.md) attempted to avoid this fate by stealing the memories of mortals to create a Water Stone, much to the displeasure of Hermaeus Mora. It is unknown if she succeeded in her plans.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMistress-4)</sup>
