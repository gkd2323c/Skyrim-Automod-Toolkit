**Wrathmen** are [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) -like skeletal [undead](Skyrim_Undead.md) found in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). Compared to the other Soul Cairn enemies such as the [bonemen](Skyrim_Boneman.md) and [mistmen](Skyrim_Mistman.md), wrathmen are much more warrior-like, wearing non-lootable [ancient Nord armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Armor) and wielding leveled two-handed weaponry. The [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell tome [Conjure Wrathman](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Conjure_Wrathman) can be found in the Soul Cairn, which summons a two-handed [dragonbone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon_Weapons) weapon-wielding wrathman for 60 seconds.

## Gallows Hall Creation
The [Gallows Hall](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) lets you summon a permanent Wrathman follower using the [Bone Forge](Skyrim_Bone_Forge.md).

- It is summoned using 1 [Bone Meal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bone_Meal), 1 [Greater Soul Gem](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greater_Soul_Gem), 1 [Frost Salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md), 1 [Soul Husk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Soul_Husk), and 1 [Ancient Nord Helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Nord_Helmet).
- The Wrathman summoned using the forge does not count towards your maximum number of summons, making it a free permanent follower.
- It does not regenerate health and is protected, being downed when its health is too low instead of being outright killed. It can be healed using the [Necromantic Healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necromantic_Healing) and [Heal Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Heal_Undead) spells.

## Notes
- Wrathmen have the highest health of any summoned creature, not including [Creation Club](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) spells.
- While [bonemen](Skyrim_Boneman.md) and [mistmen](Skyrim_Mistman.md) are immune to the [full-powered Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md) Shout, wrathmen are not, becoming ragdolled like Draugr. If you encounter Wrathmen atop a building, you can Shout them off for a quick kill.
- Wrathmen you summon through the spell will always wield Dragonbone two-handed weapons, but wrathmen encountered in the Soul Cairn carry varied two-handed weapons, anywhere from Iron to Daedric. Furthermore, what weapon they carry does not indicate how much health they have, which varies from wrathman to wrathman.
