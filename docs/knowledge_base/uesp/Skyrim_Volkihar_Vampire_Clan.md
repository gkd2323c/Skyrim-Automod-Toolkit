# Volkihar Vampire Clan


## Membership
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-quest-Bloodline.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Bloodline.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bloodline.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Bloodline.jpg) Lord Harkon bestowing his gift
### Joining
Joining the Volkihar vampires requires you to first complete the quest [Awakening](Skyrim_Awakening.md). After completing the quest, [Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md) will begin. After escorting [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md) to Volkihar Keep, [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) will offer you membership within the clan and the ability to become a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md). This is your only opportunity to join the ranks of the clan itself. Upon accepting Harkon's gift, the quest [The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) will begin.

### Benefits
These benefits should be compared to those offered by the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).

- Free access to [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md), which holds several wardrobes and bookcases that are safe containers for storage, as well as mortal [thralls](Skyrim_Vampire_Cattle.md) for safer feeding.
- The ability to transform into a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md) upon immediate membership (can be obtained later if you side with the Dawnguard).
- The [Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md), [Amulets of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power), and [Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic), which can augment your vampiric powers.
- Ability to purchase spells with the [Restore Health to Undead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Restore_Health_to_Undead) effect (one spell can be obtained without joining).
- [Cu Sith](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cu Sith) and [Garmr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Garmr), two [death hound](Skyrim_Death_Hound.md) [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md#Creatures).
- Access to [Fura Bloodmouth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fura_Bloodmouth), a master trainer for [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md), and [Ronthil](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ronthil), an expert trainer for [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md).
- Access to several additional merchants: [Feran Sadri](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Feran_Sadri), [Hestla](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hestla), and Ronthil.
- Endless supply of [radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) quests.

## Quests
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/SR-qico-Dawnguard_Vampires.png/200px-SR-qico-Dawnguard_Vampires.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-qico-Dawnguard_Vampires.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-qico-Dawnguard_Vampires.png) Volkihar knotwork as it appears in the [Journal](Skyrim_Quests.md)
### Primary Quests
The primary Volkihar questline consists of ten quests. Two [achievements](Skyrim_Achievements.md) (60 points; 1 Bronze, 1 Silver) are unlocked by the Volkihar questline.

- **[Bloodline](Skyrim_Bloodline.md)**: Choose your side!
- **[The Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_The_Bloodstone_Chalice.md)**: Fill the [Bloodstone Chalice](Skyrim_Bloodstone_Chalice.md) from the Bloodspring at [Redwater Den](Skyrim_Redwater_Den.md).
- **[Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Vampire).md)**: The time of prophecy has arrived! Locate [a Moth Priest](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md) for the vampires.
- **[Seeking Disclosure](Skyrim_Seeking_Disclosure.md)**: Bring the Elder Scrolls to [Dexion](Skyrim_Dexion_Evicus.md).
- **[Chasing Echoes](Skyrim_Chasing_Echoes.md)**: Explore [Castle Volkihar](Skyrim_Castle_Volkihar.md).
- **[Beyond Death](Skyrim_Beyond_Death.md)**: Search for [Valerica](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Valerica) in the [Soul Cairn](Skyrim_Soul_Cairn.md). ([![Beyond Death](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8c/SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png/20px-SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Beyond_Death.png) 20 pts/Bronze)
- **[Scroll Scouting](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scroll_Scouting)**: Obtain information on the Dragon Elder Scroll.
- **[Unseen Visions](Skyrim_Unseen_Visions.md)**: Perform a ceremony to read the Elder Scroll.
- **[Touching the Sky](Skyrim_Touching_the_Sky.md)**: Find and retrieve [Auriel's Bow](Skyrim_Auriel%27s_Bow.md).
- **[Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md)**: Confront [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md). ([![Kindred Judgement](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1d/SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png/20px-SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-achievement-Kindred_Judgement.png) 40 pts/Silver)

### [Radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md) Quests
- **[Amulets of Night Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amulets_of_Night_Power)**: Retrieve the Amulets of Night Power. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Ancient Power](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ancient_Power)**: Retrieve [vampire parts](Skyrim_Ancient_Vampire_Arm.md) from varied locations around Skyrim. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Culling the Beast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Culling_the_Beast)**: Kill the thin-blooded [vampires](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Deceiving the Herd](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deceiving_the_Herd)**: Frame the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) for murder. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[The Hunt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Hunt)**: Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) spy masquerading as a citizen. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[New Allegiances](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:New_Allegiances)**: Turn the target into a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Protecting the Bloodline](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Protecting_the_Bloodline)**: Destroy the [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) before it turns its allies. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))
- **[Rings of Blood Magic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rings_of_Blood_Magic)**: Retrieve the [Ring of the Erudite](Skyrim_Ring_of_the_Erudite.md) and the [Ring of The Beast](Skyrim_Ring_of_The_Beast.md). ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

### Other Quests
- **[Destroying the Dawnguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Destroying_the_Dawnguard)**: Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) leaders. (This quest becomes available as a [radiant quest](#Radiant_Quests) after successfully completing [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md).)
- **[The Gift](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gift)**: Turn your [spouse](Skyrim_Marriage.md) into a [creature of the night](Skyrim_Vampire.md).

## Notes
- After joining the Volkihar vampires, if you decide to become mortal again by talking to [Falion](Skyrim_Falion.md), upon returning to Castle Volkihar, you will be told that your mortality should be "fixed" and that you should speak to Lord Harkon (or Serana if you have completed the Dawnguard quest line), who will reprimand you for losing his gift and then bite you again, turning you back into a vampire. - However, if you contract [vampirism](Skyrim_Vampirism.md) from a regular vampire (not Serana or Harkon) they will treat you the same as if you were a [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).
- Attacking a member of the clan will make all clan members (except Serana) turn hostile to you. Sheathe your weapon or unequip your magic and they will forgive you this time. If you attack them again, they won't calm down easily.
- Attacking or even killing Cu Sith or Garmr won't make other clan members turn hostile to you. Only the death hound you are attacking will fight back.
