# Hist Sap

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/LG-cardart-Tree_Minder.png/200px-LG-cardart-Tree_Minder.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Tree_Minder.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Tree_Minder.png) A Tree Minder collecting the Hist sap, as seen in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)* Hist Sap is a substance made out of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md)'s very spirit.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TMD-1)</sup> When Hist Sap is licked by an [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md), the Hist is capable of communicating with the Argonian through visions.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup> The sap of the Hist seems to increase the combat prowess of those who drink it. It also has the ability to 'alter' living organisms, which may explain the origin of the Argonians.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TIC-2)</sup> Ingesting large quantities of Hist-Sap is often dangerous, even for Argonians. The [sap-speakers](Lore_Sap-Speakers.md) of the Miredancer Tribe, known for communing with their Hist and ingesting large amounts of sap in the process, often suffer the effects of sap-poisoning, including "gold tongue" (permanent change of mouth pigmentation to a golden hue), "bark scale" (thickening and darkening of surface scales), and unwanted hallucinations among other symptoms.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-To MMD-3)</sup> The [Blackwood Company](Lore_Blackwood_Company.md) smuggled a Hist tree from [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) to their headquarters in [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md), and extracted the sap to exploit this. However, when consumed by non-Argonian species, the Hist sap had strong hallucinogenic properties that sent them into dangerous bloodlust, though the poor treatment of this particular tree affected the sap causing similar effects on Argonians, as well.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> At the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), a recruit of the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) sabotaged the Hist extraction mechanism, destroying the tree in the process.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Oblivion-5)</sup>

A similar substance known as [Amber Plasm](Lore_Amber_Plasm.md) is also produced by certain Hist trees. Despite its appearance, it is not Hist Sap and is instead a form of [chaotic creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) which leaks into [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md) through the Hist like blood from a wound.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-NKJournal-6)</sup>

In [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md), some of the [rubber](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rubber) products were made of the Hist Sap. Among the items made with the use of the Hist sap rubber were telescoping [wamasu](Lore_Wamasu.md) prods. They were properly insulated to protect against the electrical discharges of the wamasu.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Wamasu-7)</sup>
