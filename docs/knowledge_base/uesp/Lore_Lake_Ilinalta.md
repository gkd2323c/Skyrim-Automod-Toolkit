[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-place-Lake_Ilinalta.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lake_Ilinalta.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Ilinalta.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lake_Ilinalta.jpg) Lake Ilinalta Lake Ilinalta is a lake in west-central [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md), in the middle of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s pine forest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Loading-1)</sup> It is the source of the [White River](Lore_White_River.md), and a notable Skyrim landmark.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AEGTS-2)</sup> The town of [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md) was less than a day's ride to the southeast,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AEGTS-2)</sup> and the Hold's capital of [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md) is directly south. The [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) used to have a fort along its shore, until the entire structure mysteriously sank into the water in a single day.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FJID-3)</sup> The ruins became known as [Ilinalta's Deep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ilinalta%27s_Deep). According to local legend, the place is cursed and travelers are known to go missing.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-FJID-3)</sup>
