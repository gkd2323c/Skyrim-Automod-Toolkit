# Blind Man

| Blind Man | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) | Gender | Male |

[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-skill-Curative_Runeforms-Chakram_Shields.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-skill-Curative_Runeforms-Chakram_Shields.png) Blind Man's Runes The Blind Man was an obscure [Apocryphal](Lore_Apocrypha.md) [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) entity associated with the runes of the [Arcanists](Lore_Arcanists.md). One way for an Arcanist to harness their power was by inscribing the runes of the Blind Man to create objects, such as spinning mystical discs, which could grant magical protective shields. Similarly to the [Fate Crone](Lore_Fate_Crone.md) and [Baron of Breakers](Lore_Baron_of_Breakers.md) he was associated with such discs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Chakram_Shields-1)</sup>

Arcanists sometimes attempt to seek power and knowledge from the Blind Man, and [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md) was known to have encountered him in the antihelix of the Wind. He marked that corridor to avoid, deeming Blind Man's knowledge not worth the danger.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Azandar-2)</sup>
