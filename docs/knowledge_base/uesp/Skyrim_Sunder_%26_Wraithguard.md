[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-menu-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg/200px-SR-menu-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-menu-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg) Sunder & Wraithguard [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c8/SR-prerelease-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg/200px-SR-prerelease-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.jpg) Sunder, Wraithguard, and Keening **Sunder & Wraithguard** is a [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) that completes the set of [Kagrenac's Tools](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Kagrenac%27s_Tools) from *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, adding the hammer [Sunder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sunder) and the gauntlet [Wraithguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wraithguard). It was released on December 6, 2018 for 500 Creation Credits.

Some of the art was created by [Elianora](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/3231858).

## Official Summary
*Crafted by the Dwemer Tonal Architect Lord Kagrenac, Sunder and Wraithguard represent two of the three tools created to tap into the Heart of Lorkhan. Imbued with ancient magic, the weapons are said to empower not only their wielder, but each other. Additional Art Support by Elianora. (Quest "Legends Lost" starts by reading the Lost Caravan Guard's Note at the New Gnisis Cornerclub in Windhelm.)*

## Notes
- You may encounter the lost caravan north of Bronze Water Cave even before starting [Legends Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Legends_Lost), because that's a location also used in the quest [Balance of Power](Skyrim_Balance_of_Power.md). However, the chest containing the [Carriage Driver's Note](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Carriage_Driver%27s_Note) will not appear until you begin the quest Legends Lost.
- A render of Wraithguard has been present on the [banner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-prerelease-Creation_Club_Herobanner.png) of the [Creation Club website](https://creationclub.bethesda.net/en) since its launch.
- This Creation received an update in February 2019 which made several texture and skinning fixes for Wraithguard, the left gauntlet that pairs with it, and Sunder.
