[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/AR-place-Moonguard.jpg/200px-AR-place-Moonguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Moonguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Moonguard.jpg) Moonguard Moonguard is a village in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) that is located east of the city-state of [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md). Moonguard was the home of [Gyron Vardengroet](Lore_Gyron_Vardengroet.md), commonly known as the Sage.

## History
The Sage was born some time after the formation of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md). His birth occurred under special circumstances, and he was gifted with an exceptional affinity for [magic](Lore_Magic.md). Recognizing his great talent, the local wizard instructed Gyron in the magical arts. Eventually, after surpassing his master, Gyron left his humble home in Moonguard for the city-state of [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md). In Shornhelm, he received training from the guildmaster, Morkledder, but he soon exceeded the Mages Guild and began to train at the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md). However, after exceeding the Elder of the Council of Wizards in the Crystal Tower, Gyron returned to Moonguard, taking the role of the village wizard. He lived in his hometown for years until the death of his parents. In grief, Gyron spent the rest of his life attempting to learn the secrets of immortality so that grief like his would never happen again. Eventually, he retired to the [Kurallian Mountains](Lore_Kurallian_Mountains.md) near Shornhelm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Moonguard was an active settlement ruled by Lord Tristard. It had a rivalry with [Eagle Brook](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Eagle_Brook) and the [Ebon Wastes](Lore_Ebon_Wastes.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> It was neighbored by several settlements, including [Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn.md) in the northwest and [Kings Guard](Lore_Kings_Guard.md) in the east.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Map Arena-3)</sup>

## Notes
- Moonguard was planned to appear in [the canceled Oblivion game for PSP](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion_(PSP)), where it was to have been attacked by the [Mythic Dawn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Mythic_Dawn) and an [Oblivion Gate](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion_Gate). Later in development these happenings were changed to take place in a city called "Rhalta".
