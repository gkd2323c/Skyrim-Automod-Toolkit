[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/SR-npc-Ondolemar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ondolemar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ondolemar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ondolemar.jpg) Ondolemar at Understone Keep **Ondolemar** is a [Thalmor](Skyrim_Thalmor.md) [Justiciar](Skyrim_Thalmor_Justiciar.md) living in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). Along with two [Thalmor Soldiers](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md), Ondolemar is stationed in the City of Stone to observe and prevent any kind of [Talos](Lore_Talos.md) worship, in accordance to the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md). Ondolemar will ask for your [assistance](Skyrim_Search_and_Seizure.md) to gather evidence from the house of the local [bard](Skyrim_Ogmund.md), a favor he may return during the quest [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md).

Always protected by his two soldiers, Ondolemar spends the entire day patrolling up and down the area in front of the throne room, from the kitchen to the smithy. At midnight he heads up to the northwestern strategy room where he spends six hours eating a nightly meal. If the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) take over Markarth (whether Markarth is given to the Stormcloaks during Season Unending or you take it during the Civil War), Ondolemar will die. His remains will be buried in the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Markarth).md).

Ondolemar wears [Hooded Thalmor Robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Thalmor_Robes) with [Thalmor gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Gloves) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Thalmor_Boots). He knows a variety of spells, specifically [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md), [Flames](Skyrim_Flames.md), [Ice Spike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike), [Lightning Bolt](Skyrim_Lightning_Bolt.md), [Lightning Cloak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Cloak), [Sparks](Skyrim_Sparks.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and [Steadfast Ward](Skyrim_Steadfast_Ward.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### Search and Seizure
**You're not from Markarth, I take it?** *"As if this craggy, wretch of a city could give birth to a superiorly bred Mer such as myself. No, I'm not from Markarth. I was sent here to lead the Thalmor's interests in this corner of Skyrim. It's my mission to root out all Talos worship in this city."* **Why are you after Talos worshippers?** *"It's a religious matter. The Thalmor do not recognize Talos as a god. He was only a man, and does not deserve to a place in our pantheon. The Empire has [agreed to accept our beliefs](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), and its citizens have a responsibility to cease their heretical worship. You're awfully inquisitive, aren't you? I like that. Perhaps you'd care to solve a little problem I'm having?"* **I'm interested. What is it?** *"[Ogmund the skald](Skyrim_Ogmund.md). He's old, respected, and I know for a fact that he worships Talos in his home. But the Jarl has been hesitant to call for his arrest. I want you to break in to his home and find evidence."* **Not right now.** *"Hmph. Fine."* When you return to Ondolemar:

**I found the amulet in Ogmund's house.** *"Ah, there we have it. This will be all I need to have Ogmund taken care of. I would like to personally thank you on behalf of myself and the Thalmor for your assistance. Here, for your work."* He will then hand you some gold and his disposition will be raised, causing him to greet you in a more friendly tone and be willing to help you inside the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md). Furthermore, you'll gain access to the [Thalmor Headquarters](Skyrim_Thalmor_Headquarters.md) without it being considered as trespassing if you enter, and taking anything from there will no longer count as theft.

**What brings you to this party?** *"There are those in the Empire who would wish to evade their obligations to help root out the Talos heresy. Fortunately, those most opposed to the [Emperor's](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Emperor_Titus_Mede_II) wise policy have now branded themselves traitors as well as heretics. I am here to remind the ruling classes of Skyrim that their loyalty to the Emperor requires cooperation with the Thalmor."* **Can there truly be peace between the Thalmor and the Empire?** *"There is peace now, and that peace will continue for as long as it suits our needs. And the next storm, I think, will be far deadlier than the last."* **What are your duties here in Skyrim?** *"I lead the Justiciars. We are charged with enforcing the ban on Talos worship. ## Unused Dialogue
Ondolemar has unused dialogue tied to multiple [quests](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md) he was once meant to give you. This first quest's dialogue can only be found in the game's Dialogue Views files, and not the Creation Kit.

### Freeform Markarth C
This quest would've been started upon talking to Ondolemar about his faction:

**Tell me about the Justiciars.** *"The White-Gold Concordat forbids the worship of Talos in Imperial territory. The Justiciars make sure that law gets enforced. That's why I'm here. If you see anyone worshipping Talos, you're bound by law to report them to me."* Upon returning to him:

**Ogmund is worshipping Talos in his house.** *"Oh, is he? I suspected as much, and now I have a witness. The Justiciars thank you for your cooperation. Now, if you'll excuse me, I have a criminal to bring in."*
### Battle for Markarth
Originally, the Stormcloaks would've fought through Markarth during the civil war, similarly to the Battle of Whiterun. After defeating the jarl and his men, a scene would play out between [Galmar Stone-Fist](Skyrim_Galmar_Stone-Fist.md) and the new replacement jarl, [Thongvor Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thongvor_Silver-Blood.md).

Afterwards, there would be a scene where Galmar would confront and then kill Ondolemar:

**Galmar**: *"Not so fast, Elf!"*
**Ondolemar**: *"I presume you're talking to me?"*
**Galmar**: *"On your knees!"*
**Ondolemar**: *(Ignores him)*
**Galmar**: *"I said on your knees, Elf!"*
**Ondolemar**: *"Enjoy this while you can. Soon you will all be slaves."*
**Galmar**: *"Raagh!" (Attacks and kills Ondolemar)*
**Galmar**: *"Someone get that thing out of here and burn it."*

Instead, after the Stormcloak version of [The Battle for Fort Sungard](Skyrim_The_Battle_for_Fort_Sungard_(Stormcloaks).md) is completed in the final game, the game will simply delete Ondolemar and place his belongings in a coffin in Markarth's Hall of the Dead, implying that the Stormcloak soldiers killed him when they took control of the city.

## Notes
- If you complete [Search and Seizure](Skyrim_Search_and_Seizure.md) by stealing Ogmund's amulet, and then Ondolemar dies, the amulet will be on his body. However, if you loot it from him, it will still be marked as stolen.
- If you have Ondolemar provide a distraction during [Diplomatic Immunity](Skyrim_Diplomatic_Immunity.md), he will no longer use friendly speech towards you and instead speak to you as if you had not completed [Search and Seizure](Skyrim_Search_and_Seizure.md). However, if you complete Diplomatic Immunity first and then Search and Seizure, he will use friendly speech towards you.
- Ondolemar's mouth is unique to him and unavailable for character creation without mods.

## Bugs
- Ondolemar may become hostile to you on sight, which will also cause his two attendant Thalmor soldiers to attack, and may also cause the jarl and his court to turn hostile to you, even if you have no bounty in the Reach. - This may be caused by you attacking a random Thalmor party encountered in the wilderness (even if you kill all witnesses).
- To fix this, reload a previous save and commit a minor crime, such as punching a Markarth guard, then clear the bounty using the usual methods.
- Resurrecting Ondolemar as a [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) will cause his two [Thalmor soldiers](Skyrim_Thalmor_Soldier.md) to follow him, thus following you. Attacking anyone who is not hostile, however, will turn the Thalmor soldiers against you, though Ondolemar will still fight as one of your followers. If you are detected killing either of the Thalmor soldiers, your Reach bounty will increase. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- The Thalmor Soldiers following Ondolemar will continue to respawn if Ondolemar dies or the Stormcloaks take Markarth. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.7, fixes this bug.
