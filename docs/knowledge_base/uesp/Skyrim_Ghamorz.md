[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-npc-Ghamorz.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ghamorz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghamorz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ghamorz.jpg) Ghamorz **Ghamorz** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [miner](Skyrim_Miner_(class).md) who lives in the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) of [Mor Khazgur](Skyrim_Mor_Khazgur.md). He works in [Mor Khazgur Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine), an [orichalcum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore) mine located in the stronghold, with [Gul](Skyrim_Gul.md) and [Olur](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olur).

Ghamorz spends his entire life inside the mine and never sleeps. He divides his time between hacking away on the orichalcum ore veins, relaxing or eating.

He wears a pair of [ragged trousers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Trousers) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699). He carries his [pickaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pickaxe), a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common), and the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Larak's Longhouse](Skyrim_Larak%27s_Longhouse.md).

## Bugs

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, addresses this issue. It adds a 7am to 7pm timer to the work package.
