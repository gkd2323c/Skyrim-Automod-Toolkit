[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Gisl Round-Gut was a [Nord](Lore_Nord.md) chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) in the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>

He became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying the previous chieftain [Einarr](Lore_Einarr.md) and presenting his sword as a battle trophy. He would be killed after two months by [Einarr the Younger](Lore_Einarr_the_Younger.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
