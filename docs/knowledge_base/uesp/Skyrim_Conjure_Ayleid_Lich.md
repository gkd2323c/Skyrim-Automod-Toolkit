**Conjure Ayleid Lich** is an expert level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md). The spell book can be crafted at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) with a [Great Welkynd Stone](Skyrim_Great_Welkynd_Stone.md), a [Ruined Book](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruined_Book) and a [Salt Pile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salt_Pile).

## Effects
- [Conjure Ayleid Lich](Skyrim_Summon.md), for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Atromancy](Skyrim_Atromancy.md), increases duration to 120 secs.
- [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls), lets you have two Ayleid Liches summoned at the same time.

## Notes
- **Conjure Ayleid Lich** is unaffected by [Spell Absorption](Skyrim_Spell_Absorption.md).
- Upon death, the Lich may leave behind an unlootable ash pile.
- This Spell Tome cannot be crafted until [The Consequences](Skyrim_The_Consequences.md) is completed, as the Great Welkynd Stone remains an unremovable quest item until then.
- This Spell Tome does not require the [Sigil Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigil_Stone) to craft.
- Conjured Ayleid Liches are in turn able to use Conjuration spells of their own, and can [raise high-level undead](Skyrim_Dread_Zombie.md) to fight for you such as [Draugr Deathlords](Skyrim_Draugr_Deathlord.md).

## Bugs
- The unlootable ash pile left behind has a chance to not disappear after a few days and will remain there forever.
