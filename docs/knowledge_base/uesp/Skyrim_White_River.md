[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-place-White_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-White_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-White_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-White_River.jpg) The source of the White River **White River** is the longest river in [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim_(place).md), stretching from the south of the province to the northeast corner.

The source of the White River is [Lake Ilinalta](Skyrim_Lake_Ilinalta.md), north of [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md). It flows northeast to join the [Sea of Ghosts](Skyrim_Sea_of_Ghosts.md) past [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). Along the way, it passes through [Riverwood](Skyrim_Riverwood.md) and near [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md). [Darkwater River](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Darkwater_River) and [River Yorgrim](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:River_Yorgrim) are the two main tributaries of the White River.
