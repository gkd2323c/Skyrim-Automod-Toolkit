## Quick Walkthrough
1. Travel to [Yngvild](Skyrim_Yngvild.md).
2. Collect the four journals.
3. Return to [Vekel](Skyrim_Vekel_the_Man.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough

### The Proposition
If you approach Vekel in [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) and ask him for work, he will tell you about some journals that he's been after: *"I have a buyer with a very odd taste in literature, looking for some particular books. I'm told they're the ravings of a madman, a wizard named [Arondil](Skyrim_Arondil.md) and his peculiar cravings. It's said to be in four volumes and very hard to come by. My client is offering quite a reward for them, which I'd split with you."* If you ask for him to elaborate further on the journals, he will go on to say, *"Recently, a woman was found... naked and shivering along the road to [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md). The only information the guard could get from her were tales of strange experiments and Arondil scribbling in his journals."*

Asking about Arondil won't get you much, as Vekel says, *"Only rumors. He used to have a home in Dawnstar until they burned it down. The people say he was doing unspeakable experiments on reanimation... rituals only a necromancer would perform. After he made for Yngvild, he was never heard from again."*

Once you agree to retrieve the journals, he will conclude the conversation with: *"Then we have a deal. In his later years, Andoril * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * moved into some ice caves known as Yngvild. He prefered * [[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)] * the solitude in which to perform his, um... experiments."*

### Yngvild
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-place-Yngvild.jpg/200px-SR-place-Yngvild.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yngvild.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Yngvild.jpg) The ruins of Yngvild Yngvild is an ice cave located on an island northeast of Dawnstar. To access it, find the entrance on the north side by following the pathway up the island. Once inside, you are able to find the first three journals. The [first journal](Skyrim_Arondil%27s_Journal,_Part_1.md) is located on a table near two [Yngvild ghosts](Skyrim_Yngvild_Ghost.md) and a leveled [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md). The [second journal](Skyrim_Arondil%27s_Journal,_Part_2.md) can be found on a table alongside a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) and a [scroll](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Raise_Zombie.md). The [third journal](Skyrim_Arondil%27s_Journal,_Part_3.md) can be found on a table just before the door leading to [Yngvild Throne Room](Skyrim_Yngvild_Throne_Room.md).

### Yngvild Throne Room
Once you enter the main chamber in the throne room, you will see Arondil sitting on his throne with an Yngvild ghost alongside him. The [fourth](Skyrim_Arondil%27s_Journal,_Part_4.md) and final part of the journal lies through the door behind him, on a table along with an [unusual gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md) for the quest [No Stone Unturned](Skyrim_No_Stone_Unturned.md). Once you have retrieved the journals, you can exit the cave and head back to [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) to receive your reward.

### Reward
Return all four journals to Vekel and he will remark, *"Good. I was hoping you hadn't forgotten about it. As promised, here's your payment for bringing them to me. Got it as part of a fair trade from my client."* Your reward for returning the journals is a leveled and [enchanted](Skyrim_Enchanting_Effects.md) [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md).

## Notes
- You don't have to kill [Arondil](Skyrim_Arondil.md) to complete the quest, merely get his journals.
- This quest can be started even if you're not a member of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) at all.
- Sneaking into the final chamber unnoticed and removing the Soul Gem from the pedestal next to Arondil causes the ghosts near him to attack and kill him.
- Objectives for this quest will add themselves to the quest log even if you'd never been to see Vekel about finding the books. - This is no longer the case with version 4.2.4 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch).

## Bugs
- Vekel will neither reward you on completion of the quest, nor take the journals. - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.9.26.0.8, addresses this issue. He will now reward you properly, and the journals will no longer be marked as quest items once the quest is complete, and can be dropped or sold.
- As stated above, Vekel does not actually remove the journals from your inventory upon quest completion, even with the Official Patch. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, fixes this bug.
- This quest can be started without talking to Vekel; if you go to Yngvild and pick up all four journals, you will receive a quest update telling you to return them to Vekel, even if you never spoke to him before. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0, addresses this issue. He has been given appropriate dialogue to acknowledge the fact that he had never given you the task to find the journals prior.
