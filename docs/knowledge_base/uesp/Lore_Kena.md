# Kena

Kena and Kema are honorifics used in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md), seemingly being words in [Dark Elvish](Lore_Dark_Elvish.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THo CX-1)</sup> but it can apply to non-Dark Elves as well.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPMorrowind Moonlight-2)</sup> Kena specifically is used for scholars who have graduated from college (sometimes referred to as *the* college).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THo CX-1)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> The definition of Kema is unknown, but is contextually used to refer to teachers.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THo CX-1)</sup> Kena is placed after a vocative noun or honorific (for example: "Serjo Kena") but before a full name (for example: "Kena Telvanni Hordalf Xyr").<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-THo CX-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TPPlaying Houses-3)</sup>
