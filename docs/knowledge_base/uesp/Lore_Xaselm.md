[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ee/SI-place-Xaselm.jpg/200px-SI-place-Xaselm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Xaselm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SI-place-Xaselm.jpg) Xaselm at the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) Xaselm is a ruin in the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), located atop a hill northeast of [the Fringe](Lore_The_Fringe.md), close to the Gates of Madness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup> Like the other ancient ruins that dot the realm, Xaselm was originally built by the [Predecessors](Lore_Predecessors.md), mortal inhabitants of the Isles whose civilization was wiped out during a previous [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TP-2)</sup>

## History
At the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), Xaselm served as the sanctum of [Relmyna Verenim](Lore_Relmyna_Verenim.md), a mortal practitioner of [flesh magic](Lore_Flesh_Magic.md) who was invited to the realm at [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md)'s request. Relmyna used Xaselm's ruined subterranean hallways and [root tunnels](Lore_Root.md) as bizarre experiment chambers for her cruel research into the so-called "Sixth Element" of [Flesh](Lore_Elements.md#Flesh). Here she experimented on living mortal subjects, torturing them through the use of magic, magical traps, the [undead](Lore_Undead.md), and rabid beasts in an effort to understand the true nature of pain. When these test subjects inevitably expired, Relmyna used powerful magic to resurrect them for continued experimentation.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> Although she was known to spend time elsewhere, Relmyna maintained a private quarters within Xaselm for herself and her apprentice, Nanette Don.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SI-1)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Relmyna and Nanette departed [Passwall](Lore_Passwall.md) following the death of the [Gatekeeper](Lore_Gatekeeper_of_the_Fringe.md), which Relmyna had created after many years laboring in the [Gardens of Flesh and Bone](Lore_Gardens_of_Flesh_and_Bone.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Following the start of the [Greymarch](Lore_Greymarch.md), Sheogorath sent his [Champion](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) to Xaselm to request Relmyna to create another Gatekeeper. Relmyna prepared a physical body for the creature at Xaselm while the Champion recovered the mystical components needed for her ritual from the Gardens of Flesh and Bone. Relmyna then returned to the Fringe and created a new Gatekeeper to defend the realm from the [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup>
