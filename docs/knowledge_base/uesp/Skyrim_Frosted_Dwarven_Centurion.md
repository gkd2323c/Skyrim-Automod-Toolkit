[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion.jpg) Frosted Dwarven Centurion [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c3/SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion_02.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frosted_Dwarven_Centurion_02.jpg) Using frost breath Frosted Dwarven Centurions are [Dwarven Centurions](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) that use [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md) attacks rather than steam. Two of them can be found in the Winter's Chimes section of [Runoff Caverns](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Runoff_Caverns), although only one is functional and will attack when you approach. They are located in a hidden area, guarding the [Dwarven Winter Visage](Skyrim_Dwarven_Winter_Visage.md). This location can be difficult to find; see the [quest page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Dwarven_Crown) for more information.
