# Knights of the White Stallion

[![Knights of the White Stallion symbol (Oblivion)](https://images.uesp.net/5/57/Knights Of The White Stallion.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Knights Of The White Stallion.gif) The Knights of the White Stallion (also known as the **Order of the White Stallion**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMarius-1)</sup> is a chivalric [knightly order](Lore_Knights.md) chartered by [Count Marius Caro](Lore_Marius_Caro.md) of [Leyawiin](Lore_Leyawiin.md). The order aims to put an end to banditry in the [Lower Niben](Lore_Lower_Niben.md). The knights are based in and around County Leyawiin, and own a lodge along the [Green Road](Lore_Green_Road.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBKnights-2)</sup> The symbol of the order is the city crest: a rampant ivory horse on a field of green, considered the ancient symbol of Leyawiin.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBKnights-2)</sup>

Circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Count Marius Caro named the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) and Mazoga the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) as Knights-Errant of the White Stallion for taking out the leader of the local [Black Bow Bandits](Lore_Black_Bow_Bandits.md), an Orc named Black Brugo. The count subsequently continued to fund a campaign against the Black Bow Bandits, paying a bounty to the Knights-Errant in return for each signature black bow brought to him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-OBMarius-1)</sup>
