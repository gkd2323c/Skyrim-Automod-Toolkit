Prayer to My Prince *A devotional poem dedicated to one of the lords of Oblivion* *
---
These gifts are meager,
But I beseech you!

Guide my actions,
Hear my thoughts
Across the ages, and
Through the realms

I approach the altar,
My heart full of
Devotion and love
For my Prince!
