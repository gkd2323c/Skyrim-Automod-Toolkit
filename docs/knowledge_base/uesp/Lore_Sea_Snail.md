Snails are slow-moving slimy [gastropods](Lore_Gastropod.md) that inhabit shells,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> they are known to stick to things and leave trails of slimy mucus.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> They can be found on land or in freshwater,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IV-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOFish-7)</sup> while others known as sea snails can be found in the oceans.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Conch-9)</sup> Their distinctive shells have seen use throughout Tamriel,<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Marsh Snail-10)</sup> they are sometimes used as currency by Argonians.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-11)</sup> Certains [crabs](Lore_Crab.md), such as [hermit crabs](Lore_Hermit_Crab.md), are known to make use of snail shells for protection.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup> Burglar crabs are known to eat snails and move into their shells.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-13)</sup>

Snails are used in the [cuisine](Lore_Cuisine.md) of several cultures, such as [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md).<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-15)</sup> Some Argonian chefs cook with snail slime.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-ESOSoup-16)</sup> Cuisiniers from [Shimmerene](Lore_Shimmerene.md) use long and curved snail hooks to open snails from their shells.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-Hooks-17)</sup> [Snail-Gin](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md#Snail-Gin) is a [gin](Lore_Alcoholic_Beverages.md#Gin) brewed in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) that has a reputation for killing those who drink it. A pair of Argonian siblings from [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md) worked on making non-lethal Snail-Gin in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582).<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ESOHaxal-18)</sup> The [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) were known to cook heel-snail kabobs.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Koth-19)</sup>

[Witches](Lore_Witches.md) are said to be able to turn people into snails.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-20)</sup> The [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) have some superstitions about snails. To find a black snail in one's salad means ill luck in the week ahead, while a black snail on one's smallclothes means imminent death.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-21)</sup> Likely because of this, there existed a mercenary group affiliated with [House Hlaalu](Lore_House_Hlaalu.md) known as the Black Snails.<sup>[[22]](#cite_note-22)</sup>

## Varieties

### Land and Freshwater Snails
Garlic snails are a species of land snail seen as garden pests in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[23]](#cite_note-23)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IV-6)</sup> If a garlic snail comes into contact with a form of rare crimson [Dragonthorn](Lore_Dragonthorn.md), it is said to result in an [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) reaction that creates a plume of toxic gas.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-IV-6)</sup> Brown Hat Snails are a species of freshwater snail rarely found in the rivers of the [Telvanni Peninsula](Lore_Telvanni_Peninsula.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOFish-7)</sup> Marsh snails are snails found in Black Marsh whose shells are said to have a thousand-and-one uses, though they are most commonly used as inkwells.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Marsh Snail-10)</sup> Heel-snails were used in Kothringi cuisine.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-Koth-19)</sup> Shiny silver-shelled snails are claimed to inhabit [mangrove](Lore_Mangrove.md) forests in southeastern [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-24)</sup>

A mysterious and immortal entity called the [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md), who was known to lie when not asked a direct question, claimed to have seen behemoth mind-snails while in [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md). They claimed that most people were unable to see them, which it considered a shame as it found the mind-snails kind of cute.<sup>[[24]](#cite_note-AOTO-25)</sup>

### Sea Snails
Conches are a type of sea snail found in the seas off the southern coast of Tamriel, such as the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md) and [Southern Sea](Lore_Southern_Sea.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Conch-9)</sup><sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Giant Conch-26)</sup> Some found in the Southern Sea can grow to utterly massive sizes, with some of their shells washing up on [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).<sup>[[25]](#cite_note-Giant Conch-26)</sup> Conch shells are commonly used as decorations,<sup>[[26]](#cite_note-Deco-27)</sup> musical instruments,<sup>[[27]](#cite_note-Music-28)</sup><sup>[[28]](#cite_note-Conch Trumpet-29)</sup><sup>[[29]](#cite_note-Conch Horn-30)</sup> or as desk objects like paperweights.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Conch-9)</sup><sup>[[30]](#cite_note-Conch Ink-31)</sup> It is said you can hold a conch shell to your ear to hear the sounds of the ocean, as such they are sometimes taken as souvenirs from [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md).<sup>[[31]](#cite_note-32)</sup> These sounds are also the reason the [Tide-Born](Lore_Tide-Born.md) Argonians of Solstice are known to wear carved conch shell necklaces, as they symbolize their [connection to the sea](Lore_The_Tide.md).<sup>[[32]](#cite_note-33)</sup>

Turritellas are sea snails with distinctive long shells, the shells of fossilized giant turritellas can be found on Solstice.<sup>[[33]](#cite_note-Giant Turt-34)</sup>
