*Alchemy description:* **Drain the target's Stamina by <mag> points per second for <dur> seconds.**
This effect is a variant of [Damage Stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md). It causes a total of <mag>×<dur> damage.

As with the other Damage Stamina variant, [Ravage Stamina](Skyrim_Ravage_Stamina.md), it is only available from poisons.

## Notes
- The duration of Lingering Damage Stamina potions is always 10 seconds; this does not increase, no matter how high your Alchemy skill is, chosen perks and/or worn apparel with the Fortify Alchemy enchantment. What does increase, however, is the magnitude: how much stamina is drained per second.
