# Mehra

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/MW-npc-Mehra_Milo.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Mehra_Milo.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Mehra_Milo.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Mehra_Milo.jpg) Mehra Milo Mehra was a title or honorific used by priestesses and priests of [Dunmeri](Lore_Dark_Elf.md) culture, specifically within the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tribunal Mehra Helas Dia-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGT-2)</sup> They were collectively referred to as **the Mehras**. Its exact definition is unknown, though one of their duties was preaching.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NV-3)</sup> Some mehras are associated with specific locations, such as the mehra of Koal Canton.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-LAA-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OPN-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CPN-6)</sup>

## Examples of Usage
The title of mehra seems to be typically used in place of a given name, where it becomes a proper noun.

- Mehra Drora<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWMehra Drora-7)</sup>
- Mehra Helas<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Tribunal Mehra Helas-8)</sup>
- Mehra Milo<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWMehra Milo-9)</sup>
- Mehra Nabisi<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-WWYDB-10)</sup>
- Mehra Nerendas<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-MNTT-11)</sup>
- Mehra Uvandrys<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ESOMehra Uvandrys-12)</sup>

## Notes
- [Magistrix Urili Vox](Lore_Urili_Vox.md) of the Tribunal Temple was a mehra.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-EGT-2)</sup>
