[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/SR-npc-Sickly_Farmer.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sickly_Farmer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sickly_Farmer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sickly_Farmer.jpg) Sickly Farmer The **Sickly Farmer** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) being nursed back to health in the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md) in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).

She is quite sick and will do nothing but lie on the healing altar and let [Danica Pure-Spring](Skyrim_Danica_Pure-Spring.md) or [Acolyte Jenssen](Skyrim_Acolyte_Jenssen.md) heal her. The only exception to this schedule is during the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), when she flees for safety along with the other citizens.

She wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0001be1b). She only carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md).
