[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Frofnir_Trollsbane.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Frofnir_Trollsbane.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frofnir_Trollsbane.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Frofnir_Trollsbane.jpg) Frofnir's body **Frofnir Trollsbane** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who can be found deceased in a troll's den between [Cradle Stone Tower](Skyrim_Cradle_Stone_Tower.md) and [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md). An easy way of finding him is to find the [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) that is to the west of Cradle Stone Tower and then rock climb north-northwesterly down to him, near two [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md).

He wears a full set of [iron armor](Skyrim_Iron_Armor.md) and carries a [banded iron shield](Skyrim_Banded_Iron_Shield.md), a [long bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Long_Bow) supplemented by [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md), and the unique warhammer [Trollsbane](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trollsbane).

## Notes
- There is a dead [elk](Skyrim_Elk.md) and a portion of [venison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison) as well as numerous troll skulls, other human and animal bones scattered around him. Further to the west are several bird's nests with either [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) or [rock warbler eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg).
