# Hiomaste

Master Hiomaste was an archer of great renown. Hiomaste used his skills in a long life of military service before retiring to the [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) village of Moliva.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBA1-1)</sup> Despite being retired, Hiomaste opened a school where he would take in students to impart his masterful knowledge of archery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBA1-1)</sup> The word spread causing pupils all across the province, and even [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) to travel to the school, including the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), [Missun Akin](Lore_Missun_Akin.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBA1-1)</sup>

Moliva was the closest village to estate of the Duchess of [Woda](Lore_Woda.md), an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) woman whose family had lorded over the estate even before the days of the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TBA1-1)</sup> The Duchess was not fond of the village and she and her sister Lady Villea implored the Mayor of Moliva and Master Hiomaste to vacate the territory.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup>

Hiomaste informed the Duchess that she did not own the forest and never would, and that he would not move his school.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup> In retaliation, the Duchess had the village and the school set to fire killing many innocents including Master Hiomaste.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup> Missun Akin, the Dunmer pupil of Hiomaste did not take kindly to this, and avenged his master by killing the Duchess with a well placed [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) arrow.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup>

Missun returned to Vvardenfell and kept Hiomaste's legacy alive by becoming a master trainer of archery in his own right in the stronghold of Falasmaryon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MWMissun-3)</sup> The village of Moliva was also rebuilt.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TBA2-2)</sup>
