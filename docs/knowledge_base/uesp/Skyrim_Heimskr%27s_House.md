[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-place-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-place-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg) Heimskr's House **Heimskr's House** is a small house in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) owned by the priest [Heimskr](Skyrim_Heimskr.md). It is located beside the [Gildergreen](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gildergreen) tree, between [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) and the [Temple of Kynareth](Skyrim_Temple_of_Kynareth.md). It consists of one area, *[Heimskr's House](#Heimskr.27s_House)*.

During the [Battle for Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md) rubble lands in front of the house, making it impossible to access the house for the rest of the game. Heimskr will then be forced to move to a small camp near Jorrvaskr, or be thrown into jail if you sided with the Imperials.

## Heimskr's House
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Heimskr%27s_House.jpg) Inside Heimskr's house The house is small and bare-bones, although four chairs facing a homemade podium on the northwestern wall suggest that Heimskr doesn't only preach near the Gildergreen outside. Next to the chairs is a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md), while a total of five [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) can be found near the front door. To the southeast is Heimskr's bedroom which contains a chest with a few gold coins and a small dinner table dressed with some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine), and a [potion of vigorous magicka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigorous_Magicka). The owned bed stands along the northeastern wall, while a dresser with [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md) has a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a [goat cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Goat_Cheese_Wedge), and a [potent lingering poison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potent_Lingering_Poison) on top. There are a total of four human skulls decorating his shelves and floors with no explanation given.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Heimskr%27s_House) to which is carried by Heimskr.
- There is an [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Empty [shadowmark](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) to the right of the entrance door, even though the Thieves Guild [considers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Burglary_Job) his house to be a wealthy house. The house will not be empty if you are sent here on one of [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md)'s thievery jobs.

## Bugs
- If given a Thieves Guild radiant mission with Heimskr's house as your target location after the Battle of Whiterun, it will be impossible to complete the objective due to his house being inaccessible. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.

- You may simply return to the quest giver and quit the current job without any penalties.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Another possible fix is to type `coc Whiterun Heimskrs House` into the console to get teleported inside Heimskr's House. To get back outside, you can simply use the door of the house.
