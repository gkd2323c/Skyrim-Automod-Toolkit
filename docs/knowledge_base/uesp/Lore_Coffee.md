# Coffee

*"Tea can be a stimulant, of course. But I find the buzz you experience with a nice Aetherial or Spiceberry quite pleasant. Whereas coffee is like a million gnats dancing within your mind. No thank you."*
— [Azandar al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAC-1)</sup> [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/BK-Taneth_Coffee.jpg/200px-BK-Taneth_Coffee.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BK-Taneth_Coffee.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BK-Taneth_Coffee.jpg) Taneth Coffee Coffee is a stimulant typically drunken as a [beverage](Lore_Cuisine.md) for the purpose of keeping oneself awake.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAC-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DT-2)</sup> It is made from dark brown seeds called coffee beans,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Orzorga-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jagga Balls-4)</sup> found in the fruit of the coffee plant. Some may drink their coffee with [cream](Lore_Cream.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ca CS-5)</sup> Similar beverages include tea and kaveh.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAC-1)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RCS-6)</sup> In cooking, coffee infusions can sometimes enhance the taste of the dish.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Orzorga-3)</sup>

Areas that grow and ship coffee include [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) and [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TAS-7)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Orzorga-3)</sup>

Coffee is poisonous to [bears](Lore_Bear.md), who sometimes eat it if they find it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Orzorga-3)</sup> [Nixads](Lore_Nixad.md) detest the bitter scent of coffee, and as such some traders may carry it with their shipments to drive nixads away.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BTAN-8)</sup>

## Culture and Uses
During the New Life Festival, the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) enjoy a sticky seasonal dessert called Jagga-Drenched "Mud Balls", made with bone flour, cheese, and [senche](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) -passed coffee beans.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Jagga Balls-4)</sup>

The [Coffee Scorpion](Lore_Scorpion.md#Coffee_Scorpion) of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) is named in homage to the city's [kaveh](Lore_Redguard_Cuisine.md#Kaveh), which is considered strong, dark, and sweet.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-RCS-6)</sup>

The [Baandari](Lore_Baandari.md) [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) of [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) have used sand baths to make tea and coffee since the late [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-TAS-7)</sup>

## Notes
- Those who dislike coffee may refer to it as "beanwater" or "bean stink", finding it overstimulating compared to tea, and unfavorably compare its taste to the brine of Apocrypha and its smell to the stench of the Deadlands.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAC-1)</sup>
- [Lyg](Lore_Lyg.md) is described a backwards version of Tamriel, akin to a coffee-stain, in a parallel dimension that is simultaneously on the opposite side of Nirn.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-9)</sup> A similar description of spilled coffee is also mentioned in relation to the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-TMS-10)</sup>
