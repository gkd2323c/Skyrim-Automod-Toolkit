# Armor Collector

**Armor Collector** is an Altmer who can be found at The Bee and Barb in Riften. She carries Orders, a random selection of items, and a set of orcish scaled armor, which can be bought, pickpocketed, or taken from her body as part of the quest [Gambler's Edge](Skyrim_Gambler%27s_Edge.md).

If you persuade her to leave the city, she will make her way to [Cragslane Cavern](Skyrim_Cragslane_Cavern.md), where she will remain.

## Related Quests
- Gambler's Edge CC: Search for an ill-fated gambler at the pit fights in Cragslane Cavern.
