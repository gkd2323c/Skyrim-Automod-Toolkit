**Conjure Dark Seducer Archer** is an adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell that summons a Dark Seducer Archer to aid you in combat.

## Effects
- [Conjure](Skyrim_Summon.md) Dark Seducer Archer, for 60 secs

## Perks
- [Summoner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summoner), increase range to 48 ft at first rank or 72 ft at second rank.
- [Twin Souls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Twin_Souls), lets you have two Dark Seducer Archers summoned at the same time.
