[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d2/SR-npc-Gralnach.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gralnach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gralnach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gralnach.jpg) Gralnach **Gralnach** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [child](Skyrim_Child.md) that lives at [Heartwood Mill](Skyrim_Heartwood_Mill.md) with his mother [Grosta](Skyrim_Grosta.md). His father, [Leifnarr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leifnarr), recently disappeared and Gralnach is understandably upset, although not as upset as his mother.

He sleeps in the house every night between midnight and 6am and starts the day with a one-hour breakfast. While his mother works the mill alone, he spends the day fooling around the courtyard. At 1pm and 7pm he pauses for one-hour meals.

He wears [child's clothes](Skyrim_Child%27s_Clothes.md) with [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#000e80a8). He carries a selection of [food and toys](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Child) and a small amount of gold.

## Dialogue

- *"It's just me and my mother at the mill here. My father is gone. We don't know what happened to him."*
- *"I hope you're not here to start some trouble. I'll fight you if I have to."*
- *"My mother taught me the millwork trade... you know, which trees to cut and which ones to pass up."*
- *"Someday I'll take over the mill... when I'm old enough."*
- *"I'm not afraid of anyone. Put a sword in my hand and I could defend this place if I had to!"*
- *"Millwork sure is boring, but at least I don't have to work in a nasty mine."*
- *"I can't believe my mother's gone. Now what'll I do?"* **(If Grosta is dead)**
- *"I knew it! I knew my father was a hero and he didn't just run away."* **(After the quest [Flight or Fight](Skyrim_Flight_or_Fight.md))**

## Conversations
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/01/SR-npc-Gralnach_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gralnach_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gralnach_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gralnach_02.jpg) *"Gralnach! I told you not to go through your father's things!"* With his father gone, Gralnach takes responsibility and helps his mother out:

- **Gralnach**: *"Mother, I finished cutting at the northern stand. Where do I move on to now?"*
- **Grosta**: *"There is a copse of elms to the east. We could really use that wood."*
- **Gralnach**: *"I was hoping to head into Riften for supplies, but I can do that after I'm done with the elms."*
- **Grosta**: *"Just be careful, Gralnach. That copse doesn't look safe. If you see any trouble, you run right home."*

Grosta thinks her husband ran off with another woman. A conversation inside the house shows how she handles it:

- **Grosta**: *"Gralnach! I told you not to go through your father's things!"*
- **Gralnach**: *"I'm sorry mother, I just want to know more about him. You never tell me anything!"*
- **Grosta**: *"That's because there's nothing to tell. He left us to fend for ourselves and that's all you need to know."*
- **Gralnach**: *"I wish you didn't hate him so much... I wish the whole family could be together again."*

After the quest is completed, the conversation will take a slightly different tone:

- **Grosta**: *"Gralnach! I told you not to go through your father's things!"*
- **Gralnach**: *"I'm sorry mother, now that he's dead, I just want to know everything I can about him."*
- **Grosta**: *"He was a wonderful man, and he loved us very much. I'm sorry I yelled at you."*
- **Gralnach**: *"It's okay, mother. I understand."*
