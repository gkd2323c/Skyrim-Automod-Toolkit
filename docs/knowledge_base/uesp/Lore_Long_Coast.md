[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a2/ON-map-Long_Coast.jpg/200px-ON-map-Long_Coast.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Long_Coast.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Long_Coast.jpg) Map of Long Coast circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The **Long Coast** is the southernmost region of the [Grahtwood](Lore_Grahtwood.md) region. The Long Coast is the closest to sea-level from the rest of Grahtwood, and it consists of the lower basin, along with the coast in the region. The two cities of [Haven](Lore_Haven.md) and [Southpoint](Lore_Southpoint.md) are located along the region and have foreign origins, when compared with their sister city of [Elden Root](Lore_Elden_Root.md). The township of [Brackenleaf](Lore_Brackenleaf_(settlement).md) and the Gray Mire are located along the Coast.

The Bone Orchard necropolis is one of the largest crypts in [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and it used to be the site of Arborfell before it was destroyed by an unknown entity named Dringoth.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>
