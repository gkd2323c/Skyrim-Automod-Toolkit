[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/50/SR-npc-Indara_Caerellia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Indara_Caerellia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indara_Caerellia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Indara_Caerellia.jpg) Indara Caerellia **Indara Caerellia** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) who lives at [Corpselight Farm](Skyrim_Corpselight_Farm.md) in [Falkreath](Skyrim_Falkreath.md) with her husband, [Mathies](Skyrim_Mathies.md). Their only daughter, Lavinia, was recently killed by [Sinding](Skyrim_Sinding.md), and husband Mathies will ask you to investigate in the quest [Ill Met By Moonlight](Skyrim_Ill_Met_By_Moonlight.md).

When you visit Falkreath for the first time, go to the graveyard and you will witness Indara and Mathies listening to town priest [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md) giving their deceased daughter the last rites. After the sad ceremony, they will all go their separate ways; Indara sleeps inside the farmhouse every night between 8pm and 4am. After waking up, she was supposed to be working the crops behind the house for 16 hours straight, but due to a serious [bug](#Bugs) she spends the entire day stuck between two rocks near the cemetery, along the road leading east from the [Hall of the Dead](Skyrim_Hall_of_the_Dead_(Falkreath).md). The bug also prevents her random activities, such as eating and relaxing.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000261bd), and a pair of [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gloves#000261c1). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Corpselight_Farm) to the farm as well as a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

The Nords could use more like him. So could the Imperials."* *"How could people see all the graves in Falkreath and still want to make war?"*
### Conversations
Indara was meant to partake in two conversations around town, but none of them ever play due to numerous issues:

Local priest [Runil](Skyrim_Runil.md) was meant to speak to her, but seeing as the two normally never meet, this scene can never play:

**Runil**: *"Ah, Indara. Arkay's blessing upon you and your husband. Spend as much time here as you need."*
**Indara**: *"Thank you, Runil. Your support means much to us."* She was also meant to talk to her husband, but the dialogue is actually set up to play with [Kust](Skyrim_Kust.md) and [Lod](Skyrim_Lod.md) and is only recorded in their voice types as well:

**Mathies**: *"The fields will need cutting soon."* **Indara**: *"I suppose so. Seems like it was just turning summer yesterday."* **Mathies**: *"And now winter is coming."*
## Notes
- She was supposed to have played a role in the unfinished quest [Runil's Dark Past](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#Runil.27s_Dark_Past), and her dialogue will never be heard: *"I knew he was troubled, but one of the Thalmor? I wish I could say I did not believe it. Thank you for telling me."*

The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.0.1, fixes this bug.
