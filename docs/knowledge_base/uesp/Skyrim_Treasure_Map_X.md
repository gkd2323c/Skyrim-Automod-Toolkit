## Explicit Instructions
- Travel to [The Lady Stone](Skyrim_The_Lady_Stone.md).
- From The Lady Stone face the peninsula to the east of [Ilinalta's Deep](Skyrim_Ilinalta%27s_Deep.md).
- Line up with the closest tree on that peninsula and proceed into the lake.
- Look for a grey boulder just before the lake suddenly shallows; the chest is on the northeast side of that boulder. - [Waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md) is very useful for this search.
- The boulder is closer to the peninsula than the island.
