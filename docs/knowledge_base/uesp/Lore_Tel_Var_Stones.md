# Tel Var Stones

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/25/ON-item-Tel_Var_Stone.jpg/200px-ON-item-Tel_Var_Stone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Tel_Var_Stone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Tel_Var_Stone.jpg) A Tel Var stone as seen in ESO Tel Var Stones are [magical](Lore_Magic.md) pieces of the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) that purportedly contain the essence of Creation.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Ot TVS1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPhrastus-2)</sup> According to [Tower Lore](Lore_The_Towers.md), scholars postulate that when the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) built the White-Gold Tower, they couldn't recreate their own [Zero Stone](Lore_The_Towers.md#White-Gold), so they settled for Tel Var Stones. It's believed the stones are imbued with vast magical power that lies dormant within.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONCinnabar-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Ot TVS4-4)</sup> With further study, scholars believe details of the White-Gold Tower's construction could be revealed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONCinnabar-3)</sup> Experimentation on powdered stones revealed that all [elements of magical practice](Lore_Magic.md#Spellcasting) reside within them.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ot TVS3-5)</sup> Should the stones be destroyed, some believe that the essence of Creation within them is lost.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Ot TVS2-6)</sup>

During the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), vast legions of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) overran the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). The Daedra began to chip away at the White-Gold Tower and remove the stones for an unknown purpose.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPhrastus-2)</sup> Other accounts claim that fragments of the White-Gold Tower splintered from the structure by the stresses of Molag Bal's attempt to pull it into Coldharbour.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ESOICTVSo-7)</sup>

Regardless, scholars feared that if enough stones were removed, Creation would be undone. To prevent this, certain individuals sought to recover the stolen pieces and repair the damage to the tower.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONPhrastus-2)</sup> Others believe the magical properties of the stones could be used to fight back against the Daedra.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONCinnabar-3)</sup> During this time, the Tel Var Stones were also used as [currency](Lore_Currency.md) by [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) soldiers exploring the sewers of the Imperial City, and the three Alliances competed for the stones.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-IC-8)</sup>

## Notes
- "Tel Var" is an adaptation of the [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md) word for "Silver Star".<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-ESOLive082815-9)</sup>
