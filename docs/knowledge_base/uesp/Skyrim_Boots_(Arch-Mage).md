[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-item-Boots_%28Arch-Mage%29.jpg/200px-SR-item-Boots_%28Arch-Mage%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boots_(Arch-Mage).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Boots_(Arch-Mage).jpg) The Arch-Mage's Boots The Arch-Mage's Boots are a unique pair of [shoes](Skyrim_Shoes.md) used by the head of the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md). A pair of the boots can be found on [Savos Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md), while an additional copy can be found in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md). The second pair is illegal to take initially, but can be freely used after the resolution of the issues with [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md). The boots themselves are enchanted to provide [shock resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Shock.md) by forty points, decreasing damage done from all kinds of [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md).
