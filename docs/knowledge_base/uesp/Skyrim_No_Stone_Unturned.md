## Quick Walkthrough
1. Find one of the 24 "[Unusual Gems](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md)" scattered throughout Skyrim.
2. Become a full member of the Thieves Guild (if not one already).
3. Have the gem appraised by [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) at [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md).
4. Collect the remaining stones of a full set and return to Vex.
5. Retrieve the Crown of Barenziah and return to Vex.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9a/SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned.jpg) An Unusual Gem
### What Do We Have Here?
In your travels about Skyrim, you will come upon an ornate jewel (one of twenty-four spread across Skyrim) called an [Unusual Gem](Skyrim_Unusual_Gem.md#Unusual_Gem). Subsequent actions after you acquire one of these gems depends on your membership status with the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

#### Not a Member of the Thieves Guild
If you are not a member of the Thieves Guild, picking up the first Unusual Gem you encounter will activate the miscellaneous quest objective: *"Bring the Unusual Gem to an appraiser"*.

Depending on your past travels and interactions, following your objective marker will likely take you to [Maul](Skyrim_Maul.md), a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). If you have not yet spoken with Maul, he will seek you out with some unsolicited advice. After the conversation, ask him what he knows about the gem. He will tell you that you need to show it to [Vex](Skyrim_Vex.md) of the Thieves Guild, to find out what the gem is worth. Maul will also tell you that to get to Vex, you must first become a member of the Thieves Guild. If you press for information on the guild, you will be told to find [Brynjolf](Skyrim_Brynjolf.md) in Riften's marketplace. You can find him as suggested, or in the [Bee and Barb](Skyrim_The_Bee_and_Barb.md) at night. Talking to him will activate the quest [A Chance Arrangement](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Chance_Arrangement), which is the first step towards becoming a member of the Guild. Once you have access to [The Ragged Flagon](Skyrim_The_Ragged_Flagon.md), seek out Vex for her appraisal of the gem. She will tell you that it is one of twenty-four stones that were pried from the [crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Crown_of_Barenziah#Crown_of_Barenziah) of [Queen Barenziah](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barenziah) by a thief in an attempt to cover his tracks, and that only the complete set of twenty-four stones would be worth anything. If Maul does not tell you to find Brynjolf, you can seek him out yourself.

#### Already a Member of the Thieves Guild
If you are a full member of the Thieves Guild when you pick up the first stone, the quest is immediately activated with the objective: *"Bring the Unusual Gem to Vex in the Thieves Guild"*. Find Vex in The Ragged Flagon. She will tell you that it is one of twenty-four stones that were pried from the crown of Queen Barenziah by a thief in an attempt to cover his tracks. She will further inform you that the stone is worthless on its own, but that if you can find the other twenty-three, the collection would be worth a substantial sum.

### Finding the Gems
After showing the Unusual Gem to Vex, the gems are renamed "Stones of Barenziah," and will appear as such in your inventory when in your possession. You must now find all twenty-four stones.

You'll need to complete some other tasks before you have access to all locations where the gems are to be found:

- Begin the [Dark Brotherhood questline](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) or start the [Destroy the Dark Brotherhood!](Skyrim_Destroy_the_Dark_Brotherhood!.md) quest.
- Purchase [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). (25,000 gold)
- Join the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md) by completing [First Lessons](Skyrim_First_Lessons.md). You must also start [Under Saarthal](Skyrim_Under_Saarthal.md) and progress at least to the point where Tolfdir tells you to speak with the Arch-Mage; until this point, the Arch-Mage's Quarters are locked behind an inaccessible door.

Aside from those, most other gems can actually be obtained at any point in the game. Many are located in places that you will be sent to during other quests, so you may want to consider getting the stone concurrently with the quest.

### Recovering the Crown
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/39/SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned2.jpg/200px-SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-No_Stone_Unturned2.jpg) Find the Crown within Tolvald's Cave Show Vex the full set of Stones, and she will have you travel to [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) to retrieve the Crown of Barenziah. The cave is three layers deep, and crawling with Falmer, so be sure to prepare yourself. When approaching the crown, three hostile Dunmer Ghosts will appear and attack. After returning with the Crown, you will receive the permanent effect [Prowler's Profit](Skyrim_Gems.md#Prowler.27s_Profit), which significantly increases the chance of finding gems in randomized containers.

## Notes
- If you enter Tolvald's Cave without having spoken to Vex about having all the gems, the area where the crown is found will be blocked off by rubble. This will cause you to have to return to Vex and then travel all the way through the cave again to find the crown.
- The gems that will be marked as stolen may say "steal" instead of "take" when acquiring them; in this case, they will still be marked as stolen and if you're caught taking them, you will be reported. This has been partially modified in the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md); see [bugs](#Bugs) for details.
- If your game is prior to [Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md) 1.4 and you haven't recovered the stone from within the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md), there are multiple workarounds detailed on the location page.
- There are no quest markers for the stones, so unless you intend to find all the stones by chance, it may be best to print out a list of their locations and cross each one off as you find it.
- It is impossible to remove this quest from your list without joining the Thieves Guild; if you don't want to do that, you may be better off ignoring any Unusual Gems you come across, as they have no other use in the game and cannot be removed from your inventory without Vex.
- The stone in the Dark Brotherhood Sanctuary is not generated by the game until either of the related quest lines is started.
- Text in the game code suggests that you were originally supposed to see Tonilia about the gems. The dialogue about buying the gems would have made much more sense in that context.
- Unlike loose gems (such as amethysts, rubies, etc.), the Stones are immovable where they are placed in the environment, so you don't have to worry about them getting knocked off and lost in the game world.
- With the [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) add-on, the draugrs in each blue ice deposit at the [Stalhrim Source](Skyrim_Stalhrim_Source.md) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md) will have up to 5 gems, usually of flawless quality once the quest is finished.
- Version 2.0.9 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) removes the objective to "Become a full member of the Thieves Guild" from your journal once you speak to Vex about the unusual gems.

## Bugs
- If you gather all 24 stones before getting Vex to identify them it won't advance to the stage where you need to turn them back in to her and your journal will still say to recover the gems (24/24). - The [Official Skyrim Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md), version 1.9.26.0.8, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) To fix this, type these console commands: `setobjectivecompleted TGCrown 20 1`, followed by `setstage TGCrown 30`.
- The *Prowler's Profit* may not appear in the Active Effects menu, but it should still be in effect. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.0, fixes this bug.
- Although the prompt to collect the Unusual Gems says "take" in the [Special Edition](Skyrim_Special_Edition.md), NPCs may still report you if you're caught and you'll get a [bounty](Skyrim_Crime.md) for theft if that is the case. <sup>**?**</sup>
- The activator script for the Unusual Gems generates a script error and is unable to trigger the theft alarm due to a bad use of linked references. <sup>**?**</sup>
- Once you've learned that these *Unusual Gems* are actually *Stones of Barenziah* any new stones will no longer stack in your inventory, previously collected stones will stack when presented to Vex however.
- Repeatedly pressing the activate button while picking up a Stone may shortcut this quest. Your inventory will only increase by one Stone, but your quest progress will increase by more than one, allowing you to return to Vex and move onto recovering the Crown before visiting all 24 Stone locations. This is most easily performed with all the words of Slow Time. - You can also repeatedly and quickly press the activate button while slowly approaching a gem; this will work if you haven't yet learned a word for the Slow Time shout or haven't yet reached the stage in the main questline where you can learn shouts.
- Sometimes the gems that are supposed to be in [Fellglow Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fellglow_Keep) and [Pinewatch](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md) Sanctuary don't appear and will prevent progress with the quest. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Type the following three commands into the console for a workaround: `set TGCrown Gems Found to 24`; `set TGCrown Gems Total to 24`; `setstage TGCrown 30`. This will not update the journal entry with the correct number of gems found but it will unlock Vex's dialogue to proceed with the quest.
- The finished crown has 25 stones in it, even though only 24 exist. When found without the stones, all 25 of the crown's slots are empty. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.6, fixes this bug.
- The [Dunmer Ghosts](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Ghost) in [Tolvald's Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tolvald%27s_Cave) may become hostile to each other due to an improper faction setup. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.0, fixes this bug.
- There's a chance only a single male [Dunmer Ghost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dunmer_Ghost) shows up by the crown and that it's not hostile. When talked to, he may say something along the lines of "It's been so many years...", or he may not speak at all. He can also be pickpocketed for an [Elven Shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Elven_Shield). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.1, fixes this bug.
- If you've angered Maul, he will not speak with you about the Unusual Gem, even though he's the quest target. - As noted in the text, this can be bypassed by simply joining the Thieves Guild and ignoring Maul.
- You may collect all 24 stones but the journal entry may say you have 23 and only 23 may show in your inventory.

Notes - Any text displayed in angle brackets (e.g., `<Alias=Location Hold>`) is dynamically set by the Radiant Quest system, and will be filled in with the appropriate word(s) when seen in game.
- Not all Journal Entries may appear in your journal; which entries appear and which entries do not depends on the manner in which the quest is done.
- Stages are not always in order of progress. This is usually the case with quests that have multiple possible outcomes or quests where certain tasks may be done in any order. Some stages may therefore repeat objectives seen in other stages.
- If an entry is marked as "Finishes Quest" it means the quest disappears from the Active Quest list, but you may still receive new entries for that quest.
- On the PC, it is possible to use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) to advance through the quest by entering `setstage TGCrown Misc, TGCrown stage`, where `stage` is the number of the stage you wish to complete. It is not possible to un-complete (i.e. go back) quest stages, but it is possible to clear all stages of the quest using `resetquest TGCrown Misc, TGCrown`.
