[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6d/SR-npc-Alea_Quintus.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alea_Quintus.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alea_Quintus.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alea_Quintus.jpg) Alea Quintus [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/aa/SR-npc-Alea_Quintus_Unmasked.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Alea_Quintus_Unmasked.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alea_Quintus_Unmasked.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Alea_Quintus_Unmasked.jpg) Alea unmasked **Alea Quintus**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is one of the three people that may be selected from and killed to gain entry into the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) during the [related quest](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md). She is the mother of six children. She does not act scared when spoken with; rather, she says that she has no time to deal with this and demands to be released. Furthermore, Alea states that she may have made enemies with the take-no-nonsense attitude she has had to adopt in order to raise her children.

She wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0001be1a) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0001be1b). She, like the other two captives, also wears an [execution hood](Skyrim_Execution_Hood.md).

## Related Quests
- **[With Friends Like These...](Skyrim_With_Friends_Like_These....md)**: Repay your debt to [the Family](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md), and show you are willing to kill on command.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
When approached for the first time:

- *"I don't have time for this nonsense. I've got a home to keep and children to feed. Now let me out of here!"*
- *"Cowards! Stealing a woman from her home! For shame!"*
- *"Get these things off of me!"*
- *"By the gods, when I get out of here you're dead! You hear me? Dead!"*
- *"Aggghh! Re...lease me!"*

If you kill another captive and talk to Alea, she will say:

- *"What's happening? Damn it, what are you doing?"*

Speaking to her:

- **Who are you?**
- *"None of your damned business who I am! If you're going to kill me, just do it already! As [Mara](Lore_Mara.md) is my witness, if I didn't have this damned hood on right now I would spit right in your face..."*
- **Would someone pay to have you killed?**
- *"Excuse me? What kind of question is that?"*

- - **One last time: Would. Someone. Pay. To. Have. You. Killed? (Intimidate)**
- *"I'm kneeling here with my hands bound and a sack over my head, and you have the gall to ask me that? What do you think, genius?"*

- - **It's all right. Just tell me what I need to know. (Persuade)**
- *"I'm a woman living in Skyrim with six children and no husband. I don't have the time or patience to be 'nice.' Do some people look down on me? Have I made some enemies? You're damn right."*

If you kill Astrid, you can initiate conversation with Alea to free her:

- *"You cut these bindings this minute! You hear me? You cut these bindings right now!"*
- **(Cut the captive's bonds)**
- *"Well it's about damn time. Capturing a woman from her home, keeping her bound like an animal. Ridiculous..."*

Talking to her after this will have her respond:

- *"I don't know who you are, or what's going on here, but you ought to be ashamed of yourself."*

## Notes
If you kill her, [Astrid](Skyrim_Astrid.md) will remark:

- *"Ah, the feisty goodwife. Quite the mouth on her. Someone must have wanted her dead... right?"*
- *"Hmm. The fearless wife and mother, and the [underhanded Khajiit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vasha). Had to be one of them, right? Interesting choice."*
- *"The [blubbering barbarian](Skyrim_Fultheim_the_Fearless.md) and the tenacious mother. Must have been one of them, right?"*
- *"Well, well, aren't we the over-achiever? Three possibilities, three victims. Must have been one of them, right? So why take chances..."*
