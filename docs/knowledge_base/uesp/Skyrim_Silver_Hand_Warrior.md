# Silver Hand Warrior

**Silver Hand Warriors** are deceased [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) and members of the [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md). Three can be found in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), just outside the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) headquarters [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md) at the conclusion of the quest [Blood's Honor](Skyrim_Blood%27s_Honor.md). Another Silver Hand Warrior can be found inside [Dustman's Cairn](Skyrim_Dustman%27s_Cairn.md).

They all wear a randomly determined set of [light armor](Skyrim_Armor.md#Light_Armor); at all levels, there is always a 50% chance they will wear [fur shoes](Skyrim_Fur_Shoes.md), and a set of [fur armor](Skyrim_Fur_Armor.md), while the other 50% can differ for both shoes and armor; at level 1-5 it can be [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and/or [hide armor](Skyrim_Hide_Armor.md), at level 6-18, it can be [leather boots](Skyrim_Leather_Boots.md) and/or [leather armor](Skyrim_Leather_Armor.md), and at level 19-28 it can be [scaled boots](Skyrim_Scaled_Boots.md) and/or [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) or [scaled horn armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Horn_Armor.md). Additionally, they have a 50% chance of wearing a set of leveled gauntlets, again randomly determined; it can always be [fur bracers](Skyrim_Fur_Bracers.md) but, at level 1-5, there is a 50% chance it can be [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md), at level 6-18 it can be [leather bracers](Skyrim_Leather_Bracers.md) and at level 19, it can be [scaled bracers](Skyrim_Scaled_Bracers.md). Lastly, there is a 20% chance of them carrying a leveled shield, up to elven quality at level 12.

They carry one randomly determined weapon; either a [silver sword](Skyrim_Silver_Sword.md), a [silver greatsword](Skyrim_Silver_Greatsword.md) or a leveled [mace](Skyrim_Mace.md), [sword](Skyrim_Sword.md) or [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) (up to elven quality at level 20). They also carry some random loot such as ingredients, beverages, potions or ingots and a small amount of gold.
