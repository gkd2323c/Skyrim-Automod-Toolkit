[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f1/SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg/200px-SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Japhet%27s_Folly.jpg) Japhet's Folly ca. 4E 201 Japhet's Folly is a fort on the inhospitable **Rockjoint Island** found northeast of [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md), off the coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md). It is known for its rough climate, lack of habitability, and tales of ghosts by the people of nearby [Dawnstar](Lore_Dawnstar.md). A sailor by the name of Japhet decided to build a large citadel here, only to pass away from the sheer cold and the isolation, hence the name.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JJ-1)</sup>

## History
At some point, a [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) sailor, Japhet, led a small band of warriors from Dawnstar to build a fortress on the island and prove himself. However, after the first winter, several people had left to return to the mainland. When even his couriers left the island, Japhet decided to take up farming and become self-sufficient as his father once was. With his grain supply eventually running low he wrote to his journal: "The ghosts are speaking to me now, the more of this icemoss that I eat, the more clearly I can hear them." <sup>[[1]](#cite_note-JJ-1)</sup> Eventually, Japhet found his demise in the sea caves, and ever since, the fort has been called Japhet's Folly.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SRJaphet-2)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the [Blood Horkers](Lore_Blood_Horkers.md) led by the storm mage Haldyn, took refuge in the fortress on Japhet's Folly. The pirates had struck a deal with the [Shatter-Shield Clan](Lore_Shatter-Shield_Clan.md) to destroy any passing ships between [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) and [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), except for clan ships which raised suspicion in the [East Empire Company](Lore_East_Empire_Company.md) in [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SROrthus-3)</sup> This led to a siege on Japhet's Folly by the EEC that led to the death of Haldyn and the broken deal with the Shatter-Shields.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SRRise-4)</sup>
