## Ingredients
24 guaranteed samples can be found in 12 different locations. Locations with multiple samples are:

- 4 in the [Arch-Mage's Quarters](Skyrim_Arch-Mage%27s_Quarters.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in [the Mortar and Pestle](Skyrim_The_Mortar_and_Pestle.md) ([Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md))
- 2 in [Elgrim's Elixirs](Skyrim_Elgrim%27s_Elixirs.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 2 in [Honeyside](Skyrim_Honeyside.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 2 in [Proudspire Manor](Skyrim_Proudspire_Manor.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 2 in [Sarethi Farm](Skyrim_Sarethi_Farm.md) ([The Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md))
- 2 in [Vlindrel Hall](Skyrim_Vlindrel_Hall.md) ([Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
