[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/OB-place-Reed_River.jpg/200px-OB-place-Reed_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Reed_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Reed_River.jpg) The Reed River circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9b/ON-place-Reed_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Reed_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reed_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Reed_River.jpg) The Reed River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) The Reed River is a short river that has its source in the steep hillsides south of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md). It flows southeast and joins the [Corbolo River](Lore_Corbolo_River.md) in just a few miles. [Lake Poppad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Lake_Poppad) originally fed a tributary of the Reed River circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), although this stream had seemingly dried up by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RR-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> As of then, a shrine to [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) was located there.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Ontus_Vanin-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-V-4)</sup>
