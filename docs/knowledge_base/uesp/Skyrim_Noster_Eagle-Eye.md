[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/SR-npc-Noster_Eagle-Eye.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Noster_Eagle-Eye.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Noster_Eagle-Eye.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Noster_Eagle-Eye.jpg) Noster Eagle-Eye **Noster Eagle-Eye** is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) [beggar](Skyrim_Beggars.md) and war veteran who fought in the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) but now lives in the streets of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He was on the battlefield when the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) attacked [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md). During that battle, he was injured, lost his eye, and left by his comrades for dead. Due to his injury, he could no longer make a living and now lives destitute in Solitude. He possesses extensive knowledge on how to move in the wilderness and is willing to share that [knowledge](Skyrim_Sneak.md) with you if a [prized helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Noster%27s_Helmet) he lost can be [recovered](Skyrim_Dungeon_Delving_(Caves).md).

Noster wears some [ragged robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Robes) along with a pair of [ragged boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ragged_Boots). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) along with a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

When you enter Solitude for the first time, you will find Noster in the crowd witnessing the execution of [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md). Once Roggvir is dead, Noster will start his daily routine in Solitude. He never sleeps and spends the majority of his time leaned against the pillar outside [Radiant Raiment](Skyrim_Radiant_Raiment.md), begging the townsfolk for gold. He only travels to a different spot at 1am when he spends three hours begging in front of [Angeline's Aromatics](Skyrim_Angeline%27s_Aromatics.md). The only exception to this routine is when he participates in the wedding during the quest [Bound Until Death](Skyrim_Bound_Until_Death.md).

## Dialogue
If you try to talk to him during Roggvir's execution:

*"A man opens a gate and they cut his head off? Doesn't seem right to me."* His normal greetings:

*"Spare some gold for a veteran?"* *"I hid here in Solitude for a long time but war, war follows a man until it's done with him."* *"Them Imperials left me to die in Anvil, and I had to fight my way back home. Then they followed me back here!"* *"Can you spare a septim? Lost an eye during the Great War, or I'd earn it myself..."* If spoken to:

**What's your story, old man?** *"What? You see a man fallen on hard times, and you think he's been like that forever? I was there. I fought in the Great War. I was on the field at Anvil. And when I was struck down, they left me. You understand? They left me for dead. I didn't leave them."* He will finish conversation with:

*"It's best you leave me be."* *"Sure, you make your retreat and I'll make mine."* If a Solitude citizen passes by, he may ask:

*"A coin or two for a veteran? It's not much to ask..."* *"You know you can afford a septim or two."*

### Dungeon Delving
**Were you really in the army?** *"I was the finest scout in the Legion, once. Tracked down all the places in the wilds that were causing people trouble. Lost my helmet in one of the old caves.
