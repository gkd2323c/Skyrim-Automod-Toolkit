[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/65/SR-npc-Staada.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Staada.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Staada.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Staada.jpg) Staada [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/SR-npc-Staada_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Staada_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Staada_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Staada_02.jpg) Staada without her helm **Staada** is a [Golden Saint](Skyrim_Golden_Saint.md) who set up camp under [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md)'s rock arch on the southeast side of the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md), directly under the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md). She is met during a miscellaneous quest started by reading *[A Request to Meet](Skyrim_A_Request_to_Meet.md)*, delivered by a courier after the quest [Restoring Order](Skyrim_Restoring_Order.md).

She is clad in a full set of [Golden Saint armor](Skyrim_Golden_Saint_Armor.md) and carries a random two-handed [Golden Saint weapon](Skyrim_Golden.md#Golden_Weapons).

## Related Quests
- **[Staada Quest](Skyrim_Staada_Quest.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Saints_%26_Seducers.md)</sup>: Meet with a mysterious figure regarding a [mad wizard](Skyrim_Thoron.md)'s failed ritual.

## Dialogue

- **You must be the one that sent that note.**
- *"Indeed."*

The following lead to peaceful resolutions, and she will reward you with her [own helmet](Skyrim_Staada%27s_Helmet.md) and teleport away:

- **I believe I have what you are looking for. (Give Sheogorath-Shaped Amber)**
- **I believe I have what you are looking for. (Give Sword of Jyggalag)**
- *"Very well. Here, take this for your troubles."*
- **I suppose this is goodbye.**
- *"It would seem so."*

The following leads to a battle, and the end result is that she teleports away and you lose the chance to get her helmet:

- **I have the artifact you seek, but I think I'll be keeping it. So long.**
- *"Fool! What do you think you're doing?"*

## Notes
- She doesn't actually wear the helmet she gives you, but rather a regular unenchanted one. Her gear cannot be obtained since it's not flagged as 'playable'.
- If you keep the Sheogorath-Shaped Amber, you can consume it at the [Atronach Forge](Skyrim_Atronach_Forge.md) for one of the following combinations. (You will lose the Sheogorath-Shaped Amber.) - **Summon a hostile Staada**: Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + Emerald
- **[Scroll of Conjure Staada](Skyrim_Scroll_of_Conjure_Staada.md)**: Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + Roll of Paper + Charcoal
- **[Spell Tome: Conjure Staada](Skyrim_Spell_Tome__Conjure_Staada.md)**: Sheogorath-Shaped Amber + Ruined Book + Bliss Bug Thorax + Sabre Cat Pelt
- [Staada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Staada) first appeared during [Azura's Quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Azura%27s_Quest) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*, where she was a member of the group of Daedra sent by [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) to disturb a priestess of [Azura](Lore_Azura.md) to settle a bet between the two Daedric Lords. She also appears in the *[Shivering Isles](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Shivering_Isles)* expansion for *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* during [The Helpless Army](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:The_Helpless_Army) (if you become the Duke/Duchess of Dementia), in which she is imprisoned in Brellach and must be freed to help quell the [Forces of Order](Lore_Forces_of_Order.md). She also appears as a [boss enemy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Staada) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), where she is located in the [Alik'r Desert](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Alik%27r_Desert). She is also depicted as a [card](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Brellach_Commander) in *[Legends](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Legends)*.
- There is a barrel in Staada's camp which only contains 14 [tongs](Skyrim_Miscellaneous_Items.md#Tongs). This is likely a reference to a [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Shivering:Work_is_Never_Done) in Shivering Isles where the player must gather 50 pairs of tongs and [calipers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Calipers), the latter not being brought back for Skyrim.
- On the table in Staada's camp is a two-pronged fork that cannot be interacted with. This is likely a reference to the Fork of Horripilation, a quest item used in Sheogorath's quest in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.

## Bugs
- Sometimes the courier may glitch and never actually delivers the letter which starts the quest. <sup>**?**</sup> - Wait about 5 in-game days and if nothing happens, go to her location under the Blue Palace. The north side, under the cliff. She may be there and if you have the required equipment she'll engage in normal conversation.
- It is possible to obtain infinite copies of Staada's Helmet. After choosing to give Staada the Sword of Jyggalag, you can exit the conversation and drop the sword before it is removed from your inventory. You will still receive the helmet and can pick up the sword again to repeat the glitch. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6, fixes this bug.
