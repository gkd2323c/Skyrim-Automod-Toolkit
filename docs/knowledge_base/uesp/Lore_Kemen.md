[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f4/OB-place-Kemen.jpg/200px-OB-place-Kemen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Kemen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Kemen.jpg) Kemen circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/ON-place-Kemen.jpg/200px-ON-place-Kemen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kemen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kemen.jpg) Kemen circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Kemen was an [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleids.md) settlement located in the foothills of the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). Its ruins can be found at the end of the [Blue Road](Lore_Blue_Road.md), east of the city of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Borba-1)</sup> The lower level of Kemen was known as the Varlasel,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBKV-2)</sup> or "Hall of Stars" in [Ayleidoon](Lore_Ayleidoon.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the entrance to Kemen was sealed. The nearby military stronghold of [Farragut Keep](Lore_Farragut_Keep.md) was located just to the north of the ruins and was the site of much contention during the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

By [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) the ruins had been opened, but its subterranean halls were crawling with undead.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-OBK-6)</sup> Kemen had been a well-known location among local adventurers for many years at that point.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Borba-1)</sup>
