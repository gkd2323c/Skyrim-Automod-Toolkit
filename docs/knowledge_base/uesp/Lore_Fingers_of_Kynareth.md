The Fingers of Kynareth are [spirits](Lore_Nature_Spirit.md) of the air associated with the Goddess of Winds and the Heavens [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md). Three spirits: **Amaro**, **Pina**, and **Tallatha** were given this title, but it is unknown whether there are more fingers of Kynareth. They are acknowledged by the witches of [Skeffington Coven](Lore_Skeffington_Coven.md) and occasionally requested for aid.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920HF-1)</sup>

A witch of the Skeffington Coven, Mynista Skeffington, claimed to have an intimate acquaintance with the Fingers. In [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) she requested their aid to protect [Turala](Lore_Turala.md) and her traveling companions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2920HF-1)</sup>

Despite this intimate acquaintance, the members of the Skeffington Coven believed that, although the spirits are great companions, it is hard to rely on them. Witches of the coven claimed that "the spirits, while marvelous companions and faultless tellers of truth, are often hazy about the when and hows", and that one cannot blame them, because concepts such as "when and how mean so little to them".<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2920FF-2)</sup>
