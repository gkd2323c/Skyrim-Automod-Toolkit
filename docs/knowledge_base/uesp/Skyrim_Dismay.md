**Dismay** is a [dragon shout](Skyrim_Dragon_Shouts.md) that causes enemies to flee from you. It can hit any enemies in its radius, similar to [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md).

## Possible Uses
- Occupying several weaker enemies to make way for a stronger foe.
- Lessening the odds against yourself when outnumbered.
- Clearing the way to flee out of a difficult situation.
- Buying time to safely transform into a [Werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md) or [Vampire Lord](Skyrim_Vampire_Lord.md).

## Notes
- Although its effects are similar to that of [Fear](Skyrim_Fear_(effect).md) spells, Dismay is *not* governed by the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skill and does not benefit from any perks. It also does not affect undead, Daedra, machines, or dragons.
