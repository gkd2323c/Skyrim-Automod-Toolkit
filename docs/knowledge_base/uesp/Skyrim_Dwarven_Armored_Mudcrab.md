It was released on November 30, 2017 and it has been offered for free.

## Official Summary
*It's a mystery how, why, or where this faithful companion received his special armor, but one thing's for certain - nobody's seen a Mudcrab like this before. (Pet can be purchased from Calcelmo in Markarth.)*
## Overview
This Creation allows you to purchase the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md) from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md).

## Notes
- The Dwarven Armored Mudcrab is a reference to the satirical "[Crab Armor DLC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-PC_Gamer_Crab_Armor.png)" advertisement from [*PC Gamer*](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_Gamer) in 2011, poking fun at the [Horse Armor Pack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Horse_Armor_Pack) for [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion).
- This Creation previously added the quest [My Pet Mudcrab](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:My_Pet_Mudcrab), which was automatically added to your journal upon installation and completion of Helgen. The quest directed you to [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) to purchase the [Dwarven Armored Mudcrab](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab_(creature).md) from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md). The Anniversary Edition on November 11, 2021 updated the Creation to no longer start the quest automatically.
- The Dwarven Armored Mudcrab can be obtained for free by pickpocketing the Ownership Deed off Calcelmo. The deed and Notice of Sale will lose quest item status once the Mudcrab is spoken with.
