[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/34/SR-item-Mace_of_Molag_Bal.jpg/200px-SR-item-Mace_of_Molag_Bal.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mace_of_Molag_Bal.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Mace_of_Molag_Bal.jpg) Mace of Molag Bal Mace of Molag Bal, also known as the **Vampire's Mace**, is one of [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s artifacts. It can be [improved](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) using an [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md). Its enchantment drains its victims of twenty-five points of [magicka](Skyrim_Damage_Magicka.md) and [stamina](Skyrim_Damage_Stamina.md) with every strike. Its enchantment will also inflict its target with [soul trap](Skyrim_Soul_Trap_(effect).md) for three seconds.

When walking around with the mace equipped, some NPCs may comment on it, saying: *"That mace... get it away from me. Get it away..."* or *"No.. get that accursed mace away from me..."*

## Bugs
- Though the mace can be tempered with an ebony ingot, it is *not* classified as an ebony weapon, nor is it classified as any other standard weapon material. Therefore, its upgrades do not benefit from any [smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) perks. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch) addresses this issue. It now requires the Daedric Smithing perk.
- The Damage Stamina enchantment incorrectly has the Magic Damage Fire keyword attached, meaning it, and thus the mace, are treated as having a [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) enchantment. To that effect NPCs will sometimes comment with standard lines for fire enchanted weapons, such as *"Have a fascination with fire, do we?"* or *"Don't set anything on fire, all right?"* **Mod Notes**: Ench Stamina Damage FFContact incorrectly has Magic Damage Fire keyword. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.2, fixes this bug.

- The mace's enchantments are directly affected by the [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md) perks in the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) perk tree and will increase to 31 with one perk, and 37 with the other. Similarly [Ahzidal's dragon priest mask](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(item).md) will increase the enchantments to 31, and when combined with both levels of Augmented Flames to 46. The soul trap effect is not affected.
