[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Heddic_Volunnar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Heddic_Volunnar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Heddic_Volunnar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Heddic_Volunnar.jpg) Heddic Volunnar **Heddic Volunnar** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md) who went searching through the ruin of [Volunruud](Skyrim_Volunruud.md) to find the tomb of [Kvenel the Tongue](Skyrim_Kvenel_the_Tongue.md), an ancient hero who may have been one of his ancestors. He went alone, expecting no conflict, but was killed by [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) while searching for a way to open the tomb. His [journal](Skyrim_Heddic%27s_Volunruud_Notes.md) can be found at the ruin's entrance.

He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0) with a pair of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) and [hide bracers](Skyrim_Hide_Bracers.md) and has a chance of carrying a rare [alchemy ingredient](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) and/or a [potion recipe](Skyrim_Recipes.md).
