Ralis Sedarys was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) financier from [Mournhold](Lore_Mournhold.md), whom arrived at [Raven Rock](Lore_Raven_Rock.md) in [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) and led the excavation of [Kolbjorn Barrow](Lore_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md) in [Southern](Lore_Solstheim_Ashlands.md) [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md). The [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) helped him dig out the barrow, where Sedarys was corrupted by the Dragon Priest of the Merethic Era, [Ahzidal](Lore_Ahzidal.md).
