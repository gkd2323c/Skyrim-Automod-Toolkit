[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-npc-Gerda.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Gerda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gerda.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Gerda.jpg) Gerda sweeping the main hall **Gerda** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) who works as a maid in [Dragonsreach](Skyrim_Dragonsreach.md).

Gerda works alongside fellow servant [Fianna](Skyrim_Fianna.md) and they are both tasked with sweeping the floor in Dragonsreach, specifically the waiting area near the entrance. At 8am, Gerda starts sweeping the southeastern area, while Fianna sweeps the adjacent area. At 2pm they switch places and keep sweeping until 8pm when they head down the stairs to their humble rooms in the [jarl's quarters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragonsreach_Jarl%27s_Quarters). They spend the entire night eating or relaxing, despite the presence of beds in their quarters (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

Gerda wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) along with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [lower-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Dialogue
*"I might just be a servant among lords, but don't think for a moment I don't take my duties as seriously as the [jarl](Skyrim_Balgruuf_the_Greater.md) himself takes his!"* *"If my cleanin' gets in your way, just say the word an' I'll be off."* *"This here is Dragonsreach, an' it's my task to see it looks fit for a jarl, night an' day."*
