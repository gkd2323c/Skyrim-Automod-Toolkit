[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/48/SR-creature-Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Emperor_Crab_Guardian_Spirit.jpg) Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit The **Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit** is a unique creature encountered during the [Wave Breaker](Skyrim_Wave_Breaker.md) quest. It is too large to be harmed by normal means, but can be defeated by firing catapults at it using the flaming pots collected by the [frenzied mudcrabs](Skyrim_Frenzied_Mudcrab.md) from the wrecked wagon.

## Related Quests
- **[Wave Breaker](Skyrim_Wave_Breaker.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md) is threatened by a giant crab!

## Notes
- The Emperor Crab Guardian Spirit is inspired by the Giant Mudcrab originally developed as part of the [Game Jam](Skyrim_Game_Jam.md) and featured in Todd Howard's keynote address at DICE 2012.
- From behind, the crab's shell resembles the [Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) -style houses seen in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). House Redoran have been known historically to use the shells of Emperor Crabs in their architecture.
- Technically, this is not a true creature, but more similar to scenery.
