## Quick Walkthrough
1. Read *[Bounty: Juvenile Mudcrab](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Juvenile_Mudcrab)*.
2. Speak to Viriya about taking over the bounty.
3. Obtain a juvenile mudcrab for Viriya.

## Detailed Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3a/SR-quest-In_A_Pinch.jpg/200px-SR-quest-In_A_Pinch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-In_A_Pinch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-In_A_Pinch.jpg) Mudcrab pool [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6f/SR-quest-In_A_Pinch_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-In_A_Pinch_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-In_A_Pinch_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-In_A_Pinch_02.jpg) The previous hire This quest will begin while you are doing [Angler Acquaintances](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Angler_Acquaintances), with you talking to [Viriya](Skyrim_Viriya.md):

*"The waiting... the insufferable waiting."* **What's wrong?** *"Here. I want you to have this. Don't ask. Just take it."* She will then hand you a [bounty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Juvenile_Mudcrab) for a juvenile mudcrab that she wants to study. The bounty points you in the direction of west of [Broken Fang Cave](Skyrim_Broken_Fang_Cave.md). After reading the bounty, speak to Viriya:

**I see. You hired someone to catch a juvenile mudcrab, but they never returned.** *"It would seem so."* **I wouldn't mind taking over the bounty.** *"Thanks. Good luck out there."* You can either catch a juvenile mudcrab from a fishing spot with a normal rod, or you can head to the southeast of [Gjukar's Monument](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gjukar%27s_Monument), where there is a shallow pool. Above the pool underneath a rocky outcropping is a dead [adventurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Adventurer) who has a [fishing rod](Skyrim_Fishing_Rod.md) and a copy of the [bounty](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bounty__Juvenile_Mudcrab). The pool itself contains the remains of a colossal mudcrab, a handful of hostile mudcrabs, and a juvenile mudcrab. Either defeat or ignore the hostile mudcrabs, then find and take the juvenile mudcrab within the crook of the giant mudcrab's outstretched arm.

Once you obtain the juvenile mudcrab, head back to Riften to hand it over to Viriya:

**I managed to find a juvenile mudcrab for you to study. Here you go.** *"Thanks. Couldn't have done that without you. I'm grateful for your efforts. Here, take this."* Viriya will reward you with 100 gold and a key to the Riften Fishery so that you can enter at any time. The juvenile mudcrab that you give her will be found as a mudcrab called [Snippy](Skyrim_Snippy.md) after this quest.
