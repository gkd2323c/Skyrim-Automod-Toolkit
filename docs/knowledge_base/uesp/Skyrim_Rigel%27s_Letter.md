Rigel's Letter **by [Rigel Strong-Arm](Skyrim_Rigel_Strong-Arm.md)** *A reply to a bandit's father's concerns*
---

- Now look here Da,

- You're the one who always told me that swinging an ax was the only thing I've ever been good at so what do you expect me to do? The [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) pay dung for soldiering and I'm not going to fight for those mud-sucking [Imperials](Skyrim_Imperial.md). I've got a good racket picking the traders off the road here and I couldn't give a damn what you think about it.

- Your 'little [sabrecat](Skyrim_Sabre_Cat.md)' has a tidy operation out here and I'm not going to give it up just cause you're afraid of getting caught.

- Oh and quit trying to send back the money. These louts just try to steal it and I have to beat their skull until they learn any different.

-- [Rigel Strong-Arm](Skyrim_Rigel_Strong-Arm.md)
