You can tame them and purchase saddles from stablemasters. The Creation also adds a unicorn, brought back from extinction by the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md). It was released on October 4, 2018 for 500 Creation Credits.

This Creation was made by [fadingsignal](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/users/2546964).

## Official Summary
Creation Club *The horses of Skyrim are hardy and strong, and taming them requires grit and patience. Use a hostler's map to find these animals in the wild and mount them, and try not to get bucked! Once tamed, you can register them at the nearest stable and customize their saddles, as well as the saddles of any other horse you own. You may even find the rare unicorn brought back from dead, to right a wrong of the past. (Quest to find a unicorn, "Creature of Legend," can be found by reading Soran's Journal at the Arcanaeum. A map to other horses may be purchased at any hostler.)* Sneak Peek - Aug 2018 *Traveling through the wilderness of Skyrim, you can now find wild horses to tame. Grab a map of sightings and a Detect Life spell to aid you in your search, or just set out into the wilderness! Once tamed, there are a selection of saddles you can purchase from stablemasters. The naturalist Soran Hariksen even claims to have seen a unicorn in the deep forests.*
## Summary
This Creation adds the ability to [tame](Skyrim_Horses.md#Tamable_Horses CC) up to seven [wild horses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wild_Horse) after completing the quest **[Horse Whisperer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horse_Whisperer)**. A [Unicorn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Unicorn) can also be tamed at the conclusion of the quest **[Creature of Legend](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Creature_of_Legend)**. Once tamed, wild horses will return to where they were originally found if you switch to a different mount, except for the Unicorn which will instead return to the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). This Creation also expands on the functionality of [stables](Skyrim_Stables.md) by adding the ability to purchase cosmetic [saddle](Skyrim_Horses.md#Saddles CC) options as well as give your horse a [randomized name](Skyrim_Horses.md#Horse_Naming CC). Both of these options are available to all horses, not only those that you tame.
