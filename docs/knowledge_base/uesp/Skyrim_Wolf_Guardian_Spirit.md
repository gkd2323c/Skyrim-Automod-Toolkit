[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9e/SR-creature-Wolf_Guardian_Spirit.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Wolf_Guardian_Spirit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf_Guardian_Spirit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Wolf_Guardian_Spirit.jpg) The Wolf Guardian Spirit The **Wolf Guardian Spirit** is a spectral [wolf](Skyrim_Wolf.md) found in a wolf den with several regular wolves. It will not appear until you are on the part of [the quest](Skyrim_Kyne%27s_Sacred_Trials.md) that asks you to hunt it.

Despite being spectral in appearance, the Wolf Guardian Spirit seems to retain all aspects of a normal wolf and is killable with normal weapons. When killed, it will leave behind ghostly remains, but the loot table matches a normal wolf as well.
