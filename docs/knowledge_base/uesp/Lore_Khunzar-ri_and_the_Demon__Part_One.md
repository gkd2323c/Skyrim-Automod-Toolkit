Khunzar-ri and the Demon: Part One **An ancient Khajiiti myth as recorded by Moon-Singer Azargo Long-Tale** *A Khajiiti legend detailing Khunzar-ri's conflict with dragons* *
---
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) were barely more than sixteen tribes, the great hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) went on many adventures. After Khunzar-ri defeated the [giant](Lore_Giant.md) Drol by consuming four-hundred-and-one [moon-sugar](Lore_Moon-sugar.md) pies, he met a [demon](Lore_Dragon.md).

The [demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) said, "My brothers and I shall take this land and its treasures. Even the moons from the sky!" Khunzar-ri objected, but the demon laughed. "You cannot stop us, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md)!"

Khunzar-ri said, "Not by myself, foul demon. I shall gather a Kra'Jun—a company of heroes—starting with the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md), [Nurarion](Lore_Nurarion.md) the Perfect, who communes among the buried roots of the Ayleid worms."

These are the words and the words are true.
