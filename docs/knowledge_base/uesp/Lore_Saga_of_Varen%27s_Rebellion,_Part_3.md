Saga of Varen's Rebellion, Part 3 **by [Capiton Casca](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Capiton_Casca), Poet to Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md)** *A poem portraying the start of Varen Aquilarios' rebellion*
---
Varen Victorious
By Capiton Casca, Poet to Regent Clivia Tharn

[Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md),
[Colovian](Lore_Colovian.md) hero, [Emperor](Lore_Emperor.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md),
Sat upon his Ruby Throne,
Brooding upon his fate.

After the debacle at [Fort Ash](Lore_Fort_Ash.md),
And reinforced by other rebel legions,
Varen's forces captured [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md), in the north,
And from there spread their [rebellion](Lore_Varen%27s_Rebellion.md),
Through all corners of the [Empire](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md).

The rebels fought most valiantly,
Against [Reach](Lore_Reachfolk.md) and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) alike,
Against betrayers and ambushers,
To the very walls of [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) itself.

They breached those walls,
And fought street by street,
House by house,
To the [Imperial Palace](Lore_Imperial_Palace.md).

And there Varen slayed [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md),
At the foot of the Ruby Throne,
Driving his sword into the Dragon's chest,
Spilling [Longhouse](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) blood at the foot of the dais.

Then Varen took the Ruby Throne,
Acclaimed by all his [legions](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md),
They broke the power of the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md),
And the people of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) rejoiced.

Yet Varen Aquilios was sorely vexed,
For while he sat upon the throne,
He was not of the blood of [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md),
As those who came before.

"Am I a false king?" he asked,
"Do I deserve this mighty boon?"
"I took the throne by force, not right."
"Am I worthy of my task?"

Then [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) appeared,
Now Chancellor to the Ruby Throne,
Father to [Clivia](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), wise in the ways of politics,
Who spoke these words to his Emperor King.

"It is true that you are not [Dragonborn](Lore_Dragonborn.md),"
"Yet I know how we may remedy this."
"I know a sorcerer, one [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md),"
"Who holds a secret you must know."

"There is a relic, the [Amulet of the Kings](Lore_Amulet_of_Kings.md)."
"Filled with great power and wisdom."
" It will restore the [Dragonfires](Lore_Dragonfires.md) that protect our [world](Lore_Nirn.md)."
"And make you the rightful ruler of the land."

Varen Aquilarios,
King of Cyrodiil, beloved by his people,
Left behind the Ruby Throne,
And gathered up his [loyal companions](Lore_Five_Companions.md),
To begin one final quest.
