[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/ON-place-Clott_River.jpg/200px-ON-place-Clott_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Clott_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Clott_River.jpg) Clott River The Clott River (or **The Clott**) is a waterway located east of [Cloudrest](Lore_Cloudrest.md) with two tributaries flowing from the [Eton Nir](Lore_Eton_Nir.md) into the northeastern shore of the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), to the waters of the [Auridon Strait](Lore_Auridon_Strait.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RGMap-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSM-2)</sup> One of the tributaries starts in the mountains near to the ruins of the Eldbur Monastery, an abandoned [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) facility,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Eldbur_Ruins-3)</sup> while another next to the [King's Haven Pass](Lore_King%27s_Haven_Pass.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESOSM-2)</sup>
