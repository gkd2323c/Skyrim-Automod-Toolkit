[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/ON-place-Tormented_Spire.jpg/200px-ON-place-Tormented_Spire.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tormented_Spire.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Tormented_Spire.jpg) The Tormented Spire circa 2E 582 The Tormented Spire is a volcano in southwestern [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md), in the [Varanis](Lore_Varanis.md) region, east of [Kragenmoor](Lore_Kragenmoor.md). Many [Daedric](Lore_Daedric.md) ruins can be found inside the volcano's caldera. The upper parts of the volcano could be accessed only with a key kept by [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md) in Kragenmoor.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup>

The volcano served as an entrance to [Sadal's Prison](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Sadal%27s_Prison), a small realm of Oblivion designed to imprison [Sadal](Lore_Sadal.md), one of the [Brothers of Strife](Lore_Brothers_of_Strife_(creature).md). In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md), the grandmaster of [House Indoril](Lore_House_Indoril.md), climbed the Tormented Spire and unleashed Sadal to exact revenge on the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) for the death of Tanval's son, Garyn. However, Sadal was too dangerous and had to be subdued. With the help of the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), Sadal was defeated and bound again before he could return to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CTS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OTP-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SFD-4)</sup>
