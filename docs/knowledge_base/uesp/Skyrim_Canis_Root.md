In [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), the bushes are found in birch woods near rock formations or at the base of cliffs. They are also found in swampy regions such as [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md) and along the northern coast of [Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md).

## Ingredients
51 guaranteed samples can be found in 32 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 5 in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) ([College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md))
- 3 in [Broken Tower Redoubt](Skyrim_Broken_Tower_Redoubt.md) ([The Reach](Skyrim_The_Reach.md))
- 3 in [Cragwallow Slope](Skyrim_Cragwallow_Slope.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 3 in [Dead Men's Respite](Skyrim_Dead_Men%27s_Respite.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 3 in [the Midden](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Midden) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))

With the [Dragonborn add-on](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) 22 more guaranteed samples can be found in 7 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 10 in [Tel Mithryn Kitchen](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_Kitchen.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> ([Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(settlement).md))
- 7 in [Tel Mithryn](Skyrim_Tel_Mithryn_(tower).md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- According to the book *[On Lycanthropy](Lore_On_Lycanthropy.md)*, found in [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall), a Skyrim tradition is to rub canis root on trees as a ward against werebears. However, there are no werebears present in mainland Skyrim, perhaps denoting this tradition's success.
- [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> is particularly fond of canis root tea, though you cannot actually make tea from canis roots in-game.
- Although canis root grows also in Hjaalmarch, the biggest ones tend to grow in and around the mountains of The Rift, with the largest of all being inside [Angarvunde](Skyrim_Angarvunde.md), which dwarf the height of an Altmer male in size.
