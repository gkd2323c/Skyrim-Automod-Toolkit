[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-item-Staff_of_Worms.jpg/200px-SR-item-Staff_of_Worms.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Worms.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Staff_of_Worms.jpg) Staff of Worms The Staff of Worms is a powerful staff once owned by [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md), King of Worms. The staff is able to revive the corpse of a targeted NPC. The reanimated corpse will become your ally.

## Related Quests
- **[Dreams of the Dead](Skyrim_Dreams_of_the_Dead.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Gallows_Hall.md)</sup>: Unlock the secrets of a necromancer's lair and make it your own.

## Notes
- The staff uses the [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) spell, as indicated if you try to resurrect something resistant to it ("X resisted Dead Thrall"). As a consequence, the casting time is very long, you will need to gesture with both hands, and you cannot reanimate animals or any corpse that serves as a passive container. This includes Naara, who resists any attempts to revive her.
- This staff is the only one available which casts a Master level spell and gives you access to Dead Thrall without needing to complete the [quest](Skyrim_Conjuration_Ritual_Spell.md), which would give you the spell tome.
- Uniquely, the staff can resurrect [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md), intact [skeletons](Skyrim_Skeleton.md), [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md), [rieklings](Skyrim_Riekling.md), and [bristlebacks](Skyrim_Bristleback.md) —creatures the Dead Thrall spell is unable to reanimate.

## Bugs
- Similar to the [Dead Thrall](Skyrim_Dead_Thrall.md) spell, it needs to be re-cast on an already resurrected thrall for the [Fortified Healing](Skyrim_Regenerate_Health.md) to take effect. <sup>**?**</sup>
- NPCs will react as if the player is still holding the staff with uneasy dialogue, even if the staff is unequipped and/or in a follower's inventory.
