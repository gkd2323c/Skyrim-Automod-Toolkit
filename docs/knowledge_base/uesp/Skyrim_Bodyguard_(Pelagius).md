[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-npc-Bodyguard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bodyguard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodyguard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bodyguard.jpg) Bodyguard The **Bodyguards** are two [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizens](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who sit on either side of [Pelagius the Suspicious](Skyrim_Pelagius_the_Suspicious.md) during the quest [The Mind of Madness](Skyrim_The_Mind_of_Madness.md), observing the arena fight between two [atronachs](Skyrim_Daedra.md). They wear [Dwarven armor](Skyrim_Dwarven_Armor.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Dwarven_Boots.md), [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dwarven_Gauntlets.md) and a [helmet](Skyrim_Dwarven_Helmet.md). When you hit one of them with the [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md), they will both turn into [wolves](Skyrim_Wolf.md) and kill Pelagius.
