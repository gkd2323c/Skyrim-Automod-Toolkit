# Argonian


They sometimes face prejudice in the land of Skyrim, as they look vastly different from the other humanoid races of Tamriel. The term "lizard" is often used as an insult towards Argonians.

There is also a community of Argonians who work at the docks in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md). They have their own [living quarters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Argonian_Assemblage) there.

For more information, see the [main lore article](Lore_Argonian.md).

## In-Game Description
*This reptilian race, well-suited for the treacherous swamps of their [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) homeland, has developed a natural resistance to diseases and the ability to breathe underwater. They can call upon the Histskin to regenerate health very quickly.*

## Traits
- *Resist Disease* ability (Form ID `00104acf`): Your Argonian blood is 50% [resistant to disease](Skyrim_Resist_Disease.md), constant.
- *Waterbreathing* ability (Form ID `000aa01b`): Your Argonian lungs can [breathe underwater](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md), constant.
- *Histskin* greater power (Form ID `000e40d5`): Invoke the power of the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) to [recover health](Skyrim_Regenerate_Health.md) ten times faster for 60 seconds, once per day.
- *Raw Diet*<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md)</sup> (Form ID: `xx00088d`): Your Argonian blood allows you to eat raw meat without risk of disease.
- *Weakness to Cold*<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md)</sup> (Form ID `xx000875`): Argonians become [colder](Skyrim_Cold.md) 25% faster in cold environments.
- Height: 1.01 (M), 1 (F)
- In addition, their claws do +6 damage in unarmed combat

## Skills
- +10 [Lockpicking](Skyrim_Lockpicking.md)
- +5 [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md), [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md), [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md), [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md), [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md)

## Gameplay
Argonians make good thieves, as they have the right stat boosts. The underwater breathing makes them good underwater treasure hunters and fishers; it also provides them with a good way to escape enemies by swimming. A +5 boost to Alteration and Restoration gives them good magical potential. Their claw bonus also helps them win brawls.

Their Histskin power plays a vital role (particularly in early gameplay), saving players from near death against more powerful opponents by making them essentially invincible for a minute.
