[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/40/SR-item-Ring_of_the_Hunt.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_the_Hunt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Hunt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_the_Hunt.jpg) Ring of the Hunt The Ring of the Hunt is a ring that can be bought from [Majni](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Majni) in [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md) if you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md). Its appearance is identical to a [silver ruby ring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ruby_Ring), and also has the same weight. Its enchantment allows you to [regenerate health in werewolf form](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostmoon_Hunt). Multiple copies of these rings can be bought once Majni's merchant chest resets.

## Notes
- While the ring applies the effect *Frostmoon Predator* to display the enchantment text, the actual effect is applied by a perk called *[Frostmoon Hunt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frostmoon_Hunt)* and is added separately upon transformation.
