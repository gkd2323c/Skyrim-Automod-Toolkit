[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/SR-npc-Beautiful_Barbarian.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Beautiful_Barbarian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Beautiful_Barbarian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Beautiful_Barbarian.jpg) Beautiful Barbarian The **Beautiful Barbarian** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and a possible assassination target during the recurring quest [The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md). If chosen as the target, she will appear in the center of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), just outside the [Vilemyr Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vilemyr_Inn).

She wears a full set of [Stormcloak Officer Armor](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Armor.md) and wields a [steel greatsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Greatsword).

## Related Quests
- **[The Dark Brotherhood Forever](Skyrim_The_Dark_Brotherhood_Forever.md)**: Yet another child has prayed to their [mother](Skyrim_Night_Mother.md).

## Quest-Related Events

### The Dark Brotherhood Forever
Not knowing of her fate, she will ironically say to the player:

- *"I'm dying for a rest. I swear, I could lie down right now, and just sleep forever."*

## Notes
- According to game data, she was originally meant to be found in the now nonexistent village of [Granite Hill](Skyrim_Fort_Sungard.md#Notes), rather than Ivarstead.

## Bugs
- Sometimes the barbarian will appear with nothing equipped, despite having armor in her inventory.
