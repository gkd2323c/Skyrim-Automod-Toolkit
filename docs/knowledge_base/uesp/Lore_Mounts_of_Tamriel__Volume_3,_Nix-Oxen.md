Mounts of Tamriel: Volume 3, Nix-Oxen **by [Fadus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fadus_Falto) of Skingrad** *Account of myth regarding the domestication of Nix-Oxen* *
---
I sat conversing with the dynamic and thoughtful Kylia Thando at the Abbey of Saint Delyn, an inn in the stony heart of [Vivic City](Lore_Vivec_City.md), as she related to me the fascinating history of the [nix-ox](Lore_Nix-Ox.md) and its domestication. [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and the [ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) are a cauldron of unique creatures of all kinds, and this enormous insect plays a myriad of roles across the region: caravan beast of burden, plow-pulling farm laborer, and of course sturdy riding mount.

The history of the [Dark Elf](Lore_Dark_Elf.md) [houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) hold that these creatures did not exist prior to the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md)'s arrival from distant shores. Or at least, they did not exist at the prodigious size and strength they do now. The volcanic lands in which that culture found itself following revered [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) to this new part of the world was lacking in all the beasts and birds and comforts they had known in the idyllic [Summerset isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). Myth holds that the nix-oxen were a gift to the Prophet from [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) herself. The remaking of a tiny insect into a creature capable of carrying her favored daughters and sons into the future she saw for them all.
