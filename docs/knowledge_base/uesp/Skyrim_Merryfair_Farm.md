[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/dd/SR-place-Merryfair_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-place-Merryfair_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Merryfair_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Merryfair_Farm.jpg) Merryfair Farm **Merryfair Farm** is a poor, small farm northwest of [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), owned and run by [Dravin Llanith](Skyrim_Dravin_Llanith.md) and his wife [Synda Llanith](Skyrim_Synda_Llanith.md). The farm is located just west of [Riften Stables](Skyrim_Riften_Stables.md), on the north shore of [Lake Honrich](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lake_Honrich) in [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md).

The couple make a poor living growing bushels of [wheat](Skyrim_Wheat.md), [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage) and [gourds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd). Their [cow](Skyrim_Cow.md) provides them with a fresh supply of [milk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Jug_of_Milk) and Dravin can often be seen milling wheat for their [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread).

Speaking to Dravin reveals that there was a recent break-in at the farm. Among the items that were stolen was [Dravin's bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dravin%27s_Bow). He believes the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) was behind the burglary and that they stashed his bow somewhere in the [Ratway](Skyrim_The_Ratway.md) beneath [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md). This gives you an opportunity to accept retrieving it as a [quest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bow_to_the_Master). Once you complete the quest you will be able to freely take most of the items from the farm.

### Merryfair Farm
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/06/SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm.jpg) Main room The front door is normally novice-locked. Upon entering there is a wall shelf immediately on your left holding a pile of clutter and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). There is a doorway to the right of the shelf in the west wall. There is a crate with a couple of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sacks against the north wall with a bottle of wine on the crate, next to a chair that is pulled up close to the fireplace. The fireplace is in the east wall and has a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) in front of it. To the right is a set of shelves holding a few [potatoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a [cabbage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), two loaves of [bread](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bread) and several pieces of cheese. Against the south wall is a small square wooden table and two chairs, with a bottle of wine, a cabbage, a loaf of bread and an [eidar cheese wedge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wedge) on the table. The doorway to the west leads to the top of stairs leading down to the cellar; there are barrels, sacks and a crate at the top of the stairs.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Merryfair_Farm_02.jpg) Bedroom Descending the stairs to the south brings you to a storage area with a pile of firewood and a couple of straw bales under the stairs and several crates and barrels along the north wall and in the northeastern corner. To the east is a doorway that leads into a large bedroom with a king-sized bed with an end table to the right of it and a crate in the northeastern corner. Against the east wall is a tall wardrobe containing [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and a chair in the southeastern corner. There is a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse) under the left side of the bed and a dresser against the west wall with copies of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun)*, *[Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md)*, *[Olaf and the Dragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Olaf_and_the_Dragon)*, *[The Woodcutter's Wife](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Woodcutter%27s_Wife)* and *[The City of Stone](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_City_of_Stone)*.

## Notes
- With [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md), a [clay deposit](Skyrim_Clay.md) can be found southwest of the farm, along the shoreline.
