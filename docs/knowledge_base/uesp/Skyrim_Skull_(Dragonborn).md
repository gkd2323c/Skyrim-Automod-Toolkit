[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Skull_2.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Skull_2.png) Kolbjorn Skull This Skull is located inside [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), found on a [weight sensitive plate](Skyrim_Weight_Sensitive_Plate.md) in the second main room. It differs from standard [skulls](Skyrim_Skull.md) in that it is engraved and worth 50 gold instead of the normal 5 gold.

## Notes
- The skull may be [Oddfrid White-Lip](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Oddfrid_White-Lip) from the *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* quest [The Sad Seer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:The_Sad_Seer), given the recurrent location.

- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item and calls it the **Kolbjorn Skull**.
