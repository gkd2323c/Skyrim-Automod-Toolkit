Bounty: Dwarven Investigation **by [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md)**
---
Fishermen have rumored that at the mouth of [Bronze Water Cave](Skyrim_Bronze_Water_Cave.md) lies a strange mechanism, undoubtedly of Dwarven make, whose purpose is yet unknown. As my research leaves me too preoccupied, I seek a reliable surrogate to inspect the mechanism. Any novel Dwarven objects, schematics, and the like are of great interest. Any discoveries should be reported to me at once.

Disturb me only when you have results, not before.

-- [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md)
