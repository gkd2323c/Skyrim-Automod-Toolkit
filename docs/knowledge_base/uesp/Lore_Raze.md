# Raze

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/97/AR-place-Camlorn.jpg/200px-AR-place-Camlorn.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Camlorn.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-place-Camlorn.jpg) Camlorn The Raze dynasty was a clan of royal [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) active in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) under the [Septim Empire](Lore_Septim_Empire.md), who ruled the [Kingdom of Camlorn](Lore_Camlorn_(kingdom).md) and the Duchy of Oloine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mo T-1)</sup>

## History

### Usurpation of King Raze
In [3E 385](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_385), the King of Camlorn (patriarch of the Raze family) was secretly overthrown by his brother, the Duke of Oloine, who made himself the new King of Camlorn. A child swap took place, with the Duke abandoning his daughter, Jyllia Raze, and taking on his niece, Crown Princess Talara Raze, as his own—however, the daughters were then referred to by different names, with Talara believing she was raised as if she were her cousin Jyllia.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mo T-1)</sup>

### Mystery of Talara
The Raze family was heavily featured in a series of historical fiction volumes called *[Mystery of Talara](Lore_Mystery_of_Talara.md)*, written by [Mera Llykith](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mera_Llykith) and set around the year [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405). In the books, Lady Jyllia Raze (who had grown up in [Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md), believing herself to have been a girl named Gyna) unravels a conspiracy around the Raze dynasts with the help of [Lord Naigon Strale](Lore_Naigon_Strale.md) and [Lady Brisienna Magnessen](Lore_Brisienna_Magnessen.md) of the [Blades](Lore_Blades.md), ultimately culminating in Princess Talara Raze assuming her true identity and being restored to her rightful throne.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mo T-1)</sup> The King of Camlorn, his wife, and his son Sylon Raze were then removed from power.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Mo T-1)</sup>

### Reign of Duke Senhyn
After the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md), Camlorn entered into a marriage alliance with the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md). Around [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), Duke Senhyn is described as the reigning monarch of Camlorn, and he had a daughter named Lady Kelmena who wed Prince Camaron of Daggerfall as a way of unifying the kingdom along the western coast.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3High-2)</sup> It is unknown if the Duke is related to the Raze family or not.

## Notes
- Duke Sebastien Rielle was the ruler of Camlorn around 2E 582.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLineage-6)</sup> Similarly, King Vulstaed is described as the King of Camlorn around [3E 63](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_63).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TWQ1-7)</sup> It is unknown if both or either were members of the Raze family or not.
- While the *[Mystery of Talara](Lore_Mystery_of_Talara.md)* volumes which describe the Raze family are considered in-world fiction, the [Naigon Strale](Lore_Naigon_Strale.md) and [Brisienna Magnessen](Lore_Brisienna_Magnessen.md) characters who appear in the volumes are real historical figures, indicating that the books were logically based on real events and persons.
