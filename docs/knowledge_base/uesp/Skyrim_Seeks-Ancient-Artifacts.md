[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d4/SR-npc-Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts.jpg) Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts' body **Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts** was an [Argonian](Skyrim_Argonian.md) scholar and mage. His [first journal](Skyrim_Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v1.md), left at [the Frozen Hearth](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md) in [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md), will begin the quest [The Sanctuary and the Manufactory](Skyrim_The_Sanctuary_and_the_Manufactory.md).

Artifacts was able to successfully hire [Bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) to aid him in excavating [Frostroot Cave](Skyrim_Frostroot_Cave.md) and finding the entrance to the ancient Dwemer house of Nchuanthumz. However, even though these Bandits trust him and did not betray him, they will be hostile to you on sight as you follow the trail of clues left by their employer. The Bandits will have to be Calmed or killed to proceed.

After entering the ruin, Artifacts ended up incurring the wrath of the remaining [automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md). His final resting place is the [Manufactory](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md#Nchuanthumz_Manufactory) in the basement of [Nchuanthumz](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz.md). His [second and final journal](Skyrim_Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v2.md) gives instructions on how to finish what he started, restoring the home to its former glory. The control panel he died in front of will allow you to summon a Dwarven Sphere Overseer to help you begin renovating Nchuanthumz, once you have provided the spare parts listed by the second journal.

Artifacts wears a pair of [apprentice robes of destruction](Skyrim_Apprentice_Robes_of_Destruction.md) with matching [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006b46c). In his inventory he carries 26 [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), a [Dwemer Actuator](Skyrim_Dwemer_Actuator.md), an [Iron Dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and a random ingredient.
