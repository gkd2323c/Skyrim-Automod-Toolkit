[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d1/OB-place-Lake_Poppad.jpg/200px-OB-place-Lake_Poppad.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Poppad.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Poppad.jpg) Lake Poppad circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) Lake Poppad is a small, isolated lake in the forested wilderness of the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md) region of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). It is north of the [Corbolo River](Lore_Corbolo_River.md)'s source, southwest of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), and far to the east of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and the [Red Ring Road](Lore_Red_Ring_Road.md). A shrine to [Vaermina](Lore_Vaermina.md) is on its northeastern shore. [Nirnroot](Lore_Nirnroot.md) has been known to grow on its banks. It originally fed a tributary of the [Reed River](Lore_Reed_River.md) circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), although this stream had seemingly dried up by [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Oblivion-2)</sup>
