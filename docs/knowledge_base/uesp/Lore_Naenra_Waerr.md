# Naenra Waerr

**Witch** Naenra Waerr was an ancient and legendary [witch](Lore_Witches.md) and [crafter](Lore_Smithing.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup> She was a legendary figure within [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) legends and traditions,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Witchwise Headdress-3)</sup> and was responsible for the creation,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup> or, according to other sources, [enchantment](Lore_Enchanting.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup> of [Umbra](Lore_Umbra.md), a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) designed with the sole purpose of the entrapment of souls.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup> It was created at the request of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), who wanted the sword as a tool of mischief which would send him souls from the mortal realm.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup>

According to some versions of the tale Naenra was believed to be [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) in disguise.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup>

## History

### Ancient Past
At some point before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Witchwise Headdress-3)</sup> Naenra created the [Umbra](Lore_Umbra.md) at the request of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), who wanted the sword to become a tool of mischief which would send him souls from the mortal realm. However, the blade was unstable, requiring Vile to give the witch a piece of his power to be imbued in the sword in order to stabilize it. Naenra Waerr supposedly managed to trick him, as the piece of Vile's power imbued in the sword became a sentient being that called itself Umbra, which was not the original intention of the Daedric Prince.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup>

Naenra was found and executed for her abhorrent creation, but not before she hid the sword.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TL-2)</sup>

### Legacy
The sword took on a life of its own, remaining hidden until a worthy wielder was found. Umbra then slowly takes control of the wielder. The Umbra Sword can change shape, but it is always in the form of a bladed weapon. It changed its owners over time and was present throughout the history of Tamriel. It was known to be wielded by many warriors, such as an [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) whose name was lost to history,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWUMB-4)</sup> [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) Lenwin,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-OBUMB-5)</sup> and [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) Cresitus.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-CC-6)</sup>

Naenra Waerr was remembered among the witches and held in high esteem. [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) witches who pass the final test of wisdom and power honor Naenra Waerr by crafting masks akin to one they believe she used to wear when crafting Umbra.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Witchwise Headdress-3)</sup>

## Artifacts

### Umbra
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/59/LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Umbra.jpg) Umbra (Legends) The [Umbra Sword](Lore_Umbra.md) (or simply **Umbra**) is a [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) designed with the sole purpose of the entrapment of souls. Sometime before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the ancient witch Naenra Waerr created the sword at the request of [Clavicus Vile](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md), who wanted the sword as a tool of mischief which would send him souls from the mortal realm. Naenra created the sword but it was unstable, requiring Vile to give the witch a piece of his power to be imbued in the sword in order to stabilize it. However, Naenra (sometimes accredited with being [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) in disguise) supposedly tricked him, as the piece of Vile's power imbued in the sword became a sentient being which called itself **Umbra**. Naenra was executed for her evil creation, but not before she hid the sword. The sword took on a life of its own, remaining hidden until a worthy wielder was found. Umbra then slowly takes control of the wielder. The Umbra Sword has the ability to change shape, but it is always in the form of a bladed weapon. It has been reported as a black and silver claymore, a jet black longsword and as a black sword emblazoned with red markings. The sword is also known to have the ability to absorb the health and stamina of those it strikes.

### Witchwise Headdress
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/41/ON-hat-Witchwise_Headdress.jpg/150px-ON-hat-Witchwise_Headdress.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Witchwise_Headdress.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-hat-Witchwise_Headdress.jpg) Reachfolk witch mask crafted in the likeness of the mask worn by Naenra The [Witchwise Headdress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Witchwise_Headdress) was a ceremonial mask with ram horns imagery embedded in the folklore of the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md). According to legend, it was worn by the ancient [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) [witch](Lore_Witches.md) Naenra Waerr during the creation of [Umbra](Lore_Umbra.md). In commemoration of her, many witches of Reachfolk descent have adopted similar masks, drawing inspiration from the designs of the original. These witches, upon passing the final trial of wisdom and power, as decreed by a clan elder, were granted the privilege of crafting their own headdress, a symbol of their mastery and status within the clan. [Arana](Lore_Arana.md), the leader of the [Wayward Guardians](Lore_Wayward_Guardians.md), and a former matron of the [Ghostsong Clan](Lore_Ghostsong_Clan.md) was known to wear one of her own.
