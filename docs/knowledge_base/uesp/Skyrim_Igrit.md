[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cf/SR-npc-Igrit.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Igrit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Igrit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Igrit.jpg) Igrit **Igrit** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who can be found inside [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Cavern.md), attending the banquet during [Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md). If she survives, she, along with all other survivors, will make her way out to Bloodchill Cavern. The next time you return to the area, she will no longer be present.

She wears [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md) and [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md). She wields a leveled dagger.

## Related Quests
- **[Guests for Dinner](Skyrim_Guests_for_Dinner.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>: Attend a feast.

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Before the feast:

- **Are you here for the dinner party?**
- *"That is so."*

- **Do you think the food will be any good?**
- *"Of course."*

If you save her from the vampires:

- *"Gods' blessings on you."*
