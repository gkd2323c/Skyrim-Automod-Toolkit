## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A stream flows out of the cave, forming a waterfall over a small cliff in front of the entrance. A [dead Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit_(dead).md) can be found at the base of the waterfall, with a troll skull and a humanoid skull nearby. An [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) is just east of the entrance.

### Brood Cavern
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/af/SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brood_Cavern_Fishing.jpg) Fishing supplies The partially flooded cavern features two waterfalls flowing down the cave walls, with a stream leading back out the entrance. Many scenic plants grow in the cavern, but only three [fly amanita](Skyrim_Fly_Amanita.md) fungus and a few [hanging moss](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) are harvestable. The cavern contains three leveled animals or other creatures, which may include [cave bears](Skyrim_Cave_Bear.md) or [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md). As you enter, on the left is the corpse of a female [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [hunter](Skyrim_Hunter.md) near a [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md), with a random [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md), a random [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md), and some random [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md) next to them. A copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Black Arrow, v2](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Black_Arrow,_v2)* can be found on the corpse. A dead [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) is on one of the islands of dry land. A novice-locked chest is in one of the short side tunnels off the main cavern, behind the waterfall on the right wall. A [corundum ore vein](Skyrim_Corundum_Ore_Vein.md) is just to the left of the waterfall at the rear of the room, and an iron ore vein is on the south wall.

With the [Fishing](Skyrim_Fishing.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, some [Fishing Supplies](Skyrim_Fishing_Supplies.md) can be found here; fish from [Underground](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Biomes) waters can be found here.

## Notes
- If you return to the cave after initially clearing it, the dead hunter, poison, bow, and arrows may not respawn.
- According to the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Creation_Kit), the dead hunter is named **Urwa**.
