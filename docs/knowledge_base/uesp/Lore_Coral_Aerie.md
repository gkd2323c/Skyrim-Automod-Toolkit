[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2a/ON-place-Coral_Aerie_02.jpg/200px-ON-place-Coral_Aerie_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Coral_Aerie_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Coral_Aerie_02.jpg) Coral Aerie ca. 2E 582 Coral Aerie is a small island found off the northern coast of [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UCB-1)</sup> in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). This little islet far off the coast of Summerset sports high cliffs and deep tide pools that serve as popular breeding grounds for numerous species of maritime fauna, migratory birds, and large predators, such as [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CA-2)</sup> Partially-submerged ancient [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmer.md) ruins of are one of the dominant features of the islet.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Z.27Baza-3)</sup>

At some point, a [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) from [Thras](Lore_Thras.md) named Z'Baza was imprisoned on Coral Aerie using mysterious magic unrelated to the Aldmer or [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) of Summerset.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Z.27Baza-3)</sup> Her captors were related in some way to the [Eleven Forces](Lore_Eleven_Forces.md), and chose to imprison her on the island as they lacked the means to return her to Thras.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AI-4)</sup>

Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Ascendant Order](Lore_Ascendant_Order.md) set up a base of operations on Coral Aerie and were using the islet to breed and train gryphon mounts.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-GWL-5)</sup> These activities were discovered when the Order kidnapped Jakarn, an agent of the [Society of the Steadfast](Lore_Society_of_the_Steadfast.md). A group of [Undaunted](Lore_Undaunted.md) subsequently arrived on the island to rescue Jakarn and drive out the Ascendant Order.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UCB-1)</sup> It is believed Z'Baza aided the Undaunted in this attack in order to fool the group into releasing her from her magical bindings, although she was swiftly defeated once freed.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Z.27Baza-3)</sup>
