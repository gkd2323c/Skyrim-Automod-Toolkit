Kinlord Orlemar's Notes **by [Orlemar Corelanya](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Orlemar_Corelanya)** *A letter about an attempt to use the Eyes of the Colossal* *
---
A disaster narrowly averted today.

I ordered mages from the College of Tomes to replace the statue's eyes and march it to [Sunport](Lore_Sunport.md). An [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) colossal would be a significant addition to the city's defenses, and it serves no purpose in Mor Naril. Unfortunately, the colossal ignored our commands and turned on our mages! A pair of brave knights succeeded in blinding the statue again, and we left it where it stood.

My advisors now believe the colossal must be directed by a guiding spirit. Possessed by an Argonian shaman, perhaps. How the Argonians manage such a thing, they could not say.

Until we can resolve this quandary, I think it best to keep the Eyes of the Colossal safe in my vault. In time, I am sure we will master this enchantment.

[Orlemar](Lore_Orlemar_Corelanya.md), Kinlord of [House Corelanya](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md)
Sunport, Isle of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md)
