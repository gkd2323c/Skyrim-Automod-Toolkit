# Nightside Asylum

| Nightside Asylum | |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Place |
| Continent | [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) |

Nightside Asylum was the secret headquarters of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMephala-1)</sup> which may or may not be located in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> A [Night Mother](Lore_Night_Mother.md) resided within the Asylum as the sanctuary's leader.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup> During the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Asylum's location was once under threat of being exposed by a noble in the Iliac Bay, who was seeking to root out the Dark Brotherhood's headquarters.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMephala-1)</sup> Around the same time, the Brotherhood authored their letters with the name "Nightside Asylum" written at the top.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFNobles Debts-3)</sup>

By [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), the last Dark Brotherhood sanctuary on Tamriel was a chapterhouse in [Falkreath](Lore_Falkreath.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Astrid-4)</sup> As such, it can be assumed that Nightside Asylum was defunct by this time.

## Notes
- | [^](#note_nb1) | Nightside Asylum was originally going to appear in *Daggerfall*, but was cut during development.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup> It is still referenced in the final game,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFMephala-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFNobles Debts-3)</sup> but its exact location on Tamriel has not been confirmed. |
| --- | --- |
- During a cut quest about joining the [Dark Brotherhood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Dark_Brotherhood), the player character of *Daggerfall* would have had a blindfold placed over their eyes and be taken to Nightside Asylum to meet the [Night Mother](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:The_Night_Mother) for initiation into the order.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-DFPreview-2)</sup> The following are the cut dialogue lines from said quest:

- - *"Greetings to you, `%pcn`. The Dark Brotherhood has been watching you, and knows you to be worthy of training. I will bring you to the Night Mother who will tell you of the Brotherhood, shall I?"*
- *"Very well, `%pcn`. I remember fearing the unknown myself, many years ago. Perhaps you will change your heart later. Until then, farewell."*
- *"You are blindfolded, and your body turns cold as if it were passing through the cold earth. Feeling returns, and with it, warmth."*
- *"Welcome to Nightside Asylum, sanctuary of the Dark Brotherhood. Here and elsewhere in Tamriel we can train you and offer you opportunities to become a master assassin. We require only your loyalty, and your talents when needed. Will you join us?"*
- *"The Nightmother frowns, and a blindfold is pulled around your eyes. As coldness fills your limbs, you are warned not to try to find Nightside Asylum again. Gradually, you feel warmth, and the blindfold falls from your eyes. You are alone."*
- *"Good decision, young `%pcf`. From this moment on, your codeword will be %dbp. Look for that on town bulletins and other places for directions to local chapterhouses of the Dark Brotherhood. You are now considered a %lev. Feel free to explore Nightside Asylum, but consider that any locked doors are locked for a reason."*
