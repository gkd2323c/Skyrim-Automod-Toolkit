# Dark Orbs


Dark Orbs were primarily utilized by Meridia's Daedric servants, the [Aurorans](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Auroran), although instances of their use were also documented among [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) rulers like King [Narilmor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Narilmor) and the semi-divine [Umaril the Unfeathered](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Umaril).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOB-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTUQ-2)</sup>

The [Undaunted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Undaunted) also crafted and distributed decorative replicas resembling one of the Dark Orbs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RCOOM-1)</sup>

## Colored Orbs
Different varieties of Orbs existed, referred to as **colored orbs**,<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CB-5)</sup> each granting Meridia's chosen various powers:

- The **Blazing Orb**, red and linked to fiery passion and intense energy, bestowed fire-related abilities;<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BO-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KN-7)</sup>
- The **Phosphorescent Orb**, blue and connected to calmness, inevitability, and despair, granted ice-based powers;<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-PO-8)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KN-7)</sup>
- The **Scintillating Orb**, purple and reminiscent of regality and striking night skies, conferred lightning abilities;<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SO-9)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KN-7)</sup>
- The **Radiant Orb**, yellow in hue, enhanced their defenses.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-RO-10)</sup><sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DO-11)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-KN-7)</sup>

## History
Narilmor's Auroran allies were empowered not only by a Dark Orb but also by Blazing, Phosphorescent, Scintillating, and Radiant Orbs.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DO-11)</sup> After his transformation into the Symphony of Blades, a [Blessed Sentinel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Blessed_Sentinel), Narilmor wielded the powers of the latter four Orbs.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOB-4)</sup> During Umaril's return in [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433), Aurorans in his service utilized one of these orbs in [Garlas Malatar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Garlas_Malatar), which was subsequently destroyed by the [Hero of Kvatch](Lore_Hero_of_Kvatch.md) along with the defeat of the Aurorans in their quest to kill Umaril.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-OGM-12)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTUQ-2)</sup> All known cases of Dark Orb usage occurred either in Garlas Malatar or within Meridia's realm, the [Colored Rooms](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Colored_Rooms).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-DO-11)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SOB-4)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-UTUQ-2)</sup>
