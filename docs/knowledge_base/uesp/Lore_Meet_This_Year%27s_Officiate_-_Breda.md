[![ON-render-Breda.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/ON-render-Breda.jpg/300px-ON-render-Breda.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Breda.jpg)
- To my fellow workers at the Knotty Pine Timber Mill,

- Happy [New Life Festival](Lore_New_Life_Festival.md) to you all! I'm sorry that I cannot be with you to raise a mug to the coming year. I have arranged for a fine barrel of [mead](Lore_Mead.md) to be delivered to the mill upon the [solstice](Lore_Solstice_(astronomy).md). Please take care to shut down the waterwheel and lock the blade in position before opening the barrel. We do not want a repeat of last year's unfortunate accident, though I'm pleased that Viktos has learned to manage quite well with just the one foot.

- As always, I have made my annual pilgrimage to [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) for a happy reunion with my festival friends, an occasion that always brings me great joy. This year is special, as I have been asked to help officiate at the Festival! This is not an easy task, as there are always those who oppose joy and celebration of any kind.

- Already a local politician, Housecarl Mudgeon, has begun posting notices around [Windhelm](Lore_Windhelm.md), criticizing the festival, and claiming that "the engagement of so much effort and expense for so much frivolity and hedonistic behavior in a time of such great peril is an affront to the teachings of [Jhunal](Lore_Jhunal.md) and [Stuhn](Lore_Stuhn.md)." This is precisely the kind of pig-headed nonsense that can fall like a wet blanket upon this wonderful festival of life, light, and love!

- He's not getting much support, thank [Mara](Lore_Mara.md). In fact, many of the merchants owe such a large portion of their annual income to the festival, I hear that some are offering discounts to those who tear down the bills and present them for immediate incineration.

- Enjoy the mead, and know that I will be raising my own tankard in toast to all of our good fortunes for the coming year.

- May your splinters be few and painless!

- [Breda](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Breda)
