[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/80/SR-npc-Mahk.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mahk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mahk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mahk.jpg) Mahk at work in Dushnikh Mine **Mahk** is an [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_GuardOrc1H.md) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He works in [Dushnikh Mine](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Mine.md), mining [orichalcum ore](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ore.md) along with [Ghak](Skyrim_Ghak.md), [Dulug](Skyrim_Dulug.md), and [Oglub](Skyrim_Oglub.md).

Like his co-workers in the mine, Mahk spends his entire life in the dark tunnels and never sleeps. He divides his time between hacking away on the ore veins, relaxing, or eating.

He wears a set of [miner's clothes](Skyrim_Miner%27s_Clothes.md#00080697) with [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#00080699) and [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1). He carries his [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md), the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).
