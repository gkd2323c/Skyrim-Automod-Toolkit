She is imprisoned in a cage on the ledge in the first room you enter. [Pantea Ateia](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md) mentions she is a student at the [Bards College](Skyrim_Bards_College.md) in Solitude, who apparently sold [Pantea's Flute](Skyrim_Pantea%27s_Flute.md) to the [necromancers](Skyrim_Necromancer.md) in the cave she appears in.

She only wears a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md) and [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md).

## Related Quests
- **[Pantea's Flute](Skyrim_Pantea%27s_Flute.md)**: Find a missing [flute](Skyrim_Pantea%27s_Flute_(item).md) for [Pantea](Skyrim_Pantea_Ateia.md).

## Quest-Related Events
When nearing Larina's cage, she will call out to you, to no avail:

- *"Is there someone out there?"*
- *"You have to help me, they are going to kill me!"*

When leave the room and continue further into the cave, she will attempt to yell out one more time:

- *"You can't just leave me here!"*

However, you cannot actually save her. Once you reach a certain point in the dungeon, she will be killed and placed on a table, with necromancers now surrounding her lifeless body.

## Notes
- The Ref ID of the dead version of her is `0008019C`.
- You can climb up the ledge and "free" her at the beginning of the dungeon by walking and jumping creatively near the big round rock to the right. However, she will have no unique dialogue and can't be saved.

## Bugs
- Should you manage to get near her while she's still alive and decide to attack her, then you will get a [bounty](Skyrim_Crime.md#Bounties) in [the Rift](Skyrim_The_Rift.md), as for some unknown reason, Larina is part of the [Crime Faction Rift](Skyrim_CrimeFactionRift.md) and [Town Ivarstead Faction](Skyrim_TownIvarsteadFaction.md).
