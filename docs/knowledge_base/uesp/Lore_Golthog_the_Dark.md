# Golthog

Golthog the Dark was a [necromancer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Necromancy) active in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). Rumored to serve the infamous [Underking](Lore_Underking.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARQB-1)</sup> Golthog commanded a band of [goblins](Lore_Goblin.md) that helped him overtake [Stonekeep](Lore_Stonekeep.md), a fortress governed by a king with knights that Golthog and his goblins defeated.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> Golthog and his goblins later raided the nearby major city of [Rihad](Lore_Rihad.md) where they took a horde of treasures including a parchment that had a decipher from an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) that spoke of the location of the fabled [Fang Lair](Lore_Fang_Lair.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARQB-1)</sup> The [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md) sought the piece of the [Staff of Chaos](Lore_Staff_of_Chaos.md) found within Fang Lair and so Queen Blubamka of Rihad pointed the hero toward Stonekeep to retrieve the parchment that Golthog stole.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARQB-1)</sup> The Eternal Champion successfully retrieved the parchment although it's unknown if he had a direct confrontation with Golthog.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> Queen Blubamka expressed hope that he was neutralized.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARQB-1)</sup>
