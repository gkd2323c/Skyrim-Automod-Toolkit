# Hearthfire NPCs


## [Bards](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md)
Bards may be hired through your [steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md).

- **[Llewellyn the Nightingale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Llewellyn_the_Nightingale)** — A bard who can be hired to stay in [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md).
- **[Oriella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Oriella)** — A bard who can be hired to stay in [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md).
- **[Sonir](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sonir)** — A bard who can be hired to stay in [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md).

## [Carriage Drivers](Skyrim_Transport.md#Carriage)
Carriage drivers may be hired through your steward.

- **[Engar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Engar)** — A carriage driver who can be hired at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md).
- **[Gunjar](Skyrim_Gunjar_(Hearthfire).md)** — A carriage driver who can be hired at [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md).
- **[Markus](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Markus)** — A carriage driver who can be hired at [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md).

## [Children](Skyrim_Child.md)
All of these children are homeless orphans who may be [adopted](Skyrim_Adoption.md).

- **[Alesan](Skyrim_Alesan.md)** — A Redguard boy who works as a food runner for the miners in [Dawnstar](Skyrim_Dawnstar.md).
- **[Blaise](Skyrim_Blaise.md)** — A Breton boy who helps around the stables at [Katla's Farm](Skyrim_Katla%27s_Farm.md) near [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).
- **[Lucia](Skyrim_Lucia.md)** — An Imperial girl who begs for gold in the streets of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md).
- **[Sofie](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sofie)** — A Nord girl wandering around the streets of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md) selling flowers.

## [Housecarls](Skyrim_Housecarl.md)
All new housecarls are appointed after you become [thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) of the appropriate hold. They can become [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) and stewards, and may also be married.

- **[Gregor](Skyrim_Gregor.md)** — A male [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) assigned at [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md).
- **[Rayya](Skyrim_Rayya.md)** — A female [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) assigned at [Lakeview Manor](Skyrim_Lakeview_Manor.md) in [Falkreath Hold](Skyrim_Falkreath_Hold.md).
- **[Valdimar](Skyrim_Valdimar.md)** — A male Nord assigned at [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md).

## Other
- **[Rochelle the Red](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rochelle_the_Red)** — A female Redguard who may kidnap your [spouse](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

## Notes
- Children in [Honorhall Orphanage](Skyrim_Honorhall_Orphanage.md) may now be adopted by speaking with [Constance Michel](Skyrim_Constance_Michel.md) after killing [Grelod the Kind](Skyrim_Grelod_the_Kind.md) during [Innocence Lost](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Innocence_Lost). Other children who are orphaned due to in-game events may also be adopted by talking to them.
