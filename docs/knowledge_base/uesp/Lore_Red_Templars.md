# Red Templars

The Red Templars<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup> (or **Red Dome [Templars](Lore_Templars.md)**)<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-2)</sup> were a branch of crusaders in the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) active during the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-RFM-3)</sup> They had some affiliation with the [Cult of Talos](Lore_Talos_Cult.md),<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-4)</sup> but they were considered psychotic, and were believed to drink the [dragonblood](Lore_Dragonborn.md)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Arngeir-5)</sup> of [Talos](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) to gain temporary martial [shouting](Lore_Thu%27um.md) powers.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-2)</sup> Due to the rest of the Legion hating them and the [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md)'s desires to see them dispersed, the order was pushed to undesirable and faraway regions, including [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MKP-2)</sup> They were still an active organization in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), by which time they were notorious for not taking orders, despite theoretically being a part of the Legion's chain of command.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup>

Following the signing of the [White-Gold Concordat](Lore_White-Gold_Concordat.md), the worship of Talos was banned in the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-6)</sup> It is unknown how this event affected the Red Templars.
