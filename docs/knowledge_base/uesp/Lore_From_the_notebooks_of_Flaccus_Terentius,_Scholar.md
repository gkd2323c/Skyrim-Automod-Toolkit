[![ON-concept-Imperial bridge.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/fe/ON-concept-Imperial_bridge.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Imperial_bridge.png)
- I haven't slept a full night in weeks. Every time I drift off, the vision returns. The screeching of metal and wails of the damned, the darkened sky [opening like a wound](Lore_Dark_Anchors.md), the feeling of my innards squirming as if to escape ... the voice in my head ... and the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) tumbling out, eager to spoil and ruin [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) with their filth.

- I've written reams and reams in my sleepless nights trying to capture the horror, but every line I scrawl is pitiful, inadequate. If we have words that can describe [this nightmare](Lore_Planemeld.md), I do not know them. They have the words, though. I hear them calling, coming to claim us for their lord. How can we hope to stand against such power, such relentless and wanton hate? Is it already too late—has [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) torn [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) beyond repair?
