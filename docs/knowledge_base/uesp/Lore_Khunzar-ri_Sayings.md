Khunzar-ri Sayings *Sayings attributed to the mythic hero Khunzar-ri*
---

## *[Verse One](Lore_Khunzar-ri_Sayings,_Verse_One.md)*
<center>[Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) says

War is like the two Moons

Always there

But not always seen

</center>
## *[Verse Two](Lore_Khunzar-ri_Sayings,_Verse_Two.md)*
<center>[Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) says

In the spring, when

[Moon-sugar](Lore_Moon-sugar.md) [shoots](Lore_Moon_Sugar_Cane.md) are young and tender,

[Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) must learn to step carefully

And leave no trace of their passage

This is the way of the Silent Paw

</center>
## *[Verse Three](Lore_Khunzar-ri_Sayings,_Verse_Three.md)*
<center>[Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) says

If [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) has much gold

But no claws

They have no wealth

Only shiny objects

</center>
## *[Verse Four](Lore_Khunzar-ri_Sayings,_Verse_Four.md)*
<center>[Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) says

Walk silent and unseen

To your enemy's back and

Slide a dagger in their ribs

Before disappearing in the night

That is the [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) way

</center>
