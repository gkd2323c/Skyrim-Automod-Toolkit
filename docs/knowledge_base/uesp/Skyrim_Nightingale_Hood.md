[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d7/SR-item-Nightingale_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Nightingale_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Nightingale_Hood.jpg) Nightingale Hood The Nightingale Hood is part of a set of armor given to you for joining the [Nightingales](Skyrim_Nightingales.md). Its enchantment grants a [Fortify Illusion](Skyrim_Fortify_Illusion.md) effect, which decreases the cost of [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) spells. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) the Nightingale Hood requires [void salts](Skyrim_Void_Salts.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

The full set of Nightingale equipment also includes [armor](Skyrim_Nightingale_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Nightingale_Boots.md), and [gloves](Skyrim_Nightingale_Gloves.md).

## Related Quests
