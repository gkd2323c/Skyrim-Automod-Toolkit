[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-npc-Glover_Mallory.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Glover_Mallory.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Glover_Mallory.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Glover_Mallory.jpg) Glover Mallory [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-quest-Dragonborn_06.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Dragonborn_06.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_06.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Dragonborn_06.jpg) Glover Mallory working on Miraak's shrine **Glover Mallory**, a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief), is a blacksmith in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). He is the brother of the prominent [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) member, [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md), and a member of the guild himself. In fact, he's the only member on Solstheim. He is not exactly active as a Guild member, preferring to earn an honest living as a blacksmith specializing in making and repairing Bonemold armor for the town guards: everything that has ever linked him to the Thieves Guild and his past is kept behind a key-locked door in his basement.

He wears a [blacksmith's apron](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blacksmith%27s_Apron#0005b69f) and some [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b69e). He wields an [ebony dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ebony_Dagger), and carries a [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glover_Mallory%27s_House_Key) to his house and some [upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

He starts his day at 6am with a two-hour breakfast in his house. Then, at 8am, he walks out to his forge and works for twelve hours. At 8pm he heads to [the Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) to relax until midnight, then walks to the [Earth Stone](Skyrim_Earth_Stone.md) to work on it for six hours or, if the stone has been [cleansed](Skyrim_Cleansing_the_Stones.md), he'll head back to his house and go to bed.

## Dialogue
When you approach him:

*"Glover Mallory's the name. If you're looking for a smith, you've found one of the best."* *"Either you're here to get showered in sparks, or you're looking for something to buy. Out with it."* *"[Steel](Skyrim_Steel.md), [dwarven](Skyrim_Dwarven.md), [ebony](Skyrim_Ebony.md)... if it's broken, I can mend it."* *"A lot of folks around here look to strangers for help. I hope you won't disappoint them."* *"You've got a good thing going here now that you've reopened the mines. Don't let it go to waste."* *"I've heard of stealing things from people, but never thought you could steal someone's mind."* Once he's free from [Miraak's](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) control, he'll say:

*"Stealing a man's mind... it just isn't right. Thanks for saving me from that madman."* *"I feel, different somehow. More myself."* *"You're a coward, Miraak! Why don't you come out and face us yourself instead of controlling our minds!"* When spoken to:

**How did you end up in Raven Rock?** *"A fine question, and the one that I hear most often from visitors to our town. I wish I had a more romantic tale to tell, but I was simply seeking my fortune and chose Raven Rock to ply my trade. Besides, knowing how to repair [bonemold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bonemold) armor wasn't very useful in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md)."* **Who taught you that?** *"I had a friend over there, a [dark elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) named [Vanryth](Skyrim_Vanryth_Gatharian.md)... a very talented armorsmith. Spent a lot of time with the guy swapping smithing techniques. Learned a heck of a lot, including how to repair bonemold. After he moved on to greener pastures, I decided to pack up, move out here and put those lessons to the test. Been here ever since."* Once the mines are reopened:

**How are things now that the mines are open again?** *"I've always had a steady stream of work thanks to the Redoran Guard, so it hasn't affected me much. However, I've seen the smile on people's faces and there's hope around here once again... something we've been lacking for a long time."* When exiting conversation:

*"Bring your weapons and armor to me. I'll fix it up right."*

## Conversations
Glover can often be seen talking with other Raven Rock citizens:

**[Adril Arano](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md)**: *"[Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md)'s mentioned how valuable you've become to the Redoran Guard. The [Councilor](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) and I appreciate your assistance."*
**Glover**: *"I'm glad to lend a hand."*
**Adril**: *"You must be using quite a bit of your raw materials repairing their weapons and armor. I'm sorry we can't compensate you."*
**Glover**: *"Not to worry, Adril. I'll recover my losses in due time."* **Veleth**: *"I don't know what your playing at, Glover... but something about you just doesn't add up."*
**Glover**: *"Understandable considering the differences between us."*
**Veleth**: *"I'm not talking about that. I have a knack for smelling trouble, and for some reason you reek of it."*
**Glover**: *"I promise you, I have nothing but good intentions towards everyone in this town."* **Glover**: *"[Fethis](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md)! How's that pretty young [daughter](Skyrim_Dreyla_Alor.md) of yours doing?"*
**Fethis**: *"What in the name of the [Black Hands](Lore_Mephala.md) is that supposed to mean?!"*
**Glover**: *"Nothing... I... I was just making conversation."*
**Fethis**: *"Next time you feel like having a conversation, talk to someone else."*
**[Garyn Ienth](Skyrim_Garyn_Ienth.md)**: *"So where do you hail from, Glover?"*

**Garyn**: *"Have any kinfolk?"*

## Notes
- In the basement, on a desk behind the locked door, is a *[letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glover%27s_Letter)* by Glover. This letter details Glover's past, including the fact that Thieves Guild member [Sapphire](Skyrim_Sapphire_(person).md) is actually his daughter that he abandoned.
- Despite being a member of the Thieves Guild, he will not buy stolen merchandise even after you recognize him as a member. The only way he'll buy stolen items from you is if you [invest in him](Skyrim_Speech.md#Investor) and unlock the [Fence perk](Skyrim_Speech.md#Fence); no other merchants anywhere on Solstheim will accept your stolen goods or your investment.
- It is possible to receive a [fishing job](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Fishing_Job) from [Delvin Mallory](Skyrim_Delvin_Mallory.md) with Glover as the target.
- Though his character class is technically [Combat Thief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Thief), Glover is still fully functional as a [Blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md), being in appropriate merchant factions to provide services.
