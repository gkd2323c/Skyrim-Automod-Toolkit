[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-creature-Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Dwarven_Armored_Mudcrab.jpg) Dwarven Armored Mudcrab The **Dwarven Armored Mudcrab** is a pet that can be purchased from [Calcelmo](Skyrim_Calcelmo.md) in [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md). He originally found it while excavating the ruins of [Nchuand-Zel](Skyrim_Nchuand-Zel.md). Its initial location is Calcelmo's laboratory in Understone Keep.

The crab is essential, and will not engage in combat. Its main function is to carry items, and it has a carry weight of 20 and it will follow you even if you already have another pet following you. You can instruct it to wait at a city, wait at its current location, wait at your house, or to carry items. It can also be summoned using a [teleportation spell](Skyrim_Teleport_Pet__Dwarven_Mudcrab.md). The mudcrab cannot be detected by Detect Dead or Detect Life.

If following you, it will occasionally give you leveled clutter, which could be an [ore](Skyrim_Ore.md), a Dwarven clutter item, a [gem](Skyrim_Gems.md), a [soul gem](Skyrim_Soul_Gems.md), or [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md).

## Notes
- The mudcrab will only ever speak in ellipses.
- The mudcrab can be told to wait at any of your [houses](Skyrim_Houses.md), including [Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) homes.
- While idle, the mudcrab will regularly burrow into the ground, even when indoors.
- The Dwarven Armored Mudcrab model is based on a satirical "[Crab Armor DLC](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-misc-PC_Gamer_Crab_Armor.png)" advertisement from [*PC Gamer*](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_Gamer) in 2011, poking fun at the [Horse Armor Pack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Horse_Armor_Pack) for [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion).

## Bugs
- Do not bring the Dwarven Armored Mudcrab to [Druadach Redoubt](Skyrim_Druadach_Redoubt.md) after siding with [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md) during the quest [No One Escapes Cidhna Mine](Skyrim_No_One_Escapes_Cidhna_Mine.md), as it will be hostile towards the friendly Forsworn there. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Adding the mudcrab to the [Druadach Redoubt Faction](Skyrim_DruadachRedoubtFaction.md) faction (Ref ID `E3609`) will fix this problem.

- In Skyrim Anniversary Edition, the [teleportation spell](Skyrim_Teleport_Pet__Dwarven_Mudcrab.md) will not work due to the removal of the [My Pet Mudcrab](Skyrim_My_Pet_Mudcrab.md) quest, which needs to be completed to mark the mudcrab as owned by the player. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.629.0, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Forcing the quest as complete using the `Completequest cc BGSSSE010_Pet Purchase Quest` command will fix this problem.
- The Dwarven Armored Mudcrab is not revealed by [Detect Life](Skyrim_Detect_Life_(effect).md).
