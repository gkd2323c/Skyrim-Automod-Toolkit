# Velothi

**Velothi** may refer to the following subjects:

- The [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md), who were also known as the Velothi
- [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md) that renounced their nomadic culture to settle with the House Dunmer, known now as the Velothi
- The teachings, followers, or anything related to Saint [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md) (e.g. the Velothi Prophecies or the Velothi Exodus)
