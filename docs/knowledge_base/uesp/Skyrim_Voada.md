[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-npc-Voada.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Voada.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voada.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voada.jpg) Voada [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-npc-Voada_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Voada_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voada_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voada_02.jpg) *"Voada, you spilled laundry water in the soup pot again!"* **Voada** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who works in the [Understone Keep](Skyrim_Understone_Keep.md) kitchen along with her brother [Rondach](Skyrim_Rondach.md) and bossy master chef [Anton Virane](Skyrim_Anton_Virane.md). She also handles the [dogs](Skyrim_Dog.md) that live near the kitchen area. During the [Delivery](Skyrim_Delivery.md) quest, the similar-minded [Banning](Skyrim_Banning.md) will ask you to bring her some [spiced beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spiced_Beef) for the dogs.

She sleeps next to her brother on one of the bedrolls in the kitchen, between midnight and 6am. After waking up she enjoys a one-hour breakfast then starts her workday, which mostly consists of wandering, sweeping, or having debates with Anton Virane. At noon and 6pm she pauses for one-hour meals. Her workday ends at 7pm and she spends the rest of the evening relaxing in the kitchen area.

She wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261c0) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#000261bd). She is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a selection of [lower-class loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Poor).

## Related Quests

## Dialogue
She will occasionally reassure you about the dogs in the keep. She may also refer you to Anton, or talk about her job.

*"Don't mind the dogs, they get anxious when they haven't had dinner."* *"You should talk to Anton if you need anything. He's the master chef."* *"It's been a hard day cleaning. Good, honest work I say."*
### Quest-Related Events
When you bring her the spiced beef from [Banning](Skyrim_Banning.md), she will hand you some [gold](Skyrim_Delivery.md#Reward) and say:

**I have a delivery from Banning.** *"Oh, thank you. The dogs were getting restless. Here, this is for you."*
