### A Bandit's Book
[![SR-place-A Bandit's Book.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-place-A_Bandit%27s_Book.jpg/200px-SR-place-A_Bandit%27s_Book.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-A_Bandit%27s_Book.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-A_Bandit%27s_Book.jpg) - POIReach33

On the rocky and elevated cliff paths between [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md) and the confluence of the Karth River lies a dead bandit and a copy of *[Horror of Castle Xyr](Skyrim_Horror_of_Castle_Xyr.md)*. There is also one silver ore vein.

### Brush Strongbox: Riverside
[![SR-place-Brush Strongbox Riverside.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-place-Brush_Strongbox_Riverside.jpg/200px-SR-place-Brush_Strongbox_Riverside.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brush_Strongbox_Riverside.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Brush_Strongbox_Riverside.jpg) - POIReach32

Located northeast of [Four Skull Lookout](Skyrim_Four_Skull_Lookout.md), there is a strongbox alongside an iron dagger and nirnroot found hidden in the dead bushes.

### Cradle Stone Crag
[![SR-place-Cradle Stone Crag.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c0/SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Crag.jpg/200px-SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Crag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Crag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Cradle_Stone_Crag.jpg) - POIReach15b

Located in the hills east of Valthume. When approached, two trolls can be found. [Frofnir Trollsbane](Skyrim_Frofnir_Trollsbane.md) lies dead here with him carrying the unique weapon [Trollsbane](Skyrim_Trollsbane.md). There is a dead elk and a portion of venison as well as numerous troll skulls, and other human and animal bones scattered around him. Further to the west are several birds' nests with either pine thrush eggs or rock warbler eggs.

### Dwarven Arch: Harmugstahl Falls
[![SR-place-Dwarven Arch Harmugstahl Falls.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-place-Dwarven_Arch_Harmugstahl_Falls.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dwarven_Arch_Harmugstahl_Falls.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Arch_Harmugstahl_Falls.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Arch_Harmugstahl_Falls.jpg) - POIReach05

On the south bank of the [Karth River](Skyrim_Karth_River.md), opposite the entrance to [Harmugstahl](Skyrim_Harmugstahl.md) is a Dwarven stone arch with a planter underneath and steps leading up to it. A copy of the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[Withershins](Skyrim_Withershins.md)* can be found on the planter.

[Nirnroot](Skyrim_Nirnroot.md) and three [spadefish](Skyrim_Spadefish.md) can be found in the waters behind the arch. There is also an iron ore vein a few paces further south. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-136206&y=60052&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dwarven Rubble: Druadach
[![SR-place-Dwarven Rubble Druadach.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5d/SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Druadach.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Druadach.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Druadach.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Druadach.jpg) - POIReach02

Located northeast of Druadach Redoubt, on the bank of a river. There are several arrows and an empty Dwemer Convector.

[Sorine Jurard](Skyrim_Sorine_Jurard.md)'s Satchel containing seven Dwemer Gyros for [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md) can be found here.

### Dwarven Rubble: Karth River Confluence
File:SR-place-Dwarven Rubble Karth River Confluence.jpg - POIReach23

On the Karth River's eastern bank, among the juniper bushes and just west of the Reach Stormcloak Camp is a scattered pile of dwarven stones. Among them is a small dwarven chest to pilfer from. There is also an iron ore vein.

### Dwarven Rubble: Salvius Farm Trail
[![SR-place-Dwarven Rubble Salvius Farm Trail.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6e/SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Salvius_Farm_Trail.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Salvius_Farm_Trail.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Salvius_Farm_Trail.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dwarven_Rubble_Salvius_Farm_Trail.jpg) - POIReach24

About halfway along the path from [Salvius Farm](Skyrim_Salvius_Farm.md) and [Ragnvald](Skyrim_Ragnvald.md) (almost due west of [The Lover Stone](Skyrim_The_Lover_Stone.md)) you will come across some dilapidated masonry with a [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) and/or [hagraven](Skyrim_Hagraven.md) in the same area. Next to a large round piece of masonry, partially hidden by vegetation is an unlocked Dwemer chest. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-162205&y=17198&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Dwarven Ruins: Lair of the Wispmother
[![SR-place-Lair of the Wispmother.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/07/SR-place-Lair_of_the_Wispmother.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lair_of_the_Wispmother.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lair_of_the_Wispmother.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lair_of_the_Wispmother.jpg) - POIReach19

A short distance west of the [Shrine to Peryite](Skyrim_Shrine_to_Peryite.md), on the far side of the mount that the shrine is built on, is a Dwemer tower-like structure, inhabited by a [wispmother](Skyrim_Wisp.md#Wispmther) and three wisps. There are several pieces of broken masonry and steam pipes, along with an unlocked Dwarven chest located a few paces north of the main structure. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-149762&y=34720&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Forsworn Camp: Bthardamz Outskirts
[![SR-place-Forsworn Camp Bthardamz.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/54/SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Bthardamz.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Bthardamz.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Bthardamz.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Bthardamz.jpg) - POIReach22, POIReach22b

A pair of Forsworn and their dog set up a small camp southwest of [Bthardamz](Skyrim_Bthardamz.md), but likely fell victim to the [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md) that now occupies the camp. The camp consists of a campfire and two tents, one of which holds an unlocked chest containing minor loot, while the other holds a copy of the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill book *[The Art of War Magic](Skyrim_The_Art_of_War_Magic.md)*. A few animal pelts and a tanning rack attest to the late occupants' activities. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-171873&y=38218.2&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Forsworn Camp: Gloomreach Pathway
[![SR-place-Forsworn Camp Gloomreach Pathway.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Gloomreach_Pathway.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Gloomreach_Pathway.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Gloomreach_Pathway.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Gloomreach_Pathway.jpg) - POIReach34

Located along the winding path from the main road by the river toward [Gloomreach](Skyrim_Gloomreach.md). A pair of [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md), one of which is a magic-user have a pair of hay piles around a campfire north of [Valthume](Skyrim_Valthume.md), south of [Old Hroldan](Skyrim_Old_Hroldan.md) near a cliff edge above the road and river. There is an [apothecary's satchel](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) on one of the hay piles and a random [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md) among some firewood beside the campfire. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-115688&y=-18895&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Forsworn Camp: Reachwater River
[![SR-place-Forsworn Camp Reachwater River.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0a/SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River.jpg) [![SR-place-Forsworn Camp Reachwater River 02.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Forsworn_Camp_Reachwater_River_02.jpg) - POIReach12

Southwest of [Reachwater Rock](Skyrim_Reachwater_Rock.md) is an unmarked [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) camp with three inhabitants. When approaching the camp, the Forsworn may be seen fighting with Imperial soldiers. There are four tents in the camp; one tent contains a chest and a satchel, while another contains a copy of the Block skill book *[Death Blow of Abernanit](Skyrim_Death_Blow_of_Abernanit.md)*. A few common [alchemical ingredients](Skyrim_Ingredients.md) can be found around the campfire. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-144666&y=-4759&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Hagraven Camp: Ragnvald Scree
[![SR-place-Ragnvald Scree.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/70/SR-place-Ragnvald_Scree.jpg/200px-SR-place-Ragnvald_Scree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ragnvald_Scree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Ragnvald_Scree.jpg) - POIReach18

Once you reach Ragnvald, take the winding path south, unless you pass the snow line, and enter a small Hagraven Camp. Bloodied goat appendages are strewn about, but the place is empty until you enter the tent and the Hagraven returns. There is a wooden chest in the tent. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-177712&y=18960&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Juniper Tree Ruins
[![SR-place-Juniper Tree Ruins.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1a/SR-place-Juniper_Tree_Ruins.jpg/200px-SR-place-Juniper_Tree_Ruins.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Juniper_Tree_Ruins.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Juniper_Tree_Ruins.jpg) - POIReach17

Located northeast of Valthume, further up the dirt trail past the Reachwind Burial Mound, you can find this small ruin with a stone planter containing a [juniper tree](Skyrim_Juniper_Berries.md) perched near the edge of the bluff. Pick through the rubble to find an unlocked Dwemer chest and a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) skill book *[Enchanter's Primer](Skyrim_Enchanter%27s_Primer.md)*. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-110868&y=-20932&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Lost Treasure: Purewater Run

### Lovers' Camp
[![SR-place-Lovers' Camp.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8f/SR-place-Lovers%27_Camp.jpg/200px-SR-place-Lovers%27_Camp.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lovers%27_Camp.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Lovers%27_Camp.jpg) - POIReach14

The camp is located along a small path that travels north from Old Hroldan Inn towards Soljund's Sinkhole. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-117833&y=434)

It is the site of a tragedy, the death of [Karan](Skyrim_Karan%27s_Journal.md) and [Talvur](Skyrim_Talvur.md) as they were eloping. The animal that killed them is still present. Search the tree stump behind the tent for a knapsack and some items.

Sometime after discovering the site, it will be replaced with a shrine of Mara.

### Reachman's Altar: Red Eagle Redoubt
[![SR-place-Reachman's Altar Red Eagle Redoubt.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-place-Reachman%27s_Altar_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.jpg/200px-SR-place-Reachman%27s_Altar_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reachman%27s_Altar_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Reachman%27s_Altar_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.jpg) - POIReach21

Just above the entrance to [Red Eagle Redoubt](Skyrim_Red_Eagle_Redoubt.md), south-southeast of the marker is a stone table with a large stone claw-shaped statue beside the table like those found in barrows throughout Skyrim. There are a couple of [hanging mosses](Skyrim_Hanging_Moss.md) growing from it and behind it is an unlocked wooden chest. The easiest way of reaching this is to climb the wooden stairs to the raised lookout point outside the entrance to the redoubt, where an archer [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) keeps watch and then follow the slope up to your right. There is also a [silver ore vein](Skyrim_Silver_Ore_Vein.md) slightly to the north of the table.

### The Incautious Bather
[![SR-place-The Incautious Bather.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/76/SR-place-The_Incautious_Bather.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Incautious_Bather.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Incautious_Bather.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Incautious_Bather.jpg) - POIReach03

While traversing the path west of Dragontooth Crater, head southwest at the path junction to reach a small pond with the half-naked corpse of a [woman](Skyrim_Captive.md) with forsworn arrows sticking out of her back.

She does not have anything in her inventory. However, her clothing is found near her body; [farm boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), [farm clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), and a [farm hat](Skyrim_Hat.md). With the clothing is a [journal](Skyrim_Journal_(pond).md) that documents her poor judgement, as well as a [gold ring](Skyrim_Gold_Ring.md) and [silver emerald necklace](Skyrim_Silver_Emerald_Necklace.md). There is also a [satchel](Skyrim_Satchel.md) nearby. This has a chance of containing some or all of the following: [a lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md), [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md), and a leveled [potion](Skyrim_Potions.md) of Restore Health, Restore Magicka, or Restore Stamina. Evidently, she placed these near the pond before she went swimming. [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=-152724&y=47114&zoom=17&legacy=true)

### Totem to the Dragon
[![SR-place-Totem to the Dragon.jpg](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/91/SR-place-Totem_to_the_Dragon.jpg/200px-SR-place-Totem_to_the_Dragon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Totem_to_the_Dragon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Totem_to_the_Dragon.jpg) West of Karthwasten and below the ridge where the Shrine to Peryite is located, is an alcove with a totem to Peryite. Slumped across it is a dead female Wood Elf and some bloodied bones. A random, enchanted dagger lies at the foot of the pillar, as well as a random poison and a [diamond](Skyrim_Diamond.md). A wooden chest is at the woman's feet, and contains random loot along with the [Illusion](Skyrim_Illusion.md) skill book *[Incident at Necrom](Skyrim_Incident_at_Necrom.md)*. A lone wolf often patrols the hillside just below the pillar.
