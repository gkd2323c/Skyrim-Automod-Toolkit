## Male Altmer Names

### [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena) and [Daggerfall](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Daggerfall:Daggerfall)
- Given names for male Altmer in Arena and Daggerfall seem to consist of one of twelve prefixes and one of twelve suffixes.
- The 12 prefixes for male Altmer names are: Core, Corri, Cyre, Gan, Kala, Kelkemme, Lilland, Lovi, Mith, Saru, Sau, Soli

## Altmer Family Names and Titles
Altmer have incredibly lengthy full names due to their long-standing heritage and value for family.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LFS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ADD-2)</sup> As such, most Altmer tend to not display their last names, but there are a few names that are known.

### Arena and Daggerfall
- Surnames for Altmer in Arena and Daggerfall seem to consist of one of twelve prefixes and one of twelve suffixes.
- The 12 prefixes for Altmer surnames are: Ad, Caem, Elsin, Gae, Gray, High, Jor, Lareth, Silin, Spell, Storm, Throm
- The 12 suffixes for Altmer surnames are: aire, al, binder, ian, ire, ius, lock, or, orin, thar, us, watch
