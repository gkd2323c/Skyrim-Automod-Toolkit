[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/SR-npc-Imperial_General.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Imperial_General.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_General.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Imperial_General.jpg) Imperial General at the Battle for Whiterun **Imperial Generals** are generic [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) officers which appear during various [Civil War](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) quests. They wear a set of [Imperial armor](Skyrim_Imperial_Armor.md) without a helmet and defends themselves with an [Imperial sword](Skyrim_Imperial_Sword.md) and [Imperial light shield](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Shield.md), supplemented by an [Imperial bow](Skyrim_Imperial_Bow.md) with twenty [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow). They also carry a backup [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and have a chance of carrying a random [Amulet of the Divines](Skyrim_Amulets_of_the_Divines.md) (other than for [Talos](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Talos.md)), a random [food](Skyrim_Food.md) or [drink](Skyrim_Beverages.md), and/or a small amount of gold. Curiously, despite being a higher rank than the common Imperial Soldier, they seem to be easier to kill than the common soldier.
