[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/SR-npc-Hand_Kenro_Hlan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hand_Kenro_Hlan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hand_Kenro_Hlan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hand_Kenro_Hlan.jpg) Hand Kenro Hlan **Hand Kenro Hlan** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and a [Hand of Almalexia](Lore_Hands_of_Almalexia.md) in service to the heretical [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md). He can be found in [Fahlbtharz Forge](Skyrim_Fahlbtharz.md#Fahlbtharz_Forge) and will attack you on sight. You are required to kill him as part of [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest).md).

When you enter the forge, he can be found finishing off a hostile [Dwemer ghost](Skyrim_Ghost_(Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal).md). He carries a [note](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro%27s_Note.md), the Unenchanted Weapon, gold and common loot. He wears [Kenro's Helmet](Skyrim_Kenro%27s_Helmet.md) and [Kenro's Robes](Skyrim_Kenro%27s_Robes.md) and wields an [Ebony Scimitar](Skyrim_Ebony_Scimitar.md).

## Notes
- Kenro is the only member of the Tribunal Temple who must be killed to proceed with the quest, and is the only one who will be hostile on sight with no way of avoiding a confrontation.
