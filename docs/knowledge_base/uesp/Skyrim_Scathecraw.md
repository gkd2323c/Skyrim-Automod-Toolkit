Guaranteed samples can be found in the following locations:

- 1 in [Ienth Farm](Skyrim_Ienth_Farm.md) ([Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md)), on a table downstairs
- 1 in [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md), on a shelf next to an alchemy table
- 1 in [Shaman's Hut](Skyrim_Shaman%27s_Hut.md) ([Skaal Village](Skyrim_Skaal_Village.md)), in a bucket
