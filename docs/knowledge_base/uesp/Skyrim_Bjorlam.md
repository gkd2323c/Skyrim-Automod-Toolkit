[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-npc-Bjorlam.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Bjorlam.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjorlam.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Bjorlam.jpg) Bjorlam in his carriage outside Whiterun **Bjorlam** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), and the [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) [carriage](Skyrim_Carriage.md) driver. He can be found near the [Whiterun Stables](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Whiterun_Stables), and will take you to any of the [hold capitals](Skyrim_Cities.md) on his carriage, for a fee.

Bjorlam wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000261c0), a pair of [gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md#000261c1), and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000261bd). He is equipped with an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md).

## Dialogue
You can ask him what he knows about Whiterun, to which he'll reply:

- **What do you know about Whiterun?**
- *"Well, I know the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md) make their home here. Their mead hall, [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md), is the oldest building in the city. Oh, and there's a nasty feud between two families called the [Gray-Manes](Skyrim_Gray-Mane.md) and [Battle-Borns](Skyrim_Battle-Born.md). You'll want to be careful there. The rest you can find out by asking the townsfolk. I'd start with the barkeep at [the Bannered Mare](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Bannered_Mare), or the castle steward."*

Apart from this, he shares his dialogue with other carriage drivers.
