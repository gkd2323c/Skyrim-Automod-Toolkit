[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/OBR-npc-Pelinal_Whitestrake.jpg/200px-OBR-npc-Pelinal_Whitestrake.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Pelinal_Whitestrake.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Pelinal_Whitestrake.jpg) Pelinal Whitestrake Vilius Tharn was a contemporary of [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md), [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md) and [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md) and one of the earliest known [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) ancestors of the modern noble [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) family, [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md). According to the family tradition, the Tharn family was active in St. Alessia's slave uprising<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> of years [1E 242](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_242)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SAD-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGTE-3)</sup> - [1E 243](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_243).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TSOP3-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TLKOTA-5)</sup> He served under Pelinal Whitestrake himself as **Blade-Serrator and Master of the Abbatoir**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup> One of his descendants [Abnur Tharn](Lore_Abnur_Tharn.md) claimed that while certain tales of the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) were a bit hyperbolic, it was quite clear that exceptional individuals made the establishment of the First Empire possible.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TSRMT-6)</sup>
