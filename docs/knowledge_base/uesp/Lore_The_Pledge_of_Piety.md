The Pledge of Piety *A stone's challenge to seek the favor of the stars* *
---
Know O pilgrim, the Stars' command:

Honor the [Stars](Lore_Celestial.md), for it is good and right to venerate them and hold them in awe. Each blade that crashes down upon the People's foes must first rise skyward. Let each strike land with a prayer. Be steadfast in prayer and generous in sacrifice. Only the pious and most-loved by the Stars shall find true glory. For a pious warrior is a dutiful warrior, and piety sustains the warrior through even the darkest hours.

Pledge to be pious, as the Stars command.
