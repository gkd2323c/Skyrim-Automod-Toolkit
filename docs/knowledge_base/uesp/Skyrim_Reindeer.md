[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1f/SR-creature-Reindeer.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Reindeer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Reindeer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Reindeer.jpg) Reindeer The **Reindeer** is a unique mount found at a camp north of the [Windward Ruins](Skyrim_Windward_Ruins.md). It can be bought by purchasing a [bill of sale](Skyrim_Reindeer_Bill_of_Sale.md) from its current owner, [Agrane Peryval](Skyrim_Agrane_Peryval.md). It functions identically to a [horse](Skyrim_Horse.md) mount but in appearance it resembles a [deer](Skyrim_Deer.md) wearing festive tack.

It will return to Agrane's camp if you switch to a different active mount.

It cannot be armored with Horse Armor, either steel or elven. However, it is "essential" and thus cannot be killed.
