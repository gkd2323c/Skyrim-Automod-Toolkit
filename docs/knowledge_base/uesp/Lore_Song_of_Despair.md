Song of Despair **by Anonymous** *Song on the Daedric Princes*
---
The [memory of answers torn from fate](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md)

The [destruction of all who cannot wait](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md).

At dusk and dawn, the [Rose Queen](Lore_Azura.md) rules

While thieves of night still own [the dark](Lore_Nocturnal.md).

[Green Dragon](Lore_Peryite.md)'s breath pollutes cloud and pool

As [silent spider](Lore_Mephala.md) spins webs that mark.

The broken oath and the [traitor](Lore_Boethiah.md)'s plot,

The [huntsman](Lore_Hircine.md)'s horn and sharpened spear

Make [wishes pacts](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md) that come to naught

When [madness](Lore_Sheogorath.md) claims those who would appear.

[Life](Lore_Meridia.md) stands death upon its head

As [sweet lust](Lore_Sanguine.md) joins in pools of blood.

Escape the [curse](Lore_Malacath.md) with no tears to shed

As [nightmares](Lore_Vaermina.md) arrive all in a flood.

[Disease and plague](Lore_Namira.md) are no end of life

When mastered by the [harvester of strife.](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)
