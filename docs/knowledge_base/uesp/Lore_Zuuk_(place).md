[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/ON-place-Zuuk.jpg/200px-ON-place-Zuuk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zuuk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Zuuk.jpg) Zuuk ca. 2E 582 Zuuk was a [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) settlement located in the [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) region of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). It was founded circa [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) by [Lord Zuuk](Lore_Zuuk_(person).md) when he returned to Shadowfen after the death of [Emperor Reman Cyrodiil III](Lore_Reman_III.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MS2920-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TFOZ-2)</sup> After five generations of prosperity, the settlement was renamed in his honor, and its original name was lost to time. It was built over the **Temple of Sul**, a [Barsaebic Ayleid](Lore_Barsaebic_Ayleids.md) settlement which had been sacked during the [Alessian Slave Rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md). The ruins were magically sealed by the villagers to prevent undead emerging to the surface. Like all Kothringi, the inhabitants of Zuuk fell victim to the devastating [Knahaten Flu](Lore_Knahaten_Flu.md), which wiped out the village in a single week in [2E 561](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_561). The sole survivor was a man named [Drillk](Lore_Drillk.md), who had become immune to the Flu by absorbing the power of a [keystone](Lore_Keystones.md) that he had found in the Temple of Sul.

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) invaded Shadowfen in search of the keystones. They set up camp in Zuuk, torturing Drillk and interrogating the spirits of long-dead Kothringi in an attempt to find the keystone. The [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) rescued Drillk, but the Kothringi revealed that taking the keystone's power would kill him. It is unknown if the Pact took his life or not.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
