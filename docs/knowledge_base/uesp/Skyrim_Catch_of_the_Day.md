## Quick Walkthrough
1. Talk to Swims-In-Deep-Water inside the Riften Fishery.
2. Read the book in the back room of the Fishery for the optional objective.
3. Go outside the Fishery and at the end of the dock is a Rod and Fishing Supplies where you can catch the list of fish.
4. Return to Swims-In-Deep-Water.

## Detailed Walkthrough
To begin this quest you need to speak to [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md):

- **I'd like to improve my fishing skills. Could you teach me a few things?**
- *"Of course."*
- **An entire list of fish to catch? But I only just learned how to fish!**
- *"That doesn't change anything."*

You then have three options:

- **Should I cook them for you while I'm at it?**
- *"What? Nooo!"*
- **Relax, I was just joking.**
- *"You expect me to believe that?"*
- **The fish you tend to at the Fishery must be very important to you.**
- *"It speaks the truth."*

- **I'll bring them back soon.**
- *"My thanks to you."*

- **I'll do my best. No promises.**
- *"Of course."*

He will hand you a [list of fair weather fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Fair_Weather_Fish), which requests you to catch:

- [Carp](Skyrim_Carp.md)
- [Glassfish](Skyrim_Glassfish.md)
- [Goldfish](Skyrim_Goldfish.md)
- [Pogfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pogfish)

You can find a copy of *[Fishing Mastery, v1](Skyrim_Fishing_Mastery,_v1.md)* on a shelf in the room behind Swims. This book will inform you of where you can catch each of these fish. All of the fish can be found in temperate lakes and rivers, see [this link](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Fishing_Locations) for a list of fishing locations, but these fish can all be caught in the docks of Riften and along the river that leads to Ivarstead.

Once you have caught all the fish required, return to Swims:

- **Here are the fish you asked for.**
- *"You've done well."*

He will then reward you with 200 gold.
