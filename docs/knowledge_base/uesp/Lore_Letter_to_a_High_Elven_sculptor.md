Letter to a High Elven sculptor **by [Cintelmo](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Cintelmo)** *Instructions for crafting the perfect statue of Prince Naemon*
---
Falarcil,

In response to the ridiculous question you asked in your previous correspondence: yes, you are to continue work on the statue even in light of Princess [Ayrenn](Lore_Ayrenn.md)'s return. The Prince is royalty, and he deserves a new statue to be added to the gardens regardless of whether he is to become king.

I have sent to you Prince [Naemon](Lore_Naemon.md)'s favorite portrait to use as reference. The Prince had some additional notes for you, as well: please make sure that you represent the noble angles of his nose appropriately. The last mason did a pathetic job in this regard. Make sure to represent his noble bearing and the correct shoulder width, and please do not make his legs look like sticks!

We look forward to reviewing your work and expect nothing short of perfection.

Cintelmo,

Aide to Prince Naemon
