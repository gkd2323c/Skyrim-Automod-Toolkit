## Walkthrough

### Exterior
A banner flies from a post to the right of the entrance, with a barrel with a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md) and an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) on top and a bandit to the left. An [iron helmet](Skyrim_Iron_Helmet.md), an [Imperial light helmet](Skyrim_Imperial_Light_Helmet.md), a [hide helmet](Skyrim_Hide_Helmet.md), and a [steel helmet](Skyrim_Steel_Helmet.md) are on short posts around the main post. Behind the entrance is a bedroll next to a barrel with a lit lantern and a piece of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) on top and a set of [hide boots](Skyrim_Hide_Boots.md) directly beneath.

### Swindler's Den
Upon entering there is a narrow passage descending into a large chamber where two bandits are discussing current events. There is a table with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), two bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), and a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md) on top. Beside this is a flat-topped rock with a crate, a [food](Skyrim_Food.md) sack, and three loose [potatoes](Skyrim_Potato.md). Behind this is a patch of [white cap](Skyrim_White_Cap.md) fungi, with a second sample in a bucket beside it. There is also an unowned bedroll with another bottle of Nord mead laid out on the ground, and above it all is a ledge which can be used as a shortcut back to the exit later. To the west are a practice dummy and a wooden block by a rock pillar, with a [leg of goat](Skyrim_Leg_of_Goat.md) on top, a [red apple](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md) and an open crate containing a sprig of [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md) and a bulb of [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) beside it. At the rear of the chamber, on a ledge illuminated by a hole in the roof, is an unlocked chest.

A passage out of this room is also in the rear of the chamber on the north wall. Partway along the passage is an illuminated alcove on the left, containing a random [bow](Skyrim_Bow.md) and quiver of [arrows](Skyrim_Arrows.md). The passage then emerges onto a ledge overlooking a second chamber with a campfire and two bandits attending. There is a whole skeever spitted over the fire, which you can harvest for a [charred skeever hide](Skyrim_Charred_Skeever_Hide.md), and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking_Pot.md) hanging below. There are two unowned bedrolls around the fire and a [woodcutter's axe](Skyrim_Woodcutter%27s_Axe.md) leaning against a pile of [firewood](Skyrim_Firewood.md), with two loose pieces nearby. To the left is a bucket containing a [green apple](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md), a potato, and [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md). In the western corner of the chamber is a crate with a [satchel](Skyrim_Satchel.md) and a random minor potion on top, while against the south wall is another crate with an iron helmet and a pair of [iron gauntlets](Skyrim_Iron_Gauntlets.md) on top.

There is a passage leading from this chamber on the east wall. The passage takes a couple of turns as it rises before entering a large chamber containing three bandits on the main level mostly sitting at a row of long wooden tables, with a fourth bandit, a magic-user, standing on a ledge at the rear overlooking the chamber. On the tables are four bottles of Nord mead, a [baked potato](Skyrim_Baked_Potatoes.md), a portion of [grilled leeks](Skyrim_Grilled_Leeks.md), and a [venison chop](Skyrim_Venison_Chop.md). Between two of the tables are a crate and two food sacks. Under the ledge to the east is a small square table with a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), a [silver necklace](Skyrim_Silver_Necklace.md), an [amethyst](Skyrim_Amethyst.md), and seventeen loose pieces of [gold](Skyrim_Gold.md) on top. The table may be disrupted during the melee, so the loot may end up scattered on the ground. Upon entering the chamber and engaging the bandits, at least two additional bandits may converge from the adjoining chamber via the passage on the north wall. The magic user will engage you with ranged spells from the ledge. On the ground to the left of the next passage is a [deer hide](Skyrim_Deer_Hide.md) by more firewood.

The passage passes an alcove on the right containing a straw bale with a skeleton partially buried in it and a bottle of [ale](Skyrim_Ale.md) on top, before turning to the east and climbing to the next chamber, where two more bandits can be found if they haven't already been dealt with. There is a small slightly raised section opposite where you enter containing several barrels, an unlocked chest, and a table with a deer hide on top. By the raised section are an extinguished campfire with four whole [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md) hanging over it and a fifth on a wooden block beside the fire, and two bedrolls nearby. At the rear of the chamber is a second campfire with two more bedrolls beside it. On one of these is a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Thief](Skyrim_Thief_(book).md)*, and to the southeast are several barrels and crates beside another long table with four [salmon steaks](Skyrim_Salmon_Steak.md), a [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), a [gourd](Skyrim_Gourd.md), a cast iron pot containing two [raw rabbit legs](Skyrim_Raw_Rabbit_Leg.md) and a [leek](Skyrim_Leek.md), and a potato. By the table are a food sack and a kettle containing two cabbages and a potato, while hanging above from two racks are two pheasants and three [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md). Just to the left of the exit on the south wall, on top of a rock formation are two [refined malachite](Skyrim_Refined_Malachite.md) ingots and a [gold ingot](Skyrim_Gold_Ingot.md) in an open crate and three food sacks.

The passage continues south, before turning west and leading out onto the ledge where the magic user was standing in the previous chamber. Along the front edge of the ledge on a crate is another deer hide, and the [One-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) skill book *[Night Falls on Sentinel](Skyrim_Night_Falls_on_Sentinel.md)* will spawn here if you have [Rustleif](Skyrim_Rustleif.md)'s quest [Rare Gifts](Skyrim_Rare_Gifts.md) active. Farther along to the west is a wooden block with an iron dagger on top and behind this is patch of [imp stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) fungus. Take care as there are two [bear traps](Skyrim_Bear_Trap.md) by the mouth of the passage leading onwards. Proceed through the passage as it takes a couple of turns before entering a partially flooded section. On the south and northwest walls are [Nordic barnacle clusters](Skyrim_Nordic_Barnacle_Cluster.md), with two more clusters at the bottom of the wooden ramp. The water is initially chest-high, but becomes shallow as you approach the back of a waterfall. Passing through the waterfall leads to a wooden ramp.

At the top of the ramp during the quest [In My Time Of Need](Skyrim_In_My_Time_Of_Need.md) you will find [Kematu](Skyrim_Kematu.md) and up to six [Alik'r Warriors](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Warrior.md), each clothed in [Hammerfell garb](Skyrim_Hammerfell_Garb.md), [Redguard boots](Skyrim_Redguard_Boots.md) and [Alik'r hoods](Skyrim_Alik%27r_Hood.md) and armed with a [scimitar](Skyrim_Scimitar.md), except for Kematu who has no Alik'r hood and is armed with two scimitars. On the right, at the top of the ramp, between several barrels is an unlocked boss chest. On the barrels are four bottles of Nord mead and a leveled potion of stamina. To the left is a patch of [Namira's rot](Skyrim_Namira%27s_Rot.md) fungus, with a second patch growing against the south wall. To the right of the chest, against the north wall are several crates with a whole salmon and a cast iron pot containing two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md) on top. In front of this is an unowned bedroll. A passage heads north, passing an alcove lit by a brazier with an earthen ramp leading up. Find an [iron ore vein](Skyrim_Iron_Ore_Vein.md) in the wall at the base of the ramp. Ascending the earthen ramp will lead to the ledge in the first chamber, thus completing the circuit of Swindler's Den. On your left is a crate with a [long bow](Skyrim_Long_Bow.md) leaning against it and a quiver and two loose [iron arrows](Skyrim_Iron_Arrow.md) on top. Jump down from the ledge to make your exit.

## Notes
- It is possible to climb up to the eastern ledge in the first chamber, which will allow you to backtrack through the cave and reach the boss and his chest without fighting most of the cave's inhabitants.
- It is possible to incite the bandits to fight the Alik'r, making the quest much easier. As an example, standing in the entrance to the large room and shooting an arrow up toward the entrance to the Alik'r area (with the bear traps in front of it) can draw the four bandits into a fight with the warriors. The ensuing fight can kill or wound most of the enemies, including Kematu.
- South of Swindler's Den is an unmarked [shrine of Stendarr](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Stendarr.md). On the altar is a [goblet](Skyrim_Goblet.md), a [flawless amethyst](Skyrim_Flawless_Amethyst.md), a [salt pile](Skyrim_Salt_Pile.md), a copy of the [Enchanting](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Twin Secrets](Skyrim_Twin_Secrets.md)*, and a strongbox which may contain up to two random leveled [gems](Skyrim_Gems.md), two random leveled pieces of [jewelry](Skyrim_Jewelry.md), and/or some gold.
- Southwest of Swindler's Den is an unmarked ruin containing a satchel, two [silver ingots](Skyrim_Silver_Ingot.md), a [shrine to Zenithar](Skyrim_Shrine_of_Zenithar.md), an [amulet of Zenithar](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Zenithar.md), and a copy of the [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[2920, Second Seed, v5](Skyrim_2920,_Second_Seed,_v5.md)*.
