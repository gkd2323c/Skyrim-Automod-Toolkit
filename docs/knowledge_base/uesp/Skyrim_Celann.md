[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-npc-Celann.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Celann.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Celann.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Celann.jpg) Celann **Celann** is a [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) and [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunter who can be found at [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md). Like Dawnguard leader [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md), Celann is a former [Vigilant of Stendarr](Skyrim_Vigilants_of_Stendarr.md), but they both left due to dissatisfaction with the organization. Later on, they parted ways, but naturally Celann was one of the first warriors to be offered a place in Isran's newly formed [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). Once the quest [Prophet](Skyrim_Prophet_(Dawnguard).md) has been completed, Celann will also be available as a [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) but will keep up his daily routine.

Once Awakening has been started Celann will start his regular routine. He owns one of the cots in the northern section on the ground floor and sleeps there between 8pm and 4am. After he wakes up he walks over to the western dining area where he hangs out all day long, eating, relaxing, or wandering around. However, during the quest [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md), when you return to the fort after visiting [Volkihar Keep](Skyrim_Volkihar_Keep.md) with [Serana](Skyrim_Serana.md), you will find Celann, Isran, and [Durak](Skyrim_Durak.md) fighting off an attack by vampires just outside the gate. After the battle, Celann will retake his position outside the gate, ready to protect the fort. He will stay put until you bring the reinforcements, [Sorine Jurard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sorine_Jurard) and [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md).

He is clad in a set of Dawnguard light equipment. This includes the gray [cuirass](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Armor.md) as well as matching pairs of [gauntlets](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Gauntlets.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Boots.md). He is equipped with a [Dawnguard war axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md) and carries a [torch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Torch). He knows the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell [Sun Fire](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sun_Fire).

## Quest-Related Events

### Dawnguard
When you arrive at the gate of Fort Dawnguard for the first time, along with newcomer [Agmaer](Skyrim_Agmaer.md), you will find Celann guarding the gate, saying:

- *"New recruits? Hmm."*
- *"Here to join the Dawnguard? Good."*
- *"Isran will decide if you've got what it takes. Go on, he's right inside."*

When you speak to him he will immediately share his concerns:

- *"I'll tell you, the only thing more surprising than hearing from Isran after all these years was hearing that he wanted my help. I immediately realized things must be pretty bad. Looks like I was right."*
- **You've worked with Isran before?**
- *"I have. There was a time, years ago, when we were both members of the Vigilants, and both equally dissatisfied with them. Their hearts are in the right place, of course. But Isran and I were never comfortable. We left together, but that partnership didn't last very long. I didn't agree with some of his methods."*

He will finish the conversation with encouragement:

- *"Go on inside. Isran'll want to talk to you."*
- *"May you always prevail."*

### Awakening
When you return to him after Isran's given you the quest [Awakening](Skyrim_Awakening.md), he will joyfully remark:

- *"Isran found something for you to do, eh? I thought he'd like you. Good luck!"*

### Kindred Judgment
When it is time to confront [Lord Harkon](Skyrim_Lord_Harkon.md) during the quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md), Celann will listen to Isran's speech in the main hall along with the other Dawnguard members. When Isran gives the order, Celann travels halfway across [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) to Castle Volkihar and will participate in the joint effort to kill all residing vampires, while shouting:

- *"Kill the abominations!"*

When all is said and done, he will sheathe his weapon and walk all the way back to Fort Dawnguard and live there indefinitely with the other members.

## Unused Scenes
In an earlier iteration of [A New Order](Skyrim_A_New_Order.md), you were originally meant to start out at [Stendarr's Beacon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stendarr%27s_Beacon). Here, a meeting between [Isran](Skyrim_Isran.md) and [Vigilant Tolan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vigilant_Tolan) would have taken place. At some unknown point after this, you would've been sent to Fort Dawnguard for the very first time, where you would've been greeted by this scene between Celann and [Durak](Skyrim_Durak.md):

- **Durak:** *"Here comes another one!"*
- **Celann:** *"Hold it! She's/He's the one Tolan mentioned."*
- **Durak:** *"Ugh. I thought this place was supposed to be secret?"*
- **Celann:** *"Well it was... Thank the Vigilants for ruining that."*

## Notes
- Celann is one of few followers who have no level cap. He will continue to level up with you.

## Bugs
- Celann is equipped with a set of light armor despite being more proficient in [Heavy Armor](Skyrim_Heavy_Armor.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.1, addresses this issue. He has been equipped with a set of heavy Dawnguard armor.
- Celann has the [Custom Fit](Skyrim_Custom_Fit.md) perk even though he is more proficient in Heavy Armor. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.1.1, addresses this issue. He has been given the [Well Fitted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Well_Fitted) perk instead.
- During Kindred Judgment and/or Destroying the Dawnguard, Celann may be given 15 crossbows in his inventory, which is clearly an error as it was a copypasta matching the amount of steel bolts he's given. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.7, fixes this bug.
- He has the [Champion's Stance](Skyrim_Champion%27s_Stance.md) perk even though he is more proficient in [one-handed](Skyrim_One-handed.md) weapons (and has 3 perks for them).
