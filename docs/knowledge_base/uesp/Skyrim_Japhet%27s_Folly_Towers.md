## Walkthrough

### Sea Cave
The entrance leads directly into the cave, which is used for bringing in provisions via rowboat. It is home to two [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md). There are numerous crates, barrels, and sacks of [food](Skyrim_Food.md) throughout the cave, some of which have bottles of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) or [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) on top. Against the west wall is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md). A path leading up to a short passage is to the south. This leads into a second room with more crates, barrels, and sacks, and also a hole through a wall opposite into a fort. There is another mudcrab in this second room, and any noise will alert a [Blood Horker](Skyrim_Blood_Horker.md) guard that is standing beyond the hole in the wall, helpfully facing away from you unless alerted to your presence.

Through the hole is a storeroom containing a large stack of unusable barrels, several crates and sacks containing food, and two sets of shelves holding seven bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead), a random [potion of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), and a leveled [potion of healing](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Healing). Hanging from the ceiling from racks are four [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md), three [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md), three [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braids, and four [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). There is an unlocked chest against the rear wall, and in the southwestern corner is a flight of stairs leading up to a wooden door that leads to [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md).

## Bugs
- [Japhet's Folly](Skyrim_Japhet%27s_Folly.md) has many severe glitches. See the Japhet's Folly page for more details.
- Beware a glitch that occurs when returning to Japhet's Folly via the Sea Cave after the bombardment has started. The door through which you come will now be disabled, preventing you from going back the way you came, but the top section of the tower is now barred by rubble, leaving you trapped in the bottom section.
