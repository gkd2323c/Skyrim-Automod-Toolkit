# Mihuitleel

The Mihuitleel (meaning **feathered people**) are a breed and sub-tribe of [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) that live on the island of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md), considered part of the greater [Tide-Born tribe](Lore_Tide-Born.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AWS-1)</sup> Most Argonians share a basic morphological pattern, but differences between different kinds of Argonians can range from minimal to significant.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TOBR-2)</sup> Mihuitleel have some distinct differences in their appearance compared to the Argonians most commonly seen throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

The Mihuitleel are said to be covered in iridescent green feathers that allow them to blend in with the leaves of the [palm trees](Lore_Palm_Tree.md) they like to perch upon, and they may even have the ability to fly. Locals tell stories of how the Mihuitleel have "swooped in" to save them from beasts or [cultists](Lore_Worm_Cult.md). While [Keshu the Black Fin](Lore_Keshu_the_Black_Fin.md) was unable to confirm the existence of the Mihuitleel during her trip to Solstice, she did witness several hatchlings with feathered dolls, every single one of them played with them as if they could fly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AWS-1)</sup>
