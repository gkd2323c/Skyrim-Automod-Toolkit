[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/ON-map-Lluvamir.jpg/200px-ON-map-Lluvamir.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Lluvamir.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Lluvamir.jpg) Map of Iluvamir circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) **Iluvamir** or **Lluvamir** is the southernmost region of the island of [Auridon](Lore_Auridon.md) in the province of the [Summerset Isles](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md). The Iluvamir is the closest to sea level the rest of Auridon and this reflects the area. The city of [Vulkhel Guard](Lore_Vulkhel_Guard.md) is located on the southern shores and is where the Summerset strait begins. The townships of [Silsailen](Lore_Silsailen.md) and [Phaer](Lore_Phaer.md) are located here, benefiting on the landscape. The ruins of [Tanzelwil](Lore_Tanzelwil.md) occupy the northern hills and is a very significant place in Auridon.
