[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/56/SR-npc-Traveling_Bard_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Traveling_Bard_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Bard_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Bard_02.jpg) Traveling Bard (vampire hunter) This **Traveling Bard** can be encountered during radiant [side quests](Skyrim_Dawnguard_Quests.md#Vampire_Side_Quests) for the vampires if you ally with them when playing [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md). Their appearance as a traveling bard is a disguise to cover their true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) hunter. They wear a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and [hat](Skyrim_Hat.md). They carry a [Dawnguard War Axe](Skyrim_Dawnguard_War_Axe.md), an enchanted [crossbow](Skyrim_Crossbow.md), three [steel bolts](Skyrim_Steel_Bolt.md), and an enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md).

## Related Quests
- **[The Hunt](Skyrim_The_Hunt.md)**: Kill the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md) spy masquerading as a citizen. ([radiant](Skyrim_Radiant.md))

## Dialogue

- **Play me a song, "bard".**
- *"A song? I, uh... I seem to have misplaced my lute. Another time perhaps?"*

- **You're no bard.**
- *"I beg your pardon. I don't know what you're getting at, but whatever it is, it's not appreciated."*

- **Come with me, "bard".**
- *"Why? What do you want?"*

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-npc-Traveling_Bard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Traveling_Bard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Bard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveling_Bard.jpg) Traveling Bard (vampire) This **Traveling Bard** can be encountered during the radiant [Hide and Seek](Skyrim_Hide_and_Seek_(quest).md) quest for [Gunmar](Skyrim_Gunmar.md). His appearance as a traveling pilgrim is a disguise to cover his true nature as a [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) and you will be tasked with eliminating him.

He wears merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), and he carries an enchanted [ring](Skyrim_Ring.md) and an enchanted [dagger](Skyrim_Dagger.md).
