## Quick Walkthrough
1. Speak with [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md) and receive the list.
2. Obtain a [Vampire Fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Fish).
3. Obtain a [Tripod Spiderfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripod_Spiderfish).
4. Obtain a [Glass Catfish](Skyrim_Glass_Catfish.md).
5. Obtain a [Direfish](Skyrim_Direfish.md).
6. (Optional) Read [Fishing Mastery, v4](Skyrim_Fishing_Mastery,_v4.md).

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speak to Swims:

- **I'm ready for the next fishing challenge.**
- *"Of course. Maybe you can help me."*

- **I'll explore underground and bring you the fish you need.** / **Caves are dangerous. You're lucky I'm the one doing this for you.**
- *"My thanks to you."*

He will then hand you [list of underground fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:List_of_Underground_Fish) to catch:

- [Direfish](Skyrim_Direfish.md)
- [Glass Catfish](Skyrim_Glass_Catfish.md)
- [Tripod Spiderfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tripod_Spiderfish)
- [Vampire Fish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Fish)

These fish can all be caught in underground fishing spots such as [Embershard Mine](Skyrim_Embershard_Mine.md) and [Broken Oar Grotto](Skyrim_Broken_Oar_Grotto.md), a full list of underground fishing spots can be found [here](Skyrim_Fishing_(activity).md#Fishing_Locations). (note - if fourth volume of Fishing Mastery is read, the quest shows "Eldergleam Sanctuary in Eastmarch,..." but there is no fishing spot in there. However, several Spadefish can be caught by hand in the cave's pool. 1 is needed for 'Caught in the Rain' part of this questline)

Once you have caught the required fish return to Swims:

- **Here are the fish you asked for.**
- *"Thank you. Here, this is for you."*

He will then hand you 400 gold.
