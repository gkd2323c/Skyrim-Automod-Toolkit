[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Plate1.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Plate1.png) Aretino Family Heirloom The Aretino Family Heirloom is a unique plate gifted to you by [Aventus Aretino](Skyrim_Aventus_Aretino.md) in [Innocence Lost](Skyrim_Innocence_Lost.md). In appearance and weight it is identical to a generic [plate](Skyrim_Plate.md), but is worth 100 gold compared to the 5 gold of regular plates. It can be sold to [General Goods stores](Skyrim_Merchants.md) and [Caravans](Skyrim_Khajiit_Traders.md).
