We Reject the Pact **by [Archmagister Nelos Otheri](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Archmagister_Nelos_Otheri)** *House Telvanni's formal refusal to join the Ebonheart Pact*
---
With salutations, appreciation, and deep respect to the [Mercymother](Lore_Almalexia.md), the [Master](Lore_Vivec.md), and the [Father of Mysteries](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), we the undersigned summarily reject the offer to join the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) alongside the other [Great Houses](Lore_Great_Houses.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).

[House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md) stands alone.

Our august body has been a bastion of power and excellence since [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) was young. Before the excavation of the [Library of Andule](Lore_Library_of_Andule.md), the Escharotic Schism, the [War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md), the [Battle of Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md), or the disappearance of the hated [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md), we have kept our own council and chosen our own path.

Telvanni lands east of the [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md) region and north of [Deshaan](Lore_Deshaan.md) will remain held in trust by the Council, and any attempt by Pact forces to enter these holdings will be met with a swift and vigorous rebuke.

Attached to this missive, please find several codices outlining our recommendations regarding trade, defense, maritime navigation, taxation, embassy rights, and travel protocols surrounding the ancient city of [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md).

Our coterie of representatives from the [Barristers Guild](Lore_Barristers_Guild.md) will be in touch.

Archmagister [Nelos Otheri](Lore_Nelos_Otheri.md)
