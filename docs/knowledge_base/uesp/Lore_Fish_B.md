## Bass
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Brook_Bass.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-food-Brook_Bass.png) A Brook Bass The **Bass** is a type of fish that can be caught almost anywhere in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).

- The **Brook Bass** can be found in the temperate lakes and rivers of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md).
- The **Ghastel Bass** can be found in the foul waters of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md).
- The **Jungle Bass** can be found in the lakes of [Greenshade](Lore_Greenshade.md).
- The **Rock Bass** can be found in the lakes of [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).
- The **Sea Bass** can be found in the [Abecean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), off the coasts of Malabal Tor.
- The **Spotted Bass** can be found in the sunny lake waters of the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md).
- The **Sunscale Bass** can be found in the lakes of western [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).
- The **Yellow Bass** can be found in the lakes of [Stormhaven](Lore_Stormhaven.md).

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Brook_Bass.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>, [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Blobfin
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Blobfish.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-fish-Blobfish.png) A blobfin The **Blobfin** or **Blobfish** is a type of fish that live in polluted waters. They could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).

- The **Blue Blobfin** can be found in the foul waters of eastern [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md).
- The **Histmuck Blobfin** can be found in [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md) during certain times of the year.
- The **Imperfect Blobfin**, likely named after the [Imperfect](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Imperfect), can be found in the oily waters of the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md). They were previously presumed to be extinct.
- Blobfish were susceptible to corruption from the [Wildburn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wildburn), **Wildburn Blobfish** could be found in the foul waters of the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)
