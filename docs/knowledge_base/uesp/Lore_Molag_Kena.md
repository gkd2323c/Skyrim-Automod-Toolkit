Molag Kena is a powerful [Xivkyn](Lore_Xivkyn.md) who serves [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Molag Kena-1)</sup> Referred to as the xivkyn monarch<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MKS-2)</sup> or sovereign,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ESOUrgarlag-3)</sup> she is very gifted in the use of magic, particularly in the school of destruction.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Molag Kena-1)</sup> She wields powerful lightning magic in combat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Molag Kena-1)</sup>

Molag Kena, disguised as the Empress Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), ruled over the Imperial City for an indeterminate amount of time.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ERCTSS-4)</sup> She consistently requested troops from the Imperial Legion to report to her personally, off the books.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ERCTSS-4)</sup> She moved the troops this way and that, having them defend positions with no strategic value and sending them off to die at the hands of the invading [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) for nothing.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ERCTSS-4)</sup> Molag Kena manipulated legion officials with blackmail and other underhanded tactics to do what she wanted, and called one General Valerius to the White-Gold Tower before the Imperial City was overrun by Molag Bal's forces.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ERCTSS-4)</sup>

She eventually decided to "retake" the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) in a last-ditch effort to hasten the merging of worlds.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOPO-5)</sup> Her mission was to claim an [Elder Scroll](Lore_Elder_Scroll.md) to initiate an event known as the [Planemeld Obverse](Lore_Planemeld.md#The_Planemeld_Obverse_and_the_Sublime_Brazier).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOPO-5)</sup> She was stopped by a [moth priestess](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) named Sister Terran Arminus atop the White-Gold Tower with the help of the [Undaunted](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Undaunted), who defeated her before she could complete the ritual.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ESOPO-5)</sup>

The [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) later led an attack on the Ossein Cage in [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md) to destroy the [Dolorus Cista](Lore_Dolorous_Cista.md). The cista was protected by Overfiend Kazpian, who used its powers to call upon the greatest servants of Coldharbour to defend it. Molag Kena was among the summoned Daedra, but she was banished by the Fighters Guild and the cista was destroyed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

The significance of Molag Kena allowed her to be documented within the [Infinite Archive](Lore_Infinite_Archive.md) in [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md). There, copies of various entities were subsequently created as [maligraphies](Lore_Maligraphy.md), creatures composed of vivified ink manifested by stories from books, and serve as mindless extensions of the [Daedric Lord](Lore_Daedric_Lord.md) [Tho'at Replicanum](Lore_Tho%27at_Replicanum.md). A maligraphy copy of Molag Kena was formed, which was destroyed by an adventurer or two who were aided by augmentations directly from [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LAIA-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-MM-8)</sup><sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Enemies-9)</sup>
