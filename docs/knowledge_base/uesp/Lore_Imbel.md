# Imbel

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/30/OBR-npc-Jakben%2C_Earl_of_Imbel.jpg/200px-OBR-npc-Jakben%2C_Earl_of_Imbel.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Jakben,_Earl_of_Imbel.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-npc-Jakben,_Earl_of_Imbel.jpg) Jakben, Earl of Imbel The Imbels are a noble family of [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md), with ancestors that lived on [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) (around [Suran](Lore_Suran.md)) and later in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) (around [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md), [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), and [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md)) in the second millennium of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-IG-1)</sup> In the closing days of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), a Breton noble named [Jakben](Lore_Jakben.md) held the Earldom of Imbel and he had his estate in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) where he lived.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Jakben-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-VNC-4)</sup>

## Notes
- The family's name, particularly Jakben Imbel, is an out-of-world reference to the nursery rhyme *[Jack Be Nimble](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jack_Be_Nimble)*.
- It is unknown what fief or lands the Imbels held if any, which the title of "Earl" would typically be indicative of.
- The [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) historically had ties with the [Dark Elves](Lore_Dark_Elves.md) through a trade deal with [Clan R'aathim](Lore_Ra%27athim.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) and the Kings of High Rock, where the retainers [King Moraelyn](Lore_Moraelyn.md) of [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md) were allowed to mine in the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md) for [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) and start a colony for their people in High Rock in exchange for providing ebony weapons to the Breton lords and teaching the Bretons how to smith ebony themselves.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-KE-5)</sup>
- Ruffe Imbel was an agent working for [House Dorell](Lore_House_Dorell.md) active in [Oldgate](Lore_Oldgate.md). As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) he was a potential target of the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-DBC-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-RI-7)</sup>
