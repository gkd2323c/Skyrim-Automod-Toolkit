[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Zaharia.jpg) Zaharia **Zaharia** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) member of the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md) and former [Crimson Dirk](Skyrim_Crimson_Dirks.md) who may be encountered alive or dead depending on your choices during the quest [Double-Edged](Skyrim_Double-Edged.md).

Zaharia wears a set of enchanted [Leather Scout Armor](Skyrim_Leather_Scout_Armor.md) and carries a random selection of loot.

## Notes
- There are in fact, two versions of Zaharia: - The alive version has a base ID of `FExxx81E` and a ref ID of `FExxx821`.
- The dead version has a base ID of `FExxx81F` and a ref ID of `FExxx822`.
