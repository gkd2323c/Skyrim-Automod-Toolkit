[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/ON-place-Kragenmoor.jpg/200px-ON-place-Kragenmoor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kragenmoor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Kragenmoor.jpg) Kragenmoor ca. 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/AR-map-Morrowind_%28annotated%29.jpg/200px-AR-map-Morrowind_%28annotated%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Morrowind_(annotated).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-map-Morrowind_(annotated).jpg) Map of Morrowind Kragenmoor is one of the eight major cities on the mainland of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup> located in the ash wastes of [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls.md). The city is the great nexus of the region's farming industry and it has historically been occupied by the Great [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md). While some accounts described Kragenmoor as wretched and downtrodden, others such as the one from [Flaccus Terentius](Lore_Flaccus_Terentius.md) noted the miles of [saltrice](Lore_Saltrice.md) plantations that stretched across the fertile valley. For a time in the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Kragenmoor was the capital of House Dres and was the home of the Dres Grandmaster.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TEGMW-2)</sup>

## Layout and Geography
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-place-Grandmaster%27s_Palace.jpg/200px-ON-place-Grandmaster%27s_Palace.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Grandmaster%27s_Palace.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Grandmaster%27s_Palace.jpg) Kragenmoor Manor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/ON-place-House_Dres_Crypts.jpg/200px-ON-place-House_Dres_Crypts.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-House_Dres_Crypts.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-House_Dres_Crypts.jpg) The House Dres Crypts Kragenmoor is located in the territory of House Dres, who occupied portions of central Morrowind many years ago in history.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-UHD-3)</sup> The city itself is nestled in a fertile valley that is part of the [Valus Mountains](Lore_Valus_Mountains.md) and is surrounded by miles of saltrice plantations.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-TEGMW-2)</sup> The region around it is very rural and far removed from the larger cities on the coast. The main road runs through the greater expanse and passes Kragenmoor to the north. But on the east end of that road is the path to the southern grazelands and also the [Tormented Spire](Lore_Tormented_Spire.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKragenmoor-4)</sup> a cursed mountain that is sealed in a mystic veil by the ruler of Kragenmoor. In fact, the city is also called the "gateway to the Tormented Spire."<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONHolgunn-5)</sup>

The city is built on a mountain in the middle of a valley and it has a large stone foundation around it to level it out. The city is split between an upper district and a lower district. The upper portion is on the stone foundation and it connects to other parts of the valley through bridges. Here lies the grandiose Kragenmoor Manor is located and it overlooks the main road from the high precipice. Meanwhile, the lower portion is located underneath these bridges and around the base of the stone foundation.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONKragenmoor-4)</sup> This area is where slaves and the lower class generally live and it is not as nicer as the above ground level.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoad Kragenhome-6)</sup> Kragenmoor is neighbored by several settlements, including [Karththor Dale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Karththor_Dale) to the north, [Stonefalls](Lore_Stonefalls_(settlement).md) to the northeast, [Oaktown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Oaktown) to the southeast, and the [Imperial Province](Lore_Imperial_Province.md) to the southwest, toward the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARMap-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ARMap Imp-7)</sup>

## History
Kragenmoor is an ancient city that dates back to at least the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md). Even though House Dres have been historically associated with southeast Morrowind, around their ancestral capital, [Tear](Lore_Tear.md), they have been known to expand their reach into central Morrowind where Kragenmoor is located.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-UHD-3)</sup> But when exactly the city was founded is currently unknown. In the years after the [Conquest of Morrowind](Lore_Conquest_of_Morrowind.md) and after the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) were vanquished from ancient Resdayn, the city was under the rule of Queen Yoriss of [Clan Ra'athim](Lore_Ra%27athim.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-KEX-10)</sup> In ancient times, the royal cadet branch of House Dres, the venerable Dreloth Clan came to Kragenmoor after they relocated from [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in the northeast. But despite this, their ancestral tomb is still located there, on the coast south-southwest of [Sadrith Mora](Lore_Sadrith_Mora.md).<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONLoad Dreloth-11)</sup> In the mid-First Era, the vast crypt of the royal Dres families was re-discovered in the mountains just west of the city and was promptly excavated beneath Kragenmoor by their [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) slaves.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONLoad Dres-12)</sup> It was reportedly built during the time of the [Chimer](Lore_Chimer.md) to honor the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-ONDenu-13)</sup>

During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Kragenmoor was the capital city of House Dres and by this time, they were led by Grandmaster Omin Dres and in an alliance called the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md). After a decisive battle in [Fort Virak](Lore_Fort_Virak.md) to the north, the Indoril Vanguard led by [Tanval Indoril](Lore_Tanval_Indoril.md) traveled south to Kragenmoor with the [Coral Heart](Lore_Coral_Heart.md), on their way to the summit of the Tormented Spire. When the Grandmaster refused Tanval access to the mountain, the vanguard took Omin's son hostage deep in the House Dres Crypts. Not long after, an [Agent](Lore_The_Vestige.md) of the Pact followed them to Kragenmoor and offered to rescue Sen Dres. After delving into the crypt, they were able to secure Sen Dres and acquire access to the Tormented Spire for a final confrontation.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-ONSaving-14)</sup>

In the late- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) and his [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) massed in great numbers west of both Kragenmoor and [Silgrad Tower](Lore_Silgrad_Tower.md), as well as in the northernmost borderlands. However, while the Great [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md) was willing to face the grand army right on the front, the other Great Houses of Dres and [Indoril](Lore_Indoril.md) preferred to bolster the interior for guerrilla warfare instead of placing garrisons across the western front, where they were invading from. Whereas the Great House of [Hlaalu](Lore_Hlaalu.md) would rather make peace with the burgeoning [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md). In the end, the Dark Elves had fallen and their country was incorporated into the Third Empire as the Imperial Province of Morrowind.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-OM-15)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), the city-state of Kragenmoor was an active settlement. It was ruled by King Shalpar and it had a rivalry with [Ebonheart](Lore_Ebonheart.md).<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-AR-16)</sup> On the 2nd of [Rain's Hand](Lore_Rain%27s_Hand.md), [Frathen Drothan](Lore_Frathen_Drothan.md) and his [militia](Lore_Drothmeri_Army.md) gathered in Kragenmoor before they advanced onto [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and the abandoned waystation, [Sundercliff](Lore_Sundercliff.md) in the [Nibenay Basin](Lore_Nibenay_Basin.md).<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-DJ-17)</sup> The city saw little disturbance with the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) later in the year compared to Cyrodiil, but the events that transpired in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and Akatosh's intervention had increased interest for the local shrine ten-fold and many people, ranging from skeptics to old Tribunal worshippers.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CCL-9)</sup>

## Notes
- Kragenmoor is the ancient home of the [Mirthless Order](Lore_Mirthless_Order.md), a group that protected the rite of a performance. If the performance was not prepared properly or not said verbatim, the Mirthless Order will kill the performer.<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ONLaughs-18)</sup><sup>[[18]](#cite_note-ONGeril-19)</sup>
- Despite the very similar names, there is no discernable connection between the Dark Elven city of Kragenmoor and the Dwemer clan, [Kragen](Lore_Kragen.md).<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-AMOVAB-20)</sup>
- Though Elder Scrolls Online depicts the city as associated with House Dres, Kragenmoor seems to have originally been considered a holding of House Hlaalu.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-CCL-9)</sup><sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-MWMap-21)</sup>
- *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Arena)* was originally conceived as a fighting game featuring a tournament that took the player to each of Tamriel's cities to challenge different gladiatorial teams. According to a file from that stage of development left behind in the final game, Kragenmoor's gladiatorial team would have been called "the Warhammers".<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-22)</sup>
