Kyrnil Long-Nose was a Harbinger of the [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). He was among the notable Harbingers recorded by [Swyk the Long-Sighted](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Swyk_the_Long-Sighted). He is credited with restoring the honor of the Companions after the dark periods of the Second Era.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c2/SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Jorrvaskr.jpg) Jorrvaskr, the mead hall of the Companions. After the disappearance of [Macke of the Piercing Eyes](Lore_Macke_of_the_Piercing_Eyes.md), the Companions entered a dark period.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup> A string of false and dishonorable Harbingers laid claim to [Jorrvaskr](Lore_Jorrvaskr.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> leaving it corrupted.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Emp-2)</sup> Intent on restoring honor to the faction, Kyrnil gathered the true hearts of the Companions in the wilds of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and stormed Jorrvaskr. The attack was successful, killing the usurpers and returning the old way of honor through blood.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup>

Kyrnil began the tradition of keeping a group of trusted advisors called [the Circle](Lore_Companions.md#The_Circle), named after [Ysgramor](Lore_Ysgramor.md)'s Circle of Captains. They served to set examples to the newer, younger Companions.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup> Kyrnil ensured that notions of honor could have an unbroken string of tradition, steadying the course of the Companions back to the destiny of Ysgramor's.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-GH-1)</sup>
