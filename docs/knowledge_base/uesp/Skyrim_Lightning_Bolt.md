*A bolt of lightning that does 25 points of shock damage to Health and half that to Magicka.* **Lightning Bolt** is an apprentice level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that deals [shock damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage) to Health and Magicka.

## Effects
- [Lightning Bolt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shock_Damage), 25 pts
- [Perk Impact Stagger](Skyrim_Stagger.md), 0.25 pts; if the [Impact](Skyrim_Impact.md) perk has been unlocked
- [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md), 200 pts for 1 sec; if the [Disintegrate](Skyrim_Disintegrate.md) perk has been unlocked

## Perks
- [Augmented Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Augmented_Shock), raises damage to 31.25 at first rank or 37.5 at second rank

## Notes
- Like most Lightning spells, it can be used to set off [Lightning Runes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lightning_Rune) from a safe distance.
- Has much shorter range than the equivalent [Firebolt](Skyrim_Firebolt.md) and [Ice Spike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike) spells, but the fact that it is cast instantly instead of as a projectile makes it much easier to hit moving targets so long as they're within range.
- The bolt seems to weave through its trajectory, so sometimes it misses the point of aim especially at a distance.
