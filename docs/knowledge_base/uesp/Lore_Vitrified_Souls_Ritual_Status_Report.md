Vitrified Souls Ritual Status Report **by [Penitent Ridena](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Penitent_Ridena)** *A report about the Worm Cult's actions at Oozt-Tzel* *
---
Putrescent Bonelord Kylius,

Cleanup continues after the initial invasion. As you are well aware, the new Planemeld proceeds at an acceptable pace. The local [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md) have largely been dealt with—the deathsingers and scythe knights eliminated the most troublesome individuals, while our skull archers prevented too many from fleeing the area.

We retained as many of them as possible for use as subjects in the vitrified souls rituals. Our necromancers have found that an excess of fear in the subject at the moment of ritual completion leads to a more potent final product. New methods are being devised to incrementally increase their terror to extract the maximum amount of power for our continued use.

Ritual sites continue to be relocated daily to account for energy fluctuations emanating from [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md). This is a time-consuming process, but we found that using the imprisoned test subjects for their labor greatly improves our efficiency. Moreover, refusing to tell the prisoners the purpose of the endeavor creates an aura of dread that proves useful during the ritual itself.

We continue to face a shortage of viable test subjects. Order members have been assigned the task of procuring more from the surrounding region, but we must be careful not to draw too much attention until our plans near completion. This is made more difficult by Riuzzan's immense appetite. It's grown accustomed to feeding on prisoners and test subjects, and often claims the vitrified souls for itself after each ritual. I suggest finding an alternative means of feeding the [Maldrith](Lore_Maldrith.md) until a reasonable stockpile of vitrified souls can be accrued. May I suggest the [shriven](Lore_Soul_Shriven.md) Argonians? They're of little use to us, being soulless, and hunting them may keep Riuzzan away from the rituals for a time.

Finally, there's the matter of the infidel. We've yet to locate Ojouna or work out how to reproduce her ritual foci. I have several apprentices and sentinels assigned to the task, but they've yet to prove themselves. They may also be good candidates for Riuzzan should they fail us.

Black Worm Be Sated,
Penitent Ridena
