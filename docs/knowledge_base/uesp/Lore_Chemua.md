*CHEMUA, the Running Hunger, who appeared as a mounted soldier with full helm, had the powers of Heart Roaring and of sky sickening. He ate the Chimeri hero, Dres Khizumet-e, sending the spirit back to the Hortator as an assassin. Sometimes called First Blighter, Chemua could give clouds stomach aches and turn the rain of Veloth into bile. He destroyed six Chimeri villages before he was slain by Vivec and the Hortator.*
— [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup> Chemua (also stylized as **CHEMUA**), known as **the Running Hunger**, and **the First Blighter**, was a powerful [Tongue](Lore_Tongue.md) and one of the [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) war chiefs who took part in the [Conquest](Lore_Conquest_of_Morrowind.md) of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md) by the [First Empire of the Nords](Lore_First_Empire_of_the_Nords.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

## History

### First Era
Little is known of Chemua's early life. The precise date of his birth is unknown, although he was alive during the lifespan of the Nordic Empire, in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md) and the Conquest of Morrowind.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGEMW-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE1ES-3)</sup> He was one of several war leaders known to wield the Voice vilified by Dunmer historical accounts as a "demon chieftain". According to the Vivec, he was a mounted soldier with a full helm and wielded powers he described as Heart Roaring and Sky Sickening. He caused the rains in Morrowind to turn into bile, and was capable of devouring the spirits of his enemies and sending them back against their former allies as [revenants](Lore_Ash_Creature.md#The_Ash_King). Such was the fate of the [Chimeri](Lore_Chimer.md) hero, Dres Khizumet-e, whose spirit was sent back to the [Hortator](Lore_Hortator.md) [Indoril Nerevar](Lore_Indoril_Nerevar.md) as an assassin. Chemua destroyed six Chimeri villages before he was slain by Vivec and Nerevar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup>

### Legacy
Chemua was named by Vivec alongside [Hoag Merkiller](Lore_Hoag_Merkiller.md), the Mouth of Mud, [Bhag](Lore_Bhag.md), the Two-Tongued, [Barfok](Lore_Barfok.md), Maid of Planes, and [Ysmir, Dragon of the North Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md), as one of the five greatest and most dangerous chieftains of the conquering Nords in his *[Sermons](Lore_The_36_Lessons_of_Vivec.md)*.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Vivec-1)</sup> Vivec mentioned Chemua again in his guide to [Buoyant Armiger](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) combat vestments, invoking him in comparison to those crowned as Buoyant Armigers, wreathed in verdant flares of solid flame.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CMBA-4)</sup>
