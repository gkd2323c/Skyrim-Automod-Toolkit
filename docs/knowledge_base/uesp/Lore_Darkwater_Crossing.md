[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7e/SR-place-Darkwater_Crossing.jpg/200px-SR-place-Darkwater_Crossing.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darkwater_Crossing.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Darkwater_Crossing.jpg) Darkwater Crossing circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1c/ON-place-Darkwater_Apothecary.jpg/200px-ON-place-Darkwater_Apothecary.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Darkwater_Apothecary.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Darkwater_Apothecary.jpg) Darkwater Crossing circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Darkwater Crossing is a settlement located along the banks of the [Darkwater River](Lore_Darkwater_River.md) in southern [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).

The area was first settled during the time of the [Ebonheart Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md), when a group of [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) migrants came from the west to settle at [Fort Amol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Fort_Amol). The town was not big enough to absorb them, so instead, they settled at Darkwater Crossing. Despite being an Argonian village, it was built using [Nordic](Lore_Nord.md) architecture. At some point, a [Redguard](Lore_Redguard.md) named Asmalah the Caller came to the village with his sick wife. The village healer, a Dreamwalker named Stormy-Eyes, attempted to use his powers to cure her, but instead, his methods resulted in her death. Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Asmalah got his revenge by surrounding the village with blood, causing [trolls](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Troll) to attack. The surviving Argonians fled to a nearby cave, and it was hoped that the town could be retaken after the death of the trolls' leader, Death-In-Winter.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The area was re-settled in the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) when Goldenrock Mine was opened after a Nord woman named Annekke Crag-Jumper discovered [corundum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Corundum) deposits nearby.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), during the [Stormcloak Rebellion](Lore_Stormcloak_Rebellion.md), [Ulfric Stormcloak](Lore_Ulfric_Stormcloak.md) was captured near the settlement in an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md) ambush before being taken to [Helgen](Lore_Helgen.md) for execution. The Rebellion had a bad effect on Darkwater Crossing, with soldiers passing through constantly and the miners only scraping by.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>
