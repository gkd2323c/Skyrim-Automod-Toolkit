Crimes of the Daggerfall Covenant
A Summary **by [Aicantar of Shimmerene](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Aicantar_of_Shimmerene), Sapiarch of Indoctrination** *Dominion propaganda decrying the Covenant*
---
The kingdoms of the [Daggerfall Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) are inhabited by mongrel upstarts who have cruelly suppressed our [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md) cousins: the [High Elves](Lore_Altmer.md) who are the rightful rulers of northwest [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). They were largely complicit in the rise of the dangerous and genocidal fanatics known as the [Alessian Order](Lore_Alessian_Order.md). They allow their pirates to prey on [Aldmeri](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md) shipping. The [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md) are also so indiscriminate that they have accepted the bestial [Orsimer](Lore_Orc.md) as allies. (Orsimer! Can you imagine?) Though capable of mustering substantial brute force, there is no evidence that these degraded hybrids have the wisdom or learning to deal with the mystical disaster now threatening the [Mundus](Lore_Mundus.md). They must be disciplined and subdued as rapidly as possible, so the Dominion can get on with the business of saving the world … and protecting it in the future.
