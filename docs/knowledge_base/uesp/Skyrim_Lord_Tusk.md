[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/SR-creature-Lord_Tusk.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Lord_Tusk.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lord_Tusk.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Lord_Tusk.jpg) Lord Tusk on Horker Island **Lord Tusk** is a unique [horker](Skyrim_Horker.md) found at [Horker Island](Skyrim_Horker_Island.md). His noticeable traits, which differentiate him from other horkers, are his increased size and health. By moving past Lord Tusk, one can find the unique mace, [Horksbane](Skyrim_Horksbane.md), which deals extra damage to horkers.

## Notes
- Lord Tusk respawns every time Horker Island resets.
