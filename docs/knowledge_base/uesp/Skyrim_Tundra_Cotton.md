[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/67/SR-flora-Tundra_Cotton_Plant.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Tundra_Cotton_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Tundra_Cotton_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Tundra_Cotton_Plant.jpg) A tundra cotton plant [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3b/SR-book-Tundracotton.png/100px-SR-book-Tundracotton.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Tundracotton.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-book-Tundracotton.png) Tundra cotton as depicted in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)* **Tundra Cotton** is a plant which mostly grows in the plains of [Whiterun Hold](Skyrim_Whiterun_Hold.md).

- *"Not all Nords are savages wrapped in animal skins, howling at the moon. There are a wide variety of fabrics worn throughout the land, thanks in no small part to the stubborn Tundra Cotton plant. It soaks up what rain it can in the plains west of [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md), and blooms frequently. While it does not exhibit any of the more striking alchemical properties, it is a staple in potions for not only [fortifying magicka](Skyrim_Fortify_Magicka.md), but for [resisting spells](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md) as well. I wonder if that quality is what allows it to have adapted so well to this climate."* —Agneta Falia in the *[Herbalist's Guide to Skyrim](Skyrim_Herbalist%27s_Guide_to_Skyrim.md)*.

## Ingredients
157 guaranteed samples can be found in 55 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 15 in the [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))
- 12 in the [Blue Palace](Skyrim_Blue_Palace.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 10 in [Erikur's House](Skyrim_Erikur%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 10 in [Vittoria Vici's House](Skyrim_Vittoria_Vici%27s_House.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).
