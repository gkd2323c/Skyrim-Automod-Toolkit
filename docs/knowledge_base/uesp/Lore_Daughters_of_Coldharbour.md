# Daughters of Coldharbour

A Daughter of Coldharbour is a female individual who has been granted [vampirism](Lore_Vampire.md) through a ritual with the creator of vampires, [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Val-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo V2-2)</sup> The term is partially derived from the name of Molag Bal's plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), [Coldharbour](Lore_Coldharbour.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDOB-3)</sup>

The first Daughter of Coldharbour [Lamae Beolfag](Lore_Lamae_Beolfag.md) was a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) virgin and priestess of Arkay who was raped by Molag Bal.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OLB-4)</sup> Legends recount that after the ordeal, Molag Bal shed a droplet of blood upon her brow and left her to die.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OLB-4)</sup> During the night she passed into death, but as her funeral pyre was still burning, she emerged as the first pure-blood vampire. Then she raped all the men in the village as savagely as Molag Bal had raped her.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-OLB-4)</sup>

Since Lamae, a tradition developed among cults dedicated to Molag Bal, which dictated that women be offered to him on his summoning day<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Val-1)</sup> on the [20th of Evening Star](Lore_Calendar.md#Evening_Star).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Invoc Az-5)</sup> Few survive the ordeal, and those who do emerge as pure-blooded vampires. Being selected as an offering was considered to be an honor that was not to be rejected lightly.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Val-1)</sup>

Due to their pure blood, Daughters of Coldharbour are by definition [Vampire Lords](Lore_Vampire_Lord.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> However, vampires are inherently shape-changers, and one cannot rely on late-stage vampires of differing strains to look alike.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-7)</sup> Consequentially, some pure-blooded female vampires may possess the ability to assume more varied forms. For instance, one of the elder vampires imprisoned within [Ashbound Hall](Lore_Ashbound_Hall.md) could transform into a powerful [Bloodknight](Lore_Bloodknight.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-8)</sup>

## Tyranny of the Sun
A prophecy written in the First Era by a [Snow Elf](Lore_Snow_Elf.md) named [Vyrthur](Lore_Vyrthur.md) foretold that *"The Blood of Coldharbour's Daughter will blind the eye of the Dragon."* This referred to the [Tyranny of the Sun](Lore_Tyranny_of_the_Sun.md), an ancient prophecy that foretells the eternal beclouding of the sun. This prophecy was later pursued by Lord [Harkon](Lore_Harkon.md), the progenitor of the [Volkihar](Lore_Volkihar.md) bloodline of vampires. He planned to sacrifice his daughter, [Serana](Lore_Serana.md), in order to fulfill the prophecy. Disagreeing with his vision, Harkon's wife [Valerica](Lore_Valerica.md) (also a Daughter of Coldharbour) took their daughter, Serana, and secretly fled far from his grasp, effectively putting an end to his plans.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Val-1)</sup>
