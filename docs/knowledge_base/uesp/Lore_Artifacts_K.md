## Kagrenac's Tools
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-item-Keening.jpg/200px-SR-item-Keening.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Keening.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Keening.jpg) Keening [Kagrenac's Tools](Lore_Kagrenac%27s_Tools.md) are the legendary [artifacts](Lore_Artifacts.md) crafted by the [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) Tonal Architect [Lord Kagrenac](Lore_Kagrenac.md). Each tool served a specific purpose to siphon the power of the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md), via [tonal manipulation](Lore_Tonal_Architecture.md) of the laws of nature.

Variants of the tools were created by the clockwork god [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) to manipulate the [Mechanical Heart](Lore_Mechanical_Heart.md).

**Found in:** - Morrowind ([Sunder](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Sunder), [Keening](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Keening), [Wraithguard](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Wraithguard)), Skyrim ([Sunder](Skyrim_Sunder.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md)</sup>, [Keening](Skyrim_Keening.md), [Wraithguard](Skyrim_Wraithguard.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Sunder_%26_Wraithguard.md)</sup>), Legends ([Sunder II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Sunder_II), [Keening II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Keening_II), [Wraithguard II](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Legends:Wraithguard_II))

## Kahvozein's Fang
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Kahvozien%27s Fang.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-weapon-Kahvozien%27s Fang.png) Kahvozein's Fang [Kahvozein's Fang](Lore_Kahvozein%27s_Fang.md) is a [Dragon Priest](Lore_Dragon_Priest.md) dagger that dates back to the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md). It is legendary throughout [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and is known to be sharp enough to make precise carvings from the [scales](Lore_Dragon_Scales.md) of a [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md). It was created by a particular sect of the [Dragon Cult](Lore_Dragon_Cult.md), one that worshipped the eponymous Kahvozein. They were very fond of ritual sacrifices, so much so that the high priest spent much time sharpening this dagger so that it was always ready for its next victim.

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Kahvozein%27s_Fang.md)

## Keystones
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/eb/ON-quest-The_Keystone.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Keystone.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Keystone.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Keystone.jpg) A keystone [Keystones](Lore_Keystones.md) are magical stones of significant power commonly associated with the [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) and [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md). They are primarily found in the ruins of the [Barsaebic Ayleids](Lore_Barsaebic_Ayleids.md) in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md). The keystones were created in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) using the magic of the ancient [Argonians](Lore_Argonian.md), which the Ayleid slavemasters infused in the stones through torment and fear. As such, the keystones exert power over the [Hist](Lore_Hist.md). There were also used in powerful Ayleid enchantments, such as magical barriers.

Modern research of the keystones is limited by the dangerous nature of the relics. Only certain individuals can handle the keystones without suffering from nervous breakdowns. The [Kothringi](Lore_Kothringi.md) in particular could handle them for any length of time.

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Shadowfen)

## King Orgnum's Coffer
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0e/AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png/200px-AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:AR-item-Treasure_Chest.png) King Orgnum's Coffer [King Orgnum's Coffer](Lore_King_Orgnum%27s_Coffer.md) is a legendary [magical](Lore_Magic.md) chest, small and ordinary-looking but weightless, once owned by King [Orgnum](Lore_Orgnum.md) of [Pyandonea](Lore_Pyandonea.md). Once per day a random number of gold coins appear within the Coffer, though in Orgnum's possession it was an unlimited supply.

King Orgnum lost his Coffer in a battle against Emperor [Antiochus Septim](Lore_Antiochus.md) during the [War of the Isle](Lore_War_of_the_Isle.md). With the loss of this source of endless wealth, Orgnum could not afford a fleet as great as he had had, leading to Pyandonea losing much of its military power. It is said that when found the Coffer always disappears again, but not before producing enormous wealth for its owner. Where and why it vanishes is a mystery. The coffer washed up in the Summerset Isles and has become an elusive artifact ever since. It was last seen in the hands of the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md).

**Found in:** - [Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:King_Orgnum%27s_Coffer)
