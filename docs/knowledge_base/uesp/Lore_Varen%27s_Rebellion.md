# Colovian Revolt

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b7/ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Leovic.jpg/200px-ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Leovic.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Leovic.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-trailer-Deadlands_and_Damnation-Leovic.jpg) Leovic, slain by Varen The Colovian Revolt,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CRFY-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup> also known as **Varen's Rebellion**,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VW-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Cont Map-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Cont Banner-5)</sup> was a rebellion against Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md) of the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) by Duke [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) of [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md). Following Leovic's legalization of [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) worship throughout the [Empire of Cyrodiil](Lore_Empire_of_Cyrodiil_(Interregnum).md), Varen Aquilarios marshaled the military forces of the [Colovian Estates](Lore_Colovian_Estates.md) to depose him,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EFEV-6)</sup> triggering a widespread rebellion in [2E 576](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_576).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-PQOTGC-7)</sup> Varen based his rebellion out of [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md), where he coordinated his troops as they liberated Cyrodiil's other cities to further weaken Leovic's control.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-COTFC5-8)</sup> Despite his desire to fight on the frontlines, Varen instated his nephew [Carolus](Lore_Carolus_Aquilarios.md) to defend Kvatch, who repelled attacks from Longhouse-allied forces from [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and elsewhere.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TWat D-9)</sup> Meric Renmus pledged allegiance to Varen and was given the rank of general for his loyalty.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AR-2)</sup>

The rebellion claimed victory in [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577), when Varen's forces stormed the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and confronted Leovic's last legion in a bloody battle that razed the city's Market District.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GL-10)</sup> Varen personally dispatched Leovic at the foot of the Ruby Throne before proclaiming himself emperor.<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-COTFC4-11)</sup><sup>[[12]](#cite_note-COTFC1-12)</sup><sup>[[13]](#cite_note-SHot LE-13)</sup>

## Major Battles
- **Battle of Fort Ash**: Emperor Leovic's forces initially defeated Varen's forces at Fort Ash, but after taking command of the Legion of Chorrol, Varen defeated Leovic's forces, which caused the Emperor's forces to retreat into the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-EFEV-6)</sup>
- **Liberation of Leyawiin**: Leyawiin was allegedly single-handedly freed by [Sai Sahan](Lore_Sai_Sahan.md), who fought both Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md)'s forces and his own men who had been employed by Leovic as double agents.<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-Cot FC5-14)</sup> According to some other sources he was aided by allies, however.<sup>[[15]](#cite_note-SL-15)</sup>
- **Defense of Kvatch**: The Imperial Prefect of Anvil and [Ephrem Benirus](Lore_Ephrem_Benirus.md), the Count of Anvil, sent Imperial forces stationed in the coastal city inland to seize Varen Aquilarios's property and holdings. Carolus rallied the single cohort of the [Second Legion](Lore_Second_Legion.md) in his command and the Kvatch Warders, meeting the Imperial forces on the plains below the city. The battle was swift, and the greatly reduced forces of Anvil retreated back to their city.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TWat D-9)</sup>

## Aftermath
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e5/ON-npc-Emperor_Leovic.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Emperor_Leovic.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Emperor_Leovic.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Emperor_Leovic.jpg) Leovic's rotting corpse upon a throne of bones Varen discarded Leovic's corpse in the crypts beneath the Imperial Sewers;<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-GL-10)</sup><sup>[[16]](#cite_note-LA:IS-16)</sup> in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the corpse was discovered and reanimated first as a [zombie](Lore_Zombie.md) and then as a [flesh atronach](Lore_Flesh_Atronach.md) by a trio of [Worm Cultists](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md), shortly before being destroyed by soldiers from the [Alliance War](Lore_Alliance_War.md).<sup>[[17]](#cite_note-ESO-17)</sup>

When Leovic was overthrown, the remaining Reachmen retreated from Cyrodiil.<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-RDM-18)</sup> His cousin<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-STot DF-19)</sup> [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md) set aside his Imperial title and took instead the old Reach title of ard, and purged all surviving Blackdrakes, securing his hold on Markarth against his rivals.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-HOMASIS-20)</sup> Months after the disappearance of the Empress Regent [Clivia Tharn](Lore_Clivia_Tharn.md), Councilor [Itinia](Lore_Itinia.md) sent Helvar Tarperian to Ard Caddach with the proposal to instate him as emperor. Caddach believed her true intentions were to use him as a puppet to do her bidding and declined her offer, stating that the only situation in which he would go to Cyrodiil would be with a Reach horde.<sup>[[21]](#cite_note-Mt C:AC-21)</sup>
