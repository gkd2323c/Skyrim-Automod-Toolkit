- *For 60 seconds, opponents in melee range take 8 points fire damage per second. Targets on fire take extra damage.*

**Flame Cloak** is an adept level [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell that surrounds the caster in flames, causing [fire damage](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) to any enemies within melee range and setting them on fire, dealing 30% of the immediate spell damage, scaling down quadratically over 2 seconds.

## Effects
- [Flame Cloak](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md), 8 points damage in 10 feet for 60 seconds.
- [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md): [Resist Freezing Water](Skyrim_Cold.md)

## Perks
- [Augmented Flames](Skyrim_Augmented_Flames.md), raises damage to 10 at first rank or 12 at second rank.

## Notes
- You can only have one Cloak spell of any kind active at a time. Casting a second will cancel the first.
- It is a 'smart' spell and does not damage NPCs or followers, unlike [J'zargo's flame cloak scrolls](Skyrim_J%27zargo%27s_Flame_Cloak_Scroll.md).
- Dual casting significantly increases its range (4.84 times the covered area), but does not increase damage.
- If you are a [Dunmer](Skyrim_Dunmer.md), your [Ancestor's Wrath](Skyrim_Ancestor%27s_Wrath.md) power will function identically to this spell with the same base damage. Unfortunately, unlike Flame Cloak, Ancestor's Wrath can only be used once a day.
- The spell will continue to the end of its duration even if you sheathe your weapons. It even operates underwater, making it an effective means of dealing with [Slaughterfish](Skyrim_Slaughterfish.md), since you can't use other attacks or spells while swimming.
- One strategy for using this spell is to cast it and then simply run in tight circles around your enemies. They may be unable to hit you, and the fire will slowly take them out.
- When [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, the cloak allows swimming in freezing water without taking damage or suffering additional [cold](Skyrim_Cold.md) (it does not protect from Cold outside water).
- [Estormo](Skyrim_Estormo.md) and [Pyromancers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pyromancer) are known to use this spell in combat.
- Potions of [Fortify Destruction](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) increase both range and damage.
