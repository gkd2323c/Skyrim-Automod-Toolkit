## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/ON-npc-Sotha_Sil.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Sotha_Sil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Sotha_Sil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Sotha_Sil.jpg) Sotha Sil, Farena's mentor [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/ON-quest-Divine_Conundrum.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Divine_Conundrum.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Divine_Conundrum.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Divine_Conundrum.jpg) Farena's spirit summoned by her ancestor Little is known of Farena's past. She was born a Chimer, within House Redoran,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAT-1)</sup> at some point before the [War of the First Council](Lore_War_of_the_First_Council.md) and [Battle of Red Mountain](Lore_Battle_of_Red_Mountain.md). She was a disciple and a close companion of [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), even before the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md)'s ascension to godhood, and was privy to certain secrets of the nature of the Tribunal's divinity, the [Heart of Lorkhan](Lore_Heart_of_Lorkhan.md), and Tribunal's enemies. She was also aware of [Sunna'rah](Lore_Sunna%27rah.md) and Seht's experiments with his godly powers.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Farena-5)</sup> Like most of the Chimer, she was changed by the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), became a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BOOK-6)</sup> and eventually died at some point before the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Farena-5)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup>

## Legacy
Farena's remains were interred in the Andrano Ancestral Tomb.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AAT-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DC-3)</sup> She was regarded as one of the most venerable and luminary ancestors of the family, occupying a prime branch upon their family tree.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the Tribunal god Vivec has tasked her descendant, Llevule Andrano with summoning her spirit in order to ask her several vital questions on his behalf.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-LA-2)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-LVQ-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-CV-8)</sup> Farena's answers, while cryptic, provided helpful insight, and helped Vivec during the crisis caused by [Barbas](Lore_Barbas.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Vivec-4)</sup>
