# Sword of the Moon Reiver

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d5/BS-item-Sword_of_the_Moon_Reiver.png/200px-BS-item-Sword_of_the_Moon_Reiver.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-item-Sword_of_the_Moon_Reiver.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-item-Sword_of_the_Moon_Reiver.png) Sword of the Moon Reiver The Sword of the Moon Reiver (also known as the **Broadsword of the Moon Reiver**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> or **Moon-Cursed Blade**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Campaigns-2)</sup> is a broadsword created from [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md)'s own substance. It is believed that no other weapon is capable of harming Mehrunes Dagon.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LD-3)</sup> During Dagon's invasion of the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), the sword was in the possession of Si'ess, his [Dark Seducer](Lore_Dark_Seducer.md) bodyguard and paramour. The [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) took the sword from her and used it to banish the Prince from [his own realm](Lore_Dagon%27s_Hunting_Lodge.md), resulting in the destruction of the Battlespire.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Battlespire-4)</sup>
