[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/84/MW-place-Foyada_Ashur-Dan.jpg/200px-MW-place-Foyada_Ashur-Dan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Ashur-Dan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Foyada_Ashur-Dan.jpg) Foyada Ashur-Dan Foyada Ashur-Dan is one of the several [foyadas](Lore_Foyada.md) of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md), the island district of [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The volcanic ravines (or "fire-rivers") are valleys formed by lava flows from [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), functioning as drainage basins for lava when the volcano is active, or alternatively forming pathways to travel on when dormant and devoid of lava.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MWGen F-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-MWAjira-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TPP-3)</sup> The foyada starts at the caldera of Red Mountain and travels southeast through the [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md), ending between Sanabi and Dulo Ancestral Tomb, though an opening is present on the east side near Zalkin-Sul Egg Mine.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-MWMap-4)</sup>

Odrosal and Yanemus Mine are found along the foyada.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MWOdrosal-5)</sup> Dunirai Caverns, Halit Mine, and Helas Ancestral Tomb are found between the [Foyada Esannudan](Lore_Foyada_Esannudan.md) and Ashur-Dan.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MWSujamma Dunirai-6)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-MWHrundi-7)</sup> Foyada Ashur-Dan was filled with lava at the time of the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-AMap-8)</sup> The foyada had dried up by the time of [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), and the [Ghostgate](Lore_Ghostgate.md) went through it.

## Notes
- "Ashur-Dan" is a name among male Ashlanders of the Zainab tribe.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-MWTrade Mission Zainab-9)</sup>
- Early concepts show that much of the far-reaching areas of Foyada Ashur-Dan were planned to instead be a series of large Sulphur pools called "the Great Scathes".<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-Gt V-10)</sup>
