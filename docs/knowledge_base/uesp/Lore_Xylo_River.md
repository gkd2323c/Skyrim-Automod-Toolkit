[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/08/ON-place-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg/200px-ON-place-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg) Xylo River Basin circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/85/ON-map-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg/200px-ON-map-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-map-Xylo_River_Basin.jpg) 2nd Era map The Xylo River is a waterway flowing partially along the traditional border between [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md). It begins in the [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md) region of Valenwood, and its mouth is at a location known as Shaasanath Point.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ESO-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Ctt F-2)</sup> The river has been known to switch hands between the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) and [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3SAB-3)</sup>

At last report in [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432), Elsweyr gained territory on the western bank of the river after the [Five Year War](Lore_Five_Year_War.md), and though a diplomatic attempt was made to return it, Khajiiti settlers in the area were refusing to move. The Empire thus decided to leave the situation as it was.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3SAB-3)</sup><sup>[[note 1]](#cite_note-4)</sup> The river purportedly maintains a fairly mild temperature even in wintertime, allowing for year-round swimming.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ADIF3-5)</sup>

## Notes
1. [^](#cite_ref-4) It is possible that the Xylo River was confused with the [Crescent River](Lore_Crescent_River.md) since it is closer to the Elsweyr border.

- The [Xylo pollen bat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Xylo_Pollen_Bat) pollinates flowers along the Xylo River.
