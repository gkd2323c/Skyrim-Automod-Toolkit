[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-npc-Tomb_Guardian.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tomb_Guardian.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tomb_Guardian.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tomb_Guardian.jpg) A Tomb Guardian **Tomb Guardians** are undead beings summoned by an Adept level [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell, [Conjure Tomb Guardian](Skyrim_Conjure_Tomb_Guardian.md) added by the [Necromantic Grimoire](Skyrim_Necromantic_Grimoire.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md).

As a ghost they are immune to [poison](Skyrim_Poison.md) and [disease](Skyrim_Disease.md), and has 25% resistance to [frost](Skyrim_Frost.md). They can also [walk on water](Skyrim_Water_Walking.md). On their left hand they can use the [Frostbite](Skyrim_Frostbite.md) spell to deal health and stamina damage. They also have the [Light Foot](Skyrim_Light_Foot.md) perk, [Barbarian](Skyrim_Two-handed.md#Barbarian) perk to rank 2, [Devastating Blow](Skyrim_Devastating_Blow.md), [Champion's Stance](Skyrim_Champion%27s_Stance.md), [Agile Defender](Skyrim_Agile_Defender.md) rank 1, and [Limbsplitter](Skyrim_Limbsplitter.md) rank 1.

They are equipped with a [Headsman's Axe](Skyrim_Headsman%27s_Axe.md) for combat. They wear a full set of Executioner clothing, including [Boots](Skyrim_Boots.md), [Robes](Skyrim_Executioner%27s_Robes.md), [Gloves](Skyrim_Gloves.md), and a [Hood](Skyrim_Executioner%27s_Hood.md).
