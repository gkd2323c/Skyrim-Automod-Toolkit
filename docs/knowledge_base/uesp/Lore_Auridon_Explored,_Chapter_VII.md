[Auridon Explored](Lore_Auridon_Explored.md), Chapter VII **by [Fenlil the Wayfarer](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fenlil_the_Wayfarer)** *A fragmented travel guidebook covering the isle of Auridon*
---
The ruin known by locals as [Hightide Keep](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hightide_Keep) was once a sprawling complex of defensive fortifications. A relic from the time of the [Ancients](Lore_Ancients.md), it was painstakingly maintained and manned.

As a result, [Skywatch](Lore_Skywatch.md) was able to put up an incredible defense during the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md) attack on the city. Powerful magical constructs were crafted and expended. Dozens of Sload warships were destroyed.

The city finally fell when a Sload Warcaster filled his voluminous gullet with volatile [alchemical](Lore_Alchemy.md) reagents and threw himself at the base of the cliffs. The resulting explosion toppled much of the extended rock face, and plunged the ruins into the sea.
