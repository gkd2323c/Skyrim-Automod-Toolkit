# Blessed Life-Tree


Reliefs of the life-tree are commonly found in [Ayleid ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ayleid_Ruins). Some who return from endeavors into these ruins return with the markings of the blessed life-tree on their face,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BLTFace-3)</sup> seemingly by gaining favor from one of their spirits. It is believed that having these markings can furthermore win the favor of lingering ghosts.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BLTBody-2)</sup> [Bosmeri](Lore_Bosmer.md) followers of the [Dawnway](Lore_Dawnway.md) also make use of the life-tree as a symbol of their Ayleid ancestry.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
