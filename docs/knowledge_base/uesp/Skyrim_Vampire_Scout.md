[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/SR-npc-Vampire_Scout.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Vampire_Scout.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Scout.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Vampire_Scout.jpg) Vampire Scout The **Vampire Scout** is a radiant [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) enemy found sneaking around the outskirts of [Fort Dawnguard](Skyrim_Fort_Dawnguard.md). They are always hostile and will attack in groups of two or three. However, they don't appear at all if you are a member of the [Harkon's](Skyrim_Harkon.md) [court](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md).

## Notes
- Since the Vampire Scouts appear near the entrance of [Dayspring Canyon](Skyrim_Dayspring_Canyon.md), you won't see them if you fast travel to Fort Dawnguard.
- After completing quest [Kindred Judgment](Skyrim_Kindred_Judgment.md) on Dawnguard side, they disappear permanently from game, after [Harkon's](Skyrim_Harkon.md) [court](Skyrim_Volkihar_Vampire_Clan.md) lost the war against to [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md).
