[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/49/ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg/200px-ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Fallowstone_Hall.jpg) Fallowstone Hall Dukaanfinsot was a [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name meant "Dishonor-The-White" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-3)</sup>

His enormous skull was a trophy from the time of the [Dragon War](Lore_Dragon_War.md). As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) his skull was displayed in [Fallowstone Hall](Lore_Fallowstone_Hall.md) alongside other items of importance for either the Five Hundred [Companions](Lore_Companions.md) or the Fallowstone Companions.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FH-4)</sup>

It is unknown whether Dukaanfinsot's soul was devoured by another dovah or not, and as such it is unknown whether or not Dukaanfinsot was killed permanently.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-LAD-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Arngeir-6)</sup>
