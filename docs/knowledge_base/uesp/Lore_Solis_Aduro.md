# Solis Aduro

| Solis Aduro | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Male<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-AYP-1)</sup> |
| Resided in | [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) | | |

Solis Aduro was a researcher and author known for translating or transcribing [Argonian](Lore_Argonian.md) myths and legends,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cot R-2)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-4)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup> as well as publishing the enigmatic *[Lost Tales of the Famed Explorer](Lore_Lost_Tales_of_the_Famed_Explorer.md)* series.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lost Tales-7)</sup> One of the Argonian myths he published, known as *[Children of the Root](Lore_Children_of_the_Root.md)*, he collected from an oral tradition of the [Adzi-Kostleel](Lore_Adzi-Kostleel.md) tribe, though it is not otherwise attested.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Cot R-2)</sup>

According to one account, he was one of the [Imperial Mananauts](Lore_Royal_Imperial_Mananauts.md) and a long-time friend of the [time traveller](Lore_Time_Magic.md) Thaddeus Cosma, whom they had seen many amazing things with. It is said these experiences is what enabled him to have the knowledge to write the strange ending of the *Lost Tales of the Famed Explorer* series, despite not being Matius (the central character) himself. This claim also alleges Solis was the person the character Salara was looking for,<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-AYP-1)</sup> and why she rushed for the large [moth](Lore_Moth.md) -like [object](Lore_Air_Transportation.md#Aetherial_Vessels) that appeared to have crashed into the swamp.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Lost Tales-7)</sup>
