[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/44/SR-creature-The_Forgemaster.jpg/200px-SR-creature-The_Forgemaster.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Forgemaster.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-The_Forgemaster.jpg) The Forgemaster **The Forgemaster** is a powerful [Dwarven Centurion](Skyrim_Dwarven_Centurion.md) that guards the [Aetherium Forge](Skyrim_The_Aetherium_Forge.md). The Forgemaster is nearly twice as large as normal Centurions, appearing partially molten due to its long slumber in the lava, and breathes a gout of fire, much like [dragons](Skyrim_Dragon.md) instead of steam. It has a minimum level of 24, so lower level characters will want to take extra care. Unlike other [Dwarven automatons](Skyrim_Dwarven_Automatons.md), which are immune to [frost](Skyrim_Frost_Damage.md), The Forgemaster is instead immune to [fire](Skyrim_Fire_Damage.md) and has a 50% weakness to frost. This makes it the only Dwarven automaton with any weakness whatsoever.

## Related Quests
- **[Lost to the Ages](Skyrim_Lost_to_the_Ages.md)**: Search for the Dwemer's [Aetherium Forge](Skyrim_The_Aetherium_Forge.md).

## Notes
- [Fire resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md) will reduce damage from The Forgemaster's "steam" attacks and from the fire hazards in the arena.
- Frost magic will deal extra damage to The Forgemaster, while it is completely immune to fire and has a 25% resistance to [shock](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md).
- If you have a melee follower, they may rush The Forgemaster before it leaves the lava - killing your follower almost instantly.
- If you have a high [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) skill, it is possible to sneak attack The Forgemaster before it steps out of the lava, causing it to fall backwards into the lava.
