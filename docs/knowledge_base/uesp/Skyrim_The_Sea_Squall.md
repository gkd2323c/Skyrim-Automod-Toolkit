## The Sea Squall
The boat is docked at the end of a jetty opposite the entrance to [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md). There are several crates and barrels on deck, along with three chairs that the captain and both sailors seem to spend most of their time sitting on.

## Notes
- Dawnstar guards can be seen traversing between the ship and the mine entrance.
