[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/98/OB-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg/200px-OB-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg) Lake Rumare circa [3E 433](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_433) [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/ON-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg/200px-ON-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Lake_Rumare.jpg) Lake Rumare covered in mist circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Lake Rumare is a large lake completely surrounding [City Isle](Lore_City_Isle.md) and the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). It is connected to the sea by the [Niben River](Lore_Niben_River.md), and feeds the [Upper Niben](Lore_Upper_Niben.md) to the south. It is primarily inhabited by a dangerous variety of [Slaughterfish](Lore_Slaughterfish.md) known as [Rumare Slaughterfish](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rumare_Slaughterfish).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> The lake also gives its name to the [Rumare Bream](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rumare_Bream), a type of fish native to Cyrodiil.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

Many phrases in the local vernacular refer to Lake Rumare, such as "throwing money into Lake Rumare" to save time instead of "visiting that dump (the Imperial City)".

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a veteran of the [Great War](Lore_Great_War.md) recounts that *"Their fires lit up the night. All across Lake Rumare. Like stars come to earth. It was beautiful, really."* No further details are given as to precisely what this means, but it can be inferred that the [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) may have launched some sort of naval attack on the city.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SRSalvianus-3)</sup>
