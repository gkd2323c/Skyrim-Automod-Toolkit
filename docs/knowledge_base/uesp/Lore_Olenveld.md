# Olenveld

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b4/SR-npc-Ivara_of_Olenveld.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ivara_of_Olenveld.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivara_of_Olenveld.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ivara_of_Olenveld.jpg) A necromancer from Olenveld Olenveld is a settlement on an island off the coast of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), colloquially referred to as the **island of death**, that is a day's sail in favorable conditions from [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(city).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TE-1)</sup>

Olenveld was originally a significant city, although it was abandoned sometime in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md) used Olenveld as his own personal graveyard, lining its abandoned streets with gravestones. Eventually the island city was forgotten, and Olenveld disappeared from the maps.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TE-1)</sup>

After [Hannibal Traven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Hannibal_Traven) became the Arch-Mage of the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) circa [3E 431](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_431),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> he introduced a ban on [necromancy](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Necromancy) in the guild. Forced to go into hiding, a group of necromancers set out to find Olenveld and its plentiful supply of bodies. After spending a week searching for the island, they set up shop in the old Mages Guild building and became somewhat well-known for their ability to preserve sickly children by turning them undead.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TE-1)</sup>

Necromancers still inhabited Olenveld centuries into the [Fourth Era](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), two necromancers set out from Olenveld in order to open a portal to the [Soul Cairn](Lore_Soul_Cairn.md). They established themselves within [Brittleshin Pass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brittleshin_Pass) in mainland Skyrim, where they managed to acquire the [Staff of Hasedoki](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Staff_of_Hasedoki) via the local smugglers. They planned to release Hasedoki's soul from the staff to earn the favor of the [Ideal Masters](Lore_Ideal_Masters.md), although they were both killed by the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) before this could occur.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>
