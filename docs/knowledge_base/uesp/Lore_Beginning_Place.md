# Beginning Place

| Beginning Place | |
| --- | --- |
| Type | Unknown |
| Realm | [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) |

The **Beginning Place** is a location of cosmic and religious importance within [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Origin)</sup> It is the site at which the [Aurbis](Lore_Aurbis.md) was formed from the interplay of [Anu](Lore_Anu.md)'s stasis and [Padomay](Lore_Padomay.md)'s instability.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo F-2)</sup> Later, it was where the [et'Ada](Lore_Gods.md) crystalized into existence. [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md), the [Dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) God of Time, came into being first.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Vo F-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AIR-3)</sup> Following his example, the most powerful spirits formed,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-APAYQ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Gener A-5)</sup> including [Mephala](Lore_Mephala.md), [Arkay](Lore_Arkay.md), [Y'ffre](Lore_Y%27ffre.md), [Magnus](Lore_Magnus.md), and [Ruptga](Lore_Ruptga.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TM-1)</sup>

In a paraphrased retelling of the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) myth *[The Anuad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:The_Anuad)*,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TAA-6)</sup> there exists the **Limbo Rift of Void**. Anu and Padomay are said to have [crossed from it](Lore_Magical_Transportation.md) when they first arrived in the [Void](Lore_Void.md). Due to their interplay, [Nir](Lore_Nir.md) formed, drawing the love of both entities. However, Nir professed she only loved Anu, causing Padomay to beat her in anger. For this, Anu fought and defeated Padomay, banishing him back into the Limbo Rift. Shortly after, Nir gave birth to creation serving the role of origin and thus as a personification of the Beginning Place for the [twelve worlds](Lore_Kalpa.md) of [creation](Lore_Aetherius.md). After many ages, the alignment of the twelve worlds of Creation borne from Nir allowed Padomay to open the rift and return. Hating Creation, he shattered the twelve [planets](Lore_Planets.md), drawing Anu's attention. They entered combat once more, Anu again emerging the victor. However, as Anu combined the remnants of the twelve worlds into [one](Lore_Nirn.md), Padomay attacked him by surprise. In response, Anu dragged him through the Limbo Rift, sealing it behind them both, forever.<sup>[[TRL 1]](#cite_note-Mono2-7)</sup>

According to the teachings of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md), mighty, net-like [fires](Lore_Elements.md#Fire) exist in the Beginning Place. [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md) used them to bind [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) during their [marriage](Lore_Marriage.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Sermon12-8)</sup> Later, Vivec, [Sotha Sil](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md), and [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) gathered them to contain the [ash](Lore_Ash_Blight.md) of [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md) after its eruption.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Sermon36-9)</sup>
