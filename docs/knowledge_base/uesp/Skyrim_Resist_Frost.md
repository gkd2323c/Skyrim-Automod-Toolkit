*Alchemy description:* **Resist <mag>% of frost damage for <dur> seconds.**
*Enchanting description:* **Increases Frost Resistance by <mag>%.**

**Resist Frost** increases the target's resistance to all forms of [Frost Damage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Frost_Damage), including the secondary effects (such as [Slow](Skyrim_Slow.md)) it applies. Resist Frost 50% will halve any Frost Damage the target takes.

Similar to [Resist Fire](Skyrim_Resist_Fire.md), [Resist Shock](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Resist_Shock), and [Resist Magic](Skyrim_Resist_Magic.md), Resist Frost is capped at 85%. The cap only applies to you; [followers](Skyrim_Followers.md) and enemies with 100% resistance to a particular element (such as [atronachs](Skyrim_Daedra.md)) are truly immune.

Damage reduction from Resist Fire, Resist Frost, and Resist Shock is applied after damage reduction from Resist Magic is applied. For example, a 100-point Fire Damage spell would deal only 15 points of damage with Resist Magic 85%; Resist Fire 85% would then reduce the 15 points by a further 85% to 2.25 points of final damage, resulting in 97.75% total resistance.

## Enchanting
The following items use this effect. You can learn how to enchant custom items with Resist Frost if you find one of the following items and disenchant it:

- All varieties of Armor*, Boots, Necklaces, and Shields of *[Resist Frost](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Resist_Frost)*, *Waning Frost*, *Dwindling Frost*, *Frost Suppression*, *Frost Abatement*, and *Warmth*.
- [Helm of Yngol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Helm_of_Yngol)
- [Herebane's Fortress](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Herebane%27s_Fortress) **
- [Necklace of Frost Resist](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Necklace_of_Frost_Resist)

Artifacts and unique items that use the effect but cannot be disenchanted include:

## Notes
- Resist Frost enchantments are amplified when placed on [stalhrim](Skyrim_Stalhrim.md)<sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup> equipment.
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, [Khajiit](Skyrim_Khajiit.md), [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md), and [Nords](Skyrim_Nord.md) will be 4%, 6% and 10% resistant to environmental [Cold](Skyrim_Cold.md) (respectively). A Cold level of Warm (less than 50) will increase frost resistance by 10%.
