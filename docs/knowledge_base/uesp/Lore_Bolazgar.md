— *Chief Bolazgar to Kurog* Bolazgar gro-Bagrakh was the [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) chief of [Clan Bagrakh](Lore_Bagrakh_Clan.md) during the sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo KK-1)</sup>

## Biography
Bolazgar was said to have been the chief of Clan Bagrakh for quite some time, as he had grown old, weak, and obese in his lengthy reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo KK-1)</sup>

A few years prior to [King Ranser's War](Lore_Ranser%27s_War.md) in [2E 566](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_566), [Kurog gro-Bagrakh](Lore_Kurog_gro-Orsinium.md) returned to the clan Bagrakh settlement in [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md). When he arrived, Bolazgar was with four honor guards and asked Kurog if he had come to grovel and beg for his forgiveness. Bolazgar, shaking with rage, questioned Kurog if he truly believed he could beat him in fair combat.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo KK-1)</sup>

Kurog taunted the chief saying that he had grown fat and weak in his time away, and that he had instead been fighting wars in distant lands. Enraged by Kurog's remarks, Bolazgar charged at him with a scream of pure rage. Kurog stood his ground and calmly unsheathed his sword, and countered Bolazgar's clumsy attack with a block, and then decapitated Bolazgar, killing him.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo KK-1)</sup>

Bolazgar's head bounced thrice off the ground before stopping at the boots of one of his honor guards, and Kurog succeeded him as chief of Clan Bagrakh.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCo KK-1)</sup>
