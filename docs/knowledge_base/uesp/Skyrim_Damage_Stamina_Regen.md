## Notes
- Most [alcoholic drinks](Skyrim_Food.md#Beverages) will temporarily inhibit stamina regeneration. If too many are taken at once, it will cause a long period of no stamina regeneration, due to the effects stacking with each other.
- Depriving an enemy from regenerating stamina through a poison only guarantees that they won't be able to sprint, bash (with a weapon or shield) or perform power attacks; they can still jog and attack normally.
- The magnitude of damaged stamina regeneration is always 100%. This does not increase with Alchemy skill level, perks, or worn Fortify Alchemy enchanted apparel. What does increase, however, is the duration of the poison: how many seconds it lasts on an enemy.
- If you are a vampire, your stamina won't regenerate during the day when outdoors.
- If [Survival Mode](Skyrim_Survival_Mode.md) is active, higher [Fatigue](Skyrim_Fatigue.md) levels will cause your stamina to regenerate more slowly.
