[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cc/SR-npc-Traveler.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Traveler.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveler.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Traveler.jpg) Traveler The **Traveler** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [vampire](Skyrim_Vampire.md) you may [randomly encounter](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md) in cities or settlements throughout Skyrim if you have joined the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard_(faction).md). He wears [hooded black robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hooded_Black_Robes), [vampire boots](Skyrim_Vampire_Boots.md) (armor version), and [vampire gauntlets](Skyrim_Vampire_Gauntlets.md). When he dies, he drops [vampire dust](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vampire_Dust), as well as some various [potions](Skyrim_Potions.md). He is initially unaggressive when you encounter him, but says *"Die Dawnguard!"* and turns hostile when you get close. He attacks using various magic spells, including [Summon Gargoyle](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Summon_Gargoyle).

## Notes
- The Traveler is the only vampiric NPC that wears the vampire gauntlets. No other NPC (named or random) wears them.
- He is also the only character to use the Summon Gargoyle spell, besides [Harkon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Harkon) in his Vampire Lord form.
- His editor ID `DLC1Radiant Disguised Vampire Lord` (among other data) implies that he was intended to turn into a Vampire Lord when attacking, although this isn't the case in the final game.
