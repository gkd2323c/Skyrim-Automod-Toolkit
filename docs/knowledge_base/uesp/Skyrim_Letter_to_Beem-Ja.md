Letter to Beem-Ja *A letter from a concerned father exhorting Beem-Ja to take care of his daughter Salma*
---
Beem-Ja-
Keep [my daughter](Skyrim_Salma.md) safe and you'll earn both your freedom and that damned book you want so badly. But by the [Divines](Lore_Nine_Divines.md), if any harm comes to her, I'll make sure everyone I do business with will hear about what you did in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).
