From the records of Kahanad al-Hegathe **by [Kahanad al-Hegathe](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Kahanad_al-Hegathe)** *A warrior's notes on his trials in Betnikh*
---
[![ON-concept-Betnikh 02.png](https://images.uesp.net/f/fd/ON-concept-Betnikh_02.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Betnikh_02.png)
- My sojourn has only just begun, and already trials present themselves to my blade. The merchant ship I travel with took to port at the island of [Betnikh](Lore_Betnikh.md) after a fierce storm damaged the rigging, and I hardly set foot onto the docks when I heard of trouble with giant flying insects. Eager to challenge myself against a new foe, I set off right away into the woods to seek them and aid the people of this island. This land seems much softer than what I am used to in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), with its lush forest and cool breezes, but I must remember my training and stay on my guard. This will be but the first of many challenges. If I truly wish to master the ways of the blade, I must study, practice, and seek new foes to test myself against, just as [Frandar Hunding](Lore_Frandar_Hunding.md) teaches us.
