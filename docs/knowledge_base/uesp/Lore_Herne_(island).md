[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png/200px-RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-map-West_Tamriel-1024x768.png) A map showing the island of Herne Herne is an island off the west coast of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), north of [Cespar](Lore_Cespar.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> It is most notable for acting as the temporary refuge for the [Yokudan](Lore_Yokuda.md) nobility, known as the [Na-Totambu](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Na-Totambu), during the [Ra Gada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ra_Gada) exodus to [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) in the early [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1H-2)</sup>

Although some sources claim Herne was uninhabited before their arrival,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1H-2)</sup> others claim that it was inhabited by a tribe of [Nedes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Nede), led by a warchief named Haakon. The Nedes had successfully repulsed many attacks by elves prior to the arrival of the Yokudans, but the Ra Gada ultimately exterminated them.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONTKF-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONMarch-4)</sup>

After the loss of [Eyevea](Lore_Eyevea.md) to the [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md) considered bringing Herne to the [Shivering Isles](Lore_Shivering_Isles.md), but it was not the right shape.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-CHAYQ-5)</sup> The island was considered by the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) to be Hammerfell's territory in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-MCMap-6)</sup>
