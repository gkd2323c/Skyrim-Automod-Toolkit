[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ec/ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Senche.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Senche.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Senche.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Iron_Atronach_Senche.jpg) Iron Atronach Senche Maalanrie Direnni was one of the members of the influential [Altmeri](Lore_Altmer.md) clan [Direnni](Lore_Direnni.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Once-1)</sup> and the pioneer conjuration mage. She is accredited with the summoning of the first [Iron Atronach](Lore_Iron_Atronach.md) [Senche](Lore_Senche-Cat.md) in history.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IAS-2)</sup>

The exact details of this event are unknown, however, at some point in history before [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) she was captured by a group of bandits and imprisoned inside a cage. According to certain sources she "walked through dreams" and managed to call upon the mighty Atronach steed to free herself from captivity and destroy the cage. She then killed her captors and rode on its back to freedom.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IAS-2)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the art of conjuring Iron Atronachs shaped like senche was popular among conjuration mages.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IAS-2)</sup>
