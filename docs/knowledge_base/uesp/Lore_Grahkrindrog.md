Grahkrindrog is a high [dragon](Lore_Dragon.md) whose name means "Battle-Courageous-Lord" in the [Dragon Language](Lore_Dragon_Language.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TIL-1)</sup>

Grahkrindrog was slain by the [Dragonguard](Lore_Dragonguard.md) in [2E 184](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_184), after perpetrating great slaughter in [Winterhold](Lore_Winterhold_(region).md) and [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md) in northeast [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). The dragon's name was supposedly confirmed to the Dragonguard with assistance from mages of the [College of Winterhold](Lore_College_of_Winterhold.md). His death was later documented by the Dragonguard in [2E 373](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_373).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AOD-2)</sup> In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), he was resurrected by [Alduin](Lore_Alduin.md) and caused havoc across the Holds of Skyrim. The [Sheathed Blades](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md) chased down and defeated the colossal dragon, which was said to have plagued Skyrim for centuries in ancient times.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-3)</sup> Due to the nature of dragons, Grahkrindrog may not be truly dead and therefore could rise again.
