[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/be/SR-npc-Oriella.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Oriella.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oriella.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oriella.jpg) Oriella **Oriella** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bard](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) who can be hired to stay in [Heljarchen Hall](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md).

Oriella wears farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209A6) with a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209A5). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) in combat and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
After being hired, she will introduce herself to you:

- *"Hello, my name is Oriella, the bard that you asked your steward to hire. I hope my music will be pleasing to you."*

When rehired if you have dismissed her she will say:

- *"Good! I knew you couldn't have been serious when you dismissed me. Life without music is hardly worth living, is it?"*

## Bugs
- If you hire her before building the main room, she will spawn anyway and will be heard playing from the inaccessible room. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.0, fixes this bug.
- The dialogue option with your [steward](Skyrim_Personal_Steward.md) to hire Oriella may disappear if you purchase all other house improvements beforehand. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.2, fixes this bug.
- Oriella is mistakenly set as part of Crime Faction Falkreath rather than Crime Faction Pale.
