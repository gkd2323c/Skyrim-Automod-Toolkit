[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-npc-Baral_Sendu.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Baral_Sendu.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Baral_Sendu.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Baral_Sendu.jpg) Baral Sendu **Baral Sendu** is a [Redguard](Skyrim_Redguard.md) [farmer](Skyrim_Farmer_(class).md) found lying dead on his bed in [The Stumbling Sabrecat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Stumbling_Sabrecat), a tavern within [Fort Dunstad](Skyrim_Fort_Dunstad.md). He was presumably killed by the [bandits](Skyrim_Bandit.md) who have overrun the fort. He wears [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#0005b6a1) and a pair of [shoes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Shoes#0005b6a0) and carries a [lockpick](Skyrim_Lockpick.md) and up to nine gold.

His body and blood-stained bed are removed during the course of the related quest [The Battle for Fort Dunstad](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Battle_for_Fort_Dunstad).

## Bugs
- Baral has the `Female Commoner` voice type. This results in a female voice if the body is [resurrected](Skyrim_Reanimate.md). - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.5, addresses this issue. His voice is set to the `Male Commoner` voice type.
