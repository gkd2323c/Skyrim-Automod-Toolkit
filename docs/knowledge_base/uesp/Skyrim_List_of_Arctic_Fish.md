List of Arctic Fish **by [Swims-In-Deep-Water](Skyrim_Swims-In-Deep-Water.md)** *Various fish to catch in frozen waters*
---
The fish you have brought back are getting along swimmingly. Which makes sense, given they're fish. But I mean "swimmingly" in the way people say it. I suppose both could be true.

The next set of fish I will have you catch will be tough to chew. They swim in frozen waters, and their scales are as thick as ice.

Maybe your hook will be the one to melt their scales, and their hearts.

Here is the list:

Angler Larvae
Arctic Char
Arctic Grayling
Cod
