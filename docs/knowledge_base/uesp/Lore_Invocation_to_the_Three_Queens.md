Invocation to the Three Queens *A traditional invocation of the Three Queens* *
---
(A traditional invocation to the Three Queens honored by the [Corelanyans](Lore_Corelanya_Clan.md) of [Solstice](Lore_Solstice_(island).md) and [Sunporters](Lore_Sunport.md) of various cultures and backgrounds.)

All praise to the Three Queens, mightiest of the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), they who guide our steps and watch over us. All praise to [Meridia](Lore_Meridia.md), to [Azura](Lore_Azura.md), to [Nocturnal](Lore_Nocturnal.md)!

Praise to Azura, Queen of Dawn and Dusk, who protects us in the between places and helps us transition from the black of night to the light of day. Let her rule our hearts with wisdom and grant us success in all our ventures. May she guide us as we step from one shadow and plunge into another.

Azura, hear our prayers!

Praise to Meridia, Queen of the Day, who sees our potential, encourages our choices, and witnesses the truth within our [souls](Lore_Soul.md). She guides us to the perfect state of Alaxon as her light fills the land. She is the Bright One, the Prince of Life, and the Matron of the [Colored Rooms](Lore_Colored_Rooms.md). May she shine eternally and illuminate us forever!

Meridia, hear our prayers!

Praise to Nocturnal, Queen of the Evening, who guards the night and those who pass through it. She is mystery, she is luck. She pushes us to do better, to strive harder, to walk where others dare to tread. She is unknowable, yet she knows us all too well.

Nocturnal, hear our prayers!

Three Queens, hear our prayers!
