[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8d/SR-npc-Daynas_Valen.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Daynas_Valen.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daynas_Valen.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Daynas_Valen.jpg) Daynas Valen **Daynas Valen** was a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [conjurer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Mage Conjurer) who came to Skyrim in search of the [Gauldur Amulet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Gauldur_Amulet). He first heard of the Legend of Gauldur and the Gauldur Amulet 100 years ago, and since then he had felt its power calling to him. He became so obsessed that he dedicated his life into researching the life of [Gauldur](Skyrim_Gauldur.md) and his three sons [Jyrik](Skyrim_Jyrik_Gauldurson.md), [Sigdis](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sigdis_Gauldurson), and [Mikrul](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mikrul_Gauldurson).

After learning of the final resting place of the sons and fragments of the Gauldur Amulet, he began his first expedition into [Folgunthur](Skyrim_Folgunthur.md). However, he was unable to gain entrance without the [ivory dragon claw](Skyrim_Ivory_Dragon_Claw.md) and the expedition was put on hold. After ten years of searching, he traced the location of the ivory dragon claw to a collector in the city of [Bravil](Lore_Bravil.md) in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md), who he then killed and stole the ivory dragon claw from. He then fled to [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and boarded the [Icerunner](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Icerunner) to [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md).

On the Icerunner's three week voyage from Anvil to Solitude, he met an [adventurer](Skyrim_Adventurer.md) who was going to seek his fortune in the ruins of Skyrim with his companions, and invited them on his expedition intending to use them as fodder for the traps and [draugr](Skyrim_Draugr.md) within the tombs. Unfortunately, things didn't go according to plan, and he died along with the adventurers in Folgunthur. In his possession you can find the ivory dragon claw and [his notes](Skyrim_Daynas_Valen%27s_Notes.md).
