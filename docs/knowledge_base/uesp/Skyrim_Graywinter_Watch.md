## Walkthrough

### Exterior
The cave, which is beneath the [Ritual Stone](Skyrim_The_Ritual_Stone.md) overlooking the [White River](Skyrim_White_River.md), is easily reachable from the nearby road. Along the west side of the Ritual Stone's rock foundation is a dirt path which leads directly to the cave.

### Graywinter Watch
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/73/SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Graywinter_Watch.jpg) Froki's Bow, with a nearby dead bandit There are two [trolls](Skyrim_Troll.md) in the cave; one is visible from the tunnel, and the other is hidden near the campfire. Six [blisterwort](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Blisterwort), three [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap), and four [bleeding crown](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Bleeding_Crown) fungi grow throughout the cave. To the right of the entrance is a ramp to the higher part of the cave.

At the top of the ramp is a row of nine skulls. There is a small campsite here, with a central campfire and the corpses of a [female Nord bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md#Nord_Female) and a [male Nord bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(dead).md#Nord_Male). There are two bedrolls on one side of the fire, and a tent on the other. To the right of the tent is a copy of the [Archery](Skyrim_Archery.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Vernaccus and Bourlor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Vernaccus_and_Bourlor)*, three [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow), a pile or quiver of random [arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Arrows), and the respawning [Froki's bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Froki%27s_Bow) atop a food barrel. Lastly, by the bedrolls is a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack).

## Notes
- There are fifteen humanoid skulls in the cave, though only twelve can be lifted.
- Two [corundum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Corundum_Ore_Vein) are north of the cave, across the river next to a rocky outcropping and a tree.
- With version 1.3.2 of the [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), Froki's Bow will only spawn here once.
