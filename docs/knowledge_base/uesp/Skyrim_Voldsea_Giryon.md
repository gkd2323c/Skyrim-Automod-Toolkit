[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/SR-npc-Voldsea_Giryon.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Voldsea_Giryon.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voldsea_Giryon.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Voldsea_Giryon.jpg) Voldsea Giryon **Voldsea Giryon** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [rogue](Skyrim_Rogue.md) and a sailor of [The North Wind](Skyrim_The_North_Wind.md), serving under captain [Kjar](Skyrim_Kjar.md). Since the ship is currently laying over in [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md), she takes the opportunity to walk around the city as much as she can.

She spends her time patrolling on the North Wind from 4pm to 4am, and at other times can be found relaxing in [Candlehearth Hall](Skyrim_Candlehearth_Hall.md).

Voldsea wears a set of [fine clothes](Skyrim_Fine_Clothes.md#000f8715) along with a pair of [fine boots](Skyrim_Fine_Boots.md#000cee82). She wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and also carries a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue

- *"It's good to spend some time on solid land."*
- *"A life on the river makes for weak legs, so I do a lot of walking when we dock."*
- *"Captain Kjar expects the best, but he's a fair man. He never fails to reward hard work and loyalty."*

## Notes
- An NPC by the name of **Voldsea Giryon** was originally meant to appear in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* as a resident of [Chorrol](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Chorrol). They were cut from the final game, however. - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)'s *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_IV:_Oblivion_Official_Game_Guide)* also mentions "Voldsea Giryon" under the female Dark Elf "Sample Names For Tamriel's Races".
