[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/ON-item-Nuzava%27s_Anvil_%28museum%29.jpg/200px-ON-item-Nuzava%27s_Anvil_%28museum%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Nuzava%27s_Anvil_(museum).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-item-Nuzava%27s_Anvil_(museum).jpg) Nuzava's Anvil **Forge-Wife** Nuzava was a highly respected [Orc](Lore_Orc.md) [smith](Lore_Smithing.md). She played a significant role in the [Morkul Clan](Lore_Morkul_Clan.md) as one of its prominent members, responsible for establishing their distinct style of crafting arms and armor. Nuzava was one of the most revered metalworkers of her time.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NA-2)</sup>

Her skills were so renowned that she had [a special travel anvil](Lore_Nuzava%27s_Anvil_(item).md) prepared for her, which was covered in intricate carvings that were rumored to be arcane runes. Despite her claims that her skills needed no magical enhancements, legend had it that any smith who tried to use Nuzava's anvil would suffer a work-related accident. It is uncertain whether the anvil was indeed imbued with magic, but any smith who tried to use it reportedly suffered work-related accidents, including broken hammers, shattered metal, and smashed fingers from ill-placed blows.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

Nuzava and her renowned travel anvil disappeared during her return journey from a long trip abroad. She had sent word from the Icy Shore of her imminent return, but she never arrived. Her disappearance has become a mystery and a legend among the Orcs.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-CLOS-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-NA-2)</sup>
