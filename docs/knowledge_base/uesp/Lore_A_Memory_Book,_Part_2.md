[A Memory Book](Lore_A_Memory_Book.md), Part 2 **by [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md)** *The recollections of a Daedric Demiprince*
---
*As Dictated to [Tutor Riparius](Lore_Tutor_Riparius.md) by [Fa-Nuit-Hen](Lore_Fa-Nuit-Hen.md)*

The [Baron Who Moves Like Heat Lightning](Lore_Barons_of_Move_Like_This.md)! Can you not picture him in your mind? He arcs from one cloud to another, following no predictable path, going where he will at a speed no eye can follow! There! Like that!

But no: I'm fooling myself. Those are only words, a recollection of a description of a vision. The vision itself … is lost to me. Intellectually, I know where and how we fought, who we defeated and when, but the memory of the actual events is foggy, slipping away into the dusk of the forgotten past.

I know, for example, that Heat Lightning fought beside [Seht](Lore_Sotha_Sil.md) and me when we battled the Borers from Within. I know that Seht and I dueled The Hinged Ones while Heat Lightning overcharged the Null Pistons until they cracked and burst. I know it happened. I just can't remember the event itself. Or my baron, who won the day for us all.

Help me, my Tutor. Teach me to remember the Baron Who Moves Like Heat Lightning. I am lonely for that part of me, and want to see it again. At least, that's how it feels.
