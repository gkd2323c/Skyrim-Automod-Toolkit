[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/ON-creature-Dragon_%28black%29.jpg/200px-ON-creature-Dragon_%28black%29.jpg)](File_ON-creature-Dragon_(black).jpg.md) [](File_ON-creature-Dragon_(black).jpg.md) A black dragon Ka Po' Tun (meaning **Tiger-Dragon's Empire**), originally called **Po Tun**, is a nation of [Akavir](Lore_Akavir.md). It is inhabited by a race of cat-folk also called the [Ka Po' Tun](Lore_Ka_Po%27_Tun_(race).md). They are ruled by [Tosh Raka](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Tosh_Raka), the divine Tiger-Dragon who bears similarities to [Akatosh](Lore_Akatosh.md). Ka Po' Tun has become a very great empire, stronger than [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(place).md) in all but naval battle. When the [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) attempted to enslave the [dragons](Lore_Dragon.md), the black dragons fled to Po' Tun. A great war was waged, which left both the cat-folk and the snakes weak, and resulted in the death of the dragons. Since that time, the cat-folk have tried to become the dragons. Tosh Raka was the first to succeed, and renamed the nation to Ka Po' Tun. Ka Po' Tun plans to kill the Tsaesci and then invade [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MA-1)</sup>
