*Voruse Bethrimo, fought against the terrible creatures of Dagoth Ur, but was slain while protecting those under his command as they retreated. He carried the Crosier of St. Llothis, a holy artifact which is now in the hands of our enemies.*
—Uvoo Llaren<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup> Voruse Bethrimo was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) captain of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). He was also the wielder of [Crosier of St. Llothis](Lore_Crosier_of_St._Llothis.md) and an Adept of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VB-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/02/MW-place-Dagoth_Ur_03.jpg/200px-MW-place-Dagoth_Ur_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Dagoth_Ur_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Dagoth_Ur_03.jpg) Dagoth Ur Little is known about Voruse Bethimo's past. Being an Armiger, it was likely that he hailed from the [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SOV-3)</sup>

At some point, he became an Adept of the Tribunal Temple and rose to the rank of captain of the Buoyant Armigers. He was also honored to wield the Crosier of St. Llothis the Pious, a sacred relic of the Tribunal Temple.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-VB-2)</sup>

At some point in [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) he led the expedition to the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md). He fought against the [Ash Creatures](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ash_Creatures) serving [Dagoth Ur](Lore_Dagoth_Ur.md), and died within the Inner Tower of Dagoth Ur safeguarding his followers as they fell back. Uvoo Llaren, a Master within the ranks of the Temple, asked the [Nerevarine](Lore_Nerevarine.md) to recover the crosier from their enemies and return it to her. They discovered body of captain Bethrimo and brought the relic back to its rightful place.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-UL-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-COSLTP-4)</sup>
