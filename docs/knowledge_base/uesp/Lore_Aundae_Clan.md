# Aundae Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/cb/LG-cardart-Aundae_Clan_Sorcerer.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Aundae_Clan_Sorcerer.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Aundae_Clan_Sorcerer.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Aundae_Clan_Sorcerer.jpg) Dhaunayne Aundae, matriarch of the Aundae Clan The Aundae Clan is one of the three major [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodlines found on the island of [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md). The clan consists only of [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md) vampires. The Mages of Aundae are blessed with dark powers of the mind; their path through the darkness relies on their mastery of magic.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Generic Dialogue A-1)</sup> They were led by [Dhaunayne Aundae](Lore_Dhaunayne_Aundae.md) from their headquarters in [Ashmelech](Lore_Ashmelech.md) in the [Sheogorad](Lore_Sheogorad.md) region. The clan also had a presence in the many ancestral tombs around the island. There are hints that the clan has some affiliation with [House Dres](Lore_House_Dres.md), though House Dres denies it vehemently and no proof either way exists.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-AGa D-2)</sup> They are native to Vvardenfell and are rarely ever encountered on the mainland.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-Schick2015-3)</sup>

## History
Around the time of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the Aundae Clan was making aggressive moves to reclaim locations important to their vampire heritage. During an attack on [Bal Ur](Lore_Bal_Ur.md), a [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) dedicated to [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md), they massacred an entire squadron of [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) and captured its champion, [Galur Rithari](Lore_Galur_Rithari.md). The Aundae feasted on Rithari's blood then turned him into a vampire who would come to serve the clan. Another Daedric shrine to Molag Bal of interest to the Aundae Clan was [Ashalmawia](Lore_Ashalmawia.md), which the [Worm Cult](Lore_Order_of_the_Black_Worm.md) controlled at the time.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OJOGR-4)</sup>

In [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), vampires of the [Berne](Lore_Berne_Clan.md) and [Quarra](Lore_Quarra_Clan.md) clans killed many Aundae clan members.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-6)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Garan Marethi, a Dres member of the [Volkihar Clan](Lore_Volkihar.md) for many centuries, claimed to have links to the Aundae clan from Morrowind, though no one had ever confirmed them.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PRIMA-7)</sup><sup>:510</sup>

## Notes
- The novel *[Surfeit of Thieves](Lore_Surfeit_of_Thieves.md)* features a group known as the Order of St. Eadnua. Its members were known for spelling their names backward and feasting on people. Eadnua is Aundae spelled backward.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SOT-8)</sup>
