Comments of Maxence, banker **by [Maxence](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Maxence)** *A banker's thoughts on the prosperity the Daggerfall Covenant has brought*
---
[![ON-concept-Banking.png](https://images.uesp.net/1/18/ON-concept-Banking.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Banking.png)
- "Oh, I'd say [the Covenant](Lore_Daggerfall_Covenant.md) has been a positive force for trade overall. Goods from [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md) are enjoying a broader market in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) than ever, and it has become popular amongst nobles to import certain luxuries like tapestries and embroidered throw pillows. And everyone's gotten a taste for that delicious pomegranate wine.

- Those who previously had close trading ties to [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) are suffering, of course. Not only has trade with the [Nords](Lore_Nord.md) been restricted severely, but demand for their goods has decreased due to the enmity between us. I've seen plenty of traders who couldn't adapt quickly get put right out of business. On the whole, though, High Rock's prosperity is assured and increasing, as it ever is. Wealth finds its way here."
