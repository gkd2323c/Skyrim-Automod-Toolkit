[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f5/SR-creature-Frost_Giant.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Frost_Giant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frost_Giant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Frost_Giant.jpg) Frost Giant **Frost Giants** are found inhabiting the [Forgotten Vale](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md). They are added by the [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) [add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md). There are five of them, each holding a different [Paragon](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Paragon_Platform). They do not respawn, and are found in fixed locations.

Frost giants are much stronger than the normal variety of [giants](Skyrim_Giant.md) as they have more than double the health, and can cause considerably more melee damage than normal giants. If you plan to fight frost giants, keep in mind that they are completely immune to any form of frost magic, but have a significant weakness to fire.

## Locations
- The frost giant ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 00C0EF) holding the [Amethyst Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Amethyst_Paragon) is north of the Wayshrine of Learning, up the river along the eastern river bed.
- The frost giant ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 00F7A4) holding the [Diamond Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Diamond_Paragon) is just outside the [Glacial Crevice](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Glacial_Crevice) at the end of the path to the north.
- The frost giant ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 018D42) holding the [Emerald Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Emerald_Paragon) is in the [Falmer](Skyrim_Falmer.md) village. The giant is behind a gate high up in the village along the main path.
- The frost giant ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 012631) holding the [Ruby Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ruby_Paragon) is in the [Inner Sanctum](Skyrim_Inner_Sanctum_(Vale).md). The giant is in a passage only accessed by placing the [Initiate's Ewer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Initiate%27s_Ewer) on a small altar on the western wall in the first room. It will be at the end of the passage.
- The frost giant ([xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 00E216) holding the [Sapphire Paragon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sapphire_Paragon) is north of the giant holding the Amethyst Paragon. It is up the steps that lead up the mountain. Turn right from the halfway point up the steps and the giant is across the river.

The locations of each exterior frost giant are also marked on [this map](Skyrim_Forgotten_Vale.md#Map).

## Notes
- The [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md) expansion adds the spirit of another Frost Giant, [Karstaag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karstaag).
- The [Wabbajack](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) exclusively deals elemental damage to Frost Giants, and cannot be used to transmogrify or instantly kill them.
- Despite frost giants being completely different creatures, they still drop a standard Giant's Toe on death. They also share the same attack and threat animations (the stomp, club slam, the shaking their clubs to threaten you away, etc.) as standard giants.
