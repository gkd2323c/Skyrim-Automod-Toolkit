Mounts of Tamriel: Volume 2, Wolves and Foxes **by [Fadus Falto](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Fadus_Falto) of Skingrad** *Account of use of foxes and wolves as mounts* *
---
The perspective of the city dweller holds that the [dog](Lore_Dog.md) is a loyal pet, a faithful companion sitting by the fire. Their wild relatives, meanwhile, are [chicken](Lore_Chicken.md) -eating nuisances. Perhaps even blood-thirsty beasts, if the winter gets long and cruel.

But to the resilient folk who live beyond city walls and travel long distances through the rugged frontier of the north, the largest dogs, [wolves](Lore_Wolf.md), and [foxes](Lore_Fox.md) make for boon companions and sturdy mounts. Is there any wonder? These beasts make for sources of warmth in even the cruelest blizzard, fiercely loyal protectors, and impressive hunters when food is scarce. I once met a mount breeder from [Bleackrock Isle](Lore_Bleakrock_Isle.md) who proudly showed me his beautiful hounds, carefully tended over generations till they reached the size of a [Wood Elf](Lore_Wood_Elf.md)!

I had the pleasure of riding one across the icy beaches of their home isle and it was a moving, if freezing, experience!
