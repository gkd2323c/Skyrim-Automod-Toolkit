# Imperial Library

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/a8/OBR-interior-Imperial_Palace_Elder_Scrolls_Library_02.jpg/200px-OBR-interior-Imperial_Palace_Elder_Scrolls_Library_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-interior-Imperial_Palace_Elder_Scrolls_Library_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OBR-interior-Imperial_Palace_Elder_Scrolls_Library_02.jpg) The Imperial Library [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/DF-misc-Mage%27s_Tower.gif/200px-DF-misc-Mage%27s_Tower.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-misc-Mage%27s_Tower.gif) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-misc-Mage%27s_Tower.gif) Tower Laboratory The Imperial Library,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-EGT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TUH-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dex-4)</sup> also called the **Elder Scrolls Library**<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TES4ESL-5)</sup> and the **Hall of Records**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQV1-6)</sup> lies on the fifth floor of [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) and is said to be locked with a massive [ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) door.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TES4ESL-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQV1-6)</sup> The library hosts the legendary [Elder Scrolls](Lore_Elder_Scrolls.md), brought to the tower and catalogued by the [Moth Priests](Lore_Cult_of_the_Ancestor_Moth.md) who also live on the same floor.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQV1-6)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Dex-4)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AAOTS-7)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TES4ESL-5)</sup><sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-bookoftime-8)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> The library is considered holy by their order.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-9)</sup> Besides the Elder Scrolls themselves, the library's curators also organized the Empire's official documents and records in the library; thoroughly organizing them by year, province, and kingdom.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TWQV1-6)</sup>

The Imperial Library has an Office of Estranged Volumes,<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-10)</sup> and may also be associated with the [Office of the Unseeing Eye](Lore_Office_of_the_Unseeing_Eye.md) as well.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-IGC-11)</sup>
