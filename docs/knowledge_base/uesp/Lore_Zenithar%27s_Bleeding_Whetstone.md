# Zenithar's Bleeding Whetstone

*How quickly Zenithar's bleeding whetstone was forgotten. Or Mother Mild's sickle of absolution. It wasn't always crops that Mara harvested.
A single glass is a drop in the bucket of bloody traditions, I assure you.*
— [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup> Zenithar's Bleeding Whetstone is a lesser known [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) associated with god [Zenithar](Lore_Zenithar.md). Little is known of the sickle or of the practices it was tied to, though it was said to have been associated with blood rites long before such rituals became linked with sinister or macabre connotations. According to [Sanguine](Lore_Sanguine.md) it was quickly forgotten.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SG-1)</sup>
