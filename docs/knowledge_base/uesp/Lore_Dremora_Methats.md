*I have awaited you, and have a proposal that might spare your life. Please place your weapons on the ground in front of you, and we can talk.*
—Dremora Methats to the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Methats BSD-1)</sup> (listen Link ⏵) Dremora Methats (or simply **Methats**), also known as **Lord Methats** or **Valkyn Methats** (after his promotion), is a feared and powerful [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) of [Clan Dremora](Lore_Clan_Dremora.md). Together with his comrades [Sumeer Jabran](Lore_Sumeer_Jabran.md) and [Wonshala Keriayn](Lore_Wonshala_Keriayn.md), he has filled high-ranking positions in service to the [Daedric Princes](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BSDSW-3)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Methats was part of the trio known as the Dremora Travelers, who were considered the most fearless of their kind in [Molag Bal](Lore_Molag_Bal.md)'s service.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup> He served Molag Bal as a [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) and a general of his invasion force during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Anchor G-4)</sup> The Dremora Travelers were sent to [Nirn](Lore_Nirn.md) to protect a [Dark Anchor](Lore_Dark_Anchor.md), but they were slain by the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) and the Anchor was destroyed.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Anchor-2)</sup> A [Bretonic](Lore_Breton.md) staff which they looted during their conquests was recovered after their defeat, though it later found its way to an [Ice Atronach](Lore_Frost_Atronach.md) named Nomeg Rine who terrorized [the Rift](Lore_The_Rift.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

By [3E 398](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_398), Methats and his comrades had changed their allegiance to [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md), and joined in his assault on the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md). During this time, Sumeer Jabran outranked Methats. He was assigned as the overseer of the [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) that guarded the deactivated Weir Gate.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BA-6)</sup><sup>:95</sup><sup>:96</sup> During this time, Dremora Rathine served as his lieutenant.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-BA-6)</sup><sup>:91</sup> The last surviving [battlemage](Lore_Imperial_Battlemages.md), [Clarentavious Valisious](Lore_Clarentavious_Valisious.md), tricked the Daedric forces into thinking he was bound to one of the Daedra, and intended to unlock all the anchors holding the Battlespire in place so that it would drift from the mana streams and be destroyed.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AW-7)</sup> However, after the [Apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) slew Lord Methats, Valisious agreed to help by giving the [hero](Lore_Hero.md) the Staff of the Typos Sophia.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-BSD-8)</sup>

Circa [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), Methats had risen to the station of Valkynaz, the highest rank in the Dremora hierarchy under Mehrunes Dagon. At this time, he had in his arsenal the powerful Daedric sword [Torment](Lore_Torment.md), which immolates those it strikes. Alongside Valkynaz Gatanas, he was to serve as the herald for Dagon's assault on Tamriel after the [Last Dragonborn](Lore_Last_Dragonborn.md) unwittingly shattered the liminal barrier separating Nirn from [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md). The Last Dragonborn entered the portal to the [Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) within Red Scar Cavern and defeated Methats and Gatanas, though this served only to delay Dagon, who was still building an army.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Vonos-9)</sup> Although the portal remained open, the [Vigil of Stendarr](Lore_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md) arrived at Red Scar Cavern and helped purge the [Mythic Dawn](Lore_Mythic_Dawn.md) cultists. They then stationed themselves within the cavern to watch over the portal.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-The Cause-10)</sup>
