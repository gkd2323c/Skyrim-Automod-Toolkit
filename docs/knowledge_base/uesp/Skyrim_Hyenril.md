[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/f3/SR-npc-Hyenril.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hyenril.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hyenril.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hyenril.jpg) Hyenril Hyenril is a dead [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) who can be found inside the entrance of the barrow at [Skytemple Ruins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skytemple_Ruins). Her body lies beneath the table where the Arms of the Sun and Moon, Warlock's Mark, and *[Hyenril's Journal](Skyrim_Hyenril%27s_Journal.md)* are found atop of. She was presumably killed by the undead in the ruins while attempting to use her mentor Ellane's research to activate the arms.

She wears [blue mage robes](Skyrim_Blue_Mage_Robes.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). She carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and a selection of [common loot and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).
