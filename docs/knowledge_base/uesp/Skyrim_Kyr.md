[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/6a/SR-npc-Kyr.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Kyr.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kyr.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Kyr.jpg) Kyr lies close to death **Kyr** was a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md). He was the leader of a group of bandits holed up in [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md) however they were going through a rough patch with too few passing caravans to raid. Things began to look up for him when two new recruits [Eisa Blackthorn](Skyrim_Eisa_Blackthorn.md) and [Ra'jirr](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ra%27jirr) joined their group and they made their first big find, buried chest with a couple hundred gold. After putting Eisa in charge of the dig they discovered a hidden forest underground in Frostmere Crypt. In the forest they discovered a [sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Pale_Blade) on some kind of monument which Kyr kept for himself. After the sword had been removed Ra'jirr began hallucinating about the [Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady_(creature).md) and decided to steal the sword and return it to the forest. Kyr assumed Eisa and Ra'jirr had stolen the sword together and put out a [bounty](Skyrim_Kyr%27s_Bounty.md) of 100 gold for both of them. Kyr was then ambushed when chasing Ra'jirr into the forest.

## Related Quests
- **[The Pale Lady](Skyrim_The_Pale_Lady.md)**: Find out what happened in [Frostmere Crypt](Skyrim_Frostmere_Crypt.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
When entering Frostmere Depths, one of the first things you will be greeted by are the raspy coughs and gasps coming from the mortally wounded Kyr. If you approach him, he will get some words, albeit with great difficulty:

- *"*Gasp* Finally, someone came!... I... *coughs* The cat... *gasp* Ra'jiir... ambushed me. He's trying to take the sword back! I... can't... *gasp*"*

After this, he succumbs to his injuries.

## Notes
- Kyr ties with [Grelod the Kind](Skyrim_Grelod_the_Kind.md) as one of two NPCs with the lowest health in the game, sitting at just one point.
- You can kill Kyr before he has a chance to speak (getting within range of forced conversation) with any ranged attack. He also dies prematurely if you enter conversation with him and then back out of it before he can finish what he was saying. It doesn't have any effect on quest progress if you choose to do so.
