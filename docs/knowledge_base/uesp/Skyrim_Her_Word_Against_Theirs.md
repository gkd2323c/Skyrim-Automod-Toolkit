## Quick Walkthrough
1. Receive this quest from [Matriarch Drevlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan) in [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md).
2. Get the *[Propaganda Letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Propaganda_Letter)* from [Priest Drureth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Drureth).
3. Speak with [Aphia Velothi](Skyrim_Aphia_Velothi.md) and give her two hours to translate the letter.
4. Disseminate translated Propaganda Letters to the public.

## Detailed Walkthrough

### A Task for the Goddess
This quest is offered as part of the [Ghosts of the Tribunal](Skyrim_Ghosts_of_the_Tribunal_(quest).md) quest if you choose to disguise yourself as [Hand Kenro Hlan](Skyrim_Hand_Kenro_Hlan.md) before entering [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md). Doing so will allow you to speak with the normally hostile [Matriarch Drevlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan). Offer to serve the Goddess Almalexia, and Drevlan will give you this quest along with [Buyer Beware](Skyrim_Buyer_Beware.md) and [Careless Curation](Skyrim_Careless_Curation.md). These quests are all optional and can be completed in any order, although you must do them if you intend to remain aligned with the [Tribunal Temple](Skyrim_Tribunal_Temple.md).

For this quest, you will be instructed to speak with [Priest Drureth](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest_Drureth). He can be found in one of the side rooms within the temple in Ashfall's Tear. He will hand you a transcribed *[Propaganda Letter](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Propaganda_Letter)* which will need to be translated from the original [Daedric text](Lore_Daedric_Alphabet.md) before it can be distributed.

### Information Campaign
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/33/SR-quest-Her_Word_Against_Theirs_02.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Her_Word_Against_Theirs_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Her_Word_Against_Theirs_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Her_Word_Against_Theirs_02.jpg) The Raven Rock Temple would be very interested in knowing about this letter Return to Raven Rock with the untranslated letter and speak with [Aphia Velothi](Skyrim_Aphia_Velothi.md). She is a former Temple priestess and will agree to secretly translate the text of the letter. If you have not yet encountered Aphia, she can initially be found just inside the entrance to [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md). It will take her two hours to translate the document.

With the translated letters in hand, you will be presented with two options on how to progress the quest:

- Give the letters to the [Reclamation Priest](Skyrim_Reclamation_Priest.md) within the Raven Rock [Temple](Skyrim_Temple.md). He will reward you with a leveled amount of gold.

- Distribute four copies to the general public, You will need to persuade, intimidate or bribe them to read the letter or to show the letter to their patrons. Plenty of individuals can be targets to take the letter, both in Skyrim and Solstheim, even those random encouters, a small example are listed in below table.

### Reward
The outcome of this quest depends on whether you chose to distribute the propaganda or alert the New Temple authorities, there're 4 circumstances in total, either no upgraded Ashfall's Tear, level 1 upgraded Ashfall's Tear, level 2, level 3.

If you chose to distribute the material to the public, you will return to Ashfall's Tear to find a number of new occupants. These new converts will have cleaned the temple's living areas, replacing the destroyed furnishings previously found there, all planters are now sprouting plants. Talk to [Matriarch Drevlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Matriarch_Drevlan) for gold reward, and she will give you [Letter from Matriarch Drevlan](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Letter_from_Matriarch_Drevlan), both the letter, the gold rewarded and the upgraded Ashfall's Tear have 3 versions depending on whom you disseminated the translated propaganda letters to.

- No upgrading and [large gold reward](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Gold_Rewards), if you give all four translated propaganda letters to the [Reclamation Priest](Skyrim_Reclamation_Priest.md) for gold reward.
- Level 1 upgrading and [small gold reward](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Gold_Rewards), if you give all four letters to non-business people, there will only be one new [Ordinator](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ordinator) and one new [Temple Priestess](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priestess), and the forge will be still obsolete without [Blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith.md).
- Level 2 upgrading and [medium gold reward](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Gold_Rewards), if you give at least one letter to business people except [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md), there will be 4 new [Ordinators](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ordinator) and 2 new [Temple Priestesses](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Temple_Priestess), a [blacksmith](Skyrim_Blacksmith_(NPC).md) vendor will appear along with a now-functioning [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) and his merchant chest under the floor by the forge.
- Level 3 upgrading and [large gold reward](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Gold_Rewards), if you give at least one letter to [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md), on top of level 2 upgrading, [Caretaker Ineril](Skyrim_Caretaker_Ineril.md) will appear as a new general goods vendor in the alchemical garden.

If you have ever given at least one of the translated propaganda letters to the [Reclamation Priest](Skyrim_Reclamation_Priest.md), after you complete [Ashen Heart](Skyrim_Ashen_Heart.md) and return to [Ashfall's Tear](Skyrim_Ashfall%27s_Tear.md) once again, he will appear in the company of three members of the [Redoran Guard](Skyrim_Redoran_Guard.md) and attempt to kill the heretics within, you are free to join in if you so choose, however they four are much weaker than the whole heretic, the Reclamation Priest carries [Reclamation Priest's Journal](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reclamation_Priest%27s_Journal).

## Notes
- You needn't disseminate all four letters, only one is enough to upgrade Ashfall's Tear, the rest three can be given to [Reclamation Priest](Skyrim_Reclamation_Priest.md), however [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md) is the best target because of the appearance of [Caretaker Ineril](Skyrim_Caretaker_Ineril.md).
