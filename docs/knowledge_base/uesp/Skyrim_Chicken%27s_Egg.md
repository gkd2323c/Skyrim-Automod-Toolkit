[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ad/SR-ingredient-Chicken%27s_Egg.jpg/200px-SR-ingredient-Chicken%27s_Egg.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Chicken%27s_Egg.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-ingredient-Chicken%27s_Egg.jpg) Chicken's Nest **Chicken's Eggs** can be harvested from **Chickens' Nests** which are found at most farms. The nests are usually in a little shed-like structure, but not always: nests can be just outside on the ground. They cannot be harvested from other birds' nests—those nests yield [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg) and [rock warbler eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rock_Warbler_Egg).

They are one of only three ingredients with a [Waterbreathing](Skyrim_Waterbreathing_(effect).md) effect. The [Hearthfire add-on](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md) adds two more items with this effect, giving a total of five ingredients with this effect.

- 8 in [Thalmor Embassy](Skyrim_Thalmor_Embassy.md) ([Haafingar](Skyrim_Haafingar.md))

It is categorized as an uncommon ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 15% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in uncommon- and rare-type [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- Chicken's eggs are also categorized as [food](Skyrim_Food.md), meaning they can be sold to innkeepers as well as apothecary merchants.
- Chicken's eggs have the same alchemical properties as [hawk's eggs](Skyrim_Hawk%27s_Egg.md). Combining these two ingredients will therefore result in all four effects being discovered at once.
- With the "Green Thumb" perk from the Alchemy skill tree, you can get 2 eggs per "harvest" of a nest.

## Bugs
- Chicken nests may not respawn eggs due to the seasonal chance values all being set to 0 instead of 100. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
