Rites of the Abomination *Rites of Reachmen*
---
Thorn and blood.
The [briarheart](Lore_Briarheart.md)'s limbs sway.

Feather and fear.
The briarheart's limbs awaken.

Bone and claw.
The briarheart's limbs kill.

Die, briarheart, and lurch to life again.
