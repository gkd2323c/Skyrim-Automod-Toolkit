[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/69/SR-npc-Hogni_Red-Arm.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hogni_Red-Arm.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hogni_Red-Arm.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hogni_Red-Arm.jpg) Hogni Red-Arm at his stall **Hogni Red-Arm** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) meat [merchant](Skyrim_Merchants.md) who spends his days running a stall in [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md)'s marketplace, in front of the city's gate.

Hogni wears a set of merchant [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223c) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0004223d). He is equipped with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

On most days, Hogni gets out of bed at 6am. He'll then sit down and have a two-hour breakfast in his room at the [Silver-Blood Inn](Skyrim_Silver-Blood_Inn.md). He then heads out to the market, at 8am, to work his meat stand for twelve hours. Then, at 8pm, he heads back to his room in the Silver Blood Inn, and hangs out there until he goes to bed at 11pm.

**[Hroki](Skyrim_Hroki.md)**: *"That's disgusting, get that rotting carcass away from me!"*
**Hogni**: *"What's wrong? You have to look at the blood closely to see the quality of the meat. How else will you know it's fresh?"*
**Hroki**: *"I don't need to know if it's fresh from this distance."*

**Hroki**: *"Ugh. You're the most disgusting merchant I have ever seen. The guards should take you away."*
**Hogni**: *"The only people I offend are cheats who don't pay me enough gold coin, or idiots who don't know good meat when I throw it at their faces."*
**Hroki**: *"If you ever think of throwing meat at me, I will have you dragged off to Cidhna Mine so fast the guards will yank those filthy arms of yours off."*
**Hogni**: *"What do I do to deserve such hatred? You give people good food and good prices, and all they do is insult you."*

## Quest-Related Events

### [The Forsworn Conspiracy](Skyrim_The_Forsworn_Conspiracy.md)
When [Margret](Skyrim_Margret.md) gets attacked, he'll yell:

*"Guards! Forsworn!"* If you go near Hogni, he'll say:

*"This has ruined my entire day."* When you converse with him, he'll say:

*"Did you see that madman? A Forsworn agent. Here in the city."* **I was actually looking to buy something.** *"Didn't you just see that woman getting attacked in front of us? We're closed."* **Who was he? Who are the Forsworn?** *"Think he worked down at the smelter. Lot of the laborers there are sympathetic to the Forsworn. They promise people they'll kill off all the Nords that rule over the Reach. Nothing but murderers and saboteurs."* Later in the quest, if you ask him about Margret:

**I have some questions about Margret.** *"Who? You mean that woman who was attacked in the market? Didn't know her. Guards dragged her body away. Last we'll hear of that."* However, if you saved Margret, he'll say:

*"Who? You mean that woman who was attacked in the market the other day? Don't know her. Guards dragged that Forsworn's body away, though. Last we'll hear of that."* At the end of the quest, if you helped [Madanach](Skyrim_Madanach.md), he'll say:

*"Huh. Heard you broke out of Cidhna Mine with those Forsworn. Do you have any idea how many died?"* Alternatively, if you helped [Thonar Silver-Blood](Skyrim_Thonar_Silver-Blood.md), he'll say:

*"Can't believe anyone was dumb enough to mistake you for a Forsworn agent."*
### The Taste of Death
During the quest [The Taste of Death](Skyrim_The_Taste_of_Death.md), you will encounter Hogni as a "dinner guest" in [Reachcliff Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Reachcliff_Cave). When you approach him, he'll say:

*"Now this is going to be a good feast."* When you kill [Brother Verulus](Skyrim_Brother_Verulus.md), he'll say:

*"The feast has begun!"* After you speak to [Namira](Lore_Namira.md), he'll say:

*"Namira spoke to you? I knew this feast was going to be something."* **Wait. I know you.** *"'The bloodiest beef in the Reach!' Ha ha ha."* At any time during the feast, a fellow cannibal might walk up to him and strike up a conversation:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/68/SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Banning_02.jpg) *"When was your first meal, Banning?"* **[Eola](Skyrim_Eola.md)**: *"Thank you for coming to dinner, Hogni."*
**Hogni**: *"I never miss a good meal."*

**Hogni**: *"The toes are my favorite part. I hope I get to take a few home with me."*
**[Lisbet](Skyrim_Lisbet.md)**: *"I always preferred the liver myself. My brother had such a juicy liver."*

**Hogni**: *"When was your first meal, [Banning](Skyrim_Banning.md)?"*
**Banning**: *"Do dogs count?"*
**Hogni**: *"Of course they don't count!"*
**Banning**: *"My friend Marex. We were hunting, and I thought he was an elk."*
**Hogni**: *"That must have been quite a surprise."*
**Banning**: *"Either way, I came back with meat for the table."*

Afterwards, if you see him back in Markarth, you can remark that he was at the feast, to which he'll say:

*"Don't go talking about that. Only the dinner guests can know."*
