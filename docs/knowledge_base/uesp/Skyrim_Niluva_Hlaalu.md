[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5a/SR-npc-Niluva_Hlaalu.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Niluva_Hlaalu.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niluva_Hlaalu.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Niluva_Hlaalu.jpg) Niluva Hlaalu **Niluva Hlaalu** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) who works at [Black-Briar Meadery](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Meadery.md) but is struggling to pay her rent and is worried [Indaryn](Skyrim_Indaryn.md) will fire her for being a [skooma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skooma) addict.

She starts her day early, waking at 4am. The next hour she will spend eating her breakfast before going to work at the meadery. At midday, she has lunch for an hour before resuming work for another 5 hours. At 6pm, she quits working and goes to [Haelga's Bunkhouse](Skyrim_Haelga%27s_Bunkhouse.md) for 4 hours of relaxation before heading to bed there early at 10pm.

Niluva wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000209a5). She is equipped with a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) quality, and carries a selection of [common loot and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Quest-Related Dialogue

### Spread the Love
During the quest, you may give her a [pamphlet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Warmth_of_Mara), though she won't be appreciative:

**Here, sister. Blessings of Mara upon you.** *"Mara only helps those who can afford her tithes. This is nonsense."*
### Supply and Demand
Despite playing no actual role in the quest itself, Niluva will get some new lines during it.

Before you have gone to [Cragslane Cavern](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cragslane_Cavern), she may greet you with:

*"You looking for something?"* After you have visited the place, but have not yet cleared out all the brigands, she will instead say:

*"I told you everything I know. What else did you want?"* *"I think I've said enough. Now leave me alone."* These lines, along with the lines used by [Wujeeta](Skyrim_Wujeeta.md) during the quest, imply that this quest originally progressed differently. It seems that Wujeeta would've given you information about Niluva, with you then being sent to extract the location of Cragslane Cavern from her.

## Conversations
At the bunkhouse [Tythis Ulen](Skyrim_Tythis_Ulen.md) may talk to her concerning loaned money:

**Tythis**: *"Niluva, you promised me you'd pay me back yesterday. So what's the story?"*
**Niluva**: *"Just give me a few more days and I promise I'll come up with it."*
**Tythis**: *"If you'd stop wasting your coin on that skooma, you'd have enough gold to live on."*
**Niluva**: *"Sorry, Tythis. I'll get it to you somehow."* While at the meadery, her fellow worker [Romlyn Dreth](Skyrim_Romlyn_Dreth.md) will be more understanding:

**Niluva**: *"Do you know what Indaryn told me? He said if I'm late one more time he'll kick me right out the door!"*
**Romlyn**: *"That s'wit. One day, he'll regret pushing all of us around."*
**Niluva**: *"What do you mean?"*
**Romlyn**: *"All I'm saying is Indaryn's going to push someone too far one day and they're going to push back... hard."*
## Rumors
Lastly, at the bunkhouse, [Haelga](Skyrim_Haelga.md) can be heard yelling at [her niece](Skyrim_Svana_Far-Shield.md) about Niluva's late rent:

**Haelga**: *"Svana! Has that skooma-addled idiot Niluva paid us her rent yet? Or does she have more excuses?"*
**Svana**: *"She said she'll have it soon. She still has her job at the meadery for now, so she'll be able to pay us."*
**Haelga**: *"Why do you insist on defending that woman? All she does with her money is spend it on that Dark Elf garbage!"*
