**King** Dead Wolf-Deer also known as the **Elder Lord of Teeth and Twenty Antlers**<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2E-1)</sup> was a [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md) who was transformed into a monster-mer in the [fourth century of the First Era](Lore_First_Era.md#Fourth_Century) by the [Wild Hunt](Lore_Wild_Hunt.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup> The Wild Hunt transformation turned him into a monster that had [wolf](Lore_Wolf.md), [deer](Lore_Deer.md), and even [crocodilian](Lore_Crocodile.md) traits.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RWHCodex-3)</sup>

The Wild Hunt that Wolf-Deer was a part of came about during the reign of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) High King [Borgas](Lore_Borgas.md), the last member of the [Ysgramor Dynasty](Lore_Ysgramor_Dynasty.md). Borgas was a staunch supporter of the Prophet [Marukh](Lore_Marukh.md) but a conflict was growing between the Empire and the [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md). Borgas set off for [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) to urge a joint war with Skyrim against the Bosmer. Before he could get there however, he was slaughtered at the hands of King Dead Wolf-Deer.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-PGE3V-4)</sup>

Dead Wolf-Deer later on in history was known to stalk the [Lynpar March](Lore_Lynpar_March.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1E-2)</sup> Sometime before his death in [3E 320](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_320), Emperor [Uriel Septim VI](Lore_Uriel_Septim_VI.md) and his half-sister [Morihatha](Lore_Morihatha.md) traversed the Lynpar March where they encountered the aggressive Dead Wolf-Deer who killed all of their royal escorts.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PGE2E-1)</sup> After the cataclysms of the [Nerevarine Prophecy](Lore_Nerevarine_Prophecy.md) circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), some accounts spoke of a trial put on to judge [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) of his potential crimes. These accounts claim Dead Wolf-Deer still lived and resided in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) and that Vivec had someone retrieve one of his antlers in order to summon [Azura](Lore_Azura.md).<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-RPTrial Of Vehk-5)</sup>

## Notes
- The [Ring of the Wild Hunt](Lore_Ring_of_the_Wild_Hunt.md) artifact is comprised of five pieces, one of these pieces, the Face of the Wolf Beast has been theorized to depict Dead Wolf-Deer.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RWHCodex-3)</sup>
- A recitation of the *Five Hundred Companions*<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-T500MC-6)</sup> amalgamates the slaughter of King Borgas by Dead Wolf's hands with the deeds of King [Wulfharth](Lore_Wulfharth.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-FSKW-7)</sup> In this variation, Borgas is slain by the [power shouts](Lore_Thu%27um.md) of the "Lord of the Wulf's Hart" (or "Wulf's Heart"), then "monsters of the changewinds" arrive to claim his soul.<sup>[[UOL 3]](#cite_note-T500MC-6)</sup>
