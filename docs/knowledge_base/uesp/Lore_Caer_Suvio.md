[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/77/OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg/200px-OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:OB-place-Nibenay_Basin.jpg) Nibenay Basin **Caer Suvio** is a settlement located in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). From its hilly, forested terrain one can clearly see the spires of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md). The luxurious villa of [Mir Corrup](Lore_Mir_Corrup.md), famous for its hot springs, overlooks the settlement.

During the [Four-Score War](Lore_Four-Score_War.md) in [1E 2920](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2920) the villa belonged to Lord Glavius, and was used as a base for the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md), with their tents lining the hillsides. [Catchica](Lore_Catchica.md), an [Orma](Lore_Orma.md) tribeswoman and notorious poisoner from [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md), was present at the camp when Prince [Juilek Cyrodiil](Lore_Juilek.md) was poisoned just before the Battle of [Bodrum](Lore_Bodrum.md), and so she was accused by [Potentate](Lore_Akaviri_Potentate.md) [Versidue-Shaie](Lore_Versidue-Shaie.md) to cover his involvement.

On the 8th of [Evening Star](Lore_Evening_Star.md), the [Tribunal](Lore_Tribunal.md) [God](Lore_Gods.md) [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) arrived in Caer Suvio, where he met with Lord Glavius and Versidue-Shaie to sign the [Cervant Truce](Lore_Cervant_Truce.md) for peace between the [Empire](Lore_Second_Empire.md) and [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LY:2920-1)</sup>
