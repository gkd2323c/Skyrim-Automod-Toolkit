Medno Oren was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) leader of the [Buoyant Armigers](Lore_Buoyant_Armigers.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md) of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They held the position of the Grand Marshal among the Armigers and were known as **the Exalted Bard of the First Order**.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCS-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/ON-npc-Armiger_Urnsi.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Armiger_Urnsi.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Armiger_Urnsi.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Armiger_Urnsi.jpg) Armiger Urnsi, Medno's subordinate Little is known about Medno Oren's past. Being an Armiger they likely hailed from [House Redoran](Lore_House_Redoran.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-SOV-2)</sup> although some of their contemporaneous family members held high-ranking positions within [House Telvanni](Lore_House_Telvanni.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-VO-3)</sup>

By [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Oren had already attained the rank of Grand Marshal, and earned their title of the Exalted Bard of the First Order. Around that time, they, alongside [Vivec](Lore_Vivec.md) himself and the Archanon [Tarvus Indoril](Lore_Tarvus_Indoril.md) of the [Tribunal Temple](Lore_Tribunal_Temple.md), dispatched their subordinate Armiger Urnsi, the Poet Militant and Knight-Scribe of the Seventh and Twenty-First Stanzas, on a mission to investigate the situation of the [Ashlanders](Lore_Ashlanders.md), particularly concerning Seryn of the [Urshilaku Tribe](Lore_Urshilaku_Tribe.md), a prominent figure within the tribe, and subsequently received a report from her.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MTCS-1)</sup>

Little is known about Medno Oren's life after 2E 582, but by [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the office of Grand Marshal had passed to [Galdal Omayn](Lore_Galdal_Omayn.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-GO-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ghostgate-5)</sup>
