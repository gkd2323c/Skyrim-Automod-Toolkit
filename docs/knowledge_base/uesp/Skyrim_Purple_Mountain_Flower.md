[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-flora-Purple_Mountain_Flower_Plant.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Purple_Mountain_Flower_Plant.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Purple_Mountain_Flower_Plant.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Purple_Mountain_Flower_Plant.jpg) A purple mountain flower plant **Purple Mountain Flower** is harvested from the purple-flowered variety of mountain flower. Other varieties of mountain flower yield [blue mountain flowers](Skyrim_Blue_Mountain_Flower.md), [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and [yellow mountain flowers](Skyrim_Yellow_Mountain_Flower.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup>, but the flowers have different alchemical properties. The blue, purple, and red mountain flowers are commonplace in the foothills and lower elevations of mountains (below the snowline).

## Ingredients
88 guaranteed samples can be found in 47 different locations. Locations with the greatest numbers are:

- 11 in [Frostflow Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 6 in [Solitude Catacombs](Skyrim_Solitude_Catacombs.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 5 in [the Katariah](Skyrim_The_Katariah.md) ([Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md))
- 4 centered 480 ft NE of [Red Road Pass](Skyrim_Red_Road_Pass.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=30078&y=85752)

It is categorized as a common ingredient, meaning that all apothecary merchants have a 36% chance of carrying 1-5 samples. In addition, it may be randomly found in some barrels and in [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md).

## Notes
- When gathering ingredients at night, note that Purple and Blue Mountain Flowers look nearly identical in the darkness. To avoid confusion, bring a light source (such as a torch, or the [candlelight](Skyrim_Candlelight.md) spell) to differentiate between them.
