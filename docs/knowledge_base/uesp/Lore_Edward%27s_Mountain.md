Edward's Mountain is a mountain in [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md) that is located near the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md). It is featured in the [fifth](Lore_Fools%27_Ebony,_Part_the_Fiveth.md) and [sixth](Lore_Fools%27_Ebony,_Part_the_Sixth.md) part of the [Fools' Ebony](Lore_Fools%27_Ebony.md) play.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-FE5-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-FE6-2)</sup>

## Notes
- The mountain may be named after the legendary King [Edward](Lore_Edward.md), who was known for his adventures and business related to [Ebony](Lore_Ebony.md) in the region of the Wrothgarian Mountains.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-KE-3)</sup>
