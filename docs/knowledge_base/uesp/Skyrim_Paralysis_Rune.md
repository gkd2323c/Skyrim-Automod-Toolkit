Paralysis Rune is an adept level [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell that creates a [rune](Skyrim_Rune_(effect).md) which will explode when enemies come near, causing [Paralysis](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md) for 8 seconds. You can't cast it just anywhere. When you press the cast button, the cross-hair will let you know if you are pointing at a valid surface within range. If it is red, then you can't cast the spell there.

## Effects
- [Paralysis Rune](Skyrim_Paralyze_(effect).md), for 8 secs

## Perks
- [Rune Master](Skyrim_Rune_Master.md) increases range to 100 feet.
- [Stability](Skyrim_Stability.md) raises duration to 12 secs.<sup>[*verification needed — see [talk page](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_talk:Paralysis_Rune)*]</sup>

## Notes
- Paralysis Runes were originally developed as part of the [Game Jam](Skyrim_Game_Jam.md).
- Trigger effect of this rune can penetrate closed draugr sarcophagus.
