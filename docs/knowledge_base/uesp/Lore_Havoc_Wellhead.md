[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/BS-Level6_Daedric_Camp1.png/200px-BS-Level6_Daedric_Camp1.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-Level6_Daedric_Camp1.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:BS-Level6_Daedric_Camp1.png) A military camp in the Havoc Wellhead The Havoc Wellhead (sometimes styled **Havok Wellhead**) is a realm of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) controlled by many [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) clans in the service of [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). It acts as one of Dagon's many provincial governmental centers. It was accessible from the [Battlespire](Lore_Battlespire.md), but the connection was lost after its destruction during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup>

The [Chimera of Desolation](Lore_Chimera_of_Desolation.md) links to this realm via teleportation from the Temple of the Daedric Rites in the walled town of [Granvellusa](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Granvellusa). The castle of [Daedra Lord](Lore_Daedra_Lord.md) [Imago Storm](Lore_Imago_Storm.md) is found here, which houses the gate to [Dagon's Hunting Lodge](Lore_Dagon%27s_Hunting_Lodge.md). To activate the portal, the three gatekeys must be inserted into the gate lantern. The realm is the site of many Daedric affairs. An arena stages honorable fights between clans, and "Preliminary Intelligence" tests take place for unknown reasons.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BS-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

## History
The forces of Mehrunes Dagon used the realm as a headquarters during the invasion of the Battlespire.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-10th-4)</sup> An [apprentice](Lore_Apprentice.md) from the Spire arrived in the Wellhead to save a fellow apprentice. The apprentice became an ally of Imago Storm of Clan Dremora, who was opposed to Dagon's invasion. Imago wanted Dagon and his lieutenants banished to the [Void](Lore_Void.md), so that he could restore Clan Dagon to stability in their absence. Imago promised the apprentice aid from his [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) vassals and gave him the [neonymics](Lore_Neonymic.md) of Dagon, Xivilai and Faydra, which would allow him to banish those Daedra.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The apprentice tracked down the two other Daedra Lords of the realm, [Xivilai Moath](Lore_Xivilai_Moath.md) of [Xivilai's Clan](Lore_Xivilai%27s_Clan.md) and [Faydra Shardai](Lore_Faydra_Shardai.md) of [Shardai Clan](Lore_Shardai_Clan.md), tricking and banishing them respectively to get their keys. Before the apprentice could leave, [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md)'s servant [Sirran Angada](Lore_Sirran_Angada.md) appeared and revealed Tharn's false reign and Dagon's role in it. The apprentice possibly slew him as a traitor to the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) but ultimately used the keys to depart from the Havoc Wellhead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BS-2)</sup>
