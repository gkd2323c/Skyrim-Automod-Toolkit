[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/63/SR-place-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg/200px-SR-place-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg) The Frozen Hearth **The Frozen Hearth** is an [inn](Skyrim_Inns.md) located in the city of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). It is one of the few inhabited buildings in town and is a gathering point for the remaining residents. It is located in the center of town, across the street from the [Jarl's Longhouse](Skyrim_Jarl%27s_Longhouse_(Winterhold).md).

[Dagur](Skyrim_Dagur.md) tends the counter, buying and selling [food and drinks](#Standard_Merchandise) and renting out rooms for 10 gold, while his wife [Haran](Skyrim_Haran.md) serves the customers. It consists of two areas, *[The Frozen Hearth](#The_Frozen_Hearth)* and *[The Frozen Hearth Cellar](#The_Frozen_Hearth_Cellar)*.

## The Frozen Hearth
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3f/SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth.jpg) Inside the Frozen Hearth Like many other taverns in smaller settlements, The Frozen Hearth consists of a huge taproom with a fireplace in the center and the counter at the opposite end of the room from the entrance. Several wooden tables are scattered around, all of them set with food and drink. In addition to the taproom, the inn also has three smaller rooms with single beds, one of them reserved for paying customers.

To the right of the front door, in the southern corner, is the first table which is dressed with two [salmon steaks](Skyrim_Salmon_Steak.md) on wooden plates, an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), some [sliced eidar cheese](Skyrim_Sliced_Eidar_Cheese.md), an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), a [goat cheese wedge](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), some [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), and one bottle each of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md) and [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md). In the corner stands a single food barrel, while a similar barrel can be found in the adjacent corner to the east. Next to the barrels are another table with two loaves of bread, and more wine and alto wine, two bottles of each. The next table is where you can find local drunk [Ranmir](Skyrim_Ranmir.md) around the clock. The table in front of him is dressed with three loaves of bread, two bottles of alto wine, and one bottle of regular wine. Next up is a set of shelves with two [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), six bottles of [Nord mead](Skyrim_Nord_Mead.md), and [Wylandriah](Skyrim_Wylandriah.md)'s Satchel, needed for the quest [Hunt and Gather](Skyrim_Hunt_and_Gather.md). The last table for paying customers can be found between the entrance door and the room for rent. It is set with two [chicken's eggs](Skyrim_Chicken%27s_Egg.md), a bottle of wine, and a loaf of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_02.jpg) [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md)'s room The bar takes up most of the northwestern part of the taproom and Dagur can be found behind the counter 24 hours a day. On display is a wedge, a slice, and a wheel of eidar cheese, and the same of goat cheese, a bottle of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), and a bottle of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), along with a [salmon steak](Skyrim_Salmon_Steak.md). Hidden on the shelves underneath the counter are five gold coins, an [iron war axe](Skyrim_Iron_War_Axe.md), a small [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md), six bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), two bottles of [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md), and a copy of the book *[Herbane's Bestiary: Ice Wraiths](Skyrim_Herbane%27s_Bestiary__Ice_Wraiths.md)*. In the western corner is a set of shelves with two bottles of alto wine and six of regular wine, along with four loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md). Next to the shelves are two food barrels. Hanging from the ceiling, above the stairs leading down into the basement, are seven dead [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) and two [pheasants](Skyrim_Pheasant.md). Hanging next to the game are a few ingredients, including two [garlic braids](Skyrim_Garlic_Braid.md), some [dried elves ear](Skyrim_Dried_Elves_Ear.md), and some [dried frost mirriam](Skyrim_Dried_Frost_Mirriam.md).

The room you can rent is the second room along the western wall, closest to the bar, and contains a single bed, a dresser with random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md), a chest with minor loot, and a copy of *[Walking the World, vol XI](Skyrim_Walking_the_World,_Vol_XI.md)*. The neighboring room can be used by [Eirid](Skyrim_Eirid.md), [Haran](Skyrim_Haran.md), and [Dagur](Skyrim_Dagur.md). It contains a wardrobe with clothes, a copy of *[Nords of Skyrim](Skyrim_Nords_of_Skyrim.md)*, a chest with some loot, and a bottle of [Black-Briar Mead](Skyrim_Black-Briar_Mead.md). With the [Nchuanthumz: Dwarven Home](Skyrim_Nchuanthumz__Dwarven_Home.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md) installed, *[Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts' Journal, v1](Skyrim_Seeks-Ancient-Artifacts%27_Journal,_v1.md)* can be found on the table in this room. Adjacent to these two rooms is another room, reserved for permanent resident and former [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md) member, the wizard [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md). It seems like he has been living there for quite some time and it resembles a small trading room, complete with a counter with a copy of the rare tome *[Words of Clan Mother Ahnissi](Skyrim_Words_of_Clan_Mother_Ahnissi.md)* and two bottles of alto wine on display. On the shelves underneath the counter is the [staff of arcane authority](Skyrim_Staff_of_Arcane_Authority.md) used for the quest [Some Light Theft](Skyrim_Some_Light_Theft.md). Behind the counter is a set of shelves with Nelecar's personal library, several books on [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md), and a single bottle of alto wine. In the southeastern corner sits his owned bed with an empty chest at the foot. In the corner is a chair where Nelecar usually sits, and a table with an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md), a medium coin purse, and four gold coins.

## The Frozen Hearth Cellar
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/47/SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_03.jpg/200px-SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-The_Frozen_Hearth_03.jpg) The Frozen Hearth Cellar Down the stairs from the taproom is the cellar where [Dagur](Skyrim_Dagur.md)'s wife [Haran](Skyrim_Haran.md) sleeps next to their child, [Eirid](Skyrim_Eirid.md). The door opens to the sight of three food barrels and a huge amount of cobwebs, hinting at the decline of Winterhold. In the sleeping area are two owned beds, one single and one double, whereas Eirid sleeps in the single bed, next to a basket with five [green apples](Skyrim_Green_Apple.md). In the corner is an end table with clutter, with two bottles of alto wine on top. At the foot of the double bed is a chest. In the corner sits another end table with a medium [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md) on top and, on the floor, four food sacks. There is also a wardrobe with some random [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md) and another food barrel. In the center of the sleeping area is a dinner table set with a healthy amount of food, including three [red apples](Skyrim_Red_Apple.md), three green apples, an [apple pie](Skyrim_Apple_Pie.md), two [sweet rolls](Skyrim_Sweet_Roll.md), a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md), an [eidar cheese wheel](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wheel.md), a wedge of [goat cheese](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wedge.md), three loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md) and two bottles of regular [wine](Skyrim_Wine.md). Adjacent to the dinner table is a storage area with several inactive crates and three food sacks and, wedged between a crate and a pillar, the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) skill book *[The Exodus](Skyrim_The_Exodus.md)*. In the other end of the room, behind the empty table, are three more food sacks and a food barrel.

## Notes
- If you infect Haran with vampirism during the [New Allegiances](Skyrim_New_Allegiances.md)<sup>[DG](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md)</sup> quest, a coffin will appear in the westernmost corner of the cellar and can be used to sleep and get the vampire rested bonus. Haran does not seem to use it however, and will still sleep in her old bed.
- The *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)* mentions a character by the name of **Angwe** living here, although no such person exists, and is never even mentioned. There isn't even a trace of them to be found in the game data.
