- *Heals the target 10 points per second, but not undead, atronachs or machines.*

**Healing Hands** is an apprentice level [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spell, which will slowly [restore the health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md) of the target.

## Effects
- [Restore Health](Skyrim_Restore_Health.md), 10 pts/sec for 1 sec - This spell then [tapers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tapering); it will continue to deal the original magnitude of the effect to the target for another 0.3 seconds after expiring.
- [Restore Stamina](Skyrim_Restore_Stamina.md), 10 pts for 1 sec; if the [Respite](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Respite) perk is unlocked.

## Perks
- [Regeneration](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Regeneration), increases health (and stamina with the Respite perk) restoration to 15/sec.

## Bugs
- Healing Hands is registered as an offensive spell on certain NPCs, such as [Revelers](Skyrim_Reveler.md). <sup>**?**</sup>
