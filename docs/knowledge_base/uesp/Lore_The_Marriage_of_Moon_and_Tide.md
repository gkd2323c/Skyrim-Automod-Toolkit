The Marriage of Moon and Tide **by [Clan Mother Tsradama](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Clan_Mother_Tsradama)** *A clan mother's description of the connection between the moons and the tides*
---
Any true [Khajiit](Lore_Khajiit.md) knows of the [Ja-Kha'jay](Lore_Ja-Kha%27jay.md)'s gentle waltz through the sky, but few understand that [Nirni](Lore_Nirni.md) too joins in the dance. Though she is rooted to the spot [Lorkhaj](Lore_Lorkhaj.md) made for her to bear her children, she still sways along with her siblings as they dance through the night.

"But, Tsradama," you say, "this one has never seen Nirni swaying with the [Moons](Lore_Moons.md)."

Likely, you have, but you did not understand what you saw. Just as a flea in your fur cannot tell you are dancing when you shake your tail to a merry tune, we cannot easily spot how Nirni moves. When next you watch for [Jone](Lore_Jone.md) and [Jode](Lore_Jode.md) to rise, keep your eyes on the sea and you will spot Nirni's eager waves rolling farther and farther up the shore along their path. This is a sign that Nirni sways to the music of the Moons.
