[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/88/SR-place-Shrouded_Grove.jpg/200px-SR-place-Shrouded_Grove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrouded_Grove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Shrouded_Grove.jpg) Shrouded Grove **Shrouded Grove** is a grove with a small cave located in [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md).

It is located due south of [Silverdrift Lair](Skyrim_Silverdrift_Lair.md) and west of [Nightgate Inn](Skyrim_Nightgate_Inn.md), and is inhabited by leveled [animals](Skyrim_Animals.md) and [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md).

## Walkthrough

### Shrouded Grove
The grove's only identifying features are a large stone statue head and a few stone columns. There is a leveled [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md) camouflaged against a tree trunk to the left of the cave's entrance as well as a leveled [animal](Skyrim_Animals.md) in the grove nearby. There is also a [frost troll](Skyrim_Frost_Troll.md) in the vicinity that may attack the animal.

### Shrouded Grove Cave
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b2/SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Shrouded_Grove_Cave.jpg) Interior of Shrouded Grove The entrance leads into a short, ice-covered tunnel that leads into the cave itself. Upon entering, you will see two animals attacking each other; the winner will generally be quite injured by the encounter, making it an easy target. Camouflaged against a tree trunk to the left is another leveled [spriggan](Skyrim_Spriggan.md). Once the enemies are dealt with, you are free to explore the rest of the cave. There is an unlocked [chest](Skyrim_Chest.md) against the east wall, by the back of the cave; to either side of it lie two dead [draugr](Skyrim_Containers.md#Draugr), and an open sarcophagus lies behind it on the left. The sarcophagus contains a third draugr and a copy of the [Light Armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Ice and Chitin](Skyrim_Ice_and_Chitin.md)*.
