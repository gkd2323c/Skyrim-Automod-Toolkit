[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3e/SR-item-Zahkriisos.jpg/200px-SR-item-Zahkriisos.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Zahkriisos.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Zahkriisos.jpg) Zahkriisos Zahkriisos is the mask of the [eponymous](Skyrim_Zahkriisos_(creature).md) [dragon priest](Skyrim_Dragon_Priest.md), found within [Raven Rock Mine](Skyrim_Raven_Rock_Mine.md). It gives the wearer a constant increase in [shock damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) by 25%, as well as 50% [shock resistance](Skyrim_Resist_Shock.md). It can be improved with one [ebony ingot](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md).

## Notes
- Shock spells do 25% more damage, [Shock Damage](Skyrim_Shock_Damage.md) and [Chaos Damage](Skyrim_Chaos_Damage.md) enchantments on weapons do 25% more damage. The [Storm Call](Skyrim_Storm_Call.md) shout does 25% more damage as well.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup>, [Shadowfoot Sanctum](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowfoot_Sanctum.md)</sup>, [Tundra Homestead](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead_(place).md)'s cellar<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Tundra_Homestead.md)</sup>, [Bloodchill Cavern](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Bloodchill_Manor.md)</sup>, and [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md)'s gallery<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md)</sup> contain a special holder for this item.
