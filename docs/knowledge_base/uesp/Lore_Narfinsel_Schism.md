# Narfinsel Schism


The war was held between the [Aedra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Aedra) and [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md) -worshipping [Ayleids](Lore_Ayleid.md) of [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). The war's conclusion, brought in by the Scouring of [Wendelbek](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Wendelbek), marked the end of any organized resistance to Daedra worship in Cyrodiil. The lasting effects of the Schism were theorized to have greatly contributed to the fall of the [Ayleid Empire](Lore_Ayleid_Empire.md) and the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md), nearly half a century later.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ASIV-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CAATAYQ-2)</sup>

## Major Battles
- **Scouring of Wendelbek**, 1E 198. Marking the end of the war, King Glinferen of [Atatar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Atatar) led a combined force of [Daedraphile](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Daedraphile) warriors against the traditionalist, Aedra-worshipping [Barsaebics](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Barsaebics) of Wendelbek, driving them south into [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md).
