[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/52/SR-npc-Belrand.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Belrand.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belrand.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Belrand.jpg) Belrand **Belrand** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [spellsword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:NPCclass Belrand) and [blade-for-hire](Skyrim_Followers.md) who can be found in the [Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md) in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md). He charges a fee of 500 gold.

He wears a set of [iron armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iron_Armor), a pair of [hide bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Bracers), and a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots). He is equipped with an [iron sword](Skyrim_Iron_Sword.md). He knows the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spell [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md), the [Conjuration](Skyrim_Conjuration.md) spell [Conjure Familiar](Skyrim_Conjure_Familiar.md), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Fast Healing](Skyrim_Fast_Healing.md) and [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md).

Belrand can join the [Blades](Skyrim_Blades.md), and can also be a potential [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md) partner.

## Dialogue
He will glowingly advertise his services with:

*"Versatility is the key to survival. Hire me, and you'll be well prepared to face threats both magical and mundane."* *"I fight with both blade and spell. You might say I'm equipped to handle nearly any threat. So, shall we talk price?"* *"Sure, you can hire a swordsman or a spellcaster to fight at your side, but why not get both for the price of one?"* *"By blade or by spell, if you want it dead, I'll make it dead. All you need to do is pay my fee."* *"The blade can be as lethal as the spell, but deadlier still is the one who wields both!"* If you heed his endorsement and hire him, he will gleefully say, *"What dangers and wonders shall we discover in the frozen wastes? I can hardly wait to find out!"*

After being hired, Belrand will occasionally greet you with, *"You're looking hale and healthy. It's good to see you again."* or "*Didn't think I'd be seeing you again so soon."* Like other hirelings, he may offer his services free of charge after being dismissed, saying, *"Back for more? Since we had so much fun the last time, I'll join you free of charge."* If you accept, he will tell you, *"Lead the way."*

## Notes
- He wears a heavy cuirass, despite being proficient in light armor instead. In addition, despite having a high [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) skill and a perk for Destruction, he knows no Destruction spells. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.8, addresses this issue. He is now fully clad in hide armor, and has the ability to cast [Ice Spike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike).
- In the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) data, there's an outfit named `Belrand Outfit`, which consists of a leveled piece of light armor boots, cuirass, and gauntlets of the best quality. However, it isn't in use.
