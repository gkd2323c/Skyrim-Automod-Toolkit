[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/23/DF-prerelease-Tamriel_Map.gif/200px-DF-prerelease-Tamriel_Map.gif)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-prerelease-Tamriel_Map.gif) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-prerelease-Tamriel_Map.gif) Map of Tamriel (*Daggerfall*) The Yrinthi River is seemingly a major waterway, accessible by ship from the ports of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) in the heartland of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md). It flows westward across the continent before eventually emptying into the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DUG-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup> Riding across the entire river from start to end takes a number of weeks.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DUG-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup>

## History
By [3E 405](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the Yrinthi River was notoriously infested with pirates, as was the Bjoulsae River and the Iliac Bay; highly decorated ships needed to be well-defended in order to complete a safe voyage.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DUG-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup> On mission to the [Kingdom of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall_(kingdom).md), [the Agent](Lore_The_Agent.md) took a small fishing craft from the [Imperial Province](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) across the Yrinthi and into the Bjoulsae to reach their destination.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DUG-1)</sup><sup>:5</sup>

## Notes
- The Yrinthi River has never appeared physically in the games or on any official maps of Tamriel.
- The [Cyrodiil passage](Lore_Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire,_1st_Edition_Cyrodiil.md) of the *[Pocket Guide to the Empire: 1st Edition](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:Pocket_Guide_to_the_Empire__1st_Edition)* refers to rivers in Cyrodiil connecting the Imperial City by trade to inland ports located in [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) and [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md), indicating that a river flowing from Cyrodiil into northern Tamriel exists.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE1C-2)</sup>
