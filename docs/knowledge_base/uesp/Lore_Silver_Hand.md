# Silver Hand


The Silver Hand has some connection to the [Vigil of Stendarr](Lore_Vigil_of_Stendarr.md), a group set up in the wake of the [Oblivion Crisis](Lore_Oblivion_Crisis.md) whose members are known to hunt werewolves in the name of [Stendarr](Lore_Stendarr.md), with some Vigilants fighting together with the Silver Hand and integrating canine motifs into the design of their steel armor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

In [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the group was wiped out by the Companions, in retaliation for an assault on [Jorrvaskr](Lore_Jorrvaskr.md) in which the Silver Hand had managed to kill the harbinger [Kodlak Whitemane](Lore_Kodlak_Whitemane.md) and steal all but one of the Companion's pieces of Wuuthrad.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Skyrim-1)</sup>
