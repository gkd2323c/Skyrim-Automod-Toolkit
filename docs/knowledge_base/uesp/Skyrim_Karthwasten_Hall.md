## Karthwasten Hall
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b3/SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall.jpg) Ground floor The front door opens to a bedroom area on the right and a kitchen and dining area to the left. Immediately to the right is a single bed (belonging to the [Karthwasten Ainethach House](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Karthwasten Ainethach House) faction) in the northwest corner. Farther along the west wall is a small table holding five gold coins, five [silver ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Silver_Ingot), and a medium-sized [coin purse](Skyrim_Coin_Purse.md). A non-respawning wardrobe stands along the south wall nearby, next to a woven basket containing two [potions of vigor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Vigor). A double bed, owned by the same faction, is situated to the left, with a small non-respawning chest at its foot. To the left of the bed is the home's fireplace, with a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace) on its mantel and a [cooking pot](Skyrim_Cooking.md) at its base. A hanging rack with three freshly caught [rabbits](Skyrim_Rabbit_(dead).md) can be found just to the left of the fireplace.

The east side of the north wall hosts the dining and food storage area, starting with a large dining table. The table is set with two loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md), one bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine) and two bottles of [alto wine](Skyrim_Alto_Wine.md), three [pine thrush eggs](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pine_Thrush_Egg), a [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato), a serving of [salmon steak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Salmon_Steak), a head of [cabbage](Skyrim_Cabbage.md), and two bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead). On the floor next to the table is a woven basket containing two [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), and a set of shelves which hold two more leeks, three potatoes, and a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) bulb, as well as some [sliced eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sliced_Eidar_Cheese), an [eidar cheese wedge](Skyrim_Eidar_Cheese_Wedge.md), and a [goat cheese wheel](Skyrim_Goat_Cheese_Wheel.md) sitting on top. Above the shelves are several hanging ingredients, including two garlic braids, three portions of dried [elves ear](Skyrim_Elves_Ear.md), and some dried [frost mirriam](Skyrim_Frost_Mirriam.md). A respawning food barrel can be found in the northeast corner.

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fa/SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg/200px-SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-interior-Karthwasten_Hall_02.jpg) Basement Heading downstairs will lead to the basement, which is relatively bare. A garlic braid and one portion each of dried frost mirriam and dried elves ear are hanging above a small round table which holds a single loaf of bread. A set of shelves on the south wall holds various kitchenware, and two respawning food barrels sit to the right. A training dummy stands near the center of the west wall with a non-respawning large sack nearby. A long work table sits against the north wall near the stairs, holding nothing except a medium-sized coin purse, and another respawning food barrel rests to the right.

## Notes
- The front door has a novice lock, the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Karthwasten_Hall.md) to which is carried by [Ainethach](Skyrim_Ainethach.md).
- Two respawning food barrels can be found next to the steps to the porch, which hosts a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning.md), six more respawning food barrels, and a table holding four bottles of [Nord mead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nord_Mead) and two loaves of [bread](Skyrim_Bread.md).
- If you [marry](Skyrim_Marriage.md) Ainethach, you will have the option of living here.
