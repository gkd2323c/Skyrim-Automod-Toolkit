[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-item-Ring_of_Bloodlust.jpg/200px-SR-item-Ring_of_Bloodlust.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Bloodlust.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Ring_of_Bloodlust.jpg) Ring of Bloodlust The Ring of Bloodlust is a ring that can be bought from [Majni](Skyrim_Majni.md) in [Frostmoon Crag](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Crag.md) if you are a [werewolf](Skyrim_Werewolf.md). Its appearance is identical to a [silver ring](Skyrim_Silver_Ring.md), and also has the same weight. Its enchantment allows you to [deal 50% more damage at the cost of taking 50% more damage in werewolf form](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Bloodlust.md). Multiple copies of these rings can be bought once Majni's merchant chest resets.

## Notes
- While the ring applies the effect *Frostmoon Bloodlust* to display the enchantment text, the actual effect is applied by a perk also called *[Frostmoon Bloodlust](Skyrim_Frostmoon_Bloodlust.md)* and is added separately upon transformation.
