The Order of the Waking Flame (or simply the **Waking Flame**) was a secret cult devoted to the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) of Destruction, [Mehrunes Dagon](Lore_Mehrunes_Dagon.md). The Waking Flame operated during the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md) and worked with the [Longhouse Emperors](Lore_Longhouse_Emperors.md) to create the [Four Ambitions](Lore_Four_Ambitions.md). It is unclear when exactly the cult was founded, but the earliest reference to it dates to the 2E 520s.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>
