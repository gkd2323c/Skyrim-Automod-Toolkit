Hidden Kindred Instructions **by [Blightcrown](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Blightcrown)** *Orders for a cultist plot in Necrom*
---
My fellow kindred,

As the Supreme Plague Master of our glorious order, I command you to follow these instructions to the letter. Bring the Pestilential Lord's implements to [Firewatch](Lore_Firewatch.md) and meet me at the harbor. We will complete our journey to [Necrom](Lore_Necrom.md) aboard the merchant ship Stormwing.

Make sure the vials containing the holy miasma of disease are sealed tight. It's important that [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md)'s breath remains undiluted until we share it with the Necropolis's misguided monks.

I have the robes and cowls you will need in Necrom. I shall pose as a Temple prelate and you will be my attendants. Soon we can search the Necropolis unhindered and find what we seek.

To the glory of Peryite and the Hidden Kindred!
Blightcrown, *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]*
