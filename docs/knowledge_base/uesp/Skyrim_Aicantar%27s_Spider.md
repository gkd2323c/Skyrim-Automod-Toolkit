[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c7/SR-creature-Aicantar%27s_Spider.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Aicantar%27s_Spider.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Aicantar%27s_Spider.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Aicantar%27s_Spider.jpg) Aicantar's Spider **Aicantar's Spider** is a [Dwarven spider](Skyrim_Dwarven_Spider.md) that [Aicantar](Skyrim_Aicantar.md) reassembled and can control with his [spider control rod](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Spider_Control_Rod). Picking up the spider control rod in [Calcelmo's Laboratory](Skyrim_Calcelmo%27s_Laboratory.md) will cause it to pop out of a [Dwarven scuttle](Skyrim_Dwarven_Scuttle.md). It will try to kill anyone who is hostile to the holder of the rod. The rod works like a staff, and the spider will move to the location targeted by the rod.

According to [Aicantar's journal](Skyrim_Aicantar%27s_Lab_Journal.md), found nearby, the guards are afraid of it because of several incidents where Aicantar would become mad at a guard, or a guard would draw a weapon and the spider would try to kill them. It cannot leave Calcelmo's Laboratory.

## Notes
- It appears that the spider control rod always retains its "Stolen" tag even if sold to a Fence and bought back.

## Bugs
- If anyone becomes hostile to you and you move too close to the spider, it will become hostile to you.
- It is possible that you may not be able to pick up the staff, making you unable to control the spider.
