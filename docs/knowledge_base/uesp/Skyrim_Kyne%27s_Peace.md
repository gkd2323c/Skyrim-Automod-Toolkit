Affected creatures will not fight or flee. Additionally, affected creatures will be revealed by an effect similar to [Aura Whisper](Skyrim_Aura_Whisper.md) for the duration of the shout.

## Notes
- Despite this being a "peaceful" shout, there is a slight shock-wave that is emitted that can cause harm. The shock-wave has a rather large impact area and usually do not provoke NPCs (with the random Vigilants of Stendarr in the wild as an exception), making it useful for bringing down hawks.
- The shout penetrates doors and walls.
- This shout can be used to calm animals under the control of [spriggans](Skyrim_Spriggan.md).
- This shout can be used to cause a large mess quickly in heavily decorated areas such as Dragonsreach if you so desire.
- It is useful when the message "You cannot fast travel with enemies nearby" appears, as it will tame any hostile creatures in the area, thus allowing you to fast travel.
