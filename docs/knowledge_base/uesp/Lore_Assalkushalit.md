[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2d/MW-place-Assalkushalit.jpg/200px-MW-place-Assalkushalit.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Assalkushalit.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-place-Assalkushalit.jpg) Assalkushalit circa [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427) Assalkushalit (also known as **Kushalit Sanctuary**) is a [Daedric ruin](Lore_Daedric_Ruins.md) located on the northern side of the [Red Mountain](Lore_Red_Mountain.md), inside the [Ghostfence](Lore_Ghostfence.md), southeast of [Maar Gan](Lore_Maar_Gan.md). The ruin's interior contains a shrine dedicated to [Sheogorath](Lore_Sheogorath.md), [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Madness.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Assalkushalit was recently abandoned and available for purchase as a private residence. It featured a statue to the Mad God within its stony hall and contained numerous portals connecting parts of the complex. It was accessible through the portal located in a small ruined alcove in the northern [Ashlands](Lore_Ashlands.md), south of Valenvaryon.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup>

When the Ghostfence was erected to keep [the Blight](Lore_The_Blight.md) and [Corprus](Lore_Corprus.md) monsters contained within the Red Mountain Region the Assalkushalit was isolated from the rest of the [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AATD-3)</sup><sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>

By [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427), the ruins had become occupied by a trio of hostile worshippers. A cursed ruby and a Devil Longbow were placed on Sheogorath's altar.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-MW-1)</sup>
