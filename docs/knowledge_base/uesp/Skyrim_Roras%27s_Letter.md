Roras's Letter **by Roras** *A bandit's urgings to rob their leader*
---
Come on, it won't be that bad. [Rigel](Skyrim_Rigel_Strong-Arm.md) may be paranoid, but she's as dumb as a [mammoth](Skyrim_Mammoth.md)! Body hair like one too!

I'm just saying if we can get into that [treasure room](Skyrim_Pinewatch.md) we would be set! We could just take the money and head north to catch a ship. I know a guy, whose cousin knows a guy, that could get us set up with some pirates in [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md). And in the meantime we could live high on that money for months!
