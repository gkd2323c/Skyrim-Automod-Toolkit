[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/57/SR-npc-Mogrul_2.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mogrul_2.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogrul_2.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mogrul_2.jpg) Mogrul, with bodyguard behind him **Mogrul** is a money-lending [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Combat Warrior2H) who can be found in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). He often spends his time in the marketplace next to [Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md) by the well. His bodyguard [Slitter](Skyrim_Slitter.md) can be found nearby, watching over him. He can be heard demanding money owed to him by [Garyn Ienth](Skyrim_Garyn_Ienth.md), and collecting poisons on credit from Fethis Alor. He comes to collect the 1000 gold owed to him by [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md) after you help him leave town to become [Neloth](Skyrim_Neloth.md)'s new steward. At noon, he goes to [The Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md) to have lunch until 2pm.

Mogrul wears a set of [tavern clothes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Tavern_Clothes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000d1921). He carries an [Orcish War Axe of Devouring](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_War_Axe_of_Devouring) and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Related Quests
- **[A New Debt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_New_Debt)**: Help [Drovas Relvi](Skyrim_Drovas_Relvi.md) with his debt to Mogrul.

## Dialogue
Mogrul may greet you by saying:

*"I'll give you this warning only once. Stay out of my way, and we'll do just fine."* *"If you're dealing with me, there's only one thing to remember. Pay your debts or end up hurt."* *"I might lend coin to people that need it, but I never lend to strangers. So shove off."* *"I don't know you, and the last thing I want is a stranger with his/her nose in my business. Go away."* *"I can tell you don't like me. Well guess what. I don't give a damn."*
### Reluctant Steward
You have the option of asking him if he'd like to be Tel Mithryn's new steward. He laughs at you mockingly for suggesting that and responds, surprised that anyone would consider such a thing.

**Do you want to be the new steward of Tel Mithryn?** *"Ha, ha, ha! Not on your life! I'd have to be truly desperate to do that."*
### A New Debt

So I guess I'm going to have to collect it from you."* If spoken to again if you walked away:

### Dragonborn
You can ask him about [Miraak](Skyrim_Miraak_(person).md) during [Dragonborn](Skyrim_Dragonborn_(quest).md).

**Do you know someone called Miraak?** *"No. Wait... I recognize the name, but I'm not sure why."* **What does that mean?** *"I don't know... I feel strange just thinking about it. A temple, maybe? Here on the island? Something about a temple..."*
### It's All In The Taste
You have the option of asking him to try [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md)'s new sujamma. Unfortunately, he won't be very receptive to the idea.

**Geldis Sadri wants you to try his new drink.** *"Take that swill and pour it down a well."*
## Conversations
Mogrul may be seen having some conversations with other merchants about their unusual deals at the market, as well as arguing with the local innkeeper. He can be seen confronting [Milore Ienth](Skyrim_Milore_Ienth.md) about her husband [Garyn](Skyrim_Garyn_Ienth.md)'s debts.

**Mogrul:** *"Milore, you need to talk some sense into your husband."* **Milore:** *"You can't get blood from a stone, Mogrul. As it is, we can barely make ends meet."* **Mogrul:** *"That isn't my problem. I was clear when I loaned Garyn the money that I wanted it back by the first harvest."* **Milore:** *"I'll see what I can do..."* He also confronts Garyn about the debt directly.

**Garyn:** *"Oh... Mogrul. Care for anything from my stand today?"* **Mogrul:** *"Ash yams? Disgusting. I'd rather eat cheese that's been sitting in the sun for a week."* **Garyn:** *"Look, I know why you're here. I'll get the coin, I promise."* **Mogrul:** *"If you want that beautiful wife of yours to stay that way, you'd better."* He can be seen approaching [Fethis Alor](Skyrim_Fethis_Alor.md)'s shop to pick up some poison.

**Fethis:** *"Mogrul... I got those special poisons you asked for."* **Mogrul:** *"Why don't you say that louder so the entire town can hear you, idiot."* **Mogrul:** *"Just give them to me and put it on my account."* **Fethis:** *"About your account... it's been a few months..."* **Mogrul:** *"I said to put it on my account and that's the end of it. Understand?"* Mogrul also argues with [Geldis Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md).

**Geldis:** *"Mogrul, I thought I told you I didn't want you hanging around here."* **Mogrul:** *"Yeah, I heard you before. I just don't care. You won't kick me out of here, and we both know it."* **Geldis:** *"Look, we've been over this. I paid you off, I don't owe you a thing."* **Mogrul:** *"And I'm supposed to run my operation outside in a pile of ash? I don't think so. I'm not asking you if I can stay, I'm telling you. Now that's the end of it."* Mogrul can sometimes be under [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md)'s suspicion about his activities.

**Veleth:** *"Hold a moment, Mogrul. I've gotten a report that you've been harassing some of the townsfolk. Is this true?"* **Mogrul:** *"Oh, come on. You know me, Captain Veleth. My hands are clean."* **Veleth:** *"See that they stay that way, or else I'll send you back to Skyrim in a crate. You understand me?"* **Mogrul:** *"Yes, sir, Captain Veleth, sir."*
## Notes
- Mogrul is the only male in the game who wears the `Clothes Tavern Wench Outfit`. - It's very likely that Mogrul originally wouldn't have worn these clothes, as there is an unused outfit set up for him in the [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) called `DLC2Mogrul Outfit`. This outfit would've made him wear [Orcish armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Armor) along with a pair of [Orcish boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Boots).
- Mogrul owns a bed in the [Retching Netch](Skyrim_The_Retching_Netch.md), but has no AI packages set up to make him sleep, making it go unused. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch) addresses this issue. A sleep package has been added for him; he will now sleep in his bed in the Retching Netch from 1:00 am to 7:00 am daily.
- Mogrul's combat class is set to the two-handed `Combat Warrior2H` despite him carrying a one-handed weapon. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Legendary Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Legendary_Edition_Patch), version 3.0.5, addresses this issue. His combat class has been changed to `Combat Warrior1H`.

## Bugs
- If you reanimate Mogrul's corpse, his bodyguard Slitter will continue to follow it. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 1.0.5, fixes this bug.
- Mogrul may become permanently hostile toward you. Depending on your progression through the add-on, he may be essential. This can lead to a continual fight with him whenever you are in Raven Rock. - [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) You can use the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) commands `prid [xx](Skyrim_Form_ID.md) 01959B` followed by `resetai` to temporarily make him non-hostile, allowing him to initiate conversation and start [A New Debt](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:A_New_Debt). The next time he becomes hostile, he will no longer be essential and you will be able to kill him.
