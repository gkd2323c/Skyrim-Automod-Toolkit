## *[Part One](Lore_Khunzar-ri_and_the_Demon__Part_One.md)*
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) were barely more than sixteen tribes, the great hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) went on many adventures. After Khunzar-ri defeated the [giant](Lore_Giant.md) Drol by consuming four-hundred-and-one [moon-sugar](Lore_Moon-sugar.md) pies, he met a [demon](Lore_Dragon.md).

The [demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) said, "My brothers and I shall take this land and its treasures. Even the moons from the sky!" Khunzar-ri objected, but the demon laughed. "You cannot stop us, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md)!"

Khunzar-ri said, "Not by myself, foul demon. I shall gather a Kra'Jun—a company of heroes—starting with the [Altmer](Lore_Altmer.md), [Nurarion](Lore_Nurarion.md) the Perfect, who communes among the buried roots of the Ayleid worms."

These are the words and the words are true.

## *[Part Two](Lore_Khunzar-ri_and_the_Demon__Part_Two.md)*
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) were young and like kittens at play, the great hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) gathered a company of champions to combat the [demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his [brothers](Lore_Dragon.md). They had come to [Elsweyr](Lore_Elsweyr.md) to steal the moons from the sky.

With [Nurarion](Lore_Nurarion.md) already at his side, Khunzar-ri turned his attention to the next champion he planned to recruit. "The power of the moons will be ours, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md)," taunted the demon. "How can two possibly hope to stop many?

Khunzar-ri said, "Because two shall become three, annoying demon. As soon as this one rescues [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md) from the Ayleid lair of creeping ebony plants."

These are the words, and the words are true.

## *[Part Three](Lore_Khunzar-ri_and_the_Demon__Part_Three.md)*
In the time before time, when the [sixteen kingdoms](Lore_Elsweyr.md#The_Sixteen_Kingdoms) roamed with wild abandon, the hero [Khunzar-ri](Lore_Khunzar-ri.md) gathered champions to deal with a [terrible demon](Lore_Kaalgrontiid.md) and his horde. With [Nurarion the Perfect](Lore_Nurarion_the_Perfect.md) and [Flinthild Demon-Hunter](Lore_Flinthild_Demon-Hunter.md), he sought a third companion.

The demon said, "Oh, little [cat-thing](Lore_Khajiit.md), you amuse us! How can three tiny morsels ever stand against us?" Khunzar-ri laughed, "We may be tiny, but we are persistent! And tricky, too, as you will see."

Then Khunzar-ri turned to his Kra'Jun. "One more we need, a Khajiiti queen of renown. [Anequina Sharp-Tongue](Lore_Anequina_Sharp-Tongue.md), who loves to bask in the [glow of the Big Moon](Lore_Jode%27s_Light.md) when she reflects and thinks deep thoughts."

These are the words and the words are true.
