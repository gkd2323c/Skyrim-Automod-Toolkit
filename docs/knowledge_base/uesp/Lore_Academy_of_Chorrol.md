# Academy of Chorrol

The Academy of Chorrol was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution based in [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md), active at some point before the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) in the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). Little is known of the academy, except that one of its scholars, [Juno Procillus](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Juno_Procillus), interviewed [Reachfolk Shaman](Lore_Reachman_Shamans.md) [Uraccanach](Lore_Uraccanach.md) of the [Druadach Mountains](Lore_Druadach_Mountains.md) and transcribed his words, publishing a text devoted to the [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachmen.md) understanding of the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Prince.md) [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md) named *[Aspects of Lord Hircine](Lore_Aspects_of_Lord_Hircine.md)* and one describing the clothing and weaponry associated with his [Daedric](Lore_Daedra.md) servants, the [Huntsmen](Lore_Huntsman.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-AOLH-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CM65-2)</sup>
