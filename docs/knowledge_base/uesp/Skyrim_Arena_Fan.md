[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5c/SR-npc-Arena_Fan.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Arena_Fan.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arena_Fan.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Arena_Fan.jpg) The corpse of the Arena Fan The Arena Fan is a deceased [Bosmer](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) found at a camp southeast of [Ivarstead](Skyrim_Ivarstead.md), southwest of [Geirmund's Hall](Skyrim_Geirmund%27s_Hall.md), and northeast of [Honeystrand Cave](Skyrim_Honeystrand_Cave.md). A [note](Skyrim_Arena_Fan%27s_Note.md) is found on his body that details how he journeyed to Skyrim in search of a former Bloodletter of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) Arena, [Urgnok](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Urgnok).

He is garbed in simple [clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0006C1D8) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md). In addition to his aforementioned note, he also carries a copy of *[The Crimson Dirks, v8](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Crimson_Dirks,_v8)*.
