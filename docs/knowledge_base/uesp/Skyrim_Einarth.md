[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c1/SR-npc-Einarth.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Einarth.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Einarth.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Einarth.jpg) Einarth **Einarth**, an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [priest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Priest), is one of the [Greybeards](Skyrim_Greybeards.md) living at [High Hrothgar](Skyrim_High_Hrothgar.md). He will teach you the second [word](Skyrim_Word_of_Power.md) of the Unrelenting Force shout. At the end of the quest [Alduin's Wall](Skyrim_Alduin%27s_Wall.md), Einarth can be heard lecturing [Arngeir](Skyrim_Arngeir.md) when the latter refuses to help you in your quest to learn the [Dragonrend](Skyrim_Dragonrend.md) shout, saying: *"Arngeir. Rok los Dovahkiin, Strundu'ul. Rok fen tinvaak Paarthurnax."*

Einarth wears a set of [Greybeard's robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Robes) with the matching [hood](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Hood) and [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Greybeard%27s_Boots). He carries a copy of the book *[Dwemer History and Culture](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dwemer_History_and_Culture)*, a bottle of [Black-Briar Reserve](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Black-Briar_Reserve), a [horker tusk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Horker_Tusk), one sample of [juniper berries](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Juniper_Berries), and three [leather strips](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leather_Strips).

He knows the following shouts: [Disarm](Skyrim_Disarm.md), [Dismay](Skyrim_Dismay.md), [Elemental Fury](Skyrim_Elemental_Fury.md), [Fire Breath](Skyrim_Fire_Breath.md), [Frost Breath](Skyrim_Frost_Breath.md), [Ice Form](Skyrim_Ice_Form.md), [Unrelenting Force](Skyrim_Unrelenting_Force.md), and [Whirlwind Sprint](Skyrim_Whirlwind_Sprint.md).
