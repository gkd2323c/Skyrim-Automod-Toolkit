## Quick Walkthrough
1. A [challenger](Skyrim_Challenger.md) appears.
2. Meet the challenger.
3. Accept or refuse the challenge.
4. Kill the challenger or flee.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Once you have a high level of skill in any [magic](Skyrim_Magicka.md) school, upon entering a habitable location or exiting an inn after sleeping, a [challenger](Skyrim_Challenger.md) may appear and challenge you to a duel. Regardless of your answer, a combat ensues, however if you try to back out by saying "sorry", any nearby guards will help you with your duel.

## Notes
- If you do not use magic to fight the mage, he will taunt you by saying: "This is a mage's duel. You know magic!"
- This can lead to expulsion from the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(faction).md), but there is a [rejoin quest](Skyrim_Rejoining_the_College.md) available. <sup>[*verification needed — "This" quest or "This" note? (the first note)*]</sup>
- If any of your companions join in on the duel, he will order them to stop, explaining that it is supposed to be a one-on-one duel. This does not seem to affect the quest in any way, and companions will not abide to his demands. The same happens if other NPCs nearby are hit by his spells.

## Bugs
- If you enter another cell while fighting against the Challenger, you will lose their location and be unable to finish the mini-quest. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.
- If you fight him with conjured weapons, the Challenger will taunt you as if you weren't fighting him using magic.
