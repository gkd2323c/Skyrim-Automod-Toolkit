Habd's Death Letter **by [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md)** *A Redguard's final wishes should he fail to avenge his family*
---
If you have found this note, then I have failed to kill the creatures that invaded my home and killed my family. Please, avenge us if you are able. Kill every last one of these [blighted monsters](Skyrim_Falmer.md) and take what I've left in the chest on the roof of the [Lighthouse](Skyrim_Frostflow_Lighthouse.md).

I won't be needing it.

- [Habd](Skyrim_Habd.md)
