# Census and Excise

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/64/MW-npc-Socucius_Ergalla.jpg/200px-MW-npc-Socucius_Ergalla.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Socucius_Ergalla.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:MW-npc-Socucius_Ergalla.jpg) Socucius Ergalla, chief agent of the Seyda Neen Census and Excise Office Census and Excise (also stylized as **Imperial Board of Census and Excise**<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TCT-1)</sup> and ** Imperial Census**)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Quintus-2)</sup> is an establishment operated by the Empire in an attempt to manage census counting for tax/excise purposes. Imperial taxes are based on the headcounts of citizens; with the Census and Excise being responsible for headcounts, tax assessment, and collection. Census and Excise officers also administer many Imperial licenses, e.g. importation, mercenary company incorporation.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup>

The Census and Excise have a coastguard that has a fleet of cutters. They are typically found in ports across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), such as [Seyda Neen](Lore_Seyda_Neen.md) in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md). The Census coastguard cutters docked in Seyda Neen control smuggling and piracy on the [Inner Sea](Lore_Inner_Sea.md), while the Imperial Census and Excise Commission agents of the Coastguard station process visitors.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> One known office is in the town of Slough Point in [Blackwood](Lore_Blackwood.md), with it being described as one of the largest buildings in the town.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

The killing of an Emperor's tax collector is punishable by death.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup>
