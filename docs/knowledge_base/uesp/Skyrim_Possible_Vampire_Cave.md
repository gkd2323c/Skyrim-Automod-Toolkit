Possible Vampire Cave **by [Durian](Skyrim_Durian.md)** *An observer verifies the location of a vampire hideout*
---
The information we got from the mole was spot on. At first glance, it looked like any ordinary cave.

I was ready to turn around and give up for the night, when up walk these nobles with furs fine enough to sleep on. I watched them enter the cave one by one, until there was one guy left. Then I tapped him on the shoulder, and rammed my fist so hard into his forehead that I heard his skull crunch.

He'll be all right though. But he's gonna need a new tunic, I heard a thread rip.
