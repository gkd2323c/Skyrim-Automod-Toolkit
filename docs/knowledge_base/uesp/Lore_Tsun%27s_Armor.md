# Tsun's Armor

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/46/SR-item-Tsun%27s_Armor.jpg/200px-SR-item-Tsun%27s_Armor.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Armor.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Tsun%27s_Armor.jpg) Tsun's Armor worn by a mortal [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-npc-Tsun.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tsun.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsun.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tsun.jpg) Tsun donning his armor Tsun's Armor is an artifact associated with [Tsun](Lore_Tsun.md). His attire consists of armored boots, a torc, reinforced bracers, and a wide belt with a large engraved metal buckle worn over a layered kilt with hip-protecting metal plates. It kept his chest bare and exposed.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Tsun1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Tsun2-2)</sup>

It was donned by Tsun as he fought those seeking to cross the Whalebone Bridge in [Sovngarde](Lore_Sovngarde.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Tsun-3)</sup> though it could also be found beyond [Aetherius](Lore_Aetherius.md) and worn by mortals. When worn, it restored their magicka and mended their wounds, though it also exposed them to danger.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-TAG2-4)</sup>

At some point between [4E 176](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_176) and [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201), a [Sheathed Blade](Lore_Sheathed_Blades.md) came upon the armor in the hands of a powerful [Falmer](Lore_Falmer.md) Shadowmaster.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-TAG-5)</sup>
