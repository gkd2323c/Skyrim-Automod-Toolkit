# House Spenard

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/78/ON-furnishing-Riven_King%27s_Throne.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Riven_King%27s_Throne.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Riven_King%27s_Throne.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Riven_King%27s_Throne.jpg) Spenard's throne of the Riven King House Spenard was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) royal dynasty that ruled [Shornhelm](Lore_Shornhelm.md) sometime around or after the ninth century of the [First Era](Lore_First_Era.md).<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Pale)</sup> House Spenard and its vassals were commonly accused of having [vampirism](Lore_Vampirism.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONRKT-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONOEGR-2)</sup>

## History
House Spenard ruled Shornhelm for one hundred and eighty-six years, starting with King Frestrien Spenard I and ending with King Frestrien Spenard VI. They were remembered as a despotic dynasty. While there have been many despot kings in Rivenspire, none lived up to the reign of the Spenards. Due to House Spenard's misrule, its territory was in a perpetual state of famine.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONRKT-1)</sup> [House Aurmine](Lore_House_Aurmine.md) was one of their vassals.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONAAS-3)</sup> House Spernard was dissolved after a reign of one hundred and eighty-six years by [House Dorell](Lore_House_Dorell.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONRKT-1)</sup>

A collection of documents described the line of Frestrien as bearing stern features that appeared wrought from pale marble. [Amalien](Lore_Amalien.md), a scholar of the [University of Gwylim](Lore_University_of_Gwylim.md), claimed that all six King Frestrien Spenards were actually the same individual and that he was a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONRKT-1)</sup> Their vassals were commonly accused of being vampires.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONOEGR-2)</sup>

By the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Spenard was a somewhat common surname among [Bretons](Lore_Breton.md).<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-S1-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-S2-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-S3-6)</sup>
