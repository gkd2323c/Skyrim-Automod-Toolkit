## Cannons
Cannons are large, heavy pieces of artillery.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Jokes-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-IS-2)</sup> Certain [Dwemer animunculi](Lore_Dwemer_Animunculi.md) such as architons,<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONDAS-3)</sup> [arquebuses](Lore_Dwarven_Sphere.md),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONDA-4)</sup> [colossi](Lore_Dwarven_Colossus.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONFBG-5)</sup> [delvers](Lore_Dwarven_Delver.md),<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VIDF-6)</sup> and similar constructs such as [factotums](Lore_Factotum.md),<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONReclaimer-7)</sup> possess built-in cannons on their arms that can shoot out flames or bolts of lightning.
