[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Gold Sapphire Ring.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-jewelry-Gold Sapphire Ring.png) Ahzidal's Ring of Necromancy Ahzidal's Ring of Necromancy is a ring that is part of a set of armor created by the legendary [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) enchanter [Ahzidal](Skyrim_Ahzidal_(creature).md). It can be found in [Kolbjorn Barrow](Skyrim_Kolbjorn_Barrow.md), after the second phase of digging is completed during [Unearthed](Skyrim_Unearthed.md). The ring's appearance is identical to a [gold sapphire ring](Skyrim_Gold_Sapphire_Ring.md) and also has the same weight. Its enchantment makes your reanimated explode for 50 points of frost damage. In addition, if you wear this ring along with three other relics of Ahzidal, you will receive a buff called [Ahzidal's Genius](Skyrim_Ahzidal%27s_Genius.md) that increases your [Enchanting skill](Skyrim_Enchanting.md) by ten points.

## Bugs
- Having the Conjuration perk [Dark Souls](Skyrim_Dark_Souls.md) prevents the effect from functioning, since it takes priority when applying bonus effects to reanimated creatures. <sup>**?**</sup> - This can be remedied by making Conjuration [legendary](Skyrim_Skills.md#Legendary_Skills) or using the Black Book [Waking Dreams](Skyrim_Black_Book__Waking_Dreams_(book).md) in order to remove the perk.
- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) The perk can be removed using the [console](Skyrim_Console.md) by entering `player.removeperk 000581de`.
- If you are vampire and take damage from others, the ring's effect will cause you to explode and die. <sup>**?**</sup>
