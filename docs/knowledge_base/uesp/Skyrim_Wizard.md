Ha!"* The **Wizard** is a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) who can be [randomly encountered](Skyrim_World_Interactions.md#Beneficial_Encounters) on [Solstheim](Skyrim_Solstheim.md). He can initially be heard muttering to himself, *"They said it couldn't be done. They said it. They said it. But they were wrong!"*, *"There's nothing to worry about, nothing to worry about at all..."*, *"The book in my dream showed me..."*, and *"To touch the sky like a dragon..."* before casting his experimental flying spell.

If you approach him, he casts his spell and then shoots straight up into the sky, shouting, *"It's working, I'm flying! I'm really flying! Hahahaha!"* As the spell wears off, he exclaims, *"What?! Ooooohhhhhh Nooooooooooo!!!!!"* He eventually falls to the ground and dies on impact. The *[Journal of a Madman](Skyrim_Journal_of_a_Madman.md)* can be found on his corpse.

He wears [apprentice robes of Alteration](Skyrim_Apprentice_Robes_of_Alteration.md) with an [apprentice hood](Skyrim_Apprentice_Hood.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c), and he carries a leveled [staff](Skyrim_Staves.md).

## Notes
- This NPC is a reference to [Tarhiel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Tarhiel) and his [Scrolls of Icarian Flight](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Scroll_of_Icarian_Flight). This predicament has become a [running gag](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Running_Gags#Icarus) in the series.
- This world interaction can only occur once.

## Bugs
- The wizard will sometimes survive the fall if you shout *[Slow Time](Skyrim_Slow_Time.md)* before he hits the ground. - If this happens and you wish to read his journal, you can still pickpocket it from him.
- You can also just kill him and loot him. No bounty is issued because it doesn't count as murder. <sup>**?**</sup>
