## Ingredients
8 guaranteed samples can be found in 6 different locations:

- 2 centered 530 ft NE of the [Pale Imperial Camp](Skyrim_Pale_Imperial_Camp.md) ([The Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md)) [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?x=6818&y=108172)
- 2 around [Riverside Shack](Skyrim_Riverside_Shack.md) ([Eastmarch](Skyrim_Eastmarch.md))
- 1 in [Driftshade Refuge](Skyrim_Driftshade_Refuge.md) ([Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold_(region).md))
- 1 in [Fort Snowhawk](Skyrim_Fort_Snowhawk.md) ([Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md))
- 1 in [Riften Fishery](Skyrim_Riften_Fishery.md) ([Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md))
- 1 in [Sinderion's Field Laboratory](Skyrim_Sinderion%27s_Field_Laboratory.md) ([Blackreach](Skyrim_Blackreach.md))

This ingredient is **not** in the `Vendor Item Ingredient` category that allows it to be traded by apothecary merchants (see [bugs](#Bugs)).

The only way to make this ingredient become available from apothecary merchants is to unlock the [Merchant](Skyrim_Merchant_(perk).md) perk (requires level 50 [Speech](Skyrim_Speech.md)), at which point it is a "common" ingredient.

Remaining ways to obtain already-harvested mudcrab chitin all have relatively low probabilities. The best bets are:

- [Peddlers](Skyrim_Peddler.md) may be randomly encountered in the wilderness (typically being attacked). These peddlers have a 25% chance of carrying 1-5 samples.
- 14% chance of 1-3 samples being carried by any [Silver Hand](Skyrim_Silver_Hand.md) member.
- [Vaermina devotees](Skyrim_Vaermina_Devotee.md) have a 3% chance of carrying one.
- All types of [apothecary's satchels](Skyrim_Apothecary%27s_Satchel.md) as well as Pots hanging from cooking splits have a 3% chance of containing one.
- [Warlocks](Skyrim_Warlock.md) have a 0.9% chance of carrying one.

## Creatures
All [mudcrabs](Skyrim_Mudcrab.md), including the [Mudcrab Guardian Spirit](Skyrim_Mudcrab_Guardian_Spirit.md), will drop one or two samples of mudcrab chitin, depending on their size.

## Bugs
- This ingredient cannot normally be bought from apothecary merchants, even though it should be available according to their merchandise leveled lists. It also cannot be sold to apothecary merchants; it can only be sold to general goods merchants. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2, fixes this bug.
