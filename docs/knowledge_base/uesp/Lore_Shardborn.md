# Shardborn

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/ca/ON-prerelease-Scions_of_Ithelia.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Scions_of_Ithelia.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Scions_of_Ithelia.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Scions_of_Ithelia.jpg) Shardborn *I remember our realm—Mirrormoor! Ithelia's servants awaken!*
—Torvesard<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Torvesard Quote-1)</sup> The Shardborn are a clan of [Dremora](Lore_Dremora.md) in service to [Ithelia](Lore_Ithelia.md), the [Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) of Paths. In battle they don armor of fractured glass, molded with [creatia](Lore_Chaotic_Creatia.md) by the clan's master [glass-smiths](Lore_Smithing.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> The Shardborn are native to the realm of [Mirrormoor](Lore_Mirrormoor.md), a plane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md) that was shattered and forgotten in the [Merethic Era](Lore_Merethic_Era.md) when [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) imprisoned Ithelia and wiped all memory of her from existence.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

The Shardborn re-emerged from obscurity in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) when [Torvesard](Lore_Torvesard.md) succeeded in restoring his memory of Ithelia and her realm of Mirrormoor. This knowledge allowed him to awaken the servants of Ithelia, with the Shardborn counted among them.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Torvesard Quote-1)</sup> The Shardborn invaded the Mythos of [Apocrypha](Lore_Apocrypha.md) in response to his call.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> They subsequently aided the champions of Mirrormoor in invading [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), summoned from Oblivion by ancient Mirrormoor mosaics dotted throughout the [West Weald](Lore_West_Weald.md).<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> They were led by scions of Ithelia such as Torvesard and Shardmarshal Vargas, who were imbued with a desire to restore the memory of their forgotten Prince.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Ithelia Fot FP-7)</sup>

Following Torvesard's defeat by [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), his [essence](Lore_Vestige_(soul).md) was consigned to nothingness by Ithelia, destroying him and thereby releasing him from service.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Ithelia Fot FP-7)</sup> Ithelia was subsequently banished to the [Many Paths](Lore_Many_Paths.md), allowing Hermaeus Mora to once again wipe all memory of her existence.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> Torvesard and the Shardborn were remembered only as rogue Dremora.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-9)</sup>
