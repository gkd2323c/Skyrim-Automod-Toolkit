The Ulen Matter **by [Vendil Ulen](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md)** *Plans to assassinate the Councilor of Raven Rock* *
---
Dear Councilor Saldin,

In a manner *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* of days I believe we'll be ready. Our forces hidden within [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md) have been training night and day, ready to strike when we give the signal. With [Captain Veleth](Skyrim_Captain_Veleth.md) distracted by the [ash spawn](Skyrim_Ash_Spawn.md) attacks, the timing seems perfect. I've waited nearly a decade to exact my revenge upon [Lleril Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) for the death of my ancestor and I long for the moment my blade will be drawn across his throat. The next letter you'll recieve *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* from me will include his head in a sack. Display it proudly in the halls of House Hlaalu, brother.

[Vendil Ulen](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md)
