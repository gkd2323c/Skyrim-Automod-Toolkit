# Second Legion

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fc/ON-crown_store-Second_Legion.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Second_Legion.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Second_Legion.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Second_Legion.jpg) Second Legion legionaries [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9d/ON-crown_store-Second_Legion_Arms_Pack.jpg/200px-ON-crown_store-Second_Legion_Arms_Pack.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Second_Legion_Arms_Pack.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-crown_store-Second_Legion_Arms_Pack.jpg) Second Legion arms The Second Legion is one of the many legions that make up the [Imperial Legion](Lore_Imperial_Legion.md), the militant arm of the [Empire](Lore_Empire.md). The Second Legion has a storied history from the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), especially when they served [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) in his [open rebellion](Lore_Varen%27s_Rebellion.md) against Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWATD-1)</sup> The Imperial War [Mastiff](Lore_Dog.md#Mastiff) is the mascot and symbol of the Second Legion.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMastiff-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3c/ON-card-Imperial_War_Mastiff.png/200px-ON-card-Imperial_War_Mastiff.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-card-Imperial_War_Mastiff.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-card-Imperial_War_Mastiff.png) An Imperial War Mastiff At some point, the Second Legion, specifically the 33rd Cohort, was stationed in southeast [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md), in an underground fort called Sanctuary Chimera.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-AALL-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-SFA-4)</sup>

By the mid-Second Era, the Second Legion was led by General Nipia.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MOTEC-5)</sup> During Emperor [Leovic](Lore_Leovic.md)'s reign, they were stationed in the [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md). When the Emperor issued the [Great Spirits Proclamation](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Leovic%27s_Great_Spirits_Proclamation), Duke [Varen Aquilarios](Lore_Varen_Aquilarios.md) of [Kvatch](Lore_Kvatch.md) assembled most of the Second Legion to fight his rebellion in the heartland of Cyrodiil. A single cohort of the legion was left under the command of Varen's nephew, [Carolus Aquilarios](Lore_Carolus_Aquilarios.md), to protect Kvatch.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWATD-1)</sup>

During [Varen's rebellion](Lore_Colovian_Revolt.md), the Second Legion used a cave known as Legion's Rest, located between [Chorrol](Lore_Chorrol.md) and [Skingrad](Lore_Skingrad.md), as a rest stop.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONLoad Rest-6)</sup> After an initial defeat at [Fort Ash](Lore_Fort_Ash.md), Varen recruited the Legion of Chorrol, enabling his armies to defeat Leovic's legions and force them to retreat into the [Heartlands](Lore_Heartlands.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-EFEV-7)</sup> Once the battle had encompassed central Cyrodiil, Varen and his rebellion took [Bruma](Lore_Bruma.md) as their headquarters.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-COTFC5-8)</sup> In [2E 577](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_577), Varen and forces stormed the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) and killed Leovic; Varen then declared himself emperor by right of conquest.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-SHot LE-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-COTCF1-10)</sup>

After Leovic's death, his advisors, the [Icereach Coven](Lore_Icereach_Coven.md), led by Mother [Ciannait](Lore_Ciannait.md), retreated back north to their [island home](Lore_Icereach.md) in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md),<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-ONArvnir-11)</sup> but the Second Legion, led by Optio Cornelia Midara, pursued them. They tracked the coven to [Falkreath Hold](Lore_Falkreath_Hold.md), where they fought battles to the north and west. The Legion saw major casualties on both fronts, and the Optio ordered an immediate withdrawal back to the [Jerall Mountains](Lore_Jerall_Mountains.md) for further orders. Midara then prepared a report for Second Legion High Command and advised them not to pursue the coven any further.<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-MTCCiannait-12)</sup>

Shortly after Varen left for the Cyrodilic heartland, the Imperial Prefect of [Anvil](Lore_Anvil.md) and the Count of Anvil, [Ephrem Benirus](Lore_Ephrem_Benirus.md), moved to seize Varen's property and holdings. Carolus assembled his cohort of the Second Legion and the Kvatch Warders and intercepted the Imperial forces on the plains outside the city. The battle ended in a swift victory for Carolus, and the remnants of the Imperial forces retreated to Anvil. Anvil attacked three more times that year, but Kvatch repelled them each time and left Anvil with fewer and fewer surviving troops. The conflict was settled when Varen was crowned Emperor, and Carolus became the Count of Kvatch.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TWATD-1)</sup>

With Anvil's defenses in tatters, the pirate captain [Fortunata ap Dugal](Lore_Fortunata_ap_Dugal.md) was able to capture the city with ease and then declare herself as Provincial Governor of Anvil. Following the [Soulburst](Lore_Soulburst.md) of [2E 579](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_579) and the disappearance of Varen, the Pirate Queen began harassing Kvatch, and she eventually declared her intention to annex the city and sent the Anvil Guard to take control of it. Carolus responded by sending out the Second Legion cohort to confront the Anvil Guard. The opposing forces met on the Gold Road, near the [Gottshaw Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gottshaw_Inn), where the Second Legion cohort scattered the undisciplined Anvil guards. However, an army of pirates then ambushed and slaughtered the cohort in what became known as the Gottshaw Massacre. Without the support of the cohort, Carolus had no choice but to swear fealty to an independent Gold Coast.<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-TWATPQ-13)</sup>

The greater part of the Second Legion that followed Varen established their peacetime headquarters in Bruma's vicinity.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONMastiff-2)</sup> At some point during the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md), on the 16th of [Second Seed](Lore_Second_Seed.md), General Nipia of the Second Legion attended an [Elder Council](Lore_Elder_Council.md) meeting in which he raised concerns about a conspiracy of traitors among the Imperial Legion. Before he could press further about it, the council meeting was adjourned.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-MOTEC-5)</sup>
