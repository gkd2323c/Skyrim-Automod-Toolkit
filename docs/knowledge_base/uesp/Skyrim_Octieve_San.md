[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/87/SR-npc-Octieve_San.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Octieve_San.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Octieve_San.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Octieve_San.jpg) Octieve San **Octieve San** is an elderly [Breton](Skyrim_Breton.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) living in [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md), frequently found in [the Winking Skeever](Skyrim_The_Winking_Skeever.md). He lives with his daughter, [Evette San](Skyrim_Evette_San.md), in [her house](Skyrim_Evette_San%27s_House.md). He is often critical of his own age, and he drinks and gambles heavily. Upon chatting with him he may complain about gambling debts owed to [Irnskar Ironhand](Skyrim_Irnskar_Ironhand.md), which you can assist him with in exchange for a free [Two-handed](Skyrim_Two-handed.md) skill boost. He will also start to [like](Skyrim_Disposition.md) you, and becomes a potential candidate for [marriage](Skyrim_Marriage.md).

When you visit Solitude for the first time, Octieve will be one of the spectators of [Roggvir](Skyrim_Roggvir.md)'s execution. After this, he will assume his normal schedule: upon waking up at 6am, he starts his day with a trip to the [Temple of the Divines](Skyrim_Temple_of_the_Divines.md) to pray there. At noon, he goes to the Winking Skeever and enjoys a six-hour meal until 6pm, then heads outside to carouse in front of the inn. He returns home at 2am to sleep.

Octieve wears a set of [studded armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Studded_Armor) and a pair of [hide boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hide_Boots). He wields an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) and carries a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic), the [key](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Key_to_Evette_San%27s_House) to his daughter's house, and a selection of [common items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings can be:

*"Take my advice. Never gamble. Damn Irnskar has me in debt up to the eyes."* **(If A Few Words with You has not been completed yet)** *"Being old's not so bad. Daughter keeps me fed, and my working days are done."* *"I'm too old to work, but that don't make me too old to drink."* *"You got nothin' better to do than bother an old man?"* *"You want to know the one truth I've learned in all my years? Life's too hard to face sober."* When you bid farewell with him, he will say:

*"You should speak to my daughter, Evette. She's the brains of the family."* **(If Evette is alive)** *"Always good talking to you... friend."*
## Quest-Related Events

### A Few Words with You
You can ask him if he is in debt with Irnskar and offer to help:

**You're in debt to Irnskar?** *"Too many drinking games, too many bets, and I'm too old to ever raise the coin on my own."* **What if I talk to Irnskar?** *"He's a stubborn oaf. I don't know what good it'll do."* **Sorry to hear that.** *"I'm sure. I'm sure."* Once Irnskar has agreed to forgive Octieve's debt, inform him of the good news:

**Irnskar's agreed to forgive your debt.** *"You've done a fine thing for me. You know, I used to be quite a warrior in my day. Let me show you a thing or two."* He awards you with a one-point increase in your Two-handed skill.

## Conversations
At home, he can be heard conversing with Evette:

**Evette**: *"Father, could you clean up the house a little while I'm away?"*
**Octieve**: *"Maybe. I'll see if I feel like it."*
**Evette**: *"I'm on my feet all day. Can you please do this one thing for me?"*
**Octieve**: *"Sure, whatever."* **Octieve**: *"Where've you been all day?"*
**Evette**: *"At the market, father."*
**Octieve**: *"Well, did you bring back any mead?"*
**Evette**: *"I barely make enough for us to eat as it is, father. You can't just drink it all away."*
**Octieve**: *"Humph. Some daughter you are."*
