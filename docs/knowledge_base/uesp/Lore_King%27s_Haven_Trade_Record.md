King's Haven Trade Record *Trading records between the elves and the goblins*
---
We have secured ongoing trade agreements with nearly every tribe in the [region](Lore_King%27s_Haven_Pass.md). The bounties of the sea have proven most appealing to these landlocked peoples, but textiles and tools are also in steady demand. In exchange we receive the fruits of their hunting and trapping. These tribes are adept at capturing, and even taming, the [welwas](Lore_Welwa.md) and [gryphons](Lore_Gryphon.md) that populate the area.

Even so, we benefit most from bartering for the unrefined ore being extracted from the caverns that wind throughout these [hills](Lore_Eton_Nir.md). The locals don't hold it in high value and seem to prefer to trade the raw ore for our refined goods.
