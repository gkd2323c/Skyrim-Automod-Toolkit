## Walkthrough
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/3d/SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_02.jpg) There be pirates! The three pirates, comprising a [pirate](Skyrim_Pirate.md), a [pirate mage](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_Mage), and their [pirate captain](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pirate_Captain), will attack as soon as they detect you. Once you have taken care of them, you may loot the pirate captain for the [Deathbrand Treasure Map](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_Treasure_Map). Obtaining the map will complete the miscellaneous quest to search for the treasure of [Haknir Death-Brand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Haknir_Death-Brand) and initiate the quest [Deathbrand](Skyrim_Deathbrand_(quest).md).

A campfire is in the middle of the camp, with a wooden plate holding a [venison chop](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Venison_Chop) beside the fire. There are three tents pitched around the fire. The tent nearest the small boat contains a bedroll only, and to the left of the tent is a firewood pile, a [woodcutter's axe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Woodcutter%27s_Axe), and a medium [coin purse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Coin_Purse). The middle tent contains a bedroll and a wooden plate holding a [grilled chicken breast](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Chicken_Breast) and a [baked potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Baked_Potatoes), while at the back of the tent is an expert-locked [East Empire Company strongbox](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:East_Empire_Company_Strongbox). To the west of this tent is a mead barrel on a rock with a random potion, three sacks, and a random healing potion beside it. The tent nearest land contains a bedroll, a bottle of [ale](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ale), a pair of random light [boots](Skyrim_Armor.md#Boots), and two food sacks. Outside this tent on the left is a [knapsack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Knapsack).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1e/SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_03.jpg/200px-SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Haknir%27s_Shoal_03.jpg) The treasure To the northwest of the campfire is an expert-locked ancient chest containing the [Deathbrand Helm](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Deathbrand_Helm). There are two [orichalcum ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein) in the area: the first is west-northwest of the ancient chest, and the other is farther north at the tip of the isle. Scattered around the isle are nine [spiky grass](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ashen_Grass_Pod) plants, a [Nirnroot](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nirnroot) plant, three [pearl oysters](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Pearl_Oyster), and to the northeast a [clam](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam) that can be harvested for [clam meat](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Clam_Meat). There is a small boat at the edge of the water just northeast of the fire, with a random [light armor](Skyrim_Light_Armor.md) [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md), two crates, four sacks, and a random [mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mace) inside.

On the shoreline opposite the shoal are more spiky grass plants, two more Nirnroot plants, and a [horker](Skyrim_Horker.md).
