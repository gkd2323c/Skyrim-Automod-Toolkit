Conversation with Antelas, Wood Elven scout **by [Antelas](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Antelas)** *A Bosmer's thoughts on the Alliance War*
---
[![ON-concept-Bosmer fountain.png](https://images.uesp.net/0/09/ON-concept-Bosmer_fountain.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Bosmer_fountain.png)
- "They'll probably send me out of [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md) soon. Everyone's fighting everyone out there. I'm not afraid; being part of a battle doesn't bother me as much as leaving does. I want to protect our homes, but I wish we didn't have to leave them to do it. The [Thalmor](Lore_Thalmor.md) say we can't just wait for war to come knocking on our door.

- I don't know; the wood has always kept us safe. Who from the outside can even fight their way into it to get to us? If an army attacked, it'd be easy to drive them out. The [High Elves](Lore_Altmer.md) have always done just fine sticking to [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isles.md), too, but now all of the sudden they want to do more. It seems strange, sticking out our necks like this, just inviting a blade to strike them when we have so much safety right here. I'll miss home. Hope I'll come back."
