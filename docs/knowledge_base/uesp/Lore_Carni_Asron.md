# Carni Asron

| Carni Asron | | | |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Race | Unknown | Gender | Male |

**High Wizard ** Carni Asron was a renowned teacher, mage and [enchanter](Lore_Enchanting.md) credited with creation of the legendary [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) [Mentor's Ring](Lore_Mentor%27s_Ring.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ea/LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg/200px-LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LG-cardart-Mentor%27s_Ring_02.jpg) The Mentor's Ring (Legends) Little is known of Carni Asron's background and past. Being a High Wizard, he was held in high esteem as a mage, known for instructing numerous apprentices and students under his guidance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-L2-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-L3-3)</sup>

At some point he created the Mentor's Ring. This powerful artifact was originally used as a tool to assist his young apprentices in their studies of the arcane. It possessed an extraordinary ability to enhance the wearer's intelligence and wisdom, thereby increasing the efficiency of their magical endeavors.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-L4-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-M2-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-S2-6)</sup> He died long before [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup>

Following Asron's passing, the Ring, along with several other of the wizard's possessions, disappeared and eventually became widely circulated throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-L1-1)</sup> This fabled ring appeared in both [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md),<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-M2-5)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-8)</sup> and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) during the [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md) and [Fourth](Lore_Fourth_Era.md) eras, respectively.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-S2-6)</sup>
