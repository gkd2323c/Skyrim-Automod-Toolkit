It contains a complex system of underground caves and fertile grottos that resembles a wild honeycomb. Sea creatures from the [Reef Viper](Lore_Reef_Viper.md) to the [Salamander](Lore_Salamander.md) live and thrive in these caves, and because of this, there are many fishermen from nearby [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) that come for an abundance of harvest.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONWC-1)</sup>

## History
Many years ago, when there were [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) in the Summerset Isles, the island once contained a settlement, which was known for the *Hanging Gardens*. It was a quadrangular garden that was dedicated to the escape from the [Earth Bones](Lore_Earth_Bones.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-HG-2)</sup> During the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), the island was invaded by the Storm Sharks, a naval force of [Maormer](Lore_Maormer.md) that was present across the Summerset Isle's southwest coast. It was made as a staging ground for an invasion of the mainland.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CLW-3)</sup> At the time, an angler fleet was stationed at Wasten Coraldale, and it was later ambushed by the Storm Sharks. The sea captain, Curtaros was discovered by a [newcomer](Lore_Soulless_One.md), who went to the island by the request of the captain's daughter. The two were able to rescue some of their crew members, all the while sabotaging the invasion force and killing the Maormer Commander.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONLa S-4)</sup>

At the same time, there have been several inquiries on Wasten Coraldale. The [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) sent newcomers to the island, to deal with the growing and dangerous population of Reef Vipers.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> The [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) were investigating time breaches across the continent and needed the [Augur of the Obscure](Lore_Augur_of_the_Obscure.md) to identify these breaches. The Augur was recovered on the shores of Wasten Coraldale.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONTPC-6)</sup>

During the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the village of Wasten Coridale was an active settlement. It was ruled by Lord Kelkemmirel, and it had a rivalry with White Guard.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AR-7)</sup>
