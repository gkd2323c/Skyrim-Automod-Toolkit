## Radiant Options
Any innkeeper in the Reach can give you this quest, as can [Raerek](Skyrim_Raerek.md). You will be directed to one of the possible [Forsworn](Skyrim_Forsworn.md) camps in the hold, and you will then need to obtain your reward from Jarl [Igmund](Skyrim_Igmund.md).

## Quick Walkthrough
1. Get the [bounty letter](Skyrim_Bounty_(forsworn).md).
2. Go to the Forsworn camp and kill the leader (usually a [Briarheart](Skyrim_Forsworn.md#Forsworn_Briarhearts)).
3. Return to Igmund for the reward.

## Detailed Walkthrough
Speaking with any innkeeper or Igmund about work may lead them to give you a letter of bounty requiring you to kill a Forsworn leader. Head to the camp specified in the letter and kill the marked Forsworn leader. Then return to Igmund for your reward of 100 gold.

## Notes
- This quest is contingent on Igmund being the jarl; it will not be given if the city has changed hands.
