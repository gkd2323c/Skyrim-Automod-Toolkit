# Pyrite

- *Not to be confused with [Peryite](Lore_Peryite.md).*

Pyrite,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Treasure-1)</sup> also known as **fool's gold** is a mineral known for its resemblance to gold.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Henrigg-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mojha-4)</sup>

Pyrite was utilized in [Dunmeri](Lore_Dunmer.md) ritualistic crafting, with firestrikers made from pyrite and [flint](Lore_Flint.md) adorned with imagery depicting [Saint](Lore_Temple_Saints.md) [Vorys the Immolant](Lore_Vorys.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Treasure-1)</sup>

The phrase "fool's gold" was frequently employed across Tamriel as a metaphor to characterize something that seemed valuable but ultimately proved to be worthless.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Henrigg-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-CJ-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Mojha-4)</sup>

[Foscarium](Lore_Foscarium.md), also resembling gold to some extent but significantly less valuable, has been illegally distributed by smugglers as counterfeit [currency](Lore_Currency.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Foscarium-5)</sup>
