# Fateweaver Key

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/ON-furnishing-Replica_Fateweaver_Key.jpg/200px-ON-furnishing-Replica_Fateweaver_Key.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Replica_Fateweaver_Key.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-furnishing-Replica_Fateweaver_Key.jpg) Replica Fateweaver Key The Fateweaver Key was a powerful key shaped [artifact](Lore_Artifacts.md) created by [Azandar-al-Cybiades](Lore_Azandar_al-Cybiades.md), an exceptionally skilled [Arcanist](Lore_Arcanist.md) and [mage](Lore_Magic.md) of world-class power and knowledge said to be comparable to that of [Morian Zenas](Lore_Morian_Zenas.md), after a lifetime of research and experimentation. The Fateweaver Key was created in order to allow Azandar to replicate the ability of [Hermaeus Mora](Lore_Hermaeus_Mora.md) to gaze [beyond the veil of reality](Lore_Many_Paths.md), allowing its wielder to observe his [unwoven fates](Lore_Fate.md), the "might have beens" of his life, for knowledge. The Key, however, exceeded its creator's expectations, causing his unlived fates to become "frayed" from the [universe](Lore_Aurbis.md) and become manifested within the world.

During his efforts to track down these frayed fates and use the Key to "stitch" them back into the universe Azandar encountered the Adversary, a hostile frayed fate that sought to absorb its counterparts and take his place using its own version of the Fateweaver Key. As the Adversary could not be vanquished as long as his anchor to the world, the Fateweaver Key itself, remained, Azandar instructed the [Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md) to burn the artifact out using a font of [Magicka](Lore_Magic.md) and the Adversary was banished. Acknowledging the danger and temptation the Key represented, Azandar elected to have the now inert relic cast into the ocean.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Azandar-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Mt CA-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Adversary-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Tem-4)</sup>
