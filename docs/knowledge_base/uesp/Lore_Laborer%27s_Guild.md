# Laborer's Guild

[![RG-sign-Kotaro.png](https://images.uesp.net/9/9f/RG-sign-Kotaro.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-sign-Kotaro.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:RG-sign-Kotaro.png) The Laborer's Guild<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> is a contractor organization that seems to deal in varying disciplines of manual labor.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGKotaro-2)</sup> They were known to operate out of [Port Hunding](Lore_Port_Hunding.md) on [Stros M'Kai](Lore_Stros_M%27Kai.md) circa [2E 864](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_864). One member, Kotaro, worked as a middleman, and hired the hero [Cyrus](Lore_Cyrus.md) to pick up an amulet (secretly containing [Prince A'tor](Lore_A%27tor.md)'s [soul](Lore_Soul.md)) from [N'Gasta](Lore_N%27Gasta.md) the [Sload](Lore_Sload.md), and to deliver to it [Lord Amiel Richton](Lore_Amiel_Richton.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-RGKotaro-2)</sup>
