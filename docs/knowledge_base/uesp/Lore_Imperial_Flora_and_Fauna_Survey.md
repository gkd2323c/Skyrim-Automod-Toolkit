# Imperial Flora and Fauna Survey

The Imperial Flora and Fauna Survey was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) academic institution under the [Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) in the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md). It focused on the study of ecology, encompassing research in zoology and environmental geography. The Survey maintained multiple bureaus across [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), including the Northeast Bureau located in [Morrowind](Lore_Morrowind.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup>

## Known Members
- Travis Ulchovik, Senior Zoologist<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Po M-1)</sup>
