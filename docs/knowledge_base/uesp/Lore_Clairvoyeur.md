# Clairvoyeur

The Clairvoyeur is a powerful device crafted by [Teleger the Artificer](Lore_Telenger_the_Artificer.md) for the [Eyes of the Queen](Lore_Eyes_of_the_Queen.md). It can project a golden, glowing trail to a chosen person, provided a personal item is placed in its "Sniff Slot."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>

## History
Circa [2E 258](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_258), during the events surrounding the plot of High Magistrate [Rinwaray](Lore_Rinwaray.md), an agent of the Eyes named Vintenwen supplied the Clairvoyeur to the leader of the spy network [Razum-dar](Lore_Razum-dar.md), retrieving it from the Armory for his use. Razum-dar employed it to track Teleger, who had been subjected to [Seht's Affect Inducer](Lore_Seht%27s_Affect_Inducer.md) by Rinwaray and then tied to the backside of a pier, barely keeping his head above the incoming tide. Once freed, Teleger gave Razum-dar a silk scarf belonging to Rinwaray, which he used with the Clairvoyeur to track her back to [Alinor](Lore_Alinor.md). The device led him swiftly to the top floor of the office of the Bureaureeve of the [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md), where Razum-dar managed to subdue Rinwaray and thwart her nefarious plot.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>

## Notes
- The terminology used to describe the Clairvoyeur, including its name and the "Sniff Slot", originates from Razum-dar, who often gave his own names to items rather than using those assigned by their creator, Telenger. For instance, he referred to another of Teleger's creations, [Hyperagonal Locational Determinator](Lore_Hyperagonal_Locational_Determinator.md) simply as "the Finder."<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-RDJ-1)</sup>
