[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/1b/SR-place-Dawnstar.jpg/200px-SR-place-Dawnstar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Dawnstar.jpg) Dawnstar **Dawnstar** is a city on the northern coast of Skyrim, and the capital of [the Pale](Skyrim_The_Pale.md). It is initially aligned with the [Stormcloaks](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) under [Jarl](Skyrim_Jarl.md) [Skald](Skyrim_Skald.md). The city is a relatively small fishing and mining settlement with few services. The residents will report having [horrible nightmares](Skyrim_Waking_Nightmare.md), which some initially blame on a witch's curse.

For more information on Dawnstar, see the [lore article](Lore_Dawnstar.md).

## Notes
- With *[Hearthfire](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)* installed, you can purchase [a plot of land](Skyrim_Heljarchen_Hall.md) in the Pale to build a house on. Also, two [stone quarries](Skyrim_Quarried_Stone.md) will be present.
- [Dawnstar Sanctuary](Skyrim_Dawnstar_Sanctuary.md) is a [house](Skyrim_Houses.md) granted upon completion of the [Dark Brotherhood](Skyrim_Dark_Brotherhood.md) quest [Hail Sithis!](Skyrim_Hail_Sithis!.md)
- [Dawnguard](Skyrim_Dawnguard.md) adds a permanently disabled map marker for Dawnstar Docks.
- [Quicksilver Mine](Skyrim_Quicksilver_Mine.md) is just south of Fruki's House, in the northwest corner of town.
- [Iron-Breaker Mine](Skyrim_Iron-Breaker_Mine.md) is just east of [The White Hall](Skyrim_The_White_Hall.md), at the east end of town.
- A blacksmith's forge and hide tanner are outside Rustleif's house, near the water.
- A tent is northeast of town, past the hill on which the lighthouse is situated. Inside, someone has set up a lovers' retreat, complete with two unowned bedrolls, a few articles of clothing, wine, [flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md), and an [amulet of Mara](Skyrim_Amulet_of_Mara.md).
- Dawnstar also appears in *[Arena](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Arena:Dawnstar)* and *[Dawnstar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Dawnstar:Places)*.
