Remnants of Cyrod *The legend surrounding an ancient trove of Ayleid knowledge*
---
When [Alessia](Lore_Alessia.md)'s [rebellion](Lore_Alessian_Slave_Rebellion.md) sent the [Heartland](Lore_Heartland.md) [lords](Lore_Ayleid.md) fleeing from their manses, they took their knowledge with them.

While some fled to the fens of [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) and others to the northern reaches, most flew to [Valenwood](Lore_Valenwood.md). After many years amongst the [Bosmer](Lore_Bosmer.md), they amassed a storehouse of information.

Realizing a need to preserve our knowledge against the ages, they excavated a cavern east of the mighty [Strid](Lore_Strid_River.md). There, in its alabaster depths, they carved great halls and sealed them against the ravages of erosion and time.

They summoned our greatest architect to do these things, and a great enchantment was placed upon its walls to stave off putrefaction and decay.

The ruins would stand until the [White-Gold Tower](Lore_White-Gold_Tower.md) fell to dust and bone in the ashes of old [Cyrod](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). They would deliver our memory for generations to come, and seal us as the immortal masters of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), itself.
