[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/ON-statue-Kastav.jpg/200px-ON-statue-Kastav.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Kastav.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-statue-Kastav.jpg) Emperor Kastav's Grave Excoraeus Tharn was an [Imperial](Lore_Imperial.md) member of the royal [House Tharn](Lore_Tharn.md) of [Cheydinhal](Lore_Cheydinhal.md), ancestor and descendant of numerous famous figures.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-HTON-1)</sup>. He served under [Emperor](Lore_Tamrielic_Emperors.md) [Kastav](Lore_Kastav.md) as the **Minister of Punition**. The exact years of service are unknown, but he had to serve at some point between the coronation of Kastav in [1E 2762](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2762)<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-OBSancre-2)</sup> and [1E 2804](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_2804) when his reign ended.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-R2TLA-3)</sup>
