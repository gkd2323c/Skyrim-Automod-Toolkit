[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg/200px-SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Thirsk_Mead_Hall.jpg) Thirsk Mead Hall Caccino Aurelia was a chieftain of [Thirsk](Lore_Thirsk.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md) in the fourth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>

He became Chieftain of Thirsk after slaying the Imperial Champion Claudius Anzione and presenting his sword as a battle trophy. He would, however, rule for only three weeks, as he was revealed to be a fraud and killed by [Eldjar Bear-Skinner](Lore_Eldjar_Bear-Skinner.md), who took his place as chieftain.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TAH-1)</sup>
