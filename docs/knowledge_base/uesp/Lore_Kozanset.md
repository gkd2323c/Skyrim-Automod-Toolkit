## Layout and Geography
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/96/ON-place-Town_Hall_%28Kozanset%29.jpg/200px-ON-place-Town_Hall_%28Kozanset%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Town_Hall_(Kozanset).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Town_Hall_(Kozanset).jpg) The Second Era Town Hall Kozanset in the Second Era is an open-aired town in the center of the geographical [Tigonus](Lore_Tigonus.md) region, in northeastern Alik'r. The roads here meet in Kozanset through the main central thoroughfare that goes north-south. The land the town is built on is flat, but surrounded by dunes, rocky outcrops, and steep slopes. There are several wooden bridges over these slopes that connect Kozanset to the roads leading out it.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKozanset-3)</sup> Southeast of town is Shrikes' Aerie, a mountainous area swarming with [harpies](Lore_Harpy.md). Their feathers, from adolescents and mature harpies are plucked and turned into quills by Kozanset's people.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONTreasures FQ-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONTreasures OFQ-5)</sup>

Its town square is a single water well surrounded by an assortment of buildings. West of this center is the smithy, built on an elevated platform with roads leading north to the stables and west to a lone watchtower. [Baandari Pedlars](Lore_Baandari_Pedlars.md) settle here to the north, in an open area near the stables.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKozanset-3)</sup> In the late Third Era, Kozanset was a sizeable, walled city near the [Dragontail Mountain](Lore_Dragontail_Mountains.md) foothills and the barony's southwest border.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-2)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/15/ON-quest-Feathered_Fiends.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Feathered_Fiends.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Feathered_Fiends.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Feathered_Fiends.jpg) Harpies in Kozanset In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), Kozanset was governed by Magistrate Andrin at-Lehiel.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONKozanset-3)</sup> To combat the growing dangers of the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md), the [Fighters Guild](Lore_Fighters_Guild.md) established a branch in Kozanset.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ONHjognar-6)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the town had a large harpy infestation and hired [Gold Coast](Lore_Gold_Coast.md) mercenaries to deal with them. Hearing news of this, the Magistrate's daughter, Leja at-Lehiel returned from travel and suspected foulplay from the mercenaries. With help from [the Vestige](Lore_The_Vestige.md), they learned that their leader, Daneem and her mercenaries planted harpy eggs across town, forcing them to attack Kozanset. While the Vestige went through Kozanset, collecting eggs. The mercenaries kidnapped Leja and several people, leaving a trail of dead and Leja injured in Shrikes' Aerie. Returning to Kozanset on a friendly harpy, the Vestige rallied locals to ultimately kick the Gold Coast mercenaries out and restore order.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-ONFeathered-7)</sup>

At around the same time, the Vestige and their companion, Azandar al-Cybiades traveled to Kozanset, Azandar's hometown to continue clearing his frayed [fates](Lore_Fate.md). They found two fatelines, one where he was a beggar and another a priest, after restoring them with his [Fateweaver Key](Lore_Fateweaver_Key.md), the two embarked northeast into the Lost City of the Na-Totambu where they found one fate as a tour guide and prospector, before being attacked by a powerful one called the Adversary.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ONPaths-8)</sup>

By the fifth century of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md), the Barony of Kozanset was ruled by an unnamed Baroness and the capital hosted a [Temple of Kynareth](Lore_Temple_of_Kynareth.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DFKozanset-2)</sup> When the [Warp in the West](Lore_Warp_in_the_West.md) occurred in [3E 417](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_417), Kozanset and many other regions in the Hammerfell-Iliac Bay were absorbed into the Kingdom of Sentinel, at the time ruled by Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TWITW-9)</sup>
