The journal of Mehra Nerendas, Tribunal Temple **by [Mehra Nerendas](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Mehra_Nerendas)** *The journal entry of a Tribunal priestess having a crisis of faith*
---
[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-icon-Dunmer.png)
- [Tribunes](Lore_Tribunal.md) show mercy on my weak soul. I have begged leave from my duties to cloister myself and treat my heretical thoughts by copying sacred texts. How could I question the path [Almalexia](Lore_Almalexia.md) has laid out for us? A wicked seed has taken root in me, and I must purge it!

- Since the signing of the [Pact](Lore_Ebonheart_Pact.md) I have felt it, the gnawing and insidious doubt. The thoughts that maybe—dare I write it—Almalexia steers the [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md) wrong in allying with two races of lesser stock, [our former blood enemies](Lore_Nord.md) and [slaves](Lore_Argonian.md). For shame! My faith is weak, and I am pathetic. I do not deserve to be among my brothers. I shall return to copying Their teachings and will not stop until this filth is expunged from my mind!
