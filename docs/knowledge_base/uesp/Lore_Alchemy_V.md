<sup>†</sup> *Vampire Dust in Elder Scrolls Online is not an alchemical ingredient, but only valuable as loot to be sold for gold.* **Vampire Dust** is the ash collected from deceased vampires for its alchemical properties. It is dangerous to collect, and is thus highly valued and expensive. Its alchemical effects vary as much as the environments from which the dust is collected. For example, Vampire Dust collected from vampires in [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) is the only variety known to be able transmit the [disease](Lore_Vampirism.md), but because it's the only ingredient capable of it no potion can be made which offers this effect. Dust collected from vampires in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md) and [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md) both in turn carry a resistance to diseases and invisibility, both effects carried from their vampiric source.
