[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0f/SR-npc-Ernanthil.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Ernanthil.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ernanthil.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Ernanthil.jpg) Ernanthil **Ernanthil** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [warrior](Skyrim_Warrior_(class).md) who offers the player a [rogue's job](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Rogue%27s_Job_Offer), when [the cup of Fortune](Skyrim_Fortunate_Son.md#The_Cup_of_Fortune) is chosen at the [Roadside Ruins.](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Roadside_Ruins) All interactions with him are by dead-drop letters until he finally appears in Eslaf's room in [The Warrens](Skyrim_The_Warrens.md) of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md) during your battle with his brother [Cuinanthil](Skyrim_Cuinanthil.md), the course of which he watches.

He wears typical thieves guild garb that cannot be looted. He wields a [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger) and carries [a journal](Skyrim_Ernanthil%27s_Journal.md), leveled [rich items](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Rich_Pocket_Contents) and a copy of the skill book *[Thief](Skyrim_Thief_(book).md)*.

## Related Quests

## Quest-Related Dialogue
After killing [Cuinanthil](Skyrim_Cuinanthil.md):

- *"I'm surprised you succeeded. Please accept this as payment."*

- - **It was a pleasure working with you.**
- *"Yes, yes, thank you and all that. But enough of this. Such things are best forgotten."*

- - **I still have questions.**
- *"Not my problem. But enough of this. Such things are best forgotten."*

- - **I think I'll just kill you and take it all.**
- *"What in the world..."* **(Turns hostile)**

- - **This isn't enough. You barely did any work and just showed up at the end.**
- *"It can't be helped. But enough of this. Such things are best forgotten."*

If you attempt to speak to him again he will brush you off:

- *"Yes, yes, thank you and all that. But enough of this. Such things are best forgotten."*
