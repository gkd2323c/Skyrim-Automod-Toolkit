Scorions are arthropodal [Daedra](Lore_Daedra.md#Lesser_Daedra) whose purpose seems to be collecting and transferring [magical energy](Lore_Magicka.md). Described as large "[Deadlands](Lore_Deadlands.md) -touched" arachnoids,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WFP-1)</sup> they have six legs that they use for walking and climbing, with a number of small fang-like appendages at the front, the upper two being used for channeling energy. They have a large thorax which glows red or orange depending on the type of energy collected, as well a hollow abdomen with no head, which also glows orange from its hole when energy is collected. They are rarely seen and were once used by the [Order of the Waking Flame](Lore_Order_of_the_Waking_Flame.md) to collect residual torture energy for the construction of the Antecedent Cataclyst in the Dread Cellar.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ESO-2)</sup> They possess [teleportation](Lore_Teleportation.md) capabilities.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-WFP-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-SC-3)</sup> Scorions that get swept away during eruptions are sometimes crushed into rugged gems known as Scorion Diamonds by the intense crucible of stone and flame.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-QI-4)</sup> Small ones are known as Pyrelings, which one scholar believed acted like [ticks](Lore_Tick.md) on [Dragons](Lore_Dragon.md).<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-SP-5)</sup>
