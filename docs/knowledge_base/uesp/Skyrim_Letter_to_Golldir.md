Letter to Golldir **by [Vals Veran](Skyrim_Vals_Veran.md)** *A letter from a necromancer threatening to raise a Nord's ancestors*
---
*This is a reply to a [previous letter](Skyrim_Letter_to_Vals_Veran.md) that does not appear in the game.*

---
Well, [Golldir](Skyrim_Golldir.md), I'm impressed. I know what trouble you Nords have with literacy, those big words must put such a strain on your tiny brains. And I'm surprised you can smell anything of the stink of your own filth. Why don't you meet me at [Hillgrund's tomb](Skyrim_Hillgrund%27s_Tomb.md) and I'll show you the way we deal with ancestors in Morrowind.

-Vals Veran
