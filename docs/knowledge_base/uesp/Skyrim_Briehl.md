[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/4c/SR-npc-Briehl.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Briehl.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Briehl.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Briehl.jpg) Briehl enjoying a relaxing drink **Briehl**, a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [warrior](Skyrim_CombatWarrior1H.md), is an acolyte at the [Temple of Mara](Skyrim_Temple_of_Mara.md) in [Riften](Skyrim_Riften.md), where he works under [Dinya Balu](Skyrim_Dinya_Balu.md) and [Maramal](Skyrim_Maramal.md).

Briehl never leaves the Temple of Mara and will wander around the chapel day and night, at random times eating, sleeping, conversing, or cooking. During the wedding ceremonies held at the Chapel, Briehl will sit silently in attendance while Maramal performs the ceremony.

His only possessions are the clothes he is wearing, a set of [monk robes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Monk_Robes) and a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#000bacd7).

## Dialogue
**Which of the Eight Divines do you serve?** *"This temple is dedicated to Mara, and the light of love she shines on us all. I'm only a humble acolyte, though. If you wish to avail yourself of Mara's blessing, you should speak with Dinya Balu or Maramal."* If you are doing the quest [The Book of Love](Skyrim_The_Book_of_Love.md) for Dinya, he will add:

*"They should be back shortly. You're welcome to wait here if you like."*
## Notes
- Briehl does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Sond](Skyrim_Sond_(bandit).md), [Lynoit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Lynoit), [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md), and many other generic NPCs.
