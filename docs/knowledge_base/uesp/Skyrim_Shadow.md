[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/17/SR-npc-Shadow.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Shadow.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shadow.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Shadow.jpg) Your Shadow The **Shadow** is a clone of yourself found near the hotsprings outside [Mistwatch](Skyrim_Mistwatch.md). It manifests with the same armor that you are wearing, as well as a copy of the sword version of [Shadowrend](Skyrim_Shadowrend_(item).md). The Shadow can use [spells](Skyrim_Spells.md) and even [shouts](Skyrim_Shouts.md) that you have learned.

## Related Quests
- **[Through a Glass, Darkly](Skyrim_Through_a_Glass,_Darkly.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Shadowrend.md)</sup>: Confront the darkness within yourself to obtain a [powerful weapon](Skyrim_Shadowrend_(item).md).

## Notes
- The name of this NPC depends on the name of your character: [player]'s Shadow.
- The Shadow will always be a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) if you are a human race, or a [Wood Elf](Skyrim_Wood_Elf.md) if you are an elven race (other than [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md)). This may result in the Shadow having different resistances and being slightly taller or shorter than you.
- The Shadow is immune to the [Wabbajack's](Skyrim_Wabbajack.md) transmogrify effect.
- The Shadow seems to be a valid target for the [Ebony Blade](Skyrim_Ebony_Blade.md).

## Bugs
- If the Shadow uses the [Marked for Death](Skyrim_Marked_for_Death.md) shout, it can cause permanent damage to you or your allies' [Armor Rating](Skyrim_Armor_Rating.md), and even cause your damage resistance to become negative. This results in taking increased damage from all physical sources. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.629.0.8, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) This can be fixed by opening the console, selecting the affected actor, and using the command `restoreav damageresist 9999999`.
- You may be able to pickpocket items off of the Shadow despite the fact that it immediately becomes hostile once you summon it. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.6.1130, fixes this bug.
- The Shadow can use shouts that you have learned, but haven't yet unlocked. They will also use all three words of the shout, even if you have only learned the first word.
- If you're wearing an item with an enchantment that fortifies your [attributes](Skyrim_Attributes.md) when the Shadow is summoned, the Shadow's health, magicka, and/or stamina will be higher than yours. **Mod Notes**: Your total health/stamina/magicka is considered as the base health for the Shadow, and the Shadow can then equip the same enchanted armor for more bonuses.
- Once you kill the Shadow you can get a letter from the courier given you the misc quest to "Collect the reward from 'PLAYER' for killing Shadow. <sup>**?**</sup>
