[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-item-Taron_Dreth%27s_Robes.jpg/200px-SR-item-Taron_Dreth%27s_Robes.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Taron_Dreth%27s_Robes.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Taron_Dreth%27s_Robes.jpg) Taron Dreth's Robes Taron Dreth's Robes are a set of unique robes worn by the [eponymous person](Skyrim_Taron_Dreth.md). They are similar in appearance to [Master Robes](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Master_Robes) and carry an enchantment identical to [apprentice-level destruction robes](Skyrim_Generic_Magic_Apparel.md#Apprentice_Robes_of_Destruction), granting the wearer fifteen points of [destruction magic fortification](Skyrim_Fortify_Destruction.md) and 75 points of [magicka regeneration](Skyrim_Regenerate_Magicka.md).

After completion of the side quest [Lost to the Ages](Skyrim_Lost_to_the_Ages.md), Taron Dreth may be found wandering Skyrim. Speaking to him with an Aetherial item equipped will cause him to turn hostile and attack you, after which the robes can be looted from his corpse.
