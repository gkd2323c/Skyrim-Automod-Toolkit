[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d9/ON-place-Port_Dufort.jpg/200px-ON-place-Port_Dufort.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Port_Dufort.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Port_Dufort.jpg) Port Dufort in [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Port Dufort is a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) town located in the [Systres](Lore_Systres.md), described as a small enclave on a tiny isle. It was associated with [House Dufort](Lore_House_Dufort.md), and was found southeast of [Gonfalon Bay](Lore_Gonfalon_Bay.md) and west of the Earthen Root Enclave. The town's locales include the Central Plaza, Pit Daemons Plaza, Fire Drakes Plaza, Dufort Inn, and Twin Trees.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PD-1)</sup>

## History
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/9f/ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_Rewards.jpg/200px-ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_Rewards.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_Rewards.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-render-Update_44_Battlegrounds_Rewards.jpg) A gladiatorial battle hosted in the streets of Port Dufort [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/43/ON-load-Port_Dufort.jpg/200px-ON-load-Port_Dufort.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Port_Dufort.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Port_Dufort.jpg) Port Dufort in 2E 582 The exact date of the founding of Port Dufort is unclear. At one point in history, this remote outpost was ruled by Duchess Calette Brousseau Levys Gignac Dufort the First. A jovial retired adventurer, she was much beloved by the people of her small enclave. The Duchess personally initiated the tradition of a spectacular combat festival, which continued throughout the years of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md)'s [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PD-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BG-2)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md) used the streets of Port Dufort to host dangerous gladiator battles. Fighters from all three sides of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) came to the Port Dufort battleground to fight for the glory of the Fire Drakes, Pit Daemons, and Storm Lords arena teams.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-BG-2)</sup>

The Systres was considered part of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md)'s territory by [3E 427](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_427).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-MCMap-3)</sup> It is unknown how those events affected the Port Dufort.
