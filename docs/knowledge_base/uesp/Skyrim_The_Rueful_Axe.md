[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/14/SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg/200px-SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-The_Rueful_Axe.jpg) The Rueful Axe The Rueful Axe is a unique battleaxe. It is found in the possession of [Sebastian Lort](Skyrim_Sebastian_Lort.md) during the quest [A Daedra's Best Friend](Skyrim_A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend.md), and may be given to you as a reward from [Clavicus Vile](Skyrim_Clavicus_Vile.md) for the murder of [Barbas](Skyrim_Barbas.md).

If Barbas is asked about the origin of the axe, he will tell of Sebastian Lort, a powerful wizard, and his daughter, who worshipped [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md). Sebastian prayed to Clavicus Vile to cure his daughter of her [Lycanthropy](Skyrim_Werewolf.md), and in response, the [Daedric Prince](Lore_Daedric_Princes.md) gave him the Rueful Axe.

## Related Quests
- **[A Daedra's Best Friend](Skyrim_A_Daedra%27s_Best_Friend.md)**: Help [Barbas](Skyrim_Barbas.md) reunite with his [master](Lore_Clavicus_Vile.md).

## Notes
- The Rueful Axe does **not** count as a Daedric Artifact for the Oblivion Walker [Achievement](Skyrim_Achievements.md). If you want to get the achievement you will have to accept the [Masque of Clavicus Vile](Skyrim_Masque_of_Clavicus_Vile.md) instead.
- If equipped, [guards](Skyrim_Guards.md) may comment on the axe, saying "*Tis a wicked axe you wield there, friend. That blade looks sharp enough to cut through a god.*"
- Whereas all other battleaxes have a speed of 0.7, the Rueful Axe's speed is 0.5, making it even slower than warhammers (0.6). As a result, it is the slowest melee weapon in the game.
- [Hendraheim](Skyrim_Hendraheim_(place).md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Hendraheim.md)</sup> contains a special holder for this item.

## Bugs
- When the Axe is sheathed or unsheathed, the sound effect for a one-handed sword is played instead of a battleaxe sound effect. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.0, fixes this bug.
- This weapon is not affected by any smithing perks. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch) addresses this issue. It causes the weapon to benefit from the [Daedric Smithing](Skyrim_Daedric_Smithing.md) perk.
