[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fb/SR-place-Kynesgrove.jpg/200px-SR-place-Kynesgrove.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kynesgrove.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kynesgrove.jpg) Kynesgrove **Kynesgrove** is a small [settlement](Skyrim_Settlements.md) south of [Windhelm](Skyrim_Windhelm.md).

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/0/0c/SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg/200px-SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Kynesgrove_02.jpg) Steamscorch Mine Camp [Ganna](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ganna_Uriel) and [Gemma Uriel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gemma_Uriel) came to Kynesgrove to start a lumber mill without realizing the nearby grove's trees were sacred to [Kyne](Lore_Kyne.md). The settlement contains remnants of the grove they initially cut. They now mine [malachite](Skyrim_Malachite.md) for a living, unable to afford starting a lumber mill elsewhere. They can be heard constantly arguing about the life they left behind in [Cyrodiil](Lore_Cyrodiil.md). [Kjeld](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Kjeld) runs the settlement and will [buy malachite ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md) from you. [Iddra](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Iddra) runs the [Braidwood Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Braidwood_Inn). [Dravynea the Stoneweaver](Skyrim_Dravynea_the_Stoneweaver.md) keeps the mine from collapsing and getting too warm with a potion she makes from [frost salts](Skyrim_Frost_Salts.md).

The settlement consists of [Braidwood Inn](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Braidwood_Inn), [Steamscorch Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine) (a malachite mine), and a [camp](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steamscorch_Mine#Steamscorch_Mine_Camp) containing three tents used by the miners. There is a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) outside the mine, a [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) beside the inn, and a [stone quarry](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Stone_Quarry) just east of the inn. Next to the camp is a vegetable patch containing six [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek), four [gourds](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Gourd), eight [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants, and ten [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage). A [wood chopping block](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wood_Chopping_Block) and a [tanning rack](Skyrim_Tanning_Rack.md) are also found near the vegetable patch. A short distance downhill from the inn is a chicken coop consisting of two [chicken nests](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chicken%27s_Egg) for the two [chickens](Skyrim_Chicken.md) that wander the settlement. There are a number of [dragon's tongue](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dragon%27s_Tongue) plants around the settlement as well as a [nightshade](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightshade) plant and a [snowberry](Skyrim_Snowberries.md) bush around the camp. Uphill to the south are some [beehives](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Beehive) and [red mountain flowers](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md) among the trees. To the east is a [dragon mound](Skyrim_Dragon_Mounds.md#Kynesgrove_Resurrection) where the [dragon](Skyrim_Dragon.md) [Sahloknir](Skyrim_Sahloknir.md) is resurrected by [Alduin](Skyrim_Alduin.md) during the quest [A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md).

## Notes
- Despite Ganna Uriel mentioning that she and her sister built a lumbermill at Kynesgrove, it is nowhere to be found. The [Creation Kit](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Creation_Kit) identifies the bank of the river just down the hill from Kynesgrove as "Frostbark Lumber Mill", which suggests the location she describes was cut from the game.
- Kynesgrove is one of the most common places to encounter dragons, as they often fly overhead.
- Kynesgrove also [appears](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Kynesgrove) in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online).

## Bugs
- If [Klija Wolf-Claw](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Klija_Wolf-Claw) appears at Kynesgrove but you then side with the [Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md) and kill her during the quest [Battle of the Champions](Skyrim_Battle_of_the_Champions.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Civil_War_Champions.md)</sup>, this version of her will not be disabled, and she will continue to live at Steamscorch Mine Camp. <sup>**?**</sup>
- One of the pieces of malachite ore outside the mine may be buried under the ground, making it irretrievable. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.2, fixes this bug.
- There is a broken navmesh in the mine, making the back 1/3 of the mine largely inaccessible to the NPCs when they work there. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.1.3, fixes this bug.
- After [A Blade in the Dark](Skyrim_A_Blade_in_the_Dark.md) is finished, the town guards may comment on a dragon attack here as though it took place somewhere else. - The [Unofficial Skyrim Special Edition Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch), version 4.3.8, fixes this bug.
