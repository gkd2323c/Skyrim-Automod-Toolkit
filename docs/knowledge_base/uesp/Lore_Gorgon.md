[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-ingredient-Gorgon_snake.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:DF-icon-ingredient-Gorgon_snake.png) A Gorgon snake (Daggerfall) Gorgons (also commonly known as **Medusas**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFGuide-1)</sup><sup>:131</sup> are creatures with the upper body of a woman and the lower torso of a [snake](Lore_Snake.md),<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Medusa Arena-2)</sup> as well as having smaller snakes attached to their head.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup> They writhe along using their hands and body to wind their way through their lairs. Gorgons are very strong and intelligent, and armed with a fearsome weapon, their gaze, which has the ability to paralyze their opponents. Due to their inherent magical nature, they are immune to weapons below those of [Dwarven](Lore_Dwarven_Metal.md) quality.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AD-4)</sup>

The forest of [Murkwood](Lore_Murkwood.md) is home to many medusas.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> They were found all throughout [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), and were known to be among [Jagar Tharn](Lore_Jagar_Tharn.md)'s legions.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> One was also among the specimens in the Bestiary of the [Crystal Tower](Lore_Crystal_Tower.md).<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-CTArena-7)</sup> The [Mages Guild](Lore_Mages_Guild.md) was known to have medusas patrolling their guild halls during night hours.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Medusa Arena-2)</sup> Severed [gorgon snakes](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Gorgon_snake) are found in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region, where they are used to brew [potions](Lore_Alchemy.md) that improve the consumer's climbing abilities.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DF-3)</sup>

The [Tsaesci](Lore_Tsaesci_(race).md) are described as inverted gorgons in [Vivec's](Lore_Vivec.md) [seventh Sermon](Lore_36_Lessons_of_Vivec,_Sermon_7.md).<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-36Lo VS7-8)</sup> Medusa's Gaze is a name of a paralysis [spell](Lore_Spells.md). It was classified as either [illusion](Lore_Illusion.md) or [alteration](Lore_Alteration.md) [magic](Lore_Magic.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-DAS-9)</sup><sup>[[10]](#cite_note-MIS-10)</sup>
