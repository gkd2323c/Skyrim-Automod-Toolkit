Finished this 10th day of Sun's Height in [2E 388](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_388) to honor Saint [Stental](Lore_Stental.md), who slew the demon of Overlook Hill and saved the three Sisters of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) from a terrible fate.
