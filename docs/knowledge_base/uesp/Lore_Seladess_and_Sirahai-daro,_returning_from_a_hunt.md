Seladess and Sirahai-daro, returning from a hunt *A friendly conversation between two hunters*
---
[![ON-concept-Bosmer medium armor.png](https://images.uesp.net/2/27/ON-concept-Bosmer_medium_armor.png)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-concept-Bosmer_medium_armor.png)
- *Sirahai-daro*: See something you like? Meat or pelts? Killed fresh today, all by me!

- *Seladess*: "All killed by her," she says. She forgets her good friend with the deadly bow skills so soon!

- *Sirahai-daro*: You know what I didn't forget? My perfect shot right into the throat of that [senche-tiger](Lore_Senche-Tiger.md) that pounced on you, oh mighty hunter! Where would you be without good friend Sirahai-daro?

- *Seladess*: It was just a trick; I was ready to drive my dagger into its throat at any second. You ruined the trap! You said it yourself: my armor is tougher than it looks!

- *Sirahai-daro*: Oh, yes, of course; I must apologize for spoiling your cunning trap. Let's go get this wagon to the hut! We have work to do!
