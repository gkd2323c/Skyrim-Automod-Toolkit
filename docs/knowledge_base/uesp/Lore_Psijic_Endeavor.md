# Psijic Endeavor


The Psijic Endeavor apparently formed the basis for the teachings of the prophet [Veloth](Lore_Veloth.md), who described it as a process of apotheosis which manipulates time itself. The goal of the Endeavor is to experience [CHIM](Lore_CHIM.md), and the two concepts are closely related.<sup>[[UOL 2]](#cite_note-5)</sup>

## Notes
- Despite the common namesake, the Psijic Endeavor is unrelated to the [Psijic Order](Lore_Psijic_Order.md) (both being named after [Padomay](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Padomay)'s name "Psijii"/PSJJJJ), which doesn't care much for the idea.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-PSJJJJ-3)</sup>
