## Orchid
**Orchids** are a variety of flowering plant, known for its vibrant colors and scent. The word orchid is also used to describe a vivid shade of purple, which matches the commonly known color of the flower.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Orchid Nixad-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Orchid Whisper Moth-4)</sup> The Ghost Orchid is considered one of the rarest and most coveted flowers in Tamriel, and perfumes made from it are very valuable.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Ghost Orchid-5)</sup> A species of golden orchid could be found on [Summerset](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md), and hundreds of these flowers needed be harvested to produce a single bottle of the cosmetic known as "Goldtips Bronzer".<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Goldtips Bronzer-6)</sup> Another known species of orchid is the Moonshadow Orchid, though it is unknown what relation it has to the [realm](Lore_Moonshadow.md) it shares a name with.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-Moonshadow Orchid-7)</sup>

According to tales, there was once a flora-filled cavern in Skyrim known as Orchidfall Vale. However, its location is considered long-lost.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Orchidfall Fawn-8)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Treasures_G#Ghost_Orchid_Perfume), [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Orchidfall_Vale_Fawn)
