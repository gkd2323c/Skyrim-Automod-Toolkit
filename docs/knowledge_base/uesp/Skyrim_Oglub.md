[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e7/SR-npc-Oglub.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Oglub.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oglub.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Oglub.jpg) Oglub resting in the mine **Oglub** is an aging [Orc](Skyrim_Orc.md) [warrior](Skyrim_GuardOrc1H.md) living in [Dushnikh Yal](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Yal.md), the [Orc stronghold](Skyrim_Orc_Strongholds.md) southeast of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md). He is the brother of [Ghorbash the Iron Hand](Skyrim_Ghorbash_the_Iron_Hand.md), but seems to have outgrown his chance to become chief of the stronghold. Instead, he works in [Dushnikh Mine](Skyrim_Dushnikh_Mine.md), mining [orichalcum ore](Skyrim_Orichalcum_Ore.md) along with [Ghak](Skyrim_Ghak.md), [Mahk](Skyrim_Mahk.md), and [Dulug](Skyrim_Dulug.md).

Oglub spends his entire life in the dark tunnels and never sleeps. He divides his time between hacking away on the ores, relaxing or eating.

He wears a set of humble clothing, a pair of [ragged trousers](Skyrim_Ragged_Trousers.md) and [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#0010698f). He carries his [pickaxe](Skyrim_Pickaxe.md), a leveled [war axe](Skyrim_War_Axe.md), the [key](Skyrim_Key_to_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md) to [Burguk's Longhouse](Skyrim_Burguk%27s_Longhouse.md), and a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
When approached, he will tell you about his situation:

- *"I serve my chief and my brothers by working in the mines. It's the only place fit for an aging Orc."*
- *"Sorry, outlander, but this old Orc cannot help you. Burguk is the chief here."*
- *"I don't know how Ghorbash has so much spirit. He should act his age."*
