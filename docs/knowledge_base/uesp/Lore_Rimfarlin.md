Rimfarlin is a duchy seemingly located somewhere on [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) during the late [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Palla-1)</sup> In the published work *[Palla](Lore_Palla.md)*, set at a fanciful party in [Mir Corrup](Lore_Mir_Corrup.md) around the time of the [haunting of Daggerfall](Lore_Daggerfall.md#Daggerfall_in_the_Third_Empire) of the [3E 400s](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_405), the current Duke of Rimfarlin was said to have been discussing diplomatic appointments to [Balmora](Lore_Balmora.md) with old Lady Schaudirra.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Palla-1)</sup>

## Notes
- A diplomatic appointment is typically indicative of foreign relations between one or more states, with an ambassador of one state advocating for their own state in another state's court.
