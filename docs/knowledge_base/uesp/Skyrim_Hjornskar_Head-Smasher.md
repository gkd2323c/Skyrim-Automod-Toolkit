[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/SR-npc-Hjornskar_Head-Smasher.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Hjornskar_Head-Smasher.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjornskar_Head-Smasher.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Hjornskar_Head-Smasher.jpg) Hjornskar Head-Smasher **Hjornskar Head-Smasher** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [soldier](Skyrim_Soldier_(class).md) and [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) commander who is initially located at the [Whiterun Stormcloak Camp](Skyrim_Whiterun_Stormcloak_Camp.md).

Befitting his station, he wears [Stormcloak officer's armor](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Armor.md), [boots](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Boots.md), and [bracers](Skyrim_Stormcloak_Officer_Bracers.md). He defends himself with either a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) and a [shield](Skyrim_Shield.md), or a leveled [two-handed weapon](Skyrim_Two-Handed_Weapons.md), both of which may be up to [steel](Skyrim_Steel.md) in quality. He supplements that with a [hunting bow](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Hunting_Bow) with twelve [steel arrows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Arrow) and also carries an extra [steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Dagger).

Following the completion of the [civil war](Skyrim_Civil_War.md) quest [Battle For Whiterun](Skyrim_Battle_for_Whiterun.md), and depending on which side it is completed for, Hjornskar may either be found in [Whiterun](Skyrim_Whiterun.md) (completed for Stormcloaks), or remain at the Whiterun Stormcloak Camp (completed for [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md)).
