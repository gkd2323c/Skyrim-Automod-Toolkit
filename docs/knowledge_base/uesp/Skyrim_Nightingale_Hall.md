## Nightingale Hall
The entrance to Nightingale Hall is a rather steep and narrow incline, illuminated by fissures in the ceiling and decorated with Nightingale sigil banners. The narrow trail opens to the hall: an impressive cavern, complete with waterfall and rushing stream. Crossing the wooden bridge spanning the stream, you will reach the living quarters. After the Hall is restored (see [notes](#Notes)), a small round table will hold [grilled leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Grilled_Leeks), a piece of [cooked beef](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cooked_Beef), a wedge of [Eidar cheese](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Eidar_Cheese_Wheel), a piece of bread, and a bottle of [wine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Wine). A bookshelf will hold some reading material, and an unlocked chest at the foot of the nearby bed will contain some gold. The next bed over will have an end table with a copy of the [Pickpocket](Skyrim_Pickpocket.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[Purloined Shadows](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purloined_Shadows)* on top (before the Hall's restoration, the book will be on the ground).

The next room is the circular armory with the three [Nightingale Armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Nightingale_Armor) pedestals involved in the related quest. The hallway continues southward, with branches to the east and west. Taking the east passage, to the left, brings you to an unused training room with discarded practice dummies. On the south wall is a novice-locked chest. The passage to the west leads to an additional bedroom, with two beds and a bedroll. A copy of the [Sneak](Skyrim_Sneak.md) skill book *[The Red Kitchen Reader](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Red_Kitchen_Reader)* is next to the bed on the left, and between the two beds is a novice-locked chest.

Continuing to the south, you come to the Oath room, with the three platforms representing the Trinity. While this room is a quiet place for reflection, it holds nothing of note.

## Notes
- Before [Trinity Restored](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Trinity_Restored), the entrance is blocked.
- There are two [](Skyrim_Shadowmarks_(Thieves_Guild).md) Safe [shadowmarks](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md#Shadowmarks) on birch tree stumps outside Nightingale Hall.
- Although Karliah says she will, she may not live in or visit the Hall until you [finish](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md) restoring the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md) to its former glory and become the Guild Master. She will then attend your ceremony and return to Nightingale Hall, which will now be restored and upgraded.<sup>[*verification needed*]</sup>
