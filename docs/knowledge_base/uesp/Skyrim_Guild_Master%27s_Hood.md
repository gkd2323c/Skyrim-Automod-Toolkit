[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/8/8e/SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Hood.jpg/200px-SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Hood.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Hood.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Guild_Master%27s_Hood.jpg) Guild Master's Hood The Guild Master's Hood is the headwear for the unique set of equipment given to the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md), and is part of the quest reward for completing [Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md). Alongside this item, this set of equipment includes [armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Armor), [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Boots), and [gloves](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Guild_Master%27s_Gloves). Its appearance is similar to that of the [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Hood.md) used by the regular members, except for its darker color. As it is essentially an upgrade to the regular set of guild armor, this hood has a superior armor rating and gold value to the basic set, although it weighs more. Like its similar appearance, the Guild Master's Hood also has a correlative, yet more powerful, enchantment to the more common version of this equipment. The hood [fortifies barter](Skyrim_Fortify_Barter.md) ability by twenty points, versus ten points for normal Thieves Guild Hood and fifteen points for the upgraded version. [Tempering](Skyrim_Workbench.md) this item requires a piece of [leather](Skyrim_Leather.md) and the [Arcane Blacksmith](Skyrim_Arcane_Blacksmith.md) perk.

## Related Quests
- **[Under New Management](Skyrim_Under_New_Management.md)**: Become the Guild Master of the [Thieves Guild](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_(faction).md).

## Notes
- While there are duplicates of each other piece of this set of armor for use by [Mercer Frey](Skyrim_Mercer_Frey.md) and Vex, as they do not wear hoods, there are no copies of it for them.

## Bugs
- The Guild Master's Hood, like other [Thieves Guild Hood](Skyrim_Thieves_Guild_Hood.md) variants, has no model for vampires. Vampires attempting to equip the hood will appear bald. This also enables player vampires to equip it alongside other headgear. - The [Official Skyrim Special Edition Patch](Skyrim_Special_Edition_Patch.md), version 1.5.62.0.8, fixes this bug.
