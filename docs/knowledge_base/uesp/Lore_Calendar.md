However, there is another that is specifically followed by the Argonians, see [the Argonian racial article.](Lore_Argonian.md#Culture) The months listed below include these as well, but do not describe their in-depth meanings.

## Era
**History** is typically divided into six eras: [Dawn](Lore_Dawn_Era.md), [Merethic](Lore_Merethic_Era.md), [First](Lore_First_Era.md), [Second](Lore_Second_Era.md), [Third](Lore_Third_Era.md), and [Fourth](Lore_Fourth_Era.md). The Dawn Era lacks any sense of time at all and the Merethic Era has few specific dates. The term era has been used archaically to refer to both a period of time around 800 years and one of 1,000 years. Numerous details about events in Tamriel's history are debated by scholars, whose opinions are influenced by numerous biases (desire for recognition, loyalty to the realm, racial and cultural conflict, and others).

## Year
Each year is uniquely expressed by its era and its ordinal numeration with respect to the first year of the era, in the case of the Second<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup> and Fourth,<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Skyrim Load-2)</sup> whereas the First<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BAM-3)</sup> and Third<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> progress from their respective zeroth years. The Merethic Era is dated backwards, starting at [ME 2500](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_2500), the [Convention](Lore_Convention.md) of the [Divines](Lore_Divines.md) at the [Adamantine Tower](Lore_Adamantine_Tower.md), to [ME 1](Lore_Merethic_Era.md#ME_1), the year before King [Eplear](Lore_Eplear.md) established the [Camoran Dynasty](Lore_Camoran_Dynasty.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BAM-3)</sup>

## Month
The **Tamrielic Calendar** is thus used to keep track of the date and month of the year.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFC-5)</sup> Each year is composed of twelve distinct months (sometimes called "Seasons"<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>), each around thirty days in length.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Each month has an associated [constellation](Lore_Constellations.md) which is typically in the sky throughout its duration.

### Morning Star
**Morning Star** (also spelled **Morningstar**<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> and known as **Vakka** in [Jel](Lore_Jel.md), meaning [Sun](Lore_Sun.md))<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the first month of the year. It is a winter month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Ritual.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Sun's Dawn
**Sun's Dawn** (known as **Xeech** in Jel, meaning Nut)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the second month of the year. It is a spring month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It seemingly has 28 days,<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> though some sources place a 29th day in the month.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_Leap)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Lover.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### First Seed
**First Seed** (known as **Sisei** in Jel, meaning Sprout)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the third month of the year. It is a spring month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup>. Its associated birthsign is the Lord.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Rain's Hand
**Rain's Hand** (known as **Hist-Deek** in Jel, meaning [Hist](Lore_Hist.md) Sapling)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the fourth month of the year. It is a spring month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 30 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Mage.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Second Seed
**Second Seed** (known as **Hist-Dooka** in Jel, meaning Mature Hist)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the fifth month of the year. It is a summer month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Shadow.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Midyear
**Midyear** (also spelled **Mid Year**<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-RJ2-11)</sup> and **Middle Yarr**,<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SOP-12)</sup> and known as **Hist-Tsoko** in Jel, meaning Elder Hist)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the sixth month of the year. It is a summer month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 30 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> In the [Ayleid](Lore_Ayleid.md) version of the calendar the Mid Year was fifty-seven days long and lasted "all across summer", before it was split by [Pelinal Whitestrake](Lore_Pelinal_Whitestrake.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-SOP-12)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Steed.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Sun's Height
**Sun's Height** (known as **Thtithil-Gah** in Jel, meaning Egg-Basket)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the seventh month of the year. It is a summer month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Apprentice.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Last Seed
**Last Seed** (known as **Thtithil** in Jel, meaning Egg)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the eighth month of the year. It is a fall month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Warrior.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Hearthfire
**Hearthfire** (also known as **Heartfire**,<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup> sometimes spelled **Hearth Fire**<sup>[[13]](#cite_note-2920HF-13)</sup> or **Heart Fire**,<sup>[[14]](#cite_note-AC-14)</sup><sup>[[15]](#cite_note-CS-15)</sup> and known as **Nushmeeko** in Jel, meaning [Lizard](Lore_Lizard.md))<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the ninth month of the year. It is a fall month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 30 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Lady.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup> The *[Book of the Leaves](Lore_Book_of_the_Leaves.md)* places an emphasis on the Hearthfire, highlighting its significance within certain [Druidic](Lore_Druid.md) customs and rituals.<sup>[[16]](#cite_note-OE-16)</sup><sup>[[17]](#cite_note-HL-17)</sup>

### Frostfall
**Frostfall** (also spelled **Frost Fall**<sup>[[18]](#cite_note-LTKM-18)</sup> and known as **Shaja-Nushmeeko** in Jel, meaning Semi-Humanoid Lizard)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the tenth month of the year. It is a fall month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Tower.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Sun's Dusk
**Sun's Dusk** (known as **Saxhleel** in Jel, meaning Argonian)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the eleventh month of the year. It is a winter month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 30 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Atronach.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

### Evening Star
**Evening Star** (known as **Xulomaht** in Jel, meaning The Deceased)<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TSo A-9)</sup> is the twelfth month of the year. It is a winter month.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup> It has 31 days.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> Its associated birthsign is the Thief.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-TF-6)</sup>

## Week
There are seven days in each week. A month usually lasts around four weeks, but may span up to five.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-SR-7)</sup> The days are arranged in the following order:<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-ARguide-8)</sup>

- **Morndas**
- **Tirdas**
- **Middas**
- **Turdas**
- **Fredas**
- **Loredas**
- **Sundas**

## Day
Each day is 24 hours.<sup>[[19]](#cite_note-TESVSPOGG-19)</sup> Some days have special significance as [holidays](Lore_Holidays.md).

## Notes
- The Elder Scrolls calendar shares many similarities with the [Gregorian calendar](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gregorian_calendar). - | [^](#note_Leap) | Potentially due to the observance of [leap years](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leap_year). |
| --- | --- |
- Morning Star was not available in unmodified *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* due to a bug in the game. This was caused by an "off-by-one error": although the "Game Month" value counted months from 0 to 11, the developers incorrectly set it to month 1 after reaching the end of the year, assuming the months would count from 1 to 12. On the PC version, it is possible to change the current month to Morning Star manually by using the console command "set Game Month to 0".
- The ancient [Fauns](Lore_Faun.md) were known to have months with their own variation of names, such as "Hare's Leap" and "Fawn's Cry" which correspond to unknown months.<sup>[[20]](#cite_note-Here-20)</sup>
