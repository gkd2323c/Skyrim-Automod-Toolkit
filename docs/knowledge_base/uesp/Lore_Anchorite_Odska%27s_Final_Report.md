Personal Note: This is it. Tacitus has been [killed](Lore_To_Anchorite_Odska,_From_Black_Mage_Tacitus..md) by [Hadolids](Lore_Hadolid.md). The Hadolids hold me prisoner in a shell on the beach. They're just standing around me, watching every movement I make and mimicking it.

I shot a [bolt of lightning](Lore_Destruction.md) at the bastards. It missed, but then they cast a bolt of their own at the wall, the exact way I did it!

I refuse to die to these spell-copying [crabs](Lore_Crab.md). When they aren't looking, I'll turn their innards into a stew and [reanimate](Lore_Necromancy.md) their shells!

To my fellow Worms, kill every Hadolid you see. Glory to [Mannimarco](Lore_Mannimarco.md)! Glory to the [Black Worm](Lore_Worm_Cult.md)!
