[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/6/66/SR-place-Mor_Khazgur.jpg/200px-SR-place-Mor_Khazgur.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mor_Khazgur.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Mor_Khazgur.jpg) Mor Khazgur **Mor Khazgur** is an [Orc stronghold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orc_Strongholds) in the far northwest of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md), west of [Solitude](Skyrim_Solitude.md) and north of [Markarth](Skyrim_Markarth.md).

The stronghold contains three zones: *[Larak's Longhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse)*, *[Mor Khazgur Cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Cellar)*, and *[Mor Khazgur Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine)*.

The stronghold owns and operates an [orichalcum](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum) mine containing seven [ore veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ore_Vein). Notable NPCs found here include [Chief Larak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak), a master [trainer](Skyrim_Trainers.md) in the [Block](Skyrim_Block.md) skill, and [Borgakh the Steel Heart](Skyrim_Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.md), a potential [follower](Skyrim_Followers.md) and potential [Blade](Skyrim_Blades.md).

As with all Orc Strongholds, you must become [Blood-Kin](Skyrim_Blood-Kin.md) to the [Orcs](Skyrim_Orc.md) before you are welcome in the stronghold and able to barter or receive other services.

## Walkthrough
There is just a single entrance to the stronghold's compound near the northern edge of the surrounding palisade. There is a small vegetable patch immediately on your right upon entering, which contains eleven [cabbages](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Cabbage), eleven [leeks](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leek) and eight [potato](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potato) plants. On the left is a lookout tower, while in front is a tree stump that has a [mora tapinella](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mora_Tapinella) fungus growing from it. Behind the vegetables is an open-sided hut, beneath which is an unlocked [chest](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chest) and a rack hanging from the ceiling holding a [garlic](Skyrim_Garlic.md) braid and [pheasant](Skyrim_Pheasant.md).

The path leads past the [longhouse](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Larak%27s_Longhouse), passing a wooden table and chair on the right where [Chief Larak](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Chief_Larak) is generally found sitting. Beside this is a rack holding three whole [salmon](Skyrim_Salmon_(dead).md). On the other side of the entrance to the longhouse is a practice dummy where [Borgakh the Steel Heart](Skyrim_Borgakh_the_Steel_Heart.md) is normally found training her skill-at-arms. Opposite the longhouse entrance is a second open-sided hut with an [alchemy lab](Skyrim_Alchemy_Lab.md) beside it and a set of shelves under it. These hold an [apothecary's satchel](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Apothecary%27s_Satchel), two random [potions of stamina](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Potion_of_Minor_Stamina), a sample of [fly amanita](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Fly_Amanita) and a sample of [white cap](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:White_Cap) fungi, two [purple](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Purple_Mountain_Flower) and a [red mountain flower](Skyrim_Red_Mountain_Flower.md).

Continuing past the longhouse there is a covered stack of food barrels on the right with a trapdoor leading to [Mor Khazgur Cellar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Cellar) beside them. On the left is a second lookout tower, while ahead is a covered blacksmithing area consisting of a [forge](Skyrim_Forge.md) and [grindstone](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), with a set of shelves at the rear holding a random enchanted [Orcish battleaxe](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Battleaxe), a copy of the [Smithing](Skyrim_Smithing.md) [skill book](Skyrim_Skill_Books.md) *[The Armorer's Challenge](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Armorer%27s_Challenge)*, three owned [orichalcum ingots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orichalcum_Ingot), a [steel sword](Skyrim_Steel_Sword.md), an enchanted [Orcish sword](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Sword) and an [Orcish helmet](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Helmet) and [shield](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Orcish_Shield).

The path continues up and around, passing a second tree stump with mora tapinella fungus growing on it. Further up it passes between another lookout tower and a [smelter](Skyrim_Smelter.md) to the top where [Mor Khazgur Mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mor_Khazgur_Mine) is located. On the right of the entrance are some more food barrels and three non-respawning orichalcum ingots.

## Notes
- In the Dialogue Views files for the game, there exists a line (likely meant for the gate guard) that was meant to quickly establish the stronghold: *"This is Mor Khazgur and we live under chief Larak. The rest is our business."*
- Mor Khazgur also appears in [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online) as [a mine](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Mor_Khazgur) and an [instanced Battleground](Online_Mor_Khazgur_(battleground).md).
