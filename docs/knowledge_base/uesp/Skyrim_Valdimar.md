[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/de/SR-npc-Valdimar.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Valdimar.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valdimar.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Valdimar.jpg) Valdimar **Valdimar** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [sorcerer](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:BYOHHousecarl Hjaalmarch Class) found in [Highmoon Hall](Skyrim_Highmoon_Hall.md) in [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md). He becomes your personal [housecarl](Skyrim_Housecarl.md) when you have earned the title of [Thane](Skyrim_Thane.md) in [Hjaalmarch](Skyrim_Hjaalmarch.md).

Valdimar wears [scaled armor](Skyrim_Scaled_Armor.md) with matching [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Boots) and [bracers](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Scaled_Bracers), and he wields a [steel mace](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Steel_Mace). He knows the [Alteration](Skyrim_Alteration.md) spells [Oakflesh](Skyrim_Oakflesh.md) and [Stoneflesh](Skyrim_Stoneflesh.md), the [Destruction](Skyrim_Destruction.md) spell [Ice Spike](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ice_Spike), and the [Restoration](Skyrim_Restoration.md) spells [Close Wounds](Skyrim_Close_Wounds.md), [Healing](Skyrim_Healing.md), [Lesser Ward](Skyrim_Lesser_Ward.md), and [Turn Undead](Skyrim_Turn_Undead_(spell).md).

## Notes
- Although his default gear is governed by the Light Armor skill and he has a Light Armor skill perk, his class makes him more proficient in Heavy Armor. Similarly, he's carrying a steel mace while having a perk for axes instead, although you can just switch his weapon, unlike his outfit. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.1.0, addresses this issue. It makes him more proficient in Light Armor.
- He is the only housecarl whose default armor is not made of steel.

## Bugs
- When hired as a steward, Valdimar will attempt to travel to Lakeview Manor instead of [Windstad Manor](Skyrim_Windstad_Manor.md) as expected due to an incorrect destination marker in his package. If Windstad Manor has not yet been purchased, he will venture off to [Helgen](Skyrim_Helgen.md) instead. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Hearthfire Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Mod_Unofficial_Hearthfire_Patch), version 1.0.3, fixes this bug.
- Even when not hired as a steward, he may still sometimes appear in Lakeview Manor if you wait or sleep there.
