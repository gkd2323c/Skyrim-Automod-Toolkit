[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b9/SR-npc-Mikael.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Mikael.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mikael.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Mikael.jpg) Mikael performing at the Bannered Mare **Mikael** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) bard [performing](Skyrim_Bardic_Performances.md) at the [Bannered Mare](Skyrim_The_Bannered_Mare.md), and the author of *[A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](Skyrim_A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun.md)*. He believes himself to be quite enthralling to women and is currently attempting to conquer [Carlotta Valentia](Skyrim_Carlotta_Valentia.md) romantically, though she has no interest in him.

Mikael spends all his time at the inn, alternating between playing his instruments and chatting to any women present, whether they are single or married.

He wears a set of farm [clothes](Skyrim_Clothes.md#000209a6) and [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#000209a5). He carries an [iron dagger](Skyrim_Iron_Dagger.md) along with a selection of [common items and gold](Skyrim_Leveled_Lists.md#Common).

## Dialogue
His greetings:

- *"If it's a lady you're looking for, you'd best look elsewhere. Once Mikael gets them, they're got."* (If player is male.)
- *"I'm a bard by trade. Perhaps you've heard of me?"*
- *"I don't have much of a reputation in Skyrim yet, but I'm working on that, one sweet lady at a time. Haha!"*
- *"No doubt they still tell tales of Mikael and his sweet songs across Cyrodiil, where last I traveled."*
- *"I enjoy steady patronage in the Bannered Mare. I do hope you'll watch me perform."*
- *"There was a great battle here in Whiterun. It was quite thrilling! I must compose a song about it someday."* (After completion of Battle for Whiterun.)

When spoken to:

- **Where'd you learn to play?**
- *"There's a whole college up in Solitude where they teach the arts. Song. Lute-playing. Poetry. I studied there myself. Had to leave over a misunderstanding with a headmistress. Well, more a misunderstanding with her husband."*

- **Give me the latest gossip.** (This option is only present if Mikael has taken over The Bannered Mare as innkeeper.)
- *"The Companions? In Jorrvaskr? They're recruiting new members, from what I hear."*
- *"I assume you saw the Gildergreen? Big tree, all burnt up, in the middle of the Wind District. It's part of the Temple of Kynareth. People want Danica to do something about it, but I don't know what they expect."*
- *"Been hearing some strange tales of the Jarl's children. Say the one's turning wicked, and the others have an ill-favored look to them. Best to keep clear."*

When exiting dialogue:

- *"It's the leaving that's hardest, I know."* (If player is female.)
- *"Some parting advice - women love sonnets."* (If player is male.)
- *"Yeah, take care of yourself."*

## Notes
- Internally, Mikael is marked as the boyfriend of [Ysolda](Skyrim_Ysolda.md), despite the fact that he's currently pursuing other women, and that she's a possible marriage candidate. Some lines from Ysolda do indicate that she's romantically interested in him, but they also imply that they are not yet together.

## Bugs
- He goes to the wrong kitchen wing upstairs bed, as he doesn't own that bed. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, fixes this bug.
- [A Gentleman's Guide to Whiterun](Skyrim_A_Gentleman%27s_Guide_to_Whiterun.md) states that he is an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md) and that [Adrianne Avenicci](Skyrim_Adrianne_Avenicci.md) is a Nord, which is the wrong way around. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.2.4, addresses this issue. It has modified the text to reflect that Mikael is not actually an Imperial, along with other changes.
- Depending on your dialogue choices during [A Few Words with You](Skyrim_A_Few_Words_with_You.md), you may hear Mikael refer to Carlotta Valentia as a *"Nord lass"*, though she is in fact an [Imperial](Skyrim_Imperial.md).
