Anonymous Letter **by "A loyal citizen"**

To the [Imperial Legion](Skyrim_Imperial_Legion.md),

Who I am is less important than what I know - suffice it to say that I am no friend to the so-called [Stormcloak](Skyrim_Stormcloaks.md) rebels.

Through various means it has come to my attention that <Alias=Steward> had a rather embarrassing indiscretion in <Alias.Pronoun Pos=Steward> past. I also happen to know that <Alias.Pronoun=Victim> is foolishly keeping evidence of this affair.

Should you locate that evidence, you might be able to "persuade" <Alias.Pronoun Ob=Steward> to help your fight in <Alias=Hold>. If forced to chose *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* between his dislike for the Empire or <Alias.Pronoun Pos=Steward> love for <Alias.Pronoun Ref=Steward>, <Alias.Pronoun=Steward> will most assuredly chose *[[sic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/UESPWiki:Spelling#Books_and_Direct_Quotes)]* the latter.

Sincerely yours,

A loyal citizen.
