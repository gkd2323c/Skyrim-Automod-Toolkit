# Veloise

The Veloise family is a minor [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) noble house<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-ONHCP-1)</sup> hailing from the [Systres Archipelago](Lore_Systres.md). It is specifically a merchant family synonymous with the Veloise Mercantile Trading Consortium, their family business. Their goods circulate from the Systres Archipelago to [Vvardenfell](Lore_Vvardenfell.md) and back again.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-2)</sup>
