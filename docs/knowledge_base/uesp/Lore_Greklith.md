## History
Prince Greklith was born in [3E 392](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_392), the third child of King [Camaron](Lore_Camaron.md) and Queen [Akorithi](Lore_Akorithi.md) of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-DFC-4)</sup> His name was an ancient [Yoku](Lore_Yoku.md) word meaning "strong king", and appropriately he is said to have been a very strong, very healthy boy—unlike the Crown Prince, Arthago. However, his sickly sibling vanished under mysterious circumstances in [3E 400](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_400), making Greklith the heir to the throne.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DFDC-5)</sup>

However, after King Camaron was killed in battle in [3E 403](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_403), Akorithi assumed the regency. Greklith was a regular fixture in the throne room of the Citadel of [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel.md) during her reign.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Daggerfall-1)</sup> Greklith fell victim to a plague in the years following the [Miracle of Peace](Lore_Miracle_of_Peace.md), never having taken the throne.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-NFOS-3)</sup>
