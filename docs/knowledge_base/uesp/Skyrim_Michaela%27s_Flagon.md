[](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Flagon.png) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-icon-misc-Flagon.png) Michaela's Flagon The Michaela's Flagon is a flagon in [Thonnir's House](Skyrim_Thonnir%27s_House.md) to the right of the fireplace, hidden behind a basket. Its appearance is identical to a generic [flagon](Skyrim_Flagon.md). It also weighs and is worth the same.

## Notes
- There is no NPC named Michaela in Skyrim. It's possible that this item initially had something to do with [Laid to Rest](Skyrim_Laid_to_Rest.md), since it has an ID of MS14 and is found in the same town.
