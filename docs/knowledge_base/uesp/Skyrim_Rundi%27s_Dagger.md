[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/f/fe/SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg/200px-SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Steel_Dagger.jpg) A Steel Dagger [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/9/95/SR-place-Rundi%27s_Alter.jpg/200px-SR-place-Rundi%27s_Alter.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rundi%27s_Alter.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-place-Rundi%27s_Alter.jpg) Rundi's Dagger, upon an altar Rundi's Dagger is the weapon found besides its late eponymous owner, [Rundi](Skyrim_Rundi.md), east-southeast of [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md). Its appearance and stats are identical to those of a [steel dagger](Skyrim_Steel_Dagger.md), only being notable besides due to its unique name and owner. As with all steel daggers, it requires one [steel ingot](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) to [temper](Skyrim_Grindstone.md), with the benefits of the tempering being doubled with the [Steel Smithing](Skyrim_Steel_Smithing.md) perk.

The dagger's owner is one of the missing apprentices from the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). After going off on a private expedition to try to create a spectacular recipe for mead that could compete against the popular [Honningbrew Mead](Skyrim_Honningbrew_Mead.md), Rundi died at an unmarked altar, not having completed his task.

## Notes
- This weapon was originally a quest item in the unused College of Winterhold quest, [The Missing Apprentices](Skyrim_Unfinished_Quests.md#The_Missing_Apprentices). For each of the missing apprentices, an item that would be identified as belonging to them (such as this dagger) would be taken to [Phinis Gestor](Skyrim_Phinis_Gestor.md), confirming their grim fate. Despite their quest not making it into the final game, the dagger and Rundi can still be found.
