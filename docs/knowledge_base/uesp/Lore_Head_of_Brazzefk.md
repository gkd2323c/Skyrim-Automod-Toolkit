Head of Brazzefk **by [Merethic Society](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Merethic_Society)** *Notes on a renowned Dwemer alchemist and his experiments with immortality*
---
[Brazzefk](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Brazzefk) was once a [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) alchemist of great renown. Legend holds that his search for immortality culminated in a potion designed to turn him into a living giant of stone, free from the failings of flesh. The potion worked all too well, turning his entire body into a statue, immortal but immobile.

Is this the head of Brazzefk? And is he aware of his surroundings? We can but speculate.
