# Selenu

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/ed/ON-npc-Selenu.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Selenu.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Selenu.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Selenu.jpg) Selenu, progenitor of the bloodline The Selenu are a [vampire](Lore_Vampire.md) bloodline found in the [Iliac Bay](Lore_Iliac_Bay.md) region. Those of the bloodline are resistant to elemental attacks. The bloodline dominates an area of [Hammerfell](Lore_Hammerfell.md), specifically the regions of [Abibon-Gora](Lore_Abibon-Gora.md), [Ayasofya](Lore_Ayasofya.md), [Pothago](Lore_Pothago.md), [Cybiades](Lore_Cybiades.md), [Sentinel](Lore_Sentinel_(kingdom).md), [Kairou](Lore_Kairou.md) and [Myrkwasa](Lore_Myrkwasa_(fiefdom).md). They are the enemies of the [Vraseth](Lore_Vraseth.md) and [Montalion](Lore_Montalion.md) bloodlines.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DFFactions-1)</sup>

The bloodline is named after [Selenu](Lore_Selenu_(person).md), a [Nedic](Lore_Nede.md) tribal elder from a coven of nomadic Nedes from ancient [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). She was involved in the attempted cremation of [Lamae Bal](Lore_Lamae_Bal.md), the first vampire. After awakening on the pyre, Lamae brutally ripped the throats of the women of the tribe, seemingly passing on the gift of vampirism in the process.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Sot BM-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-OLBTM-3)</sup>
