Bounty: Cleaning Our Waterways
---
By order of Jarl <Alias=Jarl>:

Some have taken to using the water that runs through <Alias=Town> as a place to dispose of empty bottles, worn boots, and other unwanted things. Not only is this unsightly, it poses a danger to our citizens. Sadly, we do not have the men to spare to clean up this mess.

Any who can fish up at least <Global=cc BGSSSE001_Radiant Obj Total> pieces of trash from <Alias=Town>'s waters shall be rewarded. Help us keep our city safe and clean for everyone.
