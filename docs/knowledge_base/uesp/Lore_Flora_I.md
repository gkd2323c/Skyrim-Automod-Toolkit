## Imp Stool
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d8/SR-flora-Imp_Stool.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Imp_Stool.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Imp_Stool.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Imp_Stool.jpg) Imp Stool mushrooms Imp Stools are short and rounded orange-capped, yellow, or golden-brown mushrooms.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONIMP-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup> They grow in caves, and outside in the shade of trees or rocks. They grow in groups of ten or more.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-BKIMP-3)</sup> They can also be found on the bodies of [Shroom Beetles](Lore_Shroom_Beetle.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ONIMP-1)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online), [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Blades)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Imp Stool](Skyrim_Imp_Stool.md) (Skyrim), [Imp Stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Imp_Stool) (ESO), [Imp Stool](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Imp_Stool) (Blades)

## Ironstalk Mushroom
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/75/ON-quest-Tarnished_Truffles.jpg/200px-ON-quest-Tarnished_Truffles.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Tarnished_Truffles.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-Tarnished_Truffles.jpg) Ironstalk Mushrooms Ironstalk Mushrooms are transient fungi that were brought to the [Clockwork City](Lore_Clockwork_City.md) by outsiders. They had adapted to the realm's synthetic and inhospitable metallic soil<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> and can be found in the Everwound Wellspring<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup> and the Halls of Regulation.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-6)</sup> If the mushrooms undergo an extensive pickling process, they can be made edible.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-7)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Online)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Ironstalk Mushroom](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Clockwork_City_Quest_Items#Ironstalk_Mushroom)

## Ironwood
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/a/ac/SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg/200px-SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-flora-Ironwood_Tree.jpg) Ironwood Trees The Ironwood is a tree that grows deep in the forests of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md). They are known for their durability, which is as hard as [iron](Lore_Iron.md), hence the name. The black variant is extremely rare and is said to produce the very succulent [Ironwood nut](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ironwood_Nut). It is believed that anyone who can break open the nut will enhance their strength.<sup>[[8]](#cite_note-SFo T-8)</sup> These trees also produce [Ironwood fruit](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md), prized for its ability to treat severe [disease](Lore_Disease.md) such as [ataxia](Skyrim_Ataxia.md).<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Nft To K-9)</sup> There is also an orange variant, found in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s warmer climes.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-ONContraband-10)</sup> In the mid- [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md), the [Order of the New Moon](Lore_Order_of_the_New_Moon.md) used dark ironwood to craft their [staves](Lore_Staves.md).<sup>[[11]](#cite_note-CM81-11)</sup> Although Ironwood trees were once a staple of [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md)'s forests, by [4E 201](Lore_Fourth_Era.md#4E_201) the tree was nearly exinct in that province and could only be found in more southernly orchards or secluded grottos.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Nft To K-9)</sup> One grove was known to survive within [Iron Tusk Cave](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Iron_Tusk_Cave) on the island of [Giant's Tooth](Lore_Giant%27s_Tooth.md) in the icy [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md).<sup>[[12]](#cite_note-12)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Ironwood_Nut), [Skyrim (Creation Club)](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md)

**Harvested Ingredient:** [Ironwood Nut](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ironwood_Nut), [Ironwood Fruit](Skyrim_Ironwood_Fruit.md)
