Elara was a [Breton](Lore_Breton.md) princess of [Evermore](Lore_Evermore.md) in the sixth century of the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). She was the daughter of [King Eamond](Lore_Eamond.md) and [Queen Arzhela](Lore_Arzhela.md) and had a brother named Adrien. Through her father, Elara was a member of [House Guimard](Lore_Guimard.md), and she was related to High King [Emeric](Lore_Emeric.md) through her mother.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TRHo KE-1)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), Elara was ambushed, gravely injured, and presumed dead.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONStenwick-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-ONAntieve-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-ONGlarikha-4)</sup> She was found in a coma on the side of a road by Stenwick Gurles, the [Viridian Sentinel](Lore_Viridian_Sentinel.md), who rescued her and took her back to the Viridian Woods to attempt to heal her.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONStenwick-2)</sup> Gurles rescued Elara because he planned for her to succeed him as the Viridian Sentinel.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-ONStenwick-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONDemara-5)</sup> With the help of an adventurer, Gurles was able to awaken Elara by channeling his energy into a salve to heal her. Gurles used all his energy to heal Elara, and he died soon afterward. Before his death, Gurles sent the adventurer to retrieve a bud from one of the Voice of the Forest's branches so Elara could undergo the ritual to become the Viridian Sentinel, which was the only way for Elara to survive for more than a fortnight. It is unknown if Elara survived the ritual to become the Viridian Sentinel.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-ESOEQ-6)</sup>
