Kintyra Septim I was the third [empress](Lore_Cyrodilic_Emperors.md) of the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) and [Septim Dynasty](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md), reigning for twelve years, from [3E 41](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_41) to [3E 53](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_53). She was preceded by her first cousin once removed, [Pelagius I](Lore_Pelagius_I.md), and was succeeded by her son, [Uriel I](Lore_Uriel_I.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup>

## Biography
Kintyra Septim was the daughter of [Agnorith](Lore_Agnorith.md), [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md)'s brother.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> She had a son, [Uriel](Lore_Uriel_I.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup> Prior to [3E 41](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_41), Kintyra reigned as the Queen of [Silvenar](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Silvenar).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> In 3E 41, her first cousin once removed, Emperor [Pelagius I](Lore_Pelagius_I.md), was assassinated by the [Dark Brotherhood](Lore_Dark_Brotherhood.md) in the [Temple of the One](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Temple_of_the_One) in the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-AMd-4)</sup> Pelagius I had no living children, so the [Ruby Throne](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ruby_Throne) passed to Kintyra.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TTET-3)</sup> Kintyra I's reign was a period of prosperity and bountiful harvests.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> She was an avid patroness of art, music, and dance.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup> In [3E 53](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_53), Kintyra I died, so her son, Uriel I, succeeded her as Emperor of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-BHOTE1-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DC-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-BA-5)</sup><sup>:16</sup>

## Notes
- There is tale of [Rajhin](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Rajhin) stealing a tattoo from the neck of Empress Kintyra.<sup>[[6]](#cite_note-VOFtit E-6)</sup> This tale was mentioned during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md), centuries before Kintyra I's reign.<sup>[[7]](#cite_note-VOFt K-7)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-Ro S-8)</sup> This could be [an error](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/General:Lore_Inconsistencies), or could refer to an entirely different empress named Kintyra.
- Kintyra I's great-great-great-granddaughter, [Kintyra II](Lore_Kintyra_II.md), was named after her.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-TQW4-9)</sup>
