# Atlatl

Atlatls (also called **flinging-sticks**) are throwing devices employed to launch spears or [darts](Lore_Throwing_Weapons.md) with increased force and accuracy,<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TC-1)</sup> in similar fashion to a [slings](Lore_Sling.md) but used for different types of projectiles.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-CA-2)</sup>

They have applications outside of combat or hunting. Similar to slings, which find use in [Orcish](Lore_Orc.md) traditional [sports](Lore_Sport.md),<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TSOC-3)</sup> atlatls are also utilized for recreational purposes in Argonian games. In the traditional Murkwater game "The Toad Flies at Dusk," players utilize enameled flinging-sticks that terminate in shallow cups. Based on the name, these specialized flinging-sticks, also known as toad-atlatls, are likely used to launch frogs. Some of them were cherished heirlooms belonging to chieftains of Argonian tribes.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-TC-1)</sup>

Some sources mention other implements known as sling-spears.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-MUT-4)</sup>
