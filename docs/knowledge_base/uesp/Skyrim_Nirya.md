[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5b/SR-npc-Nirya.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Nirya.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nirya.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Nirya.jpg) Nirya **Nirya** is a [High Elf](Skyrim_High_Elf.md) [sorceress](Skyrim_CombatSorcerer.md) and a scholar at the [College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_of_Winterhold_(place).md). She does not offer any training to you nor does she offer any kind of merchant services. She is not on good terms with [Faralda](Skyrim_Faralda.md).

Nirya will spend the morning and evening in the [Hall of Attainment](Skyrim_Hall_of_Attainment.md) and much of her day in [The Arcanaeum](Skyrim_The_Arcanaeum.md). She wears [expert robes of Destruction](Skyrim_Expert_Robes_of_Destruction.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0006b46c).

[Sergius Turrianus](Skyrim_Sergius_Turrianus.md) is also a reason for the rivalry between Nirya and Faralda, as they are both marked as his secret lovers. She visits Sergius at 11pm for three hours, before heading off to bed.

## Dialogue
Nirya may greet you with;

- *"It's a small group of us here at the College. Favors don't go unremembered. Neither do affronts."*
- *"If you're working on any experiments, please clean up after yourself."*
- *"Just like anywhere else, it's all about who you know. Lucky for you, you know me."*
- *"I think the College is due for a change in leadership."*

Should you [become Arch-Mage](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md), however, her tone will change as she will quite obviously try to win your favor by saying things like;

- *"May I just say, it's a pleasure to have you leading the College. Really."*
- *"I'm sure you've made note of who's been the most helpful during your time here, Arch-Mage."*
- *"If there's ever any way I can assist you, please let me know."*
- *"Ancano is exactly why we're so restrictive about access to the College. We'd all have perished if it weren't for you."*

When first spoken to, Nirya will vaguely comment;

- *"It's lies. All of it. I don't care what she told you, it's all lies."*

- - **Who are you talking about?**
- *"Faralda. Don't act like she didn't say anything. I know her, I know what she's been up to."*

- - **I have no idea what you're talking about.**
- *"No? Well, then she hasn't gotten to you yet. But she will. And you don't you believe a word of it."*

- - **No one has said anything about you.**
- *"Oh she will, mark my words. And don't you let her."*

- **What's your problem with Faralda?**
- *"My problem with her? She's the one who has a problem with me! She's threatened by me! By my skill as a wizard, by my elegance and posture, by my superior good looks! But she's not going to get the better of me, oh no. I won't let it get to me one bit. That's what she's after."*

## Quest-Related Events
Like most members of the College, Nirya will express suspicion of [Ancano](Skyrim_Ancano.md), [Arch-Mage Aren](Skyrim_Savos_Aren.md)'s advisor:

- **What do you know about Ancano?**
- *"Don't trust that one. He's up to something. Most here are up to something, of course, but in his case, it's not good. I haven't figured it out yet, but I will. He is rather handsome though, isn't he?"*

[Asking](Skyrim_Good_Intentions.md) if she has ever heard of the [Augur of Dunlain](Skyrim_Augur_of_Dunlain.md) will yield an unhelpful response from her:

- **Have you ever heard of the Augur of Dunlain?**
- *"It's not enough that you've stolen the spotlight with this Eye thing, now you're looking for the Augur? I'm not sure what you're playing at, but you'd do well to remember your place around here."*

As you complete more quests for the College, Ancano will eventually [unleash disaster](Skyrim_Containment.md) on [Winterhold](Skyrim_Winterhold.md) when he takes control of the Eye of Magnus, proving Nirya's suspicions. During this, she will ask you;

- *"What's going on?!"*

When you set out to [retrieve](Skyrim_The_Staff_of_Magnus.md) the [Staff of Magnus](Skyrim_Staff_of_Magnus.md), she will remark;

- *"We never should have pulled that thing out of Saarthal."*

During [The Eye of Magnus](Skyrim_The_Eye_of_Magnus.md), she can be found saying;

- *"Why doesn't someone do something?"*

### The Black Star
During [The Black Star](Skyrim_The_Black_Star.md), asking if there is an elven mage in the College that studies stars will make her suspicious:

- **Is there an elven mage here that studies stars?**
- *"Did you say an 'elf' and 'stars'? I hope you don't know what you're asking."*

- **What is it? What happened?**
- *"There was an... unfortunate incident a few years ago. An elven mage named [Nelacar](Skyrim_Nelacar.md) was exiled from the College over it. He's still in town, getting by plying spells to the people inside [the Frozen Hearth](Skyrim_The_Frozen_Hearth.md). Disgraceful."*

If you instead state that you were given a vision, she will reply with;

- **Would you believe I was given a vision?**
- *"You were given a vision? Are you serious? Whatever. The person you probably want is Nelacar. He's in the Frozen Hearth. Be careful, he was exiled from the College for a reason."*

- **Arniel**: *"Hello, Nirya."*
- **Nirya**: *"Don't even talk to me, Gane."*

Nirya may also visit the Arcanaeum where she may talk to [Urag gro-Shub](Skyrim_Urag_gro-Shub.md):

- **Nirya:** *"I was wondering. Has Faralda been in recently? Borrowed any books that you can recall?"*

- **Nirya:** *"Did she, perhaps, mention any dangerous research she might be working on? Anything that could get her in trouble?"*

## Notes
- When asked about Ancano, her voice will change and be completely different.
- Internally, Sergius and Nirya are marked as secret lovers, with Sergius and Faralda being marked as secret lovers as well. This secret relationship is hinted at by Nirya's dialogue with Urag, but is otherwise never adressed by anyone in-game, and no one will ever explicitly talk about it with you. Nirya also visits Sergius, but the two never share any scenes. Nirya also doesn't share any scenes with Faralda, and neither does Sergius. The only other time this apparent love triangle gets addressed, is in *[Prima Official Game Guide](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Books:The_Elder_Scrolls_V:_Skyrim:_Prima_Official_Game_Guide)*, in the character biographies of the relevant NPCs. - The respective summary for Nirya reads:

- - *"Nirya is supremely confident of her abilities and is far more interested in jostling for a leadership position than improving her skills. As Faralda seems to be the only other mage to share these ambitions, she's decided to take her down.
Sergius provides insight as to how to go about doing this, and while Nirya has a sense that he may be using her, she can't imagine what his motivation is and so she doesn't worry about it."*

## Bugs
- If you gain entrance to the College by telling Faralda you are the Dragonborn and showing her your Thu'um, the miscellaneous quest to [Visit the College of Winterhold](Skyrim_College_Objective_Quest.md) will remain incomplete in your log and will point at Nirya indefinitely. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.1, fixes this bug.
- Nirya will never initiate dialogue with her fellow College members inside the Hall of Attainment, as all the scenes were set up to only take place in the [Hall of Countenance](Skyrim_Hall_of_Countenance.md) where none of them will ever meet. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 1.3.3, fixes this bug.
- At times, she will completely ignore her schedule and instead follow you whenever you are in a close range to her, but not at a distance. When you are farther away, she will remain in the area she stopped at indefinitely. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Skyrim Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Skyrim_Patch), version 2.0.4, fixes this bug.

- [![On PC](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)](https://www.wikipedia.org/wiki/PC_game) Typing `setstage mgmainquestbridge 200` into the console will fix it.}
