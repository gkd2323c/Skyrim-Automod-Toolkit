[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e8/LO-map-Skyrim_%28PGE1%29.jpg/200px-LO-map-Skyrim_%28PGE1%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Skyrim_(PGE1).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:LO-map-Skyrim_(PGE1).jpg) A Second Era Map showing the location of Hrothgar Hrothgar is a mountainous territory located in central [Skyrim](Lore_Skyrim.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-PGE1Map-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-3)</sup> that neighbors or overlaps with [Whiterun Hold](Lore_Whiterun_Hold.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup><sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> It is home to the mount of [Monahven](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Monahven),<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-Adventure Game-4)</sup> better known as the Throat of the World, which is the tallest mountain in Tamriel and is where the [Greybeards](Lore_Greybeards.md) of [High Hrothgar](Lore_High_Hrothgar.md) dwell.<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-PGE1S-6)</sup> It is also said to be home to region known as Vakkervanir, a lost valley of waterfalls. [Snow Whales](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Snow_Whale) are claimed to soar in the skies over Hrothgar, around High Hrothgar specifically.<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-TPFrom The Ashes-7)</sup>

Near the end of the [Third Era](Lore_Third_Era.md) (circa [3E 432](Lore_Third_Era.md#3E_432)), the [Hörme](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:H%C3%B6rme) bandits (an uprising in Skyrim, believing that [Potema](Lore_Potema.md) and her [son](Lore_Uriel_III.md) were the last true [Septims](Lore_Septim_Dynasty.md)) attacked across Hrothgar and Whiterun. This in addition to [frost troll](Lore_Frost_Troll.md) attacks and a "series of annihilating winters of alternating floods, droughts, and fires" had "crippled the area that was once considered the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) of Skyrim."<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-PGE3S-2)</sup>
