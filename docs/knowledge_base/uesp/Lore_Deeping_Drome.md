[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/3/35/ON-place-Deeping_Drome.jpg/200px-ON-place-Deeping_Drome.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Deeping_Drome.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-place-Deeping_Drome.jpg) Deeping Drome in 2E 582 [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5f/ON-load-Deeping_Drome.jpg/200px-ON-load-Deeping_Drome.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Deeping_Drome.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-load-Deeping_Drome.jpg) Deeping Drome in 2E 582 The Deeping Drome is a [dwarven ruin](Lore_Dwemer_Ruins.md) located underneath the mountains in the northernmost parts of the [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md) in the province of [High Rock](Lore_High_Rock.md), east of [Wrothgar](Lore_Wrothgar.md) and west of [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DD-1)</sup>

As a Dwarven settlement, it was abandoned in [1E 700](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_700) when its original [Dwemer](Lore_Dwemer.md) inhabitants mysteriously disappeared.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-DII-2)</sup> Scholars of Dwemer were unable to determine the function of the mysterious Deeping Drome.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-DD2-3)</sup>

As of [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) the [Circle of Champions](Lore_Circle_of_Champions.md) began using the ruins to host dangerous gladiator battles to the death. Fighters from all three sides of the [Three Banners War](Lore_Three_Banners_War.md) came to the Deeping Drome battleground to fight for the glory of the Fire Drakes, Pit Daemons, and Storm Lords arena teams.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup> During that time it was described as "claustrophobic pathways filled with the bones of would-be champions".<sup>[[5]](#cite_note-DB-5)</sup> It was made up of large fungi-covered cavern formations surrounding a large metallic sphere in the center. Deeping Drome also consisted of slopes and small skeleton-filled tunnels beneath the central platform.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-DD-1)</sup>
