Fargrave: A City of Myth *An introduction to a book about Fargrave* *
---
[Fargrave](Lore_Fargrave.md) may seem to some just an obscure demiplane of [Oblivion](Lore_Oblivion.md), but it has a rich history and an important role to serve. Functioning as a crossroads, Fargrave finds itself well-trodden by interplanar travelers.

For example, it would not at all be surprising to see a mage hailing from [Summerset](Lore_Summerset.md) trading tales with a [Xivkyn](Lore_Xivkyn.md) [Grasp of the Stricture](Lore_Grasp_of_the_Stricture.md). Because of this, Fargrave is an exciting locale for any keen adventurer. In fact, when last I visited there, I witnessed a [Gleaner of Aubris](Lore_Gleaners_of_Aurbis.md) interrogate a pickpocketing [Scamp](Lore_Scamp.md), something you'd be hard pressed to see in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md)'s mundane countryside.

I hope that this introduction to Fargrave's rich history will make the demiplane all the more enticing to adventurers new to interplanar travel.
