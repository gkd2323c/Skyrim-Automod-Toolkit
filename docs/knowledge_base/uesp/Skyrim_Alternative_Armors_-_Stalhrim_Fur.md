## Official Summary
*Includes four pieces of heavy Stalhrim armor as featured in The Elder Scrolls: Blades! Quest by Kris Takahashi. (Quest "Ancient Ice" starts by reading Skjol's Journal at the camp south-southwest of Skaal Village.)*
