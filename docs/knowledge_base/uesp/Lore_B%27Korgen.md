B'Korgen (also referred to as **Bekor-gen**)<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ER-1)</sup> was a [Sea Sload](Lore_Sea_Sload.md) sorcerer proficient in [alchemy](Lore_Alchemy.md), [fleshcrafting](Lore_Flesh_Magic.md), and [mind magic](Lore_Mind_Magic.md). He was a member of Abyssal Cabal, collaborating with the [Court of Bedlam](Lore_Court_of_Bedlam.md) with the shared goal of sinking the [Summerset Isle](Lore_Summerset_Isle.md) during the [Interregnum](Lore_Interregnum.md).<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-B.27Korgen-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IJA-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-JF-4)</sup>

## History

### Summerset Invasion
[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/1/19/ON-quest-The_Abyssal_Alchemist.jpg/200px-ON-quest-The_Abyssal_Alchemist.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Abyssal_Alchemist.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-quest-The_Abyssal_Alchemist.jpg) B'Korgen in Welenkin Cove Little is known about B'Korgen's past, aside from his the fact that he was a Sea Sload adept in alchemy, fleshcrafting, and mind magic.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-B.27Korgen-2)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Alt-5)</sup><sup>[[6]](#cite_note-Prima-6)</sup> At some point he joined the Abyssal Cabal, collaborating with other Sea Sloads such as K'Tora, the master of mind magic, the [necromancer](Lore_Necromancy.md) K'Garza, and the [shadow mage](Lore_Shadow_Magic.md) Z'Maja.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ER-1)</sup><sup>[[7]](#cite_note-AC-7)</sup>

In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), B'Korgen journeyed to Welenkin Cove, west of [Alinor](Lore_Alinor_(city).md), turning it into a hazardous lair for his alchemical experiments. In addition to his efforts to empower Yaghra,<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-JF-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAA-8)</sup> he also undertook the preparation of Abyssal Pearls for the Abyssal Geysers plaguing the Summerset Isle at that time.<sup>[[9]](#cite_note-Miriya-9)</sup> The military of [Aldmeri Dominion](Lore_Aldmeri_Dominion.md), in collaboration with hired mercenaries, conducted thorough research on the Sea Sload incursion. By spying on B'Korgen in Welenkin Cove, they learned that B'Korgen tended to a bed of [abyssal pearls](Lore_Pearl.md). He was infusing these pearls with alchemical mixtures and strange sigils. A cloaked Altmer, associated with the Court of Bedlam, visited B'Korgen, revealing their collaboration with the Abyssal Cabal. Their conversation hinted at a larger plan involving the destructive [Abyssal Geysers](Lore_Abyssal_Geysers.md), aiming to wreak havoc on Summerset and sink the island. Urgently, the military reported these findings to the Battlereeve for further action.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-IJA-3)</sup>

The [Divine Prosecution](Lore_Divine_Prosecution.md) soon obtained information indicating that B'Korgen employed alchemy to empower the Abyssal Cabal and transform [Yaghra](Lore_Yaghra.md) into more formidable adversaries. The Divine Prosecution took notice, contracting mercenaries to eliminate this threat. The mercenaries received the mission of eradicating the tainted coral, a vital component in B'Korgen's alchemical experiments, and dispatching the Sea Sload to avert additional chaos on the island. Ultimately, their efforts proved successful, leading to B'Korgen's demise.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-JF-4)</sup><sup>[[8]](#cite_note-TAA-8)</sup>

### Alternative Fate
The Psijic Order had the means to scry secrets from [fate](Lore_Fate.md) lines beyond the bounds of Tamriel. Among these secrets were tomes that spoke of [alternative timelines and realities](Lore_Many_Paths.md), where certain key events transpired differently.<sup>[[10]](#cite_note-10)</sup> In one of these alternate realities, the Abyssal Cabal succeeded in conquering the Summerset Isles. B'Korgen instituted a program of fleshcrafting which the Altmer were "urged" to join so their bodies could be given aquatic features and weapons in preparation for a coming invasion of mainland Tamriel, Yaghra were also taken by B'Korgen as apprentices, learning the magic of fleshcrafting for themselves. <sup>[[5]](#cite_note-Alt-5)</sup>
