[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/b8/SR-creature-Bilgemuck.jpg/200px-SR-creature-Bilgemuck.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bilgemuck.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-creature-Bilgemuck.jpg) Bilgemuck **Bilgemuck** is a unique [bristleback](Skyrim_Bristleback.md) found on Solstheim. He can be encountered during the quest [The Chief of Thirsk Hall](Skyrim_The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall.md), when the [chief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Chief) asks you to bring Bilgemuck back to his pen. Bilgemuck drops [boar tusks](Skyrim_Boar_Tusk.md) and [boar meat](Skyrim_Boar_Meat.md) if he is killed.

## Related Quests
- **[The Chief of Thirsk Hall](Skyrim_The_Chief_of_Thirsk_Hall.md)**: Help out a [Riekling Chief](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Riekling_Chief) at [Thirsk Mead Hall](Skyrim_Thirsk_Mead_Hall.md).

## Notes
- During [Retaking Thirsk](Skyrim_Retaking_Thirsk.md), Bilgemuck won't turn hostile to the attacking Nords. After the hall has been retaken, Bilgemuck will remain outside indefinitely. - ![PC Only](https://images.uesp.net/d/d7/Computer.svg)
The [Unofficial Dragonborn Patch](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim_Mod:Unofficial_Dragonborn_Patch), version 2.0.2, addresses this issue. He is disabled if you side with the Nords.
