On the Chamber of Passage **by [Septima Tharn](Lore_Books_by_Author.md#Septima_Tharn)** *A warning to anyone thinking of interrupting important research*
---
Attention: All Members of the [7th Legion](Lore_Seventh_Legion.md)

Since we breached the seals on the [Chamber of Passage](Lore_Hall_of_Heroes.md), many of you have speculated on what I am doing within. Lest you entertain the idea of taking a peek, let me explain what will happen.

The magical forces in the space will permit me to exercise a unique form of punishment. When I subject you to these forces, you will experience the rapid liquification of your skin and your internal organs, your bones will then wither and crumble within minutes, and finally, your soul will be subjected to re-experiencing this phenomenon for all eternity. Please consider this before interrupting my work.

— Magus-General [Septima Tharn](Lore_Septima_Tharn.md)
