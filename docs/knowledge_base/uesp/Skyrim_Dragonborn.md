[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/2/2e/SR-cover-Dragonborn_Box_Art.jpg/200px-SR-cover-Dragonborn_Box_Art.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-cover-Dragonborn_Box_Art.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-cover-Dragonborn_Box_Art.jpg) *Dragonborn* box art ***Dragonborn*** is the third major [official add-on](Skyrim_Add-ons.md) for *[Skyrim](Skyrim_Skyrim.md)*. The existence of this expansion was first alluded to by patch files found in the [1.8 Patch](Skyrim_Patch.md#Version_1.8), and it was confirmed by Bethesda on 5 November 2012 with an official announcement trailer. *Dragonborn* takes place on the island of [Solstheim](Lore_Solstheim.md), first visited many moons ago during *[Bloodmoon](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Bloodmoon:Bloodmoon)*. The add-on's plot tasks you with uncovering the identity of the person seemingly controlling the entire population of the island, and stopping them.

## Notes
- *Dragonborn* was subject to a 30-day exclusivity deal with Microsoft, during which it was only available on the Xbox 360. In February 2013, it was released on both PC and PS3.
- The [soundtrack](Skyrim_Music.md#Dragonborn) that plays on Solstheim contains several pieces from the *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)* [soundtrack](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Music).
