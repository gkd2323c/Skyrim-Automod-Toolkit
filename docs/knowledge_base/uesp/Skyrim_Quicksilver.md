# Quicksilver

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/da/SR-item-Quicksilver_Ingot.jpg/200px-SR-item-Quicksilver_Ingot.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Quicksilver_Ingot.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-item-Quicksilver_Ingot.jpg) Quicksilver ingot [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/d/d3/SR-activity-Mining_%28Quicksilver%29.jpg/200px-SR-activity-Mining_%28Quicksilver%29.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Quicksilver).jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-activity-Mining_(Quicksilver).jpg) Quicksilver in its various forms **Quicksilver** is a metal with a low melting point that is melded with [Moonstone](Skyrim_Moonstone_Ingot.md) when [forging](Skyrim_Forge.md) [elven weapons](Skyrim_Elven.md) and [elven gilded armor](Skyrim_Elven_Gilded_Armor.md), and with [Ebony](Skyrim_Ebony_Ingot.md) and [Steel](Skyrim_Steel_Ingot.md) when forging [Nordic carved armor and weapons](Skyrim_Nordic.md).

- 3 quicksilver ores are obtained by [mining](Skyrim_Mining.md) quicksilver ore veins.
- 1 quicksilver ingot is obtained by [smelting](Skyrim_Smelter.md) 2 quicksilver ores.

## Mineral Sources
In addition to the following guaranteed sources, the best random locations are:

- Quicksilver ingots may be sold by blacksmiths starting at level 18 and general goods [merchants](Skyrim_Merchants.md) starting at level 10.
- Quicksilver ore may be randomly found in Falmer loot starting at level 5.

There are a total of 57 veins, 10 ores, and 13 ingots available in the game. The following list only includes notable locations, in particular locations where multiple veins are located near each other and interior areas containing veins or ingots. The [Quicksilver Veins](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Category:Skyrim-Places-Quicksilver_Veins) category contains a more complete list of places containing single veins, and all exterior ore veins are shown on [(map)](https://gamemap.uesp.net/sr/?world=Skyrim&centeron=the+Skyrim+map&search=Quicksilver+Ore+Vein).

## Notes
- 1 quicksilver ingot is needed at [grindstones](Skyrim_Grindstone.md) to improve [Bolar's Oathblade](Skyrim_Bolar%27s_Oathblade.md) and [Dragonbane](Skyrim_Dragonbane.md).
- 1 quicksilver ingot is needed at [workbenches](Skyrim_Workbench.md) to improve [elven gilded armor](Skyrim_Elven_Gilded_Armor.md).
- Quicksilver ingots are used to improve [Nordic](Skyrim_Nordic.md) weapons and armor. <sup>[DB](Skyrim_Dragonborn.md)</sup>
- 2 quicksilver ingots are need to build the Stendarr Shrine in each Hearthfire house [cellar](Skyrim_Main_Hall.md#Main_Hall:_Cellar). <sup>[HF](Skyrim_Hearthfire.md)</sup>
- Quicksilver ores can be sold to [Leigelf](Skyrim_Leigelf.md) for the quest [Mine Ore](Skyrim_Mine_Ore.md).
- Ore veins (all types) sometimes do not respawn normally. See [Mining (Bugs)](Skyrim_Mining.md#Bugs) and elsewhere on that article and its talk page.
