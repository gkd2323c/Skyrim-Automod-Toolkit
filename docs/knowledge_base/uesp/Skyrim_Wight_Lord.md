# Wight Lord

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/7/7c/Skyrim-anniversary-edition-oblivion.jpg/200px-Skyrim-anniversary-edition-oblivion.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim-anniversary-edition-oblivion.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:Skyrim-anniversary-edition-oblivion.jpg) Promotional image of one of Rielle's Wights from Skyrim Anniversary Edition. **Wight Lords** are an enemy type added by [The Cause](Skyrim_The_Cause.md) creation, found in the ancient ruin of [Rielle](Skyrim_Rielle.md). They are the toughest form of Ayleid [Wight](Skyrim_Wight.md) you will face, and boast similar stats to [Draugr Scourges](Skyrim_Draugr.md#Draugr_Scourge) and [Draugr Deathlords](Skyrim_Draugr.md#Draugr_Deathlord). Several of them carry Elemental Shards that you will need to open the final area of Rielle Crypt. The mages are able to cast [Ice Spike](Skyrim_Ice_Spike.md) and [conjure Frost Atronachs](Skyrim_Conjure_Frost_Atronach.md) to aid them in battle.
