# Lefthand Empire


While most sources deduce that they were a variant of [Mer](Lore_Mer.md) (presumably of [Aldmer](Lore_Aldmer.md) ancestry, like other elves), others question this narrative and claim this was a Yokudan transcription error where "Elf" and "enemy" are essentially the same, and that Lefthanded Elves were simply Lefthanded enemies.<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SH2-1)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-LHbelt-4)</sup>

Regardless, it is said their vast empire was four times the size the [Third Empire](Lore_Third_Empire.md) under [Tiber Septim](Lore_Tiber_Septim.md), which itself accounted for the entirety of [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md).<sup>[[UOL 1]](#cite_note-LVSMCt R-2)</sup> Eventually the Yokudans defeated the Lefthanded Elves and drove them out of Yokuda in the Merethic Era, although some remnants remained on outlying islands such as [Moni](Lore_Moni.md). They briefly settled in some parts of western [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md) such as on [Amenos](Lore_Amenos.md) in [1E 660](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_660) before being wiped out by the encroaching [Ra Gada](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Lore:Ra_Gada) in [1E 785](Lore_First_Era.md#1E_785).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-SH2-1)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-So A-5)</sup>
