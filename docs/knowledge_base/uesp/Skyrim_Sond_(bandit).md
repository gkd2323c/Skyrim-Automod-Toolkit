[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/55/SR-npc-Sond.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Sond.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sond.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Sond.jpg) Sond relaxing by the river **Sond** is a [Nord](Skyrim_Nord.md) [bandit](Skyrim_Bandit_(class).md) who can be found in the [Eldergleam Sanctuary](Skyrim_Eldergleam_Sanctuary.md). During the related quest, he along with [Asta](Skyrim_Asta.md), will warn you not to desecrate the [Eldergleam](Lore_Eldergleam.md), otherwise you will face consequences brought about by [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md).

He wears a [belted tunic](Skyrim_Belted_Tunic.md) and a pair of [boots](Skyrim_Boots.md#0001be1b). He carries a leveled [one-handed weapon](Skyrim_One-Handed_Weapons.md) which can be up to [elven](Skyrim_Elven.md) in quality along with a small amount of gold.

## Related Quests
- **[The Blessings of Nature](Skyrim_The_Blessings_of_Nature.md)**: Restore the [Gildergreen](Lore_Gildergreen.md) for [Danica Pure-Spring](Skyrim_Danica_Pure-Spring.md).

## Quest-Related Dialogue
Sond will greet you with:

- *"Welcome to Eldergleam Sanctuary, my friend. If you have any questions, speak to Asta."*
- *"Kynareth humbles us with her beauty."*

Once you show [Nettlebane](Skyrim_Nettlebane.md) to [Asta](Skyrim_Asta.md) or strike the Eldergleam's roots with it, he'll change his tone:

- *"Don't come any closer. I don't want Kynareth to think I'm on your side."*

## Notes
- Sond does not have a unique facial model, he instead uses the default male Nord face. He shares this quirk with numerous other NPCs, including [Erikur](Skyrim_Erikur.md), [Briehl](Skyrim_Briehl.md), [Lynoit](Skyrim_Lynoit.md), [Barknar](Skyrim_Barknar_(Dawnguard).md), and many other generic NPCs.
