## Haddock
The **Haddock** is a type of fish.

- The **Ghost Haddock** is a type of haddock that can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), off the coast of [Eastmarch](Lore_Eastmarch.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Hagfish
The **Hagfish** is a type of slimy fish. They are cooked with [garlic](Lore_Garlic.md) in [Lilmoth](Lore_Lilmoth.md) and their viscera is used in the creation of [Sload Soap](Lore_Sload_Soap.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-2)</sup>

- The **Hag Fen Hagfish** is a type of hagfish that can be found in foul waters in [Glenumbra](Lore_Glenumbra.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Hake
The **Hake** is a type of fish that can be found in the [Sea of Ghosts](Lore_Sea_of_Ghosts.md), off the coast of [Rivenspire](Lore_Rivenspire.md).

- The **Bjoulsae Hake** can be found in the saltwater of the [Bjoulsae River](Lore_Bjoulsae_River.md) in [Bangkorai](Lore_Bangkorai.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Halibut
**Halibuts** are a type of fish.

- The **Abecean Halibut** is a type of halibut that can be found in the [Abcean Sea](Lore_Abecean_Sea.md), they can be fished off the coast of [Malabal Tor](Lore_Malabal_Tor.md). They can also be found around [High Isle](Lore_High_Isle.md), an island in the western Abecean Sea. Abecean Halibut Soup is a beloved dish from the island.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-TDOHI-3)</sup>

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Hammerjaw
The **Hammerjaw** is a type of fish.

- The **Giant Hammerjaw** can be found in the cold lake waters of the [Wrothgarian Mountains](Lore_Wrothgarian_Mountains.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Hardyhead
The **Hardyhead** is a type of fish that can be found in [Shadowfen](Lore_Shadowfen.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Harpoon-Fish
The **Harpoon-Fish** is a type of fish that can be found in [Murkmire](Lore_Murkmire.md). They swim very fast.<sup>[[4]](#cite_note-4)</sup>

**Found in:** - [Lore](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Xukas#Deepmire)

## Hatchetfish
The **Hatchetfish** is a type of fish that could be found in the planetouched waters of the [Imperial City](Lore_Imperial_City.md) during the [Planemeld](Lore_Planemeld.md).

**Found in:** - [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish)

## Histcarp

**Found in:** - [Skyrim](Skyrim_Histcarp.md), [ESO](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Online:Fishing#Rare_Fish), [Blades](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Blades:Histcarp)
