# Craft Bag

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e6/ON-prerelease-Craft_Bag.jpg/200px-ON-prerelease-Craft_Bag.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Craft_Bag.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-prerelease-Craft_Bag.jpg) An adventurer rummaging through their Craft Bag **Craft Bags** are utility boxes used by provisioners, crafters and adventurers to hold onto and sort near-infinite amounts of materials, widely used during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-Craft Bag ESO.2B-1)</sup><sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Display Craft Bag-2)</sup><sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Antiquity Craft Mention-3)</sup> A downside of craft bags is that alchemical reagents could be subject to cross-contamination, leading to some preferring more specialized methods of storage.<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Antiquity Craft Mention-3)</sup> For the discerning decorator, decorative display versions of craft bags were available for one's home.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Display Craft Bag-2)</sup>
