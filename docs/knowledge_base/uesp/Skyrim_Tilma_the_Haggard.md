[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/4/45/SR-npc-Tilma_the_Haggard.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tilma_the_Haggard.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilma_the_Haggard.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilma_the_Haggard.jpg) Tilma the Haggard in the living quarters of Jorrvaskr **Tilma the Haggard** is an [elderly](Skyrim_Elder.md) [citizen](Skyrim_Citizen_(class).md) and the maid of [Jorrvaskr](Skyrim_Jorrvaskr.md), cleaning up for the [Companions](Skyrim_Companions.md).

Dedicated to her duties, Tilma never leaves Jorrvaskr and will be found on the ground floor or in the quarters downstairs, doing a number of random activities around the clock, including sweeping, eating, sleeping, wandering, or just relaxing in a chair. The only exception to this routine occurs at the beginning of the quest [Glory of the Dead](Skyrim_Glory_of_the_Dead.md), when Tilma will attend the funeral of [Kodlak Whitemane](Skyrim_Kodlak_Whitemane.md).

She wields a [Skyforge Steel dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Skyforge_Steel_Dagger) and wears a set of lower-class clothing, a [belted tunic](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Belted_Tunic) with a pair of [boots](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Boots#0001be1b).

## Dialogue
Before you join the Companions, entering Jorrvaskr for the first time will net you the sight of newcomers [Athis](Skyrim_Athis.md) and [Njada Stonearm](Skyrim_Njada_Stonearm.md) engaged in a friendly brawl while the other Companions stand around and cheer. Tilma will not participate, and will instead stay in the background, mumbling:

*"I just try to keep out of the way when they start roughhousing."* When you greet her, she will say:

*"Oh, I'm just a servant, dear. You want to talk to one of the Companions, I'm sure."* *"I've been tending to the warriors of Jorrvaskr for as long as I can remember."* *"I see everything, hear everything and know everything."* *"Oh, I'm a good keeper of secrets. Have to be, in this hall."* At this point, and until you have completed the initiation quest [Take Up Arms](Skyrim_Take_Up_Arms.md), you can ask her about the Companions, which will make her elaborate:

**Who are the Companions?** *"A good bunch of men and women, who make too much a mess, if you ask me."* She is only available for conversation at one other point, namely when [Skjor](Skyrim_Skjor.md) loses his life at the end of [The Silver Hand](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Silver_Hand). When you bring her the news, she will be quite sad and comment:

**Skjor has fallen to the Silver Hand.** *"Oh my. He was always so kind to me. A bit rough around the edges, but a good man. I... I'll get his things in order."* She will bid you farewell with either:

*"Goodbye, dear."* *"Is it cold in here? I should put more wood on the fire..."*
