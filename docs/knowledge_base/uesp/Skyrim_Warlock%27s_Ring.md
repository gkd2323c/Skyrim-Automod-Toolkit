You will need to fish it up from a fishing spot east of [Morthal](Skyrim_Morthal.md) during the quest [No Contest](Skyrim_No_Contest.md)<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>.

## Related Quests
- **[No Contest](Skyrim_No_Contest.md)**<sup>[CC](Skyrim_Fishing.md)</sup>: Take part in a fishing contest.

## Notes
- The Warlock's Ring previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Warlock%27s_Ring) in *[Morrowind](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Morrowind:Morrowind)*.
- The Warlock's Ring was originally [mentioned](Lore_Anora%27s_Journal_(old).md) in the [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch.md) [Creation](Skyrim_Creation_Club.md), although the ring was not included with that Creation and all reference to it was removed shortly after its release. Curiously, the fishing spot where you find the ring is just across the river from [Myrwatch](Skyrim_Myrwatch_(place).md).
- The [ward](Skyrim_Ward.md) created by the Warlock's Ring stacks with [Spellbreaker](Skyrim_Spellbreaker.md) and the [Staff of Hasedoki](Skyrim_Staff_of_Hasedoki_(item).md), giving you three wards.
- The Warlock's Ring benefits from the [Necromage](Skyrim_Necromage_(perk).md#Necromage_when_playing_as_a_vampire) perk.
- The Warlock's Ring activates in Werewolf Form, auto ward and Fortify Healing Rate, and Fast active effects.
