Helath Stormbinder was a [Dunmer](Lore_Dunmer.md)<sup>[[nb 1]](#intnote_nb1)</sup> warrior and champion of [Boethiah](Lore_Boethiah.md) who once owned the [Ebony Mail](Lore_Ebony_Mail.md). At some point long before the [Imperial Simulacrum](Lore_Imperial_Simulacrum.md), he lost her favor and she took back the Mail, an event often compared to the tale of [Kynareth](Lore_Kynareth.md) taking the [Lord's Mail](Lore_Lord%27s_Mail.md) from [Morihaus](Lore_Morihaus.md). Boethiah then hid the Ebony Mail away somewhere in [Tamriel](Lore_Tamriel.md), where it remained for many years. A map would eventually be discovered in a dungeon in [Black Marsh](Lore_Black_Marsh.md) which led to another dungeon in another province, where the Mail would finally be recovered by the [Eternal Champion](Lore_Eternal_Champion.md).<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-ARtifact-1)</sup>
