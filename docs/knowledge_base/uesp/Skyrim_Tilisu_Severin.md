[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/b/bf/SR-npc-Tilisu_Severin.jpg/200px-SR-npc-Tilisu_Severin.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilisu_Severin.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-npc-Tilisu_Severin.jpg) Tilisu Severin **Tilisu Severin** is a [Dark Elf](Skyrim_Dark_Elf.md) living in [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md). She is known in town as the wife of [Vendil](Skyrim_Vendil_Severin.md) and mother of [Mirri](Skyrim_Mirri_Severin.md).

Tilisu wears some [fine clothes](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086991) along with matching [boots](Skyrim_Clothing.md#00086993). She carries a leveled [dagger](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Dagger) up to [daedric](Skyrim_Daedric.md) quality, along with the [key to the manor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Severin_Manor_Key) and a [selection of upper-class items and gold](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Leveled_Lists#Rich).

She sleeps from midnight to 6am, after which she starts wandering around the house. At 11am she heads outside to go and browse the market stands for 5 hours. When her daily browsing is done she will head back to the manor, where she will idle around until she goes back to bed at midnight.

## Related Quests
- **[Served Cold](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md)**: Save [Councilor Morvayn](Skyrim_Lleril_Morvayn.md) from an assassination plot.
- **[It's All In The Taste](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:It%27s_All_In_The_Taste)**: Distribute [Sadri](Skyrim_Geldis_Sadri.md)'s [Sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Sadri%27s_Sujamma) to the citizens of [Raven Rock](Skyrim_Raven_Rock.md).

## Dialogue
Her greetings:

*"My husband Vendil feared visitors would shun Raven Rock. I'm glad to see he was mistaken."* *"Always nice to speak to someone from the mainland."* *"I hope you enjoy your stay in Raven Rock."* *"May your path be free from danger."* *"You've brought so much hope to our people, we are forever in your debt."* **([Reopened the mines](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md))** Should a beggar approach her at the market, she will only say:

*"Ask my husband. I'm busy."*
### Conversations
When in Severin Manor, Tilisu will talk with the rest of her "family", revealing that their family is just a front for an unspecified [job](Skyrim_Served_Cold.md) and that Vendil and Mirri are actually lovers:

**Tilisu**: *"What's wrong with you? Do you want to ruin everything?"*
**Vendil**: *"I don't know what you're talking about. I'm here to do a job, and that's it."*
**Tilisu**: *"I've seen the way you look at her. Do you take me for a fool?"*
**Vendil**: *"Look, "dearest", what I do on my own time is my business. You stick to your business and we'll both be fine."*

**Mirri**: *"This town bores me. How much longer must we stay in this wretched place?"*
**Tilisu**: *"If everything goes well, we won't have to stay here much longer. For now, we wait."*
**Mirri**: *"What am I supposed to do until then?"*
**Tilisu**: *"I don't know, look for someone else to hop into bed with perhaps!"*

Meanwhile, Vendil and Mirri will talk about Tilisu and how their relationship brings their plan in danger:

**Vendil**: *"I'm worried. I think she knows."*
**Mirri**: *"She's jealous. Can't say that I blame her."*
**Vendil**: *"Have you taken leave of your senses? We could be exposed... ruined. Ten years of planning all for nothing!"*
**Mirri**: *"It's your choice, "father". Anytime you want to end it, you just give the word."*

### Dragonborn

**Do you know someone called Miraak?** *"Miraak... I'm sure I've heard that name before. Haven't I? Was it in a dream?"* **Where have you heard his name? Please try and think.** *"I just can't be sure. I... I don't think I like talking about this."*
### It's All In The Taste
When asked if she wants to [taste Geldis' sujamma](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:It%27s_All_In_The_Taste), she'll accept:

*"Sure, got nothing better to do."*
### Served Cold
When [March of the Dead](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:March_of_the_Dead) and [The Final Descent](Skyrim_The_Final_Descent.md) are completed, you will be given a special task by [Second Councilor Arano](Skyrim_Adril_Arano.md) concerning an assassination plot. You will be sent to [Ulen Ancestral Tomb](Skyrim_Ulen_Ancestral_Tomb.md) where you must uncover the identity of an unknown visitor. You will eventually encounter Tilisu, in silent prayer near the central grave. If you sneak up close enough to her, the quest log will update and tell you to report back to Arano. Should you approach her openly, however, she will turn around and react surprised:

*"Oh, excuse me! I didn't realize there was anyone else here."* **What are you doing here?** *"I presume the same thing you're doing... leaving an offering for the departed. Forgive my surprise, it's just that I wouldn't expect that sort of behavior from someone who isn't Dunmer."* **You aren't an Ulen.** *"Since there aren't any Ulens left on this island, I've taken it upon myself to leave the traditional offering in their stead."* You can choose between three options as part of your investigation:

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/5/5e/SR-quest-Served_Cold_03.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Served_Cold_03.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_03.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_03.jpg) Who puts [ash yams](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Ash_Yam) in the tomb? **You're lying. You're from House Hlaalu!**
*"What kind of nonsense is that!? I'm simply honoring a fellow departed Dunmer, and you hurl baseless accusations at me?"*

**Tell me why you're really here.**
*"What do you mean? I thought I was clear. I'm simply honoring a fellow departed Dunmer."*

**I'll let you get to it then.**
*"Oh, no bother. We all have a right to be here."*

After this exchange she will brush you off by saying:

*"Now, if you'll excuse me, I'd like to meditate a bit."* If you try and talk to her after this, she will only say:

*"Please, I don't wish to be disturbed while I'm meditating"* [![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/e/e3/SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg/200px-SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:SR-quest-Served_Cold_04.jpg) Careful Mirri... You're later sent search for [incriminating evidence](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:The_Ulen_Matter) inside of Severin Manor, however, you're not alone. Tilisu and Mirri are both located within and should you get detected by Tilisu, she will call out:

*"Careful Mirri... someone's in here with us!"* *"Come on, I know you're in here."* Should you get into a fight with Tilisu here, she will angrily yell:

*"I won't allow you to ruin years of planning!"* Once you have collected your evidence, and returned it to Second Councilor Arano, he will send you to the final location, namely [Ashfallow Citadel](Skyrim_Ashfallow_Citadel.md). Tilisu will only appear here if she was spared earlier while searching through the manor. Together with her "husband", she will attempt to make a last stand in the final room of the citadel. This time Tilisu is clad in a full set of [Morag Tong armor](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Morag_Tong_Armor). While attacking you, she will have some taunts that she shares with Vendil and Mirri:

*"For House Hlaalu!"* *"Morvayn will pay for this!"* *"Years of planning, ruined!"* *"I'll spill your blood, fetcher!"* *"Die, Redoran lackey!"* When you deliver the killing blow, she will fall to the ground with her last words being:

*"House Hlaalu will... have its revenge..."*
