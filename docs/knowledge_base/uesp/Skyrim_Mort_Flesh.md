## Creatures
A single sample of Mort Flesh is dropped by all [zombie](Skyrim_Zombie.md) enemies. Note that this does not include regular [reanimated](Skyrim_Reanimate.md) NPCs and creatures.

## Notes
- Mort Flesh previously [appeared](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Mort_Flesh) in *[Oblivion](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Oblivion:Oblivion)* and had a similar model.
