# Shadowbloom Clan

[![](https://images.uesp.net/thumb/c/c4/ON-npc-Berinodh.jpg/200px-ON-npc-Berinodh.jpg)](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Berinodh.jpg) [](https://en.uesp.net/wiki/File:ON-npc-Berinodh.jpg) One of the members of the Shadowbloom Clan ca. [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582) Shadowbloom Clan was one of numerous [clans](Lore_Reachman_Clans.md) of [Reachfolk](Lore_Reachfolk.md) that lived in [the Reach](Lore_The_Reach.md) during the [Second Era](Lore_Second_Era.md). They inhabited [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) and were known to have [werewolves](Lore_Lycanthropy.md) among its ranks. Unlike most Reachfolk clans who considered lycanthropy as a transaction with [Hircine](Lore_Hircine.md),<sup>[[1]](#cite_note-LSG-1)</sup> the Shadowbloom Clan members considered it as a blessing. They considered the sounds of Markarth unnatural and preferred the wilds, where all the sounds flew at random.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Berinodh-2)</sup>

## History
In [2E 582](Lore_Second_Era.md#2E_582), the [Gray Host](Lore_Gray_Host.md) descended onto the Reach to search for an ancient artifact of great power. Many of the Reachfolk clans stayed in [Markarth](Lore_Markarth.md) at the behest of Ard [Caddach](Lore_Caddach.md).<sup>[[3]](#cite_note-Medone-3)</sup><sup>[[4]](#cite_note-CVR-4)</sup><sup>[[5]](#cite_note-ONCaddach-5)</sup> Shadowbloom Clan was one of the groups that stayed in the city of Markarth.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Berinodh-2)</sup>

At some point, during their stay in the city, a pretender who claimed to be representative of the Shadowbloom Clan attempted to get into Ard Caddach's favor in order to usurp the leadership of the Shadowbloom clan and become a new chieftain. Upon learning of the treachery, Ard beheaded the traitor personally. This decision earned him the respect of some members of the Shadowbloom Clan.<sup>[[2]](#cite_note-Berinodh-2)</sup>
